        Robert Forster Meffert, a retired member of the New York Stock Exchange, died Saturday at the Memorial Medical Center in Savannah, Ga., after a long illness. He was 78 years old and lived in Beaufort, S.C.
        He was a retired lieutenant commander of the Navy Reserve and had been active in the Boy Scouts.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Carolyn Lindhjem; three sons, Robert Jr., of Washington; Richard, of Middletown, R.I., and John, of Charleston, S.C.; a daughter, Lynn F., of Danbury, Conn., and four grandchildren.
