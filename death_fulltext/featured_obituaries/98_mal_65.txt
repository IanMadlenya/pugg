
        Eric Richards Rippel, a New Jersey executive, died on Thursday in a car accident in Alexandria Township, N.J. He was 60 and lived in Alexandria Township.
        He had suffered a massive heart attack, according to the Douglas D. Martin Funeral Home in Clinton, N.J.
        Mr. Rippel, a former member of the New York Stock Exchange, was the chairman and chief executive officer of Intersearch Institute Inc., a research and educational organization based in Annandale, N.J. He was a former president of the Fannie E. Rippel Foundation of Basking Ridge, N.J., a foundation devoted to cancer and heart-disease research.
        Mr. Rippel was a native of Madison, N.J. His survivors include a daughter, Schoenley Lester of Killingworth, Conn., and a sister, Susan Rippel of Rome, Italy.
