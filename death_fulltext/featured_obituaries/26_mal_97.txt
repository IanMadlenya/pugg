        James S. Hays, a retired New York lawyer specializing in labor and corporate law, died Thursday at his home in Scottsdale, Ariz. He was 89 years old.
        Mr. Hays was a partner in the New York law firm of Kaye, Scholer, Fierman, Hays & Handler. He joined the firm in 1922, when it had six lawyers; today it has 375. He retired in the early 1970's.
        He was a graduate of Cornell University and of its law school. For many years he was the president of the Jewish Home and Hospital for the Aged in the Bronx.
        Mr. Hays is survived by two daughters, Helen Gordon of Hartsdale, N.Y. and Judith Eiseman of Milwaukee; eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
