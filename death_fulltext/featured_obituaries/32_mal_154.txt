        Sidney R. Pine, a lawyer, died of cancer Wednesday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in Manhattan. He was 79 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Pine specialized in setting up offshore insurance concerns for international companies and gave advice on doing business in the Bahamas, Bermuda, Hong Kong and elsewhere. At the time of his death, he was of counsel to LeBoeuf, Lamb, Leiby & MacRae. Previously, he headed his own law firm. He was born in Newark and graduated from Columbia University Law School.
        His wife, Lee, survives him.
