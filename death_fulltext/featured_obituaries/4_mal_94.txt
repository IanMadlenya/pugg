        James E. Chase, who served as Spokane's first black Mayor, died of cancer Tuesday at Sacred Heart Medical Center. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Chase was elected to the City Council in 1975 and was elected Mayor six years later. He served in that position until 1985, when he decided to step down.
