        Jerome H. Dols, a senior vice president of Chase Manhattan Bank, died of cancer Friday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 54 years old and lived in Chatham, N.J.
        Mr. Dols spent his entire career with Chase Manhattan, joining the bank in 1958. He was director of organization planning in Corporate Human Resources at his death.
        He is survived by his wife, Sandra, and two children, Barry and Andrea, all of Chatham; his father, Virgil J., of Minneapolis, and a brother, Thomas, of Laurel, Md.
