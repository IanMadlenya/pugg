        Cecil B. Kelley Sr., a former publisher and president of the Palm Beach Newspapers Inc., died of a heart attack Sunday. He was 76 years old.
        In 1937, he joined The News-Herald in Panama City, eventually becoming general manager, and in 1958 he was named publisher of The Jacksonville Journal.
        In 1959 he was named a vice president of Perry Publications and moved to West Palm Beach. In 1960, he was named president and general manager of The Palm Beach Post-Times, The Palm Beach Daily News and Palm Beach Life magazine. In 1975, he was named president of the Palm Beach Newspapers.
