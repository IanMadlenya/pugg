        Juan Alberto Melgar Castro, a general who served four years as Honduras's chief of state, died of a heart attack Wednesday. He was 52 years old.
        General Melgar Castro had also been chief of the armed forces. He took power April 22, 1975, overthrowing Gen. Oswaldo Lopez Arellano, who had been chief of state since 1963. He in turn was ousted by the Joint Chiefs of Staff on Aug. 9, 1979.
