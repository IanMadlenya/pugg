        Harry A. Bade Jr., a retired vice president of the Mobil Oil Corporation, died of a heart attack Friday at the Florida Hospital Medical Center in Orlando, Fla. He was 69 years old and lived in Vero Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Bade, who joined Mobil in 1945, was named vice president of investor relations in 1976, after serving as vice president of its marketing division since 1965. He was also a former vice president of the American Petroleum Institute.
        He received degrees from Cornell University and the Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration. He served with the Army Air Forces in Europe in World War II.
        He is survived by his wife, Virginia; a son, Harry A. III, of Fair Haven, N.J.; two daughters, Vicky Craig of Waccabuc, N.Y., and Lesley Allegro of New Canaan, Conn.; a sister, Carolyn Loucks of Bronxville, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
