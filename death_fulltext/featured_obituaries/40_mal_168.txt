        Howard Whitmore 3d, a partner in Miller, Anderson & Sherrerd, an investment management concern in Conshohocken, Pa., died of stomach cancer Sunday at the University of Pennsylvania Hospital in Philadelphia. He was 52 years old and lived in Haverford, Pa.
        Mr. Whitmore was the financial and administrative partner of Miller, Anderson at his death. He was also chairman of Remediation Technologies Inc., an enviornmental engineering company based in Boston. Earlier, he had been chief financial officer for Environmental Research & Technology Inc., an environmental services company with headquarters in Concord, Mass.
        Mr. Whitmore, born in Newton, Mass., was a graduate of Yale and of Harvard Business School.
        He was one of the original principals of the MAC Group, now an international management consulting company. After seven years there he returned to Harvard as chief financial officer and assistant dean of the Business School, a position he held from 1970 to 1978.
        He is survived by his parents, Howard Jr. and Henrietta M. Whitmore of Newton, and a sister, Nancy Ridge of Carlisle, Mass. His companion for many years was Ellen Krasney of Somerville, Mass.
