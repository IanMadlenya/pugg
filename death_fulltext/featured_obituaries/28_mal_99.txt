        Robert O. Geiger, a copywriter and editor at Lord & Taylor for 15 years, died of cancer yesterday at New York Hospital. He was 62 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Geiger was born and raised in Brooklyn Heights. He graduated from Fordham University and served in the United States Army during World War II.
        Shortly after, he worked as a copywriter at Macy's for several years, then did freelance work before becoming a writer, copy editor and copy chief at Lord & Taylor, where his mother had worked as a fashion model. He also taught copywriting at the Fashion Institute of Technology.
        There are no immediate survivors.
