        Henry Mechutan, the founder and president of the Mechutan Fur Company, died of a heart attack Thursday at Beth Israel Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Mechutan started his company in 1935 and was a longtime member and a past chairman of the fur division of the board of governors of the State of Israel Bonds organization.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Toby Lak; two sons, David, of Manhattan, and Jay, of the Bronx, and three grandchildren.
