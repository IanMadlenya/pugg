        Dr. William Miller, an associate clinical professor of endodontics at the Columbia University School of Dentistry, died of a brain hemorrhage last Friday at Humana Bennett Hospital in Fort Lauderdale, Fla. He was 82 years old and lived in Fort Lauderdale.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Sara Zimler; a sister, Betty Broad of Hollywood, Fla.; two sons, Jordan, of Los Angeles, and Zachary, of Seattle, and five grandchildren.
