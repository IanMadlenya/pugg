        Carl W. Priesing, former president of Wahl Eversharp Inc., manufacturers of pens and pencils, died of natural causes Monday at the home of his son, John, in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 89 years old and lived in Southbury, Conn.
        Mr. Priesing, who earned a master's degree from Harvard, joined the company in 1926. He later became executive director of the Lead Pencil Manufacturers Association.
        In addition to his son, he is survived by a daughter, Ann Foster of Bronxville; a sister, Sophia Condos of East Orange, N.J., and five grandchildren.
