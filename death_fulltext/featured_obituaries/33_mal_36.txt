        Dr. Robert Tuby, a retired orthopedic surgeon, died of a heart attack Saturday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 82 years old and lived in Great Neck, L.I.
        Dr. Tuby practiced in Brooklyn for more than 40 years. Most recently, he served as an expert witness in personal-injury and medical-malpractice cases. He was a co-author of Volumes 6 and 7 of ''Courtroom Projection of Trauma,'' a legal text.
        He was born in Brooklyn and graduated from New York University and Bellevue Medical College.
        Surviving are his wife, Evelyn; two daughters, Joan Weinsier of Great Neck and Suellen Globus of Manhattan; a brother, Joseph, of Brooklyn; a sister, Beatrice Wynne of Boca Raton, Fla., and five grandchildren.
