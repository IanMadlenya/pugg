        Arthur J. Fiehn, a former vice president of the international engineering firm of Burns & Roe, died Wednesday at Southwestern Vermont Medical Center in Bennington, Vt., after a long illness. He was 62 years old and lived in Manchester, Vt.
        Mr. Fiehn, who was born in Brooklyn, retired from Burns & Roe in 1985, after serving as vice president for 15 years. He was a mechanical engineer and headed a team of specialists that designed and built power plants.
        He is survived by his wife, Janet; a daughter, Margaret, of Philadelphia, and a son, Matthew, of Boulder, Colo.
