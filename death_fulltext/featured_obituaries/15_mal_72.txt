        Zenn Kaufman, a marketing specialist, writer and lecturer, died of heart failure April 6 at Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital. He was 81 years old and lived in Norwalk.
        Among Mr. Kaufman's books were ''Showmanship in Business'' and ''How to Run Better Sales Contests.'' He was an authority on motivational sales techniques and for 25 years wrote a syndicated column, ''Over the Fence,'' which appeared in newspapers and trade journals.
        He is survived by two sons, Roger, of Weston, Conn., and James, of Wellesley, Mass., and a grandson.
