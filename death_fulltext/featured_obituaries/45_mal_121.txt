        Dr. Eugene Sachs, who taught psychology at Queens College since 1964, died on Saturday at his home in Jamaica, Queens. He was 57 years old.
        The cause of death has not been established, his family said yesterday.
        Dr. Sachs, who was born in the Bronx, graduated from City College and received his doctorate from the University of Rochester. He taught at the New York Medical College before joining Queens College, where he was an assistant professor at his death.
        He is survived by his father, Harry, of Manhattan; two daughters, Tamara, of Manhattan, and Dr. Sharona, of Durham, N.C., and a brother, Sheldon, of Mt. Kisco, N.Y.
