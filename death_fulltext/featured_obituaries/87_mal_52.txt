
        William E. Zeiter, a corporate law specialist in Philadelphia, died on Friday at the University of Pennsylvania Hospital. He was 59.
        The cause was a thoracic aneurysm, said his wife, Jean, of Philadelphia.
        Mr. Zeiter was a senior partner in the firm of Morgan, Lewis & Bockius in Philadelphia, which he joined 35 years ago. He was a member of the Board of Governors of the American Bar Association.
        Mr. Zeiter was a graduate of Lehigh University and earned his law degree from New York University.
        He is survived by his wife.
