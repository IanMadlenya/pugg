        Patrick J. Growney, a retired partner of the New York law firm of Shearman & Sterling, died Monday at a convalescent home in Bryn Mawr, Pa. He was 81 years old and lived in Gladwyne, Pa.
        Mr. Growney, an alumnus of the Fordham Law School, had headed the real-estate department of the firm. He was also a former director of the Goelet Estate Company and former director and president of the Rye Ridge Realty Company.
        Surviving are a son, Patrick, of Gladwyne; a daughter, Lillian, of Palm Coast, Fla., and four grandchildren.
