        Jessica E. Holland, an oral historian, died Monday when the bicycle she was riding was hit by a car in Nuit St. Georges, France, where she was vacationing. She was 42 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Ms. Holland numbered among her corporate clients the New York Stock Exchange, Chemical Bank, the Mobile Oil Corporation, the Bankers Trust Company and A. T. & T. She also did interviews for the Columbia University Oral History Research Office. She graduated from Barnard College.
        She is survived by her parents, Dr. Jesse and Ethel Holland, and a brother, John, all of Manhattan.
