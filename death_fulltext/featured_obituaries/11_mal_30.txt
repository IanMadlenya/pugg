        Edward Scher, founder of a clothing store chain for career women, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Brooklyn. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Scher, a native of Manhattan, founded Attitude Clothing Stores Inc., 25 years ago and built it into a six-store chain. He retired in January 1987, when his daughter took over the family-owned company.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lillian Berliner; a sister, Ellie Skolnick of Queens; two sons, Dr. Michael, of Los Angeles, and Kenneth, of Brooklyn; a daughter, Brooke Phillips of Manhattan, and three grandchildren.
