        Joseph F. Stein, head of the Joseph F. Stein Cadillac Agency in Asbury Park, N.J., died on Wednesday at his house in Deal, N.J. He was 93 years old.
        Mr. Stein died of congestive heart failure, his daughter, Wilma, said.
        Mr. Stein founded the Stein Cadillac Agency in the early 30's. He had been a dealer of Pierce Arrows and other high-priced cars in Asbury Park.
        Mr. Stein was the father of Wilma Stein Tisch, past president of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies of New York, and the father-in-law of Laurence A. Tisch, chairman of the Loews Corporation and president of CBS.
        Mr. Stein was born in West New York, N.J., and graduated from the Columbia University School of Journalism in 1918. He worked for the United Press until 1921, when he went into the automobile business.
        Also surviving are eight grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren.
