        Isaac Hersh, a former principal of New Utrecht High School in Brooklyn, died Sunday of Alzheimer's disease at the Krever Nursing Center in Miami. He was 80 years old and lived in North Miami Beach.
        Mr. Hersh, a native of Brooklyn, became New Utrecht's principal in 1957 and remained in the post until 1971, when he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Esther P. Cohen; a son, Robert of Roslyn Heights, L.I.; a daughter, Carolyn Garfein of Orange, Calif.; two brothers, Sidney of Bethesda, Md., and Carl of Placentia, Calif.; and three grandchildren.
