        The Rev. Frank R. Heinze, a Presbyterian minister and former information officer for the United Presbyterian Church U.S.A., died yesterday at Thomas Jefferson University Hospital in Philadelphia after heart surgery. He was 68 years old and lived in Fort Washington, Pa.
        Mr. Heinze was director of communications for the United Presbyterian Church from 1961 until his retirement in 1983. In addition, he was editor of the Presbyterian publication Monday Morning.
        He is survived by his wife, Amy; four sons, Frank 2d of Feasterville, Pa., Edward of Yardley, Pa., Peter of Houston and Paul of Ocala, Fla.; a daughter, Linda Lee of Palisades Park, N.J.; six grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
