
        Carlos Duran, a former European boxing champion and the father of a reigning world champion, died on Wednesday of injuries suffered in a car crash. He was 54 years old.
        Mr. Duran, an Italian citizen who was born in Argentina, was the European superwelterweight and middleweight champion between 1966 and 1972. He retired in 1973 with a professional record of 65-10-7 with one no contest.
        Among his survivors is his son, Massimiliano Duran, the World Boxing Council cruiserweight champion.
