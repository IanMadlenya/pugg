        Albert J. Sileo, a retired New York City fire chief, died of heart failure Friday at Mercy Hospital in Rockville Centre, L.I. He was 67 years old and lived in Seaford, L.I.
        Chief Sileo joined the Fire Department in 1944, after his discharge from the Army Air Forces. In 1969, he was appointed assistant chief, the third highest position on the force. He retired in 1975 as Staten Island commander.
        Surviving are his wife, Grace; four daughters, Linda Dragunat of Syosset, L.I., Carol Insogna of Farmington, Conn., Diane Ferlanto, of Neshanic Station, N.J., and Janet, of Seaford; two sons, Albert, of Wantagh, L.I., and Gerard, of West Palm Beach, Fla., and four grandchildren.
