        Patrick Vincent Doyle, Ireland's leading hotelier, died Saturday after undergoing heart surgery. He was 65 years old.
        His hotel empire included seven hotels in his native Dublin, one in Britain and two in the United States: the Normandy in Washington and the Stratford Inn in Connecticut. He was chairman of the Irish tourist board for the last 14 years.
