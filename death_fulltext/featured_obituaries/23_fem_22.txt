        Teresa Rosen Gordon, a retired English teacher in New York City, died of stomach cancer Sunday at her son's home in San Francisco. She was 81 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Mrs. Gordon, a native of the Bronx, taught in the New York City public schools for 35 years. From 1951 to 1968, she taught English at Erasmus Hall High School in Brooklyn.
        She is survived by her husband, Joseph; two sons, Daniel, of Atlanta, and Frederick, of San Francisco; seven grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
