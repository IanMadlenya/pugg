        Thomas Keller, president of the International Rowing Federation since 1958, died of a heart attack Thursday night in Monte Carlo. He was 64 years old.
        Mr. Keller was in Monaco on a business and vacation trip when he died, the federation's secretary-general, John Boultbee, announced here today. Mr. Keller also served in an Olympic post as president of the General Association of International Sports Federations.
