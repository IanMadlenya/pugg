
        Charles Lipkin, a certified public accountant in Manhattan for several decades, died yesterday at his home in the Bronx. He was 98 years old.
        Mr. Lipkin maintained a full-time practice until five years ago and at his death was a principal of the firm of Lipkin & Lipkin with his son Henry. He was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of City College.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Gertrude Hillman; three sons, Henry, of Manhattan, Daniel, of Dresher, Pa., and Russell, of the Bronx; a daughter, Gilda, of the Bronx; four grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
