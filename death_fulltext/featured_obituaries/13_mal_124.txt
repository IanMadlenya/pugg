        Mannes F. Lipski, for more than 50 years a factory superintendent and leather buyer for prominent manufacturers of women's shoes, died of pneumonia Tuesday at St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan. He was 100 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Lipski was associated with I. Miller & Sons, Delmans and the D'Antonio shoe companies before retiring in 1966.
        Surviving are two sons, Norman C. Lipton of Queens and Robert L. Lipton of Silver Spring, Md.; five grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
