        Dr. David Rothstein, a general practitioner in Jackson Heights, Queens, for 50 years, died of bone cancer Friday at his home in Southbury, Conn. He was 88 years old.
        Dr. Rothstein came to the United States from a village near Minsk, Russia, in 1906. He specialized in pediatrics and was associated with St. John's Queens Hospital, Boulevard Hospital and Elmhurst General Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Miriam Kreiswirth; a daughter, Judith Roeder of San Francisco; a son, Jesse, of Purchase, N.Y.; a sister, Lee Segal of Jackson Heights; three grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
