        Alex E. Weinberg, a tax lawyer and partner in the firm of Weil, Gotshal & Manges, died Sunday at University Hospital. He was 44 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Weinberg's death was caused by respiratory failure, his brother, Michael, said.
        A native of Manhattan, Mr. Weinberg was a graduate of the University of Michigan and the New York University Law School. He was with the firm of Patterson, Belknap & Webb before joining Weil, Gotshal & Manges in 1972. He became a partner in 1976. In addition to his brother, who lives in New City, N.Y., he is survived by his parents, Eugene and Emmeline, of Pipersville, Pa.
