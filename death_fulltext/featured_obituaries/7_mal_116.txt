        Clement C. Costello, editor of The Sun of Lowell for 30 years, died of cancer Wednesday in Megeve, France, where he was vacationing. He was 68 years old.
        Mr. Costello was born here in 1918 and attended Lowell High School, Phillips Andover Academy, the Clark School of Hanover, N.H., and Dartmouth College. He served in the Navy in World War II.
        He leaves his wife, Mia; a son, three grandchildren, and a brother, John.
