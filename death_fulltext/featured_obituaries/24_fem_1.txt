        Dr. Elisabeth Hoffmann Dietz, a retired professor of education at Brooklyn College, died of pnuemonia Monday at Emory University Hospital in Atlanta. She was 80 years old and lived in Sun City, Ariz.
        Dr. Dietz taught at Brooklyn from 1949 until she retired in 1973.
        She was born in New York and was an alumna of Northwestern University. She earned a doctorate in education from New York University.
        Surviving are a son, Theodore Baker Warner of Bethesda, Md.; a daughter, Nadine Rea of Sharpsburg, Ga.; six grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
