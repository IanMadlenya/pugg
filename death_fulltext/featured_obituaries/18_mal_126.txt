        Justice Theodore Dachenhausen Jr. of State Supreme Court in Westchester died Friday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center after a long illness. He was 63 years old and lived in Katonah, N.Y.
        A graduate of Yale College and New York Law School, Justice Dachenhausen spent 14 years on the bench in four different courts.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Virginia Johnson; two sons, Sgt. 1st Class Theodore 3d, of Fort Lewis, Wash., and John H., of Pleasantville, N.Y.; two daughters, Laura J. of Goldens Bridge, N.Y., and Joan of Champaign, Ill., and a brother, Harold, of Anaheim, Calif.
