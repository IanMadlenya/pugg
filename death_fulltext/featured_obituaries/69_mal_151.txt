
        Howard H. Goodkin, an educator and publishing executive, died on Tuesday at Cabrini Hospice of Cabrini Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 40 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A cause of death was not immediately available.
        Mr. Goodkin was a vice president and the editorial director of the bilingual education department at the Macmillan/McGraw-Hill School Publishing Company. He joined the Macmillan Publishing Company 10 years ago.
        He earned a B.A. in Spanish at Montclair State College in 1975, when he began to teach in the Newark public schools' bilingual program. From 1976 to 1982, he worked for the United States Army Corps of Engineers on real estate documentation.
        Mr. Goodkin is survived by his mother, Anne Goodkin of Boynton Beach, Fla., and a brother, Dr. Michael Goodkin of Rose Valley, Pa.
