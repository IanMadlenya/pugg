        Morris Giloni, a stockbroker and a member of the board of the Jewish National Fund of America, a group that represents the Herut Zionists, died on Jan. 4 at the Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center. Mr. Giloni, who had suffered a massive stroke five days earlier at his home in Manhattan, was 80 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Margot Lewinski; two sons, Mark, of Stamford, Conn., and Bernard, of Dix Hills, L.I.; a daughter, Shera Farkas of Albany, and four grandchildren.
        Correction:  January 18, 1988, Monday, Late City Final Edition
