        William Manee, a New York lawyer and real-estate investor, died of a heart attack Jan. 16 at Beth Israel Hospital in Boston. He was 91 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Manee was associated briefly with the law firm of Bingham, Engler & Jones after his graduation from Columbia Law School in 1921. He then went into business for himself in real-estate investment, retiring in the early 1950's.
        He is survived by a son, Monte, of Scarsdale; a daughter, Sandra Hutchins of Bangor, Me., and four grandchildren.
