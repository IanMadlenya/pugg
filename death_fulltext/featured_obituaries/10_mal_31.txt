        Ben Itkin, a co-founder and past president of Itkin Brothers Inc., a distributor of office furniture, died Friday at the Miami Heart Institute after heart surgery. He was 78 years old and lived in Palm Beach, Fla., and Hillsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Itkin, who was born in Brooklyn, was a violinist and band leader early in his career. He started the office furniture business in 1934 with his brothers. Over the years the company became a leader in its field, winning awards for imaginative advertising. He retired in 1975.
        He is survived by his wife, Sylvia; a daughter, Susan Sarlin of Scarsdale, N.Y.; a son, Lewis, of Lyme, N.H.; a brother, Sam, of Florida, and seven grandchildren.
