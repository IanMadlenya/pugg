
        Edwin C. McDonald, an executive recruiting consultant, died on Tuesday at his home in Bronxville, N.Y.. He was 74.
        The cause was prostate cancer, his wife said.
        Mr. McDonald was chairman of McDonald, Long & Associates, an executive recruiting company in Scarsdale, N.Y. He founded his own firm in 1977 and merged it with another in 1990. He was a 1942 graduate of Yale University and served as a captain in the Marine Corps during World War II.
        He is survived by his wife, Suzette McDonald of Bronxville; two daughters, Joan McConnell of Armonk and Heather Lameiras of Dallas, and a sister, Betty Lynn Hickel of St. Louis.
