
        George F. Dinneen, a former vice president of Merrill Lynch & Company who began his own financial planning company in New Jersey, died on Friday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 48 years old and lived in Chatham Township, N.J.
        Mr. Dinneen died of pneumonia, a funeral home spokesman said.
        Mr. Dinneen, a graduate of Holy Cross College in 1966, received an M.B.A. from Syracuse University in 1968. He worked for the accounting firm Peat, Marwick, Mitchell & Company, now KPMG Peat Marwick, and for the American Express Company before becoming a vice president of financial planning for Merrill Lynch.
        In 1988, he founded G.F.D. Associates, a financial planning company in Chatham Township.
        He is survived by his wife, Livvy; a sister, Dr. Mary Jane Dinneen of Yellow Springs, Ohio; two daughters, Meghan and Ashley, and a son, Devon.
