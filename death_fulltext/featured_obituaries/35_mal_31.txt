        Goran Jarvefelt, a Swedish opera director, died of cancer on Thursday. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. Jarvefelt had established himself at leading opera houses, including the Metropolitan Opera in New York City, the Paris Opera and the Komische Oper in East Berlin.
        He worked with both classical and contemporary themes, and his staging of Richard Strauss's ''Intermezzo'' was performed at the Santa Fe Opera in 1984 to favorable critical notice.
