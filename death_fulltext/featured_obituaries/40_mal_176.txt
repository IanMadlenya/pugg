        Earl W. Seibert, a member of the National Hockey League Hall of Fame, died of cancer Sunday in Western Massachusetts Hospital. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Seibert, a defenseman, led the Chicago Blackhawks to the 1938 Stanley Cup championship and also played with the New York Rangers and the Detroit Red Wings during his 15-year National Hockey League career.
