        James Secrest, a stage and television actor, died of cancer on Wednesday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 51 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        In 1981, Mr. Secrest played the Rev. Daniel Berrigan in a highly regarded Off Broadway revival of ''The Trial of the Catonsville Nine.'' Along with other theater work, Mr. Secrest's credits included several television series, soap operas and movies.
        He is survived by his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Lowell Bowers, and his sister, Nansetta Still, all of Thomasville, N.C.
