        Paula L. Zajan, a professor of early childhood education at Hostos Community College, died of lung cancer Tuesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. She was 59 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Professor Zajan, a native New Yorker, began her career as a public school teacher. She joined the college in 1971 and was responsible for its early childhood education program.
        She was a 1950 graduate of Hunter College and received a master's degree from Hunter in 1953 and a doctorate from New York University in 1970.
        Surviving is her mother, Mary, of Manhattan.
