        Sidney Frank, a vice president of Miller Advertising Agency Inc., died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Lake Carmel, N.Y. He was 61 years old and also lived in Manhattan.
        He is survived by his wife, Charlotte; two sons, Harley and Matthew, both of Manhattan; a daughter, Anne Frank Shapiro of Manhattan; his mother, Goldie, of Woodridge, N.Y.; a sister, Eleanor Deutsch of Clifton, N.J., and two grandchildren.
