        Francis A. Goodhue Jr., a Manhattan tax lawyer for 50 years, died yesterday at Northern Westchester Hospital Center in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was 74 years old and lived in Mount Kisco.
        He died of heart failure, a funeral home representative said.
        Mr. Goodhue joined the firm Dewey Ballantine Bushby Palmer & Wood in 1940 after graduating from Harvard Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, State Senator Mary B. Goodhue; a son, Francis 3d of Mount Kisco; a sister, Phoebe Milliken of Greenwich, Conn.; two brothers, John, of Hancock, N.H., and Stephen, of Katonah, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
