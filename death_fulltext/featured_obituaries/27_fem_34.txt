        Sheila D. Feick, a former president of the Women's Committee of the New York Zoological Society, died of kidney and bone cancer yesterday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. She was 55 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Feick, who headed the committee from 1979 to 1981, was also a fund-raiser for Memorial Sloan-Kettering for many years.
        She is survived by her husband, Willliam; her mother, Sheila Brodie of Manchester, Vt.; a son, Andrew Hultkrans of Manhattan, a brother, Franklin Driggs of Brooklyn, and a sister, Jean Pfister of San Jose, Calif.
