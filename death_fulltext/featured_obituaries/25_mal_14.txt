        C. Everett Bacon, an eight-letter athlete in football, baseball, tennis and golf at Wesleyan University who was selected as an all-America quarterback in 1912, died of congestive heart failure Wednesday at Southampton Hospital. He was 98 years old and lived in East Hampton, L.I.
        Mr. Bacon was inducted into the National Football Foundation Hall of Fame in 1966. He retired as senior partner of the investment banking firm of Spencer Trask & Company in 1968. He left no survivors.
