
        A memorial service for Skitch Henderson, the ''Tonight'' show bandleader and founder of the New York Pops, will be held on Monday at 4 p.m. in Carnegie Hall. He died on Nov. 1 at 87.
        The service, which will be open to the public, is to include performances by the New York Pops with Kitty Carlisle Hart. Among the scheduled speakers are former Mayor Rudolph W. Giuliani, Marilyn Horne, Liz Smith and Mike Wallace. The tickets will be available at the Carnegie box office, beginning on Monday at 2 p.m.
