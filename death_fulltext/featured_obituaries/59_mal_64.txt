
        Jack F. Kincannon, a retired top executive of Sears, Roebuck & Company, died Dec. 3 at Presbyterian Hospital in Dallas. He was 72 years old and lived in Dallas.
        He died of cancer, his family said.
        In a 41-year career at Sears, he became a vice president and chief financial officer when it was the world's largest retailer, retiring in 1980.
        He is survived by his wife, Mildred, of Dallas; a daughter, Judith, of Manhattan, and two brothers, Wayne of Lorena, Tex., and Neil, of near Houston.
