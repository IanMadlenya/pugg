        John H. Poelker, an accountant who served one term as Mayor after 10 years as comptroller, died today after a long struggle against cancer. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Poelker, a Democrat who began his political career in 1953 when he became the city's youngest assessor, was Mayor from 1973 until 1977. He did not seek re-election.
        Mr. Poelker is survived by two daughters and a son.
