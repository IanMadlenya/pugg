        Peter D. Kiernan, chairman of Fleet/ Norstar Financial Group, a national financial services company, died Wednesday at Massachusetts General Hospital in Boston from complications following heart surgery. He was 65 years old and lived in Newtonville, N.Y.
        Mr. Kiernan became chairman and chief executive of Fleet/Norstar last year, after a merger between the Fleet Financial Group and Norstar Bancorp. Under the terms of the merger, he was to retire at the end of 1988. He had been the president, chairman and chief executive of Norstar for 14 years. Previously, he ran a family insurance business in Albany.
        Mr. Kiernan was born in Albany and graduated from Williams College. He was a former president of the New York State Bankers Association.
        Surviving are his wife, Mary Agnes; five sons, Peter 3d, John, Gregory, Michael and Stephen; two daughters, Mary Carroll and Anne; two sisters, Carroll Devitt and Jane Gabriels, and six grandchildren.
