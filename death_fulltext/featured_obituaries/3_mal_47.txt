        Saul Miller, former information director for the A.F.L.-C.I.O., died of a heart ailment at a hospital here Thursday, the labor federation said. He was 68 years old Mr. Miller retired from the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations in 1981 after serving two years as its chief spokesman. A former newspaper reporter and editor, he had been director of publications for the federation from 1958 through 1979.
        Before joining the old American Federation of Labor in 1955, Mr. Miller worked for newspapers in York, Pa.; Richmond, Va., and Jamestown, N.Y. He leaves his wife and four children.
