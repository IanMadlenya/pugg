        Bernard J. Meislin, a corporate lawyer, died of pancreatic cancer Aug. 17 at St. Barnabas Hospital in Livingston, N.J. He was 60 years old and lived in Short Hills, N.J.
        Mr. Meislin founded the law firm Bressler & Meislin in Manhattan in 1960. In 1977 he went into practice for himself in Roseland, N.J. He was an expert in Jewish law, his family said.
        He graduated from the University of Michigan and Harvard Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, Zelda; brother, Aaron, of Manhattan; two daughters, Amy Pollack and Nancy Siegel, both of Livingston; a son, Warren, of Hoboken, and six grandchildren.
