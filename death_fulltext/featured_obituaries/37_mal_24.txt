        George Campbell Becket, a lawyer in Manhattan and Lakeville, Conn., for more than 60 years, died of a stroke yesterday at Sharon (Conn.) Hospital. He was 88 years old and lived in Lakeville.
        Mr. Becket retired in 1985, after almost 40 years as a partner in the firm of Becket & Wagner in Lakeville. He then became a consultant to Shipman & Goodwin in Hartford.
        Mr. Becket helped draft the charter for the 1939 New York World's Fair while he was with the law firm of Davis, Polk, Wardwell, Gardiner & Reed in Manhattan.
        He graduated from the Yale University Law School in 1926.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Elise Barr Granbery; two sons, James, of Lakeville and Santa Monica, Calif., and Peter, of Lakeville; a daughter, Elise Becket Smith of London; a sister, Jean, of Lakeville, and 10 grandchildren.
