        John E. Reeves, former chairman of Reeves Brothers, Inc., a Manhattan textile manufacturer, died on Tuesday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 77 years old and lived in Summit.
        Mr. Reeves was named chairman and chief executive officer in 1967 and remained in the post until he retired in 1975. He joined the family-owned company - it later went public - in the mid-1930's and became president in 1950. He was president of the American Textile Manufacturers Institute from 1973 to 1974.
        He was born in Mount Airy, N.C., and graduated from North Carolina State University in 1935, with a degree in textile engineering.
        Mr. Reeves is survived by his wife, the former Jeneil Beason; a son, John E. Jr. of Summit; two daughters, Carolyn Strong of Warrenton, Va. and Jane Mahelis of Nassau, the Bahamas, and six grandchildren.
