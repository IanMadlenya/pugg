        Ernie Blake, founder of the Taos Ski Valley resort, died of pneumonia Saturday. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Blake, who was inducted into the United States Ski Hall of Fame in 1987, was born Ernest Bloch in Germany in March 1913 and grew up near the ski resort of St. Moritz.
        In 1938 he came to the United States. He served in military intelligence in World War II, and helped to interrogate the Nazi leaders Hermann Goring and Albert Speer.
