        Renee Pollack, executive director of Westchester Jewish Community Services, died of a cerebral hemorrhage Thursday in White Plains Hospital Medical Center. She was 53 years old and lived in Hartsdale, N.Y.
        Under her leadership, Westchester Jewish Community Services became the first mental health agency in New York State to be certified as a home health agency.
        She is survived by a son, Ross, of Hartsdale, and a daughter, Janet, of White Plains.
