        Hans W. Vahlteich, a former research chemist for Best Foods Inc., a division of CPC International Inc., died Aug. 31 at his summer home in Craftsbury, Vt. He was 93 years old and lived in Cleveland Heights, Ohio. He had lived in Fort Lee, N.J., for more than 60 years.
        Mr. Vahlteich, who helped develop margarine through the hydrogenation of vegetable oils, joined Best Foods in New Jersey in 1924. He became vice president of research and quality control in 1943, a post he held for 18 years. He retired in 1961.
        He is survived by a daughter, Beverly DeLaney of Cleveland Heights, and five grandchildren.
