
        Mark D. Offen, a partner in the Manhattan law firm of Parker Chapin Flattau & Klimpl, died on Wednesday at Mount Sinai Medical Center. He was 44 years old and lived in Kew Gardens Hills, Queens.
        He died of colon cancer, his family said.
        Mr. Offen was born in Brooklyn and was a graduate of Brooklyn College and New York Law School. He joined the law firm in 1977 and became a partner in 1984, specializing in criminal defense matters.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Toby Cubac; three daughters, Lonny, Shani and Zippy, and a sister, Gwen Fabricant of Manhattan.
