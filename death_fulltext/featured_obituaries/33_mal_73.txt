        Leslie S. Pearl, a retired advertising executive, died Tuesday after a long illness at his home in Katonah, N.Y. He ws 87 years old and also lived in Blue Hill, Me.
        Mr. Pearl, who was born in London, began his career in 1923 as a copywriter at Barton, Durstine & Osborn, later Batten, Barton, Durstine & Osborn. He retired in 1958 as a director of the company. After retirement, he wrote advertisements for Wallach's stores.
        There are no survivors.
