
        Charles Burr Lamar, an architectural and interior designer, died on Monday at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. Mr. Lamar, who lived in Manhattan, was 69.
        The cause was cancer, his family said.
        Mr. Lamar was a founder, with the Preservation Society of Newport, R.I., of Historic Newport Reproductions, which manufactured and sold copies of antiques.
        Mr. Lamar is survived by a sister, Muriel Holliday of Mineola, L.I.
