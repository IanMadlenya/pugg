        Wyndham Lewis Gary, retired treasurer and member of the board of J. P. Stevens & Company Inc., a division of Fairleigh Industries, Greenville, S.C., died Saturday at the California Rehabilitation Center in Palm Springs after a long illness. He was 73 years old and lived in Fair Haven, N.J., and Palm Springs.
        Mr. Gary was a graduate of Yale University and its law school. He joined the Manhattan law firm of Breed, Abbott & Morgan after World War II service as a captain in the Army and remained with it for 14 years.
        In the 1950's, Mr. Gary was Police Commissioner of Rumson, N.J., and a member of the Town Council.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Shirley Spaulding, and a son, Wyndham Bradford Gary of Washington.
