        Rene P. Dentan, a former chief executive of Rolex Watch USA, Inc., died of lymphoma on Sunday at his home in Colorado Springs. He was 77 years old.
        A graduate of the University of Lausanne in Switzerland, Mr. Dentan was head of United States operations for the Swiss-based watch company for 45 years. He started out as the only representative of the company in the United States and rose to become president and chairman emeritus.
        He was a past president of the 24 KT Club, an organization of jewelery manufacturers and trade executives.
        He is survived by his wife, Joleen; a daughter, Denise Dalton of Hurdle Mills, N.C.; two sons, Chester, of Santa Fe, N.M., and Bruce, of Philadelphia; a sister, Marianne Benson of Jim Thorpe, Pa., and four grandchildren.
