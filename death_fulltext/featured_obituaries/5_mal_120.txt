        Joseph S. Boland, an actor who appeared on the original ''Tonight Show,'' ''Love of Life,'' and ''Playhouse 90'' on television, died here Sunday. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Boland played Father Knickerbocker on the ''Tonight Show'' with Steve Allen and portrayed Senator Ed Deakins on ''Love of Life.'' He also appeared in the movies ''Advise and Consent'' and ''The Rope Dancers.''
