        Henry Okamoto, an artist who founded the Clay Art Center in Port Chester, N.Y., died of cancer of the colon last Saturday at his home in Greenwich, Conn. He was 65 years old.
        Mr. Okamoto, who was born in Lodi, Calif., founded the center 30 years ago as a school and studio for ceramic artists.
        He is survived by two brothers, Fred, of El Cerrito, Calif., and Jin, of Lodi, and a daughter.
