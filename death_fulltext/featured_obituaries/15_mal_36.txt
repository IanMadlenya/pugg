        Bertram Jacobs, a retired deputy chief of the New York City Fire Department, died of cardiac arrest Wednesday at St. Clare's Hospital. He was 71 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Jacobs, who was born in the Bronx, was a graduate of Brooklyn College and Pace University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Myrna Klorman; a son, Mark, of Manhattan, and two brothers, William, of Livingston, N.J., and Jason, of Kings Point, L.I.
