        Msgr. Stephen J. Hudacek, retired pastor of St. John Nepomucene Roman Catholic Church on the East Side of Manhattan, died of a heart attack on Saturday at Yonkers General Hospital. He was 82 years old and had lived at the Hudson View Nursing Home in Yonkers for the last five years.
        Monsignor Hudacek, a native of Mahanoy City, Pa., was a graduate of St. Joseph's Seminary in Yonkers. He was pastor of Holy Trinity Church in Yonkers for more than three decades before becoming pastor of St. John Nepomucene in 1968. He retired in 1982.
        There are no immediate survivors.
