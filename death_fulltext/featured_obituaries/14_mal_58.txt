        Eugene Lynn, a partner in the investment banking firm of Wertheim & Company, died Tuesday at his home in Manhattan after a long illness. He was 77 years old.
        Born in Manhattan, Mr. Lynn had been in the securities business since 1927. He joined the Wertheim firm in 1953. He had been the head of the corporate bond department, specializing in fixed-income securities.
        He is survived by his wife, Amy; a son, Bruce, of Darien, Conn., a daughter, Robin, of Manhattan, and six grandchildren.
