        Arthur E. B. Tanner, former speaker of the Connecticut House of Representatives and past president of the Waterbury Foundry Company, died Sunday at his home here. He was 78 years old.
        Mr. Tanner, a Republican, served six terms in the State House of Representatives, the last, from 1953 to 1955, as speaker.
        He leaves a son, Arthur, of East Haddam; a daughter, Jane Tanner Kendrick of Woodbury, and four grandchildren.
