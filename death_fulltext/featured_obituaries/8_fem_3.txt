        Joan Showstead, the Western Hemisphere editorial director of the Platt's Group, an energy news service of McGraw-Hill Inc., died Aug. 22, apparently of a heart atttack, at her home in Iselin, N.J. She was 51 years old.
        Mrs. Showstead joined McGraw-Hill in 1974. She had been editor in chief of Platt's Oilgram Price Report, a daily information service, since 1981, and was named the Platt's Group hemisphere editorial director Jan. 1. She received a McGraw-Hill achievement award this year for longtime editorial excellence.
        Survivors include a daughter, Julia, of Egg Harbor City, N.J., and two sons, William, of Iselin, and Steven, of Clifton, N.J.
