        Frederick Haviland, the executive vice president of Haviland China Inc., died of cancer Sunday at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mt. Kisco, N.Y. He was 77 years old and lived in Somers, N.Y.
        Surviving are his wife, Marie, and two brothers, Theodore, of New London, N.H., and Harold, of Dieppe, France.
