
        Edward P. Bemberg, former chairman of the Rheingold Brewery in New York, died on Friday at his home in Buenos Aires. He was 76.
        A spokesman for the family said the cause was liver cancer.
        Mr. Bemberg was Rheingold's chairman from 1949 until 1964, and from 1979 to 1990 he was also chairman of Entreprises Quilmes, a financial holding company in Buenos Aires. He was born in Buenos Aires and graduated from Harvard College in 1945.
        He is survived by his wife, Georgia Hatch Bemberg of Buenos Aires; a son, Peter, and a daughter, Alexandra de Yturbe.
