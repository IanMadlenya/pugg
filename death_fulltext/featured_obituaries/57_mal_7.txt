
        Page Sunderland Procter, a retired advertising executive, died on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 73 years old.
        He died of esophageal cancer, his son, Benjamin, said.
        Mr. Procter worked for Time Inc., the J. Walter Thompson agency, and Thompson Recruitment, a separate concern from J. Walter Thompson, which creates advertising to recruit personnel and executives for businesses.
        Mr. Procter was born in Bloomington, Ill. He was a 1939 Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Northwestern University and earned a master's degree in English from Yale University in 1941 and a doctoral degree in that field in 1949. During the war Mr. Procter served in the Navy as a communications officer with the rank of lieutenant.
        In addition to his son, Benjamin, of Manhattan, Mr. Procter is survived by two daughters, Dorinda Canzeri of White Plains and Sundy Procter of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
