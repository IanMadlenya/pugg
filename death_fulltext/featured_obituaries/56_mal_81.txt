
        Lawrence I. List, a senior partner in the law firm of Carter, Ledyard & Milburn, died yesterday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in New York City. He was 53 years old and lived in Brooklyn Heights and Tolland, Mass.
        He died of complications from lung cancer, the family said.
        Mr. List graduated from Yale University in 1958 and from Harvard Law School in 1962. He was also a Woodrow Wilson National Fellow at the Harvard Graduate School of Arts and Sciences. After law school, he joined the New York law firm of Botein, Hays, Sklar & Herzberg, where he was a partner for 20 years. In 1990 he became a partner at Carter, Ledyard & Milburn. He was a specialist in real- estate law.
        He is survived by his wife, Bobye Goodman List; two daughters, Deborah and Mali, and his mother, Sydelle List Gross of New York City.
