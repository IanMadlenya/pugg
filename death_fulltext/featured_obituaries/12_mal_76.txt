        Ralph H. Cutler Jr., a commodity executive, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Morristown, N.J. He was 69 years old.
        Mr. Cutler, who received an M.B.A. from Harvard in 1947, was most recently a vice president of C. Tennant Sons & Company and of the KMC Metals Company.
        Surviving are his wife, Elizabeth Lee; his mother, Ruth, of Hightstown, N.J.; two sons, Timothy, of Mendham, N.J., and Stewart, of New York City, and a daughter, Cynthia, of San Francisco.
