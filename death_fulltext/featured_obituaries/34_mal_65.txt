        Dr. Farres P. Mattar, a senior research physicist at New York University, died of cardiac arrest Wednesday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 41 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Mattar, who was born in Cairo, joined New York University in 1983 and was also an associate professor at the Graduate Center of City College of New York. He received degrees from the University of Cairo and the Polytechnic Institute of New York.
        Surviving are his wife, Dr. Eliana; a son, Ken; three sisters, Nadia, of Cairo, Mary, of Montreal, and Jeanine, of Alexandria, Egypt, and a brother, Tony, of Cairo.
