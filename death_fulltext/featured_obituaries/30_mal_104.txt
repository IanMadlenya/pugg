        Max Edelman, a lawyer specializing in insurance matters, died of cancer Tuesday at St. John's-Riverside Hospital in Yonkers. He was 82 years old and lived in White Plains.
        Mr. Edelman was a partner in Wilson, Elser, Moskowitz, Edelman & Dicker, a Manhattan-based law firm which he co-founded in 1961. Mr. Edelman was born in Brooklyn. He graduated from New York University and its law school.
        Surviving are his wife, Lillian; two daughters, Lenore Saltman of Washington; and Meryl Katz of Tarrytown, N.Y.; two sons, Dr. Paul Carton of New Rochelle and Martin Edelman of Rye, N.Y.; three brothers, Edward, of Ithaca, N.Y., Sydney, of Washington and Milton, of New York City, and nine grandchildren.
