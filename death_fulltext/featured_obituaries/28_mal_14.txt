        Walter Wilson Jeffers, a retired banker, died Saturday at his home in Plandome, L.I., after a long illness. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Jeffers joined National City Bank, now called Citibank, after graduating from Harvard University in 1936. He retired as a vice president in 1976. He served on the Manhasset School Board from 1958 to 1964.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary Walden Bell; two sons, Christopher, of Portola Valley, Calif., and Landon, of Plandome Heights, L.I.; a daughter, Louise Brami of Lexington, Mass., and seven grandchildren.
