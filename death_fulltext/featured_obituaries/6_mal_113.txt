        Lewis R. Eisner, president and chief executive officer of Lewis R. Eisner & Company, a private investment banking and management resources company, died of a ruptured aorta Tuesday at White Plains Hospital. He was 62 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Eisner, who was in the commercial finance industry for more than 40 years, founded the firm in 1971. Born in New York City, he was a graduate of New York University and New York Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, Rhoda; a son, Paul, of Wassaic, N.Y., and a daughter, Jane Corby of Tarrytown, N.Y.
