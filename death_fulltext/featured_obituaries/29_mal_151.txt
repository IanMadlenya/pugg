        Robert Knittel, a former editorial director of William Collins, a London-based publishing company, died June 15 of complications following surgery at the Hospital of Charity in Locarno, Switzerland. He was 69 years old.
        Born in England, Mr. Knittel graduated from Oxford University. He retired from Collins in 1979.
        Surviving are his wife, Luise Rainer, a film actress who won two Academy Awards; a daughter, Francesca Bowyer, of Beverly Hills, Calif; two sisters, Baronesse von Bennigsen, of Bennigsen, West Germany, and Margrit Furtwangler, of Munich, and two grandchildren.
