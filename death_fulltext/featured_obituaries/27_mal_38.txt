        Dr. David I. Gaines, who taught history at City College for 40 years before retiring in 1967, died March 23 at the Jewish Home and Hospital for the Aged. He was 92 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Gaines specialized in modern European history.
        He was born in New York and received bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees from Columbia University.
        Surviving are two daughters, Judith and Laura, both of Manhattan.
