        Dermott F. McCarthy, a retired advertising executive at Young & Rubicam, died of a stroke Wednesday at his home in Albuquerque, N.M. He was 68 years old.
        Before he retired in 1983, Mr. McCarthy was a senior vice president in the Manhattan-based advertising agency. He had worked for Young & Rubicam for 39 years as a creative director in several offices.
        Mr. McCarthy was born in Los Angeles and graduated from Loyola Marymount University in that city.
        Surviving are his wife, Barbara; three daughters, Dierdre, Maeva and Moira; two sons, James and William, and six grandchildren.
