        The Rev. Dr. Samuel L. Taylor, a Brooklyn minister who served Presbyterian and Reformed Church congregations for more than two decades, died of cancer on Wednesday at his home in Brooklyn. He was 52 years old.
        Dr. Taylor was minister of the First Presbyterian Church of Brooklyn from 1969 to 1983 and minister of the Greenpoint Reformed Church from 1986 until his death.
        He was born in Huntsville, Ala., and was a graduate of Vanderbilt University and the New York Theological Seminary.
        Survivng are his wife, the former Judith Parker; a son, Granville; his father, L. M. Taylor of Huntsville, and a brother, Douglas, of Manhattan.
