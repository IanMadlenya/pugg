        The Rev. Adolf F. Meyer, a retired Lutheran pastor, died Wednesday in Newark. He was 89 years old and lived in Westwood, N.J.
        From 1923 to 1970, he was pastor of St. Mark's Lutheran Mission in Yonkers. He graduated from St. John's College, earned a master's degree from Columbia University and a doctorate from Concordia Seminary.
        Surviving are four sons, the Rev. Richard, of Pacific Palisades, Calif., the Rev. Paul, of Walnut Creek, Calif., the Rev. William, of Garden City, L.I., and James, of Silver Spring, Md.; a daughter, Ione Mensing of Hillsdale, N.J., and 13 grandchildren.
