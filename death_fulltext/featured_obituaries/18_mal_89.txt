        Seymour C. Lechter, a former executive in the apparel industry, died of cancer yesterday at the New York University Medical Center. He was 66 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Lechter held several executive positions in the retailing industry. He retired in 1982 as a vice president of the Henry I. Siegel Company, an apparel manufacturer in Manhattan.
        He is survived by a daughter, Margaret; a son Andrew, both of Atlanta, and two brothers, Max, of Silver Spring, Md., and Gerald, of Fort Lee, N.J.
