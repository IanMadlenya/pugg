        Dr. Edward I. Salisbury, a surgeon and specialist in tropical medicine, died of heart failure April 19 at St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn, L.I. He was 93 years old and lived in Manhasset, L.I.
        Dr. Salisbury is survived by a daughter, Mrs. Matthew C. O'Hearn of Fripp Island, S.C.; three sons, Edward, of Twentynine Palms, Calif., John, of Stamford, Conn., and Kevin, of Mendham, N.J., 19 grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
