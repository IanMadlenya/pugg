        Robert M. Grosse, an investment banker, died of heart failure Sunday at his home in Bondville, Vt. He was 52 years old and also had a home in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Grosse, a graduate of Yale University and the Harvard Business School, was managing director and president of the U.S.F. & G. Constitution Fund, a Connecticut investment company.
        He is survived by his wife, Gisela; a son, Eric, and a daughter, Ariana, all of Bondville; a brother, Richard, of Savannah Beach, Ga., and a sister, Gara Anthor of Washington.
