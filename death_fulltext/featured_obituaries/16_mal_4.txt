        Max Feldberg, a founder of the Zayre Corporation, a large national retail company, died of heart failure last Tuesday at Beth Israel Hospital in Boston. He was 90 years old and lived in Palm Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Felberg and his brother, Morris, opened the first Zayre store, a self-service discount department store, in 1956. Today, Zayre has 1,187 outlets.
        Born in Russia, Mr. Felberg emigrated with his family in the early 1900's. He was Zayre's chairman from 1969 to 1973 and a director until he retired in 1985.
        Survivors include his wife, Elizabeth; a son, Stanley of Hilton Head, S.C.; a daughter, Barbara Stern of Boston, six grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
