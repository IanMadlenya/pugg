        Philip W. Callanan, a former senior vice president at Hill & Knowlton, a New York advertising agency, died Friday at New York University Medical Center after a brief illness. He was 72 years old and lived in Fort Hamilton, N.Y.
        Mr. Callanan was a senior consultant for the agency at his death. He had been with the agency since 1951.
        He was born in Auburn, N.Y., and graduated in 1938 from Cornell University, where he also earned a doctorate.
        He is survived by his wife, Philbin Heath; a stepson, Maj. Bache Whitlock of the Army, and three grandchildren.
