        Robert Van Pelt a senior Federal district judge, died Wednesday. He was 90.
        Judge Van Pelt had been a member of the Federal bench since 1957. He retired in 1970 but continued to hear cases as a senior judge. He was active in the movement for public power and rural electrification in Nebraska.
        Judge Van Pelt made his mark on the national legal scene by serving as a special master appointed by the Supreme Court to handle several significant border disputes between states.
