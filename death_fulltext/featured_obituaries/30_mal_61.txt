        Dr. Max Zuger, a retired psychiatrist, died June 30 at Hillhaven Convalescent Center in Sarasota, Fla., where he lived. He was 86 years old.
        Dr. Zuger, a longtime resident of Woodmere, N.Y., practiced for 37 years before retiring in 1980. He was born in Ludmir, Russia, and was graduated from Fordham University and the New York Medical College.
        Surviving are his wife, Helen; a son, Daniel, of New York City; a daughter, Dr. Jane Z. Portnoy of Louisville, Ky.; a brother, Bernard, of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
