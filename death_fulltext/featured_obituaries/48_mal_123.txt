
        David M. McCann, a senior partner in the Newark law firm of Carpenter, Bennett & Morrissey, died yesterday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 51 years old and lived in Summit.
        He died of complications of colon cancer, a family spokesman said.
        Mr. McCann was a native of Jersey City who grew up in South Orange, and he was a graduate of the University of Notre Dame and Fordham University Law School. After graduation in 1964 he joined the Newark firm and became a partner in 1970. His specialty was corporate litigation.
        Mr. McCann is survived by his wife, Geraldine; three children, David, Lauren and Christine, all of Summit; his mother, Dorothy McCann, of Monmouth Beach, N.J.; three brothers, Frank, of Loch Arbor, N.J., John, of Rumson, N.J., and Michael, of Atlantic Highlands, N.J., and a sister, Dorothy, of Chatham, N.J.
