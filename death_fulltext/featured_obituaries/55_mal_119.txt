
        James Edward Clark, a dog sportsman and dog show judge, died on Monday in Centreville, Md. He was 64 years old.
        A spokesman for the family said Mr. Clark died of lung cancer.
        Mr. Clark was a manager at the American Kennel Club, a board member of the Dog Museum and a member of the Westminster Kennel Club and the Santa Barbara Kennel Club.
        He is survived by his wife, Anne Hone Rogers Clark.
