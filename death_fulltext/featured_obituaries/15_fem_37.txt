        Jean Picus Wright, managing editor of Seventeen magazine from 1955 to 1974, died of a lung ailment Wednesday at New York Hospital. She was 78 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Ms. Wright was born in Black River Falls, Wis. She attended the University of Minnesota. Ms. Wright came to New York and joined Triangle Publications Inc., where she became picture editor of Click magazine, before transferring to Seventeen in 1944.
        Surviving are three sisters, Pauline Reynard and Annette Overby, both of Manhattan, and Ruth Pedersen of Owen, Wis.
