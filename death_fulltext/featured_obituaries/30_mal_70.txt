        Irb H. Fooshee, a chemical engineer who retired in 1971 as an executive vice president of the Allied Chemical Corporation, died of prostate cancer Tuesday at Hilton Head Island, S.C., where he had lived since last year. He was 80 years old and had previously lived in Austin, Tex.
        Mr. Fooshee was born in Glen Rose, Tex., and was a graduate of Texas A. & M. College. He was with Gulf Oil for 16 years before joining Allied Chemical in 1947. He became head of its General Chemical division in 1958 and the following year was made manufacturing vice president of Allied, moving up to executive vice president in 1965.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lea Morgan; two daughters, Jeralea Levine of Woodbridge, Conn., and Nola Ray of Austin; a son, Richard, of Tenafly, N.J., and three grandchildren.
