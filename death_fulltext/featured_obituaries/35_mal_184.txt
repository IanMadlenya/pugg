        James J. Manderino, Speaker of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives, died early today, a few hours before he planned to announce his retirement next year, an aide said. He was 57 years old.
        Mr. Manderino was stricken at his home here, said the aide, Charles Bacas. The cause of death was not immediately known. Mr. Bacas said he had planned to issue a statement today announcing that Mr. Manderino would retire when his 12th House term ended next December.
        Gov. Robert P. Casey termed the death the loss of ''a true champion.''
