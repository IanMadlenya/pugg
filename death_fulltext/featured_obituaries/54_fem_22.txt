
        Bernice Jacobson, a retired consumer advocate who specialized in complaints about mail-order services, died on July 8 at Englewood Hospital in New Jersey. She was 75 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She died of complications after surgery, her family said.
        Her first husband, Henry Zeeman, died in 1963 and her second husband, Morty Jacobson, died in 1969.
        Surviving are a son, Roger Zeeman of Bridgewater, N.J.; a brother, Warren Hirsch of Weston, Conn., and a grandson.
