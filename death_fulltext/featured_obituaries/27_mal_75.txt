        Dr. John Dolce, an orthodontist who headed several organizations in the dental profession, died of heart failure yesterday at his home in Mamaroneck, N.Y. He was 78 years old.
        Dr. Dolce graduated from Fordham University and the Columbia University School of Dentistry, where he later taught.
        Among the professional groups he headed were the American College of Dentistry, the Bronx County Dental Society, the New York Academy of Dentistry, the Greater New York Dentists Association and the Northeastern Society of Orthodontics.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Muriel Cluen; a daughter, June Heffernan of Rye, N.Y.; three sons, Jed, of Harrison, N.Y., and Donn and James, both of Rye.
