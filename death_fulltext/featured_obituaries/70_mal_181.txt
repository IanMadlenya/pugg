
        Bernard M. Baruch Jr., a former member of the New York Stock Exchange, died on Thursday at the Mount Sinai Medical Center. He was 90 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Baruch, the son of the financier Barnard M. Baruch, was born in Manhattan.
        He was a member of the stock exchange from 1928 to 1936. He was commissioned in the Naval Reserve in 1937 and served in World War II, in Naval Intelligence.
        He is survived by his sister, Renee Baruch Samstag, of Manhattan.
