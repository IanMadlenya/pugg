        Roy Schreiber, an owner of McCreedy & Schreiber, the family-operated Manhattan shoe store founded in 1922, died of Alzheimer's disease Monday at Northport Veterans Hospital on Long Island. He was 72 years old and lived in Manhasset Hills, L.I.
        Mr. Schreiber, who was born in Manhattan and attended New York University, served as an Army officer in the European theater in World War II.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Bella Gaines; a son, Robert, of New Orleans; three daughters, Abigail, of Seattle, and Susan and Nora, both of Manhattan, and a brother, Frank, of Manhattan.
