        Dan Kelly, the broadcaster of St. Louis Blues games for the last 21 years, died of cancer yesterday at his home in Chesterfield, Mo. He was 52 years old.
        Mr. Kelly, who was born in Ottawa, Ontario, was honored in December with the Lester Patrick Award, given annually by the National Hockey League and the New York Rangers for outstanding service to hockey in the United States. He will be inducted into the broadcasting wing of the Hockey Hall of Fame in September.
        He is survived by his wife, Fran, and six children, one of whom, John, is a broadcaster for the Rangers.
