        John L. Riegel, retired chairman of the Riegel Paper Corporation and the Riegel Textile Corporation, died of pneumonia Friday at White Plains Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Hartsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Riegel, a graduate of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, was president of the American Paper and Pulp Association from 1940 to 1942. He was chairman of Riegel Paper from 1936 to 1968 and of Riegel Textile from 1941 to 1963.
        He is survived by three children, Mrs. J. Bicknell Lockhart Jr. of Orange, Calif., John L. Riegel Jr. of Lindstrom, Minn. and William M. Riegel of Duxbury, Mass.; a sister, Marjorie Ingalls of Pelham; 10 grandchildren, and 12 great-grandchildren.
