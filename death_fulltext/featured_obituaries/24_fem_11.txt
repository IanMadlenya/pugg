        Micheline Keating, a novelist and reporter, died Friday in a nursing home in Tucson, Ariz. She was believed to be about 85 years old.
        Miss Keating's novels included ''Fame,'' ''Bachelor's Heyday,'' ''City Wise'' and ''Till Heaven Cracks.'' Her articles appeared in such newspapers as The New York Daily Mirror and The Tucson Citizen.
        There are no immediate survivors.
