        David Tyler Paul, an editor and publisher of educational books and video programming, died of lung cancer Monday at Abington Memorial Hospital, Willow Grove, Pa. He was 53 years old and lived in Chestnut Hill, Pa.
        Mr. Paul was the executive editor of instruction television for the Cambridge Book Company. He also had worked as a designer for Random House, Abelard-Schuman/Criterion Books and Macmillan, and wrote several children's books.
        He is survived by a daughter, Sarah, of New York City, and a son, Adam David, of Buffalo.
