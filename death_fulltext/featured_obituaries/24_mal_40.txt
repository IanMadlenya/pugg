        Vito Titone, a retired lawyer, died of heart failure Wednesday at his winter home in Hallandale, Fla. He was 91 years old and lived in Roslyn Heights, L.I., for the last year.
        Mr. Titone worked for 40 years in the office of the New York State attorney general. After he retired in 1954, he practiced law until two years ago.
        Surviving are his wife, Elena; a son, Vito J., of Staten Island, an associate judge of the State Court of Appeals; two daughters, Jeanette Busceme of Yorktown Heights, N.Y., and Marie Ann Izzo of Glen Head, L.I.; a brother, Charles, of Queens, and eight grandchildren.
