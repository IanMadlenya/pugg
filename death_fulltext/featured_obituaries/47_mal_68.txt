
        Franklin C. Snyder, a director of the Hearst Corporation and former general manager of Hearst Broadcasting, died Saturday at Presbyterian University Hospital of complications from a stroke. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Snyder became affiliated with the Hearst Corporation in 1958 as general manager of WTAE-TV in Pittsburgh,
        In 1968 he was elected a vice president of the parent corporation and general manager of Hearst Broadcasting, which expanded to six television stations and seven radio stations. He was a director of the William Randolph Hearst Foundation.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Patricia Garvey; three daughters, three sons and 10 grandchildren,
