        Martin Jelin, a former president of the United Jewish Federation of MetroWest, coordinators of social welfare services, died Sunday at St. Barnabas Medical Center in Livingston, N.J. He was 78 years old and lived in South Orange.
        Mr. Jelin was president of the Karnak Chemical Company in Clark for 55 years. He was president of the Hebrew Youth Academy of Essex County and a board member of Rutgers Unversity.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Sima Kislak; six children, and seven grandchildren.
