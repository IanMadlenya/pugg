        Brother C. Edward Quinn, a professor of biology at Manhattan College for 30 years, died of heart failure Tuesday at the Christian Brothers Center in the Riverdale section of the Bronx. He was 63 years old.
        Brother Edward began his teaching career at La Salle Academy in Providence, R.I. He joined Manhattan College in 1959, teaching biology. He became a full professor in 1983.
        Brother Edward also wrote two books on history and served as secretary to the Bronx Historical Society and editor of the Bronx Historical Society Journal.
        He is survived by his father, Aelred Leo Quinn, and his stepmother, Josephine, of Tilson, N.Y., and a brother, Joseph, of Worcester, N.Y.
