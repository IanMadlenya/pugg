
        Paul Cushman 3d is an executive vice president at Riggs National Bank in Washington and head of its international banking operations. He is 35.
        Mr. Cushman has spent his entire career with Riggs, which operates account relationships with many United States embassies overseas. He joined the bank in  1983 as a trainee. He assumed his current post in late 1989, only a few months after being named vice president of the bank's embassy division. Previously, Mr. Cushman had responsibility for overseeing Riggs's accounts with European embassies.
