        Lottie Stillerman Gold, a retired lawyer and former government official, died of a heart attack Monday at Long Beach (L.I.) Hospital. She was 82 years old and lived in Long Beach.
        Mrs. Gold, a graduate of Fordham University Law School, practiced in Manhattan for more than 50 years. She retired in 1987. She also served as Deputy Secretary of State in New York from 1955 to 1959 and was a hearing officer with the New York City Rent Commission for many years.
        She is survived by her brother, Irving, of Mount Vernon, N.Y., and a sister, Belle Henig of Chicago.
