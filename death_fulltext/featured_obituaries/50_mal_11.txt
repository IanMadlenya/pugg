
        Arthur Arzt, a retired manufacturer of corrugated containers, died on Thursday at St. Francis Hospital in Miami Beach. He was 85 years old and lived in Bal Harbor, Fla.
        He died of a heart attack, a family spokesman said.
        Mr. Arzt was general executive manager of the Shelton Manufacturing Company from 1930 until 1949. Since then he had been a private investor. He was also involved in Jewish philanthropies in New York City and moved to Florida in 1978.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Freda Fink, and a son, Gary J. Arzt of Hong Kong.
