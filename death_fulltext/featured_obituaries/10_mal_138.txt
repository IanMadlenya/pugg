        William King, a fashion photographer, died of AIDS on Thursday at Beth Israel Hospital. He was 48 years old and lived in Manhattan and Paris.
        Mr. King had his own business, the Bill King Studio, at 100 Fifth Avenue. His photographs appeared frequently in Vogue magazine. He was a member of the American Society of Magazine Photographers.
        He is survived by his father, William King, and a sister, Janet McClelland.
