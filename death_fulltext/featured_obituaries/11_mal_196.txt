
       Frank A. Grisanti, a businessman and management consultant in the computer field, died Dec. 21 at his home in Los Angeles. He was 67 years old. Mr. Grisanti was a founding partner of the consulting firm of Grisanti, Galef & Goldress. Most recently he was chairman of Deutschman & Company.
        Frank A. Grisanti, a businessman and management consultant in the computer field, died Dec. 21 at his home in Los Angeles. He was 67 years old. Mr. Grisanti was a founding partner of the consulting firm of Grisanti, Galef & Goldress. Most recently he was chairman of Deutschman & Company.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; two daughters, Melanie Grisanti of New York City, and Mari-Jo Deutschman of Los Angeles; his mother, Mary Grisanti, and two sisters, Rose Marie Grisanti and Josephine Grillo, all of Buffalo.
