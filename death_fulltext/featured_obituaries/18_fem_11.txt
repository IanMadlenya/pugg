        Dr. Margaret Good Myers, an educator and author, died of pneumonia after surgery Monday at Memorial Hospital of Burlington County in Mount Holly, N.J. She was 89 years old and lived in Medford, N.J.
        Dr. Myers was an economics professor at Vassar College from 1934 to 1964.
        She is survived by a son, Gordon Beckhart, of Haddon Heights, N.J.; three grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
