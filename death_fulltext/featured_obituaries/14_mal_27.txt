        Thomas W. Melham, an insurance executive who served in the 1950's as Imperial Potentate of the Ancient Arabic Order of Nobles of the Mystic Shrine of North America, died of cancer Sunday at his home in Fort Salonga, L.I. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Melham was manager of the Manhattan agency of the Prudential Insurance Company of America from 1952 until 1962, when he retired.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Lucia Conte; two sons, John, of Harrisburg, Pa., and Thomas Jr., of Washington; a daughter, Mary Lou Jaeger of Kankakee, Ill., and seven grandchildren.
