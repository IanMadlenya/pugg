        Walter C. Iooss Sr., a jazz bassist, died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Riverhead, L.I. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Iooss performed with Benny Goodman, Dizzy Gillespie, Billie Holiday and other well-known jazz musicians and swing bands during the 1940's and 50's. He later played with the now-defunct WNEW radio band and the Brooklyn Philharmonic Orchestra, and was active as a studio musician in New York for many years.
        Mr. Iooss is survived by his wife, Audrey, of Riverhead, and his son, Walter, of New York.
