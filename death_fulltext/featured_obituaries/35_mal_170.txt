        Robert B. Chamine, a securities broker with Kidder Peabody & Company Inc. for 40 years and a longtime member of the New York Stock Exchange, died of a heart attack yesterday at his home in New Milford, N.J. He was 79 years old.  Mr. Chamine had also been a member of the exchange's board of arbitration. He was a native of Manhattan and a resident of New Milford for more than 35 years.
        Survivors include his wife of 56 years, the former Margaret Whalen; a son, Robert J., of Rumson, N.J., and four grandchildren.
