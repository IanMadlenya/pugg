        Victor G. Trapasso, the president and chief executive officer of the GT Realty and Management Company of Herndon, Va., died of cancer on Thursday at Alexandria Hospital in Alexandria, Va., where he lived. He was 54 years old.
        Mr. Trapasso, a native New Yorker, was an examiner for the United States Patent Office, a law clerk for the Court of Customs and Patent Appeals and a partner in an Alexandria law firm.
        Survivors include his wife of 29 years, the former Jennie Gibson; his mother, Josephine, of White Plains; a daughter, Laura Adler, of Fairfax, Va., and a son, Michael S., of Alexandria.
