        Hector Barrantes, the stepfather of the Duchess of York in Britain, died on Friday, the family said. He was 51 years old.
        The duchess, formerly Sarah Ferguson, was recently in Argentina to visit her mother, Susan Barrantes. Her stepfather was suffering from cancer.
        Mr. Barrantes, a well-known polo player, raised cattle and polo ponies at his ranch near Trenque Lauquen, a town in the pampas.
