        The Rev. George Gardner Hill, a retired Methodist minister who served in churches in Connecticut, New York and Florida, died of prostate cancer May 20 at the Connecticut Hospice in Branford. He was 68 years old and lived in New Haven.
        He graduated from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and Yale Divinity School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Keeney; a daughter, Kay, of New Haven; two sons, Gary, of Manhattan, and Steve, of Avon, Conn.; a sister, Virginia Cornett of Fresno, Calif.; a brother, James, of Pennsylvania Furnace, Pa, and four grandchildren.
