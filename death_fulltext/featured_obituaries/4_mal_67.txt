        Richard McConnell Clark, chairman of the Department of Theatre Arts at Ithaca College in Ithaca, N.Y., was among the 183 people killed in the crash of a LOT Polish airlines plane near Warsaw on May 9 while en route to New York, the college reported Thursday. He was 51 years old and lived in Ithaca.
        Mr. Clark had attended an international theater festival in Poland and also was exploring the possibility of overseas tours for Ithaca College productions. During his six years at the head of the college's theater department, it gained accreditation by the National Association of Schools of Theatre.
        Mr. Clark is survived by his wife, Pat; a daughter, Melissa, and a sister, Sandra Anderson of Kalispell, Mont.
