        Robert J. Collishaw, Chemical Bank's senior vice president for corporate affairs, died of a heart attack on Saturday at his vacation home in South Orleans, Mass. He was 55 years old and also lived in Mahwah, N.J.
        Surviving are his wife, Jacquelyn; three sons, Daniel, of Baldwinsville, N.Y., Thomas, of Visalia, Calif., and R. Scott, of Manhattan; two daughters, Karen, of Rockville, Md., and Stacey, of Quincy, Mass.; a brother, William, of Bethesda, Md.; a sister, Barbara Ross of Willet, N.Y., and six grandchildren.
