        James F. Bunting, a former general secretary of the national council of the Young Men's Christian Association of the United States, died of a stroke Tuesday at the Herman Wilson Health Center in Gaithersburg, Md. He was 82 years old and lived in Gaithersburg.
        Mr. Bunting became a Y.M.C.A. staff member after graduation from Syracuse University in 1927. In his 44 years with the association he served in Rochester, New Haven, Schenectady, Newark and Washington. He was general secretary of the national organization from 1963 to 1971, when he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Emma Lee Morgan; two daughters, Margaret Comings of Gaithersburg and Elizabeth Barnett of Wilton, N.H.; a brother, Robert W. of Holden, Mass., and four grandchildren.
