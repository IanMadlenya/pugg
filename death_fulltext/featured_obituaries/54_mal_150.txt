
        Raymond F. Shaffer, a retired president of the Greyhound Corporation, died Tuesday at Scottsdale Memorial Hospital. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Shaffer was with Greyhound for 31 years. He became president and chief executive of Greyhound Lines in 1967 and served as president of the Greyhound Corporation from 1970 until he retired in 1977.
        Survivors include his wife, Aurelbelle; a son and a grandchild.
