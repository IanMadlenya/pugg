        Milton J. Raisbeck, a retired cardiologist, died Tuesday at Children's Hospital in San Francisco. He was 99 years old.
        Dr. Raisbeck, who was born in Brooklyn, was a staff member of Flower and Fifth Avenue Hospital, now the Terence Cardinal Cooke Health Center, for many years. He retired in 1966.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marcelle Ellinger; a son, Gordon, of Portland, Me.; a daughter, Joan Tuteur, of San Francisco; seven grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren.
