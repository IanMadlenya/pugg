        Berkeley A. Cater, a retired vice president of the Jersey Plastic and Die Company in Irvington, N.J., died of prostate cancer Tuesday at his home in Fair Haven, N.J. He was 89 years old.
        Mr. Cater was a stockbroker in Manhattan for more than 20 years, with Taylor Bates and, later, with Laird & Company. In 1948. he joined Jersey Plastic and Die as a salesman. He retired in 1968 as vice president of sales.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Irene Blenheim; a daughter, Elizabeth, of Manhattan, and a granddaughter.
