        Frank X. Smith, president of Seven Graphic Arts Inc., a design and typesetting company in Manhattan, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in New Rochelle, N.Y. He was 51 years old.
        Before co-founding Seven Graphic Arts in 1974, Mr. Smith worked for the Western Publishing Company, The Reporter magazine and Finance magazine. He was born in Brooklyn and raised in Queens Village, Queens. Mr. Smith received a bachelor's degree from St. Michael's College of the University of Toronto.
        Surviving are his wife, Elizabeth; two sons, Frank Jr. and Daniel, and two daughters, Soozan and Jennifer, all of New Rochelle, and a sister, Geraldine Laraia of Bethpage, L.I.
