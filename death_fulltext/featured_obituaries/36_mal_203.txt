        James J. Downey Jr., a construction company executive and former president of the General Society of Mechanics and Tradesmen of the City of New York, died in his sleep on Tuesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 84 years old.
        He is survived by his daughter, Ann Agnew-Mailman of East Rockaway, L.I.; a brother, Edward, of Santa Clara, Calif., and four grandchildren.
