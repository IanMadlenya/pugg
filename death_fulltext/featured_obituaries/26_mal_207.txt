        Dr. Robert Cushman Hunt, a psychiatrist and former assistant commissioner of the New York State Department of Mental Hygiene, died Monday at Coral Reef Hospital in Miami. He was 84 years old and lived in Miami. Dr. Hunt was a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Medical School. He headed New York State hospitals in Rochester and Ogdensburg before becoming assistant commissioner in 1952. He became director of Hudson River State Hospital in Poughkeepsie in 1957 and retired in 1962.
        He is survived by his wife, Gertrude; a son, Robert, of Newton, Mass.; a daughter, Joan Parks of Chicago, and four grandchildren.
