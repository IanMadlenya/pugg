        Ida Sturz, a retired travel consultant, died yesterday at her home in Manhattan. She was 87 years old.
        Mrs. Sturz, who was born in Manhattan, lived most of her life in Bayonne, N.J., where she had been a volunteer coordinator at the Bayonne Hospital. She ran a travel agency in Bayonne and Manhattan.
        She is survived by two sons, Herbert and Robert, both of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
