        The Rev. Samuel Davis, rector of St. Luke's Episcopal Church in East Hampton, L.I., from 1941 until 1978, died Wednesday at Southampton (L.I.) Hospital. He was 73 years old and lived in retirement in East Hampton.
        The son of Baptist missionaries, Mr. Davis was born in Denver and spent much of his childhood in South America. He was an alumnus of the McBurnery School in Manhatan and the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, and he received his divinity degree from the General Theological Seminary.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Jean T. Lowry; a daughter, Margaret Sherman of Victoria, Va.; a son, Sgt. Timothy J. Davis of Columbia, S.C., and three grandchildren.
