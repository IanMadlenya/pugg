        David V. O'Brien, Chief Judge of the United States District Court in the Virgin Islands, died Friday at Georgetown University Hospital, where he was undergoing treatment for Hodgkin's disease. He was 57 years old.
        Judge O'Brien had been head of the Virgin Islands court since 1988 and had been a member of it since 1981. Before joining the bench, he was a lawyer in the Virgin Islands and in New York, where he also was a journalist.
        A graduate of the University of Vermont and Syracuse University, he served in the Marine Corps in the Vietnam War.
