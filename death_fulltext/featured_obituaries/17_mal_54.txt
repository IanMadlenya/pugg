        Jacob M. Miller, a retired lawyer and health planning consultant, died of cancer Tuesday at the Community Hospice at Camelot in Sun City, Ariz. He was 79 years old and lived in Sun City.
        Mr. Miller, a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, was also a founder of organizations in Manhattan for mentally ill children.
        He is survived by his wife, Babette Kass Miller of Sun City; a daughter, Eileen Walpole of Atlanta; a son, Warren, of Greensboro, N.C.; a brother, Avrum, of Rockville Centre, L.I., and two grandchildren.
