        Bishop Joseph G. Vath, the leader of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Birmingham, died of a heart attack Tuesday night at University Hospital. He was 69 years old.
        Bishop Vath was ordained as a priest in 1941 and appointed to the Ascension Church in Donaldsonville, La., where he served from 1941 to 1946. He served in several churches in Louisiana before being consecrated as a bishop in 1966. He became the Bishop of Birmingham in 1969.
