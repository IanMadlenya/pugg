        Philip Colleck, an antiques dealer in Manhattan and London, died Monday at his home in Manhattan. He was 73 years old.
        A specialist in 18th century English furnishings, Mr. Colleck, who was born in London, had a shop, Philip Colleck of London Ltd., for more than 30 years at 122 East 57th Street. The shop was moved three years ago to 830 Broadway, at 12th Street. In 1978, he opened a branch in London.
        He is survived by a brother, Louis, of London.
