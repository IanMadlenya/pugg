        John Daniel Cronin, a former partner of Eastman Dillon Union Securities in Manhattan, died of congestive heart failure Wednesday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 86 years old and lived in Summit.
        Mr. Cronin joined the securities concern in 1945 and became a partner in 1963. He retired as a partner in 1968 but remained associated with the firm until 1981.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Green; two sons, John, of Scarsdale, N.Y., and Richard, of Everett, Wash.; a sister, Beatrice Owens of Manhattan; seven grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
