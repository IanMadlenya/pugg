
        John F. Maun Jr., a former advertising director at Bonwit Teller in Manhattan, died on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 52 years old.
        Mr. Maun, a native of Queens, was associated with Bonwit Teller in the 1980's. He was advertising director from 1987 until the store's Fifth Avenue branch closed in 1990.
        He is survived by his mother, Ethel Maun of Woodhaven, Queens; a sister, Dorothy Boodaghian of Manhasset, L.I., and a brother, Stephen, of Port Washington, L.I.
