        Arnold Jacobsen, the retired head surgeon for the Workmen's Benefit Fund of the U.S.A. Inc. in Manhattan, died Saturday at Holy Name Hospital in Teaneck, N.J., after a long illness. He was 91 years old and lived in Forest Hills, Queens.
        Dr. Jacobsen, who was born in Berlin, Germany, joined the Workmen's Benefit Fund in 1946 and retired in 1975.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ann Kent.
