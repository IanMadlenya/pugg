        John Braden, a composer and lyricist, died Wednesday of complications from meningitis at Cabrini Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 41 years old, and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Braden wrote the music and lyrics for ''Etiquette,'' ''Troopers,'' ''Five Points,'' and other Off Broadway musicals and revues.
        Mr. Braden is survived by his mother, Millicent Browning of Asheville, N.C., and his sister, Georgia Rohan of Miami.
