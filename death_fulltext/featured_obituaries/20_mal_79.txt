        Robert James Longood Sr., director of speechwriting in the Office of Public Relations at the Department of Health and Human Services, died Sept. 10 in the Washington Home Hospice in Washington. He was 61 years old and lived in Rockville, Md.
        In World War II Mr. Longood served in the Army in the Pacific. He graduated from Indiana University in 1952 and later worked as a reporter with the Yonkers Herald Statesman and as a speechwriter for Gov. Nelson A. Rockefeller. In 1971, he began working for the Department of Health and Human Services.
