        Warren Casey, co-author of the musical ''Grease,'' died of AIDS Tuesday at St. Joseph's Hospital here. He was 53 years old.
        His companion, Burt Cohen, said that Mr. Casey had been ill since February but had continued to compose music.
        Mr. Casey collaborated with Jim Jacobs on ''Grease,'' a show set at a high school in the 1950's.
        Mr. Casey is survived by his mother and two brothers.
