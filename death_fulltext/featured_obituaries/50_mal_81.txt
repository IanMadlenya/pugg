
        George A. Diehl Jr., an engineer and former president of the New Jersey School Boards Association, died on Wednesday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 82 years old and lived in Chatham Township, N.J.
        He died of congestive heart failure, a funeral home representative said.
        Mr. Diehl was an engineer and personnel manager at Western Electric Inc. from 1943 until he retired in 1973. Before that, he was with Jersey Central Power and Light for 15 years. A graduate of Brooklyn Polytechnic Institute, Mr. Diehl served on the Chatham Township Board of Education for 12 years and was its president for 10 years.
        He is survived by a daughter, Ellin Lawrence of Morristown, N.J., and one granddaughter.
