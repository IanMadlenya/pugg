        Dr. Aims C. McGuinness, a pediatrician and a former professor at the University of Pennsylvania, died of pneumonia Tuesday at the Uihlein Mercy Center in Lake Placid, N.Y. He was 83 years old and had lived in Princeton, N.J.
        Dr. McGuinness was also a former director of Children's Hospital in Philadelphia. He graduated from Princeton University and received his M.D. from Columbia University.
        Survivors include two daughters, Louise Ludlow of Elizabethtown, N.Y. and Margaret Denny of Princeton; a son, Aims Jr., of Littleton, Colo., and six grandchildren.
