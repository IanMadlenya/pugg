        George Markham, president and general manager of the Carlyle Hotel, died of a heart attack Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 63 years old.
        Mr. Markham was born in Portsmouth, N.H., and graduated from Emerson College. He joined the Carlyle, 35 East 76th Street, in 1958 and became vice president and resident manager in 1967. He became president in 1975.
        There are no survivors.
