        Robert K. Campbell, chairman and president of the Pennsylvania Power and Light Company, died Saturday at the Lehigh Valley Hospital Center. He was 59 years old. The cause of death was not made known.
        Mr. Campbell, a native of Chicago, joined the utility in 1977 after serving as a general manager for Western Electric's manufacturing division.
        Survivors include his wife, Alvira, and three sons.
