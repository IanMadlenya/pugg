        Franklin H. Middleton, a retired New York banker, died of kidney failure on Wednesday at the Fort Myers (Fla.) Care Center. He was 83 years old and lived in Fort Myers.
        A 1929 graduate of Dartmouth College, Mr. Middleton was a vice president of Manufacturers Hanover Trust Company and of Franklin National Bank. He lived in Briarcliff Manor, N.Y., before moving to Florida in 1970.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Louise Blakey; a daughter, Lynn Kable of Manhattan; a sister, Janet Berg of Seattle; four stepchildren, and four grandchildren.
