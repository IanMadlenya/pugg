        Albert Joseph Merz, Jr., an investment banker, died of a stroke yesterday at Beekman Downtown Hospital in Manhattan. He was 58 years old and lived in East Dennis on Cape Cod in Massachusetts.
        Mr. Merz was a vice president at John Nuveen & Company, an investment banking firm.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary Joan; his mother, Anne Farrell; a son, Jeffrey; two daughters, Elizabeth Ann and Alison, all of Cape Cod; and a brother, James, of Santa Barbara, Calif.
