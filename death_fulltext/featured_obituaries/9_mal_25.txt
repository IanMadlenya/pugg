        Joseph F. Connor, a retired assistant chief of the New York City Fire Department, died of lung cancer Thursday at Staten Island Hospital. He was 73 years old and lived on Staten Island.
        As an assistant chief, Mr. Connor commanded the Fire Department's marine division from 1966 to 1971 and the division of fire prevention from 1971 to 1973, when he retired. For the next 11 years he was a consultant on fire prevention to the Transit Authority.
        He is survived by his wife, Caroline; a daughter, Carole Connor of Staten Island; two sisters, Marion Lorenz of Elmhurst, Queens, and Ann Oppegard of Somerset, Pa., and a brother, Michael, of Brentwood, L.I.
