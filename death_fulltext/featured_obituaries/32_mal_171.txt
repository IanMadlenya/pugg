        Dr. Joseph B. Lenzner, a retired dentist, died of a heart attack yesterday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan. He was 86 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Lenzner practiced in Manhattan from 1930 to 1974, when he retired. He was born in Trenton and graduated from the University of Pennsylvania and its dental school. He taught dentistry at the New York University College of Dentistry.
        Surviving are his wife, Eleanor; three sons, Robert and Allan, of New York City, and Terry, of Washington; two brothers, Oscar, of Scranton, Pa., and Isidore, of Miami Beach, and eight grandchildren.
