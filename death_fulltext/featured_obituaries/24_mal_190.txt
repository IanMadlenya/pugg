        Emanuel A. Salma, former associate dean of the College of Engineering and Science at New York University, died of congestive heart failure on Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 81 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ellen Dancik; a son, Steven, of Monroe, Conn.; his mother, Sophia, of Manhattan, and a brother, Daniel, of River Edge, N.J.
