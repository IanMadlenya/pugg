        John G. Mapes, former vice chairman of Hill & Knowlton Inc., a Manhattan-based public relations concern, died of complications from pneumonia Thursday at a Phoenix nursing home. He was 82 years old and lived in Litchfield Park, Ariz.
        Mr. Mapes joined the agency in 1934, became executive vice president in 1947 and vice chairman in 1965. He retired in 1967. Earlier, he had been director of public relations for the American Iron and Steel Institute.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jane Stewart; a son, Glynn D., of Brooklyn, and two grandchildren.
