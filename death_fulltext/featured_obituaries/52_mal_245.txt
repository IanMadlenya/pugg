
        John F. Dorritie, an executive in the field of medical advertising, died on Friday at Bellevue Hospital Center. He was 57 years old and lived in New City, N.Y.
        Mr. Dorritie died of a heart attack, a spokeswoman for the family said.
        He was chairman of Dorritie Lyons & Nickel, a Manhattan-based agency specializing in medical advertising, which he formed with Michael Lyons and Albert Nickel in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Carol Kelly; his father, George, of Lake Carmel, N.Y.; two sons, George, of Los Angeles and John, of New City; two daughters, Teresa and Carol Jean, also of New City, and three sisters, Mary Albertelli and Kathleen Dorritie, both of Milford, Conn., and Margaret O'Brien of Berwick, Me.
