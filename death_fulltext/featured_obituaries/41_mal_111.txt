        Willis Peter Bilderback, whose Naval Academy lacrosse teams won eight consecutive national titles between 1959 and 1966, died Tuesday. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Bilderback was a member of the Lacrosse Hall of Fame. In 14 years as Navy's head coach, he led his teams to a 132-26-2 record. He coached five undefeated teams before retiring for health reasons in 1972.
