        Dora Collazo-Levy, executive director of a development agency on the Lower East Side, died of cancer Sunday at her home in Manhattan. She was 52 years old.
        Ms. Collazo-Levy was executive director of Loisaida Inc., the community agency. In the 1960's, she was a schoolteacher and a leader in decentralizing the school system.
        Ms. Collazo-Levy graduated from Brooklyn College and New York University, Surviving are her husband, Alan Levy; a daughter, Rosita, and Marcos, all of Manhattan.
