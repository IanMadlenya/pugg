        Wallace G. Taylor, former treasurer of the American Cyanamid Company, died Nov. 8 of complications resulting from a heart ailment at Englewood (N.J.) Hospital. He was 68 years old and lived in Fort Lee, N.J.
        Mr. Taylor, who was born in LaPorte, Ind., was treasurer of the company from 1961 to 1967.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Diane Millian; a sister, Dorothy Sharp of Venice, Fla.; two daughters, Marlena Smith of Cleveland and Dorothy Buoncare of Parsippany, N.J., and a son, Robert, also of Parsippany.
