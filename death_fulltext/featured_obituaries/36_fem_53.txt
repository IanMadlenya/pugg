        Barbara M. Weldon, former school superintendent of District No. 2 of the New York City Board of Education, died on Saturday at Martin Memorial Hospital in Stuart, Fla. She was 68 years old and lived in Stuart.
        Dr. Weldon was associated with the Board of Education for 20 years. In 1982 she moved from Hampton Bays, L.I., to Stuart. She had also been superintendent of schools in Charlestown, R.I.; dean of the school of education at St. John's University, and an adjunct professor at Hofstra and Pace Universities. She was a consultant for children's educational television.
        A native New Yorker, she graduated from St John's University, where she also received a Ph.D. in psychology.
        She is survived by her husband, Frank.
