        Ernest St. John (Jack) Metz, an opera coach and vocal adviser at the Los Angeles Opera Center, died of liver cancer on Tuesday at St. Joseph Medical Center here. He was 66 years old.
        For several years Mr. Metz had worked almost exclusively with the soprano Leona Mitchell. Among the singers he worked with are Leontyne Price, Carol Vaness and Carol Neblett. For the last 10 years, he had worked at the Australian Opera in Sydney from May to July.
