        Thomas Riggs Cox, retired president and former chairman of the board of Broadway Savings Bank, died yesterday at Lake Wales (Fla.) Hospital Extended Care Facility, after a brief illness. He was 99 years old and lived in Lake Wales.
        Mr. Cox joined the bank in the early 1940's and retired in the 1960's. After his retirement, the bank merged with Emigrant Savings Bank.
        Earlier in his career he had been a member of the New York Stock Exchange.
        He is survived by a son, Thomas R. Jr., of Bloomfield, Conn.; five grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
