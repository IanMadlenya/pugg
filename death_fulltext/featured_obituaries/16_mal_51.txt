        Saul B. Ackerman, a lawyer and one of the oldest practicing alumni of the Columbia University Law School, died of a heart attack Saturday at the Mount Sinai Medical Center. He was 100 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Ackerman was a founding partner of Ackerman, Salwen & Glass, a Manhattan firm.
        Surviving are his son, Eugene, of Minneapolis; two brothers, Laurence, of Groton, Conn., and Samuel, of New Rochelle, N.Y.; four grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
