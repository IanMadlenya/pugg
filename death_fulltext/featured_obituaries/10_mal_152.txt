        Dr. Mohammed A. Aziz, senior director of clinical research at Merck & Company's research laboratories in Woodbridge, N.J., died of cancer Wednesday at the Princeton Medical Center. He was 58 years old and lived in Princeton Junction.
        Dr. Aziz, who specialized in tropical diseases, was most recently involved in developing a drug to fight a riparian parasitical disease that causes blindness. He joined Merck in 1976 after working for the World Health Organization.
        Dr. Aziz is survived by his wife, Fatima.
