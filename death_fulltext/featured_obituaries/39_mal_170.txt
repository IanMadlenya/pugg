        Raymond J. Cantwell, chief financial officer of the Babcock & Wilcox engineering company, died of cancer on Saturday at Memorial-Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. He was 73 years old and lived in Bronxville, N.Y.
        Mr. Cantwell worked for 33 years at Babcock & Wilcox and was a board member, senior executive vice president, chief financial officer and comptroller.
        A native of Yonkers, Mr. Cantwell graduated from Manhattan College and obtained a master's degree in accounting from Columbia University's School of Business.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; two sons, Joseph, of Short Hills, N.J., and Patrick, of Oakton, Va.; a daughter, Kathleen, of Hartsdale, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
