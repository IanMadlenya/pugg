        John McCook Roots, a foreign correspondent and author, died Tuesday at Mackinac Straits Hospital in St. Ignace, Mich. He was 84 years old.
        Born in Hankou, China, where his father was an Episcopal bishop, Mr. Roots attended the Kent School and graduated with honors in 1925 from Harvard College. Writing for The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, the Los Angeles Times and a wide range of periodicals, he specialized in East Asian affairs, but covered e4vents in more than 90 countries. In 1978 he wrote a biography of Zhou Enlai.
        Mr. Roots, a resident of St. Ignace and Mackinac Island, Frances Roots Hadden, of St. Ignace.
