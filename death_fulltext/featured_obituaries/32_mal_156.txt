        The Rev. Edmund F. Christy, a former president of Siena College in Loudonville, N.Y., died of a heart attack yesterday at St. Mary's Hospital in Passaic, N.J. He was 78 years old and lived at Sacred Heart Friary in East Rutherford, N.J.
        Father Christy, a Franciscan, was a native of Tidioute, Pa. He was a former director of clerics at St. Anthony's Friary in Butler, N.J., a counselor of the Franciscan Provenance and a treasurer of the Holy Name Provenance, both in Manhattan, and a board member of St. Anthony's Guild in Clifton, N.J.
