        Dr. Harold Haydn Clum, who retired as professor of biology at Hunter College in 1962 after 35 years on the faculty, died Dec. 14 at Northern Westchester Hospital Center. He was 93 years old and lived in Chappaqua, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, Florence Hess Clum; a son, Richard, of San Jose, Calif., and a daughter, Elizabeth C. Myers of Guilderland, N.Y.
