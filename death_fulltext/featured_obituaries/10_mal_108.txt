        William M. Swartz, a retired Chicago businessman, died of a stroke Nov. 11 at his home in Highland Park, Ill. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Swartz was a former chairman of the Emossograph Display Manufacturing Company and a director of the Albert Einstein Peace Prize Foundation.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary; two daughters, Ellen Godfrey of Victoria, British Columbia, and Diane Williams of Chicago; a son, Robert, also of Chicago; three sisters, Rose Barkun of Highland Park, Florence Collins of Tempe, Ariz., and Lynn Tobias of Glencoe, Ill., and five gandchildren.
