        James Dobson, who appeared with President Reagan in ''Hellcats of the Navy,'' died of a heart attack on Sunday. He was 67 years old.
        Mr. Dobson appeared on stage in ''Life With Father,'' ''The Wind Is 90,'' ''Firebrand of Florence'' and ''Mr. Adam.'' For a time he was the voice of ''Archie Andrews'' in the radio show based on the comic strip. Mr. Dobson also appeared in the films ''The Red Badge of Courage,'' ''Flying Leathernecks,'' ''The Tanks Are Coming,'' ''Force of Arms'' and ''The Rose Bowl Story.''
