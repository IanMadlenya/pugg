        Maury Stein, a music store operator, big-band musician and brother of the songwriter Jule Styne, died of a heart attack Saturday. He was 76.
        Mr. Stein operated the M. K. Stein Music Company, popular as a Hollywood meeting place for musicians, for more than 40 years. He played woodwinds, and appeared in several big-name bands, including those of David Rose and Spike Jones.
