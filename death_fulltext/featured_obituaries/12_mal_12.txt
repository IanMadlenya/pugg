        Dr. Max Siegel, a New York psychotherapist for many years, died of a heart attack Friday at Boca Raton (Fla.) Community Hospital. He was 69 years old and lived in Boca Raton.
        Dr. Siegel, who held a Ph.D. from New York University, was a past president of the American Psychological Association. He was in private practice in Brooklyn. Academic posts that he held included dean of students at Brooklyn College and a professor at Adelphi University in Garden City, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, Frieda; a son, Ozzie, of Port Washington, L.I.; a daughter, Shelly Kirschenbaum of Melville, L.I., and six grandchildren.
