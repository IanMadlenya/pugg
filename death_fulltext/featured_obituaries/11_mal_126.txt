        Herbert H. Goldberger, founder and chairman of Hills Department Stores in Canton, Mass., died Dec. 16 in Boca Raton, Fla., where he lived. He was 69 years old.
        Born in Boston, he graduated magna cum laude from Brown University and earned a master's of business administration from Harvard University. In 1957, he founded Hills.
        He is survived by his wife, Phyllis; a son, Stephen A. of Wayland, Mass; a daughter, Laurie A. of New York City ; a brother, Edward Goldberger of New York City , and a sister, Blanche Cantor of Brookline, Mass.
