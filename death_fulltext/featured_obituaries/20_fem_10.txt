        Marilyn Ruben, an interior designer, died of cancer Tuesday at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in Manhattan. She was 61 years old and lived in Briarcliff Manor, N.Y.
        She was born in Framingham, Mass., and ran her own design business in Briarcliff for 20 years.
        She is survived by her husband, Herbert; four sons, Joseph, of Santa Monica, Calif., Daniel, of Boston, John, of Albuquerque, N.M., and Michael, of Briarcliff; a sister, Mimmy Harris of Ann Arbor, Mich.; a brother, Dick Porter of Upper Jay, N.Y., and one grandchild.
