        Dennis L. O'Connor, a lawyer, died of cancer Wednesday at the home of his sister in Larchmont, N.Y. He was 76 years old and was a longtime resident of White Plains.
        Mr. O'Connor was a founding partner in the White Plains law firm of O'Connor, McGuinness, Conte, Doyle, Oleson & Collins. Born in Mamaroneck, he graduated from Duke University and Fordham University Law School.
        Surviving are two daughters, Noreen Bridgham of Leawood, Kan., and Christine Keinz of Valhalla, N.Y.; three sons, Dennis Jr. and Brian, both of White Plains, and John of Valhalla; a sister, Rosalind Edwards of Larchmont; a brother, George of Hampton Bays, L.I., and eight grandchildren.
