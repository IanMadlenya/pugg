        Herbert Hastings Foster Jr., a former general counsel for the J. P. Stevens textile company, died Friday in the Westerly (R.I.) Hospital. He was 76 years old and lived in Block Island, R.I., and Captiva, Fla.
        Mr. Foster, who retired in 1976, had been a longtime resident and civic leader in Wilton, Conn. He was a native of Plainfield, N.J., and an alumnus of the Hotchkiss School, Princeton College and Columbia University Law School. He was a major in the Army in World War II.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Jane Boone; a son, Herbert H. 3d, of Bedford Hills, N.Y.; three daughters, Grace Watkins of Royal Oak, Md., Joanne Stone of Evanston, Ill., and Lillian Ketchum of Cohasset, Mass., and seven grandchildren.
