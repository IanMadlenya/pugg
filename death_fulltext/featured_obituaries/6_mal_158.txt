        WEST NYACK, N.Y., July 30 (AP) -Harold Parrott, a former sportswriter and baseball executive, died today. He was 78 years old.
        Mr. Parrott wrote about sports for The Brooklyn Eagle and later became the traveling secretary with the Brooklyn Dodgers. He moved with the club to Los Angeles in 1958 and became the ticket manager. He also served as a ticket manager for the San Diego Padres and the Seattle Pilots and as a promotion manager for the California Angels.
        He is survived by his wife, Josephine, and three sons, Todd, Peter and Brian.
