        Jeff de Lange, president of the Diamond Manufacturers and Importers Association of America, died of a heart attack last Thursday at the St. Clare's Hospital and Health Center. He was 51 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. de Lange, who was born in Antwerp, Belgium, immigrated here in World War II. He earned a bachelor's degree at Oberlin College.
        He became a diamond importer and, when he died, was president of Haber-Worth Enterprises.
