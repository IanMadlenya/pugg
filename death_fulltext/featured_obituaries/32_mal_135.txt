        I. M. Bomba, director and consultant to Schieffelin & Company, importers of wine and spirits, died Monday at Moses Ludington Hospital in Ticonderoga, N.Y. He was 90 years old and had suffered a stroke 10 days earlier.
        Mr. Bomba, who joined Schieffelin as manager of the wine and spirits division in 1933, retired as executive vice president in 1964.
        During his career, he was chairman of the National Association of Alcoholic Beverage Imports and president and senior counselor of the American Importers Association.
        The French Government awarded him the French Legion of Honor in 1955, and he was decorated as Cavalier of the Italian Order of Merit by Italy in 1957.
        He is survived by a son, James, of Kanata, Ontario; a brother, Nelson, of Fairfax, Va., and two granddaughters.
