        Joseph Bosco, a former Assistant Attorney General of Massachusetts and former associate regional counsel for the Department of Housing and Urban Development, died of lymphoma Oct. 8 at Beth Israel Hospital in Boston. He was 79 years old and lived in Everett, Mass.
        Mr. Bosco, a graduate of Suffolk University Law School in Boston, also served in the mid-1960's as national commander of the Italian-American War Veterans of the United States.
        He is survived by his wife, Emma; two sons, Joseph of Washington and Anthony of Boston; three daughters, Mary Paisley of Chelmsford, Mass., Elaine Robertson of Chicago and Valerie Bartolo of Everett, and 14 grandchildren.
