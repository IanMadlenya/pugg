
        Mildred (Millie) Robinson, widow of the boxer Sugar Ray Robinson and executive director of the Sugar Ray Robinson Youth Foundation, has died. She was 77.
        Mrs. Robinson died of cancer Tuesday at Cedars-Sinai Hospital, the foundation's executive secretary, Thelma Miles Smith, said today.
        She is survived by two children from a previous marriage, Ramona Hughes of Rosemont, Calif., and Herman Butch Robinson of Los Angeles, and two stepsons, Ronny Robinson of New York City and Ray Robinson Jr. of San Francisco.
