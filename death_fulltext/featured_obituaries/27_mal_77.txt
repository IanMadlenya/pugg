        Martin J. Munroe, a retired vice president of United Parcel Service, died of congestive heart failure Monday at Kennedy Hospital in Saddle Brook, N.J. He was 64 years old and lived in Ridgewood, N.J.
        He is survived by his wife, Rita; five sons, Martin Jr., of Dublin; Robert, of Richmond; Brian, of Paterson, N.J.; Sean, of Manhattan, and Neil, of Cresskill, N.J.; four daughters, Therese Emond, of Marietta, Ga.; Maureen, of Fair Lawn, N.J., and Patricia and Tara, both of Ridgewood; three sisters, Mary Healy, Catherine Hoffman and Eileen Norton, all of Chicago, and six grandchildren.
