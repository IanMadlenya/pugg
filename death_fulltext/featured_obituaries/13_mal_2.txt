        Joseph J. Coffey Sr., a co-founder of Local 804, International Brotherhood of Teamsters, died Saturday at City Hospital Center at Elmhurst, in Queens, after a long illness. He was 80 years old and lived in Woodside, Queens.
        Mr. Coffey was working as a delivery man for Saks Fifth Avenue when he helped organize Local 804 in 1946.
        Mr. Coffey is survived by his wife, the former Catherine Waring; a daughter, Patricia Lynch; two sons, Joseph Jr. and Thomas; a stepson, Donald Hackett; a sister, Helen Sunders; nine grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
