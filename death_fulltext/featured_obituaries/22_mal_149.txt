        Lester H. Bogen, an executive, philanthropist and community leader in Bergen County, N.J., died of pneumonia Tuesday at Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 63 years old and lived in Englewood, N.J.
        Mr. Bogen, a graduate of Cornell University, was president of the Bogen Photo Corporation, of Fair Lawn, N.J. He was a supporter of the Israel Cancer Research Fund and served for more than 10 years on the board of directors of Bergen County Community College. He also served on the board of the Englewood Health Foundation, which oversees Englewood Hospital, and was a member of the Bergen County Economic Development Committee.
        He is survived by his wife, Mildred; two sons, Michael, of Glen Ellyn, Ill., and Richard, of Fort Lee, N.J.; a daughter, Susan, of River Vale, N.J.; a sister, Rose Sellew of Westport, Conn., and three grandchildren.
