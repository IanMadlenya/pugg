        Eunice Taylor Vanderhoef, a horticulturist in Connecticut, died Wednesday at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital. She was 91 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        Mrs. Vanderhoef, a graduate of the Winsor School, was a member of the Herb Society of America and often exhibited her gardens for community causes.
        She is survived by a daughter, Mary Amory of Washington; a brother, William Taylor of South Natick, Mass.; a sister, Elizabeth Fessenden of Sherborn, Mass.; two grandsons, and three great-grandchildren.
