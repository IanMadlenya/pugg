
        A memorial tribute to the actor Werner Klemperer will be held on Thursday at 11 a.m. at the Broadhurst Theater, 235 West 44th Street, Manhattan. Among those to speak at the tribute, which is free and open to the public, are Philip Bosco, Tammy Grimes and Regina Resnick. Mr. Klemperer, best known for his role as Colonel Klink on the television series ''Hogan's Heroes,'' died on Dec. 6 at the age of 80.
