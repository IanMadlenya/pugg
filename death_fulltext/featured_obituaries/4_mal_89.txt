        Dr. Harold D. Weinerman, a Manhattan dentist and a founder of the Long Island Committee on Soviet Jewry, died of lung cancer Tuesday at his home in Baldwin, L.I. He was 70 years old.
        Dr. Weinerman, a graduate of City College and the New York University College of Dentistry, maintained a practice on the Lower East Side of Manhattan for more than 40 years.
        He is survived by his wife, Florence; two sons, Philip, of Columbus, Ohio, and Stuart, of Manhasset, L.I.; two daughters, Anita Roth of San Francisco and Naomi Lippman of Great Neck, L.I., and six grandchildren.
