        Barry E. Gell, senior vice president of the Citizen Watch Company, died of a heart attack Thursday at Mount Sinai Hospital. He was 56 years old and lived in Fort Lee, N.J.
        Mr. Gell had been associated with the watch industry for 30 years. He joined Citzen, a manufacturer of clocks and watches in Lyndhurst, N.J., in 1980 and became senior vice president in 1986. He was born in Brooklyn, Surviving are his wife, the former Diane Levine; a daughter, Leslie, of Boston; a son, Brian, of Manhattan; a brother, Richard, of Levittown, L.I., and a grandson.
