        Frederick A. Huettner, a former president and director of Selchow & Righter, the Long Island company that manufactured Scrabble, Parchesi and Trivial Pursuit before being bought by Coleco Industries, died Thursday at University Hospital in Stony Brook, L.I., after a long illness. He was 86 years old and lived in Brightwaters, L.I.
        Mr. Huettner was with Selchow & Righter for 60 years and retired in 1986.
        He is survived by his wife, Florence, and a sister, Hanna Bischof of Hof, Germany.
