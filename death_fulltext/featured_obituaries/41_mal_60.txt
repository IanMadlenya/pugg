        Dr. James L. Green, a urologist who was a founding physician at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I., died of a heart attack on Tuesday at his home in Floral Park, L.I. He was 70 years old.
        He was affiliated with North Shore Hospital for more than 30 years and served as chief of its urology division from 1967 to 1986. Dr. Green, a graduate of Cornell University Medical School, was also affiliated with St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn for many years and was in private practice. He retired in 1987.
        He is survived by two daughters, Geraldine Nekrosius of Oak Park, Ill., and Leslyn Casey Rigoni of Manhattan, and five grandchildren.
