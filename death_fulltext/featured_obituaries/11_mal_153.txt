        Gary Pallack, a clothing manufacturer, died of a heart attack Friday at St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan. He was 41 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He was born in Manhattan and graduated from Queens College. He was president of the Arrow Clothing Company in Long Island City.
        He is survived by his father, Edward, of Pompano, Beach, Fla., and two brothers, Donald, of Dallas, and Robert of Fort Lauderdale, Fla.
