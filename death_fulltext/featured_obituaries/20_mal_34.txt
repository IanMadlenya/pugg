        Dr. Joseph John Fried, an ophthalmologist, died of complications of pneumonia Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 91 years old.
        A native of Hungary, Dr. Fried fled the Nazis in 1938 and came to the United States. He was affiliated for many years with the New York French and Polyclinic Medical School and Health Center, where he headed the eye clinic and was later named professor emeritus.
        He is survived by his daughter, Martha Suzanne Fleischer; a sister, Rose Friend, both of Manhattan, and three grandchildren.
