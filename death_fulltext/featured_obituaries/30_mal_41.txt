        Nelson K. Mintz, a retired judge of the Appellate Division of the New Jersey Superior Court, died of cancer Wednesday at St. Barnabas Medical Center in Livingston, N.J. He was 81 years old and lived in Verona, N.J.
        Judge Mintz was named a Morris County judge in 1955. He moved to the Superior Court in 1960 and in 1970 was appointed to the Appellate Division. He retired in 1973.
        A native of Morristown, N.J., he held undergraduate and law degrees from Cornell. He served in the United States Army in World War II.
        Judge Mintz is survived by his wife, Fay; a stepson, Roy Sheldon, of Dallas; a sister, Edith Kolovsky, and a brother, Dr. Alvin R. Mintz, both of Morristown.
