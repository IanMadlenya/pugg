        Curt George Segeler, a retired chemical engineer and a Boy Scout leader for almost 66 years, died of cancer on Jan. 22. He was 88 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Mr. Segeler, a graduate of Columbia University, began working with his local Boy Scout Troop in 1918, while still in college. He was Scoutmaster of Troop 24 and later Troop 8 until he retired in 1984. As an engineer, he was with several gas companies, including the American Gas Association and International Gas Union.
        He is survived by his wife, Louise, and a son, Dr. Eric, of Valley City, Ohio.
