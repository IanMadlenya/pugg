        John H. G. Munson, an investment adviser with A. R. Schmeidler & Company, died of complications from cancer Sept. 10 at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 46 years old and lived in Manhattan and Mattapoisett, Mass.
        Mr. Munson was born in Honolulu and attended the United States Naval Academy before receiving his bachelor's degree in 1963 from Columbia University. He subsequently attended Columbia Law School.
        Mr. Munson was a former member of the New York Yacht Club, he was an avid sailor and racing skipper. In 1976 and 1977 he coached the United States Naval Academy Sailing Team.
