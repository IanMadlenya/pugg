        Gertrude Waldeyer, an educator and conservationist, died Sunday at the Hilaire Nursing Home in Huntington, L.I. She was 79 years old and had lived in Queens.
        Mrs. Waldeyer founded the Oakland Lake and Ravine Conservation Committee and was a member of New York City Community Planning Board 11 for nine years.
        She was a guidance counselor and assistant and acting principal of Bayside High School, retiring in 1973.
        She is survived by a sister, Eleanor Smaridge of Huntington; a stepdaughter, Doris Waldeyer, and two grandchildren.
