        George P. Craighead, an executive-search specialist, died of cancer Wednesday at Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 59 years old and lived in Darien, Conn.
        Mr. Craighead was publisher of Craighead's International Executive Travel and Relocation Service, a newsletter. He was a former executive director of Russell Reynolds Associates, a search concern, Before joining Russell Reynolds in 1986, he was a managing partner at Egon Zehnder International, also a search concern.
        He was on the board of the Connecticut branch of Planned Parenthood and was president of the Association of Executive Recruiting Consultants.
        Surviving are his wife, Peggy; two sons, Scott and Bradford, both of Manhattan; a daughter, Catherine, of Chicago, and his mother, Janet, of Gladwyne, Pa.
