        Herbert Tobias, a screen agent who represented top stars during a career that spanned nearly 50 years, died Sunday. He was 69 years old.
        Mr. Tobias moved to Hollywood from his native Oklahoma City in 1936 and took a job in the mailroom at Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer studios. Three years later he was hired as a talent scout for the Nat Goldstone Agency, where he worked for 48 years, eventually taking over the company.
