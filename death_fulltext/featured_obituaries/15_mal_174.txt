        Sanford C. Miller, a lawyer, died of cancer Wednesday at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, N.J. He was 59 years old and lived in Ridgewood.
        Mr. Miller, who was born in Brooklyn, was a graduate of Yale College and the Yale University Law School. He was a partner in the Manhattan law firm of Haight, Gardner, Poor & Havens.
        Survivors include his wife, Alice; a daughter, Faith, of Manhattan; two sons, Jonathan, of Ridgewood and Timothy, of Brooklyn, and a brother, Lewis, of Darien, Conn.
