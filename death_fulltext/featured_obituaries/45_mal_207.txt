
        Johnny Sanders, general manager of the San Diego Chargers of the National Football League from 1976 to 1987, died today at Grossmont Hospital in La Mesa, Calif., after apparently suffering heart failure, said George Dickason, a deputy coroner. Mr. Sanders was 68 years old.
        Mr. Sanders is survived by his wife, Peggy; a daughter, Pamela, and a son, Rob.
