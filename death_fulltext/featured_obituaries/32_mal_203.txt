        William Post Jr., an actor, died of a chronic obstructive pulmonary disease Tuesday in Presbyterian Hospital in Oklahoma City. He was 88 years old and lived in Granite, Okla.
        Mr. Post's many film appearances included roles in ''The House on 92d Street,'' ''The Moon Is Down'' and ''Northside 777.'' On Broadway, Mr. Post played in ''Ah, Wilderness!,'' ''Caesar and Cleopatra,'' ''My Sister Eileen'' and ''Richard II.''
        For television, Mr. Post performed in ''The Edge of Night,'' ''Another World,'' ''As the World Turns'' and ''The Guiding Light.'' For 25 years he headed the drama department at the now defunct Finch College.
        He is survived by his wife, Doris, and a brother, Robert, both of Montclair, N.J.
