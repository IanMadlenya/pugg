        Ruth Crosby Noble, an author, died March 15 at the Allendale (N.J.) Nursing Home, where she lived. She was 91 years old.
        Mrs. Noble wrote ''The Nature of the Beast,'' a book on animal behavior based on research by her husband, Dr. G. Kingsley Noble, a curator at the American Museum of Natural History who died in 1940.
        She is survived by two sons, G. Kingsley Jr., of Portola Valley, Calif., and Alan, of Englewood, N.J.; two sisters, Vivian Wakeman of Sparta, N.J., and Constance Sewalt of Montclair, N.J., and two grandchildren.
