        Gerald Peter Plano, a record producer for 25 years, died of lung cancer Saturday at his home in Pacheco, Calif. He was 49 years old.  Mr. Plano, a native of Los Angeles, was director of arts and repertoire services for RCA/Ariola Records in New York City. He produced 6,000 special record collections, including more than 30 for Reader's Digest, during his years with the company.
        He is survived by a brother, Donald, of Grover City, Calif.
