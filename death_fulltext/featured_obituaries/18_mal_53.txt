        Emmanuel Manheim, a radio and television writer for Groucho Marx, Frank Sinatra and Art Linkletter, among others, died June 26 at Santa Monica Hospital. He was 90 years old.
        Mr. Manheim wrote for such popular radio and television shows as ''You Bet Your Life,'' ''People Are Funny'' and ''My Three Sons.'' He also wrote for Edgar Bergen, Rudy Vallee, Jackie Gleason, Al Jolson, Bob Hope, Bing Crosby and Milton Berle.
        Mr. Manheim is survived by his wife, Martha, and his brother, Het.
