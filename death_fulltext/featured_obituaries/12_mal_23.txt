        Jon Miles Kurtis, foreign sales executive with Manley Productions Inc., New York film producers, died in his sleep Sunday at his home in Woodstock, Vt. An autopsy is planned to determine the cause of death. He was 32 years old.
        In addition to his mother and stepfather, Mr. Kurtis is survived by his wife, the former Virginia Cowles, a son, Gardner, and a sister, Eileen Kleinman of Manhattan.
