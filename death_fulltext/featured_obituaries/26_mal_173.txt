        Everett Callender, a former securities analyst, died of pneumonia Tuesday at the Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital. He was 83 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        Mr. Callender, a native of New Haven, worked for Scudder, Stevens & Company from 1927 to 1950, and later for the Madison Fund Inc., and Orsun Munn & Company Inc., all of Manhattan. He retired in 1988.
        He was a 1927 graduate of Yale University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jane Robinson; two sons, Robinson, of Philadelphia, and Edward, of Reston, Va., and five grandchildren.
