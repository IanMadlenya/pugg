        John Wilson Houser, a hotel executive, died of a stroke Monday after surgery at Santa Ynes (Calif.) Hospital. He was 78 years old and lived in Solvang, Calif.
        Mr. Houser was a former executive vice president of Hilton Hotels International Inc. and a former vice president of American Express Company. He retired in 1975.
        Mr. graduated from the University of Colorado and the University of South California School of Law. He was a former trustee of Adelphi University.
        Surviving are his wife, Mary; a son, John, of Kodiak, Alaska; a daughter, Julie Cooper of Solvang, Calif., and two grandchildren.
