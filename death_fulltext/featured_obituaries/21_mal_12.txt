        David E. Satterfield 3d, a conservative Democrat who represented Virginia's Third District in the House of Representatives, died of congestive heart failure Friday at the College of Virginia Hospitals in Richmond.
        Elected in 1965, Mr. Satterfield favored increased military spending, deregulation of natural gas and the prohibition of Federal financing for abortions. He returned to private law practice in Richmond in 1981.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Anne Powell; two sons, David E. 4th and John, and a brother, Richard, all of Richmond, and two grandchildren.
