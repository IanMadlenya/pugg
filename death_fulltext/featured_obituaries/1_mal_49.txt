        John J. Meehan, a New York lawyer, died Tuesday at North Shore University Hospital as the result of injuries he suffered earlier in a fall outside his home in Plandome, L.I. He was 70 years old.
        Mr. Meehan, a native New Yorker, worked for 26 years as assistant to J. Peter Grace, chairman and chief executive of W. R. Grace & Company. He joined the company in 1960 and was vice president, secretary and a member of the board of trustees of two philanthropic organizations, the Grace Institute and the Grace Foundation, both of which are affiliated with the company.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Justine Dennehy; two sons, John J. and Terrence F., both of St. Louis, Mo.; two daughters, Dr. Justine Carr and Monica McNamara, both of Brookline, Mass.; a sister, Genevieve Hesse of Cresskill, N.J., and five grandchildren.
