        Joseph E. Parisi, a State Senator from Brooklyn from 1945 to 1948, died on Tuesday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 77 years old and lived in Rye, N.Y.
        Mr. Parisi, a Republican, won election to the State Senate in 1944. He was re-elected in 1946.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Grace Rasulo; a son, Guy, of Chappaqua, N.Y.; two daughters, Patricia Magliocco of Rye and Ann Marie Riggio of Pine Brook, N.J.; a sister, Virginia Romano of Iselin, N.J., and six grandchildren.
