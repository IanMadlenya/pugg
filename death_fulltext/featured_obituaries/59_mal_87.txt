
        Harvey Royden Jones, a retired Wall Street broker, died on Thursday at Lahey Clinic Medical Center in Burlington, Mass. He was 88 years old and lived in Plainfield, N.J., before moving to Miami Lakes, Fla.
        A native of Piscataway, N.J., Mr. Jones began his brokerage career in 1923 at the Garvin Bantel Corporation and retired in 1973 as its board chairman. He moved to Florida in 1978.
        Mr. Jones is survived by his wife of 58 years, the former Florence Dobbs; a daughter, Nancy G., of Miami Lakes; a son, Dr. H. Royden Jr., of Wellesley, Mass., and four grandchildren.
