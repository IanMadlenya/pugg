        Donald Moss, the booking manager for the Feld Ballet and stage manager for a number of modern dance companies, including Alvin Ailey Dance Theater, died of heart failure on Sunday at his Manhattan home. He was 38 years old.
        Mr. Moss, who was born in New York City, received a bachelor's degree in speech and drama from Hampton Institute.
        He is survived by his wife, Marlene, his stepdaughter, Nina, his mother, Bernice, his father, Donald Sr., and his stepmother, Lillian.
