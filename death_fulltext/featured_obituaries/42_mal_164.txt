        Sergei Paradzhanov, an Armenian film director, has died after a long illness, the official press agency Tass said today. He was 66 years old.
        He was known for films like ''Shadows of Forgotten Ancestors'' and ''Legend of Suram Fortress.''
        The death was announced at a session of the Armenian Parliament in Yerevan, the republic's capital.
        Mr. Paradzhanov died overnight, Tass reported. It did not give the cause of death or other details.
