        Bessie Brimberg, widow of Louis Brimberg, founder of the brokerage firm Brimberg & Company, died of pneumonia Monday in Doctors Hospital in Manhattan. She was 90 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Brimberg, a great lover of music and an accomplished pianist, was born in Brooklyn and married Mr. Brimberg in 1919.
        Mr. Brimberg founded the brokerage firm, a member of the New York Stock Exchange, with his two sons, Jack and Robert, in 1955. The elder Mr. Brimberg died in 1965.
        Besides her two sons, both of whom live in Manhattan, she is survived by three granchildren.
