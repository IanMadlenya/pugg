        Lily von Klemperer, who was the director of the counseling division of the Institute of International Education for 20 years, died of cancer Tuesday at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was 77 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Miss von Klemperer was the co-author of a series of editions of ''The New Guide to Study Abroad,'' published by Harper & Row, and had also been a guidance counselor in association with the National Association of Foreign Student Affairs. She was a native of Berlin.
        Surviving are three brothers, Franz, of Madison, Conn., Alfred, of Middle Neck, L.I., and Klemens, of Northampton, Mass.
