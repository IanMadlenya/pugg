        M. David Henkle, a lawyer and former assistant vice president of Prentice-Hall Inc., the publishing company, died Tuesday at his home in Teaneck, N.J. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Henkle, who worked 24 years for the publishing house, retired in 1977. He specialized in legal publications.
        A graduate of the original Townsend Harris High School, Mr. Henkle helped re-establish the school for gifted students at Queens College in 1984.
        He is survived by his wife, Esther.
