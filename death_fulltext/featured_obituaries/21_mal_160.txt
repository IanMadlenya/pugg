        Sumner W. White 3d, a retired Manhattan stockbroker, died of lung cancer Monday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 58 years old and lived in Summit.
        Mr. White, a 1952 graduate of Harvard University, was with the First Boston Corporation in Manhattan until he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, Eleanor Phillips White of Mt. Kisco, N.Y.; two daughters, Eleanor Durling of White House, N.J., and Constance, of Boston; two sons, Sumner 4th, of Chicago, and Timothy, of Manhattan; his mother, Virginia, and a brother, Edgar, both of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
