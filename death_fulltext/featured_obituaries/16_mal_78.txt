        Allen Keyes Eastman, a stockbroker, died of a heart attack Thursday at Stamford Hospital in Connecticut. He was 69 years old and lived in South Norwalk.
        Mr. Eastman was a director and officer of H. W. Wellington & Company in Manhattan.
        He is survived by his wife, Lucia Dana Simpson Eastman; two sons, Jeffrey K., of Thomaston, Maine, and Christopher O., of New Haven, and three stepchildren, Lucretia Simpson Juckett of Great Falls, Va., James Simpson of Washington and Lorraine Simpson Houston of Norwalk.
