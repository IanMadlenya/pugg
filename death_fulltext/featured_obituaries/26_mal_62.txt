        Paul J. Etzel, a former president of Brown, Harris, Stevens Inc., a realty company in Manhattan, died of complications from emphysema Monday at his home in Manhasset, L.I. He was 90 years old.
        Mr. Etzel joined Brown, Harris in 1923. He retired in 1970.
        Mr. Etzel was born in Manhattan. He graduated from Georgetown University.
        Surviving are his wife, Isabel; two sons, Allister, of Pleasantville, N.Y., and Jerard, of Southport, Conn.; two daughters, Suzanne Love of Manhasset and Antonia Kenney of East Moriches, L.I.; a brother, George, of Wilmington, Del.; a sister, Marie Cahill of Wyckoff, N.J.; 11 grandchildren, and 5 great-grandchildren.
