
        Bertrand J. Foley, a retired senior vice president of Dean Witter & Company, died on Sunday at the Hunterdon Medical Center in Flemington, N.J. He was 83 years old and lived in Pennington, N.J.
        He died of congestive heart failure, his family said.
        Mr. Foley, a native of Jersey City and a longtime resident of Tenafly, N.J., was a graduate of New York University. He joined Dean Witter in 1936 and retired in 1975.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Julia TenEycke, whom he married in 1931; a daughter, Margaret Baldwin of Pennington; a son, Bertrand, of Pompano Beach, Fla.; a brother, Lester, of Lewes, Del.; seven grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
