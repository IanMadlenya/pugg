        James J. Mannix Sr., a real-estate developer, died of a heart attack Tuesday at his home in the Prospect Park section of Brooklyn. He was 75 years old and also had a home in Vero Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Mannix was a graduate of St. John's University. He was a member of the St. John's University Council and a trustee at St. Xavier's Church of Brooklyn.
        Surviving are his wife, Mary; a daughter, Bernadette, of Manhattan; a son, James Jr., of Brooklyn, and two sisters, Josephine Reidy of Newtown, Conn., and Betty Cassidy of Brooklyn.
