        Ralph R. McCoy, a retired foundation vice president and a former oil company executive, died of a heart attack yesterday at the Waveny Care Center in New Canaan, Conn. He was 79 years old and lived in Darien, Conn.
        Mr. McCoy began his career as a service station employee with the Standard Oil Company in Washington. He became president of the Pate Oil Company, a Standard Oil affiliate, and later, president of the Oklahoma Oil Company and of the Oklahoma-Pate division of Humble Oil and Refining Company.
        When he left the oil business in 1969, he was marketing coordinator of Esso Standard Eastern. He then joined the Teagle Foundation in Manhattan, retiring in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; two daughters, Elizabeth Noel of Manhattan, and Sankey Blondin of Avon, Conn., and two grandchildren.
