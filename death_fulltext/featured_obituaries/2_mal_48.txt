        Jacob Ole Jacobsen, father of David P. Jacobsen, a former hostage in Beirut, died of natural causes Saturday. He was 92 years old.
        A native of Denmark, the elder Mr. Jacobsen joined other family members in November in welcoming his son home after 17 months of captivity in Lebanon.
