
        J. Clark Merritt, a lawyer, died on June 16 at his home in Manhattan. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Merritt, a native of Bethany Center, N.Y., was a lawyer in Manhattan for many years and specialized in workmen's compensation cases. He retired in the late 1980's.
        He was a graduate of the Rochester Business Institute and was manager of its Batavia branch from 1928 to 1931. In 1934, he received a B.S. degree from New York University and later received his law degree from Brooklyn Law School. Before entering private practice, he taught economics at Great Neck High School on Long Island.
        Mr. Merritt is survived by a sister, Lillian Babbage of Batavia, N.Y.
