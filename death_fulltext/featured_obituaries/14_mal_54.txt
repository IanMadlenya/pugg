        Samuel R. Donnellon, a civil engineer, died of heart failure Feb. 26 at Lake Wales (Fla.) Hospital. He was 103 years old and lived in Lake Wales.
        Born in Brooklyn, he graduated from Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art. He joined the Linde Air Products unit of Union Carbide Corporation in 1920 and became chief engineer, retiring in 1956.
        He is survived by two sons, John, of Mount Lebanon, Pa., and Samuel, of Wayne, N.J.; two daughters, Mary Blohm and Frances Updike, both of Lake Wales; 16 grandchildren and 26 great-grandchildren.
