        Louise Constantine, a former vice president of Aquascutum of London Inc., died of cancer Monday at New York Hospital. She was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        During her 18 years with the company, Miss Constantine first directed the women's retail division in London. When a store opened in New York, she was in charge of women's clothing. Before joining Aquascutum, she had worked for 19 years at Lord & Taylor, where she became a fashion director.
        She is survived by a sister, Koula Fotis of Fort Dodge, Iowa.
