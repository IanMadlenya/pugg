
       Mark B. Mitin, a Marxist philosopher and member of the Academy of Sciences since 1939, has died, the Tass press agency announced today. He was 85 years old. He was a member of the Central Committee from 1939 to 1961, and a member of the Supreme Soviet, the nominal legislature, from 1950 to 1962. He won a Stalin Prize in 1943.
