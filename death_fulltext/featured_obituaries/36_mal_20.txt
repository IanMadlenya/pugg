        Daniel Moffat, a retired stock broker, died of complications following open heart surgery Tuesday at New York University Hospital in Manhattan. He was 78 years old and lived in Queens Village, Queens.
        Mr. Moffat was a broker on Wall Street for more than 40 years and from 1950 to 1969 was president of Biggs & Moffat & Company, government bond brokers. He retired in 1969. Before that he was with C. F. Childs & Company, W. E. Pollack & Sons and the Irving Trust Company.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Georgina Thomson; two daughters, Marilyn Moffat Salant of Locust Valley, L.I., and Barbara Moffat Detmore of Dallas, and three grandchildren.
