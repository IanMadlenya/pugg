        Andre A. Perebinossoff, an oil-exploration engineer, died of a heart attack Sept. 16 at the Baylor University Medical Center in Dallas. He was 82 years old.
        His wife, Elisabeth, died last August. He is survived by a son, Phillippe, of Santa Monica, Calif.; a daughter, Catherine, of Manhattan, and a granddaughter.
