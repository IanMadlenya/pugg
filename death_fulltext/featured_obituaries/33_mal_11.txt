        Harold R. Fatzer, former Chief Justice of the Kansas Supreme Court shot himself to death at his home on Saturday. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Fatzer, who had been in poor health, served on the Court from 1956 to 1977 and was Chief Justice from 1971 to 1977. He was State Attorney General from 1949 to 1954.
        Mr. Fatzer graduated from the Washburn University School of Law in 1933. He was Edwards County Attorney until 1941, when he became chief counsel for the State Board of Social Welfare.
