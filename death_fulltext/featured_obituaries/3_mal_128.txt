        J. Russell McShane, a former president of the New York University Alumni Association, died April 19 at Mountainside Hospital in Montclair, N.J. He was 77 years old and lived in Glen Ridge, N.J.
        He was the founder of McShane Enterprises, an industrial paper and packaging company in Bernardsville, N.J.
        Mr. McShane was born in Brooklyn and graduated with honors from New York University in 1932. In 1981 he received its Alumni Meritorious Service Award, presented by the school for outstanding service.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Evelyn Reader; three sons, Dr. Richard McShane of West Orange, N.J.; David, of Bernardsville, and Donald, of Manhattan; seven grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
        A memorial service will be held at 11 A.M. today at Christ Episcopal Church, Bloomfield, N.J.
