        Mary E. T. Zabriskie, a retired president of the board of St. Agnes School in Alexandria, Va., died Sunday at her home in Washington. She was 90 years old.
        Mrs. Zabriskie was a 1919 graduate of Bryn Mawr College and was married the Rev. Alexander C. Zabriskie. She was executive director of the National Cathedral Association in Washington in 1958-59.
        She is survived by three sons, the Rev. George Zabriskie 2d; Dr. Philip Zabriskie, and the Rev. Alexander Zabriskie Jr., and a daughter, Mary Zabriskie.
