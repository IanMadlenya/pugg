        William N. Healey, a senior vice president and chief lending officer of the Ensign Bank, died of a brain tumor yesterday at the Rosary Hill Home in Hawthorne, N.Y. He was 58 years old and lived in White Plains.
        Mr. Healey, who was born in Queens and was a graduate of Fordham University, was in the accounting and real-estate fields before joining Ensign in 1984.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ruth Peters; a daughter, Barbara, and two sons, William and Thomas, all of White Plains; two sisters, Margaret, of Tarrytown, N.Y., and Elizabeth Winkelman of Hempstead, L.I., and a brother, Edward, of Alexandria, Va.
