        Msgr. John T. Egan, pastor of St. Sebastian's Roman Catholic Church in Woodside, Queens, died on Thursday. He was 71 years old and lived in Queens.
        Monsignor Egan died of congestive heart failure, a spokesman for the church said.
        Monsignor Egan began serving St. Sebastian's in 1973. He had been principal of Cathedral Preparatory Seminary in Elmhurst, Queens, from 1967 to 1973. He was a member of the board of The Tablet, the newspaper of the Brooklyn Diocese; a former board member of the Seminary of the Immaculate Conception in Huntington, L.I., and of St. Christopher's Home, a child-care agency in Sea Cliff, L.I.
        There are no immediate survivors.
