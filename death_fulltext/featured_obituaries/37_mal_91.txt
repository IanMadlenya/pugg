        David Bingham, a classic automobiles historian who founded the magazine Rallye in 1982, died of a brain tumor on Friday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 32 years old and lived in Port Washington, L.I.
        Mr. Bingham, who was born in Manhattan, was a graduate of the University of Denver.
        He is survived by his parents, Betty and Walter Bingham of Port Washington; a brother, Eric, also of Port Washington, and two sisters, Liza, of Manhattan and Amy, of San Francisco.
