
        TOKYO, Jan. 31
        Yasushi Inoue, a Japanese novelist known mainly for his historical fiction, died on Tuesday. He was 83 years old.
        The cause of death was pneumonia.
        Mr. Inoue's best-known works translated into English include "Tun-Huang," "The Hunting Gun," "Chronicle of My Mother," "Lou-lan," "The Counterfeiter" and "Roof Tiles of Tempyo."
