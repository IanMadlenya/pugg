        William R. Hamcke, former senior executive vice president and chief financial officer of Irving Trust Company, died of a heart attack Saturday at Stamford Hospital. He was 63 years old and lived in Stamford, Conn.
        Mr. Hamcke, who joined the bank in 1969, became an executive vice president in 1973 and a senior executive vice president in 1982. He was also an officer of the Irving Bank Corporation. He retired in 1987.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Sarah Irvine; two daughters, Barbara Miller of Kent, Conn., and Carol Jarvis of Hamden, Conn.; and two granddaughters.
