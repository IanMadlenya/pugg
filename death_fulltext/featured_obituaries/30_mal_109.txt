
       Francis X. Cucci, a television, theater and screen writer, died of AIDS on July 1 in the Cabrini Medical Center Hospice in Manhattan. He was 54 years old and lived in Brooklyn Heights.
        Francis X. Cucci, a television, theater and screen writer, died of AIDS on July 1 in the Cabrini Medical Center Hospice in Manhattan. He was 54 years old and lived in Brooklyn Heights.
        He is survived by his mother, Albina Cucci of Parsipanny, N.J.; a sister, Maria Pollina of Elm Wood Park, N.J., and four brothers: John, of Manahawkin, N.J.; Adolph, of Jackson, N.J.; Robert, of Baltimore, and William, of Chester, N.Y.
