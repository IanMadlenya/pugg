        Dr. Ralph W. Cordier, retired dean of academic affairs at Indiana University of Pennsylvania in Indiana, Pa., died Saturday at Indiana Hospital. He was 88 years old and lived in Indiana.
        The cause of death was not announced, and family members could not be reached.
        Dr. Cordier was dean of academic affairs from from 1955 to 1970, when he retired. He taught at Manchester College in Manchester, Ind., and state colleges in Illinois and Pennsylvania before joining Indiana University in 1955.
        He was born in Canton, Ohio, and graduated from Manchester College. He received a master's degree and a doctorate from Ohio State University and worked on post-doctoral studies as a Ford Foundation Scholar at Columbia University and the University of London.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Esta E. Brenner; a son, Sherwood of Kalamazoo, Mich.; two grandchildren, and a great-granddaughter.
