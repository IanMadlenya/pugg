        Robert Clermont Livingston Timpson, an investment banker, died Saturday at his home in Southampton, L.I., after a long illness. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Timpson had his own concern, Robert Timpson & Company. He retired in the late 1970's.
        He had been a managing partner in W.E. Hutton and had earlier been associated with White, Weld & Company, stockbrokers.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Hilles Morris; a daughter, Sarah, of Manhattan; two sons, Lawrence, of Boston, and Robert, of Greenwich, Conn.; a brother, Alistair, of Castle Combe, Wiltshire, England; two sisters, Katherine, of Seaton, South Devon, England, and Rosamund, of Matjiesfontein, Cape Province, South Africa, and a grandson.
