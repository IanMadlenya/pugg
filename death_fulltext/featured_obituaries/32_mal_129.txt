        The Rev. Leonard D. Di Falco, the pastor of St. Clare's Roman Catholic Church in the Morris Park section of the Bronx, died of cancer on Tuesday at Rosary Hill Hospital in Hawthorne, N.Y. He was 64 years old and lived in the Bronx.
        Father Di Falco was ordained in 1949, after graduating from St. Joseph's Seminary in Dunwoodie, N.Y. He later received graduate degrees from Fordham and Columbia Universities.
        Father Di Falco was an associate pastor at several parishes before being named administrator of St. Ann's Church in Yonkers in 1971. He became pastor there in 1972. He was named administrator and later pastor of St. Clare's in 1986.
        He is survived by his mother, Filipina, of Manhattan; three brothers, Dominic, of Commack, L.I., Jerome, of Massapequa, L.I., and Sebastian, of Staten Island, and two sisters, Catherine, of Manhattan, and Sister Mary Emanuel of the Bronx.
