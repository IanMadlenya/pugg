        Dr. C. Eugene Morris, a former dean of students at Pace University, died Wednesday at his home in Albany, after a long illess. He was 75 years old.
        Dr. Morris, a native of Memphis, Tenn., had been on the faculties of Union and Springfield Colleges and was professor emeritus of the C.W. Post Center of Long Island University. He had taught counseling at L.I.U.'s Graduate School of Education and was the founder and first director of its counseling service.
        He was also a former president of the board for the counseling service of the Episcopal Diocese of Albany.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Roosa; two brothers, Samuel C., of Smyrna, Ga., and Frank W., of Chattanooga, Tenn.; a daughter, Sherry Morris of Hingham, Mass., and two grandchildren.
