        Albert W. Frey, a former dean of the University of Pittsburgh's Graduate School of Business and a former president of the American Marketing Association, died of pneumonia on Tuesday at his home in Dresden, Me. He was 89 years old.
        Professor Frey, a graduate of Dartmouth College, taught at the Amos Tuck School of Business there from 1920 until 1961 and was assistant dean from 1930 to 1937.
        Survivors include his wife, Hope; two sisters, Marcia Haynes of Yarmouth, Me., and Helen Sorenson of Sarasota, Fla.; a daughter, Janet Harte of Corpus Christi, Tex.; four grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren.
