        Sydney Ullman, a retired Federal administrative law judge, died Thursday at a nursing home in Montclair, N.J. He was 79 years old and lived in Bethesda, Md.
        Mr. Ullman served at the Securities and Exchange Commission from 1962 until his retirement in 1979. He was a past president of the National Conference of Administrative Law Judges.
        Mr. Ullman's survivors include his wife, the former Lillian Kaufman of Bethesda, Md.; a daughter, Jane Nadler of South Orange, N.J.; a sister, Florence Ullman of Jersey City, and two grandchildren.
