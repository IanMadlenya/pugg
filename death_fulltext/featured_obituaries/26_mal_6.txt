        Lloyd William Daly, retired professor of Greek and Latin literature at the University of Pennsylvania, died Sunday at the home of his daughter, Caryl Johnson, in Englewood, N.J. He was 78 years old and lived in Englewood.
        Professor Daly joined the university in 1947 and taught there until he retired in 1977. He was the translator and editor of ''Aesop Without Morals.''
        A graduate of Knox College, Galesburg, Ill., he earned a master's degree and doctorate from the University of Illinois.
        A widower, he is survived also by a second daughter, Sara Rothenberger of Batesville, Va., and four grandchildren.
