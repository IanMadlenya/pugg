        Donald S. Curtis, a financial analyst who retired as vice president in the investment research department of Marine Midland Bank in 1974, died Jan. 3 at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital after a long illness. He was 78 years old and lived in Old Greenwich.
        Before joining Marine Midland in 1968, Mr. Curtis had been a senior investment analyst on Wall Street and an investment officer at Chase Manhattan Bank.
        He is survived by his wife, Doris Ames Curtis, and a daughter, Deborah Ames Curtis of Augusta, Me.
