        Mother Margarita Maria, a Roman Catholic nun who founded a religious order of Carmelite nuns and turned a farmhouse sanitarium into Santa Teresita Hospital here, died Wednesday. She was 85 years old. She became administrator of Santa Teresita after founding it as a tuberculosis sanitarium in 1930.
        In 1981 she helped spearhead construction of a five-story medical office building on the grounds. She retired as administrator of the hospital in 1984, but stayed on as president until 1987.
