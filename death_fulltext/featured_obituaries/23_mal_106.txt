        John D. McCallum, a former syndicated sports columnist and the author of 35 books, died Saturday after a long illness. He was 64 years old.
        Mr. McCallum was best known for books on sports figures, including a biography of Ty Cobb titled ''The Tiger Wore Spikes.''
        He was a former sports columnist for the Newspaper Enterprise Association.
        Mr. McCallum is survived by two brothers and two sisters.
