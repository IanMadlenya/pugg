        Malcolm J. Hood Sr., a senior vice president of the United States Trust Company of New York, died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Warren, N.J. He was 55 years old.
        Mr. Hood had worked for the bank 30 years and was a corporate trust officer. He was a native of Summit, N.J., and an alumnus of the Rutgers University Business School.
        Surviving are his wife, Carol; three sons, Malcolm Jr., of Hillsboro, N.J., and Clifford and Bradley, both of Warren, and a brother, Alfred S. Hood Jr., of Westfield.
