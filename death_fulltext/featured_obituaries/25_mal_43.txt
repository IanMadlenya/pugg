        Philip B. Blank, an associate dean and professor at the Pace University School of Law in White Plains, died of congestive heart failure on Sunday at White Plains Hospital. He was 54 years old and lived in Valhalla, N.Y.
        Professor Blank, who earned his degrees at Fordham, joined the Pace faculty in 1979 and became associate dean for external affairs in 1985. Earlier, he had taught at New York University and Manhattanville College.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Marcello; three sons, Arthur, Philip and Gregory, and a daughter, Patricia, all of Valhalla.
