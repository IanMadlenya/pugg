        Michael J. Cody, an insurance executive, died on Tuesday at his home in Short Hills, N.J. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Cody was associated with Guy Carpenter & Companies, a reinsurance broker that is a division of the Marsh & McLennan Companies. He joined Carpenter in 1959 and arranged reinsurance here and abroad.
        Mr. Cody was born in Westhampton Beach, L.I., and attended the University of Wyoming. He served with the Navy in World War II.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Margot Bowes; a sister, Leah Jones of Farmington, Conn., and a brother, Edwin, of Westhampton, L.I.
