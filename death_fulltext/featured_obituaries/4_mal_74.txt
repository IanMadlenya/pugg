        Hobart M. Kraner, a retired ceramic engineer and past president of the American Ceramic Society, died Friday at the Westminster Village Nursing Home in Allentown, Pa. He had resided in Bethlehem, Pa., and was 90 years old.
        He is survived by a son, Hobart W., of Bellport, L.I., and four grandchildren.
