        Mildred Seydell, a journalist who interviewed Mussolini and covered the Scopes trial, died today. She was 98 years old.
        Ms. Seydell, a resident of suburban Roswell, once said she ''much preferred to set the stage for adventure rather than the table for dinner.''
        A private burial was held today in Atlanta.
