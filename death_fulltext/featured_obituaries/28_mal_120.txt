        Gerard J. Danco, an importer and exporter of flavors and fragrances, died of a heart attack Monday at Morristown (N.J.) Memorial Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Morris Township, N.J.
        Mr. Danco, a native of Brussels, was owner and president of Gerard J. Danco International, an importing and exporting company in Morristown. He was a director of the Cosmetic Toiletries and Fragrances Association, a trade organization.
        Mr. Danco is survived by two sons, Paul, of Morris Plains, N.J., and G. Peter, of Cincinnati; two sisters, Suzanne, of Florence, and Minou Magerman of Brussels; three grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
