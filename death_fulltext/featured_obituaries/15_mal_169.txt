        C. Sterling Bunnell, a former executive of the First National City Bank and former director of Citibank, died of heart failure Saturday at Sharon (Conn.) Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Cornwall, Conn.
        Mr. Bunnell was born in Lorraine, Ohio. He joined the bank shortly after graduation from Yale College and worked there until he retired in 1966.
        Surviving are his wife, Naneen; a daughter, Shirley Dawson of Portola Valley, Calif.; a stepson, James C. Crimmins of Santa Barbara, Calif., 10 grandchildren and 4 great-grandchildren.
