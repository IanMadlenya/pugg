
        Thomas B. Haire, retired president of the Haire Publishing Company, died yesterday at his home in Little Compton, R.I.. He was 77 years old,
        He died of cancer, his family said
        Mr. Haire was born in Brooklyn and graduated from Cornell University. He had a brief career as a professional football player for the New York Giants and the Calgary Broncs before he joined the family concern in 1934. It publishes trade journals.
        He was president of the company from 1952 to 1968, when he retired. From 1955 to 1957, he was president of the Advertising Club of New York.
        Mr. Haire's wife, the former Virginia McCarty, died in 1988. Surviving are three sons, Thomas, of Summit, N.J., John, of Rye, N.Y., and Paul, of Vienna, Va.; two daughters, Virginia, of Manhattan, and Janet, of Charlestown, Mass., and seven grandchildren.
