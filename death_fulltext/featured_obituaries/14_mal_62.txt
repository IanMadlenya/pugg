        Maurice Wahl, a retired New York City Civil Court judge, died of cancer Feb. 18 while visiting his daughter in Eugene, Ore. He was 86 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Judge Wahl was an official referee of the Civil Court at the time of his death.
        He is survived by his daughter, Alice Lachman; a brother, Herman, of Silver Springs, Fla.; three sisters, Gertrude, Sylvia and Frieda, and three grandchildren.
