        Thaddeus William Hentz, a retired vice president of Foote, Cone & Belding Advertising Inc., died of heart failure May 7 at the Head Injury Center at Highgate in Troy, N.Y. He was 61 years old and lived in Allendale, N.J.
        Mr. Hentz joined Foote, Cone in 1958 and was associate creative director when he retired in 1986. He was born in Akron, Ohio. Mr. Hentz graduated from Middlebury College.
        Surviving are his wife, Carolyn; a daughter, Carrie, of Montclair, N.J.; a son, Ned, of Manhattan; his mother, Maurine Zang of Clearwater, Fla., and one grandchild.
