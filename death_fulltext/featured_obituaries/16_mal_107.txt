        Douglas E. Moran, a vice president and the controller of Macmillan Inc., the publishing and information services company, died Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 49 years old.
        A spokesman for the company, David Jackson, said yesterday that the cause of death had not yet been determined.
        Mr. Moran was born in Newton, Mass., and graduated from Boston University and the Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration. Before joining Macmillan as its controller in 1981, he worked for General Electric and Pepsico Inc.
        He is survived by his daughter, Deborah, of Rye, N.Y.; his mother, Edna Louise Moran Moore of Waltham, Mass., and a brother, Philip, of Stratham, N.H.
        A memorial service is scheduled for 11 A.M. tomorrow at the Church of the Heavenly Rest at Fifth Avenue and 90th Street.
