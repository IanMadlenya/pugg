        William Boone Nauts, a retired banker, died of pneumonia on Thursday at the Wolcott Hall Nursing Home in Torrington, Conn. He was 87 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Nauts joined the Brooklyn Trust Company in 1925. During the Depression he joined the Manufacturers Trust Company. He retired in 1967 as a vice president of Manufacturers Hanover Trust.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen L. Coley; two daughters, Nancy Dobbs of Tortola, British Virgin Islands, and Phyllis, of West Cornwall, Conn.; five grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
