        Benita S. Feurey, a television reporter and editor who specialized in consumer affairs, died of cancer Wednesday at Miriam Hospital in Providence, R.I. She was 49 years old and had residences in Manhattan and Southampton, L.I.
        Since 1986, Ms. Feurey, a native of Providence, was a consumer reporter for News 12 Long Island, a division of the Cablevision Systems Corporation. Earlier in her career, she was metropolitan editor for Good Housekeeping magazine, which she joined in December 1981. She had previously been a consumer reporter, producer and writer for WNBC-TV (Channel 4).
        She is survived by her mother, Helen Blau of Providence.
