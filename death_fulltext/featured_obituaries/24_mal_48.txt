        Dr. Michael J. Napolitano, chairman of the radiology department of the Catholic Medical Center of Brooklyn and Queens, died Thursday at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y., after a short illness. He was 59 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Dr. Napolitano, a native of Brooklyn, joined the center in 1968. He was appointed chairman in 1977.
        A graduate of Fordham University, he received his medical degree from the University of Rome.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Nancy Strawmyre, and four daughters, Lisa Ann, Laurie Ann and Nancy Ann all of Scarsdale, and Anne Regina, of Manhattan.
