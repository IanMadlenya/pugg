        William U. Gardner, professor emeritus of anatomy at the Yale University School of Medicine, died of Parkinson's disease Sunday at the Yale New Haven Hospital. He was 80 years old and lived in Orange, Conn.
        He began his career at Yale in 1933 as a research fellow, then became a faculty member and chairman of the anatomy department. He was Ebenezer K. Hunt professor of anatomy and became professor emeritus in 1974.
        He was former president of the American Association for Cancer Research.
        He is survived by his wife, Katherine.
