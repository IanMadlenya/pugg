        Dr. Michael Gilbert, a pediatrician and one of the founding physicians at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I., died of heart failure Tuesday at his home in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 69 years old. Dr. Gilbert was born in Brooklyn and graduated from Washington & Lee University and New York University Medical School. He was in private practice in Manhasset for 40 years.
        Surviving are his wife, Barbara; a son, David, of Glen Cove, and a sister, Barbara Rothstein of Manhattan.
