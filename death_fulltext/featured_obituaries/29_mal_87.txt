        William Schlesinger, former owner of Leighton's, a defunct men's store at Broadway and 47th Street, died Sunday at St. Francis Hospital, in Roslyn, L.I. He was 75 years old and lived in Tamarac, Fla.
        Mr. Schlesinger's father established the store in the 1920's, and the son joined the business in 1938. In the mid-1950's, he became president, a post he held when he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ruth Leveen; three sons, Peter, of Huntington, L.I.; Richard, of North Hills, L.I., and Dr. Fredric, of Mount Sinai, L.I.; a sister, Ruth Liss of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
