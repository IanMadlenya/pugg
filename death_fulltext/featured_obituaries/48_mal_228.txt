
        Robert E. Yancey Sr., former president of Ashland Oil Inc., died Sunday at Our Lady of Bellefonte Hospital in Russell, Ky. He was 69 years old and lived in Ashland, Ky.
        He died of cancer, a spokesman for the company said.
        Mr. Yancey, a native of Cleves, Ohio, joined Ashland Oil in 1943 after receiving an engineering degree from Marshall University. He rose to vice president in 1956 and was president of Ashland Chemical and Ashland Petroleum, subsidiaries, before becoming president of the parent company in 1972. He retired in 1982.
        He was a former chairman of the National Petroleum Refining Association.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Estelline Tackett; a son, Robert Jr. of Ashland, senior vice president of Ashland Oil; a daughter, Susan Farmer of Ashland, and six grandchildren.
