        Lyle McDowell Allen Jr., a former head of the legal division and assistant secretary of the American Home Products Corporation, died of lung cancer Thursday at the Duke University Medical Center in Durham, N.C. He was 66 years old and lived in Chapel Hill, N.C.
        Mr. Allen was a native of St. Louis and a magna cum laude graduate of Duke University. He was also a graduate of the Washington University Law School and specialized in taxation, product liability and antitrust law. In World War II, he served in the Navy in the South Pacific, rising to lieutenant commander.
        Surviving are his wife, Leigh; a son, Lyle 3d, of Chapel Hill; a daughter, Patricia, of Dallas; his mother, Dorothy, of Durham, N.C.; a sister, Patricia Perkins, of Sterling Park, Va., and two grandchildren.
