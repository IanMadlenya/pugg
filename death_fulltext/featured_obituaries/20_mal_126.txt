        Murray Cohen, who retired two years ago as senior partner of the New York law firm of Rosenman Colin Freund Lewis & Cohen, now Rosenman & Colin, died of complications from kidney disease on Friday at his home in Englewood, N.J. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Cohen, a native of Union City, N.J., graduated from Harvard Law School, where he was editor of the Law Review. He joined the Rosenman firm after graduation and remained there for his entire career.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Rita Sillman; two daughters, Deborah Shah and Jennifer Cohen, both of Manhattan; a son, Justin, of San Francisco; a brother, Alexander Courtney, of Los Angeles, and a granddaughter.
