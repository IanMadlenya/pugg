        Gilbert Miller Haas, an investment adviser and economic consultant, died of liver cancer Oct. 7 at his home in Key West, Fla. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Haas attended New York University and began his career on Wall Street in the early 1930's as a securities analyst. He was a partner in the investment consulting firm of Gilbert M. Haas & Company from 1958 to 1962 and was president from 1962 until 1985.
        He is survived by his wife, Patricia Haas; a daughter, Debra Juniper of Lake Worth, Fla.; three sons, Patrick, of Key West, Jeffrey, of Boca Raton, Fla., and Jonathan, of the San Diego area, and one grandchild.
