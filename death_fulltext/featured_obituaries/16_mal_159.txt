
       Eric Rutkowski, a 13-year-old Milwaukee boy became the youngest recipient of a Jarvik-7 artificial heart Thursday, but he died hours after the surgery when his lungs filled with fluid. Eric, a seventh-grade student at Sholes Middle School, died at St. Luke's Medical Center. The youngest previous recipient of the artificial heart was Christopher Larson, 15, of Esdaile, Wis.
