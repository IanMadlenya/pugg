        The Rev. Vincent P. Mallon, a Maryknoll missionary and teacher, died from complications of kidney disease Aug. 4 at Westchester County Medical Center in Valhalla, N.Y. He was 79 years old and lived in Maryknoll, N.Y.
        Born in Brooklyn, Father Mallon was ordained at Maryknoll and studied in Rome. He was assigned to a mission in Quintana Roo, Mexico. He later taught at the Maryknoll seminary, where he became vice rector. He returned to Mexico in 1973, as a missionary in the Yucatan. In 1987, he returned to the United States.
        Surviving are his brother, John, and two sisters, Margaret Kelly and Sister Elizabeth Mallon, all of Manhattan.
