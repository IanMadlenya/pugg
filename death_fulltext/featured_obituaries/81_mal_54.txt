
        John W. Lane, a retired Westchester real-estate broker and appraiser, died on Saturday at Northern Westchester Hospital Center in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was 87 and lived at Heritage Hills in Somers, N.Y.
        The cause was congestive heart failure, his family said.
        Mr. Lane, a native of New Rochelle, was the founding president of Lane Appraisals in Larchmont and retired 12 years ago at the age of 75. He was a past president of the Westchester County Realty Board and of the Greater New York chapter of the Society of Residential Appraisers. He was also a former Westchester County tax commissioner.
        Mr. Lane is survived by his wife of 49 years, Anne Gaynor Lane; two daughters, Theresa Lane Ferrarone of Pound Ridge, N.Y., and Kathleen Lane Nolan of Chappaqua, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
