        Alvin B. Finesman, a housewares marketing executive, died of lung cancer on Sunday at the Stamford Hospital in Connecticut. He was 60 years old and lived in New Canaan, Conn.
        Mr. Finesman, who was born in Toronto, Ohio, came to New York 25 years ago as a salesman for Dansk International and later was director of marketing for Cuisinart. After serving as president of the Robot Coupe International Corporation and Salton Inc., he headed his own firm, P N International of Mount Kisco, N.Y., which designs and distributes kitchen and household appliances.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marion Auerbach; three daughters, Cheryl Gerson of Pittsburgh, Susan Israelson of Manhattan and Michelle Finesman of New Canaan; a sister, Arlene Levinson of Mount Pleasant, Pa., and four grandchildren.
