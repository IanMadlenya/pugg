        The Rev. Cedric Fitz-Oswald Thompson, a Perpetual Deacon in the Espicopal Diocese of Newark and the assistant at Christ Church in East Orange, N.J., died of a heart attack Monday at his home in Orange, N.J. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Thompson, a 1954 graduate of Cornell University, was ordained to the diaconate in 1973. He served as the assistant at Christ Church for about nine years and previously had served at the House of Prayer in Newark and at the Church of the Epiphany in Orange. He was also a former president of the Black Clergy Association.
        He is survived by his wife, Stella.
