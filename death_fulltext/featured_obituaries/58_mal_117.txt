
        Thomas E. Hanigan Jr. a retired executive vice president of W. R. Grace & Company, a diversified chemicals concern, died on Sunday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 69 years old and lived in Rye, N.Y.
        He died of pneumonia, said a Grace spokesman, Fred Bona.
        Born in Schenectady, Mr. Hanigan went to Union College and joined Grace in 1946. He was also an executive of its Consumer Products and Services Group. He retired in 1980.
        Mr. Hanigan is survived by his wife, Olga; a sister, Joan Ryder of Lake George, N.Y.; two sons, V. Michel, of Rye, and Ian Thomas, of Saratoga Springs, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
