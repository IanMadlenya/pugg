        Warren Arthur Bahr, a former chief operating officer of the advertising agency Isidore Lefkowitz & Elgort Inc., died at his home in North Salem, N.Y., on Friday. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Bahr began his advertising career at Young & Rubicam Inc. in 1951, rising to become an executive vice president and a member of the board of directors. In 1975, he joined Isidore Lefkowitz & Elgort as chief operating officer. He served in that capacity until 1978.
        He is survived by his wife, Jean; four daughters, Amy Bahr Shields, of Westport, Conn., Sarah Bahr LaLiberte, of Gaithersburg, Md., Susan Bahr, of Manhattan, and Lisa Bahr, of Sunnyvale, Calif.; his mother, Elsie Bahr of Yonkers, and a sister, Marjorie Lagala, of Eastchester, N.Y. Services will be held at 11 A.M. today in the South Salem Presbyterian Church.
