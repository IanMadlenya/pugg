        Dr. Steven Koweity, a pediatrician, died of a heart attack Wednesday at Sand Lake Hospital in Orlando, Fla. He was 74 years old and lived in Searingtown, L.I.
        Dr. Koweity, who graduated from the Royal College of Medicine in Baghdad, Iraq, trained in pediatrics in London and New York. For the last 30 years he practiced in Brooklyn at the H.I.P. Bay Ridge Medical Center and at Brookdale Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, Eva,; two sons, Daniel and Elliot, both of Searingtown; five brothers, and two sisters.
