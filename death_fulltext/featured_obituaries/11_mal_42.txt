        Arthur John Stringer Jr., an advertising agency copy supervisor, died of cancer Nov. 20 at his home in Mountain Lakes, N.J. He was 60 years old.
        Mr. Stringer, a Duke University graduate, was copy supervisor of HBM Creamer Inc., when he became ill. He had been a vice president of the Demarty, Kurnet & Geller Advertising Company, which is defunct.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Regina M. Holl; a brother, Hugh A., of Concord, N. H.; two daughters, Margaret L., of Mountain Lakes, and Dr. Sharon A., of Youngstown, Ohio, and a son, Arthur J. 3d, also of Mountain Lakes.
