        Russell P. Westerhoff, a former president and chairman of Ford, Bacon & Davis, an engineering, construction and management consulting firm, died of cancer Sunday at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, N.J. He was 82 years old and lived in Ridgewood.
        Mr. Westerhoff received bachelor's and master's degrees from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He joined Ford, Bacon & Davis in 1928 and retired in 1973 after eight years as president and seven years as chairman and chief executive officer.
        He is survived by two daughters, the Rev. Judith Westerhoff of Little Falls, N.Y., and Sally Benton, of Ramsey, N.J.; a son, Richard, of Pittsburgh, and four grandsons.
