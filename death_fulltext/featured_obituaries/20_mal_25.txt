        Hazen G. Werner, a retired Bishop of the Methodist Church, died of heart failure Monday at his home in St. Petersburg, Fla. He was 93 years old.
        Bishop Werner, who was born in Detroit and received his divinity degree from Drew University in Madison, N.J., was elected Bishop in 1948 and WAS assigned to the Ohio area. From 1964 until his retirement in 1968 he was based in Manhattan as bishop of the church for Hong Kong and Taiwan.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen Elsea; a son, Stewart, of Richmond, Va.; seven grandchildren and a great-granddaughter.
