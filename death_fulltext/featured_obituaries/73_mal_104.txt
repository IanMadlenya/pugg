
        Frank W. Mendyk Jr., a former New York City Police Department inspector, died on Thursday at Long Island Jewish Hospital in New Hyde Park. He was 63 and lived in Albertson, L.I.
        He had melanoma, said a son, Robert.
        Mr. Mendyk was born in Ridgewood, Queens, and served in the Army during the Korean War. He joined the police in 1953 and became an inspector in 1978, overseeing divisions in Brooklyn and Queens. He retired in 1986.
        In addition to his son Robert, of Carle Place, L.I., he is survived by his wife, Helen; two other sons, Frank 3d of Commack, L.I., and Steven, of Albertson; a sister, Marie Knierim of Tamarac, Fla., and four grandchildren.
