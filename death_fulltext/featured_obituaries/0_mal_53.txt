        Dr. Milton Werbel, president of the Podiatry Society of the State of New York in the mid-1950's, died of a heart attack Jan. 8 at Palm Beach Lakes Hospital, Fla. He was 77 years old and lived in Royal Palm Beach.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Honey Ferbel; a daughter, Rita Botwin of Dix Hills, L.I.; a sister, Janet Nigen of Deerfield Beach, Fla., and two grandsons.
