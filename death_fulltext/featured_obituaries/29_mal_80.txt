        Dr. Samuel K. Levy, a pediatrician in Brooklyn for 64 years, died Friday at the Hillcrest Nursing Home in Pomona, N.Y. He was 95 years old and lived in Island Park, L.I.
        Dr. Levy was a 1916 graduate of the Long Island College of Medicine. He served at Linden General, Bushwick, and Maimonides Hospitals in Brooklyn.
        He is survived by a daughter, Audrey Youngelson of Suffern, N.Y.; a son, Dr. Joel, of Anaheim, Calif.; two brothers, Edward, of Flushing, and Lester, of Brooklyn; two sisters, Jeanette Worman of Great Neck, L.I., and Estelle Langs of Hallandale, Fla., and four grandchildren.
