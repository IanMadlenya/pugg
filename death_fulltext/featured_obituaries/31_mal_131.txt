        Dr. Arthur F. Ackerman, a pediatrician, died Sunday at the Medical Center in Princeton, N.J. He was 85 years old and lived in Hightstown, N.J.
        Dr. Ackerman graduated from the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University and began the practice of pediatrics in 1931 as a founding member of the Summit Medical Group in Summit, N.J. In the 1940's, he served as president of the medical staff at Overlook Hospital in Summit and was chief of pediatrics there for many years.
        He continued his medical practice until 1981. He served as president of the Summit Speech School and of the Reeves-Reed Cultural Arboretum.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara; a daughter, Martha Achilles of Branford, Conn.; two sons, Peter, of Scotts Valley, Calif., and Arthur, of Warren, N.J., and four grandchildren.
