        Gerard S. Dillemuth, the secretary-treasurer and chief financial officer of Weeks Marine Inc. of Cranford, N.J., a stevedoring company, died of a heart attack Tuesday at his home in Mountainside, N.J. He was 54 years old.
        A graduate of Fordham University and St. John's University Law School, he had been with the company since 1964.
        He is survived by his wife, Kathryn; three sons, Gerard Jr., of Houston and Christopher and Stephen, both of Mountainside, and a brother, Henry, of Lakewood, N.J.
