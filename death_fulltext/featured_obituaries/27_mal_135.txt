        Dr. Harold L. Ellis, a neurologist and psychiatrist, died of a heart attack yesterday at his home in the Bronx. He was 94 years old.
        Dr. Ellis had been in private practice in Manhattan since 1940. He was affiliated with Harlem Hospital and founded its first mental-health clinic. He taught at the Harlem School for Nurses.
        Dr. Ellis graduated from McGill University and its medical school.
        Surviving are two nephews, Jean Cropper of Manhattan and Harvey Cropper of Stockholm.
