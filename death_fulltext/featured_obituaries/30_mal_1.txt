        Gregory McMahon, a Republican who was a member of the New York Congressional delegation from 1948 to 1950, died Tuesday at the Sun Harbor Nursing Home in Roslyn Heights, L.I. He was 74 years old and lived in Garden City, L.I.
        Mr. McMahon, a native of Brooklyn, was a member of the 80th Congress from the Fourth District, which was then part of Queens and has since been redistricted. He later became a certified public accountant and owned his own business. He retired in 1981.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Juliette Schall; two sons, Lawrence, of Tulsa, Okla., and Gregory, of Glen Head, L.I.; a daughter, Maureen McMahon of Queens; four sisters, and four grandchildren.
