        Duncan M. Findlay, a former insurance and real-estate executive, died of lung cancer Monday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 87 years old and lived in Harrison, N.Y.
        Mr. Findlay was president of the Findlay-Noyes Insurance Company, which he sold to Helmsley-Spear Inc., now Helmsley-Noyes Inc., in the late 1960's. Earlier, he had been a vice president and director of Charles F. Noyes & Company, an insurance and real-estate concern.
        He was born in Parkville, Mo. Mr. Findlay graduated from Park College in 1922 and the Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration in 1924.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Mary Harding Simmonds; a son, Charles, of Belvedere, Calif., and two daughters, Susan Cathey of Locust Valley, L.I., and Ann Wiener of Manhattan, and seven grandchildren.
