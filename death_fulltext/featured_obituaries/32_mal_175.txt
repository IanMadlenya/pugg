        Dr. Herbert J. Buchsbaum, chairman of the department of obstetrics and gynecology at the Medical College of Wisconsin, died of a heart attack Monday at his home in Milwaukee. He was 55 years old.
        Dr. Buchsbaum joined the college as McMahon Professor last year. Previously, he was a professor at the University of Pittsburgh College of Medicine and head of gynecology-oncology at Magee Women's Hospital.
        Born in Vienna, he graduated from New York University and the University of Munich medical school.
        Surviving are his wife, Linda, and two children, Jonathan of Boston and Julie of Milwaukee.
