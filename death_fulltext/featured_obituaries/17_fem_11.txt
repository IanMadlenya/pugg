
       Elizabeth Duggan, a Broadway dancer, died Tuesday of cancer. She was 56 years old. Mrs. Duggan began her career at age 17 after studying at the Professional Children's School in New York City. Her Broadway credits included ''Brigadoon,'' ''Flahooley,'' ''Month of Sundays'' and ''New Faces of '52.''
