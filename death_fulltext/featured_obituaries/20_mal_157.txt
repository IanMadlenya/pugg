        Glen E. Covington, a singer and pianist who performed with his trio in supper clubs around the country, died on Sept. 18 after suffering a heart attack in Cleveland. He was 61 years old and lived in Indianapolis.
        Mr. Covington was born in Irving, Ky., and grew up in Louisville. One of his first major engagements was at the Copacabana in New York in the early 1950's.
        He is survived by his wife, Jewell, of Indianapolis; two sons; three daughters; a brother, and five grandchildren.
