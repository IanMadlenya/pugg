        Dr. Sidney Irving Kohn, retired chairman of the department of pediatric dentistry at the College of Dental Medicine at Fairleigh Dickinson University, died of a heart attack Friday at his home in Teaneck, N.J. He was 76 years old.
        Born in Jersey City, Dr. Kohn graduated with a degree in dentistry from Emory University. He joined Fairleigh Dickinson in 1957 and retired last year. He was a former chairman of the American Board of Pedodontics and a former president of the American Sociey of Dentistry for Children.
        He is survived by his wife, Adele; three sons, Steven, Donald and James, all of Guilford, Conn.; a sister, Ruth Lottenberg, of Hallandale, Fla., and two grandsons.
