        Joseph P. Denning, an Auxiliary Bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Brooklyn, died of a heart attack on Monday at St. John's Hospital in Queens. He was 84 years old and lived in Queens.
        Bishop Denning, a native of Flushing, Queens, was ordained in Brooklyn in 1932. He then taught for 20 years at Cathedral College in Brooklyn. He was pastor of Queen of Angels Church in Sunnyside, Queens, from 1953 to 1960, and was appointed an Auxiliary Bishop of Brooklyn in 1959. In 1960 he became pastor of Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church in Forest Hills, Queens, where he remained until he retired in 1978.
        He is survived by a sister, Kathleen Farrell of Queens.
