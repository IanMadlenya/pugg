        J. H. Raspberry, the president of the South Atlantic and Gulf Coast District of the International Longshoremen's Associationm died of a heart attack Thursday at age 56.
        His career with the longshoremen's union began in 1957, when he worked at the Houston Ship Channel. Mr. Raspberry was elected president of the South Atlantic and Gulf Coast District in 1978 and served as its leader for more than a decade.
        Mr. Raspberry was an international vice president of the union and an executive council member since 1975.
