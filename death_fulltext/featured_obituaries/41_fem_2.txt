        Grace Holmes Barbey, a consultant to the United Nations Children's Fund since its inception in 1947, died of a heart attack on Friday at New York Hospital. She was 83 years old and lived in Manhattan and in Paradise Valley, Ariz.
        Mrs. Barbey, who was attending a conference here when she was stricken, served as a liaison between UNICEF and member nations. She traveled around the world seven times on the organization's behalf and represented it at conferences on five continents. She retired from full-time service in 1965 but continued as a consultant until her death.
        Her second husband, Jean W. Barbey, died in 1973. She is survived by a brother, Samuel Parzybok of Wichita, Kans., and two sisters, Maurine Spach of Evanston, Ill., and Helen Erickson of San Diego.
