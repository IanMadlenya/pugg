        Andrew F. Peck, a securities executive, died of cancer Wednesday at Beth Israel Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 63 and lived in Manhattan. At his death he was chairman of Andrew F. Peck Associates, a discount brokerage firm that he formed in 1979. He was previously executive vice president of Kidder, Peabody & Company.
        Surviving are two daughters, Diane of Manhattan and Susan Lauderdale of Philadelphia; a son, Douglas, of New Bedford, Mass.; his father, Fremont, of Locust Valley, L.I., and a brother, Fremont Jr. of Albany.
