        Dr. Robert D. MacDougall, the dean of Cornell University's Division of Summer Session, Extramural Study and Related Programs, died of cancer May 8 at the Roswell Park Memorial Institute in Buffalo. He was 46 years old and lived in Ithaca, N.Y.
        Dr. MacDougall, who received his doctorate from Cornell in 1971, joined Cornell's College of Architecture, Art and Planning as an assistant professor in 1972. He was named dean of the division in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, Bonnie; two daughters, Carlin and Margaret; his father, Howard D., of Jamesburg, N.J., and a brother, Richard, of Oyster Bay, L.I.
