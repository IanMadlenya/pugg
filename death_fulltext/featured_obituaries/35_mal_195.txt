        Mickey L. Hooten, a television executive for the Hearst Corporation and a former chairman of the ABC Television Network Affiliates Board of Governors, died of a heart attack on Sunday at his home in Armonk, N.Y. He was 54 years old.
        Since 1984 Mr. Hooten was general manager for television for Hearst, the New York-based media company. He had joined Hearst in 1974 as program director for WTAE-TV in Pittsburgh.
        He is survived by his wife, Harvian Raasch-Hooten, two sons, Joshua, of Armonk, and Mickey L. Jr., of Port Charlotte, Fla., two daughters, Darby H. Parrish of Prairie Village, Kan., and Vicki H. Bruno of Atlanta; his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Darrell Hooten of Heber Springs, Ark., and three grandchildren.
