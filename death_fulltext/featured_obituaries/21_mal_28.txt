        Sheldon I. Kramer, the chief executive of Research Publications Inc., died of lung cancer Saturday at Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 51 years old and lived in Greenwich, Conn.
        Mr. Kramer joined the company, in Woodbridge, Conn., in 1982. The company puts newspapers, periodicals and academic and professional reference materials on microfilm. Mr. Kramer had previously been with the Xerox Corporation for 18 years.
        He is survived by his wife, Jane Wittenberg Kramer; a son, Scott, and a daughter, Loris, all of Greenwich; a sister, Shirley Kirsh of Atlanta; and a brother, Martin, of Washington.
