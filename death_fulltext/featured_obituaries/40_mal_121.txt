        William Pitkin, a theatrical designer, died of cancer on Sunday at Baptist Memorial Hospital in San Antonio. He was 65 years old and lived in Ingram, Tex.
        Mr. Pitkin designed sets and costumes for plays, operas, ballets and movies. He won an Emmy Award in 1978 for a televised production of the ballet ''Romeo and Juliet.''
        There are no immediate survivors.
