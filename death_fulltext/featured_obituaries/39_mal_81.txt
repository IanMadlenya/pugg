        Edward O. Kallmann, a financial consultant and adjunct finance professor at Pace University for 30 years, died in his sleep on Saturday at his home in East Williston, L.I. He was 89 years old.
        Professor Kallmann, a native of Brooklyn, worked in credit and financial management for 30 years in the oil, drug, coal and steel industries. From 1956 to 1972 he was executive vice president of the Stationery and Office Equipment Board of Trade.
        After his retirement from Pace, he was a financial consultant for the National Office Products Association.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Tollock; a daughter from an earlier marriage, Eleanor Lyness of Baldwinsville, N.Y.; eight grandchildren, and eight great-grandchildren.
