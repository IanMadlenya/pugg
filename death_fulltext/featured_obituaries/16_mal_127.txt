        Benedict P. Willis Jr., a former vice president of the Stauffer Chemical Company, died of colon cancer Thursday at his home in Rutherford, N.J. He was 66 years old.
        Surviving are his wife, Catherine Rubacky Willis; six daughters, JoEllen, Kim and Maureen, of Rutherford, and Patricia, Frances and Jean, of Manhattan; four sons, Benedict 3d, of East Rutherford, N.J.; Timothy, of Bloomfield, N.J., and Gerald and Daniel, of Rutherford: three sisters, Nancy Wirth of San Jose, Calif., Dr. Jean Louise Willis of Rutherford and Caroline Richardson of Bethlehem, Pa., and three grandchildren.
