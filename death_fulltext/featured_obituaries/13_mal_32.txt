        Robert Fitzsimmons, an actor and union leader, died of cardiac and pulmonary arrest on Jan. 31 at Mt. Sinai Hospital. He was 73 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Fitzsimmons appeared on Broadway in ''Abe Lincoln in Illinois,'' ''Legal Grounds,'' ''The Selling of the President'' and ''Time Remembered.'' He also took part in touring productions and educational and industrial films.
        Mr. Fitzsimmons was on the governing council of the Actors' Equity Association from 1975 to 1987, and from 1980 to 1982 he was also treasurer. He had recently been named councilor emeritus of the union and had been president of the board of the Equity Library Theater for eight years.
