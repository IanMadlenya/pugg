
        Anna Paisner, a retired businesswoman who was active in charitable affairs, died on Wednesday at Parkway General Hospital in North Miami Beach. She was 91 years old and lived in North Miami Beach.
        She died of congestive heart failure, a grandson, Daniel, said.
        From 1943 to 1953, Mrs. Paisner was a partner in the Economy Trading Corporation, importers of canned fish in Manhattan. She was also a partner in the Modern Manufacturing Braid Company, novelty braid and fabric manufacturers in Manhattan from 1935 to 1972, when she retired.
        Her husband, Benjamin Paisner, a Manhattan businessman, died in 1967.
        She is survived by two sons, Gilbert, of Roslyn Heights, L.I., and Sidney, of Great Neck, L.I.; a sister, Bonnie Gottlieb of Equinonk, Pa.; seven grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
