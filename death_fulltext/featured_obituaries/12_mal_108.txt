
       A memorial service for Leo Steiner, co-owner of the Carnegie Deli, will be held 12:30 P.M. Feb. 1 at the Sutton Place Synagogue at 225 East 51st Street. Mr. Steiner died of complications of a brain tumor Dec. 31 at Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center. He was 48 years old.
