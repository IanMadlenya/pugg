        Yustin Sirutis, a former Golden Gloves heavyweight boxing champion and a coach for many years at the City College of New York, died Jan. 6 in Rincon, P.R., after a long illness. He was 79 years old and lived in Rincon.
        Mr. Sirutis, a 1932 graduate of New York University, won both the New York City Golden Gloves and the Intercollegiate Boxing championships in 1931. From 1955 to 1968, he was the boxing coach at City College and was then a professor and chairman in the physical education department at Baruch College until his retirment in 1970.
        Mr. Sirutis is survived by his wife, Charlotte.
