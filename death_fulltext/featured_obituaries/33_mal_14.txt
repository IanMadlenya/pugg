        Robert J. Felheim, a retired partner of Neuberger & Berman, a brokerage and money-management concern, died Friday at New York Hospital. He was 85 years old and lived in Singer Island, Fla.
        Mr. Felheim was a founder of the Children's Blood Foundation, a research center at New York Hospital, and was on its board.
        He attended Phillips Exeter Academy and the University of Pennsylvania. He served in the Army Air Corps, where he reached the rank of captain. Mr. Felheim was born in Erie, Pa.  Surviving are his wife, the former Pearl Morris; a son, Robert, of Manhattan, and a brother, Lasalle, of Palm Beach, Fla.
