        Edward H. Selonick, a former retailing executive, died of cancer Tuesday in Cincinnati, where he lived. He was 66 years old.
        Mr. Selonick retired in 1984 as vice chairman of Shillito Rikes, a Middle Western department store chain, now the Lazarus chain. Since then he had been a faculty member at Miami University, in Oxford, Ohio.
        Mr. Selonick, who was born in Manhattan, is survived by his wife, Mildred; a daughter, Carol J. Solinger of Cincinnati; a son, Dr. Stuart Selonick of Annapolis, Md., and three grandchildren.
