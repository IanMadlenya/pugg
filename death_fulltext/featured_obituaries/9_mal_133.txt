        Thomas Edward Berry, an account executive, died Wednesday at Doylestown (Pa.) Hospital. He was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan and Solebury, Pa.
        Mr. Berry was a native of Toronto and a graduate of Knox College in Galesburg, Ill. At his death, he was associated with Rathe Productions of New York, which worked on industrial exhibitions and displays.
        Surviving are his wife, Elaine; five daughters, Leslie Berry of New York, Victoria Berry and Marcia B. Fogel of Lancaster, Pa., Joan Berry, of San Diego, and Melissa B. Laude of Colorado Springs; a sister, Sue Murphy of Lancaster, and three grandchildren.
