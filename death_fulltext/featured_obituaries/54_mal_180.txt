
        William Francis Florio, a self-employed design consultant from Hartford who was the brother of Gov. Jim Florio of New Jersey, died of cancer on Sunday at the New York University Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 50 years old.
        John Shure, the Governor's spokesman, said Mr. Florio had been ill "for quite a while."
        Besides Governor Florio, he is survived by another brother, Richard, and his parents, Vincent and Lillian.
