        Dr. Henry Morgenthau Fox, a Boston psychoanalyst, died at his home in Wellesley, Mass., Wednesday after a long illness. He was 80 years old.
        Born in Manhattan, Dr. Fox graduated from Harvard College and Johns Hopkins Medical School.
        In 1946, he established a permanent psychiatric unit at the Peter Brent Brigham Hospital in Boston. He was a member of the Boston Psychoanalytic Society and Institute and a professor at Harvard University Medical School.
