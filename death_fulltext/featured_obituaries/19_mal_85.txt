        Karl Nyren, a retired senior editor of Library Journal, a weekly publication for librarians, died of intestinal cancer Saturday at Castle Point Hospital in Beacon, N.Y. He was 66 years old and lived in Beacon.
        Mr. Nyren, a native of Boston, was senior editor of Library Journal from 1966 until his retirement in 1986. He also founded and edited Library Hotline, a weekly newsletter.
        A widower, he is survived by a son, Neil, of Manhattan; two daughters, Kari, of Beacon, and Eve Okawa of Kobe, Japan; and a grandson.
