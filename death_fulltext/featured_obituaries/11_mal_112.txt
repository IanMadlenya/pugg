        Joseph Grossfield, a professor of genetics at City College, died of lung cancer Saturday at the Cabrini Hospice in Manhattan. He was 47 years old.
        He was a City College graduate and earned a doctorate from the University of Texas.
        He joined the City College faculty in 1974. In recent years, he researched migratory fish under grants from the National Science Foundation, the Fish and Wildlife Service and the Hudson River Foundation.
        He is survived by his wife, Reva, of the Bronx, and two daughters, Jami and Juli, both of Redding, Conn.
