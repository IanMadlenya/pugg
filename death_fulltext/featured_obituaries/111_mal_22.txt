
        Robert A. Whittaker is a vice president of Foster Wheeler Corporation in Clinton, N.J., which provides engineering and construction services for industry. He is 47.
        Mr. Whittaker had been working on projects around the globe: from the construction of a refinery in Colombia, to a number of power projects in China.
        Mr. Whittaker, a former general manager of General Electric Company's steams turbine business, was named to head the energy subsidiary in 1992.
