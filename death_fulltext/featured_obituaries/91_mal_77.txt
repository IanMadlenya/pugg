
        Dr. Sanford J. Perlis, a psychiatrist in Westport, Conn., died on Wednesday at Yale-New Haven Hospital. He was 68.
        The cause was cancer, said his son Michael S. Perlis.
        Dr. Perlis practiced psychiatry in Westport for 38 years. He was also an associate professor at Yale University, where he supervised the psychotherapy work of mental health professionals training at the Yale University Health Services.
        He is survived by his wife, Vivian Perlis of Westport; two sons, Jonathan, also of Westport, and Michael, of Riverside, Conn.; a sister, Corinne Nayer of Huntington Woods, Mich., and four grandchildren.
