        Claire Lang von Arnold, a fashion consultant and retailing executive, died of pneumonia Friday at her home in Manhattan. She was 91 years old.
        Born in Budapest, Miss Lang, as she was known professionally, emigrated to the United States and worked as a commercial artist. She became head stylist at Lord & Taylor in the 1930's and was a senior executive vice president of Franklin Simon, a Manhattan store, in the 1940's and 1950's. She then formed Claire Lang Associates, a fashion merchandising consultancy.
        Surviving are her son, John, of Bay Shore, L.I.; three grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
