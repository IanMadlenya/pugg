
        David M. Reeves, a former secretary of the National Association of Manufacturers, died on Saturday at Greenwich Hospital in Connecticut. He was 73 years old and lived in Stamford, Conn.
        He died of cancer, said his son David.
        Mr. Reeves was secretary of the N. A. M. from 1958 to 1962, after which he worked in public relations and retailing. He attended the University of Pennsylvania.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Alva Anderson; two sons, David, of Alpine, N.J., and Bruce, of Stamford, and three grandchildren.
