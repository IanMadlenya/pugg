        Harold E. Pickett, publisher of The New York City News, died Thursday at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center of New York from complications of AIDS. He was 41 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Pickett started the New York City News, a bimonthly newspaper for the lesbian and gay community, in 1979 and closed it in 1986, when he became ill.
        His longtime companion was Jim Ferguson. Survivors are his parents, Margie and Cleston Pickett; two brothers, Douglas and Lyndell, and a sister, Valerie Perkins, all of Greensburg, Ky.
