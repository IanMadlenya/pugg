
        Arthur H. DesGrey, who served for 41 years as an administrator of athletics and a teacher at City College, died Sunday in Miami Shores, Fla. He was 86 years old.
        Loretta Olson, one of Mr. DesGrey's friends, said he had died of lung cancer.
        Mr. DesGrey, who had an undergraduate degree and postgraduate degrees in arts and education from New York University, was appointed in 1926 as a teacher and an administrator in the Department of Health, Physical Education and Recreation at City College. He served as faculty manager of intercollegiate athletics from 1951 until his retirement in 1967.
        Mr. DesGrey, whose wife, Henrietta, died in 1987, left no survivors.
