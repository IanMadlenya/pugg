
        A memorial service for Kevin O'Connor, the actor and director, is to be held on Monday at 6 P.M. at the Anspacher Theater of the Public Theater, 425 Lafayette Street, at Astor Place, Manhattan. A Kevin O'Connor Scholarship Fund has been set up at the Neighborhood Playhouse. Contributions can be sent to Harold Baldridge at the Playhouse, 340 East 54th Street, New York, N.Y. 10022. Mr. O'Connor died of cancer on June 22. He was 56 years old.
