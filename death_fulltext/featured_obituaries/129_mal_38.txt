
        Robert L. Wheeler Jr., the track and cross-country coach at Queens College for 18 years and assistant meet director of the Colgate Women's Games for 23 years, died Monday at his home in Flushing, N.Y. He was 67 years old.
        His wife, Linda, said it was determined in February that he had lung cancer.
        He was vice president and hurdles coach of the Atoms Track Club of Brooklyn, for years one of the nation's leading women's teams.
        He is survived by his wife; a son, Robert L. Wheeler 3d; a daughter, Adrienne Francis; two granddaughters, and his stepmother, Katherine Wheeler.
