
        Barry Silverman, a senior partner in the Manhattan law firm of Dornbush, Mandelstam & Silverman, died on Saturday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 57 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of lung cancer, his partner, Charles L. Mandelstam, said.
        Mr. Silverman, who was born in Manhattan, was a graduate of the University of Miami Law School. He specialized in corporate law and securitires regulation.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elaine Pancoski, and two daughters, Lisa Chelsea and Andrea, both of Manhattan.
