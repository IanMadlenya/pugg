        Edward A. Kelley, a pharmaceutical marketing consultant, died of lung cancer Sunday at his home in Doylestown, Pa. He was 54 years old.
        Mr. Kelley, who had a master's degree from the Harvard University Graduate School of Business, was recently honored by his alma mater for his fund-raising efforts. An award has been established in his honor.
        He is survived by his wife, Joan; three sons, Stephen, of Charlottesville, Va., and Shaun and David, both of Doylestown; a brother, Paul, of Milton, N.Y., and a sister, Mary E. Kimball of Glastonbury, Conn.
