        Val Bjornson, who served for 22 years as Minnesota State Treasurer, died Tuesday of congestive heart failure in his home here. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Bjornson became associate editor of The St. Paul Pioneer Press Dispatch in 1947. He was elected State Treasurer in 1950. In 1954, he was the Republican nominee for United States Senate, losing to a Democrat, Hubert H. Humphrey Jr. Mr. Bjornson returned to the Pioneer Press after the election, then was re-elected State Treasurer in 1956.
