
        Bishop Noah W. Moore Jr., a retired executive of the United Methodist Church, died on May 14 in the Linwood Convalescent Center, Linwood, N.J. Bishop Moore, who lived in Atlantic City, was 92.
        The cause was cancer, said his daughter, Carolyn Weddington.
        A native of Newark, Bishop Moore graduated from Morgan State College and in 1931 received a master of divinity degree from Drew Theological Seminary. After his ordination in 1932, he served in Maryland and Pennsylvania. He became a bishop in 1960, serving in New Orleans, Houston and Lincoln, Neb., before he retired in 1972.
        He is survived by his daughter, of Atlantic City, and two grandchildren.
