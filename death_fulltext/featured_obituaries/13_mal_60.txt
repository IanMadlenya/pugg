        Marvin Schwartz, a lawyer, died of a stroke Feb. 2 at his home in Kingston, N.Y. He was 65 years old.
        Mr. Schwartz graduated from Columbia Law School in 1947 and worked at various New York law firms, including his own, until 1980. He then served as an administrative law judge and arbitrator.
        He is survived by a son, Jonathan, of Manhattan; three daughters, Paula, of Manhattan, Diana, of Albuquerque, N.M., and Katherine, of New Paltz, N.Y., and a sister, Muriel Levy Somer of Delray Beach, Fla.
