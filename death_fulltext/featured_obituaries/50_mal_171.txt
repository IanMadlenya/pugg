
        Leslie Handler, an estate lawyer for more than 50 years, died on Sunday at his winter home in Palm Beach, Fla. He was 90 years old and also lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Handler practiced with the Manhattan firm of Friedman, Marx & Handler, which became associated with Garfunkel, Wild & Travis in 1984. He joined the firm in 1929 and retired in 1984.
        Mr. Handler was born in Brooklyn and graduated from City College and New York University Law School.
        A widower, he is survived by two daughters, Ellen Handler Spitz of Larchmont, N.Y., a writer, and Constance Greenspan of Reston, Va., a teacher; a brother, Prof. Milton Handler of Columbia University Law School; a sister, Ruth Rich of Fort Lauderdale, Fla., and five grandchildren.
