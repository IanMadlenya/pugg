        Maj. Gen. Hugh M. Milton 2d, a former Under Secretary of the Army,died Tuesday at William Beaumont Army Medical Center in El Paso, Tex. He was 89 years old.
        General Milton was named Assistant Secretary of the Army in 1953 by President Eisenhower.
        In 1958, he was appointed Under Secretary of the Army, putting him in charge of Army operations throughout the world. He continued in the job until he retired from the military in January 1961.
        General Milton is survived by his wife, Josephine, and his two sons.
