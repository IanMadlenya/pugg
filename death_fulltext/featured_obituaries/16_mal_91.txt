        Dr. Herbert D. Ayers Jr., a teacher of dentistry at Columbia University for almost half a century, died Saturday at the University Heights Nursing Home in Albany after a long illness. He was 79 years old and lived in Roslyn Estates, L.I.
        Dr. Ayers, a 1929 graduate of Columbia College, received his professional degree from the university's School of Dental and Oral Surgery in 1931. He joined the staff as an assistant in dentistry in 1936 and retired as emeritus clinical professor of dentistry 50 years later.
        For many years, Dr. Ayers had his private practice in Flushing, Queens. More recently, he practiced at his Roslyn Estates home.
        He is survived by his son, James B., of Albany; a brother, James S., of Vero Beach, Fla., and two grandsons.
