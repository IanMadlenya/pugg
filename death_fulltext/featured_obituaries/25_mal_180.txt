        Dr. Wilhelm Z. Stern, director of radiology at North Central Bronx Hospital, died of cancer Saturday at Montefiore Hospital. He was 61 years old and lived in the Riverdale section of the Bronx.
        Dr. Stern was a native of Vienna, a graduate of the University of Geneva and an associate professor of radiology at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Jessica Goldin, and three children, Michael, Alexander and Julia, all of Manhattan.
