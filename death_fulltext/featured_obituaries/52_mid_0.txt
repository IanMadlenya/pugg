
        Margaret Tallichet Wyler, who appeared in films including "It Started With Eve," "Every Man for Himself" and "Stranger on the Third Floor," died on Friday. She was 77 years old.
        The cause of death was listed as cancer.
        Miss Tallichet played a woman menaced by Peter Lorre in "Stranger on the Third Floor" in 1940 and appeared with Charles Laughton and Deanna Durbin in "It Started With Eve" in 1941.
        She married the director William Wyler in 1938. He died in 1981.
