        Dr. Chin Tang Huang, professor of medicine and chief of the division of pulmonary diseases at the Lincoln Hospital Center, died of gastric cancer Thursday at the Westchester Medical Center in Valhalla, N.Y. He was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan and Sands Point, L.I.
        Dr. Huang, a native of Taiwan, came here in 1960. He joined Lincoln Hospital in 1983. Earlier, he had been director of the intensive-care unit at Kings County Hospital. Dr. Huang was on the faculty of the New York Medical College.
        He was the first president of the National Taiwan University Medical School Alumni Association and the National Taiwan University Alumni Association. Dr. Huang was also secretary of the American Bureau for Medical Advancement in China and director and secretary for the Asia Bank.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Lena Liu; three children, Lawrence and Andrew, both of Manhattan, and Cheryl, of Palo Alto, Calif.
