        Alma Nicoll Baker, an interior designer, died of ovarian cancer yesterday at Memorial Hospital. She was 72 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Baker, who was born in Manhattan, studied at the School of Professional Design and joined the interior design concern Ellen L. McCluskey Associates in 1948. She was a trustee of the Shipley School of Bryn Mawr, Pa.
        She is survived by her husband, R. Palmer Baker Jr.; a daughter, Mary Fickett of Manhattan; a brother, DeLancey Nicoll 3d, and a grandson.
