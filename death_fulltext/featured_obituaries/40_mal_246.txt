        The Rev. Walter Allyn Rogers, an Episcopal priest and the former priest in charge of St. Simeons Episcopal Church in the Bronx, died on Tuesday at Brattleboro (Vt.) Memorial Hospital. He was 76 years old and lived in East Dover, Vt.
        Mr. Rogers was ordained in 1954 after graduating from Temple University.
        He had served as assistant rector of St. Michael's Episcopal Church in Manhattan, and from 1966 to 1980, as rector of St. James Episcopal Church in Dover Plains, N.Y. and as priest in charge of St. Thomas Episcopal Church in Armenia Station, N.Y. He retired from active ministry in June 1980.
        He is survived by a sister, Arlene Stephens of Jacksonville, Fla.
