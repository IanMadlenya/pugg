        Victor Feingold, a lawyer who was general counsel to the Distillery, Wine and Allied Workers International Union, died Monday while recuperating from heart surgery at his home in New Rochelle, N.Y. He was 77 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Postman; two daughters, Lisa Cohen of Bronxville, N.Y., and Jane Goldman of Potomac, Md.; a sister, Joan Feingold of Manhattan, and three grandchildren.
