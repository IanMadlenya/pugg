        Harold M. Olmstead, a retired engineer who specialized in public utility work, died Aug. 9 in the infirmary of the Virginian, a residence for the elderly in Fairfax, Va. He was 98 years old.
        Mr. Olmstead was a hearing examiner for the New York State Public Service Commission from 1940 until 1962, when he retired. He began his career with the Southwestern Bell Telephone System.
        He is survived by his son, Robert, of Queens; a daughter, Lucia-ann Effros of Clifton, Va.; four grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
