
       The Rev. John Lawrence Denny, a priest in the Episcopal Diocese of Newark and an associate at St. Anthony of Padua in Hackensack, died on Monday at Hackensack (N.J.) Medical Center. He was 70 years old and lived in Hackensack and in Southhold, L.I.
        Mr. Denny was an executive with Emory Air Freight from 1946 to 1958 before being ordained a priest. He served as vicar of the Church of Transfiguration in Towaco, N.J., and of the Church of the Good Shepherd in Ringwood from 1958 to 1961, before being called as rector of Christ Episcopal Church in Teaneck. He served there until 1985.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jane Irene Stewart; and two brothers, William, of Zanesville, Ohio, and Silas, of Cheboygan, Mich.
