        Albert Chin, a Long Island restaurateur, died after a stroke on Sunday at Booth Memorial Hospital in Queens. He was 64 years old and lived in Jamaica, Queens.
        Mr. Chin was the proprietor of Kwong Ming in Wantagh, Sun Ming in Huntington and Ming in Baldwin.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Nancy Lee; two daughters, Janice Lum of Merrick, L.I., and Belinda Woo of Hollis Hills, Queens; two sons, Bruce, of Jamaica Estates, Queens, and Jeffrey, of Haworth, N.J.; three brothers, Robert, of Lewiston, Me., Hubert, of Mechanicsburg, Pa., and Gilbert, of Detroit; two sisters, Helen Wong of San Francisco and Dorothy Eng of Rego Park, L.I., and 10 grandchildren.
