
        A. Louis Carrafiello, president of Carrafiello Diehl & Associates, an advertising agency in Irvington, N.Y., died on Monday at Phelps Memorial Hospital Center in North Tarrytown, N.Y. He was 64 and lived in Ossining.
        The cause was a heart attack, the family said.
        Before founding his own agency, Mr. Carrafiello held senior positions in a some of the country's largest ad agencies, including McCann-Erickson.
        He graduated from the University of Connecticut in 1949 and served in the Army during the Korean War.
        He is survived by his wife, Claire; a son, Gerald, of Ossining; three daughters, Deborah, of Stuart, Fla.; Elaina, of Boca Raton, Fla., and Alisa Robinson, of Burlington, Vt.; a sister, Josephine Vallillo, of Trumbull, Conn., and five grandchildren.
