
        John A. Scoville was chairman of the Harza Companies, a privately held international consulting and engineering firm based in Chicago. He was 64 and lived in Wilmette, Ill.
        Before joining Harza in 1957, he worked briefly for the Ohio Oil Company and  the United States Army Corps of Engineers.
        During his career at Harza, Mr. Scoville worked on projects all over the world, developing an expertise in geotechnical engineering. In recent years he worked on jobs in more than 17 countries, including a hydroelectric project in China, the largest arch dam project in the world.
        A member of Harza's board since 1971, he was named chairman in 1990.
