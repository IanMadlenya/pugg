        John Patrick Walsh, an advertising executive and former public relations official for the New York City Department of Correction, died of pneumonia Monday at St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan. He was 58 years old and lived in the Bay Ridge section of Brooklyn.
        Mr. Walsh was senior vice president of Mortimer Matz Associates for the last 10 years and was with the Correction Department for the previous 18 years.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary Ann; a son, Christopher, and a daughter, Victoria, both of Bay Ridge, and a sister, Dorothy Hunt of St. Petersburg, Fla.
