        Augustin Francis Oakes, a retired printer, died of brain cancer Wednesday at his home in Port Washington, L.I. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Oakes served as director of the Printing Industries of America, a trade organization, and as the president of the New York Employing Printers Association. He was a founder of the Young Printing Executive Club.
        Survivors include two daughters, Maureen Rickard of Garden City, L.I., and Patricia Renner of East Corinth, Vt.; three sons, Augustin Jr. of Port Washington, Gerard of Garden City and Donald of Pittsburgh, and 13 grandchildren.
