        Robert E. Heathcote, a retired manufacturer, died Monday at his home in Sebastian, Fla., after a long illness. He was 72 years old.
        Mr. Heathcote, an alumnus of Auburn University, was the former president of the Heathcote Hosiery Company of Manhattan and of the Lynne Hosiery Mills, of Mt. Airy, N.C.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; three daughters, Lynne Edgar, of Manhasset, L.I., Jean Dergay, of Mt. Airy, and Sarah Heathcote, of Dallas; a sister, Betty Schwiebert, of Julian, Calif.; and five grandchildren.
