
        Daniel A. Bailey, a senior audit partner at Deloitte & Touche, the accounting firm, died on Saturday at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, N.J. He was 49 years old and lived in Woodcliff Lake, N.J.
        His family declined to disclose the cause of death.
        Mr. Bailey, a graduate of Fordham, joined the firm in 1963, specializing in insurance and banking issues, and became a partner in 1973.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Plappert, and a son, Daniel Jr., of Wilmington, N.C.
