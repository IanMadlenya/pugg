        David Schilling Kane, a former president of the American Patent Law Association and former adjunct professor at the New York University School of Law, died of cancer Saturday at Naples (Fla.) Community Hospital. Mr. Kane was 81 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Kane was a senior partner in Kane Dalsimer, a law firm in Manhattan. In the late 1950's, he served as Mayor of Sands Point, L.I.
        He is survived by two daughters, Sheila Strenkert and Kathleen Dell, both of Naples; a son, David, of Briarcliff Manor, N.Y.; a brother, Daniel, of Centerport, N.Y.; two sisters, Eileen Dickison of Arlington Heights, Ill., and Marjorie Hoppen of Naples, and six grandchildren.
