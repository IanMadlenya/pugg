
        Peter James Prince, an interior designer, died on Monday at his home in Locust Valley, L.I. He was 63 years old.
        He died of lung cancer, his daughter, Pamela, of Los Angeles, said.
        He was in business for himself, working out of Locust Valley for the last eight years. Earlier he had an office in Manhattan, where he served residential clients.
        Mr. Prince was born in Grosse Pointe, Mich., and graduated from Brown University. He served in the Navy during the Korean conflict. After leaving the Navy, he began a career in advertising with McCann-Erickson Inc. Shortly thereafter, he switched to interior design.
        His marriage to the former Monique James ended in divorce.
        Besides his daughter, he is survived by a brother, Frederick, of Grosse Pointe.
