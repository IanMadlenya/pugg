
        Cornelius V. S. Roosevelt, a retired official of the Central Intelligence Agency, died Saturday at his home in Washington. He was 75 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, said his sister, Grace McMillan.
        Mr. Roosevelt, a grandson of President Theodore Roosevelt, was a graduate of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. After serving in the Navy in World War II, he joined the Central Intelligence Agency and, his sister said, retired from the agency in 1973.
        He was a collector of Japanese art and artifacts.
        In addition to his sister, Grace, of Glyndon, Md., he is survived by a brother, Theodore Roosevelt 3d of Northeast Harbor, Me.
