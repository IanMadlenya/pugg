        William Rion Bonham, comptroller for the law firm of Goldberg & Finmann, died of a heart attack Tuesday at Glen Cove (L.I.) Community Hospital. He was 65 years old and lived in Sea Cliff, L.I.
        Mr. Bonham, an accountant and a native of Greenville, S.C., had been associated with the Garden City firm for the last 10 years.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Joan Durbin; a son, William R., and a daughter, Elizabeth C., both of Sea Cliff; two stepchildren, James Nolan and Jeanne Lomsky, both of Sea Cliff, and a grandchild.
