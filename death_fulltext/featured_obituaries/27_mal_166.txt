        Alfred J. Cawse, a New York City Criminal Court judge from 1953 to 1972, died of a heart attack on Friday at the Berkshire Medical Center in Pittsfield, Mass. He was 80 years old and had lived in Staten Island until he moved to Pittsfield last year.
        Mr. Cawse was a native of Staten Island and a graduate of St. John's Law School. He was an assistant district attorney before his appointment to the bench. After his retirement, he worked in a private law practice until early last year.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Janet Decker; a son, James N., of Pittsfield; a daughter, Elaine Mikkelborg of Bend, Ore.; a sister, Ruth Shaw of New Smyrna Beach, Fla., and four grandchildren.
