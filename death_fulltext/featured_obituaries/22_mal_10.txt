        Clinton B. Axford, editor emeritus of The American Banker, a daily trade newspaper, died of a heart attack Sunday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Axford was associated with The American Banker from 1928 to 1962, when he retired.
        Mr. Axford was born in Rutherford, N.J., and he graduated from the Columbia School of Journalism in 1918. He was a member and past president of the New York State Society, Descendants of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence.
        There are no immediate survivors.
