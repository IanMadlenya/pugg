
        Dr. Lester S. Lipsitch, a former chief of staff of the American Hospital of Paris, died Oct. 17 at his home in Longboat Key, Fla. He was 80 years old.
        His death of a heart attack was reported yesterday by a daughter, Dr. Carol Lipsitch, of Montclair, N.J.
        Dr. Lipsitch was chief of staff at the American Hospital, in Neuilly-sur-Seine, from 1953 to 1981, when he retired and moved to Longboat Key.
        Before his work in Paris, Dr. Lipsitch was an associate clinical professor of medicine at Stanford University Hospital in San Francisco.
        In addition to his daughter Carol, he is survived by his wife of 56 years, Miriam; another daughter, Susan Butterworth of Boston; a son, Dr. Ian Lipsitch of Atlanta, and six grandchildren.
