        Dr. Thaddeus S. Danowski, a professor of research medicine at the University of Pittsburgh and an author of medical textbooks, died of a heart attack Saturday at Judson Memorial Hospital in Miami, Fla., where he had gone for an operation. He was 73 years old and lived in Pittsburgh.
        Dr. Danowski had been associated with the University of Pittsburgh for the last 40 years. He was also chief of endocrinology at Shadyside Hospital in Pittsburgh.
        He was born in Passaic, N.J., and was a graduate of Yale University in 1936 and of Yale Medical School, magna cum laude, in 1940. He was the author of 12 books and 400 articles on internal medicine.
