        Herbert J. Jacobi, a retired lawyer, died of prostate cancer yesterday at his home in Naples, Fla. He was 81 years old and also had a home in Mount Kisco, N.Y.
        Mr. Jacobi, a native of Manhattan and a graduate of Columbia College and the Columbia Law School, was a senior partner in the law firm of Wickes, Riddell, Bloomer, Jacobi & McGuire before it merged into Morgan, Lewis & Bockius a decade ago.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Carolyn M. Rienisch; a daughter, Barbara O'Reilly of Naples; two sons, H. John, of Rush, N.Y., and David G., of Carmel, N.Y., and a grandson.
