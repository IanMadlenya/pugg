        Richard Kneip, who was South Dakota's Governor for most of the 1970's, died today at McKennan Hospital of stomach cancer. He was 54 years old.
        Mr. Kneip, a Democrat, was Governor from 1971 to 1978, when he resigned to become United States Ambassador to Singapore. He returned to South Dakota in 1980. Later he was president of Nelson Laboratories in Sioux Falls.
        He ran unsuccessfully for the Democratic gubernatorial nomination in 1986.
        Mr. Kneip leaves his wife, Nancy, and their eight sons.
