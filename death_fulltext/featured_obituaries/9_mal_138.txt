        Leon Alcaly, a retired mathematics teacher at the Bronx High School of Science, died of complications resulting from leukemia Saturday at Mount Sinai Hospital. He was 78 years old and lived in the Bronx and West Palm Beach, Fla.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lillian Meltzer; two sons, Roger and Peter, both of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
