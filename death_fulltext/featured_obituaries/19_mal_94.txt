        Harold W. Schaefer, former president of the Eureka Company, the vacuum cleaner manufacturer, died Monday. He was 79 years old.
        When Mr. Schaefer worked for Majestic Radio, he invented a power supply system for the radio industry. He spent two years at the applied physics laboratory at Johns Hopkins University, then joined the Radio Corporation of America in 1943. The next year he became a division manager for the Westinghouse Electric Corporation.
        Mr. Schaefer was president of Eureka until he retired in 1980.
