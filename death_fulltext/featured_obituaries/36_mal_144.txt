        Lawrence Clayton Freer Jr., a business executive, died Saturday at Palm Beach Regional Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla., where he had lapsed into a coma after liver failure. He was 69 years old and lived in West Palm Beach.
        A 1941 graduate of Stanford University, Mr. Freer served with the Army Air Force in Europe in World War II. He later managed oil operations in the Middle East, and in the 1950's was manager of the Chase Manhattan Bank branch in Paris. After returning to the United States, he setled in West Palm Beach and worked in real estate.
        He is survived by his wife, Nancy; a son, Lawrence 3d of West Palm Beach; a daughter, Christian Freer-Parsons of Wilmington, N.C.; a sister and six stepbrothers.
