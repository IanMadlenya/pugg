        Israel Laster, former director of the New York Chapter of the American Jewish Committee, died of a heart attack Saturday at the Addison Gilbert Hospital in Gloucester, Mass. He was 72 years old and had homes in Rockport, Mass., and Manhattan.
        Mr. Laster, a native of Scranton, Pa., joined the American Jewish Committee in 1946 as a field worker. He was associate director of the New York Chapter from 1952 to 1962 and director from 1962 to 1968. He moved to the organization's national affairs staff, where he worked until he retired in 1979.
        He is survived by a sister, Hilda C. Laster of Rockport.
