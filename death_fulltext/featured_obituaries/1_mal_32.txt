        J. Philip Smith, a former vice president and director of Pfizer Inc., died Friday at his home in Boynton Beach, Fla., after a long illness. He was 72 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Margaret Bowen Meehan; two daughters, Patricia Boyle and Kathleen Colvin, both of Glens Falls, N.Y.; three sons, John, of Lisbon, N.Y., Gregory, of Laurel Springs, N.J., and Nelson, of Washington, and 12 grandchildren. His first wife, the former Patricia E. O'Connor, died in 1968.
        The funeral is to be held at 11 A.M. tomorrow at St. Michael's Roman Catholic Church in Greenwich, Conn.
