
        R. Timothy Leedy, the publisher and senior vice president of Financial World magazine, died on Saturday at his home in New Canaan, Conn. He was 56 years old.
        He died of cancer, a family spokesman said.
        He joined Financial World in early 1989 after serving as associate publisher of Manhattan Inc. magazine. He previously held senior advertising sales posts with Time Inc. magazines for 25 years.
        He was born in Youngstown, Ohio, and earned a bachelor's degree in business administration at the University of Michigan.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Lynn Allie; two daughters, Christine and Alexandra, and a son, R. Timothy Jr., all of New Canaan; a brother, David, of San Francisco, and a sister, Judy Jollay of Knoxville, Tenn.
