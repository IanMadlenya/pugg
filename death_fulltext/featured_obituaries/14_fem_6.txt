        Betty Throne-Holst, founder and president of the Swedish chapter of the African Medical and Research Foundation, died of intestinal cancer March 1 at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Throne-Holst, who spent years in Sweden, was a founder of the International Women's Club, Stockholm.
        Surviving are her mother, Rita Manning of Gunnison, Colo.; a son, Tiger, of Stockholm; two daughters, Ann Gerschman and Marika Bergman, both of Manhattan; a brother, Paul Manning, also of Gunnison; a sister, Gwen Barry of Palo Alto, Calif., and a grandson.
