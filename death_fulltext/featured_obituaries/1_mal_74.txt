        Joseph Cardinal Parecattil, retired Roman Catholic Archbishop of Ernakulam in southern India, died Friday in Cochin, the United News of India reported today. He was 74 years old.
        The news agency said the Cardinal had a heart attack several months ago and was admitted to a hospital Friday.
        Cardinal Parecattil became Archbishop of Ernakulam in Kerala State in 1957 and was named a Cardinal in 1969.
        He retired from official duties as Archbishop office in 1982, although he kept the title.
