        Dr. David Bernstein, a staff member of Maimonides Medical Center in Brooklyn for more than 40 years, died Friday at Maimonides after a short illness. He was 77 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Dr. Bernstein was director of otolaryngology in the hospital's department of surgery, as well as professor of otolaryngology at New York University Medical School.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; a daughter, Helen Young of Philadelphia; a son, Herbert, of Bellport, L.I.; two brothers, Archie, of Columbus, Miss., and Moe, of Margate, Fla., and three grandchildren.
