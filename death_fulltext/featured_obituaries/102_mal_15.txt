
        Benjamin S. Kalnick, a New York lawyer and accountant who served as Mayor of Kings Point, L.I., died on June 24 at New York University Medical Center. He was 84 and lived in retirement in Palm Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Kalnick was a former national vice president of the American Jewish Congress and a founder and chairman of the American Association for Jewish Education. He was Mayor and trustee of the Village of Kings Point, his former hometown, for more than 20 years.
        His survivors include his wife of nearly 60 years, Helene Rush Kalnick; a daughter, Sheila, of New York City; and a son, Michael, of Kings Point.
