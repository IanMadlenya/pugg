        Ben Alcock, retired executive vice president and director of creative services of Grey Advertising Inc., died Jan. 14 at Waterbury (Conn.) Hospital. He was 72 years old and lived in Heritage Village in Southbury, Conn.
        Mr. Alcock had worked for several other advertising agencies before joining Grey in 1957. He was elected executive vice president and director of creative services in 1964. He retired in 1970.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mildred Souza; a daughter, Jane Chelius of Brooklyn; a son, John, of Brooklyn, and two grandchildren.
