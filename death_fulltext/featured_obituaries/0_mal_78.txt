        The Rev. Dr. Wallace McPherson Alston, president emeritus of Agnes Scott College in Decatur, Ga., died Wedesday at his home in Lithonia, Ga. He was 80 year old.
        Dr. Alston, a Presbyterian minister, became president of the Presbyterian college for women in 1951. He served until his retirement in 1973.
        He was born in Decatur and was a graduate of Emory University, Columbia Theological Seminary in Decatur and Union Theological Seminary in Richmond, Va.
