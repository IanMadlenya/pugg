        Samuel Morrell, founder of Morrell & Company, the Wine Emporium Ltd., died of a heart attack Sunday at Mount Sinai Medical Center. He was 90 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Morrell founded the retail wine company in 1948. Originally at 326 East 49th Street, it was called 49th Street Wine and Spirits. In 1972 it moved to 307 East 53d Street under its present name and in 1986, the year Mr. Morrell retired, to 535 Madison Avenue, near 55th Street.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Charlotte Singer; a son, Peter, and a daughter, Roberta, both of Manhattan; and four grandchildren.
