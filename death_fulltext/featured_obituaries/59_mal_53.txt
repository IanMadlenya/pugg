
        William N. Kravitz, a partner in the New York law firm of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom, died on Friday at Mount Sinai Hospital in Manhattan. He was 45 years old and lived in Oyster Bay Cove, L.I.
        He died of cancer of the esophagus, said Peter P. Mullen, the firm's executive partner.
        Mr. Kravitz was born in the Bronx and graduated in 1971 from St. John's University law school. He joined the firm in 1976 and became a partner in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marilyn Piller; three young children: two sons, Eric and Jordan, and a daughter, Meredyth; and his parents, Louis and Grace Kravitz of Jackson Heights, Queens.
