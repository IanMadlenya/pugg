        Yosef Yehudai, the police chief of Jerusalem, died of a heart attack today, officials said. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. Yehudai was born in Rumania. He emigrated to Israel in 1961 and had served with the paramilitary border police and the regular police since he was 17.
        Appointed commander of the police forces in Jerusalem three years ago, he has dealt with frequent outbreaks of violence since the Palestinian uprising began in December 1987.
