        John H. Blair, the chief spokesman and lobbyist for Connecticut's insurance industry who was previously an aide to Gov. Ella T. Grasso, died of a heart attack today at John Dempsey Hospital here. He was 44 years old and lived in Farmington.
        Mr. Blair was president of the Insurance Association of Connecticut since 1982.
        Mr. Blair was a graduate of Boston College and received a master's degree in business administration from the University of Hartford.
        Before joining the insurance association in 1980, Mr. Blair had worked as an administrative aide to Governor Grasso.
        He is survived by his wife, Susan, and four children.
