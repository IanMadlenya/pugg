        David J. Fitzgibbons, retired president of Sterling Drug Inc., died Friday at Boca Raton (Fla.) Community Hospital. He was 82 years old and lived in Delray Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Fitzgibbons joined the Manhattan company in 1937 and became president in 1966. He retired in 1976.
        He was a 1928 graduate of Pace University.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Eleanor Kelly; two sons, David, of Greensboro, N.C., and Robert, of Scottsdale, Ariz.; two sisters, Estelle Carlson and Irene Laughton, both of Clearwater, Fla., and five grandchildren.
