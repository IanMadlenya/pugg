        Dashiell L. Madeira, a retired Navy rear admiral, died Nov. 29 at his home in Virginia Beach, Va. He was 90 years old.
        He was a native of Waterloo, Iowa, and a graduate of the United States Naval Academy. In World War II he led destroyer divisions. Retiring after 36 years of naval duty, he became a partner in Brown, Madeira & Company, a mutual fund specialist.
        Survivors include his wife, Elizabeth; a daughter, Anne Reich, and a brother, Aston Madeira.
