        Thomas W. Hudson Sr., the owner and publisher of Earnshaw Publications, a group of trade publications for children's products, died of a heart attack Tuesday at Mercy Hospital in Rockville Centre, L.I. He was 60 years old and lived in West Islip, L.I.
        Mr. Hudson joined the family company in 1947 and worked on its three publications, Small World, Earnshaw's and Plus Sizes.
        He is survivied by his wife, Edna; two sons, David, of Brightwaters, L.I., and Thomas Jr., of West Islip; a daughter, Elizabeth Ruocco of Babylon, L.I.; his mother, Beatrice, of West Islip, and six grandchildren.
