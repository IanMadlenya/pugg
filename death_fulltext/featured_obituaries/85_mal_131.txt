
        Robert I. Townsend Jr., an I.B.M. lawyer for 29 years, died on Monday at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, N.Y. A resident of Bedford, Mr. Townsend was 53.
        The cause was a heart attack, said his son Christopher.
        A graduate of Wesleyan University and the New York University Law School, Mr. Townsend spent his entire legal career at I.B.M. In recent years he was general counsel of the company's domestic operations.
        Mr. Townsend is survived by his wife, Wendy; his mother, Ethel Townsend of Purdys, N.Y.; a sister, Linda Curran of Convent Station, N.J.; two sons, Robert I. 3d, of Manhattan and Christopher, of Norwalk, Conn., and a daughter, Meredith Townsend of Manhattan.
