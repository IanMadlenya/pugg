        Vincent C. (Red) Reumann, a former alcohol advertising manager for Golf Digest, a magazine affiliate of The New York Times Company, died of bone cancer Monday at his home in Sun City Center, Fla. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Reumann, a former resident of Washington Township, N.J., joined Golf Digest in 1968 and retired in 1978. From 1944 to 1947, he was an advertising salesman for Esquire. He later served as alcohol advertising manager of both The Saturday Evening Post and Holiday magazine.
        He is survived by his wife, Marjorie, of Sun City Center; two sons, Richard, of Old Bridge, N.J., and Gary, of Palm Beach, Fla.; a daughter, Karen Sellers of Salisbury, N.C.; six grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
