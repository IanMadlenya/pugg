        Abraham Katz, a former vice president of David Crystal Inc., manufacturers of women's clothing, died of kidney and liver failure Wednesday at the Williamsburg Landing Health Center in Washington, Va. He was 88 years old and had lived in Freeport, L.I., for many years before moving to the Williamsburg Landing Retirement Center.
        Mr. Katz, who joined the company in the early 1960's, was in charge of the David Crystal suit division.
        He is survived by his wife, Elsie; a son, Col. Stephen Katz of Rancho Palos Verdes, Calif.; a daughter, Joan Wimbish of Virginia Beach, Va., and two grandchildren.
