
        John M. Zwach Sr., a Republican member of the House of Representatives from 1967 to 1975, died of lung cancer on Sunday at Redwood Falls Hospital. He was 83 years old and lived in Redwood Falls.
        Before entering Congress, Mr. Zwach was in state government for 32 years. In 1934 he was elected to the Minnesota House of Representatives. He served there until 1946, when he was elected to the State Senate, where he served for 20 years and was majority leader from 1959 to 1966.
        In 1967 he was elected to Congress from the Sixth District in southwestern Minnesota. He retired after four terms.
        Survivors include his wife, Agnes; three daughters, two sons and 19 grandchildren.
