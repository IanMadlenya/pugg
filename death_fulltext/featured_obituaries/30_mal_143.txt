        John C. Murphy, a lawyer and a vice president at Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, died of pneumonia yesterday at Community Hospital in Dobbs Ferry, N.Y. He was 60 years old and lived in Ardsley, N.Y.
        Mr. Murphy had previously been controller of the CIT Financial Corporation.
        He was an alumnus of the St. John's Law School.
        Surviving are his wife, Louise; two sons, Steven, of Peekskill, N.Y., and Paul, of Wilmington, Del.; three daughters, Jeanne Quagliano of Hartsdale, N.Y., Donna Fridholm of Rochester and Karen O'Connor of Yorktown Heights, N.Y., and six grandchildren.
