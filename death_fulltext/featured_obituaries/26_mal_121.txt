        Walter C. Pew, for 41 years a director of his family's business, the Sun Oil Company, died of cardiovascular disease Tuesday at the Bryn Mawr Hospital in Pennsylvania. He was 87 years old and lived in Bryn Mawr.
        He is survived by 2 daughters, Marim Hamilton, of Villanova, Pa., and Ann Curran, of Bryn Mawr; 12 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren.
