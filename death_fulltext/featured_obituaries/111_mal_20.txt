
        P. Stuart Tholan, 59, is a senior vice president of the Bechtel Corporation based in London and is president of the Bechtel unit overseeing Europe, Africa,  the Middle East and Southwest Asia.
        A Philadelphia native, Mr. Tholan has worked at Bechtel for 33 years. He oversees the company's work in rebuilding the Kuwaiti oil production facilities  after the Gulf War and was program manager for the Central Artery-Tunnel, a Massachusetts Highway Department project through the heart of Boston.
