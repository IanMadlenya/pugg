        Adele Scott Saul, the first woman in Philadelphia to run for Congress, died Dec. 6 at her former home in Rose Valley, Pa. She was 101 years old and lived in Key West, Fla.
        Mrs.Saul, a graduate of Smith College, was active for years in Democractic politics. She ran unsuccessfully for Congress in 1940.
        She was a former board member of the Woman's Medical College in Philadelphia and a former trustee of Bennington College.
        Surviving are her daughter, Barbara Sprogell Jacobson, of New Hope, Pa., and Manhattan; a sister, Mildred Scott Olmsted, of Rose Valley; six grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
