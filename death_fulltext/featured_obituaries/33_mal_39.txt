        Peter Rodney Lessler, a lawyer and senior vice president for planning and acquisitions at Dun & Bradstreet, died, apparently of a heart attack, on Wednesday at St. Clare's Hospital and Health Center in Manhattan. He was 41 years old and lived in Westport, Conn.
        An alumnus of the Columbia University Law School, Mr. Lessler joined Dun & Bradstreet in 1979 when the information services concern acquired National CSS, where he was assistant general counsel. He became vice president and general counsel of D&B Computing Services and in 1984 joined the corporate staff.
        He is survived by his wife, Karen, and two children, Adam and Amy, of Westport; and his parents, Stephen and Muriel Lessler; a brother, Robert, and a sister, Terri Green, all of Fairfield, Conn.
