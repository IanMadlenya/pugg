        Dr. Leo P. Reckford, a laryngologist, died of heart failure Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 85 years old.
        Born in Vienna, he graduated from the University of Vienna Medical School. He immigrated to Manhattan in 1939 and went into private practice, treating many actors and singers. He was a former associate clinical professor at the New York University Medical School.
        Surviving are his wife, Gene; a daughter, Jane Biba of Bernardsville, N.J.; a son, Edward, of Los Angeles, and two grandchildren.
