        McDill Boyd, a member of the Republican National Committee since 1965, died Friday of lung cancer. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Boyd, a newspaper publisher who served in numerous positions for the Republican Party in Kansas, underwent major lung surgery Dec. 29 at Wesley Medical Center, where he died, a hospital spokesman said. He was the longtime publisher of The Phillips County Review, a weekly newspaper in his hometown of Phillipsburg, and was campaign manager for Bob Dole, the Senate Republican leader, in his initial campaign for the Senate in 1962. Mr. Boyd was an unsuccessful candidate for the Republican nomination for Governor of Kansas in 1960 and 1964.
        He leaves his wife, Marie, and two daughters.
