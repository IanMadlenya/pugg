        Thelma Kineer Vint, an educator and former assistant dean of students at Hunter College, died Monday at the home of friends in Iselin, N.J., where she lived for the last two years. She was 90 years old.
        Miss Vint graduated from Hunter College in 1919, and she received a master's degree from Columbia University in 1929. She was associated with Hunter College for almost 40 years.
        There were no immediate survivors.
