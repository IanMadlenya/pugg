        Ralynn N. Stadler, a poet, writer and a trustee since 1977 of Westhcester County Community College, died yesterday at her home in Mamaroneck, N.Y. after a long illness. She was 64 years old.
        Mrs. Stadler attended the University of Miami and University of California at Los Angeles before becoming a feature editor at Mayfair magazine. She and her husband, Jack, were founders in the mid-1960's of Prism, a literary review, and were also co-founders this year of the Stadler Center for Poetry at Bucknell University.
        In addition to her husband, she is survived by a daughter, Anne Klass of Washington; a son, John, of Hanover, N.H.; two sisters, Elizabeth Rosenfeld of Larchmont, N.Y. and Bernice Newmark of West Palm Beach, and one grandson.
