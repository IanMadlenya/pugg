        Hugh Wolfe Frank, chief interpreter at the Nuremberg war crimes trials, was found dead Thursday, the police said. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Frank's body was found in a car filled with fumes. He was suffering from Parkinson's disease and was known to be depressed about his worsening health. An inquest was ordered.
