        Brother Bernard Peter May, a member of the De La Salle Christian Brothers for almost 70 years, died on Monday at the Riverview Medical Center in Red Bank, N.J. He was 85 years old and had lived at De La Salle Hall in Lincroft, N.J., since he retired in 1981.
        He died of a pulmonary embolism, officials at the residence said.
        Brother May joined the Brothers while in high school and took his first vows in 1923. He taught at schools in Manhattan, Brooklyn and elsewhere in New York State. He was principal of St. Cecilia's School in Brooklyn from 1938 to 1944, of St. Jerome's School in Manhattan from 1944 to 1947 and at the La Salle School in Albany from 1947 to 1950. He then served as supervisor at the La Salle Provincialate in Lincroft before retiring.
        He is survived by a sister, Catherine Missig of Grosse Pointe, Mich.
