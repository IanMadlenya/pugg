        Gerald Donovan, a lawyer in New York for six decades, died Thursday at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital Medical Center after a short illness. He was 96 years old and lived in New Rochelle.
        Mr. Donovan retired in 1976 from active practice as senior partner in the Manhattan firm of Sullivan, Donovan, Hanrahan & Silliere.
        Mr. Donovan is survived by three sons, Richard, of Pelham, N.Y., William, of White Plains, and Daniel G., of Greenwich, Conn.; a daughter, Geraldine Lenehan of Pelham; a sister, May McKenna of Highland Park, Ill., and 14 grandchildren.
