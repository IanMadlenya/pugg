        H. Kenneth Wangelin, a Federal judge who recently retired, died here Wednesday. He was 74 years old.
        Judge Wangelin was appointed to the bench of the Federal District Court for the Eastern District of Missouri by President Nixon in December 1970. He was named chief judge in 1979 and attained senior status in 1983.
