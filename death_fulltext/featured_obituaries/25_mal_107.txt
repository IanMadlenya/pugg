        Dr. Harold H. Abelson, retired dean of the School of Education at City College, died of heart failure on Feb. 6 at St. John's Hospital in Santa Monica, Calif. He was 84 years old and lived in Santa Monica.
        Dr. Abelson, a 1927 graduate of Teachers College, where he earned a Ph.D. in educational psychology, joined the City College faculty in 1928, became a professor in 1948 and was dean of the School of Education from 1952 to 1966. He also directed the educational clinic there from 1941 to 1952.
        He later was acting dean of teacher education for the City University of New York and until 1974 was a professor of education at Lehman College.
        He is survived by his wife, Lucie; a daughter, Jane Wildhorn of Culver City, Calif.; a son, Robert, of Boulder, Colo., and a sister, Cecile Benedict of Miami.
