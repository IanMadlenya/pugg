        Royal M. Howard, former chief of ophthalmology at Staten Island and Seaview Hospitals, died of coronary artery disease on Sunday at Staten Island Hospital. He was 67 years old and lived on Staten Island.
        Dr. Howard maintained a private practice on the island. He retired from Staten Island and Seaview Hospitals in 1975. During his career he was also a consultant to the United States Public Health Service Hospital, now Bayley-Seton Hospital, on Staten Island.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Muriel Russell; two sons, Steven and Davis, both of Staten Island; a brother, Dr. Robert, of Washington, and two grandchildren.
