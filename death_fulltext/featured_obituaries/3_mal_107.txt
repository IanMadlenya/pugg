        Quincy Doudna, president of Eastern Illinois University from 1956 to 1971, died Tuesday. He was 80 years old.
        In the 15 years that Dr. Doudna led the university, enrollment rose from 2,181 to 8,652. He directed a campus expansion that included construction of 34 buildings or additions valued at about $50 million.
        Dr. Doudna was an administrator at the University of Wisconsin at Stevens Point from 1945 to 1956.
