        Sidney Lightstone, an advertising executive, died last Wednesday at his home in Flushing, Queens. He was 94 years old. He died of cancer, his son John said. Mr. Lightstone began in advertising in Detroit with the J. L. Hudson Co. He became sales promotion manager for Gimbel Brothers in Philadelphia and headed his own business in Manhattan and in Chicago.
        He graduated from Columbia University.
        Surviving are two sons, John, of Queens and Richard, of Ambler, Pa.; a daughter, Sydney Schwartz of Queens; a sister, Betty Goodman of North Miami Beach, Fla; seven grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
