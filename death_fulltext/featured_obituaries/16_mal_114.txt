        Dr. Francis J. Griffith, a former assistant schools superintendent in New York City, died of heart failure Tuesday at St. Francis Hospital in Port Washington, L.I. He was 79 years old and lived in Port Washington.
        Dr. Griffith, who received a Ph.D. from Columbia University, served as principal at New Utrecht High School in Brooklyn and Richmond Hill High School in Queens. He was also a professor of educational administration at St. John's University.
        He is survived by his wife, Kathryn O'Donoghue Griffith; a sister, Agnes Finnigan of Lakewood, N.J., and a nephew, Dr. Neal Smith of Panama City.
