        Jerome Kidder, a retired judge of the New York City Criminal Court, died Thursday at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 79 years old and lived in Scarsdale.
        Judge Kidder was on the bench from 1968 until 1979, when he retired. He had previously been an assistant district attorney in Manhattan for 24 years until his appointment to Criminal Court.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Catherine Witherspoon; two daughters, Catherine Ware of Scarsdale and Ann Bickford of Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y.; a son, Charles, of Harrisburg, Pa.; two sisters, Marianne Kidder of the Bronx and Ann Rebecca Land of Huntington, W.Va., and four grandchildren.
