
       Antone (Tony) Pressler, the father of Senator Larry Pressler, Republican of South Dakata, died Saturday of Alzheimer's disease in a nursing home in Salem, S.D. He was 74 years old. Mr. Pressler, a farmer, spent his life in Humboldt, a town near Sioux Falls, where he and his wife, Loretta, reared their five children.
        Antone (Tony) Pressler, the father of Senator Larry Pressler, Republican of South Dakata, died Saturday of Alzheimer's disease in a nursing home in Salem, S.D. He was 74 years old. Mr. Pressler, a farmer, spent his life in Humboldt, a town near Sioux Falls, where he and his wife, Loretta, reared their five children.
        ''Tony was very low key,'' said a neighbor, Ed Lias. ''He spent most of this life right on the farm. He was a very good family man and a steward of the soil.''
