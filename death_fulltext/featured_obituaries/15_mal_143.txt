        A. Atwater Kent Jr., son of the radio manufacturer, died of leukemia April 16 at St. Mary's Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 79 years old and lived in Palm Beach; Newtown Square, Pa.; and Northeast Harbor, Me.
        Mr. Kent, born in Philadelphia, joined the family business, the Atwater Kent Manufacturing Company, which made radios. In the 1940's, it became an investment company, of which he was president at the time of death.
        He leaves his wife, Hope; four sons, A. Atwater 3d and Peter, both of Philadelphia, Christopher of Atlanta and E. Hewlett of Palm Beach; two daughters, Suzanne Hitchcock of Manhattan and Allison de los Reyes of Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic; a sister, Elizabeth Van Allen, of Philadelphia and two grandchildren.
