
        William S. Masland, former chief executive of C. H. Masland & Sons, a manufacturer of automotive products, died Thursday at the Hershey Medical Center in Hershey, Pa. He was 70 years old and lived in Carlisle, Pa.
        The cause of death was not immediately available.
        Mr. Masland, a graduate of Yale University, joined the company, founded by his great-grandfather, in 1945. He was president and chief executive from 1972 until his retirement in 1985. He was a trustee of Dickinson College and was chairman of its board for 11 years.
        He is survived by his wife, Kathryn; three daughters, Jennie Masland of Boston, Penny Smith of Harleysville, Pa., and Maeve Shank of Carlisle; a son, William Jr. of Carlisle; two brothers, Robert of Needham, Mass., and Edward of Carlisle; a sister, Elizabeth M. Griffith of Carlisle; and six grandchildren.
