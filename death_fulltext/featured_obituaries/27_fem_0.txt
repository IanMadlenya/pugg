        Dr. Dorothy L. Keur, a retired professor of anthropology at Hunter College, died March 22 at St. Vincent's Hospital in Billings, Mont. She was 85 years old and lived in the Riverdale section of the Bronx.
        In recent years, she and her husband have been working on a historic excavation in New Mexico.
        She is survived by her husband, John.
