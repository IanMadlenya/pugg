        Harold A. Webster, a past president of the National Electrical Contractors Association and also of its New York chapter, died on Sunday at Good Samaritan Hospital in West Islip, L.I. He was 86 years old and lived in West Islip.
        Mr. Webster, who was born in White Plains and attended Pratt Institute, owned the T. Frederick Jackson electrical contracting concern in Long Island City, Queens, before he retired in the mid-1960's.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ruth Ochs; two sons, Arvon, of Bay Shore, L.I., and Robert, of Babylon, L.I.; 10 grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren.
