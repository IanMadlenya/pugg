        Margaret G. Bachner, a retired lawyer who represented the Jewish Board of Guardians in children's cases in the 1950's and 1960's in Manhattan, died on Monday in Palm Beach, Fla., after a long illness. She was 88 years old.
        Mrs. Bachner, a graduate of St. Lawrence University, and her husband, Lester, a Manhattan lawyer who died in 1976, were avid tournament bridge players and won the National Mixed Team Championship in 1932. She was a trustee of Louise Wise Services and the Wiltwyck School.
        She is survived by a son, Robert, of Manhattan, and a granddaughter.
