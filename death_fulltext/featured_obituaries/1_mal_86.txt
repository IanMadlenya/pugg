        Ike Vern, a freelance magazine photographer and documentary film maker, died Monday after heart surgery at the Texas Heart Institute of St. Luke's Hospital in Houston. He was 70 years old and lived in Orlando, Fla. He was a former resident of Manhattan.
        Mr. Vern worked for Look, Life and Holiday magazines. He was a founding member and former president of the American Society of Magazine Photographers.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Harriet Nutels; a daughter, Jane Vern, of Manhattan, adn a brother, David, of Brooklyn.
