        William H. Napier, a Manhattan toy company executive, died Saturday at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. He had had heart surgery. He was 62 years old and lived in Westfield, N.J.
        Mr. Napier, a native of Brooklyn, was a merchandising executive with Games Gang Ltd., in which he was a partner. He was a founder of the company in July 1986.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Mildred De Feo; three sons, William Jr., of Manhattan, Steven, of Westfield, and Terrence, of Oak Park, Ill.; two daughters, Patricia Foster of Jersey City and Elizabeth, of Manhattan.
