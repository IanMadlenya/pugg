        Joel J. Shorin, retired president of a chewing gum company that includes baseball cards with its product, died last Sunday at the New York University Medical Center. He was 65 years old and lived in Kings Point, L.I.
        Mr. Shorin was president of Topps Chewing Gum Inc. from 1963 until he retired in 1986.
        Surviving are his wife, Ann Maria; a son, Jeffrey, of Kings Point; two daughters, Carol Desforges and Lorraine, both of Providence, R.I.; a brother, Edward, of Manhasset, L.I., and a sister, Irma Barness of Doylestown, Pa.
