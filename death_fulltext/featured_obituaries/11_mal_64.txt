        Dr. Robert Landesman, who retired this year as clinical professor of obstetrics and gynecology at the Cornell University Medical School, died Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 71 years old.
        He was a native of New York City. He graduated from the Cornell Medical School in 1930. During World War II, he served as a flight surgeon in the Pacific with the rank of major.
        Surviving are two sons, Peter, of Manhattan, and Paul, of White Plains; a daughter, Lucy Halperin of Scarsdale, N.Y., and two grandsons.
