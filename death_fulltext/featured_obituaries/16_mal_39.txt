        Robert F. Sage, a Michigan philanthropist, died April 29 at St. Joseph Mercy Hospital in Ann Arbor at the age of 72.
        Mr. Sage was president and chairman of the 34-year-old Sage Foundation, created by his father, who was co-founder of Tecumseh Products, a large refrigerator manufacturer. The foundation's focus is mainly in education and health He leaves his widow, the former Genevieve Phillips of Tecumseh; two daughters, Melissa Sage Booth of Flossmoor, Ill., and Anne Sage Price of Tucson, Ariz., and a sister, Margaret Mudgett of San Diego.
