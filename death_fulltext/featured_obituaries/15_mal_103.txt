        Joseph J. Schwab, a science professor at the University of Chicago for nearly 40 years, died of cardiac arrest Wednesday at his home in Lancaster, Pa. He was 79 years old.
        Dr. Schwab, a native of Columbus, Miss., received bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees from Chicago, where he taught natural sciences from 1934 until 1973. He wrote widely on the philosophy and teaching of science.
        He is survived by a daughter, Jill McGill Pogorah of Lancaster.
