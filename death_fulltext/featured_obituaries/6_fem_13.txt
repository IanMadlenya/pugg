        Jean Bullowa Reavey, a playwright, died Monday at Northern Dutchess Hospital in Rhinebeck, N.Y. She was 70 years old.
        Ms. Reavey, who lived in Woodstock, N.Y., wrote a number of plays and librettos, including ''Poised for Violence,'' ''Telephone Pole,'' ''Mercy Me'' and ''Who Stole the American Crown Jewels?''
        She is survived by her sister, Anne Feldman, of Mongaup Valley, N.Y. A memorial service is planned on Aug. 9 in Woodstock.
