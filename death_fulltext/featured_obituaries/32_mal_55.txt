        Dr. Lewis H. Rohrbaugh, an administrator of the Boston University Medical Center from 1962 to 1973, died yesterday at the Penobscot Bay Medical Center in Maine of complications from a hip fracture. He was 81 years old and lived in Rockport, Me.
        Dr. Rohrbaugh had been an administrator for the Federal Government, serving in the Agriculture and State departments and in overseas posts including Cairo, Athens, Baghdad and Lima. He had also been a vice president and provost of the University of Arkansas.
