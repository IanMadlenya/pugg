        The Rev. W. Ralph Ward Jr., a retired bishop of the United Methodist Church and the chaplain at Drew University in Madison, N.J., since 1980, died Tuesday at his home in Convent Station, N.J., after a long illness. He was 79 years old.
        Bishop Ward, who recently wrote a history of Methodism, is survived by his wife, Arleen; three sons, Ralph, of Huntington Beach, Calif., David, of Chester Township, N.J., and Gerald, of Portsmouth, N.H.; two brothers, Robert, of Kansas City, Mo., and James, of Cibolo, Tex.; a sister, Elizabeth Zirkel of San Antonio, and six grandchildren.
