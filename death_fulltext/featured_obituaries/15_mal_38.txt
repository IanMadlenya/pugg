        Jeff Veazey, a dancer and choreographer, died of AIDS Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 33 years old.
        Mr. Veazey danced in the Broadway companies of ''Sugar Babies,'' ''Sophisticated Ladies,'' ''The Grand Tour'' and ''Dr. Jazz,'' and in many regional productions. He choreographed several Off Broadway shows, including ''Vampire Lesbians of Sodom'' and ''Psycho Beach Party,'' as well as the ''Broadway Tonight'' revues.
        He is survived by his mother, June Veazey, of New Orleans, three brothers and a sister.
