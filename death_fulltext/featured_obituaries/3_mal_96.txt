        Charles M. Brinckerhoff, a former president, board chairman and chief executive officer of the Anaconda Company, died of pneumonia yesterday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Brinckerhoff was born in Minneapolis and grew up in Elmhurst, Queens. He earned a bachelor's degree from Columbia College and a graduate degree in metallurgical engineering from the university.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Florence Andreen; a daughter, Carol B. Kietzman of Sands Point, L.I., and three grandchildren.
