        F. W. Dietmar Schaefer, a senior lawyer with the Mobil Corporation for 27 years, died Monday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan after a long illness. He was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Schaefer, a 1957 graduate of Yale Law School, was born in Germany and served as a civilian interpreter for the United States Army in 1945 and 1946. He was an editor with the Armed Forces Radio Service and Radio Free Berlin until 1950. He joined Mobil in 1960.
        He is survived by his wife, Renate, and a sister, Dr. Martha Rinke of Bonn.
