        John Van Buren, the former dean of Hofstra University's School of Education, died of liver cancer Wednesday at Mercy Hospital in Rockville Centre, L.I. He was 57 and lived in Hempstead, L.I.
        Dr. Van Buren joined Hofstra in 1963. He was dean of the School of Education from 1974 to 1983. At the time of his death, he was coordinator of the school's Counselor Education Program.
        Born in Monticello, Ga., Dr. Van Buren graduated from Morehouse College and received a master's degree from Atlanta University and a doctorate from Boston University.
        He was a member of the Hempstead Board of Education, a trustee of the village of Hempstead and president of the Nassau County Legal Aid Society.
        Surviving are his wife, Frances; a daughter, Carol, of Hempstead, and a son, Gregory, of Boston.
