        Thomas Bassett Hubbard, chairman and chief executive of the Thinc. Consulting Group International, an executive placement concern, died at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center after a brief illness on Sept. 5. He was 65 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Hubbard founded his company in 1969. He sold it earlier this year to Right Management Consultants of Philadelphia.
        He is survived by a son, Richard B., of Santa Barbara, Calif.; two daughters, Kerri B. H. Hart of Enumclaw, Wash., and Amory H. Millard of Manhattan; two brothers, Sam, of Delray, Fla., and Bill, of Bethlehem, Pa., and two sisters, Harriet Woodhull of Little Compton, R.I., and Mary Alling of Lakeville, Conn.
