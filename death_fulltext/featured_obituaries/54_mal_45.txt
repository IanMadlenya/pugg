
        Bert S. Harmon, a lawyer who practiced in New York for more than 50 years, died on Thursday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 85 years old and also lived in Pompano Beach, Fla.
        The cause of his death was not immediately known, his family said.
        Mr. Harmon was a graduate of Yale and of the Harvard Law School. He was a founder of the Broward County (Fla.) Public Library Foundation.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Belle Kirschner; a daughter, Dr. Carol Lane of Manhattan; a son, James, of Weston, Conn., who is chairman of Wertheim Schroder & Company, the Manhatan investment firm, and five grandchlldren.
