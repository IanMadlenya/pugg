        Fred L. Heyes, a former vice president of F. Eberstadt & Company, a Manhattan brokerage firm, died on Wednesday at his home in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 70 years old.
        Mr. Heyes died of cancer, said his brother-in-law, Grover O'Neill Jr. Mr. Heyes joined F. Eberstadt & Company in 1980 and remained with it until he retired in 1986. He had also been a senior vice president with Hornblower and Weeks-Hemphill Noyes Inc.
        Mr. Heyes was born in New Bedford, Mass., and graduated from Williams College and the Harvard Business School.
        He served in the Navy during World War II in the North Atlantic and the Central and South Pacific theaters.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Catharine O'Neill; two sons, Fred L. 3d, of Orange, Mass., and Edward, of Neenah, Wis., and four grandchildren.
