        Wendell S. Merick, a retired correspondent for U.S. News & World Report, died of chronic liver disease Thursday at his home in Bethesda, Md.. He was 60 years old.  Mr. Merick was a correspondent with the United Press, now United Press International. He also worked for The Boston Traveler. He joined U.S. News & World Report in 1966 and retired in 1983.
        He is survived by his wife, Anne Morrissy Merick; his mother, Catherine Walsh of Vancouver, British Columbia; a sister, Maria Teresa McCullough, of Vancouver, and a daughter, Katherine-Anne, of Bethesda.
