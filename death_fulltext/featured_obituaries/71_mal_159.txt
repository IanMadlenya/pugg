
        William Pierce Archer Sr., a former antiques dealer, died Dec. 9 at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 82 and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Archer died of a stroke and a heart attack, said his wife of 52 years, Eugenia.
        Mr. Archer was a former president and chairman of the Troy Shirt Makers Guild and for more than 20 years was a partner with his wife in Pierce Archer Antiques of Bronxville.
        In addition to his wife, he is survived by two daughters, Kathryn Sapio of Nesconset, L.I., and Ellen Larson of Pelham Manor, N.Y.; two sons, William Jr. of Coconut Grove, Fla., and John, of New Canaan, Conn., 15 grandchildren and a great-grandson.
