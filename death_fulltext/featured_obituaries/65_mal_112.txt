
        George J. McGuirk, a Ford dealer in Bronxville, N.Y., died yesterday at his home in Bronxville. He was 72 years old.
        He died of kidney cancer, his family said.
        Mr. McGuirk was at Smith-Cairns Ford for 42 years, for the last 31 as the owner. A graduate of the University of Massachusetts in Amherst, he served as a naval aviator from 1942 to 1946, reaching the rank of lieutenant (j.g.).
        Mr. McGuirk is survived by his wife, the former Helen Aspden; three sons, Scott, of Pelham, N.Y., Bruce, of Manhattan, and Dwight, of Stamford, Conn.; four daughters, Cheryl Haverinen, of Glens Falls, N.Y., Rosemary Salvatore, of New Rochelle, N.Y., and Melanie and Amy McGuirk, both of Bronxville; a sister, Dorothy Kelly, of Port Charlotte, Fla., and five grandchildren.
