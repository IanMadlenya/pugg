        John H. Bryan, co-founder of the Bryan Packing Company, now Bryan Foods, died Monday at Clay County Medical Center here. He was 80 years old and lived in West Point.
        Mr. Bryan founded the Bryan Packing Company in 1936 with his brother, the late W. B. Bryan. Now a subsidiary of the bi>Sara Lee Corporation, Bryan Foods employs more than 2,000 people. Mr. Bryan retired as chairman of the company in the mid-1970's.
        He is survived by his wife, Catherine; two sons, John Jr. of Chicago and George of Memphis; two daughters, Kitty Dill and Catherine Harrell of West Point, 19 grandchildren and 2 great-granchildren.
