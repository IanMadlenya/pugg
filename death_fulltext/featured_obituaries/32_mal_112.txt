        Harald Thelander, a former translator at the United Nations, died of cancer yesterday at Christ Hospital in Jersey City. He was 80 years old and lived in Jersey City.
        Mr. Thelander was a Swedish translator at the United Nations from 1945 to 1969. He had worked for the United States Department of War from 1938 to 1945.
        He is survived by his brother, Claes, of Sweden.
        His companion was Scot S. Sicamut of Jersey City.
