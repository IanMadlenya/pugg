        Bishop Robert Louis Hodapp, a former bishop of Belize and a Roman Catholic missionary in that country for nearly 50 years, died on Thursday. He was 79 years old.
        He began his ministry in British Honduras, later Belize, in 1936 as a Latin and English teacher. He was consecrated bishop of Belize, on Central America's Caribbean coast, in 1958 and he served in that capacity until his retirement in 1984.
