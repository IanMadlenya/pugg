        Albert Kindler, a real-estate broker and an investor, died of congestive heart failure on Wednesday at Tisch Hospital. He was 77 years old and lived in White Plains.
        Mr. Kindler was in the real-estate business in the Bronx and Manhattan for 55 years. He was a member of the Bronx Real Estate Board and the Bronx Chamber of Commerce for many years. He was also a past president of the Bronx Rotary Club and a former director of the Northside Savings Bank in the Bronx.
        Mr. Kindler graduated from the Horace Mann School.
        Surviving are his wife, Ruth; a son, Peter; a sister, Alice, and three grandchildren, all of White Plains.
