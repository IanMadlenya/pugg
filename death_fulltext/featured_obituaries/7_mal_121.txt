        Milton Beards, a retired importer of women's scarves and other fashion accessories, died Monday of a bacterial infection at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 81 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Beards retired around 1970 as a partner of Baar & Beards, a company specializing in women's fashion accessories. Since then, he had been a volunteer in the Small Business Administration's Service Corps of Retired Executives (Score).
        He is survived by a son, Richard, of Kirkwood, Pa.; a daughter, Dorothy Beards of Belfast, Me., and three grandsons.
