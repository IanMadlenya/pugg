        Dr. Theodore Karl Himelstein, an orthopedic surgeon and an educator in Manhattan for 50 years, died of a heart attack on Tuesday at Good Samaritan Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 83 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Himelstein was a graduate of St. Andrews University in Edinburgh. He was affiliated with Metropolitan Hospital and was a professor of orthopedic surgery at New York Medical College for most of his career.He was also affiliated with Flower Fifth Avenue Hospital and was in private practice.
        Dr. Himelstein's wife Ruth died in 1970. He is survived by two daughters, Ellen, of Nashua, N.H., and Alice, of Venice, Calif.; two sisters, Goldie Kessler of Highland Park, N.J., and Ethel Kramer of Kendall Park, N.J. His companion was Stella Fishback of Manhattan.
