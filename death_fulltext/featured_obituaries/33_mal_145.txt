        Gilbert J. Sinnott, a retired insurance executive, died of heart failure Saturday at his home in Naples, Fla. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Sinnott, a native of Brooklyn, began his insurance career in 1925 and was associated for several decades with Marsh & McLennan Inc., from which he retired in 1972 as a vice president. A son, John T., is president of Marsh & McLennan Worldwide.
        In addition to his son, who is a resident of Pound Ridge, N.Y., he is survived by his wife, Esther; another son, Gilbert, of Ridgefield, Conn.; three daughters, Anne Strole of Davidsburg, Mich., Mary Michels of Greenwich, Conn., and Cynthia Angliss of Alfaretta, Ga.; 20 grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
