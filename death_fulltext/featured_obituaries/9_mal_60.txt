
       Robert Price, the American who led a local group that bought General Motors South African after G.M.'s divestment, died of a heart attack Saturday. He was 61 years old. Mr. Price, who joined General Motors Corporation in 1951, retired last year as president of G.M.'s Motors Trading Corporation subsidiary.
