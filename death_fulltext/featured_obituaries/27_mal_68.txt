        Mario Chiari, an art director and costume designer, died Sunday in a Rome hospital after a long illness. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Chiari was born July 14, 1909, in Florence and trained to be an architect. He started out in the theater and moved to the film industry in 1940. He worked on many Italian and foreign films and on television productions.
        Among his films were ''Miracle in Milan,'' ''I Vitelloni,'' ''War and Peace,'' ''The Bible'' and ''Barabbas.''
