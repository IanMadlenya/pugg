        Dr. Samuel Lewis Cohen, a Manhattan internist, died of heart failure Monday at Doctors Hospital. He was 91 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Cohen, a native New Yorker, was medical director at Medical Arts Center Hospital, a private hospital at 57 West 57th Street, from 1944 to 1984. He was associated with Doctors Hospital from 1976 to 1988. From 1939 to 1959, he was also medical director of Jewish Memorial Hospital. In addition, he maintained a private practice on the West Side.
        Surviving is his wife, the former Marie Jones.
