        David McGee, a mime and actor who taught movement at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City, died on Feb. 2. He was 43 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Associates said the cause of death was complications from Burkitt's cancer.
        Mr. McGee appeared in ''Mummenschanz'' on Broadway and in other productions at the Lincoln Center Festival, the New Jersey Shakespeare Festival and the Morse Mime Theater.
        He is survived by his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Roger McGee of Gorham, Maine.
