        Lewis George King, the president of LCP Chemicals and Plastics in Edison, N.J., died of cancer last Friday in the M. D. Anderson Hospital in Houston. He was 58 years old and lived in Colts Neck, N.J.
        Mr. King had previously been president of the Occidental Chemical Corporation and a vice president of Exxon Chemical, U.S.A., both in Houston.
        He is survived by his wife, Joan; five daughters, Michele K. Wyatt of San Francisco, and Barbara J. King, Judith E. Schiro, Mary S. King and Joan E. King, all of Houston, and two sons, Lewis, of San Antonio, and Gregory, of Houston.
