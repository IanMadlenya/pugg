        S. M. (Mike) Michaelson, the co-founder and president of Mike Michaelson Associates, fur merchandisers in Manhattan, died of heart disease Friday at Winthrop-University Hospital in Mineola, L.I. He was 66 years old and lived in Albertson, L.I.
        Mr. Michaelson grew up in the Bronx and Toronto and was a graduate of City College. He was an Army sergeant in World War II and was wounded in the Battle of the Bulge. In 1987, he was voted the man of the year by a fur industry trade group.
        Surviving are his wife, Laura, vice president and secretary-treasurer of the fur company; a daughter, Cathy, of Manhattan; two sons, Robert, of Manhattan, and Michael, of Washington, and a sister, Helen Seider of North Miami Beach, Fla.
