        Walter H. Miller, chairman of the Richmond Hill Savings Bank and a former chairman of King Kullen supermarkets, died of a stroke Tuesday at his home in Garden City, L.I. He was 55 years old.
        Mr. Miller joined King Kullen in 1963 and rose to the post of chairman in 1974. He left in 1986 to become president of Richmond Hill, and last year he became chairman as well.
        He is survived by his wife, the former MaryAnn Cullen; three daughters, Mary Kathleen, Kerry and Kelly; six sons, Michael, Matthew, Timothy, Patrick, Sean and Cullen, and a brother, Thomas Miller of Baltimore.
