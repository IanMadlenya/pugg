        Helen Hallet Kennedy, a civic leader in Bedford, N.Y., died Sunday at her daughter's home in Bedford after a brief illness. She was 80 years old.
        Mrs. Kennedy, who was born in Minneapolis, was a member of the Bedford Town Board for three terms in the 1960's and was a former member of the board of trustees of Northern Westchester Hospital.
        She is survived by a son, Laurence Jr., of St. Simon's Island, Ga.; two daughters, Helen Clarke of Atlanta and Katherine Gutelius of Mount Kisco, N.Y.; 21 grandchildren and 8 great-grandchildren.
