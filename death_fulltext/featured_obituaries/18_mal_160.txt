        John G. Edwards, a tea importer and businessman, died of a heart attack Tuesday at Little Falls (N.Y.) General Hospital. He was 51 years old and lived in Smithtown, L.I.
        Mr. Edwards, who was educated in his native England, was chairman and chief executive officer of Redco Foods Inc., one of the largest tea companies in North America. Before creating Redco in 1985, he was president of Brooke Bond Foods, a British tea company he had joined in 1961.
        He is survived by his wife, Collette, of Smithtown; and three sons, Jonathan, of Smithtown; Mark, of Hampton Bays, L.I., and Andrew, of Chester, England.
