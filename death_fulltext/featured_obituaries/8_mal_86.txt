        T. Michael Reed, a dancer in such Broadway musicals as ''Seesaw'' and ''The Magic Show,'' died of complications from acquired immune deficiency syndrome Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 42 years old.
        During the last decade, Mr. Reed served as dance supervisor on two long-running Broadway musicals, ''Cats'' and ''A Chorus Line,'' and assisted the director and choreographer Michael Bennett on ''Ballroom.''
        Mr. Reed is survived by his mother, Mary Gianoni of Arcata, Calif.; a sister, Michelle Reed Hadley of Las Vegas, Nev., and a brother, James Reed of San Francisco.
