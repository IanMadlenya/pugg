        Mark D. Soroko, a retired senior vice president and board member of Revlon Inc., died of heart failure Saturday at St. Francis Hospital in Miami. He was 87 years old and lived in Bal Harbor, Fla.
        Mr. Soroko joined Revlon in 1934, less than two years after its founding. After he retired in 1978, he was a consultant to the company.
        He is survived by his wife, Rose; four daughters, Doris, of Barrytown, N.Y., Pearl Levine and Susan Soroko Purdy, both of Manhattan, and Jacqueline Siles of Yorktown Heights, N.Y., and seven grandchildren.
