
        State Senator Jack Doland, who was head football coach and then president of McNeese State University here before entering politics, died of prostate cancer on Thursday. Mr. Doland, a Democrat, was 63 years old.
        He was an assistant football coach at Louisiana State University from 1965 until 1970, when he became head coach at McNeese. His record there was 64-32-3. He became president of McNeese State in 1979 and left eight years later to run for the State Senate.
