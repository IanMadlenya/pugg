        Martin C. Rothstein, a certified public accountant who was active in liberal Democratic politics, died of a heart attack Friday at Bellevue Hospital Center. He was 49 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Rothstein was treasurer of the New Democratic Coalition and former executive vice president of the Chelsea Reform Democratic Club.
        He is survived by a daughter, Lisa, of Manhattan, and his mother, Bess, and his brother, Jerome P., both of Jacksonville.
