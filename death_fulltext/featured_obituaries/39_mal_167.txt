        Robert Harris, a pianist who taught at The Juilliard School for 24 years, died of a stroke on Tuesday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 74 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Harris, who was born in Waco, Tex., studied at Baylor University and with noted piano teachers before establishing his own studio in New York. He joined the Juilliard faculty in 1966.
        He is survived by a sister, Jonneta Bonner of Houston, and a brother, Coleridge, of Fairfield, Calif.
