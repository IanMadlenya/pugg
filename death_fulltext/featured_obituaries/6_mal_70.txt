        John Cronin, an interior designer, died of cardiac arrest Monday at his home in North Salem, N.Y. He was 54 years old.
        Mr. Cronin, who also lived in Manhattan, was a founder and partner of Cronin-Stempler Designs Inc., an interior decorating firm with a prestigious clientele that includes former President and Mrs. Richard M. Nixon.
        He was raised in Riverdale in the Bronx and was a graduate of the Parsons School of Design.
        He is survived by his mother, Lillian M. Cronin of Manhattan.
