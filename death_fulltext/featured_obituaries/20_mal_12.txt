        Thomas Gallin, president of the John Gallin Construction Company of Manhattan, died at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital on Thursday, after a brief illness. He was 58 years old and lived in Larchmont, N.Y.
        Mr. Gallin worked for the family-owned company since 1956 and became president in 1976.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Nel Collins; three daughters, Connie, of Port Chester, N.Y., Maureen, of Larchmont and Ann-Marie Theodoropoulos of Athens; five sons, Michael, of New Rochelle, Christopher, of Patterson, N.Y., Joseph, of San Diego, and Thomas and Robert, both of Larchmont; three sisters, Mary Varian of Larchmont, Eleanor Cummings of San Diego and Sister Alice Gallin of Hyattsville, Md., and one grandchild.
