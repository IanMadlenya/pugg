
        Robert J. Dunphy, an editor and travel writer for The New York Times for 25 years, died on Thursday at his home in Woodstock, N.Y. He was 76.
        The cause was cancer, his wife, Marie, said.
        Besides his wife, Mr. Dunphy is survived by a sister, Verna Fay Dellheim of Manhattan, a stepson, John Longyear of Manhattan, and a stepgrandson.
