        Dang Viet Chau, a former Deputy Prime Minister of Vietnam, died of a brain hemorrhage on Monday in Hanoi, the official Hanoi radio said. No age was given.
        Mr. Chau died at the Vietnam-U.S.S.R. Friendship Hospital, said the broadcast, a text of which was seen in Bangkok today.
        The radio said he held the post of Deputy Prime Minister in Republic of Vietnam, which was established after North Vietnam defeated South Vietnam in April 1975. He was Finance Minister of North Vietnam in the waning years of the war.
