
        Lawrence Nirenstein, a partner in the Manhattan law firm of Chadbourne & Parke, died on Saturday at his home in Great Neck, L.I. He was 60 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. Nirenstein, a specialist in tax law, was a native of Brooklyn, a graduate of Cornell University and the Cornell Law School and held a Master of Law degree in taxation from New York University.
        He joined Chadbourne & Parke in 1988 after a career with two other firms. From 1969 to 1971, he was chairman of the committee on interstate taxation of the New York State Bar Association.
        Mr. Nirenstein is survived by his wife of 33 years, the former Bernice Gersten; a son, Alexander, and a daughter, Meredith, both of Great Neck.
