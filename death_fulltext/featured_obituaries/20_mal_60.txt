        Ralph Henry Schust, a retired vice president of Sunshine Biscuits, died of complications from Alzheimer's disease Sept 5 at the Sunnyside Nursing Home in Sarasota, Fla. He was 84 years old and lived in Sarasota.
        Mr. Schust was born in Saginaw, Mich., and joined Sunshine in 1930, after it purchased his family's baking company. He later became a director and executive vice president of sales for the company. He retired in 1965.
        Surviving are his wife, Julianna; three daughters, Penelope Merrill of Mendham, N.J., Deborah Harding of Cold Spring, N.Y., and Antonia Zegras of Westport, Conn.; a sister, Katherine McKenna of Longboat Key, Fla., and eight grandchildren.
