
        Norman W. Zaubler, a real-estate developer, died on Sunday at Mount Sinai Hospital in Miami Beach. He was 86 years old and lived in Miami Beach.
        Mr. Zaubler died of congestive heart failure, his daughter, Jolie Kelter of Manhattan, said.
        Mr. Zaubler, who built apartment houses throughout the Northeast for many years, retired in 1980. He was born in Brooklyn and graduated from City College and Fordham Law School.
        In addition to his daughter, he is survived by his wife, the former Hope Reineke; a son, Leland, of the Riverdale section of the Bronx; two sisters, Ruth Rothman and Bea Altman, both of Miami Beach, and five grandchildren.
