        Daniel Burman, an eye surgeon for more than 50 years, died Wednesday at his home in Southfield, Mich., after a long illness. He was 79 years old.
        Dr. Burman was associated with several hospitals, including Manhattan Eye & Ear in Manhattan and Share Zedek in Israel. He also wrote a book, ''The Aging Eye.''
        He is survived by his wife, the former Beatrice Lookstein; a brother, Dr. Louis R., of Miami Beach, Fla.; a sister, Dorothy Strong of Manhattan; a son, Dr. Matthew Burman of Southfield; two daughters, Danby Meital amd Danielle Krause, both of Jerusalem, and nine grandchildren.
