        Dr. David W. Mullins, president of the University of Arkansas for 14 years, died Tuesday at the age of 81.
        Dr. Mullins, who began his education career in the Depression as a small-town high school teacher, was president of the university from 1960 to 1974. In that time, the institution grew dramatically, adding three campuses.
