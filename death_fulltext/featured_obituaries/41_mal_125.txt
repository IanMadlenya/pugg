        J. P. Thibault Bouet, a fashion designer, died on Thursday at his home in Palm Beach, Fla. He was 42 years old and also lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Bouet died of complications from AIDS, a spokesman for the family said.
        Mr. Bouet began his career in the atelier of Yves Saint-Laurent, working in Paris and Tokyo. He came here in 1976 and worked for Christian Dior and later became head designer of Saint-Laurent's North and South American divisions.
        Then he founded his own couture business in Manhattan.
        He was born in Casablanca, Morocco.
        He is survived by his mother, Conception Bouet of Aix-en-Provence, France. His companion was Richard T. York of Manhattan.
