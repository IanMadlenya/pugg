
       Jorge Cruickshank Garcia, a Congressman who was co-founder and leader for two decades of the Popular Socialist Party, died of a brain tumor Tuesday. He was 73 years old. With Vicente Lombardo Toledano, Mr. Cruickshank helped found the party in 1948 and had been its secretary general since 1968.
        Jorge Cruickshank Garcia, a Congressman who was co-founder and leader for two decades of the Popular Socialist Party, died of a brain tumor Tuesday. He was 73 years old. With Vicente Lombardo Toledano, Mr. Cruickshank helped found the party in 1948 and had been its secretary general since 1968.
        Mr. Cruickshank was serving his fourth term in Congress, representing Mexico City's Federal District. He was a Senator from 1976 to 1982.
