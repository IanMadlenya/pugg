        Dr. Frank Moore Jr., a retired surgeon, died of lung cancer on Wednesday at Huntington (L.I.) Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Huntington.
        Dr. Moore had a private practice as a general surgeon and was associated with the hospital from 1950 to 1976. He then worked in the emergency room, until retiring in 1980.
        Dr Moore was born in Brooklyn and attended the United States Naval Academy and Hamilton College. He received a medical degree from the Long Island College of Medicine in 1942 and was a Navy lieutenant in World War II.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Elizabeth Newkom; a son, Maj. Frank Moore 3d, of Majuro, Marshall Islands; a daughter, Michele, of Atlanta, and a brother, John, of Pompano Beach, Fla.
