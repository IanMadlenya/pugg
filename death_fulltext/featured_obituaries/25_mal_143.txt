        Dr. Daniel G. Butera, a retired radiologist, died of leukemia Monday at his home in Bayside, Queens. He was 79 years old.
        Dr. Butera was a radiologist in private practice in Brooklyn for almost 50 years. He graduated from New York University in 1930, from the Long Island College of Medicine in 1933 and became a fellow of the American Board of Radiology in 1956.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Agnes Connolly; a son, John, of Orlando, Fla.; two daughters, Deanna Cassin of Manhattan and Dr. Janet Keefe of Brooklyn, and four grandchildren.
