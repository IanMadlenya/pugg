        Dr. Marvin Klein, a pediatric neurologist, died of cancer yesterday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 52 years old and lived in Roslyn, L.I.
        Born in Jersey City, Dr. Klein graduated from Princeton University and Syracuse University Medical School. He was chief of the department of pediatric neurology at Long Island Jewish Hospital for 14 years and had been in private practice in Great Neck, L.I., for the last three years.
        Surviving are his wife, Susan; two sons, Josh and Jason, both of Roslyn, and a brother, Howard, of Edison, N.J.
