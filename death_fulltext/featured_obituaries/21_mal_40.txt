        Robert Buchakian, a retailer who founded the Treasure Island stores, died of a brain tumor Tuesday at his home in Ridgewood, N.J. He was 52 years old.
        Mr. Buchakian, a 1957 graduate of Colgate University, founded Treasure Island Inc., a chain of stores selling patio furniture, Christmas decorations and art supplies, in 1970. At his death, he was chairman and owner of the chain.
        He is survived by his wife, Catherine; a stepdaughter, Carolyn Wallace; his mother, Hermine, of Mahwah, N.J.; and a sister, Grace Pinajian, of Wyckoff, N.J.
