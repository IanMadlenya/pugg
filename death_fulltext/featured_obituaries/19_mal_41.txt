        Edward Waldman, a retired president of Citicorp Factors, a commercial money lender, died of Parkinson's disease yesterday at his home in Westport, Conn. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Waldman, a native New Yorker, was associated with Citicorp Factors, a subsidiary of Citicorp, for 14 years before retiring in 1974. He was a graduate of New York University.
        Mr. Waldman is survived by his wife, the former Edythe Saperstone; a son, Richard, of Pasadena, Calif., and a grandchild.
