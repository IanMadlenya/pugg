        William Joseph McKeon, a former marketing professor at New York University's School of Business, died yesterday at his home in New Milford, N.J., after a long illness. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. McKeon was former vice chairman of the school's marketing department and acting director of its Institute of Retail Management. He later became president of Professional Marketing Associates, a consulting firm. He retired in 1974.  He is survived by his wife, the former Augusta Cingolani; two daughters, Nancy, of Washington, and Patricia Byrne of Ridgewood, N.J.; a son, William J. Jr., of New Milford, and two grandchildren.
