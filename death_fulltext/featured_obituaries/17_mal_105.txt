
       Marvin Roth, a former men's clothing manufacturer, died of heart failure Friday at his home in Palm Beach, Fla. He was 77 years old. Mr. Roth, who graduated from the Wharton School of Finance at the University of Pennsylvania in 1932, was the former president of Rosenmond-Shiers in Manhattan.
