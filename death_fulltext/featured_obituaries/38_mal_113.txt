        Louis Montelione, a retired New York City administrative law judge, died of colon cancer on Saturday at his home in Massapequa, L.I. He was 71 years old.
        Mr. Montelione was appointed to the city's Workers Compensation Board in 1982 and worked until three weeks before his death. From 1949 to 1959 he was in the office of the New York City Corporation Counsel and was in private law practice for the preceding 24 years.
        He was born in Brooklyn and graduated from City College and Brooklyn Law School.
        He is survived by eight children and 13 grandchildren.
