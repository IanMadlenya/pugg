
        George L. Childs Sr., an executive with Citibank for 42 years, died on Thursday at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 93 years old and lived in Bronxville.
        Mr. Childs was born in Havana and was a graduate of the New York Military Academy and Lehigh University. At his retirement in 1963 he was an assistant vice president in charge of Caribbean operations for Citibank. From 1965 until 1984 he served as a representative in the United States for Banco Nacional de Descuento in Caracas, Venezuela.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elvira Marie Auletta; a son, George Jr., of Bronxville, and three grandsons.
