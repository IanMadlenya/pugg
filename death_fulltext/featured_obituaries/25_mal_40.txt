        George F. Zahn, a retired maritime company executive, died Wednesday of lymphatic cancer at his home in Staten Island. He was 88 years old.
        Mr. Zahn retired in 1958 as vice president and treasurer of the Brewer Dry Dock Company of Staten Island. He was also president of the New York and New Jersey Dry Dock Association and president of the Maritime Association of the Port of New York and New Jersey.
        He is survived by three daughters, Lois Aimes of Mechanicsburg, Pa.; Marcella Reilley of Winchester, Va.; and Barbara Dondero of Staten Island.
