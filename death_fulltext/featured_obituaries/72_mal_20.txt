
        Richard W. Bandfield, the president of Bandfield Associates, a financial planning company, died on Friday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 65 and lived in Port Washington, L.I.
        He died of cancer, said Patricia Drivanos, a business partner.
        He began his career with the Home Life Insurance Company and later became a chartered life underwriter for several major insurance companies, including Home Life and New England Life. Mr. Bandfield worked for Shearson/American Express and Prudential-Bache Securities in the early 1980's. He established his own company in 1986.
        Mr. Bandfield is survived by his wife, Chesta; a daughter, Diana, of Port Washington; a son, David, of Oradell, N.J., and two grandchildren.
