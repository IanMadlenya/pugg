        David Rollin Du Val, a securities analyst with the Citicorp Capital Markets Group in London and formerly a vice president of E. F. Hutton & Company in Manhattan, died Tuesday in a rafting accident on the Dadu River in Sichuan Province, China. He was 38 years old and lived in London.
        Mr. Du Val was on his honeymoon with his bride, Fiona Lamont Du Val, when the accident occurred.
        Also surviving are his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Clive Du Val 2d; two brothers, Clive Du Val 3d and Daniel, and a sister, Susan Phipps, all of McLean, Va.
