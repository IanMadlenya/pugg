        Prof. Robert M. Garrels, a prominent geologist, died of cancer of the colon on March 8 at his home in St. Petersburg, Fla. He was 71 years old.
        Mr. Garrels was a professor at Northwestern University, Harvard University, the Scripps Institution of Oceanography and, at the time of death, the University of South Florida.
        Born in Detroit, he received a bachelor's degree from the University of Michigan and a doctorate from Northwestern.
        Survivors include his wife, Cynthia; two daughters, Joan Beitin of Dobbs Ferry, N.Y., and Katherine Merrick of Tucson, Ariz.; a son, James of Westfield, N.J.; a brother, John Jr. of Stuart, Fla.; a sister, Helen Murray of Grosse Ile, Mich., and 13 grandchildren.
