        H. Fletcher Eggert Jr., a retired insurance executive, died Wednesday at St. Luke's Hospital in Jacksonville, Fla., after a brief illness. He was 74 years old and lived in Ponte Vedra Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Eggert, a 1936 graduate of Williams College, was the former president of the Trans Atlantic Reinsurance Company in Manhattan, a former vice president of the Marsh & McLennan Insurance Company and a former vice president of the Signature Company.
        For many years, he headed the race committe of the Riverside Yacht Club. In World War II, he was a captain and served as a photograph interpreter with the Army Air Corps in Europe.
        He is survived by his wife, Shirley; a daughter, Pamela Epstein of Orlando, Fla.; a son, John, of Claremont, Calif.; a sister, Mary Hotchkiss of Sanibel Island, Fla., and a grandson.
