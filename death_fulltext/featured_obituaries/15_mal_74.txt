        Edwin B. Katte, a retired vice president and trust officer of the Manufacturers Hanover Trust Company, died of cancer Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Katte joined the bank in 1935. He retired from Manufacturers Hanover in 1978.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Merrill; a sister, Elizabeth Harris of California; a daughter, Sandra Roak of Milton, Mass.; a son, Edwin Jr., of Plymouth, Mass., and four grandchildren.
