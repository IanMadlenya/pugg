        Robert H. Schmid, a retired advertising executive, died of lung cancer Friday at his Manhattan apartment. He was 76 years old and also lived in Palm Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Schmid was a vice president and director of promotions for Mutual Broadcasting System from 1940 to 1955. He worked for a number of advertising and public relations agencies, including Young & Rubicam. In 1958, he bought radio station WESC in Greenville, S.C., one of the first to adopt a country-and-western format.
        He is survived by a daughter, Heidi Powers of Manhattan, and a brother, W. Kent Schmid of Mystic, Conn.
