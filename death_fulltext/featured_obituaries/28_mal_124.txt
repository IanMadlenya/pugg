        Dr. Paul Hecht, a psychiatrist and educator, died of heart disease Monday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan. He was 79 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Hecht, a graduate of New York Medical College, was a former staff psychiatrist at the Manhattan Psychiatric Center and had been assistant clinical professor of psychiatry at the Columbia University College of Physicians.
        He is survived by two sons, Mark, of Philadelphia, and David, of Manhattan; a brother, E. Lawrence, of Coconut Creek, Fla.; a sister, Anne Friedel of Delray Beach, Fla., and one grandchild.
