        Hubert F. Carr, a lawyer and steamship company executive, died of heart disease Wednesday at River View Medical Center in Red Bank, N.J. He was 68 years old and lived in Colts Neck, N.J.
        Mr. Carr for many years was secretary, vice president and general counsel of Moore-McCormack shipping company, posts he retained when the company was acquired by United States Lines in 1983.
        Mr. Carr, a native of Pittsburgh, was a graduate of New York University and Brooklyn Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Barbara Madory; two sons, Peter, of Montclair, N.J., and John, of River Vale, N.J.; a brother, Peter, of Mendham, N.J.; two sisters, Kathleen Couvrette of Florham Park, N.J., and Dorothy Sherwood of Bedford, Mass., and four grandchildren.
