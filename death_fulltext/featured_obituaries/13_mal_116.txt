        John Mackenzie Jr., an investment banker, died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 70 years old.
        Mr. Mackenzie, who graduated from Yale College and Yale Law School, was a vice president with the investment banking firm of Stone & Webster. He retired in 1966.
        He is survived by two daughters, Sidney Fulop of Manhattan and Mary Seaton of Middleburg, Va.; two stepsons, Charles T. Schulze of Stockbridge, Mass., and Peter B. Schulze of Monterey, Mass., and seven grandchildren.
