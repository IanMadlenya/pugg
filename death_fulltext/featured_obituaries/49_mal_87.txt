
        Dr. Abraham H. Fineman, an allergy specialist, died on Monday at Bon Secours Hospital/Villa Maria Nursing Center in North Miami, Fla. He was 94 years old and lived in Miami Beach.
        Dr. Fineman was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of Columbia University's College of Physicians and Surgeons. He was on the pediatric allergy staff of Mount Sinai Hospital for many years and founded the allergy department at Sydenham Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Gutstein; two sons, Dr. Lewis Fineman of Hollywood, Fla., and Stephen Fineman of Bronxville, N.Y.; a sister, Helen Katz of Forest Hills, Queens, and five grandchildren.
        Correction:  February 15, 1991, Friday
        An obituary yesterday about Dr. Abraham H. Fineman, a retired allergy specialist, omitted two survivors. They are his brothers Sidney and Myron, both of Miami Beach.
