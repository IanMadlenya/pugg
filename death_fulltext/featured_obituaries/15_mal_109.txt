        Dr. Ira Teicher, director of surgical education at Long Island Jewish Medical Center, died of a heart attack yesterday at the center in New Hyde Park, L.I. He was 76 years old and lived in Kings Point, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Phyllis Korn; a son, James, of Guttenberg, N.J.; a daughter, Carolyn Lesser of Madison, Wis.; and two brothers, Howard, of Woodmere, L.I., and Milton of Manhattan.
