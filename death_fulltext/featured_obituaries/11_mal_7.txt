        Peter F. F. Kissel of Miami Beach, whose colt Pass Catcher won the Belmont Stakes in 1971, died Saturday at the Miami Heart Institute. He was 61 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, Phyllis; three sons, Peter F. F. Jr., Frank and John; a daughter, Trudy Marckwald; a brother, W. Thorn, and a sister, Abbey Starr.
        A memorial service is scheduled for Monday in Gladstone, N.J.
