
        W. Frederick Laird, former chairman and chief executive officer of the Columbia Gas System, died of cancer Sept. 15 at Christiana Hospital in Newark, Del. Mr. Laird, who lived in Greenville, Del., was 79.
        Born in London, Ohio, Mr. Laird graduated from Capital University in Columbus, Ohio, in 1941 and from Columbia University Law School in 1943. After service in the Army, he joined a Columbus law firm in 1946, then was hired by Columbia in 1951.
        Mr. Laird, who served as chairman and chief executive from 1980 until his mandatory retirement at age 65 in 1984, is survived by his wife of 55 years, Jane; two sons, Frederick, of Plainfield, N.J., and John, of New York, and four grandchildren.
