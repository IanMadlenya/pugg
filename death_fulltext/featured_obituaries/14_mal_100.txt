        Dr. Leo E. Mindel, a dentist who practiced for 50 years in New Brunswick, N.J., died of lung cancer Sunday at his home in Highland Park, N.J. He was 78 years old.
        Dr. Mindel was born in Brooklyn. He graduated from Rutgers University and obtained his dental degree from the Harvard University School of Dental Medicine.
        Surviving are his wife, Edith; a son, Lee, of Manhattan, and three daughters, Helaine Mindel-Menkin of Somerset, N.J., and Meg Mindel-Marchese and Sunny, both of Manhattan.
