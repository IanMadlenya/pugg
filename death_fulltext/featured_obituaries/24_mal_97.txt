        Imre Cholnoky, founder and president of Sinjon Inc., a real-estate development corporation, died of cancer yesterday at his home in Greenwich, Conn. He was 68 years old and also had homes in Stratton Mountain, Vt., and Scottsdale, Ariz.
        Mr. Cholnoky, a native of Szatmarnemeti, Hungary, founded the Greenwich-based company about 40 years ago.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy G. Montague; a brother, Thomas, of Greenwich; two sons, I. John, of Darien, Conn., and Peter M., of Manhattan; a stepson, Thomas Shaw of Melbourne, Fla., and two grandchildren.
