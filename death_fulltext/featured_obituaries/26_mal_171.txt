        Timothy Meyers, an actor who received a Tony Award nomination for his performance as a tough teen-ager in the 1972 Broadway musical ''Grease,'' died of acquired immune deficiency syndrome on March 14 in Cabrini Hospital. He was 43 years old.
        Mr. Meyers also acted Off Broadway and in regional theater companies in Hartford, New Orleans, Seattle, St. Louis and, last summer, in the New Jersey Shakespeare Festival.
        He is survived by two brothers, Chris, of New Orleans, and Peter, of Jackson, Miss.
