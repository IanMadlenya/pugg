        Harold Clifford Hoffman, a retired banker and former president of the New Jersey Historical Sites Council, died of cancer Friday at his home in Elizabeth, N.J. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Hoffman, who retired as a trust officer for First Fidelity Bank in Millburn, N.J., in 1977, was also a former chairman of the New Jersey Historical Trust. He was active with the New Jersey Bankers Association, the New Jersey Investment Council, the Knights of Columbus, the Alexian Brothers Hospital Foundation and St. Elizabeth Hospital.
        He is survived by his sister, Marion, of Elizabeth.
