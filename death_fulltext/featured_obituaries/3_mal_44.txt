        Herbert A. Gold, the founder and president of Jayme Products, a developer and supplier of premiums and promotional merchandise to major marketing companies, died Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Gold was a native of Jersey City and a 1927 graduate of Harvard College.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Judith Cibel; two daughters, Jane Hans of Newton, Mass. and Amy Brett-Fitzpatrick of Wilton, Conn., and three grandchildren.
