        Friedrich Solmsen, a retired professor of classics, died of a perforated ulcer Jan. 30 at North Carolina Memorial Hospital in Chapel Hill. He was 84 years old and lived in Chapel Hill.
        Educated in Germany, Dr. Solmsen taught at Olivet College from 1937 to 1940 before moving to Cornell University, where he became chairman of the classics department. In 1962 he was named the Moses Slaughter Professor of Classical Studies at the University of Wisconsin at Madison. He retired in 1974.
        He is survived by his wife, Lieselotte, and a sister, Edith Ehlers, of Lima, Peru.
