        Edwin W. Ebel, a former vice president of the General Foods Corporation, died Monday at a nursing home in Chappaqua, N.Y. He was 86 years old and lived in Chappaqua.
        He was born in Brooklyn. Mr. Ebel served as chairman of the Advertising Council from 1965 to 1967, and as chairman of the Association of National Advertisers.
        He is survived by his wife, Anne; a brother, Jay, of Chatham, Mass; a daughter, Dina Monaghan, of Raymond, Calif., a son, Peter, of Plainville, Conn., seven grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
