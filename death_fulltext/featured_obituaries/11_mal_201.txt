        Rabbi Hershel Matt, a professor at the Reconstructionist Rabbinical College in Philadelphia and at the Academy of Jewish Learning in New York City, died of a heart attack Saturday at his home in Highland Park, N.J. He was 65 years old.
        Survivors include his wife, Gustine; a daughter, Deborah Erdfarb of Highland Park; and three sons, Rabbi Jonathan, of Israel, C. David, of Fairfield, Iowa, and Daniel, of Berkeley, Calif.
