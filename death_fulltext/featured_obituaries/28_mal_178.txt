        Joseph Pink, a business executive and a former trustee of the Textile Workers Pension Fund, died of congestive heart failure yesterday at Englewood (N.J.) Hospital. He was 81 years old and lived in Paterson, N.J.
        He is survived by his wife, Rosalind; a son, Andrew, of Washington Township, N.J.; a daughter, Debra Aronson of Englewood; a sister, Anna Malowitz of Brooklyn, and four grandchildren.
