
       Michael Kory, a former radio and phonograph manufacturing executive and later a member of the American Stock Exchange, died of a heart attack Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 60 years old. Mr. Kory held various executive posts in the 1950's and 1960's for the Emerson Radio and Phonograph Corporation, rising to vice president for sales and president for distribution.
