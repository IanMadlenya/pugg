        Hugh J. O'Rourke, an immigration and real estate lawyer, died of cancer Thursday at Southampton Hospital on Long Island. He was 82 years old and a resident of Manhattan and Westhampton, L.I.
        A former New York City assistant corporation counsel, Mr. O'Rourke was also associated for many years with the law firm of O'Dwyer & Bernstein.
        A native of County Leitrim, Ireland, Mr. O'Rourke graduated from Fordham Law School in 1934.
        He is survived by four sons, Hugh C., of Westchester, Patrick, of Nutley, N.J., John, of Westfield, N.J., and Thomas, of Manhattan; three daughters, Breffni Medcalf and Mary Alice O'Rourke, both of Manhattan, and Jeanne O'Rourke, of Westhampton; a brother, Charles, of Manhattan, and 10 grandchildren.
