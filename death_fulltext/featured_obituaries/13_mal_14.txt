        Julius Joseph Jr., a partner in the Franklin & Joseph advertising agency in White Plains, died of heart failure Jan. 21 at the Veterans' Administration Nursing Home in the Bronx. He was 79 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Joseph was a master sergeant in the Army and served in China and India from 1942 to 1945.
        Surviving are a daughter, Lynn Berge of Bay Shore, L.I.; a brother, Richard, of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
