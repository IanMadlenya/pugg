        Preston Browne O'Sullivan, a retired senior vice president of the Lincoln Savings Bank, died yesterday at Long Island College Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Mr. O'Sullivan, a graduate of the University of Vermont, retired in 1970 but continued to work as a real-estate and financial consultant to the Roman Catholic Diocese of Brooklyn and Queens. He was chairman of Holy Family Hospital in Brooklyn.
        He is survived by his wife, Frances; two sons, George, of Manhattan, and Jeremiah, of Albuquerque, N.M., and two grandsons.
