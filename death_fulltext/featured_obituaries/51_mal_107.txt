
        Thomas A. Thompson, president of Thompson Associates in Manhattan, on Friday at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. He was 59 years old and lived in Ardsley, N.Y.
        He died of cancer, a spokesman for the family said.
        He started his company in 1987. It produces reports for companies seeking Food and Drug Administration approval of new drugs and revises technical books. For the preceding 24 years he had been senior staff scientist and administrator with the Ciba-Geigy Corporation.
        Mr. Thompson graduated from Liberty State College in Liberty, W.Va., and received a chemistry degree and a Ph.D. from the University of Pittsburgh.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Aurora Pagan; a daughter, Maritza, of Ardsley, and a sister, Doris Lutz of Washington Court House, Ohio.
