        The Rev. Paul V. Callahan, a Jesuit priest and a teacher of classical languages for 33 years, died on Wednesday at St. Ignatius Loyola Residence in Manhattan, where he lived. He was 68 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, a spokesman for the Jesuits said.
        Father Callahan was ordained in 1954 and taught classical languages at Regis High School in Manhattan for 25 years.
        He is survived by a sister, Elizabeth Maxson of the Bronx.
