        Harold Field Horne Jr., former president of the Field Home, a senior-care center in Peekskill, N.Y., died of lung cancer Friday at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was 72 years old and lived in Somers, N.Y.
        Mr. Horne was born in Mohegan Lake, N.Y., and graduated from New York University. From 1939 to 1978, when he retired, Mr. Horne was an industrial sales manager of the Toledo Scale Company. The Field Home was established by his uncle and is now the Field Home-Holy Comforter. Mr. Horne was a former president of the Westchester Sons of the American Revolution.
        Surviving are his wife, Dorothy, and a son, Field, of Saratoga Springs, N.Y.
