        Mark Michtom, a former executive of the Ideal Toy Company, died of bladder cancer Saturday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Medical Center. He was 59 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Michtom was executive vice president of the company when it was sold to CBS in 1982. He was chairman of the board of the McBurney Y.M.C.A., and a graduate of Columbia University, He earned a master's degree in music from Oxford University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Paula Paster, and his mother, Hadassah Michtom, of Manhattan.
