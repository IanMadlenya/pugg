        Dr. Timothy R. Talbot Jr., who founded the Fox Chase Cancer Center in Philadelphia, died of cancer there Monday. He was 72 years old and lived in Haverford, Pa.
        Dr. Talbot joined the Fox Chase Institute for Cancer Research as director in 1957. The center joined with American Oncologic Hospital to form the Fox Chase Cancer Center in 1974, and he served as president until 1980.
        Dr. Talbot is survived by his wife, Mary; three sons, two daughters and seven grandchildren.
