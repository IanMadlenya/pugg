        Raphael Marchand, an accountant and a retired bank officer, died of cardiovascular disease on Tuesday at Mount Vernon (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 95 years old and lived in Mount Vernon.
        Mr. Marchand graduated from New York University in 1920 and became a certified public accountant in 1935. He worked for the City of New York and was the chief accountant for Mount Vernon from 1958 to 1967, when he retired.
        He joined the board of the American Savings and Loan Association in Manhattan in 1935 and was eventually named chairman. He had also retired from that post.
        There are no immediate survivors.
