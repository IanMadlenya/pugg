        Egil Aarvik, head of the Norwegian Nobel Committee, which awards the Nobel Peace Prize, died on Thursday, the news agency N.T.B. said today. He was 77 years old. The cause of death was not reported.
        Mr. Aarvik, a member of the Christian People's Party, served briefly as Social Affairs Minister in the 1960's.
