        Dr. Nestor John Totero, a psychatrist and surgeon, died of a heart attack Thursday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan. He was 81 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Totero received his medical training at New York Medical College. He was an attending surgeon at Fordham Hospital and Union Hospital in the Bronx before going into private practice in Manhattan. At age 58, he became a psychiatrist and was associated with Roosevelt Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, Lee; a daughter, Carla, and one grandchild.
