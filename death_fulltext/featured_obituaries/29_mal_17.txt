        Stuart E. Jacobson, a writer of books on gift-giving, died Saturday at his home in Dallas. He was 34 years old.
        Mr. Jacobson died of chronic pneumonia, said his mother, Ruth Jacobson.
        Mr. Jacobson wrote ''Only the Best: A Celebration of Gift Giving in America,'' and a sequel, ''The Art of Giving,'' both published by Harry N. Abrams Inc. Previously, he had been a professional model.
        Born in Dallas, he graduated from Wesleyan University. In addition to his mother, he is survived by his father, Dr. Coleman Jacobson, and a sister, Kyle Theirl, of Dallas.
