        Robert L. Turner, general manager of Avery Fisher Hall and Alice Tully Hall, died on Monday. He was 50 years old.
        Mr. Turner died of cancer, according to a family spokesman.
        Born in Irvine, Ky., Mr. Turner received degrees in music from Stetson University in De Land, Fla., and from Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge.
        He went to work for Lincoln Center in 1963, first as head usher in what was then called Philharmonic Hall, and became general manager in 1971.
        He is survived by his second wife, Heidi Upton Turner, a pianist, and four children and six grandchildren.
