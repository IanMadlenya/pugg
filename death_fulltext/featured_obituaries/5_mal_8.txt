        Thomas J. Reghanti, who retired from the Fruehauf Corporation in 1986 as president and chief executive officer, died here last Thursday. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Reghanti joined the Detroit-based manufacturer of truck trailers in 1958. After joining the company as assistant general manager of used trailers, he rose through its ranks and was appointed president in 1981.
