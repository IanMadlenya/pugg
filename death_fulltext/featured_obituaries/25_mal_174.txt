        Ted Warmbold, editor of The San Antonio Light, died of meningitis today at Humana Metropolitan Hospital. He was 45 years old.
        Mr. Warmbold had been the chief news executive at The Light since June 1981.
        A native of St. Louis, Mr. Warmbold received his degree from the University of Missouri School of Journalism.
        In 1977, he became executive editor of The Los Angeles Herald Examiner. The Hearst Corporationnamed him executive editor at The San Antonio Light in 1981. He was promoted to editor in 1986.
        He is survived by his wife, Carolyn; his mother; three sisters, and two brothers.
