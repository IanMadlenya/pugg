        Dominic John Dell'Aquila, a retired physician, died Sunday at the Franklin D. Roosevelt Veterans Hospital in Montrose, N.Y. after a series of strokes. He was 83 years old and lived in New Rochelle, N.Y.
        Dr. Dell'Aquila was born in Manhattan and graduated from the University of Michigan Medical School. From 1931 to 1978 he practiced obstetrics and gynecology in New Rochelle.
        Surviving are a daughter, Felicity, of New Rochelle; a son, Don, of Fairfield, Conn.; a brother, Anthony, of Scarsdale, N.Y.; two sisters, Rose Rastante of Philadelphia and Aida Ortolani of Oakland, N.J., and four grandchildren.
