
        Julia Albrecht Apple, the granddaughter of the founder of an Ohio grocery store chain and the mother of R. W. Apple Jr., the associate editor of The New York Times, died on Sunday in Akron, Ohio. She was 92.
        Mrs. Apple's husband, R. W. Apple, was an executive with that family company, which now operates the Acme supermarkets in northeast Ohio. He died in 1992. Mrs. Apple graduated from Wittenberg University in Springfield, Ohio, and served for many years on the Women's Board of Akron City Hospital.
        Besides her son, Mrs. Apple is survived by a daughter, Barbara A. Pittman of Rockford, Ill.; a sister, Barbara A. Minnig of Akron; two grandchildren and five stepgrandchildren.
