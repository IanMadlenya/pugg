        Bill Page, an art director and designer, died of coronary arrest at his home in Manhattan on Thursday after suffering from neurological problems. He was 60 years old.
        Mr. Page was the editorial art director at Look magazine for five years in the 1960's. He began his career in 1955 as art director of Nugget magazine and also worked with several advertising companies, including Ted Barash of Manhattan.
        Born in Mankato, Minn., Mr. Page attended the Minneapolis College of Art & Design and studied illustration with Norman Rockwell.
        He is survived by his mother, Lucy, a son, Justin, both of Manhattan, and a daughter, Rebecca, of Cold Spring, N.J.
