        Abraham Taub, a retired chemistry professor, died of pnuemonia on Tuesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. He was 88 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Professor Taub joined the faculty of the College of Pharmaceutical Sciences of the City of New York, in 1922. He became chairman of the chemistry department in 1948 and was given emeritus status when he retired to become a consultant in 1969. The college, an affiliate of Columbia University, graduated its last class in 1976.
        Professor Taub was a graduate of Columbia University.
        There are no immediate survivors.
