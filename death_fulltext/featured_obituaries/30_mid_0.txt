        Carole Lisbeth Parker, the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Maclyn Taite Parker of Fort Wayne, Ind., and Aspen, Colo., was married yesterday to Dan William Reicher at the Pine Creek Cookhouse in Ashcroft, Colo., a ghost town in the mountains near Aspen. The Rev. Gregg R. Anderson, a Methodist minister, performed the nondenominational ceremony. Mr. Reicher is a son of Dr. and Mrs. Norbert B. Reicher of Syracuse and Cazenovia, N.Y.
        Ms. Parker, who will keep her name, is the editor in chief of Environmental Forum, a policy magazine published by the Environmental Law Institute in Washington. Her father is a senior partner in the Indianapolis law firm of Baker & Daniels.
        Mr. Reicher, a graduate of Dartmouth College and Stanford University Law School, is a senior project attorney in the Washington office of the Natural Resources Defense Council, an environmental law concern in New York. His father, who is retired, was a clinical professor of obstetrics and gynecology at the State University of New York Upstate Medical Center in Syracuse.
