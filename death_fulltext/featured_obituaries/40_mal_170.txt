        Joseph R. Villano, an interior decorator and the owner of Aquarius Design, a shop in Wycoff, N.J., died on Sunday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center. He was 50 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A family member said Mr. Villano had been suffering from cancer.
        In addition to Aquarius Design, Mr. Villano was the owner of two flower shops in Wycoff, L'Eglise and Le Jardin. His work as a decorator was featured in Architectural Digest, House and Garden, and Garden State Homes.
        He was born in Paterson, N.J., and graduated from the University of Kansas, Pan-American Art School and the New York School of Interior Design.
        He is survived by his mother, Susan, of Paterson, N.J.; two sisters, Grace Barr of Cinnaminson, N.J., and Joan McNamara of Wycoff, and a brother, Ralph, of Vernon, N.J.
