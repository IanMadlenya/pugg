        Peter Francis Kelly, a former company controller in New York, died of cancer Monday at the home of his daughter in Fort Collins, Colo. He was 58 years old and lived in Lake Wales, Fla.
        Mr. Kelly was controller of the Mohasco Corporation, a maker of carpets and furniture in Amsterdam, N.Y., and the Chester Cable Corporation, a cable-television company in Chester, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, Maureen, of Sparta, N.J.; a daughter, Ellen Kelly of Fort Collins, Colo., and a son, Peter Jr., of Boston.
