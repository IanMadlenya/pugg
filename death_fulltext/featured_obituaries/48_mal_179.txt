
        John L. Swink, a retired senior vice president and treasurer of Rutgers University, died on Sunday at his home in Westfield, N.J. He was 76 years old.
        He died of prostate cancer, his family said.
        Mr. Swink was born in Rocky Ford, Colo., and was a graduate of Rutgers with a master's degree from New York University. He joined Rutgers as a mathematics instructor before serving in the Army in World War II.
        In 1945 he was appointed registrar of the University College at Rutgers and later he became dean of administration. He was named senior vice president and treasurer in 1962 and held the post until he retired in 1974.
        Mr. Swink is survived by his wife, the former Florence Glover; two daughters, Nancy Stogowski of Basking Ridge, N.J., and Annette Merrill of Southbury, Conn.; a sister, Barbara Allen of Sun City, Ariz., and three grandchildren.
