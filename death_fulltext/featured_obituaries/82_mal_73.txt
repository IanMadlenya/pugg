
        Willard R. Young Jr., a former political figure and banker in Hunterdon County, N.J., died Wednesday at his home in Lebanon, N.J. He was 81.
        The cause was pneumonia, said his daughter, Linda Kennedy.
        Mr. Young was Mayor of Lebanon Borough for 20 years starting in 1949, and was a Borough Councilman from 1972 to 1978. He served 35 years as a senior vice president and a director of the Hunterdon County National Bank in Flemington, N.J., and continued to serve when it was acquired by the First Fidelity Bank in Flemington.
        Mr. Young is survived by his wife of 57 years, Helen Beatty Young; a son, Willard R. Young 3d of Lebanon; his daughter, also of Lebanon; a brother, Clark S. Young of West Chester, Pa.; four sisters, Betty Sumpter of Roanoke, Va., Lois Dietz of Laguna Niguel, Calif., Charel Schaub of Annandale, N.J., and Anne Holjes of Lebanon, and four grandchildren.
