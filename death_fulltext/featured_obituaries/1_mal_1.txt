        George W. Wyckoff, a former vice president and governor of T. Mellon & Sons, a Pittsburgh investment management concern, died Jan. 23 at the Latrobe Hospital in Latrobe, Pa. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Wyckoff was also the personal representative of Paul Mellon, former chairman of the board of the National Gallery of Art in Washington.
        He was born in Elmira, N.Y., and was a 1929 graduate of Yale University.
        He is survived by four grandchildren.
