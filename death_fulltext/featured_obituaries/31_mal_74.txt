        Dr. William F. Mitty Jr., professor of surgery at New York University School of Medicine, died of a heart attack Wednesday while vacationing in Puerto Rico. He was 67 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Mitty also served as head of the trauma service at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center of New York and was chief of surgery at Gouverneur Hospital. He was a former vice president of the New York Academy of Medicine.
        Born in New York City, Dr. Mitty graduated from Manhattan College and the New York School of Medicine. Surviving are his wife, Dr. Virginia Mitty; a sister, Margaret Conroy of Mt. Vernon, N.Y., and a brother, the Reverend Edward J.J. Mitty of Manhattan.
