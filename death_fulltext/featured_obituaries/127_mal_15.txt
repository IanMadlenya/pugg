
        Joseph Dickson Hinkle, a Boston lawyer who was long active in community affairs, died July 24 at his home on Beacon Hill. He was 54.
        The cause was cancer, said Elliot M. Surkin, a partner in the firm of Hill & Barlow, where Mr. Hinkle practiced corporate and tax law.
        Mr. Hinkle graduated from Yale University in 1964 and from Harvard Law School in 1967.
        Mr. Hinkle is survived by his wife of 29 years, Sarah Ropes Hinkle; their son, Edward R. of Somerville, Mass.; a daughter, Sarah D. Hinkle of Manhattan; a brother, James G. of Cummaquid, Mass., and a sister, Sydney Buffum of Manasota Beach, Fla.
