        Hibben Ziesing, a retired consultant to the chemical industry, died of a cerebral aneurism Saturday at his home in Princeton, N.J. He was 89 years old.
        Mr. Ziesing, a native of La Salle, Ill., worked for several companies, including the Grasselli Chemical Company and the American Cyanamid Company. His wife, Faith Whitney, died in 1987.  He is survived by a daughter, Lucinda, of Manhattan; two stepdaughters, Whitney Wing Oppersdorff of Lincolnsville, Me., and Faith Wing Bieler of Oxfordshire, England, and four grandchildren.
