        David J. Taylor, conference services manager of the Boston World Trade Center, died Saturday at Charlton Memorial Hospital in Fall River, Mass., of injuries suffered in an automobile accident. He was 26 years old.
        Mr. Taylor, who lived in Cambridge, Mass., is survived by his parents, Philip B. Jr. and Mary Taylor of Little Compton, R.I.; his paternal grandmother, Helen Taylor of Little Compton and Naples, Fla.; two brothers, Philip 3d, of Brookline, Mass., and Robert, of Montclair, N.J., and a sister, Pamela, of Boston.
