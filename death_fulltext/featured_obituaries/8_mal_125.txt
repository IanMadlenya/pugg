        Robert E. McElfresh, a former vice president of investment research at Goldman Sachs & Company, died Tuesday at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 67 years old and lived in Sun Valley, Idaho.
        Mr. McElfresh, a former resident of Manhattan, retired from the brokerage house in 1977 after heading its investment research department since 1964. Before that he had been a financial officer with the 20th Century Fox Film Corporation from 1962 to 1964 and the Ford Motor Company from 1949 to 1962.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Wendy Saint, and a brother, William, of Sun City, Ariz.
