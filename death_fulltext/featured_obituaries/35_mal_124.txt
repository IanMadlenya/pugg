        Paul B. Richards, managing director of the General Building Contractors of New York State for 30 years until his retirement in 1987, died of a heart attack Saturday at Albany Medical Center Hospital. He was 68 years old and lived in New Salem, N.Y.
        Mr. Richards, a native of Providence, R.I., was a graduate of Brown University and received a master's degree from Cornell. He served in the Army in World War II, in the Central Intelligence Agency in the early 1950's and as an Air Force major in the Korean War.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ann Greenough; a daughter, Elizabeth, of Ann Arbor, Mich.; two sons, Stephen, of Pace, Fla., and Peter A., of Cambridge, Mass.; a sister, Hope Brothers of Nashville; a brother, Charles, of Wallingford, Pa., and a granddaughter.
