        Dr. Merrill Newton Foote, a retired surgeon, died of heart failure Sunday at the Abbey Delray Health Center in Delray Beach, Fla. He was 99 years old and lived in Delray Beach.
        Dr. Foote, who worked for many years in Brooklyn and on Long Island, was a former regent of the American College of Surgeons.
        He is survived by three daughters, Shirley F. Melum of Sanibel Island, Fla., Jane F. Kruse of Charlottesville, Va., and Patricia F. Davidson of Killingworth, Conn.; eight grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
