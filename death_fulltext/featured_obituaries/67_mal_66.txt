
        Charles A. Read, the former chairman of the Reid & Priest law firm in Manhattan, died on Wednesday at his home in Hosensack, Pa. He was 72 years old. His primary residence was in Montclair, N.J.
        He died of a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. Read joined the firm in 1947 and became a partner in 1956, chairman and administrative partner in 1980 and senior counsel in 1985. He specialized in corporate, utility, engineering, construction and international law.
        Mr. Read was born in Washington.  During World War II he served in the United States Navy.
        He graduated from the University of Virginia and its Law School. Later he served as a trustee and alumni president of both institutions.
        Surviving are his wife of 39 years, the former Marian Berky; a daughter, Susan Read of Staunton, Va.; two sons, Charles Jr. and J. Andrew, both of Washington.; a brother, Robert of Ocean City, Md.; three sisters, Ethel White of Rockville, Md., Jeanne Read of Marbury, Md., and Jacqueline Zeitinger of St. Louis, and a granddaughter.
