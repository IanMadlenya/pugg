        Katharine Randall, a former secretary of the Tarrytown Women's Civic League, died of a heart attack Sunday at the home of her daughter Frances Wood in Mt. Kisco, N.Y. She was 97 years old and lived in Irvington.
        Mrs. Randall, a native of Tarrytown, was a founder of the Hudson River Junior League and of the Thursday Club, a community service organization.
        She was a graduate of Miss Porter's School and of Teachers College of Columbia University in 1915.
        She is survived by another daughter, Holley Smith of Glen, Mont.; six grandchildren, and eight great-grandchildren.
