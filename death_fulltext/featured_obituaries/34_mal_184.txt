        Van Sinderen Lindsley Jr., a retired advertising executive, died Sunday at a nursing home in Suffern, N.Y. He was 83 years old and lived in Tuxedo Park, N.Y.
        Mr. Lindsley was former vice president of marketing for the Quality Bakers of America, from which he retired in the late 60's. He had been associated with Al Paul Lefton Inc., an advertising concern, from 1934 to 1949, when he resigned as vice president.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Anne Child; a stepdaughter, Anne Dick of San Antonio, and a sister, Isabel Joseph of Montreal.
