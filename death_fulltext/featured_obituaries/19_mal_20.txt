        Dr. Grant F. Walton, a former dean of Cook College at Rutgers University, died Wednesday at Doylestown (Pa.) Hospital. He was 63 years old and lived in Stockton, N.J.
        Dr. Walton, an authority on Arctic and desert soils, founded the environmental resources department at Cook. He was dean from 1975 to 1982. Dr. Walton was born in Philadelphia. He received bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees from Rutgers.
        Surviving are his wife, Joan; two sons, Daniel and Matthew, both of Tucson, Ariz.; a daughter, Beth Walton Beth of Downington, Pa.; his mother, Frances, of Southampton, Pa.; a brother, Richard, of Columbus, Ga., and a sister, Jean Stuckert of Middletown, Del.
