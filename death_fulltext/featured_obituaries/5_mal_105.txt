        Dr. Irvin Balensweig, an orthopedic surgeon who practiced in Manhattan for about 50 years until his retirement in 1970, died Thursday at his home in Palm Beach, Fla. He was 92 years old.
        Dr. Balensweig, who received his medical degree from Cornell Medical School, was affiliated for many years with New York Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, Edith; a son, Dr. Howard Balensweig of Manhattan; a daughter, Barbara Wilk of Westport, Conn., three grandchildren and three great grandchildren.
