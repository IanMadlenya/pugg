
        Richard Karasik, president and chief operating officer of Weeks Office Products in Long Island City, Queens, died on Wednesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. He was 62.
        The cause was complications from a stroke, said Carol Pickett, a friend of the family.
        Mr. Karasik graduated from Purdue University and Harvard Law School. He lived in New York City and was active in and a major contributor to the Anti-Defamation League, a division of B'nai B'rith.
        He is survived by his former wife, Muriel Linsky Karasik, of Kings Point, L.I., a daughter, Vickey Karasik Benalloul, also of Kings Point; a son, Charles, of Manhattan, and three stepchildren, Richard Mintzer of Laguna Beach, Calif., David Mintzer of Pleasantville, N.Y., and Elizabeth Mintzer Frankle of Manhattan.
