        Richard Sears Humphrey, retired president and chief executive of the H. B. Humphrey Company, a former Boston-based advertising agency, died Sunday at his home in Water Mill, L.I. He was 91 years old.
        Mr. Humphrey entered the family-owned company after completing his education, and became president in 1933. He stayed on with its successor, H. B. Humphrey, Alley & Richards, until he retired in 1959.
        He graduated from Harvard College in 1921 and from the Harvard Business School in 1923.
        His wife, the former Katherine Van Ingen Downey, died in 1988. He is survived by two daughters, Joan Shoemaker of Seattle and Marion Milton of Denver; a son, Richard S. Jr. of Beverly, Mass.; and two stepdaughters, Katherine Goddard of Calistoga, Calif., and Joan Humphrey of Chestnut Hill, Mass.
