        Ernest G. Weiss, a retired partner in Price Waterhouse, died of a heart attack Sunday at Northern Westchester Hospital Center in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was 71 years old and lived in Armonk, N.Y.
        Mr. Weiss joined the accounting and business advisory firm in 1938, becoming a partner in 1959. He retired in 1977.
        Mr. Weiss, who graduated from City College, received a law degree from Fordham University.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Dorothy Koch; a brother, Werner, of Sun City, Ariz.; two daughters, Carole Chisvin of Sudbury, Mass., and Leslie Tinios of Baltimore, and a granddaughter.
