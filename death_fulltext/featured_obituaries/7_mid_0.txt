        John W. Wydler, a former Representative from Nassau County, died, apparently from a heart attack, Tuesday at Capitol Hill Hospital in Washington. A resident of Garden City, L.I., he was 63 years old.
        Mr. Wydler was stricken Tuesday in the office of Representative Norman F. Lent, of East Rockaway, L.I., according to Mr. Lent's press secretary, Stephanie Taylor. She said Mr. Wydler had been watching the televised House debate on a Nuclear Regulatory Commission bill affecting the nuclear power plant at Shoreham, L.I.
        She said that Mr. Wydler was the chairman of the Long Island Development Agency and that he frequently visited Washington to see friends and keep abreast of issues affecting Long Island.
