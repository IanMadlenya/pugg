        W. Marko Antich, managing director of the Redwood Capital Group, a venture capital concern in Manhattan that specialized in the health care industry, died in a skiing accident Monday in Vail, Colo. He was 29 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Antich was also secretary-treasurer and a director of Vocaltech Inc., a company that develops products for the treatment of speech disorders.
        He is survived by his mother, Patricia; and his brother, Anthony, both of Manhattan.
        Services will be at 1:30 P.M. today at the Unitarian Church of All Souls, at 1157 Lexington Avenue, near 80th Street, in Manhattan.
