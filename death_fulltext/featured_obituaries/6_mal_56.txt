        Emanuel A. Honig, a past president of the New Jersey Bar Association, died of a heart attack yesterday at the Walkill Valley General Hospital in Sussex, N.J. He was 77 years old and lived in Hardyston Township, N.J.
        Mr. Honig was president of the state bar group from 1977 to 1978. He was a general practitioner in the law firm of Honig & Honig in Franklin, N.J., which he founded in 1939.
        He was born in Franklin and graduated cum laude from Lehigh University. He received a law degree from Columbia University in 1934.
        A widower, Mr. Honig is survived by two sons, Harvey J., of Hardyston Township, and Richard E., of Franklin, and four grandchildren.
