        Everett C. Sherbourne, a former chairman of City Federal Savings Bank in Bedminister, N.J., died Thursday at his home in Westfield, N.J., after a long illness. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Sherbourne joined City Federal in 1939 and served as chairman from 1969 to 1978, when he retired.
        He was former president of the New Jersey Savings and Loan League and a former director of the Federal Home Loan Bank.
        He is survived by a son, Roger, of New Lisbon, N.J.; two daughters, Nichols Cooney of Middletown, R.I., and Travis Jane of Tucson, Ariz., and two grandchildren.
