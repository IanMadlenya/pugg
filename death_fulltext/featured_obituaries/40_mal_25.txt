        Edward Luis Crabbe, a retired bank official, died of heart disease on Tuesday at his home in Toms River, N.J. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Crabbe joined the First National City Bank of New York in 1925 after graduating from Princeton University the year before. He retired from the bank, now known as Citibank, in 1962 as a vice president.
        In 1954 Mr. Crabbe became a director of the First National Bank of Toms River and he was named a director emeritus in 1977.
        During World War II he served in the Navy.
        He is survived by a sister, Emily Crabbe Ballou of Honolulu.
