        Wingate H. Lucas, a former Congressman who represented the Texas district now served by House Speaker Jim Wright, died Friday at his home in Bristol, Tenn. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Lucas, a Democrat who was a native of Grapevine, Tex., served in the House of Representatives from Tarrant County (Fort Worth) from 1946 to 1954. He was a former director of the Mid-Appalachian College Council.
        He is survived by two sons, Wingate Jr. of Richmond, and William of Bel Air, Md.; three daughters, Mary Dell Lucas of San Anselmo, Calif., Ellen and Christine Lucas, both of Richmond, and five grandchildren.
