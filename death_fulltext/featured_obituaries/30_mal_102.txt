        Howard B. Sohn, a founding partner of the Manhattan law firm of Squadron, Ellenoff, Plesent & Lehrer, died yesterday after heart surgery at New York Hospital in Manhattan. He was 52 years old and lived in East Northport, L.I.
        Mr. Sohn, who was born in Brooklyn, graduated from Brooklyn College and the New York University Law School. He specialized in estate planning for international clients of the law firm and in advising on trust and tax matters. He was a panelist of the American Arbitration Assocation.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Linda Erdheim; two daughters, Monika and Stephanie; his mother, Jeanette, of Brooklyn, and two brothers, Marvin, of Manhattan and David, of Chapel Hill, N.C.
