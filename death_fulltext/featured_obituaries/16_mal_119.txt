        Paul M. Kaufmann, an investment banker with L. F. Rothschild & Company, died of heart failure Thursday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 85 years old and lived in Mamaroneck, N.Y.
        Mr. Kaufmann, who was born in Manhattan, graduated from Yale College. He joined Rothschild in 1928 and became a partner. In 1983, he became an advisory director of the firm.
        He is survived by two sons, William, of New Rochelle, N.Y., and Robert, of Williamstown, Mass.; two brothers, Robert, of Boston, and Herbert, of Sarasota, Fla., and seven grandchildren.
