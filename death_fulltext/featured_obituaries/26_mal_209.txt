        Alfred Rollin Manville, president and chief executive officer of the Fischbach Corporation, Manhattan electrical and mechanical contractors, died Sunday at his home in Middletown, N.J., after a long illness. He was 72 years old.
        Mr. Manville was born in Carbondale, Pa. He joined Fischbach as a regional vice president in 1977. He was appointed executive vice president in 1977 and president and chief operating officer in 1978.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Michael Burdett; 5 sons; 7 daughters; 2 sisters; a brother, and 14 grandchildren.
