        Victor Simonte Jr., president and chief executive of Bowne & Company, a large printer of financial papers and one of the oldest companies in the United States, died yesterday at his home in Brookville, L.I., after a long illness. He was 56 years old.
        Mr. Simonte joined Bowne & Company, which was founded in 1775, in the late 1940's as an apprentice. He was named executive vice president in 1972 and president in 1974. He was also a former president of the Nassau Country Club.
        He is survived by his wife, Roberta; two sons, Victor 3d and Steven; two daughters, Laurie Ann Cordano and Christina, and a brother, Joseph.
