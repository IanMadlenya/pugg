
        Jesse H. Walker, a former executive editor of The New York Amsterdam News, the weekly newspaper published in Harlem, died on Sunday at Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center. He was 71 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Walker died of cancer, said his wife, Esther.
        A native of Brooklyn, Mr. Walker joined The Amsterdam News as executive editor in 1966. In 1980 he left to take the same position at The New York Voice, another weekly newspaper.
        Mr. Walker is survived by his wife, Esther, and two stepsons, Raymond Graham, of Oakland, Calif., and Raymond Robinson, of Arlington, Va.
