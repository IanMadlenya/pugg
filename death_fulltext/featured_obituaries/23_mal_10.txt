        Alvin B. Lebar, a lawyer, died of a heart attack Wednesday at St. Peter's Medical Center in New Brunswick, N.J. He was 66 years old and lived in Perth Amboy, N.J.
        For 40 years, Mr. Lebar practiced in Metuchen, N.J. He wrote a column for The New Jersey Law Journal and contributed to other law publications.
        He is survived by his wife, Beatrice, and two sons, Daniel, of Metuchen, and Geoffrey.
