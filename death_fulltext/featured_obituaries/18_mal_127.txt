        Dr. Calvin A. VanderWerf, an educator, former president of Hope (Mich.) College and professor of chemistry at the University of Florida, died last Tuesday at Alachua General Hospital in Gainesville, Fla. He was 71 years old and lived in Gainesville.
        He is survived by his wife, Rachel, of Gainesville; five daughters, Julie, of Gainesville; Gretchen Jones and Klasina, both of Boulder, Colo; Lisa Hawkins of Palm Beach Gardens; and Marte Singerman, of Miami; a son, Pieter, of Boston; two sisters, Anne Wabeke and Joan Brieve, both of Holland, Mich., and one granddaughter.
