
        Frederic A. Potts, retired president of the Philadelphia National Bank, died Wednesday at his home on Martha's Vineyard in Massachusetts. He was 87 years old.
        He died of cancer, his family said.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Phoebe Eschleman; two daughters, Alice Wallis of Freeport, Me., and Nancy Masland of Tucson, Ariz.. seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
