        Herman Finkelstein, the former general counsel of the American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers, died of a heart attack on Sunday in Grandview Hospital in Sellersville, Pa. He was 87 years old and lived in Sellersville.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; a son, Thomas, of Tiquisate, Guatemala; and a daughter, Margaret deLude, of Unity, N.H.
