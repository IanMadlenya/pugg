        Dr. Paul Colhoun Morton, former co-chief of surgery at St. Luke's Hospital-Roosevelt Medical Center, died yesterday at Sharon Hospital in Connecticut. He was 89 years old and lived in Washington, Conn.
        Dr. Morton was co-chief of surgery at St. Luke's Hospital in 1954 and later a consulting surgeon at the medical center. He had also taught at Columbia University.
        He is survived by a sister, Frances Morton of Kansas City, Mo.; three daughters, Sue Overton of Tenafly, N.J., Barbara Jill Prior of Fairfield, Conn., and Penelope Gibbs of Charleston, S.C., 11 grandchildren and 5 great-grandchildren.
