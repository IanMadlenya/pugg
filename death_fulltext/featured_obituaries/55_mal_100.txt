
        Daniel Haberman, a poet associated with the Poets' Corner at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine in Manhattan, died on Aug. 8 at Montefiore Medical Center. He was 58 years old and lived in Lewisburg, W.Va.
        He died of lymphoma, said His wife, Barbara Nissman Haberman.
        In addition to his wife, he is survived by two sons, Ray and Thomas, and a sister, Virginia Sendor.
