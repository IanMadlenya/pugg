        Dick Jorgensen, a field official in the National Football League for 23 years, died of cancer Wednesday. He was 56 years old.
        Mr. Jorgensen's assignments included 12 post-season games. Among those were four conference championship games and last season's Super Bowl, the final game in which he officiated.
        Mr. Jorgensen graduated in 1956 from the University of Wisconsin, where he was captain of the basketball team in his senior year. He is survived by his wife, Adlone; three daughters, Londa Dewey, Susan Fitzgerald and Sarah Thompson, and one granddaughter.
