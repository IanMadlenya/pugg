        James Edgar Broyhill, founder of Broyhill Furniture Industries Inc., and father of former Senator James T. Broyhill, died today. He was 96.
        Mr. Broyhill began his furniture business in Lenoir in 1926 after serving in World War I. Broyhill Industries merged with Interco Incorporated of St. Louis in 1980.
