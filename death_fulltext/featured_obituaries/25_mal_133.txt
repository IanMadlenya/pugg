        John H. Jackson, a retired vice president and copy chief of Geer, DuBois Inc., an advertising agency, died of a heart attack Monday at his home in White Plains. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Jackson worked for the agency from 1961 to 1981, when he retired. He was born in White Plains. Surviving are his wife, Charlotte; a daughter, Nicole, of White Plains; four sons, John 4th, of White Plains, and John Stillman, Phillippe Stillman and Mark Stillman, and two sisters, Sara Rasmussen of Rye, N.Y., and Eliza Ewing of Eastham, Mass.
