        Joseph F. Heffernan, the photographer for the Metropolitan Opera House, died of lung cancer Thursday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 64 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Heffernan, who was born in Brooklyn, had been with the Met for more than 20 years. Formerly he was a captain in the New York City Fire Department.
        He is survived by a sister, Margaret Glynn of Somers, N.Y.; two daughters, Patricia Heffernan of New City, N.Y., and Christine Page of Hopedale, Mass.; two sons, Thomas, of Montclair, N.J., and James, of Runnemeade, N.J., and six grandchildren.
        Correction:  November 19, 1987, Thursday, Late City Final Edition
