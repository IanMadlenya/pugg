        John F. Morgan, a New York real-estate broker for many decades, died Tuesday at Rex Hospital in Raleigh, N.C. He was 94 years old and lived in Pelham Manor, N.Y. He had lived for many years in New Jersey.
        Mr. Morgan, who attended Columbia University, was a partner in the realty concern of Morgan & Benkiser, and was instrumental in developing Morningside Gardens, north of the Columbia campus, one of the first middle-income housing projects in New York City.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Sara Tobin Gephart.
