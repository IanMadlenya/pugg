        Morris Goldsmith, one of the last surviving members of the Jewish Legion, died Monday at the John F. Kennedy Medical Center in Edison, N.J. He was 98 years old and lived in Toms River, N.J.
        Mr. Goldsmith came here from the Ukraine in 1909. In World War I, he fought in Palestine with the legion, a British Army unit of Zionist supporters from around the world. He was in the dairy and egg business.  Surviving are his wife, the former Esther Reichman; two daughters, Florence Blatt and Evelyn Rabinowitz of Colonia, N.J.; six grandchildren, and eight great-grandchildren.
