        Charles H. D. Robbins, a writer and editor, died Wednesday at a nursing home in Valatie, N.Y., after a long illness. He was 84 years old and formerly lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Robbins worked for the Hearst Corporation on several magazines including The American Weekly and Coronet. At his retirement in 1969, he was the executive editor of the magazine This Week.
        He is survived by his wife, Janice; twin daughters from a former marriage, Harriette Siani of Manhattan and Patricia, of Denver; a stepson, Joel Griffeths of Manhattan; four grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
