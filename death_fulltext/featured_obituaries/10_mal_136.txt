        Jacob Schulman, senior partner of the accounting firm of Jacob Schulman & Co., died Monday at Albany Medical Center. He was 72 years old and lived in Gloversville, N.Y.
        Mr. Schulman was a director of the Saratoga Performing Arts Center and the National Museum of Dance in Saratoga Springs, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Selma Shapiro; a son, Steven, of New York City; two daughters, Carol R. Lewkowicz of Upper Montclair, N.J., and Beverly Cohn of Livingston, N.J; a brother, Leon, of Miami Beach, and five grandchildren.
