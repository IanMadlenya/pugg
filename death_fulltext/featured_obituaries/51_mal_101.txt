
        William C. Fine, retired chairman of the Sherwin-Williams Company, the paint manufacturer, died Tuesday at his home in Boynton Beach, Fla. He was 73 years old and had lived for many years before retirement in Rocky River, Ohio.
        He died of Alzheimer's disease, his family said.
        Mr. Fine was born in Aledo, Okla., and was a graduate of Wichita State University. At Sherwin-Williams, based in Cleveland, he rose to become president in 1975 and chairman in 1979. He retired in 1980.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen Goodin, and a son, Robert, of Erie, Pa.
