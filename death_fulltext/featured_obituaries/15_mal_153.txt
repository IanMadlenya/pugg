        Herbert Lebovici, a maritime lawyer who specialized in labor-management problems, died March 8 at the Pascack Valley Hospital in Westwood, N.J., from complications after surgery. He was 75 years old and lived in Hillsdale, N.J.
        Mr. Lebovici was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of Brooklyn College and the Brooklyn Law School. He served in the Navy in World War II.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Freda Polett; two sons, Darrow, of Salem, Mass., and Victor, of West Newton, Mass., and a sister, Winifred Brown of Miami.
