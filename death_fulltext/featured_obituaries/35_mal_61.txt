        Robert E. Wallace, a retired insurance executive, died of heart disease on Wednesday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 86 years old and lived in Summit.
        Mr. Wallace was a senior vice president of the Chubb Corporation, an insurance holding corporation, at his retirement in 1968. He joined the company in 1927 and became a partner in 1949. He was born in Disputanta, Va., and was a graduate of Centre College, Danville, Ky.
        A widower, he is survived by a son, Robert Jr., of Falls Village, Conn.; a daughter, Louise Hickey of Atherton, Calif.; five grandchildren, and seven great-grandchildren.
