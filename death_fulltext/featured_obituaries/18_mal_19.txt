        Bartolomeu Simoes Pereira, the Minister of Planning of Guinea-Bissau, died in a car crash Monday. He was 40 years old.
        Mr. Simoes Pereira had been Planning Minister for four years. He had drawn up an economic restructuring program for his West African nation that was backed by the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank.
