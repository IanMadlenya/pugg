        Carl F. Jellinghaus, a retired executive editor of The Record newspaper in Hackensack, N.J., died of congestive heart failure yesterday in Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital. He was 71 years old and lived in Westport, Conn.
        Mr. Jellinghaus, a graduate of Yale University, joined the newspaper in 1947 as a reporter. He became managing editor in 1971 and vice president and executive editor in 1973. He retired in 1982.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Phyllis Howard; three children, Carl F. Jr., of Westport; Adrian, of Gloucester, Mass., and Lisa Williamson of San Diego, and three grandchildren.
