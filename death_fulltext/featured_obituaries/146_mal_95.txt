
        Doug Edelson, a correspondent for 28 years for 1010 WINS, the New York news radio station, died on Friday at a Fort Lauderdale, Fla., hospital. He was 75 and lived in Florida and Pennsylvania after retiring from WINS in 1992. He died of a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. Edelson was born in the Bronx, worked for NBC, and then joined WINS. He specialized in political and economic news and urban affairs, and was president of the Inner Circle, a political writers' group.
        His first wife, Frances, died in 1970. He is survived by his wife, Barbara; two daughters from his first marriage, Jeanne Edelson of East Norwich, N.Y., and Catherine Fischer of Stamford, Conn.; three brothers, Howard, of Plainview, N.Y., Charles, of Grover Beach, Calif., and Jay, of Las Vegas, Nev., and a grandson.
