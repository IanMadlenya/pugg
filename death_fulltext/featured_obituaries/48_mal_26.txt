
        Leslie Aronow, a public relations executive and president of the Alliance of New York State Arts Councils, died on Monday at his home in Suffern, N.Y. He was 53 years old.
        Mr. Aronow died of a heart attack, a spokesman for the family said.
        Mr. Aronow was a managing partner of Kanan, Corbin, Schupak & Aronow, a Manhattan-based public relations firm he joined in 1974. He was also the founder and president of Portfolio Graphic Services, the concern's graphic design and production studio.
        A graduate of Colgate University, he was a journalist until he took a public relations job with Chase Manhattan Bank.
        Mr. Aronow is survived by his wife, Gail; three sons, James, of Wayne, Pa., and Donald and Robert, both of Suffern; a brother, Dr. Eugene Aronow of Old Bethpage, N.Y., and a sister, Florence Feifer of Lancaster, Pa.
