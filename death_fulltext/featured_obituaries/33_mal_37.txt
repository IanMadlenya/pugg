        Harry A. LaBurt, a doctor and former director of Creedmoor State Hospital in Queens for 26 years, died on Monday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I., after being struck by a car. He was 91 years old and lived in Bellerose, Queens.
        Dr. LaBurt received his medical degree from the University of Buffalo in 1923. After completing his residency, he joined the staff of the New York State Psychiatric Institute in Manhattan. He was later medical inspector for the New York State Department of Mental Hygiene and director of the Harlem Valley State Hospital in Wingdale, N.Y. In 1943, he became director of Creedmoor. He retired in 1969. Dr. LaBurt had also been a professor of psychiatry at Adelphi College, the New York School of Psychiatry and the New Jersey College of Medicine and Dentistry.
        There are no immediate survivors.
