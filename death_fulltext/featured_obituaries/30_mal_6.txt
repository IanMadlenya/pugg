        W. Kenneth Pope, a United Methodist bishop, died of natural causes Monday. He was 87 years old and lived in Dallas.
        Elected bishop in 1960, Mr. Pope was head of the North Texas Conference of the United Methodist Church from 1964 until his retirement in 1972.
        After retirement, he was bishop in residence at the Perklns School of Theology at Southern Methodist University. He was a former chairman of the university's regents.
        He is survived by a daughter, Catherine Pope of Portland, Ore., and a son, Kenneth, of Los Angeles.
