
       Jess T. Adkins, the director of the Schimmel Center for the Arts at Pace University and an actor best known for his work in daytime television series, died of cancer Wednesday at N.Y.U. Medical Center. He was 62 years old. Mr. Adkins was director of the National Theater Institute at the Eugene O'Neill Theater Center in Waterford, Conn., from 1980 to 1983.
        Jess T. Adkins, the director of the Schimmel Center for the Arts at Pace University and an actor best known for his work in daytime television series, died of cancer Wednesday at N.Y.U. Medical Center. He was 62 years old. Mr. Adkins was director of the National Theater Institute at the Eugene O'Neill Theater Center in Waterford, Conn., from 1980 to 1983. In addition to his roles in daytime television series, he played Carl the Trashman on ''Sesame Street.''
        Mr. Adkins's wife, Marguerite, died in 1981. There are no survivors.
