        Otto Nuttli, an earthquake expert and professor of geophysics at St. Louis University, died Tuesday of cancer at the age of 61.
        Mr. Nuttli was known worldwide for his expertise on earthquakes and particularly on the New Madrid fault in extreme southeast Missouri.
