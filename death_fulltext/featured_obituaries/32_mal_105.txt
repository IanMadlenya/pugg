        Justice Daniel E. Macken, a retired justice of New York State Supreme Court, died Sept. 7 at his home in Fairport, N.Y. He was 84 years old.
        Justice Macken was in private practice in Rochester and was on the Board of Supervisors there before Gov. Nelson A. Rockefeller appointed him to the Supreme Court in 1960. He became an associate justice of the appellate divison in Manhattan in 1968 and retired in 1975.
        He was a 1928 alumnus of Syracuse University Law School, Surviving are his wife, Mary; two sons, Dr. Daniel Macken of Manhattan and A. Connell, of Mahopac, N.Y., and four grandchildren.
