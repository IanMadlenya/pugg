        A. Joseph Ferolie, founder and retired chairman of the Ferolie Corporation, a food brokerage, died of heart failure Friday at his home in Cresskill, N.J. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Ferolie founded the company in 1948 to represent food manufacturers to supermarket chains in New York, Connecticut and New Jersey. He retired 10 years ago.
        Born in Manhattan, he was active in charity work and a former trustee of the Academy of Food Marketing at St. Joseph's College in Brooklyn.
        He is survived by his son, Lawrence, of Alpine, N.J.; five grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
