        Dr. Glenn Sheline, a leader in the field of neuro-oncology, died of cancer last Friday at his home here. He was 70 years old.
        Dr. Sheline, professor emeritus of radiation oncology at the University of California Hospital in San Francisco, was the recipient this year of the Farber Award, presented by the American Academy of Neurology. He was chairman of the committee on radiation policy of the American College of Radiation.
        He is survived by his wife, Patricia, of San Mateo; a daughter, Moira Hughes of Salem, Ore., two sisters and a brother.
