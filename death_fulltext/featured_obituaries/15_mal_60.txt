        Martin E. Citrin, a business executive who was the only non-family executor of the estate of Henry Ford 2d, has died of a self-inflicted gunshot wound, the police said. He was 59 years old and lived in Bloomfield Hills.
        Mr. Citrin's body was found here Saturday at a motel he had checked into Thursday, the police said.
        Mr. Citrin, a former president of Citrin Oil, a gasoline distributor, co-founded Ways and Means, a health-care products concern, in 1984.
        He is survived by his wife, Myra, two daughters, two sons, his mother and a brother.
