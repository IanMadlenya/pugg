
        Hugh R. Chace, a former senior executive vice president of the Bank of New York, died March 30 at Baptist Medical Center in Ponte Vedra Beach, Fla. He was 86 years old and lived in Ponte Vedra Beach.
        He died of congestive heart failure, his family said.
        Mr. Chace, a graduate of Amherst College, joined the Bank of New York in 1932 and rose to become head of its commercial banking operations. He retired in 1969.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Cornelia Dozier; a son, Hugh Jr., of Liverpool, England, and three grandchildren.
