        Bryan E. Smith, a former chairman and president of the Liberty Mutual Insurance Company, died of pneumonia Wednesday at the Fairport Baptist Home, where he had lived for the last year. He was 93 years old.
        Born in Easton, Ill., Mr. Smith graduated from the University of Minnesota in 1925 and began his career with Liberty Mutual in 1927 as a salesman.
        He later worked for the company in Duluth, Minn., St. Paul, Pittsburgh, New York City and Boston, where Liberty Mutual is based.
        Mr. Smith was executive vice president before being appointed president in 1956. He was chairman from 1962 to 1966, when he was appointed honorary chairman. In 1970 he was named chairman emeritus. He served on Liberty Mutual's board from 1955 to 1975.
        He is survived by a daughter, Susan Johnson of Fairport; a son, Dr. Charles B. Smith of Salt Lake City, and seven grandchildren.
