        William H. Kearns, a former top official of the Ted Bates advertising agency, died of cancer Tuesday at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital. He was 78 years old and lived in Riverside, Conn.
        Mr. Kearns joined the agency in 1942 as president, a year after its founding by the late Theodore L. Bates, and remained one of its top executives for almost three decades.
        Surviving are two stepchildren, James Draper of Richmond, Vt., and Helene Gorman of Floyds Knobs, Ind., and three stepgrandchildren.
