        Liet. Gen. Donald Harry Cowles, retired, an Army deputy chief of staff in the mid-1970's, died Saturday at Alexandria Hospital. He was 71 years old.
        General Cowles was a native of Westfield, Mass. He served in World War II in Europe, and in the Berlin crisis in 1961 in West Germany. He held staff posts in Vietnam from 1970 to 1972. He was promoted to lieutenant general in 1972 and was named an Army deputy chief of staff. He retired in 1975.
        After retirement he joined the BDM Corporation, now a division of Ford Motor Company, in McLean, Va.
