
        Jon Epstein, producer of the television mini-series "Rich Man, Poor Man" and executive producer of the "Columbo" series, died of leukemia on Saturday at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center here. He was 62 years old.
        Among the many television shows Mr. Epstein produced since the 1950's were "Tarzan," "The Flying Nun" and "McMillan and Wife."
        His other credits included "The Trials of O'Brien," "Rat Patrol," "The Outcasts," "The Young Rebels," "Three's a Crowd," "The Sheriff," "Scene of the Crime" and "Owen Marshall, Counselor at Law."
