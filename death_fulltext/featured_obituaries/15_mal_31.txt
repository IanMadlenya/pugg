        Raymond Chasan, a lawyer and former corporation counsel of Jersey City, died of heart failure Tuesday at Christ Hospital in Jersey City. He was 83 years old and lived in Jersey City.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Tesse Steierman; three daughters, Muriel Leyner of Hoboken, N.J., Frances Holland of Secaucus, N.J., and Alice Chasan of Princeton, N.J.; two sisters, Gertrude Zeitlin of Jersey City and Beatrice Schanerman of Ocean Township, N.J., and six grandchildren.
