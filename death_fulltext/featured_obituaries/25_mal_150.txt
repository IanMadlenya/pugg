        Troy R. Westmeyer, a professor emeritus of the New York University Graduate School of Public Administration, died Saturday at Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 72 years old and lived in Stamford.
        Mr. Westmeyer taught at N.Y.U. from 1958 to 1981 and was acting dean of the School of Public Administration from 1968 to 1970. He was executive director of the New York State Legislative Commission on Expenditure Review from 1969 to 1978.
        Surviving are a son, Wesley, of Manhattan; a daughter, Lynn Slink of Stamford, and three grandchildren.
