        Blake Stern, an oratorio singer and a professor emeritus of music at Yale University, died Tuesday in Logan, Iowa. He was 70 years old.
        Mr. Stern graduated from Grinnell College in 1940 and served as an intelligence officer in the Navy during World War II before studying music at the Juilliard School in New York City. He was a member of the Yale faculty for 32 years until his retirement in June.
        A frequent guest artist at Tanglewood, Mr. Stern also performed at Carnegie Hall and Town Hall in New York City and made several world tours.
