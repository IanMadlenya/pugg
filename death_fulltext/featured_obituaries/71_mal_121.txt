
        Dr. Arnold L. Bachman, a New York City radiologist for many years, died on Thursday at Mount Sinai Hospital in Manhattan. He was 80 and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of lymphoma, his family said.
        Dr. Bachman was the director of radiology at Francis Delafield Hospital of Columbia University from 1950 until it closed in 1975. He was also a teacher at the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia and at the Mount Sinai School of Medicine as well as a private practitioner.
        Surviving are his wife of 53 years, Dr. Jean Pakter; a son, Dr. Donald, of Wayland, Mass., and a daughter, Dr. Ellen B. Mendelson of Pittsburgh, both of them radiologists; a brother, Herbert of Prescott, Ariz., and six grandchildren.
