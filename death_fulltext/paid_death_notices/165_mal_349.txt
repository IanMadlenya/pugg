
        EDELMAN-Jerome. Age 87 of Boca Raton, FL., formerly of Brooklyn, N.Y. Died October 26, 2000. Beloved husband of Lillian. Loving father of Martin (Carmen) Edelman of N.Y.C., and Lynn Edelman of Playa del Rey, Calif. Dear brother of Esther Levine, N.Y.C., and Isidore Edelman, N.Y.C. Cherished grandfather of Dana and Gabrielle Edelman. He was a member of the International Academy of Trial Lawyers, The Association of Trial Lawyers of America, and the NY State Trial Lawyers Association.
