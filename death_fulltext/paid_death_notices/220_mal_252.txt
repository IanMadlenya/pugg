
        SEIDEMAN--Richard. Died May 19. A 50-year veteran of the advertising industry, he was executive vice president and chief operating officer of The Rodd Group. Past agencies include BBDO, Dancer Fitzgerald Sample, and Savitt Tobias Balk. He consulted to Hearst Corporation for 17 years, and in 1964 helped secure President Lyndon Johnon's victory. Survived by his beloved wife of 53 years, Elizabeth; daughter, Ellen, sons Tony and David; granddaughters Adelaia and Eliza. Memorial service: 1 PM Tuesday, May 24, BallardDurand Funeral Home, White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to: White Memorial Foundation, 17 Whitehall Road, P.O. Box 368, Litchfield, CT, 06759.
