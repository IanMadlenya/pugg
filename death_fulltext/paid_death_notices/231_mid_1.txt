
        KRAUT--Miriam. Heartfelt condolences to Professor Benny Kraut, Director of the Queens College Center for Jewish Studies, and his family, on the passing of his cherished mother Miriam. A Holocaust survivor, she passes on her courageous legacy to her son, and his wife, Penina; their children, Racheli (Mordy Hackel), Yehuda, and Yosefa; and two great granddaughters. May her memory be for a blessing. Shiva is being held at the Kraut home in Long Island. The Queens College Center for Jewish Studies Advisory Board, Arthur Anderman, Chair
