
        AUERBACH -- William, of New Rochelle, passed away Tuesday, March 9, 2004. He was 89 years old. Mr. Auerbach was the devoted husband of the late Tess Auerbach, who predeceased him in 1992. He is survived by his loving children Sue, Allen and Melissa, and five grandchildren. Funeral services are scheduled for 1:00 p.m. today at Temple Israel in New Rochelle with interment in the Sharon Gardens Cemetery. For more information, George T. Davis Funeral Home, 914-632-0324.
