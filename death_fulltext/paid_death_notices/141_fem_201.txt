
        MANES-Florence. Died peacefully in her sleep October 14, 1998. Beloved aunt of Barbara Joan Grubman, Jack (Leslie) Manes and Jill (Robert) Rosen; sister-inlaw Geraldine Manes. Also survived by several great-nieces and nephews. Affectionately known by all of us as ''The Mayor of 8th Street in Greenwich Village'' because of all the wonderful friends she had there. We will all miss her very much. Funeral services Gramercy Park Chapel, 152 2nd Ave, Sunday October 25 at noon. Donations to Womens Memorial, Dept 560, Washington, DC 20042
