
        MARKEY-Walter. On July 17, 2002. Beloved husband of Sylvia, loving brother of Doris Chaewsky. Sylvia and Walter were the co-founders of Showcase Theater Dramatic School and Sylvia Leigh Studio of Speech Arts in New York City. Walter was a renowned chiropractor as well. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street, Friday, July 19, 2-5 and 7-9PM, with service Saturday, July 20, at 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell.
