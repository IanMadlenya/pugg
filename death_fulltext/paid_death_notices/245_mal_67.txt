
        FRIEDMAN--Gabriel (Gary), age 84 years, died peacefully from a cerebral hemorrhage on June 4. Beloved husband of Shirley, his wife of 64 years. Cherished father of Mindy Stevely and son and business partner Richard (Cynthia). Adored grandfather of Dakoda and Skye, Samara and Ross, and greatgrandfather of Zachary. Brilliant and widely respected CPA; Beta Gamma Sigma Honor Society, NYS Society of CPAs, first president, charter member and CPA of the Woodcrest Club. Avid golfer, tennis player, loved mathematical challenges. Great sense of humor and raconteur. Funeral services June 7, 10am at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Tpke, Woodbury, NY.
