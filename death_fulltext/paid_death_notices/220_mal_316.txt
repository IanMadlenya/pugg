
        HESS--Dr. Robert S. On May 27, 2005, at the age of 93. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Loving father of John and Tom. Dr. Hess practiced dentistry for 49 years. A retired Captain, he served the US Navy 1942-1971. Services will be conducted at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, corner East 81st St., 212288-3500. Please call for service time.
        HESS--Dr. Robert S. The Officers, Governors and Members of the Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Dr. Robert Hess, and extend their heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on May 31st, at 11:30 am, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, Madison Avenue and 81st St. Freddie Roman, Dean Jean Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
