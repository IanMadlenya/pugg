
        PLOVNICK-Adele K. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Queens, NY, died August 2, 1999 in Boca Raton, FL. Mrs. Plovnick was a member of B'nai B'rith Women, We Care Organization, Hadassah and past president of the Mansfield Condo Association in her community. Loving and devoted mother of Phyllis Solomon of New York City and Myra Pfeifer of Wantagh, Long Island. Loving and devoted mother-in-law of Gerry Solomon and Michael Pfeifer. Cherished grandmother of Jordan and Perry & great-grandmother of Amanda & Jaime, dedicated sister of Sylvia Levine of Jericho, New York, and beloved aunt to many nieces and nephews. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, August 4th, 1999, at 12:00 P.M. at the Parkside Memorial Chapels, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, New York. Interment will follow at New Montefiore Cemetery in Pinelawn, New York. Please consult the family for days and times of their mourning period observance.
