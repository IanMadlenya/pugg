
        SEGAL-Alix Hegeler. Beloved wife of Joel Segal. Loving mother of Hilary Good and Brian Good. Stepmother of Mark Segal and Gregg Segal. Grandmother of Nina Segal. Alix passed away peacefully in her home in New York City on October 18, 2000. Alix will be in all of our hearts forever. Alix graduated Summa Cum Laude from Fordham University in 1994 with a degree in History. She was a member of the Honor Society of Phi Kappa Phi and served on the Fordham University Board of Advisors. She was more than proud of her association with Fordham. A burial service will take place on Monday, October 23, at 3:00 PM, at St. James Church, 865 Madison Avenue at 71st St., where Alix was a longtime parishioner. A reception will follow immediately at the Mark Hotel, 25 East 77 St. In lieu of flowers, a contribution may be made to the New York Hospital Pulmonary Education Fund, Attn: Divisional Administrator, Weill Medical College of Cornell University, 1300 York Ave., Box 96, New York, NY 10021.
