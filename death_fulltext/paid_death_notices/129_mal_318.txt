
        SWEENEY-William J. III, M.D. The boards, faculty and staff of The New York Hospital and Cornell University Medical College mourn the passing of their beloved and esteemed colleague Dr. William J. Sweeney III. A dedicated physician and researcher, Dr. Sweeney was, at the time of his death, Clinical Professor of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Cornell University Medical College and Attending Obstetrician and Gynecologist at The New York Hospital. Dr. Sweeney received his medical degree from Cornell in 1949 and was a generous supporter of the Cornell University Medical College Alumni Fund. He authored over 50 publications and was recognized for his extensive work in gynecological cancer. Dr. Sweeney's commitment and contributions greatly advanced the field of obstetrics and gynecology. He was much loved and will be greatly missed. Our deepest condolences are extended to the Sweeney family. John T. McGillicuddy Chairman, Board of Governors The New York Hospital David B. Skinner, M.D. President and CEO The New York Hospital Sanford I. Weill Chairman, Board of Overseers Cornell University Medical College Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Stephen and Suzanne Weiss Dean Cornell University Medical College William J. Ledger, M.D. Chairman Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology The New York Hospital
