
        POST--George Birkbeck, Jr., the 8th generation of the original settlers of Quogue, NY on the south shore of Long Island, died July 28, 2005. He was 74. A memorial service will be held Friday August 5 at 11am at the Westhampton Presbyterian Church in Westhampton Beach, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family welcomes memorial gifts to Westhampton Presbyterian Church on Meetinghouse Rd, Westhampton Beach, NY.
