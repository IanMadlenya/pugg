
        JACOBS--Herbert E. January 26, in Palm Beach, Florida. Loving husband of Shirley, devoted father of Alan L. Jacobs (Barbara), Betty Malech (Kenneth) and Lilith Jacobs. Beloved brother of Pearl Goldman (Mynn) and Thelma Goldberg. Cherished grandfather of Emily Florin (Doug), Daniel Malech and Edward and Charles Jacobs. Services 10:00 AM Sunday at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st St. and Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be made to Thorasic Oncology Services (Lung Fund) c/o Dr. Vincent Miller, Memorial Sloan Kettering, 1275 York Avenue, New York 10021.
        JACOBS--Herbert E. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Herbert E. Jacobs. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Shirley, and their family. Jack Sussman, President Joseph Lehman, Secretary
