
        McDERMOTT--Jeanne C., 82, died peacefully on August 3, 2005 on Nantucket Island, Massachusetts. Her husband, Frank A. McDermott, Jr., predeceased her in 2003. Devoted mother of Jeanne Cohane, Joan McDermott, Frank McDermott, Jane Bogue, Stephen McDermott, Sheila Sutton and John McDermott. Cherished grandmother of 32 grandchildren and 8 great grandchildren. She will be missed by her loving family and friends and will live in their hearts forever. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville between the hours of 4 to 8PM Monday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville Tuesday, August 9 at 10 A.M. Interment immediately following at St. Mary's Cemetery, Yonkers, NY.
