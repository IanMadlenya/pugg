
        BURROS-Janet. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Janet Burros, beloved mother of Mara Sandler, who with her husband, Ricky, are devoted leaders of UJA-Federation. Ricky has been an active member of our Wall Street Division, and recently became chair of the Investment Advisors Division. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mara and Ricky; to Janet's husband, Robert; to her son, Chet; and to her entire family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair and President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP and CEO
