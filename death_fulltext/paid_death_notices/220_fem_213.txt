
        KANTROW--Sylvia. Age 95. Died as she wished--at home and independent--on May 23, 2005. Devoted wife of Aaron Kantrow. Mother of Alan Kantrow, and aunt of Dr. Ronald I. Maitland. She was the last of her kind, and her passing marks the end of an era. Funeral services 9 AM Sunday, June 12, GuttermanParkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue (at Avenue V), Brooklyn.
