
        PRITZIER-Betten Elisabeth. Of New York on September 16, 1998 of cancer. Survivors include her husband Sverre & two daughters and their husbands, Gye and Alan Brunner of Glastonbury, CT., and Anne and George Schreiber of Redmond, WA., as well as five grandchildren. Betten was a native or Norway and a memorial is planned in Sandefjord, Norway. Donations in her memory may be made to the Norwegian Seamen's Church, 317 East 52 St, NY, NY 10022. Phone number 212-319-0370.
