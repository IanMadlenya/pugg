
        CLIFFORD-Katherine Corbett, of New York and Montclair, N.J., on February 21. Wife of the late Paul Clifford. Loving mother of Denis, Joanne, Steven, Gregory, Douglas, Justin and Catherine. She also leaves seven grandchildren. Memorial service on Saturday, March 7th, at 11 o'clock A.M., at Saint Cassian's Roman Catholic Church, Montclair, New Jersey.
