
        CODDINGTON-Audrey Wheaton of New Providence, NJ, on August 11, 1998. Wife of the late Lyman Badgley Coddington, Jr. Beloved mother of Janet Halliday of Juno Beach, FL; Lyman Badgley Coddington, III of Washington, DC; Joyce Harrington of New Canaan, CT; Joan Boillod, of Summit, NJ; and Ann Mays of Wakefield, RI. Also survived by 9 grandchildren and 7 great-grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at the New Providence Presbyterian Church, corner of Springfield Ave. and Passaic Ave., New Providence, NJ on Saturday, August 15, 1998 at 11:00 am. Those who wish may contribute in her memory to SAGE, 50 DeForest Ave., Summit, NJ, 07901 or to Overlook Hospital, 99 Beauvoir Ave., Summit, NJ, 07901.
