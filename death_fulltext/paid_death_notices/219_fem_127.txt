
        SEN--Kamala. Died peacefully on April 10, 2005 in Ithaca, N.Y. She was 90. The beloved wife of Sudhir Sen, she is survived by three children, Shankar Sen, Sheila Jasanoff, Shyamoli Pyne and four grandchildren. Her life was dedicated to charity and shs became a prominent leader at the United Nations Women's Guild which funds charities for children around the world. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be sent to UNWG, DC1-775, 1 UN Plaza, NYC, NY 10017, in her memory.
