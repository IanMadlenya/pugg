
        KELLER-Gerald H. Died August 27 after a courageous battle with cancer. A devoted and caring husband of 48 years to Barbara, an understanding and supportive father to Judy and Robert, and a proud and loving grandfather to Samantha, Katie and Kendall. His strong presence and adventuresome spirit will be missed. The consummate adman. Services Friday, August 30, 11 AM at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St.
        KELLER-Gerald H. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar ''Gerald H. Keller'' on ''August 27, 2002''. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Friday, August 30, at 11:00 AM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, at 91st Street. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
