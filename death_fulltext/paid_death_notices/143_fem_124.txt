
        ZIMMERMAN-Alice (nee Parke). Of Stonington, CT, Amityville, Point O' Woods and Brooklyn, NY in her 90th year. Wife of John O. Zimmerman. Mother of Peter Zimmerman and Anne Zimmerman Rodrigues. Grandmother of two girls and three boys. Greatgrandmother of two girls. Memorial service at Calvary Church, Stonington, CT, at 11 AM on Friday, December 18, 1998. Gifts in her memory may be made to Wellesley College, Wellesley, MA 02181 or to the charity of the donor's choice. For information, Mystic Funeral Home, 860-536-2888.
