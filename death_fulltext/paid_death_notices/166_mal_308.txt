
        CUNILIO-Victor. Age 84, died November 24, 2000. Beloved husband of Geraldine McMullen Cunilio. Dear father of Marianne Cunilio Jackson of NYC, Thomas V. of Gainesville, FL, and James of Rosemont, PA. His youngest child, Cecelia, predeceased him. Loving brother of Ann Palisi, Rose Fortunato and the late Jos. Cuniglio. Devoted father-in-law of Rick, Maria and Tricia. Treasured grandfather of Dr. Benjamin M. and Tobias M. Jackson, Christopher T., Kathleen Cecelia, Jamie G., Jessica R., and Charles J. Cunilio. Also survived by four nephews, one niece and many other relatives. Graduate of New Utrecht High School, NYU, and DePaul Law School in Chicago. In WWII served in the Pacific with U.S. Marine Corps. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held November 28, 10:30 AM, at St. Thomas of Villanova Church (PA). In lieu of flowers, family requests donations to House of Ruth, 651 10th St. NE, Washington, DC 20002.
