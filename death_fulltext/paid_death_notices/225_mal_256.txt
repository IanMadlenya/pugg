
        BLINKEN -- Robert James. April 18, 1929-October 22, 2005. Survived by his wife, Allison; his brothers, Donald and Alan; his children, Robert Blinken, Rachel Herz, Anna Lonergan and Ingrid Blinken; his grandchildren, Danna, Jonathan and Adam Herz, Jared Blinken and Wylie and Oscar Lonergan. A memorial service is being planned.
        $(6$)BLINKEN--Robert. It is with great sadness that the Board of Trustees of the New York Blood Center records the death of Robert Blinken, a dedicated and loyal member of the Board from 1999 to the present. During his tenure, he was both a member of the Executive Committee and a Trustee of the Board. Throughout his association with the Blood Center, his enthusiasm for its mission and his commitment to service were highly demonstrated and very much appreciated by the Blood Center. On behalf of the NYBC and its entire staff, we extend our sincere condolences to the Blinken family. Howard P. Milstein, Chair Board of Trustees. Robert L. Jones, MD, President
        $(6$)BLINKEN--Robert J. The Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are deeply saddened by the death of Robert, loving husband of Board member Allison Blinken. Robert was a dedicated theater lover who focused his devotion on LCT, exalting in our achievements and finding virtue in all of our undertakings. We extend condolences to Allison, Robert, Rachael, Anna and Ingrid. He will be especially missed on opening nights. John Beinecke, Chairman John Chalsty, President Andre Bishop, Artistic Director Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
