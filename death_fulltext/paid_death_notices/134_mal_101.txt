
        MELE-Peter A. MD. The Medical Staff of Englewood Hospital and Medical Center is saddened by the passing of Dr. Peter A. Mele. Dr. Mele was a loyal member of the medical staff for over 30 years. Peter played a vital role in the Department of Surgery serving as director of the residency program for many years. His surgical career culminated with his being elected as the first president of the American Society of Abdominal Surgeons. His dedication and service to the hospital and to his patients was only surpassed by his love of his family. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Paola, his daughter Paola, his sons Michael, Peter, and Christopher. Barry Sussman, MD, President Richard Salzer, MD, Vice President Kenneth Rubin, MD, Secretary Treasurer Medical Staff Englewood Hospital and Medical Center
        MELE-Peter A., M.D. The Board of Trustees of Englewood Hospital and Medical Center mourns the passing of our esteemed colleague, Peter A. Mele, M.D. Dr. Mele was a member of our Department of Surgery for over thirty years serving as Director of the Surgical Residency Program. He will be remembered for his commitment to the Medical Center and to his patients. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Paola, his daughter Paola DeVita, his sons Michael, Peter A., Jr., Christopher Mele and their families. George P. Kramer, Chairman Andrew F. Durkin, Vice Chairman William G. Kuhns, Treasurer Daniel A. Kane, Pres and Secy
