
        GANEK-Bessie. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Bessie Ganek, a beloved mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother. Her son, Howard Ganek, is a dedicated leader and generous supporter of our Wall Street Division as a member of the Neuberger & Berman UJA-Federation Campaign Committee, and as an officer of the Educational Alliance and the Parker Jewish Institute for Health Care and Rehabilitation, both UJA-Federation beneficiaries. The entire Jewish community is indebted to the family for its dedication and support, and extends its sincerest condolences. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        GANEK-Bessie. The Board and Staff of the Educational Alliance are saddened by the death of Bessie Ganek, beloved mother of our esteemed Senior Vice President and 1997 Honoree, Howard Ganek. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Howard and Judy, their children and grandchildren.
