
        COULTER - Michael. Michael passed away on December 27, 1997 after a brief illness. Michael was born March 19, 1943. He was involved in the arts for most of his life, assisting, nurturing and supporting artists like Jack Smith, Andres Serrano and Laurence Fishburne. Although originally from the Bronx, Michael spent most of his life on the Lower East Side. Michael's spirit will be greatly missed by all of his friends. A Memorial Service will be Friday 11AM, Jan 2, 1998, at the Marcus Jackson FH, 132 St, 8th Ave NYC.
