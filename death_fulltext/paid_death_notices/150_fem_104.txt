
        MILLER-Marian M. The daughter of Robert Watt Miller and Elizabeth Folger Miller, died on Saturday, July 3, 1999, after a short illness. She was born on July 9, 1930. Marian had been living in Manhattan and Bridgehampton for the past thirteen years. Marian, nature and dog lover, spent her last years at her beach house in Bridgehampton. She is survived by her children, Ford Edwards, Hammond Edwards, Geoffrey Edwards, Christie Jones, and Janie Manet.
