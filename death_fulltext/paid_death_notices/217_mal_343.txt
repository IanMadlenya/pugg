
        GRUNWALD--Henry A. Services Tuesday, March 1st, 10 am, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the International Rescue Committee, 122 E. 42 St, NY, NY 10168; or the Lighthouse International, 111 E. 59 St, NY, NY 10022.
        GRUNWALD--Henry A. In sorrow, Congreation EmanuEl of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member, Henry A. Grunwald. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Secy
