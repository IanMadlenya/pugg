
        STOLZENBERG-Gussie. On February 27, 1997 in NYC where she lived for eighty-three years. Beloved wife of the late Jack. She will be remembered by daughter, Arlene and sons Louis and Richard, her 8 grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Services will be held on Sunday, March 2nd, 10 A.M., at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Avenue. Contributions in her memory may be made to The United Jewish Federation of NYC, 212-836-1486. She will be missed.
