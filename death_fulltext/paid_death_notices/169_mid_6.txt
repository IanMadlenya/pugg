
        GORDON-Caroline. On Feb. 22, 2001 in her 94th year. Beloved wife of the late Roy Gordon. Loving mother of Dr. Wayne Gordon and his wife Margaret Brown. Cherished grandmother of Hannah Brown Gordon. Star teacher of the New York City schools. Services Sunday, February 25, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue
        GORDON-Caroline. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted, longtime member Caroline Gordon, widow of the late Roy Gordon. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her son, Wayne, his wife, Margaret Brown, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
