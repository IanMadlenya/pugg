
        DAVIS-Jonas M., DDS. 83. Of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Great Neck, N.Y., on December 7, 1997. Beloved husband of Lillian, loving father of Barbara Haskell, MD; Bruce and Kent Davis. Brother of Rebecca Shore. Also survived by nine grandchildren and a granddaughter. NYU Dental School Class of '37, OKU Nat'l Honor Society and Amalgam Club. He was a kind and gentle man, and will be missed by many.
