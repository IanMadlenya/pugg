
        KWASHA-H. Charles. (Chick). Age 90. Of North Miami, FL. Born in Providence, R.I. December 2, 1906. Passed peacefully on May 4, 1997. Graduated Magna Cum Laude, Sigma Psiand Phi Beta Kappa from Brown University in 1928. Founded the Kwasha-Lipton Company, a pre-eminent actuarial consulting firm, in 1944. His company has provided actuarial services to many Fortune 500 corporations for more than fifty years and was recently purchased by Coopers and Lybrand. He contributed to many charities in his lifetime, among which are Brown University, The Alzheimer's Foundation, Boys Town of Jerusalem and Juvenile Diabetes. Survived by his wife Sylvia H. Kwasha, his children Linda D. Kwasha, Bruce C. Kwasha and Robert D. Kwasha and his seven grandchildren. Funeral service to be held graveside at Mount Nebo Memorial Gardens, 5505 N.W. 3rd St, Miami, Wednesday, 2:45pm. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the Diabetes Research Institute Foundation.
