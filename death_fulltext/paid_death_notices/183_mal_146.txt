
        KARPLUS-Esteban J., 64. Died on April 11, 2002 at home surrounded by his wife Elena and children, Natalia, Esteban, Marisa and Alexandra. He is also survived by his brother Hugo. He was a warm and gentle man with boundless intellectual exuberance. This extraordinary man touched the lives of all he met. A memorial service will be held at his home in Scarsdale on Saturday, April 13th from 1-4 P.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The American Cancer Society.
