
        TIGHE-Robert J., Jr. On September 3, 2001, in Florida, age 51. Beloved husband of Constance, son of Margaret and the late Robert, Sr., brother of Michael, son-in-law of Eleanor Nagle. A native of NYC, he graduated from Fordham Prep, Boston College and received his MBA from NYU. A memorial service to celebrate his life will be held on Saturday, October 13, 2001, at 11AM at St. Ignatius Loyola Church at 84th and Park Ave., NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made in his memory to specific charities. Inquiries may be made to P.O. Box 14608, North Palm Bch, FL 33408.
