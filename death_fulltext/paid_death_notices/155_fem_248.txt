
        SCHIFF-Jennie. 87. Died December 24, 1999, at home. Loving wife of the late Jerry. Beloved mother of Susan Wynn and Robert Schiff and mother-in-law of Susan Schiff. Devoted grandmother of Brad and Christine Wynn, Evan and Elissa Wynn, Jillian and Michael Pohly, Scott and Adam Schiff. Proud great-grandmother of Jeremy, Jacob, Stephanie, Harry, Rachel and Bryan. Contributions may be made in her memory to the American Cancer Society or the American Heart Association.
