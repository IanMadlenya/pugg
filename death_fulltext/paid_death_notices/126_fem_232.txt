
        NOVAK-Esther. Wife, mother, friend, on July 27, 1997, at age 82. She died peacefully after a full and productive life. Her counsel, compassion and love will be sorely missed. She is survived by her husband of 60 years, Abraham, two children, Joel and Isabel, four grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. For information regarding the burial service, please call I.J. Morris Memorial Home, Brooklyn, N.Y.
