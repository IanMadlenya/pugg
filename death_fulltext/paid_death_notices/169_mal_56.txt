
        RICKETTS-James. Actor, songwriter and environmentalist, born May 11, 1948, raised in Weston, CT; died January 27, 2001, in his NYC home. Mr. Ricketts attended the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in NYC. He was a U.S. Army veteran who interrupted his performing career to serve his country in Alaska in the late 1960s. He is most well known as the lead in the musical ''You're a Good Man, Charlie Brown.'' As a songwriter, he is represented on several recordings by Barbara Cook. He wrote the song ''Adam'' for Ms. Cook as a tribute to her son. In addition, his works are sung by many top cabaret performers. Mr. Ricketts was executive assistant at Environmental Defense since 1988. He is survived by his life partner Christopher Kennedy and beloved brother William of Weston, CT. Memorial services will be held at a later date.
