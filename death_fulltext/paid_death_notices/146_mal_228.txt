
        COLLINS-Leonard J. Of Mahwah, NJ, died March 9, 1999, after a long illness. He was a United States Marine Corp. pilot in World War II. Graduate of the University of Pennsylvania, where he was president of the Newman Club and a member of the Sphinx Senior Society. He was president of Park Ryan Inc., New York City, and retired from the Oppenheimer & Co. (1990). Member of NY Bond Club. Parishioner of Immaculate Conception Church in Mahwah, NJ. Survived by his wife, Dr. Margaret Jane Collins, D.D.S. (Sullivan), seven children, Joseph, Denis, Rita, Brian and wife Gina, Therese, Monica and husband James O'Reilly, Sheila and husband Tom Stanford, and three grandchildren. Mass will be celebrated on Saturday, March 13, 1999, at 11 A.M. in Immaculate Conception Church, Mahwah, NJ. Interment will be in Maryrest Cemetery, Mahwah, NJ. Friends will be received on Friday from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 P.M. in the Van Emburgh-Sneider Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Avenue, Ramsey, New Jersey. Donations to the Society for Progressive Supranuclear Palsy, Inc., Johns Hopkins Outpatient Center, Suite 5065, 601 N. Caroline Street, Baltimore, MD 21287 would be appreciated.
