
        PARKER--Norma. September 9, 1929-February 21, 2005. Died at home in Miami Beach after a three year battle with cancer. She is survived by her husband Rus, brother Howard, and son Barry (married to Michele) and grand daughters Rebecca & Carina. Resident of Manhattan in the 1950's-1960's-1970's, before moving to Florida in 1978 where she was a mover and shaker in the burgeoning arts scene. In lieu of flowers, encourage your children to boycott cigarettes.
