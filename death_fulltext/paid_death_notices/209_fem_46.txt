
        CATER--Elizabeth. Beloved mother of Melissa Mari Havercroft, devoted grandmother of William and Chloe, daughter of Irene Blenheim Cater, mother-in-law of Norman Havercroft, died on May 28, 2004 in NYC following a long illness. Her publishing career spanned 40 years, where her creative vision brought about numerous innovations benefiting teachers and students. Her energy and dedication reached beyond the world of publishing and she will be missed by all who knew her. A memorial service will be held 4 PM Monday, June 14, Chapel of the Sacred Heart School, One E. 91 St., NYC. Memorial contributions may be made to Pancreatic Cancer Action Network, 2221 Rosecrans Av., El Segundo, CA 90245.
