
        PLOTKIN-Selda. Dearly beloved daughter of the late Rabbi Benjamin and Helen of Jersey City, NJ. Most cherished and devoted twin sister of Eda. Member of Congregation Emanuel of Jersey City, NJ. Life member of Hadassa, master teacher, beautiful, talented, accomplished and committed to the cause of social justice and striving for a better world. Her vibrant presence has enriched our lives. Services private on June 14, 2001. Contributions to the Rabbi Benjamin Plotkin Memorial Fund at the Jewish Theological Seminary of New York City.
