
        VENEZKY--Richard L. University of Delaware Unidel Professor, June 11, 2004. BEE and MA Cornell University; PhD Stanford University. For over three decades he was an authority on literacy, spelling and educational technology. He was an inspiring teacher, mentor and author. He is survived by his wife, daughter, son, mother and two sisters.
        VENEZKY--Dr. Richard. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of Dr. Richard Venezky, husband of Karen Venezky, our esteemed colleague. Our heartfelt condolences go the the entire family. May his memory be for a blessing. June Walker, National President
