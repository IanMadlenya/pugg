
        GORDON-Ruth. August 26, in her 90th year, leaves Lewis, her beloved husband of 63 years; adored mother of Fred and Charles Richard; mother-in-law of Susan; cherished grandmother of Justin, Jacqueline, Rebecca; niece, Barbara. Ever mindful of her roots, she built her life around supporting numerous institutions promoting the teachings of Judaism. History teacher at Bronx Science, 40 years. A world traveler, enriched her students with the realities she observed. Funeral August 27.
        GORDON-Ruth. It is with great sorrow that the Association of Retired Teachers of the City of New York note the passing of Ruth Gordon, adored wife of our distinguished Treasurer and Board Member, Lewis I. Gordon. We are united as one voice offering our sympathies and condolences to her devoted husband and dear family. Hilda S. Klyde, President
        GORDON-Ruth. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our devoted member Ruth Gordon, beloved wife of I. Lewis Gordon. Deepest sympathy to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice-President Jack Rosen, Vice-President
        GORDON-Ruth. Park East Synagogue Sisterhood sorrowfully mourns the passing of Ruth Gordon, dedicated Board member for many years, and expresses heartfelt sympathy to her beloved husband Lewis and the entire family. Maguy Naparstek, President Beverly Greenberg, Chair
