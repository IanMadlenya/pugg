
        LORBER--Martin A. Of Sands Point, NY. Born August 15, 1940, passed away August 13, 2003. Loving husband of Annette, and devoted father of Stephanie and Fred Rigos, Jacqueline and Gaetano Scannavino, Jonathan, Victoria and Marc Shapiro, Lisa and Gregory Fortunoff, and Eve and Jonathan Winston. Proud and adoring grandfather of Jacob, Gaetano, Alexandra and Paris. Founder, owner and chief operating officer of Worldwide Footwear, Inc., Merrick, NY. Throughout his entire life, he demonstated an infallible strength of character with his family, his business and throughout his illness. This successful, generous and caring man will be dearly missed by all who knew him. Services Friday, 11AM at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
