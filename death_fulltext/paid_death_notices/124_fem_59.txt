
        BLINKEN-Ethel H., died in Palm Beach, Florida on May 7 after a long illness. She was born in New York City in 1903 and married Maurice H. Blinken in 1964. He died in 1986. She is survived by her sons, Ambassador Donald Blinken of Budapest, Robert Blinken of Bedford Hills and Ambassador Alan Blinken of Brussels, as well as nine grandchildren and six great grandchildren. Interment will be private.
