
        LEVIN-Frances (nee Hollander). Age 91, of Palm Beach, Florida and New Jersey died peacefully on April 6, 1999. Beloved wife of the late George and beloved mother of the late Richard. Grandmother of Carol Levin and Marjorie (Charles) Van Dercook. Greatgrandmother of Gina, George, Charlotte, Archie and Rebecca. Funeral services on Friday, April 9 at 11:00AM at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Forest Avenue, Paramus, New Jersey. Contributions in her memory can be made to the United Jewish Appeal or to the Richard Levin Research and Rehabilitation Foundation, One Longfellow Place, Box 1217, Boston, MA. 02114.
