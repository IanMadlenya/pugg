
        CASTRO-Andres. Died on September 2, 2000. Born July 27, 1917, in Havana, Cuba. Founder of the theater company ''Las Mascaras.'' Graduated from the Senior Dramatic Workshop of the New School for Social Research, B.F.A. Emigrated to the U.S. in 1961. Director of the Senior Dramatic Workshop at Carnegie Hall. Founder of the Westside Repertory Theater, which he directed for 25 years. Acting teacher of generations of actors. Married for 50 years to actress Antonia Rey. Visiting hours Monday, 3-5, 7-9 PM, at Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 117 West 72nd Street.
