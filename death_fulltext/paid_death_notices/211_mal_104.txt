
        GOLDE--David W. Board members and friends of The Sass Foundation for Medical Research mourn with deep sorrow the untimely death of dedicated and esteemed Board member, brilliant physician, medical researcher and dear friend, Dr. David Golde. We are grateful to David for his friendship and wisdom and for his over 15 years of service on The Sass Foundation's Medical Advisory Board, with a lifelong dedication to saving lives through cancer research, education and patient care. We offer our deepest condolences and sympathy to his wife, Geri, and daughter, Lea. Martin D. Sass, Chairman Francis P. Arena, President The Sass Foundation for Medical Research
