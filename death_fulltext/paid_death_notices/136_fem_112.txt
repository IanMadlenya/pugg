
        BENINGTON-Mathilde Johnston. Mathilde Johnston Benington died Saturday, May 16, 1998 in Hobe Sound, FL. Beloved wife of the late Arthur, devoted mother of Mathilde and George. Survived also by her sister Molly Brent Lucas, step-sister Edwina Shea Millington, and sister-in-law Edwina Leggett. A memorial service will be held at noon on Tuesday, May 26, 1998 at First Presbyterian Church, New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Hospice of Martin & St. Lucie, Inc. 2030 SE Ocean Blvd., Stuart, FL. 34996.
