
        GARFIELD-Barbara Munson, 85, died at her home in Sarasota, FL, Feb. 20, 1998. Widow of Edward Garfield, former Manhattan attorney. Mother of Gail Garfield Schwartz of Mamaroneck, New York, Susan Garfield of Oakland, CA, Wendy G. Palliser of London, England. Grandmother of five and great-grandmother of four. Remembrances may be made to the American Cancer Society.
