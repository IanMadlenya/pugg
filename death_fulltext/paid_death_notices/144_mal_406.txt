
        JAUL-Robert. Age 75. On January 24. Beloved father of Danny, Claudia and the late Michael. Loving companion of Barbara Heitner. Also survived by his sister Felicitas. Born in Wiener Neustadt, Austria. He immigrated with his parents and sister to New York, co-founded the law practice of Jaul & Strong, and helped many people with his integrity and kindness. Also survived by many dear cousins and friends. He was loved by many. Service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam and 76th, Tuesday, 10 AM.
