
        DANE--Goody. The Conservative Synagogue Adath Israel of Riverdale mourns the passing of Goody Dane, founder, and wife of first president Oscar Dane (z''l). A passionate advocate for children's Jewish education, her home and heart was open to the entire community. Rabbi Barry Dov Katz Susan J. Schwartz, President
        DANE--Goody. Beloved wife of the late Oscar, cherished mother of the late Marsha Stern (late Stanley), Gail Propp (Ephraim), Sharon Dane, and Wilma Kule (Stephen). Adored and adoring grandmother, and greatgrandmother; we looked forward to celebrating your 94th birthday this March 24. We will miss your zest for living, wise counsel, and elegance.
