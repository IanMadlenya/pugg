
        WESCHLER-Milton E. On May 3, 1998. Family and friends join in mourning the sudden passing of Milton E. Weschler, age ninetyone, of Coconut Creek, Florida, and formerly of Great Neck, Long Island. He was healthy and vibrant until the end. He was preceded in death by his loving wife, Estelle, and is survived by his three sons, Stephen, David and Kenneth; his sisters, Isabelle and Muriel, and many loving family members. We celebrate his long and joyous life. Love, His Sons
