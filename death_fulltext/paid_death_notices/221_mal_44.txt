
        SARIS--Frank E. On June 3, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Callie. Loving and most devoted father of Emanuel and George. Dearest fatherin-law of Stephanie. Cherished grandfather of Frankie and Eleni. Legal Counsel and Governing Attorney for the Dodecanesa. General Manager of the Harmonie Club of NYC, Inc., for 34 years. Most generous and helpful to all. A true European gentleman, proud American and New York Yankee fan. Reposing at the Frederick Funeral Home of Flushing, Northern Blvd at 193rd Street. Visiting hours Saturday and Sunday, 2-5 and 7-10 PM. Funeral services Monday, 11:30 AM, St. Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church. Interment Flushing Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to New Horizons Foundation for the Developmentally Disabled, 21 Van Wagner Road, Poughkeepsie, NY 12603.
        SARIS--Frank E. The Harmonie Club of the City of New York notes with deep sadness the passing of Mr. Frank E. Saris, CCM. Mr. Saris was the General Manager of the Club and was beloved by members and staff. His passing is deeply felt and he will truly be missed by all who knew him. A man of passion and professionalism, he will remain forever a part of our family. Friends may call Saturday, June 4, and Sunday, June 5, 2005, at Frederick Funeral Home, 192-15 Northern Blvd, Flushing, Queens, NY. Services will be held Monday, June 6, 2005, at St. Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church, 196-10 Northern Blvd, Flushing, Queens, NY.
