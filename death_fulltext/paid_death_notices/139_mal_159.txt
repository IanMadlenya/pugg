
        HINDELS-Dr. George W. On behalf of the faculty, alumni and students of the Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery (SDOS), Dean Allan J. Formicola expresses his sincere condolences to the family of Dr. George Warren Hindels, specialist in Prosthodontic Dentistry and long-time practicing dentist. Graduating from SDOS in 1943 and a member of the 25 Year Club, Dr. Hindels was a cherished alumnus and faculty member of the School. His contributions to the field or Prosthodontics and to the School's development were significant. He will be sincerly missed by his longtime colleagues. Allan J. Formicola, D.D.S. Dean Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery
