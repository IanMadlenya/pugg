
        WILLIAMS--The Rev. Canon Frederick B., Rector Emeritus of the Church of the Intercession in New York City, died suddenly at his home on April 4. At the time of his death, Canon Williams was a Trustee of the Rockefeller Foundation and Chairman of the Board of Harlem Churches for Community Improvement; he was also a leader of Shared Interest, A Balm In Gilead and PeaceJam. There will be a Vigil for Canon Williams at the Church of the Intercession from 4 to 8 PM on Sunday, April 9; the Bishop of New York will celebrate a Mass of the Resurrection on Monday, April 10, at The Church of the Intercession, 550 West 155th Street (at Broadway) at 11AM. Donations may be made to The Church of The Intercession.
