
        SWEET-Howard Clinton, Jr., 70. Died peacefully on May 28, 2002 in Sandy Beach, Australia. Previously of Charlestown, Rhode Island and Bronxville, New York. Graduated from Brown University with honors in 1954 and The University of Virginia Law School in 1959. He was commissioned in the U.S. Marine Corps. in 1954 and continued in the reserves for 30 years until he retired as Lt. Colonel. His legal practice was carried out in New York and Rhode Island. He is survived by his wife Jan; his two children Elizabeth and Howard and their spouses; his three darling grandchildren Emily, Grace and Adam; his dear sister Lucille; his three stepchildren and their families. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. A memorial service will be held this summer. All inquiries can be directed to 704907-9959
