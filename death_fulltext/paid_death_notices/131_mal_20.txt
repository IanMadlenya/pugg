
        KANDEL-Morton H. The Directors and staff of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York record with deep sorrow the death of Mr. Morton H. Kandel. Mr. Kandel was an active member of the BJE Board for over 40 years and made significant contributions to the agency and Jewish education in general. May his wife Lottie and the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Philip Schatten, President David Mann, Executive Vice President Stephen A. Kramer, Chairman of the Board
