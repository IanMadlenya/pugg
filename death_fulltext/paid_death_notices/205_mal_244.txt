
        HUEBNER--Carl Herman of Chatham, NJ on February 16, 2004. He was instumental in the development of Stuyvesant Town, Peter Cooper Village and Riverton, in NYC during the 1940's. Mr. Huebner had been a Director of the Empire Savings Bank of NY, a Director of the Public Development Corporation, a member of the Mortgage Bankers Association and the Real Estate Board of NY. Husband of the late Helen Strong Huebner, father of H. Douglas Huebner of Newport News, VA, and Karen H. Utzinger of Chatham, NJ. Also survived by four grandchildren and one great-grandson. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, February 21, 2004 at 11 AM at Chatham Presbyterian Church, 240 Southern Blvd, Chatham, NJ. Interment Riverview Cemetery, Essex, CT.
