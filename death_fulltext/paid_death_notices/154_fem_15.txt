
        CHAPLIN-Virginia T. The National Board of the YWCA of the U.S.A. is deeply saddened by the tragic loss of Virginia T. Chaplin, National Board member from 1970 to 1982 and one of its Vice Presidents, a member of the World Service Council from 1982 to the present and President of the YWCA of Lewiston - Auburn, Maine from 1958 to 1962. She will be remembered for her commitment and dedication to the improvement of the quality of life of the women, girls and their families both nationally and globally. The YWCA of the U.S.A., National Board and staff extend their heartfelt condolences to her family. She shall be missed. Alexine Clement Jackson, National President Prema Mathai-Davis, CEO
