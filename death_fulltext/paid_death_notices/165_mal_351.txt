
        RESSLER-Ira Richard. Died on October 27, 2000 in New York City at the age of 78. Beloved husband for 50 years of Dorothy, devoted father and father-in-law of Bruce & Ellen Ressler, Jonathan Ressler, Debra and Leon Black, Richard and Alison Ressler, and Antony Ressler and Jami Gertz, loving brother of Gladys Nachamkin and the late William Ressler, and adored grandfather of Benjamin, Joshua, Alex, Victoria, Matthew, Michael, Samantha, Jillian, Andrew, Rebecca, Oliver, Nicholas & Theo. Ira Ressler was a decorated veteran of the United States Army Air Corps in World War II where he served with distinction, receiving the Air Medal for Bravery and the American Defense Service Medal with Star. He was a respected member of the New York Bar & a highly regarded trial attorney with his own firm, Ressler and Ressler, for 52 years. His proudest achievement was his family-his wife and children, their spouses and grandchildren-around whom his life truly centered. Funeral at 10 A.M., on Sunday, October 29, 2000 at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street.
        RESSLER-Ira. We mourn the loss of a wonderful spirit and most loving and gifted father and grandfather. To his wife, Dorothy, his children and grandchildren, we offer our deepest sympathy. Laurie, Melanie, Elias and Marisa Kefalidis
