
        JAFFEE-Nathan. We mourn the loss of N. Jay Jaffee, renowned photographer, age 78, of Huntington, New York, on March 10, 1999. Beloved father and friend, he was much loved and will be greatly missed. He is survived by his loving companion, Paula Hackeling, and his two children by his first marriage, Anna Jaffee and Cyrisse Jaffee, and two grandchildren, Ashley & Jonathan. Services will be held at the M.A. Connell Funeral Home in Huntington Station on Friday, March 12, at 1 PM, visiting hours start at 12, for immediate family and friends. A memorial service is planned for a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Kidney Cancer Association, 1234 Sherman Avenue, Suite 203, Evanston, IL 60202-1375.
