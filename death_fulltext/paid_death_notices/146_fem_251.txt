
        ROGERS-Pauline Washburn, 79 of Niantic, CT formerly of New York City, died on Friday, March 19, 1999. She is the widow of Francis Day Rogers. A memorial service will be held at 11:00 AM on Friday, April 2, 1999 at the First Congregational Church of Old Lyme, Lyme St, Old Lyme, CT. Donations may be made in her memory to the First Congregational Church of Old Lyme or a charity of the donor's choice.
