
        SHATTEN--Michael Lee, retired General Attorney of Ohio Bell, formerly of Cleveland, OH, died Sunday, April 1, 2007, in Atlanta, GA, at age 88. His career with the Bell System spanned 45 years. He proudly served as a USAF Captain during WWII. Above all, he was devoted to his family. His beloved wife, Arlene Baldwin, whom he wed in 1943, predeceased him in 2000. He is survived by daughter, Jane Flowerree, and granddaughters, Laura and Christine. Cremation Society of the SOUTH, 770-420-5557.
