
        HALBREICH-Florence. Passed away peacefully at home on September 15. Devoted wife of Otto Halbreich who predeceased her in 1980. Loving and doting mother of Irwin ''Sonny'' Halbreich of NYC and Mildred Katleman of Beverly Hills, CA. Proud grandmother of Sanford Herzfeld, Patricia Herzfeld Van Der Leun, Kathy Halbreich Kohring and John Halbreich and great-grandmother to Meredith, Joanna and Jason Herzfeld, Justine Van Der Leun, Henry Kohring and Gillian and Hannah Halbreich. She is also survived by her sisters Ann Finley, Rose Shedlin, Helen Kobel and brother Harold Sheldon, and her devoted companion of nine years, Ning Agustin. In April of this year, this quiet, gracious lady celebrated her 100th birthday surrounded by family and friends at her home at Central Park West, New York City. We will always have loving memories of you. Memorial services will be held Sunday, September 17, at Riverside, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
