
        DROBNER-William S., MD. Of Short Hills, NJ, December 3, 1997 at the age of 37 after a long struggle with cancer. Dr. Drobner was a graduate of Hobart College & the Medical College of Pennsylvania. He completed a residency in Orthopedic Surgery at the Mount Sinai Medical Center, a fellowship in surgery of the hand and upper extremities at The New York University Medical Center & was affiliated with The Hospital For Joint Diseases. He leaves his devoted wife, Wendy Silverstein; four year old twins, Zachary and Rachel; his parents Dr. Virginia Drobner Katz & Sherwin Drobner; a sister Ann; A brother, Richard, and his grandmother Ruth Brodkin. Services will be held today (Friday) at 10:45am at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. Memorial contributions may be made to The William S. Drobner Living Adventure Fund. Care of The Mount Sinai Medical Center, 1 Gustave Levy Place, New York, New York 10029. The fund will benefit young adults battling cancer. The family will observe Shiva Saturday evening, Sunday and Monday evening at the residence in Short Hills.
