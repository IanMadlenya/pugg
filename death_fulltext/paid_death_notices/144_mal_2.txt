
        GALLOP-Luis A. On December 30, 1998 of Myelodysplasia at home in Chapel Hill, NC. Formerly of Old Greenwich, CT and Francestown, NH, he was an advertising executive for many years with Reuben Donnelley, Time-Life Inc., Benton & Bowles and Venture Magazine. Predeceased by his first wife Kathryn N. Gallop and a daughter Nancy Cristman Thoman. He is survived by his wife, Dr. Maggie R. Matthews of Chapel Hill, NC; Deborah Cristman McMullen of London, England, Dana Cristman Eaton of Sunnyvale, CA, Anne Gallop Pace of Sudbury, MA, Charles Andrew Gallop of Winchester, MA and eight grandchildren.
