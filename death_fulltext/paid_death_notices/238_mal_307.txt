
        NEILSEN--Craig H. The faculty and staff of the Department of Rehabilitation Medicine, Mount Sinai School of Medicine, mourns the untimely passing of Craig H. Neilsen, Chairman and CEO of Ameristar Casinos, owner of Neilsen and Company and founder of the Craig H. Neilsen Foundation. Living with the consequences of severe spinal cord injury, he was a man of extraordinary accomplishments and generosity to whom we are profoundly grateful. Kristjan T. Ragnarsson, M.D. Lucy G. Moses Professor and Chairman
