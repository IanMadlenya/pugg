
        LIPSAY-Alan W. Family and friends mourn his death, of cancer, on August 1, 1997 in San Jose, Costa Rica. Mr. Lipsay was born in Brooklyn, N.Y. in 1930, graduated from Lincoln High School, Brooklyn College and Columbia Law School. He raised his family in Manhasset Hills and was active in major New York area real estate projects for over 25 years. He later led an adventurous life in Costa Rica and in Sarasota, Florida while also engaged in real estate and vehicle financing businesses. An avid reader and crossword aficionado, he had great appreciation for the stylish use of the English language. He is survived by his sons, Seth and Craig, his granddaughters, Lauren and Allison, his sister, Ciceil L. Gross and his ex-wife, Florine Lipsay. The funeral was private. Donations may be made to Dorot, 171 West 85th Street, New York 10024, a charity benefiting the elderly and homeless.
