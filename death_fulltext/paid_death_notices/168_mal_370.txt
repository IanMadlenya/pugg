
        O'SULLIVAN-Rev. Martin J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired chaplain of Ozanam Hall Residence on January 22. Reposing in Ozanam Hall, 42-41 201 St, Bayside, Thursday, January 25 from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Wake service at Ozanam Hall, January 25 at 8 PM. Funeral Mass at Ozanam Hall, on Friday, January 26 at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
