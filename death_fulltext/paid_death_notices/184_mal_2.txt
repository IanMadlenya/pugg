
        KIRSHEN-Morris. On April 27, 2002 at age 96. He is survived by his children Carl Kirshen and Judith Levine, daughter-in-law Marjorie Kirshen, grandchildren Bob and Jim Levine and Sarah Kirshen, and brothers Harry & Max Kirshen. He was very much loved and will be remembered for his intelligence, his humor, his wealth of knowledge matched only by his modesty, and above all, for the warmth and love that was apparent every time he smiled. A businessman and a scholar, he led a complete life that touched many people. A memorial service will be held Saturday, May 4, at 2 PM, the Ethical Culture Society of Bergen County, 687 Larch Avenue, Teaneck, NJ 07666.
