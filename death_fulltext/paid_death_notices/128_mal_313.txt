
        KOURTAKIS - Emmanuel, in Brooklyn, NY, on September 22, at 79. Cherished husband of Joanna. Devoted father of Evanthia and Kostantinos, and their spouses, Emmanuel and Maria. Adored grandfather of Christina and John. His devotion to his children and sacrifices to ensure their education are deeply appreciated. He was born in Springfield, Mass., but emigrated to Crete, Greece as an infant. After World War II, he returned to start his career in the fur trade. His integrity, good nature and kind heart were known to all. Funeral services will be held on Sunday in his beloved Greece, in the village Astrika, Crete. He will be deeply missed and will live in our hearts forever.
