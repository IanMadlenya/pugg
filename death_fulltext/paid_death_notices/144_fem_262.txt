
        ROSENWALD-Ruth Israels. On January 21, 1999, at her residence in Manhattan, at age 85. Daughter of Henry Goldstein, died 1953, and Elizabeth Traurig Goldstein, died 1985. Widow of Carlos Lindner Israels, died 1969, and William Rosenwald, died 1996. Mother of Michael Jozef Israels. Mother-inlaw of Maija-Sarmite Jansons. Grandmother of Aleksandrs Lehman Israels & Peter Carlos Israels. Stepmother of Nina Rosenwald, Elizabeth R. Varet and Alice R. Sigelman. Memorial Service Tuesday, January 26. 1999, at 12 Noon at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave & 65 St., New York. In lieu of flowers, the family request contributions either to: Congregation EmanueEl of the City of New York, or the Israels Family Fund, at Amherst College, Amherst, Mass.
