
        HOPPIN--Jean Searing, 84, died May 24, 2003 in Summit, NJ. The daughter of the late Hudson Roy and Geraldine Kendrick Searing, she is survived by her husband Charles E. Hoppin, two children, Michael Moore and Meredith Hoppin, and predeceased by her daughter Melinda Moore Pick, and her sister, Doris S. Busch, also survived by four grandchildren. A Memorial service will be held Sunday, June 1st, 2003, at 3 P.M., at Calvary Episcopal Church, Summit, New Jersey. >>AD#
