
        RUDEL-Dr. Nina. Died in NYC on October 26, 2002, in her 91st year. Dearest friend and family. In the 1960s she headed the Child Health Centers in NYC. Her great interest in literature resulted in forming a book club at the Elmhurst Library which enriched the lives of many. She will be greatly missed and lovingly remembered. Services at Parkside Funeral Chapel, Queens, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Tuesday, 1:30 PM.
        RUDEL-Dr. Nina. In loving remembrance for how much you have touched our lives with love, beauty and wisdom. Jane and Paul Varkell
