
        NAGLER--Arnold L., M.D., Ph.D. Age 72, died peacefully on August 11, 2005. Beloved husband of Rosalie, loving father of Stephen M. Nagler, M.D., and Melissa and Andrew Kaufman, adoring grandfather of Madeline and Matthew. He is also survived by his sisters Mollie and Nora, brothers-in-law William and Sidney, and many nieces, nephews and cousins. A distinguished pathologist and hematologist with the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and an inaugural member of the New York College of Osteopathic Medicine faculty and Senior Associate Dean Emeritus and Professor of Pathology. He dedicated his full and vibrant life to his family, friends, colleagues and students. We will all miss him dearly. Contributions can be made in his honor to the American Cancer Society.
