
        RUBIN--Marvin K. November 1, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia. Devoted father of Stella Rubin. Loving brother of Norma Fredrics. Graduate of City College Class of 1938 and NYU College of Dentistry Class of 1942. After graduation, Dr. Rubin served in the US Air Force. He opened his dental office in 1946 and remained in private practice for fifty years. He taught at NYU College of Dentistry and was Chairman of the New York State Council on Dental Education. In retirement, Dr. Rubin pursued his interest in painting and wrote his memoir, ''Word of Mouth''. Memorial service Friday, November 4, 2005, 10AM, at the 92nd St. Y Art Gallery. Donations in his memory may be made to the 92nd Street Y.
