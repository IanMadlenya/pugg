
        HARRINGTON-Mark H., III. Died at home June 25, 1998. Beloved husband of Kyle Good and devoted father of David. Funeral 11:00 AM, Monday, June 29, All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Avenue at 80th Street. Interment at Saint Philip's Church, Garrison, NY for family and friends. Harrington, III Scholarship Fund, c/o Mr. John Hanselman, 124 Mount Auburn Street, Suite 430 North, Cambridge, MA 02138.
        HARRINGTON-Mark. The Faculty, Staff and Alumni of the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism express their sorrow on the loss of a superb journalist and friend. Through his leadership as a producer and executive at CBS and MSNBC, Mark was responsible for pacesetting television journalism. He was an Olympian in intelligence, compassion and dedication. Tom Goldstein, Dean Sandy Padwe, Associate Dean and The Faculty of the Graduate School of Journalism
        HARRINGTON-Mark. The students and parents of the Dalton School, Class of 2004, express their deepest sympathy and sadness at the untimely death of Mark H. Harrington. Mark was a devoted and valued member of the school community and we will miss him greatly. We send our heartfelt condolences to our dear friends, his son David and his wife Kyle.
