
        ACKERMAN--Dr. Martin P., 80, died April 24, 2007. Beloved husband of the late Kitty, father of Linda (Alice) and David (Cassie), stepfather of Lori and Jayni, grandfather of Grant and Grace, and stepgrandfather of Irene and Jordan. Captain, United States Air Force, Dental Corps 19491952. Practiced dentistry for 53 years. Police Department, City of New York Honorary Police Surgeon since 1970. He will be deeply missed by all. Contributions may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
