
        HEISSENBUTTEL--Robert H. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Dr. Robert H. Heissenbuttel, a cardiologist at NewYork - Presbyterian Hospital/Columbia University Medical Center, where he spent his entire medical career. A superb and compassionate clinician, skilled teacher, and dedicated researcher, he was beloved by all. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the Heissenbuttel family. Dr. Herbert Pardes President & CEO Dr. Steven Corwin, Executive Vice President & COO NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital
