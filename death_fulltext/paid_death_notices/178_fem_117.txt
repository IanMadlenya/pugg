
        SYKES-Lois (Farnsworth). 59, of Bronxville, New York, and formerly of New Canaan, CT, and Dallas, TX, died on November 9 at Hammond Pointe Nursing Home in Chestnut Hill, MA, from complications of Lupus. Daughter of the late Kenneth C. Farnsworth, MD, and Marguerite (Pelksy) Farnsworth. Loving mother of Benjamin F. Sykes of Dallas, TX. Dear sister of Barbara F. Fairburn of Greenwich, CT, and Susan F. Spooner of Boston, MA. Cherished aunt of Hilary F. Sullivan of Delray Beach, FL, Lisa F. Clark of Moraga, CA, Scott S. Fabyan of Manchester, MA, Nicholas M. Spooner of New York City, and Amanda S. Frank of Brighton, MA. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, December 8, at 12 noon at the Reformed Church in Bronxville, NY. Memorial contributions in her name may be sent to the Lupus Foundation of America, Inc., 1300 Piccard Drive, Suite 200, Rockville, MD 20850-4303 or to the Reformed Church, PO Box 397, Bronxville, NY 10708.
