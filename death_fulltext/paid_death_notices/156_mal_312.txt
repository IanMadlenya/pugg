
        WRIGHT-William R., on January 19, 2000, of Ridgewood, NJ. Mr. Wright was a Vice President with J.P. Morgan & Co. and President of Morprop Inc. He retired in 1988. Survived by two sons, William R., Jr. and David J., 2 granddaughters, Julie and Alexandra. Also survived by a sister, Dorothy Burris. Funeral services 3 PM, Sunday, January 23, at Christ Church, Ridgewood, New Jersey. Friends may call 2-4 and 7-9, Saturday, at the C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 306 East Ridgewood Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ. Donations to the American Diabetes Association would be greatly appreciated.
