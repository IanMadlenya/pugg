
        STREMEL-J. Michael. Age 34. Died in Manhattan early on New Year's morning, after a long battle with AIDS. Stremel served as Fox Searchlight's Vice President in charge of East Coast Production, where he was the executive behind the Academy Award-winning ''Boys Don't Cry'', ''Whiteboys'' and ''Slums of Beverly Hills''. He ran Searchlight's presence in New York for four years. Stremel is survived by his parents Michael Stremel, Sr. and Christine Knutte, his brothers Mark & Tony, sisterin-law Suzanne, and niece Sophia. He is also survived by his loving friends Peter Parnell, Mark Tusk, Lane Janger, Todd Rubin, Bob and Carrie Thomas, Mark Chambers and many others.
