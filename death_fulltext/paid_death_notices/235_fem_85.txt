
        MAH--Dr. Caroline. The community of Weill Cornell, NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital & Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center mourns the untimely passing of our beloved colleague, Dr. Caroline Mah. A gifted, dedicated physician, an inspiring teacher, and a lover of life, she was a constant reminder of how fortunate we are to be in the healing professions. We are grateful for the chance we had to work with her and bask in her friendship. Our hearts go out to her husband Donald, and to her family. Department of Medicine, Internal Medicine Residency, and Center for Molecular Cardiology Weill Cornell Medical College
