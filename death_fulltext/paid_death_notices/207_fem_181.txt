
        CARTER--Susan M. 76, died on April 21, 2004, of emphysema. Beloved wife of Frederic D. Carter Jr. for 53 years. She is also survived by her daughter Alice Shackelford Carter, her son Frederic D. Carter III, her daughter-in-law Andrea Husher Carter, and two grandsons Derek and Douglas. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, April 29, 11 am, at St. Peter's Church Lithgow, Millbrook, NY. In lieu of flowers gifts to the Dutchess Land Conservancy, 2908 Route 44, Millbrook, NY 12545, would be appreciated.
