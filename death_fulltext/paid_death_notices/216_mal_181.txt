
        BOROWIK--Thomas G. Died January 14, 2005 after a battle with cancer. He was born on June 29, 1929 in Warsaw, Poland. He came to America in 1941, he attended Yale, served in the military from 1950-52. He became a successful business owner and philanthropist. He is survived by his sister Joan, his children Karen, Alexandra and Paul; his grandchildren Olivia, Louisa, Sebastian and Sarah. Please make donations to Save the Children in lieu of flowers. A memorial service will be announced.
