
        STEIN-Fred M., Jr. Age 92. Died on August 22, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Hellman Stein; father of Barbara S. Scott and Virginia S. Greene; grandfather of Andrew, Lynn and Susan Greene, and Timothy, David and Gordon Scott and three great-grandchildren. His warmth, humor and hospitality brought enjoyment to all who knew him. Memorial services private. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to the Margaret Stein Memorial Fund, Visiting Nurse Service of NY, 107 E. 70 St, NYC 10021.
