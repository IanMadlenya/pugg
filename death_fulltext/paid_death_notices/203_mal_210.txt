
        NEUROHR -- Dr. Ferdinand died November 5 at home in Homosassa, FL. A board-certified prosthodontist, he practiced in Manhattan for over forty years. Active in many dental organizations, president of several; he felt his greatest honor was presidency of the American Academy of Restorative Dentistry. He attended Poly Prep in Brooklyn and donated a science lab in memory of his father and brother. Ferd served in the Navy, active and reserve, 1944-71. He is survived by his wife of fifty-three years, Barbara, three sons, Ferdinand III, Bruce and John, and eight grandchildren.
