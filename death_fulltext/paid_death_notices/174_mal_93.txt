
        MORGAN-Edwin D. Died June 28. A memorial service will be held at 10:30 AM, Wednesday, July 11, at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave, at 51 St. Eddie served with the 1st Marine Divison in Guadalcanal, New Guinea, New Britain and Peleliu. He founded Holographics, Inc., which developed useful laser technologies. In lieu of flowers send donations to City Parks Foundation, Monuments Conservation Program, Attn: Jonathan Kuhn, Parks and Recreation, Arsenal, Central Park, NY.
