
        PUFFER-James Chadbourne, 65. Died Thursday, December 6, 2001 in Wellington, FL. He was the son of James C. Puffer and Margaret Davidson Puffer. Mr. Puffer was graduated from the University of Vermont and was a member of Sigma Alpha Epsilon Fraternity. Mr. Puffer was formerly an executive with 20th Century Fox. He is survived by his wife, Karen Weygand Puffer, his sister Mary ''Pat'' Scott and her two sons, James and Charles, of Sudbury, MA. Burial will be held privately at the Puffer family plot at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery in St. Johnsbury, VT. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Margaret Davidson Puffer Educational Fund, Braintree, MA or to The American Cancer Society, POB 79604, Baltimore, MD 21279-0604, or to Hospice of Palm Beach Co., 5300 East Ave., W. Palm Beach, FL 33407.
