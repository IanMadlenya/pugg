Retired in 2003 as VP from Warrior Express, a company he co-founded. Survived by his wife Geraldine, father of Kent and Paige, step-father of James, Deirdre and Maura, brother of Nancy, grandfather of Caroline, Noelle, Tara and Kaylie.
