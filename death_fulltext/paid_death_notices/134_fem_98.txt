
        CANFIELD-Hope Brown. The Trustees of St. Andrew's Dune Church mourn the death of Hope Brown Canfield, beloved and wonderful wife of our Trustee of 21 years and Trustee Emeritus, Franklin O. Canfield. We look forward to rejoicing in the gift of her life and praying for her soul at a memorial service at St. Andrew's Dune Church in Southampton, Saturday, June 13, 1998 at 5 P.M.
