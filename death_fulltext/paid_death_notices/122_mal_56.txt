
        ENRIGHT-Thomas E., Jr. Retired New York City Police Sergeant of Manhattan, age 68. Beloved uncle of Catherine Muldoon, John, Daniel and Martin O'Brien. His grandfather was Richard Enright, former Police Commissioner of New York City Police Department. His father was Thomas Enright, Sr., former Police Lieutenant, New York City Police Department. His mother was Marcella Enright. Loving brother of Winifred O'Brien. He was a member of the New York Athletic Club. Visiting at Adams-Cordovano Funeral Home, 15 Church Street, Carmel, N.Y. (off Rte 6), Friday 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Saturday 10 AM at St. James the Apostle Church, Carmel, N.Y. Interment St. Lawrence O'Toole Cemetery, Brewster, New York.
