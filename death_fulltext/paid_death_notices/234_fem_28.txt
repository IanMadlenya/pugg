
        MEANS - Rosaline, retired professor at CUNY in Brooklyn and widow of the late Cyril C. Means, Jr., passed away on July 3, 2006. She is survived by her three children, Elizabeth Rose Means, Annette Thayer Means and Cyril C. Means III. She loved music; a pianist and a singer, she was a longtime member of the Leidenkranz Singing Club in Manhattan. Family will be receiving Thursday 2-4PM and 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, Madison Ave. at 81st St. Funeral service Friday 10AM, Church of the Resurrection. Burial at Calverton National Cemetery
