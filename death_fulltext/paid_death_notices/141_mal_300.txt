
        RAND-Harry. Of White Plains, N.Y. On October 24, 1998. A retired executive in the office of Rockefeller Family and Associates. Retired Lt. Col. Public Relations Manager, U.S.A., for KLM Royal Dutch Airlines. He is survived by his wife, Louise Rand, two daughters Lark Shlimbaum, Susan Joyce Braun, one granddaughter and two great grandchildren. Funeral service Thursday, 11AM at Saint Bartholomews Episcopal Church, White Plains, N.Y. Interment Arlington National Cemetery. Friends may call at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, N.Y. Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9PM.
