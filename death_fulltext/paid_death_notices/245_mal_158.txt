
        SPEYER--Samuel, of Greenport, New York, in his 93rd year. Devoted husband for 63 years of the late Irma Speyer, father of Jane and Jonathan Weber of Nashua, NH, Dr. James and Karen Speyer of Manhattan, and Margaret and Paul Poster of Scarsdale. Proud grandfather of Elizabeth, Ted, Julia, Andrew, Emily, Nick, Katie Rose, Ben and Michael. Great-grandfather of Amira, Avery and Bianca. A lover of reading, travel, Boston cream pie and a good single malt, he was regarded by his extended family, his clients from over 60 years of public accounting and his friends at Peconic Landing, above all, as a kind and caring man who always had time for others. Funeral 12 noon, June 15 at HortonMathie Funeral Home, Greenport, New York. In lieu of flowers the family requests donation to Eastern Long Island Hospital or the NYU Clinical Cancer Center, 160 East 34th Street, New York, NY 10016.
