
        WATERS--Dorothy F. (Hoffman), 84, Of Holiday, Florida. Formerly of the Bronx. On August 16 surrounded by her family after a courageous battle with cancer. Wife of Michael J. Waters of Manhattan, worked for AT & T for 35 years retiring in 1981. Survived by daughter, Mae Nohilly of CT. Beloved grandmother to Mae Archacki of CT, Thomas Nohilly of N.Y., Dorothy Buslewicz & Michael Nohilly of CT. Also survived by sister Lillian Grattan of N.J. & 9 great-grandchildren. Funeral services were held at Gate of Heaven in Hawthorne, New York. She will be loved and missed always.
