
        BARDA -- Clifford James. Born May 4, 1935, Alexandria, Egypt. Died May 4, 2005, Seattle, WA. He was beloved and is mourned by his wife, Susan Tomita, his daughters, Justine Barda and Jennifer Barda, all of Seattle, stepson, Jason Rabitt-Tomita of Los Angeles, CA, and brother, Donald Barda (Barbara) of Laguna Beach, CA. At a memorial service on May 7, family and friends paid tribute to a vibrant life that was the embodiment of generosity and kindness, of erudition, humor, and worldliness.
