
        VALLAS-DENNIS-Catherine. On May 28, 1999. Devoted and loving mother, wife, sister, Yia-Yia and aunt. Daughter of the late Vovo and Nicholas Vallas and adored wife of the late Paul Dennis. Beloved mother of Dean and his wife Debra, and sister of Venus Dimou, Bess and her husband John Papandon and George and his wife Monica Vallas. Cherished Yia-Yia of Zoe and Sophie and caring aunt of Dean, John Jr., Bittie and John III, Stephen and Laura, Alexi and Jen, Liz, Greg and Eric and Nicholas. Distinguished and dedicated employee of Bloomingdale's for over 40 years. As a buyer, department manager and fashion coordinator she among others was responsible for the store's glory days. Following her success at Bloomingdale's she launched and managed the boutique at the Evelyn Lauder Breast Cancer Center at Memorial Sloan-Kettering. Enthusiastic and committed volunteer with The Metropolitan Opera, the Costume Institute of the Metropolitan Museum of Art and The Guggenheim Museum. Avid traveler, museum goer, dancer and participant in life's cultural joys. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 Street, Monday, May 31 from 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral service Tuesday, 11AM at The Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74 Street. In lieu of flowers, constributions may be made in her memory to The Evelyn Lauder Breast Cancer Center of Memorial Sloan-Kettering. She will be sorely missed.
