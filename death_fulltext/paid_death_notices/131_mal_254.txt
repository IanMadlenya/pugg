
        STILLMAN-Edgar. Dec. 16, 1997. Scholar and teacher of English at the Elizabeth Irwin School and the Scarborough School. Husband of the late Joan Pratt. He is survived by his children, Madeline Iswalt, Joseph Stillman, Jaffray Cuyler, Susanna Cuyler, his grandchildren, Eli, Samuel, Richard and Mary. Memorial service Monday, December 22, 2:30PM, St. Paul's Chapel, 117 and Amsterdam Avenue.
