
        HIRSCH--Roberta (nee Lazarus). On September 24, at age 62. Beloved wife of Jack. Mother of Janet and Josh, and mother-in-law of Dara. Adoring grandmother of Michaela. Passed peacefully at her home after courageously facing cancer. She will be missed dearly by her family and friends who meant so much to her. Her kindness, humor and warmth touched countless lives. Roberta was a compassionate person; her memory may be honored by supporting the Democrats this Novmeber.
