
        KAPSALIS-Mary. Suddenly on Mar. 11, 2002. Beloved daughter of the late Louis & Calliope Kapsalis. Retired proprietor of ''The Barber Is A Lady'', & was a distinguished writer. Survived by her brother Gregory, and Chyrsoula Kapsalis, and her sister Helen and Wayne Grievo. Loving aunt of James, Leonard and Susan; and grandnephew Daniel.
