
        KORNBLUTH--Marvin ''Buddy.'' Born February 14, 1916, passed away September 3, 2003, on Monhegan Island, ME. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Frances Schachter Kornbluth, son Bruce, daughter Jane, grandson Ian, brother Robert and in-laws Jan and Arlene. He resided in North Grosvenordale, CT, and Monhegan. He served as a Merchant Marine and was a 1st Lt. in the U.S. Army WWII-ETO. He lived a full life fishing, boating, building and representing toy manufacturers. He had a big heart. He will be missed.
