
        OLER-Wesley M., III, MD. Age 80, on Tuesday, July 7, 1998. Beloved husband of Virginia Oler of Maplewood Park Place, Bethesda, MD, and beloved father of Wesley M. Oler, IV of Greenwich, CT. and Stephen S. Oler of Wellesley, MA. He is also survived by two brothers: William H. Oler and the Reverend Clarke K. Oler and by four grandchildren. A Memorial Service is planned for later in the Fall. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Wesley Oler Education Fund for Medical Residents c/o Georgetown University Medical Center, Office of Development, 2233 Wisconsin Ave., N.W., Suite 317, Washington, D.C. 20007.
