
        DE PEYSTER--James Abercrombie, age 89, died in Palm Beach, FL on November 7. He was the son of Frederic Ashton de Peyster and Alice Abercrombie Miller de Peyster. A descendant of Johannes de Peyster who came to New Amsterdam in 1645, he was of the 12th generation to be born on Manhattan Island. Survived by his wife of 67 years, Dorothy Shelby Siems de Peyster; three children: James A. de Peyster Jr., Shelby de Peyster Wyckoff and F. Ashton de Peyster III; four grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Memorial service at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda-by-theSea, Palm Beach, Saturday, November 26 at 10:30 AM.
