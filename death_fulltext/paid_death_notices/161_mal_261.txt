
        BROWN-Helen W., 87, of Lloyd Harbor, New York, on June 20, 2000. Survived by David W. Brown, husband for 58 years, friends for 75. Also survived by daughter Constance Pierce of Manassas, Virgina, son Arthur W. Brown of South Kent, Connecticut, his wife Mary and their sons Josh and Alex. A Memorial Service will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church, Cold Spring Harbor, New York, Friday, July 28 at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to North Shore Holiday House, Visiting Nurse Services of Huntington Township, or St. John's Church Outreach Program, would be appreciated.
