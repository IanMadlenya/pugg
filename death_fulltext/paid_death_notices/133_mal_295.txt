
        KELLY-William E. II, age 68, of Spring Lake, New Jersey, died on February 18, 1998 at his home in Spring Lake, New Jersey. He had been hospitalized at ColumbiaPresbyterian Hospital. Born in Brooklyn, NY, graduate of Brooklyn Preparatory, Columbia University and Columbia University Law School; former partner at Lane & Mittendorf LLP, NYC; predeceased in death by his parents, Edward L. and Edna Flannery Kelly; survived by his wife of 43 years, Grace Saunders Kelly, of Spring Lake, NJ; eight children, Edward (Alison) of Sea Girt, NJ; William E. III (Diane) of Rumson, NJ; Robert of Spring Lake, NJ; Richard (Marybeth) of Brielle, NJ; Ellen Gerard (William) of Glen Ridge, NJ; Elizabeth Lally (Joseph) of St. Paul, Minnesota; Elaine Giuliano (William) of McLean, Virginia and Michael (Patricia) of Chatham, NJ; and seventeen grandchildren. He is also survived by a sister, Maura Patricia Kelly of Alexandria, Virginia. For information contact O'Brien Funeral Home in Wall, NJ. Mass of Christian Burial will be at St. Catharine's Roman Catholic Church, Spring Lake, NJ on Saturday, February 21st, 1998, at 10:30 A.M. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be sent to the William E. Kelly Memorial Fund for Medical Research, C/O Fisher Fallon LLP, 301 Morris Avenue, Spring Lake, New Jersey 07762.
