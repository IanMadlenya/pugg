
        DAVIS-Anne. The Department of Medicine of NYU School of Medicine notes with sadness the death of our colleague Dr. Anne L. Davis, an Associate Professor of Medicine, and a full time faculty member for almost 35 years. She was to have received the ALA 2003 Will Ross Medal at the ATS International Conference in May. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her husband and children. Martin J. Blaser, M.D. Chairman
