
        DILLOF-Carolyn. On January 22, 2001, age 52, after a brief battle with pancreatic cancer. She was born in Philadelphia, where she received her R.N. from Philadelphia General Hospital School of Nursing. She had been a resident of Great Neck, NY for 28 years and an Assistant Librarian at the Lakeville Elementary School for the past 12 years. She is survived by her daughter and son, Emily and Thomas Dillof, her mother Jacqueline Frank, and her siblings Jim Bell, Sheila Hutnick, Dan Bell, Christine Ockenlander and Jeanne DiLeonardo. A memorial servicwill be held on Saturday, January 27, 2001 at 1:00 p.m. at the Unitarian Universalist Congregation at 48 Shelter Rock Rd., Manhasset, NY. In lieu of flowers please send donations in Carolyn's name to either Planned Parenthood of Nassau County, 540 Fulton Ave., Hempstead, NY 11550 or GMHC, 119 West 24 St., NY, NY 10011.
