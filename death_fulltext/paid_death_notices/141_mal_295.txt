
        FISHEL-Stanley I. Age 84. On October 25, 1998. Former chairman of Fairfax Advertising Agency, a division of Saatchi & Saatchi/ Worldwide. Graduate of Columbia College 1934. World War II Lt. Commander U.S. Coast Guard. Former National President of Zeta Beta Tau. Founding director of the Fraternity's student loans and scholarships. Named Interfraternity ''Man of the Year'' by the National Interfraternity Conference. Beloved brother of James. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison ave at 81 St, Wednesday, 10am. Interment Mt. Judah Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to ZBT Foundation, Fishel Fellowship Fund, 3905 Vincennes Road, Suite 101, Indianapolis, IN 46268.
        FISHEL-Stanley I. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Stanley I. Fishel. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        FISHEL-Stanley I. The members of the Supreme Council of Zeta Beta Tau, and the board of directors of the Zeta Beta Tau Foundation, note his passing with great sadness. He truly earned, and richly deserved, his sobriquet, ''Mr. ZBT''. The Fraternity extends its condolences to his family as we mourn the passing of a true and loyal Brother.
