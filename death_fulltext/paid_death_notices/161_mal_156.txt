
        SAGE-Arthur P. Born November 20, 1940. Husband of Earline Sage, step-father of Dr. Nicole Alford of Maryland and Robert Alford of Brooklyn. Son of Faye Sage of Forest Hills. Senior Grievance Advocate of Teamster Local 237. Founding member, past President and 1995 Man of the Year of Civil Service Bar Association. Member of B'Nai Brith. Attended NYU at age 15. Graduated with BA and JD. Former Assistant to the General Counsel of HRA. Loved the arts, opera afficionado, and Shakespeare buff. Tennis fan. He celebrated life. Viewing June 14 at the Riverside Chapel, 180 W 76 St, 6-9pm. Services Thursday, June 15 at 11:30am, Riverside Chapel.
        SAGE-Arthur. Local 237 Teamsters mourns the passing of a dear friend and colleague, Arthur Sage, a long time Civil Service Bar Association business agent and former Chairman. Visiting at Riverside Chapels, 180 W. 76 St, N.Y. Wednesday, 6-9PM, service Thursday, 11:30AM. We extend our sympathy to his wife Lee and family. Carl Haynes, President
