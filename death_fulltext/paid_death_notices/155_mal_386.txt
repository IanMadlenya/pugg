
        WYON-Reginald F.. On December 27, 1999, at age 92. Husband of the late Olive Wyon. Survived by nieces Suzanne Ghonim of Australia and Paula Wyon-Brown of England. Also survived by sister Ena Johnson of England. He was a member of the St. Georges Club and the Circumnavigator's Club and a veteran of World War II (Lieut. Col., U.S. Army, Retired). Services Bryant Funeral Home, Setauket, New York.
