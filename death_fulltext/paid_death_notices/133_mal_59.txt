
        GLOVER-Stuart. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Stuart (Bus) Glover. A member since 1931, he was a former Board Member and Officer. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to his sons, Jim and Dick Glover and their families. Andrew J. Frankel, President Judith Weil, Secretary
