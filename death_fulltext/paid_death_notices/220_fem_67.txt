
        HOYT--Lyn. Aunt Lyn died peacefully in Manchester, Vermont on May 7, 2005. Born in England on February 17, 1912, Lyn was the widow of Winthrop Hoyt whom she met in London during WW II and subsequently married. Longtime residents of New York City, they moved to Manchester permanently in 1967. She is survived by her three nephews, Charles E. Hoyt of Washington, D.C., Anthony S. Hoyt of New York City and William W. Hoyt of Hamilton, MA as well as nine great nieces and nephews and five great, great nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Ekwanok Scholarship Trust, Inc., POB 467, Manchester, VT 05254, would be appreciated.
