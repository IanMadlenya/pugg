
        HALLOCK-Beatrice Robertson Culver. On January 26th, 1998. A life-long resident of Southampton, Beatrice Hallock died yesterday at the age of seventy-eight. She was the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Clayton King Culver of Southampton. She is survived by her children, Mrs. James H. Logan of Barnsley, England, David Horace Hallock III of Huntington, Indiana, Peter Leslie Hallock of Southampton and New York City, and four grandchildren: Chelsea, Corinne Hallock of Huntington, Indiana, Sarah Elizabeth Logan, James David Phillips Logan and Jessica Culver Logan, all residing in England. Donations may be made to Southampton Hospital.
