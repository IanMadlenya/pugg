
        SCHULTE--David A. Jr. On Sunday, November 13th, 2005, at age 88. Beloved husband of Sandra Schulte, loving father of Holly Schulte Ellison, Christina Fleur and William Thatcher Dowell, and adored grandfather. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, November 15th, 1:30 pm. Interment private.
