
        MORIN--Gene, 72. Died October 9, 2006. Award winning ad agency art director for major fashion and cosmetics houses, with a second, parallel career in american antiques. Gene was also a freelance design consultant. An acclaimed photo stylist for Family Circle and other magazines, catalog retailers and The New York Times, Gene was a published photographer and contributor to several shelter and lifestyle magazines. Gene is survived by a sister Jeannine Smith, of Nashua, NH, several nieces and nephews, and a circle of loving friends who will miss him dearly. Memorial service Thursday November 9th at 3pm at the Church of St. Luke's in the Fields, 487 Hudson St, NYC. Donations in Gene's memory may be made to St. Luke's Garden Fund or to the Central Park Conservancy.
