
        HALPER-Oscar. Beloved husband of Beatrice for 45 years, adored father of Harlan, cherished grandfather of Rachel & Jennifer, and revered brother of Aaron, passed away on November 13 in Yonkers, NY at the age of 88. A graduate of NYU and the Columbia School of Social Work, where he received a MSW degree, he went on to successful careers in Academia and Business. He served for 26 years as a member and past President of the Board of Directors of the 1150 Park Avenue Tenants Association. A proponent of Reformed Judaism, he served on the Board of Temple Israel of the City of New York, and has been a member of the congregation since 1967. For all who knew him, both past and present, he will be dearly missed and always loved. Services will be held on Thursday, November 16 at 12 noon ''Gutterman's Inc'', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, L.I. Donations may be made in his memory to Temple Israel of the City of New York, 112 East 75 Street, NY, NY 10021-3297.
