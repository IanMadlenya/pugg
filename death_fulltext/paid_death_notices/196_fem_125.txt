
        CONSOR-Carolyn M. Passed away on Thursday, May 15, 2003. Beloved wife of James Consor and loving mother of Maggie and Martha Consor, she is also survived by her brother, Frank O'Donnell III of Atlanta, GA. Longtime resident of New York City, Lyn grew up in Larchmont, NY, and graduated from Chestnut Hill College in Philadelphia, PA. She began her career as production coordinator of The Dick Cavett Show. She also served as office manager of the New York Women's Foundaiton and, more recently, coordinated development and alumnae activities at the Chapin School. Lyn will forever be remembered for her grace and courage, her gentle wit and ready laugh, her empathy and her endless capacity to love. She had a gift for turning strangers into friends. She will be greatly missed by all those whose lives she touched. Her funeral will be held on May 23, at 2 PM, at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine on Amsterdam Avenue and 111th Street.
