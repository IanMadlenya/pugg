
        BARNETT-Evelyn Safran, 87, died on the 29th of June, 1997 in Bethesda, Maryland. A long time resident of New York City, she was a graduate of Barnard College, class of 1930. Devoted wife of Isaac ''Frecks'' Margolis Barnett, cherished mother of Barbara Wing and Patricia Karlsen, adored grandmother of Audrey Lipps, Marjorie Hirsch, Katherine Simon and David Wing, she took great pride in her 9 great-grandchildren. She was a volunteer for Lenox Hill Hospital, New York Public Library and Young Concert Artists. Contributions in her memory may be made to Young Concert Artists, 250 West 57th Street, Suite 1222, New York, New York 10019.
