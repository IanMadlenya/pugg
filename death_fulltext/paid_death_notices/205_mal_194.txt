
        KULLMAN--Lawrence. On February 14, 2004. Originally from Newark, NJ, currently from Manhattan. Survived by his wife Ruth Heber, his sons Seth and Michael, stepchildren Ron, Sheldon and Lorraine, as well as grandchildren Alex, Jason, Jenna, Alex and Audrey. His love of life, family, friends and the arts will be remembered and missed by all. Service Monday, 2 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in his memory to the Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation.
