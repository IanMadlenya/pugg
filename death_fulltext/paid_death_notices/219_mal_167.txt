
        BERNSTEIN--George Alexander. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, April 20, 7-10 pm, 15 Waterside Plaza in Manhattan. George died on March 30, 2005, at 89. The former WWII Army Captain and broadcaster was a Vice President at Morgan Stanley. He is survived by sons, Adam and Anthony, daughter-in-law Janice, and grandchildren Ogden and Astrid. www.georgebernstein.com
