
        EBEL-Rose D. 87, of Yonkers, NY. She entered into rest peacefully on June 29, 2002. Devoted wife of the late Dr. Alfred Ebel. Loving mother of Judith Ebel Tsipis and Deborah Ebel. Proud grandmother of Andreas Jeremy Tsipis, Jessica Morrow Young, Yanni Kosta Tsipis, David Howell Young. She was a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Hunter College. She taught math and German in the NY Public Schools. She will be missed by family and friends. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Andreas Tsipis Genetic Counseling Scholarship Fund, c/o Biology Department, Brandeis University, Waltham, MA 02454. Funeral service will be on Monday, 12 noon, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
