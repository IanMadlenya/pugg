
        MERRILL - Helen. Theatrical agent of distinction died on August 18, 1997 of lung cancer after a brief hospitalization. She was born in Cologne Germany in 1918 and came to the United States in 1939. She is survived by her sister Angelica Merrill Wender of New York City; her brother Hervey C. Merrill of Rochester NY and six nephews and nieces. Funeral services will be private and a memorial service will be announced. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
