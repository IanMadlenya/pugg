
        RUDINGER-Gerda (nee Knegtmans). 82, died August 27, 1998 after a long struggle with multiple myeloma. Lived in Armonk, NY for over 40 years. A freedom fighter in the Dutch Underground 194145, married her Viennese sweetheart, then a sergeant in the U.S. Army. A strong and giving woman with a vibrant love of life she will be sorely missed by her husband Ernest, son John, daughter Elizabeth Salon, nieces, nephew, granchildren and innumerable friends. A memorial service will be held October 12 at 11 AM, in the Bedford Presbyterian Church.
