
        CUSTIN-Mildred, 91 years old, died at her home in Palm Beach, FL, on March 27, 1997. She is survived by her nieces, Joan V. Custin of N.Y., N.Y. and Barbara (Mrs. Philip) Poverman of Chestnut Hill, MA; a great niece, Myra D. Poverman and two great nephews, Harold and Steven Poverman of Boston, MA; and a dear friend, Jerrie Rosenberg of Palm Beach. Burial will take place in Boston, MA and a Memorial Service will be held in Palm Beach, FL. Contributions may be made in her memory to either the Mildred Custin Scholarship Fund, Simmons College, 300 The Fenway, Boston MA, 01225 or to the Fashion Institute of Technology, Seventh Ave. and 27th Street N.Y., NY. 10001. For information contact: Quattlebaum-HollemanBurse Funeral Home, 1201 So. Olive Ave., West Palm Beach, FL.
