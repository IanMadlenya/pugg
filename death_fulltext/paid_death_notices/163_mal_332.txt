
        ZIMMERMAN-Max. August 27, 2000, in his 90th year. Devoted son, sibling, husband of the late Kate, father of Rita, and the late Joel, grandfather of Charles and Marian. Imbued with a love of, and respect for the young, as well as deeply involved in a life-long quest for social and economic justice, Max's life and values serve as an example to be cited and remembered. Funeral services on Wednesday, August 30th, 2000, at 11 A.M. at Mount Nebo Kendall Memorial Gardens, 5900 S.W. 77th Avenue, Miami, FL.
