
        WITT-Evelyn. On October 2, 2000 at age 90. Beloved wife of the late Ira. Loving mother of Stuart and grandmother of Jenny. Former Executive Secretary to Dean Mathey. As a Gray Lady during WWII, she comforted the troops when they returned from battle. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Wednesday, October 4th, at 12 Noon.
