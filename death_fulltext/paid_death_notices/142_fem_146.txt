
        BERNSTEIN-Clara (nee Handelman). Died on November 16th, age 83, in Goshen, NY. Formerly of Eastchester, NY. She is survived by her loving family: her husband of 57 years, Dr. Alfred J. Bernstein, daughter Anne of Berkeley, CA., son Andrew of Wayne, NJ, brother Herbert Handelman of Boca Raton, FL, daughter-in-law Linda, son-in-law Conn Hallinan, grandsons Mark and Greg Bernstein and David Hallinan, stepgrandsons Sean, Antonio, and Brian Hallinan, sisters-in-law Yetta Handelman, Minerva Mandelkern, and Katherine, a large extended family and many friends. Funeral services will be held at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, New York on Thursday, November 19 at 1 P.M., followed by interment at the Mount Hebron Cemetery.
