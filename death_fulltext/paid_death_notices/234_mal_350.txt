
        BERKOWITZ--Sol. Professor, age 84, on July 29. Preceded in death by his beloved wife Pearl, brother David, and sister Sophie Grayson. Devoted uncle and great-uncle, beloved and esteemed teacher, colleague, mentor and friend. Accomplished Broadway and televison composer, author, and revered professor at Queens College. Funeral service 12:30 pm, Tuesday, August 1, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society, the Aaron Copeland School of Music at Queens College, PBS or National Public Radio.
