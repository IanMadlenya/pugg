
        SUBKOFF-Dorothea Bary. On October 21, 1997 at home. Beloved sister of Leona, James and Jeanne. Devoted aunt of Richard and Clair, Michael, David and Reva, Jeffrey and Margaret, Andrew and Karen, and great-aunt. A New York City school teacher for many years, Dot was bright, spirited and had a wonderful sense of humor. Her family and friends will miss her dearly. A memorial service is planned for late November.
