
        TENENBAUM-Herman. Passed away May 13, at the age of 94 in Weston, Florida. Formerly of West End Avenue, he was a survivor of the Holocaust, a real estate broker, investor and philanthropist in New York, Philadelphia and Israel as well as a passionate Mets fan and tennis player. Husband of the late Eda Tenenbaum. Loving father of David of Hingham, MA and proud grandfather of Erica.
