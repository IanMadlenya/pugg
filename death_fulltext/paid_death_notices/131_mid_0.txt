
        DELIBERT-Anne Lash. Died on November 27, 1997, in her 87th year. She is survived by her husband of fifty-eight years, Joseph C. Delibert; by their son Steven, his wife Kris Reed, and their daughters Harsha and Himu; by their son Arthur, his wife Anne, and their daughter Adrienne; and by a Step-Grandson, Jeremy Blake. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Citizens' Committee for Children, 105 East 22nd Street, New York, New York 10010; or The New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64th Street, New York, New York 10023.
