
        FELD--Harold. The Yeshiva University family is deeply grieved by the passing of our cherished friend and longtime supporter who, with his beloved wife, Isabel, was a YU Guardian. Together they established the Gerald Feld Memorial Fund in loving memory of their son. Harold Feld was a great friend to YU, and offered generous assistance in many ways over the past several decades. Heartfelt condolences are extended to his beloved daughter, Stephanie, and grandson, Ben Harris. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman, Board of Trustees
        FELD--Harold. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Harold Feld, beloved husband of the late Isabel Feld, devoted leaders of the Jewish community who strengthened our ability to serve those in need and enriched millions of lives through their generosity and spirit of klal Yisrael. We extend our deepest sympathies to their beloved daughter Stephanie, to their grandson Ben Harris, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
