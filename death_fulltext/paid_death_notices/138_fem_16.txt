
        MARION-Anna. On July 3, 1998 at age 76 at home in Fort Lee, New Jersey. Beloved wife of Samuel. Loving mother of Lester and Robert & mother-in-law of Vivien and Beth. Adoring grandmother of Sara, Michael, Isadora, Davida, and Jonah. Dear sister of Meyer Steinberg and sister-in-law of Leah. Loving aunt of Phyllis Goldkrantz and Iris Steinberg. Services at Gutterman-Musicant, 402 Park Street, Hackensack, New Jersey, Sunday, July 5 at 9:30AM. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to the Hospice Program at Hackensack University Medical Center. She did it her way. She will be missed.
