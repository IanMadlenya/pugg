
        SCHULTZE--Alice. On June 18, 2004. Beloved mother of Professor Gary Schultze and grandmother of Emily Grace, Philip Aaron and Bambi. Also survived by sister Florence. A uniquely beautiful human being who brought love into the hearts of all who knew her, whose greatest joy was giving to those around her. Her spirit and bravery exhibited during her life serves as an inspiration to all who knew and loved her. As past president of Midwood Chapter B'nai B'rith, she was a dedicated fighter against all forms of intolerance. She is with her husband, Dr. Philip Schultze in a better place but will be so very, very missed by all who loved her. Services Sunday, 10:00 AM, at Weinstein Garlick Funeral Home, Brooklyn.
