
        VANDERPUTTEN--Ruth V. 93, (nee Bracht), of Westbury and New York City, died on Friday November 17th, she is survived by her sons and daughters - in - law, Thomas and Joan of Trinity, Florida; Gary and Jan of Brooklyn, New York and James and Jill of Pelham Manor, New York. She is also survived by seven grandchildren: Christine, Michael, Bryan, Joy, Christopher, and Chelsea, as well as two great-grandchildren, Peter and Sarah. Funeral Mass Monday 10AM at St. Rocco's Catholic Church in Brooklyn, New York and burial of ashes immediately following at Greenwood Cemetery. Donations can be made to the Mary Knoll Missionaries.
