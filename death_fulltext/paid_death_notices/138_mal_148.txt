
        SONNEBORN-Henry, III, PhD. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Administration of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute note with deep regret the passing of our good friend Henry Sonneborn, III. Past Chairman of the Board, he was a distinguished Trustee and a generous benefactor of the hospital. His lifetime of extraordinary dedication, enthusiasm and philanthropic support will be remembered and cherished. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, Clara Louise, his son Peter, and to the entire Sonneborn family. William A. Perlmuth, Chairman of the Board John N. Kastanis, FACHE President & Chief Exec. Officer Victor H. Frankel, M.D. President Emeritus Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief
        SONNEBORN-Henry, Dr. The Gesher Foundation records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed Board Member, Dr. Henry Sonneborn III. Our sincere condolences to the entire bereaved family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Daniel Tropper, President Marcel Lindenbaum, Chair Hillel Wiener, Exec. Vice Pres.
