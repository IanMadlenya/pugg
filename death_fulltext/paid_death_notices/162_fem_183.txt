
        RAUCH-Roxane S. On July 24, 2000. Beloved mother of Janine Rauch Eagle and David Rauch, grandmother of Jaclyn Rae Eagle, sister of Susan Cardon. Services today at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions may be made to Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical Center, Norris Cotton Cancer Center, Memorial Donation for Roxane Rauch, One Medical Center Drive, Lebanon, N.H. 03756. She is cherished by her family and will be deeply missed.
