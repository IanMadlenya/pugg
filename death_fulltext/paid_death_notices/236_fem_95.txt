
        SCRIBNER--Eunice Russell Bastedo, 96, died on August 15, 2006. Beloved by son, David Scribner Jr. of Manhattan, daughter Susan Heitner of Brooklyn, and Waterbury Ctr., VT, six grandchildren, and 10 great-grandchildren. Descendant of early Dutch and French settlers and founder of the Officers' Club of New York in 1941. Eunice was a daughter of old New York and a mother of the new. Contributions in her memory may be make to Vassar College; (845-437-5465).
