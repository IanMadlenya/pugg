
        ALPERT--Warren, late of New York City, entered into rest March 3, 2007. Founder and Chairman of Warren Equities, Inc. and the Warren Alpert Foundation. Dear son of the late Goodman and Tina (Horowitz) Alpert. Loving brother of the late Bess Litner, Benjamin Alpert, Mary R. Kaplan, and Pearl Starr. Services at the Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, 1668 Beacon St., Brookline, MA, Tuesday, March 6, 2007 at 12:00 Noon. Interment at Pride of Boston Cemetery, Woburn, MA. Memorial observance will be at the home of Glenda and Bob Fishman, Tuesday 4-8 pm and Wednesday 6-8 pm. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Dean's Discretionary Fund at the Harvard Medical School. Mr. Alpert made major donations to Harvard University Medical School which named a new research center in his honor, to New York's Mount Sinai Hospital which named the Warren Alpert Pavilion in his honor, and to Brown University which renamed their Medical School the Warren Alpert School of Medicine at Brown University.
