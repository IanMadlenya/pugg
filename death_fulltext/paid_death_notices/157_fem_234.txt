
        BRAFF-Sylvia. On February 25th. Beloved wife of Lewis; adored mother of Shelly and George, Jonathon and Lynne, and Michael and Gladys. Most cherished and devoted grandmother. Services Sunday, 10 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th and Amsterdam Ave.
        BRAFF - Sylvia. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York, records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Sylvia Braff. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        BRAFF-Sylvia. You will touch my heart and mind forever. Thank you for making me part of your family and for filling 35 years of lasting friendship with travel, music, theater and many laughs. I will always cherish your passion for life and love, dearest friend. Sue Glazer
        BRAFF-Sylvia. You made every moment such fun-so special. We thank you for making us a part of your family. There never was a better friend than you. We shall miss you and love you forever. Barbara and Norman King
        BRAFF-Sylvia. Beloved friend. A special human being. We shall miss and remember you always. Katy and Leonard Isaacson
