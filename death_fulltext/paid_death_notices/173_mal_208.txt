
        SPECTOR-Joseph R., attorney. Born April 16, 1923, died June 17, 2001. Former corporate council for Singer Corp and Vice President and General Council for UMC Inds. and Crane. Graduate of Dartmouth College '46 and Cornell Law School '52. Survived by his lifetime companion of 54 years, Ralph ''Pat'' Paterline of New York, and his brother Samuel I. Spector of Rome, Georgia. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The American Liver Foundation for Research, 75 Maiden Lane, Suite 603, New York, N.Y. 10038, are requested.
