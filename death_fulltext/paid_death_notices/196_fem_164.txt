
        WILENTZ-Pamela Endacott. Sculptor, teacher, beloved wife of Eli and exceptional mother of Grace, on May 19, at age 54. During her joyous life, Pam affirmed beauty and the human spirit in her art and in her love for others. She fought wonderfully, unforgettably, for years against her illness. Family and friends grieve the loss of her incandescent force. Survived by her loving daughter, her mother, Dorothy, and her stepchildren Sean, Eileen, and Philip. There will be a reception at home on Thursday, May 22 at 4PM.
