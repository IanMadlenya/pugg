
        BARTON-Harry Lang. Artist and illustrator, on August 12, 2001, at age 93. He is survived by daughters Linda Drohan and Joan Barton, both of Connecticut, and his sister Virginia E. Marshall and brother Stuart Barton, both of Seattle. Remembrance program Wednesday, August 29, at 2 PM, Messiah Lutheran Church, 42-15 165th St., Flushing, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be directed to the Development-Scholarship Fund for Needy Students, Art Students League of New York, 215 West 57th St., New York, NY 10019.
