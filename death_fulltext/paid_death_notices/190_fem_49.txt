
        WISE-Ethel (Pastan). Age 92, of Brookline, MA. Wife of the late Robert. Generous fountain of love. Co-founder and director of Horizons for Youth Summer Camp for inner city kids. Raised three children, Harry H. Wise, Sara W. Kane and Cheryl Skura. Cherished by her five grand and seven great grandchildren. Donations may be made to Associated camps, an agency of New York City UJA Federation.
