
        WARNER-Douglas T. January 8, 2000. Beloved husband of Ann. Mr. Warner was a prominent figure in Manhattan real estate. During his life he enjoyed horses and participated in many hunts throughout Long Island. Mr. Warner will be remembered as a true gentleman by all his friends who knew him. Memorial service Wednesday, January 12, 4 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Heart Association or Recording for the Blind.
