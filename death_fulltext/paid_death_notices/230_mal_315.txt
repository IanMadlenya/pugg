
        WEINER--Isaac N. 69, of Wayne, NJ, and Sanibel Island, FL, on March 24, 2006. Co-founder of Pike Construction Co., Paterson, NJ. Former Board Member of Barnert Hospital in Paterson, NJ, the YM-YWHA in Wayne, NJ, and Congregation Beth Shalom in Pompton Lakes, NJ. He is survived by his beloved wife Terry, loving children Michael and his wife Diane, David and his wife Ronni, Danielle and her husband John, devoted sister Bette Verp and husband Martin, and cherished grandchildren Margie, Grace, Sally, Billye, George, Nathan, and Joseph. Donations to Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation or St. Vincent's Comprehensive Cancer Center, 325 West 15th St., New York, NY 10011, would be appreciated. Services are Monday, 12:00 noon, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
