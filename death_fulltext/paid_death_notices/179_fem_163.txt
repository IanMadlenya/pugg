
        KAMIEN-Anna, 89. Died November 27, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Michel, mother of Roger Kamien and Sheila Ainbinder. Devoted mother-in-law of Anita Kamien and Dr. Alfred Ainbinder. Cherished grandmother of Mira Ainbinder, David and Joshua Kamien and Adina Kamien-Kazhdan and their respective spouses Felice, Tal and Eli. Proud great grandmother of Daniel, Eden Michel and Alicia. Composer, conductor, graduate of the Ecole Normale de Musique in Paris where she was a student of Alfred Cortot and Nadia Boulanger. Listed in Aaron Cohen's International Encyclopedia of Women Composers, 2nd edition. She gave us a passion for life.
