
        EICKELBERG-Prof. W. Warren B.., of Amityville, LI on May 15, 1999. Beloved husband of Marilyn Banks Eickelberg. Loving father of William Eickelberg, Peggy Larkin, Dr. Robert Eickelberg, Janet Beshlian, and step-father of David Banks, Jane Banks Stanfield, Stephen Banks and Lawrence Banks. Also survived by nine grandchildren and five step grandchildren. Mr. Eickelberg was a Professor of Biology and Chairman of the Pre-Medical Council at Adelphi University from 1953 to 1996. He also served as Vice President for Development and as consultant to the National Center for Disabilities Services. Widely recognized as an outstanding educator and researcher, Professor Eickelberg was named Professor of Biology Emeritus and Centennial Professor of Life Sciences. Visiting will be Tuesday, 2-4:30 and 79:30 PM at the Powell Funeral Home, Inc., 67 Broadway (Rt 110), Amityville. Mass of Requiem Wednesday, 11:00 AM, St. Mary's Episcopal Church, Amityville. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery, the Bronx. Family will appreciate donations to the W. Warren B. Eickelberg Scholarship Fund at Adelphi University Biology Department, Garden City, NY 11530.
