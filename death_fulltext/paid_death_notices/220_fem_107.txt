
        CUOCO--Laura B. On May 10. Beloved daughter of Louis and Sandra. Loving sister of Debbie. She graduated from the Bronx High School of Science in 1992, UC Berkeley in 1997 and the Yale School of Forestry in 2005. In her brief life, Laurie served 21/2 years in the Peace Corps and one year in Disaster Recovery with the International Red Cross, both in El Salvador. Laurie will be deeply missed by the many people whose lives she touched. For further information contact Sinai Chapels 800-446-0406.
