
        LIDSKIN-Diane. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the tragic passing of Diane Lidskin, beloved daughter of Rowland and Sylvia Schaefer. Rowland is an Honorary Vice Chair of ADL's National Commission and a member of the ADL Florida Regional Board. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Rowland and Sylvia, sisters Bonnie and Marla, her children and the entire family. Glen A. Tobias National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
