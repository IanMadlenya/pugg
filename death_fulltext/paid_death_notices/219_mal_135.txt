
        FISCHMAN--Herbert J., 88. Died on April 13, 2005. Adored husband and best friend to Helen for 60 years. Cherished father of Meryl Ain and Arthur Fischman, father-in-law of Stewart Ain and Janet Margolies, loving grandfather of Jonathan, Daniel and Michael Ain, Anna and Molly Fischman, grandfather - in law to Beth Ain, great-grandfather of Grace Eden Ain. WWII veteran, teacher and principal in the Brentwood Public Schools for 25 years. A man of dignity, intelligence, integrity and wisdom, may his memory be for a blessing. Funeral Thursday, 2 PM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, N.Y.
