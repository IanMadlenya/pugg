
        VEIT-Rose Brooks, 78. Of Bradenton, FL, died March 24, 1999. Born in New York City, she moved to Bradenton in 1986 from there. She is a retired Administrator & Director of alumni affairs at Columbia University. She attended New York University. She is survived by her husband Ivan; a son, Larry Brooks of Los Angeles, CA., and two grandchildren. Brown & Sons Funeral Homes of Bradenton, Florida for information.
