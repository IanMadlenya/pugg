
        AMSTER-Margaret Pope. On July 10, at age 83. An artist, free thinker, generous friend. A devoted wife to her late husband Leonard Amster. She will be greatly missed by her loving sister, Helen Manopoli of Spring Lake, NJ, her niece, Jane Manopoli Patterson of Boston, her nephew, John Manopoli of Newton, MA, and her many friends. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, July 16, at 11 AM, at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 St, NYC
