
        LONDON-George. Died peacefully on March 31, 2002, in his 84th year. Beloved father, grandfather, great grandfather, brother, and husband of the late Rose. Revered by all who knew him for his selflessness, integrity and wisdom. A professional engineer, he founded his own firm, which grew into the LKU Group. Contributions to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation would be appreciated by the family.
