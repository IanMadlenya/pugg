
        FIDELMAN-Fima, 91. Concert violinist. The last of a family of illustrious musicians who emigrated to the United States from Tiflis, Georgia in the early part of the century. Student of the eminent pedagogue Leopold Auer. Graduated with honors from the Juilliard School. First violinist WQXR. Concertmaster of the Columbia Broadcasting Symphony. Manager and Concertmaster of the St. James Theatre Orchestra. Noted for his sweet, elegant violin playing. Beloved friend and teacher. Warm, compassionate, gentle human being. His friendship will be greatly missed. Guy and Larry Parker
