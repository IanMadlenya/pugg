
        BLUM-Jean S. Age 76. Died at home May 6, 1997 of cancer. Beloved wife of the late Milton. Devoted mother of Wendy and Andrea Blum. She also leaves her brother Steven Sheinhouse, her aunt Peggy Sprung, her sister-inlaw Jeanne K. Blum and many devoted friends. Her courageous spirit and good humor was an inspiration to us all. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, Friday, May 9th, 2 P.M. Contributions may be made to Central Synagogue Program for the Homeless or Cabrini Hospice.
        BLUM-Jean. Central Synagogue Sisterhood records with sorrow the death of our valued and esteemed Board Member. We mourn with her beloved family. Susan Bayer and Audrey Zucker Co-Presidents
