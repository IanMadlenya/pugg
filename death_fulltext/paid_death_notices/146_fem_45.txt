
        BERKLEY-Esther, 77 on March 2, 1999. Beloved wife of Dr. Joseph, devoted mother of Alan, Janet and Linda; loving grandmother of Joshua, Sara, Sammy, Shannon, Benji and Brett. No wife could have given more; no mother could have loved more. She was adored by her children and grandchildren. She was a blessing and an inspiration to all who knew her and her spirit. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Association. Services Thursday 12:30 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
