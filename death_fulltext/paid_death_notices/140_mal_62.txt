
        HAM-William Taylor, Jr., 89, of Richmond, died Wednesday, September 2, 1998. He is survived by his wife Jean Anderson Ham, two daughters, Christiana Ham Read, of New York, and Elsbeth Ham, of Richmond, a son-in-law Peter B. Read, Jr., two grandchildren, Rufus and Panthea Read. Mr. Ham was a veteran of the USMC from WW II. He retired from the Department of biophysics at MCV, after completing much research in the field of biophysics. He was the recipient of the 1990 Science Museum of Virginia's Lifetime Achievement Award, and the first annual ''George M. Wilkening Award'' in March of 1997.
