
        DOEPP-John Herbert. March 4, 1999. At home in Brooklyn, New York. Born in Buffalo, NY, April 7, 1939, the son of Ethel Haller and John Martin Doepp. Brother of Edward Frederick Doepp. Uncle of Jennifer Margaret Doepp and Edward Frederick Doepp II, all of New Orleans. Graduate of Carnegie Tech (Carnegie-Mellon) 1963. A gifted artist and multi-talented designer, his credits include architecture (The Harlequin Atlanta), restaurant design (America Las Vegas) and scenic design. In addition to assisting Jo Mielziner, his own designs were seen at The Virginia Museum Theatre, Brunswick Music Theatre, Syracuse Stage and many other venues. He was a member of United Scenic Artists. Services will be private by invitation.
