
        GUNNING-(Moffett) Rosemary R. of Ridgewood, Queens, New York born 1905. Died peacefully at home among family after a brief illness. Practicing attorney and former member of the New York State Assembly from 1968 to 1972. Beloved wife of the late Lester Moffett. Dear aunt to John, Joan, Marie and Ann. Also survived by thirteen grand nieces and nephews, and her cousin and friend Sylvia Boyland. Visitors may call at the Morton Funeral Home, 580 Onderdonk Ave, Ridgewood, NY, Tuesday 2-5 and 7-10pm. Mass of christian burial Wednesday 10am, St. Brigids Roman Catholic Church. Interment Calverton National Cemetery. Donations may be made in her memory to St. Brigids RC Church, 409 Linden Street, Brooklyn, New York, 11237.
