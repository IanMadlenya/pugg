
        STORPER-David, of Great Neck, on March 14, 1997. Loving and supportive father to Sarah, Barbara, Dan. Father-in-law to Gerry Field. Grandfather to Aaron and Jessica Field. Beloved partner to Ellen Rosen. Devoted son of the late Max and Sarah Storper and brother to Toby Lichtman and the late Bernard Storper. Former husband of Natalie Reichlin and Rosemary Stigliano. An attorney in New York for over 50 years, a naval lieutenant in World War II and served on boards of several charitable organizations. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' Great Neck. His wishes are to end discrimination and prejudice. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Anti-Defamation League.
