
        SULLIVAN--Kathryn, RSCJ, prominent Catholic biblical scholar, longtime professor at Manhattanville University, died September 22 at Kenwood Convent, Albany, NY at age 101. Sr. Sullivan, of the Society of the Sacred Heart, became in 1947 the first woman elected to membership in the Catholic Biblical Association and in 1958 became its vicepresident. During and after 46 years at Manhattanville (1938-1984), she lectured at major Catholic seminaries in the U.S. and Rome. The funeral Mass is at 9 a.m. today, Tuesday, September 26, at Kenwood Convent. Memorial contributions may be made to Society of the Sacred Heart, 4389 West Pine Blvd, St. Louis, MO 63108
