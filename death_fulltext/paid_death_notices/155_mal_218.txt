
        DELSENER-Ethel (nee Klein) December 15, 1999. Age 93. Beloved wife of Ackley Vincent Delsener for 68 years. Devoted mother of Harriette Delsener and Ronald Delsener. Cherished grandmother of Robert Jeffrey Riley, Scott David Riley and Samantha Leigh Delsener and great grandmother of Enzo Riley, Marina Riley and Lily Maya Marvin. Devoted sister of Sidney Klein. Friends may call Thursday, December 16, 1999, 2-5 and 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Service Friday, December 17, 1999, 9:45 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell. Interment private.
        DELSENER-Ethel Klein-The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of Ethel Klein Delsener, beloved mother of Ron Delsener. We extend condolences to Ron and his family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Cantor Debra Stein-Davidson Donald Zucker, President
