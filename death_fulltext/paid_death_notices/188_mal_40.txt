
        LEE-Robert W. 72, died Sunday at his residence following a long illness. He and his wife Brenda owned and operated the Wishing Well Restaurant in Saratoga Springs, NY-a popular spot for summer racetrack attendees. Mr. Lee was born in 1929 in Chatham, NJ, and attended Seton Hall University before enlisting in the US Navy during the Korean War. He was appointed an NYS Racing Commissioner in 1993 and served with distinction on the Board of Directors of the NYS Thoroughbred Breeding and Development Fund.
