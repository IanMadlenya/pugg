
        SHAPIRO-Alexander. The Yeshiva University family mourns the loss of a highly respected member of the Jewish community, beloved father of Irwin (and Ruth) Shapiro and Barbara (and Manfred) Joseph. Irwin Shapiro is a member of the YU Board of Trustees, Bernard Revel Graduate School Board of Directors, and Board of Trustees of the affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary and its YU High Schools Board, and a YU Benefactor with his wife, Ruth, member, Executive Council, YU Women's Organization. Barbara Joseph is a member, National Board, YUWO. We extend heartfelt condolences as well to grandchildren and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
