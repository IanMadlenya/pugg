
        LUCKOM-Edith, nee Bindman, 93, died at her home on August 12, 2002. Devoted to her family, she was the beloved wife for 61 years of Ben; adored mother of Bram Luckom and Fran LuckomNurnberg; dear sister of Aaron Bindman; loved motherin-law of Debbie Luckom and Ronald Nurnberg; and cherished grandmother of Becky Mobly, Carolyn and Peter Nurnberg. We will miss you and keep your love in our hearts. Funeral services, Wednesday, August 14th at 1 PM at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, New York, NY.
