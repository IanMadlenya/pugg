
        PEARLMAN--Jean on November 5, 2003. Devoted wife of 47 years of the late Harold Pearlman. Beloved mother of Bernard Pearlman and Eugene (Ann) Pearlman. Adored grandmother of Rachel. Born Briansk, Russia in 1913, she and her family survived war and revolution before immigrating to America in 1922. She lived to see her children and grandchild achieve a life of security and fulfillment. Services Monday November 10, 12noon at Pinelawn Memorial Park, Farmingdale, NY.
