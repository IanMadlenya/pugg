
        POTOK-Chaim. The Jewish Publication Society extends condolences to the family of Dr. Chaim Potok, Bible translator, former JPS Editor-inChief, and long-time JPS Editorial Chairman. For almost four decades, Dr. Potok guided the Society through his notable scholarship, far-reaching vision, and incisive counsel. May his memory be for a blessing. Allan R. Frank, President Dr. Ellen Frankel, CEO and Editor-in-Chief
