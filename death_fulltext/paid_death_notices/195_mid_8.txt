
        LEWIS-Rosalie. April 12, 2003, age 75. Born in Manhattan, raised in Brooklyn, NYC resident all her life. Lover of the arts. Created gift shop for New York City Ballet, and helmed it for many years. Survived by son, Charles, brother, Salvatore LoGerfo and his family, and many friends. Service to be held 10AM, Friday, April 25, First Unitarian Church, Monroe Place and Pierrepont Street, in Brooklyn Heights.
