
        SUSSKIND-Jesse. On June 14, 1998. Devoted husband of the late Frances Sokolski. Beloved father of Jane and Stephan, brother of Pearl Rice and the late Blanche Laurie. Adoring grandfather of Pamela Narins. Larger than life, a man of vigor, intelligence and humor, he never lost joy in simply being alive, and lived gallantly and with courage into his 90's. Private services at the Riverside.
