
        YOUNG-Jane. It is with great sadness that the Board, Staff, and Friends of Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. mourn the passing of Jane Young, wife of Edgar B. Young, who is one of Lincoln Center's founders, a distinguished Director Emeritus and chronicler of Lincoln Center's history. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Edgar, his family and friends. Beverly Sills, Chairman Nathan Leventhal, President Martin E. Segal and Richard R. Shinn, Co-Chairmen of the Directors Emeriti Council
