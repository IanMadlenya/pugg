
        FIEDLER-Harold Z. Beloved husband, father and grandfather, partner and friend. Passed away December 1, 1998, 9AM. ''Heshy'' was a man's man. He was a devoted husband and father to his children and grandchildren. His family and his devoted employees will miss him dearly. His wit and charm were the mainstay of our family. May he rest in peace. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel in Mount Vernon at 11AM on December 2. The family will be sitting shiva at 160 Colonial Parkway North, Yonkers, N.Y. starting Wednesday afternoon, December 2 until Sunday, December 6.
