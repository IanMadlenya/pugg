
        DeLAMBILY-Capt. Robert W., 76, Master Mariner, USMM. Died April 7, 2001. Resident of Lanoka Harbour, NJ, and Stuart, FL. Surviving are his wife Marguerite; son Robert W. DeLambily Jr., Haddonfield, NJ; daughters Suzan Hale, Toms River, NJ, Barbara Rodgers, Sugar Hill, GA. Memorial service April 22, 2001, First United Methodist Church of Island Heights, NJ. Contributions may be made to Salvation Army.
