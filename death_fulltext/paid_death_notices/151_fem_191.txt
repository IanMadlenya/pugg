
        SEADER-Ruth. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Merrick, NY. Surviving her is devoted husband of 51 years, Dr. Saul Seader, three sons, a daughter, their spouses, three grandchildren, one brother and sister. Artist, author, editor, lecturer on spirituality and meditation, her selfless service and devotion in helping others touched many lives. Memorial service to be held Monday, August 23 at 2PM at Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton. In the Chicago area, Sunday, August 29 at 12 noon at the Science of Spirituality, Naperville, Illinois.
