
        GORDON-Arthur. Of Forest Hills, Queens. Loving husband of Alice Gordon (Buchwald). Caring father of Eric and Nancy. Devoted grandfather of Jacob and Keri. With wit and compassion, he enriched the lives of fellow New Yorkers for 81 years. Died peacefully May 14, 2000. He will live forever in our hearts. The funeral service will take place Thursday, May 18, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, NY, 718-263-7600.
