
        KELSER-Esther (Crane). On September 7, 2000 at 99 years old at Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, N.J. She was the widow of New York Times stockmarket columnist Burton Crane and of Philip L. Kelser. She is survived by a daughter Sylvia and son-in-law Laurence Eisenlohr of Phila.;three grandchildren, nine great grandchildren. Donations in her memory may be sent to Wellesley College 106 Central St., Wellesley, Ma. 02181.
