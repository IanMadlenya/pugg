
        ROSENBACH-Kurt. Died after a brief but courageous battle with cancer on February 17, 2003 at age 75. He was surrounded by Bernice, his loving wife of 50 years, his children Karen and Gary, and their spouses Tom and Susan. Kurt was cherished and adored and will always be remembered by his five grandchildren Nicole, Joshua, Brandon, Bess and Sophie. He was a positive force behind his family, a kind, gentle, generous man who will be sorely missed by all those he touched. Services will be held Wednesday, February 19 at 11:15 AM, at ''the Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
