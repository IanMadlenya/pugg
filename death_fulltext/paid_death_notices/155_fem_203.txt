
        GOLDMAN-Ruth. On December 20th in her 93rd year. Wife to the late Paul L. Goldman. Mother of Phyllis & Tammy. Sister to Ethel. Grandmother of Karen, David, Barbara, the late Yariv, Nirit, Erez & Haran. Great-grandmother to Elizabeth & Madeleine. Mother-in-law to Albert, Philip, Dan and Gabi. Grandmother-inlaw to Paul, Anne and Uki. In the causes for which you worked, you left your mark. In the many roles you played you were loved. In our hearts and memories you will live on. Services Thursday, December 23, 1 PM, at Schwartz Brothers, Queens Boulevard and 76th Road, Forest Hills. Contributions to the National Yiddish Book Center or other Jewish causes.
