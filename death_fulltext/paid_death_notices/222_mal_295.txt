
        AUER--Louis V., of Westhampton Beach, New York, and La Quinta, CA, passed away at his home in California on July 26, 2005. He was 78. He graduated Yale University in 1947. He worked on Wall Street for 30 years. He was predeceased by his wife of 50 years, Jean Patchett, and his son, Bart. He is survived by his daughter, Amy, of Westhampton Beach, his sister-in-law, Lee Auer, and his nephew, Louis Auer VI, of California. Arrangements for a memorial service are still pending. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Arthritis Foundation, 1330 W. Peachtree St. NW, Suite 100, Atlanta, GA 30309, or charity of choice would be appreciated by his family.
