
        ALLON-Dr. Otto, passed away June 21, 1998. beloved husband of Clara, loving father of Dr. Dan (Julie) Allon, cherished grandfather of Alex and Michael. Services at Gutterman - Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton, FL.
        ALLON-Otto, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Otto Allon.
