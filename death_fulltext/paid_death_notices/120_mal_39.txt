
        GEMDAR - Bertram E. Jr. 83, of Manhattan. Died of cancer Saturday, January 4, at St. Luke's-Rossevelt Hospital in Manhattan. Born in Brooklyn, a son of Bertram and Irma Gendar. He graduated from Williams College and Brooklyn Law School. He served in the U.S. Coast Guard during World War II and in the Coast Guard Reserve after the war. He retired from reserve service with the rank of Captain. He was a lawyer for Gulf Oil Company in the overseas tanker division before opening his own law practice in Manhattan. He married Jacqueline Burns, the light of his life, August 25, 1962. His daughter, Alison of Yonkers, survives. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to Dollars for Scholars of Taconic Hills, PO Box 304, Hillsdale, NY 12529.
