
        FLEISCHMAJER--Raul, MD. The Mount Sinai Alumni express profound sorrow at the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend. Chairman of the Department of Dermatology for 17 years, Dr. Fleischmajer was highly respected, admired and recognized worldwide as a leading clinician and researcher. A devoted teacher and supportive mentor, he represented the best tradition of the medical center, exemplified by his dedication to teaching and commitment to clinical excellence. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. William G. Stein, MD, Pres. Barry D. Stimmel, MD, Vice Pres Elliot J. Rayfield, MD, Secy. Betty J. Mintz, MD, Treas.
        FLEISCHMAJER--Raul. The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourns the passing of Raul Fleischmajer, M.D., Chair Emeritus of the Department of Dermatology. He was a world renowned authority on collagen and scleroderma and held leadership positions in many dermatologic organizations. He was also an outstanding teacher and mentor. We extend sincere condolences to his wife, Rita, and all his family and friends. Peter W. May, Chairman of the Boards of Trustees Kenneth L. Davis, M.D. President, CEO and Dean
        FLEISCHMAJER--Raul, M.D. The members of the New York Dermatological Society note with profound grief the death of their esteemed colleague and friend. Mark Lebwohl, M.D. President
