
        BIRD-Robert W. Of Hilton Head, S.C. died on May 27th from idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. He served in the US Army from 1945 to 1947, returned to Dickinson College and graduated in 1950. He subsequently graduated from NYU Law School in 1953 and was admitted to practice in 1954. He worked on Wall Street in the banking and investment banking fields and retired from Integrated Systems Solutions Corp., an IBM subsidiary in 1996. Survived by his wife Abby and five children by a former marriage, Robert of Littleton, CO., Lara of New York City, Christopher of Newtown, CT., Pennel of Los Angeles, CA. and Cameron of London, England and six grandchildren. The Island Funeral Home.
