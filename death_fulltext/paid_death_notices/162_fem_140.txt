
        JACOBS-Grace (Madan) on July 20. Dearly beloved wife of Robert H. Jacobs for 57-1/2 years. Wonderful mother and grandmother of ''The Fabulous 14''-our three children and their spouses, Kenneth Jacobs and his wife Dian, Nancy Martin and her husband George, William Jacobs and his wife Dawn and the eight grandchildren to whom she was so devoted. Matthew Jacobs, Peter, Jeffrey, Andrew, and Jennifer Martin, Daniel, Jill, and Adam Jacobs. She is also survived by her brother Robert Madan and his wife Peggy and by her many dearly loved cousins. And last but not least by the many friends whom she has had in her long and happy life. Donations to your favorite charity would be appreciated. Services at ''Gutterman-Musicant'' 402 Park St, Hackensack, NJ on Sunday, July 23 at 11:30am. Entombment will follow immediately at the Garden of Memories, Soldier Hill Rd Extension, Westwood.
