
        SULLIVAN--John N. (Jack), of Port Washington, NY died May 25, 2007 at the age of 79. Beloved husband of MaryAnne. Loving father of Susan Graham, Karen Garry, Kelly Dennis and John Sullivan. Cherished grandfather of Katie, Ryan, Kieran, Dylan and Riley. Graduate of St. Andrew Avellino (1941), Fordham Prep (1945) and Fordham University (1951). Student at Fordham College at 60, Lincoln Center. US Army Veteran. Dedicated Latin and Greek teacher for 41 years at Fordham Prep where he was also well-known for coaching basketball and lacrosse. Led student tours to Italy for over 25 years. Wake Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 pm at Fairchild Sons Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, NY. Funeral Wednesday 9:30 am at St. Peter of Alcantara Church, 1321 Port Washington Blvd., Port Washington, NY. Interment to follow at Calverton National Cemetery. Donations in his honor may be sent to the St. Vincent de Paul Society. Carpe Diem -Ave Atque Vale.
