
        ZLOTKOWSKI--John Michael Alan. Age 24, died December 3, 2003 at home. Poet, philosopher, and stand up comedian, Zlotkowski was raised and resided in Old Bridge, NJ all of his life. He graduated Old Bridge High School in 1997. Survivors: Father and stepmother Alan and Margaret Zlotkowski; mother and stepfather Mary Ann and Edward Castagnetta; brother Michael Alan Zlotkowski; and maternal grandmother Nella Del Prete. Funeral December 9, 2003, 9:30 AM, Most Holy Redeemer Church, 133 Amboy Road, Matawan, NJ. Viewing December 7 and 8 from 2-4 and 7-9PM at Michael Hegarty Funeral Home, 3377 Route 9, Old Bridge, NJ.
