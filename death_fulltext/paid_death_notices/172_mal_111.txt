
        JACOBS-Dr. Joseph. American Friends of The Hebrew Uni versity is deeply saddened by the passing of our dear Board of Dir ectors Member and Guardian, Dr. Joseph Jacobs. A prominent dentist and tireless campaigner to improve Israel's dental care facilities , Joe was instrumental in the development of The Hebrew University-Hadassah School of Dental Medicine and the D. Walter Cohen Middle East Center for Dental Education. He also served as a Governor of The Hebrew University, Vice President of the AFHU Western Region and Chairman of the AFHU Dental Division. For his wa rm friendship and firm commitment toward The Hebrew Universit y of Jerusalem, he was named an Honorary Fellow. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Benita, their children Renae Jacob s and David Anson and Ehren and Gaye Jacobs, their grandchildre n, and the entire family.
