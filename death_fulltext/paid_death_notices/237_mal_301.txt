
        AIN--Stuart L. Honorable Retired Judge of the Nassau County Court. Beloved by the bench and the bar. Past president of Temple Beth Sholom of Roslyn. He is survived by his best friend and companion Donna Brown, daughter Jody Ain and son Dr. Michael Ain and two grandchildren. Service at Temple Beth Sholom, Roslyn Road, Roslyn, New York at 12noon on the 31st day of October 2006.
