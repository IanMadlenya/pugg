
        SIMON-Gerda P., 87, of Somers and Forest Hills, January 19th, after a brief illness. Beloved Wife, Mother, Grandmother, predeceased by husband Martin (1983). Survived by daughter Vivian Drucker, son-in-law Dennis; son Leslie Simon, daughterin-law Susan; grandchildren, Sharon and Steven Drucker, and Richard Simon; sister Inge Simon. Vice President of NCJW. We will all miss her very much.
