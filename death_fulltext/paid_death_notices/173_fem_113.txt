
        JACKSON-Genevieve C. Of Fishkill, NY died June 8, 2001. She was 89. Born April 6, 1912 in Tampa, Florida to Frank and Loretta Mapp Glover. A bandleader and entertainer in the 1930's and 1940's with jazz bands in the South, Florida Collegians and The Original Inkspots in Harlem. She raised her family in the Bronx from 1947-1997. Lived in Nanuet, NY from 1997-1999. After retirement, she was a loving fulltime wife and mother, an excellent cook, master at crochet and embroidery. She was married to her late husband Pat Jackson from September 30, 1943 until his death March 6, 1998. She is survived by her daughters Mrs. Harolgen Chestnut of Mitchellville, MD and Theresa P. Jackson, M.D. of Spring Valley, NY, son-in-law MSGT. Mose Chestnut, Jr., grandson Patrick Darryl Chestnut, granddaughter Audrey Yvette Chestnut, great grandchildren Linton Tre'Mark, and Edward Glenn, and great - great - granddaughter Lania-Renee. Reposing Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Sniffen Segala Funeral Home, 235 Route 59, Spring Valley, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Friday, 10 AM at St. Joseph's Church, North Main Street, Spring Valley. Interment Ascension Cemetery, Monsey, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Windgate of Dutchess Rehabilitation Center, 3 Summit Court, Fishkill, NY 12524.
