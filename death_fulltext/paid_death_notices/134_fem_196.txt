
        SCHWARTZ-Hilda G. Retired Supreme Court Justice on Mar. 18, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Herman N. Schwartz. Loving mother of John M. Schwartz. Devoted sister of Edith Smolen and the late Rose Weisman, Dr. Joseph A. Gaines and Mary Princer. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81 St, Saturday 6-9PM. Funeral service Sunday, March 22, 11:30AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65 St.
        SCHWARTZ-Hon. Hilda G. Founder and past President of the New York Women's Bar Association, retired Justice, New York Supreme Court. Mentor and role model for generations of women lawyers. She will be sorely missed. Laurie Berke-Weiss, President
