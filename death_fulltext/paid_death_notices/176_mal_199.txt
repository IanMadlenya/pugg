
        VITALE-Albert G., M.D. The Department of Medicine of St. Vincent's Hospital mourns the loss of Dr. Albert G. Vitale, first Chief of Cardiopulmonary Medicine and former President of the Medical Staff. We remember Dr. Vitale as an outstanding friend and teacher, and as a compassionate and wise clinician. We offer our sincere condolences to his wife and children. Dennis M. Greenbaum, M.D. Acting Chairman, Department of Medicine St. Vincent's Hospital-Manhattan
