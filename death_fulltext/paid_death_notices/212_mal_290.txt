
        HALLORAN--Leo A., former vice-president of General Electric and chief financial officer at GE Capital who pioneered a wave of innovative financing structures throughout the 1980's, died from cancer on Tuesday at his home in Guilford, CT. Born on April 2, 1931 in Schenectady, NY, Mr. Halloran was a graduate of Union College and served in the U.S. Army during the Korean War. His wife, Marilyn Gobeli Halloran, daughter Patricia Garvey, sons Michael Halloran and Kevin Halloran and five grandchildren survive Mr. Halloran. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at 9:00 A.M. Saturday, Oct. 2nd at the Saint George Catholic Church in Guilford, CT. Mr. Halloran will be buried at the Most Holy Redeemer in Schenectady, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: Carolyn Frye Halloran Memorial, Massachusetts General Hospital Development Office, 100 Charles River Place, Suite 600, Boston, MA 02114-2792.
