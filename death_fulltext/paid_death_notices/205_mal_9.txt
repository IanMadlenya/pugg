
        ISLER--Lloyd I. Of Paradise Valley, AZ and Scarsdale, NY, on January 29, 2004. Born March 1, 1919 and graduated from Brooklyn College 1937. He received his law degree from Fordham Law School in 1942. He married Sylvia Kaplan on October 11, 1942 and practiced law for 50 years in Manhattan. Throughout his life, he cared deeply about humanitarian causes. He is survived by his loving wife of 61 years, Sylvia, his adoring daughter Wendy Alvarez (Simon) of Carmel, NY and sons Scott and Kenneth of Paradise Valley, AZ. He will be missed by all for his incisive intellect, wit, integrity and compassion.
