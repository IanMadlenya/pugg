
        WILLIG--Meg, 85 years old, died on February 4th, 2004 after a prolonged struggle to overcome the effects of a cerebral hemorrhage. She is survived by her husband of 65 years, Jack; her son, Robert, and his wife, Ginny; her grandchildren, David, Danny, Jared, Scott, Brent and Alexandra; and her great-grandchildren, Noa and Elai; as well as her sister, Helen, and brother, Seymour. Loved by all who knew her, she was a loyal and compassionate friend; a charismatic folk dance instructor and leader; and an outstanding bridge teacher to hundreds of enthusiastic players at Brooklyn College's IRPE and other centers. Funeral services will be held on Friday, February 6th, at 11:30 AM at Nieberg Midwood Chapel, Coney Island Ave. and Ave. M in Brooklyn.
