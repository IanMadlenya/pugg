
        QUINN-Mary Catherine (nee Ryan), 93, of Seaside Park, N.J., formerly of Westfield, NJ and San Diego, CA., died on Monday. Predeceased by her husband Frank and her son Lawrence. Loving mother of Michael, John, Kathleen McHale, Elizabeth McKenna, Ellen Quinn and Constance Hourihan. Grandmother of twenty-one grandchildren and twenty-one great grandchildren. Family and friends are respectfully invited to attend the funeral at 5PM, Friday, St. Catharine's RC Church, Seaside Park, NJ, where a mass of Christian burial will be offered. Visiting will be at St. Catharine's RC Church on Friday from 3-5 PM. Burial will be on Saturday at St. Mary's Cemetery, Clark, N.J.
