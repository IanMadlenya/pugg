
        HEINEMANN-Helen Lillian. Died February 1, 2001, after a long battle with cancer. Helen was the beloved wife of Harry and the devoted mother of David and Tammy, Richard, and Cindy and Kevin Gold. Proud grandmother of Eric, Max and Aaron. Loving sister to Ruth Daub and Sidney Stern. She loved her family and friends. We will miss her very much. Funeral will be held at Sinai Chapels in Queens, 11 AM, February 4, 2001.
