
        GITTLER--Robert D., M.D. The Board of Trustees, the Department of Medicine and the Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express their profound sorrow at the death of Robert D. Gittler, M.D, a distinguished member of our medical staff for more than 40 years. Dr. Gittler, a respected endocrinologist who was deeply committed to his patients, was dedicated to the highest standards of medicine. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family.
