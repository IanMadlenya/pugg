
        RUBINFIEN--David, 83, died in the presence of his loving family, April 26, 2005, in Palo Alto, CA. Formerly President and CEO of Syva Company, Microgenics and Systemix. He is survived by his wife, Shulamith, his sister, Leona Billings, his children, Leo, Elisabeth and Louisa and his seven grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the ACLU.
