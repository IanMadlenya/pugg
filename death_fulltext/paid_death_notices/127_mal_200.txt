
        GOLDBERG-Murray Lyon. Born October 21, 1912 to his beloved parents, Harry and Rose (Chertok). Adoring husband of 61 years to his beautiful Ruth, devoted sonin-law to Abe and Jennie Margolis. Loving and generous father to Jerry (Donald) and Stan (Leah). Proud grandpa to Noah. Caring brother to Lee, Gertie (Lily), and the late Irving, Julie, Louis and baby sister Esther; uncle to Gary (Gloria), Bruce (Simone); other nieces and nephews and a cherished extended family of cousins and friends. A raconteur and salesman extraordinaire, formerly of Rockville Centre, New York. At home in Aberdeen, Boynton Beach, FL., on August 18, 1997.
