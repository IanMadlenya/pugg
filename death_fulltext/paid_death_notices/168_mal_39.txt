
        HART-F. David. Of Saddle River, New Jersey, entered into eternal rest on December 22, 2000. He was a graduate of Lafayette College with a BS in Chemical Engineering. He had been a past employee of Mobil Oil, IBM, and since his retirement Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage in Saddle River. He is survived by his wife Carol (nee Anderson), a son Warren of New York, and a daughter Elizabeth Pelton of Minnesota. Also surviving are seven grandchildren. A memorial service will take place on January 5th at 10:30 AM at the St. Elizabeth's Episcopal Church in Ridgewood, NJ.
