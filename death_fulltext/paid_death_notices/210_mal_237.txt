
        HEDSTROM -- Carl Ernest, age 76, of Cape May Beach, NJ died on July 24, 2004 at his residence. Born in Norristown, PA, he formerly lived in Upper Darby, PA and Great Neck, NY. He taught at Great Neck North Senior High School for many years. He served in the U.S. Navy during WW II. Surviving are his wife Susan Joan, four children, three grandchildren and a brother. Burial will be on Wednesday at Cold Spring Presbyterian Church Cemetery. Donations requested to National Parkinsons Foundation, PO Box 5273, Kendall Park, NJ, 08824. Evoy Funeral Home, 609-886-7111.
