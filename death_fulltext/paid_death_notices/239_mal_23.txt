
        DONEGAN--Martin. Actor, director, playwright, and teacher of the stagecraft he loved, died unexpectedly at Manhattan's Mt Sinai Hospital November 20. Beloved husband of Margaret Golightly and father of Ellen Donegan, he is also mourned by his sister, Marcella Seeley, and the children of his previous marriage, Nancy Dennigan Guay, Brian Dennigan, and Tracey Dennigan, and their families. A private memorial service is planned. Contributions in remembrance may be made to the Myelodysplastic Syndromes (MDS) Foundation.
