
        LEVINE -- Esther Edelman, Ph.D. Age 82, died tragically Friday. Warm, funny, intelligent, caring, friend to all. Graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Brooklyn College. Obtained Ph.D. in clinical pysychology from NYU. Professor and Associate Dean of Educational Psychology at Queens College. She loved tennis, bridge, theater, books, swimming, jokes and Yiddish elocution. Most of all, she loved and was loved by her family, her children, Bob and Trudi, Dan and Margaret, Alice and Alex, and her precious jewels, her grandchildren, Samara, Andy, Jed, Ben and Zach. Service Sunday, April 2, 11:30 AM, Campbell's Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        $(6$)LEVINE--Esther. There will always be a chair at our table and a place in our hearts. The Starman Family
