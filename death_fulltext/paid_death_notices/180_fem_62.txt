
        WEISBERG-Blanche S. Died January 3, 2002 at age 73. Deeply loved wife of Honorable Gerard M. and adored mother of Andrea and Roland Murphy and Dr. Bruce Weisberg. Beloved sister of Sarah and Abe Hochner. The lights of Bubbie's life, she is also survived by her 4 grandchildren, Roland II, Kyle, Zachary and Brianna. There are no words to describe the immense void left by her passing. Her devotion to family and friends was never ending and will always be remembered. Her warmth and caring will be with us forever. Contributions can be made to UJA Federation. Services will be held Sunday, 1:00 PM at Weinstein-GarlickKirschenbaum Chapels, Brooklyn.
