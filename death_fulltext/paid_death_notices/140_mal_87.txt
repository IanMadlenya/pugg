
        REISMAN-David B., 75 of NYC, passed away on September 6, 1998 of bladder cancer. He was a member of the D.G.A. Beloved husband of Laurette, devoted brother of Isabel (Saul) Cohen and loving nephew of Vivian Heisner. Funeral services are Tuesday (today) at 11:30 A.M. at the Plaza Memorial Chapel, West 91st Street at Amsterdam Ave. NYC. Interment will follow at King David Memorial Gardens in Shrub Oak, New York. Donations may be made to the Conservative Synagogue of 5th Avenue at 11 East 11th Street. NY, New York 10003. We will love you always and forever miss you.
