
        LEE-Benjamin F. Of Palm Beach, FL., and Scarsdale, NY. At the age of 88. Beloved husband of Doris S. Lee. Loving father of Bruce S. Lee of New City, N.Y., and Lawrence Lee of Scarsdale, NY. Cherished grandfather. Previously deceased by his first wife Ruth. A respected and loved member of the Schulman family. Services will be held Thursday, at 11:30AM, at Temple Israel of New Rochelle. Interment Jewish Community Center Cemetery, Monsey, New York.
        LEE-Benjamin F. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Benjamin F. Lee. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Doris and their beloved family.
