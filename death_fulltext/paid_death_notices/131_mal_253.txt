
        NARKISS-Uzi. We deeply mourn the passing of General (Res.) Uzi Narkiss, a former member of the W.Z.O. executive and head of the Zionist delegation to North America. He has been dedicated to a life-long heroic military career in the Israel Defense Forces, culminating in the liberation of Jerusalem in the Six-Days War as a key commander of the I.D.F. Jewish Agency Delegation in North America Gad Ben-Ari, Chairman World Zionist OrganizationAmerican Section Kalman Sultanik, Chairman
