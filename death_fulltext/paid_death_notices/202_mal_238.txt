
        POOLE--Henry P. 40 year resident of Malverne, suddenly on November 22, 2003. Beloved husband of 47 years to Jacqueline. Loving father of Margaret, Nancy Parker (Terrence), Jacqueline Zerilli (Steven), Patricia, Michael J. (Andrea), and Daniel P. (Tara). Dear grandfather of Ryan, Janine, Joseph, Brooke, Jacki, Jessica, James, Christopher and Kelly. Cherished brother of Sr. M. Julianna Poole S.S.N.D. Longtime parishioner of Our Lady of Lourdes R.C. Church, Malverne. Henry was a graduate of St. John's University and St. John's School of Law (1957). He was the Vice-President of the Assembly of General Counsel of Merrill Lynch. He was the Executive Vice-President and General Counsel of the New York Stock Exchange, Inc. (1973-1993). Family will receive friends tonight, 7-9 pm, and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, at the Flinch & Bruns Funeral Home, Inc, 34 Hempstead Ave. (corner Peninsula Blvd), Lynbrook. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10:30 am, Our Lady of Lourdes R.C. Church, Malverne. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury.
