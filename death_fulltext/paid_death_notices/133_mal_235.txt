
        MARTELL-Paul. Age 50, of 408 Hammett Rd., Greer, SC, died Friday. Born in Brooklyn, he was founder and President of Greenville Finishing Co. He attended C. W. Post College on Long Island and Florida State University. He was an Air Force veteran of the Viet Nam Conflict. Surviving are his wife Kaye; a daughter Jessica, and son Jeffrey; his father Philip; three brothers, Richard, John and Charles; two sisters, Lynn Bezoza of NYC and Esther Gaier. He was predeceased by his mother Eleanor Martell, a New York consumer advocate. Memorial services Monday, 1 PM, at The Mackey Mortuary, Greenville, SC.
