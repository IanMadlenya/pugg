
        OXFELD--Emil. As a civil libertarian and labor lawyer he worked on many precedent making cases and causes, which involved such issues as loyalty oaths, prayer in the schools, unconstitutional excesses of Congressional committees, McCarthyism, discharge of employees for exercising constitutional rights, and especially the rights of unions and their members. Founded New Jersey chapter of the ACLU in 1960, served as its president for 25 years. Survived by his wife Edith and daughters Nancy and Ellen. Memorial service Thursday, July 24, 3PM, Menorah Chapels, Union, New Jersey. >>AD#
