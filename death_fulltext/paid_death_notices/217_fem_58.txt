
        STOCKTON--Helen Bilyeu, 91, of natural causes, in Philadelphia, on January 31, 2005. A confirmed New Yorker since 1957, widowed in 1970, retired from social work in 1979. A lifelong artist and original member of Ceres (the co-op erative women's gallery in Manhattan) where she had 6 solo shows of her paintings. Her last solo exhibition was 2003 in Philadelphia. Survived by daughters Robyn Stockton (NYC) and Joan Stockton and grandson Peter Bilyeu Stockton (both of Philadelphia). Memorial to follow in NYC. Contributions to ACLU.
