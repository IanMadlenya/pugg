
        LEAVENWORTH-Randolph J. III (Randy). Of Los Angeles, California. Beloved and devoted husband to Roberta Canale Leavenworth. Loving and devoted father to Alexandra and Nicole. Much beloved son of Serene Leavenworth Felt, and Randolph Leavenworth. Jr. passed away Wednesday, May 5, 1999. His love, friendship, humor, strength and warmth will be greatly missed by his family and by his many, many friends. Funeral services will be held at Hillside Memorial Park in Los Angeles, California, on Tuesday, May 11, 1999, at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, the family request that donations may be made to Jonsson Cancer Center Foundation, at UCLA, for the benefit of Drs. Lee Rosen and John Glaspy.
