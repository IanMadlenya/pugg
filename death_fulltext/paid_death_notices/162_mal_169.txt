
        HOLLINGSWORTH - Alvin C. Peacefully, Friday, July 14, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Marjorie. Father of Kim, Raymond, Stephen, Kevin, Monique, Denise and Jeanette. Grandfather of Mishari, Ariel, Jason, Adam, Kerry, Siobhan, Eilis and Victoria. He was an acclaimed artist with numerous national and international exhibitions, diverse commissions and part of institutional and private permanent collections. The former professor of art at Hostos Community College also was widely recognized as an author, poet and lecturer. His talents, inspiration, commitment and insights will be missed. Viewing is Wednesday, July 19, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, and funeral service Thursday, July 20, at 11 AM at the Hastings Funeral Home, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
