
        WILLINGER-Viola P. Age 95. Of New Rochelle, New York. Loving wife of the late Dr. Louis. Devoted mother of Blossom Miller, Lorraine Kolb, Larry, Warren and Lowell Willinger. Adored grandmother of 15 and great grandmother of 12. Past President, four terms, of the United Order of True Sisters. Warm and giving, her family was her life. Services Friday, January 25th, 12 Noon, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon. Interment Mount Pleasant Memorial Park, Hawthorne, N.Y.
