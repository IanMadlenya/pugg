
        COLE--Bernard Michael, 84, of Boca Raton, passed June 11, 2006. He is survived by his beloved wife Rita, by two loving children, Dr. Alan (Harriet) Cole, Stephanie (John) Eason, and by his wonderful grandchildren Dr. Lisa (Jonathan) Cole Olin, and Rebecca (Adam) Cole Lurie. He was president of The Alan Braid Co., Stribbons Ltd., Weaving Corp. of America and Georgia Narrow Fabrics, Inc., which was ultimately sold to the Blackstone Group. Peers considered him a legend in his industry. He spent the majority of his life in New York, then retired to Boca Raton, Florida, where he enjoyed life at St. Andrews Country Club. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to your favorite charities. Funeral services will be Monday, June 12th, at 2pm at The Gardens Mausoleum, Boca Raton, Florida. Shiva will be at the residence on Monday and Tuesday evening and at the home of Alan and Harriet Cole in Natick, MA, for the remainder of the shiva week. Contact Sinai Memorial Chapels, Delray Beach, Florida, 561-865-1747.
