
        LOPEZ-MORILLAS-Juan, age 83, of Austin, TX, on March 21, 1997, after a brief illness. Professor Lopez-Morillas was Ashbel Smith Professor Emeritus of Spanish at the University of Texas and Kenan Professor Emeritus at Brown University in Providence, R.I., and had a long and distinguished academic career. Survivors include his wife of 59 years, Frances, of Austin; son Martin and wife Sanda of NYC; daughter Consuelo and husband Enrique Merino of Bloomington, IN; son Julian and wife Jannie, of Oakland, CA; grandchildren Diego and Miguel of Bloomington, and Sara of NYC, one brother, Emilio of Madrid, Spain, and numerous nieces and nephews there. At his request, there will be no services.
