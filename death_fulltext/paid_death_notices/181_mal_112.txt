
        MULHAUSER-George C., 79. Of Paramus, NJ, a renowned contemporary furniture designer, died Feb. 7, 2002. He was a veteran of W.W. II and served in Okinawa. Beloved husband of Mary. Loving father of Dan and Chris, Paul and Linda, Mary Ann and Jeff, and George and Christine; and nine grandchildren. Also survived by dear brother Rev. Daniel J. Mulhauser, S.J. and his sister Mary Everts. Family will receive friends Sunday, February 10, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Zander Plaat Memorial Home, S-113 Farview Avenue, Paramus, NJ. Funeral Mass Monday 9:30 AM, Our Lady of Visitation, Paramus. Interment in George Washington Memorial Park.
