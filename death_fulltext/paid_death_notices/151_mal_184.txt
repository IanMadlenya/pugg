
        BULGER-Howard J., age 80, of Hockessin, DE, died Sunday, August 15th, 1999. Mr. Bulger, a Marine Corps veteran, was a teacher and coach at Iona College. He graduated from Manhattan College and Columbia. He is survived by wife, Jeanne, 3 children, 4 stepsons and 14 grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Manhattan College Track Program, Manhattan Parkway, Riverdale, NY 10471.
