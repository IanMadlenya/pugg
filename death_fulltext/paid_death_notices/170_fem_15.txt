
        GOLDBERG-Joan. The entire New York Community Hospital family mourns the loss of Joan Goldberg, a loving and dear friend of the Hospital for more than 18 years. Joan, a member of the Board of Trustees, made substantial contributions to the Hospital and its community. Our condolences to her son Jeffrey, her two daughters and their families. George Weinberger, Chairman Board of Trustees Lin H. Mo, President and Chief Executive Officer
