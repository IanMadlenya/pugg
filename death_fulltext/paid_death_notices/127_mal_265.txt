
        ROBERTS-Seymour. Of Atlantic Beach and Pembroke Pines, FL. 77 years old. On August 26, 1997. Adored husband of forty-five years of Sherry Stauber Roberts. Cherished father of Roy, Michael and Jolie (Mansky) and beloved grandfather of eight. Retired Vice President of Rado Watch. His love of golf and reading only exceeded by love of family, friends and life. Graveside funeral at New Montefiore, Pine Lawn, 10:45 AM Thursday. Meet at Cemetery office.
