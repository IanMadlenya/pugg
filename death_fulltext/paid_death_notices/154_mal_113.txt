
        MISEIK-Shihab, MD. The Department of Medicine at New York Methodist Hospital sadly announces the death of our colleague, Dr. Shihab Miseik. He was a knowledgeable and peaceful young doctor who will be deeply missed. Dr. Miseik graduated from the University of Khartoum in 1991 and was a member of the Sudanese Medical Association. He was a volunteer for the African Relief Agency establishing mobile clinics and delivering food to refugee camps. In July, 1999, he started his residency in Internal Medicine at New York Methodist Hospital. He is survived by his parents.
