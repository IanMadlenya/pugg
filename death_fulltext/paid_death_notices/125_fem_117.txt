
        VAN TIJN-Beatrix. Born on July 21, 1910 in Leipzig, Germany. Died June 12, 1997, in Portland, OR. Married Jacques van Tijn in 1937 and lived in Naarden, Netherlands. Fled to the US in 1940. Worked at Columbia University Library 1956-1980. Volunteered at the Metropolitan Museum of Art Library 1980-1990. She moved to Willamett Manor in Portland, OR. She is survived by her sister Valerie Meyer; two nieces; one step-daughter; five grandchildren; and six greatgrandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations to the US Holocaust Memorial Museum or the Metropolitan Museum of Art is appreciated.
