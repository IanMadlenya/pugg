
        ALFIN--Irwin (10/6/1929-4/19/ 2007). Irwin Alfin of Manhasset, NY, on April 19, 2007, at 77 years of age. Loving husband of Betsy. Cherished father of David and Susan. Devoted grandfather of David, Daniel, Dennis, Deborah, Emily and Kate. Irwin Alfin, a retired cosmetics and fragrance industry executive died Thursday, April 19, in New York City. In 1992 he retired as President and CEO of Alfin Fragrances, Inc., which he founded in 1976. The company went public in 1983 and distributed fine fragrances including Ombre Rose, Bal a Versailles, Joy, Fracas, Cabochard and Gianfranco Ferre. Prior to forming his company, Mr. Alfin served as President of Max Factor, Halston Fragrances and Chanel, Inc., USA. He started his career at Revlon, Inc. In 1985, Mr. Alfin served as president of the Fragrance Foundation and in 1999 was recognized for his contribution to the industry. Mr. Alfin served as a First Lieutenant in the U.S. Army in Korea following his graduation from OCS and Rutgers University in 1951. Funeral Home: Fairchild Funeral Home, Garden City, N.Y.
