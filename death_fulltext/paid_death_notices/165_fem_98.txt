
        HOROWITZ-Mildred. On October 3, 2000, age 96. Born Mildred Scheff in Washington, DC, last surviving youngest and most beautiful of four sisters. She had been widowed for 40 years. She lived in her own apartment until the end, loved and admired by her son, James, four grandchildren, Nina, Claude, James and Theo and one great-grandson, Nile in Paris. Funeral services are private. Sympathies (646) 414-1844.
