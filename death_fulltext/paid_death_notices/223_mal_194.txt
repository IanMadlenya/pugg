
        ROSEN--Sumner, Dr. The Faculty, Advisory Council and Alumni Association of the Columbia University School of Social Work record with sorrow the death of Dr. Sumner Rosen on August 17, 2005. A tenured distinguished Professor, social activist, a columnist, advocate of progressive social policies, and founding member of the Five Borough Institute, he will be remembered for his passionate and sweeping contributions to education, research and the social welfare of working people in New York City and the nation. Sincere condolences are expressed to the family. Jeanette C. Takamura, Dean
