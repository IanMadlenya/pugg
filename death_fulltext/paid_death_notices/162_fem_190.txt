
        GILLIES-Carol Zaiser. July 24, 2000, aged 68, at Cabrini Hospice, NY, of breast cancer, which for eight years she fought without quarter, to the very end: valiant, uncomplaining, a model of how life should be lived. Her husband, Ewen Gillies; sister, Marilyn Ott; stepsons Lindsay, Elliot and Adrian Gillies, stepdaughter Sarah Robertson Gillies; niece Carolyn Martin, nephew Dr. Kevin Martin; their families and a host of loving friends survive her. Contributions in her name should go to the Oncology Research Fund, NYU School of Medicine, c/o Dr. James Speyer, 160 East 32 Street, New York, NY 10016.
