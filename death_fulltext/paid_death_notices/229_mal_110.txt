
        MARCUS--Samuel. On February 5, 2006 at home with Sylvia, his beloved wife of over 50 years. Cherished father of Andrew & Rhonda, adored grandfather of Sydney, friend of Carolyn. He will be deeply missed and never forgotten. Love for children and nature but most of all his family made him the special person he was. Devotion to his business and all people concerned made Sam a role model. His granddaughter Sydney was the light of his life as he was for many. Services will be at RiversideNassau North Chapel, 55 North Shore Rd, Great Neck Thursday, February 9 at 12:30.
