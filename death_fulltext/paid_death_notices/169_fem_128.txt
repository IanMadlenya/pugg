
        MITTLEMAN-Bette Joblin. On Sunday, February 11, 2001. Dearest and loving mother of Brad Joblin and mother-in-law of Barbara Joblin. Cherished grandmother of Blake Joblin. Beloved wife of the late Budd Mittleman. Devoted stepmother of Michael and Peter Mittleman and Kate Arens. Former Professor of Finance and an MBA Graduate of Columbia University. A memorial service will be held at a later date. She had beauty and elegance and was a woman of achievement ahead of her time.
        MITTLEMAN-Bette S.J. The 35 Sutton Place Neighbors are deeply saddened and mourn the passing of our beautiful, feisty friend and colleague. We wish to express our condolences to her son, Brad Joblon, and his family.
