
        NESI -- Carmella. Pelham Manor, NY died peacefully at home on May 24, 2004 at the age of 96. She was born January 13, 1908 in NYC to Nicholas and Francesca (Capozzoli) Nesi. She graduated from NY University and she earned a Master's degree in Education from Columbia University. She had been Principal of PS 7 in Bronx, NY and former District Superintendent of Schools of District 11 in NYC. Carmella is survived by her sister Antoinette Nesi and devoted nieces and nephews and grandnieces and grandnephews. Visiting hours at Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave., Pelham on Tuesday and Wedesnay from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church on Thursday at 10 AM. Intombment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
