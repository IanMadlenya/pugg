
        BRAGALINE-Edward A. Age 92. At the DeWitt Nursing Home, Monday, January 18, from cardiopulmonary-arrest. Until his retirement, Mr. Bragaline was the owner/director of the firm Kent-Bragaline Inc., a supplier of decorative fabrics to the home furnishing industry. He is survived by his dear sister Olga Pedlar; his very attentive nephew Edward Speciale and five other nephews and a niece; as well as his dear friend of many years Louis W. Bowen. A private cryptside service will be held at Woodlawn Cemetery, Wednesday at 1PM, January 27. No flowers thank you, just a prayer for peace and love. A memorial tribute will be announced at a later date.
