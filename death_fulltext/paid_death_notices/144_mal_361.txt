
        TREY-Charles, M.D. The Board of Directors of the American Liver Foundation mourn the loss of one of our founders. Charles Trey was a rare individual, a leader in the science of liver disease and the treatment of tens of thousands of patients. He was instrumental in building the American Liver Foundation and the New England Chapter. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. John M. Vierling, M.D., Chair Alan P. Brownstein, Pres/CEO
