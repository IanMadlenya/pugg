
        GILMARTIN-Teresa V., a.k.a. ''Tedi''. Suddenly on November 2, 2001. Longtime partner of the late Mary Leone. Daughter of the late Katherine and Michael Gilmartin. Sister of the late Catherine Morrissey, Thomas and Patrick J. Gilmartin. Cherished aunt of Elizabeth O'Leary, James Morrissey, Patricia Killeen and Veronica Tompkins. Great-aunt to 11. Former longtime employee of Forbes Magazine. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, December 9, 2001, 10:30 AM at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue (at 20th Street) NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
