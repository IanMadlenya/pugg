
        CONNELLY--Rita, 93. Rita Gorman Connelly of Sea Island, Georgia and Saltaire, New York died peacefully at her home on March 11. Beloved wife of the late Paul W. Connelly, mother of Patricia McAllister and the late Jeanne Heurtematte and the late Paul W. Connelly III; grandmother of Cristin and Paul W. Connelly IV, Michael, Andrew and Matthew McAllister, Darcy Heurtematte Langdon and great grandmother of Grace Connelly Langdon. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated at 11AM on April 1 at St. William's Catholic Church, St. Simons Island, Georgia. Donations may be made to Trinity College, 125 Michigan Avenue, NE, Washinton, DC 20017-1094.
