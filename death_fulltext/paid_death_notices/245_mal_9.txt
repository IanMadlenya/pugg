
        NEUWIRTH--Richard. Testrite Instrument Co., Inc., mourns the loss of Richard Neuwirth. Testrite was founded by Richard's father, Herman Neuwirth in 1919. Everyone at Testrite knew Dick to be a kind and caring individual. Dick is survived by his wife Eileen, daughter Shelby Richardson, son-in-law Doug and grandson Dylan Ryan also by his sister Enid and brother-in-law Harold Rubin. Funeral will be at Gutterman and Musicant, Hackensack, NJ, 11:30 am Sunday.
        $(6$)NEUWIRTH--Richard Samuel, on May 29, 2007, at age 75, of Fort Lee, NJ, and Boca Raton, FL. Husband of Eileen, father of Shelby and her husband Doug, grandfather of Dylan Ryan, brother of Enid. Treasurer of Testrite Instruments, Inc., Hackensack, NJ. Member of Montammy and Broken Sound Country Clubs. Services at 11:30am, Sunday June 3rd at Gutterman-Musicant, Hackensack, NJ. Visitation in Fort Lee on Sunday, June 3rd and Monday, June 4th.
