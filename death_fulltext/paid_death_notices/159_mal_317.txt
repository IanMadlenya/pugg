
        FISKE-Lawrence, of Black Mountain, NC and formerly of Darien, CT and East Dorset, VT died on April 19, 2000. He was 88 years old. Mr. Fiske, a long term veteran of the steamship industry, served in various executive positions having been appointed President of Moore-McCormack Lines and its parent company Moore-McCormack Resources. He retired as Vice Chairman of the Board.
