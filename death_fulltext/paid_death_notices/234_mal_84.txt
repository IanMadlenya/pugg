
        HUNTER--John A., of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of NYC and Easthampton, died on July 3, 2006. A graduate of Columbia University and Founder and CEO of Delta Resources, a computer systems integration company. His many friends and former wife Sharman will miss his incredible charm, wit and generous heart. Burial will be at Mt. Moriah Cemetery, Fairview, NJ, Sunday, July 9, 12:30 PM.
