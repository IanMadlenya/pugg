
        ADLER--Howard J. On August 7, 2004, age 87. Columbia College and Law, 1941. Practiced law in New York City until 1990, when he retired to Clearwater from Scarsdale, NY. Admired for his forthright and direct manner in legal matters; loved for his sense of humor. Tireless advocate for equality. Passionate in his pursuits and friendships. Volunteer at Mease Hospital, Countryside Library, Suncoast Hospice, Gulf Coast Legal Services. On the Board of Friends of Clearwater Library, Pinellas Opera League. Arbitrator for NASD, NYSE. He was blessed with a loving family and is survived by his wife of over 50 years, Rochelle (Picker) Adler, his daughter Nancy of NYC, his son Michael of Elmsford, NY, and his brother Abner of Jupiter, FL. Memorial contribuions may be made to Suncoast Hospice, 309 East 30th Bay Drive, Largo, FL 33770; or NAMI.
