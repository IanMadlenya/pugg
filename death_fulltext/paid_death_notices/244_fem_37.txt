
        COHEN--Linda. The Board of Directors of the Danbury Health Systems, Danbury Hospital and the Danbury Hospital Development Fund, along with the administration, medical staff and employees of Danbury Hospital mourn the passing of Linda Cohen, a dear friend and benefactor of Danbury Hospital. Mrs. Cohen, with her husband, Stephen R. Cohen, recently established the Linda and Stephen R. Cohen Endowed Chair in Vascular Surgery at Danbury Hospital. A compassionate and caring person, Mrs. Cohen will be deeply missed by all who had the opportunity to know her. The Danbury Hospital extends its sincere condolences to Mr. Stephen R. Cohen and members of the family. John M. Murphy, M.D., Chairman of the Board, Danbury Health Systems, Inc. Frank J. Kelly, Pres. & CEO, Danbury Health Systems, Inc. Catherine S. Halkett, Pres. Danbury Hospital Development Fund, Inc.
