
        SACHS-Dr. Lucille H. Blum. The Trustees, staff and faculty of Postgraduate Center for Mental Health mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague, Lucille Hollander Blum, Ph.D. Dr. Blum, a 1953 graduate of our Adult Psychoanalytic Training Program, had a distinguished career as an analyst and educator and authored numerous books and journal articles. She joined our Board of Trustees in 1984 and we will miss her wisdom and expertise. Our deepest condolences go to her family. The Trustees, Staff and Faculty Postgraduate Center for Mental Health
