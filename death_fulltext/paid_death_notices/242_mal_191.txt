
        FENG--Shao Chang, on March 17, 2007, of Forest Hills, NY. Beloved husband of Kuo Chen, loving father of Arleen and Clarence (Dorothee), grandfather to Christine and Carolina, also survived by his brother Chen Chang. Uncle to Harry, Judy and Ling, and other nieces and nephews in Canada and China. Shao was a founding member of the Chekiang University Alumni Association. He installed aircraft radios for the Flying Tigers in World War II and went on to develop early portable transistor radios in the United States. Memorial visitation at Fox Funeral Home, Inc., 98-07 Ascan Ave., Forest Hills, Sunday, March 25, 2007, 1-4 pm. Donations in lieu of flowers to your favorite charity in his name are appreciated.
