
        BLUM-Lilly. The American Jewish Historical Society and The Center for Jewish History extend their condolences to our Trustee and friend, George Blumenthal and his family on the loss of their beloved aunt, Lilly Blum. She dedicated her professional life to unselfish medical research and in her private life enriched the lives of her family and friends. Kenneth J. Bialkin, President American Jewish Hist. Society Bruce Slovin, Chairman Center for Jewish History
