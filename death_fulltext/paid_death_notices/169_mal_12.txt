
        MINK-Joseph. On January 31, 2001, in his 93rd year. Survived by his loving wife and best friend, Florence, loving father of Marilyn Mundus and the late Phyllis Krain, adored grandfather and greatgrandfather. Service Friday, February 2, 2001, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Center for Special Studies at New York Hospital, c/o of Dr. Jonathan Jacobs, 449 East 68th Street, New York City 10021.
