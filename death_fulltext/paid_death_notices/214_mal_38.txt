
        STEIN--Dr. Ralph. November 1, 2004, suddenly taken from us at age 77. Beloved husband of Lore. Devoted father of Janet and Jonathan Schultz, Vivian and Michael Dubin, and Lydia and Stuart Bernstein. Cherished grandfather of Joshua, David, Daniel, Marc, Lauren, Kari, Andrew, Mathew, and Simone. Stepfather of Drs. Minna and Steven Selub, Nancy and Mark Horton, and six grandchildren. Loving brother of Trude and Joe Pollack. Caring uncle of Dr. Ralph Rosenberg and Alan Rosenberg. Dr. Ralph Stein was predeceased by his devoted wife of 36 years, Simone. He was a compassionate pediatrician for over 40 years, a past-president of Queens Pediatric Society, a proud Veteran, and past-president of Congregation Habonim. He will be in our hearts forever. Services will be held on Wednesday, 1:00PM at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd., and 76 Road., Forest Hills, NY.
