
        WIDMER--Robert. Of New York City, died May 28, 2005. Born in Perth, Western Australia, Bob served the United Nations since 1947. He was the beloved husband of the late Freny, father of Carolyn and Linda, grandfather of Diana, and devoted friend of Cathy Campen. Family and friends will gather 7-9 PM on Wed., June 1, at the Frank E. Campbell FH, Madison Avenue at 81 St. Memorial donations may be made to the US Fund for UNICEF, 333 East 38 Street, NY 10016.
