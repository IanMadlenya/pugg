
        WEINBERG--Nathan, Self help Community Services is deeply saddened by the passing of Nathan Weinberg, a philanthropic leader whose kindness and generosity has enriched the lives of thousands of individuals through the good works of The Harry and Jeanette Weinberg Foundation. Thanks to Mr. Weinberg's foresight, our senior citizens live with independence and dignity at our Weinberg campus. We are also most grateful for the many other significant enhancements we have received for our programs and services. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Lillian, children Donn and Sharon; Glenn and Debbi; Joseph and Debra; and to the entire Weinberg family. Victor A. Wyler, President Stuart C. Kaplan, CEO
