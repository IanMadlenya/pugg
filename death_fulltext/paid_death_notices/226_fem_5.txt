
        BECKHARD--Vera, 93, died in her home peacefully on November 1. A Holocaust survivor, she is survived by her cherished family; daughter Eva, her son and daughtersin-law Jerry, Ellie and Margo; her eight grandchildren, Susan, Ellen, Karen, Richard, Anita, Tom, Liz and Jane, and her nine great-grandchildren, Kim, Lisa, Julia, Aaron, Adam, Olivia, Alex, Carter and Bobby. She was loving, strong, and an inspiration. Always loved, she will be forever missed. Funeral service, Riverside Memorial Chapel on November 3 at 11:30AM.
