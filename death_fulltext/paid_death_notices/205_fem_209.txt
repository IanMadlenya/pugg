
        WITCHEL--Eve, on February 25th, 2004, at the age of 98. Beloved wife of the late Irwin. Loving mother of Molly Ann and her husband Jerome Goldstein, and Milton and his wife Patricia Witchel. Adoring grandmother of Janet Bell, James Goldstein, Harry Witchel and Leigh Witchel. Proud great-grandmother of Sarah and Jennifer Bell, Becky, Caroline and Sam Goldstein. She was the Accounting Clerk for the Westchester County Surrogates Court until her retirement. She was also the Vice Chairperson for the Eastchester Republican Party. Funeral service Friday, February 27, 2004, at 10 AM, from the ''Westchester Reform Temple'', 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
