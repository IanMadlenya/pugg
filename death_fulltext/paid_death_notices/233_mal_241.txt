
        MOSHER--James W. Lawyer, 65 died May 31, 2006 in Manhattan. His practice was in the theater and music industry. Prior to forming his own firm, he had been a partner in Zissu, Stein, Bergman, Couture and Mosher. A memorial is planed for June 23, 2006 at the Princeton Club in New York, 15 West 43rd Street at 5PM. Memorial gifts in his name may be made to The ACLU.
        $(6$)MOSHER--James. The Board of Directors of 186 Riverside Corp. is saddened by the loss of our colleague and neighbor, James Mosher. Jim worked tirelessly with us and we will miss his good nature and wise counsel. We extend our deepest sympathies to his friends and family.
