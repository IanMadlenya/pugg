
        DWYER-Gertrude Kirk. On July 31, 1998. Cherished wife of the late James J. Dwyer, Commissioner of Public Safety, White Plains, NY. A graduate of the College of New Rochelle and NYU, Gertrude taught for 20 years in the White Plains school system. A woman of great faith, Gertrude passed her love on abundantly to her four children and their spouses: James Jr. and Janice, William and Pamela, Dennis and Deborah, her daughter and son-in-law Teresita and Michael O'Leary and her beloved grandchildren. Friends are invited to call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Ave, NYC, Monday, August 3, from 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at Immaculate Conception Church, 414 East 14th St, on Tuesday, August 4, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the College of New Rochelle.
