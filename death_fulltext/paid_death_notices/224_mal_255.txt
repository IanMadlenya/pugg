
        LING--Alfred S.C. 77, on September 13, 2005. A graduate of Princeton University, the University of Illinois (MS, Physiology) and at he University of Maryland (MD and Ph.D, Pharmacology). Following his residency in internal medicine at the Pennsylvania Hospital, he joined Rockefeller University Faculty and was Senior Physician at Rockefeller Hospital. He retired as Vice President for Clinical Research for Wyeth Research. He served his Princeton Class of 1949 as Class Agent and President. He leaves his wife, Helen; his children, Dr. Geoffrey Ling of Maryland, Dr. Douglas Ling of New Jersey, and Karen Lee Ling, Esq. of New York; and five grandchildren.
