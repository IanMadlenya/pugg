
        KRUSKAL--William H., 85, of Chicago. Died on April 21, 2005. Ernest DeWitt Burton Distinguished Service Professor Emeritus in Statistics at the University of Chicago. His survivors include sons Vincent, Tom, and Jody; grandchildren Lily, Peter, Michael, Eliott, and Zachary; siblings Rosaly, Martin David, and Joseph. Memorial service May 21 at the university.
