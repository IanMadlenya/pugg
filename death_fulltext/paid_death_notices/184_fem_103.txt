
        MARCUS-Alice, 87. Formerly of New York City, died on Sunday, May 12. Her husband Abraham Marcus preceded her in death. She is survived by her son Robert & wife Glynda, four grandchildren and one great grandchild. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the San Antonio Jewish Community Center, 12500 N.W. Military Highway, Suite 275, San Antonio, Texas 78231.
