
        DIBNER-Esther L. Of Portland, Maine, died on January 4th after a short illness. She was 89. Mrs. Dibner was born and raised in Brooklyn and subsequently moved to White Plains. She and her late husband, Paul A. Dibner, operated two children's summer camps in Maine, Tall Timbers, for boys, and Truda for girls. She is survived by her four children, Hart K. Dibner, Charles W. Dibner, Mauri D. Schwartz, Willa Dibner and four grandchildren. A private interment service will be held in White Plains.
