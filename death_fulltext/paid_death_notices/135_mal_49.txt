
        BASS-Harry Wesley, Jr. The Council and membership of The American Numismatic Society, New York, mourn the death of their esteemed past President and benefactor, Harry W. Bass, Jr., who died April 4, 1998. A member of the ANS governing board since 1972, he served as First Vice-President, 1974 - 1978, President 1978-1984, and at his death continued as a Councillor and a member of the Executive Committee. For 25 years, he dedicated his business acumen, his numismatic expertise, and his energies and resources to the betterment of this Society. To his wife, Doris, his children and grandchildren, the ANS extends its deepest sympathy.
