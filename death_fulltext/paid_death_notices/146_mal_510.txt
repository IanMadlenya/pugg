
        KRELL-Mortimer. The Officers, Directors and Trustees of Temple Emanu-El, Englewood, NJ are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and past President, Mort Krell. Mort was a devoted and deeply committed member of our congregation. Our condolences to his beloved wife Rita, his brother David, his children and grandchildren. A generous man of honesty and conviction. He will be greatly missed. Contributions may be made to Columbia Presbyterian Renal Transplant Department, the Holy Name Hospital Dialysis Center, Teaneck, New Jersey, or Women's American ORT.
