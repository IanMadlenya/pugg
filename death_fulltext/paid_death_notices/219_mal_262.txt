
        BENNETT--John J. Jr. The Board of Trustees of the St. Giles Foundation mourns the passing of our beloved friend and fellow Trustee, John J. Bennett Jr. During his many years of faithful service we were inspired by Jack's amazing spirit and quick smile. We honor his memory by continuing the mission of St. Giles with that spirit in our hearts. Our deepest sympathies to his wife Jean and their children. Richard T. Arkwright, Pres. St. Giles Foundation
        BENNETT--John J. Jr. St. John's Residence mourns the passing of John Bennett, who has served as longtime president of the Catholic Child Care Society, and Board President of St. John's. We honor his lifelong devotion to vulnerable children and offer our deepest sympathy to his loving family. Board of Directors St. John's Residence
