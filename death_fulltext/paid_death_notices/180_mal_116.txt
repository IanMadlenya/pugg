
        BALLAN-Erik. Born January 20, 1955 in New York, died on January 5, 2002 in McKinney, TX. A devoted and loving husband and father of two. He worked for Consultants Choice in Dallas. He was preceded in death by his father, Eli Ballan, his uncle, Leon Ballan, and his aunt, Ruthie Lindner. He is survived by his loving wife, Ranay Neal Ballan; son, Matthew Ballan, & daughter, Anna Ballan; mother, Leonore Ballan and sister, Leslie Ballan. He will be dearly missed by those who knew and loved him. Funeral services will be held at Levitt-Weinstein Funeral Home Tuesday, January 8, 954-427-6500.
