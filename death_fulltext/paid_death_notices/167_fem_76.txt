
        McDOWELL - Lillian Kober. Macy's Department Store Manager for 50 years. Died on December 7 in her 94th year, formerly of Brooklyn, N.Y. Beloved wife of the late Merritt D. McDowell. Survived by her sister, Doris Flood. Visiting Sunday 2-5 and 7-9:30PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave, Floral Park, N.Y. (at RR Plaza). Religious service Sunday, 4PM at Funeral Home. Interment Kensico Cemetery.
