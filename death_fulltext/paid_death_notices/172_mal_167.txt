
        HEISLER-Irving. (A.K.A. ''Hickey''). Died May 14, 2001 at the age of 87. Beloved husband of Vivienne (deceased). He is survived by his loving son and daughter, Jeff and Toni (Jerry), his adored granddaughter Rebecca, and his dear friend Arlene. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to the New York Public Library, or The Veterans of the Abraham Lincoln Brigade. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
