
        SHEEHAN-Dennis W. Age 65, of Tappahannock, Virginia, and Lake Forest, Illinois died on Sunday, March 21, 1999. He was a member of St. Timothy's Catholic Church, Tappahannock. He was former Chairman & CEO of Axia, Inc and former Chairman of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce. Mr. Sheehan served on the Board of Directors of Axia, Inc., Allied Healthcare, National Council on Crime & Delinquency and Greenfield Corp. He was a member of the Metropolitan Club, Tastevin, Commonwealth Club, St. Louis Club, Downtown Club and Chicago Club. He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth Hellyer Sheehan; three children, Dennis W. Sheehan, Jr., (Kathleen C. Mahoney), Catherine E. Sheehan and John E. Sheehan (Maureen M.); two sisters, Janet B. Helm and Helyn S. Harris; four brothers, Greg A. Sheehan, Brian C. Sheehan, Theodore D. Sheehan and Edward F. Sheehan; five grandchildren, Tara E. Sheehan, Kelsey K. Sheehan, Claire M. Sheehan, Haley M. Sheehan and John E. Sheehan, Jr. The family will receive friends from 6:00-8:00 p.m. Wednesday at St. Timothy's Catholic Church where the Funeral Mass will be held 11:00 a.m. Thursday. Interment will be private. A reception will be held at the residence from 3:00-5:00 p.m. Thursday afternoon. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the Sheehan Family Foundation P.O. Box 2032, Tappahannock, VA 22560. Marks-Bristow Funeral Home, Tappahannock, VA.
