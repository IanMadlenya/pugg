
        SHULMAN-Max. The Bialystoker Synagogue family mourns the passing of the beloved father of its devoted President, Hon. Martin Shulman. Max Shulman dedicated himself to the values of family, religious inspiration and avodas hashem. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Yitzhak Singer Ira M.
