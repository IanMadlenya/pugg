
        DeROSA--Pat. The Officers, Governors and members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Pat DeRosa on February 6, 2004. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held February 7th and 8th, at the Vernon C. Wagner Funeral Home, 655 Old Country Rd., Plainview, N.Y. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
