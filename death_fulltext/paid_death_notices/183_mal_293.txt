
        FISCHER-Louis T. Of Yonkers on April 23, 2002, at the age of 89. Survived by his wife Edith, sons James and Stephen, and five grandchildren. Mr. Fischer was Senior V.P., and Media Director of Danzer, Fitzgerald, Sample Advertising Agency, where he was honored with several distinguished Advertising Industry Awards, until his retirement in 1977, where upon he served as Assistant to the President of Concordia College, Bronxville for 20 years. He was active in his church, serving as Trustee and President of the Village Lutheran Church, Bronxville and as an officer of the Atlantic District of the Lutheran Church, Missouri Synod. Calling hours are at The Fred H. McGrath & Son, Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 7 to 9PM Thursday and 2 to 4 PM and 7 to 9 PM Friday. Services at the Village Lutheran Church, 172 White Plains Road, Bronxville on Saturday, April 27, 2002 at 10:30 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Concordia College, 171 White Plains Road, Bronxville, New York 10708.
