
        HESSERT--Richard Collings, 83, passed on May 23rd. Born in Los Angeles on November 29th, 1923, Dick was Navy Officer during WWII and a graduate of Thunderbird. His career in International Banking took him to Cuba, Columbia, Mexico and the Carribean. In addition, his love of travel extended from the Amazon to Tibet. Known as ''Poppy'' to his family, he leaves four children, Kel, Lois, Rich and Chris, their spouses, five grandchildren and many friends and relations. He is predeceased by his wife of 55 years ''Tink'', whom he cherished. His humor, kindness and love of life will be missed.
