
        GODDARD-Ruth. The Board of Directors of the American Theatre Wing notes with sorrow the passing of our long time Board Member and devoted supporter, Ruth Goddard. Her presence will forever be missed. Isabelle Stevenson, Chairman Roy A. Somlyo, President
        GODDARD-Ruth R. On June 13th at home at almost 99 years old. A strong, wonderful woman with lots of love for all. A timeless leader with many passions. A great friend and role model for our family. Forever in our hearts. She will be missed. Jim, Susan and Hannah Baumann
