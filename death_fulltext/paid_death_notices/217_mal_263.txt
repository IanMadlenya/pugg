
        SCHECHTER -- Emanuel (Manny), 97, loving husband of the late Rosalie Cohen Schechter of Harrison, NY and father of public relations executive Stephen M. Schechter of New York City, died peacefully in his sleep after a short illness. Services will be held on Monday, February 21 at 1PM at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY. Contributions in his name can be made to the Hadassah Medical Organization, 50 W. 58th St., NYC 10019 or the American Committee of the Shaare Zedek Medical Center, 49 W. 45th St., NYC 10036.
