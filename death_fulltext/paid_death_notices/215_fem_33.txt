
        ARNSTEIN--Ethel. Age 100. Widow of Morty Arnstein, intimate sister-in-law of Gertrude Bonime, loving and loved aunt of Naomi and Allen Broadwin, Judith and Carl Felsenfeld, and Betty and Stanley Greenfield, greataunt and great-great-aunt, a significant presence wherever she dwelt and extraordinary bridge player. She will be missed.
