
        NAGASHIMA-Hideo, M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of our distinguished Professor Emeritus of Anesthesiology. A member of our faculty since 1964, he was a skilled and caring physician who also made important contributions to the advancement of anesthesiology through his pioneering research. A beloved mentor to several generations of students and residents, he exemplified the highest ideals of academic medicine in all of his endeavors. He will be greatly missed by all who had the privilege of knowing him. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Sachiko and the entire family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University.
