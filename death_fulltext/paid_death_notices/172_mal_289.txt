
        McLEAN-Malcom P. On May 25, 2001, at age 87. Beloved husband of Irena McLean and the late Margaret McLean. Loving father of Malcom McLean, Nancy McLean Parker and Patricia McLean Mendenhall. Cherished grandfather of 11 and great-grandfather of three. Devoted brother of Clara McLean, Mary McLean McFadyen, Margaret McLean Newberry and John Luther McLean. Interment private. Services will be held at 11AM on Wednesday May 30 at 5th Avenue Presbyterian Church, 7 West 55 St, NY, NY.
        McLEAN-Malcom P. Mourning the loss of a gentle genius whose contributions over 45 years changed the face of world commerce forever. We'll miss his friendship and kindness. Condolences to family and friends. Barbara Spector Yeninas
