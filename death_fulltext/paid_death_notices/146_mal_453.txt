
        DONLAN-Rev. Thomas C., O.P. Who spent the eight year period from 1989 to 1997 with us at Saint Agnes Church, 143 East 43rd St, NYC, died February 25th. We invite his friends in New York to join our parishioners at a Month's Mind Memorial Mass for him this Thursday evening, March 25th at 6:30 P.M. Bishop Edwin Broderick will be the principal celebrant and homilist. Music of Gregorian Chant and Movements from The Durufle Requiem.
