
        GUERNSEY-Otis. The Council of The Dramatists Guild of America mourns the loss of our colleague, Otis Guernsey. Mr. Guernsey was a lifetime member of the Guild, as well as the founder of ''The Dramatists Guild Quarterly,'' which he served as original editor for thirty years. Our deepest sympathies to his family. He will be missed. John Weidman, President The Dramatists Guild of America
