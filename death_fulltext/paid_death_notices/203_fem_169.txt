
        GREENBERG--Gwen J. The Brooklyn Law School Community mourns the loss of Gwen J. Greenberg, the School's Director of Alumni Relations, her mother, Lenore, and her sister, Jill Tuck, in a tragic accident in Mexico. Gwen's energy, intelligence and grace will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest condolences to the family. Joan G. Wexler, Dean Stuart Subotnick, Chairman of Board of Trustees Susan M. Karten, President of the Alumni Association
