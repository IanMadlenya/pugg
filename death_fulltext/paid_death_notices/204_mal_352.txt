
        PIERCE--Robert Henry Horace Jr., Ph.D. Chemist of Wilmington, Delaware, passed away on January 22, 2004, in Crofton, Maryland. He worked for United States Steel and DuPont. He was predeceased by his wife Marion Armbruster Pierce, a Ph.D. chemist, and is survived by two Ph.D. chemist daughters, Dorothy Helen Pierce of Maryland and Roberta Marion Pierce of Pennsylvania. Interment is at Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, New York.
