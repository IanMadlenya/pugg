
        ARMSTRONG--Gary Lee, 59, died of AIDS at home in Manhattan on July 19. A native of Shelby, Ohio, Gary was an analyst at JP Morgan. Gary participated in the first protease inhibitor trial upon which Dr. David Ho based his Multidrug protocol to counter resistance developed in early patients. a founding member of the NYC Gay Men's Chorus where he met his partner of 16 years, Glenn Peterson, Gary is also survived by their two children, Dakota Armstrong peterson, 11, and Matt Armstrong Peterson, 9; brothers Paul, Richard and sister Jocelyn McCauley, Ohio; sister Sally Delamater, Syracuse; and 17 nieces, nephews.
