
        GROSSMAN-Herbert. On December 29, 1997. Beloved husband of Sandra. Devoted father of Robin, Lynne and Laurie. Dedicated father-in-law of Matthew, Allen and Jeff. Cherished grandfather of Andrew, Jeffrey, Jackson and Joseph. Dear brother of Helen Graf. Loved and admired by all who knew him. Brilliant, honest and dynamic with much wisdom and integrity. Well known in the pharmaceuticals industry as a pioneer in marketing and strategic planning. At the time of his death, Herbert Grossman was President and Chief Executive Officer of Beacon Laboratories in Wayne, NJ. He will be dearly missed but lovingly remembered. Contributions to the American Heart Association or charity of choice.
