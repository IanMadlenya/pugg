
        KAPLAN-Muriel. Beloved wife of Sol for 59 years. Died peacefully at home on Friday, January 21, 2000, at age 85. Cherished mother of Susan Silberstein and dear motherin-law of her husband Alan. Adored Mimi to grandchildren Corey Shdaimah and Andrew Silberstein, and their spouses, Michelle and Amichai, and great-grandchildren Cliel, Elad and Olivia. Devoted friend of her housekeeper for 50 years, Ida Lee Robinson. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Services will be held at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapel,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI, on Sunday, January 23, at 1:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family has requested donations be made to the Parker Comprehensive Community Hospice.
