
        GOLDBERG-Leo Quincy. Died on December 8, in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 84 years old. He was predeceased in 1993 by his wife of 45 years, Esther. He is survived by his son Joseph Goldberg, daughter-in-law Rona and their children Marissa, Jaclyn and Michael and his daughter Mindy Goldberg-Tamarkin, son-in-law Cary Tamarkin and their son Eli. Quincy leaves behind the legacy of a life committed to the pursuit of social justice. He was an early leader in the trade union movement followed by a career in social work, creating community mental health clinics through Maimonides Hospital. He lived a passionate life and will be deeply missed.
