        MAMELOK-Alfred, M.D. The medical staff and employees of Beth Israel Medical Center are saddened over the death of our colleague, Alfred Mamelok, M.D. Dr. Mamelok was an esteemed member of the ophthalmology department at Beth Israel for many years and served as the chief of ophthalmology at Beth Israel Medical Center-Singer Division (formerly Doctors Hospital) during the 1980's. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family and loved ones. Matthew E. Fink, M.D. President and C.E.O H. Jay Wisnicki, M.D. Acting Chairman, Ophthalmology Donald W. Hoskins, M.D. Chief Medical Officer Henry S. Singman, M.D. President, Medical Board Robert L. Salant, M.D. President, Doctors Alumni Assoc.
        MAMELOK-Alfred E., MD. We are saddened to learn about the passing of Alfred E. Mamelok, MD., a distinguished ophthalmology surgeon who has been on the staff of Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital since 1953.
