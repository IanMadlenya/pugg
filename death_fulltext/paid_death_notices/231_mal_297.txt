
        MATTHEWS--MichaelFlagler. Michael passed away on April 24, 2006, in Hawaii. He was born on May 7, 1984, in New York City and raised in Palm Beach, Florida. Michael was 21 years old at the time of his sudden death. He was the beloved son of William Morrison Matthews and Jean Rhodes Matthews of Palm Beach. Michael graduated from the Palm Beach Day School. He also attended the Eaglebrook School in Massachusetts and the Choate Rosemary Hall School in Connecticut. Michael loved tennis and was a ranked tennis player in Florida. He was a gifted student, excelling in math and art. Michael was devoted to his family, who are tremendously saddened by his loss.In addition to his parents, Michael is survived by his siblings, Christina, Alexander, and Natalie Matthews of Palm Beach. He is also survived by his grandmother, Mrs. Kent Rhodes, and his uncles and aunts, Mr. and Mrs. George G. Matthews, Mr. and Mrs. G. F. Robert Hanke, Dr. and Mrs. William T. Seed, Mr. David C. Rhodes, and Mr. Mark Rhodes. A Memorial Service celebrating Michael's life will be held on Saturday, April 29, 2006, at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda - By - The - Sea in Palm Beach at 11:00 A.M. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Michael Flagler Matthews Sports Education Fund for the Community Foundation of Palm Beach and Martin Counties, 319 Clematis Street, Suite 900, West Palm Beach, Florida 33401. For information: Quattlebaum Funeral Home, (561)-832-5171.
