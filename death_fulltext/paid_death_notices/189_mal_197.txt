
        DEUTSCH-Hans P. Passed away on October 16, 2002 at the age of 78. Born in 1924 in Vienna, Austria and immigrated to Mexico in 1939. He graduated from the Mexican Polytechnic School as a Chemical Engineer, after which he owned and operated a glass factory. In 1977 he moved to New York where he continued to work in the glass industry. He is survived by his loving wife, Lore Simon Deutsch and his daughters, Judith Stern and Sandy Angevine, and his grandchildren, Sarah and Daniel Stern, and Jake Angevine.
