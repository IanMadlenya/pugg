
        ISRAEL-Ruth Lustbader. Beloved mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother, died in California, September 20, in her 101st year. Born and raised in New York City, a longtime resident and keen observer of the urban scene, she took part in many of our civic and charitable activities, from the Girl Scouts to Civil Defense during World War II to the alumni associations and bookshop of her alma maters, Barnard and Bryn Mawr. Widow of the attorney, J. Leon Israel, she was mother of Eve Finestone and Peter Israel, grandmother of Anne, Stephen & Peter Finestone and Alexandra and the late Elie Israel, and greatgrandmother of Sarah and Rachel Dolan and Miranda and Isaac Finestone.
