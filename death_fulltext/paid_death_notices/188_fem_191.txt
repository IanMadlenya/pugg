
        KOBYLANSKI-Helen (nee Meltzer). 86, of NYC, on September 20, 2002. Survived by beloved daughter, Felica of NYC; sister Rose, and the late Robert. Born in Newark, moved to Oak Park, MI, in 1952, to marry the late Jack. Returning to NYC in 1985, she enjoyed singing, was a prolific writer and consummate entertainer. Contributions to the American Friends of The Hebrew University, 11 E. 69 St., NYC 10021, where her daughter may be contacted.
