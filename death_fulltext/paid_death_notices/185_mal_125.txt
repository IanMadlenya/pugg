
        WIGHT-John Welles. Of Maplewood, NJ, formerly of Skidaway Island, GA, and Madison, NJ, on June 7, 2002. Husband of 61 years to the late Elma Needles Wight. Father of Susan Wight Poinier of Duxbury, MA, John Welles Wight, Jr. of Madison, NJ and William Needles Wight of Short Hills, NJ. Brother of Barbara Biddson, Shirley Keeney and Richard Wight. He is also survived by ten grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Presbyterian Church of New Vernon, Lee's Hill Rd., New Vernon, NJ, on Tuesday, June 11, 2002, at 11 AM. Those who wish may contribute in his memory to either Cornell University, c/o Planned Giving, 55 Brown Rd., Ithaca, NY 14850 or the Ekwanok Scholarship Trust, PO Box 467, Manchester, VT 05254-0467. For more information contact Wm. A. Bradley & Son, Funeral Directors, Chatham, NJ, or go to www.bradleyfuneralhomes.com
