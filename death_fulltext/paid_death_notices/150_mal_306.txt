
        HERSHKOFF-Martin. Age 78. Beloved husband of Sylvia for 54 years. Devoted father to Kenneth and Helen. Loving grandfather to Lauren, Joshua and Ben. Died suddenly at home on July 17, 1999. A graduate of City College, an Army veteran from WW II, a retired certified public accountant, Marty was a lover of books and ideas, and will be sorely missed by his family. Services are July 19 at noon, I. J. Morris, 1895 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn, NY.
