
        MONTGOMERY-Robert H., Jr. The partners, counsel, associates and staff of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison greatly mourn the loss of our friend and colleague, Robert H. Montgomery, Jr. Bob was the head of our Entertainment Department for many years and a member of the Paul, Weiss family for 50 years. Bob was also the Trustee of the Cole Porter Musical Literary Property Trusts for 23 years. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Henriette, daughter, Margaret, son, Sam and two grandchildren, Roland and Robert.
        MONTGOMERY - Robert. The Editorial and Advisory Boards of Partisan Review mourn the loss of a great man, valuable advisor, and dear friend. Bob Montgomery was an active board member of PR from its earliest days and will be deeply missed. William Phillips, Editor-in-Chief Edith Kurzweil, Editor Joanna S. Rose, Chairman
