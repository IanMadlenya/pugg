
        GIGES--Shirley A. Loving wife, mother, grandmother and great - grandmother passed away early Tuesday morning, the 24th of April. Born in Valdosta, GA, January 22, 1922, she moved to White Plains in 1955 with her husband, Melvin, and three children. She devoted her life to her family and was active in a variety of community organizations. The first female vice president of the Temple Israel Center, White Plains. She was especially proud of her role as membership chairman. Education and learning were important to her, and she completed her college education by earning a bachelors degree in 1976 at the College of New Rochelle. She is survived by two sisters, Jackie Wahnish of Orlando, FL, Gloria Kulbersh of Tifton, GA; three children, Marc Giges of White Plains, Carole Moskovitz of Marina del Rey, CA, Elissa Giges of Culver City, CA; daughter-in-law Nancy Giges of White Plains; five grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Funeral services will be held at Temple Israel Center in White Plains at 10am, Thursday, with burial following at Sharon Gardens in Valhalla, NY.
