
        SMYTH--Lila Harkins, of Bronxville, NY. Died at home of a recurring brain tumor on November 26, 2003, in her eighth year of life. Cherished daughter of Dr. James and Clare Hollensteiner Smyth. Beloved sister of Luke and William Smyth. Granddaughter of Joseph and Patricia Smyth and of Robert and Letitia Baldrige Hollensteiner. Great-niece of Robert Baldridge and of the late Secretary of Commerce Malcolm Baldrige and of Margaret Baldrige, and of Louise Van der Vort. Niece of Malcolm Baldrige and Carey Hollensteiner, and of Joseph K. Smyth, Una Smyth and Kathleen Smyth Daunais. Mass of Christian burial services private. Contributions may be made to ''Making Headway Foundation Inc.'', dedicated to the care of children with brain and spinal cord tumors, 115 King Street, Chappaqua, New York 10514.
