
        SILVERSTONE-Sidney M., M.D. It is with profound sorrow that The Mount Sinai Alumni mourns the passing of our distinguished friend and colleague. Dr. Silverstone served Mount Sinai for over sixty years as a clinician and teacher and has served humanity as a world renowned radiation oncologist. In 1984, the Mount Sinai Alumni presented its most coveted award, the Jacob Medallion, to Dr. Silverstone in acknowledgement of his extraordinary contributions to the specialty of radiation oncology and his life long devotion to Mount Sinai and the Alumni. A caring and beloved physician, he will be missed. Our sincere condolences are extented to his entire family. Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D., President William G. Stein, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria L. Padilla, M.D., Secretary George Raptis, M.D., Treasurer
