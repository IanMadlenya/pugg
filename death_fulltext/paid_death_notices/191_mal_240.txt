
        SULLIVAN-Daniel J. A St. Louis labor lawyer and native of New York City, died December 17 at his home in Chesterfield, MO. He was 85. A graduate of the University of Notre Dame (1940) and Columbia University Law School (1947), he was a retired senior partner at Lewis, Rice & Fingersh, a firm he joined in 1952. He was a World War II Army veteran (194246). He is survived by his wife, Anne; four children and eight grandchildren.
