
        KAPLAN--Henry. Television and stage director Henry Kaplan died Wednesday, September 14 at the age of 79. His television and stage credits included over 25 years with ABC-TV's ''All My Children'' as well as ''Dark Shadows'' and ''The Doctors.'' His stage credits included ''Any Wednesday,'' ''P.S. I Love You'' and ''The White House.'' Mr. Kaplan also directed for Granada Television in London as well as many shows for London's West End.
