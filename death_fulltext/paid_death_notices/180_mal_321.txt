
        DALEY-Joseph Michael Jr. Born July 4, 1943, in New York City, died unexpectedly. He is survived by his father, Joseph Michael Daley Sr., and his wife, Marcia Daley. Formerly of Mountain Lakes, NJ. Joe attended Morris Catholic High School and Fordham University. For info, call Crosby N. Gray, Burlingame, CA., 650-342-6617.
