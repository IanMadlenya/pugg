
        DUFFY-Sherwood T., Sr. 73, retired architect. Died August 5, 1998 in Lenox Hill Hospital. Mr. Duffy is survived by his wife Kathryn Duffy; two children Jimmie Ann Duffy and Sherwood T. Duffy, Jr.; daughter-in-law Elizabeth Duffy and granddaughter Meredith Lee Duffy. He will be especially missed by his longtime friend Kenneth Hamlett of New York City.
