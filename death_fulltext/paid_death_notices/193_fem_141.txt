
        CLARK-Frances B. Passed away peacefully on February 18, 2003. Dearly beloved wife of Robert L. Clark, who survives her. Also survived by her sister-in-law and brotherin-law Donna and Eugene Famera. Predeceased by her brother Richard Wayne Bohle. Attended Brown University and graduated from Beloit College; a talented college textbook editor and legal assistant. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Salvation Army. A private memorial service for the immediate family will be held at a later date.
