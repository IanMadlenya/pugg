
        FEDER--Walter. The Department of Medicine at Maimonides Medical Center mourns the passing of Dr. Walter Feder. Dr. Feder served as Director of our Division of Cardiology from 1967 to 1976. He is recognized for his significant contributions in the field of Cardiology and to the Medical Center. Our heartfelt sympathies are extended to his family. Edgar Lichstein, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Maimonides Medical Center
        $(6$)FEDER--Walter, M.D., Loving husband of Cecile (nee Salwen), devoted father of Martin (Danielle), Roslyn Lipsky (Marvin), Carole Simon (Noach), caring brother of Doris Feder Getzler and Aaron Feder, proud grandfather. Interment in Israel.
