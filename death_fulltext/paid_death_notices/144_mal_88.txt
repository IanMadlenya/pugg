Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Wednesday and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial, Friday, January 8, 10 A.M., at Saint Jean Baptiste Church, 184 East 76 Street, New York City.
