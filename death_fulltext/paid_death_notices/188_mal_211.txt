
        DRESSEL-Henry Francis. 88, of Marlboro, NJ, died September 20, 2002. Predeceased by his wife of 62 years, Rose Marie Valentine Dressel, in 2000. Survived by a daughter, Mrs. Diana Dressel Fradella of Morganville, NJ; three grandchildren, Dr. Henry Francis Fradella, Mrs. Camille Rose Fradella Cordeiro and Mr. Anthony Michael Fradella; and two greatgrandchildren, Juliana Marie and Vincent Salvatore Cordeiro. Calling hours Sunday, 1-5 PM, at Freeman Manalapan-Marlboro Funeral Home, 344 Route 9 North, Manalapan. Funeral Liturgy offered Monday, 10 AM, at St. Gabriel's Church, Marlboro, NJ. Interment will follow at The Lutheran All Faiths Cemetery, Middle Village, NY.
