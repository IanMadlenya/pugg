
        BECKMANN-Allan G., 81, of Boca Raton, FL and formerly of Park Slope, Brooklyn, NY, passed away on Saturday, October 18, 1997. Mr. Beckmann was a customs house broker with Daniel F. Young, Inc., for many years. He was preceded in death by his wife, C. Florence Beckmann, and second wife, Mary Beckmann. He is survived by his son, Allan G. Beckmann, Jr.; daughter-in-law Kathleen; and beloved grandchildren, Allan III, Carolyn and Melinda. Friends will be received on Tuesday and Wednesday from 2-4 pm and 6-8 pm at Babione Funeral Home. Funeral services will be held on Thursday at 10:00 am at Babione Funeral Home. Memorial remembrances in his name may be made to The American Cancer Society of Boca Raton.
