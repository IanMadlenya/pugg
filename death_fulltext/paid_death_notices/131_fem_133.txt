
        WINNERMAN-Lilli Goldman. Of South Orange, NJ, and Palm Beach, FL. Lilli passed away on December 14, 1997. Lilli (nee Burg) was born in Vienna, Austria, on June 11, 1924. She escaped the Nazis by emigrating to Palestine in 1938. There she worked for the British forces as an ambulance driver stationed in Alexandria, Egypt. She was involved in the El Alamein campaign and received two battle stars. She was also an active member of the Irgun. After Israeli independence, she moved to New York where she became a fashion designer. She married Paul Goldman and raised three boys, Ross of Maplewood, NJ, Peter of Seattle, WA, and Kenny of Short Hills, NJ. A skier, bridge master, athlete, and great Mom, Lilli married Robert Winnerman in 1983. She had with Bob among the most fun and adventurous years of her life. Lilli's sense of adventure and thirst for life was an inspiration to all. Her spirit will live onwards in her six grandchildren, Hannah, Elliot, Sam, Addis, Claire and Gus. Services December 16, at 11:30 AM, at Bernheim, Apter, Goldsticker Funeral Home, 1600 Springfield Avenue, Maplewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers, please send donations in Lilli's name to the National Ovarian Cancer Coalition, 1451 W. Cypress Creek Road, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33309.
