
        RESNICK-Monya (Minnie), 103. Died in Weston, Florida Oct. 12. Beloved wife of the late Julius Resnick. Mother of Irene Resnick and the late Murray M. Resnick. Grandmother to Byron Wright, Leslie Granoff, Jo Ann Small and Ellen Franco and also seven great-grandchildren. She was a very special person to all who knew her. Funeral services at Riverside Gordon, Miami Beach, Fl. on Tues., Oct.14 at 1:30PM.
