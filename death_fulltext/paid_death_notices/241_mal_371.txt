
        ZULLER--Norman, beloved husband of 55 years to Harriet; devoted father and father-in-law to Michael, Randi, Eliane, Dana, and Adrian; proud grandfather to Devon, Jillian, Geoffrey, Chelsea, Daniel, Alexander and Chloe. He passed away in his 80th year on February 25th after bravely battling a long illness. For 60 years he was a deeply respected New York diamond merchant with an impeccable reputation. Always a modest man, he leaves behind him a long and loving legacy of devotion, strength, kindness and humor for which he will be remembered by all. Services Tuesday 12 noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
