
        WARMINGTON-Newton D., M.D.. The Administration, Faculty, Staff, & Students of the State University of New York Downstate Medical Center mourn the passing of Newton D. Warmington, M.D., Clinical Assistant Professor of Urology. Dr. Warmington was a 1989 graduate of the College of Medicine. We are profoundly saddened by his untimely passing & extend our deepest sympathies to his family, friends, and colleagues. John C. LaRosa, M.D., President Eugene B. Feigelson, M.D., Dean of the College of Medicine
