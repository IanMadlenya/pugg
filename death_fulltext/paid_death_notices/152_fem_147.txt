
        DOMINO-Leslie Mirin. On September 17, 1999, in Boca Raton, FL, after a valiant fight with cancer. Director of Communications and Marketing for the Jewish Federation of South Palm Beach County; formerly Vice President Marketing Director at Intergold. In her earlier career as an actress and singer she performed in the road show of ''How to Succeed in Business.'' Also an accomplished writer and patron of the arts. She was prized by her friends for her elegant style and her brilliant clear thought. Beloved daughter of Molly B. and the late Jules Weintraub. Also of the late Sylvia Mirin. Devoted friend of Barbara Gleason and Arlyne Monroe. Funeral services Tuesday, 10 AM, at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY. Interment Greenwood Cemetery. In memoriams to the American Cancer Society deeply appreciated.
