
        KEATING-Dr. Raymond Francis. Of Washington, D.C. formerly of Yonkers, New York died at his home on April 1, 2002 at the age of 69. His presence was made known by his ''Zest for Life, Music, Song and Conversation.'' While a resident in Yonkers, he attended St. Casimir's Church and was a member of its St. Cecelia Choir. He is survived by his loving sister Alice Siminsky, nieces Mrs. Joseph (Janet) Ribeiro and Karen Siminsky, nephew Joseph Siminsky and three grand-nephews: Christopher, Brian and David Ribeiro, of whom he was most proud. Family will receive visitors at Duchynski-Cherko Funeral Home, Inc. 111 Yonkers Avenue, Yonkers, New York on Friday, April 26th, from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. A Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated on Saturday, April 27th, at St. Casimir's Church at 10 A.M. Interment will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery.
