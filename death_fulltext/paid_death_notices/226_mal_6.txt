
        ISRAEL--Lawrence J. Peacefully, on October 30. Age 87 years. Beloved husband of the late Beulah. Adored father of Shelly Zirkes and Bret. Loving grandfather of Valerie and Linda. Caring greatgrandfather of Samantha and Alexa. NYU graduate. W.W. II Navy veteran, and partner in the architectural firm of Copeland, Novak & Israel in Manhattan. Till last week he taught Interior Design at the Fashion Institute of Technology. Longtime resident of Freeport. Trustee of the former Union Reform Temple. Services will be at ''Gutterman's,'' 175 North Long Beach Road, Tuesday, 1 PM.
