
        LLOYD-William E. On April 10, 1999, at age 74. Adored husband of Muriel. Beloved father of Jacqueline, Stephen, Nancy and Christopher. Service Wednesday, April 14, 1 PM. Interment to follow at Kensico Cemetery.
