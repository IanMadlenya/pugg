
        LACEY--Edith Gordon. Of Tamarac, FL, formerly of Great Neck, NY, passed away on December 9, 2005, at the age of 95. Most beloved wife for 70 years of the late Robert Irving Lacey; adored mother of Carol and Edward Sacks, Joan and Ronald Rothaus; most loved grandmother of Jonathan and Barrie Sacks, Bonnie and Robert Moses, Robin Sacks, Rod and Samantha Rothaus, and Merryl Rothaus; most special GG Ma of Evan, Andrew, Ariella, Elijah and Sadie Rose. Also survived by her devoted brother Victor Gordon and brother-inlaw Julie Pepper. She was a graduate of Hunter College. Her incredible energy, her zest for life, her kind and giving nature and her unselfish love were an inspiration to all who had the good fortune to know her. She will live and be loved forever in all our hearts. Services Sunday, December 11, at noon, Temple Beth-El, Old Mill Road, Great Neck. Interment Beth Moses Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
