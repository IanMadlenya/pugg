
        GEIGER-Miriam, 78, of Sun City, Arizona, died January 16, 1997, in Walter O. Boswell Memorial Hospital. Mrs. Geiger was born in New Haven, CT., and was a mental health advisor in New York. She moved to Arizona in 1993 from New York and was a member of Temple Beth Shalom. Mrs. Geiger is survived by a son, Kenneth of Winona, Minn; a brother, Robert Sukloff of Atlanta; and two grandchildren. Service will be Ferncliff Cemetery in New York, Thursday, January 23, at 1:00 P.M.
