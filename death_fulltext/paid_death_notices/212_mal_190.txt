
        McCREERY--James B. Of Dix Hills, NY, husband of Marianne, nee Norton, father of Pat Fitzsimons, Jamie Israel, wife of Bob Israel, James P. McCreery, husband of Peggy Franck, grandfather of Caitlin, John, Sheryl, Kevin, Kyra and Evelyn. Jim was a CPA in Massapequa, NY, a graduate of NYU and the treasurer of Sigma Phi Epsilon at NYU. Born in Bronxville, NY to James Harold McCreery, grandson of the Founder of the NY Dept Store, James McCreery and Co. Favorite charities include Alzheimer's, Parkinsons, Make - A - Wish and Bide-A-Wee. He died on September 16th. Funeral services were held privately.
