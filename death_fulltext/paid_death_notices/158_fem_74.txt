
        YOUNG-Jenni Munro. Of West Islip, NY, died on March 7 at Huntington Hills Nursing Home at the age of 81. She had previously suffered a severe stroke and speech loss. Born in Columbus, OH she attended Ohio State University. During WWII she held an administrative position at Grumman Aircraft. In the post-war decades she became a much sought-after designer of exquisite interiors for many upscale homes on Long Island, Chesapeake, and Florida. She also found time to make long cruises under sail, including Bermuda, with her husband of 45 years, John F., who survives her. She was a member of Shelter Island Yacht Club for 32 years. There are no funeral plans but a memorial will be announced later. In lieu of flowers, donations to animal humane societies would be appreciated.
