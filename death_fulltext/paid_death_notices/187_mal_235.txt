
        KOENEMAN-John. A true gentleman and born optimist, John took a genuine interest in people and had a rare gift for putting others at ease. In his three years working at the American Foundation for AIDS Research (amfAR), his personal charm, can-do attitude, and sometimes outrageous sense of humor won him many friends. John combined style and sophistication with enormous natural warmth. It was impossible not to like him, and he is very sorely missed. The staff of amfAR's Public Information Department
