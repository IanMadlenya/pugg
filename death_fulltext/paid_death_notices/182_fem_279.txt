
        DAVIS-Miriam (nee Pfeffer). sorn on July 6, 1911 in Brooklyn, New York. Died March 19, 2002 in Basking Ridge, N.J. Miriam was the wife of Philip Davis for 52 years and mother of Jed Davis and Helen Davis Chaitman. She was loving grandmother to Aglaia Davis, Alison Davis Curry, Jennifer Chaitman, Larissa Davis Bailey and Rhadamanthus Davis. She was beloved aunt of Jack and Steve Schwebel. Her family will hold a memorial service for her on what would have been her 91st birthday.
