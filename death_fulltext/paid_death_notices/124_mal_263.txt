
        RUBINROTH-Martin. The Officers, Trustees and members of the Forest Hills Jewish Center mourn the untimely death of Martin Rubinroth, former Synagogue Vice President, Trustee and former Ritual Committee Chairman. May his memory always be a source of blessing and may his family be granted comfort and strength. Rabbi Gerald C. Skolnik Cantor Erno Grosz Carl Seldin Koerner, President
