
        SCOTT-S. Spencer Jr. Samuel Spencer Scott Jr., resident of Lyme, CT, formerly of New Canaan, died on May 9, after a long battle with non-Hodgkin's lymphoma. He was 77. ''Bud'' Scott grew up in Scarsdale, NY. He graduated from Phillips Andover Academy, 1942, and Princeton University, 1946. During the Second World War, he served as a lieutenant in the US Army Air Corps. The son of the late Edna Minsinger and S. Spencer Scott of Scarsdale, NY, he is survived by his wife Deborah Flagg; his sister Carolyn Dejanikus of Jackson Hole, WY; his children Samuel Spencer Scott III of Portland, ME, Steven Scott of Wenham, MA, Suzette Hearn of Fort Washington, PA; and his eight grandchildren, Carter, Story and S. Spencer Scott IV, Cameron and Caroline Scott, and George, Louise and James Hearn. Services Thursday, May 16, at 11:30 AM, at the First Congregational Church, 2 Ferry Road, Old Lyme, CT. Contributions may be made to a fund in his memory at the Connecticut River Museum, Essex, CT 06426.
