
        KLONER-Sydelle E., 76. Of Lake Worth, FL, passed away May 17, 2001. Dearest beloved wife of Nat for 54 years, loving and cherished mother of Linda Kloner and Joseph Kloner, loved sister of Natalie (Harry) Koppel and Arline (Stanley) Bauman. Much loved cousin, aunt and great aunt. We will always remember her for her remarkable spirit, joy for life and love of family & friends. Services were held in Florida.
