
        MOSTOWITZ-Elsie. The entire multi-generational membership of Congregation of Children of Jacob mourn the loss of our President Emeritus and matriarch, Elsie Bilger-Mostowitz. As a woman of natural grace, humor, compassion and wisdom, she touched all who knew and loved her. We will forever cherish her memory and be thankful for sharing her life. Martha Goldstein, President Nathan Bilger, Gabbai
