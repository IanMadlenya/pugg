
        KOLESNICK-Ruth. Passed away on October 2, 1998. Loving wife of Leon Kolesnick and the late Samuel Bederson. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY, at 9:45 AM. Ruth's family wish to thank all of the people who helped and cared for her during her stay at the Sound Shore Medical Center for their caring, dedication and professionalism.
