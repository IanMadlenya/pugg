
        HEIMER-Ralph, Ph.D. Of Philadelphia, PA in Rome on January 10. Professor Emeritus of Biochemistry at Thomas Jefferson University. He was the loving husband of the late Caryl Biren and the late Phyllis Sampson. He is survived by two sons and daughters-in-law, Robert and Noel of New Haven, CT and Paul and Nicole of Alexandria, VA, and by three grandchildren, Rawley, Alison and Madeleine. We will miss his wise counsel and his concern for humanity. A memorial celebration will be held on February 10 in Philadelphia. Contributions in his memory may be made to New Hope Housing, 8407-E Richmond Highway, Alexandria, VA 22309 or The Southern Povery Law Center, 400 Washington Avenue, Montgomery, AL 36104.
