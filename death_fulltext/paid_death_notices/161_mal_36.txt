
        SACKS-Michael, M.D. Columbia's Mailman School of Public Health extends deepest sympathy to the family of Dr. Michael Sacks. In his lifetime of dedication to the field of international public health, he was instrumental in global campaigns against smallpox, for the provision of primary health care, sanitation and clean drinking water for all, and raised the quality of life for the underserved of the world. He served the poorest of the poor in the developing world with boundless enthusiasm and sound advice. Allan Rosenfield, M.D., Dean
