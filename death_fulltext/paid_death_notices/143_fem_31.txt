
        TASHIRO-Kikue, November 5th at Calvary Hospice. Survived by her husband, Ago Terayama. A native of Japan who made NYC her home, Kikue wrote many plays, including ''Sakura: The Bandit Princess'' and ''Kensuke'', which were seen in many NYC venues as well as in Toronto, Hawaii and Europe. A memorial celebration of her life and words will be held Monday, December 7th, 8PM, at Vineyard Theater, 108 E. 15th St.
