
        SULLIVAN--Janice Oliver. Of Delray Beach, FL, passed away on Sunday July 17, 2005, two months before her 99th birthday. A sculptor and translator of books into braille for the blind, she was formerly of Westport, CT. Her husbands, Lee Oliver and William Sullivan, prede ceased her as did her only son, John Lee Oliver. She is survived by grand daughter, Vicky Oliver, and her husband, Gordon Cohen, of NYC, as well as, daughter-in-law, Anne Oliver, of East Hampton, New York.
