
        FINLAY-Rev. Terence John, D.D. Died peacefully in his sleep in his 92nd year, April 8, 1998 at Maplecrest Village, Grimsby, Ontario. Beloved husband of the late Sara McBryan Finlay; adored father of Terry (Alice-Jean) and Bryan (Carol); much loved grandfather of Sara-Jane and Rebecca, Molly and Charles; will also be sorely missed by his sister Kathleen and his brother Patrick. He served his Lord faithfully in the Anglican Church in London, Ontario, then as Rector of Holy Trinity, Winnipeg; St. John the Evangelist, Ottawa, and St. Bartholomew's Episcopal Church, New York City. A Service of Thanksgiving and Celebration of his life will be held Monday, April 13, 1998 at 2PM, St. Andrew's Anglican Church, Grimsby, Ontario. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to St. Andrew's Anglican Church, 154 Main Street West, Grimsby, Ontario L3M-1S3. Christ is Risen. The Lord is risen indeed. Alleluia!
