
        MA-David. On November 24, at home in Chevy Chase, MD. He was a Partner in the firm of Ernst & Ernst and was instrumental in obtaining approval for his firm to be the first foreign accounting firm to open an office in China, which he headed until his retirement in 1981. Surviving are his wife Teresa, and a daughter Dr. Ann Ma. He was predeceased by a son, David Ma. He was my partner and friend, and will be missed by all who knew him. LeRoy J. Herbert
