
        WALTCHER--Azelle (Brown). March 27, 1925 -May 6, 2007. Beloved wife of Irving, loving mother of Jeffrey and Dan (Barri), grandmother of Genevieve, Thomas, Sarah and David, sister of Richard Brown, devoted friend to so many and Professor of Mathematics for over 40 years at Hofstra University. Azelle was an alumna of Barnard College and she received her Masters and Ph.D. from New York University. A great supporter of Barnard, she served on the President's Advisory Council until her passing. We will miss Azelle's sparkling blue eyes, open mind and keen intellect. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Nature Conservancy New York (4245 N. Fairfax Drive, Arlington, VA 22203, www.nature.org.)
