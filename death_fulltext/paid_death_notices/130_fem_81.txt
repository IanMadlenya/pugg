
        CURRY - REILLY - Mary. d. Atty. Mary Curry-Reilly, 80, formerly of Borghland Rd., Wilton, CT, widow of Atty. Hugh Reilly, died Sunday, November 9, 1997. She was a graduate of the University of Wyoming and received her law degree from Yale Law School. Atty. Curry was a longtime member in the firm of Arthur, Dry and Dole in New York City for over 30 years until her retirement. Funeral Services were held Wednesday in CT.
