
        CUSHMAN--Allerton, 99, of Paradise Valley, AZ, died on October 25 at Scottsdale Healthcare Hospital. The cause was cardiac failure, his son said. Mr. Cushman is survived by his wife Virginia Cushman of Paradise Valley; son Allerton Cushman, Jr. of Boston; stepdaughter Michele Trevor Christensen of Paradise Valley; four grandchildren: Allerton Cushman III of New York City, Philip Parker Cushman of New Canaan, CT, Yvette Cushman Baton of Mulino, OR, and Lily Cushman of New York City; step-granddaughter Melanie Simpson of Scottsdale, AZ, as well as four great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
