
        STEIN-Anne S., 86. Died August 26 at home. Loving mother of Judith and Jonathan; grandmother of Rachel & mother-in-law of Judith E. Dear, generous sister, cousin, aunt, great-aunt to a large, wonderful family. Inspired teacher, good friend, and delight to all who engaged her mind and spirit. She was a 1931 graduate of Hunter College and was a history teacher and Dean of Girls at Erasmus Hall H.S. until 1976. For the past 18 years, she taught a challenging and popular course, ''Significant Books'', at the Learning Center of the United Federation of Teachers. Services will be held at the Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, 30 W. 68 Street, Friday, 10:30AM.
