
        BUSCH-Robert. On November 9th, 2000 at age 86. Died peacefully at his home surrounded by his beloved wife, Norma (Ponoroff), and devoted family, Carole Rako and Bonnie and John Stephens. He practiced dentistry in Freeport for over 40 years. A kind and generous man, Bob touched all who knew him. His memory and gentle spirit will always be with us. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, Long Island.
