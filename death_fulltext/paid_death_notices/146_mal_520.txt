
        VALDESBEREA-Miguel R., Artist/Portraitist, died at the age of 62 in New York, March 25th of heart failure. He was born in Havana, Cuba, in 1936 and studied there at the San Mejandro Academy of Fine Arts, and in Spain at the San Fernando School of Fine Arts and Prado Museum in Madrid. In addition to his many private clients, he counted business leaders, government officials, deans of American Universities, and clergy among his clients. He is survived by his mother, Isolina Berea; his sisters, Gladys, Margarita, and Nancy; an aunt, Erlinda Solana-all of Miami, Florida. His father, Rogelio Valdes, is deceased. Service at The Church of the Ascension, Fifth Avenue and 10th Street at 10:30am, Wednesday, March 31, 1999. In lieu of flowers, contributions maay be sent to The Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Ave, New York, NY 10028; Attn: Mrs. Emily K. Rafferty, Senior Vice President for Development.
