
        KAPLAN--Judy, of New York City died December 6, 2005 at age 61. Born October 6, 1944 in Mexico to Isaac and Naomi (Shvarzblat) Kaplan. She worked in NYC in Human Resources. Survived by her mother Naomi and sisters Sara Abramson (husband Richard), Ruth Kaplan and Lilly Combias (husband John). Funeral on Thursday 11 AM at Westchester Jewish Center in Mamaroneck. Interment at Mt. Moriah Cemetery in New Jersey. For information Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809.
