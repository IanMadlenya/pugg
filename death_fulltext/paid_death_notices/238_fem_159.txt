
        GERTZ--Kathryn Rose, beloved wife of Benjamin Ira and adored and adoring mother of Maggie. Former United Nations Guide and prolific writer whose work appeared in leading magazines and newspapers, including The New York Times. She loved New York, delighted in Central Park, and relished music, art, theater, and literature. Her humor, strength, wisdom, and warmth made her a real treasure. Forever loved, she will be sorely missed. Please make donations in her honor to Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation at 212-689-2860 or Comedy Cures at www.Comedy Cures.org. Memorial services will be held on November 28, 11:00 am at Temple EmanuEl, enter at 1 East 65th St.
