
        POLINSKY - Mollie. Died peacefully at home in Berkeley, CA at age 91. A lifelong resident of Brooklyn until she was 85. Retired Guidance Counselor for the NYC Public Schools. Beloved wife of the late Samuel. Devoted mother of Carol Gross, Judy Elliott, Alan and Debbie Polinsky. Loving grandmother of Steven and Cordelia, Miriam, Tony, Erika, Ira and Barry; and great-grandmother to Samuel David. Services Wednesday 12noon at ''WeinsteinGarlick-Kirschenbaum Chapels'', 1153 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, NY. Shiva through Saturday evening.
