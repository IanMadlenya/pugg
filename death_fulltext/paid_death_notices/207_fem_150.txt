
        BARKAN--Roberta May, in Stanford, CA, April 18, 2004. Beloved wife of Paul Barkan of Palo Alto, CA. Loving mother of Jeremy Barkan (Lisa) of Jerusalem and David Barkan (Wendy) of Los Altos, CA. Devoted grandmother of Shachar, Haim, Rachel and Naomi Barkan. An inspiration to countless students. A recognized scholar, lecturer and author in Jewish Studies and Hebrew Literature, she served as Professor at SUNY, Purchase College. A recipient of an M.A. and Ph.D. from Columbia University and the SUNY Chancellor's Award for Excellence in Teaching. Funeral services were held in San Jose, CA and burial took place in Jerusalem, Israel.
