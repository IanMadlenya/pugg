
        LAWLESS-John M., of NYC and Phoenicia, NY, on October 2nd, 1999. Beloved husband of Elinor. Loving father of Elinor Lawless Ringer and John M. Lawless, Jr. He had a distinguished career as an attorney with the Appellate Division, 1st Department, of the Supreme Court of the State of New York until his retirement in 1976. A memorial celebration of his life will be announced at a later date.
