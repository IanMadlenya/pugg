
        MENDELL-Malvina C. 103 years old, died peacefully on December 26, 2000, in Palm Beach, FL. Wife of the late M. Lester Mendell, to whom she was married for 65 years. Mother of Edward (Renee), Oliver (Shelley), and Olga (Wilbur) Toll. Grandmother of ten, great grandmother of fifteen, great great grandmother of three. Sister of Frances Kaplan, and Mussie Gellis. Mrs. Mendell was a Director of The Jewish Guild for The Blind, and The Honoree at their 1998 Dinner. She will be sorely missed by her family and friends. In lieu of flowers, memorials to a charity of your choice in her memory would be appreciated. Services noon Friday, December 29th at Riverside, 21 Broad Street. Respects may be paid at the home of Mrs. Toll on Friday after 4 P.M.
