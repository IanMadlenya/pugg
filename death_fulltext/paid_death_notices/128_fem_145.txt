
        MATTONE-Emilia, 51, Locust, New Jersey, died on September 16, 1997, at Mt. Sinai Hospital, New York, New York. Born on June 28, 1946. Beloved wife of Vincent J. Mattone, Sr., loving mother of Mrs. Michele Corvisiero, Alan and Vincent Mattone, Jr. Devoted daughter of Anthony and Helen Mascolo, adoring sister of Mrs. Linda Rizzuto and Anthony Mascolo. As a loving partner and trusted friend of her adoring husband, Emilia was the depth and soul of their union. The warmth of her shining spirit will dwell within his heart for eternity. He will forever feel the touch of her hand in his. As a compassionate mother, Emilia taught her children how to love themselves and how to share that love. Her grace and goodness will be honored through their love for one another. Emilia graced our lives as a devoted, loving, and caring wife, mother, daughter, sister, and friend. Her warm smile, gentle heart, and quiet elegance will be missed by every life she touched. Our loss can only be comforted by knowing that God and all in heaven now share in what we miss. In pursuit of furthering her education, Emilia became a member of the International Honor Society-Phi Alpha Theta as well as a member of the Honor Society at Monmouth University, Lamda Sigma Tau. John E. Day Funeral Home, Red Bank, New Jersey, will be handling arrangements, Thursday, September 18, 79PM, Friday, September 19, 2-4 and 7-9PM. A funeral mass will be held Saturday, September 20, at 11:00 AM, Holy Cross Church, Rumson, New Jersey, where she was a communicant. Celebrant Archbishop Renato R. Martino, Performance Observer of the Holy See to the United Nations. Con Celebrant Fr. Joseph Hughes, Holy Cross RC Church, Rumson, NJ and Fr. James Leone, Archdiocese of Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to her favorite charity, Path to Peace Foundation, 25 East 39th Street, New York, New York 10016. EM Forever in Our Hearts
