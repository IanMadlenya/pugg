
        DELMAESTRO-Louis ''Gigi'' died July 13, 2002, of cancer, at the home of his daughter, Madeline Tugentman, in Maplewood, NJ. He was 66. Gigi became the quintessential Hollywood restaurateur after 27 years at the Los Angeles Palm Restaurant. Gigi is also survived by daughter, Elizabeth Salituri of Maplewood, NJ; sister, Angela Delmaestro of Queens, NY; and granddaughters, Michelle, Allison and Gianna. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Louis ''Gigi'' Delmaestro Memorial Fund, C/O the USC/ Norris Comprehensive Cancer Center, 1441 Eastlake Ave., Los Angeles, CA 90033; Attention: Debbi Cordano.
