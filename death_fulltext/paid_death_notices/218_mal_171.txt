
        LEWIS--Robert A. Age 90, of Great Neck, NY, died March 9, 2005, at Good Samaritan Hospital, West Palm Beach, FL. He was born in Brooklyn, NY, son of the late Oscar and Mabel Lewis. He was a successful attorney, decorated W.W. II veteran and active member of his community. He was the loving husband of the late Lisbeth Lewis and brother of the late Ruth Bermas. He is survived by his wife Lottie Lewis of West Palm Beach, FL; three children, Beth Mittleman of Olney, MD, Ellen Mangiardi of Croton-on-Hudson, NY, and Ken Lewis of Farmington, CT, and their loving spouses Larry, Gerard and Carla. He is also survived by his sister Edith Perman Allen of Rye, NY, and six grandchildren (Kevin, Katherine, Maria, Mackenzie, Charlie and Tucker) and devoted partner and friend, Peter Bermas. Private funeral in Florida and memorial service in New York at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the New York Association for New Americans (NYANA), The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research, The Strang Cancer Prevention Center or a charity of your choice.
