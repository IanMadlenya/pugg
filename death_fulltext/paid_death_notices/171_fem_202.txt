
        BIANCHI-Anne, 53. Founder of Toscana Saporita Cooking School & author of many books on Italian culture and cuisine, died April 22nd. A lifetime resident of NYC, she is remembered by her family and friends for her boundless energy and passion for life. Survived by her mother, Maria Bianchi of Massarosa, Italy, and her sister, Gloria Piccolella of New Jersey.
