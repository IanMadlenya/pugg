
        KIRWIN-Mary Knowles, of New Vernon, NJ, on February 24, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Peter H. Kirwin. Mother of Peter, Thomas, Mrs. Eileen Cameron and Mrs. Rosemary Kirwin-Alvord. Also survived by 12 grandchildren, a great-granddaughter and many nieces and nephews. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at Christ the King Church, New Vernon, New Jersey, on Thursday, February 27, 1997 at 10 A.M. Friends may call at the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, Chatham, NJ, on Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Contributions may be made to the SE Morris Chapter of the American Red Cross. She was an inveterate reader, historian, supporter of public libraries and historical societies; & indefatigable N.Y. Times crossword puzzler.
