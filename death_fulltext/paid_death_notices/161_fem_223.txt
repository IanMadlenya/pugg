
        PERMAN-Ann K. (Age 77). Thomson, also widow of the late Alvin M. Arkin, M.D.; devoted grandmother of three granddaughters. Memorial contributions may be made to the Vocal Arts Society, 1818 24th St NW, Washington, D.C. 20008 or Hospice Care of DC, 1331 H St. NW Washington, D.C. 20005. Services private.
