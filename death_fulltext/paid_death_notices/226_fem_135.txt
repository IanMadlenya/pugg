
        CREEL--Jane Haslip. Salisbury, CT. formerly of Sharon, Ct. died November 6. The widow of Dana S. Creel who was president of Rockefeller Brothers Fund. A graduate of Duke University; One of the first Systems Service employees at IBM Corporation; later Executive Assistant to the president of IBM World Trade. She was a member of the Cosmopolitan Club of New York City. She is survived by a sister Anne Elizabeth Haslip and three nephews & a niece. Services will be celebrated at a later date.
