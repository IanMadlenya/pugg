
        SPEIER-Claire. Born New York City. Age 70. Graduate of New York University. Modern dancer with the Charles Weidman Dance Company. Married 1954 to Leonard Speier, had two children (divorced 1979). Survived by sons Andrew and Matthew. Career as dance teacher at John F. Kennedy High School until her retirement. Avid painter, spent nearly 30 summers in San Miguel de Allende.
