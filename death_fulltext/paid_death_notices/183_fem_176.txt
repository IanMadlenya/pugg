
        KEMPF-Janice Lee. Of Brooklyn, NY passed away on April 16, 2002 at New York University Hospital. She was 61. Janice was born in Portsmouth, NH on July 20, 1940 to Bruce and Mary Williamson. Janice will be remembered by us all as a kind, generous, intelligent and loving woman with a gentleness that touched all who knew her. She was a lover of Wagner, Nintendo, Egyptology and Disney World, clearly a diverse woman. She is survived by Life Partner Barbara Hershey of Brooklyn, NY, her father Bruce Williamson, Sr. Charlottesville, VA, second mother Shirley Hershey of Brooklyn, NY, brother Bruce Williamson, Jr. Charlottesville, VA, sister, Susan Heyer, Laurel, MD, daughter, Wendy Duffelmeyer, Valhalla, NY, son, Eric Kempf; grandchildren Christopher, Kevin, and Emily Duffelmeyer and numerous brothers and sisters-in-law, nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers please make a donation to the charity of your choice in Jan's memory. Calling hours at the Gannon Funeral Home, 152 E. 28 St, NYC on Thursday 6-9pm. A graveside service will take place on Friday 11am at Greenwood Cemetery, 500 25th St, Brooklyn, NY.
