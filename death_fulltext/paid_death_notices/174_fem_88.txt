
        BEANEY-Bertha I. A lifelong resident of New York City, died in her sleep on Tuesday, July 10, 2001, of natural causes. She was 90. Born on November 17, 1910, in Washington, D.C., she was the daughter of William Beaney and Mary O'Dea Beaney. Miss Beaney served in Europe during World War II in the Women's Army Corps as a Staff Sergeant and was honorably discharged on April 25, 1946. After the war she worked for McKinnon & Company and Ray Temporary Staffing in various capacities. She was a lifelong New York Yankees fan and golf enthusiast. She was affectionately known to her family as ''Aunt Buddy'' and to her friends as ''Betty.'' Miss Beaney was the third of four sisters, Mrs. Jane Beaney Patterson, Mrs. Gertrude Coleman Butler, and Mrs. Agnes Beaney Woods. Calling hours will be from 7 PM to 9 PM on Thursday, July 12, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., NY. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on July 13 at 10 AM at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave. and 84 St. Burial services will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Valhalla, NY.
