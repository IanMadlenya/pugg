
        BLOCK-Arthur L. August 5, 2002. Retired Principal of P.S. 87 New York, member of the Board of Examiners of the NYC Board of Education, and longtime Assistant Professor (Adjunct) of English at Baruch College. Husband of Adrienne Fried Block, father of Elizabeth & Ruth (daughters of the late Frances Bildersee Block), stepfather of Linda and Barbara Fried, father-inlaw of Joseph Margolick and Joseph Bankman, and grandfather of Jonathan, Alexander, Samuel and Gabriel. He will be greatly missed. A Memorial Gathering will be held on August 23 from 5-7 PM at the Graduate Center/CUNY, 365 Fifth Avenue. In lieu of flowers send contributions to Catherine Block Memorial Fund, University of Rochester Gift Office, Rochester, New York 14627.
