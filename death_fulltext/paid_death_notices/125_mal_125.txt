
        WESCOTT-Christine Cummings. On June 6, 1997 at the age of 85 of Cresskill, N.J., formerly of Cedarhust, N.Y. Beloved wife of the late George, devoted mother of the late George, Jr. and the late Marilyn Wescott-Smith. Loving grandmother of Michael Smith of Pomona, CA., and Andrea Smith of Hacienda Heights, CA. Devoted mother-inlaw of Fritz and Nancy Smith of Hacienda Heights, CA. Cherished sister of Dorothy Ryan of Rockville Centre, N.Y., Mary Gunther of Manchester, VT. and the late Alexander, George, Gordon, Allan and Lester Cummings. Loving sister-in-law of the late Edward Wescott and his wife Anne of Bergenfield, N.J. and the late Ralph Wescott and his wife Maggie of Newport Beach, CA. Much loved aunt, great-aunt and greatgreat aunt. Loving friend of the late Hank Tilp, his wife Vivian, their children Janet Riccobono, Sam, Rick, Jim and Bobby Tilp. An extraordinary woman of great courage, faith, humor and generosity who met the many difficult challenges of her life with unfailing grace. Visiting Barrett Funeral Home, 148 Dean Drive, Tenafly, NJ, Tuesday, June 10, 2-4 & 7-9pm. Service Wednesday, June 11, 10am, Church of Atonement, Engle Street, Tenafly, N.J.
