
        RUBY-Nathan, M.D., 92. Of Lauderhill, FL. Formerly of The Woodlands in Tamarac passed away August 19, 2002. A much loved and respected Pediatrician practicing in Brooklyn, NY for over 40 years. He is survived by his loving wife Helen, and beloved daughters Joan (Arthur) Berkow, Alice (Oded) Meltzer, grandchildren Kenneth (Birgit) Berkow, Ari Meltzer, Nina Meltzer; great granddaughter Emily Berkow. Predeceased by grandson Lawrence Berkow.
