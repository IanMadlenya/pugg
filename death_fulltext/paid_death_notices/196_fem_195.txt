
        PETERSON -- Sally. The Board of Directors, Staff and Children of the Girls' Vacation Fund mourn the passing of Sally Atwood Peterson, Officer and Board member since 1975. Her dedication to the girls of New York City made it possible for hundreds to enjoy camp each summer. Her memory lives on in all of their lives. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made to the Girls' Vacation Fund, 370 Lexington Ave, NYC 10017. Eva Lewandowski, Executive Director Harold D.
