
        BACON-Karen L. (Lagemann)., of Hyannis Port, died Tuesday, December 21, 1999 at Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center. She was 75. She is survived by her four children, Robin Greenwood of North Salem, New York, Carter Smith Bacon, Jr. of Sherborn, MA, Wells W. Bacon of Somesville, ME and Eric Bacon of Needham, MA. Also survived by two brothers, Derek Lagemann of Port Washington, New York, and Peter Lagemann of Greenwich, CT; 13 grandchildren, 3 nieces and 1 nephew. A Memorial Service will be held on January 3, 2000 at 2 P.M. at St. Andrew's By the Sea in Hyannis Port. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to Child and Family Services of Cape Cod, 1019 Route 132, Hyannis, MA 02601, 508778-1839. For information J.S. Waterman & Son-Waring Funeral Home of Wellesley.
