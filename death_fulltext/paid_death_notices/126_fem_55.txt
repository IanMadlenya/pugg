
        AXTHELM-M. Bonnie. Died on July 3 at her home in East Norwalk, CT., with her family at her bedside. The cause was cancer. Ms. Axthelm was the Executive Director, Corporate Marketing and Public Affairs, 3M/Europe, stationed in Brussels. She was the beloved daughter of Marjorie Axthelm Scholly and Robert Scholly, sister of Nancy and Stephen, adored aunt to Megan, Annie, Emma, Charlotte and Matthew. Funeral Mass will be held today at 10:30 A.M., at the Church of the Assumption in Westport, CT.
