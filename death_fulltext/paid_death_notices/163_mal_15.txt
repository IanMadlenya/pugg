
        DOUGLAS-Gordon Watkins, M.D. Chairman of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at New York University School of Medicine, and Director of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Bellevue Hospital, 1956-1986, founding member of The Bellevue Obstetrical and Gynecological Society. A superb surgeon, teacher, researcher and true innovator and pioneer in the field of women's health care. A compassionate and caring physician who was fiercely loyal to his staff and to Bellevue Hospital. So many owe so much to him. His passing marks the end of an era. We express our condolences to his family. Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, New York University School of Medicine The Bellevue Obstetrical and Gynecological Society
