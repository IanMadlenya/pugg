
        STAHL--Edith. 82, died peacefully in her sleep at home in Bayside, Queens, on June 9. Beloved wife of 58 years of Frank, retired Chief Engineer with Ammann & Whitney, and loving mother of David, a symphony and opera conductor of Charleston, SC, and Munich, and Robert, a physician of Atlanta, GA. She was born in Essen, Germany and came to the US in 1938. She is survived also by five adoring grandchildren Julia, Sonya, Eric, Byron and Anna, daughters-in-law Karen and Carol, her brother Alfred Cosmann, and sister-in-law Charlotte Stahl. Interment was made at Cedar Park Beth-El Cemetary in Paramus, NJ.
