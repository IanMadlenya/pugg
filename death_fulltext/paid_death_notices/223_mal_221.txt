
        ARDIZZONE--Robert L. On August 17, 2005, of West Cornwall, CT and NYC. Longtime companion of Pamela A. Jones. He leaves numerous cousins to mourn his passing. Bob will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. A graduate of The College of The Holy Cross and New York University. He served in the Navy as a lieutenant during the Vietnam conflict and later pursued a career in financial management. A Memorial Mass celebrating his life will be offered on Monday, August 29th, 11 am, at the Chapel of Xavier High School, 30 W. 16th St, NYC.
