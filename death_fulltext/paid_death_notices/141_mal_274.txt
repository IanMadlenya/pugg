
        MILEA-Carl. The Board of Regents, Administration and staff of Long Island College Hospital (LICH) mourn the passing of Carl Milea, our benefactor and dear friend, and beloved husband of Regent Faye Milea. We are deeply grateful to Carl and his family for their generous support over the years, especially their contribution to the LICH Chapel. To Faye, the entire Milea family, and all of Carl's loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. John Wren, Chairman of the Board of Regents Peter A. Kelly, Interim President Daniel D. Ricciardi, M.D., President, Medical Staff
