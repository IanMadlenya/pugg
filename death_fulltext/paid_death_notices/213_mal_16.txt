
        KARGER--Arthur, 90, on Thursday, September 30. Beloved husband of the late Helen Bock. Loving father of Jeffrey and father-in-law of Devra. Brother-in-law of Jean Bock, Elizabeth and Norman Bock. Uncle of Patti Bock, John Bock and Joan Freeman. At the time of death, he was of counsel to Gallet, Dreyer and Berkey. Former Chairman of the New York State Bar Examiners and the National Conference of Bar Examiners. Author of The Powers of the New York Court of Appeals. Services Sunday, October 3, 12 Noon, Riverdale Temple. In lieu of flowers, donations to Riverdale Temple.
        KARGER -- The Honorable Arthur. Riverdale Temple marks with sadness the passing of the Hon. Arthur Karger, President of the congregation from 1969-70. Until his death, he was active on the temple's Board. He will be missed. The funeral will be held at the temple, 4545 Independence Ave, Riverdale, NY, Sunday, October 3, at noon. Condolences are extended to his son Jeff, daughter-in-law Devra and sister-in-law Jean Bok.
