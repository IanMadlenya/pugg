
        WEINER-Sherwood Melvin., 73, died on October 29, 1999 following a long illness in Delray Beach, FL. Mr. Weiner was born in Mount Vernon, NY on October 23, 1926. He was the son of the late Barnett T. Weiner and Anne C. (Birnbaum) Weiner. He is survived by a son Albert Weiner and two daughters Deborah Weiner and Bernadette Anne Weiner. A private memorial is planned. Donations in Mr. Weiner's name may be made to the Guggenheim Museum in New York City. For information Scobee - Combs - Bowden Funeral Home, Boynton Beach, Florida.
