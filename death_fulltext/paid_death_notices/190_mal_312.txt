
        NAGLER-Harold. Died on November 26th, 2002 at the age of 96. He was a high school biology teacher and science chairman. He loved teaching, science, nature and his family. His ashes will be buried next to those of his beloved wife Dorothy in the town of Lake Luzerne, N.Y. He is survived by his loving children Michael, Eric and Patricia, his brother Gene, five grandchildren and two great grandchildren.
