
        MAZEL-Alexander. Age 89 of Forest Hills, New York, died on July 23, 1999. Loving companion of Hanni Katz; cherished uncle of Harvey and Kim Mazel; generous, adoring great-uncle of Monica and Michael Mazel; beloved brotherin-law of Leonard and Edith Mazel; devoted husband of the late Sophie Mazel. Whoever came into his life was enriched by his kindness, generosity and altruism. Thank you Uncle Alex for a lifetime of memories to sustain us all.
