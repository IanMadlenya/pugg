
        ROGERS--Harry Peter. Beloved husband of Louise G. Rogers. Died Thursday evening in New York City from complications of a stroke. He was 90 years old. He served as a Lieutenant in the Navy during World War II and had retired from Con Edison in 1979 after serving as a Mechanical Engineer for forty years. He was the father of Gordon Rogers, like a father to Victoria Leacock and is survived by his wife and his brother Eugene Rogers and his family.
