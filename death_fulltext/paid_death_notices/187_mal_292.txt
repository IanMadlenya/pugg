
        TESSER-Abraham. At 86, on August 26, 2002. Beloved husband of Toby (Turkel). Devoted father of Gary Charles and Steven Barry, Ph.D. Dear brother of Florence Tesser and Ruth Leventhal. Cherished uncle of many loving nieces and nephews. Mr. Tesser graduated City College after earning his CPA. He served in the U.S. Army and practiced public accounting for over 50 years. He was a 32nd Degree Freemason. He was respected and beloved by all, a dear, strong, gentle man who will be greatly missed.
