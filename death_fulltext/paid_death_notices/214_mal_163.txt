
        KRAMER -- Henry T. Of Branchburg, NJ died November 15, 2004. He was a former president of North American Reinsurance and SwissRe Management Corporations, and a founding member and past chairman of the Reinsurance Association of America. Born April 8, 1917 in Chicago, IL, educated at Lawrenceville and Yale University and a Navy veteran. He is survived by his wife Janet, daughters Jean and Ann, and six grandchildren.
