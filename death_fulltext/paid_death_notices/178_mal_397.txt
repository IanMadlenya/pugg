
        McCABE-Eugene J., 83. Of Rumson, N.J., died Sunday at home. Born on February 14, 1918, Mr. McCabe lived in NYC before moving to Rumson 43 years ago. He was predeceased by his parents, Eugene J. & Katherine McCabe, and his sister Catherine, all of NYC; a son Michael, of Rumson, killed in the 9/11 WTC attack; and a granddaughter Shannon. Survivng are his beloved wife of 48 years, Eleanor; two daughters, Mary Ellen Kirschner (Robert) and Suzanne, both of NYC; two sons, Eugene J. (Maureen), Newport, R.I., and Nicolas (Rob Florczak) of NYC; his daughter-in-law Lynn McCabe of Rumson; and 7 grandchildren: Cassidy, Regan, Liam, Nicholas, Kevin, Jack, and Riley. The family will receive friends Friday from 3 to 8 at the Molly Pitcher Inn, Red Bank, NJ. Those wishing to pay respects may also call at John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Ave., Red Bank, NJ during those hours. A funeral Mass will be celebrated Saturday at 10AM at Holy Cross Church, Rumson. In lieu of flowers, donations to the VNA of Central Jersey Hospice Program; Rumson First Aid, or the Michael McCabe Children's Education Fund, P.O. Box 526, Rumson, NJ, 07760, would be greatly appreciated.
        McCABE-Eugene J. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York records with profound sorrow the death of Eugene J. McCabe, a Life Member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. John H. FitzSimons, President Henry F. White, Jr., Historian
