
        BAER-James B. The Board and staff of the American Associates, Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, mourn the passing of James Baer of Boca Raton, FL, successful businessman, philanthropist and Jewish community leader. James, an esteemed member of the American Associates Board of Chancellors, along with his wife, Margie, generously supported Ben-Gurion University over the years. We extend sincere and heart felt condolences to Margie, his beloved wife of 45 years, and to his children Richard and Nancy of Palm Beach Gardens and Ronald and Marna of Coral Springs. Jules I. Whitman, President Kenneth L. Tucker, Chairman Harold L. Oshry, Hon Chairman AABGU
