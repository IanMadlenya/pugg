
        SCHWABER-David. On July 31, 1997. Adored husband for 70 years of Betty. Beloved father of Jules and Paul. Devoted father-in-law, brother, brother-in-law, uncle, cousin and friend. Adoring grandfather of Carl, Jeffrey, Mitchell, Glen and Catherine. Delighted greatgrandfather of Joshua and Daniel. He was loving and kind, the soul of responsibility and wonderful fun. His grieving family is grateful for his 95 years of accomplished life. Graveside service Sunday, 1pm, Knollwood Park Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY.
