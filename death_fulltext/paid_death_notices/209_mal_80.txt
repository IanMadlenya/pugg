
        LAWRANCE--Francis C., of Southport, CT died June 3, 2004. Devoted husband of Anne Dunn Lawrance and beloved father of Sandra Lawrance Webel of Locust Valley, NY, Francis C. Lawrance, Jr. of Redding, CT. He is also survived by five grandchildren, five loving stepchildren, Cynthia Michalis, Peter Dunn, Deborah Murray, Elizabeth Ruffa, and Alison Witherbee and nine stepgrandchildren. A graduate of Groton School and Harvard University '39, he served in the US Navy with the Pacific fleet in WW II. Until his retirement, he was president of J.S. Frelinghuysen Insurance Co. of New York, NY. In addition, he served as past Chairman of the Board of The Emergency Shelter of New York City, and of the Fairfield Orchestra. Burial services will be private. A memorial service will be held on June 29 at 11am at Trinity Episcopal Church, Southport, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Emergency Shelter Endowment of Leake & Watts Services, 463 Hawthorne Avenue, Yonkers, New York 10705.
