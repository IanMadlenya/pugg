
        HUTTON-Thomas J. Died of cancer in Santa Barbara, CA on August 23, 2000 after a long lingering illness. After graduating from The Hill School in 1960, he attended Union College for two years. He enlisted in the U.S. Marines and served two years in the Reserves. He lived abroad for four years in Rome and London. He moved to California where he resided until his death. He was a talented photographer, pianist and composer. For those who knew him he taught us a lesson in bravery and integrity. Funeral services will be private.
