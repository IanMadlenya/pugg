
        PICK-Aron M. 52, of Montvale, on Thursday, December 24, 1998, while vacationing in Nassau, Bahamas. Beloved husband of Alayne (nee Buchwalter). Devoted father of Rebecca and Naomi. Mr. Pick was a founder and Chief Executive Officer of Nydic Open MRI of America. He received a Juris Doctor from Boston University School of Law and a Bachelor of Arts from New York University. Mr. Pick was a member of the New York Bar. Memorial Service will be held Monday, December 28, 1998, at Temple Emanuel, 4:30 PM, 87 Overlook Drive, Woodcliff Lake, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Heart Association, 800-AHAUSA1.
        PICK-Aron M. The employees of Nydic Open MRI of America express our heartfelt condolences to the family of Aron Pick. Aron's wisdom, leadership and vision were an inspiration. We will miss him very much.
