
        STEWART-Edward Sheldon, age 91, died January 21 after a long illness. Survived by wife of 43 years, Shirley, sister, Beatrice (Babbie), children Helen, Sheldon (Shelley), Anthony (Tony) and Francesca (Francie), 7 grandchildren (Shellie, Scott, Sean, Michael, Jenny, Peter and Jaryn), 2 great grandchildren (Krysta and Justin), their spouses, and close companions. His eldest son, Edward (Ned) died last October. He was a happy man who enjoyed every minute of his life. We will miss him.
