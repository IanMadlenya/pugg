
        BRODY-Frances. The Officers and Board of Directors and Staff of the Jewish Federation of Central New Jersey express sincere condolences to the Brody Family on the loss of their beloved mother, grandmother, and sister, Frances Brody. Frances Brody was a true Woman of Valor, and will be deeply missed by the Jewish community of Central New Jersey. Frances and her late husband, Herb, were life-long leaders of our community. A chapter of Jewish history in Central New Jersey has gone. Our heartfelt condolences go to her children, Suzanne Brody, Wendy Keil, Patti Lehrhoff and Andrew Brody, 14 grandchildren and sisters. May they be comforted among the Mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Zygmunt Wilf, President Alfred A. Gelfond, President Elect Joseph Bier, Campaign Chair Toby Goldberger, Campaign Chair Elect Stanley H. Stone, Executive Vice President BRODY-Frances. State of Israel Bonds mourns the passing of an ardent friend and supporter of the State of Israel. Frances was a member of Israel Bonds prestigious Presidents Club, an honor society of leaders. A generous philanthropist and humanitarian who endowed hospitals, Jewish Community Centers, Universities and scholarship funds in New York, New Jersey and Israel. She was a recipient of Israel Bonds Eleanor Roosevelt Humanities Award. Gideon Patt, President Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board Irwin Hochberg, National Campaign Chairman Kenneth Rosen, Esquire General Chairman Metropolitan, NJ Lee Schwartz, Executive Director Metropolitan, NJ
