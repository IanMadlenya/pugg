
        PALATUCCI-Donald Moore, M.D. Died November 8, 2002, at his home. Beloved husband of Blanid, devoted father of Susanna, Nicholas, and Marc, and dear brother of Bonnie, Michael, Marion and Christopher. A dedicated physician, he grew up in New York and after graduating from Columbia Medical School and the Neurological Institute, he moved to San Francisco where he was in the private practice of neurology for over 30 years. He was the founder of the Association of California Neurologists, a Board Member of the American Academy of Neurology, and a Clinical Professor of Neurology at UCSF. There will be a Funeral Mass on Tuesday, November 12, at 10 AM, at St. Ignatius Church in San Francisco. Halsted N. Gray-Carew & English, San Francisco, CA.
