
        McCORMACK - Clement Royal. Mutual of America and its Board of Directors, together with all of its officers and employees, mourn the passing of our former Executive Vice President and Treasurer, Clem McCormack. Clem was a beloved friend whose contributions during his 16 years of service helped to build the strength of our company. Upon his retirement, the Board noted that his eminent career exhibited a special combination of sound business acumen with a faithful commitment to the health and human services community. We will miss him greatly. Our sincere condolences to his son, Mark, and daughters Maureen, Carol and Emily. William J. Flynn Chairman of the Board Thomas J. Moran President and CEO Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
