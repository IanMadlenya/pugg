
        BOGART--Zelda, of Manhattan, retired, died at home on August 31st, 2003. Widow of Jacob J. Bogart, pharmacist. Mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother, Eastern Star. Survived by her children, Dr. Bruce Bogart, Dr. Marc Bogart, Joel Bogart, and Vita Bogart, and her grandchildren, Dr. Tara Anne Bogart and Russell Bogart, Esq. Services at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, Friday, September 5th, 11AM, followed by interment at New Montefiore Cemetery, LI, NY.
