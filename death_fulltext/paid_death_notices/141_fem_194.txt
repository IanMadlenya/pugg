
        MARESCO-Rachel. On October 20, 1998. Beloved daughter to Isaac Cohen and Anna Colchamiro Cohen. Loving sister to Esther, Molly, Beatrice and Sophia, and the late Sarah Jacob and Diana. Aunt and great aunt to many. Rachel touched many through her constant dedication and commitment to the helping professions as an occupational therapist to GIs after WW II, a teacher to handicapped children in school and homebound and as a guidance counsellor. Her pursuits of culture and art spanned from modern and international folk dancing, jewelry making and creative craft to visiting museums, movies and theater. She loved animals especially her cat NIKKI. Her legacy lives on through all her loved ones. Services at Sherman's Funeral Home, Bklyn, NY, Thurs October 22, 9:45 A.M.
