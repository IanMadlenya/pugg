
        NUSSBAUM--Aaron of Lakewood, NJ died on May 16, 2004 at Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital @ Rahway, Rahway, NJ. He was 73 years old. Mr. Nussbaum was a loving husband, father and grandfather. He was brave, courageous and had a most generous heart and spirit. He is survived by his wife Yolette (Feldman), two sons, one daughter, two daughters-inlaw and six grandchildren.
