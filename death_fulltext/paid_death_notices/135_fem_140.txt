
        JACOBS-Marianne K. On April 19, 1998 in her 89th year. Beloved wife of the late Louis Jacobs. Dear mother of John and mother-in-law of Susan Jacobs. Adored grandmother of Jeffrey and Lori. Services Wednesday, April 22, 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. The family will be receiving at the chapel Tuesday evening 7-9 P.M.
        JACOBS-Marianne. It is with profound sorrow that we record the passing of our esteemed member. Past Officer of Sisterhood, and devoted member of Temple Shaaray Tefila for more than a generation, she will be sorely missed. We express our heartfelt condolences to her son, John, and the entire family. Rabbi Harvey M. Tattelbaum Rabbi Melinda Panken Ellen R. Saxl, President
