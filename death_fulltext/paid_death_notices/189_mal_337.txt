
        GREEN-Bernard. Died on October 23, 2002, in Williamsville, NY, of prostate cancer at age 88. Originally from Queens, NY, he retired to Poughquag, NY, in 1975, and moved to Williamsville in 2000. He is survived by his wife of 62 years, Rita Wolfe and two sons, Dr. Andrew Green and Dr. Leslie Green, and their spouses and five grandchildren. He was a textile technologist.
