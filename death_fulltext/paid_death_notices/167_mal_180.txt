
        BLOCK-Irvin. On December 12, 2000 at the age of 83. He was deeply loved by his wife of 52 years, Doris, and his children, Jessica, Amy, Rachel and Mark. He was adored by his grandchildren, Charlotte, Nadine, Leah and Nolan. He will be missed by his many friends and extended family. Irv was an accomplished writer of children's books and a science and medical writer. He loved sailing, photography, fishing, literature, and above all, his family. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, December 16 at 10AM at Whitting Funeral Home, 300 Glen Cove Avenue, Glen Head, New York.
