
        BERGER-Ruth Fanshel. Died at 69 of cancer on February 1, 1997. Beloved wife of Wallace for 51 years. Daughter of the late Hyman and Clara Fanshel. Devoted mother of Andrea and Adam (Katharine). Loving grandmother of Beth, Louisa and Clara. Sister of David (Florence), Jack (Beatrice) and the late Sol (Florence). Retired Professor of Social Work at Lehman College, CUNY. A woman of integrity and compassion, she devoted her life to issues concerning social justice and peace.
