
        SCHOR--Resia. Born in Poland, December 5, 1910, died November 26, NYC. She studied painting at the Academy of Fine Arts, Warsaw, married artist Ilya Schor in Paris. Escaping occupied France they settled in NYC in 1941. She exhibited paintings in the 1950s as Resia Ain. Since Ilya Schor's death in 1961, she created unique Judaica and sculpture. Beautiful, talented, wise, courageous, filled with burning love for her family. She lost her beloved daughter Naomi Schor December 2, 2001, in loving memory, is survived by her adoring daughter Mira Schor. Services, ''The Riverside'', 76th and Amsterdam, Friday December 1, 11:30am. No flowers.
