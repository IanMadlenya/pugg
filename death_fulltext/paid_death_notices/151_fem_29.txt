
        KAUDERER-Fay G. Of Lynbrook, New York. On July 31, 1999. Beloved mother of Bruce Kauderer and Carol Meltzer. Adored grandmother of Laura and Sarah Kauderer and Alison and Danielle Meltzer. Devoted sister of Irwin Glantz and sister-in-law Gene Glantz. Services were held August 1, 1999 at Gutterman's in Rockville Centre. A dedicated psychiatric social worker for more than 20 years and a unique and special individual who will be deeply missed by her family and many friends.
