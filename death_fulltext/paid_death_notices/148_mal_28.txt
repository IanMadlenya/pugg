
        WOODWARD-William, III. On May 2, 1999. Husband of Lisa and father of Elizabeth. Servcies at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, 5th Ave at 53 St. Thursday 11am, Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the William Woodward, Jr. Nursery School, 436 E. 69 St, NYC 10021 or the Central Park Conservancy, 14 E. 60 St, NYC 10022.
        WOODWARD-William, III. The Board of Trustees, faculty and friends of the William Woodward, Jr., Nursery School, Inc. express our extreme sadness at the profound loss of our dear friend and loyal Trustee, William Woodward III. The generous support of Mr. Woodward and his family has enabled the School to fulfill its commitment to the children of the families affiliated with New York Presbyterian Hospital and Cornell University Medical College. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, daughter and family.
        WOODWARD-William. In memory of William Woodward, a gentleman and thoughtful neighbor who lived in our building for more than 20 years. We regret his passing. The Board of Directors 141 East 72nd Street, Inc.
