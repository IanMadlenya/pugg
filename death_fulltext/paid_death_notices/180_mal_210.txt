
        STEFANOU-Peter A. 72, of Bedminster, New Jersey, formerly of Brooklyn, New York, (having moved in 2000 to be closer to his four grandchildren) on Friday, January 11, 2002. He is survived by his wife Alice (nee Foudos), his daughters Lisa and her loving husband Dean Bennett and Lainie and her loving husband Brett Carter, four grandchildren Jordan, Lucas, Zachary and Jillian. Visitation at the Gray Funeral Home, 318 East Broad, Westfield, New Jersey, on Monday, January 14, 2002, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Liturgy at The Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, Gallows Hill Road, Westfield, on Tuesday at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, please send a donation on Peter's behalf to The Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church.
