
        SNIDERMAN-Eleanore. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Eleanore Sniderman, beloved mother of Abby Milstein, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Abby and her husband, Howard, are exemplary leaders in the UJA-Federation community who support many agencies within our social-service network. Abby is the president of the New York Legal Assistance Group (NYLAG), one of our beneficiary agencies. Howard is a member of our Board of Directors and is chairman of our Jewish Leadership Forum. Together, they express an unwavering devotion to Jewish and humanitarian causes through their extraordinary generosity and commitment to those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mrs. Sniderman's husband, Marvin; to Abby and Howard; and to the entire Sniderman & Milstein families. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
