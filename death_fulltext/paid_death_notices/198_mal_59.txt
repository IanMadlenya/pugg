
        WANG-C.C. July 3, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Yuan-Su Wang. Loving father of Shou Kung, Hsien Chen Wang Chang and Yien Koo King. Cherished grandfather of 11. He was a noted artist and collector of art who will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Thursday, July 10, 25 and 7-9 PM with service Friday, July 11, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the C.C. Wang Fund, Department of Chinese Paintings at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
