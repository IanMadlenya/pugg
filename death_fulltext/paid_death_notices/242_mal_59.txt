
        MELCHER--George. The Board of Directors and Officers of Group Health Inc. (GHI), express our deepest sympathy to the Melcher family upon the passing of George Melcher, noted Physician, author, and public servant. George served unselfishly as GHI's President from 1966 to 1981, and we mourn his loss. Frank J. Branchini, Chief Executive Officer, Group Health, Inc.
