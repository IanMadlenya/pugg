
        MESNIKOFF-Wendy. Of Hastings on Hudson, NY. Beloved wife of Alvin. Devoted mother and mother-in-law of Nathaniel (deceased), Rachel and Steven, Joel, and Ann and Howard. Cherished grandmother of Jeremy, Aaron, Jacob and Sheina. Wendy received her M.A. in Human Development from the University of Chicago and her Ph.D. in Environmental Psychology from the Graduate Center of C.U.N.Y. Loving, kind, stalwart and courageous, she was loyal to her family and friends and loyal to the environmental causes to which she was devoted, both in her community of Hastings on Hudson, NY and beyond. Funeral 12 noon, Thurs., Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave., (Saw Mill Exit 17), Dobbs Ferry, NY. Memorials in support of environmental conservation may be made to the Nathaniel A. Mesnikoff Foundation, 35 Scenic Drive, Hastings on Hudson, N.Y. 10706.
