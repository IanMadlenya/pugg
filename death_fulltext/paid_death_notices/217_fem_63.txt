
        AGUADO -- Josephine, 90, died on February 6, 2005. Josephine was born in Manati, Puerto Rico and moved to New York City in 1928. She is survived by her son Bill Aguado. Services Wednesday, February 9, 10 AM at Williams Funeral Home on Broadway at 232nd St, Bronx, NY. Burial follows at St. Raymonds Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Scholarship Fund Hostos Community College Foundation in memory of Josephine. Donations may be sent to Hostos Community College C/O Ms. Sandra Ruiz, Hostos Community College, 475 Grand Concourse, Bronx, New York 10451.
