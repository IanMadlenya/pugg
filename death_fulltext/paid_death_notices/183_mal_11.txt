
        BERKOWITZ-Abraham. Flushing, NY. Born June 15, 1911, died March 31, 2002. Adoring husband of the late Eva, devoted father of Morrell, father-in-law to Harriet and proud grandfather of Jake and Max. A sweet, good, gentle soul who loved life and his family. His kindness and generosity of spirit will continue to live on and be remembered always. Private service. Contributions may be made to Temple Gates of Prayer in Flushing.
