
        CONWAY-Thomas, on January 18th, 2000, age 74, of Sarasota, FL, formerly of NY, native of Virginia, County Cavan, Ireland. Past President of Cavan P&B Association and longtime member of Cavan Gaelic Football Club. Past Treasurer of N.Y. Minor Board, delegate to United Irish Counties, member of Ancient Order of Hibernians Division 4, retired member Local 94 Int'l Union of Operating Engineers. Beloved husband of Joan (nee Clifford). Devoted father of Thomas J. and his wife Jeanie. Dear brother of Owen of England, Charles of County Cavan, Ireland, Lily McCann of New Jersey, Agnes Wilson and Kathleen Mannion of England. Reposing at the Edward D. Lynch Funeral Home, 43-07 Queens Blvd., Sunnyside, New York. Funeral Mass at St. Sebastian R.C. Church, on Saturday, 9:45 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
