
        LIVINGSTON - Elisabeth Sloan Pratt. The Board of Directors and staff of the Federation of Protestant Welfare Agencies, Inc. mourn the loss of Elisabeth Sloan Pratt Livingston, a longtime friend and generous benefactor, as was her mother, Katherine Sloan Pratt. John H. Livingston, who predeceased his wife, was a cousin of one of the founders of Federation of Protestant Welfare Agencies.
