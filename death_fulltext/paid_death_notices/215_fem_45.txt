
        SALZ--Naomi. Passed away peacefully December 3 at age 101. Beloved wife of the late Wallace. Loving mother of Judy and Leonard Goldman. Adored grandmother of Susan, David and Robin. Cherished great-grandmother of Jared, Jesse, Jamie, Michael, Taylor, Marc, Corey, Jake and Luke. We will miss her kind heart, her bright smile and her gracious spirit. Her warmth and generosity lives on in our hearts.
