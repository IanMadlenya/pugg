
        SARNOFF-Robert William. Age 78, of NYC, died Feb. 22, at Lenox Hill Hospital after a long illness. Beloved husband of Anna Moffo, brother of Edward and Thomas, father of Rosita Sarnoff, Serena Benenson and Claudia Parrot; grandfather of Adam, Toby and Felicia Jeanne Benenson. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, Feb. 26, at 11:30 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave and 65th St. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to the American Cancer Society, 19 W. 56th St, NYC 10019.
        SARNOFF-Robert W. The National Women's Division of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University extends profound sympathy to the Sarnoff family on the loss of their beloved husband and father. Through his establishment of the Women's Division's Lizette H. Sarnoff Award for Voluntary Service, Robert continued the tradition of support for Einstein initiated by his parents, the late Lizette H. and David Sarnoff, who were members of the College's Society of Founders. To his wife, Anna Moffo Sarnoff, his children, Rosita Sarnoff, Serena Benenson and Claudia Parrot, and to the entire family, we express our heartfelt condolences. Nancy Oelbaum, President National Women's Division Albert Einstein College of Medicine Judy R. Rosenberg, Chairwoman Executive Committee
        SARNOFF-Robert W. Franklin & Marshall College mourns the loss of its Trustee Emeritus and former Board Chair. Through his leadership and vision, the College was greatly advanced. Franklin & Marshall shares in his family's sense of loss, but we are comforted by knowing that his legacy will endure at the College. William E. Seachrist, Chair Richard Kneedler, President
        SARNOFF-Robert William. The Board of Trustees and the staff of Cooper-Hewitt, National Design Museum, Smithsonian Institution are deeply saddened by the loss of our loyal board member of many years, Robert Sarnoff. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his wife Anna and his family.
