
        BUCHHOLZ -- Ester. The Trustees, faculty, and students of the Institute for Child, Adolescent & Family Studies mourn the inconsolable loss of our respected colleague, brilliant teacher, and dear friend, Ester Buchholz, Ph.D. Dr. Buchholz was a founding member of our institute, and her support and commitment to the highest ideals in education and clinical work was, and will continue to be, an inspiration to all of us. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband and entire family. Ava L. Siegler, Ph.D. Director
