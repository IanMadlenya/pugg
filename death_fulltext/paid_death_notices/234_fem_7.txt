
        LOBIANCO--Anna. True partner of Bolivar Chiq Avila. Mother of Ruby and Dario. Sister of Yummy and Nina. Daughter of Dora and Tony. Ferocious mother, ferocious friend, compelling and powerful teacher and leader. Bravest of the brave young women. Her bright star shines on us all. Family and friends will be remembering her on Thursday, July 6th at the New York Historical Society, 2 W. 77th St entrance at 6pm. Donations can be made directly to Anna's family: Bolivar Avila, 14 Lockwood Road, South Salem, NY 10590.
