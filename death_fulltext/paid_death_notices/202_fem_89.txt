
        KURTZ--Elaine. Suddenly, on Tuesday, November 11, 2003. Loved wife for 47 years of Jerome Kurtz, and mother of Madeleine Kurtz and Nettie Greenstein. Devoted grandmother of Noah and Eliza Miedel, and Emma & Nathan Greenstein. She was a productive and widely respected painter of abstract and atmospheric work. Her untimely death is a shock to all who knew her. She is deeply missed. Contributions in her memory may be sent to the Center for Arts Education, Attn: The Elaine Kurtz Fund for Art Education for Children, 225 West 34 Street, Suite 1112, NY, NY 10122.
