
        ZINS--Adele D., 79, of Del Ray Beach, FL and Edgartown, MA passed away peacefully on January 25, 2004. A graduate of Connecticut College (Class of 1946), Adele earned her M.A. in psychology at New York University. Married to Eugene I. Zins, M.D., mother of James, Judith, and Jacqueline, grandmother of four grandchildren whom she loved dearly. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Planned Parenthood.
