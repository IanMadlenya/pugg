
        BERNARD-Viola W., M.D. Louise Wise Services for Children and Families deeply mourns the loss of our distinguished Honorary Board Member, Dr. Viola Bernard. She was our esteemed colleague, teacher and friend. Dr. Bernard served on the Board for fifty years and was our Chief Psychiatric Consultant for nearly forty years. Her dedication and concern for the needs of children in crisis formed the foundation of Louise Wise Services' mission. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Glenna R. Michaels, President Carl Mazza, Acting Executive Director
        BERNARD-Viola W., M.D. The officers and members of the Association for Psychoanalytic Medicine mourn the loss of their esteemed and beloved member, teacher and mentor. Burton A. Lerner, M.D., President
