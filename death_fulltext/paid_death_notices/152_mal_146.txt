
        LIHME-Edward H. Of Stuart, FL died Saturday, September 11th at the age of 89, after a short illness. He was the retired director of Lihme Properties, a family real estate company, which he operated from his office in Palm Beach. Mr. Lihme attended Roxbury School in Cheshire, Ct and the University of Virginia. He is survived by two daughters: Eleanor L. Wetmore of Old Saybrook, CT., and Anita L. Keefe of Greenwich, CT; four grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren.
