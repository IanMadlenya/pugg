
        ROSENBLATT--Arthur I. On January 10, 2005. Noted architect, former Holocaust Museum director. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. Services Friday, 3:15 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        ROSENBLATT--Arthur. The Metropolitan Historic Structures Association mourns the passing of its President, Arthur Rosenblatt, FAIA. He served as President for seven years. He was a distinguished Architect devoted to the restoration and preservation of historic landmarks. He was the first Architect of the Holocaust Museum in Washington, D.C. and orchestrated the installation of the Temple of Dendur at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. His book, ''Building Type Basics for Museums,'' was published by John Wiley & Sons in 2001. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
