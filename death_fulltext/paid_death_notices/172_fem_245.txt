
        SMITH-Suzanne Y. Died peacefully on Sunday, May 27th after a courageous battle with cancer. Wife of Baldwin Smith, Jr., mother of Alison, Baldwin, Jonathan, and Lindsay. Grandmother to Anna and Avery Shoemaker & motherin-law to John Shoemaker and Allison Pell Smith. Her enthusiasm for life, her fierce love for family and friends will be missed by all. A devoted wife, loving mother, and adored grandmother, she had an energetic & independent spirit. Suzie was an avid athlete, a dedicated community volunteer and enjoyed her small business venture. Services will be held on Friday, June 1, at St. John's of Lattingtown Church in Locust Valley, L.I. at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, her family would like contributions sent to the Green Vale School, Valentine's Lane, Glen Head, N.Y. 11545, care of Suzie Smith Fund.
