
        PRINCE-Helen Elizabeth Peirce. 76, of New York City, died on Saturday, October 10, 1998, after a brief illness. She is survived by her daughter, Elizabeth Prince de Ramel; her son, Frederick Henry Prince, and five grandchildren, Diana Elizabeth Oehrli, Guillaume and Regis de Ramel, Cynthia Elizabeth and Frederick Octavius Prince. Funeral service at 11:30AM, Friday, October 16, at St. James Church, 865 Madison Avenue between 71st & 72 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Bryn Mawr Book Store, 502 East 79th Street, New York City or The Peabody Essex Museum in Salem, Mass.
