
        SIMIDIAN--Dikran Vahan 70, died September 1, 2006. He was born in New York, son of the late Vahan and Mannig (Bouchakjian) Simidian. He was a proud graduate of Kew Forest and Brown University. He was a beloved father and grandfather who was a kind, gentle, and a good hearted man. He is survived by his loving family, his daughters Darlene, Linda, Rachael, and Amy, his granddaughter Grace and his brother Ara. A memorial service will be held on Friday, September 8, 2006 at 1 PM at the Naples Memorial Funeral Home & Cemetery, 525 111th Ave N, Naples, Florida 34108. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Naples or the Armenian Church of SW FL.
