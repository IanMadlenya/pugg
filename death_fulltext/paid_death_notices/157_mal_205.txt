
        SIMMONS-Percy. Age 93, passed away peacefully in Indianapolis, Indiana on February 15, 2000. Born in London, England, he resided in Indianapolis for 60 years. He was the former French Consul in Indianapolis, a patron of the arts, founder of the Indianapolis Chevalier du Tastevin Society, a man of myriad accomplishments. He is survived by his wife of 70 years, Hinda, his children Nina and Don Frenkel, Rica and Harvey Spivack; four grandchildren, David Spivack, Ellen Schifman, Tamar Hauptman, and Ruth Frenkel. Also seven great grandchildren. His loss is greatly felt by family and friends.
