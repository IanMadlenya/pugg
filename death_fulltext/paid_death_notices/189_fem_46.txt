
        BUNTING-Natalie Augusta. Of Hudson View Gardens, NYC, on October 5, 2002, at Calvary Hospital at the age of 82. Survived by Mary Bunting of Arizona and several nieces an nephews. Mrs. Bunting worked for the National Council of Churches as well as the United Board for Christian Education. Mrs. Bunting was a graduate of Hunter College. She was a lifelong patron of the arts. Donations in her memory may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461, or the American Cancer Socity, or any hospice of your choice. Memorial services wil be held at a later date. Mrs. Bunting will be missed by her family and many of her dear friends.
