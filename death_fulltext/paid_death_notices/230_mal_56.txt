
        McKEGNEY -- F. Patrick, M.D. A leader in the field of psychosomatic medicine and consultation liaison psychiatry died on February 3, 2006. He was 73. Dr. McKegney was a beloved husband, parent, teacher and friend. He is survived by his wife of 19 years, Ellie and by five children from his first marriage; Catherine McKegney, Teresa McKegney, Sharon Fuller, Kevin McKegney and Timothy McKegney. He is also survived by three grandchildren, Abigail Fuller, Elizabeth McKegney and Kate McKegney. A memorial service will be held on April 9 at 1PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St. in NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Vermont Kidney Assoc., POB 244, Burlington, VT 05402.
