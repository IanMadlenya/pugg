
        NEUMANN-Edith. Microbiologist. 100. Passed away on June 29, 2002. She is survived by her cousins Hanna Beck of Zurich, Switzerland, Friedel David, of London, England, and many friends and distant relatives who will always remember her love, strength and inspiration. Interment is private. A memorial service will be held in the fall. Contributions in her memory may be made to Neighborhood Coalition for Shelter, Inc.
