
        YOUNG-Anne B. (nee Brassington). Died March 8, 2002, in Sugar Land, Texas, with her family present at age 68. She was born February 22, 1934, in New York City. Survivors include Anne's children Francis X. Young, Michelle Camilli, Elizabeth Young, Daniel Young and Jacqueline Granucci, six grandchildren, her sisters Mary Cronin, Gail Kernan, Kathy Hurley and Beth Wentzel and her brother William Brassington Jr. Anne's husband Frank J. died in 1998 and her daughter Suzanne died in 1980. Her parents William and Cecelia Brassington also predeceased her. Visitation will be Tuesday and Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Ballard-Durand Funeral Home. A funeral Mass will be held Thursday 10 AM at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church, Elmsford, NY. Interment will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Arenal Mission, c/o Father Joseph Currie, Fordham University, Bronx, NY.
