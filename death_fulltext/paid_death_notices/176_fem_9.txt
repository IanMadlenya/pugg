
        DYBING-Patricia B. Died on August 30 at the age of 76. She was the beloved wife of Peter Dybing who survives her and the daughter of the late Alexander Benecke and Lina Carr Benecke. A memorial service will be held at Hitchcock Presbyterian Church, Scarsdale, NY at 1PM September 4. Mrs. Dybing was a resident of Scarsdale all of her life, until her recent move to Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers the family asks that contributions be made to Hitchcock Presbyterian Church or to Fresh Air Fund, NY, NY. The family will receive visitors at the Cox & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 East Post Rd, Mamaroneck, NY Monday, September 3 between 6-9PM.
