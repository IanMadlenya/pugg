
        COLBERT-Joseph D., Rev. Of Glendale, NY, died on October 10, 2000 at age 67. A Pastor of Saint Pancras R.C. Church, he was ordained May 30, 1959. He is survived by dear sister, Maureen McMahon. Visitation Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9 P.M., Thursday 2-5 at George Werst Funeral Home, 7141 Cooper Avenue in Glendale, NY. Mass of Transferral at 7:30. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 10:30 at Saint Pancras R.C. Church. Interment to follow at Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, New York.
