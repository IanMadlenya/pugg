
        SMITH--Anne Marie of New York City, Oak Bluffs, MA and recently Oakland, CA., died in Oakland on March 17, 2006. She leaves her two children Dr. Mark Smith and Marcia Smith, four grandchildren, and scores of family and friends. Contributions in memory of Anne and Rufus Smith may be made to the NAACP Legal Defense Fund, 99 Hudson St. NY, NY 10013. Memorial at Christ Chapel of The Riverside Church, Monday April 17, at 10AM.
