
        HILL--Morton. Age 91. On February 7, 2004 in Key Biscayne, FL. after a productive, fun-loving and adventurous life including double crossing the Atlantic in his sailboat. Founder/President of Gardner Corporation, Atlantic City Knitting Company and Colebrook, Inc. Survived by his loving wife of 66 years, Marjorie, his children, Stephen, Daniel and Sally, their spouses Leyla, Donna and Bill, and grandchildren Jacob, Aaron, Noah and Ezra. A memorial celebration of his life Wednesday, 3PM at Pino's in The Towers, Key Biscayne.
