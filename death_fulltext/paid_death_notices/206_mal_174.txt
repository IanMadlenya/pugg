
        Van NORMAN--David L., 68, of Naples, FL, passed away Friday, March 12, 2004. Mr. Van Norman was a former vestry member of St. James Church in New York City. He is survived by his wife Heather and a son Mark (Margaret). Also surviving are three grandchildren Megan, Natalie and Olivia. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Those wishing to make an expression of sympathy are asked to consider the American Lung Association.
