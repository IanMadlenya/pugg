
        KAPLAN-Lillian Y., 94. Devoted wife of Abraham, mother of Rhoda and Hazel, grandmother of Michael, Marcy, Dana, Alexandra and Jonathan, and great grandmother to Sara and Andrew, passed away on December 2, 2001. She graduated from Barnard College with honors, received her Master's degree in Education at Teachers College and her Ph.D. in Guidance from Queens College. Lillian devoted her life to the education of minorities. After retirement, she and her husband participated in The United States Executive Assistance Programs to help under-developed nations create manufacturing capabilities. They assisted with the construction of production facilities in Turkey, Korea and Colombia. Graveside service Monday, December 10th at 1pm at Beth David Cemetery, 300 Elmont Road, Elmont, NY.
