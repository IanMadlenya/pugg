
        FAGER-John W. Of Garden City, New York, age 82, on December 2, 1997. Survived by his beloved wife of 56 years, Melva Clark, MD. Distinguished graduate of Colgate University and Harvard Law School. Retired partner of Hughes, Hubbard and Reed. Adored father of John, Diane, Melva and Russell and cherished grandfather of Will, Jessamay, Clark, Joe, Caitlin, Casi & Russell. Services 2PM, Saturday, Dec. 6, the Community Church of Garden City, 245 Stewart Avenue, Garden City. In lieu of flowers donations should be directed to Pat Caprio at Colgate University, Hamilton, New York 13346, in the name of Colgate University/John W. Fager Scholarship Fund.
