
        JACKSON-Alexander. The Fresh Air Fund mourns the loss of its beloved member of the Board of Directors Alexander Jackson. Sandy was a dedicated Board member for over twenty years. As our Treasurer, he worked tirelessly on behalf of our Fresh Air children. He was admired for his leadership, strength and good humor. We extend our deepest sympanthy to his family. Sandy wil be greatly missed. Susan Newhouse, Chairman C. Stephen Heard, Jr., President Jenny Morgenthau, Exec Director
