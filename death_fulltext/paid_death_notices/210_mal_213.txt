
        STOUTE--Dr. Argyle. A psychoanalyst in the U.S. and Paris, and steadfast civil rights activist and humanitarian, died on Sunday July 18, 2004, in Westchester in the company of his family at the age of ninety-seven. Dr. Stoute is survived by his two daughters, Dr. Beverly Stoute, a psychiatrist and psychoanalyst in Westchester, and Cheryl Simpson, a mortgage consultant in New Jersey, two grandsons and a brother, Clarence Stoute. The viewing between nine and eleven A.M. and funeral service at eleven A.M. will be held at The Abyssinian Baptist Church in Harlem on Friday July 23rd. For further information: Jenkins Funeral Chapel in New York City. Donations in lieu of flowers can be made to: Dr. Argyle Stoute Memorial Fund For Psychoanalytic Education and Race Relations at: The New York Psychoanalytic Institute, 247 East 82nd Street, New York, New York 10028.
