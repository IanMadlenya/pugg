
        BLINICK-Margery Alice (Linda). Died June 19, 1998 in Princeton, N.J. Born on September 19, 1909, Curwensville, PA. She lived most of her life in New York City and worked as a nurse at Beth Israel Medical Center. Devoted wife of the late George Blinick, M.D. Beloved mother of Robert and Joseph and the late Ann Colliver. Cherished grandmother of Nancy, Cindy, Lindy, Andy, Bill & George. Loving great grandmother of Alice, Richard, Matthew, Katie, Laura, Ryan, Annie and Carter. A memorial service will be held at the Princeton United Methodist Church, Sunday, July 5th, at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the American Diabetes Association.
