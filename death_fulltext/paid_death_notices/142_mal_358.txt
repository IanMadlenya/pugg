
        NEWHOUSE-Theodore. 95. Died on November 28, 1998, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Caroline. Loving father of the late Jane. Devoted grandfather of Julie and Amy, grandfather-in-law and great-grandfather of Joshua, Isaac, Jonathan, Sarah, Jill and Emily. Services Monday, November 30th, 11:30 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65th St. Interment to follow at Baron Hirsch Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to Alzheimer's Association of New York, 420 Lexington Ave., NYC 10170.
        NEWHOUSE-Theodore. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Theodore Newhouse, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        NEWHOUSE - Theodore. The Board of Directors and staff of Career Transition For Dancers at the Caroline and Theodore Newhouse Center for Dancers express profound sorrow at the loss of our longtime benefactor, Theodore Newhouse, and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Caroline and the Newhouse family.
