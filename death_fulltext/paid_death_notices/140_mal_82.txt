
        LOEWENHEIM-Harold A. Age 87. Died on September 6, 1998. Beloved husband of Susan Weingarten Gropper Loewenheim. Predeceased by his former wife Virginia Speyer Loewenheim and brothers Arnold Loewenheim and Frank Lowell. Other survivors include his daughters Barbara Jacobs and Patricia Franke, his sons Roger Loewenheim, Allan Gropper and Carl Gropper, grandchildren Mark Jacobs, Daniel Loewenheim, Laura Jacobs, Jeffrey Loewenheim, Andrew Gropper and Richard Gropper, great-grandson Benjamin Pousada, two nieces, two nephews, five grand nieces and six grand nephews. Funeral service on September 9, 1998 at 12:30pm at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Family visitation welcome on September 8 from 3-5PM at 145 East 92 Street or on September 9 from 3:30-6PM at 232 Highbrook Avenue, Pelham, New York. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Interface Neighbors Inc, New York City or a charity of your choice.
        LOEWENHEIM-Harold A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Harold A. Loewenheim. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        LOEWENHEIM - Harold. We extend our deepest sympathy to the family. Richard M. Feldman, President Edward J. Landau, Secretary
