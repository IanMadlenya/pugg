
        SILVER--Joseph W., M.D. The Board of Governors, staff, and membership of the greater New York Hospital Association (GNYHA) mourn the passing of an esteemed and beloved physician and friend, Joseph W. Silver, M.D., who served with distinction as chief of orthopedic surgery at New York Methodist Hospital for the past two decades. We send our deepest sympathies to Dr. Silver's family, including his wife Madeline, his children Lauren and Marc, and his brother Shelly. Herbert Pardes, M.D. President & CEO, NYPH & Healthcare System Chairman, GNYHA Kenneth E. Raske President, GNYHA
        SILVER--Joseph, M.D. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the death of Dr. Joseph Silver. Dr. Silver was an orthopedist with the Department of Surgery for over 35 years. He was a valued member of the medical staff and will be sorely missed by all his colleagues, patients and friends. We extend condolences to his family. Herbert Lehman, M.D., Pres. Ira Mayer, M.D., Pres. Elect Warren Wexelman, M.D., Sec Steven Farber, M.D., Treas.
        SILVER--Dr. Joseph. The Maimonides Medical Center family notes with sadness the passing of Dr. Joseph Silver. An orthopaedic surgeon, Dr. Silver was a longtime member of our medical staff. Our thoughts are with his family. He will be missed. Martin D. Payson, Chairman Pamela S. Brier, President
        SILVER--Joseph W. Congregation Beth Sholom of Lawrence, New York records the passing of our esteemed member, Dr. Joseph W. Silver. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
