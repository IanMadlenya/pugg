
        LEPORE-Michael J., M.D. gifted clinician, internist, and educator, died at home on September 2, 2000. He received his M.D. with Honor in 1934 from the University of Rochester, served as Lt. Col in the Pacific 1942-46, and was director of the Gastroenterology Service at St. Vincent's Hospital 196675 and Professor at N.Y.U. School of Medicine since 1971. Honors include M.J. Lepore Professorship in Gastroenterology at University of Rochester, St. Vincent dePaul Distinguished Physician Award, FACP, AOA, SIGMA Xi. He was predeceased by his wife Ardean Everett Lepore. He is survived by his son Frederick and wife Adlynn, grandchildren Addie and Meredith, sisters and brothers-in-law Lucille and Ignazio Russo, Annette and Edward Urban and Phyllis Russo. Reposing Volk-Lieber Funeral Home, 789 Teaneck Road, Teaneck, N J. Funeral Mass Thursday September 7, 2000 at 10: A.M. St. Cecelia R.C. Church, Englewood, N J. Entombment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York. Contributions can be made to: Lepore Memorial Fund, 36 7 Ave., Suite 402 NY, NY 10011 Attn: Mrs. Phyllis Bloise.
