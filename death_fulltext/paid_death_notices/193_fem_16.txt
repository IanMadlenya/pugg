
        LIU-Mary Man-Li. 94, on January 24, after a long illness. Widow of Chinese Minister to Germany and Ambassador to Italy, with strength and determination she led her five children from occupied China to war time capital, then to America for their education. She is survived by four of the children, Elizabeth Kung-Ji L. Bainbridge, Margaret K., David K., Robert K., seven grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
