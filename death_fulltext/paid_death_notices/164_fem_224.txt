
        MILLER-Esther M. A memorial service celebrating the life of Esther M. Miller will be held at Immanuel Lutheran Church, 88 St and Lexington Avenue, Saturday, September 30, 2000 at 3 PM. Ms. Miller, a long-time resident of the Parkside, was an underwriter for E.F. Timme and retired from Frank B. Hall. She died on July 27, 2000 and was buried in Craig, MO.
