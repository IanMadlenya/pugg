
        ATTIE-Joseph N. The Staff Society of Long Island Jewish Medical Center deeply mourns the passing of its devoted friend and fellow member, Dr. Joseph Attie. Dr. Steven Orshan, President Dr. William Doscher, Vice Pres Dr. Ira Kalfus, Secretary-Treas
        ATTIE-Joseph N., MD. The partners and staff of Herman Wolfson & Company are deeply saddened by the sudden death of Dr. Joseph N. Attie.
