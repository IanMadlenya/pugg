
        COSENTINO-Josephine Batchelder. 75, of New York City, entered into eternal rest on Saturday, January 20. Born in Lynn, MA, she was a 1946 graduate of Wellesley College in MA. She is survived by her husband John A. Cosentino of New York City, and her two sons John A. Jr. of Simsbury, CT, and Paul B. of Richmond VA. Also surviving is a brother, Harry C. Batchelder of New York City, and four grandchildren, Jessica, Joshua and Matthew of Simsbury, CT, and Trent of Richmond, VA. She was predeceased by a grandson, Willy. All services were private. For information, contact C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, Ridgewood.
