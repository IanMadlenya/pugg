
        DOLAN-Sally (Mrs. Harry Francis Roby Dolan, Jr.), 78 years old, formerly of Wilton, CT., died on Sunday, September 14, 1997, at her home in North Palm Beach, FL. Married for 51 years, she was predeceased by her husband. Mrs. Dolan is survived by four children: Ann Tauchert of Lexington, KY; Ellen Kirkpatrick of Exeter, NH; Heidi Baldwin of Lynchburg, VA; Peter Dolan of Princeton, NJ; twelve grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. A graveside service will be held in Boston later this week. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions in her memory to the Lost Tree Chapel, 11149 Turtle Beach Road, North Palm Beach, FL 33408, or to Brimmer and May School, 69 Middlesex Road, Chestnut Hill, MA 02167.
