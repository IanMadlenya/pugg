
        GRIFFASI-Christina. Minardi Salon, New York City, mourns the loss of Christina Griffasi who died after a courageous battle with cancer on June 24, 1997. During the past ten years Christina served as Salon Style Director and appeared with Carmine and Beth Minardi on numerous educational platforms throughout the United States. Christina will be remembered as a model of professionalism. Her demand for perfection, unsurpassed talent and unequaled dedication to the beauty profession serve as inspiration to all who knew her. Christina is survived by her husband Carl, her daughter Chloe, by her parents, a brother and sister. Services will be held at Gallagher's Funeral Home, Greenwich, Connecticut.
        GRIFFASI-Christina. Of Greenwich, CT. On June 24. Wife of Carl. Mother of Chloe. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held Friday at 10:15 A.M., in St. Mary Church, Greenwich. Calling hours will be held today from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM and 1 hour prior to the Mass on Friday at the Leo P. Gallagner & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch Street, Greenwich.
