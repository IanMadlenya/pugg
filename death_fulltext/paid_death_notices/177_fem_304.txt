
        JULAY-Terka. Died September 17, 2001 at the age of 97. Dr. Julay was a psychotherapist in New York City from 1946 to 1995, when she moved to Maine. She will be missed by her many friends, the Lax family, former patients and her family. Survivors include her sister Anne Marton, a nephew Dr. J. Osvath and two nieces, Esther Lacognata and Katalin Diossy.
