
        FEDER-Abe H. On April 24, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Ceil Feder. Loving brother of Theodore Feder and the late Sara Feder Keyfitz and the late Martha Tanner. Loving uncle of Beverly Widder, June Jaffee, Alan Tanner, Dr. Eugene Feder, Ann Pomerantz, Joan Kelman and Shulamet Halperin. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Saturday, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, on Sunday, April 27th, at 11:30 A.M.
        FEDER-Abe H. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Abe H. Feder, on April 24, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave, on Sunday, April 27, at 11:30AM. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
