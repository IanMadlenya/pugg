
        GROSSMAN--Morton. Of Los Angeles, CA, died on Friday, November 14, 2003. Dear father of Benjamin and Andrea; beloved brother of Edythe Gottlieb and Florence Bauman; loving uncle of Fern, Steven, Madeline, Carole and Patricia; devoted friend to so many. As beautiful inside as he was outside. His kindness, sense of humor and zest for life will be sorely missed. To know him was to love him.
