
        ALTMAN-David. The Trustees of the Hunter College Foundation and the entire College community mourn the loss of David Altman who, along with his wife Floria V. Lasky, was a tireless supporter of the College and its mission of research, education and service. The former chairman of the board of Altman Stoller Weiss Advertising, Inc., Mr. Altman was a dedicated, active and enthusiastic participant in countless College-related activities. We will miss Mr. Altman's commitment, loyalty and dedication to the College and we extend our condolences to his wife Floria, his daughters Emily and Dara, and his grandchildren. Evangelos J. Gizis Interim President Hunter College Allan H. Kurtzman Chair Hunter College Foundation
