
        RUDDER-Esther Koblenz, age 92, of Aventura, FL, passed away January 30, 1997. Cherished and adored mother of Gail Rudder Kent, of South Orange, NJ, and Neal Rudder of Hollywood, FL. Beloved sister of Ethel Lau, Rose Koblenz Goldberg and Herbert Koblenz. Beloved grandmother of Debra, Ives, David, Stacey, Erin, and Penelope and great grandmother of Jessica and Spencer. Former New York City school teacher for more than 40 years. Hospital volunteer in Florida during her retirement. A truly extraordinary woman of elegance and grace who will be missed forever. Donations may be made to the American Humane Society. Interment to take place at King Solomon in Clifton, New Jersey.
