
        HUNTER--William, on December 25, at age 65. Beloved son of Mildred, loving husband of Roberta, proud father of David and Julie, dedicated teacher and assistant principal at John Jay High School, Brooklyn. Passionate theatergoer, avid reader, and lover of life's gifts. He will be missed by the many people he has inspired with his enthusiasm and passion. Services at Weinstein Garlick Funeral Chapel, 4:00, Wednesday, December 27, Brooklyn.
