
        O'DONNELL-Frank X., Jr.. died in his ninetieth year on Monday, November 22, 1999 at home in Atlanta, GA. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Myers O'Donnell, father of Frank X. O'Donnell III of Atlanta and Carolyn O'Donnell Consor of New York City, he is also survived by seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Born in New York City on January 15, 1910 to the late Frank X. and Helen Gallagher O'Donnell, Mr. O'Donnell was raised in New York, graduated from Fordham University (1933) and Fordham Law School (1936) where he served on the Law Review. He had been a partner at the New York law firm of Burke and Burke, which he joined in the 1940s and retired from in 1976. When first married in 1936, he and Mrs. O'Donnell lived in New York City, moved to Larchmont, NY in 1942, retired to Albuquerque, NM in 1976, and finally moved to Atlanta, in 1995. He and his wife had been avid sailors at the Larchmont Yacht Club from 1949 until thier move to Albuquerque. Mr. O'Donnell was very active in the Community Chest and politics in Westchester and subsequently in Alubquerque, where he served as president of the Albuquerque chapter of the Service Core of Retired Executives (SCORE). A private funeral service was held on Friday, November 26th at the Church of St. Thomas More in Decatur, GA. In lieu of flowers, donations be made in his memory to Fordham University, Development Office, 30 W. 60th St, New York, NY 10023.
