
        SAWCHAK--Dr. Judith A. Devoted wife of Brian Henry. Loving mother of Joanna. Cherished daughter of Elsie and the late John Sawchak. Dear sister of the late Jerry. Physician and Professor of Neurology at SUNY Health and Science Center and Kings County Hospital, both of Brooklyn, NY. Despite her severe and chronic medical conditions she served her patients, students, colleagues and friends with great courage and fortitude. Reposing Leahy - McDonald Funeral Home, Atlantic Ave. at 111 St., Richmond Hill. Funeral mass Thursday 9:45 AM, St. Savior Church. Visiting Tuesday 7-9 PM, Wednesday 2-4, 7-9 PM.
        SAWCHAK -- Judith, M.D. (Mrs. Brian Henry).
