
        SCHREIBER--Toby. The family of Richard and Helene Rubin of Purchase, New York extend their deepest sympathies to Rita Schreiber and her family on the passing of their dear husband, father, and grandfather Toby Schreiber, in San Francisco, on January 14, 2005. Toby was our very close and beloved friend for nearly fifty years. Toby's generous spirit, his friendship and concern for others will be greatly missed by all of us who knew and loved him.
