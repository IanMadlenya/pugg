
        LASDON--Gene S. Beloved wife of the late Stanley S. Lasdon. An active philanthropist, she will always be remembered for her humor, kindness, elegance and generosity of spirit. Survived by her loving sons, Richard and Jeffrey, mother-in-law of Jeannette, survived by her sister Peggy Siporin, three grandchildren, two greatgrandchildren and many nieces and nephews who adored her. Gigi leaves behind a void no one can fill. Funeral services Thursday 9:30am at Frank E. Campbell (The Funeral Chapel), Madison Ave at 81st. Entombment, Temple Israel Cemetery.
