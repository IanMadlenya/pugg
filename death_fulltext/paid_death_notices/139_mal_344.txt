
        STUART - Douglas Kleinpeter. Died Friday, August 28, 1998, in NYC, at the age of 48. A Louisiana native and longtime resident of New Orleans, ''Klein'' lived in NYC since 1988. He studied in Italy and was a graduate of Loyola University of New Orleans and Parsons School of Design in NYC. Klein was a nationally recognized interior designer who had residential and commerical clients along the eastern seaboard. Klein is survived by his parents Walter Bynum Stuart III and Rita Kleinpeter Stuart of Lafayette, LA, and four brothers Walter Bynum IV, of Houston, TX, Robert Kenneth of Riyadh, Saudia Arabia, Ronald Sidney of Pensicola, FL, and Scott Matthew of Greenwich, CT. Other survivors include 11 niececs and nephews. Services will be held in Baton Rouge, LA, on Tuesday, September 1. Visitation will begin at 12:30 PM, followed by a funeral mass at 2:30 PM, at St. Joseph's Cathredral, 412 North St., Baton Rouge. A memorial service will be held in NYC at a later date. Rememberances may be made to The Aaron Diamond AIDS Research Center, 455 First Ave., NYC 10016.
