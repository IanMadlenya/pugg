
        TIGHE--Robert F., of New York, on May 18, 2007. Devoted father of Jeffrey K. and his wife Anna. Dear brother of Ethel Rickey. Bob was survived by and loved by friends everywhere. Bob worked on Wall Street for 35 years, he was former partner of Bear Stearns, also worked for Sandler O'Neill for many years. Bob left Wall Street to become President of High Voltage. Bob was a member of several golf courses on Long Island among them was Shinnecock Hills for 40 years. Reposing at the Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Causeway). Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial Thursday, 11:00 am at St. Patrick's Roman Catholic Church, Bay Shore, L.I. Visiting Wednesday 2 -4:30 and 7-9:30pm.
