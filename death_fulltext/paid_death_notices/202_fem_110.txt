
        HAZEN--Irene A. (nee Blitstein). At 74, peacefully, on November 13, 2003 at her home. Beloved wife of Joseph for 55 years. Cherished mother of Stephanie and Andrew. Adored grandmother of Shane and loving sister of Gladys. She was a wonderful woman whose kindness and wisdom is an inspiration to us all. Those who knew her loved her, and will hold her always in their hearts and thoughts. Services on Sunday, November 16th, at 2:00pm at Temple Beth El of Northern Westchester, 229 Bedford Road, Chappaqua, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made to: James F. Holland Research Fund, Mt. Sinai Medical Center, Box 1129, 1 Gustave Levy Place, NY, NY 10029 or to a favorite charity.
