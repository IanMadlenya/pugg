
        BARON-Jay B. On February 12, 1997. Passed away peacefully at age 70. Survived by beloved sister, Ellen Ritt, dear companion Patricia Wild and her family, and by many friends and former business colleagues. Also survived by his friend, Owasco. Beloved son of the late Julia and Lawrence Baron. Service on Friday, at 12 noon, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Interment in Salem Fields Cemetery.
