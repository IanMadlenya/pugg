
        McBRIDE--Robert Gene, born December 30, 1931 in Clarkson, MS, died peacefully in his sleep, February 8, of natural causes. He was 75. Bob served in the US Air Force and worked as a journalist, filmmaker, and TV producer. Bob was nominated for an Oscar in 1970 for ''The Gifts''. Bob had an indomitable spirit and limitless kindness. He will be greatly missed.
