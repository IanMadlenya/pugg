
        EPSTEIN-Ethel S., 83. Died at her home in Manhattan on May 4. Wife of the late Benjamin Epstein. Mother of Ellen Howe and David and Kate Epstein. Aunt of Thomas J. Schwarz. Grandmother of Benjamin, Caroline and Louis Howe and Lillian, Grace and Raphael Epstein. Service Friday, 12:30PM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Contributions in her name may be sent to the Scholarship Funds of Deerfield Academy and the Groton School.
