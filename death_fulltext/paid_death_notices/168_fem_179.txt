
        KESSLER-Deena. Died on January 15, 2001 after a two-year battle with ovarian cancer. Deena was born May 19, 1942 to Pearl and Abraham Kessler. Deena was preceded in death by her mother on January 5, 2001 and her father 28 years ago. Her sister Bonnie died in 1977. Surviving is her husband Robert LoPinto her sister Carol, a niece, four nephews, two great nieces and two great nephews. Service will be held January 18th at Schwartz Bros Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd. 1-3 pm. 718-263-7600
