
        BEDELL-Sylvia. Wife of the late Herbert R. Bedell died Thursday, July 6, 2000. Mother of Eileen Kraus and Mitchell Bedell; Sister of Shirley Brown and the late Martin Rubin; Sister-in-law to Joseph Brown. Mother-in-law to Michelle Bedell and Elliott Kraus. Grandmother to Lisa Kraus-Goldenberg, Nicole Kraus, Arie Bedell and Harris Bedell. Sylvia spent many wonderful years with her husband travelling all over the world. she loved to read and learn and continued her education throughout the late years of her life. She will be missed by her family.
