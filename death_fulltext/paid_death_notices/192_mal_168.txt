
        KONOWE-Milton M. Attorney with Local 1199 NBF & Local 115. Photographer, sculptor, died January 10, 2003. Survived by loving wife Doris, four children; Maureen, Bonnie, Valerie and Steven, nine grandchildren, one great-grandchild, brother Joseph and sister Shirley.
        KONOWE-Milton. 1199SEIU New York's Healthcare Union, mourns the passing of Milton Konowe, attorney and Secretary-Treasurer of SEIU District 1115, and later an official of the 1199SEIU Greater New York Benefit Fund. Milton's warmth, good humor, compassion and loyalty were the hallmarks of this wonderful labor leader for nearly fifty years. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Doris, his sister Shirley, his brother Joseph, and his children, Bonnie, Valerie, Maureen and Steven. Dennis Rivera, President George Gresham, Secretary-Treasurer Jay Sackman, Exec. V.P.
