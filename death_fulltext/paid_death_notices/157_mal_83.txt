
        BEER-Abraham. The Philip and Sarah Belz School of Jewish Music of the Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, Yeshiva University, and its affiliate organization, Cantorial Council of America, express their condolences to Cantor Bernard Beer, Director of the Belz School of Jewish Music and Executive Vice President of Cantorial Council of America, and to his family upon the loss of his beloved father, Abraham Beer. Shiva being observed at 1429 47th Street, Brooklyn, New York.
