
        TALMADGE - Arthur. Of Palm Beach, FL, on May 25, 2006 at the age of 92. He was the former Executive Vice President and Co-Founder of Mercury Records, former President of United Artists Records and former President and Founder of Musicor Records. He was born in Chicago, IL, in 1913, and served his country as an Army Sergeant during World War II. Mr. Talmadge was predeceased by his wife, the late Dorothy (nee Burg) who died in 1992. He was the loving father of Richard Talmadge, and Joyce Talmadge, and her husband Eric Lane. He was the adoring grandfather of Adam, Joshua, Taryn, Courtney and Travis. He was the dear brother of Grace Dorf and Norman Talmadge. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, May 30, 2006, 12:30 P.M., at ''Westchester Reform Temple,'' 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY. For additional information, please contact Hellman-Garlick Memorial Chapel, Briarcliff Manor, NY 866.762.5501.
