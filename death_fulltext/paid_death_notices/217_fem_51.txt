
        FANTA--Helen Kulka, 97, on January 25, 2005. Caring and devoted wife of the late Eric Fanta, her survivors include her niece, Margaret Frija, step-daughter, Marlene (Fanta) Shyer, and step-grandchildren, Kirby, Christopher and Alison. She died peacefully after a long illness and will be remembered at a memorial service at the Fox Funeral Home, 98-07 Ascan Ave., Forest Hills, on Thursday, February 10th at 1PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
