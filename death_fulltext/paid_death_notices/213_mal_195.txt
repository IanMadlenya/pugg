
        SAUNDERS--Bernard. 91, of Lake Worth, FL. Formerly of White Plains, NY. Died peacefully in his sleep on October 16, at midnight. Survived by his loving and devoted wife Reita, daughters Lynn Hyman, Barbara Addy and Ellen Smith, son Richard Chadakoff and brother William Saunders. Caring grandfather of Robert, Alex, Hilary, Nanci, Adam, Evan. Great-grandfather of Emma, Harrison and Owen. His gentle nature and kindness will live in our hearts forever. Service will be held Tuesday, October 19, 10 am, at Riverside Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mount Vernon, NY. Interment will follow at Mount Pleasant Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY.
