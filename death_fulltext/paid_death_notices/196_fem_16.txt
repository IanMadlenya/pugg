
        COOLIDGE-Mary W. Of Cambridge, MA, died April 27, 2003, age 91. Daughter of Ralph W. and Mary B. Welch. Wife of the late John P. Coolidge, former Director of Harvard University's Fogg Art Museum. She is survived by a daughter, Mary C. Cost, two grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Memorial service Christ Church, Cambridge, 11 AM, Monday, May 12. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be sent to the Harvard Art Museums, for the John Coolidge Objects Laboratory; or the the Arnold Arboretum.
