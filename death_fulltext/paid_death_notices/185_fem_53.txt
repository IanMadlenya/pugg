
        ROTKER-Elaine (nee Bienstock). A resident of New Rochelle, NY for 50 years, passed away on June 7, 2002; she was born on November 20, 1927 in Brooklyn, NY. Beloved wife of the late Martin Rotker. Together they cofounded Big Top Stores. Elaine was devoted to her family and friends. She was a homemaker and known throughout her community as ''Mrs. Big Top''. Dear mother of Ronnie (Abraham) Deutsch, Allen, Steve (Joy) and Glenn (Nancy). Beloved grandmother of Michelle (Michael) Grossberg, Benjamin (Melissa), Jonathan, Adam, Rachel, Jennifer, Lindsay, Alicia and Harry. Cherished great-grandmother of Jordan, Matthew, Jacob and Justin. Services are at Beth-El Synagogue, Northfield Road and North Avenue, New Rochelle, New York, Sunday at 10:30 AM. Interment, at Sharon Gardens Cemetery.
