
        WILLIAMS-William L., of Hilton Head Island, S.C., a long time resident of Bronxville, New York, and a retired senior partner of the international law firm of White & Case, died on Saturday, April 12th at his home. He was 83 and had retired in 1980. He is survived by his wife, the former Margaret Morrill; by five children: Centes C. Williams of Bronxville, N.Y., William L. Williams, Jr. of Franklin, TN, David M. Williams of New York, NY, Margaret W. Loftus of Scarsdale, NY and Abbe W. Winn of Atlanta, GA; by his brother, Charles R. Williams of West Point, GA; and by six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, April 17th at 2:00 PM at Christ Church, Bronxville, New York. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to St. Albans School, Mount St. Alban, Washington, D.C. 20016.
