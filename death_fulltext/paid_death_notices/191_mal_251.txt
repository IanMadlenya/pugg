
        GROSSI-Olindo. Died at his Manhasset home December 20, age 93. He was Dean, Pratt Institute School of Architecture until 1969, then Dean of Architecture at N.Y. Institute of Technology until 1983. He leaves his wife Martha, daughter Susan Forsyth, sons John and Thomas, and four grandchildren. Donations to American Academy in Rome or Columbia University. Memorial Service January 11, 2pm, Unitarian Universalist Congregation, Shelter Rock, Manhasset.
