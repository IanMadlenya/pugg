
        ALLEN -- George. (AKA George Axiltree). Passed away September 21, 2004. One of New York's premier singing teachers. Worked at HB Studio and his own private practice. Member of Actors' Equity and the Dramatists Guild. Popular teacher/ writer and lover of museums, Broadway shows, nature and life in general. George was the son of the late Alexander and Mary Allen (Hanic). Brother of Charles Allen and Mildred Johnson. Memorial service Saturday, October 2nd, 2004 at Greenwich Village Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., N.Y.C., 2-6pm.
