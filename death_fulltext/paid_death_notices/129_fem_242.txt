
        WEDGE-Sonia R. Longtime member of the New York Botanical Garden library staff, in her 96th year. Widow of the late New York Sun writer, Will Wedge. Loving mother of Eleanor and Nicholas Wedge; grandmother of Catherine Miranker, Elizabeth Wedge, and William T. Wedge; great grandmother of Emily and Mollie Miranker. Graveside service Monday, November 3, at 2PM, Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Contributions to the NY Botanical Garden Library in her memory welcome.
