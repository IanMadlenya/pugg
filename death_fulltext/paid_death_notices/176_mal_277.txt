
        BECKER-Robert. Yeshiva University Museum is deeply saddened by the loss of a cherished friend and advisor. Bob's aesthetic sensibility and skilled designs are reflected in YUM's exhibitions, galleries and offices. YUM presented Bob with the Omanut award in recognition of his two decades of dedicated service. We will miss Bob's enthusiasm, creativity and thoughtfulness. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Ruth, and his entire family. Erica Jesselson, Chairman Sylvia Herskowitz, Director
