
        EASTMAN--Monique de T., of New York City and Easthampton, NY, passed peacefully on May 17 following an heroic battle with a rare blood cancer. Beloved wife of Richard H. Sprayregen and Lee V. Eastman. Mother of Peter (Lucy), Paul (Emmeline), and Philip (Laurie) Sprayregen; stepmother of John Eastman (Jodie), Laura Malcolm (Donald), Louise Weed (Charles), and Linda McCartney (Paul). Grandmother of 17 and greatgrandmother of five. Family and friends will sorely miss Monique. She was a woman of dignity, elegance and selflessness. No formal services are planned. A memorial will be announced later. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Cancer Research and Treatment.
