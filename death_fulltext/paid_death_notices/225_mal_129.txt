
        SCHWARZ--Mannes. Beloved husband of Malka. Adored father of Ghita Tirzah & Chuck. Loving grandfather of Navah and Mia. Devoted brother of Herman and Ita. Born in Germany and raised in Poland, he survived the Holocaust and immigrated to the United States in 1956. He was a past Vice President of the World Federation of the Bergen-Belsen Association, member of President Carter's Commission on the Holocaust and member of the Board of Advisors of the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Council. He is a retired principal of David G. Steven Inc. and an active member of Park Ave Synagogue. His greatest pride and joy was his family, who adored him. Services will be held at 9 AM, Wednesday October 12 at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91 St.
