
        COX--W. Bingham. On May 21, 2004, at age 93. Beloved husband of the late Elise Conley Cox. Loving father of W. Bingham Cox Jr., Louis D. Cox, Peter B. Cox, Elise E. Cox and Jan Cox Weber. Devoted grandfather of ten and great - grandfather of 12. Friends may call at The Abbey, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Monday, May 24th, 2-5 and 7-9 p.m. Funeral Mass at St. Jean Baptiste, Lexington Avenue at 76th Street, Tuesday, May 22nd, at 10 a.m. Interment to follow at St. Mary's Cemetery, Ridgefield, CT. Contributions in his memory may be made to Xavier High School, 30 West 16th St, NY, NY, 10011.
