
        JAIVEN-Saul J., M.D. 85 of Stamford on Thursday June 29, 2000, at Stamford Hospital. Dr. Javien was a member of the State Medical Association and the Stamford Medical Association. He served in the U.S. Army Medical Corps, during WWII in the European Theatre. Survivors include two sisters Beatrice Heine of Cherry Hill, NJ and Gladys Young of Armonk, NY, a brother Leon W. Jaiven of Stamford, several nieces and nephews and great-nieces and nephews. A funeral service will be held on Sunday, July 2, 2000 at 11 AM, at The Nicholas F. Cognetta Funeral Home, Stamford, CT. In lieu of flowers donations be made to the charity of choice.
