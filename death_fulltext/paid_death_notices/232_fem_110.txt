
        SKYDELL--A. Harry. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of A. Harry Skydell, beloved uncle of Gabrielle Riback Propp, a treasured friend whose deep commitment to our Women's Campaign enriches Jewish life in New York and around the globe. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Gabrielle and to her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
