
        GAIGE-Truman. Died April 17, 2002 at The Actors' Fund Homes in Englewood, NJ at the age of 95. An actor of consummate skill and diversity, he began his career at the age of 16, made his Broadway debut in ''Blossom Time'' in 1931, and made his last Broadway appearance in ''Hadrian VII'' in 1969. He will be missed by his many friends. Memorials to The Actors' Fund of America, 729 Seventh Ave., New York, New York 10019.
