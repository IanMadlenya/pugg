
        SOLOMON-May W. Of Environ, FL, passed away on February 7, 1999. She is survived by her husband Irving, her loved children, Theodore, Warren, Erica, her niece Gayle, and daughter-in-law Anne Marie. By profession a master teacher K-12; an honored member of the International Society of Poets, published by the Library of Poetry. Services Wednesday at Old Montefiore Cemetery, St. Albans, New York.
