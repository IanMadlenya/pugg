
        NGUYEN-Ngoc San. Age 88, on December 25, 1998. Former President of the Saigon Bar Vietnam. Vice president of the International Association of Lawyers. Honorary Attorney at Law, Paris Bar, France. President of the Vietnamese Laywers Association Overseas. Interned at Fairview Cemetery, Westfield, NJ, the 29th of December, 1998. His wife, Ngoc Hai and sons, Kinh Luan, Kinh Quoc, Kinh Vy, Kinh. They are shocked and saddened by his death.
