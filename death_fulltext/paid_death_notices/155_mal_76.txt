
        CIVIC-Norman Age 77 died suddenly of a heart attack on November 30, 1999 in Boca Raton, FL. Norman was a graduate of the Wharton School. He fought in World War II in the China-Burma area with the Flying Tigers. He was founder and President of Ruth Norman Designs with his wife, Ruth, a fashion designer. Norman is survived by Ruth, his wife of 43 years; his daughters, Dr. Diane Civic of Seattle, WA, and Melanie Civic, Attorney in Washington, DC and also by a sister, Mimi Kerpen of NYC and Southampton. Memorial Services will be held at Temple Beth-El, Great Neck, NY on Thursday, December 9, 1999, at 12 PM. For directions call (516) 487-0900.
