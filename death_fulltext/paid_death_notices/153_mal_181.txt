
        STAAB-William du Pont Of Essex Fells, N.J., age 93, on October 8, 1999. He was architectural partner of Krahmer, Staab and Immen of Newark and Glen Ridge, N.J. Graduate of the University of Pennsylvania, School of Architecture in 1934. Former President of the Essex Fells School Board of Education. Pre-deceased by wife Jane Hart Staab. Survived by sons William du Pont, Jr. of NYC, Christopher Ely of Honolulu, Hawaii and Jonathan Ray of New Rochelle, N.Y., daughter Jane Newton Staab of Boston and five grandchildren. Memorial Service at 11AM, October 16th at St. Peter's Episcopal Church, 271 Roseland Ave., Essex Fells, N.J. Contributions to St. Peter's Episcopal Church or to the West Essex First Aide Squad, P O Box 662, West Caldwell, N.J. 07007.
