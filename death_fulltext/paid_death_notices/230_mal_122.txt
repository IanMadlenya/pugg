
        TSANG--Chih. Age 98, died on March 6, 2006 in New York. Born in Imperial China, his life successfully bridged the many changes that China underwent in the 20th century and demonstrated the opportunities available in post WWII America. The father of three, grandfather of seven, and great-grandfather of five, he also helped educate many nephews and nieces in the U.S., contributing to both his native and adopted lands.
