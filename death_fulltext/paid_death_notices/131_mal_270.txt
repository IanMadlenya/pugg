
        COBURN-Charles E., of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of New York City, died peacefully on December 7, 1997. Son of the late Charles Coburn and Ruth Earnshaw Coburn, brother of the late Robert L. Coburn and the late Constance C. Busch. Uncle to Peter, Connie, Lane, Connie and Stuart. A private service will be held at a later date. He will be deeply missed.
