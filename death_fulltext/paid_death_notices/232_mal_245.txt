
        STEINER--Alfred, M.D. A Clinical Professor of Medicine at The College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University and noted cardiologist for 50 years died May 22, 2006. He was 95. He was predeceased by his wife Margaret Allen Steiner. He is survived by his son Joseph Steiner, and his daughters Julie Stone, and Betsy Cabot, and five grandchildren. Memorial contributions in his name may be made to Columbia University, care of Alfred Steiner Award for Medical Student Research.
