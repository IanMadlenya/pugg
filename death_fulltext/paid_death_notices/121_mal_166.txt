
        SQUIRE-Judge Sidney. On Feb. 12, 1997. Beloved husband of Helen; devoted father of Walter and Jason; cherished father-in-law of Sara Abramson, M.D.; loving grandfather of Harrison, Russell, Zachary and Andrew. Funeral service 12 noon, Thurs, Feb 13, 1997 Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Congregation Or Zarua, NYC or to a charity of your choice.
        SQUIRE-Judge Sidney. The Directors and Staff of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York record with great sorrow the passing of a long-time, dedicated member of our Board of Directors, The Honorable Sidney Squire. May the Squire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Philip Schatten, President Stephen A. Kramer, Chairman of the Board David Mann, Executive Vice President
        SQUIRE-Sidney. Prominent attorney and distinguished jurist, superb author of judicial opinions, Trustee to the Brooklyn Bar Association and numerous Jewish philanthropic causes. He established standards of integrity and ability that we follow to this day. He was a friend to my family and an inspiration to me. Roger Bennet Adler
        SQUIRE-Judge Sidney. Congregation Or Zarua mourns the death of our member Judge Sidney Squire. May He who consoles the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem bring comfort to his family. Dr. Harlan J. Wechsler, Rabbi Dr. Samuel C. Klagsbrun
