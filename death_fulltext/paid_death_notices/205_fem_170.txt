
        LOKIEC--Esther. 87, on February 20, 2004. All who knew her bear witness to her limitless compassion, strength and love. Devoted wife of the late Mitchell, mother of the late Alice. She is survived by her son Stephen, Alice's husband Sandy, her granddaughter Nell, and niece Ellen. In a lifetime dedicated to the cause of social justice, she was an active member of the Workmen's Circle and the Jewish Labor Bund. Services will be held at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, on February 24 at 12:15 pm.
