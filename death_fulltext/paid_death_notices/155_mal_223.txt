
        MARKOWITZ-Max. The staff, faculty and Trustees for the Postgraduate Center for Mental Health were deeply saddened to learn of the loss of Max Markowitz, M.D. Dr. Markowitz was affiliated with the Center from its inception in the 1940s through his retirement in the 1970s. He served brilliantly as Medical Director of the entire facilty and as Senior Supervisor and Training Analyst in both the adult and group psychoanalytic training programs. His compassion, integrity and intellectual prowess will be missed. Dr. Jacob Barak Acting President & CEO
