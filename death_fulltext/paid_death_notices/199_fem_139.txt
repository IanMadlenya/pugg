
        McGINNIS--Carolyn, 60, of NYC and Allenhurst, NJ, died Saturday. She was manager of Administrative Services for Howard Hughes Medical Institute sites at Rockefeller U., Memorial Sloan-Kettering and NYU. She graduated from Rosemont, worked for Memorial Sloan-Kettering as administrator of the National Lung Program, Office of Support Services and Office of Industrial Affairs, and was V.P. at Simmons-Boardman until she was recruited by HHMI in 1990. Sisters Patricia McGinnis and Kathleen Stein and brother Arthur McGinnis survive. Parents Arthur and Roselind McGinnis and sister Roselind Mullen predeceased her. Buckley Funeral Home, Asbury Park, NJ.
