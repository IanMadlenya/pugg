
        COFFEE-Margaret Jennings, 77. Of Red Bank, New Jersey, formerly of Seneca Falls, N.Y., on August 10, 1998. Born in N.Y.C. Beloved and devoted wife of Joseph D. Coffee, Jr. for 56 yrs. Loving and loved mother of James Jennings, Mary Joyce Dies, Barbara Grace Wolf, Matthew Brian, Margaret Erin Giovannini, Ann Ellen Beach and the late John Allan. Grandmother of 20; great grandmother of one. Always supportive and involved in her husband's career who was formerly President of Eisenhower College & a longtime officer of Columbia University. For over forty years she shared with her husband in the direction of corporate/union scholarship programs. She was an alumna of the College of Notre Dame of Baltimore. Visitation Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at the John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Avenue, Red Bank, N.J. Funeral Mass Friday 10 A.M. at St. James R.C. Church, Red Bank, N.J.
