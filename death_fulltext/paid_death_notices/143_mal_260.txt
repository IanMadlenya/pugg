
        COHEN-Yehudi Aryeh. On December 17, 1998. Died peacefully at home after a long illness. He was Professor Emeritus of Anthropology at Rutgers University. He is survived by his daughter, Lisa Peet, grandson, Gideon Peet, and sister, Hadassah Gordis. He was the son of the late Meyer and Nehama Cohen and brother of the late Rabbi Gerson D. Cohen.
