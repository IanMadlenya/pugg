
        GOSS--Chester Curtis, 83, died May 8 at his home in Tinton Falls, NJ. He retired as President and CEO of CIT Corp. and CIT Leasing Corp., NYC. He was a member of the New York Bar Association. He was predeceased by three children, Christine, David and Deirdre Goss. Surviving are his wife, Adrienne (Lucey); five children and their spouses, Dr. Graydon and Eileen Goss, Robert and Nancy Goss, Virginia and Steven Pollack, Madonna and Alan Robinson and Adrienne and Gregory McCabe; 13 grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Interment will be private. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated on Friday at 10:00 AM at St. Dorothea's Roman Catholic Church, Eatontown, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to Fr. Flanagan's Girls and Boys Town, 13940 Gutowski Road, Boys Town, NE. 68010-7535.
