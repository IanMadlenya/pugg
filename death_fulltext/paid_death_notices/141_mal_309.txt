
        FELS-Bernard. Born December 25, 1912, died October 26, 1998, joined his wife of 53 years. Survived by his brother David, sisterin-law Helen, sister Hilda, brotherin-law Lou, son Charles Livingstone Fels, daughter Susan Casella, her husband John, grandchildren Lauren, Michael, John, Stephanie, Elizabeth and Robert. A horseman, former jockey and racehorse trainer. Services today 1 PM at Schwartz Bros., 11403 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. Donations to National Museum of Racing, 191 Union Avenue, Saratoga Springs, NY 12866-3566 or The Heart Fund.
