
        JACOBSON-Robert N.. On April 9, 1999, age 73, at his home in Harrison, NY. He was born to Daniel and Selma Jacobson on April 16, 1925, in the Bronx, NY. He served in the US Navy during WW II. Cornell University graduate with a BA in 1949, and MBA in 1950. On October 19, 1969, he married Siri Hauge, in NYC. Mr. Jacobson was Vice-President, Director of Customer Relations for International Flavors and Fragrances, NYC. He is survived by his wife Siri. Loving father of Patti, David, Per Robert, Kari and Lisa. Devoted grandfather of Travis and Megan. Visiting on Monday, 2-4 and 6-9 PM, at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY. Funeral service at the Rye Presbyterian Church, on Tuesday, 10 AM. Interment to follow at Greenwood Union Cemetery, Rye, NY. Memorial contributions would be appreciated to HHT Research in honor of Robert N. Jacobson, Yale University School of Medicine, 100 Church Street South, PO Box 7611, New Haven, CT 06516, attn: B. Sargent.
