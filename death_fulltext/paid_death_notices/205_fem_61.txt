
        GARFIELD -- Evelyn (nee Gerstein). Pioneer movie critic died February 2, 2004, age 102. Your elegance and love of beauty will always inspire us. Gracious lady to her whole family, beloved wife of the late Gustave B. Garfield. Mother of Diana Valenti and the late Oliver Garfield. Devoted grandmother of Robert Valenti and the late Matthew Valenti, Maurice and Joshua Garfield; great grandmother of Jonah and Benjamin Valenti. Memorial service is planned in the Spring.
