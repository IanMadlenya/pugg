
        CHEMLA--Fernand Joseph (Pino). Died peacefully on Thursday, June 8, 2006, at the age of 92. He was greatly loved by his wife Edmee, his children Franck, Alexandre, Muriel and Daniele, his children-in-law Claude, Lori and Claude as well as his grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the Cimetiere du Montparnasse on June 13 at 3 PM. Please no flowers.
