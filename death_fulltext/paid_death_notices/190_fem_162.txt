
        BILLINGS-Josephine Swift. The Board of Trustees and Staff of The Brooklyn Hospital Center are saddened by the death of Mrs. George M. Billings. Mrs. Billings joined The Brooklyn Hospital Center as a Member of the Board of Trustees in 1957, serving as President from 1970 to 1982, and as Chairman of the Board from 1982 to 1993, at which time she became Chairman Emeritus until her death on November 14, 2002. A Memorial Service is planned for Monday, December 2, 4:00 P.M., in the Reception Room of Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims, 75 Hicks St, Brooklyn, New York.
