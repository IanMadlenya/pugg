
        PALITZ--Arlene M. On January 13, 2006, after a two month illness. Survived by stepdaughter Wendy Palitz Robbins and family, stepson Stephen Palitz, cousins Carole Labov and Patricia Feuerstein. Widow of William Palitz. Member of the Art Students League and the National League of American Pen Women. She will be deeply missed.
