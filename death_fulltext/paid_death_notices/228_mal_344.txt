
        SCHNITZER--Raymond Leroy, 84. Died on January 29, 2006, in NYC. Beloved and devoted husband of Shirley. Adored and adoring father of Michael. As a long-time radio announcer at Station WNYC, he hosted many classical music programs, anchored news broadcasts and was the host of a long-running popular afternoon news and information series. Services Wednesday 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
