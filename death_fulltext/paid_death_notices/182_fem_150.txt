
        McCORD-Susan Hobson. 71. Died on March 11 at her home in Manhattan. She was the wife of Dr. Colin McCord. Mother of Mary, Andrew and Anne McCord, motherin-law of Alex Okun and Emily Singer, sister of Anne Hobson Freeman and grandmother of Ada and Evan Okun, and Rebecca and Elizabeth McCord. During the 1980's she worked at Literacy Volunteers of NYC. A service will be held 12:30 PM Saturday, March 16 at St. Paul's Chapel on Columbia University's Morningside Heights Campus. Instead of flowers, the family asks that memorials be sent to The American Friends Service Committee, 15 Rutherford Place, New York, NY 10003.
