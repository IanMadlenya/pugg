
        PAUKER--Lewis. 58, on February 25. Loving husband, father, son and brother, he is survived by his devoted and caring wife Judith, sons Benjamin and Edward, mother Norma Rosenthal, sister Genette Lieb, and many adoring cousins, nieces, nephews and friends. He loved and admired his father Edward, who preceded him in death. An international business man of great intelligence and integrity, he devoted himself to family, friends and community. He was a warm and caring man who lived fully and courageously despite a long illness. Funeral Monday, 1:30 pm, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Dartmouth Hillel, Roth Center for Jewish Life, Dartmouth College, 5 Occom Ridge, Hanover, NH 03755; or Jewish United Fund, Ben Gurion Way, 1 So. Franklin St., Chicago, IL 60606-4594.
        PAUKER -- Lewis. ''NEM'' cousins and the extended Pauker Family express their deep sense of loss and sadness on the passing of Lewis Pauker. His memory will be a source of strength to Judy, Eddie, Ben, Norma, Genette and all who were privileged to know him. We will miss him dearly.
