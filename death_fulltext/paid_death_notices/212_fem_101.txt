
        MAYER--Sylvia. 91, of Great Neck, NY. Passed away peacefully September 11, 2004. A strong and wonderful person. Caring wife, best friend and partner of husband Cyrus, who recently passed away. Devoted and beloved mother of Michael and Carole, Chester and Kathy, Amy and Alan Goodstein. Beloved grandmother of Scott and Annie, Robert, Bobby, Kacy, Shauna, Zachary and Alyssa. Rest in peace, much love. Services Monday at 2 pm, Temple Beth-El of Great Neck, NY, 5 Old Mill Road, 516-487-0900.
        MAYER -- Sylvia. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
