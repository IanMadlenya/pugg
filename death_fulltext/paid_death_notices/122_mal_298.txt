
        KANE-Irving. The Board of Delegates, Officers and Staff of the Council of Jewish Federations mourn the passing of Irving Kane. From 1959-62, he led CJF as its President and in the ensuing years he continued to play an influential role as a member of our Executive Committee. We shall miss his wise counsel and guidance. Dr. Conrad L. Giles, CJF President Martin S. Kraar, CJF Exec. VP
