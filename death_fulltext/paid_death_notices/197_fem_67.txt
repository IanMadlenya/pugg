
        YELLIN--Lynn R. (nee Yarry). Died peacefully in her home in Cobble Hill-Brooklyn, NY, on June 5, 2003. She fought a courageous and inspirational battle with cancer. She was one of the top real estate brokers at the William B. May Company in Brooklyn. She also worked at McGraw Hill in Marketing Department. She is survived by her loving husband, musician Peter Yellin, loving daughter Allegra and their wonderful family, loving and inspirational mother Jeanne and loving brother Mark and his wonderful family. A memorial tribute will take place in a few weeks. Her spirit will be with us forever.
