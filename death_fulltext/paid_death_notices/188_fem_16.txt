
        HENNING-Martha E. (nee Norbury). Of Washington Township NJ. On August 31. Beloved mother of Jean H. Cockcroft and the late Carol H. Shaw of New York City. Dear wife of the late Arthur F. Henning, VP of Chase Manhattan Bank. Devoted grandmother of Tristan Henning Cockcroft and John V. Cockcroft IV. For many years a treasured executive secretary with Union Carbide. Martha was nearly indomitable and never a quitter, she had survived pancreatic cancer for ten years before other ailments took her. She was blessed with a last great pleasure of participation in her grandson Tristan's wedding in late July. Friends may call at Becker Funeral Home, Westwood NJ on Tuesday, 79PM and Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9PM. Funeral at Church of Our Lady of Good Counsel, Washington Township on Thursday at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research or to the charity of your choice would be appreciated.
