
        O'LEARY-Michael J. Jr., MD, of Ossining, NY died suddenly on September 26, 1999. Dr. O'Leary was born on July 20, 1946 in Manhattan and was the son of Michael J. Sr. and Mary Regina O'Leary. He was a graduate of Iona College where he earned a B.A. Degree Cum Laude in 1968 and a B.S. Degree Cum Laude in 1969. He received his medical degree in 1974 from Downstate Medical College and completed residencies in family practice and ophthalmology at J.F.K. Medical Center in Edison, NJ and Westchester Medical Center in Valhalla, NY. He entered private practice for Ophthalmology in Putnam County, NY in 1988 and had practiced emergency medicine several years prior. Since 1995 he had been a member of the voluntary teaching staff in the Department of Ophthalmology at Westchester County Medical Center-New York Medical College. On November 2, 1984, he married Anne Compton at Sacred Heart Church in Hartsdale, NY. He is survived by his wife Anne, his father Michael J. Sr, his stepmother Mary, three daughters Jeanmarie, Aileen and Meaghan, one son Michael J. III, his brothers John and Vincent, his sister Jean, and several nieces and nephews. Visiting hours will be held Tuesday, 7-9 and Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9PM at the Dorsey Funeral Home, 14 Emwilton Place, Ossining, NY. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Thursday at 10AM at St. Augustine's Church in Ossining. Interment will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the American Heart Association, 320 Westchester Avenue, Purchase, NY 10577.
