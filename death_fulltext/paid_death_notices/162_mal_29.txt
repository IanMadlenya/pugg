
        REITMAN-Albert A., D.D.S. Of Roslyn, NY and Pompano Beach, FL, on July 2, 2000. Loving husband of Skip; beloved father of Jeffrey and Cherie, Judy and Richard Faber and Abbey Krantz. Adored grandfather of Kim, Glenn, Zackary, Brooke, Josh, David and Eric. Dear brother of Florence Reitman Kinsburner. A special man who loved his family, his orthodontic profession and his friends. He taught residents at Long Island Jewish Hospital Dental Department for more than 40 years and at Nova University Orthodontic Department. He was a Diplomate of the American Board of Orthodontics, FACD, American Academy of Dentists for the Handicapped and an Orthodontic Consultant for the Department of Health of Nassau County. He was also a member of the Garden City Rotary. Funeral services 2 PM Monday, July 3, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury. Interment at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Pinelawn. A donation in his name can be made to the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital or charity of your choice.
