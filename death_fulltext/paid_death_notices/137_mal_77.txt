
        KAZIN-Alfred. The directors and staff of The Library of America note with sadness the death of Alfred Kazin, a long time friend and literary advisor. With admiration for his many contributions to American literature, and to our organization, we extend condolences to his family and friends. Cheryl Hurley, President Richard Poirier, Chairman The Library of America
