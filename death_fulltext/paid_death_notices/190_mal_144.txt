
        RENNA-Anthony W., DDS. On November 9, 2002, past Member of N.Y. Italian Dental Society, residence in Brooklyn, and Sag Harbor, NY. He was beloved for his humor, and kindness. His loss is deeply mourned by his wife, Dorothy, of 54 years, his children, Patricia Cartelli, Michael Renna, Rosemarie Tirone, and their spouses, his six grandchildren, brothers, Alfred and Eugene, sister, Eleanor, many nieces and nephews. A Memorial Service will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Calvary Hospital, Bx, NY.
