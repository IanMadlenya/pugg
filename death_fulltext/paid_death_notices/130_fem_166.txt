
        KUTTNER-Sharland Trotter. After an inspirational 3-year journey, at home, November 25. Loving wife of Robert Kuttner, sister of John Trotter, mother of Gabriel and Jessica, mother-in-law of Kate, and daughter-in-law of Polly. Grace was her middle name. Memorial service Wednesday, December 3, 11 A.M., at Memorial Church, Harvard Yard. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent in Sharland's name in honor of Dr. Lowell Schnipper to Boston's Beth Israel Hospital.
