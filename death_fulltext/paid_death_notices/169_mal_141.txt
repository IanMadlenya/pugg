
        ANTOSOFSKY-Cantor Irving. February 10, 2001, age 93. Loving husband of Gussie for 70 years. Beloved father of Rabbi Marvin Antosofsky and Loretta, Barbara Kasower and Irving, Marcia Christie and Roy, eight grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. A cantor for over 65 years in synagogues in Brooklyn, Bronx and Manhattan. Recognized for his beautiful operatic tenor voice and consummate knowledge of ritual. His life was devoted to family and music demonstrating integrity, care and professionalism in everything he did. He lived fully and joyously with humor and an appreciation for all that life had to offer. Services at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
