
        YOUNGELSON-Reuben S., of Suffern, NY, died June 5th, 2000. Reuben is survived by his wife, Audrey, and sons, Mark and Paul. Reuben was a scholar and a gentle man, a Holocaust survivor, and officer of the Russian Army in WWII and of the Six Day War in Israel. Funeral services will be today at the Monsey Jewish Center, Monsey, NY, at 12:30 PM. Donations may be made to the MD Anderson Cancer Center-Fund of Dr. R. Komaki, 1515 Holcombe Blvd., Houston, TX 77030.
