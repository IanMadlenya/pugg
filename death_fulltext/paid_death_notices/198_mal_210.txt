
        MICHAELS--Melvin ''Mel'', on July 23rd, 2003. Loving husband of Helen. Caring father of Nicki Dewar (Nicholas), Craig (Susan), and Ian. Adoring grandfather of Noah, Alana, and Esther Michaels. Devoted brother of Miriam and Sidney Michael, and Frances Randall. A veteran of World War II, Mel entered as a private and exited five years later as a Major in the AAF. He was a former owner of Capitol Distributors Inc.
