
        SELIGSOHN-Selma. Migdal Ohr Institutions in Israel and the Board of Directors of the American Friends of Migdal Ohr deeply mourn the passing of Selma Seligsohn, mother of our dear friend & Board member, Jerry Seligsohn. We join all who knew her in remembrance of a loving mother to Jerry and his wife Sandy, grandmother to Lisa and Danny and wife to her departed husband, Max Seligsohn, o'bm. May the Almighty console the entire Seligsohn family among the other mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Yitzchok Dovid Grossman, Founder and Dean, Migdal Ohr Chief Rabbi, Migdal Ha'Emek Rabbi Eliezer Rosner, Migdal Ohr Institutions, Jerusalem Max Thurm, President Ronni Amster, National Director American Friends of Migdal Ohr
        SELIGSOHN-Selma. The friends and family of Advance Group Ltd. and Fada Industries mourn the loss of our longtime matriarch, Selma Seligsohn, loving mother of our President, Jerry Seligsohn. Throughout the years, Selma's humor, wit and insight together with her late husband, Max and her brother, ''Uncle Yitzy'' was always a source of cheer and good will within the company. She will be greatly missed. May the Lord comfort Jerry, his wife Sandy, and their children Lisa and Danny among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
