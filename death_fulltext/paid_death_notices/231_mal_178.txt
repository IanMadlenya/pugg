
        JOYCE--John. Of Bronxville, NY, born in Ballyconlought, Hedford, Co. Galway, Ireland, died on April 14, 2006. Former restaurateur of Joyce's Restaurant, New York City. Beloved husband of Julia Dymphna, and loving father of John and Anne. Cherished grandfather of Sean, Julia, Peter and Meghan. Brother to Richard, Bridie McGuire, Thomas and the late Mary Cunningham. Uncle to many nieces and nephews. He will forever be remembered forhis love of, and dedication to, his family. Visitation on Tuesday, from 2-4 pm, and 7-9 pm, at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, NY, on Wednesday, April 19, 2006, at 10 am. Interment following at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please send donations in his memory to Catholic Charities of New York, 1011 First Ave, NY, NY 10022.
