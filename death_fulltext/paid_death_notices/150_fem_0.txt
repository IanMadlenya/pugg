
        ALFORD-Emily Sweetser. Age 83. On June 19, 1999 at Hanover, N.H. Wife, for fifty-seven years, of Newell G. Alford. Mother of Elizabeth F. A graduate of Winsor School and Bennington College. Gifted teacher for forty years at Dalton, Chapin, City and Country and Elizabeth Seeger, where she was Founder and Trustee. Memorial in New York City in the Fall. Donations to High Horses Therapeutic Riding Group, Box 404, Fairlee, VT 05045.
