
        KAHN-Lawrence. Of Flushing on April 28, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Beatrice; devoted father of Robert Kahn and Diana Taylor; loving grandfather of Brian and Rebecca Taylor; beloved brother of Joseph and the late Henry; dearest father-in-law of Harris Taylor and Patrice Lyons; loving grandfather-in-law of Marguerite Taylor. A tower of strength, integrity, devotion and love, he set an example of humanity at its finest. He was respected and loved deeply by family and friends. We will miss him forever. Services on Sunday, 9:45 AM, Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY. Contributions may be made to Temple Gates of Prayer, Fund for Education.
