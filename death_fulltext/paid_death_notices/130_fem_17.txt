
        POPKIN-Lorna G. On November 1, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Lionel. Loving mother of William and mother-in-law of Prema Popkin and adored grandmother of Carol Smart, Meera Popkin and Lionel A. Popkin and great grandmother of Andre William Smart. Dear sister of Carlyn Strasser. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Monday, 7-9 PM with service Tuesday, 11:30 AM. Interment private.
        POPKIN-Lorna G. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Lorna G. Popkin. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        POPKIN-Lorna G. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation EmanuEl of the City of New York records with great sadness the death of our esteemed Honorary Board Member, Lorna G. Popkin, and expresses deep sympathy to her family. Eve Haberman, President Sivia Brodsky, Recording Secy.
