
        STEVENS-Leonard A. Died on October 11 in his native New Hampshire, age 80. Author, raconteur, fighter for good causes. Loving father of Tim, Brooke, Sara and April. Devoted grandfather to Sam, Fenner, Sophie, Willa, Phoebe and Maddy. A memorial gathering will be held in Lyman, NH on October 20 at 2 PM and in Connecticut at a later date.
