
        FULLER--Joseph Sr., AIA. Of Westport, CT, died peacefully at home on Sunday, October 5, 2003. Mr. Fuller was born on November 18, 1926 in Czechoslovakia. He was a member of American Institute of Architects and founder of Fuller and D'Angelo, Architects in Elmsford, NY. He is survived by his wife Zdenka, son Joseph, and his wife Susanne, daughter Victoria, and her husband Howard, grandchildren Elizabeth and Alexandra.
