
        CARAVAGGI-Bruno. On May 30, 1999, Bruno, beloved husband, father and friend, died peacefully after a long illness. Bruno, former owner of New York's Quo Vadis Restaurant, is survived by his loving wife Anita, a son Robert, a daughter Claudette, grandchildren James and Alec, a sister Angelina, and a nephew Giorgio. A funeral Mass will be held today at 10:00 AM at Immaculate Conception Church, Jamaica Estates, Queens, New York. For information, contact R. Stutzmann & Son Funeral Home, Queens Village.
