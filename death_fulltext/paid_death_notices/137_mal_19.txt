
        ROELLKE-Robert Louis. Beloved husband of 47 years to Betsy Anne (Quad) Roellke, loving father of Robert Jr, Elizabeth, Richard, Susan, John, David, Christopher, and devoted grandfather of sixteen grandchildren, died Saturday at the age of 73. His gifts of love and inspiration are everlasting. A Memorial Service will be held June 6th at 11 A.M. at Dennis Union Church, Dennis, MA.
