
        PARSON-William B., 72. Of Trumbull, CT. died March 18, 2002 at Shelton Lakes Nursing Home, Shelton, CT. Born in Shenandoah, Iowa on May 1, 1929, he was the son of the late Walter and Grace Parson of Centerville, Iowa. A longtime resident of Stamford, CT, he was employed at the New York Times as the Director of Management Services for 26 years. He received his Bachelor Degree in Print Management from Carnegie Mellon University in 1956 and his MBA from University of California Berkeley in 1959. He is survived by his wife, Dorothy Parson of Trumbull, CT, one son, Gary Parson of Trumbull, CT; four daughters, Margo Parson of Norwalk, Sheila Taccone and Shari Lamson both of Trumbull, CT and Michele Hope of Shelton, CT; two surviving brothers, Schubert Parson of Brookfield, MO., Victor Parson of Milwaukee, Oregon and one surviving sister, Martha McEldrry of Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Three brothers, Maurice Parson, Marion Parson and Wayne Parson, predeceased him. He is also survived by his father-in-law, Lloyd Brown of Centerville, Iowa, a brother-in-law, Jerry Brown of Cedar Rapids, Iowa, nine grandchildren and many nieces and nephews. Visiting hours will be held on Thursday, March 21 at Abriola Funeral Home, between 4-8 PM. Funeral services will be on Friday, March 22 at 1:00PM at Abriola Funeral Home in Trumbull, CT. Interment will follow in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Trumbull, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 607 Main Avenue, Norwalk, CT. 06851.
