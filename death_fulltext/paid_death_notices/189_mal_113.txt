
        KRAMER-Emmanuel J. Of Lake Worth, Florida, passed away on Monday afternoon, October 7. He was the beloved husband of Sally Kramer to whom he was married 60 years. Wonderful father of Howard, David and Fred Kramer and loving brother to Ruth Perlman and Helen Bader. Cherished grandfather of Nathaniel, Katie, Idria and Jarrett. Services will be held Wednesday afternoon, October 9 at Nassau North Chapel in Great Neck, New York.
        KRAMER-Emmanuel. With great sorrow we say good-by to our dear friend of 50 years. We will miss his incomparable wit and style. He made a great difference in our lives. The Wolfsons & the Winikoffs
