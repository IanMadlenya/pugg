
        DiFRANCESCO - Christina (Teenie). Passed away on March 22, 2001. Beloved wife of Harry. Adored mother of Jennie and Mike Lardiere and Chris and John Bricker. Cherished sister of Charlotte and Dominick Natale. Dearest grandmother of Tony and Francesca Quinn and John Charles Bricker. She is joining her parents Jennie and John D'Antonio in heaven. She was an exquisite human being who leaves a legacy of laughter, warmth and strength. She will be missed deeply. In lieu of flowers donations should be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Leukemia Research, in her name. Services will be at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Saturday, 3/24 and Sunday, 3/25, 25:00 PM and 7-9:00 PM. Mass of Christian burial St. Ignatius Loyola Church on Monday at 10:00 AM.
