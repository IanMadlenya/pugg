
        BECKER--Norma. We former staff members of the 5th Ave Vietnam Peace Parade Committee mourn the death on June 17 of Norma Becker, unsung hero of the Anti-Vietnam War movement. Others spoke or wrote; Norma acted. She was the indispensable dynamo of American dissent.Alan Barnes, Robert Eberwein Laurie Sandow, Glenn Tepper Joshua Brown, Wendy Fisher
