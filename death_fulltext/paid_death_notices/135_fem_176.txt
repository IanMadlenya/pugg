
        WARREN-Vivian Zoe. Beloved mother of Linda Tirdel. Grandmother of Edward Haber. Sister of the late Metropolitan Opera baritone Leonard Warren and the late Martin Warenoff. Passed away April 23. She was an active member of the Metropolitan Opera Club, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Leonard Warren Foundation, and devoted patron of the vocal arts. Memorial service 9:30, Tuesday, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to either the Leonard Warren Foundation, 510-855-2000, or to the cancer fund of your choice.
