
        EYTAN-Walter. The Acting President, Boards of Governors and Overseers, administration, faculty and students of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion, record with deep sadness the passing of Ambassador Walton Eytan. A cherished honorary alumnus of the College-Institute and esteemed and dedicated member of our Jerusalem Board of Overseers, from its very inception, Ambassador Eytan's participation in our deliberations reflected his love of Israel and the Jewish people. He will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his beloved family. Dr. Norman J. Cohen, Acting Pres. Mr. Burton Lehman, Chair Board of Governors
