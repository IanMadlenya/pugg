
        SCHRATWIESER--Marge. Suddenly on March 15, 2005 in San Diego, CA, after enjoying 85 years of life. Marge is survived by six of her seven children, Bob, Barbara, Beth, Bill, Susan and Mary Jean and eight grandchildren, Jason, Jeremy, Justin, Ashley, Jennifer, Brooke, Kyle and Gavin and five great grandchildren, Wyatt Joseph, Lauren, Madi, Jordan and Grace. Marge is now reunited with her beloved husband, Joseph J. Schratwieser, and cherished daughter, Meg Hectus. We will see you in every ocean wave and every tree and mountain and flower, Mom. We love you dearly and will miss your love and generosity and vitality through all the coming years.
