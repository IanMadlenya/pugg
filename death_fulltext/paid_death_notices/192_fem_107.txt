
        FURMAN - Dorothy. Born June 12, 1917, died January 10, 2003, in Sarasota, FL. A former resident of Lake Mohegan, NY, she was the beloved wife of the late Abraham Furman and sister of the late Rhoda Zak and Burton Loesberg. Her loving children Andrea, Kim and Daniel, and grandchildren Erika, Sean, Jon, Kelly, Rachel, Jeffrey and Brian survive her. For information contact Hellman's Funeral Home, Spring Valley, NY.
