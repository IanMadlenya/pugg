
        STUTZ -- Geraldine. The Board of Trustees and staff of Theatre Development Fund are deeply saddened by the loss of our dear friend and colleague, Geraldine Stutz. As a devoted Trustee, whose passion for the theatre inspired us all, she brought vision, style and flair to TDF for over 20 years. Her unique marketing skills and tireless advocacy helped the organization grow immeasurably. She will be deeply missed. John F. Breglio, Chairman Victoria Bailey, Exec. Dir.
        STUTZ-GIBBS--Geraldine. Died on April 8, 2005, peacefully at her home.
