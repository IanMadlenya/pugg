
        ROTH-Nathaniel. Age 89, on November 18, 2001. Adored husband of Sophie and the late Dorothy. Cherished father of Vivian and the late Steven. Beloved brother of Esther and Martin. Devoted grandfather, great-grandfather, father-in-law, uncle, stepfather and brother-in-law. Teacher, photographer, scientist, scholar. Services 11:15 AM, today, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Crohn's & Colitis Foundation of America, 386 Park Avenue South, New York, NY 10016.
