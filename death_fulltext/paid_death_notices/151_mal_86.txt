
        SELIGSON-David J., Rabbi. The Brotherhood of Central Synagogue deeply mourns the loss of our beloved Rabbi,and Chaplin David Seligson. As he stood on the bima, his long life was our link with the past. As he blessed our children, he traced that history into the future. Victor P. Muskin, President Richard C.
