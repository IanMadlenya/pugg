
        KAHN-Rabbi Benjamin M. The Hillel family mourns the passing of Rabbi Benjamin M. Kahn, z''l, a pioneer and leader of the Hillel movement. Rabbi Kahn served as Hillel Director at Penn State University from 1940-1959, and as International Director of Hillel from 1959-1971. His work inspired generations of Jewish leaders. He is survived by companion, Lillian T. Shevitz, daughter Jeanette Kahn, son Si Kahn and daughter-in-law Elizabeth Minnich, and grandsons Simon, Jesse and Gabe Kahn. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Charles Newman, Chairman Hillel Board of Directors Richard M. Joel, President and International Director
