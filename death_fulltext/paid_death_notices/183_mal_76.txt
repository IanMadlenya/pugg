
        LEVY-Captain Jerome E., USNR (Ret.), passed away on April 5, 2002. He was a distinguished naval officer, businessman and philanthropist. The Board of Directors of the Anna Sobol Levy Foundation wish to express their sympathy and condolences to his family and friends. He will be sorely missed by the Jewish community and the American people. Professor Howard L. Adelson Vice Chairman Hariet Maran Herbert Finkelstein Mark H. Adelson
