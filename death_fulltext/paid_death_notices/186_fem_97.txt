
        STERN-Ghity Lindenbaum. On July 15, 2002 at home, with dignity and grace, surrounded by her family. Wife of the late Max Stern and the late Nathan Lindenbaum. Mother of Marcel (Belda), Maidy (Maurice), Armand (Jean), the late Henry (Ruth), Leonard Stern (Allison), Gloria Kisch, and the late Stanley and Marsha Stern. The matriarch of our family, her memory will live on in her children, her 24 grandchildren, and her 37 great grandchildren. Her life was a blessing to us all. Funeral Tuesday, July 16, 12:30 PM, the Jewish Center, 131 West 86th Street, NYC.
        STERN-Ghity. The Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Ghity Stern, mother of Armand Lindenbaum, a Chairman of the Board of the Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach, and his wife Jean. Ghity Stern was also the mother of esteemed member Leonard Stern, a Trustee of the JCAB, grandmother of esteemed members Dr. Alan and Judy Spiegel and Bennett and Rebecca Lindenbaum. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and know no further sorrow.
        STERN-Ghity. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of our distinguished member, Mrs. Ghity Stern, wife of the late Mr. Max Stern, past president and pillar of our community, and mother of our esteemed member, Mr. Armand Lindenbaum. The funeral will take place at 12:30 P.M., at The Jewish Center, 131 West 86th Street. May the entire Stern and Lindenbaum family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jeruselem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
        STERN-Ghity. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi Ira Leon Rennert, Chairman J. Ezra Merkin, President
