
        KAPLAN-Dr. Louis N. 90, Hollywood, FL, April 29, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Dora Haiblum Kaplan, loving father of Dr. Marcia Rudy and Dr. Mark H. Kaplan, dear brother of Herman Kaplan and the late Frank Caplan and Alex H. Kaplan. Loving grandfather of Debra Rudy Reichman, Jennifer Rudy, Noah, Ethan and Daniel Kaplan, and father-in-law of Carol Dosik Kaplan. He was a dedicated physician for 40 years of practice in Brooklyn, and as a Captain in the US Army during WWII. We were blessed with his love and devotion to family, his love of culture and art, his value of hard work and his determination and optimism.
