
        DRAPKIN-Arnold, M.D. The family of Arnold Drapkin, M.D. announces the establishment of the Arnold Drapkin, M.D. Memorial Lectureship in Medicine at Mount Sinai School of Medicine. The lectureship will focus on the science and art of medicine as it applies to excellence in patient care. Gifts for the Arnold Drapkin M.D. Memorial Lectureship in Medicine may be sent to the Development Office, Mount Sinai Medical Center, One Gustave L. Levy Place, Box 1049, N.Y., N.Y. 10029.
        DRAPKIN-Arnold, M.D. A wonderful, kind, and endearing person. A brilliant doctor who made us feel better just by seeing him. We shall never forget you. You are in our thoughts, always. Louis and June Kay
