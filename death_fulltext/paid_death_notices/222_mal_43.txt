
        MANNING--Joseph V. of Solana Beach, CA, passed away peacefully at home with his family at his side on Sunday, July 3, 2005. The flight engineers of American Airlines are indebted to him for his 30 years of dedicated service as president of the Flight Engineers Union. There will be a private family burial service on July 6. He was a devoted husband to his wife, Grace, and a loving father to his children Jerry, Gail, Mike and David. We will keep his memory in our hearts forever. Donations may be made in his name to San Diego Hospice, 1-866-688-1600.
