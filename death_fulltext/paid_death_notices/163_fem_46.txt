
        CONNOLLY-Alice Joiner. Beloved teacher and matriarch of three generations, died of natural causes in her home on City Island at the age of 93 on July 23, 2000. In 1931 she married Clement Connolly (d. 1990), her husband of 59 years, and is survived by three children, Dr. C. Stephen Connolly of New York; Mary McDonald of Chicago, and Dr. John Connolly of Northampton, MA. She had eight grandchildren and nine greatgrandchildren. A service is scheduled for 11AM Friday, August 11, at St. Mary's Star of the Sea Church on City Island. Donations may be made to the Alice J. Connolly Scholarship Fund at P.S. 175.
