
        STIEGLITZ-Barbara aka; Ma, MeMa, Bobby, Bobs; caring daughter, loving wife, proud mother, peer of her grandchildren, friend; died in her sleep Thursday, September 14, 2000. Gentle, interested, kind, genuine describe her well. It is best to fight and win, but to fight knowing you cannot win is courageous. She will be missed.
