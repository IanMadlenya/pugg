
        BERENSON-Mitchell. Family and friends are deeply saddened by the loss of their beloved Mitchell Berenson who died on February 20, 2002 at the Hudson Valley Hospital Center in Cortlandt Manor. He was 85. A unique personality whose vision and innovation inspired many, he will be greatly missed by his loving wife Lotti Tobler Berenson, his sons Mark and Pelle and his grandson David. A memorial service will be held at the Friends' Meeting House, 420 Quaker Road, Chappaqua, NY on Sunday, February 24 at 2:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the charity of choice.
