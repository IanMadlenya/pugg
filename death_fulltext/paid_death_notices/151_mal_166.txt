
        MANDEVILLE-William Walton. Age 90, of Manhattan, on August 13, 1999. He was the son of the late Henry W. and Edith Mandeville. A graduate of the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, he was an internal auditor at the New York Telephone Company, employed there from age 20 to his retirement at age 65. During World War II he was a Master Sergeant with the 389th Port Battalion, serving at Churchill on Hudson Bay, Oran, Africa and Salerno, Naples and Anzio in Italy. He was a member of Beta Gamma Sigma and the Disabled American Veterans. He is survived by his sister, Grace Mandeville, also of Manhattan. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
