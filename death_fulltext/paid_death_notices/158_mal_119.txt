
        LUSKO-Robert A. The New York/ Connecticut Ford Dealers are deeply saddened by the death of a dear friend and leader. After many years of loyal service as our Regional Sales Manager at Ford Division, Bob retired in only December to follow his dreams. There is a deep sense of loss amongst all of the Ford Dealers. Bob will be greatly missed. Funeral services will be held on Monday, March 13th at 11:30AM at the Church of the Presentation in Upper Saddle River, NJ.
