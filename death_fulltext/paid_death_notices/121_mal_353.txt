
        ARONOWITZ-Albert M. The attorneys and staff of the law firm of Aronowitz and Singer mourn the loss of Albert M. Aronowitz, Esq. He was a kind and gentle man and a father figure to us all. For almost 60 years he was a devoted lawyer, diligently representing his clients with skill and compassion. Our condolences to daughters Susan and Judy, and all eight grandchildren. Services 12:15 PM, Monday, January 27, 1997, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
