
        COLLINS-Daniel Gerard. Suddenly on Sunday June 16th at age 72 after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Anne Weld Collins, dear brother of Muriel Collins, devoted father of Caitlin Ahl, Deirdre, Charles and Geoffrey Collins. Loving step-father of Serena, Evelyn, Alexander and Blake Crawford. Adoring grandfather of five. A memorial service will be announced at a future date.
        COLLINS-Daniel G. The trustees, faculty, staff, alumni and students of New York University School of Law are saddened by the loss of Professor Daniel Collins, himself a graduate of our Law School in the class of 1954. Professor Collins served NYU Law School with loyalty, teaching Contract Law among other courses for over 40 years. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Dan's family on behalf of the generations of law students he taught with his unique combination of superior pedagogy and gentle humor. He will be greatly missed. Richard Revesz, Dean NYU School of Law Lester Pollack, Chair NYU School of Law Foundation
        COLLINS-Daniel G. The Board of Collective Bargaining and the staff of the New York City Office of Collective Bargaining express deep sorrow at the passing of Daniel G. Collins, who served with great distinction and dedication as an Impartial Member of the Board from 1980 to the present. Professor Collins was instrumental in the evolution of public sector labor law in New York City and was a guiding influence on the Board. His insight, keen intellect and sound judgement will be missed. Our sincere condolences to his wife, Anne, and family. Marlene A. Gold, Chair Board of Collective Bargaining
