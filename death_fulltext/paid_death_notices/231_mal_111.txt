
        RABINOWITZ--Herbert J., 81. Died peacefully in his home on April 7. He is survived by his wife, Norma, son Daniel and sister Beatrice Fox. He was a graduate of Dewitt Clinton HS and Ohio State University. He served with 75th Division during the Battle of the Bulge. Before his retirement he was director of merchandising in Monsanto's Textile division and later excutive director of The Knitted Textile Association. His love of life and sense of humor were contagious. His death leaves a void in the lives of all who knew him.
