
        ABRAMOVITZ -- Max. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of Max Abramovitz, a renowned architect who served with distinction as Trustee, Honorary Trustee and Life Trustee for 57 years. Max chaired the Building Planning Committee during the founding of the Mount Sinai School of Medicine, and oversaw the creation of the Annenberg Building to have the school. His dedication will long be remembered. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Abramovitz family. Peter W. May, Chairman Kenneth L. Davis, M.D., President & CEO
