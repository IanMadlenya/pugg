
        FRITZ--Barry R. 64, of Stony Creek and Woodbridge, CT, peacefully on September 17, surrounded by his loved ones. Son of the late Harry and Belle. Most beloved husband of Mary and devoted brother of Robert. Adored stepfather of Jed and Katie Doyle. Favorite uncle, cousin, friend. Wise teacher of 37 years to students at Quinnipiac University. Barry's warmth, kindness, wit and gentle guidance will be remembered by all of us who knew him, as his spirit will live within us. Graveside service Sunday, September 19, 2 pm, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Queens, NY. Memorial service in Connecticut to be announced at a later date.
