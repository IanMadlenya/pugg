
        GOLDMAN-Marion. Of Rego Park on March 27, 1998. Beloved wife and lifelong companion of the late Samuel. Devoted mother of Seymour and David. Loving mother-in-law of Suzanne. Dear sister and best friend of Laura and Jeannette. Sister-in-law of Norman. Adored grandmother of Lorin Michelle, Evan Daniel, Brian Seth and Diana Beth. Cherished aunt of Adrianne, Arlene and Phyllis. Esteemed President of the Sisterhood of Rego Park Jewish Center. A woman of valour. Her goodness, radiance and love will live forever in the hearts of all who loved her and all whose lives she touched. Services on Sunday, March 29, 1998, 2 PM at ''Parkside Chapel'', Queens Blvd. at 66th Avenue.
