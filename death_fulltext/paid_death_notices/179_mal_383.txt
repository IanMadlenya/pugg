
        SINCLAIR-Bert. Peacefully, December 29, at the Hebrew Home in Riverdale, NY, where he had resided for several years. Born in Brooklyn, May 5, 1913. Served honorably US Army, South Pacific, 1943-1945. Owner and operator of Oscar's Restaurant, Brooklyn, NY. Predeceased by his wife Pearl Suben Sinclair. Survived by his sister Martha Sinclair Zimmerman of Fort Lee, NJ. Graveside service Wednesday, January 2, 2002, New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI, NY, at 1:30 PM. For details: Weinstein - Garlick - Kirschenbaum Chapels, 718-377-4848.
