
        SHAPIRA-Mary, 91. Died January 24, 2002. Manhattan resident, born in Meadowlands, PA. Beloved sister of Sylvia Spielberg and the late Sara Shapira, Esther Givelber, & Larry Shapira. Devoted aunt of Allan Spielberg and the late Eleanor Spielberg Feldman, Jack Shapira and Skippy Baumoel. Tootsie will always be in our hearts and her generosity and compassion never forgotten. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
