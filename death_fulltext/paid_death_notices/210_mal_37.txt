
        FERON--James. After four decades as a reporter and foreign correspondent with The New York Times and 20 years with Parkinson's Disease, Jim died June 19, 2004, at Hillcrest Commons in Pittsfield, MA. His family and friends will celebrate his joy in a lifelong dedication to journalism from 2-4 PM, Sunday, July 25, at the Parish House of the Congregational Church at 1 Heathcote Rd., Scarsdale, NY. For more information, call 301-649-0715 (bob@brasilemb.org or after July 16, mniles@ emmawillard.org). A news obituary appeared in The New York Times on June 21, 2004.
