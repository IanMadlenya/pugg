
        HAHN--Jerry. Boca Raton Synagogue on the Hahn Judaic Campus mourns the loss of our esteemed member Jerry Hahn. Noted philanthropist, loving friend and author, who touched the lives of all he encountered. May his wife Lynn, sister June, sons Jonathan, Ari, Steven and their families be comforted among the mourners of Zion & Jerusalem. Kenneth Brander, Rabbi Efrem Goldberg, Assistant Rabbi Hommy Tannenbaum, President
