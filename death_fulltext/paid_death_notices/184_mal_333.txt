
        LIEBOFF-Harry. Cedar Park Cemetery graveside service held May 28, 2002. Died May 25, 2002 at age 94. Loving and devoted husband to Janet (pre-deceased). Loving brother, uncle, grand uncle, and companion to Natalie. Beloved friend to many. Veteran serving during WWII. He practiced law in New York City since 1933 in private practice and for the City of New York. Administrative Law Judge for the City of New York.
