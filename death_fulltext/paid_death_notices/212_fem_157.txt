
        RAPHAEL--Lucille Natalie. Born February 20, 1922 died September 11, 2004. It is with great sorrow that we mourn the loss of our dear mother who valiantly lost her fight to cancer. She will be sorely missed by her daughter Joanne, her two sons Stephen and Mark, their wives Marjorie and Deabi and her loving grandchildren Jackie, Eric, Lee and Franchesca. Donations may be made to FLAIR C/O National Jewish Medical Center, 1400 Jackson Street, Denver, CO. 80206
