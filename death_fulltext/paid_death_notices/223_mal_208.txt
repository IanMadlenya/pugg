
        FISHER--Marvin J. Died at age 78 on August 16 following a long illness in New York City. He was the owner and president for more than 50 years of Charles Fisher & Sons Dental Lab Inc. Beloved husband of Elaine Bernstein Fisher, loving father of Dawn Fisher Braulis, Gary Fisher and Julie Fisher Sinisgalli and adoring grandfather of Sean, Angela and Candice.
