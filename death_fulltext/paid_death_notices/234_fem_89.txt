
        LEAVY--Fay L. March 17, 1916--July 12, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Morton L. Leavy and mother of Annette and Jane. Devoted grandmother of David, Nick and Emma. Teacher and community leader. Her life will be celebrated at a memorial service, July 29, 2Pm at the Roslyn Bryant Library, Paper mill Road, Roslyn, NY. Donations to the Friends of the Bryant library.
