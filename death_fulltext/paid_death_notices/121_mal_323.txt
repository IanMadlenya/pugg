
        BROMSEN-William E., of Cliffside Park, New Jersey, died Monday, February 24, of complications resulting from Alzheimer's Disease. Beloved husband of Dawn (nee Katz). Devoted father of Ellen K. and Amy Bromsen. Father-in-law of William Parker. Brother of Maury A. Grandfather of Daniel Bromsen and Laurel Bromsen Parker. Also survived by his loyal friend and business partner, Marlowe Marcus. Cremation was private. His family has requested that expressions of sympathy in the form of memorial donations be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 299 Cherry Hill Road, Suite 305, Parsippany, N.J. 07054. For information, call Hunt Stellato Funeral Home, Fort Lee, NJ.
