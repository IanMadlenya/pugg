
        SMITH--Sheldon, died in Manhattan, on November 1st, of complications due to stroke. He was 78. Mr. Smith worked for many years in motion picture distribution. After retiring from Columbia Pictures, he became an avid movie memorabilia collector, and supporter of many charities. He is survived by a sister, Roz Post, niece, Hillary Post, and two great-nephews, Tommy and Kyle Williams, all of Chelsea. He will be lovingly remembered as an expert movie buff, enthusiastic babysitter, skilled bowler, and excellent poker cheat. An informal memorial, will be held at his sister's home, on Wednesday November 8th, from 6-9pm.
