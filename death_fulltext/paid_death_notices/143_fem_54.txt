
        BARMACHE-Stella. On November 21, 1998, in her 83rd year. Internationally known fashion coordinator and loving wife of the late Leon Barmache. In her long career she was in charge of running fashion shows in all the major capitals of the world for the most illustrious names in the fashion industry. Though living in Manhattan, she was an honorary citizen of Monaco where she spent a great deal of her time. She and Leon, for the past 40 years, spent their summers in Westhampton where they were known for their enthusiastic and expensive personalities. She is survived by her nieces Andrea Tannenbaum, Long Beach, NY, and Holly Aloisia, Queens, NY. A memeorial service will be held on December 10, 11 AM, at Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton, NY.
