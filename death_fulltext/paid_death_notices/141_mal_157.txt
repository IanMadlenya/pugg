
        GOLDSTEIN-Sidney. The Officers and Directors of Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Center sadly mourn the untimely passing of Sidney Goldstein, beloved father of our long-standing and esteemed Board Member and President of Metropolitan Jewish Health Systems, Mark L. Goldstein. All of us join in grieving for this distinquished and honorable individual. We share in the family's sorrow at this difficult time. May his legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom he touched. We extend sincere condolences and deepest sympathies to his dear family. John H. Wolff, President Eli S. Feldman, Chief Executive Officer
