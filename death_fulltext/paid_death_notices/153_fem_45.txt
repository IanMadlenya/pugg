
        FENN-Elizabeth, died on October 2nd, 1999. An artist, art director, fashion and advertising illustrator in Paris and New York; former president of the Pen and Brush Club of New York. She was the mother of Suzanne and Karen Fenn; the grandmother of Vincent Puig and Aline Puig-Colart; and the great-grandmother of Blandine, Alice and Marine Puig. She will be deeply missed by her family and many friends.
