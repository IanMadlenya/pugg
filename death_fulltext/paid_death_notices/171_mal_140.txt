
        CANTOR-Arthur. The Board and Staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and the Israel Museum, Jerusalem, are deeply saddened by the death of their friend, Arthur Cantor. As a Trustee of the Billy Rose Foundation, Arthur Cantor helped foster the growth of the Israel Museum for three decades. Our condolences to his family and to his fellow trustees. Maureen Cogan International Chairman, I.M.
        CANTOR-Arthur. His friends at the Harlem Educational Activities Fund express their sorrow at the loss of Arthur Cantor, a good man, and an early and enthusiastic supporter of HEAF. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Daniel Rose, President Harlem Educational Activities Fund
        CANTOR-Arthur. The Officers and Directors of The Billy Rose Foundation extend their sincere condolences to the family of Arthur Cantor, our esteemed friend and associate. His guidance and wisdom in fulfilling the goals of the Foundation from its inception will be sorely missed.
        CANTOR-Arthur. Friend, gentleman, patron of the arts. We will miss him. Karen Cooper, Bruce Goldstein Film Forum
