
        KANNER--Dr. Morris, 94, died April 13th, in his home in North Miami Beach, one year after the death of his wife, Madeline. The last of the old Brooklyn GPs, he is survived by sons Drs. Steven, Joshua and Ronald Kanner, grandchildren Laura, Daniel, Seth, Tracey, Sarah, Rebecca, and Michael, and great-grandchildren Alex, Lindsay, Ryan, and Danielle.
