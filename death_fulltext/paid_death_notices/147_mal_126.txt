
        LYONS-Edward P.. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of Edward P. Lyons, devoted husband of alumna Margaret Whyte Lyons and member of the Board of Trustees for almost 20 years. Edward Lyons was passionate about the mission of this College. His wise counsel which he shared unreservedly and his quiet witness of vibrant faith helped to shape the College through the past three decades. Generations of students, faculty and staff will benefit from his extraordinary devotion and faithfulness to this College. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU Chair, Board of Trustees Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D., President
