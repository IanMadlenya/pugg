
        FORSTER--Louis P. Jr. 93. Cornwall, PA. Formerly of New York City. Died March 11, 2004 in Cornwall Manor. Retired Associate Editor of the New Yorker Magazine. 1933 graduate of Harvard. Member of Harvard Club of New York City. Last of family. Survived by friends, Douglas and Joan Fenwick, Richland, PA. At his request private services will be held.
