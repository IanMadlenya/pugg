
        HILDT--John, 88. of Gates Mills, Ohio, died of complications from emphysema Saturday, August 13, 2005. Beloved husband of Dorothy (nee Tremaine); devoted father of Anne H. Geddes (Robert), J. Bradley Hildt (Elizabeth), David T. Hildt (Kate), Daniel T. Hildt, Cary H. Mathews (James) and stepfather to K. Bryant Wick (Emily) and Dorothy C. Wick. He is also survived by eight grandchildren, one greatgrandchild and five stepgrandchildren. His wife of 36 years, Patricia Hildt (nee Delehanty), predeceased him, succumbing to cancer in 1977. Mr. Hildt grew up in Baltimore, class of '35 at The Taft School, and attended John Hopkins University. In 1941, he served as an officer in the Signal Corps attached to the Army Security Agency. In 1946 he lived in Locust Valley, New York, and was an officer at the Bank of New York. In 1952, Mr. Hildt moved to Hunting Valley, Ohio, and joined The National City Bank of Cleveland, until his retirement in 1981 as Senior Vice President. While a resident of Hunting Valley from 1955 to 1974, he was a member of the Council, the Finance Director and Treasurer. Mr. Hildt was a member of the Chagrin Valley Hunt Club, The Kirtland Country Club, The Union Club, the Everglades Club and the Bath and Tennis Club of Palm Beach. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, August 31st, at 4 PM, at St. Christopher's By The River in Gates Mills, Ohio. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that contributions be made to the VNA Hospice and Palliative Partners of Ohio, 2500 East 22nd Street, Cleveland, Ohio 44115.
