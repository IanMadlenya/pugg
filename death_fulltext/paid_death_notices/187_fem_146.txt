
        STODDARD-Margery Pepperrell. August 20, 2002. Beloved daughter of the late Francis R. Stoddard and the late Eleanor Wipple. Loving sister of Dudley W. Stoddard of New York City & Frances S. Dunn of Albany, Georgia. Devoted aunt of Lucy H. Hanafourde of Miami, FL., Sherburne W. Dunn of Olathe, KS., Townsend H. Dunn of Wayne, PA., Abigail A. Dunn of Old Hickery, TN., Katherine D. Gray of Scottsdale, AZ. and Deborah S. Moulton of Southampton, N.Y. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. She was a member of the National Society of Colonial Dames, The Huguenot Society and Daughters of Cincinnati. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St Wednesday, August 21, 7-9PM. Interment to take place Thursday, August 22nd, 1 P.M., at the Memorial Cemetery of Saint John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor, New York. Donations in her memory may be made to the American Bible Society.
