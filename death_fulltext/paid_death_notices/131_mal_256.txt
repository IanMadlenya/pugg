
        TUCHMAN-Lester R., 93. Died on December 17 at his home in Connecticut. Pre-deceased by his wife Barbara, loving brother of Martha and Lucille, adored father of Lucy, Jessica and Alma and doctor, friend and helper to hundreds. The world has lost a huge heart. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Temple Sholom, Greenwich, Connecticut or to the Lester R. Tuchman Scholarship Fund at Mount Sinai Hospital in NYC.
