
        MARGOLIS-Allan. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the untimely passing of our dear friend, Allan Margolis, a member of our National Commission. A longtime leader and generous supporter of the League, Allan was active on many ADL committees and served as Chairman of our Florida Regional Board. We extend our condolences to his wife, Mia and the entire family. Glen A. Tobias National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
