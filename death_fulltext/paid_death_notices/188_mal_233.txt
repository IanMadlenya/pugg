
        ROGER - Ronald. Passed away on Septemeber 20, 2002. He is survived by his beloved and loving wife Bobbie Koppel Roger, his children Robin Roger Goodspeed and son-inlaw Roger Goodspeed, of Larchmont, NY, and Randall Koppel Roger and daughterin-law Kathi Nuckolls Roger, of Boca Raton, FL, and grandchildren Lily, Elizabeth, Sam, Juliana and Brandon. Born, raised and married in Brooklyn, he and Bobbie lived in University Heights, OH, for most of their lives before moving to Chautauqua, NY, and Boca Raton, FL. Ron was a successful sales representative for many childrens' clothing companies, but his life was his family and many friends. His sense of humor, kindness, generosity and intelligence were extraordinary, and his spirit lit the world. He will be fondly remembered and sorely missed.
