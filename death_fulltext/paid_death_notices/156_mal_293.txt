
        THURSZ-Daniel, Dr.. On Tuesday, January 18, 2000. Dr. Daniel Thursz of North Bethesda, MD. Beloved husband of Hadassah N. Thursz; devoted father of Debby T. (Bruce) Bleiweis, New York; David A. Thursz, Baltimore, MD; Deena T. (Ben) Klopman, and Tammy T. (Steve) Truland all of North Potomac, MD., and loving grandfather of Jason, Michael, Jesse, Jonathan, Rachel, Alex, Grace and Ali. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, January 20 at 1:30PM Danzansky, Goldberg Memorial Chapels, Inc., 1170 Rockville Pike, Rockville, MD. Interment Judean Memorial Gardens, Olney, MD. Shiva will be observed at his late residence. Memorial contributions may be made to B'Nai Brith Youth Organization, American's For Peace Now, or to the charity of your choice.
        THURSZ-Daniel. The Leadership, Members and Staff of B'nai B'rith International mourn the loss of Dr. Daniel Thurz, Honorary Executive Vice President, a great innovator, teacher, leader and friend.
