
        CANTON-Vivian (nee Potenzano). Peacefully on June 17, 1999. Predeceased in 1995 by her husband, Ralph Canton. Most recently of Port Washington, Mrs. Canton was a resident of Flower Hill, Manhasset, NY for 36 years. She was ardently interested in politics, public issues, finance, and genealogy. Mrs. Canton devoted her life to raising her children. She was the proud, beloved mother of Donald Canton of Pembroke, ME., and Elaine Canton of Manhasset. Mother-in-law of Robert Pacent. Cherished Nanny of Paige Canton Pacent. Also survived by three loving sisters, two loving sisters by marriage, and numerous nieces and nephews. A memorial Mass will be held for family at St. Mary's Church, Manhasset followed by interment at Locust Valley Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory to the American Cancer Society are welcome.
