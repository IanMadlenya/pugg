
        ZABARSKY-Joan Rubin. Age 70, of 3 Westomere Terrace, New London, died peacefully at her home on Wednesday. She was born on June 12, 1930 in New London, CT, the daughter of Louis and Sara Rubin. Mrs. Zabarsky graduated from Smith College in Amherst, MA. She later received her masters degree from University of RI. Mrs. Zabarsky retired as a Media Specialist from the Groton CT school system in 1990. She last worked as a teacher in Boca Grande, FL, teaching reading and writing. She was married to Robert S. Zabarsky who died in February of 1999. She is survived by a son, John Zabarsky of Southbury, CT; a daughter Katherine Zabarsky-Washburn of Stowe, VT. A memorial service will be held on Friday, July 28 at 1:00 PM at the Thomas L. Neilan & Sons Funeral Home, 12 Ocean Avenue, New London. The burial is private. There are no calling hours.
