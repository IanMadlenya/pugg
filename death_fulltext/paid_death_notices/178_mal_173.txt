
        VASATKA-Peter Emmons. ''Artist at Large'', died unexpectedly on October 19th. He was 36. Survived by his parents Hugh and Roberta; siblings Laura (Robert), Gregory, Maren (Rick), Tria (Bradley) and nieces Lera, Jamie, Jessie and nephew Aaron. A celebration of Peter's life will be held on November 17, 10 A.M. at The Plaza Hotel, Versailles room. In lieu of flowers contributions preferred for a living memorial endowed to Peter in Central Park. Make checks payable to Central Park Conservancy and mail to Maren Vasatka, 2342 Monkton Road, N. Ferrisburg, VT. 05473.
