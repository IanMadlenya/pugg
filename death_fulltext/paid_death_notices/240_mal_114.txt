
        KRAMER--Victor H. (Vic), age 93, prominent anti-trust attorney, pioneer in public interest law, and law professor, and a resident of Washington D.C. since 1938, died on January 8, 2007. In addition to his wife of 40 years, Solveig Grette Kramer, he is survived by three children from his late first wife (Miriam Tickton Kramer) -Ruth Ziony, of Los Angeles; Edith Kramer Wilairat and Stephen P. Kramer, of New York City; and two step-daughters, Kristin Kelly, of Los Angeles; and Carolyn Wolfe, of San Diego; five grandchildren; one stepgrandson; a brother, S. Paul Kramer; and a niece, Theresa Kramer, of Washington D.C. Following a private interment on Thursday, January 11, 2007, the family will observe Shiva and receive friends at the residence of Edith Kramer and Kawin Wilairat on the evenings of Thursday and Saturday after 7:30pm. A memorial service will be held at Temple Sinai, 3100 Military Road, N.W. Washington, D.C., on Sunday, January 21st, at 2:30pm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to either the Petoskey-Harbor Springs Area Community Foundation, 616 Petoskey St., Petoskey, MI 49770 or the Institute for Public Representation at the Georgetown University Law Center, 600 New Jersey Ave., N.W. Washington, D.C. 20001.
