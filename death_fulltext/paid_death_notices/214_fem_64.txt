
        TREEGER--Helen Felstiner, 95, on Sunday, November 7, 2004. Beloved wife of Clarence R. Treeger. Dear mother of Thomas Treeger (Madge) and Betsy Zander (Michael). Cherished grandmother of Nicola Zander, Jonathan Zander, Jennifer Peters (Eric), and Anne Treeger; great grandmother of August and Charles Peters; sister of the late Will Felstiner and the late Louis Felstiner. Dear aunt and friend. Funeral Thursday, November 11th, 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions preferred to VISIONS, 500 Greenwich St., NYC 10013.
        TREEGER -- Helen. The Board of Directors and staff of VISIONS/Services for the Blind and Visually impaired records with great sorrow the passing of Helen Treeger. She was a most dedicated supporter of VISIONS and Vacation Camp for the Blind and member of the Board of Directors and Advisory Council for 58 years. Mrs. Treeger served with distinction on the Public Relations Committee and as editor of the newsletter and annual report. She was an avid fundraiser and participant in development activities. Our heartfelt sympathy is expressed to her husband Clarence, who is a former VISIONS Board President, and her entire family. Nancy T. Jones, President Nancy D. Miller, Executive Director
