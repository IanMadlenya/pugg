
        LAKOW-Samuel. Formerly of River Vale, NJ, died at home on June 28, 2002, in Boca Raton, FL, after a long, courageous battle against cancer. Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Samuel was a graduate of Parsons School of Design and New York University. An owner and principal of Lakow-Weiss Planning Organization, dedicated to providing services to some of New York's most prestigious companies. From all of us who loved him, we celebrate his life, take pride in his accomplishments, and honor his dignity, courage, and sense of humor throughout. Beloved husband of Jeva. Devoted father of Robert, Scott, and Kristin. Cherished son of Helen. Proud grandfather of Lauren. At the family's request, donations can be made to the American Kidney Cancer Foundation in his honor.
