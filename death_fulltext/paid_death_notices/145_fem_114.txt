
        PATTERSON-Eleanor Morgan Beloved wife of the late Alexander E. Patterson, a former President of Mutual of New York (MONY), died peacefully on February 12, 1999, at the Osborn Home, Rye, NY, at the age of 103. She is survived by her son Alexander E. Patterson, Jr., of Greenwich, CT, her daughter Portia Patterson Westerfield, of Mulberry, FL, seven grandchildren and eight greatgrandchildren. In lieu of flowers, contribtutions may be made to the Osborn Home, Rye, NY.
