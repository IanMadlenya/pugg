
        JUDSON--Carole S. Born March 22, 1935--died November 27, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Arthur G. Judson, favorite daughter of the late Harry W. and Shirley Brenner. Loving mother of Robin Judson and Gerald B. Stoughton and Lisa JudsonSimon and Michael J. Simon. Adoring grandmother of Kate and Andrew Stoughton and Charlotte Simon. A devoted friend to many.
