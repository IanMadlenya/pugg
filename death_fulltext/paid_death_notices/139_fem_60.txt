
        SLADKUS-Charlotte D. On August 8, 1998, in her 90th year. Loving wife of the late Samuel H. Sladkus. Devoted mother to son Harvey, daughter-in-law Frances and grandsons Steven and Jeffrey. Sister of Ethel Ulberg and the late M. Berger and E. Reiss. Proud and dedicated member of AWVS during WW II. Private family service. She is now in God's care.
