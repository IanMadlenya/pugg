
        D'AMICO-Grace M. Executive secretary for the New York Times. Services were held for Grace M. D'Amico, 74, of Pompton Plains, New Jersey. Grace died on Monday, October 25, 1999, in Biloxi, Mississippi. Born and lived in New York City for 65 years, she lived in Pompton Plains, New Jersey, since 1991. Miss D'Amico was an executive secretary for the New York Times for 28 years until her retirement in 1991. The Journal American previously employed her. Miss D'Amico is survived by four brothers, Salvatore, Joseph, Anthony and Richard, two sisters, Annette Scifo and Barbara Gambino (Buttercup), 21 nephews and nieces, 26 great-nephews and nieces, and one great-great-niece. Her sister Gloria Petino predeceased her.
        D'AMICO-Grace M. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Grace D'Amico, associated with The Times from 1969 until retiring in 1991.
