
        GOLDSTEIN - Patricia Vamos. Died February 9, 2002. She was the widow of Bernard Goldstein, an attorney with the IRS and professor at Baruch College and Pace University. She was a well respected expert, collector and dealer in antique jewelry. Shortly before her death she graciously donated her personal collection to the Victoria and Albert Museum in London. She will be missed by her many friends. Memorial services Wednesday, 6 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
