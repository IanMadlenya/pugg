
        PHILLIPS--Anne Marie, MD, 56, Avon, CT, wife of Dr. Walter M. Phillips died April 11, 2006. She was an eminent clinical and forensic psychologist in Connecticut. Survived by a son, Jonathan, daughter Elizabeth, brother Dr. Joseph M. Boyle. Calling hours Monday 6-9 P.M., Vincent Funeral Home, Simsbury, CT. Mass of Christian Burial on Tuesday, 10 A.M., Church of St. Ann, Avon, CT. Donations to the Doctors Without Borders, USA, P.O. Box 5030, Hagerstown, MD 21741-5030.
