
        STEINBERG-Sam ''Nat.'' Of Miami Beach, FL, formerly of Williamsburg, Brooklyn. On March 27, 1998, age 95. Husband of May. He was predeceased by his first wife, Ethel. Foster father of Ernest and Herta Gottschalk and grandfather of Mark and Mathew Steinberg. Member of Knesset Israel of Miami Beach and Young Israel of Willliamsburg, Brooklyn.
