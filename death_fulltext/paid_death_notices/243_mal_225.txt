
        POPA--Ervin A., of New York City, on April 18th, age 98, formerly of Bucharest, Romania. Beloved father of Ingrid and Alda; grandfather of Domnica and Adriana; and father-in-law of Mircea. Beloved husband of his late wife Ariane. Visiting hours at Campbell Funeral Home, Friday 2-4pm and 7-9pm. A funeral service will be held on Saturday, April 21st, at St. Ignatius of Loyola Church on Park Avenue. The Board of Directors of the Iuliu Maniu American-Romanian Foundation are saddened by the passing of their respected and beloved member, who inspired us forever. Justin Liuba, President
