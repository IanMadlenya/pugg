
        HOLDERNESS-Benjamin Stilwell. Died August 8, 2000. Mr. Holderness was born at West Point, NY on September 5, 1915. He was a graduate of the Salisbury School, Salisbury, CT and received a BS from Yale University in 1939. During WWII he served as a pilot in the US Army Air Corps, ''Fireball Airline.'' He was married in 1942 to Zoe C. Thorne of Bedford Village, NY and moved to Bedford in 1950, where they raised four children, John, David, Timothy, and Mary. In 1945, Mr. Holderness founded Aqua Gun Company and was a pioneer in underwater photography, scuba diving and equipment design. He is survived by his wife, four children and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Bedford in September.
