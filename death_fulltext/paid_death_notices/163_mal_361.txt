
        TAYLOR-Donald Mackay. Died suddenly in Venice, Italy, in the early morning hours of August 27. He was 50 years old. ''Mackay,'' as an actor, polyglot and world traveler, touched many people with his courageous, kind and enormous spirit. His sparkling presence will remain unforgettable. He is survived by his brother, Kenneth C. Taylor of Maryland, and by a myriad of friends, both here & abroad, who consider Mackay their brother. Funeral services will be held in Venice on September 9 and a memorial service will be held in New York at a date to be announced.
