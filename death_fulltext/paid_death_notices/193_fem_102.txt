
        MILANO-Sally, of Yonkers died February 10, 2003. She was 80 years old. Sally was a Real Estate Appraiser for almost 50 years, employed most recently by Appraisers & Planners Inc. of New York City. Loving sister of two brothers, Frank of Yonkers & Peter of Bedford and a sister Anne Dalrymple of Coral Springs, FL. Another sister, Angela Antonaccio predeceased her. Calling hours will be at Flynn Memorial Home, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, NY, on Wednesday & Thursday from 2-4 & 7-9 pm. Funeral Mass Friday, 10am, at St. Joseph's Church in Bronxville. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
        MILANO-Sally. The Officers and Staff of Appraisers and Planners Inc., extend their heartfelt sympathy to the family of Sally Milano. Their profound personal loss is shared by the countless benefactors of her knowledge and kindness over many years. She was a highly respected, gracious lady, possessed of great strength, wisdom, and spirit. Her presence will be sorely missed by all of us privileged to know her both personally and professionally. She was a member of The Appraisers and Planners close-knit family for almost thirty years, and will be deeply missed. James L. Levy, President
