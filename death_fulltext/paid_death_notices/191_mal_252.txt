
        PASSICK-Irving. On Dec. 25, 2002. Of Fort Lee, New Jersey and Saltaire, Fire Island, New York, by way of Brooklyn and Sokaleefka. He was 85 years old. Husband and soulmate of the deceased Naomi for 59 years. Father of Louise and David. Grandfather of Sarah and Daniel Medress & Sofia and Jacob Passick. Son of the late Liza and Sol Passick. Biological son of the late Judah Spector. Brother of Marvin Passick and Shirley Mosner. Father-in-law of Terri. Services Riverside Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam Avenue, Manhattan, 11:30 AM, Friday, December 27, 2002.
