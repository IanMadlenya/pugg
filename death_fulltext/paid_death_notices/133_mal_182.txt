
        WALL-Albert S. Age 78. Retired editor, died Wednesday, February 4, 1998 at his home in Dobbs Ferry, New York. Survivors include his wife, Mary Sprague Wall of Dobbs Ferry, a son, Anthony Wall of Jackson Hole, Wyoming, and a daughter, Katharine Wall of Cambridge, Massachusetts. Memorial donations may be made to The Dobbs Ferry Library. A memorial service in celebration of his life will be held at The Tarrytown Boat Club in April.
