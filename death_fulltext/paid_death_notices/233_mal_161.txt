
        PAULENOFF--Irving. 92, of Miami, Fla., formerly of Tamarac, Fla. and Roslyn, N.Y; passed away peacefully on June 13, 2006. He is survived by his loving wife Marion of 67 years, daughter Ellen Kaywin and son-in-law Paul, sons Alan and Michael Paulenoff and daughter-in-law Susan, and his four grandchildren Arden, Gordon, Eric and Jenny. Graveside service will be held at Riverside Cemetery, 12 Market St., Saddle Brook, N.J. on Monday, June 19th at 1:30pm.
