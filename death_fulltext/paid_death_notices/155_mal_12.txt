
        BLANKSTEN-Richard A., died on November 20, 1999, after a long illness. He received the Chancellor's Award for Music Teacher of the Year. He holds a BA and BM degree from Oberlin College and Conservatory and a Master's in Music Education from NYU. He will be missed by his longtime companion, Bill J. Johnson, and musical protege, Chris Pitkins, and three generations of cousins. There will be a memorial service at the Grace Congregational Church, 310 W. 139 St., NYC, at 2:PM, December 4, (212-694-4262).
