
        SCHUMAN -- Rita Zwecker. On December 24, 2003. After a brief illness but long and valiant effort with the ''long forgetting''. Devoted wife for over 50 years of the late Jack. Beloved mother of Mady. A woman of sharp intellect, unique style and great humor, she will never be forgotten. Services Friday December 26th 11:15 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the Jewish Home & Hospital 120 West 106th Street, NYC 10025.
