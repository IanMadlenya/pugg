
        KISS-Tibor, MD, 77. On April 26, 2000 after a prolonged illness, surrounded by his family Linda and their children Barbra and Andrew (Kim). Born in Novi Sad, Yugoslavia to the Chief Rabbi Henrik Kiss and Gizella. After surviving WWII he became a surgeon in Hungary and later immigrated to the United States during the Hungarian Revolution. During his career as a Physiatrist he was on the staff of Bellevue Hospital, the Manhattan VA Hospital and St. Vincent's Hospital Departments of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation. Graveside services private. The family will receive visitors at 4 Washington Square Village, Apt. 4A on Friday afternoon from 1 to 6PM and on Saturday after sundown at 104-60 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, Apt 11E.
