
        LAWRENCE-Adele Marie (nee Cohen). Of Lincoln, MA. Formerly of Freeport, NY. On July 1 at age 78. She will be deeply missed by her devoted and loving husband Jack. Her memory will be cherished always by her children Jon Lawrence and Vicki Blier, their spouses Gerd and Malcolm, and her grandchildren Stefan, Marika, Sam and Zack. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Amyloid Research Center at Boston University Medical Center.
