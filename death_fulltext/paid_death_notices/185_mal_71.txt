
        KATZ-Adam Jeffrey. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the tragic passing of Adam Jeffrey Katz, son of Howard C. Katz who is a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Our hearts and prayers are with the Katz family at this very difficult time and we extend our condolences to Howard and his wife, Ellen; to Adam's wife, Julie, and their three sons, Zachary, Nicholas, & Jonathan Henry; to Adam's mother, Holly Katz Klein; and to the entire Katz family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Board Chair John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & Chief Executive Officer
