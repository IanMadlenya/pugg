
        CLIVE-Robert. Passed away on October 4, 2002. Bob had a distinguished career as Art Director of Life Magazine, the Best of Life, Time Magazine, the Daily News Magazine and Ogilvy & Mather. During his latter years, he returned to his roots as an artist and sculptor. Survived by Mella, Peter, David (Valerie), Paul, Adam (Melissa) Clive. Grandfather of Sarah and Rachel. Brother of Herb and Lea. Companion of Ellen Kostroff. Donations may be made to the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research, 1111 Stewart Avenue, Bethpage, NY 11714. Bob was loved by all those whose lives he touched.
