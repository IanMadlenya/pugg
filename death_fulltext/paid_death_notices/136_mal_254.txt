
        OLFF-Carmen Alva. Died peacefully May 18, 1998, losing a courageous battle against lung cancer at 55. Well-known in the music business, long-time Manager of the Lone Star Roadhouse, NYC, he immeasurably enriched the lives of all who knew him. He is survived by his beloved children Cassandra Davidson and Erik Christian Olff. He will be remembered each day with love.
