
        GLICK-Rose. On October 28, 1998. In Miami Beach, FL. Beloved wife of the late Rubin Glick. Devoted mother of Carl Glick and Rita Moskowitz. Adored grandmother of Marianne Rohrlich, Susan Glick and Cary Moskowitz. Great-grandmother of Justin and Monica Rohrlich. Service Sunday, 11:15 a.m., Plaza Memorial, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        GLICK-Rose. The board and staff of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged note with sorrow the passing of the inspiring mother of Carl Glick, Chairman Emeritus and esteemed leader of the JASA Board for more than two decades. We extend our deepest sympathy to Carl, to his sister Rita Moskowitz, and to their children and grandchildren. Marilyn Friedman, President Steven M. Jacobson, Chairman David J. Stern, Exec. VP
        GLICK-Rose. The Leadership of America-Israel Cultural Foundation expresses sincerest condolences to Carl Glick, our Honorary President, and his family on the death of his mother, a poet loved by all. Vera Stern, President Harvey Rothenberg, Treasurer
