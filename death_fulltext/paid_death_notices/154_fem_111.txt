        KRISER-Anne. The New York Chapter of the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation mourns the death of Anne Kriser, beloved wife of Sidney Kriser and loving mother of our esteemed Board Member, Judy Feldstein and her husband, Richard. Mrs. Kriser has been a longtime, generous supporter of the Foundation and will always be remembered by her generosity, loving heart, and most of all, her indomitable courage. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband, her loving children, cherished grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. James M. Lurie, President New York Chapter
