
        GELBAUM--Bernard R. Age 83. On March 22, 2005 in Laguna Beach, CA. Beloved husband of Beatrice. Father of Daniel, David, Martin and Ethan, grandfather of eight and great grandfather of one. He was born in Bayside, Queens and served in WWII as a 2nd Lieutenant in the U.S. Signal Corp. He did his under graduate work at Columbia College and earned a Master's degree and Doctorate in Mathematics from Princeton University. He was a Professor of Mathematics for over 60 years, and on the faculty of the University of California at Irvine, the University of Minnesota and at the State University of Buffalo for 271/2 years. He was a superb teacher and his students adored him. We will all miss him beyond words.
