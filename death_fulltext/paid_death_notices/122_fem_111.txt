
        BEECHER-Margery. Age 86. On March 13, 1997 after a prolonged illness. Beloved wife for 63 years to Lawrence (Larry) Beecher. Loving mother of Andrew and Robert. Dear grandmother of Amy and Owen. Devoted sister of Stanton Gottlieb. Family will receive friends Saturday 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street with service on Sunday 11AM. She will always be remembered for her style, humor and love of the ballet, opera and travel. She will never be forgotten. Contributions in her memory may be made to your favorite charity.
        BEECHER-Margery. Run where the flowers are. All my love, Larry.
