
        LEWINE--Richard. Of New York City and Charleston, SC, Broadway composer and television producer, died peacefully at home Thursday, May 19th, at age 94. He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth Rivers Lewine, his children, Peter Lewine and Cornelia Fortier, daughter-inlaw Deborah Gobble Lewine, son-in-law Russell Fortier, and four grandchildren: Richard Russell Fortier, and Mary, Annie and Eliza Lewine. Contributions may be made to the Dramatists Guild Fund, 1501 Broadway, NY, NY 10036, or The Billy Rose Theatre Collection of the New York Public Library for the Performing Arts, 40 Lincoln Center Plaza, NY, NY 10023.
