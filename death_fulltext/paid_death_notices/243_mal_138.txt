
        PREECE--Warren E., on Wednesday, April 11, 2007, age 85 of Philadelphia, PA, formerly of Key West, FL, North Orange, MA, Glencoe, IL, California, and Woodstock, CT. Beloved husband of Late -Deborah (Weeks) Preece, former editor for Encyclopedia Britannica. Father of Scott (Laurel) Preece, Mark (Patricia) Preece, Thayer (Bruce). Also survived by five grandchildren. Relatives and friends are invited to attend the Memorial Service on Saturday, April 14, 2007, at 2:00pm, at Trinity Episcopal Church, 966 Trinity Lane, King of Prussia, PA 19406. Memorial contributions may be made in his memory to: Episcopal Relief & Development Fund, 815 Second Ave., NY, NY 10017.
