
        STERNLICHT-Lola. Of Forest Hills, NY, died at age 92. She was predeceased by her husband, Jacob Sternlicht. Beloved mother of Mark Sternlicht of Stamford, CT and Celina Zborowski of Jamaica Estates. Mother-in-law of Harriet Sternlicht and Marvin Zborowski. Loving grandmother of five grandsons: Dr. Andrew (Anne), Barry (Mimi), Russell (Missy) Sternlicht and Mark (Judith) and Ziggy (Galit) Zborowski, and 14 great grandchildren. Shiva will be observed at the home of the deceased at 70-25 Yellowstone Boulevard, Forest Hills, New York, until Sunday morning.
