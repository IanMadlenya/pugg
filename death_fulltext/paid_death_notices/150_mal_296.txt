
        LEEDS-David, Ph.D. Of Woodmere, N.Y. Died July 12, 1999 at the age of 77. A noted psychoanalyst and founder of the Leeds Institute, Dr. Leeds was in private practice on Park Avenue, Manhattan for over thirty years. He graduated from New York University and received his Ph.D. from Yeshiva University. He was analytically trained at NPAP and specialized in family therapy. Dr. Leeds was a proud veteran who served as a Lt. J.G. in the Merchant Marines during WWII. He is survived by two sisters, his sons Rodd Leeds and Richard Leeds, his daughter Eilene Brostoff, four grandchildren and Roberta Leeds. Services July 14th, 11:30AM at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel in Hewlett, L.I.
