
        MARSHALL-J. Julian Howard Jr., age 74, of Biddeford Pool, Maine and formerly of Irvington, NY, died Tuesday at a Maine Hospital. He graduated from Princeton University where he was on the 1941 National Champion LaCrosse Team. He served in W.W. II as a liaison pilot with the 43rd Artillery in the Pacific, attaining the Rank of Captain. In 1949 he graduated from Harvard Law School. Practiced Corporate Law with Root, Ballantine, Harlan, Bushby and Palmer, later with Dewey, Ballantine, Harlan, Bushby, and Palmer and Wood 1949-1955. Also a partner in Morgan Lewis and Bockius 1978-88. Retired in 1988. Trustee of the Masters School, Chairman of the Irvington Planning Board 1966-1989. A memorial service will be held 2:30PM, Saturday at the Church of St. Barnabus, Irvington, NY. Resident of Irvington from 1956 to 1996. There will be a service on Friday at 2:00 PM at St. David's Episcopal in Kennebunk, and Fr. Gerald Burt will be officiating. Contributions to be made to The American Diabetes Foundation, 1660 Duke St., Alexandria, Va.
