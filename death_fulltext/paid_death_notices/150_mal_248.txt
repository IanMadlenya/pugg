
        RAMIREZ-Carlos D. We mourn the passing of our friend, the beloved President and Publisher of El Diario-La Prensa. Carlos passed away Sunday after a long and valiant fight with pancreatic cancer. He will be missed by all of us whose lives he touched. He is survived by his parents, Don Carlos and Dona Maria Ramirez; a son, David; a daughter, Christine; a sister, Magdalena Ramirez; his fiancee and companion Pamela Merlo-Balfour; and a granddaughter, Angelica. Latin Communications Group, Inc. Martin D. Payson, Chairman
        RAMIREZ-Carlos D. The Board of Trustees and Staff of El Museo del Barrio mourn the loss of former Trustee, Carlos D. Ramirez. In grateful remembrance of a valued friend we extend our heartfelt sympathy to the Ramirez family.
