
        SARNA--Nahum. The Jewish Publication Society extends condolences to the family of Dr. Nahum Sarna, distinguished Bible scholar and translator, author of two JPS Torah Commentaries, JPS Scholar of Distinction. For almost four decades, Dr. Sarna inspired colleagues, students and learning communities through his consummate scholarship, meticulous research and outstanding teaching. May his memory be for a blessing. David Smith, President Dr. Ellen Frankel, CEO and Editor in Chief
        SARNA--Nahum. The Board and Steering Committee of Celebrate 350 mourn the death of Prof. Nahum Sarna, father of our chief historian Jonathan D. Sarna. His scholarship is an enduring contribution to the community and his memory a blessing. Robert S. Rifkind, Chairman Alice Herman, Exec. Director
