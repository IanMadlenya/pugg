
        OKEN-Abraham, 93. Of New York City. Died peacefully on Sunday, September 5. Loving husband of the late Clara. Beloved father to Rita, Jack and Nancy. Grandfather to Andy, Rob, Jeff, Jennifer, Gregory, Cindy and Toni Ann. Great-grandfather to Matthew & Gianna. We will miss his strength and wisdom. Services will be held 10AM, Tuesday, September 7 at Gutterman's, 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York. Contributions may be made to the New York Lighthouse for the Blind.
