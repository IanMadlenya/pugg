
        THEISE--Lee (nee Weiss). On October 9, 2003. Widow of Henry. Beloved mother of Joyce Jonas and Richard Theise, grandmother of Jillian Jonas, Stephanie Jonas Stone and Peter Theise, great - grandmother of Charles Kamuti Stone, sister of Stanley Weiss, mother-inlaw of Marti Theise and Gilbert Jonas. Lee was born in Poland and her family left for Palestine in 1933. In 1938 she went to New York City with her father for the World's Fair and was joined by the balance of the family shortly thereafter. She was a member of the Standard Club and the Ahavath Achim Congregation in Atlanta until her husband Henry's death in 1979 and of Temple Emanuel in West Palm Beach for the remainder of her life. She was an active supporter of the Simon Wiesenthal Foundation, ORT, the Jabotinsky Library in Tel Aviv and of several other charities. Lee was an extraordinary woman and will be remembered by all who knew her. The funeral will take place in Atlanta on Tuesday, October 14. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Institute for Brain Aging and Dementia.
