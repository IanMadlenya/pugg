
        DRESDALE-David, Dr. The Department of Medicine records with sorrow the passing of Dr. Dresdale. Dr. Dresdale was a leader in the field of Cardiovascular Diseases and made many important contributions during his many years as a member of the Department of Medicine at the Maimonides Medical Center. He was a pioneer in the field of Cardiac Catheterization and in studies of pulmonary hypertension. His contributions will be long remembered and he will be missed. Our condolences to the family. Dr. Edgar Lichstein, Chairman of the Department of Medicine
