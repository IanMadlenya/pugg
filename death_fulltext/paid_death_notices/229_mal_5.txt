
        GALLO--Samuel E. Sculptor & muralist. 1925-2006. Educated at Oberlin and the Accademia di Belle Arti in Rome. His work graces many private and corporate collections around the world. Beloved husband of Mildred (nee Coiro) and devoted father of Eliza & Carla. Deeply loved and missed by all who knew him. Service Friday, 11 A.M., St. Ignatius Loyola, 980 Park.
