
        POPPER-Victor. Dearly beloved and loving husband of Mara, died peacefully in his sleep on June 13. He lead a full and beautiful life and died a beautiful death. A renowned artist, former member of the Salmagundi Club, he was an official Coast Guard artist. He will be mourned and missed by us all, his sister Jean Rogers and family, his brother Gordon Popper, Dr. Jacqueline Maier-Leifer and her children who were like his own, Mark, Nicole, Daniella, Lori and David, Walter and Susan Maier and family, Joseph P. Begley and family, and many, many friends and relatives. Today was to have been his 86th birthday. He will live on in our hearts forever.
