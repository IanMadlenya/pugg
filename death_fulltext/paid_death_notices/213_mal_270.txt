
        PLOURDE -- Gerard. O.C. 1916-2004. At his residence on October 18, 2004, in Montreal, Canada. Predeceased by his wife Jeannine Beaulieu Martineau, he leaves to mourn his daughter Monique Ogilvie (Palm Beach, FL), his sons Pierre (the Honourable Judge Brigitte Gouin Plourde), Marc-Andre (Jean). He was president and CEO of UAP, Inc. (1951-1970), and Chairman of the Board (1970-1986). He was a director of numerous companies and received the Order of Canada.
