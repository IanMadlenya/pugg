
        VILLA-Jose Garcia, 88. Poet, Jose Garcia Villa, national artist for literature of the Philippines, born in Manila 1908, has resided in the Unite States since 1929. His first book of poems, ''Have Come, Am Here'' (1942) brought him immediate recognition, praised by Mark Van Doren, Dame Edith Sitwell, E.E. Cummings to name only a few. On Villa's poetry, Cummings wrote, ''and i am alive to see a man against the sky.'' Villa's many awards include The Poetry Award of the American Academy of Arts and Letters. Villa is survived by sons, Randy and Lance and three grandchildren. Viewing Saturday and Sunday, 2:00-5:00, 7:00-9:00 PM at Perazzo-Greenwich Village Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St. Funeral will be held Monday at Our Lady of Pompei Church 10:15 AM. Burial St. John's Cemetery.
