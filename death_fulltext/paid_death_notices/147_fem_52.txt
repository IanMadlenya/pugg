
        GREENE-Doris M. Loving mother of Paul, Marc, Eric their wives Sheryl and Elysa. Grandchildren Daniel, Kali and Leah. April 2, 1999. Known for her caring and humor, her love of nature spurred membership and generosity in a wide range of organizations working to solve today's problems. Born in Montreal ''Luckie'' moved to NYC in 1943 and was past President and Treasurer of both NYS and NYC Associations of School Psychologists, and Secretary of Canadian Universities Graduate Society. Memorial gathering to be held Saturday, June 19, 2PM NY Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions for memorial flowering trees can be made to Eric Foxman, 3741 Mitford Lane, Clinton, Washington 98236.
