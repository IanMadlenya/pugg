
        AARONS -- George ''Slim'', Photo-journalist of Katonah, NY, died Tuesday May 30. Slim is survived by his wife, Lorita Dewart Aarons of San Francisco, their daughter Mary Dewart Aarons, and her partner Robert Kent of Gloucester, MA, nine nieces and nephews and eight great nieces and nephews. Slim also leaves behind his many friends all over the world. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his name to the Overseas Press Club of America, 40 W 45th St NY NY, 10036. A celebration of Slim's life will be held in the Fall.
