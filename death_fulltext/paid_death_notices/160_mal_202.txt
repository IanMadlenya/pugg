
        McALONEY-Michael. Actor, Director, Producer. On May 16 at the Actor's Fund Home, Englewood, NJ. He appeared in numerous plays and television programs including Witness for the Prosecution, The Hostile Witness and others. He directed and produced plays throughout the world including his native Ireland, England, South Africa, U.S. and Canada. He produced Borstal Boy on Broadway and won the 1970 Tony and Drama Critic's Award as Best Play. He had been married to Julie Wilson, renowned Cabaret and Musical star and to actress Donna Pearson. He leaves surviving a son Holt, an actor. Memorial gathering of friends and family at Kennedy's, 327 W. 57 St., Friday May 19, 5:00 PM. For information, ''Wien & Wien,'' 1-800-322-0533.
