
        COHEN--Ruth B. Of Forest Hills, retired medical librarian at Bronx Lebanon Hospital, died Saturday, November 22, 2003. Loving wife of the late Harvey W. Cohen and mother of Barry R. Cohen, and the late Kenneth P. Cohen, and daughters-in-law Ruthie and Naomi, beloved grandmother of Dana, Samara and Daniel, darling aunt to Mel and Gail, Roberta, Karen, Ellen, Janice and Mona. Loving friend to many. She will be greatly missed and always remembered in our hearts. Funeral service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel at 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, on Monday, November 24, at 12:45 PM.
