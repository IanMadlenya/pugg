
        SILBERLING--Anne, on November 26, 2003. Cherished sister to Diane and beloved aunt, great-aunt and greatgreat-aunt to so many in the Silberling family who mourn her passing. She loved her life in Greenwich Village, her fierce independence and her pursuit of all New York's cultural riches. She will be deeply missed by her family and friends. She was preceded in death by sisters Lillian, Sam, Suche, Fannie, Belle and brothers Lou, Shim and Meyer. Services today, November 28, 12:45 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
