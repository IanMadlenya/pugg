
        MAClEAN-Dougald Lachlan, D.D. It is with deep regret that the Officers, Board of Managers and Members of The St. Andrew's Society of the State of NY acknowledge the passing of our esteemed and beloved past president. He will be fondly remembered for his rendition of ''To A Haggis'' by Robert Burns and sorely missed by all who knew him. Our love and condolences are exteneded to his wife Mary. Roderick E.
