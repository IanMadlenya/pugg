
        DESVIGNE--Lois McCalla. 77. On February 24, 2006. Pioneer educator for 32 years. Beloved teacher, principal and mentor. Devoted mother and wife. Survived by husband Lloyd of 57 years, five of her six children, six grandchildren, six great-grandchildren. Services March 2 and 3, J. Foster Phillips Funeral Home, Queens; 718-526-5656.
