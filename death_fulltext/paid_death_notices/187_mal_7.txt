
        ROBERT-R. Michael. On July 30, 2002. Beloved husband of Vittoria. Devoted father of Miles and his wife Alena and Marco and his wife Veronica. Cherished grandfather of Jessica and Maxim. He was the Chairman of Robert Paper Company. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, 81 St. NY., Thursday, August 1, 2002, 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to The Heart Institute of St. Lukes-Roosevelt Hospital, Development Department, 555 W. 57th St. New York, New York 10019.
