
        DAVIDSON-Colin Miles, age 46, on June 15, 1997, very peacefully at his home in Madison, New Hampshire. A publisher, farmer, & devoted father to Heidi, Carter, Charles, Colin, and Jacob. Father and step-mother Gerald and Catherine Davidson. Mother and stepfather Dorothy and Rennie Pennock. His fiance Anne O'Driscoll. Memorial Services 10:00AM, Saturday, June 21, 1997 at All Saints Episcopal Church, Littleton, NH. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Mt. Washington Observation, P.O. Box 2310, N. Conway, N.H. 03860 or American Highland Cattle Assoc., Suite 200, Livestock Exchange Building, 4701 Marion Street, Denver, CO. 80216.
