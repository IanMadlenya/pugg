
        JABEN--Seth Paul, 47, of New York City, NY, died December 19, 2003 from complications of AIDS. He graduated from the Rhode Island School of Design and operated his own illustration and design studio in New York City. His surrealist designs have appeared in The New York Times, Newsweek and on product packaging. His artworks were the subject of the Seth Jaben Watch Collection, launched internationally in 1989. He taught design classes at Parsons School of Design. In 2003, his work was exhibited in US Design 1975-2000 at the Denver Art Museum and Museum of Arts and Design in New York City. Burial was in Kansas City, MO. Son of the late Lawrence Jaben and survived by his mother Diane Jaben, brother Steve Jaben and sister Marcia Holtz.
