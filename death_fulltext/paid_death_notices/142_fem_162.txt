
        ROTHSCHILD-Rhona S. Passed away on November 17, 1998, at the untimely age of 52. She leaves behind her husband, Jay, son, Jared, daughter, Jordana Sontag, son-inlaw Richard Sontag, and grandson, Jacob Sontag. She will be always be remembered as a devoted and dedicated mother and grandmother, and as a person who unselfishly gave to others. Her family, friends, and pets will miss her dearly. Funeral services 1 P.M. Thursday November 19th at Riverside Nassau North Chapel in Great Neck. In recognition of her passing, the family requests donations to be sent to The Canavan Research Fund, 16 School Street, Rye, NY 10580.
        ROTHSCHILD - Rhona. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
