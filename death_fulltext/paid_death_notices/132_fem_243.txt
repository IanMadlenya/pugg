
        CRYSTAL-Marjorie. On January 26, 1998. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Bernard Crystal. Loving mother of Thomas, Bernard and Sydney and caring mother-in-law of Ann, Joan and Bill. Proud grandmother of Matthew and Joseph and of Michael, Paul, Susan and their partners, Karen, Sybille and Mark and great grandmother to Evan. She was an ardent New Yorker whose interest in the cultural life of this City never waned, a lady with amazing will and spirit. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, February 22, at 2 P.M. at the 92nd Street YMHA.
