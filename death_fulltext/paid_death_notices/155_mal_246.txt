
        LEVINGSON-Alfred. On December 18, in his ninety-second year. Beloved and devoted husband for 67 years of Ruth Weinstock Levingson; loving and much loved father of Jill and Dr. Charles Nechemias and Abby and Robert J. Levine; adored and adoring grandfather of David and Nancy and Peter Levine; caring brother of Jean Kates and the late Saul and Lester Levingson and Edna Katz; loved and respected by all whose lives he touched. Services Monday, at 1:30 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave at 81 St. Shiva at his late residence Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday, 6:30 to 9 PM.
