
        GINSBERG-James. Died peacefully after a courageous battle with mesothelioma. Jimmy will be rememberd by his family, many longtime friends, close business associates and loving companion Ceil Berman. Contributions to the James Ginsberg Fund, New York Public Library for Performing Arts at Lincoln Center, attention: Robert Marx, would be appreciated. Services will be held at Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, Sunday, 1:45 PM.
