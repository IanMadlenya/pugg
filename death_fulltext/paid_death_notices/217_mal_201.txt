
        STERN--Herman, 91. February 12, 1914--February 13, 2005. Of Tenafly, New Jersey. Born Cologne, Germany. Beloved Patriarch of the Stern family. Known to all as Uncle Herman. Predeceased by his dear wife, Lily. Survived by his siblings, Rose Stern Lipshitz, Emanuel Stern, Emma Stern. His niece, Sylvia Lipschitz. His nephews, Jack Lauren, Christian Stern, Gary Stern, Jerome Stern and Oliver Stern. Uncle Herman, we will miss you.
