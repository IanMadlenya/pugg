
        TIERNAN-Thomas Francis, 78, late of New York, NY & Hawley, PA died on December 19, 1999. Mr. Tiernan was a retired senior vice president for Blue Cross/Blue Shield of NY, NY. He was married to Rosemary (Stevens) Tiernan. Survived by one son, Steven. Mass of the Resurrection at the Church of the Epiphany, 22nd St. and 2nd Ave., New York, NY on December 23, 1999 at 11 A.M. Friends may call at Gannon Funeral Home, Inc., 153 East 28th St., NY, NY on December 22, 1999 from 2-5 & 7-9PM. Burial in Holy Cross Cemetery, North Arlington, New Jersey.
