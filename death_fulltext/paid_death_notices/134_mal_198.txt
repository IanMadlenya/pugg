
        WEAVER-John H. Former husband of Nancy Weaver, cherished brother of James D. and Charles G. Weaver, dear friend of Ruth Merrill, loving uncle of Anne Weaver and also survived by one uncle, Dr. Neil Lebhar. A memorial service will be announced at a later date.
        WEAVER-John H. The Officers and Employees of Brown, Harris, Stevens, record with sorrow the death of our former colleague, John H. Weaver, on Saturday, March 14. Jack retired from the Company as Senior Vice President in 1995, after thirty-seven years of loyal service. For more than 25 years, Jack was actively involved in the conversion of rental buildings to cooperative ownership, at which he was especially talented and effective. We mourn his loss, and offer our condolences to members of his family.
