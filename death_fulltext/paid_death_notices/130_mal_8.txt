
        BOICHIS-Hayim, M.D.. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of a distinguished pediatrician and medical educator who was associated with our medical school for 35 years. A uniquely gifted teacher, Dr. Boichis influenced and inspired colleagues and generations of our students and residents alike. He will be missed and remembered. We extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
