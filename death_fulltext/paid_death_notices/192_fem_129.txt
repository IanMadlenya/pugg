
        VOSE-Pamela Daly. Formerly of New York City and Quogue, LI, died at La Jolla, CA, January 11, 2003, at age 72. She attended the Masters School in Dobbs Ferry, NY, of which she was a trustee in the 1970's, and was a member of the Class of l951 at Smith College, Northampton, MA. She graduated from the University of California at San Diego in 1985, and taught there until 1994. She had been a member of the Blue Hill Troupe, Ltd. since 1956. Private burial at St. Paul's National Historic Site, Eastchester, NY. Contributions may be made to the Masters School Scholarship Fund.
