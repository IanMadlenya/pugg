
        EPSTEIN-Ethel S., 83. Died at her home in Manhattan on May 4. Wife of the late Benjamin Epstein. Mother of Ellen Howe and David and Kate Epstein. Aunt of Thomas J. Schwarz. Grandmother of Benjamin, Caroline and Louis Howe and Lillian, Grace and Raphael Epstein. Service Friday, 12:30PM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Contributions in her name may be sent to the Scholarship Funds of Deerfield Academy and the Groton School.
        EPSTEIN-Ethel. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Ethel Epstein, wife of the late Benjamin R. Epstein, ADL National Director from 1947-1978. Ethel was Ben's life partner and continued his legacy as a champion of civil rights, America's democratic values and security for the Jewish people by her close relationship with ADL. We will miss her. We extend our deepest condolences to her children, Ellen and David, and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director Arnold Forster, General Counsel
        EPSTEIN-Ethel S. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Ethel S. Epstein, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
