
        SCHUB-Hannah. Beloved sister of Marion Newman and aunt to seven nieces and nephews whose lives she touched deeply, passed away this week. She was predeceased by her husband Sam Horowitz, along with whom she successfully started and operated Debutogs Rainwear. She was greatly respected in the industry for her integrity and true business savvy. She was also predeceased by her loving husband Sam Schub. They resided in Palm Beach and Manhattan. She will remain in the lives of the surviving family members and friends forever.
