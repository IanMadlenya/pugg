
        LEVY--Jay W. 70, died peacefully on November 12, 2005. Husband to Norma, father to Joe and Christine Levy, Jennifer and Ron Fiore, adoring grandfather to Ria and Rachel. His wit, selflessness, generosity and most of all his courage, with which he fought his illness, will always serve as a source of inspiration to all who knew him. Contributions to the Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation, 51 Locust Avenue, Suite 201, New Canaan, CT 06840.
