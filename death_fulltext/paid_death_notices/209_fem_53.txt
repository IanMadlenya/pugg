
        SCHOONOVER--Shirley W. On June 4, 2004. Writer of novels including ''Mountain of Winter'' and ''Sam's Song,'' as well as O. Henry and Martha Foley Award-winning short stories. A lover of words, music, theater and fashion, her wit and spirit will be missed by friends and family. Mother of sons Scott and Noel and daughter Robin. Donations to MacDowell Colony, www.macdowellcolony.org. For information, Riewerts Memorial Home Inc, Bergenfield, NJ.
