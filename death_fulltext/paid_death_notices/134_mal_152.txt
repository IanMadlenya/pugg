
        COHEN-David C. The entire Nassau Association for the Help of Retarded Children family mourns the death of David C. Cohen. A lifemember of Nassau AHRC, he was a very generous benefactor and a strong advocate for protecting the rights of all individuals with mental retardation and other developmental disabilities and their families. Our deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Jack Garofalo, President Michael Mascari, Exec Director Association for the Help of Retarded Children, Nassau County Chapter
