
        PRESCOTT - Jake. John Sherwin Prescott III of New York City and Barcelona, Spain, 47 years old, died in a drowning accident while vacationing with his family in the Dominican Republic on December 30, 2002. He is survived by his parents, Robin and John S. Prescott, Jr. of New York and Lovell, ME; three children, Andre Javier (11), Ana Robin (9), and Eric Raphael (5), all of Barcelona; his brother Robert and two sisters Ann and Lyle. Contributions to benefit his children may be made to The Jake Prescott Family Educational Fund, P.O. Box 774, Bath, ME 04530. A memorial service will be held at The Unitarian Universalist Church at Central Park West and 76th St on January 25th at 5 PM.
