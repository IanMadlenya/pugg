
        DORIAN--Roger. Colonel Roger J. Dorian of Virginia Beach, VA passed away on January 18, 2006. He was born and raised in New York City and attended CCNY and NYU Graduate School of Business. Colonel Dorian served in the U.S. Marine Corps for 33 years. He was a V.P. of the Bank of New York and first V.P. of the Australian and New Zealand Banking Group. He served as Treasurer of Weehawken, NJ; CFO of Northvale, NJ; and Freeholder of Hudson County prior to relocating to Virginia Beach. He is survived by his former wife Marilyn Dorian and his children Mark, Lisa, Michele, Regina, Christina, Ann Marie, Stephen, and ten grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to Marine Corps Scholarship Foundation, P.O. Box 3008, Princeton, NJ 08543. Services will be conducted at the Old Post Chapel at Ft. Meyer, VA on March 2, 2006 at 10:45 AM.
