
        BUTTI-Lewis C. Of Douglaston on March 6, 1998. Retired school administrator and a veteran of World War II. Mr. Butti was a principal and Director of Instruction in District 28 in Forest Hills and he ismentioned in ''Who's Who in the East''. He leaves his wife Mary, two children, Lawrence and Claire Myers, two grandsons, David Myers and Christopher Butti, and a sister Josephine Cornacchio. Visitation at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, Sunday 3-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass on Monday at 9:45 A.M., St. Anastasia's R.C. Church, Douglaston. Interment following at Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his name to the charity of one's choice.
