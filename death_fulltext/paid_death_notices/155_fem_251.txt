
        KORAL-Sylvia S. Ten years have passed since our dear mother died; she is remembered with deep love and missed every day by her daughter Marianne Smythe, son Alan Koral, son-inlaw Robert Smythe, and granddaughters Katherine Newman and Sarah Lane. Had she lived, her pleasure in life would have been multiplied by the birth of her great-grandson Max K. Newman, named for her much loved husband Max Koral, died 1968.
