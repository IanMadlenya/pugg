
        HOLZMAN--Philip S. of Cambridge, Esther & Sidney R. Rabb Professor of Psychology Emeritus at Harvard University, on Tuesday, June 1, 2004. For 58 years, the beloved husband of Hannah (Abarbanel) Holzman. Devoted father of Natalie Bernardoni and her husband Gene of Chicago, IL, Carl Holzman of Chicago, IL and Paul Holzman and his wife Mira Kopell of San Francisco, CA. Loving grandfather of Joseph Bernardoni and Neena and Daniel Holzman. Brother of Sylvia Steinbrock. Services are private. A Memorial Service will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to National Alliance for Research on Schizophrenia and Depression, 60 Cutter Mill Rd, Suite 404, Great Neck, NY 11021
