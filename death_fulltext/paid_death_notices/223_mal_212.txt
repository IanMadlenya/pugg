
        NAGEL--Abraham. He was a child of the Depression who knew hunger yet became a symbol of generosity. A funeral director at Isidor Nagel and Sons, Inc., his success was based on his sterling reputation for competence, honesty, and sensitivity. He was the beloved patriarch of four generations of his family. He was a leader in the Masons, Pythians, Odd Fellows and scores of lantsmanschafften. He was the loving husband of the late Molly Nagel. He is survived by his children, Stewart, Gerald and Ilene and their respective spouses Ronni, Hadasa and Craig; sister Min Jaroslow; grandchildren; great-grandchildren and many others who loved him.
