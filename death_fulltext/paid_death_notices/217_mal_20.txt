
        ITURREGUI--Jose. Died February 1, 2005 at Calvary Hospital after a brief illness at the age of 77. Resident of the City of New York for 60 years, born in Vizcaya, Spain. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends. He is survived by his wife of 41 years, Irma Balbi Iturregui, his sister Purificasion Larrizaval and brother Pedro Iturregui. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, 81 Street and Madison Avenue. Family will receive friends Wednesday 7 to 9PM, Thursday 2 to 4, 7 to 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 10AM St. Ignatius Loyola RC Church. Interment Calverton National Cemetery.
