
        SCULTHORPE--Mary Catherine (also known as Kaye Russell) of New York City, Water Mill, NY, and County Clare, Ireland died peacefully at her Manhattan home on June 27, 2006. Beloved wife of Robert Sculthorpe, loving mother to Patrick M. Wall, Jr. and his wife Maria of New York City. Proud and adoring grandmother to Zachary and Olivia Wall. Loving stepmother to Heather Steer (Ridgefield, CT) and her sons Nicholas and John, and to Heidi Sculthorpe (Rumson, NJ) and her children Jameson, Bridgette, Devon and Paige. Daughter of the late Catherine and Thomas J. Russell of New York City, and sister of Eileen Russell of New York City and Thomas M. Russell of Denville, NJ. Survived also by many nieces and nephews and brother-in-law Thomas Wall of White Plains, NY. Catherine, an avid wildlife photographer and former Ford fashion model, enriched our lives beyond measure with her indomitable spirit, avid conversations, intelligence, elegance and beauty. She will be missed by all her family and many friends around the world. Viewings will be at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue (81st Street) from 2pm to 4pm and from 7pm to 9pm on Thursday, June 29, 2006. Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated at the Church of Saint Thomas More at 65 East 89th Street at 10:30am on Friday, June 30th. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center at P.O. Box 27106, New York, NY 10087.
