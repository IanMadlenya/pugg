
        WHITEHEAD-Nancy Dickerson. October 18,1997. Beloved wife of John C. Whitehead, devoted mother of Elizabeth Earls, Ann Pillion, Jane Dickerson, Michael Dickerson, John Dickerson, Anne Whitehead, Gregory Whitehead, Robert Chartener and Sarah Whitehead. Loving sister of Mary Ellen Phillip. Cherished grandmother of eleven. Mass of Christian Burial, Wednesday, October 22 at 10am at St. Patrick's Cathedral, NYC. A reception will follow. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Covenant House, 346 West 17th St, NY, NY 10011 Attn: Sister Mary Rose McGeady.
        WHITEHEAD-Nancy Dickerson. The New York Public Library mourns the passing of its esteemed Trustee, Nancy Dickerson Whitehead. A loyal friend of the Library, she served with great dedication as Co-Chair of the Annual Fund Committee. We will always be greatful for her leadership and her strong commitment to the Library's mission. Her enthusiasm and intelligence inspired us all and will be sorely missed. On behalf of the Board of Trustees and Staff of the Library, we extend our deepest sympathy to Nancy's beloved husband, former Library Trustee John C. Whitehead, and their children and grandchildren.
