
        CURRY-Robert Emmet. Age 88, of NY, NY. Retired insurance executive. Husband of the late Rose (Mooney), father of Robert Emmet Curry Jr., father-in-law of Margaret Kennedy Curry, grandfather of Robert Emmet Curry III and Bevin Kennedy Curry, brother of Bernard F. Curry and brotherin-law of Katherine M. Mooney. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Church of St. Vincent Ferrer, Lexington Ave at 66 St, Wednesday, 10:30 AM. Interment private.
        CURRY-Robert E. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of Robert E. Curry a Life Member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Timothy G. Reynolds, President Henry F. White, Jr., Historian
