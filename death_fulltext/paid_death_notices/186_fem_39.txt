
        HERZ-Elise M. A longtime resident of Larchmont, NY, passed away on Sunday, July 7, 2002, at home. She was 85 years old. She was the widow of John W. Herz. Survivors include her son Andrew and daughter-in-law Jill of Larchmont, son James and daughter-in-law Jane of Webster Grove, MO, son William and daughter-in-law Gwenn of Riverside, CT, brother Charles L. Miller and sister-inlaw Ann of White Plains, NY, and sister Marian Steele and brother-in-law Thomas of Norcross, GA, and eight grandchidlren, Adam (wife Melanie), Peter, Matthew, Robert, Dana, Daniel, Michael, Zachary and longtime friend Virginia Bartee. The family will receive friends at her home Monday, July 8, from 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Elise M. Herz Prize in Ceramics, c/o the Mamaroneck Board of Education, Mamaroneck, NY.
