
        DEMPSEY-David Knapp. Of Rye, New York. Died January 13, 1999. He was the husband of the late Evangeline Dempsey, and father of Ian Dempsey, of New York City, the late David Kevin Dempsey, and Karen Dempsey McKelvey. Memorial service will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to: The Rye Free Reading Room.
