
        MUELLER-Reverend Anthony E. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the Parochial Vicar of St. Thomas-More-St. Edmund Church, Rockaway Point, on July 14. Reposing at Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221 St, Queens Village, Tuesday, July 17 from 2 to 9PM. Vigil Service at Our Lady Queen of Peace, Tuesday, July 17 at 7:30PM. Funeral Mass on Wednesday, July 18 at 10:30AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
