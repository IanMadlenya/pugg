
        RUTTENSTEIN--Kal. June 9, 1936--December 8, 2005: Distinguished colleague, close confidant. He had guts, glamour, intuition and intellect. For 35 years he was my cherished friend. My deepest sympathies to his beloved aunt Cee Cee (Cecelia) Karnofsky and his dearest uncle Charlie (Charles) Ruttenstein. Interment will take place on Wednesday, December 14, 2005 at Forrest Lawn Cemetery, Buffalo, NY. Kenneth Karlstein
