
        MURRAY-Verne L. Jr. 73. Of Hilton Head Island, SC, died Wednesday, August 26, in his home. He was born in Indianapolis, IN. Son of the late Verne and Katherine Wocher Murray, Sr. He attended Choate Academy and was a graduate of Amherst College, class of 1947. He was a Navy veteran of WWII. He was owner of Murray Harrington & Co. of San Francisco and Los Angeles and was affiliated with the New York Stock Exchange. He was Adjutant of Theodore Roosevelt American Legion Post in New York and was honorary member of the New York Police Honor Legion. Survived by wife Barbara Harrington Murray; four daughters Delanne Virginia Marcussen and Katherine Lyn Schaer both of Tustin, CA, Carolyn Arcure of Ann Harbor, MI, Laura Christina Murray of Reno, NV; a son Verne Lee Murray III of Diablo, CA and seven grandchildren. Graveside services Wednesday, September 2, 10 AM at Ferncliff Cemetery, 280 Secor Rd, Hartsdale, NY. For more information contact Island Funeral Home, Hilton Head Island, SC, (843) 681-4400.
