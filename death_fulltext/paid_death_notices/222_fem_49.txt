
        LOCKARD--Elizabeth Eastment, 52, at her home in Warwick, NY, July 8, 2005. Survived by her husband Arthur; four brothers: Anthony, Jeffrey, John and Robert Eastment; stepmother, Cleunice Eastment. Graduated Friends Academy, Locust Valley, NY, 1971 and Middlebury College, VT, 1975. Employed by Varig Airlines 1976-1991. Memorial at Warwick Conference Center, Hoyt Road, Thursday, July 21, 6-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations to Hospice of Orange & Sullivan County, 800 Stony Brook Road, Newburgh, NY 12550.
