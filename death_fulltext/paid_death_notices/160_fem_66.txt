
        VOGEL-Bella (nee Schneersohn). Age 88. Died Friday, May 5, 2000. She was born in Vaslui, Romania, and moved to the U.S. when her husband Alfons Vogel (deceased) joined the Romanian Diplomatic Corps in Washington. She is survived by her loving children Andrei Vogel and Anita Vogel Hoffman, both of New York City, her adored grandchildren David Lehman Hoffman, Jr. and his wife Linda of Riverside, CT, Susan Vogel Hoffman of Los Angeles, and Cathy Hoffman Glosser and her husband Richard of Los Angeles and New York. She cherished her five great-grandchildren Hannah and Zachary Glosser, Sam, Noah and Brett Hoffman. She was full of joy and zest for life, an inspiration to us all. ''La Revedere, Scumpo.'' Memorial services Monday, May 8th, 4 PM, at Campbells, 1076 Madison Ave, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations to City Harvest, 575 8th Ave, NY, NY 10018, or Epilepsy Foundation, POB 17105, Baltimore, MD 21298-8618.
