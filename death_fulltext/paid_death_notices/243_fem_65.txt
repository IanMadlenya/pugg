
        GREEN--Beatrice Stella, 91, of Oak Hill Manor, Ithaca, New York, died on April 3. She was born in Brooklyn, New York on September 1, 1915 to Yetta and Wolf Kosofsky. She is survived by her daughters, Victoria Borus and Stefanie Green, and her grandson, Joseph Lehman. She loved poetry, history, music, art and horticulture. She had a boundless curiosity about the world and the people who live in it. She will be deeply missed by her family and friends.
