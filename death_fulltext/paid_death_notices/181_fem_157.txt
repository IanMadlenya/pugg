
        HOTCHKISS-Mary Potts. The East End Hospice Board of Directors, staff and volunteers mourn the death of Mary Potts Hotchkiss, founding member of the East End Hospice Board of Directors and our dear friend and patron. Mimi will long be remembered for her steadfast dedication to bringing about compassionate end of life care for the people in her communities on Long Island's East End and in Florida. We shall truly miss her wisdom and warm ways. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, John, her children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. W. Michael Pitcher, President Priscilla A. Ruffin, Exec. Director East End Hospice, Inc. Westhampton Beach, New York
