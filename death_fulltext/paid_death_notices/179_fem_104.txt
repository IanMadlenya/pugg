
        PACK-Vivian B. Died at home December 12, 2001, three days before her 84th birthday. Mother of Leonard, Frederick and Jennifer Pack and mother-in-law of Adele Weisman and Sandra Pack. Wife of the late Howard D. Pack. Sister of Walter Brecher. Grandmother of Jesse, Justin, Jason and Anna. Service Friday, December 14 at 10:00 A.M. at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Contributions to The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund, UJA-Federation, Church St. Station, P.O. Box 4100, NY, NY 10261-4100.
        PACK-Vivian B. The Board of Columbia/Barnard Hillel mourns with deepest sorrow the passing of Vivian Pack, mother of our esteemed Secretary and Board member, Leonard Pack. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Leonard, Fred and the entire family. Ruth E. Horowitz, Co-President Richard E. Kobrin, Co-President Earle W. Kazis, Chairman Arthur O.
