
        MASTERS-Charles Day. Age 70, died peacefully at his home in East Hampton, CT, August 19, 1999. Born in Pawhuske, OK. Longtime resident of Great Falls, VA, and founder of the USGS's World Energy Resources Program. He is survived by his wife Anna Ruth (nee Sandin), his brother John, one son, two daughters, two sonsin-law, a daughter-in-law and seven grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to your local Habitat for Humanity or Middlesex Hospital Homecare, 51 Broad St., Middletown, CT 06457. A service will be held in Haddam Neck, CT, on Wednesday, August 25, 1999.
