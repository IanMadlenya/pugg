
        KAHN-Harry. 82, of New York City and Truro, MA, after a brave battle with congestive heart failure and pneumonia, on August 20, 1999. A retired partner of Neuberger & Berman, he was the husband of the late Margery Kahn. He is survived by his daughter Dr. Jenny Elizabeth Kaufmann; son in-law Dr. Peter Kaufmann, granddaughters Sarah Alexandra and Rachel Victoria Kaufmann; stepdaughters Susan and Evelyn London; and his partner Ruth Bowman. Funeral services will be held at 11:30 AM Sunday, August 22, at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Avenue at 81 Street with burial at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY. A memorial service will take place in the fall.
