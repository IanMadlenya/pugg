
        DEUTSCH-Dr. Martin. At the age of 76, on June 26, 2002 in Norwalk, CT. Beloved husband of Dr. Cynthia Deutsch of Weston, CT, father of Benjamin Deutsch and his wife Wendy Gerstmann of Maplewood, NJ, Jonah Deutsch of Spring Valley, NY, grandfather of Alexandra, brother of Dr. Stanley Deutsch of New York, NY. He was Professor Emeritus of Psychology at New York University. Services Friday June 28, 1:00 PM at Temple Israel, 14 Coleytown Rd., Westport, CT. For information call the Abraham L. Green & Son Funeral Home.
