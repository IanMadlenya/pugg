
        SCHMELTZ - Rabbi Benjamin. With deep regret Bnai Zion announces the sudden passing of Rabbi Benjamin Schmeltz of the Murray Hill Synagogue on June 5, 1997. Funeral to be held in Monsey, N.Y. 10AM Friday June 6 at Bais Torah, 89 West Carlton Road. Shiva will be observed at the home of Dr. and Mrs Irwin Schmeltz, parents, 3 Miriam Lane, Monsey from Saturday 9:30PM June 7 through Tuesday morning June 10.
        SCHMELTZ-Rabbi Benjamin. The Riverdale Jewish Center extends profound condolences to Jonathan Schmeltz on the loss of his brother, Rabbi Benjamin Schmeltz. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
