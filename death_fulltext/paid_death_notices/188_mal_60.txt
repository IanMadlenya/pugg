
        MOSS-Ronald J. The entire AMREF family mourns the loss of Ronald J. Moss, Esq. A Board member, mentor, and stalwart supporter since its early days as the flying doctors of East Africa, Ron devoted his boundless intelligence, enthusiasm, integrity, and compassion to advance the cause of better health for all in Africa. Our deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Charles H.F. Garner Chairman, AMREF USA African Medical & Research Foundation, Inc
