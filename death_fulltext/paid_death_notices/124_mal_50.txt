
        GREENBERG-Bernard, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the death of Dr. Bernard Greenberg. Dr. Greenberg was a pediatrician with the Department of Pediatrics for over fifty years, practicing until he became ill a few months ago. He was active in the governing body of the Medical Staff, serving as President in the late 1960's. He served as Chairman of the Memorial and Dedication Committee for over twenty-five years. He will be sorely missed by all his colleagues, friends and patients. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. David Eddi, M.D., President Warren Wexelman, M.D. President Elect Jacob Shani, M.D., Secretary David Feldman, M.D, Treasurer
