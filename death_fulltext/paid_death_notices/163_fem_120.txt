
        HARRIOTT - Marian Francis (Fran). Loving daughter of Jeannette (nee Scherf) and Albert, and beloved member of the Sisters of Charity for 50 years, died on August 14, 2000, at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville. An educator for many years, Francis taught for 23 years in the parochial school system and for 20 years in the Yonkers public school system. Her giftedness as a teacher of English and Drama was recognized when she was awarded the coveted PTA Jenkins Memorial Award in 1994. A graduate of the College of Mount Saint Vincent, Francis had a Masters of Fine Arts degree from Catholic University, as well as a Master's Degree in Drama, Media and Technology from the New School of Social Research. She served as a Docent for the National Trust at Kykuit and Lyndhurst estates for the past five years. Contributions in lieu of flowers in Sister Marian Francis' name can be made to the Retirement Fund of the Sisters of Charity, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, NY.
