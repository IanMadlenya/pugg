
        GARDNER-Nettie, of Brooklyn, NY, September 28, 1997. Devoted wife for 63 years of Dr. Hyman Gardner; beloved mother of Michael, Marjorie (Joel), and David (Debra), and grandmother of seven. Founding president of Shalom Group of Hadassah and president of Brooklyn Central Chapter of Hadassah. She was an active member of the East Midwood Jewish Center, participated in many charitable organizations, and was an accomplished artist. Her devotion to Jewish learning was exemplified by becoming a Bat Mitzvah at the age of 74. Services Tuesday, September 30, at 12 Noon, at Garlick Funeral Home, 1700 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn. The family requests memorial donations be made to Hadassah.
