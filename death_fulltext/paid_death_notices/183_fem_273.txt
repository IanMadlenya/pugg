
        LEVIT-Sherry A. Strumph, 53. Of Shokan, NY, died April 29, 2002, after a courageous battle with mesothelioma. She was the founder of Transcript Associates, Inc. Surviving are her husband Hiram, her daughter Kristin Hooten, her sister Eileen Forte, and brother Joseph Strumph. A celebration of her life will be at the Tibetan Buddhist Monastery, Woodstock, NY, on Wednesday at 1 PM. Contributions to Family of Woodstock, PO Box 3516, Kingston, New York 12402.
