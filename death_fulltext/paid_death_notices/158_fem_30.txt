
        MORRISON-Estelle Butler, of Larchmont, NY, on March 2, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Joshua Morrison, mother of Alan Butler Morrison and Peggy Morrison Horan, mother-in-law of Anne Scherck Morrison and Richard Horan. Grandmother of Nina Rauh Morrison, Rebecca Butler Morrison, Lucia Rose Morrison Horan and Jasmine Star Morrison Horan. Sister of Ada Butler Gravin. Also survived by two sistersin-law, 13 nieces and nephews, and countless friends. Funeral services at Larchmont Temple at 12:30 PM Sunday, March 5. Interment will follow at Mt. Hope Cemetery. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Joshua and Estelle Morrison Endowed Scholarship Fund, Clark University, Worcester, Massachusetts.
