
        FREIBERGER--Phyllis. The Board of Trustees, Associate Board and Administration of Parker Jewish Institute mourn the passing of Phyllis Freiberger, beloved mother of our dear friend and CoChairman of the Associate Board, Jonathan Freiberger. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Jonathan, his wife Elise, their children, and the entire Freiberger family. May the fond memories of a loving mother help ease your pain during this most difficult time. Jerry Landsberg Chairman, Board of Trustees Michael N. Rosenblut President and CEO Steven Olken, Co-Chairman, Associate Board
