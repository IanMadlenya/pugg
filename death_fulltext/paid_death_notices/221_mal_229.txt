
        COHEN--Isidore. Manhattan School of Music is deeply saddened by the loss of Isidore Cohen. A renowned member of our chamber music faculty since 1993, his wisdom, vigor, kindness and compassion enriched the lives of our many students privileged to work with him. His incomparable musical knowledge and artistry that were his gifts to all of us will be profoundly missed by all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. David A. Rahm, Chairman Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
