
        COPPEL--Irving G., age 76, of New York, NY, died at home on April 21, 2007. Born in Brooklyn, he graduated from Tulane Medical School and served residencies in Houston, San Francisco and Baltimore. After serving as faculty at the University of Illinois and a research fellow at Stanford University, he pursued his career as a pathologist with the Veterans Administration Hospital in East Orange, NJ. He is survived by his wife of 45 years, Suzanne Coppel, and sister, Helen Coppel. Friends may call Friday, April 27, 2007, from 11:00am to 1:00pm at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., NY, NY.
