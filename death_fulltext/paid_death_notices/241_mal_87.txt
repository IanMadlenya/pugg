
        DUNN--William T., Jr., died on February 5, 2007, in Hobe Sound, FL. Mr. Dunn was born October 7, 1920, in NYC, and grew up in Great Neck, NY. He graduated from Cornell University in May 1943. He served as a first lieutenant in the US Army Field Artillery during world War II, in the European Theatre. He spent his business career in the insurance industry, and became a partner of Johnson & Higgins Co. in 1966, until he retired in 1983. After his retirement he was active in many civic and charitable organizations. He served as a trustee of the College of Insurance in New York City, and Portledge School in Locust Valley, NY. From 1957-1993 he and his wife lived in Mill Neck, NY. He served for eight years as Mayor of Mill Neck and as director and president of the North Shore Wildlife Sanctuary. He served for 13 years as a trustee of the St. Johnland Nursing Home in Kings Park, NY, and became director and chairman of the Long Range Planning Committee. In 1993 he became a resident of Florida, and lived on Jupiter Island, Hobe Sound. He served on the vestry of Christ Memorial Chapel, on Jupiter Island, from 1994 to 1997 and as senior warden from 1997 to 1998. He served as Jupiter Island town commissioner from 1995 to 1999.
