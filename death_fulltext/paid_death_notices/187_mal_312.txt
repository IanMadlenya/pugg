
        MORTILLARO-Louis F. August 28, 2002. Veteran of World War II, retired Sargent Major. Served his country until 1985 from Fort Hamilton Base. He loved his country and served it well. He loved his family even more. Beloved husband to Anne, beloved father and father-inlaw to Lois, Gerard, Francine, Anthony and Alfred. His grandchildren Geoffrey, Alexandra, Isabella and Sofia loved him dearly and will miss him very much. He will be missed and greatly remembered for his kindness, good will and generosity. Mass Saturday, August 31, St. Mary Star of the Sea, Carroll Gardens, Brooklyn, NY.
