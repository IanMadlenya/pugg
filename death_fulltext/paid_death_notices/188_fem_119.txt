
        WERNER-Georgianna. Mrs. Georgianna Werner on June 19, 2002 at her home. She was the wife of the late Ludlow. She is survived by her sister Freida H. Wormley of Washington, D.C., a stepson Ludlow Werner, Jr. of Chicago, IL, and numerous nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, September 21, 2002 at 1:00 PM at the Religious Science Building, 14 East 48th Street, New York, New York.
