
        WASSERMAN -- Al. Died March 31, 2005. Pioneer TV documentary filmmaker, and in retirement photographer and collagist. He is survived by his wife Barbara, his son Paul, his daughter Vicki Lane, his stepson Peter Alson, his grandchildren Jason, Carrie, Julia and Jory, his son-in-law Gary Lane and his daughterin-law Peggy Conte. He was loved by us all for his kindness, wit and intellligence, and respected for his talent, his professionalism, his artistry, and his tennis skills. We miss him greatly. A memorial service will be held in May.
