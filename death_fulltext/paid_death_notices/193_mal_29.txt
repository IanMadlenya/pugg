
        GEDDES-John A. Died on January 31, 2003, in Williamsburg, VA, at age 86. Formerly of Darien, CT, he spent his career in radio advertising in New York City with CBS, WNEW and WQXR. He is survived by his wife Mary Ellen, son John M. Geddes, daughter Barbara Loh and a granddaughter Andrea Claire Loh.
