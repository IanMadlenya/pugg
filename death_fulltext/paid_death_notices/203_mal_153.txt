
        SHEMESH--Murray, age 79. Beloved husband, father, grandfather and cherished friend who grew up in Northern New Jersey and who worked in the food industry in various capacities. He was known for his generous spirit, kind heart, love of sports and family. He is survived by his wife, Mildred, daughter, Lorraine, son, Marc, son-in-law, Jack, and grandchildren, Jennifer, Joshua and Ryan. He will be missed but never forgotten. Services Sunday, 10:30 AM, ''Gutterman-Musicant,'' 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ.
