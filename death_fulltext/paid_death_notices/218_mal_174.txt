
        SPRAFKIN--Samuel M. Age 93. On March 14, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Ida. Devoted father of David and father-in-law of Hildy. Adoring and proud grandfather of Mara and Jenna, friend to many. A WWII Army veteran, Sam was an avid sailor and an active member of the New York Bar for over 70 years. Funeral services Wednesday, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave corner 81st St. Interment private. Contributions may be made to the Alliance for Justice, 11 DuPont Circle, N.W., Washington, D.C. 20036.
        SPRAFKIN--Samuel M. We mark the passing of our friend and mentor on March 14, 2005. Admitted to the Bar in 1934, he was a vigorous and successful advocate in the courts of New York and in the U.S. Supreme Court during his 71 year career. Sam inspired us with his quiet wisdom, his persistent and careful lawyering and his wry humor. Our deepest condolences to his son David C. Sprafkin and all his family. Peter J. Kelley, Warren Forsythe, Robert P. Kern, Matthew J. Harris, Mark Bastian & Staff
        SPRAFKIN--Samuel M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Samuel M. Sprafkin. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C.
