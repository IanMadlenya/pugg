
        LEFKOWITZ - Sally. The Trustees of the Baruch College Fund wish to express their profound sadness at the passing of Sally Lefkowitz, widow of Nat Lefkowitz, Chairman Emeritus of the William Morris Agency. Sally was a devoted and tireless supporter of education, as well as health care, the arts and the USO. Baruch College, her husband's alma mater, was a long standing beneficiary of her generosity. For many years, Sally was a regular attendee at the Baruch Fellows Dinner Dance, where her humor and courage was an inspiration to all. We send our deepest sympathy to her children and grandchldren. Larry Zicklin, President The Baruch College Fund
        LEFKOWITZ-Sally. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted, longtime member, Sally Lefkowitz. Sally and her beloved, late husband Nat, began a four generation legacy in our congregation. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her daughter Rona and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
        LEFKOWITZ-Sally. The Officers, Directors and staff of the USO Metropolitan New York extend deepest sympathy to the family of Sally Lefkowitz, a USO Board member and friend for over twenty years. Stephen J. Scheffer, Chairman R. Jeffrey Lobb, Pres.
        LEFKOWITZ-Sally. The William Morris Agency mourns the death of Sally Lefkowitz, the widow of our late Chairman. She was a friend and inspiration to all of us. We will miss her.
