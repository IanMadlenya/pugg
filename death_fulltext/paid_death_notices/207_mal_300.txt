
        DAVIES -- Thomas James. Died suddenly on April 25th. Beautiful baby boy of six months will be deeply missed by his parents, Jed and Heather, brother William, grandparents Helen, Edward and Suzanne, aunts and uncles, and eight cousins. The family will receive friends and neighbors on Wednesday evening at The Cosmopolitan Club (122 East 66th Street) between 6:30PM and 8:30PM. Services will be held on Thursday at The Brick Presbyterian Church. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Central Park Conservancy's Thomas Davies Fund for the 96th Street playground.
