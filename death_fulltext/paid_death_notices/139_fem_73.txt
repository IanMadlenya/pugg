
        CLARK-Louise F. Died peacefully at home, August 8 in her 90th year. Loving wife of the late F. Douglas Clark and sister of the late John Campbell Henry. Devoted mother of Louise Smith, Katharine DeLong, Anne Koester. Grandmother of seven & great-grandmother of one. Memorial service Friday August 14, 11 A.M., St. Mark's Church in New Canaan, Conn.
