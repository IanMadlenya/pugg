
        GARFINKEL-George J. Of Fairfield, N.J. and Boca Raton, FL. On Friday, November 20, 1998 from Gutterman's Funeral Chapel, Hackensack, N.J. Loving father of Lynn Gaines and Peter Garfinkel. Also survived by his sister Florence Shustak, brother Stanley Garfinkel and four grandchildren. He was the owner of Cater-Mat Systems, Fairfield, N.J.
