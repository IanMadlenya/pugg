
        KROLL-Douglas A., 34. Died April 8, 2002 of complications due to leukemia. He was a Staff Social Worker for the youth counseling league division of the Jewish Board of Family and Children Services. Doug, once described a hero as someone who touches the life of everyone he meets and that was exactly what he did. He was everyone's center and represented the very best of all of us. Doug was a devoted friend and treasured son, brother and husband. He married Lucy Del Priore on Sept 8, 2001. In addition to Lucy, he is survived by his parents Arthur and Lois Kroll of Scarsdale and his sister Pamela Kroll of Manhattan. A Memorial Service will be held Friday, April 12th at 11 A.M. at Scarsdale Synagogue Tremont Temple.
