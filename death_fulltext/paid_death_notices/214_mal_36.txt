
        RONNER--Irving H. On November 1, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Gladys, devoted father of Madeleine Kaufman (Dr. Stephen Kaufman) and Dr. Hilary Ronner (Ronald Feiman), and revered ''Poppa'' of Justin, Craig, Kimberly, and Jed.
