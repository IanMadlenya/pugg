
        DREIER-Laura. 84. Of Phoenix. Formerly of New York City. Passed away on February 7, 2001 at home. She was a beloved and devoted wife to Fred, her husband of 57 years. Laura was deeply loved and will be missed by her husband, daughter Jill and son-inlaw Bert Alanko. Her dear son Jonathan passed away in 1986. Laura's two grandchildren, Lisa and Adam Goldring and her daughterin-law Tami, along with her three great-grandchildren, Alex, Jacob and Ashley will hold her forever in their hearts. Laura was a beauty both inside and out. She always embraced and encouraged her many friends. Her generous gifts of financial help including those through the Jonathan Dreier Foundation have and will continue to benefit many. Her talent and love for writing poetry leaves us with her private collection ''Through Love, Laughter and Tears'' to treasure always. We will miss her. Viewing and memorial services will be held at Green Acres Cemetery and Mortuary, 401 North Hayden Road, Scottsdale, AZ on Monday, February 12, 2001 at 12:00 noon and 1:00 PM respectively. In lieu of flowers, donations are requested to the Laura Dreier Breast Center at Good Samaritan Hospital, 1111 East McDowell Road, Phoenix, AZ 85006.
