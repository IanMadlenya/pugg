
        OLLENDORFF-Ulrich, M.D. Born in Breslau, Germany, May 10, 1906. Beloved husband of Anne Ollendorff and father of Stephen and David Ollendorff. Devoted grandfather of Linda, Arthur and Robert Ollendorff. Dr. Ollendorff left Germany in 1938 with a young family and established a sucessful ophthalmology practice in Washington Heights where he practiced for over forty years. He was first and foremost a man of great compassion for people and for life. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, December 31, 1998 at 10 AM at Gutterman-Musicant, 402 Park St, Hackensack, New Jersey.
