
        GOLDEN-Bella L. Died peacefully at age 101, Friday night, July 18, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Samuel; wonderful and loving mother to Irene Dash and Allen Golden and mother-in-law to Martin Dash; adoring grandmother of Deborah Dash Moore and MacDonald Moore, Deena and Sidney Kushner, Glynnis Golden and the late Gera Golden; and delighting greatgrandmother of Mordecai and Mik Moore, Aaron and Ze'eva Kushner, and Gabriel Golden. A fervent Reconstructionist, member of West End Synagogue, and fine silversmith, may she rest in peace after a long and caring life. Funeral services Sunday, July 20, at 1:45 PM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
