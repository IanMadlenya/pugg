
        SPAMPANATO-Paul. It is with great sadness that we note the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Mr. Paul Spampanato, manager of the Edson. Paul was a dedicated, loyal and integral part of the Intrepid Museum team. He will be dearly missed. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, the Fishers, and all of our friends, we extend our sincerest condolences to his father, Gary Spampanato, Director of Protocol. Lt. General Martin R.
