
        POPKIN--Jack L. age 87. A true gentleman loved by all who knew him, for his integrity, generosity and kindness. Established Jack L. Popkin Co. in 1947. He will be dearly missed by all, especially by his wife Rita, his children and his grandchildren. Services today 10am at ''Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer'' 114-03 Queens Boulevard (Cor 76th Road) Forest Hills, NY.
