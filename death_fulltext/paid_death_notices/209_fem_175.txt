
        ELLINS--Ruth R. On June 23rd, 2004, shortly after celebrating her 100th birthday with family and friends. Beloved wife of the late Arthur Ellins. Survived by three loving, devoted nieces, Adrienne Doniger, Frances Gorfinkle and Sue Weiland and many grand and great grand-nieces and nephews. Mourned by neighbors and friends who dearly loved her. She was cared for most tenderly by her loving helpers, Mamie McGriff and Megan Cox. Services private.
        ELLINS--Ruth R. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Ruth R. Ellins. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
