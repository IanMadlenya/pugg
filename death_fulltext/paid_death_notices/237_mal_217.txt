
        OUTERBRIDGE - Joseph, died on September 29, 2006 in Macau. A graduate of St. Paul's School and Maine Maritime Academy, he served in the U.S. Navy for two years before moving to Hong Kong and then Macau. He resided in Macau for over forty years and worked for Deak & Co. He is survived by his devoted friend Ah Fong, his brothers David R. and Samuel M. Outerbridge, his sister Patience O. Banister, one nephew and two nieces. A Memorial Gathering will be at Seawanhaka Corinthian Yacht Club, Oyster Bay, NY on November 18, 2006. Donations in his memory may be made to St. Paul's School, 325 Pleasant Street, Concord, NH 03301-2591.
