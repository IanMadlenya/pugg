
        TERRY-Frances Newton. Of Stonybrook, L.I., NY on September 15, 1997, in her 78th year. Beloved wife of the late Francis ''Deac'' Terry. Loving mother of Rev. Dr. Robert N. Terry and Anne Dee. Dear mother-in-law of Suzanne and Jon. Cherished grandmother of Jennifer, Robert II, Christina, Kristen, Tara and Terri. Retired Chairman of the Math Dept. at Earl L. Vandermeulen High School, Port Jefferson where she taught for 38 years. Past President of the Lake Ronkonkoma Historical Society. Active member and descendant of the Founder of the United Methodist Church, Lake Ronkonkoma. An accomplished organist, she was Phi Beta Kappa at Swarthmore College and received her Master's degree from Columbia University. A world traveller and had a love for opera, music and all people. Reposing at Moloney's Lake Funeral Home, 132 Ronkonkoma Ave., Lake Ronkonkoma, where religious services will be held 8 P.M., Wednesday. Funeral Service 11 AM, Thursday at United Methodist Church, Lake Ronkonkoma. Interment following at Lake Ronkonkoma Cemetery, Lake Ronkonkoma. Visiting on Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations be made to United Methodist Church, 792 Hawkins Avenue, Lake Grove, New York 11755.
