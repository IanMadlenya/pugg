
        FALK--Paul, born July 21, 1922, passed away at Woodside Hospice, Pinellas Park, FL, on Wednesday, April 25, 2007 at the age of 84. The adored husband of June, celebrating their 60th anniversary on Thanksgiving Day 2006. He was the cherished son of the late David and Ray Falk; beloved brother of Norma Landsman, Pearl Forman, and the late Henry Falk; and unforgettable uncle to numerous nieces and nephews, grand and great. Paul was for many years a vocational rehabilitation counselor and chief vocational guidance counselor. After retirement he volunteered for the Metropolitan Museum until 2006. A creative and sensitive individual, poet, potter, and photographer, Paul will always be remembered as a warm, comforting, kind person by all who loved him.
