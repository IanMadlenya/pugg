
        WILTSHIRE-Carmen S. Age 73, died on February 5th, 1999 in Las Vegas, NV. Originally from NYC, she had lived in Las Vegas for 17 years. She is survived by husband, Raymond Wiltshire, two sons, Raymond Wilshire, Jr. and Garland Jeffreys. Services to be held February 10 at Hites Chapel, Las Vegas. Interment in Texas.
