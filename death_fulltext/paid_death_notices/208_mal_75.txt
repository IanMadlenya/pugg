
        De CEUNYNCK--Robert. On May 4, 2004. Beloved husband of Harriet. Devoted father of Andrew (Robin) of Seattle, WA and Matthew (Claire) of Chicago, IL. Cherished brother of Simone Anderson of Nebraska City, NE, Suzanne McBride of Murrieta, CA and Lea Siders of Medford, OR. Was a member of the music staff at NYC Opera and the Metropolitan Opera. Services at 3 PM Saturday, May 8, Grace Church, 254 Hicks St., Brooklyn Heights, NY. Gifts in his memory may be made to Save the Met Broadcasts Fund, C/O Metropolitan Opera or to NYC Opera, Lincoln Center, NY, NY 10023.
        De CEUNYNCK--Robert. It is with profound sadness that New York City Opera notes the passing of one of the most respected members of our music staff. As a member of the City Opera family for 25 years, he was an integral part of the team responsible for many of the company's major successes. His enthusiastic support of his colleagues and artists was exemplary. He championed young singers and brought both a steadying influence and a unique sense of humor to the opera house. He will be missed. Paul Kellogg General Director
        De CEUNYNCK--Robert. The Metropolitan Opera mourns the untimely death of assistant conductor Robert De Ceunynck, a valued and esteemed colleague for the past ten years. We will miss his thorough and dedicated musicianship as well as his warm, genuine friendship. Joseph Volpe General Manager James Levine Artistic Director
