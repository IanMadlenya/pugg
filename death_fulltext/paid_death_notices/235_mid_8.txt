
        ALBRIGHT--Veronique Joan. The Trustees and Staff of the Museum of Jewish HeritageA Living Memorial to the Holocaust mourn the passing of Veronique Joan Albright, beloved wife of Trustee Emeritus Harry Albright, Jr. A loving wife and devoted mother, her charm and grace will be remembered by all. We send our heartfelt condolences to Harry and his family. Robert M. Morgenthau, Chair David G. Marwell, Ph.D., Dir
