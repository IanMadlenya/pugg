
        CLARKE-Laurence Warner, Jr. Age 77, of Greenwich, CT and Ocean Ridge, FL, died peacefully on February 3, 2001. He was a graduate of Philips Exeter Academy and Yale University. He was a former member of the New York Stock Exchange. He is survived by his wife, Helen, 2 sons Langdon and Laurence III, 2 daughters Frances Rourke and Helen Gonzalez, and 8 grandchildren. A private service will be held on Saturday, February 10th. Donations in his memory to Nathaniel Witherell Nursing Home, 70 Parsonage Rd., Greenwich, CT. 06830, would be greatly appreciated by his family to honor this gentle, loving man.
