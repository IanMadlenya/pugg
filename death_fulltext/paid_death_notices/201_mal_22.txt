
        SIMMONS--Martin, 92, on September 30 after complications from a fall. He was the beloved husband and is survived by his wife, Doreen. He was a member and former governor of the Union Club, a past president of the British Schools and Universities Club, a member of Holland Lodge, St. George's Society, and Brooks's Club in London. He was a most elegant, articulate gentleman, kind and generous, and will be sadly missed by his many friends. Service will be private.
        SIMMONS -- Martin. The Board of Directors and Shareholders of 455 East 57th Street note with regret the death of long-term resident and former President, Martin Simmons, and extend their condolences to his wife, Doreen. Joan A. Bell, President
