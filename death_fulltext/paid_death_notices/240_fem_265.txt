
        EDELBAUM--Hedda, (nee Liverman), 93. Late of NYC and Katonah passed away in Hightstown, NJ January 29, of natural causes. She was predeceased by her husband Saul Edelbaum and her son Paul D. Zimmerman, she is survived by her grandchildren Ian Zimmerman of Brooklyn and Kirsten Zimmerman of Manhattan, two stepsons Jon and George Edelbaum of Long Island.
