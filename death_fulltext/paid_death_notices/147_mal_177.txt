
        LORD-Jere W. Jr, M.D. On April 20, 1999 of Bedford, New York. Vascular Surgeon. A pioneer of heart surgery. A Professor of Clinical Medicine at University Hospital. Graduate of Gilman School, Princeton University 1932 & Johns Hopkins Medical School 1937. Member of The American College of Surgeons, The American Surgical Association, The Southern Surgical Association, The Eastern Surgical Society, President in 1982, James IV Association of Surgeons, Assistant Secretary from 1965 to 1967 and Secretary from 1967 to 1975. New York Heart Association, President from 1957 to 1959. Survived by his beloved wife Margot Graham Lord. Loving father of Harrah Lord Argentine, Jere W. Lord, III & Lonna Lord Davis. Also survived by six grandchildren. Memorial service at Saint Matthew's Episcopal Church, Bedford, New York, on Friday, April 23 at 11AM. Interment is private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice Care in Westchester, 100 South Bedford Road, Mount Kisco, New York 10549.
