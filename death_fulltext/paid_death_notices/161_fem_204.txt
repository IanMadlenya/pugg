
        TRYNIN-Evelyn J. On June 22, 2000 at age 93. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Adored mother of Margot Aronsohn, Janet and James Stovin. Cherished grandmother and great-grandmother. She was loved by all who knew her. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Sunday at 10AM. Interment at Knollwood Cemetery.
        TRYNIN-Evelyn. Central Synagogue Sisterhood notes with sorrow the death of our honorary Board Member, Evelyn Trynin, mother of our past President, Janet Stovin. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her family. Central Synagogue Sisterhood, Shirley Steinhauser, President
