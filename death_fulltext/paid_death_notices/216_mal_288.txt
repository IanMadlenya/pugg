
        CAMP--James E., poet and retired professor, died peacefully January 19, 2005 at the age of 81. He is survived by his wife Jocelyn, son Robert, daughter-in-law McCormick Templeman, sister - in - law Edie Allen and nephews Christopher and Nathan Stull. Memorial services will be held on Monday at noon at West End Collegiate Church, West End Avenue & 77 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Amnesty International.
