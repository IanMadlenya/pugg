
        CRONE--Harold Arthur. Died November 22 in Boca Raton, Florida. He was born January 2, 1915 in New York and was a fourth generation New Yorker. Mr. Crone was a proud US Navy combat veteran of World War II. He had a distinguished career in retailing including serving as President of the Hecht Company in Washington DC. After retirement, he volunteered with the International Executive Service Corps in Chile and Taiwan. He is survived by his son, Lawrence Crone and daughter, Penny Crone, four grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
