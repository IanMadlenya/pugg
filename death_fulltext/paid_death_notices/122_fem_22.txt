
        ABRAMS - Thelma Silverstein. Died on March 1, 1997, in Louisville, KY. Former resident of NYC. Beloved wife of the late Jules. Loving mother of Dr. Susan Eichenberger of Louisville, KY, and Ralph Abrams, of Ann Arbor, MI. Survived by five grandchildren and one great-grandchild. She was a past President of the Hillside Chapter of Hadassah and the Israel Center of Hillcrest Manor, and a Life Master and teacher of bridge. Services Tuesday, 1 PM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd (76th Rd), Forest Hills, NY.
