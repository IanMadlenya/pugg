
        LEBER-Alexander. Friend and lover. Hero. Cherished life mate of Adam Waitt. Born Wiesbaden, March 19th, 1952. Son of Angelika. Brother of Thomas. Uncle of Simon. This most wondrous of German imports, Alex, had an unyielding passion for life. His courage and dignity awe inspiring. Death came peacefully on November 25th at his home in New York. We are grateful for your life, Alex. Goodnight, sweet prince.
