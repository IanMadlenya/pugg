
        CHAFETZ-Benjamin. Age 80, passed on peacefully in his sleep May 25, 1998. Beloved husband of Rosalind (nee Balterman) for 59 years. Devoted father of Helen Frank and Judy Good. Proud and loving grandfather of Bob, Ronni, Adam, Lauren, Bryan and Allison & uncle of several nieces. Retired CPA and Veteran of W.W. II 10th Mountain Army Division. Services Wednesday at 12:15 P.M., at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, Dix Hills, Long Island. Interment following services at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pine Lawn, New York.
