
        SCHWARTZ - Jeanette. Loving wife of the late Sidney. Devoted mother of Kiala and Lila, and sons-in-law Al and Gene. Adored grandmother of seven, greatgrandmother of 10, and caring friend. Died on February 14, 2002, at age 94. We will miss our beloved matriarch. From Brooklyn to Florida to Boston she spread her sweetness, gentleness and radiance. Whether it was bridge, 500 rummy or life, her winning hand came through. She was our champion. Service at I. J. Morris, 21 East Deer Park Rd, Dix Hills, NY, 2:15 PM, Sunday, February 17.
