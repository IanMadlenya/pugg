
        WINN-Gilbert E., D.D.S., age 62. Beloved husband of Eleanor, devoted father of Mitchell & Maxine, Tracey and Robert, cherished grandfather of Amanda, Alison, Jason and Benjamin. Cherished brother of Dr. Stephen L. and Carolyn Winn, dear brother-in-law of Richard, Michael and Joanne Fleischman. A family dentist for over 35 years, he served as a Captain in the Army Dental Corp. in Korea and as a full teaching attending dentist at the Jamaica Hospital in Queens, NY. He will be remembered for his compassion, skill, intelligence and wit by all who knew him. Funeral Tuesday at Gutterman's Funeral Home, Woodbury, N.Y., at 11:30 AM.
        WINN-Dr. Gilbert. The Board of Directors and membership of Glen Head Country Club announce with profound sorrow the untimely passing of our former esteemed member, Dr. Gilbert Winn, brother of our esteemed member Dr. Stephen Winn. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Martin Scheman, President
        WINN-Dr. Gilbert. We will miss you, dear friend. Your ever present smile, courage and wit will be in our hearts forever. Our deepest sympathy to the best wife a man could wish for, Elly, and the family. Love, Ruth and Fred Greenwald
