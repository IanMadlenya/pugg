
        SCHOLL--Hannah Holzman, died May 1, 2007, at Cabrini Hospice. Born in the Bronx, New York. Beloved wife of Harold M. Scholl, devoted mother of Eric and Roger. B.A., Hunter College 1948, M.A. 1949, Ph.D. 1959, Teachers College, Columbia University, in Speech Science and Correction. Professor of Speech Pathology and Linguistics at East Stroudsburg State College, East Stroudsburg, PA, (retired) Co-Author, Children With Conduct Disorders, Basic Books, 1991, Speech and Language Faults of Schizophrenic Children, 1972. Member, Association for Psychoanalytic Medicine, American Speech-LanguageHearing Foundation. Memorial service Friday, May 4, 12:15 pm at Plaza Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st Street. Donations if desired, to CARE Program at Lenox Hill Neighborhood House, 331 East 70th St., New York, NY 10021. Hannah went to sleep and never woke up. She will be missed dearly.
