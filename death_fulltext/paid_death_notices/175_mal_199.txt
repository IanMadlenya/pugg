
        BERNHARDT-Mortimer, 81, of West Bloomfield, MI, died August 16, 2001. He was a jewelry dealer for 50 years. He is survived by his wife Jacqueline Bernhardt, sons Ric (Gail) Bernhardt, Greg Bernhardt, and granddaughter Justine Bernhardt. Graveside services today 10:30 AM at Beth Kehillah Cemetery in Egg Harbor, NJ.
