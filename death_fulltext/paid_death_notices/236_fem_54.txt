
        MILLER--Betty Ann, of Poughkeepsie, NY, passed away peacefully in her home on Friday, September 1, 2006. She was born in New London, CT to William and Phyllis Richmond. She married Mr. Mark Miller on April 19, 1975 in her parents' home in Mystic, CT. She is survived by her husband Mark, her son Timothy, and her sisters Phyllis and Barbara.
