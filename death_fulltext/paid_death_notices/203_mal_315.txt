
        DeLUCA--Nella. Passed away peacefully on December 28, two months short of her 91st birthday, after a courageous battle with cancer. Nella was married to Matthew I. DeLuca, Sr, for 57 years. He passed away November 1993. They had a wonderful life together and leave their daughter Adele and son Matt, Jr. and his wife, Marguerita, grandchildren Vincent, Gina, Robin, Jay, Matt III, and great grandchildren Dakota and Matthew. The family will honor their mother's request for mass at St. Joseph's R.C. Church in Garden City on Tuesday, December 30 at 10AM, followed by interment at St. Charles Cemetery (there will be no wake). In lieu of flowers, kindly make a donation to Good Shepherd Hospice or to an orphanage of your choice.
