
        SILVERSTEIN-Rabbi Ralph. Friends and family announce with great sorrow the passing of this distinguished clergyman and community leader on February 11 at the age of 86. Ordained in 1940, he served as Rabbi at The Ninth Street Temple, The Madison Jewish Center and Temple Sinai, all in Brooklyn. Beloved brother of Milton and Jerome Silverstein, Deborah Freedman, Yvette Greisman and the late Ruth Brooks. A graveside service will take place Sunday, February 16, at 2:30 PM, the Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Long Island.
