
        GREENE-Sylvia. Beloved mother of Madeline & David and the late Lynne. Devoted wife of the late Casey. Adoring Grandma Sylvia to Jackie and Casey Max. Loving sister, aunt and friend. Services on Sunday, November 15, 9:30 AM at Riverside Funeral Chapel, Mount Vernon, New York. Interment at Mount Ararat Cemetery in Farmingdale.         GREENE-Sylvia. The Board of Overseers of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with sorrow the passing of Sylvia Greene, beloved mother of Madeline & David J. Klein, who are dear friends of our institution, respected members of our Society of Founders and Guardians of our University. A past chairman of the Einstein Young Men's Division, Mr. Klein serves as a member of its executive committee. To Maddie and David and the entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura, Dean Stephen R. Karafiol, Chairman
        GREENE-Sylvia. The officers and employees of International Blind Contractors, Ltd express their deepest condolences to Madeline Klein on the untimely passing of her beloved mother. May she be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
