
        WHITE-Robert W. The Musicians, Trustees and Staff of the Marlboro Music School and Festival join in celebrating the life of Dr. White, and extend their affection and sympathy to his son, David, and other family members. A devoted Marlboro Board member, Chairman of the Board (1970-73) and Trustee Emeritus, Dr. White made an inestimable contribution during formative years of the School and was a valued friend to the entire Marlboro community.
