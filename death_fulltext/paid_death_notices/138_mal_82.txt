
        FEHR-Robert O., Ph.D. on July 7, in Greenwich, CT. Born 1911, Berlin, Germany. Beloved husband of Edith. Father of David, Grandfather of Daniel and Adam. Graduate of E.T.H., Zurich Mechanical Engineering. Engineer at General Electric, Schenectady, NY. Professor of Mechanical Engineering, Cornell University, Editor of the Journal of Audio Engineering Society. Member of A.S.M.E., I.E.E.E., Audio Engineering Society, Acoustical Society. Interment Private. Contributions in his memory may be made to American Committee for the Weizman Institute, 51 Madison Ave, NY, NY 10010 or Purchase College Foundation, 735 Anderson Hill Road, Purchase, New York 10577-1400.
