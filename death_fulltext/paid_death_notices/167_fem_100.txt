
        REGLER-Margaret Paul. Of NYC and Tepoztlan, Mexico, age 96, at home in Manhattan. Widow of Gustav Regler, noted German author and veteran of the Spanish Civil War. A founder in 1933 of the Miquon School, Miquon, PA, and co-author in 1997 of the primary source textbook ''Uneasy Neighbors-Cuba and the United States.'' Companion of the late German archaeologist Alexander von Wuthennau of Mexico. Daughter of Henry and Paul and Margaret Crosby Butler Paul of Philadelphia. Survived by her children Paul Irwin of Watsonville, CA, Sydney Baiman of Chicago, and Ann Bourgois, NYC, eight grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren.
