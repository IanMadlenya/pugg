
        MATTIA--Louis. Known from 1960 to 1995 for his antique light fixtures at his Second Ave. store, Mr. Mattia died peacefully at age 87 in his Manhattan apartment on Sunday, March 7. Friends may call at Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Avenue, Thursday, March 11, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Memorial service on Saturday, March 20, 11 AM, St. Ann's Church, 25 Eastern Avenue, Ossining, New York, interment St. Augustine's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations can be sent to cancer, heart or kidney foundations. Mr. Mattia leaves a wife and two daughters.
