
        HEILSHORN-John W. ''Jack.'' Of Litchfield, CT, age 75, died January 18, 2001, from complications of diabetes. He was an Executive Vice Presient with Travelers Corporation of Hartford, CT, from 1985 to 1988, when he retired from a 39 year career in the financial services industry. Prior to that, Mr. Heilshorn was an Executive Vice President at Citibank N.A., NY, NY, from 1960 to 1985. From 1949 to 1960, Mr. Heilshorn served as Chairman of Corporate Strategy and Development as well as Group Head of Investment Management and Domestic Consumer Groups with the New York Trust Company. Among his many charitable pursuits he was Chairman of the Salvation Army's Greater New York Advisory Board from 1981 to 1985, as well as serving on the Executive Committee of the Private Sector Survey on the Cost Control (Grace Commission) from 1982 to 1985. Mr. Heilshorn was also a Chairman of the Forman School Board of Trustees in Litchfield, CT. Born December 28, 1926, in Floral Park, Queens, Heilshorn attended New York University Graduate School of Business Administration from 1950 to 1955. He also attended Rutgers University from 1954 to 1955. Mr. Heilshorn graduated from the United States Merchant Marine Academy with a bachelor of science degree in 1947 and served in the U.S. Navy from 1947 through 1949. He is survived by Helen, his wife of 48 years. Daughters Janet Lynne Cohen of Cherry Hill, NJ, Carol Lee Ebersol of Cornwall, CT, and sons John William Jr., of New York, NY, and the Reverend Mark Lang of Woodbury, CT, ten grandchildren and a sister Muriel Kilbirde of New Canaan, CT. Memorial service will be held at the Litchfield Congregational Church in Litchfield, CT, on January 22, 2001, at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Islet Gene Fund, attention: Gordon Weir, Joslin Clinic, Boston, Massachusetts 02215.
