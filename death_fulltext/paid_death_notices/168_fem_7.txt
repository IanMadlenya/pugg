
        CHAMPION-Eleanor Stevens. Of Greenwich, CT. on December 30, 2000 at home surrounded by her family. Wife of the late George Champion, former Chairman of the Board of Chase Manhattan Bank. Survived by her son George Champion Jr, daughter Emily Champion Knobloch, 7 grandchildren and four great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at Second Cong. Church, 139 East Putnam Avenue, Greenwich, CT. on Wednesday, January 3, 2001 at 11:00 AM. Interment at Spring Grove Cemetery in Darien, CT.
