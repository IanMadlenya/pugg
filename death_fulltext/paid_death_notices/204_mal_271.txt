
        HAUSEN--Rod, 52, accomplished and beloved pianist, composer, arranger, musical director, and teacher died on January 18, from cancer. He is survived by his parents Roy and Ruth Hausen and his loving spouse, Susan. A wake will be held from 3 PM to 9 PM on Wednesday, January 21st, at Flynn and Son Funeral Home, 23 Ford Ave., Fords, NJ. (732) 826-0358. A Mass will be held January 22nd, 11 AM at Trinity Church, 33 Mercer Street, Princeton, NJ.
