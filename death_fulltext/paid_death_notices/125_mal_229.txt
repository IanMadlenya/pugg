
        AWN-John J., Jr. Died suddenly on June 21, 1997 at 41. His untimely passing is mourned by his fiancee Joann Sweeney, his brother Christopher and his sister Linda. Dear son of the late John J., Sr. and Louise (nee Alouf). He was a graduate of Xaverian High School and Fordham University Summa Cum Laude 1978. He was a mortgage-backed securities broker with Chapdelaine Corp. Securities. We are all richer in having known John. He will be remembered by his family and friends for his high-spirited intelligence, his sense of humor and the extraordinary manner in which he shared his life with all those he touched. A true New Yorker, who loved the Yankees, the Dead, Cohibas, and politics. He will always be remembered. Visiting on Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 4th Avenue, Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial on Wednesday at 10 AM, St. Anselms Church. In lieu of flowers, you may remember John through NARSAD, National Alliance for Research on Schizophrenia and Depression, Great Neck, New York, 516-829-0091 or AMI/FAMI, Friends and Advocates of the Mentally Ill at 432 Park Avenue South, New York.
