
        ROSENKRANTZ-Essye. Oct. 8, 1997 in her 92nd year, Charlotte, NC. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Maurice C. Rosenkrantz (formerly of Scarsdale, NY and Hollywood, FL). Devoted mother of Edmund and Diane Rosenkrantz and Judith and Milton Tager. Adored and adoring grandmother of Beth Benamy, Bonnie Draluck, Deborah Buchman and Elizabeth Rosenkrantz. Precious great-grandmother of Mark and Ross Draluck, Brittney and Madison Buchman, and Joseph and Sari Benamy. Proud Life Member of Hadassah. Graveside services and interment on Oct. 13, 1997 at 11 AM at Temple Beth-El Cemetery, Davie, FL.
