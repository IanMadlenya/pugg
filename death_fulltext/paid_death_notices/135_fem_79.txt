
        GOLDSTEIN-Bernice R. 82, of Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Kingston, PA, died on April 11, 1998 in Washington, DC. Beloved wife of the late Eugene H. Goldstein. Loving mother of Sallie and Edwin H. Stern, III of New York and Barbara and Richard Kline of Chevy Chase, MD. Proud grandmother of Rebecca and Nicholas Stern and Gillian and Matthew Kline. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Herbert Irving Comprehensive Cancer Center at Columbia University, attention Ann Mitchell, 100 Haven Ave. Ste. 29D, NYC 10032. Memorial service to be held at Washington Hebrew Congregation, Washington, DC, Sunday April 19, 1 PM.
