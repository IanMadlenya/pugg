
        FOWLER-Robert G., formerly of Lattingtown and New York City, died of heart failure on May 20, 1997 in Jacksonville, FL. A 1941 graduate of Cornell University, Mr. Fowler spent his career in the apparel industry in New York. Mr. Fowler was awarded the Silver Star for heroism in World War II. Mr. Fowler is survived by his wife, Frances S. Fowler, of Quechee, Vermont, & Jacksonville, Florida, his sister, his four children and eight grandchildren.
