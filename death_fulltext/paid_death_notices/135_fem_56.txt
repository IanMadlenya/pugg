
        COSTELLO-Genevieve. Of Head of the Harbor, N.Y. on April 9, 1998 in her 81st year. Beloved wife of Dr. Timothy W. Costello. Devoted mother of Peter, Joseph, John, Jeannie Costello, Mary Kay Theoharidies, and Barbara Lyons. Dear sister of Peggy Curley. Cherished grandmother of fourteen. Reposing Moloney's Hauppauge Funeral Home, 840 Wheeler Road (Rte. 111), Hauppauge, NY, where a religious service will be held on Saturday afternoon. Funeral Mass 11 A.M., Monday at St. Philip & James R.C. Church, St. James NY. Interment following in Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury NY. Visiting Friday and Saturday 2-4 & 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers the family request donations be made to the Smithtown Parkinson's Center.
