
        MOSS-Edith. (nee Tolins). On Tuesday, December 28, 1999. Born April 9, 1904. Beloved wife of the late David Moss. Loving mother of Laura Rosen and devoted grandmother of Wayne and Peter Rosen. Last survivor of a family of six girls and one boy. An outstanding elementary school teacher for 40 years, she was also a remarkable fashion designer and a tireless worker for charities before and after retirement, including Hadassah, Roosevelt Hospital and the Guild for the Blind. Edith was a warm, energetic, outgoing person, cherished by her many friends and relatives. Graveside service Thursday, December 30th, 11AM in Beth Moses Cemetery, Pine Lawn, New York.
