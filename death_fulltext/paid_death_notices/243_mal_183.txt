
        GRANT--William R., 82, Venture capitalist, investment banker, and philanthropist passed away Sunday, April 15, 2007, following a year's illness from metastatic melanoma. He is survived by his beloved wife, Adele Grant, and is predeceased by his beloved wife of 43 years, Dorothy Annetta Grant, a son, Gregory Scott Grant, and a daughter, Melissa Grant Davis. Mr. Grant is survived by his daughters, Deborah S. Grant and Elise Grant Seeley Lauinger and his son, Byron A. Grant, and two grandchildren, Jeremy Deschapelles Seeley and Molly Hall Seeley. A Mass of Resurrection will be celebrated at 3pm on Thursday, April 19th, at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, 869 Lexington Avenue, New York City. Burial services will be private. In lieu of flowers, his family requests that donations be made to the Gregorian University Foundation, 106 West 56th Street, New York, NY 10019.
