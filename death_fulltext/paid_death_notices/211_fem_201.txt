
        DROSSMAN--Anne, 88, of Brooklyn, NY, on August 29th, 2004. Beloved daughter of Lena and Joseph Rosenberg and sister of Abe (deceased) and Murray. Adored wife of Edward (deceased). Everyone she touched became a part of her family. Services 10 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 180 West 76th Street.
