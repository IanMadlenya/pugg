
        SELBST--Ronnie. (nee Appel), 52. Of West Orange, New Jersey, native of Brooklyn. Graduate of MIT and Rutgers Law School with highest honors from both. Funeral services November 13, 10:30 AM at Bnai Keshet, 99 South Fullerton Ave., Montclair, NJ. Interment at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI. Beloved mother and friend. Survived by children Andrew and Vanessa, parents Sylvia and Hy Appel, sister Sheila and brother Ken.
        $(6$)SELBST--Ronnie. The board and staff of the Reconstructionist Rabbinical College note with deep sorrow the passing of Ronnie Selbst, a valued board member of the College since 1996. Ronnie, of West Orange, NJ, died suddenly on November 11. Our hearts and prayers are with her two children, Andrew and Vanessa, and the entire family. Ronnie received a J.D. from Rutgers Law School in 2005 and graduated Phi Beta Kappa with a B.S. from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1973. She was serving as a judicial clerk for the Essex County, NJ, Criminal Courts at the time of her death. Before entering law school, she worked 11 years as an options trader on the floor of the American Stock Exchange. She also worked as an actuary, specializing in employee benefits and pension consulting. In addition to her devoted service to the College, she also provided significant leadership to Bnai Keshet, her synagogue in Montclair, NJ. The funeral is scheduled Sunday at 10:30 A.M. at Bnai Keshet, 99 S. Fullerton Ave. May her soul be bound up in the bond of Life.
