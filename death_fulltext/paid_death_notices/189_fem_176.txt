
        LYTTLE-Ada, beloved sister of Joan Birman, Helen Kimmel and the late Ruth Satter; sister-in-law of Joseph L. Birman, Robert Satter and Martin Kimmel; aunt and greataunt died at age 71 on October 23, 2002. She retired to a life of quiet dignity after a long career as a schoolteacher in New York City. Service Friday 9:45AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. & Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in her memory to the Museum of American Folk Art, NY.
