
        LANGLEY-Stephen G. On June 7, 1997. Of Brooklyn Heights and Saugerties, NY. Age 58. Professor and Director of Performing Arts Management Program at Brooklyn College. Graduate of Emerson College and University of Illinois. Author of standard textbook on arts management, ''Theatre Management and Production in America,'' and other books. Formerly General Manager of Falmouth Cape Cod Playhouse. Beloved by his many students. A memorial service, June 19, 1997 at 4:30PM at Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims, Orange St. between Henry and Hicks Streets, Brooklyn Heights. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Stephen Langley Scholarship Fund at Brooklyn College.
