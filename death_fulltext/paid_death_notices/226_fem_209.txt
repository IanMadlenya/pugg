
        WEINGART--Vivien. Age 59 years, on November 28, 2005 of leukemia. Preceded in death by her husband Laurence. Survived by her son Michael, her sister Gloria Merker, as well as aunts, nieces, nephews, cousins and in-laws. She loves her family, her friends, her cats & all the beauties of nature. She went forth in peace & joy. May her memory be for a blessing.
