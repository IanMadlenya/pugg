
        DUBROFF-Charlotte Weisman. One week short of 103. Cherished wife of the late Dr. Nathan Dubroff. Dear mother of Florence Shelley. Grandmother of Carolyn Shelley LeBel and William Shelley. Great-grandmother of Natasha and Melissa LeBel. A beacon to us all. To celebrate her life, a ceremony of remembrance at the Shelley home in New Rochelle, January 1, from 2 to 5.
