
        KIRKPATRICK--Nancy G. 86, of East Windsor, N.J. died June 5, 2003 at Meadow Lakes. She had been a resident of Princeton, N.J. for over 50 years before moving to Meadow Lakes in 1996. Born in Maplewood, N.J., she was a graduate of Columbia High School and Chatham Hall. She was a member of Trinity Church in Princeton. She was active in the Garden Club of Princeton, the Princeton Hospital, the Retinitis Pigmentosa Foundation and Planned Parenthood. She was predeceased by her husband Roger, her eldest son Roger died in 1982. She is survived by a sister Barbara Donaldson of Newport News, Va., her other three sons Sandy of Bradenton, Fl., Peter of Ashland, OR, and Stuart of Muttontown, N.Y., as well as seven grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at Meadow Lakes in East Windsor, N.J. on Monday, June 9th. Memorial contributions may be made to Princeton Hospital, Princeton, N.J. 08540. >>AD#
