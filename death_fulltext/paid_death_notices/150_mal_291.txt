
        COHEN-Eugene J., M.D. Died at home on July 16, 1999 at the age of 87. Devoted husband of the late Regina Gluck Cohen, M.D., for 58 years. Loving brother of Lorraine Silberthau. Brother-in-law of Sylvia & Irwin Grossman & Dorothy Margolin. Dear uncle of many nieces & nephews. Services Sunday, 1:00 PM at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers memorial gifts to the Regina Gluck, M.D. Memorial Fund, North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset, New York 11030.
        COHEN-Eugene, M.D. It is with great sorrow that we note the passing of Eugene Cohen, MD, a respected and much-loved endocrinologist and philanthropist. Dr. Cohen was the embodiment of excellence in medicine. Our deepest sympathies go to his family. Mervin Silverberg, M.D. Chairman/Dept. of Pediatrics North Shore University Hospital at Manhasset Rubin Cooper, M.D. Chief/Div of Pediatric Cardiology, North Shore University Hospital at Manhasset
