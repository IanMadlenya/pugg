
        PARR-Alfred Andrew, 99. Died peacefully at his home in Cairo, NY on Wednesday, January 9th, 2002. Born in Richmond Hill, Queens, he left school at 15 to work in the New York offices of US Steel. He then became a steelworker in the mill at Pittsburgh. Returning to New York, he began working as a bookkeeper, eventually becoming resident manager at The Dakota in the 1950s. Everyone who met him was charmed by his good nature and personal kindness.
