
        MASCIANA-Teresa. On Oct 4, 1997. Born in Reggio, Calabria on April 15, 1924. Beloved wife of the late Bruno. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave (at 20 St.), Tues, Oct 7th, 2-5, 7-9 p.m. Mass of Christian Burial 10 a.m. Wed at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment Green-Wood Cemetery. Donations can be made in her name to the American Cancer Society, 19 W. 56 St. NYC 10019; attn: Memorial Dept.
