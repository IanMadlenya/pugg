
        CHERRY-James R. The Board of Directors and Staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and the Israel Museum, Jerusalem, are deeply saddened by the death of their friend Jim Cherry. As a Trustee of the Billy Rose Foundation, Jim Cherry helped foster the growth of the Israel Museum for three decades. He will be sorely missed. Our condolences to his family and to his fellow trustees. Teddy Kollek, President, I.M. Judy Steinhardt, Pres., A.F.I.M.
        CHERRY-James R. We note with sorrow the death of James Cherry. Mr. Cherry served as counsel to our firm for the past 15 years. He was an extraordinary lawyer and a true humanitarian. We are grateful to have had him as our colleague. Vedder, Price, Kaufman & Kammholz
