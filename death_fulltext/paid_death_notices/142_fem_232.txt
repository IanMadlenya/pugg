
        BERNSTEIN-Harriet (nee Hoberman). Died November 28, 1998, peacefully, of Manhattan, West Palm Beach and the Berkshires. Will be dearly missed by those who loved and knew her. Beloved wife of the late Bernard. Devoted mother of Myra and Michael Tweedy and Fran and Robert Krivit. Adored grandmother of Shana and Jeremy Tweedy, Illyana and Sam Krivit, Joshua, Robert and Melissa Bernstein. Loving sister of Kenneth and Tammy Hoberman. Her wisdom, guidance and loving presence will be sorely missed. Services today, November 29, 9:30 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
