
        WIRECKI-Marek, M.D. Died Mar. 31, 2002. He was a survivor of the Holocaust, a physician, a scholar, a writer and a man lovingly dedicated to his wife of 57 years, Janka Wirecki. He is survived by his wife, his daughter Barbara, his son Theodore, four grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. He will be remembered for his strength, bravery, heroism, commitment to medicine and his complete devotion to his family.
