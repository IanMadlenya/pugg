
        LEAHY-Thomas F. Of Bronxville on March 8, 2002. Beloved husband of Patricia, loving father of Patricia Ann, Allison, Thomas Jr., Kirsten, and Caitlin, grandfather of Gaige, Pierson, Tate, and Thomas III. Thomas was the former President of CBS Television Network and most recently an independent producer of film and television. Calling hours at Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM, Monday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, on Tuesday, March 12, at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The American Melanoma Foundation, 3914 Murphy Canyon Rd., Suite A132, San Diego, CA 92123.
        LEAHY-Thomas F. The Board of Trustees and staff of Theatre Development Fund mourn the passing of our admired colleague. While serving as TDF's President from 1992 through 1997, Tom's dedication and ability helped TDF grow and flourish in many important areas. We are deeply grateful, not only for the vast experiencee he brought to TDF, but for his generosity, kindness, sense of humor and style. Our deepest condolences to his wife, Patty, and their children. He will be sorely missed by us all. John F. Breglio, Chairman David D. Holbrook, Chairman Emeritus
