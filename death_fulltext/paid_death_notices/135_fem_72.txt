
        KIRSH-Ethel. Beloved mother of Judith Sara Kirsh. Caring aunt of Sonya Cooperman, Sylvia Schatzberg and William Coleman. Strongly identified with her Hebrew heritage, she supported many organizations and held office in her local chapter of Hadassah. The last of a dying breed, she will be sorely missed. Interment was at B'nai Jacob Cemetery, New Haven, Ct.
