
        LEVIN--Donald, 70, died October 3rd in Woodland Hills, CA of pancreatic cancer. He is survived by his loving wife, Donna, children, Allison and Richard Brightman and Philip Levin, and grandchildren, Michael, Erica and Mia Brightman. His formidable presence will be sorely missed. To honor him, donations may be made to The Help Group, 13130 Burbank Blvd., Sherman Oaks, CA 91401.
