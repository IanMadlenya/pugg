
        WEISBERG--David Charles, passed away in Los Angeles on August 31, 2003. Beloved husband of Katherine; father of Chloe and Victor; son of Alan and the late Marian Weisberg. VP of the architecture firm Wimberly Allison Tong & Goo. To our deep sadness, David's remarkable courage, determination and humor could not defy his battle with leukemia. Donations may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society
