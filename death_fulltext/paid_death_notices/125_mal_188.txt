
        GELBARD-Bruce. Age 45, of Annadale, Staten Island, a retired corporate secretary and a Board of Education official, died Saturday, Staten Island Hospital. Surviving are his wife, the former Lila Schaefer and daugher, Debra Reinish, his parents David and Jean Gelbard, a brother Dr. Mark Gelbard and a sister Trudy Markowitz. The funeral is today with a service at noon in Menorah Chapels, New Springville, Staten Island, 2145 Richmond Avenue. Rabbi Norman Strizower will officiate. Interment Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey.
