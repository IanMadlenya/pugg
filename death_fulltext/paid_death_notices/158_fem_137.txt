
        SIEGEL-Pearl. On March 15, 2000. Beloved wife of Rabbi Reuven Siegel. Loving mother of Judy (Gil) and Arnon. Adoring grandmother of Alexandra, Tori and Sydney. Dear sister of Silvia Horowitz. Service Thursday 1:15PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue
        SIEGEL-Penny. The Officers, Board of Trustess and membership of The East 55th Street Conversative Synagogue mourn the loss of Penny Siegel, wife of our beloved Rabbi Reuven Siegel. She was a dedicated and devoted worker for our synagogue, for the Jewish Theological Seminary and Conservative Judaism. She was truly a ''woman of valor''.
