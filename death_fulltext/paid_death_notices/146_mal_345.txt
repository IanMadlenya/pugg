
        FEINBLATT-Allen S., age 68. Died on March 16th, 1999, after a long illness. A lifelong resident of New York City, he was a graduate of New York University and worked as a stockbroker. Allen was a scholar with a deep appreciation of history, music and art. He is survived by several cousins and close friends. Service Thursday, 10:30AM, at ''Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
