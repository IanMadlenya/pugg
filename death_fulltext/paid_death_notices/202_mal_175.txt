
        GILLMAN--William G., 88 of Eastchester, died November 18, 2003. Survived by his wife Marcelle; two daughters Alecia Gillman and Eileen Choron; son-in-law Greg Choron; grandchildren Rachel, Deborah and Rebecca Choron. Retired executive of Faber, Coe & Gregg, and AnCorp National Services. Graduate of Fordham University and Xavier High School, he proudly served in WWII as Lt. Colonel. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday, November 20 at 10:30AM, Immaculate Conception Church, Tuckahoe.
