
        SILVER-Hon. Simon. Retired NYC Criminal Court Judge, passed away July 23, 1998 at age 92. A graduate of NYU Law School, he also served the City with the Corporation Counsel. Simon was always lovingly available to family and friends with sage advice. Judge Silver leaves his beloved wife Ethel, whom he married in 1932. Also surviving are his devoted sons Arthur and Roger, dear sisters Miriam Avidon and Ann Gorin, and loving grandchildren Valerie, Diane and Larry. Funeral service will take place July 26th, 11:30AM at the Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 2nd Ave, Manhattan.
