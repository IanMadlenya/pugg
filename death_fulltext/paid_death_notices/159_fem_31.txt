
        BYNE-Frances (nee Cohen). On April 4, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Paul. Devoted mother of Robert (Marti) Byne and Judith (Norm) Maxwell. Cherished grandmother of Helen, David and Joel. Loving sister of Gerti Dalinka, Maley Cohen and the late Anna Thorne, Jack, Samuel and Louis Cohen. Fanny was committed to her family and charity work, and died tragically on her way to her volunteer work at Putnam Community Hospital. Graveside services Thursday, 11:30AM, Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, N.Y.
