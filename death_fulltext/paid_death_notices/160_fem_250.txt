
        SHEA-Anna F. Of Somers, New York, formerly of Jackson Heights and Rockaway Beach died on May 30, quietly at her home. She was 93. Beloved wife of the late William V. Loving mother of William A. Devoted grandmother of Paul, Christopher, Gregory and Jeanne Shea and Clare Gasparri & great grandmother of Gregory, Elena, Alexia, Gabrielle, Bobbie, Grant, Lindsay and Stephanie. Reposing at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, New York, on Thursday from 4-8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Josephs, R.C. Church, Croton Falls, New York, Friday 10 AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
