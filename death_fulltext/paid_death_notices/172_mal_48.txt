
        ST. GEORGE-Andrew. Journalist and photographer, died May 2, 2001. Born Andras R. Szentgyorgyi in Budapest Hungary, he was 77. His pictures chronicling the Cuban Revolution, many published in Life Magazine, won the Overseas Press Club and Sigma Delta Chi awards in 1958. Beloved husband of Jean St. George (nee Hoopes), loving father to Andrew H. and Tom Szentgyorgyi.
