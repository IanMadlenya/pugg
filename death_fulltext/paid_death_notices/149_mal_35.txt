
        WIENER-Harold H. Passed away Sunday evening, May 30, 1999, at his home in West Palm Beach, FL. He is survived by his wife, Eva, and one son, Martin, a daughter, Judie, who predeceased him, also survived by seven grandchildren, and one great grandchild. Services were held at The Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel in West Palm Beach. Interment at Beth Yeshurun Cemetery in Houston, TX.
