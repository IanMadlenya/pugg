
        WHITE-Michael Silva. Of Summit, N.J., and Edgartown, MA., October 29, 1997. Husband of Molly (nee Edmonson) White and father of Cameron S., Jameson F. and Sean M. A memorial service will be held Saturday, November 1, 1997, 1PM, the Calvary Episcopal Church in Summit, New Jersey. Interment Martha's Vineyard, MA. In lieu of flowers, please donate in his name to Hamilton College, Clinton, New York 13323 or the National Cancer Institute.
