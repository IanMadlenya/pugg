
        KASHUK-Doris. Left this world Sunday, August 31, 1997. She was comfortable at home in the loving care of her family. Although Doris rarely spoke of her illness, she corageously battled cancer for more than a year. Beloved mother and best friend of Barbara; devoted mother or Richard and mother-inlaw of Nuna; cherished grandmother of Reesa. She has earned a peacefully rest with her adoring husband Jerry and loving mother Mollie. Services, 1:15 PM, Tuesday, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Memorial contributions to Guiding Eyes for the Blind, Yorktown Hts, NY 10598.
