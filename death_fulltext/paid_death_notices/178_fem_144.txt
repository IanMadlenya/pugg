
        PEARCEY-Violet Hall. On September 26, 2001, of New York City, passed away at the age of 89. She is survived by her sister, Rose Hall of New York; cousin, Cameron (Maggie) Moseley & niece, Peggy of Connecticut; stepdaughters, Joan (Eric) Cooke of Edmonton, Alberta; Lynn (George) Allen of Mossyrock, WA, and Gail (Roger) August of Winnipeg, Manitoba; seven grandchildren, nine greatgrandchildren and close friend, Nell (Rod) Kempton and family.
