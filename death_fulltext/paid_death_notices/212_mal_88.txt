
        CUMMINGS--Victor, M.D. On September 6, 2004 after a long battle with unremitting disease. Graduated NYU College of Medicine, served in Korea earning a Bronze Star. He organized the Department of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation at the Medical College of Ohio and retired as Chairman in 1992. His kindness, dedication and sense of humor will be missed. Condolences to Jacqueline, Nina and David. E.S. and Eileen Siker Dan Schube Ira and Mary Barash Pauline Alexander
