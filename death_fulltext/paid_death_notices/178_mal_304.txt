
        CLEMENT-Alphonse V. Jr., M.D. Died Nov. 19, 2001 surrounded by his family following a lengthy illness. Born to Dr. Alphonse V. Sr. and Constance Cannella Clement on April 25, 1928. Adored husband of Maryjane. Beloved father of Ardith Colonna (Sal), Jeanine Robotti (Bruno), Debra Clement, Gina Fedeli, Anne Walsh Talberth (Jamie) and Thomas Walsh Jr. (Nereida). Devoted Poppy to Maya, Nicholas, Guy, Jennifer, Nicole, Alisha and Daniel. Dr. Clement graduated University of Notre Dame (1949), attended medical school in Bologna, Italy and graduated Temple University School of Medicine, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania (1956). During his thirty years of service to the city of New Rochelle, he was a general practitioner at Sound Shore Medical Center, formerly New Rochelle Hospital Medical Center, from 1963-1993. In addition, he served as City Physician for the Police Department and Board of Education from 1973-1993, and was an active member of the New Rochelle Rotary Club, serving as their president from 1986-1987. He retired to Florida in 1993, but recently returned to New Rochelle.
