
        TARASOW-Marc Simon, Dr. Died on May 30, 2002. Born March 14, 1966 in Buffalo to Helene (nee Toub) Tarasow and the late Rabbi Morris Tarasow. A graduate of SUNY Binghamton & Downstate Medical School. He worked in a variety of Emergency Medical Care Clinics. A funny man who was full of life, Dr. Tarasow lived his life to the fullest for the past 7 years before succumbing to brain cancer. He is survived by his mother Helene, loving partner, Henry Grullon, sisters Deborah Salomon and Lisa Langwell, nieces Micole, Gabi, Madison and Erin & his dog, Sammy. A funeral service will be held on Sunday 11AM at the pavillion at Dolphin Cove in Stamford, CT. Burial will be at New Montefiore Cemetery in Farmingdale. For more information, Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 E. Boston Post Rd, Mamaroneck, www.zionmemorialchapel.com phone 914-381-1809.
