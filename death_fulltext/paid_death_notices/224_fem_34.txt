
        LEVITAN -- Geordie. The Board of Directors of 140 East 28th Street Owners Corp. expresses its sympathy to the family of longtime shareholder, Board member and Board president, Geordie Levitan. Geordie's intel ligence, leadership and compassion were an inspiration to all who knew her. Her contributions to 140 E. 28th Street and its community are too numerous to list. She will be missed at 140 E. 28th St. for so many reasons, most importantly for her friendship. Board of Directors 140 East 28th Street Owners Corporation
