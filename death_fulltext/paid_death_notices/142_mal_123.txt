
        LANDIS-Leonard R. Age 75. On November 10, 1998. Beloved husband of Elayne. Loving and proud father of Carolyn, Dean and his wife Mara, Andrew and his wife Hilary. Devoted grandfather of Teddy. Dear brother of Donald. A resident of both Manhattan and Sagaponack, he was a leader in commercial finance, serving as the long standing President of United Credit Corporation. Friends may call Wednesday, 3-5 and 79PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Services Thursday, November 12th, at 12 Noon at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street.
        LANDIS-Leonard. It is with great sorrow that the Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Leonard Landis, a prominent financier and a cherished friend and contributor. Our deepest sympathies go to: his loving wife, Elayne; his children Andrew, Carolyn and Dean; his brother, Donald and his wife, Betsey, both dedicated leaders and longtime supporters of UJA-Federation; and to the entire family. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
