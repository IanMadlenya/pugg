
        JUTKOWITZ--Bernice E. Of Rye, NY. Died March 27, 2005, at age 85. Born June 3, 1919, in Manhattan to Samuel and Ethel (Rosenblum) Epstein. Survived by her husband J. David Jutkowitz, a daughter Linda Jutkowitz Meyers, grandchildren Karen Meyers Arrese (Gregory) and Hope Meyers Bond (Mogolodi), great grandchildren Julia, Isabella and Grace, and a sister Evelyn Hyman. Predeceased by a son, John G. Jutkowitz, and a brother, Herbert Epstein. Funeral service Tuesday, 10 am, at Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, NY (914-381-1809) followed by an interment at Mount Hope Cemetery. Donations to the Lou Gehrig ALS Center of Columbia University, NY, NY 10032 are suggested.
