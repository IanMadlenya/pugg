
        SCHAPIRO-David. A Holocaust survivor, born in Wilno, Russia, February 18, 1893. Beloved father of Nina Holzman, father-in-law of Sy Holzman. Grandfather to Luba Mayo and Larry Holzman. Great grandfather to Jason, Ronni and Michael Mayo. He died peacefully at his home December 31, 1998. Funeral on Sunday, January 3, 11AM at I.J. Morris Hempstead. He was a very special individual and will be greatly missed.
