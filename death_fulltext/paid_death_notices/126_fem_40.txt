
        ALEXANDER-Leah. Mrs. Leah Alexander of Stuyvesant Town died July 1. Mrs. Alexander was an employee of the Metropolitan Life Insurance Company and retired in 1974. She was devoted to and loved by family and friends. Mrs. Alexander is survived by her sister, Harriet Rosenzweig of Tamarac FL., eight nieces and nephews, six grandnieces and nephews and two great-grandnephews.
