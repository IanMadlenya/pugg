
        STEWARD-Charles Jackson II, born in Augusta, GA on March 29, 1920, passed away in Las Vegas, NV on January 16, 2000. The beloved husband of Rose, he is survived by four of his six children, Harris, Robert, Janet, Michael and his loving brother, Williams Dean. His life was marked by devotion to his country, his family and his friends. He enriched the lives of all those he touched.
