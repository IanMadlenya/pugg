
        HORAK-Theodore J. On June 27 at home in Sta. Barbara de Nexe, Algarve, Portugal, at age 71, of pancreatic cancer. Survived by his partner Nora Ann Smyth, sister-inlaw Jeanne Horak and her seven children, nieces Rosalie Cook, Barbara Roessle and Lillian Perrone, nephew John Harms, and many cousins. Funeral and burial in Sta. Barbara de Nexe on June 30. Condolences to Apartado 101, 8001-501 Sta. Barbara de Nexe, Algarve, Portugal.
