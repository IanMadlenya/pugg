
        FITZGERALD -- Celia. Died peacefully surrounded by family and friends on Tuesday, August 10, 2004 after a long and heroic battle with cancer. Celia was born in Cork, Ireland on November 22, 1941. She immigrated to New York in October, 1969. Celia was instrumental in the development and expansion of a Manhattan-based consulting engineering firm that focuses primarily on the buildings of federal, state and city govermental agencies in New York City. Celia is survived by her companion Albert P. Kung of Forest Hills, N.Y., daughter Louise and son-in-law Douglas Hepworth and a grandson Dashiell of New York City; son Jerry and daughter-inlaw Bernadette Scanlon and grandchildren Emer, John, Stephanie and Kate of Dublin, Ireland. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street, Friday 7-9 PM with a service on Saturday at 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell.
