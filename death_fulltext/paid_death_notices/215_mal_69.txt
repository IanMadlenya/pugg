
        BECK--Franklin B. After a long illness. Beloved husband of Shirley. Devoted father of Lois Garfinkel and Clifford Beck and father-in-law of Lester. Cherished grandfather of Auston and Dyllan Beck and Jilyenne Garfinkel. Graveside services Monday, 1:30 pm, at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson.
        BECK--Franklin. The Board of Governors and Members of Glen Oaks Club express our deepest sorrow at the passing of our esteemed, longtime member of 64 years, Franklin, and extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Shirley and his family. Board of Governors Alan Goldberg, President
