
        GRAY-Andrew, writer, historian, and Richard Wagner scholar, died in his Washington home on August 30th. He was 69. Mr. Gray was married to Marlis Carmon of Hamburg, Germany in 1964. The union ended in divorce in 1982. He is survived by his sister, Patricia Critchlow of Newburyport, MA; his daughters, Corinna Fisk of Boston, MA, and Ellena Gray of Bradenton, FL; and his son, Piatt Gray of Birmingham, AL.
