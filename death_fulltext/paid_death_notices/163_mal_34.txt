
        BRAGG-Everett C., M.D. Passed away at his home on August 2, 2000, at the age of 86 years. Everett was the devoted husband for 59 years of Mary Bragg (nee Holt). Loving father of Everett E. (Sherrill) of Lima, Peru, James B. (Deborah) of Darien, CT. and Malley (Thomas) Heinlein of Sawkill, NY. Proud grandfather of 6 grandchildren and 1 great-grandchild. Dear brother of Oliver B. Bragg of Sterling, VA. Everett was a graduate of Cornell University and Cornell Medical School. He was a professor and orthopedic surgeon at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center in New York City. He was also the head of orthopedic surgery at St. Francis Hospital in New York. In 1979, Everett became an Orthopedic Surgeon Consultant at Milwaukee Industrial Clinics which later became the Occupation Medical Clinics. Contact Feerick Funeral Home, 414-962-8383.
