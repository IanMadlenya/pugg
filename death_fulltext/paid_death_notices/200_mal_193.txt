
        MASSEY--John Clifford, was born on June 16, 2003, in New York, NY. He died in his sleep on September 14, 2003, in Houston, TX. John Clifford was the light of our lives. He was only with us on this earth for a short time, but his precious little smile will live in our hearts forever. No one has ever been more loved. John Clifford was preceded in death by his great grandparents: Mary Beth and Ray Allen Walker; Clifford Walter Ligon; and Marie Noonan and Eugene Aloysius Massey. John Clifford is the beloved child of parents Kendall and Tom Massey of New York, NY. He is also survived by his great grandparents Joy Barringer Ligon and Katherine and John Brennan Mowry; his grandparents Robert and Linda Ligon of Houston, TX and Mary Ellen and Eugene Aloysius Massey, Jr., of Washington, DC; his aunts Lacey Ligon Thomas and her husband, James Thomas; Elizabeth Whitney Ligon; and Elizabeth Massey Hopkins and her husband, Ryan McGill Hopkins; his uncles, Robert Walker Ligon and Michael Patrick Massey and his cousin Walker Michael Thomas. Services will be held in New York on Saturday, September 20, at 11am at the Church of St. Ignatius Loyola, 980 Park Ave at 84 St. Interment services private. A reception for family and friends will be held later that afternoon. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions be made to the Central Park Conservancy, 14 E. 60 St, NY, NY 10022 and designated for the John Clifford Massey Memorial Fund. A bench at the Central Park Children's Zoo will be dedicated in John Clifford's memory.
