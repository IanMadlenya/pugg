        TYLER--Harold R., Jr. Legal Services for Children notes with sorrow the passing of the Hon. Harold R. Tyler, Jr., an esteemed member of its Board of Directors. Judge Tyler will be remembered for his dedication to the highest standards of legal practice and his abiding concern for disadvantaged children. Legal Services for Children will miss him greatly. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the family. Warren J. Sinsheimer, Pres.
