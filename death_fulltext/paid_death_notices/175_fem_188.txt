
        AGREE-Dr. Rose H. (8/25/13-3/24/ 01), Wife of the late Morris Agree, mother of Peter and Joel. A passionate partisan in the cause of social justice and on behalf of the arts; a dedicated school librarian, & teacher. Close friends and dear relatives mark her 88th birthday in sorrow & reflection. Contributions in her name to the American Newspaper Repository, PO Box 643, Rollinsford, N.H. 03869.
