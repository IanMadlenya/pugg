
        SCHWARTZ-Jean. The Partners, Associates and staff of the law firm of O'Donnell, Schwartz, Glanstein & Rosen are saddened by the passing of Jean Schwartz, wife of our Founding Partner, Asher Schwartz. Jean's charm, intellect, perceptiveness and grace will always be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathies to Asher and his children and grandchildren.
