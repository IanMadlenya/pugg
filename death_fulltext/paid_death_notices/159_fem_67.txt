
        RHODES-Margaret Duryee. Wife of the late A. Whitney Rhodes. Died peacefully at home in her 96th year on April 4, 2000. A lifelong resident of Englewood, N.J., she was the loving mother of Barbara R. Zinke of Briarcliff Manor, N.Y. and Douglas B. Rhodes of Providence, R.I., and beloved grandmother of eight and great grandmother of thirteen. Interment will be private.
