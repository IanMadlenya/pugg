
        COHEN--Robert L. May 23, 1928-March 24, 2004. Our beloved Bob died suddenly in Bayside, N.Y. He is survived by his loving daughter Aimee Cohen and her partner Beverly Greeb of Martinez, CA., son Steven Cohen, cherished sisters, Beverly (Ray) Howell and Rosemary Green of Los Angeles, and Bob's much loved companion, Sandy Apfel of Bayside, NY. He also leaves adoring nieces, nephews, and cousins who loved spending time with him. Bob was preceded in death by his wife Phyllis in 1992 and his second wife, Eileen in 1998. A devoted family man, we will treasure his memory and miss his quick wit, handsome appearance and love of life. A celebration of his life will be held at noon on May 16th at the Pickwick Gardens in Burbank, CA. Donations in his name may be made to the Children's Center at Temple B'nai Hayim, 4302 Van Nuys Blvd., Sherman Oaks, CA. 91403 or to ARMDI, 888 7th Avenue, NY., NY 10106.
