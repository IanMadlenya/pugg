
        FARB-Gene. Visionary entrepreneur, president Whole Earth Access, passed peacefully at home June 13, 2001, of colon cancer. Gene was 55, born and raised in Brooklyn: BA, City College, NY and MA GWU. Gene created the the New Earth Exposition pioneering solar energy and alternative lifestyles, and co-founded Whole Earth Access, the eminent ''yuppie'' emporium which grew to an eight-store $180 million Bay Area chain, one of the first retailers of personal computers. Two sons Noah & Bryan, wife Toni, parents Jack and Estelle, brothers Larry and Fred, his sister-in-law Laura, cousins Ellen and Claudia will carry on his love of tennis, sports, business and life.
