
        JACOBSON--Sheila. National Council of Jewish Women, Peninsula Section, records with profound sorrow the death of our past president and advisory board member, Sheila Jacobson. Our heartfelt condolences to her family. Joan Riegel and Raela Bloom Co-Presidents
        JACOBSON--Sheila Krell, beloved of Sheldon, mother of Craig (Jessica), Robert (Elisabeth) and Steven (Rachel). Grandmother of John, Samara, Gianna and Kevin. Past president of NCJW Peninsula Section, recipient of the Hannah G. Solomon award in 1986, and the honoree at the annual dinner dance in 1987. A devoted wife and mother who believed strongly in the bonds of family, who provided a warm and loving home for her extended family. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to NCJW, 53 West 23rd Street, New York, NY 10010, 212-645-4048 or The Komen Foundation, PO Box 650309, Dallas, TX 75265-0309.
