
        SALINGER-Frances G. (Crystal). Age 88, of Palm Beach, Florida. Passed away on February 23, 2000. Mrs. Salinger, formerly of New York, was active in many community, religious and charitable organizations. She was the second woman in the country elected President of a Jewish synagogue as well as serving as the United Nations Representative of the National Council of Jewish Women. She was a woman of uncommon strength, character, dignity and intelligence and admired by all whose lives she touched. Survivors include loving children Richard (Marilyn Crystal), Andrew Crystal and Libby (Robert Rappaport). Beloved sister of Thomas, Marge and Richard Grumbacher and Ann Lowengard. Adoring grandmother of Deborah, Daniel, Bryan and Emily Crystal; Paul Rosenberg and Alex; David and Toni Reamer. Great grandmother to Courtney, Jacob and Samuel Reamer. Aunt Fran to countless nieces and nephews. Funeral services will be held Friday, February 25th, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, New York, N.Y. Family will receive friends Saturday night from 6PM to 9PM at the home of Richard Crystal, and Sunday from 2PM to 7PM at the home of Andrew Crystal. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the 92nd Street YMYWHA.
