
        GREENBLATT -- Gedalje (Sam), 87, died in Chesterfield, MO, on December 26, 2004, following a brief battle with cancer. Mr. Greenblatt was born in Poland and was a Holocaust Survivor. He and his deceased wife Sara had lived for many years in Bayside, NY, where he was known to his neighbors as being very handy and always willing to help.
