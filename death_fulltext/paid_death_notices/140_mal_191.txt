
        HOLZER-Erich. The Officers and members of the Boards of Governors of The Jewish Home and Rehabilitation Center & The Jewish Home at Rockleigh note with profound sorrow the passing of our fellow Board Member, dear friend and benefactor, Erich Holzer. An extraordinary man & gentle soul, we will miss him. We extend our deepest sympathies to his beloved wife Eva, daughter Vivian and son Robert, and their families. May his name be for a blessing. Carl Epstein, President Charles P. Berkowitz, Executive Vice-President
