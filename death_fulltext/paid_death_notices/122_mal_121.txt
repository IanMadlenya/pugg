
        HARENBERG-Dorothy. The officers, directors, administration and staff of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center extend their heartfelt sympathy to Assemblyman Paul E. Harenberg and his family on the passing of his mother Dorothy, a resident of the Gurwin Center. Assemblyman Harenberg has been a longtime supporter of Gurwin and actively involved since inception. His mother will be missed by all. Joseph Gurwin, President Alfred W. Levy, Chairman of the Board Herbert H. Friedman, Executive Vice President
