
        PAPERT--Diane Keedwell. Beloved wife, devoted mother and grandmother, died in her New York City home on July 13th. The cause of her death was a metastatic carcinoid. She was 83. She is survived by her husband of 53 years, Fred Papert, two daughters, Emily Lloyd Papert of San Francisco and Lisha Papert Lercari of New York City, and her granddaughter, Vida Miriam Lercari. $(6$)PAPERT--Diane K. The board and staff of Bryant Park Corporation mourn the passing of Diane K. Papert, wife of one of our founders, Frederic S. Papert, and extend our deepest sympathy to all of her family and friends. Michael Fuchs, Chairman Daniel A. Biederman Executive Director
