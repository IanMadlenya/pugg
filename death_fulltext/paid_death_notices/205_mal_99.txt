
        FRANCIS--Martin. Died on February 7, 2004, at the age of 87. He was born and educated in New York City, served with distinction in the United States Army in France and Germany during World War II and spent his business career in the menswear industry. He was the loving husband of Esther for 63 years, devoted father of Linda and Judy, and cherished father-in-law of Niels Kampmann and Arthur Zankel. Services will be held on Sunday, February 8, at 11:45 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St, Manhattan. The family will be receiving visitors between 6 and 8 PM, on Sunday, February 8, at 920 Fifth Avenue, and on Monday and Tuesday in Valley Cottage.
