
        SMITH-Margaret H., ''Peg'', resident of RiverWoods at Exeter, NH died peacefully in her sleep December 8, 2002 at age 90. Peg was born in South Orange, NJ where she and her husband Cornelius H. Smith lived until retiring to Dorset, VT. She is survived by one son, two daughters, five grandchildren and one great grandchild. A memorial Service will be held at Riverwoods on December 26, 2002 at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Peg's memory to the Staff Appreciation Fund at RiverWoods at Exeter, 7 RiverWoods Drive, Exeter, NH 03833.
