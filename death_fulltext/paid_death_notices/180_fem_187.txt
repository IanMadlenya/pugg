
        LIEBER-Winifred M. Age 82, passed away on January 17, 2002. Beloved daughter of the late Joseph & Marie Lieber. Cherished sister of the late Priscilla Lieber Kaufman. Survived by loving and loved sister Sylvia Lieber Kass and numerous nephews & nieces. Ms. Lieber was the medical librarian at Roosevelt Hospital from 1962 until her retirement in 1984. She was a volunteer at the NYC Department for the Aging until the present. A memorial will be held at a later date.
