
        WEINTRAUB--Morris ''Tiny''. Died peacefully in his sleep on October 25. Beloved husband for more than 60 years of Selma. Devoted father to Allen and Carol, Connee, Floyd and Joanne, Amy and El. Adored grandfather to Jennifer and Tom, Rebecca, Alexandra, Neal and Joelle, Heather and Jeff, Scott and Jennifer, Abigail, Annie, Todd, Guy and Michale, and Ron. Loving great-grandfather to Nicole, Courtney, Benjamin and Jacob. Dear brother to Pauline. Shiva at the Weintraub family home: 120 East 87 St., NYC. Contributions in Tiny's memory may be made to Union College in Schenectady, NY; the Jewish Braille Institute in NYC; or the Hebrew Home for the Aged, Riverdale, NY.
        WEINTRAUB--Morris ''Tiny''. JBI International (formerly the Jewish Braille Institute) is deeply saddened by the passing of our longtime Trustee, Morris ''Tiny'' Weintraub. Our heartfelt condolences go to his wife Selma and his wonderful family. Tiny's contributions to the welfare of the Jewish blind and visually impaired, and to the broader Jewish communtiy are legion. We also remember that he was a hero in World War II. We extend the deepest sympathy to his entire family, certain in the knowledge that his memory will serve as a blessing. Barbara B. Friedman, Pres. Dr. Jane Evans, Honorary President Dr. Ellen Isler, Executive Vice President
