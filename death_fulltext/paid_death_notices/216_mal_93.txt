
        GLYNN--John R. on January 1, 2005 in Redding, CT, after a lengthy struggle with Alzheimer's disease. Longtime resident of Manhasset, NY. Beloved husband of 61 years (Janet), father (John III, Jim), father-in-law (Carol, Elise), grandfather (John, Janet, Cary) and dear friend to all who knew him. Born in West Haven, CT, graduate of Trinity College and Columbia University (MBA). During WW II, served with US Army in Guam, rising to rank of Major. With Sperry Corp. for many years, retiring as a Corporate Vice President of Human Resources. Founding partner and President of The National Mortgage News, a winner of the Peabody Award for journalism.
