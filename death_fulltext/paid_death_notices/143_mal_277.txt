
        FEELEY-Brig. Gen. James Augustine, Jr. (U.S.M.C. Ret.), December 6, 1998 at Laguna Niguel, CA, of cancer, age 82. Husband of the late Inger Holm Culbertson Feeley; father of Sarah Feeley Johnson; stepfather of the late James Willis Culberston Feeley and of Constance Culberston Feeley Reed. His second wife, Carol Lee Call Feeley, survives.
