The Cooper Union's Trustees, Faculty, staff, students and the Cooper Union Alumni Association mourn the passing of mechanical engineering alumnus Jack Gould who continuously served his Alma Mater since graduating in 1927. Past president and tireless member of the alumni association, a generous donor and dear friend, he will be missed. We offer our deepest sympathy to his wife Esther and their family. Ronald W.
