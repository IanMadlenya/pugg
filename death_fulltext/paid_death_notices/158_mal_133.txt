
        KOSSOWSKY-Warren, M.D. Dr. Warren Kossowsky, 65, died peacefully in his home on March 10. Devoted husband of Susan, loving father of Alisa Strauss and Andrew Kossowsky, father-in-law of Jay Strauss and Cori Burros, grandfather of Sophie and Jessica Strauss, brother of Bob Kossowsky, brother-in-law of Nancy Kossowsky and Richard and Alison Dundy. A dedicated, caring doctor, loyal friend, athlete and scholar, Warren's depth of kindness will live in our hearts forever.
        KOSSOWSKY-Warren, M.D. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center record with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Warren Kossowsky, Member and Professor of the Division of Cardiology for 35 years. Dr. Kossowsky was Chief of Coronary Care since its inception in 1968. Our sincere condolences to his wife Susan, daughter Alyssa and her family, and son Andrew. He will be sorely missed. Edwin L. Schulman, Chairman Board of Trustees David I. Schachne, Chairman Executive Committee Alvin I. Kahn, M.D. President and CEO Richard J. Fogler, M.D. Chief Medical Officer
