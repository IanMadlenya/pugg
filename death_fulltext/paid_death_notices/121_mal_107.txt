
        WILCOX-Herbert B., Jr., M.D. February 5, 1997. Husband of Elizabeth, father of Herbert B. Wilcox, III, Gordon W. Wilcox, Thomas E. Wilcox. Born in 1908, Dr. Wilcox graduated from St. Paul's School, Concord, NH, Yale College and Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. He served in World War II with the US Army's Second General Hospital in Europe. On returning to the US he practiced medicine in Manhattan and Riverdale, NY and was an a Associate Professor of Medicine at Columbia University's College of Physicians and Surgeons. In addition to his wife and sons, he is survived by three daughters-in-law Catherine, Virginia and Whitney and five grandchildren: Alex, Tanya, Kate, Christopher and Virginia. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Redding Ridge, CT at 1:30 PM, Saturday, February 8th. In lieu of flowers contributions may be given to the College of Physicians & Surgeons, Columbia University or the Memory Disorders Unit, Massachusetts General Hospital, Boston, MA. 02114.
