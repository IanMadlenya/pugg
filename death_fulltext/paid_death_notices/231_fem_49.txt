
        GOLD--Leona. Died April 6, 2006 at age 91. Loving wife of the late Isadore, devoted mother to Daniel and the late Robert, and beloved sister of Harold Cotton. Cherished grandmother of David, Michael and Abigail. Respected and longtime employee and board member of The Henry Street Settlement. She loved New York City and took advantage of all it had to offer. Graveside services Monday, 11 am, at Wellwood Cemetery in Pinelawn, NY.
