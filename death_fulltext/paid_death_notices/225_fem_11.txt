
        HEYEL--Isabel Oldham, on September 24, 2005, in Vero Beach, FL, at the age of 84. A Westchester County native, she moved to Los Angeles, CA, in 1971 where, until retirement in 1997, she had a very successful career in the field of Decorator Fabrics. Isabel is remembered with love by four sisters; children Wendy Vance, Lucinda Gedeon, and Stuart Heyel; four grandchildren; two great grandchildren and 23 nieces and nephews. An ardent Democrat, and supporter of environmental causes and human rights, in lieu of flowers contributions may be made to habitat.org; secondharvest.org; or the Democratic National Committee. A celebration of her life will be held at Rye Presbyterian Church, 882 Boston Post Road, Rye, N.Y. on Saturday, October 15 at 1:30PM
