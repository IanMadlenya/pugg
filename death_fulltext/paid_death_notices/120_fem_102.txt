
        TURETZKY-Ann C. Died peacefully on January 16, 1997 at Mount Sinai Hospital, New York City. Born in Poland to Bertha and Nathan Turetzky, she emigrated with her family to New York City where she attended various art schools and pursued a life-long interest in painting and jewelry design. She was the sole proprietor of Constance Jewelers, a jewelry boutique in Manhattan which she operated for many years. Survivors include her two sisters: Sonia Kivel and Ethel Luft of Palm Springs, CA., her nephews Joseph and Lawrence Kivel and niece Barbara Turoff, several grand nieces and nephews and a great grand nephew. Interment Palm Springs, CA.
