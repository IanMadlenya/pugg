
        SACK-Harold. The Board and Members of The National Antique & Art Dealers Association of America, Inc., mourns the death of our long-time and valued member, Harold Sack, an outstanding leader in the field of American furniture. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his partners, his brothers, Albert and Robert, and the family. Andrew H. Chait, Pres. Arlie Sulka, Sec'y
