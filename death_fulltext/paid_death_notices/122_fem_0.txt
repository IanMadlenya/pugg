
        BRIGGS-Gertrude, 84, on Feb. 27, 1997. Survived by daughters, Susan Briggs Myers and Aprille Briggs; three granddaughters, Sarah Rodriguez, and Molly and Emma Myers. Widow of Harold Briggs; they owned Books N' Things on East Tenth and East Seventh Streets for 50 years. She had many friends in the arts. Memorial service to be held Mar. 16.
