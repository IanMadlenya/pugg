
        CASTELLI-Leo. The Board of Trustees and Staff of The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation deeply mourn the loss of Leo Castelli. No other art dealer has had such a profound effect in shaping the direction of contemporary art. He was a consummate gentleman known for his warmth, eloquence, and generosity, for which he will be long remembered. We extend our most heartfelt sympathy to the members of his family. Peter B. Lewis, Chairman Thomas Krens, Director The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation
        CASTELLI-Leo. The Trustees and staff of The Museum of Modern Art, are deeply saddened by the death of long-time friend and Honorary Trustee, Leo Castelli. His commitment to an extraordinary group of contemporary artists, fostered through his gallery, is legendary and secured his unique place as an art dealer of great vision, taste, and humanity. We will long remember his warmth and generosity, and we offer our profound sympathy to his family. Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, NY
