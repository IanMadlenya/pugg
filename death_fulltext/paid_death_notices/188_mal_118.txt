
        ABER-Samuel of New Rochelle, died September 11, 2002. He was 90. Born August 12, 1912 in the Bronx to Charles and Fannie (nee Schwartz) Aber. He married Mildred Eigenmacht on November 26, 1939. In addition to his wife he is survived by his children Glenn, Charles and Arlyne McClancy, ten grandchildren and nine great grandchildren. Predeceased by siblings Abe Aber and Gertrude Wisser. Shiva services are Friday and Sunday 11 AM to 5:30 PM at the family home, 1 Stratton Road, Purchase, NY 10577. For information Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809.
