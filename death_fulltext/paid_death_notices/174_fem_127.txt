
        BAUM-Lila, 73. Of Lake Worth, Florida, passed away on July 12, 2001. Mrs. Baum was born in Brooklyn, New York. She will be remembered as brilliant, funny, caring & the best wife and mother. Mrs. Baum is survived by her son Jonathan Baum of Lake Worth, Florida. She was predeceased by her husband of 50 years Jess Baum in August of 2000. The Funeral services will be private and are entrusted to Dorsey Funeral Home, 3525 S. Congress Ave, Lake Worth, Florida 33461.
