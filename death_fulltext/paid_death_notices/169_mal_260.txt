
        JOHNSON-Helmer. Died in Washington, D.C, February 23, 2001. Age 88. He was the husband of the late Ramona Louise Grattan, father of three sons, Grattan, William and Craig, and grandfather of Lindsey Elizabeth. He was Attorney at the Justice Department from 1939 to 1946. He later practiced in New York at the firm Willkie Farr Gallagher and Fitzgibbon until retirement in 1978. He will be missed. Private memorial plans.
        JOHNSON-Helmer Robert. The Partners of Willkie Farr & Gallagher extend their deep sympathies to his family. He died February 23, a resident of Georgetown, D.C. since 1982. He was a Senior Law Partner of our Firm for 33 years until his retirement in 1979.
