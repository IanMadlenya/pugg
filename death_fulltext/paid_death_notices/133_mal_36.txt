
        KRAMER-Philip. The Officers of Amerada Hess Corporation express their deep sorrow at the loss of their esteemed colleague and close friend, Philip Kramer. Mr. Kramer's leadership as President of Amerada Hess from 1972 to 1986, and his advice and guidance as a Director of Amerada Hess from 1970 to 1993 were instrumental in the growth of the Corporation. His contributions have been of great value and will be missed. We express our deepest sympathy to his wife Sarah, his daughters Marilyn and Noelle and his son Glenn.
