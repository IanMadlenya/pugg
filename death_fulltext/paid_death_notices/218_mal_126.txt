
        HOLLANDER--Leo L. Of Boca Raton, Florida, died peacefully on March 9, 2005 at the age of 68. Survived by his wife of 45 years, Cynthia Hollander. Dear father, grandfather and brother. Mr. Hollander was Chairman and CEO of Hollander Home Fashions in Boca Raton, FL. His vision, integrity and passion for his family and friends will be sorely missed but imbedded in those who loved and cared for him. Donations may be made in his memory to Congregation B'nai Israel Strategic Planning Fund, 2200 Yamato Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33431.
        HOLLANDER--Leo. The officers and staff of Rosenthal & Rosenthal, Inc. and the entire Rosenthal family mourn the loss of their longstanding client and friend. During his 40-year relationship with Rosenthal, Leo established personal friendships with many of the firm's senior officers and his daily communications regarding worldly events will be deeply missed. We all came to know Leo, not only as a leader in the home furnishings industry, but also as a philanthropist and a loving husband, father and grandfather. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Hollander family.
