
        ROSSI-Urico M. Age 85. Born in Waterbury, CT. Died Thursday, June 14, 2001 in Winter Park, FL. Mr. Rossi was a Professor of Music at Indiana University from 1948-82. He was first violinist of the Berkshire String Quartet, in residence at Indiana University and Music Mountain in Connecticut. He attended Juilliard and Eastman Schools of Music, and Yale University. He is survived by his wife Hazel, daughters Paula, Lisa, Gina and Carla, and his sister Mrs. John C. (Melba) Coolidge in MA.
