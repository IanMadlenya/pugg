
        COOK -- Richard A. The Trustees and staff of the Seamen's Church Institute of New York & New Jersey, together with George M. Isdale, Jr., Chairman of the Board of Trustees, and the Rev. Jean R. Smith, D.D., Executive Director, express their deepest sorrow at the passing of Richard A. Cook. Since 1998, Mr. Cook served on the Institute's Board of Trustees with distinction and was devoted to our ministry to mariners. We send our condolences to his family.
