
        CHOPE--Robert Lincoln II. Died suddenly on February 12 at his home in Marietta, GA. He is survived by his wife of 56 years Martheen Baker Chope and his four children; Christine Peters of Richland, WA, Nancy Lezinsky of Marietta, GA, Robert Chope III of Toms River, NJ and Pamela Mozier of Derby, CT, four grandchildren, and his sister Patricia Bauman of Louisville, KY. Private services were held.
