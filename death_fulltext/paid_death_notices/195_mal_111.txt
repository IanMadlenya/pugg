
        LIEHR-Joachim Georg, 60, died April 8, 2003. Dr. Liehr is survived by his loving wife, Kitty, and his beloved sons, Christopher and Patrick. Dr. Liehr dedicated the last 30 years of his life to battling cancer and his pioneering research resulted in treatments that ended the suffering of many. In lieu of flowers the family suggests a donation to the Stehlin Foundation for Cancer Research.
