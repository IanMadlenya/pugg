
        MARTIN--Gladys ''Aunt Gladys.'' Formerly of Miami Beach, FL, and was currently residing in Roslyn Harbor, NY, passed away peacefully at the age of 97 on July 21, 2003. All donations in her memory may be made to Martin/Lehrer Foundation, 1800 Northern Boulevard, Suite 101, Roslyn, New York 11576.
