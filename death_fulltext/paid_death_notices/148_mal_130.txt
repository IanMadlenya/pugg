
        REILLY-Rev. John H. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of St. AnthonySt. Alphonsus Church, 862 Manhattan Avenue, Brooklyn, on May 12. Reposing at Leahy-McDonald Funeral Home, 111-02 Atlantic Avenue, Richmond Hill, Thursday, May 13, from 2 to 5 and 6 to 10 P.M. and on Friday, May 14, from 9 A.M. to 6 P.M. Funeral Mass Saturday, May 15, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
