
        MOSES-Barbara. On Saturday, June 28, 1997. Formerly of Hillsdale, New Jersey, after a long illness. Dear wife of the late Seymour Moses. Devoted mother of Deborah Visser and Paul Moses. Loving mother-in-law to Barbara Moses and William Visser. Proud grandmother of Joanna, Timothy and Evan. Loving sister of Marjorie Lee. Expressions of sympathy in her memory may be made to the Alzheimers Association.
