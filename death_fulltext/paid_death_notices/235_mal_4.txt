
        HADLEY--Richard Parker, 90, of Maplewood, NJ and Ocean View, DE, died on July 29th. He graduated from Oberlin College and Columbia University law School. A former senior partner of Wall Street's Reynolds, Richards law firm, he specialized in Trusts and Estates. He is survived by his loving wife of 61 years, Thelma, children Carol, Richard, Jr. and John, five grandchildren, and one great grandson. A Memorial Service will be held at Morrow Memorial Methodist Church, Maplewood, NJ on Thursday August 3rd at 10:30 AM. For info Jacob A. Holle Funeral Home, Maplewood, NJ.
