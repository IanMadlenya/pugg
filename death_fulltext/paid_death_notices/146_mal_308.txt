
        SCHREIBER-Leonard I. Of Westport, CT, on March 13, 1999, at age 84 at home. Lee was a graduate of Columbia College, Columbia Law School, and was active in alumni affairs. He practiced law in New York until recently as Senior Partner of Schreiber and McBride. He served as Director and General Counsel to Atari Corp. and Commodore International. He is survived by his two daughters, Ellen W. McBride and Laurie L. Baker, five grandchildren, Seth McBride, Justine Fellows, Jonah Fernald, Jason Fernald, Mallory Baker, and great-granddaughter, Kallie Fellows. Services Tueday, at 12 noon, at the Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Rd, Fairfield, CT.
