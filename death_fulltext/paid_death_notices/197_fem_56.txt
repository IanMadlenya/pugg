
        STEWART--Elizabeth Jean. 88, died May 28 in Anaheim, CA. Jean was Reverend Emeritus of Christ Church Unity in Anaheim, which she founded in 1962. Among many beloved survivors: nephews, Richard Decius of Napa, CA, Steven Stewart of Baltimore, MD, Robert Boyd of Hilton Head, SC and a niece, Nancy Rearick of South Glastonbury, CT. Services June 10 at 11 a.m. at Christ Church Unity, Anaheim. >>AD#
