
        STONE-Leo, M.D. Died July 29, 1997, in his 93rd year. Beloved husband and best friend of Marta. A loving brother, father, grandfather and great grandfather. He will be greatly missed. He was a kind and gentle spirit who touched us all. His memory will be cherished by family, friends, colleagues and patients forever. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue on Friday, August 1st, at 3:30 P.M.
