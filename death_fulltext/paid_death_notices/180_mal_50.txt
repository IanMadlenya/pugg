
        HARRIS-Wilford Linwood, Jr. Matrid Alleyne Harris mourns the loss of her husband, born April 20, 1932, in Harlem. Son of Kate and Linwood Harris, Sr. He is survived by two sons and four grandchildren. Wilford Jr. was a graduate of New York University, where he received his MPA. We shall miss him dearly and will have him in our hearts for eternity.
