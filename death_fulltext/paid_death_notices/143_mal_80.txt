
        GEIGER-Richard L., 81, (Captain, USN Ret.), died peacefully on November 29, 1998. Burial will be at Arlington National Cemetery, December 10, 3PM. Devoted husband of the late Emmy Lou, father of James and Ellen, an exemplary husband and father. A native New Yorker and graduate of CUNY and NYU, he saw action during WWII at Pearl Harbor. A partner in the venture capital firm Geiger & Fialkov, he helped to found Microsemi, Inc. and served as Chairma of the Board for many years. Their endownment at the Polytechnic University in Brooklyn funds scholarships for deserving engineering students. A memorial service will be held in January. A quiet but loving man, he will be dearly missed.
