
        PAGE-Donald Ormsby, 69, died April 27th, suddenly but peacefully at home. The son of the late Mr. and Mrs. Donald Ormsby Page of New York City and Far Hills, New Jersey. Loving brother of Shirley P. Schneirla of Stanton, New Jersey, and beloved uncle to Dorothy S. Hover, Peter C. Schneirla, of New York City, and the late William Sutfin Schneirla, Jr. He will be sorely missed. Funeral and interment were private at Woodlawn Cemetery, New York City. Services were conducted by his devoted brother-in-law, the Very Reverend William S. Schneirla.
