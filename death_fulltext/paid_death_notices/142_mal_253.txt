
        DULTZ-Melvin William, 71, Owner of Travcoa World Tours and a Chicago native died Tuesday, November, 10 in Seoul, South Korea. Mr. Dultz is survived by his daughter, Olga; sisters, Selma (Arnold) Ekerling and Betty Phillips; and nieces and nephews. A memorial service is planned for 11 a.m. Saturday at Pacific View Memorial Park and Mortuary, 3500 Pacific View Drive, Newport Beach, Ca.
