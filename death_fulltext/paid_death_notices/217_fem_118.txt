
        PANNY--Medea (Strasser), age 82 years, on February 9, 2005 after a long battle with cancer. Only daughter of Anna and John Strasser. Beloved wife of the late Frank Panny. Loving mother of Susan Panny and Linda Goldberg, beloved grandmother of Laura Lattman, Stephen Goldberg and Kyle Goldberg. Dear mother-in-law of Eaton Lattman and Norman Goldberg. May she dance among the stars. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St, Saturday, 7-9 PM and Sunday, 25 and 7-9 PM with service Monday, 11 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to animal sanctuaries or Doctors Without Borders.
