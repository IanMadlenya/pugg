
        PRAGER-Julius, 81. Of Boca Raton, Florida, passed away on March 12, 2002. He is survived by his loving wife, Muriel, of 59-1/2 years; cherished sister, Ruth; beloved son, Mel (Alice) Prager & daughter, Arlene (Les) Weinstock; grandchildren, Gregory, Jeffrey, David, Marc, Amy, Jill. Chapel services were held on March 14, 2002, Star of David Memorial Chapel with entombment following on the grounds of the Star of David Memorial Gardens.
