
        MACCHIA-John J., 84. Died March 9, 1998 in Fort Lauderdale, FL. He was born January 29, 1914 in Bronx, New York. Mr. Macchia attended St. John's College and Fordham Law School. He was admitted to the New York Bar in 1942 and enlisted in the US Army that same year. In 1950 he married Katheryn Satterfield of Galletin, TN, and moved to Chappaqua, NY. They remained married until her death in 1984. Mr. Macchia practiced Law in New York City for over 30 years and was a senior partner with the firm of Townley, Updike, Carter, and Rogers. He retired to Reading, Vermont in 1979. In 1990 he moved to Fort Lauderdale, Florida where he lived until his death. He is survived by a son, John J. Macchia, Jr., Fort Lauderdale, FL, a daughter, Mary K. Macchia, Tequesta, FL, a sister, Marion Raimondi, Hopewell Jct, NY, Nieces and Nephews. He was pre-deceased by three brothers, James, Joseph, and Tony Macchia. Services: Saturday, March 14, 1998, at 11:00 A.M., at Our Lady of the Snows Catholic Church in Woodstock, Vermont. Visiting hours will be Friday evening from 6 to 8 PM at the Cabot Funeral Home in Woodstock, Vermont. Memorial donations to the Ottuaquechee Health Center, 32 Pleasant Str., Woodstock, VT 05091.
