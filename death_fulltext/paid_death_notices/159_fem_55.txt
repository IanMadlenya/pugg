
        FRIESER-Sadie. Age 84. Devoted wife of Martin. Beloved mother of Paul, Barbara, Francine & David. Sister of Shirley and Irving. Adored grandmother and great grandmother of Neil, Barry, Michael, Scott, Jennifer, Daniel, Maia, Gabrielle, Grant, Joshua and Ross. She was co-founder of Beekman Liquors with her deceased husband, Martin. She was an inspiration to all who met her, known for her unconditional acceptance and generous spirit. Funeral services will be held Sunday, April 9, 12:30PM at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 2nd Ave, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made to Jacob Perlow Hospice, c/o Beth Israel Hospital, 1st Ave at 16 St, NY NY 10003 or Dress for Success, 130 W. 30 St, Suite 501, NY, NY 10001.
