
        GROSBERG-Marion. On Feb. 12, 2002 at the age of 91. Beloved wife of the late Harold. Loving mother of Harvey & Julia Grosberg, and Jill and Joel Greenstein. Proud grandmother of Michelle, Alisa, Haley, Rebecca & the late Rachel. She was a devoted, lifelong member of Hadassah and a supporter of the State of Israel. Service on Wednesday, Feb. 13 at 12:15 PM, at ''Hellman-Garlick Memorial Chapel'', 1300 Pleasantville Road, Briarcliff Manor, New York. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hadassah.
