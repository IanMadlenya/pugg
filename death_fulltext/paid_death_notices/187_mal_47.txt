
        SULLIVAN-Robert V. Deceased July 27, 2002, at the age of 90. Resided in Palm Beach, Florida, and Fort Lee, New Jersey. Prominent New York jeweler. Executive Vice President with Van Cleef & Arpels from 1972 until 1984. Survived by Arax, his beloved wife of 65 years, sons Dennis and Shawn, three grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren.
