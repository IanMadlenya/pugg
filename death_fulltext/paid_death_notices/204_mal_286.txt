
        RODEWALD--Clark G. The faculty, administration, staff and alumni/ae of Bard College mourn the passing of long-time faculty member, alumnus and beloved friend, Clark G. Rodewald '59, who, for more than 30 years, instilled in his students a love of learning and a passion for literature. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Carolyn Blatchley, and to his children and grandchildren. David E. Schwab II, Chairman Leon Botstein, President
