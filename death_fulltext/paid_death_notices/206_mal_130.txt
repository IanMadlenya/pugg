
        MINDLIN--Michael Jr. Entertainment industry veteran of New York and Los Angeles, died Sunday, March 7th. Son of Michael and Betty Mindlin, devoted brother of Barbara Lichter and the late Constance Hartman. Loving husband, uncle, and godfather. Served in the Army Air Force in World War II. His gift for friendship leaves a myriad of mourners.
        MINDLIN--Michael, Jr. Best friend and dear husband you will be greatly missed; your caring heart and brilliant wit, among many talents, will live on through all who had the good fortune to know you. Freda
