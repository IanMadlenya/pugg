
        LEVY-Lillian G., 94. Of Bethel, CT. Widow of Bertram R. Levy. Died Saturday, May 8, 1999. Born February 10, 1905. Daughter of the late Anna and the late Maurice Goodman. Graduate of the Columbia School of Journalism, she was a reporter for the Brooklyn Eagle, author of magazines, stories and books. Loving mother, grandmother, great-grandmother and sister. She will forever be remembered. Funeral services on May 10, Temple Israel, 914-478-1343.
