
        DEGENSHEIN-Edward David, 87 years old died peacefully on Jan. 4, 1999. Beloved husband of Pearl. Loving father of John Degenshein and Beth Diskin. Devoted grandfather of Robyn and Jonathan Diskin. Also survived by his sister Pearl Gordon. Graveside service Wednesday 11 A.M., at Montefiore Cemetery, St. Albans, New York.
