
        SMITH-Arthur Rockwell. A resident of Truro, MA, and South Kent, CT, died at home on October 15, 1999. He was the husband of Margaret Walker Smith. He was predeceased by his brothers, George and Hayden Smith. Mr. Smith was born on April 26, 1910, in New York City, the son of Arthur and Alice Smith. He was a graduate of Saint Bernard's School in New York, Saint Paul's School in Concord, NH, and Yale University. He started teaching at South Kent School in Connecticut in 1954 through 1979. In addition to his wife, he is survived by his daughters, Margaret McAvoy, of Kent, Ellen Knapp, of Boulder, CO, and his son, Lawrence, of Exeter, NH, five grandchildren. In his honor, the family requests that gifts be made to South Kent School, 40 Bulls Bridge Rd., South Kent, CT 06785 or to The American Cancer Society.
