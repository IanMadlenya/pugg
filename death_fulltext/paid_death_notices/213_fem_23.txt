
        FEINNE-ROTH--Linda E. 40. Passed away on October 3, 2004. Born in Mount Vernon and raised in New Rochelle, Mrs. Feinne-Roth earned a degree from University of Pennsylvania Wharton School of Business and an MBA from Columbia University. She was a member of Phi Beta Kappa, formerly a Vice President of JP Morgan, head of the International Asset Back Security Division. Survived by her loving husband Jeffrey Roth, devoted parents Martin and Charlotte Feinne, cherished brother David. A chapel service will be held on Monday, at 11 am, at George T. Davis Funeral Home. Interment to follow at Wellwood Cemetery.
