
        GRIMES-Margaret. Of Mamaroneck, on January 8, 2000. She was 72. Born to Michael and Anna (nee Billow) Prehoda in Grandville, NY, on October 6, 1927. She is survived by her husband Charles; a son Christopher; sisters Mary Macura and Anna Prehoda. Predeceased by brothers George, Michael and William. Visiting Monday, 2-4 and 7-9, at Coxe & Graziano, 767 East Boston Post Rd, Mamaroneck. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 11 AM, at Most Holy Trinity followed by interment at Greenwood Union.
