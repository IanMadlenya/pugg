
        CHUDNOW--Avrum. Jewish National Fund family mourns the loss of friend and benefactor, Avrum Chudnow of Milwaukee. Founding member of JNF's World Chairman's Council, Chudnow's vision and love of Israel culminated in the creation of one of Israel's most prominent national parks, Park Timna. His leadership and generosity will continue to inspire generations to come. Ronald S. Lauder, JNF Pres. Russell F. Robinson, JNF Chief Executive Officer
