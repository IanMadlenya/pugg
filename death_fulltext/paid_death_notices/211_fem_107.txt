
        CONDON--Madeleine Montabert, 82, passed away at her home on Martha's Vineyard August 12 after a long illness. Mrs. Condon spent her childhood years in Geneva, Switzerland, and came to this country at age 16, not knowing a word of English. She graduated from Mount Holyoke College in 1943 and remained active in the Mount Holyoke Alumni Association. For many years she was the President of F.G. Montabert and Company, a multinational packaging company, located in New York City and Midland Park, N.J.. She is survived by her husband of 61 years, Carlton J. Condon. and by her children Fred M. Condon (Carlene) of Martha's Vineyard and Jacqueline Condon Johnson (David) of Ridgefield, Conn. She was a loving and vibrant grandmother to Scott, Sean, Christopher, Matthew, Alexis and MacKenzie. A gathering of family and friends to celebrate her life will be held Saturday, August 21 3pm-5pm at her home, 10 Tamarack Lane, (Farm Neck), Oak Bluffs, MA. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Martha's Vineyard Hospital, MVH Development Office, Box 1477, Oak Bluffs, Massachusetts 02557.
