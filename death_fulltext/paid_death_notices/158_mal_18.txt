
        HOWSON-Thomas D. age 81 died peacefully of lung cancer on February 24, 2000. His wife, Anne Fraser, whom he met in England after the war, died previously. He is survived by his sister Joan H. Clarke; one niece Juliet Clarke; two nephews, David Clarke and Benjamin Clarke; four greatnieces and four great-nephews. A memorial service will be held March 12, 2000, 2:00-5:00 PM at 10 Taunton Lake Road, Newtown, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers donations should be made to the Hospice of the Visiting Nurses Association of Northern NJ Inc., 38 Elm St. Morristown, NJ 07960-9743. For information please contact the Rowe-Lanterman Home for Funerals, 973-538-0520.
