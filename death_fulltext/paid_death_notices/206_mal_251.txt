
        BUXTON--William Perrin. 87, died peacefully at home in Fairfield, CT on March 16, 2004. He is survived by his wife of 62 years, Anne Taylor Buxton, his four children, Sally Buxton of Coral Gables, FL, Jeffrey Taylor Buxton and his wife Janelle Pote Buxton of Yardley, PA, Lucy Buxton of Bel Air, MD, and Mary Perrin Buxton of Broomall, PA, and his granddaughter, Katherine Taylor Buxton of Broomall, PA. Former Vice President and Director of Advertising at The New Yorker Magazine until 1980, Mr. Buxton honorably served his country in World War II and the Korean War and was discharged with the rank of Captain from the Army in 1952. Services will be private. The Redgate-Hennessy Funeral Home, 4 Gorham Pl, Trumbull CT 06611. Contributions may be made to the Americares Foundation, 88 Hamilton Ave, Stamford, CT 06902, Connecticut Audubon Society, Pequot Library Assoc. or the Bridgeport Symphony Orchestra.
