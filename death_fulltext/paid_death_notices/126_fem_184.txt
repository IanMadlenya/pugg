
        RIDDLE-Grace. July 23, 1996, of Princeton, NJ. Mother of George & Adele. Grandmother of Margaret, Adele, George. Sister of Harriet Brown. Dear friend of Jennie Marinella. Second only to the love of family, her vibrant and enthusiastic approach to life and love of NYC are sadly missed. Mrs. Riddle was known professionally in the theater as Rita Newton.
