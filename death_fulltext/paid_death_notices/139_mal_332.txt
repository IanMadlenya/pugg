
        WOLFE-Harry G. Loving husband and dear friend to Ann, died suddenly on August 25, 1998. He served in the U.S. Navy in World War II and was cited for heroism during the invasion of Sicily. He received his B.S. and M.A. degrees from Columbia University and taught at W.C. Bryant and L.I.C. high schools. He is survived by his wife Ann, sister-in-law Rita, five brothers William, Francis, Robert, John and Raymond, and twentyfive nieces and nephews. He was a gentle man and a gentleman, and will be deeply missed. Services will be held at Lloyd Home, 214-43 39th Ave., Bayside, today and Sunday 2-4PM and 7-9PM.
