
        SCHLESINGER-Mary Eddy. Of Lakeville, CT, and Martha's Vineyard, MA, died on January 4th. She was 85. She was the wife of the late Dr. Edward B. Schlesinger. Born in Newington, CT, she graduated from the Oxford-Kingswood School and Smith College, Class of 1938. She was a former resident of Dobbs Ferry, NY, and Alpine, NJ, and a long-term member of the New York-Presbyterian Hospital Auxiliary, The Cosmopolitan Club and the Garden Club of Dobbs Ferry. She was a former member of the Junior League of Westchester. A supporter of land preservation efforts, she gave property to conservation trusts in Salisbury, CT, and Sheffield, MA. She was an accomplished violinist and pianist, an avid sportswoman who excelled at golf and skiing, and a lifelong gardener. Her generosity of spirit, sparkling intellect and gracious manner will be greatly missed. She is survived by Jane Schlesinger of Santa Fe, NM, Mary DePasquale of NYC, Ralph Schlesinger of Malvern, PA, Prudence Adler of Washington, D.C., and seven grandchildren. A service to celebrate her life will be held on Saturday, January 26, at 1PM, at the Congregational Church in Salisbury, CT. Donations may be made in her memory to the Salisbury Visiting Nurse Association, Salisbury, CT. 06068.
