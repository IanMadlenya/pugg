
        MEADE--James, WWII veteran, age 83, of Dauphin Island, AL, and summer resident of Deer Isle, ME, died February 26, 2007, of cancer. He grew up in King City, MO, received his art and academic degrees in NYC, and is best remembered as an artist, educator, and leader in the art community in both Alabama and Maine. His wife of 59 years, Barbara Meade of Dauphin Island, AL; a daughter, Penny Mincho of Spicewood, TX; and a brother, William Meade, of St. Joseph, MO, survive him.
