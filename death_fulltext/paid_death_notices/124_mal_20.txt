
        ALFANO-Michael A. On April 30th in Massachusetts. Husband of the late Lorraine (Shields). Beloved friend of Nellie Furtado. Father of Michael of Long Island and Joanie Alfano. Brother of Anthony Polcari. Army Veteran of World War II. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Italian Childrens Home, 1125 Centre Street, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130. For information contact Rogers Funeral Home, 380 Cambridge St., Cambridge, MA 617-876-8964.
