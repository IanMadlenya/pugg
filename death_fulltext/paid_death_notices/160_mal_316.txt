
        SUNSHINE-Howard F. Sarasota, FL, died May 25, 2000. Formerly of NYC. He retired after 35 years as vice president of real estate at Manfacturers Hanover Trust Co. Survivors are his wife, Beatrice; sister, Winifred Tighe of San Diego; several nieces and nephews. Private memorial service. Inurnment Sarasota Memorial Park.
