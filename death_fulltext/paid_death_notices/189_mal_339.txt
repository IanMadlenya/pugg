
        HOXIE-R. Gordon. Long Island University mourns the death of General R. Gordon Hoxie. Dean, President, and Chancellor, Dr. Hoxie played a major role in developing the C.W. Post Campus and the University as a whole. His legacy is the eighth largest private University in America. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Roger Tilles Chair, Board of Trustees David Steinberg President
