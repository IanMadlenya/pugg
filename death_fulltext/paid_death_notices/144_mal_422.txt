
        GREENLEE-Martin, (aka Morton Greenstein). On Monday, January 25 of cancer. He is survived by his mother Bessie, brother Eli, sisters Natalye Brown and Geri Lerner and sister-in-law Rita. He is deeply mourned by his beloved nieces and nephews, Gail Wade, Carol and Nick Epstein, Myra and Peter Cole, Beth Brown and Carol and Hal Lerner as well as six grandnieces and grandnephews. Mort loved acting and he shared his love of NYC with the public as a tour guide with Gray Lines Bus Company for almost fifty years. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends. Funeral today, January 26 at 2PM at Gutterman's in Woodbury, L.I. Donations to Mercy Hospice, 1220 Front Street, Uniondale, Long Island 11553, would be appreciated.
