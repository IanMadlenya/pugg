
        ROSENTHAL-Victoria. On November 18, 2001 at age 83. Her husband, M.L. Rosenthal, a writer & poetry critic, predeceased her in 1996. Survived by her son Alan Rosenthal, daughter Laura McGarry, brother Julius Hemmelstein, grandchildren Daniel Rosenthal, Margaret McGarry, James McGarry and Julia McGarry. She was a social worker who received her B.A. and M.S.W. from the University of Chicago. She was a psychiatric social worker at the Rockland County Community Mental Health Center. Memorial service Sunday, Decembr 9th, 4PM, Riverside Chapel, 76 Street & Amsterdam Ave, New York City.
