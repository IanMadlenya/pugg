
        DOMENITZ--Arnold. March 10, 1915--November 28, 2005. Survived by his wife Rosalind, daughter Ellen Levin & son Andrew. Beloved grandfather and great-grandfather. Loved and adored by all who knew him.
        DOMENITZ--Arnold. The officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of Engineers Country Club mourn the passing of our beloved member, Arnold Domenitz on Monday, November 28, 2005 and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Roz, son Andrew, daughter Ellen and their family. Les Genatt, President
