
        GOGEL--Lettie Wisser, in her 97th year, on January 5, 2004. Lovingly remembered by her children Bruce and Mary Ellen, Bill and Marge, and her grandchildren Brian and Stacy, Nancy and Catherine. She will always be remembered for her love of life and devotion to her family. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions can be made to the George and Gertrude Wisser Memorial Library, NYIT, Northern Blvd., PO Box 370, Old Westbury, NY 11568.
