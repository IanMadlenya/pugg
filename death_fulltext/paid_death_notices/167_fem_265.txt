
        BAUM-Claire C. Well-known resident of Jupiter, FL, passed away Thursday, December 28, 2000 at her residence. She was a resident for eighteen years moving here from Brookville, LI, NY. Mrs. Baum was a valued member of St. Jude Catholic Church, Turtle Creek Country Club and a former member of Meadow Brook Country Club in NY. Mrs. Baum was an accomplished entertainer, singing in three languages at the Stork Club, the Rainbow Room in NY and the Mayflower Hotel in Washington, DC, among others. A close friend of Irving Berlin's, she was the featured singer on Ed Wynn's Texaco Hour on CBS radio. Survivors include her daughter, Joanne Claire (Bonnie) Baum of Long Beach, CA., and nieces and nephews. She was preceded in death by her husband, Seymour Baum and her son, Robert Brian Baum. The family suggests contributions in Mrs. Baum's memory to Hospice of Palm Beach County, Inc, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL. 33407. A memorial mass will be celebrated at 10 AM Wednesday at St. Jude Catholic Church, 214 North US Highway #
