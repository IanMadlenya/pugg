
        HOCHBERG--Marvin. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the Riverdale Y note with sadness the passing of our former executive director, Marvin Hochberg. His dedication to Jewish communal service is an inspiration to the generation which follows him. Our thoughts and prayers are with his wife Bella and children Rifki, Zev and Shani. Miriam Westheimer, President January Akselrad, Chairman Simon Jaffe, Executive Director
