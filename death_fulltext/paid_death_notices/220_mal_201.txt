
        ZALOOM--Edgar A. Passed away peacefully in his 92nd year, Thursday, May 12. Survived by his loving wife Betty, of 63 years, his devoted sons Anthony of San Francisco and Peter of New York, four grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. A longtime resident of Douglas Manor, NY, and John's Island, FL, Edgar served in the Army Air Force during World War II. He was a prominent figure in the nut and fish importing business, an avid sportsman and competitive sailor. His sense of humor and warm, loving personality will be greatly missed. Private services were held at the Columbarium in San Francisco.
