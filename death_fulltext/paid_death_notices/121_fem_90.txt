
        DENNARD-Doris W. Beloved wife of Frederick E. Dennard and cofounder of Harlem Interface Counseling Service, passed away Sunday, February 9, 1997, at 12:15 PM. A celebration of her life will be held on Friday, February 21, 1997, at 6 PM, at the Riverside Church, Main Sanctuary. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Peacemakers Inc., 75-5 West Hartsdale Ave, Hartsdale, New York 10530.
