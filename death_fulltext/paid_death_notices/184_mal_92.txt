
        FENLON-Thomas B. Of Pelham, New York. Died May 4, 2002. Husband of the late Juliet Ludford Fenlon. Father of Mary Ann Knowles, Rev. Thomas B. Fenlon, Juliet Fenlon Nagle, Lois Fenlon and the late Henry L. Fenlon. Brother of Margaret Pratt and Hugh Francis Fenlon. Grandfather of 18 and great-grandfather of 23. Visiting hours at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Avenue, Pelham, NY, on Thursday from 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Catharine's Church, Pelham, on Friday at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to United Way of Westchester, 336 Central Park Avenue, White Plains, New York 10606.
        FENLON-Thomas B. The partners of Emmet, Marvin & Martin LLP note with sorrow the death on May 4, 2002 of their retired partner Thomas B. Fenlon. Emmet will always be indebted to Tom Fenlon for his long and productive career with the firm from 1928-2001. We will miss him.
