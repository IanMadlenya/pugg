
        SHORE--Irene Freeman, age 97, on January 19, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Josselyn Shore for 72 years. Cherished mother of Wini and Mike Freund, and Toni and Freeman Shore. Adored grandmother of Jenny and Mike Pardee, Mia and Ramsey Walker, and Julie and Peter Strauss. Loving greatgrandmother of Ben, Jake, Samantha, Abby, Charlie and Katie. Contributions in her memory to Temple Beth-El.
        SHORE--Irene. We've lost our dear friend. You'll be in our hearts forever. Clara, Gail, Herb, and Marilyn Haft
