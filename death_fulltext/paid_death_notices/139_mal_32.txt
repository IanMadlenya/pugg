
        BRISCOE-Rudel Milton. Passed on Aug. 2, 1998. Son of Alexander & LadieAnne Briscoe, was born in Phila. & raised in Harlem. He was a noted hair designer and artist, who attended Cooper Union & coowned ''Coif Camp'' beauty salon & others. His art has been exhibited in Manhattan & Washington, D.C. Visitation: Wednesday, August 5, 7-9PM, funeral service: Thursday, August 6, 11:30AM both at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St.
