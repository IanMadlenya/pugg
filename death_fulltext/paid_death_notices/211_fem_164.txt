
        ZIEGLER--Harriet. Beloved wife, mother, and grandmother. Harriet passed away on August 21, 2004, at New York Hospital. She had a serious case of esophageal cancer. Harriet was totally dedicated to her family. Her husband Stephen, her two children, David Ziegler and Debra Schwartz, two in-law children, Jacqueline Ziegler and Jeffrey Schwartz, her four adoring grandchildren, Andrew, Jaime, Melissa and Randi. Harriet had a distinguished career as a social worker. She worked for over 25 years for New York State helping the physically and mentally challenged and their families. In 2003 she was named Employee of the Year for her agency and was honored in Albany. Harriet was a tremendous advocate in getting care for her husband when he was seriously ill. Services today, 10 am, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI. Family would prefer donations to the American Cancer Society.
        ZIEGLER -- Harriet. Harriet will be sadly missed by Rosalie and Jack Dobosh. Our thoughts and prayers are with Steve and the family.
