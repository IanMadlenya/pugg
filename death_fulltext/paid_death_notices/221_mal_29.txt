
        McCARTNEY--Charles G. Age 80, and a longtime resident of Pelham Manor, New York, died June 2, 2005. Survived by Joan Maust, his wife of 54 years, father of John, Mary, Drew, Ray and Margie, and grandfather of Charlie, Madeleine, Sam, Lauren and Liam. Veteran of the Battle of the Bulge, awarded Bronze Star and two Purple Hearts. Graduate of Cornell University, coal industry executive, member of New York Coal Trade Association and American Legion. Visiting hours at Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave, Pelham, on Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor, on Tuesday, at 10 A.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Elliot Strong Chair, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. or New Rochelle Humane Society.
