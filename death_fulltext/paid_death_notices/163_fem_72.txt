
        MACE-Virginia (nee Mendes). A long-time resident of Westchester Co, N.Y. passed away July 26, 2000 in Sun City Center, FL. She was born in Saint Louis, MO. December 13, 1917. She was a graduate of Trinity College and served with the Red Cross during WWII. She is survived by daughter Michelle, grandchildren Lauren and Ryan, a sister Mary Ellen and a brother Henry. Services will be held at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Friday, August 11th, 11AM.
