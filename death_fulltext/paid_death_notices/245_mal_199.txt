
        BLOOMBERG--Daniel, 89. Died June 16th, 2007. Loving partner of Betty Sandler, devoted father to Susan (Arthur) Landau, and Neil (Cathy) Bloomberg, beloved Papa Danny to Meredith, Rebecca, Ken, Leigh and Kay. For over 22 years, he lived his dream in Sarasota with Betty by his side. Predeceased by his wife, Frieda. He was heroic to the end and he will live in our hearts forever. Memorial contributions may be made to Tidewell Hospice, 5955 Rand Blvd, Sarasota 34238.
