
        ROCKEFELLER - Mary Clark. April 21st. Beloved mother of Rodman, Ann, Steven, Mary and the late Michael C. Rockefeller, loving grandmother to 15 grandchildren and 23 great grandchildren, she was a tireless supporter of nursing education and the nursing profession. The example of her life and love is an inspiration to all who knew her. Services at Riverside Church, 490 Riverside Drive at 2:00pm Sunday April 25. Memorial contributions may be made to the Mary C. Rockefeller Fund of the Riverside Church.
