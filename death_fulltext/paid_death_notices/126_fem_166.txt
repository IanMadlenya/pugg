
        DAVIS-Elvira J. July 21, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Joseph J. Davis. Loving and adored mother of Mary Ann Davis Goldstone and Richard J. Davis. Survived also by three grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Most giving of her time for many charities for many years, volunteering at hospitals and translating textbooks into Braille. A dignified, kind, and gentle lady to whom her family always came first. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Services will be private.
