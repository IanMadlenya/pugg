
        DOSIK-Helen. Died February 5, 1998, at age eighty-five. Daughter Carol Kaplan, son Michael, grandchildren Noah, Ethan and Daniel Kaplan and Lia and Diana Dosik, son-in-law Mark, daughter-in-law Lyn and the legions of friends and family that adored her mourn her premature death. Predeceased by husband Albert, sister Sophie and brothers Martin and Henry. Bright, accepting and loving, she captivated young and old with her gentle spirit.
