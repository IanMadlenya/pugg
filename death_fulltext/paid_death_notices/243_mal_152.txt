
        BROWN--Jack M., died peacefully at his home at Princeton Windrows, Princeton, NJ on April 10, 2007 after a long illness. He is survived by his wife Dorothy Taylor Brown, his devoted sister Madelyn Brown Harris of New York and nephews Jack Brown Harris of New York and Steven Brown Harris of Washington, DC and their families. He was active in real estate as President of Frederick Brown, Inc. as well as various philanthropic and charitable causes in New York and St. Croix. He was a graduate of Horace Mann, Princeton University class of 1935 and the Harvard Business School class of 1937. He was former Chairman and President of the Jewish Hospital and Home for the Aged in New York City. An avid sailor, he was Commodore of the Storm Trysail Club and a member of the New York Yacht Club. He also belonged to the University and Harmonie Clubs. Interment is private. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to a charity of your choice.
