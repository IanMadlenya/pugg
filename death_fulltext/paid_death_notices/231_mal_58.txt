
        NUTT--Richard Sherman. Of Congdon St., Providence, RI died Saturday, April 1. He was the husband of the late Edith (Woolsey) Nutt. He was an English teacher and librarian at the Moses Brown School in Providence for 32 years. A memorial service will be held at S. Stephen's Church, George Street, Providence on Saturday, April 29 at 11 AM. A complete obituary may be found at projo.com.
