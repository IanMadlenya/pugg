
        WAGNER-Ralph C., 84, a resident of Paradise Valley, AZ, died September 10, 1999. He moved to Arizona in 1995 from Fairfield, CT. He was President of Sales for Encyclopedia Britannica and played professional football for the Chicago Bears. He also loved to coach football and baseball and was a scratch golfer. He is preceded in death by his lovely wife, Louise. Mr. Wagner is survived by two sons, Larry H. and Ralph Michael Wagner, one nephew, Gary Wagner, and four grandchildren. Services will be held in Connecticut on a later date.
