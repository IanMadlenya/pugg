
        HARMAN--Selma C. On December 6th, 2004 at the age of 90, died with courage and dignity. Beloved wife of the late Abbot. Dearly loved and mourned by her daughter Leslie, her husband David Lurie, Steven Simon, son Sandy and daughter-in-law Mady. She loved her grandchildren who were the light of her life. She left them a rich heritage of love and family...Jamie and Richie, Buster, Beth and Matthew.
        HARMAN--Selma. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our beloved, longtime member Selma Harman, widow of the late Abby Harman, and mother of Sandy and Leslie. We will miss her loyalty to our congregation. Our deepest sympathy is extended to all her dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
