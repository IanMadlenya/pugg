
        WEISSER-Max. 88. A well known trial attorney in the state of New York. Died on Sunday, November 22, 1998 at Ormond Beach Memorial Hospital. He was born and raised in New York City and was graduated from St. John's University and Brooklyn Law School. He served in the U.S. Army during WWII. Mr. Weisser was in Daytona Beach for several months visiting with his brother Herman M. (Hy) Weisser, the builder and former owner of the Desert Inn Resort Hotel in Daytona Beach and sisterin-law Betty Jane Boone (Weisser), a retired nationally known realtor and a former Daytona Beach City Commissioner, as well as other local friends. Services will be held Wednesday, 1:30PM, at Louis Hirsch & Sons, 1225 Jerome Avenue at 167 Street, Bronx.
