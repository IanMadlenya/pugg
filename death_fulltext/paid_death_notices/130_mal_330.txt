
        FERNANDEZ-Dr. Xavier A. On November 23, in Fort Myers, Florida. Internationally recognized scholar with respect to classical Spanish theater and, in particular, the works of Tirso de Molina. Professor emeritus from State University of New York at Albany. Survived by his wife, Dr. Laura B. Fernandez, by his sons, Xavier J. Fernandez and Dr. Peter G. Fernandez, and by his daughter, Anita F. Palmer. Contributions may be made to the St. Vincent de Paul Church Building Fund of Fort Myers.
