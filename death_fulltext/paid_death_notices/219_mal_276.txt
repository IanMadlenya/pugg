
        FIELD--Fiske. Mutual of America, its Board of Directors, together with all of its Officers and Employees, mourn the passing of our former Senior Field Vice President and respected colleague, Fiske Field. For over fifteen years, Fiske provided Mutual of America and its clients with faithful and dedicated service. We will miss this man of great curiosity and passion and extend our sincere sympathies to his wife, Nancy, and his entire family. William J. Flynn Chairman of the Board Thomas J. Moran President and CEO Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
