
        COMISSIONA--Sergiu. Manhattan School of Music mourns the loss of our longtime friend, Sergiu Comissiona. Maestro Comissiona visited the school as guest conductor on numerous occasions. Our student musicians have been privileged to share the intellect, musicianship, passion and humanity that made Maestro Comissiona's presence in the orchestral world so remarkable. We are grateful for all he did to encourage and support the enrichment of our young musicians. With heartfelt sorrow we extend our condolences to his wife, Robinne, and family. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
        COMISSIONA--Sergiu. New York City Opera mourns the passing of our friend and former Music Director, Sergiu Comissiona. Though he was sought after worldwide and perhaps best known in the U.S. for raising the Baltimore and Houston Symphony Orchestras to their current high level, his stellar musicianship and warm humanity made a deep impression here at City Opera even before and after his brief tenure as Music Director from 1987-88. Our deepest sympathies go to his wife Robinne, his family and his many friends around the world. Paul Kellogg General Director
        COMISSIONA -- Sergiu. The American Symphony Orchestra mourns the passing of Sergiu Comissiona. He served as Music Director of the ASO 1978 to 1982. His death is a great loss to Music. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family. Leon Botstein, Music Dir Robert Fippinger, Chair Lynne Meloccaro, Exec Dir
        COMISSIONA--Sergiu. The Musicians and staff of the Jerusalem Symphony Orchestra are deeply saddened by the death of Sergiu Comissiona. He was chief guest conductor of the JSO and will be greatly missed. Our condolences to his wife & family. Leon Botstein, Music Dir Yossi Tal-Gan, Managing Dir
