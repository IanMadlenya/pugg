
        GRIBETZ--Allen, M.D. The Mount Sinai Medical Center and its Boards of Trustees mourn the passing of Allen Gribetz, M.D., Assistant Professor in the Department of Medicine. Dr. Gribetz, a preferred pulmonary consultant, joined Mount Sinai in 1971 and gained the highest praises from his colleagues for his intelligence, enthusiasm, diligence, generosity, and exemplary relations with patients, and staff. We extend our sincerest sympathy to his family and friends. Peter W. May Chairman of the Boards of Trustees Kenneth L. Davis, M.D. President, CEO and Dean
