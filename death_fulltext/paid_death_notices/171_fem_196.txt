
        GOLDSTEIN-Rita. 96, of Sagamore Lake, Carmel, died on Saturday, April 21. She was the devoted and loving wife of the deceased Charles Goldstein. She is survived by her sister Sara Katzman, nieces Beverly Katzman and Rochelle Selig, nephews Franklin Roth, Peter Goldstein, grandniece Mara Goldstein, and brother-in-law Harold Goldstein, who was an eternal source of love and support. Rita was the ''adopted grandmother'' of Bill, Penny and Hilary Knack and Stella Goldstein. Her departure leaves a profound void. Private services arranged by family. For information contact CassidyFlynn Funeral Home, 288 Main St, Mt. Kisco, NY 10549; 914-666-4840.
