
        HORNER-Martin McGregor. Of Orient, New York and Riviera Beach, Florida died suddenly at home in Florida on November 25, 2000. He was born February 25, 1924 in Newark, New Jersey and was a graduate of Phillips Academy Andover (1942) and Princeton University (1946). He served as an ensign in the U.S. Navy in World War II. He is survived by his brother Stephen Horner, his daughter Petria Fossel, his sons J.W. Maitland Horner and Martin Horner, his stepdaughter Linda Jesel, his stepson Alan Tramposch, nine grandchildren and five step-grandchildren. His daughter Sylvia Baker and his wife Dorothy Horner predeceased him. Funeral services will be private. Memorial services will be held at a later date in both Florida and Long Island. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Martin's name to Princeton University, Box 5357, Princeton, NJ 08543 or The American Cancer Society, 2200 Lake Boulevard, Atlanta, GA. 30319.
