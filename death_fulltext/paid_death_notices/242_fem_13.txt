
        ROGERS--Irene L. (Milligan), 89, of Easthampton, MA died February 19, 2007. Wife of Burton H. Rogers of Monson, MA and sister of Doris Hamilton-Jones, both deceased, she was born November 12, 1917 in Brooklyn, NY and lived in Port Washington for 40 years. She is survived by son David, daughter Janice, and their families. The Ahearn Funeral Home, 413587-0044, is in charge of services.
