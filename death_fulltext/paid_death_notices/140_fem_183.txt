
        LUKACH-Marion L. The Board of Directors, clinical and administrative staff and volunteers of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York mourn the loss of Marion L. Lukach, a beloved Director for more than 30 years, life-long volunteer and benefactor of the agency's home care and public health services. Her interest in nursing education and patient care, especially maternal and child health care, improved the health and well-being of countless New Yorkers. Her unwavering support of the agency for more than 50 years and commitment to high quality, compassionate healthcare will be remembered always. We extend our profound sympathy to her son, Arthur Lukach, a member of our Board of Directors, her daughter, Ellen Perlmutter, and to her grandchildren and great grandchildren. Douglas D. Broadwater Chairman of the Board Carol Raphael President and CEO
