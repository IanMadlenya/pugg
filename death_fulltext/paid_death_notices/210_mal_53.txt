
        PHILLIPS--Robert M., retired from the New York City School System, 94, of Lenox, MA, died on July 3, 2004. He formerly lived in Mt. Vernon, NY. In his unique way he was a ''man of all seasons.'' Devoted husband of Anne Phillips. Beloved father to Deborah Phillips of Great Barrington and Carl Phillips of Watertown, MA. Loving grandfather of Gordon and Jess. We can honor Bob's memory best by voting against the present administration.
