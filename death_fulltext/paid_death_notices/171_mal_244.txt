
        VERMUT-Hyman, 83. Beloved husband of Sydelle, passed away on April 17, 2001. He is survived and will be deeply missed by his children, Stephan and Barbara and Leslie and Tom, and his adoring grandchildren, Jennifer, Felix, Aaron, Ann, McGee, Lauren and Alex. The family will be sitting shiva at home. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the National Kidney Foundation of FL.
