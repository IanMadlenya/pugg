
        LEHR-Leo, 82, passed away on February 25, 2000 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Shirley, adored father of Janet & Gerald Lazar and Marilyn Lehr. Grandfather of David & Amy Lazar, Jeffrey Lazar and Deborah and Paul Galant. Cherished grandsons William and Andrew Galant, Michael Lazar. Surviving sister, Helen Mark and brother, Sidney Lehr. He will be dearly missed by all of his family and friends for his strength and courage, wonderful heart and sense of humor. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to The Parkinson Foundation. Graveside service to be held on Sunday, February 27, 2000 at 2:00PM at Montefiore Cemetery in Springfield Gardens, Queens.
