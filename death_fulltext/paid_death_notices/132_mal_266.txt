
        MATTERSDORF-Gustav. Age 93 of Lakehurst, NJ, January 19, at home. Beloved husband of Doris Koedding Mattersdorf for 22 years. Mr. Mattersdorf was a CPA for the State of New York, Dept. of Labor, NYC, at the time of his retirement. He was Area Director Field Tax Service Man-Bronx Area (35 years retiring). Survived by stepson John F. Newman of Middletown, NJ; stepdaughter Carolyn R. Thomas of Roswell, GA; brother of the late Leo (1984); niece Stephanie of Jupiter, FL; 2 grandnephews and 1 grandniece. Service and burial private.
