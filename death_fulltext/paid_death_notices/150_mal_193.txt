
        BIDERMAN-Nathan B. Age 95 in Palm Beach, FL, on July 9, 1999. Husband of the late Ruth and the late Evelyn. Father of Burt and Mark. Father-in-law of Carol and Wendy.         BIDERMAN-Nathan. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the passing of Nathan Biderman, beloved father of Mark Biderman, our esteemed President. May his memory be a blessing. We extend our deepest condolences to Mark, Wendy, and their entire family. Clergy, Board of Trustees, Staff and Members of Congregation Rodeph Sholom
