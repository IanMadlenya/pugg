
        SMITH-Melvin, 70. Died Sunday, December 21, 1997. New City, N.Y. resident, formerly of Yonkers, NY, retired attorney. Son of the late Sol and Henrietta Epstein Smith. Educated in NYC and was a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, member of NYS Bar Association and B'Nai B'rith, Army Veteran. Survived by his wife Judy, three daughters Melinda of Brooklyn, Donna Gilligan of New City and Debbie Reardon of Cincinnati, his brother Lawrence of Bardonia, NY and one grandchild. Memorial contributions may be made to the Huntington Disease Society of America, Nyack, New York 10960.
