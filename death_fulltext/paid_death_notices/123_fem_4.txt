
        KAUDERER-Ida, 90, of PA, formerly of NYC, died March 30 at home. Born in Poland, daughter of the late Jacob and Sarah Margoles, wife of the late John Kauderer. A graduate of Belview School of Nursing, she was an RN in several hospitals in NYC, and a charter member of Riverside Church in NYC for 70 years. Survivors, son Dr. John G. Kauderer of PA, daughter Janet Hutcheson of MD, four grandchildren and one great-grandson. Services by the Lanterman and Allen Funeral Home, East Stroudsburg, PA. Cremation in Riverside Church Collumbarium, NYC. Memorial donations to Riverside Church, John and Ida Kauderer Nursing School Scholarship Fund, 490 Riverside Drive, New York, New York 10027.
