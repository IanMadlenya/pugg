
        MC COY-Esther. Passed away on October 6, 1997 at age 93. Born in Spokane, Washington. Graduate of University of Iowa. Accountant active in business for several decades, active with Ladies of Charity, Archdiocese of New York and numerous other charitable and cultural organizations. She is survived by her beloved cousins Mary and Gerard Ryan of River Edge, NJ and Br. Patrick Flanagan, S.J. of St. Peter's College, NJ. Visiting at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 W 72nd St, Tuesday 6-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Corpus Christi R.C. Church, Wednesday, 10am. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
