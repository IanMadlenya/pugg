
        PILLAR--Richard Louis. Beloved father and grandfather, devoted husband and son, cherished brother and uncle, and special friend passed away at the age of 66 on April 15, 2004. He dedicated his life to raising a loving family with his late wife, Arlene, and fostering close friendships filled with happiness and laughter. Known as ''Papa D'' to more than just his grandchildren, he always had a warm smile for everyone. He will be greatly missed by his three children, Russell, Meredith, and Matthew; his wife, Ursula; his five grandchildren; his brothers, Robert and Charles; and all of those dear family members and friends whose lives he touched. Funeral services will be held at Gutterman's Memorial Chapel at 175 North Long Beach Road in Rockville Centre, NY, on Sunday, April 18, 2004, at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations be made in celebration of his life to the research program of the National Kidney Foundation, 30 East 33rd Street, New York, NY 10016.
