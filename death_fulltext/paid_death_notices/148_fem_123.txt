
        AMKRAUT-Sarah. Beloved sister, grandmother, great-grandmother and friend. Died on Monday evening at the age of 85 in Brooklyn. She had a heart attack while recuperating from back surgery. Mrs. Amkraut, widow and life long companion of the late Hyman Amkraut, will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. Fondly known as Grandma, Aunt Syd, and Grandma Sarah, her affection touched the lives of many. She is survived by her sister, Pauline, grandchildren, Eric, Brian, Daniel and Fran, granddaughters-in-law, Helen and Davida, and greatgrandchildren, Julian, Ruthy, and Shai.
