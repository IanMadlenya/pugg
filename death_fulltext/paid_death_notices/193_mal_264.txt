
        de KAY-George Colman. President of M. Evans & Co., book publishers, died of cancer at his NYC home on February 22, at 76. He also lived in Stonington, CT. He is survived by his wife Miranda; children Colman, Sarah & Charles; brother James Tertius de Kay; & two step-children, Alexandra Booke Millner and Nicholas Booke, and four adoring step-grandchildren: Henry, Milo, Sam and Katerina. A Memorial Service will be held in the spring.
