
        STOLL--Harry. The Board of Directors, Administration and Residents of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center mourn the passing of Harry Stoll, the special friend whose passion and advocacy for the Jewish elderly helped bring about the establishment of our center. His vision touched the lives of many. He leaves behind a legacy for generations. Our heartfelt condolences to Helen and the entire family. Arthur Goldstein, President Larry Simon, Chairman Herbert H. Friedman, Executive Vice President
