
        McGUIRE-Mary Mechtilde, long time resident of Bay Ridge and retired Supervisor for Pfizer Corp. died on October 13, 1999. Daughter of the late John V. McGuire and Annie McGuire Welsh. She was a charming and dignified lady who sacrificed greatly for many years to assure the health and well-being of her invalid mother. Loving sister of Rev. John V. McGuire CssR of Saratoga Springs, Edward of Brooklyn, Thomas of Merrick, NY, Loretta Deken of St. Louis, MO and the late Maurice, Joseph and Teresa Kaldheim. She is survived by her loving brother-in-law Neil and sisters-in-law Ellen, Peggy, Katherine and Mary. She is also survived by many loving nieces, nephews, grand-nieces and grandnephews. Reposing at McLaughlin Funeral Home, Third Avenue at 97th Street, Friday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday 11:45AM at St. Anselms Church.
