
        PAGE--Patrice Dominique. Humanitarian aid worker and Executive Director of Medecins Sans Frontieres (MSF) in Canada, on December 12, 2005 at age 33. His loss is mourned by his father, Andre Page, and Andre's partner, Micheline Harbec; his sister, Natalie Page; his girlfriend, Stacy Baker; and all his colleagues and friends at MSF around the world. Visitation and ceremony at JacquesCartier Urgel Bourgie Funeral Home in Longueuil, Quebec, Tuesday, December 20, 2005. In lieu of flowers please send donations to an organization that cares for women and children living with HIV/ AIDS in Africa--one of the many causes Patrice defended with extra-ordinary talent and passion.
