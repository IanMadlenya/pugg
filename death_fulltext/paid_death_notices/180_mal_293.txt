
        MEINHOFER - Joseph Martin, M.D., 89. On Saturday, January 12, 2002. Beloved husband of Frances H. Meinhofer & Evelyn Meinhofer. Loving father of four children, nine grandchildren and 7 great grandchildren. Respected physician in the Brooklyn community for over 50 years. His friends and colleagues will remember him for his tireless efforts on their behalf. He will be greatly missed by all those who knew him. Donations in his name to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
