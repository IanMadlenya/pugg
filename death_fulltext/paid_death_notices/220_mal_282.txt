
        MERCHANT--Ismail. amfAR mourns the passing of Ismail Merchant, 68, a great friend to the Foundation and to the 40 million women, men and children all over the world living with HIV and AIDS. As a leading supporter of Cinema Against AIDS and countless other amfAR events, Ismail Merchant lent his boundless energy and inexhaustible creativity to advancing global understanding of HIV, eradicating discrimination, and prolonging life for those living with AIDS. We are saddened by the loss of his talent, his warmth, and his commitment. Kenneth Cole, Chairman of the Board Board of Trustees Staff The American Foundation for AIDS Research (amfAR)
        MERCHANT--Ismail. The members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and longtime friend, Ismail Merchant, and send condolences to his family.
