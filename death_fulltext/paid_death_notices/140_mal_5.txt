
        PHILLIPS-Robert A. 87 of Lynbrook, LI. Peacefully in his sleep on August 31, 1998. Beloved husband of Regina. Loving father of Robert A., Jr. and Margaret M. Longto. Father-in-law of Chikee Phillips and Frank J. Longto. Dear grandfather of Brian and Kathleen Phillips. Survived by his twin brother James of Melbourne, Florida, devoted Uncle of James N. King and many other nieces and nephews. Veteran of US Navy WWII and US Army Korean Conflict. Retired from Irving Trust Company. Memorial mass 9:45 AM, Thursday at Saint Raymond's R.C. Church of East Rockaway, LI. In lieu of flowers, family would appreciate contributions to the Make-A-Wish Foundation or The American Heart Association.
