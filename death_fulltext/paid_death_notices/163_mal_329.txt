
        O'GORMAN-Michael Patrick. Age 57. Died at home Saturday, August 26, 2000 following a long, courageous battle with chronic lymphocytic leukemia, Beloved brother of Maureen O'Gorman Wasserman & James M. O'Gorman. Survived by nieces Maura O'Gorman Doerr, Patricia O'Gorman Jepsen, and Denise Wasserman Byle. Also survived by five great-nieces and one great-nephew. He dedicated himself through action & activity to ''Friends in Deed''. Visitation Monday & Tuesday 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 West 72 Street, NYC. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10AM, at Holy Name of Jesus, 207 West 96 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name can be made to Friends in Deed, 594 Broadway, Suite 706, New York, New York 10012.
