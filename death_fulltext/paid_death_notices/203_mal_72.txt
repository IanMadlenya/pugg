
        MINSKY--Abraham. Passed away peacefully on December 5, 2003. He was a WWII veteran. More importantly, he loved his family. He was the beloved husband, partner and best friend of his adoring wife Mollie. His daughter Arlene, his son Jerry and wife/ daughter-in-law Lynne, as well as his loving grandchildren Geoffrey, Gregory and Tracy, will miss this wonderfully gentle, smart and handsome father and grandfather. Contributions can be made to Jewish War Veterans Memorial, Abe Cohen-Lehman Post No. 50, c/o Victor Krumholz, 2814 West 8th St, Brooklyn, NY 11224, Apt 12C.
