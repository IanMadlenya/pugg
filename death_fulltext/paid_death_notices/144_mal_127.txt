
        MATSAKIS-Steven C.., of Wyckoff, N.J., died Tuesday, January 5, 1999, at the Hackensack University Medical Center. Beloved son of Victoria (nee Lucas) and the late Constantinos Matsakis, who died in 1986. Cherished husband of Mary (nee Finlayson), and adored father of Louise. Much loved brother of Demetrios and Nicholas. The grandson of an immigrant, Steve was born May 1, 1958, in Passaic, N.J., grew up in Rutherford, resided in Lyndhurst for 15 years before moving to Wyckoff 3 years ago. Mr. Matsakis attended the Rutherford public schools, received his undergraduate degree from New York University, graduating Summa Cum Laude, and also received his M.B.A. from N.Y.U. He began his career at Bankers Trust Company and in 1980 was elevated to the position of Vice President of the Corporate Finance Department. He later became an Executive Vice President of the Atlantic Bank of New York City. He was also C.E.O. and Cofounder of super Video Stores that started with one store in 1987 and flourished into a 14 store chain in northern New Jersey. In 1996 the company was sold to West Coast Entertainment Corp. Steve was a strong believer in the values and wonderful opportunities America provided. To help others Steve recently established a scholarship fund to benefit Rutherford High School graduates. His loving and passionate persona will be greatly missed. Relatives and friends are invited to attend the funeral on Saturday, January 9, 1999, 9:15 AM, from the Thomas J. Diffily Funeral Home, 41 Ames Ave in Rutherford, and 10 AM at St. George Greek Orthodox Church in Clifton, N.J. Interment at Hillside Cemetery in Lyndhurst, N.J. Visiting Thursday & Friday, 2-4 & 7-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Victoria & Costantinos Scholarship Fund at the Rutherford High School, would be appreciated.
