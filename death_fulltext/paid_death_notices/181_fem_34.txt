
        SPEARE-Elizabeth Burnett, 94, died November 30, 2001, Medford, NJ. For over 20 years, she was Executive Secretary of NYC-Bronx Welfare Councils. She was a graduate of Mt. Holyoke College and held two Master's degrees from Columbia University. She is survived by four nephews and nieces. Private interment at the Boundbrook NJ Cemetery.
