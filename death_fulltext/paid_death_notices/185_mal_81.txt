
        AUERBACH-Leonard W. On June 3, 2002. 84, in New York. Associate Professor Emeritus, Chair, Theatre Arts, SUNY Stony Brook. Production stage manager on Broadway for Raisin In The Sun, Purlie, Owl and Pussycat and others. He leaves his wife Elizabeth Aaron, children Ellen Hans (Kevin), David and granddaughter Madeline Hans.
