
        WELDEN-Richard Clinton. June 29, 1998. Husband of Mary Thenebe Welden. Father of Thorn, Wickford and Trafford. Brother of Robert E. Welden. He was a partner with the law firm Decker, Hubbard, Welden and Sweeney, NYC. Memorial service Tuesday, July 14, 1998, 11AM at the Brick Presbyterian Church, Park Avenue at 91 Street.
