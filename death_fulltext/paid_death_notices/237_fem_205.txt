
        RUEDI --Mary-Sophie passed away October 26, 2006 at noon. She was in the Saint Vincent's Hospital HomeHospice Program. She died of breast cancer at 69 years old. She had been ill for an extended period of time. She was the former national secretary of The Catholic Women of America.
