
        GLIEDMAN-Anthony. On May 31, 2002. Beloved husband of Virginia. Loving father of Gregory and Christopher. Cherished son of Rae. Dear brother of Kenneth. Service Tuesday, June 4, 11:45 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in his memory may be made to Prospect Park Alliance, 95 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn, NY.
        GLIEDMAN-Anthony B. The Board of Directors and staff of New York Foundation for Senior Citizens deeply mourn the passing of our beloved Director, Tony Gliedman, and extend our sympathy to his wife, Ginny, his sons, Greg and Chris, daughter-in-law, Beth and mother Rae. A brilliant, genuinely caring Board Member, who has been strongly committed to and supportive of the Foundation for over 2 decades, Tony Gliedman was also a true friend to so many, who loved him in return. He will be greatly missed and lovingly remembered.
