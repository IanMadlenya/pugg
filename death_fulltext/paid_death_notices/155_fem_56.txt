
        WRIGHT-Mary Catherine Rita (nee Hehir) Died on December 3, 1999 in Overlook Hospital, Summit, NJ after a brief illness. Born in NYC, July 25, 1912. Resided in Brooklyn before moving to Scotch Plains, NJ 44 years ago. Maintained a summer residence in Breezy Point, NY for many years. For many years she worked at the former Hahne's Department store in Westfield. Beloved wife of the late David Francis. Cherished sister of Sister Thomas Marie, CSJ (Ethel). Loving mother of David (Patricia), Donald (Eileen), Robert (Helen), Richard (Linda), and Mary Green (Donald). Grandmother of 13 grandchildren and great-grandmother of six greatgrandchildren. She was a member of Immaculate Heart of Mary Rosary Altar Society, an active member of the Scotch Plains YMCA, and a volunteer at the Ashbrook Nursing Home. Funeral from the Memorial Funeral Home, 155 South Avenue, Fanwood, NJ on Monday, December 6 at 9 AM. Mass of the Resurrection at Immaculate Heart of Mary R.C. Church, Scotch Plains at 10 AM. Interment Fairview Cemetery, Westfield. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Mary's honor to the Sisters of St. Joseph, Maria Regina Convent, Brentwood, NY 11717.
