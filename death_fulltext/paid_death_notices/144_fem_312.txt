
        GOLDSTEIN-Arlene. January 28, 1999. Beloved aunt. She fought tirelessly on behalf of the healthcare consumer as a member ofthe State Board of Nursing, NYC Health and Hospital Corporation Board, Bellevue Community Board and NENA Health Council. Her work exemplified selfless public service, intellectual honesty, fearlessness in challenging the powerful on behalf of the powerless and absolute integrity. Services Tuesday 10:30AM, Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 W. 83 St.
