
        MILBERG-Max, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Max Milberg. Dr. Milberg was associated with the Department of Medicine for over 35 years. He also served as Director of Infectious Disease for many years. He will be sorely missed by all his colleagues and friends. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Jacob Shani, M.D., President David Feldman, M.D., Pres. Elect Salvatore DeGliuomini, M.D., Secy Steven Farber, M.D., Treasurer
