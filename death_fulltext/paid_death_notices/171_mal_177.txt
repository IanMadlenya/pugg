
        ASIP-William. The Bard College community mourns the passing of William Asip, former athletic coach and business manager of Bard and a long time friend of the college. His many years of dedication and commitment to excellence on behalf of the college will long be remembered, and he will be sorely missed by all who knew him. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab, II. Chairman Board of Trustees
