
        SCHWARTZ-Lillian Linder, 68, of Lenox, Ma. and Sarasota, Fl. Died on Tuesday, August 17, 1999. She worked as a teacher and guidance counselor in the N.Y. City School System. She was a fighter for peace, social justice and a better world. She leaves her husband, Sol Schwartz; a son, David Schwartz; a daughter, Risa Schwartz; and a grandson, John. Friends wishing to, may make donations to the Lenox Library Association for the Lillian Linder Schwartz Fund to provide large type and talking books for the visually impaired, 18 Main St, Lenox, Ma 01240.
