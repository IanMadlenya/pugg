
        HOCKENBERRY - Raymond N. Jr. On November 21, 2002, in New Rochelle, NY. A longtime resident of Bronxville, NY, he was born February 21, 1913, in Portland, OR. Survivors include his children John Hockenberry of Bethesda, MD, Hope Yelich of Williamsburg, VA, and James Hockenberry of Princeton, NJ.
