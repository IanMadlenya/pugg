
        WIENER -- Constance Ellen ''Connie'' Gordon. Died on November 26, 2004, at New York University Medical Center. The family asks that memorials be contributed to the East Sixties Neighborhood Association, or to the donor's favorite charity. At her request, Connie's ashes will be scattered in the Poudre River Valley, near her birthplace in Fort Collins, Colorado. The ceremony will be private.
