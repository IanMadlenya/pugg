
        GORDON-Bernard. Age 78, died at home in Glencoe, IL on June 4, 2000. Cherished son of Morris and Tina Goomnitz (deceased). He was the beloved husband of the late Margaret C. Gordon and is survived by his loving children Anne (Phiroshaw), Jon (Kristie), and Al (Lynn) and his loving grandchildren Griffin, Jacquelynn, Blair, Tory, Peter and David. He is also survived by his sisters Edith Harris (Louis) and Carolyn Lieber (Arthur) and by his dear friend Joan Adler. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Bernard Gordon Award for Magazine Publishing at the Medill School of Journalism, Northwestern University, would be appreciated.
