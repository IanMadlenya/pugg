
        HARTMANN-Audrey M. Beloved wife of Peter W. Loving mother of Karin, Christiane and Anton. Grandmother of ten and good friend of many. Died on Sunday September 16, 2001 at Lawrence Hospital. She is survived by her five siblings. Visiting hours are at the The Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, 20 Cedar St, Bronxville, N.Y. on Thursday, September 20 from 5 to 8PM. Services are on Friday, September 21 at Christ Church, 17 Sagamore Rd, Bronxville, N.Y. at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be sent to either the Metroplitan Opera Association, Inc, Lincoln Center, New York, NY 10023 or Christ Church, in memory of Audrey M. Hartmann.
