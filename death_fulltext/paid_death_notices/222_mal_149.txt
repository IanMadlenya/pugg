
        SCHULTE--John H., 85, of State College, formerly of Brooklyn and Massapequa, New York, died Tuesday, July 12, 2005. Born October 7, 1919 in New York City, he was a son of the late William and Rose Casey Schulte. He grew up in Brooklyn during the Depression and helped support his mother, disabled father and younger brothers. On November 27, 1943, he married Edna May Donegan, who died July 20, 2000. He was the Delivery Foreman for the New York Times, retiring in 1983 after 27 years. He was former member of Our Lady of Lourdes Catholic Church in Massapequa, New York. He was a retired member of the Newspaper & Mail Deliverers Union in NYC. He is survived by his son, David L. Schulte and his wife Patricia of State College, three grandchildren, two great grandchildren, and a brother, Frank Schulte of Rancho Santa Fe, CA. He was preceded in death by two brothers, William and George Schulte. Burial will be in Old Stone Fort Cemetery in Schoharie, NY on Saturday July 16 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Our Lady of Lourdes Church, 855 Carmans Road, Massapequa Park, New York 11762.
        SCHULTE--John H. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of John H. Schulte, associated with The Times from December 1946 until retiring in June 1983.
