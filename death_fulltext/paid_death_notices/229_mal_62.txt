
        GOODMAN--Norman H. 83, died peacefully January 24, at his Greenwich Village home, with his wife Barbara at his side. They had been married 60 years. Besides his wife, he is survived by his daughters, Vanina and Chryse, their husbands, six grandchildren, and his brothers, Peter & Thomas and their families. After time in college, military service and art school, Norman joined the family hosiery business. When stretch nylon was introduced to the U.S., he was instrumental in the development of the first Danskin tights. He had a lifelong passion for photography and a keen interest in the arts.
