
        MELLOR--Geoffrey B., 51, died on March 13, 2005. Man of many talents, a true Renaissance man; he will always be remembered for his intellectual curiosity and the passion he gave everything he did. He lived a rich life, full of many interests, ranging from wildlife and environmental conservation to small ensembles and choral singing to studying mushrooms and bird watching. He was also a devoted companion and wonderful friend. He is survived by his life partner Arpad Jaromy of NYC, his brother James of Ridgefield, CT, his sister Missy of NYC, his sister Jenny of Baltimore, and his father Schuyler Mellor of Easton, MD. A memorial service will be announced.
