
        MILLER--Ruth F. Of Tucson, AZ. She also leaves four grandchildren. A memorial service planned for Sunday, November 23, 2003, 2 P.M. at Congregation Anshei Israel, Epstein Chapel, 5550 E. 5th Street, Tucson, AZ.
