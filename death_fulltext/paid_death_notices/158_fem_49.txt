
        WARREN-Mildred. 84, passed away peacefully on March 3. A native of New York City. She is survived by her husband of 59 years, Al. Also surviving are her children Robert Warren and Linda Greenberg, son-in-law Richard, four grandchildren, Danielle, Amos, Matthew and Jason, and two sisters, Mathilda Kahn and Jean Gang. Services have been held.
