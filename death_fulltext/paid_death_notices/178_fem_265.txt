
        DANIELL-Blanche, 99. Of Rye, NY, died on November 24, 2001. Mrs. Blanche N. Daniell was the first wife of F. Raymond Daniell, a correspondant for The New York Times. She was a writer for the Voice of America during and after World War II. She is survived by her two children, Curtis Guild Daniell, of Germany and Elizabeth Lake Daniell of New York City. Funeral services will be held at the Rye Presbyterian Church, Rye, NY on Friday, November 30, 1 PM.
