
        BLOOM--Sylvia. We mourn the passing of the Honorable Sylvia Bloom, former CUNY trustee (1981 to 1994) and member of the CUNY Construction Fund. Her enduring dedication to and love for the City University's student body will be long remembered. Our deepest sympathies are extended to her beloved family. Benno C.
