
        ROBERTS-Norman A. On September 27, 2002, died peacefully at home at age 85. Beloved husband of the late Bettina. He is survived by his sister Eleanor Rosenblatt. He was predeceased by a sister, Pearl Markowitz and two brothers; Dr. Sidney Rosenfeld and Irving Rosenfeld. Contributions may be made to the Norman & Bettina Roberts Foundation, Inc., C/O Gruntal & Company, Attn: Joseph Canciglia, 2125 Center Avenue, Fort Lee, NJ 07024.
