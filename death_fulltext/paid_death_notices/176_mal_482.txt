
        JURGENSEN-Rev. John W. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicar of St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church, 94-40 118 St, Richmond Hill, on September 25. Reposing in Lehy-MacDonald Funeral Home, 111-02 Atlantic Avenue, Richmond Hill, Thursday, September 27 from 2-4:30 and 7-9 P.M. and Friday, September 28 from 2-4:30 P.M. Mass of Transferral at St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass at St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church, Saturday, September 29 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Reverend Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
