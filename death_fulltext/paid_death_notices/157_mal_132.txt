
        TISCHLER-Dr. Stanley. State University of New York-Health Science Center at Brooklyn, Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology was saddened to hear of Dr. Stanley Tischler's death. Dr. Tischler was a clinical professor in the Department. He was a member of the faculty for a period of 23 years. He retired from his faculty position in 1996. The Department extends its heartfelt sympathy to his family.
