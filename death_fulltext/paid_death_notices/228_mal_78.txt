
        MORRISON -- Dr. Walbert ''Bill''. 80. Of Jupiter, FL and NYC died on January 5. Husband of the late Dr. Eleanor Morrison, father of Dr. Leslie Faerstein and Robert ''Mo'' Morrison, beloved grandfather and father-in-law. Donations can be made in his memory to ''Teachers College, Columbia University'' noting ''Drs. Eleanor B. and Walbert Morrison Scholarship Fund'' 525 W 120 St, NY, NY 10027.
