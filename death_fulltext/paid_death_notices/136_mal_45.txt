
        GREGOR-Harry P., Ph.D. Age 81. Professor Emeritus of Columbia University and Distinguished Research Professor at Polytechnic University. Beloved husband of Elinor for 57 years. Loving father of Laurel Porter, John, Charles (Marjorie) and Polly (Brian Guillorn). Adored grandfather of Stephen Porter, Ephriam, Aryeh and Rachel Gregor, Martha, Gregory and Sally Guillorn and great grandson Daniel Porter. Also survived by his devoted sister Mary Molner other relatives and many nephews, cousins and loving friends. Memorial contributions may be made to The Polymer Research Institute, Polytechnic University, 6 Metrotech Center, Brooklyn, New York 11201. Shiva will be observed at 410 Riverside Drive, Apt 72A through May 10th.
