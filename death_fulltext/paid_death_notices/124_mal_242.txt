
        LEFKOWITZ-Louis L. The Board of Trustees, the Medical Staff and the Auxiliaries of Long Beach Medical Center record with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague and Past President, Louis L. Lefkowitz. For forty-five years he served our medical center with honor and distinction. He was a visionary who helped shape the growth of our institution and worked constantly to insure its future. He will be sorely missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his beloved wife, Rebecca, his children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. Alfred I. Osterland, Chairman, Board of Trustees Martin F. Nester, Jr., Chief Executive Officer Mordecai Bluth, MD, Chairman Medical Staff Gloria Nicolosi, President, Auxiliaries
