
        AGUINALDO-Dr. Jose. March 5, 2002. Beloved son of Mercedes Aguinaldo. Long time partner of Edward Jones. Devoted brother of Val, Chato, Tony, Lery, Chad and Tante. He will be deeply missed by his many loving family members and friends. He graduated from Santo Tomas School of Medicine in Manila, trained as a pediatrician in New York City. He was a member of the American Academy of Pediatrics, currently on the staff of Gouverneur Hospital, New York City. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Thursday and Friday 3-5 & 79pm. Service Saturday, March 9, 11AM, at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine, 112 St. and Amsterdam Ave., NYC.
