
        KRINSKY-Arnold L. Died March 12, 2000 at the age of 68 after a courageous battle with myelodysplasia. Loving husband to Doreen for 15 years. Also survived by daughters Danelle, Laurel, Nancy and Carolyn and grandchildren Joshua, Jeremiah, Angela, Gabriel and Sepharia. Sisters Arlene (Norman), Vivian (Jack), Florence (Alvin). Arnold taught at Erasmus Hall High School for 30 years and built Tax City, a successful income tax business for 41 years. Arnold was an intelligent and generous man who loved life and lived it to its fullest. He will be missed.
