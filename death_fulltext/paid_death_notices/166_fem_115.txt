
        TAMARIN-Lillian, M.D. Wife of the late Alfred Tamarin and the late Carlton Barlow. Mother of Susan Eluard (Alain). Grandmother of Nathalie Hurwitz (Jonathan) and Lauren Eluard. A graduate of NYU Medical School, she was a member of the American Academy of Psychoanalysis and a practicing Analyst for 50 years. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Hospital. A Memorial Service will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave., on Saturday, November 18 at 3:30 PM.
