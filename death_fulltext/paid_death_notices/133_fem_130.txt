
        GOLDMAN-Carol Petry of Tequesta, FL, passed away, Thursday, February 12, 1998 at the Jupiter Medical Center, Jupiter, FL. Born in New York City, she had been a resident of Tequesta for 15 years, coming from Greenwich, CT. She was a member of the Turtle Creek Country Club, the Turtle Creek Ladies Golf Assoc. and was a past officer of the Tequesta Chapter of the American Needlepoint Guild. She is survived by two sons, LeGrand Snowden Redfield, Jr. of Norwalk, CT and Edward Petry Redfield of Boston, MA; a daughter, Elizabeth Kehoe Petry Goldman of Stratford, CT; a sister, Barbara Petry Annear of Palm Beach Gardens, FL; a grandson, Harley Ellis Petry Redfield and a nephew, William J. Mathews, III. She will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. The funeral service will be held, 3PM Sunday, February 15, 1998 at the Aycock Funeral Home Chapel, Jupiter. Interment will be in Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY. Friends may call Sunday, February 15, from 11-3PM at the Aycock Funeral Home, 1112 Military Trail, Jupiter, FL.
