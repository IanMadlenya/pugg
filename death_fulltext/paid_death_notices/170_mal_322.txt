
        HOLT-John R., 90, of 48 Pierrepont St., Brooklyn, died Wednesday morning at 7:25 AM. The memorial service will be held at the Church of the Incarnation in Manhattan on Monday afternoon at 4 PM. Mr. Holt, a highly decorated veteran of World War II, had lived in Brooklyn following the end of World War II, having worked for the Hartz Company for many years. He was an active member of the Church of the Incarnation.
