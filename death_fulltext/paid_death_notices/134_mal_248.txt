
        MADSEN-Viggo Holm, longtime resident of Albertson, New York died at Doniphan, Missouri, March 20, 1998, after a long illness, at the age of 72. A well-known artist and art educator, he taught art at Roslyn High School for 22 years and was Adjunct Professor of Art at Nassau Community College for 28 years. His prints were exhibited widely in this country and Europe. He is survived by his widow, Lois, son Kirk and daughter Karen and three grandchildren. Memorial gifts may be made to Anderson University, 1100 East 5th Street, Anderson, Indiana 46015 to be used for needy art students.
