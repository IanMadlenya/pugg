
        HENNESSY-Raymond J. The New York Stock Exchange community mourns the loss of our valued friend and respected colleague, who served as our Vice President, Member Firm Regulatory Development and Services. For two decades, Ray distinguished himself throughout the securities industry with his commitment to excellence and his tireless work to strengthen the integrity of the equities markets. His warmth, professionalism and wise counsel will be greatly missed. The thoughts and prayers of the entire Exchange community are with the Hennessy family.
