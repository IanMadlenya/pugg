
        SHATZKES-Bella, Reb. Emunah of America mourns the passing of Reb. Bella Shatzkes, beloved mother of Charlotte Dachs, Honorary National President. She was a great lady who was devoted to her family and left a legacy of Chesed for Am Yisrael, always choosing Emunah's children in Israel as a priority in her life. She will be sorely missed. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marcia Genuth, Nat'l Pres. Melanie Oelbaum, Hon. Nat'l Pres. Shirley Singer, Exec V.P. Carol Sufian, Exec Dir.
