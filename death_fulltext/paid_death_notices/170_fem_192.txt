
        McSTEA-Elizabeth M. Of Fort Lauderdale, FL. Formerly of New York City. Died peacefully March 19, 2001. Survived by loving siter Kathleen L. Delaney and niece Kathleen M. Delaney. She attended NYU and Columbia University, achieving a Master's Degree in Journalism & was the first woman in the U.S. to receive the National Public Relations Award. She will be buried in the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, N.Y.
