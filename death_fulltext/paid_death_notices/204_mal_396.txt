
        WITKIN--Dr. Ned, 47, of Atlanta, GA, died January 29. Dr. Witkin was a renowned doctor of optometry and low vision specialist who helped the visually impaired regain their independence. He was a researcher and inventor, but most of all, a compassionate clinician. Survived by wife Beverly, and daughter Sehra. Donations to Ned Witkin's Scholarship Fund, 245 Shadowbrook Drive, Roswell, GA 30075. 770-451-4999.
