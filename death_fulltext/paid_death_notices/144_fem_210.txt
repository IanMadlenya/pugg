
        KIBEL-Lili. Riverdale Country School extends its deepest sympathy to the family of Lili Kibel, dear wife of former Trustee, Henry Kibel, and adored mother of Charles, Adam and Jami. Her steadfast dedication to Riverdale Country School continued after her children had graduated. She remained an active member of the Riverdale family. Most recently, she served on The Campaign for Riverdale Committee. Her gracious presence shall be missed. David F. Stein, Chairman John R. Johnson, Headmaster
