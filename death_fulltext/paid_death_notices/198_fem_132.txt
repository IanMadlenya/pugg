
        ALPERIN--Ruth. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ruth Alperin, beloved mother of Barry Alperin, a cherished friend of the Jewish community. She expressed her devotion to social and humanitarin causes through exceptional philanthropy, a legacy that continues with her children. We extend our deepest sympathies to Barry; his wife, Mimi; and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO >>AD#
