
        ELSON-Esther Cohn. 91, a resident of Palm Beach, Florida, formerly of Atlanta, Georgia, and Norfolk, Virginia, passed away April 29, 2001. Predeceased by her husband Harry. Loving mother of Ambassador Edward Elliott (Suzanne) Elson of Palm Beach, Florida. Cherished grandmother of Charles M. (Aimee) Elson of Newark, Delaware, Louis G. (Sarah) Elson of London, England, and Harry (Lisa) Elson II of New York, New York. Adored great-grandmother of Isabel, Everett, Edward and Caroline. Funeral services to be held 2 PM, Monday, at Temple Israel, 1901 No. Flagler Dr., West Palm Beach, Florida 33407. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to: Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407, or Ann Norton Sculpture Garden, 253 Barcelona Rd., West Palm Beach, Florida 33401. Interment will be private. For information, contact Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 5411 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach, Florida 33417.
