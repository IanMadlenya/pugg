
        KLEIMAN-Barbara. On October 7, 1997. Beloved daughter of Bella Kleiman and the late Murray Kleiman. Sister of Michael and Mark Kleiman and Isabel Byron. Aunt of Molly Kleiman. Cherished of Stan Diglio. Assistant Commissioner, NYC Dept of Homeless Services. Passionate advocate for services to the poor. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mount Vernon, N.Y. Thursday, October 9th, 10AM. Contributions in her name may be made to local services for the homeless.
