
        SOEKAWA--Ichiro, 72, born in Inada, Japan, passed peacefully on January 31st, 2005 in Raleigh, NC. A longtime New Yorker, avid Yankees fan and owner of Akasaka Restaurant, Ichiro is survived by his children, Michael, Sumiko, Emiko and their mother, Frances, and by his mother, brother and sister in Japan. We love you.
