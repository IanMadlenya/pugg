
        RANDLE--Bill. 81, radio deejay, talk show host, lawyer, and college professor, died Friday in Cleveland, OH. Credited with discovering Elvis Presley. Introduced Elvis on ''Stage Show'' on CBS in January, 1956, Elvis's first national TV appearance. Deejay and interviewer at WCBS Radio in New York in the 50s and 60s.
