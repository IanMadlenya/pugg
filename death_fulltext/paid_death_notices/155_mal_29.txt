
        FEDER-Max. Of Sunrise, FL, and former longtime resident of Brooklyn and Forest Hills, NY, on October 29 at age 90. Son of Philip and Ida. Beloved husband of 59 years of the late and deeply missed Rose. Devoted father of Michael. Loving grandfather of Jonathan. Caring brother of Shirley and the late Irving, Betty, Mary and Ebby. Private services and interment were held. Contributions in his memory may be made to The United Lubavitcher Yeshivoth, 841-853 Ocean Parkway, Brooklyn, NY 11230. He was a man of faith and humor-and an independent spirit who will live in our hearts forever.
