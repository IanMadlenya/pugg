
        LANDI-Ellen C. (Coleman). Of Heritage Village, Southbury, CT, in her 88th year, after a brief illness, at St. Mary's Hospital, Waterbury, CT. Originally of Pleasantville, New York, she was the daughter of the late Henry M. and Mary (Spencer) Coleman, and was married to Frank J. Landi, who died in 1953. Mrs. Landi retired from IBM's patent law department in 1975. She is the beloved mother of Frank J. Landi, Sr. of New Rochelle, the cherished sister of James, John, and Henry Coleman, and the deceased Thomas Coleman, Marie Scheland and Ann Brennan. She is the loving grandmother of Matthew, Peter, Frank and Lucy, and great grandmother of Isabella Landi. Reposing at the McMahon Funeral Home, 491 Mamaroneck Ave, White Plains, NY. Visitation on Wednesday, 13 January 1999 from 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM. A funeral mass will be held at 10 AM on Thursday, 14 January at Holy Family Church, Mayflower Avenue, New Rochelle, New York. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
