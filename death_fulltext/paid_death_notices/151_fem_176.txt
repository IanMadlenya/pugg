
        DICKSTEIN-Rosalind. Died suddenly August 18, 1999. She was the beloved wife of the late Abe Dickstein and devoted mother of Steven and daughter-in-law Janet. A most loving and caring grandmother of Nate. She will be deeply missed by her twin sister, MarionReimer, her brother Irving Greenberg and wife Rita, and many nieces and nephews. Predeceased by her brother Larry Greenberg. Her warm personality won her many friends who will miss her greatly. Funeral services on Sunday, August 22, 1999 in North Miami Beach, FL.
