
        PAASHAUS -- Sylvia (nee Chuse), formerly Gomperts. Died peacefully on December 8, 2004 in San Diego, CA at age 83 after a brief but challenging illness. She is survived by her beloved husband Robert, brother Sidney Chuse (Claire), nieces Ellen Chuse, Loren Chuse, Karen Gustafson, Valerie Chuse, Merle Chuse, cousin Emanuel Chuse and dear friend Joan Loomis, as well as numerous grand nieces and nephew. She will be deeply missed by all.
