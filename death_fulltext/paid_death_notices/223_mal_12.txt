
        FEDER--Stuart, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians, nurses and staff in the Department of Psychiatry in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and beloved colleague, Stuart Feder, M.D. A long-time member of the Psychiatry faculty, Stuart will forever be remembered for his extraordinary contributions to the field of psychiatric medicine and, most particularly, for his passionate commitment to the education and medical training of the physicians of tomorrow. He will be greatly missed by all who knew him and worked with him. To his family, friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. David J. Shulkin, M.D. President and CEO Stephen P. Haveson, M.D. President, Medical Board, President, Doctors Alumuni Association Arnold Winston, M.D. Chairman, Department of Psychiatry
