
        SIEGEL--Joseph. Of Riverdale, NY, age 98, died early Saturday morning. Beloved husband of the late Edith Joffe Siegel, he is survived by his children Mary Bleiberg and Robert Siegel; Mary's husband, Charles, and Robert's wife, Jane; his grandchildren Kathryn Bleiberg (and her husband, Gary Brendel), Nicholas Merchant Bleiberg (and his wife, Ekem), Erica Siegel, and Leah Siegel. A graduate of New York University, he was a veteran of WWII, a teacher and supervisor in the New York City public school system, a mentor to students and teachers alike. He loved people, books, theater, music, travel, and his family, who mourn his passing and celebrate his long, full life. Funeral service noon, Monday, January 12, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue at 91 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions to East End Temple, 245 E. 17 St, New York, NY 10003, where he was a member for more than 50 years, would be appreciated.
