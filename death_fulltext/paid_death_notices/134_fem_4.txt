
        BURNS-Ethel, died on February 27, 1998. For many years she was connected with educational broadcasting on WNYE-FM and WNYETV channel 25, with many of her program series winning Emmys for CBS-TV, where they were broadcast. Among the programs she produced for television and radio were American Musical Theater, Dial M For Music, Around The Corner, and It's Fun To Sing. She will be missed by her TV and radio friends.
