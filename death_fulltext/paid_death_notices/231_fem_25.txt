
        VAN DYNE--Esther, 89 years old. Died on April 3, 2006. Beloved wife of the late George Van Dyne. Devoted mother of Phyllis Van Dyne & Lenore and David Kristof. Loving sister-in-law of Dr. Joseph Roger Van Dyne. Graveside services on Wednesday 11 AM at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. Donations in her memory may be made to The United Cerebral Palsey Women's Division in New York City.
