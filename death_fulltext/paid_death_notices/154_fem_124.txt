
        DELAFIELD-Margaret Gade. Of Vero Beach, Florida died Friday, November 12 at home. She was born in Rochester, New York in 1910 and was the widow of Lieutenant Richard Delafield USNR who died in 1945. She is survived by her children: Cecily Delafield of Vero Beach. Felicia Delafield Van Praagh of New York City, Richard Delafield of Paradise Valley, Arizona, Penelope Delafield Johnson of Haydenville, MA, Astrid Delafield of NYC, Margaret Delafield Brandt of Latham, New York, her nephew J. Dennis Delafield of NYC, 12 grandchildren, and nine great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. James Episcopal Church in St. James, NY at 11 A.M. Saturday, November 27. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to the National Multiple Sclerosis Foundation, 733 3rd Avenue, New York, N.Y. 10017.
