
        MEYER--Jerome. On July 26th, 2004. Devoted husband of Charlotte, dear father of Bennett (Sharon), and the late Janet, loving grandfather of Jessica and Ashley. Retired President of the Troy Chemical Co. and Tuttles Elixer Co. Founder and first President of Yama-Ki Bonsai Society and author of ''The Bonsai Book of Practical Facts''. Former President of the Free Synagogue of Flushing, New York. He will be forever remembered and loved by all who knew him.
