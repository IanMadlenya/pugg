
        INDYKE-William. On April 9, 2002. Survived by his wife Paula, sons Jay and Barry, daughter-in-law Andrea, grandchildren Matthew and Ross, and sister Estelle Schlenger. Mr. Indyke served as a Cantor on high holy holidays for synagogues across the country for many years, and was a microbiologist for the NYC Department of Health. A beautiful voice, a gentle soul. Services will be held today, 11:15 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
