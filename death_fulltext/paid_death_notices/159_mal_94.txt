
        MARCUS-Ben. The Jewish Community Relations Council of New York extends its heartfelt condolences to our esteemed Board Member Joel Marcus on the recent passing of his beloved father, the founder and first President of the Sephardic Jewish Center of Canarsie. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Gedale B. Horowitz, Pres. Michael S. Miller, Exec. V.P.
