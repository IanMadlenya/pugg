
        MULVIHILL-Frank J. The officers, members and staff of New York Electrical Contractors Association are saddened by the loss of their friend and associate, Frank J. Mulvihill. For many years Frank was an important part of our electrical industry. His counsel and wisdom will be missed by all. We express our deepest sympathy to the entire family. Edward A. Dougherty, President Joseph D'Angelo, Exec Secy
