
        EASTMAN-John Merritt. John Merritt Eastman of Rye, NY, died on September 21, 2000, after a struggle with cancer. He and his family lived in Rye for 44 years where he was a committed member of the Manursing Island Club. A lifelong devotee of sailing and racing, he was also a member of the Storm Trysail Club and a Corinthian Yachtsman. Mr. Eastman was born in Westbury, NY, and graduated from Friends Academy in Locust Valley. He attended Columbia College where he was selected to work on the Manhattan Project. He spent his career as a senior marketing and advertising executive in New York, most notably with the firms Jack Tinker and Partners and Doyle Dane Bernbach. Mr. Eastman is survived by his wife, Juliette, of Rye, NY; daughter Sarah Eastman and husband Kevin Pinto of Manhattan; daughter Polly Scott and husband Eugene of Manhattan; son Kim Eastman of Rochester, NY; brother Dr. Gerard Lester Eastman of Huntington, NY; and four adoring grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Christ's Church, Rye, Saturday, September 30, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Christ's Church, Rye.
