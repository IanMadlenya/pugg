
        FISHER-Philip, 94 years old. Lived in Belmont, CA, formerly of Elkins Park, PA., and Miami Beach, FL. Beloved husband of the late Kathryn (Cubby) who passed away six weeks ago. He didn't want to live without her. Married 65 years. Devoted father of Dr. Herbert (Lilliann) Fisher of San Mateo, CA, Jane (Larry) David of NYC, and Barbara (Constantine) Raitzky of Barto, PA. Brother of Sherry Silverman of Boca Raton, Florida. Grandchildren Colin, Cliff and Mallie Fisher. Established Fisher Bindery in Philadelphia, PA, 1933-1967, retired to Miami Beach, loved raising orchids and playing golf. He had a wonderful wicked sense of humor. He will be sorely missed.
