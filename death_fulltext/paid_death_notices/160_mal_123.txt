
        GLADSTEIN-Irving. On Tuesday, May 9, 2000 of West Caldwell, NJ. Beloved husband of Sylvia (nee Steinberger). Devoted father of Beverly Stern, Ned Gladstein and Lynne Mesznik. Adored brother of Sylvia Yanow and Doris Gordon. Loving grandfather of nine grandchildren. Funeral services conduced from Congregation Agudath Israel of West Essex, Caldwell, NJ on Wednesday, May 10, 2000 at 1PM. Interment Menorah Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. For information call Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 908-964-1500.
        GLADSTEIN-Irving. The Jewish Theological Seminary family records with sorrow the passing of Irving Gladstein, beloved father of our devoted friend and chairman of the Rabbinical School board of overseers, Ned Gladstein. Irving Gladstein imparted to his children a deep love for Judaism that played a central role in their lives. We extend our deepest sympathies to his son, Ned; his daughters, Beverly Stern and Lynne Mesznik; his wife, Sylvia Gladstein, and their nine grandchildren on their tragic loss. May his memory be for a blessing. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Chairman
