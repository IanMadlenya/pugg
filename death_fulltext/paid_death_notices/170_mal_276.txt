
        LOCKE-Richard Morson (Dick). Of Tampa, FL, died March 19, 2001 at home. He was 81. Dick Locke was a native of NYC & a veteran of the 7th Regiment. He is survived by his wife, Louise Crawford Brigham Locke, four children (Richard Jr., Peter, Lendra Hamre, and Margaret Bush), six grandchildren, & his sister Nancy Graber of Montclair, New Jersey. He will be buried in Bushnell, FL.
