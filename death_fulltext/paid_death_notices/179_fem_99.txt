
        SILVER-Elaine. AMIT is profoundly saddened by the passing of our dear friend and beloved sister of our generous benefactors, Marvin (Hadassah) Bienenfeld. A true woman of valor, Elaine Silver was very caring, compassionate and devoted to the well-being of our AMIT children in Israel. She will always be remembered for her vision and her professionalism. Of particular note, was Elaine Silver's production and choreography for more than a decade of AMIT's Convention Fashion Shows. Heartfelt condolences to her beloved husband, Dr. Walter Silver, children and grandchildren. Sondra Sokal, President Marvin Leff, Executive Dir
