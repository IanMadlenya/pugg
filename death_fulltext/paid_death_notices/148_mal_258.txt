
        WEPRIN-Joel. We are saddened by the loss of a very special man, Joel Weprin, on May 22, 1999. He brought love and sunshine into all our lives, and will always be remembered by his family, friends & the students that he influenced. He is survived by his wife, Amy (Kassin) & his children, Julia, Loy (Martin), Peter & Eric. He leaves his sister, Ruth, his brothers, Barry & Earle, and his grandchild, Conor Alexander. Services were held on Sunday, May 23.
