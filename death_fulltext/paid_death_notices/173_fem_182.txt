
        CONNELL-JoAnn Dallas. Died after a courageous battle with breast cancer on June 18 at Kendal in Kennett Square, PA. She is survived by five children, Thomas Hilbert, Jefferson Joseph, Ann Dallas Clarke, Nancy Day Connell, Abigail Sanford Connell and nine grandchildren. JoAnn lived in Princeton, NJ for thirty years. She attended Smith College and received an MFA from Columbia University. She was a world traveler, an opera lover, a docent of the Princeton Art Museum, and worked at the Institute for Advance Studies Library at Princeton University. In lieu of flowers donations to Doctors Without Borders.
