
        WHITE-Bernard S. The Israel Colloquium mourns the loss of one of its founders. We will miss his wise counsel and advice. A lifelong Zionist he was a leader of the Washington, D.C. Jewish Community and of many national Jewish organizations. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Ruth, his children and grandchildren. We will cherish his memory. Jacuues Torczyner, President Dr. Kitty Cohen, Vice-President
