
        DISTLER-Betsy, an extraordinary human being. The President, a Fellow, a Training Analyst and a Supervisor of the Institute for Psychoanalytic Training and Research. A creative person who was a source of love, a pillar of strength, and a well of understanding for her husband David, her sons Daniel and Josh, and countless friends, colleagues, patients and students, even through her ten year private battle with breast cancer. Her life will be a continuing inspiration. She will be dearly missed. Funeral service will be held Sunday at 1:45 PM, at the Plaza Memorial Chapel, at 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations to Betsy Distler Memorial Fund, at IPTAR.
