
        SILVER--Robert J. Passed away at his home in New York City on July 17, 2003 after a long illness. He was 75 years old. A graduate of the Taft School and Yale University, he was a stock specialist and member of the New York Stock Exchange for 53 years. A past president of the Buttonwood Club and a founding member of the Yale School of Management, Robert Silver is survived by his beloved wife of 47 years, Sandra Kadin Silver and his two children Susan deMenil and John Silver. In addition he was the loving father-in-law and grandfather to Francois deMenil, Heather Silver, John deMenil, Sophie deMenil, Conrad deMenil, Henry Silver and Stella Silver. Services Tuesday, July 22, 9:30AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65 St. Friends may call on Monday, July 21 from 4-7PM at the Silver's home. In lieu of flowers, contributions should be made to Lenox Hill Hospital, Department of Pulmonary Medicine.
        SILVER--Robert J. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Robert J. Silver, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation who was admired for his generosity and compassionate concern for the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Silver family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO >>AD#
