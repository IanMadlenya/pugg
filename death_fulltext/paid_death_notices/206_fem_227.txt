Friends may call on Wednesday, March 24, 2004 from 6PM to 8PM at the Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, 745 North Federal Highway, Delray Beach, FL. A funeral mass will be celebrated on Thursday, March 25, 2004, 10:30AM at St Vincent Ferrer Catholic Church, Delray Beach FL. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the NICU Fund @ Lankenau Hospital, Philadelphia, PA or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, NY, NY
