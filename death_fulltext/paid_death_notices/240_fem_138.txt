
        PORTE--Eunice, May 21, 1932 to January 2, 2007. Loving mother, sister, teacher, mentor, world-traveler, watercolorist, friend, and wife of Dr. Daniel Porte Jr. Regarded as a great friend and story-teller, she will be missed by friends and family worldwide. A tribute will be held in La Jolla February 25th.
