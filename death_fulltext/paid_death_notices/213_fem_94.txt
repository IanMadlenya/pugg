
        KERNAN--Una Greene. On September 30, 2004 in Guilford, CT, age 94. Mrs. Kernan was born in Dublin, Ireland in 1909. Mrs. Kernan is survived by her husband, her son and daughter-in-law Sean and Karen, of Stony Creek, her daughter Brigid, of Baltimore, and her sister Elizabeth Ballagh of Dublin. Memorial Mass November 15 at St. Thomas More Chapel of Yale University, 11 AM. Donations to PeacePeople, Fredheim, 224 Lisburn Rd, Belfast BT96GE, Northern Ireland.
