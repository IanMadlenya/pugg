
        BLAKELY--Nicholas Gervas. Retired Professor of Spanish Studies at Dutchess Community College in Poughkeepsie, NY, died unexpectedly last weekend at his home in New York City. He was 69 years old and had successfully fought multiple myloma for four years. His death was caused by pneumonia-related complications. Mr. Blakely was passionate about arts, literature and Spanish culture. Other interests of his included foreign languages, poetry and amateur photography. He is survived by his sister, Sara Sieker; his son, Alexander Blakely; his daughter, Nicole Blakely; and his grandson Kaimana Seely. They will miss him dearly. Friends and family are invited to attend a memorial and reception at Alta in the West Village, on Tuesday, March 29th, from 6:00-9:00 PM.
