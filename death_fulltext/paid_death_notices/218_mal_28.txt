
        DAYAN--Raymond. PhillipsVan Heusen Corporation mourns the loss of Raymond Dayan, our business partner and friend of over 40 years. Through his leadership, his company, Dayan Hermanos, built the Van Heusen brand into a leading apparel brand in Panama, but we admired him most for his dedication to family values, tradition and philanthropy. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, Linda, his children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Bruce J. Klatsky Chairman & CEO
