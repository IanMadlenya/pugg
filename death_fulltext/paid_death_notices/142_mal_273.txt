
        LEVINSON-Henry. 1907-1998. In Dania, FL. A remarkable man, a loving husband, a devoted father. Lucy Warshaw (Robert), June Nemchin (Robert), Rose Carelli (John). A proud grandfather, an adoring great-grandfather, a very distinguished graduate of Cooper Union. Hank loved NYC and spent most of his adult life making a better life for others in NY and everywhere else he traveled. Contributions in memory of Henry Levinson may be made to: The Cooper Union Alumni Fund, Class of 1929, Cooper Square, NY NY 10003.
