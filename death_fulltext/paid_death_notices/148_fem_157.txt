
        HICKS-Ruth Ilsley. 91, died May 9, 1999, in Menno-Haven, Chambersburg, PA. Born May 12, 1907, in Jamaica, Long Island, NY. Daughter of the late Shirley N.C. and Minnie Clarke Hicks. Graduate of Vassar College and Johns Hopkins University. From 1933-34, she was Assistant Curator of Classical Art at the Brooklyn Museum. She was Professor Emeritus of Classics at Wilson College, Chambersburg; and received the Linback Teaching Award and Eminent Educators Award. Burial will be in Norland Cemetery, Chambersburg. Memorials to Classics Collection, c/o Wilson College, 1015 Philadelphia Ave., Chambersburg, PA 17201.
