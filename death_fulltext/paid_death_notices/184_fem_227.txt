
        GOLDMAN-Judith. Beloved wife of Harold. Adored daughter of Rabbi Gilbert and the late Libby Klaperman and stepdaughter of Susan Klaperman. Cherished sister of Joel and Renee Klaperman, Frieda Klaperman, Carol and Ira Morrow and step-sisters Beth Alter and Shira and Tuvia Book. Devoted aunt of Gabriel, Jeremy, Jessica and Harrison. Loving niece of Bernice Wigder and Sylvia and Irving Tuchman. Always caring, giving and gracious, she charmed all who knew her and illuminated their lives. Judy was our dearest treasure and a constant source of joy and blessing. Her courage and determination and love for God, her people and Israel are her eternal legacy. Funeral today, 11:30AM at Congregation Beth Sholom, Lawrence, NY. Shiva at the Klaperman residence, 64 Muriel Ave, Lawrence.
        GOLDMAN-Judith. Congregation Beth Sholom records the passing of Judith Goldman, beloved daughter of our esteemed Rabbi Emeritus Dr. Gilbert Klaperman. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Rabbi Kenneth N. Hain Danny Hiller, President Gary Miller, Chairman of the Board
        GOLDMAN-Judith. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the sister of its devoted member, Carol Morrow. May her entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
        GOLDMAN-Judy.
