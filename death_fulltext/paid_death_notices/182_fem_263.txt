
        TOBIN-Janet Williams (formerly Janet Williams Medina), 85. Of Delray Beach passed away peacefully on Monday, March 25, 2002. She was born in NYC, daughter of the late Herbert D. and Nina Williams. Mrs. Tobin had been President of the Board of Trustees of The Spence School, the first woman on the Board of the Legal Aid Society of NY, and served on the Boards of the YWCA of the City of NY, the Grosvenor Neighborhood House & many others. She & her late husband, Harold R. Medina, Jr. moved to the Country Club of FL. in 1967. They were married for 53 years. She is survived by her devoted husband of nine years, Joshua B. Tobin, sons Harold III and Robert, and daughter Ann Medina, and grandchildren Scott Medina, Elizabeth Whitney and Rachel Medina, in addition to her first great-grandchild, George Whitney. A small private family service will be held. Donations can be made to The Critical Care Fund of the Bethesda Hospital Foundation at P.O. Box 2200, Delray Beach, Florida 33447, or to The First Presbyterian Church of Delray Beach at 33 Gleason St, Delray Beach, Florida 33483.
