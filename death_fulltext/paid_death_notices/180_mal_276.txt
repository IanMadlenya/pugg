
        LYNN-Marvin S. Age 73, of Great Neck, NY died peacefully in his home on Wednesday, January 16, 2002. After 50 years of marriage, he remains adored by his wife Nancy; beloved father of Amy Freed & Richard Lynn; treasured grandfather of Janelle & Hope Lynn and Julia & Gregory Lynn; dear brother of Renee Secof. He will always be remembered for his devotion to his family. Services will be held at Temple Beth-El of Great Neck on Friday, January 18th at 11:00 AM.
