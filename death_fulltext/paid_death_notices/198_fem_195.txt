
        BAUMRITTER -- Dorothy Doob. Distinguished professor of speech pathology at Hunter College, NYC, for thirty-six years, died peacefully. She was inducted into The Hall of Fame at Hunter College in 1975. She was involved in several organizations in the treatment and care of children and adults with cerebral palsy. She was a Founding Member of The Scholarship and Welfare Fund of The Alumni Association of Hunter College, where an award is presented to a student each year in the name of her loving husband of forty-five years, Louis Baumritter. After Dorothy's retirement, she continued private practice & taught poetry at West Palm Community College, FL, where she was also honored & admired by her students. >>AD#
