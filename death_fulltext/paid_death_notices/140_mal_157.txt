
        SCHLOSSBERG-Michael. On September 9, 1998, at the age of 100. Beloved husband of the late Pauline. Dear brother of Phillip and Hyman Schlosberg and Mollie Orloff and of the late Irving, Charlie, George and Sam Schlosberg. Adored father of Elinor Amlen and Brenda Tepper. Precious uncle and great-uncle. Cherished grandfather of David and Jennifer Amlen, Jonathan and Elizabeth Tepper and father-in-law of Seymour Amlen and Jesse Tepper. Adored great-grandfather of Carly Amlen. A very thoughtful and generous man and wonderful friend. He will be deeply missed.
