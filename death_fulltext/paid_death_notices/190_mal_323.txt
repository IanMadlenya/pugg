
        LUSTBADER-Fred. Age 82, of New York, New York and Boynton Beach, FL. Passed away peacefully November 26, 2002 in Florida. Eagle Scout, World War II veteran (awarded the Purple Heart and Bronze Star for service in the Battle of the Bulge), and co-founder of Fabco Enterprises, a retail shoe and real estate concern. Survived by his beloved wife Pearl, and adored sons Ned and Ken. He was a great husband, dad, and friend. Contributions may be made to the National Parkinsons Foundation, 10 Union Square East, Suite 5L, New York, New York 10003.
