
        WYLER-Wilfred. Died January 29, 2002 at age 93, after a long illness. Survived by his wife of 63 years, Marjorie, his daughters, Ruth W. Messinger and Dr. Barbara W. Gold, five grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Founder in 1937 and senior partner of Wilfred Wyler and Company, CPAs. His community commitment and loyalty are reflected by his half century as a Trustee and Treasurer of the Jewish Home and Hospital, and as Chairman, Treasurer and Officer of the Joint Retirement Board, and member, Board of Overseers for the Jewish Theological Seminary. He was a graduate of Harvard College, B.S. 1928 and NYU Graduate School of Business, M.B.A. 1934 and attended Columbia Business School 1935 to 1938.
