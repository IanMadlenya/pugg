
        SIPPERLY-Mary Shaw. Of Stony Brook, NY. On October 10, 1999, peacefully after a sudden illness. The 23rd member of her family to graduate from Mount Holyoke College, she worked as an editor of children's books for Charles Scribner and Sons in Manhattan. She is survived by her husband, David P. Sipperly; daughters Margaret S. Salyer of Oklahoma City and Anne S. Braswell of Ocala, Fla.; granddaughters Margaret C. Salyer and Molly S. Braswell. A memorial service will be held at 11am on Friday, October 15 at the Stony Brook Community Church, Stony Brook, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the John T. Mather Memorial Hospital Foundation or Mount Holyoke College, South Hadley, Mass.
