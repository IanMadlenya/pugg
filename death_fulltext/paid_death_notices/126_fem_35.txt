
        LUNZER - Shoshana B. (nee Skidelsky), July 2, 1997. Beloved wife of Julius Peter. Loving mother of Benjamin, Jay, Francesca & David. Cherished grandmother, dear sister. Services were held. Shiva through Wednesday morning, July 9, 1997 at home.
        LUNZER-Shoshana. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of its longtime devoted member. We fondly recall her great chesed, her faith, and her strong devotion to her family. We extend our condolences to her beloved husband Peter, to her brothers Harry and Irving Skydell, to her children, and to all the members of her family. May they all be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
        LUNZER-Shoshana. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun, its Men's Club, Sisterhood and Couples Club mourn the passing of the beloved mother of their devoted member and Gabai Jay Lunzer and dear sister of their dedicated members Rita Kaufman and Adrian Skydell. We extend heartfelt condolences to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Fred Distenfeld, President
        LUNZER-Shoshana. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of Shoshana Lunzer, sister of our generous benefactor, Rita Kaufman and mother of our parent, Jay Lunzer. We extend heartfelt sympathy to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Principal David N. Kahn Chairman, Board of Trustees
        LUNZER-Shoshana. W.S.I.S. sorrowfully mourns the passing of Mrs. Shoshana Lunzer, the much admired sister of our beloved member, Chana Chanowitz and the entire family. May the Comforter of Zion and Jerusalem comfort the grieving family. Moshe Morduchowitz, Rabbi Richard Wigler, President
