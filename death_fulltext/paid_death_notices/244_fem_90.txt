
        BORD--Marilyn. The family and friends of Marilyn (''Mickey'') Bord mourn her death on May 3rd. During and after her career as a special education teacher, she volunteered for the Alvin Ailey Dance Company. In the 1950's she dyed and sewed costumes. Later she became the company's archivist. She also volunteered with the South Street Seaport and a local veterinarian. She loved to attend theatre, opera and concerts and travel. She is survived by her brother, Marshall, and wife Linda, her cousin Bruce and wife, Dale, cousin Susan and husband, Dick, cousins Mindy and Richard and their children and grandchildren.
