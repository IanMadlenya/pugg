
        PARKER-Frances This 14th day of May saw the passing of Frances Parker, devoted wife of the late Irving Parker, loving mother of James Parker and the late Joan Cohen, adoring grandmother of Kevin and Bret, and loyal friend to so many whose lives she touched. Her strength, wisdom and wit will be missed by all who enjoyed her friendship and by the family who cherished her for all of her 82 years. James Parker Bret Parker Kevin Cohen
