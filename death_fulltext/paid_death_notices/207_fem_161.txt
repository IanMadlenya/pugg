
        PARK -- Rosemary. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Barnard College are saddened by the death of Rosemary Park, who provided inspiring leadership as President of Barnard from 19621967. A vital figure in higher education and a leading advocate for women's advancement, she was a wise and thoughtful administrator and scholar who devoted her life to students at Barnard and at Connecticut College, where she was professor and president, and at the University of California at Los Angeles, where she served as vice chancellor until her retirement in 1970. Barnard expresses its deep sympathy to her family, friends and colleagues. Judith Shapiro, President Barnard College
