
        MARGULIES-Walter. Died on Feb. 3, 2002 in Palo Alto, California at age 92. He was buried next to his beloved wife, Phyllis, of 67 years who died in January 2001. Walter graduated from New York Law School in 1930 and enjoyed a private practice in New York City interrupted only by his three years service in The Army during World War II. He was also the attorney for and a Director of Conair Corporation. His two sons, Jay and Richard, five grandchildren and one great-grandchild survive him. A memorial service will be held at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 12:45 P.M. on Sunday, February 24th.
