
        SCHNEELOCH-Martin, 100, of Southport, CT died January 25, 2000. He is survived by his beloved wife of 75 years, Marion Schneeloch; three daughters, Helen-Claire Martin of Gansevoort, NY; Nancy K. Nelson of Boulder, CO, Linda Jones of Greenwich, CT; 5 grandchildren and 2 great-grandchildren. A memorial Service will be held at 4 P.M. at the Shaughnessey F.H., 50 Reef Rd., Fairfield, CT on February 4, 2000. Donations may be made in his memory to the Pequot Library 720 Pequot Road, Southport, CT. 06490.
