
        BISSELL-Tallman. Died on Thursday, Dec. 30, 1999 of complications of pneumonia in New York City. He was 80. Mr. Bissell is survived by his three children: Galey Bissell Sergio-Castelvetere, John Tallman Bissell, & Katherine Maloney. He is also survived by a brother Louis Bissell, Jr. and a sister Amoret Bissell Mayberry. A memorial service will be held in New York City in April.
