
        CHIAT-Jay. The Trustees and Staff of Dia Art Foundation mourn the loss of our friend and Trustee, Jay Chiat. A great supporter of Dia, its collection and programs, Jay was tireless in his commitment to contemporary art, artists, and architects. Jay will be deeply missed for his friendship and spirit and his personal and intellectual engagement with contemporary art. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Leonard Riggio, Chairman Michael Govan, Director
        CHIAT-Jay. We remember the vivid and rare intelligence of Jay Chiat. Gaetano Pesce and Ruth Shuman
