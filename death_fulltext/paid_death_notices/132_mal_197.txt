
        BULLOUGH-Pierre. Age 44. On January 14, 1998 in Southampton New York, of complications due to AIDS. Beloved son of Henriette and the late Arnold Bullough. Dear brother of Claudine Achter, Eugene, Jeffrey and David Bullough and special nieces and nephews. He is survived by his longtime companion Mel Katzman. He possessed the ability to see the simple as special, thereby bringing pleasure to all who loved and knew him. Funeral services will be held in Salt Lake City, Utah, Saturday, January 17. Donations in his memory may be made to the David Rogers, M.D. Center for HIV/AIDS Care, 335 Meeting House Lane, Southampton, New York 11968.
