
        CHAPPELL -- George She pard III, 70 died August 20 in Chicago, IL. Mr. Chappell graduated from Groton and Yale University. He was a lifelong resident of Lake Forest, IL. He was a graphic designer and photographer. He is survived by his wife, Patsy, his children, George S. Chappell, IV and Sally Chappell; his stepchildren, Melissa (Brett) Kedzior, Sean Kennedy, Brett Chappell and Lulu Cannon; his sisters, Barbara Copello and Susan (Ted) McCabe; and three grandchildren, Miles Kedzior, Caroline Kedzior and Casey Quast. A memorial service was held in Lake Forest. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to Cancer Program Northwestern Memorial Foundation 676 N. St. Clair, Suite 2050, Chicago, IL 60611.
