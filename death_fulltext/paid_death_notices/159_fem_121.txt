
        SCHAGER-Eunice Shevlin, 81, of Cleveland, Ohio and Dresden Mills, Maine, at home on April 8. Born in Dresden Mills, Maine, on October 14, 1919, daughter of Vincent James Shevlin of Brooklyn and Dresden Mills, and Bernice Woodward Shevlin. Survived by her husband Richard J. Schager, five children, Catherine Dudley of Matawan, New Jersey, Margaret Susor of Oregon, Ohio, Richard Schager, Jr. of New York City, Marilyn Baker of Mentor, Ohio, and Vincent Schager of Atlanta, Georgia, and two sisters, Geraldine Swenson of Seattle, Washington, and Catherine Shevlin of Dresden, Maine. Predeased by her brother, Vincent James Shevlin, Jr.
