
        SITT--Eddie M. The entire Magen David Yeshivah family are deeply saddened by the loss of our highly respected past president and cherished friend, Eddie M. Sitt. Our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife Gloria, his children Rochelle, Morris, Jeffrey, David, his grandchildren and the entire family.
