
        CHAVE-Paul T. Age 87, November 2, 2000. Survived by his beloved wife Rita M. Chave (nee Harrington), pre-deceased by daughter Anne Marie in 1944, also survived by several nieces and nephews. He was an executive in the textile industry in Manhattan for 50 years and most recently CEO of Chave & Earley, Inc. Visiting at John Krtil Funeral Home Saturday from 7-9, Sunday 2-4 and 7-9. Funeral Mass 10am on Monday at St. John the Evangelist Roman Catholic Church, 55 St at 1st Ave. Interment following at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to Jacob Perlow Hospice of Beth Isreal Medical Center would be appreciated.
