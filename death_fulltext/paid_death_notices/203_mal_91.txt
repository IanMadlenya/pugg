
        MILLHISER -- Ross. Altria Group, Inc. (formerly Philip Morris Companies Inc.) notes with great sorrow the death of Ross R. Millhiser, the former Vice Chairman of Philip Morris Incorporated and a former director of Philip Morris Companies Inc. An energetic and passionate businessperson with broad-ranging interests, Mr. Millhiser played a central role in the growth and development of our companies during a 40year career. In extending their deepest sympathy to his family, the Altria Board and employees of the Altria companies pay warm tribute to Mr. Millhiser's memory, his many accomplishments, and his service to his country. Mr. Millhiser is survived by his brother, four children and eight grandchildren. A graveside service will be held at Hollywood Cemetery, 412 South Cherry Street, Richmond, VA., Wednesday, Dec. 10, at 3:00 p.m. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Greater Richmond chapter of the American Red Cross, P.O. Box 655, Richmond, Va., 23205
