
        YACHNES-Eleanor, M.D. On July 29, 2000. Beloved mother of Candy Systra, Laura Systra and Andrew Yachnes. Loving mother-in-law to Michael, grandmother to Daniel and Alexander, aunt to Matt, Lucy and Emily Gertz, Paul and Linda Yachnes. Psychoanalyst and vigorous lover of life. She will be missed by family and friends. Services August 1, at 1 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Doctors Without Borders.
        YACHNES-Eleanor. The Boards of Directors of the American Institute for Psychoanalysis and the Association for the Advancement of Psychoanalysis of the Karen Horney Psychoanalytic Institute and Center note with deep sorrow the passing of Eleanor Yachnes, M.D., esteemed psychoanalyst, sypervisor, teacher and faculty member. Her spirit, contributions and depth will be sorely missed. Henry A. Paul, M.D., Pres., AIP Judith Fox-Fliesser, M.D., President, AAP
