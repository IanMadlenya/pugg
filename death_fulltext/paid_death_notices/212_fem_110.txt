Special aunt of Alexandra, Larry and Daniel Jeydel. Devoted aunt of Susan Stephens, Wendy Bachrach, Jonathan Gordon, Tricia Allgood and Alison Watters. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to East Hill Woods Memorial Fund, 611 East Hill Rd, Southbury, CT 06488 or a charity of your choice.
