
        PARKE-Robert Beatty. Beloved husband of Margaret. Died at his home in Bedford, NY, on August 11. He served as a B-17 pilot during WW II and flew transports to Japan and Greenland in post-war activities. He was awarded two Air Medals and a Distinguished Flying Cross. In 1952 he entered the aviation publishing field, spending 35 years with Flying magazine and served as its editor and publisher for 15 years. In 1978 he became associated with Businesses/Commercial Aviation magazine, working on assignments both in this country and abroad. He retired in 1997 at the age of 80. He is survived by his son James and his daughter Robin, and eight grandchildren. He will be remembered with gratitude and joy. A memorial service is planned for September 12 at 3:30 PM at St. Mary's Church in Bedford. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Hospice Care in Westchester and Putnam, 100 South Bedford Rd., Mt. Kisco, NY 10549.
