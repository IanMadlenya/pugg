
        MASLOW - Lester. Died peacefully at 91. Served in the U.S. Army as an officer in WWII. Father of Jane and James. Grandfather of Jamie. Stepfather of Patricia Sheridan and Michael Cantor. Brother of Arthur and Robert. Longtime member of Fresh Meadow and Boca Rio Country Clubs. President Beth Manufacturing for more than 50 years. Strong supporter of UJA/Federation throughout his life. Chapel service Monday, June 5, 2006, 10 a.m. at Frank E. Campbell, corner of E. 81 St. and Madison Ave, NY, NY, 212-288-3500.
