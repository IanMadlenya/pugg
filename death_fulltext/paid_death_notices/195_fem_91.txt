
        BERKOWITZ-Gertrude (Nana). Great Neck, NY. Died on April 13. As a 97-year old, she was always the life of the party. She will be sorely missed by her children, Norma Polatnick, Janet Rudnick and Sammy Berkowitz, her seven grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Services Tuesday, 12 noon, at RiversideNassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
