
        FINKELSTEIN-Irwin. The Officers and Directors of Metropolitan Jewish Health System sadly mourn the untimely passing of Irwin Finkelstein, beloved brother of our esteemed fellow Board Member, Dr. Paul Finkelstein. May his legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom he touched. We pray that his fond memories will sustain you and the love and prayers of those who care, will comfort you. We share in your loss. Our profound sympathy andsincere condolences to the family. Mark L. Goldstein, Chairman of the Board Eli S. Feldman, President, Chief Executive Officer
