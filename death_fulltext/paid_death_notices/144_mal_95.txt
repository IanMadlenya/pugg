
        THORNTON-Richard. Died on January 4th in Selden, LI. He was born in the Bronx in 1941. He worked in advertising for JTJ Printing Corporation in New York City. He was the son of Arthur and Helen Jackowicz Thornton, both predeceased. He was the widower of Susan Thornton. He is survived by a brother, Robert A. Thornton of Washingtonville, NY, a niece, Kerriann Thornton and a nephew, Robert M. Thornton. Services will be held at the David T. Ferguson Funeral Home on Thursday, January 7th from 2-4, 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be offered on Friday, January 8th at 10:00 A.M., at St. Mary's Church. Interment is at St. Mary's Cemetery, Washingtonville, New York.
