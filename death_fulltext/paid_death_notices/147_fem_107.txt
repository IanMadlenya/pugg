
        LEVITZ-WERTLIEB-Lillian. Age 80. Founder of Colonial Terrace, a well-known social catering establishment, died on April 10, 1999, in Delray Beach, FL. Lillian is survived by her husband Jacob Wertlieb, and her children Sheila Drogy, Jack Levitz, Rosanne Elkins, and Norman Levitz; Michael and Fred Wertlieb; and her thirteen grandchildren, and four greatgrandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the First Hebrew Congregation of Peekskill, 11 AM, on Monday.
