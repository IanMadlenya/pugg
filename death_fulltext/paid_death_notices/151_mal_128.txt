
        ABRAMS-Joseph. Of Bedminster, NJ, 63, died August 11 of cancer. He was born in New York City on February 2, 1936. A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School and an MBA from New York University, he was a founder of AGS Computers Inc., and served as its President and Chief Operating Officer until his retirement in 1990. He was active in several charities including The Valerie Fund and was a board member of The American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee and was on the executive committee and served as Vice President of the United Jewish Federation of MetroWest, NJ. He is survived by his beloved wife Judith, loving children Brad and Douglas and Miranda and nephews and nieces Robert and Janet and Penny and Joel and Andrea and Virgina. The funeral will be held at 2PM, Thursday, August 12 at Bernheim Apter Goldsticker Funeral Chapel, 1600 Springfield Ave. Maplewood, NJ. 973-763-3505 for directions. Donations should be made to the The Valerie Fund, 2101 Milburn Ave., Maplewood, New Jersey, 07040.
