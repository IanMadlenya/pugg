
        HELLER-Constance Quint. Formerly of Newton, MA, New Rochelle and Harrison, NY, died in Sarasota, FL. July 9. Beloved wife of George, loving mother of Bruce, Betsy, Alan and Martha, grandmother of ten. Graduate of ColbySawyer, Class of '43. Dedicated artist who experimented with various techniques and devoted time to foster the ideals of art groups including the Florida Artist Group, Womens' Caucus For Art, The Longboat Key Art Center, Sarasota Art Association & The National Association of Women Artists. Memorial service will be held July 11 at 5:30PM, at the Selby Gardens in Sarasota. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Longboat Key Art Center, 6860 Longboat Drive South, Longboat Key, FL. 34228.
        HELLER-Constance. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow, the passing of our beloved member, Constance Heller. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to her family. Andrew J. Frankel, President Judith Weil, Secretary
