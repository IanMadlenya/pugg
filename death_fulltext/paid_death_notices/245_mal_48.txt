
        MILLER--Bernard,died June 1, 2007 at age 91. The owner and manager of Miller and Miller Real Estate Company in Manhattan. He was a comedy writer in the early days of television. A graduate of Harvard Law School, he was the writer of Captain Marvel Comics for Fawcett Publications. He donated the third largest private collection of books of humor to Brown University. He was also an active fundraiser for many local and international charities and served on the Town of Harrison Planning Board for 10 years. Survived by his beloved wife Harriet(Schutz), children Robert Miller (Tamora) and Steven Miller (Astrid), grandchildren, Siri, Cara and Marisa, greatgrandson Gavin Vaquer, and siblings Saul Miller, George Miller(Roz) and Hannah Wolff. Funeral service will be held at JCC of Harrison with interment following at Sharon Gardens.
