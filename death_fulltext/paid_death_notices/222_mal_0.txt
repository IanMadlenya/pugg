
        DRUCKER--David L., 73 years of age, a longtime dentist in Queens, died on Monday, June 27th, 2005 in Portland, ME. He was born in Brooklyn and was a graduate of Queens College and Columbia Dental School. He is survived by his wife Barbara Schulhoff Drucker of Portland, ME, two daughters Claire and Julie Drucker, both of California, and one brother Edmond of Great Neck. Also surviving is one step-son and two step-daughters. It was the family's request that there be no formal funeral service.
