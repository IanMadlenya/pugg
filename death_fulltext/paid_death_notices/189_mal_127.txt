
        RAFF-Leonard. On October 8, 2002. Loving father of Susan and her husband Kevin Pflaumer, adoring grandfather of Jared and Sofia, dear brother of Irma Nemoytin and the late Siena. He worked for CBS News in New York for 53 years as a broadcast editor. He covered history making events like the Vietnam War, the Kennedy Assassination, President Nixon's visit to China and the Iran hostage crisis. Veteran of WWII proudly serving on the U.S.S New Jersey. Service Friday 2PM at the ''Riverside'', 76th St., and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
