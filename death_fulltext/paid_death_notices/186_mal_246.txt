
        WASHBURN-Alan L. Of New York and Easthampton, died unexpectedly on July 22, 2002 at the age of 76. Beloved husband of Joan for nearly 49 years, father of Brian and Susan, brother of Doris W. Angst and her husband Charles and father-in-law of Kerry Greene. Born in Mt. Kisco, NY in 1926 and grew up in Pleasantville, NY. He proudly served his country in WW II as a paratrooper with the 11th Airborne and in the occupational forces in Japan, graduated from the University of Denver and married Joan Thomas in 1953. After a 30 year career in production at Time Magazine, he retired and became VP of the Joan T. Washburn Gallery, NY. His winning smile, irreverant wit and dogged determination captured everyone's hearts. The animals he so loved, Missy, Satra, Whitney, Tweedy, Mac and Page are with him now. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Phelps Memorial Hospital, Sleepy Hollow, NY. A private memorial in Easthampton will be held on a date to be announced.
