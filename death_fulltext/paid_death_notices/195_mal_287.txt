
        VanALEN-William L., on April 24, in Palm Beach, Florida, at 95. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Kent VanAlen for 71 years and beloved father of William L. VanAlen Jr., James L. VanAlen II, and Cassandra Ludington. Grandfather of nine and great-grandfather of seven, who all admired and adored him for his love and wisdom. Architect, sportsman & philanthropist, he worked hard, played well & helped many. What a run!
