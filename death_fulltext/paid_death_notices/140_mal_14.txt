
        ATLAS-Theodore, 85. Of Bal Harbour, Florida. Formerly of New York City. Passed away Monday, August 31, 1998 following a stroke. Devoted husband of his late wife Lillian, devoted father of Robert of Rockville, MD., and David of Red Bank, NJ, sadly mourned by his grandchildren Lauren, Brian, Anthony, Shira and Etai and loving brother Martin of Brooklyn and his dvoted nurse and friend Pancia Gerald. Mr. Atlas, a retired tax accountant, was a long time active volunteer for the Shul of Bal Harbour. Graveside services to be held 1:15PM, Thursday, September 3, at Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, N.J. The cemetery can be contacted for further information at 201-262-1100.
