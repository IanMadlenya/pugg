
        ABRAMSON-Samuel. Cherished and beloved husband of almost fifty years to Jeanne (nee Quint). Devoted and respected father of Radine and Bill Spier, Ellis and Helene Abramson. Adored grandfather of Rosalyn, David, Michael and Jason; step-grandfather to Jonathan Spier, Debby and Bruce Prince, Evan and Malka Spier; and G.G. Sam to Zev and Benjamin. Brother of Dr. David A. Abramson. A most honored brother-inlaw, uncle and great-uncle. Sam, a long-time educator in New York City, was a gentle soul whose compassion, humor & love of music he shared with all who knew him. He will be deeply missed.
