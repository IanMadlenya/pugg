
        TORRES-Roberto. Of Queens, NY most of his life, died August 29, 1997 at Skyview Rehabilitation and Nursing Home in Wallingford, CT. He was the beloved husband of Nery Yero Torres and is survived by his children, Robert Torres, Jr., Hector Torres, Walter Torres, Edward Quintela, Elena Dorsey and Nancy Vazquez. He was formerly employed with Marriott Essex House in NYC until his recent retirement. Our beloved husband, father, grandfather and greatgrandfather dedicated his life to his family and friends. He remains in our hearts and minds forever.
