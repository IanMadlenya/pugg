
        LEVINE--Howard R., on July 6th. Devoted father, passionate lawyer, lifelong New Yorker, he is survived by sons Scott of NY and Todd of Fridley, MN, as well as former wife DeLane, also of MN, and brother Robert of Titusville, FL. He was a proud alumnus of Horace Mann '51, University of Pennsylvania '55, and St. John's Law School '58. Memorial service will be held Friday, July 9th, at 2:30 P.M., at Plaza Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave and 91st St. Contributions to Stephen Wise Free Synagogue.
