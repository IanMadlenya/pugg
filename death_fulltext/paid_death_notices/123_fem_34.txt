
        WITTEN-Kathleen R., 79, widow of John D. Witten and daughter of Raymond and Regina Rubicam, died at Manatawny Manor, Pottstown, PA. Saturday, March 29. Mrs. Witten, who was born in Philadelphia, was a resident of Bronxville, NY, Portland, CT and West Boothbay Harbor, ME. She was a graduate of the Rosemary Hall School, Vassar College and Columbia University. She was a reference Librarian at Wesleyan University, Sarah Lawrence College and the State University of New York at Purchase. Mrs. Witten is survived by two sons, two sisters, a brother and two granddaughters. Burial will be in Long Island National Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY, on May 12, 1997 at 1:00 P.M. The family suggests that memorial contributions be made to the Sierra Club.
