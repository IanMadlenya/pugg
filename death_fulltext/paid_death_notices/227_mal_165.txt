
        FREIDSON--Eliot died December 14th at the Laguna Grove Zen Hospice in San Francisco. He leaves his wife, Helen Giambruni, his children, Jane and Matthew Freidson, daughter in-law RachelBurr, grandchildren Sam, Molly, Charlie and Conrad, and sisters Adele Feldman and Ruth Kavesh. A distinguished Professor and writer on the Sociology of professions Eliot was a pioneer in the field of Medical Sociology. He was Professor Emeritus at NYU, and the author of numerous books including the award winning ''Profession of Medicine''. Contributions in his honor may be sent to Electronic Frontier Foundation, Doctors Without Borders or Human Rights Watch.
