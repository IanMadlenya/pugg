
        de LEON--Raymond, 79, died December 16 in Gorham, ME. WW II veteran. Longtime social studies teacher in NYC high schools and Special Assistant to the Chancellor for Bilingual Education. Avid reader and conversationalist. Survived by daughter Andrea de Leon, her husband Todd Burrowes, and children Haley and Carter Burrowes, all of New Gloucester, ME, and his sister, Gloria English and husband Arthur of New City, NY.
