
        LIEBERSON - WOLFE - Brigit ta. The Board of Directors of the George Balanchine Foundation mourn the passing of our friend and fellow Board Member, Brigitta LiebersonWolfe, professionally known as Vera Zorina. Her contribution to dance and theatre on the ballet stage, on Broadway, and in Hollywood, enhanced by her kindness and personal modesty, will remain an inspiration. Barbara Horgan, Chairman Paul H. Epstein, President Lourdes Lopez, Exec. Dir. Nancy Reynolds, Dir. of Rsch.
