
        O'CONNELL-Adele Q., (nee Quilgan), 91. Of Little Silver, New Jersey, formerly of New York City, NY on Tuesday, October 30, 2001. Wife of the late James Timothy O'Connell, former Under Secretary of Labor during the Eisenhower Administration. Mother of (U.S. Army Ret.) Col. James T. O'Connell, Jr. and daughter-inlaw Pauline O'Connell, Katherine O'Connell, Anne Latona and sonin-law Donald Latona, Dorothy Cherry and Mary O'Connell and son-in-law Peter Grua. Grandmother of ten, great grandmother of seven. Visitation at the John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Avenue, Red Bank, NJ Friday 2-4 & 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday, November 3, 2001 at 9AM at Church of the Nativity, Hance Road, Fairhaven, NJ. Burial St. Raymond's R.C. Cemetery, Bronx, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to Mount St. Vincent's College, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Riverdale, NY 10471, or the Little Silver First Aid Squad, 543 Prospect Avenue, Little Silver, NJ 07739 or the Visiting Nurse Association of Central Jersey, 141 Bodman Place, Red Bank, N.J. 07701.
