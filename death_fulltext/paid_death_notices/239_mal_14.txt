
        STAMM--Julian Lawrence Dr. of Scarsdale, NY age 90 passed away on November 30th, 2006 due to heart failure. Beloved husband of Caryl, father of Patricia, Linda, and Cathy, grandfather of Jason, Jennifer, Justin, Ashley, Adam, and Alexis, father-inlaw of Jerry Kaufman, and John Willig, son of Emanual, and Rebecca Stamm. Eldest brother of Lois, Henry and Eugene. Julian earned his B.S. at Columbia University, and his M.D. at the University of Arkansas Medical School. He served as a veteran in World War II, in which he achieved the rank of Captain. Dr. Stamm became a psychiatrist, psychoanalyst and graduated from New York Psychoanalytic Institute, where he continued to serve as a training analyst. He published extensively and was internationally known in the psychoanalytic field. He was also recognized by the American Psychiatric Association for his lifetime of achievement. He was a man of great intellect, and was passionate about his life, family, work, nature, and music. A chapel service will be held on Sunday 10am at Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave, Scarsdale, NY 10583. Interment to follow at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Christopher Reeves Foundation for Stem Cell Research.
