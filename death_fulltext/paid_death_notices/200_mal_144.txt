
        RIFKIN--Bernard M. Beloved brother of Arthur and Lois. Uncle of Beth, Terry and Leah. Great-uncle of Judah and Keli, Alana and David. He was the Executive Vice President of Ticor Title Insurance Company and past Chief Counsel of Title Guarantee New York. A past Chairman of the Real Property Section of the New York State Bar Association, he was a recent awardee for professionalism by the organization. He was a member of the American College of Real Estate Lawyers. An ardent Zionist and a longtime member of the Zionist Organization of America, he served as national Chairman of the finance committee. Bernard met life's challenges with courage and grace. We miss him. The Rifkin Family
