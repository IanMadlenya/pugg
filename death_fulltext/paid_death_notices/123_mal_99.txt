
        MESKIN-Edward, of New York City. Died April 8, 1997. Husband of Ruth. Father of Stephen, Jacob and Benjamin. Brother of Freda Oster. Grandfather of Aaron and Sarah. Friend of all he met. A gentle person but a tenacious fighter for labor and all humanity. Memorial service will be held on Sunday, April 20, at 2:00 P.M., at The New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64th Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Jewish Currents magazine, 22 East 17th Street, Suite 601, New York, New York 10003.
