
        BROWN-Honour Dickerman. Of Southbury, CT, formerly of NYC & Washington, CT, died Waterbury Hospital, Aug. 31, 2001. Daughter of the late William Carter Dickerman and Alice Carter Dickerman. She is survived by sister Cornelia Dickerman Jahncke, by daughters Alice Brown Carroll and Sara Brown Maclin, and son James Oliver Brown Jr. Also survived by seven grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Graduated from Ethel Walker School, attended Bryn Mawr College, & graduated from The Juilliard School of Music. Member of The Colonial Dames of America, the Colony Club, and former board member of Virginia Day Nursery.
