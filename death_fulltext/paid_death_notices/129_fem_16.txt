
        KAI-Kockmay Irene. Passed away 9/27/97. Beloved wife of Heng Tan. Loving mother of Quinnie Sg Tan. Born 10/20/50. Received her degree in Gemology, Hong Kong Baptist College. Member of Fellowship of Gemological Assocation of Great Britain. FGA/ GIA appraisal diploma. Owner of Abacus Jewelry, Inc. Truthful, sincere, lofty character, diligent, dynamic her whole life, but the killing illness shattered her dream. Burial on Saturday, October 4, at 10 AM, Evergreen Cemetery, Brooklyn.
