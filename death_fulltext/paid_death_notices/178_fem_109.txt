
        FREIBERG-Janet. On November 8, 2001. Loving mother of Jill and Edward. Her goodness, warmth and humor will be greatly missed by her many friends and family. Visiting Saturday 7-9 P.M. Service Sunday, 11:45 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
        FREIBERG-Janet. November 8th, 2001. We deeply mourn the passing of Janet Freiberg, long serving Board Member of the Northside Center for Child Development. Her warm, kind, generous and sensitive nature will be missed by all who knew her. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to her family and friends. Board of Directors, Staff and the Children and Families of Northside
