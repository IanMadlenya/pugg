
        ZIMROTH--Ruth, age 85, died September 20, 2004, in Bridgeport, CT. She is survived by her beloved husband, Sol Zimroth, and her children, Alice Kelly and her husband, George, Peter Zimroth and his wife, Estelle Parsons; grandchildren, David, Matthew, Rachel & Abraham; brother & sisters, David Sadowsky, Sara Trabish and Estelle Rynston. Funeral services will take place on Wednesday, September 22nd, at 11:00 AM, at Congregation B'nai Israel, 2710 Park Ave. in Bridgeport, CT., with interment following at B'nai Israel Cemetery in Monroe, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to the Social Action Fund of Cong. B'nai Israel or to a charity of one's choice.
