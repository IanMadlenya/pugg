
        SURINACH-Carlos. On November 12, 1997, 82, of New Haven, CT., formerly of Miami, FL., and New York. The officers and executives of BMI mourn the loss of our dear friend and colleague, Carlos Surinach. Carlos was a distinguished Composer and Conductor who won particular acclaim for his music for dance. His wide-ranging contributions to our musical culture will live on for generations to come, and we shall greatly miss his warmth and wise counsel. Funeral services will be conducted Friday, November 14, 1997, at 1 P.M., at Woodlawn Cemetery, Jerome Avenue, Bronx. For information the SpinelliMalerba Funeral Home, 62 Beaver Street, Ansonia, CT.
