
        MCMAHON-John H., 84. On February 21, at home in Ridgewood, NJ. Noted for accurate polling as radio reporter for New York Daily News. Was SCAA Director of Communications and Legislative Information; his Albany Bulletin widely read and respected. Served on Moreland Commission for social welfare policy. Survived by his loving wife, two daughters, one granddaughter. Donations to Valley Hospice, 15 Essex Rd, Paramus, New Jersey 07652.
