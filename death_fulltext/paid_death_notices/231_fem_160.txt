
        LEOPOLD--Margaret. Beloved wife of the late Edward Philip Leopold, passed away peacefully on Wednesday, April 19, 2006. Loving sister of Julius and Milton Klein and the late Dr. Morris Klein. Survived by her children Jackie (Norman), Leslie (Arthur) and Paul (Karen) and her adored grandchildren Jonathan, Karen, Stacy and Erica. She was a very smart and caring wife, sister, mother and grandmother. Her intuitiveness, generosity and kindness will be missed by all who were lucky enough to have known her.
