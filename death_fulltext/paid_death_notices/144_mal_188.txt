
        SARDONO-Charles. The Board of Directors, Tenants/Shareholders and employees of 480 Park Avenue express our heartfelt sorrow at the untimely passing of our longtime employee and friend. Mr. Sardono's concern and manner were always appreciated by those who knew him as he cheerfully performed his responsibilities and more during the past 27 years. Our thoughts and codolences are with his wife Theresa and children Charles Jr. and Margaret. 480 Park Avenue
