
        FINKELSTEIN-Robert. The Association of Electrical Contractors, Inc, is deeply saddend by the passing of our dear friend, Bob. He served as an officer in the US Navy, graduated from Cornell, EE, and became President of RobertAllen Associates. Bob, representing his firm, became a member and director of our association for over 20 years. We all benefited as he unselflessly gave of himself, his time and talent. We will all miss his warm smile as he spoke kindly and softly. To Bob's loving family, Phyllis, Jennifer, Cynthia and Allen, we extend our deepest condolences. Officers, Directors, Members AEC
