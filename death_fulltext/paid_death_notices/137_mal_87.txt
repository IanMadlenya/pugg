
        MEYER-Joseph. Born April 3, 1916 in Port Chester, N.Y. Died June 8 in Los Angeles. Joseph was married for 55 years to Anita Meyer, who preceded him in death in April. Survived by his children, Donna Goodman and Robert (Gloria) Meyer, loving grandchildren, Suzanne, Emily & Molly, and many loving relatives. Services Thursday, June 11, at Hillside Memorial Park, Los Angeles.
