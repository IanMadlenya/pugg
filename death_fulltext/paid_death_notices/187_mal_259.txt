
        ZUCKER-Dr. Richard A. Of Wilton, Connecticut, died at 78 on August 18. He cared for generations of patients from 1954 until his retirement last March and was beloved for his kindness, skill, dedication, and humor. He is survived by his former wife Judy; their children John, Peter, Daniel, and Ellen; six grandchildren; and his brother Robert. His life will be commemorated on Saturday, September 14, 1:30 P.M. at Wilton Playshop. Donations may be made to Wilton Library, 137 Old Ridgefield Rd., Wilton, CT. 06897, or Whittingham Cancer Center, Norwalk Hospital, 34 Maple Street, Norwalk, CT. 06856.
