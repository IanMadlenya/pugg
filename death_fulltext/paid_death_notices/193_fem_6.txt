
        TILSON-Phyllis Wiegand, 93. Of Old Chatham, NY, died January 29, 2003, after a brief illness. She was the daughter of American landscape artist, Gustave Wiegand. She was predeceased by husband Paul in 1986 and is survived by two daughters, Phyllis Piotrow and Ann Burnham, and six grandchildren and twelve great-grandchildren.
