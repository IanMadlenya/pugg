
        FEIDEN-Joseph. Beloved husband of the late Jewel Eliasberg Feiden. Most cherished father of Margo Feiden, owner of the Margo Feiden Galleries Ltd. Died August 26, 1999, in his daughter's home after a long illness. Brother of the late Raymond Feiden, Martin Feiden and Sally Baker, loving father-in-law of the late Julius Cohen. He is also survived by his beloved grandchildren Bambi and Jeremy, brother The Hon. Murray T. Feiden, former Brooklyn Supreme Court Judge, and loving nephews and nieces. Shiva Will be observed at the home of his daughter.
