
        SHAPIRO--Murray, 85, Brooklyn, died on Wednesday, May 28, 2003, at his home. He was the husband of 57 years of Beatrice Shapiro. At 15, he graduated from Boys High School. He attended N.Y.U. and Brooklyn College, where he was a member of the soccer team. Mr. Shapiro was a decorated army veteran of WWII, and was a member of the D.A.V. He was a New York/New Jersey 3 cushion billiards champion, a nationally ranked handball player in the 1930's, and was an entertaining opera singer. Besides his wife, he leaves his daughter, Judy Kallet of South Windsor, CT; his son, Steven Shapiro of Queens and five grandchildren. Burial was held in Connecticut. >>AD#
