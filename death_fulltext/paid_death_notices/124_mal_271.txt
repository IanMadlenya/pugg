
        MARKS-Arthur D. The officers, fellow board members, and staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind note with sorrow the passing of Arthur D Marks. Mr. Marks was the father of Andrew Marks, our esteemed colleague and long time board member of The Guild. We extend our deepest sympathy to Andy and his family. James M. Dubin, Chairman John F. Heimerdinger, President
        MARKS-Arthur. The Board of Governors and Members of Inwood Country Club record with sorrow the passing of Arthur D. Marks, Jr. Longstanding member, and we extend deepest sympathy to the family. Robert Scharf, President Edwin Stein, Secretary
