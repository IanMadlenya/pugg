
        MEADE-Christopher, An Attorney and Real Estate Broker with Daniel Gail Realty of Port Washington died suddenly on July 17, 1997, at the age of 45. Born and raised in Manhasset where he lived most of his life. He graduated from Lasalle Military Academy in Oakdale, L.I. He was also a graduate of Fairfield University Class of 1974 and later earned his law degree from Bridgeport School of Law in Bridgeport, CT. He became a member of the Bar of the State of New York in 1983. In 1996 he obtained his real estate license and embarked on a second career after many years as a registered broker on Wall Street. Christopher Meade will be remembered for his avid love of history, politics and the ocean as he so often vacationed in Montauk, L.I. where the family maintains a home. The children of the St. Christopher-Ottilie Orphanage of Sea Cliff where he enthusiastically coached the boy's soccer team will also share this loss. His friends remembered ''Chris'''' as a person of unimpeachable character, one fiercely loyal to both family and friends. He was the proud son of the late George and Mary Meade and is survived by loving brothers and sisters, James Meade of New York City, Thomas Meade of Great Neck, Jerome Meade of Port Washington, Anne Meade of New York City, and Maria Meade of Port Washington. A mass of Christian burial will be held at St. Mary's Roman Catholic Church in Manhasset, NY, Saturday, July 19, 9:45 A.M. Donations in Christopher Meade's name may be made to his favorite charity, The Juvenile Diabetes Foundation.
