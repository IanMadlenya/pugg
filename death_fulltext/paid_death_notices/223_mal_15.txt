
        MILLER--Manuel ''Manny''. Formerly of Dix Hills, NY on July 30, 2005 at the age of 78. Beloved father of Steven Miller and Joan Kimmel. Adored brother of David Miller. Loving grandfather of Rachel, Jason, Alexa and Jillian. He is predeceased by his wife of 40 years, Judy. Member of the Suffolk County Democratic Party and former, long time member of the Freeport Chamber of Commerce. A memorial service will be held at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, Dix Hills, NY, Tuesday, August 2 at 10:15 AM. Donations can be made to Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center. A man who truly loved his family. We will miss you deeply.
