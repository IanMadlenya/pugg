
        LILIAN-Jordan. On July 28, 2001, surrounded by his loving family. Loving husband, proud father and grandfather. He is survived by Elaine, his wife of 11 years, his children Stuart, Jonathan, Ann and Brad, and nine grandchildren. His first wife Sylvia predeceased him in 1989. He was in the furniture business for 50 years and retired as president of A. L. Myers in 1995. He was a member of Congregation Sons of Israel in Briarcliff Manor, co-founded by his grandfather A. L. Myers. Services will be held at Congregation Sons of Israel, 1666 Pleasantville Rd., Briarcliff Manor, on July 31 at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Congregation. For further information, call Hellman Garlick Memorial Chapel 914-762-5501.
