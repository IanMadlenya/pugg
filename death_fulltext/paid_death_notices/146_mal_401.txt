
        GOODMAN-Nathan. The Officers and Members of the Boards of Governors of the Jewish Home and Rehabilitation Center and the Jewish Home at Rockleigh note with profound sorrow the passing of our Board Member Emeritus, dear friend and longtime supporter, Nathan Goodman. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Phyllis, and his entire family. May his name be for a blessing. Carl Epstein, President Charles P. Berkowitz, Executive Vice President
