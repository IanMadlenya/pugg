
        FONFA-Stanley N. Age 79 of Palm City, FL died after a brief illness on May 16, 2001 at Martin Memorial Hospital. Beloved husband of Greta, cherished father of Ann, Gudrun, Andrew and stepson Scott, dear father-in-law of Jodi Fonfa and Steve Wolman, and darling grandfather of Raven, Brett, Evan and Haley Fonfa, and Casey Manning. Stanley was a very special human being who cared deeply about people and justice. He will be missed by friends and family, and by all who knew and loved him.
