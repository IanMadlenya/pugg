
        RAHM-Herbert. On September 11. Devoted husband of Sylvia. Loving father of Jason Rahm and Susan Holden. Beloved grandfather of Jillian, Wendy, Danielle, Jonathan and Kevin. Interment at Knollwood Park, Ridgewood, NY, September 12 at 2 P.M.
        RAHM - Herbert. Congregation Rodeph Sholom notes with sorrow the passing of our esteemed Honorary Trustee, Herbert Rahm. Herb was a devoted Trustee of our Temple and past President of Brotherhood, and endeavored to further interfaith relations through his devotion to Jewish Chautaqua. We will miss his wonderful dedication and warmth. We extend our deepest sympathy to his loving wife, Sylvia and to all their dear ones. Rest in Peace. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Jack B. Levitt, President and the Board of Trustees of Congregation Rodeph Sholom
