
        FOLEY-Joan Poole. Age 79. Of New Vernon, N.J. on January 13, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Edward J. Foley Jr. who died in 1997. Devoted mother of John C. and Edward J. III and daughter-in-law Noel Kade Foley of Morristown and Matthew Wicks Foley of Cherry Hill, N.J. sister of the late Margaret Robb. Grandmother of five and great grandmother of two. The service will be held Saturday January 16, 1999 at 3 P.M. in the 1st Presbyterian Church, Lee's Hill Road, New Vernon. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her memory to the Bancroft Foundation, C/O Development Office Hopkins Lane, Haddonfield, N.J. 08033 would be appreciated. For information Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home, Basking Ridge, NJ.
