
        WEINTRAUB-Pauline Sherman, 93. Died Feb. 25 at home surrounded by her family. Beloved wife of the late Morris Weintraub, cherished mother of Howard, Vivian, & Leonard. Loving grandmother of Robin, Alexander, Bonnie, Scott, Melissa, Randy, Sherry and Craig. Adoring great grandmother of Brandon, Griffin, Kayla, Taylor, Jake, Sidney, Max, and Natalie. Dear friend of Ida James. Services will be held at ''Boulevard-Riverside'' Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett,, Long Island, on Tuesday, February 27, at 1 PM.
