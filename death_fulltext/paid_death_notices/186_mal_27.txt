
        DEUTSCH-Professor Martin. The faculty of The Steinhardt School of Education of New York University mourns the death of our esteemed colleague Martin Deutsch, Professor Emeritus. Professor Deutsch was an eminent psychologist, early leader of research and evaluation of pioneering pre-school programs, and valued academic colleague. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife and longterm research partner, Cynthia Deutsch, his sons, Benjamin and Jonah, and his granddaughter Alexandra. Ann Marcus Dean, The Steinhardt School of Education New York University
