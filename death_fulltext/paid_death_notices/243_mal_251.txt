
        RICHLAND--Irwin S., 96, Formerly of Riverdale, NY, Fort Lauderdale and Tamarac, FL, died peacefully in his sleep on Friday, April 20, in Weston, MA. After a 45 year advertising career with Richland Advertising in NYC serving the fashion industry, he continued in his beloved career in Florida until his early 90's. An avid athlete himself, he wrote exercise is life! A graduate of DeWitt Clinton High School he entered NYU where he discovered his passion for advertising. He was always the most gracious gentleman who enjoyed sharing his enthusiasm and vigor for life. He lived with the philosophy that the world operates on the essence of goodness. He was predeceased by his beloved wives, Ethel Gips and Edith Kurtz and beloved brother Sidney. He will be missed by his loving family. A graveside service will be held at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, April 24, 11 am.
