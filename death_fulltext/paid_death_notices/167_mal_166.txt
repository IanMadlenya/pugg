
        KOBLER-John. Died December 11, 2000 in New York City, age 90. Beloved husband of Rita Stein Kobler. Loving father of Jay Kobler, Lynn Hannon, Karen Reiser and Andrea Bagg. Grandfather of Lisa, James, Jennifer and Adam. Great grandfather of Caitlin and Samuel. Journalist and author of ''The Reluctant Surgeon: A Biography of John Hunter'', ''Capone'', ''Ardent Spirits'', ''Damned in Paradise: The Life of John Barrymore'', and numerous other books and articles. He will be missed by all who loved him.
