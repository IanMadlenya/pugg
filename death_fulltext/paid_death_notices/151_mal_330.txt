
        CORSO-Anthony J. On August 27, 1999. Beloved husband of Jean. Loving father of Salvatore, Anthony and Stephanie Granite. Dear father-in-law of Mary, Suzanne and Thomas Granite. Devoted grandfather of Salvatore John, Christopher Anthony and Samantha Rose. Cherished brother of Domenica Guarino, Joseph and Catherine Fillipone. 48 years of service to the Wall Street community. 25 year Member of the New York Stock Exchange. Member of Knights of Malta for his dedication and faith to the Church. Humanitarian and philanthropist. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Members Handicapped Childrens Fund, sent to the New York Stock Exchange, c/o Anthony Corso Jr., 11 Wall St., New York, NY. Reposing at Andrew Torregrossa and Sons, 1305 79th St., Brooklyn, NY. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 9:30 AM, at St. Bernadette R.C. Church. Interment Moravian Cemetery.
        CORSO-Anthony. The employees of LaBranche & Company mourn the passing of our friend, Tony Corso, a member of the New York Stock Exchange community for 47 years. He was the father of our Managing Director, Anthony Corso, Jr. We are all very saddened by his sudden passing. Tony Corso will be much missed by us all.
