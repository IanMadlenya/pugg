
        HEIFERMAN-Celia nee Friedman. Died in her home January 16, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Solomon; cherished mother of Ruth Prenner and the late Esther (Cooky) Signet; treasured grandmother of Emily and Elizabeth Prenner and David and Michael Signet; adoring great-grandmother, and dear friend to many. She was a gentle and gallant lady who was admired by all who knew her and deeply loved by those lucky enough to have known her well.
