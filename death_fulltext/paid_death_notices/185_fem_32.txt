
        BEATMAN-Frances. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Frances Beatman, a cherished friend who was a devoted board member of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services, a UJAFederation affiliated agency. Mrs. Beatman will be remembered for her extraordinary generosity and dedication to Jewish and other humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Beatman family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Joan Ginsburg, Women's Campaign Chair
