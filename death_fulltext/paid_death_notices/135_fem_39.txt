
        WEBER-Millie. The Orthodox Union mourns the passing of the beloved mother of our esteemed dedicated Chairman of the National Board, Marcel Weber, and Suzy Ross. She was a devoted mother, mother-in-law, grandmother and great grandmother, staunchly committed to many Torah causes worldwide. May the family and all Israel be consoled among the mourners for Zion and Jerusalem. Mandell I. Ganchrow, M.D., Pres. Dr. Marcos Katz, Ch., Bd of Gov. Rabbi Raphael Butler, Exec V.P. Rabbi Pinchas Stolper, Sr. Exec
