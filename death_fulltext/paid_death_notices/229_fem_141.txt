
        COHEN--Anna (Honey). Vice President of the Ladies Auxiliary of the Sephardic Home. She was our Raffle Chairperson for over 30 years. We extend our deepest condolences to her children, David and Len, and their families. She will be remembered for her dedication to the residents of the Sephardic Home and to all who were privileged to know her. The Board of Directors of Sephardic Home and LASHA
