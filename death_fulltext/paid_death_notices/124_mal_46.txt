
        ZULLER-Philip A. On Saturday, May 3, 1997. Beloved husband of Esther (nee Ditkowsky). Devoted father of Phyllis and Rabbi Phillip Goldman, Helene and Fred Zauder and Joel and Eva Zuller. Cherished grandfather and greatgrandfather. He served as a Licensed Public Accountant for over 60 years and he was loved by all who knew him. Active in the Young Israel movement, Kingsway Jewish Center and supporter of many charitable causes. Burial was on Sunday, May 4, 1997 at Montefiore Cemetery. Shiva period will be observed at 1546 East 31st Street, Brooklyn, N.Y. until Friday, May 9, 1997.
