
        HAINES -- Alexander died peacefully on September 8, 2003. Born in London on July 11, 1914, Alec served with honor in the RAF in Africa and Italy during WWII, and emigrated to New York in 1946. A past secretary of the Designer's Club in New York's garment district, Alec was also an active member and past Trustee of the Riverdale Temple, and past Vice President of the Temple's Men's Club. Alec, beloved partner of Theodora ''Doris'' Yuden and pre-deceased by beloved wife Catherine ''Kitty'' Haines, is survived by his brother Mark, as well as by seven nephews, three nieces, three grand step-children, three great-grand step-children, and numerous grand nieces and nephews. Services Thursday 9 am at Riverdale Temple, West 246th Street and Independence Avenue, Riverdale, New York.
