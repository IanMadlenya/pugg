
        WOLFSON-Jack, 91, of Palm Beach Gardens, FL passed away July 19, 2000. Loving husband of Louise, and beloved father of Miriam ''Mimi'' Kende and Philippa ''Penny'' and Neil Van Dyne, grandfather to Sabrina and Ethan Kende and Lisa and Alexander Van Dyne and his wife Lisa and two great-grandchildren. Services were held at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, LI. Memorial donations to your local Diabetes Association.
