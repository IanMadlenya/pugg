
        McELROY--D. Keith, MD. Orthopaedist of CPMC, June 23 at 90 from a stroke. He invented the Bilateral/Lateral Spinal Fusion operation and instruments and Injection Therapy procedure. Survived by Muriel Adamy of Harrison, NY, and sons, Frank and Don of Marblehead, MA, and grandchildren, Ian, Madeleine, Kevin and Anna. His remains donated to NY Medical College, Valhalla, NY. Memorial service at later date.
