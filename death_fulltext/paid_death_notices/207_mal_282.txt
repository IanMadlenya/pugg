
        McDONNELL--Hubert Jr. Of Greenwich, CT, died April 23, 2004. He was a longtime Greenwich resident. In 1948 he married Aline Elwes. In 1953 he joined the family firm of McDonnell & Co. He is survived by his wife Aline, two daughters, Ann Louise and Rosamond McDonnell, and his brother David McDonnell. Burial will be private.
