
        BOULTON-J. Howard Jr. Suddenly taken from us on September 11, 2001, in the World Trade Center tragedy while working at Euro Brokers. He was a loving and devoted husband of Vigdis. Father of Frederick Tomas. Son of J. Howard Boulton Sr. and Renata Szokoloczi both of Caracas, Venezuela. Brother of Alfred, Aura Louisa and Claudia Rocafort. Son-in-law of Hrafnhildur and Kristjan T. Ragnarsson. Brother-in-law of Hildur and William Schmidt, Thorunn and Benjamin Zimmermann and Kristin Ragnarsson and a friend of many. Memorial service will be held on Friday, September 21, at 2 P.M. at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave and 66th St, Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Frederick Tomas Boulton Trust, 15 South Dr., Larchmont, NY 10538.
