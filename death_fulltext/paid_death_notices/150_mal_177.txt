
        ISAACS-Harry Columbus. Former Senior Vice President of The BVD Company; civic leader in his home community of Rye, New York, which honored him with a key to the city; a founder and president of The Rye Community Synagogue and active volunteer with The Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and Jewish Welfare Board; founding member of Roosevelt College in Chicago; former president of SUNY/Purchase affiliates; and honorary Hopi Tribal member. Harry Isaacs is survived by his wife of 63 years, Natalie; his daughter, Naomi; his son Richard; his son Stephen and daughter-inlaw Ava; his grandchildren Ilan, Ariel, and David; and his brother Irving and sister-in-law Joan. A memorial service will be held in August.
