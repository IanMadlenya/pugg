
        CRONYN--Hume. With enduring memories of a treasured relationship, the Academy mourns the loss of its esteemed alumnus Hume Cronyn. Through his efforts and support, a Cronyn/Tandy scholarship was established at the Academy in 1988 to help Canadian students. Our heartfelt sympathies go out to his wife, Susan, and the entire Cronyn family. Roger Croucher, President The American Academy of Dramatic Arts
