
        DALY--Margaret A., ''Peggy'', (nee Hurley), of Edison, NJ on Thursday, July 21, 2005, age 66. Loving wife of Daniel F. Daly; devoted mother of Daniel J. Daly of Edison and Kathleen Kozak and her husband Bryan of San Antonio, TX; beloved sister of Jack Hurley of Long Island, NY and twin sister, Maureen Maroney of Jamesville, VA; cherished grandmother of two. Funeral services will be held on Monday, July 25, 2005 at 8:30 AM at the Gosselin Funeral Home, 660 New Dover Road, Edison NJ, followed by a 9:30 AM Mass at St. Helena's R.C. Church, Edison, NJ. Burial to follow in Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY. Visitation Saturday 7-9 PM and Sunday 2-4 & 7-9 PM. Memorial gifts in honor of Peggy Daly may be made to: St. Jean Baptiste High School, 173 E. 75th St, NY, NY 10021 or NJ Coalition for Inclusive Education, 9 Auer Ct, Suite H., East Brunswick, N.J. 08816.
