
        BONAN-Seon Pierre. Real estate developer and philanthropist, best known for the ground-breaking urban renewal project, Charles River Park in Boston, died Saturday in Palm Beach, FL, of complications from a brain injury. He was 82 and maintained residences in Palm Beach, Wilton, CT, and New York City, and was a former longtime resident of Greenwich, CT. Mr. Bonan is survived by his wife of 52 years, Janet Ross Bonan, a sister, Ethel Hoeffler, a brother, Ferdinand, three children, Elizabeth Bonan, Charles Bonan and Virginia Adelson, and six grandchildren. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. NYC, Wednesday, January 26, 11:30 AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Mt. Sinai Hospital Juvenile Diabetes Center.
