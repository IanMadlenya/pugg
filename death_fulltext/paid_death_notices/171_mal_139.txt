
        BROGAN-Thomas J., Jr. President of Brogan Cadillac-Oldsmobile Company, Totowa, NJ. Died Monday, April 9, 2001, age 75. Lived in NYC & Southampton, New York. Until January, Mr. Brogan was an active member of a three generation Cadillac Oldsmobile family. His father, Thomas J. Brogan, Sr., began the four dealerships in 1927. He was honored Master Cadillac Dealer numerous times and member of Oldsmobile Elite. Formerly on the Better Business Board of New York and NJ, First Fidelity Bank, and President of Wholesale Auto Supply Company. In addition to his wife, Patricia Kelly Brogan, he is also survived by two daughters, Susan Patricia Brogan and Kelly Brogan, and a grandson, Nicholas Taylor Brogan, all from Florida and Southampton, his sister, Mrs. Joseph L. Ely of McLean, VA, and Spring Lake, NJ, his brother, J. Peter Brogan and his wife Claire of Florida and NJ, and his step-mother, Mrs. Thomas J. Brogan, Sr. of Florida and Spring Lake, NJ. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. on Monday, April 16th, from 2-5 and 7-9PM. Memorial Mass St. Thomas More Church, 65 E. 89th St., on Tuesday, 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to Youth Counseling League, 386 Park Ave. South, NY NY 10016, would be greatly appreciated.
