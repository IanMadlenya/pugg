
        GARILLI-Anne. Her family and her colleagues at ''Architectural Digest'' mourn the passing, on March 27, 1997 in Staten Island, of a beloved relation and cherished friend. Mentor to many at Conde Nast Publications and ''Architectural Digest''. Her zest for life and incomparable warmth enriched the lives of all who knew her. We will miss her and remember her with joy. For information contact Ceraso-Gennarelli Funeral Home, 718-853-1900.
