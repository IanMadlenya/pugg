
        HALPERIN -- David. The Mount Sinai Medical Center Department of Psychiatry deeply mourns the loss of Dr. David Halperin, beloved colleague and devoted teacher. Dr. Halperin, an Associate Clinical Professor of Psychiatry, was an important contributor to the department and in particular to our residency training program as he co-led several process groups and touched the lives of so many students, residents and colleagues over the past few decades. We are grateful for his many contributions to the department and extend our heartfelt symapthy to his wife Gayle, daughters Samara, Ilana and Maia and the entire family. He will be missed.
