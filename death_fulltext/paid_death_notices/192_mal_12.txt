
        SEGAL-Benjamin. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member Friar Benjamin Segal on December 27, 2002. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services to be held at Quinnipiac Club, 221 Church St, New Haven, CT. at 6PM Thursday, January 9, 2002. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
