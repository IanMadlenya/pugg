
        RAEBURN--Robert A. The employees of Vanguard Industries wish to pay tribute to Robert A. Raeburn, who passed away on April 17, 2007 in New York. Mr. Raeburn was a friend, competitor, and business associate for over 50 years. He will be missed by all who knew him. He was a man of integrity, honesty, and character. He taught us all that ''his word was his bond.'' Bill and Debra Gershen, Michael Harrison, and Mr. Herbert P. Sydney
