
        O'LEARY-Michael John, of Ft. Pierce, Florida died peacefully on May 12 after a short illness at age 84. Mr. O'Leary was a Public Relations executive with AT&T for more than 30 years until his retirement in 1983. He was educated at Fordham Preparatory School and received degrees from Fordham University (B.A.) and Columbia University (M.A.) in New York. He served proudly in World War II and was a talented writer, artist & actor with a keen intellect and a lively wit. He will always be remembered for his kindness, genuine decency and wonderful, irreverent sense of humor. He is survived by his wife Mary Harnett O'Leary, his sons John and Vincent, his daughter Jean, numerous stepchildren and grandchildren, his daughters-in-law and son-in-law. His first wife, Jean Donohoe O'Leary, and his eldest son, Michael J. O'Leary Jr., M.D., predeceased him. Visiting hours at The Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home in Bronxville, NY from 2-4 PM & 7-9PM on Thursday, May 15. Mass of Christian Burial at Annunciation Church in Crestwood, NY at 10 AM on Friday, May 16. Friends may send memorial donations in his name to Fordham Preparatory School, East Fordham Road, Bronx, New York 10458.
