
        GERSTEN-Maurice R. Of Simsbury and Hartford, CT, died on March 18, 2002. Maurice was one of 14 siblings, a graduate of Simsbury High School, and he received undergraduate and law degrees from George Washington University. Maurice was an active legal practitioner from 1946 to the present, a legislator, a judge and an active Republican Party member and advisor. Maurice was a Talmudic Scholar. He is a living presence to his extended family and friends.
