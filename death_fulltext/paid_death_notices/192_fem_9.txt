
        WEIXEL-Lola. 81, on December 23, 2002. Beloved wife of David. Loving mother of Eugene and Miriam. Grandmother of Goldie, Rose and Joanna. Aunt, great-aunt, sister-in-law and friend to many. A welder during WWII, known as ''Rosie the riveter'' for her role in the classic documentary of that name. Memorial service Sunday, January 5, at the home of David Weixel.
