
        SCHICK-William, Dr.. Of Riverdale, Bronx, New York, January 13, 2000. Beloved husband of Jessica (nee Cotins) Schick for 51 years, devoted father of Gary & Sandor Schick and Teri SchickBilenker, cherished grandfather of Daniel and Carrie Bilenker. Dr. Schick was Professor Emeritus at Fairleigh Dickinson University, where he taught electrical engineering for 33 years, served as chair of the electrical engineering department, assistant dean of the College of Science & Engineering, and assistant dean for academic computing. A memorial service was held at the Riverdale-Yonkers Ethical Culture Society on January 16, 2000. Contact family members for information on donations in his memory.
