
        MURPHY-Mary Judith, M.D., M.P.H. On May 16, 2002. Born on July 23, 1943 in New York City, daughter of Francis X. & Margaret (nee McNally) Murphy. Graduate of College of Mt. St. Vincent, NY Medical College and Columbia University. A loving mother, wife & pediatrician, she was a member of the faculty at Columbia University until she pursued a career in the pharmaceutical industry with Pfizer. Mary Judith was an expert on childhood infectious diseases and a Fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics. Surviving: husband, Dr. Joseph H. Graziano, Hastings - on - Hudson; daughter, Caitlin Judith Graziano; sisters Kathleen Murphy Mallinger of San Diego, CA, Anne Murphy, M.D. Baltimore, MD, brothers Francis X., Jr. of Philadelphia, PA, Christopher of Lakewood, CO, Timothy of Silver Springs, MD; Joe's children Michelle McCarthy and Joseph N. Graziano, M.D. Funeral service 1PM Saturday at EdwardsDowdle Funeral Home 64 Ashford Avenue (exit 17 Saw Mill) Dobbs Ferry, NY. Visiting Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Contributions to Friends of Children's Fund at American Academy of Pediatrics, 38367 Eagle Way, Chicago, IL 60678-1383.
