
        GOLDMAN-David. Wurzweiler School of Social Work of Yeshiva University records with sorrow the passing of founding Board member and longtime friend David Goldman. Heartfelt condolences to his daughter, Irene Goldman-Price, and son, William Goldman. David and his late wife, Muriel, were YU Guardians who supported many initiatives at Wurzweiler and established the Muriel and David Goldman Scholarship Fund. May his family and friends be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman YU Board of Trustees David I. Schachne, Chairman WSSW Board of Governors Dr. Sheldon R. Gelman Dorothy and David I. Schachne Dean, WSSW
