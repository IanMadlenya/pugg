
        BLADER-Rubin. On May 3, 2000, at age 94. Devoted husband to Dorothy. Beloved father to Susan, Jay and Linda. Loving grandfather to Brian and Jonathan. Died peacefully in Hanover, NH. A successful businessman, devoted to his family. He touched the lives of all he knew. He will be greatly missed. A memorial service was held on May 5 in the Roth Center for Jewish Life at Dartmouth College.
