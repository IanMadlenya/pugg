
        SAVITZ-Sy. On October 5, 2000 at age 74. Adored husband of Nita. Loving father of Jane and Tony Mangiaracina and Susan and Michael LaValle. Devoted grandfather of Bianca, Jayson, Michelle and Michael. Dear brother of Judy Rand. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, 11:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Neurological Research Fund, c/o Dr. Michael Fetell, Room 204, Columbia University, 710 W. 168th St., NYC 10032 or The Bertolli Brain Tumor Lab, c/o Dr. Jeffrey Bruce, Room 434, 710 W. 168th Street, NYC 10032.
        SAVITZ-Sy. The Officers and Directors of the Seaview Association of Fire Island, NY, note with sorrow the death of our fellow Director, Sy Savitz. His many friends and neighbors in Seaview admired Sy's honest and direct manner, his sense of humor, his energetic and longstanding service to our community, and his example of courage in the face of serious illness. On behalf of the entire community, the Board extends wishes of sympathy and support to Nita and the extended Savitz family.
