
        POTTER-Helena. The Board of Directors of The Grenfell Association of America expresses its deep sorrow and sympathy to Dr. Robert Potter, the Association's President, and his family at the untimely passing of his wife, Helena, whose wisdom and unique contributions to the organization are greatly appreciated and will be sorely missed. David C. Oxman, Vice-President R. Keating Hagmann, Treasurer Debra R. Anisman, Secretary
