
        ARMSTRONG - Jack Ar mond, 77. Of Bath, OH, died peacefully on October 15, 2002 surrounded by family members. He served his country on Iwo Jima in WWII; he worked for the Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co for 40 years. He leaves behind his wife of 55 years, Marva (nee Bartholomew), his children Patrice Clareman, Ralph, Robert and Thomas Armstrong; his children-in-law Lloyd Clareman, Susan, Tracy and Robbi Armstrong; and his grandchildren Billy, Rebecca and Bart Clareman, Jenny, Sam, Heath, Maddie, Emma and Cole Armstrong-all of whom adored him and mourn his loss.
