
        WHITEHEAD-Robert. The Board of Trustees and staff of Theatre Development Fund deeply mourn the loss of our dear friend, Robert Whitehead. Bob was a true champion of the theatre who nurtured the greatest playwrights of the Twentieth Century. In addition to his innumerable gifts to the theatre, he provided invaluable counsel and guidance to TDF-first as a trustee from 1985-1992, and from 1993 to the present, as the Honorary President of TDF's Chairman's Council. Robert Whitehead's quest for artistic excellence and his extraordinary skills as a producer will provide a benchmark to the field for generations to come. Our deepest condolences to Zoe, Charlie and Sam. John F. Breglio, Chairman Victoria Bailey, Executive Director Theatre Development Fund
        WHITEHEAD-Robert. The Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are deeply saddened by the death of Robert Whitehead, an elegant man of the theater whose intelligence, passion, taste and energy informed a brilliant body of work over half a century. As the Founding Director of The Repertory Theater of Lincoln Center, Bob set the bar high for those who followed. We send our condolences and love to Zoe, Sam, and Charlie. Linda LeRoy Janklow, Chairman of The Board Andre Bishop, Artistic Director Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
        WHITEHEAD-Robert. The League of American Theatres and Producers mourns the passing of the Vice President of our Board of Governors, a legendary producer and our friend. His like will not be seen again. Cy Feuer, Chairman Jed Bernstein, President
