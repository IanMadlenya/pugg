
        LOPEZ--Benito M. Jr. Born March 17, 1933, in NYC, to Benito and Teresa V. Lopez, died January 21, 2005. Attended All Hallows High School, '50; Fordham College, '54; Harvard Law School, '57; The Juilliard School. Partner Dewey Ballantine LLP, '57-'89; VP Iona College, '89-'92; Executive Director, Association of Catholic Colleges and Universities, '92-'95. A devoted husband, father, designer and musician. Survived by his loving wife of 37 years, Rosalie; brother Ramon; sons Matthew (Patricia), Christopher (Carrie), and Daniel (Marsha); grandchildren Isabel, Camila and Marco. Viewing: Bennett Funeral Home, Scarsdale, NY, Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral: Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, NY, Wednesday, 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to All Hallows High School Scholarship Fund, 111 E. 164th St, Bronx, NY 10452.
