
        MCNALLY--John F., 77, of Wellington, Florida, passed away at home on Thursday, August 7, 2003. He was born in New York City on June 16, 1926. John attended Fordham University in New York and was a US Army Veteran. He was a Supervisor of Investigations for the Corp Counsel Office for the City of New York, retiring in 1991. John and his beloved wife of 51 years, Mary ''Maura'' moved to Wellington, FL in 1992. He was also a past-president of a local New Jersey chapter of AARP and the first vice president of Wellington's Senior's Club. John is survived by his wife Mary ''Maura'', son Stephen McNally of Edgewater, NJ and daughter Linda McNally of Houston, TX. Interment will be Friday, August 15, 2003, 1:00PM, at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The American Heart Association, P.O. Box 21475, St. Petersburg, FL 33742.
