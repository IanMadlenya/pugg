
        SIEGEL - Fred. Greenwich, CT, on May 25, at the age of 46, after a three and a half year fight with leukemia. Buried May 28. Beloved husband of Jane, father of Yale, son of Eve, brother of Reva and Laura, nephew of Abe, Mimi, and Paul, cousin of Julia, Solange, and Diane, sonin-law of Marjorie and Victor, brother-in-law of Gwynne, Neill, and Thomas. Good and loyal friend to many. Graduate of Yale and Wharton, Vice President of Morgan Stanley, member of the Greenwich Chabad. We love him and will miss him. His love and delight in life lives in us. Gifts to honor his struggle can be made to Memorial Sloan - Kettering Hospital, Greenwich Hospital, or Chabad of Greenwich.
