
        JACOB--Fr. William Lawrence. Born in Pittsburgh, PA, February 17, 1920, passed away in NYC on November 26, 2004. Beloved son of the late Hubert and Anna. Loving brother of Jack, Peggy Griffith, Joan Megill and Adele Gordon. Also survived by many nieces and nephews and great-nieces and greatnephews. Professed Redemptorist August 2, 1944. Ordained a Priest June 23, 1946. He served in Puerto Rico for 21 years, seven years in Pittsburgh, PA, and 29 years in St. Cecilia's Parish in NYC. Reposing at St. Cecilia's R.C. Church, 106th Street between Lexington and Park Avenue, Monday and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 p.m. Mass of Resurrection St. Cecilia's Church, Wednesday, 10 a.m. Interment Mt. St. Alphonsus Cemetery, Esopus, NY. For more information contact Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 212288-3500.
