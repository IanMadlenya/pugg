
        HAIGHT-Marjorie nee Callaghan, October 2, 2000, Morristown, New Jersey. Born April 8, 1920, New York City. Daughter of the late Justice Stephen Callaghan and Ethel Callaghan. Wife of the late Reverend John Malcolm Haight of Massapequa, NY. Mrs. Haight is survived by her daughters, Ellen Gibson of Scotch Plains, NJ, Alison DeMaio of Morris Plains, NJ, Anne Markham of Bay Shore, NY, Vanessa Haight of Princeton, New Jersey, and seven grandchildren. Contributions can be made to the American Cancer Society.
