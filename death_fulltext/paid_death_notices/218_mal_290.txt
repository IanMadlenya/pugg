
        HYMAN--George A., M.D. Passed away suddenly at his home in Palm Beach, FL., at the age of 82. Dr. Hyman was born to Samuel and Blanche Hyman on August 11, 1922 in New York City. He graduated from Columbia College in 1942 and received his MD degree in 1945 from the College of Physicians and Surgeons. After two years of military service, he returned to Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center where he was an outstanding researcher, teacher, and clinician. He was beloved by his patients for his devoted care and was well respected and formed close friendships with his peers. He spent 35 years sharing an office on 5th Ave with his brother Dr. Julian Hyman specializing in oncology. He is survived by his brother Julian Hyman and his sister-in-law Elaine Hyman and his children Lynn Butler, Dr. Barry Hyman and his wife Mimika; his daughter Terry and her husband John Walsh; his son Robert and his wife Nicola; devoted grandfather to Olivia and Natalie, Ian, Sabrina and Samuel. Services will be held at The Riverside Memorial Chapel on 76 St and Amsterdam Ave on Sunday, March 27th at 11:45AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.
