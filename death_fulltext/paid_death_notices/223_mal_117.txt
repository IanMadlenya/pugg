
        WEINER--Max. The New York Philharmonic mourns the passing on August 6, of Max Weiner, a violinist of the Philharmonic from 1946 to 1994. Mr. Weiner performed with the Pittsburgh, Detroit, and Chicago symphonies before joining the Philharmonic. We extend condolences to his wife, Carolyn, and his children, Shelley and David. Lorin Maazel, Music Dir. Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Zarin Mehta, Pres./Exec. Dir. Members of the Staff
