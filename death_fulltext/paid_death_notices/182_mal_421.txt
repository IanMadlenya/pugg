
        BROWN-Kenneth Ridgeway, 77. On March 19, 2002 in Toronto, Canada. Born in Newcastle, England, graduated from the Durham School and University of London (engineering). Served in the Royal Navy during World War II on aircraft carrier HMS Battler in the North Atlantic. Immigrated to Canada in 1954 during 36 year career with The General Electric Company PLC. CEO of English Electric Corp. in New York for 9 years until his retirement in 1983. Member of several boards of directors. Survived by his wife Diane of 47 years, sister Ann (Bridges), sons Christopher, Graeme and David, daughter-in-law, Anne, grandsons Trevor and Derek. A memorial service to celebrate Kenneth Brown's life will be held in Bracebridge, Ontario in June.
