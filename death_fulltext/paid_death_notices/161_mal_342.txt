
        GOLDSTEIN-Arthur David. Beloved husband of Muriel (nee Kessman). Deeply loved father of Rochelle and Mark Schaevitz, Jane and Gary Buscemi, Warren Goldstein and Victoria Spetter. Proud grandfather of Jessica, Samantha, Julia, Benjamin and Elisabeth. Cherished brother of Robert and Michael Goldstein. Arthur Goldstein was a founding member of the New Democratic Coalition. He was instrumental and played a leading role in almost every liberal movement in New York State. He developed the charter of the Congress of Racial Equality and was the braintrust of much civil rights legislation. For many years he served on the local Democratic County Committee and as zone leader. Graduate of Cornell Law School, President of the Student Council at Brooklyn College. Lover of birds, gems and photography, he will be greatly missed. Arthur died on June 28, his 47th wedding anniversary, at the age of 70 after fighting a major battle with colon cancer. Funeral services will be held at Temple Beth El in Great Neck on Friday, June 30 at 11 AM.
