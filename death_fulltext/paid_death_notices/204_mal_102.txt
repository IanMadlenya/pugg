
        McCAULEY--William P. Died December 30, 2003. He was 63 years old. He worked in the Advertising Department at the Wall Street Journal. Surviving are a sister, Patricia McCauley Ekeland, a brother Thomas McCauley, many nieces and nephews. Inquiries Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court St., Bklyn, N.Y. 11201.
