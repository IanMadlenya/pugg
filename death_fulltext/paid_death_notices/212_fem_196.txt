
        BLINDER--Debra ''Debbie.'' Age 7, of Las Vegas, died September 24, 2004, in an automobile accident. Survived by loving parents Norman and Dawn Blinder, brother Steven, grandparents David and Laura Blinder, Robert and Arlene Popowcer, and several aunts, uncles and cousins. Her giving spirit lives on as a Nevada Organ Donor. She will be sorely missed and forever remembered.
