
        SPELKE--Judith of Delray, FL, formerly of Stanford, Ct, passed away peacefully on May 5, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Henry, devoted mother of Robert and Clifford, mother-in-law of Leslie, and grandmother of Craig, Samantha, Jenna and Brandon. Her strength and wit will be missed by her family and many wonderful friends. A memorial service will be held on Sunday May 16, 2004 at Congregation Agudath Shalom, 301 Strawberry Hill Avenue, Stanford, Ct at 10:30 am.
