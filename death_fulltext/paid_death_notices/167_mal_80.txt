
        MEHLER-Conrad, M.D. Of New York, NY on December 4, 2000 at the age of 85. Beloved husband of Tuci for 51 years, loving father of Rya and Charles Rosenzweig, and proud grandfather of Jessica and Erica. Resident of Czernowitz, he attended medical school in Bologna before making Aliyah to Israel during th Holocaust. In his new homeland, he fought bravely in the Israeli army and delivered thousands of babies at Degainia Hospital in Jaffa. After immigrating to the U.S. in 1957, he became a psychiatrist and eventually the medical director of Bronx Psychiatric and a member of the faculty of Metropolitan Hospital. A gentle and caring man and a wonderful listener, he will be missed by his family, friends, and patients.
