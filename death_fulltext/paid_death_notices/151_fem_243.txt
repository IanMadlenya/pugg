
        de FLINES-Margriet. Of Rowayton, CT, on August 28, 1999. Wife of Ernst de Flines. Mother of Tessa de Flines. She was an active volunteer for many years in the Rowayton Civic Association and Rowayton Library. Funeral services will be held at United Church of Rowayton, CT, on Tuesday, August 31, 1999, at 4 PM, Rev. John S. Livingston officiating. Memorial donations may be made to Rowayton Civic Association, P.O. Box 302, Rowayton, CT 06853.
