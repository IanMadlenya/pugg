
        WARREN--William ''Bill''. November 6. Loving husband of Vivian for over 50 years and devoted father to Robert. Adored and esteemed by Wendy and Gary Seiden, Lora and Irving Shepard. The voice and mind of radio station WMCR, Oneida, NY. Bill will be remembered for his gregarious nature, keen intelligence, love and loyalty to friends and family.
