
        ROSS-Miriam Wolff. Actor, director and writer, known to the stage as Miriam Wolfe. Peacefully at her home in Toronto, Friday evening, 29 September 2000, at age 78. In the caring presence of her son Michael and friend Lanie. After a lengthy, determined and exhausting battle with breast cancer, she finally rejoins her late husband John Forrest Mackay Ross. A small, private gathering of family and friends will be held. Donations to Dying with Dignity, 55 Eglinton Ave East, Suite 705, Toronto, Ontario, M4P 1G8, will be gratefully acknowledged, as both Miriam and John strongly supported choice in dying.
