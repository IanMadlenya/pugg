
        KLASS--Seymour S., on April 29 in Florida after a very brief illness. A Veteran of the China-India-Burma Corps. A devoted father and grandfather, he is predeceased by his wife Martha and survived by his children Emily and William Kaplan, Evan and Suzy Klass and grandchildren Elliott, Nathan, Sarah and Peter, as well as by Adele Gittleman.
