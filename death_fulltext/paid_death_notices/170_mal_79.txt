
        McLAUGHLIN-Edward F. Of NYC and Milford, CT, died March 7, 2001. Beloved husband of Viola. Brother of Dr. John McLaughlin of York, PA. After college he entered platoon leaders class at Quantico, VA. When the war broke out he joined the Navy and served as Lt. on the aircraft carrier USS Guadalcanal whose task force was instrumental in capturing the first man-of-war on the high seas since the War of 1812. This was the German submarine U-505. He graduated from the Univ. of Virginia Law School and practiced law in NYC until his retirement in 1994. Memorial service will be at St. Bartholomew's Church on Park Avenue and 50th Street on Friday, March 9th at 11 A.M.
