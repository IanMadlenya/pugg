
        ANDERSON--Harry B., Jr., 88, died Oct. 18 in Princeton, N.J. He graduated from Yale University in 1939. He entered the U.S. Marine Corps and was awarded the Silver Star for bravery and the Navy Commendation Ribbon in World War II. He joined Merrill Lynch in 1947 and later became Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Merrill Lynch International and Vice Chairman of the Board of Merrill Lynch & Co., Inc. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, the former Claire (Patty) H. Matz; sons Alexander M., Joseph C., and Jeffrey M.; daughter-in-law Joie A. Anderson, wife of Harry B. Anderson III, who died in 1990; and seven grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Monday, Oct. 23, at Trinity Episcopal Church, Princeton, N.J., at 11:00am. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Hospice Program at PMC, 208 Bunn Drive, Princeton New Jersey 08540.
