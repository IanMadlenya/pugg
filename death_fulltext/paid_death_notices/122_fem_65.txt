
        SHERNOFF-Lindy Carole, 38. Beloved daughter of Hortense and Sumner Zabriskie; cherished sister of Joan and Michael Bilt; adored aunt of Steven & Marty Bilt; loving and devoted friend to so many. She will be deeply missed for her goodness, her incredible artistic talents, her depth of character and intelligence and her compassion. Her spirit will carry on within each of us. Lindy graduated from Parsons School of Design with a bachelor's degree in Fine Arts and was later certified to practice, and was working, as a radiologic technologist. Funeral Sunday, 9:30 AM, Schwartz Bros. Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills; interment in Cedar Park, Westwood, N.J.
