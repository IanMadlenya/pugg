
        KLEIN--Hortense Ciner. On November 15, 2003, at age 89. Beloved wife of the late Daniel J. Klein. Loving mother of Carolyne and Edwin A. Levy and Robert and Susan Klein. Adoring grandmother of Jim and Sigal Levy, Matthew and Sharon Levy, Andrew and Alyssa Klein and Jeffrey Klein. Cherished great-grandmother of Danny and Lauren Levy and Olivia and Ellie Klein. Services were held at Frank E. Campbell. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
