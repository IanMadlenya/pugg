
        JACOBS--Harvey Lee, 81. Beloved husband of Arline Rubin, brother of Arthur, uncle of Jim, Ken, Joanna & Tom. His integrity, warm personality and generosity built life long friendships remembered by many. Harvey's humor and story telling brought laughter to everyone. Arline's loving care was inspiration to continue his fight to stay with us. After army service in WWII he practiced as a CPA and attorney, retiring as a senior Appeals Agent for the IRS in NYC. Services will be held on Thursday August 17 at 12:00 noon Plaza Memorial Chapel 91st St & Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be sent to B'Nai Brith, Planned Parenthood of NYC, Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Hospital, American Heart Association, United Jewish Appeal.
