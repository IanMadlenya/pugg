
        D'HONAU-Charles E., 69, died on February 5, 1997 at University of California San Francisco Medical Center, of complications from C.O.P.D. A graduate of The Pingry School and Princeton University, he was Asia Publisher of TIME Magazine, based in Tokyo before retirement. He is survived by his wife, Maureen, and sons, Charles and William, all of San Francisco. Funeral services at Old Mission Dolores, San Francisco de Asis will be private. Friends who wish may contribute to Princeton University, Class of '49, P.O. Box 46, Princeton, New Jersey 08544-0046.
