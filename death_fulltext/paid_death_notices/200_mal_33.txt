
        JASPAN--Norman. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community, husband of the late Dr. Jeanne Rafsky Jaspan, father of Michael (and Terry) Jaspan and Ronald (and Michele) Jaspan and brother of Ruth Hauser. The University is grateful for his and his family's enduring commitment to the Bernard Revel Graduate School of Jewish Studies as directors of the Harry and Jane Fischel Foundation. Our sincerest condolences to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Dr. Norman Lamm Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman Board of Trustees Mordecai D. Katz, Chairman BRGS Board of Directors
