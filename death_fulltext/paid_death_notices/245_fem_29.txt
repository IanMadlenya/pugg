
        BIGGER--Nora K. (Stewart). Age 93, of Mt. Lebanon, on Monday, June 4, 2007; formerly of Flemington, NJ, where she was a children's librarian. Born in Hamilton, Ontario, Nora was the beloved wife of the late Richard Paul Bigger, loving mother of Elizabeth J. Bigger, Sarah K. Burton, H. Stewart Bigger, Richard P. (Karen) Bigger Jr., and Dr. Joel R. (Peggi) Bigger; devoted grandmother of Daniel (Elizabeth) Case, the late Edwin Case, Richard (Megan) Case, Kayne (Thomas) Anderson, Sage (Wesley) Andrews, Rory and Kevin Bigger; also survived by four great - grandchildren. Services will be private. Internment in Stanton, NJ. Family suggests memorials to a library of one's choice. Arrangements by Laughlin Memorial Chapel, Mt. Lebanon, PA. (412)531-5100
