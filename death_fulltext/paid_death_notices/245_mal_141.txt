
        MARX--Peter Maxwell, died on June the 11th, 2007, after a brief illness. Peter was born in New York City and attended Oakhurst Day School in Deal, New Jersey. He also attended Clark School on the Dartmouth College campus, Cornell University, The Citadel and Pennsylvania Military College. He was an Adjutant General's Aide in the U.S. Army. He was a broadway producer and produced three operas. Later in his career he joined Judson Realty and also studied at the Modern Psychoanalytic Institute in New York City. He is survived by his beloved wife of 42 years, ''Tommi'' Gwendolyn K. Marx. He leaves two nephews and one niece and their 10 children, 12 godchildren, and his very special cousins, Klee and John Zaricky of Lawrence, Kansas. Peter was always surrounded by many loving friends in New York, Maine, and Kansas. A memorial service will be held Monday, June 18 at 4:00 pm at 20 Heron Lane, Cumberland Foreside, Maine. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to the Animal Refuge League, PO Box 336, Westbrook, ME 04098. Services are being handled by Jones, Rich & Hutchins Funeral Home, 199 Woodford St., Portland, ME 04103.
