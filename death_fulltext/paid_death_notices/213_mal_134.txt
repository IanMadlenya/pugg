
        FITZGERALD--John B., Jr. 39, on October 12, 2004 of Upper Saddle River, NJ. Beloved son of John B. Fitzgerald Sr. and the late Catherine (nee Mannix) Fitzgerald. Interment will follow at Ascension Cemetery in Monsey, NY. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society, 20 Mercer St., Hackensack, NJ 07601-5600.
