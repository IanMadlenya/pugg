
        ROBERTS-John. The Trustees, Advisors and staff of Outward Bound USA mourn the loss of John Roberts, a trustee and friend whose intelligence, humor and grace set him apart, and at the same time connected him to us and to everyone. We offer our sympathy and support to his wife Rona, his daughter Jennifer and his son Douglas. Frances Rubacha, Chair Richard Bangs, President Outward Bound USA, Garrison, NY
