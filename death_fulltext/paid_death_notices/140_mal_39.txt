
        LISMAN-Jack V. M.D. Age 84. Died on August 30, 1998. Survived by his wife of 54 years Sylvia; children Richard and Lee Lisman, Jane and Robert J. Katz; sister Leona Rothstein; four grandchildren, Hilary, Bogert, Cyrena L. Fink and James and Emily Katz, and great-granddaughter Brigitte J. Fink. Distinguished staff member and past Chief of the Division of Opthalmic Plastic Surgery at Manhattan Eye and Ear Hospital. Private services were held Tuesday, September 1st, in Hastings-onHudson, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to NYU School of Medicine or The Orbital Disease Foundation (635 Park Avenue, New York City 10021).
