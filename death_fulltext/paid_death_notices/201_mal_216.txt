
        SPENCER--Bryan. Born Scituate, MA., 1949. Died New York City, October 19, 2003. A life-long artist and lover of art in all its forms. Graduated McGill University with a degree in Music Composition and held a Ph.D. in the school of courageous survival. Performer, poet, composer, lyricist, brother, uncle and friend. Bryan gave all he had to give, always. He is survived by brother Pete, sister-in-law Marilyn and nephews Scott and Shawn. We love you Bryan: Marilyn and Norm Bartner & family, Dale Soules, & Peter Randsman. Viewing Friday, October 24, 2-5 and 7-9PM, Buckley Funeral Home, 445 W. 43 St, N.Y.C.
