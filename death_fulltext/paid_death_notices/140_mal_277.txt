
        DELAFIELD-Charles B. The North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System was deeply saddened by the passing of Charles B. Delafield, a long-time President of the former Community Hospital at Glen Cove. Mr. Delafield served our community with skill and compassion. We extend our sincere condolences to his entire family. Saul B. Katz, Chairman Board of Trustess John S. T. Gallagher, Chief Executive Officer David R. Dantzker, MD., President North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System
