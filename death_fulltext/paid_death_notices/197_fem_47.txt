
        FINDLAY -- Harriet Jones, died peacefully at her home in New Canaan, CT, on May 25th. She was 95 years old and had lived in New Canaan for 62 years. She pursued many interests during her lifetime, including Christian philanthropy, Bible studies, horticulture and gardening, antiques and art collecting, painting, farming, land conservation, historic preservation and photography. She was predeceased by her husband and her daughter, H. Lindsay Findlay. She is survived by two sons, David B. Findlay Jr. of New Canaan and H. Peter Findlay of Salisbury, CT, six grandchildren and 11 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, June 14th, at 11 am at the Congregational Church of New Canaan. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Mid-Fairfield Hospice, Wilton, CT, or to the New Canaan Garden Club. >>AD#
