
        WINSTON-Herbert G., MD. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, and friend. Died November 10, 2002. Revered clinician, professor and a founder of Albert Einstein College of Medicine. For over 50 years a practicing and teaching OB/GYN, he brought so many people into his world and taught us all how to live with grace, dignity, and compassion. There have been so few like him and we are greatly diminished by his loss. Funeral Service Wednesday 12:30 PM at the Weinstein Memorial Chapel Inc., 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, New York. Interment Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, New York.
