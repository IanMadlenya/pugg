
        SCHNEIDER--Jeff Stewart, 51, on July 23 in Boca Raton, FL. Cherished son of Phyllis, and of Jack who predeceased him. Loving brother of sister Ann, brother Bill and sister-inlaw Dr. Catherine Irwin. Loving stepfather of Cherie Hourigan and of his special companion, the chihahua mix he rescued, Sadie Mae. Jeff will always be remembered for his humanity, his gentleness and loyalty to all who crossed his short life's path.
