
        McNULTY-Raymond Patrick. On January 19, 1998, of New York City. Beloved husband of the late Irene. Loving father and father-inlaw of Kevin and Mary, Diane and Jim Wolfe, Patricia and Gerald Langlade, and Maureen and Frank Pichl. Devoted grandfather to Jennifer and Juliette, Tommy Finn, Laura and Tracey, and Charlie, Tommy and Emma. Beloved brother-in-law of Frank and Jack Short. His rapier sharp wit and love of a good duel earned him the nickname ''The Swordsman''. He did it his way. Friends will be received at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave, on January 21 and 22 from 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass January 23, 10 AM, Epiphany Church, 22 St. & 2 Ave.
