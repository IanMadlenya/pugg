
        NAVON--Shulamith Eisner. Jewish Life Network/Steinhardt Foundation mourns the untimely passing of our dear colleague Shulamith Navon. We will miss the friendship, compassion and generosity of this extraordinary woman who served as Executive Director of the Steinhardt Family Foundation in Israel. May her family and multitude of friends be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Michael H. Steinhardt, Chairman Rabbi Yitz Greenberg, President Rabbi David Gedzelman, Executive Director
