
        FORD-Charles E., Ed.D. Dr. Charles Ford will be remembered with gratitude by New York Medical College for his years of dedicated service as Dean of the Graduate School of Health Sciences, the Graduate School of Basic Medical Sciences and most recently, Academic Development. He was admired and respected for his devotion to excellence in teaching and for the spirit of collegiality he fostered in students and faculty members alike. The entire College community extends heartfelt condolences to his family. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D. Provost Dean, School of Medicine
