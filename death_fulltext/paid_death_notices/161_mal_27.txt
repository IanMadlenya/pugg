
        SARFATTI-Azaria, age 70, died after a long illness, June 1, 2000. Born October 23, 1929 in Salonica, Greece. Immigrated to the U. S. in 1956 with his mother and brother after suffering through the war years. Employed for 44 years at the firm of Isaac Hazan & Co. as the Production Manager. Caring and extremely competent at everything he did. He leaves his wife Anna; daughters Esther and Emily; grandsons David and Kenneth; and brother Isaak. From his other family the employees and management of Isaac Hazan & Co.
