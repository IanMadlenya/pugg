
        DAVIS-Arthur L. Loving and devoted husband of Lenore for 59 years, father of Nan, Ruth and Rob, father-in-law of Nina Davis and Bill Drew, grandfather of Jonathan and Jesse Davis, David and Mark Drew. Died peacefully surrounded by his family Tuesday, September 26. He was 81 years old. He was a founding member of Temple Sinai, Tenafly, NJ. Past president of the New Jersey American Institute of Architects, contributor to many charities and organizations throughout his long and healthy life. He was a man of great humor and a great lover of all things cultural. Services at ''Gutterman-Musicant'', 402 Park Street, Hackensack, NJ, Thursday, at 11:30 A.M. In lieu of flowers a donation to your charity of choice would be appreciated.
        DAVIS-Arthur. Temple Sinai of Bergen County deeply mourns the death of our longtime member, Arthur Davis, beloved husband of Lenore Davis. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire family. Dr. Bruce Block, Rabbi Jane Koch Gellman, President
