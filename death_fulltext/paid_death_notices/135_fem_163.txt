
        BENEROFE-Shirley. The officers and staff of the Westchester/ Greenwich division of State of Israel Bonds deeply mourn the passing of Shirley Benerofe, mother of Andrew, James and Mitchell and mother-in-law of Froma, Robin and Ada. She was a staunch supporter of many Jewish and community causes. Her presence among us will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt condolences to her family. Westchester/Greenwich Division Chairman Philip M. Schlussel Executive Director Ivan Helfand
