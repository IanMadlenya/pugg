
        LAPIN-Florence I. Eisenberg, 94, of Main Street, Stockbridge, MA, and formerly of 20 East 9th St., New York City, died Monday. Mrs. Lapin is survived by her daughter: Charlotte R. Isaacs of Stockbridge, Massachusetts; her son and daughter-in-law: Earl M. and Illean Lapin of Potomac, MD; her sister: Dorothy Bernard of New York City; and many nieces and nephews. Services were held on Wednesday at the Stockbridge Cemetery, Stockbridge, MA.
