
        COLVERD-Martin. Of Ojai, Calif., September 22 1928-March 16, 1999. Born in London and educated at Balliol College (Oxford), Columbia University and California State University-Northridge, he Worked as a librarian or records manager for American Electric Power, Columbia University, Louis Dreyfus and the N.Y. Metropolitan Transportation Authority. Cremation private. Contributions in his memory would be appreciated to a charity of your choice. We shall always recall with affection his English accent and how wondrously it withstood 44 years of American assault, his insights and wit, his passions for movies and beaches and views.
