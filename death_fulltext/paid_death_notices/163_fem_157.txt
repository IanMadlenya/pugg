
        LOEMKER - Dorothy Rowden. Died in Bridgeport, CT on August 10, 2000 of complications following pneumonia. She was born on February 27, 1901 in Woodsville, NH. A 1921 graduate of Simmons College, the culmination of her long and distinguished professional career was as Assistant Director and Secretary of the John and Mary R. Markle Foundation a position she held for 20 years. She is survived by twin children Thomas R. Loemker of Fairfield, CT., and (Mrs. Edward) Elizabeth Furber of Southport, ME, 6 grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren.
