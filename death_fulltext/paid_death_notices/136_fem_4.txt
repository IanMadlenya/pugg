
        MILLER-Ann Rosenthal. Age 63 on April 30, 1998, of Scarsdale, NY, after a long and courageous battle with ovarian cancer. Dear wife of William S. Miller. Loving mother of Elizabeth M. and Randolph Guggenheimer, III, Mark R. and Tonya Miller, Rachel L. Miller, and Steven W. Miller. Beloved grandma Ann to Laura Claire Guggenheimer, Brian James Guggnheimer and Samuel Yong Miller. Devoted sister, aunt, niece, colleague and friend. Service at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY at 10:00 AM on Monday, May 4, 1998. In Ann's memory a fund will be established at The Westchester Reform Temple to provide additional support for those in need of healing as well as comfort for the bereaved.
        MILLER-Ann. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its devoted member, co-founder and guiding light of our Caring Community, wife of our past President William S. Miller, and extends sympathy to her bereaved family. Marjorie Miller, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi Judith Schindler, Assoc. Rabbi
