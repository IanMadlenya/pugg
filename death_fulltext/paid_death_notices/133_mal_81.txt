        GEWANTER-David. The Officers, Executive Board, and Members of The Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York, Inc, mourns the passing of our member David Gewanter of Lipsky and Rosenthal Inc. Mr. Gewanter over the years made a valuable contribution to the plumbing industry on various Committees and Boards. Our deepest sympathy to his son, Michael and all his bereaved family. Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York Lawrence J. Levine, Pres. John O'Donnell, Exec Vice President
