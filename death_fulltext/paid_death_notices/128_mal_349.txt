
        POSSES-Frederick. On September 22, 1997, in Kansas City, MO. Former resident of New York City and Amityville, LI. Son of the late Max and Minnie Posses; former husband of the late Catherine Moynahan Posses. Survived by his daughter Mary Posses of Kansas City, and by nieces, nephews and cousins. Born Brooklyn, NY, 1911. Educated at James Madison High School, the University of Alabama, and Brooklyn Law School. Served in U.S. Army during WW II. Practiced law in New York, Amityville, and London. Author of several books published in England.
