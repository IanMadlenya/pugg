
        FREUND - ROSENTHAL - Miriam K. On January 16, age 92. Wife of the late Milton B. Freund and of the late Harry Rosenthal. Mother of Matthew and Blanche Freund, and of Harry and Matta Freund. Grandmother of Michael and Sarah Freund, Rebecca Freund, John Freund, Carmel and Franco Kostolitz, Gilead and Geri Freund, and Liza Greenberg. Great-grandmother of ten. Former National President of Hadassah and Trustee of the United Israel Appeal, the Jewish National Fund, State of Israel Bonds and American Friends of the Hebrew University, author and lecturer. Services were held.
        FREUND - ROSENTHAL - Miriam. We extend heartfelt condolences to Harry I. Freund, on the loss of his beloved mother, Miriam. As Founders of the Medical Center and patrons of the Jesselson Heart Center, Harry and his wife, Matta, have helped to fulfill the hospital's mission to heal the sick and ease the pain of the afflicted. May the loving memories of this very special ''woman of valor'' bring consolation to Harry, his brother Matthew, their children, grandchildren and the entire family. May they be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, President Stuart Tauber, Executive Director
        FREUND - ROSENTHAL - Miriam K. A woman of valor, who can find? Blessed are you that you found her in your matriarch Miriam Freund-Rosenthal. May G-d grant you comfort and consolation. Her spirit will surely live on. Rebbetzin Esther Jungreis, President Hineni Barbara Janov, Exec. Director
        FREUND - ROSENTHAL - Miriam. Young Israel of Harrison mourns the passing of Miriam FreundRosenthal, beloved mother of its devoted member, Harry Freund and extends heartfelt condolences to Harry and Matta and the entire bereaved family. Yaakov Bienenfeld, Rabbi Ed Leventhal, President
