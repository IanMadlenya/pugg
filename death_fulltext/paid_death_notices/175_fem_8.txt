
        TURKEL-Minda. Passed away on Sunday, July 29, 2001. She had been a psychiatric social worker at Jacobi Hospital and had a private practice as well. She was a graduate of NYU and continued to be active in alumni affairs. She leaves behind a large group of family and friends who were devoted to her. She will be greatly missed by all of us. Funeral to be held at Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, NY on August 2 at 11:30AM.
