
        HODES-Anne R. At age 88. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Horace L. Hodes. Loving mother of Ruth Rabin and Dr. David Hodes. Adored grandmother of Betsy Rabin Gabay, Laura and Elizabeth Hodes, and the late Thomas Rabin. Great grandmother of Thomas and Benjamin Gabay. Valued community leader of the Manhasset School District and member of the Congregation Temple Beth El of Great Neck, she maintained high standards. Funeral Services Riverside Nassau North Chapel, Great Neck, New York, 12:30 PM, January 11.
        HODES-Anne. The Department of Pediatrics at the Mt. Sinai Medical Center notes with sorrow the passing of Anne Hodes, widow of longtime esteemed Chairman and beloved faculty member, Dr. Horace Hodes. We extend our sympathies to her son Dr. David Hodes and entire Hodes family.
        HODES-Anne. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its long time member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
