
        KARO--Timothy T. Age 38, died at New York Presbyterian Hospital on July 24, 2006, following a courageous battle with AIDS. Tim was born December 29, 1967 in New Britain, CT. He resided in NYC and worked as Training Manager at the New York Helmsley Hotel. He is survived by his mother and sister, Lenore and Carrie Karo of NYC, and his father Thomas Karo of TN. Memorial services will be held at 11AM, August 12 at Rutgers Presbyterian Church, 236 West 73 St. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Global Health Organization, 153 East Ave, Suite 32, Norwalk, CT 06851.
