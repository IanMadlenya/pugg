
        ROSENBERG-Barbara Schwab. Entered into rest July 24, 1997. Cherished wife of fifty years to Fred Rosenberg of Mamaroneck, NY. Beloved mother of Robert and his wife Diane of Princeton, NJ; Ann and her husband Eric Walsh of Portland, Oregon; Charles and his wife Jan of Port Chester, NY; Jean and her husband Peter Southwick of Arlington, MA. Lovingly remembered by her grandchildren Rebecca, Jessica, Robin, Dylan, Natalie and Lindsay. Funeral arrangements private. Memorial donations to Harvard Medical School, Office of Resource Development, 25 Shattuck Street, Boston, MA 02115, attn: Renal Research Fund.
