
        SHERMAN--Florence. On November 7 at home at age 85. Cherished, beloved and loving wife of Julius (John, Jules, Ginsy) for 62 years; passionately devoted mother of Rita and husband, Jeffrey Seward, and of Ray. NYC high school math teacher, artist, idealist, good friend, she changed the world through her deeds. Cared for magnificently by Dr. Norman Relkin, Dr. Arthur Lebowitz, Dr. David Duffy, and by Selena Baksh. She is always in our hearts. For those wishing to make a donation: NYC Chapter Alzheimer's Assoc, 360 Lexington Avenue, NYC 10017, www.alznyc.org
