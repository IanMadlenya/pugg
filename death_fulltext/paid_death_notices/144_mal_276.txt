
        SIEGEL-Fred K., 88, of Harrison, NY., and Palm Beach, FL. Died Thursday, January 14, 1999 after a long illness. Beloved husband for sixty years of Joan. Loving father of Kenneth and grandfather of Joseph and Eileen, father-in-law of Mary Claire, devoted brotherin-law of Ruth and Murray Schoen, loving uncle to Robert Schoen and Ronni Fitzgerald. He was a humanitarian and a philanthropist, former Chief Executive Officer and Chairman of the Board of National Shoes, Inc., a national retail shoe chain whose offices were in the Bronx NY. He will be fondly remembered by family and friends. Memorial services will be held in Palm Beach FL at Temple Israel in West Palm Beach Sunday, January 17 at 1 P.M. Graveside services will be in New York Tuesday, January 19 at 2 P.M. at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, Queens.
        SIEGEL-Fred. It is with deep sorrow that we mourn the passing of Fred Siegel, devoted friend and supporter of the United Hebrew Geriatric Center for many years. Condolences are extended to the bereaved family. Malcolm Lazarus, President David Shatz, Chairman Rita C. Mabli, Executive Vice President
