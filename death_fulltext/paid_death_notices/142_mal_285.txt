
        COONEY-David O. In Laramie, WY, on November 20, at age 58. Professor of Chemical Engineering at the University of Wyoming. Devoted husband of Peggy, and father of Eric and Jonathan. Survived also by his father, Edward Cooney of Winchester, MA, his sister, Kathleen Corrigan of Southport, CT, several nephews and a niece. David was predeceased by his brother Edward Cooney, Jr. Memorial service November 28, 11 AM, at the University of Wyoming Concert Hall.
