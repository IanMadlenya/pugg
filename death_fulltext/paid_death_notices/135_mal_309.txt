
        SAMPSON-Harvey E. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of Cornell University Medical College and The New York & Presbyterian Hospital are deeply saddened by the passing of our beloved friend and esteemed Cornell University Medical College Life Overseer Harvey E. Sampson. Mr. Sampson was a founding member of the Board of Overseers, rendering distinguished service as Vice Chairman of the Board from 1988 through 1990 and as Chairman from 1990 to 1992. He also served as Chairman of the Joint Board of The New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center for several years. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. Sanford I. Weill Chairman, Board of Overseers Cornell University Medical College Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Stephen and Suzanne Weiss Dean Cornell University Medical College Daniel B. Burke Co-Chairman, Board of Trustees The New York and Presbyterian Hospital John F. McGillicuddy Co-Chairman, Board of Trustees The New York and Presbyterian Hospital David B.
