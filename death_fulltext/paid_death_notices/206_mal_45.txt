
        BENJAMIN -- Emily (nee Stein). The Board and staff of F.E.G.S. note with sorrow the passing of Emily Benjamin, mother, grandmother, wife of Jack C. Benjamin and beloved sister of Joseph Stein, Jr., chair and president of F.E.G.S. Mrs. Benjamin was active with Temple Sinai in New Orleans, where the funeral services will be held. Joe and the Stein family have been deeply involved in philanthropy and Joe has served with great distinction on the board of F.E.G.S. since 1980. We offer our deepest condolences to Joe and the entire Stein family. Al Miller, CEO, F.E.G.S.
        BENJAMIN -- Emily (Stein). The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Emily Ann Benjamin, beloved sister of Joseph Stein, Jr. Joseph, a leader in the Jewish community, has served as president and chair of UJA-Federation's beneficiary agency F.E.G.S. since 1993. We extend our deepest sympathies to him and his family, and to the entire Benjamin family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
