
        QUARATO--Eleanor. Of Cortlandt Manor, died on Friday, September 8, 2006 at the age of 76. She was born in the Bronx on April 22, 1930 to David and Rose Amodeo. On May 2, 1959 she married Anthony Quarato. He predeceased her in March, 1995. She is survived by her two sons, Rev. Fr. Robert A. Quarato, pastor of Holy Rosary Church, Bronx, Kenneth and his wife, Susan, and three grandchildren, Adam, Emily, and Eric Quarato, all of Cortlandt Manor. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 11:00 a.m. Tuesday at St. Columbanus Church, Cortlandt Manor, followed by interment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla. Visiting at Dorsey-Carlone Funeral Home, Peekskill, Monday 2-4 and 7-9 p.m.
