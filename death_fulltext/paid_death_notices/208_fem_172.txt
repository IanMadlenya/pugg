
        ADLER--Frances, 87, died May 20, 2004 in Coral Springs, FL. She was married to the late Jack Adler for 59 years. She is survived by her devoted daughter Gloria and her husband Michael; her loving son Paul and his wife Janet. She is also survived by four beautiful grandchildren, David, Deborah, Marc and Bethany and her brother Edward Salzman. She was dearly loved by all her family and friends and will be missed by all. Services will be Sunday, May 23, 2004 at Noon at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, Great Neck, NY.
