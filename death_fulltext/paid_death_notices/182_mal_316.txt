
        PARSONS-Reverend Harold J. On March 21, 2002. Ordained in 1929. Oldest priest in the Archdiocese of New York. Last assignment was at St. Peter's, RC Church, Barclay St, NYC. Served many parishes in the Archdiocese and affected many people in the practice of their faith. His dedication to the Catholic Church, knew no bounds. Survived by one cousin, Irene Smith of Vermont. Reposing at Vanella's Funeral Chapel, 2860 Long Beach Rd, Oceanside, LI. Mass of Christian burial, Sacred Heart RC Church, Island Park, Saturday, 10:00 AM. Visitation 2-5, 7-9
