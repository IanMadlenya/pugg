
        SUNSHINE--Margaret, 89, on January 26, 2005. Well known make up designer for Broadway, movies, TV. Personal make up designer for Lauren Bacall, Ann Reinking, Joanne Woodward, Meryl Streep, Swoozie Kurtz, Barbara Harris, Bette Davis, etc. Everyone she touched became more beautiful. She will always be loved & missed. Survived by son, Paul; daughter, Carol, and grandchildren, Angela, Peter, Susan & Michael.
