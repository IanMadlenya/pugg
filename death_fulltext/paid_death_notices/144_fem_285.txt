
        DURBIN-Elizabeth. 62. Of NYC and Shelter Island. Suddenly, on January 25. Beloved wife of Seymour Weissman. Loving grandmother of Zachary, Vanessa, Hannah, Jenna, Kyle, Laura and Nicholas, stepmother of Judy, Eric, David and Adam. Courageously outspoken in defense of the vulnerable, humanist, Professor of Economics at NYU's Wagner Graduate School of Public Service for nearly 30 years. She was a devoted friend, colleague and teacher to many, and a caring, compassionate and generous person. She will be missed by all who knew her. Services on Friday, January 29th, 12 noon at ''The Riverside'' West 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations to Shelter Island Senior Council, Shelter Island, New York 11964.
