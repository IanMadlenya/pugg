
        PARKINSON-Ellott F. On September 25, 2001. Beloved husband of Lee Ward Parkinson. Devoted father of Ann Eraugey of England. Loving brother of John and Doris of England. Stepfather of Dr. Richard Ward and Dr. Kenneth Ward. Cherished grandfather and stepgrandfather. He was a British citizen who served on a British Navy aircraft carrier in WWII. He was a very special person and he will be missed very much. Family will be receiving friends on Saturday, 2-4, 6-8PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Funeral service on Sunday, 1:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to Lenox Hill Hospital, 100 E 77 St, NY, NY 10021.
