
        MILBANK-Helen Kirkpatrick, 88. Journalist, war correspondent and author, died Monday, Dec. 29, 1997, at her home in Williamsburg, VA. The daughter of the late Lyman B. Kirkpatrick, Sr., and Lyde Paull Kirkpatrick, she was born in Rochester, New York, October 18, 1909. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Smith College, Mrs. Milbank was a foreign and war correspondent for the Chicago Daily News from 1939 to 1947. She published two books and numerous articles dealing with international politics. A memorial service will be held at 11 A.M., on Saturday, January 10th, at Bruton Parish Church, Williamsburg, Virginia. A second service and burial will follow at a later date in Nelson, New Hampshire.
