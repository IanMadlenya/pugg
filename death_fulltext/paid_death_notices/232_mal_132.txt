
        GRAYSON--Dr. Howard Bertram, on Sunday May 14, 2006 age 88. Of Larchmont, NY and Boynton Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Blossom. Devoted father of Sandra Grayson (Dr. Perry Garber) and Laurie Grayson Kunkes (Dr. Jeffrey Kunkes). Loving grandfather of Diana, Russell, and Douglas Garber and Craig and Glenn Kunkes. Beloved brother of Arnold. Graduate of Cornell University and University of Pennsylvania Dental School. Former Attending Dentist at Westchester County Medical Center of Valhalla, NY. All services will be private.
        $(6$)GRAYSON -- Howard. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Howard Grayson, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Charles J. Block, President
