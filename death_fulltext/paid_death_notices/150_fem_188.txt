
        TASKA-Margaret Ford. Of NYC died July 13, 1999. She is survived by her husband, her brother and two nephews. A memorial service will be held in the fall. A graduate of Connecticut College for Women, Mrs., Taska worked for the Office of War Information in London, in Luxembourg and in Germany. Upon returning to NY after the war's end, she joined the newly created CARE where she became executive secretary to the successive heads of that organization until her retirement in 1986. She was also active in the League for the Hard of Hearing.
