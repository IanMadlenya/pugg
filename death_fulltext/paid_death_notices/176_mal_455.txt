
        LARSON-Jerry Ole. On September 20, 2001, age 64, of Palm Beach, FL and Owings Mills, MD. Longtime resident of New York City. Retired account executive for TV Guide. He is survived by his best friend Marilyn Meyerhoff, two sons Graham Larson of Santa Monica, CA and Corbin Larson of New York City, and daughter Brooke Larson Thomas of Washington, DC, three brothers and 12 grandchildren. A memorial service was held at the Second Presbyterian Church (Baltimore, MD) on September 22. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Pulmonary Department, Johns Hopkins Hospital at Bayview, Baltimore, MD 21224.
