
        DAVIS-Maria N. (Nee Nekos) of Summit, NJ, and South Hampton, L.I., on September 21, 1997. Wife of George A. Davis, mother of Stephen of New York City, Carol Fiske of Hartford, Conn., and Leslie Dahl of Greenwich, Conn., sister of Peter Nekos of Pleasantville, NY, and three grandchildren. A Memorial service will be held on Thursday, Spetember 25, 1997 at 2 p.m. from Calvary Episcopal Church, Summit, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations in her name may be made to the Fresh Air Fund, New York City, or The Summit Speech School, Summit, NJ. William R. Dangler Funeral Home, Summit, NJ.
