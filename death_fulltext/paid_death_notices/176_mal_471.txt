
        STEWART-Richard H., 35, is missing from the World Trade Center disaster. A bond trader with Cantor Fitzgerald, he formerly worked at Lehman Brothers. A memorial service to celebrate his life will be held Friday, September 28, 11 A.M. at The Lawrenceville School chapel. In his memory, an Ice Hockey Scholarship fund has been established at The Lawrenceville School, P.O. Box 6125, Lawrenceville, New Jersey 08648.
