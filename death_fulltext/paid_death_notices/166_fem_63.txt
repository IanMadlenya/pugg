
        WEINER-Phyllis (nee Lifton). On November 3, 2000, while traveling in China with her devoted husband of 39 years, Howard. She will be remembered and missed by her children, Jeffrey, Cynthia, Michael and Jeanne Gerstley Weiner, for her spirit, humor, and warmth. She is the cherished sister of Martin and Ellie Lifton of Old Westbury, N.Y., & Dr. & Mrs. Robert Weiner of Denver, CO. She is the beloved grandmother of Zach, Jake, Emmy and Allie. She will be sadly missed by her many nieces, nephews, cousins and close friends, whom she adored. The family will receive visitors Saturday, November 11, from 7-9PM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 St. A funeral service will be held Sunday, November 12, at 11:45AM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to Smith College, Northampton, MA. 01063, The New York Philharmonic, Avery Fisher Hall, 10 Lincoln Center Plaza, NY, NY 10023, or Lenox Hill Hospital, 100 East 77 Street, New York, New York 10021.
