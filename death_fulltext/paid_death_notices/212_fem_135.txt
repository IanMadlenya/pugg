
        BENVENISTE -- Jaye. 87, passed away peacefully at home on September 17, 2004. She successfully battled pancreatic cancer for many years. She will be forever in the hearts and souls of her loving nephews Stuart Shikiar and Albert Shikiar, her greatnephews Samuel Max Shikiar and David Shikiar, and greatnieces Karen Masters and Susan Santillo. Her wit, wisdom, courage and grace will be fondly remembered by the many people she touched in her rich life. Service Tuesday, September 21, at 11:30 AM, Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
