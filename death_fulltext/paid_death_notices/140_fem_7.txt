
        MACLAY-Jennifer Truslow. Of Charlottesville, VA. Formerly of Wilton, CT. Died in a car accident on August 28 in Charlottesville. She is survived by her sister, Susan Finnis of Cheshire, England & four children, Michael Truslow of Charlottesville, Sara Truslow Hardin of N.Y., Elizabeth Salvatore of Lucas, TX, and John Truslow of Oyster Bay, N.Y., as well as 11 grandchildren. Services will be held at St. Matthew's Episcopal Church, Wilton, CT., on Thursday, September 3rd at 11:00 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Wilton Library Association, Wilton, CT.
