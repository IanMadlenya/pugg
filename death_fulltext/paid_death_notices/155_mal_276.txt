
        JACOBSON-Samuel K. Long Island homebuilder, died on December 17 at the age of 80 at his home in Deerfield Beach, Florida. He is survived by his loving wife of 36 years, Sylvia; his four children Martin Klepper, Carin Doben, Richard Klepper, and Ellen Delgado; his sister Muriel Wolff; and 13 grandchildren. He moved to Florida in 1969 from Great Neck, Long Island, and was an Officer in the Coast Guard during World War II. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him.
