
        ZUCKER--Gary, Dr., on July 20, 2006. Husband of Ruth Zucker. Father of Paul (Sherry) and Norman (Katherine). Grandfather of David. Dr. Gary Zucker was a former Assistant Chief of Medicine at Beth Israel Medical Center, NY. Dr. Zucker was the developer of the music therapy program in association with the Louis Armstrong Foundation. He was also a physician and board member for NYANA New York Association for New Americans. Dr. Zucker was also an avid gardner. Interment will be held Monday July 24, 11:00 AM at Garden of Remembrance Cemetery, Clarksburg, MD.
