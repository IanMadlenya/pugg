
        TUCK-Catherine. The Clinical Faculty of the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University mourns the untimely passing of Dr. Catherine Tuck. Dr. Tuck's career exemplified commitment to excellence in clinical medicine. Her particular love of teaching both of medical students, house officers, and patients will be her enduring legacy. We extend our most sincere sympathies to her family. Joseph Tenenbaum, M.D., Chair Columbia Faculty Practice Organization
