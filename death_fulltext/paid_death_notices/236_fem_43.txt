
        WALLISON--Pearl L., on September 6, 2006, beloved Mom to Peter and Frieda, and Jane and Hugh; Nana to Ethan, Jeremy and Kristina, Elizabeth and George, Rebecca and Chris, Amanda and Tom; and great-Nana to Jonathan, Skylar, Matthew and Allegra. A teacher, a music-lover and full of ''joie de vivre,'' she loved and was beloved by her children, her friends and the late Wally. She was 96.
