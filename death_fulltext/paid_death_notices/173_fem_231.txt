
        TIERS-Margaret. Died April 19, 2001 of natural causes. She was born in Paris, France, January 16, 1912. Her parents were Cornelius Tiers of Paris, and Margaret Drumm Tiers, of Montreal. Mrs. Herbert was married to E.N. Potter of N.Y. She leaves son, Allan Robbins Potter, and wife Norma, of Mission Viejo, CA, daughter, Margot Potter Denny, and husband Robert, of Tucson, AZ, a grand daughter, Margot Kiser Jones, and husband Peter.
