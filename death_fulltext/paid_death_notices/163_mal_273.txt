
        ROSEN-William, age 89. Died on August 20th, Los Angeles, Calif. Survived by devoted wife Ray, daughter Judy (Barry) Wollman, granddaughters Elaine (Douglas) Mura, Nancy (Todd) Albinger, and Wendy (Brian) Globerman, great grandchildren Michael, Dustin, and Bailey Albinger, Cathryn, Eric, Jacob Mura and Cassidy & Gracie Globerman. Daughter Susan (Ted) Curran predeceased him. Brother Jesse Rosen survives. For many years he resided in White Plains, New York, and retired to Rancho Bernardo, California.
