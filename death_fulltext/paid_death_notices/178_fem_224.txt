
        STRUHL-Anne. The Yeshiva High School Board of Directors, Faculty and Students mourn the loss of the Struhl family matriarch. By her example, Anne Struhl taught her family the importance of being leaders in Jewish education. Her legacy is immortalized in her children, grandchildren and greatgrandchildren's actions. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Kenneth Brander, Founder Mrs. Pam Turk, President Rabbi Perry Tirschwell, Principal
