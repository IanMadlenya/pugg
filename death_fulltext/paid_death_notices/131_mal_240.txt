
        MECHCATIE-Reza, M.D. Born in Rasht, Iran on July 7, 1929, to the late Abdullah and Marzieh Mizrameshkatikhan, died in Rye, New York on December 16, 1997. A graduate of the University of Tehran and the University of Paris Medical School, Dr. Mechcatie was an internist and cardiologist with a private practice in Rye and was associated with United Hospital Medical Center for over 30 years. He is survived by his beloved wife Audrey Yearsley Mechcatie, his sons Oliver C. and Anthony P. Mechcatie, his daughter Elizabeth M. Vanegas, her husband Enrique and their children Sabina Grace and Maximilian. Dr. Mechcatie will be deeply missed by his family and friends, colleagues and patients, and will always be remembered for his dedication to his community. His family will receive friends at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, New York from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM and from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM on Friday, December 19, 1997. Funeral Services will be held Saturday, December 20, 1997, 1:30 PM at Christ's Episcopal Church, Rye, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to United Hospital Medical Center, 406 Boston Post Rd., Port Chester, New York 10573.
