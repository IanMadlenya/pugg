
        RICHARDS - Lloyd. The Board of Trustees and staff of Theatre Development Fund mourn the loss of our beloved colleague and friend. Lloyd Richards was truly one of the greatest voices in the American theatre during the past 50 years. TDF was honored to have had Lloyd guide us as our Board President from 1977-1981. During the intervening years he remained a great supporter and provided counsel to us. He will be greatly missed. Our condolences to his family. David D. Holbrook, Chairman Victoria Bailey, Exec. Dir. Veronica Claypool Managing Director
