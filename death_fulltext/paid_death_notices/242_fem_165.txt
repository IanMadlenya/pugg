
        FERGUSON--Katharine, (nee Crowninshield), 72, died of cancer on March 18, 2007 in Gulf Stream, FL. She is survived by her husband, David L. Ferguson, her sister Louise C. Riemer, two nieces, two nephews, a great-niece and a great-nephew. A service will be held on March 29, 2007 at Trinity Church, Boston, MA at 11 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory can be made to Tower Hill Botanic Garden, 11 French Drive, Boylston, MA 01505, or Garden Club of America, 14 E. 60th St., NY, NY 10022. Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, Delray Beach.
