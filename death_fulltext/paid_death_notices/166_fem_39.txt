
        STUX-Linda (born Bayless) died peacefully October 27, 2000 at age 56. Wife of Stefan and 7 year old Alva, sister of Shirley Flinchum of Cincinnati, Ohio. Educator and cofounder of Stux Gallery of Boston and New York. Universally loved and admired for her extraordinary compassion, gentleness and idealism. Contributions in Linda's memory may be made to The Solomon Schechter School of Manhattan, 50 East 87th Street, New York, New York 10128.
