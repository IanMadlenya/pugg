
        RIBED NIEULANT-Pilar, 87. Died in Seville, Spain on May 14, 2002. Her New York family joins her family in Spain in mourning her passing with deep sorrow and affection. She lived a long & creative life & devoted herself passionately to her children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. She had boundless enthusiasm for life, family and friendship. She will be greatly, sadly and deeply missed by all who knew her and the many lives she touched.
