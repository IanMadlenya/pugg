        ROSENBLATT-Emanuel G. AMIT is profoundly grieved by the passing of the beloved brother of our devoted national vice president, Bashie R. Selevan. Emanuel Rosenblatt personified the attributes of warmth, kindness and generosity. A lover of Israel, Zionism and benevolent patron of AMIT's children in Israel, he will be sorely missed as a majestic leader, philanthropist and friend.         ROSENBLATT-Emanuel.
