
        MORAN--Laura Regina de Bonneville. Of NYC and Palm Beach, FL died Tuesday, February 10, 2004, at Good Samaritan Hospital. Laura, the daughter of the late George F. Moran and Gertrude E. Corbett Moran of Manhattan, NY, is survived by numerous loving cousins, nieces, nephews and friends who cherish the memory of her sterling wit and joy for life. She was a graduate of Manhattanville College of the Sacred Heart, class of 1953, and was a member of the New York Junior League. A funeral mass will be held at St. Vincent Ferrer Church in NYC at 10 AM on Tuesday, February 17, 2004 with burial to follow at St. John's Cemetery in Queens, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in memory of Laura R. Moran to St. Edward's Church or to St. Vincent Ferrer Church.
