
        GINSBERG-Frances Mary (Porcelli). Died at 80 years of age. Beloved wife of the late Robert Ginsberg, President of the Fur Association of America and devoted mother of Roger Ginsberg; PGA Touring professional and current Golf Director of Muttontown Country Club, and Frank C. Ginsberg, Chairman, CEO & Creative Director of Avrett, Free & Ginsberg Advertising. She was also the proud grandmother of 3 granddaughters as well as 3 greatgrandchildren. Frances and Robert were one of the first families to move to the Woodlands in 1968 and members of the Woodlands Golf Club. Frances Ginsberg was devoted in her younger years to many charities in the Riverdale, Fieldston, New York area.
