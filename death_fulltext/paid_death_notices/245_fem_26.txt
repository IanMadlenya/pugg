
        REED--Constance Culbertson Feeley. Beloved wife of Henry Hope Reed, died peacefully on May 30th, 2007. She was the daughter of Brigadier General USMC James A. Feeley. Graduate of Punahou School, Honolulu and Stanford University, Class of 1953. Reporter for The Washington Post and short story and staff writer for The New Yorker. A Requiem Mass will be held for her on Wednesday, June 6 at 3pm at St. Thomas Church, Fifth Avenue on 53rd Street. Private burial will take place at St. James the Less Church in Philadelphia.
