
        SNYDER--Arthur I., M.D. With immense sadness, the family of Dr. Arthur I. Snyder of New York City and Washington, CT. announces his death on March 15, 2004 from complications resulting from pneumonia. Dr. Snyder was an internist and rheumatologist for 54 years, practicing most recently as a founding partner of New York Physicians, Inc. He graduated from Columbia University and Columbia College of Physicians & Surgeons, and served in the U.S. Navy and the U.S. Coast Guard. He was an adoring husband of 32 years to Marilyn Benstock Synder, and a kind, loving and ever-supportive father to daughters Margaret Hamilton and Katherine Snyder, and their husbands Robin Hamilton and Tim Culvahouse, and to step children Eric Berkeley and Nancy Berkeley Bynum, and their spouses Christina Berkeley and Frank Bynum. He took tremendous pride in his grandchildren, Rebecca, Emily, Jessica, Sasha, Theo, Kit and Georgia. Dr. Snyder is also survived by his sister, Judith Jaffe. He graced our days with his warmth, elegance and dry wit. Arthur, you were the best part of us and will be missed more than words can say. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Arthritis Foundation, New York Chapter, 122 E. 42 St, 18th Flr, N.Y., N.Y. 10168. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, April 13th, 2:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St.
