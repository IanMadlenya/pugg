
        MIRKIN--Julian, 77, April 5, 2006 from Myositis. Grew up in Brooklyn, Korean War vet, Master Mason of the Victoria Seagate Masonic Lodge in Brooklyn. Survived by his wife of 52 years, Florence; daughters, Cheryl (Alan) Fink, Marcy (Adam Schutz); 3 granddaughters, Jana, Deena and Leah; and sister, Myra Raykoff. Services Sunday, April 9, at Gutterman's, Rockville Centre, NY, 12 Noon.
