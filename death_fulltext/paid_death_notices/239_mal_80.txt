
        JACOBSON--Alan J., 88, passed away peacefully surrounded by family Monday, December 4th. He is survived by his loving family, wife of 53 years Jeanne R. Jacobson, two daughters, two son-inlaws and four grandchildren. He was preceded in death by brother Walter. WWII veteran, lifelong resident of NYC, freelance photographer. Alan Jacobson was a life force who leaves a legacy of energy, passion and kindness, and his absence will be greatly felt by his family and friends. Memorial service Friday, December 8th, Temple Emanuel, 1 E. 65th Street, 12 noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Temple Emanuel or to the Alzheimer's Association.
