
        ARNOWITZ--Janece of Boca Raton, FLorida died Wednesday at the age of 68, surrounded by her family. Her husband of 40 years, William Arnowitz, predeceased her in 1995. She is survived by her four children, Deborah Ashe, Holly Arnowitz, Sherman Arnowitz (Pam), and Daniel Arnowitz (Susan), and her seven grandchildren Hannah, Nathan, Samantha, Alex, Isabel, Aimee and Jillian. Donations may be made to The Visiting Nurse Service of New York Hospice and The American Cancer Society.
