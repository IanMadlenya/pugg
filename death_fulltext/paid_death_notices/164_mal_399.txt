
        PRINCE-Herbert I. Formerly of NYC and Boca Raton, FL, passed away gently in his sleep September 23, 2000. Beloved husband of Marian for 41 years. Father of Robert J. Prince, Sarabelle, Laurie Ann. Dear grandfather to Molly, Abigail, Benjamin, Danny, Julie and Jennifer. Served as an officer in the U.S. Air Force during WWII. Founded Serascreen Corp. in 1951. Retired in 1985 after a successful career as a respected leader in the screen printing industry. A kind, loving, gentle man with a warm sense of humor, you will always be in our hearts. We are grateful you are at peace. We knew you as a true ''prince.''
