
        SEGAL-Marion Hasterlik. Of Miami Beach, FL, on August 20, 1999. Age 90. Born in Chicago, IL, past resident of Great Neck, NY. Adored wife of the late Arthur B. She is loved and greatly missed by her daughters Liz Berenson, Susan Glass, and Marjory Sheppard. Beloved mother-in-law to Joel Glass and Stuart Sheppard. Cherished grandmother to Cindy Bernstein, Michael Harris, J.J. Harris, Melissa Sheppard, Arthur Sheppard. Truly a lady, beautiful inside and out. She touched the lives of all who knew her.
