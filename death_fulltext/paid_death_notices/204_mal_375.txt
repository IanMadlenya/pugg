
        KLEIN--Ira E., D.D.S. The Greater New York Academy of Prosthodontics belatedly regrets the passing of Dr. Ira E. Klein, a charter member of the Academy, founded in 1954. He was a distinguished past president, an honored and respected teacher, and a dedicated and devoted family man. His presence in our Academy will truly be missed. Dennis N. Morea, D.D.S. President
