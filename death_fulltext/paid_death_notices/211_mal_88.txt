
        SPIEGEL--Ed. On August 8, 2004, beloved husband of Deanne, cherished father of Elana and Brad and devoted brother to Barbara Iskowitz. Ed was a former partner of Goldman Sachs and involved in several charities including The National Neurofibromatosis Foundation, The Lymphoma Foundation and St. Judes. His love and generosity knew no bounds. Funeral Tuesday, August 10 at Community Synagogue, Sands Point, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made in Ed's memory to the National Neurofibromatosis Foundation, Inc. 95 Pine Street, 16 Floor, N.Y., N.Y. 10005, attention Jackie Medina.
        SPIEGEL -- Edward. The Board of Directors, staff and volunteers of the National Neurofibromatosis Foundation Inc. mourn the untimely passing of our longtime friend and board member, Edward Spiegel. His leadership and generous support enabled the Foundation to implement many crucial programs that directly benefited patients and families with Neurofibromatosis. We will greatly miss his strength, warmth and intelligence. We send our deepest condolences to his wife, Deanne, and his children, Brad and Elana. Rick Horvitz, Esq., Chairman Peter Bellermann, President John Risner, Exec. Director
