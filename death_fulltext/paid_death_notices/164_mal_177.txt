
        BERKUN-Mordecai, MD. We note with sorrow, the death of our friend and colleague. Dr. Berkun was a distinguished member of the Department of Medicine who served actively at North Shore University Hospital for more than three decades. His patients and colleagues will remember him with respect and gratitude as an excellent clinician and teacher. As his heritage, he leaves a record of accomplishment and achievements with the knowledge that he has contributed greatly to the health of his community. Lawrence Scherr, MD, MACP Chairman, Department of Medicine North Shore University Hospital
