
        BLACKER-Natalie, of Delray Beach, Florida, died August 8th 1997. She was a devoted wife and best friend of Arthur for 55 years, loving mother and mother-in-law of Ellen and Joel Walker and Elyse and Larry Faltz, and proud and loving grandmother to Brian and Allison Walker. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hospice By the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33486.
