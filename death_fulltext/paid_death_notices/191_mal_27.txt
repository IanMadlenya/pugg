
        ALLEN-Andrew S., 60. Died of cancer December 1st in Passaic. A Harvard graduate with a Ph.D. in Linguistics from the University of California-Berkeley, he taught abroad in Togo, Benin and Saudi Arabia, and in this country at the University of Tennessee and Appalachian State University. He was a Fulbright Scholar in Rumania. At his death, he was a member of the English Department at the University of Puerto Rico-Mayaguez and of the Executive Committee of the Comparative Romance Linguistics Section of the Modern Language Association. A caring brother, faithful friend, and dedicated student and teacher, Andy worked selflessly for the equality of all people.
