
        SCHWARTZ-Saul A., M.D. 91. Professor Emeritus, New York Medical College. Formerly of Mt. Vernon, NY. Passed away Sunday in Cherry Hill, NJ. He practiced medicine in the Bronx for 65 years. He established a chapter of Alpha Omega Alpha, the honorary medical society. He worked with Dr. Burrill Crohn at Mt. Sinai and Montefiore Hospital. Dr. Schwartz was a Diplomate of the American Board of Pediatrics and the American Board of Internal Medicine and was past president of the Bronx Society of Internal Medicine. Father of Richard Schwartz, Cantor Michael Schwartz and Marilyn Axler. Brother of Irving Schwartz. Also survived by nine grandchildren. Funeral service is 12:30 PM today, ''Riverside Memorial Chapel,'' 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon. The family will return to the home of Cantor Michael and Diana Schwartz.
