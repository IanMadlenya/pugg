
        PIERCE--Nancy W., on July 8th. Beloved wife of Morton. Loving mother of Matthew and Nicholas. Devoted sister of Larry Washor. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Service Sunday, July 10th, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
        PIERCE--Nancy. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Nancy Pierce. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner, Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. George Asch, Secretary
