
        BOYD - Walter J. Jr. of Summit, New Jersey, on January 6, 1997. Husband of Doris Boyd (nee Kuka), father of Robert, Richard, John, and Barbara Yosaitis, also survived by seven grandchildren. A Funderal service will be held on Friday, January 10, 1997 at 11:00 AM from St. John's Lutheran Church, Springfield Ave. Summit, NJ. Visiting will be held Thursday Jan. 9, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM the William R. Dangler Funder Home, 309 Springfield Avenue, Summit, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name may be made to St. John's Lutheran Church, Summit, NJ. Interment in Somerset Hills Cemetery, Basking Ridge, NJ.
