
        LOTZ-R. James, Jr. 74. Saturday, November 22. Born in Reading, PA. Descendant of Col. Nicholas Lotz of the Revolutionary Army. Chairman and Cofounder, International Management Advisors, Inc. Survived by his wife, Constance Klages Lotz, his daughter, Brenda Davidson, his son, Roy James Lotz, III, and grandchildren Nicholas, Cecelia and Ian. Memorial services today, 12:30, Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church (at 55th Street).
