
        FISHMAN-William Harold. Born in Winnipeg, March 2, 1914, died in La Jolla, Calif., January 25, 2001. Scientist and cancer researcher & founder of the La Jolla Cancer Research Foundation, now the Bernham Institute. A private funeral has been held. He is survived by his wife, Lillian (nee Waterman); & children, Joel, Nina and Daniel, and 3 grandchildren. Contributions in Dr. Fishman's memory may be sent to the Bernham Institute, La Jolla, CA.
