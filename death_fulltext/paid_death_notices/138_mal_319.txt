
        LAUFER-Simon. We are deeply saddened by the passing of Simon Laufer. His cheerful dispositon and extraordinary benevolence will be missed by all. Beloved and devoted husband to Fran, our dynamic founding President. Together they helped establish and nurture our organization as they did with countless other institutions. To Fran, and to the entire family, we offer our heartfelt sympathy. May the Almighty console you among the other mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rivkah Laufer Bikur Cholim
        LAUFER-Simon. Young Israel of Hillcrest mourns the passing of one of its builders and movers. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his devoted wife Fran and the entire family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Simcha Krauss, Rabbi Sandy Cohen, President Sidney Schwarzenberger, Chairman of the Board
