
        ATKINSON-Ruth S., age 78, of Rossmoor, Jamesburg, NJ, passed away Wednesday, March 14th, at home. Born in NYC, she was a lifelong resident of NYC before moving to Rossmoor for approximately 15 years. She is survived by her husband, Ralph H. Atkinson of Rossmoor, her sister, Juliana Nixon of New Bern, NC, her 3 nieces and her cousin, Peggy Schilling of NYC. Memorial services will be held Monday, March 19th, at 11 AM, at Rossmoor Community Church Meetinghouse, Village Mall, Monroe Township, NJ.
