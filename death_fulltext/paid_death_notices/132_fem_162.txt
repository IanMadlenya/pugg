
        GERGES-Florence June. Devoted wife of Abraham G. Gerges, Justice of New York State Supreme Court. Beloved mother of Nina Kurtz and mother-in-law of Donnie Scott Kurtz. Loving grandmother of Sarah and Jamie. Cherished sister of Murray Benenson. Former Assistant to the New York State Deputy Housing Commissioner. She spent her life volunteering for good causes. The Mayor's Volunteer Action Committee, school volunteer, Girl Scouts. She was a beautiful woman inside and out. She was happiest helping others and will long be remembered. She was our best friend. Funeral will take place Tuesday, January 20, 1 P.M., at Congregation Mt. Sinai, 250 Cadman Plaza West, Brooklyn. All donations to the American Cancer Society.
