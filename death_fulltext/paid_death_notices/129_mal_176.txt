
        HICKEY-Robert J., 80. Of Keene, N.H. In Keene on October 13. Formerly of New York City. Was Regional Chief Inspector, Eastern Division of U.S. Postal Service. He was a bridge life master. Father of Delina Hickey, Keene, Elizabeth M. Hickey, Denver, brother of Joseph Hickey, Fort Lauderdale, FL., two grandsons. A mass of Christian burial Friday 10AM at St. Bernard Church, Keene. Donations to Thorne-Sagendorph Art Gallery, Keene State College, 222 Main Street, Keene, NH. 03431.
