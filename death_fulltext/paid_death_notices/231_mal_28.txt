
        MITCHELL--Craig K. A longtime resident of Southampton, died at his home on March 30, 2006. He was 89 years old. Mr. Mitchell attended St. Paul's School and Princeton Unviversity, during WWII, he served as a pilot in the U.S. Army Air Force. He is survived by his wife Nicoletta Gioia and three nephews. In lieu of flowers, please send donation to ARF in Wainscott, Long Isl, NY.
