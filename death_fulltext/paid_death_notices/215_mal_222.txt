
        FLESCH -- Irving ''Jimmy''. Temple Shaaray Tefila records with profound sorrow the death of our long time and loyal member, Irving ''Jimmy'' Flesch, at age 100. Heartfelt condolences are extended to our members, his wife, Elizabeth ''Liz'' Flesch, grandchildren, David and Alison Bauer, and great grandson, Benjamin. May his memory be for blessing. Rabbi Jonathan A. Stein Michael H. Laufer, Pres.
