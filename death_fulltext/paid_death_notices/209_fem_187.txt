
        OTTLEY--Mabel J.F. Of Manhattan, beloved wife of the late Peter Ottley and devout Catholic, on June 24, 2004 at the age of ninety-eight. Born on December 24, 1905, Mabel worked in the millinery department at Bloomingdales, in Manhattan, until she was eighty years old. Visiting Sunday 2-5 at the Bernard F. Dowd Funeral Home, 165-20 Hillside Avenue, Jamaica, NY. Funeral Mass Monday 9:30AM at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs RC Church, 110-06 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, NY. Interment at Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, NY.
