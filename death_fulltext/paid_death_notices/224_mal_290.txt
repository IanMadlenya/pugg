
        HILBERT--Bill, 83, born Corning, NY, May 23, 1922, died on September 27 in Haddonfield, NJ. He lives on in memory and impact as a loving husband to his wife Lorraine (''Larry''), loving father to his son Chris, loving grandfather to William and Mark, brilliant mind, lover of art and antiquity and practical and sensitive observer of life. Gilbert & Sullivan, Audubon, Yale University and America have all lost a great fan.
