
        VIETOR-Barbara Diane. 63, of Manhattan. Daughter of the late Henry Theodore and Mary (Stoker) Vietor, died April 17, 2001. A graduate of Tufts University, Barabara was a long time employee of Pan American Airlines and most recently of Random House. She leaves her brother Richard Vietor and his wife Lucinda and their children Nicholas, Christopher and Meredith. She is also survived by cousins. Burial will be in Amesbury, MA. Contributions in Barbara's memory may be made to the American Lung Association, 432 Park Avenue South, 8th Flr., NY NY 10016.
