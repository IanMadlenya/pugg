
        ROGGEN - Tobie B. 87, died on Jan. 8, 1997 at Wesley Care Center. She was born June 26, 1909 in New York city to Harry and Hattie Roggen. Until six months ago she was a lifelong resident of New York. Graveside service, Thursday, Jan. 9, 1997 at 2PM at Union Fields Cemetery, Queens, NY.
