
        van BEELEN-Sybil Grace (nee Alexander). Died peacefully on August 29, 1997 after a short illness in Delray Beach, Florida. Born in London, England on September 5, 1903. She was widowed from Pieter van Beelen on June 4, 1990. Funeral services took place in Florida at Scobee-Ireland-Potter Funeral Home, Delray Beach, Florida. A memorial service is scheduled for Sunday, September 28, 1997 at 2 P.M. in St. George's Church, 209 East 16th Street, NYC, New York 10003. In lieu of flowers contributions to St. George's Church will be appreciated.
