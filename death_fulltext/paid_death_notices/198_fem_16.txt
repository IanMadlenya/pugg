
        PERRY -- Virginia Gregory, wife of the late Albert L. Perry, died on Sunday, June 29th. Born in East Orange, NJ on October 12, 1911, she was active in civic affairs in Wilton. CT. She was a member of the Wilton Garden Club and the Wilton Congregational Church for over 50 years. A member of the Women's Board of the Norwalk Hospital, the Treasure House, the Minks to Sinks Sale and the Soup Kitchen. Mrs. Perry is survived by her sons Albert L. Perry III and Gregory M. Perry and her daughter Ginger Perry. She was predeceased by son Stephen T. Perry, who was killed in action in Vietnam in 1969. She is also survived by her five granddaughters and seven great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, July 3rd at noon at the Wilton Congregational Church. Memorial contributions may be made to the Wilton Congregational Church, 70 Ridgefield Rd., or the Wilton Historical Society, 249 Danbury Rd., Wilton, CT 06897 >>AD#
