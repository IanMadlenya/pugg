
        STATTER-Humphrey. Died on Tuesday, August 14, 2001 in Greenwich, CT at the age of 93. He was born in Sioux City, IA, and graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy, Harvard College, and Harvard Law School. During W.W. II, he served as a Lieutenant Commander in the U.S. Navy. Until his retirement, he was in private law practice in NYC. He served as President and a Trustee of the Huguenot Society of America, and was a member of the Union Club, the St. Nicholas Society, and the Sons of the Revolution. His wife, Julia Brokaw Statter, died in 1998. Surviving are his daughter, Phyllis Statter Oxman, of Greenwich, CT, his son, Humphrey Statter III, of Melbourne, Australia, his grandchildren, Elena Grear Oxman and Lee Warren Oxman, his nephew, Fairfield Pope Day III, and his niece, Stella Day Parsons. Funeral services are private.
