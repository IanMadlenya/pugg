
        JONES-Harold S. Of Rumson, NJ, and Palm City, FL, died on February 22, at the age of 66. Born in Red Bank, at Riverview Hospital to Harold J. and Elizabeth Patterson Jones. ''Mickey'' was married to Grace Healy of Rumson, NJ, in 1955. He founded the Surfside Beverage Corp in 1962, which went on to acquire the Pepsi Cola Bottling Co. of Asbury Park, NJ. He is survived by his wife Grace, a daughter, Kathleen, and a son, Thomas. Visitation will be at the John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Ave., Red Bank, NJ, on Monday from 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be Tuesday, 9 AM, at St. James Roman Catholic Church, Red Bank, NJ. Burial will be at Mt. Olivet Cemetery, Middletown, NJ.
