
        SILVER-Amy Frank. Died peacefully on June 12, 1998 in her 95th year. She was the loving and beloved wife of the late Louis H. Silver, devoted mother of Loretta Silver Lifton and Carol Silver Greif. She was the adored grandmother of Betsy Hooper, Karen Healy, Deborah, Diane and Linda Greif and the great-grandmother of Matthew, Meg, Amy, and Daniel. Private services were held in Chicago, Illinois.
