
        SCHEINBERG-Louise G. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with sorrow the passing of the beloved wife of our colleague, Dr. Labe Scheinberg, who served as acting dean of our medical school and is now professor emeritus of neurology. To Labe and to his entire family, we extend our deepest sympathy. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean
