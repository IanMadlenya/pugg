
        SINGER-Frances K.. On November 5, 1999. Cherished wife of Dr. Benjamin A. Singer. Loving mother of Pamela Singer and son-inlaw Robert M. Unger. Adoring grandmother of Samuel. Dearest sister of Sarah Fisher, Martha Zelman and the late Roberta Feldman. Devoted friend and teacher. Adored aunt and great-aunt. Her zest for life, unique style and elegance are her legacy to us all. Service Monday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
