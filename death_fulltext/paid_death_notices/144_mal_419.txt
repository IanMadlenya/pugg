
        FINNERAN-Gerard L. Born on August 9, 1909, died on January 23, 1999, in Palm Beach, FL. Formerly of New York City, and ''Robin HIll,'' Newtown, CT. Son of the late Honoria and Edward Finneran, and husband of the late Mary Ray Trimble. Gerard graduated from The University of Notre Dame. He was a member of The New York Athletic Club, The Friendly Sons of Saint Patrick, president of the Robert Mize Isa White Trimble Foundation, and Lieutenant Commander USNR (retired). Gerard was a loving uncle of many nieces and nephews, and grand nieces and nephews, whose lives were blessed to have such an extraordinary uncle, whose love, devotion, and generosity had no bounds. Frends may call at The Abbey, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, January 26th, 5-8 P.M. Funeral Mass at Saint Jean The Baptiste Church, Lexington Avenue at 76th Street, Wednesday at 10 A.M. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
