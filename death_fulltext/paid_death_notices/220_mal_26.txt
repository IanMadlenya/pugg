
        BUDICK--Dr. Harry. Age 97. Died May 1 in Jerusalem, Israel. As an optometrist, he was devoted to improving the vision of others. As a dedicated Zionist, he was a visionary who inspired all who knew him. Husband of the late Sylvia, he is survived by his beloved sons Sanford, Arthur and Burton and their wives. He leaves six grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren.
