
        HARRIS--Burt I. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) mourn the loss of their warm and steadfast friend Burt I. Harris of Los Angeles, Calif. A pioneering cable television executive, Mr. Harris served the ATS as president of the Southern California Chapter, a national board member and life trustee of the ATS Board of Regents. Devoted philanthropists, Mr. Harris and his wife Shirley established the Shirley and Burt Harris Chair in Communications and the Burt and Shirley Harris Scholarship Fund, and supported a variety of Southern California Chapter projects. An honorary life member of the Technion International Board of Governors, Mr. Harris was recognized with an honorary fellowship from the Technion in 1987. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Shirley, children Janie, Buzz, Melissa and Natalie, and the entire Harris family.
