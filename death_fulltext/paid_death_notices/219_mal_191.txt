
        SCHMIED--Joseph. 97, Richmond Hill, NY. Best known as ''Uncle Joe'', died Friday, April 15, 2005. Born June 3, 1907, in Brooklyn, NY. Started his career with the Consolidated Edison Electric Company in the 1930s. Later created his own company called (Schmied Neon Signs) at 852 Wyckoff Avenue, Brooklyn, which endured for more than 50 years. During his long and ambitious lifetime aspired to many high level positions at non-profits and fraternal organizations, some of which include Trustee - Wyckoff Heights Hospital, member of the Shriners and respective hospitals, Masonic Fraternity-(America Lodge), Honorary New York City Fire Chief, F.D.N.Y. Steuben and St. George Associations, NYC Police Square and Cycle Clubs, Glendale and Ridgewood Kiwanis and Rotary Clubs, The Bushwick Club, The Elks Club, and many more. He was a people magnet, gifted with the charisma of making and keeping longtime friendships. His charity work will be long remembered. He had no children, but did have a priceless commodity (close friends). Funeral ceremony Tuesday, April 19, 2005, 9:45 a.m. at funeral home. Interment at Evergreen Cemetery, 1629 Bushwick Avenue, Brooklyn, NY, 718-455-5300.
