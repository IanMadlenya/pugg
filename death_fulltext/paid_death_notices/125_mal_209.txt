
        GORDON-Herman. On June 16, 1997 at the age of 90. Lover of music, of discussion with friends, avid reader especially of The New York Times. Fighter for social and economic equality for all. His cherished survivors are his brother, David Gordon of Cleveland, his sister Gertrude Weiss of Laguna Hills, California and his close friend Mary Taylor as well as a number of nieces and nephews, grand-nieces and grand-nephews and their progeny.
