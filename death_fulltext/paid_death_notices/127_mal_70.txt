
        SMITH-Robert, of Lenox, MA., died on a fishing trip in Coburn Gore, Maine on August 4, 1997 at the age of 91. Distinguished author of fiction and non-fiction. Beloved husband of Jean (nee Kelly) Smith, father of Anvil, Pam, Bob and Nora, father-in-law of Deejay and Dian, grandfather of Ben, Emlen and Rosie. A memorial service will be held at the Roche Funeral Home, 120 Main Street, Lenox, MA., 10 A.M. on Saturday, August 9. Contributions in his memory may be sent to the Robert Smith Memorial Scholarship Fund, 17 West Street, Lenox, MA. 01240.
