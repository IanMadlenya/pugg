
        DYSTEL--Marion. Of Rye, NY, passed away on November 2, 2003, at White Plains Hospital due to complications of pneumonia. Born Marion Deitler on February 7, 1915, in Bridgeport, CT, to Louis and Fanny Deitler. She was raised and educated in public schools in Bridgeport, where she met and married her high school sweetheart, Oscar, on October 2, 1938. She served as a volunteer with the American Red Cross during WWII and with the United Hospital in Port Chester and the National Multiple Sclerosis Society Southern New York Chapter. She is survived by her husband of 65 years, Oscar Dystel of Rye. Also surviving are her loving daughter Jane and her husband Steven Schwinder, and her grandchildren Jessica and Zachary, all of New York City. Her son, John, predeceased her in June, 2003. She was also predeceased by her sisters Belle Cone, Jeanne Slachter, and her brothers Leo Deitler and Irving Deitler. Contributions may be made to the John Dystel MS Nursing Fellowship, 50 Main Street, Suite 1000, White Plains, NY 10606.
