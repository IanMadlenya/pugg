
        WIMPFHEIMER -- Victorine Kops. 90. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Seymour. Loving mother of Doris and David Finkel, Susan and Toby Abend, Ellie and Dr. Tom Slovis, Ann and the late Rabbi Baruch Bokser, and David, adored grandmother of 15 and great-grandmother of 19. Sister of Daniel W. Kops. Educator, community leader and friend. Her dynamic and compassionate life will forever be an inspiration to the many people whose lives she touched. Contributions may be made to the Vicki Wimpfheimer Memorial Fund at the Jewish Communal Fund, 130 E. 59th St., NY, NY 10022. Services Monday, 9:30AM, Park Avenue Synagogue, 87th and Madison. For information call Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 212769-4400.
