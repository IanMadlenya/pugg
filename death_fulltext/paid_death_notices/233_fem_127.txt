
        ANGLISANO--Suzanne (nee Fridman), born Paris, France, Oct. 21, 1932, died suddenly June 15, 2006. Loving mother of Allen, Laura (Noah), Felicia (Ken); proud grandmother of Craig, Phillip, Paul, Danielle, Doron, Simone and Alex; great-grandmother of Isahai; devoted sister of Charles and Barbara Fridman; loving aunt of Russell (Amy), Michele (Steve), Lara (Gavin); and great aunt. As a young child Suzanne survived the Holocaust and she continued to have a difficult life. We pray she has found peace.
