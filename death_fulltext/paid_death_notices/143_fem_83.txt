
        RUSH-Jane Gilbert. Of New Canaan, CT formerly of Delray Beach, FL and Rye, NY died Saturday, December 5, 1998. She was the wife of the late Kenneth Rush, former President of Union Carbide and Ambassador to both the Federal Republic of Germany, and France. Survivors include a daughter, Cynthia Monahan of New Canaan, CT, 3 sons, Malcolm Rush of Bennington, VT, John Rush of Fairfax, VA, Kenneth Rush, Jr. of Brooklyn, NY; a sister, Huldah Payson of Salem, MA, 13 grandchildren and 4 great-grandchildren also survive. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, December 11, 1998 at 4:30 pm in St. Marks Church, Oenoke Ridge Road, New Canaan, CT. Interment will be held in Manchester, VT. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Connecticut Hospice, c/o Norwalk Hospital, Norwalk, CT 06854.
