
        FLORENZ-Ruth (nee Florence Levine). Born December 18, 1896. Died May 17, 1997. Last survivor of seven siblings: Samuel, Julius, Charles, Charlotte, Jess, Sidney and Al. Devoted friend of the late Grace Hooper. Her nieces and nephews, grandnieces and nephews, great-grandnieces and nephews and sisters-in-law, Frances Levine and Lillian Levin, mourn the passing of a remarkable woman.
