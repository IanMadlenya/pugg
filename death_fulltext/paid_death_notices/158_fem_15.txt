
        BILLICK-Susan G. Beloved sister and aunt, good friend and of giving spirit passed away on March 2, 2000. Susan was born in New York City on September 6, 1941. She is survived by her sister, Barbara Melamed, and her niece Jennifer Iannuzzi and husband, Dr. Christopher Iannuzzi, and friends. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society or a charity of choice.
