
        SEELIG--Michael J. On Janurary 10, 2007, at the age of 93. An amazing man, who was the beloved husband of Phyllis; the loving father of Patty, and the late Kenny; and grandfather of Annie and Lisa. He was a First Sergeant in the United States Army Air Corps and was honorably discharged February 11, 1946, which was also his first wedding anniversary. A funeral service will be held Friday, January, 2007, 12 pm, at ''Westchester Reform Temple'', 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
