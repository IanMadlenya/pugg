
        ABRAMS-Martin, 69. Died November 29, 1998. Beloved husband of Toni Grossi Abrams. Survived by Hillel Joseph Abrams, Robin Diana Cardinal and their mother Sheila E. Brown. Loving grandfather of Madeline, Gabrielle and Sabrina. A man whose integrity was only exceeded by his warmth. The world is a sadder place with his passing.
