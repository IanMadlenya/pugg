
        COLEMAN-Benjamin, M.D. At age 90. Beloved husband of Alice for 62 years. Devoted father, grandfather & great grandfather. Peacefully passed away on April 24, 2002. Retired surgeon who loved life, medicine and his family. Graveside services Thursday, April 25th, at 11 A.M. at the New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York. For information call Riverside-Nassau North Chapels.
        COLEMAN-Benjamin, Dr. The Board of Directors and Membership of The Glen Head Country Club announce with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Dr. Benjamin Coleman. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Louis M. Kerner, President
