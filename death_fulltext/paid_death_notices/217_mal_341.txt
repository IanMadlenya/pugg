
        STRAUSS--Sigmund. Died on February 24, 2005 at the age of 99. Resided in Erie, PA, Chicago, Harrison, NY and Stamford, CT. He was the beloved husband of Zelda Marks, loving father of Patricia Spencer and Anita Kraus, adoring grandfather and great-grandfather of Brian Kraus, Lisa and John Spencer, Lynn Hendricks, Matt Kraus, Ryan and Connor Donnelly, Liz, Katie and Tori Hendricks.
