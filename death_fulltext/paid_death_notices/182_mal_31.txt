
        EPSTEIN-Joseph B. Died on March 2, 2002, in Carlsbad, California, at the age of 85. He founded National Machinery Exchange in New York City. He was beloved by his wife of 57 years, Lila; his children, Sydele, Lawrence and Nina, and Harvey (deceased); and his grandchildren, Maggie Golston, John and Robin Golston, Isabel and Rishon Blumberg, Amy and Will Gadsden, and Brian Epstein. Services will be held on Thursday, March 7th, at 12:30 P.M. at the Broadway Riverside Chapel in Hewlett, NY, followed by burial at Mount Lebanon Cemetery.
