
        BRINTON-Joan Marie. Age 56. Born in Seattle, Wa. Died at her longtime home in Brooklyn. Loving mother of Adam, Anthony and Matthew. She was an original who will be missed by many. Devoted to family, friends and community. Her kindness, selflessness, knowledge and gentle nature touched our hearts. Her positive outlook, strength of spirit and determination continued to give us comfort in this difficult time. She will always be a part of us and we have been blessed by the joy she spread. Service to be announced.
