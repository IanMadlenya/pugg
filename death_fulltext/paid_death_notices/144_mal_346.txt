
        PECKERMAN-Edward R. Jr. The Horace Mann community records with deep sorrow the death of our esteemed alumnus, Edward R. Peckerman, Esq., Class of 1921. Always a warm, intelligent and caring person, he served as Vice Chair and Chair of the Horace Mann Board of Trustees from 1967 to 1970. The recipient of the Alumni Association's Award for Distinguished Achievement in 1976, he later served as an exemplary Honorary Trustee of Horace Mann until the time of his death. We shall miss his presence. We express heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife Kristen. Michael D. Hess, '58 Chair, Board of Trustees Dr. Eileen Mullady, Head of School
