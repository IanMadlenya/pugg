
        BRODSKY-Philip C., on January 24, 1997 of cancer. Beloved and adored husband of Jeanne, cherished father and grandfather of sons Paul, Gabriel and Eugene, daughter-in-law Karen, children Nancy, Marc and Sue Lazarus and grandchildren Sascha, Kirsten, Anya, Kate, Emma, brother Nathan and sister Ruth Leopold. His elegance touched everyone. Service Sunday, January 26th at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 1:15 P.M. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Yale Drama School.
        BRODSKY-Philip C. The members of The Lotus Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and friend, Philip C. Brodsky, and send condolences to his family.
