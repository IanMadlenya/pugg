
        WILKINSON-Frederick D., Jr. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of the Weill Medical College of Cornell University and the New York Presbyterian Hospital are deeply saddened by the passing of our friend and esteemed Life Overseer Fred Wilkinson. A founding member of the Board of Overseers, Mr. Wilkinson was a man of great charm, dignity and wisdom. He will be greatly missed. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his wife, Jeane, and the Wilkinson family. Weill Medical College of Cornell University Sanford I. Weill Chairman, Board of Overseers Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Dean New York Presbyterian Hospital Daniel B. Burke John F. McGillicuddy Co-Chairmen, Board of Trustees David B. Skinner, M.D. Vice Chairman and CEO William T. Speck, M.D. President and COO
