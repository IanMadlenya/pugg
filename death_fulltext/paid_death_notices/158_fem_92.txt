
        SLOTKIN-Bonnie. On March 8, 2000 of Potomac, MD. Adoring Mommy of David and Jon-her love for her boys was unconditional. Dearest mother-in-law of Sharon. Smitten MomMom of Jayme. Loving, selfless wife and best friend of Jay. Cherished daughter of Shayne and Harold. Beloved Ba-Ba of Melanie and Marc. Loving soulmate of Abbey. Treasured daughter-in-law of Marjorie and Raymond. Her vivacious love for life was contagious. She navigated any obstacle with the utmost poise. She taught us to keep our heads high in the face of any adversity. Her smile and laugh lit up our lives. She shielded us from pain, while radiating her vitality, love, and humor unto us. She was, and always will be, our source of strength. We will never forget you. Her synagogue, B'nai Israel Congregation, 6301 Montrose Road, Rockville, MD will embrace her one last time on Sunday, March 12, 2000 at 1:00 P.M. Burial at Judean Memorial Gardens. Shiva will be observed at the Slotkin residence through Tuesday. Memorial contributions may be made to The Jay Slotkin Fund for Neuro Muscular Research, Johns Hopkins Hospital, 600 N. Wolfe St., Meyer 5-119, Baltimore, MD 21287. Edward Sagel Funeral Direction, Inc., Rockville, MD 1-800-217-9403.
