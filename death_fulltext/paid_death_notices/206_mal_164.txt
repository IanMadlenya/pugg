
        GOLDSMITH--Hal. 73, died on March 13, 2004. Beloved husband of Del Rene, adored and adoring father of John and Robert, devoted friend and colleague. Graduate of Stuyvesant High School and City College of New York. NCAA fencing champion, member of US Olympic teams in 1952, 1956 and 1960, and two-time Pan-American Games Gold Medalist. Past President of Westchester Jewish Community Services. His strength, warmth, intellect, sharp wit and passion for life inspire us, and he will be deeply missed. Funeral services at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, Monday, at 1:15 pm. Burial Tuesday, on Martha's Vineyard.
