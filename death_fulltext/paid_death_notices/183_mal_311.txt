
        FREEMAN-Jack. Of New York and Sarasota, Florida, 94 years old, passed away on April 26, 2002. Devoted husband of Estelle Freeman and Edna Freeman. Beloved and adoring father of Merrill and Raymond Saperstein, grandfather of Richard, Robert and Lawrence Saperstein and great-grandfather of Noah, Annie, Charlie, Asa and baby Joe Saperstein. A long full life spent caring for and protecting his loved ones. Funeral services will be private at graveside with family. Donations may be made to the Israel Emergency Fund of UJA.
