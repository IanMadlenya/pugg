
        GRUENEBAUM-Thomas. An international artist living in San Miguel and Santiago de Queretaro, Mexico, on May 28th at age 63 of a heart attack. In addition to his wife, Marilyn, he is survived by his mother, Corina, sister, Mary Weiss of Palm Beach, and five children, Alicia Hammang, Arturo Gruenebaum, Joslyn Cooke, Mary Phillips, and Suzanne Miller, and six grandchildren. He had just completed an exhibition of his work at the Museo de Arte in Queretaro.
