
        BEIL-Barbara. Beloved wife of Sheldon. My beloved wife died June 3rd, 2000. Vivacious, beautiful, compassionate and with a dynamic spirit, she sparked my days and illuminated my nights. She added enrichment to life and fulfilled her last few years with spirit, strength and a zest to live. She was unique and unforgettable. All who new her loved her. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI, Sunday 2:15pm. Devoted to her infirmed brother, Leonard Miller. She raised money and worked to help the severely handicapped. Donations to The Disabled And Alone For The Handicapped, 352 Park Avenue South, New York 10010.
