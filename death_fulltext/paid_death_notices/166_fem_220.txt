
        ROSEDALE-Dr. Glenda S. On November 9, 2000 in her native New York. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Columbia University where she earned her BA, MA and Ph.D. She taught and did research at the same university. Many, many people, both friends and acquaintances, were charmed by her warmth & her sense of humor. Her interests lay in music, art and literature. She herself possessed brilliant talent in music-she played jazz piano-and had a remarkable sensitivity to language-This was shown in her editing the work of another and in her own writing. She should also be remembered for her perceptiveness about people and life in general. For information about services, please call Frank E. Campbell at 212-288-3500.
