
        TATTA--John L. on February 3, 2005, age 84, resident of Fort Lauderdale, FL formerly of Oyster Bay, NY. Beloved husband of Anne (nee Frasca). Loving father of Debbie DeCabia and Lisa Crowley. Cherished grandfather of Mark, Kevin, John, Stephanie, Christopher, Jenna, Patrick and Amelia. Devoted uncle of AJ Pino. Founding member and president of Cablevision. Visiting at the Oyster Bay Funeral Home, 261 South St, Oyster Bay, NY Saturday and Sunday 3-5pm and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian Burial Monday 9:30am, St. Dominic RC Chapel, Oyster Bay, NY. Interment Locust Valley Cemetery. In lieu of flowers the family is requesting donations to the Long Island Alzheimer's Foundation, 5 Channel Drive, Port Washington, NY 11050.
        TATTA--John. The management, staff, and trustees of St. Francis Hospital, the Heart Center, in Roslyn, NY, mourn the passing of John Tatta, former President of Cablevision Systems Corporation and a member of the St. Francis Hospital Board of Trustees from 1986-1992. We will be forever grateful for John's friendship, dedication, and generosity. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Anne, and her family at this time of loss.
