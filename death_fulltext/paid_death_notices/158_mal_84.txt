
        TAYLOR-Reginald M. MD. Of Wilton, Conn. formerly of Bedford Hills, NY. Died on March 4. He was a Neurophysiologist for Columbia Presbyterian Hospital for many years. Stepfather of John Klein, Karen Ripley and David Campbell. Also survived by five stepgrandchildren and eleven step greatgrandchildren. Funeral service at St. Matthews Episcopal Church, Bedford, NY, Thursday 11am. Interment St. Matthews cemetery.
