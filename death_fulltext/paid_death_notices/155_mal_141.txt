
        GARLIN-Sender. Tireless fighter in the struggle for social justice, died peacefully at his home in Boulder, CO on December 6 at age 97. Author of the book ''Three American Radicals'' and many articles and pamphlets as a labor journalist. A captivating speaker with incisive wit and remarkable historical memory. The Garlins: his wife, Martha Millet Garlin, daughter Emily, son Alexander, daughter-in-law Virginia Chavez, granddaughter Annelise of Boulder, CO; son Victor, daughter-inlaw Marjorie Baird Garlin, and granddaughters Amy and Rachel, of Berkeley, California.
