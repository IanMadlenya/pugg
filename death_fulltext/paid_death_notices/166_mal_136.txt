
        JEFFER-Alvin M. Of Jericho, NY and Boca Raton, FL, at home, lovingly surrounded by family on November 12, 2000. He was 75. For 53 years dearly treasured husband of Irma, loving and devoted to his children Rhonda and Eric Moore, Debra and Sanford Gips and the late Kenneth Robert Jeffer. Adored grandfather of Harris, Gordon, Spencer and Julia. Beloved brother of Medwin B. Jeffer and dear brother-in-law of Sanford and Sandra Smith. He was at one time the owner of Jeffer Funeral Home, with which he remained affiliated, helping peple in their time of need with tremendous compassion and kindness. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral service at ''Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel,'' Queens Boulevard and 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY, Tuesday, November 14, at 1 PM.
