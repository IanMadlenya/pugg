
        UPPMAN -- Theodor. On March 17, 2005. Longtime baritone at the Metropolitan Opera. The family will receive friends informally on Thursday, March 24, 3PM to 6PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        UPPMAN--Theodor. Mannes College of Music, a division of New School University, mourns the passing of Theodor Uppman, inspiring voice teacher and presence at Mannes for a quarter century. All those who studied with him or knew him will miss his artistry and musical imagination. Philip Scaturro, Chair, Board of Trustees Bob Kerrey, President John Beerbower, Chair, Mannes Board of Governors Joel Lester, Dean Mannes College
