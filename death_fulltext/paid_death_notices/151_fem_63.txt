
        LEVY - Nachama (Natalie)(nee Friedman). Born June 12, 1923 in Rochester, N.Y. Died suddenly on August 1st. at her home in Indianapolis, Indiana. She is survived by her son Joel Levy of Seattle, Wa. and two granddaughters Galit and Keryn Pelled of Jerusalem and pre-deceased by her daughter Elana Pelled. Donations may be sent in her memory to the Hadassah Organization of America, 50 W. 58 St., NYC10019.
