
        KASSIRER--Karen of Manhattan died May 16 after a long, brave battle with breast cancer. An artist and Senior Partner and Manager of Editorial Services at Ogilvy & Mather, she was recently honored as the 2006 Colby Artist at the Buffalo Seminary. Predeceased by her father, Earle L. Kassirer Esq., she is survived by her mother, Norma Kassirer; her sister, Susan Kassirer; brother-in-law, Thomas Langner; niece, Laura Langner; and cousin, Janet Kaufman. Donations in Karens name can be made to the ACLU, Public Citizen, or the NAACP.
