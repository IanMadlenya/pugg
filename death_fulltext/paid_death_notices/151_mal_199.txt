
        DEFREITAS - William Richard ''Dick.'' Of Charlottesville, died Tuesday, August 17, 1999 in Charlottesville, VA. He is survived by his wife, Jane Wyatt DeFreitas of Charlottesville formerly of Washington, DC, New York and Stamford, CT, two sons, one daughter and six grandchildren. A funeral service will be held 11:00 A.M. Saturday August 21, 1999 at Hebron Lutheran Church in Madison, VA with interment in the church cemetery. Memorial contributions may be made to the Hospice of the Piedmont, 1290 Seminole Trail, Charlottesville, VA 22901 or the Westminster Canterbury Of The Blue Ridge Foundation, 250 Pantops Mountain Road, Charlottesville, VA 22911-8686. For information, Preddy Funeral Home of Madison, VA, 540-948-3600.
