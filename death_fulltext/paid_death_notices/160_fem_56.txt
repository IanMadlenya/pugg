
        LYDECKER-Sally McCormick, 85, of 88 Notch Hill Road, North Branford, CT died January 20, 2000 at Yale New Haven Hospital. She was the widow of Garrit A. Lydecker. She was born in Leonia, NJ, August 8, 1914, a daughter of the late Howard and Josephine Newell McCormick. She was a longstanding member of Darien Book Aid Plan and the Rowayton (CT) Garden Club. She is survived by two daughters Nancy L. Jones of Newton, MA, Laura Carr of Park Hall, MD, two sons Robert Lydecker of Windsor, John Lydecker of Winsted and six grandchildren. She was predeceased by a sister Nancy M. Scott and a brother Peter Newell McCormick. For information contact Oak Grove Cremation Service, West Haven, CT.
