
        WERNER-Steven. 1898-1998. Died on May 30. Mourned by his beloved wife Klara, children Dr. Andrew Werner and Elaine and granddaughter Andrea. Born in Budapest, Hungary. A man of outstanding intellect who excelled in his profession of mechanical engineering. Came to this country in 1956, during the Hungarian Revolaution, as a refugee and built a successful life. Leaves behind an unfillable void for all of those who knew and loved him. Funeral arrangements are private.
