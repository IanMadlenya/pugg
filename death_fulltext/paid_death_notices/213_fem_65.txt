
        REID--Anne T. October 8, 2004. Beloved wife of the late John J. McCormick and John Reid. Longtime resident of New York City and Spring Lake, NJ. Benefactor of the College of Mt. St. Vincent and member of Board of Directors of Casita Maria. Survived by her niece and nephew Kathleen and James McCormick. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Sunday, 25 and 7-9 pm. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, October 12, 10 am, St. John the Martyr Church, East 72nd St., off 2nd Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Casita Maria, 928 Simpson St., Bronx, NY 10459.
        REID--Anne. The Board of Directors and staff of Casita Maria mourn the loss of our devoted supporter and benefactor. She cared deeply about the less fortunate whom we have served for over 70 years. Jacqueline Weld Drake, Chairman Norrisa O'Keeffe, Chairman Emeritus
