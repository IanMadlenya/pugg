
        MARGOLIS-Deborah (Pessin). On Saturday, March 3, in her 92nd year, of New York City. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Benjamin D. Margolis. Loving mother of Helen and Melvin Michael of Newton, MA. Devoted grandmother of Daniel and Emily Michael. Graveside services were held at Temple Emeth Memorial Park, Baker St., West Roxbury, MA, on Sunday, March 4. A memorial service in New York is being planned. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to The Center for Modern Psychoanalytical Studies, New York, NY. For information contact Levine Chapel, Brookline, Massachusetts.
