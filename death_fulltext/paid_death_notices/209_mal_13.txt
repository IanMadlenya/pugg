
        DAY--Herbert I. Jr. of North Caldwell and Mantoloking, NJ, 73. Beloved son of the late Herbert I. Day, Sr. and Aurora Day. He was born in the Bronx, NY and received his B.S. from Fordham University, A direct marketing executive, Day retired at 72. He is survived by his beloved wife, Barbara; daughters, Deborah, Wendy and Jennifer Day; son Jeffrey; brothers, Roger and Alan; sister, Missy and nine grandchildren. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, June 2nd from 7-9PM at the Dancy Funeral Home, Caldwell, NJ. A memorial mass on Thursday, June 3rd at Notre Dame R.C. Church, North Caldwell, will be offered at 10:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hospice.
