
        MOSES-William A. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of William A. Moses, beloved father of Cynthia Moses Manocherian and father-inlaw of Jeffrey Manocherian, two cherished friends of UJA-Federation who are deeply devoted to helping those in need. Throughout the years, Mr. Moses has been recognized for championing the redevelopment of disadvantaged communities in New York City. We extend our deepest sympathies to Cynthia and Jeffrey; to his grandchildren, Amanda, Margo, and Olivia; and to the entire Moses family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Leonard A. Wilf, Chair of Real Estate Division
