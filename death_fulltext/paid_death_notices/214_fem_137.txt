
        SCHWARTZ--Grace. Died on November 19th at home. Grace is survived by a loving family; children Barbara and Kenneth Onish, Steven Schwartz and Sanford and Alyce Schwartz; grandchildren Russell and Jennifer Onish, Alison and Donald Rosen, Robert and Jessie Schwartz, Jonathan Schwartz, Megan Schwartz and Joshua Schwartz; greatgrandchildren Dina and Aaron Onish. Grace lived a full and active life as a teacher, fundraiser, community activist and full time business partner of her late husband David. She had a strong personality and left her mark on all she touched. A funeral will be held Sunday, November 21st at 11AM at Garlick's Funeral Parlor at 1153 Coney Island Avenue in Brooklyn. Donations can be made in honor of Grace to UJA.
