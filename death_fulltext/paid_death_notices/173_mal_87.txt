
        SIEGMAN-Howard, 1922-2000 Howard Siegman taught dramatic literature at Hofstra University for some thirty-five years. Most references to him have to be theatrical, from Oedipus to Blanche DuBois and all the great plays in between. They made him come alive and, in turn, he did the same for them. Students cherished him for this gift as did his friends. There was more, lots more. The love he inspired comes close to saying it all
