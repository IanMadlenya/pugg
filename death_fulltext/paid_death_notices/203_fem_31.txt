
        HOROWITZ--Eleanor. Heartfelt condolences to Esther Felsen, on the loss of her beloved mother, a woman of great dignity and elegance. Esther, a member of the Women's Division Board, exhibits the loftiest values of Jewish tradition and ethics that she learned from her mother. Together with her husband Harvey, a member of the National Board, the Felsens are known for their many quiet acts of chesed, inspiring all who know them. May Esther and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
