
        BOLTINOFF-Henry. 87. Of Lake Worth, FL. He was a well-known syndicated cartoonist best known for ''Stoker the Broker'' and ''Hocus-Pokus'' syndicated with King Features in over 300 newspapers around the world. Survived by daughter Bebe Davoli of Syracuse, NY and son Herb Boltinoff of Green Falls, Montana. Services were held in Florida.
