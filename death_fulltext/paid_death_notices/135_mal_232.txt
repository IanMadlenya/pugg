
        ROLLER-Iser. With deep sorrow we record the demise of our dear benefactor and member of the Board of Governors, Iser Roller, who together with his dear wife, dedicated the Iser and Anne Roller Diningroom and Lecture Hall at Yeshivat Sha' alvim in Israel. Funeral services will be held today at 1 PM at the West End Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, New York. Our sympathies and condolences to his wife and the entire family. Friends of Yeshivat Sha' Alvim Rav Avraham Avidan, Rosh Hayeshiva Rabbi Mallen Galinsky, Dean Rabbi Philip Harris Singer, Executive Chairman
