
        SLIGH-Charles R., Jr. The Directors and Staff of the Deafness Research Foundation note with sorrow the passing on January 25th in Delray Beach, Fl, of its former Chairman, benefactor and loyal friend. Mr. Sligh was former Chairman of the Sligh Furniture Co. of Grand Rapids, MI and former President of the Natioanl Association of Manufacturers. He will be greatly missed. We extend our sympathies to his widow and his family. Collette Ramsey-Baker, Founder, DRF
