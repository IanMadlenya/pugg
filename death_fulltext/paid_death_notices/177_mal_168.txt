
        RODRIGUES-Antonio. Age 35, of Port Washington, Long Island on September 11, 2001. A Port Authority Police Officer, he was a hero of the World Trade Center disaster. Beloved husband of Christina. Loving father of Adam and Sara. Dearest son of Jose and Cecilia of Portugal. Dear brother of Marisa Silvestre. Reposing at the Austin F. Knowles, Inc. Funeral Home, 128 Main Street, Port Washington. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday 1 PM Our Lady of Fatima Church. Interment in Nassau Knolls Cemetery.
