
        GOLD-Herbert. Of Monroe Township, NJ formerly of Glen Cove, NY, died at home, Feburary 7. He succumbed to lymphoma after having fought it ferociously. BA and MA, Hofstra University; had been a fiscal consultant. Served overseas with Allied Forces HQ, WW II. Survived by his beloved wife of 30 years, Ann; dear sisters and brothers-in-law, Gloria and Ed Sedran, Harriet and Sandy Popper; his much-loved nieces and nephews Howard Sedran, Cheryl Laks, Jonathan Popper, Terri Sedran Weinschenk. Donations to VNA-Hospice, 141 Bodman Pl., Red Bank, NJ, 07701.
