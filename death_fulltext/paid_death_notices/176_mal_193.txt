
        SEMINARA-Leonard G. Of Fort Lauderdale, Fl on September 6, 2001. He leaves behind his devoted wife Mary Jane, sons Matthew and George, daughter-in-law Laurie and grandson Jackson. He was a lifelong travel/bridal magazine executive with Brides, Modern Bride and most recently VP Advertising Bridal Guide. Many have been touched by this gentle soul and join his family in mourning his passing. Memorial services will be held in NYC and Fort Lauderdale. Be a registered organ donor.
