
        DEMITZ-Carol K., 49. Senior Vice President, Chief Corporate Counsel and Secretary of Fiduciary Trust Company International, died September 11, 2001 during the attack on the World Trade Center. Ms. Demitz is survived by her husband Fred Brewer and their daughter Anne, brothers Woody, Charlie and Michael Demitz and sister Susan Demitz. A Memorial Service is scheduled for Monday, October 22 at 5:30 PM at the All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Avenue, NY, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Carol K. Demitz Memorial Trust for Anne, C/O Fred Brewer, 510 East 86 Street, Apt 17A, New York, New York 10028.
