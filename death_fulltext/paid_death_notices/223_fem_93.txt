
        KAPLAN -- Ginette Leder man. On Sunday August 7 in her 103rd year. Beloved wife of the late Georges Kaplan. Cherished mother of Liliane (Robert) Schiff and Serge (Adrienne) Lederman. Devoted stepmother of Monique (Irwin) Winsten, Nicole (Jerome B.) Alenick and Jacques (Violaine) Kaplan, and the late Maurice Kaplan. Grandmother, great-grandmother and great-greatgrandmother. This beautiful lady will be missed by all who knew her. Interment private. A memorial serivce will be announced shortly.
