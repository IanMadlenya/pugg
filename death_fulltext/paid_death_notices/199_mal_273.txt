
        LEISURE--George S., Jr. At age 78, on August 25, 2003. Resident of Sea Island, GA, formerly of NYC. Partner at Donovan, Leisure, Newton & Irvine (1957-93). Survived by his beloved wife of 54 years, Joan Casey; four children: Constance Keeton Leisure (Steven Landers) of Litchfield, CT; sons Timothy (Annette) of Ponte Verda Beach, FL; Matthew (Victoria), York, PA; and Dr. George S. III (Dr. Marketa) of Charlottesville, VA; and a brother Hon. Peter K. Leisure (Kathleen) of NYC; and eight grandchildren. Memorial service August 30 at 11:00AM at St. Williams Church, St. Simons Island, GA. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. David's School, 12 East 89 St, NYC.
