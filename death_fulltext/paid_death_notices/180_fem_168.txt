
        DUNNING - Mary Margaret Bourne. Died January 12, 2002, at home in Manhattan after a long illness. Born July 26, 1911, and formerly of Decatur, IL, she was the beloved wife of the late James Dorr Dunning Sr., mother of James Jr. Cherished grandmother of James III and David, all of New York City, and dear friend to many. Funeral service will be held at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue at 51st St, Thursday, January 17, at 4:30 PM. Friends are requested to make memorial donations to St. Bartholomew's Church.
        DUNNING-Mary. Women's Bible Society of New York remembers fondly the 19 years of dedicated service on the Board of Managers and extends condolences to the family of Mary Dunning who went to her eternal home on Saturday, January 12, 2002.
