
        MAGNUSON--Paul A. Of Maplewood, New Jersey, passed away on September 7th. A Professor of English Literature at New York University, Paul is survived by his wife, Bonnie, his two daughters, and his brother. A private memorial service will be held. In lieu of flowers, please send a donation in his name to the New York Public Library, Friends Office, Room M-6, 476 Fifth Avenue, NY, NY 10018. For information: The Jacob A. Holle Funeral Home, Maplewood, NJ.
