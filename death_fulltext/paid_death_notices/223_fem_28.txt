
        GUTNER--Gloria F. (Friedman). 81, of Mamaroneck, NY, died peacefully at home surrounded by loved ones on August 4. Received her undergraduate degree from the College of New Rochelle and a Master's degree in Human Development from Bank St. College. Predeceased by her most beloved husband, Leonard B. Gutner, M.D. and his brother Monroe Gutner. Most loving mother to her children Rodney Gutner, Merri Rubin, and Nancy Clark, and their respective spouses. Cherished grandmother of Rebecca, Michael and Samuel Gutner; Tristan and Cassidy Gutner; Spencer and Carly Rubin; and Ian Clark. Survived by her devoted brother Evan Friedman and sisters - in - law Susan Friedman and Lillian Gutner. Services will be held Sunday, August 7, 11 AM, Congregation Emanu-el of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave. East, Rye, NY. 914-967-4382. In her memory contributions may be made to the organization of your choice.
        GUTNER--Gloria. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Gloria Gutner, and extend sincere condolences to her family. Charles J. Block, President
