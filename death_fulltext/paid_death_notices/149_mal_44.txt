
        MATTHEWS-John Clark, Ed.D. Professor emeritus at Adelphi University, of Garden City, New York, on Sunday, May 30th, following a long illness. Former chairman of Adelphi Department of Education. Survived by his devoted wife Margaret, sister Carrie of New Brunswick, Canada, and loving children Clark, Thane, and Nancy. Visitation Friday 2-5 and 7-9 PM at the Fairchild Chapel, 1201 Franklin Avenue, Garden City, N.Y. Memorial service 8 P.M. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations in Dr. Matthews' name to the Parkinson's Disease Research Team at New York-Presbyterian Medical Center, Sergievsky Center, (212) 305-9086.
