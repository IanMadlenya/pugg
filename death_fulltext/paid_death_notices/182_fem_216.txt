
        COBERN-Charlotte Forman, 81. Of New York City, died March 16th in NYC. She was an honored volunteer for the National Kidney Foundation and the International House of the McBurney YMCA. Beloved widow of Harry I. Cobern, she is survived by her sister, Florence Barotz of Delray Beach, FL; sons Martin Cobern of Cheshire, CT. and Bruce Cobern of Flushing, NY; granddaughters Susan Cobern Chasen of Boston and Amy Cobern of Cheshire; and numerous nieces and nephews. Donations may be sent to the American Cancer Society.
