
        SILLECK-Sidney B. Jr. Of Scarsdale, NY, on March 6, 1998, at the age of 83. Loving husband of Katherine Wallace. Also survived by his four children: Katherine, Bayley, and Thomas of NYC and Gordon of Irvington, NY; three grandchildren, Sean, Siri, and Elizabeth; and three great-grandchildren. He was a graduate of Deerfield Academy (1932) and Princeton University (1936) and served as a warrant officer in the Merchant Marine during World War II. He was a well known advertising and marketing executive who specialized in innovative cereal product premiums and sponsorship of early television shows such as Ed Sullivan's ''Toast of the Town,'' ''Howdy Doody,'' and ''Rin Tin Tin.'' A lifelong rail fan, he led a successful campaign to save several NYC trolleys from demolition. Sid will be deeply missed by all his friends and family. A memorial service will be held at the Church of St. James the Less, Scarsdale, NY, on Thursday, March 12, at 3 PM. Contributions in his name may be made to the church.
