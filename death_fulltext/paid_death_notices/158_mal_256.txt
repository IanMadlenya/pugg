
        KELLER-Leo, MD, of Woodbridge, CT formerly of Neponsit, NY, died on March 20, 2000. For almost his entire professional career, Dr. Keller practiced in NYC most of which was spent as Director of Radiology at The French Hospital. He attended NYU for college and medical education, receiving his MD in 1938. During WWII, he served as a captain and a physician for the US Army Engineering Corps in the South Pacific. After the war, Dr. Keller returned to work in NYC and lived in the Rockaways raising his family with his wife Freda who predeceased him in 1993 after 42 years of marriage. He is survived by his two sons, Marc S. Keller, MD of Woodbridge, CT and Jeffrey Keller of Upper Brookville, NY and two granddaughters Leslie and Meredith Keller of Woodbridge, CT. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, March 22 at 1:30pm at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY.
