
        WHITAKER-Robert Martin. Born January 19, 1906 in Gloucester, NJ, died December 1, 2000 in Sarasota, Florida. He was a son of Milton C. and Mabel Martin Whitaker. He had lived in New York City and Westport, CT, and in Putnam Station, NY where there is a family summer home. He was a graduate of Yale College, Class of 1927, and of The Harvard Business School, and was with Brown Brothers Harriman & Co until his retirement in 1961. He was an honorary trustee of The Leopold Schepp Foundation, having been an active trustee until he and his wife moved to Siesta Key nearly forty years ago. He was a member of The Field Club, The Sanderling Club, and The Fishermen, all in Sarasota. He was predeceased by his wife, the former Eleanor Alexander. Survivors include his son, Robert M. Whitaker, Jr. of Saratoga Springs, NY and Riverport, Nova Scotia, two daughters, Anne W. Eaton of Stonington, CT, and Sally W. Freud of Cherry Hill, NJ and Putnam Station, NY, ten grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. He was also prdeceased by his brother, John M. Whitaker. Memorial contributions may be sent to The Marie Selby Botanical Gardens, 811 South Palm Ave., Sarasota, FL 34236 or to The Lake George Associaton, Inc., PO Box 408, Lake George, NY 12845. A service was held at The Sanderling Club, Siesta Key. Burial will be at Bonaventure Cemetery, Savannah, GA at a later date.
