
        McGUIRE-Peter Patrick. Of Briarcliff, NY, died suddenly August 9th. He was a devoted educator and father. He is the son of Ethel & the late Peter McGuire and the brother of Maureen, Dorothy, John, Carol & Delores. Resting Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY. Mass of Christian burial will be Monday 10 A.M., St. Theresa's Church, Briarcliff. In lieu of flowers donations in his name to The Scholarship Fund at St. Augustine School, 1176 Franklin Avenue, South Bronx, New York 10456, would be greatly appreciated.
