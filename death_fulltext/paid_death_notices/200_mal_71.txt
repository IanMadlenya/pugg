
        CABRAL--Pedro Chapman, beloved husband of Susan, father of Anna, Lorelei and Maggie, father-in-law of Josh Drew and Kit Griffith, died at home in his sleep on August 30, 2003. He was 80 years old. Pedro served in the 4th Infantry division of the US Army as a first aid man in World War II and received a Purple Heart. He was an avid bridge player and enjoyed discussing bidding theory and challenging hands. He wll always be remembered for his famous anti-percentage play of 7 NT in a team game tournament. More recently, he was a wellknown member of the Briarcliff Community, walking Jasper and chatting animatedly in town over breakfast. Funeral arrangements are private. For information Waterbury & Kelly Funeral Home, Briarcliff Manor, New York 10510.
