
        CARDON-Joan Ascher. Of Wheaton, MD, age 76, on Sunday August 29, 1999 at home. Born in New York City, daughter of the late Charles S., and Helen S. Ascher, she attended Bennington College and was a graduate of Columbia Presbyterian Hospital School of Nursing, and Columbia Teachers College. She was Assistant Director of Nursing at Chestnut Lodge in Rockville, MD, 1953 to 1954, and Accessor of Peace Corp candidates from 1962 to 1963. Survivors include her husband of 45 years, Dr. Philippe V. Cardon, of Wheaton, MD, daughter, Cynthia Cardon Hughes, of Barrington, RI, son, Charles P. Cardon, of Caliente, CA, brother, Dr. Robert C. Ascher, of New York City, NY, and four grandchildren.
