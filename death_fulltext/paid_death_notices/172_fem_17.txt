
        KLAR-Beatrice. Born June 27, 1914. Passed away peacefully on May 2, 2001. Survived by Arnold, her loving husband for over 60 years, and by her loving children Regina, Neil and Eileen, Deborah & Elliott, and loving grandchildren Lisa and Jennifer Klein, Dan and John Klar and Harrison Marks. Bea was a teacher for many years and her life was filled with love for her family, a thirst for knowledge and reading, a need to travel and experience. She has been a shining example and is missed by all who have known her. Funeral service Thursday, May 3, 2001, at 12 Noon, Temple Shaaray Tefila of Northern Westchester, Baldwin Road, Bedford Corners, New York. Interment is in Sharon Gardens. Donations in Bea's memory may be sent to the Larchmont Chapter of Hadassah, C/O Arlene Kaplan, 27 Prior Manor Road, Larchmont, New York 10538.
