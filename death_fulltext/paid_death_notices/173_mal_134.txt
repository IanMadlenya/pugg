
        WADE-Roger W., 82. Died peacefully at home in Kissimmee, FL. on June 7th. Roger was a pioneer in the audio-visual production field with his company, Roger Wade Productions. Beloved husband of Florence. Loving father of Theodosia Sedgwick, Carolyn, Roger, Jr. and Alexander. Dear brother to Dorothy Beckstrand, Clifford and the late Elizabeth. Adored grandfather to nine grandchildren and a great-grandchild. Donations may be sent in his name to the American Cancer Society. A memorial service will be held on July 8th at Scarborough Presbyterian Church in Briarcliff Manor, New York.
