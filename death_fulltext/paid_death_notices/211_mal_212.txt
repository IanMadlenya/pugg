
        SCHLOSSBERG -- Jack. Passed away August 23, 2004 after a brief illness. He is survived by his beloved wife Phyllis, his sons Brent and Billy, his brother Neil, his stepsons Michael and Robert Settler, his daughters-in-law Brenda Schlossberg, Roberta Willet, Mona, Susan and Cissy Settler. He was the son of the late Anna and Joseph Schlossberg. ''Poppa Jack'', as he was known by his ten adoring grandchildren, will always remember the wonderful times they spent with him. Ryan, Kevin, Corey, Sean, Summer, Chloe, Lauren, Julia Settler and Dylan and Dana Schlossberg were his special grandchildren who brought light into his life! Jack served as an Air Force Captain during WWII. He was an attorney and certified public accountant and will be missed by his many clients and friends. Services at Riverside Chapel, Tuesday, August 24th at 11:15AM at 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave, NYC. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, New York. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the American Cancer Society, 1 Executive Blvd, Suffern, New York 10901.
