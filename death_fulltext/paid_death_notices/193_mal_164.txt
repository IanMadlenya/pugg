
        REGAN-Martin J. On February 13, 2003, age 74. Survived by his wife Rosemary. Devoted father of Martin Jr., Michael, Kathleen, Reed, Patricia, Patrick and the late Eileen. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Ave. (at 20th St.), NYC, Friday 2-5PM and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial Saturday 10AM at the Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment St. Peter's Cemetery, S.I., NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Mary Manning Walsh Nursing Home, 1339 York Avenue, New York City, 10021.
