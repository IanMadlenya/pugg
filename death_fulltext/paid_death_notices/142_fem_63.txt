
        NASH-Lili. Died peacefully October 17 in Naperville, IL. She was the loving mother of a son, Thomas Nash, and daughter-in-law, Madeleine Nash, of Warrenville, IL, and wife of the late Edward Nash. A longtime resident of New York City, she was an art historian specializing in old masters. She was born in Kassa, Hungary, August 12, 1908. Memorial service, December 19, 10:30 AM, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. For information please call 630-355-0213.
