
        LAWRENCE--Edward. Age 88, died February 20th, in his home in NYC after a long illness. He received during this time loving care from his daughter Linda. Ed taught in the NYC school system for 35 years. He was a devoted husband to the late Eileen and a close friend to Isabella Lee. He was the beloved brother of Robert, Kathleen, Raymond, Jeanette, and the late Dorothy. He leaves behind a large family who will remember him always as he was so loved. Memorial services will be held in Michigan.
