
        ROCKOWITZ--Anna C. The Hunter community mourns the passing of our loyal and esteemed alumna. A graduate of Hunter College High School and Hunter College, she and her late husband devoted their lives to their children, public education and Jewish causes. The Doctor Murray and Anna Rockowitz Writing Center was dedicated on the Hunter campus in appreciation of her generosity. We were so fortunate to have her with us at the dedication where she shared many wonderful stories and memories. Hunter extends its deepest condolences to her sons Noah and Eli and to her entire family. Jennifer J. Raab, President Hunter College
