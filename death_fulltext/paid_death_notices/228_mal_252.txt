
        MORRISSEY--Thomas J. On January 18, 2006. Beloved husband of Clovene. Loving uncle of numerous nieces and nephews. Mr. Morrissey was awarded the Silver Star during WWII as a commander of an LST ship. He was First Vice President of SmithBarney Citicorp. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral service Wednesday, 3:30 pm, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St.
