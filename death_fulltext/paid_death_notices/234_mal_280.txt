
        ELSTER--Samuel K., M.D., 83, died peacefully July 19th in Santa Rosa, CA. Beloved husband of 61 years to Maxine, devoted father of Charles, Pat, and the late James and Amy. Cherished Zeyde of Tamar, Nicholas and Aiden, loving Uncle Sam to three generations of nieces and nephews, foster father of Tereza and Pablo. Skilled and respected cardiologist and friend to his many patients and colleagues. Memorial services at Temple Sinai, 1 Engle Street, Tenafly, NJ, Tuesday, July 25th, 1pm. Donations in his memory may go to Dr. Amy and James Elster Research Fund, 1 Gustav Levy Place, New York 10128
