
        GOLTZER--Harold. The New York Philharmonic notes with sadness the death, on December 4, of Harold Goltzer, 89, Associate Principal Bassoonist of the Philharmonic from 1958-1983. Mr. Goltzer, who studied at The Juilliard School on a New York Philharmonic scholarship, performed in various recording and television orchestras, and was a beloved teacher for many years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his brother Albert and sisterin-law Doris, his son Eric and daughter Dana Coppolino, and to his entire family. Lorin Maazel, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Zarin Mehta, President Members of the Staff
