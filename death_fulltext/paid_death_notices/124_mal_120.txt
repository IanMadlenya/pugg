
        SCHLOEDER-Francis X., M.D. Of Brooklyn. Age sixty-seven. Of a heart attack in Houston, TX, on May 4, 1997. Renowned nephrologist in the Canal Zone, Houston and Montgomery. Beloved husband of Ilana Reisz. Loving father, stepfather, proud grandfather, cherished brother and dear uncle. He will be remembered for his lovability, humor, generosity, ceaseless quest for truth and knowledge and love of naturea loss to all, much too soon.
