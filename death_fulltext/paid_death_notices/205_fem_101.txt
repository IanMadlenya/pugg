
        BERNSTEIN--Selma D. Died February 2, 2004 at age 90. Wife of Lester. Mother of Robert and Joel. Grandmother of Benjamin, Ariel, Simon, Emily, Jacob and Haylee. Teacher, past President of Hadassah (New Rochelle) and of her condominium. She will be praised at the Gates by her very own deeds. Contributions in her memory should be made to Hadassah (New Rochelle).
