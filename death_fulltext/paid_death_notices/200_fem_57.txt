
        WOLFMAN--Marjorie. A lifelong Brooklyn resident, Marjorie Wolfman, 93, died Wednesday in a Norfolk, VA, hospital. She was pre-deceased byh her husdband, David, and will be remembered with love by scores of friends and family, her sister Sylvia Pollinger of Brooklyn, a son, Conrad Wolfman of Dallas, TX, and a daughter, Peggy Earle of Norfolk. In lieu of flowers, please consider a donation to public television or radio in her name. A memorial is pending.
