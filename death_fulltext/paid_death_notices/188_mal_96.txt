
        MESTEL-Elias, 89, of Pompano Beach, FL, formerly of Middle Village, NY passed away September 7, 2002. Beloved husband of Shirley. Devoted father of Lynn, Andrea and Karen. Grandfather of Ted, Rebecca, Willa, Owen, Blair, Matthew and Peter. Beloved brother and uncle. 2nd Ltd. in the Army in WW II; practiced law in Middle Village for 50 years. He was a man of true integrity, honesty and principle who helped many throughout his life. He will be missed.
