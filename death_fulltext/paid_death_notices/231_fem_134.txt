
        SHERMAN--Diane F. The Jewish Board of Family & Children Services and its Pride of Judea Community Services Division mark with grief and sadness the passing of Diane Sherman, (Mrs. Harold), one of the Pride's earliest founders and benefactors, from our earliest history to the present time. May her name be for a Blessing. John A Herrmann, Jr., President Jeffrey C. Bernstein, Chair, Divisional Committee Gail Wahle, Co-Chair Shoshana Garber, Director
