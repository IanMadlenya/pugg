
        BANGS--Jean Gridley, died on November 18, 2003, eight days before her 77th birthday; on Kiawah Island, SC; after brief illness. Beloved wife for 54 years of Nathaniel Swift Bangs; loving mother of Cally and Betsy Bangs; grandmother of Cally's son Peter; sister of Bill and Harry Gridley. Memorial service next summer on Madeline Island. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Madeline Island Library or the Madeline Island Historical Society, LaPointe, WI 54850
