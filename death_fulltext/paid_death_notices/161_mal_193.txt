
        FEDER-Samuel Lamie, MD. The Department of Psychiatry of Weill Medical College of Cornell University, New York-Presbyterian Hospital, notes with sorrow the passing on June 6 at his home in Lenox, MA of Dr. Lamie Feder, esteemed colleague, mentor and friend. Professor of Clinical Psychiatry at Cornell University Medical College from 1975 until his retirement in 1986, Dr. Feder served as Clinical Director, as Acting Medical Director, and later as Associate Medical Director and Senior Psychiatrist at the Westchester Division of the New York HospitalCornell Medical Center. Faculty and staff who were privileged to work with Lamie extend heartfelt condolences to his wife Marie and to his children and grandchildren. Jack D. Barchas, MD, Psychiatrist-in-Chief Philip J. Wilner, MD, Medical Director Virginia L. Susman, MD, Associate Medical Director
