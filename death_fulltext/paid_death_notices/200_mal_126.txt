
        LASKY--Solomon. Of Fresh Meadows, NY, passed away peacefully at 94 years of age on September 10, 2003 with his family by his side. Loving and devoted husband to Ruth for 60 years, wonderful father to Diane and husband Joel Kushins, Malla Pollack, and Gail and husband Mark Posner, loving and kind grandfather to Lisa Ryder and Michelle Goldstein, Leah and Rachel Posner, and greatgrandfather to Ethan Ryder. He was a founder of the Fresh Meadows Jewish Center and officer for over 40 years. Services are to be held Thursday 1:30PM at Mount Sinai Chapel, 162-05 Horace Harding Blvd., Flushing NY.
