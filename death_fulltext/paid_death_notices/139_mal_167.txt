
        DAIGNAULT-Alexander T., 82. Former Director and Executive VP of Finance for W.R. Grace & Co., died August 13 at his home, 575 Osgood St., North Andover, MA. He leaves his wife Elizabeth (Kendrick) Daignault, a daughter Anne Hartman of Brookline, MA, a son Alexander T. Daignault, Jr., of Gibson Island, MD, along with four grandchildren, Emily and Cindy Daignault and Peter and Ned Campion. A memorial gathering will be held in Quissett Harbor, MA at a later date. Donations in lieu of flowers should be sent to The Visiting Nurses Association, 67 Ter Heun Road, Falmouth, MA. 02540.
