
        BIRNBAUM--Howard K., professor emeritus at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign died on January 23 in Urbana, IL. He was a fellow of AAAS, APS, AAAS, ASM, and a member of the NAE. Birnbaum was born October 18, 1932, in Brooklyn. He is survived by his wife, Freda Silber, his children, Elisa, Scott and Shari; his sister Sybil Licht; and four grandchildren. Graveside service at Mt. Golda Cemetery, Huntington Station, 26 January.
