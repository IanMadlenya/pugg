
        TURECK--Rosalyn. All are invited to attend a memorial service for the renowned keyboard artist, scholar, author and teacher, Rosalyn Tureck, to be held at the Lowenstein Auditorium of Temple Emanu-El, 10 E. 66 St, New York City, on Tuesday, September 9, at 5PM. Donations (fully tax deductible) in her memory may be sent to the Tureck Bach Research Institute, Inc., 2500 Johnson Avenue, 17A, Riverdale, New York 10463.
