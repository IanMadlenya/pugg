
        LEVERE-Arthur, M.D., age 75, died on March 1, 2000 in Naples, Florida. A veteran of the U.S. Army during W.W. II, Dr. Levere was a physician practicing for over thirty years in Spring Valley, NY. Survivors include his wife, Bunny, his daughters, Andrea and Debra, his son Steven, four grandchildren, and his brothers, Herbert, Julius, and Richard.
