
        BULOVA-Ernst, Dr., respected educator, psychologist, writer and beloved founder, with his late wife Ilse, of Buck's Rock Performing and Creative Arts Camp, New Milford, CT passed away January 11, 2001. Ernst's innovative vision for a creative, productive and noncompetitive environment for teenagers, has inspired many thousands of young people since it was founded in 1943. We extend our deepest sympathies to the bereaved family and we invite alumni, campers and staff members, to a Memorial Service, celebrating Ernst and his life's work, on May 6, 2001 at Buck's Rock. Details will be posted at bucksrockcamp.com. Ernst, we thank you for Buck's Rock and we will continue to honor your life every summer at the magical place you created. Mickey and Laura Morris, Owners/Directors
