
        SOLOMON--Pauline, 91. On March 21, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Leo Solomon. Devoted and cherished mother of Diane Solomon Jarett and Joan Weiss, and their spouses Barry and Allan. Adored grandmother of Brian and Michael Weiss. She was a teacher for many years at James Madison High School in Brooklyn. She had been a chorus girl in student productions at City College and danced her way through life, lighting up hearts and lives along the way.
