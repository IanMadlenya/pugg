
        DALY--John Kieran. BornMarch 15, 1956 in Staten Island, NY and most recently lived in Manhattan. John was a partner with Harvey Gladstein & Partners, NY. His career in Law began as Assistant District Attorney, Bronx County. He was a brilliant trial attorney with great respect for the practice of law. John was a proud graduate of Brooklyn Law School, Fordham University and Monsignor Farrell High School. At Farrell, John was a nationally ranked track star and member of the school's winning Penn Relays team. An Accomplished guitarist, John loved all forms of music. He was as well versed in Sinatra as he was in the Beatles. Kieran, as he was known to his family, was a dedicated son to Jack and Marie, a loving brother to Regina, Jay and Marybeth. He was a dear friend to Colleen and funny Uncle Kieran to his nephews and nieces. John embraced life and will be known best for his loving heart.
