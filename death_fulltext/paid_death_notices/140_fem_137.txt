
        SOFTNESS-Leona Golden. 66, of Manhattan and Coconut Grove, FL, passed away September 18, 1998. Mrs. Softness is survived by her beloved husband John, to whom she was married 44 years; devoted children Barney (Betsy) Softness, Randy (Michele) Softness, Danny (Ellen) Softness; brothers Jerry Golden, Robert Golden; and adored grandchildren Ben, Melissa, Sara, Kenny, Alex, Nicki and Bailey. Funeral services will be held 11 AM, Sunday, September 20, 1998, in Miami, FL. Donations in memory may be made to the Multiple Sclerosis Society. For information, contact Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 1-800759-1151.
