
        RAND-Alfred. The Board of Directors of The Lombardy, the residents and staff mourn with sorrow the passing of Alfred Rand on November 24, 1997. Mr. Rand, a resident of The Lombardy for 29 years dedicated his services to the organization as a Member Director, Chairman of the Board and Treasurer. During his tenure on the Board of Directors spanning over 20 years, his commitment and professional guidance greatly contributed to the success of our organization. To Mrs. Rita L. Rand and members of the family, we extend our heartfelt condolences.
