
        KAPLAN-Lillian Y., 94. Devoted wife of Abraham, mother of Rhoda and Hazel, grandmother of Michael, Marcy, Dana, Alexandra and Jonathan, and great-grandmother to Sara, Andrew and Jamie, passed away on December 2, 2001. She graduated from Barnard College with honors, received her Master's degree in Education at Teachers College and her Ph.D. in Guidance from Queens College. Lillian devoted her life to the education of minorities. Graveside service Monday, December 10th at 1pm at Beth David Cemetery, 300 Elmont Road, Elmont, NY.
