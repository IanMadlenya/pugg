
        McGILL-Ann Rowe. The President and Trustees of Columbia University express deep sorrow at the passing of Ann Rowe McGill, the wife of our late former President, William J. McGill. Columbia University pays tribute to her memory and extends its profound sympathy to her daughter, Mrs. Thomas B. (Rowena) Springer, her son, William R. McGill, and her two grandsons. George Rupp, President of the University Stephen Friedman, Chair of the Trustees
