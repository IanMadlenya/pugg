
        WALTON--Marcia Dana, passed away peacefully on March 29, 2007 after a short illness. Marcia was born on June 25, 1920 in Muskegon, MI to James and Dorothy Dana Walton. Her early years were spent in Madison, WI. After the early death of her father she moved to New York City where she and her mother spent many happy years. Marcia worked as a City Librarian and later as a stock market analyst. She was a world traveler and a dedicated patron of the Metropolitan Opera and the New York City Ballet. She recently stated that, she ''was grateful to have had a very long, happy and adventurous life''. Marcia will be deeply missed by her New York City friends as well as her friends in Wisconsin. She was preceded in death by her parents and her sister, Judith D. Walton, M.D. At Marcia's request, no funeral or memorial services were held.
