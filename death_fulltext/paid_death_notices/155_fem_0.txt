
        BELL-Wilma S. Age 90. A resident of Larchmont, N.Y. for most of her life, died Sunday, in Calvary Hospital in the Bronx, N.Y. after a six month battle with cancer. She was predeceased by her husband Clinton M. Bell. Mrs. Bell is survived by her son Stanley P. Bell and his wife The Rev. Cynthia Bell of Larchmont, two grandsons Stephen W. Bell of Amherst, N.Y. and Stuart L. Bell of Princeton, N.J., six great grandchildren and her niece Barbara Thomsen of Mamaroneck, N.Y. A memorial service will be held December 11th, 11AM at Saint John's Episcopal Church, Fountain Square, Larchmont. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461.
