
        WITENBERG-Earl G., M.D. A Memorial Tribute heralding the work of the distinguished Earl Witenberg, Director Emeritus of The William Alanson White Psychoanalytic Institute will be held Friday April 5, 2002 at 7 P.M. at The Institute. Dr. Witenberg whose formidable presence at The Institute spanned almost half a century, will be remembered for many innovations not the least of which was his proficiency in anticipating and enlisting concepts and techniques that have become fundamental to contemporary psychoanalysis. For further information please contact M. Connell at 212-873-0725 Ext 15.
