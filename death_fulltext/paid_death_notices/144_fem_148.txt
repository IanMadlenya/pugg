
        FOREM-Mrs. Esther Amster. Died Wednesday, Janaury 13 in Richmond, Virginia. Beloved widow of Theodore Ross Forem; mother of Wendy Forem Alpert of New York City and Linda Forem Donohue of Richmond; grandmother of Mayme Beth and Benjamin; mother-in-law of Dave and Gary; sister of Morris Amster, Deane Amster Robbins, Jack Amster and Leo Amster. Mrs. Forem was a frequent contributor to the Editorial Page of The Richmond Times-Dispatch and was named Correspondent of the Day in 1998. She will be missed by all who knew her.
