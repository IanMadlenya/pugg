
        MARBURG-Walter. Of Fort Lee, N.J. Beloved husband of Gerda. Loving father of Annette Berg, Mark and Susan Berg. Devoted grandfather of Jeffrey and Robert Berg. He was the Founder and President of The New Synagogue of Fort Lee. His loss will be felt by his extended family and friends from around the world. Services Monday, 12 Noon, ''GuttermanMusicant'', 402 Park Street, Hackensack, N.J. Contributions to The New Synagogue of Fort Lee would be appreciated.
        MARBURG-Walter. The New Synagogue of Fort Lee mourns the loss of our beloved Founder and President, Walter Marburg. Our condolences to his beloved wife, Gerda, and to his family and friends.
