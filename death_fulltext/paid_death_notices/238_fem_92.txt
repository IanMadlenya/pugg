
        HINDES--Betty, 81, of Croton, NY, a gifted sculptor, painter and jeweler, died September 21, 2006. Born in the Bronx, she graduated from NYU and studied painting with Jack Levine. At 22, she married Manhattan dentist George Hindes with whom she had two children. She later married jazz composer Manny Albam, with whom she lived until his death in 2001. Hindes is survived by sons Paul and Andrew Hindes and grandchildren Kate, Sam, Coleman and Lena Hindes.
