
        MURPHY-Walter J. The Board of Directors and Staff of The National Counsel on Alcholism and Drug Dependence, Inc, mourn the passing of Walter Murphy, a former Executive Director, and extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Debbie, and family. Walter dedicated his later life to fighting the stigma associated with addiction and will forever be remembered for his role in ''Operation Understanding'', a watershed event in the nation's capital, when more than fifty prominent individuals publicly acknowledged their recovery from alcoholism. Gorden Steinhauer, Chair Stacia Murphy, President
