
        KORN-Lee. Passed away on October 11th, 2001, age 95. Widow of the late Irving Korn with whom she shared 69 years of wonderful marriage. Survived by her cherished children, Sheldon and Irys Korn, and Robert and Joan Korn, and by her beloved grandchildren and great grandchildren, Ilene and Dave Zatt, Stacie, Gary, Ross and Lucas Spielfogel, Melanie, Keith, Casey and Dylan Stanton, Gary, Shari, Jordan, Jamie and Emily Korn, and Wendy, Erik, Daniel and Matthew Bronstein. She was a loving and intelligent woman, a very devoted wife and mother, and a wonderful grandmother, great grandmother and friend. She will live on in our hearts always. Services will be private.
