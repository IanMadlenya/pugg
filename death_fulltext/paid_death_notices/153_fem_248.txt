
        FEINBERG - Elizabeth (nee Smith). On October 21, 1999. Respected and beloved former teacher at New Dorp High School, Staten Island. Former Chairman of the Department of English, Walton High School, Bronx. Beloved wife of the late Sol Feinberg, dear mother-in-law of Wera Hildebrand, loving grandmother of Andrea and Kirsten Hildebrand, devoted great-aunt of Thomas Miles. A memorial service will be held Saturday, November 6th at the Horn-Dannecker Funeral Home, 336 W. 23 St, NYC at 2PM. In lieu of flowers please make donations in her memory to either The Recordings for the Blind or Save the Children Foundation of Appalacia.
