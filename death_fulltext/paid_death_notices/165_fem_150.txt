
        WILFORD-Sophie, 86 years old of Port Washington and Glen Cove, New York. A former employee of The New York Times, she died on October 8, 2000. Wife of the late Olaf, designer for Tiffany & Company. Mother of Marion S. Quinn (John E.), Margaret Wilford-Tyson (Richard B.). Sister of the late Harry Shibuk (Velia) of Florida, and half sister of Charles Shibuk. Grandmother of Arthur G. (Colleen) and Andrea M. A Prayer service and burial was held at Nassau Knolls Cemetery, Port Washington, New York.
