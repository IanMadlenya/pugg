
        DARST-Susan McGinnis, 89. Died peacefully on January 11. A 43-year resident of Nashville. Widow of Guy B. Darst. Beloved mother of Guy B. Jr., David M., Charles L., Daniel S. and the late James M. Darst. Dear grandmother of Elizabeth, David Jr., Devin, Winnie, Owen, Jim and Kathryn. She will be fondly remembered.
