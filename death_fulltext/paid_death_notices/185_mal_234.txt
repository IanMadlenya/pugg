
        LEGUILLOU-Yves Corbel, 93. Of Forest Hills, Queens, died May 21, 2002. He is survived by his wife, Eleanor, three children, Peter, James and Eleanor Ingbretson, and three grandchildren. Burial was in Roudouallec Cemetery, Roudouallec, Brittany, France. There will be a memorial mass at Our Lady of Mercy Church, Forest Hills, Friday, June 28 at 10:30 AM.
