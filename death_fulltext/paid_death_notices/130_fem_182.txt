
        FRANZHEIM-Melita. Died Friday evening at 9:20 surrounded by her family and friends. She was an art student in New York and Europe and also resided at 5143 Green Tree, Houston, Texas. She was 28. Born October 28, 1969, in Houston, she was the daughter of Ambassador Kenneth Franzheim II and Barbara Lynn Franzheim Dror. She attended Bard College, The New School for Social Research, New York University and the Albe Steiner School of Mosaics in Ravenna, Italy. She is survived by her mother, father and her sisters Pamela, Larisa, Roberta and Sabrina and a brother Daniel Dror II. A private viewing for friends will be at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home on Madison Avenue at 81st St. Sunday evening. An Unbirthday Party will be held for her at an undisclosed location this weekend. Please contact Sabrina Franzheim to r.s.v.p. and for further details. Contributions in lieu of flowers should be sent to the American Brain Tumor Association, 2720 River Road, Des Plaines, IL 60018.
