
        GOLDMUNTZ-Lawrence Allen. On Friday, September 19, 1997, of Washington, DC. Beloved husband of Barbara Goldmuntz. Devoted father of Ellen Goldmuntz of Washington, DC, Jane (Randall) Grady of St. Louis, MO, and Elisabeth Goldmuntz (Frederic G. Barr) of Philadelphia, PA. Loving brother of Janet Liebowitz of New York, Doris Vogel of Bridgeport, CT. Adoring grandfather of Justin and Peter Metz, Jacob and Caleb Grady, and Julia Goldmuntz Barr. Funeral service on Tuesday, September 23, 1997, 10 AM, at Adas Israel Congregation, Washington, DC. Interment at Vineyard Haven, MA. In lieu of flowers expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to the Lawrence A. Goldmuntz Memorial Fund at Jewish Communal Fund of NY, 130 E. 59th, NY, NY 10022. Additional information call Danzansky-Goldberg Chapels, Inc. 301-340-1400.
