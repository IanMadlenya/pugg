
        KORNREICH-Doris J. (nee Greenberg) aka Doris Schwarz and Doris Deutsch. Beloved wife of the late Benjamin Kornreich, loving mother of Ronald and Richard Deutsch, devoted sister of Sissy Greenberg, adored aunt, cousin and wonderful friend to many. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to Israel Humanitarian Foundation for the Doris Kornreich Art and Music Project, 276 Fifth Avenue, Suite 901, New York, New York 10001.
        KORNREICH-Doris. The community of West End Synagogue will greatly miss the vibrant smile and wit of this gutsy, funny, spirited lady who always knew what was really important. Her family has all our sympathy. West End Synagogue: A Reconstructionist Congregation Rabbi Yael Ridberg Cantor Deborah Jacobson Jane Weprin-Menzi, President
