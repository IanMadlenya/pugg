
        PEABODY-Joseph Haven. Died peacefully and unexpectedly at his home in Naples, Florida on Saturday October 28, 2000. Born March 31, 1921, son of the late George and Ruth Peabody of fairfield, Conn. Survived by his wife Lois, daughters Cornelia (Anthony Sroka) and Melissa, beloved grandfather to Kyley and Christopher Sroka. Joe served with the Americna Field Service in North Africa and later with the Marine Corps. He was educated at St. Paul's School in New Hamphire, Trinity College in Hartford, CT, and the University of Virginia. For most of his work life, he was employed by Texaco and Petrofina Canada, retiring to Naples, Florida in 1979. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends. There will be a memorial service at Saint John's Episcopal Church, 500 Park Shore Drive, Naples, on Thursday November 2nd at 4 PM. with burial in the family plot in Southhampton, Long Island at a later date.
