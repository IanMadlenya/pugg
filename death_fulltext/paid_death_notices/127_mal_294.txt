
        ZLOTNICK-Martin. Beloved husband and father and step-father, died August 26, at Georgetown University Medical Center (24th of Av). In his 47 year career he worked in research and development of aircraft, missile defense, energy technology and policy research. At various times he was afflitated with Nichols Research Corp, ABMDA, DOE, AVCO and Hudson Institute. His publications appeared in ''Science,'' ''Omni'' and technical jounals. He is survived by his wife Ruth and children Hila, Cheryl, Adam, Daniel, Naava, Tali and Debra. Donations in his memory may be made to Kesher Israel Congregation, Washington, DC.
