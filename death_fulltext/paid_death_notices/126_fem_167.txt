
        FEFFERMAN-Barbara Ohrbach. Of Cathedral City, California. Died July 17, 1997 at Desert Hospital Hospice in Palm Springs. She is survived by her mother Ree Ohrbach, her husband Frank, five children and six grandchildren. Services will be held noon Monday at Ramon Chapel, in Cathedral City, Calif. She will be sorely missed. The family suggests donations to Neuro Fibromatosis Foundation in New York City.
