
        KUNSBERG--Marcella. Died peacefully on May 2, 2005, at the age of 89 at her home in Los Angeles. Predeceased by her husband Stanley, she is survived by her four children Carolyn Gore, Bette Dee Goldberg, Sandra Kunsberg Rosenfield and Philip Kunsberg, by her ten grandchildren and by her six greatgrandchildren. Family matriarch, ever-caring 'Nana' she will be greatly missed.
