
        LASCHER-Michael F. DDS, Colonel (Ret.) US Army, of Bay Shore, NY, and Olympia, WA. Suddenly on July 20th, 2000, in his 55th year. Devoted son of Charlotte (Rogals) and the late Frank H. Lascher. Loving brother of Jane Lascher, Nancy Lascher, Charlotte Vescova and Gail Rogals. Nephew of Joseph Muller and George Lascher. Also survived by many cousins. Dearest friend of the Joseph Mulrean and Paul Kennedy families of Olympia. Graduate of the United States Military Academy Class of '67 and Temple University Dental School Class of '76. Decorated veteran of the Vietnam War and Desert Storm. Practicing oral and maxillofacial surgeon in Olympia. Visiting Wednesday and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, at the Overton Funeral Home, Inc., 172 Main St., Islip, NY. Funeral Mass Friday, 10 AM, St. Patrick's RC Church, Bay Shore, NY. Interment with military honors Holy Sepulchre Cemetery, Coram, NY. In lieu of flowers, please make donations in memory of Michael to St. Patrick's Hospitality Center, 9 Clinton Ave., Bay Shore, NY 11706 or West Point Fund Class of 1967 Gift Account, Association of Graduates, 698 Mills Rd., West Point, NY 10996. The family wishes to thank Michael's West Point classmates, the US Army Medical and Dental Corps, the many civilian dentists and oral surgeons, his friends and patients for their support and compassion during his hospitilization in Seattle.
