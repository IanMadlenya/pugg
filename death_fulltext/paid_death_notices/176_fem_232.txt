
        SCHOENBERG-Eleanor A. Suddenly on September 25, 2001. Beloved wife of Saul. Devoted daughter of Anna Melenkivitz. Dear sister of Edward and Joseph Melenkivitz. Cherished aunt to Adam, Rory, Amy and Laura. Sister-in-law to Judith Bindler and aunt to Robert Bindler. Loving friend and colleague. Eleanor possessed true beauty, style and grace. She suffered greatly during her last years and can now rest in everlasting peace. Memorial service to be announced.
