
        MILLER--Seymour A., M.D. Age 91, of Scarsdale, NY. Passed away at home on November 6, 2004, after a valiant struggle with Alzheimer's. Dr. Miller first trained as an anesthesiologist and served in the Second World War. He worked for many years at Mt. Sinai Hospital in New York City. After a second residency in psychiatry, he served as a consultant at Hawthorne Cedar Knolls and had a private practice in White Plains. He leaves his beloved wife Marjorie; daughters, Wendy Mnookin and Susan Miller; sons-in-law, James Mnookin and Harold Poster; grandchildren Seth, Abigail, Jacob and Julia. A funeral service will be held at 11 AM, on November 8, at Westchester Reform Temple, 225 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Hawthorne Cedar Knolls or Westchester Reform Temple.
        MILLER--Dr. Seymour A. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Dr. Seymour A. Miller. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Marjorie, and to the entire family. James M. Dubin, President Richard J. Bronstein, Sec'y
