
        HALL--Martha Parker. Beloved, age 89, of Wellfleet, MA. and Manhattan, died March 5, 2006 in Nashville, Tn. Collage artist, great friend, lover of jazz and Manhattan. Former wife of Edward Gemmell, Monroe Hall, and widow of John Hughes Hall, she leaves two step-daughters, a step-son, two goddaughters, many step-grandchildren and great grandchildren; her sister Harriett Alexander of New York and cousin Ruth Dove of New York.
