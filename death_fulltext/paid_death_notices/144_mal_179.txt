
        COHN-Martin Lewis III. The Trustees and staff of New York University School of Medicine extend their deepest condolences to Laurence Tisch, distinguished former Chairman of the Board of New York University, and his wife Billie on the loss of their brotherin-law Martin L. Cohn. Family members and friends who wish to make a contribution in memory of Mr. Cohn are requested to send their checks to NYU School of Medicine, Office of Development, Martin Cohn Fund, 316 East 30 Street, New York, N.Y. 10016. Robert M. Glickman, M.D. Dean, NYU School of Medicine
