
        GOLD--William (Bill). On August 9. Survived by his beloved wife Cindy of 51 years; sister Muriel; daughter Laurie and partner Nancy; son Mark and wife Lisa; grandchildren Alex, Jessica, Sydney and Ross. Graduated Bronx High School of Science '46, Harvard '49. Since then he has been an active volunteer fund raiser for Harvard as a ''thank you'' for accepting him and giving him a scholarship. Ater graduating Columbia Law School he was an Assistant DA in Manhattan before becoming a trial lawyer. For the past 38 years he has been an active member of the Great Neck community, and recently served as Village Judge and Trustee of Temple Beth El. His keen wit, charm, intelligence, warmth and friendliness will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral Tuesday, August 12, 10 AM, at Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhasset.
        GOLD--William. Temple Beth El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its Trustee and longtime devoted member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
