
        ROBB - Audrey Wagstaff. Died peacefully on April 11, 2003, following a long illness. Predeceased by her husband J. Hampden Robb. She is survived by her devoted daughters, Diana Robb McDowell and Christy Robb, her son-inlaw, Tony McDowell, and her two grandsons, Robb and Alex McDowell. She is survived also by her half sister, Debby Wainwright of West Yorkshire, England, and her half brother Philip Wagstaff of Leeds, England, and by her step-children, James, Wendy, Cynthia and David, and ten step-grandchildren. In lieu of flowers a contribution to the New York Society Library would be greatly appreciated. A memorial service will be held later this month in NYC.
