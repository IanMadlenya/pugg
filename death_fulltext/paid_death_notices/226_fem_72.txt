
        LAGIN--Florence. November 8, 2005. Devoted wife of Herbert. Loving mother of Arlene, Fred, Sheri and Robin. Beloved by her husband, children, grandchildren, brother, sons-in-law and great-grandchild. She made each feel special and loved. She was a woman of great strength and dignity, even in death. She will be missed by all whose lives she touched. Memorial service Friday, 10:30AM, Riverside Chapel, Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, donations in memory of Florence Lagin, C/O NYU Medical School.
