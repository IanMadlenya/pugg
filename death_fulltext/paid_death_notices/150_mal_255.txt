
        BUCHANAN-Charles Nicholas. On July 13th, 1999. At age 59. Of Mesothelioma. Beloved son of Lucie (nee Trincellita), and the late Douglas, brother of Douglas and Jane, uncle and godfather of Nicholas. He was a long-time resident of Hoboken, where he grew up, and a graduate of Fairleigh Dickinson University. A memorial service will be held in September.
