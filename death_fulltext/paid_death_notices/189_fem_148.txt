
        KAROSEN-Mary Vivian. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Kansas City, passed away on October 19, at her home in Boca Raton, FL. Services will be held Tuesday, 1 PM, at Louis Memorial Chapel in Kansas City. Mary was born in Kansas City and was a graduate of Central High School. She was the ''Swing Girl'' for Swing Magazine in 1948. An accomplished model, actress and devoted wife, mother and grandmother. She was a member of Boca Rio Golf Club and of B'nai B'rith Foundation, Council of Jewish Women, Hadassah Foundation and Brandeis Foundation. She was preceded in death by her loving husband Leon Karosen. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law Valerie K. and Ludwell Gaines of Shawnee Mission, KS, her son Kent Lawrence Karosen of New York City, her grandson Reed Alan Markowitz of Las Vegas. She is also survived by many loving family members and many loyal friends, all of whom enjoyed the glow of her beauty and the kindness of her heart. She will be missed. The family requests donations be made to the Cantor Fitzgerald Relief Fund at 135 East 57th Street, NY, NY 10022.
        KAROSEN-Mary. The membership and Board of Governors of the Boca Rio Golf Club, Boca Raton, FL, announce with deep regret and sorrow the passing of our dear friend Mary Karosen and extend our sincerest condolences to her son, and our esteemed member, Kent, and their family. Robert P. Adler President Boca Rio Golf Club, Inc.
