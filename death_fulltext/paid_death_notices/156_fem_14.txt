
        SPURGEON-Gertrude (nee Terry). On December 29, 1999 age 94. Beloved wife of the late Thomas W. Spurgeon, Jr. Loving aunt of Lee Salisbury of Fairbanks, Alaska and Robert Salisbury of St. Petersburg, Florida. She is also survived by seven great-neices and great-nephews. A pillar of the nursing community, she was Head Nurse at Montefiore Hospital for many years. Funeral service on Tuesday at 10:00 a.m. at John F.X. McKeon & Son, Inc. 3129 Perry Avenue, Bronx, NY. Interment will follow at Orient Central Cemetery, Orient Point, Long Island, NY.
