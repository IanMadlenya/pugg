
        MOGIL -- Kenneth. Beloved husband of Randi, father of Bari and Dylan, brother of Jeffrey and Robert, son of Merle and the late Alvin Mogil. Ken was a partner in the Mogil Organization, an insurance management firm based in New York City. He was actively involved with the Cornell University Alumni Association since his graduation from Cornell's School of Hotel and Restaurant Management in 1978, and was recently named president of his class. Ken and his wife were volunteers and wish-granters for the Make-A-Wish Foundation. Services Monday, 1:30 PM, at Temple Shaaray Tefila, Baldwin Road, Bedford Corners, NY. Memorial contributions should be sent to Mt. Pleasant PBA, P.O. Box 223, Hawthorne, NY 10532, designate contributions to: Dive Team Equipment.
