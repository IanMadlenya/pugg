
        PLAPINGER--Jerome Stanley, of Washington, D.C., on May 31, 2003. A graduate of Townsend Harris, City College of New York and New York University School of Law, he had a lengthy and varied career as a lawyer in both the U.S. Government, where he served for more than 32 years, and the private sector. His wife of almost 53 years, Alice Einstein Plapinger, predeceased him in February of this year. He is survived by his son, William A. Plapinger, and his wife, Kathleen J. Murray, and their children, Alexander, Elizabeth and Thomas; his daughter, Elizabeth Schaap Plapinger, and her husband, John L. Berger, and their children, William and Chloe; his sister, Carol Plapinger; and his sister-in-law, Barbara Asch. Memorial services will be held in private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Iona Senior Services, 4125 Albemarle Street, N.W., Washington, D.C. 20016. >>AD#
