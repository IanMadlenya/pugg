
        EHRENBERG--Shirley. Died January 2, at age 92. Adored wife of the late Francis Ehrenberg, beloved mother of Arlene Hammond and her husband Bill, the late Joan Schlanger and her husband Michael, and Robert Ehrenberg and his wife Eleanor. Cherished grandmother to David Hammond and Elizabeth Portnoy, Steven, Margo, Karen and Daniel Schlanger, and Andrew Ehrenberg, Stacy Gilpatrick and Jodi Valpey and loving great-grandmother to their children. Her devoted attention to her family and friends will be sorely missed. Services Sunday, January 4 at 10:30 AM at Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett. Contributions in her memory to the Jewish Theological Seminary, 3080 Broadway, NY, NY 10027.
