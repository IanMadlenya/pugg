
        JACOBS-Murray. Of Forest Hills, N.Y., and Princeton, NJ. Executive Officer of Celebration Trading, Inc., passed away peacefully at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, on January 22nd, leaving his beloved wife Eleanor, his devoted daughter Arlene, and son-in-law Henry Opatut, loving grandchildren Jessica & Daniel, and brother Zigmund Jacobs. A Holocaust survivor, concert violinist, and designer, known & loved in the Garment District for almost fifty years. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations be sent to The Dysautonomia Foundation, 20 East 46 St., New York, NY 10017. Funeral will be held on Sunday, January 24th at 2 P.M. at The Jewish Center of Princeton, 435 Nassau Street, Princeton, New Jersey.
