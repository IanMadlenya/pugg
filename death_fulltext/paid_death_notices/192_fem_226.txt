
        DENNIS-Gertrude Weyhe. The members of the Art Dealers Association of America mourn the passing of their colleague, an ADAA member since 1963. Gracious, elegant and knowledgeable, she was for many years an outstanding dealer in American prints and drawings. Our sympathy and condolences to her daughter, Deborah and her brother, Arthur. Richard Gray, President Gilbert S. Edelson, Admin VP
