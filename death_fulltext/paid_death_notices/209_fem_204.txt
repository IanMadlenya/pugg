
        THOMAS--Doris Lang. Died June 22nd, 2004. She will be missed by her godchildren-David Hurvitz, Martha Klein, Karen and John Shea (Zachary and Molly)--and her many friends and family. Always independent, a pioneer and a role model, she was passionate about her work, her interests, and the people she loved. Please join us as we celebrate her life at the New York Society of Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64th St. at Central Park West, NYC on Monday, July 12, at 7 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Opera Index, c/o Bob Crosby, 142 West End Ave., NY NY 10023.
