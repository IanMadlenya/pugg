
        BERTRAND-Maurice A. Age 75, of Palm Beach Gardens, FL, died suddenly on Sunday afternoon, May 2, 1999. Former New York City restaurateur of LeManoir, Clos Normand, La Rotisserie. Survivors include his beautiful wife, Genevieve and loving brother, Valentin. Service May 6 at Price Funeral Home, North Palm Beach. Interment later at Vannes, France.
