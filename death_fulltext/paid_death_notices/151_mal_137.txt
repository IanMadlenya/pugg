
        EASTON-Robert. A retired engineer at Bell Laboratories and longtime teacher at Fortune Society, died yesterday at his home of a heart attack. He was 68 years old. Mr. Easton was born in Munich, Germany, moved with his family to Chicago where he was in the Hutchins Program at the University of Chicago, and received a Master's Degree from Cal-Tech. He joined Bell Labs in 1954 and specialized in underwater cable development. Mr. Easton was also active on the Board of the Village Community School. In addition to his wife Paula and son Matthew, Mr. Easton is survived by his sister, Eda.
