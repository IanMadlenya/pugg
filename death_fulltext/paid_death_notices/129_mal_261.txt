
        POLIKOFF-Hy. On October 22, 1997 (Simcha Torah). Age 85. He died after a long goodbye. Beloved husband of Ruth. Devoted father of Phyllis and Steve Opochinsky and Rhoda P. and Joseph Traum. Proud ''Papa'' of Laurie, Juliet, Jillian and Seth. A man for all seasons. Services on Sunday, 10:30 AM, at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, New York.
