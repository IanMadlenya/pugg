
        SCOTT-Ethel (nee McCullough). Known as ''Babs'', formally of New York City, died January 23, 2000. She was the widow of William Rufus Scott. Survivors include a daughter and two sons. A Celebration of her life will be held at the Carriage Barn at Bennington College on Saturday, January 29, at 2 p.m. Contributions may be made to a musical organization of ones choice through the office of the Mahar & Son Funeral Home, 628 Main St., Bennington, VT 05201.
