
        MELNICK-Gerald J. We at M. Melnick & Co., Inc. wish to express our deep sympatheis and sorrow to the family of Gerald Melnick: Eleanor, his devoted wife and lifecompanion; his children, Edith and Howard; their spouses, Scott and Jill; and his grandchildren, who were his prime delight. Gerry was an honest businessman, a hard worker and a good soul. We will miss him as will all who knew him. May his family take comfort in the memory of this sweet, gentle man
        MELNICK-Gerald. Of Morris Plains, NJ, on Monday, April 9, 2001. Beloved husband of Eleanor. Devoted father of Edith Yaffe and Howard Melnick. Dear brother of Irving Melnick and Laura Braverman. Also survived by four grandchildren. Funeral services will be conducted from Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Road, Union, NJ, on Tuesday, April 10, 2001, at 11:30 AM. Interment Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
