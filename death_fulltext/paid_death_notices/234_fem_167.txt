
        BRONSON--Virginia C. ''Ginnie'' Bronson, 83, died Sunday, July 23, 2006. She was a registered nurse, working at Bellevue Hospital in New York City, as a supervisor for 40 years. Virginia was a member of the Audubon Society. Her family includes her brother, Charles Bronson; two nieces, Cynthia and Laura; two nephews, Warren and Mark; and her partner of 45 years, Barbara Foley. Memorial donations may be made to the Nature Conservancy. For information South Brevard Funeral Home, Melbourne, FL. (321) 724-2222
