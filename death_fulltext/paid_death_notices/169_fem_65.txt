
        NETTER - Kimberly Lawrence. The Rockefeller University community notes with sorrow the passing of Kimberly Netter, beloved wife of our Council member Donald T. Netter. Her life story is deeply inspiring. We will always remember her enormous courage and amazing spirit in the face of her struggle with breast cancer, and her tireless advocacy on behalf of others afflicted with this terrible disease. We extend our deepest condolences to her husband, Donald, to our friend Richard Netter and to the entire Lawrence and Netter families. Arnold J. Levine, President Richard B. Fisher, Chairman Richard M. Furlaud, Chairman Emeritus and R.U. Council Chairman David Rockefeller, Honorary Chairman
