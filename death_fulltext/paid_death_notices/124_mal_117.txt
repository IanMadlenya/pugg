
        ORTIZ-Anthony John. Tony died May 6, 1997, at age 42, after a long battle with AIDS. His Christmas cards for MOMA delighted thousands every year. Known to many in the AIDS community as the ''little guy with the big voice at demos,'' he will be remembered for his high spirits, spectacular smile and love of white McCoy pottery. Survived by his lover, Michael Longacre; Vanessa Longacre; his mother, Petra Ortiz, sister, Rita Linn, and brother, Bart Ortiz of Greensboro, NC; sister, Patty Grace of Mason, MI; his father, Antonio Ortiz preceded him in death in 1994. Memorial contributions to Housing Works, 594 Broadway, New York City 10012.
