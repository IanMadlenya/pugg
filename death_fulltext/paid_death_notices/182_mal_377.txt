
        EFROS-Allen. Of Brooklyn, NY, died early Tuesday, March 26, 2002 at the age of 87. Beloved husband of the late Belle Efros, to whom he was married for more than 50 years before she died in December 1997. Survived by nieces and nephews, Mr. Efros was a well respected accountant, practicing in New York City for more than 50 years, highly esteemed by his colleagues and clients. He was a supporter of many charitable organizations, and beloved by friends and family. Graveside services will be held on March 27, 2002 at Wellwood Cemetary, Farmingdale, NY at 10 AM. For more information call Weinstein, Garlick, Kirshenbaum Chapel at 718-377-4848.
        EFROS-Allen. Totally loyal, selfless, whose life was one of meticulous integrity and responsibility. We join in mourning the loss of our long time friend and former accountant. Herzfeld & Rubin, P.C. Herbert Rubin
