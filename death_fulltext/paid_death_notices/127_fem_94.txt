
        BALAKIAN-Dr. Anna. The Faculty and Alumni/ae of The Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism extend their condolences to Haig and Suzanne Nalbantian and their families on the death of their mother. Dr. Balakian and her sister, Nona, Class of 1943, had a long and abiding friendship with the Journalism School, encouraging and supporting the development of literary journalism. We honor Dr. Balakian for her devotion to literature and to cultural reporting. Friends and colleagues at the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism
