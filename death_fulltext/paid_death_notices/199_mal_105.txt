
        HOUSTON--Doug. A Boston advertising executive, died on July 29 at his home in Portland, OR. He is survived by his wife Victoria Hawkins Houston, his mother Elaine Cline, his two sons Spencer and D.W., his daughters Lindsay and Mallory and his brothers Stan and Paul. Mr. Houston owned three ad agencies, Houston Hampell Stefanides in New York City, Houston Effler Herstek Favat in Boston and Houston Helm in Los Angeles. His shops serviced clients like Fidelity, NEC Computer, Converse, ITT Hartford and Castrol Motor Oil to name a few. He was taken from us too soon at 52 years old.
