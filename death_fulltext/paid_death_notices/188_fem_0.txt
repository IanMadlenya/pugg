
        ALLEN-Doris A. (nee Condon). Age 70, of Leonia, NJ. Beloved wife of the late Robert Allen. Mother of the late Robert, Richard and Terry Stotts. Sister of James (Patricia) Condon, Jane (Illinois State Senator Wendell E.) Jones and Warren (late Carol) Condon. Aunt of Mildred Schroeder and many nieces and nephews. Memorial services will be scheduled for a later date in New Jersey. Contributions to JCC On The Palisades Therapeutic Nursery, Tenafly, NJ. Info: 847-358-7411
        ALLEN-Doris. Albert Einstein College of Medicine mourns the passing of a respected colleague and member of our Department of Psychiatry for more than 25 years. As a teacher of autistic children and a researcher in the field, she made a profound impact on the lives of her young students and their families. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family and friends. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn & Stanley M. Katz Dean
