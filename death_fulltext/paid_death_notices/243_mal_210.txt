
        HERZ--Wolfgang A., passed away peacefully at home on April 16th, 2007, at age 77 after a short and sudden illness. He is survived by Heide, his beloved wife of 44 years, and his loving daughters Anya and Audrey. Wolfgang was a collector of art and rare books, a philanthropist, and an entrepreneur. As a founder and president of American Contex Corporation, he was instrumental in the development of specialty motors for the car shredder industry. Wolfgang was the consummate husband, father and friend and the personification of dignity and elegance. We will miss his presence in our lives, his love and devotion to his family, his generosity of spirit, sense of humor and passion for life. Funeral service: Friday, April 20, 11am, St. Ignatius Loyola, Park Avenue at 84th Street. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his memory to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Account 205568
