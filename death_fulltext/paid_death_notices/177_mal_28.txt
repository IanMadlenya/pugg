
        STRAUB-Edward William. On September 11, 2001. President of AON Compensation Consulting, Edward was a World Trade Center Attack Victim. He was fortyeight. Ed was born in Bennington, VT; grew up in Williamstown, MA, and moved to Morris Township seven years ago. Ed left behind his beloved wife Sandra, his four sons Michael, Jonathon, Aaron and Sam as well as his two grandchildren, Joshua and Zachary, his parents Gloria and Edward, his grandparents Adam and Bea, as well as his two brothers Matthew and Stanley. He was deeply loved.
