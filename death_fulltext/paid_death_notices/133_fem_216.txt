
        GOLDMEIER-Harriet. Beloved wife of Larry, adoring mother of Allen (Cheryl), Sharon (Ted), Steven (Debbie). Loving grandmother of David, Lindsey, Jason, Julie, Lori, Alex and caring sister Rita. Harriet in her short life touched so many people with her love, wisdom and innate beauty. She redefined the meaning of the word ''family'', which was her sole underlining purpose in life. She lived to the fullest. Donations may be made to the North Shore Hospital Pulmonary Research Fund or American Lung Association. Funeral services Sunday 12 noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York. Interment Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
