
        LEWINSON - Alice. We mourn the loss of my beloved mother, Alice (nee Rossheimer), December 31, 1909June 27, 2006. Born Bamberg, Germany. Married Edward Lewinsohn (deceased) in 1934; lived in Weissenfels, near Leipzig, until 1939. Fled Germany after Crystal Night for America in 1940; lost her beloved parents, Rosa and Hugo, in the Shoah. Twentynine frugal years in New York, 31 good years in Florida and six challenging years in New Jersey. Survived by her son, Thomas. She remained devoted to the memory of her sister and brother, Marie Lorenz and Stephan Rossmer, both deceased.
