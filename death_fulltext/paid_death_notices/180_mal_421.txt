
        SCHILLER-Carl, M.D. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the death of Dr. Carl Schiller. Dr. Schiller was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center for over 50 years in the Department of Surgery. In 1952, he set up the service of Plastic Surgery and served as the Chief of Plastic Surgery for 40 years. His smile will be missed by all his friends, colleagues and patients. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. Steven Farber, M.D., President Herbert Lehman, M.D., President Elect Ira Mayer, M.D., Secretary Amin Hakim, M.D., Treasurer
