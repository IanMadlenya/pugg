
        NEWBOURNE-Joan Teichman. Died February 19, 2000 at Harbor View Nursing Home, Jersey City, N.J. at 97 years of age. Single mother, pioneer in the '40's. Export Manager for General Pencil Company of Jersey City. Started her own business, The Victoria Service Company, and Pen and Pencil Company of North America, maintained until 1988. Survived by her son, three grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Closed casket memorial service at Leber Funeral Home, 20th and Kennedy, Union City, N.J. 11AM12N Wednesday, February 23rd.
