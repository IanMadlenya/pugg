
        SHARZER--Mildred (Goodman), a woman of uncommon valor, entered into rest May 3, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Edward Sharzer. Devoted mother of Rabbi Leonard A. Sharzer and his wife Lois of NYC and Risa Nyman and her husband Philip of Chestnut Hill. Loving sister of the late Paul and Harold Goodman. Cherished grandmother of Jessica and Rebecca Sharzer, Brenda Malkiel, Pamela Nyuman and Devora Reiss. Proud great-grandmother. Graveside services will be held at the Crawford St. Cemetery, 776 Baker St., W. Roxbury today at 9am. Shiva will be at the home of Risa and Philip Nyman and also at the home of Leonard and Lois Sharzer. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to the N.E. Chassidic Center, 1710 Beacon St., Brookline, MA 02445 or to the Jewish Theological Seminary, 3080 Broadway, NY NY 10027.
