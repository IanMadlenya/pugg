
        SHEMO-Thomas E. of Somers, New York, died May 30th, 2000 at his home. He was 49 years old. Mr. Shemo was born November 11th, 1950 in Somerville, NJ to Thomas and Dorothy Shemo. He received his BA and MBA from Harvard. He was an Associate Partner with Andersen Consulting in New York, NY. He will be remembered for his kindness, generosity, dedication and intellect. He is survived by his beloved wife, Catherine and loving daughters Ariel of Somers, NY and DeAnna Shemo of Dover, DE. A memorial funeral service will be held Monday, June 5th, at 10am at Temple Shaaray Tefila of Bedford, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to Amnesty International USA, 322 8th Ave, New York, NY 10001
