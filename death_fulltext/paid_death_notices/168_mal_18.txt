
        WYNN-Milton. Died Jan. 1, 2001, at the age of 90. Son of Max and Sarah and brother of Henry and Harold. Beloved husband of Ruth Bass, all deceased. Revered father of Richard Wynn and Joan Flum. Cherished father-in-law of Joan. Adored grandfather of Stephanie, Joshua, Andrew and Alexander. And great-grandfather of Kathryn and Sarah. Admired grandfatherin-law of Nada Bayoud and Douglas Latto. Dear companion of Ruth Schlesinger (deceased). His was a long and formidable life. May he rest in peace.
