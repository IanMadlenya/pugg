
        MASTERSON-Dr. Charles F. Of Brooklyn, NY died Saturday May 31, 1998 at Methodist Hospital. Dr. Masterson is survived by his beloved wife Vivian (nee Reppke), son Michael, daughter Susan Forrest, granddaughter Jennifer Masterson, and Helen Overbey of Mishawaka, IN. He was predeceased by his sister Misty Webb of Phoenixville, PA. Long associated with the National Safety Council and the New York City Mission Society, he was also an Elder of the Presbyterian Church. During the Eisenhower Administration, he served as Special Assistant to the President on the White House Staff. A nationally ranked tennis player, he was a member of the International Lawn Tennis Clubs of the USA. Interment will be at Green-Wood Cemetery. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the City of New York, Park and Recreation Olmsted Center/Tennis Programming, Flushing Meadow Park, NY 11368.
