
        FUCHS--Sylvia. Our lovely sister and aunt passed away on May 22nd, 2007, at her home in Boca Raton, FL. She was our helper and friend all her life. We will always remember her gentle kindness and her beautiful smiling face. She is survived by Albert and Judy Fuchs, and Melvin Fuchs, and her niece Blair Ford and nephew Jack Fuchs, and grandnephews and grandnieces.
