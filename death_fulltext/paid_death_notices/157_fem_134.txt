
        BAUMGARTEN-Bessie (nee Largeman), on February 13, 2000 at the age of 97. Beloved wife of the late Max. Loving, devoted mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. Dear aunt, friend and dedicated volunteer at White Plains Hospital for over 20 years. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her, but most especially by Mal, Joyce, Beth, Scott, Doug, Lauren, Jennifer, Jamie and Amy. Interment private. Friends welcome at the home of Mal and Joyce on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings.
