
        PHELAN-Sondra. The Lincoln Hall Board of Managers acknowledges with deep regret the passing of Sondra Phelan who faithfully served on the Board for 23 years. Mrs. Phelan served on the Executive and Scholarship Committees. She was a strong advocate for childrens' rights and was dedicated to helping the youth in our care. The staff and Board extend sincere condolences to Mrs. Phelan's family on their loss.
