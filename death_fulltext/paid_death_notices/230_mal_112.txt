
        GEWIRTZ--Marvin H., Ph.D., 78. Extraordinary public servant, research scientist, peace activist, he guided N.Y.C. Department of Health research interns, influenced child health and urban education programs and urged public outcries to end nuclear weapons proliferation. Active for his community to the end, he was loving and dedicated as husband to Michelle M. Boston, father to Michelle Gewirtz, grandfather to Emma and Nora Kako, brother to Jerome Gewirtz, son of the late Hyman and Fannie. A good friend, an athlete, a man of moral fiber, he endeared himself to all who knew him. Memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel (76th St and Amsterdam), on Sunday, March 19, at 4:00. Memorials to City Parks Foundation to support athletic programs for youth.
