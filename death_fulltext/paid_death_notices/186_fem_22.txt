
        BROWN-Betty. Died peacefully at home in New York City on July 4, 2002, at the age of 95 years. Beloved wife of the late Louis A. Brown, her husband of 67 years. Devoted mother of Judge Richard A. Brown and Carolyn Straker, and mother-in-law of Rhoda Brown and Dr. Norman Straker. Loving grandmother of Karen, Todd, Lynn, David and Wendy. Cherished greatgrandmother of Leah and Alana. Contributions in her memory may be made to a favorite charity. Services will be private.
