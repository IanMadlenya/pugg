
        PANKEN--Ethel J. Beloved wife of Dr. Irving J. Panken, mother of Wendy, Kathy and Eric, grandmother of Ben, Becky, Evan, Adam and Daniel, mother-in-law of Andy and Michelle, died peacefully February 6, 2007, after a long illness. Ethel was known by her wonderful laugh and commitment to her family, friends and the International peace movement to which she devoted her life. A memorial will be held in June.
