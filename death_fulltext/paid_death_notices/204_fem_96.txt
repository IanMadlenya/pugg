
        McCABE--Patricia. 75, of NY, NY died Tuesday, January 6, 2004 at the Connecticut Hospice in Branford, CT. Born October 4, 1928 in Albany, NY, she was the daughter of the late Eugene and Dorothy Mosher McCabe. She was an administrative assistant to photographer Mr. Irving Penn. She is survived by her two sisters, Catherine M. Concannon of Wilton and Janet M. Newman of Westport, one brother Eugene F. McCabe of Great Falls, VA and several nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. A Memorial Mass will be held on Saturday, January 10, 2004 at Our Lady of Fatima Church, Wilton, CT at 10:30am. Interment of ashes will follow at Assumption Cemetery in Westport. There will be no calling hours. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate donations to The Connecticut Hospice, Inc., Homeport Cove, 100 Double Beach Rd, Branford, CT, 06405.
