
        DeLUCA--Edward Ernest Sr. Age 70, of Milford, CT. Formerly of Avon, CT. Died peacfully, after a short illness, in his home on Friday, February 4, 2005. He was born on February 23, 1934, in New York City, to the late Vincent and Mary Garguilo DeLuca Sr. He served as a Lieutenant in the US Navy from 1956 to 1959, and he graduated from Fordham University and Harvard Business School. Mr. DeLuca is survived by his beloved wife of 43 years Joan Chiarchiaro DeLuca, adoring children Edward DeLuca Jr. of NYC, and Gregory DeLuca and his fiance Judith of Hoboken, NJ, and caring siblings, nieces, nephews and cousins. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, February 9, 2005, at 10 am, at the Smith Funeral Home, 135 Broad St, Milford, CT. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to Connecticut Hospice, 680 Bridgeport Ave, Shelton, CT 06454.
