
        RAEBURN-Ben. 1911-1997. Founder, president, editor, Horizon Press, NYC. Renowned for integrity and quality. Published dozens of pre-eminent writers and creative thinkers, including his close friends Frank Lloyd Wright, Irving Howe, Harold Rosenberg, B.H. Haggin, James Hanley. Admired for his idealism, ethics, high standards, & brilliant, independent intellect. Loved for his graciousness, generosity and wit. Family requests donations to Alzheimer's associations or research.
