
        MINTZER-William Land. On July 23, 1997, at age 52. Broadway lighting designer, founder and director of the Professional Conservatory Program in Theater Design/Technology at Purchase College, SUNY. Co-owner of The Mountain Road Resort in Stowe, VT. Survived by Jeffrey H. Knox, his partner. Memorial service to be announced, Purchase College, early September. In lieu of flowers contributions to William Mintzer Scholarship Fund would be gratefully received.
