
        DEMAS-Dr. Nicholas Constantine. On January 8, 1997, in Amherst, MA. Formerly of Stuyvesant Town and Mount Kisco. Age 87. Practiced dentistry in New York for forty-six years. Husband of the late Electra Guizot Demas. Survived by his daughter, Corinne Demas Bliss of Amherst and grandchildren Austin Constantine Bliss and Artemis Demas Roehrig. Loved by all who knew him.
