
        MANNING-William F., Rev., CSP. On September 4, 2000 in New York at age 83. Born in Richmond Hill, Long Island, NY, he was ordained a Paulist priest on January 25, 1944. Fr. Manning spent seven years in mission work in Minnesota and Tennessee. His perceptive counseling talents showed themselves in the years he served in the Paulist formation program at the major and minor seminaries. He served parishioners in Grand Rapids, MI, San Francisco, CA, Minneapolis, MN, Greely, CO, Houston, TX, and during his senior years at Saint Paul the Apostle parish, NY. A wake will be held on Wednesday, September 6, 2000 from 2 to 9 P.M. at Saint Paul the Apostle Rectory, 415 West 59th St. New York. A Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated on Thursday at 10 A.M. at Saint Paul the Apostle Church, 60 St. & Columbus Ave., NY. Burial will be at Mount Paul Cemetery, Oak Ridge, NJ.
