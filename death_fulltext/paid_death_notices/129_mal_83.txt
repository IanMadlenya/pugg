
        FAURA-Oscar R., 80, died on September 30, 1997, in Atlanta, Georgia. Mr. Faura served in the United Nations for 25 years. He is survived by three daughters, 4 granddaughters, and one greatgrandson. The memorial service will be at 4 PM, Thursday, October 9, 1997, at All Saints Episcopal Church in Atlanta.
