
        RAVAGE-Maurice. Of NYC, on August 1, 1997. He lived 89 years and, for 66 of them, was the beloved husband of Anita. He was the devoted father of Susan Herron, Alan Ravage, and the late Margot Marek, six loving grandchildren, and 3 great-grandchildren. Formerly Assistant Corportion Counsel of NYC, he was for many years Assistant General Counsel of CIT Financial Corporation. Originally from Minneapolis, he attended the University of Minnesota, where he received his BA and LLB, and was editor of the Law Review. At Yale, where he received his JSD, he was a Sterling Fellow. He was a contributor to several professional journals, and was a sometime contributor of letters to the editor of The NY Times. His honors include Phi Beta Kappa and the Order of the Coif.
