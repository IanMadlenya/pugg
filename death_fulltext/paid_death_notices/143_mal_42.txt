
        COSLOY-Irving. Of Del Ray Beach, FL., and formerly of Patterson, NJ. Beloved husband of Florence for 57 years, devoted father to Barry & his wife, Sherry and to Sharon and her husband, Edward Blank, cherished grandfather of Rebecca, Michael, Amy & David. Also survived by brother, Herman and his wife, Frieda and sister-in-law, Betty Krieger. Died on Dec. 1st, 1998 at the age of 79. He had an indomitable spirit and a kind heart, Coz was loved by all who knew him. Services Dec. 4, 11 AM, Schoem's Menorah Chapel, Route 4, Paramus, NJ. Contributions may be made to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society.
