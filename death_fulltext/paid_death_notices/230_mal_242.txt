
        HUDDLESTON -- Jackson Noyes, Jr. of Seattle, born in Huntington, WV in 1938, passed away of pancreatic cancer on March 11, 2006. He spent his life as a student, businessman, teacher and author culminating in the book ''Gaijin Kaisha, Running a Foreign Business in Japan''. He is survived by wife, Keiko; daughters, Shannon Lea Lucansky & Sayako Huddleston. In lieu of flowers remembrances may be made to Princeton in Asia www.princeton.edu/pia or to Univ. of Washington Huddleston Men's Rugby Endowment www.uwfoundation .org/huddleston
