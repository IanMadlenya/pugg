
        KRUGMAN - Sylvia Stern. Aged 90, widow of Saul Krugman, MD, on December 14, 2002. Beloved mother of Richard, Carol and daugher-in-law Mary. Grandmother of Scott, Joshua, Todd, Jordan and great-grandmother of Daniel, Ryan and Jack. A woman of extraordinary warmth, intellect and generosity who will be missed by all whose lives she touched throughout her long and accomplished life. In lieu of flowers donations in her honor may be made to the MPI Foundation, 4455 LBJ Blvd, Suite 1200, Dallas, TX 75244, for the Sylvia Stern Krugman Scholarship Fund.
