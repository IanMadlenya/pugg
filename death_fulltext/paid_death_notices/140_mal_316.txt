
        KILBANE-Paul L. Left us suddenly and peacefully on September 26, 1998, after a courageous and dignified fight with cancer. He was 64. Loving father of Hilary Allinson (and her husband Edward) and Tara Dixon (and her husband Peter). Grandfather of Carter E. Allinson and Miles J. Dixon. Former husband of Ginger Kilbane. He also leaves his close friend Carolyn Albert. He was Vice President of Marketing at E.T. Browne Drug Company and a former Senior Vice President at McCann-Erickson Worldwide, Inc. A true gentleman with charm, wit, style, and grace. He will be missed by many. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
