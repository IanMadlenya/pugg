
        MORRIS-Norman M., 99. In his sleep on November 24. Beloved husband of the late Adele (nee Groden) for sixty years. Devoted father of Arline and Marvin Lubin and Robert and Sandi Morris. Proud grandfather of Kenneth Lubin, Wendy and Paul Aglietti, Carol and Michael Hundert, Lee and Merry Morris, David and Eta Morris, Richard Morris, Jeffrey and Lauren Morris and Deborah Morris and adored by eleven great grandchildren. Devoted brother to Edward and the late Rose and the late Bessie. Services 12:30 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        MORRIS-Norman. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with profound sorrow the death of Norman Morris, beloved husband of the late Adele Morris. Norman and Adele were true and deeply valued friends of the Hebrew Home and Founders of its Judaica Museum which is housed in the Norman & Adele Morris Wing. Very much loved and respected, Norman's genuine warmth and sensitivity, his wisdom, vivacious spirit and generosity shall be greatly missed by all of us. We extend our sincere sympathy to Norman's devoted children Arline (Marvin) Lubin, Robert (Sandi) Morris, adored grandchildren Kenneth Lubin, Wendy (Paul) Aglieti, Carol (Michael) Hundert, Leland (Merry) Morris, David (Ita) Morris, Richard Morris, Jeffrey (Lauren) Morris, Debbie Morris and his eleven great grandchildren. We hope that your loving memories of Norman will ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec V.P.
        MORRIS-Norman. The Board of Directors and the White Plains Hospital Center community record with sorrow the passing of our esteemed friend, Norman Morris. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his loved ones. H. Guy Leibler Chairman of the Board Jon B. Schandler President and CEO
