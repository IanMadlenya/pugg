
        CURRIER-Michael. The Board of Trustees, on behalf of the entire Putney School community, deeply mourns the death of our friend and colleague Michael Currier. A Putney graduate, class of '79, and an active and long-time Trustee, Michael personified with passion and grace the values of the Putney School. We shall remember him with affection and gratitude, and we extend our most heartfelt condolences to his wife Karin and his family. Sarah Kerlin Gray, Board Chair Brian Morgan, Director
