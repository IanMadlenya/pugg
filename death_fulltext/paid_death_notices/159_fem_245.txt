
        HARVEY-Sucille M. ''Missy''. Of Williamsburg, Virginia. Died in Riverside Hospital, Newport News, Virginia, on April 26, 2000. Mrs. Harvey was the owner of Yacht Charter Unlimited and the founder of The American Yacht Charter Association and The International San Remo Yacht Meeting. Mrs. Harvey is survived by her husband Edward J. Harvey, her daughter Heather Sumner, of Seattle, Washington, and sons Ned Harvey, of New Jersey, Todd Harvey, of Florida and Blake Harvey, of New Jersey. A private memorial is planned.
