
        JACOBS - Herma. Died peacefully May 15, 2006 at 97. Loving mother of Miles and Steve Scheffer. Beloved grandmother of Bill, Andrew, Jenny, Alli and Beth. Adoring great grandmother of Ben.
        $(6$)JACOBS--Herma. Of New York. The Directors and Officers of the National Mah Jongg League mourn the loss of our esteemed founding member. She was involved for 60 years holding every position from Secretary to President. Her energy and love for the Organization was boundless. Her input continued long after her retirement. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her and loved her. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Ruth Unger, President National Mah Jongg League
