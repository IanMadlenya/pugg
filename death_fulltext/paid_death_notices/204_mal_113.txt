
        CARTY--Thomas F. on January 6, 2004 at the age of 85. Dear son of the late Thomas and Elizabeth (nee Finnegan). Brother of Joseph and the late Margaret C. Brock. Also survived by his cousins, nieces and nephews. A veteran of W.W. II, and past commander of the American Legion. President and owner of Carty Heating Corp, NYC, founded 1915 by his father. Thomas was an attorney, real estate investor, contractor and apartment house owner in Manhattan, Bronx, and Queens. He served as a former City Councilman of Yonkers, and Chairman of the Democratic Party of Westchester County. He had been a National President of the Mechanical Contractors Association of America, President of the Mechanical Contractors Association of NYC and President of Queens Daughters Day Care Center in Yonkers, New York. Reposing Thursday and Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Riverdaleon-Hudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Ave, Bronx. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday 9:45 at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment in Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
