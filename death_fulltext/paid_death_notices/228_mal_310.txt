
        BRENNER--Everett. Of Port Washington, NY. Loving husband of Joan. Cherished father of Rick Brenner, Dan and Beth Brenner and Amy and Howard Mitz. Beloved grandfather of Ben, Rebecca, Aden and Jack. Dear brother of Kenneth Brenner & brother-in-law of Billie Diamond. He will be remembered for his zest for life, his passion for the arts and his boundless devotion to his family. Services were held Friday, January 27, 2006 at the Community Synagogue of Sands Point. To honor his memory contributions may be made to Community Mainstreaming Associates Inc, New Hyde Park, NY.
