
        MAGER-Sylvia. Beloved mother of Peter and Alison, loving wife and devoted widow of Nathan, and for many years the beloved friend and devoted companion of Sam Levy and a gracious relative to her extended family and loyal supporter of her many friends, died Sunday, February 5 of a heart attack in Cartagena, Colombia after being evacuated from a cruise ship. A private memorial service will be held Sunday, February 13 at the Marvel Platoff Memorial Chapel of Mt. Moriah Cemetery. The family is requesting that in lieu of flowers gifts may be made to Doctors Without Borders.
