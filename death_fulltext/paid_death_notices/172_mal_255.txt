
        STEINHARDT-Henry. Died age 80 on April 13, 2001. Architect, photograher and environmentalist, he is survived by his wife Helene, his daughter Elizabeth Duffett, his sons Maxwell, II and Henry, III, his sister Leigh Steinhardt Cauman, and six grandchildren. Memorial service will be held at the Emmanuel Episcopal Church, Mercer Island, WA. on Friday, June 15, 2PM.
