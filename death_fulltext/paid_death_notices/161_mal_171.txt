
        POLSKY-Ned, retired publisher, sociology professor, book dealer, and author of ''Hustlers, Beats, and Others,'' died at home, June 13, at the age of 71. He is survived by a daughter, Claudia Polsky, a granddaughter, Talia Mermin, both of Berkeley, Calif., a brother, Leon Polsky of New York City, and a companion, Sarah White, also of New York.
