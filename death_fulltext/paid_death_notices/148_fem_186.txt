
        WELCH-Mary M., 96. Died Tuesday, May 25, 1999, in Centerville, Ma. Beloved daughter, sister and aunt. She is survived by numerous nieces and nephews. Mary resided in NYC from 1935-1973, where she directed public relations for the Elizabeth Arden Company. She retired in 1973 to West Hyannisport, MA., and Fort Lauderdale, FL. With a generous heart, quick wit and lively spirit, Mary was devoted to family and friends. A funeral Mass will be held at 10:00 AM on Saturday at Our Lady of Victory Church in Centerville, MA.
