
        KLING-Arthur S., M.D. died on Friday, March 28 at his home in Los Angeles after a long illness. Dr. Kling was Chief of Psychiatry at the Sepulveda VA Medical Center and former Vice Chair of the Department of Psychiatry at UCLA School of Medicine. He was Professor of Psychiatry at Rutgers Medical School, 1970-1980. Funeral services were held on Monday, March 31 in Sepulveda, CA. Dr. Kling is survived by his mother Rose, his wife Rosalyn, his sons Mitchel, Joel and David and three granddaughters. Contributions in his name may be made to the American Cancer Society.
