
        GABEL, Emil, 79, of Fairlawn, NJ and Delray Beach, FL. passed away at his home in Delray Beach, FL. on Jan. 9, 1997. He is survived by his devoted wife Helen, loving daugther Lissa, and son-in-law John, of Holicon, PA, son Bruce of Wayne, NJ, two granddaughters, sisters Pearl Goldfarb and Dorothy Marcus, precedeased by son Peter. He was the former owner of G&G Distributors of Saddle Brook, NJ. People always worked with him, not for him. He was a very down-to-ear type of person with very high ideals and a great deal of integrity. He was a member of the Fairlawn Jewish Center, Fairlawn Jewish Center Men's Club, Humboldt-Ezra Lodge $114 F. & AM American Legion Post #0171, Jewish War Veterans Post $651, B'nai B'rith Fairlawn Lodge #1970, B'nai B'rith International Israel Lodge. He served in the US Army as Sergeant in the 4th Armor Division. He received a European, African and Middle East Theater Ribbon and Distinguished Service Medal. Services will be held at Robert Schoem Menorah Chapel in Paramus, NJ on Jan. 12, Sunday at 10:00 AM. Interment will take place in the Abraham & Sarah Mausoleum Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
