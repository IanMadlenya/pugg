
        LOVELL--Douglas G. Of Philadelphia, PA. Born August 17, 1927, died November 17, age 76. He is remembered for his travels to the world's great archaelogical sites, his avid interest in nature, and his infectious enthusiasm. Loving husband to wife Alida, and devoted father to his children; Douglas Lovell, Lexy Lovell, and stepchildren; Lylee Van Pelt, Ellen Jordan, and William Van Pelt, and grandfather to eight. His life will be honored with a celebration in May--in the tradition of the festivals he traveled so far to see--with music, color, and stories. Donations may be made in his name to the University of Pennsylvania Museum of Archaeology and Anthropology.
