
        BAKST-Jerome Henry. On June 7, 2002, age 83, in Verona, N.J. Retired National Director of Research and Evaluation for the Anti-Defamation League of B'nai B'rith. Former reporter and editor for N.Y. newspapers. Editor at The Voice of America. 1940 graduate of Harvard College. Survived by his wife Barbara, children Larry and Ellen, and beloved daughter-in-law Cristina and grandson Dylan. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave, N.Y.C. Monday, June 10th, 1:30PM. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations be made to Harvard College.
