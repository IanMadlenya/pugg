
        MATTISON-Victoria. Passed away on September 25, 2002. Beloved daughter of the late William C. and Mary Mattison; stepdaughter of Ann Mattison; cherished sister of William, Andrew, Laure, Carolyn, Marian, and Amy. She was Director of Corporate Development at Fannie Mae. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory can be made to The Washington Humane Society, 7319 Georgia Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20012 or Hospice of Washington, 3720 Upton Street NW, Washington, DC 20016. Friends may call at Gawler's Funeral Home, 5130 Wisconsin Avenue NW, Washington, DC, on Friday, September 27 (today). Visitation hours will be from 12 to 4 P.M., memorial service 4 P.M.
