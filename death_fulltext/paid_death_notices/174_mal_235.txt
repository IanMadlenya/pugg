
        KIRK-Richard Rodney, on July 12th, 2001 at his home in St. Croix. Born in Hendersonville, NC, he graduated in 1955 from the University of the South in Sewanee, TN and in 1958 from the General Theological Seminary in NYC. He was ordained in Newton, NC and in 1968 received a master's degree in psychiatry and religion from Union Theological Seminary. He was on the staff of the Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine from 1969 until 1976. He helped organize the events surrounding the 1976 Bicentennial celebration in NYC. He was a Director of Manhattan Plaza from 1976 until his retirement in 1997. Requiem Mass on Wednesday, July 25th, 11 AM, at St. Mary the Virgin, 145 W. 46th St.
