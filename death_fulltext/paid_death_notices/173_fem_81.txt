
        BASS-Janet G. Of Auburndale, MA. Widow of Leonard L. Bass. Daughter of Louis and Anna Galaton. Beloved mother of Lucille Bass of New York City, Martha Riquier of Needham, MA, and Barbara Baumgardner of Sudbury, MA, and cherished grandmother of Andrea and Adam Riquier and Sarah Baumgardner. Passed away peacefully on June 6, 2001, at the age of 84. She will be deeply missed by family and friends. In lieu of flowers a donation may be made to the American Heart Association. A private memorial service will be held at a later time.
