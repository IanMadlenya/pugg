
        GARDINER--James Bronson II. Sons of the Revolution in the State of New York Inc. marks with great sadness the death on December 11, 2003 of James Bronson Gardiner II, its esteemed member of the Board of Managers, and Life Member. In addition to serving on the Board of Managers at various times during the past 20 years, he rendered valuable service as chairman and member of several committees. In his memory, the Society's flag at Fraunces Tavern will be flown at half-staff for 30 days. We extend our deep sympathy to his family. Kevin F. Hansley, President Eric Kramer, Secretary
        GARDINER--James B. II, The Society of Colonial Wars in the State of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our long-serving Secretary and devoted member, James B. Gardiner II. David N. Platt, Governor
