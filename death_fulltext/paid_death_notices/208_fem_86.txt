
        MAYER--Liesel. On May 7, in her 100th year. Devoted wife of the late George Drew and Albert Mayer. Beloved mother of Peter Drew. Loving grandmother of Jessica and great-grandmother of Anna and Paige. Cherished stepmother of the late Lawrence Mayer and mother-in-law of Mary Mayer, step-grandmother of David and Elizabeth and step-great-grandmother of Maud.
