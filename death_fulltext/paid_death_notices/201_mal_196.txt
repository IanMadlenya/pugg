
        BAILEY--Lloyd Lee. Funeral services for the award-winning designer and author Lee Bailey, 76, of New York City, will be at 2 P.M. Saturday, October 25, in the chapel of Hixson-Ducote Funeral Home, 302 St. John Street, in his native Bunkie, LA. Visitation will begin at 1 P.M. and burial will follow in the family plot at Pythian Cemetery.
