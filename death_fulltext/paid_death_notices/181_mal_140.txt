
        LOSEE-Wilmot H. On February 9, 2002, at the age of 89, in Englewood, FL. Devoted father of Wilmot Hurst Losee Jr. and Steven Thomas Losee and father-in-law of Jean Gilpatrick Losee. Adoring grandfather of Elizabeth Losee McGurl, Geoffrey Andrew Losee, James Alden Losee, Stephanie Losee, Sandy Losee-Woods and Sharon Rodriguez. Also survived by nine great-grandchildren. He was predeceased by his wife of 51 years, Virginia See Losee. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on July 14, 1912, he was a proud U.S. Navy veteran of World War II and retired president of Major Market Radio. Burial will be private.
