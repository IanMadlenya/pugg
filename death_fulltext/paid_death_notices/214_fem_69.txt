
        GREENBERG--Carolyn. CIVITAS Citizens, Inc. mourns the death of its Executive Vice President and Board Member, Carolyn Greenberg. An accomplished social work professional and community leader, she brought intelligence, energy and enthusiasm to the many projects she undertook for CIVITAS. We will sorely miss her determination, her caring concern for others and her good cheer. We have lost a great leader and a good friend. Genie Rice, Chair T. Gorman Reilly, President
        GREENBERG--Carolyn. The Faculty, Advisory Council and Alumni Association of the Columbia University School of Social Work are deeply saddened by the untimely passing of Carolyn M. Greenberg on November 6th. A 1983 graduate, she was a valued member of the Alumni Association Board of Directors, and a respected social work professional and civic leader. Sincere condolences are expressed to her family and friends. Jeanette C. Takamura, Dean MaryEllen Bigham, Pres. Alumni Association
