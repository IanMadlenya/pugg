
        PETER-John. Age 81, beloved father of Laurie Peter, Wendy Peter Abt, Sarah Peter and Molly Peter, and grandfather of Thomas, Emily, Rachel, Katy, Jacob, Lightning, Isaac, Noah and Lilly. Died on May 1 in transit to New York City, his home. Memorial services at the Art Directors Club, 250 Park Avenue South, on Tuesday, May 12th at 6 P.M.
