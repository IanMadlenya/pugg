
        FRIEDMAN--Ida Wortis of Canton, MA. Passed March 26, 2005 at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Abraham. Beloved mother of Hetty and Chris, Ella and Michael. Cherished grandmother of Janna and Rick, Ethan and Maria, Alex and Michaela. Greatgrandmother of Evan, Matthew, Elijah and Aliyah. Daughter of the late Solomon and Fanny Wortis. She was an inspiration to all who knew her, a tireless advocate for peace, equality and human rights. Funeral service Monday, March 28, 2005, 11:30am Parkside Memorial Chapels, 2576 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn NY. The family will be remembering Ida Monday through Wednesday after 4 pm at 15 Waldorf Court, Brooklyn NY. Donations can be made to BCBC.
