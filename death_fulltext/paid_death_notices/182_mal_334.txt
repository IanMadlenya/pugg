
        HANISCH - Herman. Professor Emeritus of Mathamatics at City College of New York, passed away on March 11, 2002, peacefully at home in Riverdale, NY, at the age of 72. He graduated from Stuyvesant High School and the City College, and received his Ph.D. in Mathematics from New York University. Professor Hanisch did research at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences of New York University and collaborated on a number of scholarly papers which have been published both in the U.S. and abroad. He is survived by his wife of 32 years, Greta, his brother Bernard Hanisch & sister-in-law Toula Hanisch, two nephews Arthur & William Hanisch & several cousins.
