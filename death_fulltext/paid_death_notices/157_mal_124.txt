
        WORTHING-Frank. The Lincoln Hall Board of Managers notes with deep regret the passing of Frank W. Worthing who served on the Board from 1983 to 1995. Frank's extensive background and epertise regarding the insurance industry made him an invaluable asset as Chairman of the Audit and Insurance Committee. The Board and staff extend sincere condolences to his wife, Grace, and his family on their loss. James G. Nugent President Board of Managers Douglas W. Wyatt Vice President Board of Managers
