
        IRGANG--Doris of Bayside, NY. Beloved wife, friend, soul-mate and life partner of Alan, loving mother of Scott and Mark, cherished motherin-law of Carole and Laura, devoted grandmother of Emily, Hannah and Dylan and dear sister of Shirley, passed into Heaven on September 27, 2005. Her love for, and influence upon, all of us ran deep into our hearts. Fun was never done with Grandma Doris.
