
        HICKEY-Shannon E. 19, of Jupiter Island, FL, passed away Sunday, October 27, 2002 as the result of an automobile accident. He was born and raised in East Rockaway, NY, had been a resident of Jupiter Island for five years, coming from New York. Shannon was a 2001 graduate of Jupiter High School, attended Palm Beach Community College and planned on attending Florida State University. He was an avid outdoorsman enjoying boating, golfing and fishing. He was a friend to many and loved by all. Survivors include his parents, Thomas and Valerie Hickey of Jupiter Island; his two Brothers, Ryan Thomas and Colin Jesse Hickey, both of Jupiter Island; his maternal grandmother, Elizabeth Guarnieri of East Rockaway, NY; his paternal grandparents, Thomas and Maureen Hickey of Franklin Square, NY and many aunts, uncles and cousins. He was predeceased by his maternal grandfather Louis Guarnieri. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated 10am Friday, November 1, 2002 at St. Jude Catholic Church, Tequesta, FL. Those who wish, may make contributions to St. Jude's Children's Hospital, 332 North Lauderdale, Box 318, Memphis, TN 38101-2729. Friends may call Thursday, October 31 from 1-3 & 6-9pm at the Aycock Funeral Home, 1112 Military Trail, Jupiter, FL
