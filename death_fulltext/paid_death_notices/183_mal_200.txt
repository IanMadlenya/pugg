
        ROTHENBERG-Robert, M.D. The Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart and the Medical Staff of Cabrini Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend who served our institution as a practicing surgeon and Director of Surgical Research. Dr. Rothenberg was a dedicated physician who always showed sincere concern for his patients and for Cabrini Medical Center. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his family. Lawrence Ottaviano, M.D. President, Medical Staff Cabrini Medical Center Robert Chaloner President and CEO Girolamo G. Cuppari, M.D. SVP, Medical Affairs
