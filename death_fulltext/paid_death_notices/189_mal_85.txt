
        JENSEN-Robert Eric. On October 4, 2002. Beloved husband of Helen. Devoted brother of Daniel C. Loving uncle of Danielle and the late Karen. His career in public relations included Vice President of A.J. Lazarus for 17 years, prior to that. Vice President of Gibbs and Soell for 10 years. In the words of his colleagues, ''You're head and shoulders above the rest,'' and ''Bob Jensen, you're the best PR writer in the country.'' In addition to his love of art, Bob enjoyed both jazz and classical music. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Sunday and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, with a service Monday evening at 8 PM, at Frank E. Campbell. Interment will take place on Tuesday, at Evergreen Cemetery, Morristown, NJ. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or a charity of your choice.
