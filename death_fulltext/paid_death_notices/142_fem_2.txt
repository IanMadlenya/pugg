
        HINRICHS-Marion McDonald. 87, of Greenwich, CT and Quogue, NY died peacefully at her home in Greenwich on October 29, 1998. Mrs. Hinrichs was born in Evanston, IL in 1911. She was a graduate of Wellesley College, and had been an active volunteer at Lawrence Hospital, Bronxville, NY, the American Heart Association and Wellesley College. She was preceded in death by her husband Hans Hayo Hinrichs. She is survived by her son, Richard Dimes Buckley and his wife, Connie of Greenwich, CT; a daughter, Martha Ann Fahnoe and her husband, Norman of Camarillo, CA; two granddaughters, Jennifer Buckley of Greenwich, Patricia Jay of San Mateo, CA, and a grandson, Eric Dimes Fahnoe of Anaheim Hills, CA. A memorial service will be held at the Gallagher Funeral Home, 31 Arch St., Greenwich, Tuesday, November 2 at 10 AM. Burial will be private in Quogue, NY. In lieu of flowers, it is suggested that memorial contributions be made to the Greenwich American Red Cross Disaster Relief Fund.
