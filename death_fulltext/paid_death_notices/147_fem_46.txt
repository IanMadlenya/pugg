
        SHURTLEFF-Mary (nee Princess Troubetzkoy). On April 3, 1999. Widow of Otis Lynn Shurtleff, passed away peacefully in her home at The Tolstoy Foundation Center in the presence of her loving stepson, nephews and nieces. Burial at St. Nicholas Russian Orthodox Cemetery in Norwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Tolstoy Foundation Center, 104 Lake Road, Valley Cottage, New York.
