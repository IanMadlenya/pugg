
        WEMPLE-Virginia Waller. Age 88, of Plandome, NY, on April 23, 2002. Daughter of the late Reverend and Mrs. Henry Davey Waller of Flushing, NY. Survived by her husband, Francis Holland Wemple; a son, Francis Holland Wemple Jr.; and three daughters, Virginia Wemple, Martha Wemple, & Anne Wemple Marks. Predeceased by two sisters: Lisa Katherine Hunter and Jane Davey Hamilton. A funeral service will be held on April 29, 2002 at 11 AM at St. Stephen's Church, 9 Carleton Ave., Port Washington, NY 11050. Interment, Flushing Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to St. Stephen's Church in her memory.
