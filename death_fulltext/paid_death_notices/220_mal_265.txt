
        COOPER--Melvin L. The family of Melvin L. Cooper mourn his passing on May 21, 2005 after complications from heart surgery. Son of Benjamin and Eva Cooper, and born on May 13, 1927 in Brooklyn, NY, he was the former CEO and chairman of the Board of Union Corp., NYSE listed, from 1986 until 1998 when the company was sold. Prior to that, he was a venture capitalist with American Diversified Enterprises and on the board of Golden Cycle Gold Corporation. He was also the vice president and treasurer of Sonesta International Hotels from 1960 to 1970 and formed Universal Hotel Company AG, based in Marbella, Spain. Mel, as he was known, served on the board of his Manhattan condominium and was the president of Dune Alpin Farm Corporation in East East Hampton, LI for the past 11 years. He is survived by his wife Gunilla, his sons Jeffrey and Kevin, and their mother Dorothy, Mr. Cooper's first wife and many, many friends. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made in Mel Cooper's name to the charity of your choice. A celebration of his life will be held on Saturday, June 4 at 3 p.m. at the Swedish Seamens Church, 5 East 48th St. in Manhattan. A private funeral service will be held in Sweden.
