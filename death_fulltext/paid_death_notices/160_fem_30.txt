
        PODSTUPKA-Dorothy. Died on May 2, 2000 in Driftwood, Texas. Dorothy was preceded in death by her parents, George and Beatrice Ginnis, and her husband, John Podstupka. Dorothy is survived by many cousins and friends who mourn her passing, but will forever hold her in their hearts. Dorothy was a beautiful person with a wonderful sense of humor, a giving heart, and a boundless zest for life. She will be sorely missed. A graveside funeral will be held at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, New York at 2:30 P.M., Friday, May 5, 2000. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Deborah Hospital Foundation. For information Pennington Funeral Home, San Marcos, Texas, (512) 353-4311.
