
        MASLOW - Grace. Passed away on March 6, 2003 at age 76 in her winter home in Boca Raton, FL. Beloved wife of Lester Maslow and widow of Armand (Rocky) Cantor; loving mother of Patricia Sheridan and Michael Cantor, stepmother to Jane Maslow and grandmother to Jamie; sister of Randolph and Philip Weinsier and adored friend of many. Her elegance and grace will always be remembered. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Hospice by the Sea of Boca Raton, FL. A memorial service will be held Sunday, March 9th at 2:00 PM at Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 7205 West Atlantic Avenue, Delray Beach, FL (561) 496-7550.
        MASLOW-Grace. It is with profound sorrow that the officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of the Fresh Meadow Country Club record the passing of Grace Maslow, a member of the Board of Governors and an esteemed, longtime member of our club. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband, Lester, her children, Patricia and Michael, and to the entire family. Robert Fortunoff, Pres Roger Blumencranz, Secy
