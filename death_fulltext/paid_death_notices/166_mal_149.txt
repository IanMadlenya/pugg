
        BLAUNER-Robert Alexander. On November 12, 2000. Robert died in peace at age 23 after a heroic fight with Hodgkin's disease. He is survived by his parents, Allan S. and Loretta; his siblings, Stephen, Peter and Patsy; and his grandmother, Josephine Steinberg Libman. A memorial service for Robert's friends and family will be held in several weeks. For information contact lsb@compucore.net. Memorial donations may be made in Robert's name to Yale Stem Cell Program, c/o Dr. Dennis Cooper, Yale University School of Medicine, Medical Oncology, 333 Cedar Street-WWW220, New Haven, CT. 06510.
