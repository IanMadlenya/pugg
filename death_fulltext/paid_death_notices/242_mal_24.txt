
        HARRICE--Cy. The Offices, Governors, and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Cy Harrice on February 14, 2007. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. A memorial will be held this summer in Wyoming. Jerry Lewis, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Stu Cantor, Scribe John-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
