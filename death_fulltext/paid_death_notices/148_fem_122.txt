
        TAGER-Frieda. AMIT is deeply grieved by the passing of the beloved Past Chairwoman of our National Memorial Committee and long time member of our National Board. A caring, compassionate, sensitive and true Woman of Valor, Frieda Tager lived a life devoted to Torah, the State of Israel, and Klal Yisrael. Heartfelt condolences to her children, Ruth Shulman and Eddie Tager, and the entire family. Evelyn Blachor, Nat'l President Marvin Leff, Executive Director
