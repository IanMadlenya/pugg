
        FELDSHER--George. On February 10, 2004 at age 88. Survived by loving sisters Anne Roemer (Mark) and Gertrude Orion (Henry), many nieces and nephews, and many devoted friends from his days in show business.With deep sorrow and great regret he took a cab. Funeral services on Friday, February 13 at 10 AM, Schwartz Bros.Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, NY. Interment Mt Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, NY.
