
        RUKEYSER--Louis. PBS and its member stations mourn the loss of Louis Rukeyser. Louis wasn't just a pioneer of financial journalism. He also was an icon of public television. His charm, elegance and brilliant mind made him one of a kind. We extend our deep condolences to his wife, Alexandra, and family. Mary G.F. Bitterman Chairman of the Board Paula A.
