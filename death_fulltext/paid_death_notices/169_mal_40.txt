
        SHAPOFF-Mary. The leadership of Continuum Health Partners joins with the physicians and staff of its member hospitals in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Mary Shapoff, beloved mother of our dear friend and colleague, Edward Shapoff, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer for Continuum. To Ed, his family and all of his mother's loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Peter A. Kelly, President and CEO
