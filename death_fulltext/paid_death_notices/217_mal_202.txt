
        TEPLIN--Milton A., 94, of New Rochelle, New York, on Saturday, February 12, 2005 at The Hebrew Home in Riverdale, NY. Son of Sarah and Joseph Teplin. He was the husband of Ruth Prager Teplin, who died in 1994, and the father of Judith Meltzer of New Rochelle and Amy Post of New York City. He was the father-in-law of Martin Post and the grandfather of Jill Toneatto, Nancy Gottlieb, Kim Linn and Wallis Post. He is also survived by his sister, Florence Blumenthal, and by four great-grandchidren. He was loved and admired by family & friends and will be greatly missed.
