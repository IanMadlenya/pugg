
        CELLA-Dorothy Anne, 74 of Glen Rock, NJ, died February 2. Mrs. Cella was a Vice President with U.S. Trust Co., NY, NY before her retirement in 1995. Survived by her husband Carlo D. Jr., 3 daughters: Dorothy K. Gallagher, Margot A. O'Connor, Elena V. Cella., four sons, Carlo D. III, John D., Michael F., James C., a brother Frederick J. Driscoll Jr., ten grandchildren and one great grandchild. A Mass will be held at 10 A.M., Saturday, February 5th, at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church, Ridgewood, NJ. Friends may call from 2-4 and 7-9 Friday at the C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 306 E. Ridgewood Avenue, Ridgewood, New Jersey.
