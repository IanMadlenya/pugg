
        KRAUSZ-Leslie, M.D., retired psychiatrist, died on July 8 at age 85 at the Manor Care Nursing Home Bethesda, MD. Dr. Krausz immigrated to the U.S. in 1961 with his family from Transylvania via Israel. After completing his training in psychiatry, he established a practice in New Brunswick, NJ. He also held positions as psychiatrist in Brooklyn Veteran's Clinic as well as New York State Clinic. He retired from active practice at age 70. He is mourned by his loving daughter Mariette, his granddaughter Melissa, and his son-inlaw Dr. Ronald Klein as well as his ex-wife Adrienne Krausz Friede, MD and her husband George Friede. He is remember with great affection by all his friends and relatives.
