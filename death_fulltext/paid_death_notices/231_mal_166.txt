
        KARCHES--Peter Frederick of Locust Valley, NY died on April 13, 2006 at the age of 54. Mr. Karches waged a valiant battle with chronic lymphocytic leukemia for several years. He loved his wife Susan of 30 years and his four children, Amy, Pamela, Peter Jr. and Nancy who adored him. He is the son of Clara and the late Lincoln Karches and brother to Robert. He was loved by many dear friends, brothers and sisters in-law and nieces and nephews. Peter attended Georgetown University and upon completion of his MBA at Columbia University, began his career at Morgan Stanley. He retired from Morgan Stanley in 2000 after 25 years with the firm where he was President and Chief Operating Officer of both Institutional Securities and Investment Banking. He served on the boards of Georgetown University, Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, Friends Academy and was Co-chairman of the New York Racing Association. Peter will be remembered for his leadership, generosity, quick wit and love for his family and friends. Peter was an avid golfer and horse racing enthusiast who will be missed by all those who knew and loved him. Visiting at the Beney Funeral Home, 79 Berry Hill Road, Syosset, NY Monday 2-5 and 7-10. Funeral Mass St. Dominic RC Church, Anstice Street, Oyster Bay Tuesday 10AM. Interment Locust Valley Cemetery. In lieu of flowers the family request donations to CLL Research Fund, Long Island Jewish Medical Center, New Hyde Park, NY 11040 (Attention Dr. Kanti Rai, MD).
