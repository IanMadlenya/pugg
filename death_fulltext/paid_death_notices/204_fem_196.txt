
        ABRAMS--Frances (Loeb), 90, devoted mother of Milton and Cary and their wives, Linda and Joan, passed away peacefully on January 19. Devoted grandmother of Max and Carly, predeceased by her beloved husband, Max. She was President of the Sisterhood of Congregation Emunath Israel, B'nai Brith and her sons' PTAs. Frances loved Broadway opening nights, travel and life in NYC. Services at Riverside Memorial, 180 West 76 St, Wednesday 11:15. Donations may be made to Lincoln House, 120 Hill St, Barre VT 05641
