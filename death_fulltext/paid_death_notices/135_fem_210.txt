
        FINCH-Emily Grant. Age 84. Died on April 25, 1998 in Green Valley, Arizona. after a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Philip Fiske Finch Jr. and loving mother of Philip III, Catherine (Kay), Christopher and Reynolds. She leaves nine grandchildren and four greatgrandchildren. She was a graduate of Elmira College and earned a MSW at Western Reserve University. She spent many years as a full time mother and was a social worker, both paid volunteer, specializing in children and families. Former resident of Elmira, New York, Reading, PA., Westport and Fairfield, CT. Services Friday, May 1, at 2PM, Green Valley Presbyterian Church. In lieu of flowers contributions to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
