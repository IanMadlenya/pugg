
        ROSENBERG-Lillian H. Died on August 9, 1998 at the age of 103. Remembered teacher of the IGC classes of PS 99, Brooklyn, New York. Beloved aunt of Joan Reisfeld, Renee Greer, great aunt of four, great great aunt of six. She led a happy, useful life and was loved by all. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. at 76th St., NYC, Wednesday, August 12 at 12:OO noon.
