
        WARNER-Marion. In Branford, CT, February 22, 2001. Wife of the late MacDonald S. Warner. Sister of Gertrude Ahrens Dyson and Eunice Pollard. Sister-in-law of Robert S. Warner. Aunt of many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by a brother, Richard Henry Ahrens Jr. She worked for the OSS in Washington, DC, in World War II and was an active member of the Society of Preservation of New England Antiquities, the National Trust for Historic Preservation and the University Club of New York. Friends may call Tuesday, 3-6 PM, at W.S. Clancy Memorial Funeral Home, 504 Main St., Branford, CT, www.wsclancy.com
