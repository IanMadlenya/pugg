
        HOFFMEISTER-Anne (Schaetzel), age 87, formerly of New York City, died on January 23, 1999 in Plymouth, NH. The widow of Theodore H.C. Hoffmeister. Sister of Helene Genzmer. Also survived by one nephew. She was the administrative assistant to the Dean of Students at Hunter College, until retirement in 1969. A private graveside service will be held in the New Hampshire Veterans Cemetery. Memorial donations maybe made to the Pemi Baker Home Health Agency, 258 Highland Street, Plymouth, NH 03264
