
        MILLER-Marjorie Cohn. Died Sunday, April 22. Beloved wife of the late Joseph F. Miller. Brother of Peter Arnold. Loving mother of Kenneth and Keith Miller and Katharine Burger. Grandmother of Alison, David, Daniel and Jeffrey Miller, Pamela Bonney, Ethan and Wyatt Sprague. Greatgrandmother of Craig Bonney Jr, Nicholas and Matthew Miller. Mother-in-law of Lenore Miller, Liz Baxter and Knox Burger. Lifelong resident of Deal, New Jersey, World War II Red Cross Motor Corps Driver, tireless hospital volunteer, avid golfer. Services Wednesday, April 25th, 11 AM, Temple Beth Miriam, Elberon New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, contributions to American Liver Foundation.
        MILLER-Marjorie. The Board of Govenors & members of Elberon Bathing Club mourn the passing of our dear friend, Marjorie Miller, one of the founders and long time member of Elberon Bathing Club. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Robert Fagenson, President Jane Guadagno, Secretary
