
        ARMSTRONG - John Kremer. Died of heart attack on April 5, 1997 at his home in Lawrence Park West, Bronxville, New York. He is survived by his wife, Mieke, his children Marca and Jeb, his brother Alan Armstrong and by his future son-in law Kevin Ewy. He was a partner at the New York law firm of Carter, Ledyard & Milburn. Services at 3PM, Thursday, April 10, 1997 at Christ Church, 17 Sagamore Road, Bronxville, NY.
        ARMSTRONG - John K. The Trustees, Auxiliary and staff of Brooklyn Botanic Garden have lost a good friend. Past Chairman of the Board and longtime member of the Garden family, John is suddenly and sorely missed. Our hearts go out to Mieke and the family. Lois Carswell, Board Chairman Judith Zuk, President Allan F. Kramer II, Auxiliary Pres.
