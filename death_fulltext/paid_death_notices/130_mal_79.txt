
        McLAUGHLIN-James P. Devoted husband, father, grandfather and philanthropist passed away peacefully in his home in San Antonio, Texas on Sunday morning, November 2, 1997. He is survived by his wife of nearly 55 years, Genevieve and seven children, Mary Caruso, Dorothy Petell, Jean McLaughlin, James, Robert P., Paul and Mark McLaughlin and 13 grandchildren. The funeral mass and burial will be held Friday, November 7, 1997 in Williamstown, Massachusetts. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to St. Peters/St. Joseph's Children's Home in San Antonio, TX through the office of the George M. Hopkins Funeral Home, 61-67 Spring Street, Williamstown, MA 01267.
