
        FEIN-George. Age 94, on March 13, 2002. He lived a full life and made the lives of those who knew him fuller. Beloved husband of the late Pearl Javer Fein. Survived by his loving wife, Rachel Glazer Fein; his devoted children, Barbara and Richard Kraft, Edward and Ronnie Fein; his dear stepchildren, Stanley and Richard Glazer; his adoring grandchildren, Courtney and David Levi, Matthew Kraft, Meredith and Gregory Lichtenberg, Gillian Fein and Russell and Sandra Glazer; and proud greatgrandfather of Parker and Chloe Levi, Zev Lichtenberg and Jacob, Samantha and Talia Glazer. Service Thursday, 12:45 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76 St and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NYC 10021.
