
        KLEIN--Harold, 92, peacefully on February 16, 2005. Survived by his most beloved wife Lillian of 66 years, his three children, Ellen Siroka Robinson, Jane W. Parver and Tony Klein, his son-in-law Don Robinson, his daughter-in-law Jennifer Boyer Klein and his three grandchildren Matthew Siroka, Jesse and Tim Klein. His life was meaningful, he lived it fully, and he will be missed by his loving family and many dear friends.
