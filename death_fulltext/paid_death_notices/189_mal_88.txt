
        WEISSMAN-Lester. Of Tenafly, NJ, passed away October 6, 2002, at the age of 80. He is survived by his loving wife of 52 years, Vivian, his sisters Blanche (Leif) and Martha of Queens, NY, and his son Robert of Sunnyvale, CA. A graduate of City College of New York, he was a successful businessman and owner of the retail stores Danforth Factory Outlet in Jersey City, NJ, and Danforth Wearhouse in Closter, NJ, until his retirement in 1984. A devoted patron of the arts, he loved the theater and museums of New York, fine literature, and music of all sorts. He was as admired for his wit and humor as for his business acumen. He was greatly loved and will be greatly missed. Private services to be held.
