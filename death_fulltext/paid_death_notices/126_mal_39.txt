
        MOHOVICH - Roger. Brother, uncle and dear friend, Roger died Fri., June 13, 1997 of AIDS-related lymphoma. A writer, historian and avid collector of early TwentiethCentury Americana, he had an abiding love for New York City. He will always be remembered and loved for his generosity, wit and singular sense of humor. Memorial service, St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue and 50th Street on Saturday, July 26, 3 PM. Donations to GMHC, 119 West 24 Street, New York, New York 10011.
