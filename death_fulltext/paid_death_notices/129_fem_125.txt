
        HOFMANN-Regina. On October 15, 1997 at the age of 85. Wife of the late Max Hofmann, M.D. Mother of Peter Hofmann, M.D. Services at the Hebrew Tabernacle, 551 Fort Washington Avenue, NYC, Sunday, October 19, 11 AM. She will be missed by her many friends and her family in whose hearts she will remain. Her wit, integrity and loving kindness endeared her to all who had the privilege of knowing her.
