
        KUSSOY--Eric George. Of Manhattan. Suddenly on June 25, 2005 at age 37. Beloved son of Doris and Paul Kussoy. Loving brother of Jayne and Steve Gordon, Andrew Kussoy and Patricia Kussoy who predeceased him last year. Devoted uncle of Rebecca and Rachel Gordon. Eric was a partner in the Labor Department at Kelley Drye focusing on labor and employment law with an emphasis on HR counseling, discrimination and affirmative action matters. He received his B.S. degree from the School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University and his J.D. from Emory University. He had a heart of gold and will be greatly missed by all those whose lives he touched with his kindness, generosity and love. For shiva information please call ''Gutterman's'' at 516-921-5757.
        KUSSOY--Eric G. The law firm of Kelley Drye & Warren LLP deeply mourns the loss of our partner and friend, Eric G. Kussoy, Esq. He died on Saturday, June 25, 2005, in New York City. He was 37 years old. Mr. Kussoy graduated from Cornell University -NYS School of Industrial and Labor Relations and received his law degree from Emory University. Mr. Kussoy originally joined Kelley Drye in 1995 and was elected to the Firm's partnership in 2004. His practice focused on labor and employment law. On behalf of Kelley Drye's attorneys and staff, we extend our profound condolences to Mr. Kussoy's family and friends. We will always remember him as a highly skilled attorney who was remarkable in his integrity and friendship to all he encountered.
