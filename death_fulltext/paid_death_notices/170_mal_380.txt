
        WHITE-John Hazen, Sr. The Metropolitan Opera Association mourns the death of our good friend, John Hazen White, Sr., a member of our official family for many years. His dedication to our company was invaluable, in particular, his commitment to special projects. We extend our condolences to his wife Happy and family. Mrs. Gilbert W. Humphrey, Chairman Emeritus Bruce Crawford, Honorary Chairman James W. Kinnear, Chairman Paul M. Montrone, President
