
        MARKER-Feroza. The staff, patients and patients' families of the Mount Sinai Hospital of New York City express their sadness and condolences to the family of Feroza Marker on her untimely death. Ms. Marker was employed as an occupational therapist in the Alzheimer's Disease Caregiver Program for the past four years. She will be remembered for her kindness, gentleness, caring and humorous nature with all people with whom she came in contact. She will be sorely missed.
