
        LASDON-Lawrence H. Age 58 died February 16, 2001, of a heart attack at his home in Palm Desert, CA. A former resident of New York he was a graduate of Florida Southern University and a former member of the NYSE. He is survived by a brother, Rand M. of New York and two sons, Lance and Chad of Los Angeles, CA. Services will be at Campbell's Funeral Chapel, Madison Avenue and 81st Street at 1 PM, Sunday.
