
        STERN-Roslyn B. On July 27, 2000 at age 88. Beloved wife of the late Irving, loving mother of Peter and his wife Joan, loving grandmother of Jeffrey & Amy & her husband David. She lives on in the hearts of all who knew her. Services at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave, NYC, 11:30 AM, Sunday, July 30, 2000.
        STERN - Roslyn. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Roslyn Stern. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
