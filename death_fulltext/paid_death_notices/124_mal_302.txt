
        COHEN-Nathaniel, M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni records with sorrow the passing of their longtime colleague and friend. Dr. Cohen joined the staff in 1956 and for over 30 years distinguished himself as teacher and clinician. Devoted to his patients as individuals whom he cared for with expertise and compassion and a conscientious mentor who touched the lives of countless medical students and residents, he was respected by his peers and loved by his patients. Dr. Cohen will be remembered as a dedicated physician, teacher and friend. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Wayne S. Fuchs, M.D., President Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary
        COHEN-Nathaniel. The Dr. Henry D. Janowitz Division of Gastroenterology, Mount Sinai Medical Center, mourns the passing of our friend and colleague, Dr. Nathaniel Cohen. Chief of our GI clinics for many years, he was a respected teacher to generations of students, role model for residents and fellows, and devoted care-giver to his patients. His medical research, his professional example, and above all his joy in practice, courage in adversity, and love of people will be his enduring legacies. David B. Sachar, M.D., Director, GI Division
