
        ALBERT-Irving, 80. Died suddenly on May 13, 2002 at home. Born in Poland, came to the United States in 1938. An honored union organizer and committed leftist. He served in the U.S. Armed Forces in the invasion of North Africa, landed in Anzio and marched to Rome. Supported many Jewish causes. And was an Alaskan entrepreneur. Husband of Alicia and the late Dorothy, father of Marc and David, father-in-law of Orna, adoring grandfather of Ben, and relentless competitor at pinochle. Service Sunday, May 19, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. The family will at home this week.
