
        LEVINE--Arthur B. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Arthur Levine, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation who was deeply devoted to the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to his beloved wife, Sybil, and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
