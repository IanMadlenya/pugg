
        ABRAMSON - Benjamin. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff at the Hospital for Joint Diseases are deeply saddened by the loss of Benjamin Abramson, devoted father of our good friend and colleague, Dr. Steven B. Abramson, Chairman of the Department of Rheumatology and Medicine at the Hospital for Joint Diseases. Dr. Abramson is a longstanding member of our medical staff, who has served with distinction for many years. Benjamin Abramson leaves a grieving family and many friends who will remember him for his kindness and devotion to his family. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, Miriam, their children, Steven, Lewis and Susan Nevins, their grandchildren and to the entire family. William A. Perlmuth Chairman of the Board John N. Kastanis, FACHE President and CEO Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief
