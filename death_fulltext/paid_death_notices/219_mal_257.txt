
        TAUBMAN--Joseph L., age 81, died April 21st, 2005. Devoted husband of Sylvia of Boynton Beach, FL, beloved father of Edward and Paul J., cherished father-in-law of Nancy, adored grandfather of Michele and Brendan, loved brother of Isaac and dear uncle of his many nieces and nephews. A veteran of W.W. II who served in Europe, he went on to become a highly respected and accomplished CPA. To us, he was our patriarch, who proudly passed on his Jewish heritage. He was a man whom we all loved and admired, respected and treasured. He was, is and will always be our hero. A private service was held. Contributions can be made to the American Cancer Society.
