
        COOPER-Eli. Beloved husband of Harriet, Loving father of Douglas and Neil, Dear brother of Isadore, Elaine Roth and the late Samuel and Nathan. Cherished grandfather, uncle, great-uncle and cousin. WWII vet, Trustee of the Jewish Museum, Member of the Board of Directors of the Committee for Weizmann Institute of Science, Member of the Executive Committee of Shvut Ami, Member of the Board of Directors of Baron de Hirsch Fund. Contributions in his memory may be made to American Friends of the Shvut Ami, 14037 69th Road, Flushing, New York 11367 Attn: Rafi Gruber. Services were held on Thursday at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Forest Hills, New York.
