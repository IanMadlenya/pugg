
        DOHERTY-Thomas Charles. On August 4, 2000 in the Bronx, NY. A memorial service will be held Saturday, October 14, 11 AM at St. Philip Neri RC Church, 201st Street and the Grand Concourse. Mr. Doherty was a staunch advocate for housing for disabled senior citizens. He leaves two sons, Thomas and Michael, two daughters, Patricia Chase and Joan Breakell; his devoted companion of many years Lillian Allen; nine grandchildren; one great grandson, numerous nieces and nephews; and two sisters, Beatrice O'Connor and Geraldine Lavan. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Disabled American Veterans, POB 14301, Cincinnati, OH. 45250.
