
        JEMMOTT--Marion E. The trustees of the Taraknath Das Foundation remember with gratitude the devoted service to the foundation over more than 4 decades of Marion Jemmott who died at 80 on April 30th. She gave her energy and acute intelligence to helping South Asian Students, to aiding women in their quest for equality and fulfillment, and to making Columbia University a finer place. Her family asks that contributions in her name be made to the Foundation, care of Southern Asian Institute, Columbia University, New York, New York 10027.
