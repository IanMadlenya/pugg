
        ALGOZZINE-Jane Taylor Barrett, 78, Of Katonah, N.Y., formerly of NYC, Albany and Cairo, NY. Died Jan. 28, 1998 after a long illness of a rare brain disorder. Jane spent 25 years teaching in upstate NY schools before joining the NYS Education Department in 1965. She became the Bureau Chief of Reading Education in 1967 and the Director of Language Skills in 1983. Jane was also the Director of the State Right to Read Programs. She is survived by her son John R. Algozzine of Katonah, NY and her daughter Linda Starr of Walden, CO., 3 granddaughters and six great granddaughters. She will be missed greatly. Donations may be made to the Bachmann-Strauss Dystonia & Parkinson Foundation at the Mt. Sinai Medical Center, 1 Gustave Levy Pl, Box 1490, NYC, 10029. A memorial gathering of family and friends will be held at the University Club at 141 Washington Avenue, Albany, NY, Saturday, February 14, 1998 at 1:00 P.M.
