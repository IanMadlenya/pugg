
        SCHULMAN--Elliot. 54, of Los Angeles, CA, December 10, after a courageous battle with lung cancer. Beloved brother of Phyllis Kessler and her husband Carl. Adored uncle of Susan and Elizabeth. He had the love of many relatives, friends and colleagues to whom he was devoted. His sense of humor, artistic flair and culinary talent will be missed by all. A memorial service was held at his home on December 12.
