
        HELPERN-Irving E. Age 90, on January 12. Beloved husband of Leona for 60 special years. Loving and proud father of David, Anne Bookstein, and Robert, and of their spouses Joan, Herbert Bookstein, and Jacqueline. Adored grandfather of Lauren; Peter, Scott and his wife Desiree, Jeffrey, and Meredythe Bookstein; and Michael and Joshua. Cherished great-grandfather of Ethan Bookstein. Devoted brother of the late Dr. Gottlieb Helpern. Funeral services today at BoulevardRiverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, at 9:30 A.M. His caring heart finally gave way. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to the American Heart Association or UJA-Federation of New York would be appreciated.
        HELPERN-Irving. The partners, associates and staff of Newman Tannenbaum Helpern Syracuse & Hirschtritt are saddened by the passing of Irving Helpern, the father of our partner Bob Helpern. Irving was a devoted husband, father and grandfather. His love and dedication to his children was marked by his constant concern for the growth and success of their personal and professional lives. We are grateful for the support Irving gave our law firm from its inception which contributed to our growth and success. Our heartfelt sympathies to his wife Leona, his children Bob and Jacqueline, David and Joan, Ann and Herb, and all of his loving grandchildren. Newman Tannenbaum Helpern Syracuse & Hirschtritt
        HELPERN-Irving. The Seawane Club records with sorrow the loss of our beloved member, Irving Helpern. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his wife Leona and the family. Robert Roman, President
