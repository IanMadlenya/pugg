
        WATTLES--Gurdon Weller. 102, died on November 5, 2004, at his residence in Far Hills, NJ. Beloved husband of Elizabeth ''Betty'' Bayne and subsequently to Anita ''Peter'' Bradshaw, both of whom he survived. His son James Howard Wattles died in 1947. His son, Gurdon Bayne Wattles and his wife Kathleen of Little Compton, RI, survive him. Three grandchildren: Elizabeth ''Wendy'' Wilkes, Gurdon Stanton Wattles and Alexander Bayne Wattles. Eight great-grandchildren. In addition, four stepchildren and their descedants survive him. A private family graveside service will be held on November 10, 2004, at 1 PM, at New Vernon Cemetery. A Memorial Service will be held on Saturday, November 20, at 11 AM, at the Lamington Presbyterian Church in Lamington, NJ. For more information, contact Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home 101 S. Finley Avenue, Basking Ridge, NJ.
