
        BORNEMANN-Hellen. The people of Saint Peter's Lutheran Church are deeply saddened by the untimely death of our beloved friend and co-worker Hellen Bornemann this past Thursday morning, May 1. Hellen is survived by her son, Paul, her daughter-in-law, Heather, her grandsons, Zachary and Alexander, and of course, the family of Saint Peter's. A Memorial Eucharist and Inurnment in thanksgiving for Hellen's life and gifts will be held on Saturday, May 10, at 6:00 P.M. with a reception to follow. Donations may be made to Saint Peter's Church in loving memory of Hellen.
