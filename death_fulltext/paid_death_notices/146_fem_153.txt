
        GOLDFRANK-Brenda. Of Manhasset, NY, died on March 11, 1999. She was predeceased by her loving husband Richard and son Bruce. She is survived by her daughter Diane Richards and her grandson David Goldfrank. Mrs. Goldfrank was born in 1915 in Northampton, MA, and attended Colby-Sawyer College. During her life she and her husband divided their time between their homes in Manhasset and Tangiers, Morocco. Donations may be made to the Humane Society of the United States (HSUS).
