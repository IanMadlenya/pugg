
        RUEBEN--Stanley. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Stanley Rueben, treasured friend and colleague, who selflessly served as Director of Administrative Services until his retirement in 1989. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Jeri; to his children Leonard and Karen; and to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
