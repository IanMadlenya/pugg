
        TUCKER-Frank Philip. Died peacefully at home on December 9, 1999 at age 92. Mechanical Engineer and Industrialist who founded Estey Metal Products, the largest manufacturer of library shelving in the world. He leaves his wife of 65 years, Helen Crater, children Frank Philip, Jr., and Joan Cox, seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Private interment services are for the immediate family in Bernardsville, N.J.
