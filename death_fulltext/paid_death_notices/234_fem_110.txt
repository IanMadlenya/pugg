
        REILLY--Jessie (nee Rothouse) at the age of 100 on July 16, 2006. Loving wife of the late Harry. Survived by her longtime cherished friend, Joan O'Connor ''the daughter she never had.'' She is also survived by a niece, Paulene Goldberg, nephews Herb Orlan, Ted Ronick and Mel Ronick; 13 great nieces and nephews, 25 great-great nieces and nephews, and nine great-great-great nieces and nephews. Her love, warmth, compassion, and generosity will be sorely missed. She was an independent woman of great strength, character and intelligence. Jessie spent her entire career which spanned 70 years at the Marx Realty and Improvement Company where she was the office manager, secretary treasurer, and close associate of the late Leonard Marx. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to The Delaware Hospice, 600 N. Dupont Highway, Suite 107, Georgetown, DE. 11947.
