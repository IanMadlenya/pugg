
        HERZ-Vivian D. Of Lauderhill, Florida, died peacefully at age 90 on Tuesday, December 3, 2002 after a very active, rich and rewarding life. Beloved wife of the late Alfred Herz. She is survived by her longtime companion Patrick Fiorillo; her niece and nephew Roger and Dale Winston; and great-nieces and nephews Peter and Michael Winston, Christopher, Marnie and Melissa Danson. Funeral services will be private; memorial services will be announced at a later date.
