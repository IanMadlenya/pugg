
        FERNANDEZ-Augustin. On October 19. Born in Fontan, Spain. Father of John Paul. Gus was a man who marched to the beat of a different drummer. He loved life, a good meal and good wine. His spirit was drawn to the sea. He lived in Belle Harbor, Queens where the ocean was at his door. A celebration of life will be held on Wednesday, October 23, Casa Galicia, 3709 31 Ave, Astoria, NY, 6-9. Bring a picture, a memory and a story. Dress accordingly, turtleneck sweaters required. Contributions to Calvary Hospital Fund, Tree of Life, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
