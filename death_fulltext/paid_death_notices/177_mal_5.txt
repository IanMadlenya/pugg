
        JELINEK-Hugo. Born 1901, in Austro-Hungary. Immigrated to the United States on October 12, 1939. He was the founder of H. Jelinek Dental Alloys, Inc. Beloved husband of the late Frances. Devoted father of Ruth Schapira and Judith Gerstl. Adored grandfather of Ralph and Paul Schapira, and Jonathan and Janis Gerstl. Loving great-grandfather. Graveside service was held.
