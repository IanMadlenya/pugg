        LUSTGARTEN-Ira. The Officers and Board of Trustees of The Arnold P. Gold Foundation expresses condolences to the family and friends of our treasured Board Member, Ira Lustgarten. Arnold P. Gold, M.D., President Myron Rosner, Esq., Secretary
