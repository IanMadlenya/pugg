
        SCHEINBACH--Seymour C., DDS, on February 18, of Royal Palm Beach, FL. Beloved husband of the late Roslyn. Cherished father of Myrna, Esta Joy, Jayne and Deborah. Adored grandfather of Jennifer, Rachel and Jessica. Blessed great-grandfather of Zachary and Jacob. Dear brother of the late Milton. Dr. Scheinbach served as a captain in the US Army during WW II and was a 1941 graduate of the NYU School of Dentistry who practiced in New York until his retirement to Florida in 1987. Funeral services will be held at ''Gutterman's,'' 175 N. Long Beach Rd., Rockville Centre, NY, at 11 AM, Sunday, February 22.
