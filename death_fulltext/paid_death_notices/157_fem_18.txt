
        WELCH-Mildred E. (nee) Silver. Died January 31, 2000. Beloved wife of James for 57 years, loving mother of Janet, Lauren and John, cherished grandmother of James Jan. She was born in Boston, MA (West End), March 29, 1916, where she spent the happiest years of her childhood. She married her husband, James, in 1942 and following World War II moved to New York City. Along with her husband, James, a gifted trombonist, they enjoyed Manhattan life, especially the finest pastry shops the city has to offer. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue (81 St), Saturday, February 5, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. She will be laid to rest near her beloved mother and sister, Eva, on Sunday, February 6 at Sharon Memorial Park, Sharon, MA. (781-828-7216)
