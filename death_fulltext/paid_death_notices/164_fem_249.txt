
        STERNBERG-Ursula (nee Hertz). Of Chestnut Hill-Philadelphia, Pa., on Friday, September 22, 2000. Born in Germany, Mrs. Sternberg was a well respected painter who exhibited extensively throughout Europe and the U.S. Surviving are her husband of 43 years, symphony conductor Jonathan Sternberg, daughter Tanya Pushkine Rojas of New York, grandchildren Luca and Lara-Sophia Rojas, son Peter Raphael Sternberg of Paris and sister Renee Haferkamp of Brussels. Private celebrations of her life will be held in Philadelphia and Paris.
