
        SCHWARTZ-Rose A. Passed away February 6, 1998, in West Palm Beach, FL. Native of New Haven, CT. Devoted wife of the late Jack Schwartz. Loving mother of Joseph B. Schwartz (Marilyn) of Manhattan and Judith Merriam of Madison, CT. Caring grandmother of Jill and Jeffrey Merriam. Services Monday in New Haven. She was immensely proud of her family and her many sacrifices on their behalf will always be remembered. Donations may be made to the Schwartz/ Goldstein Lecture Fund, Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, 30 W. 68th Street, NY, NY 10023.
