
        MALLORY-Stephen, 74. Of Middleburg, VA., and longtime resident of NYC, died February 27, 2003. He was born in Boston. After serving in the Army during WWII, he graduated from Parsons School of Design in NYC. He created with James Tillis the renowned design firm of Mallory Tillis, which later became Stephen Mallory Associates. His furniture line, known as Stephen Mallory Preferred, was highly respected and ahead of its time. He will be remembered by all who knew him for his great style and inspiration. He lived many years in France and recently returned to the States with his wife, Jennifer. He is survived by two children, Christopher and Brooke; and three grandchildren, Annabelle, Reilly and Kate. A memorial service will be held at 10 A.M. on March 19th at Christ Church of Georgetown in Washington, D.C.
