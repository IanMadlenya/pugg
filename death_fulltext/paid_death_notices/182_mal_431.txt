
        LEVIN-David K. Devoted husband to his late, beloved wife Dorothy. Loving and devoted father to his children Stuart (Pam), Kenneth and Wendy. Loving brother to his sister Sarah (Al),and loving uncle to all his nephews and nieces. A WWII veteran, a talented teacher (Lafayette High School), artist, sculptor, musician, engineer and religious scholar. His guiding principals were truth, integrity and kindness. His unwavering faith drove him on to maintain the most precious gift of life. He will be missed by all who knew him. Gifts can be sent to Congregation Israel of Kings Bay, 3903 Nostrand Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11235.
