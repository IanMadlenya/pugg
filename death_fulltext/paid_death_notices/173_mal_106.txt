
        GIBSON-Harold Dean. Age 74, on June 10, 2001. Beloved husband of Patricia. Loving father of Patrick & Leslie and their spouses Bonnie and Edward Maluf. Adoring grandfather of Celia, Nicholas, Sofia and Eric. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 PM with service Friday 9:30 AM.
