
        MORRONE-Joseph P. On October 26, 2001. Loving son of Lee and Joe. His whole world was his loving mother. Joseph leaves behind his loving sister Dr. Lee Morrone, brother-in-law Joe Fierro and his special niece and Godson Allegra and Joseph Jr. Joseph worked for the Office of US Customs for 18 years and recently escaped the tragedy of the World Trade Center. Funeral Mass Monday at 11 AM, Regina Pacis Shrine. Reposing at Ralph Aievoli and Son Funeral Home, 1275 65th St., corner of 13th Ave., Brooklyn, Sunday, 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 PM.
