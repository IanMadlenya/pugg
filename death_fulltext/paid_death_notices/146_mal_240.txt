
        OSWALD-Frank Graf III. Killed in an automobile accident on March 7 in Amado, Arizona. He is survived by his wife Laurinda Middleton Oswald; his two children Frank Graf and Julia Skye; his mother Audrey Clinton Oswald of Newport; his father Frank Graf Oswald Jr. of Port Washington; his brother David of Stamford; and his sister Diana of New York City. Memorial Services in Newport and New York City to be announced. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Newport Boys and Girls Club would be appreciated.
