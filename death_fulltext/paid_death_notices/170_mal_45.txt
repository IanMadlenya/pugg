
        COWEN-David, M.D., 93. On March 5, after a short illness. Professor Emeritus of the Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons, the Presbyterian Hospital, and was associated with both Lenox Hill Hospital and the New Jersey Veterans Hospital. He was an internationally known neuropathologist and published over seventy papers during his 50-year career. He was President of the American Association of Neuropathologists and received an award in 1979 for his dedication and contributions to neuropathology. A kind, gentle, and quietly humorous man, he will be missed by all who knew him. He is survived by his nephew Robert Cowen, Jr., and seven grand nephews and nieces. Services will be private.
