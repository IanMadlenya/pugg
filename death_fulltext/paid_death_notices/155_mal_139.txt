
        ZOPPEL-Ronald, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with profound sorrow the death of Dr. Ronald Zoppel. Dr. Zoppel was with the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for over thirty years. He was very active in the leadership of the medical staff and served as its President. He was loved and admired by all who knew him and always had a smile for everyone. He will be sorely missed by all his colleagues, patients and friends. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. David Feldman, M.D., Pres. Salvatore DeGliuomini M.D. President Elect Steven Farber, M.D., Sec'y Herbert Lehman, M.D. Treasurer
