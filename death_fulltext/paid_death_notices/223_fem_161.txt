
        WEISSBERG - Snooks (Fe bruary 5, 1920 to August 25, 2005). Loving wife of Ned Weissberg for 58 years. Snooks (nee Augusta Reitman) was a mother of three. She was a fiercely loyal wife, mother and friend, possessing a feisty zest for life and a singular sense of humor. Snooks died peacefully at home and is survived by her husband Ned & three children, Kenny, Ellen Gould and Roger. She is also survived by five grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
