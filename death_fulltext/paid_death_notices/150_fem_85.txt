
        WEEKES-Carol Thatcher, 60, died July 5 in Richmond, NH. Beloved wife of Robert and devoted mother of John (of Bloomington, IL), Jeffrey (of Stroudsburg, PA), Elizabeth (Mrs. Benjamin Kunz of Hamden, CT), Jennifer (Mrs. Daniel Koprowski of Amherst, MA) and Julia (Mrs. Joseph Spuria of Auburn, NH). She also leaves eight adoring grandchildren and a sister, Mary Louise Tate (Rye Beach, NH). Carol attended Vassar College and lived for many years in Garden City, NY where, while raising her loving family, she was employed by the Cathedral of the Incarnation. For the past 10 years, she lived with her husband of 41 years at Fox Call Farm in Richmond, NH. During this time, she served on the Vestry and Altar Guild of St. James Episcopal Church in Keene, NH, and the Board of the Fitzwilliam Garden Club, and volunteered at Applewood Nursing Home. In recognition of a life time dedicated to charitable causes, she was designated a Community Hero by the U.S. Olympic Committee in 1996 and carried the Olympic Torch as it passed through Keene on its way to the Summer Games in Atlanta. Carol was also the leader of the Monadnock Parkinson Support Group, an organization she founded in 1997. A memorial service will be held at St. James Episcopal Church, in Keene, NH, on Monday, July 12, at 10:30 AM. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to the American Parkinson Disease Association, 1250 Hylan Boulevard, Staten Island, New York 10305.
