
        LADENSON--Sam, April 5, 1927-March 2, 2004, in New York City. A prolific artist who worked extensively in collage for many years. He attended the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts and later studied at the Ecole des Beaux Arts while stationed in Paris as a Master Sergeant in the Army. His work is in private and public collections in the United States and Europe. He leaves his wife, Irene, and daughter, Elisabeth.
