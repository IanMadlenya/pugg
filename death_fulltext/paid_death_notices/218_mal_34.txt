
        KAPOR--Jesse. On February 28, 2005. He is survived by his beloved wife, Phoebe, his brothers, David and Leo, his daughter, and son-in-law, Gabrielle and Rabbi Steven Tucker, his son, Mitchell Kapor, and daughter-in-law Freada Kapor Klein, and his grandchildren, Samuel, Jonathan, and Ezra Tucker, and Adam and Molly Kapor. He graduated as a civil engineer from The City College of New York in 1942, served his country in World War II as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy, and subsequently founded and ran a successful business in the corrugated container industry for over forty years. He will be remembered for his devotion to his family, his love of the outdoors and his many acts of kindness. Contributions may be made to: Temple Ramat Zion, 17655 Devonshire Street, Northridge, CA 91325, or to: The City College Fund, 160 Convent Avenue, Shepard Hall, Room 166, NY, NY 10031.
