
        SHAPIRO-Marion. A strong and beautiful woman, died on May 14, at the age of 85, in San Francisco. Formerly from New York City, Port Washington, and Delray Beach. Loving daughter of Yetta and Nathan Britt. Beloved wife of Nat Shapiro. She is loved and missed by her children, Richard and Judith, their spouses Nancy and David; and her granchildren in San Francisco, Summer and Jeremy, and in Israel, Yael, Shaul, Liron and Netta. Services at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont Road, Elmont, NY, at 11 AM, Tuesday, May 19. The family may be visited or contacted at the Garden City Hotel, 45 7th St., Garden City, NY, 516-747-3000.
