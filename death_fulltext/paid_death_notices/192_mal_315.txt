
        BLITZER-Arnold. died on January 23, 2003 at the age of 93. Beloved husband of the late Florence Kaiden and the late Lillian Aurit. A proud Colonel in the U.S. Army, he also served his city for many years in the Planning Commission. He will be profoundly missed and is survived by his sister Rose, brothers Victor and Leon, sister-in-law Pauline and many loving nieces and nephews. Service 11 am Monday, January 27th at Sutton Place Synagogue, N.Y.C.
        BLITZER-Col. Arnold. Sutton Place Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed benefactor and former long time member of the Board. Husband of the late Florence. Our heartfelt condolences to the entire bereaved family. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
