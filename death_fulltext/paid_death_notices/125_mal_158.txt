
        MILLS-Harold Mark, Jr., on June 11, 1997, of Forest Hills, New York. Devoted son of Harold M. Mills, Sr. Beloved brother of Nancy L. Fardelmann and Margaret M. Michael. Cherished uncle of Robert M. Becker, William N. Becker, John O. Becker, and Alexandra M. Michael, and also two grandnieces and two grand-nephews. Reposing at Fox Funeral Home, 98-07 Ascan Avenue, Forest Hills, New York, Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral Service Monday, 10 A.M. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery. His courage and determination to live life to the fullest in the face of difficult illnesses inspired and moved us all. He will forever be in our hearts and memories.
