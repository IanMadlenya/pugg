
        LIPTON-Tess, on January 24, 1997. Resident of Las Vegas, Nevada. Formerly of New York City, Shrub Oak Park, Century Village, Deerfield Beach, Florida. Beloved wife of the late Koppel Lipton. Loving mother to Howard, Paul and Daughter-in-law Elaine. Missing her will be grandchildren: Ross, Jeffrey and his wife Randi. Nieces and nephews Barbara Leonard, Marilyn and Ike Hazan. Edward and Chris Gitelson, Paul and Nancy Gitelson. Sister-in-law Bertha Givertzman and other cousins and their children. She was quick with a smile, generous and a very caring member of her family. She leaves a painful void, but will always be remembered with love, admiration and respect. Services were held Sunday, January 26, 1997, Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue, NYC. Contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
