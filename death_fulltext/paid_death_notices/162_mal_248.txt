
        TEVLIN-Irving, 87. Beloved husband of Rose and loving and devoted father of Susan and Helen. Passed away on July 23 at The Glen Arden Health Care Center in Goshen, NY. He formerly lived in Mount Vernon, NY for 45 years where he was active in the Jewish Community. Funeral services wil be held at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY, on Tuesday, July 25, at 12PM. The family requests any donations be sent to The Zuckerman Cultural Fund of the Workmen's Circle, 45 East 33rd Street, New York, New York 10016.
