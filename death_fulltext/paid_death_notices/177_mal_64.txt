
        SMITH-Hardy William. On October 3, 2001. Beloved friend of the late Peter Glenville. Theirs was a partnership radiant with the warmth of their associations, the creativity of their love for life and the distinction of their achievements together in theatre and film. Adored uncle of Holly Book and Meridel Jellifer. Loving great uncle of and inspiration to Asher Book and his other nieces and nephew. There were none more loyal and generous, kind and true than Hardy. He will be ardently missed. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Thursday, 2-4PM and Friday 2-4 and 68PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10:30AM at Saint Vincent Ferrer, Lexington Ave at 66th St.
