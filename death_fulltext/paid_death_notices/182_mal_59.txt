
        ROSENBERG-David M. Of New York City, on Monday, February 25, 2002, at age 45. Beloved son of Beatrice and Alex, devoted brother of Ted, esteemed editor at The Wall Street Journal and The New York Times. His positions included assistant foreign editor and deputy national editor of The Wall Street Journal, managing editor of The Asian Journal, managing editor of The Wall Street Journal Television News, and business-news assignment editor at The New York Times. His dedication to the highest standards of journalism was a source of inspiration to his colleagues. Memorial service at Riverside Chapel on Sunday, March 10, at 3:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to The National Brain Tumor Foundation, 414 13th Street, Suite 700, Oakland, CA 94612-2603.
