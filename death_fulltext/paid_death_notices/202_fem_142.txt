
        KELLY--Billie Ann Levine. On November 14, 2003. The partners, counsel, associates and staff of Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP are deeply saddened by the sudden and tragic loss of our beloved friend and colleague, Billie Ann Levine Kelly. Billie's career at our firm began on July 9, 1979. As Director of Legal Personnel and Recruiting, her remarkable commitment, passion, dedication and spirit during the past 25 years touched countless people throughout their careers at our firm, and she has been a true and valued mentor, friend and member of the Willkie family. Our deepest sympathies are sent to her beloved husband and our friend, Brian Kelly, to her two sons, Daniel and James, her step-daughter Elizabeth, and to the entire Kelly and Levine families. Billie will be remembered with love and deep respect. She will always remain in our hearts and thoughts. On behalf of Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP
