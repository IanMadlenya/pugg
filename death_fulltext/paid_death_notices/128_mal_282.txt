
        KAHAN-Joshua. On September 17, 1997, 2 year old Joshua Kahan lost his courageous battle against leukemia. A fund has been established to help find a cure for Acute Myelocytic Leukemia (AML). If you wish to help, Jeff and Michele request that you contribute to the battle against childhood cancers. Please send checks payable to THE JOSHUA KAHAN FOUNDATION, Inc., 46 Downing Lane, Voorhees, NJ 08043
