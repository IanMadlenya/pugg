
        KASHINS-Geraldine H. Died Thursday, May 15th at age 101. A woman ahead of her time, Ms. Kashins was a retired businesswoman who worked for Proctor & Gamble and R.J. Reynolds. A talented tarot card reader, who predicted the future. Born in NYC, she traveled the world and delighted friends and family with stories. Survived by nieces, nephews and friends. She will remain an inspiration to all.
