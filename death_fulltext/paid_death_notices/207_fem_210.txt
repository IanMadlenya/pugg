
        ROSEMAN--Reta (nee Mills), 85, of Yonkers, N.Y., Narragansett, R.I. and Ft. Lauderdale, FL, died April 24, 2004. She was the beloved wife of Lou Roseman, proud mother of Gael Roseman Sutter and Dr. Bruce Roseman, adored grandmother of Justin and Lauren, Michele and Daniel and brothers Irving and Albert Mills. A woman of valor, a true matriarch, Reta will be in our hearts and minds forever. Donations may be made to Leukemia Society of America and Chai Lifeline.
