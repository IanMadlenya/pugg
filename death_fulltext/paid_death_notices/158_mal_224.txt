
        SILVERSTEIN-Philip I. On March 17, 2000, at the age of 88. Beloved husband of the late Esther. Dearly loved father of Harriet and Dr. Jerome Seiler, Jerome and Judyth Silverstein, Marsha Mirsky and Stanley Silverstein. Cherished grandfather of Drs. Sharon Seiler and Charles Spielholz, Cindy and Ronald Albert, Jr., Drs. Stephanie Seiler and Robert Welikson, Jonathan Mirsky and Justin Silverstein. Dear great-grandfather of Erin and Jason Albert and Talia Spielholz. Beloved brother and brother-in-law of Murray and Yetta Selby, Milton and Rose Held, Victor and Marilyn Silverstein and Rebecca Knaster. Cherished uncle and great-uncle. He was an inspiration to all. He will be sorely missed. Service Sunday, March 19, 11:30 AM, I.J. Morris, Flatbush, Ave, Brooklyn, NY.
        SILVERSTEIN-Philip I. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the father of its devoted member Stanley Silverstein. May his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
