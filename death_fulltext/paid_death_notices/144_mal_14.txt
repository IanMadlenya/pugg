
        PERLIN-Marshall (Mike). The family of Loretta Rowe-Perlin mourns the loss and celebrates the life of Loretta's beloved husband, Mike. they were deeply devoted to each other for more than 20 years. Mike was the cherished son-in-law of Lillian Rowe-Lynch, trusted friend and brother-in-law of Kathleen Rowe and proud uncle of Bernie, Robert, Thomas, Virginia and Peter Rowe-Eck. The love and joy he expressed in caring for Loretta was an inspiration to us all. He will live on in our hearts.
