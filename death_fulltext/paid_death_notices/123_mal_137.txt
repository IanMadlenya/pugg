
        HECKSCHER-August. The members of The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art are deeply saddened by the death of their beloved member, August Heckscher. Distinguished twice Chairman of the Council, which he joined in 1958, he was greatly revered. We shall miss his friendship and wisdom. Our profound sympathy is extended to his wife Claude and to his sons Steven, Philip and Charles. Sir Brian Urquhart, Chairman Jo Carole Lauder, President
