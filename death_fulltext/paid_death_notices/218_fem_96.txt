
        OLEET--Mildred. Of Mount Vernon, NY, age 82, passed away Wednesday March 9, 2005. Widow of Harold K. Oleet, survived by her devoted children, Ronald and Eileen Oleet, of Hartsdale, NY, Nancy and Albert Meckel, of Stamford, CT, and Karen Oleet, of Miami Beach, FL., and grandchildren, Jason, Lauren, and Joanna Oleet. Also survived by her beloved sisters and brother, Hilda Grossman, Bede and husband Robert Manister, and Dr. Howard Matrick, as well as her many nieces and nephews. Funeral services at 1 P.M. Friday March 11th at Sinai Free Synagogue, 550 North Columbus Avenue, Mount Vernon, NY. Interment at Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. For information Riverside Memorial Chapels, Mt Vernon, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The MVH Development Fund-Restricted to Zev Carrey, M.D., North 10th Avenue, Mount Vernon, NY 10550.
