
        KAPLAN-Samuel S. on December 23, 1997, aged 84 in Lauderhill, Fla. Loving husband of the late Naomi (Alter), devoted father of Rick (Diane Gocs) and Jaime (Michael Grubin) and grandfather of Ben, Sarah and Andrew. Dear brother of Julia, Annette, Gertrude and the late Jack. His deep philosophical beliefs determined his actions always. No formal services will be held. Donations in his memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
