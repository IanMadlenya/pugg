
        FREIBERG-Sylvia. July 15, 1911 to October 20, 1998. Stronger than she realized, Sylvia lived almost three years after the death of her beloved husband, Sam, bringing much joy (and much laughter) to her family and friends. Now her ashes lie next to his beneath cherry and crabapple trees planted by their children, sons-in-law and grandchildren. How we miss them both. The Family
