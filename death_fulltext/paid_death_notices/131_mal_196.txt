
        OAKMAN-Carl, MD. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Carl Oakman, MD, a longtime attending physician and past President of the Medical Board. He was a beloved and revered colleague to a generation of surgeons who are able to appreciate and learn from his extraordinary devotion to patient care and teaching. He exemplified our finest traditions of medical excellence and will be sorely missed. To his wife Arlene and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Ronald C. Ablow, MD President Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
