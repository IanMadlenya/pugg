
        MAZOR--David M., M.D. On October 10, 2005. Beloved husband of Helen. Cherished father of Eric (Cara Lee, fiancee) and Melissa (Ronald). Services will be held at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, October 12, at 10 AM. Donations may be made to the National Kidney Foundation.
        $(6$)MAZOR--David, M.D. The Long Island Jewish Medical Center Staff Society mourns the loss of our colleague, Dr. David Mazor. Dr. Mazor was a highly respected member of the medical staff for over 30 years. Dr. Mazor began his career at LIJ as an intern in 1965. He continued his career at Long Island Jewish Hospital as a member of the active staff. He distinguished himself as a superb surgeon and then as a critical member of the Department of Quality Management. His wise counsel and leadership will be missed. We offer condolences to his family and thank them for sharing him with us over these many years. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, October 12, at Temple Beth-El of Great Neck. Randall S.
