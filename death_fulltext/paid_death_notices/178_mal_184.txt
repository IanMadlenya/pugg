
        KOENIG-Lawrence Jerome, 79. Passed away on November 11, 2001. Loving husband of Hannah for 57 years, adored father of Susan & husband Daniel Dengate, Bruce Koening and cherished grandfather of Raegan Koenig, Craig and Bradley Martin. He was a devoted husband, father, grandfather and friend to all he met. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him. Gone from our sight but never from our hearts, may he rest with all the souls in Zion.
