
        BLOCK-Morton H. Husband of Audrey Ossip Block. Brother of Leila Berkowitz. Father of Ellen Frommer. Died August 3, 2001. Graveside services Monday at Mt. Ararat, Farmingdale, L.I., 631957-2277
        BLOCK-Morton. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Morton Block on August 3, 2001. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Private services will be held on Monday, August 6, 2001. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
