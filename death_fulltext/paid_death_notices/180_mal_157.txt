
        FOGEL-Edward. The American Conference of Cantors records with deep sorrow the untimely passing of Cantor Edward Fogel, President of the Conference from 1989 through 1991. We will miss his beautiful voice, his sense of humor and his warm spirit. The Conference sends it deepest condolences to his family. Cantor Richard Cohn, President Cantor Scott E. Colbert, Exec V.P.
