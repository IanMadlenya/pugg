
        NOVEMBER-Josephine, 94 years, of Great Neck, died on June 12, 2000. Widow of William. Mother of Robert and the late Peter and mother-in-law of Barbara and Eleanor. Grandmother of Michael (Debbie), Ellen (Peter) Rigby, Peter and David and great grandmother of Adam, Pauline and Hannah. Community leader and advocate - United Community Fund, Visiting Nurses Association and Open Door. Tireless worker, Great Neck Library. Devoted friend and colleague. Memorial services 12 noon Sunday, June 18 at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Visiting hours Saturday 8-10pm. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to the United Community Fund of Great Neck or the Great Neck Library.
