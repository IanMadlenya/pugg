
        GLASS-Leslie. Leslie Glass, who was a popular Penthouse Pet, lost her valiant struggle against cancer on August 5, 2000. Her beauty left us breathless and her dedication to helping abused and homeless animals enriched us all. Bob Guccione, the publisher of Penthouse Magazine, and the entire General Media family extend their sincerest condolences to her family and friends. In lieu of flowers, donations in Leslie's name can be made to the Kathy Keeton Foundation in support of cancer research or Pets for Pets, an organzation Leslie formed to help abused and homeless animals. For more information, please contact General Media at 212-702-6000 ext. 1465.
