
        COLIE-Elizabeth S. Of Haverford, PA, formerly of South Orange, NJ, on March 9, 1998, at the age of 84 after a short illness. Graduate of Bryn Mawr, Class of 1935, and retired teacher from The Chapin School in New York, she is survived by her sister-in-law Frances Colie of Mystic, CT, and her nieces and nephews: Edward M. Colie IV of Columbia, S.C., Laura Kuhlmann of Carmel, CA., Matthew Colie of Belleville, MI., Marguerite Schnepel of Providence, RI, and Ellen Schnepel of Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Bryn Mawr College.
