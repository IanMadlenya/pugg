
        WILLIAMS--Mary G. Died March 15, following a hospital procedure. Born in Cleveland, OH, she was the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. John Quincy Adams of Youngstown, OH, and a graduate of Wooster College. She was 89. After working as a reporter on the Youngstown Vindicator, she served for 3 years during WWII in India and Europe in the American Red Cross. After the war, she was a secretary to the foreign editor of the New York Herald Tribune. For nearly 56 years she was married to John D. Williams, a veteran journalist with the Wall Street Journal. She loved to follow politics. Visitations will be held Monday, March 19, at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel at 81st and Madison 2-4pm and 6-8pm. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Red Cross would be appreciated.
