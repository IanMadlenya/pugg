
        STARKMAN--Herbert B., 88, of North Miami, FL, on October 30th. Beloved husband of the late Dorothy. Devoted father of Jeffrey, Steven, James and Valerie. Dear brother of the Hon. Charles B. Starkman and Rosalind Kahn and brother-in-law of Rosemary Starkman and Walter Kahn. Loving uncle and great-uncle. Herbert was the epitome of selflessness, generosity and humor. He served in the U.S. Army 57th Signal Battalion WWII for five years. Decorated Soldier's Medal, Purple Heart, Bronze and Silver Stars for bravery. Honored by ''Crime Watch,'' Miami. Graveside service Monday, November 3, at 1 PM, Beth-El Cemetery, Oradell, NJ.
