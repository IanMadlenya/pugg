
        FUCHS-Bernard A. Age 86, died Saturday in Tallahassee, FL, after an extended illness. Mr. Fuchs had been President of USV Pharmaceutical Corporation. He is survived by sons, Larry Fuchs, Director of the Florida Department of Revenue, and Dennis Fuchs, District Court Judge in Salt Lake City, UT; brothers Dr. Bertram Fuchs in Northport, NY, Morton Foxe in Silver Spring, MD; and sister Susan Pearlstein in Port Jefferson, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to Big Bend Hospice, Tallahassee.
