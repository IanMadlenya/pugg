
        SCOTCH-Theodore S. 86, of Longmeadow, MA, formerly of Brookline, died Saturday. He was the husband of Frances (Gauchman) Scotch. Father of Dr. David S. Scotch of Westport, CT, and Elaine Fialky of Longmeadow, MA. Father-in-law of Barbara Scotch and Gary Fialky. Brother of Manuel Scotch of Winthrop, MA. Grandfather of Jeffrey and Joshua Fialky, and Jonathan and Peter Scotch. Great-grandfather of Justin Scotch. Funeral and burial are Monday. Memorial contributions may be made to Jewish Geriatric Services, 770 Converse St., Longmeadow, MA 01106, or American Cancer Society, 53 Capital Dr., West Springfield, MA 01089.
