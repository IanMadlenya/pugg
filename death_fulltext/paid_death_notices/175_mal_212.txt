
        MENDELSON-Nathaniel (Tom) H. Age 92, of Boca Raton, FL., formerly of New York City, died August 20, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Yvette M. Mendelson. Dear brother of Jesse Mendelsohn, Albany, NY. Cherished uncle of Lori (Steven) Strong and Allan (Eileen) Mendelsohn. He started his career in the Youth Fashion Industry after graduating Union College and joining the Carl Company in Schenectady. Mr. Mendelson was the former Executive Director of the Youth Fashion Guild, Past President of the Association of Buying Offices and the Infants, Childrens, Teens Buyer Association. He was an author & professor of Children's Wear Merchandising. He was on numerous Government Advisory Committees on Sizing and Standards for the Children's Wear Industry and was also active in fund raising for both the United Jewish Appeal and Israel Bonds. He was an avid gardener and world traveler. Mr. Mendelson was presently the President of the Cedar Cay Home Owners Association in Broken Sound. He will be missed by all who knew him. Memorial services will be held Wednesday 1 PM at the Broken Sound Country Club, 2401 Willow Springs Drive, Boca Raton, FL. Donations may be made to UJA, Israel Bonds, or the Alzheimer's Association. Services entrusted to Philip M. Weinstein's Shalom Memorial Chapels, Margate, Florida, phone 1-877-554-7878.
