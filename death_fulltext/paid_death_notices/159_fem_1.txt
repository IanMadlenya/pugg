
        DONOGHUE-Nora Ann. On April 1, 2000, in her 34th year. Formerly of Anbally, Cummer, Tuam County Galway, Ireland. Beloved wife of Michael and dear mother of Michael Martin Donoghue. Adored daughter of Martin and Peggy Furey. Cherished sister of Mary, Margaret, Helen, Micheal, Theresa, Martin and Ann. Loving aunt, sister-in-law and daughter-in-law. Services Monday, April 3, 10 AM at Blessed Sacrament Church, W. 71 St. and Broadway, NYC. The family will be receiving at ''The Riverside'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave., Sunday, April 2, 3-5 and 7-9.
