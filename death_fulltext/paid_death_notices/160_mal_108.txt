
        SHEFTELL-Joseph age 87 of Lake Worth, Florida died May 6, 2000, after a protracted illness. Born in Russia to Benjamin and Hoda, he emigrated to America as a young boy. He had two sisters, Irene and Selma. After serving in the Battle of the Bulge in World War II he began his career as a business agent for local 325 in Brooklyn, New York and emerged as an important force in the labor movement. He was devoted to Israel and was visited by Golda Meir as a young man in recognition of his efforts. He was later honored for his achievements in fund raising for Israeli Bonds. He leaves behind his loving and devoted wife of 62 years Wilma, his loving sister Selma, two sons Fred and Steven, his daughters in law Karen and Vincetta and four grandchildren Jason, Lauren, Justin, and Melanie. Service Menorah Gardens Cemetery Monday, May 10, 2000.
