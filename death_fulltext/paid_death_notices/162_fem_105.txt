
        DUNKHORST-Isabel. 82, of Emerson, formerly of New Milford, on July 14, 2000. She graduated from Guilford College, Greensboro, NC, in 1939 with a Bachelor of Science degree. Before retiring in 1989, she was Vice President of the Chase Manhattan Bank, NYC, and worked there for over 30 years. Interment will be at Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY, on July 17, 2000, at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to your favorite charity.
