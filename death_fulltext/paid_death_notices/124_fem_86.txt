
        RICHMAN-Evelyn S., age 81, beloved wife of the late Jack B. Richman, after a long illness, died peacefully at her home in Hollywood, FL., on May 11. She is survived by her children: Benjamin (Buddy) and Sandy Richman, of Bedford, NY; Penny and Irwin Abouaf of New York City; & David and Janet Richman of Phoenix, AZ; grandchildren: Michelle, Mark, Nicole, Samantha, Mary and Bobby; and great grandchildren Sandy and Alexandra. Funeral services will be held at Riverside/Gordon Chapel, Hollywood, Florida, on Tuesday, May 13, 1997 at 3:30 PM. Interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY at 11 AM, on Thursday, May 15, 1997.
