
        RIDLEY-Katherine Raub. The Preservation League of New York State mourns the death of our Staff Counsel, Katherine Raub Ridley, Director of the League's Legal Services Program. Kathie was known across our state and the United States for her singular role in advancing and defending preservation in New York State. The League's trustees have created a dedicated fund to maintain her extensive body of work and continue providing legal services. Contributions may be sent to the Preservation League of New York State, 44 Central Avenue, Albany, New York 12206 for The Katherine Raub Ridley Legal Service Fund.
