
        CALDER-Abbie Ingalls Knowlton, on July 15, 1997. Known professionally as Dr. Abbie Knowlton, Clinical Professor of Medicine in Endocrinology at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. She is survived by her husband, Kenneth T. Calder, M.D.; son, Thomas Calder; daughter, Mary Calder Johnstone; and many loving relatives and friends. Memorial service, Monday, July 21st at 5pm at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street; reception to follow. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in the memory of Dr. Abbie Knowlton to Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons, 630 West 168th Street, BB2-250, New York, New York 10032.
