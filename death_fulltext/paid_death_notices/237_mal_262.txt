
        BRAISTED--Charles H.B. Of Cornwall, VT died October 24, 2006. He is survived by his wife Gayl, daughters Christina and Melissa, and grandchildren Miles, Charlotte and Caelan. A graduate of Hamilton College and St. John's University, he practiced law with Davis, Polk & Wardwell for 30 years and subsequently was an adivsor on state securities issues. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society or the March of Dimes.
