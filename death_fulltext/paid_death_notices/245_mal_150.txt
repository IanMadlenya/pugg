
        BOGATIN--Philip F., 87, cherished husband of Sandra, beloved father of Jeffrey and Susan, Lisa and Barry, and Patrice and Andrew, and loving grandfather of Rachel and Edmund, Rebecca, Andrew, Kate, Jack and Jane. He was a graduate of Pennsylvania State University. He was a Major in the US Army and served in WWII. He was a successful businessman and philanthropist.
