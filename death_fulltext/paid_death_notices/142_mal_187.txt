
        CASTIMORE-John Tillman. 75. Formerly of Scarsdale, NY. Died of cancer November 10, 1998, at his Waldorf, MD, residence. He was a retired marketing/advertising representative. He was a member of the Congregational Church of Scarsdale, NY, and served with the Merchant Marines during WWII. He was co-captain of his sailboat ''John Dog'' and loved music, woodworking and playing his harmonica. He graduated from Amherst College in Amherst, MA. Born October 20, 1923, in Brooklyn, NY, he was the son of the late Clarence Castimore and the late Elisabeth Tracy Castimore. He is survived by his beloved Judith Nathan; nieces Patricia Castimore, Marjorie Brian, nephew Thomas Castimore, niece Anne Castimore; sister-in-law Marjorie Castimore; grand-nephew Will Castimore; stepchildren Richard and Leslie Nathan, Karen Day and Hallie Holland and grandchildren Susanna and Benjamin Nathan, Lauren and Graham Day and Jesse Holland. Services were held November 13 in Waldorf, MD. Send contributions to National Institutes of Health-Cancer Research, 9000 Rockville Pike, Bldg. 31, Rm. 11A18, Bethesda, MD 20892.
