
        WONG--Yip May Dip (Edna Wong). Born in Toishan, China on December 2nd, 1935. Passed away on January 14, 2007 in Zhuhai, China. She was not only a beloved and devoted wife but also a loving mother and grandmother, a caring sister, and a treasured friend. She is survived by her husband Stephen Wong; her daughter Donna, and sons Keith and Ted; her grandchildren Sean, Kayla, and Alicia May; and by her siblings Daisy, Morris, Edward, and Bill. She is at peace with her brother Thomas. The family will receive friends on Thursday 4-7pm at Frank E. Campbell 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Funeral services will be held at 9:30am at Frank E. Campbell. Interment to follow at the Kensico Cemetery.
