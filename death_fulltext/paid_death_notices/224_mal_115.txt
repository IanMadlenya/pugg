
        PREISLER--Simon. Died on September 11th, 2005 in Frankfurt, Germany. Born on October 10th, 1925 in Fancsicovo, Czechoslovakia. He was a survivor of Auschwitz who lived his life to the fullest. Beloved husband of Baba, adored father of Lydia Preisler Shorenstein (Douglas) and Renee Preisler Barasch (Richard) and devoted Opa to his grandchildren, Brandon, Sandra, Danielle, Emily, Benjamin and Natalie. He will be in our hearts forever. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Hurricane Relief Fund at the UJA.
