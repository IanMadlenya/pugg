
        GOODWIN-Harold. Died of cancer on April 11th at his home in Lyme, CT. at age 81. He served with the RCAF as a navigator in W.W. II. He was a mural, portrait & genre painter, & author-illustrator, teacher, and the first President of the Lyme Academy of Fine Arts. He is survived by his wife, Barbara Eckhardt - Goodwin, daughter, Georgiana Goodwin, and granddaughter, Sophia Harvey. There will be no public service.
