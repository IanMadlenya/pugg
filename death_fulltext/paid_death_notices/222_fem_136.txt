
        FITZGERALD--Geraldine, died July 17 at the age of 91. Geraldine fought a lengthy battle with Alzheimer's Disease. Her powerful constitution prevailed, but her beautiful mind was lost. Funeral service will be private and a memorial service will be held in the fall. Contributions may be made in her name to: Alzheimer's Association, 360 Lexington Avenue, 5th floor, NY, NY 10017, (212-983-0700).
