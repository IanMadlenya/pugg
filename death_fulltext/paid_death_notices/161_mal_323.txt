
        BOTWINICK - Benjamin. Died peacefully today June 28, 2000. Beloved husband of Bessie. Loving father of Edward Botwinick and Elaine Wolfensohn. Cherished grandfather of Sara, Naomi, Adam, Andrew & Eric. He will be remembered for his love and generosity of spirit, his extraordinary intellect and for his philanthropy. He remains a model for his entire family & his many friends. Service on Thursday 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. A fund will be set up in his name at the Police Athletic League and The Jewish Theological Seminary.
        BOTWINICK-Benjamin. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Bessie and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Sr. Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
