
        ENGLER--David. Age 83, of Pittsburgh, PA, formerly of New York, NY & Wilmington, DE, died Friday, March 10, 2006. Born December 31, 1922 in New York, NY, he was the son of the late Edward and Antoinette Fiedler Engler. Mr. Engler had been a publisher, writer & editor with McGraw-Hill and was the founder of General Educational Media, Wilmington, DE. He is survived by 2 sons, Edward Engler of Sewickley, PA & Richard E. Engler of Silver Spring, MD; a sister, Herta Danis of Greece. For information Glenn-Kildoo Funeral Home & Cremation Svcs., Inc., Cranberry Twp., PA.
