
        ROEBUCK-Waldemar A. On November 1, 1999. He was the first and former president of the Board of Directors of Strycker's Bay Apartment, Inc. He was president of the Franciscan Lay Apostolate in the U.S. & Canada. He also held several community posts. Reposing at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 West 72 Street, Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass, St. Francis of Assisi Church, on Friday at 10 A.M.
        ROEBUCK-Waldemar A. The Members of the Pierre Toussaint Guild mourn the loss of our beloved Director, Mr. Waldemar A. Roebuck. A saintly, dedicated Catholic gentleman, Mr. Roebuck worked diligently for many charitable causes, especially for the canonization of the Venerable Pierre Toussaint. May Waldemar A. Roebuck now rest in peace with God. Rev. Msgr. Robert M. O'Connell Vice Postulator
