
        FENHAGEN-Margaret McCann ''Margo''. Age 63, of Pound Ridge, N.Y., died July 24, 2002. Wife of William E. Fenhagen. She is also survived by her children Carolin I. Raleigh, Laura I. Imrie and Douglas P. Irwin. Sister of Franklin W. McCann and Jonathan W. McCann. Memorial service to be held at the Fenhagen home, Pound Ridge, N.Y. Saturday, July 27th, 4PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Bendheim Cancer Center, Greenwich Hospital, 5 Perry Ridge Rd, Greenwich, CT 06830 and the Breast Cancer Alliance, Inc., 1 Hillside Dr., Greenwich, CT 06830.
