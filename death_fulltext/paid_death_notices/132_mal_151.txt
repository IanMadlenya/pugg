
        KRAMER-Martin. January 7, 1998, born June 6, 1907. Inventor, mentor, teacher, (aeronautics and math, East New York Vocational High School; insurance, Columbia University). Retired, Northport, FL, community leader, founding chair education center, organizer of Peace Day. He offered a clarity of thought, ingenuity, and unbounded generosity, which guided the careers of his family, friends and students. Survived by Esther Kramer, wife of 62 years, daughters, Nancy Paige, Fredrica Kramer, sons-in-law, David Paige, Michael Keane, grandchildren, Tara and Daniel Paige, Edmund Keane.
