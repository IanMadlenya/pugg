
        BOGDAN - Barbara Cates. Died peacefully on January 16. A longtime resident of New York City she was born in Salt Lake City, Utah on November 14, 1907, the daughter of Louis Shattuck and Helen Cates. She was the widow of Norbert Andrew Bogdan. Barbara is survived by two of her children from her first marriage to Robert Cullen Wilkin, Barbara Washburn of Ojai, California and Louis Shattuck Cates Wilkin of San Rafael, California. Her eldest daughter Julia Marks of Buffalo, New York predeceased her. Barbara Bogdan has nine grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. She will be remembered with love by all.
