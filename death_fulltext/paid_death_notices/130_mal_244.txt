
        GURLAND-Haim. Beloved husband of Estelle for 55 years. Devoted father of Judith and Robert, father-in-law of Warren Blaker and Gaby Jordan. Adored grandfather of Avram, Lauren and Emily. Loyal brother, uncle and friend. After a valiant struggle with cancer in his 93rd year. Services were held November 18, 1997, at Plaza Memorial Chapel, New York City.
