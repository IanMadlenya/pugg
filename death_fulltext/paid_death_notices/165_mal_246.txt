
        COOPER-Harry. Legendary golf professional, inspired and touched so many, passed away October 17. Services Friday, October 20, from 7 PM to 9 PM & Saturday, October 21, 9 AM-10 AM at the Graham Funeral Home, Rye, New York. COOPER-Harry E. The Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club note with saddness the loss of Harry Cooper an Honorary Member and Our Club Professional for 26 years. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Emma. Jay M. Rosen, President Corey B. Rabin, Secretary
