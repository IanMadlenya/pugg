
        OTTAVINO-Adam George. Age 84 died at home on May 29th, 2002. Devoted husband of Mary Pigott Ottavino. Loving father of Margaret Mary Ottavino, Katherine Agnes Ottavino Parker & Sallie Ann Ottavino Elkordy. Grandfather of Mary Elizabeth Elkordy, Arbas Octavius Elkordy, Hannah Marie Parker & Margaret Agnes Parker. Resident of Forest Hills Gardens and Point Lookout, NY. He was graduated from Brooklyn Preparatory School in 1936 and the Catholic University of America, School of Architecture in 1940. He served for 4-1/2 years in the U.S. Navy during W.W. II as Captain, with the full rank of Lieutenant, of the minesweeper U.S.S. Hilarity. He served in both the Atlantic and the Pacific. He was a leader in the stone industry & president of A. Ottavino Corporation, founded in 1913 and served as president from 1958 to 1998. A stargazer who will be missed for his excellent wit and refreshing candor. Visiting at Fox Funeral Home, Inc., Ascan Avenue corner of Metropolitan Avenue, Forest Hills, Thursday and Friday 2-5 & 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Saturday, June 1st at 9:00 AM. Interment Cemetery of the Holy Cross, Brooklyn, NY.
