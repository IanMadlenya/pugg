
        ROGIN-Rita. Died on September 18, 2002, in Del Mar, CA. She was born Rita Farrer on Januray 17, 1925, in New Brunswick, NJ, to the late Lena and Herman Farrer. She will be remembered for her dedicated work for Haddassah, Douglass College and National Organization for Women. She was a patron of the arts, an avid reader, a gourmet cook and a talented gardener. She was committed to her extended family. She is survived by three children, Barry, Heidi and Abner Taub, and three grandchildren. Her marriage to Jerome Taub ended in divorce. Funeral services are Sunday, September 22, at 1 PM, at Crabiel Funeral Home, 239 Livingston Avenue, New Brunswick, NJ.
