
        GOLDMAN -- Patricia M. passed away peacefully on March 11, 2004. Loving wife of the late Albert S. Goldman. Wonderful mother of Andrew & Nancy Goldman, Lynn & Larry Schan, Susan & Gene Resnick, Jane Goldman, and John & Wendy Goldman. Adored grandmother, greatgrandmother and step-grandmother. Dear friend of the late John Kubie. Born in London, England, she was a woman of grace and elegance, and was devoted to community and charitable causes. Funeral service at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, Westchester Ave, Rye, NY on Sunday March 14, 2004 at 2:30pm. Family will receive visitors at home following the service on Sunday and on Monday March 15 from 69pm. In lieu of flowers, please send gifts to the Sarah Neuman Center for Healthcare and Rehabilitation, a Division of the Jewish Home and Hospital, 845 Palmer Ave, Mamaroneck, NY 10543.
