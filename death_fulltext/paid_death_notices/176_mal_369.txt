
        COHEN-Edward Arthur, 76. Formerly of Astoria and Lynnbrook, NY. Devoted son of the late Louis M. & Rose (Krum) Cohen. Loving father of Lewis and Leslie (San Diego, CA), grandfather of Allison Rose Cohen. Also survived by his former wife Mary-Jane Rund and his dear cousins Barbara Fox, Arlyne Krum, & Edith Sapan. Mr. Cohen was a proud veteran of W.W. II during which he survived nine months as a POW. He received several medals including a Bronze Star and Purple Heart. Ed's unique character and wit will be remembered by his family and friends. A memorial will be held September 28, at 11 AM, at Mt. Ararat Cemetery in Farmingdale.
