
        GILL-Robert Lee. Age 87, died on Thursday, September 24, 1998, in New York City, from complications of a stroke. Survivors include his wife, Melanie Gill, and from his first marriage, a son, Peter T. Gill of Bronxville, NY, and a stepson, John D. Chapman of Fort Pierce, Fla., and two sisters, Barbara Betts of West Hampton Beach, NY, and Mary Roby of Rochester, NY; grandchildren, Caroline and Elizabeth Gill, John, David and Steven Chapman, and seven step great-grandchildren. Born in South Orange, NJ, October 10, 1911. Married Caroline Townsend Chapman in 1939, who died in 1984. Member of the New York Stock Exchange for over 40 years. Past president of the NYSE Luncheon Club, member of the NY Racquet and Tennis Club. Distinguished and well-known collector of fine American art and antiques. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Henry Francis du Pont Winterthur Museum, DE 19735. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, on Sunday, 2-5 PM, and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Service private.
