
        JURGENS-Thomas E. 26 year old Senior Court Officer at Manhattan Supreme Court, heroically on September 11, 2001. Tommy was also a Captain of Engine 332 of the Meadowmere Park Volunteer Fire Department and was honorably discharged from the United States Army, Army Reserves, and the National Guard where he was a combat medic. Tommy is survived by his proud and loving family which includes his wife Joan, father Joseph, stepmom Barbara, mother Linda, stepfather Alan, two brothers Joseph and Bradley, a sister Jessica, a grandmother Margaret Dracker, a grandfather Robert Blakeman, mother and father-in-law Joan and Andy Schmitt, a God daughter Kayla Schmitt as well as many relatives and friends. Tommy will be deeply missed and will always be remembered for his loyalty, generosity, sincerity, sense of humor, and most of all his heroism and great love. A memorial wake will be held on Whitting Funeral Home, 300 Glen Cove Ave, Glen Head, NY, on Thursday, November 15, from 2 PM to 5 PM, and 7 PM to 9 PM. There will be a Fire Department Service held at 8 PM, at the funeral home. A memorial Mass will be held at St. Raymond's Roman Catholic Church, 263 Atlantic Ave, East Rockaway, NY, on Friday, November 16, at noon. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Cancer Society, 839 Stewart Ave, Garden City, NY 11530 (1-800-227-2345), or the Nassau County Burn Center, PO Box 246, Levittown, NY 11756 (516572-3208).
