
        PIZITZ - STRELZOFF - Fran ces, M.D. Age 103. Died Friday, May 2, 2003. Dr. Pizitz was born in Russia, received her medical training and license in Berlin, came to the US during WWII. She earned another license to practice in America as a pediatrician. This was followed by training and license to practice psychoanalysis. She was affiliated with William Alanson White Institute in NYC. She was the widow of Samuel Strelzoff. She is survived by her step - daughter Irene Preiser, five grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren. Services were held this past Sunday.
