
        WEBB-Lucy Hoffman. 71 years old. Died on November 21st after a long illness battling cancer. She was married to Lt. Colonial Charles Webb, Jr. and was the daughter of the late Colonial Albert Lincoln Hoffman and Elaine Sullivan Hoffman. Mrs. Webb was the granddaughter of the late Mr. and Mrs. Francis Burrall Hoffman of New York and the late Mr. and Mrs. James Francis Sullivan of Philadelphia and descendant of Richard Nicolls, first British Colonial Governor of New York. She is survived by her three children, Charles Hoffman Webb, Lainey Webb Moseley, Livingston Rivers Webb and two grandchildren, Jack Moseley and Leta Moseley. Funeral services will be held at Arlington National Cemetery, on Thursday, December 3rd at 3PM, Fort Meyers Chapel.
