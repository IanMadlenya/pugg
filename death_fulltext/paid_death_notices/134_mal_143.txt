
        COSTIKYAN-Granger. Count Costikyan died on March 10, 1998, in York, Maine at the age of 90. He made his home in York Harbor and Rancho Mirage, California. A 1929 graduate of Yale College, he was a life long banker, becoming President of First Bank System, Inc. in Minneapolis and a partner of Brown Brothers Harriman & Co. in Chicago and Philadelphia. He is survived by his wife, the former Margaret Lawrence Taylor, 3 daughters: Gail Lawrence Costikyan of Chicago, Joan Ransom Jarvis of York Harbor, Maine, and Wendy Warren Costikyan Smith of London, England; five grandchildren and one great grandson. Memorial services will be held at the First Parish Congregational Church in York, Maine on Sunday, March 15, 1998 at 4 P.M. Interment at a later date in Buffalo, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the First Parish Congregational Church, York Street, York, Maine 03909.
