
        DIESENHOF--Ellen W., of Genesee, CO, was born in Brooklyn, New York on November 23, 1940. She was raised and educated in Brooklyn, meeting her future husband, Martin Diesenhof, in kindergarten. She and Martin spent summers at Masad Camp in Pennsylvania. She attended the University of Michigan and married Martin soon after graduation in 1962. Ellen was a devoted and wellloved teacher of English and College Counselor at Ramsey High School in Ramsey, New Jersey for 23 years before moving to Colorado in 1998. She taught English at Red Rocks Community College from 2000-2006. Ellen loved to work with teenagers and tutored many generations of students preparing for college entrance examinations and b'nai mitzvot. She had recently turned her attention to her adored grandsons. Ellen was the loving wife of Martin of Genesee, CO; mother of Kenneth Diesenhof, MA and Michael (Cynthia) Diesenhof, Avon, CT, and grandmother of Abraham (Avi) and Eli, of Avon, CT. Ellen will also be missed by the multitude of friends, colleagues, and students whose lives she touched. Funeral service will be held on Wednesday, April 11, 2007 at 10:00am at Congregation Beth Evergreen, Evergreen, CO. Interment in Mt. Nebo Memorial Park in Aurora, CO. The family suggests that memorial contributions be made to the Congregation Beth Evergreen Scholarship Fund.
