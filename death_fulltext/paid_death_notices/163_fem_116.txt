
        WILSHUSEN-Agnes Russ. She was 89. Died August 12, 2000, in Troy, NY. Widow of William Wilshusen; sister of Helen Blaschke, Lillian Johnson Pamentel, and Louis Russ; sister-in-law of Otto Blaschke, Anne Russ, John Johnson, Frank Pamentel; survived by nieces & nephew Lillian Blashke, Joyce Diwan, Betty McDonald, John Johnson and grandnieces & nephews Devin, Molly, Alex, Ann, John. Longtime Flushing resident; retired from General Motors; volunteer at Flushing Hospital and Queens Botanical Garden; member of Redeemer Lutheran Church, Bayside. In lieu of flowers, please donate in her name to Queens Botanical Garden or Redeemer Lutheran Church. Reposing at Howard L. Hallet, Inc. (Gleason Building), 149-20 Northern Blvd, Flushing, Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Services are at 8 PM Tuesday & 9:30 AM, Wednesday. Interment Cypress Hills Cemetery.
