
        BERNSTEIN--Harold, 75, of Hillsdale, N.J. died Saturday, August 28, 2004. He is survived by his wife Ruth, son Clifford and his wife Shinee, daughter Nancy and three adored grandchildren Eli, Emma and Reuben. He was predeceased by his son Richard and daughter-in-law K.J. as well as his brother Milton and sister Honey. Harold worked as a physicist, being a graduate of MIT and Boston University. His passions included sailing, playing the violin, and later in life, the ukulele as well as participating in competitive bridge. His jokes were appreciated by some, but he will be greatly missed by all. A memorial service will be held on Friday, September 3, 2004 at 10:45 A.M. at The Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah in Paramus. N.J. (201) 262-1128. Donations may be made to: United Nations Foundation, Attn: Polio Eradication, 1225 Connecticut Ave., NW, Suite 400, Washington, DC 20036
