
        GOTTLIEB--Inez Bellow, 89, former Director of Public Affairs Programming at Philadelphia's CBS - owned WCAU-TV, Saturday 2 December in NYC. Her program ''Now Is The Time'' with Ossie Davis and Ruby Dee on black Civil Rights won a 1967-68 Emmy. Surviving are two sisters, Olive McGarrity and Monnie Callen, of NYC; two children, Vicki of Albuquerque, NM, and Richard of NYC; and five grandchildren. Memorial: 11 am, Sunday 7 January 2007 at The Philadelphian, 2401 Pennsylvania Avenue, Philadelphia.
