
        ECKSTEIN--Phillip A. The Horace Mann School mourns with great sorrow the tragic passing of Phillip Eckstein '03. Phillip's unflagging optimism, his appreciation for life and his great sense of humor were noteworthy. We remember his free spirit, his friendly smile and his senior project work on the wonderful Zen Garden. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his parents, John Eckstein and Carolyn Kohn and the rest of his family. Dr. Thomas M. Kelly Head of School Robert J. Katz '65 Chair, Board of Trustees
