
        TAGER-Rose B. Of Brooklyn, New York. Died on February 27. Loving wife of the late Harry L. Tager, M.D. Loving mother of Michael G. Tager, M.D. of Westport, CT and Charles W.         TAGER-Rose B. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of Rose B. Tager, beloved mother of our Trustee, Dr. Charles Tager. We extend our deepest condolences to Charles and his family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
