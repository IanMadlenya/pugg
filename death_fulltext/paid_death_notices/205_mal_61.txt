
        DILLON--George Burford, of Manchester, died Thursday, January 29, 2004 at New Brunswick, NJ. Born in Chicago, he lived in Teaneck, before moving to Manchester in 1984. He was a decorated Army veteran, serving during World War II. He was an insurance broker with Marsh McLennan, NY, NY for many years before retiring in 1986. Mr. Dillon was a brilliant and irreverent man with a myriad of interests. He could often be found reading poetry, swimming in the ocean or discussing political topics. He was adored by his family and his love for them will endure. Mr. Dillon was a charismatic and charming man who will be greatly missed. Mr. Dillon was predeceased by his wife Ruthie in 1972. He is survived by his daughter and son-inlaw, Lori Dillon and John Ager; granddaughters, Georgiana and Belle; his dear brother and sister-in-law, Gregory and Jenine Dillon and his beloved nieces and nephews. A memorial service will take place on Friday at 2:00PM at Christ Church, 5 Paterson Street, New Brunswick, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the charity of your choice or just buy something nice for yourself.
