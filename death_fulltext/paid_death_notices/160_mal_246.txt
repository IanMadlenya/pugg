
        SLIFKIN-Morrie. Of Ardsley on Hudson, formerly of Tarrytown, died of cancer on Saturday, May 20, 2000, at home. He was 89. Judge Slifkin worked as a Supreme Court Judge for the State of New York in the 9th Judicial District. He was with the 9th Judicial District for 17 years before retiring in 1986. Judge Slifkin was born June 11, 1910, in Morristown, NJ, USA, to Samuel and Rebecca Velitzkin Slifkin. He was educated in Tarrytown, NY, and graduated from Washington Irving High School in 1927. He earned a B.S. degree in psychology from NYU, NYC, in 1931. Additional degree: Columbia Law School, 1934. On November 27, 1935, he married Rosalind Projansky. Mr. Slifkin lived in Ardsley on Hudson for the past 20 years. Before that, he lived in Tarrytown, NY, from 1910 to 1980. Activities: Tappan Zee Mental Health Center; Tarrytown Hospital-Director; Phelps Memorial Hospital-Director and Secretary; Easter Seal Society; Planned Parenthood; Hudson River Speech Center; United Nations Committee-Chairman; UJA-Local Chairman; Tarrytown Lions Club-President; Masonic Member; Temple Beth Abraham-Trustee Emeritus; Television Actor-CBS-1959-1963''Verdict is Yours''; monograph: ''Effect of Music on Performance of a Monotonous Task.'' He is survived by wife Rosalind, daughters and sons-in-law Barbara and James Lister, Susan and Douglas Levin, granddaughters Rachel and Rebecca Levin. Calling hours are one hour prior to memorial service at Temple Beth Abraham, Tarrytown. The funeral is at 2 PM, Monday, May 22, 2000. Friends may send memorial donations to Phelps Memorial Hospice, Temple Beth Abraham.
