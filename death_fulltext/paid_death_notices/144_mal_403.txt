
        BINDER-Nathan. On January 23, 1999. Beloved husband of Lillian (Chickie). Dear brother of Ray. Cherished father of Barbara, Michael and Steven. Loving grandfather of Charles, Peter, Nancy, Amy and Zoey. Delighted greatgrandfather of Bradley and Emily, and devoted father-in-law of Alan, Nancy and Kristen. He was a veteran of World War II, a practicing dentist in Mt. Kisco, New York for over forty-five years, and an active member of the Jewish community. Services at Levayah-Garlick Funeral Home, 1091 Yonkers Avenue, Yonkers, New York, at 10 AM, Monday, January 25. Expressions of sympathy in his memory may be donated to the American Heart Association.
