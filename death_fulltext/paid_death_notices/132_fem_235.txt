
        BOGART-Esther, 72. Died Jan. 26, 1998. She lived in Douglas, MI. for the past five years, having moved from East Williston, LI. She had worked for Lord & Taylor, and Payne, Wood and Littlejohn. She was a member of the Brookville Reformed Church, Community Church of Douglas, and H.A.S.P. of Hope College. Surviving are a sister, Janet Wolbrink of Fennville; nieces and nephews. A celebration of thanksgiving for her life, will be held at the Brookville Reformed Church, Brookville, New York on Saturday, January 31, at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the charity of one's choice.
