
        McALPIN-Helen B.. Formerly of Convent, N.J., Meadowlakes, Heightstown, New Jersey, and Brandreth Lake, NY, December 28, 1999, in Palm Springs, CA., at age 93. Beloved wife of the late Malcolm E. McAlpin, she is survived by two children, David and Ann; three grandchildren Merrill, Melissa and Bambi; and two greatgrandchildren Riley and Schuyler. Born in Cleveland, OH. on January 15, 1906, she was captain of the 1936 U.S. Olympic Women's Ski Team, & a member of the U.S. Ski Hall of Fame. A memorial service will be held in Morristown, NJ at St. Peter's Church, Thursday, January 13 at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Peck School or The Seeing Eye.
