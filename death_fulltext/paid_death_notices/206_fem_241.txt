
        SCOTCH--Sally of Astoria, Queens, at 91. Survived by husband George, children Arlene Wysong and Bill Schreiber, Carol Tweedy, Marty and Mary Alterman and grandchildren Justine Wysong, Jacob and Josh Alterman. First woman board member of the Board of Directors of the Astoria Center of Israel and President of its Ladies Guild. Loved by those touched by her passion for life and commitment to her community.
