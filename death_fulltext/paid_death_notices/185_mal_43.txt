
        SHATZKIN - Leonard. Died on May 11, 2002, at home, aged 82. Survived by his wife of 62 years, Eleanor; son, Mike; daughters, Karen and Nance, three granddaughters. Publishing executive for The Viking Press, Doubleday, Collier Macmillan, and McGraw-Hill. Publishing industry analyst, author of the book ''In Cold Type'' and the monograph ''The Mathematics of Bookselling.'' Through International Executive Service Corps, worked with publishing enterprises in Latin America, Africa, and eastern Europe. Scientist on the Manhattan Project during World War II. Loved for his intellect, unfailing sense of humor, generosity, and social conscience. Family requests that his memory be honored by gifts to Amnesty International or The American Civil Liberties Union.
