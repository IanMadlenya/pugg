
        FOREMAN-Margaret (nee Heydecker), 79. Of North Granby CT, formerly of Pelham Manor, N.Y. Widow of Charles W.L. Foreman. Died Sunday, Jan. 4. Memorial services will be held Saturday, January 24 at 2 PM, at the Simsbury United Methodist Church, 799 Hopmeadow St., Simsbury CT. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Salvation Army, 855 Asylum Av, Hartford, CT 06105
