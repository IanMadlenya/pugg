
        WRIGHT - Joseph G., 80, on Dec. 25, in Tamarac, FL, after a long illness. Served in the Army during WWII on YANK, was a Vice President of This Week Magazine and the McCall Printing Co. and retired from the Reader's Digest in 1984. Survived by his wife, Dorothy, a daughter, Nancy, and two granddaughters, all of S. Florida, and a son, Donald of Brooklyn Heights.
