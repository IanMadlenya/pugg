
        ZWEIG-Robert M. of Boca Raton, FL, age 76, died February 12, 1997 at 11:40 PM. Born Brooklyn, September 16, 1920, to Pinkus and Rebecca, he graduated from Queens College and West Virginia University. Veteran WW II. President John Chatillon and National Scale Manufacturer's Association. Past Master, Masons. Retired to play tennis, fish, swim, write poetry and become a sculptor. He leaves: his wife of forty-nine years, Estelle; two sons, Paul (with Sandy in MD); and Michael (with Marie in CA); his older brother Sol (with Harriet in FL); and six grandchildren (Rachel, Aaron, Ben, Megan, Casey and Sarah). His spirit, as always, will inspire us forever.
