
        GOLDENBERG-Isabella Z. Beloved wife of the late Morris. Loving mother of Billy of Toluca Lake, CA and Lucille Goldshlag. Dear sister of Ruth Katz and Dr. Maurice Leon. President of the League for Carolians, an organization dedicated to the rehabilitation of handicapped children. She was a manager of the Great Neck Symphony, a teacher of violin and violla, who played in various chamber and symphonic groups. Her love of music echoed in the hearts of those privileged to know her. Services today, 3 PM, ''Sinai Chapels'' 162-05 Horace Harding Expwy., Fresh Meadows, NY.
