
        BAGATELLE--Warren D. Devoted husband of Hedy, his wife of over 44 years; father of David, Tracy and Adrien; grandfather to Olivia, Molly, Emma and Alexander passed away peacefully the morning of Thursday, May 31, 2007. Warren was a wellknown investment banker and partner at Loeb Partners Corporation. He also served on numerous corporate and not-for-profit boards. Prior to joining Loeb Partners, he ran the NYSE member firm of Rosenkrantz Ehrenkrantz Lyon & Ross. Prior to his career on Wall Street he was a turn-around specialist and ran several publicly traded and privately held companies. He began his career at Arthur Andersen & Co. He was educated at Union College (Class of 1960) and received an MBA from Rutgers University. He was a Certified Public Accountant. He proudly served his country as a member of the United States Coast Guard. He was well respected for his integrity, honesty, dignity, and intelligence. His favorite pastimes included spending time with his friends at his summer home on Lake Hopatcong, NJ, his antique wooden boats, his family, and Union College. He was an outstanding husband, father, grandfather and friend who will be greatly missed by all whose lives he touched.
