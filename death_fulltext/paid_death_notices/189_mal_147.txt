
        ANNENBERG-Hon. Walter. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our dear friend, Walter Annenberg. His generosity, and deep knowledge of and love for the arts have set an extraordinary example for future generations. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to his wife, Leonore, his family, and friends. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Robert J. Hurst, President Maxwell L. Anderson, Director
