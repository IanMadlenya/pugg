
        ABDELA-Catherine. On September 9, 2002. Died suddenly from cardiac arrest at 60. She leaves behind her bereaved husband Angelo, her devastated daughter Daphne and her distraught mother as well as her family and friends worldwide. A service will be held on Thursday, September 12 at 3PM at West End Collegiate Church, 368 West End Avenue and 77 St. Donations may be sent to North American Conference on Ethiopian Jewry, PO Box 757, Milwaukee, WI. 53201-9338.
