
        SUSSKIND-Jack. The American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science mourns the passing of Jack Susskind, husband of dedicated ACWIS Board of Directors member Leah Susskind, generous supporter of the Weizmann Institute, and benefactor of the Jack and Leah Susskind Postdoctoral Fellowship in Cancer Research at the Weizmann Institute. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Leah, daughter Sara and her husband Tom, daughter Tania, grandson Zachary, and the entire family. Robert H. Asher, Chairman, American Committee Albert Willner, M.D., President Martin Kraar, Executive V.P. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Haim Harari, President
        SUSSKIND-Jack. We extend our sincerest condolences to Leah Susskind on the untimely passing of her beloved husband. Jack was a shining role model-kind, gracious, humble and honorable-and a person who embodied the highest values of Jewish tradition. A member of our New York Board, he and his wife Leah are Diamond Founders and major supporters of the Woman and Infant Center and the Department of Emergency Medicine. May Leah, daughters Sara and Tania, sister Anna and the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem
