
        LOHSE--Austin W. died on April 1, 2004 at his home, he was 78 years old. Born in NYC, NY, he was a lifelong resident of Short Hills. He is predeceased by his wife, Virginia Lohse (nee Butler) and his brother, Robert V. Lohse. Surviving are his daughter, Connie Lohse and her husband, Robby Brewer of Chevy Chase, MD, his son, Dan Lohse and his wife, Sarah of Branchburg, NJ, and his grandchildren, Robert Scott and Laura Elizabeth Brewer, and Jonathan Watts and Andrew Butler Lohse. Received his BA from Dartmouth University in 1947. From 1947 to 1952, he worked as a credit analyst for Irving Trust Co. in NYC. From 1952 to 1973, he worked for American Express International Banking Corp. in NYC where he was Assistant Vice President from 1958 to 1961, Vice President from 1961 to 1968, and Senior Vice President from 1968 to 1973. He worked for Charterhouse Group International, Inc. in NYC from 1973 to 1978. From 1978 to 1981 he worked at RT Madden & Co, Inc, NYC. From 1981 until his retirement, he was President of his own investment banking corporation, the AW, Lohse & Company, Inc. He was also the former director of American Express Bank GMBH, Frankfurt, Germany, American Express Bank SPA, Rome, Italy, American Express Securities SA, Paris, France, and LB/Amex Ltd, London, England. He was a member of Casque and Gauntlet Society and Beta Theta Pi both in NYC. He belonged to the Short Hills Club and the Knickerbocker Club, NYC. Funeral services and interment will be private. Relatives and friends may visit from 2-5 on Sunday, April 4th at the Bradley, Smith & Smith Funeral Home, 415 Morris Avenue, Springfield, NJ.
