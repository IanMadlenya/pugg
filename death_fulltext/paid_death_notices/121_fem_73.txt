
        PEARCE-Marion Gengler, 81, of Fairfield, CT., died peacefully at her home Sat., February 8, 1997. Born in NYC, she was a graduate of the Shipley School in Sewickley, PA, and attended Barnard College. She was predeceased by her husband, Arthur W. Pearce. She is survived by her three sons: Arthur Pearce, Jr. of Ithaca, NY, Gerald Pearce of Fairfield and William Pearce of Tarpon Springs, FL.; four daughters, Patricia Brodersen of Pompano, FL., Marion Pearce of NYC, Lucy Standing and Elizabeth Bonney, both of Fairfield; 1 sister Jean McCann of Newtown, CT.; 12 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren. Friends are invited to attend a Memorial Mass today at 1:00 P.M., at St. Pius X Church, Fairfield., CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to: St. Vincent's Medical Center Foundtion, 2800 Main Street, Bridgeport, CT. 06606.
