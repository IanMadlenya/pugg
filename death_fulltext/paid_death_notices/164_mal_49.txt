
        KENNY-Eugene E. Died at the age of 86 on September 9, 2000 at his home in Manhasset, NY. Gene was born in Manhattan and raised in Brooklyn. Loving husband of Ursula. Cherished father of Constance, Eugene and Michael. Dear brother of Margaret Butler. He graduated Manhattan College in 1937. Gene joined the NYC fire Dept and rose through the ranks to Battalion Chief. In 1958 he was awarded the William Todd Memorial Medal for Valor. He served as a naval officer in Pearl Harbor in WW II. After retiring from the Fire Dept in 1965 he joined the real estate firm of Helmsley Spear Inc. where he bacame a Vice President, retiring in 1994. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, Wednesday and Thursday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass on Friday, St. Marys R.C. Church, Manhasset. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery. Donations would be appreciated to A.C.L.D. Adults and Children with Learning and Developmental disabilities, 807 S. Oyster Bay Road, Bethpage, New York 11714.
