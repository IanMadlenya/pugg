
        FLOETER -- Kent, 66, on Thursday, February 12, 2004. Born Saginaw, Michigan, studied at Boston University School of Fine Arts 1961, Yale University School of Arts and Architecture 1964, Fulbright Scholar. An artist and educator, Kent was a respected and collected artist for the past thirty years throughout the world. His friendship, his advice and his artistic challenges will be missed. He is survived by his daughter Amy Lieber, her husband Tom, his son Milo Addica, his wife Kerry, his grandchildren Rose, Charis and Leaf, a sister Marsha Floeter and countless friends. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date. Contributions may be made to the Kent Floeter Foundation, care of Andrew Miller, Box 362, Ocean Beach, New York 11770.
