
        HEILICER-Bernard, 79, of Plainview died July 2, 2000, of natural causes after a short illenss. He enjoyed a successful career as an aeronautical engineer at Fairchild Republic. His loving and cherished wife of 57 years, Norma, passed on in September, 1999. Cherished father of Sandra Barmak, Lorraine Glaser and Shelley Gross. Loved father-in-law of Len Barmak, Gary Glaser and Michael Gross. Loving and adoring grandfather (''Pop'') of Allan and Julie Barmak and Lesley and Michael Reilly, Samantha and Evan Glaser, and Jeffrey and Amy Gross; loving and adoring great-grandfather of Taylor Barmak. Services on Thursday, July 6, 12 noon at Guttermans Memorial Chapel, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury. Donations may be sent to the Alzheimers Association or to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Center. He will be greatly missed.
