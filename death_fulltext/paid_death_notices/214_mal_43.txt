
        LEIPZIGER--Arthur F. Age 82. On November 2, 2004, still hoping for a regime change. Much loved by many, especially his wife of 58 years, Evelyn; daughters, Karen and Deidre; grandkids, Izzy and Kiran; brothers, Herb and Eddie; in-laws, nieces and nephews. Memorial service Monday, November 8th at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave at 91 St. In lieu of flowers, for those who wish, the family would appreciate a donation to the NYC Ballet or WBAI.
        LEIPZIGER--Arthur F. The partners and staff of Leipziger & Breskin LLP mourn the passing of a founding partner of our firm, Arthur F. Leipziger. He was a friend, a mentor and a gentle man. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Evelyn and his daughters Karen and Deidre and granddaughter Izzy.
