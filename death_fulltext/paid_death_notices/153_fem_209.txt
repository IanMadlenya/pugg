
        HAYNES-Nancy Townsend. Age 86, of Naples, FL, formerly of Bronxville, NY, died on Friday, October 22, 1999. She was born in NYC and was a graduate of Vassar College and The Brearley School and was a member of Christ Church, Bronxville, since 1952. Surviving are her children Lathrop T. Haynes and Patricia C. Haynes. Mrs. Haynes was preceded in death by her husband Lathrop L. Haynes in 1973. Private graveside services will be held at Kensico Cemetery.
