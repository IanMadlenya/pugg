
        DIENSTAG - Leo Nathan, M.D. Brooklyn-born, navy corpsman during WWII, graduate of Drew University and New York Medical College. A boarded internist, he taught and practiced his profession with dedication in Manhattan for more than 40 years. Survived by his wife, Grace, sons, Mark and David, grandchildren, brother, Jerome (Jacqueline), to all of whom he was deeply devoted and who now mourn his passing.
