
        RICE--Sol. 90, a veteran, retired NYC fireman and accountant died Saturday, April 23 in Freehold, NJ. He was a 53 year resident of Merrick, LI, where he was a member and Past Commander of the Jewish War Veterans. He is survived by his children, Naomi Kalmus and Daniel Rice, their spouses, David Kalmus and Laurie Rice, his grandchildren, Lisa and Jocelyn Kalmus and Erica Rice, and his sister Gloria Juda. Funeral services today at Guttermans Funeral Home in RVC, NY.
