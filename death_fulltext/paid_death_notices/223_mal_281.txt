
        CHEW--Beverly Cory.Known to friends and family as Peter, died of lung cancer on Friday August 26, 2005 at Westminster Canterbury, in Charlottesville, Virginia, at the age of 77. Mr. Chew was born on June 18, 1928 in Rochester, New York, to Philip Frederick Chew and Jane Cory Chew. Raised in Syosset, New York, he was a 1947 graduate of St. Mark's high school in Southborough, Massachusetts, and a 1953 graduate of Trinity College, in Hartford, Connecticut. After college, Mr. Chew served in the U.S. Navy as an aviator and later moved to New York City where he worked as an investment counselor. There he was a member of the St. Nicholas Society of New York and the Society of the Cincinnati, an organization of the descendants of officers who served in the Revolutionary War. In 1989, Mr. Chew retired and moved to Charlottesville, VA, with his family. His wife, Constance DeWilde Chew preceded him in death in 2002. An avid outdoorsman, recreational artist and loving father, Mr. Chew is survived by his three children: Alexander Beverly Chew and his wife Renata Milare Chew, Frederick Chew DeWilde and his wife Michelle Dethier DeWilde, and his daughter Cory Livingston Chew. Memorial services will be held at 2 PM on Saturday, September 3rd at St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Ivy, VA. Family and friends will be received following the service. Memorial donations may be made to the St. Nicholas Society of New York and the Society of the Cincinnati. For more information contact Hill and Wood Funeral Home, Charlottesville, VA, 434-296-6148.
