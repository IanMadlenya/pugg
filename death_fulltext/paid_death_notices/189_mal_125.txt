
        MERINO - Frank Joseph. Died Saturday, October 5, 2002, tragically struck by an automobile while riding his bicycle. Graduated from Browning High School and Fordham University. Proud parent of Daniel and Jessica Merino. Successful businessman in Novato, CA. Dear and devoted son of Drs. Francisco and Rose Merino of La Jolla, CA, and formerly of New York City. Memorial Mass at St. Vincent's Church in San Rafael, CA, on October 10 at 1 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the San Diego Hospice.
