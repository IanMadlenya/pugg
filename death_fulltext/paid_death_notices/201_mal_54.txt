
        CROWN--Paul, Age 75, of Boynton Beach, FL, born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA, formerly of Long Island, NY, died October 6, 2003. Long time business owner in New York City, active member of Community Reform Temple of Westbury, NY and Congregation B'nai Israel in Boca Raton. Survivors include his wife of 52 years, Joanne (nee Newman); daughters, Nancy Crown of Boca Raton, Valerie Crown of New City, NY; grandchildren, Rebecca, Adam, David and Elizabeth; three sisters, Jeanette Goldreyer, Annabelle Weissman, and Leatrice Blesser. Funeral services will be 11:00 AM Thursday at Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 7500 N. State Road 7, Coconut Creek, FL (954) 427-6500. Entombment will follow at Eternal Light Memorial Gardens, Boynton, Beach, FL. The family respectfully requests contributions in his memory be made to the Center for Justice at Congregation B'nai Israel, 2200 Yamato Road, Boca Raton 33431. ''He will be missed by those he loved and those who loved him.''
