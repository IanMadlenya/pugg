
        BARNES-Jack Kellers, 76, former VP of Dan River Mills died Jan 17, 2001 in Spartanburg, SC. A member of the Princeton Club in NY. He was a W.W. II veteran with the 100th Div. of the 7th Army, receiving the Purple Heart. Services were held in Spartanburg. Survived by wife, Margaret, daughter Mary Jo, sons Jack K. and Peter J., grand-daughter Abigail O.
