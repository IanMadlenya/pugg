
        THOMPSON - R. Patrick.The Board of Directors and Staff of Hamilton Madison House mourn the untimely passing of R. Patrick Thompson, husband of its Board Vice President, Debra Thompson. Pat was a loyal and generous friend to the House. He will be sorely missed. We send our deepest sympathy to Debbie, Jackie and the rest of the Thompson family. Joseph P. Ritorto, President Frank P. Modica, Exec Director
