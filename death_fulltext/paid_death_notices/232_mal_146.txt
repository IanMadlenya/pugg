
        HOUSTON -- Douglas W., Ph.D. of Bronxville. Husband of Rose, father of Alan. Dr. Houston received his Doctorate in Modern European History from the University of Pennsylvania. He was a Fullbright scholar, U.S. Dept. of State historian and Professor of History at Fordham University for 28 years. He served in the U.S. Army in both World War II and the Korean War. Memorial Gathering at, Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, Friday, May 19, 6-8 PM.
