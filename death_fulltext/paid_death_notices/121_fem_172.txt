        SIM0NS-Joyce Fields. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Joyce Fields Simons, beloved wife of Robert Simons, and devoted mother of Richard Simons and the late Judith Fields Alviani. Mrs. Simons helped to raise millions of dollars over 34 years, through her work on the Bridge Tournament Committee. To Robert, Richard, and the entire family, may you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
