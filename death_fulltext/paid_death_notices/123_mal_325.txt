
        CASRIEL-Carol, on April 27, after a courageous 29 year battle with Parkinsons disease. Beloved husband of 42 years of Tamara (nee Rippner Casriel). Darling father of Laurie Casriel, Cathy Gyory, and Eric Casriel. Esteemed father-inlaw of Andrew Gyory. Adored grandfather (Papa Carl) of Madeleine, William, and Lila Gyory. Funeral services will be Tuesday, April 29, 1997, at 2:00 PM from the Bloomfield-Cooper Jewish Chapels, 2130 Hwy 35, Ocean, NJ. Committal services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Jewish Family & Childrens Services, 705 Summerfield Avenue, Asbury Park, NJ.
        CASRIEL-Carl. Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mr. Carl Casriel. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
        CASRIEL-Carl. The Board of Trustees of Jewish Family & Children's Service of Greater Monmouth County, N.J. records with deep sadness the death of our dear friend and benefactor, Carl Casriel, and extends our heartfelt sympathy to his family and friends.
