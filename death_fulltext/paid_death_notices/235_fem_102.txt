
        SHAMASH--Diane, founding director of Minetta Brook, died at home on Sunday, August 13. She will be waked at home, 96 Remsen Street, Brooklyn, NY, on Tuesday, August 15 from 1-4 p.m. A service will follow at 7:30 p.m. She will be buried on Wednesday, August 16, at 11:30 a.m. at Cedar Lawn Cemetery in East Hampton, NY. She is survived by her husband Joseph Bartscherer, her son Max Joseph, and her sister Beba Shamash. Donations may be sent to Minetta Brook. www.minettabrook.org.
