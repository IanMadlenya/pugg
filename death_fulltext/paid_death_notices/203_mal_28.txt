
        BAKER--William F., of Troy N.Y. on December 1, 2003 at age 90 at home in Londonderry, VT. Devoted husband of Helen (nee Foley). Beloved father of Ellen Thorner, Susan Carlucci, and Edward T. Also survived by six grandchildren, two great grandchildren, a brother Edward of Alabama and many nieces and nephews. Mr. Baker graduated from Manhattan College in 1933. He was the Controller and Director of Corporate Development for Cluett Peabody & Co. He retired in 1976. Memorials may be made to the Mountain Valley Medical Center in Londonderry and the Vermont Visiting Nurses Association of Chester whose care and devotion was vital in helping us provide a loving and peaceful environment for him over these past few years.
