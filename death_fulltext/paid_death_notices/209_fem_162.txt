
        KANTROWITZ -- Deborah (nee Starr) of Fort Lee, NJ and Palm Beach, Fla died on Monday, June 21, 2004, after a long illness. She is survived by her beloved husband, S. Edward Kantrowitz: children Pam & Matt Gladstein, Lori & Chuck Ludmer, Jamie & Mark Lorberbaum; grandchildren, Nicole, Barry, Scott, Carly & Matt; sister, Judy Smith; brother & sister-in-law Stanley & Iris Starr; Penny, Peaches & the late Boo. She was a member of Brandeis, Hadassah and the National Council of Jewish Women. In lieu of flowers, donations to either Hospice of Englewood Hospital or Sloan Kettering Hospital would be appreciated. Services are today, 10:00am at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
