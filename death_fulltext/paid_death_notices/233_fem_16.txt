
        WENDER--Sylvia, age 83, formerly of Forest Hills, NY, passed away on May 22, 2006. She was pre-deceased by her beloved husband Leonard and is survived by her sons Stephen (Sharon) of Plantation, FL and Toby of Atlanta, GA; grandchildren Alison, Matthew and Jaime Wender of Plantation, and her sister Sara of New York City. Services were held. Donations in Sylvia's memory may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
        $(6$)WENDER--Sylvia. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Sylvia S. Wender, associated with The Times from July 1969 until retiring in March 1990.
