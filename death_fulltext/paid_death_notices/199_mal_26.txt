
        RAISBECK--Edward A., Jr., age 98, on July 28, 2003 at Stamford, CT, after a short illness. He was a 1926 graduate of Dartmouth College, World War II veteran (Col., U.S. Army and recipient of Legion of Merit), and retired Senior Partner of Thorndike Deland & Associates, with over 60 years service in retail executive recruiting industry. Loving husband of the late Olivia, he will be greatly missed by his sister, and his three children, nine grandchildren and eleven great-grandchildren. A private service will be held at a future date. >>AD#
