
        FREEDMAN--Dr. James O. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member, James O. Freedman. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. George Asch, Secretary
