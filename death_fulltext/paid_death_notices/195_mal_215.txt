
        STEIN-Bernard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Bernard Stein, a cherished friend admired for his extraordinary generosity and devotion to the New York Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Marion; their children, Rob and Ellen; and their entire family. Larry Zicklin President Morris W. Offit Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay Exec. VP & CEO
