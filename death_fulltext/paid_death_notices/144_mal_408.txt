
        LANDAUER-Walter E. Passed away peacefully on Saturday, January 23, 1999, after a courageous battle with the after-effects of a stroke. Beloved companion of Prudence Opperman, loving father of Megan Landauer, and survived by his brother, Rolf Landauer. Graduated Cooper Union School of Engineering in 1947. In 1973 he founded Purvis Systems Inc, which he headed until his stroke in 1995. Funeral services Tuesday, 10:30 AM, at Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 West 83 St, New York, NY.
