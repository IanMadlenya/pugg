
        KRAVER-Richard M. The Stern School of Business at New York University has lost a close friend and alumnus, Richard M. Kraver, who received his undergraduate degree in 1968. He was an active member of the Stern Alumni Association Board of Directors, having joined the Board in 1987, and was instrumental in helping the School build a strong alumni network that today is the foundation for the School's global status as a leading school of management education. Mr. Kraver founded the law firm of Kraver & Levy and was generous in offering his professional expertise to fellow graduates seeking a legal career. He received his law degree from Syracuse University in 1971 and appeared in ''Who's Who in American Law.'' Richard Kraver was born in Brooklyn in 1946 and is survived by his wife, Harriet, and sons, Barry and Michael.
