
        SILBERMAN--Rosalie. Died July 16, 2005, after a lengthy illness. She was born in New York City. She married Alfred Silberman on April 6, 1941. She is survived by her two children, Karen Scott, News Director of WPIX TV, Alan Silberman, a guidance counselor from Baltimore, MD, along with a daughter-in-law, Ellen, three grandchildren, Paul, Evan, and Charles, and several nieces and nephews. Memorial services will be at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 Street & Amsterdam Avenue in Manhattan, 1:15 P.M., Wednesday, July 20, 2005. She will be buried next to her husband, Colonel Alfred Silberman in Arlington National Cemetery, August 10 at 1:30 P.M.
