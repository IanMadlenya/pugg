
        BENENSON--Marjorie L. She died in Deerfield Beach, FL. May 6, 2007, age 93. Mother of Richard Benenson and the late Karen Allinson, motherin-law of Thea Benenson and Richard Allinson, sister of Monroe Lipton. She also leaves four grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. After a long and healthy life our Auntie Mame has died. Memorial services at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave and 81st St. on Friday, May 11 at 1pm. Reception following at the home of Richard and Thea Benenson.
