
        STONE-Irving. The Roshei Yeshiva, Presidium, and Board of Governors of Beth Medrash Govoha, Lakewood, New Jersey, mourn the passing of longtime friend and philanthropist, Mr. Irving Stone. His loss is a grievous blow to our entire community. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Mrs. Helen Stone; and to his children, Neil Stone, Judy and Morry Weiss, Myrna and Dr. Harold Tatar, Hensha and Rabbi Hirsch Gansbourg. May their furtherance of his sterling legacycharity, support of Torah study, love of his people, and dedication to humanitarian needs on a global scale-elevate his eternal soul, and generate ongoing merit. May the Al-mighty comfort his family among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem, and may his memory be blessed. Rabbi Aryeh Malkiel Kotler-Dean Abraham Biderman, Yussi Rieder, Gedaliah Weinberger-Presidium
