
        REZAK--Anne, 93, concluded her exemplary life peacefully on April 22 and was buried on April 23. Widow of Nathan W. Rezak, beloved mother and family matriarch, her unconditional love and unfailing optimism will be missed by her children Ira Rezak, Joan Sadinoff-Katz, and Lawrence Rezak, their spouses Brigitte Miriam Bedos-Rezak, Henry A. Katz, and Linda Rezak, her eleven grandchildren, 27 great - grandchildren, and many friends.
