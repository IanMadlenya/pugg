
        GANGONE-Paula M. (Cucinello), 69, of Ramsey, N.J. formerly of Monsey, N.Y. on December 11, 2000. Beloved wife of Joseph A. for 45 years. Loving mother of Joe D. Gangone of Mahwah, N.J., Yvonne Ingui of Sugarloaf, N.Y., Paul Gangone of Suffern, N.Y. and the late Stephen Gangone (1981). Devoted sister of John Cucinello of Monsey, N.Y. Cherished grandmother of Justin, Stephen A., Joseph, and Stephen G. She was the Executive Vice President of United Publishers Library Services in Ramsey, N.J. for 20+ years. She was also a former member of Sacred Heart R.C. Church in Suffern, N.Y. A funeral mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Friday December 15, 2000 at 10 A.M. at Sacred Heart R.C. Church in Suffern, N.Y. Interment to follow at Ascension Cemetery, Monsey, N.Y.
