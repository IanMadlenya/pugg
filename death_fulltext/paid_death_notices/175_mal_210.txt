
        DANES-Peter. Beloved husband and father, died on August 20, 2001 at the age of 74. Peter was born in Czechoslovakia, but was forced to leave during World War II. He came to America as a young man in search of a better life. Peter went on to graduate from both Harvard and Harvard Business School and enjoyed a successful career on Wall Street. He was loved and respected by all who knew him, and will be deeply missed. He is survived by his wife, Liliana, and his son, Ari. We love you and will never forget you. Memorial services will be held on August 22, 2001 at 6 P.M. at Riverside Memorial, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
