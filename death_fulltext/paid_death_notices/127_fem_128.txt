
        FRANCIS-Rose J. Keating. Cherished and beloved wife of Joseph Henry Francis died unexpectedly, Wednesday, August 20, 1997. She was a passionate student of life, people, places and things, a compassionate and caring teacher, a leader of many causes that bettered the lives of children and their families. Her husband, children; Jo, Barbara, Judi, Louise, Paul, Kerry and their spouses, thirteen grandchildren, brothers, sisters-in-law and countless relatives and friends spanning the globe wish her well on her next journey. Memorial service at 4 P.M., on Saturday, August 23 at The Pasadena Presbyterian Church, St. Petersburg, Florida.
