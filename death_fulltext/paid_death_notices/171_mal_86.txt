
        GOODMAN-Albert. Of Hartsdale, New York. On April 5, 2001. Age 77. Survived by his loving wife Janet, his adoring children Mindy, Steffi and Scott, 2 grandchildren Maksim and Elias and one sister Doris Bonem. A graduate of New York University and served in the U.S. Army in World War II. Past President of Colonial Dye Works in the Bronx and, most recently, Houlihan-Lawrence as a real estate broker in White Plains. Services will be held Sunday at 11AM at Ferncliff Crematory, Secor Road, Hartsdale, N.Y. Contributions in his memory may be made to Gilda's Club of Westchester, 8 Maple Avenue, White Plains, N.Y. 10601 or the Elmsford Animal Shelter, Warehouse Lane South, Elmsford, New York 10523.
