
        TUNICK-Mildred S. At the age of 91. A graduate of Hunter College and holder of graduate degrees from New York University and the Sorbonne, she taught art at Hunter High School for 15 years. Wife of the late H. Sol Tunick, she is survived by her son Jonathan and his wife Leigh, her daughter Alice and her husband Richard Friedman, two grandchildren and a legion of devoted nieces, nephews, cousins and friends. Memorial service Thursday July 6, 2000 at 3:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
