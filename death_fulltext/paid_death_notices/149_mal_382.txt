
        FRANK-George W. The board and staff of Hospice Care Network express their profound sorrow upon the loss of their longtime supporter, mentor and friend. A pioneer in regional hospice care, Mr. Frank was a founding member and first president of Hospice Care of Long Island, which later became HCN. He will be remembered for his wisdom, compassion and sincere concern for the welfare of others. Our deepest condolences to the family. Maureen Hinkelman, CEO John King, Chairman of the Board The board and staff of Hospice Care Network
