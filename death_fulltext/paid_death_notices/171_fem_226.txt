
        STEERE-Dorothy Osborne, 92. Died at her residence in Bronxville on April 23. She was known as the ''First Lady'' of the New York Botanical Garden, where her late husband, William Campbell Steere, Sr., was President from 1958 to 1972. Mrs. Steere served on the executive board of the Garden. A celebration of the life of Dorothy Osborne Steere and Eucharist will be held on Sunday, April 29th at 10 A.M. at Christ Church, Bronxville, her parish church for over forty years. Survivors include her son, William C. Steere, Jr. of Rye, New York and her daughter, Mrs. Joseph Coulombe (Alice) of Pasadena, California, ten grandchildren and eleven great grandchildren. Private committal service will be held in Ann Arbor, Michigan. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Christ Church, 17 Sagamore Road, Bronxville, New York 10708, or the New York Botanical Garden, 200th Street and Kazimiroff Boulevard, Bronx, N.Y. 10458.
