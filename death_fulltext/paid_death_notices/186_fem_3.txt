
        McLEES-Mary W. On June 28, 2002. Aged 98 years. Loving sister of the late Msgr. Archibald W. McLees, and the late William J. McLees. Graduate of Adelphi University, class of 1924. Retired teacher for 40 years at Richmond Hill High School, her alma mater. Funeral Tuesday morning. For information: The LeahyMcDonald Funeral Home, Richmond Hill, New York.
