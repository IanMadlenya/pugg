
        DEMAREST-Leanne Hansen, on May 5, 2003, into her sixty-fourth year. Survived by her husband Kenneth L. Demarest Jr.; her sons, William H. Taft and Timothy P. Taft; & her brother, Stephen C. Hansen. Educated at the Lenox School, Briarcliff College & Columbia University. She was a resident of New Vernon, NJ. She was active with Seeing Eye, Inc. and the Neighborhood House, both of Morristown, NJ. A Memorial Service will be held at the First Presbyterian Church of New Vernon, NJ on Saturday, May 10, 11:00 AM. Interment at Locust Valley Cemetery, Long Island is private. In lieu of flowers, those who wish may contribute in her memory to the Neighborhood House, 12 Flagler Street, Morristown, New Jersey 07960. For information call Wm. A. Bradley & Son, Funeral Directors, Chatham, New Jersey.
