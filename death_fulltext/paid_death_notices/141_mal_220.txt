
        LEVENBERG-Irving. Of Huntington, New York, suddenly on Friday, October 16th, 1998 while on holiday in Turkey. Adored husband of Thelma & the late Evelyn. Beloved father of Seth (Ellen), Merrit (Betty) & the late Warren. Much loved by his brothers Fred, George and Phil, his step children Jonathan and David Rothstein and Laura Levine. Adored by all of his grandchildren, Ivy, Scott, Sari, Erica, Jason, Michael, Justin, Alyssa, Allie, Jake, Lara, and Kaela. Loved by all his friends and respected by his many clients, he will be sorely missed.
