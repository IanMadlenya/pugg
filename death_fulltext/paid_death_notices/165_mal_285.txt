
        SIMONS-Alexander. Of Palm Harbor, FL. Died October 19 in Safety Harbor, FL. Born in New York City, Mr. Simons moved to Florida in 1964. An entrepreneur, he was in retail furniture sales in NY, an importer and was a builder and developer in Pinellas County, FL. Surviving family includes his son Steven Simons, Beldon, MS; daughter Rosalind Steinmetz, Palo Alto, CA; companion Gwen Huising, Palm Harbor; seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Funeral services and entombment in Palm Harbor, FL. Call Curlew Hills Funeral Home for more information.
