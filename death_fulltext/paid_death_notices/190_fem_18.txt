
        CAVE-Natalie Helene Jacobs. The famiily sorrowfully announces her passing on October 29, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Vernal G. Cave, M.D., she was a long term resident and community activist in Brooklyn. A 50year employee of the Veterans' Administration, she retired as Chief of Social Work Services. She is survived by her sisters Frankie Gillette and Marie Jones, her brother A. Taylor Jacobs and a host of beloved family members. A memorial service will be held November 9, 2002, 11 AM at James United Methodist Church, 660 Monroe St., Brooklyn, NY.
