
        DWYER--Jane Estelle Broadus (Hudson). Dancer, actress, teacher, garden grower, wife, lover, mother, grandmother, and creative force extraordinaire. Died of cancer in Pittsfield, MA on Monday morning, April 26, 2004, two days short of her sixtyfifth birthday. Her loss is mourned and her life celebrated by her husband, Jim, of Stephentown, NY; her daughters, Peri and Karli; their husbands, Michael Luse, and Paul-Hector Gannon; her grandchildren, Kyrie, Amanda, Maya, Benjamin, and Orion; her brother, John Broadus, and his family, of Daphne, AL; and innumerable others whose lives she touched and enriched. ''Out, out, brief candle.''
