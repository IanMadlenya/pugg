
        RORKE-Jacqueline (nee Billingsley), 72, of Hackensack, NJ, formerly of Manhattan NY and Hollywood FL on October 25, 1998. She was the daughter of Sherman and Hazel Billingsley, owners of the Stork Club in New York City. Mrs. Rorke had attended the Spence School and graduated from Finch College, both located in New York. A former actress, Mrs. Rorke had also spent some time as a Real Estate broker. She is survived by her two sons, Alexander III of Evanston, IL, Brian of Haverhill, MA, a daughter, Hazel Rorke of Hackensack, NJ, two sisters, Barbara Mohler of Scottsdale, AZ and Shermane Billingsley of New York City. She is also survived by three grandchildren. She had been pre-deceased by her husband, Alexander, Jr. in 1963. A graveside service will be held on Tuesday, October 27 at 11:15 am at the Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, NY. For further information contact C. C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 201-445-0344
