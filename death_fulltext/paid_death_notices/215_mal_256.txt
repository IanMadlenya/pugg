
        WILDER--David E., Ph.D. On December 22, 2004, of Pound Ridge, NY. Faculty of Columbia University and NYS Psychiatric Institute. Beloved husband of Priscilla Bollard Wilder.         WILDER--David E., Ph.D. We mourn the death and honor the memory of our friend and colleague, David E. Wilder.
