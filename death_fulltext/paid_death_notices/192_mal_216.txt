
        AGNEW-George Bliss Jr. Of Kennebunkport, ME, and Vero Beach, FL, died Monday January 13, 2003, at his home in Vero Beach. He was born in Mt. Kisco, NY on September 9, 1909, son of George Bliss and Emily Dutilh Agnew. He graduated from Haverford in 1928, and Princeton University in 1932, with a degree in psychology. He worked at The Pentagon during World War II. After the war and work on Wall Street, NY, he became Vice President of The Putnam Trust Company in Greenwich, CT. He is survived by his wife, Phyllis Boven Agnew; his children, George Bliss Agnew III, of North Stonington, CT, Thomas Gibb Agnew, of Big Timber, MT, and Emily Dutilh Agnew, of Santa Monica, CA; his sister Madelaine Agnew Dykema; his brother David Paton Agnew; five grandchildren and one great-grandchild. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Vero Beach Visiting Nurse Association, 1111 36th Street, Vero Beach, FL. 32960, or to The Indian River Memorial Hospital Foundation, 1000 36th St, Vero Beach, FL 32960.
