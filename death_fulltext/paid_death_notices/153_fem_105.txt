
        STEWART-Sylvia Lichtenstein. March 21, 1914 to October 8, 1999. Formerly of Roslyn, New York. Lovingly remembered by her brother, Harold Lichtenstein, her sons, Carl and Mark, daughter-inlaw, Lori, grandchildren, Rachel, Kim, Ian, and Lily, great grandchildren and other loving family and friends. Memorial Service will be held on Saturday, October 30, 1999, at 4 P.M. at Finnerty & Stevens Funeral Home, 426 Main Street, Great Barrington, MA.
