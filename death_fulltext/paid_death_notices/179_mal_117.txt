
        SCHWARTZ-Meyer. On December 7, 2001. Meyer Schwartz, a true New Yorker, died in San Francisco. An attorney admired by his colleagues, he loved the law and became General Counsel of Nikon Inc. A loving and beloved husband, father, grandfather, great-grandfather and uncle, we will miss his wit, integrity and gentleness. He is survived by daughter Susan Zegans, grandsons Marc and Michael Zegans and great-grandchildren, Molly, Rebecca, Max, Daniel and Kate. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM, at Riverside, West 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
