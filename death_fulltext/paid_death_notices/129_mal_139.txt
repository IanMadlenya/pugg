
        SIMON-Joseph H. Much loved by his family, died October 11, in his 100th year. He was the husband of Bertha, his wife for 57 years, devoted and loving father of John Simon and Patricia Scheines; fatherin-law to Laura Lee and Morton, adored grandfather of Terri, James, and Andrew Simon; Richard and Elizabeth Scheines and their spouses, Eric Levine, Audrey and Nancy Simon, Martha Harty and Ron Elton, and great grandfather of ten. He is also survived by his sister, Dorothy Fisher, and a host of nieces and nephews. Following a small family service, the family will be at the Scheines home in New York from 3 PM Monday and 4 PM Tuesday, and at the Simon home in Connecticut from 7 PM on Tuesday.
