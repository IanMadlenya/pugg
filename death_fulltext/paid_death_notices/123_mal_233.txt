
        SAFIAN-Dr. Alfred. Age 83 of NYC, died April 18, 1997. He served as a captain in the medical corps during World War II where he received four campaign ribbons and a purple heart. He worked as an anesthesiologist from 1948-1976. The last hospitals in which he worked where Elmhurst General and as an Associate Professor in the Mt. Sinai School of Medicine. After retiring, he worked for the Social Security Administration. He was the beloved husband of the late Dorothy Lazarus Safian. He leaves behind his brother Murray, his two sons Robert and Lee and his daughter-in-law Mariuza. Funeral services will be held April 20, at 12:15 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St, NYC.
