
        FORCE--Elizabeth Sculthorp, January 23rd. Born March 25th, 1902, in Sea Bright, NJ. Pioneer in family-life education. Beloved teacher in Toms River; author, lecturer, consultant, world traveler. Director of family-life programs for the American Social Health Association. Onetime president of National Council on Family Relations. Coordinated with UN for 20 years. Survived by grandnephew, Allan Terhune, and his children, Allan Jr. and Kristen. Memorial service February 17th at 11am, Grace Church, 10th and Broadway.
