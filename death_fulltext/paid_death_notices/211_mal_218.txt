
        BURKE--Russell Ely, II. 83, of Richmond, VA, passed away Sunday, August 22, 2004. He was preceded in death by his wife, Theo Welles Burke. He is survived by his children, Martha Deprez of Portland ME; Russell E. Burke, III of New York, N.Y.; Laurance G. Burke of Richmond, VA.; seven grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Born in Plainfield, N.J., Mr. Burke attended Taft School and Yale University. A memorial service will be held September 10 at 11 A.M. in Hillside Cemetery in Scotch Plains, N.J. In lieu of flowers, please consider contributions to the American Cancer Society.
