
        CLARIN-Eric B. MD. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the untimely death of Dr. Eric B. Clarin. Dr. Clarin was associated with Maimonides Medical Center as a Pediatric Pathologist. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. Salvatore DeGliuomini, MD, Pres. Steven Farber, MD, Pres. Elect Herbert Lehman, MD, Secretary Ira Mayer, MD, Treasurer
