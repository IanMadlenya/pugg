
        TAYLOR-Malcolm P.. of Darien, CT on January 11, 2000 in Darien, CT. He was founder of the TaylorReed Corporation in 1939 with college classmate Charles M.D. Reed in Mamaroneck, NY. Husband of the late Marianne Bevan Taylor. Father of Mick, Scott, Mark, Barbara and Deborah. Funeral Services will be held at the Noroton Presbyterian Church, 2011 Post Road, Darien, CT on Friday, January 14, 2000 at 1:00 PM. A private interment will be held at Spring Grove Cemetery, Darien. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be sent to the Stamford Rotary Club Scholarship Foundation, P.O. Box 8026, Stamford, CT 06905.
