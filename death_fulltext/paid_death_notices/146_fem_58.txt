
        STEVENS-Rosemary, M.D. Of Sarasota, Florida. A retired Optomologist. Widow of John Martin Stevens, formerly of New York, N.Y. Passed away in her sleep on 1 March 1999 after a lengthy illness. She was born in Berlin, Germany as Rose Marie Czellitzer, on 23 December 1907. Surviving are sons Michael (Marilyn) and Thomas (Marny), five grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held Friday, 5 March, noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York, New York.
