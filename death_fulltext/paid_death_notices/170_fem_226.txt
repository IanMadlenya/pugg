
        RIVES-Wendy. The faculty and staff of NYU School of Medicine and the Department of Psychiatry note with great sorrow the passing of Wendy Rives, M.D., attending physician and Director of the Comprehensive Psychiatric Emergency Program at Bellevue Hospital Center. A gifted clinician, Dr. Rives was also an inspiring leader, mentor and teacher. Her legacy of wisdom, wit, force and clinical savvy will not be forgotten. We extend our deepest condolences to her family and loved ones. Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean NYU School of Medicine Robert Cancro, M.D., Chairman Department of Psychiatry NYU School of Medicine Manuel Trujillo, M.D. Director of Psychiatry Bellevue Hospital Center
