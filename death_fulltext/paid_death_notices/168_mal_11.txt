
        GELLERT-Peter Joseph. Died on Dec. 23, 2000 from complications resulting from aplastic anemia. Born on August 6, 1926 to Greta (Petschek) Gellert and Oswald R. Gellert in Prague, Czechoslovakia, he attended The Lawrenceville School, Harvard College, and Yale Law School. He served in the US Army during World War II, including a tour in the Pacific theater. For over 40 years, he was a senior executive at United Continental Corporation, managing the affairs of the Gellert & Petschek families. He was an amateur radio enthusiast, using the call sign W2WSS & published a monthly newsletter. He is survived by his wife, Anna (Monser) Gellert; his children, Alexander G. and Anthony E.; his brothers, Martin F., Michael E., and Hubert J.; his sister, Catherine G. Ross; his grandson, Alexander G.; and numerous nephews, nieces, & cousins.
