
        NEMEROFF-Frances ''Birdie''. Died peacefully January 18, 2002 in Naples, Florida shortly before her 93rd birthday. She was predeceased by her husband Bernard G. Nemeroff and survived by her children Gladys Agell (Bertil), Susan Cohen, Michael Nemeroff (Sharon), Karen Cohn (Martin) and her eleven grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. A graveside service will be Sunday January 20 at 1pm at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, LI.
