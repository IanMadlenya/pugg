
        SCHWARTZ--Rose, 97, formerly of Far Rockaway, NY, North Miami Beach, and Boca Raton, FL, passed away peacefully on August 11, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Moe Schwartz. Rose was chairman of the United Parents Association of the Rockaways and past President of the City of Hope of North Miami Beach for 12 years. She was loved and will be greatly missed for her wisdom and strength. She is survived by her loving sons Mark (Hally) Shaw and Stephen (Susan) Schwartz, and cherished grandchildren Jared (Candace) Shaw and Scott (Elizabeth) Shaw, greatgrandchildren Harleigh, Melanie, Zachary, Brooke, and Harrison Shaw. Contributions may be made in her memory to: City of Hope or Boca Friends for Cancer Research. Services will be private.
