
        SELKE-Jack L. Of Aventura, Florida, on July 10, 1998 devoted husband to Esther for 64 years; beloved father to Gerald & Marlene, Marilyn & Phil, Marjorie & David; cherished grandfather to eleven, great grandfather to nine; caring brother to Lee and Esther. He is a graduate of New York University, and retired as the Financial Controller of Macy's. Mr. Selke is Past Corporate Vice President and Treasurer of Uni Shops, Former Vice President of Central State Bank and Gran Way, Past President of Madison Jewish Center of Brooklyn, N.Y. and Past Treasurer of Aventura Jewish Center, FL. May his name be for a blessing. Services are at Temple Israel, Glengary Road, Croton on Hudson, on Monday, July 13th, 1998, at 1:00 P.M.
