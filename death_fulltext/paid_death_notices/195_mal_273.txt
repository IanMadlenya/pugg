
        BEIER-Michael P. Of Manhattan (formerly of Massapequa), on April 25, 2003, of ALS (Lou Gehrig's Disease), at the age of 39. He was a loving husband and father to his wife Theresa and his children Carly, 6, and Dustin, 4. Beloved son of Robert and Kathleen. Dear brother of Bob, Susan, Joseph and Kathleen. After his diagnosis, Michael became involved with the MDA and was named a national vice president of that organization. He worked tirelessly to raise public awareness about ALS and to raise funds for research to find a cure. Michael was a Director of Equity Trading for Credit Suisse First Boston in Manhattan and enjoyed a Wall Street career for 18 years. He leaves many family members and friends to remember his wonderful spirit for life and his generous nature that never waned even in the face of a terrible disease. He will be sorely missed by us all. The family will receive friends Sunday and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Services will be held Tuesday, 10:30 AM, at Unitarian Church of All Souls, 1157 Lexington Avenue at 80th Street. Michael asked that in lieu of flowers donations may be sent to MDA Wings Over Wall Street, 1140 Avenue of the Americas, Suite 1801, New York, New York 10036. wingsoverwallstreet.org
        BEIER-Michael. The Rosa Mexicano Restaurant community mourns the loss of Michael Beier. His wisdom, generosity and humor will be deeply missed but his memory will continue to be an inspiration to all of us. Our prayers are with his wife Theresa, children Carly and Dustin, and all of his family. The Shareholders and Staff of Rosa Mexicano
