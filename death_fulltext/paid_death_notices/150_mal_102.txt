
        BEHA-James J. Loving husband, father and grandfather, died July 3, at United Hospital. He was 82 years old. His family includes his devoted wife of 51 years, Macy Ann, his son James A. Beha II and his wife Nancy, his daughter Ann Beha and her husband Robert A. Radloff. He is also survived by his grandchildren Mary Alice, James J. and Christopther R. Beha, and Macy and Allison Radloff. Mr. Beha graduated from Williams College and the Harvard Law School. He was a senior partner at the law firm of Gasser & Hayes, and served as trustee of numerous charitable and civic organizations in New York City including the NYU Medical Center. Mr. Beha was a Knight of Malta, and a member of The Order of The Holy Sepulchre. He was also a loyal member of The Church of the Resurrection in Rye, NY. He was also a member of the American Yacht Club. Friends may call Tuesday, July 6, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. Mass of Christian Burial will be Wednesday, July 7, 11 AM, at The Church of the Resurrection. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Church of the Resurrection, 910 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY 10580, or Constableville Public Library, Constableville, NY 13325.
