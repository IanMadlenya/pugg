
        BADE-John von Benten, 88, died peacefully on May 9, 1998. He loved life and was beloved by his family and friends. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Dorothea, his daughters Lorna Goodman and Lisa Cermak, their husbands, Edwin Goodman and Frank Cermak, his beloved grandchildren Sara and Thea Goodman, Jon Adams and Noah Bade Cermak. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Portledge School, Duck Pond Road, Locust Valley, New York, 11560. Memorial service, Monday, May 11, 6:00 PM., Reformed Church of Locust Valley, 115 Ryefield Road, Locust Valley, New York.
