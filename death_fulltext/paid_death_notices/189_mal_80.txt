
        CLIVE-Robert B. On October 4, 2002, at Albany, NY. Formerly of NYC. Mr. Clive had been the art director of Life magazine, as well as the art director of Time magazine. He was an art director at Ogilvy Mather in NYC, and the art director of the Daily News Magazine. Loving companion of Ellen Kostroff. Father of Peter, David, Paul and Adam Clive, and husband of their mother Milla Clive. Brother of Herb Cohen and Lea Dalvin. A memorial service to be held at Congregation Beth Shalom, Clifton Park, NY. For information contact the Levine Memorial Chapel, Albany, NY.
