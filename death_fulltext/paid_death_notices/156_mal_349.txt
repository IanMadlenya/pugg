
        ELFMAN-Sidney. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member, Sidney Elfman. Sidney and his beloved wife, Doris, have been members of Rodeph Sholom for over thirty years. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his wife, his daughter, Lois, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
