
        GINSBURG--David Mark. On December 8, 2005, age 70, of River Vale, NJ, and East Hampton, NY, formerly of Newton, MA, and Oberlin College. Beloved husband of Susan, devoted father of daughters Lisa and Wendy, son-inlaw Avi, and adoring grandfather of Jacob. Above all else, he was the rock of his family. He will live in our hearts forever. Burial will be private. Family will receive guests on Monday, 1-9 pm, at 505 Dorchester Dr, River Vale, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made in his name to Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center, PO Box 27106, New York, NY 10087.
