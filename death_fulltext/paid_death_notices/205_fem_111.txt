
        D'AURIAC--Mme. Gladys M. Hartnett. Of Larchmont, NY, died February 8th, 2004 at the age of 79. She was born in Paris, France on December 4th, 1924. Mrs. Hartnett, also known as, Mme. D'Auriac, taught at Convent of the Sacred Heart in Greenwich, CT for 27 years. While at Sacred Heart, Mrs. Hartnett was innovative in the teaching of her native tongue. Her crowning achievement was being appointed Head of the Language Department. She has been described as inspirational, captivating and loyal by all who knew and worked with her. She was loved by many and will be missed by all. A fund has been created in her memory. Donations can be sent to The Michelle D'Auriac Fund, c/o Convent of the Sacred Heart, 1177 King St, Greenwich CT 06831.
