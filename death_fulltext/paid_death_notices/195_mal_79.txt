
        HAESEKER-Donald H. Age 81, of Glen Allen, Virginia, died Sunday, April 6, 2003. He was retired from Chubb & Son Insurance Company after 45 years in the Surety Department. Survivors include his wife, Beryl Barbuti Haeseker; daughter, Jane H. Sketchley and her husband, Garsed; son, Thomas P. Haeseker and his wife, Karen; grandchildren, Andrew B. and Gregory B. Jernigan, and Joshua T. and Rebecca P. Haeseker. A memorial service will be conducted 2 p.m. Saturday at the Parham Chapel, Woody Funeral Home, 1771 Parham Road, Richmond, Va. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the American Heart Association or the American Lung Association.
