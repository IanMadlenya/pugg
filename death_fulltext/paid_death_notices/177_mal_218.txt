
        CUSIMANO-James M. September 17, 2001 at age 58. Beloved husband of Beverly (nee Haferbier) for 36 years. Preceded in death by his parents Salvatore and Elsie (nee Piper) and by brother Charles and sister Arlene Fredrichsdorf. Jim was a graduate ABD of University of Wisconsin, Madison. His work was in public relations and freelance writing. He is survived by brother David, sisters Angeline Hacherian and Marion Johnson, many nieces, nephews and a host of friends. Graveside services were held privately in Milwaukee. A celebration of his life will be held at Downtime, 251 West 30 St, NYC between 7 and 9PM on October 24. Please contribute in Jim's name to your favorite charity.
