
        TOMBACK--Lester. The Officers, Board of Governors and members of Metropolis Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed Honorary Life Member, Lester Tomback, who died at the age of 98 and extend our heartfelt condolences to his son and daughter-in-law, Peter and Roberta Tomback, his daughter Elinor, his grandchildren Andrew and Melissa and his family. Richard D. Katcher, Pres. Neda Birbrower, Secy.
