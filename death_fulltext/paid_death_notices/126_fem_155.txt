
        HUTTON-Maridean Watt, of New York City, died in Edina, MN, on July 13, 1997. Survived by mother Christine Watt and sister Carolyn Reynolds. Meridean taught art in Yonkers, NY. In recent years she created a program for children at the Museum of American Folk Art in NYC. Memorial service to be held at the Cremation Society of MN, Edina Chapel at 2 PM, July 26th. Memorials preferred to the Museum of American Folk Art in New York City.
