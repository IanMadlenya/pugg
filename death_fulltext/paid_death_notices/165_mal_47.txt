
        WEINBERG-Samuel. Peacefully, in his sleep on October 1, 2000. Born March 28, 1908, son of Bertha Katz and Morris Weinberg. Attorney-at-law, senior partner at Sperry, Weinberg & Cohen. Loving husband of the late Mildred Wolton Weinberg. Beloved father of Ladd and the late Michael. Caring father-in-law of Catherine. Adored grandfather of Gillian Ladd and Joanna Weinberg. Devoted brother of Sophie Karp and dear uncle of Marilyn Silverman and Martin Karp. Fond friend of Carol Cohen. We are all richer for having shared his warmth and long life. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81st St. on Tuesday, October 3, 7:00-9:00 PM. Services to be held Wednesday, October 4, 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation, 381 Park Ave. South, New York, NY 10016.
        WEINBERG-Samuel. The New York Chapter of the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation International records with profound sorrow the death of our esteemed and valued friend. We extend our deepest appreciation for his many years of support and dedication to our cause. He will be remembered forever. Our most sincere sympathy to the entire family. Lawrence B. Sorrel, President New York Chapter, JDFI
        WEINBERG-Samuel. Congregation Kol Ami records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member and conveys sincere condolences to his bereaved family. Rabbi Shira Milgrom Rabbi Tom Weiner Ken Simon, President
