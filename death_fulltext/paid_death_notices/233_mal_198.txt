
        INGRAHAM--David Jr. Died suddenly on June 13, 2006, at home in Laurel Hollow, NY, at the age of 62. He was a graduate of Boston University and Columbia University Business School and a former Vice President of Smith Barney and partner at Lafer Amster & Co. He is survived by his mother Laura Jennings Ingraham of Hanover, NH, sister Alice I. Davies, Weston, MA, brothers Frederic B. Ingraham, Laurel Hollow, John W. Ingraham, Tulsa, OK, seven nieces and nephews, and many friends.
