
        GILBERTO--Frances. Age 87, died peacefully in her home in New Port Richey, FL, on February 18. She leaves her beloved husband Pasquale of 62 years, her devoted son Pasquale and daughter-inlaw Linda of Yonkers, NY, cherished grandchildren Christine, David, Richard and Gerard, and dear greatgranddaughter Kayla Marie. She is also survived by her sister Ida Giusto, her many loving nieces, nephews, their families and many caring friends. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Hernando Pasco Hospice, 4422 Grand Blvd, New Port Richey, FL 34652.
