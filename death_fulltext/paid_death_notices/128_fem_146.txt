
        OTTENHEIMER-Lilly, M.D. On September 16, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Julius. Dearest friend of Edith and Mel Smilow, Anne and Frank Weill, Lisa Loose and Eva Hauptman. Respected and loved by her many colleagues, patients and friends. Service is on Friday, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        OTTENHEIMER-Lilly, M.D. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the American Academy of Psychoanalysis mourn the death of Lilly Ottenheimer, MD, Founding Member of the Academy. She was a cherished colleague and teacher and a beloved friend to many. Douglas H. Ingram, President Joseph P. Merlino, MD, MPA, Secretary
