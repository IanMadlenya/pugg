
        RAVICH-Ruth (nee Wohl). Age 80, passed away at her home after a long illness on Friday, August 3rd. Beloved wife of the late Robert A. Ravich, MD. She is survived by her sisters, Jean Zamore and Anne Gold, children Carol and Martin Kempner, William and Elaine Ravich, grandchildren Katie and Francis Trullenque and Martha Kempner, future grandson-in-law Edward Keenan, and great-grandchildren Maxwell and Alexandria Trullenque. Mrs. Ravich founded the Department of Patient Representation at The Mount Sinai Hospital in NYC and was a founding member and first president of the Society of Patient Represenatatives. Funeral service wil be held at 3:30PM on Sunday, August 5th at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Bard College Clemente Course in the Humanities (Bard College, Annandale, NY).
