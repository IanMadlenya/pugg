
        LIEBOWITZ-Jonathan S., Esq. On December 17, 2001. Age 69. Son of the late Sidney and Florence Liebowitz. Cornell University ILR, BS 1954; JD Columbia University 1959. Member N.Y. Bar Association, U.S. Supreme Court, U.S. Court of Appeals and District Court. Served in the U.S. Army. Was an Arbitrator, Special Master, Mediator and Fact Finder. Taught at Rutgers, Pace and Cornell Universities. Member National Academy of Arbitrators and the American Arbitration Association. Authored published articles. His wife, Helen K., daughter Robin K. and son Daniel S. survive. Funeral service Thursday, 2PM, Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, N.Y. Memorial contributions to White Plains Hospital Center, Oncology Unit, 5F Davis Ave, White Plains, N.Y. or WNET Channel 13, 450 W. 33 St, N.Y., N.Y. 10001-2605.
