
        ORLAND--Paul I., 95, a 1933 graduate of the NYU School of Commerce, passed away on Wednesday, April 25. Beloved husband for 62 years of the late Helen Kurtz Orland. Survived by his loving children Laura (Franklin) Adler and Malcolm (Andrea) Orland and cherished grandchildren Amy and Gabrielle Adler and Daniel and Adam Orland. Funeral services will be held Monday in Los Angeles. In lieu of flowers, donations requested to the charity of your choice.
