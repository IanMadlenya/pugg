
        HEYN--Ethel Kenyon. Loving wife of the late Ernest Heyn, adored mother of Dalma and the late Suzy Lochridge. Beloved mother-in-law of Richard Marek and Willard Lochridge and grandmother of Diana, Christie and Julie. Died at home in New York on January 22, 2004 at age 99. Star of movies in the 1930s, Effie's unique beauty and wit endured throughout her life and continue as an indelible memory to her family and friends. Without her radiance, the world is a little darker.
