
        LEVINE-Diane Perlroth. In Stamford, August 23, 2002, of 211 Greens Farms Road, Westport, formerly of New Haven. She was born in New Haven June 7, 1941, and lived a short time in New York City before returning to Connecticut where she spent the past 34 years. She is survived by her husband, Bert, mother Marion Perlroth, of New Haven, a daughter, Alicia of Boston, a son and daughter-inlaw, Stuart and Janine of New York City, and a granddaughter, Isabelle. She was predeceased by her father, William S. Perlroth. She was active in real estate management and brokerage in CT and NY. A funeral service will be held Monday, August 26, 12 noon, at Temple Shalom, 259 Richards Ave., Norwalk, (203866-0148). Interment will be in The Mishkan Israel Cemetery, New Haven. Memorial contributions may be made to The Bennett Cancer Center, c/o Stamford Health Foundation, Stamford Hospital, Stamford, CT 06902. Period of mourning will be observed at the Levine residence in Westport through Wednesday. For further information contact Weller Funeral Home, New Haven, 203-624-6912.
