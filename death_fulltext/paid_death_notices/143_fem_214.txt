
        KATZMAN-Hinda. The Community of West End Synagogue is saddened by the recent death of Hinda Katzman and extends its condolences to her family and friends. Hinda, with her late husband Jack, was a founding member of our synagogue and a long-time ardent Labor Zionist. We will miss her incisive voice during our discussions and her tireless presence at our activities. Rabbi Avi Winokur Rabbi Yael Ridberg Donald Shapiro, President
