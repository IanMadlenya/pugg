
        SHAFIROFF-Dr. Benjamin G.P. Of Brooklyn, NY, on October, 6, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Edith, cherished father of Paul and Edith, David and Carol, Mark and Marsha, Henry & Jean, Naomi, Martha, Laura DeVivo, Diana and John Casalino, Adam and Peggy, & Chia. Father-in-law to the late Jane Shafiroff. He will be dearly missed by his 18 grandchildren and 11 great grandchildren. Served as Lt. Colonel in the United State Army Corp in World War II, 35 years as Chief of Surgery at Caledonian Hospital, and Professor of Anatomy at New York University. Service will be held Tuesday, October, 10, 2000 at 10 A.M. at Parkside Memorial Chapel, Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, New York.
