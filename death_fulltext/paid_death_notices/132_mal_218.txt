
        LIVINGSTONE-Seabourn Scherer (Skid). Beloved husband of Kay Will Livingstone, died suddenly of a heart attack at his home in Hobe Sound, FL at the age of 74, on Wednesday, January 14, 1998. Mr. Livingstone was born in Detroit, MI and graduated from the Berkshire School and Yale University. He was a retired manufacturer. He was an avid golfer and hunter. He is also survived by his stepson and daughter, John Wyckoff Mettler and Susan Salant Wierdsma and numerous grandchildren. A private Memorial Service will be held in the garden of Christ Church on Jupiter Island, FL. For information Aycock Funeral Home, 1112 Military Trail, Jupiter, FL 33458.
