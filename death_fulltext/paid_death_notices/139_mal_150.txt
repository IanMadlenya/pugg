
        PALM-Michael D. The Board, Staff and Volunteers of the Treatment Action Group mourn the passing of Michael Palm. His passion and commitment to focused, intelligent and effective solutions for the AIDS crisis has left an enduring legacy. Michael's visionary generosity helped lay the basis for TAG's growth and development as AIDS research and treatment advocate. We will honor Michael's legacy as we continue fighting for safe, effective AIDS treatments and for the ultimate goals of a cure and a vaccine. We will miss him. Barbara Hughes, President Odell Mays, II, Exec Director Mark Harrington, Senior Policy Director
        PALM-Michael D. The many friends and colleagues of Michael D. Palm at the Zurich Centre Group mourn his passing. Michael will be fondly remembered both as a founder of the Company and as our esteemed business partner. He has made an indelible personal and professional contribution to the Company and to each of us with his keen intelligence, humor, generosity and friendship. The Zurich Centre Group
