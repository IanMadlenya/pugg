
        FEINBERG--Belle W. Age 94, of Lauderhill, Florida. Formerly of Brooklyn, New York. Passed away November 5, 2003. Cherished wife of the late Irving H. Beloved mother of Hon. Michael and Frank, mother-in-law of Marlene and Anita, grandmother of Roberta, Felicia, Andrew, Adam and Sharon. Greatgrandmother of Austin and Travis. A teacher for the NYC Board of Education for 33 years and a graduate of NYU. She will be missed by all. Services at Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1895 Flatbush Avenue (at Avenue L) today at 10:00 am.
