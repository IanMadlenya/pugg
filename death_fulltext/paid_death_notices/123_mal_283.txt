
        BALINT-George, M.D. 72., Physician, internist and anesthesiologist for 47 years, died at New York Hospital April 24, 1997. Dr. Balint devotedly cared for his more than eighty-thousand patients during his lifetime. As a young doctor he worked at the Peterffy Hospital in Budapest, Hungary and at St. Barnabas Medical Center in Attendorn, Germany. In New York City he served at New York University and V.A. Hospital, The Hospital for Special Surgery-Cornell Medical College and at Mt. Sinai Medical Center. Dr. Balint was a charitable human being, a cultured man with great integrity and dignity. He enjoyed music, the fine arts and appreciated the company of his loyal colleagues and friends. He trusted and was grateful to his own caring physicians and medical associates, nurses, throughout his illness until the end of his life. Maria, beloved wife of 37 years, is mourning him. He is survived by his aunt, Nelly Szasz, Budapest, Hungary, his late brother's widow and her daughter Eva Balint of Los Angeles, and Agnes Liebhardt of California. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in the memory of Dr. Balint to Mt. Sinai Medical Center, Department of Anesthesiology and/ or Department of Surgery, 1 Gustave L. Levy Place, New York, New York 10029-6574.
