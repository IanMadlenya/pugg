
        GREENBAUM-Melvin. The Jewish Theological Seminary family records with sorrow the death of Melvin Greenbaum, father of Rabbi Michael Greenbaum, vice chancellor and chief administrative officer of JTS. In addition to his son, he is survived by a daughter, Alyse, his wife Helen, three stepchildren and eight grandchildren. He is predeceased by his first wife, Sylvia. With Jewish community at the center of his life, he was an active member of his congregation, Temple Israel of Daytona Beach, FL. Contributions to JTS. May his memory be for a blessing. Ismar Schorsch, chancellor Gershon Kekst, board chairman
