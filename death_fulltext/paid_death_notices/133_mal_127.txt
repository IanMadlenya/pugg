
        BERGSON-Arnold M. Age 80, of New York, N.Y. died February 9, 1998 in Palm Beach, FL. Survivors include his wife, Jeanne H. of New York, N.Y.; two daughters, Jane Hersh of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL and Barbara McGraw of Riverside, CT; brother, Martin Bergson of Providence, R.I. and three grandchildren. No services.
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Officers and emplopyees of Acordia Northeast deeply mourn the passing of Arnold Bergson, Chairman Emeritus. Arnold was the founding father of the predecessor companies in N.Y. and a great leader. He was a man amongst men and will be truly missed. Our sympathies are extended to his lovely wife Jeanne, his daughters, Jane and Barbara and their families.
