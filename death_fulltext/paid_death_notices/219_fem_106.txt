
        MURPHY--Marion L., 90, died Wednesday in NYC. A resident of Summit, NJ, she was the widow of Joseph F. Murphy, Esq., an insurance executive who was New Jersey Insurance Commissioner. In the early 1940's Mrs. Murphy was private secretary to selfhelp author Dale Carnegie, author of ''How to Win Friends and Influence People.'' Survived by her daughter, Dr. Elizabeth M. Whelan of NYC, sons Kevin B. Murphy of Pottersville, NJ and Brian S. Murphy of Chatham, NJ, and five grandchildren. Services at Doyle's Funeral Home, Morristown, NJ, 8:30AM Saturday, April 16.
