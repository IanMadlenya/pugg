
        FOUGERAY -- Catherine Walsh. Died peacefully March 21, 2004. A resident of Murdock Woods, Mamaroneck, she was the daughter of the late Peter and Catherine Walsh of New Rochelle. With her late husband Louis, she was the co-proprietor of Fougeray Jewelers in Manhattan. Upon retirement, she devoted herself to volunteer work at New Rochelle Public Library. She will be missed by her beloved nieces Elizabeth and Mary Catherine, and nephews Peter, Paul and Gregory, as well as her adoring grandnephew James. Family will receive friends at Fox Funeral Home, 2080 Boston Post Road, Larchmont, on Monday, 7-9 pm, and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10 am, at Sts. John and Paul Church. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name to the Friends of the New Rochelle Public Library.
