
        STEIN--Samuel J. , M.D. On June 8, 2003. Beloved husband of Gladys. Loving father of Rosanne and David. Adoring grandfather of Ilene, Andrew, Heather and Rainbow. Great grandfather of Daniel. Selfless physician for over 70 years. Honored World War II veteran and amateur artist. Admired by everyone who knew him. Service Sunday 2 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
