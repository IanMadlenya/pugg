
        OPERT--Sam J. 96, Palm Beach County, FL; formerly of Bronx, NY, Worcester, MA, and Falmouth, MA; on November 9, 2005. Adored husband of the late Ida (Gerrol), his wife of 69 years; beloved father of Frederic, Lawrence and wife Stephanie Mitchell, Judith and husband James Sandler; revered grandfather of Lauren and Derek. Proprietor of the Emily Shop, Fitchburg, MA; member of Musicians Union, NY, NY. Studied law at St. John's University; athlete; exceptional man and inspiration to all. Private service in Falmouth; donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County.
