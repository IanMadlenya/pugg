
        DAVIS-Patricia Murdock, age 65, died on November 21st, 1998 in Scarborough, NY. Loving wife of Robert W., beloved mother of Thomas O. Williams, Jr., Phoenix, Md and Marjorie W. Vandenberg, Rye, NY. Also survived by her adored brother John J. Murdock, III of London, a wonderful daughter and son-in-law Nancy and Fred, as well as her second-tonone sister-in-law Elizabeth. She also leaves five cherished grandchildren, Lindsey and Owen Williams and Halsey, Jeremy and Rebecca Vandenberg as well as three dear nieces Merrick Greenhaus of Reston, VA., Lee Murdock of San Antonio, Tx. and Bronwen Murdock of London, England. She was the daughter of the late Marjorie and Abraham Bienstock and the late John J. Murdock, Jr. A memorial service will be held at St. Mary's Church, Rt. 9, Scarborough, NY on Saturday, December 12th, at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in her name to the Putnam County Children's Committee, P.O. Box 187, Carmel, NY 10512, will be greatly appreciated.
