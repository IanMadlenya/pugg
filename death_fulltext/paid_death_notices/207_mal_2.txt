
        CAMPBELL -- J. Kenneth (Ken), Sr. Of Saltaire, NY, and Rye, NY (formerly of Delray Beach, FL., and Garden City, NY). 83, in a tragic fire. Predeceased by his loving wife, Dorothea (nee Burke), and survived by his seven loving children, Carol, J. Kenneth Jr., Barbara Rosenblum, Richard, Mary Cullen, Donald and Jean. Grandfather of fifteen and great grandfather of three. Former Senior Partner at Alexander and Green, Attorneys At Law. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st Street on Thursday 7-9, Friday 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, April 3rd at 10 A.M. at Saint Thomas More Church, 65 East 89th Street, NYC.
        CAMPBELL--J. Kenneth. The Village of Saltaire notes with profound grief the sudden death of Ken Campbell, former Trustee and longtime Village Justice, and extends its deepest sympathies to his children and their families. Judge Campbell was a beacon of truth and honor in public service and private life. Board of Trustees
