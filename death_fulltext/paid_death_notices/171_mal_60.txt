
        MATISOFF-Nathan L., DPM. Died peacefully at his home Monday, April 2. He was the beloved husband of the late Edna; devoted father of Louise; cherished brother to Lillian Rubenstein and the late Rhoda, Esther & Bea, caring uncle to numerous nieces and nephews and a friend to all. He was the last of the Old World gentlemen. We cherish our memories of him. Memorial services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Thursday, April 5, at 12:15.
