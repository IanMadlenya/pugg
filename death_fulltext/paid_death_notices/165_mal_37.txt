
        FELDMAN-Abraham. Age 83, of Danbury, CT. Died Saturday, September 30, 2000 at Danbury Hospital. He was the husband of Fay E. (Cohen) Feldman. Mr. Feldman was a self-employed member of the Natl. Electrical Contractor's Assoc. He is survived by his son William Feldman, daughters Heidi Spiegler and Candace Feldman, brother Jack Feldman, sisters Miriam Kravitz and Eleanor Rosen, and four grandchildren. Services were held Monday at Cedar Park Cemetery in Westwood, NJ.
