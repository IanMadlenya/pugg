
        HART-William B., Sr., 83, of Stonybrook Road, died Jan. 13, 1997, in Speare Memorial Hospital. Born in Ansonia, CT., the son of Peter and Catherine (Vensel) Hart. Beloved husband of Christine (Ballantyne) Hart. Father of William B. Hart, Jr. of South Berwick, ME., the Rev. Philip B. Hart of Plymouth, NH, and James F. Hart of Hebron, NH. Three grandchildren. A memorial service will be held February 9th, at 4:00 P.M., in the Plymouth Congregational Church, Main Street, Plymouth, New Hampshire. Donations in his memory may be made to Mowglis, School-Of-The-Open, P.O. Box 9, Hebron, NH 03241.
