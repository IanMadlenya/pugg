
        FRACTENBERG - Harry. On March 1, in New York. He was a trial lawyer in New York City for over 65 years, and was actively practicing until three weeks before he died. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Lillian, and is survived by his daughter and sonin-law, Monica and Jonathan Kantrowitz of Fairfield, CT., two grandchildren, Bethany and Ralph, & by several nieces and nephews. Services Wednesday (today) March 3, 1999, at 12:00 noon from the Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Road, Fairfield, CT.
