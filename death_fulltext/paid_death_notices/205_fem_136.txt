
        RUBINOW--Laura Caroline. Passed away February 14, 2004. Laura was a 15 year old student at Horace Greeley High School and a principal dancer in the Dance Emotions Performance Troupe. She is survived by her Parents Kristen and Scott Rubinow, Sister Rachelle and Brother David and her Grandparents Bernard and Edith Rubinow, her Grandmother Caroline Lochrie, several Aunts and Uncles and Cousins. Laura will be remembered by her extended family, devoted friends, neighbors and teachers as a bright, fun-loving, talented girl. Our Community will miss her very much. A memorial service will be held on February 19, at 3PM at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, Mt. Kisco. All are welcome. A reception will follow in the Great Hall. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the Make A Wish Foundation, 828 South Broadway, Tarrytown, N.Y. 10591 or to the Laura Caroline Rubinow Dance Scholarship Fund to be established at Dance Emotions of Chappaqua, 75 South Greeley Avenue, Chappaqua, New York 10591.
