
        BERNSTEIN-Charlotte Z. Of Elmwood Park, N.J., died on June 20 at the age of 71 after a long illness. She was retired from Ramapo College of N.J. where she worked for many years as an administrator in the Human Resources division. She is survived by her loving husband, Jules, her daughters Ellen and Hildy, and her grandson, Noah. Services 11:30 AM Friday at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4West), Fairlawn, N.J. Visitation 7-9PM today. She was loved by many.
