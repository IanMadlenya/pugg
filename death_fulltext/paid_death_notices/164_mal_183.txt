
        KRAMER-Norman M. Passed away at age 87 on September 6. Devoted husband of Bernice, loving father of Joan Tucker and father-in-law of Barry Tucker , adoring grandfather of Amy Tucker Meltzer (Jonathan) and Robert Scott Tucker. Great-grandfather of Alexa and Matthew Meltzer and devoted brother of Milton J. (Buddy) Kramer. Services Friday, September 8 at 1:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
        KRAMER-Norman M. The Pace University community notes with sorrow the passing of Norman M. Kramer, father of Joan K. Tucker, executive vice president for University Advancement. Sincere condolences are extended to Norman's wife, Bernice, his daughter, Joan & her husband Barry Tucker, two grandchildren, Amy Meltzer and Robert Tucker, and two greatgrandchildren. Services will be held at 1:30PM, Friday, September 8, at Campbell Funeral Chapel on Madison Avenue & 81st Street. David A. Caputo, President
        KRAMER-Norman M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Norman M. Kramer, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing.
