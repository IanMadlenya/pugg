
        MANHOFF--Mildred. Born in Brooklyn, died Monday, November 24, 2003. Renowned dancer, winner of many awards, she taught dance in Jamaica, WI, appearing in a couple of films dancing with the likes of Sean Connery. Mildred was partner with her husband, Harold, in Manhoff Studios decorating the Copacabana, the Savoy Ballroom, Helena Rubinstein's penthouse, and Billy Rose's Horseshoe Club. Survivors include a son, Ira, & a daughter, Helen Cuccaro of Boulder.
