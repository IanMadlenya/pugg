
        FROSCH-John, M.D. The Editorial and Administrative Boards of the Journal of the American Psychoanalytic Association mourn the loss of John Frosch, M.D., the Journal's founding editor, who served with distinction from 1953-1972. We are committed to maintaining the standards of scholarly excellence he applied both to his own work and to the contributions to others. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Arnold D. Richards, M.D., Editor Journal of the American Psychoanalytic Association
