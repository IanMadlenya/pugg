
        DANIEL--Yvonne. October 15, 1899 - December 3, 2003. Wife of the late Louis and beloved mother of Marcella (and Edward), Pauline (and Jean), Lynne (and John), and her son, the late Louis Daniel. Cherished grandmother of sixteen, great-grandmother of thirty-six, and great-great grandmother of eight. Always loved and forever in our hearts.
