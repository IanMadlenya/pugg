
        ADLER-Theodore W. The partners, associates and staff of Wolf Haldenstein Adler Freeman & Herz LLP announce with sorrow the passing of our beloved senior partner, Theodore W. Adler, who, during almost 70 years with us made a major and long lasting contribution to the vitality, spirit and pursuit of excellence of our firm. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his widow, Martelle, and his daughter, Louise. We will truly miss him.
