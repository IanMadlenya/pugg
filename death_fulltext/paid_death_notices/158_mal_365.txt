
        WEINBERGER-Arthur. On March 29, 2000. Much loved by his family, associates and friends. Devoted husband of Geri. Cherished father of Mitchel and David Weinberger & Jamie Runyon. Adored fatherin-law of Bridget and Jennifer Weinberger and Adam Runyon. Dear son-in-law of Alene Schmidt. Brother of the late Martin Weinberger. Uncle of Nathanael Weinberger. Respected by his colleagues in the textile and yarn industries. He was a special person and will be greatly missed. Funeral service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Friday, March 31st at 11:30am. Interment to follow at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Contributions in his memory may be made to Lexington School for the DeafCenter For The Deaf, 30th Ave & 75 St., Jackson Hts, NY 11370, attention Development Office.
