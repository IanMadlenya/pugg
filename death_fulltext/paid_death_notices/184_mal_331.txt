
        GOULD-Stephen J. On May 20, 2002 at age 60. The New York University community mourns the passing of Stephen J. Gould, world renowned scholar, cherished teacher and valued friend. His absence from our midst will be keenly felt. We extend our deepest sympathy to Dr. Gould's family & loved ones. A Memorial Service will be held tomorrow, at NYU Vanderbilt Hall, 40 Washington Square South, 4 P.M. In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial contributions be made to: Scholars Copywrite Fund, Alexandria Library, 62 Greene Street, New York, New York 10012.
