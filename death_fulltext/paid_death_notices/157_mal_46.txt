
        DOMINICK-Bayard, of New Canaan, CT and Southern Pines, NC, died January 25, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Elizabeth Yallalee Dominick., Loving father of Joan, Gayer, Margot, Ned and Wilder and big, wonderful ''Bear'' to twelve grandchildren and seven great grans. Formerly Chairman of Dominick & Dominick, Inc., he served as Governor of the NY Stock Exchange, President of the Association of Stock Exchage Firms and member of the Business Advisory Committee of the U.S. State Department. He was the Founding President of the New Canaan Community Foundation, and served on the boards of Waveny Care Center, the Fairfield County Council of Boy Scouts, and Westover School, Trustee of Union Theological Seminary and Roosevelt Hospital. Memorial services at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, February 12,11AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to New Canaan Community Foundation, PO Box 1285, New Canaan, CT. 06840.
