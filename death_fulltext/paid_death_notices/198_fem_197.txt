
        FEUCHTWANGER -- Helen Hyman, born March 6, 1909. Died July 24, 2003 at 94 years young. Beloved wife of Sigmund Feuchtwanger, who predeceased her. Cousin, aunt, godmother and friend to a large and loving extended family. A native of New York and a graduate of Cornell University, Helen touched many lives with her independence, loyalty, zest for living and caring for those whom she loved. She will be remembered with great affection and love by her family and friends. >>AD#
