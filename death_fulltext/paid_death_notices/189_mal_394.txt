
        SZERLIP-Jack L. Suddenly, October 29, 2002. Beloved husband and best friend of Judy; loving father of Ann, Bill (Marcia) and Lynn Cleaver; adoring grandfather of Emily and Michael Cleaver and Joshua Szerlip; brotherin-law of Arnold and Joan Wolfe. He proudly served as a pilot in W.W. II, completing 25 missions in Italy. His war experience was followed by a long career at Lehman Brothers. Memorial Service at Levitt Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 5411 Okeechobee Blvd, West Palm Beach, FL. Thursday, October 31, 2 PM.
