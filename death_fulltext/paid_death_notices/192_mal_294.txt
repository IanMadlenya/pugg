
        DESHMUKH-Madhukar M., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Adminstrators and medical staff of St. Barnabas Healthcare Facilities, Bronx, New York are deeply saddened by the death of Dr. Madhukar Deshmukh on January 20, 2003. Dr. Deshmukh joined the medical staff in February 1971 and served with distinction for many years. He was truly a valued member of the St. Barnabas family. We extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Victor R. Wright, Chairman Ronald Gade, M.D. President Scott Cooper, M.D. Medical Staff President
