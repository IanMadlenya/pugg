
        ATKINSON--Robert Milo, of Rye, NY, formerly of Manhattan. Adored by his many, many friends. His contagious personality drew people to him and his loyalty made him a friend not easily forgotten. Survived by his two loving nieces, Susan and Laurie, and two loving nephews, John and Robert. Bob was an avid tennis player and devotee of Fordham Athletics, particularly football and basketball. He graduated from Fordham University in 1950 and NYU Law School in 1956 and went on to practice law in Manhattan for 50 years. Bob served as a lieutenant in the USAF from 1950 to 1952 in Bermuda. A celebration of his life will be held in the formal dining room of The Osborne, Rye (914-925-8000) on Sunday, April 17th, at 3 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, on Wednesday, April 20th, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Fordham University, c/o Judith Katz, Fordham University Development Office, 113 W. 60th St., NY, NY 10023.
