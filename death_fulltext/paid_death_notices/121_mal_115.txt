
        WALLINGFORD-Eldon R., Jr. 72, formerly of Bedford, NY, died of natural causes at his home in Somers. Born in Ashland, Kansas, Mr. Wallingford graduated from Dartmouth College, and served as an officer in the U.S. Marine Corps during World War II & the Korean War. He retired as a Vice President of Manufacturers Hanover Trust Company in 1983. He was a member of St. Matthew's Episcopal Church in Bedford, and the Friends of John Jay Homestead. Survivors include his five children Dick of Napa, CA, Peter of Seattle, WA., Jessie Dean of Alexandria, VA., Amanda Martin of Cedar Grove, TN., & Matthew of Somers. He is also survived by his father Eldon Wallingford of San Francisco and a brother and sister. A Memorial Service will be held at 11AM Friday, February 14, at St Matthew's Church in Bedford.
