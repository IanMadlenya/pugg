
        FRIEDMAN--Cornelius W. On October 30, 2004. Beloved wife of Lillian Behren. Devoted father of Anne and Richard. Cherished grandfather and great-grandfather. Services Wednesday, 10 am, at King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ.
