
        SWITSKY--Jean Brickman, died Tuesday, March 29. Loving wife of the late Julius. Cherished mother of Steve Switsky and Ellen Orso. Devoted grandmother of Jonathan Graynor and Robert & Mattan Newhouse. Much loved mother-in-law of Dominick Orso and Doris Switsky. President of the Brightwater Towers Senior Citizens for over 20 years and a friend, companion and confidante to all who knew her. Excellent Mah Jong player but not so great at Canasta. She will be deeply missed. Services Thursday March 31 at Parkside Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY at 10AM.
