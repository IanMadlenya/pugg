
        TATTELBAUM-Pauline. The entire congregation of Temple Shaaray Tefila notes with profound sorrow the loss of Pauline Tattelbaum, beloved mother of our esteemed Rabbi, Harvey M. Tattelbaum. Our deepest sympathy is extended to Rabbi Tattelbaum, Meryl, Adam and Nancy, Polly and Gordon, Kate and Howard and the entire Tattelbaum Family. May the wonderful memories of her life be a blessing and a comfort for all those who mourn. Ellen R. Saxl, Co-President Michael H. Laufer, Co-President
