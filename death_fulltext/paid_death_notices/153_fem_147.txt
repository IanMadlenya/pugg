
        HORSTMAN-Dorothy. Country and Western Music Historian and Author died September 13, 1999 in New York City. Survived by daughters Fritzi and Madi. Songwriter and pioneer in music journalism. Born Dorothy Ann Bernis, wedded James R. Horstman in 1959. Author of ''Sing Your Heart Out, Country Boy'' and ''America's Best-Loved Country Songs'' (will be published posthumously). Her spirit will live on anywhere country music is heard and loved. She was a true friend. Her memorial will be on October 29th in New York City.
