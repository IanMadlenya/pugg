
        KOHN-Bertha. Died February 3rd, 88 years old. Lived in Hollywood, Florida. Formerly of Brooklyn and Lake Mohegan, New York. Wife of the late Philip Kohn. Survived by her son Martin Kohn, and daughter-in-law Laura Sacks Kohn, granddaughters Maggie and Anna Kohn. All of Hunington Woods, Michigan and her sister Rose Frankel of Hollywood, Florida. Memorial service at 2 P.M., Friday, at Trafalgar Towers South, Hollywood, Florida. Memorial contributions may be made to The Social Action Fund at Temple Emanu-el, 14450 West 10 Mile Road, Oak Park, MI. 48237.
