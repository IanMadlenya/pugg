
        SPINRAD--Phineas. Age 98, died on Wednesday, April 19, 2006 in Brighton, MA, surrounded by his loving family. Mr. Spinrad leaves his daughter, Paula Charry and Susan Ebert; their husbands Rabbi Marim Charry & Dr. Ronald Ebert; his grandchildren, Jonathan Charry, Judith Charry-Nelkin, Joshua Charry, Elizabeth Ebert, Dr. Deborah Ebert-Long, and Rebecca Ebert; and his beloved great-granddaughters, Navah and Avigayil Charry, Jessica and Sarah Nelkin, and Gabriela Long. Donations in his memory may be made to Meir Panim Relief Centers, c/o American Friends of Meir Panim, 5316 New Utrecht Ave. Brooklyn, NY 11219. Services will be held Sunday, April 23, 12:45pm at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street.
