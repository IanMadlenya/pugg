
        SCHULTZ--Kenneth, 67. Of Hoboken, NJ on April 13, 2006. Son of the late Arthur Schultz and Edna Hahn. Services Saturday 2PM at St. MatthewTrinity Lutheran Church, 8th & Hudson Streets, Hoboken, where he was very active. He was a world renowned antiques dealer and collector of memorabilia of the great ocean liners which led him to be an advisor on the film about the Titanic. He also had an extensive collection of Hollywood memorabilia and was a curator of the Hoboken Histocial Museum. For info: Lawton - Turso Funeral Home, Hoboken.
