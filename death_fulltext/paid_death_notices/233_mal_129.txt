
        WOLF--Alfred C. Of McLean, VA and Wellfleet, MA. died June 9 at Cape Cod Hospital, Hyannis, MA of complications following a hip fracture. He was 94. He devoted his life to public service and to the welfare of his family, who mourn him deeply, including his wife of sixty years, the former Agnes Strauss, their four children: Sally, John, Steven and Andrew, and their four grandchildren: Alex, Tyler, Katie and Sage. Memorial services are pending. Donations may be made to the Class of 1934 Scholarship Fund, Harvard College Fund, 124 Mount Auburn Street, Cambridge, MA. 02138.
