
        PAVESE-Edith. The Baruch College community record with much sadness the passing of Edith Pavese, our long time Publications Director. Her high standards and impeccable taste left us a lasting legacy of excellence. Her warmth and charm touched us all. Our deepest sympathies go out to her family and all who knew her. Sidney I. Lirtzman, Interim President Baruch College
        PAVESE-Edith. Devoted mother of Marc. Dear sister of Dorothy and sister-in-law of Fred. Loving aunt of Christopher and Jennifer. Cherished friend of many. Services Friday, 9:45AM, at Gutterman's Funeral Home ''Riverside Building'', 180 W. 76 St., NYC.
