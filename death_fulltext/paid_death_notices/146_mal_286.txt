
        LUDLOW-Benjamin, Jr. Died on March 12 in his home in Amherst, MA. He was 88 years old. A graduate of Swarthmore College and a life-long musician, he was born in Ardmore, PA. He is survived by his wife Claire of Amherst, MA; his brothers Crozer and Alden of PA; his son John and family of El Paso, TX; five grandchildren, three great-grandchildren all of El Paso. Three step-children reside in the Boston area. A memorial service will be held at South Congregational Church in Amherst, MA on March 20, 1999 at 10:30 AM.
