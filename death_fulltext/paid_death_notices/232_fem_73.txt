
        HYNES--Adele L. On May 12, 2006, 92 years of age. Youngest child of the late James B. Landay and Lillian Slone Landay. Beloved by her husbands, Stuart L. Haupt and William J. Hynes. Dear mother of Ira Haupt II and Florence Haupt Teiger. Lovingly remembered by nine grandchildren and 22 great-grandchildren, one great-great granddaughter & many nieces and nephews. A true lady, a gorgeous woman with a great heart, wonderful friend, artist. She was a woman of all seasons and will be missed by those who knew her. Friends may call Saturday, May 13, 4-8PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave (81st St). Service Sunday, May 14, 10AM at Frank E. Campbell. Donations to the Visiting Nurse Service of New York/Hospice.
