
        DAVIS-Beatrice, 96, devoted wife of the late Sam, beloved mother of Malcolm and Ilene, George, Theodore and Cindee, adored grandmother & loving greatgrandmother, sister of Edith Epstein. Bea was a wonderful and giving ing mother and person. She was full of fun, an active Red Cross volunteer during World War II at St. Albans and North Shore Veterans Hospitals. Active in UJA, Hadassah, Zionist Organization and Hebrew Institute of Israel. Her memory will live in our hearts forever. Services Thursday, August 3, 12:45 at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam.
        DAVIS-Beatrice (Bea). The Congregation of Temple Beth El of Cedarhurst records with sorrow the passing of its member. We express our sympathy to the family. Dr. Sholom Stern, Rabbi Richard Holland, President
        DAVIS-Beatrice. The Woodmere Club sorrowfully records the passing of our beloved member, Beatrice Davis. The Board of Governors The Woodmere Club
        DAVIS-Beatrice. With love and respect we say farewell to a dear in-law, and friend, with deepest sympathy. Bernice Bernstein and family
