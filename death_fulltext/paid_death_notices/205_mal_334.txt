
        SCHEINBERG--Dr. Labe C. The family and friends of Labe mourn his death on February 22, 2004 at age 78 and celebrate his life as husband, father, physician, scientist, author and educator. Dr. Scheinberg was a Neurologist and neuro-scientist specializing in the treatment of Multiple Sclerosis. He was formerly Dean of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Chairman of its Neurology Department and founder of the Multiple Sclerosis Comprehensive Care Center. Dr. Scheinberg is survived by daughters Susan Kristol, Ellen Ranard, Amy Jones and son David Scheinberg, and by 10 grandchildren. He was married to the late Louise Goldman Scheinberg. Services are planned at the Westchester Reform Temple in Scarsdale, Wednesday, February 25, at 10:30 AM. Donations may be made to the Labe C. Scheinberg Scholarship Fund, Consortium of MS Centers, 718 Teaneck Road, Teaneck, NJ 07666.
        SCHEINBERG -- Labe. The Board of Trustees, staff, and members and volunteers of the New York City Chapter of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society extend our heartfelt condolences to Amy Jones, Susan Kristol, Ellen Ranard, and Dr. David Scheinberg, on the loss of their beloved father, Labe Scheinberg. Labe was a clinical pioneer in, and a strong supporter of, the fight against MS. He was an inspiration to his colleagues, and patients, and will be deeply missed.
