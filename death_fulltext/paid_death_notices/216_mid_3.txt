
        ROSEN--Miriam Nimkoff. On January 8th, 2005 at age 89. Beloved wife of the late Abraham Rosen. Loving mother of Jonathan Rosen and his wife, Jeannette. Devoted grandmother of Sarah and Adam Rosen. Dear sister of Michael O. Nimkoff. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, January 11th, 11:30 A.M. Interment private. Contributions in her name may be made to Tribute Program in Memory of Miriam Rosen, US Fund for UNICEF POB 346, Lunberton, NJ 08048-0346.
        ROSEN -- Miriam N. The Board and staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum are deeply saddened by the death of Miriam Rosen, mother of our Treasurer and staunch supporter of our Egyptian Art Department, Jonathan (and Jeannette) Rosen. We offer our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Ronnie Heyman, President A.F.I.M. James S. Snyder Anne & Jerome Fisher, Dir. I.M.
