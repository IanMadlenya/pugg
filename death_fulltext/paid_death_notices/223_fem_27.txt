
        WIDNEY -- Marilyn Perry. Died August 2, 2005, at age 71, in New York, New York. Her beloved husband, Charles Widney, predeceased her in 1981. She had been host of the TV program International Byline, a series of documentaries on the U.N. and the author of the reference book, Leaders of the World - 2003. Recipient of numerous film awards. Memorial service Monday, August 8, at 9:30 AM, at the Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
