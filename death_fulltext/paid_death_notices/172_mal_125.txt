
        McDOUGAL-Walter A. (Bub). Age 79, of Garden City on May 10, 2001 after a brief illness. He was the former Chairman, President and CEO of Richmond Hill Savings Bank. Beloved husband of the late Phyllis O'Connor McDougal & the late Dolores McDougal. Loving father to Stephen, Carol, Walter, Dolores (Ditta), Paul, Nancy, Mary Ann (Peanut). Devoted step-father to the O'Connor family: Mary, Eileen, Julie, Billy, Terry, Eddie, Johnny, Moira and Tommy. Proud grandfather of 33. Also survived by his brothers Jerome, George and the late Edward as well as his sisters Dorothy Schnedeker, Marion Sullivan and the late Jane McGowan. He will be missed by 53 nieces and nephews. Friends may call Saturday and Sunday 2-5 & 79:30 PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 125 Hillside Avenue (at City Line), New Hyde Park, New York. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held Monday 10AM at St. Anne's R.C. Church, Garden City. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury. In lieu of flowers, donations in memoriam can be made to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, New York 11590.
