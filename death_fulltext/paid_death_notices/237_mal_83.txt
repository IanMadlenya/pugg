
        KAHN--Elliot P. Age 55, on October 8, 2006 of Manhattan. Beloved son of Albert and the late Sylvia. Dear brother of Kenneth M. Kahn. Adored uncle of Lauren, Matthew and Stacey Kahn. Much loved brother-in-law of Frances. Mr. Kahn was a graduate of SUNY Binghamton and worked as a computer programmer for banking institutions during his life. We mourn the death of this quiet gentle man whose flair for music and sports endeared him to many. A graveside funeral service will be held on Tuesday at 1 PM at Floral Park Cemetery, 104 Deans Rhode Hall Road, Monmouth Junction, New Jersey 08852. 732-297-2336.
