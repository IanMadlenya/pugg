
        RAND-William L. On December 19, 2000. Loving husband of the late Muriel. Dearest father of Kathleen and Ruben Oliva. Most cherished grandfather of Nicole Valerie. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street, Friday, Dec. 22 at 11:30 AM. Contributions to his Memory may be made to the Jewish Museum 1109 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10128, or to a charity of your choice.
