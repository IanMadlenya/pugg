
        PERRY-Arnold Bernard. Born April 25, 1925. Died August 17, 2001. Arnold Perry passed away at the age of 76 after a courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. He is survived by his beloved wife Muriel (Sonnenblick) Perry; his three children, David Mark Perry, Richard Cayne Perry and Jennifer Dana Perry; and his five grandchildren, Samantha, David, Amanda, Cameron and William Perry. Arnold was raised in Chicago, Illinois, and Muncie, Indiana. He graduated from the University of Indiana, where he was President of his fraternity. During World War II, he served as an officer within the Seabees division of the US Navy in the Philippines and New Guinea. As an adult, Arnold lived in Chicago, Los Angeles and New York. He was formerly CEO of Bohn Rex-Rotary and the principal owner of Me-Books Publishing Company. He had many dear friends and he will be missed. The funeral service is private. A memorial service will occur at the Beverly Hills Tennis Club on Tuesday, August 21, at 3:30 PM. The family requests that all contributions be made to the American Cancer Society.
