
        WHITNEY-Lisa Lentnek. Of Dobbs Ferry, NY, at age 35. Beloved wife of Klaus and loving mother of Matthew, age seven, and Steven, age five. Lisa was the daughter of Donald and Ruth Lentnek of Woodmere, NY, and sister of David and Marci Klein and their daughter Ayana. Lisa was a graduate of Lawrence High School 1983, and the University of Pennsylvania in 1987. Lisa and Klaus were married September, 1991, in NYC. She will be missed by all who knew her. Funeral today, 1:30 PM, Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave. (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY. Memorial contributions to Extended Health Care Fund at Woodlands Community Temple, 50 Worthington Rd., White Plains, NY 10607.
