
        HERENDEEN-Thomas G. Jr. of Manhattan died on October 13 following an extended illness. During World War II he served in the US Navy taking part in seven invasions including D-Day. He retired from Stern Brothers after 27 years as an advertising executive. He is survived by Noemi Carrera, his wife of 42 years, four children, two sisters, and twelve grandchildren. Services will be held October 17 at 3:00PM at The Riverside Church, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Diabetes Association, 149 Madison Ave., New York, New York 10016.
