
        NEWBERRY--Elsa Kingman. Of Pomperaug Woods, Southbury, CT. Widow of F. Stark Newberry died November 17 in her 96th year. Daughter of Dr. Russell B. and Ethel (Spencer) Kingman, she is survived by three children, Anne Newberry Pineda, Andrew Todd Newberry and Barbara Newberry Lindsley, numerous grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be on Tuesday, November 22 at 9:30 A.M. at the Episcopal Church of the Epiphany, Southbury. Interment private.
