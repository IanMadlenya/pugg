
        LIPSCHUTZ -- Ilse Hempel. Died peacefully on June 22, 2005 at home in Poughkeepsie, NY. For her husband Lewis and her family, she was the best wife, mother, grandmother, sister and aunt they could have had. She is remembered as an outstanding Art Historian, French Professor at Vassar College, mentor and friend to many. A memorial service will be held this fall. A research fund in her name has been set up at the National Parkinson's Foundation, 800-327-4545.
