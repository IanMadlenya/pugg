
        SELMAN-Abbe J. The Board of Directors of the New York County Dental Society is saddened by the passing of our distinguished past President. Dr. Selman served in 1974 as a standard bearer for our profession. His guidance and wisdom wil be missed. Our deepest sympathies to Edy, Josh and Richard. Memorial Services to be announced. Elliott Moskowitz, President Mark A. Mintzer, President Elect Donna Rumberger, Vice President Jack Hirsch, Secretary Robert Raiber, Treasurer
