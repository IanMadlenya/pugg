
        JONES-Mary Myers,l71. Of Albany, NY, November 19 after a courageous battle with cancer. A longtime resident of Woodstock, NY, she was the daughter of the late Howard Myers, publisher of the Architectural Forum, and Louise W. Myers. She attended the Brearley School and Bard College. She is survived by her husband of 48 years, Roger Jones, 4 children; Barbara LaPointe, Sara Jones, Michael Jones, and Nicholas Jones; eight grandchildren; and her sister Barbara Greenwood. Donations in her memory may be made to: The Community Hospice, ''Mary M. Jones Educational Fund'', 295 Valley View Boulevard, Rensselaer, New York 12144.
