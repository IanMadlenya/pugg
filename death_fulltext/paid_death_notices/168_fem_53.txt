
        TAYLOR-Martha S. ''Lee'', 97 of St. Petersburg, FL died Wednesday, January 3 at Suncoast Manor Health Center. Born in Sheffield, IL She moved to St. Petersburg in 1997 from Delray Beach, FL. Was a resident of Southport, CT. for many years. Survivors include son Thomas W., daughter Martha Hayne, brother Dr. Sam W. Smith, five grandchildren and three great grandchildren.
