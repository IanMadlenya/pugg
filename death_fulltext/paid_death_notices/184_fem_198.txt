
        FINN-Patricia Havens. 81, died peacefully after a brief illness on May 25, 2002. Born in London, she grew up in Brooklyn, attended Packer Collegiate and graduated from the Yale Drama School. She designed costumes for the Barter Theater, the Poet's Theater and the Hasty Pudding Club, and taught costume design at Emerson College. A longtime resident of Marion, MA, she volunteered for the League of Women Voters, the Marion Arts Center and the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston Ladies Committee. She is survived by her children, Mark Finn of Chappaqua, NY, Lucinda Tobin of Ashland, MA, Perdita Finn of Woodstock, NY, and five beloved grandchildren. A memorial service will be held this summer in Marion. Contributions in her name may be sent to the Marion Public Library.
