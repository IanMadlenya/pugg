
        SOKAL-Blanche. AMIT is deeply grieved by the passing of the beloved mother of our dear friend and supporter, Dr. Myron Sokal, and cherished mother-in-law of our most dedicated National Treasurer, Sondra Sokal. A ''True Woman of Valor,'' caring educator and consummate volunteer, Blanche Sokal epitomized the highest ideals of Torah values and piety. Heartfelt condolences to her loving children, Vivian and Myron, beloved sister Gertrude, and the entire family. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Director
