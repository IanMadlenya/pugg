
        WOLDAR--Jerome. The Jewish Chapel Fund of the United States Military Academy mourns the loss of our past Vice President, Major General USAF (Ret.) Jerome Woldar, a founding member and great benefactor of the West Point Jewish Chapel Fund. Jerry was always available for any assignment that would benefit the West Point cadets. We will miss his invaluable guidance, great sense of humor and positive leadership. Lt. Col. Andrew Lotwin, USA (Ret.) Vice President, West Point Jewish Chapel Fund
