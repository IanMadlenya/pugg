
        SILVERMAN-Doris Elise. Died at the Jewish Home and Hospital after a long battle with Juvenile Diabetes. Caring sister of J. Herbert and his wife Betty R. Silverman, aunt of Braden L. Silverman and his wife Dr. Amelia Case, Chicago, and Nora E. Kinne and her husband Aaron Kinne, Boston. Ms. Silverman was a graduate in 1935 of the Calhoun School for Girls, a leading member of that school's debating team and a graduate of Columbia University in 1939. She was a founding staff member of Travel Weekly Magazine and held an executive post at Yachting Magazine among other publishing associations. Contributions in her memory to the American Diabetes Association would be appreciated.
