
        CHUNG-Ruth W. Of New York City, passed away on October 18, 1999, at the age of 83. Family matriarch and beloved wife of over 50 years of the late C.Y. and sister of the late Irene Lee, Elsie Wu, and Dale Bing Wong. Devoted mother of Janice Lee, cherished Auntie of Betty, Doris, Gloria, Kent, Alan, Carol, Ronald, Linda, and Andrew. Adored great aunt to many grand nieces and nephews. She will always be remembered for her loving warmth and kindness, her bountiful generousity and fiesty spirit, her distinctive laugh, and her everlasting beauty. The family will receive visitors at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Thursday from 25 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral services will be held on Friday at 11:30 A.M. Interment will follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
