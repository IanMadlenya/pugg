
        FRIAS--Victor J. International Chess Master died January 15, 2005 after a brief illness, age 48. Born in Chile, he was a highly ranked player, Fide 2493 in 2004. He taught chess in New York for 20 years and inspired hundreds of young players, including Grandmasters Wolff and Gurevich. His charm, intelligence and humor will be greatly missed by his family and friends. He is survived by his parents, wife and teenage sons. Memorial service Saturday, January 22nd, 1 PM at the Marshall Chess Club, Manhattan. Donations for college trust fund for his sons may be sent to U.S. Chess Trust, c/o United States Chess Federation, 3068 U.S. Route 9W, Suite 100, New Windsor, New York 12553.
