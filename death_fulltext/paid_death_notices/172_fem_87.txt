
        SILVERMAN-Sybil. Beloved sister of the late Muriel Schwartz and George Silverman, aunt and great aunt, died May 9, 2001. Born on September 25, 1918, Baltimore, MD-moved to NYC as a child. Worked for Standard & Poor's for over 30 years. Served proudly as a sergeant in the U.S. Women's Army Corps (WAC) during World War II and the Korean War. She will be greatly missed by her nephew, Robert and niece, Laura, their spouses Sara and Edward, and her great nieces and nephews Elana, Aliza, Seth, Jessica, Gabriel and Daniel. Service today, 9 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
