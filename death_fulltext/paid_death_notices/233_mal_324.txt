
        DRETZIN--David (Duff) died, surrounded by his family, on Monday June 26 at St.Francis hospital, Poughkeepsie, NY as a result of injuries sustained in a car accident. Adored husband of Joanna, cherished father of Rachel and Julie, admired father-inlaw of Barak and Sam, and doting grandfather of Noah, Jesse, Ruby and Ben. With a gentle heart and a luminous intelligence, he fought tirelessly for justice in the law and righteousness in life. We will miss him with all our hearts. Donations in his name may be sent to the Equal Justice Initiative of Alabama, 122 Commerce Street, Montgomery, AL. 36104.
