
        SCHMIDT-Nancy Montgomery. The City University of New York extends its deepest sympathy on the passing of Mrs. Nancy Montgomery Schmidt, stepmother of Benno C. Schmidt Jr., Vice Chairman of the CUNY Board of Trustees. Mrs. Schmidt's philanthropic and charitable activities will be long remembered. We express condolences to her loving family and friends. Herman Badillo, Chairman, Board of Trustees Christoph M. Kimmich, Interim Chancellor Matthew Goldstein, Chancellor-Designate
