
        WAXMAN-Mitchell W. Loving husband of Harriet (Sparber); father of sons Richard and Laurence (Randee); grandfather of Ian, Jamie, Ashley and Corey. Survived by brother Arnold (Evelyn) and sisters Elaine (Leo), Sue (Frank) and Penny (deceased), and many nephews and nieces. He loved his family. He served in the Pacific during World War II.
