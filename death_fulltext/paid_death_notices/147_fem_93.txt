
        LEVEY-Jane F.. Writer, thinker and teacher. Died at home of acute myelogenous leukemia on Sunday, April 4, 1999 surrounded by loving friends and family. She is survived by her father Marvin Levey of Englewood, N.J., stepmom Suzan Gordon, three sisters, Marcy, Edee and Hilary, and brother Harlan. Jane graduated from Brown University in 1984, worked for several years at Yale Press and was a graduate student in U.S. History at Yale University. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, April 18th, at 1:30PM in New Haven, CT. at Battell Chapel, Elm and Cottage Streets.
