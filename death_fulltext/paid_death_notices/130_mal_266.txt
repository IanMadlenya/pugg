
        DRESDALE-David, MD. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. David Dresdale. Dr. Dresdale was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Cardiology for about 50 years. He was the Founder of the Cardio Catheterization Laboratory. He was President of the Medical Staff in 1971-1972. He will be missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Warren Wexelman, MD. Pres. Jacob Shani, MD. Pres. Elect David Feldman, MD. Secy. Sal DeGliuomini, MD. Treas.
