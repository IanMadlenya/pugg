
        MARTIN-Richard. Parsons School of Design's faculty, students and administration mourn the passing of our beloved faculty member and cherished colleague, Richard Martin. His visionary intelligence, collaborative skills, and sincere humanity helped to make him a leader in curriculum development and academic programming. In addition, his recent gift of several thousand books to Parsons' library ensures his lasting legacy at an institution where he will be much missed.
        MARTIN-Richard. The Educational Foundation for the Fashion Industries of the Fashion Institute of Technology mourns the passing of Richard Martin, who served so ably as Executive Director of the Foundation. Dr. Joyce F. Brown, CEO John J. Pomerantz, Chairman of the Board
