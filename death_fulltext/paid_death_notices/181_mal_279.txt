
        GALSTAUN-Dr. Lionel. Died unexpectedly on February 22, 2002. Retired from a long, distinguished career as a chemical engineer. He was recognized as a Fellow of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers, an Emeritus member of the American Chemical Society and a member of the New York Academy of Sciences. He was a recipient of the St. Nerses Shnorali Medal of Honor from the Catholicos of All Armenia, the St. Gregory Man Of The Year Award and the St. Kevork Award. He was one of the founders of The Armenian Assembly of Armenia and was actively involved in securing grants for furthering scientific studies in Armenia. Lionel leaves behind a sister Diana, his brother Vanick and numerous nieces and nephews who will miss him dearly. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to St. Gregory the Enlightener Armenian Church or the St. Nerses Seminary. Viewing will be Monday, February 25, at St. Gregory's in White Plains from 7-9 PM. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, February 26, at 11 AM at St. Gregory's.
