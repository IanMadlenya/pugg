
        JAFFE--Beatrice, 84, died on March 17, 2005. She was the beloved wife of the late Irving Jaffe, loving and devoted mother of Mark (and Carol) Jaffe, Elise (and Andrew) Brownstein; beloved grandmother of Adam (and Jennifer) Jaffe, Susannah (and Bernard) Goldstein and Alex and Julia Brownstein. She was a cherished sister and aunt. Services were held on Friday, March 18 at The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam Avenue. We will always remember her protective spirit, determination, strong will, and most of all her love for family.
