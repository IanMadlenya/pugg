
        CHOU-Ya-Lun. On February 20, 2000, at 2:00 PM. Devoted husband of Pauline (nee Honadel) for over 36 years. Proud father of Carole, Allan and Barbara. Caring fatherin-law of Ricky, Charlene and Howard. Loving grandfather of Daniel, Justin, Sarah, Katie and Elizabeth. Born on July 7, 1923, in Chungking, China, Allan (as he was called by his friends and loved ones) received a Ph.D. in Economics at the University of Pennsylvania, published numerous books, including ''Statistical Analysis'' and ''Probability and Statistics for Decision Making,'' and contributed many articles to economic, financial and statistic journals. A brilliant statistician and teacher, Allan was a Professor of Quantitative Analysis and Operations Research and Statistics at St. John's University, New York, from 1968 until 1993. In 1995, he taught at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, China, as a Visiting Professor. A private service will be held on Thursday, February 24, at Quinn-Fogarty Funeral Home, 162-14 Sanford Avenue, Flushing, NY 11358. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory can be made to the World Wildlife Fund, 1250 Twenty-fourth Street, NW, P.O. Box 97180, Washington, DC 20090-7180, an organization that he dearly loved and supported.
