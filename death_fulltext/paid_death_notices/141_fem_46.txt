
        HOFFER-Miriam. Age 96. On October 3, 1998. We mourn the loss of a person of great vitality, warmth, hospitality and zest for living. Beloved wife of the late Harry Ralston and the late Fred Hoffer. Loving mother of Donald Ralston and the late Dr. Bruce Ralston. Devoted mother-in-law to Carole and Shirley Ralston. Adoring grandmother of Beth, Lowell, Diana, Jim, Harry and Ilene. Great-grandmother of six. Cherished sister to the late Lester and Herbert Levy. Services will be private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Hebrew Home for the Aged, Riverdale, New York.
