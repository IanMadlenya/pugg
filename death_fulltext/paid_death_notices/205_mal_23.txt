
        JOSEPHSON--Alan S., M.D. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center commuity mourns the passing of Alan S. Josephson, M.D., Professor of Medicine and Chief of Allergy and Immunology. In his four decades at Downstate, he made enormous contributions to the health of the people of Brooklyn and trained countless physicians. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife Adele, his children, Michelle, Neil, and Debra, and his entire family. John C. LaRosa, M.D., President Eugene B. Feigelson, M.D., Sr. Vice President of Biomedical Education and Research and Dean, College of Medicine Edmund Bourke, M.D., Interim Chair of Medicine
