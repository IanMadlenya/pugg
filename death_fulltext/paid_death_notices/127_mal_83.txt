
        HOWE-Robert K., age 65, died of congestive heart failure on August 4, 1997. Mr. Howe, the son of the late Luman B. Howe and Dorothy (Hallett) Howe, grew up in Montpelier, Vermont. He attended Montpelier schools, and graduated from Kimball Union Academy in Meredith, New Hampshire. He was a 1954 graduate of Colby College in Waterville, Maine. Following his graduation, he worked as a banker in New York City until his retirement in 1989. He is survived by his sisters: Priscilla Triebs of Manchester, Massachusetts, Carol Schlank of Pittsford, New York, five nieces, and four nephews. A memorial service will be held at the Broadway United Church of Christ, 225 W. 99th St., on August 11, at 7 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to a charity of one's choice.
