
        MURRAY-Matthew J. September 4, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Rita C. Murray. Devoted father of Thomas M. Murray, Rita V. Meyer, Matthew J. Murray, Peter E. Murray, Christopher K. Murray and Vincent P. Murray. Loving brother of Elizabeth Fontaine, Thomas Murray and Mary Martin. Cherished grandfather of eighteen and great-grandfather of six. He was a New York Attorney, former Special Agent of the FBI, co-founder of New York Racing Olympic Parking Service & an Economics professor at Fordham University for over 20 years. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, September 6, 2000, 10 AM at St. Vincent Ferrer, Lexington Avenue at 66th Street. Cemetery service private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to St. Vincent Ferrer Church.
