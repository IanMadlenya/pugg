
        KUNSTLER-Phyllis Friedman. Of Rye, NY, died suddenly on May 9, 2000. Wife of the late Michael J. Kunstler, beloved mother of Susan Y. Kunstler, and the late William H. Kunstler. Children's Librarian for over 20 years at Port Chester Public Library. Beloved sister, aunt, and cousin to so many who loved her dearly. Services Friday, May 12th at 2 P.M. at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions to charity of choice.
