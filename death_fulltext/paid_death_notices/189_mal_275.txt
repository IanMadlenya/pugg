
        CHANLER-William A., Jr., 98. Died October 12, 2002. Born in Great Neck, NY on September 24, 1904, he was the son of Congressman William A. Chanler and Beatrice (Ashley) Chanler. Surviving is his beloved wife, Innes J. Chanler of Camden; two sons, William A. Chanler 3rd, of Rowayton, CT; Bayard S. Chanler of Camden; two grandchildren. Memorial Service 12 noon, Saturday, November 9, 2002 at St. John's Episcopal Church in Barrytown, NY.
