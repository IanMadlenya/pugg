
        MANDEL - Florence. American Friends of The Hebrew University deeply mourns the passing of Florence Mandel, wife of Joseph Mandel. Through the Mandel Foundation many programs at the University have benefited from their generosity. We extend our deepest condolences to her husband Joseph, daughters Michele Beyer and Penni Weinberg, and the entire family. American Friends of The Hebrew University Ira Lee Sorkin, President Keith L. Sachs, Chairman of the Board
        MANDEL-Florence. The Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member, Florence Mandel, and extends sincere sympathy to her family and friends. David S. Mack, President Michael Stein, Secretary
