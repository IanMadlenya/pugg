
        DOW-Helena M. Four time U.S. National Fencing Champion and member of the 1948 Olympic Fencing Team. Died April 22, 1998. Reposing at her son's house at Ridge Road in Tuxedo Park, Friday, April 24, 1998 from 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Church services at St. Mary's in Tuxedo Park at 11:00 AM, Saturday, April 25, 1998. Her husband Warren, also an Olympic fencer passed away in 1965. She is survived by her son Robert, her daughter-in-law Christina Seix Dow and granddaughter Lindsay.
