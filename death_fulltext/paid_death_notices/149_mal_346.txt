
        MULLE-Joseph S., M.D. F.A.C.S. On June 20, 1999 of Jamaica Estates, NY. Born in Greenpoint on November 24, 1911. Educated at Boys High, he graduated from St. John's University in 1933. He received his medical degree from St. Louis University in 1937, specializing in orthopedic surgery. He served in the United States Army as a Lt. Colonel from 1941 to 1946. Former Chief of Orthopedic Surgery of Brooklyn State and Mary Immaculate Hospitals, and Director of Orthopedic Surgery at Catholic Medical Center from 1970 to 1982. In 1998 St. John's University conferred upon him the degree of Doctor of Humane Letters. He was a member of the Knights of Malta. He was a member of Hempstead and Mariner Sands Golf Clubs. Beloved husband of the late Anne, loving father and father-in-law to Tony and Antoinette. Cherished grandfather of Joseph, Anne Marie, Thomas and his wife Caryn. Fond brother of Anthony of Nashville, TN and Catherine Schnakenberg of Wallingford, CT. Visiting will be held Tuesday 7-10 P.M. and Wednesday 2-5 and 7-10 P.M. at the R. Stutzmann & Son Funeral Home Inc (224-39 Jamaica Avenue, Queens Village, NY). A funeral mass will be held on Thursday at 10:15 A.M. at the Immaculate Conception Monastery, Jamaica Estates, NY. Entombment will follow at St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461-9955 would be greatly appreciated.
