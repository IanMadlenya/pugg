
        GRUMET--Leonard. On January 13, 2005. Of Wantagh/ Massapequa, LI. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, uncle and friend. Services Sunday, 10 AM, at Temple Sinai, 425 Roslyn Rd, Roslyn Heights, Long Island.

Len provided wise counsel over many years and will be sorely missed by all who had the privilege of knowing and working with him. We extend our sincere condolences to Evelyn, Marc and Steven and the whole family. His friends at Kahn Brothers and Co., Inc.
