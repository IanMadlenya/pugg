
        CITRON--Rose. Died peacefully Friday September 8 with her family by her side at S loan-Kettering. She is survived by her daughter Dassi (Bill Woolis), son Jerry (Jenny Tate) and granddaughters, Simone, Danielle, Chloe, Eve. A holocaust survivor, she will be remembered for her tireless energy, resilience, courage and perseverance. She touched many and was well loved. Services at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st and Amsterdam on Monday September 11, 11:30am. Donations: Sloan-Kettering Memorial Hospital or Elie Wiesel Foundation for Humanity.
