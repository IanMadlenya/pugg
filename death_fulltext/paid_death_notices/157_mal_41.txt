
        SHEPARD-Sidney. Died peacefully February 2, 2000, after a courageous fight against heart disease and cancer. Sidney is survived by his wife, Helaine, with whom he was madly in love for over 53 years. They were best friends who always traveled life's pathways together through good times and bad. He was a wonderful father to Steven and Bruce and his wife, Emily. Sidney's strength and courage was an inspiration to his cherished grandchildren, Michael, Eric and Brian, who saw how hard he tried to stay alive for them. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him. Services Thursday, 12:30PM, at ''BoulevardRiverside'' Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI.
        SHEPARD-Sidney. Temple Israel, Lawrence, NY mourns the loss of its devoted member and extends deepest sympathy to his bereaved family. Brooks R. Susman, Rabbi Alan J. Hartstein, President
        SHEPPARD-Sidney. Farewell, old friend. You fought the good fight and now it's time to sleep. We will miss you but never forget you. Rest in peace. Harriet and Buddy
