
        RICKEY-George. 95. Passed away peacefully on July 17, 2002, at his home in St. Paul, MN. While the world of art will miss an illustrious figure, his family mourns the loss of a generous and supportive father and grandfather. He is survived by his sons, Stuart, San Francisco (Virginia), Philip, St. Paul (Mary), and grandchildren, Owen and Eleanora. A future memorial is planned. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of George Rickey for care of those in need may be sent to: Hospice of The Twin Cities, 10405 Sixth Ave. North Suite 250, Plymouth, MN 55441.
