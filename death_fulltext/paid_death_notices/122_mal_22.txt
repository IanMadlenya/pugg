
        LEDERMAN-Paul. Of Long Beach, LI, and Delray Beach, FL, died on March 1, 1997. He will be remembered by his wife Marilyn, daughters Loren Fogelson, Sherri Mandell and Nancy Lederman, sons-in-law Richard Fogelson and Seth Mandell, and seven grandchildren in NY and in Israel. His warmth and humor will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Servies graveside, March 3, 1 PM, at Mount Moriah Cemetery, Fairview, NJ.
