
        KOPMAN-Nathan P. Age 79. Died January 5, 1998. Devoted husband of Julia and the late Shirley. Beloved father of Michael (Rosie) and Cynthia (Harold) and stepfather of Larry (Ashley). Cherished grandfather of Spencer, Jacob and Emma. Served his country honorably as a Lieutenant. in WW II and later in civilian life as an IRS Appellate Officer.
