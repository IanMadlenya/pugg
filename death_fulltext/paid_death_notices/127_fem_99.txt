
        MILLS-Helen K. Of New York City. Died at her home, August 7, 1997, of heart complications. Her career was in book publishing, as designer and as design director, for Harcourt Brace and for Macmillan. Daughter of the late Professor Frederick C. Mills and Dorothy Clarke Mills. Survived by brothers, William and Robert L. Mills; nieces and nephews, Charles, James and Robert C. Mills; Edward, Jonathan and Dorothy Mills; Katherine Anderson and Susan Warner-Mills; and great-nieces and nephews. An informal interment service will be held in Vermont. Friends are invited to write c/o Robert L. Mills, Dept. of Physics, Ohio State University, Columbus, OH 43210.
