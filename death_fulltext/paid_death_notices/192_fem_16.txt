
        SMELSER-Hailey Mae. Of Goldens Bridge, NY born on August 20, 2002, died on December 31, 2002. She was 4 months and 11 days old. Hailey was born with a genetic disorder known as Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA). Hailey possessed a smile and a set of the brightest, clearest most telling eyes. She communicated her life through those eyes and her gripping smile. Visitation services will be held at Beecher Funeral Home in Pleasantville, NY today (Friday) from 2-4 & 7-9 & Saturday from 11 to 12. A funeral mass will be held on Saturday at 1 PM at St. Michael's Church in Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Families of SMA, P.O. Box 196 Libertyville, IL 60048-0196
