
        STORCH--Ruth. The Board of Directors of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City mourns the loss of our former Board Member and Co-Founder of The Womens' Division, Ruth Storch. A timeless supporter and volunteer for almost sixty years, she will be missed by all. Our heartfelt condolences go to her husband, Harold, her son, Gerald, daughter, Elaine Reddick, as well as her grandchildren, and great grandchildren. Martin Hausman, President
