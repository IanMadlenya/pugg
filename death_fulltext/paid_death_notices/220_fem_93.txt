
        LESCHNITZER--Maria E. on May 11, 2005. Beloved and devoted mother of Michael Lesch and daughter-in-law Judith. Adored Granny of Sara and Benjamin and their spouses Gabriel and Ali, and great grandmother of Ana Maria Mesa. Was married for 43 years to Adolf F. Leschnitzer who died in 1980. Born on August 22, 1909 in Berlin Germany, she was among the last Jewish women to receive a Ph.D. from the University of Berlin in Nazi Germany. Emmigrated from Germany in March 1939 and, after a stay in England, arrived in the U.S. in May 1940. Here she and her husband established the American Institute of Foreign Languages where hundreds of fellow immigrants learned English. In later years, she taught at Hunter and Queens Colleges and was actively involved in the Leo Baeck Institute. Graveside service Friday, May 13, 2pm at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY.
