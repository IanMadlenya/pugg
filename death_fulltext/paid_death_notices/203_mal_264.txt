
        JOSEPH--Jim. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a highly respected Jewish communal leader, a long-time friend and champion of Jewish education, who helped to establish a distance learning program for Jewish day schools through YU. Heartfelt condolences to his children: Devorah, Joshua and Rachel; sisters: Linda and Renee (and Jack) Slomovic. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman Board of Trustees
