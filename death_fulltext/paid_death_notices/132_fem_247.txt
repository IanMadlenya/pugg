
        HOFFMAN-Leona V., 95. Beloved mother of Richard and Kenneth Hoffman. Adored grandmother and great grandmother. The Hoffman family celebrate her long life that touched so many with her love and her gentle grace. We will cherish her memory forever. Services are today, 12 noon, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rte. 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
