
        WEINBERG--Arnold, of Palm Desert, California, formerly of Woodmere, New York and Cornwall Bridge, CT, died March 1, at the age of 80 after a valiant battle with cancer. Beloved husband, honored father and respected businessman, he is survived by his wife Carol, his children Sydny Weinberg Miner, Wendy Weinberg, Ilene Melman, and Marc Elbaum, and his five grandchildren, Robert Miner, Jonathan Miner, Chelsea and Alexa Melman, and Stephanie Elbaum, his sister Janet Rogers and his brother Howard Weinberg. In lieu of flowers, contributions to STOP CANCER would be appreciated.
