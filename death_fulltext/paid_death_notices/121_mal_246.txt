
        PRUSCH-Michael. 86, On February 16. Beloved husband of Grace for 58 years. He was a Charter Member of Motion Picture Editors Local 771 for 44 years, serving as Vice President for 18 years and President for 6 years. His Film Libriarian career extended over a 60 year period with Fox Movietone News and Hearst Metrotone News. Viewing on Wednesday at Becker Funeral Home, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, Westwood, NJ. Funeral on Thursday, 11 am at St. John the Baptist R.C. Church, Hillsdale, NJ.
