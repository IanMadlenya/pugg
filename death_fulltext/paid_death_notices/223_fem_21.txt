
        HELLER--Barbara Rita. July 11, 1933 to August 3, 2005. Barbara, age 72, of Manhattan, died after a long struggle with cancer. A longtime educational researcher at CUNY, she was co-author of the best selling ''The Only Math Book You'll Ever Need'' as well as a talented painter and jewelry maker. She was predeceased by her husband Norman and is survived by her loving family and friends. Cotributions in her memory may be sent to the American Institute of Cancer Research.
