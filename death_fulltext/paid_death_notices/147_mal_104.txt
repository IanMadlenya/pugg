
        SILBER-Harry D. Age 81. Of Tamarac, FL and Kew Garden Hills, Queens. Died April 5, 1999. Beloved husband of Lucille. Loving father of Michelle Cohen, Gail and Sheldon Silber. Cherished grandfather of Michael and Deborah Cohen. Mr. Silber was born in Brooklyn where he attended Yeshiva Chaim Berlin. He served in the Army Air Corp during WWII. He developed and owned the Butterfield Market in Manhattan, invested in real estate and devoted most of his time to his family and work. He was a spiritual man who donated to many religious causes. He leaves behind a brother Sam and two sisters, Mollie Greenberg and Miriam Harris. Memorial services will be held today, April 9th, at 1 P.M., at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, New York. Interment follows at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, New York. He will be missed by his family.
