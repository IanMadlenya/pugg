        PIERPOINT-Powell. The Board of Directors, the President's Council, the managers and staff, mourn the loss of Powell Pierpoint, our beloved friend and dedicated supporter who devoted thirty years of service to The Legal Aid Society and its clients. His strong belief in the cause of equal justice for the poor and his zealous advocacy for the work of the Society were an inspiration to all who had the good fortune of working with him. During his presidency of the Society, he vigorously led the fight to preserve civil legal services for the poor. We shall miss his wise counsel, his wonderful sense of humor and his devotion. We extend our profound sympathy to his family. D. Stuart Meiklejohn, Chair Daniel L. Greenberg President and Attorney-in-Chief
        PIERPOINT-Powell. We mourn the loss of our friend, neighbor, and fellow Board member of many years. Jeb N. Embree, President Board of 155 East 72nd St Corp.
        PIERPOINT-Powell. Hughes Hubbard & Reed LLP mourns the loss of its revered and beloved partner of more than 40 years. Our heartfelt condolences to his daughter, Harriett and her family.
