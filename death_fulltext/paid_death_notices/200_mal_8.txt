
        JUDELSON--Lawrence David, of White Plains, succumbed to pancreatic cancer on August 31, 2003. Mr. Judelson was born in the Bronx on February 24, 1952. A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania and the Sloan School of Management at MIT, Mr. Judelson worked for Citigroup as a VP in Information Technology in Long Island City. Larry loved playing tennis and golf as well as tinkering in his workshop. He is survived by Penny, his wife of twenty-six years; his daughters Rebecca and Amanda; a brother Robert Judelson, and numerous cousins, nieces, and nephews. The funeral services will be held at Beth El Synagogue on North Avenue in New Rochelle on September 2, 2003 at 11:30AM.
