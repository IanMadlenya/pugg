
        HEYMAN-Isidore. Cherished husband of Yetta and loving father of Ellen Rachich and Barry Heyman, father-in-law of Ellen Heyman and Milian Rachich, and zaidy of Andrew, Marni, Adam, Danielle and Ethan. A graduate of NYU Law School. He successfully practiced law for 68 years. Graveside service at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, N.J., 1:30 P.M., March 5. Donations may be made to Van Cortlandt Jewish Center, Bronx.
