
        ASPINWALL--Valerie. The Board of Trustees of The Altschul Foundation regrets the passing of Valerie Aspinwall, a devoted and longtime trustee and officer of the Foundation, and offers its condolences to her family and friends. Valerie brought compassion, enthusiasm and dedication to her work on behalf of the Foundation and the various charitable causes she supported. Her commitment to the Foundation, her counsel to the Board, as well as her friendship, will be greatly missed. Susan Rothstein Schwimmer President
