
        ESCOBAR--Ralph Eugene, 74, passed away suddenly Saturday, March 3, 2007. He is survived by his three sons, Christopher, David and Matthew; four daughters, Tammy, April, Chrissy and Theresa. Mr. Escobar, a native New Yorker, was a system design engineer at Grumman Aerospace where he worked on the Lunar Module and the life support systems of the Apollo space program. He will be remembered for his wit, wry humor and the motto with which he signed his letters: ''Love, Joy and Peace Mitzvah.'' A Funeral Service will be held at the J.F. Rice Funeral Home, Inc. 150 Main St., Johnson City, NY Saturday, March 10, 2007, at 3pm.
