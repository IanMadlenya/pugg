
        MARANTZ -- Irma. Beloved wife of the late Morris, died peacefully November 16. Her son Howard, granddaughter Gayle Nobel, sisters Madeleine Kesselman, Rose Schindelheim, Hermine Gewirtz, Janice Feinson mourn her passing. She gave much and grew old with dignity. Services Tuesday at 11:30 at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        MARANTZ--Irma. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Irma Marantz, beloved sister of our devoted members, Hermine and Chairman of the Board, Julius H. Gewirtz. Our deepest sympathy to the bereaved family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
