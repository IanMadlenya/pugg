
        PREZIOSO--Olga Antona. 90, of Crestwood, NY, on May 26, 2006. Daughter of the late Ubaldo and Matilde Pecci Prisco. Past president of the Women's Club of Crestwood. Predeceased by her husband Alfred, and survived by her sons Alfred E. (Arlene) Preszioso of Hamden, CT, and Richard V. (Barbara) Prezioso of Briarcliff Manor, NY. Also survived by three grandchildren and several greatgrandchildren. For information, contact Fred H. McGrath & Son, Funeral Home, Bronxville.
