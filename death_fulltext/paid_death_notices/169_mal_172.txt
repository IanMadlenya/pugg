
        RESTLER-Samuel. Age 97 of Roswell, Georgia. Passed away peacefully on Tuesday, February 13, 2001. Mr. Restler was born in New York City, April 29, 1903. He formerly resided in Rockville Centre, NY and Ocean Ridge, FL. His survivors include his wife of 66 years, Leah, his three sons and their wives, Richard, Donald, and Peter, eight grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Contributions may be made to Hospice Atlanta.
