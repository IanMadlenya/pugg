
        RASCHKO-James M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni record with sorrow the untimely death of Dr. James Raschko, School of Medicine graduate of the class of 1982. An outstanding young physician, he will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to the entire family. Avi Barbasch, M.D., President Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D. Vice Pres. Maria L. Padilla, M.D. Secretary William G. Stein, M.D. Treasurer
