
        CARLTON--Elizabeth Harral, 96, who oversaw some of the first fee-for-adoption agencies in the U.S., died in Philadelphia on May 30, 2005. Until earlier this year she was a longtime resident of East 68 St., Manhattan. She was the widow of the writer Henry Fisk Carlton. A gathering of friends and family will be held at 1 PM, July 9 in the Chapel of the Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church.
