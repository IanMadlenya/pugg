
        O'MALLEY-Thomas J., Jr. Age 62, of Pelham Manor, NY, died Tuesday Dec. 29, 1998. He was the dear brother of Mary Theresa O'Malley. Mr. O'Malley was a graduated of All Hallows High School, Holy Cross College and Fordham Law School. He was formerly an Assistant District Attorney in Bronx County. He was currently employed as Special Senior Counsel for The Department of Justice in Washington, D.C. Visiting hours on Saturday and Sunday 2-4 and 79 P.M. at Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Avenue, Pelham, NY. Mass at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor, NY on Monday at 10 A.M. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
