        $(6$)FREED--Ethan Harrison. Susan and Jeffrey Freed announce with extreme sadness the sudden passing of their son Ethan Harrison on Monday, July 3, 2006. Ethan became a Bar Mitzvah on June 10, 2006 and attended the Landon School. Ethan is survived by his parents, Susan and Jeffrey Freed of Betheseda, MD; brothers Adam and Alex; grandparents Jane and Fred Gold of Greenville, SC; Ruth and Joshua Berman of NY, NY. Funeral services will be held at the Washington Hebrew Congregation, 3935 Macomb Street, N.W., Washington, DC on Friday, July 7, at 11 AM. A private burial service will follow. In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests that any memorial contributions be made to the Youth Renewal Fund (Israel/NYC) or the charity of the donor's choice.
