
        O'DOHERTY-Marni Pont, 31. Of Armonk, NY. Missing at the World Trade Center on September 11th, 2001. Beloved wife of Joseph O'Doherty, adored daughter of Edward and Alice Pont, much loved younger sister to Stefanie and Steven, and doting aunt to Michael. A Senior Vice President in Research with Keefe, Bruyette and Woods, she was a talented and much respected member of the business community & gifted graduate of New York UniversityStern School of Business. Her family and many friends will miss her bright light always. A commemoration of a life well-lived will be held on Sunday, September 30th. In lieu of flowers, contributions in Marni's name may be made to September 11th Fund C/O United Way, 2 Park Ave., NY, NY 10016.
