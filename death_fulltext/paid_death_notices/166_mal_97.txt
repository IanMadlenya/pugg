
        CHOATE-Thomas Hyde. Of Locust Valley, L.I. on November 8, 2000. Age 85. Beloved husband of Jane Harte. Father of Jane Beck and Caroline de Chazal. Brother of Anne Hyde Patterson. Grandfather of Thomas, Rowan, Justine and David. Great-grandfather of Mary Elizabeth. Service St. John's of Lattingtown, Saturday, November 11th at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers contributions to Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center would be appreciated.
        CHOATE-Thomas H. The Board of Stewards express their deep sorrow over the passing of Thomas H. Choate, their esteemed colleague and Steward Emeritus. We shall miss him greatly and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Jane, their children and grandchildren. Board of Stewards Meadow Brook Club
