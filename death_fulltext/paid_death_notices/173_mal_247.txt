
        COLLINS-Kenneth R. Age 46, of New York City, formerly of Grenada, MS, died at his home in New York on June 22, 2001. Kenn was preceded in death by his father, Clinton U. Collins. He is survived by his mother Ruby S. Collins of Grenada, MS, three brothers Jan Collins, Kevin Collins, and Kelly Collins, one sister Cathy C. Bowles, all of Jackson, MS, and his companion Daniel Barker of Brewster, NY. Funeral services will be held at the Chapel of St. Bartholomew's Church located at 50th St and Park Ave on Monday, June 25th, at 10 AM, with a reception immediately following on the third floor terrace at St. Bartholomew's. To honor Kenn's life, please make memorial contributions to the School of American Ballet, 70 Lincoln Center Plaza, NYC 10023, for sponsorship of deserving ballet students.
