
        CECI--Joseph William. 79, on December 23, 2003. Beloved husband of Virginia. Loving father of Vincent, Christine, Timothy and the late James by his first wife, the late Lynn Ceci. Affectionate step-father of Gary, Greg, Denise and Louis, Jr. Dear brother of Rosemary, Anna and the late Eleanor. Beloved grandfather of Shannon, Richard, James and William. Veteran of World War II and Korean Wars. Graduate of CCNY/Queens College. New York based Captain with American Airlines for more than 30 years. Active leadership and board roles with Town of North Hempstead Urban Renewal Agency, Locust Valley Library, Allied Pilots Association Flight Safety Group. An extraordinary man whose inspiration, love and vitality, will live in the hearts of many always. Memorial service to be held on Saturday December 27th at 3PM at the North Shore Universalist Unitarian Society, 48 Shelter Rock Rd., Manhasset, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Joseph W. Ceci, St. Francis Development Office, St. Francis Hospital, 100 Port Washington Blvd, Roslyn, NY 11576.
