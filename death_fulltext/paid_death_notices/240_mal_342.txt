
        CROSS--George R., died peacefully on January 17, 2007, in Punta Gorda, FL, at the age of 83. He was born in New York City May 9, 1923. He was a First Lieutenant in the US Airforce during WWII. He was an attorney and held various senior executive positions in the insurance industry. He was a Supreme Court attorney. Survivors include a large, loving family and many friends. National Cremation Society is in charge, interment will be in Hancock, NY, at a later date.
