
        PURCELL-Edward Raymond. Of Tewksbury, N.J., died at home on Sunday, November 23, 1997. He was a respected attorney and real estate developer in Morristown, NJ for 29 years. He is a graduate of Bergen Catholic High School (1961), Boston College (1965) and New York University Law School (1968). He was the beloved husband of Pamela, devoted father of Jennifer, Paige, Nathaniel and Regan, and loving son of Eleanor.
