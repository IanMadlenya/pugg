
        JACOBSEN-Gaynor. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of The American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) mourn the passing of our friend and colleague Gaynor Jacobsen of Phoenix, Arizona. Gaynor's work saved lives and played a critical role in Jewish history. Principled and esteemed, his contribution and sacrifice will never be forgotten. Our condolences to his wife, Florence and the family. Jonathan W. Kolker, President Michael Schneider, Executive VP Ambassador Milton A. Wolf, Chairman of Board
