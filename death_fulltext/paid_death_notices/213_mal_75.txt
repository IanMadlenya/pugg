
        RIVERA--Cesar. Adored husband of Elsa, Amazing father of Cesar, Yvonne, Eddie and Alexandra. Loving grandfather to Maria, Isabel and Michelle. Beloved member of the St. Vincent de Paul and Resurrection Ascension Church, Cesar passed away on August 27, 2004 after a long battle with cancer. He was also a dedicated employee of The New York Times and retired in 1994 after 27 years of employment. He was laid to rest at St. Michael's Cemetery in Astoria. During the 72 years of his life he touched many lives for the better and he will be sorely missed. Donations may be made in his honor to St. Vincent de Paul at Resurrection Ascension Church, 61-11 85th Street, Rego Park, NY 11374.
