
        BASTABLE-Elizabeth ''Betty'' (nee Brown). 86 yrs old, died August 10, 2000, at Ft. Lauderdale, previously of Lakewood, NJ, and Flushing, NY. Mother was a graduate of Manhattanville College of the Sacred Heart and being a mother was her vocation. She had been a devoted member of the 3rd Order of St. Francis for over 52 years at St. John's Chapter in NYC. Along with Neil, she was a faithful volunteer and Eucharistic Minister at St. John's Nursing Home in Lauderdale Lakes for 18 years. ''All for His greater glory'' was mother's guiding principle. Betty was the beloved wife of Cornelius (Neil) for 64 years, and the loving mother of one son, Neal (Pam) and four daughters, Rosemary (Frank) McGarry, Marian Napoli, Peggy (Jim) Parker and the late Elizabeth (Betty) Comiskey. There are also 17 loving grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren. Visitation Sunday 2-5 and 7-9 at Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd, Flushing, NY (718-359-6300). Funeral Mass Monday, August 14, 2000, 10:15 AM, at St. Andrew Avellino Catholic Church, 35-60 158th St, Flushing, New York. Interment Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, NY. In lieu of flowers Masses for the deceased are desired.
