
        WEINSTOCK--Ida. Born July 22, 1914 in Brooklyn, NY. Died July 8, 2005 in Los Angeles, California. She is survived by her son Robert, M.D. and her daughter Diana, Ph.D. Her husband of 66 years, George, predeceased her. She was a devoted, wise, inspirational, loving, treasured wife and mother. She filled the room with warmth, selfless generosity, good humor and empathic insight. She had been a distinguished teacher in the NYC public schools. She enriched the lives of all who knew her.
