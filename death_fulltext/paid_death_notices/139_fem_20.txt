
        SMITH-Elizabeth ''Betsy.'' Of Northport, NY, on August 1, 1998. In her 74th year. Devoted wife of the late New York State Senator Bernard C. Smith, Sr. Loving mother of Bernard C. ''Chip,'' Jr. of Kings Park, NY, Terry of Wisconsin, Craig of Northport, NY, Tim of South Carolina and Linda of Northport. Cherished grandmother of eight. Also survived by her brother John R. Reynolds and her sister Jean R. Drake. Visiting at Nolan & Taylor-Howe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Avenue, Northport, NY. Funeral service at Trinity Episcopal Church, Northport, NY. Private cremation. Donations may be made to the Association for the Help of Retarded Children, 2900 Veterans Highway, Bohemmia, NY 11716. Hours 2-5 and 7-9 PM, Sunday and Monday.
