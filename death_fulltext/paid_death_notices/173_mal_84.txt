
        McGOVERN-John H., M.D. The Department of Urology at Weill Medical College of Cornell University profoundly mourns the passing of our esteemed colleague, John H. McGovern, M.D. Dr. McGovern was internationally recognized for his contributions to urology, especially in the field of pediatric urology. He also served as President of the American Urological Association. Dr. McGovern was a vital contributor to the Department of Urology at New York Presbyterian Hospital for over 40 years as both a practitioner and teacher. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Mary Alice and their sons. Memorial services are scheduled at 11 AM, on Monday, June 11, at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave and 84th St. E. Darracott Vaughan Jr., M.D. Chairman, Department of Urology
