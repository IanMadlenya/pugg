
        SULLIVAN--Kathryn (RSCJ), 101 years old, died September 22nd at the Kenwood Convent of the Sacred Heart in Albany, New York. Sr. Sullivan graduated from Manhattanville College in 1926 with a bachelor's degree. Two years later, she entered the Society of the Sacred Heart and, in 1937, earned a doctorate from the University of Pennsylvania. She taught history and scripture at Manhattanville College until 1970. Well recognized as a biblical scholar worldwide, Sr. Sullivan left a long legacy with generations of Manhattanville students. On behalf of the entire Manhattanville College community, we send our sincere condolences to her family and the Religious of the Sacred Heart. Richard A. Berman President, Manhattanville College
