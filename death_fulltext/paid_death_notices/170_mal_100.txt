
        BIRKE-Sidney. On Friday, Mar. 9, age 87, of New York, in Lexington, Massachusetts. Beloved husband of Margaret. Cherished father of Ted Birke and Pat Pershan. Grandfather of four and greatgrandfather of six. He was deeply loved. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to the American Cancer Society, 30 Speen St., Framingham, MA 01701. Graveside services are at the Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, on Sunday, March 11 at 2:00 PM. For information call Levine Chapel, Brookline, MA at 617-277-8300.
