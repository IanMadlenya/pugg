
        KLAPPER-Blanche. Beloved wife and best friend of Marvin Klapper, her loving husband for 58 years. Born on September 20, 1923, died February 4, 2002 in South Nassau Hospital after a short illness. Prior to her retirement in December, 1992, she had been a Secretary to the Principal of South Side Middle School and Junior High School for 31 years. Survivors, in addition to her adoring husband, include her daughter Sherry Ann, two sisters Estelle Feldman and Janet Waters, both of Florida and her loving sister-in-law Bertine Fields. Services will be private with interment to follow in Mount Lebanon Cemetery in Queens.
