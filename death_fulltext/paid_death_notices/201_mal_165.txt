
        SACKLER--Alvin M., DDS. October 11th. Son of the late Dr. Henry and Yetta Sackler. Beloved husband and best friend to Mildred (nee Weisman) for 52 years. Cherished father to Michael (Lori) and Dr. Helen (Harry Rosenberg). Delighted grandfather to Henry and Eliot-Evan and Madeline. Brother to Dr. Jay Sackler. Always a scholar. A gentle man dedicated to the practice of periodontics and the care of his patients. An esteemed teacher at Dental College of New York University and Fairleigh Dickinson Dental School. Concerned with politics and the welfare of people. A veteran of WWII. Will be greatly missed.
