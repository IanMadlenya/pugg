
        URQUHART-Norman R. Of Pearl River, N.Y. on February 21, 2003. Survived by his wife Dorothy Urquhart, five daughters Rhea Fletcher, Amy Urquhart, Heidi Besold, Julia Cescot and Vera Schofer; two sons Norman Urquhart and Angus Urquhart and 11 grandchildren. Mr. Urquhart was an agricultural and commodity economist for Citibank. He was also an Economist for the Chicago Federal Reserve Bank and the U.S. Department of Agriculture. Funeral service will be 10:30AM Monday at the Good Shepherd Church, Pearl River, N.Y. Burial will be at St. Paul's Cemetery, Nanuet, N.Y. Visitation will be Sunday 3-5 and 7-9PM at the WymanFisher Funeral Home, Inc. Donations may be made to the Good Shepherd Lutheran Church in Pearl River, N.Y. or the Pearl River Ambulance Corp.
