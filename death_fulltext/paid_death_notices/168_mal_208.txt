
        LASH-Carl K. Of Boynton Beach, Fl. formerly of Rockville Centre and Lido Beach, LI. Passed away January 13, 2001. Loving husband of Sylvia to whom he was married 55 years. Adored father of Glenn (Stephanie), William (Jean) and Melissa Lash. Cherished grandfather of Stuart and Sarah Lash and Richard Schmidt. Brother of Corinne Travis. Mr. Lash was the owner and director of Camp Tamarac in Becket, Mass. ''Gutterman's'' Rockville Center, LI.
