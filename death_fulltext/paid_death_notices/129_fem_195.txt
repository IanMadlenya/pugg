
        GARAVANI-VILLANI-Wanda, beloved sister of Valentino Garavani, passed away suddenly in Paris on October 20, 1997. She played an important role in the organization that benefits children with AIDS called L.I.F.E., which was founded by Valentino Garavani and Giancarlo Giammetti. In addition to her brother, she is survived by two sons, Piero and Mauro Villani. The funeral will take place on Friday, October 24, at 11:00 A.M., at the San-Lorenzo-in-Lucina Church in Rome.
