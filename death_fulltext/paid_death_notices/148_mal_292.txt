
        BOORSCH-John Peter. Age 54, of Princeton, NJ, on May 28, 1999. He leaves his wife Dorothea Dietrich, his father Jean Boorsch of North Haven, CT, his brother James of New Milford, CT, and his sisters Suzanne of New York City and Mary Louise Vogler of Redding, CT. He was a painter, graphic designer and educator, and a friend to many. A memorial service in Princeton will be announced for a later date.
