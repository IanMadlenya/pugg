
        ZUPNICK--Elliot. Died on Thursday, December 25 after a courageous battle with prostate cancer. He is survived by Lillian, his beloved wife of 57 years, his loved and loving children Judith and Henry (Judith), his adored and adoring grandchildren Daniel, Benjamin and Sarah, his brother Sidney (Toby), his sister Ruth Hendel (Philip), his aunt Ruth Beck (Alex). Also by many in-laws, cousins, nieces and nephews. He was a brilliant and charismatic professor of economics for almost fifty years, first at CUNY and then at Columbia University where he taught, encouraged and inspired many generations of students. A modest and gentle man of great integrity, he will be greatly missed. The funeral will take place on Sunday, December 28 at Plaza Community Chapel, 91st and Amsterdam Avenue at 10AM
