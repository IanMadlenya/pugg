
        DREXEL--John R., III. The Board of Governors of the American Society of the Order of St. John and the Officers of the Priory in The United States of the Order of the Hospital of St. John of Jerusalem are deeply saddened by the death of John R. Drexel III, KStJ, on Friday April 13th. A Member of the Order for almost 50 years, Mr. Drexel served as Chancellor of the American Society of the Order from 1989-1994 and was a Life Governor. His family and colleagues are touched by the outpouring of sympathy from Members of the Order from around the world. Should you wish, contributions may be made in his memory to the American Society of the Order of St. John, 150 East 55th St., NYC, NY 10022.
