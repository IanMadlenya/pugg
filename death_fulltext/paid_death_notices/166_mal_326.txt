
        CAMCHE-Margarita. November 26, at age 66 after bravely battling cancer and a life of giving & loving. Beloved wife of Joel, mother of Leonor and Peter, grandmother of Danielle, sister of Jaime, Angel and Carmelo. Services Thursday 11:45 AM, visitation on Wednesday 6-9 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. CAMCHE-Margarita. The Entertainment Committee of the Association of the Bar of the City of New York extends its deepest sympathy to its longtime member, friend and accompanist Joel Camche on the death of his wife Margarita after a long illness, who will be greatly missed.
