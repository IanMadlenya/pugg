
        SPALDING - Hobart A. Tuesday, Jan. 7, 1997, Hobart A. Spalding, former President of the Opera Society of Washington. Husband of Constance L. Spalding. Father of Katharine Spalding Cunningham and Hobart A. Spalding, Jr. of NYC and Joan Spalding of Aspen, CO. Grandfather of Sasha Anawait and Katharine Dorst. He is also survived by 5 great-grandchildren. Memorial services will be held of the Washington Home and Hospice, 3720 Upton Street, N.W. Wash. DC. on Sunday, January 12 at 11 A.M. interment private.
