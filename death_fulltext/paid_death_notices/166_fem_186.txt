
        GOLDBERG - Rosalie Slocum. Ninety-three, at Greenport, L.I. on November 23, 2000. Accomplished artist, author and public relations and development executive. Beloved wife of the late, Hon. Arthur H. Goldberg, attorney and Criminal Court Judge in New York City. Survived by her dear sister, Ruth Sander of Southold, L.I. and nephews, James and Thomas Sander and their families. There will not be a funeral service.
