
        WEINTRAUB-Sheila E. Died of cancer on October 5, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Sidney. Cherished mother of Roy (Nell) and Neil (Dee). Proud grandmother of Matt (Keri) and Garth. A memorial service will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The People's Light & Theatre Company in Malvern, Pennsylvania.
