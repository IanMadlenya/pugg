
        POTTER-Ralph Fred, died after a brief illness on November 3, 1999, in a hospital near his home in Ormond Beach, Fla. Earlier he had owned his own independent insurance agency in Palmer, Mass. He is survived by his wife of 57 years, Helen Mansfield Potter; three sons, Frederick, Thomas and Ralph, and two grandchildren, Stephen and Philip. His family will miss him greatly and appreciates all of his many contributions.
