
        DALY--James J. On Thursday, October 21, 2004, James J. Daly passed away at Cabrini Medical Center in New York City. James was born in New York on November 18, 1918, the son of Edward and Mary Sheridan Daly. Brother of the late Kathleen Daly. James attended Xavier High School in New York. He received a bachelor's degree from St. Peter's College and a masters degree from Columbia University. James taught for many years at Regis High School. During WWII, James worked as a translator and rose to the rank of captain in the army. James is survived by many close and caring cousins. Viewing will be at Redden's Funeral Home, 325 W. 14th St., NYC, on Sunday from 2 PM until 5 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Monday at 10:30 AM at St. Francis Xavier Church where James was a very active parishioner. Interment will follow at Calvary Cemetery, Queens.
