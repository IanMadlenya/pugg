
        MEAGHER-Joseph William. On June 16, 2001. Dear husband of Lee, loving brother of Ruth Powers and the late Robert, Walter and Agnes Meagher. Devoted uncle to his nephews and nieces. Author of ''Through Midnight Streets,'' ''The Tenement of Dreams'' and ''Tippy Locklin.'' ''He was my North, my South, my East and West, My working week and my Sunday rest...''
