
        ALLEN-Ethan, at age 92, on July 17th. at his home after a long illness. He was a Partner in the Law Firm of Willkie Farr & Gallagher from 1939 until his retirement in 1995. He is survived by two grandsons, Dr. William Ethan Allen and David Brice Allen; and by his great granddaughter Claire Allen. A memorial service will be held at a date to be announced.
