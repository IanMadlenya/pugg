
        SEGAL--Ethel. Died May 23 at 99. Even in her 100th year, she took great joy in life and communicated it to all who knew her. She was much beloved by her sons, David and Joel, her seven grandchildren and her five great-grandchildren. Ethel was active in the NYC Opera and Ballet Guilds, Brandeis University Women's Committee and The Lucy Moses School. She was a remarkable woman whose passing left a great void in the lives of all who loved her. Donations to The Lucy Moses School in NYC.
