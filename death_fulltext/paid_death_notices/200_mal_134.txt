
        BENNETT BENETSKY--Rudolph. Of Marshfield, MA, formerly of NYC and Camden, ME. September 9, 2003. Retired Broadway music director, vocal coach and orchestra conductor. Beloved husband of Barbara Regan Bennett. Loving father of Marissa Benetsky of NYC. Also survived by his dog Jake. Donations in Rudi's honor may be made to The Actors Fund, 729 Seventh Ave., NYC 10019 or to St. Jude Children's Hospital (800-873-6983). There will be no funeral service. Memorial services in New York will be announced at a later date. Amazing teacher, dedicated artist, he will be missed.
