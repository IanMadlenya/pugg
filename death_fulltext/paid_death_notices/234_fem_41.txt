
        GOLODNER--Syril Kirson, 82. On July 5, 2006. Born and raised in Manhattan, resided in Park Slope, and was a resident of Boca Raton, FL since 2000. A homemaker, business manager and children's clothing buyer, Syril was an elegant, graceful and caring woman who was deeply loved by her family and friends. Syril had a keen sense of humor, which stayed with her, and pleased those around her, throughout her life. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Harry Golodner. Devoted mother of Dr. Ellen Golodner, Adam Golodner & Meg Quiat and mother-in-law of Eileen McMahon and Daniel Quiat. Adored grandmother of Caitlin and Sarah Quiat. Services Sunday, July 9th, 11 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to HospiceCare of Boulder County, 2594 Trailridge Dr. Lafayette, CO 80026.
