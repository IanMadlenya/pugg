
        KLEIN-Estelle Kosches, 99 years of age. At her home in Manhattan on April 29, 1999. Devoted wife for 73 years of the late George Washington Klein. Survived by eight nieces and nephews; 13 grandnieces and nephews; and nine great-grand-nieces and nephews. Services will be held on Sunday, May 2nd at 10:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. to be followed by a private burial at Mt. Neboh Cemetery, Glendale, NY. The family suggests that in lieu of flowers contributions be made to Estelle's favorite charities: The Council for Jewish Women (New York Section) or the U.J.A. Federation of Jewish Philanthropies or the Anti-Defamation League.
