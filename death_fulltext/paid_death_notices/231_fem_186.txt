
        POLLEN--Ida. Died Sunday April 23, 2006 in Port St. Lucie, Florida. Beloved sister, aunt, and great-aunt, generously gave her love and heart to everyone who knew her. Daughter of Nathan Diamond and Sarah Polikoff, was born October 31, 1908 in Malden, MA. Ida married the late Al Pollen and lived in Brooklyn, partnering in their APID knitting business until his death, successfully and proudly continued operations until her semi-retirement some 25 years later. She is survived by her sisters Anne and Rose, and many nieces, nephews, whom she loved and cherished dearly. Her kindness, generosity, and humor will be terribly missed by the entire family.
