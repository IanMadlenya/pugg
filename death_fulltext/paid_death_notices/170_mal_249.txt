
        YARMARK-Ronald W. Father of Elisa Cohen. Grandfather, uncle, loving brother of Muriel S. Kessler. Realtor and entrepreneur. Paratrooper in W.W. II. With his brilliant mind, kindness, wise counsel, generosity and magnificent heart, he brightened and enriched the lives of those fortunate enough to have known him. Scholarship at Florida Memorial College, North Miami, Florida is being established for the underprivileged in his honor.
