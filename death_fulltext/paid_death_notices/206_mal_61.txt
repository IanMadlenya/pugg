
        BIER--Professor Lionel D., 61, died March 4, 2004. Son of the late Judy and Max Bier, he taught art history at Brooklyn College for more than three decades. Survived by brother Ronald G. Bier of Emeryville, CA and fiancee Maria Aurenhammer of Vienna, Austria. Memorial serivce will be held in Spring. Donations may be made to the New York Public Library.
