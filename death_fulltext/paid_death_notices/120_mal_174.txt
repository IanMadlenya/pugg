
        JABLONSKI-Dr. Robert Daniel, on January 14. Beloved husband of Carolyn. Loving father of Robert Jr., Maria and Terri. Adored father-in-law of Marie. Dearest grandfather of Lindsay. Son of Estelle and brother of John. Prominent orthodontist who served on the Board of Governors of the Dental Society of the State of New York and the American Association of Orthodontists. An avid golfer, he was the Secretary of the New Jersey State Golf Association at the time of his death. He always saw the good in people and will be sadly missed by all those whose lives he touched. Friends may call on Friday at the Joseph Sorce Funeral Home in West Nyack, New York.
