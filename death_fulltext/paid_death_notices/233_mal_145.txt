
        CHEIFETZ--Daniel E., 80. On June 9 after a brave fight against Cancer. Adored husband of Justine, beloved father of Amy (Michael Littleton) and Richard, ''Granddan'' of Nikolai and Antonia. Freelance writer, teacher of children's drama workshops (authored ''Theater in My Head'') and published poet, he will be missed by the many who benefited from his joy and wisdom. Memorial planned.
