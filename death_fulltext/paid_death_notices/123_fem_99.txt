
        FERRY-Harriet (Butts), 81, of Yarmouth Port, MA, formerly of New York City, died at Cape Cod Hospital, Hyannis, MA on April 12. Wife of the late Robert R. Ferry. Mother of Sebern F. Fisher of Cummington, MA; sister of William Butts of Pullman, WA., and Phillis Garber of Morgan City, LA. Also survived by 4 grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by a son, Hugh C. Ferry and a daughter, Kevyn Deal. A graveside service will be held Thursday, April 17 at 2:00 P.M., in Sleepy Hollow Cemetery, North Tarrytown, New York. Donations in her memory may be made to American Heart Assn. For information contact Doane, Beal & Ames Funeral Home, Hyannis, Massachusetts.
