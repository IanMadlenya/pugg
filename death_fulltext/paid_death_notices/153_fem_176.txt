
        LAZAR-Rosa. Age 86. Passed away peacefully on October 19, 1999 at her home. Beloved wife of Arthur Lazar for what would be 58 years on this November 9. Loving mother and mother-in-law of Matti and David Schlang and the late Laurence Lazar. Cherished grandmother of Lisa and Julie Schlang. Loving sister of Blanche Blank and the late Harold and Morton Davis. Beloved sister-in-law, aunt and great aunt. Rosa was a kind, generous person who brought joy to everyone around her. She will be greatly missed by all whose lives she touched. A service will be held today at 11AM at Mount Carmel Cemetery, Glendale NY.
