
        GRONOWETTER - Adeline. Died peacefully on Thursday, July 18, 2002, at the age of 91. Loving wife of the late Frank Gronowetter. Mother of Arlene, Rita and son-in-law Fred. Grandmother of Debbie Edelstein, and the late David Silberman. Greatgrandmother of Alex, Spencer and Danielle. Sister of Hazel Wallock, and the late Estelle Siegel. Her sweet, kind and gentle way will always be remembered.
