
        HEUSTON-George Franklin. Artist from Garrison, NY. On 9/16/01. Born South Bend, WA 8/24/06. Husband of Gwendolyn R. Heuston 6/ 23/12-6/20/96. A fine artist, studied anatomy at P&S Columbia, drawing with George Bridgman, Art Students League and painting at National Academy of Design. Painted in lifeboat during 7 days after his merchant ship, SS Santa Rita, was sunk by U-boat. Believed only paintings created under such conditions. Exhibits 1939 through present. Son, Paul Heuston survives. Joint service for Gwen and George to be held at St. Philip's Church, Garrison on 9/21. Gifts to National Maritime Historical Society, Peekskill, NY appreciated.
