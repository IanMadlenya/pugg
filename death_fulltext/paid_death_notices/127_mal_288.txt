
        PADRONE-George W., 67, of Rancho Mirage, Calif. On August 27. Formerly of Bellerose, Queens. He was the son of Vito and Catherine Jones Padrone and was for 20 years the companion of Robert E. Armstrong of NYC and Rancho Mirage. For thirty-one years Mr. Padrone was on the staff of the IBM Corporation, capping his career as Administrator of the IBM Club of NYC. A memorial is planned for Calif. in the fall.
