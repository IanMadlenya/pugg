
        LISCHNER--Celia R., of New York City, passed away peacefully on Sunday, May 30, 2004 in the Inpatient Hospice Unit at Lehigh Valley Hospital, Allentown. Born in Firth, Germany, she was the daughter of the late Max and Gertrude (Eisenberg) Lischner. She was retired as a librarian from the Queens Borough Public Library. She was an enthusiastic figure skater, reader, ballroom dancer, Tai Chi, Yoga and adult education student, and a volunteer at N.Y. Presbyterian Hospital, Central Park, and the N.Y. City Marathon. She will be sadly missed by her cousin, David J. Lischner of Allentown, his wife Elizabeth, their four children and the many dear friends she leaves behind. A memorial service will be held at 1:00 pm on Sunday, June 13, 2004 at John Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 1st Avenue, New York.
