
        WILLIAMS-Paul W. The trustees, administration, faculty and alumni/ae of Bard College mourn the passing of a loyal colleague and friend, Paul Williams, Life Trustee Emeritus of Bard. His leadership as Chairman of the College's Board of Trustees from 1964 to 1974 and his distinguished legal career and commitment to public service will long be remembered. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman, Board of Trustees
