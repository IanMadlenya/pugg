
        CULLMAN -- Joan Paley Straus. The Artists, Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are dismayed by the grievous news of the untimely death of our extraordinary Vice Chairman. Joan has been a powerful force in the re-creation of this theater, a devoted Trustee whose passionate advocacy and irresistible charm advanced the cause of the theater at every opportunity. We miss her so much and wish succor and comfort to beloved Joe, Tracy and Barney. John B. Beinecke, Chairman John Chalsty, President Andre Bishop, Artistic Dir. Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
        CULLMAN--Joan Straus. Bay Street Theatre's Board of Trustees, staff and friends are deeply saddened by the loss of Joan Straus Cullman, a founding Trustee. Her unique spirit and love of theater will remain with us always.
