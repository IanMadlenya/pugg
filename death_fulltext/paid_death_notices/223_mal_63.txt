
        JANOWITZ--Murray. Still a kid at heart at 87, Murray Janowitz of UPS, Nestle/Gerber Cheese, Hasbeen/Squire Softball and Piper's Kilt Notoriety, died suddenly Thursday, July 28, 2005. He leaves behind Don Janson and Eileen. Funeral services were private. The family will be celebrating Murray's life at a later date in his beloved Las Vegas.
