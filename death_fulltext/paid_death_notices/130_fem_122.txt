
        CLARK-Scarlett. Died at her Southampton home, November 17 at the age of 91. She is survived by her husband, Robert Clark; a sister Anita Weinstein; and a brother Walter Fried. In accordance with her wishes, there will be no funeral. Memorial donations can be made to International Fund for Animal Welfare, Box 193, Yarmouthport, MA. 02675-0193.
