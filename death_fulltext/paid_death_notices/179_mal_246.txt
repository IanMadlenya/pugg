
        GRESSENS-Robert. Former president of GTE International, died at his home in Atherton, California on Thursday, December 13. Mr. Gressens, who was 76, had been suffering from late term pulmonary disease. He died peacefully, surrounded by his family. Survivors include his wife, Jeanne; his stepmother, Dulcie, of Evanston, Illinois; his brother, Peter of Baltimore; his son, Robert Jr., of San Francisco, and daughters, Sally of Stanford, California and Kathy Crawford of San Carlos, California; and his grandsons, Peter and Matthew Crawford. Services were held held at the Church of the Nativity in Menlo Park on Tuesday, December 18 at 11 AM. Donations may be made to the Stanford Hospital, Pulmonary Clinic/Dr. Rizk in memory of Robert Gressens.
