
        KAUFMAN-Carole. On December 31, 1999. Beloved wife of Morton, loving mother of Richard and Joanne, devoted mother-in-law of Robin and Alan, dear sister of Mildred, Gerald, Ted and Susan, adoring ''Grammy'' of Melissa, Jenny, Jesse and Zak. Her warmth, love and extraordinary capacity for giving meant so much to her friends and family. As a teacher, her dedication and creativity touched many lives. She will always be with us. Services will be held Tuesday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
