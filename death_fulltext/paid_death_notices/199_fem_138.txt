
        KATZ--Evelyn. 86, August 24, 2003. Beloved wife of Harry. Devoted and loving sister to Doris, Arthur and Martin. Cherished aunt to two generations of nieces and nephews. A graduate of New York University, Evelyn worked as an accountant and later taught math in the Lawrence school system. For the past many years she selflessly devoted her time and energy to the National Council of Jewish Women. To all who knew her and loved her she was an example of devotion to family and commitment to community. Services will be held Tuesday, 11:30 am, at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapels,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett.
