
        SAVAGE-W. Lee, age 69. The Board of Directors of Riverkeeper note with sadness the death on September 6 in Ossining, New York, of our honorary Director, a lover of the Hudson, a wise & witty artist, animator and film maker and a member of The National Academy of Design. We extend our condolences to his wife Karen, his children Peter, Will, Kate, Adam and Miranda and his stepdaughter Kristin.
