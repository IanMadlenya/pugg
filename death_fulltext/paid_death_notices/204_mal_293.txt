
        HERZBERG--Herbert. Born December 21, 1913, Berlin, Germany, died January 18, 2004, New York, NY. Beloved husband of Rita Guibert Herzberg and the late Luisa Herzberg. Cherished father of Horacio Herzberg, Ernesto Herzberg, and Ana Maria Neuman. Loving Vati to Gabriela and David Herzberg; James, Emanuel, and Walter Herzberg; Andrew and Peter Neuman. Great grandfather to Spencer Neuman. His life and memory are a blessing to all who knew and loved him.
