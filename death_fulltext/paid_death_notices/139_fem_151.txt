
        ESIBILL-Nancy Lynne, Ph.D., C.R.C. Died August 22, 1998, at age 59 after a short illness of cancer. She was a courageous woman of wry good humor and intelligence. A mentor and invaluable source of inspiration to colleagues and students alike, Nancy was a beloved and impassioned teacher. As Chair of the Rehabilitation Counseling Program at New York University, she was recognized as a Distinguished Professor of the School of Education and had recently received an award from the Women's Studies Commission, of which she was a founding member. Visitors are welcomed to Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Ave., Ridgewood, NJ, Monday, 7-9 PM. The funeral service will take place Tuesday, 11 AM, at Feeney Funeral Home. It is Nancy's wish that in lieu of flowers, contributions be sent to Gilda's Club, 195 West Houston St., NY, NY 10014.
