
        HYMAN--Wilbur Z., 85, a retired agent for Mutual Benefit Life Insurance Company and a resident of Clifton, NJ, for 50 years, died peacefully December 14th, 2006 at the Jewish Home and Healthcare Center, Somerset, NJ. Born and raised in NYC, he was a graduate of Seward Park High School and The City College of New York. He was pre-deceased by a son, Gary Hyman, M.D. of Wilmington, NC, and his former wife Beatrice Hyman of Montville, NJ. Survivors include a daughter Hillary K. Hyman, Ph.D., of Edison, NJ, a son, Donald Hyman of Fairfield, CT, and five grandchildren.
