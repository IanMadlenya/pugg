
        SHERR-Abraham I. On August 25, 2000, at age 90. Beloved husband of the late Jean F. Sherr. Loving father of Rita, Barbara and Alan. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Sunday, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Salem Fields Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        SHERR-Abraham I. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. Mr. Sherr was an esteemed former President and Benefactor of our Sherr Institute of Adult Studies. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd Paul S. Schreiber, President
