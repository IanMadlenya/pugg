
        CARROLL - Edith McWilliams. Age eighty-one, died at Waverly Heights, Gladwyne, PA on Saturday, March 10, 2001. Her husband Ralph C. Carroll, Jr. of Greenwich, CT predeceased her. She is survived by her daughter Margaret C. Tattersall of Lawrenceville, NJ, granddaughter Edith C. Tattersall and her brother John W. McWilliams of Chestnut Hill, PA. A memorial service will be held in Greenwich, CT at the convenience of the family. Memorials in her name may be given to Red Cross or Pine Manor College.
