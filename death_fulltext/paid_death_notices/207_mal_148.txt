
        OSBORNE--Stanley de J. The Boards, Physicians, and Staff of New York-Presbyterian Hospital mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague and friend, Stanley de J. Osborne. Mr. Osborne became a member of the Board of Governors of The New York Hospital in 1957 and served as President of the Board from 1975 to 1980. His presidential legacy to the Hospital included the establishment of the William Randolph Hearst Burn Center, the first comprehensive burn center in New York City and the largest civilian burn care facility in the nation. Following his term as President, he continued to give distinguished service to the Board as a Life Governor. In 1998, he became a Life Trustee of New YorkPresbyterian Hospital. We extend our deepest condolences to his children, Richard de J. Osborne, Mary Osborne Witherbee, and Cynthia Osborne Hoskin. John J. Mack Chairman, Board of Trustees Herbert Pardes, M.D. President and CEO NY-Presbyterian Hospital
