
        WHALEN--Thomas C., Amagansett, New York. Succumbed to cancer on Friday March 10. He is survived by his wife Emily, daughters Emily DeMello of Forest Hills and Annie Petrocelli of New York City and grandsons Thomas and Richard. He is also survived by a brother Edward of Hillsboro, Fl. and a sister Mary of Milton, MA. A Memorial service is planned for later in the spring. Any contributions in his name may be made to Calvary Hospital in Bronx, New York.
