
        STONE-J. Jacques. The officers, leadership, and staff of UJAFederation of New York mourn the passing of J. Jacques Stone, an active supporter of UJA-Federation and a devoted Jewish communal leader who was particularly focused on assuring our Jewish continuity. Mr. Stone served on UJA-Federation's Domestic Assembly and on the boards of our agencies, the Hebrew Immigrant Aid Society & the Board of Jewish Education, where he was a pastpresident. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Janet, his children, Toni and Freeman Shore and Carol and Alan Pomerantz, his grandchildren, Peter and Julie Strauss and Michael Romano, and the entire family. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        STONE-J. Jacques. The Board of Directors of the Roosevelt House Association and the Hunter College community mourn the loss of Roosevelt House Director and steadfast friend of Hunter College, J. Jacques Stone. We extend our sympathy to his wife, Janet and to his family. Claire G. Miller, Chairman Bd of Directors, Roosevelt House David A. Caputo, President, Hunter College
