
        LOVE-Brett J., 41. On April 12 in Los Angeles after a courageous battle with cancer. A professor, writer and filmmaker, he is survived by his loving wife, Laura Chavez Silverman, his mother and stepfather Yveta Synek Graff and F. Malcolm Graff of New York, father Robert L. Love of Sante Fe, brother Steven and sister-in-law Jeanie, nephew Chase Graff Love, extended family, friends and students. A graduate of AllenStevenson, St. Paul's School, Stanford University and USC Film School, Brett taught writing in the Humanities Division at Pepperdine University, specializing in film and popular culture. Memorial Service Saturday 1 PM, Pepperdine University Chapel. Contributions in his name may be made to Pepperdine University, Malibu, CA.
