
        KRULL--Hyman. Of Boynton Beach, FL. and Englewood, N.J. on November 22, 2004. Born on September 8, 1916 in Bayonne, N.J. Loving brother, husband, father, grandfather and great grandfather. Survived by his caring wife of 64 years, Sylvia, daughter Elise Herz, sons Arthur, Albert and David, grandchildren Harold, Ellen, Michael, Mark, Jeremy, Kathryn and Marissa, and great granddaughter Erin. A leader in his community. His humor and compassion will be missed by all.
