
        POPOLIZIO-Maria A. nee Tarangelo, 84. On August 30, 2002. Devoted mother of Valerie Lindquist, Donna Rosenfield and the late Hon. Michael F. Popolizio. Employed by the Grand Lodge of Free and Accepted Masons of NY for more than two decades and President of the Greenwich Village Chapter of the American Committee on Italian Migration from 1976 to 1978. Maria was married to the late Emanuel P. Popolizio for 43 years. In addition to her daughters, she is survived by her brother, Robert C. Tarangelo of FL, and five granddaughters. Reposing at the Perazzo Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., NYC, Tuesday, 7-9 PM, and Wednesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Thursday, 9:30 AM, at Our Lady of Pompei Church. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
