
        FORSYTH-William H. Died on May 14, peacefully, in his 96th year. Survived by his five children, Agnes Kuenkler, Caroline Elischer, William H. Forsyth Jr., Marian Weekly and Therese Hare, ten grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Curator Emeritus of Medieval Art at the Metropolitan Museum of Art and The Cloisters. Memorial service at Trinity Church, 33 Mercer St, Princeton, NJ, on May 21, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Trinity Church.
