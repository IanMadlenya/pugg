
        NEWMAN - Margaret S. (Maggie). Died October 19, 2002. Loving and beloved wife of Harold M. Newman, daughter of the late Albert Strauss and Gertrude Greenwald, mother of Robert P. Newman, mother-in-law of Katheryn, sister of Helen Strauss, sister-in-law of Betty Abrams. Born 1921. Graduate of Barnard College Class of 1942. Former longtime member of the Board of Directors of the YWCA of White Plains and Central Westchester, and Literacy Volunteer. Evergracious, courageous and uncomplaining in the face of Alzheimer's Disease and cancer. Services private. Donations in her name may be made to the Dysautonomia Foundation, 633 Third Avenue, 12th Floor, NY, NY 10017-6706.
