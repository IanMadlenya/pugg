
        MALAWER-Sylvia, age 98, on Sunday July 21 at her home in Silver Spring, MD, after a long and enriched life. Beloved wife of the late Jack Malawer. Loving mother of the late Sidney J. Malawer (Carol) and Donald (Susan) Malawer. Cherished grandmother of Judith (Kyle) Walker, Leslie (Chris) Keenan of Maryland, Steven (Nicole) Malawer of Connecticut, and David (Michelle) Malawer of NY; and five great-grandchildren. Services today 12 noon at ''Gutterman's'', 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to the National Kidney Foundation.
