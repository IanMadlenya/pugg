
        EISENBERG-Stanford E. Of Boca Raton, FL. and Hackensack, N.J., on January 9, 2002, after a 2-1/2 year illness. Beloved husband of Helen. Father of Ann Friedlander and Amy (deceased). Father-inlaw of Stephen R. Friedlander. Mr. Eisenberg graduated from Rutgers University. He served in the U.S. Navy during WWII as Executive Officer on the USS Hudson. Mr. Eisenberg was a generous man who was active in many charities including the United Jewish Appeal, American Friends of Hebrew University and ADL. His friends and relatives will all miss Stan. We will miss his wit, his song, his poetry, his integrity, the Times Sunday crossword undone, in all we will miss you, Stan. Funeral service at Gutterman-Musicant, 402 Park Street, Hackensack, N.J. Friday, January 11th, at 1:30PM, 201-489-3800. The family will be receiving visitors at their home, The Excelsior II, 170 Prospect Avenue, Hackensack, N.J. through Monday evening, January 14th.
