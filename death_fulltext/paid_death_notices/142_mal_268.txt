
        FOWKES-Robert A. of Bronxville, NY on November 18, 1998. Husband of Angela Vescio Fowkes, father of Robert Jr., Richard and William, grandfather of six, greatgrandfather of three. Services at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, on Monday, November 23, at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to NYU Scholarship Fund or St. David's Society of New York.
        FOWKES-Robert A. The Beta Chapter of Phi Beta Kappa at New York University mourns the death of our esteemed colleague. Professor Fowkes' dedication and leadership in Phi Beta Kappa included past president and historian of the Beta Chapter, and former senator of the national Phi Beta Kappa Society. As a scholar and teacher of languages and linguistics he has been a model of Phi Beta Kappa's highest intellectual attainments. We will greatly miss his warm friendship and ready wit. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife Angelina and to his sons and their families. Roger Deakins, President John Delgrosso, Secretary Frederick Schult, Treasurer
