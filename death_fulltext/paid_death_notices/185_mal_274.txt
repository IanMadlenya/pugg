
        WILKENS-Samuel. Of Hollywood, FL, formerly of NY, June 20, 2002, at age 88. Cherished husband of Ruth. Beloved father of Judy Lev, Fred and Jaemes. Adored grandfather of Russell and Laurie Green, Corey and Joanne Lev, David and Lucia, Michael and Michelle, Marni and David Finkel, Adam and Derek. Greatgrandfather of Crystal, Olivia, Julia, Philip, Phylicia and Jesse. He possessed a deep passion for music, art, and golf. He was a kind, wonderful human being with a great sense of humor. He loved life and his family. He worked his entire life as a leader in the bicycle industry. We will miss him forever.
