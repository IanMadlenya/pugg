
        CRUIKSHANK-Robert Lane. Beloved husband of Marianne, loving father of Christina and Douglas and his wife Lisa, grandfather of Lindsey, died as a result of the terrorist attack on the World Trade Center. He is also survived by his mother, brother and two sisters. Services Saturday, September 22, at The Reformed Church of Bronxville, Pondfield Road, New York, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be sent to Princeton University in memory of Robert L. Cruikshank.
