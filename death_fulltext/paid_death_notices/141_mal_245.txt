
        ZIRIN-Morris G. The New York Law School community pays tribute to Morris G. Zirin, lawyer and writer, graduate of the class of 1933, who died last May at the age of 94. To his son, James D. Zirin, member of the Board of Trustees at NYLS and to the rest of his family and friends, the School pauses on the eve of his memorial service to remember Morris G. Zirin, attorney, father and friend.
