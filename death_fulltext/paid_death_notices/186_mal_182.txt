
        SCHAFFNER-Charles E. The Cooper Union Alumni Association mourns the passing of Charles Schaffner (CE '41). Charlie's activism and dedication to his alma mater were exemplary and he provided strong leadership on the Alumni Council, particularly as President and Vice President, and on The Cooper Union Board of Trustees. His professional accomplish ments and personal commitment to The Cooper Union were recognized with our three highest awards: The Gano Dunn Award, Alumnus of the Year and and The Presidential Citation. Jacob Alspector, President
        SCHAFFNER - Charles E. Teacher, engineer and author of NYC Building and Fire Codes, colleague and friend. Our condolences and love to Olga, the children and grandchildren. Jack and Susan Rudin and family
