
        GROVES--Pauline Spencer, passed away on January 30, 2007, wife of Samuel A. Groves, is survived by daughter, Nancy Groves, daughterin-law Linda S. Groves, granddaughter Ceaira Groves, brother Frank N. Spencer, sister Jane S. Burke as well as 16 nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by son, Robert S. Groves and her husband Samuel A. Groves. Graduate of the Masters School, Dobbs Ferry, NY and the Cambridge, MA School of Architecture. She was born in Bronxville, NY. She and her late husband, moved to Delray Beach, FL 30 years ago from Wellesley Hills, MA and Hancock, NH. At her request, the services were private. Memorials in her memory may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
