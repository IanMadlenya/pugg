
        KLINGENSTEIN - Paul. Of Scarsdale, NY, and Longboat Key, FL, died peacefully at home on January 5, 2003, at age 88. He was surrounded by his cherished family. Loving and dearly loved husband of Selma for 56 years, devoted father of Dr. R. James Klingenstein (Susanne) and Irene Hess. Adored grandfather of David (Diane) and Suzanne Hess, and Rachel and Julie Klingenstein. Treasured brother of Max. A prominent figure in the photographic industry for more than 50 years, he later in life applied the same energy and creativity to charitable pursuits as he had to his career. A man of dignity and compassion, he was admired and respected for his warmth, wisdom and generosity. He will be missed by all who knew him and whose lives he touched. Services were held at Westchester Reform Temple, Scarsdale, New York, on Tuesday, January 7. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory made to the American Friends of the Hebrew University, 7280 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33433, would be appreciated.
