
        TAYLOR-Clyde C. 64. On January 5, 2001. Vice President, Curtis Brown Ltd., literary agent for 20 years and former publisher, G. P. Putnam. A much loved friend, beloved father of Katie Taylor Legnini of Woodstock, NY, Emily Taylor of Knoxville, TN, and Andrew Taylor of San Francisco, doting grandfather of Lucia Legnini, loving friend and former husband of Brenda Taylor. His humor and loyal friendship will be terribly missed by everyone who knew him. Visiting hours on Sunday and services on Monday at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave; for times, call 212288-3500. Flowers may be sent to Campbell's or donations may be sent to the Democratic National Committee, earmarked for Congressional elections 2002.
