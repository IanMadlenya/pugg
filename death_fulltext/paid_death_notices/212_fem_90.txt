
        FRIEDLAND--Cynthia Blau, on September 6, of cancer. Her life was dedicated to music, family and friends. She worked with lifelong friend Lainie Kazan; produced the music TV program, Night Flight and was VP, Scene Int'l. Survived by daughter Lilah, partner John Keese, and Stephen Friedland. Donations to Memorial Sloan Kettering Hospital or The Wisdom School, Santa Fe, NM. Contact: friendsofcynthia@ hotmail.com
