
        MOSKOWITZ-E. Lucille. 99. Died at home April 21, 2001. A strong, generous, unique women, she taught kindergarten for 40 years. She devoted her retirement years to caring for her beloved husband, Louis (died 1974) and to volunteer work. She is survived by her loving children Bob (Jo An) and Alyce (Irwin) Domenitz, four beloved grandchildren and four darling great - grandchildren. Memorial service held in Clearwater, FL.
