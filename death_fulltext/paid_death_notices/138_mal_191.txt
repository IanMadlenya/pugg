
        JOHNSON-Hugh G., 78. Of Vero Beach, FL. Died on July 5. Mr. Johnson was a retired Engineer and Trustee of the Dime Savings Bank of NY. Survived by his wife Rebecca, four sons Hugh, Jr, Timothy, Henry and Ian and seven grandchildren. Memorial service Saturday July 25, 11AM, St. Johns Church, Cold Spring Harbor. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to: VNA-Hospice, 1111 36 Street, Vero Beach, FL 32960.
