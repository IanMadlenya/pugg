
        MORTOLA-Edward. Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts Inc. and Lincoln Center Institute express deep sorrow at the loss of Dr. Edward J. Mortola, Chancellor Emeritus of Pace University. As Chairman of the Institute from 1987 to 1992 Dr. Mortola provided dynamic leadership and vision to the education arm of Lincoln Center. During his tenure, the Institute entered a new phase of growth through the expansion of research and program development and through the acquisition of a permanent facility in the Rose Building on the Lincoln Center campus. On behalf of the Board of Directors and Staff, we extend our sincere condolences to his wife Doris and family. Bruce Crawford, Chairman Reynold Levy, President Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. Sandra Priest Rose, Chairman Scott Noppe-Brandon, Executive Director Lincoln Center Institute
        MORTOLA-Dr. Edward. The College of New Rochelle community records with sadness the death of Dr. Edward Mortola, educational statesman and former member of the Board of Trustees. His deep values and unshakable integrity have enriched the field of higher education administration. The beneficiaries of his precious wise counsel and support, we mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, PhD, President Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU Chair, Board of Trustees
