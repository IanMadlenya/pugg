
        McDONAGH-Reverend Monsignor Charles G. On April 23, 1999. Devoted priest of the Archdiocese of New York. Beloved son of the late Hannah and Charles McDonagh. Cherished brother of Margaret Douglas, Joan Ferrara, Ann and the late Genevieve, and Eugene. Cherished uncle of six, and adored great-uncle of 12. He was a Chaplain at St. Vincent's Hospital, Staten Island, and formerly Secretary to Terrence Cardinal Cooke and John Cardinal O'Connor. Viewing at the David J. Hodder & Son Funeral Home, Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, 899 McLean Ave. (near Kimball), Yonkers, NY. Mass of the Christian Burial Tuesday, Saint Patrick's Cathedral, 10 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory to the Society for the Propigation of the Faith, 1011 First Ave, NYC 10022.
