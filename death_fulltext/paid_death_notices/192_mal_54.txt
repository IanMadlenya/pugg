
        EYRE-Major General Sir James, KCVO, CBE, of East Garston, Berkshire, England, died January 3, following a brief illness. He leaves his wife, Monica; children, Major James Eyre, Annabelle Eyre; daughter-in-law, Sophie Eyre; and grandchildren, Hugo and Isabelle Eyre; brothers, Edward, Peter and Patrick Eyre; sister, Caroline Eyre of England; brother, John Eyre of Newport, R.I., and sister, Dorothy Reminick of Jupiter, FL.
