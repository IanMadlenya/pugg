
        RANZAL-Edward. The family of Edward Ranzal mourns his passing on April 1, 1997. He is survived by his two sons and their wives, Ted and Eleanor, and Gerry and Barbara, and his 4 grandchildren, Robin, Susan, Scott, and Stacey. He joins his wife Celia who passed away April 9, 1996. He was a newspaper reporter on the staff of the New York Times for over 50 years. During that time his beat included the Bronx courthouse, the federal courthouse at Foley Square, and Mayors Lindsay and Koch. Since his retirement he has been a resident of Deerfield Beach Florida. If so desired, a memorial donation may be made to: The Alzheimer's Association, or The Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Hospital.
