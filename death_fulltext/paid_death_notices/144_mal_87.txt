
        LENARSKY-Maurice, M.D., on Tuesday, January 5, age 95 of Natick, MA, formerly of Riverdale, NY. Beloved husband of the late Mary (Bresnahan). A pediatrician, he graduated from NYU, Phi Beta Kappa and Johns Hopkins Medical School and was affiliated with several New York hospitals. He was on the faculty of Albert Einstein Medical School. A Navy veteran of W.W. II and Korea, he retired as a Commander. He is survived by a daughter, Jane, of Natick, MA. Donations in his memory may be made to the Dr. Maurice Lenarksy Scholarship, Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, Office of Medical Alumni Affairs, 1620 McElderry Street, Baltimore, MD 21205-1911. Graveside services, Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, Long Island, New York, on January 7 at 1:00 PM. Levine Chapels 617-277-8300.
