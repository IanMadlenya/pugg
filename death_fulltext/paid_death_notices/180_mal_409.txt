
        WOLFF-Werner. Born June 11, 1911. Husband for 60 years of Alice E. Wolff, father of Steven and Mark H. Wolff, grandfather of Dr. Elizabeth R. Wolff and Avery M. Wolff. Werner Wolff was a prominent photojournalist who worked with Alfred Eisenstaedt and later went on to photograph such notables as Albert Einstein, Eleanor Roosevelt and John F. Kennedy, among many others. He died at home after a long illness with his family at hand.
