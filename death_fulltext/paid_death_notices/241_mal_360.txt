
        STUART--Irv. 88 years. Completed his journey on earth February 22, after a valiant battle with leukemia. Extremely devoted husband of 63 years to Flora; much-loved father to daughter Joan and son Ronald, and grandfather to Jennifer, Marc, Sean and Amber. Long-time resident of both Montauk, NY and Palm Beach, FL. He was a very sweet, gentle man who will be missed by many. The Stuart Family
