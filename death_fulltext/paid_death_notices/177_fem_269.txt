
        BAGLEY-Mildred J. (Sokol), 93, formerly of Great Neck & Miami Beach, peacefully passed away Friday, October 19, 2001, at home after suffering a long, courageous battle with illness. Devoted wife of the late William A. Bagley and Milton Sokol; loving mother of Lynne & Murray Norkin, and Michael & Maria Bagley. Cherished sister of Gladys Danoff & Phyllis Newman. Dear stepmother of Barbara Lee & Richard Kahn and Judy & Martin Sokol. Beloved grandmother of Staci, Jeffrey, Elizabeth, Sophia, Margaret; adored great-grandmother. We mourn her loss. She will be deeply missed by all her beloved family, nieces & nephews.
