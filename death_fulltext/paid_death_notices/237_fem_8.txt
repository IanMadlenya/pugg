
        SCHNEIDER--Lillian. 95, formerly of Long Beach, NY, currently of New York City, NY and Boca Raton, FL passed away on Thursday, September 28, 2006. She will always be remembered by those near and dear to her. Her zest for living life to the fullest was immeasurable. She lived by the motto ''laughter is the best medicine.'' Lillian's greatest joy in life was her family, her son & daughter-in-law, Steven & Ann, her daughter, Dorothy, her grandchildren, Jodi, Gary, David & Rosanne, & Lisa & Scott, her great grandchildren, Joshua, Melissa, Zachary, Stephanie & Ian, her two sisters' Edna & Eunice and her brother, Lou, as well as her nieces, nephews, cousins and friends. ''Lilly,'' you'll always be in our hearts! Services will be held Sunday, October 1st at 11:30 AM, Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, New York City, NY.
