
        LEWITTES-Aaron. Died August 27, 2001 at the age of 88 years. Cherished husband of the late Estelle. Devoted and proud father and father-in-law of Deborah and Dr. Morris Stampfer, Naomi and Dr. Jack Levenbrown, and Ann Lewittes. Adored grandfather of Dr. David and Amy Stampfer, Sharon Stampfer and Dr. Matthew Weitzman, and Andrea Levenbrown. Loving great-grandfather of Alexandra & Jessica Stampfer and Gavriela and Micah Weitz man. Beloved brother of Dr. Jacob Lewittes. Aaron, an attorney, was a partner in the New York law firm of Leventritt Lewittes and Bender. Earlier he served the United States government on the National Labor Relations Board, as Special Assistant to the Attorney General of the United States, and as a legal advisor to the White House during the Truman Administration. Funeral services will be held at Boulevard-Park West Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY Tuesday, August 28 at 11:30 A.M.
        LEWITTES-Aaron. The Congregation of the Malverne Jewish Center mourns the passing of our dear friend and longtime member Aaron Lewittes. Condolences to his daughters, sons-in-law and grandchildren. Michael Singer, Rabbi Herbert Brodsky, President
