
        FORGERON--Harry V., of Baldwin, NY, age 86, died May 24, after a two year battle with cancer. Born and raised in Chatham, MA, Harry attended NYU School of Journalism. After serving in the Army Air Corps during WWII, he joined the Sports Department of the New York Times, and enjoyed a 42 year career retiring in 1988. A memorial service will be held Thursday, May 31, at 7pm at Cecere-Pensa Funeral Home, 2283 Grand Ave., Baldwin.
