
        FEGAN - Helen Alvord (nee Brown). Daughter of George A. Brown and Charlotte (nee Pitts). Predeceased by her dearest brother Cyril Brown, beloved sister Ethel Mae Fegan (Brown) and devoted husband Matthew W. Fegan. Initially a librarian in the Brooklyn Public Library, she was for many years Library Director of the East Rockaway Free Library on Long Island until her retirement. Survived by her cherished nephews, Matthew W. Fegan, Jr. and Jerard Gliner. Reposing Sunday at Connell Funeral Home, Huntington, NY 3-5 & 7-9 P.M.
