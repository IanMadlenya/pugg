
        BARRIE--Jeffrey E. Jeff lost his life on March 8 when the taxi he was taking from the airport to his Moscow home lost control. Born in Baltimore, Maryland on January 6th, 1941. He is survived by his family; his mother, Mickey, brother Andy, his sons Marc and Brian, their mother, Fran, their grandchildren Katya and Evan, his second wife, Maureen and Jeff's partner in life, Marina Barkoskya.
