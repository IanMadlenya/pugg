
        DeBOTTON-Gilbert. The Weizmann Institute of Science deeply mourns the suddenly passing of Gilbert DeBotton, the stalwart benefactor and member of The Executive Council, a prominent member of The Board of Governors, and its Former Deputy Chairman, President of The European Committee of The Weizmann Institute of Science. The Institute extends sincere condolences to his widow, The Honorable Mrs. Janet DeBotton, and to the bereaved family. Gershon Kekst, Chairman, Board of Governors Professor Haim Harari, President
