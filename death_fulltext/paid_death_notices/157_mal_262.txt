
        BINET-Dennis. Age 76, on Feb. 19, 2000. Survived by his partner, Toni Sacks, and her family, his sons, Larry & Tommy, & their families; four grandchildren; two greatgrandchildren; nieces and nephews & many friends. Hungarian immigrant, served in US Army in WWII, co-founder of 47 year old Universal Elevator Company, Inc. In lieu of flowers please make a donation to a cancer-related organization or other charity or hospice of your choice. Memorial service Sunday Feb 27th at 1:45PM at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. A gathering to celebrate his life immediately following at Empire Hotel, 44 W. 63rd St., Mezzanine Ballroom, 3:30-5:30PM.
