
        SPIEGEL -- Mathias Lloyd, age 77, died at home, January 21, 2004. A lawyer, a potter and former official of the City of New York, he is survived by his sons, Paul and Andrew Spiegel, and his grandsons Drew and Matthew. His extended family includes his long-time companion, Harriet Yarmolinsky, her four children, and their families. Donations may be sent to Haystack Mountain School of Crafts, P.O. Box 518, Deer Isle, ME 04627.
