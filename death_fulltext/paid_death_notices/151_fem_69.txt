
        GIBSON-Barbara Sue. The Division of Substance Abuse and Adiction Medicine of the Department of Psychiatry of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine-Montefiore Medical Center express sincere condolences on the sudden accidental death of our colleague and friend, Barbara Sue Gibson and her mother, Susan Gibson. Barbara was a consumate professional and cared deeply about quality care for the patients we treat and was devoted to upgrading the skills of those who work in our programs. Our deepest sympathy to our friend and Einstein Berle Award Recipient, Dr. Beny J. Primm and to her family, friends and colleagues. Ira J. Marion, Executive Director
