
        McSHANE-Terence A. Of West Islip, L.I. Died heroically in the line of duty at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. Devoted husband of Cathy. Adored father of Aidan, Sean and Colin. Cherished son of Jeanne and the late John P. McShane. Beloved brother of Kieran M. McShane, Brian McShane, John McShane and Maribeth Eccleston. Dear sonin-law of Robert and Pauline Watt. Special uncle of John, Daniel and Kieran A. McShane. Cherished friend to all that knew him. Beloved brother Firefighter at Ladder 101 and Engine 202, Brooklyn, N.Y. and Engine 308 Queens, N.Y.
