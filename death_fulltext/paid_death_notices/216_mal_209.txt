
        SMITH--Judson Stewart. 81, of Pine Plains, NY, died peacefully on Sunday, January 9th, 11:45 am. Mr. Smith was born June 21, 1923, son of the late Caroline Stewart Smith and the late Howard Clark Smith. He interrupted his studies at Harvard to join the US Air Corps during World War II, serving as a fighter pilot. After the war, Mr. Smith graduated from Harvard University and from the Harvard Business School. He then became associated with De Coppet and Doremus in New York City and was a member of the New York Stock Exchange. He is survived by his wife Mary Ann Smith, three daughters, Kimball Wheeler, Victoria Moore, Elodie Emig, a sister Harriet Smith Collins, and stepdaughters Anna K. Marrian and Samantha Free. A memorial service will be held later in the spring.
