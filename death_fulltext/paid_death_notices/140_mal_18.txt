
        GREENBERG-Marilyn. The Officers, Trustees, congregants and clergy of The Jewish Center of the Hamptons mourn the passing of Marilyn Greenberg, beloved wife of Rabbi Emeritus David Greenberg, and mother of Rachel, Ruth, Ethan, Adam and Susannah. We extend deepest condolences to Rabbi Greenberg and his family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Cantor Debra Stein-Davidson Joseph Oppenheimer, President
