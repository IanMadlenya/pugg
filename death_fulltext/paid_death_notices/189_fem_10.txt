
        ANDERSON-Joyce Lenore. On September 22, 2002. Beloved wife of Earl. Devoted mother of Mark, Wendy Petersen, and Scott. Loving grandmother of Brooke Paige and Danielle. Cherished sister of Susan Weiner. Daughter of the late Louis and Sarah Weiner. Born September 16, 1930 in Brooklyn, New York. Had 30 year career as a Homebound Teacher with the N.Y.C. Board of Education. Longstanding member of the Unitarian Church of Shelter Rock and member of the women's group. Private memorial service. Donations may be made in her memory to the American Cancer Society.
