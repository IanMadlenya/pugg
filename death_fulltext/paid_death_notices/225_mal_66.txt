
        CASALINI--Angelo Frank. Age 83, died on September 30, 2005, in his home in New York City. Beloved husband of the late Frieda May, dearest brother of Victoria Petronio, Antoinette Psathas, and Mary Papadelis, dearest uncle of Hope, Paula, Michael, Deborah, Fred, Norm, and Rosalind, and devoted grandfather of Gille. Angelo was the manager of The City Center Theater for many years, a long-time member of The Association of Theatrical Press Agents and Managers, and former member of the Ticket Sellers Union. He was a W.W. II veteran, serving as a medic in the Army. He loved the theater and travel. He loved to tell stories and make people laugh. Most of all, he loved his life with his wife, and they are now reunited. Angelo was a sweet, caring, generous, gentle man, whose loss leaves a hole in the hearts of his family and friends. Services will be held on Sunday, October 9th, from 9-11 A.M., at The John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Avenue, Red Bank, NJ 07701. The funeral will be held on Sunday, October 9, at 1 P.M., at The New Mount Carmel Cemetery, 83-45 Cypress Hills Street, Glendale (Brooklyn), NY 11385. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The American Heart Association, 2550 Route 1, North Brunswick, NJ 08902.
