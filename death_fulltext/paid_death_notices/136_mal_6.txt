
        MEARES-Charles W. V. The Board of Directors, Administration and Medical Staff of Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital mourn the passing of Charles W.V. Meares on April 29, 1998. Mr. Meares was a dedicated member of our Board of Directors for 42 years, including 12 years as Chairman & most recently as Honorary Chairman. He served as Chairman of the Budget Committee and as a member of the Executive, Planning, Building and Development Committees. His leadership helped guide our hospital, and his wisdom and wit inspired us all. To his daughter, Jennifer Meinert and all his family and friends, we extend our sincere condolences. Lindsay C. Herkness, III President George A. Sarkar, Ph.D., J.D. Executive Director David R. Edelstein, M.D. President, Board of Surgeon Directors
