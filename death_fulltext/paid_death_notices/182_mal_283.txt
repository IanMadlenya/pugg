
        GROSSMAN-Edward N. Beloved husband and friend of Selma for 50 years, died March 17, at home in Scarsdale. He will be forever loved by son Jeff and wife Sorena, daughter Jody, and grandchildren Casey, Jesse & Joel. Ed's humor, integrity and love made him an incredible husband, father, friend and teacher. He taught for 48 years in Scarsdale and Levittown, and will be remembered by all the people whose lives he touched. Donations may be made to Hospice of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, White Plains, New York 10601.
