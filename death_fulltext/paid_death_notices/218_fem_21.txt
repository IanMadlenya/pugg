
        NORWICK--Therese Thoisy. Born January 10, 1918, died February 20, 2005. Adored wife of 65 years, beloved of many. During World War II she worked for the War Dept. and as a speaker for the Voice of America. Later as one of a two-person team, she helped establish the French presence in American commercial electronics for a company now named Thales. She is survived by her husband, Braham, her son Noel and by many friends here and family in France.
