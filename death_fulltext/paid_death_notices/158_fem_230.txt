
        KING-Joan Hone. Died March 26, 2000 at age 70. Writer and international art dealer. Beloved wife of the late Nicholas LeRoy King. Loving mother of Sarah, Bayard & Ledyard. Grandmother of Harry, Marina, Nicholas, Samantha & Gregory. Sister of Frederica Neff, Eugenie Angles and Philip Hone Auerbach. Funeral Thursday, March 30, Saint Columba's Chapel at the Cathedral of Saint John the Devine, Amsterdam Avenue at 116 St, 3PM. Interment Friday, 12 noon at Island Cemetery, Newport, R.I. Donations in her memory may be made to the Edward King House senior citizens' center, 35 King St, Newport, R.I. 02840.
