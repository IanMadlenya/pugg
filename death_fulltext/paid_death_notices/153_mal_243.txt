
        HIRSCH-Ida. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of Ida Hirsch, beloved mother of our dear friend, Michael D. Hirsch. Michael, an esteemed memeber of the executive board of our Young Men's Division, and his wife, Jane, are respected members of our Society of Founders. To Michael, Jane, and the entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine David H. Schwartz, Chairman Young Men's Division
