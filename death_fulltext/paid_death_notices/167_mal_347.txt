
        SPIEGEL-Leo, the son of Harry and Sadie Spiegel, and retired president of Harry Spiegel, Inc., died on Monday in New York. He was 89 years old. Mr. Spiegel is survived by his loving and devoted wife, Evelyn of Scarsdale, three sons Robert M. Spiegel of Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, Dr. Edward F. Spiegel of Scarsdale, New York, and Dr. Allan M. Spiegel of Palm Harbor, FL. He also leaves seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren.
        SPIEGEL-Leo. Past Potentate. Mecca Shrine Temple. Services 10:30 A.M. at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, New York.
