
        TWERSKY-David, Grand Rabbi. A distinguished and respected Hasidic leader who has guided thousands with counsel and referrals on matters of medical health and domestic affairs. A scion of the royal family of Tzchernobel, he was affectionately called the Skwere Rebbe of Boro Park. He is survived by his Rebbetzin, five sons and two daughters. In keeping with sacred tradition, a funeral was held Friday, February 2 with thousands of mourners. Interment was at Beth David Cemetery just hours after death. Shiva is being observed until early Thursday morning at Congregation Khal Skwere, 1336 47 St. Brooklyn, NY.
