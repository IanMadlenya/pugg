
        GARDNER-Irene. On April 7, 2003 in her 90th year, after a long illness. A resident of Amityville, LI, Mrs. Gardner was the daughter of the late Dr. and Mrs. Alfred Loewenstein and the wife of the late Frederick Gardner. A native of Ichenhausen, Germany, she served in the British Army, North African Campaign, during WW II. She was educated at the Sorbonne, Columbia Univerity and NYU and maintained close association with the latter institution all her life. She was a passionate supporter of many causes - mainly animal protection and conservation organizations, the Democratic Party and Public Broadcasting. She loved literature and books, and with her husband, was a member of the Long Island Book Collectors. She was a vibrant and active woman who traveled extensively, and will be missed by family and friends all over the world.
