
        REILLY -- Frank. Beloved husband of Genevieve, father of Dorine, Brian and Brendan, stepfather of Michael, grandfather of Jonathan, Jeremiah, Megan and Amy, brother of Mary and Martin, dear uncle of many nieces and nephews, died on March 20. He was 79 and was predeceased by brothers James, John and Gerard and stepson, Shaun Murray. His boundless good will, optimism, joy and energy made his company a delight to all whom he touched. A mass of Christian Burial will take place at St. Boniface Church, 145 Glen Ave, Sea Cliff, NY on Saturday, March 27, 9:30AM.
