
        SALAMONE--Alfred W. ''Al'', 83, passed away May 6 in Key Biscayne, FL. Survived by his companion Maxine and his brother Frank and family of Harrison, NY. Son of Mr. and Mrs. Antonio Salamone. Al, a lifetime member of Westchester, NY Professional Golfer Association, a retired Army captain having served in five Theaters of war and landed on D-Day in France during WWII, received the Purple Heart for his outstanding military service, served as a member of the board at Rye Bank and Professional Golfer Association. During his active playing years, he won many outstanding tournaments. A past member of a Surf Club in Miami Beach, he will be missed by his friends and family.
