
        KATZ-Eleanor. The Officers, Members and Staff of the American Jewish Committee are deeply saddened by the death of Eleanor Katz, who had served as AJC's Director of Personnel for more than 20 years when she retired in 1977. We send our heartfelt condolences to her family. Bruce M. Ramer, President David A. Harris, Executive Dir. Shulamith Bahat, Assoc. Exec. Dir.
