
        WHITE - Elizabeth Cleveland Spear (Mrs. William R. White), F.G.B.S. The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society regrets the passing of its 20th president (1983-1986) since its founding in 1869. Mrs. White's notable work was as member of the Society's publication committees since 1936, and as editor maintaining the Society's ''Record'' as a leading genealogical publication in America. A long-time trustee (1963-1993) and Fellow she was Honorary President at her death on February 4, 1998. Henry Middendorf, President Henry C.B. Lindh, Chairman Executive Commietee Edward F.L. Bruen, Secretary
