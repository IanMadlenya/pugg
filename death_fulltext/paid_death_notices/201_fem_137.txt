
        GIFFORD -- Marjorie Otis, composer, choir master and music teacher, died at her home in Katonah, NY, on October 8 at the age of 94. Starting in 1930, she taught at various schools, notably Wykeham Rise in Washington, CT; Nightengale Bamford and Lenox in Manhattan; and the Bedford Rippowam School. She then served for 15 years as a volunteer in the Bedford Correctional System teaching both men and women. Mrs. Gifford was a powerful influence in the lives of her many pupils. A memorial service will be held on November 8 at 2 PM at Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts in Katonah. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Caramoor, 149 Girdle Ridge Rd, Katonah, NY 10536
