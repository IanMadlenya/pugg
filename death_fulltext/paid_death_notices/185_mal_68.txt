
        GILLIES-Ewen. Of Manhattan, June 3, 2002, age 77, of cancer at Calvary Hospital. He was founder of Gillies and Zaiser, a public relations firm. Beloved husband of the late Carol Zaiser Gillies. He will be greatly missed by his surviving sons Lindsay, Elliot and Adrian, his daughters-in-law Sarah, Jeanne, Michele and Chrissa, his ten grandchildren, his sister-in-law Marilyn Ott and her family, his longtime business associate Jan Myers and his many friends in this city, the U.S. and across the world. Contributions in his name can be made to the Oncology Research Fund, NYU Cancer Institute, c/o Dr. James Speyer, 160 East 32 St, NY, NY 10016. Memorial service to be announced.
