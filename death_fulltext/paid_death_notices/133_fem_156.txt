
        LOMAZOV-Elsie. The Officers, Directors, Administration and Staff of the the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center extend their heartfelt sympathy to Bernice Zwanger, member of the Board of Directors, on the passing of her mother, Elsie Lomazov. Elsie had been a loved resident of the Gurwin Center and will be missed. Joseph Gurwin, President Alfred W. Levy, Chairman of the Board Herbert H. Friedman, Executive Vice President
