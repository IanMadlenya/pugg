
        MEEKIN-John R. Died on January 8, 2003 at the age of 77. A retired vice president of the Chase Manhattan Bank, he was a warden emeritus of the Church of the Ascension, a trustee of the Church Club of New York, and a member of the University Club and the Grolier Club. He was a graduate of DePauw University with distinction and served as a naval officer in World War II. A service will be held at 11 AM on Wednesday, January 15 at the Church of the Ascension, Fifth Ave at 10th St. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Music Fund of the Church of the Ascension.
