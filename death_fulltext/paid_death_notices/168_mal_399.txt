
        BALISH-Leonard. Beloved husband of Jacquelyn. Artist, photographer, antique dealer. Born in NYC August 12, 1921. Died suddenly at his home in Englewood, NJ, on January 24. Mr. Balish was a veteran of the Army Air Corps World War II 1941-1945. Student at the Art Students League. His photographs are in many private, public and museum collections and have been shown in numerous exhibitions. He was an antique dealer with his wife for over 30 years, exhibiting at many New York venues, as well as the Boston Ellis and University Hospital Show in Philadelphia. He was a member of the Antique Dealers of America and a founding member of the Ephemera Society of America.
