
        JOLOWICZ-Peter. Age 87 of New Haven, CT, died January 29, 2000. Father of Katherine of Los Angeles, brother of Raymundo in Mexico and Franz Dieter Jolowicz of NYC. Also survived by stepdaughter Sasha Dobush of Tenafly, two step-grandchildren Jennifer and Nick, two nephews Alan and Ivan. Mr. Jolowicz received his engineering degree from the Technische Hochschule in Zurich and was a long time New Haven resident. He was fluent in Spanish and during his professional career was frequently sent to Latin American countries to supervise construction projects or feasibility studies. After retirement he continued to accept such assignments in Egypt, Panama Canal and the Phillipines. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
