
        BERNHEIM-James. Died on March 28, 2001. Born in 1919 in New York City. He was the son of Alfred and Sara Bernheim. He was a graduate of Johns Hopkins and the Columbia University School of Law. After law school he practiced with the firm of Parker Duryee in which he became a partner, and retired in 1983 to Ft. Lauderdale. He is survived by his wife Helen Bernheim, nee Helen Patestides; father of Joseph Bernheim, Katherine Gwin, Ann Lewent and Vicki Parks, and grandfather of Susanannah, Joseph, Kimberly, Alexis & Gabriel. Services will be private. The family would like to express their gratitude to the medical staff of Broward General Hospital for their exceptional care of James Bernheim during the last month of his life.
