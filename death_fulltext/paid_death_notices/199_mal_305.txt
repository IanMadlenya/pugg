
        HOLMES--Neal H. Of Pittsburgh, PA., on Wednesday August 27, 2003. Mr. Holmes was co-founder of Allied Security Inc. where he served as C.E.O. and Chairman of the Board. He also served as Chairman of the Board for the Allegheny County Port Authority and a Board member of the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center. He was a veteran of the U.S. Army. Survived by wife June Holmes, Children Renee Holmes, Neal H. Holmes II. stepchildren Jamie Beck and James Aiello Jr.
