
        NORMAN-Frederick Cole. 92, husband of the late Lillian Mitchell Norman and father of Gilbert B. Norman and Barbara N. Bankson, died peacefully at his home, ''Edgehill,'' in Stamford CT. Survived also by his stepson and daughter, three grandchildren, two nieces, three grand nieces, two grand nephews and one great grand nephew, he loved and was loved by his family. A memorial service will be held at the Presbyterian Church of Old Greenwich, 11 AM, September 8.
