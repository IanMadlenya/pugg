
        WERNER -- Maribeth. 60, passed away on Tuesday, June 21, 2005 following a 25 year battle with cancer. The gallantry with which Maribeth consistently fought this disease was an inspiration to many, and the enormity of this loss cannot be expressed in words. Maribeth, daughter of Elizabeth and Albert Lubell is survived by William, her loving husband of 33 years, daughter Cori and sonin-law Scott Neustein, son Rick, granddaughter Erin, Stacey Lubell, step-children Debi and Steven Neustadter, Glenn and Cookman Campbell Werner, and countless friends. Services will be held on June 23, 2005 at 11:30 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to Damon Runyon Cancer Research Foundation, 675 Third Avenue, New York, NY 10017, 1 - 877 - 7CANCER or www.drcrf.org.
