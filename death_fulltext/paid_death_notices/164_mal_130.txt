
        GENGLER-Thomas Edmund. On September 1, 2000 of Malverne, N.Y. Devoted husband of Eleanor for 49 years and father of Tom (Mary), June (Jay), Bill, Jane (Kevin) and Barbara. Grandfather of Marie, Nancy, Tommy, Ed, Laura, Matt (Jaime), Joe, Robin, Kevin, Ryan, and George. Also survived by his brother Al and his wife Liz, his sisters Madeline & Rosemary his sisters-in-law Marge and Jackie, his brother-in-law Harold, (Loretta) and served in the U. S. Army in WW II in Italy and Africa. Active alumni and friend of Pace University, Class of '49. Semi-retired since 1981, after 19 years as President of Year Round Sales, a Tupperware distributorship. Tom and Eleanor traveled extensively for Tupperware, including two years in Malaysia. Tom will be greatly missed by his family, his Tupperware family, and many other friends from all over the world. Friends may visit Macken Mortuary, 516-766-3300, 52 Clinton Ave, Rockville Centre, NY. Visiting hours Tuesday 2-4PM and 7-9PM. Funeral mass will be held at Our Lady of Lourdes Church, Malverne, Wednesday at 11:15 AM. Interment St. Johns Cemetery, Middle Village, N.Y.
