
        GLADSTONE - Robert ''Bob.'' Longtime broadcaster, on August 3, in California at age 72, of heart failure. He is survived by his devoted wife Barbara, loving daughters Wendi and Dayna, and brother William. There was a private service and cremation. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Humane Society, US. ''There is a special providence in the fall of a sparrow. If it be now, 'tis not to come; it if be not to come, it will be now; if it be not now, yet it will come: the readiness is all...'' (W.S.)
