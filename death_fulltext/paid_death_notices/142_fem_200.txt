
        FELDMAN-Betty, of Bal Harbour, Florida, formerly of Forest Hills, New York, age 91, on November 22. Beloved wife of the late Sidney Feldman. Much loved mother of Joan, Robert and Linda. Devoted sister to Grace, David and the late Herman. Adored grandmother of Jane, Gerald, Andrew, Liz, Andrea, Stephen, Wendy and Karen and great grandmother of Cole. Private services. Shiva will be observed at the home of Linda and Robert Feldman. Contributions in her memory may be sent to the Betty and Sidney Feldman Memorial Fund, John Carter Brown Library, Brown University, Box 1894, Providence, Rhode Island 02912.
