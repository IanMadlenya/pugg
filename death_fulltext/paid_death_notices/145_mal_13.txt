
        HERLIHY-Edward J. January 30, 1999. Beloved husband of Fredi. Loving father of Lori Selden, Jeannie, Donald and Stephen Herlihy. Cherished grandfather of Tara Heimberger, Brian Bergen and Graham Lehr, Taylor and Logan Herlihy. Cherished greatgrandfather of Jake Heimberger. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10 AM, St. John the Evangelist Church, 348 E. 55th St. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Actor's Fund or League for the Hard of Hearing.
        HERLIHY-Ed. The League for the Hard of Hearing mourns the passing of a dear friend, a warm and caring man, and recipient of our ''great communicator'' award in 1994. Our deepest sympathy to Fredi and family. John M. Scopaz, President League for the Hard of Hearing
        HERLIHY-Edward J. We mourn the loss of our beloved and esteemed member, Ed. A wonderful story teller, a fine gentleman and great company in The Grill. We will miss his counsel and his presence. Our love to Fredi and their family. The Players
        HERLIHY-Edward. The Actors' Fund of America mourns the loss of Edward Herlihy, a loyal and compassinate Trustee who cared so deeply about all entertainment professionals in need. Tom Dillon, President Joseph Benincasa, Exec Director
