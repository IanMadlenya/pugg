
        LEEDS-Harold Eliot. On October 29th, 2002 in his 89th year in NYC. He founded the graduate program of Interior Design at the Pratt Institute where he was head of the department. He is survived by his companion Wheaton Galentine. A memorial will take place on Wednesday November 20 at 5 P.M. at The Century Association, 7 West 43 St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to The Village Nursing Home, 607 Hudson St, NY, NY 10014.
