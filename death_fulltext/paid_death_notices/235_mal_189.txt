
        BAUMAN--Mortimer. On August 19, 2006. Beloved husband of Carolyn, father of Larry, John and Sherry. Loving grandfather of Laura. Leader in the lamp manufacturing industry, veteran, longtime member of Rockrimmon Country Club. Loved by all who were fortunate enough to know him. In his 92nd year, still playing golf, rooting for the Yankees and Mets, and quoting Henny Youngman right to the end. Memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th St, Monday, 12:40 p.m.
