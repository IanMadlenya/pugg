
        KUSHEL-Lillian. 86, on November 23rd, 2001. Beloved wife of Bernard, devoted mother of Kenneth, cherished grandmother of Marika. Sister of Milton Shapiro, the late Miriam Fox, Edwin Shapiro, and Adele Leonard. She was a revered professor of fashion, buying, and merchandising for over forty years. The lives of thousands of her students were graced by her wisdom, expertise and generosity. Services Sunday, noon, at Sherman's Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, NY.
