
        KOYCE-Mary (nee Petrie). Of Morristown, NJ, on Thursday, January 8, 1998. Wife of John P. Koyce, Jr, mother of Patricia Wanniski of Morris Twp, NJ, sister of Donald, John, Alexander,and Robert Petrie. She was a secretary at Dancer Fitzgerald & Sample Advertising Agency in New York City from 1948-1960. Funeral Mass on Monday, January 12, 1998 at the Church of Christ the King, New Vernon, NJ at 11AM. Hours of visitation at the Doyle Funeral Home, 106 Maple Ave, Morristown, NJ on Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM.
