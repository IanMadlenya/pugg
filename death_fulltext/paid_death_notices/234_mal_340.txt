
        LIEBERMAN - Marvin, 77. Died July 28, 2006. Retired President of Lauren Knitwear. He is survived by his loving wife Gloria, his children Stephanie, Jeffrey and daughter-in-law Gail, grandchildren, Amie and Jesse, as well as his sister and brotherin-law Eleanor and Jerry Schneider, his brother Jerry and his wife Sheila. He will be greatly missed by his nieces and nephews, Jennie, Jed, John, Hedi Ann and their children. His loving and generous nature will be remembered by all who knew him.
