
        RUSSELL-VANDER POEL-Wendy. A lifelong resident of Oyster Bay, Long Island, died peacefully at home on March 9, after a long illness. Mrs. Russell is survived by her daughters, Wendell Miller Steavenson of London, England, and Gretchen Miller Elkus of Ligonier, PA, six grandchildren, William M. Duryea III, Melissa Duryea Lewis, Wendell E., Michael McL. and Alexander H. Steavenson, and James M. Elkus, and five great-grandchildren. She was born on February 6, 1919, to Gretchen Billings and William Halsted Vander Poel of New York and Oyster Bay. Mrs. Russell served for many years on the boards of The Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, The EyeBank for Sight Restoration and The Hutton House Lectures of C.W. Post. A memorial service will be held on March 17th at 11 am, at Christ Church, 61 East Main St, Oyster Bay. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, 1 Bungtown Road, Cold Spring Harbor, NY 11724; or The Eye-Bank for Sight Restoration, Inc., 120 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005. For further information please contact the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home, Inc; 516-922-6700.
