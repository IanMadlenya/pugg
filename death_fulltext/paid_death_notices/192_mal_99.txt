
        LEVEY-Arthur J., 79. San Diego, CA. formerly of NYC. Died January 6, 2003. Husband of Marsha, father of Sue Levey, Jayne Charles and her husband Stan, and Randee Weinberger, brother of Martin, uncle, grandfather, stepfather and friend. He will be forever loved and always remembered with tears and laughter. Donations in his memory can be made to the National Kidney Foundation, 30 East 33 St, NY 10016.
