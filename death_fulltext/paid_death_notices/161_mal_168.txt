
        KIRKWOOD-Robert Thomas, Sr. 88, died peacefully at his home on June 13, 2000. He is survived by his beloved wife Frederica and children Jane, Diane and Robert, Jr., grandson Michael Clancy and great granddaughters Shawn and Miranda Clancy. Funeral service Thursday, June 15, 2000 7:30 pm at the Leahy-McDonald Funeral Home corner of Atlantic Avenue and 111th Street, Richmond Hill, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the United Methodist Church of Richmond Hill Memorial Fund 112-14 107th Avenue, Richmond Hill, NY 11419. Interment private.
