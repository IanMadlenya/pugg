
        ROTHSCHILD-Dr. Jacob. The Advanced Center for Psychotherapy of Queens, New York, mourns the passing of Dr. Jacob Rothschild, esteemed colleague and distinguished member of our staff for forty years. His dedication to his patients and generous sharing of knowledge with fellow clinicians were highly valued and will be missed. We extend very sincere condolences to his family. Administration, Board of Directors, Staff Advanced Center for Psychotherapy
