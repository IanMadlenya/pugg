
        BERMAN--Joseph. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) mourn the loss of Joseph Berman of Forest Hills, NY. A Technion graduate with a degree in Civil Engineering and Construction, he and his wife Miriam were longtime and steadfast ATS supporters. We extend our sincere condolences to Mr. Bermans beloved wife Miriam and the entire Berman family. American Technion Society Joan Seidel, President Stephen A. Laser, Chairman of the Board Melvyn H. Bloom, Executive Vice President Stanley Shirvan, Chairman, New York Metropolitan Region Alan Forman, Chairman Elect, New York Metropolitan Region Nancy Gad-Harf, Director, New York Metropolitan Region
