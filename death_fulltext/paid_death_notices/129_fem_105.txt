
        KLATTSKY-Bessie Klatt. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community, beloved sister of the late Sidney Weisner, a YU Benefactor. We extend heartfelt condolences to her son, Robert Klatt; grandchildren, Brad Klatt and Bambi Goode; great-grandchildren, and the entire Weisner/Klatt family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
