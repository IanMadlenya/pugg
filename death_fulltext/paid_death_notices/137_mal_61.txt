
        KRONENBERG - Bernard, M.D. Died June 4, 1998 after a long illness. Survived by his beloved wife Gloria, father of Lori, Stephen and Debra. Grandfather of Stephanie and Michael. Celebration of his life at Beach Point Club, Mamaroneck, NY on June 14, 1998 at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers, memorials to the Jewish Braille Institute of America or the Jewish Museum.
        KRONENBERG - Bernard, M.D. The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary notes with sorrow the passing of an esteemed member of the medical staff for more than 50 years. We extend our sincerest condolences to his son, Dr. Stephen Kronenberg and the entire family. Robert C. Della Rocca, M.D. Chairman Medical Board Joseph P. Corcoran President and CEO
        KRONENBERG - Bernard, M.D. The members of the New York State Ophthalmological Society are deeply saddened to note the death of their friend and esteemed colleague, Bernard Kronenberg, M.D. His contributions to his profession, his leadership and vision as the second President of the Society, and his caring, gentle manner serve as an inspiration to all.
