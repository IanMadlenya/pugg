
        GARLICK-Harold. Died peacefully Tuesday, December 30, 1997 at his home in Boca Raton, FL. A long-time resident of New York, Harold and his wife moved to Florida in 1993 where he established his permanent residence in Boca Raton, FL. He was formerly the President of Garlick Funeral Homes, also on the Board of the Jewish Funeral Directors of America, a Kentucky Colonel. He was a member of the Knights of Pythias, Veritus Lodge and other Pythian lodges. He is survived by his dearly beloved wife Eileen, his nephews Saul, Jonathan and David Garlick, and his great nieces and nephews. Services will be held Sunday, January 4th at 12PM at Garlick's, 1700 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, New York. Contributions are to be made to your favorite charity.
        GARLICK-Harold. Departed this world December 30. An employer who had no equal, his caring and devotion to his employees/friends cannot be matched. His kindness and extreme generosity will never be forgotten. His Staff/Friends
