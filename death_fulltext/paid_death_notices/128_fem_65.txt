
        STRAUS-Marjorie Lewengood. 98. Died at home September 5, 1997, after a lengthy illness. Beloved wife of the late Charles Bennett Straus, dear mother of Charles, Jr. and John. Affectionate mother-of-law of Gwyned and Mary, fond grandmother of Charles, III and Timothy and great grandmother of Taylor, Spencer and Hunter. She also leaves her friend and nurse of 19 years, Anne Flynn. A private memorial service is planned.
