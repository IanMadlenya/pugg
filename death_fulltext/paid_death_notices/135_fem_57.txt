
        HOWELL-Louise. Died April 6 in Lynchburg, VA. Born Mendham, N.J. she graduated from Saint Johns Baptist School and Wilson College Chambersburg PA. After affiliation with Oberlin College and over 10 years with the New York Stock Exchange she was Dir of Capital Dev at The Spence School. Miss Howell will posthumously receive the distinguished Alumnae Award of Wilson College on June 6. She is survived by three sisters and 1 brother. A memorial service will be held 4:00 PM Easter Sunday at St. John's Episcopal Church in Lynchburg, VA.
