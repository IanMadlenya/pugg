
        KRAMER--Milton S., 88, at home in Livingston, NJ on April 21, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Lenore Kramer. Father of Evan Kramer, Esq. of Livingston, NJ, Dr. Kenneth Kramer of Easton, PA and Dr. Neil Kramer of New York City. Loving brother of Julian Kramer of South Orange, NJ and Sanford Kramer of Boynton Beach, FL. Survived by thirteen grandchildren and four greatgrandchildren. After leaving the Department of Justice in 1946, he practiced law until the day he died. Widely respected by his colleagues and friends he brought countless thousands of persons to citizenship. He loved the opera, crossword puzzles and books and was a strong liberal all his life. A modest, great man. Graveside services King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ.
