
        COOKE-Len B. Jr. On May 17, 2002, of Bellrose Village. Beloved husband of the late Patricia and former husband of Constance. Loving father of Len B. III, Barbara, Laurie and Duncan. Cherished grandfather of Robert, Leslie, Justin, Patricia and Emily. For over 50 years Mr. Cooke has been well known and respected in the Title Insurance Industry and will be remembered for his charitable work with the YMCA, Salvation Army, Boy Scouts of America, Garden City and Jamaica Rotary, Masons, Bellrose Village Fire Dept and the Floral Park United Methodist Church. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave at 12th St, Garden City, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Monday and Tuesday. Religious service at the funeral home Tuesday, 8 PM. Interment Wednesday, Long Island National Cemetery, Pinelawn. In lieu of flowers donations to Margaret Tietz Center for Nursing, Hospice Unit, 164-11 Chapin Parkway, Jamaica, NY 11432; or the Floral Park United Methodist Church, appreciated.
