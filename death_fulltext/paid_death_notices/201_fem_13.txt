
        WESSLER -- Margaret M. (Muhlfelder) in N. Andover, MA, on October 3, at her residence, 82, formerly of Albany and Rye, NY. Beloved wife of Stanford Wessler, loving mother of John Wessler and Fredi Harwitz of Andover, MA, Steve Wessler and Beth Nagusky of Litchfield, ME, and Jim Wessler and Susan Goodman of Newton, MA, cherished grandmother of seven. Services private. Contributions in her memory may be made to Sarah Lawrence College, Swinford House, One Mead Way, Bronxville, NY, 10708, or to an educational institute of your choice.
