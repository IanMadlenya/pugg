
        BERMAN-Alfred, M.D. FACS. 88 years of age, of Bayside, NY. Beloved husband of Bernice for 53 years. Father of Steven and Wendy Berman of Vermont; Michael and Rachel Berman, and Peter and Jean Berman of New York. He was a devoted family man who took pride in his grandchildren, Megan, Travis and Josh, Ava and Eric, and Jason and Matthew. Dr. Berman was combat surgeon during WW II, and during his career served as Chief of Surgery at Flushing and Booth Memorial Hospitals. His life will be celebrated in remembering how he used his gifts and compassion to ease the suffering of others and save many lives. He will be remembered for his dedication to his golf game, his love of the New York Yankees, and for his great sense of humor.
