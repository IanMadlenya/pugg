
        SOCCI--Susanna (Sue). On February 8, 2004. Born in Camden, NJ, of the late Basilio and Josephine Pizzino. A true lady possessing old world grace and charm, keen intelligence and a uplifting and happy demeanor, with a bottomless well of strength and support for those who needed her in any way. A vital and cherished member of the Flushing community she lived in for over 50 years, her sweetness and kindness also touched many around the world through her gifted correspondence. Widow of Donato, her husband of 52 years, loving mother of Christopher, Silvia Composto, Donna, Lauretta, Anthony and Linda Von Ancken. Devoted and nurturing grandmother of Nicole, Daniela and Gabriella Composto, Brian and Victoria Von Ancken, and Andrea, Michael and Daniel Socci. Reposing at the Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd, Flushing. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, 11:15 am, St. Andrew Avellino R.C. Church. Interment Mount St. Mary Cemetery. Visiting Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-10 pm.
