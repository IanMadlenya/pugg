
        SMOOT--William Sidney, on October 1st, 2003 at age 89. Husband of Katharine S. for 57 years. Father of Sally Smoot Ingraham; grandfather of David Smoot Ingraham and Sarah Jennings Ingraham; brother of Charles Head Smoot and the late Barbara Smoot Ross. A mechanical and electrical engineer, he was an associate in Seelye Stevenson Value & Kneckt and later partner in Wall & Smoot Consulting Engineers. Among his civic and community activities he served as Mayor of the Village of Laurel Hollow from 1969 to 1985 and as President of Action for the Preservation and Conservation of the North Shore of Long Island. Funeral at St. John's Episcopal Church, Cold Spring Harbor, 11 AM Monday, October 6. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
