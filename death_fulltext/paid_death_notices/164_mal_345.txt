
        SHARWELL-William G. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of William Sharwell, past member of the Board of Trustees and beloved friend of long-standing. An unwavering admirer of St. Angela Merici and enthusiastic about the College's Ursuline heritage, Dr. Sharwell was passionate about the College's mission. His wise counsel which he so generously shared, his faithfulness and deep devotion have strengthened the College immeasurably. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D., Pres. Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU, Chair Board of Trustees
        SHARWELL-William G. The Trustees, Residents, and staff of International House, note with sorrow the passing of our Trustee and friend, Bill Sharwell. His wise counsel will be sorely missed. Paul A. Volcker, Chairman Donald L. Cuneo, President
