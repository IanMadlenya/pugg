
        Van BIEMA-Mary (nee Epstein). Beloved wife of Gerhard, mother of David and Michael and grandmother of Joshua and Alex. She received an AB from Vassar College and MLS from Columbia University in Library Science, and was a Librarian at the Mannes College of Music for 15 years. She was an active member of the Tenafly New Jersey League of Women Voters. She loved music and words. We will miss her whimsy and playfulness, her curiosity and her caring. Memorial details will be announced. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to: Frances Lehman Loeb Art Center, Development Office, Vassar College, Box 725, 124 Raymond Avenue, Poughkeepsie, NY 12604, in Honor of Mary Van Biema.
