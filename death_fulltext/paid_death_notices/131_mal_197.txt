
        SCHLESSINGER-Richard H. December 11, 1997 in Rochester, NY. Professor of Chemistry at the University of Rochester since 1963. He is survived by his wife, Lisa M. Demeter; brother Edward D. Schlessinger of CA; nieces, uncle and aunts. Friends may call Sunday, 2-4, 7-9 PM at the Anthony Funeral Chapel, Rochester. Services will be held Monday, 10 AM at the University of Rochester Interfaith Chapel. In remembrance of Dick's compassion for homeless animals, those wishing may direct contributions to the Humane Society Capital Campaign, P.O. Box 66075, Fairport, NY 14450.
