
        GOULD-Lawrence R. paased away on June 25, 2000, after a long illness. Age 85. Brilliant lawyer, inspiring speaker, patient advisor, beloved husband, cherished father, revered grandfather, loyal friend. Greatly respected, enormously loved. You were the steady ship that guided us through the rough waters. We will always be grateful for the gift of your integrity, wisdom, kindness and guidance in our lives. He is survived by his devoted wife of 59 years, Diana Klotz Gould, his loving daughters, Jane G. Bensahel and Suzanne G. Davis, both of Manhattan, and his cherished three grandchildren, Nora J. Bensahel, Bryan J. Davis, and Ariana V. Davis. Memorial service Wednesday, June 28 at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Boulevard, New Rochelle. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to UJA/Federation or to the American Red Cross. GOULD-Lawrence. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its cherished member and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. Amiel Wohl, Rabbi
