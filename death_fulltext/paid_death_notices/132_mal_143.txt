
        STANTON-Thomas J., Jr. The executives and staff of Reliance Group mourn the loss of our board member Thomas J. Stanton Jr. who, for more than thirty years, generously shared his knowledge and wisdom with his colleagues. As a leader in civic and educational affairs, he will be missed throughout our community. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family.
        STANTON-Thomas J., Jr. The Board of Directors and employees of Loral Space & Communications express deep sorrow at the death of their esteemed colleague. Tom Stanton was a man of rare intelligence, integrity, dignity, wit and human kindness. He was a pillar of strength at Loral for more than 20 years, and we will sore miss his wise counsel and warm friendship.
