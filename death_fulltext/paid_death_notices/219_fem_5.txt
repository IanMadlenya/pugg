
        ESTERSOHN -- Betty Hum bert. Painter, educator, and video producer, passed away on Tuesday, March 29th, in Stamford, CT. Born in New York City on December 1st, 1930, she attended Hunter Elementary and High School, Tyler School of Fine Arts at Temple University, and Columbia University. She is survived by her husband Carl, son Pieter, grandson Elio, and sister Susan Zuch.
