
        FOGELSON-David. The International Center of Photography's Trustees and Staff note with sorrow the death of David Fogelson and extend their deepest sympathy to his daughter, ICP Trustee Ellen Liman and to her family. The Center owes a debt of gratitude to the many years of support and encouragement from the Fogelson and Liman families. Cornell Capa Founding Dir. Emeritus Willis Hartshorn, Dir.
