
        DWECK-Harry S., M.D. The Dept. of Pediatrics of New York Medical College/Westchester Medical Center mourns the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague. A giant in the field of neonatology, he pioneered innovative care for sick infants. He established the Regional Tertiary Care Neonatal Center in Westchester and fostered ideals and standards that will improve quality of life for sick infants for generations to come. He will be surely missed.
        DWECK-Dr. Harry. Riverdale Jewish Center mourns the loss of our member, Dr. Harry Dweck. We extend profound condolences to his wife Natalie and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
