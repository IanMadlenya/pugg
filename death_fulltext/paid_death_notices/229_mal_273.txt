
        THOMPSON--Richard M.H. Thompson, a chairman of a pioneering fuel-cell technology company, died yesterday at his home in Florida of cancer. He was 71. He served with Britain's Household Cavalry as a Queen's Lifeguard and graduated from Cambridge University and Harvard Business School. His father, Sir John, was in the Indian Civil Service and served as Governor of Bengal. He is survived by wife Elizabeth, first wife Daniele, children Caroline, Christopher, Victoria and Antonia and grandchildren Anna, Madeleine and Marcel. He will be greatly missed. He will be buried in England with a memorial to follow in Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County.
