
        KINGSLAND-Elizabeth Corbin. Passed away peacefully October 15, 2000, in New York City. She leaves behind a son, Nicholas Corbin Kingsland, of Washington, D.C., & a daughter, Lucia Gesinus Visser, of Versoix, Switzerland. She was devoted to both, and they will both miss her very much. She will also be much missed by her two brothers, R. Beverley Corbin, Jr., of Newport, R.I., and Robert E. Corbin, of Palm Beach, FL. She grew up in Newport, R.I., and New York City, where she spent her final years. Service on Monday, October 23, 2000, 1:30 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, NYC.
