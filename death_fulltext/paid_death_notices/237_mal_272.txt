
        SCHUMAN--Walter Z. , DDS. Of Boca Raton, Fl died on October 24, 2006. Beloved husband of Olga, father of Norman, Mitchell, Joel and Beth (Rosenman), father-in-law of Varda, Ellen, Carole and Mark, and grandfather of Lloyd, Stefanie, Randi, Jason, Laura, Alexandra, Eric, Ari, Josh and Liana. We will always love and remember him. A graduate of NYU (BA) and Temple University School of Dentistry (DDS), he served with distinction in the US Army in WWII and the Korean War.
