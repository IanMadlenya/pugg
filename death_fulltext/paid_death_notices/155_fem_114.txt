
        PRINCE-Suzanne Jill. Writer, died December 8, 1999 in Los Angeles, Calif. Born November 19, 1950 in Brooklyn New York, she spent her early career as a writer for several of the top advertising agencies in Manhattan. She moved to Los Angeles, her new favorite city with her companion, producer Terry Studanski in 1992, where they worked together & generally enjoyed Southern California living. She is survived by her beloved mother, Amy Prince of Delray Beach, FL., her brother and sisterin-law, John and Margie Prince of Stony Point, NY, a niece & nephew and numerous cousins. Memorial gathering in her memory will be held at a later date.
