
        ZIMMERMAN-William. Of North Palm Beach Florida, died September 9, 2000. He was 80. Survived by his sisters, Ida Rubin, Rose Zimmerman and Sarah Tepper, his three children, Stuart Zimmerman, Marc Zimmerman and Susan Chroman, and ten loving grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Mr Zimmerman rose from a modest background to become one of America's most successful retailers, founding closeout specialist Pic 'N' Save (now part of Consolidated Stores) in 1950 and managing it until his retirement in 1985. A great supporter of children's causes and entrepreneurial education, Mr Zimmerman and his foundation were significant contributors to the Boys and Girls Clubs of America and the National Foundation for Teaching Entrepreneurship. Memorial services to be held in Los Angeles Sunday September 24.
