
        MARCUS--Dr. Jules A., 86, retired physics professor, died at home in CT. on November 5, 2005. Son of Julia Parks and Alexander Marcus, attended Ethical Culture and Walden Schools; graduated Yale, '40; married Ruth Barcan, '42; worked for the Navy in WW II; received PhD, Yale, '47; taught at Northwestern U., '49-'89; survived by his sister, his former wife, four children and three grandchildren.
