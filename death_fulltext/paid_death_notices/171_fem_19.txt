
        McCONNELL-Patricia Harden. Patricia was taken from her loving family on April 1, 2001, after a short illness. She will be missed by many people who knew and loved her. She is survived by two sons, Paul Harden and his wife Amy of Boston, Massachusetts, and Adam Harden and his wife Maria of McConnellsville, NY. Included in her family are her two step-daughters, Michelle Sandler of Miami, FL, and Sheila Harden of La Jolla, CA. Her grandchildren, Callie, Ford, Jenna, Frank, Matthew, Nathaniel and Andrea will also miss Patricia. Memorial services will be held at 2 P.M. on Wednesday, April 4, 2001, at the Royal Poinciana Chapel, Palm Beach, Florida, and at 2 P.M. on Saturday, April 7, 2001, at the McConnellsville Community Church, McConnellsville, New York. For information Quattlebaum - Holleman - Burse Funeral Home, (561-832-5171).
