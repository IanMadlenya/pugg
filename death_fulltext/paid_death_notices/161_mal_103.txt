
        BELL-Dr. John B. 80, died June 8, 2000 in Williamsburg, VA. Mr. Bell was a former member of the American Chemical Society, a member and past president of the Chemical Marketing Research Association. He is survived by his wife of 55 years, Ruth Palmer Bell; three sons; six grandchildren. Funeral service Monday, June 12, 2 PM at Williamsburg United Methodist Church with burial on Wednesday, 2:30 PM in Fair View Cemetery, Middletown, NJ.
