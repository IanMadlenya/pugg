
        MARIA-May H. Died on June 8, 2001, in Austin, Texas. She was the first woman to receive a Ph.D in mathematics in 1929 from Rice University in Houston, Texas. In 1938 she married Dr. Alfred J. Maria and both taught at Brooklyn College. She is survived by numerous nieces and nephews. Funeral services June 13, 2001 at 10 A.M. Cook-Walden Funeral Home, 6100 N. Lamar Blvd. Austin, Texas. Memorials may be made to Fondren Library, Rice University, P.O. Box 1892, Houston, TX. 77251-1892
