
        BLUMENGARTEN-Jeanne. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Jeanne Blumengarten. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        BLUMENGARTEN-Jeanne. Beloved mother and grandmother. Retired educator who loved her family, friends and city, and was an active volunteer all her life. Services to be held Thursday 10am at Nieberg Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Ave, Brooklyn
