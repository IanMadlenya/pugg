
        MELLEN - Susan (Maier), age 57. Beloved wife and soul mate of Seymour Mellen. Adored and cherished mother of Richard Phillips. Devoted daughter of Antoinette Maier and the late Ernest (Tex) Maier. Fought bravely and taken too soon. Funeral private.
        MELLEN-Susan M. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Susan Mellen, on April 24, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
