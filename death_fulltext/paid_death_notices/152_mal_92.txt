
        FISHMAN-Julius. Of Palm Beach, Florida on Monday, September 6, 1999, served in the Army Air Corps as a gunner and radio operator during WWII based in England, cofounded Royal Dairy Foods Company, Inc., now a subsidiary of the Fleming Foods, Inc. and was active in many philanthropic causes. He is survived by his wife Ruth nee Berkowitz-Green, sister Libby Seskin of Delray Beach, Fla., son Ted Fishman of Clarksberg, NJ, daughter Joan Brown of New York and beloved grandchildren.
        FISHMAN - Julius. Greenbrook Country Club members and staff extend their condolences to Mrs. Julius Fishman and family for the loss of Julius Fishman, beloved husband and friend, who passed away on September 6, 1999.
