
        BLITZER--John R. 78, died at home on Thursday, June 3. Dr. Blitzer graduated from Harvard Medical School in 1948, and trained in adult psychiatry at the Payne Whitney Clinic of New York Hospital, the teaching hospital of Cornell Medical School. He is survived by his four children and his wife of 56 years, Amy Hughes Blitzer. A memorial service will be held at the Meeting House in Bradford, NH, on August 15, at 11 AM.
