
        ISAACS-Lillian. January 25, 1908June 28, 1999, some two months after her husband, Abe, at 91, on April 22. A wonderful, loving woman, a gentle soul. The Good One. Beloved mother of Stan and Dianne Dunne. Loving mother-inlaw of Bobbie and Tommy, adoring grandmother of Peter, Michael, Nancy, Ann, Ellen and great-grandmother of Laura, David, Danny, Jeffrey, Sara and Andrew. Devoted sister of Jessie Yablonsky.
