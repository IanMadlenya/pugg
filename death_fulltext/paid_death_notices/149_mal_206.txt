
        JENKINS - Thomas (Edward), Brother, F.S.C. age 85, of De La Salle Hall, Lincroft, New Jersey on June 14. He was born in NYC and had received the habit of the Brothers of the Christian Schools in 1931. He began his teaching career at Bishop Loughlin High School, Brooklyn, NY in 1935 and remained there until 1951 when he became the recruiter of the New York Province. In 1958 he was one of the four brothers who opened a new mission in Kenya, East Africa. He returned from his overseas apostolate in 1969. Since then he worked at Christian Brothers Academy, Albany, New York, St. Peter's High School, Staten Island, New York, and Christian Brothers Academy, Lincroft before entering De La Salle Hall in 1995. Calling hours will be held at De La Salle Hall, Lincroft Wednesday from 7-9 PM and Thursday after 2 PM. The Funeral Liturgy will be celebrated at De La Salle Hall on Thursday at 7:30 PM. The Rite of Committal will be held at De La Salle Hall on Friday at 9:30 AM followed by interment at St. Gabriel's Cemetery, Marlboro, New Jersey. Memorial donations to De La Salle Nursing Development, 810 Newman Springs Road, Lincroft, N.J. 07738 would be appreciated. Higgins Memorial Home, Freehold, NJ.
