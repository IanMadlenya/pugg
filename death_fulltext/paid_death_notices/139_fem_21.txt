
        AROND-Irma. EMUNAH of America mourns the untimely passing of Irma Arond, beloved mother of Carol Finkel, our newly appointed Executive Director. Irma's friendship, devotion & spirit will be missed by all who knew her. Condolences to her husband Lionel, her daughters Carol and Miriam, & her sister Estelle Roth. May the family be comforted together with all the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld National President Shirley Singer, Executive VP
