
        PRECHTL-Clement. Age 84, died March 30, 1997. Born in CT, lived in NY and Chicago, then retired to Fort Lauderdale 20 years ago. Veteran of WW II, Lieutenant in Military Intelligence, served in European and North African theaters. Graduated from NYU with a degree in chemical engineering. His life's work was master brewer, his pleasure was traveling the world. Survived by sister-in-law Herta, nephews Donald and Kenneth, grand-nieces and nephew Nicole, Christopher and Sarah. Memorials in his memory may be made to the Hospice Unit, Broward General Medical Center, 1600 South Andrews Ave, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33316.
