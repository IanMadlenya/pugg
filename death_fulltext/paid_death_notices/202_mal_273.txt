
        KRUPP--A. Philip of Chestnut Hill, MA and Palm Beach, FL, entered into rest November 26, 2003. He was the beloved husband of Bernice (Carlin). Dear son of the late Gussie and Joseph Krupp. Devoted father of George and Lizbeth Krupp (Rickles), Douglas and Judith Krupp (Hurwitz) and Joanne Krupp. Cherished grandfather of Michael, Richard, Daniel and Alex Krupp and Justin Sacks-Wilner. Loving brother of William Krupp, and the late Henry, Frederick and Maxwell Krupp. Brother in-law of Doris Krupp, Selma Krupp, Evelyn Medoff, Charlotte Sokoloff, Helen Rubin. Also survived by many loving nieces and nephews. Services will be held Sunday, November 30, at 12:00 Noon at Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, 1668 Beacon St, Brookline, MA. In lieu of flowers, donations in Mr. Krupp's memory may be made to the Anti-Defamation League, 40 Court Street, Boston, MA 02108, or to Ben Gurion University, 2150 Washington St., Newton, MA. 02162.
