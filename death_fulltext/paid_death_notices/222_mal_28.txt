
        YOUNG--T. Gordon. On July 2, 2005 at the age of 88, formerly of Briarcliff Manor and Ossining, NY. Retired Director and Vice President of Hospital for Special Surgery, NYC for 35 years. He served as President of the Greater New York Hospital Association, member and President of the Board of Directors of Mary Manning Walsh, NYC. Member of the Community Board of Phelps Memorial Hospital, Sleepy Hollow, NY. He was a WW II veteran and alumnus of New York University. Survived by daughter Judy L. Young, daughters-inlaw Margaret Chalmers and Rose Mary Young and granddaughter Elizabeth Young. Predeceased by his wife of 61 years Nola I. Young and son Roger Young. Reposing Waterbury and Kelly Funeral Home, Route 9, Ossining on Monday, 7-9 PM, and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Church of the Magdalene, Bedford Road, Pocantico Hills, on Wednesday at 10 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Contributions to Mary Manning Walsh Home, NYC.
