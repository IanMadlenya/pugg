
        SAMET--Greta. Of New York, died on February 28th, 2005, and was buried next to her sister in Israel. She married Joseph Blumberg, who died in 1964, and later Ilya Samet, who passed on in 1970. Born in Frankfurt in 1914, to David Friedrich Weil and Recha Rosenberg, she lived in Paris from 1934 to 1941, where she was involved in issuing false visas to German Jews. She arrived in New York in 1941 after a harsh trip on the SS Navemar. She began work at an entry-level position at L. Oppenheimer Co. and retired as company Vice President. Her enormous capacity to enjoy life and to be immersed in the lives of those around her will be greatly missed by her family and friends.
