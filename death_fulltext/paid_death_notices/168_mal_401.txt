
        DZIUBAN-Stanley J. ''Stan'' of New London, Ct. Formerly of Yonkers died November 12, 2000. He was 88. Stan was a certifed New York State CPA employed by United States Underwater Cable. He was a loving father to Lisa Ishimuro, Peter Dziuban and Nancy Balph. Beloved brother of Marie Dziuban of New London, Ct. Devoted uncle to his nieces and nephews and will be remembered and prayed for. We will miss Stan.
