
        MONKMEYER-Hilde Ruth. On February 19, 2001 at age 94. Bold and independent beyond compare. Beloved wife of the late Paul Monkmeyer. Loving mother of Peter Monkmeyer and Peggy Mastroianni. Devoted grandmother of Susan, Jim, Bob, Christina and Antonio and great-grandmother of four. After earning her doctorate from the University of Berlin, she worked as a journalist in Germany and, in 1937, emigrated to America with her husband and young son. A pioneer in the field of photojournalism, she founded Monkmeyer Press Photo Service where she and her husband worked side by side for many joyful years.
