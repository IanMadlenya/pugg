
        SELIGMAN--Henry J., DDS. 86 years old, died on April 6, 2006. Beloved husband of Elaine (nee Gruber). Devoted father of Bill & Gail Seligman and Ron and Angie Seligman. Cherished grandfather of Hannah and Joel and Abbigail and Rebekah; brother of Walter and Bridget Selby. Dr. Seligman graduated the Class of 1960 from Columbia University Dental and School of General Studies. He had a private practice in Manhattan. He was an active member of Temple Emanuel of Great Neck for many years. Services Friday 11:30 AM at Temple Emanuel, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Holocaust Memorial Fund, Temple Emanuel of Great Neck, or Long Island Alzheimer's Foundation in Port Washington, N.Y.
