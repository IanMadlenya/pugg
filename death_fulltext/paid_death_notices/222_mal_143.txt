
        AGRY--Warren Cram, Jr. of Greenwich, CT died on July 12. He was 82. His wife Nancy Vogt Agry predeceased him in 2000. He is survived by his son Bradford W. Agry of New York City. A long time resident of Rye, New York, Mr. Agry was a member of the Rye Presbyterian Church and The Apawamis Club. A memorial service will be held Monday, July 18, 10 AM at the Rye Presbyterian Church. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Rye Presbyterian Church, or Dartmouth College, Att: Nancy Bates.
