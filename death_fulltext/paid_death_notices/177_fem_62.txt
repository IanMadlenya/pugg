
        FEINBERG-Belle ''Buddy'' (nee Guttesman), age ninety-two, on October 8, 2001. Devoted, cherishing and cherished wife of the late Lawrence Feinberg. Devoted mother of Paul Feinberg & Linda Alwitt. Beloved grandmother of Karyn and Daniel Feinberg, Josh and Philip Alwitt, and great-grandmother of Kenzo Lawrence, Jacob Morris, Estelle and Olivia. Caring mother-in-law of Cyndy Feinberg and Robert Alwitt. Buddy brought her zest for life and her love to her family, her friends, and the pupils she taught in New York City schools. We will miss her.
