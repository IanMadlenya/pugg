
        GUNNING-Elizabeth Sand, 44. Beloved wife of Kenneth Gunning of Manhattan, died June 26th, 2001 at home after a courageous battle with advanced ovarian cancer. Born in Killeen, TX, she lived in NJ and CT. before moving to NYC 22 years ago. She graduated from St. Leo College (FL) with a degree in theater and drama. She was employed by the New York Hilton and worked for many years in professional theater. She is also survived by her parents, John and Carol Sand of Bristol, CT, 2 sisters, Kathy of Sarasota, FL and Ellen of Bristol, and a brother, Stephen and his wife Patricia of Farmington, NY. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81 St., on Thursday, June 28th, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. A Funeral Mass will be held on Friday, June 29th, at Ascension Church, 221 W. 107 St, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Ovarian Cancer Research Fund Inc., 1 Pennsylvania Plaza, New York, New York 10119.
