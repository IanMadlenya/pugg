
        KIRSHENBAUM-Dr. A. Hyman. Young Israel of the West Side mourns the passing of a longstanding, devoted member, Dr. A. Hyman Kirshenbaum. A distinguished neurosurgeon, he brought compassion and skill to his patients. He was faithful to G-d and Man. May the Comforter of Zion console his bereaved family-his wife Judy, daughter Wendy (Lipstadt) and son Barry and their families. Rabbi Emanuel Gettinger Sheldon Fine, President
