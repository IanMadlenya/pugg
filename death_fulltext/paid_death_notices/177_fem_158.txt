
        ROCHKIND-Hilda (Hamerling), 92. Died at home in Manhattan on October 14, 2001, after a brief illness. The sixth of seven siblings, she emigrated from Poland in 1920 and lived in New York City for her entire life, except for a 20-year period ending in 1995, when she lived in West Palm Beach, FL. Her husband, William, an attorney, died in 1955. She is survived by her daughter, Esta Walsh, and son, Mark Rochkind, both of New Jersey; and by a granddaughter, Tara Rochkind, of New York, and a grandson, William Rochkind, of Memphis, TN.
