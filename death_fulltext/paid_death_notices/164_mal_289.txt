
        IBANEZ-Rafael Gilberto. Mr. Ibanez born Ponce, Puerto Rico and resident of NYC since 1926 died September 15, 2000 after a long illness. Mr. Ibanez was a retired postal employee, who served in WWII in Europe. He is survived by his daughters Grace Friedman, Martha Baas Wahmann, Aida Regan, Vivian Rodriguez and Miriam Burgos. He is also survived by seven grandchildren and his sole surviving sister, Elsie Pena. Visiting Sunday, 7-9 PM, and Monday, 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM, at the Fox Funeral Home, 98-07 Ascan Avenue, Forest Hills, NY. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 10 AM, at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs R.C. Church. Interment Mount Carmel Cemetery, Englewood, NJ.
