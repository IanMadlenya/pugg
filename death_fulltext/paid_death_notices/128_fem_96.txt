
        WALLDOV-Liz (Elizabeth), at home September 8, 1997. New York native, community activist, naturalist and world traveler. Liz set a shining standard of service. She did it with tough questions, hard work, good humor, and finesse. She enjoyed music, dance, and the company of good friends. Marga Walldov Parent, her sister, and nephews Mark and Steven Koenig suggest donations to The Perlow Hospice of Beth Israel Medical Center or The New York Chapter of The Nature Conservancy.
