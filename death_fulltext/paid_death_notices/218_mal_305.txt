
        AVEDON--Richard, 78, on March 27. He and his wife Ronnie lived in the Roslyn area with their son Jeffrey. He was a caring and deeply loved husband and father, and a devoted son and brother to his late parents, Jessie and Jack, and to his three sisters, now deceased. He will be remembered for his generous, helpful nature and his sharp sense of humor. Graveside services at 1 pm, Tuesday, at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn.
