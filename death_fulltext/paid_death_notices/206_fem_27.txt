
        SANTINI--Dorothea C. Of Yonkers on March 1, 2004. She was born on October 27, 1916 to Godfrey E. Santini and Quintilia Michelini Santini. Beloved sister of the late Godfrey Santini, Quentin Santini, Maureen Knebel and Alice Santini. She was VicePresident of Operations for the family business, The 7 Santini Brothers Moving Co. Calling hours at The Fred H. McGrath & Son, Funeral home, Bronxville between the hours of 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM Thursday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Ann's Church, Yonkers on Friday, March 5 at 10:30 AM.
