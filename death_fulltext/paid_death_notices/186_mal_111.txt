
        BYER-Samuel. The leadership and staff of The Workmen's Circle/Arbeter Ring mourn the passing of the President of the Workmen's Circle Multicare Center and former member of our National Executive Board, Sam Byer. His vision and devotion have been the guiding forces of the center for the past several years. His dedication to the dignity of our residents and excellence of care will be his lasting legacy. Martin Krupnick, President Robert Kestenbaum, Executive Director
