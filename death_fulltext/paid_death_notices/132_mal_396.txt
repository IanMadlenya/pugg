
        SNIZEK-John Edward. January 28, 1998, in Largo, Florida, at age 81. Longtime resident of Bayside, NY, he is survived by his wife of 50 years, Nellie, and daughter, Jean Reddish, of Sherman, Texas, sons, Michael of Westfield, New Jersey, and Paul of Harrisburg, PA. Longtime meatpacker and employee of the Golden Packing Company of New York. He will be missed by all who knew him.
