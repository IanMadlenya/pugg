
        SAMTON--Henry A. Founder of Miltenberg & Samton 60 years ago. He developed M & S to be a respected and promiment company in the worldwide confectionery industry. His business friends and associates will remember him for his dedication, knowledge, trust and hard work. In 1985, Ronald Kehle acquired M & S and Henry continued his strong desire to remain active until 1997. On behalf of Ronald Kehle and the staff of M & S we express our deepest sympathy to his family.
