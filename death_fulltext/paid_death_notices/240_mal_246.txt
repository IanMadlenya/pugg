
        ZAKARIN--Leo Samuel, January 14, 1918 to January 19, 2007. Survived by Pearl, his beloved wife of nearly 61 years, his four children, Ellen (Jeff), Don (Mindy), Cheryl (James), Eric (Barbara), nine grandchildren, one greatgrandson and his treasured brothers, Victor, Irving and of blessed memory, David. The lessons you taught will stay with us always. We will miss you forever but are grateful you are now at peace.
