
        SIEGEL--Benjamin, 85, Cantor Emeritus of Temple Israel, Great Neck. Honored on numerous occasions for enriching the lives of his congregants and bringing joy through his beautiful chantings and melodies for over 40 years. As Chaplain Siegel, during WWII, he organized choirs and sang at concerts throughout the country raising funds for Navy Relief. Loving father of Rita Bloom (Ron), Phillip (Susan) and David (Barbara). Cherished grandfather of Bryan, Lisa, Jennifer and Samantha. Beloved brother of Bessie, great grandfather of Samara and Jack. Survived by former wife Helen Siegel. He will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral services were held at Temple Israel of Great Neck on October 4, 2004.
