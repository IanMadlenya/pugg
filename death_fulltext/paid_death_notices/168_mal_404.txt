
        FELDMAN-Victor. Former employee of The New York Times passed away in his home in Canaan, New York, on January 14, 2001. He is survived by his loving wife Phyllis, devoted daughters, Lisa Feldman Reich, Julie Feldman, son-in-law Fred Reich and grandchildren, Aaron Feldman Reich and Claire Feldman Reich. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him.
