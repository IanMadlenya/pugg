
        SCHWARTZ--Bernard S., on February 14, age 94. Beloved husband of Ida, father of Jonathan, father-in-law of Marcella, grandfather of Alexander. Attorney, investor, poet. He will be terribly missed. Private service.
        SCHWARTZ--Bernard S. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Bernard S. Schwartz, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
