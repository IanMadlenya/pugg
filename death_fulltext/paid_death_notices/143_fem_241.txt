
        KAYE-Etta Cummins. On December 23. Devoted wife of the late Abraham, beloved mother of Robert, Laurie and Michael, cherished grandmother of Elizabeth, Robin and Andrew, Hilary and Lowell Kronowitz, Elizabeth Wright and Anthony Kaye, and infatuated great-grandmother of William and Jonathan. Also survived by adoring sisters and brothers. She was a woman who placed family above all else.
