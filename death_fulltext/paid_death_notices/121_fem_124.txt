
        KEEGAN-Mary Hurley. Wife of the late Peter V. Keegan. Mother of Patricia Keegan Peck and Kathleen Keegan Mattessich, and grandmother of Adam R. Mattessich. Sister of Edward Hurley and Margaret Weatherly. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., 81 St., Wednesday. Funeral Mass at St. Elizabeth of Hungary Church, 211 East 83 St., Thursday, at 9:30 A.M. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Retired Clergy Fund, 1011 First Avenue, New York City 10022.
