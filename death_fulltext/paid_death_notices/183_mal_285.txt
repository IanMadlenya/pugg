
        SALTZ-Peter. Age 84. Died April 20, 2002. Resident of West Palm Beach, FL. & former stockbroker from Queens. He is survived by wife Eve, children Carl, Anita and Ralph, and 7 grandchildren. A civil rights activist, he had a fifty-year association with the Jamaica NAACP, where he was President in 1969 and held numerous other key positions. He also served on the Executive Committee of the Jamaica Service Program for Older Adults, Executive Board member of the Justice Chapter of the American Jewish Congress, Executive Director of the Queens Hospital Center Community Advisory Board, and, in his later years, wrote over 250 columns for the Florida Photo News, a minorityowned newspaper in Palm Beach County.
