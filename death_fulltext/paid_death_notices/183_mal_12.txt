
        BRACHOCKI-Edward M. Of East Meadow, NY on March 30, 2002 at the age of 84 after a lengthy battle with Alzheimer's disease. He was born in Brooklyn to Edward J. and Eleanor Brachocki, and is survived by his devoted wife, Virginia; daughter Barbara and her husband Joseph of Ithaca, N.Y.; son Edward and his wife Susan of Scottsdale, Arizona and two grandchildren, Jennifer and Sean, both of Ithaca. He served in the Pacific during WW II, and spent the early part of his career with the Pennsylvania RR. In 1952, he joined the James H. Burns Company, one of the leading Industrial Real Estate consultancies in NY at the time. His career was one of his great passions. He became a partner in the firm in 1965, was an active member of the Society of Industrial Realtors (SIR), and was past president of the Industrial Real Estate Brokers Association of NY. He served for many years on the Town of Hempstead Senior Citizens Housing Authority and actively participated in the planning of many of today's senior citizen housing projects on Long Island. Outside of business he was an avid golfer and was an active member of the Hempstead GC for 40 years.
