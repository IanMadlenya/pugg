
        JONES-Michael V. The faculty, residents, and staff of the Residency Program in Social Medicine and the Department of Family Medicine of Montefiore Medical Center mourn the sudden and painful loss of our revered and esteemed colleague, Dr. Michael V. Jones, who brought wisdom, humor, and social commitment to all those who knew him. Our deepest sympathies go out to his mother, Mrs. Classie Guye, and daughter Marjana, and all his family and friends. Memorial Service will be on Thursday, February 6th, at the Unitarian Universalist Church, 160 Central Park West at 3 P.M. Reception: 4-6 PM.
