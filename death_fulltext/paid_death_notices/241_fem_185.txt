
        ORZA--Carolina P., on February 20, 2007 at age 92. Loving daughter of the late Aniello Q. Orza and Maria Talamini Orza. Cherished sister of Amelio Q. Orza and the late Claire R. Orza. A memorial service will be held Saturday, February 24, 2007, 9am at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave (at 20th St). Interment to follow at Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
