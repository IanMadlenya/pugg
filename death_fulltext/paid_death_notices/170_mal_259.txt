
        KESSENICH-Mark F., Jr., 62, of Palm Beach, Florida, died Tuesday, March 20th at his home in Westhampton, New York after a 6-1/2 year struggle with ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease). He is predeceased by his wife, Beverly Barry Kessenich, his parents, Hazel Johnstone Kessenich and Mark F. Kessenich, Sr. He is survived by a daughter, Barbara Kessenich Robertson of Greenville, South Carolina, sons, Mark F. Kessenich III of Westhampton, New York, and Paul T. Kessenich of New York City, and his sister, Joanne K. Knief of Ocala, Florida. Mr. Kessenich is also survived by eight grandchildren and by Diane Kessenich, mother of his three children. Visitation will be at Follett and Werner Funeral Home, 60 Mill Road, Westhampton Beach, NY on Friday, March 23rd from 2-4PM and 79PM. Funeral Mass at Immaculate Conception Church, Westhampton Beach on Saturday, March 24 at 10AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Kessenich Family MDA/ALS Center, C/O MDA, Robert Ross, 3300 East Sunrise Drive, Tucson, AZ 85718-3208.
        KESSENICH-Mark F, Jr. The Board of Governors and membership of Deepdale Golf Club note with sorrow the untimely passing of our esteemed member. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. John D. Martin, President
