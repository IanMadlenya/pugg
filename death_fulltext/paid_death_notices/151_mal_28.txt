
        NEIGEBORN-Sam. Died August 1, 1999 at 85. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Adoring father of Lenore, Amy, Beth, and Burt Neuborne. Father-in-law of Scott Brustein, Paul O'Halloran, Walter Angilly and Helen Neuborne. Grandfather of Ellen and Lauren, Allyson and Greg, Hannah and Emily. Great grandfather of Henry. Brother of Abe. His life was filled with love and kindness. A memorial service will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave, Tuesday, Aug. 3, 2:45PM. Friends may visit at the home of Helen and Burt Neuborne, 12 West 17 St, NYC Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday.
