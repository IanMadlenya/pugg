
        GORDON-Evan W., M.D. The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at State University of New York Health Science Center at Brooklyn was very saddened to hear about the death of Dr. Evan W. Gordon. Dr. Gordon was an active member of the department for a period of 16 years at State University Hospital of Brooklyn and Kings County Hospital Center. Dr. Gordon was appointed to the faculty in the department as a clinical instructor at State University of New York Health Science Center at Brooklyn and as an attending physician at State University Hospital of Brooklyn and Kings County Hospital Center in 1970. In 1984, he was promoted to the level of a Clinical Assistant Professor. Dr. Gordon was an excellent teacher to the medical students and residents. The department extends its deepest sympathy to the family.
