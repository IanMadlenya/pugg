
        KURLANDER-Judith Levey. The members of the Board of Directors, the staff, volunteers and youth of the All Stars Project express deep sadness at the death of Judith Levey Kurlander, the mother of our dear colleague, friend and organization President, Gabrielle Kurlander. Judith generously shared her love of theater and the arts with her family, friends and community. Our sincere condolences to Gabrielle and her family. Susan Massad, M.D., Chair All Stars Project
