
        TURETSKY--Rabbi Arnold. The officers, trustees, clergy and members of Temple Israel Center of White Plains mourn the passing of our distinguished Rabbi Emeritus. Rabbi Turetsky was a teacher of Torah, and provided spiritual guidance to our community with strength and determination for 22 years. We extend to his loving wife, Noemie, and to his wonderful children, Justin, Jonathan and Wendy, Daniel and Andrea, and David and Jill, our heartfelt condolences. Gordon Tucker, Senior Rabbi Neil Zuckerman, Rabbi Jacob Mendelson, Cantor Robert Jossen, President
