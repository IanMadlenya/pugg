
        GROSSMAN--Selma, beloved wife, mother, grandmother and sister, died Tuesday, June 8th, 2004 after a long illness. She leaves her three sons, Harris (with his wife Nicole) of Nedde, France; Norman (Naftali); and Marvin (with his wife Susan); and her two grandchildren, Rena and Jason of Little Neck, NY; and her brother, Fred Kreitstein of Forest Hills, NY. A lover of life, justice and independence of thought and a giver of life and love to her children and grandchildren whom she cherished. May she find peace and comfort. Services Sunday, June 13th, 11 AM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 No. Station Plaza (opp. LIRR), Great Neck, NY. The Grossman Family
