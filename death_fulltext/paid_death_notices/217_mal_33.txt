
        KRANTZ--Irving, 81. Of Sunrise, FL. Passed away January 31, 2005. He is survived by his loving and devoted wife, Dorothy, his beloved children Richard (Gwendolyn) and daughter Ellen (Jeff), brothers Sol and Abe and his cherished grandson Joshua. Irving served his country in World War II, and was very active in the Free Sons of Israel and was a successful businessman. We will carry your love in our hearts always.
