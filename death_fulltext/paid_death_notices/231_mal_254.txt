
        WHITELY--William L. of Coconut Grove, FL died on April 20 at the home of his daughter, Patricia, surrounded by his loving family. Whitely, a former resident of Port Washington and Rye, NY is a graduate of Xavier High School, University of Notre Dame and Cornell School of Law. He is survived by his loving children Patricia Whitely (George Shoffner), Ellen (Cory) Slicklen, William Whitely Jr. (Karen), Meg (Paul) Bloshuk, Paul and Andrew Whitely and Monica and (Stephen) Levy. He is also survived by his adored granchildren Charles (CJ), Sara May, Grace and Jack Slicklen, Amanda and Paul Bloshuk, Jacqueline and William L. Whitely III and Megan Shoffner. Also survived by his sister Madeline Delohery, his brother Jack and sister Theresa Ruane and many nieces and nephews. A Mass of Resurrection will be held on Friday, April 28 at 9:30am at St. Peter of Alcantara Church, Port Washington. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center, University of Miami Miller School of Medicine, Office of Advancement, POB 016960 (m867), Miami, FL 33101
