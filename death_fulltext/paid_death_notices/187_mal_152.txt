
        KANE-John Francis. 78, on August 12, 2002, of Washington Heights, NY. Beloved lifelong friend of Teresa Lamb of North Bergen. Dear brother of Ann Reilly and Irene Mable. Loving uncle of 14 nieces and nephews, 29 grand-nieces and nephews and many beloved friends. Funeral Mass Friday 11:00 AM, St. Mary's Church in West New York, NJ. Visitation 2:00-4:00, 7:00-9:00 PM at the Konopka Funeral Home, 9046 Palisade Ave., North Bergen, NJ. He was a veteran of WW II, US Air Force and of the Korean War. He received a graduate degree in public health from NYU. He was a fund-raising consultant, and he invented golf exercise equipment for which he obtained several patents. He was an Officer of the NY Traffic Postmasters Club. He also played the saxophone during the Big Band era.
