
        MILLER--Geraldine of Boynton Beach, FL, wife of the late Alvin M. Miller, died peacefully at age 82 on January 17. She is survived by two sons, Jeffrey and Peter, and their spouses Angela and Deborah, grandchild Eric and wife Amy, and grandchild Paul. She will be missed by her family and especially her oldest and dearest friends, Mike and Florence Benay. A memorial service in Florida is planned for February.
