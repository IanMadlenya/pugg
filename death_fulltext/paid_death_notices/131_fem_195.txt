
        LIFFLANDER-May. On December 22, 1997 of Hastings on Hudson, NY at age 92. Beloved wife of late Ben. She is survived by her son Matthew Lifflander, her daughter and son-in-law Nan and Ed Perell, four grandchildren: Stephanie Krause, Clay Lifflander, Justin Lifflander and Tim Perell, and five great grandchildren: Cameron, Isabel, Olivia, Max and Ken. Services were held Tuesday. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Hudson River Museum, 511 Warburton Avenue, Yonkers, New York 10701.
