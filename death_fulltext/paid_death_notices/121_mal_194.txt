
        CURRIE-Robert D. The entire City Center family is deeply saddened by the sudden passing of our extraordinary production manager, Bob Currie. Bob's commitment, professionalism, and expertise, as well as his renowned sense of humor, will be greatly missed. We offer our heartfelt sympathy to Bob's longtime companion, Susan Bell, and to his brothers Alan and William. A memorial service will be held at City Center (W. 55 bet. 6 and 7 Aves.) on Friday, April 4, at 3:30 PM. Judith E. Daykin President and Executive Director
