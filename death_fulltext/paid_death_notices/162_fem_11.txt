
        ROBERTS-Doris Lewis. On June 24, 2000. Age 80. Beloved wife for 57 years of Wayne. Devoted mother of Jim and Laurie and motherin-law of Pat. Adoring grandmother of Alexandra and Rosie. Dear sister of Leah and Goldie. Doris was an outstanding organizer, fundraiser and adminstrator: a District Organizer for the American Student Union. Field Organizer for the United Electrical Workers Union and for 19 years Associate Director and Director of the Womens Division of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies. A memorial celebration of her life will be held on Wednesday, July 12 at 1:30pm at All Souls Unitarian Church on Lexington Avenue between 79th & 80th Streets. Contributions in Doris's memory may be made to MADRE, 121 West 27 Street, NY, NY 10001
        ROBERTS-Doris. The Fund For New Priorities In America mourns the loss of Doris Roberts, beloved wife of Board Member Wayne Roberts. Her lifelong devotion to peace and justice issues and dedication to the creation of a better world will be sorely missed.
