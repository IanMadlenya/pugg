
        ETKIND--Anna B. Park East Synagogue Sisterhood sorrowfully mourns the passing of Anna B. Etkind, beloved mother of Polly Etkind Hochberg, our dedicated Past President. She will missed by us all. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Lois Kirsh, Co-President Ninette Stiskin, Co-President Maguy Naparstek, Chairman
        ETKIND--Anna B. Park East Synagogue mourns Anna B. Etkind, beloved mother of our devoted members Polly Etkind Hochberg, Past President of Sisterhood, and Vice President, Herman Hochberg, grandmother of Marisa. To them, and the entire family, our deepest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
        ETKIND--Anna B. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Park East Day School mourns Anna B. Etkind, beloved mother of our founders, Polly Etkind and Herman Hochberg. Polly, member of the Board of Education, and Herman, have endowed scholarships in support of Jewish education. Condolences to the family.
        ETKIND--Anna B. Park East Synagogue Men's Club extends heartfelt condolences to Polly Etkind and Herman Hochberg, on the passing of their beloved mother. Dr. Barry Zimmerman, President
