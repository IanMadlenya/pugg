
        PALMER--Paul R. Died September 1, 2006. Longtime resident of New York City until 2002, when he moved to Cincinnati, OH to be closer to his family. Companion for many years of the late Kenneth A. Lohf. Brother-in-law of Mary Louise Palmer and uncle to David Y., Richard C. and William C. Palmer. Long-term employee of Columbia University Libraries. Memorial service at St. Thomas Church, 1 West 53rd St., New York on Thursday, November 9, 2006 beginning at 2:30pm. Memorials may be sent to the Paul Palmer Fund at St. Thomas Church. For information contact the Elden A. Good Funeral Home, Cincinnati, OH.
