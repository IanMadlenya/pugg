
        COLE-Miriam Gordon. Of Southport, CT., and Palm Beach, FL. Died peacefully on Feburary 24th, in Palm Beach. Much loved wife of Martin C. Cole. She is also survived by her children, Richard S. Proud ''Gammy'' to great-grandsons Nicholas and Spenser. Sister of Dorothy Samelson, of Boca Raton. Memorial donations may be made to the Miriam Cole Endowment Fund, St. Vicent's Hospital, 2800 Main Street, Bridgeport, CT. 06606.
