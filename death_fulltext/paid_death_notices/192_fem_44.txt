
        VALENSTEIN-Alice Sternberg, 98, wife of the late Lawrence Valenstein, founder of the Grey Advertising Agency, died peacefully at home on December 24. She had lived in Scarsdale NY for over 60 years. She was known in the community as a prize-winning artist. Survived by two children, John Valenstein of Old Greenwich CT and Linda Elkind of Portola Valley CA, five grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Services private. In lieu of flowers the family prefers donations in Mrs. Valenstein's memory to Hospice of Westchester, The Esplanade, 95 S. Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601.
