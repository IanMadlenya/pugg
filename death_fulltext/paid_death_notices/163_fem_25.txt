
        BALDINGER-Edith Marks. In her 101yr she died peacefully at home. Wife of the late Carl Marks and the late Irving Baldinger; Cherished Mother of Edwin and Nancy Marks, Marjorie Boas Levins and Jack Levins and the late Robert S. Boas; Loving Grandmother of Richard Boas, Susan and Mark Claster, Andrew and Carol Boas, Carolyn Marks Blackwood, Linda and Richard Katz, Constance Marks Miller and James Miller; And Loving ''G.G.'' to 13 great grandchildren, Daniel, Katherine, Gabriel, Matthew, Benjamin, Hallie, Bari, Rebecca, Esther, Jessie, Laura, Samuel and Sophia. Her family wishes to express deep gratitude to her devoted friend and caretaker, Gay Munroe. Edith was beloved, and a joy and inspiration to all who were blessed to know her. Funeral services will be held 11:30 AM Friday, August 4, 2000 at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St.
        BALDINGER-Edith R. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York, records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Edith R. Baldinger. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
