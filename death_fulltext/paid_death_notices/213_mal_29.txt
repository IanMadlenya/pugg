
        SCHWARTZ--Ivan H. CEO and founder of Omni Offices Inc., age 84, on September 28, 2004 following a valiant struggle from kidney failure due to complications from diabetes. Beloved husband of Fritzi Mark Schwartz. Loving father of Howard and his wife Jan Shaw, and Barbara and her husband Furman Gardner. Loving grandfather of Jonathan, Jordan and Chad, and brother of Rhoda Polachek. A celebration of his life will be held in the near future in New York City.
