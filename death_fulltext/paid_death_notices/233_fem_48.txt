
        ENG--Suzanne nee Reardon, passed away peacefully on June 9, 2006 at age 59 after a 30 year valiant struggle with multiple sclerosis. Her husband Jimmy Eng and two children Jimmy Jr. and Michele sustained her in the long illness with a love and care rarely seen and they serve as an inspiration to all who loved her. She is survived by brothers James, Gerard, Dennis and Michael and sisters Betty Reardon and Kathleen DiPersio (Richard) as well as 22 nieces and nephews. For information Stutzmann Funeral Home 718-465-3210. Donations may be made to The National Multiple Sclerosis Foundation.
