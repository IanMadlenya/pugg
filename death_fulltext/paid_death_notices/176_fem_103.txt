
        DVONCH-Mara Sebrionsky, violinist, nee Bess Simon died August 30, 2001 on Bainbridge Island, WA. She was born in New York City, New York in 1914. Mara attended Juilliard School of the Arts. She was a Naumberg Prize winner and played under Stokowski, Steinberg, Previn and Beecham. She was associate concertmaster for the Pittsburgh Symphony from 1969-1979. She is survived by one daughter, Margaret Dvonch Swingle, Bainbridge Island; three grandchildren and one great granddaughter. Memorial services will be held at Kass Funeral Home, 163 Wyatt Way, Bainbridge Island, on September 14 at 1:00PM.
