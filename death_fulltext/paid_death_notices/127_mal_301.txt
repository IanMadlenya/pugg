
        EVES-Samuel R., 94, musician and educator, died August 12th. Survived by one sister, Julia Eves Small of Havertown, PA; numerous nieces and nephews, grand nieces and nephews, great grand nieces and nephews. Predeceased by Grace Gilmore Eves, his wife of 62 years. A memorial service will be held at a later date in Bloomsburg, PA. Memorial contributions may be sent to Millville Friends Meeting, P.O. Box 123, Bloomsburg, PA. 17815.
