
        PRESSMAN--Alina K., M.D. Of Bayside, NY died on June 19, 2005 at the age of 84. Alina was an extraordinary mother and a devoted friend to many. Born in Warsaw, Poland, she lost most of her family in the Holocaust. Despite this, she retained a positive outlook on life. She spent some of her best years after the war studying at the Casa dello Studente in Torino, Italy. Arriving in New York in 1951, Alina began the medical career she cherished and only stopped practicing at the age of 75 due to illness. She will be truly missed by her daughters, grandchildren, sister, brother-in-law and numerous friends. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, 1000 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, New York 11225.
