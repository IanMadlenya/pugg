
        GILMAN-Sylvia P. On August 4, 1999, at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Loving mother of the late Howard and Charles Jr. She is survived by members of the Gilman, Phillips and Marcus families. Services and interment private.
        GILMAN-Sylvia P. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Sylvia P. Gilman. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Secy
