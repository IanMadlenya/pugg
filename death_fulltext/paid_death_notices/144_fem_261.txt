
        McGIRR-Margaret L. On Thursday, January 21, 1999, in her 96th year. Loving aunt of Patricia A. Bastoni, John T. McGirr and Edward J. Howard. Viewing was held on Friday at Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Boulevard, Flushing, New York. Mass of Christian Burial was celebrated on Saturday at St. Andrew Avellino R.C. Church followed by burial at Calvary Cemetery.
