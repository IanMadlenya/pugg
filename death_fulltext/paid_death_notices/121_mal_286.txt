
        PASTERNACK (PASTOR)-Jack. Born on the Lower East Side on 22 December 1902; died at Lenox Hill Hospital on 15 February 1997. Box office manager of the Palace Theater back-when; song plugger known as The Boy with the Golden Voice; drummer; comedian; singer; master of ceremonies; merchandiser; husband; father; grandfather; and all-around nice person who will be missed by all he met. Survived by his wife of fifty-eight years, Lillian; son, Fred; and grandsons, Alexander and Gregory. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Lenox Hill Hospital would be appreciated.
