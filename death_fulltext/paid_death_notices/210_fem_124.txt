
        DELLAY--Mirianne Marshall Ralli, died July 13, 2004 after a long illness. As Mirianne Marshall she performed in many films, on stage and in commercials. For the past few years she lived in Miami, Palm Beach and New York City. She was a member of the Surf Club in Miami and the Dutch Treat Club of New York City. She is survived by a son Robert and his family. She will be greatly missed.
