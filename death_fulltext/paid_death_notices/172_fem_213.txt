
        PRICE-Helen (nee Gusikoff). Born September 9, 1922-Died May 26, 2001. Devoted wife of Erwin. Loving mother to Laurence, Karen and Frederic. Adoring grandmother to Brian. Selfless volunteer and friend. We will miss your strength and courage and cherish your memory always. Service Sunday 11:30 a.m. at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
        PRICE-Helen. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Helen Price. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
