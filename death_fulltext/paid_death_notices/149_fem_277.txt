
        CHRISTENSON-Janet Siegel. Died suddenly on June 6. Loving sister and sister-in-law of Brenda and Herb Jacobson, devoted aunt of Kim and Jeffrey, grand aunt of Madeleine Anne.Her husband of 25 years, Hugh Rolf Christenson, passed away December 1996. Jan will be deeply missed by her many friends and professional colleagues in the library community, where she served in NYC and San Francisco as a public librarian, and in NYC as a school and academic librarian. Contributions may be sent to a charity of your choice.
