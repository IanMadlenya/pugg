
        KRENTS-Milton E. Died January 8, 2000. Funeral services today, 11:30AM, Frank E. Campbell, 82nd St and Madison Ave.
        KRENTS-Milton. The Jewish Theological Seminary family records with profound sorrow the death of Milton Krents, a visionary who produced ''The Eternal Light,'' JTS's award-winning religious series that ran on NBC radio and television for over 40 years. A beloved colleague, he will long be remembered for the indomitable spirit & irrepressible confidence that enabled him to blaze a trail for religious broadcasting in this country. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Irma, his children and grandchildren and his entire family. May his memory be for a blessing. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Chairman
        KRENTS-Milton. So many warm memories of sunny days at the beach and cool nights on ''the hill''. Gardner and Korman Families
