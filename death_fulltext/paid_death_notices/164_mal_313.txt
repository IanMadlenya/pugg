
        SANDS-George E. Died peacefully in his 100th year on September 16, 2000. Beloved husband of Marjorie U. Sands. Devoted stepfather of Charlotte Stadler and Julia Shubik. Grandfather to Claire Shubik, Daniel, Alex and David Stadler. Great-grandfather to Henry and Lily Stadler. Memorial services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, September 19, 11:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Long Island Caddie Scholarship Fund c/o John Buckley, 15 Homestead Drive, Wheatley Heights, NY 11798 or to a charity of your choice.
        SANDS-George E. The Executive Committee of the Metropolitan Golf Association and the Board of Directors of the MGA Foundation record with deep sympathy the passing of our esteemed past president. His more than 70 years of dedicated service to the game will always be cherished by his friends in the golf community. His volunteer work touched the lives of countless people in golf-juniors, seniors, caddies and many others. We extend our deepest sympathy to Marjorie and the family.
