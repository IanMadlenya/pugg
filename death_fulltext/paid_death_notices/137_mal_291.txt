        MARKS-David N., M.D. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed life member and former President. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Adele, and his family. Murray B. Hirsch, President Howard Ecker, Secretary
