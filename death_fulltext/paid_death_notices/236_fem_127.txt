
        BELSON--Ruth. Beloved wife of David Belson (pre-deceased). Noted and distinguished labor attorney. Coauthor with her husband of a series of successful ''how to'' books. Ruth enjoyed good health during her 98 years. She passed away peacefully in Laguna Hills, CA. She will be missed by her surviving, loving nephews: I. Victor Belson and Jerome Belson and their families. Jerome and Maxine Belson
