
        HOLLOWAY - Joseph Bruning. Age 47. Of New York. Formerly of Wheeling, W.V. Died on Friday, May 27, 1999. Son of the late Walker Peterson Holloway. Surviving are his mother Barbara Bruning Holloway Locke of Wheeling, two brothers, Peter, Jr. and Dr. John Holloway, both of Wheeling. Also surviving are four nephews and Mark Nadler of New York.
