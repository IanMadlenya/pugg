
        FELL--Judith. On August 29, 2005 after a long illness. Beloved wife of Barry Okun, daughter of Red and the late Babs Fell, sister of David (Amy) and Happy (Adair) Fell, aunt of Lauren and Sarah Fell. Friend to too many to name. We will miss Judy's wit, intelligence, and fearless honesty. Memorial service on Wednesday, September 7, 2005, 11 AM, Congregation Beth Elohim, 247 Garfield Place (corner of 8th Ave), Brooklyn, N.Y. Memorial donations in Judy's name to the Brooklyn Botanic Garden or the National Council of Jewish Women-New York Section. $(6$)FELL--Judith. NCJW NY Section staff and members mourn the loss of our courageous colleague and friend. Her strength of character and spirit will be missed. Our condolences to Barry and the Fell and Okun families. Natalie Katz, President Judith R. Golub, Exec. Dir.
