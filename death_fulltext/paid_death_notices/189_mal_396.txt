
        TURNBULL - William. The Board of Directors and staff of Sheltering Arms Childrens Service note with sorrow the death on October 23, 2002 of William Turnbull, past President & longtime Board member. Bill Turnbull was dedicated to improving the lives of disadvantaged children, and to the work of Sheltering Arms. The agency benefited greatly from his wisdom and thoughtfulness. Henry A. Lambert, Chairman Wayne R. Mucci, Executive Director
