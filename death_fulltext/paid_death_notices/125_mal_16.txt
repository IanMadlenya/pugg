
        NATHANSON-Morton Dr. Died early June 1 after a brief illness at age 79. Dr. Nathanson was the founder and Chairman of the Department of Neurology at LIJ/ HMC . He was a mentor, teacher and guiding force of neurologists throughout the United States. Surviving are his wife, Margret, his children, David, Madlyn Nathanson Flavell and Laura Nathanson Kroehling, 5 grandchildren, and his sister Roslyn Klarish. There will be no memorial service.
        NATHANSON-Morto n, M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni records with sorrow the passing of their longtime collegue and friend. He will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Wayne S. Fuchs, M.D., President Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary
