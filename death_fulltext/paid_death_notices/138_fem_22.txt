
        COHEN-Emma Mabel. Age 96. Died on July 2, 1998 in Media, Pennsylvania. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Joseph George Cohen, who was former Dean of Teacher Education, City Colleges of New York City. Cherished mother of Vera (Mrs. Joseph) Gottlieb of Charlottesville, VA, and Michael Cohen of Wynnewood, Pennsylvania. Loving grandmother of Jane Rogers, Robert Gottlieb, Adam Cohen, Jonathan Cohen & Alison Cohen. Devoted great grandmother of Megan Elsensohn, Jonathan Gottlieb and Alexander Gottlieb. She was a dedicated teacher of English, volunteer librarian and inspired gardener.
