
        WEINSTOCK - Lillian. 94, Pompano Beach, FL, January 29, 2006. Survived by herloving children, Leonard (Terry) Weinstock and Deborah (Martin) Bloch, her grandchildren, Kenneth (Jacqueline) Perlmutter, S. Michael Perlmutter, Lauren (Rob Widell) Weinstock, and Adam Weinstock, great grandchildren, David, and Jeffrey Perlmutter, and her brother, Saul (Lois) Ricklin.
