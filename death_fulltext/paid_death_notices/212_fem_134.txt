
        WIESEN--Mae (nee Halperin). Died Friday, September 17 in her 94th year. Beloved wife of the late Seymour. Loving mother of the late Dorothy Sokolski and David Wiesen, PhD. Loving grandmother of Pearl, Joy, Rachel, Seth and Jonathan. Motherin-law of Ellen Wiesen. Sister of Berenice Adams. Devoted sister-in-law and aunt, proud great-grandmother. Graveside service Sunday, September 19, 2:00 P.M. at Union Field Cemetery, Ridgewood, New York
