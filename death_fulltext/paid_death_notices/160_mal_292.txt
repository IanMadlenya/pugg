
        WALKER-Randolph. 71, on May 22, 2000 of New York, NY. Beloved father of Katharine Hoffman; father-in-law of Richard Hoffman; grandfather of Michael & Steven Hoffman. Brother of Gerard Walker and Virginia Johnson; and dear friend of Stephen Morello. Memorial service on May 26 at 6:00 PM at Church of the Holy Cross, 329 West 42nd Street, N.Y. In lieu of flowers donations to the Actor's Fund, 212-221-7300.
        WALKER-Randolph St. George. Manhattan Playhouse mourns the tragic loss of our cousin and colleague Randolph Walker. He brilliantly gave life to numerous, complex, sensitive roles, balancing consummate technique with emotional adventure, thrilling every audience. We miss you, Ranny.
