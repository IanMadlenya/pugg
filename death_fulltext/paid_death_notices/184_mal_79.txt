
        HAVENICK-Arnold Joseph. Of Palm Beach, FL, died on May 6, 2002. He was born in New York on April 26, 1916. Devoted husband of Tillie; father of Nancy (Stephen) Fiverson and Fred (Barbara) Havenick of Coral Gables, FL; devoted grandfather of Izzy Havenick, Jenny Havenick, Alex Havenick, Michael Havenick of Coral Gables, FL, Peter Fiverson of New York and Lori (Marc) Cooperman of Livingston, NJ. Funeral service will be held at Temple Israel in West Palm Beach, 1901 North Flagler Drive at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Israel Emergency Fund.
