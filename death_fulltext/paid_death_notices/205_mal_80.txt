
        BARR--Donald, died February 5. Noted author and educator. Former teacher and administrator at Columbia University, where he founded the Science Honors Program. Former Headmaster of The Dalton School and Hackley School. Predeceased by his beloved wife of 53 years, Mary Ahern Barr. Survived by his sons, Christopher, William, Hilary, and Stephen, and by eight grandchildren. Viewing at Kenny Funeral Home, Norfolk, CT, 7-9 PM, February 13. Funeral Mass at Immaculate Conception Church, Norfolk, CT, 11 AM, February 14. Memorial donations to Mount Aviat Academy, 399 Childs Rd., Childs, MD 21916.
