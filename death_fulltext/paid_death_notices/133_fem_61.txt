
        DUNKLE-Matilda Louise Reinhardt. On February 6, 1998 at age 85. Resident of Scarsdale for 50 years. Wife of the late Heber Hamilton Dunkle Sr., mother of Laine M. Stephens, Carol B. Keenan, Heber H. Dunkle Jr., and Jonathan R. Dunkle. Grandmother of nine and great grandmother of three. A memorial service is planned for a later date. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory can be made to Scarsdale Congregational Church, 1 Heathcote Road, Scarsdale, N.Y. 10583
