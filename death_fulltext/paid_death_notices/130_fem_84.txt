
        LAZAR-Anne I., 88. One-of-a-kind, fun-loving, vital personality; a private person, generous, with strong family ties. She leaves behind: two sisters, Edith Stern and Miriam Magnifico; brother Nat Lazar and his wife, Roberta; and Sadie Ruth Lazar, with whom she shared a wonderful home for 45 years. She was loved and will be remembered by an extended family and many friends. Services today, 1 P.M., at Gutterman's, Rockville Centre, Long Island.
