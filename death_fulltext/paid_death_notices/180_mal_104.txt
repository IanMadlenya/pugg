
        TINKEL-Leonard J. Died peacefully at home on December 31. Judy, his wife of 67 years, predeceased him; survived by his son Jack and daughter-in-law Kathleen. Engineer, NYC teacher, passionate skiier, enthusiastic breadmaker and leader of hikes and other adventures at Lake Dunmore, VT. A Memorial Service will be held in the spring. Contributions in his memory can be made to Cooper Union Annual Fund, 30 Cooper Square, NYC 10003, which Len loved because it gave him a free education when he needed it.
