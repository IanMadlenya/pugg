
        KOVNER-Benjamin Long-term resident of Mamaroneck, NY, on July 17 at the age of 97 in Greenwich, CT Hospital. Beloved husband of Kathleen Jane Kovner. Loving father of Michael, Peter and the late Kay Kovner and father-in-law of Roberta. Adored grandfather of Matthew and Alexandra. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday, July 20, 6-9 PM, with service Wednesday, 11:30 AM. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the National Eye Institute, 2020 Vision Place, Bethesda, MD 20892.
