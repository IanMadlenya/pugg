
        TARPLIN-Ely. Died July 1, 1997, 79 years of age. Died after a long battle against Alzheimer's disease in Pittsburgh, PA., where he had lived for the past five years. Surviving are his wife of fifty years and sixteen days, Penny Breyer Tarplin, children Josh and Diane, Howard and Jo, and Susan and Mack, very special, loving and nourishing grandchildren Henry, Drew, Olivia, Madeleine and the late Jessica. Remembrances may be made to either the Alzheimer's Association or a charity of your choice. Interment at Mount Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, Long Island, Thursday, July 3, 1 P.M.
