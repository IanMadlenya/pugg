
        BERKMAN--Allen H. Manhattan School of Music offers its deepest sympathy to David Rahm, Chairman of the School's Board of Trustees, and his wife Susan Berkman Rahm, on the passing of her beloved father, Allen. Susan Berkman Rahm and her family are enthusiastic supporters of music and education. Their commitment to Manhattan School of Music has been demonstrated through their generosity of time and invaluable guidance to our school. We extend our most sincere and heartfelt condolences to all members of their family. Marta Istomin, President >>AD#
