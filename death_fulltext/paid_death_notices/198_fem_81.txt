
        BASSETT -- Deborah (nee O'Brien), 47, died on Sunday, July 6th, 2003, at her home in NYC. Deborah succumbed to complications from several surgeries and a failure to thrive, having had six hospitalizations in the past year. Deborah was a prominent NY antiques dealer with a national following. She is survived by her husband, William H. Bassett IV, her parents, Kevin and Deborah O'Brien, her sister, Karen O'Brien, and Karen's two children, Brendan and Siobhan. No services are planned per Deborah's wishes. >>AD#
