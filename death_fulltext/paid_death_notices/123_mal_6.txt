
        TULLMAN-Harold D., age 77, died Saturday March 29, 1997. A longtime resident of Boca Raton, Fla. Mr. Tullman was formerly of Hewlett, N.Y. He is survived by his devoted wife Irene, his loving children Jeffrey and Elaine; beloved daughter-in-law Hollace Jackson M.D. and son-in-law Peter Lustig; and cherished grandchildren Matthew, Gillian and Adam. A gentle and wise man, Mr. Tullman will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Services Tuesday, April 1 1997, 11AM at Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton, Fla. In lieu of flowers, the Tullman family suggests donation be made to a charity of personal choice.
