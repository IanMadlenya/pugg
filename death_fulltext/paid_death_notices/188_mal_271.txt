
        BISHAR-John J., Sr. Of Blue Point, NY, age 83 on September 26, 2002, after a short illness. Beloved husband of the late Mildred Bishar, beloved father and father-in-law of John and Noreen Bishar and loving grandfather of Kim (Gregg) Hession, Kelly Bishar and Lauren Bishar. Veteran of World War II, serving in the Phillipines. Mr. Bishar was a retired executive of ITT Inc., to which he gave 42 years of dedicated service. In his capacity of Vice President for ITT's Far East and Pacific Group, he traveled extensively over the world, visiting nearly every country on the globe. Visiting at Fairchild Funeral Home, Franklin Avenue at 12th Street, Garden City, New York on Friday, September 27, 2002, 2:004:00 pm and 7:00-9:00 pm. Interment at Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY, on Saturday, September 28, 2002. In lieu of flowers donations to the Scott Hession Memorial Scholarship Fund, 88 Josephine Drive, Wading River, NY 11792, would be appreciated.
