
        KORN-Edith. On Nov. 12, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Robert Korn, dear mother of William (Adrian), Robert and Edward. Cherished grandmother of Rebecca & Emily. Dear sister of Malcolm Lieberman. In her life a painter, sculptor, dancer and teacher. She was a graduate of Hunter College Class of 1938. You can meet with the family at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Friday, November 16, 2001 at 11AM. Interment is to follow at Bayside Cemetery, Ozone Park Queens, New York.
