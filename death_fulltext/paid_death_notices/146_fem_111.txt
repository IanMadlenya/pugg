
        EISENBERG-Esther. The Board of Trustees and President of Manhattan School of Music offer heartfelt condolences to longtime piano faculty member and devoted Trustee, Constance Keene, on the passing of her mother, Esther. In honor of her parents, Ms. Keene has established The Eisenberg Concerto Competition Award at Manhattan School of Music. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
