
        MURRAY-Matthew T. On August 20, 2001 in Port Chester, New York, after an illness. Eldest son of the late Matthew and Agatha Murray, devoted father of Matthew, Mercedes, and Catherine. Proud grandfather of Matthew, T. Murray IV, and Hypatia Luz, beloved brother of John, Morgan, Arthur, and Parker. He was a U.S. Navy veteran of The Pacific in WW II. ''Bro'' will be deeply missed and remembered forever. Mass 11AM Thursday at St. Jaochim's Church, Cedarhurst, L.I., NY. Donations to Maryknoll Fathers & Brothers, Maryknoll, New York 10545.
