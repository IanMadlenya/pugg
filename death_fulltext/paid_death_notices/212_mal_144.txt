
        LIPPMAN--Barry. Age 73. Of Maplewood, New Jersey, died September 15, 2004. Beloved husband of Paula, for 49 years. Devoted and loving father of John (Stacey) and Douglas (Jane). Adoring grandfather of Tommy, Taylor and Danielle. He was Vice President of Investments at the Jeffrey Matthews Financial Group. He was a member of Mountain Ridge Country Club and Admiral's Cove Country Club. He was proud to serve as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy. He was a graduate of the Wharton School of Finance at the University of Pennsylvania. He was loved by many and will be missed by all. A private tribute will be held next week. Donations may be made in his name to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
