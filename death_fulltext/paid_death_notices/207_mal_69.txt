
        SMITH--William W. Former General Electric executive. Died on April 2, 2004, in Rye, NY, at age 88. His wife of 63 years, the former Joyce Hartman, three sons, seven grandchildren and four greatgrandchildren survive him. A memorial service will be held at the Rye Presbyterian Church, Rye, NY on Saturday, May 8, 2004, at 10 AM.
