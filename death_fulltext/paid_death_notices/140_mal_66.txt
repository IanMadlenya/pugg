
        REARDON-James E. Author of ''The Sweet LIfe of Jimmy Riley'' and ''Big Time Tommy Sloane'', died suddenly on August 17 in Deerfield Beach, FL at the age of 80. Formerly of NYC & Westport, CT. he was predeceased by his loving wife of 55 years Marjorie, and eldest son Michael. He is survived by his daughters Sheila, Valerie, Jane and Julie and son, James Jr., 13 grandchildren, and one great-grandchild. A memorial service is to be announced.
