
        DEAN-Richard S., 68. December 23, 1997 in Mansfield Twp., NJ. Died of Gastric Cancer. Worked as Human Resource Executive with ''SITA'' Societe Internationale De Telecommunications in New York City, NY for 13 years and then retired. Member of American Management Association and member Society of Human Resource Management Association. Survived by his beloved wife of 25 years Dorothy Newman Dean of Mansfield Township, New Jersey. For information Martin Funeral Home. Services private. Donations may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, C/O Research Fund, 1275 York Avenue, New York, N.Y. 10021.
