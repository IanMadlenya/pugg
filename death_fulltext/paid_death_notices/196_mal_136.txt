
        KASTANIS - George. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff at the Hospital for Joint Diseases are deeply saddened by the loss of George Kastanis, devoted father of our good friend and colleague, John N. Kastanis, President and Chief Executive Officer of the Hospital for Joint Diseases. Mr. Kastanis is a long standing member of our institution and has served with distinction for many years. George Kastanis leaves a grieving family and many friends who will remember him for his kindness and devotion to his family. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, Bessie, their children, John and Maria & to the entire family. William A. Perlmuth, Chairman of the Board Steven B. Abramson, M.D. Physician-in-Chief Andrew Rosenberg, M.D. President, Medical Staff Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief
