
        GAMBINO-Thomas, 42. Of NYC, is presumed to have died in the collapse of the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. Mr. Gambino was a graduate of West Point and Harvard Law School. He was the son of Thomas Gambino of Chadds Ford, PA., and the late Frances L. Gambino. Also survived by his brother and sister-inlaw, Michael and Laura Gambino of Greenville, DE., and sister, Maria Renee Gambino of Philadelphia. Private memorial services will be held in November. Donations may be made to the American Red Cross Relief Fund or charity of your choice.
