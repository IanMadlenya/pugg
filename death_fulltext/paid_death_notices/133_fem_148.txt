
        LIBMAN - Hannah (Silverman). Aged 88, on Feb. 18, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Morris, loving mother of Jill, Kit Howard (Robert), Hillard (Elaine). Adored grandmother of Brett, Jodi (Steven), Jim, Jon (Kathleen). Greatgrandmother of Griffin and Gage. Co-founder in 1929 of Camp Hillard, Hartsdale, New York, she will be sadly missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral services will be held at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street at 11 AM, Friday, February 20. Shiva will be observed at the home of Kit and Robert Howard, Sunday through Tuesday.
        LIBMAN-Hannah. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Hannah. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
