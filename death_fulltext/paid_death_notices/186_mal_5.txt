
        MAHER-Thomas F. Of Garden City, NY, passed peacefully in his sleep on June 29, 2002. Beloved husband of Marjorie. Loving father of Susan and Joseph Festa, Eileen and Wayne Browning, Michael and Jean Maher, Christine and Ken Rathgeber and Patricia Maher. Cherished grandfather of 12 grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. Predeceased by his wife Margaret and son Thomas. Mr. Maher was a 48 year employee of Travelers Insurance Co., New York. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Franklin Ave. at 5th Street, Garden City, 10 AM, Tuesday. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury.
