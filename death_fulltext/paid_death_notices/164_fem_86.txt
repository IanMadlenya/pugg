
        SOMERVILLE-Natalie Bossert. Formerly of Brooklyn NY, Columbus Ohio, Middletown and Rumson NJ. On September 6,2000. She was from a prominent Brooklyn family that helped develop Brooklyn Heights, NY. Her grandfather and father built the Bossert Hotel on Montague Street, Brooklyn Heights. The family's former residence is now the South Wood Hole Country Club, Bayshore, LI. NY. Wife of John S. Somerville, Jr. and of the late John Heermans, Jr. Mother of John Gildersleeve Heermans, III, Charles Thorne Heermans, Patience Heermans Martin and Holly Heermans Smith. Grandmother of fourteen, great grandmother of fifteen. She is also survived by her husband John's children: Richard and Thomas Somerville, Jean Skinner, Sarah and Eleanor Somerville and his eight grandchildren. Sister of the late Louis Bossert and Isabelle Ruddy. Funeral service Saturday 10AM at the First Presbyterian Church at Tower Hill, Red Bank, NJ. Visitation at the John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Avenue, Red Bank NJ, Friday 79PM. Memorial donations may be made to the Parkinson's Disease I&R Center, A.P.D.A. Chapter, Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital, 1 RWJ Place, New Brunswick, New Jersey 08901.
