
        HOFFMANN--George, 91, on January 19, 2007. Beloved husband of the Hon. Anita Florio, Associate Justice of the Appellate Division, 2nd Department. Devoted brother of the late Paul Hoffmann. Cherished uncle to Michelle and Paul Hoffman Jr. and grand uncle to Marina, Monica, Paul III, and Erich Hoffmann. Loving step-father to George and Donna Grasso. Adored grandfather of James and Alexandra Grasso. Dear brother-in-law of Daniel and Carol Florio. Cousinin-law of Rosemary and the late Michael Durso. Cousin of Florine and Alfred Schwarzler. Fond uncle to Barbara and Richard Price, May and Bill Webb, and June and Bob Mueller. Also survived by his sister-in-law Gertrude Briedenbach. George was a former President of the Premium Point Association and Director of the Anchor Savings Bank and the Union Hospital. He and his late brother, Paul, constituted the second generation of George Hoffmann & Sons, Inc., General Contractors and Real Estate Developers. He was an accomplished and avid yachtsman and lifelong member of the New York Athletic Club, the Storm Trysail Club, the New York Yacht Club, and the Cruising Club of America. Friends may call at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, New York on Tuesday and Wednesday from 2-5 and 7-9pm. A Chapel Service will be held at the funeral home on Thursday at 10am. Funeral Services will follow at Ferncliff Chapel, Hartsdale, New York.
