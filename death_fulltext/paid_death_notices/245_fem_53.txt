
        KAHAN--Lillian (Gitlin), 85, of Springfield, MA, died Thursday at home. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her. She was predeceased by her husband of 60 years, Herbert Kahan, in 2003. She is survived by her children Mark (Linda) Kahan of Silver Spring, MD, Robert (Candace) Kahan of Longmeadow, MA, Linda (Richard) Meier of Durham, NH, and Susan (William) Rifkin of Brooklyn, NY, her eight grandchildren David (Michal) Kahan, Jonathan (Deborah) Kahan, Mayer (Israela) Kahan, Ari (Emily) Kahan, Joshua and Zachary Meier, and Brian and Michael Rifkin, and her four great-grandchildren Elie, Ariel, Aliza, and Maayan Kahan. The funeral was Friday at the Ascher-Zimmerman Funeral Home in Springfield, MA.
