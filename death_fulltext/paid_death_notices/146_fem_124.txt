
        EVANS-Ruth L. On March 9, 1999. Beloved wife of Dr. Hugh Evans. Devoted mother & mother-in-law of Margo, Marc, Mr. David Manspeiyer and Dr. Adelaide J. Evans, cherished sister and sister-in-law of Mr. & Mrs. Laurence B. Orloff. Loving daughter of the late Dr. Aaron & Sara Orloff. An educator and businesswoman, her wisdom, industriousness, self-reliance and radiant personality will be forever remembered. She will be dearly missed by all who knew her. Services private. Contributions to the Ruth L. Evans Memorial Fund, Englewood Hospital Medical Center, would be appreciated.
