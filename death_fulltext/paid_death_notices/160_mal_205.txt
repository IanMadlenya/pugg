
        STRAUS-Alexander H. Of Southbury, CT. Formerly of Manhasset, N.Y. On May 15th. Husband of Mary Ellen (Simon). Father of James, Patti and John (Diane). Grandfather of Alexis, Johnny and Jessie. Lt. Commander Straus served in the U.S. Navy aboard the George E. Badger and Daly from 1940-1945. His business career was in the hosiery industry. The family will receive friends on Sat., May 20th. from 2-5PM in Darien. In lieu of flowers, contributions to St. Francis Hospital, 100 Port Washington Blvd., Roslyn, NY 11576 or Southbury Training School, P.O. Box 872, Southbury, New York 06488, would be appreciated.
