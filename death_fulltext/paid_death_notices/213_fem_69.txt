
        BIBLO--Frances D. Widow of the late Jack Biblo, co-owner of Biblo and Tannen Booksellers of 4th Avenue in Manhattan, on Friday, October 8, 2004, at her home in Brooklyn Heights after a short illness. She was 93. A retired NYC school teacher, prior to her death she was active as the proprietor of Biblo Books, an antiquarian bookstore in Brooklyn Heights that she and Jack established after his retirement. She is survived by her sister Aida Berkman Lizardi of BelAir, MD, and her nephew Philip B. Moser of Cheshire, CT, two grandnieces and a grandnephew. Viewing Tuesday, October 12 at Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court Street, Brooklyn, from 9:30-11 am. Burial private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Brooklyn Botanic Gardens.
