
        BUDABIN-Dr. Murray, on April 25, 2000. Beloved husband of Angelica. Loving father of Nicole Wong, Alexis Budabin and Alexandra Cosima Budabin. Cherished grandfather to Samantha, Hanna and Savannah Wong. Services Friday 9:30AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        BUDABIN-Murray, M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our long-standing colleague and Associate Attending in The Department of Neurology. Dr. Budabin completed his residency and fellowship at Mount Sinai and also received extensive training in Physics. He will be remembered for his clinical skills and expertise and for more than 40 years of service to the institution. Outside the field of medicine, he was known as a charming raconteur, a wine aficionado and for his appreciation of fine dining. We extend our sincere condolences to his entire family. Stanley Tuhrim, M.D., President Terry Weill, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
        BUDABIN-Murray, MD. Dr. C. Warren Olanow, Chairman, and the Department of Neurology of the Mount Sinai School of Medicine, mourn the passing of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Murray Budabin. He did his residency at Mount Sinai in Neurology from 1958-59 and 1961-64, and completed a Fellowship in Neurology/Physics at Mount Sinai from 1964-65. He was a captain in the U.S. Air Force from 1959-61. Dr. Budabin has been in the Department of Neurology at Mount Sinai since 1965. He was a colleague, a teacher and a friend. We extend our sincere condolences to his entire family.
        BUDABIN-Murray, M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni express profound sorrow at the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend. He will be missed. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. Avi Barbasch, M.D., President Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria Padilla, M.D. Secretary William G. Stein, M.D. Treasurer
