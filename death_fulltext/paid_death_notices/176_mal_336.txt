
        SAND-Eric Martin, 36. Of Hawthorne, NY. Died tragically on Setember 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Michele (Rosenblum) Sand, loving father of Aaron Michael, 19 mos, devoted son of Lloyd and Sandra Sand of Lake Worth, Fl. Dear brother of Elise and Stuart Fraser, adored uncle of Tommy, Scott and Samantha, loving son-inlaw of Barry and Sandy Rosenblum of Lake Worth, Fl, and brother-in-law of Steven Rosenblum and family. Eric was an equity trader at Cantor Fitzgerald. He was born in Scarsdale, NY, attended Scarsdale H.S. and graduated from Perkiomen School and Tulane University. He was a loving and cherished friend to so many. We will all miss him forever. A service to remember Eric will be held on Sunday September 23, 2001 at 11:30AM at Congregation B'nai Yisrael, 2 Banksville Road, Armonk, NY 10504, 914-273-2220.
