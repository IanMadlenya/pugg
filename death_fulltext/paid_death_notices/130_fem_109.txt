
        BRUSH-Brenda M., PhD. Age 64. Died November 17 at home in Bethesda, MD. Born 27th November, 1932. Wife of William E. Spencer, sister of Craig B. Brush. Graduate of Miss Hewitt's Classes and the University of Michigan. After teaching philosophy at Ohio State University, she worked in the Bureau of Indian Affairs and in the Equal Economic Opportunity Commission as an expert on opportunity law and policy. Donations to the National Breast Cancer Coalition, 1707 L St N.W., Washington, D.C. 20036.
