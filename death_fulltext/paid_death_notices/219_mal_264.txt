
        HART--Orson Henry, Ph.D. April 22, 2005, in Bethel, CT. Born in Hartford, CT, February 22, 1913, he was a graduate of Trinity College and of the Yale School of Graduate Studies in Economics. He was the retired Executive Vice President and Director of Economic Studies for the New York Life Insurance Company. Mr. Hart is survived by his wife, Susan; his brother, Stephen Hart, two daughters, four grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren. Contributions in his memory maybe made to Trinity College Alumni Fund, Office of Development, 300 Summit St, Hartford, CT.
