
        SOLOMON-Dr. Irving. The Workmen's Circle/Arbeter Ring mourns the passing of Dr. Irving Solomon, member of our medical panel for fifty years, former Medical Director of our Health Center (1966-1990), President of our Medical Board (1985-1989) among other positions. His commitment to our organization and his caring for our members was an inspiration to our leadership and staff. Our deepest condolences to his family.
