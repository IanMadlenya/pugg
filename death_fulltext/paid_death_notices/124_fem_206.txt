
        LEVINSON-Grace, age 90, died on May 29th, 1997 after a brief illness. Devoted teacher at Morris High School in the Bronx for 35 years and member of the United Federation of Teachers executive board. Avid tennis player and world traveler. She will be missed by her sister-in-law, Lillian Levinson, formerly of NY, grand-nephew, Jonathan Adolph of MA, two grand-nieces, Janis Jordan of MA and Alison Adolph of NY, and her cousin, Susan Pollack of NY. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave., on Sunday, June 1, at 10:45 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Jewish Labor Committee Holocaust Education Fund.
