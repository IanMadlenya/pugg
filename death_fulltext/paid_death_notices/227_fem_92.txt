
        WHITBECK--Mary Ranck. 94, died peacefully at home on December 10, 2005. Predeceased in 1994 by Brainerd H. Whitbeck, her husband of 53 years, she is survived by two sons, B. Hunt Whitbeck Jr. of New York City, and John V. Whitbeck of Paris, and six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions may be made to Vassar College in lieu of flowers.
