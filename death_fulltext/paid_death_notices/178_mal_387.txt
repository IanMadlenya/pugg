
        GLACKIN-Edward J., Jr. Of Huntington, NY, on November 27, 2001, died peacefully at age 72 surrounded by family. He will be lovingly remembered by his wife Bobby, his 10 children and their spouses, his 25 grandchildren, and many friends and family whose lives he filled with joy over the years. The wake will be held at A. L. Jacobsen Funeral Home in Huntington Station, NY at 1380 New York Avenue on Thursday, November 29 between 2 and 5 PM and 7 & 9 PM. The funeral service will take place Friday, November 30 at 1 PM at St. Patrick's Roman Catholic Church in Huntington, New York at 400 Main Street.
