
        BUCKINGHAM -- Raymond J.D. Well known singer and vocal pedagogue died at the age of 75 in Holbrook, MD. He is survived by his loving wife Nancy and four children Christopher, Georgia Yambanis, Elizabeth and Peter. Raymond began his career in musical comedy in England, followed by a successful classical singing career in the US. He was also known as a prominent voice teacher who developed a highly effective technique for developing and building voices. A memorial service will be held at 1PM on Thursday, July 17 at Temple Sharey Tefilo-Israel, 432 Scotland Rd, South Orange NJ. >>AD#
