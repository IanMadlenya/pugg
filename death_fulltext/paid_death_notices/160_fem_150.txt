
        SZANCER-Irena (Zilz). On May 17, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Henryk Szancer. Loving mother of Elizabeth Szancer Kujawski and husband Nathan. Proud and adoring grandmother of Melissa and Stephanie. Cherished sister of Melita Huppert and husband Victor. Dear aunt of Frances Huppert (Jeffrey Fishman) and Arthur Huppert (Miriam Field). Born in Krakow, Poland, and a graduate of the law school of the Jagellonian University in Krakow. Came to the United States in 1947 after surviving World War II. Devoted member and chapter officer of The Woman's League for Israel. Her legacy of warmth, courage, and the love of life will live on in the many lives that she touched. Services private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Jewish National Fund of Greater New York for the Irena and Henryk Szancer Memorial Water Project, 42 E. 69 St., New York, NY 10021, att: Mrs. Dadiani.
