
        DUBINSKY-Morris Hazzan. The Cantors Assembly records with sorrow the passing of a revered colleague of over three decades. A wonderful Cantor who quietly and gently served the spiritual needs of his congregants with dignity, warmth and care. We extend our sympathy to his family and loved ones. May his memory abide as a blessing. Hazzan Sheldon Levin President Hazzan Stephen J. Stein Executive Vice President Hazzan Abraham B. Shapiro Exective Administrator
