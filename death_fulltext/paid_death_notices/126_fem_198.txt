
        ROTH-Royce. Trustees and Staff of the Public Education Association mourn the passing of Royce Roth, Trustee of more than thirty years. She served as Chair of several committees during her long and influential service to PEA. Her incisive approach and critical thinking added an important dimension to PEA's policy development. Our sincere condolences to her family. Irving S. Hamer, Chair Mary Ellen Fahs, President Emeritus Raymond J. Domanico, Executive Director
