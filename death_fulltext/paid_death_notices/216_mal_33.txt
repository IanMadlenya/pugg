
        HUDSON--Lt. Col. (Ret.) Howard Penn, APR. January 1, 2005. A descendent of Henry Hudson, Wiliam Penn and Ralph Waldo Emerson. He was founder of Public Relations Quarterly in 1955 and Hudson's Washington Directory, now in its 38th year. He was President of Hudson Associates and Newsletter Clearinghouse. Calling hours are January 5th, 9 to 11 am, at Dapson Chestney Funeral Home, 51 West Market St., Rhinebeck, NY (www.dapsonchestney.com). Funeral services are 11 am, Wednesday, at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to: The Howard Penn Hudson Scholarship, University of Maryland from the Newsletter Publishers Foundation, given annually; or The Howard Penn Hudson Memorial Award, for the student who exhibits excellence in journalism, c/o Poughkeepsie High School, Forbus St., Poughkeepsie, NY 12601.
