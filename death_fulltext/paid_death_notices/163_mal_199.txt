
        KLUGMAN-Joseph, 83. Beloved husband of Shirley. Cherished patriarch of a large and loving family who adored and respected him. Father of Dr. Peter Klugman (Marthanne), Nancy Rubin (Ronald) and Jane Ockers (Thomas). Grandfather of Deborah (James), Rebekah (Matthew), David (Amy), Michael, Jennifer, Matthew, and Daniel, and enriching his later years, great-grandfather of Sarah, Morgan, Benjamin, Amanda, Timothy, Brennan, and Hannah. He brightened the lives of all who had the good fortune to know him and he has left a void in our lives. Interment Friday, 12:30, Beth David Cemetery, Elmont.
