
        HYMAN-Joyce. On July 2, 2000, at age 74. Devoted wife of Dr. George Hyman and beloved wife of the late Robert Rubin. Cherished mother of Ginger Feuer and her husband Mike. Loving grandmother of Matthew Feuer and his wife Nicole, and adoring greatgrandmother of the love of her life Adam Robert Feuer. She will be treasured for her warmth and generosity to her family and friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street, Tuesday, 7-9 PM, with service Wednesday, 9:30 AM.
        HYMAN-Joyce. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her devoted husband, George, & to her loving daughter, Ginger Feuer, and her husband, Mike. They have sustained a great loss. We will always remember Joyce with great affection. Rona and Irwin Feuer
