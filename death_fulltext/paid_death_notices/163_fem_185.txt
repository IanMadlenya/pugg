
        SELLIN-GREHER-Sarah L., 93, Passed away in Lauder Hill, FL on August 25, 2000. Her life was filled with spirit, joy and love. Her husband Murray Greher, nephew Robert F. Baer, grandnieces Susanne Jacoby-Hale and Rochelle Jacoby-Sanchez and greatgrandnieces and nephew Chelsea, Matthew and Sarah Hale survive her. Her large and loving family and the students who studied under her will fondly remember her.
