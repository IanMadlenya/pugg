
        REITER--Frederick J. Age 96, on July 29, 2004. Husband of the late Marion Reiter. He is survived by his daughter Susan, son Leonard (Elyahu), daughter-in-law Teva and five grandchildren. Born in Breslau, Germany, a man of great intellect and wide-ranging achievement, he was a businessman, lawyer, author, historian, lyricist and dog trainer. He founded his own music publishing company, represented European record labels and became vice president at United Artists Music. His writings include the 1995 book ''They Built Utopia: The Jesuit Missions in Paraguay 1610-1768.'' His family, including the children and grandchildren of his late sister Eva Nostro in Italy, and friends mourn the loss of this unique and multi-faceted man.
