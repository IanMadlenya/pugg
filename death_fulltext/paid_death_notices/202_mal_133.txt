
        KLEIN--Donald. Of Armonk, NY. Died peacefully on November 15, 2003. Respected lawyer and loyal friend. He is survived by his wife Barbara, son Jason, daughter Zoe and other beloved family members. His family would like to express gratitude to all of those who showed their love and care. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date. Donations in his honor can be made to: The Musella Foundation, 1100 Peninsula Blvd, Hewlett, NY 11557. All donations will go towards brain tumor research.
        KLEIN--Donald S. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the untimely death of our close friend and former Board member, Donald S. Klein. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Barbara, children Jason and Zoe, and to the entire Klein and Gantz families. James M. Dubin, President Richard J. Bronstein, Secy
