
        GREENHILL-Thomas R. 67, suddenly on February 21, 2002. Loving husband and best friend of Carol, beloved father of Diane and Thomas, father-in-law of Joe and Gina, adored poppa to Ryan, Tyler, Emma and Joseph. Korean War veteran and Tin-Can Sailor, USS Gyatt. NYNEX retiree and allaround good guy. He will be sorely missed by family and friends. Visiting Monday and Tuesday, 2-4 & 7-9 at M.A. Connell Funeral Home, 934 New York Ave, Huntington Station. Mass Wednesday morning at St Patrick's Church, Huntington. Burial St Charles Cemetery.
