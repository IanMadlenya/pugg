
        SUTTLE-Virginia Liggett. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center and its parent corporation, Continuum Health Partners, Inc, mourns the passing of Virginia Liggett Suttle. She and her late husband, Harold Suttle, were loyal and devoted supporters of our hospital for nearly half a century. A Member of the Board of Trustees since 1956, she chaired numerous major events, most notably the Ball of Roses, Roosevelt Hospital's first annual dinner dance. To her family and friends, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington, Chairman of the Board, St Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Ctr Morton P. Hyman, Chairman of the Board, Continuum Health Partners Robert G. Newman, MD, Acting President Carlton Boxhill, MD, President of the Medical Board
