
        SHEARD-Wendy Stedman (Williams). Friends and colleagues mourn the passing of Wendy Sheard, age 62, on March 25th in Stony Creek, CT. She was the beloved wife of Michael and a devoted mother and stepmother to Amanda (Bourque), Tony (Stedman), Paul, Russell and Craig Sheard. She was the loving sister of Jill Williams and was blessed with seven grandchildren, Emma, Laura, Zoe, Keegan Sheard, Julia, Maddie, and Katie Bourque. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Connecticut Hospice, 61 Burban Drive, Branford, CT 06405.
