
        STEVENS-Douglas K. 54, born and raised on Staten Island, passed away December 26 in Port St. Lucie, FL. Survived by his wife, Judith Gaddis Stevens, a daughter, Kelly Stevens of Piscataway, NJ, sons Christopher Murray, FDNY, of Staten Island, and Michael Murray of Hickam AFB in Hawaii, a sister, Susan Stevens of Port St. Lucie. Douglas worked for GM for more than 31 years, retiring in 1997. He held various positions as an active member of U.A.W. Local 595. A Funeral Mass was held December 31, at St. Lucie Catholic Church. A memorial service will be held at St. Paul Church on Staten Island, January 26.
