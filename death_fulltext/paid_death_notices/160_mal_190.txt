
        STONE-Theodore (Ted), age 83. Former New York Times photographer, naval photographer, President of Tilton Textile Corp and of Temple Israel of Northern Westchester, Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y. Born New York City. Attended Wharton; his U.S. naval photo collection is now at the Mariner's Museum. Loving husband of the late Lily Calderon (Spitz), father of the late Alfred, and of Michael. Beloved cousin to Ted and Erica Braude. Contributions may be sent to Temple Israel, Glengary Road, Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y. 10520 or the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 633 3rd Avenue, New York, New York 10017.
