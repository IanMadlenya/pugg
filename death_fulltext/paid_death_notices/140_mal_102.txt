
        METZGER-Juan. September 6, 1998. Beloved husband of Florence, devoted father of Joe, Tim and Jacqui, cherished grandfather of Jill, loving brother of Miriam. Service Thursday, September 10, 9:45 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Browning School, 52 East 62 Street, New York, New York 10021.
        METZGER-Juan E. The Browning School community notes with sadness the death of Juan E. Metzger, generous supporter of the school and jovial resident of our neighborhood on East 62nd Street. Our thoughts are with his wife Florence, former president of the Parents Association and past Trustee, and with their son Joe '02. Stephen M. Clement, III Headmaster Allan L.
