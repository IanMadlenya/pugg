
        RICHARD-Mary E. The Board of Directors, staff and volunteers of the Theodore Roosevelt Sanctuary, Inc., deeply the mourn the untimely death of our Executive Director, Mary E. Richard. We extend our heartfelt condolences to all the members of her family and to her many friends. Mary's commitment and dedication to the sanctuary and its mission have been and will continue to be an inspiration to us all. Becky Stern, President
