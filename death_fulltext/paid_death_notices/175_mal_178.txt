
        DEAN-E. Douglas, Ph.D. August 15, 2001. The Board and members of the American Society for Psychical Research (ASPR) mourn the passing of E. Douglas Dean, Ph.D. Dr. Dean was a much beloved man of science who will be greatly missed; his wisdom, graciousness and lifetime commitment to research will not be forgotten. Memorial services will be held at the ASPR and at the Princeton Religious Society of Friends in the fall. Patrice Keane, Executive Dir.
