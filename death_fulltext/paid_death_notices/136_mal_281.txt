
        RIPOSANU-Pamfil. On May 22, 1998. Beloved husband of Marguerite. Loving father of Ann Maria and son-in-law Jan Ter Haar. Also survived by a brother-in-law, two sisters-in-law and ten nieces and nephews. Lawyer and former Ambassador from the Kingdom of Romania to Washington. A very special, gentle and giving man who loved people and always will be remembered by his family and friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, May 26, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, May 27, 10 AM, St. Catherine of Sienna Church, 411 East 68 Street.
