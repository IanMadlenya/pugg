
        BLACK--Ernest S. (Sandy), 83, on October 6, in Naples, Florida after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Jean Gumport. Devoted father of Sandra (Sam) Black-Pipkin and her husband Jon, of St. Louis, son Gary of Dallas and dear friend to grandson Ray of Dallas. He was predeceased by son Bruce. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 9th at 2PM at the Bentley Village, West Clubhouse Auditorium. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations in his memory to Hospice of Naples, 1095 Whippoorwill Lane, Naples, Florida 34105, or to a charity of your choice.
