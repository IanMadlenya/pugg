
        HEIDELL--Flora S. of Scarsdale. Passed away on August 24, 2004. Flora was born on June 19, 1928 in NYC to Alexander and Anna Siegel. She married James Heidell on June 26, 1952 also in NYC. James predeceased her on April 18, 1986. Beloved mother of Beth Underwood, Pam Heidell and Jim Heidell. Grandmother of five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday August 28 at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, New York at 1 PM in the chapel.
