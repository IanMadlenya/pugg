
        DEERY-John Robert. Died of cancer on April 29, at the age of 59 in Portola Valley, California. John was educated at Temple and Rutgers Universities, served in the Army as a communications specialist, and spent over 30 years as a telecommunications executive. He is survived by his wife Mary Anne, their daugters Susanne, Julianne, Kathleen & Mary Jeanne, and his mother Mary Covington, as well as 17 nieces & nephews.
