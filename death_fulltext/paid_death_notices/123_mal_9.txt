
        KARFUNKEL-Louis. The administration, faculty, and students of Touro College record with sorrow the passing of the beloved father of Mr. Michael Karfunkel, a member of the Board of Trustees of the College, and chairman of the Flatbush Division Board of Directors, & his brother Mr. George Karfunkel, a member of the Flatbush Division Board of Directors. Mr. Louis (Yisroel Yehudah) Karfunkel, an author of scholarly Talmudic treatises, together with his beloved wife, Clara, imbued his sons with a passion for Torah learning, a commitment to tzedakah, & a dedication for nurturing future generations of the Jewish people through philanthropic support and leadership of 7enters of Torah scholarship and institutions of higher learning. His legacy lives on in the examplary deeds and generosity of his sons, Michael and George. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Clara Karfunkel, the matriarch of the family, and to her sons. May they be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Bernard Lander, President Mr. Mark Hasten, Chairman of the Board
