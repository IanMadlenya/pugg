
        MARIN--Shiela. Of Woodmere, L.I. passed away on March 1, 2006. Devoted wife of Murray, and adored mother of Reina, Michael, and Morisa. Beloved grandmother of Rachel, Elissa, Alan, Jordan, Jason, Jacqueline, Benjamin, Laurence and Gregory. Cherished mother-inlaw of Emilio, Marla and Gene. Dear sister of Melvin and Jay, and sister-in-law of James, Mildred, Marcia and Dena. Dearest friend of Daniella and Lilia. Her dedication to her family and friends was admired and unparalled. Community activist, she served as an officer and fund raiser for the Five Towns Music and Art Foundation and the Woodmere Park Association for many years. Her spirit and warmth will be missed by all whose lives she touched. The funeral service will be held at BoulevardRiverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, N.Y. on Friday, March 3rd at 11:30 A.M. Directions at 516-295-3100.
