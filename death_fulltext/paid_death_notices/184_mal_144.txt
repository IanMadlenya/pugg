
        OSTROVE - Lester L. M.D., F.A.C.S., May 4, 2002. Inspiration to three generations of family, friends and colleagues for his integrity, steadfastness, generosity of spirit and love of learning. Born July 4, 1913. Served as flight surgeon in WW II. Practiced general surgery at Lincoln Hospital; Bronx-Lebanon Hospital, where he was President of Medical Board; and St. Barnabas Hospital, where he was Director of Surgery. Served as President of the Bronx Chapter of the American College of Surgeons and was Clinical Assistant Professor of Surgery at Albert Einstein College of Medicine. In 1975 he was honored by the United Jewish Appeal for his service to the organization. In retirement, he continued to serve the medical community in various capacities. Well into his eighties, he was a force to be reckoned with on the fairways of Alpine CC. He is survived by his wife Kate, a daughter Sue Ann Heller, and grandaughters Samantha and Alison Heller; son-in-law, Charles Heller, and two grandsons-in-law, Raj Bhattacharrya and Glen W. Roberts II; and a brother Bert Ostrove. His nieces and nephews will also miss his love and wisdom. He was devoted to his family and much loved and respected by all who knew him.
        OSTROVE-Dr. Lester. Alpine Country Club, its Officers, Board of Governors and members deeply mourn the untimely passing of their fellow member, Dr. Lester Ostrove, and offer their deepest sympathy to his wife Kate and the entire Ostrove family. Alpine Country Club, Mel Konner, President
