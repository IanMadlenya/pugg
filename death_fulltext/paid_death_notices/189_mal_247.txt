
        REXSITE - Seymour. The Board of Directors and the staff of the Folksbiene Yiddish Theatre are deeply saddened by the passing of the great artist and Yiddish music and theatre icon, Seymour Rexsite. We extend our condolences to Mel Freedman and his entire family over the loss of their dear uncle. Seymour leaves us a legacy of song and good deeds that will live on in the hearts of Yiddish lovers forever. Zalmen Mlotek, Exec. Dir. Robert Kaplan, President
