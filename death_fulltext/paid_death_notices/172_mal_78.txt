
        STREETER-Henry S. Of Wenham, MA, May 4. 81 years, husband of Mary Ann (Dexter) Streeter. Father of Cornelia (Nina) V.R. Streeter of Topsfield, Natalie T. Streeter of Midway, UT, and Frank S. Streeter II of Lancaster. Survived by four grandchildren. Brother of Lilian S.L. Chance of Malvern, PA, Frank S. Streeter of New York, NY, and Thomas W. Streeter of Pacific Grove, CA. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, May 9, in the Christ Church, 149 Asbury St, So. Hamilton, MA, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the Peabody & Essex Museum, 150 Essex St, Salem, MA 01970.
