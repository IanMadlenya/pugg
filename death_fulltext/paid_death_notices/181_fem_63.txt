
        SCOTT-Dorothy Sullivan. Of New York City died February 5, 2002, at age 96. Widow of S. Lytton Scott and daughter of Dr. Eugene C. Sullivan, former Chairman and President of the Corning Glass Works. She attended Antioch College where she was the first woman editor of the student newspaper. Later she wrote a column Lobbing with Lucy in Tennis Week Magazine for 15 years. She is survived by two sons, Timothy S. Scott of Kent, CT, and Eugene L. Scott of New York City, 5 grandchildren, Timothy Jr, Eldon, Katherine, Lucy and Sam and two greatgrandchildren, Drew and Dana Rae. A memorial service will be announced.
