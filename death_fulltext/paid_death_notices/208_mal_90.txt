
        SNYDER--Martin F. 94, of NYC and Miami, Fla on May 3, 2004. Cultured, elegant and a veteran traveler, he enjoyed fifty years operating Martin Snyder Travel and taking special audiences to theatrical events around the world. Survived by his sister Sylvia Zimmerman, nieces, Elyn Zimmerman, Marcy Gran and nephew Charles Zimmerman. For information Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 212-288-3500. Interment Friday May 7, 1PM at Montefiore Cemetery, Philadelphia, PA.
