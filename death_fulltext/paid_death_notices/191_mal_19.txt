
        ANDERSON-Arnold S. ''Andy''. 82, of Old Greenwich, CT died peacefully on November 30, while in Newport, RI. Retired sales executive for Champion International, he was predeceased in 1994 by wife of 43 years, Sally Stilwell Anderson. Survived by devoted daughters Dr. Susan G. Anderson of Woodside, CA, Sandra A. Boles of Old Greenwich; granddaughters Kelly and Shannon Boles, and his sister Eris White. Memorial service on Saturday, December 7, 11AM, First Congregational Church, Sound Beach Avenue, Old Greenwich.
