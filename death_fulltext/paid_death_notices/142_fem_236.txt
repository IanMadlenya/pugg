
        CORPUEL-Belma Teich. Beloved wife of Jules; mother of Michael, Noel, Peter and Jeffrey, Kathy, Carol and Georgeann; sister of Elsie Seckel and the late Victor Teich; sister-in-law of Edythe Teich; brother-in-law of the late Robert Seckel; grandmother of Stacy, Adena, Josh, Alison, Jessica, Rebecca and great grandmother of Aaron, passed from our lives and we will dearly miss her. Belma was Director of Camp Wayne since 1952. Although she will not be with us physically, she will always be in our hearts. We have all gained strength, determination and love from her. A memorial service will be held at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel in Hewlett, LI on Monday, November 30 at 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Association for the Help of Retarded Children.
