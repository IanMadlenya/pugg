
        McCAULEY-Barry. New York City Opera mourns the death of our good friend and colleague, Barry McCauley. He joined our company in 1980 and sang in many of our productions over the years, providing many memorable & exciting evenings in the theater. He was a gifted artist, and a generous and joyous colleague, who died much too soon. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife Deborah, and his children Bryan and Meghan. Paul Kellogg General Director
        McCAULEY-Barry. The Richard Tucker Music Foundation mourns the passing of Barry McCauley, a beloved artist and winner of the 1980 Richard Tucker Award. Our sincere condolences go out to his wife Deborah and his children. Barry Tucker, President Jeffrey S. Tabak, Chairman
