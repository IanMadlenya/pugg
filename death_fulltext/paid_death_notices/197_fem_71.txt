
        SCHOEMAN--Linda H. The staff and Board of Trustees of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services are deeply saddened by the passing of Dr. Linda Schoeman, our highly respected and loved colleague. Dr. Schoeman was a gifted psychologist and made a difference in the lives of countless children and families over her distinguished career. Our sympathies to her family. Jean L. Troubh, Pres.
