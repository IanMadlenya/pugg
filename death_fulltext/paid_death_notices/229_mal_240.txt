
        VOLKMAN--Samuel. 97. The family of Rabbi Samuel Volkman sorrowfully announces his death on February 18. His rabbinical leadership endured for over 70 years with brilliance, a profound sense of duty and great dignity. His wife was the late Sally B. Volkman. He is survived by two beloved daughters and a son-in-law, Suzanne & Nancy and Edward Skloot, and cherished grandson Joseph. Funeral services will be February 21 at Danzansky-Goldberg, Rockville, MD at 11 AM.
