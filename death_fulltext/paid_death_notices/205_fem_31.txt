
        SNYDER -- Eleanor M., passed away on February 1 at the age of 88. A beloved mother and devoted wife, she was predeceased by her husband of 47 years, Philip. Visitation will take place on Wednesday, February 4 between 2-4PM at the Meserole Funeral Home, 145 Lord Ave, Inwood, NY. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, February 5, 11AM at Trinity-St. John's Episcopal Church, 1142 Broadway, Hewlett, NY 11557. Interment will follow in Trinity Churchyard.
