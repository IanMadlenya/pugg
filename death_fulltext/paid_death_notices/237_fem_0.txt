
        ABADIE--MJ. Died Wednesday, September 13, 2006, in Nacogdoches, Texas. She authored and co - authored many published books, assisting with and designing The Mythic Image (1974) by Joseph Campbell, with whom she worked for many years. She met Mr. Campbell when she edited for Bollingen Press. A woman of great brilliance, generosity, and talent, Ms. Abadie counseled people, traveled Europe and was a gourmet cook. She had moved back to Texas to be with family and to write. She is survived by her nephews Victor Hugo Abadie III; J. Paul Abadie; Marcus G. Abadie, MD; Carl Abadie; and her only niece, Margaret Jeanne Marie Abadie Whittaker.
