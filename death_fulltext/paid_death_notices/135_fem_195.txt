
        KOCH-Pearl (Ferber), 92. Of Forest Hills, NY, passed away on April 26, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Cherished mother of Jerome S. Koch and Gale Baim. Dearest mother-in-law of Judith Koch and Sheldon Baim. Loving grandmother of Ilene Becker, Irene Koch and Ron Adelman, Robert and Laura Koch, Jonathan and Leslie Koch, Jill and Michael Gold. Adoring great-grandmother of Sophie Adelman and Alexander Gold. Devoted sister of Bertha Diamond. Dear friend, officer and adviser of the Hannah Senesch Group of the Forest Hills Chapter of Hadassah. She will live in the hearts of all who knew and loved her. Private graveside service.
