
        SINGSEN--Mary Ellen, 88, of Edgartown, MA, previously of Scarsdale, NY, and Winnetka and Glencoe, IL, November 19, 2006. An advocate for peace and justice all her life, and a lifelong lover of books, art, and music. Survived by her two brothers, four children, 11 grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Interment Saturday, December 2, 12:00, Purchase Friends Meeting Cemetery, Purchase, NY. Memorial service Sunday, December 3, 11:00, Scarsdale Friends Meeting, 133 Popham Road, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to American Friends Service Committee, 15 Rutherford Place, NYC, NY 10003.
