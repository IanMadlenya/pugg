
        FAYYAZ - Shaikh Mo hammed. Passed away on June 8, 2002 in Stamford, CT. Prominent banker with Habib Bank Ltd. in Karachi, Colombo, Beirut, New York and London. He loved his family and he helped a lot of people along the way. He was a pioneer with a mischievous sense of humor. Survived by his wife of 51 years, Zamurad, five children, 5 grandchildren, 3 daughters-in-law, nieces, nephews and cousins. Prayer and funeral services were held on June 10, 2002. Quran Khawani will be held on Saturday, June 15, 2002 at 6 PM at The Westchester Muslim Center, 22 Brookfield Road, Mount Vernon, New York.
