
        JASPER - Gertrude Rathbone, PhD. of Hampton Bays, NY and formerly of New York City died May 22, 2002 at age 94 in Westhampton, NY. Born July 7, 1907 in New York City, she was a graduate of St. Agatha's School and Wells College. She held a Doctorate degree in French and a Master's degree in Library Science. In addition to teaching at Hunter College in New York and Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, she was co-owner of the millinery business, Toby Coppock, Inc. She is survived by her cousin, Martha Dwyer, along with many other cousins. Predeceased by her sister, Juliet Absolom. Visiting hours 9-10 AM, Saturday, May 25, followed by a service at 10 AM at the J. Ronald Scott Funeral Home, Hampton Bays, NY. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Wildlife Trust, 61 Route 9W, Palisades, NY 10964 or The Brooklyn Museum, 200 Eastern Parkway, Brooklyn, NY 11238
