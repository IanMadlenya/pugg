
        McCLELLAND -- Pamela Turner, of Grosse Pointe Farms, MI, died at home Thursday, February 24, 2005. Born in Detroit, MI, Mrs. McClelland was a graduate of the Masters School and attended Smith College and the Sorbonne. She married James Farley McClelland, Jr. in June 1945 and lived in New Haven, CT and Grosse Pointe Farms, MI. She is survived by her daughter, Pamela K., and her son, James F., III, both of Washington, DC. She is predeceased by her brothers, James M., Ernest T., John D. and Richard H. Turner. Memorials may be made to the Huron Mountain Wildlife Foundation, 5075 Warren Rd., Ann Arbor, MI 48105 or to the Masters School, 49 Clinton Ave., Dobbs Ferry, NY 10522. Memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Grosse Pointe, Wednesday, March 2.
