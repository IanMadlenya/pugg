
        OSTERWEIS--Steven L. The Board of Directors and Officers of Phillips Van Heusen Corporation mourn the passing of our longtime Director and friend, Steven Osterweis, a man of extraordinary integrity and wisdom. His advise and view of the future carried with it a unique combination of historical perspective and youthful optimism and energy. His sage advise and support will be missed more than words can express. Our sincere condolences to his beloved wife, Harriet, his children and grandchildren. We are all better for having known him. Bruce J. Klatsky, Chairman and CEO
