
        THORBECKE - HOCHWALD - Jea nette, M.D., Ph.D. Wife of Gerald Hochwald, M.D. Loving mother of Bertrand, Neal and Steven. Cherished grandmother and friend. Died unexpectedly on Friday, November 16, 2001. Born in the Netherlands and resident of Douglaston, Queens, NY. She was Professor of Pathology at New York University Medical Center for more than 45 years. Closed services will be held on Wednesday, November 21, 2001. Memorial service to honor her life will be announced at a later date.
