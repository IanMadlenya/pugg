
        SILBER--Cecil, 94, co-owner of 1 Christopher Street for 72 years, passed away on July 25, 2004. Beloved husband of Madelyn for 66 years, loving father of Carole and Jimmy, devoted brother of Barbara. He was a genteel man with an uncanny sense of wit, style and grace, who will be dearly missed. Services at Riverside-Nassau, Great Neck, 11AM Tuesday, July 27th.
