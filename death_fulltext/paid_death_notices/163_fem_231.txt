
        CAYTON-Doris. Died on August 30. Devoted and beloved wife of Bill Cayton, her loving husband of 58 years. Cherished mother of Brian, Patricia and Merrie. Loved mother-in-law of Meryl and Kurt. Loved grandmother of Vanessa and Gabrielle. Treasured sister of Morton Roberts and his wife Dolores and their children Geri and April. Adored sister-in-law of Harold Goltzer and his children Dana and Eric. She was loved by Dana's children Alyssa and John and Eric's children Andrea and Liz. Doris Cayton was an extraordinary daughter to her deceased parents Minnie and Abe Rothback, and devoted daughter-in-law to the late Esther Cayton. Her cousins and family meant everything to her. She dedicated her life to making her husband's and children's life happy while working full time at home as her husband's trusted and indispensable business partner. Brilliant and beautiful, she always lived her life with dignity and elegance, never having a bad word to say about anyone. She will remain in our hearts forever. Services Friday, 11:30 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue.
