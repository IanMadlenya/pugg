
        YUE-Shyh-Jong, M.D. On July 13, 2001, in Philadelphia, age 88. Survived by loving family: wife NaiTchi, son David, daughter-in-law Charlotte, and grandchildren Frances, Timothy and Emmalyn. Buddhist service will be scheduled at a later date. To share memories and anecdotes, e-mail shyhjongyue@ yahoo.com.
        YUE-Shyh Jong, M.D. The Board and membership of the Chinese American Medical Society mourn the passing of Dr. Shyh Jong Yue, Director Emeritus of Physical Medicine at St. Lukes Hospital. Dr. Yue was a founding member and past President of the Society. His leadership and strength will be missed. John C. Wang, M.D., President
