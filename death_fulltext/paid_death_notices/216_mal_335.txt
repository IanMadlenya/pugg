
        WRISTON--Walter B. The Greater New York Councils, Boy Scouts of America, acknowledges with deep sorrow the passing of our friend and benefactor, Eagle Scout Walter B. Wriston. Our thoughts and prayers go out to his family and his many friends during this difficult time. Mr. Wriston's leadership in banking and finance was legendary, as was his compassion and kindness to charity generally and the Boy Scouts of America in particular. John F. McGillicuddy, Pres. Charles E. Rosser, CEO
