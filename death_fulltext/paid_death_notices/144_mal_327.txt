
        McLOUGHLIN-Rev. William J., The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Margaret Mary's Church, 215 Exeter Street, Brooklyn, on January 18th. Reposing at Marine Park Funeral Home, 30-24 Quentin Road, Brooklyn, Tuesday, January 19th, from 7 to 10 P.M., and Wednesday, January 20, from 2 -5 P.M. Mass of transferral, on Wednesday, January 20, at St. Margaret Mary's Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass, Thursday, January 21 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
