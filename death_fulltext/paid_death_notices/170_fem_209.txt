
        SCHIFF-Selma Saslow. On March 25, at age 86. Beloved wife of the late Bernard A. Saslow & the late Sidney L. Schiff. Loving mother of Judy Grace, Bill Saslow and Nancy Edelstein. Loving mother-in-law of Norman Grace, Peter Edelstein and Diane Saslow. Cherished grandmother of Bob & Stephanie Grace, Wendy Edelstein, Jodi Edelstein Strati, Emily and Michael Saslow. Great grandmother of Sarah, Julia and Alexandra Grace. Also survived by the children, grandchildren and great grandchildren of the late Sidney Schiff, who treated her with great love and respect. Beloved sisterin-law of Selma F. Sulken. Services Tuesday, March 27, 12 noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Visitation Tuesday, March 27, at her Great Neck residence. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to the American Heart Association or to the charity of your choice.
