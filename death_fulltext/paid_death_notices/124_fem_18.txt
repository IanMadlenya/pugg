
        WALLACH-Mildred. On May 2, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Emil. Adored and cherished mother of Eugene Wallach and Alice Kulick Klein and mother-in-law of Howard Klein. Loving grandmother of Genevieve and Robert, Peter and Ann Mette, Phillis and Renee and Tommy. Great grandmother of Bret, Nicole, Siri Lynn and Isabella. Services Sunday, 10:30 A.M., ''Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels'', 76th Road and Queens Boulevard in Forest Hills.
        WALLACH-Mildred. The officers, leadersip and staff of UJA-Federation record with great sorrow the passing of Mildred Wallach, generous supporter of the organization for many years and mother of Eugene and Alice Kulick Klein, dedicated leader and former chair of our Westchester campaign. Her legacy of generosity and concern for the Jewish people lives on through her daughter Alice, granddaughter Rene Brody and grandson-in-law Tom Brody, exemplars of our next generation of leadership. To Alice and her husband, Howard, Rene and Tom Brody and the entire family, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Larry Silverstein, Chair of Brd Louise Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec VP
