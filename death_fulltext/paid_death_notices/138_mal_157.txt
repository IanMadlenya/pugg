
        MILLETT-Daniel Caldwell. At 80, of Southbury, CT. Died on July 13. Beloved husband of Mary Allison Millett of Southbury, loving father of Daniel A. Millett of New Zealand, Allison M. Deen of Westminster, VT and Emilie M. O'Malley of Santa Monica, CA; grandfather of five. He graduated from St. Mark's School in Southborough, MA and from Yale University in 1941. He was a U.S. Navy veteran of WW II and was a retired executive with AT&T. Memorial service Wednesday, July 22, at 11 AM, at United Church of Christ, 238 Main St. N, Southbury, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to United Church of Christ Memorial Fund, 238 Main St. N, Southbury, CT. 06488; or Trinity St. John's Church, 1142 Broadway, Hewlett, NY 11557.
