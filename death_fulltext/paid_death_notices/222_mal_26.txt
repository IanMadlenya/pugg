
        LENNARD--Henry L. June 22, in Venice, Italy. Professor, Columbia University and Berkeley, expert in family studies. Co-author of ''The Anatomy of Psychotherapy and Patterns in Human Interaction.'' He challenged the drug industry in ''Mystification and Drug Misuse.'' With his wife, Suzanne Crowhurst, he created the International Making Cities Liveable Conferences and authored 12 books. Besides his family he had three great loves: Martin Buber, Venice and the movies, especially ''Beat the Devil.'' He will be deeply missed by wife Suzanne, children Erica, Elizabeth and Jonathan, the Meyers family and Elaine Lennard. The service was held on Isola di San Michele. A memorial will be held in New York in the fall.
