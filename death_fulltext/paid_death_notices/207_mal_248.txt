
        BOBER--Arnold. Born August 4, 1917 in The Hague, Holland. Died peacefully on April 21, 2004. Beloved husband of Senta and loving father of Franklin and the late Jack. Loving grandfather of Suzanne, Sara, David, Gabrielle, Bronwen and Jenia. Greatgrandfather of Ted. Brotherin-law of Henry Solan. An intellectual, investor, linguist and businessman. He was loved and he will be missed. Services at 9AM Friday, April 23 at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, New York City.
