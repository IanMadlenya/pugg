
        ROSENBLATT-David Alan. On February 18, 1998, of Manhattan, NY, devoted father of Pamela and Matthew Rosenblatt, succumbed to complications of diabetes after putting up a heroic battle. Beloved first-born of Claire and William Rosenblatt of Boca Raton, FL. Loving big brother of Stuart, Mark and Laura, cherished uncle of Sarah, Kevin and Jacob, and dearest brother-in-law to Elizabeth, Luann, and Carl. David was a man of incredible kindness, generosity, and humor who befriended all those who crossed his path. His mastery of the printing and advertising field was universally admired by both colleagues and competitors. Services at 11:15 AM on Sunday, February 22, at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, West 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York. Interment to follow at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
