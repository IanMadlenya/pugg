
        CONNOLLY--Marc Edward. Of Manhattan, NY, passed away suddenly on October 10, 2005, at age 31, in Egypt while on vacation fulfilling a dream of exploring the land of the pharaohs. Beloved son of Marilyn and Matthew Connolly. Loving brother of Matthew. Devoted, loyal and loving friend to many. A graduate of Stuyvesant High School, the State University of New York at Albany and Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law. Marc was one of the nations top political consultants. In his short life, he revolutionized the way modern political campaigns were waged. His expertise in this area is recognized by all in the field. For those whose lives he touched, he will always live on in their hearts and memories. Family will receive friends at Kearns Funeral Home, 61-40 Woodhaven Blvd, Rego Park, NY, on Saturday and Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Mass Monday, October 17 at 9:30 A.M. St. Stanislaus Kostka RC Church, 57-15 61 St, Maspeth.
