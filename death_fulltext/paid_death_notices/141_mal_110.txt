
        De KAY-Ormonde. Writer, historian, poet, editor. Beloved husband of Barbara, father of Thomas, brother of George and James Tertius, stepfather of Lenore and Philip Roosevelt, step-grandfather of three, friend of hundreds, maybe thousands. Wrote books, film scripts, light verse and articles over a 50 year career. Served on destroyer escorts in WW II and Korean War. Born in New York City on December 17, 1923. He died there after a brief illness on October 9, 1998. Interment will be private at St. George's Church in Hempstead, New York. A memorial service will be announced later. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Authors League Fund for writers in financial distress.
