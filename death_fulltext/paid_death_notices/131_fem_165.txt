
        NORBAN-Joetta. On December 18, 1997, in New York City, at the age of 83. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Norban. Loving mother of Linda Lane and Wendy Carduner. Devoted grandmother of Lisa, Julie, Stacey, Jason, Marc and Eric. Adoring great-grandmother of Alex, Madison, and her newborn grandson. Dear mother-in-law of Jules and Andrew. As long as we are here, you will be here with us, in our lives and hearts. The funeral will be private. A memorial service will be held in January. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to Dr. Valentine Fuster, Director of the Cardiovascular Institute, Mount Sinai Medical Center.
        NORBAN-Joetta. We will truly miss you. Your spirit has been part of our lives forever. You left a legacy of love and caring which was evident by the wonderful, tireless care and attention given you by our dearest friend, Linda, and your grandchildren. You were truly loved. Ellie and Martin Lifton
