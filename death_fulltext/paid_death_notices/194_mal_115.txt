
        JACOBSON-Julius F. Passed away on March 8, 2003. Julius was the beloved father of Michael Jacobson, father-in-law of Lynn Chancer and grandfather of Alexander Jacobson. Adored husband of Phyllis Jacobson. He will be missed profoundly by his family, friends and colleagues in the democratic socialist movement where he was the respected editor of the journal New Politics. A memorial will be held in April.
