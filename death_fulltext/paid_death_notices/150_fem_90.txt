
        McCLUSKEY-Olga Newton. On July 4. Beloved wife of the late J. Sterling McCluskey. Beloved step mother of Shirley Simms. Adored step grandmother of Carson and James Simms. Mrs. McCluskey was the founder and president of the New York Chapter of the Friends of Winston Churchill Memorial and Library at Westminster College in Fulton, Missouri and was also a member of the Board of Governors of the Winston Churchill Memorial and Library. She was awarded an M.B.E. and an O.B.E. by Queen Elizabeth for her tireless work for British charities. Interment private. A memorial service will be held Friday, July 9, at 3:30PM at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Winston Churchill Memorial and Library, 501 Westminster Ave, Fulton, Missouri 65251.
