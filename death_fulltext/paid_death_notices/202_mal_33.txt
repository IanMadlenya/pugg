
        GUENETTE--Robert. Awardwinning filmmaker and cofounder of RGP, LAMEC and IDA died at Cedars Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles on October 31 of brain cancer. He was 68. Predeceased by his wife, Frances, he is survived by son Dr. Mark D. Guenette, mother-in-law Basia Gudemann, siblings Sheila Kurtz, Gary, Terry, Bruce and Sharon Atwell, niece Patricia Bylicki and longtime partner Robert Leeburg. Donations in his honor can be made to www.notinourname.net. Services were held November 2 at Mount Sinai Cemetery in Los Angeles. A celebration of his life will take place on November 9.
