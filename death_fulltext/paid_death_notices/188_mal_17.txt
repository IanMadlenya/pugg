
        LEVINE-Lawrence Arnold. January 23, 1931-August 25, 2002. Beloved and devoted father, born in New York, attended Wharton School of Business at University of Pennsylvania, successful career in commercial real estate, spent last 35 years in Florida. His daughters Ali Lindsay and Laine Alexandra, both in New York, were his life and they loved him just as much. Also survived by his sister Elaine Lombardi and brother Stephen Levine.
