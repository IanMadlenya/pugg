
        O'LEARY-James F. Of Spring Lake, New Jersey, formerly of Chatham Twp., NJ on December 27, 1998. Beloved husband of Anne Murnane O'Leary. Father of Laura O'Leary of Summit, NJ; James F., Jr. and his wife Laura Ann of Madison, NJ; Lynne Buckalew and her husband John of Howell Twp., NJ. Grandfather of Brooke. Son of Rita O'leary Smith of Whitestone, NY. Brother of Robert O'Leary of Denville, NJ and Joanne McNeill of Huntington, LI. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. A Funeral Liturgy will be celebrated at Corpus Christi Church, 234 Southern Blvd., Chatham, NJ on Wednesday, December 30, 1998 at 11 am. Friends may call at the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, 345 Main Street, (Route 124), Chatham on Tuesday from 24 and 7-9 P.M. Those who wish may contribute in his memory to the LaSalle Academy, NYC.
