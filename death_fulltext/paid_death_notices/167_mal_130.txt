
        SCHECHTER-Stanley. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Stanley Schechter, a dear friend of the Jewish community. Mr. Schechter expressed a deep concern for Jewish and humanitarian causes through his generous support of our Diamond, Jewelry & Watch Group. As past chair of this division, he was highly respected for his leadership and dedicated efforts. We extend our sincerest condolences to his beloved wife, Betty; his daughter, Roberta; his son, Joel, and daughter-in-law, Kim; his grandchildren, Michael and Daniel; and the entire family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair & President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Marvin Markman, Chair, Diamond, Jewelry & Watch Group
        SCHECHTER - Stanley. There could be only one Stanley and the world will truly miss him. He meant so much to so many. The lessons that I learned, the examples that he set, the values that he stood for, and the compassion that he portrayed are my enduring memories of a genuinely great man. My heartfelt condolences to Betty, Roberta, Joel and the entire family. Steven M. Merdinger
