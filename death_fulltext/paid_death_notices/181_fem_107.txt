
        COHEN-Ida Phillips, born April 5, 1906 in Paterson, New Jersey, died in Pompano Beach, Florida on Sunday, February 10, 2002. She was the devoted wife of the late Samuel Cohen and the loving mother of Beryl (Murray) Zankel and Alan (Carol) Cohen. She was the grandmother of six and the great-grandmother of six. She is also survived by a brother, Harry M. Phillips of Paterson. Services are planned for 10 AM Wednesday, February 13th at the Jewish Memorial Chapel, 841 Allwood Road, Clifton, New Jersey.
