
        McELWAIN-Jack White, M.D. Of Huntington, formerly of Muttontown, N.Y., on January 30, 2002 in his 80th year. Beloved husband of Ruth (Kelly). Dearly loved father of Valerie Flynn and Donna McElwain Helder. Cherished grandfather of Jay Flynn. Devoted brother of Dorothy Will and loving uncle of Richard and Eric Will of Syracuse, N.Y. Jack will be forever fondly remembered in the hearts of his family and the countless friends who knew him. A graduate of Syracuse Medical School, a respected and dedicated physician, Jack was an Associate Clinical Professor at SUNY, Stony Brook Medical School. As Head of the Department of Colon and Rectal Surgery at Nassau County Medical Center for 35 years, he founded a training program for general surgeons in colon and rectal surgery. Jack's professional affiliations included past Vice President of the American Society of Colon and Rectal Surgeons, President of the Nassau County Medical Society, the Nassau County Surgical Society, the New York Society of Colon & Rectal Surgeons, the Pennsylvania Society of Colon & Rectal Surgeons, and founder and first president of the Northeast Society of Colon and Rectal Surgeons. He was active in the Nassau County Cerebral Palsy Association for 40 years. Reposing at the Beney Funeral Home, Inc. of Syosset, 79 Berry Hill Road. Visiting on Friday 2-5 and 7-10PM. Private cremation with interment to follow at a later date in Syracuse, N.Y. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the American Society for Colon & Rectal Surgeons Research Society, 85 W. Algonquin Ave., Suite 550, Arlington Heights, IL 60005 Att: Stella Seclalis.
