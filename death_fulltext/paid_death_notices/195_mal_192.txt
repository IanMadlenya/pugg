
        MILLER-Robert Myles. 77. Died April 17th after a long courageous battle with emphysema. Robert, born, lived and loved New York, died at home. He leaves his wife of fifty-one years, Selma (nee Gruenstein); son, Robert Myles Jr. and wife Jill Winitzer, daughter Amy Ruth and husband John Klossner; daughter Linda Ellen and husband Mark Minton; grandchildren Jeremy, Aliza, Lane, Chhany, Benjamin and Surya. He founded Lee Myles Advertising Firm. Visiting at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison at 81 St, Saturday April 19, 7-9PM with service Sunday 11:30 AM Burial private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Association for Help of Retarded Children, 200 Park Avenue South, New York, NY 10003.
        MILLER-Robert Myles. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Robert Myles Miller, on April 17, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Sunday, April 20, 11:30 AM, at Campbell's Funeral Home, Madison Avenue and 83rd Street. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        MILLER-Robert Myles. The Board of Directors, staff, participants, and members of AHRC NYC convey their deepest sympathy to Director Selma Miller upon the loss of her husband, Robert Myles Miller. You are in our thoughts and prayers. Genevieve O'Neil, President Michael Goldfarb, Exec. Dir.
        MILLER - Robert. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member, Robert Miller. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Samuel Wasserman, President
