
        KLINE--Henry Chapman, retired Vice President of Grumman Aerospace Corporation (Bethpage), formerly of Smithtown, NY and Stamford, CT, died Thursday, July 10, 2003 in his adopted city of Buffalo, NY. For 52 years Harry was the most devoted husband of Juliet Emma Gentile Kline, the proud father of four daughters, Elizabeth Mullan of Pacific Palisades, CA, Victoria Band of Chapel Hill, NC, Anne Amato of Harrison, NY and Mary Bacon of Buffalo, NY and one son Henry Kline, Jr. of NJ. Dear grandfather of seventeen adoring grandchildren who will miss him dearly. Family will receive friends on Monday July 14, 2003 from 24PM and 7-9PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. Funeral service will be held at 10 AM on Tuesday, July 15, 2003 at St. Francis Episcopal Church, 503 Old Long Ridge Rd., Stamford, CT. Interment will follow Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. >>AD#
