
        GORDON -- Arthur J. 85, passed December 23, 2003, in Arizona. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Muriel; children Andy Gordon and Meg Singer; son-in-law Jeff Singer; grandchildren Debbie and Pam Singer; Zena Bagatelle, his loving companion; and brother Jerry Gordon. Arthur was admired by friends and family alike. He will always be remembered as a humorist, raconteur, athlete and first-rate card magician. Arthur will be missed dearly.
