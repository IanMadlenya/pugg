
        MAILICK--Sidney. Age 80, of Forest Hills, NY, on November 28, 2003, in New York City. Beloved husband of Mildred for 56 years. Devoted father of Daniel and Marsha. Father-in-law of Lydia Read and Gary Seltzer. Cherished grandfather of Beth, Rebecca, Adina and Rachel. Loving brother of Belle and Helen. He was Professor of Public Administration at New York University from 1958 through 1993 and consultant to the Government of Israel, the United Nations and the U.S. Department of State. His passion for Israel and the Jewish people knew no bounds. Services will be held today, 12 noon, Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd at 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to a charity of your choice.
