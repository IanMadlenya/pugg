
        NICHOLS-Joyce N. On March 3 after battling Parkinson's Disease. Retired as Director of Scarsdale Public Library (1982-1992), she lived in Nyack, NY. Survived by her husband Robert J. Trudell, Retired Greenburgh Library Director; Children, Briar DeVito, Tobin Nichols and Kyle Nichols; stepchildren Ann and Robert H. Trudell and three grandchildren. Born in Washington State, Joyce was a descendant of Robert Louis Stevenson. She was a Senior Librarian in Smithtown, L.I. and Nesconset's first library director. Her family, friends and untold readers will miss her humor, resolve and intelligence. Joan and Louis Slovinsky
