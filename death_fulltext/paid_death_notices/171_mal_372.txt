
        RIZK-Khalil. It is with deepest sorrow that the Board of Directors and Officers of Venetian Heritage, Inc. mourn the premature death of their beloved President, Khalil Rizk. Khalil's enthusiasm for restoring beautiful monuments and works of art, his tireless ability to bring us new friends, his imagination & flair in creating wonderful events here and abroad, and his generosity in supporting us financially and morally, have made the deepest possible mark on us personally, on our organization, and indeed on the City of Venice. We offer our deepest sympathy to his family and all his friends who loved him. Countess Cristiana Brandolini, International Chairman Lawrence D. Lovett, Chairman Marchesa Barbara Berlingeri, Marchesa Lesa Marcello, Mrs. Donald K. Miller, Mrs. Lawrence Copley Thaw, Vice Presidents Alexis Gregory, Treasurer
        RIZK-Khalil. With deep sorrow the Officers, Directors and Members of The National Antique & Art Dealers Association of America, note the suddden passing of their esteemed member, Khalil Rizk. Khalil established The Chinese Porcelain Company in 1984. his gracious presence will be missed by all who knew him. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Andrew H. Chait, President Arlie Sulka, Secretary
        RIZK - Khalil. The Officers, Trustees and Staff of the Asia Society express profound sadness over the sudden passing of our beloved friend Khalil Rizk, distinguished patron of our Asian Art Fair benefit previews, Trustee and staunch supporter of our institution. To his family, we extend our deepest sympathy.
