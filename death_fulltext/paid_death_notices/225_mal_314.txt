
        METZGER--Marvin H. On October 28. Devoted and beloved husband, father and grandfather to Maxine his wife, Carol and Robin his daughters, and Sally his precious granddaughter. Loving brother of his sisters Florence and her husband Harry James, Hortense and her husband David Serby and caring uncle to their children and grandchildren. He served with honor as a WWII Navy Veteran who was proud of his country and service to it. Above all his family was his life. Services will be Sunday 11AM at Wellwood Cemetery. For information ''Gutterman's'', 175 N. Long Beach Rd, Rockville Centre.
