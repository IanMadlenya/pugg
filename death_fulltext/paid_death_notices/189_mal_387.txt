
        WELLSTONE-Senator Paul. The American Parkinson Disease Association, Inc. greatly mourns the death of Senator Paul Wellstone, who was a staunch advocate in the fight against Parkinson's disease. His dedication to aid in the struggle of those who suffered with this disease will never be forgotten. We and the Parkinson's community will miss him enormously. Vincent N. Gattullo, President Joel Gerstel, Exec. Director American Parkinson Disease Association, Inc.
