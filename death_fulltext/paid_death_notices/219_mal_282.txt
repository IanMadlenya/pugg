
        RUBIN--Stanley of Scarsdale, NY and Palm Springs, CA. Died March 8, 2005 at age 92. Born September 6, 1912 in Manhattan to Max and Celia (Raphael) Rubin. He was a Textile Broker for Berkshire Hathaway in New York City. Survived by daughters Patricia Watson (Robert) and Ronnie Rubin, granddaughters Samantha Shanken Baker and Allison Cohen and great grandchildren Jake and Lauren Cohen. Predeceased by his wife Edith (Horner) Rubin and a sister Adeline Rubin Silverman. Memorial Service Friday, April 29, 2005 10:30 at The Osborn Home in Rye, NY 914-925-8200. Donations to the Contemporary Arts Council at the Palm Springs Desert Museum, PO Box 2310, Palm Springs, CA 92263, are requested.
        RUBIN--Stanley. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Stanley Rubin, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Charles J. Block, President
