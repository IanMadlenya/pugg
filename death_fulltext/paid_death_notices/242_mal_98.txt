
        HALL--Laurence Bartlett, M.D., aged 87, on March 1st, 2007, in Denver. Pioneer in psychoanalytic medicine and education in Denver. A Mayflower descendant and tireless defender of the revolutionary democracy of his ancestors. Beloved husband of Janet, dear father of Ben and Denise Hall, Maggie and Steve Feinsilver, the late Elizabeth Moe, and Tom Moe. Adored grandfather and great-grandfather. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to the Endowed Chair Drive, denverpsychoanalytic.org.
