
        GROSS-Nelson Gerard, on Wednesday, September 17, 1997, after succumbing to an unprovoked vicious attack by three thugs who inflicted multiple stab wounds to his chest and back. He is the beloved husband of Noel; the loving father of Neil and Dinah; the devoted brother of Michael and Paul and the adoring son of Albert. A private funeral service was celebrated on Friday, September 26. The family asks that in lieu of flowers, donations be made to The Thoroughbred Retirement Foundation, Inc., or The Harness Retirement and Youth Foundation where endowment funds will be established in the memory of Nelson Gross. For information, Gutterman-Musicant 1-800-522-0588.
