
        DICKER-Vivian M. Age 82, on March 4, in her home in Scarsdale, NY. Devoted, loving mother of Owen G. Dicker. Beloved sister of Judge Millard L. Midonick and sister-in-law of Jill Claster Midonick and Milton and Shirley Dicker; widow of Richard Dicker, formerly CEO of the Penn Central Railroad Corporation. Vivian, a loyal Barnard College alumna, was an economist for the OPA during WWII. She then devoted her life to the care of her family, friends and her philanthropy. For all who knew and needed her, Vivian was always there to help. She will be sorely missed. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Richard Dicker Residence of the Post-Graduate Center for Mental Health, 222 E. 86th St, NYC 10028, or to Earth House, East Millstone, NJ 08875. Services will be held on Tuesday, March 13, at 1 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 76th St., NYC.
