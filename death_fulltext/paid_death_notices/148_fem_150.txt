
        LIFSCHITZ-Eva. AMIT is deeply saddened by the passing of the beloved mother of Esther Landsman, our most devoted Associate Chairwoman of the AMIT National Board. A true Woman of Valor, Eva Lifschitz was a lifetime benefactor of our AMIT children and lived a life devoted to Torah, Israel, family and loving kindness, continuing the tradition of her mother Bluma Zisfein. She will be very much missed by all who knew her. Our hearts reach out with consolation to her beloved husband, the respected attorney Hyman Lifschitz, devoted daughters, Sheila (Rabbi David) Halpern, Esther (Dr. Stanley) Landsman, and beloved son, Joseph (Miriam) Lifschitz, and the many loving grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Evelyn Blachor, Nat'l President Marvin Leff, Executive Director
        LIFSCHITZ-Eva Judith. Emunah of America mourns the loss of its devoted benefactor and member of the Yerushalayim Chapter, Eva Judith Lifschitz. To Eva, a most caring and loving woman, Emunah's children and Israel were always a great priority. She will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. Condolences to her beloved husband, Hyman Lifschitz, her children, Rabbi David and Sheila Ruth Halpern, Rabbi Joseph Dov and Miriam Lifschitz, Dr. Stanley and Esther Hannah Landsman, her grandchildren, and great-grandchildren. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld, National President Beverly Segal, Honorary National President Shirley Singer, Executive Vice President
