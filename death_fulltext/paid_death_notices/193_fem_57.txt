
        CLARK-Adele B. (nee Gaudet). Died on January 30, 2003. Devoted mother and grandmother, friend and neighbor. Teacher, volunteer and community activist. She is survived and remembered by her family: her son, Dr. Christopher Osgood, professor at Old Dominion University in Norfolk, VA; her daughter-inlaw, Eileen Osgood, CSW; and her grandson, Nicholas Osgood. She joins her late husband, George Clark.
