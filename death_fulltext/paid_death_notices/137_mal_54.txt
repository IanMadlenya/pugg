
        ABRAMS-Ted E. Died May 31, 1998, at 78 years old in Boca Raton. Formerly of Brookville, LI. Predeceased by daughter Amy. He is survived by his beloved wife of 54 years Adelaide and his loving children Dr. Steven Abrams, Brian Abrams and Susan Schmieder. Adored by his ten grandchildren. Treasured friend to many. He will be mourned and missed by all. Our hearts are broken, but his love will remain with us forever.
