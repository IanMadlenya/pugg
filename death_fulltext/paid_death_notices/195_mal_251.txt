
        LeFRAK-Samuel. The members of the Board of Directors of the Children's Blood Foundation express their condolences to Denise LeFrak Calicchio and John Calicchio upon the death of Mr. Samuel LeFrak. To honor the incredible spirit and many contributions of Samuel LeFrak, the Children's Blood Foundation will establish a fellowship fund in his name under the Division of Pediatric Hematology and Oncology at New York-Presbyterian Hospital.
