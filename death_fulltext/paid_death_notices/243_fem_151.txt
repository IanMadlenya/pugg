
        MEGROZ--Barbara Huntington, of Mamaroneck, died April 22nd, 2007, at age 83. Barbara was born February 15th, 1924, to Majorie and Kenneth Huntington in New Rochelle, NY, a city founded by her French ancestors, the Hugenots. She was also a direct descendant of John and Priscilla Alden, members of the Mayflower Company, on her mother's side. She was educated in New Rochelle and graduated from Colby Junior College. She was a member of the American Yacht Club and had a long involvement with the New Rochelle League for Service. Most recently she was a very active member of the United Methodist Church in Mamaroneck. She loved to garden and was a very talented artist. As an avid sports enthusiast, she was an accomplished ice skater. Bobbie, as she was known to her friends and family, is survived by her husband, Jacques, retired from Time, Inc., three sons, Andy, Chris and Roger, and her daughter Shelley. In addition she also leaves behind five grandchildren. Funeral service will be held today, 1:30pm, Mamaroneck United Methodist Church, on Boston Post Road in Mamaroneck. Contributions can be sent to the American Cancer Society. For information, Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home in Mamaroneck: 914-698-5968.
