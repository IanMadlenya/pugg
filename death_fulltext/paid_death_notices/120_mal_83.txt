
        SUMERS - John. Members of the Board of Directors and the Medical Staff of The Parkway Hospital note with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague, John Sumers. MD, on January 3, 1997. A founding member of The Parkway Hospital, former Chairman of the radiology department, and past President of the Board of Directors, Dr. Sumers will be remembered for his generosity of spirit, compassion to his patients, and wise counsel and integrity to his colleagues. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Molly, his daughters, Nina and Karen, and his son Elliot.  Alan Zeitlin, MD,  Pres. of the Board of Directors  Board of Directors and Medical  Staff of The Parkway Hospital
