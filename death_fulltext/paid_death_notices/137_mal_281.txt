
        MARQUIT-Henry R. The Board of Directors of Herbert G. Birch Services is saddened by the death of Henry R. Marquit on Thursday, June 25, 1998. Henry was a former member and Vice Chairman of the Board, and passionately devoted to our mission. He will be respected and missed always. Henry was on the cutting edge of implementing the Willowbrook Consent Decree. An exquisite mind, a gentle soul, an absolute integrity and a devotion and commitment to social justice were ever present and all pervasive as he, ''Walked Easy on the Earth.''
        MARQUIT-Henry R. Of Setauket, NY, died on Thursday, June 25, 1998 of cancer. He is survived by his loving wife Dee, cherished brother Harold, nieces, nephews and devoted friends.
