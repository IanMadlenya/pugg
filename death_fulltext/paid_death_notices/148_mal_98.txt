
        ANDERSON-Chris (Arturo Fortunato Palma). Died May 7, 1999. Born on November 15th, 1928, to Luigi and Elvira Palma. Theatrical entrepreneur, author, producer, actor and artist. Survived by his wife, Sharon; daughter, Dr. Melody Anderson; sister, Ann Confessore, and numerous nieces and nephews, and a host of friends who loved him and dearly miss him. Visitation Tuesday, 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue (at 81st Street), N.Y.C. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM, at the Funeral Home. Interment Moravian Cemetery, Staten Island, NY.
