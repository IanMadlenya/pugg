
        CLARK--Donald G.C., M.D. Age 85, died November 4, 2005. Survived by his wife of 61 years, Ann Kiersted Clark, by daughter Alison Zorman and by sons Graham, Michael, and Peter. Also survived by eight grandchildren and one great granddaughter. Long-time attending surgeon, Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center; founding director, Mote Marine Laboratory, Sarasota, FL. Memorial service Wednesday, November 9 at Scarsdale Golf Club, Hartsdale, NY, 3:00 P.M. Donations may be made to Mote Marine Laboratory, 1600 Ken Thompson Parkway, Sarasota, FL 34236-1004. Beloved husband, father, grandfather: ''... and flights of angels sing thee to thy rest.''
