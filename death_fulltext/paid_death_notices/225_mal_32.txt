
        REIF--Gary C. Too young, too soon. Loving husband of Tracy and loving father of Henry. Devoted brother of Richard. An inspiration and friend to all who knew him. Graduate of The Bronx High School of Science and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, founder and former CEO of GERS Retail Systems. A proud member of YPO and WPO. He always listened and gave advice to those in need. His intelligence, charm and wit will be a guiding light for his family and friends. His spirit and fight against pancreatic cancer showed us all how to live life to the fullest. A boy from The Bronx who fell in love with San Diego but always remembered his roots. In lieu of flowers please send donations to PanCAN, 2141 Rosecrans Ave., El Segundo, CA 90245. We love you and miss you and you will always be in our hearts and minds.
