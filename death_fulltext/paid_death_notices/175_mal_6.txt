
        ROLLHAUS-Philip Edward, Jr. Passed away peacefully at home in Palm Beach, FL on Saturday, July 28th, after a courageous battle with cancer. Mr. Rollhaus, 66, was the founder and chairman of Quixote Corporation, headquartered in Chicago, whose highway safety products have saved over 40,000 lives and countless serious injuries. Mr. Rollhaus is survived by his loving wife, Barbara, two wonderful children, Natalie (Matthew) Burton of Los Angeles, CA, and Philip E. ''Ned'' Rollhaus III (Catherine) of San Francisco, CA, his loving sister Barbara (Warren) Quimby of Albany, NY, adored granddaughter, Sydney Grace Burton, and several cousins, nieces & nephews. Burial services will be private. A memorial service will take place at The Fourth Presbyterian Church, Michigan Avenue at Delaware Pl in Chicago, IL, on Tuesday, August 7th at 4:00 in the afternoon. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the Shelter Island Friends Meeting, Box 520, Shelter Island, New York 11964.
