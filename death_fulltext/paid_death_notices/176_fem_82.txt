
        SMULL-Mavis Lyons. Of Tamworth, NH, formerly NYC, passed away on September 5, 2001. Mavis dedicated her life to film editing, working on numerous documentary and educational films. She was an active member of the Motion Picture Editors Guild. Beloved wife of William Smull. Devoted mother to Michael Lenauer, stepmother to Tony Smull and Julia Smull, aunt to Barbara and Kippy. Loving grandmother to Serena and Jacob. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Tamworth Community Nurse Association, Tamworth, N.H. 03886.
