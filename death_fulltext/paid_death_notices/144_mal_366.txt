
        GOULD-Joseph L. died on January 21, 1999 at age 95. Mr. Gould was born in London, England and came to this country in 1910. He practiced law until he was 94 years old. He is the beloved father of June B. Naiburg and Arnold J. Gould, and the cherished grandfather of Jennifer and Marnie Naiburg. Graveside services will be at 2PM Sunday at Wellwood Cemetery.
