
        ZESZOTEK--Mark, formerly of Ohio passed away October 10, 2004. A graduate of Miami University, Ohio. He worked for Jim Henson Productions for 20 years as a puppet designer/builder. While there he won 4 Emmy awards. Survivors include his partner Arthur Ansalone, his brother Brett, a niece Abby & sister-in-law Susan; he was predeceased by his parents.
