
        SCHEFFLER--Allan J. April 15, 1941--March 2, 2005. Former town of Bedford resident, deputy town attorney, prosecutor and judge. Loving son of the late Leo and Adele Scheffler, and brother to Mark. Husband to Frances, and Marilyn of blessed memory, father to Eric, Jill and Elizabeth, daughter-inlaw Karen, and grandfather to Samantha and Allison.
        SCHEFFLER--Allan. Members of Shabbat Chavurah mourn the passing of our beloved founder, Allan Scheffler, and extend deepest sympathy to his entire family.
