
        STERN--Charles, 88, of Monroe Township, NJ died peacefully at the home of his son Gary and daughter in-law Amy on Tuesday, April 11. Beloved husband and best friend of Ethel. Also survived by loving daughter Leslie Stern and his adoring grand children, Amanda, Samuel, Elizabeth and Matthew. Graduate of Thomas Jefferson High School, Class of 1933. He was a veteran of WWII serving with the U.S. Army in India. Associated for over 40 years with Emerson Radio Corporation. Services Wednesday April 12, 1:30PM, Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Paramus, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to United Hospice of Rockland, 11 Stokum Lane, New City, NY 10956.
