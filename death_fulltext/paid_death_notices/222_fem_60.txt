
        YANG--Mrs. Von Sung. Widow of Dr. Ho Ching Yang. Died July 9, 2005. She was born in China, April 7, 1902, and died at her apartment in Manhattan with three of her seven children and two grandchildren at her side. Mrs. Yang witnessed the 20th century almost in its entirety and was personally touched by a number of its most visible events and people. Two of Mrs. Yang's children, Gertrude and Stella, preceded her in death. She is survived by Annie, Alvie, John, George and Lucy. Mrs. Yang was a strong woman, never to be forgotten by her large and active family.
