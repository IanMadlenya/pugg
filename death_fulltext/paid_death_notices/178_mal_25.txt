
        ISNER-Jeffrey Michael, M.D. The staff of the Zena and Michael A. Wiener Cardiovascular Institute of the Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of our friend and colleague, Jeffrey Michael Isner, M.D., Professor of Medicine, Tufts Medical School. Dr. Isner was an outstanding clinician and pioneer in cardiovascular gene therapy research. Our profound condolences to his wife Linda and children. Valentin Fuster, M.D., Ph.D. Director Cardiovascular Institute Mount Sinai School of Medicine
