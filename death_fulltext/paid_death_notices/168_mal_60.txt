
        POWERS-Rev. Anthony F. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former parochial vicar of St. Michael's Church, 352 42nd St., Brooklyn on January 3. Reposing in Codis Funeral Home, 63-03 Flushing Avenue, Maspeth, Friday, January 5 from 2-5 & 79PM. Funeral Mass at Transfiguration Church, 64-14 Clinton Ave, Maspeth, Saturday, January 6 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of The Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
