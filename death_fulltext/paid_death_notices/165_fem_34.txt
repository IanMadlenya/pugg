
        BRAVMAN-Celia (nee Kahn). Beloved wife for 67 years of the late Moses, adoring mother of Irving (Rose), treasured Oma to Elizabeth (Jonah), cherished GreatOma to Noah, Sarah, Rebecca, Daniel and Gideon, sister of Rosel Schwarzenberger and Senta Eulau. A truly pious women who died on Rosh HaShana after a very full life; she remains a role model to the three generations she leaves behind.
