
        ANYAEGBUNAM-Dr. Akolisa M. On February 27, 1998. The staff of Jacobi Medical Center and North Central Bronx Hospital deeply mourn the tragic and untimely passing of our Chairman of Obstetrics, Gynecology and Women's Health. As a professional, he will be remembered as a much admired, respected and renowned young physician whose exceptional career spanned 17 outstanding years as a superb clinician, compassionate physician, dynamic teacher, prolific author, and able administrator. On a personal level, we will deeply miss ''Aki's'' warmth, wit and unique charm. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Joseph S. Orlando Senior Vice President North Bronx Healthcare Network Jacobi Medical Center/ North Central Bronx Hospital
        ANYAEGBUNAM-Akolisa. The Council and Fellows of the New York Obstetrical Society record with profound regret the death of their Active Fellow, Dr. Akolisa Anyaegbunam on February 27. David Rosenfeld, MD, President Richard L. Berkowitz, MD, Secretary
