
        WARDWELL-Frederic Koppe. Of Brooklyn, NY, died November 24, 1997. Husband of Frances Jacoby Wardwell (deceased); father of Paul Wardwell. Served in WWII, United States Merchant Service. Graduate of The University of Chicago. He is survived by his son Paul and sisters Dorothy, Kathryn, Marjorie and Laura. Visitation 7-9 PM, Wednesday, November 26, 1997, Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The American Heart Assoc. Interment and services at Oak Grove Cemetery Logan, OH.
