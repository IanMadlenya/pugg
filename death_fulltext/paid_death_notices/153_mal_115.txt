
        GITENSTEIN-Milton P., age 87. Suddenly on October 7, 1999. Son of the late Israel and Rose Gitenstein. Loving and beloved husband of 57 years of Bernice (Narins) Gitenstein. Devoted father of Deborah C. Brudno (David Bernstein), Judith A. Gitenstein, Henry J. Gitenstein (Harriet), and the late Ross George Gitenstein. Proud grandfather of Eric and Maxwell Gitenstein. Caring brother and uncle. A gentle and compassionate man, he was a good friend who touched many lives with his generosity of spirit and his devotion to social causes. He will be greatly missed. Funeral services will be held on Monday, October 11th at 11AM at Community Synagogue of Rye, 200 Forest Ave., Rye, NY. 914-967-6262.
        GITENSTEIN-Milton. Community Synagogue of Rye mourns the loss of their Past President, Mr. Milton Gitenstein, who represented his synagogue with distinction. Our deepest condolences to his wife Bernice and family.
