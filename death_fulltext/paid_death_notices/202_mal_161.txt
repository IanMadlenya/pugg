
        KIRSHENBAUM -- Morris Behr. Died on November 17 in Pittsburgh at the age of 85. A funeral service will be held for him on Wednesday, November 19 at the Ralph Schugar Funeral Home, 5509 Centre Avenue, Pittsburgh at 11AM, followed by burial at Shaare Torah Cemetery. An artist and advertising executive, Mr. Kirshenbaum was the founder and President of the Ivy School of Professional Art in Pittsburgh. He is survived by Joy Fox Kirshenbaum, his wife of 54 years, his brother, Bernard Kirshenbaum, his children Adam, Lyn and Susan Kirshenbaum, Ava Kirshenbaum Doppelt and his sons-in law Jack Ostrofsky and Arthur Doppelt.
