
        ROTHSCHILD--Maurine. Of New York and Dark Harbor, on February 20, at her seasonal home in Palm Beach Gardens. Survived by Robert, husband of 63 years, and children, Katherine Jackson and Peter Rothschild, and grandsons, Oliver, Gregory and Will. Educator, activist promoting rights of women in developing countries, and supporter of visual arts, her leadership has been essential to Equality Now (New York) and The Farnsworth Museum (Rockland, Maine). A memorial is being planned for May or June.
        ROTHSCHILD -- Maurine. Equality Now is deeply saddened by the loss of Maurine Rothschild, a founding member of its Advisory Council. Mrs. Rothschild was a tireless advocate of women's rights. We will establish The Maurine Rothschild Fund.
