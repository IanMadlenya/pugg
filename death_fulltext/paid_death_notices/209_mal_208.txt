
        KNEE--Sidney ''Whitey.'' Of Brooklyn, 1913 to May 2004, at 91. All city high school four years, pro basketball. Loved tennis, skiing, golf, piano and learning. WWII Signal Corps, England, great decoding operation. Lived Japan, France and Germany after war. Predeceased by wife Jeanette of 48 years, 1987. Is deeply mourned by son Joseph of Seattle, daughter Alice Miller and grandchildren Melissa and Jonathan, brother Roy and nephew Michael of Brooklyn, brother Alan of Aventura, FL, and his beloved Henrietta.
