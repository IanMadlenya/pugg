
        STOLIAR--Evan Arthur. Beloved son of Joan and Arthur, loving brother of Lee Dufresne and brother-in-law Leonard, adoring uncle of Abby and trusted friend of many. Skillful webmaster and award winning website designer, lighting technician and stagehand for numerous offBroadway events, member of the Coast Guard Auxiliary, deep water sailor and former Captain of the Shearwater. He provided devoted and indispensable support to Trout in the Classroom and Project Access programs. Evan was an excellent teacher. We are heartbroken at his passing. Burial services will be private. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date. The family will be at home at 46 Jane St, NYC, on Tuesday, July 13, and Wednesday, July 14, from 6-9 PM.
