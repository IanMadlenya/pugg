
        MATTES--Minnie Watt Fite, of Tuxedo Park, NY, wife of the late Edward Charles Mattes, Sr., died April 27, 2007. She was 88. A daughter of Minnie Watt Pierce and Ernest Baxter Fite, of Hamilton, AL, she graduated from the Juilliard School of Music and the University of Alabama. She was married for 23 years to her husband, a private investor, and was a past treasurer of the Garden Club of Orange and Dutchess Counties, NY, and a member of the Tuxedo Club, the Colony Club, the Bathing Corporation of Southampton, the Daughters of the American Revolution, the Daughters of 1812, and the United Daughters of the Confederacy. She is survived by three children, Edward Charles Mattes, Jr., of New York, Laura Laurens Mattes Cox, of Durham, NC, and John Baxter Mattes, of Duxbury, MA; and two grandsons, John Baxter Mattes, Jr., and James Elliott Lindsley Mattes. Contributions can be made to the Tuxedo Park School, the Tuxedo Volunteer Ambulance Corps and St. Elizabeth's Chapel in Eagle Valley, NY.
