
        MEAD-Henrietta Mills. Cherished wife of Robert H. Mead. Beloved mother of Robert, Slade & Winter; grandmother of six small children, and sister of Edward and Slade Mills. A graduate of Westover School and Phi Beta Kappa from Vassar College Class of 1976; member of the faculty of Salisbury School for more than 20 years, and a Commissioner of the State Gaming Commission of the State of Connecticut, appointed by Gov. Lowell Weicker. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Norfolk Library, P.O. Box 605, Norfolk, CT 06058. A memorial service will be held at Norfolk Congregational Church at 1:00 P.M., on Saturday, November 6, 1999.
