
        BROWNE--Morgan T. 84. Sarasota, FL, died January 7, 2004, at Sarasota Memorial Hospital after a brief illness. He was born in Chestertown, MD, and came to Sarasota in 1989 when he retired as President of Bill Communications. He was past chairman of the American Business Press and was on the ethics committee, Washington legal committee, and the business press advisory committee of the Advertising Council. In 1988 he was named McAllister Fellow of the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University. He was a Navy veteran of WWII. Survivors include his wife Ann, a daughter Elizabeth Mallory Kohler of Charlotte, NC, a son Morgan Thomas, of Princeton, NJ, and six grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Friends Service Committee, 1501 Cherry St, Philadelphia, PA 19102.
