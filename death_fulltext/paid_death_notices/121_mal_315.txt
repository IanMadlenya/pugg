
        SAPPERN-Yale W. In New Haven, Ct., Feb 22, 1997. Husband of Pietrina Rizzo Sappern of 7 Melba St., Milford, Ct. Also survived by his mother Sally Sappern of Stamford and brother Donald Sappern of Westport. Funeral Wed 1PM at Celentano Funeral Home, 424 Elm St., New Haven, Ct.
