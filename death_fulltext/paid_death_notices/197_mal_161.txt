
        READE--Richard. Age 78, of Oldfield, NY, on June 8, 2003. Graduate of Cornell University 1949; U.S. Naval Aviator 1942-1945; continued as an active Naval Reservist specializing in helicopters until his retirement as Captain. Engineer in the aviation industry, eventually retiring from Grumman. Predeceased by his wife, Thomasine Hocart Reade. By request, private services. Donations may be made in his name to Naval Aviation Museum Foundation, 1750 Radford Blvd, Suite B, NAS Pensacola, FL 32508. >>AD#
