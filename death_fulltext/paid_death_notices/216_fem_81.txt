
        ROSE--Josephine Grohe. Of Briarcliff Manor, NY, died on January 4, 2005, in Wellesley, MA, at age 94. She was born in The Bronx, NY, on December 6, 1910, the daughter of Josephine and Albert Grohe, and sister of Walter Grohe. She was predeceased by her husband of 50 years, William A. Rose. She was a graduate of Barnard College (1931), where she majored in mathematics. She and her husband Bill were married on July 3, 1937. Josephine leaves three sons, William A. Rose, Jr. and his wife Sandra, of White Plains, NY, Donald W. Rose and his wife Carol, of Mt. Kisco, NY, and Alan D. Rose and his wife Janet, of Wellesley, MA, and a daughter, Nancy Aktas and her husband Gurbuz Aktas of Izmir, Turkey. She also leaves thirteen granchildren, five greatgrandchildren, and two nieces. She was a devoted member of the Scarborough Presbyterian Church, Scarborough, NY. She was also a member of the Ossining Choral Society, the Oratorio Society of NY, Sleepy Hollow Country Club, New York Athletic Club, and was a benefactor of the Metropolitan Opera. She and her family formerly lived in Port Washington, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent in her memory to Scarborough Presbyterian Church, Route 9 and Scarborough Rd, Box 35, Scarborough, NY 10510, where a memorial service will be held on Monday, January 17, 2005, at 12 noon.
