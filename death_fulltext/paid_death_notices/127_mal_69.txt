
        PRAGER-Carl. Of Westport, CT. and New York, N.Y. Died Tuesday, August 5, 1997, at the age of 84. Beloved husband of Ellen. Loving father of Catherine and Michael Devine. Grandfather of Jacqueline and uncle of Lawrence, Eileen and David. A loyal friend and wise counselor. Funeral services will be held at the Willowbrook Cemetery in Westport, CT., on Thursday, August 7, at 1 P.M. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made in his memory to the Make a Wish Foundation.
