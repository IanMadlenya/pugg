
        BERNSTEIN-Abraham A., 93, of Southbury, CT, formerly of NYC. Loving and beloved husband of Belle (Zap). Cherished father of Felice Yager (Henry). Proud grandfather of Alison and Jessica. Devoted brother of Lillian (Steinberg), Fannie (Hardy), and the late Harry and Bernetta (Smith). Affectionate uncle of numerous nieces and nephews. A graduate of City College and Ph.D. from Columbia University. Professor Emeritus, Brooklyn College. Author of short stories, novels and innovative graduate level textbooks. We will miss his warmth, wit, compassion, keen intellect and zest for life. Contributions in his memory may be made to Connecticut Hospice, Branford, CT. 06405.
