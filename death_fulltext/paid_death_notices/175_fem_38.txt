
        KAYE-Violette Wolfgang. Born in Brooklyn, November 2, 1918, died July 30, 2001, in the Jewish Home and Hospital in the Bronx. Beloved mother of Roni Natov & Melanie Kaye/Kantrowitz; grandmother of Jonathan Natov; sister of George Wolfgang. Dancer, self-educated reader, stained glass artist, craftswoman, gourmet cook, and political activist. She will be deeply missed. Donations can be sent to: Women's International League for Peace and Freedom (215563-7110).
