
        EISENBERG-Lillian R. On January 20, 2000 in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late Louis; dear mother of Nancy and Richard Grabow and Susan and Stanley Simon; adored grandmother and great-grandmother. She will be remembered and adored for always striving to be the best she could be. Graveside services today, 2PM, at Mount Carmel Cemetery, Glendale, NY.
