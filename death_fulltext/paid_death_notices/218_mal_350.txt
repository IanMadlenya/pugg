
        WAGNER--Rodney. The Board and staff of the Community Service Society of New York mourn the loss of a friend and generous supporter. His genuine concern for all those in need will be surely missed. Our deepest sympathy goes to his wife, our former Board member, Sukey Wagner and her entire family. Harvey W. Schultz, Chair of the Board of Trustees David R. Jones, Pres & CEO
        WAGNER -- Rodney. The Board of Directors and staff of Iris House deeply mourn the loss of Rodney Wagner on March 24, 2005. Rodney was instrumental in the founding of Iris House and since then has demonstrated unfailing commitment to our work with women with HIV and AIDS. We offer heartfelt condolences to his wife, Sukey, an indispensable member of the Iris House board.
