
        EWALD--William F. 82, whose distinguished journalistic career spanned nearly four decades, including influential positions at five national magazines, among them ''Time'', ''Newsweek'' and ''People'', died December 20 in La Jolla, California. A gifted editor and devoted father, his interests ranged from fine art and serious literature to fine wines and notso-serious baseball. He is survived by his wife, Kristin, and son, Jake, of La Jolla; son, Patrick, of Los Angeles; daughter, Augusta Garrett, of Surrey, England, and two grandchildren. Contributions may be made to the Ewald Scholarship Fund at the Bishop's School, 7607 La Jolla Blvd., La Jolla, CA 92037.
