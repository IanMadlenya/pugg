
        KOPELL-Frieda Ginsberg. Frieda ''Fritzi'' Kopell, a resident of New Rochelle died Monday July 30 at the age of 90. Pre-deceased by her beloved husband Meyer Kopell and sister Alice Kaye. Survived by daughters Linda Levine and Nancy Kopell. Sons-in-law Mark A. Levine and Gabriel Stolzenberg, sister Lois Katz, grandchildren David (Susan) Levine and Robert (Laurie) Levine and one great-grandchild Matthew Levine. Funeral service will be Wednesday 11AM at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, where the family will receive friends from 10 to 11AM. Interment will follow at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, N.J.
