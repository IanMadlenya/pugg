
        HELLER--Richard, Esq. Died on Friday, September 2, 2005. He is survived by his loving wife, Jean Nathan Heller; three children, Bette Heller, Nancy Heller & Evan Krichevsky, Paul & Katharine Burdsall Heller; and four grandchildren, Aliza & Jared Krichevsky, Kyle & Melanie Heller, who are celebrating his long and wonderful life. He was predeceased by a sister, Frances Cahen, and survived by a sister, Phillis Rosenthal. Funeral at Larchmont Temple, Sunday, September 4, at 10 AM. Donations to Anatol & Arthur Charitable Foundation, c/o Dr. Dan Costin, 2 Longview Ave, Ste. 201, White Plains, NY 10601 or Visions, 500 Greenwich St., NY, NY 10013. $(6$)HELLER -- Richard. The Board of Directors and members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with sorrow the passing of our esteemed member of over 50 years, Richard Heller. We send our sincere condolences to his wife, Jean, and the entire family in their time of sorrow. Steven M. Slaven, President Thomas W. Brock, Secretary
