
        CLARKE-Ruth Herzog. Died on February 27, 2001 at the age of 82 at Venice, Florida. She was born in New York City and lived and worked there before moving to Silver Spring, Maryland and Venice, Florida. She graduated from Hunter College and Katherine Gibbs School in New York City, working many years for BristolMyers Squibb Company before taking over her family's chauffeured limousine service, Herzog Cadillac Rental Service in New York City. She retired in 1988. She was an honorary member of the Madison Golf Club, Madison, New Jersey, active member of the Metropolitan Club, New York City, Columbia Country Club, Chevy Chase, Maryland, the Plantation Golf and Country Club and the Yacht Club in Venice, Florida. Survivors include her husband William H. Clarke of Silver Spring, Maryland and Venice, Florida and six step-children. Burial and services will be in Madison, NJ.
