
        RUFF-Carl N., 84. Died on April 7, 1998. He is survived by his wife Edith Jordan Brener Ruff, his daughter Carla Ruff and his son Charles Ruff, his granddaughters Carin Ruff, Christina Wagner and Eliza Strauss, his nephews Clark Ruff and Todd Ruff, and Edith's son, Thomas Brener, and daughter, Jordan Brener.
