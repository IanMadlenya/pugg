
        SCHWARTZ-Hilda G. Retired Supreme Court Justice on Mar. 18, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Herman N. Schwartz. Loving mother of John M. Schwartz. Devoted sister of Edith Smolen and the late Rose Weisman, Dr. Joseph A. Gaines and Mary Princer. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81 St, Saturday 6-9PM. Funeral service Sunday, March 22, 11:30AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65 St.
        SCHWARTZ-Justice Hilda. The Members and our entire personnel express our heartfelt condolences to our esteemed Partner John Schwartz and his entire family, on the passing on March 18, 1998 of John's beloved mother, Hilda Schwartz, retired Supreme Court Justice, a pioneer among women in government and outstanding public servant. Her influence was and will continue as an inspiration to her family, friends and the entire community for many years. Herbert Rubin, Herzfeld & Rubin, P.C.
        SCHWARTZ-Hilda G. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member Hilda G. Schwartz. With her family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of her life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved her. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C.
