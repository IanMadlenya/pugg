
        RETAN-George Walter, 78, on February 1, 1999, of cancer at New York Hospital. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Wood Retan & devoted father of Kathy and Betsy and proud grandfather of Isabel Yao Meyers. Much loved brother of Jeannette R. Jarecke and uncle of Peter, Ann and George. Publisher, editor and author of childrens' books, he had most recently completed a biography of childrens' book author Richard Scarry.
