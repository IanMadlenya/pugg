
        MANDOLFO-Anthony. Died at home November 13, 1998. Devoted husband of the late Dolores (Dori) Mancini Mandolfo. Survived by a beloved sister Tina Shea of Cromwell, CT. Born in Middletown, CT. Tony was educated and worked in Boston. A noted interior designer, he worked at several major architectural firms in NYC, and was a principal in Owens & Mandolfo. Memorial Services will be in Middletown, December 5, 11 AM at St. Sebastian's Church and December 12, 10:30 AM at St. Joseph's Church, 371 Sixth Avenue, NYC. Reception will follow.
