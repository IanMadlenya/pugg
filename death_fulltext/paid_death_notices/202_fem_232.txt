
        HORN--Claire. Of Laguna Hills, CA, and formerly of New Rochelle, NY, died on November 28, 2003, at 88. Adored wife of Melvin Horn, beloved mother of Rita Horn Cassan and Margo Horn, loving sister of Sylvia Anfang and Rabbi Paul Steinberg, and loving aunt and greataunt. We will miss her sweetness, laughter and love.
