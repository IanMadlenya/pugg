
        FREEDMAN--Doris Rosenblum, of Lauderhill, FL, and formerly of West Orange, NJ, beloved wife of Bernard, mother of Steven (Lee) and Grace (Oz), grandmother of Tom, Adam and Amos, sister of Miriam Friedman and the late Leonard Rosenblum and Pauline Gruhin, and dear aunt of many nieces and nephews, died of complications of pneumonia on December 4, 2006 at age 85. Donations in her memory may be directed to Hadassah.
