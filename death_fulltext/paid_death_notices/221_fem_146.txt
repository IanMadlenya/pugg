
        SEGRE--Ann. Passed away on June 11, 2005 at the age of 93 in New York City. Writer, editor, publisher. Best friend and inspiration to all who knew her. Beloved wife of the late novelist Alfredo Segre, caring sister to the late Gertrude, Max, Mathilde, and Helen, doting aunt of Elly Weiss (Lewis Straus) of New York City, William Barnette (Betty) of Albany, Neema Barnette (Reed McCants) of Los Angeles, and Fausta Fano of Milan, Italy. Also survived by seven great nieces and nephews, as well as ten great-great nieces and nephews. Contributions may be made to the Children's Defense Fund or Bide-A-Wee. A memorial service will be held on June 26 at noon at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue.
