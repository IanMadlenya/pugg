
        SUSSMAN--Evelyn D., 89. Of East Meadow, on April 17, 2006. Beloved wife of William Sussman. Devoted mother of Linda Sussman and Alice (Bruce) Geismar. Loving grandmother of Keith (Ruth) and Jennifer Geismar. Great grandmother of Jack Ryan and David Geismar. Dear sister of the late Florence (Harold) Berlin. She will be remembered in the hearts of many friends and former students. Service 12:00 P.M. Sunday, I.J. Morris, 46 Greenwich Street, Hempstead, NY.
