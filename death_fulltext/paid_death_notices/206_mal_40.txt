
        LAWRENCE--William C., 92, formerly of Stuart, FL on March 1, 2004. A graduate ('33) of the first class of Aeronautical Engineering at Georgia Tech, he went on to become Vice President, Engineering at American Airlines and President of Aerospace Lines in Santa Barbara, CA. His wife, Charlyne Oechsner, predeceased him. He is survived by a son, William G. of Winnetka, IL and a daughter, Gail Jaroff of Chappaqua, NY, six grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren.
