
        HOF--Frederic B. Of Staunton, VA, age 86, husband of the late Marjorie (Anderson) Hof, died Thursday, April 6, 2006. He was born June 5, 1919 in NYC, a son of the late Henry and Lillian Hof. A resident of Forest Hills Gardens for many years. The last of his generation, he is survived by his daughters, Lynette Koplitz and Susan Treiber, son, Frederic A. Hof, granddaughters, Kristen, Cori, Katy and Lynette, and great-grandchildren, Lillian, Kathryn and Adam. Contributions in his memory may be made to St. John's United Methodist Church, 1716 North Augusta St., Staunton, VA 24401. Hamrick Funeral Home, Staunton, VA, 800-513-9822.
