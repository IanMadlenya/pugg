
        SATO-Anna Turid, Wednesday, October 15, 1997, Turid Sato of Washington, D.C and Flesberg, Norway. She is survived by her son, Thomas Sato Danforth, her partner, William E. Smith, her mother, Tora Hoimyr, her sister, Solveig Sato, and many other close friends and family in the United States, Norway and many other countries. Memorial service will be held at St. Margaret's Episcopal Church, 1820 Connecticut Avenue., N.W., Sunday, Oct. 19, 1997 at 2 P.M. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Organizing for Development, an International Institute (ODII), for the Turid Sato Trust.
