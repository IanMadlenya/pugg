
        PACTER-Gertrude Geller. 94. Mrs. Pacter, a retired buyer for Associated Merchandising Corporation, passed away on August 13, 1999. She is survived by her sister Rose Drexler, nephews Paul and Richard Pacter and niece Marilyn Geller Block. She was a loving and giving person and all family and friends will greatly miss her. In keeping with her wishes, no service will be held. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
