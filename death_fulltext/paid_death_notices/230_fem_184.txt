
        FEURMAN--Anne. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Anne Feurman, beloved mother of Fran Szczesny, whose leadership and generosity deeply impacts millions of lives in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We extend our deepest sympathies to Fran and her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
