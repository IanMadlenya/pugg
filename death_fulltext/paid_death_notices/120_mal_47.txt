
        TURNURE - Michael de Burbure, on January 2 in Lakeville, CT, at age 60. A graduate of Princeton, 1957, he owned and headed Community Lumber and Hardware, Inc. in Lakeville. He is survived and deeply mourned by his wife Joan; by five children: Lillian Bassett, Virginia Morgan, John, Barbara, and Richard Turnure; by his mother Moarian Wilson Turnure and sister Lisa Oldenburg. Services at St. John's Episcopal Church, Salisbury, CT on Tuesday, January 7 at 4 PM.
