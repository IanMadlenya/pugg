
        HOPE--Dorothea Stater. Age 89. On Sunday, September 14, 2003 in South Nyack, NY. Widow of William Hope, she is survived by her children, Suzanne Hope Graham of South Nyack, Thomas Hope of South Gouldsboro, ME., Margaret Hope of Mechanicsburg, PA., four grandchildren, one great-grandchild, and her sister, Phroso Stater of Cliffside Park, NJ. Graduated from Wellesley, 1935 and received her Sc.D from Johns Hopkins School of Hygiene and Public Health, 1939. Memorial service at Grace Episcopal Church, 130 First Ave., Nyack, NY. on Saturday, September 20th at 11 AM.
