
        LOWE-Jeanette Marmott. With heavy hearts and profound sadness we mourn the loss of Jeanette Marmott Lowe (Beauty), October 8, 2000. Precious mother of Gail Lowe Maidman (Richard), devoted grandmother of Starr Elizabeth Haymes and Evan Adam Haymes, sister of Edith Bloch, Perry Zitter, and the late Ernest Marmott, daughter of the late Louis George and Esther (Blumenthal) Marmott. We will miss our Beauty and the tender, loving, kindness she showed us. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel at 11:15 AM, Wednesday, October 11, 180 West 76th Street (Amsterdam Avenue). Shiva at Gail's home from 4PM to 7 PM, Wednesday, October 11. Continuing Thursday from 11AM to 1 PM and 4 PM to 7 PM. Concluding Friday 11 AM to 4 PM. Contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
