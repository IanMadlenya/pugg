
        ALEXANDRE - Olivia Dulany Wheeler Post. Died quietly at home on Saturday, January 21st. She was born in 1903, was twice widowed and leaves three stepchildren: Nancy Post Magro, George B. Post and L. Jerome Alexandre. A memorial service will be held at St. James Church Saturday, February 17 at 11 A.M. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Church at 865 Madison Avenue, NY, NY, 10021.
