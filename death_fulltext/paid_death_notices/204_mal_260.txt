
        TAFT--Nathaniel B. A lawyer specializing in the regulation of health care and life insurance, died Sunday, January 11 at age 84. A graduate of Harvard Law School (Class of '48), his biography appears in Who's Who in America, Who's Who in the World and Who's Who in American Law. For 54 years, Nat was married to the late Norma Pike Taft and is the father of two sons, Charles of NYC and Stephen of White Plains, and has three teenage grandsons and one granddaughter.
