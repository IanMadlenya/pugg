
        PHILLIPS-John McQuiggan, 64, of Scarsdale, New York, died unexpectedly of a heart attack on January 19, in St. Bartholomew's, Guadeloupe (FWI). He was a graduate of the Phillips Exeter Academy, class of 1951, and of Yale Univ., class of 1955. At Yale he played guard on the varsity football team in the 1952-1954 seasons and was a member of the Wolf's Head Senior Society and Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity. Following graduation he entered the U.S. Army and served for two years as a First Lieutenant in the Tenth Mountain Division based in Aschaffenburg, Germany. Mr. Phillips was a Vice President in the Municipal Bond Dept. of the First Boston Corporation, retiring after 31 years of service in 1989. During his career he was active in the Municipal Bond Club of NY. He was a member of the Yale Club of NYC and the Fox Meadow Tennis Club and was formerly a member of the Downtown Athletic Club. He maintained a residence at Point O' Woods, on Fire Island and served as President of the Club at Point O' Woods. He is survived by his wife of 32 years, Barbara Becker; a daughter, Julia Hawley; a son, James McQuiggan and three brothers, Alfred C., Paul B. and Robert O. For those who wish, contributions may be made to the Church at Point O' Woods, Point O' Woods, New York 11706. Reposing Friday, January 24, at Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave, Scarsdale, N.Y., from 7-9 P.M. Services will be held on Sat., January 25 at 11 A.M., at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, NY.
