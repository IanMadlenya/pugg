
        FELDMAN-Helen G. At age 94. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Harry. Loving mother of Joan Glance and Steven Feldman. Cherished aunt. Services Tuesday 11:45AM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
