
        AVALLONE--Phyllis Jewell. Died peacefully in her home on Saturday, July 12, 2003. She was a long time resident of the Crestwood section of Yonkers, NY. Born in Manhattan in 1929, Phyllis was raised in New Rochelle, NY. She is survived by her devoted husband Mario Avallone, children, Mark, Michael, Martin and Margo. A fifth child, Norma predeceased Phyllis in 1961. Phyllis was the loving grandmother of 11 grandchildren. A 12th grandchild is due in August. A memorial service will be held at Asbury Methodist Church, Crestwood, NY on Tuesday, July 15 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, the family request donations sent to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, NYC 10021 >>AD#
