
        AGRESTA-Hon. Thomas S., Justice, Supreme Court, NYS (Queens Co.), Ret., on March 27, 1999, in his 90th year, of Belle Terre, NY and Vero Beach, FL (formerly of Maspeth, Queens). Beloved husband of the late Anna (nee Zimatore). Devoted father of Adrienne A. Ciafardoni and Thomas S. Agresta, Jr. Grandfather of fourteen and great grandfather of nine. Reposing at Nolan & TaylorHowe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Avenue, Northport, NY. Funeral blessing Thursday, 10AM, at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Centerport. Interment at St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village. In lieu of flowers donations to the Memorial and Honor Program of St. Jude's Children's Research Hospital, PO Box 3704, Memphis, TN 38173-0704 appreciated. Hours Wednesday, 2-5, and 7-9 PM.
