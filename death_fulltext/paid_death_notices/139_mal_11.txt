
        HERBSTMAN-Harold S., Esq. Passed away on July 15. Founder and Director, Dove Theater Company, 1966-1978. Francophile, actor on Yiddish stage, musician, writer, educator and public defender. Survived by his wife Natalie Rogers, daughter Colette, brother Sheldon, sisters Mildred and Florence, many nephews, one niece.
