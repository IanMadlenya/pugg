
        SHATIN--Harry. The Mt. Sinai Medical Center mourns the passing of Harry Shatin, M.D. Dr. Shatin was the first full time Chairman of the Dept of Dermatology at Mt. Sinai. He was a key figure in shaping the residency training programs in dermatology at Mt. Sinai and affiliated institutions. A noted venereologist, Dr. Shatin was admired by colleagues, students and patients. We extend our sincere sympathy to his family and friends. Peter W. May Chairman of the Boards of Trustees Kenneth L. Davis, MD CEO, President and Dean
