
        PORGES-Suzanne F. Died at home in NYC on September 13. Beloved mother of Robert F. and George W. Cherished mother-inlaw of Felicia and Nicole. Grandmother of John A. and Vicki Eisner. Great-grandmother of Dylan Robert Porges. Born in Vienna in 1908 she escaped the Holocaust and immigrated with husband and young children in 1938. She lived for many years in Forest Hills, NY and West Stockbridge, MA, where she had a long, prolific career as a portraitist, still-life and landscape painter. Her strength, courage, humor and adaptability will always be an inspiration to her family. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Friday, September 21, at 5 PM.
