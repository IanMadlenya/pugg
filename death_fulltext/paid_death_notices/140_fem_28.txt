
        WILSON-Jane Wylie. 71. A retired literary agent died at her home in Manhattan on Friday, 4 September. The cause of death was a complication from emphysema. Ms. Wilson was a graduate of Barnard College Class of '49. Her career as an agent began at the William Morris Agency while working for Helen Strauss in the literary department. Jane later joined the late John Cushman in forming John Cushman Associates which became the JCA Literary Agency. She is survived by her brother, Dr. Dwight R. Wilson, Jr. also of Manhattan. A memorial service will be planned for a future date.
