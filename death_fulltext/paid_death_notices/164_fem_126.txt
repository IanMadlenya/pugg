
        EPSTEIN-Ruth nee Pressner, 81. Of West Orange, NJ and Deerfield Beach, FL, on September 8, 2000. Survived by her sister, Bernice Goldin, beloved mother of four children and their spouses, grandmother of 13, and great-grandmother of 11. She proudly served as Chairwoman of New Jersey Council of AMIT Women, National Vice President of AMIT Women & Board member of Stern College for Women. Interment was at Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, New Jersey, Sept. 8th. Shiva will be observed through Thursday morning. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
