
        ALPERSON--Leo, on May 24, 2007. Beloved husband of June, loving father of Ruth, Myra and Philip, father-in-law of Rockwell and Jane, doting grandfather of Jeremy, Sadie and Reva. A sweet, wonderful and caring man, beloved by all, he was born on May 31, 1920 in Omaha, NE. He served in the US Navy in WWII and lived in White Plains, NY. Funeral Services Sunday May 27, 1:30pm at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt Vernon. Donations in his memory to a favorite charity.
