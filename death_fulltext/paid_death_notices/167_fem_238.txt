
        SCIOS-Margaret. Of Haverstraw, N.Y. died Tuesday, December 26, 2000 at Northern Manor Nursing Home. She as 83. Margaret worked in New York City as a CPA for various accounting firms including a personal tax accountant for the late Bernard M. Baruch. She is survived by her brother and sister Donald and Gloria, nieces and nephews. Friends may call Thursday and Friday, 2-4 and 79PM at the T.J. McGowan Sons Funeral Home, Haverstraw. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Saturday, 10AM, Saint Peter's Church.
