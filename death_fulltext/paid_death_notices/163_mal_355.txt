
        JUNGREIS-Theodore, 75. Died on Saturday, August 26, 2000 in New York, of cardiac arrest. He is survived by his loving wife, Loretta, his three children and their spouses, five grandchildren, his sole surviving brother, Irving, his wife's family, numerous nieces and nephews and their children, and his former wife, Madeline, as well as dear friends in Florida and New York, all of whom will sorely miss him.
