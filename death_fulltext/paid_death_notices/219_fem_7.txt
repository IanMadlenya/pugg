
        GROSS--Anna, 100, of Old Greenwich, CT, peacefully March 30. Immigrant from Austria, self-made woman, founder of the Clam Box Restaurants in CT. Survived by her three children, ten grandchildren, 12 great-grandchildren and one great greatgrandchild. A role model for us all, we will miss her but remember her fondly. Donations to Reconstructionist Rabbinic College, 1299 Church Rd., Wyncotte, PA 19095.
