
        STOKVIS--John on July 21, 2004, 88 years, of Port Washington. Born in the Hague, Netherlands. Educated at Bilton Grange, Stowe and Cambridge (UK). His business career included RS Stokvis & Sons, Rotterdam, Whitehall Distributors (NY), and 1951 founding Stokvis Multiton Corp., a LI-based leader in the manufacture of materials handling equipment. Beloved husband for over 60 years of Ivette, fantastic father to Jack and Robert, generous grandfather to Jeffrey, Mark, John, Michael and William, fun-loving father-in-law of Anne and Evelyn. A private memorial service will be held July 23 at 2PM at Fairchild Sons, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to North Shore-LIJ Health System Foundation, 125 Community Drive, Great Neck, NY 11021.
