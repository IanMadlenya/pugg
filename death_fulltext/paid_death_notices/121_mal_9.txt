
        WORTZEL-Murray N. Passed away Jan. 30, 1997 after a heart attack. Cherished brother of Bertha Belmar and Esther Friedfeld. He was a professor of Library Science at Lehman College and founder of Leonard Eisner Memorial Foundation. He devoted his time and efforts to the Special Libraries Association and Kurt Weill Foundation. Services Sunday, 10 AM ''Kirschenbaum's'', 1153 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn. Donations may be made to the Leonard Eisner Memorial Foundation.
        WORTZEL-Murray. The NY Chapter of The Special Libraries Association mourns the death of Murray Wortzel, who worked devotedly for the goals of special librarianship. You inspired us with your dedication to the profession and your commitment to lifelong learning. Your many friends and colleagues will miss you.
