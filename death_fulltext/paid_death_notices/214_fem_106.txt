
        ABELL--Liese L., PhD. At the age of 95. Beloved daughter of the late Paula and Eduard Baerwald, devoted sister of the late H. Fred Baerwald, cherished aunt of Jessica Baerwald Bourgeois, and adored great-aunt of Alexander and Claire Bourgeois. Her decades of groundbreaking work in the field of biochemical research and testing are a continuing inspiration to her loving family, loyal friends, professional colleagues and intellectual heirs. Her steadfast independence and unstinting devotion are a continuing model for all who knew and loved her.
