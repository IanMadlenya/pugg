
        DIAZ-Gloria. On October 25, 1997. Beloved mother of Coco Morris, Ketty Pelaez and Tina Ramirez. Devoted grandmother of four and great-grandmother of three. For over fifty years, Gloria was dedicated to improving the quality of life for New York's Puerto Rican community. Her family will miss her dearly. Friends may call at Walter B. Cooke, 117 W. 72nd St, NYC, Sunday, October 26, 2-9 PM. Funeral Monday, October 27, at 10:30 AM. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery, Bronx, NY. Donations in her memory may be made to The Jewish Home and Hospital for the Aged, care of Liz Friedman, 120 W. 106th St, NYC, 10025-3797.
        DIAZ-Gloria. The Ballet Hispanico Board of Directors is deeply saddened by the death of Gloria Diaz, mother of Artistic Director Tina Ramirez. We extend heartfelt condolences to Tina and all the members of her family. Jody Gottfried Arnhold, Chairman Thomas W. Ostrander, President Ballet Hispanico
        DIAZ-Gloria. The dancers, faculty, accompanists and staff of Ballet Hispanico mourn the passing of Gloria Diaz, beloved mother of Artistic Director Tina Ramirez, and extend deepest sympathy to Tina and her entire family. Verdery Roosevelt, Exec Director Ballet Hispanico
