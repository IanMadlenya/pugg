
        TESSLER-Arthur N., M.D. The faculty, staff and students of NYU Medical Center mourn the passing of our friend and colleague, Arthur N. Tessler, M.D. An outstanding clinician, Dr. Tessler was one of the Medical Center's most accomplished urologists. Throughout his long career as a physician, Dr. Tessler embodied the finest traditions of the medical profession. He will be deeply missed by all who had the privilege to know and work with him. Our sincere condolences to his family. Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean, NYU School of Medicine CEO, NYU Hospitals Center Theresa A. Bischoff, President NYU Hospitals Center Herbert Lepor, M.D., Chairman, Dept. of Urology, NYU School of Medicine
        TESSLER-Arthur N., M.D. The Society of the Alumni of Bellevue Hospital is deeply saddened by the death of our fellow member. He will be greatly missed by us all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Tessler family. C.J. Li, M.D., President Nicholas Tzimas, M.D., VP
