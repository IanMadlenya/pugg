
        BRANDT-RAUF-Oren. The Board of Directors and staff of the New York Association for New Americans, Inc. (NYANA) express its deep sorrow and shock at the tragic death of Oren Jacob Brandt-Rauf, beloved grandson of our longtime Board member, Frances Brandt. We send heartfelt condolences to Frances and her entire family at this difficult time. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Michael Loeb Chairman of the Board Mark Handelman President and CEO
