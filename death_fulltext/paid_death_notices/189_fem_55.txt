
        KORNGOLD-Pola. Of NYC. Passed away September 21, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Israel Korngold. Devoted mother of Manya, Aaron and Marsha. Cherished motherin-law of Barry and Israel. Proud grandmother of Daniel, Lauren, Lisa and Eric. Her zest for life, courage and caring will be deeply missed. In life she was an astute businesswoman and delighted in world travel. As she was a survivor of the Holocaust, the family asks that contributions be made to Simon Wiesenthal Center, Shoah Foundation, or Holocaust Museum, Washington, DC.
