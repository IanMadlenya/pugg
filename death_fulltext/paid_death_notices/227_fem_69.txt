
        LAVORI--Mary E. December 9, 2005 at home in Yarmouthport, MA, surrounded by her five children. Beloved wife of the late Dr. William P. Lavori. Mother of Philip, Nora, Joseph, Angela Lavori and Judith L. Keiser. Grandmother of Liana and Alexander Sterling, Daniel and Cathryn Lavori, William Keiser and Mary Marudas. A woman of unusual accomplishment, optimism and warmth. A gifted teacher, community activist and extraordinary friend. Donations in her memory to Hospice Foundation of Cape Cod, 270 Communication Way, Hyannis, MA 02601.
