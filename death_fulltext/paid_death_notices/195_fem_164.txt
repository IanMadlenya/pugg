
        RUBENFELD - Charlotte (nee Weissman), age 81 of Monsey, New York. She was born in New York and was united in marriage to the late Jack Rubenfeld. She is survived by daughters Marjorie Wetmore and Jayne Rubenfeld-Waldron; son, Stephen; six grandchildren and three great-grandchildren; brother, Lester; dear friend, Edward Hall. Shiva at her home in Monsey, New York, Thursday evening to Wednesday morning. Donations Doctors Without Borders, Hadassah Medical Center.
