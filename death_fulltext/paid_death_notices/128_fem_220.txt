
        FINER-Sylvia. Passed away in Israel on 9/27/97. The devoted daughter of the late Jacob and Frances Arond, beloved wife of the late Rabbi Morris H. Finer, mother and mother-in-law of David and Miriam Finer, Bennett and Paula Finer, and Sharon and Yoel Ben Avraham, adoring grandmother of 11, proud great-grandmother of 4, caring sister of Lionel Arond and Rhoda Hoschander. A woman of strength, dynamism, sweetness and commitment to Israel. She will be missed by all her family and many friends.
