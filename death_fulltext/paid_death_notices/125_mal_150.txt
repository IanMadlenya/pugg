
        DAVID-Rabbi Arthur, on June 11, 1997. Beloved husband of Mildred. Devoted father of Miriam and Jonathan, and father-in-law of Kenneth and Heli. Cherished grandfather of Hannah, Emily and Nora Gray, and of Samuel David. Loving brother of Morton. Senior Chaplain, Nassau County Police Dept. He will be remembered always for his devotion to Judaism, love of learning, and respect for his fellow man. Services Friday 1:30 PM, Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island, 516-295-3100. Contributions in his memory to The Jewish Theological Seminary of America will be appreciated.
