
        SHAPIRO-Leo. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our devoted Trustee Leo Shapiro who with his wife Gertrude were Founders and Benefactors of the Sam and Esther Minskoff Cultural Center and Rabbi Arthur Schneier Park East Day School. To his beloved wife Gertrude, daughter Semoia, son Robert, and the entire family, our deepest sympathy. Services at Park East Synagogue, 163 East 67th Street, on Sunday, November 24, 10:00 A.M. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
