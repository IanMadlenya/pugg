
        EINWOHNER-Seymour Arthur, 81, died August 17, 1999. He is survived by his wife, Joan Einwohner; his daughter, Sara Giulietta Einwohner, of Cold Spring, N.Y.; his sister, Ruth Zavitkovsky, of the Hebrew Home for the Aged, Riverdale, N.Y.; and his brother and sister-in-law, Henry and Helene Warner, of Annapolis, Md. A memorial service will be held at 1:15 PM Friday, August 20, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th Street, Manhattan. Contributions may be made in his name to the Alzheimer's Association, New York City Chapter, 420 Lexington Avenue, Suite 610, New York, N.Y. 10170-0695. The family will be at home from 2:30-6:00 PM Friday, Saturday and Sunday.
