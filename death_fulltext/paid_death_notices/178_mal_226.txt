
        ROUTH-John S., Jr., 81. Of Boca Raton, FL., and Greenwich, CT, passed away on Wednesday, November 14th, 2001. Mr. Routh ''Jack'' was President of the Pittston Coal Company and Founder and Owner of the Manhattan Coal Company. Mr. Routh is survived by his wife, Dorothy, six children, Rylee Routh, Brenda Whitacre, Lorraine Nelson, Isabel Reddy, John S. Routh, III, and Michael R. Routh, and nine grandchildren. Babione Funeral Home, Boca Raton, FL, 561-395-8787.
