
        HSH PRINCE RAINIER III of Monaco. The Trustees and staff of the Princess Grace Foundation--USA are profoundly saddened by the death of HSH Prince Rainier III of Monaco. Through His thoughtful and effective leadership, the Foundation was established in 1982 to carry out the ideals and goals of His beloved wife, Princess Grace of Monaco: to support young, emerging theater, dance, and film artists in America. HSH Prince Rainier's commitment and continued dedication to seeing The Foundation thrive has enabled the support of over 400 artists nationwide. We deeply mourn the loss of our founder for His vision and humanitarian efforts. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the Princely Family and the people of Monaco.
        RAINIER--Prince of Monaco. The members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and longtime friend, Prince Rainier, and send condolences to his family.
