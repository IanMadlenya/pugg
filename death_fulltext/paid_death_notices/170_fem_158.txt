
        ANKER - SIMMONS - Bay. Died March 10, 2001 in Ponte Vedra, FL. Born Fannie Echols in Fort Smith, AK, she was the fourth child of Fannie Johnson Sandels and William Joseph Echols II. She married Graham Wilson Ferguson in 1950. Widow of Ronald Anker-Simmons in 1982. She is survived by her daughter Jane Stuart Ferguson. In lieu of flowers, please send donation to The Julie Gould Fund for Medical Research in Bedford, NY.
