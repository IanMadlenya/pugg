
        SOLNIT-Albert J. The Western New England Psychoanalytic Institute and Society deeply mourn the death of our beloved colleague, Albert J. Solnit, M.D., visionary child psychoanalyst, child psychiatrist, compassionate physician; editor of ''The Psychoanalytic Study of the Child''; and Director Emeritus of the Yale Child Study Center. His energy, kindness, and wise counsel will be missed by his innumerable friends, students, colleagues, and the world's children.
