
        ter KUILE--The Honorable Barbara J. Beloved wife of Curtis R. ter Kuile passed away on Friday, November 5, 2004, at Southampton Hospital, Southampton, NY. She was the devoted mother of Janice K. ter Kuile, Jacqueline A. ter Kuile and Curtis A. ter Kuile, and leaves six wonderful grandchildren. Born February 18, 1929, in Brooklyn, NY, Barbara raised her family in Litchfield, CT, during which time she served as the Representative from Litchfield to the Connecticut State Legislature.
