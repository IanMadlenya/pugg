
        CANTOR--Lou. The Board of Directors of The City College Fund mourns the loss of its distinguished member and former secretary, Lou Cantor. A devoted alumnus of the College and a member of its track team, he never lost his deep feeling for his alma mater and its students. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Stanley H. Kaplan, Chairman Herbert Robinson, Esq, President James L. Schatz, Executive Director
