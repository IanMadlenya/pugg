
        LEVENTMAN-Julius. On Friday, February 28, 2003, of Fair Lawn, NJ. Cherished and beloved father of Leslie Leventman and David Levent. Former husband of Gladys Leventman, grandfather of Evan Levent, dear brother of Herman and Irv Leventman. Mr. Leventman was an Air Force veteran of WWII, serving in the south Pacific, was a sergeant, and served his country for 39 months. He earned his BA degree in Education and his MA degree in Vocational Rehabilitation Administration from New York University. Services 12 noon, Monday, at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4 West), Fair Lawn, NJ.
