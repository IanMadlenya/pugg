
        BARROW - Henny Henriette. Daughter of Leon Ringer of Antwerp, Belgium passed away on January 1, 2002. She fled the Nazi Holocaust escaping through the south of France in 1942, spending the remainder of the war in Cuba, finally entering the United States in 1947. Always interested in teaching English to the foreign born, she headed a program at The New School University to tutor international students in English conversation for the past 20 years. She will be dearly missed by husband Edward & her daughter, Frances Barrow-Hirsch of Tel Aviv. There will be a memorial service at The United Nations Chapel, 1st Avenue at 44th Street on Sunday, January 20th at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to Hadassah.
