
        HECHT-Milton. Born December 18, 1915 in Brooklyn, NY, died at home Sunday, March 16, 2003 after a long illness. His wife of 58 years, Ruth Hecht, predeceased him March 5, 2003. The family lived in Yonkers from 1950 to 1995. They moved to Leisure World in Laguna Woods in 1995. Funeral services are Thursday, March 20, 2003 at 2:00 P.M. at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. He is survived by: Brother Joseph Hecht (Helen), FL; Daughters Goldee Hecht-Meyer (Bill Barton), NY and Lois Hecht Oppenheim, CA; Grandchildren Lewis Meyer (Joetta), KY; Aviva Meyer (Glenn Grasso), NH; Christine Barton, CA; and Lisa Boger (Henry), CA; Amy Oppenheim, CA; and Benjamin Oppenheim (Elizabeth Wells), England; and Great Grandson: Marz Meyer, KY. Family will sit shiva at 16 Robin Lane, Alpine, New Jersey Thursday and Friday and at 3505 Knobhill Drive, Sherman Oaks, CA, Saturday through Tuesday. Contributions may be made to your favorite charity in his honor.
