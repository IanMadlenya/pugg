
        COLLAND-Joe R., 82. On March 28, 2003, in San Francisco, CA. Preceded in death by his first wife of 24 years, Susie Colland, and his second wife of 36 years, Melitta Colland, daughter, Joan CollandCarlson. Survived by his son, Obo Help, his son's partner, Jane Logan, both of San Francisco, his step daughter, Ashera Cinnamon, of Knoxville, TN, and brother, Rolf Cohen, of Amsterdam, Holland. Born Josef Cohen in Amsterdam, came to the U.S. in 1939, served in the U.S. Army during World War II, and spent most of his working life on Wall Street. A small family memorial was held. We dearly miss him.
