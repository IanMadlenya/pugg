
        JOHNSON-Deane F. The Boards of Overseers and Managers and Staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center are deeply saddened by the death of their colleague and friend, Deane F. Johnson. Mr. Johnson was a member of Memorial Sloan-Kettering's Boards from 1982 to 1996. During his long association with the Center, he was actively supportive of the Center and its mission. We have been most grateful for Mr. Johnson's wise counsel, guidance, and generosity. Our heartfelt condolences to his family. James D. Robinson III, Honorary Co-Chairman Laurance S.
