
        WALLACH-Samuel. Left us at the age of 98. Sound of mind and humor. He lived to a maturation of loving wisdom. He assessed the world's troubles with sympathy and hope, and entertained us with high spirits and poetry. We are more for him having been here. Devoted husband to the late Blanche. Father of: Alfred, Richard and Samoa Joan. Grandfather of Pamela, Jennifer, Jodie, Clifford, Joshua and David. Visit at Syosset, Saturday and Sunday; Forest Hills Sunday afternoon. Scarsdale Saturday after 4pm.
