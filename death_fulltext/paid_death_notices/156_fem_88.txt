
        TIETZE-Sarah Lewit. 87, of Davidson, NC, NYC and Washington DC, on January 4, 2000. Editor, wife of late Christopher Tietze. She contributed time to The Population Council, Planned Parenthood and others. Siblings were Lee, Alferd Julian, Rose and Anne. Contributions to Christopher Tietze Fellowship Endowment Fund at The Population Council, NY, NY 10017.
