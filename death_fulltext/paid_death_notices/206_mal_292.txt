
        SOLOMON--Morton. Died March 21st in Sunrise, FL at the age of 84 after a long and valiant battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Sylvia for 52 years. Cherished father of Eileen and Susan. Adored grandfather of Benjamin and Anna, and Melissa and Douglas. Loving father-in-law of Joshua Schwartz and Fred Stockton. Dear brother of Doris Perlman. He worked for over 30 years in the garment industry as a dress salesman and dress manufacturer. He was an active member of the Dress Salesman's Association and of his synagogue, The Marathon Jewish Community Center in Douglaston, NY. In 1983, he retired to Florida. A kind and generous man with a strong devotion to his family, his community and the state of Israel, he will be deeply missed by his family and friends. Contributions can be made in his memory to the Harvard Hillel, Cambridge, MA or to any educational institution of your choice.
