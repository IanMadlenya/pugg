
        HARRIS-Blanche E. Age 92. Died peacefully at William Breman Jewish Home in Atlanta, Georgia on April 17. She is survived by her nephew Harry B. Bernhard of Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, her grand-nephew Dr. Michael H. Bernhard of State College, Pa., her grand-niece, Dr. Nancy E. Bernhard of Somerville, Mass. and grand-nieces and nephews. A resident of Far Rockaway for most of her years, she was a devoted daughter of Barnett and Bessie Harris. The family mourns this gentle soul who lived a blessedly caring life. Interment will be graveside services at Mt. Hebron Cemetery at 11 AM Friday. Contributions in her memory may be made to the William Breman Jewish Home at Atlanta, Georgia.
