
        JONES-Dr. Wallace L. Former CEO of Consortium for Graduate Study in Management, died Dec. 2nd at his home in Chillicothe, Ohio at the age of 63 from a sudden heart attack. Dr. Jones served CGSM for 30 years before retiring in January 1997. He is survived by three brothers, Lloyd D. Jones, Omer A. Jones and Beecher A. Jones; and a host of other family and friends. Funeral services will be held Monday, December 8th, at Zion Baptist Church in Chillicothe. In lieu of flowers donations to the Wallace L. Jones Fellowship Fund can be sent to CGSM, 200 South Hanley Road, Suite 1102, St. Louis, Missouri 63105.
