
        RIBBONS-Douglas W., Ph.D. Loving father and professor of Biochemistry and Microbiology. Passed away October 7, 2002 in Miami, FL. His achievements include nominations for Nobel Prize in Chemistry and over 150 publications in leading scientific journals. His children, Jeanette Johnson, Gary Ribbons, Tamsin Ribbons and Duncan Ribbons, and five grandchildren survive him. For information, call Stanfill Funeral Homes, 305-667-2518.
