
        LAZOFF-Lillian. On March 21, 2002 at age 100. Beloved wife of the late Louis and mother to Rita (Lionel) Sobel and the late Robert. Grandmother to Dory (Tom) and Ted (Mary). Great-grandmother to Adam, Lucy, Julia, Rebecca and Louis. Her life spanned a century of dramatic change and throughout she remained a loving, caring and wonderful mother, grandmother and great-grandmother and friend to us all.
