
        SIKET-James Jr. Of Hampton Bays, Retired Executive Director of the NYC Board of Elections. Died on May 23, 1999 at the age of 84; for 62 years beloved husband of Louise (Antal); Loving father of the late James Michael Siket and survived by his daughter-in-law Debra Siket; Dear brother of Elizabeth Schiffer. Also survived by his granddaughter Lauren Siket, his niece Linda Tenney and her husband James Perrone and his grand-niece Julie Tenney. Religious service at the J. Ronald Scott Funeral Home, Hampton Bays, NY on Tuesday, May 25th at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society would be appreciated.
