
        NORMAN--Dr. Sidney. On April 18, 2005. Loving husband of Joan for 55 years; devoted father of Amy, Susan and David; proud grandfather; and brother of Milton and the late Sonia Goldrick. A dentist for more than half a century, he made a difference to many families in the Brooklyn community where he practiced. He served his country as a captain in the U.S. Army during World War II and as a Democratic activist on Long Island. Graveside service at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, at 11 AM today.
