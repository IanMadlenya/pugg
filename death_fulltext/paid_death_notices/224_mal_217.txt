
        REED--Samuel. The Board of Directors and shareholders of 953 Fifth Avenue Corp mourn the loss of Samuel Reed, a longtime resident and Board member. Mr. Reed passed away on Saturday, September 17, 2005. The Board and shareholders wish to express their sympathy to his family, Annette de La Renta, Beatrice Niven, Eliza Bolen, and Charles Reed. He will be sorely missed by all.
