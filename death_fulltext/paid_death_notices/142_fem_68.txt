
        FAY-Marie McCafferty. A longtime Bedford resident died at her home in Bedford on Saturday, November 7, 1998. She was 68. Mrs. Fay was born September 6, 1930, to William J. McCafferty and Ann Curry McCafferty at Fort Slocum in New Rochelle, NY. She graduated from the Ursuline School and the College of New Rochelle. Mrs. Fay was a self-employed real estate broker and a parishioner of St. Patrick's Church in Bedford. On January 29, 1955, she married Lawrence F. Fay at the Church of the Annunciation in Crestwood, New York. She is survived by her loving husband, Lawrence F. Fay of Bedford, dear mother of Kathleen Fay Tell of Point Pleasant, NJ, Kevin J. Fay of Bedford, NY, Jennifer Fay Rice of Seattle, WA, Brian P. Fay of Bedford, NY. Cherished sister of Patricia Walter of Hilton Head, SC, William J. McCafferty, Jr. of Bronxville, NY, Joan Lahey of Southport, CT. Adored grandmother of Caroline and Elizabeth Tell, Ashley and Lyndsey Fay, and Kayleen Rice. Friends may call at the Oelker & Cox Funeral Home, Inc., 262 Main Street, Mt. Kisco on Monday, from 7 to 9 PM, Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, 10 AM, St. Patrick's Church, Bedford, NY. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
