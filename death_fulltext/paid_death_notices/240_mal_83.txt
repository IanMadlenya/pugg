
        SANDS--Harry, Ph. D. The members of the New York State Psychological Association are saddened by the death of our respected and beloved colleague and Past President (1985-1986), Harry Sands. Harry was an acclaimed psychologist, winner of APA's Gold Medal for Lifetime Achievement, and a friend and counselor to the world. He first joined NYSPA in 1952. Our condolences to Harry's loving wife, Helene, and his family.
