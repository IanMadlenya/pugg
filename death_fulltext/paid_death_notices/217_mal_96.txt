
        URBONT--Dr. Carl. The 92nd Street Y Board and staff mourn the passing of Dr. Carl Urbont, our former Executive Director and Executive Vice President. We extend our deepest sympathies to his beloved wife of 57 years, Genia, his children, Al and Aviva, and grandchildren, Naomi, Julian and David. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Michael Goldstein, President Matthew Bronfman, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
