
        VLACHOS-Alice M. Died on September 29, 2000. Beloved wife and loving mother, she leaves behind her husband of 52 years, James, and her adoring children: her sons, Paul Vlachos and Nicholas Christopher, and her daughter-in-law, Constance Christopher. She was the joy and light of her family and her many friends. Memorial service was held on October 7 at Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church.
