
        BIALOS--Joseph, 79, of Deerfield Beach, FL & formerly of Westbury, NY, on March 14. Beloved husband of the late Edith M. Bialos, who is dearly missed, father of Jeff Bialos & Ellen Fielding, & grandfather of Tyler, Tiffany & Tiara Fielding & Jeffrey Bialos. WW II veteran, salesman, small businessman. His spirit, love of life & family, and zest for politics will be deeply missed & remembered. Memorial Service March 28, George Wash. Manor, Roslyn. 516-938-2330 for info.
