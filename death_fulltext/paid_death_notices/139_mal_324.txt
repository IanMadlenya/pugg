
        DUNDON-Reverend Raymond. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Mary Magdalen Church, Springfield Gardens, on August 28, 1998. Reposing at Saint Vincent Ferrer Rectory, 1603 Brooklyn Avenue, Brooklyn, Sunday, August 30, from 2 to 7 P.M. Mass of Transferral will be held at St. Vincent Ferrer Church on August 30, at 7:30 P.M. Funeral mass at Saint Vincent Ferrer Church, Monday, August 31 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
