
        SHIRK-Stanley E. Former executive who served as a member of the Board of Directors of Bache Securities and many of the Prudential Mutual Funds, died on December 7 at a hospital in Manhattan. He was 83. Mr. Shirk also received an Honorary Doctorate from his alma mater, St.John's University in 1994. Mr. Shirk is survived by his wife Barbara; a daughter, Susan Shirk; a son, Richard Shirk, and two grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Friday, December 17, at 11AM at All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Ave (at 80th St). In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Stanley E. Shirk Scholarship Fund, St.John's University, 8000 Utopia Parkway, Jamaica NY 11439.
