
        SEDER-Robert. The entire Bard College community mourns the death of writer and Bard faculty member Robert Seder. As a teacher, a friend, and a vital and creative member of the Bard community, he will be profoundly missed. We extend our deepest condolences to his spouse, Jean Churchill, professor of dance and chair of Bard's Arts Division, and his children, Nathan and Clare. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman
