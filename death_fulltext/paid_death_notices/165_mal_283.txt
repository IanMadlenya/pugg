
        SHEPPARD-William M. Died on October 10. 2nd Lieutenant at RMA Sandhurst. Ph.D at Brooklyn College, professor for 25 years. Baritone, actor on London stage. Producer, creator, host of TV series ''Inside The Arts'' 1967-76. Producer, narrator of TV documentaries. Taught voice at Yale Drama School. Ink and watercolor artist. Tremendous presence, incredible wit and true compassion were his hallmark, as artist, professor, actor, writer and friend. Survived by Renny, Derek, Robin, and Toby. Memorial November 12th Grace Church, Brooklyn. Call before.
