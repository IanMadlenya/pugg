
        GLASS-Rose. Died July 30, 1997. Age 106. Eldest resident of Menorah Home in Brooklyn. Beloved wife of the late Nathan. Loving mother of David (Renee), Seymour (late Norma), and the late Irving. Beloved grandmother of Bonnie, Daniel (Deborah), Steven, Paula, and Robert and greatgrandmother of Sean, Maxie, and Liam. Emigrated to America from Austria. Longtime resident of Brownsville and East New York, Brooklyn. Her independence and triumph over difficulties were an inspiration to all. A true woman of valor. Our lives were enriched by her many years with us.
