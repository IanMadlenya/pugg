
        HOFFMANN--William J. Jr. Long associated with broadcasting, advertising and musical circles, died on February 3, 2006. He was 91 years old and lived in Larchmont, New York. He is survived by his beloved wife Judith Telep-Ehrlich, son William J. Hoffmann III and daughter Elizabeth Patricia Hoffmann, and George S. Hoffmann, brother, of Winter Harbor, Maine.
