
        GRUEN--Carola S., 96. A resident of Palm Beach, FL., formerly of Riverdale, N.Y., died peacefully December 9, 2003. She is survived by her loving children Louise and Stephen Grayer of Chappaqua, N.Y. and John and Lois Gruen of Ossining, N.Y. She is also survived by four devoted grandchildren and seven great grandchildren. Services were private.
