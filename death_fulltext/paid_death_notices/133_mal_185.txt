
        FRANKEL-Kenneth. Lynne Meadow, Barry Grove and everyone at The Manhattan Theater Club are deeply saddened by the death of our friend and colleague Kenneth Frankel. Ken's artistry graced our stage and he enriched and warmed our lives with his friendship. We miss him dearly. To his wife, our long-time colleague, Donna Isaacson and daughter Hanna we extend our love and deepest condolences.
