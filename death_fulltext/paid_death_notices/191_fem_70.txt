
        SCHWARTZ - Dorothy. Of Rockville Centre. Devoted wife of the late Louis. Beloved mother of Miriam and Oscar Alers, Barbara (deceased) and Harry Mahn, Carol Greenwald of Chevy Chase, and Edward Schwartz of Pasadena. Adored grandmother of nine and greatgrandmother of thirteen. Past President of RVC Elana Chapter of Hadassah. A Hofstra graduate at 49, she reveled in her teaching career in North Bellmore. Her generous spirit enriched all who knew her. May her memory be for a blessing.
