
        RAILING-Lewis Ira. A memorial service for Mr. Lewis Ira Railing, formerly of New York City, will be held on Friday, November 13, 1998, at 10:00 A.M., at the Drake Hotel, 440 Park Avenue. Mr. Railing died September 24, 1998 at the Newport Hospital, in Newport, Rhode Island. Mrs. Dorrie Richardson, sister of Mr. Railing, and Chip, Bill and Andrew Railing, sons of Mr. Railing, their wives, as well as his eight grandchildren invite you to share in honoring Mr. Railing.
