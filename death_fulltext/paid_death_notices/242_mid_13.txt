
        WEBER--Anna, 97, on March 28, 2007. Born October 13, 1909, in NYC to Peter and Gertrude (Auer) Young. She married John Weber on September 8, 1929. He died in 2000. They owned Weber's Bakery on Fordham Road, in the Bronx. Survived by many nieces and nephews and great-nieces and nephews. Funeral 10:30am, Saturday, Edwards - Dowdle Funeral Home, (Saw Mill exit 17). Visiting 2-4 and 7-9pm Friday. Memorials to the Dobbs Ferry Volunteer Ambulance Corp or Police Department.
