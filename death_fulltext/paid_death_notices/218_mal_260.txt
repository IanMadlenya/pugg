
        MITRANI--Aaron. The Board of Directors, staff, alumni and camp families mourn the passing of beloved mentor and friend Aaron Mitrani, retired Executive Director of Berkshire Hills-Emanuel Camps. Aaron's knowledge and innovation in camping were unparalleled. His relationship with the camp spanned over 50 years. Our condolences to his wife Debbie, children Andrea, Carl, Trudy and Sherri, his four grandchildren and family.
