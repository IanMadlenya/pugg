
        SCHUTZER-Seymour, MD. The medical staff of North Shore University Hospital expresses its deep sorrow on the death of its distinguished colleague. A dedicated and compassionate individual, Dr. Schutzer served his patients and his community at the highest level as a personal physician and through his expertise in diabetes. His integrity and desire for excellence always earned for him the utmost respect from his patients and peers. Throughout his 42-year tenure at this institution, he contributed immensely to our clinical and academic programs. We extend our sincere condolences to his family and friends. Lawrence Scherr, MD, MACP Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Peter R. Walker, MD, Chairman, Medical Board Paul E. Karafiol, MD President, Medical Staff North Shore University Hospital
