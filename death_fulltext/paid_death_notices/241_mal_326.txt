
        YUDELL--Allen C., 67, of Boca Raton, formerly of Forest Hills and Roslyn, died on February 21 following a nine-year battle with kidney cancer. His family and friends are deeply saddened by this terrible loss. Beloved husband to Jane for almost 42 years, loving father to Michael and Andrea, father-inlaw to Jacqueline, grandfather to Sophia, brother to Martin, and son of the late Sarah and Julius. Allen's love of family, life, food and negotiating a deal will be greatly missed by those that loved and knew him. Funeral services to celebrate Allen's life to be held Sunday, February 25 at 11:15am at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave., NYC, 212362-6600. In lieu of flowers please consider donations in his name to the Lymphoma Research Foundation, 111 Broadway, 19th Floor, NY, NY 10006 or at www.lymphoma.org
