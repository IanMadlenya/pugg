
        NEMEROV--Arthur. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Arthur Nemerov, beloved father of Wendy Maurer, and father-in-law of Jeffrey Maurer, devoted friends whose dedication strengthens our efforts on behalf of the Jewish community worldwide. We extend our deepest sympathies to Arthur's beloved wife, Shirley; to Wendy and Jeffrey; to Wendy's brother, Jeffrey and his wife, Carol; to the grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
