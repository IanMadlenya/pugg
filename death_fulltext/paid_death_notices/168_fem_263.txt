
        LORENTZ-Elizabeth Meyer. On January 27, 2001, after a long illness. Daughter of the late Eugene and Agnes E. Meyer, wife of the late Pare Lorentz, survived by her sisters, Katharine Graham of Washington, D.C., and Ruth Epstein of Bronxville, New York, and by twelve nieces and nephews and twenty grandnieces and grandnephews and one great-grandniece; also by her late husband's children, Pare Lorentz, Jr., of Frankfort, Kentucky, and Tilly Grey of Eastham, Massachusetts. Interment private. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
