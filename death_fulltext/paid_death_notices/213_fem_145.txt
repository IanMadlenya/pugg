
        LACHER--Phyllis, 84, of New York City, passed away on October 19, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Emanuel Lacher, devoted mother of Elyse and Bruce, loving grandmother of Pamela and Steven. From its founding in 1967, Mrs. Lacher was an intrinsic part of Cy Mann International, a major furniture resource to the interior design market. A well respected collector and connoisseur of modern and antique paper weights, she was a woman of style, grace and dignity. She will be missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral services will be held on Friday, October 22, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street, and Amsterdam Avenue.
