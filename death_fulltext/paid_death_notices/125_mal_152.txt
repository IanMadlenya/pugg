
        HOGAN-Carroll. Former consultant and appraiser of fine art objects for Sotheby's. Died on Friday, May 30, 1997 in Iowa City, Iowa following a short illness. He is survived by two siblings, Beth Flander of Winterset, Iowa and brother Eugene Hogan of MidWay, Arkansas. Memorial to the Ardon Cemetery, care of Beth Flander. 223 East court avenue. Winterset, Iowa 50273.
