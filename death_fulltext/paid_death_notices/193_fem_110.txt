
        ANGLESEY-Zoe. Of Brooklyn, NY, passed away on February 12, 2003 surrounded by her family. Beloved mother, sister, grandmother and comrade. We mourn the loss of a committed writer, activist, teacher, critic and cook. A celebration of Zoe's life with jazz and poetry will be held at Lafayette Avenue Presbyterian Church, 85 South Oxford Street, Brooklyn on Sunday, February 16 at 4 PM.
