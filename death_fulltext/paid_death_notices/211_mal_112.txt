
        GOLUB--Leon. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of Leon Golub, a beloved artist in our permanent collection. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to his wife Nancy Spero, his family and friends. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Robert J. Hurst, President Adam D. Weinberg, Director Trustees & Staff of the Whitney Museum
        GOLUB--Leon. Leon was our friend and neighbor for more than 35 years. We will miss his pragmatism, his optimism, and above all, his large spirit. This is a loss we will never replace. Dinah and Stephen Peter Diana
        GOLUB--Leon. The members of the American Academy of Arts and Letters note with sorrow the death of their esteemed colleague whose passing will be mourned by the world of art.
