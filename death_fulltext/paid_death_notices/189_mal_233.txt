
        GOLDSTEIN - Edward. Of Mission Viejo, CA, formerly of East Meadow, New York, passed away on October 14, 2002 at the age of 89. New York Times employee for thirty-five years, 1963-1998. Survived by his wife Nessa, daughter Karen Berns, son Larry Goldstein, and three grandchildren, Allison, Samantha and Michael.
