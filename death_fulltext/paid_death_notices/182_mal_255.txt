
        IMBERMAN-Jacob. On March 11, 2002. Devoted husband of Arlyn, adored father of Joseph and Diane, and cherished grandfather of Samuel, David, Daniel and Elizabeth. He is survived by his wife, Arlyn, his two children, Joseph Imberman and Diane Albert, a sister, Ruth Taxel and a brother, Milton Imberman. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made either to: The Center for the Study of the Presidency, 1020 19 St, NW-Suite 250, Washington, D.C., 20036 or The Jacob Imberman Fund, U.J.A./Federation of NY, 130 E. 59 St., NY, NY 10022
        IMBERMAN-Jacob. The Center for the Study of the Presidency observes with great sadness the passing of its distinguished Trustee Emeritus, Jacob Imberman. A prominent figure in the legal profession in New York, he was meticulous in his attention to all Center matters within his purview giving invaluable counsel in the crucial areas of personnel and budget. His legal expertise, scholarship and deep personal interest in the American presidency gave him a unique perspective; his substantial contribution to the history of the Center is a permanant legacy.
        IMBERMAN-Jacob. Our members are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Jacob Imberman, husband of our colleague and past-Vice President, Arlyn Imberman. Our deepest sympathy goes out to Arlyn and the entire Imberman family. The National Society for Graphology
