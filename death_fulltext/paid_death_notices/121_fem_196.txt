
        SEXTON-Marjorie McC., January 24, 1997 at the age of 86. Resident of Essex, CT., and formerly of Lakeville, CT. Wife of the late Dorrance Sexton. She is survived by two sons Dorrance Jr. of Sargentville, ME., and David of Rye, New York, and 7 grandchildren. Private services will be held at the convenience of the family.
