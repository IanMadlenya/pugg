
        VOGEL--Dorothy Lapin Reiner. Died at the age of 87 after a short illness at her home in Sag Harbor, NY on September 1. Loving mother of Nora Reiner Gluck and Louise Reiner Baker. Loving grandmother of Benjamin Gluck and Janina Reiner. Dorothy was a poet, tennis player, folk singer, artist, advocate for civil and women's rights and peace activist. A private memorial service will be held at her home in Sag Harbor on October 26. Contributions may be made in her honor to East End Hospice 631-288-8400 and the Women's International League for Peace and Freedom 734761-7967.
