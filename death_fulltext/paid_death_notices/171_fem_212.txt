
        STARK-Jane. The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary Alumni Association & Staff Society regretfully announce the passing of Jane Stark in California this past Thursday, April 19, 2001. Ms. Stark served as the Executive Secretary of both the Postgraduate Institute and Alumni Association for many years. Her dedication and enthusiasm were greatly appreciated. She is survived by daughter Stephanie and two grandchildren. Dr. Jeanne Rosenthal Dr. Douglas F. Buxton
