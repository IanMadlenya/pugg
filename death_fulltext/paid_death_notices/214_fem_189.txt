
        FELDMAN--Estella Menes. A native of Boston and long time resident of NYC, died in Manhattan November 19 in her 101st year. The widow of Louis Feldman, she is survived by their daughter Leda F. Hanson Murray also of NYC. A concert pianist, she worked with music students as an impresario. She was a graduate of the New England Conservatory of Music in Boston. She was treasurer of the Alumni Association of the Manhattan School of Music. She was a lifetime member of Technion and a member of the Park Avenue Synagogue and was buried in Boston.
