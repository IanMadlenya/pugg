
        KOPPELMAN-Ruth. The Board of Directors and Administration of the Gurwin Jewish Senior Residences extend their heartfelt condolences to Charles Koppelman and his family on the passing of his mother, Ruth Koppelman. Charles' devotion to his parents was illustrated most profoundly when he helped position the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center's annual Leonard Ugelow Memorial Gurwin Classic as one of the top ten fund raising events in the nation. May the Koppelman family find comfort in the memory of this special lady. Martin Eglow, President Joseph Gurwin, Chairman of the Board Herbert H. Friedman, Executive Vice President
