
        BLUTH--Irwin M., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center record with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Irwin Bluth, past Director of the Department of Radiology for 40 years. Our sincere condolences to his wife Harriett and the entire Bluth family. Alvin I. Kahn, M.D. Chairman, Board of Trustees David P. Rosen, President and CEO Richard J. Fogler, M.D. Senior Vice Pres and CMO
