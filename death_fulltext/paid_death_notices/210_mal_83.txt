
        SCHNECK--Sidney H. Of Chappaqua, NY, died July 7, from Parkinson's disease. Survived by his three children and their spouses, Susan and Charles Sawyers, Karen and John Reilly, and Allison and Robert Schneck; along with his five grandchildren and sister. Predeceased by his wife, Sally Dene Roos Schneck. A dedicated husband, father and friend, Sidney was a quiet gentle man whose integrity and genuine kindness will always be remembered.
