
        CASTER-Garnet H. (Gary). On Saturday, August 11, 2001, passed away peacefully at St. Michael's Hospital, Toronto, Canada after a long and courageous battle with Alzheimer's disease. He was age 86. Garnet dedicated his life to the pursuit of learning & the education of children and youth. He later expanded that role to include psychiatric social work in the Edmonton Public School System, Alberta, Ca. He tirelessly dedicated his free hours to community service and volunteer efforts to improve the lives of those around him. Lovingly remembered by all the family.
