
        GOODMAN--Louis. Of Riverdale, NY. Born January 6, 1934, died April 18, 2005. Beloved husband of Carole. Devoted father of Jessica and Gabrielle. Loving grandfather of seven. Adored brother of Arlene and Maxine. Uncle and friend. Architect, Building Official of New Rochelle and Code Consultant. Lou will be missed by all who knew him.
