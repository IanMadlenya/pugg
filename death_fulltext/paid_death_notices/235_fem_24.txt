
        FISCH--Edith. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Edith Fisch. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Marcia Waxman, Pres. George Asch, Sec'y
