
        MOSS-Rochelle. The Board of Directors and staff of The ALS Association Greater New York Chapter mourn the passing of Rochelle Moss, the first Executive Director of the national ALS Association and longtime supporter. With the passing of her father to ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease) thirty years ago, Rochelle together with her family worked tirelessly to advance ALS research and patient care. Rochelle will always be remembered for her leadership and commitment to the ALS community. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her children, Amy, Bari and Adam, her sister Madelon and brother-inlaw Larry Rand, co-chair of the Chapter's annual gala benefit. Alan R. Griffith, Chairman John Ernst, Chairman Emeritus Dorine Gordon, President Laurie Novick, Executive Director
        MOSS-Rochelle. Manhattan Chapter BCAA mourns the passing of our beloved founder and president, Rochelle Moss. Our deepest sympathies to her family.
