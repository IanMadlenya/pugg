
        ROSENFELD--Naomi. The Birch Wathen Lenox School mourns the passing of Naomi Rosenfeld. During her years as a parent and trustee of BWL she contributed her skilled leadership to the school while her daughter, Elizabeth, was enrolled. We will remember Mrs. Rosenfeld and her family in our prayers. Frank J. Carnabuci, III Headmaster The Birch Wathen Lenox School New York City
