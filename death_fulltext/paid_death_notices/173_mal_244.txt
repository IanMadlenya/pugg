
        WHITE-Charles S., Rev. CSP. On June 21, 2001, at age 83. Born in Holyoke, MA, he was ordained a Paulist priest on May 1, 1948. Fr. White served as a missionary, preaching and serving in Winchester, TN; Los Angeles, CA; Chicago, IL. His pastoral gifts were realized as a parish priest in Austin, TX; Lubbock, TX; New York, NY; Clemson, SC; and Boston, MA. Twelve of his priestly years were in service to the parishioners at Good Shepherd Parish in New York, seven years at the Paulist Center in Boston, and fifteen of his final years at Saint Paul the Apostle Parish in New York. There will be a wake on Monday, June 25, 2001, from 6 to 9 PM, at Saint Paul the Apostle Rectory, 415 West 59th Street, New York, New York. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10 AM, on Tuesday, June 26, 2001, at Saint Paul's. Burial will be at Mount Paul Cemetery, Oak Ridge, NJ.
