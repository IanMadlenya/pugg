
        NIEWEG--Louis. 92. It is with great sorrow we announce the passing away February 13, in Miami, FL. Formerly of New York, he dedicated his life to philanthropic endeavors to organizations in Miami, New York and Israel. Brother of Robert A. Nieweg and his wife Vera, uncle to Joyce Lesley Nieweg and her children Adam and Haley. Graveside services Monday, February 16, at 1:30 PM, at Shearith Israel Cemetery, Brooklyn.
