
        GEARHART-Robert P., MD. The Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express their sorrow at the passing of Robert P. Gearhart, MD, a distinguished member of the Pulmonary Section for 45 years and its Chief from 1964 to 1968. A distinguished and dedicated physician, he exemplified the highest standards of the medical profession. We honor his memory and extend our deepest sympathies to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO Allen H. Collins, MD, MPH President, Medical Board
