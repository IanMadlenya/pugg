
        CHASE-Anthony C. Died August 20, 2001 in San Francisco, of acute leukemia. Born in New York City, August 29, 1936. Graduated from Yale in 1958, he worked in advertising and insurance in New York, then transferred to San Francisco in 1960. He loved life, filling it with people and adventure-a pilot, race driver, and yachtsman, he created friends worldwide. Survived by a partner of forty years, Karl Keesling; sister, Ann Hendrie, of Little Compton, RI and San Francisco; brother, Martin, of Kentfield, CA; brother, John, of St. Helena, CA; and eight nieces and nephews.
