
        COLLINS-John W. (Jack). On December 2, 2001. In 1991 he was voted into the United States Chess Federation Hall of Fame and, a couple of years later, they awarded him the distinction of ''Chess Teacher of the Century.'' Among his students were Robert (Bobby) Fischer, Robert Byrne, William Lombardy and many other world chess dignitaries. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. on Thursday, 7-9 PM. Funeral service on Friday 11:00 AM. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery, New Windsor, NY.
