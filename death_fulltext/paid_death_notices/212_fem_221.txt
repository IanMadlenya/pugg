
        WHITMAN -- Janet Russell. The Society for the Relief of Women and Children mourns the death of Janet Russell Whitman, who served on our Board for over 50 years. Her dedication, generosity and lively spirit will be deeply missed. Elizabeth Jacks Scott President
        WHITMAN--Janet R. The Board of Directors extends its heartfelt sympathy to the Whitman family on the passing of Janet R. Whitman, our past President. She had been a very active Board member over the past 35 years and will be greatly missed. Stony Wold-Herbert Fund
