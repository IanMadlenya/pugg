
        BRESLER--Alan L. Born in Memphis, a resident of Boca Raton for the past eight years. Alan passed away suddenly on September 9, 2004. His zeal for life took him many places. He was formerly a resident of New York City, London and Beverly Hills. He is survived by his loving wife Jeri Poller and his brother Bob Bresler and his many cherished friends. He was in the financial services industry for over 35 years. ''Gutterman's'', 800-992-9262.
