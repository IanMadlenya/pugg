
        BERNE-Robert M., M.D. It is with profound sorrow that The Mount Sinai Alumni mourns the passing of our distinguished friend and colleague. In 1987, The Mount Sinai Alumni presented its most coveted award, the Jacob Medallion, to Dr. Berne in acknowledgment of his profound professional achievements and his extraordinary service to the Medical Center and the Alumni. A caring and beloved physician, he will be missed. Our sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D., President William G. Stein, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria L. Padilla, M.D., Secretary George Raptis, M.D., Treasurer
