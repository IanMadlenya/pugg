
        GUTHMAN-Pat. The Art & Antique Dealers League of America sadly announces that its member, Pat Guthman (75), died Monday, January 28, at the Connecticut Hospice in Branford, after a long illness. A noted antiques dealer, lecturer and author on the art of hearth cooking, Pat was a founder of the Antiques Council. She is survived by her daughter, Pamela; son, Scott; daughter-in-law, Bernadette; grandchildren, Rachel and Laicey, and sister, Babette Leidner. A memorial service will be held in Westport, CT, on February 16.
