
        FIEDEKE-Donald Kurz. Died October 19, 2001 at the age of 70. Donald started his working history with J. Walter Thompson. He then travelled to Spain and owned and operated three restaurants. Finally returning to the U.S. to become executive chef at Saks Fifth Avenue, Batus & Triangle Industries. He then opened a successful B & B in Brooklyn. For anyone wishing to pay their final respects you can make contributions to the American Cancer Society. Burial was at the Goshen Indiana Cemetery in the Fiedeke family plot.
