
        SARDELL--Dr. Harry, 89, of Great Neck, NY. Died February 18. Beloved husband of Marilyn (nee Glassman). Loving father of Robert Sardell and Susan Weisman. Adoring grandfather of Sarah, Samantha and Julia. Father - in - law of Mary Robertson and Mitch Weisman. Devoted to family and friends. Warm, funny, intelligent and brave. He will be missed by all whose lives he touched. Services Sunday, February 25, 2:30 pm at the ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
