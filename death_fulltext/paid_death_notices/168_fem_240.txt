
        GREEN - Dr. Miriam. The Trustees, Faculty and Students of the Institute for Child, Adolescent & Family Studies mourn the sudden loss of Dr. Miriam (Mimi) Green. A devoted teacher, an inspiring supervisor and a beloved friend, Mimi will be missed by all of us. Our heartfelt sympathy goes to her husband, children and grandchildren in this time of bereavement. Dr. Ava L. Siegler, Director
        GREEN-Dr. Miriam. The Society of the Institute for Child, Adolescent and Family Studies is saddened by the loss of one of its founding members. Dr. Green's colleagues at SICAFS extend our condolences to her husband, children and their families.
