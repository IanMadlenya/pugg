
        APPLETON-Charles W., formerly of Darien, Connecticut, died on August 12 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, where he resided. A long-time Darien resident, he is survived by two sons, Charles W. Appleton, Jr. and Oliver Appleton, II, a sister, Joan G.A. Stone, and several nephews and nieces. Mr. Appleton was active in the finance industry for many years. At one time he was a partner at the firm of Adams & Peck and he also had a seat on the New York Stock Exchange. He was graduated from the Kent School, Kent, Connecticut, and St Lawrence University, Canton, New York. He was an active member of the St. Lawrence University Alumni Association. A memorial service will be held at St. Luke's Episcopal Church in Darien on Wednesday, August 20, at 11:30 a.m. A private burial will be held in Canton, New York on Friday, August 22. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to St. Lawrence University or to a charity of the donor's choice.
