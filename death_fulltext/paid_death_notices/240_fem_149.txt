
        MILLER--Elizabeth W. (Jody), age 82, of New Vernon, NJ, died at home on December 29, 2006. She was predeceased by her loving husband, Hasbrouck B. Miller. She will be deeply missed by her daughters, Kimberly Miller, Stacey Miller, Sloan Simmons and her husband Hardwick; and her son Hasbrouck B. Miller and his wife Donna; as well as her grandchildren, Taylor and Reed Simmons and Fraser, Bailey and Grayson Miller; and her many friends. An adventurous traveler, wonderful cook and woman of elegant taste, she touched the lives of all who knew her. Memorial service on Saturday, January 20, 11:00 at the First Presbyterian Church in New Vernon, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Family Service of Morris County.
