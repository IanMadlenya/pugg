
        STRAUSS--Aaron, 79 years old, adored husband of Diane for 52 years; beloved father of Barry, Scott and Judy; dear father-in-law of Marcia, Karen and Jonathan; loving grandfather to Daniel, Jeremy, Sylvie, Michael, Marty, Allison and Jordan, died on April 8, 2004, after a brief illness. He served as Director of Market Research for Bestfoods. Funeral will be held at Hellman Memorial Chapel, 15 State St., Spring Valley, at 9:30 AM, Sunday, April 11. Shiva will be observed in Suffern, Belle Mead, NJ, Washington, DC, and Ithaca, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the American Cancer Society.
