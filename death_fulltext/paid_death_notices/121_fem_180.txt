
        BLACK-Ethel. Died at 100 years. Wife of the late Witherbee Black, President and Founder of Black, Starr & Frost. Survived by her daughter Ethelette Tucker Ridder, stepson James Gordon Black, grandchildren Eric Ridder, Jr., Susan Lane Ridder, great grandchildren Eric Ridder, III, Catherine Tucker Ridder. Funeral services will be held at St. James Church, 175 Madison Avenue Thursday on February 27, 1997 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Columbia University-EyeEdward S. Harkness Eye Institute to support research in Macular Degeneration.
