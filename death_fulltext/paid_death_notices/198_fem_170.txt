
        MILONE -- Vera Christine (Joeckle). Born on October 3, 1898 in New York City, passed away in Calgary, Alberta, Canada at the age of 104 on July 19, 2003. She is survived by son, Dr. Eugene (Helen) Milone; two grandchildren, Bart (Lynn) and Marie; and four great - grandchildren; with extended family in New England including Ruth Bastin and in Germany. Funeral services will be held at Foster's Garden Chapel, Calgary (403-297-0888) on Sunday, July 27, 2003 at 3PM. >>AD#
