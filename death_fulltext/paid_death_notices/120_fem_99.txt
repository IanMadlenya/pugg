
        MINICK-Lottie. Devoted wife of Irving. Dearly beloved and cherished mother of Sheila Berger and Arlene Minick. Mother-in-law of Sid Berger. Adored grandmother of Felecia and Steve Rozansky and Paula and Jeffrey Berger. Dear great grandmother of Daniel and Eric Rozansky. Loving aunt to many nieces and nephews. She will be remembered always for her abiding love of family and her outgoing kindness and friendliness. Her unique spirit, optimism and great inner strength served as a model to her family and dear friends. She will always be loved and forever missed.
