
        CAREY-Edith S., age 91, of Westhampton Beach, Long Island, passed away Feb. 4, 1997 following an accident in Palm Beach, FL. Beloved wife of the late Thomas I. Carey, D.D.S. She is survived by daughter, Patricia A. Ryan, of Rye, NY, son, Anthony W. Graziano, Jr. of Richmond, IN., and Thomas Carey, Jr, of Milford, CT. Beloved grandchildren: Elizabeth C. Moran, William F. Moran, Robin Virginia Graziano, Anthony W. Graziano III, Michelle, Megan & Michael Carey, and great grandchildren: Megan, Brian, and Colin Moran. A truly extraordinary woman of elegance and grace will be missed forever. The wake will be held on Friday, February 14 from 4-6 P.M., at the Follett & Werner Funeral Home, 60 Mill Rd., Westhampton Beach. The Mass of Christian Burial will be offered at the Immaculate Conception RC Church, Westhampton Beach at 10 A.M., on Saturday, February 15, 1997. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Little Flower Children's Services, Wading River, New York 11792 or the Charity of your choice.
