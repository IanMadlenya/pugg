
        MOCCIO-Carmine. It is with great sadness that we note the passing of a dear friend and colleague, Carmine Moccio, M.D. A respected vascular and thoracic surgeon at North Shore University Hospital for more than 20 years, Dr. Moccio was a compassionate physician who took excellent care of his patients.
