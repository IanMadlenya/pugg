
        PRESSMAN--Larry. On February 22, 2004, of Lynn, MA. Wonderful and caring big brother to his sister Florence Pressman of Lynn, formerly of Manhattan. He was an incredible human being who fought his illness with unbelievable courage. He was a dedicated father who loved and was loved by his son and daughter-in-law, Brian and Leslie Pressman of Charlotte, N.C., his daughter and son-inlaw, Lauren and Kurt Anderson of Peabody, MA. He cherished and spoke with great joy and pride about his four granddaughters, Alyssa and Laura Pressman and Kaitlin and Jennifer Anderson. He leaves dozens of lifelong friends. Funeral Service Tuesday, February 24th, 11:00a.m., Torf Funeral Chapel, 151 Washington Ave, Chelsea. Shiva will be observed at his late residence through Thursday evening. Donations in his memory may be made to American Diabetes Association, 330 Congress St., 6th Floor, Boston, MA. 02210 or The Kidney Foundation, 11 Vanderbilt Ave, Norwood, MA. 02062.
