
        MANNES-Evelyn. The administration and faculty of Mannes College of Music mourn the passing of Mrs. Leopold Mannes, who served on the Board of Trustees from 1979 to 1989 as the widow of a distinguished former President of the College, the son of the founding family. We extend our deepest sympathies to her daughter, Elena. Joel Lester, Dean Robert J. Morgado, Chair, Board of Governors
