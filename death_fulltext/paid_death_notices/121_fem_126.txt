
        SIMON-Elaine, R.N. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with deep sadness the passing of our long-time friend and colleague, Elaine Simon, R.N., a distinguished and highly respected member of our nursing department from 1970 to 1996. An alumna of the Phillips Beth Israel School of Nursing and a long-time board member of the school's alumni association, Elaine will forever be remembered for her outstanding commitment to patient care and education of the nurses of tomorrow. To her husband Al, her children Beth and Eric, the entire Simon family, and all who loved her, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Robert G. Newman, M.D., Pres. Gail F. Donovan, Sr Vice President for Operations Theresa Rejrat, R.N., Acting Vice President for Nursing Rose Muscatine Hauer, R.N., M.A. Dean Emerita, Phillips Beth Israel School of Nursing
