
        CAMPBELL-Alan Lockhart, Jr. Died on Tuesday, August 14 at his home in Falls Village, Connecticut, in his 74th year. He is survived by his sisters Marion C. Strong and Jessie C. Sibert, as well as nephews Edward M. Strong, William C. Strong, William C. Sibert, Jr., Alan C. Sibert, John W. Sibert and niece Jane Colby McManus. A funeral service will be held on Saturday, August 18 at 11AM at Trinity Episcopal Church, 484 Lime Rock Road, Lime Rock, CT. Contributions in Alan's memory may be made to Trinity Episcopal Church, Lakeville, CT 06039 or the Salisbury Visiting Nurse Association, Box 645, Salisbury CT 06068.
