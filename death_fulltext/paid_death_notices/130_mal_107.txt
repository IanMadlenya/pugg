
        DUTTON-Ben Joseph. On November 8, 1997, in New York. Gentle and loving husband of Pauline Kasmedor. Inspirational and devoted father of Deborah and Pamela. Cherished grandfather of Alexandra and Sebastian. Twin brother of Chester. His proud bearing, absolute goodness, generosity of spirit and wise counsel will be, as they have always been, a model to us all. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., on Monday, November 10, from 2:30-8:30. Mass of Christian Burial on Tuesday, November 11, at 10 AM, at St. Thomas More Church, 65 East 89 St., New York City. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memeory may be made to the Calvary Fund, Inc., 1740 Eastchester Rd., Bronx, NY 10461.
