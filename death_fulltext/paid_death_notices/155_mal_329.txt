
        CROHN-Michael Herbert, on Friday, December 24th, 1999 at home. Beloved son of Janet and George. Loving companion to Marian O'Neill. Dear brother to Jeffrey and Kathleen. Special uncle to Nolan and Leah. Michael was a truly remarkable person who touched the lives of many. He fought a courageous battle over a long time against the devastating diseases Dysautonomia and Muscular Dystrophy. Michael will be deeply missed by his friends and co-workers as well as countless cousins and family members. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY, on Monday, December 27th, at 12 noon.
