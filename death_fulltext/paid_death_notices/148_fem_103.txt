
        DICKMAN-Frances. May 11, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Milton, loving mother and mother-in-law of Stephen & Sharon Dickman of Syosset, NY and Lucy & Ronald Wetzel of Knoxville, TN devoted grandmother of Jocelyn Dickman and Paul and Nathan Wetzel. She spent a lifetime devoted to public service, professionally in her dedication to the New York City Public School system, privately by her involvement in many organizations. She had as well a life-long love of literature and the arts, which she enthusiastically shared with family and friends, who celebrate her life and mourn her passing. Memorial service to be arranged.
