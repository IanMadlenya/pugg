
        LINSKEY-Dorothy Marie. Of Stratford, CT. Daughter of the late Patrick and Mary Alice Lynch Flynn, born February 5, 1902 in Brooklyn, NY. She was the Deputy Director of Administration for the Civil Court for the City of New York. She graduated from St. John's University. She leaves a daughter Marie L. Cullen of CT., and a son Edward G. Linskey Jr., of AZ. For information Cody-White Funeral Home, Milford, CT.
