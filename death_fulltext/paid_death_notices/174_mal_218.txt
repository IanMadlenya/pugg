
        ARONOW-Don. Passed away after a courageous battle with cancer on July 19, 2001 on Cape Cod, surrounded by his loving family. Ithaca College graduate and brilliant founder/chief executive of DNA Creative, a dynamic marketing and graphic design firm in New York City. He is the son of the late Les Aronow and is survived by his beloved wife Jill, mother Gail Aronow Kaufer, stepfather Marshall Kaufer, brothers Rob, James and sister-in-law Trish. Services on Sunday, July 22, 1 P.M., Wanamaker and Carlough Funeral Home, 177 Route 59, Suffern, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Hospice of Cape Cod, 923 Route 6A, Yarmouth Port, MA 02675 and/or the Melanoma Research Foundation, 23704-5, El Toro Rd. #
