
        TUTTLE--Ralph N. Age 64, of East Windsor, New Jersey, passed away Monday in John F. Kennedy Medical Center in Edison. Born in Penn Yan, New York, he grew up in Harrison, New Jersey. He was a U.S. Army veteran, having served in Germany as a military policeman. Mr. Tuttle was a previous employee of the Star Ledger and Newark News. He was currently employed as a mailer with The New York Times. Mr. Tuttle was a member of the Harrison American Legion and Elks and the CWA-Communications Workers of America. He is survived by his wife, Thelma Tuttle, his mother, Ruby E. Tuttle, a daughter, Stephanie Tuttle, a son, James Tuttle, six stepchildren, a sister, Isabel Cantlupe, a brother, Kenneth Tuttle, and his 12 grandchildren. Cremation will be private. Relatives and friends may call 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM Friday at Glackin/Saul Funeral Home, 136 Morrison Ave., Hightstown, NJ. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to American Heart Association, 2550 Route 1, North Brunswick, NJ, 08902 or to American Cancer Society, 846 Main Street, Fords, NJ 08863.
