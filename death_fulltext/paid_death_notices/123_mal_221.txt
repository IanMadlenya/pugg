
        BUCKLEY - Michael Conrad. 1963-1997. Died suddenly at home on April 17, of heart failure. Greatly beloved by his two mothers Conrad Paulus and Susan Buckley, his sister Annabel Wildrick and Ben, his brother David Buckley and Kate Simpson, and many friends. Within his hard life Michael found joy to delight all those who knew him. A memorial service will be held April 29, 6:30 PM, at St. Jean Baptiste, 76th St at Lexington Ave. Contributions may be made to the Fresh Air Fund.
