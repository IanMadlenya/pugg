
        COCA-Imogene. (1908-2001). Whose rare artistry, charm and endearing wistfulness have entertained untold millions and left an indelible imprint on all of television. Board of Governors, March 31, 1979. Academy of Television Arts and Sciences. The Imogene Coca Charitable Foundation. A not-for-profit foundation dedicated to supporting human rights, the fair and good treatment of animals and furthering the artistic spirit of the theatre. At Miss Coca's request, there will not be a funeral or memorial service. Donations in her memory may be made to The Imogene Coca Charitable Foundation C/O Price Financial Group, 37 North Avenue, Norwalk, CT. 06851.
