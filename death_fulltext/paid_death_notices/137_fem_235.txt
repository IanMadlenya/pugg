
        PFEFFER-Maxine S. The partners, associates and staff of the firm of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison express profound sorrow at the passing of Maxine S. Pfeffer. Maxine was a former associate and beloved friend who will long be remembered for her many contributions and accomplishments. We extend our deepest sympathies and condolences to her husband, Khalid, her son, Sami, and to all the other members of her family.
