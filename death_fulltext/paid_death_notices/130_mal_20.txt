
        DEGEN-Abraham George. 66, passed away peacefully in his sleep, November 1, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Sandra, loving brother of Ralph Degen and the late Jerry Degen. Loving stepfather of Susan and Brett. Devoted son-in-law, brother-in-law, cousin, uncle and dear friend. President of Degen Fabrics and highly respected member of the textile industry and Garment Center Congregation. A kind and generous man who will be sorely missed. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his name to the Parkinson Foundation at the Columbia Presybterian Medical Center. Funeral will be held at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Tuesday, November 4, at 12:30 PM.
