
        SCHWEITZER--Dr. Daniel Lawrence, passed away peacefully at his home in Scarsdale on the evening of March 14th. He was an eclectic, loving, spirited man who will be greatly missed and who made a lasting impression on all those that knew him. He is survived by his daughter, Dina Schweitzer Leitch, son-in-law, William Leitch, and his two granddaughters, Kaylia and Madeline Leitch, all of San Anselmo, California; his son, David Schweitzer and companion Daisy Luna; his dedicated and caring partner, Susan Rowley, and her two daughters, Betsy and Chrissy Rowley; his brother Richard Schweitzer, sister - in - law, Nancy Schweitzer, and niece and nephew, Marlowe and Jonathan Schweitzer. He is also survived by former wife Carole Wiley Henry. His brother Leonard prede ceased him. Dr. Schweitzer is the son of the late Honorable Mitchell and Sylvia Schweitzer of New York City. Services will be held Tuesday, March 20th at noon at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY 10552. Telephone 914-664-6800. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Jansen Hospice, 69 Main Street, Tuckahoe, NY 10707.
