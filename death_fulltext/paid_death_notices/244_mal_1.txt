
        GARY--Raymond Bedros, Age 78 died peacefully on Saturday, April 28, 2007 after a short illness. He was the beloved husband of Prudence Gibb Gary for 36 years and the adored father of their daughter Amelia Mai Gary. He is also survived by his brother Captain Stanley Platt Gary and his family. B.A. Yale University 1950, M.A. Harvard University Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, 1951. U.S. Navy 1951-1954, Morgan Stanley 1955, Partner 1964, Advisory Director 1983. Raymond was on the Board of Directors of the Third Street Music School Settlement and Stone Oil Corporation. There will be a service held at the Union Chapel, Fishers Island, N.Y. at 12:30pm on Friday, May 4, 2007 and a private family burial. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Third Street Music School Settlement, 235 East 11th Street, New York 10003 would be appreciated by the family. $(6$)GARY--Raymond. The Board of Directors of Park & 76th Street Inc. deeply mourn the passing of our neighbor and former Board Member Raymond Gary. Ray has provided our Board with steady guidance for many years. He was a wonderful person to work with. We will miss his intellect, his commitment to excellence, his sense of humor, and his smile. On behalf of his neighbors in 840 and 830 Park Avenue, we extend our deepest sympathy to Prudence and the family.
