
        BERCOVITCH-Hanna M. The officers, directors, and staff of The Library of America are deeply saddened by the death of their colleague, Hanna M. Bercovitch, who served as editor-in-chief for many years and contributed greatly to the organization's mission. Her devotion to books and to American writing, her learning and enthusiasm, and her commitment to the highest levels of scholarship inspired everyone who came to know her. We extend our condolences to her family and friends. Cheryl Hurley, President Richard Poirier, Chairman, Board of Directors The Library of America
