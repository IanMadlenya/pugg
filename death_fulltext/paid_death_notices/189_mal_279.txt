
        GRUSKIN-Samuel, DDS, 93. On October 20, 2002, born April 24, 1909 in Brooklyn, NY. He surpasses his wife Dorothy Lieberman of 67 years. He graduated from NYU and 1st in his class at Columbia School of Dentistry. He practiced for fifty years. He is survived by his son Dr. Philip Gruskin and wife Antoinette, his daughter Regina Appelbaum and husband Lewis, and his devoted grandchildren, Steven Appelbaum and Susan, Neal Appelbaum and Garth, Phyllis Sienkiewicz and David. Funeral services will be held 1:30 PM, Tuesday, October 22nd from the Neill Funeral Home, Camp Hill. Interment will follow Wednesday in Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York. Contributions may be made to the Jewish Home, 4000 Linglestown Road, Harrisburg, PA 17112. For information Reese Funeral Home, Harrisburg. www.reesefh.com
