
        CAFFREY--Patrick, of Bayonne, NJ, and Venice, FL, former mailer for The New York Times in Carlstadt, New Jersey, passed away on June 3, 2005. He served two tours in Vietnam in the Army 1st Air Cavalry Division. He was one of the most highly decorated soldiers in the Vietnam War, having received the Silver Star, seven Purple Hearts, nine Bronze Stars with Valor, eight U.S. Air Medals, a Vietnam Medal of Honor, and several Commendations. He is survived by his mother, Grace Caffrey, and many aunts, uncles and cousins. He was buried in Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, Queens.
        CAFFREY--Patrick J. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Patrick J. Caffrey, associated with The Times from September 1983 until retiring in October 2004.
