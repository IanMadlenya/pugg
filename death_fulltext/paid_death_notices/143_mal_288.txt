
        CHEW-Sek Jin, M.D., Ph.D., F.R.C.S. The Glaucoma Foundation profoundly regrets announcing the death of Dr. Sek Jin Chew, 39, Professor of Ophthalmology at the Singapore National Eye Center and Deputy Director of the Singapore Eye Research Institute, Singapore. A brilliant ophthalmologist and scientist, Dr. Chew was a key member of the Scientific Advisory Board of The Glaucoma Foundation and played indispensible roles in its research program and Annual Think Tank on Optic Nerve Regeneration and Rescue. He was a pioneer in myopia research and was President of the Myopia International Research Foundation. Professor Chew received his Ph.D. in Neuroscience from Rockefeller University and was the recipient of numerous scientific and humanitarian awards. Despite suffering from brain cancer for the past two years, Dr. Chew maintained his usual vigorous pace of research, teaching, and patient care. He is survived by his wife, Esther. Condolences may be sent c/o of the Glaucoma Foundation, 33 Maiden Lane, New York, NY 10038. Joseph M. La Motta, Chairman Robert Ritch, M.D., Medical Dir. John W. Corwin, Executive Dir.
