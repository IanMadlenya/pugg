
        WILSON-Monsignor John F. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of Holy Name Church, 245 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn, on September 29, 2001. Reposing in M.J. Smith Funeral Home, 248 Prospect Park West, Tuesday October 2nd, from 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral at Holy Name Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass at Holy Name Church, Wednesday, October 3rd at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Reverend Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
