
        ROSE--Harold J., DDS, Dorset, VT, 90, died peacefully at his Miami Beach home on April 9. Graduated 1949 Temple School of Dentistry; member AO and John Kolmer Honorary Society; 25 year practice in Bayside; then 5 years in Manchester Center, VT. With the late Dr. Saul Kaman, he treated the developmentally challenged at Queens General and seniors at LI Jewish Geriatric Center. Leaving his loving wife of 55 years, Mildred, children Steve (Kathy), Martha, John; beloved granddaughter Julia; caring brother, Dr. Murray Rose. Devoted to his family, profession and world peace. Graveside service Sunday April 17, 10AM, Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, LI.
