
        LIECHTUNG-Herbert. The Officers, Employees and Trustees of Atlantic Realty Trust are deeply saddened by the passing of Herbert Leichtung, a former Trustee, and Past President of RPS Realty Trust. We very much valued his wise counsel and guidance, and cherished his friendship. He will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Charlotte, and to his family. Joel Pashcow, Chairman
