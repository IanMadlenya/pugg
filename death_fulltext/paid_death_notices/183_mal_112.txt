
        KOROBKIN-David Bruce. Died April 7, 2002, age 47, in New Canaan, CT, of colon cancer. Beloved husband of Laurie, devoted father of Katie and Benjamin, cherished brother of Barry of Newton, MA, and Nancy of Riverside, CT. A real estate attorney, he was also the owner with his wife of Economy Handicrafts. David had extraordinary courage and tenacity; he lived his life with energy and verve despite three heart transplants over a period of seventeen years. Contributions in David's memory can be made to the Heart Transplant Unit at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center in New York.
