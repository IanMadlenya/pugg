
        HELLER-Jerome S. Died July 19, 2000. A resident of NYC and Sparta, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Louise (nee Annus), devoted brother of Sally Gwirtzman. Also survived by cousins, nieces and nephews. He served in World War II and retired as a Lieutenant Colonel to a private Law practice. Always our ''Rock of Gibraltar'', a rare individual of great intellect, perseverance, decency and kindness.
