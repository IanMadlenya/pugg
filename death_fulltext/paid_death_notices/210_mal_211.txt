
        STAMFORD--John S., passed away on July 21, 2004 after a lengthy illness. A lifelong lover of the music and the sea. Born in Chicago, entered the US Coast Guard and served in the North Atlantic during WW II. Master's Degree from Manhattan School of Music followed his military service and Johh made his New York City Opera debut in 1964. After more than a decade as a principal tenor he began a career in the insurance business and was captain of his own vessel the Gunga Din. We will take comfort that a good man is at peace. Donations may be made to the ALS Association, 2700 1 Agoura Rd, Suite 150, Calabasas Hills, CA. 91301-5104.
