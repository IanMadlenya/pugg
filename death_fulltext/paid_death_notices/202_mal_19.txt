
        BARRETT--Peter. Age 62, on October 29, in New York City, after a long illness. Well known among friends and the New York City press corps as a witty character, avid reader and loyal Red Sox fan. He spent his entire career serving the City of New York. He was most recently employed by the New York City Transit Authority, from which he retired in December, 2002. He will be remembered with great affection by his wife Andrea Green, Bob and Buffy Barrett, and by his many friends and colleagues. Funeral services were private. Contributions in his memory may be sent to Francis's Friends, Inc., 25-02 23rd Ave, Astoria, NY 11105, or your favorite charity.
