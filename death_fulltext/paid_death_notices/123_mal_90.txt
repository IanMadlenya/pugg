
        BRADENBAUGH-Baker A., age 77, died on Tuesday, April 8, 1997. He had been a winter resident of Naples for eighteen years. His home is in New Milford, CT. Mr. Bradenbaugh attended Shadyside Academy in Pittsburgh, PA. He graduated from Princeton University, class of '41. He was a Navy Carrier pilot in W.W. II, and received the Distinguished Flying Cross. He was a corporate officer with U.S. Aviation Underwriters. He is survived by his wife of 37 years, Eleanore. Services will be private and interment will be at Arlington National Cemetery at a later date. For more information contact Hodges Funeral Chapels (941-261-1237).
