
        FARKAS-Benjamin, 79. After a short illness. Devoted and beloved husband to Ellen Tanner Farkas for 52 years. Cherished father of Dr. Paul and Esta Farkas, and Drs. Lynn Farkas Schwartz and Gary Schwartz. Doting grandfather of Melanie and Joshua Farkas, and Allison & Erica Schwartz. Brother of Gertrude Berhang and brotherin-law of Harold and Nicki Tanner. Ben was a graduate of City University of New York, active for his entire career in real estate and a founder of the Community Housing Improvement Board. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society or the American Jewish Committee. Funeral services will be at the Westchester Reform Temple, Center for Jewish Life, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY at 1 PM on Sunday, April 7. Interment will be at the Sharon Gardens Cemetery in Valhalla, New York.
        FARKAS-Benjamin. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its member and extends sympathy to his bereaved family. Amy Lemle, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi
