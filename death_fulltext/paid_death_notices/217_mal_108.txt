
        SHUCHAT--Morris (Red), 90, of Deerfield Beach, FL., formerly of Bayside, N.Y., peacefully on February 4, 2005. Survived by his wife of 65 years, Molly, sons Alan and Jeffrey, daughters Alix and Jacqueline, grandchildren Mark, Jacqueline, Vera, Allison and Jake, and great grandson Harry. He was the heart and soul of our family. Pop had a long, wonderful life and will be missed every day.
