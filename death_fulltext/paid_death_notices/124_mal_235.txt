
        BAUM-Allyn Z., died Saturday May 17, of cancer. He is survived by a family of close friends including Anne Trevor Brown. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, June 3rd at the All Souls Unitarian Church, 80th Street and Lexington Avenue at 3 P.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions to that church in his memory would be greatly appreciated.
        BAUM-Allyn Z. We mourn the loss of our dear fellow member since 1972. He was a fixture in the grill and loyal to the ideals and spirit of our club. He will be sorely missed. The Players
