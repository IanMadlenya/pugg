
        SCHWEITZER--Meyer (Mac). Died peacefully June 10, 2006 at the age of 94 at New York Hospital. Mac left with the wish for peace on earth. World War II Sergeant, USAAC, world-class art dealer and founder of The Schweitzer Gallery. Survived by his beloved wife Frederika, son David, sister Francis, stepdaughter Dr. Adele Mackintosh, her son Gregory and sadly missed by his family in Arizona and friends from around the world.
