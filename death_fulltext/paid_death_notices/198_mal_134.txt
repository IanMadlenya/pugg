
        TEITLER -- Morris. Died peacefully in his home on Sunday, June 13, 2003. Born 1912. Known as ''Morty'', he was a WWII Navy veteran. Morty was a devoted husband to his late wife Fay and loving father of Norman, and Jay and his wife Karla, cherished grandfather of Raquel, Jennifer, Amanda, Joshua and Nicole. Past President of Tova League, U.C.P. A Master Mason for forty years. Funeral Wednesday, July 16th, 10AM, Schwartz Brothers Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. >>AD#
