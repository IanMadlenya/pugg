
        HANFT-Burton. 82, on September 19, 2002, in Boca Raton, FL. A pioneer in entertainment law, he was a vice president of Screen Gems, NBC and Paramount Pictures, and the senior partner of Sargoy, Stein and Hanft. He is mourned by his wife Cora, his sister Joy Collins, his children Steven Hanft and Laura Korobkin, and grandchildren Rachel Tess Korobkin, Robert Korobkin and Sam Hanft.
