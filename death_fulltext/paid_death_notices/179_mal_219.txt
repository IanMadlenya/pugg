
        CLARKE-Francis K. (Frank or Kenny). Of East Meadow on December 15th in his 78th year. (Veteran of World War II A.A.F. member of VFW Post Franklin Sq. and retired pressroom Supervisor at The New York Times). Loving husband of Josephine. Devoted father of Michael (Sandra), Kenneth, and Jeffrey. Cherished grandfather of Michael. Fond brother of John E. and the late Harold, Philip, Clarence, James, Edna, Mary, Katherine and Ruth. Frank is survived by many nieces and nephews. Friends may call Tuesday 2-5 and 7-9:30 PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home Inc., Levittown Chapel, 2786 Hempstead Turnpike (2 blocks east of the Wantagh Parkway). Mass is on Wednesday 10:00 AM at the Holy Family R.C. Church, Hicksville. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury.
