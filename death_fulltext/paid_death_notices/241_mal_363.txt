        $(6$)KOENIGSBERG--Jerry, born August 21st, 1921 died Monday, February 19th, 2007 at his winter home in Key Biscayne, Florida. Jerry triumphed over advanced colon cancer for nine years and after a valiant fight succumbed to other complications. Funeral services will be held Sunday, February 25th, 2007, 11:30 am at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue and 81st Street. He is survived by his dearly loved wife of the past 57 years Ronnie, children Bob, Richard (Rebecca), Bill (Jessica), and Jill (Gary) Blumenfeld, nine grandchildren John, Steven, Jamie, Brian, Scott, Jennifer, Zachary, Megan and Emily, and sister Lillian Trumper. He will be greatly missed on the golf course and tennis courts in Key Biscayne and Lido Beach, and forever remain in the hearts of all who loved him. In lieu of flowers please donate to Memorial Sloan Kettering, specify the memo notation to Dr. Nancy Kemenys Pump Research Fund.
