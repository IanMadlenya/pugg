
        ROTHBAUM-Milton W. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of Milton W. Rothbaum, the first Hadassah Associate in Southern New Jersey and beloved husband of our esteemed colleague Bess Rothbaum, past National Treasurer. May Bess, her children, grandchildren and the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marlene E. Post, Nat'l President
