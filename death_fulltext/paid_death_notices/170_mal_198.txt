
        LEDOUX-Louis Pierre. On March 17, 2001, 88 years. Born August 1, 1912, in Cornwall-on-Hudson, NY. He graduated from Gunnery School in Washington, CT, and Harvard, Class of 1935. He was President of the family company, Ledoux and Co., a metalurgical laboratory. He is survived by his wife of 59 years, Joan F. Ledoux, and his daughter Jeanne-Nicole Ledoux. The funeral will be in Cornwall-on-Hudson; details will be announced.
