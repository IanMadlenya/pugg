
        FIELD-Edward, Sr., 96, passed away August 7, 1997 at his home in Newport Beach, CA. He was born in Rye, New York in Westchester County, on April 29, 1901. He met and married Emogene Lewis, his wife of 61 years, in Olathe, Kansas, in October of 1935. He was formerly the president of Childs Restaurant chain, based in Manhattan, during the 1930's and 40's. In 1958, after moving to California, he cofounded the Denny's Restaurant chain, formerly Danny's Donuts of Anaheim, CA and remained actively involved until its sale in 1978. He is survived by his wife, Emogene, son, Dr. Edward C. Field, Jr., granddaughters, Holly Field and Kimberly Light Knight, greatgrandsons, John Benjamin Knight IV and Clifton James Robert Knight. Funeral services were held Saturday, August 9, 1997 at Pacific View Mortuary, 3500 Pacific View Drive, Newport Beach, CA. Following cremation, inurnment will take place on Wednesday, August 13 at 3:00 p.m. at Pacific View Memorial Park. Donations in Edward's memory may be made to St. Andrew's Presbyterian Church, 600 St. Andrews Road, Newport Beach, CA 92663.
