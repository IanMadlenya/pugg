
        MANDEL--Stephan Marton. On May 15, of lung cancer. Beloved husband of Heidi S. Fiske. Beloved brother of Linda E. Mandel and beloved brother - in - law of Pierre Clerk. At his death at age 67, President of the Columbia County Historical Society; for many years President of Summer Stores Corp., a chain of family apparel stores. A graduate of Yale College, he attended Harvard Law School. He loved so many things, notably opera, gardening, art and, above all, his family and friends. The family will receive 5-7 pm on May 18 and 19. Friends should call for location. A memorial service will be held at a later date. If you wish, please send memorial contributions to the Columbia County Historical Society, PO Box 311, Kinderhook, NY 12106; or to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, attn: Dr. Vincent Miller, 1275 York Ave, NYC 10021.
        MANDEL--Stephan Marton. The Columbia County Historical Society, Board of Directors, and Staff sadly mourn the loss of our inspired President who devoted his intellect and passion to our Society. His strong and energetic leadership will be sorely missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Heidi, and family. Sharon S. Palmer, Executive Director
