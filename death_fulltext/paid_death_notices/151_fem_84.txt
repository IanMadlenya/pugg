
        SHRIFTE-Evelyn. On August 8. Sister of Bernice Woll, the late Ruth Appel, Estelle and Herbert Shrifte. Sister-in-law of Milton Woll. She will be missed also by her nieces and nephews and their children. Evelyn Shrifte was, for many years, the President of the Vanguard Press, the publisher of many important works of fiction and non-fiction until acquired by Random House in 1986. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
