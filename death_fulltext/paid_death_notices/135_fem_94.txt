
        EGAN-Sr. Jogues, RSHM. Tarrytown, NY, Monday, April 13. Sister Jogues was a teacher at Marymount Academy, Tarrytown, N.Y., Academy of the SHM Park Terrace Manhattan, Marymount School Los Angeles, CA. Assistant Professor and Academic Dean of Marymount College, Tarrytown, President of Marymount College, Boca Raton, FL., President of Marymount Manhattan College, Provincial Superior of the Religious of the Sacred Heart of Mary. Sister joined the faculty of Empire State College in 1972 where she served for over 20 years, retiring with the title of Professor Emeritus. Visiting Wednesday, April 15 at Marymount Convent, 32 Wilson Park Drive, Tarrytown from 3-7:30 PM. Vigil service 7:30 PM. Mass of Christian burial will be held at the Marymount Convent, on Thursday at 10:30 AM. Interment Mt. Calvary cemetery, White Plains. In lieu of flowers, gifts in her name may be made to the Religious of the Sacred Heart of Mary, 50 Wilson Park Drive, Tarrytown, or to Marymount College, Tarrytown or to Marymount Manhattan College, 221 East 71 Street, New York City. For information call the Dwyer Funeral Home, 914-631-0621.
