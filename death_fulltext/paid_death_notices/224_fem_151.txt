
        ANTELMAN--Joan Pauer, 71. Died September 21. Survived by son Bruce (Maria), daughter Ilene, and brother Stanley Pauer (Edith). Resident of Hallandale Beach, FL. She was born in Brooklyn and attended Bryn Mawr and Brooklyn colleges. A homemaker in Clifton, N.J., and a bridge Grand Master, she then became a computer programmer and a teacher of bridge. Services Friday, 12:45PM at Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 20955 Biscayne Blvd, Aventura, FL.
