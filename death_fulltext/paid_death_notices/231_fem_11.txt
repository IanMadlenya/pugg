
        SCHLAFFER--Helen. Attorney, born in Oshawa, Canada, died on March 26, 2006. Wife of the late Alex Schlaffer. Daughter of the Reverend Vladamir Kupchynski and Theodora. She practiced law with her sister Olga Kupchin, also an attorney, among the Ukranian immigrants who came to the US after WWII. After 40 years of law practice she devoted her life to church work at the Church of the Covenant. She is survived by her sister Olga Kupchin and two nieces, Mary Pohli and Joan Mahony. A memorial will be held at the Church of the Covenant on East 42nd St. on Thursday, April 6, at 11AM.
