
        KOO-Teh-Chang. Age 82. Died on July 9 in Kissimmee, FL. He was the eldest son of Dr. V.K. Wellington Koo. As a Lieutenant Colonel in the Chinese Air Force, he served with the Flying Tigers under General Chennault. He founded Dirksen, Inc., an international trading company in Taiwan. He is survived by his loving wife of 30 years, Joanna; three sons, David, Norman and Jimmy; three daughters, Kathy, Linda and Irene; six grandchildren; and his sister, Patricia Koo Tsien. Funeral service will be held in Orlando, FL, July 18.
