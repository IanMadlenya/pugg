
        POTTER--Lester. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Howard A. Stecker, Senior Rabbi Hon. Richard Kestenbaum, President $(6$)POTTER--Lester, 93, died peacefully at his home in Great Neck, NY on March 29. A wonderful man who achieved success in life, both personal and professional. Lester was a loving, and beloved husband, father, grandfather and brother who grew up wild on the streets of Coney Island to live the American dream. Graduating in the first class of Lincoln High School during the Depression, he went on to open his first automobile dealership on Surf Ave. After four years in the Air Force during WWII, he established Pepper and Potter, Inc. in downtown Brooklyn, a successful Buick dealership for almost 50 years. He leaves his beloved wife of 59 years, Annette, daughters Bonnie and Tina (Andrew Freireich) and son Robert (Mirjam), grandchildren Alix Freireich, and Anya and Nina Potter. He is also survived by a brother, Theodore Potter, three step-grandchildren and many nieces and nephews. Dad, we will love and miss you always. Services Sunday, 11:30 am at Riverside-Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, Long Island, 3281 Veterans Memorial Highway, Ronkonkoma, NY 11779.
