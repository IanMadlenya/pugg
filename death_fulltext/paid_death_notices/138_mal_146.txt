
        SCHUCK-George, Jr. The Officers, Board of Directors and Members of the Association of Electrical Contractors, Inc, mourn the loss of George Schuck, Jr, chairman of the Joint Industry Board of Affiliation Electrical Contractors and Local No. 3, IBEW. He was a dedicated representative of our industry and will be sorely missed. We wish to extend our condolences to his wife, Jeanie, and their family.
