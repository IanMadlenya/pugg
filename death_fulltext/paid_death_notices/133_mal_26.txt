        SCHAPIRA-Dr. Hans. The Department of Urology at Mt. Sinai Medical Center mourns with deep sadness the passing of Dr. Hans Schapira, Clinical Professor of Urology. Inspiring teacher, compassionate physician and caring friend, he touched and influenced the lives of countless patients, colleagues and students through his knowledge, his wisdom, his selflessness, and his old-world grace. We extend our condolences to his wife Ruth and his sons Ralph and Paul and their families. He will be sorely missed, but always remembered. Michael J. Droller, M.D., Chairman, Department of Urology
