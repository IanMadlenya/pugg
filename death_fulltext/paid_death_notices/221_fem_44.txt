
        COHEN--Florence. 99, June 3, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Murray. Caring, loving mother of Arnold, Howard and George. Mother-in-law of Barbara, the late Lynda, and Ellen Cohen. Grandmother of David, Richard, Shari, Lisa and Matthew. Great-grandmother of eight. She took two boys, aged seven and four, and made them her own. Without her love they might not have succeeded in life. We will forever be grateful. Burial graveside in Hollywood, FL. Donations to the Alzheimer's Association of New York, 360 Lexington Ave., 5th Flr., New York, NY 10017, or the Boca Raton Community Hospital, would be appreciated.
