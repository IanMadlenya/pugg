
        AUERBACH-Alice C. Wife of the late William H. Auerbach. Survived by her sister Madeleine Marx, aunt and greataunt, dearly loved, passed away on March 15, 2003, at the age of 100. Daughter of the late Olga C. Warburg and Paul Kohn Speyer. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., 9 AM, Tuesday, March 18, 2003.
