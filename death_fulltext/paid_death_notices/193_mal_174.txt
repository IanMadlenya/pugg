
        COLIN-Samuel. Age 76. On February 12, 2003 after a valiant battle surrounded by his family. Devoted father of Amy and Andy Brief and Leslie and J.J. Rosen. Dear stepfather of Mark and Caryn Israel and the late Richard Israel. Beloved fiancee of Debby Israel. Cherished grandfather of Rachel, Jacob, Matthew, Brandon and Lily. He was an inspiration and a hero to all who knew and loved him and he will be in our hearts forever. Services were held. Contributions may be made to the National Kidney Foundation 30 E. 33rd St. NYC 10016 or Food Bank of NYC, Food for Survival, 355 Food Center Drive, Bronx, NY 10475 ATT: Accounting Dept.
