
        KRAMER-Darryl J. Born May 20, 1947, died July 27, 2002. Beloved husband of Engie Lee Kramer. Cherished father of Courtney Kramer, Vanessa Kramer and Kane Lee. Dear son of Shirley Kramer. Dear brother of Ted Kramer. He was best friend and father and brother to all who knew him, a brilliant attorney and business advisor, gifted with wisdom and humor, and an enthusiastic naturalist. He was a perennial diplomat. His good works are remembered by all he touched. A great soul, he lives in our hearts forever. Services at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue (81st St), Monday, July 29, at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Research.
