
        KLOTZ--Patricia Hopkins. A lady who brought beauty and kindness into the lives of many died peacefully on May 30, 2004 at her home in McLean, VA. Beloved mother of Radford and Leslie of New York City and Taylor of Arlington, VA. Proud grandmother of Herbert Klotz and Marcus Klotz of New York City. Devoted wife of Herbert Klotz who predeceased her. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM Thursday, June 3, at the Bethlehem Chapel, Washington National Cathedral, Washington, DC. Donations in lieu of flowers may be sent to Washington National Cathedral.
