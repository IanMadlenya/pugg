
        STREETEN-David H. P., M.D., D.Phil. F.R.C.P., F.A.C.P. Age 78, died Friday, September 29, at University Hospital, Syracuse, NY. Dr. Streeten was born in Bloemfontein, South Africa. Beloved husband of Dr. Barbara Streeten, and father of Robert, Elizabeth and John Streeten. Funeral services will be held at 11 AM at St. Paul's Cathedral in Syracuse, NY, Tuesday, October 3. Dr. Streeten was beloved in his role as teacher, lecturer and physician by his students and a large number of patients for whom he cared. He was also a very productive contributor to medical research, publishing over 200 research papers, and serving on the editorial boards of five clinical and research journals. He had a special interest in orthostatic disorders of the circulation, publishing a book with this title in 1987, and reported original descriptions of several new syndromes in this and related areas.
