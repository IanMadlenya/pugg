
        BLACKMON-William Andrew, Jr. On July 23, at age 83. Longtime resident of Greenwich Village and retiree from Price Waterhouse. Survived by his wife Helen Sessinghaus Blackmon, daughter Rosemary Grove, and son William Andrew Blackmon III. Contributions in memory may be made to Poly Prep Country Day School in Brooklyn or Wesleyan University in Middletown, CT. Plans for a memorial service will be announced at a later date.
