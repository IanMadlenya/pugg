
        BERLIN-Bess. Beloved wife of the late Roy, devoted mother of Robert and Mark, cherished grandmother of Daniel, Michael, Robert, Joanna, Brian, and Steven. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM,at The Reconstructionist Synagogue, 1001 Plandome Road, Manhasset. BERLIN-Bess. The Reconstructionist Synagogue of the North Shore deeply mourns the passing of its founding member, our beloved Bess. Her vision and resolute spirit were dedicated to the establishment of our congregation and the survival of the Jewish people. She avidly and generously supported Jewish education and established an endowment for that purpose in our institution. Those who knew and loved her know the loss we feel. Lee Friedlander, Rabbi Lee Parker Michael Lindner, Presidents
