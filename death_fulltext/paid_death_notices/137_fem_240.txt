
        LYNN-Tamar. The Emergency Department of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Tamar Lynn. The cherished wife of our revered colleague and Director of the St. Luke's Hospital Emergency Department, Stephan Lynn, MD, and a beloved mother to her children Rachel and Sarah, Tamar will be profoundly missed. She made a lasting impact on her friends and neighbors through her tireless efforts on behalf of Community Board 7, the Stephen Wise Synagogue, and the Westside Crime Prevention Program. To her husband Stephan and her entire family, we offer our condolences. Gregg Husk, MD Chairman Marc Borenstein, MD Vice Chairman
