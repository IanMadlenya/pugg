
        SAPER-Murray, DDS. On November 21, 1998. The officers, directors, members and staff of The New York County Dental Society mourn the passing of their distinguished colleague, friend and past president. Murray's warmth, humility and love of the profession, his unselfish devotion to family, friends and community, combined to establish him as a man of honor and integrity. We wish to express our sympathy to his family on their loss. Jeffrey R. Burkes, DDS, President, NYCDS Mitchell G. Kuhn, Executive Director, NYCDS
