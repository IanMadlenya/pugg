
        SPERLING-David E.. 81, of Sherman, Ct., and formerly White Plains, NY, passed away November 9, 1999. Husband of Phyllis H. for 58 years, father of Margaret, Alice, David L., Sharon, Louise; grandfather of Daniel, Sarah, Gideon and David J. He remained a lifelong learner and natural teacher. Much beloved by friends and family for his quiet dignity, humor and quality friendship. He was a wonderful man. A Spring Memorial Service is planned in Sherman, Ct.
