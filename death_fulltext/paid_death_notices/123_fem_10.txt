
        ALPERT-Anne. Passed away March 30. Pajarito, the name bestowed on her by her first husband Matta, the father of two sons Gordon Matta-Clarke and SebastianMatta both deceased, will be sorely missed by her friends and family. She was an extraordinary gracious and loving lady. Memorial service will be held in Sag Harbor, where she had a summer home, in late spring.
