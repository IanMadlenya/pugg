
        FITZGERALD--Libuse O. (Liby), 84, of Valhalla, formerly of Eastchester, NY, died on October 21, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Edward. Loving mother of Eileen (Paul Rosenblum) Fitzgerald and Kevin (Linda) Fitzgerald. Cherished grandmother of Leona Rosenblum. Liby was an accomplished ceramicist, Liby also gave generously of her time to community services and organizations. She will be greatly missed by her family and many friends. Service at Westchester Funeral Home in Eastchester on October 25th at 11 AM. Visitation Thursday 7-9 PM and Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM.
