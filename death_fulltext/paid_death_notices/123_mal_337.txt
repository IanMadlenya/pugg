
        CASRIEL-Carl, on April 27, after a courageous 29 year battle with Parkinsons disease. Beloved husband of 42 years of Tamara (nee Rippner Casriel). Darling father of Laurie Casriel, Cathy Gyory, and Eric Casriel. Esteemed father-inlaw of Andrew Gyory. Adored grandfather (Papa Carl) of Madeleine, William, and Lila Gyory. Funeral services were held on Tuesday, April 29, 1997, at 2:00 PM from the Bloomfield-Cooper Jewish Chapels, 2130 Highway 35, Ocean, NJ. Committal services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Jewish Family & Childrens Services, 705 Summerfield Avenue, Asbury Park, N.J.
        CASRIEL-Carl. Officers and members of the Elberon Bathing Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime member, Mr. Carl Casriel. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Robert Fagenson, President Ellen G. Winik, Secretary
