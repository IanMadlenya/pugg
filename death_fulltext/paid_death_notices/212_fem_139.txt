
        FALK--Audrey. September 16th, in Oklahoma. Born 1929 in London; she lived in New York, Honolulu, and Lawton, Oklahoma. In New York, she volunteered for the Actors' Fund as a ''buddy'' to performers dying of HIV/AIDS. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Lee Falk; children, Willy Falk and Cathy Caccioppoli; son-in-law, Dr. Giuseppe Caccioppoli; granddaughters, Nina and Joanna.
