
        ROYER-Jim. Former long time Pro Personnel Director of the New York Jets and beloved husband of Linda and father of Brian and Jamie. Spent 20 years with the Jets before retiring in 1996, with a total of 25 years working in the NFL as a scout and coach. A 1957 graduate of the U.S. Naval Academy and member of the Marine Corps, 1957-1961. Born in Baltimore, MD September 15, 1932 he grew up in Philadelphia. Jamaica Ave, Valley Stream, Saturday, 9:45 AM. Flowers may be sent or donations made to the National Colorectal Cancer Research Alliance.
