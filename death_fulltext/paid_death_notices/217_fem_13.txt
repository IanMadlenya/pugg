
        STERN--Irene. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Irene Stern, a devoted friend who together with her beloved late husband, Gustav, and through the continued efforts of her son, Steven, strengthen our mission to serve those in need in New York, Israel, and around the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to Steven and his wife, Bonnie, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        STERN--Irene. A truly unique woman has left us with a void that can never be filled. She toiled with her husband, Gustav, and helped found Hartz Mountain Pet Foods. World traveler, rancher, sports woman and philanthropist. She was a devoted mother, adored grandmother & greatgrandmother. Sleep peacefully, Oma. We love you. Jim, Joyce, Andrew, Tiffany, Jill and Alana Herlands, Steven, Bonnie, Dara, Evan, Sasha, David and Heather, Roy, Judi, Sarah, Adina, Meira, Imanuel, Gidon, Noam and Yael Stern
