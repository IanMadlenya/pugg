
        SCHACHTER-Scott A. 74, of Levittown, NY, died suddenly on August 31, 2000. Beloved and devoted husband of Justine, for 51 years. Cherished father of Linda Wright, Barbara Costa, Peter Schachter and David Schachter. Treasured father-in-law of Robert Wright, Antonia Schachter and Scott Hunt. Adored grandfather of Jeffrey and Bryan Wright, and Nicholas and Jonathan Schachter. Dearest brother of Paul Schachter. Loving cousin, uncle, nephew, and friend. Scott was a retired NBC audio engineer and two time Emmy Award recipient for his distinguished work in live television. He delighted in traveling the world with his wife, laughter with his family and friends, and doing The New York Times Crossword Puzzles in ink. Service on Sunday at 3 PM, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I.
