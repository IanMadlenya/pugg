
        JORDAN--Josephine C. On November 13, 2005. We mourn the loss of an energetic, independent and life-affirming woman. Daughter of the late Hortense and Joseph Straus. Talented long-time executive with Clothiers Corporation. Devoted member of Temple Emanu-El, member of Opera Index. She is and will be deeply missed by her friends. Memorial Services Wednesday 2PM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65th Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Congregation Temple Emanu-El of New York.
        $(6$)JORDAN--Josephine C. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York is deeply saddened by the death of our devoted and devout member, Jo Jordan. Ms. Jordan was actively involved in all aspects of temple life. A woman, diminutive in size, but of an enormous professionalism and commitment. She will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. George Asch, Sec'y
