
        REDDIG -- William McKee han, Jr., 75, formerly of Westport, CT and New York City, died on May 21st at home in Fayetteville, AR. After graduating from Colgate University, he served as an officer in the United States Navy from 1952 until 1956. He worked as a reporter at the Richmond News Leader, Northern Virginia Sun, and Washington Evening Star, an editor at Newsweek and Forbes, a freelance journalist for several financial publications and finally in public relations at Hill & Knowlton in New York until his retirement in 1995. Survivors include his wife of 53 years, Dorothy Jean (Poindexter) Reddig of Fayetteville, sister Barbara (Reddig) Smith, children Andrew, Abigail and Eugene, along with 13 grandchildren. A memorial service will be conducted in the fall in Fayetteville, AR. Contributions may be made to Washington Regional Hospice, 34 Colt Square, Fayetteville, AR 72703.
