
        COLLINS-Joe, died peacefully in his sleep on Thursday, September 12, 2002. He worked as a Foreign Correspondent for the London office of the New York Times for many years during and after W.W. II and will be sadly missed by all of his family. The funeral was held in Worthing, Sussex, England on Friday, September 20. He is survived by his wife of 52 years, Eha.
