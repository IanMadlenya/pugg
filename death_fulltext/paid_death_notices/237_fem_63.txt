
        BRICK--Sylvia, 93, died on September 9. Mrs. Brick was the widow of the late Seymour Brick, the loving mother of Richard and the late Kate. She was the cherished grandmother of Noah, devoted sister of Alice, aunt of Jessica and Jonathan, beloved mother-in-law of Sara Bershtel and the long-time friend of Helen Ashley. Mrs. Brick taught English in New Jersey High schools 1934--42. She was employed for many years at the Professional Placement Center of the NYS Dept of Employment, and then for many years as a counselor at the NY Foundation for New Americans. She enjoyed the distinction of being the oldest serving volunteer guide at the NY Public Library, retiring in her 80's.
