
        STOLBOF-Bernard. Of Syosset, NY. Passed away on September 2, 1998, at age 70. He is survived by his wife Betty. Children Paul, Edward, Jesse and Daniel and four grandchildren. Retired from the House of Seagram where he worked for 35 years. He had a history of Parkinson Disease and died of pneumonia. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the National Parkinson Foundation, 1501 NW 9th Avenue, Miami, Florida 33136.
