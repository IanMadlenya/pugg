
        RILEY-Dr. Mary C. (Jones), 81, of 32 Sunset Lane, Petersham, MA died Saturday, January 9, 1999, in her home. She was known to her friends as Polly. She leaves her husband of 52 years, Dr. Richard L. Riley; a son, Richard C. Riley of Ithaca, NY; two daughters, Mary L. Riley of Hong Kong, China, Susan H. Riley of Petersham; a sister, Peggy Brooks of New York City, NY; and two grandchildren. She was born January 18, 1917, in New York City, N.Y., the daughter of T. Catesby and Louisa (Brooke) Jones and was a resident of Petersham for 22 years coming from Baltimore, MD. where she resided for 27 years. She graduated from Brearley School in New York City. She received a B.S. from Bennington College, Bennington, VT, and went on to receive her M.D. from Johns Hopkins Medical School, Baltimore, MD, in 1948. She worked in several clinics in the Baltimore area specializing in internal medicine with emphasis on chest diseases, and achieved the rank of Assistant Professor of Medicine at The Johns Hopkins School of Medicine. She was also active in the Baltimore chapter of the League of Women Voters. She enjoyed singing, listening to music and painting. She was active in town activities including the Petersham Board of Health, the Library Committee, the Carpenter Concert Committee, the Petersham Branch Alliance, and the Petersham Craft Center. In recent years she was a regular attendant at the Monday lunches of the Petersham Council on Aging. A memorial service will be held in the Petersham Unitarian Church Sunday, January 24, 1999, at 3 P.M. Burial will be at the convenience of the family. There are no calling hours. For information Fiske Funeral Home, 1356 Main Street, Athol, MA 01331.
