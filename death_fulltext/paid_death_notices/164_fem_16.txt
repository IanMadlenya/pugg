
        FERBER-Jane F., MD. On September 19th. Peacefully at home after a long illness. Loving mother of Elizabeth and Joshua; beloved mother-in-law to Josh and Prasthano; devoted grandmother to Gideon and Tallulah; inspiring companion to Richard. Graduate of Barnard College, 1957, and Columbia Univ. College of Physicians and Surgeons, 1961. Most recently Associate Residency director at Creedmore Psychiatric Center and Asst. Clinical Prof. of Psychiatry at Columbia University. She maintained a private psychiatry practice in New Rochelle, NY. she enlarged the life of everyone she knew. In addition to her family, she leaves a wide circle of grieving friends who will cherish her memory always.
