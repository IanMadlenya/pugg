
        McMANIS--Douglas R. The American Geographical Society notes with sadness the death on September 30th of Douglas R. McManis, editor of the Geographical Review for 20 years and 2001 recipient of the society's Samuel Finley Breese Morse Medal ''for the encouragement of geographical research.'' Over many decades he helped to shape the careers of countless young geographers. He was a stalwart friend and supporter of AGS. He will be deeply missed. John E. Gould, Chair Jerome E. Dobson, President Mary Lynne Bird, Exec. Dir.
