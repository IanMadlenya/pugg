
        GOULD-Robert, MD. The faculty and staff of the department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Science at New York Medical College note with sadness the passing of Dr. Robert Gould, our friend and esteemed colleague. Dr Gould was an exemplary teacher in the medical school and a respected clinician at Metropolitan Hospital Center. Dr. Gould held dual appointments as Clinical Professor in Psychiatry and Obstetrics and Gynecology. His many students and colleagues will miss him.
