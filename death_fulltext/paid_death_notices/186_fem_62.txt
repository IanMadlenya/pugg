
        SORIA-Dorle. The Board and Staff of The Metropolitan Opera Guild are deeply saddened by the death of Dorle Soria. As a press representative, writer, and recording executive, Dorle was a keen participant in the musical life of New York. The wife of our late Managing Director, Dario Soria, she was a friend and valued counselor to The Guild, whose members extend their sincere condolences to her family. Winthrop Rutherfurd, Jr, Chairman Susan S. Braddock, President Rudolph S. Rauch, Managing Director
