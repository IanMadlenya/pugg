
        FASTENBERG--Milton. Died on October 15, 2003 at the age of 91 in Phoenix, AZ. Milton practiced internal medicine on the Upper West Side for more than 40 years and dedicated his life to helping those in need, always studying so as to better serve others. He was cherished by his family and beloved by all who knew him. He was a most wonderful man. Adored father of Nathaniel and Francine Fastenberg of Phoenix, AZ. Beloved grandfather of Rachel, Bryan and Sara. Treasured brother of Abner, Marvin, Selma and William. Precious uncle to many. He will be sorely missed, but remembered with love by all. Services Friday October 17th, 11:45 at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
