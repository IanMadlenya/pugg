
        BERNARD--Madeleine, 88, on September 27, 2005. Worked for Alfred De Liagre, Rex Stout at the Writers War Board, and at the American Theater Wing, where she met her husband Graham Bernard. A devoted wife and mother, she is lovingly remembered by daughters Meg, Katharine, & Martha, sons-in-law Rod & Vincent, and grandsons Vaughan, Greg, Nik & Sean. Donations may be made to Catskill Area Hospice Hope Foundation.
