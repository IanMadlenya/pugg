
        GLASS-Gerald G., Ph.D. Devoted husband of Esther. Loving father of Susan, Jonathan and Matthew; grandfather of Emily, Abigail, Anna and Sam. Proud World War II veteran, recipient of the Bronze Star and Purple Heart. Professor of education at Adelphi University since 1958. Raised in the South Bronx, he called Patchogue, L.I. and Locke Mills, Maine home. His humor, generosity and spirit live on in us all.
