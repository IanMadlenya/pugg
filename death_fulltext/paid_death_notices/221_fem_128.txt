
        LOCKE--Esther S. Died on June 15, 2005, at the age of 96, in New York City. Her energy, wit, intelligence, compassion and enormous gifts and skills as a teacher and educational administrator affected the lives of thousands of people in the course of her 72-year career, and she will be missed by many. A devoted and generous mother and grandmother, she is survived by her son Richard Locke and his wife Wendy Nicholson, and by three grandchildren, Sarah J. Locke, Rebecca J. Locke, Nicholas B. A. Nicholson, of New York City, and by her sister, Jeanette Manson, of Deltona, FL.
