
        DEUTSCH-Rabbi Sender. The World Council of Orthodox Jewish Communities, has suffered the profound loss of its Senior Vice President and dynamic leader, editor of ''Der Yid'', who inspired a generation of the Chassidic community with his teachings and writings. We join in the sorrow of his devoted wife, sons, daughters and grandchildren in mourning his demise. May his bereaved family be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Hertz Frankel, Vice President Eliezer (Louie) Kestenbaum, Chairman Executive Committee Rabbi Chaim M. Stauber, Executive Vice President
