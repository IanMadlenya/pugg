
        COWARD-Marion W. (Vanessa). Of Bedford, New York on January 4, 1998 at 99-1/2. She was the last survivor of the ''White'' sisters, ie. Katharine W. Caulkins and Jane S.W. Canfield. She is survived by one of her two daughters, Jenifer (Gregg) Sims and 3 grandchildren, Peter and Jenifer Sims and James Marquand, the son of the late Sue Marquand. Memorial services on Friday, January 16 at 11 A.M., St. Matthews Church, Bedford, NY. Contributions in her memory can be made to Planned Parenthood of New York City for the Jane Canfield fund for Women's Health Care, 26 Bleecker Street, N.Y.C. 10012 or your favorite charity.
