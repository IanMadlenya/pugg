
        BIJUR--Mildred R. (Mickey) Pollack. Beloved by her sons David and Jason, her four cats, Becky, and her many friends. Her children request that instead of flowers you send a donation in Mickey's memory to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, P.O. Box 27106, New York, NY 10087 or www.mskcc.org.
