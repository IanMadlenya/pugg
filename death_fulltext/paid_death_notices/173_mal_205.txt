
        MILNER-James. Beloved husband of Jean. Devoted father of Mary Wittner and Robert Milner their spouses Andrew Wittner and Saralee Milner. Cherished grandfather of Pamela D'Alexander, Diane Wittner and Steve Wittner, and their spouses Jon Acheson, Doug D'Alexander and Jane Wittner. Adored great grandfather of Baird, Gabriel, Domenic, Steven and Beth. Died quietly in his sleep in his 94th year leaving a legacy of quiet strength, implacable moral standards and a 75-year history of giving musical joy. We mourn his passing and cherish his memory.
