
        VOLCHOK-Saul. The Officers and Staff of State of Israel Bonds mourn with profound sorrow the passing of an outstanding leader and devoted friend and supporter of the State of Israel. Saul served with great distinction as a National Campaign Vice-Chairman of Israel Bonds and as General Chairman of its Philadelphia campaign. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Susan Weikers-Volchok, Israel Bonds' former National Campaign Chairman, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Gideon Patt President and CEO Burton P. Resnick Chairman of the Board Irwin Hochberg National Campaign Chairman Michael Siegal Vice-Chairman of the Board Roberta Goldstein Nat'l Assoc. Campaign Chrmn.
