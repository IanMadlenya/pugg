
        LERNER -- Rabbi Israel. Passed away in his beloved Jerusalem. Respected Principal of the Hebrew Institute of Boro Park, Rabbi Lerner was exemplary in living a life devoted to the love of Torah, the people of Israel and the land of Israel. His life is an inspiration for his family and the Jewish community. Hon. Jerome Hornblass, Pres. Yeshiva Etz Chaim Fdtn Inc.
