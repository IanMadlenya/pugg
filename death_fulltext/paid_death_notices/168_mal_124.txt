
        HOROWITZ-Josef, DDS. Died on January 8, 2001 at MemorialSloane Kettering Hospital of cancer. His career as a dentist included service as a Captain in Germany during World War II. He leaves his devoted wife Leatha Lykins; daughter Lynne Van Engel, her husband Daniel and his grandchildren, Emily and Madeleine. He is also survived by his son, Martin Horowitz, his wife Heidi and children, Nathaniel and Ana and a second son Glenn Horowitz, his wife Rebecca; and his sister, Rosalie Lang of Seattle. Dr. Horowitz was a gentle, good and compassionate man who will be sorely missed. Gifts may be sent in his honor to Meals On Wheels.
