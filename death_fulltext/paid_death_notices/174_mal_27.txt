
        KNASTER-Baruch. Born in Lodz, Poland, passed away in his 96th year. Husband of Cila, beloved father of Rebecca and Mirka and her husband Larry Jacobs. Holocaust survivor of numerous slave labor and concentration camps. A man whose concern for the welfare of his family was first and foremost. Services on Tuesday, at 10 AM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st Street, New York City.
