
        BEATUS-Howard Z. Closed his eyes peacefully on October 11, 2001, after a courageous fight against pancreatic cancer. He is survived by his wife, Betty Beatus, daughters Allison Asaro, Carolyn Lockett, and grandchildren, Christopher and Kelly Lockett. We will mourn his death and celebrate his life forever. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Monday, October 15th, 9-11 AM, with services 11:30AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Doctors Without Borders, 6 East 39 Street, 8th Floor, New York, NY 10016.
