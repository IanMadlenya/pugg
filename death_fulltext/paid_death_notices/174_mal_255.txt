
        MARBACH-Dr. Joseph J. New York Chapter of Hadassah notes with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Joseph J. Marbach, beloved husband of Judy, member of the Executive Board and editor of Highlights. May his family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Contributions in his memory may be made to New York Chapter of Hadassah. Arline Kempner, President Lois Shallit, Exec. Dir.
