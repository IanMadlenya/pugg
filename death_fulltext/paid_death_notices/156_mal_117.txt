
        WOLMAN-Benjamin B. 91, on January 3rd. Author/editor of 42 books in psychology. In private practice of psychotherapy for 50 years. Founder/editor of International Journal of Group Tensions. Editor & Chief of the Encyclopedia of Psychiatry, Psychology, Psychoanalysis & Neurology. He was Professor at Adelphi University and Long Island University. He is survived by his wife Jean, daughter Danielle, brother Moshe and Joel of Israel, nieces and nephews.
