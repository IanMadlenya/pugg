
        GRANT--Frances. Beloved wife of William for 67 years. Wonderful mother of Edward Grant (wife Jane) and Alice Brofman. Also survived by grandchildren Michael (wife Penny) Grant, Gregory (wife Elisabetta) Grant and Ben Brofman, and a great-granddaughter, Antonia LewinsGrant. Best friend and sister to Shirley Brown and Anita Moss. Loving aunt to many nieces and nephews. She was special to all who knew her and will be sorely missed. Funeral service Monday, 11:30 am, at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Rd in Mamaroneck, 914381-1809, followed by an interment at King David Cemetery in Putnam Valley, NY.
