
        LASKER-Abby Rachael. Born November 7, 1969, died August 29, 2001. Abby, known as Rachael, was a spunky and outgoing young woman. Rachael fought a long and courageous battle with cancer. She did it with grace and spirit. Rachael is survived by her parents Betty and Milton Lasker, her sister Cynthia Lasker, a brother Seth Lasker, his wife Staci, their children Moriah and Alex and loving fiancee John Hebert. Funeral services will be held at Temple Sinai, 500 Swift St., So. Burlington, VT on August 31 at 11AM. Donations in Rachael's memory to the Respite House of Vermont, 99 Allen Brook Lane, Williston VT 05495.
