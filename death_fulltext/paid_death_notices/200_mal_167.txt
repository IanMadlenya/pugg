
        STRASSER--Andrew. 84, died on September 11, 2003. He was predeceased by his wife, Florence. Andrew was the revered father of Michael Strasser, Joyce Monaco and Deborah Adler. Beloved grandfather of Christopher and Victoria Monaco, Shane and Collin Adler. Cherished companion of Lois Perlman. Graveside funeral at King David Cemetery, Monday, September 15, at 11 AM.
