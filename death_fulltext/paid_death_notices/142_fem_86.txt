
        SCHARFF - Constance Kramer. Wife of the late Harry. Mother of Matthew and Carol. Grandmother of Karen Scharff (James Coe), Thomas Zucker-Scharff (Sue) and David Scharff. Great grandmother of Aram, Ilana, Jonah and Rachel. Sister of Jack Kramer and the late Sybil Yedlin Ginsberg, Dorothy Greene and Leona Hart. She was a special person whose love for learning persisted throughout her life, and a gifted artist. Services at 11 A.M., Thursday, Larchmont Temple. Contributions to Larchmont Temple.
