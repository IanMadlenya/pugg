
        ALTFEST--Beth, 59, died peacefully on August 19, 2005 surrounded by family and friends. She was a mother, wife, daughter, sister, and friend who deeply touched the lives of all who knew her. Her wit, candor, selflessness, generosity and most of all, the bravery with which she fought her illness, will forever serve as a source of inspiration to us. She is survived by her husband Robert, daughter Rachel, parents Frances and Seymour, brother Harvey and his wife Connie. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 331 Amsterdam Avenue on Sunday, August 21, 2005 at 12:45 PM. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to: Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Ovarian Cancer Research, www.mskcc.org.
