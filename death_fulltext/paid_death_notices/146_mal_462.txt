
        COWEN-Lester G.. Of Armonk, NY, March 23. Adoring husband of the late Helen. Loving father of Nancy (Steve) Patchen, Margie (Robert) Cohen, and Mark (Paddi) Cowen. Beloved grandfather of David, Michael, Jennifer, Daniel, Jonathan, and Elyse. Dearest brother of Sander (Dolores). Devoted brother-in-law of Alice Rosenfeld. In his lifetime he wore many hats: U.S. Navy veteran; principal of Garfield-Wolar; chicken, duck, and sheep farmer; carpenter extraordinaire; mechanical maven; and producer of delicious smoked salmon. We'll always miss you, Daddy/Poppi. Funeral private. Family receiving Saturday 7 to 9 P.M., and Sunday and Monday 1 to 9 P.M. at the Cowen residence, 21 Penny Lane, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to: American Diabetes Association or Food Patch.
