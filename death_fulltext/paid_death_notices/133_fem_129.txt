
        ANDERSON-Jane M. May 23, 1908-February 15, 1998. Editor, writer and painter. Survived by her son Greville Healey, her daughters Stephanie Healey Owen and Jane Anderson Bernstein, seven grandchildren and eleven great grandchildren. Her oldest daughter, Joanna Healey Shurbet died in 1980. Mrs. Anderson was the widow of Cmdr. Clarence M. Anderson, U.S.N.
