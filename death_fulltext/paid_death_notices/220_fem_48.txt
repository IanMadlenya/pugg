
        JONES--Virginia Kelsey, beloved wife of Frederick E. Jones, passed away on May 5, 2005 at her home on Jupiter Island, FL. She was born in Detroit, MI, on November 26, 1909, she was the daughter of Margarette Kerr Dallas and John Kelsey. She attended Liggett School in Detroit, she also attended Bennett College in New York. She was a member of Broad Street Presbyterian Church, Columbus, OH, also a member of Christ Memorial Chapel, Jupiter Island, FL, Jupiter Island Club and Hobe Sound Yacht Club. She was preceded in death by her brother, Dallas Kelsey. Survivors include, a son, Frederick E. Jones, Jr, and his wife, Ann of Jupiter Island, FL, a daughter, Margarette J. Owen and her husband, David of Jupiter Island, FL, six grandchildren, Bradley C. Drowne, Kelsey Kerr Leachman, Rhodes F. Drowne, Cathryn J. Williard, Frederick E. Jones, III, and Samantha J. Obrien and seven great grandchildren, Chris Jones, Mackenzie Jones, Findley Obrien, Carter Drowne, Avery Drowne, Hannah Leachman and Olivia Leachman. In lieu of flowers those who wish may make memorial gifts for Virginia K. Jones to the Hobe Sound Bible College P.O. Box 1065, Hobe Sound, FL, 33475 and Jupiter Island Clinic Medical Fund P.O. Box 375, Hobe Sound, FL, 33475. A Memorial Service will be held in November on Jupiter Island at Christ Memorial Chapel. For info The Young & Prill Funeral Home in Stuart, FL.
