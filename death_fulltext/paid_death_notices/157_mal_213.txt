
        STERNHEIMER-Rudolph Max. Suddenly on February 16, 2000 at age 73. Retired Senior Physicist at Brookhaven National Laboratory where he worked for over 40 years. Beloved husband of Betty Sternheimer. Dear brother of Paul Sternheimer and his wife Hanne and Michael Sternheimer and his wife Karine. Devoted uncle of John, Philip, William and Jennifer Sternheimer. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Saturday, Sunday and Monday 2-5 PM with services Tuesday 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Woodlawn Cemetery.
