
        LILLYS--Edmund C. Died peacefully on December 30, 2003 following complications from a blood clot. Beloved husband of Iris, he also leaves a sister, nephews and cousins. Born in 1908, of Greek parents in Istanbul, Turkey, he came to the United States at an early age, soon entering the the highly competitive restaurant business, eventually founding the renowned fish and lobster restaurant, Gloucester House. He was an Archon of the Ecumenical Patriarchate of Constantinople and a longtime member and benefactor of the Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, which he served as Vice President. Funeral services, Saturday, January 3 at 10:00am at Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Cathedral, 319 East 74 Street, Manhattan.
