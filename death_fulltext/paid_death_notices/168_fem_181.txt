
        LEVENE-Inez Cohn. Of Somers, NY and formerly of Mount Vernon, NY on Wednesday, January 17, 2001. Inez was a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Hunter College. Beyond her love of family she was devoted to her sense of community responsibility. While a resident of Mount Vernon, she served proudly as a member of the Human Rights Commission. Inez was active in the Mount Vernon, Somers, Westchester County and New York State League of Women Voters, serving in many varied capacities during her lifetime. In 1999. she was recognized for her contributions by being named the Somers' League of Women Voters Woman of the Year. Inez was the devoted wife of Norman for more than 51 years and the loving mother to Arthur and Harold, and daughters-in-law Laurie and Sue. Beloved grandmother to Beverly, Brian, Cori, Dianna and Jason. She was an adored sister-in-law to Elaine and Herman Slotoroff and lovingly remembered by many cousins, nieces, nephews and dear friends. A funeral service will be held on January 19, 2001, Friday at 11am at the chapel of Temple Israel Cemetery, Hastings-On-Hudson, NY with interment following immediately after. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to Phelps Hospice or Temple Beth Elohim New Building Fund.
