
        McALLISTER--Anthony, Jr. The Trustees and staff of the South Street Seaport Museum express their deepest condolences to the family of Anthony McAllister, Jr. Since 1864, McAllister tugboats have enhanced the role the shipping industry played in the development of New York City. The company is now led by one of his brothers, Brian A. McAllister, a Trustee of the Museum.
