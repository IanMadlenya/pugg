
        McGOWAN-Edward J. Formerly of Massapequa and Saltaire, N.Y. On October 5, 2001 at age 78 after a long illness. Dear husband of Jane. Loving father of Edward and Judith, Terence and Suzanne, Michael and Anna, Denise and Joseph, and Christine. Devoted grandfather of Henry, Patrick, Madeleine, Charlotte, & Katherine. A private Mass of the Christian Burial will be held at Our Lady of the Magnificat R.C. Church, Kinnelon, N.J. on October 13, 2001. The State Street Firefighters were so kind to Ed in his final illness, and they lost four of their brothers in the WTC attack. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Engine Company 226, NYFD, 409 State St, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11217.
