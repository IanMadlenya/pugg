
        REINGOLD--Haim, 93. Beloved husband of Badonna Levinson Reingold and the late Leah Jacobson Reingold, father of Edward (Ruth), Arthur (Gail Bolan) and David, grandfather of seven grandchildren, great grandfather of five brother of the late Samuel, Dvora and Jacob. Born in Lodz, Poland, raised in Tel Aviv and came to U.S, in 1930 where he was educated at the University of Cincinnati. Dr. Reingold taught mathematics for almost 70 yrs. and was chair of that dept. at Illinois Institute of Technology. Donations may be made to Akiba-Schecter Jewish Day School, 5235 S. Cornell, Chicago, 60615.
