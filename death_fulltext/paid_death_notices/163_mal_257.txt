
        SLEVIN-James V. On Saturday August 19, 2000 of Long Eddy, NY, formerly of Wayne, NJ. Before retiring in 1991, he worked for New York Life Ins. Co. for 38 years as a General Manager of the Knickerbocker Office in the Empire State Building in Manhattan. A devoted family man, Jim is survived by his wife of 46 years Patricia (nee Dunigan); son James and his wife Elizabeth along with their children Michael and Lori; daughter Gayle DeNicola and her husband Dante; son Thomas and his wife Kimberly. Also surviving are his mother Bridie (nee Daveny), brothers Jack, Patrick and sister Eileen. His cherished daughter Maureen predeceased him in 1975. Relatives and friends are invited to his visitation at Vander May Funeral Home in Wayne, NJ on Tuesday from 2-4pm and 7-9pm. A funeral mass will be offered Wednesday 11 AM at Our Lady of Consolation RC Church, Wayne. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to the St. Benedict's Prep. Alumni Fund, 520 Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. Newark, NJ 07102-9128 or Holy Cross Church, Star Route 97, Callicoon, NY 12723 would be appreciated. For more information and a photo visit vandermay.com.
