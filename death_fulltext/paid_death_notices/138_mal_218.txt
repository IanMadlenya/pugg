
        BAESZLER-Donald. Passed away July 18, 1998. Formerly of Beverly Hills CA., and Staten Island, NY. He was a retired 27 year veteran homicide detective of the New York City Police Department. He was a recipient of the medal of valor from the police department. He is survived by a son Donald, of Finland, a daughter Lynn of Sarasota, FL, 4 grandchildren, a sister, Mrs. Doris Anglin and a nephew Jack Anglin, both of Staten Island, and dear friends Mr. and Mrs. Robert Tanenbaum and family of Beverly Hills, California. A funeral service will be held on Wednesday at the Good Shepherd Church, Beverly Hills, California.
