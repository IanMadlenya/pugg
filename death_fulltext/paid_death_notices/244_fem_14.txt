
        WEISS--Paula, mother of Peter, mother-in-law of Cora, grandmother of Judy, Tamara and Danny, great-grandmother of Jules, Noah, Maria, Catherine and Simon, died April 29, age 104. She lived a courageous life, as a mater familias, a business woman, early feminist, opera fan and inspiration to her family, friends and admirers. Service at Riverside Chapel 75th St and Amsterdam, Friday May 4th at 4pm. Contributions to Center for Constitutional Rights, 666 Broadway, NYC 10012.
