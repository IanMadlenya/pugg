
        DiIORIO--Julio. 63, born in the Bronx, father, friend and advertising creative. Lost his 18 month battle with emphysema on May 19, 2004, after a courageous fight. Through the most difficult of times, Julio never lost his charismatic wit, his willingness to laugh and his ability to deeply move those he knew. His bravery and love of life are an inspiration for his daughters Nicole and Mia, grandson Nicolas and his family, who he loved and supported until his death. Services on May 25, 11:30 am, The Immaculate Conception Church, Gunhill Road, Bronx, NY.
