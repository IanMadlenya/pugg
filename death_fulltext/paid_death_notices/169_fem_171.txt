
        CONWAY-Adelyn C. (nee Rosenfeld). A lady of infinite wisdom and incredible devotion passed quietly on February 16. Beloved mother of Carol Jane Conway Castano. Cherished mother-in-law of Robert Castano. Adored grandmother of Caroline Aaron. Graduate of University of Pennsylvania, Yale Graduate School and Georgetown School of Foreign Service. She was very proud to be one of the first women to serve in the US Foreign Service, being stationed in Germany, England and Burma. In addition, she served here and abroad for the American Red Cross. She will be desperately missed in the lives of many. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to the Women in Military Service Memorial Foundation, Washington, DC, and the Janssen Memorial Hospice of Tuckahoe, NY. Services today, 3 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment private.
