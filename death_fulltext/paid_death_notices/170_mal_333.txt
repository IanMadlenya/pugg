
        KAUFMAN-David. Aged 48. In San Antonio, Texas, Sunday, March 25 of renal cancer. He leaves adoring wife Susan, children Brooks, 13, Emmet, 10, parents Ira and Dawn, brothers Charles, Jonathan, Thomas, Justin, four nephews and four nieces. David was a 1977 Law graduate of Emory University and practiced Energy Law. His life was filled with joy in virtue and devotion.
