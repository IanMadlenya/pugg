
        BROCK-Cornelia Robb, age 76, on September 11, 2000, after a short illness. Born on June 4, 1924 in New York City. Mrs. Brock was married to the late Josiah V.K. Thompson of Uniontown, PA and Alexander Biddle Brock of Philadelphia, PA. She is survived by three children; Cornelia Thayer Mellin of Annapolis, MD, Josiah V.K. Thompson III and Beatrix H. Jakotich both of Portland, OR; one grandhcild Joshua A. Dunivant; one sister, Mrs. Robert Wolcott of Boston, MA; one brother, Mr. Bayard Van Rensular Robb of Vero Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to The American Cancer Society. A memorial service will be held Friday, September 15, 2000 at the Springfield Residence, 551 East Evergreen Avenue, Wyndmoor, PA 19038 at 10:00 a.m. Kirk & Nice Funeral Home, Inc., Stenton Avenue and Butler Pike, Plymouth Meeting, PA 19462.
