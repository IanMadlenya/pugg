
        LIPKIN-Nathan Joseph, D.D.S. 75. Of Hollywood, FL, formerly of New York. Passed away March 10, 1999. Beloved husband Gladys. Loving father of Harriet (Chris Sautter), Alan (Barbara) and Rebecca. Dear brother of Shirley Rosenthal. Cherished grandfather of Richard, Sam and Molly Lipkin, Josh and Stephanie Sautter. Graduate of Johns Hopkins University and the New York University School of Dentistry and its Graduate Orthodontic course. He died peacefully and painlessly. His life will be celebrated in a memorial service at Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1921 Pembroke Rd., Hollywood FL. 954-921-7200, Friday, March 12, 1999 at 2PM. Graveside services will take place on Sunday, March 14, 1999 at 1PM at Beth David Cemetery on Elmont Rd in Elmont, Long Island. Shiva will be at the Lipkin residence in Hollywood, FL starting Monday, March 15, 1999.
