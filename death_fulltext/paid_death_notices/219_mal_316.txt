
        GOTTESMAN--Milton M. The Jewish Primary Day School of the Nation's Capital mourns the passing of our dedicated benefactor Milton Gottesman, who shared our dream of a Jewish day school in DC. His early support, devotion to Judaism and our city enriched the lives of our students and other cultural institutions. May his memory be a blessing. Jeffrey Colman, President Susan Koss, Head of School
        GOTTESMAN--Milton. The Society for the Protection of Nature in Israel mourns the passing of Milton Gottesman, a true friend and generous supporter of environmental protection in Israel. Our condolences to his family.
