
        WILKINSON-Frederick D. Jr., the Founding Co-Chair of our Board of Overseers, died peacefully on Sunday. We will miss his friendship and his leadership and will not forget him. Our heartfelt sympathies to Fred's beloved wife, Jeane, and to their children, Sharon, Ambassador to Burkina Feso; Dayna of Weston, Virginia, and Frederick III of Los Angeles. The Board and Staff Brookdale Center on Aging of Hunter College
