
        MANES-Joseph F. at his residence, Croton-on-Hudson, New York. Beloved by his wife Helen, his son David J., his daughter Victoria E. and his four grandchildren and three sisters. A private Catholic Mass was held on May 5, 2000. Mr. Manes practiced Law for fifty years and served as Town Justice in the Town of Cortlandt for sixteen years. In lieu of memorial contributions, the family has requested donations to Phelps Hospice in Sleepy Hollow, N.Y., or to his Church, Holy Name of Mary in Croton-on-Hudson, New York.
