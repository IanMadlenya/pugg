
        DECKER--Robert E. Family and friends are invited to attend a memorial service for Robert E. Decker at 11:30 AM, Sunday, July 13th, 2003, I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 1895 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. Robert, 88, was a longtime resident of Coney Island. He was born September 13, 1914 in Coalville, Washington, and died on May 22, 2003. He is survived by his loving wife of 40 years, Gertrude; daughter, Susan (Decker) Hunt; stepson, Ivan Rubin; grandson, William Hunt; granddaughter, Laura Rubin; and nieces, Frances ''Mickey'' Hooks and Josephine ''Jo'' Reiter and their families. Robert was preceded in death by siblings, Florence McLean, Frances Greenwald, Dorothy Baker, Donald Decker; and grandson Donald Hunt. In lieu of flowers contributions to the New York Chapter of the American Civil Liberties Union or a favorite charity are appreciated. >>AD#
