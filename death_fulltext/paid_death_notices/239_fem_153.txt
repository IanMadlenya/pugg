
        GERTZ--Helen. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Helen Gertz, beloved sister of Muriel Goldberg, whose generosity has enabled us to enrich the Jewish community worldwide. We extend our deepest sympathies to Muriel, her husband Bernard, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
