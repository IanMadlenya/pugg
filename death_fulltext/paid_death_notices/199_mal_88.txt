
        FRAISER--Thomas R. Retired Professor of History and Chairperson of the History Department at Baruch College, author of ''The Underside of American History'', died at home on July 25, 2003. He was born on May 4, 1931 and received his PH.D from Columbia University. Dr. Fraiser was the beloved husband of Marion Fraiser, father of Dawn Cusher and Mark Fraiser, brother of Bill Fraiser and son-in-law of Murray Goldberg. He will be missed by them, his extended family and his many friends.
