
        WALKER-Adelaide George. Died December 5, 1998, at the age of 93. Born in Laramie, WY. Wife of the late Charles R. Walker. A memorial service is planned next August in Wellfleet, MA, where she lived since 1937. She leaves two sons, Charles and Daniel, and five grandchildren, Libby, Matthew, Timmy, Sheafe and Daisy.
