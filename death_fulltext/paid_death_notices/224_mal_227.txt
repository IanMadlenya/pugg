
        FUTTER--Victor, 86, died peacefully at home in Manhasset, Long Island, on September 21, 2005. Beloved husband of Joan for 62 years, loving father of Jeffrey, Ellen and Deborah, father-in-law of William Cohan and Susan Sommer and cherished grandfather of Jillian, Allison, Claire, Anne, Elizabeth, Theodore and Quentin. He was an esteemed lawyer, author and lecturer dedicated to public service and numerous not-for-profit organizations and activities, but above all an adored and devoted husband, father and grandfather. He will be forever treasured, admired and remembered. The funeral is private and a memorial service will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Victor Futter Fellowship For Public Service at Columbia Law School. The family of Victor Futter
