
        MASS - Roslyn, 75. Died peacefully at home June 12, 2002, following a long illness. A brilliant, fascinating and powerful woman, she was a gifted professor of film and English at Baruch College of CUNY, a generous and loving friend, a caring sister, and a devoted and adored aunt, great aunt and cousin. Contributions may be sent to the Hunter College Film Program (Hunter College Foundation), Department of Theatre & Film, 695 Park Ave., New York, NY 10021. Memorial service to be announced.
        MASS-Roslyn. Roz, you were one of a kind and your star burned brightly until the end. You enriched our lives and made us feel so special. We will always love you, your niece Jane and cousin Judy.
