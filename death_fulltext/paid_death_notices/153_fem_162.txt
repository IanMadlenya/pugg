
        SHAPIRO-Beatrice F. 73, of Las Vegas, NV, formerly of West Orange, NJ and Hoboken, NJ, on October 18, 1999. Beloved wife of Arthur Shapiro, mother of Alexis Peter and Oliver Simon; grandmother of three. Sister of Paul Fogelson, aunt of seven. Dear friend of many. The lives of all who knew her well were enriched by knowing her and are diminished by her passing. Brava, Beatrice! Memorial contributions can be made to the Metropolitan Opera. Services will be held on Tuesday, 12 noon, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd at 76 Road, Forest Hills, NY.
