
        SCHWARTZ--Sheila Schiff. Of Syracuse, N.Y. Died on June 8 after a long debilitating illness. Cherished by her devoted husband Irving. Deeply mourned by daughters Suellen Carman, Sally Lang & sister Ellyn Berman. She is lovingly remembered by grandchildren, nieces and nephews and dear friends as a woman who valued family, the arts, sports and the world around her.
