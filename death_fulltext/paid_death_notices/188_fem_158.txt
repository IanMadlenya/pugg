
        PRATI-Helen, 78. Of Riverdale, NY, on September 17, 2002. Born October 9th, 1923 in Nesquehoning, PA. Registered nurse, trained at Bellevue, NYC. Loving mother, devoted wife, always gracious, kind and beautiful. She will be sadly missed but fondly remembered by her son Armand, family and friends. Memorial service Saturday September 28, at Saint Gabriel's Church, Riverdale, NY.
