
        D'AGUANNO-Anthony. Age 60, died in Scottsdale, Arizona, on March 7, 1998. He was born in Queens, New York. He was a retired Managing Director with Smith Barney. Survivors include his wife, Mary Louise; sons, Anthony, Louis and Joseph: mother, Elizabeth and 2 grandchildren. Funeral Services will be held Wednesday, March 11, at 9:00 AM, at Our Lady of Joy Church, Carefree, AZ.
