
        SILVERMAN--Anne, 85. Formerly of Livingston, NJ. Died in her sleep Sunday, January 30, 2005 at a nursing home in Washington, DC. With her late husband Edward she owned and operated ''Silverman's on the Plaza'' in Livingston for 40 years. She was active in the Women's Auxilliary of the Lions Club. She was born September 18, 1919 in Baltimore, MD. She is survived by her son Art Silverman of Washington, DC; Nancy Silverman of Albany, CA; grandchildren Peter and Thomas Silverman and Laura and Aaron Waterhouse. Her two sisters, Janet and Minnie, reside in Florida.
