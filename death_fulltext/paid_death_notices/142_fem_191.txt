
        MARMON-Aline Greenebaum. Age 76. Of White Plains, NY. Died Saturday, November 21, 1998, at White Plains Hospital Center. Born in NYC, Aline was the daughter of the late Milton Joseph Greenebaum, founder of Kirby Block & Co. and co-founder of P.A. Bergner's Department Stores and of the late Sylvia Samler Greenebaum of Philadelphia. She was the loving wife of the late Dr. Martin G. Marmon, a respected dermatologist of Westchester and Scarsdale who died in 1984. A 1939 graduate of the Calhoun School in NYC, she attended Skidmore College and then began working as a part-time buyer for Kirby Block & Co. until she married. She later volunteered for the American Red Cross and assisted with the bookkeeping in her husband's medical practice. She also was a member of the Phelps Memorial Hospital Women's Auxiliary. She was a member of Temple Emanu-El of Westchester. She is survived by James and Leah Marmon of Greenwich, CT; Gary and Karen Marmon of West Palm Beach, FL, and Lawrence and Beth Marmon of Larchmont, NY. She is also survived by five grandchidlren Lane, Nicole, Megan, Emily, and Andrew. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Red Cross, 106 N. Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601. Funeral services will be held Monday, November 23, 1998, at 5 PM, at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY.
