
        DARMANTE--Emanuel A. (Mike). Beloved husband of the late Lucy. Devoted father of Frank and Rose Darmante and Lucille Kandakis. Cherished grandfather of Michael, Dale, and Dawn. Loving great-grandfather of seven and great great-grandfather of two. Emanuel was Deputy Chief of New York Auxiliary Police. Director of Police Bands. Retired Director of Music at St. Johns Home for Boys. He was a veteran of WW II and Member of the VFW. Funeral from Colonial Funeral Home, S.I. Visiting will be Tuesday and Wednesday 2-5 & 7-9PM. Funeral will be Thursday at10:15AM at St. Charles Church, S.I. Interment will be St. Charles Cemetery.
