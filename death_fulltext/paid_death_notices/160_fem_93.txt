
        GORDON-Mildred. Beloved wife of the late Rabbi Julius Gordon. Devoted mother of David, Ruth and Naomi. Loving grandmother of Rachel, Julie, Paul, Joel, and Judith.         GORDON-Mildred. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Mildred Gordon. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
