
        SESHOLTZ--Anna, on April 27, 2004, age 94. Beloved mother of Dr. Marilyn Sesholtz of NYC and Walter Sesholtz of Secaucus, NJ. Cherished grandmother, greatgrandmother, sister, aunt, and friend. Her love of life and family, indomitable spirit, and sense of humor were present to the last. She will be missed by all whose lives she touched. Services private. Donations in her memory may be sent to a charity of your choice.
