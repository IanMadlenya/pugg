
        WYETH--Flavia Helen, scholar, artist, good neighbor, loyal friend, born in Ohio on February 27, 1908 and died at her home in Huntington, NY May 15, 2003. An innovative, dynamic teacher dedicated to excellence in education, she taught English and Humanities at Huntington HS for 30 years. A memorial service will be held June 9, 12:30 PM the United Methodist Church of Huntington, 180 West Neck Rd, Huntington. In lieu of flowers donations to the Art League of LI, 330 Cuba Hill Rd, Huntington, NY 11743, or VNS-Hospice of Suffolk, 505 Main St, Northport, NY 11768, would be appreciated. >>AD#
