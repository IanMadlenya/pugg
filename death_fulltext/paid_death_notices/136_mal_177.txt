
        LEVY-Louis. On May 13, 1998, of Lauderhill, FL, formerly of Brooklyn. ''A mensch to everyone.'' Loving husband of Edith and the late Clara. Adored father of Rabbi Jerrold (Ruth) Levy, Vivian Levy and Sheila (Dr. Harry) Romanowitz. Cherished grandfather of Judith, David and Cherilyn, Ari, Avra and Daniel. Edith's children Joan and Ron, Arthur and Sandy, Jay and Barbara, and their children and grandchildren were like his own. Memorial contributions to your UJA Federation Campaign. Interment 11:15 AM Sunday, May 17, 1998, at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. For further information, Gutterman's Inc., Rockville Centre, Long Island. (516) 764-9400.
