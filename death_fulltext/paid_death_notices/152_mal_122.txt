
        CAMHE-Morris On September 9, 1999. Husband of the late Libby. Beloved father of Jack, Renee, Pamela, Louis, Mona and Samuel. Cherished grandfather of Mace, Todd, Charles, Sara, Robert, Lee, Lauren, Alexis, Melanie and Little Mace. Devoted brother and uncle. He will be missed by the many people whose lives he touched and by the fashion industry in which he was a potent force. Visiting Sunday 7:00-9:00 PM, service Monday, September 13, 1:45 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. Interment immediately following at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. As a tribute to his generous nature contributions may be made to the Sephardic Home, 2266 Cropsey Ave., Brooklyn, NY 11214.
