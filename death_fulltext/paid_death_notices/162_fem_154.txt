
        SWAIN-Jean Adair, 76, of Manhatan, N.Y. on July 17. Smith College, class of 1945. Cellist, pianist, singer, teacher, composer, arranger. Many enduring friendships formed through her chamber music groups. Beloved sister of Nancy Swain Overton. Dear aunt of Richard and Steven Overton and beloved by all who had the privilege of knowing her. Private services were handled through Funeral Service of New Jersey, Inc., 1070 North Broad Street, Hillside, New Jersey. A memorial gathering is planned for this fall.
