
        GARFUNKEL-B. Julian. December 10, 2001 at age 89. Spirited and loving husband of Edith for 65 years; proud father of George and Sandy, Ronald and Sande; loving and involved grandfather of Jon and Betsy, Jill, Stacy and Rob; adoring great-grandfather of Emily and Maddie. Survived by his sister Professor Florence G. Roswell. Beloved uncle to three generations of nieces and nephews. Community activist and founder of Service Directions, Inc. Services at 11:30AM Thursday, December 13 at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pine Brook Blvd, New Rochelle, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to: Global Source Education Network, Inc., P.O. Box 11316, Bainbridge Island, WA 98110 and to The Garfunkel Child and Family Training Institute of North Shore Child and Family Guidance Association, 480 Old Westbury Road, Roslyn Heights, New York 11577.
        GARFUNKEL-B. Julian. The Garfunkel, Wild & Travis, P.C. Family mourns the passing of our friend Julian Garfunkel, father of our partner, George M. Garfunkel. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Julian's wife of 65 years, Edith, to his sons and their wives, George and Sandy, and Ron and Sande, and to the entire family. May their cherished memories of Julian help to lighten their hearts at this sad time.
        GARFUNKEL-B. Julian. The SDI family mourns the loss of our esteemed founder. His vision and entrepreneurial courage founded a company which continues in the spirit of innovation and superior customer service that still flourishes after 50 years. Service Directions, Inc.
