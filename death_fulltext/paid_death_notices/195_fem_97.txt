
        KENNEDY - Lilian Tarbell (Greta). Died peacefully in her sleep at Riddle Memorial Hospital in Media, PA, in the early hours of her 96th birthday on April 10th. Among many charities, Mrs. Kennedy was a state and national officer of the Daughters of the British Empire. She is survived by a brother, the Rev. Canon Albert W. Tarbell, Albuquerque, NM, three sons, Anthony III of Rose Valley, PA, where Mrs. Kennedy lived for over 60 years, Richard T. P., NYC and Rose Valley, Jackson W. T., of Toms River, NJ, and Eaglesmere, PA, six grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Services will be Wednesday, at 11 AM, at Christ Church, 311 South Orange St, Media, PA. Burial will be private at the US Naval Academy, Annapolis, MD.
