
        LEVENSON - Lawrence ''Larry King of Swing.'' Died January 23. Graduate of Nichols College. Owner of Plato's Retreat. Survived by his mother Renee Levenson; his sons Daniel, Michael and Harold; sister Karen; and brother-in-law Edward M. Posner; niece Michele Berman and nephew Charles Posner; and great-nephews Jake and Scott Berman. Services 1 PM, Tuesday, Boca Raton Mausoleum. Gutterman Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton, 1-800992-9262.
