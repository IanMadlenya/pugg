
        WASSMAN - Eleanor Elizabeth Humphrey. Of Larchmont and Brant Lake, NY. Beautiful and beloved wife of E. Robert Wassman. Loving mother of Diane Wassman Darst, E. Robert Wassman, Jr., and Debra Wassman Lanman. Adored grandmother of Elizabeth, David, Jr., Eleanor, Jonathan, Anna Cecelia, and E. Robert Wassman, III. Active community volunteer and eight year regent of the Larchmont Chapter, NSDAR. Funeral service to be held at the Larchmont Avenue Presbyterian Church on Tuesday, July 7, at 4 PM. Donations may be made in her memory to The New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. She lives in our hearts forever.
