
        KING-Harry J. Died December 30, 1997, in his 90th year, husband of the late Fidelia Bettes King, survived by his three children, Joan Dreyfus, Fidelia Rice, John King, and five grandchildren. Fordham Prep '23, Fordham College '29, Fordham Law '33, tennis pro at Sleepy Hollow Country Club, society writer at Herald Tribune.
