
        ZISSON-Gertrude (Rosenhirsch). Of Narragansett, RI, died Wednesday at home. She was the wife of the late Max Zisson. She leaves two sons, Harry and William and daughter-in-law Nancy. She was the grandmother of Alex, Robin and Daniel. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Miriam Hospital Foundation, Box H, Annex Station, Providence, RI 02901.
