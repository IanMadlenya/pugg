
        GINSBURG-Eli. Survived by his wife, Suzan, daughters Ellen, Lynn and Amy and sisters Pat Rudnick and Shirley Engel. Worked fifty years in the magazine publishing industry holding various circulation and marketing positions. Past President of READ and Board Member of the 25 year Club. Family man, friend and baseball ''maven.'' Services will be on March 25, 1998 Temple B'Nai Shalom, 1:30 P.M., East Brunswick, NJ. Rest In Peace.
