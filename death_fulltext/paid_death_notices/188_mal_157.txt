
        CAMPBELL-Donald L., 98. Of Bay Head, New Jersey formerly of Short Hills, NJ died September 14, 2002. Beloved husband of Elizabeth (Duff) Campbell, loving father of Michael Duff Campbell and Mrs. J. Morgan Macom. Also survived by eight grandchildren and nine great grandchildren. Visiting hours will be held on Tuesday from 2-4 & 79 PM at Van Hise & Callagan Funeral Home, 812 Arnold Avenue, Point Pleasant Beach. A funeral mass will be held on Wednesday at Sacred Heart Church, Bay Head. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to Sacred Heart Church, Bay Head, New Jersey.
