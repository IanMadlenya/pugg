
        WARTUR-M. Harry, in Queens age 92. Beloved husband of the late Bertha Stern Wartur. Loving father of C. Lawrence Wartur of Queens, and Susan W. Wolfson of West Haven, CT. Dear father-inlaw of Susan Joy Wartur & Steven Wolfson. Beloved grandfather of Lisa Wartur Cohen and her husband Robin Jay Cohen of Queens & Ellen Wolfson of San Francisco and Roger Wolfson of New York City. Mr. Wartur received his Civil Engineering Degree from Cornell University in 1927 and had a distinguished career, serving as chief engineer in the construction of roads, bridges, highways and shipyards in Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, Connecticut, New Jersey, Egypt, Panama, the Dominican Republic, El Salvador and Santo Domingo. He also conducted projects on railroads, telecommunications, water supply, sewerage and sewer treatment in several states. Mr. Wartur was a licensed professional engineer in New York and New Jersey, and was a Fellow of the American Society of Civil Engineers, a Member of the Moles, and a panelist for the American Arbitration Association. He served as a member of the Board of Directors of the Rego Park Jewish Center. Funeral services will be held at the Parkside Memorial Chapel at 98-60 Queens Blvd, Rego Park at 11:15 A.M. Friday, October 29, 1999. Donations may be made to the Rego ParkJewish Center at 97-30 Queens Blvd, Rego Park, N.Y. 11374.
