
        BERKOWITZ-Lorraine. Of Hampton Bays, N.Y. and Sarasota, FL. Passed away suddenly on Friday morning, August 31, 2001 at Southampton Hospital at the age of 69. Lorraine was a New York City school teacher for 25 years and a long-time resident of Flushing, N.Y. She is survived by her husband Seymour, children Lynn, Ted and her late son, Michael, her daughters-in-law Jo and Lisa, sonin-law Brad, grandchildren Sara, Sam, Greg and Alana, brother Murray and wife Millicent, sister Shirley and husband Leo as well as many nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends in New York and Florida. Lorraine loved to dance, enjoyed reading and traveling, had many interests including a love for Judiasm and family. Services Sunday 1PM at ''Gutterman's Inc.'' 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, L.I. Donations may be made to the Michael Berkowitz Lung Cancer Research Fund, Beth Israel Hospital, NYC, 212-387-6800.
