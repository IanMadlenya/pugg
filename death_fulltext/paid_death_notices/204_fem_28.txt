
        HAUPTMAN -- Selma. 87, passed away December 31, 2003. She is survived by her husband of 64 years, William Hauptman, her son Fred, grandchildren Julia and Matthew, great-grandchildren Jacob, Jasmine and Hannah and her helper Dora. She worked for many years as a geriatric social worker at Montefiore Hospital. Dedicated professional, caring wife and mother, avid reader and bird lover, she will be missed by all who knew her.
