
        MACKIE-Helga Joles. Died on November 23, 1999, in NYC. Born in Vienna, she arrived in this country at age 11 during W.W. II. Beloved wife of Vernon & mother of Joshua. Her memory will live in the hearts of all whose lives she has touched. Funeral Service on Friday, November 26, 10AM Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91st Street.
