
        DOONER--Miriam (''Mimi'') Elizabeth Louise. Died June 4, 2004, at Memorial SloanKettering. She was 26. Most recently, a resident of Santa Cruz, California, she held a B.A. degree from American University in Washington, D.C. and had embarked on a career in graphic design. She was the beloved daughter of John J. Dooner Jr. and Cindy Stewart Dooner and sister of Jaclyn Ann Dooner of Pelham Manor, New York. Granddaughter of Elizabeth Forrest Dooner and Miriam Stewart Van Scoter. A memorial gathering for family and friends to be announced. Memorial donations may be made to American Cancer Society (Research).
