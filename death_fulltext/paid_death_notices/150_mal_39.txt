
        WEINBERG-Yaakov, Rabbi. The Orthodox Union deeply mourns the Rosh Yeshiva of the Ner Israel Rabbinical College of Baltimore. A brilliant intellectual and master of Torah scholarship, his piety, genius, saintly example and fatherly concern leave his many students and all of Klal Yisrael in deep mourning. May the Lord comfort his family and all Israel as He will surely comfort the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Mandell I. Ganchrow, M.D., Pres. Marcel Weber, Chmn., Bd. of Dir. Rabbi Raphael B. Butler, Exec. VP Rabbi Pinchas Stolper, Sr. Exec. Howard Tzvi Friedman, Chairman, NCSY
        WEINBERG-Yaakov, Rabbi. The Rabbinical Council of America mourns the loss of HaRav Yaakov Weinberg, Z'TL, a true Gadol and Marbitz Torah whose teachings and leadership will forever inspire. ''Woe for our great loss''. Rabbi Kenneth Hain, President Rabbi Steven Dworken, Executive Vice President
