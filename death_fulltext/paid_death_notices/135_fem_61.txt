
        DOUGLASS-Rosalind nee Kaplan. Died April 10, 1998, at 83, of Hempstead and Shelter Island, NY. Proprietor of Jay Russell of Garden City and Manhasset from 1937 to 1987. She is survived by her husband, Russell. Beloved mother of Michael and Judy Raboy. Grandmother of Frank, Josh, Amanda and Sara. Memorial service at her home, Monday, April 13, 1 PM.
