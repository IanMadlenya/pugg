
        KAPP--George. 90. Passed away on October 21. Beloved husband of Doris. Devoted father, grandfather, greatgrandfather, brother and companion of Philip, Lucy, Edna and Evelina, David, Janine, Mara, Danny, Tida, Taisen, Tommy, Artie, Ira, June, Natalie, Ray and Rosalie. A lifelong science teacher in the NYC school system. His dedication to education, peace, truth and justice was legendary. He served as mentor to many and will be greatly missed by all. A memorial service will be held at a future date.
