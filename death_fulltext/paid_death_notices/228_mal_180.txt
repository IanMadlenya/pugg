
        KINDRED -- G. Estabrook (Brook). January 7, 2006. A gentleman who loved life, had a sense of humor and a passion for people. He will be missed. He is survived by his wife of 50 years Molly; his daughters Valerie and Dorothy, his grandchildren Molly, Sam, Tabitha and Brook; and brother John. A memorial service will be held at St. James the Less, January 21 at 3:30 P.M. A reception follows at Fox Meadow Tennis Club, Wayside Lane, Scarsdale. Donations may be made to the Platform Tennis Museum & Hall of Fame Foundation, PO Box 413, Scarsdale, NY 10583 or St. James the Less, Crane Rd, Scarsdale.
