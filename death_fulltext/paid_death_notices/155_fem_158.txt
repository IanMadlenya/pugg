
        GILGAN-Lois M. Of Cos Cob, CT. Died December 7 in Greenwich of breast cancer. Born November 29, 1940 in Chicago. Daughter of the late Carl and Lora Zehnle. Graduate of St. Phillip's High School in Battle Creek, MI; alumnae of St. Mary's College in South Bend, IN; Masters degree in Special Education from Manhattanville College, Purchase, NY. She taught at the American Community School in Beirut, Lebanon 1977-1978 and at Greenwich High School 1978-1981. In the 1980s, she worked at Jacobsen and Company in Stamford, CT; Nordico Energy in New York City and Greenwich; and Khouri Capital in Greenwich. In 1991, she began a career with Reuters in New York City as an Account Executive and Project Support Specialist until shortly before her death. A loving and caring mother, devoted to family, friends and Nikki. She held an interest in the arts and humanities and traveled extensively. Other residences include Colorado Springs, CO; Oak Park and Schaumberg, IL. She lived in Greenwich from 1970 until her death. Survived by her son, Joseph C. of Cos Cob, CT; daughter, Jennifer A. of Cincinnati; sister, Carlene Kaniasty of Aurora, CO; and two brothers, Thomas Zehnle of Morrison, CO and Mark Zehnle of Bailey, CO. Predecessed also by her brother, Robert Zehnle. A memorial service is scheduled for 7:30 PM Saturday at the First Congregational Church in Old Greenwich, 108 Sound Beach Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the American Cancer Society, 1599 Clifton Road N.E., Atlanta, GA 30329.
