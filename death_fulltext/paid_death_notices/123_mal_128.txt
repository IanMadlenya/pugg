
        LIEBERMAN-Dr. Joseph D. The Hebrew University and American Friends of the Hebrew University are deeply saddened by the loss of our dear friend. A Benefactor of The Hebrew University, Dr. Lieberman and his late wife, Natalie, established a fund to support the Dental Clinic for Handicapped Children, which bears their name, and for graduate students in the life sciences. A man of compassion and conviction, Joe was devoted to Israel and The Hebrew University. He will be buried on the Mount of Olive in Jerusalem. We extend our sincere condolences to his daughter, Irene, and the entire family. Professor Adam Stabholz, Dean, The Faculty of Dental Medicine American Friends of the Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs, President Lawrence Newman, Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan, Executive Vice President
