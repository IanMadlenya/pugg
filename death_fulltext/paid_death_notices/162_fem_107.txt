
        KOCHEN-Elizabeth (Beth). Of Mamaroneck, NY, and Montreal, Canada, died suddenly on July 15, 2000. Beloved wife of Joseph and mother of Madeline, Neil, Pamela and Allison, and Bubby of Matthew, Jonathan, Jacob, Leah, Rebecca, Jesse and Arden. Daughter of Ida and the late Jacob Kraminer and sister of Bayla, Ted and Mark. Her death leaves an unfillable void in the lives of her family and community. Funeral service at Bet Am Shalom Synagogue, 295 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, on Monday, July 17, at 1 PM.
