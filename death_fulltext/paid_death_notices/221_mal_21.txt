
        LIEBERMAN--William S. The Trustees and staff of the American Federation of Arts are deeply saddened by the death of our good friend and longtime Trustee, William S. Lieberman. We will always be grateful for his guidance and council during his many years of service on AFA's Exhibitions Committee. His achievements during his distinguished career of over half a century enriched the art world and therefore all of our lives. Barbara Weeden, Chairman Gilbert H. Kinney, President Julia Brown, Director
