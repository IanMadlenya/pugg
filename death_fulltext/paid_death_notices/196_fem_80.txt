
        SHAWE-Suzanne. Suddenly passed on May 5, 2003, in NYC. Dearly beloved daughter of Mildred Epstein. Adored mother of Bradley, Brian and Lisa. Cherished grandmother of Denise, Michael and Jennifer, Mallorie, Rachel and Spencer. Devoted wife of the late Arthur Shawe. Her devotion to her family and friends was heartfelt and enduring. Her love of art, expressed with understanding, and sensitivity colored her life and that of the many artists she influenced. Suzanne's spirit will remain in the hearts of all who knew and loved her. To honor her, contributions may be made to the American Folk Art Museum, Whitney Museum, Diabetes Association or a charity of your choice.
