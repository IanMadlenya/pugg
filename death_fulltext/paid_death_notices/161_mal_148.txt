
        HESSE-Frank J. Age 87 of Basking Ridge, New Jersey formerly of Monclair on June 12, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Florence E. (Martin) Hesse who died in 1973. Devoted husband of Lois E. (Liebegott) Hesse, father of Paul Hesse of Beaufort, S.C. Judith Grace of Chester, N.H. stepfather of Victor Myers of Akron, Ohio and Janet Andrus of Columbus, Ohio, grandfather of seven and great grandfather of three. The service will be held Thursday June 15, 2000 at 11:00 A.M. in the Liberty Corner Presbyterian Church, Church Street, Liberty Corner, New Jersey. Interment Rosedale Cemetery, Orange, N.J. Those planning an expression of sympathy are asked to consider contributions in his memory to Fellowship Village Residents Benevolence Fund, 8000 Fellowship Road, Basking Ridge, N.J. 07920 or the Liberty Corner Presbyterian Church Memorial Fund, P.O. Box 55, Liberty Corner, N.J. 07938. Relatives and Friends may visit at the Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home, 101 S. Finley Ave. Basking Ridge Wednesday from 3-7 P.M.
