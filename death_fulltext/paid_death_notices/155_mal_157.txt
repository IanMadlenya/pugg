
        McKENZIE-Thomas, M.D.. The Medical Staff of Metropolitan Hospital Center and the New York Medical College Department of Medicine mourn the passing of our colleague, Thomas McKenzie, MD. Dr. McKenzie served the patients of Metropolitan's East Harlem community as a compassionate, competent primary care physician. He was also a highly regarded teacher and great friend to everyone. He will be deeply missed by his residents, medical students and colleagues. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. The Medical Staff of Metropolitan Hospital Center
