
        EIGERMAN-Hyman. On Saturday, January 24, 1998, in Cambridge, MA, a lifelong New Yorker. For 65 years, the beloved and loving husband of Leah. Always caring father of Daniel, Joel and Sarah. Loving, proud and supportive grandfather of Hannah, Nathan, Edward and Jared. Teacher, Scholar, Educator, Raconteur, Chessmaster, Punster and Dogged Controversialist. Author of ''The Poetry of Dorothy Wordsworth,'' 1940. He leaves his work and the family he created. A memorial service will be held at a later date. For more information call Levine Chapel in Brookline, MA.
