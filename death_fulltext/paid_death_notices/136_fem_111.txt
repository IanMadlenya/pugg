
        TOBEY-Virginia Harrison. Died peacefully on May 16, 1998, at age 93. Devoted wife of the late William Tobey. Loving mother of Charles, Jane (Addis) and Paul. Dear sister of Gladys Scroggins. Mother-in-law of Judy and Ronnie. Grandmother of Jackie, Emily, Deborah, Jesse, Michael, Eric and Brett, and great-grandmother of William, Justin and Lily. She touched all our lives with her warmth, generosity and sense of humor. We will always be inspired by her creativity. Services Wednesday, May 20, 2:45 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be made to the American Parkinsons Disease Foundation.
