
        LEVY--Michael H. Founder and former CEO of the Standard Security Life Insurance Company of NY, died from complications resulting from a reaction to a diagnostic injection on February 25. At age 92, Mike was still working full time as a Financial Planner. Mike was a highly decorated Ranger Captain in WWII and a member of the 1932 US Olympic swim team. A man of encyclopedic knowledge and indomitable spirit, Mike was also a passionate civil rights crusader and environmental conservationist. Mike leaves behind his beloved wife of 48 years, Helen, his daughters Michel & Barbara and his much adored grandsons Jon, Keith and Ben.
