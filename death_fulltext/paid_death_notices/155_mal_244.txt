
        GREENWAY-Gilbert C. 88, of Nassau, Bahamas, and formerly of Washington, DC, died on December 17, 1999, at home in Lyford Cay. He is survived by his son Lauder and his daughters, Cooby Greenway and Sara O'Dea, and eight grandchildren. His eldest son, Gilbert C., Jr. died in 1981, and his wife Lucia T. died in 1994. Burial will be on January 15, 2000; memorial services to be determined. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to Lyford Cay Foundation, POB N7776, Nassau, Bahamas.
