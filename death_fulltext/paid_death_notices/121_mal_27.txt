
        WORTZEL-Murray N. Passed away Jan. 30, 1997 after a heart attack. Cherished brother of Bertha Belmar and Esther Friedfeld. He was a professor of Library Science at Lehman College and founder of Leonard Eisner Memorial Foundation. He devoted his time and efforts to the Special Libraries Association and Kurt Weill Foundation. Services today, 10 AM, ''Kirschenbaum's'', 1153 Coney Island Ave., Bklyn. Donations may be made to the Leonard Eisner Memorial Foundation.
