
        SPRITZER-Marjorie. The President, Chancellor, Boards of Governors and Overseeers, faculty, administration and students of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion record with profound sorrow the tragic death of Mrs. Marjorie Spritzer, a dedicated and devoted member of the Board of Governors and New York Board of Overseers. She was a tenacious advocate for the wellbeing of Reform Judaism and her commitment to Jewish education and the needs of her Miami Jewish community were well known to all who knew her. Her broad, winsome smile embraced both old and new friends. To her dear husband Michael, their children Jeffrey and Blaire, her mother Anne and her family, we extend our heartfelt condolences. May Marjorie's memory be a blessing for us all. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman, President Dr. Alfred Gottschalk, Chancellor Burton Lehman, Chair, Board of Governors Robert M. Heller, Chair, NY Board of Overseers
        SPRITZER-Marjorie. With profound sorrow, the Officers and Board of Trustees of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations mourn the death of a devoted Vice Chair and cherished friend. A vital and vivacious leader, Marjorie gave of her time and talents with a loving heart and caring concern. She taught us all the meaning of courage in her valiant struggle against grave illness. We grieve with her bereaved family and convey heartfelt sympathy to Michael, their children, and all her loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President Rabbi Alexander M. Schindler, President Emeritus
