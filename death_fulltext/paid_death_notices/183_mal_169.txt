
        KATZ-Leonard R. Of New York passed away at his Florida home on April 4, 2002. He was 81 years old. Loving son of Rebecca and William Katz. Founder of Katz's Delicatessen. Leonard Katz owned and managed Katz's until his retirement. A Captain in the U.S. Air Force, he was a decorated officer and prisoner of war. He is survived by his beloved wife Gladys Katz and his four children, Willa Fasten of Northampton, MA, Richard Katz of Milwaukee, WI, Vicki Lapinsky of Baltimore MD. and Robert Katz of New York. Devoted grandfather to his eight grandchildren, he will be missed by everyone who knew him.
