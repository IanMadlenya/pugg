
        RICKS--David F. 77, died at home, April 13. Professor of psychology at Harvard, Brandeis, Columbia, Kabul University, City College, and Cincinnati. Survived by wife Annie, who will remain in Shepherdstown, WV; and by his children, David, Peggy, Tom, Anne, Sarah, their spouses, his son Rick's widow J. E., and by 13 grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
