
        MURRAY -- Dorothy (nee Stephan). On Tuesday, August 3, 2004. Beloved sister of Frank Stephan and the late Louise Howe and Bertha Ferguson. Also survived by ten loving nieces and nephews, 19 great-nieces and nephews, 24 great-great-nieces and nephews and several friends. Ms. Murray was a resident of the Barbizon Hotel for 24 years prior to coming to Flusing in 2000. She retired as an executive secretary with the Financial World Magazine in 1993. Funeral Mass was celebrated on August 7 at St Andrew Avellino RC Church, Flushing, NY. Arrangements were handled by Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 14920 Northern Blvd., Flushing, NY.
