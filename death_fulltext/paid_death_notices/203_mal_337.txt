
        RIPSTEIN--Charles, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center is saddened by the death of Dr. Charles Ripstein. Dr. Ripstein was the first full time Director of Surgery at Maimonides Medical Center. He was renowned as a cardiac surgeon as well as a general surgeon. He was an excellent teacher of both the attending staff and the house staff. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Herbert Lehman, M.D., Pres. Ira Mayer, M.D., Pres. Elect Warren Wexelman, M.D., Sec Steven Farber, M.D., Treas.
