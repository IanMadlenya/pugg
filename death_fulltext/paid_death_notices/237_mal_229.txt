
        BRESLAUER--Joel C., on October 17, 2006. Survived by beloved wife Miriam, his son K'thiel (Keith.), daughter-inlaw Lauren, grandchildren Arianna, Sophia, Samantha and Aaron and sister Susan Lee Nelson. Mr. Breslauer was a devoted father and husband, scholar and friend to many. Originally from NYC, he was active in the real estate industry in the US and Europe. He was involved for many years in building Jewish communities across America and was a dear friend of the Yemenite Jewish community. He helped people from all walks of life and people felt better about themselves by simply knowing him. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends.
