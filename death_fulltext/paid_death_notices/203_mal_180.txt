Funeral services at Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims, Orange St between Hicks and Henry Sts, Brooklyn Heights, Tuesday 4pm. Contributions in his memory may be made to Plymouth Church, 75 Hicks St, Brooklyn, NY 11201 or the Brooklyn Red Cross, 100 Pineapple Walk, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
