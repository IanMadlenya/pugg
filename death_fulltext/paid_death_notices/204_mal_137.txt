
        MALINOVICH--Stanley. Retired professor of philosophy at Brooklyn College, passed away suddenly and peacefully on January 7 after battling with early onset Parkinson's Disease for thirty years. Like a true philosopher, his values shaped his life. He handled his disabilities with great courage. He was a passionate and dedicated teacher and scholar, and a wonderful human being. His daughters Nadia and Alisa, and other family members and friends will miss him greatly. Funeral services will be at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th at Amsterdam Avenue at 1 p.m. on Sunday, January 11. Donations in his memory can be made to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, Columbia University Medical Center, 710 West 168th Street, New York, N.Y. 10032.
