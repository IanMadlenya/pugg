
        BLUESTONE--Harvey, M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of its professor emeritus of psychiatry and behavorial sciences. He was a devoted physician dedicated to providing high-quality care to the underprivileged, as well as a beloved mentor of several generations of psychiatrists. His sweet gentility and uncommon wisdom will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Eleanor, and the entire Bluestone family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University $(6$)BLUESTONE--Harvey, M.D. The Mental Health Association of New York City mourns the loss of Harvey Bluestone, M.D., esteemed member of the Board of Directors for more than 50 years. His compassion and dedication to the MHA of NYC was a reflection of his deep, tireless commitment to helping people with mental illness. He was a leader in the field, serving as Chair of the Association's Professional Advisory Committee for over 25 years. His wisdom and guidance will be missed by all. Our deepest condolences to the Bluestone family and the entire mental health community.
