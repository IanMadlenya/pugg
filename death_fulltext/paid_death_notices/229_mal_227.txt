
        OHLSSON--Alvar. Died February 17, 2006, of melanoma. Born in Jonkoping, Sweden, he became a US citizen and worked at Electrolux as manager until retirement. He also worked briefly with AGA Industries of Sweden, then headed up CAG ONE Skate Sharpeners. Avid tennis player, proud of partnering the former King of Sweden. His ashes will be returned home to Sweden. He is survived by his wife Paula and son Garrick. In lieu of flowers, a donation to Phelps Hospice, Phelps Memorial Hospital Center, 701 North Broadway, Sleepy Hollow, NY 10591, is welcomed with appreciation for their help in this sad time.
