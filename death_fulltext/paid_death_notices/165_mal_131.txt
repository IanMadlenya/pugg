
        COLBERT-Rev. Joseph D. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of St. Pancras Church, 72-22 68th Street, Glendale on October 10. Reposing at George Werst Funeral Home, 71-41 Cooper Ave, Glendale, Wednesday, October 11, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM and Thursday, October 12, from 2 to 5 PM. Mass of Transferral at St. Pancras Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass on Friday, October 13, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
