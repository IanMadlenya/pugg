
        BALLOFFET--Circe N. (nee de Rioja). Died May 26, 2003, at home in Tucson, AZ, where she had retired in 1995. She was 84, a native of Argentina who lived, for many years, in Hastings on Hudson, NY. Her husband, Armando Balloffet, passed away in 1992. She is survived by seven children, 18 grandchildren and three great - grandchildren. Circe was an avid reader, music lover and accomplished needlewoman, and she was a mentor for her grandchildren and their friends. Interment will be in Westchester County, NY. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Guiding Eyes for the Blind, Inc, 611 Granite Springs Rd, Yorktown Hts, NY 10598. >>AD#
