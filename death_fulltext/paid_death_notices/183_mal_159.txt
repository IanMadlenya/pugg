
        TRESCHER-Robert Lincoln. Age 89, suddenly on April 10. Loving husband of Glendora (nee Rollins). Loving father of Victoria Agnich of Dallas, Texas; Ellen Haas of Kirkland, WA. Grandfather of four. A memorial service will be held Monday, April 15, 2 PM at the Bryn Mawr Presbyterian Church, 625 Montgomery Ave, Bryn Mawr, PA 19010. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made in his memory to The Law School of University of Pennsylvania, 34th & Chestnut Streets, Philadelphia, PA 19104, or the University of Pennsylvania Museum of Archeology and Anthropology, 33rd & Spruce Streets, Philadelphia, PA 19104. For information contact Stuard Funeral Home, Ardmore, PA.
