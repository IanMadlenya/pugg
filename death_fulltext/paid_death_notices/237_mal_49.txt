
        DANA-BASHIAN--Phillip. Age 72, on September 19, 2006, of cancer. Beloved husband of Mary Beth Halpin, father of John (Rebecca Dietzel) and Alexis. Brother of John DanaBashian, Joan Rappleyea, Pauline Dana-Bashian and Helen Moore. Son of the late John and Helen (Paddock) Dana-Bashian. He was one of the founders of, and President of Pension Parameters, Inc., a pension administration and investment firm located in New York City and Aberdeen, NJ. Born in Brookline, MA, Phillip received his undergaduate degree from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and an MBA from Columbia University. He and Mary Beth lived in New York City and Tivoli, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice Foundation, Inc., 374 Violet Ave, Poughkeepsie, NY 12601, Milan Rescue Squad, 15 Eagle Lane, Clinton Corners, NY 12514 or American Cancer Society, 45 Reade Pl, Poughkeepsie, NY 12601.
