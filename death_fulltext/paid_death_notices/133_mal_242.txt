
        DUFF-William. Died February 9, 1998 in Shelter Island. He was born in New Castle, Pa. on September 17, 1916. His daughter, Christina, pre-deceased him. He is survived by his son, Alexander Duff, and his daughter, Fiona Duff, both of New York City, and by his former wife, Odile Utard Duff of New York City and Shelter Island. A memorial service will be held at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, officiated by Fred Anderson, Pastor, on Friday, February 20 at noon.
