
        RANSON-Maria D. On Thursday, December 9, 1999 at 5PM, Maria, the loved, adored and amazing wife of Gerald Ranson, passed away. She is survived by her two sons, Matthew and Timothy and her loving mother Kathleen. Friends and family mourn our loss. There will be a memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, West 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC, Thursday, December 16th, 12:45PM. In lieu of flowers, a fund has been set up in her name at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Pediatric Department.
