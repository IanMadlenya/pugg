
        LIPKIND--Camilla. 95, of Sunrise, FL, formerly of Bronx, NY, passed away on October 26, 2006. She is survived by her loving daughter Bernice (Sam) Greenberg and pre-deceased by her son Steven Lipkind, survived by her daughter-in-law , Marilyn Lipkind; four cherished grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Camilla will be sadly missed by all who knew and loved her. Chapel services at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Blvd. Queens, NY (718-896-9000), at 12 noon, Sunday, October 29. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. For information: Star of David, N. Lauderdale, FL.
