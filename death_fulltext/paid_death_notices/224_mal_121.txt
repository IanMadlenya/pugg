
        FOSTER--Donald L. On September 7, 2005. Retired Assistant Conductor at the Metropolitan Opera, composer, pianist, and accompanist. He will be sorely missed by his many friends and colleagues. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions in Donald's memory may be made to: Save the Met Broadcasts Campaign, The Metropolitan Opera, Lincoln Center, NY, NY 10023 or Lambda Legal, 120 Wall St, Suite 1500, NY, NY 10005.
