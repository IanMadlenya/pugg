
        REA--Vice Admiral William Freeland III, United States Coast Guard, passed away December 11, 2004, Coral Gables, Florida. VADM Rea had a long and wonderful career, with his last command being Commander Atlantic Area United States Coast Guard, Governors Island, New York. He is survived by his wife, Ruth Jackson Friend Rea, and his three children, Bernardine Friend Rea, Amelia Ruth Rea Maguire and William Sheip Rea. Funeral services will be held at Arlington National Cemetery on Thursday, April 28, 2005 at 1:00 p.m. The family has requested in lieu of flowers a donation be made to: VADM William F. Rea III Marine Safety Scholarship Endowment, Coast Guard Foundation, Inc., 394 Taugwonk Rd, Stonington, CT 06378-1807.
