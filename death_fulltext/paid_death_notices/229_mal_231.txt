
        CAPUTO--Daniel V., 72. Emeritus Professor, Queens College, died of a heart attack on Thursday, February 16. He was Chair of the Psychology Dept. from 1974 to 1977, a Fellow of the New York Academy of Science, and he maintained a practice in clinical psychology. His research was in the area of family processes among schizophrenics and developmental issues associated with neonatology and prematurity. He is survived by his life partner, Ernest Cusson, and brothers, Frank and Joe.
