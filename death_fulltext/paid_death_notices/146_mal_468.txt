
        MOROSS-Charles M.. of White Plains, NY, died on March 25, 1999 at the age of 77. Born on July 1, 1921 in NYC. Survived by his beloved wife Lila (Kravis) of 49 years. He is also survived by his daughter Barbara Moross and her husband, Renaud Da, and his loving grandchildren, Melissa, Jeremy and Frederic Da, and his beloved nieces Susanna, Sonya and Gladys. He was a theatrical payroll manager for Handley-Walker, Inc., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations to American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Pl., White Plains, NY 10601.
