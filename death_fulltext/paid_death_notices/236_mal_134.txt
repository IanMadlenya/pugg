
        TEITELBAUM--Irving, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Yonkers. Died September 9 peacefully at home with his loving wife Eve at his bedside. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, uncle, and brother to Pauline Halpern and Marvin Tyson. Master bridge player, believer in social justice and passionate follower of world events. Above all, he loved his family who will sorely miss him.
