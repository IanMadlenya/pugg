
        DELANO-Thalia Holmes, 78, native of Cleveland, Ohio, died July 18, 2000, in Jacksonville, Florida following a brief illness. Mrs. Delano is survived by her husband, Frederic Adrian Delano II, two daughters, Daphne Broun and Diana Gardner; two grandchildren, Dawn Broun and Rod Gardner; and one great-grandchild, Brittany Hanley. A Memorial service will be held today in Jacksonville. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Mark's Episcopal Church, 4129 Oxford Avenue, Jacksonville, Florida 32210.
