
        PAULSEN-Harold Midskaug. Age 66, of Staten Island, NY, died Friday, August 25, 2000, at home following a long, courageous battle with prostate cancer. Beloved husband of Gloria. Devoted father of Nancy Baione, Marylinda Schoifet, Geralyn, Michael and Kenneth Paulsen. Survived by eleven grandchildren and one greatgrandson. Visitation on Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9:30 PM, at the Richmond Funeral Home, Staten Island. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Patrick's Church, Richmondtown, Staten Island, on Tuesday, August 29, 2000, 10 AM. Interment Moravian Cemetery, Staten Island. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name can be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Prostate Cancer Research Division, 1275 York Ave., NYC 10021.
