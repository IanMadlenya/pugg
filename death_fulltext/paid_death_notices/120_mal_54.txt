
        FRIEDMAN - Isaac S. M.D. on January 5, 1997. Outstanding and dedicated physician, scholar and Professor of Medicine; ardent and generous friend of Israel and of the cause of peace and justice throughout the world. Beloved husband, brother and uncle. Survived by his devoted wife Ruth Broun Friedman and by his sister Shirley Vinacour. He deeply touched the lives of those who knew him. Graveside services Wednesday, January 8, 1997, 2PM at Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, NJ. Memorial services Sunday January 12, 1997 11AM at Riverside-Gordon, 7205 West Alantic Avenue, Deiray Beach, FL.
