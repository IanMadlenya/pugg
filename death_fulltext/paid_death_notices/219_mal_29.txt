
        McGOVERN--Peter J., 90, of Westport, CT. Publicist, died March 31. Beloved husband of Phyllis, father of Scott and Josefina McGovern, Michael and Paula McGovern, grandfather of Jillian. For the past years wrote a weekly column for the Minuteman newspaper. He attended Hartwick College in Oneonta, NY, and during WWII served as Major assigned to the War Department. For over 25 years was personal publicist to comedian Jackie Gleason. In lieu of flowers, a contribution to the Westport Public Library would be appreciated.
