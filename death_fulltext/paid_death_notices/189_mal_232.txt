
        GOLDMAN-Sidney. Of Delray Beach, FL and Quogue, NY, passed away Thursday, October 17, in Baltimore, MD. A native of NYC, he served his country in the Air Force during WWII and was a passionate volunteer, as well as being an executive and, late in life, a professional singer. Sid was the devoted husband of Gloria and beloved brother of Frances Eberman. He was the loving father/father-inlaw of Neil Harold and Diane Goldman, Charles Mitchell and Susan Goldman, and the ardent supporter of his grandchildren, Jenna, Andrew, Matthew and Rachel. A private funeral service will be held on Sunday, October 20. Donations in lieu of flowers can be made to Hope Lodge, 636 West Lexington Street, Baltimore, MD. 21201.
