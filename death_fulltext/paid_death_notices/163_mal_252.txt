
        OWEN-Neil. Age 48, died suddenly on August 16, 2000, while hiking the Appalachian Trail in Maine. Beloved husband, partner and best friend of Dee. Cherished brother of Roy and Nell. Devoted uncle of Matthew, Keri, Garth, Evan and Lee. Much loved brother in-law of Guy and Elisa. Treasured friend of Greg and Kenlyn. Neil's intelligence, humor and integrity touched the lives of innumerable friends, colleagues, clients and fellow mountain hikers. All those who knew and loved Neil will profoundly miss his wisdom and guidance. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, October 15, at 2:30 PM, at The Wainwright House in Rye, NY.
