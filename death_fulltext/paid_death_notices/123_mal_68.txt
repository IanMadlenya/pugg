
        BREWER-Orlando Swift. Age 99, died at his home in Brookline, VT on March 12th. He retired as Senior Partner in Phelps, Fenn & Co. in 1963, having joined the firm in 1922. He is survived by his daughter, Prudence Read, his son, James E. Brewer, 6 grandchildren, and 11 great-grandchildren.
