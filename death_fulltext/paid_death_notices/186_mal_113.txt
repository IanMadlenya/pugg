
        CONSTANTINER-Dr. Jaime. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University deeply mourn the passing, in Mexico, of Dr. Jaime Constantiner, Deputy Chairman of Tel Aviv University's Board of Governors. Dr. Constantiner's dedication to Israel and his support of Tel Aviv University will always be remembered. American Friends of Tel Aviv University extends sincere condolences to his family. Alan Aufzien, Chairman Sam Witkin, Exec V.P.
