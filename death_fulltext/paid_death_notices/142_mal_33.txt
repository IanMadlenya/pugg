
        SILVERMAN-Jack. Age 81, of Hollywood, FL., and New York. Passed away Nov. 1, 1998 after a sudden illness. Beloved husband of the late Florence. Loving father of Bonnie and Jeffrey Silverman and Joan and Ronald Silverman. Dear brother of Francine Edelson. Cherished grandmother of Susanna, Valerie, Daniel and Hallie. Devoted companion of Jean Cantor. World traveler, bon vivant. He will be missed by all of us. Services are at Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels, Hollywood, Florida, 1-800343-5400.
