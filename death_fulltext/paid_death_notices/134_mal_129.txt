
        ISRAEL-Rosa. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a respected member of London's Jewish community and dedicated Zionist, beloved sister of Romie (and Blanche) Shapiro, member, Board of Directors, of YU's Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law. Mr. and Mrs. Shapiro are longtime devoted leaders of YU and its schools, YU Guardians who also established CSL's Romie Shapiro Program in International Law and Human Rights. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        ISRAEL-Rosa. The Board and staff of The American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum, Jerusalem, are deeply saddened by the death of Rosa Israel, sister of our fellow Board member and Honorary Chairman, Romie Shapiro. We extend condolences to Romie, Blanche and the entire family. Judy Steinhardt President, A.F.I.M. Teddy Kollek, President, I.M.
        ISRAEL-Rosa. The Board of Directors of America-Israel Cultural Foundation extends its heartfelt sympathies to Executive Committee Co-Chair, Romie Shapiro, on the loss of his beloved sister. Jan Prokop-Hamburg Co-Chair, Exec. Committee Vera Stern, President
