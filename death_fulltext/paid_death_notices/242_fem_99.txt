
        COLEMAN--Julia Montgomery, (age 85) of Washington D.C. on March 10th, 2007 following a stroke suffered the previous day. Born in Tuxedo Park, NY, the daughter of Henry Eglinton Montgomery and Florence Gayley Montgomery. Graduate of St. Timothy's School and Sarah Lawrence College. Served in the Office of Strategic Services in Rome, Italy 1944-1946. Her first husband, James Alexander Seymour died in a plane crash in 1955. She is survived by her husband Francis I.G. Coleman, her daughter Anne Coleman, and sons Peter and Christopher Seymour, and Bruce and Craig Coleman. She is also survived by her sister Sylvia Montgomery Erhart of New York City, and eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in June at St. Mary's By the Sea in Northeast Harbor, ME, followed by burial there. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to St. Timothy's School, 8400 Greenspring Avenue, Stevenson, MD 21153.
