
        HEUTLINGER--Fay S. In her 93rd year, Fay died peacefully on September 28th in Boca Raton, FL. She was the mother of Ruth H. and Paul Eisen and Mark and Bonnie Heutlinger; grandmother of Andrew, Jonathan, and Gregg Eisen; great-grandmother of four. Formerly a fashion consultant in Newark and West Orange, New Jersey, Fay was a woman of elegance and style, intelligence and independence.
