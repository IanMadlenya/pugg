
        MARGOLIS-Gladys Belinker (nee Haber). On December 1, 2000, at the age of 92. Beloved mother of Dr. Robert Margolis (Barbara) and Barbara Sheerin (Ray). Loving grandmother of Lisa Melartin (Jussi), Michael Waitzman, Dr. Jennifer Fryer (Dr. Eric) and Steven Margolis (Jenifer). Adoring great-grandmother of six. Cherished aunt and sister, caring friend. Her adventurous, loving spirit and inquisitive mind will remain an inspiration to all who knew her. We will love and miss her always.
