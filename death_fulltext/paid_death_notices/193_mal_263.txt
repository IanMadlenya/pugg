
        ALLEN-Alton K. Died peacefully on February 22. Loving husband of Silvia. Devoted father of Harvey, Lois and Donna. Dear brother of Rosalind and brother-in-law of Murry. Beloved uncle of many nieces and nephews. Founder of A.K. Allen Corp. and Allenair Products. Friend and mentor to his many longtime, devoted employees. Services Monday, 12 noon, at ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite the LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
