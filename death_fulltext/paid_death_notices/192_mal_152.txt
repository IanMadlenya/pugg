
        SUSARRET-David Matias. Born in El Barrio of New York City on April 18, 1954 sadly passed away on December 18, 2002 in Santa Cruz, California. David was CEO of his own company, Caribe Submarine and Satellite Technologies. El Centro, The Puerto Rican studies department at Hunter College and Casita Maria, a neighborhood house in Spanish harlem, benefitted from David's philanthropic endeavors. He is survived by his mother Edith Susarret Marrero and his five siblings, Manuel, Ruth, Delia, Chola and Daniel. ''Doc Sus'' inspired and was loved by everyone whose life he touched.
