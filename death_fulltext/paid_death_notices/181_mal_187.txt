
        HALPERN-Sol. Beloved husband of Freda. Loving father of Gail Winston Rudin (Frank), Marvin Max Halpern (Sandra), Judith Cohen (Edward). Devoted grandfather to Cary, Donna, Adam, Tricia, Dahlia, Rachel, Jocelyn, Jody and Emily. Cherished great-grandfather to Jake, Alex, Bailey and Sophie. Dear twin-brother to Herman and Dove. Loved brother-in-law to Bernard Cooperstein and Florence Halpern. Sol died of complications of diabetes. He served in the U.S. Navy in WWII. He was the Founder and President of H and S Tool and Mold Corp. from 1952 until 1986 when he retired. He was a founding member of the Lake Success Jewish Center. He loved fresh water fishing all his life and was a hunter as well. His stories will always be remembered. Sol will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Services today 12 noon at ''Gutterman's'', 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I.
