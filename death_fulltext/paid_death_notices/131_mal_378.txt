
        OSIS-Dr. Karlis. The Board, staff and members of the American Society for Psychical Research mourn the passing of Dr. Karlis Osis, Research Director Emeritus. Dr. Osis was an internationally renowned psychical researcher who conducted landmark experiments at the American Society for Psychical Research, exploring outof-the-body experiences and the question of life after death. He was a visionary whose kindness and wisdom will be missed but not forgotten. He is survived by his wife, three children, eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday in NJ. American Society for Psychical Research Patrice Keane Executive Director
