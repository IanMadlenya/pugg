
        ASKER-Gunnar. The Board and staff of Young Audiences/New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Gunnar Asker, beloved husband of Emeritus Board Member, past President and longtime arts education advocate, Louise Kjellstrom Asker. Gunnar's warmth, generosity and passion for learning will be remembered always. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family as they mourn the loss of this truly extraordinary man.
