
        DEFENDORF-Charles E. On January 13, 2002, of North Salem, NY at the age of 89. Former president and CEO of Seelye, Stevenson, Value & Knecht and former chief engineer of the Penn Central Railroad. Survived by his wife of 53 years, Beatrice, and sons, Charlie, Richard and Robert and one grandson, Charles, Jr. Reposing Waterbury & Kelly Funeral Home, 1300 Pleasantville Rd, Briarcliff Manor, New York on Wednesday, 2-4 & 7-9PM. Services Scarborough Presbyterian Church, Route 9 at Scarborough Road, Scarborough, on Thursday, January 17, at 10AM. Interment Ferncliff Cemetery Mausoleum.
