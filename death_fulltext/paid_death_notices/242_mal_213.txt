
        BRONFMAN--Naum. The loss of husband, father and grandfather Naum Bronfman is mourned by his loving family members Polina, Elizabeth, Yefim, Yael, Daniel and many other relatives and friends both in the United States and Israel. Mr. Bronfman died at the age of 95 on Tuesday morning, March 20, 2007. He is greatly missed by all whose lives he touched. A memorial service was held for Mr. Bronfman on Thursday, March 22, 2007 at 11:30am at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, in Manhattan.
