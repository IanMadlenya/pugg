
        KAUFMANN--Elsa (Steiner). Peacefully passed away at age 99 on August 20, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Milton, loving mother of Mark and wife Carole and Donald and wife Lillian. Devoted grandmother of Jon and wife Amy; Susan and husband Alan Lewis; Andrew; Jeffrey and wife Randi; and Rachel. Also survived by great-grandchildren: Melissa, Sophia, Theodora, Adam, Julia and Lauren. ''Nana'' will be greatly missed for her strength, wisdom and unconditional love. Services Monday, August 22nd, at 11:45am at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to: The Sisterhood of Temple Israel of the City of NY, or the Jacob Perlow Hospice of Beth Israel.
