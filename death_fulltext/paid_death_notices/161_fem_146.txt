
        KAUFMAN-Greta. Died May 30, 2000 after a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Howard. Adored, cherished and devoted mother of Laurie. Dear sister of Harold Hoffman. Always a New Yorker in heart and spirit. A unique personality, she will be remembered for her indomitable will in facing life's challenges, and her endearing loyalty and strength of character. Three kisses mommie.
