
        GEIGER--Jeanne. On February 18, 2005, after her heroic battle against catastrophic injuries suffered in a tragic accident. Adored and adoring wife of Julian; unconditionally loving mother of Teya, David, Rachel and Michael; proud grandmother of Nathan; caring sister of Robert and Alan Muller; devoted and generous friend of so many. Visionary designer and creator of blue, the inn on the beach, Plum Island, MA. Services to be announced. For those so inclined, contributions to The Women's Crisis Center, 2 Harris St., Newburyport, MA. 01950, would be appropriate in lieu of flowers.
