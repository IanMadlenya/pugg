
        NAIDICH--Rose Bitko. Died September 14, 2005, age 90. Beloved wife of the late James Naidich, sister of Evelyn Rubinowitz, devoted mother of doctors James B. (Joy) and Thomas P. (Michele) Naidich, adored grandmother of Michelle, Stephen, Nathaniel and Sandra Naidich, great grandmother of Scott and Mark Miller, and revered teacher of generations of school children in Queens and Nassau County. For nearly 20 years, she was an active participant and group leader in PEIR (Professionals and Executives in Retirement) at Hofstra University. Her family and her students are her legacy.
