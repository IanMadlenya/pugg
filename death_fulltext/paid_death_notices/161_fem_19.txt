
        FOWLER - Dorothy Ganfield, Waukesha, WI formerly of London Terrace Apartments, 460 W. 24th Street, New York, died Saturday, May 27, 2000. She was born July 2, 1902. She was predeceased by her mother and father, sisters: Jane Ganfield of Lafayette, Indiana and Elizabeth Ganfield Laatsch of Waukesha, brother A. Boardman Ganfield of Lake Forest, California and her husband, Emmett in 1992. She is survived by a sister Ruth Ganfield of Manitowoc, WI and a nephew William Ganfield Laatsch of Sturgeon Bay, WI. Memorial Services will be held July 2, 2000 at 4:00 P.M. at the First Presbyterian Church of The City of New York, 5th Avenue at 12th Street. The Rev. Dr. James G. Speer will officiate.
