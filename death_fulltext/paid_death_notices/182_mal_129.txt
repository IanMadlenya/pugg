
        LUBOW-Sol B., Bronx, NY, passed away on March 7, 2002. Mr. Lubow is a retired NYC educator. He taught at Stuyvesant H.S. in NYC and Columbus H.S. in the Bronx. He held the position of principal at P.S. 168 Manhattan and P.S. 105 Bronx. He is survived by his sisterin-law, Mildred Lubow; beloved niece, Susan Chorman, and her husband, Jeffrey, and his grandnieces and nephews. He was also a dear friend of Mary T. O'Malley. He was predeceased by his brothers, Irving & Benjamin, and sister, Gus, and a niece, Barbara Leinwand, and a cousin, Carl Erdberg. Services will be held graveside at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, NY, on Sunday, March 10, 12 Noon.
