
        LUBELL--Arthur, on December 14th, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Mary. Loving father of Ruth Tallury, Diane Hochner and Lewis Lubell. Cherished grandfather. Services Tuesday, 10 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        LUBELL--Arthur. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Arthur Lubell. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at the Riverside Memorial Chapel on Tuesday, December 16 at 10 AM. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebo, Exec.
