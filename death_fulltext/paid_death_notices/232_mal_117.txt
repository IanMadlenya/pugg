
        NAPOLITANO--John Alexander. Of New York, NY, passed away on May 13, 2006. He was the loving husband of Beth Forman, father of Gabrielle Napolitano-Swift, son of Johanna Napolitano, brother of Joy Elkort and Jeffrey Napolitano, grandfather of Chloe and Dante Swift, uncle to Jacqueline and Zachary Elkort and Anthony and Donna Napolitano, friend to Charle Caffiero and so many more. He touched all of our lives in so many ways, from his passion for his work, his friends, his family and the environment around him. We will never forget his sense of humor and his individual style. For more information contact Perazzo Funeral Home, NY, NY. Donations may be sent to the American Cancer Society.
