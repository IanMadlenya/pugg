
        PENKOWER-Rabbi Murry. AMIT is deeply saddened by the passing of the beloved husband of our esteemed national JNS representative and national board member, Lillian S. Penkower. A revered rabbinical and Jewish communal leader, Rabbi Penkower was dedicated to the advancement of Torah and religious Zionism and was a proud supporter of our AMIT children in Israel. Heartfelt condolences to his devoted wife, daughters, sons and family. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Director
