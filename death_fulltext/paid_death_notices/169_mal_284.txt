
        EDELMAN-Jordan. 76, on February 25, 2001 after a valiant struggle with lung cancer. Jordan Edelman was born in the Bronx on November 25, 1924. Beloved son of Abraham and Sylvia Edelman, devoted brother of Theo Heller, adored father of Abbe Hesketh and Marti Christianson and cherished grandfather of Joel, Leah, Beth and Samuel. Educated at the University of Pittsburgh and Harvard University, Jordan received his PhD in Psychology. A US army veteran of World War II, he was an administrator of mental health at Anna State Hospital in Anna, IL. He was blessed with a wonderful sense of humor, a warm and caring nature and will be missed by all of us who loved him.
