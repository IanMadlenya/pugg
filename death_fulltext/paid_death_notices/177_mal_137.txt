
        MEISELS-Dr. Morton, 86. Died with dignity on October 7, 2001. Beloved husband of Emmy. Loving father of Penina, Peter, David, Michael, Steven and Susan. Devoted grandfather of 7 grandchildren. Graduate of Cornell University School of Veterinary Medicine, Class of 1941. Served as a Captain in the U.S. Army during W.W. II. Founder of Meisels Animal Hospital. All will his insight, advice and guidance. A memorial service will be announced. In lieu of flowers make donations to Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Avenue, Montgomery, AL. 36104.
