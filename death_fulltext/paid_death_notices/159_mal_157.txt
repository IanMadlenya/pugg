
        ADSIT-Willcox Brown, retired Executive Vice President of General Motors Acceptance Corporation (New York), died on Sunday, April 9, at his home in Mountain Lake, Lake Wales, Florida. Mr. Adsit was 90 years old. He was in charge of Borrowings, Overseas Operations and Public Relations. Mr. Adsit was born in Baltimore, Maryland, the son of Dr. Henry Adsit and Peachey Polythress Brown Adsit. He spent his early years in Buffalo, New York, where he attended Nicholas School. Mr. Adsit is a graduate of Williams College in Williamstown, Massachusetts in 1930, with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics. He retired from GMAC in 1974 and divided his time between Lake Wales, Florida and Dorset, Vermont. Mr. Adsit is survived by his wife of 61 years, Harriet McNulty Adsit. Private service will be held in Florida. Friends may make contributions to Williams College, Williamstown, Massachusetts 012676, or the Good Shepherd Hospice, P.O. Box 7129, Winter Haven, Florida 33883.
