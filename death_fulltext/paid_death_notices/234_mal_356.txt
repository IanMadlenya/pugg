
        LEVINE--Lawrence (Larry). The East 55th Street Conservative Synagogue, our clergy, officers, trustees and members, mourn the loss of our dedicated and beloved Vice President, past Chairman of the Board and active congregant for 30 years. We will greatly miss his intelligent guidance, his legal expertise, his general excellence and his menschlichkeit. We extend our condolences to his wife Barbara; his daughter Stephanie; and his son-in-law Ryan.
        $(6$)LEVINE--Lawrence E. We sadly note the passing of Larry Levine. A special man who was a good friend to many and a great friend to us. We will miss him dearly. Our sympathies to Barbara, Stephanie and Ryan. Marilyn and Marshall Bernstein and family
