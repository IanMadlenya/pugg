
        TUCKER-Philip. On October 22, 2000, at age 82. Loving husband of Leonor and her sons, Halbert and Juan Carlos, father of Jeffrey and Stephen (deceased), father-in-law of Cheryl & Melanie, grandfather of Lacey. A certified public accountant and former Chairman of the Accounting Department at Bronx Community College. We all mourn the loss of this highly moral, compassionate, humble man. Services were held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 ST, Tuesday, October 24, 11 am. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Westside Federation For Senior And Supportive Housing, 2345 Broadway, NY, NY 10024.
