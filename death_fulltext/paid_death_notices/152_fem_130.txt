
        KANREK-Aurelia. Of Brooklyn, NY, born in Vienna, Austria, passed away on September 13, 1999 at Beth Israel Medical Center. Beloved wife of Sidney, cherished mother of Marilyn and Victoria, devoted grandmother of David, she was always a loving and caring person, beautiful in all ways. We will miss her dearly and she will live forever in all our hearts. Funeral services at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Avenue, New York City, at noon on September 15, 1999, interment at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. The family requests that in lieu of flowers donations be made to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum.
