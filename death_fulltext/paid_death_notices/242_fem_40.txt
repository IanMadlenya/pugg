
        EISNER--Lorraine, 79, of Bergenfield, NJ, on March 4, 2007. A Chemistry teacher of forty years at Bergenfield High School, NJ. Predeceased by her husband Bernard of 47 years. Survived by her son Peter, daughter Wendy, brother Dr. Jerry Gropper, and granddaughters Isabel and Marina. Donations to The Eisner Memorial Science Scholarship in Bergenfield, NJ. Services are today at 3:30pm at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
