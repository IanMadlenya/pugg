
        SMELENSKY--Helen, be loved wife of the late Simon Smelensky, M.D., and loving sister of the late Julius Berkowitz, devoted mother of sons Michael C. and Robert E. Sterling, M.D., adoring grandmother to Eric Sterling and Lilia Sterling, died peacefully on February 20, 2007. She was admired as a matriarch, pianist, teacher and repository of the Smelensky and Berkowitz families' histories. She recently was a resident of Stamford, CT. Graveside services will be held at Beth Moses Cemetery, Long Island, on February 22, 2007 at 11 AM. Shiva will be held on Thursday, Friday and Sunday, February 22, 23, and 25, at the homes of Michael and Robert Sterling.
