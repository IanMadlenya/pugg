
        BERKOWITZ-Eva. Born July 29, 1913. Died on September 18, 2000. Devoted wife of 53 years to Abraham, loving mother to Morrell, proud grandmother of Jake and Max, & who adored her daughterin-law Harriet. A good, kind, gentle and giving person whose likes are not often seen. Contributions can be sent in her name to Temple Israel, 112 East 75 Street, New York, New York 10021.
