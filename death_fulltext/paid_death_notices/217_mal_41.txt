
        COLLIER--Clayton Stafford, born 14 February 1923 in New York City, passed away 1 February. He grew up in Phillipse Manor, NY, son of Robert Leo Collier (nephew of PF), and Marshe Neil Bass, daughter of Ula LeHentz. He served in the Navy during World War II. Clayton held a BS from UVM and MBA from CEI, Geneva, Switzerland. He was an aeronautical engineer. He is survived by a large family. A memorial service at O.B. Davis Funeral Home, Port Jefferson, NY, 5 February. Donations may be made to hospice.
