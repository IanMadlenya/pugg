
        SILFEN-Daniel W., DDS (Dan) on September 6, 1999 in his 78th year. Beloved husband of Mildred (deceased), dear father of Eric (Marilyn) and Michael (Lucy), brother of Selma and Harvey (deceased); grandfather of Lauren, Diana, Benjamin and Lydia. Attended undergraduate, as well as dental school, at the University of Pennsylvania. A long time resident of Riverdale, NY, where he practiced dentistry for over 40 years. He was an active congregant of the Conservative Synagogue Adath Israel, where he served as a vice president and board member. A graveside service will be held Thursday, September 9, 11AM, at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Contributions may be made to Sarah Neuman, Jewish Home and Hospital, Mamaroneck, New York.
