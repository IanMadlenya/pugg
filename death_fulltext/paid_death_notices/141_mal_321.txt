
        O'MALLEY-William J. Esteemed member of the St. Clare's Hospital Board of Trustees, passed away October 26, 1998. A revered member of the theatrical community, Bill used his talents to create the Helen Hayes Gala for the benefit of St. Clare's. We greatly appreciate those many years of service spent on our behalf. A cherished and respected friend, he was the distinguished recipient of many awards and honors. He will be greatly missed by the St. Clare's family, and our hearts are with his wife, Connie. Viewing at John J. Barrett & Son, 424 West 51st Street on Thursday, October 29th, from 2-5PM and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Mass at St. Paul The Apostle Church, 415 West 59th Street on Friday, October 30 at 10 A.M.
