
        KREIDIE-Munir R. Retired Assistant Commissioner General of the U.N. Relief Works Agency. Died November 19, 1998, at age 84, in King of Prussia, PA. Survived by wife Lisa, sons Munir, Marwan and Hamid, daughter Linda Freiser, and two grandchildren. Graduate of Cairo and Columbia Universities, he had been associated with the United Nations from its inaugural session in San Francisco. His posts included Director of its Postal Division. He also served on the U.N. delegations of Lebanon, Saudi Arabia and Syria. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Arab-American Foundation, 918 16th St, N.W., Washington, DC 20006.
