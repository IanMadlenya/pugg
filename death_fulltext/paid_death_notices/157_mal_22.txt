
        KREY-Warren C. On January 29, 2000. Beloved husband of Margaret, loving brother of Nathalie and father of Lewis, Russell, and Douglas. Adored grandpa of Rebecca, Lauren, Nathaniel, Kathleen, Mary Margaret, Adrienne, Jocelyn, and Theodore. He was Vice President and Director of Art Services at Dancer, Fitzgerald & Sample until 1986, then he contributed actively in several advisory capacities to the government of his home town of Teaneck, N.J. His love, counsel and support will be always remembered and cherished. Visitation, February 1, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Volk-Leber Funeral Home,Teaneck Rd Teaneck NJ. Funeral service 11 AM, February 2, at Trinity Lutheran Church, 167 Palisades Ave, Bogota, N.J. Burial at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Parkinson Disease Assn or the National Parkinson Disease Fndton. or Trinity Lutheran Church Bogota, NJ.
