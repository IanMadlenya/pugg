
        HENDREN -- Kathryn Ann. Died on Friday, September 19 in New York Cornell and Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. Loving daughter of the late Col. Ed V. Hendren, Jr., of Anniston, AL, and the late Dorthy May Hendren of Arlington, VA, and the stepdaughter of Mrs. Irene Hendren of Rome, GA. Beloved sister of Mr. Ed W. Hendren of Chico, CA, Judy Hendren Mello of New York, NY, and the late Jerry Hendren. Adored aunt and grandaunt of 28. She fought valiantly and now rests in a quieter place. There will be no service.
