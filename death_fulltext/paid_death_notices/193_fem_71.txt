
        BARBER-Virginia McClamroch. Widow of James F. Hoge and Anthony Barber, at age 95, at her Manhattan home on February 7, 2003. She is survived by two sons, James F. Hoge Jr. and Warren M. Hoge, a daughter, Virginia Hoge Verwaal, seven grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren; a daughter, Barbara Hoge Daine died in 2001. A native of North Carolina, Virgina Barber was a resident of NYC for 70 years and a longtime patron of the Metropolitan Opera, the New York Philharmonic Orchestra and Carnegie Hall. She was an active alumna of Hollins College and a devoted volunteer at the New York YWCA, Grosvenor Neighborhood House, the North Carolina Society, the Colonial Dames and the Blue Ridge School. Service Brick Presbyterian Church, Park Ave at 91 St, Tuesday, February 11, 10 AM.
