
        BERK--Joseph P., 77, of Cambridge, MA on March 12, 2004. A graduate of Wyoming Seminary and Harvard College, Joe was founder and President of Pathways of Sound, a recording company ''founded for children and others who share their good taste.'' Over a span of four decades, its recordings were done by important figures of 20th-century culture. He is survived by his wife, Adeline, of 37 years, a sister, Marian Barr, his children, Stephanie Berk of Cambridge, John Berk, MD of Newton, MA, a stepson, Nicholas Gomez of Los Angeles, and five grandchildren.
