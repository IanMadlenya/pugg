
        CONOVER-Robert F. A distinguished New York artist and teacher died on October 5, 1998 of natural causes. Mr. Conover was born in Trenton, New Jersey in 1920. His career in the arts began in the 1940's, following his service in World War II and continued throughout his life. His work is represented in many collections, including the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art & the Metropolitan Museum. He taught painting, drawing and printmaking at the New School for Social Research since 1958, and received many honors throughout his career. He is survived by his daughter, Chris Conover of New York City, his sister Mrs. Nancy Anderson of Holland, PA, his brother David Conover of Oshkosh, WI., and Jaqueline Heaslip, his longtime companion. Memorial service to be announced.
