
        NELSON-Rose S. On February 16, 2002, at age 93. Beloved wife of the late Irving S. Nelson, devoted and proud mother and mother-in-law of Nancy Nelson, Jane Waterman and Philip Jr., Peter Nelson and Xenia. Loved and admired grandmother of Philip Waterman III, Anne Waterman, Carolyn Clayton and Christopher, Hilary Cooper and William, and Sophie Nelson. Adored great-grandmother of Emma and William Clayton, and Madeleine and Annie Rose Cooper. Rose was a beautiful, elegant, wise and remarkable lady. An early career woman and an exhibited artist, most of her life was devoted to husband and family. She gained personal satisfaction from a love of art, books, and creating many warm and beautiful homes for her family. Her loving presence will be deeply missed by all. Services will be private. Donations in her memory can be made to Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York, 1 East 65th St., NYC 10021.
