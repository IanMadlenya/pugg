
        HERSCHAFT--Jean. Concern for Helping Animals in Israel (CHAI) deeply mourns the passing of Jean Herschaft, longtime journalist for the Jewish Post and Opinion, dedicated activist for animals, locally and globally, and valued friend of CHAI. We extend condolences to her family. She will be greatly missed. Memorial donations can be sent to CHAI, PO Box 3341, Alexandria, VA 22302, www.chai-online.org. >>AD#
