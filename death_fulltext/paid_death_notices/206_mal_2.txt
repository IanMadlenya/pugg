
        GALEF--James Martin. Of Greenwich, CT, died on Thursday, February 26, when the plane he was piloting crashed near Erie, PA. Mr. Galef was 47 years old. He was President of J.M. Galef and Co., a private investment firm he formed in 1985. Mr. Galef was born and raised in NYC. He was active on the Boards of both the Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club in NYC, and the Boys and Girls Club of Greenwich. He was also on the Board of The Collegiate School in NYC, from which he graduated. Mr. Galef graduated from Colorado College and received his MBA from the Harvard Business School. He is survived by his wife Kirsten Solsvig Galef and five children: Lily, Ben, Annabelle, Charlotte and James, all of Greenwich. He is also survived by his brother Jonathan Galef of Mt. Kisco, NY, and half-brothers Jeff Galef of Toronto, Canada, and Jack Galef of Hoboken, NJ. A non-denominational service will be held Wednesday, March 3, at 3:30 pm, at Temple Shalom, 300 E. Putnam Ave, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be sent to the Southampton Fresh Air Home, PO Box 244, Southampton, NY 11969; or the Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club, 1930 Randall Ave, Bronx, NY 10473.
        GALEF--James Martin, 47. With profound sadness we note the tragic, untimely passing of our beloved brother Jim on February 26, 2004.
