
        CONSTANT-Maud Carter. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center (BIMC) family joins with the physicians and staff of the Departments of Anesthesiology and Administration in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague Maud Carter Constant. She will be sorely missed by all who knew and worked with her. To the entire Constant family and all who loved her, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President Erwin Lear, M.D., Chairman, Department of Anesthesiology
