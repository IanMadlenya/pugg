
        EPSTEIN-Eli. Died Oct. 22 after a brief illness. A Social Worker who was committed to social justice throughout his life. A loyal and devoted friend who will be missed by his family and his many friends. Services at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 2nd Avenue at 10th Street, Thursday at 2 PM. Donations may be made to Jews For Racial & Economic Justice, 140 W. 22nd Street, NYC.
