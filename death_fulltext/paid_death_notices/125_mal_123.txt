
        TANAKA-Mitsuyoshi Bartholomew, died on June 7, 1997 at the age of 52. He is remembered as beloved husband to Michiko, and loving father to Amy and Hirohisa. He will be sorely missed by his colleagues at BNL, where he was a Physicist for 20 years, and his friends. He leaves behind his mother, Kikue Tanaka and brothers, Shigeyuki and Hideyuki. For more information contact Rocky Point Funeral Home, Inc. with services being held at St. Anselm's Church in Shoreham. Visiting will be Tuesday 2-4, and 7-9PM, with a mass Wed. at 10 AM. The family is requesting that no flowers be sent.
