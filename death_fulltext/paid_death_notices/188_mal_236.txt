
        BOSSY-George H. On September 18, 2002 at age 74. He was an economist with the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. He is survived by his wife Sanda, three sons, three grandsons, two sisters and a brother. A memorial service will be held Saturday, September 28th, 3 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
