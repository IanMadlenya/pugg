
        GIBBONS-George Billings Jr. April 5, 1914-January 16, 2003. 88, lifelong resident of Rye, NY, and Nantucket, MA, died on January 16, 2003. He was the son of the late Marion and George Billings Gibbons of Rye. He is survived by his wife, Carolyn duPont Gibbons; six children Marion L. Gibbons of Trinidad, George B. Gibbons III of City Island, Carolyn G. Abernethy of Rye, Pauline G. Green and Robert duPont Gibbons of Denver, and Lois G. Sager of Mattapoisett, MA, and 10 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the American Yacht Club at noon, on Tuesday, January 21, 2003. The family requests that in lieu of flowers contributions be made to the Carver Center, Port Chester, NY, or the Rye Free Reading Room, Rye, NY.
