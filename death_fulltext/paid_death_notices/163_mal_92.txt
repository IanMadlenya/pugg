
        MARVIN-John T. Of Manhattan, died suddenly Tuesday morning at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. He was 70. Born and raised in New Haven, John Marvin graduated with the highest honors from Taft School and Yale University, Class of 1950. He earned a Master's Degree at Stanford University and served as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy in the Pacific. After the Navy, he lived and worked in New York City. He was a Senior Vice President and money manager at Schroder & Co. He was the beloved husband of Leslie M. Marvin and beloved father of three children Alexander (Carey) Marvin of Longwood, FL, Melissa E. Laico of Warwick, NY, and Christopher Marvin of Weston, CT. He was cherished by his two step-children Anne S. Van Derzee and Jefferson M. Van Derzee. There will be a memorial service to celebrate John's life at 4:30 PM, Thursday, August 10 at St. Jane's Episcopal Church, 865 Madison Ave. & 71 St.
