
        GROSS-Leon. Died on February 3, in Paris, France. Beloved husband of Dolly. Loving father of Steven and George. Devoted brother of Sam, Fred and daughter-inlaw Ellen, as well as his cherished grandchildren. He was a wonderful, kind and honorable man, loved by all who knew him. His friends in America wish that his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem.
