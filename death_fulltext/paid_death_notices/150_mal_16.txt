
        ROSENZWEIG-Gary. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Gary Rosenzweig, a dedicated supporter who shared his family's devotion to Jewish causes. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife, Ruth; his daughter, Joy; his son, Claude; his grandchildren, Jake, Danielle, Aaron, and Esther; his brother, William; and his nephews, Jeffrey, Charles, Stephen, and Ronald. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
