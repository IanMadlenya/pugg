
        SMITH-Henry. Of Palm Beach, FL on April 22, 1999 at age 91. Beloved husband of the late Theodora A. Smith. Loving father of Gordon and Norma Smith and Fran Heller. Cherished grandfather of Randy Smith and Peter Aberg, Dr. Robin Smith and Dr. Kaare Kolstad, Melissa Haveson Pavese and John Pavese & Kim Haveson. Adoring great grandfather of Ashley Aberg and Alan Aberg. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Sunday 11:30AM. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Horace Mann School.
        SMITH-Henry. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, the Head of School and the Horace Mann Community, we deeply mourn the death of this most respected Horace Mann '53 Honorary Trustee, who was both a trusted friend and loyal supporter of Horace Mann. We extend our sincere heartfelt condolences to all the members of his family. He will be deeply missed. Michael D. Hess '58 Chair of HM Board of Trustees Dr. Eileen Mullady Head of School
        SMITH-Henry. Our sincere condolences to our friends Norma and Gordon and their family on the passing of their beloved father. You made life beautiful for Henry. Lilo and Fred
        SMITH-Henry. The Employees of the Gordon H. Smith Corporation offer their heartfelt condolences to the family on the loss of their beloved father, Henry Smith.
