
        SANGER--O. Ann, 82, died peacefully on August 15th in Edgewater, NJ, after a long illness borne with courage. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Cyril Sanger, sons David and Peter, daughtersin-law Sally and Michelle and grandchildren Jonathan, Freyja, Elizabeth and Ashley. Her smile and kindness touched all who knew her and she will be deeply missed. A Memorial Service will be held at St. Paul's Episcopal Church, 113 Engle St., Englewood, NJ on September 9th at 2:00pm. Donations in her memory may be made to The Center for Chest Diseases, CPMC, VC-3-365, 622 West 168th St. New York, NY 10032, Attn: Dr. P.F. Simonelli.
