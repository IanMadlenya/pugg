
        REYES--Amelia, M.D. On November 15, 2003 in Port Washington, NY. Beloved pediatrician for more than 20 years, who loved to garden. She leaves behind her mother Adelaida, three sisters, four children, 15 nieces and nephews and many dear friends including the Rev. Diana Austin. Religious services will be held at the Lutheran Church, 12 Franklin Avenue, Port Washington on Wednesday, November 19 at 4:00 PM.
