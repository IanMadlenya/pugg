
        WALDROP-Gideon W. The Manhattan School of Music mourns the passing of Gideon Waldrop, who served as the School's President from 1986 to 1989. We appreciated and admired his contributions as a musician, composer and conductor. Mr. Waldrop was a respected leader and advocate of arts education worldwide and leaves an enduring legacy to the institution he served. He will be missed by all who knew him. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
        WALDROP-Gideon W. The Juilliard School deeply mourns the death of Gideon Waldrop, Dean of the School from 1961 to 1986 and Acting President from 1983 to 1984. An accomplished composer and distinguished educator, he helped shape the programs in drama, dance and music that exist to this day at Juilliard. His legacy and caring ways are remembered by generations of Juilliard alumni. Sincere condolences are extended to his family and friends. Mary Rodgers Guettel, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
