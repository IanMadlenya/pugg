
        TRAVERS-John H., MD. The Medical Staff of Mercy Medical Center mourns the recent passing of John H. Travers, MD, a well-respected and loved member of the Department of Internal Medicine since July, 1972. He served for many years on the Executive Committee of the Medical Staff and founded the Ethics Committee in 1991, which he continued to chair until his passing. Dr. Travers also chaired the Coronary Care Committee and has represented Mercy on the Board of Directors of the American Heart Association. In 1999, Dr. Travers was the recipient of the prestigious Nassau-Suffolk Hospital Council's Theodore Roosevelt Award for Outstanding Service. His dedication to his patients, fellow physicians, staff members and the hospital will long be honored and remembered.
