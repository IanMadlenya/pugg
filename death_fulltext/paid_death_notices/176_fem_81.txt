
        SANDERS-Rose. 86, of Brooklyn, NY. Died of natural causes peacefully at home on September 6, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Charles Sanders. Devoted mother of Eleanor Rovell, Mitzi White and Ira Sanders. Grandmother of seven and great-grandmother of three. Devoted much of her time to The Children's Asthmatic Home in Denver, CO and Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital in NY. Her life was filled with many fine accomplishments and blessed events of her children and grandchildren. She will be missed by all. Services at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave., at 1 PM on Sunday, September 9, 2001.
