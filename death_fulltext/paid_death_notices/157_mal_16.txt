
        BARDOS-Leslie, (Laszlo), MD, died peacefully at home on January 30, at age 91. Born in Hungary, received medical diplomas from Szeged, Hungary and Vienna, Austria. Practiced internal medicine in Manhattan. He will be greatly missed by his wife, Susan Bardos, his daugher, Judith Lichtmann, son-in-law, Dr.Albert L.Lichtmann, grandchildren Katherine (Kitty) and Evan Dockser, Vivian and Eric Keller; great-children Samantha, Drew, Julia and Benjamin.
