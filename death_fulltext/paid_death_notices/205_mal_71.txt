
        CONLAN--Richard J., 62, of Larchmont, NY, died on February 4, 2004. Devoted husband to Denise, loving father to Martha (Andy) Meyerson, Sharyn (Tim) Boswell, Peter (deceased), Matthew and Maureen, proud grandfather of Keara, Peter and Alexander, and brother to Edward and Gregory. Dick was a First Vice President/Senior Financial Advisor at Merrill Lynch where he worked since 1968. Calling hours at John J. Fox Funeral Home at 2080 Boston Post Rd. in Larchmont on Friday, February 6, from 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Funeral service at Sts. John and Paul Church on Weaver St. on Saturday, February 7, at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Larchmont VAC, 155 Weaver St., Larchmont or a charity of your choice.
