
        STONE-Leon, 88. Dec. 21, after a long illness. Loving and beloved husband of Edna, and devoted family member. He was a man of strong principles, broad interests, great understanding, taste and discernment. His business acumen was much admired. He enjoyed music, art, travel and sports, and followed world affairs avidly. He could be gentle or firm, brilliant or quietly amusing. He was genuinely concerned for others. Leo was a true Renaissance man. Contributions in his memory to UJA or the N.Y. Times Neediest Cases would be appreciated. Services: 12:15 P.M., Wednesday, December 24, at ''the Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
