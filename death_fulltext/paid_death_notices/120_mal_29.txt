
        ELLMAN - Marc A. We mourn the loss of our beloved Marc Amlyad Ellman who left us too early on Dec. 27, 1996. Devoted son of Bernard and Batshua Ellman. Loving father of Tzvi Ellman. Loving brother of Zeva E. Williams. Granduate of The Hillel School, attended Ramaz Yeshiva and graduate of SUNY Binghamton. Marc was a devoted Zionist and veteran of the Israeli Army. We will miss him.
