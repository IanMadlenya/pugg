
        SCHUBART-Mark. The Board of Directors, Artists, and Staff of The Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center are deeply saddened by the passing of Mark Schubart, a friend, colleague and inspiration to many of us. His pioneering vision for arts education at Lincoln Center in its earliest days set a standard for the nation. We extend our sincere condolences to his family and friends. Donaldson C. Pillsbury, Chairman Peter Frelinghuysen, President David Shifrin, Artistic Director Jacqueline M. Taylor, Exec. Dir.
        SCHUBART-Mark. Chairman Albert Bellas, Executive Vice President Nancy Lassalle and the School of American Ballet mourn the passing of long-time friend, colleague and guiding spirit, Mark Schubart. As the founding director and former board chair of Lincoln Center Institute, Mr. Schubart had a profound impact on education and the arts, and he will be sorely missed.
