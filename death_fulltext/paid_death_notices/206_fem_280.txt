
        HOROWITZ--Bernice. 80, died March 26, 2004. Loving mother of Anne and Sara, adoring grandmother of Natalie and Rachel, big sister to Marvin. Dedicated teacher and educator, former head of academic studies at Townshend Harris High School. Longtime resident of Brooklyn Heights and East Hampton. Supporter of labor and women's movements and progressive causes. We'll miss her.
        HOROWITZ -- Bernice. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons mourns the passing of Bernice Horowitz, and extends deepest condolences to her family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
