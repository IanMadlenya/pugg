
        WEISS-Ellen Sesser. On May 7, 2000. Of New York City and Fire Island. A young senior. Beloved wife of the late Jerome A. Weiss. Mother of loving son Michael Armand and wife Katherine. Grandmother of precious twins Mark Alexander and Daphne Rebecca. Sister of beloved, darling Shirley Bakal, Sue Blum and Sydney Sesser. Daughter of the late Rose and Samuel Sesser-a gift from heaven to have had them as parents. Loving aunt, great aunt, cousin, friend, and faithful friend of housekeeper for many years, Nerissa Oliver. Services Wednesday, 12:45PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory to the Humane Society or charity of choice.
        WEISS-Ellen Sesser. Tifereth Israel-Town and Village Synagogue mourns the loss of its beloved member of long standing and extends heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Laurence A. Sebert Asst Rabbi Abby Sosland Carol J. Green, President
