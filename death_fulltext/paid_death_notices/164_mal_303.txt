
        WOLFE-Warren J. Warren J. Wolfe of Great Neck and Westhampton Beach, died on September 1st at the age of 83 due to emphysema-related complications. Prior to his retirement in 1976, Mr. Wolfe was the Director of Advertising for The New York Times. Mr. Wolfe served as Board President of both his residences - Park Point Cooperative Apartments, Inc. in Great Neck and Harbor House in Westhampton Beach. In addition, he served as a Trustee of the Village of Westhampton Beach. In earlier years, Mr. Wolfe was a boating enthusiast and enjoyed some of his most pleasurable times on the waters surrounding Westhampton Beach. He leaves a number of very close friends, but no family survivors. Memorial contributions may be sent to the American Lung Association.
