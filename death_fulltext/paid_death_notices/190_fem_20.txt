
        GOULD-Paula H. An indomitable presence with spirited intellect and nimble witted humor. Improvisational pianist, teacher and accompanist for the New Dance Group, New Duncan Dancers and Hunter College. Champion of progressive politics & education and reproductive rights. Remembered lovingly by her daughters Diana and Emily Gould and their partners, her grandchildren, nieces and nephews, and many friends on Manhattan's Upper West Side and Northport, Long Island. Memorial at Ethical Culture Society, 2 West 64th St., Sat., Dec. 14th at 3 P.M. Contributions to Planned Parenthood or Three Rivers School, Paine Turnpike, Berlin, VT 05602.
