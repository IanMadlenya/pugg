
        TROBE--Jacob. The Trustees and Staff of Jewish Child Care Association are saddened to learn of the death of our former Executive Vice President, Jacob Trobe. Mr. Trobe's first professional jobs were with JCCA predecessor agencies beginning in 1937. During and following WWII Mr. Trobe served overseas helping to establish services for displaced children. He rejoined JCCA in 1961 when he was appointed Executive and served with distinction until his retirement in 1977. He was a committed social worker and advocate for children and families. Our sympathy to his children Robert, Toni and their families. Leonard Elman, President Barbara B. Mann, Chair Richard Altman, CEO
