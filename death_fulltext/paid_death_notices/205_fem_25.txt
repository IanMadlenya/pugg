
        CONDELLES--Christine. The Board of Directors and the Staff of the League Treatment Center mourn the passing of Christine Condelles and express their sympathy to her family on their loss. Mrs. Condelles was a courageous woman, a wonderful mother, and a warm and loving grandmother. She is survived by her daughter Stella Filippelli, a member of the Board of Directors of the League Treatment Center and President of the League Center's Parents Association, and by her grandson James. She will be missed by everyone who knew her. Hannah Kinn, Executive Director Dr. Steven Katz, President Board of Directors League Treatment Center
