
        GOLD-Paul Dana, MD, of Wallingford, Pennsylvania died on June 11, 2000 at home. Dr. Gold was born in New York on January 25, 1952 the son of Florence (Schneider) and the late Louis Gold. A graduate of State University of New York -Stonybrook & University of Noreste School of Medicine, Tampico, Mexico, with a fellowship at Cooper Hospital/ University Medical Center, Camden, New Jersey. Board Certified in Critical Care and Intensive Care, he served at Jefferson Park Hospital Philadelphia, Pennsylvania as director of Emergency Medicine and Director of Critical Care, Assistant Director Emergency Department. Allegheny Health Systems Elkins Park Campus, Assistant Professor Emergency Medical College of Pennsylvania, Emergency Department staff Physician Mercy Community Hospital, Havertown, Pennsylvania. He was noted for his compassion and devotion in the care of his patients and articulated a strong patient advocacy. In addition to his mother he is survived by two brothers Gene & David. Memorial Services will be held at a later date. Contributions in his memory to the Southern Poverty Law Center Montgomery, Alabama.
