
        DOWLING--William C., Jr. On October 3, 2003 at age 77. Owner of Cardow Jewelers, the Carib Beach Hotel and the Emerald Beach Resort. Son of the late William C. and Marion Dowling. Mass of Christian Burial at Cathedral of St. Peter and Paul, St. Thomas Virgin Islands, Friday October 10th, 1:30PM. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury NY, Tuesday October 14th, 11AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, attn. Dr. Jedd Wolchok's Research, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021.
