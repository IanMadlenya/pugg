
        EPSTEIN--Esta Miriam, 101, died peacefully November 13, 2006 in Carbondale, CO. Wife of the late Harold Epstein of Larchmont, NY, mother of Susan Cherbuliez of South Freeport, ME and Laurel Loeb of Carbondale, CO. Devoted grandmother to five granddaughters and six great-granddaughters. Donations may be made in her honor to Burke Rehabilitation Hospital, Community Relations, 785 Mamaroneck Ave, White Plains, NY 10605 or to Wave Hill, 675 West 252 St, Bronx, NY 10471.
