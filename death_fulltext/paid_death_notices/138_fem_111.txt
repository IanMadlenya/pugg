
        PHILLIPS-Merle Patricia. Died peacefully on July 13, 1998 at Georgetown University Medical Center. Born in Falmouth, Jamaica. Patsy was a loving wife to the late Orville Phillips, devoted mother to Robert and Sean and dear sister to Rey, Fay, Barbara, Jean and Joy. She will be sadly missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may pay respect Friday, July 17, 1998 7-9 PM at the HinesRinaldi Funeral Home, 11800 New Hampshire Avenue. Viewing will be held on July 18, 1998, at 2:00 PM followed by the funeral at 3:00 PM at the First Alliance Missionary Church, 14500 New Hampshire Avenue, Silver Springs, MD. Please make donations to the Georgetown Lombardi Cancer Center, Georgetown Medical Center, 3800 Reservoir Road, NW Washington, D.C. 20007.
