
        MAYER - Gerda Gernsheim, Ph.D., on March 2, 1999 in her 91st year. Born Worms, Germany. Beloved wife of the late Mor Mayer. Sister of the late Hildegard Gernsheim Mayer. Cherished cousin. Long time medical researcher in New York City at Mount Sinai and Columbia Presbyterian Hospitals. Private services were held.
