
        INDYKE-Paula. Died peacefully on November 30, 2002. Born in Brooklyn, NY; resident of Tamarac, Fl. Awarded educator in New York City public school system for over 30 years. Beloved mother of Jay and Barry, loving grandmother of Matthew and Ross, dear sister of Evelyn, Helen and Philip. Intelligent and outspoken, she will be sorely missed. Memorial service Monday, December 9, 4:30 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
