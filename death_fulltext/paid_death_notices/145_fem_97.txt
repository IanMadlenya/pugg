
        PENNEBAKER-Sylvia Bell of New York City and Sag Harbor, L.I., died at home in Manhattan on February 5 after a lengthy illness. Beloved daughter of the late Samuel and Isabel Bell of Chestnut Hill, PA, and Coconut Grove, FL, she is survived by her 3 devoted children: Stacy, Frazer and Linley (now Linley Pennebaker Whelan) in addition to her brothers, Samuel and Paul Bell, and 7 grandchildren. Burial in Oakland Cemetery, Sag Harbor, February 13.
