
        LAPSLEY-John Willard. Died January 26, 2002, following a prolonged illness. He is survived by his wife, Hope Whitney Lapsley; a daughter, Adelaide Mulry of Glen Cove, NY; two sons, Howard of Needham, MA, and Robert of Seattle, WA, and three sisters, Marion Schwarz of New York, NY, Nina Alexander of Bozeman, MT, and Katherine Beug of Kinsale, Ireland. He also leaves three grandsons and four granddaughters. Jack was born in New York City in 1935, the son of Howard Lapsley and Eleanor Hallowell Lapsley. A graduate of St. Paul's School in Concord, NH and Harvard College, where he was a member of the Porcellian Club and captain of the Varsity Heavyweight Crew for two years. An avid sailor and lover of the waters of the Maine coast, he also had a fondness for bird shooting in Great Britain. In addition, he gave many friends great pleasure with his extraordinary talent for story telling. Most of his professional career was with J.P. Morgan & Co., where he retired as senior officer in 1996. A memorial service will be held Thursday, January 31, at 11AM, at The Church of The Advent in Westbury, L.I. Contributions may be made in lieu of flowers to: Plastic Surgery Development Fund at the New York Presbyterian Hospital, c/o Dr. Lloyd Hoffman, 525 East 68th St., Box 115, New York, NY 10021.
