
        HODES-Dr. M.E. The Board of Trustees, Scientific Advisory Board, and professional staff of the Israel Cancer Research Fund mourn the loss of a dear colleague and friend, Dr. M.E. (''Ed'') Hodes. Chairman Emeritus of ICRF's International Scientific Council, he was a dedicated humanitarian, deeply respected by all who knew him. His warmth, wit, wisdom, and devotion to ICRF will be sorely missed. Yashar Hirshaut, M.D. President Daniel G. Miller, MD President Emeritus Leah Susskind Chairman S. Donald Friedman Chairman Emeritus Donald Adelman Executive Vice President
