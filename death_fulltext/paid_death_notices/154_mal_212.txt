
        SANDERS-Milton L. 81, beloved husband of Audrey; devoted father of Judith, Jane and Betty-Ann; caring father in-law of Michael and William; loving papa of Jonathan, Rebecca and Elizabeth; dearest brother of Beatrice. Milton was House Counsel and Treasurer of Yellow Book U.S.A.; co-founder of Sanders & Daniels, Co.; pastpresident of Woodward Mental Health Center; avid photographer, talented sculptor. He will be missed by his family and friends, upon whom he unselfishly bestowed love, solace and guidance. Loved by all for his vitality, exuberance and passion. Funeral at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, Thursday, November 18, noon. Donations may be made in his memory to the Jewish Day School, 2313 Pennsylvania St, Allentown, PA 18104, attn: Principal.
