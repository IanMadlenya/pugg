
        FITZSIMONS--John F. on March 28, 2004 at age 96. Never without his Homburg and tie. A long time fixture of Forest Hills Gardens, NY. Loving husband of the late Gertrude McLoughlin FitzSimons, father of Ellen Ann (Chamberlain) and John H. and grandfather of Colby and Elizabeth Chamberlain and Amanda and John A. FitzSimons. Loyal graduate of Regis High School, Fordham College and Fordham Law School. Tenor in the Friendly Sons of Saint Patrick Glee Club for almost 70 years. Former trustee and devoted member of the Congregation of Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church for over 60 years. Cmdr USN ret. Friends may call at Fox Funeral Home, Ascan Ave and Metropolitan Ave, Forest Hills on Monday from 7-9PM & Tuesday from 2-5 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass Wednesday at 10:15AM at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Ascan Ave and Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY.
        FITzSIMONS--John F., The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of John F. FitzSimons, a Veteran Member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Robert J. Reilly, Pres. Henry F. White Jr., Historian
