
        GOODMAN-Sarah, 93, on December 3rd, 1998. Beloved wife of Saul L. Goodman, father of Jacob Eli, mother-in-law of Josy, and grandmother of Rachel and Naomi. Born in Baranovka, Ukraine, she came to this country at age 16 and unselfishly devoted her life to her family and to the advancement of Jewish education. A graveside service was held at Cedar Park Cemetery on Friday, December 4th.
