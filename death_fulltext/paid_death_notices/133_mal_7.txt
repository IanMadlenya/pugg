
        GOULD-Solomon. The Trustees of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center note with profound sorrow the death of a distinguished past member and Honorary Trustee. Since 1978 Mr. Gould was a dedicated and inspired leader, an enthusiastic organizer and supporter of many fundraising events as well as a man of great vision. His efforts contributed to Brookdale's emerging as a leading provider of healthcare in the communities it serves. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Frank J. Maddalena, President and Chief Executive Officer
