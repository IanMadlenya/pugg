
        ELIAS-Sylvan H. The members of Vladeck, Waldman, Elias & Engelhard, P.C., their associates and employees, mourn the passing of Sylvan H. Elias, a founding member of the firm. He was a superb lawyer, a good friend, and a kind and gentle man. Services will be held Sunday, March 1, at 12:15 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, New York City.
