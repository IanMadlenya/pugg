
        BEILIN--Frank. Friday, February 17, at age 85, after a long illness. He grew up in NYC, was the caring brother of Irwin, and leaves many loving friends who will miss his enthusiasm and generosity. After a theater career, he headed Saks Fifth Avenue's direct mail operation, then becoming advertising manager. Ultimately, as a well known photographer's representative, he worked closely with the Estee Lauder Company, until his retirement. Burial will be February 24, at Green River Cemetery, Springs - Easthampton, LI. Memorial service will be held at a later date.
