
        KRAMER--Saul. The Board of Governors and Members of Fenway Golf Club records with profound sorrow the passing of Saul Kramer, a member of the Club for 67 years. We extend our sincere condolences to his family and to his friends. Jeffrey I. Citron, President Lawrence Handelsman, Secretary
        $(6$)KRAMER--Saul. The Board of Directors and Administration of the United Hebrew Geriatric Center mourn the passing of Mr. Saul Kramer, Past President and Chairman of the board of directors, ardent benefactor, friend and supporter of the UHGC since 1946. His dedication to those in need was unparalleled, as evidenced by the Lola and Saul Kramer Pavilion of the UHGC. We extend our heartfelt sympathy and prayers to his bereaved family. His wisdom, counsel and kindness will be sorely missed by all. Malcolm H. Lazarus, President Michael Rozen, President-Elect Rita C. Mabli, Chief Executive Officer
