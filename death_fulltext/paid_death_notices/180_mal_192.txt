
        TAPLINGER-Jean R., 89. Beloved husband of the late Marjorie. A renaissance man: linguist, artist, thespian, singer, inventor, mechanical engineer, son of a newspaper man. Assistant Commissioner, Boy Scouts of America. Survived by his beloved daughter Sandra Sobel, son Lee R. Taplinger, grandsons Geoffrey & Alan Sobel, sister Harriet Leland, brother Arthur Taplinger. Graveside services 4 P.M. Sunday, January 13th at the Temple Israel Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson, New York
        TAPLINGER-Jean R. Loyal colleague, admired cousin, talented Chief Engineer for almost fifty years at Solo Products Corp. He will be remembered by all who knew him at Solo. Horace Solomon
