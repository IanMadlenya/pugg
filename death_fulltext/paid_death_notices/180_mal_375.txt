
        LANDES-Robert N. January 25, 2002. Of New York City and Naples, FL. Beloved husband of Phyllis Markman Landes. Loving father of Jeffrey Mark Landes, Kathy Braddock and Lucy Harrop. Dear father-in-law of Mark D. Harrop and Andrea C. Landes. Cherished grandfather of Philip Wildman Braddock. Charles Gibson Braddock and Sophie Eva Landes. Devoted brother of William Landes. Services at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave. at 65th St., Wednesday, January 30th, 11:30 AM. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society, 19 West 56th St., New York, NY 10019.
        LANDES-Robert N. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Robert N. Landes, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
