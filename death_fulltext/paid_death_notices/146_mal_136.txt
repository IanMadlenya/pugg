
        DENNEN-Rev. Gerard. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former parochial vicar of Saint Joan of Arc Church, Jackson Heights, on March 1st. Reposing at Gleason's Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd, Flushing, on Friday, March 5, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Funeral Mass on Saturday, March 6, at Mary's Nativity Church, 46-02 Parsons Blvd, Flushing, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
