
        KESSLER-Erich G. Died of a heart attack Thursday, August 13, 1998, in Geneva, Switzerland, while vacationing with his daughter and granddaughter. He was a resident of Manhattan. Mr. Kessler was born in Vienna, Austria, on February 10, 1915, and came to the United States in 1938. Before his retirement he was a partner at H. Hentz and Vice President of the international sales division at Hornblower, Weeks, Noyes and Trask, Inc. He is survived by a daughter, Gwen Luce of Palo Alto, CA.; her three children, Katherine, Stephen & Christina; and his brother, Peter Kessler of Quebec, Canada. The family requests in lieu of flowers donations be made to the New Rochelle Humane Society.
