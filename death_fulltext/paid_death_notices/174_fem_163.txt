
        BRANDT-Grace Borgenicht. Of NYC, age 86, on July 19, 2001. Beloved wife of Warren, loving mother of Jan Schwartz, Berta B. Kerr, Lois Borgenicht, stepmother of Isabella Brandt Johansen, sister of Shirley Black Kash, grandmother of Kendra, Adam, Regina, Orin, Max, Daven, Nicholas, greatgrandmother of David and Sarah. Her integrity and grace will be remembered. Service Sunday, 10 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Jewish Museum, 1109 5th Ave., NY, NY 10128.
