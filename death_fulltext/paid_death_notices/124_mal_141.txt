
        ASCH-John. Died May 10, 1997 at the age of 90. Beloved husband of Mary Blume-Asch, devoted son of author Sholem Asch, loving brother of Ruth Shaffer, of Brighton, England, and Nathan and Moses Asch, both deceased. Stepfather of James and Frederick Blume, father-in-law of Kathryn Frank and Mary Blume, grandfather of Zach, all of whom greatly mourn his loss. We will sorely miss him.
