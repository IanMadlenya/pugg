
        OKRENT-Odette. The National Board of Directors and the Membership of American Red Magen David for Israel record with profound sorrow the passing of Odette Okrent, beloved mother of Jacqueline Goldman, longtime, dedicated member of the Board. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Jacqueline, Nathan, their children and grandchildren. May the memories of her life be as a blessing to the family. Stuart A. Jackson, Chairman/Treasurer Robert L. Sadoff, MD, National President Benjamin Saxe, Exec. V.P. Robert L. Schwartz, National Director
