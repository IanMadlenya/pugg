
        MARCUS-Rouben. Beloved husband and companion of the late Frances, father of Richard, fatherin-law of Barbara, grandfather of Jay and Sam, brother of Esther Backer (Max) and Berta Weiner, loving uncle and great uncle. He was a veteran who served his country. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died January 25, 1998 in Silver Spring, MD. Funeral to be held at Wien & Wien Funeral Home, 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ, January 27 at 12:30 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Misler Center for Jewish Senior Day Care, 1801 East Jefferson St., Rockville, Maryland 20852. His loving smile and quiet dignity touched us all. He will be dearly missed. May his memory be a blessing.
