
        SLAVIN-Jacob. Beloved father of Leontine Temsky of Nyack and Steve Slavin of Brooklyn, brother of Ted, grandfather of Eric and Jonah, great grandfather of Eleni, Elizabeth, Justine, Sophie, and Daniel. Jacob Slavin taught mathematics at Madison, Lincoln, and Brooklyn Tech from the mid 1930's through the late 1960's. His memory lives on in the hearts and minds of his family and students.
