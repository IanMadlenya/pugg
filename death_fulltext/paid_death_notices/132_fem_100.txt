
        KIRSCH-Ida. On January 9, 1998, in her 101st year. Beloved mother of Sylvia and Leslie Berger, Lillian and Bernard Frankel, Robert and Angie Kirsch. Cherished grandmother of Leonard and Diana Frankel, Paul and Barbara Frankel, Joel and Jodi Berger, Bonnie and Scott Canner, Alecia and Charlene Kirsch. Adoring greatgrandmother of Jordana, Carly, Ilona, Justin, Peri and Lauren. Former Matron of the Eastern Star, President of the Liberty Ladies Auxiliary. She lived with grace, dignity and good humor and was loved and respected by all. Services Sunday, 11 AM, Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, New Jersey.
