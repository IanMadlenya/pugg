
        SEDGWICK-Fannie P. Of 2500 Q Street, NW, Washington, D.C., died unexpectedly, Thursday, February 26, 1998 at her apartment. She was 66. During her life Ms. Sedgwick worked for a variety of public and not-for-profit organizations, including the Peace Corps, NYC Planning Commission, the NYC Human Resources Administration, the Institute for World Order and the Citizens Democracy Corps. Most recently she was director of executive search for the Citizens Democracy Corps.
