
        MAYER--Frederick R. The Trustees and Staff of the American Federation of Arts are deeply saddened by the loss of Frederick R. Mayer, former AFA Trustee and husband of our dear friend andChairman Emerita Jan Perry Mayer. We will always be grateful for Frederick's wise council and generosity and always remember his humanity and joy for living. Our sincerest condolences to Jan and their family. Jeffrey M. Loewy, Chairman Frank B. Bennett, President Julia Brown, Director
