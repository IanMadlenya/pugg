
        WINSTON-Joseph. Of Scarsdale, NY, and Lakeville, CT. Age 81. Died on February 4, 1999. Beloved husband of the late May. Devoted father of Allan and David. Fatherin-law of Diana and Deborah. Grandfather of Joshua and Julia. He practiced law in New York for fifty-five years. He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from City College of the City University of New York. He earned an M.A. in French Literature from Columbia University and received his LL.B. from Columbia University. Law School. Fluent in six languages, he led an exemplary life devoted to his family, his clients, and the study of law. He loved the Torah, Israel, and dedicated himself to Jewish causes and charities. Funeral service on Sunday, February 7th, 1PM, at Temple Kol Ami, White Plains, New York.
