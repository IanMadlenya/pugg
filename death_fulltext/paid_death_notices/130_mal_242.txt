
        GARFINKEL-Henry ''Hank'', at home on November 21 at age 57 after a valiant battle with cancer. A Ph.D. from Columbia University, his creativity shone through during his career as a toy inventor. Proud to be Andrew and Allison's father, beloved and adored husband of Susan, devoted son of the late Sol and Katherine, brother of the late David, special uncle to Heather and Adam and grateful brother-in-law to Penny. Services today, 11:30 AM, at ''Gutterman's,'' Woodbury, LI. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in his memory to Hospice Care of LI.
