
        MALLOW-Thomas T., of New York and Florida, died on July 13, 2000. Survived by his beloved wife Roberta and loving children, Deborah Mallow (Brian) Chenensky and Peter (Miranda Benedict) Mallow. Adoring grandfather of Dean Harris Chenensky and David Robert Mallow. He will always remain in our hearts and minds a devoted, kind and loving soul. Private services will be held at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, N.Y.
