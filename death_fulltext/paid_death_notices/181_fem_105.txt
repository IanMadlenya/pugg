
        WALLACH-Arleen Gail. On February 9, 2002. Beloved and adored wife of Henry. Devoted mother of Louis and Ty. Dear mother-in-law of Allison. Loving sister of Dr. Joel Wilentz and sister-in-law of Linda. Cherished grandmother of Charlie. Donations in her memory can be made to Anne Moore Breast Cancer Research Fund, c/o Dr. Anne Moore, 428 East 72nd Street, Suite 300, NYC 10021. Services Monday, 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
