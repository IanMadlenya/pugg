
        GILBERTSON--Dr. Francis E., 81, of Rumson, New Jersey, on August 10, 2005. Beloved husband Susan Van Wyck Gilbertson. Father of Hank, Mark and Peter Gilbertson and Alison McHeffey. Stepfather of Carl and Stephen de Gersdorff and Susan Stamos. Loving brother of Mary Kay Arliss and Patricia Newhouse, adored grandfather of seven. An accomplished surgeon and avid sportsman, he graduated from Princeton University 1945 and Jefferson Medical College. Served as Chief of Surgery from 1980-1985 at Riverview Medical Center, Red Bank, New Jersey. Funeral service on Friday, August 12, The Church of the Nativity, Fair Haven, New Jersey at 11AM.
        GILBERTSON--Dr. Francis E. The Trustees of the Museum of the City of New York are saddened by the passing of Dr. Francis E. Gilbertson, renowned surgeon and father of museum Trustee Mark Gilbertson. The Trustees extend their deepest sympathy to Mark and his family. Newton P.S. Merrill, Chairman Susan Henshaw Jones, President and Director
