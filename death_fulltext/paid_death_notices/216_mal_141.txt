
        WOOLVERTON--William H., Jr., on January 6, 2005. Much loved father of William III, Frederick, Benjamin & Celia, brother of the Rev. John F. Woolverton, and grandfather of Zander, Alice, Peter, Drew, Maggie and Kate. Devoted to his former wife Ethel and her family. Attended Choate and Yale. Enlisted in U.S. Army in 1941 and saw action in Italy and France, participating in covert operations with the OSS. Rose to the rank of Captain. Was President of family business for many years. He attended St. James Church in New York and enjoyed the company of friends and family on the tennis court and traveling in France. He was kind and generous and cared deeply for his family. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Red Cross.
