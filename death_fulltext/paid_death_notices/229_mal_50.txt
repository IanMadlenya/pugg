
        SILVERMAN--Marty. On behalf of the Board of Trustees and the students, faculty, and staff of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, the nation's oldest technological research university, we mourn the passing of a noble son of Troy and our visionary benefactor. To his family we extend our sincere condolences and our everlasting gratitude for sharing with us Marty's boundless heart. Samuel F. Heffner, Chairman Dr. Shirley Ann Jackson, President
