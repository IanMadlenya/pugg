
        ADAMS--D. Nelson. The National Hypertension Association express great sadness upon the death of D. Nelson Adams, beloved and revered Trustee.
        $(6$)ADAMS--D. Nelson. The Board and staff of the Youth Counseling League, a division of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services, acknowledge with sadness the death of Nelson Adams, esteemed lawyer, devoted philanthropist and husband of the late Diana Adams. Among his many charitable activities, Nelson generously carried on the commitment of his beloved wife Diana to the work of the Youth Counseling League, which she served as president and longstanding trustee. We were privileged to have honored her memory with Nelson and his family at a ceremony naming the YCL conference room for Diana Adams. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his son Dan, and grandsons Bryan and Bryce. John A Herrmann, President, JBFCS Mrs. Lewis Burke Frumkes Mrs. David Schiff Co-Chairs, Youth Conseling League Divisional Board Joan Adams, YCL Director
