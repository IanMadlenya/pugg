
        MACLENNAN-Helen J. Died peacefully on December 17, 1999. Born in Manhasset in 1908, she was a resident of Memorial Place most of her life. She worked for many years at the bank in Manhasset. Helen also worked with her brother, Daniel, who owned Maclennan Pharmacy until their retirement in 1978. She is survived by her sister Orca Huxley, niece Heather and nephews Daniel and Thomas. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, Tuesday 11-1 PM. Religious service 1 PM. In lieu of flowers donations to a charity of your choice would be appreciated.
