
        BLEETSTEIN-Elaine Nonas. Died on March 19 in her 94th year. Beloved wife of the late Monroe Bleetstein. Adored mother of Nona Bleetstein and Marjorie and Michael Grunebaum. Cherished grandmother of Lauren, David, Christine and Ted Grunebaum. Loving sister of Miriam Blechner, Betty Lash, Dr. Gerson Nonas and Elliott Nonas. Services Wednesday, 11AM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, N.Y. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, New York Chapter.
