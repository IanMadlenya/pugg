
        FOSTER-Catharine Osgood, 90, of Bennington, VT, on February 13 in Bennington. Educated Mount Holyoke College. Taught at Sophie Newcomb College in New Orleans and later at Bennington College for 30 years where the Catharine Osgood Foster Chair for Distinguished Teaching was established in her honor. Survived by husband Thomas H. Foster, Bennington, and several cousins. Burial Office offered Monday, February 16, 11 AM, St. Peters Episcopal Church, Bennington, VT. Interment at a later date. Old Bennington Cemetery, no visitation. Memorial gifts to Mt. Anthony Preservation Society or Crossett Library at Bennington College in care of Hanson-Walbridge Funeral Home, P.O. Box 957, Bennington, VT 05201.
