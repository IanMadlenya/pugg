
        TARTAGLIA-Tullio F., MD, 79, a graduate of The Chicago Medical School, and a decorated Korean War veteran, died June 10. Dr. Tartaglia was a licensed physician for 50 years and a past-President of the NY Society of Internal Medicine, Bronx Chapter, and the Bronx County Medical Society. A devoted husband to Dolores, he is survived by his 4 children, Lydia, Joseph, Alfred and Gregory, and his grandchildren, Laura, Andrew, Christina, James, Julia, Sarah and Alexandra. For information call Graham Funeral Home, Rye, NY.
