
        MORGAN-Maud (Cabot). In Cambridge, MA, March 14, 1999. Mother of Victoria M. Benedict and Alexis Morgan both of Cambridge. Also survived by two nieces, her nephew and several grand nieces and grand nephews. Memorial services in the First Parish in Cambridge, 3 Church St., Harvard Square, Cambridge, MA Thursday, March 18 at 3P.M. In lieu of flowers gifts in her memory may be sent to the Maud Morgan Prize Museum of Fine Arts, 465 Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA 02115.
