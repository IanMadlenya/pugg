
        YUTER-Ben. The entire New York Community Hospital Family, Trustees, Physicians, Employees and Volunteers mourn the loss of Ben Yuter. Mr. Yuter was a member of the Board of Trustees for 16 years. He contributed greatly to the Board and his assistance to administration for the successful, turn-around of the hospital these past five years will always be remembered. Our condolences to his wife Claire, his children and his grandchildren. George Weinberger, Chairman, Board of Trustees Lin H. Mo, President and Chief Executive Officer
        YUTER-Benjamin. Age 83. A World War II veteran, he and his beloved wife of 57 years, Claire, were the first Jewish GI couple united in marriage while serving in uniform. Loving father of Arthur, Mona, Edward. Devoted grandfather of Kate, Tess, Remi. Father-in-law of Roni, Kurt Brokaw, Mary. President of Beth El Jewish Jewish Center for 27 years. Funeral Services at Beth El Jewish CenAvenue T and Homecrest Avenue, Brooklyn, Sunday at 11:30 A.M.
        YUTER-Ben. The New York Metropolitan Region of The United Synagogue of Conservative Judaism mourns the untimely passing of our beloved Board of Directors member. May his memory be for a blessing. Eugene D. Zinbarg, President Bruce F. Greenfield, Exec Dir
