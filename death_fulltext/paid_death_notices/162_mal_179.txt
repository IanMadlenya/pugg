
        WAGNER-George R. Of Rye, NY, died July 11, after a long illness. He was 83 years old. Following distinguished service in the U.S. Army during World War II, Mr. Wagner rejoined the ship brokerage firm of Charles R. Weber in 1946. Over the course of 40 years at the firm Mr. Wagner became a ''dean of the ship brokerage community'' in the words of one of his colleagues. In 1970 he became principal owner and president of the firm & served in that capacity until his retirement in 1986. He is survived by his wife of fifty-five years, Alice, & their three children, Robert, Kathryn and Richard, as well as three grandchildren. A private service will be held. Memorial donations may be made to the Joslin Diabetes Center, 1 Joslin Place, Boston, Massachusetts 02215, Attn: Development Office.
