
        KIMMEL--Dana Spector, died on May 22, 2007. Beloved wife of Scott. Devoted mother of Jack, David, and Lily. Loving daughter of Joseph and Janet Spector, and daughter-in-law of Louis Kimmel and the late Charlotte. Dearest sister of Douglas Spector and Lisa Rothkrug. Services were held on Thursday. Donations in her memory may be made to The Dana Spector Kimmel Memorial Scholarship Fund, c/o Great Neck South High School, 341 Lakeville Road, Great Neck, New York 11020.
