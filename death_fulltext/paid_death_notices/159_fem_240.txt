
        BLAND-Miriam (Doniger) entered into rest, April 24, late of Brookline, Massachusetts, formerly of Roslyn Heights, Long Island. Beloved wife of the late David Bland. Devoted mother of Diane & William Brauer of Evanston, Illinois & Jane & Bradley Honoroff of Chestnut Hill, MA. Cherished grandmother of Benjamin & Jacob Honoroff & Gretchen Sligh. A memorial service in Mrs. Bland's memory will be held at her late residence, 1731 Beacon St., Brookline, Massachusetts on Sunday, May 28, 2000 at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy in her memory may be donated to The Jimmy Fund, 44 Binney St., Boston, Massachusetts 02215.
