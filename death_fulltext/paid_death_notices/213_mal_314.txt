
        ROSEN--Lincoln, 89, died October 20, in Maplewood, NJ. Married for 61 years to his late beloved wife Dorothy, he leaves his children, Bob, Nan and Edward, his grandchildren Jonathan, Melissa, Claire, Lillie, Charlotte and Camille, and his great-grandchildren, Cameron and Spencer. He was graduated from Harvard College Class of '35 with high honors and served overseas during W.W. II as a Captain in the U.S. Army, Office of the Adjutant General. A man of high ethical standards and a keen interest in history, economics and international relations, he pursued successful careers in both law and business. A memorial service in celebration of his life will be held in December. Contributions in his memory may be made to the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 St., New York, NY 10023.
