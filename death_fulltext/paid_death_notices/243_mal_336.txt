
        HASHMALL--Frank, 87. Born in Manhattan. City College graduate,1940. Pratt Institute graduate with Honors, 1962. President of L.M. Rabinowitz Company until retirement at 75. Married 65 years to Sylvia Deutsch who predeceased him in September 2005. Loved politics, history, classical music and progressive causes. Possessed great intelligence, sense of humor and warmth. Died peacefully at home on April 14, 2007. Lovingly remembered by two children, six grandchildren, one great-grandchild, extended family and friends.
