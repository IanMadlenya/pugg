
        CORNING-Ursula. The Board, staff and participants of The Student Conservation Association, Inc. (SCA) sadly note the death of Ursula Corning. For nearly two decades, Miss Corning was a staunch and generous supporter of SCA's conservation and youth development programs. She particularly loved to receive letters from the young people she supported in the field. Miss Corning's devotion to youth and the environment will be greatly missed.
