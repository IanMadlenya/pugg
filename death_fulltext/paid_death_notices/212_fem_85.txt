
        HUGHES-CAMM--Jean Frances, 34, died September 8, 2004, after a courageous battle with Hodgkins disease, at the M.D. Anderson Cancer Center in Houston, Texas. Jean grew up in the Bronx, attended Monsignor Scanlan High School and the State University at Albany, where she graduated in 1991. Jean married David Camm in 1992 and moved to Florida where she lived close to her father, Martin Hughes. Jean is surivived by her husband, David Camm, her daughter Caitlin, 3, her father Martin Hughes and his wife Marjorie, her mother Phyllis Hughes, her loving brother Thomas Huges of Brooklyn and many loving aunts, uncles and cousins. Services will be held at Stower's Funeral Home, 401 West Brandon Boulevard, Brandon, FL. 813-689-1211, on Sunday, September 12, 2004, 4pm to 8pm. A mass will be celebrated on Monday, September 13, 10am, at St. Stephen Church, 5049 Bell Shoals Road, Valrico, FL.
        HUGHES-CAMM--Jean Frances. It is with overwhelming sadness that we mourn the loss of our beautiful niece and cousin, Jean Hughes-Camm. We will miss our courageous gentle Jean.
