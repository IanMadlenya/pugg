
        JOSEPHSON--Anne, 95. Died peacefully in her sleep Monday. She was a retired speech therapist, an avid reader and bridge player. She was the wife of the late Abe Josephson and the sister of the late Becky Goldspinner and Dr. Morton Polokoff. She is survived by her loving children and their spouses, Dr. Barry Josephson and his wife Dixie Roberts Josephson, Harriet Josephson and her husband Jack Sachs. She was the proud grandmother of her two grandsons and their spouses, Mark Josephson and Megan Josephson and Dr. Braden Josephson and his wife Dr. Sarah Church. She was the delighted greatgrandmother of Henry, Eliza and Theodore Josephson. She will always remain in the hearts of those who loved her. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, May 24 at 11:30AM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St., New York. Shiva will be observed at her daughter's home on Thursday and Friday from 3-6 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to Women and Infants Hospital, Office of Development, The Program in Woman's Oncology, 101 Dudley Street, Providence, RI 02905.
