
        DICK-Frederick S. On August 17, 2001. Beloved husband of Marion and his late wife Ruth. Father to Barbara and Michael. Grandfather to Alexandra, Matthew, Rebekka & Sofia. Born in Toronto, Canada, September 5, 1913. He practiced medicine for 62 years including during his service in the U.S. Army in World War II. He will be missed by all who knew him. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Sunday, August 19th at 9:30 A.M. Interment to follow at King David Cemetery, Putnam Valley. The family will be receiving at his home from Sunday, the 19th through Tuesday the 21st. In lieu of flowers please send donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, Melanoma Research, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
        DICK-Frederick S. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Frederick S. Dick our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
