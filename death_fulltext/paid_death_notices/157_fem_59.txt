
        MATSUSHIMA-Anne, MD. Thirtysix, passed away on Thursday January 20, 2000 in Minneapolis. She worked as a physician at the Presbyterian Hospital of Columbia University, New York, from 1989-1998, and thereafter at the University of Minnesota Medical Center. Her husband Matthias J. Szabolcs, mother Reiko Matsushima, and brother Peter Matsushima mourn her death with deep sadness. A memorial service will be held Thursday, Feb. 10 at 3 P.M. at the Columbarium of the Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine at 110 St. and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations may be made in her memory to this church.
