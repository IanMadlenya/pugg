
        SCHOLL-Walter A. Age 79, retired vice president and member of the New York Stock Exchange passed away at home on Sunday, May 17, 1998 in Boynton Beach, Florida. Mr. Scholl was born on Staten Island, NY and graduated from Cornell University where he distinguished himself on the football field and baseball diamond. He was elected to the Cornell Athletic Hall of Fame and threw the famous fifth down pass play that beat Dartmouth in 1940. He served as an officer in the Army Air Corps during WWII and received many citations including the Distinguished Flying Cross and the Silver Star. He was a member of the Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 2007 of West Palm Beach, the American Legion Post 0012, the Buttonwood Club of the New York Stock Exchange, Vasa Order of America Royal Palm Lodge #
