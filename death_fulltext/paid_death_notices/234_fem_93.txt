
        McMULLEN--Mary Patricia, died Thursday, July 13, from cancer. Born March 19, 1930 in New York City to Thomas and Marion Hughes, she is survived by her sister Bernardine Whitmore of Los Angeles, two nieces, and also by her former husband Donald McMullen. Services will be held Monday 10:30 AM at Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, NY. Mary found comfort in life's simple pleasures and we are happy she is now at peace.
