
        MARKEL-Edward. The family of Edward Markel mourn the death of their husband, father and grandfather. Mr. Markel was the former city editor of the New York Daily Mirror retiring in Nov. 1963. He was a former trustee for the Manhattan Savings Bank and a member of the board of directors of the Madison Square Boys Club. Contributions in Mr. Markel's memory may be made to the Madison Square Boys Club or the John E. Markel Park. For information Beecher Funeral Home, 1 Putnam Avenue, Brewster, NY
