
        ROSEN-Natalie. Age 72, of Franklin Lakes, NJ, on February 2, 2002. Survived by her husband of fifty years Philip, sons Dr. Joseph Rosen of Brattleboro, VT, Daniel Rosen of Wayne, NJ, Mark Rosen of Franklin Lakes, NJ; brothers Murray and Leo Simpson of West Hempstead, NY and Seymour Simpson of Petach Tikvah, Israel; grandchildren Lena, Gershon, Bennett, Matthew, Jason and Sarah. Services were held Sunday. Contributions in her memory may be made to Stahl-Rosen Building Construction Fund at Brattleboro Area Jewish Community, P.O. Box 2353, Brattleboro, VT 05303.
