
        BIERMAN-Jordan B. On Jan. 13, 2002 at age 73 after a courageous battle with brain cancer. Beloved husband of Ellen, caring father of Daniel and his wife Alice, devoted stepfather of Karen Aibel and Jeffrey Aibel and wife Kathryn, doting Poppa of Aaron Bierman and Sydney Aibel, brother of Judie Linowes and Eugene Munves. Recently retired senior partner of Bierman, Muserlian and Lucas. He was a brilliant intellectual property attorney. Member of Mensa and Metropolis Country Club. Memorial services at Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Avenue, White Plains, NY, Thursday, January 17th, 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to Hospice of Westchester, 95 S. Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601, or Cancer Care, 275 7th Avenue, New York, NY 10001.
