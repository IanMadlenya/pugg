
        SIMON-Jacob L., 94. Died April 17 in Syracuse. Formerly of Lawrence, NY. Graduate of NYU. A former member of Temple Beth El of Cedarhurst. Author of ''It's Your Business'', and contributor to many accounting journals. Mr. Simon's firm, J.L. Simon and Company, Certified Public Accountants had offices in New York City. His wife Frances died in 1998. Devoted father of Dr. David and Dr. Robert, loving grandfather and great grandfather. Services and burial were in Syracuse.
