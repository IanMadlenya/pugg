
        EFFRON--James W., 73, died peacefully after a brief illness on July 21 after a happy and well-lived life. He was the beloved husband of Dawn; adored father of Craig (Caryn), Blair (Cheryl), Drew (Sandra) and Brooke (Ross); and cherished grandfather of eight. He was born and lived proudly his entire life on the same street in Poughkeepsie, NY, and was a true and warm friend to all who knew him. After graduating from Princeton University in 1953, he served his country in the Army. He spent his career with Effron Oil Company, serving as its President for many years. Funeral services Monday, 11 AM, at Vassar Temple, Poughkeepsie. Shiva at the Effron home in Poughkeepsie on Monday and Tuesday. Shiva in New York at 129 East 73rd Street on Wednesday from 6-9 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, to be credited to Dr. Stephen Nimer Leukemia Research.
        EFFRON--James. We will miss our skiing buddy! Our trips together and your unique ''storytelling'' will always be cherished. Our love to the entire Effron family. The Rosens: Debbie, Brett, Jamie, Rachel and Kyle
