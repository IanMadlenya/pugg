
        GOODMAN-Rita, Vice President Emeritus, The Johnson Foundation, Racine, Wisconsin died February 28, 2000, in Sarasota, FL. She was preceded in death by her husband, Arnold Goodman. She is survived by three sons and daughter-in-law, David and Kazuko, Norman and Kathy, Andrew and Sharon; five grandchildren, Yael, Kai, Moshe, Meira, and Alexandra; and her companion, Sanford Solender. At her request, her remains were cremated and scattered in the Gulf of Mexico. Memorial services will be held at 3:00 PM, Sunday March 5, 2000 at Temple Beth Isreal on Longboat Key, FL. Memorials to your favorite charity will be appreciated. For more information contact Toale Brothers Funeral Home, Colonial Chapel, Sarasota, FL.
