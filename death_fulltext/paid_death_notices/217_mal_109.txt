
        BOXER--Robert, M.D. The Physicians and Staff of the Department of Pediatrics, Columbia University Medical Center and the Babies Hospital Alumni Association wish to express our heartfelt sorrow and condolences to the family of Robert Boxer, M.D. Bob completed his residency 1972-1975, a fellowship in pediatric cardiology 1975-1978 and was an attending at Babies Hospital from 1978-1980. He will be greatly missed by his friends and colleagues. John M. Driscoll, Jr., M.D. Reuben S. Carpentier Professor Chairman, Department of Pediatrics Neil I. Minikes, M.D. Babies Hospital Alumni Association, President
