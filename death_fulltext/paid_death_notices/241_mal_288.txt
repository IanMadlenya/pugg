        LEVINE--Harold. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Harold Levine, beloved brother of UJA-Federation board member and Lawyers Division chair, Alan Levine; and nephew of Doris and Stanley Tananbaum. The Levine and Tananbaum families' wholehearted and generous commitment to the Jewish community has enabled us to serve those in need, profoundly enhancing lives in New York and around the world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Harold's wife, Carol; to Doris and Stanley; to Alan; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
