
        TOMS-John L., 72, passed away April 19, 1997 after a brief battle with cancer. Born February 9, 1925 in Bronx, New York, he served with the U.S. Army during WWII in the Pacific Theater. He attended Iona College and worked for the NY Central R.R. For the last 36 years he worked for Everlast Sporting Goods in the Bronx and known as ''Mr. Everlast'' in the boxing arena, he was Vice President of Sales at Everlast for 25 years where he was involved in every major event in the industry. During all of this, he managed to raise and support two families of three children and support several charities in his hometown. He was an avid Yankee fan and also Notre Dame's Fighting Irish. He is survived by his devoted wife, Jean Toms of Houston and his loving children: Linda Lea D'Onofrio, of West Creek, NJ; John Toms, Jr. of Huntington, NY; Christina Levi of Hicksville, NY; Jeff Schmidt of East Patchogue, NY; Dean Schmidt of Monterey, CA; Michael Schmidt of Houston, TX.; and seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Pinelawn National Cemetery, Melville, New York, on Monday, April 28, 1997 at 10:45 AM, (516) 249-6100. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
