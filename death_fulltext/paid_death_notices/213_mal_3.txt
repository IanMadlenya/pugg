
        GINDICK--Robert A., 51, died Wednesday, September 29, after a long battle with HIVrelated illness. He is survived by his beloved partner, James Anderson of NY, NY. Mr. Gindick was founder and president of Gindick Productions Ltd., a film production company and wholly owned subsidiary of Grey Advertising Inc. His life was marked not only by his business success and enormous creativity, but also by his warmth, friendship, humor and indomitable spirit. A memorial service celebrating his life is being planned for a later date. Contributions may be made in his memory to Friends in Deed, 594 Broadway, Suite 70, New York, New York 10012.
