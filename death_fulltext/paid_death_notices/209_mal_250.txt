
        LIEBERMAN--Warren. Died unexpectedly June 24, at 56. Beloved husband of Linda Hirsch, adored father of Jacob Lieberman, loving brother of Lila Winder and Harriet Avramescu, devoted brotherin-law, uncle and friend to so many. A creative, gentle spirit, gifted photographer, artist, teacher, and acclaimed advertising creative director. His love for his family was unbounded. Our loss and grief are beyond expression. Services Sunday, Plaza Chapel, 91st & Amsterdam, 11:30 AM.
