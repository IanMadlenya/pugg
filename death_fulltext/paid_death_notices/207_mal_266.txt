
        CONN--Alexander. Born June 23, 1923, died April 21, 2004. Beloved husband of Maxine and respected and honored father of Carin, Joel and Barbara, and stepfather of Laurie, Paul, Peter and Stephen. A generous and gentle man, he was a loyal and trustworthy friend. Funeral services will be held at Ballard Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY on Sunday, April 25 at 12:30PM. In lieu of floral tributes, please send donations to American Cancer Society.
        CONN--Alexander. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member and past president, Alex Conn. On behalf of all our members, we express our deepest sympathy to his wife, Mickey, and their entire family. Jacqueline Bellsey, President Dale Winston, Secretary
