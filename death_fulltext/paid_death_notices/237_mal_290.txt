
        KROBOT--Charles J., Jr. Founder Kilroy Metal Products, 89, on October 26, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Sue, loving father of Charles and his wife, Stephanie, proud grandfather of Jonathan and Jennifer. Founder and retired Chairman of Kilroy Metal Products. Founder of Subcontractors Trade Association. Benefactor of local charities and organizations. WWII veteran. Viewing Saturday, October 28th, 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Funeral services private.
