
        GARZON-Manuela Diaz, widow of Julio Garzon, resident of Tamarac, FL, and formerly of Miami, Washington, D.C., and New York, NY, passed away in Tamarac on Friday, July 3, 1998. She was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, on August 13, 1909. She was a longtime member of the Puerto Rican American Women's League. She is survived by her daughters Maria Garzon of Los Angeles, CA, and Laura Wyman of Washington, DC, three grandchildren and two great - grandchildren. Memorial service will be at St. Helen Catholic Church, 3033 NW 33rd Avenue, Ft. Lauderdale, at 12 PM on July 8, 1998.
