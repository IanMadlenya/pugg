
        WEINBERG-Celia Damsey. On January 29, 2003, approaching her 97th birthday, Cele Weinberg died peacefully and with the love of all who knew her. She leaves her loving and beloved children Jay Weinberg and Rita Bikel (Theodore) and her adoring and greatly adored grandsons, Robert Bikel (Judi) and Daniel Bikel, who learned the pleasure of family and fun from her. In lieu of flowers the family would like to have contributions made in her honor and memory to the Rockville Centre Chapter of Hadassah, which she served with pride as a lifetime member.
