
        ATHINEOS-George. On July 16, at the age of 78, of Riverdale, NY. Beloved husband of Evangeline whom he adored, and they adored each other very much. He was a devoted and loving father of Elizabeth and Stephen Athineos, and cherished grandfather of his grandchildren. They will miss their Papou. Services to be held today, Sunday, July 21, from 2 to 4 and 6 to 9 PM, at the Riverdale Funeral Home, 5044 Bway, NYC. Funeral at the Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, New Rochelle, NY, on Monday, July 22, at 10 AM. George, you touched everyone's heart that you met and were respected by all. You will be forever missed and never forgotten.
