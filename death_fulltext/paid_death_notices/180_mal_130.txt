
        SUMPTION-James D. Of Bronxville, NY, and Vero Beach, FL, age 62, passed away on January 6, 2002. The beloved husband of Julie, loving father of Scott and brother of R. Stephen Sumption was born in Philadelphia, PA, the elder son of Horace Y. and Emily A. Sumption of West Chester, PA. He received a BA from Colgate University in 1961 and served as an officer in The United States Navy from 1962-1965. He began his business career with U.S. Trust Company and later established and was President of Pin Oak Capital, Ltd., a New York investment firm, a subsidiary of Stifel, Nicolaus & Company, Incorporated. He was an Elder at The Reformed Church of Bronxville and a past President of Siwanoy Country Club. He was also a member of the Union League Club of New York, Red Stick Golf Club and Orchid Island Golf and Beach Club of Vero Beach, Florida. A memorial service will be held at Tthe Reformed Church of Bronxville on Saturday, January 12, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to The Deacon's Fund of The Reformed Church of Bronxville or to Colgate University.
