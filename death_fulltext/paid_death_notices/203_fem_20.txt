
        MULLANE--Cesiah Toro. We mourn the loss of an irreplaceable community activist and tireless President of the Board of Directors of the Cypress Hills Local Development Corporation. We extend our condolences to Cesi's beloved husband Michael, and all her children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. Cesi will be sorely missed by those whose lives she touched and enriched.
