
        FESLER -- James William, Alfred Cowles Professor of Government Emeritus at Yale University, died April 26 in Branford, CT. He was 94. A graduate of the University of Minnesota, he received his doctorate from Harvard. After 16 years at the University of North Carolina (Chapel Hill), in 1951 he joined Yale as the Cowles Professor and Chairman of the Department of Political Science, retiring in 1979. Predeceased by his wife Frances, Professor Fesler is survived by his daughter Janet Fesler, son James Fesler, daughter-in-law Jacqueline Fesler, granddaughter Lillian Fesler, and step-grandson Sacha Twarog. Memorial contributions may be made to The Community Foundation for Greater New Haven, 70 Audubon St, New Haven, CT 06510.
