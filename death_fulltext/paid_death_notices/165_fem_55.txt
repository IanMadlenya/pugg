
        VIETA-Henrietta E. Countiss. Peacefully at home on September 28, 2000. Beloved wife of the late John O. Vieta, M.D. Loving mother of Dr. Paul, Frances, Jona. Dearest mother-in-law of Leila Vieta and Richard Larm. Cherished grandmother of Kathryn, Amy, Paul, Laura, Robin, and Jonathan. Dear sister of Barbara Lee of Denver, Colorado. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Friday, October 6, at 5:00 PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the John O. Vieta Memorial Lecture, care of Lenox Hill Hospital, 100 E. 77 St., NYC 10021.
