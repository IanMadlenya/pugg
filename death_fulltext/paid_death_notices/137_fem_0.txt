
        DOUGLAS-Harriet (Meyer). Of Goshen, NY, formerly of New York City, died May 30, in Goshen at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Harold S. Douglas. Loving mother of Dr. Robert L. Douglas (Marjorie), Susan D. Feibus (Howard). She is survived by nine sisters, one brother, predeceased by one brother. Cherished grandmother of Michael Feibus (Debbie), Jonathan Feibus (Mary), Andrew Douglas and Elizabeth Douglas. Great-grandmother of Matthew Beau Feibus. She was a Beth Israel Hospital nurse and social worker. Services noon, Tuesday, June 2, Temple Sinai, 75 Highland, Middletown, NY. Interment in Montefiore Cemetery, Queens. Contributions to charity of one's choice.
