
        MILSTEIN--Barbara (nee Adduchio). Age 74. Died at home on March 15, 2006 surrounded by her family after a brave four-year struggle with ovarian cancer. A resident of Greenwich, CT and Jupiter, FL, she lives on in the hearts of her husband of 50 years, Sidney, three children, six grandchildren and scores of close friends. A graduate of New York University and Bridgeport University, she, along with her husband, led Aegis Productions, a documentary film production company in Manhattan. She later worked as a counselor in weight control programs. She served as a president in the League of Women Voters and as a trustee of William Patterson College. Contributions may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County (www.hpbc.com) or to the Ovarian Cancer Research Foundation (www.ocrf.com).
