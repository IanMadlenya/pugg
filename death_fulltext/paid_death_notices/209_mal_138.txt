
        ROSSI--John David. On May 31, 2004 in San Clemente, CA. Born June 4, 1927 in the Bronx to Anita and John Rossi. Served in the US Army in France and Germany in the aftermath of WWII. He had two passions in life, his family and the FDNY. Loving husband of Mary Carey, father of five, John (Marienza), Maureen (Bill), Patti (Danny), Michael (Becca), Stephen (Kim) and grandfather of seventeen. He had 32 years on the job and retired as Deputy Assistant Chief. He is also survived by two sisters, Helen Peter and Sylvia Brown, and two brothers, Roland Rossi and Albert Rossi. Memorial Mass will be July 30, 2004, 10 AM at St. Edward Catholic Church, Dana Point, CA. Donations can be made to the New York Firefighters Burn Center Foundation, 21 Asch Loop, Bronx, NY 10475.
