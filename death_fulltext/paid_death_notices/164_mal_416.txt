
        GOLDFRANK-Herbert John. Died at his home in Thornwood, N.Y. on September 9, 2000. He would have celebrated 93 years on September 11th. He is survived by his wife Helen, his son Lewis and daughter-in-law Susan, his daughter Joan and son-in-law Michael, five grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Donations may be made to the Ethical Society of Northern Westchester, Ossining, N.Y. 10562.
