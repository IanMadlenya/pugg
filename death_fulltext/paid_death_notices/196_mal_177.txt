
        HAIGNEY - John E. The Sound Shore Medical Center of Westchester (formerly New Rochelle Hospital) notes with great sorrow the death of John E. Haigney. Jack was a valued member of the Board of Governors for over 20 years, serving as vice chairman for several terms. He was chairman of the law committee and a member of the executive, joint conference, medical affairs and compensation committees. His willingness to devote his time and efforts to meet the hospital's needs was genuinely appreciated. He will be deeply missed by his colleagues on the board and the administrative staff. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. Lawrence J. Ruisi, Chairman/ Board of Governors John R. Spicer, President and CEO
