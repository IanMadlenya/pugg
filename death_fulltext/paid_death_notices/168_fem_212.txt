
        BENISCH-Evelyn Eckenroth. Of Plandome, New York, died Wednesday, January 17, 2001, at home in her 98th year. Beloved wife of the late Charles Edward Benisch. Loving mother of Charles and John, and loving mother-in-law of Nancy and Muriel. Adored grandmother of Susan, Christopher, Loel, Erik, Diane, John, Joanne, and Jason. Devoted great-grandmother to Stephen, Emily, Lindsey, William, John, Peyton, and Spencer. Private memorial service for her family on Wednesday, January 24, 2001, at The Congregational Church of Manhasset. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests that memorials be given to the Memorial Fund of The Congregational Church of Manhasset.
