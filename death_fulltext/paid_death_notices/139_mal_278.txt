
        CAMPBELL-John C., Jr., Brigadier General. The East End Hospice Board of Directors, staff and volunteers note with great sadness the death of John C. Campbell, Jr., devoted husband to Hospice board member and patron, Jane S. Campbell. John was a loyal supporter of Hospice. We are truly grateful to have had his counsel and friendship. We extend our deepest sympathy to Jane, his children, grandchildren, and to his many friends. We shall miss him greatly. W. Michael Pitcher, President Priscilla A. Ruffin, Director East End Hospice, Inc. Westhampton Beach, New York
