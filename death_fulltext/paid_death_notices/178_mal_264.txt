
        LEWIS-Ogden Northrop, Jr. Suddenly on November 14, 2001, at age 22. Cherished son of Ogden and Susan Lewis, adored nephew of Margaret Patterson High and William Hall Lewis, III. Beloved by countless friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to The Buckley School, 113 East 73 Street, New York, New York 10021, or Groton School, Box 991, Groton, Massachusetts 01450, would be greatly appreciated. Funeral arrangements incomplete.
        LEWIS-Ogden Northrop Jr. The Boards of Directors and the staff of Citizens Union of the City of New York and the Citizens Union foundation extend their deepest condolences to the Chair of Citizens Union's Board, Ogden N. Lewis, and his family on the untimely death of his son.
