
        KANIN-Garson. The Producers, Cast and Crew of ''Three Tall Women'' mourn the passing of Garson Kanin whose lifetime devotion to the theatre will be sorely missed by his family, friends and theatre-goers. He contributed to the lives of many with his unique talent and humor. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Marian Seldes, our dear friend and colleague.
        KANIN-Garson. The Dramatists Guild Council mourns the loss of our colleague and fellow Council Member, Garson Kanin. We have no doubt that his work will live forever. Our deepest sympathies to his family. He will be missed. Peter Stone, President The Dramatists Guild
