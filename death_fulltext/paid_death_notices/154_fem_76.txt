
        HANAUER-Lilo (nee Growald). Died peacefully after a long illness, November 10th, 1999 at the age of 87 in her home in New York City. Beloved wife of Bernard Hanauer for 53 years. Sadly missed by daughters Elaine Hanauer Ravich and Linda Hanauer, son-in-law William Ravich and grandaughters Madeline Ravich & Emily Ravich. Beloved nursery school teacher at Stern Kindergarten for over 25 years. Services Thursday, November 11, 1:00 PM at Louis Hirsch & Sons, 1225 Jerome Avenue, Bronx.
