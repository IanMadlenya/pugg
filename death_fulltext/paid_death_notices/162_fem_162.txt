
        RIGGS-Frieda Wildy. 92, died on July 20, 2000 at home in Bronxville, New York. Born on September 8, 1907, in Hemingford, Nebraska, Frieda was the daughter of Calvin and Anna Wildy. She graduated from the University of Colorado at Boulder, and received a master's degree in social work from Bryn Mawr College. After a career in social work, she was a devoted volunteer with her church, the Bronxville School, and the Public Library. Frieda was predeceased by her husband of 53 years, Arad McCutchan Riggs, and her sisters, Lois Wildy and Ruth Wildy. Survivors include one sister, Mary Wildy Zimmer of Denver, Colorado; two children, Robert McCutchan Riggs of Bronxville, New York, and Meredith Riggs Spangler of Charlotte, North Carolina; daughter-in-law Wendy Riggs; son-in-law C.D. Spangler, Jr.; granddaughters Anna Wildy Spangler Nelson, Abigail Riggs Spangler, Jennifer Lois Riggs Vetter, and Sarah Brenda Riggs; and one great-granddaughter, Meredith Spangler Nelson. A memorial celebration of Mrs. Riggs' life will be held on Friday, July 28, 2000 at 3 o'clock p.m. at the Reformed Church in Bronxville.
