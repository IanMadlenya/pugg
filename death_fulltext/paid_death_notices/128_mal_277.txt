
        ROSEMAN-Jack. The National Offices of EMUNAH of America mourn the passing of the beloved husband of our honorary National President, Betty Roseman. Jack Roseman was a staunch and generous friend of EMUNAH who earned the respect of everyone who knew him. We express our heartfelt condolences to Betty, children Surella and Joseph Feit, Rena and Michael Reinhard, Chani and Simon Lichtiger and grandchildren. Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld, Nat'l Pres.
