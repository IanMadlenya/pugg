        Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Sisterhood mourn the passing of our very devoted senior member and extend heartfelt sympathy to all members of the bereaved family. Haskel Lookstein, Rabbi Joshua Lookstein, Assnt. Rabbi Fred Distenfeld, President
