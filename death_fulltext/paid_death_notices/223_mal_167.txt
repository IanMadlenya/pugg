
        FRIEDMAN--Jack. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum mourns the death of Jack N. Friedman. As a Museum Founder, his commitment to Holocaust remembrance and exceptional generosity helped sustain the growth and success of the Museum. The Museum extends its deepest condolences to his wife Lorraine, his children Lynne, Mitchell, and and Freddy, and his grandchildren. Fred S. Zeidman, Chair Ruth B. Mandel, Vice Chair Sara J. Bloomfield, Director United States Holocaust Memorial Museum
