
        EICHBERG-Joseph. Age 90, died in Atlanta, Feb. 1, 1997. He was President of the American Lecithin Company for over 60 years. Predeceased by wives Lucille Rosenfeld Eichberg and Florence May Eichberg, and daughter Susan Eichberg Whitfield. Survivors include son Joseph Eichberg, Jr., son-in-law Larry Whitfield and five grandchildren.
