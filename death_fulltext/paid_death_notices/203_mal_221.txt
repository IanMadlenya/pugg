
        BROWNER--Harry M., 96, Associate Professor of Political Science and History at Palm Beach Community College; taught economic-geography in NYC; professional photographer; employed by the post office. Born in Brooklyn of immigrant parents, he quickly became his father's interpreter; his father was a builder and he translated Yiddish into English for the other workers. Educated at NYU and Columbia University. A member of the Hillcrest Jewish Center, Hebrew Congregation of Somers, Temple Beth Shalom in Mahopac, and Temple Beth El in Palm Beach, FL. His late wife of 57 years was Lee Carlin Browner. Survived by two daughters and sons-in-law, Eleanor and Bill Greco of Forked River, NJ, and Barbara and Charles Schiller of Yorktown Heights, NY; grandchildren, Laura, Andrew and Zachary.
