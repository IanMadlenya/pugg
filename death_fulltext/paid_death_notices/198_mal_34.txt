
        WEINBERG--Charles H. The members and employees of Mazur, Carp & Rubin mourn the passing of our dear colleague, partner, and friend Charles H. Weinberg on June 27. Charlie brought light to every discussion and a smile to every face. His erudition, wisdom, kindness to all and joie de vivre can be emulated, but never replaced. Our sympathies to his wife Dorothy, daughters Elizabeth & Nancy. Mazur, Carp & Rubin, PC >>AD#
