
        LEVY--Jon R., died on April 29, 2004, in Southampton, NY, of cancer at the age of 63. He was the founder of the Gillian Group of which he was the president and CEO for 24 years until 1995. At the time of his death, he was working on Wall Street as a research analyst. He is survived by his three daughters, Jennifer Levy, Amanda Laserson and Hannah Levy, his son-in-law Jesse Laserson and his granddaughter Sophie, as well as his sister Diane Roth and his brother-in-law Herbert Roth. Memorial services will be held Sunday, May 2, at Riverside Memorial Chapel in Manhattan, at 11:15am.
