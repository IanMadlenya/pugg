
        BLACKWELL--Elsie Wilson, 93. Of Basking Ridge, New Jersey, formerly of Dorset, VT, Short Hills, NJ, and Point O'Woods, NY, on November 26, 2005. Daughter of Susie M. and Edward T. Wilson of Denver, CO, she graduated from Mt. Vernon Seminary where she later served as a trustee and Sarah Lawrence College. Her husband of 71 years, Edward S., Jr. predeceased her in July. She is survived by her brother, Robert B. Wilson, her children, Janet Bent, Edward S. Blackwell III, and Marion Meserole, nine grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. Memorial service at Christ Church, Short Hills, NJ at 10 A.M. on Friday, December 2. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Southern Vermont Arts Center, P.O. Box 617, West Road, Manchester, VT. 05252.
