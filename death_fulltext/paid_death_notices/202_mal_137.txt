
        MILLER--Wade, 79, of Millburn, NJ, died on Sunday, November 9, 2003. He was an actor/dancer in theatre, film, and TV in Hollywood and New York City. He was Stage Manager and then General Manager of Paper Mill Playhouse in Millburn, NJ. Services held at The Chapels at Short Hills, Millburn, NJ, Monday, November 17, 2 PM.
