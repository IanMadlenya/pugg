
        HOFFMAN-Joseph. Passed away April 4, 1999 in Hollywood, FL at the age of 96. Formerly of Great Neck, N.Y. He leaves his devoted wife of 64 years Ruth, son Richard of Hallandale, FL. and daughter Frances Swirsky. Papa Joe to Peter and Douglas Genzer and great granddaughter Rachel. He will be remembered as a loving, kind and gentle soul. Contributions may be made to The Boys' Club of N.Y. and Joe DiMaggio Children's Hospital-Memorial East, Hollywood, FL. We will love you forever.
        HOFFMAN-Joseph. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Sidney Freedman, President
