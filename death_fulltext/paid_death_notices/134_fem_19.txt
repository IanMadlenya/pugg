
        KESTER-Leah. Formerly of Bayside, New York, died February 27 in Hershey, PA. Surviving are her husband, Allen; two sons, Dr. Mark of Harrisburg, PA and Eric of Bayside; a sister, Lillian Fink of Rochester, and two grandchildren. Burial was Sunday in Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Queens. Shiva will be observed at 18-75 Corporal Kennedy Street, Apt. 5-G, Bayside, NY. Memorials to the Jewish National Fund, 42 East 69th Street, New York, NY 10021, or the American Heart Association.
