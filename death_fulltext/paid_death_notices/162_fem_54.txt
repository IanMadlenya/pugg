
        JUNGMANN-Gert. Died June 21, at 91 in Rangeley, Maine. The widow of Martin Jungmann, M.D. of Vienna, Austria and New York, she advanced his innovative concept of the Levitor, an orthotic device to treat chronic back and muscular disorders, helping to establish fifty Levitor centers worldwide. Donations may be sent to the National Levitor Center, Kirksville College of Osteopathic Medicine, Kirksville Missouri, 63501. She leaves her sister, Edith Wolter of New York and her son, Henry Jungmann and family of Tyngsboro, Massachusetts.
