
        WHITMORE-Edward Knight. On June 12, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Virginia R. Whitmore. Devoted father of Joy W. Goldstein and Ned Whitmore. He also leaves six grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. May his love of life live on forever in the hearts of those who cherished his memory. Memorial service on Friday, June 19, at 11 AM, All Saints Episcopal Church, Bay Head, NJ. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made in his name for the memorial fund of All Saints Church, Lake Ave, Bay Head, New Jersey 08742.
