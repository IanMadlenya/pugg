
        BAGLANEAS-Reverend Theodore E. Formerly of New York, Rhode Island and Cape Cod, died Saturday. He was the husband of the late Mary (Joakim) Baglaneas. He is survived by a son John T. Baglaneas of Northboro, MA, and a daughter Anita Devlin of Centerville, MA, also a brother Spiros Baglaneas and sister Anatoula Baglaneas both of Athens, Greece, also four grandchildren. A presanctified Liturgy will begin at 9 AM, Tuesday at the George Greek Orthodox Church, Rte 28, Centerville, MA, followed by a funeral service at 11 AM. Burial will follow at Mosswood Cemetery in Cotuit, MA. Visiting hours will be Monday 5 to 9 PM, at the Doane, Beale & Ames Funeral Home, 160 West Main St, Hyannis, MA. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Boston Alzheimer's Center c/o the Reverend Theodore E. Baglaneas Memorial Fund, 434 Jamaica Way, Boston, MA 02130.
