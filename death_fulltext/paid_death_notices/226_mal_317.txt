
        PREZIOSO--Silvio. We note with sorrow the passing of Silvio Prezioso on November 24, 2005, at the age of 94. Beloved husband of Jennie for 70 years. Father to Sylvia, MaryJane and Geraldine. Beloved father-in-law to Silvio and Danny, grandfather of seven and great-grandfather of twelve. Past president and leader for over 35 years for the Display Fixture Workers Local 2682 United Brotherhood of Carpenters. He will be loved and missed by all!
