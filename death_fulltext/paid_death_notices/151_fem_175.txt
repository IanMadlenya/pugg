
        ALEXANDER-Hannah Margolis on August 19, 1999, of N.Y. City and East Quogue. Beloved wife of the late Josef; loving mother of Gregory, Jonathan, Matthew and the late Lewis.Cherished mother-inlaw of Karen, Hanita and Elaine. Adoring grandmother of Jessica, Gabriel, Daniel, Seth, Benjamin and Ethan. Devoted aunt of Phyllis, Doris, Leslie and Rita. Poet, author and loving friend. She will be profoundly missed. Her life was a celebration of creativity, beauty, kindness and thoughtfulness. Service Sunday 11am at the ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
