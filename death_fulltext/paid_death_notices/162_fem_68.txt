
        CARPENTER - Patricia, passed away July 8, 2000 at her home in Yorktown Heights, N.Y. She was 77 years old. Patricia was born on January 21, 1923 to Daniel and Dorothy Carpenter in Del Rosa, CA. Student Composition and Theory with Arnold Schoenberg, 1942-1949; Student Piano with Ethel Leginska, 1943-1948. Chairman Department of Music, Barnard College; 1974-. An Essay in The Phenomenology of Musical Form, 1971, Arnold Schoenberg: The Musical Idea, 1995. Patricia is survived by her long time companion Dr. Silvio Baez. Cherished neices and nephews; Patty Davis, Mike Staples, Laurie Mendiones and Pamela Andersen. Funeral services are private. Contributions may be made: American Cancer Society.
