
        ROSEVEAR-Kenneth White, 93. Died September 15, 2000. Husband of the late Leslie (nee Ferguson), he is survived by two daughters, Cora Rosevear and Linda Greenberg, and two grandchildren, Leslie and Paul. A longtime resident of New York and Roslyn, Long Island, he lived in Sykesville, Maryland, for the last decade. Memorial service at St. Barnabas Church, Sykesville, on October 14th, at 10:00 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The New York Public Library, 42nd Street Branch, Division of Art and Architecture.
