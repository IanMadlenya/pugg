
        LEIGH--Jane Horn. Died peacefully on February 22, 2006 in Boca Raton, Florida. Beloved wife of Samuel Leigh, devoted mother of Steven Horn Leigh and Nancy Leigh. Grandmother of Cassandra ''Casey,'' Jordan and Brian Leigh. Sister of Sola Lee Kaye of NYC. She was fiercely devoted to family, and her sense of humor will be sorely missed. She was an excellent bridge player and an avid gardener. Her flowers will miss her too. Interment was private.
