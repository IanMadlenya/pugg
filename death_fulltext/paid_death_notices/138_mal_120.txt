
        HENNEMAN-John B. Jr. Age 62, died July 7 in Princeton, NJ, of cancer. A scholar of French Medieval history. He was first a professor then a librarian in an academic career of more than 30 years. Since 1983, he was History Bibliographer at Firestone Library at Princeton University. He is survived by his wife Margery, his mother Esther, his brother Edward, three children John, Charles, Laurie, and two grandchildren John and Elizabeth. A burial service will be held in Buckingham County, Virginia on July 13 at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Woodberry Forest School, Virginia or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
