
        BESSELAAR--G. Hein, M.D., Ph.D., 70, Chairman of the Board of PharmaNet Inc, an international drug development company, died on March 13, in Madrid, Spain. In 1976, he founded G.H. Besselaar Associates and built it into a worldwide leader in outsourced clinical drug development. Corning Inc acquired the company in 1989 and Dr. Besselaar continued to manage it until 1992. Corning became Corning Pharmaceutical Services and later Covance, a large publicly traded company. In 1996 Dr. Besselaar founded PharmaNet.
