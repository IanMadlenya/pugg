
        OSTROW-Rose (Rosie). Passed away Monday June 4, 2001. She was the devoted wife of the late Sidney Ostrow, beloved mother of Linda Lawrence, and Joan Gottlieb, and revered grandmother of Rand Lawrence, Tracy Lawrence-Brookman, Nicole and Jordan Gottlieb, as well as idolized sister of Louis Hoch, and cherished sister-in-law of Pearl Gumora, and Dorothy Ochser. Funeral service will be held at Boulevard-Riverside, Hewlett, LI at 10 A.M. Wednesday June 6th. A woman whose life was devoted to giving and receiving the unrequited love of her family.
