
        HARRIS-Joseph. December 13, 1900-April 1, 1983. Dearest loving husband of Sylvia & dearest loving father of J. Robert and James B. Dearest loving grandfather and great-grandfather. A great business & theatrical legend in his own time. Original producer of Broadway shows, ''Sweet Charity'' and ''Mame''. Dad, you're the greatest ever. We all dearly love you and miss you so very much. God bless you forever. All our love. Your loving sons, Bob and Jim
