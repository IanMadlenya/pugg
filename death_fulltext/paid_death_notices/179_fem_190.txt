
        BATDORF-OSGOOD-Terrie L. On December 27, 2001, after a long and heroic battle with cancer. Beloved wife of Harvey. Loving stepmother to Hilary & Todd. Adoring grandmother to Jessica & Jacob. Enthusiastic booster of her hometown, Wauseon, OH. Loyal alumnus of Ohio University. Dedicated employee of Citibank for over twenty-five years. She was loved and respected by friends, family and co-workers near and far. The funeral will be in Ohio. Contributions may be made to Cancercare.
