
        LAWRENCE--John S. Died on August 12, 2006, at age 97. Devoted husband of Florence for 69 years. Beloved father of Jim Lawrence and Betsy Pinover Schiff. Adoring grandfather of Justin Lawrence. Father-in-law of Helene Lawrence and Edward L. Schiff. A proud and optimistic man who came from humble beginnings and devoted his latter years to charitable endeavors. He was a role model of strength and dignity. His warmth, optimism and true spirit of generosity touched the lives of everyone he knew and will live in the hearts of family and friends forever.
