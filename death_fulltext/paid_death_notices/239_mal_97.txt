
        ZAHN--John H., of Piermont, NY. Son of John and Irene Zahn. Passed away on December 5th, 2006 at the age of 73. John served four years as the Mayor for the Village of Piermont and nine years as a Village Trustee. John is survived by his wife, Janet M. Zahn, four children and six grandchildren. A mass of Christian burial will be held on Saturday December 9th, 2006 at 10 am at St. John's Church, Piermont, NY.
