
        SUTER-Janice L. 53, died January 29, 1999 in Venice, FL of breast cancer. President of Suter Associates, an executive search firm in Stamford, CT, she resided in NYC from 1968 until 1988 and worked at J.C. Penney Co. She belonged to the Sandbar Beach Club of Quogue, the Junior League, NYC, and the Church of the Ascension. In lieu of flowers donations to Bennett Cancer Center, 2015 Main Street, Stamford, CT. 06902.
