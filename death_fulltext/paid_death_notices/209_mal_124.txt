
        FENTON--John, of New York City died June 8th, 2004 at the age of 75. Devoted husband of Yuri Fenton and beloved father of John Fenton, Anne Fenton-Wojtala and Agnes Mironov. He is also survived by six grandchildren. Graduate of Wesleyan University. He worked at U.N. University (Tokyo) as Chief of Information Services. He was an avid reader and loved to travel, to talk about politics and to laugh. The memorial service will be held July 17, 2004 at the Princeton Club of New York from 4-7PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Wesleyan University, Middletown CT 06459.
