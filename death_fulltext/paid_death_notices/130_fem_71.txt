
        PARKS-Lillian Rogers. On Thursday, November 6, 1997, in Washington, DC. Beloved godmother of Peggy B. Holly. Also surviving are her cousin Lillian Byrd, dear friends Louise Carrera, Cornelia Stokes and Inez Dade, and a host of other friends. On Wednesday, November 12, from 10 AM until service at 11 AM, friends may call at St. Luke's Episcopal Church, 1514 15th Street NW, Washington, DC. Interment Lincoln Memorial Cemetery in Suitland, Maryland. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to St. Luke's Church Candle Guild.
