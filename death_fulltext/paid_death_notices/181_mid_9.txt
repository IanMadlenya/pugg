
        WOLLMERINGER - Friedel (Roos) age 101. Entered into rest February 14th. Beloved wife of the late Robert Wollmeringer. Devoted mother of Bernard and Marion Wollmeringer of Framingham, MA. Dear grandmother of Peter and his wife Lynda Wollmeringer of Winchester, MA., and Robert D. & his wife Elizabeth Wollmeringer of Marlborough, MA. Great-grandmother of Britney and Joseph. She is also survived by many nieces, nephews and friends. Services on Tuesday, February 19 at 1:00 P.M. at the Park West-Riverside Chapel, 333 Amsterdam Avenue, New York City. Donations in her memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
