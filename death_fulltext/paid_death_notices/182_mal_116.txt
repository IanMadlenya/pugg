
        RODIE-Reverend Robert R., Jr., 79, of Lakeville, CT, died at Sharon Hospital, Sharon, CT, on March 3rd. He was rector of Christ Church, Riverdale, NY 1965-1984. Survived by his wife, Polly Wilmer Rodie, and two daughters, Elizabeth Rodie Jones of Meriden, CT, and Jean Rodie of Fanwood, NJ. Services on March 8, 2 p.m., at St. John's Church, Salisbury, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to Episcopal Relief and Development, 815 Second Ave., New York, NY 10017.
