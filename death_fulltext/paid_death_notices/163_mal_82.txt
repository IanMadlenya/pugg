
        PINCUS - Julie. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted, long-time member, Julie Pincus. Julie and his beloved wife Anne, have been members of Rodeph Sholom for over 40 years. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his wife, his children, Edward and Jane, Martin and Pat, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
        PINCUS-Julius. On August 6, 2000 at age 93. Beloved husband for 70 years to Anne. Loving father of Dr. Martin and his wife Patricia, Edward & his wife Jane. Adoring grandfather of Sami, Ben, Matthew and Eli, great-grandfather of Jordan. Dear brother of Lee Spellman. Mr. Pincus was the Founder and President of APSCO Fabrics and one of the first post war traders with Japan. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday 1:30PM. Interment to follow at Mt. Hebron Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
