
        SOLTZ-Max G. Died peacefully on September 19, 2002 at age 94. Beloved husband of Christine. Loving father to Ivy, Billy and Mark. Doting grandfather to Nicole, Julia and Carolyn. Adored fatherin - law of Belinda and Yvonne. Cherished uncle of Karin and Robert. Loyal and devoted friend of many. He served his country with honor during World War II. He was a loving and compassionate man whose memory will remain forever in our hearts.
