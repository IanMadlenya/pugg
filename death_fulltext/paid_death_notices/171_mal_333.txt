
        FEUER-Alex, 71. Of Clifton, NJ on April 23, 2001. Holocaust survivor. Beloved husband of Sylvia Feldman. Devoted father of Kevin, Robert, Karyn Liss and Laura Nelson. Father-in-law of Jon Liss and Dr. Lewis Nelson. Cherished grandfather of Daniel, Ilana, Adina, Joshua & Benjamin. His life was an inspiration to all. He will truly be missed. We will always honor his memory. Donations may be made to the Holocaust Resource Center, C/O Jewish Federaton of Greater Clifton-Passaic, 199 Scoles Ave., Clifton, NJ 07012, or the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 45 Springfield Avenue, Springfield, NJ 07081.
