
        BRY-Thea. Beloved wife of the late Gerhard Bry, mother of Ava Bry Penman (of Brookline, Mass.) and the late Peter Bry, grandmother of Deborah Bry, David Bry and Joshua Penman, motherin-law of Brenna Bry and Lewis Lockwood. Died November 29, 2000 at age 89, of cancer. Devoted clinical psychologist: over 60 years of private practice in New Jersey; former Chief Psychologist of Newark's Head Start Program; cofounder of Institute for Training in Infant and Preschool Mental Health of the Youth Consultation Service, Montclair, N.J. Her enthusiasm for life will be dearly missed by family and colleagues.
