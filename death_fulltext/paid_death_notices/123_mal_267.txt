
        SCHNEIDER-Howard I. On April 20, 1997 at the age of 75. Beloved husband of Selma. Devoted father of Les and Michael. Cherished brother of Doris. Adored grandfather of Ben, Philip and Ross. Admired and loving uncle, cousin, nephew, inlaw and cherished friend to all whose life he touched. His passing saddens us all and yet we will remember his charm, humor, grace, honesty, integrity, directness and zest for life. Services Friday, 1 PM, at Gutterman's, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York.
