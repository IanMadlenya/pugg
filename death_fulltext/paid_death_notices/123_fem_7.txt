
        SACHS-Alice, age 91, died at Hallmark Nursing Centre in North Granville, NY on Saturday, March 29, 1997. Miss Sachs was born Dec. 18, 1905 in Kansas City, MO., the daughter of Charles and Flora (Weil) Sachs. Survivors include her brother, Robert Sachs of West Granville, NY and one nephew, Charles Sachs of Kansas City, MO. Also two great nephews. She is making an anatomical gift of herself to Albany Medical School, in Albany, New York. Services will be held at a later date.
