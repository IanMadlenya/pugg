
        GLADSTONE--Douglas E. 58, Evironmental Scientist and Entrepreneur died of liver cancer October 10th in Gaithersburg, MD. Helped start Environmental Strategies Corporation in 1986, an environmental consulting firm. He had a BA in Biology from Columbia University and a Ph.D. in Ecology and Environmental Science from the University of Pennsylvania. Survived by his wife of 22 years, Lenore Gladstone, daughter Jillian and son Scott. Also survived by his mother Jean Gladstone and brother Peter Gladstone.
