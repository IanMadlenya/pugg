
        BOOTH-Rev. L. Venchael. The American Bible Society mourns the death of the Rev. L. Venchael Booth, 83, a senior trustee of the Society who died November 16, 2002. Rev. Booth was the longtime pastor of Zion Baptist Church in Cincinnati, OH. He was elected to the Bible Society Board in 1967 and elected as senior trustee in 1999. He is survived by his sons Paul, Lavaughn, Rev. William, and daughters Anna-Marie Booth and Dr. Georgia Leeper.
