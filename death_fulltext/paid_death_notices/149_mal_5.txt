
        HABER-Michael. Passed away at the age of 87. He will be missed by his wife Ida, his loving children Rona Haber Mittleman and Robert Haber. He was adored by his loving grandchildren Zachary and Jaime Mittleman, Andrea and Lauren Haber. Michael was a kind and loving man who enjoyed life's simplicities. He lived each day and enjoyed each one to the fullest. He was a man of dignity who looked for and found the best in each human being and in each day. He will be missed by all who knew him. Services Tuesday, 1:30PM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
