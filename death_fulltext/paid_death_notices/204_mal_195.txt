
        SAVITS--Irving A. Beloved husband of Ruth, father of Cheri, Carol, Steve and Robert; grandfather of Mark, Danny, Jenny, Andrea and Toby. A gentle and kind man passed away Sunday, January 11, 2004. He loved his wife, family, heritage and country. We should all know the love Irving and Ruth shared. Funeral is 1 PM, January 13, at Ohev Shalom Synagogue, Wallingford, PA. 610-874-1465. Contributions graciously accepted at UJA-Federation, 130 East 59 St., Room 402A, New York, New York 10022.
        SAVITS--Irving A. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Irving Savits, beloved father of Cheri Friedman and father-in-law of Steven Friedman, cherished friends of UJA-Federation and deeply devoted to the New York Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Cheri, Steve, and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
