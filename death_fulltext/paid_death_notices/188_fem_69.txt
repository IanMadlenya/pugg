
        LAPIN-Lee (Leah). Age 85, of Sarasota, FL, died September 6, 2002. She was born November 16, 1916, in Paris, France and moved to Sarasota from the New York area in 1988. A widow of the late William Lapin. Survivors include a daughter, Lucy L. Lapides of Lakewood Ranch, FL, a son, Philip of Frenchtown, NJ, four grandchildren one greatgrandson. Services will be private. Memorial donations may be made to the American Diabetes Association or a charity of the donor's choice.
