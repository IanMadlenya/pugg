
        OMANOFF--Benjamin. Born September 18th, 1920. Departed September 13th, 2003. Survived by his brother, Samuel Omanoff, nephews, Richard Omanoff, Norman Ochs, Marvin Ochs, and nieces, Judy Sullivan, Julia Omanoff and Ellen Omanoff. Benjamin appeared in several movies including ''Act of Love'' with Kirk Douglas. He owned and ran a successful car business in France and real estate business in NY. He served in the army in Burma/India/China and was an avid moviegoer and baseball fan. He will be deeply missed by all who loved him. Graveside services Wednesday, 11:15, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, Queens, NY. Information: call Nieberg Midwood Chapel, 718-377-2700.
