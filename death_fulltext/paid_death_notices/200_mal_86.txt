
        WILLIAMS--Robert P. of New York and Putnam Counties, passed away on August 28. Survived by Jean Williams, his wife of 44 years; daughters, Dr. Catherine Williams and MaryBeth Williams; sons, Dr. Peter B. Williams, Dr. Wilfred A. Williams, Francis X. and Joseph S.; grandchildren, Ariel, Melissa, Meaghan, Jessica, Jacqueline, Alexander, Abigail and Jasmine; sisters, Carol Julien and Barbara Moomau; brothers, Percy, Edwin and Thomas, and their families. Graduated from St. Ignatius Loyola College Prep in Chicago. Served in the United States Air Force. Graduated from Long Island University and Teachers College Columbia University. Worked as a laboratory technician for Sloan-Kettering Institute and Columbia University Research Division #
