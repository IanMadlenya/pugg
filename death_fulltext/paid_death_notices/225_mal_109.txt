
        MUSCHEL--Louis H. On October 8, 2005. A scientist, an educator at the American Cancer Society, New York City, and a microbiologist at the University of Minnesota. Lieutenant Colonel in the U.S. Army and graduate of Yale and NYU. Beloved husband of the late Anne. His family and friends will miss him very much. Memorial service Monday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th and Amsterdam Ave. Burial to follow at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
