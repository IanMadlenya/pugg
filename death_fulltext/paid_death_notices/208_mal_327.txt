
        GABRIELSON--Dr. Robert M. 87, of Morristown, NJ, on May 27. He is survived by his wife Dorothy (Vaughan); a daughter, Katryn of North Yarmouth, MA; three sons, George of Middlebury, CT, Max of Wilton, CT, and John of Casper, WY; his sister Muriel Tilley of North Carolina; and eight grandchildren. A funeral service will be held at St. Peter's Episcopal Church, South St. and Miller Rd., Morristown, NJ, on Tuesday, June 1, at 10 AM. Burial will follow at Greenwood Cemetery in Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made in the memory of Dr. Gabrielson to the Planned Parenthood of Greater Northern New Jersey, 196 Speedwell Ave., Morristown, NJ; the Morristown Public Library, South St. and Miller Rd., Morristown, NJ; or the Memorial Fund of St. Peter's Episcopal Church, South St. and Miller Rd., Morristown, NJ. For information, Rowe-Lanterman Home for Funerals, Morristown, NJ.
