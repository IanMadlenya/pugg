
        MORGENS-Marie. A Holocaust survivor, died on July 27, the day of her 87th birthday. She was an adored and devoted wife, a loving and wise mother, grandmother and mother-in-law. She was a good friend to many. As a talented artist, she leaves behind a legacy of beauty for her husband, Frank Morgens, her daughters, Anne Lewin and Sylvia Karchmar, and her grandchildren, David Lewin, Lara Karchmar-Orr, Matthew Lewin and Dorian Karchmar. The family will welcome visitors at her home on Thursday, July 30 from 3-8PM.
