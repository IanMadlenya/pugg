
        RUANE--Joy M., 65, of New York City, widow of William J., died Thursday, October 12th, of lung cancer. A devoted wife, mother, she is survived by her daughter, Paige, her son, Thomas, her stepson, William, and her stepdaughter, Elizabeth, her brother, Stephen Mehrbach and her sister, Doreen Errickson, and four granddaughters. Visitation will be Saturday, October 14th, 3-6 PM, at Frank E. Campbell--The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Burial will be private in Connecticut.
        $(6$)RUANE--Joy. It is with great sadness that The Society of Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center notes the death of Joy Ruane, a longtime friend and supporter. Her focus was always on improving patient care initiatives at the Center. As a tribute to her many years of generosity and dedication to The Society and to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, she received The Society's 2004 Award for Excellence in Philanthropy. She will be greatly missed. Mrs. Bijan Safai, President The Society of MSKCC
