
        BERGER--Edward. Age 88 on February 10, 2006. Beloved father, grandfather, brother & uncle. Decorated WWII combat veteran and ex-POW. Retired 38-year employee of R.H Macy's. Will be remembered by all who knew him for his enjoyable sense of humor and love of life. Interment will be private. In his memory, donations may be made to the Disabled American Veterans, P.O. Box 14301, Cincinnati, OH 45250-0301
