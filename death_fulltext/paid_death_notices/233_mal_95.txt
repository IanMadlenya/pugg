
        ROTHMAN--Lester D., in New York City on June 6 at the age of 94. Born in Brooklyn, New York, Dr. Rothman graduated from NYU School of Medicine and practiced Internal Medicine in New York for over 60 years. He was also a veteran of World War II, serving as a Flight Surgeon in the Army Air Forces in the Asiatic-Pacific Theatre. Survivors include his loving wife, Judith Sadoski, son Dr. Peter M. Rothman of Fort Wayne, Indiana, grandson Wilson J. Rothman of Fort Lee, New Jersey, granddaughter Caroline Rothman Victory of Sarasota, Florida, and many nieces and nephews. Dr. Rothman was predeceased by his first wife, Rosemarie Brancato Rothman, and his two sisters, Jean Rothenberg and Sylvia Palant. In accordance with his wishes, no memorial service is planned. Acknowledgements of his passing may be sent to the American Heart Association.
