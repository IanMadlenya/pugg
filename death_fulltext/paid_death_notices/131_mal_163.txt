
        SAX-Julius M. The Board of Trustees of Sanz Medical Center/ Laniado Hospital expresses deep sorrow on the passing of Julius Sax, generous benefactor, respected businessman, scion of a most illustrious family. Our heartfelt condolences to his esteemed children Helen and Dr. Daniel Potaznik, grandchildren Yehuda, Arye and Rina Margolit. May you be comforted amongst all mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Henry Spitzer, Secretary Mordecai Schreiber, MD Chairman, Pediatric Dept
