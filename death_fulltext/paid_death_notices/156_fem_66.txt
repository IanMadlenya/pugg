
        OPPENHEIMER-Kathe. The Officers, Board of Trustees and Staff of Helen Keller Services for the Blind are saddened by the loss of Kathe Oppenheimer who through a long and remarkable lifetime of achievement served as an example to all that visual handicap need not be a handicap to a full and rewarding life. Her generosity to Hellen Keller Services for the Blind through the years was deeply appreciated and will be long remembered. Fred McPhilliamy, President
