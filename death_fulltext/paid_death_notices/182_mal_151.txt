
        PATES-James Johnston at ninety-five years on February 20, 2002 in Brisbane, Australia. He leaves his beloved wife Meta, and family James and Marilyn Pates, Meta Margaret and John Goodman, and John and Peggy Pates. He is missed by his brother Bill Pates and sister Adaline Potter, by his seven grandchildren, and by his friends around the world. Memorial service at Memorial Church, Harvard Yard on March 15, 11am.
