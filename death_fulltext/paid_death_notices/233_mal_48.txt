
        SAWYER--Norman Radack, of New York City and Sharon, CT on June 3rd. Beloved husband, father, grandfather and brother. He is survived by Annette his wife of 59 years and children Rachel Sawyer and James Little, son Herb, grandchildren Nina and Billy Sawyer and brother David Sawyer of Sherman, CT. A diamond broker, he was an alumnus of Cornell University Class of 1939. He served on Cornell's Secondary School Committee, was the president of The Cooper-Stuyvesant Little League and was a member of Community Board 6. Services will be held Monday June 5th at 2 PM at Plaza Memorial Chapel at Amsterdam Ave & 90th St.
