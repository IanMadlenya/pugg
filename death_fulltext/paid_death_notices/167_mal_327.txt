
        BARISH-Matthew, 93. On Dec. 23rd. He managed the greeting card department of the Hebrew Publishing Company for 35 years. After his retirement, he taught at the School of Visual Arts and Parsons School of Design. He is survived by his wife of 22 years, Dr. Natalie Goldberg, his son David (Nadene), and his daughter Emmy and her husband Clark Josephs of Hopewell Junction, NY. He has 4 grandchildren: Jennifer (Bob), Tzvi (Rivka), Debbie (Josh), and Karen Josephs as well as nine great-grandchildren. Donations to Visiting Nurse Service of NY Hospice Care, 1250 Broadway, NYC 10001. Calling hours today from 2-7 at his home.
