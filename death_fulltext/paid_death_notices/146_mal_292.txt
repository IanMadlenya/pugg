
        OSWALD-Frank Graf III. Killed in an automobile accident on March 7 in Amado, Arizona. He is survived by his wife Laurinda Middleton Oswald; his two children Frank Graf and Julia Skye; his mother Audrey Clinton Oswald of Newport; his father Frank Graf Oswald Jr. of Port Washington; his brother David of Stamford; and his sister Diana of New York City. Memorial service Friday, March 19th, 2PM, Saint James Church, Madison Ave and 71 Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers contributions to Zenith Center for the Arts, 330 E. 7th St, Tucson, AZ. 85706 would be appreciated.
