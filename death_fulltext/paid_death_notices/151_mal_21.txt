
        GOLDBERGER-Irving, 88. Of Sarasota, Florida, formerly of New Rochelle, died on August 1, 1999 in Harrisburg, PA. He was retired president of Kate Greenaway Frocks, New York. Loving father of Lynn Zeitlin, Neil, Roger, and Peter; beloved grandfather of eight and great-grandfather of four. Graveside services Tuesday 11 AM at Montefiore Cemetery, Queens. For information, Reese Funeral Home, Harrisburg. Memorial contributions may be made to the Jewish Home of Greater Harrisburg, 4000 Linglestown Road, Harrisburg, PA. 17112.
