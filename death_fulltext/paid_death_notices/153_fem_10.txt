
        WAGMAN-Shoala R. of Rye, NY. Died October 1, 1999. She was 67. Born April 24, 1932 in Brooklyn to Joseph and Minnie Blitz. In 1954 she married Leonard W. Wagman in Brooklyn. Shoala was a Real Estate Developer for over 15 years. In addition to her husband she is survived by a son David of Rye, NY, three grandchildren, a brother Sam Blitz of Long Island and a sister Lenore Stillson of Florida. She was also a member of the League of Women Voters. Please contact the Zion Memorial Chapel for the funeral arrangements 914-381-1809.
