
        van BIEMA--Anne J., 88, died January 14, 2004 in Manhattan. She was predeceased by her husband, Hendrick H. van Biema. She was active in the Women's City Club, founded and became president of The Ukiyo-e-Society of America and bequested her collection of Japanese prints to The Sackler Gallery of The Smithsonian Institute. She is survived by her sister Ernie J. Eden. Contributions may be made to NYU Hematology Research Fund, AmeriCares or New York Public Library.
