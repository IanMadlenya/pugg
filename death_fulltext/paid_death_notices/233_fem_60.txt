
        LINSKY--Joan Laura. Beloved wife of the late Alexander Jaskewicz died peacefully on June 13 after a long illness. An avid conversationalist and superb hostess she was admired and loved by her many friends. Also survived by her sister Patricia Linsky of San Diego, CA. Visitation Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Follett & Werner Funeral Home, 60 Mill Road, Westhampton Beach. Mass of the Resurrection Friday 10 AM at the Church of the Immaculate Conception. In lieu of flowers donations in memory of Joan may be made to East End Hospice, Dominican Sisters or ARF.
