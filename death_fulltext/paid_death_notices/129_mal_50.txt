
        MORRIS-Robert. Age 85. Of West Palm Beach, formerly of Great Neck, New York. Passed away on September 28, 1997 after a short illness. Survivors include his wife Mildred M. Morris, children Bernard, Marthe and Barbara and 4 grandchildren. He was a practicing attorney in New York City for over 50 years. He was very active in social and community affairs in both Great Neck and West Palm Beach. He was a member of the Glen Oaks Country Club in New York and the Sherbrooke Country Club in Florida. Private services were held in West Palm Beach and a memorial service will be held at a later date.
