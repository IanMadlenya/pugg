
        DEAN-Ruth Josephine, 100. Of 165 West 66th Street, passed away on February 3. A native of Manhattan, she was born March 10, 1902. Internationally recognized as an Anglo-Norman scholar, Dean taught at Mount Holyoke College for 33 years. Since retirement in 1967, she has worked primarily in England and France on the publication of her 30 year's worth of study. In November 2001, Dean's book was awarded the Prix Honore Chavee by the Academie des Inscriptions et Belles-Lettres. She is survived by her sister, Blanche Dean DuBois of Bradenton, FL, nephew Dean DuBois, niece Judith Osterholt., and good friend Hya Bassier. Services will be held at 12:00 PM, Friday, February 7, at the First Presbyterian Church of Bradenton, Florida. For information Shannon Funeral Home. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made in her name to Mount Holyoke College, South Hadley, MA 01075.
