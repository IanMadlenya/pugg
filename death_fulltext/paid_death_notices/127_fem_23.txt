
        MORSE-Helena Barbieri. Passed away peacefully at her Manhattan home August 3, 1997 at the age of 89. Beloved sister of Rosalie Stynes, loving daughter of Frank A.G. and Rose Barbieri. A person of dignity, elegance, wisdom and generosity. Prior to her retirement, distinguished designer of haute couture. Survived by her brother-in-law Brendan Stynes and her nephews Jim, Frank and Chris Stynes. Her memory will be cherished by her great-nephews Chris, Jeremy, Ian, Michael and Patrick, and her great-niece Danielle.
