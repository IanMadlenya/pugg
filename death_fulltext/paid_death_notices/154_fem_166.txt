
        SEIDMAN-Judith. On November 21, in her 88th year. Deeply beloved wife for 63 treasured years of her lifelong friend Lloyd. Incomparable mother of Elinor Berlin and David, and to their spouses Robert Berlin and Ruth Greenstein. Devoted grandmother of Daniel, Michael and Joanna Berlin. Her inspiration remains with countless friends, relatives and students, here and abroad. A memorial service will be announced.
