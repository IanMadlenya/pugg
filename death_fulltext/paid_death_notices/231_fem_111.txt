
        PLAYER--Doris. Died April 10 in Rhinebeck, NY. She was the widow of Willis R. Player, former Sr. Vice-President of Pan American Airways. Born in Norristown, PA., she graduated from the University of Michigan and married her grade-school classmate, Mr. Player. She is survived by their daughters Nan Hermus of Germantown, New York and Jill Player Cotton of Sydney, Australia and three grandsons and one greatgranddaughter. Contributions can be made to St. Pauls Church in Tivoli, NY, or the American Cancer Society.
