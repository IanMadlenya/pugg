
        HATTERSLEY - Virginia Luke. On June 27, 2002 at her home in Sarasota, FL. Born September 23, 1909. Longtime resident of Locust Valley, New York. Beloved wife of the late Robert C. Hattersley and mother of Julie Righter, Melissa Schnirring, Robert C. Jr. and the late John G. Grandmother of six, greatgrandmother of 4. Memorial services to be held in Sarasota on July 14 and at St. John's of Lattingtown, Lattingtown Road, Locust Valley Tuesday, July 30 at 11:30 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to St. John's.
