
        HOLZINGER -- Claire, died peacefully on February 15, 2004 in North Salem, New York at age 91. Her husband John F. Holzinger predeceased her. She is survived by her daughter Dana Dyas and grandchildren Linsey and Tamesin of Montignysur-Loing, France as well as by her son John F. Holzinger Jr. and granddaughters Kimberly and Victoria of North Salem, New York. She was much Loved and will be missed by her immediate and extended Family and Friends.
