
        SHANE--Eleanor, 74. Longtime resident of New York City, passed away on February 19, in Boynton Beach, FL, after a long illness. While in New York, Eleanor was founder and President of the Shane Group, an executive recruitment firm. An active artist during her lifetime, Eleanor's paintings are part of the permanent collection of the Johnson Museum of Art at Cornell University, from which Eleanor graduated in 1953. In later years, she was a prize winning artist in the South Florida area. Eleanor is survived by her loving and devoted companion, Arnold Levy, her two daughters, Susan Herbin (Richard) and Amy Busch (Warren), grandsons Noah and Harris and brothers Nathan and Harold Shane.
