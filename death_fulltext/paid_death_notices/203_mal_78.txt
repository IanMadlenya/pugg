
        WISHNY--George. December 5, in his 94th year. Loving husband of the late Etta. Beloved father of Marsha Hanlon, Shelley Alkin, and son-in-law Frank Alkin. Adored grandfather of Joshua and Benjamin. Devoted brother of Sylvia Spitzborg and the late Jack Wishny. He was a true New Yorker, an all city basketball player for Dewitt Clinton High School, the 92nd St Y, and a graduate of NYU. He worked in the liquor industry for over 35 years. He will be remembered always for his wit, wisdom and devotion to his family and friends.
