
        HOWLEY-Edith, 98. Daughter of Martin Howley of Greenwich, died March 30. She is survived by her sister Elizabeth Ahlstrand of Vero Beach, FL. A devoted aunt, great-aunt and great-great aunt to Donna Ahlstrand, Patricia Kropp and Richard Buchta and to Jennifer, Marty, Douglas, Gretchen and little Kyle. We will miss her.
