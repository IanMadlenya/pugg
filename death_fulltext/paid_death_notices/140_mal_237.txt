
        KELLOGG-Junius. The NYC Consortium for Independent Living is deeply saddened by the death of Junius Kellogg. As President of the Harlem Independent Living Center & in so many other capacities, he graced our lives with his kindness, wisdom, & vision. Junius Kellogg was a champion of equal opportunity for people with disabilities, the personification of dignity and integrity, and will always be a role model for us all. Our sincere condolences to his family.
