
        KAYSTON-John. The Board of Directors and staff of JTA, the Global News Service of the Jewish People, note with deep sorrow the passing of John Kayston, beloved Executive Vice President Emeritus of JTA. Kayston retired as EVP in 1984, nearly 50 years after joining JTA. We will remember his warmth, passion and dedication to his work and family. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Ruth; daughter Anita Rapp; and grandchildren. Norman H. Lipoff, President Mark J. Joffe, Publisher
