
        BLOMQUIST-Arnold R. January 3, 1930-September 25, 1998. Of New York City. Survived by stepdaughter Karin Naess of Norway, two grandchildren, sisters Alice Blomquist and Margaret Christiansen of FL. He was pre-deceased by his wife Judith & two brothers, William and Alfred. Cremation was private. Memorial service Tuesday, October 13, Norwegian Seaman's Church, 317 E. 52 St, NY.
