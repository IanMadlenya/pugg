
        LENOK--Paul, 55, of New Rochelle, New York. With great sadness we announce his sudden passing on November 17th, 2006. Beloved husband of Arlene, devoted father of David and Amanda, loving brother of Amy, and loyal friend and confidant to many. A man of unyielding strength, courage and conviction. He will be greatly missed. Donations can be made to the Children's Brain Tumor Foundation, 274 Madison Ave. Suite 1301, New York, New York 10016.
