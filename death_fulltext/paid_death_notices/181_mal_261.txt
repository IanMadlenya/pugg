
        BOWNE-George D. In his eightieth year. Passed February 21, 2002 at Plainview New York. Survived by his wife Edna and children George and Debra. Mr. Bowne was a distinguished veteran of WWII and Korea and a charter member of the 1st NY Continental Chapter (NYC) Sons of the American Revolution. It is with deep sorrow that we say farewell to our fellow compatriot. A. John Pappas, President FGG Charles T. Galbraith, FACG Founding President 1st. NY Continental Chapter, S.A.R.
