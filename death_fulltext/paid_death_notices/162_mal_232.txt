
        COHEN-Frederick, EdD. On July 22, 2000, at age 75. Loving and devoted husband of Jessie Wilkinson Cohen, beloved father of Barbara Student, Donald and Kenneth Cohen, father in-law of Michael Student and Jennifer Cohen, grandfather of Sanford Student, Katherine, Kyle and Alec Cohen, and brother of Betty June Siegel and Alan Cohen. Retired Chief of Speech Pathology and Audiology, Bronx Veterans Administration Hospital and Professor of Speech Pathology and Audiology, William Paterson University. Prior to a brief illness he taught at the Hallen School, New Rochelle, NY. A service will be held on Tuesday, July 25, 2000, at the William Graham Funeral Home, 136 Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY. He is cherished by his family and will be deeply missed.
