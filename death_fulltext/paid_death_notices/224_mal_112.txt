
        LUCE -- Henry III. The Trustees and Staff Members of The Seamen's Church Institute of New York and New Jersey, together with George M. Isdale, Chairman of The Board of Trustees, and the Rev. Dr. Jean R. Smith, Executive Director, express their deepest sorrow at the death of Henry Luce III. The Henry Luce Foundation has been supportive of The Institute's programs for mariners and the chaplains who served them. We send our heartfelt condolences and prayers to his family.
