
        BROWN-William. Peacefully on April 22, 1999, at home in East Rockaway, NY, after a brief illness. Loving husband of Gertrude, his wife of more than 60 years, and proud father of Robert, Alan and Carol, and grandfather of Nicholas, Deborah, Joshua, Rachel and Wendy. After graduating from Pratt Institute, Bill became a sound man in the radio studios of CBS in 1939. From 1941 to 1947 he was a senior engineer at RCA. In 1951, he founded Hermetic Aircraft Instrument Corporation, a facility to test and repair aircraft instruments, which became a worldwide full-service repair station with close links to the French aircraft industry. He also co-founded Aerospace Lighting Corporation, a manufacturer of aircraft lighting systems for business jets, which was recognized in 1998 as one of Long Island's 25 fastest growing companies. Bill served as a Director of Norstar Bank and as a member of the East Rockaway Board of Education. He will be remembered for his enthusiasm, vigor and devotion to family and friends. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, April 25, at 1:30 p.m. at Boulevard Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island.
