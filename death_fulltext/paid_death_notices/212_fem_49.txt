
        HENLE--Beatrice Rosenthal, beloved wife of the late John A. Henle, Jr., died peacefully on September 1st at the age of 97. Mrs. Henle resided at the Gramercy Park Hotel, Manhattan, for 65 years. She leaves two nephews, Leonard and Stanley Star of Hudson, OH and Naples, FL. She is also remembered by her many friends at the Hotel and from her long business career in Lower Manhattan. She was interred at the Mt. Judah Cemetery in Ridgewood, Queens, New York, on September 3rd.
