
        ADLER--Ralph M. Born January 5, 1925 died April 10, 2006. Resident of Roslyn, New York. Retired professional photographer, he leaves a legacy of his life in photographs. Beloved husband of the late Anna Adler, beloved partner of Doris Brill, devoted father of Michael R. Adler, and his wife Barbara, adoring grandfather of Julian, Simon and Owen. He embraced Doris's son Robert Brill, wife Wendy & children Alexander, Gabrielle and Natalya as his own. He was a caring man, loved by all family and friends. Services were held on April 11. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society and Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital.
