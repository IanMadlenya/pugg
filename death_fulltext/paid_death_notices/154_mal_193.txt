
        CORVINGTON-Serge, 88, on November 6, 1999. Born in Haiti. Great Diplomat to the United Nations and Consul General to New York. After his Master's Degree in Librarian Sciences, Columbia University he worked 20 years at New York Public Library. His greatest treasures were those of the mind and soul. He will be dearly missed. Survived by wife, Edith and sons, Robert and Serge Jr.
