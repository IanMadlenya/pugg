
        RESNICK--Libby, 86, of Brooklyn, NY died Friday, February 23. A teacher of the deaf, she was an inspiration to all. She was a leader at B'nai Israel in Brooklyn. Libby was the beloved wife of the late Sidney Resnick, the loving mother of Penny Novich, and Steve Resnick. Libby, revered by her grandchildren, Jonathan, David, Michael, and Jeremy Novich, and AJ, Ali, and Jill Resnick, was the great-grandmother of Daya and Mia Novich. Donations may be made to the MS Society.
