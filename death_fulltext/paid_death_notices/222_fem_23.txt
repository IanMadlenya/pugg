
        ENGLANDER -- Betty, of Manhattan, passed away peacefully on July 5, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Philip Englander and the devoted and loving mother of Robert Englander and Rita Sands. Cherished grandmother of Adam, Alex and Andrew Englander and of Jessica and Hillary Sands. Loving and devoted mother-in-law of Nancy Englander and Jeffrey Sands and loving grandmother-in-law of Gretchen Englander. Devoted to and adored by her family, she lived a full, rich life and enjoyed a lifelong passion for contract bridge, for which she earned the title Life Master.
