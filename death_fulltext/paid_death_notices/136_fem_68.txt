
        ROCKMORE-Clara. On May 10, 1998, at the age of 88. Violin protege of Leopold Auer, pre-eminent virtuoso of the Theremin, now reunited with her pianist sister, Nadia Reisenberg. Mourned by her other beloved sister, Anna Sherman, her nephews Alexander, Robert and Ron, their children and the family of her late husband, Robert Rockmore. Services on Wednesday, May 13, 12 noon at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
