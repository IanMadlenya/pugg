
        LOEWENBERG-Anne Marie Cassirer. Wife of the late Dr. Walter Loewenberg. Beloved mother of Marianne Davis, and Gerhard Loewenberg and wife Ina. Grandmother of Ted and Andy Dimond, Deborah Loewenberg Ball and Michael Loewenberg. Also survived by eight great grandchildren. Remembered with love by her nephews and nieces, Eric and Wilfred Cass, Illa Saphra, Nellie Fink, June Sidman, Franklin Lonberg and by her friend Barry Head. She was devoted to her entire family and to the health of children, serving for 40 years as attending physician at the New York Infirmary and for 30 years in the Day Care Division of the Department of Health of the City of New York. In lieu of flowers donations to US Fund for UNICEF, 333 East 38th St., NYC, 10016. Services Sunday 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
