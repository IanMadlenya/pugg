
        FROST-Winston Henry. Of Middleburg, VA, 85, died in West Palm Beach July 22 after a long illness. Mr. Frost, an attorney, was associated with Appleton, Rice, & Perrin in NYC. An avid sportsman, he excelled in polo and court tennis. Graduated from Harvard University ('35) and University of Virginia Law School, he was a Lieutenant Commander in the Navy during W.W. II. He is survived by his wife, Margaret Lawrance Frost of Southampton and Dora Frost of Bridgehampton. Services are to be announced later.
