
        HARRIS-Lily H. Died peacefully December 2, 2002 at age 95. Lily was one of four children. Beloved mother of Jay and Ann. Loving sister of the late Myer Handler, a foreign correspondent at The New York Times, the late Leo Handler and the late Pearl Weisman. Cherished grandmother of Lisa, Chester and James. Great grandmother of Samantha and George. She will be missed by all who knew her. Cryptside service will be held Wednesday, December 4th, 2PM at Riverside Cemetery, Saddlebrook, N.J.
