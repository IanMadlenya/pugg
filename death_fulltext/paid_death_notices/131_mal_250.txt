
        KENNEY-F. Donald. Suddenly on Dec. 17, 1997. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Jean Baptiste Church, Lexington Ave. at 76 St, Monday, December 22, 10:00 A.M. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the National Academy of Design, NYC, or St. Bonaventure University, Olean, New York.
        KENNEY-F. Donald. The President, Director, Council, Advisory Board, and Staff of the National Academy Museum and School of Fine Arts note with great sadness the sudden death of the Chairman of their Advisory Board. He was an important force in the institution for the past seven years and his wise counsel and leadership will be missed. William King, President Annette Blaugrund, Director
