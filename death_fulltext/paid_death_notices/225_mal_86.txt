
        WEISELBERG--Dr. Martin. Died October 6, 2005. Professor of Endodontics at NYU College of Dentistry for 40 years. An extraordinary man, he touched the lives of so many. His presence will be greatly missed by family, friends and students. Predeceased by Celia, his wife of 62 years. Survived by his wife Beverly, brother Howard and wife Moriel; children Alan and Joanne and Ted and Christine; grandchildren Dara and Mark, Scott and Danielle, Jenny, Ali and Tara. Greatgrandchildren Brandon, Jake, Andrew, Jonathan and Brett. Services Sunday 9:15 AM ''Gutterman-Musicant'', 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ. Contributions in his memory to New York University College of Dentistry.
