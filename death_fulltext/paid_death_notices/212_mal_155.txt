
        SOSLER--Stuart N., 68, of Stuart, FL died September 17th, 2004. Beloved father of Neil and Andree and fatherin-law of Heather Katzen Sosler. Adoring grandfather of Caroline. Loving husband of the late Carolyn Atlas Sosler. He is also survived by sister, Francine Sosler. Stuart was born in New York City on March 17th, 1936. Graduate of the University of Pennsylvania and former partner of Bear Stearns & Co. Services 2:00 PM, Sunday, September 19th, 2004 at Riverside Memorial, 1920 Alton Rd, Miami Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to an organization supporting the election of John Kerry.
