
        AMAKER-Norman C. The Board of Directors and staff of the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, Inc., note with sorrow the passing of Norman C. Amaker of Chicago, Illinois. He was a staff attorney at the Legal Defense Fund throughout the 1960's, and was involved in numerous civil rights cases, challenging racial discrimination. We mourn the passing of a great lawyer and colleague and we will treasure his memory. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Services will be held Tuesday, June 13, at New Mt. Zion Baptist Church, 140 West 140th St., NYC, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers the family has requested that contributions be made to the NAACP Legal Defense & Educational Fund Inc., 99 Hudson St., Suite 1600, NY, NY 10013.
