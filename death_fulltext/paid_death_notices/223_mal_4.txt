
        MALCOM--John Winthrop. Of NYC and Jamestown, RI, died on July 30, 2005, in Providence, RI. Husband of Judith and father of Lily and Benjamin. Born in New York City on July 11, 1929. Youngest son of the late Mr. and Mrs. George Ide Malcom of Norfolk, CT. Brother of William S. Malcom of Carlisle, MA, and the late Charles H. Malcom. Graduate of St. Paul's School 1948 and Williams College 1952. He served as a Second Lieutenant US Army in Japan and Korea 1953-54, post hostilities. Private burial. Memorial service in NYC, September 12th at 11 am, St. James Church, 865 Madison Avenue.
