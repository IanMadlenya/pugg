
        KAPLANSKY-Susan. The members of the NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery, are deeply saddened by the untimely passing of Susan Kaplansky, dear daughter of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Alfred D. Grant and his wife Ellen. Dr. Grant has been a dedicated member of the department for over forty years. Susan will always be remembered for her kind and gentle ways and as a devoted daughter, wife and mother. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband Eyal, their children, her parents and to the entire family. Joseph D. Zuckerman, MD Chairman NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery William Jaffe, MD, Vice-Chairman NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery
