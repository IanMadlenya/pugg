
        TULLER-Dr. Martin Abraham. Formerly of New York City. Died April 10, 2002. At age 72. Martin was a physician in NYC and most recently moved his practice to Westchester. He is survived by his wife Sally, children David, Joanne, Judy Campion (husband Terry) & Wendy Schwartz & grandchildren Laura and Matthew Campion. Donations in Martin's name can be made to the Harvard Medical School Scholarship Fund. Contact the Zion Memorial Chapel of Westchester at 914-381-1809, for additional information.
