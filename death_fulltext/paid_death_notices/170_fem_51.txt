
        LOTH-Jean. Women's American ORT joins the Loth family in mourning the passing of Jean Loth. The members of WAO are honored to have known and worked with Mrs. Loth who was dedicated to the WAO mission and served the organization in many capacities. Among her many valued roles, Mrs. Loth was a member of the Executive Committee, the national treasurer and the first president of the Long Island Region. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to her husband, Arnold, and their family. Pepi Dunay, President Carol Linch, First VP Alice Herman, Executive Director
