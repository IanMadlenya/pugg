
        ROSS-James K. On March 5, 2002. Beloved father of Valerie, fatherin-law to Robert Gould and grandfather to Lila Brea Gould. Most cherished friend of Shirley Kaplan. Known as a man of ethics with a keen understanding and love of the arts. Worked as an editor and subsidiary rights director for Doubleday, Lippincott, and Harper & Row. He was known for his extensive experience and success in foreign rights. After a distinguished career in publishing, he became the director of administration at Timber Hill LLC in New York City, Valhalla, and Greenwich, CT. until he retired. Loved dearly by his family and friends, he will be greatly missed by all who knew him. A memorial service will be held in May.
