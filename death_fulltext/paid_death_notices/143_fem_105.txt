
        DEMAREST-Elsie Nemeth. On November 30, 1998 of Garden City, NY, in her 96th year. Beloved wife of the late Lester. Mrs. Demarest was a registered nurse for 45 years. Memorial Service Saturday, December 19, 1998 at 1 PM, the Presbyterian Church in Garden City, 91 Chester Ave, Garden City. Donations may be made to The Presbyterian Church in Garden City or Mill Neck Manor Lutheran School for the Deaf Inc., Frost Mill Road, Mill Neck, New York 11756.
