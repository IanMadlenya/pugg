
        LEVINE--Sylvia (Raboy), 90, on December 31st 2006. Beloved wife of the late Morris Levine. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Arna and Dr. Michael Goldstein of West Bloomfield, Michigan; son and daughterin-law Gary and Peggy Levine of Ridgefield, CT; grandchildren Jennifer Goldstein, Jonathan Goldstein and wife Robin Schwartz, and Anna Levine. Contributions may be made to Hebrew Free Loan Association or the Jewish Federation of Metropolitan Detroit. Private services will be on January 3rd, 2007.
