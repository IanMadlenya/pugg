
        LANGSAN--Lillian. On November 9, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Ira N. Langsan. Loving mother of Susan Black and her husband Eliot and Nancy Langsan and her husband Daniel K. Bernstein. Cherished grandmother of Ariana Ciri Bernstein. Funeral service Friday, November 11, 2005, 9:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St.
        LANGSAN -- Lillian. The Board and staff of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged notes with profound sorrow the passing of Lillian Langsan, mother of JASA Trustee Susan Black. Mrs. Langsan's brother, Joseph Howard Katz, was one of JASA's founding trustees and she and her late husband Ira N. Langsan were strong supporters of JASA's work for many years. We are saddened at her passing and extend our condolences to Susan and Eliot Black, Susan's sister Nancy Langsan and the entire Langsan family. David M. Warren, President Aileen Gitelson, CEO
