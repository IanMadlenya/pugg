
        LICHTENSTEIN - Ray. Yeshiva University Women's Organization expresses its grief and sorrow upon the passing of Ray Lichtenstein, a staunch, devoted member who held many important positions during her long tenure with Y.U.W.O. We extend our warmest sympathy to her daughters Gail Daitch and Diane Jacobs, brothers, Morris Sukenik, Rabbi Julius Sukenik and Aaron Sukenik, their children and grandchildren and all the family. Dinah Pinczower, National Chairman of the Board
