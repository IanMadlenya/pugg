
        GORDON-Miriam (Mimi) nee Acker, 60, passed away peacefully at home with her family at her side on July 20. She is survived by her husband Bruce, children Jon and his wife Danielle of Cleveland and Louis of Seattle, her mother Eva Acker Goldin of New Jersey and sister Terry Youngelson and husband Jim. Grandmother of Gabrielle of Cleveland, aunt of Jack and Sara Youngelson and sisterin-law of Ellen Gordon. We already miss her smiling face, warm hugs, good cheer and constant love. Please join us in celebrating her life and not her death as this is what she wanted. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, NYC on Sunday, July 23, 12:45pm. Interment to follow at Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. The family will receive visitors at home in New York on Sunday evening and Monday, in Cleveland and Seattle later in the week. Donations may be made to Gordon Family Scholarship Fund, Development Office, Brandeis University, Waltham, MA 02454
