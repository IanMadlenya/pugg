
        OSTASHEVSKY--Joseph Y. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center community mourns the passing of Joseph Y. Ostashevsky, Ph.D, Research Professor in the Department of Radiation Oncology. Among his many accomplishments, Dr. Ostashevsky studied chromosome structure and the effects of radiation on the cell's hereditary material (DNA). We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, daughter, son and grandson, and also to his many colleagues and friends. John C. LaRosa, M.D., President >>AD#
