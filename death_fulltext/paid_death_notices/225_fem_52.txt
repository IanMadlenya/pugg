
        BUCHBERG--Vera L. 82, died October 6, 2005 after a brief illness in Sarasota, FL. Formerly of Ardsley, NY. Social worker and volunteer for various social causes. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Abraham S. Buchberg. Survived by son Carl of NYC; daughter Lisa (Ralph Kaywin) and granddaughters Emma and Nora, of Berkeley, CA; and her sister Hannah (Moses) Malkin of Sun City Center, FL. She was the sweetest person, beloved by her family and a treasured friend to many. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice of Southwest Florida, 5955 Rand Boulevard, Sarasota, FL 34235.
