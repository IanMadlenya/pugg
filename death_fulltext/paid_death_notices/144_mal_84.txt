
        GOLDBERG-Emanuel. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) mourn the passing of our Honorary Board member, Emanuel Goldberg of Rochester and Palm Beach. He served with great distinction bringing to bear his broad Jewish communal experience, his deep commitment, and his broad global outlook. Our condolences to his wife, Nathalie and the family. Jonathan W. Kolker, President Michael Schneider, Executive Vice President Ambassador Milton A. Wolf Chairman of the Board
