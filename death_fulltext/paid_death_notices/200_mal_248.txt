
        DINITZ--Simcha. The Governors, Officers, Faculty and Students of the Schechter Institute of Jewish Studies in Jerusalem mourn the loss of our esteemed friend and colleague, Ambassador Simcha Dinitz, a distinguished and committed member of the Schechter Institute's Board of Governors. We remain eternally grateful for his life of service, wisdom, guidance and commitment to promoting pluralistic Jewish education in Israel. To his wife and children and all those who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Robert S. Rifkind, Chairman Board of Governors Professor Alice Shalvi, Chair of the Executive Committee Professor David Golinkin, President Raquel H. Flatow, National Executive Director
