
        MYERS-Robert. The Staff of Steinway Child and Family Services, Inc. deeply regrets the untimely passing of our colleague and friend, Robert Myers. Bob was a compassionate, intelligent man who cared deeply about children and worked tirelessly to improve the child serving system. His leadership, dedication and friendship will be missed by all. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his wide, children, mother, the NYCDMHMR & AS and all who knew him. Mary R. Redd, President & CEO Pasquale DePetris, PhD, VP/COO
