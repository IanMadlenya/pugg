
        BROWN-Earle, July 2, 2002. A leading American composer of the New York School. Dear husband of Susan Sollins-Brown, survived also by his sister Marilyn Krysil, a niece Tanya Morrissey, nephews Steven Krysil, Stephen Sollins & Andrew Farnsworth, and four grand-nephews. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Earle Brown Music Foundation, 718 Broadway, Suite 2A, N.Y., N.Y. 10003. A memorial concert will be announced.
        BROWN-Earle. The Board of Directors and staff of the New York Foundation for the Arts mourn the loss of Earle Brown, a great composer and contributor to the field of new music. Our heartfelt sympathies go to his wife, Susan Sollins, who has served on NYFA's Board for many years.
