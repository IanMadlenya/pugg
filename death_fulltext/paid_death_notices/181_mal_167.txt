
        WIIG-Roy. Of White Plains, NY, on February 11th, 2002, age 78. Born on Staten Island, New York. A veteran of WW II, graduate of City College, completed graduate studies at Harvard, employed by I.B.M. for 33 years. Survived by Elaine Wiig, David Wiig, Caroline Margagliano, John Wiig, Elsa Wiig Denno, and five grandchildren. Predeceased by Helen Wiig Loery. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to WNYC Radio. Private services will be held by his family.
