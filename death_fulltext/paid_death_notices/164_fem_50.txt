
        YOUNG-Lucile Halle. A native of Cleveland, Ohio; died at the age of 89 on September 1, 2000 after a long illness. She attended Miss Fuller's School in Ossining, NY. She was married to the late Fritz Wolff and the late Lewis B. Young of Drexel, Burnham, Lambert & Co. She began her career in the children's wear department at Jane Engels. Afterwards she started the Infant and Children's Department at Henri Bendel. Lucile was recognized as one of the industry's leading buyers of fine children's clothes until she retired in 1962. She is survived by her daughter Susan W. Spiegel and her husband Andrew of Highland Park, Illinois; and stepdaughter Linda Barnes. Adored grandmother of Sally Spiegel and Jill Spiegel Brozic and her husband Joe of Minneapolis. Stepgrandmother of Andrew and Kimberly and her husband Al and great-stepgrandmother of David Alexander. Friends will be received at 1075 Park Avenue from 6-9PM on Thursday September 14. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Alzheimers Foundation.
