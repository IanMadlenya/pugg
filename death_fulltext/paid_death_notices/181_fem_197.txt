
        ABRAHAM-Helene. On February 19, 2002, in Palm Beach, Florida. Beloved wife of Sandy, devoted mother of Nancy and Jimmy, and mother-in-law of Andrea Abraham and Arnold Moss. Loving grandmother of Lauren, Antonia & Karen Abraham. Helene wished that there be no formal funeral service, but hoped that her family and friends would remember her for all the joy she brought to the living of her life. Contributions in her memory may be made to The 92nd Street Y.
        ABRAHAM-Helene. The Board of Directors and staff of the 92nd Street Y mourn the passing of Helene Abraham, adored wife of our longstanding and esteemed board member Alexander Abraham. Our hearts and prayers are with their daughter and son-in-law Nancy and Arnold Moss, son and daughter-in-law Jimmy and Andrea Abraham, and their grandchildren Lauren, Antonia and Karen Abraham. Contributions in Helene's memory may be made to the 92nd Street Y, 1395 Lexington Avenue, New York, NY 10128. As her life was a blessing, so may her memory be as well. Matthew Bronfman, President Philip Milstein, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
