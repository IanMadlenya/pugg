
        BAAS--Charles William. Of Easley, SC, formerly of Keowee Key, SC, East Hampton, NY and New Jersey; husband of Gertrude Rosenast, died Monday, February 27, 2006. He served 41 years as Treasurer of the American Bible Society; additionally he served as Treasurer of the United Bible Societies and was Director of the Astor Place Real Estate Co. Surviving in addition to his wife of 61 years are two sons: Charles W. Jr. and Bruce Warren. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to: Providence Presbyterian Church, 4000 Earle E. Morris Jr. Highway, Greenville, SC 29611.
