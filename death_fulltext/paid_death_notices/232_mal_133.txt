
        GREEN--Mark S. Of Morris Township, NJ on May 13, 2006 at New York Presbyterian Hospital. He was 57. Mark is survived by his wife of 33 years, Sharon; son Brett M. Green of Hoboken, NJ; daughter Wendi (Green) and her husband Adam Aspes; and grandson Hudson S. Aspes of New York City. He is also survived by his mother Ruth Fishman of Palm Beach, Florida; his brother David Green and his wife Gabrielle of Nashua, New Hamsphire; his niece Andrea and nephew Alexander; as well as many adoring aunts, uncles, cousins and friends. Funeral service was held at Temple B'Nai Or Morristown, followed by interment at Beth Israel Cemetery, Cedar Knolls. Donations in Mark's name can be made to: American Heart Association, 2550 US Highway One, North Brunswick, NJ 08902.
