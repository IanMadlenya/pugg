
        BUCHTER-Robert Alan. Died on December 21, 1997 after a brief illness. Mr. Buchter was a principal in the architectural firm of Knappe and Johnson of Garden City, New York since 1957 where he practiced until his death. Under his direction the firm designed numerous schools, churches and other public facilities. Mr. Buchter was born in New York City and attended Flushing High School and Cornell University. Before joining Knappe and Johnson in 1957, he practiced architecture in Pennsylvania and Connecticut. He served in the Navy, where he met his wife, Monie, who died in 1994. He is survived by his brother Ted of St. Augustine, Florida and sons, Jonathan of Cleveland Heights, Ohio and Eric of St. Petersburg, Florida. No services are planned.
