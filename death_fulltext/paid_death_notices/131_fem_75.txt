
        BALLAS-Lila. Age 89, passed away in Manhattan on Saturday December 6, 1997. Born in Brooklyn, she was a long time resident of Surfside, FL. Beloved wife of the late Thomas, loving mother of Dianna de Martino and Virginia Marcus, dear mother-in-law of Leonard de Martino and Leonard Marcus, cherished aunt. Her vivacious spirit and caring ways will be remembered by family and friends. The warmth, wisdom and kindness she imparted will be greatly missed. Memorial service 1:30 P.M. Wednesday, Dec. 10 at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, gifts to The Lighthouse, 111 East 59th Street, NYC, would be greatly appreciated.
