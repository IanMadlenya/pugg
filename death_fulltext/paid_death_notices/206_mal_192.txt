
        MOTZ -- Lloyd. Professor Emeritus of Astronomy, Columbia University. Adored teacher, distinguished scientist and humanitarian. Survived by his wife for 70 years, Minnie, son, Dr. Robin Motz, daughter Julie Motz, grandchildren Jeremy, Nicole and Benjamin and great granddaughter Isabella. Services will be held on Wednesday, March 17, 12:30, at ''The Riverside'', 76th St., and Amsterdam Ave., NYC. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the LLoyd Motz Scholarship Fund, School of General Studies, Columbia University, NY, NY 10027.
