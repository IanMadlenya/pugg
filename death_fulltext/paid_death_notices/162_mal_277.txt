
        OLICKER-Stanley, MD. We note with sorrow, the passing of a pediatrician who was widely respected by his colleagues and his many friends. Stanley Olicker's career was distinguished by devotion to his patients, enthusiasm for teaching new physicians, and his active role as a member, and as President of the medical staff. Our deepest condolences are offered to his loving family. Mervin Silverberg, MD. Chairman, Department of Pediatrics North Shore University Hospital
