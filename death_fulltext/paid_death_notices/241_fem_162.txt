
        CUBA--Ruth, on February 17, 2007, at the age of 92. Beloved wife of the late Abraham V. Cuba. Loving mother of Barbara and Leonard Rich, Gary and Wendy Cuba, and Jacqueline and Scott Norkin. Dearest Mama of Kevin, Andrew and Jessica, Jonathan, Victoria, Joey, and Julianne. Proud great-grandmother of Coby and Casey. She was predeceased by sisters Pearl Goldstein and Phyllis Barron. She was a life member of Hadassah and ORT and life-long member of The Fort Greene Jewish Center and The Beach Haven Jewish Center. Funeral service Tuesday February 20, 11:30am at ''Westchester Reform Temple'' 255 Mamaroneck Rd., Scarsdale, NY. For further information Rosenthal Memorial Chapel (914)773-0030.
