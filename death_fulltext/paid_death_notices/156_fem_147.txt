
        LE BOW-Toby. 80. Died Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, cancer, Friday, January 14. She was first wife and lifetime friend of Guy Le Bow, TV talk host. A gifted hair stylist and cosmetician, she owned her own beauty salon and later managed several others. She worked in Democratic and Liberal Parties. She was born in Canada, came to the US as a child, lived in Riverdale, NY, past 40 years. Ms. LeBow leaves a son, Stephen Bogart Le Bow, Esq., daughter-in-law Graciella, a ballet teacher, grandson Adam, and Guy Le Bow. She was adored by all who met her.
