
        PARNESS-Joseph. Bnai Zion mourns the loss of its longtime member, national leader and father of its Exec. Vice Pres., Mel Parness. He spent a lifetime in the service of the Jewish people and Bnai Zion, setting an example of devotion to generations. We extend our condolences to his family. He shall indeed be missed. Funeral services on Tuesday, Oct. 29 at 12 noon at Plaza Memorial, 92/Amsterdam, NYC. Contributions in his memory to Bnai Zion Foundation, 136 E. 39th St., NY, NY 10016. 212-725-1211. Michael J. Lazar, President Jack Grunspan, Nat'l Sec'y
