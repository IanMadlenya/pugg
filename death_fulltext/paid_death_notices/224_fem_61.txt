
        GARFUNKEL--Leonore, 84, died September 8th, 2005 after a brief illness. Beloved sister of the recently deceased Benjamin Garfunkel. Devoted aunt of Michael Garfunkel, Julie Spencer and Marjorie Derven and sister-in-law of Helene Garfunkel. She was an assistant principal at I.S. 44 in Manhattan before she retired. She was a loving greataunt to Zachary Spencer, Samuel and Rebecca Derven, and Gabrielle and Cecile Garfunkel. She was an active member of the Temple Emanu-El in Long Beach, NY for many years. She will be missed for her colorful stories and participation in all family gatherings. Contributions in her memory may be directed to the Red Cross for the Hurricane Katrina Fund.
