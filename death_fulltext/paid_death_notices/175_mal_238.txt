
        MINTZ-Marvin. Of Manlius, New York, died August 23, 2001. Loving husband of Susan and devoted father to Jonathan and Elizabeth. Father-in-law to Beth. Son of Selma Silverman. Marvin, a retired psychologist, was owner of Bouckville Books. He will always be remembered for his compassion, kindness and gentleness. We will miss Marvin forever.
