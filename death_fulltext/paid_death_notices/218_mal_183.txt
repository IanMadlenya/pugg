
        HOWARD--Nathan Southard. On March 12, 2005, at age 63. Committed and respected Vice President of the Energy Divison of The Bank of New York. Beloved father of Ercil Howard-Wroth and Lyndsay Howard-Zita. Adored grandfather of Elisabeth, Nathan, Alexander and Theodore. Son of Catherine Southard and the late Cone Howard, Jr. of Marysville, OH. Loving brother of Ercil Howard Casey, Lynn Howard Schaefer and Nicholas Hathaway Howard. Cherished friend, colleague and mentor to many. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Thursday, March 17th, 7-9PM. Funeral services at Saint Thomas Episcopal Church, 5th Ave at 53rd St, Friday, March 18th, 1 PM. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Peoples' Symphony Concerts, Suite 1C, 201 West 54th St, New York, New York 10019, or Lakeside Summer Symphony, 236 Walnut Ave, Lakeside, OH. 43440.
