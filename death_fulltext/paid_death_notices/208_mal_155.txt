
        APPELBAUM--David, 76. A most esteemed Pythian Knight died May 12, 2004. David served in WW II and Korea in the US Navy. His wife Marcia, children, Anita, Linda, and Howard, and four grandchildren survive him. David served the Knights of Pythias as Grand Chancellor of NY 1996/1997, and as Deputy Supreme Chancellor. David's Pythian and public charitable service earned him the Golden Spur Award. The Grand Lodge and 20,000 Pythians of NY mourn his loss.
