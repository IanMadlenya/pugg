
        ALDERMAN--Joseph. Age 82 of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. on September 23, 2006. Survived by his loving wife of 57 years Cylvia (Freedland) and four children, Mark (Sue Ellen), John (Nancy), Laurie and Ellen (Bill Harwood). Devoted grandfather to eight grandchildren. Joseph was born in Syracuse, New York, received his LL.B. degree from Columbia University and an LL.M. degree from the George Washington University. During WWII, he served as a lieutenant with the Air Force in the Pacific. He began his legal career at the Department of Justice in Washington D.C. and later became V.P. and Senior Counsel at RCA. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends who will always remember to ''accentuate the positive.'' Donations may be made for ALS research at the Jackson Laboratory, 610 Main Street, Bar Harbor, ME 04609.
