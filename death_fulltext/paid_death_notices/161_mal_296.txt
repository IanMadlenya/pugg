
        LIBERMAN-James B. The Partners, Associates and Staff of Berlack, Israels and Liberman LLP express their profound sorrow at the loss of their founding partner, dear colleague and friend Jim Liberman. In his over fifty years at the Firm, Jim exhibited a level of skill and dedication to the practice of law that was an inspiration to all of us. Jim was a legendary guiding force in the public utility bar and his influence will always be remembered. He was forever loyal to the Firm and his Partners, and had served as a mentor and role model for those who had the privelege to work with him. We extend our deepest sympathy to his dear wife, Nadine and his entire family. Berlack, Israels & Liberman LLP
        LIBERMAN-James B. Thelen Reid & Priest LLP mourns the loss of a friend and colleague. Jim was one of the deans of U.S. public utility law for the past half century. He was an outstanding lawyer and professional of great candor and wit. We extend our most sincere sympathy to his wife Nadine Zamichow and family members.
