
        HOHENBERG-John. August 6, 2000. The Pulitzer Prize Board extends its profound sympathy to the family of John Hohenberg, distinguished journalist, author, and administrator of the Pulitzer Prizes from 1954 to 1976. With wisdom, skill and unstinting energy, he devoted himself to maintaining the highest standards of excellence in journalism and the arts. Edward Seaton, Chairman, Pulitzer Prize Board Seymour Topping, Administrator Columbia University
