
        PAYNE-David MacGregor of Lyme, Connecticut, peacefully of cancer on July 4, 2000, at Connecticut Hospice, Branford, age 80. A graduate of Yale University (1941), he served in the U.S. Navy during WW II as Commander of a PT boat Squadron in the Pacific. He joined the New York law firm Winthrop, Stimson, Putnam, and Roberts in 1948, retiring as partner in 1985. Inveterate explorer and mountaineer, dedicated bibliophile and community servant, he had also been a member of the Board of Directors of the Lyme Public Library and a Trustee of the Lyme Academy of Fine Arts. He is survived by his wife Sally, daughters Marion P. Klickstein and Margaret P. Lannamann, stepdaughters Lyn Littlefield Hoopes, Carol Littlefield McIntyre, stepsons William Littlefield and John Littlefield, and fourteen grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday July 8, 2000 at 11AM at the First Congregational Church of Lyme, Hamburg Rd (Route 156). In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the Middlesex Hospital Hospice Unit, 28 Crescent Street, Middletown CT 06457 or Connecticut Hospice Inc, 110 Barnes St, Branford CT 06405
