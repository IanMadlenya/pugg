
        GURIAN-Bernard, DDS. Died January 9 at age eighty-six. Beloved husband of Anita. Devoted father of Ellen and Andrew and caring uncle of Martha. He was adored by all his friends and colleagues. Bernie will be remembered for his generosity, his keen interest in his profession, his artistic creativity, his volunteer work at the American Museum of Natural History and his efforts on behalf of the visually handicapped. Services Tuesday, 12 noon at RiversideNassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
