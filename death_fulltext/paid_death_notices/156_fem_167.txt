
        STARR-Patricia Morrill, 81, of Mill Reef, Antigua W.I. and Bernardsville, NJ, died on January 17, 2000. Wife of Ogden P. Starr, mother of Patricia Starr Jones of New Canaan, CT, Ogden P. Starr Jr. of New York City and Michael M. Starr of Antigua. Grandmother of S. Bancroft Jones, Christopher S. Jones, Christina Starr and Jennifer Starr. A memorial service will be held at St. Philips Church in Antigua on Saturday, March 18, at 5 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to Miss Porter's School, Farmington, CT or to the Mill Reef Fund, P.O. Box 133, Mill Reef, Antigua, W.I.
