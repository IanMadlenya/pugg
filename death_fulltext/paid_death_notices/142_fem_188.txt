
        ARKIN-Stephanie. Born May 31, 1945, died November 20, 1998. Daughter of the late Edward and Irma Arkin. Sister of Kay Arkin. She will be remembered for her generosity. Services will be held today, 10 AM, at Hirsch & Sons, Jerome Ave and 167th St, Bronx. Donations to any homeless charity in her name would be appreciated.
