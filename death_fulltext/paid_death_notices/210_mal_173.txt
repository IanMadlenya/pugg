
        KENT--Abe, 88, died on July 12, 2004 in Atlantis, Florida. A prominent leader in the toy industry. Abe was Chairman of the Toy Manufacturers of America and retired in 1980 as Senior Vice-President of the Ideal Toy Corporation, a company he served for 45 years. Abe was the beloved husband of the late Lillian Kent. He is survived by his companion Anne Guttman; his daughters Barbara Attie and Gail Swift; his grandchildren Alisa Burnham, Jessica Attie, and Michael Attie; his great-granddaughters Caroline and Lindsey Burnham; and his sons-in-law Bruce Swift, Sandy Burnham, and Jeremy Heymsfeld.
