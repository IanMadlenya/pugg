
        GOLDENSOHN--Edith F., 75, passed away March 14, 2007, at home in Los Gatos, CA, after a short illness. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Sidney Goldensohn and daughter of the late Bella and Jack Friedman, dear stepmother, proud grandmother and cherished friend. Edith was a versatile, independent minded, creative and generous person whose career and activities spanned the fields of Law, Italian literature, Opera and Volunteer work to name a few. She will be greatly missed by the many friends and relatives whose lives she touched. She is survived by her stepchildren, Dr. Joan Fiorello of Closter, NJ, and Paul Goldensohn of Henderson, NV, and her grandson Matthew Fiorello. Services will be held at The Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., New York, NY at 11:00 am of Friday, March 16th. In lieu of flowers please make donations to the Antidefamation League.
