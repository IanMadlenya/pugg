
        BERNSTEIN-Michael C. Died January 31, 1999 at 92. Founding law partner of Bernstein, Weiss, Coplan, Weinstein and Lake in NYC. Survived by daughters, Davida Goldberg, Holly Bristow, Deborah Berman; six grandchildren and two sisters, Esther Bernstein and Myrt Morris. He will be greatly missed and always remembered with much love. Services today noon, Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, NY.
