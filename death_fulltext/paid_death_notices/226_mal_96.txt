
        GOLDEN--Philip. Of Rye, NY, died November 4, 2005, age 92. Born in Poland, April 3, 1913, to Samuel & Sophie Golden. A WWII veteran having served in the Army, he worked as an attorney. Beloved husband of the late Harriet Golden. Cherished father of Sid Golden (wife Linda), Rita Fried (husband Stephen), & Audrey Lesser (husband Barry). Loving grandfather of Rayna, Scott, Shanna, Alana, David, Brett (wife Justine) and greatgrandfather of Ava Brooke. Dear brother of Arnold Golden. Funeral Service Tuesday, 12 noon, Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 E. Boston Post Rd, Mamaroneck; 914 381-1809. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery.
