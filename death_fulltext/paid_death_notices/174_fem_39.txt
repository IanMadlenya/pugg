
        GLICKSMAN-Lillian. On July 5, 2001, at the age of 96. Wife of the late Charles. She is survived by her sons Sam (Elaine), Hal (Janet), grandsons Richie (Cynthia) and Charlie, sisters Ruth Newman, Emily Fine and Helen Landsman, as well as numerous nephews and nieces. Services Sunday, 10 AM, at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave (Ave J), Brooklyn. She found purpose and joy in helping others.
