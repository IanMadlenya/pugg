
        HARTEL-Michael D. Of NYC, and Easton, MD, formerly of Westport, CT. Founder and CEO of Omnimark, Inc., of Hartel Catalano & Gornick, Inc., and of BakerHartel Advertising, on Tuesday, December 15, 1998, in Tampa, FL, of a heart attack. He leaves his loved children Steven, Christopher, Michael, Jennifer and his two grandchildren. A famly service will be held in Westport, CT. He is deeply mourned by his partner Barbara Eveland, and by his friends and associates who will hold a memorial service for Michael at Omnimark. Details are now being determined.
