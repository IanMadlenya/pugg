
        COHEN--Selma Breslaw. 90, of Washington, DC and formerly of Schenectady, NY and Delray Beach, FL, died of leukemia on August 2, 2006. Loving wife of the late Louis Cohen, MD of Schenectady, NY, and beloved mother of Royce E. Cohen of Alexandria, VA and Jane C. Bergner (Alfred P. Bergner, deceased) of Bethesda, MD and grandmother of Lauren J. Bergner of New York, NY and Justin L. Bergner of Stamford, CT. Donations may be made in her memory to Simmons College (300 The Fenway, Boston, MA), the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (100 Raoul Wallenberg Place, Washington, DC), the Jewish Women's Archives (138 Harvard Street, Brookline, MA) or the charity of your choice.
