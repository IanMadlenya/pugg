
        ARANYOS-Patrick Michael, age 26, of N.Y.C., formerly of Jupiter, Florida and Amersham, England, died tragically on September 11, 2001 at the World Trade Center. He was a bond broker for Euro Brokers. Beloved son of Alexander P. and Winifred O'Reilly Aranyos. Dearly loved brother of Alex and Stephanie. Cherished grandson of Gertrude Aranyos. Loving nephew of Frank, James and Eugene O'Reilly, Mai Whyte and Vivian Aranyos Garcia. A memorial mass will be held at 11 A.M. on Saturday, 6th October, 2001 at Blessed Sacrament Church, 152 W. 71st St., N.Y.C. and at 10:30 A.M. on Friday, 12th October, 2001 at St. Patrick's Church, Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made payable to the Patrick M. Aranyos '97 Memorial Fund at Boston College.
