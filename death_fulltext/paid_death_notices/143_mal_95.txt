
        VAUGHAN-Virginius Nelson, Jr. Of Chatham Borough, NJ, on December 4, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Sally Craig Vaughan. Father of V. Nelson, III of Chatham, NJ, and James Brandon of Summit, NJ. Grandfather of Marshall Blanton and Spencer Elliott. Uncle of Federal Judge Martha Craig Daughtrey of the U.S. 6th District Appellate Court. Also survived by numerous cousins in Virginia, Georgia, and North Carolina. Funeral services were held at Ogden Memorial Presbyterian Church in Chatham, NJ. Donations may be made in his memory to the Memorial Fund of Ogden Memorial Presbyterian Church, PO Box 445, Chatham, NJ 07928. For more information, call William A. Bradley & Son, Funeral Directors, Chatham, NJ.
