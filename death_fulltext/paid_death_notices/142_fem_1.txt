
        GALLEN-Betty, died peacefully at her home Friday, October 30, 1998. Beloved wife of Herbert for 61 years, loving mother of Joan Megibow and Nancy Scheriff, dear sister of Doris Konner, devoted grandmother to Terri and Andrew Megibow, Michael, Adam and Todd Runyon, Tracy and Megan Scheriff, adoring great-grandmother to Melissa and James Megibow, Jordan and Abigail Runyon. Services Sunday, November 1st, 9:30AM, Gutterman Musicant Funeral Home, Hackensack, NJ.
        GALLEN-Betty. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the death of Betty Gallen. We extend our sincere sympathy to her husband Herbert, a true and valued friend of the Home, and their children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. We hope that your loving memories of Betty will ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec V.P.
