
        WEINSTEIN-Claire. Of Lake Worth, FL, formerly of Rockville Centre, NY, passed away on Sunday, December 9. Beloved wife of the late Bernard Weinstein, affectionate mother of Kenneth (Merle) and the late Jill Ascher. Adoring grandmother of Brian (Dana) and Kevin Ascher. In days gone by she enjoyed the company of her many friends. She will be greatly missed by all who knew her. Funeral will be graveside at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, on Tuesday, Dec. 11, at 11 AM.
