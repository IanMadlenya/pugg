
        DEANE - Hugh Gordon. Jr. 1917-2001. Lifelong architect building US-China Understanding and Friendship. A founding member of both our National and NY Chapter, he served as Officer, Organizer and Editor of its publications to the end of his life. His memory will be long cherished, inspiring the continual promotion of peoples' friendship. Memorial service is on Sunday, September 23, 1 PM, NY Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64th Street, New York City. US-China Peoples Friendship Association
