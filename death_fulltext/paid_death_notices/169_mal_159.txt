
        STANDARD-John Edward, MD. of Southbury, Connecticut, formerly of Naugatuck and Middlebury, Connecticut, and Saltaire, New York, died Sunday, Feb. 11, 2001 of complications due to Alzheimer's disease. Besides his wife Gloria, he is survived by his sister, ten children, 14 grandchildren. Memorial Service will be held on Saturday, February 17th, 11AM, St. Paul's Church, Woodbury, CT.
