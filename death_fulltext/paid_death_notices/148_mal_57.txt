
        SCHWARTZ-Gene. Beloved husband of Barbara, loving father of Toni and Douglas, beloved brother of Bob, died May 4, 1999. Gene was both an owner and a well liked and respected creative director of Laurie Records. He discovered and produced Hall of Famer Dion. He was also responsible for recording or discovering many other artists such as The Chiffons, Gary U.S. Bonds, the Snoopy Records, The Belmonts, The Mystics and many others. He died in Newport Beach, CA after a long illness. Gene is also survived by sisters-inlaw Vickie, Gloria and Jean, as well as three nieces and a nephew. Services at Abbey Bat Kol Chapel, 3340 John St. in Los Angeles, CA.
