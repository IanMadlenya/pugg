
        FREEDMAN-Harriette. On February 25, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Irving Freedman who was a trustee and counsel to Park East Synagogue. Loving aunt to Arthur, Ellen, Sandford, Estelle, Irwin, Dorothy, Josephine, Nat, Marilyn, Rosalie and the late Lois. Great aunt to her many nieces and nephews. She will be dearly missed. Graveside service Tuesday, 1 PM, at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery.
        FREEDMAN-Harriette. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Harriette Freedman, Sisterhood Trustee, who with her husband Irving (Ike), former Trustee and Counsel of Park East Synagogue, were founders of the Minskoff Cultural Center, Park East Day School. Their generosity and leadership is gratefully remembered. Our deepest sympathy to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
