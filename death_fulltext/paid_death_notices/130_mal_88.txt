
        TURNER-William Heyward Homer, noted corporate philanthropist, founder and Executive Vice President of the U.S. Steel Foundation, died recently in Stamford, Connecticut. He was 93. A native of Atlanta, Dr. Turner will be buried in Atlanta on Monday, November 10. He is survived by his wife Veda, his sons, Roger and William Heyward from his marriage to the late Clara Andres, several grandchildren and greatgrandchildren.
