
        CLARKE-Catherine C., 95. On July 22, 1999, of Katonah, New York. Wife of the late Richard W. Clarke. Mother of H. Cartan Clarke and Richard W. Clarke Jr. and the late Michael deS. Clarke. Also survived by eleven grandchildren and nineteen great-grandchildren. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Mary's R.C. Church, Katonah, New York, on Monday, 11 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Michael deS. Clarke Memorial Fund, C/O Amherst College, 100 Boltwood Avenue, Amherst, MA. 01002-5000.
