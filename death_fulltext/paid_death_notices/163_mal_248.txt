
        KILBURN-H. Thomas Jr. 69. Of Greenwich died Saturday at home. Born on August 1, 1931 in New York, New York, Mr. Kilburn was the son of the late Henry T. and Florence Cross Kilburn. Mr. Kilburn was a graduate of The Hotchkiss School Princeton University and Columbia University School of Law. Managing Director Paine Webber Inc., NYC 80-88, Advisory Director Paine Webber 88 to Present, Member of the New York Bar Association, Securities Industries Association, Links Club, Princeton Club, NYC, and the Belle Haven Club. Husband of Victoria Tyner Kilburn, brother of Richard Fleming Kilburn of Stowe, VT, uncle to two nieces and one nephew. A graveside service will be held at 10:00AM on Wednesday August 23, in Putnam Cemetery Greenwich CT followed by a memorial service at 11:00AM in Christ Episcopal Church, 254 East Putnam Ave., Greenwich, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to Camphill Foundation, P.O. Box 290, Kimberton, PA. 19442.
        KILBURN-H. Thomas. Our deepest condolences to the Kilburn family. Tom's extraordinary courage and dignity in his last illness and Victoria's steadfast devotion and love provide a shining example for all. Betsy and Bill Herman
