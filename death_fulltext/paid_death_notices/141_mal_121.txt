
        ROBBINS-Jacques Andre, ne Seligmann, of Greenwich, CT, on Wednesday, October 7, 1998. Son of the noted late art dealer of Paris and New York, Andre Jean Seligmann and the late Claude Simone Barrou Robbins of New York. He is survived by his loving wife J. Suzanne Geiss Robbins, Greenwich, CT. Loving father of Jacques Mel Robbins, New Rochelle, NY. Loving brother of Claude Seligmann Delibes of NYC and Water Mill, NY. Mr. Robbins was an investment analyst with Lazard Freres in Paris and New York and also an Executive Vice President at the Banque Nationale de Paris in New York City. Since his retirement in 1985 he has achieved acceptance and success as a figurative, portrait and landscape artist. He recently had his first one-man show in Roxbury, CT. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, October 13, 1998 at 11 AM at the First Presbyterian Church, 18 Lafayette Pl., Greenwich, CT. Calling hours 69 PM Monday, October 12, at the Gallagher Funeral Home, 31 Arch St., Greenwich, CT. Memorial contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to the Bruce Museum Inc., 1 Museum Dr., Greenwich, CT 06830 for the Jacques A. Robbins Memorial Fund.
