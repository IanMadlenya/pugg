
        WOLF-John S., M.D. Of White Plains, NY. Died December 1, 2000, at the age of 89. He was a physician for 46 years and a Lt. Col., US Army Medical Corps, during World War II. Dr. Wolf was the loving husband of Peggy Wolf for 58 years and the devoted father of Susan (Robert) Post, Barbara (Bob Kegan) Wolf, and Richard (Mary) Wolf. He was the devoted brother of Janet Wolfe, and the proud grandfather of Laura and David Post, Lucia and Joshua Kegan, and Jonathan and Julie Wolf. Dr. Wolf was a graduate of Yale University and Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. A memorial service will be held at Congregation Kol Ami, Chapel in the Woods, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY, at 10 AM, Wednesday, December 6. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the National Alliance for Autism Research, 414 Wall St., Research Park, Princeton, NJ 08540, or to the White Plains Hospital, Davis Ave. at East Post Rd., White Plains, NY 10601.
