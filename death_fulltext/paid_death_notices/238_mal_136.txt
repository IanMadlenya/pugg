
        DE LEON--Jack (Christopher Weeks)ctober 16, age 81, of complications from heart and kidney disease. A brilliant comedian, impressionist, actor, most remembered as ''Marty'' in the Barney Miller television series. Survived and beloved by his son, Ricky, daughter - in - law Coleen, grandchildren Nicole and Sean, brother George, sister-in-law Nancy and nephew Joshua, Jack created art, friendship and laughter.
