
        HEINKELE-E. James. Of Atlantis, Florida, on September 5, 1998. Formerly of Douglaston and Quogue, Long Island. Beloved husband of fifty-two years of Peggy Rabbett Heinkele. Cherished father of Peggy Anne Heinkele Dineen & Mary Heinkele Swinford. Adored grandfather of Danielle Anne Dineen. Respected father-in-law of Daniel Blake Dineen & John A. Swinford. Loving brother of Francis W. and John L. Heinkele. Burial at Saint Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, New York, Wednesday, September 9, at 1 P.M. Contributions in his memory may be made to Little Flower Children's Services, Wading River, New York.
