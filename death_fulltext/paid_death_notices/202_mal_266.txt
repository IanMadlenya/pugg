
        FAWER--Gertrude. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Gertrude Fawer, beloved mother of Martin Fawer. Martin, his wife, Estanne, and his sons, David, Mark, and Andrew, are cherished friends and devoted supporters of UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Fawer family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        FAWER -- Gertrude. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of the mother of our esteemed member, Martin Fawer and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Yaakov Y. Kermaier, Rabbi Ira Leon Rennert, Chairman J. Ezra Merkin, President
