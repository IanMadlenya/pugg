
        SACHAR-Ruth. A very special lady passed away on Friday, September 3, 1999. Beloved, and cherished wife of the late Irving Sachar. Devoted and adored mother of Randi and Michael. Loving mother-in-law of Sherry and devoted grandmother of Taryn, Nicole and Max. Survived by her loving sister Benice Brilliant. Her positive attitude for life and people along with her strength and guidance for those she touched will be honored and remembered forever. Her life long love of music, art and her commitment to her family, friends and those in need was the essence of Ruth Sachar. She will be deeply missed forever by many. May she rest in peace for eternity. Service Sunday 11:15am at ''The Riverside'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave.
