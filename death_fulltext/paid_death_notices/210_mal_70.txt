
        ISLER--Dr. Stanley M., of Stratham, NH, died July 4, 2004. A longtime resident of Westport, CT, until his retirement from the New York City School District 5 where he was a psychologist. Dr. Isler is survived by his daughter, Jenny of Stratham, NH; son, Loren, and grandchildren, Russell and Jessica. His kindness, wisdom, art and sensitivity will be missed by all those whose lives he touched. Services will be held at the Rose Hill Memorial Park, 101 Mill Street, Putnam Valley, NY, Monday, July 19, 2:00 PM. Donations may be made to National Kidney Foundation.
