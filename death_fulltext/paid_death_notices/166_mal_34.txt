
        DAVISON-Charles Hamilton, 73, died Thursday, November 2nd, at his home in Vero Beach, FL. Formerly Deputy Chairman of Peat Marwick Mitchell & Co. (precursor to today's KPMG); Vice Chairman of Smith Barney; Chairman and owner of Paramount Cards, Pawtucket, RI. He was a graduate of Dartmouth College and received an MBA from NYU. Mr. Davison was very active on a number of civic and educational boards including a Trustee of Kenyon College, Trustee of Northwestern's Kellogg School of Management, Chairman of United Charities of Chicago, Boards of New York City Ballet and City Center of New York. He is survived by his wife, Lessie, and three sons: Hamilton and Dorothy of Providence, RI; Lang and Mary Jeanne Connors of Mill Valley, CA; and Andrew and Brooke of Boulder, CO, and three grandchildren. Donations in his memory may be made to Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH or Kenyon College, Gambier, OH.
