
        WILLNER-Blanche. It is with great sadness that the American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science mourns the passing of Blanche Willner, cherished friend and benefactor of the Weizmann Institute, and beloved wife of Dr. Albert Willner, President of the American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science. Together with her husband, Mrs. Willner was a generous and devoted patron of the Weizmann Institute. Through the Willner Family Foundation, their gifts to the Weizmann Institute include the Willner Family Center for Vascular Biology and scholarships for high school students and graduate students. We extend our deepest heartfelt condolences to Dr. Albert Willner, son Dr. Joseph Willner and his wife Dr. Judith Willner, and daughter Dr. Jane Bloomgarden and her husband Dr. David Bloomgarden, and the entire family. Robert H. Asher, Chairman, American Committee Martin S. Kraar, Exec. V.P. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Prof. Ilan Chet, President
        WILLNER - Blanche. New York Medical College mourns the passing of Blanche Willner. With her husband, Albert Willner, M.D., trustee and alumnus, she was a devoted friend and longtime benefactor of the university and its mission. Their generosity stands as testimony to their high regard for tomorrow's physicians and scientists. We extend our deepest sympathy to Dr. Willner and the family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Rev. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D. Provost and Dean School of Medicine
        WILLNER-Blanche. Delaire Country Club mourns the loss of their devoted member and extends their sincere sympathies to her beloved husband, Dr. Albert and her entire family. Mal Levenson, President
