
        FREILICH--Ella. On August 6, 2004 in Riverdale, NY, in her 87th year. Wife of the late Rabbi Samuel Freilich, mother of Hadassah Freilich Lieberman and Ary Freilich, grandmother of Ethan Tucker, Hana, Matthew, April and Rebecca Lieberman and Sarah and Elizabeth Freilich, great-grandmother of Eden Migdal Tucker and Tennessee and Willie Lieberman, and mother-in-law of Joseph Lieberman and Judy Freilich. She survived enormous turmoil with indomitable spirit and lived to see her family grow and prosper in a new land. Funeral services 11AM, Monday, August 9, at the Riverdale Jewish Center, 3700 Independence Ave, Riverdale, NY. May her memory be a blessing.
        FREILICH--Ella. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale deeply mourn the passing of Ella Freilich, wife of the late Rabbi Samuel Freilich, beloved mother of our dear friends Hadassah Lieberman and Ary Freilich, and motherin-law of Senator Joseph Lieberman and Judy Freilich. Ella was an extraordinary woman who escaped tyranny, and saw her family achieve the pinnacle of success in this great nation. Her strength, love of family and delightful warmth, were inspiring. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her children and grandchildren and hope they will be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Daniel Reingold, Exec. VP
