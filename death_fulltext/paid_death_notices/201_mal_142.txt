
        BERGER--Arthur. The directors and officers of The Aaron Copland Fund for Music, Inc. note with sorrow the death of Arthur Berger, Trustee of the Fund since its inception. Arthur was a composer of great vitality, integrity and aesthetic purity. He contributed greatly to the shaping and guidance of The Copland Fund. He was the author of the first biography of Aaron Copland and remained one of the primary experts on Copland's music. The Trustees honor his life in music, and are grateful for his contribution to our work. We extend heartfelt condolences to members of his family. The Aaron Copland Fund for Music, Inc. John Harbison, President
