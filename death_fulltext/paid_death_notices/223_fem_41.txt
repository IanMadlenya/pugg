
        HILL--Jane E., age 95, of Old Black Point, Niantic and NYC, died peacefully, July 12, 2005 at Mariner Healthcare at Bridebrook. She was born in NYC May 4, 1910, the daughter of Charles and Sarah Knipe Herrmann. She was the widow of William E. Hill, a management consultant. Mrs. Hill graduated from the Emma Willard School in Troy, NY; Rosemary Hall in Greenwich, CT and Wells College in Aurora, NY. She was employed at Saks Fifth Avenue in NY as buyer of accesssories and a window dresser. She also was a volunteer for over fifty years at the Babies Hospital at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. She is survived by two daughters, Susan Hill McLaughlin of Cleveland Heights, OH; Sarah K. Hill of NYC. She also is survived by three grandchildren, Mark H. McLaughlin; Peter D. McLaughlin; Chris K. McLaughlin; a great granddaughter Valentina Mc Laughlin. A sister, Mary Herrmann Kennedy and a brother John K. Herrmann predeceased her. A memorial service will be conducted in St. John's Episcopal Church, Niantic, CT at a date to be announced. Memorial gifts in lieu of flowers may be made to Wells College, Aurora, NY; or to the Alzheimer's Association, 2911 Dixwell Ave, Suite 104, Hamden, CT 06518.
