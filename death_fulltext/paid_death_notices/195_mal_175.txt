
        LIEBLING-Walter, MD. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center (SLRHC) family joins with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and longtime colleague, Walter Liebling, MD, a former member of the faculty of the Department of Medicine. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Margaret, his wife of 53 years and a member of the hospital's Associate Trustees, his children, grandchildren and all of his loved ones. Morton P.
