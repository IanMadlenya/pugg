
        COOPER-Eve. The Officers and Board of Directors of the Jewish National Fund of Greater New York extend our sincere sympathy and condolences to Malcom Thomson, our President, and his wife Barbara on the passing of Barbara's mother, Eve Cooper. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Bertrum L. Podell, Chairman Ronald S. Lauder, National President Michael T. Cohen, Chairman Executive Committee Russell F. Robinson, National Exec. Vice President Matania Z. Kochavi, Special Israeli Representative
        COOPER-Eve. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of the mother of its esteemed member, Mrs. Malcolm Thomson, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family.
