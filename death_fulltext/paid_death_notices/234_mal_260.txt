
        ALTSCHUL--Rolf, 88, of New York City, passed away July 16th, 2006. Born in Dusseldorf, Germany on January 24, 1918, he received a Ph.D. in Chemistry from Harvard University in 1940. He taught chemistry at Sarah Lawrence College for over forty years. Rolf Altschul was preceded in death by his wife PatriciaMcGowan Altschul. He is survived by his two children: Peter J. Altschul in Washington DC and Jennifer A. Hart in Basalt, Colorado.
