
        LeFEVRE-Edwin Jr. Age 96, died August 7. He was born October 21 1902, the son of author and ambassador, Edwin LeFevre Sr. He married Charlotte (Griffith) LeFevre. Mr. LeFevre worked on Wall Street as a partner in the brokerage firm of DeCoppet and Dormus. A memorial service will be held at the United Church of Dorset and East Rupert on Sunday, August 15, 11 AM, followed by a graveside committal in Maple Hill Cemetery in Dorset. For those who wish, memorial gifts may be made to the Dorset Nursing Association, in care of Brewster Funeral Service, PO Box 885, Manchester Center, VT 05255.
