
        TYSON--Patricia Hale, of N.Y. City and Portland, ME, wife of Dr. C.J. Tyson, Jr., died March 6th, 2005. Graduate of Wayneflete and Madeira Schools and Barnard College, former President of the Women's Auxiliary of Presbyterian Hospital, member of the Cosmopolitan Club. Survived by her husband, six children and six grandchildren. Funeral Mass Friday, 10:30 AM, St. Thomas More R.C. Church at E. 89th St. For further information: Frank E. Campbell, 212-288-3500.
