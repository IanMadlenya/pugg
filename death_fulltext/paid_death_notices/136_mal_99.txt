
        COHEN-Paul, CPA. August 17, 1914-May 3, 1998. Delray Beach, FL. Formerly of Lido Beach, L.I., NY. Former managing partner of Harry Abramowitz & Co., certified public accountants. New York University graduate, class of 1935. Adored husband of Regina (Schoenberg). Loving father of Edward, Steven & Linda. Beloved grandfather of Andrew and Glenn. Great grandfather of Michael, Ariel, Aaron and Ashley. He will be missed and remembered by all for his kindness, generosity, goodness, compassion, understanding, wisdom and ethics. We loved him dearly (his family).
