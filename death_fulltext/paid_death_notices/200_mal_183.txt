
        LESSER--Stephen. Of Westport, CT, aged 50, died peacefully at his home after a long battle with lung cancer. He was the beloved husband of Carol Burton, devoted father of Jackson and Rosalie, adored son of Bert and Mimi Lesser and dear brother of Robin Simon, brother-in-law of David Simon and uncle of Nicholas and Alizah. Steve was a well known architect in Westport, CT as well as an avid golfer, poker player, and witty friend. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, September 20 at 5 PM at the Westport Unitarian Church on Lyons Plains Road in Westport, CT.
        LESSER--Steven. One of the great people whose warmth and humor will stay with us forever. Judith Fields Jurney Your Quaker Ridge Pal
