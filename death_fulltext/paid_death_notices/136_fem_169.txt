
        GALIMIR-Suzanne. The Musicians, Trustees and Staff of the Marlboro Music School and Festival share the loss of and extend deepest sympathy to the family of Suzanne Galimir. For over forty years, she was instrumental in helping to plan and arrange events at Marlboro that brought generations of exceptional young musicians together into a warm and caring community that was as much a family as it was an inspiring musical environment. Suzanne will be missed by us all.
        GALIMIR-Suzanne. Mannes College of Music mourns the passing of Suzanne Galimir, beloved wife of Felix Galimir, one of the world's beautiful and shining spirits.
