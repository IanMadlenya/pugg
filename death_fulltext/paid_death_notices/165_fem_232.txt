
        MENSCH - Constance (nee Giobbe). On Oct. 22, 2000. Beloved wife of Homer. Loving sister of Susan Dull. Dear cousin of Gloria Ficalora. President of the Board of Trustees, The Dalcroze School of Music, President of The Bohemians, Chairman of the Young Artists' Committee of the Musicians' Club of New York. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Wednesday 2-5 & 7-9 PM with service Thursday, 1:30 PM. Interment to follow at Mount Saint Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, N.Y. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Dalcroze School of Music, or the Musicians' Foundation of the Musicians' Club of New York.
        MENSCH-Constance. The Board of Directors of the Musician's Foundation mourns the passing of our dear friend and colleague Constance Mensch. Her stalwart support for musicians was legendary. She will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt sympathies are extended to her husband Homer Mensch and her sister Susan Dull. Anne Bigelow Stern, President Hans Tausig, Vice President
        MENSCH-Constance. The Board of Directors of The Bohemians mourns the passing of our beloved president, longtime Board member and fellow Bohemian. She will be remembered as a valiant woman, consummate musician, generous patron of the arts and dear friend. With our deepest sympathy to her husband, Homer, and to her family, The Board of Directors The Bohemians
        MENSCH-Constance. The Board of Directors of 165 West 66th St mourn the passing of our esteemed Board member. She served with distinction & devotion. We extend our heartfelt condolences to husband Homer.
