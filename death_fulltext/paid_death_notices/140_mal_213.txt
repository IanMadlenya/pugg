
        RASMINSKY-Louis C.C. Former Governor of the Bank of Canada. On September 15, 1998, lucidly and serenely in his 91st year at home in Ottawa, surrounded by the love of his children Michael and Lola, his daughter-in-law Judy, his grandchildren Sonya, Jonathan, Jeremy and Abigail, his special friend Jerry Grey and his longtime helper and friend Vicenta Pardo. Funeral is at the Jewish Memorial Chapel, 1771 Cuba Avenue, Ottawa, Ontario on Thursday, September 17 at 2 PM.
