
        ORMISTON - KENWORTHY - Virgi nia (Ginger). Victim of the September 11, 2001 tragic attack on the World Trade Center. Wife of Jim Kenworthy and mother of Beth and Bill. Survived by her mother, Jeanette, and sisters, Nancy and Sarah. A memorial service will be held at 1:00 PM Saturday, September 29, at the First Presbyterian Church, 12 West 12 St., NYC. In lieu of flowers the family asks that donations be made to police or fireman rescue funds.
