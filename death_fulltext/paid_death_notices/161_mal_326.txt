
        GOLDBERG-Nathan, M.D., age 92, on June 28, 2000. First doctor in Stuyvesant Town, dedicated physician for 55 years. Predeceased by Rose, his wife of 47 years. Survived and missed by his daughter Joan Udell of NYC, son Dr. Paul Goldberg, and daughter-in-law Caren, of Stamford, CT; grandchildren Christopher Udell, Jennifer Udell (Daniel Newsome) and Tony Goldberg (Robin Hadley) of Champaign, IL, Catherine Lipper (Graeme Lipper), of Boston, MA. Also survived by his companion of 21 years, Lillian Gottesman.
