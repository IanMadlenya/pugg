
        ROSS--Harold. Died July 7, 2004, in Poughkeepsie, NY, at the age of 81. Born in Rochelle, IL, August 5, 1922, son of Harold and Dorothy Davis Ross. Worked for IBM in Poughkeepsie and Kingston, NY, as a manager of the Engineering and Data Systems Development. He earned numerous scientific awards and patents. He retired in 1977. On December 27, 1946, he married Helen Ray of Oak Park, IL. She predeceased him on January 2, 2000. Both were active in the Poughkeepsie community. He is survived by brothers William and Andrew; a special and personal friend, Mary Jane Yurchak. Services will be at a later date.
