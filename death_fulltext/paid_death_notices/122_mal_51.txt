
        ROBINS-Walter F. Passed away March 3, 1997 at his home in Fort Meyers, FL., of cancer. He served in World War II, 343rd Infantry. Retired as a Civil Engineer. Originally from New York. He lived 33 years in West Nyack, later retired to Fort Myers. Beloved husband of Esther, cherished father of Marci DeFillippo of Thiells, NY. Grandfather of Kelsie also of Thiells, brother of Marjorie Kase of Doraville, GA., and brother of Robin Burris of Savannah, GA. Many nieces, nephews and grand nieces and nephews. Services will be held Thursday, March 6, in Fort Myers. Donations may be made to the Hospice of your choice.
