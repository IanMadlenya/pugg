
        BORER--Lee N., DVM. On October 19, 2006. (Texas A&M) Class of 1943. Beloved husband of Rita, loving father of Dr. Jeffrey Borer, grandfather of Justine and John. Founder and retired president of All World Trading and Import company. A distinguished, elegant and cultured man, a philanthropist and a lifelong Giants fan. He will be greatly missed by his family, friends and co-workers. Service on Sunday 12 noon at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, Madison Ave. at 81st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Boys Town of Jerusalem or the Jewish National Fund.
