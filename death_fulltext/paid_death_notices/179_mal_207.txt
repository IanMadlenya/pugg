
        PATTI-Alfred J. On December 13, 2001. Al was born in Queens and was 55 years old. Beloved husband of Judith (nee Appelle). Loving father of Stephen & Daniel. Loving son of Mary Patti Lascar and the late Alfred Patti. Loving brother of Janet Fusaro. Al was a graduate of Archbishop Molloy High School, Fordham University and St. John's University. He retired in 1998 as the managing director for H.S.B.C. He was a member of the White Plains Rotary, the Westchester Golf Association and Westchester Hills Golf Club. The family will receive friends at the McMahon, Lyon & Hartnett Funeral Home, 491 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains on Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at Our Lady of Sorrows R.C. Church Monday 10 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please make donations in his name to the Cure for Lymphoma and the American Lung Association.
