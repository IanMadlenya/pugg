
        LOSEE-Thomas Penny Jr. On February 2, 2002, after a prolonged battle with kidney disease. Resident of Gulf Stream, FL, and Glen Cove, NY. Beloved husband of Muriel Hahn Losee. Devoted father of Thomas Penny Losee III and Kendall Losee Graebner. Loving father-in-law to Paige York Losee and Clark Edward Graebner Jr. Survived by his brothers Alan Wilmot Losee, William Keith Losee and sister-in-law Danelle Hahn Kensbock. Affectionately called ''T.P.'' by his adoring grandchildren Clark Edward Graebner III, Lars Thomas Losee, Ella Nell Losee and Skyler Louise Graebner. Son of the late Jeanne G. and Thomas P. Losee. Mr. Losee enjoyed a long career in the magazine industry, serving as Publisher of Architectural Digest for thirteen years. Loyal supporter and active alumnus of The Taft School in Watertown, CT, and Duke University. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made to the National Kidney Foundation, 30 East 33rd St, Suite 1100, New York, NY 10016. Service at 10 AM, Tuesday, February 5, First Presbyterian Church, 33 Gleason St, Delray Beach, FL. A memorial service will take place at a later date in New York.
