
        DEUTSCH-Gertrude R. Died Friday, April 23, 1999 at Leisure Park Center, Lakewood, NJ. She was 87. She, along with her late husband, Samuel, owned Peterson's Sunset Cabin in Lakewood and were proprietors of numerous hotels in Lakewood, the Catskills, and Miami Beach. She is survived by three sons: Stephen of Scarsdale, NY, Lewis of Toms River, NJ, and Warren of Lakewood; seven grandchildren and one greatgranddaughter. Memorial donations to any cancer research center.
