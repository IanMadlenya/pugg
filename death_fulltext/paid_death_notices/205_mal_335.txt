
        SPERBER--Sanford M. On February 24, 2004. Passed on after a valiant battle with acute leukemia. He leaves behind his beloved wife Sheila, his son Richard Goldblatt and his wife Jeanette, his son Stephen Goldblatt, his brother Gary and his beloved mother Sylvia. He will be missed by all who knew him, including his Masonic brethren. Services today 10AM at ''Parkside Memorial Chapels'', 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn (corner Avenue V). Interment Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Long Island.
