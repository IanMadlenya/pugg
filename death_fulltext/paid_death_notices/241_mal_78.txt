
        MENOTTI--Gian Carlo, 95, composer, died peacefully in Monte Carlo, on the eve of the production of his opera ''The Medium'' directed by his adored son, Francis. Deeply loved father and most loving grandfather to Claudio and Cosimo. Those wishing to attend, his funeral will take place at the Cathedrale de Monaco, Saturday February 10th, 10:30am. Followed by a concert in the Salle Garnier, Opera de Monte Carlo at 6pm. Burial in Gifford, Scotland by his own request.
