
        WISHNER-Joseph, M.D. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Administration of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute note with deep regret the passing of our good friend and collegue, Joseph Wishner, M.D. Dr. Wishner had the distinction of being the first orthopaedic surgery resident at HJD. He was a distinguished teacher and orthopaedic surgeon, a profession he fully retired from in his 80's. His lifetime of extraordinary dedication, enthusiasm and philanthropic support will be remembered and cherished. We grieve his passing as we celebrate his life and extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Wishner family. William A. Perlmuth Chairman of the Board John N. Kastanis, FACHE President and CEO Victor H. Frankel, M.D. President Emeritus Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief
        WISHNER-Joseph. Age 101, on September 7th, 1998 in New York City. Born near Kiev, Russia, he emigrated to New York in 1906. He practiced as an orthopedic surgeon here for 70 years. A kindly and outstanding person, he will be missed. Predeceased by Rose his beloved wife of 68 years, he is survived by loving daughter June W. Snow of Portland, Maine, sister Laura Morse of Florida, four grandsons, 4 great grandchildren and cousins, nieces and nephews. Memorial service scheduled Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York City, on Sunday, September 13 at 11:00 A.M.
