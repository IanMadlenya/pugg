
        AUERBACH-George. 89. Of Howard Beach, N.Y. Passed away in Tamarac Fl. on February 16, 1998. He is survived by his loving wife of 65 years, Sophie, devoted children, Sandra (Herbert) Weiss, Robert (Elizabeth) Auerbach, four cherished grandchildren, and six great grandchildren. A member of the Masons and Shriners. Mr. Auerbach was the Founder of Ashland Desk Co. which later became Furniture Rental Associates, Inc., UFO, Inc., a Director of Wheels Plus Roller Rinks and an owner of Casa del Ray Bingo. The stockholders and employees of Furniture Rental Associates, Inc. and its allied companies mourn the passing of our beloved Founder, George Auerbach. Chapel services will be held on Wednesday, February 18 at 2:30 P.M. at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, Tamarac Fl. 954-523-5801. Interment will take place in New York on Friday, February 20, 11:30 A.M. Beth Moses Cemetery, Farmingdale, N.Y.
