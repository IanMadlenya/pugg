
        AGIN-Elaine. The Executive Board, Board of Trustees and Congregation of the Free Synagogue of Flushing mourn the passing of Elaine Agin on Tuesday, January 8, 2002. Beloved wife of our Rabbi Emeritus Dr. Charles G. Agin, mother of Adele, Deborah Shine (Richard) and Susan. Loving grandmother. Beloved by all, she will be sorely missed.
        AGIN-Elaine. Sisterhood of Free Synagogue of Flushing is devastated by the passing of Elaine Agin, beloved wife of our Rabbi Emeritus Dr. Charles G. Agin. She will live in our hearts forever as a woman of valor.
