
        OPPENHEIMER-Kathe. Born December 25, 1900. Died December 31, 1999. Beloved mother of Axel (deceased), Stephen, Ruth, Andrea and Coco. Studied law at University of Munich, married Alfred Jarislowsky. Widowed 1929; took charge of a major German industrial group including an investment bank, gradually sold off major items during the Hitler days, remarried Heinz Oppenheimer and emigrated to Holland, then via France to the U.S. in March 1941. She leaves 13 beloved grandchildren and five great grandchildren. There will be a gathering of friends and family Friday, January 14th, 1-4PM at her home. Contributions to The Helen Keller Foundation.
