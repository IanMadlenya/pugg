
        ELTON-William R. (Professor). Loving son of the late Mollie and William Ribakoff. Devoted brother of Mildred Boyd and Sylvia Gans. Cherished uncle of David Boyd, Susan Gans and Karen GansSchwartz. He was an accomplished poet, a respected scholar and an esteemed member of the faculty of the Graduate Center of the City University of New York. His work was his passion, and he will be fondly remembered by those whose lives he touched. Services will be held on Tuesday, August 29, 12 noon, at Nieberg Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn. Interment to follow at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery.
