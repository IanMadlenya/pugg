
        FEDERBUSH--Herbert, 91, of 5581 Glen Abby Ct., Delray Beach, FL. March 30, 2007. Beloved husband of Norma, devoted father of Judy and David, brother of Harold. His benevolence and irrepressible sense of humor will be missed by family, friends, and all who knew him. Graduate of NYU, Levittown Eagle Columnist, Vice President for advertising and sales promotion for many years at Executone, Inc. of NYC. Moved to FL in 1992, occasional newspaper feature writer. Services at Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, 11115 Jog Rd., Boynton Beach, FL, 561732-3000 on Monday, April 2.
