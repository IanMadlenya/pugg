
        JACOBSON - Marjorie. On August 19, 2002. Beloved wife for 59 years of Leslie A. Jacobson. Devoted mother of Sue Berland and David Jacobson. Beloved motherin-law of Phuong Jacobson & Joseph Berland. Cherished grandmother of Leslie Meyers, Maxwell Meyers, & James Berland. Step-grandmother of Laura BerlandShane and Kenneth Berland. The love and concern of her family and many friends helped to sustain her through her last illness. Donations may be made to the Katonah Museum of Art, Katonah, NY.
        JACOBSON-Marjorie. The Officers, Board of Trustees and Staff of The Katonah Museum of Art mourn the passing of Marjorie Jacobson, a cherished friend, colleague, and beloved wife of Leslie. Mrs. Jacobson's inspired leadership, keen wit, commanding spirit and impeccable taste will be missed by all who knew and loved her. We offer our sympathy & condolences to Leslie Jacobson, Trustee and Overseer of the Katonah Museum of Art and the entire Jacobson family. Victoria F. Morris, President, Susan H. Edwards, Executive Director
