
        MILLER-Leonore Olinsky, 94. On September 4, in Kent, CT, widow of Henry, mother of John and Richard, grandmother of Ivan, Lucas, and Emily, mother-in-law of Joan McD Miller and Carol Schneider. She was the daughter of an artist, an artist herself, a lover of the arts, and most compellingly, a lover and protector of her family & friends. Her remains will be buried at Mt. Pleasant.
