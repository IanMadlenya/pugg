
        BRESLER-Rita. Beloved wife of Jay, mother of Marjorie Thompson, grandmother of Kirsten Thompson, after a short illness on April 16. A memorial service is planned for later this year. Donations in her memory can be made to the Library Fund of the New York Hospital for Special Surgery for which she volunteered for over 30 years.
        BRESLER-Rita. The Board and shareholders of Round Dune, Inc., mourn the death of longtime resident Rita, and extend sincere condolences to her husband Jay, Board member, her daughter Marjorie and granddaughter Kirsten.
