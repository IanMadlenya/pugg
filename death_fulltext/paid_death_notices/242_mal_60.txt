
        PLATT--Harvey, 99, at his home of the past 14 years, the Jewish Home and Hospital, Bronx Campus. Former Executive Vice President, District One, B'nai B'rith and inspirational voice to the community. Beloved husband of Miriam Barth Platt (deceased) and dear father of Judy (deceased) and Lois. A righteous man whose values will live on through his cherished grandchildren, Elana, Elie, Dvir and Netanel and 10 great-grandchildren he would have adored. The family is deeply grateful to the devoted staff of the Alzheimer's Unit, Bronx JHH.
