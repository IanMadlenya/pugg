
        MELTZER--Joseph, longtime resident of Stamford, Ct. Died on December 19, 2005 in Stamford Hospital. He was 90. Loving father, beloved grandfather. Husband of Lillian Meltzer. He is survived by his son Gordon, daughterin-law Dorothy, grandson Jordan and friend Elaine Tashman. Joe defended his country with honor in World War II, flying combat missions in Europe in 1943 and 1944. Funeral services will be at Temple Beth-El, Stamford, Wednesday, December 21 at noon. Burial to follow at BethEl Cemetery. For more information contact Leo Gallagher and Son, Stamford, Ct.
