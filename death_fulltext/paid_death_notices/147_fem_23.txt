
        GALLAGHER-Ellen Lloyd, R.N., Ph.D. The Board of Directors, Administration, Medical Staff, Employees and Volunteers of Our Lady of Mercy Medical Center and Saint Agnes Hospital, mourn the death of our esteemed colleague and friend, Ellen Gallagher, Vice President of the Florence D'Urso Pavilion of Our Lady of Mercy Medical Center. Ellen was a dedicated health care professional whose commitment to quality patient care was an inspiration to all who had the privilege of working with her. Our deepest sympathies are extended to her beloved husband, Jim, and loving children, Joan, Gavin, Nancy, Graham, James, Devin, and Wade. Edwin S. Mruk, Chairman Board of Directors, Our Lady of Mercy Healthcare System & Our Lady of Mercy Med Ctr William F. Harrington, Esq. Chairman, Board of Trustees Saint Agnes Hospital Gary S.
