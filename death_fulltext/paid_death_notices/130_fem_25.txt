
        MATTMANN - Barbara Moore. Daughter of the late Albert V. Moore, co-founder of Moore McCormack Lines. Passed away on November 1, 1997, after a prolonged illness. She is survived by her devoted husband Charles T.; beloved daughters Sandra Gros, Barbara Walkley, Wendy Read, Debbie Mattmann; and ten loving grandchildren. Service St. John's of Lattingtown, Thursday, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to North Shore University Hospital at Glen Cove.
