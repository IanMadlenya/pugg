
        ITALIANO-Michael G. Died April 12, 2001 at St. John's Riverside Hospital, Yonkers, of congestive heart failure. He was 95 years old and lived in Yonkers for the last 45 years. Retired at 63, he and his wife Mildred were active members of the Yonkers Senior Citizens Program, Group 7. Loving husband of Mildred (nee DiNapoli) and father to JoAnne and husband John, Anne and husband Mel, Phyllis and husband Isidore. Devoted grandfather of nine and great-grandfather of four. Calling hours will be at the Flynn Memorial Home, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, 914-963-5178 on Friday from 7PM-9PM and Saturday and Sunday from 2PM-8PM. Funeral Mass Monday, 10AM at St. Anthony's Church, Nepera Park, Yonkers. Interment Kensico Cemetery.
