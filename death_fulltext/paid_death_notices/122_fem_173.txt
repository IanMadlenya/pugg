
        RUDIN-Sadie. Beloved wife of the late Harold Rudin. Born Manhattan, December 27, 1903, to Tama Green and Bernard Feldstein. Died Friday, March 21. Loving sister to the late Isador Feldstein, Esq., and Martha. Devoted aunt to three generations of nieces and nephews. Graveside service Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Sunday, March 23, 11 AM. Shiva observed through Wednesday at her home with her nephew Jerome Feldstein in Stony Brook, NY.
