
        BURACK-Zahava. State of Israel Bonds mourn the passing of Zahava Burack, a devoted leader and a passionate supporter of Israel. She was Chair of Palm Beach Israel Bonds, the Westchester Women's Division, and served on the National Women's Division Board. Her untiring work for Bonds helped raise millions of dollars in investment capital to build Israel's economy. Zahava's dedication to Israel and the Jewish people inpired all who knew her. We extend our condolences to the entire family. Gideon Patt, President & CEO Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board Michael Siegal, Vice Chairman of the Board Irwin Hochberg, National Campaign Chairman Deena Kaplan, National Women's Division Chair Michael Lazar, Southeast Regional Chairman
