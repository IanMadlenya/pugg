
        MILLER-Maxwell E. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) mourn the passing of Maxwell E. Miller, whose deep commitment to the Technion is reflected in his attainment of the rank of Technion Guardian and his status as an Honorary Life Member of the Technion International Board of Governors. Together with his wife Frieda, he was instrumental in establishing the Maxwell E. and Frieda Miller Dormitory. We extend condolences to his widow, children and grandchildren, and all members of the bereaved family. American Society for TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Lawrence S. Jackier, President Irving A. Shepard, Chairman Melvyn H. Bloom, Executive VP Jonathan Sohnis, New York Metropolitan Region Chair Joseph Huber, New York Metropolitan Region Director
        MILLER-Maxwell E. On March 26, 1999, cherished friend and mentor. Winston Kullak & Family
