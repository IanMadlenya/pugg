
        SURREY--Mildred Roth Teitelbaum, ''Mimi'', on Saturday, April 16, 2005. Formerly of Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, and lastly, a Florida resident. Daughter of Dena and Louis; sister of Adele; wife of Albert; beloved mother of Ruth and Lewis; and grandmother of Dean. Her devotion to family, life, and others was unmatched. Active in Jewish causes, constantly putting others first. Earth's loss is Heaven's gain. An exceptional person full of dignity, warmth, and uncommon sense of humor. Loved by all. She will be greatly missed. Sleep well. Services to be held at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 46 Greenwich St., Hempstead, New York, on Tuesday, April 19 at 12 noon. For information call 516-486-2500. Burial at New Montefiore Cemetery. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Foundation.
