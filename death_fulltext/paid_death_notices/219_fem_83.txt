
        STUTZ -- Geraldine. The Board of Directors and management of Jones Apparel Group, Inc. are saddened by the loss of Geraldine Stutz, who served with distinction on our Board of Directors since 1991. Gerry Stutz was a major force in the fashion world for decades, and her presence, perspective and advice were greatly valued by her colleagues at Jones. We extend our condolences to her family and many friends. Peter Boneparth, President and Chief Executive Officer Jones Apparel Group, Inc.
        STUTZ--Geraldine. Mentor and great friend, thank you for being part of my life. Your bright light has been extinguished, but your spirit, knowledge, and flair will live on forever. Robert Rufino
