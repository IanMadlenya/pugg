
        QUINN--Brother Charles B., CFC. The College of New Rochelle Community records with sorrow the death of Br. Charles Quinn of Iona College, beloved friend and colleague of long-standing. One of the giants of the Congregation of Christian Brothers, we admired his intense faith, rigorous standards of excellence and passion for Catholic higher education. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D, President Michael N. Ambler, Esq., Chair, Board of Trustees
