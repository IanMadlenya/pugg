
        ISQUITH--Santley. February 28, 1907-May 22, 2004. Husband of Rita. Father to Fred and Aaron. Grandfather to Daniel, Fred and Rebecca. Santley was the youngest of ten brothers and one sister. He had a full life: student, baker, attorney, holder of two master's degrees, businessman, counselor and served overseas in World War II. Santley suffered from cardiovascular disease but died with a strong heart and love for his family. Services and interment private.
