
        OBERKOTTER-Paul. The National Center for Disability Services mourns the passing of Paul Oberkotter, a dear friend and supporter of programs for children and adults with disabilities. His efforts leave a lasting legacy that will improve the lives of countless future generations. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him. The National Center for Disability Services expresses their heartfelt condolences to his daughter Mildred, grandchildren, greatgrandchildren, family and friends. Edmund L. Cortez, President and CEO The Board of Directors, National Center for Disability Services
