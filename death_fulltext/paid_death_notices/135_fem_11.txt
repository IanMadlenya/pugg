
        GREW-Alma Clayburgh, age 82. Of Boston, MA, formerly of New York City. Wife of the late Dr. James H. Grew. Mother of Alma, Edward S. and James H. Grew. Aunt of Jill Clayburgh of CT. She was the daughter of the late Albert and Alma Lachenbruch who was a well known concert singer in New York City. Services will be held on Thursday at Emmanuel Church in Boston. Private burial at Woodlawn cemetery in Bronx, NY. Memorial gifts may be made to The Alma C. Grew Memorial Fund, Museum of Fine Arts 465 Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA. 02115. For information call J.S. Waterman & Sons, 781-235-4110.
