
        SCHLITTEN-Lawrence A., Lt. Col., USA. Entered into rest February 6, 1998. Late of Canton, MA, formerly of Belle Harbor, NY. Beloved husband of Renee (Stettner). Devoted father of Mark Justin and Dr. Elliot W. Justin. Adored grandfather of Grant, Eric and Eleanor. He leaves us in his 82nd year with a legacy of love, admiration, honor and integrity.
