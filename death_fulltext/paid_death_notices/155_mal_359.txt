
        LANSER-Mannie. On Dec. 24, 1999. Beloved husband of Lillian, loving father of Steven and Marc, devoted grandfather of Eric, Amanda, and Toby, caring fatherin-law of Deborah and Lisa, much loved stepfather of Lucy, stepfather-in-law of Jennie, stepgrandfather of Lily and Jacob. He had a profound enjoyment of life and communicated that enjoyment to everyone around him. He fought the good fight in Spain with the Abraham Lincoln Brigade, served his country honorably in World War II, and continued to fight all his life for the things he believed in. We will all miss him and honor his memory. Cremation arrangements private. A memorial at-home meeting for family and friends will be planned.
