
        GREENBAUM-Judith. The Yeshiva University family mourns the tragic murder of a gifted student at our Azrieli Graduate School of Jewish Education and Administration, who died in the Palestinian terroist bomb attack in the center of Jerusalem yesterday. Her devotion to chinuch and the advancement of Israel and the Jewish people will always inspire us. Our hearts go out to her husband, Schmuel; her parents, Mr. and Mrs. Alan Hayman of Los Angeles; her uncle, Dr. Peter E. Hayman, a YU alumnus; and the entire family. Hashem yinkora damah. May the Almighty comfort them among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Moshael J. Straus, Chairman Board of Directors Azrieli Graduate School
