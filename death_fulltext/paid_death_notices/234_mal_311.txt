
        RUBIN--Alan J, 79, of Boca Raton, FL and New York, died July 25th, 2006 peacefully after a short illness. He is survived by his loving daughter Sandra Rubin Seibold of Cincinnati, OH, son Mark Rubin of Stockton, CA, and Scott Leach of New York, NY, brother Howard Rubin of New York, loving granddaughters , Jordan and Jessica Seibold, and son-inlaw Richard Seibold. Memorial services will be held at Gutterman Warheit Memorial Chapel on Thursday, July 27th at 1pm, 7240 North Federal Hwy, Boca Raton, FL. Contributions may be given to the charity of your choice.
