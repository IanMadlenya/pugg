
        KAUFMAN--Berdie L. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Berdie L. Kaufman, beloved mother of Myron Kaufman; and mother-in-law of Joan Kaufman; devoted leaders whose commitment has strengthened our ability to serve those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Myron and his wife, Joan, to their children, and to the entire Kaufman family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
