
        BOETTIGHEIMER-Ludwig. Born in Frankfort 93 years ago, Luddi died March 23, after a long illness. Devoted husband of more than 60 years of Marianne. A lover of culture, the arts and Judaism. For decades, a leading light amongst the volunteers of Channel 13. A man of firey intellect, intelligence, humor and gentleness, he will be missed by all who knew him. Funeral 11 AM, Monday, Congregation Habonim, 44 West 66th St. Geoffrey, Terry, Benjamin Weill
