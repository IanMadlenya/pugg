
        CASHMAN-Alvin H. Died on October 20 at the age of 94. He is survived and missed by his wife of 70 years, Sylvia Cashman, by his son Kenneth, by his grandchildren (Bill, Elise Bass, and Keven) by his five great-grandchildren, and by his sister Selma Florman. His funeral service will be held at 11:30 on October 24 at the Nagel Funeral Home, 352 East 87th Street. Visitation begins at 10:30.
