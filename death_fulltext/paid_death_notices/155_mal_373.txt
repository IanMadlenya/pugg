
        REISNER-Edward H.., Jr., MD. The entire family of St. Luke'sRoosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Dr. Edward Hartman Reisner, Jr. An oncologist at St. Luke's Hospital for over four decades, Dr. Reisner upheld our finest traditions of medical care, clinical excellence, and groundbreaking research, and he will be sorely missed. His important research on Vitamin B12 and his numerous publications on nutrition and cancer have led to crucial advances in the treatment of disease. To his children Barbara, Christine, Edward, and Tom, and to his entire family, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Sigurd H. Ackerman, MD President and CEO Carlton Boxhill, MD President of the Medical Board Michael Lesch, MD Chairman, Departmant of Medicine
