
        BERLINGERI--Vincenzo on December 2, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Noushie, loving father of Andrea and Alexei, cherished brother of Paolo, Maria Cesari, Lugi, and the late Nicolo, loving brother-in-law of Patricia Teymourtache, devoted grandfather of Alexandra, Isabella, and Nicolino.
