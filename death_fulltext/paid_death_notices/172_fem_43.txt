
        SCHENKEL-Elizabeth Johnston of Vero Beach, FL, formerly of Garden City, NY, died May 2. She graduated Packer Collegiate Institute, Class of 1934 and became the first woman to graduate from New York Law School, Class of 1937. In 1992 she was honored by the law school and was awarded the Distinguished Alumna Award. She was the wife of James M. Schenkel, Vice President and Actuary for Home Life Insurance Company in New York. Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach.
