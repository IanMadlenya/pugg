
        GILBERT--Lionel. The 92nd Street Y Art Center staff, faculty and students mourn the passing of Lionel Gilbert, artist and long standing painting and drawing instructor. Our hearts and prayers are with him and his family. His service to the Y and to his students will be missed. Michael Goldstein, President Sol Adler, Executive Director Robert Gilson, Director, School of the Arts Rachel Selekman, Director, Art Center
