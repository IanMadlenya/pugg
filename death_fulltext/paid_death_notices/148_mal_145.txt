
        WEINBERG-Sidney R. age 73. Musician, beloved husband of Karen and father of John Short and Valerie Kaley, died of cancer on May 5. His humor, wisdom and companionship will be greatly missed. A Celebration of his life will be held June 5, Maitland (FL) Art Center. Remembrances may be sent to Central Florida Jazz Society Scholarship Fund, P.O. Box 540133, Orlando, FL 32854. For information call Central Florida 469 Direct Cremation. 407-292-7572.
