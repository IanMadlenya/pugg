
        DANTER--Meyer ''Mickey''. 86, of Brooklyn, N.Y., on December 26, 2003. Beloved husband to Natalie for 63 years. Loving father, grandfather and great grandfather. A true friend to so many. Mickey gave to so many, never expecting anything in return. He taught us how to live and how to love. We will miss him dearly. Contributions in his memory may be made to Pythian Camp, Knights of Pythias, 2347 Jericho Tpk, Garden City, N.Y. 11040.
