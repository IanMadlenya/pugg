
        WOOD-Irving W. 75, died February 2, at his home in Northport, LI. Distinguished audio engineer, inspired jack-of-all-trades, devoted sailor, irrepressible collector and fixer of electronic equipment, great friend. Survived by his dear sister Judy Spock, nieces and nephews, and Pat Meier, sister of his late wife Ruth. Thimble Islands/ Stony Creek friends mourn Woody's passing.
