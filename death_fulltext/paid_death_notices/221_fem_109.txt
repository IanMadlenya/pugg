
        MARSHALL--Karen J. Died peacefully at her home, with her view of the park and family surrounding her on June 11, 2005. Predeceased by her father Sidney Spitzer and mother Louise Spitzer. Survived by her children Claudia Gordon, Peter Marshall, Andrew Marshall and Jennifer Marshall, grandchildren Jessica Gordon, David Gordon and Jake Marshall, extended family, adopted family, Molly, and many friends. She touched the lives of so many people with a depth of spirit that was remarkable to those who crossed her path. She will be missed. A memorial to celebrate her life will be held at Frank Campbell Funeral Home in NYC on June 25th at 1:30 PM. In her honor, donations for nursing training can be made to Hospice of New York directed to Dr. Fred Schwartz with a memo ''nondeductible''.
