
        MEISELS--Ida Ruth. Age 93. Died in Miami, FL, on February 12, 2004. She was a pianist, composer, and arranger of traditional and modern Hebrew and Yiddish songs, and accompanist for her late husband, Cantor Saul Meisels. She is survived by her son Samuel and his wife Alice, and by her daughter Florence, as well as four grandchildren and a great-granddaughter. Contributions may be made to the Ida Ruth Meisels Scholarship Fund of the Cantor's Assembly of America, 3080 Broadway, NY, NY 10027.
