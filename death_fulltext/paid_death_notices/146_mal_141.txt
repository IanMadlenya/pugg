
        JANOWSKI-Moses, 87 on February 28. Beloved husband of the late Roza Janowksi, father of Aaron, Benjamin, and Batyah, grandfather to Daniel, Larry, Lisa, David, Peter, and Ephraim, brother of Berl, Rachel and Hannah. His humor, enthusiasm, respect for scholarship, integrity, dignity and caring for others will live on in the memory of all who knew him.
