
        ELLIOTT -- John Jr. The Browning School notes with sadness the death of ''Jock'' Elliott '38. With his brother ''Oz'' '41, he was a mainstay of the Browning Alumni Association and a living link to the school's past. Their father, John Elliott '08, was one of John A. Browning's first students. Jock's generosity of spirit and civic commitment will be deeply missed. James S. Chanos President of the Board Stephen M. Clement, III Headmaster Jeffrey M. Landes '83 President, Alumni Assoc.
