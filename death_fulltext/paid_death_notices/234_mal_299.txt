
        MURRAY--Anthony F. of Manhattan and Setauket, L.I. died July 22, 2006 at his apartment. Born Govan Glasgow August 25, 1936. Youngest son of Daniel and Catherine Murray. Following early years in Scotland emmigrated to New York City in the early 1960's. Active in fashion media and sales. Survived by brother James Murray and his wife Jenny of Sidney, Australia, fourteen nieces and nephews and thirteen great nieces and nephews in USA, Scotland, England and Australia. Funeral Services at St. Thomas Moore at 89th Street, Friday July 28th at 10:30 am.
