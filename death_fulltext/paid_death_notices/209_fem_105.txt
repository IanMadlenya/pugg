
        KLINK--Sandra Scott. Was lost to us after a long illness on June 10, 2004. Beloved mother of Kirsten Morehouse and Trina Morehouse of NYC. Cherished grandmother of Theodora Lindsay Scott Smith, wife of Fredric J. Klink. Editor at New York Magazine and King Features Syndicate. She was loved for her passion for animals, folk art, travel and literature, all shared with her many treasured friends and family. A dedicated volunteer, she donated time and energy to others. Her creativity and graciousness will live on in her adored girls. A celebration of her life and prayers for her final peace will be offered at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Thursday, June 17th, 4PM.
