
        OKEN--Ethel (nee Greenstein). Of Boynton Beach, FL., formerly of Bayside and Glen Head, N.Y., died August 15, 2004 at age 81. She was born in The Bronx, graduated from NYU, and received her Master's degree from Columbia University. She was a long-time teacher and chair of Health and Physical Education at Bayside High School. She was an avid tennis and golf player. She is survived by her husband of 57 years, Morton, daughter Marilyn (Jeffrey) Margulies of Stony Brook, N.Y., son Barry (Melanie Fried) of Portland, OR., six grandchildren (Amy, Paul and Lee Margulies; Kiva, Adam and Corey Oken), sister Frieda Nelson and brother Philip Green. Funeral services will be held at Sinai Memorial Chapel, 15200 Jog Road at 10:30 A.M. on Wednesday, August 18th.
