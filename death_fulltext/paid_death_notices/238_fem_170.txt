
        VILLAMAR--Elena Kerr. Born April 23, 1924 in Mexico and died Sunday, November 26th in New York with her daughter holding her hand, as she would have wanted it. She was self-taught and well read. Having been deprived of formal education herself and widowed young, she worked all of her life and put her daughter through Marymount High School in Los Angeles, and then Stanford University and Stanford Law School. She also was well traveled, having been to 50 countries, many of them several times. She took her daughter with her everywhere as a child, and, when she grew old, her daughter took her mother everywhere with her. She was witty and loved to talk. She was a force of nature. Most of all, she was very loving and very loved. She will be missed every day by her daughter, Carla Kerr Stearns, her granddaughters, Elena Ann and Elizabeth Frances Stearns. She was the loving wife of the late Robert F. Kerr. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, Madison and 81st, on Wednesday, November 29th, 2006 at 7-9pm. Services will be held at the Church of St. Paul the Apostle, Columbus Avenue and 60th Street, on Thursday, November 30th, 2006 at 10am.
