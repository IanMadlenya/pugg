
        LALLY--Veronica M. (nee McCarthy) entered peacefully into eternal life on January 18, at the age of 83, after a brief illness. Beloved wife of the late Martin J. Lally, NYPD. Loving mother of Veronica M. Kehoe, the late Martin J. Lally III and Maureen B. Lally. Cherished grandmother of Allise McAuley Dickson, Martin J. IV and Stacy Ann Lally. Friends may visit at the John Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Ave (70th St) Tuesday and Wednesday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday, January 22 at 10 AM at Our Lady of Good Counsel Church, 230 East 90th Street, Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Her life and memory are a blessing to all who knew her.
