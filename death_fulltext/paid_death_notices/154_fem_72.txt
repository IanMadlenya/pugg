
        ADLEMAN-Jeanne. In San Francisco, on November 6, after a long illness. Therapist, Author, Educator, and Feminist and Anti-Racist Activist. Survived by her loving children, Martha and Donald Mahoney of Miami, Stepdaughters, Judith Mahoney Pasternak of New York and Joan Mahoney of Detroit, and thirteen grandchildren and step-grandchildren. No flowers, please (for health reasons); donations may be made in Jeanne's memory to either Gay and Lesbian Outreach to Elders in San Francisco or The San Francisco Women's Building.
