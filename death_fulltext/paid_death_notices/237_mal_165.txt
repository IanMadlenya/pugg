
        SALSITZ--Norman. The Officers, Board of Trustees and staff of American Friends of Magen David Adom-Armdi mourn the passing of Norman Salsitz. Norman was President of the Suburban AFMDA Chapter in Springfield/Union, NJ and an active and committed member of the organization. Our sincerest condolences to his daughter Esther Dezub and his three grandchildren. Mark D. Lebow, President Stuart A. Jackson, Chairman Daniel R. Allen, Exec. V.P.
