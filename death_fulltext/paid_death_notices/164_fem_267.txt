
        MacAUSLAND-Jean C. Of Palm Beach, Fla. On September 24, 2000. Predeceased by her husband, Earle R. MacAusland, and a niece Jean Dickson. Survived by her brother, Robert Cowger, her sister, Helene Dickson, both of Santa Fe, New Mexico, eight nieces and nephews and many grand nieces and grand nephews. A service celebrating her life will be held October 4, 2000 at 11AM at the Sconset Chapel, Nantucket, Mass. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Nantucket Garden Club.
