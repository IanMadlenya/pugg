
        FRANKLE-Jean. Died peacefully on December 9, 2000. A former teacher and guidance counselor at Fort Hamilton High School in Brooklyn, she resided in Jamesburg, NJ, and was involved with the Deborah Hospital Foundation and the Rossmoor Arts and Crafts Association. Her late husband, Max H. Frankle, was a prominent labor attorney in New York City. Jean leaves her adoring children, Alice, Bert, Hilda and Joel, 12 loving grandchildren, and 12 precious great-grandchildren. Contributions can be made in her memory to the charity of your choice.
