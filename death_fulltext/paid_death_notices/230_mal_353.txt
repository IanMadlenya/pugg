
        IASON--Girard (Jerry). Born November 12, 1918, the son of Lawrence and Sadie Robinson Iason, died on March 29, 2006. Much loved father of Larry, John and Barbara. Devoted father-in-law of Cara, Valorie and Rob. Extraordinary Poppy of Scott, Sara, Lizzie, Jessica and Emily. Loving husband of the late Jeanne Hirsh Iason. Special friend of Edie Finck. Loving care provided by Carol Houser, Carren Serrette and Ann Williams. Jerry was an alumnus of Phillips Exeter Academy (1935) where he was elected captain of the first lacrosse team, and he was also an alumnus of Yale College (1939). Jerry volunteered for military service after Pearl Harbor and became an officer in the U.S. Navy. On D-Day he participated in the Normandy invasion. He also participated in the landings at Sicily, Salerno and Anzio. After the war he worked with his father at N. Erlanger, Blumgart & Co., Inc. where he became president. He enjoyed squash, golf and travel, but his greatest love was his family. He was an extraordinary father and husband who thoroughly enjoyed life and left his children and grandchildren a great legacy through his devotion to each of us. ''A man's reach should exceed his grasp or what's a heaven for.'' Services private. Family receiving Sunday 28pm. Contributions to St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.
        IASON--Girard. The Board of Governors and Members of Inwood Country Club record with sorrow the passing of Girard Iason, beloved member of the past 54 years. We extend deepest sympathy to the family. David Horowitz, President David Bernstein, Secretary
