
        KRIEGER-Jerome. Of New Hyde Park, NY, passed away at Northshore Hospital on August 12, 2001, surrounded by his wife, sons and family. Born in New York City, on March 6, 1916, he was a graduate of New York University and served with the U.S. Army in WWII in Europe. He is survived by his loving wife Ruth, sons Arthur and Paul, devoted sister-in-law, Gloria Singer and grandchildren, Allyson, Christy, Daniel, Jonathan, Rebecca and Rachel. Funeral will be held at Riverside Chapel, Great Neck, NY, at 1:30 PM Tuesday. Donations may be made to the Long Island Jewish Pulmonary Rehab.
