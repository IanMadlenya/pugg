
        MANLEY-Helen Swenson. At Lakewood, NJ, on January 23, 1997. Beloved wife of the late John Howard Manley. A longtime resident of New York, she was a valued member of the support staff of Sullivan and Cromwell, as secretary to Eustace Seligman, a prominent senior partner. Survived by her brother Victor H. Swenson, Jr., of Mantoloking, NJ, and several nieces and nephews. For information contact D'Elia Funeral Home of Lakewood, NJ.
