
        JACKEL-Harry. Of Glen Rock, NJ. On October 30, 2000. Husband of the late Shirley. Father of Joan S. Polk. Grandfather of Sylvia Woodman and Alexander Polk. He was a retired postal worker for the Grand Central Post Office in New York. He retired from Forbes Magazine as a mailroom employee. He was an editor for the New Renaissance Literary Magazine. Funeral services Wednesday 12PM at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4 West) Fair Lawn, NJ. Donations to the Parkinson Foundation.
