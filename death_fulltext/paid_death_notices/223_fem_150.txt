
        BRODIN--Dorothy R. On August 20, 2005 age 87. Widow of Dr. Pierre Brodin, former Director, Lycee Francais de New York and noted scholar of modern French literature. Graduate of Bryn Mawr College, MA, Ph.d, Columbia University. For nearly thirty years a beloved teacher of French at Hunter College High School, the Lycee Francais de New York and Lehman College (CUNY). She will be missed by her many former students and her friends in the U.S. and France. Visitation Wednesday 7 to 9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Funeral Mass Thursday 10 AM at Holy Name R.C. Church, West 96 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. A memorial service to be announced at a later date.
