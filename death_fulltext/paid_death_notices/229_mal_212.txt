
        LUQUER--L. Shippen. 74, Died peacefully February 7 in Milan. He is survived by his devoted wife Giovannella (nee Cherichetti), a son Dominick, a daughter Nathalie du Bot, and five grandchildren. He also leaves a brother Peter C. in Vermont, and a sister Grace L. Madeira in Philadelphia. Burial took place February 11 in Champoluc, Italy.
