
        ROBBINS-Robert T. Of Boca Raton Florida, age 62, entered into Rest on April 18, 2000. He was born in Long Island on July 24, 1937, the son of the late Dr. Abner and Bebe Robbins. Mr. Robbins, an outstanding athlete in his youth, was a graduate of the Wharton School. He was a caring and loving father for his two sons, Gregory and Steve Robbins. He is survived by his brother, Jay Robbins, as well as two nephews. A grave site ceremony will occur at Beth Moses Cemetery (516-295-3100) on Sunday, April 30 at 2 P.M. Relatives and friends are invited to attend.
