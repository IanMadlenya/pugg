
        HENRIKSEN-Arthur F.X., of Patrick Road, died peacefully Sunday June 8, 1997 at his residence after a lengthy illness. A U.S. Army veteran of World War II, he served as a Sergeant having earned the Asiatic Pacific Service Medal, Distinguished Unit Badge, Good Conduct Medal, World War II Victory Medal, Philippines Liberation Ribbon, and the American Service Medal. He was a member of the Ashokan American Legion Post#
