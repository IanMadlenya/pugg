
        JOHNSON-Ward L. Jr. Age 81, of Essex, CT, and Cold Spring Harbor, NY, died in his sleep February 20, 2003. Loving husband of Jean Taylor Johnson; devoted father of Pamela, Timothy, Taylor and Brown; proud grandfather of Sara, Katharine, William and Louisa; great-grandfather of Ian. Graduate of the Kent School and member of St. Anthony Hall at Williams College Class of '43, WWII Army Air Force, avid sailor, navigator, and a great civic leader. He worked at J.C. Penny, Help Start Purnell School, and founded Asset Management. Mentor and friend to many. Service Monday, February 24, 2003 at 11AM, St. John's Episcopal Church, Main and Cross Streets, Essex, CT.
