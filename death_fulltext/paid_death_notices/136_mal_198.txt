
        KAMISH-Harry. The Board of Directors, staff and families of the Association for Children with Retarded Mental Development deeply mourn the passing of our beloved colleague and dear friend, founder and former Associate Executive Director. An advocate for people since the 1940's, he was widely known for his lifelong dedication to those with developmental disabilities and their families whom he served. We were all his friends. Funeral services will be held at the Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, New York, at 11:30AM, Tuesday, May 19, 1998. Jefferson W. Woodcox, President Lawrence Hirsch, Vice President Rose Podolsky, Secretary
