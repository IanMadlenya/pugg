
        JOSEPH-Julian, on May 4, 1997, born in the Bronx, NY, Dec. 20, 1907. He worked in retail luggage sales and repairs in the theater district for many years and had many famous customers ask for ''Joe'' when they came to town. Julian was a beloved son, brother, husband, father and grandfather. Graveside service today, 10:30 AM at Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, New Jersey.
