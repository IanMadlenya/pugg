
        PARKER--Gerald Scott, 87, on January 3, 2004, in Exeter, NH. Beloved husband of Alice (nee Carroll) and father of Katharine, Alison, and Caroline. Proud veteran of WWII. Born in Staten Island, he was a graduate of Williams College and a Vice President of the Guardian Life Insurance Company of America, where he worked for 30 years. An avid sailor, he was a member of Riverside Yacht Club and lived in Old Greenwich, CT, for most of his life. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Rockingham VNA and Hospice, 137 Epping Rd., Exeter, NH 03833.
