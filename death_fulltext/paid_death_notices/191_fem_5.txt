
        QUINN-Rita M. On November 16, 2002, with her family at her bedside. Beloved sister, aunt and godmother. A legend in TV news, Rita worked for and was a devoted friend to Mike Wallace and Diane Sawyer. Warm, generous, intelligent, vivacious, loyal, Rita will be missed by all who knew her and loved her. The family thanks Diane and Mike for their unwavering support throughout her illness.
