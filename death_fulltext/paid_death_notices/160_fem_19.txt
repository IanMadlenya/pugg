
        HALL-Margaret Dunham, in Marion, MA, April 29, 2000, age 94 years, of Rochester, MA. Educator, founder of Camp Snipatuit in Rochester, MA. Sister of Constance D. Schell of Myerstown, PA., also survived by many nieces and nephews. A memorial service will take place on Sunday, May 7th at 2 P.M. in the First Congregational Church in Rochester, MA. It is suggested remembrances be made in her name in care of her nephew John Hall, 453 Snipatuit Rd., Rochester, MA. 02770. For information contact the Wilson Chapel, 479 County St., New Bedford.
