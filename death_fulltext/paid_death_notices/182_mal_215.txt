
        BALLA-Rabbi Imre. We mourn with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in Budapest in 1973, he served pulpits in Hungary, Vancouver, and Sudbury, Ont. At the time of his death, he was the rabbi of B'nai Israel in St. Catherines, Ont. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Iris Cohen and sons, Peter and Adam. Rabbi Reuven Hammer, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP The Rabbinical Assembly
