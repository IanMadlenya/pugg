        NOYES-Mrs. Rosita (Julius W.). The officers and Board of The Spanish Institute give their deepest condolence to the family of Mrs. Julius W. Noyes and particularly to her surviving son, Jose. Rosita, as she was known to her family and friends, loved her native country, Spain, and throughout her life was involved with The Spanish Institute, serving on the Board for a number of years. Her work was acknowledged by His Majesty King Juan Carlos I of Spain and the Spanish Government through the bestowal of the honor, The Lazo de Dama Isabel la Catolica. The Spanish Institute gave Mrs. Noyes a special Gold Medal in 1986, in recognition of her contribution to US-Spanish cultural exchange. Mrs. Noyes will always be remembered.
