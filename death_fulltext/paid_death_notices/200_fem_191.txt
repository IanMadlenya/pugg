
        ZWERLING--Gertrude R. Of Stamford, CT, died peacefully on Thursday, September 25, 2003. Mrs. Zwerling was born on September 18, 1907 in New York City. She is survived by her daughters, Louise Orkin and Alice Kaplan, by her son, Steven Drucker, and by eight grandchildren and ten greatgrandchildren. She was blessed with a rich and rewarding life. A graveside Funeral Service will be held on Monday, September 29, 2003 at Mt. Ararat Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY at 11:30am. Donations may be made in her memory to Ziv Tzedakah Fund, 384 Wyoming Ave, Millburn, NJ, 07041.
