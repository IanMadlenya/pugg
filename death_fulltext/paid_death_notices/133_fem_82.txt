
        FAIRCHILD-Helen Wing. Age 76. Of Hobe Sound, Florida. Died Sunday February 8, 1998 at her residence in Hobe Sound, Florida. She was an interior designer in New York, Jamaica, West Indies and South Florida area for many years. She was an active environmentalist and loving supporter of fine arts. Survivors include her son, William S. Fairchild, her daughter, Pamela Van Beek, two sisters and seven grandchildren. For information, Young and Prill Funeral Home, Stuart, Florida.
