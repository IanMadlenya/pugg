
        ROFFE-Ruth. 82. Died at home on May 23, 2002. Loving wife of the late Hal Roffe. Devoted mother of Ken, Andrew and Barbara; cherished grandmother of Peter, Michael, Sarah, Lucas, Amanda and Matthew. Beloved mother-in-law of Valerie and Anna. She will be forever in our hearts. Funeral service-Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 10 AM, Sunday, May 26, 2002. Memorial contributions may be made to Senior Rabbi's Discretionary Fund, Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd, New Rochelle, NY 10804
        ROFFE-Ruth. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its cherished member and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. Patricia Lampl, President Michael Z. Cahana, Rabbi
