
        GRAIFMAN-David. Age 40. Loving husband to Christine Huhn. Cherished son to Ruth and Julius Graifman. Loving brother of Gary and Brian Graifman. Dear son-inlaw of Marilyn and Richard Huhn. Also survived by many sisters-inlaw, brothers-in-law, nieces and nephews. Vice-President of Keefe, Bruyette & Woods located at 2 World Trade Center, 89th Floor. David was a kind, tolerant, generous, intelligent, witty and caring human being, who lived life to the fullest. He will be forever missed by his entire family and his dear friends. A memorial service will be held Sunday, October 7 at 1 PM, Temple Beth-El, 415 Viola Rd, Spring Valley, NY. Donations may be made in his name to David Graifman Scholarship Fund, c/o Kantrowitz, Goldhamer & Graifman, 747 Chestnut Ridge Rd, Chestnut Ridge, NY 10977.
