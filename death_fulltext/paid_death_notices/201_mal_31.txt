
        DUNLOP--John T. Of Belmont, MA, on October 2, 2003, at the age of 89. Beloved husband of the late Dorothy W. Dunlop; father of John B. Dunlop, Beverly D. Donohue and Thomas F. Dunlop; Grandfather of Maria Tobias, John S., Olga and Catherine Dunlop, Michael and Philip Donohue; great-grandfather of Elizabeth and Gregory Tobias, John B. and Jacob Dunlop and Sophia Muys; Lamont University Professor Emeritus of Harvard University; distinguished economist, expert in labor, management and government relations and dispute resolution; public servant; formerly Secretary of Labor, Director of the Cost of Living Council, Dean of the Faculty of Arts and Sciences at Harvard University and chairman of the Harvard Economics Department; Chairman of the Massachusetts Joint Labor-Management Committee for Municipal Police and Fire. His family deeply mourns his loss and draws strength from his life. A private funeral will be followed by a memorial service at a later date. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the John T. Dunlop Fund at the Labor and Worklife Program, Harvard Law School, 1350 Massachusetts Avenue, Suite 731, Cambridge, MA 02138. For information Brown & Sons Funeral Home, Belmont.
