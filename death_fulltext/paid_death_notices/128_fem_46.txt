
        ROBERTS-Elaine F. In loving memory of our wife, mother and grandmother who gave generously with her heart and soul, died September 2, 1997. Her loyalty to family and friends and unbroken spirit for life has been passed on to survivors husband, Lloyd, children, Lisa and Joshua, Lisa and Steven and granddaughters, Sydney, Richelle and Perry. Funeral services were held on Friday, September 5.
