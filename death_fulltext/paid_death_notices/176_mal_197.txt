
        SWAINE-John F. On September 11, 2001, at the World Trade Center. Beloved husband of Suzanne. Adoring father of Sarah, Emily and Hanna. Devoted son of Frank and Maryanne. Dear brother of Dianne, Susan and Kate. He was employed for the last decade as a corporate bond broker for Cantor Fitzgerald on the 104th floor of the North Tower of the World Trade Center. Memorial service Tuesday, 11 AM, at Sts. John and Paul Church in Larchmont. In lieu of flowers donations benefiting the families of the Police and Firemen slain at the World Trade Center would be appreciated.
