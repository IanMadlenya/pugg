
        GILRAIN--Gerard J. Private services were held for Gerard J. Gilrain, 74, of Montville, NJ. Mr. Gilrain died at his home on August 13 after a long illness. His career as an executive in the printing industry spanned over fifty years. Mr. Gilrain attended St. John's University and had been a first lieutenant with the 7th Regiment of the New York National Guard. He was a member of the Upper Montclair Country Club, Clifton, NJ, the New York Athletic Club and Gardiner's Bay Country Club, Shelter Island, NY. Surviving are his wife Sally, a son, John R., a daughter Joanne C. and four grandchildren, Kristin, Jennifer, Robert and Gerard. A memorial fund has been established in Gerard Gilrain's name at Memorial-Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, PO Box 27106, New York, NY 10087; phone 646-227-3546.
