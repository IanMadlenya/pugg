
        KAUFMANN-Martha Martel, died Thursday, November 27, at age 82, following a heroic battle against breast cancer. Beloved wife of 55 years to Harold, cherished mother of Renee, Carol, Joan, Charles and Isaac, and adored grandmother of Robert, Marion, Victoria, Sasha, Amelia, and Samuel. A woman of valor, former army nurse, friend to all who knew her. She will be missed. Services Sunday, November 30, 11 AM, Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Rego Park, New York.
