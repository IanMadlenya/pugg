
        ZWERLING-Irving. Age 69. Died Monday July 7, 1997 among family members at his home in New York City. A devoted and loving husband and father as well as a brilliant lawyer. Irving will be missed by his wife of forty years Michele, his 3 daughters Caroline, Fabienne and Patricia, his son-inlaw Michael and his three grandchildren Justin, Gabby and Dylan. Mr. Zwerling will be cremated and services will be private.
