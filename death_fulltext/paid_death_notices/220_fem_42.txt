
        LEVY--Lauren A. The Horace Mann School is deeply saddened by the tragic passing of a cherished and beloved alumna, Lauren Levy '99. Her sense of humor, warmth and gentle spirit filled the halls of HM. Lauren's courage and devotion to family and friends will remain with us forever. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to her parents, Wendy and Larry A. Levy and to the rest of her family. Dr. Eileen Mullady Head of School Robert J. Katz '65 Chair, Board of Trustees
