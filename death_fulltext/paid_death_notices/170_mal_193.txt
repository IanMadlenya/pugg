
        WALSH-Daniel P., Sr. It is with deep sadness that we note the passing of Daniel P. Walsh, Sr., father of Winthrop-University Hospital's President and Chief Executive Officer, Daniel P. Walsh. His legacy can be found in his large and loving family. On behalf of the Hospital's Board of Directors, Administrative Staff, and Medical Staff, we extend sincere condolences to his wife, five children, 23 grandchildren and 23 greatgrandchildren. Our thoughts and prayers are with them all in this time of sadness. Richard A. Newhouse, Jr. Chairman, Board of Directors The Administrative Staff and the Medical Staff of Winthrop-University Hospital
