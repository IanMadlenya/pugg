
        REISMAN-William, 95, on Feb. 24 in Vero Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Edna for 70 years; loving father of Robert and daughter-inlaw, Toby; cherished grandfather of Larry, his wife Lee, and greatgrandfather of Rhett, all of Vero Beach; and granddaughter Shari of Atlanta. Formerly of Washington Heights and owner of Style Vogues. Services in Florida.
