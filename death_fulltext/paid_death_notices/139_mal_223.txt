
        NEWIRTH-Samuel A. Age 90. On August 18 at Calvary Hospital. Born in Poland and raised in Koln, Germany. He came to the United States in 1936 where he established an international reputation as a scrap metals expert and publisher of a highly respected newsletter on the subject. He is survived by his daughter, Wendy, son Charles and three grandchildren, Casey, Willy and Emma and sisters, Martha and Sophie. Services on Thursday 11:30am at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
