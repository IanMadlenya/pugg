
        MARTINEZ-Alice Eugenia Johnson. On October 27, 1999 at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Arturo Martinez. Mother of Alice Eugenia Aragones. Grandmother of Jay Johnson Aragones, Alice Eugenia Vergara, and Patricia Courtney Aragones. Great grandmother of Peter Augustine, Benedict Xavier, and Alice Christina Vergara. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Sunday, 3-7 PM. Funeral mass at St. Thomas More Church, 65 East 89 Street, Monday, 10:30 AM. Interment private. Because of her strong desire for the education of young women, contributions in her memory may be made to The Convent of the Sacred Heart, 1177 King Street, Greenwich, CT. 06830.
