
        SILVER--Claire. Died peacefully and with dignity at her home in Woodland Hills, CA. Devoted wife to David, loving mother to Jillian and Jan, Carole and Harvey, Joe and Renee, adored Grandma to Annie, Matt, Scott, Adam and Jenny, and Great-Grandma to Hayden. She lived her life caring for her family, friends, and whomever knew her. As a pioneer in the insurance industry, her business acumen and abilities served as a model for all women. Her wisdom, grace and gentle nature will be forever missed. Services Wednesday, noon, at Riverside Chapels, Great Neck, NY.
