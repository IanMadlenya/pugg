
        SECONDARI-Helen Jean Rogers. On Saturday, March 28th. While in college she was elected to the NSA. She graduated Magna Cum Laude and Phi Beta Kappa from Catholic University. Received M.A. from Radcliff. Taught at Harvard as one of first female teaching fellows. Associate Producer for ABC News where she met husband John H. Secondari. Together they made many TV movies and won five Emmys and 2 Peabodys. She is survived by son John Gerry of Washington, D.C. and daughter Linda Helen of NYC. Memorial will be held May 2nd at 2:30PM at the Basilica of the National Shrine of the Immaculate Conception on the campus of Catholic University (Michigan Ave and 4th St, N.E.). Donations in her name to the Father Hartke Drama Endowment, c/o Catholic University, Washington, D.C. 20064.
