        FROELICH -- Denise McN. September 5th, 2004. The Board of Directors and staff of the Women's Commission for Refugee Women and Children mourn the passing of Board Member Denise Froelich, a true friend to refugees around the world.         FROELICH--Denise. The residents and Board of Directors of 875 Park Avenue mourn the passing of Denise Froelich, a longtime friend, neighbor and past president of our Board of Directors.
