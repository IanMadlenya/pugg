
        GOODMAN--Myron S. The Committee of Past Presidents of the Hebrew Academy of Five Towns & Rockaway notes with sadness the passing of Myron S. Goodman, a strong supporter of HAFTR in its early years. Myron's kindnesses, enthusiasm and positive attitude were very much appreciated by HAFTR. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Israel. Committee of Past Presidents HAFTR
