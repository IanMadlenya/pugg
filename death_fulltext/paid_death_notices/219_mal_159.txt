
        GINSBURG--David, 89 years old, died on April 15th, 2005. Beloved husband of Pearl. Devoted father of Sandra and Charles Anjard and Howard and Sharon Ginsburg and stepfather of Ronald Steinberg, Arnold and Diane Steinberg, Sharyn and Rabbi Moshe Edelman and Alan and Orly Steinberg. Cherished grandfather and greatgrandfather. Former Board Member of the Great Neck Synagogue, past President of the Great Neck Synagogue Men's Club and past Deputy Grand Chancellor of the Knights of Pythias, Levittown Lodge 774. Resilient, optimistic, devoted to family and friends. He will be sorely missed. Services Sunday, 11 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
