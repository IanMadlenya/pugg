
        CERINO-Natalie. For over 59 years the adored wife of Raphael, died October 22, 2002. Mrs. Cerino was the loving and beloved mother of Carol Baer and Paul Cerino, sister of Ira and George Kapp, sister-in-law of June and Doris Kapp, Elaine Karpf and the late Dorothy Waage. Her many interests encompassed music, art and social issues. Her elfin humor and understanding heart will be missed by everyone whoever met her.
