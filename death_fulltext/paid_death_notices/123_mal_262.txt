
        KELMAN-Norman, M.D. The Association for the Advancement of Psychoanalysis, Inc of the Karen Horney Psychoanalytic Institute and Center, the executive council, and membership are greatly saddened by the passing of our dear friend, colleague, and mentor. Dr. Kelman, was a man of inspiring qualities, direct and committed to his words. His genuine human concern as well as his intellectual and moral integrity was a model for all. We deeply miss him and wish to express our profound sympathy and condolences to his dear family and friends. Nicole Dintenfass, Ph.D., President
