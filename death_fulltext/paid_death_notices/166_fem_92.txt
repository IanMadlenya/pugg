
        VALLA-Florence. Died November 10, at Roosevelt Hospital. Lifelongfriend of the late Pearl Rubenstein. Graduate of Cooper Union. Advertising agency art director. Co-owner with her friend Ruth Stark of Stark-Valla Emporium in Greenwich Village. Manager of the Mt. Sinai Hospital Gift Shop. Service 12 noon, Monday, November 20, at Buckley Funeral Home, 445 West 43 St. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
