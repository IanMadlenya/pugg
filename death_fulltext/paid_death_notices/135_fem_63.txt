
        LAKE-Joan, 61. Died April 6, 1998 in Manhattan. A graduate of Skidmore College, Saratoga Springs and Columbia University School of Social Work, she was a social worker in New York for many years. Leaves her companion Felix Transport and several cousins. Burial in Cambridge, NY, Tuesday, Apr. 14. Memorial service, Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64th Street, 3 P.M., Friday, April 17.
