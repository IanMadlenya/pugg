
        MANES-Geraldine. Of Palm Beach, FL and New York City, died Sunday, July 16. Loving wife of Jules Manes, who predeceased her. Beloved mother of Jill Rosen and Jack Manes. Devoted grandmother of Adam, Leah, Ross, Evan, James, and Deena. Great grandmother to Jena and Max, and sister to Irma, Marshall and Marilyn. Jerry did everything she attempted well. She was a dynamic personality, strong willed but loving and caring to her family and friends. She was unique and will be greatly missed.
