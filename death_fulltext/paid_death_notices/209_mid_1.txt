
        MINTZ--Ruth Klinghoffer. Of New York City, died of Alzheimer's on June 3, 2004. Loving mother of Rayna Ragonetti and Peter Mintz and his wife Karen, special grandmother of Philip Mintz and his wife Alexis Hilton Mintz, and many other grandchildren, great-grandchildren, nieces and nephews. A kind and gentle spirit, she will be missed by all who knew her. Graveside service at 11 AM, Monday, June 7, Knollwood Cemetery, Queens, 718 386-6700.
