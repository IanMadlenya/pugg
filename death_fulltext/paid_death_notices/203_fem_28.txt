
        WIMPFHEIMER -- Victorine Kops. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Seymour. Loving mother of Doris and David Finkel, Susan and Toby Abend, Ellie and Dr. Tom Slovis, Ann and the late Rabbi Baruch Bokser, and David, adored grandmother of 15 and greatgrandmother of 19. Educator, community leader and friend. Her dynamic and compassionate life will forever be an inspiration to the many people whose lives she touched. Contributions may be made to the Vicki Wimpfheimer Memorial Fund or the Jewish Communal Fund, 130 E. 59th St., NY, NY 10022. Services Monday, 9:30AM, Park Avenue Synagogue, 87th and Madison. For information call Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 212769-4400.
        WIMPFHEIMER--Vicki. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of our honorary trustee, a devoted and beloved congregant. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln Senior Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft President
