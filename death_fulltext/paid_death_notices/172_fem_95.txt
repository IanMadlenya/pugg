
        HORMAN-D. Elizabeth. At age 96. Gifted artist. Passed away peacefully on May 10, 2001. Graduated from Barnard and studied at NYC Art Students League. Beloved wife of the late Edmund Horman. Loving mother of the late Charles Horman who was killed in Chile in 1973 during Pinochet's violent coup as told in the movie Missing. Dear sister of Daniel Lazar, Lawrence Lazar and the late Ruth Lazar. Cherished mother-in-law of Joyce Horman. Elizabeth's many friends will miss her greatly. Memorial service at All Souls Unitarian Church, Lexington Avenue at 80th St., Wednesday, May 16th, 3PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Center for Constitutional Rights for the Charles Horman Truth Fund, POB 2331, NY, NY 10021.
