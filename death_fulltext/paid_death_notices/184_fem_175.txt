
        WALLMAN-Natalie. May 22, 2002 at age 82. Cherished wife of Pete Wallman; devoted mother of Barbara Fass and Bill Wallman; loving grandmother of Bob Fass; beloved sister of Phoebe Horowitz and Herb Levin. She touched many lives, and will be greatly missed. Burial and graveside service on Sunday, May 26, 11:00 AM at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Route 109 and Southern State Parkway (Exit 33), Lindenhurst, NY.
