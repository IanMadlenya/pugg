
        MANTELL-Dorothy (nee Fishman), on April 7, 2002, at the age of 86. Beloved wife of the late Bertram. Loving mother of Edmund, Michael, and Anna. Dear sister of Seymour Frost. Loving grandmother of David, Adam, Ruth, Laura, Deborah, Seth, Joel and Fran. She was active in civic and community affairs in both New York City and New City, NY. Graveside service Wednesday, April 10, 10:30 AM, at the Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hastingson-Hudson, NY.
