
        BOLSTERLI-Andrew. The President, Chancellor, Boards of Governors and Overseers, faculty, administration and students of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion express our heartfelt condolences to John and Marianne Slade, beloved members of the HUC family, on the untimely and tragic death of their cherished grandson, Andrew. May his memory be a comfort to all those who mourn him, his dear parents and sister, family and friends. May God comfort them among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman President Dr. Alfred Gottschalk Chancellor Burton Lehman, Chair Board of Governors
