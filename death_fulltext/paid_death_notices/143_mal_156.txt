
        ROSENBLUM-Laurence (Larry) of Tucson, AZ. (formerly of New York), December 9, 1998. Beloved husband of fifty-six years, devoted father, grandfather and greatgrandfather, fun loving uncle, he taught us how to love and laugh and how to be brave and strong. Larry is survived by his wife, Marcia, sons Alex (Evelia), and Shelly (Jeri), daughter Betsy (Richard), grandchildren Edward (Nicole), John (Denise), Adriana and Holly and great-grandchildren Levi and Isaac. Services will be held on Sunday, 10AM Plaza Memorial, at 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
