
        BERLINER--Dorothy. On December 28, 2004, at age 89. Loving and beloved wife of the late Joseph Berliner. Devoted and loving mother of Helga and Susan, adoring grandmother of Marc, Jonathan, Julia, and Michael. A true eshet chayal, she emerged from the ashes of Auschwitz to find her husband and again raise a family. The ferocity of her love for her family and for life is an inspiration to all who knew her. The funeral service will be held on Thursday, December 30 at noon at Congregation Ahavath Torah, 240 Broad Ave, Englewood, NJ. Contributions may be made to American Society for Yad Vashem, 500 5th Ave, NY, NY
