
        EVARTS-M. Richard. Born March 5, 1920 in Holyoke, MA to Frances and Benjamin Evarts. Died March 21, 2002 in Tucson, AZ. Survived by his wife, Carol; daughter Jill Evarts of Nantucket, MA; son Max of New York City; and sister Abby Meyer of Chicago, IL. Preceded in death by son, David. He will be greatly missed. All of our love. Memorials will be made to the National Jewish Center, Denver, CO.
