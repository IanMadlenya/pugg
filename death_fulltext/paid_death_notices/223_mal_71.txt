
        WHITE--William R., Jr. 68, suddenly on August 1, 2005 in Sarasota, FL. He is survived by his son, William R. White III of Venice, FL, brother David R. White, Chatham, New Jersey and sister Deborah W. Andersen, Aiken, SC. He was born in NYC to the late William R. and Elizabeth White. He attended Friends Seminary, graduated from Trinity Pawling School in 1954 and Bucknell University in 1958. He retired from his banking career in NYC as a Senior Vice President of Irving Trust Co. in 1986. He then formed and managed an investment advisory firm in Venice, FL. He had a great love of singing and the performing arts. Memorial services will be at Cronrath Funeral Home, Lewisburg, PA. at 11 A.M., Saturday, August 20. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be sent to American Diabetes Ass'n, South West FL Office, 4902 Eisenhower Blvd., Suite 295, Tampa, FL. 33634.
