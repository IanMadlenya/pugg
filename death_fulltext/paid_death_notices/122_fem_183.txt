
        SNEPVANGERS-Hannah K. On March 23, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Rene Snepvangers. Loving mother of Yseult and Morton Freilicher. Dear sister of the late Samuel R. Kan. Hannah was a gifted and extraordinary French teacher whose personality radiated wit, life and warmth to all who knew her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, March 24th, 7-9:30 PM. Interment private. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions in her memony may be made to the Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Ave, NYC 10021.
