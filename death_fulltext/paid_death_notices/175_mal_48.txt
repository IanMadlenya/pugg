
        HIRSCH-Bert. On August 5, 2001. Beloved husband for more than fifty-three years, of his loving and devoted wife, Irene, loving father of Vivian (and Alan), and Irv (and Janet), cherished Opa of Allison, Sara, and Matthew. Dear brother of Hilda; brother-in-law, grand uncle, uncle, cousin, and special friend to many in Washington Heights. Served with distinction as a Vice President of Congregation Shaare Hatikvah. Born in Berwangen, Germany, on January 4, 1917, emigrated to this country in 1939. Sgt. in The U.S. Army during World War II. A gentle, sensitive man, who led by quiet example with grace and dignity. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral services on August 7, 2001 at 10 A.M. at Hirsch and Sons Funeral Home, 1225 Jerome Avenue, Bronx, NY.
