
        BLAU-Harold. Teacher, Educator and Author of ''Dictionary of English Sounds, '' on October 16, at age 85. Loving father of Stephen and his wife Patricia, Francine Blau and her husband Lawrence Kahn and Amy Marie Blau. Devoted brother of Charles and Helen Blau, Seymour and Gerda Blau, Rosalind B. Paulson and the late Milton Paulson, Janice Blau, the late Sidney Blau, and brother-inlaw of Molly, wife of the late Jerome Blau. Cherished grandfather of Daniel and Lisa Kahn. Beloved uncle and great-uncle. Service was held at Frank E. Campbell, Sunday, October 17th.L
