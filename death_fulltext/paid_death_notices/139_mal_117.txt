
        KOFFLER-David, M.D. It is with great sorrow that the Mount Sinai Alumni mourns the passing of our highly exteemed and beloved colleague. Dr. Koffler joined the staff in 1958, and for twenty years distinguished himself as pathologist, scientist and teacher. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Wayne S. Fuchs, M.D., President Avi Barbasch, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary William G. Stein, M.D., Treasurer
