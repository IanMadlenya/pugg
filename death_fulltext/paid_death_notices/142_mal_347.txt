
        SIZEMORE-John Randall. Of New York City and East Hampton, died of AIDS at his Manhattan residence on November 27, 1998. He was 48 years old. Mr. Sizemore was the beloved partner for 25 years of Norbert Alan Anuskiewicz, as well as a devoted brother to Saundra Joe Sizemore of Southfield, Michigan, and loving son of Betty Gable Sizemore of Cullman, Alabama. Mr. Sizemore was an alumnus of Wayne State University, Michigan and worked for many years in Editorial Operations at ''Fortune'' magazine. Respects may be paid at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Saturday, November 28th from 2:004:00PM and 7:00-9:00PM. Funeral services will be at Dovin Funeral Home in Lorain, Ohio on Monday, November 30th with interment at Calvary Cemetery in that city. In lieu of flowers, it is requested that contributions be made to S.A.G.E., 212-741-2247.
