
        POWERS--Helen F., 92, of NYC, on Saturday, December 23rd, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Maxwell Powers, cherished mother of Judy and Sharon, adoring grandmother of Olivia, Daniel, Sarah and Eve. Social worker, Program Director, Associate Director of National Council of Jewish Women, Director of Greenwich House Camp, esteemed member of West End Synagogue. Services Tuesday, December 26th, 11am, at Plaza Chapel, 91st and Amsterdam Ave.
        $(6$)POWERS--Helen. The community of West End Synagogue, a Reconstructionist Congregation, is diminished by the death of our founding member Helen Powers. The spirit and soul of this gracious and elegant lady enlivened all of our comings-together, and we will miss her greatly.
