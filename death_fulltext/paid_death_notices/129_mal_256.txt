
        SELIGSOHN-Max. Migdal Ohr Institutions in Israel and American Friends of Migdal Ohr deeply mourn the passing of Max Seligsohn, father of Gerald Seligsohn, Board Member of American Friends of Migdal Ohr. We express our condolences to wife, Selma, and grandchildren, Lisa and Danny. May the A-lmighty comfort the family in their time of deep bereavement and may they be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Yitzchok Dovid Grossman Dean and Founder, Migdal Ohr Morris Talansky Chairman of the Board Max Thurm, President Shalom Siegfried, Exec. Director
        SELIGSOHN-Max. Fada Industries mourn the passing of Max Seligsohn, father of Gerald Seligsohn, and husband of Selma Seligsohn. He was held in great esteem by all who knew him, his family, friends and business associates. His impact, positive outlook and influence was profound. His wisdom and counsel were treasured by all who knew him. He will be sorely missed. Michael Kronenberg
