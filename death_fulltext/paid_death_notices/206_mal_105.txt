
        LOVELACE--Richard S. Age 86. Naval Architect. Born in Baltimore, MD. June 21, 1917. Died February 29, 2004 at Essex, CT., after a long illness. Princeton 1940, MIT B.S., 1942. WWII Naval Reserve Officer as MIT Instructor, Naval Architecture and Pearl Harbor Submarine Salvage Director. With National Bulk Carriers, designed and managed world's largest dredge to keep Orinoco River open. Beloved by all who knew him, especially wife Caroline, daughter Caroline, son Richard, a sister, brother, seven nieces and nephews. Contributions in his memory may be made to Essex Land Conservation Trust, Box 373, Essex, CT. 06426.
