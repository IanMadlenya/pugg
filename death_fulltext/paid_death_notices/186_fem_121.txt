
        TOPOL-Ruth Tedi. Mrs. E. Raymond Topol known to all as Tedi passed away Monday morning, July 15 2002. Tedi, wife of Dr. E. Raymond Topol, was known for her sense of humor and her love of life. She was a member of the Elmwood Country Club, a former president of the Women's Metropolitan Golf Association, and the Women's Tri County Golf Association. Tedi graduated from New York University and was named the most beautiful coed of NYU in 1941. Her brother Ed, daughter Jane and son John survive her. She will be deeply missed by her grandchildren Casey, William and Jason who lovingly called her Grandma Tedi.
