
        WOODCOCK-Frances Bennett, 82, of Hope Valley, RI. Died Tuesday, October 6, 1998, in Westerly, RI. She was the widow of Robert B. Woodcock. Born in Chicago, IL, a daughter of the late Clinton Alexander Bennett and Frances Forshew Bennett. She is survived by two daughters, Mary W. Johnson of Hope Valley, RI, and Frances W. MeVay and Adriane Bennett MeVay, and a nephew, Carl David Elliges of Brooklyn, NY. A memorial service will be held Saturday, October 24, at 2 PM, in St. Andrews By the Sea Episcopal Church, 182 Willow Ave, Little Compton, RI. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sakonnet Preservation Society, Box 945, Little Compton, RI 02837.
