
        KAMINSKY-David. Of Fort Lee, NJ, died of cancer Tuesday night, Nov. 10. He was a graduate of City College of NY. An accountant, he built a successful private practice of corporate and individual clients. He is survived by his wife Mildred, also of Fort Lee, a daughter Sue, and two grandchildren, who live in the Washington, D.C. area, and a brother and sister who live in Florida.
