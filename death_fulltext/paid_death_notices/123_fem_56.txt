
        MOSBACHER-Rose Neumann, on April 5, 1997, in her 98th year. Beloved widow of Dr. Emil Mosbacher. She was born in Wurzburg, Germany and emigrated with her family to the U.S. in 1938. She is survived by her loving daughter and son-in-law, Marianne and Peter Flack, two granddaughters, Stephanie and Jennifer, two greatgrandchildren and four nieces. Burial services were private.
