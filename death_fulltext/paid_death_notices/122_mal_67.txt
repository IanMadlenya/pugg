        RECANATI-Ya'acov. We express our heartfelt condolences to Raphael Recanati on the loss of his beloved brother. As Golden Founders of the Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem and devoted benefactors of the Annual Concert and our National Women's Division, Dina and Raphael Recanati have helped the mission of the Hospital to heal the sick and ease the pain of the afflicted. Their exemplary commitment to Tzedaka and Chesed has made a vital difference to Israel's ''Hospital with a Heart and to the people of Jerusalem. May the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee of the Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Charles H.
