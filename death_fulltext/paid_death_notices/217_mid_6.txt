
        SELINGER--Rose. Age 79, died peacefully February 17, 2005 from complications from Alzheimer's disease in Pembroke Pines, FL; former resident of North Woodmere and Lawrence, L.I. Loving wife of the late Martin Selinger; beloved mother of Ann and her husband Robert Kirsch and Dennis Selinger and his wife Juana, cherished grandmother of Harris and David. Funeral service Sunday, February 20th, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St,NYC.
