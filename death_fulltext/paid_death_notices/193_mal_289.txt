
        PHIPPS-Thomas W. Boys & Girls Harbor, Inc. joins in Thanksgiving for the life of its longtime Trustee, Thomas Phipps. Writer, playwright, soldier, golf champion; loving husband, father, and friend. Heartfelt love and thoughts to Mary, Sally, Lang and Jana. Funeral services at St. John's Episcopal Church, South Main Street, Southampton, New York, Saturday, March 1st, 2PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent in his name to Boys & Girls Harbor, Inc., 1 East 104th Street, Room 544, New York, New York 10029.
