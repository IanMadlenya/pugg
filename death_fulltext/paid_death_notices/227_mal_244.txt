
        DELAUNAY--Joan H. On December 21, at the young age of 106. Climbed on Mt. Everest, also a model, and an antique store entrepreneur. Beloved wife of the late Max. Cherished aunt of Richard and his wife Ruth Vlock, Marsha and her husband Jay Sternin and the late Dr. David Vlock and his wife Maria. Survived by 10 great- and many great-great-nieces and nephews. Private cremation.
