
        UPBIN-Israel. Died January 14, 2002 at age 90. Beloved husband of Charlotte, devoted father to Robert, loving grandfather to Daniel, Rachel and Barry. He received rabbinical ordination from Yeshiva University, where he graduated valedictorian in the first class of the school. He then worked for ten years as the first Executive Director of Young Israel. He joined Barton's Candy, where he became Vice President of the company over a 35 plus year career. Service Friday, 11:30 AM, at Boulevard-Park West Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY.
        UPBIN-Israel. Congregation Kneseth Israel, Far Rockaway, N.Y. mourns with heartfelt sorrow the passing of our esteemed member and extends condolences to the entire family. Tzvi Flaum, Rabbi Ralph Pelcovitz, Rabbi Emeritus Jeffrey Sinensky, President
