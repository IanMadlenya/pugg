
        REICH--Ismar M., 82, of Merrick, NY, on April 5th, 2007. Loving and beloved husband of Diane Wechsler Reich, father of Helen Rothman, Arnold Reich and Andrew Reich, father-in-law of Donna LaMagna, grandfather of Jonathan Rothman and Jordan Rothman, and brother of Annabelle Bernard. He was an honored chemical engineer, holder of 11 patents, and retired as Vice-President of Research and Development, Chock Full O' Nuts Corp. Service Friday, April 6, 9:45 am at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street (at Amsterdam Avenue), New York, NY.
