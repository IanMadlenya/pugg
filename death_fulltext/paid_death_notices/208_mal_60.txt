
        PRINCIPE--Francis J. Passed away quietly on Monday morning May 3, 2004 at the very caring St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn. Considered ''Mr. Maspeth'' for his lifelong civic activism. Mr. Principe, a civil engineer, was a maverick in the concrete industry through the development of its standard acceptance throughout New York and the nation. Beloved husband of the late Virginia Anne. Devoted father of Lee James and Virginia Herman. Cherished grandfather of Douglas, Gary, Christopher and Jonathan. Reposing at Papa Vero Funeral Home, 7227 Grand Ave., Maspeth on May 5, 6 and 7 from 2-5 and 79PM. Funeral procession along Grand Avenue Saturday May 8 at 10AM. Funeral Mass St. Stanislaus Church 10:30AM. Interment St. John's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to Maspeth Town Hall in his memory.
