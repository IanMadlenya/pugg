
        GRIGGS--Henry Little, Jr. ''Harry''. Died March 6, 2004 at age 78. Survived by his loving wife Barbara L. Griggs; children Henry, Elisabeth and Peter Griggs; step-daughters Penny Kjellberg and Pamela Blumberg, brother Thomas S. Griggs, Sr.; grandchildren Cornelia and William Griggs; step-grandchildren Caroline and Jessica Kjellberg. A Yale graduate and World War II veteran, he worked at the Hartford Courant, the Patent Trader and then joined NBC News in 1962, with posts in Washington, London and New York. Visitation 5 to 7 PM and services at 7 PM Wednesday, March 10, at the Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Avenue.
