
        KRACKOV--Estelle W. 101, died on Friday, November 11, 2005. Dearly loved by three generations: children Adele, Everett & Bobbie, grandchildren Barry, Beth & Jonathan, Pat, Mimi & Oren, Beth & Joe, and great-grandchildren Alicia, Gabriel & Kate. She shared her zest for life with nieces, nephews, grandnieces, grandnephews & many, many friends. She will be remembered and missed by them all. Mom--here's to music, mysteries, crosswords, Sinatra and super Scrabble in the sky! Funeral Monday, November 14, 9:30 am, Riverside Chapel, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
