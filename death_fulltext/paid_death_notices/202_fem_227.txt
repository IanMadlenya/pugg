
        BOER -- Michele Tortomasi aka Peggy. Devoted and beloved sister, aunt, cousin and friend. Cherished wife of the late John Boer. Peggy was born December 16, 1912. She passed November 22, 2003. Business representative in the education department of ILGWU. Retired in 1975. Lifelong union activist. Cat lover extraordinaire. Involved in animal rights and an outstanding gourmet cook. Will be remembered with love and dearly missed. Funeral services on Monday, December 1st, 12 PM at Trinity Cemetery located at 153 Street and Riverside Drive.
