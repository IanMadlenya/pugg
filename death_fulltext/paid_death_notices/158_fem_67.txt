
        McGERR-Rita. On Friday, March 3 of Sleepy Hollow, NY. A believer in the power of education, she taught Literature for four decades. Wife of the late Gerald McGerr, daughter of Maria Fanelli of Southbury, Conn. and the late Charles Fanelli. Mother of Michael McGerr of Bloomington, IN., and Celia Regan of New Rochelle, NY. Grandmother of Katie, Maggie, Sally and Patrick. Sister of the late Joseph G. Fanelli. Burial private. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions in her memory may be made to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, New York, New York 10021.
