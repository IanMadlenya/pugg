
        QUINN--John G., 62, of Pinehurst, NC, died Saturday, March 27, 2004 at First Health Moore Regional Hospital in Pinehurst. He is survived by his wife, Joan, his two sisters, Ellen Anderson and Kathleen Quinn Schmidt, and ten nieces and nephews. The family will receive friends on Tuesday from 6-8 PM at Boles Funeral Home in Pinehurst. A Mass of Resurrection will be held on Wednesday at 2 PM at Sacred Heart Roman Catholic Church in Pinehurst with Father William L. Pitts as celebrant. Burial will follow in Pinelawn Memorial Park.
