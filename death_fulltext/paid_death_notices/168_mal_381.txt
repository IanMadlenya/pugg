
        MARTIN-Edward John. Of Florida and New York, 90, suddenly on January 22, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Katherine (nee Bennett). Survivors include his wife, Grace B. Schwefel Martin. Devoted father of Edward, Katherine, Elizabeth and Patricia, Karen S. Butler and Charles Schwefel, 7 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren. Retired president of John P. Maguire & Co., New York City, who served his country honorably as a Lieutenant, U.S. Navy, during W.W. II. Dedicated treasurer of the Southside Hospital, director of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick and member, Cardinal's Committee of the Laity. Memorials to Southside Hospital.
