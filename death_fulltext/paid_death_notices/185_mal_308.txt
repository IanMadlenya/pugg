
        BURNHAM-I.W. II. June 24, 2002, age 93, died suddenly and peacefully at sea aboard his beloved Encore V, off the Virginia coast. Devoted husband of Lawrie Weil Burnham, who predeceased him in 1997. Adored father of Jon M. Burnham and Miriam Arnstein Burnham, and Wendy Burnham Morris and Michael Morris. Cherished grandfather of Debra Burnham Hyman, Suzanne Burnham Mankoff, Kimberly Burnham, Jon M. Burnham, Jr., Anthony C. Kaye, Hilary Kaye Kronowitz and Andrew B. Kaye. Blessed with nine loving great grandchildren. Services will be held at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave East, Rye, NY Friday June 28, 2PM. Please no flowers.
        BURNHAM-I.W. The Partners of Burnham and Company, the firm he founded in 1935, express our sympathy to the family of I.W. (Tubby) Burnham. He was a great leader, good friend and towering figure in the securities industry. We were all affected by his enthusiasm and constant commitment to high principles. We shall miss him. The Partners of Burnham and Company
