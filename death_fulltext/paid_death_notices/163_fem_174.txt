
        WAIS-Pauline. August 21, 2000. Beloved sister of the late Bertha and Frances. Daughter of the late Clara Wallkind and Phillip Wais. At 95, she has touched the lives of innumerable friends and colleagues. All those who knew and loved Pauline will profoundly miss her for her graciousness and compassion. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to your favorite charity. Acknowledgements to the home of Pauline Wais.
