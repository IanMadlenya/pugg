
        KENNAN -- Ambassador George F. The Board of Trustees and members of the National Committee on American Foreign Policy mourn the passing of its legendary Honorary Chairman, who articulated the policy of containment, was a proponent of secret diplomacy, won two Pulitzer Prizes, was awarded the Medal of Honor and much more. We extend our deep-felt sympathy to his beloved wife, Annelise, his daughter and our Trustee Grace, and his entire family. William J. Flynn, Chairman George D. Schwab, President
