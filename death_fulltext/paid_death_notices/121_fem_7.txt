
        JAMES-Beryl Clarke. The Greater New York Links regrets to announce the passing of their sister Link, Beryl Clarke James, on Thursday, January 30, 1997. We will miss her sorely. Funeral service Wednesday, Feburary 5, 11 AM, Christ Church, 252nd St. and Henry Hudson Parkway, Bronx.
