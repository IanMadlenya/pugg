
        PAGANO-Helen Baum Laitin. 81, of White Plains, NY, died December 23, 2001, of leukemia. Beloved wife of Frank Pagano, loving mother of Julie Laitin and Laurie Laitin Bergner, grandmother of Joel and Elizabeth, great-grandmother of Jacob. We will miss her spark, energy and joie de vivre. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, December 26, 10:30 AM, at the Ballard - Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains.
