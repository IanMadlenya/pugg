
        SHAFER-Margaret Creal. The Bard College community deeply mourns the passing of Margaret Creal Shafer, a longtime friend and integral member of the Bard family and the Hudson Valley cultural community. Her vitality, creativity and energy have played an essential role in the life of the college and of the surrounding communities. She will be deeply missed. Our condolences go out to her children, grandchildren, and great grandchild. Services will be held at the Church of St. John the Evangelist, in Barrytown, New York on Saturday, August 4th. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab, II, Chairman
