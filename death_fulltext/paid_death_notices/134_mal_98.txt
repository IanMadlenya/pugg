
        JACKSON-Pat H. Jr. Age 81. A retired state employee. Formerly of the Bronx. Residing at Northern Manor Nursing Home, Nanuet, NY. Passed Friday, March 6 after a prolonged illness. He was a WW II veteran stationed in the Pacific. Survived by his loving wife of 54 years Genevieve Carmen Jackson of Nanuet, NY. Beloved father of Mrs. Harolgen L. Chestnut of Maryland and Theresa P. Jackson, MD of Rockland County, NY. Also survived by two grandchildren and 3 great grandchildren. Reposing at Sniffen-Sagala Funeral Home, 235 Route 59, Spring Valley, New York, Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 11AM at St. Joseph's Church, 333 Sneden Place West, Spring Valley. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Northern Manor Geriatrics Center, 199 North Middletown Road, Nanuet, NY 10954 or United Hospice of Rockland, 18 Thiells Mt Ivy Road, Pomona, NY 10970. Dad, we know you are in God's grace, farewell.
