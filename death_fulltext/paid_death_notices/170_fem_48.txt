
        FREIBERG-Sheila Muller. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Sheila Muller Freiberg, a dear friend of the Jewish community. We extend our sincerest condolences to her beloved daughters and their husbands, Patricia and S. William Green and Carole and Marvin Leichtung, leaders admired for thier extraordinary generosity and commitment on behalf of UJA-Federation and its beneficiary agencies. We offer our deepest sympathies to the entire family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair & President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
        FREIBERG-Sheila Muller. Beloved wife of the late Emanuel J. Loving mother of Carole Leichtung and Pat Green. Dear mother in-law of Marvin Leichtung & Bill Green. Cherished grandmother of Jim, Julie, Betsy, Cathy and Louis. Devoted great grandmother of Jackie, Eric & Ben. Services Thursday, 11:30 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 81st St. and Madison.
