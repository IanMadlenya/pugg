
        KRANTHER-Esther. On March 19, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Jules. Devoted sister of Beatrice and the late Ruthie. Loving aunt to Paul, Nancy, Linda, Phyllis and Adele. Past president Sisterhood of Temple Israel of the City of New York. A pioneer in the fashion industry, she will never be forgotten. Services Tuesday, 12 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        KRANTHER-Esther. Sisterhood regrets the passing of our past president, Esther Kranther. We extend our condolences to her family. Her warmth and graciousness will be missed. The Sisterhood of Temple Israel of New York City
