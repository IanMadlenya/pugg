
        ZEICHNER--Rita Mae (nee Silverberg). 76, passed away on Tuesday, August 24, 2004 at her residence on the island of Nevis in the West Indies. She formerly resided in New Haven, CT Washington, DC, San Francisco, CA, Vancouver, BC, Tucson and Paradise Valley, AZ. She is survived by her sons Adam, Jonathan (Monica), Erich (Gabrielle), Walter, her grandchildren John, Yasmine, Oliver, Loulie and Jobim and her brother Robert Silverberg (Robyn). She gave (Joy and Sweetness) to those who knew and loved her and will be dearly missed. Contributions to your favorite charity will be appreciated.
