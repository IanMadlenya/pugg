
        ROSENBERG--Sylvia On November 17, 2005, age 90. Much loved and cherished mother and grandmother, loving and devoted sister. Member of the 92nd St Y, Hadassah, Dorot, and Ansche Hesed. Services Sunday, 11:45 am, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, corner 91 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
