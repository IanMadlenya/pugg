
        McGOVERN--Maggie. Died on February 15, 2005. Profoundly appreciative of the beauty of life, Maggie, a painter, leaves behind not only her loving husband David, daughters Alexandra and Justine, son-in-law Paul and grandchildren Ivy, Nate, George and Daniel, but also many friends and fans. A service will be held Tuesday, February 22 at 11 A.M. at the American Cathedral of Paris. Donations in her memory may be made to the Alzheimers Foundation.
