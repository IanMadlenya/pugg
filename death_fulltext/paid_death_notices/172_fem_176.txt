
        SMITH-Molly. Passed away on May 15, 2001 in Plantation, Florida at the age of 83. She was a former resident of Brooklyn, NY. She is survived by her son, Warren Smith and his wife Anita of Glen Oaks, Queens. She is also survived by granddaughter Judith Dajnowski of Bayside, Queens and a great-granddaughter Catalina Dajnowski also of Bayside, Queens.
