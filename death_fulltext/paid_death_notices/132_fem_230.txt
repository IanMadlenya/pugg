
        SCHEFF-Frances L. 89 years old. On January 25th, 1998. Widow of Samuel Scheff, mother of Trudy Brown and Joan Lipsitz, father-inlaw of Julius Brown and Paul Stiller. Devoted grandmother of Cathy, Susan, Annie, and Jonathan. Great grandmother of Gabby, Zoe, Lucy Kate, Aaron Jeremy, and Zachary. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Emily's List or the Children's Library of Reconstructionist Synagogue of the North Shore.
