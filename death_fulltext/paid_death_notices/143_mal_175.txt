
        COYLE-Philip J. Retired N.Y.P.D. Of Patterson, NY, formerly of NYC, died December 12, 1998, in his 92nd year. For exceptional police duty, he was the third NYC police officer to be honored with the Police Combat Cross. Beloved husband of the late Catherine (nee McConnell). Loving father of Alice McKenna, Geraldine Connelly, Catherine Moylan, Joseph Coyle and Donald Coyle. Adored by twelve grandchildren and 19 greatgrandchildren. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10:45 AM, at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel R.C. Church, Ridgewood, NJ. Interment in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, please donate to your local Police Athletic League. Visiting Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Feeney Funeral Home, Ridgewood, NJ 201-444-7650.
