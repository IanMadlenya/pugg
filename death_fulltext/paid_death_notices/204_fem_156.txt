
        COHEN--Ethel H., on January 15, 2004. Cherished wife, partner and best friend of 59 years to Ted. Loving and devoted mother to Sande (Mitch), Judy (Bengt) and Howard. Adored grandmother to Lisa, Elizabeth and Erik. Beloved sister to Sylvia and the late Mae. Dutiful and devoted daughter. Loving and caring cousin and aunt. Wonderful and tireless friend to everyone in her life. Services Sunday, January 18, 11am, at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road (corner of Middle Neck Road), Great Neck, NY 11023. Contributions in Ethel's memory may be made to the Temple's Capital Campaign Fund at the above address. ''There never was, and there never will be, another like you.'' Watch over us and rest, dearest love, mom, grandma. You will live on inside of us forever.
        COHEN--Ethel. Temple Beth El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its dear longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
