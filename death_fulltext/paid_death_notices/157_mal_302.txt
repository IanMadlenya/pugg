
        JACOBS-Nathan P. Age 91, of NYC, and Palm Beach, FL. Died peacefully in his sleep on February 27, 2000. He is survived by his beloved wife, Lucille (Lucy) and his loving children Carol Ann Leibner, Judith Lynn and Daniel Pace, Alan S. Jacobs and step-daughter Kay Hope Seligson. Cherished grandfather of Matthew, David and Elizabeth Leibner, Michelle Golda and Garrett Charles Pace and great-grandfather of Joseph Daniel Pace. Dear brother of Mildred Lipton, Lillian Ott and Esther Goldberg. He is also survived by many nieces and nephews. Services at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC, Tuesday, at 12:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the Anti-Defamation League.
        JACOBS-Nathan P. We deeply mourn the passing of our dear friend, our ''brother'' and extend our heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife Lucille, his devoted children Carol Ann Leibner, Judith Lynn and Daniel Pace, Alan S. Jacobs, his grandchildren, sisters and extended family. He was a sensitive and generous man devoted to his family and friends, a major contributor to educational, cultural, medical, national and worldwide charities. We will miss him profoundly. May his memory be for a blessing. Simon and Gertrude Kaplan
