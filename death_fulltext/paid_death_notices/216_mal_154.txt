
        SILVER--Dr. George A. Died at home on January 7, 2005 at age 91. He is survived by his beloved wife of 68 years, Mitzi; three children James, Jane & Judith Barrett; brother Paul; sister Mildred Granatir; five grandchildren; four great grandchildren. He spent his life as physician, scholar and dedicated public health advocate. In lieu of flowers donations sent to GW University, School of Public Health, Health Policy Dept. in honor of George A.
