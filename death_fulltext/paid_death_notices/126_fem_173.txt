
        TALLEY-Madelon DeVoe, 65; married to Truman M. Talley in 1953, she is also survived by her brother, Raymond F. DeVoe, Jr., and leaves three children, Mrs. Melanie T. Whatley, Macdonald W. Talley and Marina H. Talley. Born in New York City, Mrs. Talley attended Columbia and graduated from Sarah Lawrence. After raising a family, Mrs. Talley began a highly successful career in the business and financial world, published two books, and has had major board positions in a variety of NY financial institutions. In 1995, Mrs. Talley was appointed by Governor Pataki as the Commissioner of the NY/NJ Port Authority. Visitation will be held on Wednesday, July 23rd, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street from 4-7 P.M. A funeral mass will be held at Saint Ignatius Loyola Church, 84th and Park, on Thursday at 11 A.M. Burial will be private.
        TALLEY-Madelon DeVoe. On July 18, 1997. The Board of Commissioners of The Port Authority of New York and New Jersey expresses its profound sympathy at the untimely death of a cherished colleague and friend. Appointed to the Board in 1995, she brought outstanding intellect, commitment and dignity and worked tirelessly to serve the people of New York and New Jersey. The employees of the Port Authority join us in extending deepest condolences to her entire family. She will be missed by all of us.
