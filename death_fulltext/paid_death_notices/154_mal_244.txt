
        DANKS-Robert Douglas. Died November 18, in Palm Beach, FL, at the age of 97. Founding Partner of Ernst and Co., and former Governor of the New York Stock Exchange. Fomrer resident of Rye, NY. Beloved husband of the late Anne Danks. Survived by his wife Gil and daughter Evelyn Corbetta of Darien, CT. Devoted grandfather of Jane Yezzi, Maryann Cousins, Allison Radin, Robert and Richard Corbetta. Also survived by eight great-grandchildren. Family will receive friends Monday, November 22, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Post Rd., Rye. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10 AM, Resurrection Church, Rye. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
