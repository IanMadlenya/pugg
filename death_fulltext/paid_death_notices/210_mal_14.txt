
        SOFFER -- Robert, 83, of Montclair, NJ, on July 1, 2004. His outstanding artistic sensibility manifested itself in every aspect of his life, including music, photography, and his love of European Culture. He was Professor of German and French at Montclair State University for 24 years. He is survived by his son, Robert Jr. of Bronx, NY. Services today, 10:30 AM, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
