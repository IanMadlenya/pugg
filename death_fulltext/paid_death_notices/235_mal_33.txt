
        SOHLBERG--Russell(aka Eric Russell). 82, died July 27,2006 from complications following a fall. Actor and dancer, was loved in the NYC Downtown modern dance and theater community. He danced with luminaries: Jose Limon, Martha Graham. Born in Schenectady on March 24, 1924 to Eric G. and Margaret T. Sohlberg. Graduated from University of Chicago, a technical appraiser for United Technologies, also a WWII veteran. His wife, Arlene, pre-deceased him. He is survived by brothers, Eric, and Karl, and his son, Gregory.
