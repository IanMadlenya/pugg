
        CASHION-Archibald, H., 89, of Madison, NJ on Oct. 17, 1999. A man loved by family and friends who was a partner of the law firm of Whitman & Ransom. He graduated from Columbia University Law School and was a 50 year resident of Madison. Memorial Services will be held at the Grace Episcopal Church, 4 Madison Avenue, Madison, NJ on Sunday, October 24, 1999 at 2:00 P.M. For information: Wm. A. Bradley, 973-635-2428.
