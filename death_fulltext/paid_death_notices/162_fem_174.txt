
        SANDFORT - Margaret Nelson. Died July 20, 2000 in Connecticut, born in Springfield, MA. in 1912, daughter of the late Grace and Andrew Nelson. Professionally she was known as Margaret D. Nelson. Having a special interest in textiles, she joined the textile firm of Stroheim & Romann in New York as Design Director, becoming the only woman vice president in 1963 and later retiring to Southbury, CT. She leaves a daughter, Linda Sparrow, and son-in-law, Anthony Sparrow of Milford, CT, and family in Charleston, SC and New Castle, NH. A memorial service will be held Saturday, August 19th at 2:00 P.M. at North Congregational Church, Woodbury, CT. Suggested memorials to Pomperaug Woods Endowment Fund, 80 Heritage Road, Southbury, CT 06488; or North Congregational Church, Woodbury, CT. 06798.
