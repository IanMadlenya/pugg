
        JASPER-Julius D., M.D. Suddenly on December 4, 2000. Beloved husband of Lois (nee Guterman), devoted father to Steven and Nancy, Richard and Millie, and Barbara and Stuart Aferiat. Loving grandpa to Robert, Lily, Kyle, Ben, Alex and Corey. Caring brother to Gabriel. Dr. Jasper graduated from the University of Tennessee with an M.D. degree in 1948. He practiced internal medicine in Mount Vernon for 48 years, retiring in 1998. Prior to that, he served as physician in combat with the 3rd Infantry Division during the Korean Conflict. Funeral arrangements are at Riverside Memorial Chapel in Mount Vernon at 12:00 noon, Wednesday, December 6, 2000.
