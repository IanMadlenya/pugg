        $(6$)KERZNER--Howard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the untimely passing of Howard Kerzner, an active leader in the Jewish and business communities whose generosity strengthened our ability to serve those in need in New York and around the world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his father Sol, wife Vanessa, and his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
