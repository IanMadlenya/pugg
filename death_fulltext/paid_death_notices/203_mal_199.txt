
        MILLER--James. On December 14, 2003. Beloved husband of Arlene. Devoted father of Mark and Barbara, and sonin-law John Palefsky. Loving grandfather of Harrison, Susan and Michael. He was appointed a midshipman at the U.S. Naval Academy by President Harry S. Truman and graduated with the class of 1951. After serving on the U.S.S.F.D.R. aircraft carrier, he resigned his commission to enter the business world. He attended Cornell University under the combined business-law program, receiving an M.B.A. in 1958. He was a Fellow of the Life Insurance Management Association and was a member of the New York Society of Security Analysts. He owned businesses, managed property and wrote articles and books on financial matters.
