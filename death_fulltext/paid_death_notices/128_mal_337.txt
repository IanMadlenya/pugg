
        OSTERWEIS-Harry L. 88. Died in NYC September 25, 1997. He was the husband of May Louise Bodenheimer Osterweis. He was the son of the late Rose and Gustav Osterweis. Born February 5, 1909 in New Haven, CT. He graduated Yale College in 1931 and later Yale Law School. He served in the Army Air Force during WW II leaving as Lieutenant Colonel and also served in the Army Intelligence Division. He practiced international patent law working for the international firm of Schwartzkoff Industries. He was a member of the Yale Club of NY. Funeral service will be private and interment in Mishkan Israel Cemetery, New Haven CT.
