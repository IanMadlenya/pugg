
        BURGIE - Page Elizabeth Turner Burgie. Died on June 28, 2003, after a long illness. She was born May 31, 1930, in Richmond, Virginia. She earned a B.A. in English and a Master of Theology at Virginia Union University. In New York, she was the Head Counselor of Girls and Camp Director for the New York City Mission Society's Camp Minisink. In 1956, she married renowned lyricist-composer Irving Louis Burgie Sr. Later, they traveled with their family to Africa, Europe and other lands. She was very active at the Presbyterian Church of Saint Albans, Hollis Presbyterian Church and the Presbytery of New York City. She is survived by her husband of 47 years, two sons, Irving Jr. and Andrew, a granddaughter, Joni, brother Cabell Turner and daughterin-law Patricia, in addition to a host of other loving relatives and friends.
