
        YEO-Kiat Huat, age 50, of Red Bank, New Jersey, died last night at Riverview Medical Center. Dr. Yeo was born in Malaysia and came to the USA to pursue his medical studies. He completed his undergraduate studies at Columbia Union College and the University of Maryland at Baltimore. Dr. Yeo attended medical school at St. George's University School of Medicine, Grenada, West Indies, where he received his Doctor of Medicine in 1983. He completed his post-graduate training in internal medicine as well as a subspecialty fellowship in pulmonary diseases/ critical care at Coney Island Hospital, Brooklyn, New York. Dr. Yeo has been an associate of the Medical Health Center in Middletown since 1995. He was also on staff at Riverview Medical Center, Jersey Shore Medical Center, Monmouth Medical Center, and Bayshore Community Hospital. Dr. Yeo was a compassionate doctor who always had time to sit and talk with his patients and their families. He was a member of the American Medical Association and the American College of Chest Physicians. Dr. Yeo is survived by his wife, Mei; his sister, Mrs. Itwaru; his niece, Vivian and many loving colleagues and friends. The family requests that memorial donations be made in his memory to the American Red Cross September 11th Fund, c/o PO Box 4148, Middletown, NJ 07748.
