
        BIRDSALL-Blair, of Allendale, N.J. formerly of Glen Rock, N.J. on June 25, 1997. Beloved husband of Elizabeth (nee Figueroa). Loving father of Elizabeth J. Evans of Fl., William B. Birdsall of N.J., James B. Birdsall of PA., Maria R. CelisWirth of N.Y., N.Y., Jose R. Celis of N.J., Dr. Rodolfo Celis of GA. Cherished grandfather of 16 grandchildren and one great grandson. Predeceased by his first wife Helen S. Birdsall in 1972. Mr. Birdsall was a member of the American Society of Civil Engineers. An honorary member of the American Society of Senior Wire Rope Engineers and a member of the Princeton Club. He was a self employed Civil Engineer wtih Steinman, Boynton, Gronquist, Birdsall in N.Y., N.Y. Birdsall who was bestowed the title ''Mr. Bridges,'' had just observed his 90th birthday on May 21. His career spanned more than 70 years at all levels of structural engineering. He was most recently serving as a consultant for the bridge engineering firm Steinman on the world-record Great Belt East Bridge in Denmark. A 1929 Civil Engineering graduate from Princeton University, Birdsall had an illustrious career highlighted by his work on the Golden Gate Bridge, Forth Road Bridge, Chesapeake Bay Bridge, and the Great Belt. Friends will be received at the funeral home on Friday from 2-4 & 6-9 PM.
