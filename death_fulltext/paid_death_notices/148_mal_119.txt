
        GRANADY-Dr. Alwyn Hershaw, a 35-year resident of New Rochelle, N.Y., passed away Sunday, May 9, at New York Presbyterian Hospital. He was 74 yrs old. After serving as a sergeant in the United States Army during World War II, Dr. Granady completed his undergraduate and medical education at Howard University in Washington, D.C. He served as an attending physician at Harlem Hospital and internist in the office he once shared with his father at 2588 Seventh Avenue for 37 years before retiring in 1995. A devoted husband, father, brother and friend, Dr. Alwyn H. Granady is survived by his loving wife of forty years, Mercedes; daughters, Dr. Lynelle Cecile Granady of New York, N.Y., and Lynda Ruth Granady of Washington, D.C., his brother, James T.W. Granady, Jr. of New York, N.Y., as well as several cousins and faithful friends. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, May 13, at 11 A.M., at St. Martin's Episcopal Church, 230 Lenox Avenue, New York, New York.
