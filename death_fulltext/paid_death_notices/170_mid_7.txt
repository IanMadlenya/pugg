
        KAPLAN-Dorothy. 94, on March 8, 2001, in Mamaroneck, NY. Beloved wife of the late Samuel W. Kaplan. Loving mother of Jack and Sandra Kaplan. Devoted grandmother of Steven Kaplan and his wife Barbara and Cynthia Kaplan and her husband David Froelich and great-grandmother of Sarah and Lillian Kaplan and John Froelich. Interment at Roosevelt Cemetery, Philadelphia on March 11, 2001.
