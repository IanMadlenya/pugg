
        HOSCHANDER-Emanuel. Passed away on December 23. Son of the late Professor Jacob and Bertha Hoschander. Beloved husband of Rhoda (Arond). Adored father of Michal and Ronald Malen. Loving grandfather of Susannah, Abigail and Yacov Malen. Devoted brother of Grace Gerenstein. A man of integrity and dignity, and devotion to family and Judaism. In his unassuming way he enriched the lives of many. May his memory be for a blessing.
