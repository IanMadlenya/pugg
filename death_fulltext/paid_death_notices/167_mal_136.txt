
        COCHRAN-Thomas P. 86, of Erie, PA and formerly of NYC, died December 7, 2000. Preceded in death by brothers Paul B., Joseph L., Col. Phillip G. and John M. Survived by many nieces and nephews. Served as an associate producer for Jack Paar with the Tonight Show and later on the David Frost Show. Received three Emmys for his work in radio and TV. Memorial Mass Tuesday, December 12, 11 AM, St. Jude RC Church, Erie. Memorials may be made to Cochran Memorial Scholarship Fund, Gannon University, 109 University Square, Erie, PA 16541, or to a charity of one's choice. For information contact Quinn Funeral Home, Erie.
