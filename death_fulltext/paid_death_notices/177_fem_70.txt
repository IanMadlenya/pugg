
        SCHLANGER-Joan. Age 64. Died at home late on October 8, 2001, of colon cancer. Survived by her husband Michael; her mother Shirley; her children Steven, Margo, Karen & Daniel; her grandchildren Harry and Leila; and her brother and sister Robert and Arlene. Her good counsel and humor lit our days and we will sorely miss her. Service Wednesday, 12:45 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Sierra Club, 85 2nd Street, San Francisco, CA 94105.
