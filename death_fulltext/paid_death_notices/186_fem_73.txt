
        BISGYER-Pauline Steckler (nee Brenner). On July 12, 2002, of Hewlett, NY and Delray Beach, FL. Graduate of New York University. Loving wife of Melville L. for 40 years and the late William Steckler. She is survived by her adored and adoring children, Margery Gogel (William) and Robert M. Steckler, M.D. (Debbie) and her grandchildren and great-grandchildren, Bruce and Debbie, Amy and Michael, Brian and Stacy, Nancy, Gregg, Sophia, Daniel and Alexandra. She was predeceased by her brothers, Pfc. Monte Brenner and Dr. Seymour Brenner. Funeral services graveside at New Mount Carmel Cemetery, Sunday, July 14, 2002 at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions to Schepens Eye Research Institute, 20 Staniford St., Boston, MA 02114. Mom always hated saying goodbye: we do too.
