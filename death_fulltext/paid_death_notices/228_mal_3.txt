
        HUTCHISON -- William Robert. Of Cambridge, MA, died on December 16, 2005 at the age of 75. Dr. Hutchison taught American religious history at Harvard Divinity School for 32 years. He leaves his wife Virginia (Quay), a sister Mary Fletcher, 4 children, and ten grandchildren. Services will be held at Cambridge Friends Meeting on January 21 and at Memorial Church at Harvard on April 28. Donations may be made to the William R. Hutchison Fund at Harvard Divinity School.
