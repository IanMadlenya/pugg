
        THRALL-Donald Stuart. 82. Longtime resident of Plymouth, MI, and NYC, Guggenheim Fellow, art professor, painter of numerous modern works, including ''Alhambra,'' world traveler and author of ''Spanish Journal.'' A dear friend and gentle person died on January 15, 2000, in NY. Viewing at Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 117 West 72 St., Monday, 2-7. Memorial service at his home January 23, 2 PM.
