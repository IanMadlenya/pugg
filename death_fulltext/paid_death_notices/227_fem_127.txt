
        BAIG--Shirley. Born in 1921 in Canada she lived most of her life in the New York metropolitan area. Shirley passed away on Sunday, December 18 in Santa Monica, California. She is survived by her sister Dell Looney, her nieces Karen Byrd and Anne Greene and her nephew David Brown. Shirley worked for 40 years for the New York Times until she retired in 1986. Her titles ranged from Manager of News Services to head of photo operations at various points. She set a great example for women in the workforce in general and specifically for those coming to work at the New York Times. She obtained her BA from Fordham University in 1973. She became a bridge player late in life continuing her quest for intellectual challenges until the end. She will be greatly missed by all who knew her.
