
        SHENGHIT-Betty, February 9, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Max Shenghit. Loving mother of Cliff and Susan and her husband Arthur Bookbinder. Dear grandmother of Jeffrey and Seth. Devoted sister of the late Nat Dobkin, the late Lou Dobkin, the late Mike Dobkin and the late Rose Shapiro. She was an elegant lady who will be deeply missed. Service Wednesday, February 10, 1999, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
