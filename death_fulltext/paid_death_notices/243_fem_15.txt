
        REILLEY--Elizabeth Kals. The Board, staff, and friends of The New York Botanical Garden are deeply saddened by the loss of Elizabeth Kals Reilley, garden lover, bibliophile, and librarian. She donated to the Botanical Garden her collection of more than 400 of the most important publications on European landscape design from the late Renaissance on. Featured in a 2003 special exhibition, European Pleasure Gardens, the Elizabeth Kals Reilley Collection continues to be available in the Gardens LuEsther T. Mertz Library for pleasure and study. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Wilson Nolen, Chairman of the Board Gregory Long, President Susan Fraser, Director, LuEsther T. Mertz Library
