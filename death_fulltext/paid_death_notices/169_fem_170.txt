
        BREWSTER-Marion D. On February 15, 2001 at age 100. Beloved wife of the late Warren. Mother of Isabel and Vance Van Dine, Thomas S. and Mary, Samuel D. and Eliza and Richard W. and Barbara Brewster. Grandmother of Sylvia and Dale Brewster-O'Connor, Scott, Maxwell, Sophia, Darrah Brewster and the late Rose Van Dine. Sister of the late Jean Thomas. Funeral service at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 5th Avenue at 90th Street, on Tuesday at 12 Noon. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Ladies Christian Union, 118 West 13 Street, New York City 10011.
