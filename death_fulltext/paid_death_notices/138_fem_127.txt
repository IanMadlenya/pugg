
        BUTLER-Bernice (nee Healy). Died on July 18, 1998. Beloved wife of the late John A. Butler. Survived by brother Harold J. Healy and sister-in-law Judith S. Healy. A native New Yorker and longtime resident of Fort Lee, NJ, and Washington Heights, NY. In her lifetime she held executive positions with the law firm of Donovan, Leisure, Newton & Lombard and the Mennan Oil Co. Mass of the Resurrection at 12 noon on Tuesday, July 21, at the Chapel of Our Lady Queen of Peace of Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Interment to follow. For further information contact Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, Madison Ave at 81st St.
