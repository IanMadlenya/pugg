
        SAUTER-Lilianna, MD. Died Mar. 28th, 1998. She was a dedicated physician and talented dermatologist, Associate Professor at Mount Sinai Medical School, Life Fellow of the New York Academy of Medicine. Memorial service will be held Friday, April 3 at noon in the second floor chapel, Guggenheim Pavilion, Mount Sinai Hospital, 101 Street and Fifth Avenue.
