
        VALENCY-Janet. On April 7th at age 91 after a long illness. Widow of playwright & Columbia University Emeritus Professor Maurice Valency, she was a distinguished designer & head of her own studio for many years. A woman of great style, vivacity, and charm, she will be missed by her many friends in NYC, Grenada, & North Salem. Survived by her brother John Cornell and sister-in-law Suzie of West Chester, PA, her niece Diane Cornell of Washington, DC, and her nephew Dr. Peter Cornell of Los Angeles, CA. No service at her request. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Art Students League.
