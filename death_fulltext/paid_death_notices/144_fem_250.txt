
        BURDMAN-Sylvia. On January 21, 1999. Beloved wife of Murray Burdman, devoted and loving mother of Meryl Levenson & Joan Josephs, loving sister of Shelley Goren, cherished grandmother of Jonathan, Glenn, Erin, and Brittany, great-grandmother of Zack. She had a deep spiritual committmemt to family, she will be greatly missed, and forever in our hearts. Our lives were blessed to have such an extraordinary mother whose love, devotion, support, had no bounds. Graveside service Sunday 12:30 at Beth Moses.
        BURDMAN-Sylvia. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved sister of its esteemed Honorary Chairman, Mr. Shelley Goren, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi, Hermann Merkin, Founding Pres., David Yagoda, Chairman, Dr. William Schwartz, President
