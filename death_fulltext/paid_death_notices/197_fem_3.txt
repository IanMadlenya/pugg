
        CHAINEY -- Dolores. The Lindsay Park Board of Directors, Management, Maintenance and Security mourn the loss of our colleague and friend, Dolores Chainey. Her tireless leadership of Lindsay Park for more than 30 years has preserved the quality and spirit of cooperative living at Lindsay Park for generations to come. She is remembered with admiration and love. Reposing at B. Anastasio and Son, Inc., Funeral Home, 533 Lorimer St., Brooklyn. Visiting Sunday and Monday, 2-5 PM and 7-9:30 PM.
