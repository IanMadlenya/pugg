
        CIPRUT-Solomon, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center is deeply saddened by the untimely death of Dr. Solomon Ciprut. Dr. Ciprut was associated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Surgery for over 40 years. He was a staunch supporter of the Medical Staff and played a very active part in its leadership, serving as President from 1973 to 1974. He was loved and respected by all who knew him. He will be sorely missed by everyone. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. As per the family's wishes, donations to the Solomon Ciprut, M.D. Memorial Fund at Maimonides Medical Center can be sent to the Medical Staff Office. Warren Wexelman, M.D., Pres. Jacob Shani, M.D., Pres. Elect David Feldman, M.D. Secretary Salvatore DeGliuomini, M.D., Treasurer
