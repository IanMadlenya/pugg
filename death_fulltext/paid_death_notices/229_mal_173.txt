
        RISLEY--William S. Educational consultant and longtime resident of Washington, CT. Died February 12 of postoperative complications at Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital in New York. Beloved husband of Jane, father of Mandy and Emily, stepfather of John and grandfather of Julia. He is deeply mourned as well by his stepmother, Virginia Risley deCourcy of Washington; brother John; sister Mary; and stepbrothers, Dr. Charles de Sieyes and David de Sieyes and a host of friends in Litchfield County, CT. A memorial service will be conducted Saturday at 11 A.M. in the Washington Club Hall on Route 47 in Washington. The family requests that remembrances be made in the form of contributions to the Steep Rock Association, Inc., the land trust, P.O. Box 279, Washington, CT 06794.
