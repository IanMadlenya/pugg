
        MOTT-Basil J.F., PhD. Dean Emeritus of the School of Health and Human Services at the University of New Hampshire, died at his home in Durham, NH. He was 78 years old. Dr. Mott graduated from Amherst College and received his doctorate from Harvard. Mott spent 40 years studying, teaching, and writing about the politics of health care. Mott directed a study of the NY State Coordinating Commission for Inter-Departmental Health and Hospital issues and produced a book entitled ''Anatomy of A Coordinating Council''. Dr. Mott leaves his wife Ruth, a sister, three children, and two granddaughters.
