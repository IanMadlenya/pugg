
        WALL-Virginia Gardner Fincke. Born in Boston, MA. October 28, 1905. Died at home March 11, 1999. Widow of Albert Carey Wall. Resident of New York City and Fishers Island, N.Y. She is survived by two daughters, Virginia Fincke Thors, Maria Wall Patterson, and six grandchildren. Funeral services to be held Tuesday, March 16th, 11AM at Saint James' Episcopal Church, Madison Ave at 72 St, NY, NY 10021. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Saint James' Church or the Shirley-Eustis House, 33 Shirley St, Boston, MA. 02119 would be appreciated.
