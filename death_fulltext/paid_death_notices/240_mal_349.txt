
        McCARTHY--Thomas H., III, DSA -Kane Lodge No. 454 F. & A. M. mourns with profound sorrow the loss of our esteemed brother on January 20. He was a member of the Lodge for 26 years and recipient of the Dedicated Service Award in 1996. He served on the Relief Committee for a number of years and shall be greatly missed. Paul D. Reitz, Secretary Hayrettin Kilic, Master
