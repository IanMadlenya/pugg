
        LANDER-Margaret Slade. On Wednesday, January 8, at her home in Manhattan, at the age of 89. Former head of the Personal Shopping Department and the Control Center at Macy's, in her retirement active quiltmaker and member of Empire Quilters. Widow of William Hall Lander of New York City; mother of Louise Lander of Guanajuato, Mexico, Patricia Lander of Nanuet, N.Y. and Slade Lander of Toronto; grandmother of Alison Elizabeth Lander Alpern, Adam Lander Alpern and Bryn Lander. Interment at TrinityChurch Cemetery will be private.
