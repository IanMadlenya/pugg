
        LONDON-Frances A., originally of Butler, PA, on September 25, 1999, at the age of 86. Beloved wife of the late Robert London. Loving mother of the late Roy London. Devoted teacher of many children at J.H.S. 54 in Manhattan until her retirement. Sister of the late Abe Abes. She had a great sense of humor and a remarkable zest for life, navigating her way through New York City like a native daughter, almost right up to the end. She died with great grace and courage. She is survived her brother, Harry Abes, and her loving son, Charles (Chuck) London. She will be sorely missed. A private memorial is being planned. For details call 718-399-6916.
