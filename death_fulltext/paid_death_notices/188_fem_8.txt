
        PRINCE-Cecelia, on August 28, 2002, at age 102, in Fort Lauderdale, Fla. Dear wife of the late Joseph E. Prince. Beloved mother of Dr. Ivin B. Prince and Suzanne Prince Benjamin. Cherished grandmother, great- and greatgreat-grandmother. Loving sister of Blanche Taxier and Arthur C. Bittker. Devoted daughter of Eva and Isaac Bittker of NYC. She lived with love for others, always finding the goodness in people. She lived with a closeness to God. May she rest in peace. Interment Sunday, September 1, 2002, 11 AM, at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
