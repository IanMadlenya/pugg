
        EISENBERG-David Abram. Suddenly on April 3, 1997 in Los Angeles. Deeply loved by family, Melvin, Helen and sister Bronwyn Bonnie, Grandmother Laura, and many friends. Practiced law for five and one half years in L.A. Graduate of U.C.L.A. Film School and University of California, Berkeley Law School, Boalt Hall. Widely travelled throughout the world, he had interests in electronics, film, and music. He was an accomplished writer. He will be sorely missed and long remembered by all who knew him. Burial was on April 11, 1997. Home of Eternity, Oakland, CA.
