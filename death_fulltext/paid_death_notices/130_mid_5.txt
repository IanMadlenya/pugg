
        SEYMOUR-Abby Lewis. Actress, active in radio, television and theatre for sixty-five years, widow of John Davenport Seymour, also of the theatre, died November 27, 1997. She is survived by his son Peter Seymour and his daughter Katherine Hollingshead and seven grandchildren. Also surviving her are her sisters Beulah L. Smith, Fredrica L. Yates, Sue L. Wimberly and brother Hunter W. Lewis. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Saturday from 4-7 PM and Sunday 2-4 P.M. Interment to follow in New Mexico.
