
        O'NEILL--Leo C. The board and staff of Scenic Hudson grieve the loss of a passionate, committed board member and dear friend. Leo C. O'Neill has inspired and benefited our work to enhance the environmental and economic health of the Hudson River Valley through his deep conceptual intelligence, decisive leadership, business acumen, compassion and humor. The communities in the Valley and those of us who worked with him will long benefit from the clarity of his thinking and the warmth that informed his guidance. We will miss him deeply and offer heartfelt condolences to his family. Marjorie L. Hart, chair Ned Sullivan, president
