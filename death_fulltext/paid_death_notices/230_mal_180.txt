
        MAXWELL--Herbert. Accomplished businessman and entrepreneur, died Sunday evening of leukemia. He was 84. Following his honorable service in the Air Force during WWII, Mr. Maxwell worked successfully in the radio business and subsequently in the garment industry before establishing himself as a workout specialist for bankrupt publicly traded companies. Mr. Maxwell worked extensively in that field until shortly before his death. He is survived by Phyllis, his beloved wife of 58 years; his daughter Victoria and her husband William Plon; his son Rick and his wife Danae; his son Mitchell and three grandchildren, Zachary, Tia and Charlotte. Funeral services are scheduled for Wednesday, March 15, 2006 at 10:30AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue.
