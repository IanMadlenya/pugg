
        NEMERSON-Roy E. A screenwriter with an unstoppable sense of humor, died of cancer on June 25th in Los Angeles. His friendship, as well as his wit and one-liners, will be missed greatly. A caring son, a loving brother and uncle, an unabashedly sentimental pet lover, and an avid softball player, he has left the world a better place for having been here. His friends mourn his passing.
