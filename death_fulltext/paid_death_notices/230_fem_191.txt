
        FRISCH--Jean. On March 21, 2006. Beloved wife of Irwin J. (Mike) Frisch. Devoted mother of Barbara, Adrienne and Richard; cherished grandmother of Jeremy, Jesse, Andrew, Jonathan and Katherine. An active community member of Wynmoor serving as Director for 17 years, President of Antigua II for 13 years, Council Rep for eight years, and as a member of various committees; involved herself in all aspects of community life. Other previous service included Hadassah's Woman of the Year and typing braille for the blind. She will be dearly missed. Funeral services will be held Friday, March 24 at Star of David Chapel in North Lauderdale, FL. at 11:45 AM.
