
        MOROWITZ-Morris. The YIVO Institute for Jewish Research mourns the passing of Morris Morowitz, lifelong member of the Workmen's Circle, and father of Jacob Morowitz, devoted member of the YIVO National Board of Directors, Co-Chair of the National Program Committee & President of the Chicago YIVO Society. May his memory be for a blessing. Bruce Slovin, Chairman Dr. Carl J. Rheins, Exec. Dir.
