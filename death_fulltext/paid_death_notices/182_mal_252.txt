
        HATCH-Peter. Of NYC, died March 3, 2002 at the age of 80. A funeral service was held on March 7 at the First Presbyterian Church, New York City. Burial was at the Cedar Hill Cemetery, Hartford, CT. He is fondly remembered by his wife of forty years, Jane Maddox Hatch, a brother, Christopher of Dorset, Vermont, and a niece and nephew, Carol Cidney Hatch and James Hatch, both of NYC.
