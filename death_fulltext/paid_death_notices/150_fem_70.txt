
        BALSAM-Rita. On behalf of the Partners and Associates of Loeb & Troper we are saddened to learn of the loss of Rita Balsam, beloved mother of our Executive Partner, Arnold Balsam. We extend sincere condolences to Arnold, his brothers, Philip and Howard, and the entire family. Rita Balsam was a devoted mother, grandmother and great-grandmother who took pride in transmitting to her family, her deep values of tradition and heritage. She will be forever remembered for her special traits. May the family be granted comfort and consolation with all of those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Loeb & Troper
