
        MALLORY-Margaret P. On November 3, 1998, age 87 in Santa Barbara, CA. Beloved sister of Clifford D. Mallory and Barbara Hathaway. Her love and knowledge of art and music touched many lives as did her generosity and energy which she devoted to many worthwhile causes. A celebration of her life and accomplishments will be held in Santa Barbara in January. Friends may remember the University of California at Santa Barbara Art Museum, Music Academy of the West, Mystic Seaport Museum, or the Santa Barbara Art Museum.
