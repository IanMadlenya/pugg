
        BENNETT--John J., Jr. On April 22, 2005. John was a lifelong resident of Brooklyn, graduate of Xavier High School (1944), Fordham University (1950), and Columbia Law School (1953). A US Navy veteran of WWII and member of the Catholic War Veterans. He was past president of the Catholic Child Care Society and St. John's Residence for Boys. John also was a Board member of the Bay Ridge Day Nursery, St. Giles Foundation and Lutheran Medical Center. A devoted family man, he was an active member of St. Anselm's Parish for 53 years, member of the Parish Youth Council, the Holy Name Society, the Finance Committee and Trustee of the Parish. He was a member of the Knights of Columbus and the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick of Brooklyn. With his wife Angela, he was received into the Order of the Holy Sepulchre of Jerusalem. He was a partner in the firm of Baar, Bennett & Fullen for over 25 years; currently of counsel with Brauner, Baron, Rosenzweig & Klein, LLP. Beloved husband of Angela (nee Amorosi) for 53 years. Loving father of John, Leo, Thomas (Jacqueline), Carolyn (Martin Clark) and Jeanne, and grandfather of Gavin. Cherished brother of Mary Lou Reese, Joan Carey and Evelyn Vitale. Dear son of the late Evelyn (nee Cogan) and John J. Benett, New York State Attorney General (1932-1944). Visiting Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, at Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 4th Ave, Bklyn. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Anselm's RC Church, Tuesday, 10 AM. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Anselm's RC Church.
