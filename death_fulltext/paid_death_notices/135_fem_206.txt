
        ABRAMSON-Dr. Belle. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Dr. Belle Abramson. A dedicated supporter of UJA-Federation, Belle served with distinction on the board of directors of Surprise Lake Camp, one of our member agencies, and on the board of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies, a predecessor organization. We extend our deepest sympathies to her entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
