
        GENUNG-Alfred Gawthrop. Died October 24, 2001, in Southbury, CT. He was 92. A retired banker, he lived most of his life in Plainfield, NJ, and Princeton, NJ, where he was active in numerous civic affairs. He was predeceased in 1980 by his wife of 43 years, Dorothy Madsen Genung. His second marriage ended in divorce. He graduated from Philips Exeter Academy and Princeton University. He is survived by a daughter, Sarah Genung Booth of Westville, NJ, a son, Frederick M. Genung II of Woodbury, CT, and 4 grandchildren. A memorial service will take place at 11:30 AM, Monday, October 29, at The Crescent Avenue Prestyberian Church, 716 Watchung Avenue, Plainfield, NJ, 07060. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to Crescent Avenue Church.
