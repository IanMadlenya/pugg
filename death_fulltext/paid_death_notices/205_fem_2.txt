
        EIGERMAN--Leah G. Died peacefully at 95 on January 23, 2004. Beloved by the family she created and numerous colleagues and friends. She had a full and honored teaching career and in retirement kept on at the Institute of Retired Professionals of The New School. Wife for 64 years of the late Hyman, she leaves her brother Joseph Gordon, her sons Daniel and Joel, daughters Sarah Jane, granddaughter Hannah, grandsons Nathan, Edward and Jared and Elizabeth, Amelia, Samuel, James and Rory, the great-grandchildren who delighted her last years.
