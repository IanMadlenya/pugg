
        SHERMAN-Irving B., 96. On March 28th, 2001, in Los Angeles, California. A New York resident for 93 years, he was a senior executive for several firms spanning the furniture (Ludwig Baumann), restaurant and wine industries, &, most recently, the Greater NY Home Furnishings Association. He survived beloved wives Fannie Rudnick Sherman and June Morrison Sherman, children Roger Sherman and Marian Sherman Lurie, and is survived by five grandchildren and five great grandchildren, all of whom have treasured & will deeply miss him. Burial services: Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, New York, 11 A.M. Sunday, April 1st. Contributions: Anti-Defamation League of B'nai Brith, 823 UN Plaza, NY, NY 10017. For information 212-490-2525.
