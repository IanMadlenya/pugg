
        BRUNNER-Freda (nee Debold). Born in New York City, September 30, 1899; died February 4, 2000 in Pompano Beach, FL at age 100having lived in 3 centuries. She formerly resided in Forest Hills, NY. She was preceded in death by her husband of 57 years, August Brunner, on March 12, 1980. Surviving is her daughter, Bernice Brunner DeBragga of Greensboro, NC; sister, Elsie Debold Barth of Rego Park, NY; four grandsons and five greatgrandchildren. Interment will take place at a later date in Mt. Olivet Cemetery, Maspeth, NY.
