
        HOWELL-Alleyne Carleton Jr., age 85, of Fairfield, husband of Jean Field Howell, died Tuesday, September 16, 1997, in Bridgeport Hospital of heart failure. Mr. Howell lived on Greenfield Hill in Fairfield. He founded and until recently managed the Howell Corporation, Stratford, which designs and manufactures electric systems for application in the United States and abroad. He attended Saint Paul's School in Concord, New Hampshire, and graduated from Yale University in 1934. He also received a degree from the University of Virginia Law School. He served in the U.S. Army during World War II achieving the rank of Captain. In addition to his wife he is survived by a son, Alleyne C. Howell III of Vershire, Vermont; a step daughter, Beverly Borger of Jacksonville, Florida; a brother, John Howell of Greenwich; and two grandchildren.
