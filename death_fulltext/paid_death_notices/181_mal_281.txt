
        GREENBERG-Dr. Reuben. 91, died Sunday, February 24, after a short illness. Our hearts are broken for the loss of this kind and gentle soul devoting 50 years of his life to patients in his medical practice and who served as a military surgeon during World War II. He is survived by children Lynda, Dan, Mark, Faye, grandchildren Anna and Isabel and a sister and brother. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Margaret in 1976. Services at The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam, Tuesday, February 26, at 9:45 AM.
