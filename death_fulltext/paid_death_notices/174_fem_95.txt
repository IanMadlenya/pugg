
        STERN-Carol G. Of Hollywood, FL. Passed away on Tuesday, July 10, 2001. She was born in New Haven, CT and resided for many years in Greensboro, N.C., until the death of her husband, Henry J. Lavietes. She then married the late Julian Stern and moved to Hollywood, Florida. She was active in many charitable organizations. She will be remembered as a woman of remarkable beauty, grace, taste, and graciousness by all of those who were fortunate enough to know her. She leaves behind family, friends and most of all, admirers. We miss her already. Funeral arrangements entrusted to: Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 2230 Hollywood Blvd, Hollywood, FL. 33020 954-921-1776.
        STERN-Carol. The Board of Governors and the Members of Glen Oaks Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of Carol, the wife of our former esteemed member Julian Stern, and extend their heartfelt sympathy to her family. Board of Governors Lowell Kwiat, President
