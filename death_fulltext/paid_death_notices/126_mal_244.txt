
        SILBERBLATT-Dr. John M., 88, on July 22, 1997. Beloved husband of Barrie Berns Silberblatt. Retired surgeon, Bellevue Hospital, 1979. Continued practicing until his death at East Harlem and Riverside Clinics in Manhattan. Graduate NYU Medical School, 1936. Former Major, U.S. Army World War II, Fellow American College of Surgeons, American Urological Association, American Board of Urology. Affiliated with LI Hospital. Active member City Athletic Club. Viewing Wednesday, 7-9PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel. Funeral services Thursday, Noon, at Riverside Chapel.
        SILBERBLATT-John, M.D. On July 22, 1997, physician for New York City clinics, beloved husband of Barrie Berns-Silberblatt, father, stepfather, grandfather. Services on Thursday, at 12 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Visiting hours on Wednesday, 7-9 P.M.
        SILBERBLATT-John, M.D. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Dr. John Silberblatt. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        SILBERBLATT-Dr. John. The Board of 885 Park Avenue Corp mourns the loss of its esteemed and dedicated colleague, Dr. John Siberblatt. We will miss his wise counsel. Our most sincere condolences to his wife, Barrie, and his family.
        SILBERBLATT-John. The Members of the City Athletic Club mourn the loss of its esteemed member, Dr. John Silberblatt, and wish to extend heartfelt sympathy to his family. The Board of Governors
