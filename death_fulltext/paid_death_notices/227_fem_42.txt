
        HARRINGTON--Peggy. Cultural coordinator for the Interfaith Center of New York, formerly at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine. A warm friend and mentor to many, beloved mother, lover of life and the arts, we will miss her terribly. An exceptionally brave, original and independent woman, she was an inspiration. The memorial service will be held December 18, 2PM at St. Mary's Church, 521 West 126 St, NYC.
