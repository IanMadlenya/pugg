
        SASLOW--Max S., 91, longtime resident of Palm Beach, FL., formerly of Riverdale, NY, died January 16, 2005. Predeceased by his wife, Ethel, on November 19, 2004. Surviving are son, Carl, his wife, Joan; granddaughters, Rebecca and Caryn; sister, Ruth, and brother-in-law, Sol Kroll; brother, Seymour, and sister-in-law, Marjorie, and many nephews and nieces. Long interested in public service, served in U.S. Army Air Corps during World War II, assistant personnel director of the City of New York, Riverdale Man of the Year, active in city planning and other community projects. Deeply loved and respected by all who knew him.
