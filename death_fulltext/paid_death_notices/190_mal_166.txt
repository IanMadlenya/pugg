        BRAHAM-Dr. Robert L. The faculty of the Department of Medicine at New York Presbyterian Hospital and the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons mourns the sudden death of Dr. Robert Braham. He held the position of Clinical Professor of Medicine and Director of the Primary Care Internal Medicine Residency. He shared his extensive experience in clinical medicine and in the training of medical students and resident physicians with us over the past two years. We will miss his dedication, energy, and compassion. We extend to his family our sincere condolences. Contributions in his name may be directed to the Visiting Nurse Service, or the Red Cross.
