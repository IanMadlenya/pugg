
        KAMMANN-Karl J., Jr., of Carlisle, MA, formerly of Manhattan, died June 27. Floral designer, former owner of Trepel's in Rockefeller Center. Husband of the late Edith E. (Tenney). Father of Edith E. McEvoy, Katherine M. Kelly, Elizabeth A. Murphy and Karl J. III. Brother of Richard W. Many grandchildren and great grandchildren. Funeral 11 AM Wednesday, June 30, at St. Irene's Church, Carlisle, MA. Visitation prior to Mass in Joseph Dee & Son Funeral Home, Concord, MA. Contributions in his memory to Emerson Hospital Hospice Program, ORNAC, Concord, MA 01742 or to your local library for a horticultural book.
