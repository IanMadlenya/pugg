
        JOHNSON--Albert Edward, Ph.D. Weill Cornell Medical College of Cornell University mourns the passing of our dear friend, Dr. Albert Edward Johnson, beloved husband of Dr. Madelyn M. Antoncic, our esteemed Overseer. Together with Madelyn, Dr. Johnson was a steadfast supporter of the Medical College, and his outstanding commitment to furthering advances in education, research and treatment in the area of lung cancer will forever be remembered at Weill Cornell. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Madelyn and the entire Johnson family. Sanford I. Weill Chairman Board of Overseers Dr. Antonio M. Gotto, Jr. Dean Weill Cornell Medical College
