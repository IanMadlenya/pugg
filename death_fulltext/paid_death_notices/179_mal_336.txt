
        LEBOW-Sylvan, December 25, 2001. Beloved husband of Ruth for 69 years, loving father of Mark and his wife Patricia E. Harris and Joy and her husband Dr. William Cohen. Proud grandfather of Michael, Lauren, Jeffrey and Alexandra. Loyal brother of Alan and Stanleigh. He was a dedicated advocate for the National Federation of Temple Brotherhood, Jewish Chatauqua Society, Zionist Organization of America and the National Conference of Christians and Jews. Services Friday, December 28, 2001, 11AM at Temple EmanuEl, 5th Avenue at 65th Street.
        LEBOW - Sylvan. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with sorrow the death of Sylvan Lebow, a faithful and longtime member of our temple. Mr. Lebow, in his capacity as Executive Director of the National Federation of Temple Brotherhoods, served reform Judaism with unique wisdom and inspirational creativity. With his family and loved ones we mourn his passing, assured that his memory will endure as a cherished legacy. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Senior Rabbi Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        LEBOW-Sylvan. The National Board of Directors and membership of American Red Magen David for Israel record with profound sorrow the passing of Sylvan Lebow, the beloved father of Mark Lebow, ARMDI National President. We extend heartfelt sympathy to Mark and his wife Patricia E. Harris, and his mother Ruth, and all members of the bereaved family. May the memory of his life be a blessing to the family. Robert L. Sadoff, M.D., National Chairman Benjamin Saxe, Executive Vice President
