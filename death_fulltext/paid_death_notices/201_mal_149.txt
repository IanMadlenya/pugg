
        SAPHIER--Peter, 89, of Great Neck, NY. on October 14, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Beatrice Saphier. Brother to Helen. He is survived by his loving life partner, Nancy Stohn. He was the loving father to Michael and Stewart and daughters-in-law Ruthann and Jane. Adored grandfather to David, Adam, Elisa and Brian and grand-daughter-in-law Jenny. He was a partner in the accountancy firm of Turetsky and Saphier in New York City. He was a member of the Glen Oaks Country Club in Old Westbury, L.I. and of Temple Israel of Great Neck. Services on Sunday, October 19, 2003 at 2:00 PM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Interment at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. Memorial contributions may be sent to American Friends of the Alyn Hospital, 51 E. 42 St., Suite 308, New York, NY. 10017.
        SAPHIER--Peter. It is with great sorrow that we mourn the loss of our dear friend, Peter Saphier, whose generosity to the handicapped children of Alyn Hospital in Jerusalem, Israel, made all the difference. We send our sympathies to his loving family, friends and life partner. His love of children survives him. Minette Brown, President American Friends of Alyn Hospital Cathy Lanyard Executive Director The Board of Trustees
