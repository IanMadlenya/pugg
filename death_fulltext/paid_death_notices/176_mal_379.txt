
        HESS-Nathaniel E. American Friends of Neot Kedumim, the biblical landscape reserve in Israel, mourn the loss of our Board Chairman, one of our Founders, and long-term past President. His love of Israel and his horticultural expertise inspired his strong support. Nogah Hareuveni, Founder of Neot Kedumim, Helen Frenkley, Director, and we join the family and other friends in sorrow.
