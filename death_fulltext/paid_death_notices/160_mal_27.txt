
        BARNES-Robert McKinny, 79, in East Hampton, NY. He is survived by his four sons, Michael, of Sierra Vista, Arizona, Rush, of New York, NY, Robert, Jr., of Easthampton, NY, and Alexander, of Seoul, South Korea. He is also survived by four grandchildren, and five great grandchildren. The family will receive friends Wednesday from 7 to 9 P.M. at Williams Funeral Home, Newtown Lane, Easthampton. A Memorial Mass will be offered at the Most Holy Trinity Church, Buell Lane, Easthampton, 11 A.M. Thursday.
