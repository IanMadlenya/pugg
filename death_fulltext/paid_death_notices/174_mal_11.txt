
        REISMAN-Dr. Otto I., Professor of physics at New Jersey Institute of Technology. Born in Vienna, Austria, 1928, he fled to Italy, where he lived through the early 1950's, when he moved to New York City. He was survived by his beloved wife, Eva, of 44 years. Also the devoted father of Deborah Weiss and Edith Reisman. Loving brother of Ines Rosner. Services were held Wednesday, June 27, 2001. Shiva at the Weiss residence through Monday.
