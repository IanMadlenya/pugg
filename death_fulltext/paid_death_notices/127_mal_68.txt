
        GENAUER-Rabbi Sam. On August 5, 1997. In his 91st year, after a life of goodness, devotion to family and community, and learning and teaching Torah. Beloved husband of Sylvia Genauer. Loving father of Judy and Irv Brickman, Blu and Yitz Greenberg, Rena and Shelly Schlaff. Devoted brother of Ben, Mendel and Jack Genauer and the late Isaac and Zvi Hirsch Genauer. Cherished grandfather of Sandy and Mark, Ellen, Nachy and Helene, Moshe, David and Mindy, Deborah and Jonathan, J.J., Goody and Eric, Lisa and Shira. Adored greatgrandfather of sixteen, esteemed patriarch and uncle of the extended family. Funeral at Riverdale Jewish Center, 3700 Independence Avenue, on Thursday, 9 A.M.
        GENAUER-Rabbi Sam. Congregation Kneseth Israel, Far Rockaway, New York, mourns with heartfelt sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, beloved husband of Sylvia. TZVI Flaum, Rabbi Ralph Pelcovitz, Rabbi Emeritus Sheldon Steinmetz, President
        GENAUER-Rabbi Sam. The Riverdale Jewish Center mourns the loss of our member, Rabbi Sam Genauer. We extend profound condolences to his wife Sylvia, daughter Blu and son-inlaw Rabbi Yitz Greenberg, his children, grandchildren, great grandchildren and the entire family. May they be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
        GENAUER-Samuel. Bar-Ilan University in Israel and its American Friends extend their sincerest condolences to the extended family of Rabbi Samuel Genauer. For all who knew and loved him, he was the model Jew who walked with God and men every day of his life. Need more be said! Jane Stern Lebell Emanuel Rackman Yehuda Halevy
        GENAUER-Rabbi Samuel. The SAR Academy family mourns with the entire Riverdale Jewish community the passing of Rabbi Sam Genauer whose example of Torah U'madah has been a model for our school. We extend condolences to the entire family. Min Hashomayim Tenuchamu. SAR Board of Directors
