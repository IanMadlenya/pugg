
        LINDSAY--Sally, 86, of Manhattan. On February 7, 2005. Survived by nieces Ann Fenichel (Frank Murphy), Ann Lindsay and Maud Lindsay and by nephews Eric Egas (Carolyn) and Peter Lindsay; sister-in-law Alice Lindsay. Predeceased by siblings Joan Fenichel and David Lindsay. Graduate of Swarthmore College and Yale University School of Drama. Worked for the OSS, Canadian Film Board, Saturday Review, and as an editor for Natural History Magazine. Sally was a world traveler, avid reader, generous supporter of environmental causes and the New York Public Library, and a staunch Democrat. She lived life on her own terms. She will be missed. Cremation private. Memorial service to be held at a later date. Info: Hastings Funeral Home, 914-478-0600.
