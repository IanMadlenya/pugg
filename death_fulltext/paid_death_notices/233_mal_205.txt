
        CAPUTO--Anthony J. of Bronxville on June 18, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Doris Burke Caputo. Devoted father of Mac and Bruce Caputo. Dear grandfather of Mac Jr., Scott, Tony and Britt. Survived by brother Bic Caputo. Mr. Caputo was a graduate of NYU, 1933 and NYU Law School, 1936. Mr. Caputo was a member of the New York Bar Association since 1937 and a member of the American College of Trial Lawyers. Mr. Caputo had his law firm in White Plains for more than 40 years and was a practicing attorney for 60 years. He was an avid golfer and curler. He was a member of the Siwanoy Country Club of Bronxville, Winged Foot, the Ekwanok Club, of Manchester, VT, the Country Club of Florida and the Ocean Club of Florida. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM Friday. Service at The Reformed Church of Bronxville on Saturday, June 24 at 2 PM.
