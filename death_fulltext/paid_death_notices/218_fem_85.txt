
        SWINT--Ann Brede, on March 8, 2005, after a brief illness. Beloved wife of Samuel H. Swint, Jr. of Southampton, NY and her devoted daughter Amy H. Swint of Charleston, SC. A wake will be held on Friday, March 11, 2005 from 6:30 to 8:30 PM at the Brockett Funeral Home, 203 Hampton Road, Southampton, NY. Funeral Service 11 AM Saturday, March 12, 2005 at St. John's Episcopal Church, Southampton. Interment will follow at Southampton Cemetery. A Memorial Service will be held later on Sunday, June 12, 2005 at 4 PM at the St. Andrew's Dune Church in Southampton followed by a reception at the Meadow Club. In lieu of flowers memorial donations to the Southampton Hospital Foundation, 240 Meeting House Lane, Southampton, NY 11068 would be appreciated.
