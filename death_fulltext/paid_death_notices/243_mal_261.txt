
        REIN--Herbert, 67, on April 21st. Beloved husband of Merle, loving father of Sebastian, Damon, and Aimee, grandfather of Jack, son of Rose, brother of Charles. An extraordinary man. A life teacher whose vitality and drive inspired everyone he touched. A New York City original, half of a kind, exactly twice as rare as one of a kind. Services Tuesday, 11:45am, at Riverside Memorial, 180 West 76th St.
        $(6$)REIN--Herbert. Our Class has lost one of our finest. Herb was the most loyal of friends who always opened his home and his heart to us. To Merle, Damon (Aimee), Sebastian, and his most special grandson Jack Hudson, we send our deepest sympathy and love and want you to know that he will never be forgotten. Birch Wathen class of 1957
