
        KINGMAN-Dong, age 89, of New York. World-renowned watercolorist whose artistic career spanned seven decades, died peacefully at home on May 12 in Manhattan. Survived by his sons Eddie and Dong Jr., grandchildren Jerry, Kelly, Melissa & Jonathan, sisters Sylvia Wong, Esther Fong, Lily Tang and Oi-Ying Ho. Memorial service to be announced. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made payable to either American Watercolor Society or The Church of St. Mary the Virgin. Please send to E. Kingman, Apt. 5E, 150 E. 79 St, NYC 10024 for forwarding.
