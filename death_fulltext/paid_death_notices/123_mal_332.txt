Funeral services to be held at Temple Israel, 140 Central Ave., Lawrence, L.I., on Tuesday, April 29, at 11:30 AM.         LORCH-Joseph. Temple Israel, Lawrence, NY, mourns the loss of its devoted congregant and extends deepest sympathy to his family. Brooks R. Susman, Rabbi Jeffrey Scherr, President
