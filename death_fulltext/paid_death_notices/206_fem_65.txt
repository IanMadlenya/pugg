
        KIM--Tongrim. Founder of Whanki Museum and Director of Whanki Foundation. She died on February 29, 2004, at her Manhattan, New York residence. She was born in Seoul, Korea, January 20, 1916, and came to the United States in 1964. She was the wife of Whanki Kim who is renowned and the most represented of the modern Korean artists. As the Director of the Whanki Foundation, she promoted young artists works from all over the world. In addition to her son Wha Young, she is survived by two daughters.
