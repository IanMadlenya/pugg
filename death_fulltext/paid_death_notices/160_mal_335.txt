
        WOLF-Thomas Lester. Of La Jolla, California. On May 28, 2000. Survived by his wife of 56 years, Joy R. Wolf, son Peter of Oakland, California, two daughters, Katherine Wolf of San Diego, California and Barbara Schwartz of New York City; one loving grandchild and a brother, Dr. Richard E. Wolf of Cincinnati, Ohio. A graduate of The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, he retired as the CEO and President of a national men's clothing manufacturer. Moving to La Jolla in 1982, he actively pursued a new career as a consultant for independent community associations.
