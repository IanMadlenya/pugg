
        SIMON-Max M., on September 13 in Harrison, NY. Beloved husband for over 58 years of Rose. Loving father of David and Donna Simon and Lois and Robert Reich. Adored grandfather of Randi and Daniel Reich and Jared and Dana Simon. Dear brother of Philip and Percy Simon, and Bea Bloom.
        SIMON-Max. The Jewish Community Center of Harrison announces with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed founding member. We extend deepest sympathies to his wife Rose, his children Lois and David, and to the entire family. Norton D. Shargel, Rabbi Linda P. Goldberg, President Julian Bernstein, Chairman
