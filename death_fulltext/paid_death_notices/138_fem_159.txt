
        APALE-Gloria B., M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians, nurses and staff of the hospital's Methadone Maintenance Treatment Program in acknowledging the sudden and tragic death of our dear friend and colleague, Gloria B. Apale, M.D., a member of the MMTP medical staff since 1983. Dr. Apale will be greatly missed by her co-workers and patients alike, and all whose lives were touched by her loyalty, warmth and dedication to care. To her husband, Ambrosio, her son, Cosmas, and the entire Apale family and all of her loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President and CEO, BIMC Jerome S. Breslaw, M.D., President, Medical Board Stephen G. Baum, M.D., Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Beverly L. Richman, M.D., Chief, Medical Services, Methadone Maintenance Treatment Program Marie Marciano, Administrator, MMTP Howard J. Rosso, R.N., Director of Nursing, MMTP
