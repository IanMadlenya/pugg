
        DEAN-Jesse W., 89. Died April 3, 2003, after a long debilitating illness. Adored and beloved husband of Diana and father of Elizabeth (Leesa). A designer, inventor, and former chief copy editor of Women's Wear Daily, as well as other Fairchild publications for 35 years. He was the inventor of the Tressy doll and the first adjustable TV table. His other designs spanned the unusual from cutting-edge furniture to a chess set made out of lamp parts. A lifelong socialist with a heightened sense of social justice he lived his life with impeccable integrity. He will be remembered always for his generous exuberant, courageous spirit and passions for family, tennis and life.
