
        CHARLES-Richard Lincoln. The Actors' Fund of America mourns the loss of its beloved Trustee, Richard Lincoln Charles. Chair of The Fund's Nursing Home and Assisted Living Facility Committee, he was also admired for his leadership of The Lambs Club and Foundation, where he served as Shepherd. He led by example and will be remembered with love, respect and admiration. Tom Dillon, President Joseph P. Benincasa, Executive Director
