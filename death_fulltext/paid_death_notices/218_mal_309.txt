
        PONEMON--Richard David. Formerly of Roslyn, NY, died on March 24 at his home in San Luis Obispo, CA. He was a Captain in the US Army during World War II and president of many years of Pyroferric International. He was a man of many talents and good works who gave generously of his time and money. He is survived by his loved wife of 63 years Rheta, and beloved son David and his life partner Terry Childers.
