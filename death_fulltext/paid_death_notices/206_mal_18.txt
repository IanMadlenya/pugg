
        CLIVE -- Jonathan Miller, Ph.D., 58. Biostatistician Jonathan Clive, longtime director of biostatistical research at the University of Connecticut Health Center, died on Friday after a long illness. Charismatic, mercurial and brilliant, Jon was beloved to family and friends, as well as distinguished in the realm of applied science. At Duke University and later at UConn he spearheaded research and turned dreaded courses into popular electives. Jon is survived by his mother, two brothers, a sister-in-law, two nieces and a loving extended family. Contributions may be sent to: The UConn Foundation-Jonathan Clive, Ph.D. Biostatistical Fund, 263 Farmington Ave, Farmington, CT. 06032.
