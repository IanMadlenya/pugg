
        NUSSBAUM - Dr. Harvey E. of South Orange, New Jersey, on Thursday, January 9, 1997; beloved husband of the late Barbara (nee Comando); devoted father of Dr. Peter Nussbaum and Margaret Nussbaum Lederer; dear father-in-law of Murie Nussbaum and Bertram Lederer; loving brother of Rita Posner; fond brother-in-law of Dr. Aaron Posner; adoring grandfather of Ruth and Elizabeth Nussbaum and Jacob Lederer. Funderal service Sunday, January 12, at 1:00 PM in Temple B'Nai Jeshurun, 1025 South Orange Avenue, Short Hills, New Jersey. Interment in Old B'Nai Jeshurun Cemetery. For those wishing to make a contribution in memory of Dr. Nussbaum, the family requests that in lieu of flowers, contributions be sent to the Saint Barnabas Development Fund, Old Short Hills Road, Livingston, NJ 07039 for the Harvey E. Nussbaum, MD, Research Institute at Saint Barnabas.
        NUSSBAUM - Harvey, M.D.  The Mount Sinai Alumni records with sorrow the passing of their longtime colleague and friend. His dedication as a clinician, teacher and colleague has earned him the respect of the alumni and all who knew him. He will be missed. We extend out sincere condolences to his family.  Albert M. Lefkovits, M.D. Pres.  Alison B. Goldfarb, M.D. Secy.
