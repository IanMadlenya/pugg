
        FRANCAIS--Jacques Pierre. Died on February 4, 2004 in New York City at the age of 80 after a long battle with Parkinson's disease. He is survived by his wife Beatrice Mathews Francais, his daughter Isabelle Francais, his granddaughter Daniella Valentine Saarma and two stepdaughters Hilary and Diana Combs. Jacques was born in 1923 in Paris emigrating to the United States in 1948. He became one of the leading dealers of stringed instruments in the United States and a world recognized expert. A funeral service will be held at Frank E. Campbell at 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Wednesday, February 11 at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to Young Concert Artists Inc., 250 West 57 St, Suite 1222, NYC, NY 10107-0103.
        FRANCAIS -- Jacques. The Bowdoin International Music Festival expresses its deepest regrets to Beatrice Francais on the loss of her husband. He will be deeply missed both personally and professionally. Lewis Kaplan, Director
