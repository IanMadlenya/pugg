
        VINTON-Jean R. Died peacefully February 24, 2002 at her home in Lexington, MA, aged ninety-one. Vassar graduate, beloved wife of the late Alfred M. Vinton. She lived a life of service to family, church and community. Survived by sister Frances Baikow, Miami, FL; children, Alfred Jr. of London, John of Wellesley, MA, Helen Lally of NYC, Margaret Cole of Corinth, VT; thirteen grandchildren, four great-grandchildren, three nieces and two nephews. A memorial service will be held at Hancock Church, Lexington, MA, March 8, 11 AM. Memorial contributions may be made to United Hospital Medical Center, Port Chester, NY, or Vassar College.
