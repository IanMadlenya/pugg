
        BLUESTONE -- Dr. Harvey. The Board of Trustees of Bronx-Lebanon Hospital Center, as well as its medical, nursing, administrative, and Department of Psychiatry staff mourn the passing of Dr. Harvey Bluestone. Dr. Bluestone served, with distinction, as Chairman of Bronx-Lebanon's Department of Psychiatry. During his 40 years of service, the hospital's Department of Psychiatry emerged as a leader in the mental health field, widely recognized for its comprehesive, innovative, and results-oriented programs at the inpatient and outpatient levels. The Bronx-Lebanon family extend our sincere condolences to the Bluestone family. Shirley Rodriguez Remeneski Chairperson, Board of Trustees Miguel A. Fuentes, Jr. President and CEO Dr. Milton A. Gumbs, VP Medical Affairs, Medical Director
