
        FURMAN-Evelyn Katz. September 9, 2002. Beloved wife and mother. Trained as a medical social worker, she was guidance counselor at Riverdale Country School, The Fieldston School, and Brooklyn Poly Prep. Longtime member of the Board of Trustees of the Bronx Organization for the Learning Disabled (BOLD) and its President for 13 years. It is a major provider of services to learning disabled children and young adults in the South Bronx, where the Furman Evaluation Center has been named in her honor. She is survived by sons Bruce, Gary and Neil, and brothers Louis, Walter, Frederick, and husband Seymour. Donations to BOLD, 770 Beck St., Bronx NY 10455, or Anne Moore Breast Cancer Research at Cornell Medical Center, 428 East 72 St., NY. Services Wednesday, September 11, at 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
