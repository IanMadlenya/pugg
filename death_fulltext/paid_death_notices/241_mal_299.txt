
        KOENIGSBERG--Gerald, Beloved uncle, brother-in-law and cherished friend. We will miss you always. The Zingman Family
        KOENIGSBERG--Gerald, Born August 21, 1921, died on Monday, February 19, 2007 at his home in Key Biscayne, FL. Jerry triumphed over advanced colon cancer for nine years and after a valiant fight succumbed to other complications. He is survived by his dearly loved wife of the past 57 years, Ronnie, children, Bob, Richard, (Rebecca), Bill (Jessica), and Jill (Gary) Blumenfeld, nine grandchildren and sister Lillian Trumper. He will be greatly missed on the golf course and tennis courts in Key Biscayne and Lido Beach, and forever remain in the hearts of all who loved him. Funeral services will be held at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 1920 Alton Rd., Miami Beach, FL. Thursday, February 22, 11 am, and in NYC at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Sunday, February 25th, 11:30 am. Interment Mt. Lebanon Cemetery. In lieu of flowers please donate to Memorial Sloan Kettering, specify the memo notation to ''Dr. Nancy Kemeny's Pump Research Fund ''
