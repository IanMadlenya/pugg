
        FLEISCHER-Gustave. On February 19, 2000, at age 63. Devoted and loving husband to Helen. Beloved father of Cheryl, Linda and son-inlaw Stewart. Graduate of MIT. He was a member of the Moles, American Society of Civil Engineering, the Military Engineer and the British Tunneling Society. He was a remarkably strong individual whose love for life kept him positive even after circumstances became hard. Donations may be made in his memory to the American Cancer Society. Services Monday, 10 AM, Riverside Chapel, Mount Vernon, NY.
