
        HAVRAN-Morris. Congregation Orach Chaim deeply mourns the loss of our beloved senior member and daily and Sabbath congregant. A Holocaust survivor, his devotion and service to our Congregation will be eternally remembered and honored. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Sylvia, and sons, Leo and Jack. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Michael D. Shmidman, Rabbi Wolf Scheck, President
