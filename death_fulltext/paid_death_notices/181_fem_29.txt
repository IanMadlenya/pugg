
        MATISON-Edith Green. January 20th, 2002, in Acapulco, Mexico. Beloved mother of Esteban and cherished friend of the Ann and Joe Mullins family. Born 1909 in NYC, married Michael Jaffe and later photographer Leo Matiz. Friend to many artists, Edith opened the first art gallery in Acapulco. Renowned for her adventurous spirit & her determination to live life fully, she was admired for her love of the arts, her writings, and her critical acumen. Courageous, redoubtable & loyal, Edith will be greatly missed. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Acapulco Against AIDS, acasida@aca.quik.com
