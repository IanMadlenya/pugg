
        COHEN -- Rose Yorkofsky. Aged 92 passed away on Tuesday, April 18, 2006 at the Chelsea Jewish Nursing Home, Chelsea, MA. She leaves behind two sons, Louis and Arthur, her grandchildren Daniel, Monica, Jonathan and Oliver, and great granddaughter Ellie. Rose was a great lady with a zest for life. She loved her family, the arts and a good game of gin rummy. She will be missed and remembered with love by all who knew her.
