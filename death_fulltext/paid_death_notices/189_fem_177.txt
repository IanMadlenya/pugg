
        MAUER-Rosalyn Bederson, 82. Life-long resident of the Bronx and a committed educator and public schools advocate, died peacefully Wednesday. Loved by Arnold, her husband of 59 years, and her children Peri and Richard, her brother Ben and sister-in-law Betty, her daughter-in-law Barbara, grandchildren Jessica and Michael, and nephews Joshua, Geoffrey, Aron and Benjamin and their families. Services 9:30 AM Sunday, at Hirsch & Sons in the Bronx.
