
        RUBENSTEIN-Allan A., M.D. On March 12, 2001 in Plantation, FL. Formerly of North Woodmere, NY. A pediatrician and endocrinologist in practice for many years on Long Island associated with the Long Island Jewish Hospital. Served in World War II as a Captain in the Army Medical Corps. He leaves a wife Marilyn, a sister Esther and two nieces.
