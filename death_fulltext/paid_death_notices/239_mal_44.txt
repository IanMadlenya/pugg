
        KAMINS--Walter born December 27, 1922 in New York City, died December 2, 2006. Devoted and beloved husband; adoring father, grandfather and father-in-law; cherished uncle, and brotherin-law. His is survived by his wife of 57 years Judith (Weisz) Kamins, daughters and sons-in-law Toni Kamins and Harold Itzkowitz of New York, NY, Erica and Nicholas Farrell of Richmond VA, Jennifer and Richard Bullock of Edison, NJ, Dana and Jeffrey Jacobs of Lake Worth, FL, and seven grandchildren. Donations to the Henry Street Settlement.
