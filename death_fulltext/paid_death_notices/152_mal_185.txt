
        SCHONBERGER-Henry V. May 2, 1924-September 12, 1999. Longtime employee of New York Life Insurance Company. Honored member of the New York chapter of Jewish War Veterans. Resident of Pompano Beach FL. Henry was born in NYC to Joseph and Olga and attended DeWitt Clinton High School. Everyone whose life he touched should be grateful for knowing and loving him, ponytail and all. He was a generous, kind and gentle person and will be missed by his friends, children and many grandchildren. Please remember him by loving your family unconditionally. Stop Smoking Now!
