
        JOVINO-Philomena. Of Staten Island, New York, age ninety, formerly of Manhattan and Brooklyn. Died February 7, 1999. Born in Manhattan, second daughter of John Jovino, a supplier of police equipment in lower Manhattan, & Clothilde Astorita. Taught piano for over 50 years in Brooklyn and Staten Island. Among her pupils was the recently deceased opera singer, Joann Grillo. She is survived by her sister Lillian Ida. Funeral Mass at 9:15 A.M., February 9, 1999 at Our Lady of Guadelupe Church, 7201 15th Avenue, Bklyn. In lieu of flowers, contributions to her memory to The National Office of The Devotees of Padre Pio, 1154 First Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
