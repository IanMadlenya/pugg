        GORDON-Isidore. AMIT is deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend, supporter and financial advisor, who together with his late beloved wife, Yaffa Gordon, served our cause of helping the forgotten children of Israel for almost half a century. A warm and compassionate man, Isidore Gordon will be remembered for his Torah scholarship and his devotion to Israel and the Jewish community. May his sons Nahum, David, and Daniel, their wives and children be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Sondra Sokal, President Marvin Leff, Executive Director
        GORDON-Isidore. We are deeply saddened by the passing of Isidore Gordon, father of our Senior Partner Nahum and Barbara, our former partner David and Karen, and Daniel and Beatrice, whose wise counsel and guidance we valued professionally and personally for many years. May his memory serve as a blessing to his children, grandchildren and great grandchildren, and may they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Sherman & Gordon, P.C. Isaac and Judith Sherman Jonathan and Sharon Sherman Joanne and Jeff Ingerman
        GORDON-Isidore. We extend our sincerest condolences to David Z. Gordon and his brothers, Nahum and Daniel, on the loss of their beloved father, Isidore. Silver Founders of the Medical Center, David and Karen Gordon personify the values of kindness and generosity which they learned from this special man. May the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med. Ctr. in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, Pres.
