
        BECKERMAN-Bessie. Age 93. On April 29, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Henry; dear mother of Ruth (Robert) Goldschlager and Nancy (the late Stuart) Leavitt; dear grandmother and great-grandmother. Past president and life member of Hadassah to whom contributions in her memory may be made or to the Stuart J. Leavitt Memorial Foundation for Pediatric Oncology. Graveside services will be held Friday, 11AM, at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
