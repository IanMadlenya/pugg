
        REISSER-Elisabeth. On April 5, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Otto Reisser. Service Friday, April 11, 8:30AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the NYU College of Dentistry or the NYU Medical Center Child Life Endowment Fund.
        REISSER-Elisabeth. The Auxiliary of Tisch Hospital, NYU Medical Center, deeply mourns the loss of our beloved and highly respected Board Member, Elisabeth Reisser. Actively involved in the Auxiliary for over 35 years, Elisabeth was instrumental in initiating and developing the Child Life Program in the Pediatric Unit of Tisch Hospital. Her lifelong generosity and committment will continue to inspire all those who knew her. Donna Soloway, President.
