
        BEY-Samuel E. The Rabbis, staff and students of The Les Turchin Chabad House at Rutgers University mourn the passing of our esteemed friend Samuel E. Bey. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Mollie, and his children Stanley Bey, Arne Bey, and Sharyn Turchin, wife of our Honorary Chairman, Les Turchin. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Yosef Z. Carlebach
