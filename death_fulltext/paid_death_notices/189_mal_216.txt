
        BLATT-Leon. Of Holden, MA, formerly of Nanuet, NY. Died Friday, October 18, in Worcester, after an illness. His wife Rose Roth (Blatt) died in 1969. He leaves a son Howard N. Blatt and a daughter-in-law, Melissa A. Blatt, both of Holden, a brother David Blatt, of Rockville Centre, LI, four grandchildren, and a great-granddaughter. Graveside services 1:30 PM, Sunday, October 20, in Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, LI.
