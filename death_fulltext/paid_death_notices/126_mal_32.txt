
        STRONG-Woodbridge, 87, formerly of Highland Park, NJ and Lenox, MA on June 21, 1997. Son of the late New Jersey State Senator Theodore and Cornelia Livingston Van Rensselaer Strong. Mr. Strong was an attorney for almost 50 years with the family firm of Strong & Strong in New Brunswick, NJ. He was a combat veteran of World War II serving with the 11th Armored Division. Husband of Julie DeViry Strong. Father of Alan, Harmon, Timothy and Benjamin Strong. Brother of Robert L and Philip L Strong. Also survived by 5 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. A memorial service was held June 25, 1997.
