
        SILBERMAN-Charles L. Died November 1, 2001, at age 87, in New Rochelle, NY. Formerly of New Milford, NJ, and New York City. Loving husband of the late Lenore (Guth). Father of Shoshana and Julie; father-in-law of Dianne; grandfather of Sonya, Alex, Rachel & Dan. Also survived by brothers, Paul & David, extended family and friends. Union organizer and longtime editor of the American Communications Association newspaper. Throughout his life he was committed to creating a world of peace, justice & equality.
