
        OTTEN-Louis. On December 20, 2000, age 93. Devoted husband of the late Marjorie Levy Otten, loved father of Lois Robbins and Michael Otten, father-in-law of Evelyne Otten and Michael Robbins, cherished grandfather of Juli Robbins Greenwald (Dr. Daniel), Jil Robbins Pollock (John Henry), Sylvie Otten Sollod (Kurt), Polly Robbins White (Bracken), Dr. Daniel Otten and Marc Otten, great grandfather of Alex and Oliver Greenwald, Stewart and Claire Pollock, Stephen and David Sollod and Andrew White. Retired family court judge of the City and State of New York. Memorial gathering January 7, 2 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to Green Chimneys, JCCA or Princeton University.
        OTTEN-Louis. The Officers, Trustees and Staff of the Jewish Childcare Association express their profound sorrow at the loss of Judge Louis Otten, a valued and esteemed member of JCCA'S Board of Trustees for over 20 years. His committment to children and families and his significant contribution to the Two Together, Daycare, Foster Home and Pleasantville Cottage School Programs will be long remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Joyce L. Kramer, President Irwin U. Goldberg, Board Chair Paul Gitelson, CEO
        OTTEN-Louis. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member, Louis Otten. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
