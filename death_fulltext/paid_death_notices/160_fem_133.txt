
        BERARD-Frederica Stevens, died May 12, 2000 at age 92, in Middletown, R.I. Daughter of the late Frederic William Stevens and Alice Caroline Seely Stevens. Predeceased by her beloved husband Philippe Roger Berard and her daughter Joan Hone King. Survived by her daughter Frederica Stevens Neff and Eugenie Gallatin Angles, her son Philip Hone Auerbach, twelve grandchildren and twelve great - grandchildren. Funeral to be held at the American Cathedral in Paris on June 9, 2000 at 2:30PM. Donations may be made in her memory to Columbia University Alzheimer's Disease Research Center, 230 West 168th Street, New York, NY 10032.
