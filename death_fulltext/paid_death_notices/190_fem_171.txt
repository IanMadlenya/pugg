
        SPRAFKIN-Ida Squire. Age 94, formerly of Kings Point and Brooklyn, New York on November 22, 2002. Beloved wife of Samuel, devoted mother of David and daughter-in-law Hildy, adoring grandmother of Mara and Jenna. Interment private at Beth-El Cemetery. Contributions may be made to the Brooklyn Public Library.
        SPRAFKIN-Ida. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Ida Sprafkin. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Secy.
