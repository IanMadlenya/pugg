
        BICK-Marie Jensen, on April 13th, 2002, of Garden City, NY. She died suddenly at home. Beloved wife, mother and grandmother. President Emeritus of the Clark Botanic Garden Auxiliary. She leaves behind her husband Alan and her children John, Alan and Lucinda. She will be greatly missed by her grandchildren Jessica, Anna, Katie, Jack and Amanda. She left us too soon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Fanny Dwight Clark Memorial Garden, 193 I.U. Willets Road, Albertson, NY 11507-2208.
