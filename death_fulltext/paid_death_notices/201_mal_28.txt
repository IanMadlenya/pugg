
        ROTHSTEIN--Warren. Chabad's Children of Chernobyl mourns the untimely and sudden passing of a dear friend. Cherished Board Member, Warren Rothstein was one of our earliest supporters. His wise counsel and generosity of spirit, his open manner and deeply felt concern will be hugely missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Deborah, his children Zachary and Brandon, and his entire family. Jay Schottenstein, Chairman Alan Clingman, President David Tessel, Chairman Exec Committee
