
        WEISENFELD-Mildred Mosler. The Board of Directors, staff and friends of Fight for Sight, Inc. remember our founder and executive director of fifty-one years on the first anniversary of her death, December 6th, 1997. Mildred Weisenfeld was a visionary leader who contributed her extraordinary talents to finding causes and treatments for blinding eye diseases, helping to save sight for millions of people. Her legacy continues at Fight for Sight
