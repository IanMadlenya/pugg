
        RHEA--Suzanne Menocal. Died at her home at The Greenbrier on October 19, 2005. Mrs. Rhea was the wife of the late Alexander D. Rhea III (Princeton 1941) & mother of the late Mark Alexander Rhea. Suzanne who was noted for her extraordinary beauty and the grace with which she entertained friends and business associates, traveled and lived throughout the world with Alex, who was an executive of General Motors. She is survived by a brother, Martin Menocal. Friends are invited to a service of remembrance and committal at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, White Sulphur Springs, WVA at 2 P.M. on Saturday, October 29.
