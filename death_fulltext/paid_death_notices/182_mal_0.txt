
        BASKIR-Philip. On February 27, 2002. A retired fund-raiser for North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset. Beloved husband of Sylvia Warren. Loving father and father-in-law of Lawrence and Marna and Mark and Carol. Dear brother of the late Irving Cohen and brother-in-law of Phyllis. Adoring grandfather of Cecily, Micah, Lauren and Matt. Services Friday 12:00 noon at ''RiversideNassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR) Great Neck, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset, NY.
        BASKIR-Philip. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Philip Baskir, a distinguished professional of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies. He served the organization for more than 35 years. We extend condolences to the entire Baskir family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
