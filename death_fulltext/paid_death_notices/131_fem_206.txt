
        KERMAN-Helen. Of Roslyn Heights, NY, died suddenly on December 20, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Bertram Kerman, devoted mother of Judythe (Martin Isserlis) and Betsy (Peter Carey). Beloved grandmother of Geoffrey and Shana Carey. Beloved sister of Stanley Gardner (Blossom). She was strong, caring, independent. You will be in our hearts forever.
