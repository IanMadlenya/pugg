
        BECKER--Frederick G., 90, of Amherst, MA, June 30th. Renowned print maker/artist. Prof. Emeritus University of Massachusetts, Amherst; Former Prof. Washington University, St. Louis. Active in the W.P.A. Federal Art Project, SW Hayter's Atelier 17. Predeceased by his beloved wife Jean Morrison; dear father of Anton and Carla; loving grandfather to Emily, David and Benjamin; devoted companion of Alberta Booth. Memorial service August 15 in Amherst.
