
        NADLER-Seymour. Of Boca Raton, Florida and formerly of Fort Lee, New Jersey and the Bronx passed away peacefully Thursday, September 23, 1999. Mr. Nadler is the beloved husband of Doris Nadler; loving father of Michael (Adrienne) Nadler and Vicki Schindler. He will always be special to everyone who knew him. Services and Interment were held on Friday, September 24, 1999 at Eternal Light Memorial Gardens in Boynton Beach, Florida. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice by the Sea in Boca Raton, Florida. Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel in Coconut Creek, Fl.
