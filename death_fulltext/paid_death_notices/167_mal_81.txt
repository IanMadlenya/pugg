
        MURPHY-Reverend Kenneth, Order of Carmelites, unexpectedly on December 3, 2000. Member of The Order of Carmelites and The American Province of St. Elias. Loving son of the late Thomas and Virginia (nee Mackey). Cherished brother of Thomas (deceased) and Patricia Nerad (Patrick). Devoted uncle of Amanda. Fr. Murphy earned his Bachelors Degree in Economics and Finance at Catholic University and his Masters of Divinity Degree at The Washington Theological Union, Washington, D.C. Following his ordination Fr. Murphy was assigned to Our Lady of the Scapular Church where he served as Associate Pastor and House Procurator from 1981-1985. In 1985, Fr. Murphy served as Pre-Novice Director and Provincial Procurator in Purchase, NY. In 1990 Fr. Murphy returned to Our Lady of the Scapular and St. Stephens Church were he served as Pastor until 1997. In 1997 Fr. Murphy was assigned to St. Elias Priory in Boca Raton, FL were he served as Campus Minister at Pope John Paul II High School. In June of 1998, Fr. Kenneth was reassigned to Our Lady of the Scapular and St. Stephens where he served as Chaplain at Bellevue Hospital. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Thursday, December 7 at 10:30 AM. Burial will be at Carmelite Cemetery, Carmelite Drive, Middletown, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Carmelite Education, P.O. Box 868, Carmelite Drive, Middletown, NY 10940.
