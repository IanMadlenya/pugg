
        ARNOLD--Herbert W. Died peacefully on January 12 with his family at his side. Devoted husband to Barbara & father to Phyllis and Staci. Loving grandfather of Liam & Isabel. Successful garment district figure for many years, he was known and loved by many. He proudly served his country in the United States Army and his community as an active member of B'nai B'rith. May his soul rest in peace and his memory be a blessing. Donations may be made to Memorial SloanKettering Hospital.
