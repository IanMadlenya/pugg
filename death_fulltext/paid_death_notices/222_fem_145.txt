
        FINK--Jean C. A longtime Scarsdale resident and tireless advocate for court reform and juvenile justice died on July 14, 2005 at age 88. Mrs. Fink was the daughter of Jacques Coe & Clara Bartels Coe. She was predeceased by her husband, attorney Marvin S. Fink and is survived by two daughters, Nancy Ceccorulli of Arnold, MD and Lisa Fink of Brunswick, ME and four grandchildren. A celebration of Jean Fink's life will be held on Sunday, July 31 at noon at the Center for Jewish Life at the Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale.
