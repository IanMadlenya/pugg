
        PICKERING-Martha Holding, 70. Of Princeton, NJ and West Palm Beach, FL, on July 15, 1999 of breast cancer at home in Princeton. Wife of Donald A., mother of Stowe H. Tattersall, Samuel C. Tattersall and Martha T. Giancola, grandmother of David H. Giancola and Edith C. Tattersall. Sister of Audrey H. McCargo, stepmother of Donald A. Pickering, Jr. and Dorothy Pickering Bossidy, stepgrandmother of Stuart C.C. Pickering. She was predeceased by her sister, Nancy H. Tansill. Interment private in Edgartown, MA. Memorial service at 11 AM on Saturday, September 11, at Trinity Church, Princeton, NJ. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Martha Holding Pickering Memorial Fund at Trinity Church, 33 Mercer St., Princeton, NJ 08540.
