
        HAPPEL-John. 93, on December 19, 2001. Noted Chemical Engineer, he served as Chairman of the Chemical Engineering Department at NYU and was Adjunct Professor at Columbia University. Dr. Happel held 46 patents. An avid hiker, he was a summer resident of Lake Placid, NY, for over 50 years. Survived by his wife Dorothy Merriam Happel; children Jill, George Syms and Ruth Emilie; sister Ruth Smiley; granddaughter Robin Jane. Memorial service 11 AM, January 12, 2002, Zion Episcopal Church, Dobbs Ferry, NY.
