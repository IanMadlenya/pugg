
        GLASER - TAUBIN -- Jeanette. Died July 14, 2004 at the age of 81. Born May 3, 1923 in Russia. Formerly of Katonah, NY. Beloved mother of Matthew Glaser of Cambridge, MA and Claudia Glaser-Mussen of NYC, grandmother of Jacob. A warm talented woman who sang like an angel, whose voice brought pleasure to many. A life filled with struggle and courage. She will be greatly missed by her family. Funeral service will be held at Riverside Chapel, Friday, July 16th at 10:45 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Alzheimers Association at www.alz.org
        GLASER - TAUBIN -- Jeanette. Died July 14, 2004. A loving loyal sister, beautiful of face and spirit. Barbara Greene
