
        NESHAMKIN-Oliver M., M.D. July 8, 2001, peacefully in his home in Manhattan. Beloved husband of Lucy. Devoted father of Paul and his wife, Ruth, and Linda and her husband John W. French. Proud grandfather of Alexandra and Jennifer French. Staunton Military Academy '31, Columbia College '35, Anderson College of Medicine, Glasgow, Scotland '42. Combat surgeon in European Theater of WWII. Dedicated physician. Former staff of Midtown and St. Clare's Hospitals. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Columbia College Alumni Fund. Memorial service later. Call Paul Neshamkin at 201-714-4881 for information.
