
        BREGMAN--Martin A. Of Pinehurst, NC, formerly of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, died on March 12, 2004, at the age of 90. He is survived by his beloved wife of 50 years Kathryn Rettew, his devoted daughter Ann (James) Magee, his cherished grandaughter Melanie (Samir) Saigol and his adoring greatgrandchildren Rafeh and Tanya, his loving sister Margery (David) Forer of New York, niece Katherine E. Forer and nephew David B. Forer (Mimi), as well as his great-nieces Chessa and Katherine, and great-nephew David William. Born in New York City, he graduated from Columbia University. He then spent over half a century in Brazil where he was an important leader in the Brazilian business community. He will always be remembered by those who were fortunate enough to have known him as a man of warmth, integrity and stature.
