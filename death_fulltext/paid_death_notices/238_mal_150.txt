
        TAXON--Saul, CPA, 87. Short Hills, NJ, died November 10, 2006. Husband to Sybil (nee Sylvia Schwartz) of Brooklyn; father of Marsha Gershberg of Scottsdale and Short Hills, and Carol Taxon of Manhattan; grandfather to Cheryl, Meredith and Samantha Gershberg. Funeral services were held on November 10, 2006, at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery. A graduate from Baruch College, he went on to establish a successful accounting practice in NYC until his retirement in 2002.
