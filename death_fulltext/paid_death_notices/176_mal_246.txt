
        BECKER-Robert Benham. Husband to Ruth. Father of Peter, Paul and Michael. Brother to Carol. Grandfather to Rachel, Erica and Josh. Died Monday, September 17, 2001, at his home in North Salem, NY. He was surrounded by family and friends who loved him, and by a love created out of his selfless and unconditional ability to give. A gifted artist and designer who contributed his support, without reserve, to the Yeshiva University Museum. He will be missed, but ever kept alive in our hearts and in our prayers. Services 10 A.M. Wednesday at The Community Synagogue of Rye, NY. The family prefers memorials in his memory to the Alzheimer's Foundation of Westchester and the Hospice of Westchester.
