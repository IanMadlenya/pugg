
        JACKSON-David F., 61, in New York, July 3, 1999. Born in Camden, Maine. He is survived by brothers Glenn and Thomas and his stepmother Pearl Wadsworth, all of Maine. He was a graduate of Parsons School of Design. His interior design studio, Hollyhocks, Ltd., was well known in Manhattan and Southold. Friends, clients and family will miss the sparkle of his style and the lilt of his laughter. For information on memorial services, please call 570-296-6249.
