
        SCHIFF-Herbert H. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a highly respected international leader of world Jewry from Columbus, Ohio, who was the founding Chairman and Chairman Emeritus of the Board of Governors of the Wurzweiler School of Social Work, where he established the Herbert H. Schiff Chair in Management and Administration. A Yeshiva University Honorary Trustee, he and his wife, Betty, are Benefactors of YU, where he will always be remembered with abiding gratitude for his dynamic leadership. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to Betty and to his children: Suzanne Gallant, Patricia (and Richard) Hershorin, and Jane Carpenter. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman YU Board of Trustees David I. Schachne, Chairman WSSW Board of Governors
        SCHIFF-Herbert H. The officers and directors of the Wurzweiler School of Social Work, Yeshiva University, mourn the passing of Herbert H. Schiff, Chairman Emeritus, our founding leader. The Board extends its heartfelt sympathies to Betty & her family. Dr. David I. Schachne Board Chairman
