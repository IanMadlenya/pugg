
        HOCHMAN--Henry. The entire Continuum Health Partners family acknowledges with sadness the passing of Henry Hochman, beloved father of our dear friend and longtime colleague, Steven Hochman, Esq., a member of the Continuum Board of Trustees. Steve's parents' generous support of Beth Israel was recognized with the naming of the Henry and Ida Hochman Division of Cardiovascular Interventions and the Henry and Ida Hochman Emergency Medical Services, both at Beth Israel's former Singer Division. To Steven, his brother Bruce, his sister Marilyn, the entire Hochman family and all of their father's loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman Board of Trustees Stanley Brezenoff President and CEO
