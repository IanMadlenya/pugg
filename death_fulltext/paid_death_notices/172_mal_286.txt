
        CUMMINGS-Maxwell In Montreal in his 104th year on Wednesday May 23, 2001. Husband of the late Queenie Wener. Beloved father and father-in-law of Robert and Marion, Norma and the late Jack. Cherished grandfather of Jane, Peter and Julie, James and Alice, Steven and Susan, Richard and Joanne, Nancy and Marc Gold. Loving great grandfather of 15 great grandchildren and 2 great great grandchildren. Predeceased by his brothers Nathan, Ben, Ralph, Harold and sister Minnie Abbey. Funeral service and burial took place on Friday in Montreal. Contributions may be made to the ''Maxwell Cummings Memorial Fund'' Jewish General Hospital Foundation 3755 Cote Ste Catherine Rd, Montreal H3T 1E2 (514) 340-8251.
