
        WOLFF--Herbert. The Officers and Directors of Metropolitan Jewish Health System and Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Foundation sadly mourn the untimely passing of Herbert Wolff, beloved father of our esteemed fellow Board Member, John Wolff. May his legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom he touched. We pray that his fond memories will sustain you, and the love and prayers of those who care will comfort you. We share in your loss. Our profound sympathy and sincere condolences to the family. Steven L. Topal, Chairperson of the Board Eli S. Feldman, President Chief Executive Officer
