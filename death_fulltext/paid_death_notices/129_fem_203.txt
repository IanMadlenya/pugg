
        CURTIN-Claire M. Of Bronxville, N.Y. on October 24, 1997. Devoted wife of Brian J. Curtin, M.D. Beloved mother of Edward, James, Thomas Curtin and Deirdre Hommel. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 24 and 7-9 P.M. Sunday. Mass of Christian Burial Saint Joseph's Church, Bronxville, on Monday, October 27, 10 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to College of Saint Elizabeth, Convent Station, New Jersey.
