
        KESSEL-Marie Adler. Age eightythree of NYC, on September 25, 1999. Esteemed member of the Board of Trustees of Audrey Cohen College for over ten years. Her dedication and generosity to the College was exemplary. The Board acknowledges her outstanding achievements as a philanthropist, businesswoman, social worker, design professional and author. The Board of Trustees and the entire Audrey Cohen College community extends its sincere condolences to the Kessel family on the passing of a woman of consummate professionalism and extraordinary grace. Mildred Robbins Leet Chairwoman, Board of Trustees Stephen R. Greenwald, President Audrey Cohen College
