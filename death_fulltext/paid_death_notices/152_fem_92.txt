
        ROPER-Marion E. Died in Manhattan, September 8, 1999 after many years of heart disease. Born and raised in Gainesville, GA and graduated from Brenau College and retired from Con Edison in NY. Survived by her loving sister, Margaret Roper Carroll; cherished friend Carole Edwardes; many nieces and nephews and a host of loyal and devoted friends. Memorial service to be announced later.
