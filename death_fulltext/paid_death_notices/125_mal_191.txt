
        RAEBURN-Samuel. Of Florida, formerly NYC. Died Friday, June 13, 1997, after a long, courageous battle with leukemia. Sam was a WW II veteran and die maker. Beloved husband of Harriet Shatz; loving father of Alan (Hilary) and Roberta (Terry); adored grandfather of Adam and Samantha; dear uncle, friend and neighbor. Services 1 PM, Monday, June 16, at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Boulevard. He will be sorely missed.
