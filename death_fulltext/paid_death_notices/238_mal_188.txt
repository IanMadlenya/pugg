
        FALK--Arthur, 79. Died suddenly and unexpectedly November 14 at home. Beloved husband of Sue (Chase). Devoted father of Janet Falk, Andrea Wertheimer and James Falk. Loving father-inlaw of Michael Rindler, Stan Wertheimer and Nina Falk. Cherished ''Pop Art'' of Rebecca and Sandra Rindler, Joel and Perry Wertheimer, and Jacob and Belinda Falk. Burial private. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Association. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date. May his memory be for a blessing.
