
        EVANS--Tilghman Boyd, age 74, of Bainbridge Island, Washington, died on January 8, 2007. He is survived by his wife, Margaret Terry Red Evans, of Bainbridge Island, Washington; a brother, Elwyn Evans, and sister, Katharine Reese May, both of Wilmington, Delaware; a daughter, Shelley Evans, of Cambridge, Massachusetts; a son, Lloyd Tilghman Boyd Evans of Rosemont, New Jersey, and five grandchildren. Services by Kass & Cook Family Funeral Home.
