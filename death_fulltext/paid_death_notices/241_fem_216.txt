
        ROGOFF--Theresa. Beloved mother, wife, sister, and grandmother of four, died on Saturday, February 24. Theresa Rogoff had a long and distinguished career as a registered nurse. She is survived by her three children, Beatrice Plasse, Deborah Rogoff, Sidney Rogoff, and the late Frances Rogoff, and her brother Robert Steinberg. Her loving husband, Joseph Rogoff passed away in 1984. Services will be held at the Riverdale Temple, 246 Street at Independence Avenue, Bronx, New York, on Tuesday, February 27 at 10:30am.
