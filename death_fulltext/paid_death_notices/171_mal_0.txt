
        ALSOP-Peter Schuyler. Died on March 29 at Calvary Hospital. He was 64 years old. A graduate of Princeton University, Peter also attended the NYU Graduate School of Arts & Sciences, and the NYU Business School. He was a member of The Union Club. In addition to his work as an independent investment counselor, he was devoted to the arts. Peter was a member of Blue Hill Troop, Ltd., a charitable theatrical group dedicated to the works of Gilbert & Sullivan. He served on the Board of Trustees of The Hewitt School & in recent years devoted tireless energy to increasing the availability of technology in the public schools. A loving father and an inspiring friend and mentor, he will be sorely missed. In lieu of flowers, donations in Peter's name to Saint James Church or The Calvary Fund, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461, would be appreciated. Peter is survived by his daughter Schuyler, his sister Elizabeth H. Hinchman, and two nieces and two nephews. Funeral services will be held at Saint James Church, 865 Madison Avenue, Tuesday, April 3 at 2:30. There will be a reception at The Union Club, 101 E. 69th St, immediately following the services.
