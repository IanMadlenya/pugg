
        WEAVER-John G. On November 6th, at age 82. After Wittenberg University he started as a sportswriter, and following service in World War II he held marketing positions at Procter & Gamble, Swift & Co., Crosley and Benton & Bowles. Later he was Communications Director for the U.S. Committee for UNICEF and the Valley Health Plan in Amherst, Mass. An avid sailor, he was a longtime member of the Larchmont Yacht Club. He leaves his loving wife of fifty-six years, Betty A. Weaver, sons Thomas, David and Richard, and grandchildren Elisabeth, Nicholas and Emma.
