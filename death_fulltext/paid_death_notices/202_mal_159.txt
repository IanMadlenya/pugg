
        HOCHWALT--Father Richard A., M.M., 79, of Dayton, Ohio, a Maryknoll missioner to Africa, died November 16 at St. Teresa's Residence, Ossining, NY. A priest for 52 years, he is survived by a brother, Carroll Hochwalt, Jr., of Upper Montclair, N.J., and nieces and nephews. Wake services: Tuesday, November 18, 7:30 p.m, funeral Mass: Wed, November 19, 11 a.m., at Maryknoll Society Center, Ossining. Memorial donations may be made in his name to Maryknoll Fathers and Brothers. P.O. Box 302, Maryknoll, N.Y. 10545
