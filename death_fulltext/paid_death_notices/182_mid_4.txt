
        SCHNEIDERMAN-Norma. Died on March 9, 2002 at age 90. Formerly of the Bronx. Beloved wife of William. The Board of Directors extends its sympathy to Loan Officer Paul Schneiderman on the death of his mother. She was a courageous woman who jumped off a Nazi death train. Condolences to children, Roberta and Larry, Paul and Hanita, grandchildren, Michelle, Joe, Jesse, and David and great grandchildren, Justin, Emma, and Zoe. Progressive Credit Union Robert Familant, Treasurer
