
        RUSH-Al. Television industry leader for over 45 years peacefully passed away at home on January 14, 2003, in Beverly Hills, CA, age 76. He most recently served as Executive of Special Projects for Paramount Television Group and previously held a long career with MCA, becoming Chairman of MCA Television Group from 1986 to 1991. Also held Executive VP positions at NBC and NBC Sports. He will be fondly remembered for his communications expertise, strong personal ethics and the value he placed on the camaraderie of his colleagues. Al is survived by his three beloved sons Bruce (Lynn), Robert (Deirdre) and Jeffrey (Mary Ann), his seven treasured grandchildren, and cherished sister Rita Grobisen. For the last six years he was the devoted partner to Dale Engelson. He was a graduate of Columbia College and Columbia Law School. Funeral Services will be held on Tuesday, January 21, at First United Methodist Church, 1008 11th Street, Santa Monica, CA, at 12:30 PM.
