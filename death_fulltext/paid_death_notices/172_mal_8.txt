
        MARBACH-Rt. Rev. Monsignor Joseph F. Age 83 died April 28th at his home in West Palm Beach, FL. He was a graduate of Regis High School and Holy Cross College and was ordained in 1943. Msgr. Marbach received his doctorate in Canon Law and served for a few years on the Church Court in New York. He was then made Vicar General to Cardinal Spellman as Chancellor of the Military Ordinariate in charge of all Catholic chaplains in the U.S. Armed Forces around the world and served for over 30 years until his retirement in 1993. Msgr. Marbach is survived by his brothers, the Hon. John, Dr. Bernard, Jerome, Mrs. Anthony (Mary) Marano. He is predeceased by his brothers Robert, Francis and Lt. Paul J. Marbach. Visiting hours Tuesday (May1) 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at the McMahon, Lyon & Hartnett Funeral Home, 491 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 11 A.M. at Our Lady of Sorrows Church. Interment Mount Calvary Cemetery.
