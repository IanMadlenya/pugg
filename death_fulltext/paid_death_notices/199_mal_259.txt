
        STRAUSS--Rear Admiral Elliott B., USN, died August 19, 2003 in Washington, DC at the age of 100. He is survived by his widow, four children, four grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. The funeral will take place at the Ft. Meyer Chapel on September 10 at 9 AM. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to the Naval Historical Foundation, 1301 Dalgren Ave., SE, Washington Navy Yard, Washington, DC 20374.
