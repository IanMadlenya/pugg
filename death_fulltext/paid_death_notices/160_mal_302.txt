
        HAAS-Carl E., 87, of Sarasota, FL, on May 5. Formerly of Jamaica, Great Neck and Manhattan, NY. Beloved husband for 60 years of Roslyn (nee Schachne). Much loved and loving father of Edward J. of Rhinebeck, NY and David S. of Riverdale, NY, grandfather of Gregory J. of Boston, MA, and Elizabeth J. of Kitty Hawk, NC, greatgrandfather of Nicole Fegan, brother of Charlotte Reiss of Portland, OR, Bernard of Somers, NY and Phillip of Oceanside, NY. He obtained his Chartered Life Underwriter designation in 1938 and had a long and distinguished career in the life insurance industry as a general agent for the Continental Assurance Company. He was a former president of the Brooklyn Life Managers Association, the Great Neck Square Club, and the Brooklyn Kiwanis Club; former Master of the Rufus King Masonic Lodge 1091, Jamaica, NY, founder and charter member of Steppingstone Masonic Lodge 1141, a member of Temple Beth Sholom in Sarasota, and since 1981 was a member of Sahib Shrine Temple in Sarasota. Memorial donations may be made to a charity of your choice.
