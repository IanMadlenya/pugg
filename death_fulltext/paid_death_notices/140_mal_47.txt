
        SALTA-Romeo C. Aged 94, a pioneer of Northern Italian cuisine, passed away on August 31, 1998. Mr. Salta began his restaurant career in 1938 in Los Angeles, opening Chianti Restaurant, which became home to the most popular celebrities and business people. Following the shift of the entertainment industry to New York, Mr. Salta moved as well and in 1953 opened his world renowned restaurant on West 56th Street, which bore his name. Mr. Salta is survived by his wife Antoinette, his sons Salvatore, Romeo, and Remo, all of New York. Services and interment will be held in Italy. A Memorial Service will be held in New York at a later date. For information call Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 212-288-3500.
