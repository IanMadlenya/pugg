
        MURPHY--Andrew T., 83, originally of Chicago, but a longtime New Yorker, died Thursday, July 1. Beloved uncle of Sharon (Lloyd) Nelson, great uncle of Gail (Paul) Claytor, Craig (Kathy) Nelson, Jeffrey (Dina) Nelson and greatgreat uncle of five. Andy was an actor, a member of the Screen Actors Guild, American Federation of TV Actors and was associated with the TV show Saturday Night Live for over 25 years. He also served in the United States Army during World War II. A memorial service in Chicago is pending.
