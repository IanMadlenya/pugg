
        GEIGER-Mildred Kaufman, on October 31, 2002. Adored and adoring wife of the late Howard, mother of Stephen, Julian and Jeanne, grandmother of Teya, David, Rachel and Michael, sister of Stanley Kaufman, Sheldon Kaufman and the late Bernard, Maurice and Julian Kaufman. Cherished friend and mentor to many. Funeral service will be held on Monday, November 4th, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Interment to follow at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. For those so inclined, contributions to the Women's Campaign for UJAFederation would be meaningful.
        GEIGER-Mildred Kaufman. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Mildred Geiger, a dear friend of UJA-Federation. Known for her warmth, love, and devotion to the Jewish people, Mildred served in many leadership roles, including chair of the Women's Campaign and member of the Board of Directors. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her sons, Stephen & Julian, & to the entire Geiger and Kaufman families. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O. Joan Ginsburg, Chair of the Women's Campaign
