
        BLAUNER-Wendy. Age 91, died peacefully at her home in Palm Beach, Florida, on November 27, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Milton. She is survived by her son Jon and her daughter-in-law Irma, three grandchildren, Michael, Wendy and Andrew, and six great-grandchildren Joshua, Julia, Molly, Matthew, Katie and Benjamin. Born January 17 in Manchester, England, she came to the United States in 1937. She maintained a residence in Woodmere, NY until 1986, and then moved to Palm Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers her family requests that donations be made to the Alzheimer's Foundation. A private graveside ceremony will take place at 11 AM on Sunday, December 1, 2002, at the Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey.
