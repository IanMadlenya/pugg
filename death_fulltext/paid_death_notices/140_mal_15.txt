
        BEDELL-Sol. Died peacefully on August 31 in his 80th year. A native of New York City where he worked as a social worker, he relocated to Philmont, N.Y. in 1983 where he was active in the mental health movement. In 1991 he retired to Spring Hill, FL. to spend his years continuing his volunteer service to troubled youths. He leaves behind wife Doris, three wonderful children and their families, and four grandchildren.
