
        DAVIDIAN-Grace A. Beloved wife of David, loving mother of Peter (JoAnne) and Katy (Heros). Devoted grandmother to Olivia, Ani, Salbi and Alisha. Beloved sister to Lawrence and Florence Shahbegian. Grace was a remarkably beautiful woman known for her creativity as an artist and fashion illustrator for more than 50 years. Noted for her great wit, incredible courage and her caring love of family, Grace was much loved and will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral service 10 AM, Tuesday, St. Gregory's Armenian Church, White Plains, NY. Visiting Monday, 7-9 PM, Edwards - Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Avenue, Dobbs Ferry. Memorials to her church.
