
        DREXLER-Lynne Mapp, 71 died December 30, 1999 at her Island home. Born at Newport News, VA, May 21, 1928, educated at Richmond Professional Institute, College of William and Mary and at Hunter College Graduate School. Was a prolific painter, exhibited her work in New York, Los Angeles, Honolulu, and Maine. Survived by husband John Hultberg of New York City. Funeral service January 31 at the Monhegan Community Church. Donations to: Lynne Drexler Memorial Fund, PO Box 366, Monhegan Island, ME 04852. Information: Davis Funeral Home, 35 Knox St., Thomaston, ME 04861.
