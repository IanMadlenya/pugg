
        WEINSTEIN - Rosetta. Passed away Tuesday, March 2, 1999 at the age of 92. Services will be held at Riverside, 21 West Braod Street, Mt. vernon Friday, March 5 at noon. Mrs. Weinstein founded in 1928 the first private vocational school for medical and dental technicians. She served as President of Sutton Place Chapter of Hadassah, where she was known for her outstanding abilities as speaker and fundraiser. She is survived by her daughter, Dr. Aileen Kass; grandchildren Madeline June, Dr. Robin May Banks, Michael R. Banks and Sharon September; and great-granddaughter, Paige Daniela Banks.
