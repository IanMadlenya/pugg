
        STAUB-Susan. 54, a New City resident of 27 years, died on Wednesday, March 24, 1999 in Nyack Hospital. Beloved wife of Michael and devoted mother of Amy and Craig. Susan was the middle school science teacher and lower school science coordinator at Reuben Gittelman Hebrew Day School for the past 18 years. She was a member of the Coordination Committee for the Rockland County Science, Inventions and Technical Exhibition, a member of N.O.W., where she was involved in the Women's Health Task Force and a founding member of the Mother's Center of Rockland County. Susan was actively involved in health and environmental awareness education. We shall cherish the memory of a great woman and an inspirational teacher forever
