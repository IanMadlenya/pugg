
        SCHUSTER-Victor. Retired architect. 68. Passed away Monday, November 29, from respiratory failure at St. Barnabas Hospital in New Jersey. He is survived by his beloved wife of forty-nine years, Magda. He is also remembered by their two children Zsuzsa and Andras, and six loving grandchildren. Victor fled the oppression of the Romanian government and emigrated here in 1968 from Koloszvar, Transylvania. He will be dearly missed by all.
