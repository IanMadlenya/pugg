
        STRAUS-Hilde. Passed away peacefully in her sleep on Sunday August 26, 2001. She was convalescing at th Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale following a serious illness which befell her earlier this year. Beloved wife of 50 years of Walter, mother-in-law of Orit and Joseph Straus and Debbie and Carlo Bachrach, grandmother of Irad, Itai, Amit, Michelle and Jonathan. Sister of Tirzah and Carlo Rothschild and sister-in-law of Frances Rothschild. A woman of great beauty, her poise, faith, charitable and caring nature and her great strength of character will forever be an inspiration to all who knew her. Funeral services at ''The Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 180 W 76th Street, on Tuesday August 28, at 11:30 AM.
        STRAUS-Hilde. We extend sincerest condolences to our dear friend Walter Straus on the loss of his beloved wife. Together, as Diamond Founders of the Medical Center and supporters of the Annual Dinner and Annual Concert for decades, they made a vital and lifesaving contribution to providing thousands of people in Jerusalem with state-of-the-art medical care. Their warm smile and generous heart have played an important role in our efforts to build and strengthen Israel. May the warm memories of the time spent together help to console him. May Walter, children Dr. Joseph Straus and Mrs. Debbie Bachrach and siblings Tirza Rothschild and Dr. Carlo Rothschild be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem
        STRAUS-Hilde. Riverdale Jewish Center extends condolences to our member, Walter Straus, upon the loss of his wife. May the families be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi David Sable, President
