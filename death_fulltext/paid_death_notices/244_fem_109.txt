
        SCHNEIER--Yetta, 94, of Manhattan and Boca Raton. Died May 11, 2007. Wife of the late Nat Schneier. Mother of Alan Schneier and wife Leah and Maxine and Joseph Copulsky. Adoring grandmother of Erica, Dena, Nicole, Adrienne, and Ilene and greatgrandmother of Sydney, Gabriel, and Alexander. She will be greatly missed by her family and friends.
