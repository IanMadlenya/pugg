
        GELERNT--Jules, PhD, 74, of Monroe Twp., NJ (formerly of The Bronx) on June 17, 2003. Retired Professor of English & Comparative Literature and former Chair of English Department of Brooklyn College. Professor Gelernt received his BA and MA in English from City College, and PhD in English & Comparative Literature from Columbia University. His teaching career spanned more than 45 years, continuing after his official retirement, and also extended to his children and grandchildren. Husband of Rosalie (Sacks). Father of Mark David Gelernt and his wife Jana and Karen Gelernt and her husband Moshe BenEzra. Grandfather of Anya, Eva and Edward Gelernt and Sara and Ariel Ben-Ezra. Memorial gifts may be made to National Public Radio or PBS. Services were private. For information Mount Sinai Memorial Chapels, East Brunswick, NJ. >>AD#
