
        KENNEDY-Henry M. March 14, 1997. Of Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, N.J. Husband of the late Mary Bingham Kennedy. He graduated from Kent School in 1928 and Princeton University in 1932. He is survived by two sons Henry M. Jr of Ridgewood, N.J. and Bingham of McLean, Va. and 5 grandchildren. A past Vice President of the Prudential Insurance Company, he served as President of the Life Advertisers Association and as a Director of the Association of National Advertisers. His interest in health care lead to his serving as the first Chairman of the New Jersey State Health Planning Council, as well as other regional councils. He served as a trustee of the Hospital Center at Orange Foundation. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Presbyterian Homes Foundation, P.O. Box 2184, Princeton, N.J. 08543.
