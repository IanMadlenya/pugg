
        BLASENHEIM--Paul. On September 24, 2004, at the age of 80, of Lambertville, NJ. Beloved husband of his beautiful wife of 59 years, Gloria. Best father of all time to his daughters, Lois, Laura, Joanne and Beth. Adored grandfather of Max and Alex. ''Pinny'' to his family and friends in the Bronx. ''Blitz'' to his WWII band of brothers and ''Chef Paul'' to the multitudes he fed through the years. He never gave up and he will never be forgotten. Graveside services on Wednesday, September 29, at 12 noon, at the Rose Hill/King David Cemetery, 101 Mill St, Putnam Valley, NY 10579.
