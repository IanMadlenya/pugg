
        ALLISON-James C., 73. Died Friday, July 2, 1999, at Hospice By The Sea, Boca Raton, FL, after a brief illness. Mr. Allison, who had served as a volunteer at the Hospice, died of complications from lung cancer. He was a resident of Delray Beach, FL. Born January 23, 1926, in Live Oak, FL, Mr. Allison was a retired fashion designer and had moved to Florida 13 years ago from New York City and Connecticut. He had attended Washington University in St. Louis, MO, and was a graduate of Parsons School of Design, New York City. He was an Air Force veteran of World War II, serving in the Pacific theater. Mr. Allison is survived by his companion, James N. Baldwin, of Delray Beach; a sister, Ann Montgomery, of Talladega, AL; a brother, Edward, of Fort Worth, TX; and four nephews. A memorial service was held Wednesday at Unity Church of Delray Beach. Contributions in his name may be made to Hospice By The Sea, 1581 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL 33431.
