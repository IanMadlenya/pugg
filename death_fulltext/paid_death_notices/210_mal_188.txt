
        DOERFLER--Leo G., Ph.D. Age 85, on July 7. Husband of Alice, now deceased, father of Dennis, Donald, David, and Ann; 11 grandchildren. Dr. Doerfler was considered one of the founding fathers in the field of audiology. He established The Audiology Dept at The University of Pittsburgh, where he was full professor and Director for almost thirty years, and thereafter had a distinguished career in private practice. He published widely on various issues of audiology, served as President of The American Speech and Hearing Association, and was the recipient of many honors and awards in the field.
