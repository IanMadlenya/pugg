
        FEINGOLD-Norman S., 78. On October 24, 2001 after a long and courageous fight against lymphoma. Born in Brooklyn, NY on December 11, 1922. Most dearly beloved husband of Natalie (Arond). Cherished father of Carol Feingold Neiditch, Roberta Palant (Hy Shuster), Elise Feingold. Proud and loving grandfather of Shana, Emily, David, Jana and Mitchell. Pre-deceased by his sister Ruth Kopilow. Graduate of CCNY School of Engineering (1943) & member of the SKT fraternity family. Innovative, introspective, logical & analytical. An independent person of high moral standards and integrity, he lived by his principles & imparted them to his children and grandchildren. True to his personal heritage and proud supporter of American manufacturing industry. Mentor to many. He was highly respected and left a lasting impression on everyone who met him. Truly one of the Greatest Generation. Contributions may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 1311 Mamaroneck Ave, White Plains, NY 10605 or the American Red Magen David for Israel, 888 7 Av, Ste 403, NYC 10102.
