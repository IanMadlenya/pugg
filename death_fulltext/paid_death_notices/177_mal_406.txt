
        PURDY-Frazier R. 71, longtime creative director of Young & Rubicam Advertising, at his home in Roxbury, CT, on Saturday, October 27, 2001. Before retiring in 1991 he had a 37 year career at Y & R, starting as an assistant art director in 1954. It culminated in his becoming senior vice president, creative director of Y & R New York in 1975; Senior Vice President, Creative Director of Y & R USA in 1979; and executive vice president and executive creative director of Young & Rubicam Inc. Worldwide in 1984. He is survived by his wife, the former Susan Soroko; his twin sister, Noa Purdy Politi of Southbury, CT, formerly of Darien, CT, three sons from a previous marriage, Mark, Kevin, and Christopher and four grandchildren Ray, Alan, Keith, and Julia, all of whom live in California. He is also survived by his former wife, Joan Wolverton Smith Purdy. There will be a memorial service at Lillis, 58 Bridge St, New Milford, CT, on Monday, November 5, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the New Milford Visiting Nurses Association, 68 Park Lane Rd, New Milford, CT 06776.
