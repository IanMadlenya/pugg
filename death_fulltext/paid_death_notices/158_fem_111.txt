
        LIFSHUTZ-Frieda. Resident of the Sarah R. Neuman Nursing Home in Mamaroneck and formerly of Brighton Beach, Brooklyn. Passed away Saturday, March 11, at the age of 100 years. She was predeceased by her husband Max and sons Charles and B. William Laudor. She also leaves her daughter-in-law Ruth Laudor, five grandsons, Richard, Daniel, Michael, Seth and Ian Laudor, and a great-grandchild Joshua Brandon Laudor. A graveside service is scheduled for Tuesday, 11 AM, in the Washington Cemetery, Deans, NJ. For information call George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle, NY, 914-632-0324.
