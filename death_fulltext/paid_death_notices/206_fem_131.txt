
        SCHUCKMAN -- Elaine. 79. Died on March 12, 2004, after a brief illness. Devoted wife of the late Herman. Loving mother to Gil and Aileen, Nancy and Jim, and predeceased by daughter Roberta. Grandmother to Sasha, Matthew, Amanda and A.J. Sister of Sheila and dear friend of Jessica. Public school educator for many years. She will be missed by her many friends and colleagues.
