
        MEDBY-Magnus Erling. Born in Tromsoe, Norway, Magnus was a resident of Staten Island, N.Y. He died April 29, 2002 at 70 years of age. Beloved & devoted husband of Louise (Wechsler) & cherished brother-in-law of Joyce Wechsler. He leaves family in Norway: a brother, two sisters, children Per Tore and Mary Ann, and six grandchildren. He spent most of his working life in the Norwegian merchant marine rising to Chief Engineer, then thirteen years as Building Manager at Helen Keller Services. In lieu of flowers, donations to the charity of your choice.
