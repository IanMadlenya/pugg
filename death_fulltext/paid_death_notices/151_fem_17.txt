
        ADLER-Else nee Feitel. Peacefully in her 90th year, at home on July 25, 1999 in Great Neck, NY following a long illness. Wife of the late Dr. Hans Adler. Survived by her children: Madeleine Conway of Westport, CT, Dorothy Bennett of Falls Church, VA., and Jacqueline Adler Walker (Arthur) of Scarsdale, NY. Devoted grandmother of Ann Bennett, Andrew Conway, Victoria Newman and Alexandra Conway and beloved great grandmother of Zachary, Arielle, Jonathan and Jared. Contributions in her name may be made to the charity of your choice. Private services were held.
