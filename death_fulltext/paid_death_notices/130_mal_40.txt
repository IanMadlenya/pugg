
        BOODMAN - Julius, of Long Beach, New York on November 3, 1997. Born December 25, 1908 in Brooklyn, New York. A retired retailer. Died of heart failure, after a long, honorable, productive and fruitful life. Survived by his wife Helen of 64 years, a son Richard, of New York City and Long Beach, New York, a son Barry of Stamford, Connecticut, and two grandsons, Ben and Charlie, of Boulder, Colorado. Services will be private at graveside. There will be no calling hours. Donations to any of the following charities in lieu of flowers would be appreciated: The Anti-Defamation League of B'nai B'rith, The Southern Poverty Law Center, The Simon Wiesenthal Center, United Jewish Federation.
