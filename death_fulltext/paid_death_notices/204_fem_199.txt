
        HERSHAFT--Florence, 89, on January 18, 2004. Devoted wife of Philip. Adoring mother of Helen Divak. Mother-inlaw of Arthur Divak and Nancy Hershaft. Services are today, 12:15 PM, at ''Sinai Chapels,'' Fresh Meadows, NY.
