
        CARR-Dorothy Healey, born on July 17, 1917, loving wife of Peter, & mother of Kevin, Brian, Michael, Kelly Anne & Colin, passed away peacefully June 25, 2000 at the San Diego Hospice. Dorothy suffered with CHF, COPD, HTN, Dementia of Alzheimer's Type. Following a family service at the Church of Nativity, Rancho Santa Fe, CA, her ashes were scattered at sea. After graduating Cum Laude from Trinity College, Washington D.C., Dorothy served in the last class of The Women's Auxiliary Service Pilots (WASPS) in World War II. Dorothy & Peter Carr were married January 23, 1954 at St. Patrick's Cathedral, NY where they resided until they moved to Greenwich, CT. in 1957. In 1983, the Carrs moved to Rancho Santa Fe, CA. In addition to her husband, Peter, Dorothy is survived by four of their five children. Michael passed away in 1995. Kevin, wife Randa & daughter Caroline reside in Simsbury, CT. Brian, his wife Nancy & their children Ryan, Victoria & Daniel are living in Cos Cob, CT. Kelly Anne, husband Neil Winterbottom & two girls, Caitlin & Jennifer live in San Mateo, CA. Colin currently resides in Terrebonne, OR. Those wishing to honor the memory of Dorothy Carr may make donations to the San Diego Hospice, 4311 Third Avenue, San Diego, CA. 92103.
