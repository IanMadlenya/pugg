
        LEVI-Enrico. The Board of Trustees, President David C. Chang, Chancellor George Bugliarello and other members of the Polytechnic University community are saddened by the passing of Enrico Levi on January 30, 2000. Professor Levi was a 1956 and 1958 graduate in electrical engineering, Polytechnic professor for twenty-five years and recipient of the University's 1998 Distinguished Alumnus Award & numerous international awards for his work in electrical machinery and magnetoplasma technology. We are honored to have known such a brilliant and inspiring teacher and express our heartfelt condolences to his family.
