
        MAIER--Bonnie Bogard. TV producer, friend and mentor to many, died Tuesday in Los Angeles after a heroic, five year battle with cancer, never surrendering. Survived by her daughter Molly Rose, brother Paul Bogard and mother Lorraine Bogard, as well as a legacy of strength, joy and creativity. Preceded in death by her husband, Bonnie will be buried at his side. The funeral will be Sunday, Feb. 1, 11AM, at Brueggemann FH, 522 Larkfield Rd., East Northport. Contributions can be made to the Revlon Run/Walk for Women, PO Box 4059, Santa Monica, California 90411. Courageous, passionate, intelligent, adorable, we will miss every freckle on her face.
