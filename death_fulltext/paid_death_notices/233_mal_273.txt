
        MARCHIONY--Lawrence, Jr. of Montclair, NJ on June 15. Larry served as VP of Creative Development for the Howard/Marlboro Group in New York City, co-foundedHidden Valley ski area in Vernon, NJ and was a founding member of the Montclair Ski Club. He was a grandson of Italo Marchiony, inventor of the ice cream cone. He is survived by sons David, Bill, John, his brother Bill, and his best friend, Susan Goodman. A memorial service is planned for 2 PM on Sunday, July 9, at the Montclair Art Museum. In lieu of flowers, please contribute to the Montclair Art Museum Art School Scholarship Fund.
