
        KAUFMAN -- Jerry. Dear brother of Carol Goldberg and her husband Arthur; uncle of Mark, Beth, Josh, and Penny; and great uncle of Samantha, Jason and Laelah. Died suddenly and unexpectedly at age 77. Publicist, playwright and ardent lover of theater, film and New York City. He had the love of many relatives and friends, to whom he was lovingly loyal. His good cheer, optimism, sense of humor and sweet heart will be missed. Funeral rites to be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, Friday, December 10th at 11:45 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be sent to the American Cancer Society in memory of his father Mark Kaufman.
