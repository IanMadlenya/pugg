
        WRIGHT-Damon, playwright, 48, on December 28, 1998, after a long illness. Beloved son of Artis and Birdell Wright, and loving brother of Joni and Lydia, all of Long Beach, California, and of Carter and Rosemary, his sister-in-law, of Jefferson, TX. He will be sorely missed by his family and friends. Contributions in his memory may be made to New York Theater Workshop, 79 East Fourth Street, New York, New York 10003. A memorial gathering will be held in SoHo in Manhattan on Saturday, January 2, at 3 P.M. at 113 Prince Street, Apartment 4-EF, between Greene and Wooster Streets. For information 212-502-3502.
