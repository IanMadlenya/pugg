
        TIEFFENBERG-Leon, 86, died March 8, 1998 at his apartment in Manhattan after a long illness. He is survived by his wife Josefina Rubinstein Tieffenberg; his daughter Magdalena Tieffenberg Tosteson of Boston and her husband Daniel Tosteson and their two children Joshua and Ingrid; and his son Jacobo Tieffenberg of Buenos Aires and his wife Irene Woods Tieffenberg and his children Gustavo, Jorge, Raul, Veronica and Ilan. In his memory, please send contributions to the Leon and Josefina Tieffenberg Fellowship Fund, Harvard Medical School, 25 Shattuck St., Boston, Ma. 02115.
