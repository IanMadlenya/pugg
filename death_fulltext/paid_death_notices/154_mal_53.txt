
        WOLINS-Joseph, on November 3, 1999, devoted husband of the late Selma, adored father of the late Sarah of Sag Harbor, New York, survived by sons John of Philadelphia, PA, Richard Lazaar of Tarrytown, NY and David Lazaar of the Bronx, NY, beloved brother of Louise Page of Barstow, CA, five grandchildren, one great-grandchild and numerous cousins. Mr. Wolins was an accomplished artist and was represented in the permanent collections of numerous museums worldwide. His warmth, wit and unique personality will be sorely missed. No memorial service is planned. Donations in his memory may be made to the Epilepsy Society, 257 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10017.
