
        LONDON-Ruth. Peacefully died January 26, 1997. Loving mother of Roberta Martell and Sheila Mandell; grandma of Troy, Philip, Elyse and Peter; sister of Shirley Rubinstein and sister-in-law of Martha Walter; and dear aunt, great-aunt, cousin and friend, who fought against injustice and worked for peace and union rights. She will be missed, remembered and loved. Friends and family may call at 2630 Cropsey Avenue, Apartment L5, Brooklyn, NY, on Sunday, February 2, 1997, 2-5 PM.
