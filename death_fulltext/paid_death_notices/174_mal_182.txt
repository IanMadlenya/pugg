        COWEN-Arthur S. Overseas Shipholding Group, Inc. is saddened over the passing of Arthur S. Cowen, beloved father of our friend and colleague, Robert N. Cowen, our Chief Operating Officer and member of our Board of Directors. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to Bob, his family and loved ones.
