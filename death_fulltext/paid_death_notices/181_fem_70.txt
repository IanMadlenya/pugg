
        PROSKE-Beatrice Gilman. Died February 2, at the age of 102. She was curator of sculpture at The Hispanic Society of America in New York City for 53 years and an honorary trustee at Brookgreen Gardens in South Carolina. Memorial contributions may be sent to The Hispanic Society of America, 613 West 155th Street, NY, NY 10032 or Brookgreen Gardens, Murrells Inlet, S.C. 29576.
