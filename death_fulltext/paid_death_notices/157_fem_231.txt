
        MAJTHENYI-Gabriella, 91 of White Plains, NY. Died February 23, 2000 of a heart attack. She was born August 21, 1908 in Pecs, Hungary to Ilona and Joseph Roboz. Gabriella is survived by her son Alexander Majthenyi of White Plains, her daughter Klara King of England and 5 grandchildren.
