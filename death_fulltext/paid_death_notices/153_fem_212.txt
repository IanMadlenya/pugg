
        WASSERMAN-Marilyn M. Wonderful wife of Arnold J. Wasserman for 52 years, beloved mother of Andrew Roy Wasserman and Joanne Dougan, and loved grandmother of Nici Wasserman and Fiona Starr Dougan. Died on Thursday, October 21, at Alta Bates Hospital in Berkeley, CA, from a cerebral hemorrhage. She also leaves a loving brother, Dr. Howard I. Maibach and sister-inlaw Siesel Wile Maibach. The burial will take place at 2 PM, on Tuesday, October 26, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery in Flushing, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the National Alliance for Breast Cancer.
