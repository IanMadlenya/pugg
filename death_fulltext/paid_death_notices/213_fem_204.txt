
        HELMAN--Minnie, age 94, on October 27. Beloved wife of the late Hon. Nathaniel T. Helman. Mother of Howard and Stephen. Grandmother of Corinne, Deborah and Maielle. Great-grandmother of Cary. Her life was filled with good deeds for community and family. She was loved deeply. Services Friday, 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
