
        STALLMAN-Daniel J., 77. Died November 14, 2001. Predeceased by his parents Frieda Kranz & Charles Stallman. and son Adam S. Stallman. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to Simon Wiesenthal Center, Tribute Dept, 1399 South Roxbury, Los Angeles, CA. 90035.
