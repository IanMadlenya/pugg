
        WILMOT-Virginia L. A resident of Greenwich and Nantucket, died suddenly Wednesday, August 26, at Greenwich Hospital. She was 61 years old and died of natural causes. Mrs. Wilmot is survived by her husband, Clifford R. Wilmot, her mother, Laura Catalano Lancellotti of Garden City, NY, and her brother, William G. Lancellotti, Jr. of Williamsburg, VA. In addition, Mrs. Wilmot is survived by six stepchildren, eight grandchildren, and one great-grandchild. Mrs. Wilmot had a long career in the advertising industry working for such companies as McCannErickson, Pepsico, The Magnavox Company, and Richard K. Manoff, Inc. Born on October 27, 1936, she was the daughter of Laura Lancellotti and the late William G. Lancellotti. She graduated from Centenary College for Women and the Katharine Gibbs School. Friends may call at the Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch Street, Greenwich, CT, Sunday, August 30th from 2-5 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Monday, August 31 at 10AM, St. Mary's Church, 178 Greenwich Avenue, Greenwich. Donations may be made in her memory to the Greenwich Hospital Association, 31 Perryridge Rd.
