
        GOLDSCHMID-Rose. A longtime NYC resident died on January 11, 1999. She was 100. Born June 18, 1898 to Abraham and Tillie Brown Zeigler in Wilmington, DE, Rose married the late Elliot. Survived by nieces and nephews Robert and Nancy Sylvor, Richard and Toby Gamsu, R. Anthony and Mona Zeigler and Lynne Zeigler. Grandnieces and grandnephews Elyse and Jeffrey, Andrew and Lauren, Andy and Theresa and Jeffrey. Predeceased by brothers David and Irving and sister Molly Gamsu.
