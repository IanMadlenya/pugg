
        STOLBOF--Mary Elizabeth (Betty). Of Syosset, NY, passed away on January 7, 2006, after a brave battle with cancer. Beloved wife of the late Bernard Stolbof. Loving mother of Paul, Edward, Jesse and Daniel. Dear grandmother of Stephanie, Matthew, Andrew, Henry and Catherine. Sister of Richard Sonia. Mother-in-law of Lorie and Debbie. Graduate of Kent Place School and Mills College. She enjoyed tennis, golf, bird watching, the arts, crossword puzzles, bridge and the circle of friends who joined her in these activities. She was an inspiration to all she touched and lived with tremendous grace, dignity and courage. We will miss her very much. Donations can be made in her memory to the American Cancer Society or Hospice Care Network of Long Island.
