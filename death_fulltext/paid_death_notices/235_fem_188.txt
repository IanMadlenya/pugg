
        SULLIVAN--Amely. Her family and friends are saddened by her sudden death on Friday, August 25th. Daughter Janet, sister Steffi, son-in-law Armand and many others knew her as a woman of valor, gallantry and brilliance. Vivacious, literary, proud and cultivated, her spirit and light will shine on. Funeral Wednesday, August 30th, 11:30 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave
