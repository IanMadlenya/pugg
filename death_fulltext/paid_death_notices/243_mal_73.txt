
        WINN--Rodger J., M.D., 69, died of esophageal cancer on April 4. Winn, an expert in quality of cancer care, was a consultant to NQF, chaired NCCN's Guidelines Steering Committee and was EditorIn-Chief of JNCCN. He founded MD Anderson's CCOP program. He was on the boards of Planned Parenthood and Capital Area Hospice. Winn is survived by wife, Patricia; son, Matthew; daughter, Amanda; three grandchildren, William, Nicholas and Fielden; and sister, Dilys Winn. There will be a memorial at the Cosmos Club, in Washington, DC at 11 AM on April 14.
