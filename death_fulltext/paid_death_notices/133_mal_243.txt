
        GOLDSMITH-Charles. Age 90. Of West Palm Beach, FL, died February 14, 1998 at Good Samaritan Medical Center there. He is survived by Lee, his wife of 60 years, and by son Henry and daughter-inlaw Tessa and was predeceased by his son James Theodore. A 1929 graduate of Dartmouth College, he devoted his career to Metro Goldwyn Mayer Pictures Inc. Starting in 1931 as an Assistant Manager in Brazil, his career spanned 31 years, culminating as Chairman and Managing Director of MGM the United Kingdom. A Memorial Service will be held at 2 PM on Wednesday, February 18th at Fountainview, 101 Executive Center Drive, West Palm Beach, FL. The family suggests that donations may be made to the American Cancer Society in lieu of flowers.
