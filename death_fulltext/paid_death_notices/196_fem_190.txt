
        GAULT--Louise Mellor, in New York City on May 23, 2003 of complications from Myelodisplastic Syndrome (MDS), a blood marrow disorder. She had been battling other forms of cancer for many years. She was 68. Beloved wife of Stanley A. Bowker of New York City. Louise is also survived by two brothers: Robert, of West Newton, Massachusetts; and James, of Rockford, Illinois. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday May 26th 6-8PM. Services to follow at 8PM. Interment will take place on Thursday May 29th at the Allegheny Cemetery, Pittsburgh, PA. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York City Ballet. >>AD#
