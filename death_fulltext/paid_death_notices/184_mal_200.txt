
        LANDSMAN-Irving. 94. On May 12 of Long Beach, New York. With deep affection we mourn the loss of the beloved husband of Shirley, devoted father of Richard, Linda and Jules, loving grandfather of Lena, Benjamin, Kira and Nerys. Noted attorney, valuation expert, entrepreneur, teacher, author, civic leader, equestrian, swimmer, and track athlete. Strength, courage and drive be his forever. Interment at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, New York. Donations may be sent to the Irving Landsman Memorial Scholarship Fund, Long Beach High School, Chairperson, Guidance Department, Lido Beach, New York 11561
