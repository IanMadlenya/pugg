
        GOLDSTEIN -- Herbert. Professor Emeritus of Nuclear Science and Engineering at Columbia University, on January 12, 2005, at age 82. Noted for his scholarship on classical mechanics and reactor shielding, Dr. Goldstein received the E.O. Lawrence Award from the U.S. Department of Energy and the Great Teacher Award from Columbia. His book, ''Classical Mechanics'', has been the standard text in the field since 1950. He was a founding member and served as president of the Association of Orthodox Jewish Scientists. He is survived by his wife Channa, his children, Penina, Aaron Meir, and Shoshanna, and ten grandchildren.
