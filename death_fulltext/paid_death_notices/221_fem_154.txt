Adored grandmother of Josh, Jenny, Alex, Elizabeth, Jane, Peyton, Noah and Jamie. Loving mother-in-law of Jacob, David and Nancy. Darling Aunt Bebe to her nieces and nephews. ''Many daughters have attained valor, but you have surpassed them all.''
