
        METZGER--Angela (Serpe). At rest on November 29th, 2006 in Summit, NJ. Angela was a mother, homemaker, published writer, avid reader, active churchwoman and volunteer. She was born in 1938 in Chicago. She valued most the love that is nurtured in families and grounded in faith. Her husband of 49 years, five children, nine grandchildren and three sisters survive Angela. In lieu of flowers, contributions in Angela's memory may be sent to Oak Knoll School at 44 Blackburn Road, Summit, NJ 07901 or your favorite charity. Services are with William R. Dangler Funeral Home, Summit, NJ, 908-273-2323 Requiescat in pace.
