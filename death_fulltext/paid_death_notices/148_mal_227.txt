
        LAWRENCE-Rev. Dennis. Of Old Greenwich, CT. Died May 13th. Ordained Deacon of the Anglican Church of the Advent in Greenwich. Fleet Chaplain Old Greenwich Yacht Club. Former member RAF Squadron 206. Survived by his wife Jane E. (Crusoe) Lawrence, daughters Hilary Lawrence, Avery L. Belicka, Alison L. Buxton and five grandchildren.
