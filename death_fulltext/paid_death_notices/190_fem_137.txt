
        KEISER-Ruth. The Board of Trustees of the Jewish Board of Family and Children Services notes with sadness the death of Ruth Keiser, beloved grandmother of Laurie Netter Sprayregen; a devoted agency trustee and past Chair of the Ittleson Center. Mrs. Keiser was a generous supporter of JBFCS and Ittleson, where a room is named in memory of her late husband Harry Keiser. Our condolences to Laurie, her parents, Alice and Richard Netter, and the entire family. Jean L. Troubh, President Joseph S. Kaplan, Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, PhD, Exec VP & CEO
        KEISER-Ruth. The Ackerman Institute for the Family shares the sadness of our Board Member, Alice Netter, at the loss of her mother. Ruth and her late husband Harry were true supporters for many years. We wish to express our condolences to the entire family during this most difficult time. Arthur M. Maslow, MSW Chairman, Board of Trustees Peter Steinglass, MD Executive Director
