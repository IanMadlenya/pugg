
        POMPOSELLO-Joseph R. Of Yonkers, died May 5, 2002 at age 60. Born November 5, 1941 in the Bronx to Arthur and Connie Bellafatto Pomposello. He was a hair stylist, NYC dance instructor and award - winning ballroom/Latin dancer. Survived by brothers, Arthur and Thomas Pomposello. Visitation on Thursday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Sinatra Memorial Home, 601 Yonkers Ave, Yonkers, NY. Funeral Mass, Friday, 10 AM at St. Francis Chapel, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
