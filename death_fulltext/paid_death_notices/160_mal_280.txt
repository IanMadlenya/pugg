
        GOLDBERG-Alan L., M.D. The Bronx County Medical Society expresses profound sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member and past President, Alan L. Goldberg, M.D. His unselfish devotion to his patients, his students and his fellow physicians will always be remembered. We shall miss our colleague and dear friend, and extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Marilyn Pearl, M.D., President Koku Gasinu, M.D., Secretary
