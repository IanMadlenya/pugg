
        STURGIS-Norman R. Of Fort Myers, FL. and Small Point, ME. died of cancer on February 4, 1998. He is survived by Linda Terry Sturgis, his wife of fifty-five years, two sons, Nathaniel R. Sturgis and John T. T. Sturgis, both of Missoula, MT, six grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Also survived by two sisters, Mrs. David T. Cook of St. James, NY and Mrs. Melvin E. Schoonover of Miami, Florida. Contributions in his memory to the Sanibel Captiva Conservation Foundation, Box 839, Sanibel, FL. 33957, would be greatly appreciated by his family.
