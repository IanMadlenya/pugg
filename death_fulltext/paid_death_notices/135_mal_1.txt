
        FINKLE-Joel. Ohr Torah Stone Institutions of Israel mourns with sadness the passing of Joel Finkle, a loving and beloved Jew, a philanthropist with vision and with heart, a self-made leader of his people. May his most courageous life partner, Maria, find the solace and strength to continue her noble endeavors on behalf of our people. Shlomo Riskin, Dean Fred Ehrman, Pres
        FINKLE-Joel. The Trustees of the Elaine Kaufman Cultural Center (formerly The Hebrew Arts School) mourn the passing of Joel Finkle who gave the center his caring, concern and support during its early days. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Leonard Goodman, Chair Elaine Kaufman, President Lydia Kontos, Executive Director
