
        DAVIS-F. Kelso, 83. Of Boca Grande, FL and Essex, CT, died August 3 after a brief illness. Born on February 23, 1916, in Hartford, CT, he is survived by his wife, Joanne Manzani Davis and his five children: Jay Davis of Belfast, ME, Philip Davis of Westbrook, CT, Debbie Neely of Milton, MA, State Rep. Jefferson Davis of Pomfret, CT., and by Nelle Davis of Lyme, CT. and New York City. A memorial service will be held Friday, August 6 at 11:30 at St. John's Episcopal Church in Essex. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to the CT River Museum, Essex, The Middlesex Hospital, or The Oxford Academy, Westbrook, CT.
