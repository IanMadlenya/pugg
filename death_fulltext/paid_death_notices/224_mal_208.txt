
        WONG--Richard K. Habitat for Humanity-New York City mourns the loss of Richard K. Wong, a beloved colleague, who devoted his life to building homes, building community, and building brotherhood in the city he loved. Richard will be honored and remembered at a memorial service Friday, September 23rd, 11 A.M. at Lafayette Avenue Presbyterian Church, 85 South Oxford St., Bklyn, NY 11217. 718-625-7515. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to Lafayette Avenue Presbyterian Church's Capital Campaign.
