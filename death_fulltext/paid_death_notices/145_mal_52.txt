
        WOLFF-Ezra A., M.D. The membership of the Medical Society of the County of Queens express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their beloved colleague and friend, Ezra A. Wolff, M.D., on February 2, 1999. A distinguished and dedicated physician he served the Society well for over 50 years as President, Trustee, Secretary and Chairman of various committees. Our deepest sympathies to his daughter Lila Wolff Wilkinson and his granddaughter Eleanor Wilkinson. Richard A. Gasalberti, MD. President Fred N. Flatau, MD. Chairman Board of Trustees
