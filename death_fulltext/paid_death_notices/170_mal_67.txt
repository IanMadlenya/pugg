
        WALSH-H. Robert. Of New York, N.Y. Age 76. Beloved father of Meredith W. Walsh and Eliot W. Walsh. Brother of Francis X. Walsh. Passed away Wednesday, February 28, 2001. Born in Milton, MA., Mr. Walsh was a graduate of Harvard Univesity, Class of 1948. He served in the U.S. Army in Germany during WWII, attaining the rank of 1st Lt. Upon his graduation from Harvard, he received a Fulbright Scholarship to the Sorbonne in Paris, France. He then went on to earn a Master's Degree in Business from Columbia University. Bob spent his entire career at the advertising firm Young & Rubicam, Inc. in New York City where he ultimately became Senior Vice President and Director of the Strategy Board. He retired in 1987 and resided in Clinton, CT. and Palm Beach, FL. Memorial services will be held at the Church of Saint Thomas More, 65 E. 89 St, N.Y., N.Y. at 10:30AM on Thursday, March 8th. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made in his name to SpenceChapin, 6 E. 94 St, NY NY 10128.
