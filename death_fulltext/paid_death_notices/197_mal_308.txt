
        VITRIOL--Herbert A., of Toronto, formerly of Brookville, NY and Manhattan, died peacefully on June 23, 2003 at age 86. Beloved husband of Muriel (Courshon) for 57 years. Cherished father of Nancy V. Salzman (Lorne) of Toronto and William A. Vitriol (Laurie Ellen) of Manhattan. Loving grandfather of Eric and Lynn Vitriol, Mark and Jason Salzman. Services held June 26th. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. >>AD#
