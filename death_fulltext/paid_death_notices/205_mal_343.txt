
        RIKER--Walter F., Jr., M.D. We are deeply saddened by the passing of Dr. Walter Riker, former Chairman of the Department of Pharmacology at Weill Cornell Medical College and former Attending Physician at New YorkPresbyterian Hospital/Weill Cornell Medical Center. Dr. Riker spent his entire 53-year medical career at Weill Cornell and led the Department of Pharmacology for 27 years, beginning in 1956. A distinguished educator, clinician, and research scientist, among many achievements, he was responsible for major advancements in the diagnosis and treatment of neuromuscular diseases. Dr. Riker will be greatly missed. Our sincerest condolences are extended to his family. Dr. Antonio M. Gotto, Jr. Dean Weill Cornell Medical College Dr. Herbert Pardes President and CEO New York-Presbyterian Hospital
