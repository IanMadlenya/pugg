
        MAHER -- Edward John ''Ned''. Of Wilton, CT, formerly of Bayside, NY on March 8. Predeceased by his beloved wife, Lillian. Cherished father of Jayne (Don) Weitz, Judith Frederic, Beth Maher, and Ned Jr. (Lisa) Maher. Loving grandfather of Craig, Matthew, Leah and Kelsey. World War II U.S. Army Captain. Awarded the Silver Star, Bronze Star and two Purple Hearts. Retired Chairman and Chief Executive officer of H.C. Boerner, and retired Vice Chairman of The Harvey Group. His warmth, humor, courage and integrity made him loved by all who had the good fortune to know him. There will be a private memorial. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to United Spinal Association, 75-20 Astoria Blvd., Jackson Heights, NY 11370.
