
        CALLAWAY-Dorothy Harding. Of Southbury, CT, formerly of Cold Spring Harbor, LI. Beloved wife of the late Samuel R. Callaway, died peacefully on Sunday, October 31, 1999 at Danbury Hospital, Danbury, CT. She was 84 years old. She is survived by two daughters, Joan C. Pratt of Brentwood, NH, and Dorothy C. Munson of Richmond, MA; a son, Samuel R. Callaway, Jr. of Redding, CT; seven grandchildren, two step grandchildren and four great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the United Church of Christ, Southbury, CT, on Sunday, November 21 at 2:00 PM. A memorial service followed by interment will be held at a later time at St. John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor, LI 11754, where contributions in Mrs. Callaway's memory may also be made.
