
        VIDOCKLER-Murray, 79. Of Boca Raton, Florida, and New York. Passed away Jan. 29, 1999. He was the husband of Ruth. Father of Stuart (Lynda Clare) Vidockler of Boston, MA., and Francine (David Krigman) Vidockler of Newton, MA. Grandfather of Eliza and Rachel. Brother of Arthur (Belle) Vidockler of Lake Worth, Bernard (Helen) Vidockler of Wellington & Gertrude (Stanley Papkin) Hirsch of West Palm Beach. Mr. Vidockler was founder of Brighton Travel Bureau and British Caledonian Airlines, and a member of ASTA. He served in the Navy during WWII and was a graduate of Townsend Harris High School and City College of NY. Funeral service will be at 11 A.M., Tuesday, February 2, 1999 at Boulevard Riverside Chapel, 312 Coney Island Avenue ''at Prospect Park'' Brooklyn.
