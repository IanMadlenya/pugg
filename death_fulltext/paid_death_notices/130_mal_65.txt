
        MITCHELL-Leo. Age 76, after bravely fighting a long illness. Beloved husband of Claire. Cherished father of Judy, Gene, Gerry and Matt. Loving grandfather of Madeleine and Andrew. Devoted brother of Faye Bacal. Founder and owner of Command Electronics in Farmingdale, NY. His adoring family and many friends will miss him and will always remember his humor, generosity and gentle loving nature. Interment in Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY on November 7 at 1PM. The family requests that in lieu of flowers donations be made to the National Kidney Foundation or the American Heart Association.
