
        HEYERT-Murray, age 86, died unexpectedly on September 8, 1998 in the company of his beloved wife Florence of almost 65 years. He is also mourned by his daughter Elizabeth Heyert and her husband Douglas Elliott, his brother Arthur Heyert, and his brother inlaw Edwin Horen and his wife Myra. Memorial service was held in his honor in Tamarac, Florida. His family and friends will miss him greatly.
