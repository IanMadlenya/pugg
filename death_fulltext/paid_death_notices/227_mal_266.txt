
        BELLUCCI--Richard J., M.D. New York Medical College mourns the loss of our benefactor and friend, Richard J. Bellucci, M.D., former professor and chairman of the Department of Otolaryngology. Pioneering surgeon and skilled teacher of countless new physicians, he received many medical, civic and religious honors for his selfless contributions to humanity. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Rev. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett, President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D., Provost and Dean School of Medicine
