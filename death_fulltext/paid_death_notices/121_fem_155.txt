
        NUGENT-Helen Mara. Age 89. Died February 21, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Joseph C. Nugent and the late John V. Mara. Loving mother of Maura Mara Concannon and the late Timothy J. Mara. Dear sister of Lillian Phelan and Jeanne Rohrs. Cherished grandmother of Christine Geithner, Kathleen Anderson and Sheila Concannon, and great-grandmother of Peter and Clare Geithner. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., on Monday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Chirstian Burial St. Ignatius Loyola R.C. Church, Park Ave at 84th St., on Tuesday, 11:15 AM. Burial Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions to John V. Mara Cancer Research Unit, St. Vincent's Medical Center, 153 West 11 St., NYC 10011 or Catholic Big Sisters, 60 Lafayette St., NYC 10013.
        NUGENT-Helen Mara. The Sisters of Charity, Board of Trustees, medical staff, and Auxiliary of St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center note with sadness the passing of Helen Mara Nugent, beloved wife of the late Joseph C. Nugent and the late John V. Mara. Over the years, St. Vincent's has been blessed with a long association of friendship and generosity with Mrs. Mara Nugent. The entire St. Vincent's family is grateful for her philanthropic activities on behalf of the John V. Mara Cancer Research Fund at St. Vincent's. We express our heartfelt sympathy to the Mara and Nugent families. Karl P. Adler, M.D., President Thomas A. Coleman, Chairman of the Board St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center of NY
        NUGENT-Helen M. The Catholic Big Sisters sends its deepest sympathy to the family of Mrs. Joseph C. Nugent, who was a valued and longtime member of our Board.
