
        PARKS-Harold Freeman, retired actor and theater director in New York City for many years, died Wednesday, May 10, in Beth Israel Hospital. Born October 18, 1918 in Medford, MA, he was the son of the late David and Annette Robillard Parks and was a Captain in the U.S. Army Air Corps in World War II. He was a member of the Lambs Club and the Actors Equity Guild in New York City for many years. Mr. Parks is survived by three nephews, Dr. Richard P. Tobin of New Jersey, Ronald B. Tobin of California, and David F. Tobin of Connecticut, two nieces, Tracy A. Kirkpatrick and Linda R. Klugman, both of California, and a brother-in-law, Richard A. Tobin, also of California. Graveside services will be held on Saturday, May 13, 2000 at 10AM in Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan, CT. There will be no calling hours. For information contact F.
