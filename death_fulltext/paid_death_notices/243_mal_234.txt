
        WING--Earl, 85 years of age died at his Jersey City residence on Wednesday, April 18, 2007. Son of the late Alfred E. Wing, and Cornelia DeGraw. Born in Ontario, California he resided in New York City from 1944. He was employed at Walgreen's Soda Fountain on 42nd Street, before joining the McGraw-Hill Company in 1961 where he worked as a project manager until his retirement in 1999. He is survived by his son Willie Melia, and many dear friends. He had a passion for cooking, sense of humor, and generous spirit for life. Visitation Saturday evening 7-9pm, and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9pm, at the Introcaso-Angelo Funeral Home, 143 Brunswick Street, Jersey City, NJ 07302 (201653-2494). Private cremation to follow.
