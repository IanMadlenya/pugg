
        CUTLER--Beverly Waring. 86, of Bryn Mawr, PA and Edgartown, MA on Tuesday, January 6. Predeceased by her husband of 64 years, E. Newton Cutler Jr. Born February 6, 1917 in Plainfield, NJ, she graduated from Miss Porter's School in Farmington, CT. She is survived by her children Earl N. Cutler III, Michael W. Cutler, Carolyn C. Goodman and Allison C. Fox. Also survived by eleven grandchildren, ten greatgrandchildren and by her sister Carolyn W. Verbeck. A Memorial Service will be Friday, January 9 at 2pm at St. David's Episcopal Church, Wayne, PA. Memorial contributions may be made to Miss Porter's School, Main St, Farmington, CT, 06032. Or to Sheriff's Meadow Foundation, RR 1 Box 319X, Vineyard Haven, MA, 02568. Stuard Funeral Home, Ardmore, PA.
