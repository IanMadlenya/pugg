
        KANTERMAN-Edgar A., D.D.S. A devoted and beloved husband of Phyllis Cogin Kanterman. He was graduated from Tilden High School, City College & NYU School of Dentistry. In World War II, he was a Captain with the Military Police in Massachusetts, Maine, United Kingdom, France & Manila and served with great distinction. Then he established a dental practice in Bayside in 1949 and he also attended the Institute of Graduate Dentists under his mentor, Dr. Myron Lieb. At the school, he taught and ran the family dental clinic for many caring years, he was a member of the board of trustees. His hobby was photography, at which he spent many happy hours in the field and in the dark room as a member of the Great Neck Camera Club, where he had many of his photos shown.
