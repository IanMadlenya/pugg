
        WEILL-Hazel Cohen. Age 92, died June 17, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Robert David. Loving mother and mother-in-law of Barbara Weill and Michael Greenberg. Adored Bummy of Joanne and Buffy Weill-Greenberg. Hunter College Class of 1927. We love and miss her. Contributions can be made to groups working for peace, equality and social justice.
