
        STEIN-Dorothy Fracht, 94, Devoted wife of the late Samuel B. Stein, died March 22 at New York University hospital. For more than 50 years, Mrs. Stein was the publisher and president of Chemical Publishing of New York. She is survived by her two nephews, Wallace and Elliot Magaziner, her stepson, Peter Stein, and a large extended family. Her vibrant and enthusiastic approach to life will be missed by all privileged to have known her.
