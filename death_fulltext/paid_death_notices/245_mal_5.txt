
        GOLDBERG--Elliott ''Ski'', of Fort Lee, NJ, 86, died at home on May 30. Loving, supportive and deeply dedicated to his late wife Shirley, his children Sandra, Jeffrey (deceased), Neil, and Howard; his son-in-law Paul, daughterin-law Kara; his grandchildren John, Brian and Nathan; his sister Mildred, his late brother Herb; his brother-inlaw Manny and sister-in-law Muriel; his companion Lillian; his nieces, nephews, cousins, and his many, many friends. He will be deeply missed.
