
        COLLINS-Ann Ward. 94. Died at home peacefully on June 10, 2002 in the presence of her family, as she wished. Ann was a woman of great spirit, independence and compassion. Her joy of life was unwavering, as was her sense of humor, and she shared both freely. Know that she left life as she lived it, with dignity, serenity and love. She will be greatly missed. Ann was a former President of the Casita Maria and was a member of the Order of the Holy Sepulcher. A longtime resident of New York and Palm Beach, Ann is survived by her daughter Jill Hyland Frankenhoff, her son-in-law William, and her grandchildren William Jr. and Christine. Ann is also survived by her stepchildren, Monsignor Timothy S. Collins, Richard W. Collins, Marianna Tostanoski and their children. A Mass of the Resurrection will be held at 10AM, Thursday, June 13, at St. Thomas More Church, 65 E. 89 St. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Casita Maria, 928 Simpson St., Bronx, New York 10459.
