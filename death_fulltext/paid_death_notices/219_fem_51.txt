
        WELICHANSKY -- Kay of Bronx, NY on April 7th, 2005. Born June 23rd, 1911 to Harry and Yetta (Kleinman) Lerner. Beloved mother of Jonathan Sternberg; dear mother-in-law of Janice Siglin; loving grandmother of Benjamin. Cherished sister of Ada Schaiewitz and Milton Lerner. She will be greatly missed.
