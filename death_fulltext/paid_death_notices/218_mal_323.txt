
        TORRIANI--Albert J., 91, of Brooklyn, entered into eternal rest on March 26, 2005. He is survived by Ronnie, his loving wife of 61 years, dearest father and father-in-law of James & wife Sandra, Linda, Bob and wife Arlene, Ronald and wife Barbara. He is also survived by six grandchildren. He worked for many years with the international Ladies Garment Workers Union and was an ardent advocate of the trade union movement. He also taught labor relations on a volunteer basis at the Xavier Labor Institute in New York City. Al was proud to be a Liberal Democrat and was an avid New York Yankee fan. He will be missed by all. Immediate services will be private with a memorial service to be scheduled at a later date. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 633 3rd Ave. NY, NY 10117 would be appreciated.
