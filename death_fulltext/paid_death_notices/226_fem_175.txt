
        FEDDOES - Sadie. SUNY Downstate Medical Center mourns the passing of Sadie Feddoes, longtime civic leader and member of SUNY Downstate's University Council. A retired vice president of Citibank and a columnist for the Amsterdam News, Ms. Feddoes shared her expertise with numerous public and private agencies furthering economic development, education, communications, and the arts. She likewise brought her discerning intellect and enormous energy to Downstate's SUNY Council. Our deepest sympathies are extended to relatives, colleagues, and friends. Funeral Services will be held Tuesday, November 29, 9:30 AM, at First Presbyterian Church of Brooklyn, 124 Henry Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201. John C. LaRosa, M.D. President
