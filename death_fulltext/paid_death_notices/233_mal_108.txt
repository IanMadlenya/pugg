
        SAFON--David M. Age 43, suddenly, on June 10, 2006. A partner in the New York office of Ford & Harrison, he was a widely respected labor and employment lawyer, admired for his personal integrity and character, as well as his knowledge and skills. Unassuming and forever giving, he was a former president of his synagogue, Congregation Ohav Sholom, and a source of wise and compassionate counsel to his family, friends, colleagues, and clients. He will be dearly missed by everyone his life touched. In addition to his beloved wife, Lisa, he is survived by his children, Jenny, Keith and Noah. Funeral services are scheduled for Monday, June 12, 2006, at 11 o'clock A.M. at Congregation Ohav Sholom, 145 South Merrick Avenue, Merrick, NY. For directions, see www.ohav.org. In lieu of flowers, donations should be made to Chai Life Line or Congregation Ohav Sholom.
