
        SCHEFF-Samuel Edward, 89 years old, on August 15, 1997. Husband of Frances L. Scheff, father of Trudy Brown and Joan Lipsitz, father-in-law of Julius Brown and Paul Stiller. Devoted grandfather of Cathy, Susan, Annie and Jonathan. Great grandfather of Gabby, Zoe, Lucy Kate, Aaron Jeremy, and Zachary. Believer in Zedakah, corporate leader, humanitarian, dynamic and loving patriarch. Contributions in his memory may be sent to the Anti-Defamation League of B'Nai Brith or Pride of Judea.
        SCHEFF-Samuel. The Board of Governors and Members of Cold Spring Country Club deeply mourn the recent passing of our longtime member. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife Frances and the Scheff family.
