
        McDERMOTT--James Oliver, 82, on February 3, 2005 in Livingston, NJ. Born in Summit, NJ in 1922, he served as an officer in the U.S. Merchant Marine during WW II. He spent his business career with the overseas division of General Motors Corp. in South America, the Near East, Africa and New York City. Subsequent to retirement he resided in Coral Gables and Sarasota, FL. He was predeceased by his wife, Alice Jane McDermott and is survived by his daughters, Devon M. Poor and Deborah M. Wight of Short Hills, NJ and by seven grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Funeral Services will be private. His memory will be cherished and he will be deeply missed. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate donations to the New York Times Neediest Cases Fund.
