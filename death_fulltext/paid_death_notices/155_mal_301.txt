
        BERGER-Adolph R.., MD. Died on December 17, 1999 at age 89. The devoted husband of his beloved late wife, Margaret Lielman Berger. Dr. Berger was a graduate of NYU School of Medicine and an Attending Physician and Professor of Clinical Medicine at Bellevue and Tisch Hospitals for more than 50 years. He trained many physicians, imparting to them the high standards he demanded of himself. To the end he maintained a great interest in the relationship between physicians and their patients. He will be missed by his former colleagues and his friends, but they are comforted by the knowledge that his final wish, to join his beloved Margaret, has been fulfilled.
