
        ENGELHARD -- Jane. The worldwide family of Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem mourns the loss of this cherished friend and benefactor. During difficult economic times in the late 1970s, she played a vital role in helping to build our new medical center complex by sponsoring the Catheterization Suite in our Institute of Cardiology, through the exemplary support she provided from the Charles W. Engelhard Foundation. Sincerest condolences to the entire family. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, President
