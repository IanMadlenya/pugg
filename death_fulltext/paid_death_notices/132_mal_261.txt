
        GOTTLIEB-Edward. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute are saddened by the passing of Edward Gottlieb, distinguished Trustee, committed friend and loyal benefactor of the hospital. His lifetime of dedicated work, encouragement and philanthropic support of our major initiatives will always be remembered. His friends and colleagues at HJD grieve his passing as they celebrate his exemplary life. We extend our condolences to his wife, Janet, his children, Elizabeth and Richard, and to the entire Gottlieb family. William A. Perlmuth, Chairman of the Board Victor H. Frankel, M.D., President John N. Kastanis, FACHE Chief Executive Officer Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D., Surgeon-in-Chief
