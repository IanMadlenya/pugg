
        LOGAN -- Sheridan Ashton. 102, died at the family home in Saint Joseph, Missouri, on November 5, 2003. Harvard College 1923; First National Bank, New York; Executive Secretary, George F. Baker Trust; Treasurer, Harvard Club of New York City; Treasurer, Cooper Union. Wrote two books, ''George F. Baker and His Bank, 1840-1855, A Double Biography,'' ''Old Saint Jo, Gateway to the West, 1799-1932.'' Survived by his niece, Lisa Logan Armbruster. Services 1:30 PM, Sunday, November 16, 2003, First Presbyterian Church, St. Joseph, Missouri. For information contact Meierhoffer Family Funeral Service, 816232-3366.
