
        ARIEL-Harry Aharon, on 9th of Nisan, 5759. Beloved father, devoted husband, cherished uncle and brother, and sharing friend. A gifted engineer and modest individual, he was admired by all who knew him. As a former soldier who fought in the battle for Israel's independence, he remained a steadfast supporter of Eretz Yisroel, Torat Yisroel and Am Yisroel. Kevurah on Har Hazeitim. Upon return from Israel, shiva to be observed at family residence.
