
        SYMS MERNS-Stephen. On August 29, 1998, at age 45. Beloved husband of Karen. Devoted father of Jillian, Daniel and Olivia. Loving son of Sy Syms and Ruth Merns. Adored brother of Marcy Syms Merns, Laura, Adrienne, Robert and Richard. Stephen was Vice President of Syms Corp and member and Secretary of the Board of Directors of Syms Corp, a chain of 43 apparel stores in 16 states. Steve gained most of his business savvy from his father, Founder and Chairman of the Board, Sy Syms. Early in his development at the Company, he began as a buyer and soon assumed a major role in the senior management of the Company. Stephen attended Pace University. One of his successes prior to joining Syms Corp included the development of his own export business. He was keenly interested in sports and contributed to United Jewish Appeal-Federation of Jewish Philanthropies of New York, Inc., and Floating Hospital and Lighthouse Charities. Services Tuesday, September 1, at 1 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapels, 181 West 76th Street, Manhattan. Contributions can be made in Stephen's memory to the National Committee to Prevent Child Abuse, c/o Syms Advertising, 1 Syms Way, Secaucus, NJ 07094.
