
        BETCHER-Albert, M.D. On October 15, 1999 at age 88. Beloved husband of Gertrude. Loving father of Diane Dodge, Peter and William Betcher. Devoted grandfather of Karen Trister, Allison Dodge, Benjamin Betcher and Shannon Dodge. Dear brother of Ronnie Griff. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Sunday 11:30AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Alzeimer's Association, 360 Lexington Ave, New York, NY 10017.
        BETCHER-Albert M. The members of the Department of Anesthesiology of the Hospital for Joint Diseases mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague, mentor and friend. Albert M. Betcher touched the lives of all of us at the hospital. He was a pioneer in the field of anesthesiology. Dr. Betcher achieved national recognition in anesthesiology. He was president of the American Society of Anesthesiologists, president of the American Board of Anesthesiology, received the Distinguished Service Award of the American Society of Anesthesiologists, and was president of the New York State Society of Anesthesiologists. He was also a member of the Academy of Anesthesiology from which he received a Citation of Merit. Ralph L. Bernstein, M.D. Chairman, Department of Anesthesiology Andrew D. Rosenberg, M.D. Vice-Chairman, Department of Anesthesiology
