
        FRANKEL-Arnold. The Board and Staff of the Queens Child Guidance Center are deeply saddened by the death of their past President, whose wisdom and generosity were decisive in enabling the agency to better serve the children of Queens. The Arnold Frankel Clinic of the Center will perpetuate his memory. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his widow Mimi and the entire family. The Board and Staff of the Queens Child Guidance Center Hank H. Auffarth, President Sandra Hagan, Exec. Dir. Walter O. Michael, Past President
        FRANKEL-Arnold. The Trustees and staff of The Jewish Museum mourn the loss of Arnold Frankel, our beloved Life Trustee, benefactor and friend. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Miriam, his family and friends. Robert J. Hurst, Chairman Philip A. Laskawy, President Joan Rosenbaum, Director
        FRANKEL-Arnold J. The Officers, Directors and employees of Aceto Corporation honor the life and mourn the death of Arnold Frankel, a founder of our Corporation active in its management and success for more than 50 years. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife Miriam, his children and grandchildren.
