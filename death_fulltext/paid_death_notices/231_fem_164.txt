
        ROSENFELD--Naomi E. The Board of Directors, members and staff of the Metropolitan Opera Club mourn the loss of our dear friend and fellow member, Naomi Rosenfeld. A member of the Opera Club since 1987, Naomi served as a Director of the Club and Chairman of the Program Committee with boundless energy and enthusiasm. Her wit, wisdom, knowledge and grace will be sorely missed and long remembered. We extend our sincere sympathy to her husband Steven and her entire family. The Board of Directors Metropolitan Opera Club
