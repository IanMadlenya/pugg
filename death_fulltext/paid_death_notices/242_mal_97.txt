
        GRISTEDE--Charles, 97, of New York, died December 20, 2006. He graduated from Dartmouth College. He worked for Gristede Grocery Stores, which were founded by his father and uncle. He was Vice President when he retired. Charles was married to Zorah White and had one daughter, Amy, both deceased. He is survived by three nieces, several grand nieces and nephews. He was a longtime member of the New York Athletic Club. Interment will be at Woodlawn Cemetery in the family plot. Memorial donations may be sent to Madison Ave. Presbyterian Church, Manhattan. Memorial Service, at Madison Ave. Presbyterian Church, March 16th at 1 pm.
