
        GRIFFINGER-Edythe . The partners, associates and staff of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison express deep sorrow at the passing of Edythe Griffinger, former Administrative Assistant to the late Judge Simon H. Rifkind, and beloved colleague and friend. We extend our deepest sympathies and condolences to her niece, Barrie, her nephew, Michael, and to all the other members of her family.
