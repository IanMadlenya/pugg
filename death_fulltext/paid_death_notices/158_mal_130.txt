
        GUIRY-Arthur R. (loving and devoted husband of Margaret (Starrs) Guiry for 36 years) died Thursday, March 9, 2000, after a short illness at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, New York. Mr. Guiry was President of Chromatics Color Sciences International Inc. A long time member of the Saint Andrew's Golf Club in Hastings-onHudson. Mr. Guiry served as President and member of the Board of Governors. Born in Bronx, New York, Mr. Guiry was a graduate of Cardinal Hayes High School and Manhattan College, where he earned a degree in physics. Son of the late Arthur R. and Helen V. Guiry. Mr. Guiry is survived by 2 brothers and one sister. Dennis of Yorktown Heights, New York, Thomas of Sunset Beach, North Carolina, and Maureen Guiry (Pignataro) of Fountain Hills, Arizona. Uncle to 22 nieces and nephews. Granduncle to 12 and Godfather to eight. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, 2-4 and 7-9 PM Saturday and Sunday. Mass of Christian Burial Annunciation Church, Crestwood on Monday March 13 at 10 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to ''Lawrence Hospital Development Fund'' in the memory of Arthur R. Guiry, 55 Palmer Road, Bronxville, NY 10708. Checks payable to The Lawrence Hospital.
