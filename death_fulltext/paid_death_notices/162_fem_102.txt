
        MacKENZIE-Phoebe Connor. Of Santa Barbara, CA. On June 30, 2000 in Alexandria, VA after a brief illness. Beloved mother of Phoebe Elizabeth (Pemy), Gair, Jim, Donald and Scott, who survive her. Also survived by brother Hayden Connor, seven grandchildren and two step grandchildren. A 1956 graduate of Vassar, she was a longtime resident of Darien, CT and a former real estate agent there for Country Living Associates. She was a lifelong Christian Scientist who was devoted to her children and loving and generous to all. A private memorial service has been held. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the summer camps she loved, Camps Newfound and Owatonna, 4 Camp Newfound Rd., Harrison, ME 04040.
