
        CLARKE--Lucile King, of Bill Hill Road, Lyme, Connecticut died Monday, September 22, 2003. Mrs. Clarke was born on September 22nd, 1919 in Los Angeles, CA the daughter of Gale Curtis Peck. Mrs. Clarke is survived by her husband Harry R. Clarke, a daughter, two brothers and two grandchildren. A Funeral Service will be held on Saturday, September 27th, 2003 in Lyme. Donations may be made in her memory to the Lyme Garden Club, Judy Davies Treasurer, 332 Joshua Town Road, Lyme, CT. 06371.
