
        FIELD--George. One of the founders of Freedom House and its Executive Director from 1941 to 1967, has died at age 101 in Kennett Square, PA. Mr. Field devoted his life to the pursuit of freedom, democarcy and human rights. After serving as Program Director of WEVD Radio, he became the first Executive Director of Freedom House, a position he held until his retirement in 1967. Mr. Field was predeceased by his wife Molly (nee Mass). He is survived by two children, Paul Field and Ellen Balkan; five grandchildren, Gary Field, Craig Field, Laurie Liberty, Donna Balkan and Liza Balkan, and six great-grandchildren. In his memory, donations may be made to Amnesty International.
