        MADDEN-John L., M.D. The faculty and staff of New York Presbyterian Hospital and Weill Medical College of Cornell University are saddened by the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague, Dr. John L. Madden. A renowned cardiothoracic surgeon, Dr. Madden served as adjunct clinical professor of surgery and attending surgeon at the hospital and the medical college for several years prior to his retirement in 1995. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his family. New York Presbyterian Hospital David B. Skinner, M.D. Vice Chairman & CEO William T. Speck, M.D. President & COO Weill Medical College of Cornell University Antonio M. Gotto., Jr., M.D. The Stephen & Suzanne Weiss Dean O. Wayne Isom, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Cardiothoracic Surgery
        MADDEN-John L. The New York Academy of Medicine records with sorrow the death of Dr. John L. Madden, a distinguished surgeon and teacher. Dr. Madden was elected to Fellowship in the Academy in 1948 and served as Chairman of the Section of Surgery and as a member of the Board of Trustees. He was President of the Academy from 1965 to 1966. The Trustees, Fellows and Staff of the Academy extend sincere condolences to Dr. Madden's family. Stanley Brezenoff, Chairman of the Board of Trustees Jeremiah Barondess, President
