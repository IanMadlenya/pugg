
        WOHLSTETTER -- Rose Teed. On May 6, 2006, of natural causes, at 84. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Loving mother of Philip of Seattle, Washington, John of Washington, DC and Rand of Los Angeles, CA. Cherished grandmother of Isabel and Julia of Seattle, Washington. Born in Chicago May 23, 1922, a professional dancer and Broadway veteran who appeared in High Kickers (1941), The Ziegfield Follies of 1943, also co-produced Promenade All (1972) and The Innocents (1976). A longtime member of the Board of Directors of The American Theater Wing. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, May 13 at 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The American Theater Wing, 570 7th Avenue, NY, NY 10018. Tel: 212-765-0606. $(6$)WOHLSTETTER--Rose. The Board of Directors and staff of the American Theatre Wing mourn the passing of our dedicated board member and friend. From her earliest days as a model and dancer, with performances in ''High Kickers'' and ''The Ziegfeld Follies,'' to her 18 years of generous service on the Wing board, Rose's love for and support of the arts, and theatre in particular, were an example to us all.
