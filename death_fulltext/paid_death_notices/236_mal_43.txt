
        SHATKIN--Alfred, 87, of Great Neck, NY. On August 31st, 2006, in Santa Rosa, CA. Beloved and devoted husband of Toby. Cherished and loving father of David and Barbara, father-in-law of Paula and Andy, grandfather of Nina and her husband Yaniv, Lily, Jesse, Aaron, and Annalise, and great-grandfather of Aiden and Talia. Adored and loved by many cousins, nephews, nieces, and sister-in-law Muriel. Devoted friend of many, and childhood friend Melvin. He was a very, very dear person sweet, gentle, thoughtful, and unusually generous and caring, and he will be greatly missed by all who knew him, and whose lives he touched.
