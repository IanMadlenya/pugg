
        THOMAS-Carole. Nassau Suffolk Home Care and Aides, Inc., expresses our condolences to the entire Thomas family on the loss of our President, Carole Thomas, who showed great courage in her battle against breast cancer. She will be sorely missed. Donations in her memory may be made to Adelphi University, Breast Cancer Hotline, where Carole was a volunteer. Funeral service will be held at Gutterman's in Woodbury on Tuesday, 3 PM. Suzanne G. Olivet, Assistant to the President Nassau Suffolk Home Care and Aides, Inc.
