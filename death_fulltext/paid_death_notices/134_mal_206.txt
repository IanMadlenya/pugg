
        SAFRAN-Melvin H. On March 16 at home. Age 85. Born in South Amboy, New Jersey, and graduated from Rutgers University. He served in the Pacific during World War II. He was a founder of the real estate firm Safran Brothers, together with his late brother, Ira. He served as President of the Middlesex County Board of Realtors, the N.J. YMHA-YWHA Camps and Hollywood Golf Club. He was a longtime Director of Amboy Bancorporation. He is survived by his beloved wife of 47 years, Nan, two sons and their wives, Andrew and Linda of N.Y.C., Charles and Donna of Boston, and a daughter, Barbara Esrig of Gainesville, FL. He is also survived by six adored grandchildren, Rachel, Julie, Caroline, and Lily Safran, and Aaron and Ariel Esrig, and two great grandchildren, Jacob and Molly. Services will be held Wednesday, March 18, 11 AM, at Temple Beth Mordecai 224 High Street, Perth Amboy, New Jersey. The family will be receiving friends on Wednesday and Thursday evening at their home in N.Y.C. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Monmouth Medical Center Health Care Foundation or the Lighthouse, Inc.
        SAFRAN-Melvin H. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend, member and past-President, Mr. Melvin H. Safran. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
