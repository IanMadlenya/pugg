
        GILBERT--James F., 92, of Sea Girt, NJ, on Monday, September 15, 2003, at his home surrounded by his three children. Devoted husband of his wife and partner of 59 years of marriage, Eva (nee Vogel) Gilbert, who predeceased him in 1994. Beloved father of James G. Gilbert of Morristown, NJ, Janine Cook of Morristown and Lynne Timothy of Kinnelon, NJ. Dear grandfather of eight and great-grandfather of eleven. Relatives and friends are invited to attend a Mass of Christian Burial on Wednesday, September 17, at 9:30 AM, at St. Mark's R.C. Church, Sea Girt. Burial will follow in St. Catharine's Cemetery, Sea Girt. Contact O'Brien Funeral Home, Wall, NJ, 732-449-6900. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Pierre Toussaint Food Pantry, 528 Martin Luther King Blvd, Newark, NJ or to St. Mark's Church, 215 Crescent Pkwy., Sea Girt, NJ.
