
        BERNS-Julie. Widow of Leo Berns, passed away peacefully on March 9, 2000 after a long illness in El Paso, TX, her residence for the past twenty years. She is survived by her daughter Sharon Abrahams (Michael), her son Jay Berns (Shirley), three grandchildren Steffie, David and Heather and brother Charles Hyman (Ruth). Funeral services Weinstein Memorial Chapel, Yonkers, NY, Sunday, March 12th, 10 AM.
