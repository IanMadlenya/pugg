
        YEUNG--Wai Sing (Frank). The Headmaster, Board of Trustees of Collegiate School, and the entire Collegiate community extend heartfelt condolences to Esther Yip (Vice President, Board of Trustees) on the death of her father. We hope the shining light of Mr. Yeung's life will comfort his family and all who grieve at his passing. Our thoughts are with you. Lee M. Levison, Headmaster David Forer, President, Board of Trustees
