
        GIBSON - Constance D. The Trustees of Gramercy Park regret the passing of their Honorary Chairman, Constance D. Gibson on March 10, 1997. Mrs. Gibson was elected a Trustee of the Park in 1964 and served as Chairman from 1980 until 1992. Her wisdom and charm will be missed. Funeral service will be held today 12noon at St. George's Episcopal Church, Rutherford Place at E. 16 St.
        GIBSON-Constance. Board member, friend and devoted supporter of The Horticultural Society of New York and former Chairperson of its Library Committee. Our library is a continuing memorial to her commitment to improve the City of New York through horticulture.
