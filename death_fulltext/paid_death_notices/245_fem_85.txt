
        ROBIN--Wilma. Wilma was the daughter of Edwin J Robin (architect) and Isabel Abeles Robin (weaver and fabric designer). She was the sister of Rosemary Robin Kaplan and sister-in-law of Seymour Kaplan, aunt of Sara, Daniel and Matthew. Greataunt of Jacob, Robin, Amy and Jesse. Wilma was a graduate of the State University of Iowa at Iowa City and did graduate work at New York University. She was an award winning book designer at Doubleday Dell and Delacorte. She loved books and was a great reader, making good use of the Hammond Public Library after moving. There will be a memorial service at the convenience of the family. Owens Funeral Home, Calumet Ave. and 119th St., Whiting, IN, in charge of services. Online condolences may be made thru owensfuneralhome.com
