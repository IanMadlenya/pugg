
        STERNBACH-Louis, age 90, died on November 1, 1998. He resided in Westport, CT, and Delray Beach, FL, and was both an attorney and CPA. He was married to Ruth Sternbach, recently deceased. He had three children, Dorothy Sternbach, Julia Sternbach, and Willa Rosenberg. He founded the Manhattan Accounting Firm of Louis Sternbach and Company. Funeral arrangements are private.
