
        CRIFASI--Frank J. The Board of Trustees and Administration of Maimonides Medical Center mourn with profound sadness the loss of Dr. Frank Crifasi, a member of the Board of Trustees since 1992. Dr. Crifasi served as Chairman of Maimonides Community Board for Ambulatory Services for more that 20 years, as President of the Council of Community Organizations and as a member of Community Planning Board 12. Frank Crifasi, a member of the Brooklyn Chiropractic Society, was an outstanding Trustee and community leader who also served as a Trustee of Saint Catherine of Alexandria R.C. Church. We express our condolences to his daughters Francine, Denise, Louise and Terese, and to the entire Crifasi family. Martin D. Payson, Chairman Pamela S. Brier, President
