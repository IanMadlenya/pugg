
        WASSERMAN-Herbert, 69. Died September 8, 2000 at his home in Tyringham, MA., after a two year battle with colon cancer. Born on July 30, 1931, he graduated from Harvard College & Harvard Law School. Lawyer, producer/impresario, and business executive, he is survived by his wife of 44 years, Cecille; daughters Emily, Stefanie & Wendy and son-in-law Michael Schiffer. A daughter, Julie, died in 1973. Memorial service Sunday, September 10th at 4:00 P.M., at Hevreh Temple, Route 23, Great Barrington, Massachusetts.
