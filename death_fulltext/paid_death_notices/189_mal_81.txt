
        DANIEL-Eugene E. Of Fair Lawn, NJ, on Sunday, October 6, 2002. He was the retired Director of Finance for the Catholic Charities of New York and St. Joseph's Seminary, Yonkers, NY, which were under the direction of the New York Archdiocese. Mr. Daniel was a 1951 graduate of Regis High School, New York, NY, and went on to graduate from Fordham University, Bronx, NY, 1955. Beloved husband of 46 years of Eileen (nee Sheerin) Daniel. Loving father of Eugene E. Daniel Jr., Kevin Daniel, Maura Wright and Susan Biamonte. Devoted brother of Patricia Daniel. Cherished grandfather of ten. Funeral Mass Thursday, October 10, 2002, 11 AM, at St. Anne RC Church, Fair Lawn, NJ. Interment immediately following the Mass. The family will receive their friends Tuesday and Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Vander Plaat Colonial Home, Inc., 13-31 Saddle River Road, Fair Lawn, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations to either the Carmelite Chapel, Bergen Mall, Route 4 East, Paramus, NJ 07652 or Regis High School, 55 East 84th Street, New York, NY 10028 would be appreciated.
