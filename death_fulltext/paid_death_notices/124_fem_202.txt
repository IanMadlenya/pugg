
        VOGEL-Helen M., on May 28, 1997 in her 96th year. Cherished wife of the late Peter Vogel, M.D. Devoted mother of Dr. James M. Vogel and Josephine V. Wolk. Adoring grandmother of Douglas D. Wolk and Jennifer N. Vogel. Beloved sister of the late Ruth J. Mandlebaum and the late Daniel Mandlebaum. Funeral service Sunday, 2 PM, at the Universal Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        VOGEL-Helen M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Helen M. Vogel, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
