
        McCABE-Michael Oliver. 23, passed away on Tuesday, April 29, 2003. Deeply loved by his mother Eleanor Brickner McCabe, his father and step-mother Ross and Kathleen McCabe, and his brother Samuel and his sister Julie. He will be remembered but greatly missed. His passing is sadly acknowledged by so many who knew him as the shy, gentle, innocent and curious young man that he was. Donations can be made to the Michael McCabe Memorial Fund, attn: Mr. Jeff Harris, Edward R. Morrow High School, 1600 Ave L, Brooklyn, NY 11230.
