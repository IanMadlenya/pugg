
        BECK-Joan. A longtime columnist for the Chicago Tribune, died December 10, in Lake Forest, IL. Her children Christopher Beck and Melinda Beck Neger, her son-in-law Peter Neger, and granddaughters Emily and Hillary Neger will miss her deeply and will always remember her love and inspiration. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Northwestern University, for the National High School Journalism Institute, 2020 Ridge Ave., Evanston, IL 60208.
