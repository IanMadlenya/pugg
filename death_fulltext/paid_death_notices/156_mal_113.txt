
        SCHWARTZ-Estell. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our friend, Estell Schwartz, beloved wife of our esteemed colleague and benefactor, Albert M. Schwartz, M.D., who founded the Division of Vascular Surgery at Beth Israel and who served as a distinguished member of our medical staff for 58 years. Their generous philanthropic support of Beth Israel is remembered forever through the naming of the Estell and Albert M. Schwartz, M.D., Division of Vascular Surgery. To Albert and all his wife's loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, M.D. President and CEO Henry Singman, M.D. President, Medical Board Michael Silane, M.D. Chief, Estell and Albert M. Schwartz, M.D., Division of Vascular Surgery Robert L. Salant, M.D. President, Doctors Alumni Assoc.
