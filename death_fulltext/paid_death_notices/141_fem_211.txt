
        KANAREK-Adele. Yeshiva University Women's Organization expresses its grief and sorrow upon the untimely demise of Adele Kanarek, a gracious long-time member of our organization and a National Vice President of Y.U.W.O. We extend our warmest sympathy to her husband, Dr. Irwin Kanarek, their children and grandchildren and all the family. Dinah Pinczower, National Chairman of the Board
