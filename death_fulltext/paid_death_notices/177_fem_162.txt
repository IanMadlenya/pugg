
        BURNS-Kathleen A., 48. Of Staten Island, NY on September 11, 2001 as a victim of the attack on the World Trade Center in New York City. Kathy was born on Staten Island where she lived most of her life. She was employed for more than 15 years by Fiduciary Trust Company International, most recently as Vice President for Office Automation. Kathy will be forever missed as the loving companion of her parents John and Eunice Burns and the treasured friend of her brother John J. Burns, her aunts Shirley Donohue and Helen Susarchick, her uncles and cousins, her co-workers and the many friends she made throughout her life will always remember her frequent smiles, zest for life and endless interests in the wider world. A memorial service will be held on Friday, October 19th at 3:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Friends are invited to visit with the family one hour before the service. In lieu of flowers contributions in her name to The American Red Cross would be appreciated.
