
        DUBERSTEIN-Sylvie B., 85. Formerly of Brooklyn, died January 12, 1998 in Kingston, NY. Wife of Louis Emanuel Duberstein, mother of Mrs. Lucy Goldfarb and her husband Dr. Ned M. Goldfarb and the late Seth Franklin Duberstein. Funeral services and burial will be in Kingston, NY on Wednesday. Memorial contributions may be made to AIDS Related Community Services, 2269 Saw Mill River Road, Elmsford, New York 10523.
