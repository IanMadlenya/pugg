
        JACKSON--Helen A. Widow of Paul E. Jackson of Tuxedo Park, NY, died peacefully on July 30, 2004. She is survived by a son Nathaniel A. Jackson of Tuxedo Park, a daughter Elisabeth J. Amory of Charleston, SC, and was predeceased by another daughter Constance E. Jackson. She is also survived by three granddaughters and two great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at St. Mary's-In-Tuxedo at 11 am, on Wednesday, August 4, which would have been her 97th birthday. In lieu of flowers contributions to St. Mary's Outreach Fund.
