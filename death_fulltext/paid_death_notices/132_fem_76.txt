
        KASSNER-Bertha. Died January 7, 1998 at the age of 100 in NYC. Beloved wife of the late Jacob Kassner. Loving mother of Henrietta Feinerman, Bella Baron, Rita Ulman, the late Lila Rosenberg and sister of Max (and Georgia) Zimmer. Devoted mother-inlaw of William Feinerman, the late Daniel Baron, Reuben Ulman, Saul Rosenberg. Adored grandmother of 13 and great grandmother of 15. Funeral service Thursday January 8 at 10 A.M., at United Hebrew Community, 201 East Broadway. Shiva at the Kassner residence at 477 FDR Drive, Apt 206M.
        KASSNER-Bertha. Young Israel of Fifth Avenue mourns the death of the mother of our esteemed member, Henrietta Feinerman, and extends to her and the bereaved family our heartfelt condolences.
