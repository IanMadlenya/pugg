
        LEVY-Albert. On March 30, 2002. Loving life partner of Adele Dressner, devoted brother of Maurice Levis and Elisa Wheeler, and Ivan and Pupy Dressner, adoring uncle to Amelia and Peter Foster, Lydia and Kevin Brandt, Jon and Lisa Dressner, Juliane Dressner, Clifford Levy and eight grandnieces and nephews. Native of Greece, he never lost his affection for his homeland, though he thrived in his adopted New York. Even in his final days, he displayed the wit, intelligence and humility that had long made him so beloved by family and friends. Service at 11 AM, April 2, at Nieberg-Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn.
