
        GERARD--Dr. Bert S., 79, on October 9 after a long battle with Parkinson's Disease, at home in Chandler, AZ. Educator, writer, poet, storyteller, and basso profundo, his impact on public and religious education continues to be felt in schools and synagogues from Manhasset to Phoenix. He is survived by Francine, his devoted wife of more than 57 years; sons Jonathan, Edmond, Jeremy, & Michael; and eight grandchildren. Olav ha Shalom, Dad.
