
        STERNFELS-Lilly (nee Bloch). Died peacefully Monday, July 24, 2000 at the age of 91, after a long illness. Graveside service will be held at Beth El Cemetery, New Jersey, Wednesday, July 26th, 11AM. Beloved wife of the late Benno Sternfels and beloved sister of the late Alfred Bloch. Loved by her nephews, nieces, sister-in-law and cousins.
