
        SISITZKY-Dorothy (Ertman). Age 77, of Ironwood Lane, West Hartford, CT. Wife of Leonard H. Sisitzky, died suddenly at home on September 12, 1998. Born in Hartford, November 28, 1920, to Abraham and Lena (Eisenberg) Ertman, she lived in the Hartford area all of her life. She was a First Lieutenant in the Army Air Forces during World War II. Besides her husband, she is survived by a daughter, Nancy S. Alderman and her husband John of Scarsdale, NY, and a son Brian R. Sisitzky and his wife Laura M. of Winchester, MA. She will live through her grandchildren who she adored, Charlie E. Alderman and Alexa T. Sisitzky. She is also survived by a sister, Eva Katz of West Hartford, CT. Funeral services will be held today at 3 PM, in the Chapel of the Weinstein Mortuary, 640 Farmington Ave., Hartford, CT, with interment in the Emanuel Synagogue Cemetery, Wethersfield, CT. A memorial period will be observed at her home through Tuesday. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the National Parkinson's Foundation, 1501 NW 9th Ave., Bob Hope Rd., Miami, FL 33136.
