
        ADLER-Rebecca Betty. 84, of The Eddy Memorial Geriatric Center, Troy, NY, died Friday at home after a long illness. Born in New York City, she was the daugher of the late Louis L. and Augusta Silberstein Adler. She had resided in New York City most of her life, retiring to Dania, FL, in 1977 and had resided at the Eddy since 1994. She was educated in New York City schools. Miss Adler was office manager for Kansas Meat Packing Company in NYC for 30 years, retiring in 1965. Survivors include a sister, Lillian Burns of Clifton Park. Funeral service was held Sunday. Contributions may be made in memory of her to the charity of one's choice.
