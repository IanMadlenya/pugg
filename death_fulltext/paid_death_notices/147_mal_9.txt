
        EHRLICH-Carl. Cherished husband of Helen. Devoted father of Richard and Ruth, Michael and Susan, Amy and Joseph Fins. Adoring grandfather of Joseph and Robert, Daniel and Jacob. Caring brother-in-law of Jefferson B. Cohen. A man of great character. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I., 9:30 AM Friday. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Temple Emanu-El of Lynbrook or City Harvest.
        EHRLICH-Carl. The Rabbis and Cantor, Officers, Trustees and members of Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York convey to our dear colleague and revered teacher, Rabbi Amy B. Ehrlich, our heartfelt sympathies and condolences on the occasion of the death of her father, Carl Ehrlich. For Fifty glorious years Carl and his beloved Helen walked at each other's side with a devotion that was beautiful to behold. From their love came three wonderful children and four glorious grandchildren. He will be remembered by them, and by all those lives he touched, with everlasting gratitude. Thus his memory will forever be a blessing. Rabbi Ronald B. Sobel Rabbi David M. Posner Rabbi Richard S. Chapin Cantor Howard Nevison David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
