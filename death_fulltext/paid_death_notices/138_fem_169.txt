
        STONEHILL - Norma Liebling. Died on July 24, 1998 at age 89 in New York City. Beloved wife of the late Harold Stonehill for 59 years. Sister of the late A.J. Liebling and daughter of Joseph and Anna Liebling. Devoted mother of Arthur, John, and Robert Stonehill. Friend and mother-in-law of Kari, Judith, and Carlyn Stonehill. Loving grandmother of Robert, Jr., Jeffrey, Alexandra, and David Stonehill and Inger Crumpton. Great grandmother of Kristen and Colton Stonehill and Ivana Crumpton. Norma Stonehill lived in retirement for 22 years with her husband in Bordighera, Italy where she will be remembered for her beautiful garden and the children's library that she founded. She was a serious linguist, an avid reader, and a great admirer and supporter of educational and literary endeavors. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st Street on Monday, July 27, 1998 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Connecticut College, New London, CT 06320, where she was a member of the Class of 1930.
