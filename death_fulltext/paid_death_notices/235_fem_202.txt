
        BRUMER--Etta. On August 29, 2006. Beloved mother of Marshall (Kathy). Devoted grandmother of Harris and Jessica. Adored aunt of Carol, Barbara, and Paul. Etta taught in the NYC school system for five decades. She will be remembered for her love, inspiration and guidance to family, students and friends. A graveside service will be held Friday 1PM at Old Montefiore Cemetery, Springfield Gardens, NY. For more information call Parkside Memorial Chapels, Rego Park, NY.
