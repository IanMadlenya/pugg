
        LORD-Walter, 84. Author of historical books, including ''A Night to Remember'' and ''Day of Infamy.'' Died May 19, after suffering for many years from Parkinson's. No immediate survivors, but a host of devoted friends. Memorial gathering at 12, Monday, June 10, at The New-York Historical Society.
        LORD-Walter. The Board of Directors and the staff of Union Settlement Association celebrate the gallant spirit of Walter Lord. Warm, gracious and witty, Walter had a commitment to the Settlement's work in East Harlem. A Director for 42 years, he was Chairman from 1962-1964. He was actively involved in mentoring students of color in the Settlement College Readiness Program and continuously involved in the broad range of programs at the Settlement. We will miss him. Board of Directors, Union Settlement Association
