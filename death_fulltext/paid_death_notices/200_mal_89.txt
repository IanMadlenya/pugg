
        BIANCHETTI--Alfred Joseph. On September 4, 2003. Beloved husband of Alice. Loving brother of Edward. He is also survived by his children Robert, Richard and Susan, and grandchildren Matthew, Christopher, Samantha, Patrick, Caroline and Ryan. A truly accomplished man, Alfred was a very successful member of the business community which included becoming partner and member of the Board of Directors for Dean Witter Reynolds, Outside Director of Soloman, Smith Barney, President of the Municipal Bond Club, member of the Board of Directors for the Public Securities Assocation and a member of the National Association of Securities Dealers. Perhaps his proudest moments were in the volunteer field where he served as the Athletic Director for the Ramsey CYO, was a volunteer in the New Jersey Juvenile and Crimanal Justice System, served as a Volunteer Probation Counselor, was on the Child Placement Review Board for the State of New Jersey, was an active Big Brother and on the Board of Directors for Catholic Big Brothers and coached baseball in Ramsey, NJ, for 43 years. Alfred served in WWII as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy and was called back for active duty in the Korean War. He graduated St. John's University in 1955 with his law degree. He was a truly wonderful man and will be deeply missed.
