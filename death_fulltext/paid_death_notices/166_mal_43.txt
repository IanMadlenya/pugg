
        DANIELS-Richard C. Of Palm Beach, FL, and formerly of Worcester, MA, died on October 31, 2000. He was a well-loved philanthropist known for his contributions to community arts and education. A veteran of World War II, he received a bachelor's degree from Clark University, studied drama at Columbia University, religion at Union Theological Seminary, and earned a joint master's degree in education at Columbia University. Following careers in theatre and religious education, Mr. Daniels became a distinguished public relations consultant and fundraiser. He was preceded in death by his wife Susan Archdeacon Daniels, who died in 1991. He is survived by his brother, William T. Daniels, Jr. (Julie); two nieces, Donna L. Lewis and Joyce Montenegro; two great-nieces and three great-nephews. A memorial service will be held at 12 noon on November 20, 2000, at the First Baptist Church of Worcester, MA, with private interment to follow at Hope Cemetery. Memorial donations may be made to: Scrogin Memorial Great Issues Fund, c/o First Baptist Church, 111 Park Ave., Worcester, MA 01609. For information, contact Quattlebaum Funeral Home.
