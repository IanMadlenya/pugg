
        CAMPBELL-Patrick J., 79. Former General President of The United Brotherhood of Carpenters and Joiners of America. Died Feb. 21, 1998 in Palm City, Florida. He is survived by his wife Catherine, two sons, Patrick and Kevin, daughter Cynthia and six grandchildren. Viewing at Higgins Funeral Home, New City, New York, Wednesday and Thursday February 25 and 26 from 2-4:30 and 7-9 P.M. Burial mass at St. Anthony's Church in Nanuet, NY on Friday, February 27 at 10 A.M.
