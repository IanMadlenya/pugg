
        van der STRICHT -- Paul Robert, 96, of Greenwich, CT, died August 10th at the Greenwich Hospital of natural causes. He is survived by his wife of 65 years, the former Nora Eaton, of Toronto, Canada, by his son, Robert (Susan Rae), and a grandson, John Paul, of Rochester, NY, and his brother, Jean van der Stricht, of Brussels, Belgium. A memorial service is planned for a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Greenwich Land Trust (PO Box 1152, Greenwich, CT 06836-1152), Connecticut Audubon Society (2325 Burr St., Fairfield, CT 06824-1806) or the World Wild Life Fund.
