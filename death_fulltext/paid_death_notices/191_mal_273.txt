
        BLOCH-George. Cherished, caring husband to Harriet, his wife of 62 years, adored father to Brian (Randee), Susan Goldstein (Arthur) and Steven (Marilyn). George leaves a legacy of love and kindness to his grandchildren Heidi and David Binstock, Meredith and Corry Prohens, Carrie, Noel and Stephanie Goldstein and Melissa and Sean Bloch. For many years George was a respected Trusteee for Local 812 of the Soft Drink Workers Union. Until the very end, his sharp sense of humor brought joy to his family and those whose lives he touched. He will be remembered with warmth and love.
