
        COWEN-David, M.D. The Department of Pathology at Columbia University mourns the death of David Cowen. Dr. Cowen graduated from Columbia College in 1928 and from Physicians and Surgeons in 1932, and spent his entire career at Columbia, becoming Professor in 1963 and Professor Emeritus in 1976. He remained active in the department for many years after that. He served as President of the American Association of Neuropathologists in 1961 and was honored for a lifetime of contributions to our understanding of nervous diseases by the Association in 1979. He is remembered warmly as an outstanding physician, excellent teacher and beloved colleague. James E. Goldman, M.D., Ph.D. Director Division of Neuropathology Michael L. Shelanski, M.D., Ph.D. Chairman Department of Pathology
