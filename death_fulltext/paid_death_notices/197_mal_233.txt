
        FERBER--Mel, 80, of NYC and Encino, CA, on June 19, 2003, of heart disease. Mel is survived by his beloved wife Bette, four children, six grandchildren, and four siblings. A graduate of CCNY and recipient of the Croix de Guerre in WWII, Mel enjoyed a long and successful career as a television director/producer.
