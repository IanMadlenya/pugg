
        KRETCHMER--Steven. Died July 8, 2006 in a tragic accident. He was a highly respected, internationally renowned, award winning jewelry designer. He was a brilliant, creative master of his craft. He was a special person with a passion for life and for his art. He will be remembered and missed by all. He is the son of Dr. Norman Kretchmer, deceased. He is survived by his beloved wife Alma, his daughter Claudia, his mother Midge, his brother Paul and sister Pam Senuty. A scholarship fund is being established in Steven's memory. Please check www.stevenkretchmer.com for details.
