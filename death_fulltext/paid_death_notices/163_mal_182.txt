
        BRODSKY-Edward. The Board, Staff, and Friends of the Garden are saddened by the passing of Edward Brodsky, husband of our esteemed Board member Cynthia. As a pre-eminent member of the Bar and public servant, he made far-reaching contributions to the civic life of New York City. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Wilson Nolen, Chairman Gregory Long, President The New York Botanical Garden
