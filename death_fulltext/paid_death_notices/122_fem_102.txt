
        PROSNITZ-Gerry, age 95. We mourn the passing of Gerry, a prominent journalist and activist. She fought tirelessly for social and Jewish causes throughout her life. She was founder and president of the Jewish Association of Retired Professionals until the age of 90. She is survived by sons Eugene and Leonard, her husband Herman and sister Bessie, her daughters-in-law Sandra and Beatrice and grandchildren Susan, Eric and Robert. Service on Friday, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 1:30 PM.
        PROSNITZ-Gerry (Mrs. Ludwig). The Officers and Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of their dear friend and fellow member, Gerry Prosnitz. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to her children and other members of her family. Robert K. Low, President
