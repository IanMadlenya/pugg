
        GREENWALD-Helen Newman. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community, beloved wife of Herbert Greenwald and mother of Rose (and Morton L.) Landowne. YU Guardians with the late Louis Landowne, Rose and Morton Landowne are Fellows of our affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary and its High Schools. Mr. Landowne is a member of the RIETS Board of Trustees. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband, Herbert; daughter, Rose (and Morton L.) Landowne; grandchildren; great-grandchildren; and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        GREENWALD-Helen. The Lincoln Square Synagogue mourns the passing of Mrs. Helen Greenwald, beloved mother of our member, Rose Landowne. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Ken Cappell, President
