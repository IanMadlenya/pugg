
        SHAINSWIT - Seymour. Passed away peacefully on October 26, 2002, at the age of 82, of lymphoma. He is survived by his loving wife of 52 years, Justice Beatrice Shainswit of the New York State Supreme Court, and his brother Bernard. He was a graduate of the City College of New York and of New York University Law School, and a distinguished member of the Bar Association of the City of New York, the New York State Bar Association, and the American Bar Association. He was the finest and most brilliant of commercial and libel litigators, a man who loved the law, and an honor to the bar. Services will be on Monday, October 28, 2002, at 11:45 AM, at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. His sweetness, warmth, and love will be with us forever.
