
        GROSS-Allen. 78. On May 4, 1999 of cancer. His passing is marked with great sorrow by Helene, his wife of forty-eight years, his children Daniel, Laurie, and Martin. His sister Carolyn, and his five grandchildren. He was a NYC teacher and school administrator and above all, a family man. He held graduate degrees from LIU and Columbia. Services Wednesday, 12 noon at Gutterman's Inc., 175 Long Beach Rd, Rockville Centre, LI.
