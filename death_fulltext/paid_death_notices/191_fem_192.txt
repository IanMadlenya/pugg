
        NEISLOSS-Irma. The faculty and staff of the Schneider Children's Hospital in New Hyde Park, note with profound sadness, the passing of Irma Neisloss. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her children, grandchildren and extended family. Mrs. Neisloss will be warmly remembered for her generous support of the Division of Pediatric Hematology/Oncology and interest in providing quality medical care to young patients in our community and beyond. She set an extraordinary example of philanthropy to her family and others, which we admire and cherish. May the recent memory of time spent together bring comfort to her children and grandchildren. Philip Lanzkowsky, M.D. Chief-of-Staff Schneider Children's Hospital New Hyde Park, NY
        NEISLOSS-Irma. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Irma Neisloss, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Irma was deeply devoted to the Long Island Women's Campaign and supported Jewish and humanitarian causes through her extraordinary philanthropy. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Neisloss family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O. Judy Baum, Chair, LI Women's Campaign Stuart Tauber, LI Director
        NEISLOSS-Irma.
