
        DICKSTEIN-Reuben. Of Elmwood Park, New Jersey, & Boca Raton, FL passed away on July 23, 1998. He was the devoted & loving husband of Elaine Davick Dickstein, the beloved uncle of Jay and Robert Ackerman, Bill, Dale and Chad Borer, and Mark, Robin, Melanie and Emily Gelfand, and the loving brother-in-law of Betty Dickstein, Carol Dickstein and Lila Ackerman. His love and warmth will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Services 1:30PM on Sunday at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4 West), Fair Lawn, NJ. The family will be sitting shiva at the home of Mark & Robin Gelfand, 27 Westminster Road, Syosset, New York 11791.
