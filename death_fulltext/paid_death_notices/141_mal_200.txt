
        BELL-O. Nile. On Monday, October 19th, 1998. O. Nile Bell of Potomac, Maryland, beloved husband of Joanne Mackey Bell, devoted father of Anne Ashley Bell, brother of Margaret Bell Smith. He is also survived by several brothersin-law, sisters-in-law & numerous nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Joseph Gawler's Sons, 5130 Wisconsin Avenue at Harrison Street, N.W. on Wednesday from 6-8 P.M. Services will be held at the National Presbyterian Church, 4101 Nebraska Avenue, N.W. Washington, D.C. 20016, Thursday at 11 A.M. Interment will be held in Clarksville, Missouri. Memorial contributions may be made in his name to the National Center for Leadership, C/O The National Presbyterian Church or the charity of your choice.
