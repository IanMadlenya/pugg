
        HALPER--Barry. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Barry Halper, on December 18, 2005. Services will be held on Tuesday at B'nai Jeshurun, 1025 South Orange Avenue, Short Hills, N.J. at 11AM. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
