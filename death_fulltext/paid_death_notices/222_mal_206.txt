
        DUKE--Paul. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, staff and volunteers of Educational Broadcasting Corporation, licensee of Thirteen/WNET and WLIW21, we express our profound sadness at the passing of Paul Duke and extend our condolences to his family. A journalist of the utmost integrity and fairness, Paul Duke represented the highest ideals of the profession. Always on target, always asking the right questions, he illuminated the critical issues of the day for countless Americans during his long tenure as moderator of ''Washington Week in Review'' on PBS. Paul Duke was an inspiration to all who work in public broadcasting, and we will strive to live up to the legacy of brilliance he left us. Steven Rattner, Chairman William F. Baker, President
