        CROLL-Irma. The President, Boards of Governors and Overseers, Faculty and Students, and Administration of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion record with profound sorrow the passing of Irma Croll, beloved widow of Abram Croll and generous benefactor of the Abram and Irma Croll Scholarship Fund. Irma Croll was an inspiration to all knew her. Her commitment and devotion to the mission of the Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion and the ideals and values of Judaism remain an abiding legacy. We shall not see her kind again. Generations of students will be grateful to her for her zealous constant interest and support through the years. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to her beloved family whose sorrow we share. Burton Lehman, Chairman of the Board Rabbi David Ellenson, President Rabbi Paul M. Steinberg, Special Assistant to the President
        CROLL-Irma Lowenstein. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Irma Lowenstein Croll, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        CROLL-Irma. The Womens Auxiliary of Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York sorrowfully notes the death of our esteemed, longtime Board member, Irma Croll. We extend sincere condolences to her family. Eve Haberman, President Elaine Ramson, Secretary
