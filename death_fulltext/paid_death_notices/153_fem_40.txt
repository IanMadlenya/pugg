
        SPENCER-Marian. The Board of Directors of ARC, Fort Washington Senior Center, on behalf of the seniors and staff, mourn the passing of their dedicated Director. We extend sincere condolences to her family and friends. A Memorial Service will be held Friday October 8, 1999 at 11:30 A.M. at ARC, 21 Wadsworth Avenue. Donations may be made to ARC, Fort Washington Senior Center.
