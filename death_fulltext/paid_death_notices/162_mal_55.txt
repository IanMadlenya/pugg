
        MUSTALISH-Anthony W., on July 3, 2000, age 84, of Iselin, NJ. Husband for 61 years of Wanda (Machnowski). Father of Dr. Anthony C. and his wife Dr. Elayne Klarsfeld Mustalish and Paula Salzano and her husband Michael and Dr. Roger W. and his wife Sue Fisher Mustalish. Grandfather of Rachel, David and Peter Mustalish and Paula Salzano Scott and Michael Salzano. Born in Newark NJ, employed as a Quality Control Inspector for the Homer Tool & Die Company of Newark for 25 years before retiring in 1989. Funeral from the Thomas J. Costello Funeral Home, 200 Cooper Ave., Iselin, NJ, on Friday July 7 at 9AM. Funeral Liturgy at 10AM in St. Cecilia Church, Iselin. In lieu of flowers , family suggests contributions to St. Cecilia Church.
