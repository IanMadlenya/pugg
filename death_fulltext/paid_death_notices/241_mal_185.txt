
        KOLATCH--Alfred J., on February 7, 2007. Rabbi, founder of Jonathan David Publishers, noted author of ''The Jewish Book of Why'' and many other titles. Laid to rest on February 8, 2007. Mourned by Thelma, Jonathan, David, Duan, Arthur, Helen and all those whose lives were touched by his kindness and his writings. His contributions to the Jewish people live on.
