
        SCHRADER-Martin. Died peacefully on May 28th, surrounded by the family he created. Beloved husband of Ceil, adored father of Howard, Dan and Esther and blessed grandfather of Hannah and Gregory. Magazine publisher, writer, intellect, adventurer, creator, raconteur, Dad was a man of unparalleled energy and unstinting love. He built a life of extraodinary success, happiness and devotion to us all. Services today 12noon at Temple Israel of Northern Westchester, Croton, New York.
