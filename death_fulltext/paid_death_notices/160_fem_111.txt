
        JACOBS-Shirley. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society express profound regret at the loss of Shirley Jacobs of San Diego. We extend heartfelt condolences to her son, Dr. Irwin Jacobs, and daughter-inlaw, Joan Jacobs. Dr. Jacobs, chairman and CEO of Qualcomm Incorporated, received the Albert Einstein Award, the American Technion Society's highest honor, and will receive an Honorary Doctorate from the Technion in June.
