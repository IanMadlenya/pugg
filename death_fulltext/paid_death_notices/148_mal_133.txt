
        VOGEL-Wynn E. Mr. Erwin (Wynn) Vogel known as a true Renaissance man passed away Sunday, May 2nd, 1999. Mr. Vogel was an arranger and composer of music, a poet, a painter and designer, and an author of many ''how to'' books. A member of the Christian Science Youth Forum, Mr. Vogel was also an avid hiker. Wynn did it all, may he rest in peace.
