
        ALLEN-Davis B. 82. Six year Fort Lauderdale resident, coming from Hampton, NY, died Thursday, May 13, 1999, in Fort Lauderdale. He was a member of the Yale Club and the Santara Association. Survived by brother Thomas Allen of Santa Fe, NM, and friend Juan Garcia of Fort Lauderdale. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, May 18, 1999, at 7 PM, at Fred Hunter's Fort Lauderdale Home, 718 South Federal Highway, Fort Lauderdale, FL.
