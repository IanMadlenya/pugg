
        RODEN-J. Kenneth of Elizabeth, NJ. Devoted grandfather of April Stone of Tinton Falls, NJ & Jennifer Jones of Elizabeth, NJ. Press agent, educator, publicist and founder of the Junior Achievement of Union County died on January 6, 2000. A concelebrated funeral Mass was offered at the Immaculate Conception RC Church, Elizabeth, NJ on January 10th. Entombment Holy Cross Cemetery, North Arlington, NJ. Contributions in his memory may be made to Junior Achievement of Union County, 1101 Bristol Road, Mountainside, NJ 07092.
