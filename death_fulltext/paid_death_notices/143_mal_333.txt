
        BLUM-Jerome W. Age 94, on December 24, 1998. Beloved husband of Selma, devoted father to Anne Kelleher (deceased), loving grandfather of eight and greatgrandfather of nine. Previous marriages were to Edith Victorson & Dorothy Brill (both deceased). Retired as Executive V.P. of United Merchant's & Manufacturers in 1968 after 35 years. Among many honors, listed in Who's Who in America for several decades, a founder of the American Arbitration Assn., elected President of Textile Fabrics Assn., elected President of Textile Fabric Distributors, Assn., and granted lifetime membership in the National Trust for Historic Preservation has become one of the country's leading antique dealers since retiring. He will be greatly missed by his family, many friends and business associates over the years. Services will be December 29, at 12:15 P.M., at Riverside Chapel at 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue in N.Y.
