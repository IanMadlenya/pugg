
        STANIFORD--Ellen Breed, 76, of Lawrence, LI on April 27, 2005. Beloved wife of Foye F. Staniford, Jr. Mother of Ellen, Louise, Foye M. and William, grandmother of Benjamin, Sarah, Cordelia and Caroline. An adventurous, spirited and giving soul. She was a devoted docent at the American Museum of Natural History and The Asia Society in New York City. She will be remembered and missed by many. A Memorial Service will be held 11AM Saturday, April 30, at Trinity-St. John's Church, Hewlett, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: Volunteer Office, American Museum of Natural History, Central Park West at 79 Street, NY, NY 10024 or to: St. Timothy School, 8400 Greenspring Ave, Stevenson, MD 21153.
