
        TOBIN-John E., of Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ, in his 73rd year, died Jan. 19, 1997. He was senior partner with the law firm Dorsey & Whitney, NYC. Beloved husband of Margaret (nee Swope). Loving father of John E. Jr., Brian D., Paul C. and the late Catherine Julia Tobin. Adoring grandfather of 9, devoted brother of the late Margaret C. Tobin. A memorial mass will be celebrated Saturday, Jan. 25, 1997 at 11 AM in St. Luke's RC Church, Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society would be appreciated. For information, Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, New Jersey 201-444-7650.
