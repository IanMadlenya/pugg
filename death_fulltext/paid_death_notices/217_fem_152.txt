
        SLIFMAN -- Doris (Wein traub), February 15, died peacefully from complications of lung cancer. Beloved wife of the late Ted. Adored mother of Audrey Weiner and Jeff Solomon, Sheree and Joel Green. Devoted and loving grandmother of Todd and Walter Green. Michael, Jenny and Lucy Solomon knew how much she loved them. Family was the most important part of her life and she will be missed every day. Funeral services are private. Shiva will be observed.
