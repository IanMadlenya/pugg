
        SAYOVITZ-Irene Gertzman. 89, on January 29, 1999, at home, 18446 Aberdeen Rd., Jamaica Estates, NY. Devoted wife of David. Beloved mother of Carol Brennan and Paula Beck. Adored grandmother of Richard Beatty, Joanna Maddock, Bonnie Roston and Wendy Sauer. Proud greatgrandmother of Sarah, Andrew, Steven, Thomas, Lauren, Elyse, Brett and Jenna. Love and loyalty were the creed she lived by. We who basked in her warmth are deeply grateful. We will remember and miss her for the rest of our lives. Services at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, NY, Sunday, 12 noon.
