Dear friend of Margaret Bakhorie, Jonathan Anshel, Susan Gould, Helga Karlin, Lisa Karlin, Anita Francis, Lila Katz and Hassan. We cherish the memories of her strength, independence, creativity, intelligence, wit, and zest for life. A celebration of her life is planned at a date to be announced.
