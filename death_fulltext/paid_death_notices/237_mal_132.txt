
        KENNEDY--Will. Died at home on October 12. Survivors: his wife Frances, children, Katharine, Billy, Jane and Emily, and 10 grandchildren. Will was professor at Union Theological Seminary from 1979-1994, formerly on staff at the World Council of Churches and recently Mayor of Black Mountain, NC.
