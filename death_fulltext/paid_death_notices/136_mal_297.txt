
        STRAUSS-Simon D. The Trustees and Administration of the Manhattan School of Music mourn with deep sadness the death of our dear friend, Simon D. Strauss. A member of our Board of Trustees for two decades, we recall his wisdom, generosity and commitment. His friendship and loyalty will be remembered with warm affection. We extend our deepest sympathies to his dear wife Janet and all the members of the Strauss family. Marta Istomin, President David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees
