
        LAUREN-Charles B., 95. Of North Branford, CT, Dorset, VT and formerly of Bronxville, NY, died peacefully on December 31st in North Branford. Beloved husband of the late Priscilla Lauren, father of Cynthia Zellmer of Golden, CO and Clark Lauren of Sarasota, FL, and grandfather of Matthew Zelmer and Wendy Bellas of Denver, CO and Alexandra Lauren of Sarasota, FL. He is also survived by three great - grandchildren. Memorial service will be held at 3 PM, January 11th, at Zion Episcopal Church, Manchester, VT. Donations in his name may be made to Hartwick College, 1 Hartwick Dr., Oneonta, NY 13820.
