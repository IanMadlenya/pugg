
        STEINER-Fannie Glasser. On Sunday, March 2, 1997, died peacefully in her 85th year. Loving and caring mother of Albert and Arthur. Sister of Harry Glasser. Grandmother of Gary, Allison, Adrienne, Ashley, Gigi and Alison. From all who knew her best she will be forever missed. We love you and please ''do not worry.'' Service Monday, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
