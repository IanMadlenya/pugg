
        FRIEDMAN--Murray, 95, died April 27, 2004. Loving and trusted brother and partner of forty years of Harry Friedman and the uncle of Steve Sharp and Robert Friedman, of the Friedman & Friedman Agency. He will always be remembered with love and respect. He was a trusted and consummate insurance broker whose exuberant joy of life and high ethical standards and embracing personality was inspirational.
