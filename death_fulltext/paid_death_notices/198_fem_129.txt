
        FRIEND--Elizabeth Pierson. Age 69, on July 18, 2003, at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania. Devoted wife, mother, and volunteer. Exhibited and prize-winning painter in water colors, acrylics, and oils. Survived by her loving husband Theodore; brother John; sons Tad and Pier; daughter Timmie; and grandson Wilson. Interment private, at the Bryn Mawr Presbyterian Church. For information, call the Chadwick and McKinney Funeral Home, Ardmore, PA; 610-6426303. Memorial service at the same church in late October. Donations may be sent to Swarthmore College or The Shipley School. >>AD#
