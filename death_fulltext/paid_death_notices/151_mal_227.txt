
        MORSE-Julius P., 93, at his Brooklyn home, August 7, 1999. A lover of words, music, science, mathematics, and astronomy, he was a professional photographer and offset lithographer. He became an enthusiastic folk dancer, and an active member of the Senior Citizens League of Flatbush. He once appeared on stage with the Pina Bausch Dance Company, and was officially honored in 1997 as an Extraordinary Elder. He is mourned by his loving wife Anne, children Robert and Linda Heller, and two surviving brothers, Henry and Irving. His kindness, wit and good humor will be missed by family, friends, and all who had the good fortune to know him.
