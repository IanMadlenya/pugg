
        CANTRELL--Nina M. (nee Majorino). Renowned stone sculptor, of Port Washington, NY, passed away on March 27th, 2004, after a courageous battle with cancer. Nina was a creative spirit who inspired everyone she touched. She was a visionary and pioneer in the use of colored, semiprecious stone as a sculpted medium. A teacher for over 40 years, Nina was a guest lecturer on the art of stone sculpture, both here and abroad. The recipient of many awards, her body of work lives on as a tribute to her fearless creativity and intense determination. Nina was preceded in death by her beloved husband Simon Larach, brothers Richard, Gus, Dino, Henry and sister Blanche. She is survived by her sister Sylvia. She was the loving mother of daughter Gail Farrell (Richard Friedlander) and son Richard Cantor (Jill). She was also the loving grandmother of Max, Leon, Erika, Jamie and Daniel. She is also survived by her husband's dear sons, David (Marilyn) and Richard Larach. Nina also leaves behind many special fiends and colleagues. Reposing at Austin F. Knowles, Inc, Funeral Home, 128 Main St, Port Washington, Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Interment Pinelawn Memorial Park, Pinelawn, LI.
