
        STOTT - Catherine (Cappe) Searles. Age 79. Died July 1, 1999 at her home in Wickenburg, AZ. She is survived by her son Robert Doulton Stott, Jr. of Grand Junction, CO., her daughter Deborah S. Stasi and granddaughters Marly and Laura Stasi of Red Bank, N.J. Memorial service will be held 10AM Saturday, July 24th at Saint Eustace Church in Lake Placid, New York. Memorial donations may be made in her honor to the American Red Cross.
