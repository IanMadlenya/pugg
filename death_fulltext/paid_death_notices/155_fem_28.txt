
        PETERS-Viola (Funaro). On November 30, 1999 at 99 years of age. Retired, long-time vocal coach and pianist, faculty member at Juilliard School of Music. Widow of the late Bruno Funaro. Reposing Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 117 W. 72 Street, N.Y. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, 10AM, Holy Trinity Church, W. 82 St. Contributions to your favorite charity.
        PETERS-Viola. The entire Juilliard School community is deeply saddened by the death of Viola Peters who was a member of the faculty from 1931-1994. With dedication, enormous energy, and exceptional expertise, she worked with singers for many decades, and instilled in them a love of their art and of the profession. Her legacy continues through their artistry. Mary Rodgers Guettel, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
