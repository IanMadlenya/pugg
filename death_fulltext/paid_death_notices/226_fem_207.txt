
        SMOLEN--Selma. Passed away peacefully at home on November 28, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Arthur E. Smolen, adored mother of Ellen Smolen, cherished sister of Charlotte Davidoff, brother-in-law Ed Davidoff, and sister-in-law Fay Teviowitz, beloved aunt and dear friend to many. Her smile, sharp wit and loving heart will be dearly missed by all who knew her. Funeral services to be held Wednesday, November 30, 2005, at 11:30AM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St & Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers donations to the Alzeimher's Association would be appreciated.
