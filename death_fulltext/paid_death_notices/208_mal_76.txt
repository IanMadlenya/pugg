
        DOWLING--John L. 66, on May 4, 2004, died at his home in Boca Grande, Florida. Beloved husband of Hebe S. Dowling. Devoted father of Meaghan, John and Peter Dowling. Cherished father-inlaw of Michael Chorske and Rebecca Dowling. Loving grandfather of Jack Chorske. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Sunday, May 9, 7-9 PM. A funeral mass will be held at St. Patrick's Cathedral, Fifth Avenue at 51st Street on Monday, May 10 at 10:30 AM.
        DOWLING--John L. The Board of Trustees of St. Patrick's Cathedral records with sorrow the passing of John L. Dowling who has served as a Trustee of St. Patrick's Cathedral for 15 years with great distinction and dedication, serving as Corporate Secretary and for many years as Chairman of the Cathedral Maintenance Committee. His total commitment to the Board, his professional skills and leadership will be sorely missed by the members of the Board. To his bereaved wife and children we extend our heartfelt condolences. Edward Cardinal Egan President Rev Msgr James K. Vaughey Executive Director
