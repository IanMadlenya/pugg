
        PLATT-Lillian Gross.Died June 23, 1999 in Belfast, Maine. Born in New York on June 1, 1912, she resided in Neponsit, N.Y. for many years. She is survived by her children Jane of Liberty, Me., Wendy of Briarcliff Manor, NY and Jeffrey of Davis, Fla. and six grandchildren. Funeral services are private.
