
        FAULKNER--Avery. Washington, DC area architect, Avery C. Faulkner, FAIA, died in Delray Beach, FL on Feb 21, following a stroke. For his kindness, optimism, and charming story-telling technique, he will be dearly missed. Donations may be made to St. Alban's School in Washington, or the National Cathedral. A memorial service will be announced.
