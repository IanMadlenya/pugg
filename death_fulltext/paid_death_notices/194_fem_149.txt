
        ROTHENBERG - Laura E. Died comfortably at home on March 20th, 2003 at age 22. Beloved daughter of Dr. Jon & Mary Rothenberg. Loving partner of Bryan Doerries. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Children's Hospital for the Laura Rothenberg Children's Hospital Fund, C/O Laura's parents, or Children's Hospital Trust, 138 Harvard Street, Brookline, MA. 02446.
