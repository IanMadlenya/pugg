
        WEDEEN-Dr. Peter. Of Brooklyn, N.Y. died after a long illness. A graduate of Columbia University & NYU Medical School. He served in the U.S. Navy. He was on the staff of Brooklyn Jewish Hospital, assistant professor of medicine at Downstate Medical Center and assistant chief medical officer for the NYC Fire Department. He was the husband of the late honorable professor, Shirley Ullman Wedeen of Brooklyn College and trustee of The City University of New York. Survived by his sons Van and Glenn and his sister Bernice Goodman and grandchildren Sophia, Henry, Alexander and Benjamin. Donations to the NYC Fire Department. Services today 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
