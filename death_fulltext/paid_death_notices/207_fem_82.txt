
        CASTILLO--Julia Falla, of Key Biscayne, Florida and formerly of Darien, CT and Havana, Cuba died on April 11, of cancer. In addition to her beloved husband, Pedro Castillo, she is survived by her three children, Pedro Castillo III of Manhattan, Miguel Castillo of Hilton Head, SC and Ana Castillo Ericksen of London, their spouses, seven grandchildren and one greatgrandchild.
