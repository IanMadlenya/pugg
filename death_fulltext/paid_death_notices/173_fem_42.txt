
        KLEIN-Judy Graf. Dear sweet Ma. Mother, daughter, sister, friend, artist and architect. Judy Graf Klein passed away in her home on June 1. She is survived by her daughters, Adina Sabrina Klein and Oleanna Joanna Graf Klein; her mother, Diane M. Graf; and her brothers, Peter M. Graf and Ronald J. Graf and their families. The space your absence creates in our hearts is as vast as the universe. The funeral will be held Monday, June 4, at 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue (81st Street). Donations may be made to Greenwich House Pottery, 16 Jones Street, New York, NY 10014.
