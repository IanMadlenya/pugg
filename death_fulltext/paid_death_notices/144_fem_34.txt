
        CHAMBERS-Isabel. Died on January 3rd, 1999. Born in New York City on July 2nd, 1909. She was a life-long resident of New York City. Daughter of the late Margaret and William H. Finegan; beloved wife of the late Norman; mother of Denise; grandmother of Elaine, Sue, Marilyn and Debora; great grandmother of Jennifer, Douglas, Skye, Randall and Brandon. She will be missed and remembered with love.
