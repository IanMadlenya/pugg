
        RAY--Talton F. Editor and publisher, The Francis Press, age 66. Died peacefully at home in Washington, DC, on December 31, 2005, after a courageous battle with brain cancer. Beloved husband of Lilli for 42 years, he is also survived by daughters Justina and Nicola, grandson Kai, son-in-law Jay Malcolm, brothers Lansing and David Ray, and sister-in-law Gina, sister and brother-in-law Herta and Richard Benke, and eight nieces and nephews. Resident of Saltaire, Fire Island, since 1968. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions can be made to a fund in Talton's memory at Mount Hope Housing Co, Inc, attn: Shaun Belle, 2003-05 Walton Ave, Bronx, NY 10453; 718299-2051. A private memorial service is being planned.
