
        JORDANO--Don R., 82, of Murray Hill, NYC, died suddenly at home on Wednesday, April 26. A mass will be celebrated 11am on Saturday May 6th in the Church of the Visitation, Brick, NJ. An Air Force veteran of WW II, he was a recipient of the Purple Heart and Silver Star with two Oak Leaf Clusters. A member of the NY Boys Club, he also volunteered with Catholic Charities and the Metropolitan Museum and Opera. Born in Jersey City, NJ, he lived in Newark, NJ before moving to Manhattan in 1957. He is survived by his sister, Janet A. Cosimo and six nieces and nephews.
