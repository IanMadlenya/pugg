
        ELSTER-Grace (nee Eil), 92, on January 19, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Loving mother of Sondra Elster Mayer and the late Charles Elster. Devoted grandmother of Jeff Mayer, Jessica Mayer and Jared Elster. Cherished sister of Betty Sonken, the late Max Eil, Harry Eil, Ruth Gross and Bernard Eil. Survived by many loving nieces and nephews. Fled Czarist Russia through Ellis Island in 1908, married, built a home and family, and lived to see a new millennium. She will be greatly missed. Services Sunday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY, (516) 487-9600.
