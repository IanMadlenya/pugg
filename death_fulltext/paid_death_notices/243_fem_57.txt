
        LOEWY--Hanna, of Manhattan, died March 31. She was 81. Born in Vienna to Psychiatrist/Neurologist Paul and Alice ''Lili'' Loewy, she immigrated in 1938 and later became a vital part of the circle of intellectuals, writers, and artists that gathered at the Princeton home of her mother and stepfather, the philosopher Erich Kahler. Burial was in Princeton Cemetery. Memorial Service will be announced.
