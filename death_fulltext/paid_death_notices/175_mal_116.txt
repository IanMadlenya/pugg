
        WEISS-Lewis F. Age 74. President of H. Weiss Co., Inc. died August 10, 2001, after a prolonged illness. Beloved husband of Ellen Berlin Weiss; father of Dr. Roberta Weiss, James Weiss, and Elizabeth Weiss, father-in-law of Dr. James Edelstein, Jeffrey Dellon, and Lori Ross Weiss; grandfather of Dana, Emily, Hilary, and Michael; brother of Marian Cantor and Suzanne Silver. Mr. Weiss graduated from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, after serving in the Army Air Corps during World War II. Funeral services will be held Sunday, August 12, 2001, at Riverside Memorial Chapels, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY, at 2 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
