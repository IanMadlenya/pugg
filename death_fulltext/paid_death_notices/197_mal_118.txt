
        BENFORADO--David M. of Woodbury, MN, 77, formerly of New York, NY, passed away peacefully on Sunday, 1 June 2003, at the Pillars HealthEast Hospice, with his family at his side, after a long battle with cancer. He was born in New York City, NY, 17 November 1925, graduating Columbia University in 1948. He is survived by an adoring family who will miss his loving and compassionate ways, children Mark, Marcia, Dean and their families. There will be a Memorial Service 26 July 2003, at 10:30 A.M. at the Woodbury United Methodist Church in Woodbury, Minnesota. >>AD#
