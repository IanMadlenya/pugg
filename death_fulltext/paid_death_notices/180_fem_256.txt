
        MILLER-Barbara. Suddenly on January 26, 2002, died in Brooklyn, NY. Graduate of NYC's High School of Performing Arts and Columbia University. Playwright, editor, publisher, author, real estate sales representative. Beloved life partner of Donna Barnes. She is also survived by her sister Joyce Westover, nieces Julia, Darcelle, Jennifer and nephew Alfred. Her love of good food, cherished friends, spirited conversation, music and travel contributed to Barbara's vibrancy. Interment private. Donations to Vieques (Puerto Rico) Humane Society.
