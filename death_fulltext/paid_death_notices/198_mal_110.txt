
        BONSAING--Edward G. ''Jerry'', died at his home at age 57 in Southampton, L.I. after a long battle with cancer. He is survived by his beloved sister, Barbara Feeney and his brother-in-law Patrick Feeney and was the loving uncle of Patrick Joseph Feeney, his wife Daisy and their daughters Isabella and Juliana. Barbara Ann Milmore and her husband John and their children Elizabeth, Kaitlyn, John and Christina. Jerry held several important posts at leading advertising agencies among them Wm. Esty and Bates USA and was one of the start up employees at the Backer & Spielvogel Agency. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Sunday, July 13, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial will be on Monday, July 14, 11AM at Blessed Sacrament R.C. Church on West 71st Street between Columbus and Broadway Avenues. Graveside services at Sacred Heart of Jesus and Mary Cemetery in Southampton, L.I. at a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, NYC. >>AD#
