
        HEDER-MANDY-Thomas T. 55, on May 22, 1998 in Sarasota, FL. Loving husband of Sondra Kay. Father of Tyson, Christy Collins and Brian Hutson. Son of Klara Heder and nephew of Laszlo de Mandy. Tom spent his entire career in the hotel and club business and was the General Manager of the Yale Club of NYC from 1987-1994. A memorial service in New York will be planned for a later date. Memorial contributions may be made to the Marie Selby Botanical Gardens, 811 South Palm Avenue, Sarasota, FL 34236.
