
        CARLSON-Jane. The Juilliard School mourns the loss of Jane Carlson, honored last year for fifty years of distinuished service as a member of the College and PreCollege Division faculties. A noted piano pedagogue, her dedication to her work has been an inspiration to her many students, friends and colleagues around the world. Mary Rodgers Guettel Chairman, Board of Trustees Joseph W. Polisi President
