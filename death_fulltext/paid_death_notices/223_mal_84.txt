
        RATHER--James B. III. Passed away August 6 at his home in Port Washington, NY. He was 65. A former federal prosecutor, he was diagnosed with Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis in 1990. Survivors include his wife Amalia, two sons, a daughter, sister, brother and four grandchildren. He battled ALS courageously, never despaired, inspired all who knew him and was deeply loved. Memorial service 9:30 AM August 11 at the Congregational Church of Manhasset. In lieu of flowers send donations to the ALS Association Greater NY Chapter, Suite 1304, 116 John St., NY, NY 10038.
        RATHER--James B., Esq. The Board of Trustees and staff of The ALS Association Greater New York Chapter are deeply saddened by the loss of our esteemed Board member and good friend, James B. Rather, Esq. Jim's efforts contributed greatly to raising awareness of ALS (Lou Gehrig's Disease) and to advancing research in the search for a cure. His courage served as an inspiration to us all. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his devoted wife, Amalia, and family. Alan Griffith John L. Ernst Dorine Gordon Jacqueline Reinhard
