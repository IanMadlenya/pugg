
        LOW--Martin L., of Sleepy Hollow, NY, died on May 25. A WWII fighter pilot, he was at Pearl Harbor and D-Day. He owned a motion picture studio in Manhattan. He was recognized by the NY State Assembly for his work as Chairman of the Ossining Open Door Family Medical Centers. He is survived by his loving wife Alice, two surviving children, Andy and Kathy, and four admiring grandchildren. Memorial service at a later date.
