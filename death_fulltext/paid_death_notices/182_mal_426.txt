
        FRANK-Hans J. The Boards of Directors and staff of Selfhelp Community Services and United Help are deeply saddened by the loss of our esteemed Board member, Hans J. Frank, Esq. As a dedicated advocate for the Nazi victim community, Mr. Frank generously brought his legal expertise and commitment to the Boards' work during his 28 years of service. We will miss his inspired leadership and unwavering support. Our sympathy is with his family.
