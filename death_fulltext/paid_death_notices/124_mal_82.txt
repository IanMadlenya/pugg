
        RABB-Norman S. The officers, members and staff of The American Jewish Committee are deeply saddened by the death of Norman Rabb, a devoted and long-time leader of AJC. He gave generously of his time, energy & resources, serving as an AJC Vice President, member of our Board of Governors, Chair of the Interreligious Affairs Commission, Honorary President of the New England Region, and Chairman of our Palm Beach Campaign. We were proud to honor him with our Distinguished Service Award and our National Human Relations Award, along with his wife Eleanor. His infectious spirit, wise counsel and unyielding commitment to the Jewish people and to the betterment of the human condition will be sorely missed. We send our heartfelt condolences to Eleanor and the rest of the grieving family. Robert S. Rifkind, President David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
        RABB-Norman. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Jeannette Solomon, a long-time member of our executive committee, on the loss of her dear brother. Vera Stern, President On behalf of the Board of Directors of America-Israel Cultural Foundation
