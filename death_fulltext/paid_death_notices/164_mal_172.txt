
        WELSH-Vernon M., 92. Of Old Lyme, CT, died peacefully at his home on September 4, 2000. Mr. Welsh, formerly of Ridgefield, CT, was a resident of Old Lyme for many years. He served as chairman of the Old Lyme Historic District Commission and his home on Lyme Street is on the National Register of Historic Places as a Historic District Property. He also was past Vice President of the Library Board in Old Lyme. Mr. Welsh was past Vice President of Communications at General Dynamics and was one of three editors of Dynamic America, a history of the General Dynamics Corporation. He also was a consultant for CEIR and Boeing Corporation before he retired. He graduated Cum Laude from Dartmouth College in 1931. Mr. Welsh was preceded in death by his wife, Glenna M. Welsh, his grandson Calley Grant Nickerson and his granddaughter Victoria Welsh. He is survived by his wife Marcia Welsh of Old Lyme, Connecticut, daughter Jane Hamlin of Austin, TX, son Michael Welsh of Helena, MT, daughter Suzy Nickerson of Chatham, Massachusetts, son Mathew Welsh of Alexandria, Virginia, nine grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made, in his name, to the charity of your choice.
