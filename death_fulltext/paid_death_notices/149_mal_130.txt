
        PORTER-H. Boone, Rev. Canon, D.D. Age 76, of Southport, CT, former editor of The Living Church Magazine, and a revisor of the Episcopal Church's present Book of Common Prayer, husband of Violet Monser Porter, died on Saturday, June 5, 1999. Burial Service will be held Friday, June 11, 1999, at 11:00 AM in Trinity Episcopal Church, Southport, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to The Living Church Magazine, 816 East Juneau Avenue, Milwaukee, WI 53202.
