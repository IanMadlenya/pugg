
        SPIERER-Ida. Died peacefully on Dec. 7, 2001, at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Sol. Loving mother of Cynthia (Noah) Fleschner, Sandra (Nat) Geller and Rosalind (Jacob) Weinstein. Adored grandmother of nine and great grandmother of 21. She was the matriarch of our family and will be sorely missed.
