
        GREENBERG--Esther. The American Jewish Committee is deeply saddened by the death of Esther Greenberg, beloved mother of Alan C. Greenberg. Alan is one of AJC's most dynamic, generous and dedicated leaders. His allegiance to the Jewish people is resolute, as is his commitment to achieving a world at peace and in harmony. Such deeply rooted feelings of compassion and human kindness are surely Esther's legacy to her children, grandchildren and greatgrandchildren, as well as to us all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Alan and his family. E. Robert Goodkind, Pres David A. Harris, Exec Dir Jaime Kelstein, Asst Exec Dir
