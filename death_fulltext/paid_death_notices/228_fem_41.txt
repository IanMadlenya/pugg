
        EPSTEIN--Rita. The Officers and Members of the Board of Governors of the Jewish Home at Rockleigh, Russ Berrie Home for Jewish Living, note with profound sorrow the passing of Rita Epstein, wife of our Past President and Board member Carl Epstein who currently serves as President of the Jewish Home Foundation. The Epsteins' have been longtime friends and supporters of the Jewish Home. Rita was a very special person to all who were privileged to know her. She will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to her beloved husband Carl, her children Norie and Judy, her son-in-law Eric and her grandsons Michael and Zachary. May her name be for a blessing. Maggie Kaplen, President Charles P. Berkowitz Executive Vice President
