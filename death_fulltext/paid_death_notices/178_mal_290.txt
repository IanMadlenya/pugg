
        WALSH-Dr. George V., 78, November 8, 2001, Salisbury, MD. Professor of Philosophy Emeritus at Salisbury University. He was a graduate of Williams, Brown, and Princeton. He also taught at Hobart and Eisenhower. He leaves his wife, Catherine, three children, Michael, Maraed and Amy, grandchildren Henry and David, a sister, Margaret, 2 nieces and a nephew.
