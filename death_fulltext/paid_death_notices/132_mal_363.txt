
        MELINKER-Nathan. Born on January 17, 1923 in Brooklyn, died January 16, 1998 in Castro Valley, CA. He served in the Pacific during W.W. II and earned a Bronze Star and two Purple Hearts. He graduated from NYU in 1951 with a BSEd and was a licensed Physical Therapist. His beloved wife, Yolanda, predeceased him. He is survived by his daughter, Sharon Bond and three grandchildren; Fiona, Alana, and Michael.
