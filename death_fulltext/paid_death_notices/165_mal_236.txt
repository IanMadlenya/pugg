
        FARENGA-William T. On October 16, 2000. Dear friend of Henry O. Golightly, brother of Charles Farenga, long term member of NSID. He was a noted interior designer in the metropolitan area for many years. Memorial Service Friday October 20, 2000, at 11 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to The Animal Rescue Fund, Easthampton, New York.
