
        SCHOENBACH-Sophia Wolf, 97. Died Tuesday, March 26, 2001 at the Jewish Home and Hospital in New York City. Wife of the late Louis; loving and beloved mother of Morton and Jill; grandmother of Michael and Robert; great grandmother of Billy, Elizabeth and Eric, and Dana, Jamie and Jake. Long active in Women's American ORT, the Milk and Egg League of the City of Hope, and other philanthropic organizations, she was a cheerful dispenser of songs, jokes, and Hershey miniatures. We miss her dearly.
