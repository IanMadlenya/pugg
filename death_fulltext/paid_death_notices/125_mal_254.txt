
        BOWLING-James Chandler. Philip Morris Companies, Inc. notes with sorrow the death of retired Philip Morris Director and Executive James Chandler Bowling. Mr. Bowling served as a member of the Philip Morris Companies, Inc., Board of Directors and as Assistant to the Chairman of the Board from August 1969 until his retirement in 1984. He also served as Director, Corporate Affairs, and was a member of the Board of Directors of Miller Brewing Company. Mr. Bowling, a descendent of John Rolfe, the first colonist to raise tobacco in America, joined Philip Morris in 1951. The funeral will be held at 3:00 PM Saturday at Saint Luke's Church in Darien, CT. A graveside service will be held at 3:00 PM Monday at Lexington Cemetery in Lexington, KY. Memorial contributions may be made to the University of Louisville, University of Kentucky, Midway College or Berea College. The Philip Morris Board and employees pay warm tribute to his memory and extend their deepest sympathy to his family.
