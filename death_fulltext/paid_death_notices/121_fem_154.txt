
        HYMAN-Josephine. Age 92, of New York City. Widow of S. Frank Hyman. Beloved daughter of late Louis and Sophie Cohen. Devoted sister of Jeanette Goldfarb, Adele Zemansky and E. Malcolm Cohen, M.D. Survived by caring sister-inlaw Sylvia Cohen and numerous nieces and nephews. Always gracious. Graveside services at Beth David Cemetary on Sunday, February 23, in Elmont, NY.
        HYMAN-Josephine. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the sister of its longtime member, Dr. E. Malcolm Cohen. May her entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
