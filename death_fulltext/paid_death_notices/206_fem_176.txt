
        WHALEN--Ruth Anne Lynch, 93, of Albany, NY died, Tuesday, March 16, 2004 at Saint Peter's Hospice Inn. She was the wife of the late Frank M. Lynch and the late Thomas M. Whalen, Jr. She is survived by a son John E. Lynch of Block Island, RI, two daughters Mary Lynch Toomy of Irvington, NY and Elizabeth Lynch Haluska of Atlanta, GA, also many other relatives. A Mass will be celebrated Saturday 11am at Saint Pius X, Crumitie Rd, Loudonville, NY and 11am at Saint Francis of Assisi Chapel, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
