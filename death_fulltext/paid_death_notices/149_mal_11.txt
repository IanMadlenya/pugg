
        MAUTNER-Milton S. Age 85, on May 29, 1999. Loving and dearly loved husband for 30 years of Peggy (Margaret Park) Mautner. Widely read, musically engaged, he was a man of fine intellect, wit, talent, goodness and dignity. He was for many years a writer and editor and was a veteran of World War II. In addition to his wife he is survived by an aunt Abeline and two nieces, Jean Ann Fineberg and Ellen Joan Fineberg. Irreplaceable and already missed. For further information please call Krtil Funeral Home, 212-744-3084.
