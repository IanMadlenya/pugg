
        GROSSMAN-Joseph Richard. On April 4, 2002, at 85 years of age. Beloved husband of Suri Grossman for 61 years. Loving father of Margaret, Eleanor and Leonard. Adoring grandfather of Joshua. A brilliant, thoughtful, gentle, kind man with a great sense of humor. He was loved and respected by all who knew him-family, friends and colleagues. He will be greatly and deeply missed. Shiva will be observed in the home of Suri Grossman.
