
        HENIG-Carolyn. Devoted wife of the late Philip, her husband of 61 years, loving mother of Andrea Clair, her husband Davis Iszard and the late Peter Henig, and beloved grandmother of Bradley and Elizabeth Clair, died at home on March 14, 2002, at the age of 84. Carolyn contributed to the art film industry in various capacities over the years. She devoted herself to her children and grandchildren, tirelessly supported them in difficult times, and imbuing them with her love of the arts. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street, Sunday, March 17th, 2-5pm.
