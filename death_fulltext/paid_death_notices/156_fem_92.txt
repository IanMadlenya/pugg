
        SCHEINBERG-Louise G. The family mourns the passing of Louise, beloved wife of Labe, loving daughter of Isabel Goldman, devoted mother of Susan Kristol, David Scheinberg, Ellen Ranard, Amy Jones, and grandmother to Rebecca, Anne and Joe Kristol, Steven and Carolyn Scheinberg, Ben and Daniel Ranard, and Sara, Emily and Claire Jones. Born February 7, 1930. Died January 8, 2000. Services will be held at 1 PM, on January 10, at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Rd, Scarsdale, NY.
        SCHEINBERG-Louise. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its longtime member and extends sympathy to her bereaved family. Marjorie Miller, President Richard J.
