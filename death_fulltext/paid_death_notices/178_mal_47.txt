
        HOLLANDER-Bertram. Attorney, 93. Native of New York. Died October 24, 2001. Beloved husband of Edith for 62 years. Loving father of Michael and father-in-law of Clare. Brother of the late Helen Fink and his twin brother Edward who died at the age of three days. Also survived by numerous nieces and nephews. Graduate of the University of Pennsylvania. Forever in our hearts.
