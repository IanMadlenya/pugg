
        FORELLE--Conrad. Of W. Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Westhampton Beach and Muttontown, NY, on June 27, 2004 at age 89 years. Husband of Blanche for 64 years, father of John, Anne, Jane and Frank; grandfather of Kevin McCarthy, Charles, Luke, Chas, Taylor Casey, Elizabeth and Grace. He was born in New York City and obtained his Bachelor and Master degrees in Civil Engineering from the City University of New York. After professional positions in Bermuda and New York, in 1950 he cofounded Schumacher & Forelle, Inc., an engineering and construction firm, from which he retired in 1979. Active in various charities of the Catholic Church on Long Island, he was invested in the Order of the Knights of the Holy Sepulcher over 40 years ago.
