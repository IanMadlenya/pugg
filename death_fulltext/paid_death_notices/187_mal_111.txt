
        RUBIN-Charles R. Justice of the New York State Supreme Court, died peacefully at 95 after a wonderful life filled with all God's blessings. He will be loved and remembered always by his beloved wife of 73 years Pauline, adoring children Alan and Sandra Rubin, and Arline and Howard Herman. He is the cherished grandfather of eight and treasured great grandfather of four, and leaves a sister, Sylvia Ray. A man of honor and integrity with a brilliant mind and a joyful and generous heart. His life was a blessing for all who knew him. Services Sunday 11:30 AM at Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
