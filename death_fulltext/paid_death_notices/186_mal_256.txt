
        ROSENBERG-Harold (Hal), 86, of New York City, devoted husband of Dorothy and brother of Martin, died July 2, 2002. A decorated First Lt. in the Army Air Force in WWII. He later helped establish U.S. Postal Service employee assistance programs. A wellknown and beloved member of Alcoholics Anonymous, he celebrated 40 years of continuous sobriety on January 14, 2002. A memorial service in celebration of Hal's life will be held Tuesday, July 30, 6pm, Marble Collegiate Church, 29th Street and Fifth Avenue, New York City.
