
        KYLE--Rita Murphy L. Beloved wife of the late Grattan B. Kyle and loving mother of Leslie, Lauren, Grattan and Daniele. Cherished grandmother of Hilary, Kyle, Marissa, Ryan and Fiona. Rita lived an exceptional life; with special verve, good humor, enormous generosity. Rita had the ability to make everyone she came in contact with feel special. She lived well, laughed often, and loved much. A mass of celebration and thanksgiving will be held December 30th, 10:00 am, at the Church of St. Mary Star of the Sea, 600 City Island Avenue, Bronx, New York.
