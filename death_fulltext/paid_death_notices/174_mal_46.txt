
        COLE-Donald, M.D. Died accidentally on June 8th at age 74. Survived by 3 children, Jonathan Cole of San Francisco, Deborah Cole of Palm Beach, FL & Alyson Cole-Frankel of Ann Arbor, MI. Dr. Cole was one of the leading specialists in cancer research and Chief of Surgical Oncology at St. Vincent's 1960-1975 and the author of numerous articles and books. He was a lifelong art collector and in his youth, was a professional woodwind musician in several big bands. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Y-Me Foundation, Branford, CT. 06405.
