
        AMOS-Harold. The Board of Directors and staff of the Josiah Macy, Jr. Foundation record with deep sorrow the death on February 26th of Dr. Harold Amos, an Honorary Director of the Foundation and member of the Board since 1974. We mourn the loss of our good friend and distinguished colleague and extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Clarence F. Michalis, Chairman of the Board June E. Osborn, MD, President
