
        BLAKE-Melvin N., DDS On behalf of the Faculty, we are saddened to learn of the loss of Melvin N. Blake, DDS, Clinical Professor of Pathology at Columbia University, on March 14, 1999. Dr. Blake was an esteemed member of the Columbia University Faculty for over 30 years & he was deeply devoted to the profession of dentistry and to the education of our dental students. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his brothers, Arnold & Ernest, to his sister Rhoda, and to his nieces and nephews. Memorial service April 11, 1999, 3PM Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St. New York, New York. Dean Allan J. Formicola, Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery
