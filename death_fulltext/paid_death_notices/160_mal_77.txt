
        SHOWE-Alexander Robert. Born July 14, 1920, died May 5, 2000. Mr. Showe passed after a sudden illness in Palm Beach, FL. Mr. Showe was born in Papa, Hungary. He graduated from a private preparatory school and came to the United States in 1939 to study at Columbia University. He served as a M.A.S.H. medic in the United States Army, stationed in the Pacific. After the war, Mr. Showe married Janet Lehrfeld of Philadelphia, PA. He is survived by his daughter, Mrs. Katherine Elizabeth Chavkin of Manhattan and his son Alan Robert Showe of Manhattan. His wife Janet Lehrfeld Showe predeceased him in 1994. In addition, he is survived by his two granddaughters Ms. Jessica Chavkin and Ms. Arielle Chavkin as well as his son-in-law Jeffrey Stuart Chavkin. Mr. Showe was President and founder of Showe Furniture and Showe Realty. He was associated with Scott Gordon Realty. He was founding member of the Bank of New Haven and served on various civic, business and charitable boards in both Connecticut and Florida. Among other positions, he most recently served as Chairman of Israel Cancer Association, USA. He will be remembered by many friends in Connecticut, New York, Florida and around the world. Services on Monday, 10 AM, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, corner of 91 Street.
