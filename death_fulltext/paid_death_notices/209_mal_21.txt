
        ALLEN--Walter B., Jr., longtime resident of Norwalk, CT, on May 31, 2004. Graduate of St. Paul's School, 1944 and Yale University, 1948. He was an avid sportsman and sailor. Beloved father of Kimberly Allen Walsh, and loving companion of Charlotte Vale-Allen. Brother of Polly Allen Mellon, Leslie Hart Tapscott and Patricia Wall. Memorial Service will be held at the Lawrence Funeral Home, Darien, CT on Friday, June 4, 2004 at 12 noon. Memorial donations may be sent to High Watch Farm, 62 Carter Road, Kent, CT. 06757.
