
        DAVEY-Joseph ''Mark''., 67, of Bradenton, Florida died January 9, 2000 in Bradenton. Born in the Bronx in, 1932 he moved to Bradenton after retiring from his position as Guidance Counselor at George Washington HS in NYC. He was the husband of Martha Davey and father of Colin, Joshua, Owen and the late Valerye. A memorial service is being planned and will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to any PBS or NPR broadcasting station, ACLU or any of its affiliates or Greenpeace.
