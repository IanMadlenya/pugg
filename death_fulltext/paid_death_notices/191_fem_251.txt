
        MARUM-Jean. 72, of New York City. Born in Jersey City. Died December 27th. Beloved daughter of the late Alf and Gertrude Marum. Jean worked at Chemical Bank and the YWCA of New York. During her retirement, she was involved in programs at the New School for Social Research. Jean was a supporter of the New York City art scene, an avid traveler and a loyal Democrat. She is survived by her cousins and devoted friends. Visiting Sunday, 2-4 PM, at McLaughlin Funeral Home, Jersey City, 201-798-8700. Private burial.
