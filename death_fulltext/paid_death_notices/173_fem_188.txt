
        FEUERSTEIN-Ruth S., 97. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY and Hallandale, FL, died on June 21, in Somerset, NJ. Beloved wife of the late Murray M, Mrs. Feuerstein was a 6th grade elementary school teacher in the NYC School system for 35 years. A dedicated life member of Hadassah, she received a National leadership award. She ran fundraising events for the Jewish Federation of Broward County and for the Democratic party. She was co-president of the Residents Council at the Central NJ Jewish Home, Somerset and was a bat mitzvah there in May. Survived by a daughter and son-in-law, Frances and Daniel P. Gold; a granddaughter, Suzanne Gold; a grandson and granddaughter - in - law, Nicholas and Marcy Gold; and two great grandsons.
