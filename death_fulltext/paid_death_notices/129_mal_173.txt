
        GROSSMAN-Max, 98, in Clifton, New Jersey on October 15, 1997. Beloved husband of Mollie Grossman for 70 years. Devoted father of Gloria, Bernard, and Sarah, and father-in-law of Elliott, Lenore, and Michael. He is also survived by seven grandchildren and one great-grandchild. He was founder in 1933 of M. Grossman & Son Inc. and Betmar Hats, and was active in the Millinery Manufacturers Association of New Jersey. His love and counsel will be missed.
