
        CARR--Dr. Elizabeth M. The Department of Dermatology at SUNY Downstate Medical Center and Kings County Hospital Center profoundly regrets the passing of our beloved faculty member, attending physician and dear friend, Dr. Elizabeth M. Carr. Dr. Carr was a superb physician who was dedicated to the care of patients with skin diseases and the teaching of dermatology. She was loved by her colleagues, patients and staff, all of whom extend their deepest sympathy to her family. Alan R. Shalita, M.D. Chairman
