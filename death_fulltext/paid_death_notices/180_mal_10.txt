
        BYERS-Paul. December 31, 2001. Survived by his wife Lucy Schneider, son Peter Byers of Seattle, Washington, granddaughter Jessica Byers of Arlee, Montana, and former wife Happie Byers of Seattle. An anthropologist who taught at Teachers College, Columbia University, he continued his research in biological rhythms underlying human communication after his retirement. Funeral private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the New York Steiner School Foundation, 15 East 79th Street, NY, NY 10021.
