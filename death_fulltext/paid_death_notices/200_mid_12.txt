
        HARFIELD--Henry, on Saturday, September 13, 2003 in New York City at age 89. Beloved father of Susan Harfield Leness Peck. Survived also by grandchildren Amanda Leness Gubbin and her husband Keith of London, England, Susan Leness Gilbert and her husband David of New York City, Anthony Harfield Leness and his wife Katharine of Boston, MA, Sarah Piel and her husband Douglas McAlinden of New York City and Katherine Piel of West Hollywood, CA. Great-grandfather of Henry Maxwell Gilbert, Eliza Leness Gilbert and Griffin Piel McAlinden. Pre-deceased in 1999 by his wife of 59 years, Marion Bussang Harfield and in 1996 by his daughter, Ellen Harfield Piel. Yale '34 and Columbia Law School '37. At his death, he was of counsel to Shearman & Sterling. A memorial service will be held at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 90th St & Fifth Avenue at 10:30am on Thursday, December 18, 2003. Memorial donations may be made to Yale University, Class of '34, P.O. Box 2038, New Haven, CT 06521-2038 or to East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978.
