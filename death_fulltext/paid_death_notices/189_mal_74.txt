
        TERIAN-Peter G. The Board of Trustees, Staff, Administration and Families at the New York Foundling Hospital mourn the loss of Peter G. Terian, whose generosity of spirit so enhanced the lives of the thousands of children in our care. We send our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Juliana, daughter, Olivia, and to his friends and associates.
