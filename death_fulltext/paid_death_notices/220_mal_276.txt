
        ROBINS -- Stanley, 80, of Ocean Township, formerly of West Orange, died Tuesday, May 24 at St. Barnabas Hospice, Long Branch. He was born in the Bronx, NY and raised in Brooklyn. He is survived by his loving wife of 54 years, Janice; his children Andrew Robins and Dr. Michele Schultz Robins of Wayside and Rachael and William Barish of West Orange; and his three grandchildren Jake, Evan and Ali Robins.
