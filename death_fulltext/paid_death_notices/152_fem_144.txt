
        MEHRER-Sophia. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our beloved longtime member, Sophia Mehrer. We will miss her loyalty to our Congregation and deep commitment to our Homeless Shelter and issues of social justice. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her daughter, Joanna Kapner, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President Jay Kranis, Community Concerns Chair
