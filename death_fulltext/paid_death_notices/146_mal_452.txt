
        TOM-Henry. Died on March 22, 1999. Veteran of W.W. II, received a Purple Heart. Served in African and Italian invasions. Graduated from Long Island University. Dear brother of Ann Fong, Jane, Alfred, Arthur, Elizabeth Yee and Jack Yee and the late Gertrude Chin and Helen Seetoo, as well as many nieces and nephews. ''Ginny'' (Hank) will be missed by all who loved him. Friends may call on Friday, March 26, from 3-8PM at Chinese Cheung Sang Funeral Home, 22 Mulberry Street, New York, New York.
