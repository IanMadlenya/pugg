
        COUFOS-Louis P., 82 of Bay Harbor Islands, Florida, formerly of Jamaica, New York, passed away June 18, 2000. Former proprietor of Constantine's Restaurant, Jamaica, New York. Archon of the Ecumenical Patriarchate of Constantinople. Longtime member of the Wheatley Hills Country Club, Long Island. Member of O.S.S. during WW II. He is survived by his loving wife of 53 years, Ellie. His devoted children: Peter (Elizabeth) Coufos, Queens, New York and Valerie (Burton) Bachrach, Bay Harbor Islands, Florida. His cherished grandchildren: Eleanor and Penelope Coufos. His brother: Alexander P. Coufos, Brooklyn, NY and Brother & Sister-in-law: Harry and Ann Voziou, New York, NY. The family will receive friends on Wednesday, June 21, 2000, 7:00-9:30pm at St. Demetrious Greek Orthodox Church, 815 NE 15th Avenue, Ft. Lauderdale, FL 33304 (954-467-1515) where funeral services will be Thursday, 11:00am. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests contributions be made in his memory to St. Demetrious Greek Orthodox Church, Ft. Lauderdale, Florida.
