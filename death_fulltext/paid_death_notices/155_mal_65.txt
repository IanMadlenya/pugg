
        GREEN-Walter. On November 26, 1999 in Palm Beach, Fl. at age 90. Devoted husband of the late Eileen Green. Lovingly remembered by his daughters, Cornelia Stebel and Lizabeth Conescu, his sister, Florence Radbill, his sonsin-law Michael Stebel and Paul Conescu, his grandsons, Ronald Conescu, Jeremy Conescu and Jacob Stebel, and many nieces and nephews. Those who wish may make contributions in his name to the Lake Worth Public Library, 15 N. M St, Lake Worth, FL. 33460.
