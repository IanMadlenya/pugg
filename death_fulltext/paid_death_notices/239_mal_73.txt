
        SAXL--Sherman. We mourn the loss of our dear friend of over 50 years, while we are thankful for his life. As a fellow Law Clerk to a Federal Judge, Fellow Assistant U.S. Attorney, and esteemed Fellow Member of the Bar, Sherman earned the high reputation he had. Most important, his love for his wife, children and grandchildren exemplified his high character and the valuable heritage he leaves to all who were fortunate to be his friend. Sheila and Gerald Walpin
