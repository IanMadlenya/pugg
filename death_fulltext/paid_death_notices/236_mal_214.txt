
        LIEBERMAN--Julius. Age 92 on September 20, 2006. Beloved husband for 65 years to Hortense, loving father to Janet, Laurie, Edward. Dear brother to Shirley Yourish and the late Samuel. Cherished grandfather to Jennifer, Amy, Shoshana. Father-inlaw to Barbara. He died after a prolonged courageous battle with heart disease. A graduate of Columbia Dental School, professor at CUNY, he was an accomplished classical pianist. He was loved for his gentleness, wit and intelligence. We will greatly miss him. Services will be grave side Thursday, 2pm at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, Long Island.
