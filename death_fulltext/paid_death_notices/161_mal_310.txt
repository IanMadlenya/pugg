
        RABINOWITZ - Benjamin. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the death of Benjamin Rabinowitz. We extend our sincere sympathy to his wife Ruth, son Stanley, and our good friends, daughter Phyllis and son-in-law Gerald Yablons, and the entire extended family. We hope that your loving memories of times together will bring you comfort during this time of sadness.
