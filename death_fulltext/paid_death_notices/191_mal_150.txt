
        BRESLER-Horace J. 83, died November 30, 2002. Graduated Magna Cum Laude, Harvard '41 and MSW from Columbia. Served in Pacific in WWII. Moved to Charleston, SC, where he did social work until retirement. Survived by children, Charles Bresler and Miriam Smith-Strelnick; five grandchildren, Noah, Kayla, Dylan, Todd and Deb. He loved musical theater and sports. He will be missed.
