
        JONES--Mary Lea Bird, of Brooklyn, NY, and Saugatuck, MI, died April 8, 2007, age 80. Beloved wife of James I. Jones, Sr., Mother of Carol A. Peterson, Steven A. (Cathleen) Jones, James I. (Caroline, M.D.) Jones, Jr., M.D. Grandmother of Evan J. Peterson, James I. Jones III, Megan E. Jones, Emily C. Jones and Owen S. Jones. She will be remembered and deeply missed by all. A gathering of friends will be held later.
