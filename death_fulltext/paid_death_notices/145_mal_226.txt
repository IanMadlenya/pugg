
        EISSLER-Kurt R., M.D.. The Board of Trustees of Sigmund Freud Archives expresses its profound condolence to the family, friends and colleagues of Dr. Kurt R. Eissler. Dr. Eissler was one of the Founders, and for many years, the Secretary of Sigmund Freud Archives. He died on February 17, 1999. Harold P. Blum, M.D., Exec. Dir. Alexander Grinstein, M.D., Pres.
