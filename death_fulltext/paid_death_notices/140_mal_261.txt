
        HOPKINS-John J. The Board of Trustees and the Priests, Religious, Lay Staff and Volunteers of The Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of New York mourn the death of our devoted trustee and admired friend. We cherish the memories of his wisdom, his gentle encouragement and his open-hearted generosity and loving kindness. We extend our warm sympathy to his beloved wife Joan, and his family. May our Gracious God reward him abundantly and grant him the fullness of love and peace forever. John Cardinal O'Connor, President The Board of Trustess of The Catholic Charities of The Archdiocese of New York
