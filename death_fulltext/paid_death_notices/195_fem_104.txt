
        PARNES - BIRNBAUM - Mar cia (nee Gabor). Born in 1930 in Brooklyn, NY, longtime resident of Boca Raton, FL and Englewood, NJ, passed away on April 9, 2003 at her home in Florida. Loving wife of the late Burton Parnes. Cherished and beloved mother to her three sons, Howard, Fred and Mitchell, Howard's wife Kristen, Fred's companion Isabelle, and a dear sister to Ruth Heller. An avid and accomplished bridge player and instructor for 30 years and author of the instruction book ''The Bridge Notes of Marcia Parnes.'' She will be greatly missed and deeply mourned by all who knew her, including her many friends, relatives, nieces, nephews and Alan and Jonalyn Birnbaum, the son and daughter-in-law of the late Milton Birnbaum, her devoted and loved second husband. We all adored and loved you and will miss your smile always. In lieu of flowers please send donations to DRADA, 600 North Wolfe St., Meyer 3-181, Baltimore, MD 21287. Services will be held at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York, on Wednesday, April 16, at 11:15. Burial to follow at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
