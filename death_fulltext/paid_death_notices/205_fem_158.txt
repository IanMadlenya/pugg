
        HUNTER--B. Barbara of Crestwood Village, Whiting, NJ died December 17, 2003. Mrs. Hunter was the widow of F. Lansing Hunter, former Mayor of Westbury, LI, NY where they lived for 40 years before coming to Crestwood in 1982. She was born and raised in Birmingham, AL. Mrs. Hunter was known in the theatre and as a professional fashion model under the name of ''Billie Blanchard''. She was a member of the Ziegfield Club in NY and recently was a part of the 100th anniversary celebration marking the opening of the New Amsterdam Theatre in New York in 1903. Surviving are a son, F. Lansing Hunter, Jr. of Madison, NJ, a sister, Frances Larkin of Whiting, NJ, and a brother Stuart Balsam, of New Bern, NC. Services private.
