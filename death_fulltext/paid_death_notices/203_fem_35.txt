
        SPITZER--Mabel, 93, passed away peacefully on December 3, 2003 after a prolonged illness. She was born in Woodhaven, NY, spent most of her married life in the Five Towns, Long Island, NY, raised three sons there and moved with her husband to Miami Beach in 1963. She is predeceased by her husband, Charles, after 47 years of marriage and her oldest son, David. She is survived by her sons Joel and Harold, daughters-in-law Evonne and Zahava, grandchildren Martin, Jeffrey, Lisa and Peter, and great grandson Odin. In lieu of flowers, donations should be made to Unity On The Bay, 411 Northeast 21st Street, Miami, FL 33137 (for memorial of Mabel Spitzer).
