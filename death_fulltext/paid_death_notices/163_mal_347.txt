
        BOYLE-John M. On August 28, 2000. Attorney and former Chief Librarian NYS Supreme Court, World War II veteran and former President of The Knollwood Country Club, Elmsford, NY. Survived by three sons, Judge Denis, John and Bill Boyle, two daughter-inlaws and six grandchildren. Predeceased by his wife, Mary Ellen in 1990. Calling hours at the Dwyer Funeral Home, 90 North Broadway, Tarrytown, NY on Thursday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass Church of the Transfiguration, 268 South Broadway, Tarrytown on Friday at 10am. Interment Calvary Cemetery, White Plains. Friends may send memorial donations to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York, or the David C. Purdy Memorial Fund, C/O Knollwood Country Club.
