
        STEINER-Lee N. The members, attorneys and staff of Loeb & Loeb LLP mourn with profound sadness the loss of Lee N. Steiner, our partner for more than 40 years. A towering figure of international renown in the entertainment industry, Lee was a pioneer in the practice of entertainment law. Lee was an inspiring leader within the firm, an outstanding mentor and a great friend. We enjoyed his warmth and humor and greatly admired his dedication to his family, his clients and a myriad of philanthropic endeavors. We will miss him dearly. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife, Roz, his cherished daughters, Cathy, Terry and Nancy, and the entire Steiner family.
        STEINER-Lee. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Lee Steiner on March 28. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Wednesday, April 3, 4PM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., NY. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        STEINER-Lee N. The Trustees and staff of Carnegie Hall mourn the loss of Lee Steiner, esteemed Board member from 1977 to 1989. A commemorative plaque in Box 13 of Stern Auditorium forever bears witness to his longstanding devotion to the Hall. We offer our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Rosalind, his daughters Terry, Catherine and Nancy, and to the rest of the Steiner family.
