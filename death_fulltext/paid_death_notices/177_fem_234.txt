
        SARNOFF-Barbara Hamilton. We mourn the loss of our beloved mother and grandmother, the beautiful Barbara, on October 22, 2001, at 94 years. She fought valiantly against Alzheimer's. Lately of Easton, PA, and previously of Westport, CT, and Palm Beach, FL, she leaves daughters, Elizabeth Burrell, of Toronto, and Barbara Rothkopf, of Easton, PA, and four grandchildren. Burial to be private.
