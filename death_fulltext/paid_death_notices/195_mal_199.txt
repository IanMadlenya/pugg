
        DUNCAN - Jiman. The trustees, faculty, staff, students and parents of the Buckley School deeply mourn the passing of Jiman Duncan, our inspiring music instructor, coach and mentor. We will sorely miss Jiman's vibrant dedication to teaching and to challenging all those in his charge always to strive for excellence and team commitment-in singing, in sports and in life. Ogden N. Lewis President, Board of Trustees Gregory J. O'Melia Headmaster The Buckley School in the City of New York
