
        WOLF-Ruth. Of New York City, born in Berlin and recently living at Glen Arden, Goshen NY died June 1. She lived a long, full life. Ruthie was a pioneer in creating high fashion for pets. Her successful company, Ruth Wolf Dog Accessories was known throughout the world. She was loved and will be missed by all who knew her. We will think of her and her dear husband Jackie every day. Gloria, Martin, Teddy and Jennifer Greenstein
