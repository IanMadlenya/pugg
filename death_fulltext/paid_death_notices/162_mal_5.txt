
        CHACK-Bernard. Age 86, formerly of Stamford, CT, died on July 1, 2000, in Boynton Beach, FL. He leaves his loving family: wife, Shirley; daughter, Holly and Richard Calabrese; son, Steven and Cindy; daughter, Laurie and Lynn Purnell and granddaughters, Lana, Sadie and Jessica. Mr. Chack was a pioneer of employee food services for major industrial companies. He founded and was President of Eastern Food Service in Stamford, CT, where he lived until he retired in 1985.
