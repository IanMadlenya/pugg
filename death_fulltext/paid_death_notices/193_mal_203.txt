
        BERES-David, M.D. Age 99, Past President of The American Psychoanalytic Assoc., on February 15, 2003 at The Carolton in Fairfield, CT. Predeceased by his wife, Dinah and son, Robert. He is survived by his son, Paul Beres, M.D. of Westport, CT; his grandchildren, Linda Karen Beres, Mary Ellen Holden, Carol Ann Strickberger, Susan Amy Schwartz, William Beres, John Beres, Valerie Ross, Hal Ross and seven great-grandchildren. Services will be private.
