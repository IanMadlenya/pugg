
        VALENTI-Frank A., 89, of Queensbury, NY, May 20. Son of the late Antonino and Lena. Born and raised in NYC; graduated from Alfred Univ. and Master's from Columbia Univ. WW II Army veteran, taught Special Education, John Jay HS, Brooklyn, over 30 years. His first wife Olga died in 1966. Survived by Elaine Murphy (married 1967), two sons, Frank (Andrea) and Richard (Maryann), four grandchildren, Frank, Alexandra, Danielle and Amanda, and his sister, Alfonsina Valenti. Burial was at Long Island National Veterans Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
