
        WINDERBAUM--Harold. November 21, 2004. Devoted husband of the late Harriet. Loving father of Michael (Rae) and Larry (Helene), Cherished grandfather of Anna (Gary), Nomi (Jim), Elise and Eli. Adoring greatgrandfather of Vera, Zachary and Ezra. Dearest son, brother, uncle and friend. 92 years in the Bronx, 60 full years in the garment center. In his later years he was an active member of the Riverdale YMHA Senior Center and frequent caller (a/k/a Harold from Riverdale) on talk radio. He was treasured by all who knew him. Funeral service Tuesday Plaza Memorial Chapel 91 St. & Amsterdam
