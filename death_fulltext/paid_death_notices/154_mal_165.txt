
        VANDERPUTTEN - Herbert Joseph. Of New York City, formerly of Westbury, LI. On November 12, 1999. Beloved husband of Ruth, his wife of 59 years. Devoted father of Peter (deceased), Thomas, Gary and James. Loving father-inlaw of Joan, Jan and Jill. Cherished grandfather of Christine, Michael, Bryan, Joy, Victoria, Christopher and Chelsea. Funeral Mass of Christian Burial at St. Agnes R.C. Church, 43rd Street at 3rd and Lexington Avenues. Interment of Ashes immediately following at Greenwood Cemetery in Brooklyn. Family will receive friends at the home of Gary Vanderputten after interment. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to God's Love We Deliver or Maryknoll Missionaries.
