
        GORIN--Dr. Leonard J., DDS, 88, of Monroe, died May 3, 2006. Son of the late Louis & Anna (Hottenstien) Gorin, he was born in Brooklyn, December 16, 1917. In addition to his private practice on 72nd St. in Manhattan, he was the clinical director of dental services for the NYC Fire Dept., attending dentist for Mt. Sinai Hospital, chief of dental research for Cabrini Health Care Center. He was a fellow of the Royal Society of Medicine, Royal Society of Health, Academy of General Dentistry & member of the Knights of Malta, ADA and a Mason. He served with the USPHS during WW II. He is survived by his beloved wife Geraldine F. Whittington, loving stepson, William S. Whittington, both of Monroe, devoted cousins, Sam Levinrad of Rockville, MD & Lois Pepkin of Virginia Beach, VA. Graveside services will be held at 11:30 AM on Monday, May 8, 2006 at Seamanville Cemetery, Monroe, NY. Donations to the FDNY Widows & Orphan's Fund. For info Smith, Seaman & Quackenbush Funeral Home, Monroe, NY.
