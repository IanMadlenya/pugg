Popular and active Mason R.W., D.S.A., and Trustee affiliated with St. Cecile Lodge No. 568 F. & A.M., Franklin Lodge No. 447 F. & A.M., Midwood Lodge and Musicians Square Club. Laid to rest Sunday, November 30, 1997. Profound condolences to his family. He will be sorely missed.
