
        DOHERTY-Randolph Jr. age 61 of Mountain Lakes, NJ and Santa Fe, New Mexico, formerly of Englewood, NJ. Died suddenly on December 18, 1998. President of the Benjamin Eastwood Company in Paterson, NJ, he attended Phillips Andover Academy and William College in Massachusetts and the Columbia University School of Business in New York. He is survived by his fiancee, Greta Tibbetts of Ridgewood, NJ & his sister Ruth Doherty Reed of Englewood, NJ. Memorial service Saturday, January 16, 11:00 AM, at St. Peter's Episcopal Church, 215 Boulevard, Mountain Lakes, NJ.
