
        DRINKWATER--Frederica Plimpton, in Bedford, MA, April 21, 2007, at the age of 90, after a brief illness. Formerly of Panton, VT and Hingham, MA. Devoted and beloved wife of the late Davis C. Drinkwater. Frederica and her husband lived an adventurous life, 17 years of farming in Vermont, followed by many years of sailing. She was also involved in her community of Panton, VT serving as Overseer of the Poor for many years and working as a volunteer at Planned Parenthood in Burlington, VT where she won an award for her contribution to that work. She is survived by her brother, John Plimpton of Sherborn. Loving mother of Susan P. Martin of Stonington, CT, Frederica P. Perera of New York City, Polly B. Drinkwater of Lincoln, MA, and Davis C. Drinkwater, Jr. of Nashville, TN. Also survived by 11 grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. A private Memorial Service will be held in May.
