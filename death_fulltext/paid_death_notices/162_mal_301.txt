
        RADIN-Edwin F. On Friday, July 28, 2000, of Pompano Beach, FL, formerly of New Jersey and LI, NY. Beloved husband of the late Fredda. Devoted father of Amy R. Stein and her husband Marvin, and Jonathan C. Radin and his wife Jenique. Dear brother of Burton and Arthur. Adoring grandfather of Andrea, Elizabeth, Victoria and Katherine. Mr. Radin was the former President of Victory Container Corp. He was a war hero, earning the Distinguished Flying Cross and the rank of captain in WWII. Services 11 AM, Monday, at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4 West), Fair Lawn, NJ. Memorial donations may be made to National Parkinson's Foundation.
