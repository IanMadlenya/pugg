
        LOURIA-Lee K. On December 2, at age 74, in Madison, CT. Beloved husband of Barbara. Loving father of Christopher (deceased), Leigh, Susan Louria Ruggiero, Tyler, Amanda, Reed and Myles. Brother of Dr. Donald B. Louria and Dana Cless. Grandfather of four. A retired Singer Company labor relations attorney and graduate of Amherst College 1946, and Syracuse University Law School 1953. A celebration of his life at the Swan Funeral Home, Madison, December 6, 10 AM. Contributions to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Pancreatic Cancer Research, PO Box E, 1275 York Ave., NYC 10021.
