
        BROWN-Lillian. Age 93, on May 30 in Miami Beach, FL. Co-founder and co-owner with her beloved husband Charles (deceased) of Brown's Hotel, Loch Sheldrake, NY. Loving mother of Lonnie Rowley of West Palm Beach, FL. Beloved sister of Jean Harrison of Clifton, New Jersey. Devoted grandmother of Bruce Turiansky, Boca Raton, FL., Wayne Turiansky, Shelburne, VT and Cliff Turian of New Milford, CT., and six great grandchildren. Services Sunday, 11 A.M., Garlick Funeral Chapel, Monticello, N.Y.
