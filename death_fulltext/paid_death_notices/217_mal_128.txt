
        TENGA--Carmine M. on February 7, 2005. Retired acting Superintendent of New York State Banking Department. Former President of Cross County Federal Savings & Loan, and currently on the Board of Directors of Atlantic Bank. Beloved husband of Joyce. Dear brother of Michelina and Grace. Cherished uncle of many nieces and nephews. Also survived by best friend Mia. Friends may call Friday, 2-5 & 7-9:30 PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave, Floral Park, NY (at RR Plaza). Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 9:30 AM Our Lady of Victory RC Church. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery.
        TENGA--Carmine M. The management, Board of Directors and the entire staff of Atlantic Bank of New York wish to acknowledge our heartfelt and deepest sympathies on the passing of our devoted colleague and outstanding member of our Board of Directors, Carmine M. Tenga. Through his untiring commitment, wise counsel and progressive leadership, Carmine has contributed greatly to the success of our company and he has earned our highest esteem and gratitude.
