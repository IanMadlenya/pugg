
        ASHWORTH-William. Born October 9, 1920, died April 16, 2002, after a lengthy battle with melanoma. Bill was loved by most everyone he met and was particularly loved by his wife Lou and his three children, Margaret, Polly and Warren. He was considered the best of all possible fathers-in-law by Lloyd Van Lunen, Rob Horner, Susan Kander and the late Henry Merryman Wilson. He was grandfather to six spirited boys. A memorial service will be held in Larchmont on May 31. Contributions in lieu of flowers to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St, Tuckahoe, NY 10707 or Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Rd, Bronx, NY 10461.
