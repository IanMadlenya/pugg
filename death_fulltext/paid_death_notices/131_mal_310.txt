
        TUCHMAN-Lester R., MD. The membership of the Medical Society of the County of Queens expresses their profound sadness on the loss of their beloved colleague and friend Lester R. Tuchman, MD. A distinguished and dedicated physician, he served the Society well as its twice-elected president, board member and committee chairman. Our deepest sympathies to his family. Fred N. Flatau, MD, Chairman, Board of Trustees Leah S. McCormack, MD, President
