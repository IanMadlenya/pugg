
        FEIRING-William, MD, FACP, age 95. Died January 23, 2002 at his home in Waterside Retirement Estates, Sarasota, FL. He was born in New York City on June 25, 1906 and came to Sarasota in April 1972. He was a graduate of CCNY and NYU and practiced medicine as an internist. He was married to the late Ruth Rosenthal in 1941 and served as a medical officer in the Air Force from 1942 to 1946. He was a member of Temple Beth Sholom. He is survived by his family, Hilda Feiring and Ruth, Stephen and Eugene Rosenthal. A memorial service will be held at Waterside Retirement Estates at a later date.
