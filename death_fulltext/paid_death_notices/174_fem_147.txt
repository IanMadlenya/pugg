
        REYNOLDS-Mildred Ellis. Of Bridgeport, CT, formerly of New York City and Washington, CT, died July 15 at the age of 101. She is survived by her son David C. Reynolds, two grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the First Congregational Church, 6 Kirby Road, Washington, CT., on Monday, July 23, 2001 at 2:00 PM. Interment will follow at Washington Green Cemetery.
