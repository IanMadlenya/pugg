
        LEE--Kelvin, M.D. The faculty, residents and staff of the Department of Otolaryngology mourn the passing of colleague and friend Kelvin C. Lee, M.D. A graduate of Yale University and University of California, San Francisco, Dr. Lee was recruited to NYU School of Medicine as Associate Professor of Otolaryngology, Director of Residency Education and as Associate Chief of Service at Bellevue Hospital Center. He was internationally renowned for his innovations in rhinology and sleep surgery. Dr. Lee was a compassionate physician, a superb teacher and a wonderful colleague who left a positive impact on all of our lives and will be deeply missed. Anil K. Lalwani, M.D., Chair Dept Otolaryngology, NYU School of Medicine
