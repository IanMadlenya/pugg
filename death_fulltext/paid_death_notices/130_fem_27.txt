
        PAPPENHEIM-Karl. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community in Israel, a former school principal in Switzerland and Israel; beloved husband of Beate; father of Rachi (and David) Grossman, Avraham (and Nechama) Pappenheim, Elisheva (and Yitzchak) Altman, and Binyomin (and Miriam) Pappenheim; and brother of Erica Jesselson and Lucy Lang. Mrs. Jesselson is Chairman of the YU Museum Board. She and her late husband Ludwig Jesselson, distinguished philanthropist and longtime YU Board Chairman, are Benefactors who have played a leadership role in a wide range of University programs. Mrs. Lang is a member of the Museum Board and a dedicated supporter of YU, as was her late husband, Edmond. We extend our heartfelt condolences as well to grandchildren, great-grandchildren, nieces, nephews, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        PAPPENHEIM-Jacob Karl. The Riverdale Jewish Center extends profound condolences to Erica Jesselson and Lucy Lang on the loss of their beloved brother. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
