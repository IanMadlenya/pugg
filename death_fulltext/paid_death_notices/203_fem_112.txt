
        SHERMAN -- Lydia. Hadas sah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., mourns the passing of Lydia Sherman, beloved wife of Nathan, of blessed memory, and cherished mother of Shelley Sherman and David Diamond. Lydia was a generous benefactor of Hadassah, devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her legacy will live on through the projects she supported at the Hadassah Medical Organization. We extend our deepest condolences to her family. June Walker, National President
