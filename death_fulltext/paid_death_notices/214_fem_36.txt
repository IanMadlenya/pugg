
        POLLACK--Creilly H. 73, Sarasota, FL, died October 21, 2004. Her husband (deceased) Peter Pollack was curator of photography at the Art Institute of Chicago and later director of the American Federation of Art. The couple lived in New York City, where she worked for the Eggers Partnership, Architects and Planners, developing interiors for some of the most prestigious buildings in the city from 1968 to 1974. She is survived by three nieces, Barbara Ronk of Sarasota, Nicole Creilly Boesken of Appleton, WI, and Mary Riley of Los Angeles, a nephew Ferrin Cooper, with the U.S. Army, and a stepson Frederick Pollack. A rememberance service will be held November 13, at her Sarasota residence.
