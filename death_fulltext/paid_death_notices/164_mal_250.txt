
        CASAMASSIMA-Emanuel J. of Old Brookville, formerly of New Rochelle and Manhattan on September 12th, in his 80th year. Beloved husband of Margaret (nee) Rella. Cherished father of Matthew, Gene and the late Frances and Emanuel. Dear father-in-law of Carole, Ellen and Frank. Survived by his brother, Anthony, of New York City. Loving grandfather (Pop-Pop) of twelve and greatgrandfather of three. Also survived by loving sisters-in-law, brother-in-law, and many nieces and nephews that hold a special place in his heart. Founder and C.E.O. of Westbury Transport, Port Washington, for the past 57 years. Member of North Hills Country Club. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Home Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, Wednesday 7:00 p.m.-9:00 p.m., Thursday 3:00-5:00 p.m. and 7:009:00 p.m. Funeral Mass on Friday 10 a.m., at St. Mary's Roman Catholic Church, Manhasset. Interment Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to Don Monti Foundation at North Shore Hospital would be appreciated.
