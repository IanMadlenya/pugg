
        BAKER-George. Devoted and loving husband, father. He is survived by his wife of 58 years, Hilda, his daughters, Wendy Baker and Joy Weber and his son-in-law, Dr. Norman Weber. Also his grandchildren, Aron Weber and Cindy Epstein and great-granddaughter, Jocelyn. He leaves one brother, Dr. Ben Baker. He will be missed by all who knew him. Donations may be made to The Queensboro Hill Jewish Center, 156-03 Horace Harding Blvd., Flushing, NY 11367, Rabbi Philip Rabinowitz, officiating.
