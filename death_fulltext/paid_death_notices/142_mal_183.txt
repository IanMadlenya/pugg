
        SHAPIRO-Ivan. On November 5, 1998 at age 70 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Maria. Proud father of Lisa J. Kubiske and Alex Shapiro. A prominent real estate attorney, civic leader and civil libertarian, Ivan inspired those who knew him. His commitment to ethical and moral issues and to solving social problems are evident in his years of Board membership for NARAL, Center for Reproductive and Sexual Health, the New York Civil Liberties Union and Asia Watch. He served as President and Board member of the Ethical Society of New York and Chairman of the Ethical Culture Schools. His strong interest in New York City was shown in his membership on the Board of the Graduate School and University Center of City University of New York and on the Board of the Brooklyn Navy Yard Development Corporation. He served as President of the Board of Urban Pathways, which named a residence for homeless people after him in 1996. A memorial service will be held on Monday, November 16th, 11AM at New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 Street, New York, NY.
