
        KILBORNE-Virginia W., 86. Of Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, NJ died April 10, 1998. Graduate of Vassar College, received Master's from NY University Institute of Fine Arts. Wife of the late William S. Kilborne, survived by her sister, Mrs. Bethuel Webster, 3 nieces, Elizabeth Mills, Susan Hyde and Penelope Davis and four stepchildren. Memorial service, May 16, 11 AM, Nassau Presbyterian Church, Princeton, New Jersey.
