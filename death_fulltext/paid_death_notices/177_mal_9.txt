
        SIMON-Alan M. Age 69, died at his home in Manhattan, on September 29, 2001. Beloved husband of Toni Lautman Simon. Loving father of Andrew and Leslie Simon and father-in-law of Nancy Simon and Marc Bender. Devoted grandfather of Allison and Samantha Simon, and Arlo and Philip Bender Simon. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, 6-8 PM, with service Tuesday, 11:30 AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Cancer Society, The Horace Mann School or Yale University.
        SIMON-Alan. The Board of Governors and Members of Elberon Bathing Club mourn the untimely passing of our longtime friend and member, Alan Simon. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family. Robert Fagenson, President Jane Guadagno, Secretary
