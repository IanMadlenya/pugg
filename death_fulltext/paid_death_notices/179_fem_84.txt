
        TREVILLIAN-Mary Lou. Of Greenville, S.C., wife of Dr. Wallace D. Trevillian, a Dean Emeritus of Clemson University, died Sunday. She was a graduate of Spence School, New York and Sarah Lawrence College. Other survivors include two sons, three grandchildren and a sister. She was predeceassed by her parents Bonnie E. and W.W. McEachern.
