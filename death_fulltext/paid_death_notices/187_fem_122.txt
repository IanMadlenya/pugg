
        TRIMBLE - Marie Gibbon. Died on August 10, 2002, in Palm Beach, FL. A longtime resident of New York City, and Southampton, NY, she was born in Charlottesville, VA, to the late John Edward Gibbon and Helen Betts Gibbon. She was a graduate of St. Ann's Episcopal School in Charlottesville, VA, attended Randolph-Macon Women's College in Lynchburg, VA, and was a graduate of George Washington University in Washington, DC. She was the widow of John Thomas Trimble. She is survived by her daughter and son-inlaw, Helen Trimble Nauman and Spencer Gilbert Nauman Jr, of Bowmansdale, PA, and three grandchildren Spencer Gilbert Nauman III, of Palm Beach, FL, Helen Gibbon Nauman of Palm Beach, FL, and John Trimble Nauman, of Ft. Lauderdale, FL. She was a member of the Everglades Club in Palm Beach, FL. A graveside service was held in Charlottesville, VA, on August 13. Memorial contributions may be made to The American Cancer Society, 3042-D Berkmar Drive, Charlottesville, VA 22901.
