
        KOSLOW-David. Passed away peacefully on September 20, 1997. David is survived by ''Sammye,'' his loving wife of 54 years; his devoted children Ron, Bob (Tracy), Ken, Judy (Stefan) Zweig. Services will be Tuesday, September 23, 1997 at 2:00 p.m. in the Hillside Memorial Park, Los Angeles, CA.
