
        MORGENROTH -- Steven. Born December 2, 1944, passed away November 18th in Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Steve was the adored and adoring husband of Margaret (nee O'Shaughnessy) and the loving and proud father of Martin. He was pre-deceased by his parents, Carolyn and Julius, and his brother Marty. Steve's kindness, sense of humor and love of the NY Yankees will be forever part of the wife and son he cherished, his cousins Lynn, Mike, Teddy and Jon Friedman, and the many friends he has made over the years. We were lucky to have known you. The funeral service will be held on Sunday, November 23rd at 3 P.M. at LevittWeinstein Memorial Chapels, 5411 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach, FL. In New York, the family will receive visitors on Tuesday, November 25th from 2:30-9:00 P.M. at the Friedman residence, 50 East 89 Street, between Park and Madison Avenues.
