
        GREENFIELD-Myron J. Of Teaneck, NJ, died peacefully in his sleep on Sunday, December 9, after a long illness. His memory will be cherished by his wife, Sophie Greenfield, his children, Peter Greenfield and Phyllis G. Ross, his grandchildren, Lillian and Sarah Ross, his great grandson, Jacob Ross, his brothers, Jonas and Martin Greenfield, his sisters, Rita Charney and Muriel Shookhoff, along with their families and the many friends whose lives he brightened.
