
        FEINBERG - Maurice. On July 27, 2002. At age 82. Devoted husband of Carol Jaffa Feinberg. Loving father of Nancy Feinberg and John Tobin, Hope and Paul Schroy and David and Marsha Feinberg. Proud grandfather of Gregory, Brian, Jacob, Rebecca, Charles, Nina, and James. Beloved brother of Frank and Herbert. Adored son of Harry and Dorothy Feinberg, and Charles and May Jaffa. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday, July 29, 7-9 PM. Funeral service Tuesday, July 30, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Old Mount Carmel Cemetery.
        FEINBERG-Maurice. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member and former member of the Board of Trustees, Maurice Feinberg. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Samuel Wasserman, Pres.
