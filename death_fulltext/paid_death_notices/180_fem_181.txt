
        POOR-Anne. The Trustees and Governors of the Skowhegan School of Painting & Sculpture mark with sadness the loss of our friend, Anne Poor whose father, Henry Varnum Poor, was one of the School's Founders. We celebrate Anne's many achievements as an artist and will treasure the memory of her friendship and support. Ann L. Gund, Chair Judson P. Reis, President Skowhegan School of Painting & Sculpture
