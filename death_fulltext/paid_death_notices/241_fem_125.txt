
        HART--Katherine P., MBA. Died February 14th, 2007 at the age 58. Vice President at Merrill Lynch where she worked for 29 years. Survived by her husband Gregory G. Stephenson and her brothers James, Thomas, and The V. Reverend Joseph of Rochester, NY. Friends may call on Friday 2-5 pm and 7-9 pm at Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court Street, Brooklyn, 718-875-1640. Funeral on Saturday noon, St. James Cathedral, 248 Jay St at Tillary in Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory would be preferred, College of New Rochelle or St. Vincent Hospital, NYC.
