
        NADLER--Nancy Sharon (nee Hertz), on December 27th. Beloved wife of the late Arch Nadler, adored mother of Steven, Lauren, and David. Step - mother of Linden. Grandmother of Rose, Cameron, Ben, Caroline, Sydney, and Savannah. Sister of Roy Hertz, Ruth Kahn and the late Doris Wolfson. A great athlete who coached her children's little league and softball teams, she loved swimming, skiing, tennis, and especially golf. Also a successful businesswoman who always made time to encourage her children in all their endeavors. All who knew her will miss her vivacity, energy, love and warmth. May her memory be for a blessing.
