
        MICHAELS-Max. Born in Drohitchyn, Poland in 1905. Devoted husband to the late Ann (nee Wasserman). Loving father to Linda and Marty. Devoted grandfather and uncle. Max had a successful business career of 50 years with Farber Brothers in NYC. Former 21 year resident of Century Village, West Palm Beach, FL. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who love him. Graveside services Tuesday, May 20, at Beth David Cemetery. information: Levitt-Weinstein, 800-343-5400.
