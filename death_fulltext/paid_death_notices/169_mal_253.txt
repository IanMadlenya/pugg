
        DICKMAN-Louis. Passed away On February 5th, 2001. Beloved husband of Mollie Dickman. Father of Mark Dickman, Michael and Darlene Dickman, Richard and Kim Dickman and Howard and Martie Dickman. Grandfather of Ryan and Mindy Dickman, Chris Dickman and Stephen and Julie Dickman. Louis was a devoted husband, father and grandfather. He retired from The New York Times after forty-one years of dedicated service. You will always be forever in our hearts.
