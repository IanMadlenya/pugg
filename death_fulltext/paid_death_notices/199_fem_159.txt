
        MACHNIKOFF--Sophie, beloved wife of the late William, passed away in her 90th year. Loving mother of Dorothy and Philip, grandmother and great-grandmother.         MACHNIKOFF--Sophie. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale deeply mourn the passing of Sophie Machnikoff, beloved mother of our dear friends, Phil Machnikoff and Dorothy Fox. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her children and grandchildren, and hope her family will be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
