
        BOTWINICK-Joseph. 88, of Edison NJ on January 31, 2000. Born February 1, 1912. Husband of the late Helen (Shlisky), father of Paul and Jill Botwinick, Michael and Harriet Botwinick, grandfather of Jonathan, Daniel, and Lora. A founding member of The Endymions in Bayonne NJ, he worked for the B. Manischewitz Co. from 1931 until his retirement as company Safety Director in 1977. Funeral services 10AM Wednesday February 2 at Bernheim-Apter Funeral Home, 1600 Springfield Avenue, Maplewood NJ. 973-763-3505
