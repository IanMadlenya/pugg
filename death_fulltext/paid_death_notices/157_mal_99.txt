
        HILLS-Lee. The Trustees and Staff of the American Federation of Arts are profoundly saddened by the passing of their beloved friend, Lee Hills, a steadfast Trustee of the AFA and active champion of the arts, education, and freedom of expression. We will miss his enlightened leadership, farsighted thinking, and moral resolve. Heartfelt condolences to Lee's cherished wife Tina and the family. Robert Meltzer, Chairman Jan Mayer, President Serena Rattazzi, Director
