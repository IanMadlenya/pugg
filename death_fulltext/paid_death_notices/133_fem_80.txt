
        AHN-Lisa Denham. 38. On February 8, after a valiant struggle with breast cancer. Adored wife of her beloved Henry. Cherished daughter of Elma Denham of New York City and William Denham of Washington, D.C. Loved by sister Stacey Denham and Stacey's husband Tim Wood and her nephew Boris Wood in Hamburg, Germany. Everyone who ever met and knew Lisa loved her. She was special but her kindness, gentleness and caring made everyone else feel special. A funeral service will be held on Friday, 12 noon, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ, 201-843-9090. In lieu of flowers, please make a contribution in Lisa Denham-Ahn's name to SHARE, 1501 Broadway, suite 1720, New York, NY 10036. A memorial in New York City will be announced later.
