
        ORKIN-Frances L., of Manhattan. Born July 11, 1916, died Thursday March 25, 1999 after a long illness. A former Information Officer for The United Nations and a longtime successful entreprenuer. Ms. Orkin loved people, loved life and loved New York City. She is survived by family members and dear friends who mourn the passing of our caring, elegant, eloquent and witty friend. Ms. Orkin is the daughter of the late Louis and Fanny Orkin. There will be no funeral although a memorial gathering will be held at her request in her memory. Contributions may be sent to Jacob Perlow Hospice or to Saint Vincent Cancer Center.
