
        DINITZ--Simcha. American Friends of The Hebrew University and The Hebrew University of Jerusalem mourn the profound loss of Simcha Dinitz, former vice president of The Hebrew University, and senior fellow and chairman of its Leonard Davis Institute of International Relations. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Simcha's wife Vivian and to his children Michael, Tamar and Dorit. Ira Lee Sorkin, President, AFHU Keith L. Sachs, Chairman, AFHU Peter T. Willner, Executive VP, AFHU Prof. Menachem Magidor, President, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem Yigal Arnon, Chairman, Board of Governors, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem
