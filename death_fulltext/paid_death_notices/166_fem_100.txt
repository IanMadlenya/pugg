
        ROTHMAN-Tillie. Died November 11, 2000 at home in her 100th year. Loving wife of the late Leo. Devoted mother of son Seymour and daughter Freda Hirsch. Loving grandmother of Louise, Erik, Shoshana and Lee Hirsch. Great grandmother to Nava, Elisheva and Ezra. Tillie was a beacon of strength and inspiration to us all.
