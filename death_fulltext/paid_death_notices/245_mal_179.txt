
        KLIOT--Boris. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Boris Kliot, beloved husband of Jacqueline and father of Michel, Gene, and Jason. Boris' devotion to Jewish philanthropy has strengthened our ability to serve those in need. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
