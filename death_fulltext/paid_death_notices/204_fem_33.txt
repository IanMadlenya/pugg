
        McGUIRE--Helen Slane. On January 1, 2004, of Southbury, CT, formerly of Flushing, NY, wife of the late James F. McGuire. She retired in 1968 as secretary for Milbank Memorial Fund. Predeceased by brothers Peter, William, Joseph, Thomas, Raymond Slane, and sisters Kathleen Donaldson, Mary Slane, Agnes Vaughan, Marguerite Baxter. Funeral Mass January 6, at 11 AM, at Sacred Heart Church, Southbury, CT. Burial: Cemetery of Gate of Heaven, Hawthorne, NY. Contributions: Sacred Heart Church, PO Box 686, Southbury, CT 06488; or Pomperaug Woods Memorial Fund, 80 Heritage Road, Southbury, CT 06488.
