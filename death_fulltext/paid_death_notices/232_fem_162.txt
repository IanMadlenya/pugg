
        CONNELLY--Elizabeth. The friends, members, Board, staff and participants of AHRC express their profound grief at the loss of a good and loyal friend Betty Connelly. We extend condolences to her loving family on this sad occasion. She will always be in our hearts, memories and prayers. Laura Kennedy, President and Michael Goldfarb, Executive Director, AHRC
