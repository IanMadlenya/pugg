
        MARACHE-Herbert W., Jr. Beloved husband and father, died May 28, 1997 in Greenwich, CT. Born in Brooklyn, NY and educated at St. Paul's School and Yale College. He is survived by five children: Nina M. King, Victoria B. Marache, Eugenie M. Pavelic, Herbert W. Marache, III and Mark A. Marache, a sister, Suzanne Geyer and six grandchildren. Memorial service, 3PM Monday June 2 at Christ Church, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Eagle Hill School, 45 Glenville Road, Greenwich, CT.
