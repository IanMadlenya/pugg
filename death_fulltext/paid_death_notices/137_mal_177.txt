
        VOLCKER-Barbara. Of NYC on June 14th, 1998, after a prolonged illness. Beloved wife of Paul. Devoted mother of Janice Zima and James Volcker. Daughter of Edna Bahnson. Also survived by her brother William F. Bahnson and three grandchildren. Services private. Memorial contributions may be made to the Barbara Volcker Center for Women and Rheumatic Disease, 523 East 70th Street, New York, New York 10021.
        VOLCKER-Barbara. The Appeal of Conscience Foundation mourns the passing of Barbara Volcker, beloved wife of our devoted Trustee, Paul Volcker. Our deepest sympathy to him and the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier, President Reverend Joseph A. O'Hare, S.J. Dr. David H.C. Read, Vice Pres.
        VOLCKER-Barbara. The American Assembly extends deepest condolences to its trustee, Paul Volcker, and his family, on the passing of Barbara Volcker. Stephen Stamas, Chairman
