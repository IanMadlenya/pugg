
        ALT-Dorothy. On January 3, at age 95. Wife of the late Mannie Alt. Mother of Stanley A. and Susan Alt and the late Bernard Alt. Grandmother of five; great-grandmother of five. Services Monday, January 5, 12:45 PM, Gutterman's, 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre.
        ALT-Dorothy. Temple Israel of Long Beach records with sorrow the passing of Dorothy Alt, longtime member, life member of Sisterhood and mother of our V.P. Stanley A. We share the grief of her loss and wish to Stanley and Susan, grandchildren and entire family, comfort and prayers that they have no further sorrow. Jacob Chiger, Rabbi Mark I. Samuels Bruce A. Sklover Co-Presidents Marcia Kaskel, Sisterhood Pres.
