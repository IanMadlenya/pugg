
        FALK-Pauline B. On Tuesday, November 21, 2000 at 90 years of age. Loving wife of the late Myron S. Falk, Jr. (Johnny). Mother of Patricia, Michael and Nancy. Grandmother of Christopher, Brooke, Melanie, Amerin, Gregory and Logan. Great grandmother of Ruby, Zoe, Jasper and Leon. Sister of the late Herman Baerwald, Jane Aron and Florence Doubilet. Also survived by devoted family and friends. Funeral service Tuesday, November 28th, 12 Noon, Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65 St, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services, 120 West 57 St, NY, NY 10019.
        FALK-Pauline B. The Faculty, Advisory Council and Alumni Association of the Columbia University School of Social Work record with sadness the death of Pauline B. Falk. An outstanding leader in the social work profession for almost seven decades, she was a valued member of the Development Council of the Center for the Study of Social Work Practice, a joint program of the Jewish Board of Family and Children Services and the School. She will be remembered for her pioneering efforts and lifetime dedication to the field of human services. Sincerest sympathies are extended to the family. Ronald A. Feldman, Dean
