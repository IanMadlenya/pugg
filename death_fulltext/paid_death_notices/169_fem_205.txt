
        KAUFMAN-Helen R. On February 21, 2001 at age 84. Beloved wife of the late Judge Irving R. Kaufman. Loving mother of James M. and the late Robert H. and Richard K. Kaufman. Also survived by grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Services at Park Avenue Synagogue, Madison Ave. at 87 St., Friday, February 23rd, 11:30 AM. Interment Mt. Pleasant Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Robert H. Kaufman Scholarship Fund established at Mt. Sinai School of Medicine.
        KAUFMAN-Helen. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Hospital and Mount Sinai School of Medicine deeply mourn the passing of Mrs. Helen Kaufman. Mrs. Kaufman and her husband, the late Irving R. Kaufman, a distinguished justice and Mount Sinai Trustee, were dedicated supporters of Mount Sinai. The Irving and Helen Kaufman Scholarship Fund was created by Mrs. Kaufman and her late husband in memory of their son, Robert. We extend our sincere condolences to her family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman, Board of Trustees, Mount Sinai NYU Health Barry Freedman, President Helene L. Kaplan, Chairman, Board of Trustees, Mount Sinai School of Medicine Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean
        KAUFMAN-Helen. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
