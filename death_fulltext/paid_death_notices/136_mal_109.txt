
        ROGERS-Herman ''Pete''. Age 88. Died on May 3rd, 1998. Beloved husband of Reita (nee Berlat). Loving father of daughter Beth & son Toby. Cherished grandfather of John Andrew Rogers Katz. Loving father-in-law to Jeffrey S. Katz and Francine Nayman. Your strength and courage will be an inspiration forever. The most gentle man with a sense of humor who wrote beautiful poetic verse will be remembered in the hearts of all he touched. A Paterson, N.J. boy who never forgot his roots. We all love you.
