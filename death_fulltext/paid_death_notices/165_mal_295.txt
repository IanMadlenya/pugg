
        WEINREB-Marvin S. Age 74, of Oakland, CA, a dedicated dermatologist, loving family man, and humanitarian, died on October 14, while volunteering in Mexico with The Flying Doctors. He is survived by his wife Ilene, former mayor of Hayward, CA, daughters Rachel, Deborah and Judith, all of northern California, sister Anita Weinreb Katz of Manhattan, brother Joseph Weinreb of Florida, and four granddaughters.
