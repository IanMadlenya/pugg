        LEVINE--Florence. The Jewish Theological Seminary community mourns the death of Florence Levine, mother of Alan Levine, a member of the JTS's Board of Trustees. A life long humanitarian and Jewish community activist, Mrs. Levine was tireless in her philanthropic efforts and will be greatly missed. May her family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
