
        BALLIN-Harold, on December 23, 1999 at his home in Riverdale, NY. Mr. Ballin was President of Excel Paint Products Co., deceased by his wife, Zena, and his sister, Grace Herbst Rosenthal. Survived by his brother, Dr. Bert Ballin, and four nephews and two nieces. Memorial service Sunday, January 16th, at 1 PM at Riverdale/Yonkers Society of Ethical Culture, Riverdale.
