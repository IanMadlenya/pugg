
        ANDERSON-Lorna McClenahan. Family and friends mourn the passing of Lorna Anderson, beloved daughter of Rachel Breck, sister of Leith Speiden and Doug McClenahan, aunt of five devoted nieces and nephews. She died on January 20, 2002 in her Connecticut home. At the time of her death, she was the president of L.M. Anderson Securities. Memorial service Wednesday, January 30 at 1:15 PM in the chapel of St. Bartholemew's Church at Park Ave. and 51st St. Reception at the Cosmopolitan Club. Contributions may be made to the Northeast Salem Animal Shelter, 204 Highland Avenue, Salem, MA. 01970.
