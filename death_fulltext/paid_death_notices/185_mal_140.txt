
        BROWN-Wallace A., Jr. Of NY City and Ponte Vedra, FL. Passed away on June 9, 2002. Born July 8, 1920 in Atlantic Beach, FL. to Wallace A. and Nelle C. Brown. Graduated from St. Andrews Academy, Sewanee, Tn. and from North Carolina University, Chapel Hill, class of 1943. Member of the North Carolina Society, The University Club, Doubles and the Ponte Vedra Golf and Tennis Club. Reception at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Wednesday, June 12, 2002 at 6-8 PM. Funeral Service, Thursday, June 13, 2002 at 12:30PM at The Church of the Epiphany, Episcopal, 74th Street at York Avenue.
        BROWN-Wallace A., Jr. The Board of The North Carolina Society of New York mourn the passing of their past-President, Wallace A. Brown, Jr. He graduated from The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1943. We in the North Carolina community in New York will miss Wally's presence as he was a wonderful support to all of us.
