
        BOXER--Robert Alan, MD, age 58, died on February 7th, 2005 after a long, courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. He was the beloved, adored, cherished son of his mother and father, dearly beloved by his brothers, nephews, wife, children, patients, colleagues, and friends. He was a gifted cardiologist and intensivist at Schneider Children's North Shore Hospital. You will always be in our hearts forever. Life will never be the same without you. Rest in peace, dear and loving son. Donations to the Lustgarten Fdn for Pancreatic Cancer. Dr. and Mrs. Leon Boxer, Mother and Father Metuchen, New Jersey
