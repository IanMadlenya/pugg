
        SILLS-Nathaniel L., 93 years. Devoted husband of the late Ruth, father of Larry and Susan, Arthur and Sarah, Peter and Susan, grandfather of Deborah and Adam, Eric and Leah, Matthew, Dylan, Daniel and Noah, greatgrandfather of Jacob, Eli & Laila. He was Chairman of the Board of Standard Motor Products but his main passion was his family. He had a profound influence on all of us. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at Schwartz Brothers Jeffer Memorial, Queens Blvd and 76th Road, Forest Hills. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to American Parkinson's Disease Association, 650 West 168 St, NYC 10032.
        SILLS-Nathaniel L. The employees of Standard Motor Products, Inc. are deeply saddened by the passing of their esteemed Chairman of the Board, Nathaniel Sills, on Tuesday evening, October 3, 2000, at the age of 93. He served his company for more than 57 years. A recipient of the Ellis Island Medals of Honor 2000, he served his community with distinction. He made life richer for all who knew him. Services will be conducted at Schwartz BrothersJeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills at 12 noon on Sunday, October 8, 2000.
        SILLS-Nathaniel. The Board of Directors and Staff of The Structured Employment Economic Development Corporation (Seedco) notes with great sadness the passing of Nathaniel Sills, beloved father of Lawrence Sills, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Seedco. We extend our deepest sympathy to the entire family. William J. Grinker President Seedco
