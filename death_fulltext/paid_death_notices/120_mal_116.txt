
        STRICKER - Sidney G. Jr. Died Tuesday evening, January 7, after an illness of several months and a life of 75 years filled with love and irrepressible enthusiasm - for his wife Lesley, and son Rob; for his work; for his sisters, brother, nephews, nieces, loyal colleagues and innumerable friends; for his accomplishments as a photographer, for jazz music, and for the Cincinnati Reds. The family will receive visitors at home on Sunday afternoon, January 12, after 4:00 P.M.
