
        SAURWEIN--Paul G. Of Cambridge, MA, on November 25, 2003. Former husband of Margaret (Hammond) of Nashua, NH. Father of Margot Powell of Lincoln, RI, George Saurwein of Riverside, RI, and the late Lisa Pajeski and Eric Saurwein.
