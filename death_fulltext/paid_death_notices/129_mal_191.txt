
        McCARTHY-William J. The family of William J. McCarthy notes with sadness his passing October 14, 1997 in New York, NY. Beloved husband of Maureen. Cherished father, 2 sons Brian and Matthew and daughters-in-law Catherine and Monica and devoted grandfather to Eoin and Anneliese. He will be much missed by all who knew him. A memorial mass at 11AM, Saturday, October 18, 1997 at Mother Cabrini Church, 701 Fort Washington Avenue. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to: Friends of Incarnation Children's Center, 142 Audubon Avenue, New York, New York 10032.
