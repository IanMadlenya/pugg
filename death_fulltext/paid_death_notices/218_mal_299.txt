
        GILMAN--Aaron, on March 20, 2005, age 85. Beloved husband of Betty. Loving father of Lynda, Mark, Bill and his wife, Mitzi. Adoring grandfather of Cory and Marla. Lifelong business partner and devoted brother of Bob and his wife, Eileen. President of Intersea Fisheries, former president of Vita Foods and Universal Seafoods. Philanthropist, great humorist, passionate horse lover and devoted friend. He was a spirited, sensitive and caring man with a great love of life and people. His dignity, integrity and positive attitude inspired all who knew him. The world is an emptier place without him. He will be greatly missed. Contributions in his memory may be made to Congregation Beth Shalom Rodfe Zedek, PO Box 438, Chester, CT 06412, or the charity of your choice.
