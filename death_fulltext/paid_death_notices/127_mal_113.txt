
        HERLING-Albert. Age 82. Union labor journalist. Of New York City and Washington, D.C. Died May 31, 1997. He is survived by his wife, Frances, three children Karene Lagrone, Erica Magrum and Konrad Herling, two grandchildren Sheila Marie Robinson and Bryan Magrum and three sisters, Paula Jacob, Nettie Ducas and Cecil Ostrove.
