
        FREEMAN-Clarence W., Jr. Of New York, NY, and Sherman, CT. Died at his home on December 12, at age 89. Graduate of Horace Mann. Attended Brown University. Served in the US Army in Europe during World War II. He is survived by his daughter Rosario Delgado and her husband Manuel; his nephew Francisco Olazabal and his wife Betsy; his grandchildren Manuel F., Jr., Maria, Magdalena, and Joe Delgado; his great-nephew David and great-niece Mary Olazabal; and five great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, December 15, at 10:30 AM.
