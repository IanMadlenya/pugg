
        CARNEY-Thomas A. Died peacefully in his home on July 28 in Belle Harbor, Queens, beloved husband of Margaret (nee Rochford) and the late Thelma (nee Steventon). Until his death he was Vice President of Operations and Management for the Mendik Realty Co. where he served for 28 years. Born in Brooklyn in 1919, he served in The New York State Assembly in the 1950's. Active in Brooklyn and Queens Republican political circles for over 50 years. Stationed in the European Theatre in W.W. II and participated in the Normandy Landing. Also survived by his dear stepchildren, Michael, Patricia, Dennis and Thomas, of New York, and Peggy Murphy, of Winnepeg, Manitoba, and proud grandfather of 8 grandchildren. Fond brother of the late Mary (Mae) Quinn. Visitation on Wednesday 7-10 PM, Thursday 2-5 and 7-10 PM, at Marine Park Funeral Home, 3024 Quentin Road, Brooklyn, NY. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 9:45 A.M. at Saint Francis De Sales Church, Belle Harbor. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations in his memory to The Sisters of Saint Joseph, Maria Regina Convent, Brentwood, New York 11717 or Cabrini Hospice, 227 East 19th Street, NYC 10003.
