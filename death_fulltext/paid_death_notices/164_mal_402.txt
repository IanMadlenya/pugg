
        STERN-Robert B. Age 71. Died Sept. 19, 2000, in Miami, FL, after a valiant battle with cancer. He was predeceased by his daughter Erica Ann in 1992. Survived by his cherished wife Rhoda; Beloved daughters Claudia and Lydia Stern; His extended family, daughters Toby and Terry and son Jeffrey; Granddaughters, Christina and Ryelee; Sister Carole Selig; Nephew Gregory; Brother-in-law Allan; and his many adoring friends. His wit and charm and extraordinary personality melted the hearts of all who knew him. He is loved forever and will never be forgotten. Memorial service was performed at Eternal Light Funeral Chapel in Miami. New York memorial service to be announced.
