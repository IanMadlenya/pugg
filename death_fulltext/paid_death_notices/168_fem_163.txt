
        MILLMAN-Beverly Bard. Beloved wife and mother, died January 12 at 82. She was preceded in death by her husband of 56 years, Dr. Louis Millman. She is survived by her 3 children; Jonathan Millman of Mobile, AL; Paul Millman of Palo Alto, CA., and Amy Faxon of NYC; 2 sisters Audrey Wallach of Port Chester, Olga Farber of San Diego, and six grandchildren. Services have been held.
