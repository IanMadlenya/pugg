
        ROSENTHAL--John P. On March 27, 2004 in LaQuinta, CA at age 71. Cherished husband of Jacqueline.         ROSENTHAL--John P. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, John P. Rosenthal, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing.
