
        SCARPATI-Anthony (Don Anthony). On December 4, 2000 after a long illness. Loving husband of Gloria. Dear father of Nancy and Joseph & brother of Nancy, Helen and Anna, grandfather of Josh and Ari and many nieces and nephews. He was a successful singer, performing mostly in the 1950's and 60's. In 1968 he had a Top 10 Hit ''Anyone Can Move a Mountain''. He went on to be a successful producer. He devoted his life to helping others and touched everyone he ever met. He will be sorely missed. Funeral Thursday, December 7 at the Farenga Brothers Funeral Home, 920 Allerton Ave, Bronx. Services to follow at 9:30AM at the Immaculate Conception Church, 754 East Gunhill Road off White Plains Road.
