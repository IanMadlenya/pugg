
        FRIEDMAN-Yerachmiel. The Orthodox Union and NCSY mourn the passing of the father of our National Vice President and Chairman of our National Youth Commission, Howard Tzvi Friedman. Yerachimel Friedman was a Torah scholar, a builder of Torah, and an effective leader of the Baltimore Orthodox Jewish Community. May his wife and children be comforted among the mourners for Zion and Jerusalem. Mandell I. Ganchrow, M.D., President Marcel Weber, Chairman, Board of Directors Dr. Marcos Katz, Chairman, Board of Governors Rabbi Raphael Butler, Executive Vice President Rabbi Pinchas Stolper, National Director NCSY Paul Glasser, Exec.
