
        KESSEL-Marie Adler. Age 83, of NYC, on September 25, 1999. Cherished wife of the late Lawrence. Adoring mother of Laura K. Shlomm. Loving and devoted grandmother of Alexander and Daniel Shlomm. She was a member of the Board of Trustees of Audrey Cohen College and the founder of The PIBLY Foundattion. Private services were held. Donations in Marie's name may be made to Audrey Cohen College or ''Palliative Care Program,'' Box 1070, Mt. Sinai School of Medicine, NY, NY 10029.
