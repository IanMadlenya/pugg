
        HARTZ--William N. July 13, 2005 at age 83. Beloved husband of Joan (Fellerman). Devoted father of Randee, David, Cindi, Suzie and the late Kenneth. Cherished grandfather of eight. Loving brother of Toby Hornstein and Allan Kaplan. Alumnus of Baruch College of CCNY. Bill's integrity, intelligence and skill made him a leading producer at Salomon, Smith Barney from 1957 to the present. Service Sunday, July 17th, 1:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Cornell-NY Presbyterian Hospital, Division of Cardiology, 525 E. 68 St., NY, NY 10021.
