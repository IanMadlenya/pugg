
        ASCH--Amy Ruth (nee Cohen). Beloved wife of Sidney. Beloved mother of Jane and Nancy. Adored grandmother of Emily. A pillar of her community. A beacon for her family. A gifted social worker by instinct and by training at UCLA and Columbia. Always outspoken, her piercing insight into the lives of others was reflected in her warmth and humor. Her unflagging loyalty and devotion to her family will be deeply missed. She will always be in our hearts. Funeral services Monday, 10 am, at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel.'' Condolence visits MondayWednesday, 4-9 pm, at her home and Thursday, 6-9 pm, at the home of Jane Asch. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Riverdale Mental Health Association.
