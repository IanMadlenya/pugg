
        KOFF-Leah. Emunah of America mourns the passing of Leah Koff, beloved sister of our National President, Marcia Genuth. Leah was a woman of valor who was beloved by everyone who knew her. Our sincerest condolences to her children, Rabbi Zvi Koff, Chavi Goldberg, Abie Koff and Yitzchok Koff, and to her sister, Tamar Tratner. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Sylvia Schonfeld, Chairman of the Board Carol Sufian, Exec. Director Shirley Singer, Exec. Vice President Melanie Oelbaum, Hon. National President
