
        GROSZ--Melvin Kyle. Age 61, of Falmouth, MA, formerly of Bedford, NY, died at his home on December 28, 2005. Kyle worked for IBM for thirtythree years, and retired in 2001 from his position as Vice President of Finance for Large Servers. He is survived by his wife, Ann Lindley Grosz; his daughter, Ingrid, of Seattle, WA; his two sons, Erich and Devin, both of Brooklyn, NY; his mother, Agnes Smith Grosz of Short Hills, NJ; his brother, G. Kurt Grosz of Laguna Beach, CA; and his sister, Kristin Grosz of Boca Raton, FL. A private memorial service will be held at a later date. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice and Palliative Care of Cape Cod, 270 Communication Way, Hyannis, Massachusetts 02601.
