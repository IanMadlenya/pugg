
        JOHNSON--Howard Cooper, Jr., of Fort Myers, Florida, passed away on May 19, 2003, at the age of 94. He had a long career in public service, including serving with the Joint Chiefs of Staff during WW II and The Ford Foundation, and as a Director of the United Nations Planning Staff of the Department of State and Portfolio Manager of the World Bank. In his banking career, he worked for Morgan Stanley and Co. and as President of The World Banking Corporation of Nassau, Bahamas. He is survived by his wife of 30 years, Elizabeth Hovey Johnson, and three children, Pamela Young Johnson, Richard Doane Johnson and Christina Johnson Rosenberg. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Habitat for Humanity of Lee County, 1288 North Tamiami Trail, Fort Myers, Florida 33903. >>AD#
