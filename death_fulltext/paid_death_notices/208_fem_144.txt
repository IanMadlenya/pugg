
        SPIEGEL--Claire. The Solomon Schechter School of Westchester notes with deep sadness the death of Claire Spiegel, mother of our esteemed Headmaster, Dr. Elliot Spiegel. Claire Spiegel, age 92, resided in Lancaster, California, and was the mother of Elliot, Steven and Sala (Simonds), the grandmother of nine, and great-grandmother of three. May her memory be a lasting blessing. M. Mac Schwebel, Chairman, Board of Trustees, James M. Dubin, Vice Chairman
