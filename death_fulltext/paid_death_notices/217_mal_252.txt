
        BERMONT--Hubert, 80, NYC native, lately of Chicago, on February 12. Son of the late Georges and Naomi (Horowitz), he was an NYU Graduate, WW II veteran and Purple Heart recipient. Executive Director of Brentano's Bookstores in the Washington, D.C. area (1957-67). His abiding love of books was the driving force of his life's work as a book industry consultant, author and publisher. His laughter, love for life and devotion to his surviving wife Kimberlee, sons Joshua and Sheldon, daughter - in - law Merrilee and grandchildren, Vincent and Elena, will be missed and fondly remembered.
