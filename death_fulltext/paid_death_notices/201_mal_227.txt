
        ABELSON--Stuart R., 60, of Chicago, IL, died Wednesday October 22. Raised in Glencoe, IL, Stuart graduated from Harvard College in 1965 and Harvard Law in 1969. He practiced poverty law in El Paso, TX, and New York, NY, before returning to Chicago, where he worked in his family's business, Barton Brands. Stuart devoted his life to family, travel, photography and philanthropy. Stuart leaves a philanthropic record that reflects his passions for both arts and policy reform. Stuart is survived by his sons Jesse and Jamie, his partner Olivia Boyce-Abel, his sister Kathy and his mother Hope.
