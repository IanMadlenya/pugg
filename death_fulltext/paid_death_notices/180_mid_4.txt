
        HEFFER-Zelda Rubinstein. Died peacefully at Long Island College Hospital in Brooklyn on January 6. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Ernest T. Heffer. A lifelong resident of Brooklyn, born on May 16, 1914. Graduate of the University of Chicago, Teachers College and Pratt Institute. Predeceased by parents Abraham and Esther Rubinstein and brother Daniel. Survived by sister Judy R. Rosenberg; daughter Karen Locke and her husband Gordon Locke; son Michael Heffer; and grandchildren Steven Locke and his wife Laura Edidin; Scott Locke & his wife Jill Gressin Locke; & Jeffrey Locke. Services at The Riverside, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street, Tuesday, January 8, 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests a donation to the charity of your choice. Family receiving at the home of Karen & Gordon Locke in New Rochelle on Wednesday and Thursday, 3-5 PM and 7-9 PM.
        HEFFER-Zelda R. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved sister of our dear friend and Benefactor, Judy R. Rosenberg, who has participated so greatly in the growth and development of our medical school over many years as a member of our Board of Overseers and as the guiding spirit of our National Women's Division, where she currently chairs the executive committee. To Judy and to the entire family, we offer our deepest sympathy. Yeshiva University Dr. Norman Lamm, President Robert M. Beren, Chairman Board of Trustees Albert Einstein College of Medicine Robert A. Belfer, Chairperson Board of Overseers Dr. Dominick P. Purpura The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Alice Kent, President National Women's Division
