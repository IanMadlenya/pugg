
        LASKER-Pauline. Passed away quietly at home on June 16, 1999. Beloved mother of Jerry and Michele Lasker and Meri and Burton Kaiser, beloved grandmother of Jonathan and Michael Lasker and Hal and Stacey Kaiser, beloved aunt of Richard and Vera Kaye, Alan and Angela Kaye, Stanley and Joan Markowitz and Marvin and Sally Markowitz and their families. Donations in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society and the Jefferson Market Regional Branch of the New York Public Library.
