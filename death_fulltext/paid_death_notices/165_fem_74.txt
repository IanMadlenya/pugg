
        VOSPER-Dena Brown. In Guilford, October 3, 2000, of 35 North St., Guilford CT. Wife of the late Richard Waynard Vosper. Mother of Dinah Hatfield Vosper of Guilford, CT. Sister of Dorothy Brown Fugate of Greenfield, MA. Also survived by three nieces, JoDean Fugate of Wilmington, DE, Judith Fugate-Bahiri of New York City, NY, and Jana Fugate Cummings of Greenfield, MA, and three great-nieces Christina and Colleen Kreisel of Wilmington, DE, and Emma Mae Cummings of Greenfield, MA. Predeceased by two brothers, Jack and Melvyn Brown. Mrs. Vosper was a former librarian at Yale University Department of Public Health and Epidemiology. Prior to Yale, she had been a librarian at Dartmouth College and she was also employed at the US State Department, during WWII. A Graveside Service will be held in Cornish, NH at the convenience of the family. Services in care of the Guilford Funeral Home, 115 Church St, Guilford, CT.
