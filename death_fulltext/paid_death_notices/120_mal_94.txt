
        LOHSE - Roland Henry, 74, beloved husband of Margaret M. (nee Kirk), died January 7, 1997. Also survived by Helen and Gus Beiscacher, Eileen and Robert Cordo, 14 nieces and nephews, grand nieces and nephews, and great grand nieces and nephews. Retired Assistant Vice President of Citibank, NYC, 1938-1981. He served as the President of the Culross Corporation, and was also a decorate army officer. Visiting will be held at the Rocky Point Funderal Home at 603 Rt. 25A in Rocky Point, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM Thurs. with the interment Friday at Calverton National Cemetery.
