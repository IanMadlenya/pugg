
        PRINCE-Esther Ellen Greenleigh, 63, who was a teacher, customer service representative and member of the Weston area community, died of cancer at Rutland Regional Medical Center on Wednesday. Ms. Prince was born in Los Angeles, CA in 1938, and moved with her family to Larchmont, New York in 1948. She graduated from Vassar College in 1960 where she was the editor-in-chief of the college newspaper. For the next 30 years Ms. Prince was a teacher in various public and private school programs in the New York City area. After spending annual vacations in the Weston area for several years she made it her permanent home in 1995. During her time in Vermont, she was a customer service representative at area department stores and a devoted and active member of the Weston community. She is survived by a son, Josh, of San Francisco, CA and a brother, Stephen Greenleigh of Rockville, Maryland. Graveside services will be held at 2PM on Sunday, September 9, 2001 at the Maple Grove Cemetery in Weston.
