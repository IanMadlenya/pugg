
        SNYDER-Jacob M. The Directors and Staff of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York records with sorrow the death of Jack Snyder, Honorary Trustee and former Administrator of the BJE. May Faigel and his entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Philip Schatten, President David Mann, Executive VP Stephen A. Kramer, Chairman of the Board
        SNYDER-Jacob. The Board of the Jewish Reconstructionist Federation (formerly FRCH) mourns the passing of Jack Snyder, a longtime leader in the Jewish Community, and our beloved former President. Our sympathies to his dear wife, Faigal, and his family. Jane Susswein, President, JRF
        SNYDER-Jacob. The Society for the Advancement of Judaism note with profound sorrow the death of longtime, devoted, member Jacob Snyder. We extend our condolences to the entire family. Julie Schonfeld, Rabbi Lawrence Budish, Bd. Chm.
