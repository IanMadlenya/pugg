
        ROGERS-Franklin M. Passed on to eternal life. Born in Irvington, NJ, graduate of Seton Hall University in 1941. A W.W. II naval officer aboard the USS Taylor & PT Boat Squadron Commander in South Pacific, fighting in Guadalcanal, New Guinea, and providing escort for General MacArthur's return to the Philippines. Later a jewelry executive, rose to VP of Bulova Watch Company. Predeceased by his mother, father, sister, Agnes, Matthew and Marion. Survived by his children, Randall, Kevin, Richard and Judith, five grandchildren and three great grandchildren. A dedicated and loving father who will be missed.
