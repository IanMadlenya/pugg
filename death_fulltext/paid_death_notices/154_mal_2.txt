
        FEIN-Samuel Wolf. Born May 13, 1912. Died October 27, 1999. Beloved husband of 60 years of the late Evelyn Leibowitz, loving father of Michael, Cynthia and Eric. Cherished grandfather of Lara and Karen Stolman. Loving brother of Sofie and Evelyn and the late Harry, Bella and Sally. Caring uncle to many nieces and nephews. A sweet, kind and gentle man who loved to sing. Services were held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, on Friday, October 29, 1999. He was loved dearly and will be greatly missed. The angels in heaven will sing more sweetly now.
