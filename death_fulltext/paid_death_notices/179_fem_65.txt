
        SANDLER-Hilda. 85, on December 7, formerly of Miami Beach, FL. Beloved mother of Eli Sandler, mother-in-law of Susan Gershon Sandler of Stamford, CT. Loving grandmother of Matthew Sandler and Tracy Izzo and great-grandmother of Jared Sandler and Hunter Izzo. Honored in 1959 by Rabbi Alexander S. Gross and the Jewish community in Miami Beach for her significant role in raising funds required to build The Hebrew Academy of Miami Beach. She later had a career with Mt. Sinai Hospital in Miami Beach. Services on Monday at 11 AM, at Leo Gallagher Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St, Stamford, CT 06905.
