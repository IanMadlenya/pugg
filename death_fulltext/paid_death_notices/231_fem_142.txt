
        HUGEL--Patricia. 78. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Isadore Hugel died Monday, April 10, 2006 after a brief illness. A lifelong resident of NYC, Patricia recently moved to Milford, CT. She was predeceased by her brother and sister-in-law, Rodney and Thelma Munroe. Pat is survived by three nieces, two nephews and nine great nieces and nephews.
