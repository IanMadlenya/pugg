
        REDMOND-Dewitt Jr. On October 26, 2000, in his 68th year. Loving husband of Amanda (Jones). Brother of Hertacine Redmond, Jamaica, NY, Cleta Dutton, Benny Redmond and Matthew Redmond of Gulfport, Miss, and Joseph Redmond of Atlanta, GA. He was employed by The New York Times newspaper for over thirty-five years until his retirement in 1994. Visitation on Friday is from 9-10 A.M., followed by religious services at 10 A.M. at Paul Lane Funeral Home, 115-33 Sutphin Boulevard, Jamaica, New York. Interment is to follow at Fairlawn Cemetery in Fairlawn, New Jersey.
