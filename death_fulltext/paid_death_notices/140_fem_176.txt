
        RADL-Dr. Lucia, born in Odessa, Russia in 1900, passed away on September 20, 1998 at Lenox Hill Hospital in New York City. A physician and psychiatrist, Dr. Radl was trained in Europe and in the United States. Professionally active for many decades, she was on the staffs of the Joint Diseases Hospital and Lenox Hill, and served as a Senior Analyst and Faculty Superviser at the Alfred Adler Institute of New York. She was an emeritus member of the American Medical Association & belonged to the North American Society of Adlerian Psychology. Dr. Radl was also the author of numerous articles & professional manuals. She will be missed by family, friends and colleagues. Funeral was private. A memorial gathering will be held for her on November 1, 1998 from 2-4PM at the Olcott Hotel, 27 West 72 St., NYC. In lieu of flowers, please donate to the charity of your choice.
