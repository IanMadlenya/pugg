
        SIPSER-I Philip. March 31, 2001, at age 82. Devoted husband of Martha. Loving father of Margaret, Jane, Henry, William and father-in-law of Leonard Leibowitz and Dean Kaplan. Loving brother of Sophie, Morris and Kenneth. Proud ''Papa'' of Max, Tovah and Emily. He was a tireless attorney dedicated to defending and assisting working people. His commitment to the cause of labor was boundless. Funeral services at Riverside Mt. Vernon, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY, 12 noon, today. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the ACLU or the charity of one's choice. To: Max, Tovah and Emily. I Philip Sipser was a gifted and creative attorney who spent a lifetime working for civil rights, civil liberties and political freedom.
