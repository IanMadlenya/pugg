
        BLACK-Irving, age 90, on April 10, 1997, of Fair Lawn, New Jersey. He was the founder of two electronic manufacturing companies; Astron Corp. of East Newark, NJ, and Elmag Corp. of Newark, NJ. Survived by his son, Gary; brother, Robert; sisters, Beatrice Barbara, and Molly Levine. Services are Sunday, 11:30AM, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
