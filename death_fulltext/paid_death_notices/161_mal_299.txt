
        SISKIND-William. The Board of Trustees, Staff and Faculty of Postgraduate Center for Mental Health note with sorrow the loss of William Siskind, father of our dear friend and colleague, Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive Vice President of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services. We send our deepest condolences to Dr. Siskind, his wife, Marla, and the members of his family. Aniello A. Bianco, Chairman of the Board Postgraduate Center Dr. Jacob Barak, President & CEO Postgraduate Center
