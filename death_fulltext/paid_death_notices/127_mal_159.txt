
        RAPPAPORT-Carl. The Board of Directors, Staff and Families of the Association for Children with Retarded Mental Development joins the Rappaport family in mourning the passing of Carl Rappaport, an incorporator of the Association and a past President of the Board. An advocate for people with mental retardation since the 1940's. Husband of the late Ida Rappaport, long-time Executive Director and Founder of the Association for C.R.M.D., Inc. He was always an exceedingly generous and willing supporter. We shall miss him. Funeral services will be held at the Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York, Friday, August 15 at 10:30 A.M. Jefferson W. Woodcox, President Lawrence Hirsch, Vice-President Rose Podolsky, Secretary
