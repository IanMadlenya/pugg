
        WADE-Sean. Native of County Mayo, Ireland. Peacefully died at home on Monday evening, June 7, 1999. He is survived by his beloved wife of 47 years, Rosaleen (nee O'Shea), his children, Rory, John and Ann Marie, and his five grandchildren whom he adored. He owned and managed Andy's Meat Market in Washington Hts. for over 25 years where the friendship, jokes and love were gratis. Mass Thursday, 11AM, St. Anthony's Church, Northvale, NJ. Visiting Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9PM at Pizzi Funeral Home, 120 Paris Ave., Northvale. 201-767-3050.
