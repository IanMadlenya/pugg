
        MOHR-Edward J. Jr., 72, retired engineer, who worked on the development of the first IBM digital computer, died September 28 of cancer. He also participated in monitoring early nuclear tests in Nevada. His ballroom dance friends will miss him very much. He is survived by a brother Richard Mohr, and sister Constance Sheppard, six nephews and nieces and four grand-nephews. Burial is Thursday at the Veterans Cemetery in Calverton, Long Island
