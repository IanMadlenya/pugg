
        SHILLIETO-David Stanley, 47, 406 West 48th Street, New York, N.Y. died September 6, 1999, from cancer at his parents home in Burlington Green, N.Y. He was an accomplished pianist, choral director, and singer affiliated with Julliard and other organizations in Manhattan. For information call HoukJohnston-Terry Funeral Home, Edmeston, N.Y. 607 965-8242.
