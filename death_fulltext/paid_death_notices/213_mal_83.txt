
        KAUFFMAN--Bernard. Age 99, on October 4. A lifelong resident of Flatbush, Brooklyn. He presided over H. Kauffman & Sons Saddlery Co., a landmark retail institution in New Yorkestablished in 1875. Married 67 years to the late Elsie Kauffman, father of Charles and Ronald, grandfather to six and great grandfather to one grandson. Services Sunday, October 10, 10 AM, Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1895 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to a charity of your choice is appreciated.
