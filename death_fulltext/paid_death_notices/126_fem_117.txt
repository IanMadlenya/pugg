
        LEICHTMAN - Esther. Dearest mother of Francine Tancer (Michael). Devoted wife of the late Jacob Leichtman. Loving grandmother of Joan Tancer and Susan Tolchin. Cherished great grandmother of Jennifer and Eric. Died peacefully at home after a long and wonderful life. She was a lifelong supporter of Hadassah and she and her husband were involved in many worthy causes. Her marvelous sense of humor and story telling will be remembered by everyone who knew her. Her grace, charm, wit and beauty will be with us always. Her life was an inspiration to us all.
