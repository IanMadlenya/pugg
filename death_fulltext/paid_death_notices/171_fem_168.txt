
        FLANAGAN-Judith Ann. Died peacefully in her home in Monterrey, California on April 8. 2001. A pioneering midwife, Judith was born in Brooklyn in 1937 and spent the majority of her professional life in California. She is survived by her loving daughter Laura and her brothers Michael and Peter. A memorial service will be held in Carmel, California, on April 28. Contributions can be sent to the American College of Nurse Midwives in Washington D.C.
