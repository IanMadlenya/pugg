
        LANDAU-Janette. May 1, 1997. Beloved mother and friend, missed by daughter Iris Scharer, son Charles Landau and persons all over the world. She was a talented sculptress and artist and a generous supporter of many charities. Her compassion and understanding benefitted many. A memorial service will be held at the Theosophical Society, 242 East 53 St., 6:30 PM, Friday, May 23.
