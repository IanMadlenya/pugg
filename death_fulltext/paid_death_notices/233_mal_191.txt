
        BERMAN--Dr. William. At the age of 91. Dentist 47 years, formerly of Westbury, NY. Loving husband to Lillian, father to Janet and Phyllis, grandfather to Alexis, fatherin-law to Al and Michael. WWII Captain and only ''painless dentist in New Guinea'' and JFK. Selfless service to the community and his patients: Chancellor Commander Knights of Pythias, cancer care, volunteer dentist for cerebral palsy and handicapped children at LIJ Hospital, teen dentist Westbury High School and Sun City Center Emergency Squad. Services Tuesday, 1 pm, from Gutterman's Inc, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
