
        ROSS-Patricia Kerr. The Board and Staff of the New York Foundation for the Arts is deeply saddened by the death of our friend and colleague Patricia Kerr Ross, the former director of the State University of New York's Programs in the Arts. We extend our deepest condolences to her family, and her many friends and colleagues. She will be deeply missed by the artists and arts community throughout the State. Theodore S. Berger, Executive Director Penelope Dannenberg, Director of Programs
