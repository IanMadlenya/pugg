
        GREEF--Howard A., of Essex Fells, New Jersey died Wednesday, June 13 at the age of 69. He was the beloved husband of Mimi, cherished father of Leslie Yarusinsky, Charles Greef; stepfather of Elissa Krebs and Erica Cohen; and adored grandfather of William, Lexa, Jolie, and Phoebe. Mr. Greef was a C.P.A. and financial consultant with offices in Roseland, New Jersey, New York City and Paris. He served as Treasurer of the Board of Trustees of The Marilyn Horne Foundation, on the Board of Advisors of The Academy of Clinical and Applied Psychoanalysis in Livingston, New Jersey, and was formerly the Treasurer of the Board of Trustees of The Orange Lawn Tennis Club in South Orange, New Jersey. Funeral services will take place at Bernheim Apter Kreitzman Funeral Chapel, Livingston, New Jersey on Friday, June 15 at 12 noon.
