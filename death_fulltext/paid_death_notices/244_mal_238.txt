
        GLAZER--Bennett, 81, passed away at his home on May 22, after a long battle with respiratory disease. Bennett was and will continue to be known as the founder of Graphic Image and for his 50 years of design work in the publishing industry. His company, now run by his children, created books that are among the finest leather bindings in the world. His diary designs stand as his masterpiece and are treasured by loyal users everywhere. He is survived by his loving wife Phyllis, his two surviving children, Tom (Elizabeth) and Carol O'Connell (John), and the late Janice Barbone (Luca), and eight loving grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
