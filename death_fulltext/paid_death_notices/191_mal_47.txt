
        VANDERBILT-Douglas R. Age 81. Died peacefully on December 3, 2002 at his home, attended by his family. Devoted husband to the late Mary Forsyth and father of Christine, Kim, Douglas, Kip, Lori, Chad, Peter and Bob Vanderbilt. Father-in-law to Julie, Tony and Gail. Loving grandfather to Douglas, David and Travis Vanderbilt, and Joshua Bien. His gentle guidance, steadfast support to those in need, and quick humor will be remembered by his family and friends. Service 9AM Saturday, Dec 7th, Saint Mary's R.C. Church, Colts Neck, N.J.
