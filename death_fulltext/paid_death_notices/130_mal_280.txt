
        RAND-Alfred. Age 89, November 24, 1997. Beloved husband of Rita Lewis Rand and loving father of Dr. Kenneth Rand, and devoted stepfather of Sara Stone and Laura Ross, and adoring grandfather of Erik, Colin, Betsy-Gail, Andrea, Ralph, Elizabeth, and David, and great grandfather of Jamie and Charlie. Services are on Tuesday, November 25, at Park Avenue Synagogue. In lieu of flowers, contributions to his memory may be made to The New York Service For Handicapped.
        RAND-Alfred. The Board of Directors and Staff of the New York Service for the Handicapped/ Camp Oakhurst note with sadness the death of Alfred Rand, husband of Rita Rand, a Board Member for many years. To Rita and her family go our heartfelt condolences. Marilyn Friedman, Pres Marvin A. Raps, Exec Dir
        RAND-Alfred. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife, Rita, and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Alan Levine, Chrmn of the Board Robert P. Antler, President
        RAND-Alfred. The Officers, Trustees and Members of The Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mr. Alfred Rand. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
