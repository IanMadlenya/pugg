
        KAPLAN-David. Dean Ira B. Lamster, Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery, records with deep sorrow the death of our colleague, Dr. David Kaplan, Special Lecturer in Dentistry, on January 31, 2002. Dr. Kaplan made many contributions to the School's clinical, administrative, and didactic activities, including the dental auxiliary utilization program, for more than 35 years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Ellen, sons Abbe and Joshua, daughter Susan and to his mother. Ira B. Lamster, D.D.S., M.M.Sc. Dean, Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery
