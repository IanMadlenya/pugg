
        ABRAMS-Mildred. Passed away peacefully on October 24th. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Devoted mother of Franklin and Leslie, Elliott and Rachel. Cherished grandmother of Courtney, Jason, Jacob, Sarah and Joseph. Loving sister of Samuel and the late Joseph and Saul Kauder. Sister-in-law of Rosalind and Beatrice Kauder. We will always treasure her vitality, thoughtfulness and great devotion to everyone in her extended family. Services Sunday 11:30AM at RiversideNassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
        ABRAMS-Mildred. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of the mother of our Temple President, Franklin S. Abrams. We extend deepest sympathy to Frank, Elliot, and their bereaved families. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
