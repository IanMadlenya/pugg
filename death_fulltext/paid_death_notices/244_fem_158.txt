
        COHN--Florence, age 93, died peacefully at home May 19 in Laguna Woods, CA. Born Florence Weiner, daughter of Sam and Rose of Beaver Falls, PA. Sister of Milton, Ruth, Stephen and Helen. A lifelong social activist and tireless advocate for peace and justice. Came to NYC in her late teens where she became a union organizer, married Max Cohn, raised four children, and established a distinguished career in University Press publishing. In her California retirement she began a series of spoken book reviews in Santa Monica which became a popular feature in Leisure World. She also served in leadership positions for Concerned Citizens. She was a gifted artisan and pianist. Florence was loved and admired by many and will be missed by all. Memorial celebrations are planned for the Laguna Woods Community, and for family and friends in NYC.
