
        BENJAMIN-Irving. 85. June 16, 1998. Husband of the late Helen (nee Miller). Brother to Jack, the late Martin (George) and the late Claire Bergen. Born in NYC he was a graduate of Stuyvesant H.S. and City College. A civil engineer for NYC he helped design and build the Whitestone Bridge and Major Deegan Expressway. Irving was admired by all.
