
        LEO-Paul Herbert. 86. Died peacefully April 30, at home after a bout with cancer. Married to Gladys for the 62 happiest years of their lives; beloved father of Marie and Jerry (deceased); Gianna's loving Grandpa; father-figure to his devoted nephews, Jamie & Erik, Jerry's partner Rolando, and Gianna's dad Joe. An autodidact, his wisdom was sought and respected by family and friends. He was dedicated to workers' rights as a trade unionist. A lifetime fighter against injustice. He was a humanist.
