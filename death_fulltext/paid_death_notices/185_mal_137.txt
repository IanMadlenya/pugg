
        WARD-Benjamin. On June 10, 2002, at age 75. Beloved husband of Olivia. Devoted father to Benjamin Ward, Jr., Jacquelyn Ward, Gregory Ward, Margie Ward Lewis and Mary Ward. Loving grandfather of nine and great-grandfather of two, with numerous nieces, nephews, cousins and godchildren. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Wednesday and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Saint Patrick's Cathedral, 5th Ave. at 51 St., Friday, 10 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Lupus Foundation, 149 Madison Ave., NY, NY 10016 or the American Diabetes Association, 149 Madison Ave., NY, NY 10016.
        WARD - Benjamin. The Brooklyn Law School community is deeply saddened by the loss of its distinguished graduate and former Adjunct Professor, Benjamin Ward. Although his presence will be missed, his contributions to law enforcement, legal education, and the legal community will be long eduring. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Paul Windels, Jr., Chairman, Board of Trustees Joan G. Wexler, Dean
