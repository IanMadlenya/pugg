
        WOLFF-Barbara E. Age 58, October 22, 1999, peacefully at home on the upper westside. She was an Assistant Commissioner of NYC, she served as Treasurer and member of the Baord of Directors of Cap 21. She enriched our lives with her brillance, humor and dedication. She is survived by her devoted brother Bill, her loving cousins, many dear friends and colleagues and her beloved city. The family will receive visitors Sunday, 1-7 PM, Monday and Tuesday 4-8 PM, at 395 Riverside Dr, Apt 11G. In lieu of flowers please contribute to your favorite charity.
