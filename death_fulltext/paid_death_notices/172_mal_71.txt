
        FAATH-Harry W. Jr. Of Ridgewood, NJ, on May 4, 2001, of cancer. Beloved husband of Margaret (Bettman). Devoted father of Sharon and Michael Fremer and Robert and Gayle Faath. Graduated Bogota, NJ High School. Attended Columbia University. A Marine Corps World War II fighter pilot, recalled during the Korean Conflict, he retired as a major. He worked in the muncipal bond industry, retiring in 1985 as Vice President at Tucker Anthony. Funeral arrangements private. Donations to the cancer charity of your choice.
