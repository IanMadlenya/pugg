
        TEICHER-Mildred (Mickey). Died in Miami, FL. on June 17, 2001. Beloved wife of Morton for 60 years. Cherished mother of Phyllis and Alvin Goldman, Oren Teicher and Alison Greene. Proud grandmother of Elizabeth, Mattis, Mark, Carrie, Jessica and Zachary. Devoted daughter of the late Esther and Morris Adler. Sister of the late Samuel & Herbert Adler. Sister-inlaw of Bunny Adler, Sylvia Adler & Gerald Teicher. Loving aunt and great aunt to her many nieces and nephews. Former resident of New Rochelle, N.Y. She earned her Bachelors in Art History at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill & her Masters degree in Jewish Studies at Barry University, Miami. A Jewish Art Historian, she served as the Director of the Beck Museum of Judaica, Beth David Congregation, Miami. Services at Bet Am Shalom, 295 Soundview Ave, White Plains, New York, Wednesday, June 20, 10AM. Interment at Sharon Gardens, Mount Pleasant, N.Y. Shiva will be held at the Teicher home, 10 Richbell Road, White Plains, NY.
