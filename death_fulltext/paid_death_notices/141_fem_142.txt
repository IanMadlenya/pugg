
        LUKENS-Kathleen. The College of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of Kathleen Lukens, compassionate champion of the developmentally disabled, 1984 recipient of the Doctor of Humane Letters degree, and beloved wife of Dr. John Lukens of the psychology department. We mourn her loss and celebrate her life. Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU, Chair, Board of Trustees Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D, President
