
        McCREERY--Robert L. Of NYC and Rhinebeck, NY, on June 8, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Saunders O'Keefe. Loving father of Marina McCreery. Devoted brother of the late William C. McCreery, Gladys McCreery, Beatrice Flynn and Florence Rick. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Graduate of Fordham University and St. John's Law School. He was a U.S. Attorney for the Treasury Department. Funeral services were held on June 12, 2004 at the Church of St. Thomas More.
