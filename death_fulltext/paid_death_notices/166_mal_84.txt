
        JEWETT-Charles Wood, 87, of Mt. Archer Rd., Lyme, CT died Friday, November 3, 2000 at his home. Mr. Jewett was a prominent attorney, banker and former lieutenant governor of Connecticut. Mr. Jewett is survived by his wife of 62 years, Mary ''Molly'' Sheafe Jewett, two sons, Jonathan Jewett of Lyme and New York City, Charles Wood Jewett Jr. of Las Vegas, NV; a daughter, Jennifer Jewett Smith of Silver Spring, Md., nine grandchildren and a greatgrandson. A memorial service will be held at 11:00 am on Saturday, November 11, 2000 at the First Congregational Church of Old Lyme. Burial will be private. There are no calling hours. Donations may be made in his memory to the Visiting Nurse Association of Southeastern Connecticut, PO Box 264, Waterford, CT 06385 or the Lyme Public Library, Route 156, Lyme, CT 06371. The FultonTheroux Funeral Home, Beckwith Lane, Old Lyme is in charge.
