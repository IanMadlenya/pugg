
        PRIME--Jane Kerr of Pound Ridge, NY, died at her home on Saturday July 24, 2004. She is survived by her brother John Alex Kerr, her children, Eve, Alexandra and Corry, and her dogs Sadie and Pooh Bear. Adored by many, she will be fondly remembered for dancing in the kitchen, wearing crazy hats and for her love of a good party. Jane inspired us all with her courage and strength of spirit in confronting the obstacles life presented. She will be sadly missed. Service to be held at St. Matthew's Church in Bedford, NY on Saturday July 31, at 2PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Humane Society or to Gilda's Club.
