
        DEANE--Marjorie. The Board and staff offer its deepest condolences to the family of Marjorie Deane. Marjorie epitomized the values inherent in Girl Scouting as a dedicated director for over 20 years. All who knew Marjorie enjoyed learning from her and working by her side. Susan Nitze, President Susan Greenbaum, CEO Girl Scout Council of Greater New York
