
        SCHRECK-Michael Henry, 98. Internationally recognized artist, died peacefully August 2nd in his home in Florida. His life was dedicated to his family and his art. He will be greatly missed. Mr. Schreck was a member of The Royal Society of Arts, London Accademia Italia, The American Federation of Arts. His work is in many fine art museums. He is survived by his loving wife Lizzi, daughter Evelyn Wyman of Roslyn, NY, son Peter Shreck of Cornwall, UK, five grandchildren and his great-grandson.
