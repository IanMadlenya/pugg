
        FLYNN-Ursula, age 87, died peacefully on March 5, 2000, at The Carolton in Fairfield, CT. Ursula had been a long time resident of Peter Cooper Village in NYC. Predeceased by her loving family, parents John and Mary McCabe Flynn, sister Vera Flynn, and brother David Flynn. She is survived by many devoted cousins, godchildren and friends. Born in NYC on October 31, 1912, she graduated from the College of New Rochelle in 1934, and completed a long and distinguished career in executive secretarial positions at PS 183 in NYC. Ursula loved travel, culture, bridge, The NY Times, her Church, NYC, her Irish roots, and, above all, her family and friends. A Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated at 11 AM, on Monday, March 13, in St. Francis of Assisi Chapel at The Cemetery of the Gate of Heaven in Hawthorne, NY. Burial at the cemetery will follow. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to The Arthritis Foundation, 122 East 42nd St., 18th Fl, New York, NY 10168-1898. For information CodyWhite Funeral Home in Milford, CT, (203) 874-0268.
