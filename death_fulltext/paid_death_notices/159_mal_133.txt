
        ARUNACHALA-Bhakta Bhagawat, founder in 1966 of Arunachala Ashrama, Bhagavan Sri Ramana Maharshi Centers in New York and Nova Scotia passed away on April 10th. He is survived by his son, Bhaskar, daughter-in-law Hena, and four grandchildren, and many hundreds of devotees of Sri Ramana Maharshi, the great Indian sage. Viewing will be at Nufeld Funeral Home, 88-04 43rd Ave., Elmhurst, Queens, Thursday from 3-5 PM & 7-9 PM; cremation service Friday at 10 AM.
