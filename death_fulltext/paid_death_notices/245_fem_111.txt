
        SILVERMAN--Sara. A glorious 94 years, she passed away on June 15, 2007. Daughter of Rose and Morris Sokoloff. Widow of Max Silverman. Beloved mother of Abby Garfein and Eric Ober, children of her first husband Ralph Ober. Sister of Irving Sokoloff. Grandmother of Karen, Matthew, Nicholas, Barbara, Irene, Shelley, Phyllis and Erica. Great-grandmother of Hannah, Jacob, Jeremy and Diana. Mother-in-law of Jack Garfein and Lisa Anderson and sister-in-law of Mildred Sokoloff. On her last day, she won all her hands playing bridge with pals Shirley, Shula and Muriel. What could be better. She loved everyone and they loved her.
