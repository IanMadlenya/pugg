
        AMESBURY - Cecile Dudley passed away of natural causes on February 29, 2000 at the PA. Hospital. She was born in NYC and has resided in Cincinnatti, OH, Washington, DC, and for the past 38 years resided in Philadelphia. She is survived by her husband Walter Raleigh Amesbury Jr. Visitation will be Saturday after 11AM in St. Patrick Church, 242 S. 20 St. Philadelphia. Funeral Mass 12 noon.
