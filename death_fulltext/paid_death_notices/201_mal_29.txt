
        BENNETT--Jay I. On September 30, 2003 at age 77. Adoring husband of Madeleine of 53 years. Devoted and loving father of Bonnie and cherished grandfather of Lily. He was a true confidante, advisor and trusted friend. Jay enjoyed life and people to the fullest. He devoted over half a century to Revlon, where he played a major management role from the company's modest beginnings to its huge success. He will be greatly missed and forever warmly remembered. Funeral services were held on Occtober 2, 2003.
