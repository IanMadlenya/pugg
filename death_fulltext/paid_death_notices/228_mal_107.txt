
        O'SULLIVAN - Kevin. WLIW21 New York Public Television mourns the passing of our Board Member, generous supporter and friend, Kevin O'Sullivan. His commitment to educational television through the O'Sullivan Children Foundation enabled us to fulfill our role in the community, and his legacy will live on in the thousands of children we reach each day. Our condolences to Carole and the O'Sullivan family. Terrel L. Cass, President and General Manager Barry Shapiro, Chairman Board of Trustees, LIETV Council, Inc.
