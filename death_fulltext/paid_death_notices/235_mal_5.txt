
        KATZ--Esther. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Esther Katz, beloved mother of Arthur Katz, a treasured friend of the New York Jewish community whose dedicated leadership and support strengthens our ability to serve those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Arthur and his wife Barbara; to Arthur's sister, Debbi Ecker; to Esther's sister, Lucille Fishbein; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
