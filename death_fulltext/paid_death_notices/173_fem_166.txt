
        MARX-Hedwig (Nee Simon). Died June 18, 2001 in her 95th year, after a long and courageous battle with Alzheimer's. She is survived by her daughter Ellen Kramer and much loved and wonderful son-inlaw, Burt, with whom she resided the last years of her life. She will forever be remembered for her great indomitable spirit and love of her family. Unselfish & devoted wife of the late Max, mother of Gerard her late beloved son. Dedicated grandmother of Judith and husband Kevin, Daniel and wife Michelle, and Eric and devoted daughter-in-law Silvia, all of whom she loved with unending joy and pride. At her request services will be private.
