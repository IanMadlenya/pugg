
        KAYE-Florence R. Of Brookville, LI, on March 11 in her 87th year. Beloved wife of the late Arthur A.; daughter of the late Mortimer H. and Mae Luyster Rudyard; granddaughter of Florence Jackson Luyster and great-granddaughter of Thomas T. and Mary Clement Jackson of Glen Cove. Survived by one stepson, Stephen C. Visitation at Dodge Thomas Funeral Home, Glen Cove, Wednesday, 46. Service St. Paul's Church, Highland Rd, Thursday, 11 AM. Donations may be made to the church.
