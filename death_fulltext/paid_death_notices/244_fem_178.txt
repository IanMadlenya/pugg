
        JOHNSTON--Leslie (nee Berkman), wife of Derek Johnston, sister of Jane and Robert Berkman and sisterin-law of Solvig Berkman and Steven Wolfson, died May 16 after a brief illness. Leslie worked as a special education teacher for over twenty years for the Department of Education in the City of New York. She also leaves behind many friends and her nephews Sam Wolfson and Theo Berkman and her nieces Talia Berkman and Quinn Berkman, as well as her cousins Paul, David, Daniel and Julie Ehrlich. Service will be held at the Greenwich Village Funeral Home at 199 Bleecker St. (between Sixth Ave and McDougal St.) at 10:30 am, Friday, May 25th. Interment will be private at a later date.
