
        WATSON-James Lopez. His friends and colleagues at HEAF (The Harlem Educational Activities Fund) note with profound sorrow the loss of Judge James L. Watson. A founding director of HEAF and a continuing source of encouragement and support, Skizz brought wisdom, compassion and unfailing good humor to our deliberations. He will be sorely missed and long remembered. The Harlem Educational Activities Fund Daniel Rose, President Courtney Welsh, Exec. Director
