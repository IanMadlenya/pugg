
        McDONOUGH - James Pa trick. 82, of Jackson Heights, NY, died peacefully surrounded by his children after a brief illness. Devoted husband of Joan (nee Day) to whom he was married for 56 years. Brother of Mary Gargiulo. Loving father of James, Michael, Kevin, Mary and John. Cherished grandfather of Alicia, Jude, Conor and Keira. Viewing to be held at Conway's Funeral Home, 82-19 Northern Blvd., Jackson Heights, NY, Tuesday, July 2, 2-5 and 7-9, 718-639-3220. A burial Mass will be held at St. Joan of Arc Church in Jackson Heights, NY, Wednesday, July 3, 9:30 AM. His guidance, love, humor and strength will be missed forever.
