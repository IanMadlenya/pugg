
        KATZMAN--Ruth B., died on February 22, 2007 after a long battle with ovarian cancer. Beloved daughter of the late Max Katzman and the late Lilian Goldfarb Katzman, Ruth was a cum laude graduate of New York University. Her father was the original inventor of the electric vaporizer and founder of Kaz, Inc., a company which manufactures vaporizers, humidifiers and other health products. She was a 50% stockholder in the company after her father retired and was Vice President and Secretary of Kaz, Inc. She is survived by her brother, Lawrence, and two nephews, Richard and John, a niece, Julie, and a grandniece and grandnephews. Service Monday 10:00 am at ''The Riverside'', 76th and Amsterdam Ave.
        $(6$)KATZMAN--Ruth. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Ruth Katzman. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Marcia Waxman, Pres. George Asch, Sec'y
