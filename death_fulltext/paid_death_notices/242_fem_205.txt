
        NADELSON--Sally, passed away on March 20th, 2007, at the age of 92 after a brief illness. She was the quintessential Jewish mother, devoting her life to the health and wellbeing of her family. She is survived by two sons, Gerald Nadelson of Long Island and Kenneth Nadelson of Manhattan, and one daughter, Hon. Eileen N. Nadelson of Manhattan. She is also survived by four grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.
