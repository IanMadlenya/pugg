
        LEVINE-Harry J. On August 13, 2002, at the age of 102. Beloved husband of Rose. Loving father of Richard and Andrew, and father-in-law of Judith. Cherished grandfather of Joshua, David and Adam. Devoted brother of Florence Wishnow, Beatrice Flagler, Dr. Arnold E. Leeds and the late Dr. Meyer R. Levine, Arthur M. Levine and D. George Levine. He left a lasting impression on all who knew him. His love and devotion to family and community will be a source of joy and strength all the days of our lives. Services today, 10AM at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave (Ave J), Brooklyn, N.Y.
