
        HUTSCHNECKER-Dr. Arnold A. Died peacefully on December 28, 2000, at his home in Sherman, Connecticut. at age 102. Beloved brother of Greta Hutschnecker Plattry. Loving uncle of Ruth Schwarz, Gaby Spellman, Lauren Hutton, Dorianne Samuels, Dorina Link, Alexander and Wolfgang Zuckerman, Ronald and Alan Kramer, 15 grand nieces and nephews and great-grand uncle of eight. A German immigrant who came to New York City in 1936 practicing internal medicine, turning to psychiatry in 1952. Dr. Hutschnecker was a pioneer in psychosomatic medicine. Author of 5 books including ''The Will To Live''. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St, Tuesday, January 2, at 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Westchester Hills Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
