
        KATZ--Thelma. On July 1. Beloved wife of the late Harvey Katz. Wonderful mother of Bennett. Thelma retired as a partner with Harvey in Holiday Wine & Liquor in Grand Central Terminal. She will be missed by all who knew her. Donations in Thelma's memory may be made to Project Ezra, 197 E. Broadway, NY, NY 10002.
