
        LONGHI--Vincent J. (Jim), attorney, playwright, author, died peacefully at his home in Manhattan after a short illness, at 90 years of age. He is survived by Gabrielle, his wife and companion of 63 years, by his son Jaime, his daughter Gaby, his granddaughters Allegra and Laura, and his dog Pucci. At Jim's request, the funeral will be private. Messages can be left at Legacy.com
