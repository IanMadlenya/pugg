
        KALLMAN-Mildred Cox died at home in Locust Valley, NY on June 29, with children beside her. Born in Irvine, KY., daughter of Walter Cox and Annie Allen Elgin. She attended Centre College and the University of Kentucky '41, Kappa Kappa Gamma. Predeceased by James Perry Patton she then married Raymond Kallman. She bore five children: James P. Patton Jr., Curtis Patton, Nancy Ann Patton, Laura Kallman, Peter M. Kallman and thirteen grandchildren. Loyal, loving, kind, she bestowed humility and generosity to all. Memorial service is Wednesday, July 3rd, 4:30 PM at Trinity Church, 1579 Northern Blvd., Roslyn Harbor. Donations can be made to the Nassau AHRC.
