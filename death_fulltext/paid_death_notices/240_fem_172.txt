
        KRAUS--Janet Hoenig, born in The Bronx, passed away peacefully at her home in Houston, Texas on January 11, 2007. She is survived by her husband of 65 years, Phil; daughters Beth Johnstone (Edwin), Suzanne May (Don); grandchildren Andy Turboff (Amy), Jeff Turboff, Alexa May and Chloe May; and great-grandson, Will Turboff. Janet and Phil moved to Houston in 1978 to be near their children and grandchildren. Always the life of the party, she will be remembered for her wonderful sense of humor, beautiful knitting and fabulous chopped liver.
