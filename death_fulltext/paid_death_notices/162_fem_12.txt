
        BOYLE-Catherine J. 86, of Branford Court, Farmington Woods, Avon, CT., died Saturday, July 1, 2000 at home. Born in New York City, NY, daughter of the late James and Margaret (Fox) Boyle. Prior to retiring, she was an accountant with Madigan-Hyland Co. She is survived by two nieces, Deirdre Boyle of New York City and Kathleen Humen of Somers, NY. Burial will be in Immaculate Conception Cemetery, Montclair, NJ today at 2:00 PM.
