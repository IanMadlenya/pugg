
        ZIMMERMAN - Elaine Beverly Wolf. Passed away October 20, 2000. Beloved and cherished wife of Dr. Theodore Zimmerman. Loving mother of Rick, Jacqui, Ron and Deborah Zimmerman. Devoted sister to Renee Wolf Steinberg. Adoring grandmother to David, Aaron, Ezra and Joseph Zimmerman. Services will be on Monday, October 23, 12 Noon, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Westchester Hills Cemetery. She touched our lives with beauty and love.
