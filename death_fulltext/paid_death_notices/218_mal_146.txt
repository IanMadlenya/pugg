
        HUTTON--William L., 82, on March 4, 2005, at home in Whispering Pines, N.C. Formerly of New York City, East Hampton, NY, and Vero Beach, FL. Survived by his wife of 22 years, Marcia Breen Hutton; daughters, Consuelo, Linda, Cynthia and Mimi, nine grandchildren. The gentlest of gentlemen, dearly loved by his family.
