
        NUSBAUM-Marilyn (nee Brooks). Of Fort Lee, New Jersey. Age 68. On January 20, 1997. Wife of Arthur. Mother of Barbara Selick, Robert and Howard Nusbaum. Sister of Janet Sorkin. Grandmother of 6 grandchildren. Mrs. Nusbaum was formerly a teacher. She belonged to several oranizations, among them A.A.U.W. and Flat Rock Brook Nature Center, but she will most be remembered as the bedrock of her family.
