
        WOLFE--Rose. On September 8. Beloved mother of Paula W. Brill, M.D., and the late Stephen Wolfe. Cherished grandmother of Laura and Stephen Brill. Beloved greatgrandmother of Jackson, Sophie and Benjamin. Loving grandmother-in-law of Frances Wolfe and Ellen Evans. She was a retired New York City school principal and wife of the late Dr. Jack M. Wolfe, professor of mathematics and founder of the Department of Computer Science at Brooklyn College. Services Wednesday, September 10, 11:30 AM at Temple EmanuEl, 5th Ave. at 65th St.
        WOLFE--Rose. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Rose Wolfe our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
