
        SILBERSTEIN-Samuel. Of Munich, Germany, (originally of Warsaw, Poland). Died suddenly on May 30, 2000. Beloved husband of Luba. Dedicated father of Yankel, and grandfather of Benjamin and Joab. A survivor of the horrors of World War II, he rebuilt his life through much hard work and became a successful and loyal member of his community. He will be remembered for his generosity & love of family. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him.
