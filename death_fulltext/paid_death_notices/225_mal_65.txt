
        BRUSTEIN--Dr. Milton W. Age 85, died at home October 5, 2005 after a long heroic struggle with emphysema. He was a graduate of NYU Dental School, Class of 1943. He is survived by his beloved wife of 51 years, Alice. He was the proud father of Dennis (Susan), Sheryl (Glenn Corwin) and Barry, and devoted Poppy to Andrea (Michael Firestone), Evan, and Sarah and Jared Corwin. Funeral at Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY, Friday, October 7, at 12:30 PM. Shiva at the Corwin residence Saturday night through Wednesday.
