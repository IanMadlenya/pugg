
        GINZBERG-Dr. Eli. ORT Boards of Directors mourn the passing of Dr. Eli Ginzberg, Columbia University professor and Chairman of the World ORT Academic Advisory Council. Eli was an adviser to eight U.S. presidents and a leader in the field of economic and social rights of children. His death is a great loss to the academic and Jewish communities.
