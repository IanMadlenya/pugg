
        BRANDRUP-Clair L. Passed away peacefully at his home in San Francisco, CA on August 13, 2001 at the age of 86 after a long and fulfilling life. Loving father of Douglas W. Brandrup of Stamford, CT and Judith Claire Kerwin of San Francisco, CA. Devoted and loving grandfather to Kendra Claire Brandrup of NYC, Monika E. Hauge of Stony Creek, CT, Peter W. Brandrup of Ft. Lauderdale, FL, and Shannon Claire Kerwin of San Francisco, CA. He was the adored father-in-law of Patricia R. Brandrup of Stamford, CT and the beloved step grandfather of Phillip R. Corryn of New Canaan, CT and Barclay F. Tuck of NYC. A memorial service will be celebrated Friday, August 24, 2:00 PM at the First Unitarian Church, 1187 Franklin St., San Francisco, CA. Memorial contributions may be made to Save the Sound, Inc., 185 Magee Ave., Stamford, CT 06902 or to the San Francisco School, 300 Gaven St., San Francisco, CA 94134.
