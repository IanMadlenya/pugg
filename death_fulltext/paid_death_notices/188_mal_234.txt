
        SANDLER - Lewis N. Of South Orange, co-founder of the Sandler & Worth, Inc., retail carpet chain, died Saturday at the Inglemoor Care Center of Livingston. He was 82. A funeral service will be held today at 12:30 PM, in the Bernheim - Apter - Kreitzman Suburban Funeral Chapel, 68 Old Short Hills Rd, Livingston. Pre-deceased by a daughter, Janet, he is survived by his wife, Florence; two daughters, Emily Anthony and Amy Sandler Levin; a son, David; a brother, Richard; and seven grandchildren.
