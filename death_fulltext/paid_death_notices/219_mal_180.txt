
        SOKOLOFF--Karel. Of ''The Greatest Generation,'' a Brooklyn College grad, WWII Army Vet, died April 12. Beloved husband of Florence, devoted father of Elisabeth, Madelon, and Natalie, grandchildren Nicholas, Erica, Gabriel, Harry and Loughlin, and two great granddaughters. President of Prospect Industries. Loved tennis, literature, conversation, The Times crossword. He had a remarkable marriage and beautiful soul. We love you and will always have you in our hearts.
