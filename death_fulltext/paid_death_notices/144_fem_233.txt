
        KAUFMANN-Kathleen Fennelly died suddenly on January 19th in her home in Burlington, Mass. She had been a longtime resident of Manhasset and Tuckahoe, New York. She was preceded in death by her husband Richard Kaufmann as well as her sisters Sally Lorenz, Anne Burns, brothers Martin, William & James Fennelly. She is survived by her sister Margie Grace and many nieces and nephews. In repose at The Fairchild Sons Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset on Friday from 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass on Saturday at 9:45 AM, at St. Mary's R.C. Church. Interment Holy Mount Cemetery, Tuckahoe, New York.
