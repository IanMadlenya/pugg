
        KAUFMAN-Vickie, 72, of Alcoa, TN, died at 3:50 AM, Saturday, August 7, 1999, at Blount Memorial Hospital, Maryville, Tenn. Preceeded in death by her beloved husband, Ralph Kaufman. She is survived by her sons Michael Kaufman of Cherry Hill, NJ, Steve Kaufman of Alcoa, TN, and Will Kaufman of Hebden Bridge, England; her sister, Amelia Seril of New York; and six grandchildren. Services will be held Friday, August 20, at King Solomon Memorial Park in Clifton, NJ. For more information, contact Smith Mortuary, Maryville, 423-983-1000.
