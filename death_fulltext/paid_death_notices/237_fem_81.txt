
        SCHNEIDER--Charlotte Brolat, M.D. 97, October 8, 2006. Beloved mother of Martin Schneider and Marion Brown, grandmother of Nicholas and Deborah Brown and Monica and Leo Schneider. A 1935 graduate of the University of Berlin, she married Louis Schneider, M.D., a 60 year partnership, and practiced medicine until 1984 in East Rockaway, NY. Memorial on October 14th, 2pm, at The Osborn, Rye, NY. www.theosborn.org
