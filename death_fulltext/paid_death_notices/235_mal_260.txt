
        MALARA--Anthony C. In recognition of his many years of service as a Director and Member of the Executive Committee, the Broadcasters Foundation of America has announced that special Memorial Tributes to our beloved friend and esteemed colleague Tony Malara may be made to the Endowment Fund in his honor and loving memory. Anthony C. Malara Memorial, Endowment Fund, Broadcasters Foundation of America, 7 Lincoln Ave., Greenwich, CT. 06830. Philip Lombardo, Chairman Gordon Hastings, President
