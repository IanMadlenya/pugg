
        SAFFER-Sylvia. 82 of Pompano Beach, FL. Passed away on August 9, 1998. She is survived by her loving children: Sheila Bloom and her fiance Gil Rosenberg, and Arlene Greenstein and her husband Alan. She is also survived by 11 cherished grandchildren, 16 greatgrandchildren, beloved sister Muriel Anzman and devoted companion Sid Liebowitz. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, August 12, 11:30 AM, at Schwartz Bros., 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, NY, 718-793-3883, with interment to follow at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
