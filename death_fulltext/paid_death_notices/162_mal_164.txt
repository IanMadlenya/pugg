
        COCHRAN-Leon Khay. Son of Delores Cochran and Prince Bagration de Moucriani. Singer, former Wall Street broker, Farmer, collector of Russian art and many other areas, toured the world in the Don Cossack Choir, performed in summer stock, and in Russian theater in Manhattan for 40 years, a person of many talents, died July 11, at the age of 73, in the tragic explosion of his home in Boerum Hill in Brooklyn. A funeral and memorial service will be held Monday, July 17, 7 PM, at Lafayette Avenue Presbyterian Church at 85 South Oxford St., Brooklyn. He will be buried in his hometown, of Louisiana, Missouri.
