
        GREEN-Muriel Busch. of Stamford, CT, formerly of Jericho, NY, died of pneumonia November 14, 2000 at the age of seventy-seven. Mrs. Green leaves a loving family: two sons and their wives, Peter and Carin, of Port Chester, NY and Roger and Enid, of Newtown, PA, a brother and sister-in-law, Bob and Renee Busch of Fair Lawn, NJ, a sister-in-law, Sylvia Fleischmann of Delray Beach, FL, one nephew, two nieces, four grandchildren and many loving friends and extended family members. We all remember her courage, tenacity, warmth, commitment to family, intellect, grace and generosity, and we all will miss her terribly. A memorial service is being planned for Sunday, November 26 at the home of her son, Peter, in Port Chester, NY. The family requests that any donations be made in her name to the Arthritis Foundation.
