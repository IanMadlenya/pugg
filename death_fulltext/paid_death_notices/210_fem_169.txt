
        RAMSING--Martha Wrightson. Of natural causes, at home on July 28. Daughter of the late Josephine and William G. Wrightson. She was the loving wife of Thor H. Bornhorst, Christopher C. Zoubek and Krista E. Palmgren, and great grandmother of Nicholas W. Zoubek. Contributions may be made to the Vineyard Conservation Society, PO Box 2189, Vineyard Haven, MA 02568.
