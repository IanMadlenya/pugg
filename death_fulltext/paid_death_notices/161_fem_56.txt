
        DEL TREDICI-Helen. Died May 19, 2000 of leukemia and cancer. Beloved mother of five children, Pulitzer Prize-winning composer David Del Tredici of New York City, Robert Del Tredici of Montreal, Canada, Richard Del Tredici of Pleasanton, Calif, Peter Del Tredici of Watertown, Mass, and Ann Del Tredici of El Sobrante, Calif; and six grandchildren. Her husband, Walter, died earlier. As well as a housewife, Helen was a school teacher, tutor and gardener with a bachelor's degree in German from the University of Calif at Berkeley. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made in Mrs. Del Tredici's name to Hospice of Marin, 150 Nellen Ave., Corte Madera, CA 94925
