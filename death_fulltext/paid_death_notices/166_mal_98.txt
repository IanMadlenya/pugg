
        COHEN-Rabbi Herschel. Manhattan Day School/Yeshiva Ohr Torah mourns the passing of Rabbi Herschel Cohen, Associate Rabbi of Lincoln Square Synagogue. Committed to his family, his congregation, his community and his Yeshiva, he will be missed by all. We extend our condolences to his wife Shulie, his mother Frumie, his children Yehuda 1987 and Lisa, Devora 1989 and Menachem, and his brother Sidney. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Mordechai Besser, Principal George Karasick, President Elliot Gibber, Chairman of the Board
