
        MEADOW-Virginia R. On August 30, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Franklin Meadow. Loving mother of Dorothy Meadow Sobol and Carolyn Meadow Shechtman. Devoted grandmother of Emma, Julia and Edward Sobol and Jonathan Shechtman. Dear sister of Mildred Bellew Leonard. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, 3:30 PM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Saybrook College, Yale University, Virginia R. Meadow Fund.
