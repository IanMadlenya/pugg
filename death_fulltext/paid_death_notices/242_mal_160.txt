
        HEINZE--Frank J. 70, longterm resident of Ramsey, NJ and Westhampton Beach, NY died on March 15, 2007. He was born in Bronxville, NY in 1936, was educated at Iona Prep, the University of Notre Dame (class of 1958) as a chemical engineer, and Columbia University as a Master of Chemical Engineering. He was a chemical process engineer with American Cyanamid Company, Stauffer Chemical Company, and American Home Products Corporation. He was a lifetime member of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers and greatly enjoyed his hobbies of deep-sea fishing and train-spotting. He is survived by his wife of 45 years, Carol O'Connell Heinze, his son John and daughter-in-law Joy De Vries Heinze, his daughter Martha and son-in-law Paul Hansen, his son Mark and daughter-inlaw Claudia Villegas, his son Neal and daughter-in-law Ellen Lafontaine Heinze and his beloved grandchildren Justin, Emma, William, and Caroline Heinze. Visitation will be held on Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 pm at the Van Emburgh-SneiderPernice Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Ave, Ramsey, NJ. A Funeral Service will be held on Monday March 19, 2007 at 10am at the funeral home. Interment will follow at Westhampton Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to Heifer International or the University of Notre Dame.
