
        RUTHERFURD -- Georgette Whelan. Died February 14 in Manhattan. Beloved wife of 66 years to Guy G. Rutherfurd. Mother of Guy Jr., Leith R. Talamo, Alec, and Morton, who predeceased her. Dear sister of Sidney S. Whelan Jr. Grandmother of 12. Lifelong artist. Friends may call at F. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., Thursday, February 19, 4-8 PM. Funeral service at St. Thomas More Church, 65 East 89th Street, Friday, February 20, 11 AM. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations can be sent to the Society of Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center.
