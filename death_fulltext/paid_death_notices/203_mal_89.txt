
        MANDEL--Elliot J., age 54 of Stamford, Connecticut, died on December 3, 2003. Mr. Mandel was a Shareholder in the law firm of Epstein Becker & Green, P.C. in New York. He leaves his devoted wife, Kathryn Mandel and two devoted children, Sarah Mandel and Joshua Mandel, and his mother, Sylvia Mandel. Funeral services were held December 5. A trust fund has been established for his two children. Donations may be made to ''Kathryn Mandel, Trustee of The Sarah Mandel and Joshua Mandel Trust'' and may be mailed to Epstein Becker & Green, 250 Park Avenue, NY, NY 10177.
