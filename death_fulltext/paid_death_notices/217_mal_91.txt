
        FLYNN--Thomas C. Age 87, of Beechhurst, NY, on February 5, 2005. Loving father of Ann Marie, Thomas, Regina and Maryellen Flynn-Coffey. Devoted father-in-law of Susan Flynn and Joseph Coffey. Beloved grandfather of Marielle Foy, Caitlin Ashlie and Jillian Marie Coffey and Madeline Elizabeth Flynn. Loving brother of the late John and James Flynn. Devoted son of the late Michael and Anna Rowland Flynn. Predeceased by Mary Ellen Foy Flynn. An ardent National League/Giants/Mets fan, he spent 20 years with the New York City Police Department and retired from Union Carbide. He served in WWII and the Korean War, for which he received the Bronze Star. He retired from the U.S. Army Reserves as a Lieutenant Colonel after 25 years. He loved to travel and visited all 50 states and five continents, but surrendered his passport once his granddaughters arrived. Reposing at Edward D. Lynch Funeral Home, 43-07 Queens Blvd, Sunnyside, NY, Monday and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9:30 pm. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 11 am, at St. Luke's RC Church, 16-34 Clintonville Street, Whitestone, NY. Interment Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the Dominican Academy Board of Trustees Scholarship, 44 E. 68th St, NYC 10021.
