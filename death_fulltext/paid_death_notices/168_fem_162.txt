
        MILLER-Joan Heller. Daughter of Ascher and Rosalie Heller, died January 13, 2001. An NYU graduate, she served as President of New Jersey's LWV. Survivors include two sons, their families, aunt, cousins, nieces, nephews, brother, Charles Heller of Bayonne, sister, Betty Heller Rosania (Edward) of Wethersfield, CT.
