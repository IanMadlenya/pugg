
        WEINSTOCK-Charles, MD. The William Alanson White Psychoanalytic Institute and the William Alanson White Psychoanalytic Society note with profound sorrow the death of an Institute graduate, a Society member, a colleague and friend. Dr. Weinstock's earnest and assiduous endeavors towards the well-being of the Institute and the Society touched many hearts. His gentle presence will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wonderful family. Marylou Lionells, PhD, Director, White Institute Allison Stern Rosen, PhD, President, White Society
