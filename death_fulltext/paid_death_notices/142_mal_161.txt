
        STRASSFELD-Frank E. It is with profound sorrow that the Hollis Hills Jewish Center announces the passing of our beloved ''Shames'' Rev. Frank E. Strassfeld, at the age of 75. For 49 extraordinary years, Frank has served our community with devotion, sincerity and passion. He was and always will be in our hearts and in our souls. Funeral services will take place Friday morning, November 13, 1998, at 9:30AM, in our Center Sanctuary, 210-10 Union Turnpike, Hollis Hills, Queens, New York. Rabbi Doctor H. Joseph Simkes Cantor Sol Zim President Michael B. Wolkofsky
