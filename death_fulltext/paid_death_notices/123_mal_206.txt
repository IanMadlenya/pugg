
        KNEEN-Harold Fitch, 94, of Guilford, CT., died on April 16, 1997. Survivors are his wife, Mary Banks Kneen, a daughter, Carolyn Evans Rawson & a son, Brewster, five grandchildren and six great grandchildren, a stepdaughter Mary B. Calhoun and a stepson William M. Foord, III. Services will be held April 25, 2 PM at the First Congregational Church, Guilford, CT., and a reception afterwards at the Kneen residence.
