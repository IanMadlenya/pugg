
        POTTER-Hamilton Fish, Jr. The Chapin School is deeply saddened by the death of our former Board member, Hamilton Fish Potter, Jr., who served on the Board of Trustees from 1975-1981 with dedication and distinction. His wise counsel and loyalty to the school will be sorely missed. Our deepest sympathy goes to his family. Cera B. Robbins, President Board of Trustees Mary Gordon Roberts, Chairman Board of Trustees
