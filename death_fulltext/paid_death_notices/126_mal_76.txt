
        LAWSON-Neil S. Died June 30, 1997, at Sarasota Memorial Hospital in Sarasota. FL. He was 84. Born in Bremerton, WA., he lived in Florida for ten years. Prior to that in lived in Morris Plains, NJ for 35 years. Mr. Lawson was the owner and president of Olsen and Lawson Construction Co. in Dover, N.J. He was a member of St. John's Masonic Lodge #
