
        WEIDMAN--Peggy, 91, died peacefully in her sleep on June 3, 2005. Gracious, charming, elegant, and witty, she is survived by her sons, John and Jeff, her grandchildren, Jonathan and Laura, her daughter-in-law, Lila, and her best and oldest friend Jane, all of whom appreciated her, enjoyed her, and loved her very much. A Memorial Service will be held at All Souls Church in September on a date to be announced.
