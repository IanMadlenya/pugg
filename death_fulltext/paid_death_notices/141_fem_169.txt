
        WOOD-Dorothy F. Age 85, formerly of Manhattan, died October 16, 1998, in Middletown, NY, after a long illness. Born in Middletown, NY, November 3, 1912. She is survived by her sister Elizabeth Pippin of Old Greenwich, CT, her niece Elizabeth Leahey of Southport, CT, her nephew Curtis Pippin of Norwalk, CT, and her grandnephew Cosden Leahey. Graveside services will be held on October 23 at 11 AM at Hillside Cemetery, Middletown, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Wellesley Collge, Wellesley, MA 02181, or one's own selection.
