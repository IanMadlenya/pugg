
        TAI-Ping Y. The American Heart Association in New York City mourns the loss of Mrs. Ping Y. Tai. Mrs. Tai and her family have enhanced the fight against heart disease and stroke through their longtime generosity and sponsorship of research and young medical scientists. We extend our warm condolences to Yuan Tai and members of the Tai family. Gayllis R. Ward, Esq., CPA, Chairman of the Board Diane M. Sixsmith, M.D., President Michael L. Weamer, Executive VP
