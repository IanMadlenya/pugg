
        HIRSCH--Charlotte. Age 87 of Yonkers, NY, died peacefully at home on April 22, 2005. Devoted wife of 54 years to the late Sol Hirsch. Loving and cherished mother of Gerald Hirsch, Cecelia Hirsch Gedinsky and Lawrence Hirsch and daughter-in-law Susan Zimmerman Hirsch. Adored and revered grandmother of Ilyse, Kelly, Ari, Stephany, Emily, David, Hillary and Howard. This beautiful woman of unyielding courage, strength and devotion forever leaves a void in our hearts only to be filled with the rich memories of her laughter, joy, compassion and unconditional love. Services to be at Riverside Chapel, NYC.
