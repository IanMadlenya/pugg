
        GOODMAN--Gerald, resident of Heritage Village and formerly of Hewlett Bay Park, Long Island, died on January 21st. He was president of Ben Goodman & Son in New York City and was a dedicated volunteer for many years of the Woodmere Fire Department. Gerald was predeceased by his two sons Michael and Randy and two brothers Maurice and Howard Goodman. He leaves his wife Joyce, son Todd, nieces and nephews. Burial and services will be at New Montifore Cemetery in Farmingdale, Long Island at 1:00pm on January 25th.
