
        FRENKEL--Claudia. 96, died April 14, 2005. Wife of the late Lester and beloved mother of the late Charles and Enid. She is survived by three grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Former President of the Women's Auxiliary at The Manhattan Eye, Ear and Throat Hospital. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., Monday, 9:30AM. Entombment at Washington Cemetery, Brooklyn, N.Y.
