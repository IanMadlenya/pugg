
        RUSSELL -- Wendy Vander Poel. The Board of Directors, Medical Advisory Board and staff of The Eye-Bank for Sight Restoration wish to express our sincere condolences to the family of Wendy Vander Poel Russell, our Honorary Director who served on the Board of our organization for 27 years and as President from 1981 to 1988. She imbued strength and optimism and through her generous support of eye donation, helped to save thousands of men, women and children from blindness. Her contributions to society and her wonderful spirit will be missed. The Eye-Bank for Sight Restoration, Inc.
