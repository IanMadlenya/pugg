
        HUTCHINGS--Heather MacMillan. 52, of Deerfield Beach, FL, passed away suddenly at her home on Sunday, November 20th, 2005. Beloved daughter of Charles Stewart MacMillan and Elizabeth Ann Davin, she was born July 26, 1953, in New York City, NY. She was a member of St. Gregory's Episcopal Church, Boca Raton, FL. She attended Michigan State University. She had a long career in advertising and retailing, most recently, the last ten years, for Talbot's in Boca Raton. She was a wonderful friend, a generous and devoted aunt, loyal and loving sister who touched many lives, and she will be truly missed by all who knew her, especially her family and friends in the United Kingdom and United States. She is survived by her husband, Paul E. Hutchings; sisters Bernadette Dale Stewart Burner MacMillan, Geneva, Switzerland, and Adele Stewart MacMillan of East Hampton, NY; nieces Alixandra MacMillan Fiedel of Geneva, Switzerland, and Elizabeth Ann Stewart Meyers of East Hampton, NY; nephew Scott Stewart Meyers of East Hampton, NY; stepmother Marianna Maleeva MacMillan of Geneva, Switzerland, and Sofia, Bulgaria; a stepson, Todd Hutchings of Doylestown, PA; a stepdaughter, Amy Hutchings Gursky of Honeybrook, PA. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, December 1, 2005, 10:30 AM, at St. Gregory's Episcopal Church, 100 NE Mizner Blvd, Boca Raton, FL. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation. For information, Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, Delray Beach, FL.
