
        SCHUUR--Robert G., of New York, NY, died on March 3, 2007, surrounded by his family. Born in Kalamazoo, MI, Bob was a graduate of the University of Michigan Law School and a veteran of the United States Navy. A New Yorker for over 50 years, Bob was a partner at Reid & Priest and one of the city's most enthusiastic walkers. He is survived by his wife Susan, children Arah and Jeremiah, daughter - in - law Lauraine, grandson Henry, brother Jerry and nephews Peter, Ted and Robert. In lieu of flowers, please consider contributions to the Central Park Conservancy, a cause close to Bob's heart. Bob will be remembered at a memorial service at 11 am on Saturday, March 24, at the University Club, One West 54th Street.
