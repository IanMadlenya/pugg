
        SHAPIRO--Albert (Ozzie), of Forest Hills, NY. July 15, 1917 December 18, 2003. He is survived by Gertrude, his beloved wife of 62 years, daughters, Elaine Stimmel and Diane Saatchi and sons-inlaw Allan Stimmel and David Saatchi, grandsons Lee Stimmel (Ilana) and Jonathan Stimmel (Kristine) and three great grandchildren. He was a very important man and will be greatly missed.
