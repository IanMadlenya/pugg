
        WAGNER-Marvin J. Of West Palm Beach, Florida passed away on December 11, 1998. He was the beloved husband of Elizabeth Wagner and the loving father of the late Caryl Grutman, Roger (Rona) Wagner, and Howard Wagner. He also leaves behind his cherished grandchildren, Teena, Gordon, and Benson. Marvin J. Wagner was a lifetime member of A.I.C.P.A. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407. For information Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels, West Palm Beach, Florida 1-800-94-SHALOM.
