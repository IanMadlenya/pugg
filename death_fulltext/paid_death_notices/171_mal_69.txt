
        EPSTEIN-E. Jerry, 62, of New York City and Monterey, Massachusetts died on April 3, 2001. He is survived by his loving family, wife Laurily, son Gregory, daughter Deborah, daughter-in-law Naomi Baigell, son-in-law Lisandro Varela, and grandchildren Tali & Soyer Epstein. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell the Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street, on Friday, April 6, at 1:30 PM. His family and friends will miss him. In lieu of flowers, donations in Mr. Epstein's memory can be made to The Actors Company Theatre, 161 6th Ave., 14th. Floor, N Y, NY 10013.
