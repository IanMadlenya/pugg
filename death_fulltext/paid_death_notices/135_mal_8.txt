
        ROBESON-David Paul. On March 27, 1998, age 46. Son of Marilyn and Paul Robeson, Jr., brother of Susan, nephew of Harold Graham, grandson of the late Paul Robeson. Folklorist, performance artist and director of Ancestral Spirits, a performance group of stilt dancers in the African-Caribbean tradition. He spent a major portion of his life in researching and preserving the cultural tradition of the African diaspora. Services April 4th, 11 A.M. at Mother A.M.E. Zion Church, 140 West 137th Street.
