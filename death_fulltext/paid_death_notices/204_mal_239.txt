
        MARINO -- James Vincent. Passed on January 15, 2004. Born October 23, 1907 in Brooklyn, NY. Preceded in death by his parents, Frank and Jenny Pendula Marino, and his wife, Rose Cacioppo. Dearly missed by his son, Jim Marino, and granddaughter, Kelly. James was a Harvard Dental School grad who practiced dentistry on Wyckoff Ave., Brooklyn, retiring in 1980. He was an officer in Army Air Corps during WWII. Viewing is Tuesday, 10-2, at the Daniel George & Son Funeral Home, 1852 Bath Ave., Brooklyn. Interment will be at St. John's Cemetery Cloister Chapel, 18-01 Metropolitan Ave., Middle Village.
