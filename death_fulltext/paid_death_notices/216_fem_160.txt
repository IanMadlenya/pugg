
        TELSEY -- Dr. Jacqueline Messite. Died January 14, 2005, at age 79. An innovator in public health. She received an M.D. from Long Island College of Medicine in 1949. A Diplomate of the American Board of Preventative Medicine, she received numerous awards, including the Exceptional Service Award from the New York Academy of Medicine. Wife of the late Dr. Daubert Telsey. Beloved mother of Dr. Aimee Telsey and Suzanne Telsey, and grandmother of Danielle and Nicole, and Emma and Isabel.
