
        ASTROVE-Bertram. Age 78, of Wellington, passed away March 25, 1999. He moved to South Florida from White Plains, NY, and was a retired decorated World War II pilot. He is predeceased by his loving wife, Anita. Mr. Astrove is survived by his sons, Dr. Andrew Astrove of Boca Raton and Skip Astrove of Banks, Oregon; two brothers, Alan and Edgar, and six grandchildren. The family will have services in New York at a later date.
