
        BLUM--Henry ''Joe''. Died March 26, 2005. He is survived by his beloved wife of sixtyfour years, Mary, his children, Betty Kleinberg (Les) of NJ, and Linda Goldfinch (Brian) of FL, and his grandchildren, Bennett Kleinberg (Julie) of CT, and Sara Kleinberg of NYC. Joe was a proofreader for the New York Times until his retirement in 1978. He was an avid duplicate bridge player and earned the designation of ''Life Master''. Graveside services will be held on Wednesday, March 30th at 11:30AM at Beth Moses Cemetery in Pinelawn, LI.
