
        VAADIA-Yoash. The Hebrew University of Jerusalem and the American Friends of the Hebrew University mourn with great sadness the passing of Professor Yoash Vaadia, former Vice-President and Director-General of the University and a Founder of the US-Israel Binational Agricultural Research and Development Fund. Professor Vaadia served the University with distinction providing wisdom and leadership throughout his career. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his wife, Professor Deborah Delmer, his children and the entire family. The Hebrew University of of Jerusalem Menachem Magidor, President Alex Grass, Chairman of the Board The American Friends of The Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs, President Lawrence Newman, Ch. of Board Adam B. Kahan, Executive VP
