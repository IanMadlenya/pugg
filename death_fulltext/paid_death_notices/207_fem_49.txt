
        MCINTOSH--Mary (nee Donovan). 70, of New York, New York, on April 5, 2004. Mary is survived by her loving husband Jack, her sons, George A. Pandaleone, and Thomas E. Pandaleone, their wives, and four grandchildren. Mary retired as Assistant Dean for Community Services at Westchester Community College, White Plains, NY, in 1985. She also worked at Queensboro Community College, Queens, NY, and in The Chappaqua New York Public School system. She was an alumna of Smith College and Bank Street College of Education. Mary loved New York City, the arts, travel, and her wide circle of friends. She will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved her. Contributions in her memory may be made to: Dr. Richard Barakat's Ovarian Cancer Research at Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021. A Memorial Service will be held at 2 P.M. on Saturday May 8, 2004 at Trinity Baptist Church, 250 East 61 Street, New York, NY.
