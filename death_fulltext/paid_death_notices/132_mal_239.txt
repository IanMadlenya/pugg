
        CLARK-Cameron. January 15, 1998. Beloved husband of Sheila. Father of Eliza and Simon. Brother of Timothy and Ian Clark. Son of Blair Clark and the late Jessie Holladay Philbin James. Memorial service, Tuesday, January 20, 10:30 AM at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church at 73 St., NYC.
        CLARK-Cameron. The partners, associates and staff of the firm of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison are profoundly saddened by the passing of Cameron Clark, our partner and friend for nearly 30 years. His strong commitment to public service as well as to his many clients was evident in his leadership both within the firm and in the legal community at large. We express our deepest sympathy to his wife, Sheila, his children, Elizabeth and Simon, his father, Blair, and brothers, Tim and Ian, and their families.
