
        STEINBERG-Lionel S. The Officers, Board of Directors and Staff of the American Associates, BenGurion University of the Negev mourn the passing of our dear friend and Board of Chancellor Member, Lionel Steinberg of Palm Springs, California. Lionel was a successful farmer, philanthropist and political activist. We extend heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife of 25 years Katrina, and their children Billy, Barbara and Mary and grandchildren Janine and Ezra. Jules I. Whitman, President Kenneth L. Tucker, Chairman Robert H. Arnow, Chairman Emeritus, BGU Board of Governors
