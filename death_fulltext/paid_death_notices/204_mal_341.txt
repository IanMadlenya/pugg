
        QUILTY--Arthur M. Died on January 23, 2004 at Easton Hospital in Easton, PA. Born in Boston, MA, he served as a lieutenant in the U.S. Navy from 1943-1946. He graduated from Yale in 1948 where he received a BA in English Literature. In 1950 he graduated from Harvard Business School where he received his MBA. He served as a member of the Board of Directors and the Executive Committee of Johnson & Johnson in New Brunswick, NJ, from 1972 until his retirement in 1989. Survivors include his wife of 48 years, Jean M. Quilty of New Vernon, NJ; his daughter Ellen E. Quilty of Basking Ridge, NJ, and two sons, Michael J. Quilty of Basking Ridge, NJ, and Matthew R. Quilty of Upper Makefield, PA; three grandchildren Taylor Quilty, Roxanne Quilty and Mason Quilty. Graveside services will be held on Wednesday, January 28, 2004, at 1 pm, at Somerset Hills Memorial Park Cemetery. For more information, Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home, 101 S. Finley Avenue, Basking Ridge, NJ; 908766-0250.
