
        PARNES-Aaron. 86. On October 27, 2001. Beloved husband of Martha. Survived by his niece Carole Parnes-Kaufman; his cousins Irving Parnes, Eleanor Silver, Judy Winters; sister-in-law Alice Parnes; and loving friend Arnold Brown. Mr. Parnes was founder of Parnes Feinstein of NYC. Original Trustee and Vice President of The Garment Center Congregation; a U.S. Army Veteran of WWII and Purple Heart recipient. Services will be 11:30 Monday, October 29th at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
