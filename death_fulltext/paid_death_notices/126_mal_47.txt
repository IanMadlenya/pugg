
        ROMANELLI-Umberto, 89, on June 30. A graveside service was held on July 2 at Sharon Gardens Cemetery in Valhalla, NY. Mr. Romanelli is survived by his wife of 58 years, Nedda of New York City; his daughter, Rosanna Frank of Chappaqua, NY; two sons, Joseph of Jerusalem, Israel, and Daniel of Los Angeles; seven grandchildren and a great-grandchild. Donations in his memory are being accepted by The Jewish National Fund at 42 East 69th St., New York, NY 10021
