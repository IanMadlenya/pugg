
        FREEDMAN-Beatrice S. Born June 20, 1909 to Jennie and Louis Sheiman. Died on June 16, 2001. Loving wife of the late Jerome. Adored mother of Lee, Vivienne, and Ruth. Devoted grandmother of Jill, Bruce, Andrew, Georgia, David, and Leah. Cherished great grandmother of Ryan, Erica, Alexander, Jared, Isabel, and Jalynn. Loving sister, sister-in-law, and aunt. She devoted her life to the achievement of peace and social justice. Funeral services 10:30 A.M. June 20, 2001, Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, NY.
