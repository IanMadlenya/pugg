
        COLE-Paul C., 86, of Merriam, KS, died December 13, 1999. Funeral services will be at 10 AM Dec. 20, 1999 at St. Michael and All Angels Episcopal Church, Mission, KS. Paul was born in 1913 in Brooklyn, NY. Paul worked for R.H. Macy & Co., 1933-1993. He was vice president and director in Missouri and Kansas, 1950-1965; senior vice president in New York, 1965-1978; and consultant, 1978-1993. He was a former member of the Church of the Transfiguration. He served as Grand Marshall for the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade in 1948 and 1949. He was preceded in death by his wife, Florence. Survivors include his son, Garrett Cole, two granddaughters and one great-grandson.
