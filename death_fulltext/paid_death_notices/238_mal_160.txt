
        PATTERSON--Dr. Andrew H. (Drew). Of Bronxville, passed away on November 11, 2006 at Roosevelt Hospital in New York City. He was 74. Beloved husband of Pat, father of Jim (Rachel), Hugh and Andrew (Jennifer). Grandfather of five. Brother and uncle. Memorial service Saturday, November 18, at 2 PM, at the Reformed Church, Bronxville. Memorial gifts to the Natural History Museum of the Adirondacks, 45 Museum Drive, Tupper Lake, NY 12986.
