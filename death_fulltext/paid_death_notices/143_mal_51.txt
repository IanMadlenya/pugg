
        SIMON-Pierre F. December 2, 1998. Beloved husband of Jacqueline Albert. Loving father of Lisette and her husband Paul Jacobs, Orville Simon and his wife Stephanie. Adoring grandfather of Alexia and Caroline Jacobs. Mr. Simon was an internationally known industrialist in the electronics and aircraft industries. A memorial service will announced at a later date.
        SIMON-Pierre. The Officers and Directors of the Children's Blood Foundation mourn the loss of their fellow Director, Pierre Simon. Mr. Simon had been a founding member and longtime benefactor of CBF. We wish to extend our sincerest sympathy to his wife Jackie and their family. John Calicchio, Chairman Margaret Hilgartner, M.D., Pres. Lawrence Bass, V.P. Susan Byrne, Executive Director
