
        ZWECK - BRONNER - Marilyn Anne. Age 62, a resident of Vero Beach, Florida and summer resident of Dorset, Vermont for the past 11 years, died at the Dartmouth-Hitchcock Medical Center in Lebanon, NH following a courageous battle with cancer. Mrs. Zweck-Bronner was born in Westchester County, New York the daughter of Philip and Maryl (Browning). Lochner. Marilyn made her debut in 1955 at the Westchester Catillion. She received her early education at Greenwich Academy and continued at Wheaton College in Boston and Manhattanville College in Purchase, NY. Marilyn graduated with a master's degree from Loyola College in Baltimore, Maryland. She married Stanley ZweckBronner January 3, 1960 in Rye, NY. She is survived by her husband and her two sons, Michael and Stephen. A funeral mass will be held Friday June 23, at 1:00 pm at Saint Paul's Church in Manhchester, VT. For further information please contact the Brewster Funeral Service in Manchester Center, VT.
