
        KAIN-Arthur B. Age 84, of Lake Worth, FL, formerly of Lynbrook, NY. Beloved husband of Esther for 60 years. Brother of Norman, devoted father of Stephen, Karen and Steven Bachmann, Janet and Edward Greenberg, adored grandfather of Seth Kain, Jill Karp, Jennifer Sweetwood, Michael Bachmann, Amy Bachmann, and Jonathan Greenberg. Loving greatgrandfather of Alexander Kain. He was a graduate of NYU, Class of 1935, a dedicated and respected CPA, past-president of Congregation Beth David, Lynbrook, NY, a kind and gentle man, he will be missed by all. Services Thursday, 12:00 noon at Gutterman's, 175 North Longbeach Road, Rockville Centre, New York. In lieu of flowers, family requests contributions to your favorite charity.
