
        MERRIFIELD-Philip Ralph, Ph.D. Born in Abilene, KS on April 12, 1921. Died June 14, 1998 in New York City. Professor Emeritus at New York University, Department of Educational Psychology. Beloved husband of Ora. Also survived by Kathryn Merrifield and their beloved daughters Karla and Signe. His dedication & generosity was an inspiration to his many friends, colleagues and students. He will be greatly missed. A memorial services will be held Wednesday, June 17, 4PM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street, NYC.
