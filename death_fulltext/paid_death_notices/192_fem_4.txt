
        O'KEEFE-Sr. Maureen, Little Sisters of the Assumption. On December 29. Entered the Order in 1953. A lifelong dedication to the sick, poor and needy as a caretaker in the home. She obtained an MSW from Fordham and became skilled in law & government to better serve those without access to either in hopes to better their lives. She has touched many hearts. Remembrances to: Little Sisters of the Assumption, 100 Gladstone Avenue, Walden, New York 12586.
