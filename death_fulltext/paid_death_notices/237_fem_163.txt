
        ORLOFF -- Mollie (Schloss berg). Passed away peacefully in her 94th year on October 21, 2006. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Buddy (Herman) Orloff. Loving mother of Audrey and Dr. Henry Pollak and Jay and Bonni Orloff. Adored grandmother of Deborah Orloff, Janet and Jeffrey Pollak, Robin and Michael Halperin, Michele and Seth Pollak, Tracy and Marc Orloff and David Orloff and her great-grandchildren Bari, Ethan, Justin, Samantha, Jack, Naomi, Grace and Alexander. She has left an indelible mark on every life she touched and was loved by all who knew her. We will miss her. Funeral services will be held at Weinstein Memorial Chapel in Yonkers, 1652 Central Park Avenue, NY 10710, on Monday, October 23, 1 PM.
