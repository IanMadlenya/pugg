
        MAUER-Irving, M.D., F.A.C.S. This notice is to advise of the untimely death of Dr. Mauer on June 9, 1999. He was a gifted, resourceful orthopedic surgeon. In lieu of an official notice from the Hospital for Joint Diseases where he trained and served, this is to alert his colleagues and friends of his passing. Murray E. Burton, M.D.
