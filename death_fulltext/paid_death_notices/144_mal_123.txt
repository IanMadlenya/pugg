
        KAVESH-Nathaniel., 89. Last of the six sons of Rose and Isaac. Dearest brother of Fannie K. Goldstein of Canton, MA, generous uncle and grand-uncle and great-grand-uncle of many who loved him like the children he never had. A wise man who graduated CCNY and served in World War II. His life was characterized by sage wit and he will always remain in our hearts. Services on Sunday 1:30PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street & Amsterdam Ave.
