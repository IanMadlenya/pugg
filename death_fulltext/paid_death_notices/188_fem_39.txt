
        McCLOSKEY-Elizabeth M. Of Point Lookout, LI. On September 4, 2002. Born May 8, 1912, to John F. McCloskey and Elizabeth R. Mulhern. Ms. McCloskey was a graduate of Mount St. Vincent in Riverdale, NY, and took her law degree at St. John's University in Queens. She was President and owner of Seal Lock Burial Vault, Inc. in Forest Hills, NY. Long and charitable friend and fund raiser for the Sisters of St. Joseph Convent in Brentwood, LI, member of American Association University Women, Officer of Point Lookout Civic Association, Point Lookout Over 50 Club and many other professional organizations. She will be missed by many loving cousins and friends. Reposing after 2 PM, today, Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 603 Wantagh Avenue (Exit 28N SS Parkway), Wantagh. Funeral Mass Saturday, 8:30 AM, at Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal R.C. Church, Point Lookout, NY. Interment St. Jonh's Cemetery, Middle Village, New York. Family will receive friends Friday, 2 to 5 and 7 to 10 PM.
