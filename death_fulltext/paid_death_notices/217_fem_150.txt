
        SELUB -- Florence Barkan. Age 86, died Sunday, February 13th, 2005 in St. James, NY. Devoted sister of Eleanor Siegal and Irma Spivack. Cherished mother of Dr. Laura Selub-von Schmidt and Dr. Steven Selub, beloved grandmother of Justin and Joseph von Schmidt, and Rachel, Sam, Jonathan and Nathaniel Selub. Her boundless devotion to her family was the touchstone of her life. We will miss her.
