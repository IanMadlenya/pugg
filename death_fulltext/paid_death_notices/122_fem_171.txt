
        MAYPER-Rose. (nee Zide). Died peacefully on March 18th at Jacob Perlow Hospice at age 93. A graduate of NYU Law School, she was Controller at Golding Bros. A woman who challenged the limitations of her times. Wife to the late Samuel. Mother to Fannie Ginsberg and Marcia Mayper. Sister to Betty Ginsburg and Bell Bachrach. Grandmother to Norma and Judith Ginsberg.
