
        O'CONNOR-James Joseph, 81 years, on July 28th, 1997, of Manhasset, NY. Beloved husband of Jane. Devoted father of Patricia Brown, Susan Boyd, Catherine O'Connor, James J. O'Connor, Jr. and stepfather of Marylove, Jeffrey and Richard Thralls, Barbara Peck and Joan O'Brien. Adored ''Papa Jim'' to nineteen grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. A broadcast engineer with WOR Radio, Mutual Broadcasting and the BBC. He handled radio communications during the Gemini VI, Apollo and Skylab programs.
