
        DREIER--Sidney, 90. Beloved husband of Mildred for 60 years. Extremely loving father of Francine (Burtt), Marc, Mitchel (Nancy). Cherished grandfather of Julie, David, Spencer, Jackie, Katie and Benjamin. Born in Otynia, Poland in 1915, and educated in Bratislava, Czechoslovakia, to be a physician, he arrived in America in 1939, a captain in the Polish army, to represent his country at the World's Fair. WWII intervened and he remained in America, forging a career as an independent motion picture executive, becoming known and respected throughout the industry. Services will be held on Monday, June 18 at 2:15, Star of David Chapel, 7701 Bailey Road, Tamarac, Florida. Shiva will be observed at home in Boca Raton, Monday following the services, and Tuesday from 2-8 PM, and at his son, Marc's home at 150 East 57th Street, NYC, Wednesday, 6-9PM and Thursday 2-8PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL 33486.
