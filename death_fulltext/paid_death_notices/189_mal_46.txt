
        JOHNSON-James D. The Officers, Members, Executive Board and Staff of RWDSU/ UFCW Local 338 regret to announce the passing of retired Secretary-Treasurer, James D. Johnson, on October 2, 2002. Mr. Johnson was a member of Local 338 since 1937, and served in a variety of positions. Finally, in 1977, he became Secretary-Treasurer until his retirement in 2000. All those whose lives he touched mourn his passing. He was a fighter for the rights of the working man and an ardent believer in the labor movement and its responsiblity to the working men and women it served.
