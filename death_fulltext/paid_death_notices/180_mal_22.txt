
        WINTERBERG-Samuel Jay, 98. A Larchmont, NY resident for 59 years, died at his home on December 29, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Mary S. Winterberg, he is survived by his daughters Marla W. Frankel and Judie W. Cohen, four grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. A civil engineer, he was a graduate of The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art and remained a life member of both the Second Century Society and the Golden Legion. He was also a life member of the New York State Society of Professional Engineers and the American Society of Civil Engineers and the Moles. Notable career projects included the Throgs Neck Bridge and the Empire State Plaza in Albany. An avid sailor, he served The Beach Point Club in Mamaroneck, NY as a member of the Board of Governors from 1960 to 1975 and as its president from 1971-1975. A memorial service will be held on Friday, January 4, 2002 at noon at Congregation Emanu-el of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Avenue East, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art.
