
        BALLOCH-Anthony Edward, 81. Of Atlanta died January 13, 1998. Formerly of Wilton, CT. Survived by his wife Mary Chase Balloch; daughters Pat Tuff of Atlanta, Joy Ahrens of Yarmouth, ME; sons Howard Balloch of Beijing, China, Hugh Balloch, Darien, Connecticut; sixteen grandchildren. A memorial service for friends and family will be held in Jamestown, RI in the summer. For information H.M. Patterson & Son Funeral Home.
