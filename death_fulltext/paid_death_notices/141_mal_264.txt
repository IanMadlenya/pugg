
        LEFCOURT-Albert Dr., retired optometrist, died on October 21, 1998, in Hallandale, FL. He was 87 years young and is survived by his devoted wife of 40 years, Rachael, of Hallandale, his two sons, Robert and Gerald of NYC, his brother Harold, of Levittown, PA, his cherished nephew Ian Erlich of Oregon, & his adoring grandchildren, Jenny, Daniel, Jeffrey, and Karen of NYC. Memorial contributions in his memory to National Foundation of Cancer Research, 4600 East West Highway, Bethesda, MD 20814. Service Sunday, 11 A.M. at the ''Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
