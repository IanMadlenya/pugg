
        ZALMAN-Alexander, 83. Died on Saturday, May 4, 2002 in Tamarac, FL. He is survived by Freda, devoted wife of 55 years, brother Gilbert, sister Peggy, three sons, Richard, Joseph and Andrew, two daughters-in-law and seven grandchildren. A native of Brooklyn, NY, Alex was a highly decorated WWII veteran & an innovative inventor and manufacturer of products for his business, Electro-Optix. A memorial service will be held at Star of David at 11:45 A.M., on Tuesday, May 7th in Tamarac, FL.
