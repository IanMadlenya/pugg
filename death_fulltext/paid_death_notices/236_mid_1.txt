
        KUPERBERG--Jeanne. The Officers and Directors of the Associated Builders and Owners of Greater New York were saddened to learn of the death of Jeanne Kuperberg, beloved wife of ABO Governor, David Kuperberg. Our condolences and prayers are with David and his children, Russell (Rusty) and Danielle. May she rest in peace. Jerome Belson, President Michael Kerr, Co-Chairman Arnold Sollar, Co-Chairman Nicholas LaPorte, Jr., Executive Director
