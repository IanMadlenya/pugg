
        NECHES--Jacob. 96, longtime resident of Deal Park, NJ. A seventh generation direct descendent of the Vilna Gaon. A psychologist and attorney, Jacob was also former NYC regional administrator for the New York State VA. Past Commander of the Sidney Rosenberg Post (NY) of the American Legion, long standing member of Temple Beth El, Oakhurst, NJ. Surviving are his beloved wife of 53 years, the former Evelyn Tepper Brooks, son Dr. Richard Neches of Scarsdale, NY, and brothers Abraham and Philip, both of Florida.
