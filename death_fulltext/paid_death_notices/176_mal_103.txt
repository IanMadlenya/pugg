
        RING-Alois Louis. 84, of Spring Valley, NY, passed away peacefully on September 7, 2001. Survived by his sons Raymond and Robert, and their wives Pamela and Mary. Devoted grandfather to Eric and Tara. He served in the U.S. Navy in WWII. He received five bronze stars, for battles in Guam, Luzon, Leyte, Iwo Jima, and Okinowa. He worked for the Jacob Ruppert Brewery in NY, and the Schaefer Brewing Co until his retirement. He will be greatly missed. Services Monday; interment to follow at the Brick Church Cemetery.
