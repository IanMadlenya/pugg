
        PITASSY-Caesar L. Of Larchmont, on January 29, 2000. He was 85. He was born in New York on December 26, 1914, to Julius and Julia (nee DiSalle). Mr. Pitassy was married to Margaret (nee Cotter) in Mt. Vernon in 1942. Mr. Pitassy attended New York University and was a graduate from Fordham Law School, where he was Editor in Chief of the Fordham Law Review and a Lecturer. He retired as a managing partner of Rogers & Wells law firm. Mr. Pitassy received the Fordham Law Alumni Medal of Achievement and was President of the Fordham Law Alumni Association. He was also a member of the NY Bar Association, the NY County Lawyers Association, where he was Director and Chairman of the Judiciary Committee, and the Association of the Bar of the City of New York. He is survived by his wife Margaret, a son, Richard, and siblings Vivian McGowan, Helen York and the late Reginald Pitassy. He is also survived by grandchildren Diane and Kristin. Reposing Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 East Boston Post Rd., Mamaroneck. Funeral mass Wednesday, 10 AM, at Sts. John and Paul R.C. Church, followed by entombment at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
