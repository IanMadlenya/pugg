
        SULLIVAN-Kathleen A. With heavy hearts, the Yale Law School Community mourns the passing, on June 27th, of Kathleen A. Sullivan, Clinical Professor of Law at Yale, our colleague, teacher, mentor and friend. Her compassion for others, her warmth and her dignity will be sorely missed by all who were fortunate enough to have known her. We extend our sincerest condolences to her husband, Stephen Robinson, and daughter, Victoria. Anthony T. Kronman, Dean Yale Law School
