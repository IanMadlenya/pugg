
        ROSENBERG -- Helene J. (nee Cohn). Of Brooklyn and Chestnut Ridge, NY, died peacefully on November 27, after a courageous battle against esophageal cancer. A deep void will be felt in the lives of her family and friends. She is survived by her beloved husband Paul, loving son Edward (Estelle), and grandson Michael of Brooklyn, NY, devoted daughter Lisa and life partner Mary Shanley of NYC. They are forever grateful to have been graced by her joy of life, her laughter and her love. Thank you to all the good people who made her smile. Donations in Helene's honor can be made to United Hospice of Rockland, 11 Stokum Lane, New City, NY 10956-3505.
