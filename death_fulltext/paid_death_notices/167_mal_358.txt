
        SCHILDWACHTER-Hannah B., 93, a long time resident of New Rochelle, died December 27, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Devoted mother of David Carl Schildwachter, Sr. and his wife Wendy, Ann Catherine Brohm, and Martha Louise Schildwachter. Also survived by six grandchildren and five great grandchildren. The family will receive friends Friday from 2-4PM and 7-9PM at the Westchester Funeral Home, Inc. in Eastchester. A funeral service will be held at the Trinity Lutheran Church, Scarsdale, Saturday December 30 at 10AM. Interment to follow at Woodlawn Cemetery. The family would appreciate donations to The Trinity Lutheran Church, Crane Road, Scarsdale, NY 10583, or the Wartburg Home, Wartburg Place, Mt. Vernon, NY, 10550, in Hannah's memory.
