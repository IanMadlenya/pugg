
        ZOREK--Warren W. (Winnie), on December 22, 2006, in his 82nd year after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Jane, devoted father of Jennifer Pressman and Michael Zorek, father-in-law to Mark Pressman and Shelly Friedland, adoring grandfather of Jessica, Jeremy, Jeffrey and Diana. For four decades, an invaluable volunteer with the American Red Cross serving as a disaster relief coordinator, locally, nationally and internationally. Services Tuesday, December 26, 10:30am, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street. A memorial service will also be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Red Cross.
