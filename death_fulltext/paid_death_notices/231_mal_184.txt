
        SABBAH--Maurice (Chico). Israel Elwyn extends its deepest condolences to Zmira, Ronnie and Leeor Sabbah on the passing of Maurice (Chico) Sabbah, longtime Israel Elwyn board member and major benefactor. Through his vision & generosity, Israel Elwyn has become a leader in providing support services to children and adults with special needs in Israel. Chico will be sorely missed by the Israel Elwyn family and will continue to inspire all who knew him. May his memory be for a blessing. David Marcu, Exec. Director Morris Willner, Board Chair
