
        GOLDSTEIN-Abraham. On May 5, 1998. The Society of Jewish Science mourns the loss of its Leader, Chairman and 63-year member at the age of 90. The builder of our home and the guiding light of our Society, may he find eternal peace in G-d's resting place with our other great ones. Memorial services to be held on June 28 at 11am at the Society of Jewish Science, 109 East 39 Street, NY NY 10016. For info 212-682-2626
