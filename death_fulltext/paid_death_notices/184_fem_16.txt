
        MILGROM-Ida. JCRC NY/Coalition for Soviet Jewry sorrowfully mourn the passing of Ida Milgrom, mother of Natan Sharansky, Deputy Prime Minister of Israel. An ardent fighter for freedom, she imparted enormous strength to her family, the Soviet Jewry movement and human rights advocates around the globe. May her sons Natan and Leonid and their families be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. JCRC/Coalition for Soviet Jewry Ezra G. Levin, President Michael S. Miller, Exec. V.P. Melvin Stein, CFSJ Co-Chairman Susan Green, CFSJ Director
        MILGROM-Ida. The Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations mourns the passing of Ida Milgrom, beloved mother of Israel's Minister of Housing Natan Sharansky. She fought for her son's release with devotion and strength and later fulfilled her dream of living in Jerusalem, along with her family. May Natan and the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Mortimer B. Zuckerman, Chairman Malcolm Hoenlein, Executive Vice Chairman
