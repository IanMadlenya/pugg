
        ARTZT-Walter. Tel Aviv University and its American Friends note with deep sorrow the passing of our cherished friend, Walter Artzt. He was a dedicated member of the University's Board of Governors and was admired for his great warmth and creativity. His commitment to scholarship and academic excellence found a home at Tel Aviv University, where he established the Betty and Walter Artzt Chair for History of Poetry and Literature, along with his generous support of many other programs at the University. Our heartfelt condolences to his daughters, their families, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Alan L. Aufzien, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Robert J. Topchik, President Stephen Lecker, Exec. V.P.
        ARTZT-Walter. We have lost a wonderful friend whose generosity together with his beloved wife, Betty, helped us create the America-Israel Cultural House in the early '60s. Our deep condolences to his children, Paulette, Ken, Karen and the whole younger generation. Vera Stern, President America-Israel Cultural Foundation
