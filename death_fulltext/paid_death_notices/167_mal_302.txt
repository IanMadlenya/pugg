
        SASMOR-James A. Died of a sudden illness on Friday, Shabbat eve, the second night of Chanukah. Dearly beloved husband of Martha Greenhouse Sasmor for 51 years, loving father of Kenneth Samuel, adored father-in-law of Sheila, devoted grandfather of Jonathan and Steven, cherished brother of Daniel Sasmore and Vivienne Grossman, valued brotherin-law of Eli A. Grossman and Beth Greenhouse. Respected veterinarian on the Upper East Side for over 40 years. Services will be held at the Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Sunday at 10 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the cause of your choice.
