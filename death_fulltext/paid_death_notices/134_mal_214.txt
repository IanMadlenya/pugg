
        LAVERY-Peter Rutherford Randolph. An accomplished OffBroadway stage manager, director and multimedia producer, died of a heart attack March 12. He was 60 years old. A former council member of Actors Equity from 1971 to 1977. He is survived by his sister, Sarah Fach of Newton, MA, and a daughter, Gwendolyn Berland-Lavery. A memorial service will be held Friday, March 20 at noon, in the chapel at St. George's Episcopal Church, 209 East 16th St. In lieu of flowers, the Lavery family would like donations to be made in his name to United Action for Animals, PO Box 635, Lenox Hill Station, NY, NY 10021.
