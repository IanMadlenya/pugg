
        FRIES-Sophia. With great sorrow but with appreciation for the many treasured memories she leaves with us, the Fries family announces her peaceful death on August 8, 1999 at age 94 in Boca Raton, FL. She will be missed but remembered with pride by her loving and beloved husband, Jack, her sons and their wives, Stuart and Ellie, Joseph and Fredi and Arthur and Cindy, and her six grandchildren & spouses, and her five great-grandchildren. Services August 11 at 1:30 PM at Parkside, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn.
