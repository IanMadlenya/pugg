
        VALAIRE-Rosemary. Age 68. Born on June 11, 1930 in Crawley, Sussex, England. Died May 7, 1999 in her home in Los Angeles, CA. Member of the Royal Ballet from 1946 to 1954. Co-director of the Westside School of Ballet in Los Angeles from 1966 to 1999. Donations in her memory may be sent to the Rosemary Valaire Scholarship Fund, c/o the Westside School of Ballet, 1711 Stewart Street, Santa Monica, CA. 90404.
