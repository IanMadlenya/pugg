
        KREMEN - Joseph (Yus). Died June 9, 2002, age 82. Survived by his beloved, cherished wife, Ruth S.; adoring father of Erica and Douglas Rosenthal, Andrea and Arne Kotlen; special, doting grandfather of Ben, Mila, Lizzie Rosenthal, Jessica and Gillian Kotlen. During WWII, a meteorologist in the US Army Air Force, served in the Pacific. Graduate of CCNY, graduate work at NYU. Had a lifelong passion for Yiddish language and culture. Was loved by all who knew him. Services Wednesday, June 12, 11:15 AM, ''The Riverside,'' Amsterdam Ave and 76 St.
