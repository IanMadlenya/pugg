
        HENRY-John Campbell. On January 30, 1997. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Whitmel Powers Henry. Survived also by a daughter, Margaret Smith, a son, John Campbell Henry, Jr., a stepson, Eric Knaus, two grandsons and a sister, Louise Henry Clark. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
        HENRY-John Campbell. The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society regrets the passing of its esteemed Trustee, Assistant Treasurer and Assistant Secretary. We extend deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Henry Middendorf, President Henry C.B. Lindh, Chairman of the Executive Committee Edward F.L. Bruen, Secretary
        HENRY-John Campbell. The Huguenot Society of America records with great sorrow the death of John Campbell Henry, a Life Member since 1938, Assistant Treasurer, and former Treasurer, and Vice President of the Society. William W. Reese, President
