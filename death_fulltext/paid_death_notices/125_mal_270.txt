
        MacISAAC-Robert. Born August 17, 1944 in New York City, died Tuesday, June 24, 1997. He was the son of Angus and Marjorie (Elliott) MacIsaac. Robert last resided at the Stony Creek IRA in Hadley, New York. Concerned friends or family should call the Capital District DSO Director, Michael Dillon, at (518) 581-3038.
