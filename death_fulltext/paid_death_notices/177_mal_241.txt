
        TOLL-Wilbur. Longtime Scarsdale resident, died October 15, 2001 at the age of 82. He is survived by his wife Olga (nee Mendell), three sons: David (Marce), Stanley (Julie) and Lawrence, one brother Lionel and several grandchildren. Mr. Toll had been in the book publishing business and upon his retirement did volunteer work in Westchester County. Please make any contributions to your favorite charity in remembrance of Wilbur. Funeral service is on Thursday, October 18, 2001, 9:30 A.M., at ''Westchester Reform Temple'', 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, New York. Interment will follow at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. The family will receive friends at the Toll residence, Thursday afternoon, 44 Huntington Avenue, Scarsdale.
        TOLL-Wilbur. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its longtime member and extends sympathy to his bereaved family. Amy Lemle, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi
