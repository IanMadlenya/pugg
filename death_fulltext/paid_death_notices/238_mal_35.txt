
        SEADER--Saul, D.V.M., 82, died November 1, 2006, resided in Boca Raton, Fl., formally of Merrick, and East Hampton, NY. Retired Veterinarian and owner of Sunrise Animal Hospital of RVC, husband of the late Ruth Seader, he is survived by devoted children Richard, Bill, Robert and Joan, spouses Suzanne, Diane and John, adoring grandchildren, Jamie, David and Scott, loving companion, Carol Silverman. Cemetery Memorial Sunday, November 5th, 1pm, Mount Ararat, Farmingdale, NY.
