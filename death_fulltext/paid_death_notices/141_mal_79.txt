
        MORSE-Milton. Of Fort Lee, N.J. Died Monday in his home. He was 85. Mr Morse founded APM HEXSEAL Corporation of Englewood, N.J. in 1946 and remained active as its President and CEO until his death. Holder of more than 100 patents worldwide, he was recently inducted into the N.J. Inventors Hall of Fame. Survived by his son David and daughter-in-law Carol, his sister Selma Eisler and three grandchildren, Sara, Susanna and Michael. Services ''GuttermanMusicant'' 402 Park St, Hackensack, N.J. today, 2PM.
