
        ISRAEL--Paul. 65, died on Saturday, October 25, 2003. Incredibly beloved husband to Madeleine; father of Nancy and Erik; father-in-law of Victoria; grandfather of Daniel and Julia; son of David; brother of Stuart and Joanna; brother-in-law of Elsie, Marianne and Richard; son-inlaw of Sidney and Bibi; uncle to Sharon, Robert, Josh, Hillary and Alison and friend to all. He also will be fondly remembered by many students, teachers and administrators in the NYC School System. Paul was a lifelong liberal, jazz lover, teacher, sports enthusiast and above all a man who lived life fully. $emorial services will be held at Ancram Town Hall, Ancram, NY at 1 p.m. Wednesday, October 29, 2003. Contributions in his memory may be made to People For The American Way, 2000 M Street NW, Suite 400, Washington, DC 20036.
