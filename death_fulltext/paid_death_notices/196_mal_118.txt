
        GLASSMAN-Ephraim, M.D. The Department of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery at North Shore University Hospital expresses with sorrow the passing of Dr. Ephraim Glassman. Dr. Glassman, former Chief of Cardiology at NYU, and ultimately senior consulting Cardiologist at North Shore University Hospital, made great contributions to clinical and interventional Cardiology. His leadership and role as educator will be remembered. Our condolences to Larry, wife Jessica, grandchildren, and to the entire family. The Staff and Surgeons Dept. of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery at North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset
        GLASSMAN-Ephraim. The faculty and staff of the Division of Cardiology and the Department of Medicine note with sadness the death of our colleague, Ephraim Glassman, former Professor of Medicine and Director of the Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory at New York University Medical Center, who set the standard for education in interventional cardiology. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family. Glenn I. Fishman, M.D., Arthur C. Fox, M.D. and Martin J. Blaser, M.D.
