
        BARKHORN--Richard E., age 82, of New York City and formerly of Summit, New Jersey, died at home on March 12, 2004. A reporter and editor for more than thirty years with the Newark Evening News, he was a graduate of Dartmouth College where he was co-editor of the College newspaper. Born and raised in Newark, N.J., he graduated from Weequahic High School. He served in the Pacific Theater during World War II. He is survived by three sons: Ivan of Colorado; Peter of Brooklyn; and Jonathan of Chatham, N.J., four grandchildren and a sister, Mariette Barkhorn Buchman of New York. He will be especially remembered for sharing his love of knowledge, and for his incisive thinking and wit.
