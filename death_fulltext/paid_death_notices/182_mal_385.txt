
        WATKINS-Ted J. Died peacefully at home on March 24, 2002 at age 82. He is survived by his beloved daughter Pamela K. and devoted son-in-law Robert J. Seligman of Manalapan, N.J., and cherished granddaughter Tiffany Dawn Seligman of Manalapan. He is a veteran of W.W. II, serving in the Army from 1942-1946. He was a longtime employee of the Communications Workers of America. He last served in the national office as Executive Assistant to the President prior to his retirement.
