
        KEARNS-Joseph G. All of us at Lutheran Medical Center mourn the loss of our trusted friend and generous supporter, Joseph G. Kearns. Joe served on our Board of Trustees for over 30 years, including over two decades as Vice Chairman. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Agnes, and his entire family. All of us who knew Joe Kearns will be forever grateful for his warmth, generosity and strength of character. Howard Smith, Chairman, Board of Trustees Wendy Z. Goldstein, President Raymond P. Girnys, MD, President Medical and Dental Staff Lutheran Medical Center
