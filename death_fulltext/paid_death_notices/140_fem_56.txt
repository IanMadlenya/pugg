
        ASHER-Gertrude. Aged 81. An educator and progressive, died September 5, after a short illness. She is survived by her son Robert, of Willimantic, Conn., her grandchildren Tanya Arenson & Samuel Arenson, her nephews Paul Stamler and Paul Steinberg, and her niece Jane Steinberg Zucker. A private service will be held in Los Angeles. Contributions may be sent to Environmental Support Center, 4420 Connecticut Avenue, NW, Washington, D.C. 20008-2301.
