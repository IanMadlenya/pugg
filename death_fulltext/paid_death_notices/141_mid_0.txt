
        RIBAKOFF-Corinne A. ''Corky'' of Palm Beach, FL. Beloved wife of Eugene J. Ribakoff; devoted mother of Betsy, wife of Richard Sheerr of Bryn Mawr, PA; and Charles Ribakoff and his wife Patricia of Boston. Beloved sister of Marie Ruth Kearns of Cleveland and cherished grandmother of Deborah J. Gordon, Benjamin H. Gordon and Jacob B. Ribakoff II. Funeral services will be held today, Friday, at 2PM in Temple Sinai, 661 Salisbury Street, Worcester, MA. Memorial contributions may be made to Cancer Patients Care Program, C/O Massachusetts General Hospital, 55 Fruit Street, Boston, MA. 02114.
        RIBAKOFF-Corinne (''Corky''). The Board of Governors of the Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member and dear friend, Corky Ribakoff, and offers its heartfelt sympathy to her family and friends.
