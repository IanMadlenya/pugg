
        FRANK--John Harrison, 83, of New York, N.Y., passed away on November 24, 2003 surrounded by his loving family. He is survived by his devoted wife of 57 years, Gloria, his three loving daughters Penny, Rosemary and Terri and six grandchildren. John was a graduate of Yale University and owner of Atlantic Home Products. Memorial Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., Tuesday, November 25th, 5:30PM.
