
        HOGAN-Richard John. Of Fairfax, VA, formerly of Forest Hills, NY, died September 3, 1999. Son of Madeleine M. Hogan and the late John V. Hogan, father of Lesley Elisabeth Hogan and Scott R.J. Hogan, brother of Douglas R. Hogan and Russell P. Hogan, former husband of Joanne Sherman Hogan. Graduate of Phillips Exeter Academy and the University of Pennsylvania. Popular culture magazine editor and writer, singer/ songwriter. We love and will miss him deeply. Memorial service Saturday, Septmeber 11, at 2 PM at St. Luke's Episcopal Church, 85 Greenway South, Forest Hills, NY.
