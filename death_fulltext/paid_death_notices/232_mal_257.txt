
        LECHER--B. Douglas, MD. Born in Brooklyn, NY, October 23, 1919, passed away in Tucson, AZ May 25, 2006. Prominent physician, beloved husband, father and grandfather. Survived by the love of his life--wife Muriel, married 58 years. Proud and loving father of Andrew (Donna), Peter (Debbie), and Tom (Pam). Beloved and loving Grandpa ''Pop'' of Liza, Todd, Martin, Matthew and Alexander. Brother of Ruth Schatz, loving uncle of many nieces and nephews. His family was the most important part of his life. Private services held in Tucson, Arizona. Information: East Lawn Palms Mortuary, 520-885-6741
