
        WOLVEK-Sidney, 74. Died on October 2, 1997. Loving husband of Irma and surived by sons Joseph and Ethan. Services will be on Sunday at 4 PM at Temple Beth Emeth, 83 Marlborough Road at Church Ave., Brooklyn.
