
        YADGAROFF--Musia Wick. Of Palm Beach, Florida, peacefully on June 28, 2004. Predeceased by husbands Jack Yadgaroff and Samuel Wick. Cherished mother of Loretta (Arthur) Holland. Adored grandmother of Seth, Sandford and Nicole. Musia's strength, compassion and humor will be sorely missed. All who knew her came to relish her good counsel and her love of the arts and all things beautiful. Rest in Peace, our China Princess. Donations may be made to Special Needs Fund, Beth El Synagogue, New Rochelle, New York 10804.
