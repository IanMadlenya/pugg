
        LARSEN-Laurence Putnam, 45, died in San Juan, PR on December 21, 2000. He is survived by his wife, Scynthia Macovik Larsen, mother Marcia Tirrell Larsen, father H. Allen Larsen, sisters Kristen and Leslie, brother Keith. Born in Concord, MA, Laurence graduated from Bowdoin College and Columbia Business School. He was with Citibank in New York City, San Juan and London, UK. Later there with Trust Company of The West. At the time of his death, Laurence was Exec. Vice President, Oriental Financial Group, San Juan. Memorial services are being planned in London and at Bowdoin College.
