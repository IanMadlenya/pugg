
        MALBIN-Henrietta S. The adored Henrietta Malbin died peacefully on September 26 at the age of 90. She was the beloved wife of the late Irving, devoted sister of Sylvia Schulman and the late Phil Shapiro, wonderful aunt of Susan and Jeffrey Moss, Dennis and Jill Schulman, Eleanor and Kurt Muenz, Joan and Max Tochner, Sharon Shapiro, and greatest great-aunt to nine lucky great nieces and nephews. Services Sunday, September 29, at 1PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations to Knesset Israel Garment Center Congregation.
