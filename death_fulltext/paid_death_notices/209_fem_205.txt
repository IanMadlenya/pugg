
        ALCOTT--Rica. Died peacefully in her sleep at home on June 27, 2004, at age 97. She was the beloved wife of the late Fred Alcott for 69 years. Devoted mother of Mimi Levine of Chicago, and Virginia Sadock of NYC. Loving grandmother of David, Richard, Michael, James and Victoria, and great-grandmother of Nicholas, Annie, Alice, Ben and Max. She was beloved by all and will be deeply missed. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81 St, Tuesday, 9:30 am. Interment to follow at Knollwood Cemetery.
