
        KRAKEUR - Henry Arthur. Age 84 died September 3rd. Henry lived the teaching, ''Do all the good you can for your fellow man'' until the day he died-delivering food and raising funds for the hungry, teaching children and adults, counseling the sufferingwith his hands and heart. He will be greatly missed by his wife of 57 years, Betty, and his children, Connie and Jerry Von Korff, Art and Shelley Krakauer, and grandchildren, Joshua, Michael, Benjamin, Margie, Lesley and Daniel. Services Thursday, 11:30AM, at Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY 10606, or Jewish Community Center of Harrison, 130 Union Ave., Harrison, NY 10528.
        KRAKEUR - Henry. With hearts filled with gratitude for the gift of Henry Krakeur, and overwhelmed with sadness for our loss, Congregation Kol Ami remembers our Past President, friend and teacher. Henry was a lover of Torah, a friend of all humanity, and a tzaddik-a righteous person. A man of the deepest spirit, he was generous in sharing his wisdom and love. Many suns will have to blaze brightly where his life once shone. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Betty, his children and grandchildren. Rabbi Shira Milgrom Rabbi Tom Weiner Cantor Annie Lynn Bornstein Ken Simon, President
