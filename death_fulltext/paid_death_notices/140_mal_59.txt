
        DIBA-Mahmoud. The Board of Trustees, the Staff and friends of the Brooklyn Museum of Art mourn the tragic death of Mahmoud Diba, beloved husband of our good friend and colleague, curator Layla Diba. A member of the Asian Art Council, he was a longtime friend of the Brooklyn Museum of Art. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Layla, their son, and to every member of the family. Robert S. Rubin, Chairman, Board of Trustees Arnold L. Lehman, Director
