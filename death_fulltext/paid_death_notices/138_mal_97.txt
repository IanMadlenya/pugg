
        FEHR-Robert O., Dr. The Directors of the Heineman Foundation for Charitable, Educational and Scientific Purposes Inc. note with profound sadness the passing of Dr. Robert Fehr, a longtime member of the Board. Bob's wide range of knowledge and his dedication to the purposes of the Foundation were instrumental in guiding the Board over many years. Above all, Bob was a much loved and devoted friend, whose presence will be irreplaceable.
