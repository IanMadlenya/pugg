
        von STACKELBERG-Ellen Biddle. Of NYC (born 1912), Salisbury, CT, as well as 15 years in Germany, died peacefully in her home of 38 years in Albany, VT. Ellen, youngest of three children of Nicholas Biddle and Elizabeth Leroy Emmet, will be deeply and sadly missed by her children Olaf Patrick, Elizabeth Emmet, John Roderich and Nicholas Temple, her twelve surviving grandchildren, two great-grandchildren, and her eight nieces and nephews. Services Albany, VT, January 2, 1999, at 2 PM.
