
        CURRY-Robert Emmet. Husband of the late Rose (Mooney), father of Robert Emmet Curry Jr., father-in-law of Margaret Kennedy Curry, grandfather of Robert Emmet Curry III and Bevin Kennedy Curry, brother of Bernard F. Curry and the late John F. Curry Jr., Veronica C. McCarthy and Francis R. Curry, and brother-in-law of Katherine M. Mooney and the late Florence M. Halleran, Daniel J. Mooney Jr., and Helen M. Gretsch. He is also survived by numerous nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. A son of John F. and Mary Curry, he was born at home on April 4, 1913. His father was a leader of Tammany Hall from 1929 to 1933. He graduated from the Collegiate School, where he was captain of the basketball team, and from Georgetown University. He served in the US Army from 1935 through 1946, with a two-year hiatus, and was discharged as a Lt. Colonel while an instructor at West Point. Thereafter he spent his career at the John F. Curry Agency, an insurance brokerage, retiring as Chairman of the Board. Active in civil affairs, he served as Vice Chair of the first Board of Corrections of the City of New York from 1957-1960, and as Chairman through 1964. In addition to being a former President of the Catholic Big Brothers of New York, he was a founder and director of the Big Brothers/Big Sisters of America. He also founded and later served as President of the Georgetown University Club of Metropolitan New York. He was a life member of The Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York and for over 40 years, a member of the Rockaway Hunting Club. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Church of St. Vincent Ferrer, Lexington Ave. at 66 St., Wednesday, 10:30 AM. Interment private.
