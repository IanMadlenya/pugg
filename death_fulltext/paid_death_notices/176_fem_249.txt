
        GRIEB-Yoland J., 45-year resident of Northport, on September 28, 2001, in her 87th year. Longtime member of Catholic Daughters and The Rosary Society. Enjoyed gardening, bowling and traveling. She visited all 50 states. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Loving mother of Marie Edison, Caroline Schlegel, Christopher, and Virginia Acker. Devoted grandmother of nine. Dear sister of Stewart Walker, Yvonne Vekasy, and Yvette Meehan. Reposing at Nolan & Taylor-Howe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Ave, Northport, NY. Donations to American Diabetes Association or The Arthritis Foundation in her memory appreciated.
