
        GIBBS-Howard G., 82. Longtime executive at Boys Clubs of America and former Chairman of the Board of the Chautauqua Institution died in his summer home in Chautauqua, NY on Tuesday. He is survived by his wife of 49 years, Janet Stang Gibbs, his children Christopher and Nancy, and granddaughters Charlotte and Galen May. Donations may be made to the Gibbs Scholarship at Chautauqua, where a memorial service will be held July 28.
        GIBBS-Howard G. The Chautauqua Institution mourns the loss and celebrates the life of Howard G. Gibbs, 1919 to 2002, Chairman of The Board of Trustees, 1978-1985.
