
        BLAU-Rose. Yeshiva University and its Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law are saddened by the passing of the loving wife of the late David Blau and beloved mother of Harvey Blau, longtime member of the Board of Directors of Cardozo. Harvey, along with his wife, Arlene, are YU Guardians and CSL Fellows. We extend our heartfelt condolences as well to brother Dr. Jeffrey (and Lynn) Blau, grandchildren and great-grandchildren, and to all members of the bereaved family.
