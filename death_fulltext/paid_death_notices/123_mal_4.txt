
        STEINBERG-David, passed away March 31, 1997 after a brief illness. He was the beloved husband of Lee Ohrbach Steinberg and devoted father of Linda Motelson and Ellen Greenfield. He was a cherished grandfather of Peter and Glenn Motelson and Mandy and Adam Greenfield. Mr. Steinberg was predeceased by his first wife Dorothy Lepow Steinberg. Mr. Steinberg was a Principal of Steinberg Kass Inc, a resident buying office. Services were held in Miami Beach. Interment will be at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY Thursday, April 3, at 10 AM.
