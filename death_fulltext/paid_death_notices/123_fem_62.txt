
        GRUBER-Selma Salinger. On April 8, 1997. A warm, wise and gentle woman. Her youthful, vital spirit touched so many. A true gourmet who loved to bring people together in celebration. A wonderful and loving mother of Julia and Jessica. Devoted, caring wife of the late Manny Gruber. A special mother-in-law of Larry and Hunter. In lieu of flowers, a donation to the Developmental Chemotherapy Service at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital will be appreciated. Service Thursday, April 10, 12 Noon, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91st Street.
