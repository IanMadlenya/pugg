
        McEVILLY-William. Of New Rochelle, NY died on January 29, 2002 at home. He worked as a producer for WOR-Radio ''Rambling with Gambling'' in NYC for 43 years before retiring in 1992. He was a member of Wykagyl C.C. in New Rochelle and served in the Army from 1945-47. He is survived by his sister, Jane Marie Mahoney of Utica, NY. He was predeceased by his wife, Joan, and his brother, John. Friends may send memorial donations in his memory to the Perry Fund C/O Highwatch Farm, P.O. Box 607, Kent, CT. 06757.
