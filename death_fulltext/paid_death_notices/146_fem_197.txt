
        GREENAWALT-Martha S. The Members of the League of Women Voters mourn the passing of our leader, mentor and dear friend. Martha was President of the Greenburgh, Westchester, N Y State, and Tri-State leagues, a LWVUS Board Member and a life Member of the Scarsdale LWV. We extend deepest sympathies to her family and friends and to all those who lives she touched. Evelyn Stock, Pres., LWVNYS Jo Brill, Pres., LWVWestchester Marcia Kusnetz, Pres., LWV Scars.
