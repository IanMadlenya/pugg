
        LITTLE--William Spencer, 93, on July 6, 2005. Bill was the husband of the late Verbena May Little and the father of Wm. Brian and Christopher, both deceased. He had a 47 year career with Becton Dickinson and Co. After living for 40 years in Ridgewood, NJ and Essex, CT he moved to Santa Barbara, CA and resided at Casa Dorinda. He is survived by his daughter-in-law, Judith Little; two grandchildren, Jackie and Greg; and five great-grandchildren. There will be a private memorial service for family only.
