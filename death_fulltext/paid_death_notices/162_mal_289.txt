
        SAFRO-Paul. The American Friends of Beth Hatefutsoth, the Museum of the Jewish Diaspora in Tel Aviv and the entire museum management, mourn the passing of Paul Safro, our vice president, friend, benefactor and guiding spirit for so many years. He will surely be missed by all. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Rose, and his children. Sam E. Bloch, Chairman Stephen M. Greenberg, President Gloria Golan, Director David Alexander, Director General Beth Hatefutsoth
