
        OBOLENSKY--Mary. The officers, board of directors mourn the passing of Mary, wife of our esteemed board member, Ivan Obolensky, past president and founder of the Childrens Cancer and Blood Foundation. A celebration service of her life will be held at 11 A.M. at the Church of Incarnation, 30th of August 2006, Madison at 35th Street, NYC. Reception to follow at SSMA Club, 283 Lexington Avenue, New York City. In lieu of flowers, donations will be appreciated. John Calicchio, Chrmn of Bd Nancy Raquet, President
