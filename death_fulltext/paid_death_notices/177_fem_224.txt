
        PRESTON-Susan G. 70, October 20, 2001, at home in St. Simons Island, GA. Beloved mother of Diane P. Dolinsky, Carmel, IN, and Julie P. Kennedy, Oxford, MI. Cherished sister of Linda Goetz Holmes, New York and Shelter Island, NY. Devoted grandmother of William, Jamie and Jessica Kennedy and Paul, Monica and Kristofer Dolinsky. Born in White Plains, NY, April 17, 1931. Predeceased by her husband, Macon A., her daughter, Susan M., and her parents, Theodore and Florence Goetz. Services Wednesday, October 24, at Lovely Lane Chapel, St. Simons Island, GA, and Friday, October 26, at graveside, Forest Lawn Cemetery, Buffalo, NY.
