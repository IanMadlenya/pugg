
        GROSS-Steven. The Board of Director, Volunteers and Staff of the American Heart Association mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague. Through his fervent commitment, creative spirit and generosity, Mr. Gross and his family have enhanced the fight against heart disease and stroke. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Janice and members of the Gross family. Morgan B. Stark, Chairman Charles A. Dennis, MD, President Michael L. Weamer, Executive Vice President
