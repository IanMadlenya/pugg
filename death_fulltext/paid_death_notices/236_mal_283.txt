
        LEE--Booker, 90, died August 31 in Fort Lauderdale, after residing many years in NYC, Scarsdale and Greenwich and following a distinguished career with the United Nations as Training Officer for Translation Services. Left to cherish his memory are wife Dorothy; son Benjamin; daughter Victoria; daughterin-law Jianying Zha; granddaughter Siri; sisters, brothers, beloved friends. Memorial service Saturday, October 7, 2 PM, Ferncliff Cemetery Chapel, 280 Secor Road, Hartsdale. In lieu of flowers, please donate to UNICEF at 333 East 38th Street, NY 10016 or 1-800-4UNICEF.
