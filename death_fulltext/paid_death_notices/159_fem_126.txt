
        MUNSON-Elizabeth Delza (Hurwitz). Born February 9, 1893. Died April 13, 2000. She was a dancer, dance teacher, and teacher of the Alexander Technique to singers. She compiled her late husband's, Gorham Munson's, last book, editing his notes and writings concerning the emerging literature and literary scene of the 1920's. She is survived by nieces and nephews.
