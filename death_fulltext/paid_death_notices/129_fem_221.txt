
        PRINDLE-Florence. The Board of Trustees, and faculty past and present, of the Ethical Culture Fieldston School note with sorrow the passing of Florence Prindle, teacher of art in the Fieldston Lower School from 1962-1987. Her thoughtful involvement in the life of the school was immense. Children, parents, and colleagues will remember her with deep affection. A memorial service will be held in the Student/Faculty Center of the Fieldston School, November 22 at 2 PM. Ethical Culture Fieldston School
