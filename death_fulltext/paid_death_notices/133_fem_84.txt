
        HUTSON-Jean Blackwell. The Schomburg Center mourns the passing of Jean Blackwell Hutson, Curator, Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture, The New York Public Library, from 1948 to 1980. She passed on February 4th, 1998, at age 83. Viewing, Thursday, February 12, 3-7 P.M. at St. Philip's Episcopal Church, 204 West 134th St. Funeral Mass, Friday, February 13th, at 10 A.M. at St. Philip's. Memorial Service, Saturday, March 7th, 12 noon, at St. Philip's. She will be deeply missed by friends, colleagues and the community.
