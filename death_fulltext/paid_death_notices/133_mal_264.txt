
        LEVY-Walter. With profound sorrow the Officers and Board of Trustees of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations mourn the death of our devoted friend and colleague. Wally served Reform Judaism for many years, giving of his time and talents with loving dedication. A man of the book, his life was an expression of the greatest values of our faith. We express heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife, Bea, and all his loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President Rabbi Alexander M. Schindler, President Emeritus
        LEVY-Walter. The Rockville Centre Israel Bond Committee mourns the passing of its long time Co-Chairman. Our deepest condolences to Bea and the entire family. All who knew him were enriched, he will be sorely missed. George Fried & Abner Beder
