
        CAMAC-Gussie. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of the Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the passing of Gussie Camac, beloved sister of our Chairman, Bernard Goldberg. For 16 years Gussie graced the Hebrew Home with her warmth and charm. She was part of the fabric of the Hebrew Home community. We will miss her greatly and extend our condolences to her entire family. Milton A. Gilbert, President Daniel A. Reingold, Exec VP
