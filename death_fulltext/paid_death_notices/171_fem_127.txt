
        PREMICE-Josephine. On April 13, 2001. Actress, singer, wife and mother. Loving mother of Enrico Fales & Susan Fales-Hill. Devoted sister of Adele Premice. Also survived by her husband Timothy Fales and many loving relatives and friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Sunday April 15th, 6-9 PM. Services and interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to The American Lung Association.
