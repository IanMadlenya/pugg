
        EPSTEIN-Betty, on October 14, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Louis J. Loving mother of Edward Jay Epstein and Linda J. Nessel. Dear sister of Mildred Fleischman. Adoring grandmother of Richard, Lori, Kenneth, Daniel and James Nessel. Proud and inspiring greatgrandmother of Jacob and Gensiana Cicero and Timothy Louis Nessel. She will be greatly missed and will live on through her art and sculptures. Service Sunday 12:00 Noon at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory to The Creative Center for Women with Cancer.
