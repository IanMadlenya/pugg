
        SHAPIRO--Frank, 60. On December 3, 2005, after a courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. Beloved husband of Carol; devoted father of Lisa and her husband Craig Berry, and Amy Shapiro; doting grandfather to Max and Alex Berry; and brother to Alan Shepherd. Services for Mr. Shapiro will be at 10:30 A.M. Tuesday, December 6, at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Boulevard. (718896-9000). Donations may be made to Hospice Care Network, 99 Sunnyside Boulevard, Woodbury, NY 11797. $(6$)SHAPIRO--Frank. The partners of Anchin, Block & Anchin LLP mourn the loss of their esteemed partner Frank Shapiro, 60, of Wantagh, Long Island, on Saturday, December 3, 2005, after a long and courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. His spirit and his demeanor throughout his lengthy illness were an inspiration to all who knew him. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Carol; his children, Lisa and her husband Craig Berry, and Amy Shapiro; his grandchildren, Alex and Max Berry; and his brother, Alan Shepherd. He was a long-time partner of Seymour Schneidman & Associates and Anchin, Block & Anchin LLP. We extend our deepest sympathies to the family.
