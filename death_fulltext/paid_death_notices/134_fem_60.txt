
        BERKOWITZ-Fannie. Age 89. March 8, 1998, at North Shore Hospital, Manhasset, NY. Wife of the late Joseph Berkowitz. Beloved mother of Rita and Moe Pasternack and Alan and Carol Berkowitz. Adored grandmother of Barry and Andrew Berkowitz, Gail and Joel Leder, Jan and Fern Pasternack, Barry and Amelia Pasternack, and nine great-grandchildren. Her family was her life. Services today, 1 PM, at Sinai Chapel, Fresh Meadows, NY.
