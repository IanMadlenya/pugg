
        LUKOFF--Irving F., Ph.D. The Columbia University School of Social Work records with profound sorrow the recent death of Professor Emeritus Irving F. Lukoff on February 27. A member of the faculty from 1968--1991, he was former Director of the School's Center for Demonstration and Research and a distinguished educator and author who made lasting contributions to the profession. Heartfelt sympathies are extended to his family. Jeanette C. Takamura, Dean
