
        BERRIGAN-Paul Dunn, Brigadier General, Corps of Engineers, U.S. Army, Retired. In San Francisco, January 2, 1998. Husband of the late Mary Fay Berrigan. Survived by his beloved first cousin, Mary Dunn Schreiner of Boston, his faithful housekeeper, Rosita Corpuz, and eleven nieces and nephews. A graduate of the United States Military Academy, class of 1927. Memorial contributions may be made to the West Point Fund, USMA, West Point, New York 10996. For service information, please contact McAvoy O'Hara Evergreen Mortuary of San Francisco at 415-668-0077.
