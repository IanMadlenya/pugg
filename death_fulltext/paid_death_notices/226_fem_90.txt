
        EFRON--Lillian Westerman. Age 95, of Rockville, MD, died peacefully on November 7, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Benjamin Efron, loving mother of Sheila (Steven) Efron, Taube and Richard (Kay), grandmother of Michelle and David (Somer) Taube, Amanda and Elenia Efron. Great grandmother of Rebecca. Beloved sister of the late Abe Westerman and Pearl Mizrachi, survived by sister, Ann Steindler. She worked tirelessly to insure that all people had equal opportunities. She will be missed by all her family and friends.
