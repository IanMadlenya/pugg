
        KRONE-Irene (nee Beckmann). Beloved mother of Kathryn and wife of the late Helmut Krone. Died suddenly in a traffic accident in Martinique, on Wednesday, November 29, 2000. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, December 7, 2000 at 1 P.M., at The Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church at 73rd Street. Donations in her memory may be made to the Helmut Krone Scholarship Fund at The One Club, 32 East 21 Street, New York, New York, 10010.
