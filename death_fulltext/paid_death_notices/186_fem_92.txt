
        LYON-Nina (Miller). Died July 14 in Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of Delray Beach, Florida and Scarsdale, New York. Beloved wife of the late William W. Lyon. Member of the Huguenot Society and the National Society of Colonial Dames. She is survived by her son William W. Lyon III of Rohnert Park, CA., and her sister Barbara Eversole of Ponte Vedra, Florida.
