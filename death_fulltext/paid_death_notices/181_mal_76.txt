
        NICHOLS-Albert L., M.D. Physician, entrepreneur, loving husband, father and grandfather died of a heart attack at age 67 on January 27, 2002. He was founder, chairman, and CEO of Nichols Institute from 1970-1994 when NI merged with Corning Life Sciences. Memorial services February 6, 2002, 11AM-2PM at Four Seasons, Newport Beach, CA. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to either Institute for Brain Aging, UCI, California, or Marine Conservation Action Fund, New England Aquarium, 1 Central Wharf, Boston, MA. 02110.
