
        GASPARI--Contessa Antoinette Francois in Gaspari died peacefully on October 5, at the age of 79, at her home in Vallerana, Italy. Born on June 2, 1924 in Geneva, Switzerland, Toinon was the daughter of Professor Alexis Francois of the University of Geneva and Marthe Francois. She is survived by her husband Pierluigi Gaspari, her children Philip Littell, Peter Littell, Anita Repp, Carola Di Carlo, and eight grandchildren.
