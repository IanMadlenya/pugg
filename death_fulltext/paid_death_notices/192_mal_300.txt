
        KITZINGER-Ernst. On January 22, 2003 in Poughkeepsie, NY. Husband of the late Margaret Susan Kitzinger. Survived by his children Stephen Anthony Kitzinger, Margaret Rachel Kitzinger and Adrian Nicholas Kitzinger, and three grandchildren. A. Kingsley Porter University Professor emeritus of Harvard University, former director of studies at Dumbarton Oaks, Washington, D.C.
