
        SILVERMAN-Sarah. May 30, 1903-February 18, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Alexander, extraordinarily loving mother of Walter, thrilled grandmother of Aaron, Rachel, Thomas and William. Deeply devoted daughter of the late Rose and Nathan, loving sister of the late Celia, Jeanne and Max. Graduate of the Cooper Union and the Beaux Arts in Paris, silk screen designer, successful and creative businesswoman, owner of Kay Studio, friend and woman of culture, grace and refinement. She loved her life, family and friends immensely. Laid to rest in private graveside services at Wellwood Cemetery. Sarah will forever remain in the heart of her son.
