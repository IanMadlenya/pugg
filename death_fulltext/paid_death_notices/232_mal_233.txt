
        LAPPIN--Peter J. Passed away quietly at his South Carolina home, May 17, 2006. U.S. Navy Veteran. He is survived by two sons James and Glenn, grandson Keith; sisters Anne, Gertrude, Liz and Winnie. New York Times Pressman for thirty years. Memorial service at Thomas F. Dalton, 29 Atlantic Avenue, Floral Park, on Thursday, May 25, 6-8 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Heart Association.
