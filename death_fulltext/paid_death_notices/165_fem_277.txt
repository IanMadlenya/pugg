
        MORGAN-Phylliss Utz. Expired on October 27, 2000. A graduate of the Harlem School of Nursing. Mrs. Morgan directed the LPN Nursing Program at the Harlem YMCA. She was a longtime resident of St. Albans and Howard Beach. She will be funeralized Thursday morning, November 2, 2000, at St. Catherine's of Sienna in St. Albans.
