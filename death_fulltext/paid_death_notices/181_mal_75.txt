
        MESSINGER-Dr. Henry. Age 89, of Southport, CT., a highly respected and much loved family practioner, died Sunday, February 3, 2002. He is survived by his wife, Beatrice Koblin Messinger and his daughter, Maris Messinger, both of Southport and his son, Dr. Barry Messinger and his wife, Geri of Manchester, CT. Services Tuesday, February 5, 2002, 11:00 AM from Abraham L. Green and Son 88 Beach Road, Fairfield, CT. Friends may call at 10:00 AM, with interment at Temple Israel Memorial Park in Blauvelt, NY.
