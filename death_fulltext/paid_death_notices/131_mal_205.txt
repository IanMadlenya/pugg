
        GIANNELLI-William H. 87 of Boca Raton, FL died at home on Tuesday, December 9, 1997. Born in NYC, he resided there until 1951, when he and his family moved to Westchester County. Between 1978 and 1986 the Giannellis were residents of Bronxville, New York. Mr. Giannelli was a Partner of John T. Brady Construction Corporation in NYC until his retirement in 1973. He was a concerned citizen who contributed to many worthy causes hoping to make the world a better place for all. Through his example of love and devoted involvement with his family he has left a profound legacy. William was preceded in death by his loving wife of 55 years, Marie, on February 3, 1988. He is survived by his daughters, Mrs. Raymond Martin (Marilyn) of Boca Raton, FL and Mrs. Richard Schineller (Eileen) of Bronxville, NY and his 12 beloved grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Joseph's Catholic Church, Bronxville, NY on Wednesday, December 17 at 10:00 AM, followed by burial at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. A memorial Mass will be held in Boca Raton at St. Joan of Arc Church at a later date for friends and family. Contributions may be made to American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, NY 10601.
