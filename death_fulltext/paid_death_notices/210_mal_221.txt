
        FARBER--Allan. Died July 16, 2004 in Boca Raton, FL at age 100. Survived by his two daughters, Phyllis Burger of Greenwich, CT and Sandra Horvitz of NYC, granddaughters Susan and Judith Burger, and sister, Lillian Feinberg, he was a long time Worcester, MA resident, businessman, and national and local philanthropist. Contributions to the Allan Farber Endowment Fund, C/O the Anti-Defamation League, 823 United Nations Plaza, NY, NY 10017.
