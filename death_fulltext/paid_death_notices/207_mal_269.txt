
        HENDERSON--Paul Bargas Jr. Born November 20, 1928 died April 22, 2004 due to a sudden accident. His last job was Sr. Vice President of strategic planning for the Federal Reserve Bank of NY. He retired in 1984. He is survived by his wife Betty. Celebration service will be held at St. Matthew's UMC, Monday, April 26, 2004 at 2:00 PM, in Annandale, VA.
