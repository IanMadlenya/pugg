
        OVERLANDER-John Craig. 69, longtime resident of Eastchester and Bronxville died following a long illness on April 21, 2001. Born in New York City to John J. and Mary E. Overlander. Mr. Overlander, a US Navy Veteran, was graduated from Iona College in 1958 with a Bachelor of Business Administration. He was retired from the J.C. Penney Co where he was a merchandise manager. Mr. Overlander was the beloved husband of Sally-Anne. Loving brother of Clare of North Hampton, MA. Devoted father and father-in-law of Craig and Nancy of New Canaan, CT, Keith and Kimberlee of Manhasset, NY, and Karen (Overlander) and Owen Morgan of Peachtree City, GA, and the cherished grandfather of Ashley, Jessica, Kelley, Kacie, Colleen, Blake, Jake, Brennan, Kevin, Haley and Hank. Visitation will be at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, on Monday at 7-9, Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday at St. Joseph's Church in Bronxville, NY, at 10 AM. Interment will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers those who wish to donate in Mr. Overlander's name may send contribtions for Thoracic Research to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center Development Office, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021; or the Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St, Tuckahoe, NY 10707.
