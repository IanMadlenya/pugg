
        McGRAW-Anne Schuyler Williams. Of Gulf Stream, FL and of Wianno, MA., passed away on December 22, 1999 after a long illness with cancer. She was married to Donald C. McGraw, Jr. for over 50 years and had four sons, Donald III, Robert, David and the late Peter McGraw plus thirteen grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on August 8, 2000 at 11AM at St. Peter's Episcopal Church in Osterville, MA 02655. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to either St. Peter's Episcopal Church or St. Paul's Episcopal Church, 188 S. Swinton Avenue, Delray Beach, FL. 33444. For information contact Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, Delray Beach, FL.
