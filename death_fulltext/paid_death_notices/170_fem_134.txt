
        PETERSON-Marion A. 79, formerly of West Nyack, NY. Died on March 16, 2001. She was predeceased by her husband Richard J. Peterson in 1999. She is survived by three sons, Richard Peterson of Falls Church, VA, Charles Peterson of Pittsburgh, PA, and Robert Peterson of Scotch Plains, NJ; eight grandchildren; one sister, Kathleen Sussingham; three brothers, Frank, Gerald and Joseph Cohane. Reposing Hawthorne Funeral Home, 21 Stevens Ave., Hawthorne, NY, Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass Monday, 12 noon, St. Francis of Assisi Chapel at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Interment following. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory to St. Anthony's Church, Nanuet, NY.
