
        GOLDBERG-Ben. 83, retired President of SEIU Local 3036 (The Taxi Drivers Union), died December 28, 2001, following a brief illness. He was the beloved husband of the late Leah Duhl Goldberg. Father of Lawrence Goldberg and Cindy Wapnitsky. Father-in-law to Bryn Johnson and Ken Wapnitsky. Grandfather of Leah, Jack and Pheobe. He dedicated his life to his family, friends and to the labor movement. The family pledges to carry on his good work. Funeral 10 AM, Thursday, January 3, 2002, at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY. Contributions may be made to Calvary Hospital, Bronx, or to The Jewish Labor Committee, NYC.
        GOLDBERG-Ben. SEIU Local 74 mourns the loss of the esteemed President Emeritus of SEIU Local 3036 (the Taxi Driver Union). Ben dedicated over 40 of his 83 years to the labor movement. He cared deeply about his members and was devoted to his family. Our condolences go out to his son Larry, daughter Cindy, and to his grandchildren Leah, Jack and Phoebe. Jack Ryan, President
