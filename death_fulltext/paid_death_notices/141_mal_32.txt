
        BABINGTON-David H.A. 62, died recently in London after a brief illness. He is survived by his wife Ann and their children, Katharine and Philip. His career as an investment banker and financial consultant spanned over three decades in Frankfurt, Paris and London. A memorial services will be held on Friday, November 6 at 3:30PM at St. George Church, Campden Hill, London. Condolences may be expressed to Ann Babington, Flat 2, The Mount, 76 Bedford Gardens, London W8, England.
