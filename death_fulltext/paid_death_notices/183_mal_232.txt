
        BARNETT-Dr. Lester A. April 18, 2002. The Boards of Trustees, members of the Medical and Dental Staff and employees of Monmouth Medical Center and Monmouth Health Care Foundation are deeply saddened by the passing of Lester A. Barnett, MD, a distinguished surgeon and ardent supporter of the hospital. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family and many friends. A memorial service for he and his wife will be held Wednesday, May 15 at Hollywood Golf Club in Deal at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Monmouth Health Care Foundation, 300 Second Ave, Long Branch, NJ, 07740 to the Lester A. Barnett, MD Education and Research Fund.
        BARNETT-Lester A. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Lester A. Barnett. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Paul M. Weltz, President Burton J. Esrig, Secretary
