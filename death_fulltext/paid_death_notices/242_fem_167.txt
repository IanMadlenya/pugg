
        COHEN--Selma. After a long illness and just prior to her 94th birthday, Selma passed away on the morning of March 21, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Harris (Harold), devoted mother of Richard and Paula, loving grandmother of Jonathan, Lisa and the late Harris and adored greatgrandmother of Jake and Benjamin. Her passions beyond family included her professional life as a self employed Interior Designer and Decorator for over 60 years and her love of and excellence in the world of Duplicate Bridge. Her determination and courage led her to overcome enormous obstacles after the premature death of her husband almost 50 years ago. Graveside service.
