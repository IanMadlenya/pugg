
        SOLOMON-Samuel M. Died at home on February 13, 2001. Following his WWII service in the South Pacific, he dedicated his professional life to supporting the economic development of Israel. He had served as: National Director of Institutional Sales of Israel Bond Drives; Director of the Eastern Region of the Israel Bond Organization; and as Executive Vice President of the American Friends of Tel Aviv University. In 1984 the State of Israel Bond Organization presented him with the Gates of Jerusalem Award in appreciation of 30 years of dedicated service on behalf of Israel's economic growth. He is survived by his devoted wife Alexandra, daughter Valery Luce Cristol, brothers Herbert and Adrian, nieces nephews and their families. Services Friday 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St & Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his name to Jacob Perlow Hospice would be appreciated.
