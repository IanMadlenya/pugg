
        KAPLAN-Abraham R. Of Sands Point, NY, and Pomano Beach, FL, died Saturday, December 12, 1998, in Florida, in his 90th year. He was predeceased by his wife of 61 years Bertie Kaplan. He is survived by his wife Barbara Janoff Kaplan, two sons Morton and Don Kaplan, both of NY, and four grandchildren Gregory, Jennifer, David and Lynn. Funeral service Monday, at noon, Sinai Chapel, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY, 718445-0300. Burial at Mr. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
