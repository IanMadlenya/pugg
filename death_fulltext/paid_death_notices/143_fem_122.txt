
        SIEGEL-Marsha (Furman). Beloved wife of Sidney H. Adored mother of Don, Monica, Naomi (Duval) and Mindy (Ohringer). Loving grandmother of Jonathan and Lesley Siegel, Claire and Grant Miner and Noah Ohringer. Devoted aunt of Beverly Katzman, Alan Rems, Linda Simon, Martin Furman, Kathy Schiff and Bonnie Sachs. Caring sister-in-law of Pauline Sachs. Loving motherin-law of Richard Ohringer. Psychologist, poet, violinist, art and opera lover. Staunch Democrat, strongly committed Jew. We treasure all of her magnanimous gifts to us. Her tremendous love and deep wisdom will be with us all the days of our lives. Services Tuesday, 1:30 PM, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Tpke., Woodbury, LI.
