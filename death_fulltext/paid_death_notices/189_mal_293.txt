
        WERTHEIMER - Lothar, M.D. Physician and friend, he taught and practiced his profession with dedication and respect for each and every human being in New York City's municipal hospitals and private practice for half a century. Survived by his loving wife, Antonia, and by Ellen, Robert, Michelle, Eddie, Lisa, Elliot, Christopher and Serena, to all of whom he was deeply devoted and who now mourn his passing. Admired and loved by many, he will long be remembered by all who knew him. Services Thursday, 11AM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. N.Y. Donations may be made to the Simon Weisenthal Center, 1399 South Roxbury, Los Angeles, CA. 90035
