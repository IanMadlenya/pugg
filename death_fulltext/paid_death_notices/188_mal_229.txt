
        YOUNG-Eliot, M.D. Of Somers, N.Y., formerly of Milton, MA. On September 19, 2002. Dr. Young, who practice in Quincy, MA., was a cardiologist on the faculty of Harvard Medical School for many years. He was an attending physician at Brigham and Women's Hospital and the Deaconess Hospital in Boston. Dr. Young is survived by his loving wife of 51 years, Dorothy, his caring sister Eleanor Tankel and three beloved children, Susan Geringer, Dr. Lawrence Young, Beth Goettisheim, and seven loving grandchildren. Donations may be sent to the Yale Cardiovascular Research Fund, 135 College Street, Room 301, New Haven, CT. 06510-8056.
