
        PARKER-Phillip. Beloved father of Alan and Carole, grandfather of Sophie and Michael, widower of the late Sylvia Zimet Parker, husband to Rose Lerner Parker, died peacefully at his home in Florida on February 6. Interment occurred on February 8 in New York. His family and friends will mourn him and miss him. Contributions in his memory can be made to the American Cancer Society or the National Kidney Foundation.
