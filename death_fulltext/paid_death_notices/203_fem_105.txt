
        GREBE--Sylvia T. Sylvia Thresher Grebe, 96, died December 10th in Hamden, CT. Daughter of artist Brainerd Bliss Thresher and Mary Colby Thresher and an artist herself, she was born December 21, 1906, in Dayton, Ohio and married to the late Dr. Frank Grebe, minister, Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church. She is survived by her daughter, Cynthia Lang, of Cambridge, MA, 5 grandchildren & three great - grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Stephen C. Grebe Memorial Scholarship, American University, 4400 Massachusetts Avenue NW, Washington, D.C. 20016.
