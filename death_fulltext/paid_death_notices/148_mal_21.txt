
        GOLDSHOLL-Leon Harold, 79. On May 3, 1999. Of Rego Park, NY. Devoted husband of Evelyn for 53 years, beloved father to Ann Lynn Varsano and David Goldsholl, adoring grandfather of Joshua, Douglas and Jamie. Raised in Bronx, NY and Springfield, Mass., before graduating from DeWitt Clinton High School, with his father Abe and brother Saul, he founded Ann Revere Lingerie. From 1960 to 1990 he was a registered Representative, concluding his career with Gruntal and Company. In retirement, he was an arbitrator for the NASD and New York Stock Exchange. He was a member of the American Arbitrators Association. Memorial donations may be sent to the ACLU.
