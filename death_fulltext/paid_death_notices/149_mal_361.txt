
        COUGHLIN-Thomas F. On June 20, 1999. The Board of Directors, administration, staff and residents of Isabella Geriatric Center in New York deeply mourn the passing of Thomas F. Coughlin, our former President and Chief Executive Officer from 1977 to 1992. His contributions to Isabella will be long remembered and he will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy and condolences to his daughters Barbara Damon-Hill, Carol McKay and his entire family. Richard B. Harding, Jr., Chairman Mark J. Kator, Pres. and CEO
