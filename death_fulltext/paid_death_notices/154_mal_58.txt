
        KRAUSS-Mitchell. 68 of Westfield, NJ. Mr. Krauss was the co-owner of NJ's oldest advertising agency, J.M. Kesslinger & Assoc. of Union, NJ, and an active leader in the advertising industry in the state. He is survived by his wife Joy, son Bill (Stephanie Voss), daughters Susan and Caroline (John) Bogart, brother Harold and sisters Hannah Walitsky and Sylvia Friedman. Services at Temple Emanu-El Westfield, NJ on Sunday, November 7 at 1:30pm. For information, call Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Rd., Union, NJ
