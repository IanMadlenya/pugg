
        FENSTER--Milton. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Milton Fenster, on June 16, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel on Wednesday, June 18, at 11:15 AM. (212-362-6600).
