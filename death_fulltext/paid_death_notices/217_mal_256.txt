
        CORWIN--Irving J. February 4, 1924--February 12, 2005. He was a model husband, father and friend who will not be forgotten. He was deeply generous and aware of the abundance of his life. As a partner with the firm Helfat & Gross, he was more than just an accountant to his clients. He is survived by his wife Rita, daughters and grandsons. ''You're alright kid''.
