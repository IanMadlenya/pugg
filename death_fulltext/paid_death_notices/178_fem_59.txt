
        WEXNER-Bella. The Israel Education Fund of UJC and the Jewish Agency mourns the death of Bella Wexner. A Jewish leader and philanthropist who helped build projects in Jerusalem and Israel. A true leader in philanthropy. We send our condolences to her daughter, Susan Wexner, and to the family. Terry Rubenstein, President-IEF Yitzchak Shavit, Vice Pres.-IEF
        WEXNER-Bella. Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., expresses its deep sorrow at the passing of Bella Wexner, beloved wife of Harry of blessed memory, cherished mother of Susan & Leslie, and adoring sister of the late Dr. Izador I. Cabakoff. A life member and generous benefactor, Bella was devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her legacy will live on through her good name. She will be missed by all who knew her. Bonnie Lipton, National President
        WEXNER - Bella. Congregation Orach Chaim expresses its heartfelt condolences to our devoted member, Susan Wexner, on the loss of her beloved mother a blessed memory. Founder of The Limited and of the Wexner Foundation, Bella Wexner was the epitome of an Aishet Chayil and matriarch of a distinguished family in Israel. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Michael D. Shmidman, Rabbi Wolf Scheck, President
