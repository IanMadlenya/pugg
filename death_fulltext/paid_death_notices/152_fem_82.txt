
        UNGER-Shirley, died peacefully at home on August 7 after a long illness. She was preceded in death by her husband, Richard Unger, and is survived by her brother, Melvin Zimet, and her nephews, John and Paul Zimet. Mrs. Unger was a former president of the New York Metropolitan Chapter of the National Association of Housing and Redevelopment Officials. She is remembered by her relatives and friends for her generosity, wisdom, devotion to her loved ones, and the fullness with which she lived her life. A memorial service celebrating her life will be held at 12 Noon, on September 18, at La MaMa Annex, 66 East 4th St. (between 2nd and 3rd Ave.)
        UNGER-Shirley. The Talking Band deeply mourns the loss of Shirley Unger, a longtime member of our Board of Directors, a passionate theatergoer and a patron of the arts.
