
        DUBITZKY-Simcha (Motro). Of Miami Beach, FL. Widow of Aaron Dubitzky. Died September 25, 1998. She was born in Israel and had lived most of her life in the United States. She leaves a son, Theodore Dubitzky and his wife Deborah in Israel; two daughters, Ann Cope and her husband Bernard of West Hartford, CT, and Aviva Budd and her husband Martin of Stamford, CT; two brothers and two sisters; eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. The burial was Monday in Beth El Temple Cemetery, Avon, CT.
