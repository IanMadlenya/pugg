
        ADLER-Irma S. Of Charleston, SC, formerly of Brooklyn. Died on November 28, 2000, of post operative complications. Devoted wife of Willy for 56 years, beloved mother of Roger, Vicki, and Lauri. ''Grammy'' to Aimee, Jessica, Melissa, Matthew, and Kristin, great grandchild, Julia. A graduate of NYU Class of 1944, she devoted her life to her family's well being. She will forever be missed by those who loved her. Shiva Saturday evening and all day Sunday at 10 Clinton Street, Brooklyn, NY.
