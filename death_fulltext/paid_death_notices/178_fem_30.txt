
        GOLDSTEIN-Augusta L. Passed away on October 28, 2001. Born October 18, 1916 in New York City, where she lived for all but several years during WW II when her work with the Federal government took her to Washington, DC. Augusta had a long and proud career as Executive Secretary to the Hon. D. N. Edelstein. Augusta loved to do crosswords, play Scrabble, visit with friends and pursued interests in art and interior decoration. Augusta traveled extensively, both vacationing abroad and visiting family around the U.S. She was also active in HIAS. August was predeceased by her parents Sam and Sadie as well as her brothers Sidney, David and Phillip. Survivors include her sister-in-law Selma Goldstein of Reno, NV, and many cousins, nieces, nephews, grand nieces and grand nephews. A graveside service will be held at 1 PM Friday, November 2, at Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Please call 800-522-0588 for directions.
        GOLDSTEIN-Augusta. October 28, 2001. In memory of a loyal, longtime and dear friend who will be sorely missed. Signed with love and tears. Betty Ellerin, Selma Pundyk, and Jean Roberts
