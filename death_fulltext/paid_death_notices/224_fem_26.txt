
        JORDAN--Janith, age 62, on August 24, 2005. Metropolitan College of New York and its Board of Trustees mourn the passing of its Vice President and longtime colleague, Janith Jordan. She was one of the pioneers of the College and worked closely with our founder, Audrey Cohen, on the development of PurposeCentered Education. Janith's contributions to the College are incalculable and she will be missed by all of us. A memorial service will be held at MCNY on Friday, September 16, 2005 at 11am. Stephen R. Greenwald, Pres. John Rodgers, Chair of the Board
