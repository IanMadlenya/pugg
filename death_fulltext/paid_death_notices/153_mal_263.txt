
        SCHUMAN-Jack. 80, retired Executive Vice President of The Fink Baking Corporation, after a long and valiant struggle. A leader in the food service industry for over 50 years, he leaves behind his devoted wife, Rita, and beloved daughter, Mady, both of New York City. Service Friday 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Visiting hours Thursday 6-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Care.
