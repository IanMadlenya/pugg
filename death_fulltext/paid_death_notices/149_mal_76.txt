
        DOUGLAS-John A. 74. Died suddenly April 12, 1999 in Naples, FL. He is survived by his beloved wife Donna; seven children; seven grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. He was co-owner and CEO of Colonial Art Decorators, Inc. for 40 years and served on the Board of the Master Painters Association of America. A memorial service is being held on Saturday, June 12, 1999 at the Holy Family Church in New Rochelle.
