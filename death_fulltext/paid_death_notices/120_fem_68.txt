
        VERRA - Sr. Mary Basil. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, College of Mt. St. Vincent, on Friday, January 10, 197. Former school teacher at St. Mary's, Rosebank, Staten Island. Volunteered for the last 11 years at Incarnation school. survived by a sister Lucy Cattaro and 2 brothers John and Santo Verra. Also survived by many nieces, nephews, grand and great nieces and nephews. Predeceased by her brothers James, Joseph, Gandolfo and Anthony and a sister Concetta Consoli. Reposing at the Convent of Mt. St. Vincent, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx Saturday 6-8 PM and Sunday 1-8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Monday 10 AM in the Chapel of the Immaculate Conception at the College of Mt. St. Vincent. Interment following at St. John's Cemetery Middel Village, NY.
