
        BUNKER-Mavis McGuire. Born December 24, 1915, New York City. Daughter of Mr. & Mrs. Thomas Burt McGuire. Died at home surrounded by family in New York City, August 4, 2002. Beloved mother of Charles Sheffield Bunker, Sheila Nickerson and Mavis Taintor. The sister of Gerald McGuire and the late Burt McGuire, Bradford McGuire, and Robert McGuire and the late Virginia Von Keller and Emma Miller. Devoted grandmother of six and great-grandmother of three. She was predeceased by her son Peter Cantine Bunker. Funeral services will be held at St. Thomas More's Church, 65 E. 89th St., 10 AM Tuesday, August 6. The family requests that in lieu of flowers donations be made to the Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60th Street, NY, NY 10022.
