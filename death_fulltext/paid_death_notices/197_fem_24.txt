
        WEINGARTEN--Nikki. May 21, 2003. Wife of the late Herbert. Despite sickness and adversity, Nikki had an unflagging spirit and lively intellectual curiosity. Her warmth and engaging personality were the rewards for those who knew her. She leaves her sister Selma, niece Barbara and her husband Mannie, grandniece Gina and dear friends Marie and Joe. Donations to Glaucoma Association of New York. >>AD#
