
        SAYRES-Ruth H., 90, died on Monday, Jan. 6, 1997 in Greenwich, CT. Widow of Philip C. Sayres, she is survived by Barbara S. Williams of Greenwich, Philip C. Sayres, Jr of East Haddam, CT., and 4 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held February 15 at 11:00 AM at The First Congregational Church of Old Lyme, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the Nature Conservancy, 55 High Street, Middletown, CT. 06457 or to The Country School, 341 Opening Hill Road, Madison, CT. 06443.
