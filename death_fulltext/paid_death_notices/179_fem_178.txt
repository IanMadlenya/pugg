
        FREY-Helen T. Retired past President of the Flatbush Federal S&L Association of Brooklyn. Died December 25, 2001. She had been with the Association from 1941 and served as President from 1957 to 1987. She is survived by two sisters, Florence M. Becker and Dorothy J. Murray, niece Patricia M. Sheehan and nephew George W. Becker. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn, N.Y.
