
        MAILLARD-Dr. Leslie Woodrow. On November 29, 1997. Former Chief of Proctology at Flushing Hospital. Respected and loved by his patients and peers. World War II Lieutenant Colonel, Physician for the Pacific Theater Air Corps. He will be missed by his wife Peggy and sons Steven and Robert, and their families. Also survived by his six grandchildren and four great-grandchidlren who all loved and respected their ''Pop-pop.'' In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, Monday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Religious service 8 PM, at the funeral home.
