
        GUTTAG--Irwin. The Chancellor, Board of Trustees, staff and faculty of Syracuse University mourn the passing of Irwin Guttag, alumnus, generous benefactor, Trustee Emeritus, former member of the Corporate Advisory Council and former member of the College for Human Development Board of Visitors. Our deepest sympathy to his wife, Marjorie Vogel Guttag, his daughter, Addie, his son, John and their families.
