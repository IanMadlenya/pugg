
        HACKER-Seymour. Died on December 19th, 2000. Dearly beloved husband of Linda Hacker, dearly beloved father of Melissa Hacker & Emily Hacker. Beloved father-in-law of Anne Keating. Dearest grandfather of Olivia Ruth Hacker-Keating. Dearest brother of Laura Lawrence and uncle of James B. Dwyer IV and Maggie Dwyer. He was the last of the great art booksellers. He was friend and father to all who knew him and a great soul. Live in our hearts forever. Service today at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 2 P.M.
