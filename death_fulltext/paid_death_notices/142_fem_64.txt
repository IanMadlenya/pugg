
        NEWMAN-Anne B. Age 63, died peacefully at her home in Scarsdale, NY, on November 7, 1998. Beloved wife of Dr. Herbert A. Newman. Cherished mother of David, Peter and Tracy. Devoted grandmother of Michael. Sister of Ellen Davis. Anne was a caring, compassionate, special person whose motto was ''That's what friends are for.'' She will be sorely missed. Services private. Memorial contributions may be made to Corporate Angel Network, 1 Loop Rd., White Plains, NY 10604.
