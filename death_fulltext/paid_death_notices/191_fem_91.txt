
        KRULWICH - Beatrice Sloane. Beloved wife of the late Irvin. Loving mother of Andrew, Jeffrey and the late Theodore. Cherished motherin-law of Linda. Adoring grandmother of Allison, Benjamin, Joshua, Michael, Thea Krulwich, and Jonathan, Jennifer and Andrew Pingle. Great grandmother of Jack, Hayden and Annie Pingle. Treasured aunt to many nephews and nieces. Graduate of Hunter College and NYU Law School, past president of Manhattan Chapter of Technion, and Sisterhood of The Jewish Center. Services at Riverside Chapel, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave., 11:30 AM on December 10th. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Technion, 810 7th Ave, N.Y., N.Y. 10019.
