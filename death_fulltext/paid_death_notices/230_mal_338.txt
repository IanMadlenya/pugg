
        TENNEY--Daniel G., Jr. The Trustees, the Women's Board and the staff of The Boys' Club of New York express their deepest sympathy to his wife, Connie and the family of their dear friend. He was elected to the Board of Trustees in 1950 and gave unsparingly of his time and talents to help many thousands of boys to a better life. His generosity, good humor and commitment to the welfare of youth will be remembered by us all with deep affection. As friend, supporter and Trustee, Dan embodied The Boys' Club of New York's core principles of character, civility and community. The Boys Club of New York
