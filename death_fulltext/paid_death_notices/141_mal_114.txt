
        GABLER-Charles Kopel. We mourn the passing of our dad and grandfather, Charles Kopel Gabler, age 83, of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Woodside and the Bronx, on October 2, 1998. Linotype operator with the New York Times for 30 years. Husband of the late Mary (Malakoff) Gabler for 54 years and recently of Marlene Gabler. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association. We will miss him. Rita, Ted and Elena Rover
