
        HEDDEN-Mabel Minton., 94, died peacefully November 27, 1999 at her home in Walnut Creek, CA. She and her late husband Stuart Hedden, lived in New Milford, CT, Vero Beach, FL, and Orinda, CA before settling in Walnut Creek. She will be missed by her four grandchildren, Stuart Hedden Green of Redding, CT, Thomas Allen Green of San Francisco, CA, Susan Green of Washington, DC, and Douglas Arthur Green of Clark Fork, ID, as well as her eleven great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by her husband, Stuart Hedden, her brother, Arthur Minton, and her daughter, Margot Woodard. At Mrs. Hedden's request, there will be no formal funeral services.
