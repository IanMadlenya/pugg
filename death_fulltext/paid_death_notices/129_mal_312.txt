
        POPKIN-George. On October 26, 1997. Attorney at law. Adored husband of Anne. Loving father of Susan Wadsworth and Lenny Popkin. Beloved grandfather of Rebecca Diallo. Cherished brother of Goldie Warren and Joseph Popkin. Beloved of Charles Wadsworth, Joseph Warren, Penny Popkin, Ibrahima Diallo and Carol Tristano. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday 6-8 PM, with service Wednesday, 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the Anti-Defamation League, 823 United Nations Plaza, NYC 10017, or the organizations with which his children are associated: The Lennie Tristano Jazz Foundaton, PO Box 30273, NYC 10011 and Young Concert Artists, Inc., 250 W. 57 St., NYC 10107.
