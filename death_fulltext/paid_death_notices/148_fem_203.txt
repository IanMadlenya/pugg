
        HARFIELD-Marion Bussang. In her 90th year on May 29, 1999. Beloved wife of Henry Harfield for 58 years. Mother of Susan Harfield Leness Peck and the late Ellen Harfield Piel; grandmother of Amanda, Susie and Tony Leness, and Sarah and Katherine Piel. Daughter of the late Mabel and R. William Bussang. Graduate of Emma Willard School '28, Smith College '32, and Columbia School of Journalism '35. Funeral service will be held at Westhampton Presbyterian Church, 83 Meetinghouse Rd., Westhampton Beach, NY 11978 at 4 PM on Monday, May 31. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be made to the church.
