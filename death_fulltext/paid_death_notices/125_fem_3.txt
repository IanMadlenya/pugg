
        ROOS-Julie Ann. Beloved daughter of Roberta and Edwin, loving sister to Andrew and Peter, sisterin-law to Susan and Elisabeth, devoted aunt to Alexander, MacAlister Ethan, Kaile and Emma. Cherished friend of many around the world. Passed away from us to a better place on Tuesday, May 27, 1997. Her smile and courage touched all those who knew her, and will ease the pain of our loss. She will be dearly missed. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The National Marfan Foundation, 328 Main St, Port Washington, NY 11050. Arrangements for a memorial service are being finalized.
