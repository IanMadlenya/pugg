
        VALLE-Michael Benjamin. Born January 5, 1959, raised in Richmond Hill, Queens, died March 25, 2001, a resident of Los Angeles. Wordsmith, playwright, screenwriter, beloved son, brother and friend. He recently sold his blockbuster script, ''Sherlock Holmes and the Vengence of Dracula'' to Columbia Pictures.
