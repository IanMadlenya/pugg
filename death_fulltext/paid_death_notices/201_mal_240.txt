
        BENNIS--Leslie Jacob. 87 years old, died on October 23, 2003. Beloved husband of Florence and the late Marion Bennis. Devoted father of Robert and Judith and fatherin-law of Marion, Audrey and Beverly. Loving step-father of Stephen, Nancy and Karen. Cherished grandfather of Alyssa, Jonathan, Jenn, Matthias, Zachary, Richard, Heather, Jennifer and Ellen. Dearest great-grandfather. Services Monday, 10 AM, at Riverside-Nasau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Interment at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. Donations in his memory may be made to Kornreich Technology Center at the National Center for Disability Services, Albertson, NY.
