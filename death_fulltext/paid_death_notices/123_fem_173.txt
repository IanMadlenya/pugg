
        VANDERRYN-Henriette, S.E. On April 23, 1997, at her home in Great Neck, New York. Beloved wife of the late Herman G. Vanderryn. Mother of Frederika Danziger, Jack Vanderryn, Sim Van der Ryn and Heleen Kurk. Also survived by her nephew, Herman Hartog of the Netherlands, one sister, fourteen grandchildren, eight great grandchildren and two grand nephews. A remembrance service will be held Friday, April 25, at 12 noon ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapel,'' Great Neck, New York. Interment immediately following at Mount Eden Cemetery, Valhalla, New York. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Metropolitan New York Coordinating Council on Jewish Poverty.
