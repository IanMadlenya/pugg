
        DULWORTH--Vera Reesor. Born April 21, 1901 Kevil, Kentucky. Died August 20, 2003, Jackson, Michigan, age 102. An extraordinary woman. Her children are Anita Burck, Jack, Bill, Jim, Edward, and Carolyn Sue, deceased 1934. She was proud of 23 grandchildren, 55 great-grandchildren and two great-greatgrandchildren. Services and burial August 23, Hudson, Michigan next to husband Zachary Taylor Dulworth.
