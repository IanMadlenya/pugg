
        HARRIS-Bernard C., beloved husband, father and grandfather, died September 2nd in Pentwater, Michigan, at the age of 82. Born in Victoria, British Columbia, on October 12, 1914, he is survived by his wife of 50 years, Charlotte; five children, William of Stamford, CT, Thomas of Tucson, AZ, Charles of New York City, Mary Pat of Virginia Beach, VA, and Robert of Virginia Beach, VA; and eight grandchildren. Mr. Harris was the Founder and Chairman of the Bernard C. Harris Publishing Company, Inc., a directory publisher with headquarters in White Plains, New York. Funeral services were held in Pentwater, Michigan, on September 5th. A memorial service in the New York area will be held at 11:00 A.M. on Friday, September 12th, at Sts. John and Paul Church in Larchmont, New York. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be sent to Hospice of Mason County, 10 Atkinson Drive, Suite 3, Ludington, Michigan 49431.
