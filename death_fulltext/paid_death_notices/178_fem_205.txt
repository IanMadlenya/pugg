
        McKENNA-Catherine. November 19, 2001. Native of County Monaghan, Ireland. Beloved sister of the late Mary McEneany. Loving aunt of Sr. Mary Carmel, O.P., Kathleen Singer, Edward J. McEneany and Sr. Barbara McEneany, O.P. Also survived by several nieces and nephews in Ireland and England. Family will receive friends in the Chapel of Frances Schervier Nursing Home, 2975 Independence Ave., Bronx, Wednesday at 11:00 AM where a Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 12:00 noon. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Donations in her memory may be made to Sisters of St. Dominic Retirement Fund, 496 Western Hwy., Blauvelt, NY 10913.
