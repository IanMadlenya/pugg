
        BEHR-Hilda. Born July 9, 1902 in Limersheim, Germany; died July 29, 2001 at Anchin Pavilion, Sarasota, FL. Beloved aunt, grandaunt and great-aunt. She was industrious throughout life and was very devoted to family. She was a lifetime member of the Great Neck chapter of Hadassah, where she was a regular volunteer. She fled Nazi Germany in 1939 and lived in Great Neck until 1999. She will be sorely missed by her nieces and nephews and all those whose lives she touched.
