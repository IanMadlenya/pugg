
        JOSEPH-Maxine Stern. Age 81, passed away February 4, 2000 after a brief illness. She was the Medical Director for the Methadone program for the New York State Narcotics Addiction Control Commission. She was the beloved wife of the late S. Seymour Joseph, M.D. Loving mother and motherin-law to Marilyn Joseph and Warren Regelmann, cherished grandmother of Adam Gustave Regelmann and David Joseph Regelmann. Devoted sister to the late Frederic Stern, also survived by dear sister-in-law Diane Stern. Memorial services will be held in St. Paul, MN. Contributions are preferred to the Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Association in memory of her late husband.
