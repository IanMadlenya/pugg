
        LEEDS-Richard C. On April 2nd, 2002, from complications of a kidney biopsy at age 85. ''Dick'' was a member of the New York Mercantile Exchange from 1940-1991, was Vice Chairman and served on Board of Governors for 20 years. Known for his wit, charm, and joy, and love of life, music and sports. May he rest in peace. Survived by the love of the life of his wife, Rhoda. Loving father of Douglas and best buddy to Robert. Dear brother of Julian. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street, Tuesday, April 9th, 11:30 AM. Burial to follow at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery.
