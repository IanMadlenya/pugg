
        MOSS-Greg (Gimpy Moskowitz). Died January 28, 2002, in Los Angeles, at 75 years of age. Loving husband of Lois. Devoted brother and brother-in-law of Morris and Dorothy, Ruth and Al Brodt, Rosalie and Clement Ripa, Hillard and Rosalie, Helen Zimmer, Dennis and Evelyn, Sandra and Perry Berg, Barbara and Bill Wallace, Jeffrey and Cheryl. Predeceased by brother, Raymond, sister, Gertrude Calem, and brother-in-law, Abram Zimmer. Caring uncle of 38 nieces and nephews and 28 greatnieces and nephews. He was a constant source of inspiration for his family, friends and colleagues. Our loss can never be filled.
