
        PHILLIPS--Giovanna December 31, 1913 -May 27 2006 died peacefully after a short illness at her home in West Palm Beach. A native of Florence, she worked as a free-lance writer and was active in charities in New York and Palm Beach. She is survived by her sons Anthony and Guy, her daughter Beatrice Ballard, and two grandchildren. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated at St Juliana Church in West Palm Beach on Wednesday June 7 at 11 AM. Please send donations in her memory to the Rehabilitation Center for Children and Adults, 300 Royal Palm Way, Palm Beach FL 33480.
