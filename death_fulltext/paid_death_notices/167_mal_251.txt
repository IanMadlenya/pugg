
        EDSTROM-Richard. 63, industrial designer, recipient of an IDESA and three CLIO awards, died Sunday, December 17, 2000 of cancer. He held patents for numerous mechanical devices. His glass bottles designed for major products, including Listerine and Murphy's soap, are utilized today and were featured in an article in Smithsonian Magazine. This beloved, kind, spiritual, gentle man with quiet dignity and special humor will be missed by his many friends and associates. He is survived by his beloved wife Wendy, his mother and three sister.
