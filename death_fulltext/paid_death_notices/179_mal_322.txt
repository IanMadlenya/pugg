
        EVANS-John Arthur, M.D., FACR, Emeritus Professor of Radiology, Radiologist-in-Chief and Chairman of the Department (1952-1976), died on December 24, 2001 at the age of 92. Beloved husband of Anne Wilson Evans. Father of Linda Franklin, Jane Carreyrou, John T. Evans, Susan Evans Sterling. Grandfather of nine. Great-grandfather of three with his deceased wife Dorothy Riley Evans. He was also stepfather of Suzie Odell and Alison Odell Smedley. A private burial will be held at a later date. Memorial donations may be made to Mashomack Preserve-Nature Convervancy, Shelter Island, New York 11964, or to Memorial Tree Fund, Gardiner's Bay Country Club, Shelter Island, NY 11965.
