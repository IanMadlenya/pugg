
        TOAN-Arthur B. Jr, 84, of Saddle River, NJ, died Tuesday, July 13, 1999. Magna Cum Laude Graduate of Dartmouth College Class of '36. Retired National Director of Management Advisory Services and Partner of Price Waterhouse Coopers. Author of more than 75 articles & three books on management oriented topics. Survived by his wife Ethel and children Marion Jones and Donald, two grandchildren. Memorial Service will be held August 7, 1999, at 10 AM at the Unitarian Society of Ridgewood, Ridgewood, NJ. For information, contact C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, Ridgewood, NJ 201-445-0344
