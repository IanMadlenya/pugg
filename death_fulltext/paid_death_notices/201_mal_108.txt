
        LASKIN--Benjamin. Age 94. Of NYC, NY. Died October 11, 2003. He served his country in World War II/US Air Force. ''Although he was an educated and knowledgeable individual, what he valued most was his family.'' He is preceded in death by his late wife, Rosemary Laskin. Survivors: two daughters, Barbara Nissenbaum (husband Mark) of Birmingham, Miriam Laskin of New York; grandchildren Joshua and Michael Nissenbaum; sister Ida Handel of New York; and nieces and nephews. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, doantions may be made to the charity of your choice.
