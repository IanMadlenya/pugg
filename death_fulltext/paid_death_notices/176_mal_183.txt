
        HENENFELD-Dr. Milton, 86 years old, of Monroe Township, NJ, died peacefully on September 14, 2001, after a brief illness. Adoring and devoted father of Nancy Rosenfeld of New York City. Cherished grandfather of Erica Rosenfeld. Loving brother of Florence and Karolyn. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Ruth. He was a prominent podiatrist in NYC for over 50 years, a quiet man of great compassion, a talented tenor, opera lover and avid golfer. Interment private. Memorial service at Concordia in Monroe Township to be announced.
