
        CYTRYN-Jack, at home in his 87th year. Loving husband and best friend of Helen for over 55 years. Survived by his loving and devoted daughters Joy and Jane Cytryn. Father of Allan Cytryn and grandfather of Steven. Devoted son of the late Avram Menachim and Feiga Mindel Cytryn who perished in the Holocaust. Survived by many cousins including Hadassah and Beno Hirschbein who survived the Holocaust. He was a graduate of the University of Poznan. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. 1:30PM Tuesday. In lieu of flowers donations to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
