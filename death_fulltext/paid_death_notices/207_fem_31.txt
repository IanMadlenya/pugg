
        GREENE--Dorrit. On Friday evening, April 2nd, at age 82. Beloved wife of Daniel. Loving mother to Karin Grouf, Sherri, Ronald and Kenneth Greene, and grandmother to Caitlin and Ryan. Mother-inlaw to Diane, sister to Fred Hill, and friend to many. Accomplished doctor of psychology, avid student of all types of education and animal lover. She will be missed. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81st St, Sunday, April 4th, 25 and 7-9 pm, with service Monday, 11:30 am. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Humane Society, the ASPCA or Bide-a-wee.
