
        CHRISTENSEN-Ronald Julius, a celebrated artist and member of the National Arts Club, New York City, died in Naples, Florida, on November 5, 1999. He was 76. During his career, Mr. Christensen had 75 one-man exhibitions. His work is included in many museums and hundreds of private collections. Donations in his memory may be made to the National Arts Club, 15 Gramercy Park South, New York, NY 10003.
