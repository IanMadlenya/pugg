
        BARLOW-Frances (Fran). Wife of the late Meyer (Mac) Barlow. Beloved mother of Seldon and Lydia, Susan and Clive. Very loving grandmother to Paul, Mindy, Michael and Jessica Barlow, and Jill and Eric Sussman. Great-gandmother of Morgan, Aaron, Cori, Mitchell and Justin. Passed away on October 7, 2000. From her family and friends, from the poor and underprivileged, our last goodbye. Private graveside service on Tuesday.
