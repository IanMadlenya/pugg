
        PEARLMAN--Alexander W., M.D. Died August 24, 2004 in Jamaica Estates, NY at age 90. Beloved husband of the late Sarah Birstein Pearlman; loving father of Beth Smith, Deborah Pearlman and Ellyn Shander; father-in-law of the late Lewis Lofsky Smith; cherished grandfather of Ethan and Rachel Smith and Eytan Shander. His life was dedicated to medicine, family and the arts. We will remember and miss him.
