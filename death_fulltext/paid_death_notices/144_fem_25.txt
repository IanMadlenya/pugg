
        DIAMOND-Babette Waldstreicher. 86, of New York City. Died Sunday, January 3. Loving mother of Judy Bergner. Loving mother-inlaw of Bob Bergner. Beloved grandmother of Michael, Eric and Scott Wilker. Great-grandmother of Jordon, Melissa and Samantha Wilker. Beloved sister of Eugene Carlin. Sister-in-law of Lillian Carlin. Also surviving, her husband Irving Diamond. Mrs. Diamond and her late husband, Harold Waldstreicher operated the Babette Shop in Norwalk and Westport, CT, for many years. Private funeral services were held.
