
        LAMPEL-Roy. Age 50, Red Hook, NY, Monday, July 27, 1998, following a traffic accident. He owned several manufacturing businesses in New York and was active in trade associations in the Manhattan garment center. Father of Adam, son of Milton and Sylvia, brother of Neal, brother of the late Susan Lampel Levy, companion of Theresa. Private funeral services were held on Wednesday, July 29, 1998, at Burnett, Rockefeller & Hand Funeral Home, Red Hook, NY, 914-758-5042.
