
        LEWIS--Desiree. April 6. Beloved wife of the late Mortimer B. Lewis. Loving mother of Merry Lewis and Jane Lewis-Nichols and her husband Ned. Fondly remembered by nieces, nephews, cousins and friends for her great wit, charm and generosity. Graveside service Friday, April 8, 1 PM at Mount Zion Cemetery, 59-63 54th Avenue, Maspeth, NY.
