
        PASTORE-Emil M. Died November 5, 1997 in Fla. at age 91. Born in Italy, September 26, 1906 he immigrated to Waterbury, CT in 1912. He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Amherst College in 1928 and received his masters in history in 1929. His career was at the New York Telephone Company and AT&T, retiring as V.P. of New York Telephone in 1966. He is survived by his wife, Virginia S. Pastore, his children Richard Pastore, Susan Rollinson, Grace Boyd and John Pastore, his fourteen grand and twelve great-grandchildren.
