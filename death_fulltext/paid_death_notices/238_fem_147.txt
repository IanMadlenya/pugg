
        KAVANAGH--Mary Joan. Former Director of Wagner Nursing Care, East 58th St., NY, formerly of Bruce, Gorey, Co. Wexford, died November 23, 2006 in Middletown Nursing Home, Courtown, Co. Wexford, Ireland. Deeply regretted by all her family and friends. Funeral Service was held Saturday at River Chapel in Courtown, Ireland.
