
        STAVOLA--Dr. Joseph J., 46, and his beloved wife, Jeanne C. Serocke-Stavola, 44, both of Pelham Manor, NY, passed away on the evening of June 9, 2007. Joe and Jeanne's lives revolved around their family and steadfastly supported and nurtured their sons, James and William. In addition to their sons, they leave behind her aunt Elizabeth Weed, and her husband Rick of Breezy Point, NY; his mother Sylvia Stavola and his sister Beth Ann Stavola, both of Hartford, CT; his brother John Stavola and his wife Patty, of Glastonbury, CT; as well as longtime family friend Ed Rak, of Dennis, MA; and many cousins and dear friends. Visiting hours will be Thursday, June 14th from 2-4 and 7-9pm at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave, Pelham, NY. Mass of Christian Burial on Friday at 11am at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor, NY. Interment St. Charles Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the following organizations: Hawthorne Country Day School, Heartsong, Autism Speaks and The Miracle League of Westchester.
