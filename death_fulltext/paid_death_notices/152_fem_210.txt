
        MEZAN-Edith Rochlin. A highly regarded pianist and teacher, graduate of the Juilliard School of Music and the Teachers College of Columbia University, died Saturday, September 25, in Stamford, CT, where she was born February 21, 1909, and lived her whole life. Her husband, Samuel, a prominent New York attorney, predeceased her in 1987. She is survived by two sons, Dr. Peter Mezan of NYC, and Richard Mezan of Chappaqua, NY, four grandchildren, Adam, Kate, Paul and Hannah, and a sister, Estelle Seely of New Canaan, CT. Funeral service will be held on Tuesday at 11 AM at Temple Beth-El in Stamford.
