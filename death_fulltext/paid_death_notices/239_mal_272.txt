
        MEISTRICH--Philip H. of Teaneck, formerly of Paramus, on December 25, 2006. A veteran of World War II, he was a First Lieutenant in the U.S. Army Air Corp. He was a navigator for the 453rd Bomb Group, 8th Air Force, and received the Distinguished Service Cross and Air Medal. Mr. Meistrich was a leader of the Augenblick Chapter Jewish War Veterans. He was a long time member of the Jewish Community Center of Paramus and Knights of Pythias. He was the Chairman of multiple committees at the Classic Residence of Teaneck. Beloved husband of Claire (nee Fassler) of 49 years. Devoted father of Benn, Matthew, and son-in-law Shaw. Dear brother of Edna Baron. Loving brother-in-law and uncle to nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. Funeral Wednesday, 12:30pm, at Gutterman and Musicant Jewish Funeral Directors, 402 Park Street, Hackensack, NJ.
