
        KATZ-Rabbi Dr. Leon. Religious Zionists of America Mizrachi mourns the passing of a very distinguished and devoted leader of our movement for decades. He served as Chairman of Vaad Lechinuch, founded our branches in Passaic and New Jersey. He was instrumental in attracting to our cause many supporters. We express condolences to his Rebbitzin Rivka and entire family. Rabbi Simcha Krauss, President Isrel Friedman, Executive VP
