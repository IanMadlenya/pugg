
        BYER-Samuel. June 26, 1923 to July 5, 2002. President, Board of Directors, Workmen's Circle MultiCare Center, Vice President International Ladies Garment Workers Union, General Manager/ Secretary Treasurer of Dress Joint Board (Local 89-22-1) and has served on the boards of the Jewish Labor Committee, William Patterson College, Bergen Pines Hospital, former national chairman of The American Veterans Committee. He is survived by Gloria, his wife of 57 years, his sons David and Steven, his daughters-in-law Moira and Joan, his grandchildren Nathaniel and Alexandra, his sister Gloria Kaufman and brother Richard. The family requests donations be made to the Workmen's Circle WeCare Inc. Building Fund, 3155 Grace Ave, Bronx, NY 10469.
