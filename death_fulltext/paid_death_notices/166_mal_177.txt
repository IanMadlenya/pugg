
        ERENBAUM-Merwin. The Israel Education Fund of United Jewish Communites mourns the death of Merwin Erenbaum, a trustee of the Posnack Family Foundation and a friend of Israel, who helped build numerous projects in Israel for the benefit of children and the elderly. We send our condolences to his wife, Beverly, his family and the trustees of the Posnack Foundation. Jane Sherman, President, IEF Yitzchak Shavit, Vice President Marina de Moses, SE Executive Director
