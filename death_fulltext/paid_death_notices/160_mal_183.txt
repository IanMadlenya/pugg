
        KATZ-Jack. The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center records with deep sorrow the passing of Jack Katz, Vice Chairman Emeritus of the Associate Board of Directors. In 1980, he received the Brookdale Medal, the institution's highest honor. Mr. Katz was Chairman of the Cadillac Raffle Campaign and an active member for four decades. Our sincere condolences to his sons Ronald, Steven and Alan, and the entire family. Edwin L. Schulman Chairman, Board of Trustees David I. Schachne Chairman, Executive Committee Hon. Samuel Plotkin Chairman, Associate Board of Directors Alvin I. Kahn, MD, President & CEO Cosmo J. LaCosta Executive Vice President & COO
