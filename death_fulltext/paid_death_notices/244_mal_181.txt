
        LAWRENCE--Jacob (Jack), 83, on May 16. Retired from his veterinary practice to pursue his many passions. Committed to social justice, an avid fisherman, folk dancer and lifelong learner. His endless curiosity and zest for life inspire us all. He had two wonderful marriages, first to Adele and then to Miriam (Friedman). Father to Jon Lawrence (wife Elisabeth), Vicki Blier (husband Malcolm). Stepfather to Rachel Friedman. Grandpa to Stefan and Marika Lawrence, Sam and Zack Blier. Brother of Rachel Lawrence.
