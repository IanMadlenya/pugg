
        SAX-Jules M. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our dedicated past president, Jules M. Sax, known for his integrity and generosity. He and his late wife Rose were Founders of our Minskoff Cultural Center and Day School and played a significant role in its development. To his children, Helen and Dr. Daniel Potaznik and to the entire family, our sincere condolences. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President

        SAX-Julius. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of its esteemed alumni parent, current grandparent and benefactor and extends heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein, Principal David N. Kahn, Ch., Bd of Trustees
