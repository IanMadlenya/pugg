
        TERRY--James H., M.D. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our longtime friend and former colleague, James H. Terry, M.D. Prior to his retirment, Dr. Terry served with great distinction as a member of the hospital's surgical faculty and enjoyed an excellent reputation in the field of head and neck surgery. To his family, friends and all of his loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Richard F. Daines, M.D. President and CEO William P. Winkler, M.D. President, Medical Board George J. Todd, M.D. Chairman, Dept of Surgery Richard A. Marks, M.D. Vice Chairman
