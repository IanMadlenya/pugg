
        CHENE--Armand H. Age 99, on October 17, 2003, passed away suddenly and peacefully. He left behind his wife Raymonde, his children Claude and Aenne, and his beloved grandchildren Jasper and Oliver, as well as a multitude of loving family and friends. A memorial service will be held at Riverside Memorial, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th St, Sunday, October 19, at 11:30 AM.
