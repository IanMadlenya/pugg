
        GREENE--Sylvia. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Sylvia Greene, beloved mother - in - law of Gwen Greene, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation and the Wall Street Division. We extend our deepest sympathies to Gwen and her husband, John, and to their entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
