
        SALAZAR-Carmen Emelia S. September 18, 2001. Beloved daughter of Carmen and the late Carlos Salazar, devoted sister of Miriam S. Leidesdorf and her husband William A. Loving aunt of Alexandra M. S. Leidesdorf. Beloved granddaughter of the late General Marco A. Salazar, who was one of the founders of the Republic of Panama. She was Vice President-Creative Director of the International Bravo Group of Young and Rubicam and will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Wednesday, September 19, 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday, 10 AM, St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave and 66 St.
