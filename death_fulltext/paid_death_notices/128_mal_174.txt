
        EDELSTEIN-Louis. Uncle of Joann Thier, Amy Thier and Alex Martin. He is also survived by his devoted sister-in-law, Sonya Edelstein; the wife of his dear late brother, Samuel Edelstein. Mr. Edelstein was a well known philanthropist. Graveside services were held on September 11th in Philadelphia, PA. Roth-Goldsteins Chapel, Atlantic City.
