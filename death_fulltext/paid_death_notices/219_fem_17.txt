
        HAMILTON -- Madeline Thacher. 89, died in Boston, MA, on April 1, 2005. Born in Minneapolis, MN, brought up in Hackensack, NJ, she raised her family in Ramsey and Englewood, NJ. She moved to Woodstock, VT, where she and business partner Elsa Ross established a real estate and decorating business. She is survived by her daughters Gale Hamilton of New York, Toni Doggett of Boston, MA, three grandchildren, five great-grandchildren and by her sister Mrs. Beatrice T. Patterson of Nokomis, FL. She was precedeased by her brother Russell Thacher. A memorial service will be held in Woodstock, VT, Saturday, April 9, at 11:30 am, at St. James Episcopal Church. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Massachusetts General Hospital Development Fund, 100 Charles River Plaza, Suite 600, Boston, MA 02114.
