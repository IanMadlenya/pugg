
        MAY-Dora died peacefully at her home at the age of 87. A holocaust survivor who with her deceased husband Joe saved her infant son by fleeing into the woods. Her courage and devotion to her family will never be forgotten. Loving mother of Marcia May, Sanford and his wife Louise. Cherished grandmother of Barry and Scott. Devoted aunt of Max Shor. Funeral services Wednesday at 12 noon from Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 76 Road and Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
