
        HOPPER-Henry G., 88. Of Indian River Shores, FL, died December 18th, 2000. He was a resident of Monmouth County, NJ for over 60 years before moving to Vero Beach. He was pre-deceased by his wife of 60 years, Wilma Rose Hopper. Mr. Hopper was retired from the U.S. Trust Company of New York City. Survivors include his daughters Lynn Martin of Ormond Beach, FL; Gail (Tim) Wheeler of Jacksonville, FL, four grandchildren and four great grandchildren. For information: Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach, FL. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice of Indian River County, 1111 36 Street, Vero Beach, FL. 32960, or the charity of your choice.
