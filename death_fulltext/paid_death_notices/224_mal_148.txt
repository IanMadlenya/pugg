
        GABERMAN--Edward. Of Somers, New York, beloved husband of the late Pearl Gaberman, passed away on August 2, 2005 at the age of 91. An avid music lover and accomplished artist, he is survived by his two daughters, Emily Gaberman, and Fredi Shonkoff, their husbands, four grandchildren, and his companion, Joan Machson. Donations to The Art Student's League of NY, 215 West 57 St, NY, NY 10019.
