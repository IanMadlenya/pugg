
        WHALEN-James. The Officers, Executive Board and Members of The Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York, Inc. mourns the passing of our member James Whalen of Kay Mechanical, Inc. Mr. Whalen served as President and Executive Board Member for many years and made a valuable contribution to the plumbing industry. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Kay, his brother Ed and all his bereaved family. Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York Lawrence J. Levine, Pres. John O'Donnell, Exec. Vice Pres.
