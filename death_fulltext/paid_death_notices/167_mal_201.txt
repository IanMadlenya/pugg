
        DONOHUE-Francis T., Major General USAR (Retired) and Assistant District Attorney of Westchester County for 37 years. Mr. Donohue, age 63, died at his home on December 15, 2000. Beloved husband of Virginia (Stiles), devoted father of Valerie Donohue, Suzanne Speichinger and her husband Eric, Douglas Donohue and his wife Courtney and their three children Nicholas, Traynor and Lily. Dear brother of Margaret Slevin. Reposing at the McMahon, Lyon and Hartnett Funeral Home, 491 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains on Sat. and Sun., 2-4PM and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial Mon. 12 Noon at the Church of the Resurrection, Rye, NY. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the Anatol and Arthur Charitable Foundation, 2 Longview Ave., White Plains, NY 10601. Money will be used for cancer research.
