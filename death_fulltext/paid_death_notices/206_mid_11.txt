
        LEVIN--Fanny. Of Great Neck, NY at age 93. Loving wife of the late Milton. Devoted mother of Genie Heller and James Levin and his wife Emily. Adored grandmother of Michael (Rhonda) Heller, Kenneth (Lynda) Heller, Joanna (Billy) Karp, Stephanie (Jaime) Torres, and Alexander Levin. Cherished great grandmother of Matthew, Jordan, Brandon, Ryan, and Joshua. Dear sister of Charlotte Deutsch and Maurice Gruber. Services Friday March 26, 11AM at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite the LIRR), Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers, fruit or food contributions in her memory may be made to the Great Neck Chapter of Hadassah or the Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Bronx, New York.
        LEVIN--Fanny. The Board of Trustees, Administration and staff of Parker Jewish Institute deeply mourn the passing of Fanny Levin, beloved mother of our dear friend and Trustee, James P. Levin. We extend our heartfelt condolences to James and his wife Emily, his sister Gene Heller, their children and grandchildren. May your time of grief be eased by the warm love you shared and the fond memories you hold. Jerry Landsberg, Chairman Board of Trustees Michael N. Rosenblut, President & CEO
