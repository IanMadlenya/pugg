
        CONARD-William C., ''Bill''. 41, of Manhattan on September 13, 1997. Beloved partner of Arthur Fama of Manhattan and Ulster Park, NY, loving son of Thelma Conard of London, OH and adored brother of Patricia, Susan, Linda and Diana all of Ohio. Services and burial were held in Ohio on Wednesday Sept. 17, 1997. Bill will be deeply missed by his many firends and family. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in his memory to GMHC, AMFAR & Holy Cross Episcopal Church, 30 Pinegrove Ave, Kingston, NY 12401. Memorial services are planned at a later date for Manhattan and Kingston.
