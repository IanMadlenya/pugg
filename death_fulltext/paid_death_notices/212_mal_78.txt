
        BIERHOFF--Frederic A., 78, of Pleasantville, NY and Leicester, VT, died peacefully on September 6, 2004. He was the best friend of his wife, Ruth, loving father of Alan and Alison, and cherished grandfather of Rebecca. Fred was a navy veteran of World War II. His parents Alan and Janet and his brother Richard predeceased him. He was a graduate of Ethical Culture Fieldston School, attended MIT and was a graduate of New England College as a civil engineer. Frederic was a man of humor and generosity with the gift of making people from all walks of life feel happy in his presence. A memorial service will take place on September 19 at 2PM at the Ethical Culture Society of Westchester, 7 Saxon Woods Road, White Plains, NY. All friends and relatives are invited to attend. As an expression of sympathy, contributions can be made in his name to his favorite organization: Planned Parenthood Federation of America, 434 West 33rd Street, NY, NY 10001
