
        EPSTEIN-William. Of Manhattan and Shenorock, N.Y. On January 26, 1998. Beloved husband of Janice (Kaufman). Adored father of Carla Silber and loving father-inlaw of Dan. Dear brother of Theodore, the late Dr. Alexander and Lillian Carlish. Brother-in-law of Mary Kaufman and Priscilla Epstein. Cherished grandfather of Rebecca and Abigail. Dear uncle. He was the long time President of Bookazine Co., Inc. and Chairman of the Board of the Jewish Book Council. Funeral service on Wednesday, January 28th, 12:30 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
