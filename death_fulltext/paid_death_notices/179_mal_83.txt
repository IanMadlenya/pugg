
        WAKS-Meyer. Of Delray Beach, FL, on November 30, 2001 at age 89. He is survived by sons, David and Leonard, and grandchildren, Mark Waks, Ruth Bennett, Allison Teger, Michael Teger and Sjoma Fadeev. A longtime teacher and principal in the NYC schools, he retired in 1975. A memorial service will be held in Delray Beach on December 12. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Townsend Harris Forever! Fund, C/O Ted Teck, 25 East 83rd Street, New York, New York 10028.
