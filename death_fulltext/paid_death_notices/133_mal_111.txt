
        DANA-Richard. On February 7, 1998. He wrote and directed radio programs for many years before moving to Young & Rubicam Advertising Agency as a Creative Supervisor. In subsequent years he directed productions and taught communications and acting at Manchester Community College in CT. He then became a professional actor appearing on television, in films, on stage and in commercials. Husband of the late Mildred Dana he is survived by his daughter Barbara Dana Arkin; three grandsons Anthony, Matthew and Adam Arkin, and one great-granddaughter Molly Arkin. Services Tuesday, 12 noon, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
