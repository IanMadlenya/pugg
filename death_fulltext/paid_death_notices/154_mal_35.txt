
        MCMAHON-Kevin Daniel, 38. Of New York City and Los Angeles, CA, passed away on October 31, 1999. He attended Parsons Art School and The School of Visual Arts, and led a successful career as a producer of popular television shows and documentaries. He is survived by his eight sisters and brothers and his parents. A Memorial Mass will be held on Saturday November 6, 1999 at 7:30 P.M. at Sacred Heart Roman Catholic Church in Clifton, New Jersey.
