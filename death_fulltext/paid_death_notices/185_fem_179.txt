
        LANIGAN-Frances Olmsted. Beloved wife of the late John B. Lanigan, died on June 23, 2002, in Exeter, New Hampshire. She was a graduate of Vassar College and was an active volunteer in Irvington, New York, where she lived from 1948 to 1995. Mrs. Lanigan is survived by her three children, Tim of Washington, DC, Susan of Exeter, NH, Bob of Dover, MA., and four grandchildren. Mass of the Resurrection 10AM Wednesday Immaculate Conception Church, Irvington, New York.
