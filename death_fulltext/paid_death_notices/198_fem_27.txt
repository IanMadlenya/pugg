
        SAMUELS--Gertrude. First and for many years the only staff writer for The Times Sunday Magazine, award winning journalist, best-selling novelist (Run, Shelly, Run!), biographer (of David Ben-Gurion), playwright and writer-photographer, on July 3, at the age of 93. Mourned with love and admiration by her son Paul Oppenheimer, and her grandchildren Julie and Ben Oppenheimer. >>AD#
