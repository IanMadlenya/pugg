
        WENGROW -- Alice Hirsh, March 7, 1915-December 25, 2004. Artist, illustrator, art teacher, psychotherapist, life long political activist. Survived by son Bill Hirsh (Pam) of Eugene, OR, daughter Diane Wolman of Seattle, grandchildren: Aaron, Rachel (Dean), Anna and Becky; great grandchildren: Maia and Noah. The world has lost a truly colorful person. She will always be in our hearts.
