
        SCHONBERGER - Lois. The Trustees and staff of The Jewish Museum acknowledge with great sadness the passing of Lois Schonberger. Lois and her husband, Eli, have been generous friends and supporters of the Museum throughout the years, and we have admired her great strength in the face of her recent illness. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Eli and their extended family. Robert J. Hurst, Chairman Joan Rosenbaum, Director
        SCHONBERGER-Lois. The executives of Pace Advertising Agency are deeply saddened by the passing of Lois, wife and soul mate of our former partner and President, Eli Schonberger. She was admired by us throughout a relationship spanning many decades. Our sympathy to the Schonberger family and particularly to Eli who has given new meaning to a husband's devotion.
        SCHONBERGER-Lois. Beloved wife of Eli Schonberger. Mother, grandmother and our dearest friend. We celebrate your life even as we mourn your passing. Rickey, Dick, Jane, Susan, Stanley and Doug.
