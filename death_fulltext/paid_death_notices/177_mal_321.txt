
        SCHWARTZ-Jack. 76, ''Papa Jack'' peacefully on October 22. Beloved husband of Sherrie, his ''Babe''. Devoted and loving father of Jeffrey, Kenneth, Judy and Richard and their spouses Debbie, Betty and Bob. Cherished grandfather to Jason, Matthew, Jessica, Danny, Sam, Alex and Lara. Brother of Norbert (deceased). Born in Austria, migrated by ''Kindertransport'' to England and ultimately Chicago. A true original, he will be remembered for his infinite devotion to his family, wonderful sense of humor and unique style and use of the English language. A man with a whole-hearted zest for life, we will never forget you.
