
        SOLL-Maxwell. The Faculty of the NYU (Medical Center) Psychoanalytic Institute and the members of The Psychoanalytic Association of New York mourn the untimely passing in mid November of Maxwell Soll, M.D., our esteemed colleague. After graduating from the institute in 1972, and moving to Dallas in 1978, he had a creative and distinguished career. Our condolences to the family. Michael Singer, D.O., Director NYU Psychoanalytic Institute Leonard Barkin, M.D. President, PANY
