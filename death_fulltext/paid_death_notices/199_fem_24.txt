
        DONOHOE--Louise F. Died August 3, 2003. Born Cuddy, Pennsylvania, June 11, 1915, she graduated from Bellevue School of Nursing in 1936 and served as captain in the Army Nursing Corps from 1942-46. She was the widow of Charles J. Donohoe and is survived by a son Charles D. Donohoe, M.D., Mission Hills, KS, Louise A. Resor, M.D., Greenwich, CT, Michelle A. O'Reilly, Melville, NY, and nine grandchildren. A memorial mass will be held 11 AM, August 6, at Immaculate Conception Church, East 14 Street, NYC. Donations in her memory may be made to the Salvation Army.
