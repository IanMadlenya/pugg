
        BERGER-Leo V. We were deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Leo V. Berger, a humanitarian who cared deeply for others. His support of both Long Island Jewish Medical Center and North Shore University Hospital was exemplary. Our sincerest condolences are extended to his family. Saul Katz, Chairman, Board of Trustees John S.T. Gallagher, Chief Executive Office David R. Dantzker, President North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System
