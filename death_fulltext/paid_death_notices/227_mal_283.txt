
        KUSHNER--Stanley. In loving memory, at 75 years old, passed away peacefully on Monday, December 26 at his home in Stratton Mountain, VT. After retiring from his life long career as an electrical contractor and President of Kushner Electric Company., he persued his dual passion of golf and skiing. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Barbara, his three children, Cheryl, Caryn and Carl, his two sons in-law Richard Mintzer of Mamaroneck, NY and Peter Davis of Jericho, VT., and his four grandchildren, Ross, Emma, Melissa and Skyler. May he rest in peace and forever in the hearts and minds of all those he loved and all those who loved him. He will be deeply missed. Funeral services will be held at ''Guttermans Inc.'', Rockville Centre, LI at 12 noon on Wednesday, December 28. $(6$)KUSHNER -- Stanley. The Board of Governors of Middle Bay Country Club of Oceanside, NY, on behalf of its membership, wishes to express its sympathy and condolences to the family of its esteemed member and past president, Stanley Kushner. He will be sorely missed.
