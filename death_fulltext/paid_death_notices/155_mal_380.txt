
        BRYANT-C. Willard., 92. In New London, New Hampshire, on Dec. 29, 1999. Formerly of Greenwich, CT. General Electric employee for 47 years. His wife of 58 years, Dorothy (Johnson) Bryant, died in 1993. He is survived by his daughter, Susan B. Chacho, of New London, NH; a grandson, Marine Captain Jonathan D. Foster, of Frankfurt, Germany; a granddaughter, Kimberly F. Hickman, of Knoxville, TN; seven greatgrandchildren and several nieces and nephews. No calling hours. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM Saturday, January 8, 2000 at the Chadwick Funeral Home, 174 Main Street, New London, NH. Services at Christ Church in Greenwich, CT., at a later date.
