
        COSTA-Rena. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rena Costa, a cherished friend of the Jewish community in New York and in Israel, who expressed her devotion to social and humanitarian causes through exceptional philanthropy. We extend our deepest sympathies to Rena's sister, Trudy Gildin, and to all who knew & loved her. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O. Joan Ginsburg, Chair of the Women's Campaign
