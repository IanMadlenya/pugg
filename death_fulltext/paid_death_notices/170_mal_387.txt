
        WALACH-Jeffrey, M.D. The Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart and the Medical Staff of Cabrini Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague & friend who served our institution for many years. Lawrence Ottaviano, M.D., President, Medical Staff Bonnie Simmons, D.O., Medical Director Paul Candino, President and CEO, Cabrini Medical Center
