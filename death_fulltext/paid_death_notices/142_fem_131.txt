
        del Campo-Cristina Erhart, representative and head of Christie's Argentina, passed away on October 19, 1998 in Buenos Aires after a short but valiant struggle with lung cancer. In addition to her position at Christie's, Ms. del Campo was very active as a member of the Multiple Sclerosis Society of Argentina and worked closely with her husband during his career as Minister of the Argentine Embassy in Tel Aviv and then later as Ambassador to Ireland and Sweden. In addtiion to her husband, Cristina is survived by her parents and her children, Angie and Juan Diego, as well as many adoring friends and colleagues. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, November 22, at 2:00 p.m., St. Vincent Ferrer, 869 Lexington Avenue at 66th Street.
