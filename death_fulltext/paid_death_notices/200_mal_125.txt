
        GEORGIS--Ted W., born 1924 in Chicago, died September 8, 2003, in San Diego, CA. He was 78. Ted Georgis is survived by his wife and dearest friend of 50 years, Mitzie of La Jolla, CA; a daughter and two sons: Demetra Bowles Lathrop, and spouse Thomas Lathrop, of San Francisco; Dr. Theodore W. Georgis, Jr., and spouse Tracy, of Rancho Santa Fe, CA; and William T. Georgis, and partner Richard Marshall of NYC. He is also survived by a brother, Nick Georgis of Oakbrook, IL; and five grandchildren. Servicesto be held at 10:30am, Thursday, September 11, at Saints Constantine and Helen Greek Orthodox Church, Cardiff-bythe-Sea, CA.
