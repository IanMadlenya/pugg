
        BROWN-Harold B. Age 70, of Marietta, GA, died Thursday, August 6, 1998. Mr. Brown was a New York native and lived in Marietta for seven years. He was an entrepreneur in the jewelry business. He is survived by his sons, Harold J. Brown and Gerald T. Brown, both of Chattanooga, TN. A memorial service will be held in the family cemetery in Dunlap, TN.
