
        GUASTELLA--Arthur R. 79, of Brooklyn, died, January 31, 2007. He was a US Army Veteran, and a graduate of Columbia University. He was former editor of the ''Bond Buyer'' newspaper. In 1966 he joined Wainwright & Ramsey; later he worked at SmithBarney, and Blyth Eastmen Paine Webber. He was past president of the Financial Writers Assn.
