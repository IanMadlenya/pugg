
        PETERSON-Sharon, died Sat., Feb. 8th. She was a beloved member of the Brooklyn Ethical Society, WBAI Radio and Park Slope Food Co-op. We acknowledge with sadness the passing of Sharon, who so loved life, people and social justice. A medical librarian at Brooklyn Interfaith Hospital, she leaves two sons, David and Kevin, and a multitude of friends. A memorial service will be held today, Feb. 11th, at 1:30PM at the Brooklyn Ethical Society, 53 Prospect Park West. Donations should be sent to Sharon's Fund. For information call 718-499-5802.
