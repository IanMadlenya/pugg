
        ROBERTS-Pamela A. Passed away peacefully, surrounded by friends and family, May 4, 1998. She was a loving mother and a much loved daughter, sister and friend, and a very talented artist. Much admired in L.A.'s underground art community. She is survived, and greatly missed, by her loving companion Dave, her daughter Ava, her sister Cindy, her brother Derek, her mother Lorna and her father Arnold. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name to the American Cancer Society.
