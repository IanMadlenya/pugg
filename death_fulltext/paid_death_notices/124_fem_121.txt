
        O'HARA-Mary W., 58, on May 15, 1997. After a difficult five-year battle with cancer. Died at peace at her home in Bronxville, New York. Director of Special Events at The College of New Rochelle. Educated at College of New Rochelle and Harvard University. Wife of Charles Schramm (predeceased), daughter of Eileen O'Hara of Riverdale, New York and Thomas (predeceased), sister of Thomas and Diane O'Hara, and loving aunt of Sean and Eileen O'Hara. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main Street, Tuckahoe, New York 10707. Visitation will be at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Cedar and Kraft Avenues, Bronxville, New York on Sunday, May 18, from 2-4, 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial celebrated on Monday at St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville at 10 A.M.
        O'HARA-Mary. Sister Dorothy Ann Kelly, OSU, President, and the College of New Rochelle Community are deeply saddened by the passing of Mary O'Hara, devoted alumna and esteemed colleague. Her dedication, warmth and friendship will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to her family.
