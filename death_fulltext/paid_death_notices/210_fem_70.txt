
        BERMAN -- Alvera -''Chickie'', on July 11, 2004. Died peacefully at her home in Sarasota, Fl. Loving wife of the late Edward M. Berman. Devoted mother of Judy (Earle) Kazis, Rick (Elizabeth) Berman and the late Tom Berman. Loving grandmother of Deborah Kazis (Gideon Taylor), Kerry (Brad) Phillips and Tom, Eddie and Molly Berman. Greatgrandmother of Hannah and Naomi Kazis Taylor and Jordan Phillips. Funeral service at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, NYC, on July 14, 2004 at noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Alzheimer's Association would be greatly appreciated.
        BERMAN--Alvera.
