
        RABI--Helen Newmark, 102, on June 18, died peacefully at her home at 450 Riverside Drive. She was the beloved wife of the late Professor Isadore Isaac Rabi, loving mother of Nancy Lichtenstein and Margaret Beels, devoted grandmother of the late Peter, and of Alice, Elizabeth, Jessica and Alexander, and of four great-grandchildren. She confidently gave her children and grandchildren wisdom and guidance that they always cherish. She was the ever strong support of her husband's noble work in science and in public affairs. Interment private.
