
        TONKIN-Celina. On January 11, 1998. Survived by her husband Joseph Tonkin, four daughters, eight grandchildren, one greatgrandchild and many dear friends ... most notably Robert Tompkins. Services Wednesday, January 14, 11 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65 Street. Interment to follow at Mount Neboh Cemetery.
        TONKIN - Celina. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Celina Tonkin, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
