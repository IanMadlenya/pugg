
        GREENBERG-James E. Age 99. Of West Palm Beach, FL. Died Saturday, February 14, 1998. Loving husband of Betty, cherished by Debby and Rusty, his grandchildren Elaine, David, Lorre and Douglas, great grandchildren Daniel, Russell and Vanessa, Stephen and Alice Carb, and their children Brian, Evan, Alison, Daniel, Alexander and Laura. His kindness and warmth will live in the hearts of everyone who knew him. Expressions of sympathy may be made to the Anti-Defamation League of B'nai B'rith.
