
        NIKOLIS-Artie. On June 12, at age 74. A longtime resident of Port Washington, NY, she owned Festival of Food. Beloved wife of the late Peter. Devoted and cherished mother of Bill, Anna, Phillip (Sally) and the late Gary. Loving sister of George Kosefas and dear cousin of George & Mary Mihalakos and many others. She leaves behind her most beloved grandchildren, Faye, Corinne, Patrice, Harry, Anastasia and Alexander. She will forever be in the minds and hearts of those she touched. Funeral service will be held on Thursday, June 14th, at 11:30 AM at St. Paul's Greek Orthodox Cathedral, Hempstead, New York.
