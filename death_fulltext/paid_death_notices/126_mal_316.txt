
        COHEN-Ronald H. On July 27, 1997, beloved husband of Roslyn, devoted father of Sally Cohen Swift, cherished grandfather of Jonathan, Katie, and Elisabeth. Loving stepfather of Janie and Michael and step grandfather to Evan, Josh, and Jonathan. B.A., Amherst College '34; J.D., Northwestern U. '39; U.S. Coast Guard 1942-'46; married Estelle Sakofsky 1947, deceased 1988. N. American Council, Sabena Belgian Airlines 1953-'85. Decorated Officer of Order of Leopold II (Belgium) for U.S. - Belgium Int'l Relations. Funeral Services Friday, 2:30 P.M., at ''Schwartz Brothers,'' Forest Hills, New York.
        COHEN-Ronald. The members, Board of Governors, and staff of Pine Hollow Country Club mourn the passing of our member and good friend. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Roslyn and the family. The Board of Governors Pine Hollow Country Club
