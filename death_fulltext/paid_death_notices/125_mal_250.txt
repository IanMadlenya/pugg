
        STEYER-Roy H. Died at his home on June 25, 1997 after a long illness. Deeply mourned by his beloved wife, Marnie and his sons Hume, James and Thomas. Also survived by his brother Murray, his sister Frances Martinson and nine grandchildren. Retired partner of Sullivan and Cromwell, where he practiced law for over fifty years after service as a prosecutor at the Nuremburg Trials. Honored by all for his integrity, wisdom and courage. Funeral service Tuesday, July 1, 2PM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street, New York, New York. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be sent to the Yale Law School.
        STEYER-Roy. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member, Roy Steyer. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
