
        KATZ--Jerome. On April 24, 2007. Beloved husband of the late Florence, cherished father of Alan and Joyce and Susan and Seth Truwit. Adored ''Pops'' of Marjorie, Brittany, Alison, Elana, Noah, and Jillian. His kindness, wisdom and wit will always be remembered. ''The Room would be quiet and Jerry would be a riot''. Graveside services at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, on Wednesday, April 25, 2007, at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
