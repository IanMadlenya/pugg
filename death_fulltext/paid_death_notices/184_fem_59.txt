
        SCHWEEN-Audrey L., RN, MSN. Pioneer in nursing education, died at age 66 after a long illness on May 1, 2002 surrounded by family and friends. Professionally and privately her skills, generosity, energy, warmth, creativity and sense of humor have brought comfort and happiness to a multitude. She is survived by husband Peter H. Schween, M.D., and daughter Astrid, cellist of the Lark Quartet. Funeral services were held at The Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine.
