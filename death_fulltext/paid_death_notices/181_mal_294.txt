
        MALINO-Jerome R., Rabbi Emeritus of the United Jewish Center, Danbury, CT, died February 25th, 2002 in his 91st year. The Board of Trustees and the congregation mourn the passing of our beloved spiritual leader and friend for 66 years. Funeral service Wednesday, February 27, 2 PM, the United Jewish Center, 141 Deer Hill Ave., Danbury, CT., 203-748-3355. Carleen Blum, President
