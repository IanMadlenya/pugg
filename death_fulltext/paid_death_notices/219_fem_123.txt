
        MARKAY--Dorothy Fish. On April 13, 2005, in Schenectady, NY. Wife of the late Charles H. Markay. Beloved mother of Jesse B. Markay of Albuquerque, NM, and the late David K. Markay and daughterin-law Barbara Scott Markay of Santa Monica, CA. Graduate of Skidmore College, Class of 1940. Retired as Associate Director of Nursing at Long Island Jewish-Hillside Medical Center in 1985. She was a woman of uncommon dignity, wisdom, and warmth.
