
        TERMAN--Bruce, Ph.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is deeply saddened by the tragic, untimely death of its associate professor of medicine (cardiology) and of pathology. An internationally recognized investigator, his findings regarding the molecular regulation of angiogenesis held great significance for the advancement of research in cardiovascular disease and cancer. A member of the Einstein faculty since1999, he was a devoted mentor and enthusiastic collaborator who will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Susan, and the entire Terman family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
