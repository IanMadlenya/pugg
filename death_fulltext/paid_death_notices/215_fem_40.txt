
        KESSEL--Shirley. Widow of ''Life'' magazine master photographer Dmitri Kessel, died November 28, after suffering a massive stroke. She was 77. Her brother Dr. Donald Farmer of Sebastopol, CA, survives her. All her friends are invited to a ''Remembering Shirley'' gathering at The Goat Alley Gallery, Division St, Sag Harbor, NY, on Friday December 17, at 3:00pm.
