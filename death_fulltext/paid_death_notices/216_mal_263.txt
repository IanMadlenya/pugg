
        WENDELKEN--John F. Jr., of Syosset, NY age 66, suddenly on January 18, 2005. Survived by his wife, Brenda, son John F. III, daughters Karen & Susan Wendelken, and grandson, Ethan. Dear brother of Robert, Richard, Gerard, and Helen Wendelken, Rosemary DiGangi, Kathleen Smith, and Anne Marie Schneider, his mother Helen C. Wendelken and twenty nieces and nephews. Before his retirement Mr. Wendelken was Managing Director of the Chemical Bank. Reposing at the Beney Funeral Home, Inc, of Syosset, 79 Berry Hill Road. Visiting Thursday and Friday 2-5 & 7-9pm. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:45am at St. Edward Confessor RC Church, Syosset, NY. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY.
