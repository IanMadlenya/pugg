
        CHIA-Frances. The Board of Trustees of NYU Downtown Hospital joins with the physicians and staff in expressing sorrow at the passing of Frances Chia, a distinguished member of the Board for many years. Her wisdom and generosity will be missed by all. We offer our sincere condolences to her husband, Peiyuan Chia, and to her children, Kathy, Douglas and Candice. Ralph M. Mastrangelo, Chairman Susan Brown Roschen, Vice Chairman Ronald H. Menaker Vice Chairman
