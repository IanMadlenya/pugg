
        MASCOLO-Constance ''Connie.'' Age 79, of Trumbull, CT. A homemaker, died on Saturday, July 29, 2000. Born in NYC and resident of Trumbull, CT, for 32 years. She was the widow of Frank C. Mascolo. Surviving are her daughters, Joan Gershen and husband Joel of Merrick, LI, Carol Golden and husband Byron of White Plains, NY, and Arleen Mascolo and husband David Gould of Trumbull, CT; and three grandsons. Friends may call today, 4-8 PM, Abriola Parkview Funeral Home, 419 White Plains Rd., Trumbull, CT. 203-373-1013.
