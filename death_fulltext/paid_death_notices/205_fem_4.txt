
        FLASTER--Dorothy, 89, of Lake Worth, FL passed away on January 28, 2004, peacefully at home in the loving presence of her daughter Wendie Teya, devoted caregiver Noreen Williams, and expert care of the Hospice of Palm Beach County Green Team. Married 61 years to the late Seymour Flaster (2002), she is survived by her son Jeffrey (Linda) and daughter Wendie Teya (Michael Reichmann). Devoted nana to Caroline (Sam) Taverner, Jason and Joshua. Great grandmother to baby Luca of Barcelona. The funeral service will be held 11 AM Sunday, February 1, 2004 at Levitt Weinstein Memorial Chapel, West Palm Beach, FL. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
