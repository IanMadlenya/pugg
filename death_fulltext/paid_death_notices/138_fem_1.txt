
        HAY-Agnes D'Arcy. In her 90th year on June 29, 1998 of Setauket, Long Island. Miss Hay was the daughter of the late John B. Hay and Agnes D'Arcy Hay. She was a retired school teacher at the Thomas Jefferson High School in Brooklyn. Funeral Service Friday, July 3, 1998, 1PM at the Caroline Church Setauket, L.I. Interment Caroline Churchyard Cemetery. Funeral information contact the O.B. Davis Funeral Homes, Port Jefferson Station, Long Island.
