
        KOLATCH-Esther. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Sisterhood mourn the passing of their senior member and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Fred Distenfeld, President
        KOLATCH-Esther. The officers and staff of The American Jewish Congress mourn the passing of Esther Kolatch, the much-loved and much-admired former Director of our Women's Division. She served with great distinction and devotion for many years. Along with her many friends and family, we will miss her. David V. Kahn, President Phil Baum, Executive Director
