
        SPERBER--Bernard, 92, 1933 graduate of NYU Law School, passed away July 31, 2003 in Boca Raton, Florida. Beloved husband of the late Helen Tobias Sperber. He is survived by daughter, Peggy Flanders of Syracuse, New York; sister Lillian Wechsler of Boynton Beach, Florida; grandsons David and Eric Flanders; five great-grandchildren and one nephew, Dr. Stephen Wechsler. Bernard retired in 1986 as President of Rich Plan of the Southern Tier and remained active his entire life with study, golf and service on the Legal Committee and Board of Boca Greens Country Club. Service at 1:00 PM on Sunday, August 3, 2003 at Mount Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, New York. >>AD#
