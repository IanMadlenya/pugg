
        PUENTE-Tito. With the passing of Tito Puente, the worlds of latin, jazz and pop have lost a vital and joyous creative force for music and education. Tito's songs and recordings reflected his special musical genius and his life-affirming personality. Tito Puente's fellow ASCAP members join with his family, friends and millions of fans in mourning the loss of ''El Rey.'' Marilyn Bergman President and Chairman, ASCAP
