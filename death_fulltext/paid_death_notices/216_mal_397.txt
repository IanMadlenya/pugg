
        LIPSYTE--Sidney I. After 100 good years, he died on January 20. Father of Robert and Gale, grandfather of Sam and Susannah, great-grandfather of Alfred, father-in-law of Lois, grandfather-in-law of Ceridwen and Ben, he will rejoin his late wife of 66 years, Fanny in their endless conversation. Sidney retired as director of New York City's Bureau for what was then called Socially-Maladjusted Children. Donations may be made to the charity of your choice or the Morris Raphael Cohen Library, City College of New York.
