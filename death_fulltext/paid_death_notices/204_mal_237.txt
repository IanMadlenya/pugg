
        FEDER--Theodore D., age 89, died January 15th. Beloved husband of Marika. Devoted father of Gene, Ann and Joan. Caring grandfather of Ellie, Ben, Josh, Jojo, Elizabeth, Jacob and Sam. Loving uncle of Beverly, June, Alan and Lami. Ted devoted his long professional life to the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee, his second family. Services Sunday, 10 AM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91 St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Ted Feder Fund, Joint Distribution Committee, 711 Third Ave., NY NY 10017.
