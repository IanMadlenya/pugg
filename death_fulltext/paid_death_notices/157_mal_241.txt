
        MARSCHALL-Richard H. Died Friday, February 18th in Fort Lauderdale of cancer at 63. He was formerly a practicing psychotherapist in New York City for the last 20 years. Survived by his daughter Susan Marschall Patterson of Maynard, MA, his mother Ann Egan Marschall of Stamford, CT., and a brother Brian. A funeral Mass will be held Thursday, February 24th, 10 AM, Our Lady Star of the Seas Church, 1200 Shippan Avenue, Stamford, CT. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to American Cancer Society, 372 Danbury Road, Wilton, CT. 06897.
