
        CICERO-Michael J. Founder and President of the Michael J. Cicero Civic Association and President of the Virginia Michael J. Cicero Foundation, Inc. He passed away on July 9 at Seaview Community Rehabilitation Center on Staten Island. He leaves behind thirty nieces and nephews who live in the New York area. He is laid out at the Virginia Funeral Chapel, 1707 Highland Blvd, Staten Island, July 10 and 11, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Mass at St. Sylvester's Church in Concord, Staten Island Saturday, July 12 at 10 AM.
