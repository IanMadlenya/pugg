
        FUERST-Myron M. Of Rhinebeck, NY, died March 25, at his home. A graduate of Cornell University and former president of Fuerst Brothers, Inc. A memorial service will be held April 20, 12 PM, at Dapson-Chestney Funeral Home, 51 West Market St., Rhinebeck (845-876-6000). Memorials may be made to the Myron Fuerst Scholarship Fund, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY 12853.
