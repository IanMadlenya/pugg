
        POPPER-Belina. Died at age 91 after a brief illness on November 30, 1998 at Sound Shore Medical Center. She was born to Iancu and Bertha Saraga in Bucharest, Romania March 9, 1907. She married her beloved husband, Emil, in 1929. Her husband died in 1983 after 53 years of marriage. She was also predeceased by her sisters, Diana and Renee. She loved her gardens, dance, music and the arts. She is survived by her sons, Alex and Serge, her sister, Stefi, three grandchildren, nieces and nephews. The family will receive friends on Wednesday, December 2, from 4-6PM at the John J. Fox Funeral Home in Larchmont, NY. Private entombment will take place at Ferncliff Cemetery. Memorial donations may be sent to the charity of your choice.
