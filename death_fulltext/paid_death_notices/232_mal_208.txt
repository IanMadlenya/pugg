
        BROWN -- Joseph S., 89. Passed away peacefully surrounded by family Saturday, May 20, 2006, in Somers, NY. He leaves behind his beloved wife of 64 years, Shirley, daughters Regina (Mark) and Marilyn (Meredith), sister Lillian Lublang, and many nieces, nephews, relatives and friends who loved and adored him. He was a veteran of the Second World War where he taught celestial navigation in the Air Force, and was a member of the Counterintelligence Corps. He was a top notch printing salesman, former National Vice President of Bnai Zion, former President of the American Zionist Federation of Long Island, and an active supporter of Israel, an adventurer, sailor, and one of a kind individual. He kept his sense of humor until the end. ''How in heaven are you, Joe Brown?''
