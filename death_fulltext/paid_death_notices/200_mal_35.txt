
        PLOTKIN--Zalman, MD. On September 2, 2003. Renowned internist and cardiologist, whose 59 years of medical practice were in NY and CA. An ardent Zionist, he was medical student delegate to the Second Zionist Congress in Vienna; President of Revisionists Western States; Close personal friend of Menachim Begin; honored by the Technion along with Edward Teller, Edmund de Rothchild and Kirk Douglas. Physician devoted to his patients. Loving and passionate husband and father. Survived by his wife Paula and his daughter Dr. Frieda Plotkin of NYC. Services Thursday 11:30 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
