
        BLUM-Ray. The Board of Directors and Staff of New York Society for the Deaf (NYSD) extend their deepest condolences to Judy Blum and her family at the passing of her mother Ray Blum, friend and supporter of NYSD. Ms. Blum passed away on Sunday, March 25 at age 97. She has passed her legacy of service onto her daughter who continues to be an Advisory Board member of NYSD.
