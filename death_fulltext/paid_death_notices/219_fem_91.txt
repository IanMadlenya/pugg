
        STANLEY--Harriet Murdock. Died at the age of 100 on April 9, 2005, at Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, New Jersey. She was the widow of Cyril S. Stanley, former General Counsel of Rockefeller Center Corporation. She is survived by her daughter, Judith S. Burks of Princeton, N.J. and a son, James S. Stanley of Ormond Beach, FL. Also, by six grandchildren and 14 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on April 18, 2005 at Trinity Church, Princeton, N.J. at 11AM. Contributions in her name may be made to Princeton Area Community Foundation, 15 Princess Road, Lawrenceville, N.J. 08648
