
        WASSERMAN-Louis R., M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of The Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our outstanding colleague. Dr. Wasserman served as the Director of Hematology for nearly 20 years and helped establish Mount Sinai as a leading center in this area. He was a world expert in the field of polycythemia and received numerous awards in research, clinical affairs, and teaching. He will be honored and remembered as one of the ''giants'' and for his commitment to excellence. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Julia and to his family. Stanley Tuhrim, M.D., President Terry Weill, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
