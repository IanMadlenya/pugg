
        JACOBS-Nathan P. Congregation Emanu-El of the city of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Nathan P. Jacobs, our deepest sympathies on the occassion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        JACOBS-Nathan P. We mourn the death of Nathan P. Jacobs. For 50 years Nat was Ben Kaufman's partner and friend. For 65 years he was Jerry Kaufman's mentor and friend. Uncle Nat was a major part of our childhood and even greater part of our business life. He taught us how to do business, he showed us how to give charity. He was always there in our need. We shall sorely miss him, and our hearts go out to his family. Jerry and Carol Kaufman
        JACOBS-Nathan P. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of our long time and generous supporter, Nathan P. Jacobs. Nate was a dear friend, committed to furthering the work of ADL. He will be sorely missed. We exend our condolences to his beloved wife Lucille and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
