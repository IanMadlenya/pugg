
        MERKIN--Michael Eric. Born in the Bronx on January 12, 1939. Died March 27, 2004 in the Bronx at Calvary Hospital after a long illness. Son of the late Hermann and Mabel Merkin. Brother of Henry and Carl. US Army veteran. Graduate of City College. Employee of the Bronx Zoo for 20 years before his recent retirement. He will be missed by all.
