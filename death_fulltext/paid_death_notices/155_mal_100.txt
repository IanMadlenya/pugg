
        FEINERMAN-Mordecai. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a beloved alumnus of Yeshiva College who was deeply respected in the Jewish Community. Sincere condolences are extended to his children Lewis Feinerman, Dr. Charisse (and Dr. Jonathan) Reiner, and Gwen (and Jeffrey) Grossman, to his brothers Aaron S. (and Marilyn) Feinerman and Judah (and Ruth) Feinerman, and to his sisters Esther (and Rabbi Maurice) Wohlgelernter and Ricki (and Dr. Irving) Goldsmith. The entire Feinerman family are long-time friends, supporters and leaders of our institution, where Judah Feinerman serves as a member of the YU Board of Trustees and Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary and, with his wife, Ruth, is a Guardian of Yeshiva University. May the entire Feinerman family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman Chairman, YU Board of Trustees Herbert Smilowitz Vice Chairman, RIETS Board of Trustees
