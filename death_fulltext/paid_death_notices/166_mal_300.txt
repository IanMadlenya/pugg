
        SHINE-Saul. Aged 89. Died in New York City on Thursday, November 23, 2000. Beloved husband of Marge. His loving family and friends will sorely miss his keen intellect and generous nature. He fought the good fight until the very end. There will be a memorial service held at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue on Sunday, November 26 at 3:30 PM.
