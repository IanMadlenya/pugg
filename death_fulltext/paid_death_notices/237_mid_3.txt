
        DOWNING--Catherine S. Suddenly on October 16, 2006. Beloved wife of John M. Downing Sr., adored mother of Michael B. Downing and his wife Tara and Patrick S. Downing. Cherished stepmother of Brenda and John M. Downing Jr., Marguerite and Ernest Peck and Alison and William Metzler and grandmother of eight. Cathy was a loving, giving wife, mother, stepmother and grandmother and an accomplished singer and attorney. We will miss her deeply. Funeral services are private at Frank E. Campbell.
