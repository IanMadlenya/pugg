
        HART-David Montgomery on May 22, 2001, in Garrucha, Spain. Age 74. An anthropologist, Mr. Hart was the author of several books on the Berber-speaking peoples of Morocco. Born in Philadelphia, Mr. Hart lived in Morocco for almost 20 years while performing research and then resided in Spain. He was pre-deceased by his wife of many years, Ursula Cook Kingsmill. Mr. Hart is survived by a brother, Dr. Brandon Hart of Massachusetts, and a sister Sarah Brodsky of Yonkers, NY; three stepchildren, Carrol Johnson of Oxford, England, Christine Rosenkrantz of St. Petersburg, Florida, and Stephen Boycott of Namaimo, Canada.
