
        TAYLOR--William J., formerly of Bayside, NY and a retired founding officer of Local 1199 and National Hospital Union of Health Care Workers died of natural causes on November 12 in Lake Worth, Fla. A veteran of WWII awarded two Bronze stars. A devoted husband for over 60 years, avid reader, golfer and traveler, survived by his son William and three grandchildren: Daniel, Troy and Tiffany.
