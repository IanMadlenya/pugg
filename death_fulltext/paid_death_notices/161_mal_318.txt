
        MEAGHER-Cormac K. of Hollywood beach, Fla. and SAG HARBOR NY, formerly of Manhasset NY passed away peacefully on June 26th. Cormac was the devoted husband of Peggy for 61 years and loving father of Brendan, Cathy, Kieran, Declan and Brian. He was a loving grandfather of ten grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Cormac was a dedicated & accomplished educator who retired as Community School Superintendent in Queens, NY; he was also an active Glee Club and parent body member for over forty years of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in N.Y. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset on Wed from 2-5 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass is at St Mary's RC Church, Manhasset on Thursday at 10AM.
        MEAGHER-Cormac K. The Glee Club of the Friendly Sons of Saint Patrick mourn the passing of a devoted second tenor and extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Margaret, his children, and to all the bereaved family.
