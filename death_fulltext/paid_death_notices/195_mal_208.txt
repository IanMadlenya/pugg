
        MOLINAS-Max. Age 81, Bronx, New York, died April 17, 2003. A loving and devoted man who dedicated his life to the education of others while appreciating the beauty in everyday life. His warmth, kindness and humility will be deeply missed. He is survived by Dorothy, his wife of 51 years; his children Arvin, Sharon and Sandra; sons-in-law Yale and Sam; grandchildren Elana, Matthew, Sophia, Elazar and Max; and his brothers Victor, Albert and Milton. Donations in his honor can be made to Sephardic Shaare Rahamin Congregation, phone 718-671-8882.
