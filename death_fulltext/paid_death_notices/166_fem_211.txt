
        SMITH-Pamela Polhemus. 44, died November 22, 2000. She was born in Stamford, Connecticut on April 3, 1956, the daughter of Robert B. and Patricia A. Polhemus. In addition to her mother of Darien, CT, she is survived by her beloved husband of nineteen years, Peter Smith; children, Katrina, Samuel and Duncan, and Sister, Barbar Pholhemus, all of Bass Harbor, ME; brother, Richard Polhemus of New Milford, CT. A memorial service will be held, 3:00 PM, December 2 at the Bar Harbor Congregational Church, Bar Harbor, ME.
