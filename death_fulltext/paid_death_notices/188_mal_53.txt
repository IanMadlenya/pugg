
        STONE-W. Clement. The Board of Governors, Trustees, staff and members of Boys & Girls Clubs of America wish to express their profound sorrow over the passing of Mr. W. Clement Stone, our longtime Trustee, friend, supporter and a true champion for children. Mr. Stone gave more than 50 years of active service to the Boys & Girls Club cause. The W. Clement Stone Outstanding Achievement Award, honoring exemplary service by a board volunteer, is awarded annually in his honor. A recipient of the Herbert Hoover Humanitarian Award from Boys & Girls Clubs of America for his passionate commitment to America's youth, Mr. Stone's positive influence will be felt for generations to come. We will miss him greatly. Boys & Girls Clubs of America Arnold I. Burns, Chairman Roxanne Spillett, President
