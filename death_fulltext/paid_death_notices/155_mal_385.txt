
        VANACORE-Gennaro. On December 27, 1999 at age 40, after a courageous battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Jennifer Handler Vanacore. Loving father of Dominick Maxwell. Devoted son of Dominick & Nancy. Loving brother of Stephen and Elizabeth. He was a noted restaurateur and proprietor associated with Caffe Grazie, Demi, Mimi's Pizza and Splendido of the upper east side of Manhattan. His warm and loving spirit will always be remembered by his devoted family and many friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, Dec. 30, 11:30 AM, at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave at 84 St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Leukemia Society, 475 Park Avenue So., N.Y. 10016.
