
        TEXON--Meyer, 96, on January 10, 2006. Beloved husband of 64 years of Ami. Devoted father of Sylvia Rogers and Cantor Stephen Texon. Loving grandfather of Robert, Jessica, Jason and Raphael. Renowned cardiologist and former President of the New York County Medical Society. Associate Professor of Forensic Medicine at the New York University Medical Center. Noted author on how hemodynamics, and not cholesterol or diet, is the primary cause of atherosclerosic heart disease. Whether with family or professionally, he was all about heart. He will live in our hearts forever. Service at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Thursday, January 12, 1:30pm.
