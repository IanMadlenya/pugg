
        SPIER - ALBANO--Frances Disosway, 97, died in peace at home in Manhattan on May 2, 2007. From an old New York family, one branch dating to Staten Island in the 1700s another to Massachusetts in the 1600s, she lived her entire life in New York City, within blocks of where she was born, also at home. A graduate of The Brearley School, widely traveled, she was first married to Charles L. Stillman, of Time Inc., then to Lester C. Spier, M.D., and lastly to Salvatore Albano, all deceased. She was the proud mother of three devoted children Charles L. Stillman, Jr. (deceased), Stanley Wells Stillman, and Louise Stillman Lehrman and two stepdaughters -Marcia Ann Spier (deceased), and Carol Spier Riley. Her children gave her 15 grandchildren and 29 great-grandchildren. In addition she is survived by one niece and three nephews. A memorial service is planned for Monday, June 4th, at 3:00pm, St. James Church, 865 Madison Avenue at 71st Street, NYC 10021. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be to St. James Church, clergy discretionary fund, or to her cherished Huguenot Society of America, Staten Island Fund, in memory of Frances Disosway Johnson, 122 East 58th Street, New York, NY 10022
