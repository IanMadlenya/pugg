Service followed by reception will be held on Sunday, February 4th, 2007 from 2pm at: Diamond Hill United Methodist Church, 521 East Putnam Avenue (Rt. 1), Cos Cob, CT 06807, (203) 629-5567. In lieu of flowers, we would appreciate any donations either to the Diamond Hill United Methodist Church or the Greenwich Rotary Club.
