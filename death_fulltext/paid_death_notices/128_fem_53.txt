
        MANFREDI-Pierina Jaccod, 96, of Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of Manhattan and Riverdale, New York, died Tuesday, September 2, 1997. She was born on July 28, 1901 in Saint Pierre Aosta, Italy. Burial will be Wednesday, September 10, 1997, at 11:00 A.M., in Mount Olivet Cemetery, Maspeth, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the favorite charity of your choice, in Mrs. Manfredi's memory.
