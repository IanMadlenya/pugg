
        COMBE-Ivan D. 88, of Greenwich, CT and Dorset, VT. Died January 11, 2000. Beloved husband of Mary Elizabeth Combe, devoted father of Diana Bickford, Juliette Larson and Christopher Combe. He is also survived by six granddaughters and a grandson. His friends and associates are grateful for his many years of inspirational leadership, wise counsel and caring for others. A memorial service for family and church friends will be held Saturday, January 15 at 2 P.M. at the Round Hill Community Church in Greenwich, CT. In addition, a service for all his friends & associates will be held Saturday, February 5 at 2 P.M. at the Christ Church United Methodist in New York City. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to the Round Hill Community Church or the Ivan D. Combe Fund at Greenwich Hospital, Attention Development Office.
