
        EISENBERG--Norma. On December 18, age 88. Loving wife for 62 years of the late Paul Eisbenberg. Devoted mother of Judith Bieber and Richard Eisenberg. Beloved grandmother of Michael Bieber, Wendy Jacobs, Alan Eisenberg, Robin Friedman, Robert Eisenberg, and greatgrandmother. Devoted daughter, sister and aunt. Resident of Cryder Point for 48 years. She was elegant, kind and generous. A woman of wit and intelligence. She will be missed by all who knew her. Funeral service at Schwartz Brothers Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd on Sunday, 10 am. Donations may be made in Norma's memory to the Alzheimer's Association.
