
        SARACHEK -- Shirley. Be loved wife of the late Julian. Devoted mother of Joe (Heather), Russell (Libellule), Maggie (Paul) Weiss, and Jonas (Bonnie) Rosenthal. Adored grandmother of Jake, Sydney, Josh, Jason, Severin, Griffin, Achille, Julian, Ariel, and Gillian. A long-time, dedicated social worker with The Great Neck Public Schools for thirty-five years, she leaves behind many dear friends who she loved so much. Contributions in her memory may be made to: The Shirley and Julian Sarachek Foundation at The Reconstructionist Synagogue of The North Shore, 1001 Plandome Road, Manhasset, New York 11030. Arrangement entrusted to Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY
