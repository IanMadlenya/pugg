
        NAKAGAMA--Sam Isamu. 81, passed away on Wednesday, March 8, 2006, at Bayshore Community Hospital in Holmdel, NJ. Born in Delano, CA, he resided in Holmdel, and formerly, in Rumson, NJ. He is survived by his wife, Mary Frances Nakagama, two daughters, Anne Michi Magee and Eunice Kimi Nakagama, two sons, David Ken and Clark Sho Nakagama, one sister, Fumi Kato, and two grandchildren, Catherine and Stephen Magee. A memorial service will be held at the Unitarian Universalist Congregation of Monmouth County, 1475 West Front Street, Lincroft, NJ, on Wednesday, April 19, 2006, at 5 pm.
