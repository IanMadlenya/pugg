
        GOODMAN--Sol, 92, passed away October 9, 2004. He leaves his beloved wife Vera, daughters Nancilee Wydra and Robyn Goodman and grandson Zachary Wydra. His generosity of spirit and sense of humor touched all he met. A memorial will be held December 12, Temple Israel, Palm Beach, Fl at 1 PM. Donations can be made to Sol and Vera Goodman Endowment for Children of Single Parents in care of JCC Metro West, 760 Northfield Avenue, West Orange, NJ 07052.
