
        SCHIFF-Stanley, Ph.D. The Board of Directors and Executive Staff of New York Psychotherapy and Counseling Center mourn the passing of Stanley Schiff, a long time member of our Board of Directors. He was a warm, generous and loyal friend and colleague. His dedication and guidance in our treatment of mentally ill adults and emotionally disturbed children will be sorely missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his family and friends. Rabbi Seymour Brickman President, Board of Directors Rabbi Isidore Klein, CSW Executive Director Jack Schnee, M.D., F.A.P.A. Medical Director
