
        WEISS-Herbert J., Rabbi. On August 15, 2001. Formerly Rabbi of Temple Anshe Emeth of Pine Bluff, Arkansas, and Temple Emanu-El of Sarasota, Florida. Founder of Herbert J. Weiss Associates of New York, a financing consulting firm. Beloved husband of Deborah. Devoted father of David. Services Sunday 10:30 A.M. at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street, NYC.
        WEISS-Herbert J. Rabbi. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Rabbi Herbert J. Weiss. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Sr. Rabbi Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
