
        LEVITT-Sam. Of Boca Raton, FL. passed away at the age of 83 on January 6, 1998. He will be remembered as a devoted Husband to Pearl; loving father (and father-inlaw) to daughters, Mona, Tanya and Ann and sons, Mark and Arthur; caring grandfather to Eileen, Michael, Jennifer, Jason, Stuart, Jeffery, and David; and greatgrandchildren: Paige and Brooke. He will be in our hearts and thoughts forever. Funeral services will take place at Gutterman Funeral Chapel, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury. 10:45 A.M., on Thursday, January 8.
