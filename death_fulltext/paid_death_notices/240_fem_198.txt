
        GRUMBACH--Madeleine Francis, died peacefully at home in San Francisco at the age of 86. Born in Tuxedo Park, NY, graduate of CU College of Physicians and Surgeons, child Psychiatrist, lifelong partner of Melvin Grumbach, devoted mother to Ethan, Kevin and Anthony, grandmother of five, sister of L. H. Butt. She lived a full life.
