
        FRANCIS-Arlene. The Board of Directors of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City, Inc., mourns the death of our most valued friend and supporter Arlene Francis. For over 30 years she graced us with her presence as co-host of the UCP Telethon and through many other volunteer efforts. Thousands of people with disabilities are leading better lives due to her efforts. We remember her fondly and extend our sympathies to her family. Leo Hausman, Chairman
