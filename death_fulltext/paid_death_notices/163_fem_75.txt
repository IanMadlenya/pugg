
        FERRE-Sister M. Isolina. The entire community of Bank Street College of Education mourns the loss of Sister M. Isolina Ferre, a former trustee and friend of the College, whose inspiring leadership and devotion to the disenfranchised earned her one of the United States' highest honors, the Medal of Freedom. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family and loved ones. Augusta Souza Kappner, President Elizabeth S. Pforzheimer, Chair Bank Street College of Education
