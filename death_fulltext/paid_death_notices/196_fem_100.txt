
        BANNARD FESSENDEN Marion S. Formerly of Cold Spring Harbor, N.Y., died peacefully on May 6, 2003 in Greenport, N.Y. at the age of 80. She was married for 45 years to the late William N. Bannard, and is survived by her husband of nine years, Edward E. Fessenden. Loving mother of Marie B. Lockwood of Dover, MA., David N. Bannard of Richmond, VA., and Barbara B. Haller of South Salem, N.Y. Devoted grandmother of 11 grandchildren: Thomas and Charles Cartier, Preston, Thomas and William Bannard, Jason, Whitney, Ashley, Macky and Madison Haller and the late William B. Cartier. She also leaves three stepchildren: Edward Fessenden, Jr., Joan Hunt and Suzanne Fessenden, three step-grandchildren and four step-great grandchildren. Mimi was a longtime member of St. John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor. She volunteered her services tirelessly to many organizations including the Multiple Sclerosis Chapter of Long Island, The Garden Club of America, and the Red Cross Blood Services of N.Y. She was an avid gardener, accomplished bridge player and enthusiastic traveller, who most especially cherished her time spent at her second home, ''Cloud High'', Smith Parish, Bermuda. She will be deeply missed by her adoring family and a wealth of friends. A Memorial Service will be held on May 20, 2003 at 11AM at St. John's Church, Route 25A, Cold Spring Harbor, N.Y. Contributions may be made in her memory to St. John's Church, P O Box 266, Cold Spring Harbor, N.Y. 11724 or Eastern LI Hospital, 201 Manor Place, Greenport, N.Y 11944.
