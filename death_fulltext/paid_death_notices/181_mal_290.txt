
        JEANETTE-Steve, 71. Of Englewood Cliffs and Silver Beach, N.J., died February 23, 2002. He had a long and successful career working on the floor of the American Stock Exchange, and was a stock specialist and Vice President at Spear, Leads & Kellogg. Born in Brooklyn, N.Y. on July 15, 1930, he proudly served his country in the military during the Korean War, and was a member of the Honor Guard in Berlin. Beloved husband to Constance. Loving father to Eric and his wife Kim. Eva Marie and her husband Jason, and Stephanie. Doting grandfather to Tyler, Aimee and Alexandra. Devoted brother to Terry and Mary. Steve's undying love for his family was always apparent and enveloped us all in warmth and comfort. His quick wit, easy manner, kindness and generosity made him a faithful friend to nearly everyone he met. The light that Steve brought to our lives will never be extinguished, and his presence will remain as a steady guide to those he loved. Respects to the family can be paid at PattiMikatarian Funeral Home in Fort Lee, N.J. on Wednesday, February 27, between 3-5PM and 7-9PM. The funeral service will be held on Thursday, February 28 at St. John the Theologian, Greek Orthodox Church in Tenafly, New Jersey with interment to follow at George Washington Memorial Park in nearby Paramus, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Dover/Brick First Aid Squad, P.O. Box 172, Normandy Beach, New Jersey 07839.
