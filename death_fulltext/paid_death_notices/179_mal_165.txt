
        GUNSBERG - Amos. Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., mourns the loss of its dear friend Amos Gunsberg. His great generosity has furthered our work at the Hadassah Medical Organization and the Hadassah College of Technology in Israel. We express our condolences to his friends and family. May his memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, National President
