
        SIBLEY-Elvira T. At her home in Brownfield, Maine, April 16, Elvira Throwbridge Sibley aged 86 years. Beloved widow of Retired Major General Alden K. Sibley who died February 18, 1999. A memorial service for both will be held at 2:30, Saturday, May 1, 1999 at the Brownfield Maine Community Church. For further information: the York Funeral Chapel, Maple Street, Cornish, Maine.
