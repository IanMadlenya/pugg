
        HEALY--Sybil Gilmore. Died November 23, 2004, aged 93, in California. Born in Brooklyn March 15, 1911, she lived most of her life in Babylon, Long Island. She was much loved by her sister Ruth Stevenson, daughters Sharon Healy and Sybil Pagnamenta, grandchildren Carla, Anthony, Robin and Zoe, cousin Mary Gilmore Thomas, her nieces and nephews and a large extended family. Memorial mass, Friday, December 3, 10:45, St Joseph's RC Church, Babylon. In lieu of flowers, donations to Southside Hospital, Bay Shore or Good Samaritan Hospital Baxter Guild, West Islip.
