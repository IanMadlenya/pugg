
        HERRMANN -- Minerva W. Age 96, passed away on March 1, 2004 in West Palm Beach, FL. A retired NYC teacher, she received a BS degree from the University of Pennsylvania in 1929 and her Master's degree from Hunter College in 1964. Her commitment and unselfishness was obvious in all of her voluntary activities. She will be remembered for her intelligence and sensitivity by all whose lives she touched. She is survived by her loving family, son Mark, daughter-in-law Judy, grandchildren Joanne and Laura, sons-in-law Douglas, Milch and Jeffery Ehoodin, great grandchildren Olivia and Jack and her sister Lillian Burack. Those who wish may make a donation in her memory to the Cochran School of Nursing, St. John's Riverside Hospital, 967 N. Broadway, Yonkers, NY 10701.
