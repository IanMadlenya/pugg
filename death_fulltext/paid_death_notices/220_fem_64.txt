
        SZABO--Ruth (nee Simon) 72, of Bronxville, NY, died May 4, 2005. Widow of Dr. Tibor Szabo. Sister of Hilda Daily of Garden City Park, NY. She was a Registered Nurse and member of The Oaks Committee of The Alumnae Association, Mt. Sinai Hospital School of Nursing. A memorial service will held at the Reformed Church, Bronxville on Wednesday, May 11 at 11 AM. Memorial donations may be made to Jansen Memorial Hospice or to any pet rescue or children's organization.
