
        WOODS-Edelmira Lopez. Died February 13, 2002, at home in her sleep. Born on March 27, 1910, in Naranjito, P.R. She is survived by her son John of Harrisburg, NY, and daughter Irene Catanzaro of Brooklyn, NY, five grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. The memorial service will be held March 12, 2002, at 10 A.M. at St. Margaret's House, 49 Fulton Street, Manhattan, New York.
