
        GREEN-Arthur H., M.D. The Columbia University Center for Psychoanalytic Training and Research mourns the passing of Dr. Arthur H. Green. Dr. Green was a major advocate and innovator in the assessment and treatment of abused children. His work has enriched our understanding and ability to help this form of human suffering. We extend our sincerest sympathy to his wife Carol and their children Susan and Jonathan. Robert A. Glick, M.D., Director
