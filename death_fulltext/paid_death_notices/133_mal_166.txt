
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Officers and employees of Acordia Northeast deeply mourn the passing of Arnold Bergson, Chairman Emeritus. Arnold was the founding father of the predecessor companies in N.Y. and a great leader. He was a man amongst men and will be truly missed. Our sympathies are extended to his lovely wife Jeanne, his daughters, Jane and Barbara and their families.
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Fischman family regrets the passing of our onetime leader and longtime friend Arnold Bergson. He provided us with an opportunity in our early years and continued to offer encouragement and guidance throughout. We send our heartfelt sympathies to Jeanne, Jane and Barbara and their families. Elyse and Michael Fischman
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed life member, and extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Jeanne and his family. Murray B. Hirsch, President Howard Ecker, Secretary
