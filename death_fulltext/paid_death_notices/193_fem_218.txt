
        MARKOWITZ - Judith. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Judith Markowitz, beloved mother of Amy Stavis, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. A board member of the JCC of Mid-Westchester, Amy and her husband, Rob, a member of our Board of Directors, are dedicated leaders and deeply committed to Jewish and humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mrs. Markowitz's husband, Raymond, and to the entire Markowitz and Stavis families. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
