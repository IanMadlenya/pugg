
        WELLS-Herta G. Beloved and loving mother of Nora (Ted) Weinreich, Peter (Joan) Wells; adored and adoring grandmother of Karen, Elizabeth, Ralph, David, Jennifer and Rebecca; and pre-deceased by her husband Egon F. Wells, died at her home in Miami, Florida, peacefully at 6 AM, Sunday, November 1, 1998. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Temple Emanu-El, New York City, Dayton Jewish Federation, or Miami City Ballet. Services Tuesday, November 3, 1 PM, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
        WELLS-Herta G. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Herta G. Wells. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
