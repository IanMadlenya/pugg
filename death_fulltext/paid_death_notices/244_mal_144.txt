
        BIRNBAUM--Robert, a loving husband, father, grandfather and cherished one of many, passed away on May 12, 2007. Bob was born in Brooklyn in 1938 and enjoyed life in Los Angeles, New York and Seattle with a loving family by his side. He was an accomplished TV producer, and touched the lives of all of his family and friends across the country. We will celebrate his great life and strong spirit on Wednesday, May 16, 2007 at 5pm at Riverside Memorial on 76th Street at Amsterdam in Manhattan.
