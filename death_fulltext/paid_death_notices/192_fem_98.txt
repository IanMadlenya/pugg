
        Witchel-Ruth. (nee Goodman) on January 4, 2003. Ruth has left us to join her beloved Jess. She will be cherished and missed by her son Carl, her daughter-in-law Lucie Rodrigue, her two grandsons Alexandre and Simon Rodrigue-Witchel, her loving sister Hannah Stern as well as family and many friends. Services Sunday 11:15 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment to follow at Mt. Judah Cemetery, Glendale, NY.
