
        TRIPP-Samuel, S. On July 27, 1997 in his 94th year. Beloved husband of the late Mary Tripp. Devoted father of Grace and Norma. Grandfather of eight. Great-grandfather of ten. A passionate and committed lawyer and servant of the law for 68 years. Service 1pm, Wed, Schwartz Bros, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills.
        TRIPP-Samuel S. Queens County Bar Association records with deep sorrow the passing of its distinguished member, Samuel S. Tripp, Past President 1951-1952. Services will be at Schwartz Bros. Funeral Home located in Forest Hills, 11403 Queens Blvd., at 1 PM, Wednesday, July 30th. Melvin Wolinsky, President
