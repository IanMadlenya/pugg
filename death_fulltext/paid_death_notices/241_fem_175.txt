
        BOCK--Janet. The directors, officers and employees of Hess Corporation express their deepest sorrow and sense of loss on the death of their dear friend Janet Bock, Senior Executive Assistant to the Chairman of the Board. As an employee of the company for over 32 years, Mrs. Bock had the highest standards of professional excellence and extraordinary personal qualities. Her dedication to our company and many contributions will be greatly missed. It was our privilege to have worked with her. We express our deepest heartfelt sympathy to her husband Brian and her family.
