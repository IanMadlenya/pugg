
        SLOAN-Sol. The Officers and Members of Temple Sinai of Roslyn record with profound sorrow the passing of Sol Sloan, founding member of our congregation. Husband of Anita, father of Barbara and Ronnie, grandfather of Craig Lapp, Mark Lapp M.D., Jodie Lapp and Mitchell Stern, brother of Vivian Zemel. We extend our deepest condolences to all who cherish his memory. Rabbi Michael A. White Rabbi Norman Kahan, Emeritus Daniel S. Reich, President
