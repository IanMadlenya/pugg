
        McCARTHY-John T., M.D. of Tenafly, N.J. on May 10, 2000. Beloved Husband of Carol (nee Wacewiz). Devoted father of Ann E. Gardiner, Dr. Denise McCarthy Abel and Caroline Krasnoff. Dear brother of Dr. Denise McCarthy. Loving grandfather of David, Thomas and Lauren. Friends may call at the Barrett Funeral Home 148 Dean Dr. Tenafly, NJ on Friday 24 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass 10AM Saturday Mt. Carmel Church Tenafly, New Jersey. Interment Mt. Carmel Cemetery Tenafly, NJ. Donations to a charity of you choice would be appreciated.
        McCARTHY-John T., MD, Editorin-Chief of ''Cutis'' (Quadrant HealthCom Inc.) a journal with the largest readership of any dermatologic journal worldwide, died yesterday. Dr. McCarthy received his MD degree from the State University of New York Medical Center at Syracuse in 1954. During his career, he was Chief of Dermatology at Lenox Hill Hospital (NYC) from 1966-1968. He was also past president of the New York State Society of Dermatology, the North American Clinical Dermatologic Society, Metropolitan Dermatologic Society, and the Manhattan Dermatologic Society. He is survived by his wife Carol, daughters, Ann Gardiner, Dr. Denise McCarthy Abel, Caroline Krasnoff, sister, Dr. Denise E. McCarthy and grandchildren David Gardiner, Thomas Abel, & Lauren Krasnoff.
