
        DEERMOUNT--John, 89, died at New York Hospital on July 13, 2004. Mr. Deermount was retired as owner/president of the Blue Print Company in NYC. He leaves behind a wife, Dorothy Virginia, a son John, Jr. and five grandchildren, Cory C. Deermount, Kathleen Muzyl, John A. Deermount, Michael I. Deermount and Amy R. Deermount. Visitation will be held Thursday, 6-8PM at Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Avenue (69-70th St), NYC. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory preferred to the hospice program of your choice.
