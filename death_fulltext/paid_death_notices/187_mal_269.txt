
        BERNSTEIN-Elliot. Of Stamford, CT, died Monday, August 26, 2002. Producer for CBS News ''Sunday Morning''; former Executive Producer ''CBS Morning News''; Producer for ''60 Minutes.'' Bernstein began his 46 year career in journalism at UPI, worked at ABC News, then came to CBS News in 1979. Bernstein was winner of a Peabody Award, an ABA Silver Gavel Award, an Overseas Press Club Award and an Emmy Award. He is survived by his wife Marcy; four children, Daniel, Rhea, Julie and Lisa; a granddaughter, Kemper, and his sister, Betty Seiden. Funeral at noon today at Greenwich Reform Synagogue, 257 Stanwich Rd, Greenwich, CT.
