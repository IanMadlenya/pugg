
        BROWN-Paul R. of Stuart, Florida, died on December 26, 1999. A retired dentist, Dr. Brown was a long time resident of Scarsdale, New York before retiring in 1987. He is survived by his beloved wife of 56 years, Anne; four children and two grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Hobe Sound, FL, December 30, 1999, under the direction of the Young & Prill Funeral Home, Stuart, FL.
