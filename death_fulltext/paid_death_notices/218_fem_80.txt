
        KATZ--Rosalie. On March 7, 2005 at age 62. Our mother was and always will be our rock and our anchor. She filled our lives with love and laughter. She embodied the highest standards of compassion, generosity, and integrity. Her courage, wisdom, and insight will continue to guide our lives and the generations to come. She was the loving wife of the late Daniel; cherished mother of Wendy Katz, Meredith Gantcher, and Gary Katz; adored grandmother of Danielle Gantcher and Yaira, Gidon, and Matan Katz. Services Thursday, March 10, 11 A.M. at Temple Israel of Great Neck, 108 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, NY. Contributions in her memory should be directed to The Rosalie Katz Internship for Leadership at the Jewish Theological Seminary or to The National Marrow Donor Program, New York.
        KATZ--Rosalie. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rosalie Katz, beloved wife of the late Daniel Katz. Rosalie was a cherished friend of the Jewish community whose generosity and support enriched lives in New York, Israel and around the globe. We extend our deepest sympathies to Rosalie's children Wendy, Meredith, and Gary; to her grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
