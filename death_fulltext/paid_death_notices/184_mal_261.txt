
        MYERS-Robert Holt. 84. Died on May 11 at home in Chevy Chase, Maryland. He was born on March 2, 1918, in Washington, D.C. Mr. Myers practiced law as a partner of the Washington, D.C. law firm Williams, Myers & Quiggle. He served on the board of the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation and then became its Chairman. He was an avid hunter and occasional tennis player. He was a member of the Chevy Chase Club, the Rolling Rock Club, the Princeton Club of Washington and New York, the Metropolitan Club, the Oyster Harbors Club, and the Oglethorpe Club. He is survived by his four children.
