
        STOWE-Dr. Nathan. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member, Dr. Nathan Stowe. Dr. Stowe met his beloved wife, Florence, at Rodeph Sholom and have been members for almost 65 years. We all miss Nat. Our deepest sympathy is extended to Florence and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
        STOWE-Nathan, DDS. Devoted husband of Florence M., beloved brother of the late Lilian Woolf, Michael and Peter. Service Sunday, 11AM, Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
