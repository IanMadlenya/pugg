
        JUNKER--David S., a retired employee for American Home Products, a long-time area resident and a former resident of Scarsdale, NY, died Monday, February 5th at St. Lukes Hospital, New York. He was 89. The son of Harold Junker and Esther Seguin Junker Tripp, he was born on February 21, 1917, in Freeport, IL. He was a graduate of Princeton University, class of 1939, and earned a J.D. from Harvard University in 1942. He was known for his love and knowledge of New York City, photography, stamp collecting, love of music and was an avid reader. Survivors include several nieces and nephews. Visitation will be at Ballard - Durand Funeral Home, White Plains, NY, on Friday, February 9th, at 11:00 am. Internment will be private at Kensico Cemetery. Memorial gifts/donations may be made to the Childrens Aid Society, NYC or Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center.
