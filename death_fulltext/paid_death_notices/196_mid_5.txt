
        GOLDMAN-Etta Louise, 80, died Saturday, (May 10, 2003). A 50-year New York City resident, she was a retired designer of fine women's clothing, a supporter of animal shelters and the arts, and a Board member of the Hudson Guild. She leaves her nephews Robert Goldman, and his wife Amy, of Aurora, IL; David Goldman, and his fiance Cheryl McGrath, of Spokane, WA; and Stuart Lavine, and his wife Brenda, of Coventry, CT; her cousins Gladys Schreiber of Queens, NY, and Bud Bunim of Cranberry, NJ, four great nieces and many dear friends. Interment will take place at Sharon Memorial Park, 120 Canton Street, Sharon, MA. on Sunday, May 25 at 11:45 A.M. A memorial service will be held at the Hudson Guild, 441 West 26th St, New York City, on Sunday, June 1 at 2:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Hudson Guild.
