
        NEFF-Richard B. Age 90, died March 23, 2003, in Manhattan, where he had resided since 1935, after an illness of medium duration. Survived by a son, Richard B. Neff Jr., of Palm Beach. Burial to be in Athens, GA. In lieu of flowers, contributions to St. James' Church, 865 Madison Avenue, New York, New York 10021, would be appreciated.
