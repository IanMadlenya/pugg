
        MARX-William (born Wolf) Louis, 92, died Wednesday May 26, after a valiant fight. Beloved husband of Murielle. Adored father of Jane and of the late Paul. Devoted brother of Nettie Rosenblum, Anna Goldberg, Adelaide Rubin and the late Benjamin. Cherished brother-in-law of Elaine and the late Kenneth Kleinman and Gloria and Gilbert Bloom. Devoted uncle He received a B.S. and M.S. from NYU and attended Harvard Business School. He was a proud veteran of W.W. II and a recipient of two Bronze Stars. He loved being an accountant and auditor. His compassionate life reflected his soul. Services today at 1:30 Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York.
