
        RYAN--John F. II, 71, died peacefully on September 20, 2003 at home in Barnegat, NJ. Former Deputy Mayor of Barnegat, he was extremely active in the Ocean County Democratic Committee. Survived by Dorothy, his wife of 50 years, his daughter Lisa, sons Robert, John F. III, and Steven, all of New Jersey and grandchildren, Justin, Kali, Dylan, Sean, Danny & Derek. He will be remembered by family, friends and colleagues for his charm, wit and love of all things Irish. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Southern Ocean County Hospice and Home Care Services, 1140 Route 72 West, Manahawkin, New Jersey 08050.
