
        STEIN-Melvin. The officers, leadership, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Melvin Stein. Mr. Stein, his wife, Elaine, and his family have long been generous & devoted supporters of UJA-Federation. His sister-in-law, Elaine Roberts, is a longtime, dedicated friend of the Women's Campaign. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Elaine; his children, Stuart and Roger; his sister, Toby Rozen; Elaine Roberts; his grandchildren; and the entire family. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
