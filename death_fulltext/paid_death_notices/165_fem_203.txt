
        RUDMAN-Maxine A. Of Great Neck, NY. Died peacefully in her sleep the morning of the 20th of October in her 70th year. She was, and always will be, the beloved wife of Burton for almost 50 years; devoted daughter of Sophie Field, treasured sister of Douglas Field; cherished mother of Ellis, Rick, Seth and Lori; adored grandmother of Sarah, Jessica, Gabrielle, Amanda, Rachael, Adam, Ezra, David and Lauren. She was a dedicated teacher of Great Neck's young children for over 18 years. You were such an important part of our lives. Our memories of you will never be forgotten. Services Monday, 11:30 AM, at ''RiversideNassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Expressions of sympathy may be sent to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, NYC, NY.
