
        CORNWALL--L. Peter, 87, of New Canaan, CT, on May 2, 2004. Husband of the late L.W. Cornwall. He retired after 38 years with Pan American. Author of ''Ride The Sandy River'' and other railroad books and articles. He is survived by his son Peter, sister Priscilla Norwood and friend Carol Smith. A memorial service on May 13 at 10:30AM at the Congregational Church, New Canaan, CT.
