
        AWAD--Dr. Elias Steelo, Ph.D., 80, New York, June 12, 2007. Brilliant, strong and admirable man, with contributions to life, science and the arts in ways well beyond his time. An eternal scholar and mentor, he helped shape the lives of many. He will be forever loved and remembered as a dedicated and caring father who embraced all that is good in the world around him. Dr. Awad is survived by two loving sons. Services will be held at the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral, 319 E. 74 St., on Saturday June 16 at 9:30am.
