
        BISSON-Carl Louis. Beloved life partner of John Lembo, departed this life on May 1, 1998, in New York City. A friend, confidant, and teacher to all who were blessed by his spirit, his ministry survives him. To have known this sweet man was to have experienced the true love and grace of God herself. Memorial service to be held on Friday, May 15, 1998 at 7 PM at Rutgers Presbyterian Church, 236 W. 73rd St., New York City.
