
        LINN-William J., Jr. 60, of Scarsdale, NY, on Sept 27, 2001. Beloved husband of Geraldine TummoloLinn. Beloved father of Frank and Margaret Tummolo. Loving son of Josephine (nee Hulley) and the late William J. Linn, Sr. Devoted grandfather of Gianna, Danielle, Frankie and Lisa Tummolo. Cherished father-in-law of Gina Tummolo and brother-in-law of John R. and Andrea Ahern. Bill was born June 18, 1941 and served as a captain in the U.S. Air Force during the Vietnam War. He was the owner of William J. Linn Inc., a large school supply company in New Jersey and a licensed real estate broker in Westchester County. Visiting hours Friday, Sept 28, at Flynn Memorial Home, 1652 Central Park Ave, Yonkers, NY (914-963-5178), 2-4 & 7-9pm. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:45am at Immaculate Heart of Mary R.C. Church, Route 22 at Carmen Rd, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Hospital, New York, NY will be kindly appreciated.
