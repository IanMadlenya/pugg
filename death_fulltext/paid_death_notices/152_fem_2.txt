
        BLACKMORE-Joan, 64, died on Friday, August 27, at her home in Greenwich, CT. from cancer. She was the wife of Robert C. Blackmore. Born in Cape Town, South Africa, July 14, 1935, she was the daughter of Muriel P. Ruick and the late Felix DeCola. Mrs. Blackmore grew up in Los Angeles where she graduated from Westlake School for Girls and UCLA. She was the mother of Robin Jalajas of Newburyport, MA., and Karyn Hagy of Westlake Village, CA. Grandmother of four. A memorial service was held in Greenwich. Memorial Contributions may be made to Expedition Inspiration Breast Cancer Fund, PO Box 4289, Ketchum, ID. 83340.
