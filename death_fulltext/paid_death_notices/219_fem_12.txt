
        ROSENGARTEN--Helen, M.D. The faculty and staff of the Department of Psychiatry mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend Helen Rosengarten, M.D., Associate Professor of Psychiatry. She was a skilled researcher and clinician, and distinguished author. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. She will be sorely missed. Robert Cancro, M.D., Chairman, Dept of Psychiatry NYU School of Medicine
