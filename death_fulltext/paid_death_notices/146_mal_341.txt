
        BERG-David. Attorney and philanthropist, died at age 94, loving father of Betsy Berg. Service on Friday 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment, Mt. Hebron Cemetery.
        BERG-David. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a longtime friend, a highly respected philanthropist and leader of the Jewish community. A YU Benefactor, he was a former board member of YU and its Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law. He established the David Berg and Family Scholarship for YU undergraduates entering Cardozo. An honorary alumnus, he was a Founder of our Albert Einstein College of Medicine and a Fellow of our affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his daughter, Betsy, and all the family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        BERG-David. The Board of Directors and Staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum, Jerusalem, are saddened by the death of David Berg. He was an early Founder of the IMJ and served on AFIM's Executive Committee for many years. The exquisite Berg 18th Century English Dining Room will forever be a tribute to his passion for Israel and the arts. Our condolences to his daughter Betsy. Judy Steinhardt, President, AFIM Teddy Kollek, President, IMJ
