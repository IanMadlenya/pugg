
        NARDONE--Louis V., 86, founding partner of Nardone Brothers Pizza Baking Company in Wilkes-Barre, PA. Died Wednesday. He was the father of Dr. Maria Nardone of New York City and Mario, Vincent and Louis Nardone of Shavertown, PA. Funeral Mass will be celebrated Monday in Saint Nicholas Roman Catholic Church, WilkesBarre. Friends may call Sunday 2-4 & 7-9 PM at McLaughlin Family Funeral Service, Wilkes-Barre.
