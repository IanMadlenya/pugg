
        SOSHUK-Marianne. On January 4, 1998 in Miami Beach, FL. Wife of Levi; mother of Rona and David, grandmother of Stephen, Daniel, Robyn and Rachel. Marianne taught history and social studies in Brooklyn public high schools. In retirement she travelled extensively, was active in Jewish and civic matters, and continued teaching in the Dade County adult education program. I miss you terribly, Mom.
