
        FRITCHEY-Mary Knowles. On July 9, 2002, at her home in Washington D.C. Born June 28, 1912. Beloved wife to Frank Gardiner Wisner and later to Clayton Willis Fritchey. Mother to Frank, Ellis and Graham Wisner and to Elizabeth Hazard. She is also survived by seven grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. Valued friend to many in Washington, New York, and around the world. Board member or trustee at the Phillip's Collection, St. Timothy's School, The Shakespeare Theatre, All Hallows Guild of the Washington National Cathedral, The Women's Campaign Fund, Arts in Embassies, Anacostia Development Corp., Women's National Bank and first Chairwoman of the Kennedy Center. Memorial services will be held at the Bethleham Chapel of the National Cathedral on September 4, 2002 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Phillip's Collection, 1600 21st Street, N.W., Washington, DC 20009, or St. Timothy's School, 8400 Greenspring Avenue, Stevenson, MD. 21153, or the Shakespeare Theatre, 516 8th Street, S.E., Washington, D.C. 20003-2834.
