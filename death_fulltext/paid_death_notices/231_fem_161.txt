
        MARCUS--Ellen T. On April 5, 2006. Beloved wife of David. Devoted mother of Michael, Jeffrey and Kenneth. Also survived by mother Ida Turoff; sister Nancy Roth; three daughters-in-law; six grandchildren. 30-yr resident of Manhasset where she served 9 years as dedicated member and president of school board. Outstanding nurse and educator. She will be dearly missed and remembered.
