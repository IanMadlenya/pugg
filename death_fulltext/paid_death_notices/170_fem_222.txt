
        LAUER-Rachel M., Ph.D., founding Director of the Straus Thinking and Learning Center and Professor of Psychology, Pace University, died suddenly on Saturday, March 24. Dr. Lauer was a pioneer and an intellectual leader in the field of adult development, leadership and critical thinking. She will be greatly missed by her family, friends, students, clients, and colleagues, whom she both learned from and inspired througout her life and her life's work. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, May 1, 2001, 3:30-5PM at the Pace University Student Union (New York City campus). Further information is available at 212346-1791. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Straus Thinking and Learning Center, Pace University, 41 Park Row, New York, New York 10038.
