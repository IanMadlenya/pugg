
        WOLINETZ--Leonard. The medical center's world-wide leadership expresses its sincerest condolences to Harvey Wolinetz, Brenda Schlanger and Jean Zucker on the loss of their cherished father and her dear brother. A quintessential and hardworking ''family'' man who enjoyed life, Mr. Wolinetz showered love and affection on his family, especially on his beloved ''diamond'' Edith, of blessed memory. As a ''Patron of Medicine'' in support of our Department of Oncology, Harvey continues the legacy of tzedekah and chesed that he learned from his parents. May Harvey and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Prof. Jonathan Halevy, Director-General Menno Ratzker, Vice Chair Jack C. Bendheim, President Paul S. Glasser, Nat'l Executive Director
