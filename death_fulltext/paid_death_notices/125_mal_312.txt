
        REGAN-James P. On June 27, 1997. Beloved husband of Florence. Loving father of Brian and Maureen Regan Clark. Dear grandfather of James, Erin and Brianna. Fond brother of Helen Regan. Lifetime resident of NYC, veteran of World War II, retired Battalion Chief, FDNY, founder of James P. Regan and AssociatesInstitutional Fire Safety Consultants. Memorial mass at St. Thomas More Church, Breezy Point, Queens, 9:30 AM, Tuesday. Donations to NYC Firefighters Burn Unit at NY Hospital Cornell University Medical Center in his memory would be appreciated.
