
        KEENAN-Ronald Gregory, 51, of Verona, N.J., died June 27, 1999, at St. Elizabeth's Hospital, Utica, N.Y. A memorial service will be held at 7:30 PM Sunday, August 1, in the sanctuary of Union Congregational Church, Upper Montclair, N.J. Mr. Keenan was born in Middletown, Conn., and lived in Montclair and Verona for 30 years. Mr. Keenan graduated from Notre Dame High School, New Haven, Conn. He received BS and MA degrees in Economics at Lehigh University. He was elected to Beta Gamma Sigma, the National Economics Honor Society. Mr. Keenan was the managing director of American Re Securities Corporation in Princeton, N.J. Previously, he was the managing director of Chase Securities in New York City. Mr. Keenan was a member of the Board of Directors of the Liberty Brokerage Investment Corporation, the Mortgage Backed Securities Clearing Corporation, and the Bond Market Association, where he chaired the Mortgage and Asset-Backed Committee. Mr. Keenan was a member of the Union League Club in New York City, the Adirondack League Club in Old Forge, N.Y., and the Montclair Golf Club. He served as chairman of the Parents' Fund Committee of Smith College. He is survived by his wife, Eileen, and his daughter, Anne. Donations may be made in Mr. Keenan's memory to Smith College, Northampton, Mass.
