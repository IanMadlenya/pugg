
        ADELSOHN-Florence, nee Feldman. On January 17, 1999. Beloved wife of Yale, mother of Lawrence (Lynn) and Marc, adored grandmother of Jodi (Mrs. Gregg Ruff) and Keith, beloved sister and aunt, after a long illness. Services are today, 12 Noon, January 19, 1999, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Route 4 East, Paramus, N.J. followed by interment at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, N.J.
        ADELSOHN-Florence. It is with profound sorrow that Kingsbrook Jewish Medical Center/Rutland Nursing Home, its Board of Trustees, Medical/Dental staff and all personnel mourn the passing of Florence Adelsohn, beloved wife of Yale Adelsohn, our esteemed Board member and dedicated Director of Development. Sincere sympathy to Yale, his sons and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. David Minkin, President Emeritus Stanley Mandel, Chairman of the Board Milton Gutman, Chief Executive Officer Mohammad Zahir, MD, Executive Medical Advisor Linda Brady, MD, Medical Director
