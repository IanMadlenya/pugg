
        MOSKOWITZ--Sunny, in her 90th year, died peacefully at home. Devoted wife of the late Herbert Moskowitz. Cherished mother of Roanna Shorofsky and Miriam Ben Natan and beloved motherin-law of Morris and Rafi. Adored grandmother of Karren, Sharon and Keren and loved by Alex, Carolyn and Allan. Loving great-grandmother of Rebecca, Julia, Jonathan, Noa and Maya. A woman of grace, dignity and goodness who inspired and elevated her family and all who knew her. Services Thursday, 1PM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        MOSKOWITZ--Sunny. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Sunny Moskowitz, beloved mother of our devoted members, Roanna and Dr. Morris Shorofsky, Miriam and Rafi Ben Natan, caring grandmother and greatgrandmother. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Arthur Schneier
