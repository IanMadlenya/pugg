
        STERN-Morton J., D.D.S. We record with deep sorrow the death of our colleague, Dr. Morton J. Stern, Associate Clinical Professor of Dentistry, on March 25, 2003. Dr. Stern served the School and its students with unstinting devotion for 45 years. He was renowned for his devotion to dental education. The Dental School faculty and students will miss his many acts of kindness. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Susan, and family. Ira B. Lamster, D.D.S. M.M.Sc., Dean, Columbia University School of Dental & Oral Surgery
