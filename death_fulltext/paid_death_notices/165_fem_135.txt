
        REYNOLDS-Peggy Brady. Of NYC, and Westhampton, L.I., on October 11, of heart failure after an extended illness. Survived by her husband Jack, son Thomas, grandchildren Tommy Jr., Scott and Katlyn. Predeceased by daughter Meg. Loving sister of Anne, Marion, Eileen and Joan. Classical designer of sports wear for Evan Picone and colorful swimwear for Peter Pan. She built a beautiful home in Westhampton, decorated with a loving touch to welcome family and friends. She was a smart, stylish, gutsy and generous woman-as beautiful on the inside as the outside. Reposing at John Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 1st Ave., near 70 St., NYC, Friday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10 AM, Church of the Holy Family, 315 East 47 St.
