
        BENJAMIN-Frances Zisman.Died on February 15, 2000, age 97. She outlived two husbands Louis S. Zisman and Abraham I. Benjamin. She was mother of two, grandmother of six, great grandmother of 12. She was the family matriarch with the beauty of a Rachel, the forecefulness of a Rebecca and the fighting spirit of Deborah in her battles against illness and injustice. An era ends. She goes with our love.
