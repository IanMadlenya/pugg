
        TERNER-Emanuel M., 89. On May 5th, 1998. Cherished and beloved husband of Mathilda (nee Weisenfeld). Loving father of Nancy Behrman, Elaine Cooper and Carol Lederman, father-in-law to Stanley and Mark. Devoted grandfather of Peter (Josephine) Grayson, Alan (Kim) Mnuchin, Steven Mnuchin, Amy (Jeff) Verschleiser & Judy Lederman. Adoring great grandfather of Bradley Louis Grayson. Dear brother of Selma Slater. Friends may call Thursday evening 6-8PM. Services Friday, May 8, at 12 noon at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        TERNER-Emanuel M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Emanuel M. Terner, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        TERNER-Emanuel. Congregation Orach Chaim extends its heartfelt condolences to his daughter Carol Lederman, wife of our dedicated congregant and supporter Mark Lederman. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Michael D. Shmidman, Rabbi Wolf Scheck, President
        TERNER-Emanuel M. The Board of Governors of the Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member Emanuel M. Terner and extends its heartfelt sympathy to his family and friends. David L. Rudnick, President Robert S. Puder, Secretary
        TERNER-Emanuel. The Anti-Defamation League Women's Division joins Selma Terner Slater and her family in mourning the loss of her beloved brother. Audrey Magid, Chair Rhoda W. Korman, Director
