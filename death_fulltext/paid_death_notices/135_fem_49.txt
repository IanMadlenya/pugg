
        CARROLL-Andree. On April 8, 1998 at the age of seventy-nine years. Former Senior Associate Counsel for Olin Corporation of Stamford, CT. Devoted wife of the late Walter J. Carroll, WWII Ace. She received her B.A. from Hunter College, Class 1939 and her LLD from New York University in 1943. Dear sister of the late Edmond G. Herve, Sr. She is survived by her nephew Edmond G. Herve, Jr., and his wife Janet L. Herve, niece Barbara A. Johnson and her husband Robert W. Johnson, a great nephew Christopher R. Johnson, and her sister-in-law Grace M. Herve. Reposing at the Charles P. Nagel Funeral Home, 352 E. 87 St, NY, NY, Friday 2-5 and 7-9 PM.
