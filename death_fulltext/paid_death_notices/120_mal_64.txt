
        SEXTON - Richard J. Ph.D. on January 3, 1997 of Forest Hills, NY. Professor Emeritus Fordham University. Beloved husband of the late Georgina McSweeney Sexton. Survived by his beloved wife Virginia Staudt Sexton, Ph.D. Devoted father of Nancy E. Georgina S. Mary Sexton Althouse and Richard E. Sexton. Loving grandfather of Katherine Althouse, Laura and Patricia Sexton. Brother of Genevieve Connell. Reposing Fox Funeral Home, Forest Hills, Tuesday, January 7, 1997 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church Wednesday 9:30 AM. Donations to Fordham Prep, East Fordham, East Fordham Road, Bronx, NY 10458-5175 appreciated.
