
        SAICH-Margaret Brady. Of Sparkill, NY, formerly of Yonkers and Rye, died on Wednesday, March 17 at the age of 85. She is survived by one son, William of Hartsdale, NY; two daughters, Helen Senko of Brewster and Sister Margo Saich, O.P., a member of the Sparkill Dominican Order. For funeral information call Moritz Funeral Home, 914-359-0890.
