
        DWIGHT-Richard W., M.D. Age 95, of Westwood, MA, formerly of Chestnut Hill, MA, died Friday, November 20, 1998. Son of the late Dr. James Dwight and the late Marie (Iasigi) Dwight. He is survived by his nephew Philip T. Dwight of Hancock, NH, and his niece Veronica Richard of Ft. Myers, FL. Also survived by several great-nieces and great-nephews. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held Tuesday, November 24, 1998, at 11 AM, at the Church of St. Lawrence, Rte 9, Brookline, MA. Interment at Forest Hills Cemetery, Jamaica Plain, MA. Remembrances may be sent to the American Cancer Society, Massachusetts Divison, 255 Bear Hill Rd., Waltham, MA 02154. For information call J. S. Waterman & SonsWaring, 592 Washington St., Wellesley, MA.
