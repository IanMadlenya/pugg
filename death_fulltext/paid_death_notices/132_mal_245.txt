
        WORTH-Samuel. On January 17, died at his home in Palm Aire, Pompano, FL. Cherished husband of Goldie for 61 years. Devoted father to Harvey and to Theona Deutsch, loving grandfather to Danny, Michael, Douglas, Beth and Jacquelyn. Founding member and former president of Hollis Hills Jewish Center. Funeral services at Sinai Chapels, 162 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, Queens, Monday at 9 AM.
