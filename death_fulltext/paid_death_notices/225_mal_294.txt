
        LEVITHAN--Lou. January 11, 1912-October 26, 2005. LUCK and LOVE.
        LEVITHAN--Louis, age 93, on October 26, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Alice. Survived by his sons, Allen, Jack and Robert and daughters-inlaw Beth and Roberta, brothers Harold and Morris and sisters Gladys Grody and Estelle, grandchildren David, Adam, Joshua, Sarah and Susannah and great-granddaughter Paige. Devoted uncle and brother-in-law. Former NYC Police Officer and US Army Captain. Owner of Lou's Cliff and Victorian Restaurants. Services Friday 12:45 pm at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations to Friends in Deed-Levithan Fund, 594 Broadway, Suite 706, NY, NY 10012
