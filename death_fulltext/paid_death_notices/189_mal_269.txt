
        SALZER - Richard. The Board of Trustees, management and staff of The Jewish Home and Hospital Lifecare System mourn the passing of our devoted trustee of 20 years. The treasurer of our Board and the mainstay of our Westchester/Sarah Neuman Center, he will be missed for his sage financial counsel and commitment to quality of care. We extend our sincere sympathies to his beloved wife, Betty. He leaves us a great legacy by which he will always be remembered. Fred M. Lowenfels, Chairman of the Board Amy K. Pollinger, Chair, Sarah Neuman Center Sheldon Goldberg, President/CEO
