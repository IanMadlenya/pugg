
        OLMSTED--Robert Groves, 94, of Palm City, FL. died on August 12 in San Diego. A graduate of Exeter, Yale and Harvard Business School, he was a retired finance and education executive. He worked at LILCO 1938-1960, was vice president of Columbia University 1960-1965, and president of Adelphi University 1967-1969. He leaves his wife Mary, his children Nancy and Robert, two grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. Donations to United Methodist Foundation of Western PA for Olmsted Manor Retreat Center.
