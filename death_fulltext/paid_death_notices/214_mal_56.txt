
        LEVINE--Morris, 91, passed quietly away on November 3, 2004. He is preceded in death by his son Richard and wife Selma. ''Great Uncle Morris'' is survived by his children Steven (Patty) Levine, Debra Tobin, grandchildren Brenda and Scott Tobin, his sister Charlotte Gottesman, many generations of nieces, nephews and friends, and his lifetime friend Harold Chase. Services today 10 AM at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 76th Rd. & Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY.
