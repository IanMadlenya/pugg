
        REGOSIN--Martha D. (nee Davidson), 96. On January 28, 2005 in Brooklyn, New York. Wife of 53 years of the late Herman A. Regosin. Survived by children Jerry and wife Ina; Jay and wife Yehudit (of Jerusalem). Grandchildren Jonathan, married to Randi Berkowitz; Yaffa, married to Noam Ohring; greatgrandchildren Talia, Geffen, Shachar, Amichai & Carmel. Her zest for life will be missed by all who knew her.
