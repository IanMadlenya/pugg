
        KASSLER-Elizabeth Bauer. The Trustees and staff of The Museum of Modern Art express deep sadness at the passing of their longtime friend and colleague Elizabeth Bauer Kassler, former curator and director of the Department of Architecture and Design. Her tenure at the Museum from 1938 to 1946 spanned a vital period in our history and her contributions are fondly remembered. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director Terence Riley, Director, Dept of Architecture & Design The Museum of Modern Art, N.Y.
