
        BROWN-Mary Murray. Beloved and loving wife of Leland Scott Brown. Loving mother of Morgan Scott Brown and Janet Brown Jussel. Beloved mother-in-law of Suzanne R. Brown and Christian Jussel. Sister of Janet Mahony Wilson. Grandmother of John Bradbury, Sara B. Robbins, Catherine M. Jussel & David S. Jussel. Died February 16, 2000. She was known for years of service to the Bedford Free Library, Bedford, New York, and her deep love of Chappaquiddick Island. A memorial service will be held Monday, February 21 at 11:00 at St. Matthew's Episcopal Church, Bedford. In lieu of flowers it is suggested that gifts be made to the Bedford Free Library, Bedford, New York 10506.
