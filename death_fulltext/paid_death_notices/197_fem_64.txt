
        CADWELL--Franchellie Margaret. On May 25, 2003, at her home in New York. Memorial Mass at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 65 E. 89 St, Friday, June 13, 2003 at 10:30am. One of the first women to own a national advertising agency. Ms. Cadwell was known in the advertising world as ''Frankie''. She was an award-winning copywriter and author who was renowned for changing the way women were depicted in advertising. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the President's Council of Cornell Women Fund, Ithica, NY, Iris Cantor women's Health Center, 425 E 61 St, NY, NY 10021 or Hospital for Special Surgery, 535 E. 70 St, NY, NY 10021. >>AD#
