
        ZELNER-Hyman. Of Brooklyn, New York, in his 95th year. Passed away May 4, 1999. Will be remembered with love by Anna, his wife of 66 years; his sons, Martin and Larry, and their wives, Barbara and Marjorie; and his grandchildren, Bennet, Emily and Jonathan. Dedicated officer and active member of the Ocean Parkway Jewish Center. Services were held on May 6th at the Ocean Parkway Jewish Center in Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Ocean Parkway Jewish Center.
