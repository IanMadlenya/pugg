
        BORDEN-Barbara Zimmer de Jurenev. Of Tryon, NC passed away peacefully in her sleep on July 11, 2002 in her 98th year. Barbara is survived by her daughter, Nicole de Jurenev of Santa Fe, NM, her niece, Virginia Zimmer of New York City, and her stepsons, Phillip Borden of Laughlin, NV, & Christopher Borden of Pioneer, CA. For information, contact McFarland Funeral Chapel, Tryon, NC.
