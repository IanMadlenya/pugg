
        BERMAN-Gertrude. The American Friends of the Soroka Medical Center and the Management of the Soroka Medical Center of the Negev of Beer Sheva, Israel mourn the passing of their esteemed benefactor and friend. Her vision and support of the first comprehensive breast health center of the Negev will save lives for years to come. She will be sorely missed. Heartfelt condolences to her husband, Bennett and family. Mona Abramson Executive Director, AFSMC Prof. Shlomo Mor-Yosef General Director Soroka Medical Center
