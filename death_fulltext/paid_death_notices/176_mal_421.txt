
        CAPSON-Arthur. Retired Science teacher and Junior High School Principal in Brooklyn. He often said his greatest achievements were his daughter Alice, a high school math and computer teacher in Kansas and a graduate of Mt. Holyoke and Harvard and his grandchildren, Beth a high school senior and Jules a junior at Yale. He and his wife Eleanor traveled to the six continents and had been sent by the State Department in 1948 to study the Marshall Plan in Europe. He was a model husband and father and was looking forward to his 90th birthday in two months. Services Tuesday 11:30AM at the ''Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Interment will be in Kansas City, MO.
