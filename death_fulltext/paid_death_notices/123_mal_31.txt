
        KOCHWA - Shaul, PhD. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mt. Sinai Medical Center note with sadness the passing of our esteemed colleague, Shaul Kochwa, PhD, Professor of Medicine Emeritus. Associated with the Division of Hematology for morethan four decades, Dr. Kochwa was a renowned investigator in immunoglobulin studies who made many original contributions to the field and gave generously of his knowledge to others. We extend our condolences to his wife Hadasa, daughter Varda Kruman, granddaughter and to the entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, M.D., President Nathan G. Kase, M.D., Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President Wendy Z. Goldstein, Director Kristjan T. Ragnarsson, M. D. President, Medical Board
