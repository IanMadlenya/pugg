
        PROSKAUER--Henry G. Beloved son of the late Dr. Curt and Erna Proskauer and very dear brother of Dr. Paul F. Proskauer. Passed away on December 6th, 2006 at the age of 91. Henry was born in Breslau, Germany he studied architecture at the ETH in Zurich, Switzerland from 1935 to 1939 and came to America in 1940, where he was an architectural designer and office planner for four decades. He was a most wonderful, kind and extremely helpful son and brother. Funeral private.
