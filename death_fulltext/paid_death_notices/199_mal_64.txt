
        FARBER--Gene. Of Westport, CT, NYC and Roussillon, France. On August 1st, 2003, age 65, after a courageous 17 year battle with prostate cancer. Gene was the owner, along with his wife Joanna of Parc Monceau Country French Antiques in Westport. His family, Joanna, Jonathan, Samantha, Anna and Nicole deeply mourn his loss. Dad, you will be in our hearts forever.
