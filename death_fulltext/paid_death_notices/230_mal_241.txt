
        WINSTON--Richard Alan Passed away on March 15, due to complications following surgery. He was 78. He is survived by his wife and best friend of 18 years, Susan Harnick Winston; daughters Lisa Winston Wilentz and Stephanie Winston-Vann; granddaughter Dana Wilentz: sonsin-law Wayne Wilentz and Christopher Van; sister Carol Herman Janis and beloved cats Marcel, Winnie, Trixie and Norton. He was predeceased by his wife of 24 years, Zena Lakriz Winston; parents Charles and Gertude Winston and cat Rex. A baseball and swimming star at Horace Mann School, where he was also president of his senior class of 1945, he chose to forego a possible future on the pitcher's mound to join the family business, Harry Winston Inc., founded by his uncle. He worked at Harry Winston for 56 years as sales executive and Vice President. Richard will be remembered by everyone who knew him for his gift of making everyone he met feel like a friend for life, his passion for fishing, and most of all his love for his family, friends and life. A memorial service will take place April 20 at 2:30 PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, New York, New York. Contributions in his memory can be made to The New York Hospital Weill-Cornell Breast Center 525 East 68th Street, Box 123, New York, NY 10021
