
        BOAS-Ruth. Aged 98. On September 23, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Benjamin, loving mother of Muriel & her husband Sol Cantor, and the late Robert and daughterin-law Marjorie Levins; adored grandmother of five; great-grandmother of fifteen. Funeral private. ''She walks in beauty like the night of cloudless climes and starry skies, and all that's best of dark and bright meet in her aspect and her eyes.''
