
        BOETTIGHEIMER-Ludwig. On March 23, 2002, in his 94th year. Beloved husband of Marianne, devoted friend to many. Services today March 25, 11 AM, at Congregation Habonim, 44 West 66th St. Contributions to Congregation Habonim will be appreciated.
        BOETTIGHEIMER-Ludwig. The clergy, Board of Trustees and the entire membership of Congregation Habonim mourn the passing of our beloved founding member and past President, Ludwig Boettigheimer, a true builder and renaissance man. His strength, wisdom, and guidance will be greatly missed by all of us who knew and loved him. Leo Asen, President
