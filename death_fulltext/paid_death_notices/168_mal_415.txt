
        DAVIDMAN-Isidore. 74, of Boca Raton, FL, and Hopewell Junction, NY, died on January 27, 2001. He will be missed by his family and friends. He is survived by his devoted wife Irene, loving daughter Annette and husband Ted, loving son Stephen and wife Barbara. Three granddaughters Erin, Samantha and Nicole. He was a Senior Partner at the accounting firm of Kipnis and Karchmer. He also received a law degree at Brooklyn Law. He retired from practice in 1991. He will be missed by all who knew him. Services will be held at Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, at 2:30, Monday, January 29, Delray Beach, FL; 800343-5400.
