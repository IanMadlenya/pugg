
        MARANTZ -- Joan Lakin, Ph.D. The members of the Greater New York Chapter of Imago Relationship Therapists mourn the passing of Joan Lakin Marantz, Ph.D, our Chapter President, colleague, friend and mentor. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband Dr. Robert Marantz, her daughter Lisa Sloane, son-in-law, daughter-in-law and grandchildren. We will miss her leadership, intelligence, hospitality, warmth, charm, smile, generosity, enthusiasm, spirit, authenticity and boundless energy. We can genuinely say Joan Lakin Marantz has profoundly touched all of our lives for the better. >>AD#
