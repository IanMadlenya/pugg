
        LESE--Walter A. Age 104 on January 27, 2004. Survived by children Barbara Gunther of Bayport, NY, Elliot Lese of Carmel, IN, and Henri Lese of San Rafael, CA, five grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Mr. Lese, a retired CPA was Senior Managing Partner of Klein, Hinds and Finke, when the firm merged with Alexander Grant & Co. (now Grant Thornton Int'l). He received an honorary degree from Baruch College in 1999. Mr. Lese belonged to the Brotherhood Synagogue for 40 years and was an honorary member of B'nai Israel Reform Temple, Oakdale. Interment with military honors at Calverton National Cemetery was private.
