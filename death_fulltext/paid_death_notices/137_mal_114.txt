
        ROMMERDAHL-Jens M. A Memorial Service will be held at the Zion Lutheran Church, Saddle River, N.J. at 2PM, Saturday, June 13, 1998. Jens Rommerdahl was the General Manager of New York Marine Terminals, Port Authority of New York and New Jersey. He is survived by his wife Carla and the families of his sons Kurt and Lance. He received honors from Queen Margrethe II of Denmark for service to the Danish community in New York. He received honors presented by Queen Beatrix of the Netherlands for service to the Dutch Navy. He was awarded the Ellis Island Medal of Honor awarded to non-native American citizens for outstanding citizenship. He graduated Cum Laude from New York University.
