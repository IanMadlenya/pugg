
        WEISSMAN-Kenneth, M.D., age 55, of Watchung, NJ on August 6, 1999. Surviving are his wife Audrey Shachnow-Weissman, two daughters Candace and Taryn Weissman, one stepson Daniel Satterwhite, a brother Frederick T. Weissman and a sister Lorraine Glazer. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, August 8 at Higgins Home for Funerals, 752 Mountain Blvd, Watchung, NJ at 11:30 A.M. Interment Congregation Kneses Tifereth Israel Cemetery, Glenville, CT. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Holocaust Museum, New York, NY, American Cancer Society or the UJA Federation.
