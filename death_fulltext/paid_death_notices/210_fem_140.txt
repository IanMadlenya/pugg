
        ROMARY--Darlene. Died on July 23, 2004, in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, after a long illness. Born in 1937 in Goose Creek, Texas, she graduated from St. Anthony High School in Beaumont, Texas, and attended Lamar University and the University of Houston. She was employed as executive secretary to the president of Tenneco Chemicals in Houston. Darlene resided for 26 years in Westport, CT, where she served as President of the Westport Young Women's League. She was also a member of the Board of the Westport WMCA. An avid golfer, she was a member of Longshore Club, Westport, Shorehaven Country Club, East Norwalk, CT, Brooklawn Country Club, Fairfield, CT, and Old Marsh Golf Club, Palm Beach Gardens. Darlene is survived by her husband, O. Jules Romary of Jupiter, Florida; three children: William Romary of Malibu, CA, Thomas (wife Linda) Romary of Seattle, WA, and Mary (husband Sam) Wender of Larchmont, NY; two grandchildren: Sydney Alice Wender and Daniel Jules Romary; two sisters: Norma Touchstone and Ruth Reeves of Texas; and numerous nieces and nephews. The family will receive friends from 7 to 9 pm, Monday, July 26, at Howard Funeral Home, 754 U.S. Highway One, North Palm Beach, where a Rosary Service will be held 7 pm, Monday. A Mass of Resurrection will be celebrated at Saint Patrick Catholic Church, 13591 Prosperity Farms Road, Palm Beach Gardens, on Tuesday, July 27, at 10 am, with Reverend Father Bernard J. Dooley, S.J., as celebrant. Interment will follow at Riverside Memorial Park, Tequesta, FL. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of Darlene Romary may be made either to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL 33407 or to the Damon Runyon Cancer Research Fund.
