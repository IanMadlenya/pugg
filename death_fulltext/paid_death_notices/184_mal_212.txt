
        MURPHY-Mary Judith, M.D., M.P.H. The faculty, students, alumni and staff of Columbia's Mailman School of Public Health deeply mourn the passing of Mary Judith Murphy, loving wife of our dedicated & distinguished faculty member, and longtime head of the Department of Environmental Health Sciences, Joseph H. Graziano. Pediatrician and expert on childhood infectious diseases, Mary also enjoyed a career in the pharmaceutical industry. To Joe, their daughter, Caitlin, and his children, Michele and Joseph, and the Murphy family, we extend our deepest sympathy. Allan Rosenfield, M.D. Dean
