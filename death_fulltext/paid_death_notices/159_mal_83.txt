
        PIERCY-George T. The Board of Trustees, medical staff and administration of the Hospital for Special Surgery are deeply saddened by the death of our dear friend, George Piercy. Throughout his illustrious career at Exxon, from where Mr. Piercy retired as a Senior Vice-President and Exxon Board Member, he always found time to help his friends at HSS. We extend our prayerful condolences to his wife and family. Memorial services will be held on Monday, April 10th at 1:00 p.m. at Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church Chapel, 7 West 55th St., NYC. Richard L. Menschel, Chairman Aldo Papone, Co-Chairman Board of Trustees Russell F. Warren, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief Stephen A. Paget, M.D. Physician-in-Chief John R. Reynolds, President and Chief Executive Officer
