
        SOLENDER -- Sanford. The Trustees and Staff of The Jewish Museum mourn the loss of our esteemed Life Trustee, Sanford Solender. For over two decades, the Museum benefitted greatly from his attentive involvement and thoughtful presence on our Board. His unflagging enthusiasm for our programs was an immense source of encouragement to all. Our heartfelt condolences to his children, grandchildren, and the entire family. Susan Lytle Lipton, Chairman John L. Vogelstein, President Joan Rosenbaum, Director
        SOLENDER -- Sanford. The board and staff of Jewish Community Centers Association of North America (formerly JWB), mourn the loss of Sanford Solender, JWB's executive director from 1960-1970 and an inspired leader of the JCC movement. A consummate professional and mentor, he had an extraordinary impact on the JCC world. Our deepest condolences go out to his entire family and to all who had the good fortune of knowing him. Edward H. Kaplan, Chair Allan Finkelstein, President JCC Association
        SOLENDER--Sanford. JBI International (formerly The Jewish Braille Institute) expresses its heartfelt condolences to our esteemed Trustee, Stephen D. Solender, and his family, on the passing of his father, Sanford. We pray that his memory will serve as a blessing. Barbara B. Friedman, Pres. Dr. Ellen Isler, Executive Vice President
