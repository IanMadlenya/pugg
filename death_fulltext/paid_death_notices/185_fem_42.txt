
        PEALE-Jean Jarvie Darling, 65, a former instrumental music teacher, died June 3, 2002 at her home. She was the beloved wife of James, mother of Frances A. Peale, James G. D. Peale, and Christie E. Peale, and grandmother of James P. Mills, Alison C. Mills and J. Sullivan Peale. A Memorial Service will be held at 3 PM, Sunday, June 16th at her home in Bronxville, N.Y. Contributions may be sent to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St., Tuckahoe, NY 10707.
