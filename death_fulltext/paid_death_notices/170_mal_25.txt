
        CALFON-Alfred Fradji. A generous and humorous man, devoted physician, died on March 1, 2001. Loving father of Vivian, Daphne, Thomas, Theresa and Marcella. Husband of Maria, brother of Joseph, Jacques, and Nellie. He will be dearly missed by family and friends. Should friends desire, contributions may be made to any diabetic foundation. Service Sunday, 11:15 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
