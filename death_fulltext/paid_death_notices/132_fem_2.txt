
        d'ANCONA-Carolyn Tilles. On December 29, 1997. Widow of the late Benjamin d'Ancona. Her courage in the face of long term and severe disabilities was very special. Her intelligence and her musical and acting talents were considerable. She is survived by her brother, Bud Tilles, her niece and nephews, Wendy, Daniel, and Peter Tilles. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Arthritis Foundation, 122 East 42nd Street, New York, N.Y. 10168.
