
        SANGHVI - Prafulla. Beloved brother and uncle, passed away peacefully in New York on Wednesday, August 11th, 1999. After taking his Ph.D.in Economics from Cambridge University, Dr. Sanghvi had a distinguished career as a senior economist in the United Nations Development Program (UNDP) in New York for two decades. Upon returning to India, he served as a member of the Monopoly and Restrictive Trade Practices Commission (MRTP) of the Government of India. Just before retiring from his professional career, he was a Deputy Director of the Institute for Applied Economics Research in New Delhi. Dr. Sanghvi is survived by four brothers, four sisters, and numerous nieces and nephews. Family will receive friends on Friday, August 13th, from 7:00-9:00 PM, and services will be held on Saturday, August 14th at 12:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 81st St. and Madison Avenue.
