
        MALLAH-Louise. Born December 19, 1919, died January 19, 1999. Beloved wife of Sam for 53 years. Adored mother of Lily and Alice, and mother-in-law of Richard and Mike. CHerished grandmother of Charles and wife, Sangeeta, Shari and Laurence, Lisa and husband Stuart, and Seth. Loving great grandmother of Natalie, Rebecca and Zamir. A survivor of the Holocaust, she will be remembered for her strength, courage and love. She will always be in our hearts. Funeral services on Thursday, January 21 at 10 A.M. at RiversideNassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck.
