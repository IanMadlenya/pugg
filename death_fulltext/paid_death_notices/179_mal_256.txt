
        ZELENSKY-Samuel, 92. Of New York, NY, passed away on Dec. 19, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Jewel Zelensky, son of Max and Eva Zelensky. He is survived by loving brother, Arnold Allen (Phyllis) & many nieces, nephews, great-nieces, great-nephews, god children and friends. Dear brother to the late Beatrice Nelson and Lillian Fractenberg. A very special man to all who knew him. Esteemed attorney and graduate of New York University Law School, he also served our country in the World War II War Crimes Unit. Services will be held at Riverside, 76th Street & Amsterdam Avenue on Thursday, December 20, 2001 at 11:15AM. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations be sent to New York University Law School for the Sam and Jewel Zelensky Scholarship Fund.
