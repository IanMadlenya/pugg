
        SALANT-Jack. Died at age 91, on April 17, 1999. He was a devoted father and husband. He worked for over 40 years for the New York State Department of Insurance, and was President of the Association of New York State Insurance Department Examiners in 1953 and 1954. He served in the Air Force during World War II. He painted and wrote beautiful poetry. His intelligence, his extraordinary command of language, and his sense of humor were a source of delight to all who knew him.
