
        LIPTON-Fay Gardner., devoted mother, grandmother and great grandmother died 6/19 three weeks after her 94th birthday. She was born in St. Louis, MO and lived for over 70 years in NYC. She was predeceased by her husband of 60 years, Dr. Benjamin G. Lipton who died in 1986. Fay Lipton was the cherished mother of Alice Eckstein, June Kreisberg and Brian Lipton, mother-in-law of Gerald Kreisberg, Harry Eckstein and Lois Lipton, grandmother of Lee, Amy, Mark, Martine, Evan and Mia, great grandmother of Nicole, Kelsey and Daniel.
