
        PALAZZO--Joseph J. Age 52. On August 14, 2003. Loving and cherished father of Melissa and Joseph. Beloved grandfather of Marissa. Life partner of Dean Steigauf. Also survived by his former wife and loving friend, Colleen Dubois. Joseph passed away of a heart attack on the Queensboro Bridge while walking home during the Blackout of 2003. He will be forever missed, loved, and always in our hearts. Family and friends may visit at Leo F. Kearns Funeral Home, 6140 Woodhaven Blvd, Rego Park, N.Y. on Tuesday 2 to 5 and 7 to 10PM. Funeral Mass Wednesday Resurrection Ascension Church, 9:45AM. Interment Cypress Hills Cemetery. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
