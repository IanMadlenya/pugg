
        RUBIN-Dorothy (Slotnick). December 7, 2001, three weeks before her 82nd birthday. Predeceased by her beloved husband Jack and sister Lee. Avid reader, film buff, grammarian and the true spirit and historian of the family. She lived in Delray, FL, but her heart was in New York. She was the most wonderful, generous, beautiful, loving and proudest mother of Sandi and Steven Spivak, and Ellen and Neil Stepman, and grandmother of Lisa, Jessica and Amy. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hospice by the Sea.
