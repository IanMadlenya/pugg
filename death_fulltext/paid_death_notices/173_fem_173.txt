
        BERTRAND - Marion Barrett (Mrs. John J.) of Greenwich, CT, died on Monday, June 18, 2001. She is survived by her daughters, Barbara (Mrs. T. Brock Saxe) of New Canaan, CT, Marion (Mrs. Robert K. Eagan) of Harrison, NY, and her daughter-in-law, Linda (Mrs. Charles B. Bertrand) of Palm City, FL. Her husband, John, died in 1982, and her son Charles in 2000. She was the sister of Gertrude (Mrs. Arthur Davidson), Ruth (Mrs. Jack Lande), and Edward J. Barrett. She is also predeceased by her sister Helen (Mrs. Stephen P. Glennon) and her brother, Dr. Charles V. Barrett. Mrs. Bertrand is survived by 11 grandchildren and 5 great grandchildren. Family will receive friends at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Post Road, Rye, NY on Wednesday, June 20, 5PM-8PM. A Mass of Christian Burial, St. Michael's Church, North Street, Greenwich, CT, 10 AM Thursday. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Vincent's Hospital, North Street, Harrison, New York, would be appreciated.
