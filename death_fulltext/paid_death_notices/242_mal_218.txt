
        NATHANSON--Irwin B., of Palm Beach Gardens, FL and Purchase, NY died suddenly on March 20th, 2007 at age 85. Born in Pittsburgh. He graduated from George Washington University. His long and successful business career included working for Monsanto and senior executive positions for United Merchants and Manufacturers and Troy Mills before founding Trans World Products. He served on the boards of Jewish Board of Family and Children's Service and Linden Hill Schools. He was a member of Quaker Ridge Golf Club and the Chemists Club. In his retirement, he lived life to its fullest through golf, his grandchildren and travel. He is survived by his devoted wife of 54 years, Sally, his brother Bennett, his loving sons Larry and Andy and their wives Larraine and Elyse and his beloved grandchildren Lilly, Brian, Charlie, Alison, Kevin and Jack.
