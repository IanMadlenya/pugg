
        RITTMAN-William Campbell, of Bronxville, NY, died suddenly, May 10, 1999. He was a graduate of Harvard College and Harvard Law School. During World War II Major Rittman flew B25's in the 14th Air Force Squadron attached to the Chinese Air Force, spending three years in China. After the war, Mr. Rittman was involved in international business and retired as Vice President in charge of International Operations for Richardson-Merrell. He is survived by his wife Patricia, two sons, Walter F. and Frank S., daughter-in-law Fran, and two grandchildren.
