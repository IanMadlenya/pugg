
        PADOVANO-Louis, S.J., M.D. On September 20, 2000. Beloved son of the late John and Margaret Padovano. Loving brother of John Padovano, Jr. and Angela Alaimo. Dear brother-in-law of Consuelo Padovano and Joseph Anthony Alaimo. Cherished uncle of Lisa and Christopher Alaimo, John III, Margaret and Cathleen Padovano and Monica Buttagliola. Dear friend of Rev. Patrick J. Frawley. Dr. Padovano was a graduate of Georgetown University and the Georgetown University School of Medicine. He maintained a practice in OB/GYN before entering the Society of Jesus in 1964. He was ordained a priest on June 12, 1969. After ordination he completed an additional residency in psychiatry at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center and maintained his practice in New York until his passing. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell ''The Funeral Chapel'' 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St on Thursday, September 21 and Friday, September 22, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial St. Ignatius Loyola RC Church, 980 Park Ave at 84 Street, Saturday, September 23, at 11 AM. Interment in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hanover, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Jesuit Seminary and Mission Bureau, 39 E. 83rd St, NY, NY 10028
        PADOVANO-Louis, S.J.,M.D. Our beloved uncle, may flights of angels sing thee to thy rest. We love you. John, Monica, Mia and Anita
