
        GOODYEAR--Austin. 85, died at his home in West Brooklyn, ME of bladder cancer August 14. Mr. Goodyear purchased Ellsworth Builders Supply in 1970. Under his leadership, the company grew from one store to ten branches, stretching along the Maine coast from Calais to Rockland. He was predeceased by his first wife, Louisa Robins Goodyear. He is survived by his wife, Sara Sulari Goodyear, two daughters, Grace Roosevelt and Cullen Goodyear, and one son, Tom Goodyear.
