
        CONNELL-John T., 77, July 22, 2001. He was a lifelong resident of the Bronx, parishioner of St. Brendan's Church, and a U.S. Army veteran, having served during WW II in Europe and the Pacific. An accountant for H.P.D. who was retired in 1985. Beloved husband of the late Mary Connell. Dear father of James J., John T. and his wife Maureen. Brother of Dorothy Connell and the late Eleanor Palmer. Also survived by four nieces and two nephews. Family and friends are invited to call Tuesday and Wednesday, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM at John F.X. McKeon & Son Funeral Home, 3129 Perry Avenue, Bronx. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Thursday at 9:45AM at St. Brendan's Church. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla.
