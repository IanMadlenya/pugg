
        VIRDEN-Lillian Sudler. Born in Sussex County Delaware, died on October 17, 2001 in her 108th year. Devoted wife of the late Prospere Shelton Virden, loving mother of Ellen Virden White and the late Prospere S. Virden, Jr. and Suzanne V. Patterson. She is survived also by 13 grandchildren and 24 great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held on Saturday, October 20 at 12:30PM, Barratts Chapel, Frederica, Delaware. Contributions in her memory may be made to Historic Houses of Odessa, Odessa Delaware and the Amercan Red Cross
