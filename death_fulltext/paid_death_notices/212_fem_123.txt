
        DAVID--Else. Died peacefully September 14th at age 101. Beloved cousin of Ernest W. and Amy Michel and family, Lotte Rein and families, Israel. Widow of Cantor Arthur David. Wonderful friend, Holocaust survivor, longtime volunteer, active in her Synagogue who named Golden Age Club in her honor. Services 12 Noon Sunday, September 19th, Congregation Ohav Shaloum, 4624 Broadway.
