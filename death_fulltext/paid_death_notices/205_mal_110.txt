
        WILSON--Donald E. On February 6, 2004, in Manchester, NJ. Born September 27, 1921. Graduated Stuyvesant H.S. and NYU. Served in WWII as Master Sgt. in 1st Division. Awarded Silver Star for bravery on Omaha Beach on D-Day, Purple Heart and Bronze Star. Retired sales executive at American Cyanamid. Leaves Theresa, beloved wife of 58 years, his brother James, and four sons, Thomas of NYC, Donald Jr. of Blauvelt, NY, Robert of Pearl River, NY, and Gregory of NYC, and eight grandchildren. Rest in peace, dad.
