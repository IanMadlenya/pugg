
        YOUNG-Dr. Daniel M. November 8, 1998. Survived by his parents and sister of Woonsocket, R.I., his wife, Mary Monroe, of Yonkers, two daughters, two stepsons, and five grandchildren. Admired and cherished by his family, friends, and colleagues, and by the many whose lives he touched as Jungian analyst, Psychotherapist, and Clergyman. His was a rare and trustworthy soul, and his loss is deeply mourned. Memorial service on Sunday, November 15 at 2PM, at the Church of St. James the Less, Crane Road at Church Lane, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Church of St. James the Less, the Church at Point O' Woods, Point O' Woods, New York, or to the charity of your choice.
