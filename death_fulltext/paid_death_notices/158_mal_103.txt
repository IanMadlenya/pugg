
        RAHMAN-Mujibur, M.D. The New York Medical Group PC, the Board of Directors and the entire medical and administrative staff mourn the loss of Mujibur Rahman, MD on Tuesday, March 6, 2000. Dr. Rahman was affiliated with the New York Medical Group, PC since March 20, 1978 and became a shareholder of the corporation in September, 1980. He was the Center Medical Director for the Grand Concourse Center until his untimely passing. He will be sorely missed by the patients he served so faithfully over the last 22 years. His numerous friends within the health care community also join in sympathy. Moshe Labi, MD, Medical Director New York Medical Group, PC
