
        MURASE-Ichiro. With deep sorrow the family of Ichiro Murase notes his passing on June 4, 2002. His compassion, kindness and concern for all will be remembered. Ichiro was a computer consultant and held a Ph.D. in Economics from Columbia, a J.D. from New York Law School and a M.B.A from NYU. He is survived by his wife Vlasta, his daughter Sayo, his sisters Chiyoko and Kimiko, and his brother Jiro.
