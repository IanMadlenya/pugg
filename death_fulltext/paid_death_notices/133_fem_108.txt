
        FORREST-Barbara (nee Heimerdinger). On February 11, 1998 in her 72nd year. Adoring mother of Terry; beloved sister of John F. Heimerdinger; loving aunt to Linda, Daniel and Charles Heimerdinger; five grand-nephews and one grand-niece. Funeral services will be held at St. Agnes Church in Lake Placid on Monday, February 16. A Memorial service will be held on Long Island on February 18.
        FORREST-Barbara. The Officers, Board of Directors and Staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind note with deep sorrow the passing of Barbara Forrest, beloved sister of John F. Heimerdinger, Past President of The Guild. We extend our deepest sympathies to the family. James M. Dubin, Chairman Alan R. Morse, President & C.E.O.
