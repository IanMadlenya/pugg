
        SCHEIDER--Edward J. passed away on Sunday, October 22, 2006 in NYC. He was 90 years old. He was a resident of NYC and Fleischmanns, NY. Mr. Scheider was born in NYC and attended the Graduate School of Commerce, and received his MBA from the NYU Stern School of Business where he was also an adjunct professor fo finance for 25 years. He served as a Marine in World War II. Mr. Scheider was a partner and senior Vice-President at Clark Dodge and Kidder Peabody for 48 years, a former chairman of the board of South Atlantic Financial Corp, a member of Albert Gallatin Association, former President of the Lotos Club, and financial columnist for the Journal of Commerce. He served on the Board of Independent Holding Corp, was the founder of Kids in the Kaatskills, and a member of the Grand Lodge of Free and Accepted Masons of the State of New York. Mr. Scheider was predeceased by his beloved wife, Kaye. He is survived by his niece, Pauline Schmidt, and his nephews, George Josberger and Edward Josberger. Services will be held on Wednesday, October 25, 2006 from 2-4 and 7-9 at The Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1081 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. An additional memorial will be held in Fleischmanns, NY on Saturday, October 28 at 11AM at Mr. Scheider's farm, Longview. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Kids in the Kaatskills, PO Box 1017, Margaretsville, NY 12455.
