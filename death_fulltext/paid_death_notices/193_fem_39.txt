
        FRIEDLANDER-Anna. Age 99. Died February 2, 2003. World traveler, beloved mother to Seymour, Vera and Barbara, grandmother to Paul, Beth, Debbie, Michael, Joanna and Clifford. Great grandmother to Joel, Emily and Jordan. Took her final trip to rest with her late husband Morris and beloved daughter Pearl. An independent woman with a zest for life, only age could defeat her. Graveside services at Riverside Cemetery, NJ Wednesday, February 5 at 11AM.
