
        DUNAWAY-Walter Hubert. Retired EVP of Jeffries Avlon, Inc., NY, NY, June 7, 1999 in Lumpkin, Georgia. A Navy Veteran of the Korean War, he was a survivor of the USS Cochino which went down in the North Sea in 1949. Survived by wife Colleen of Lumpkin, GA (formerly of Hastings-on-Hudson, NY); daughter Elizabeth Levin of Berkeley Heights, NJ; brothers John & Royce and sister Carolyn Taylor and two grandchildren. Donations in his memory to the Lumpkin, GA Volunteer Fire Dept.
