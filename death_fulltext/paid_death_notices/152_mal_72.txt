
        KNAPP-Harry. Of Stonington, CT, born in New York City, he was the son of Elizabeth M. and Harry K. Knapp. He served as Investment Advisor and Board Member of the Bafflin Foundation, a non-profit ecological group. Survived by his wife, Marie-Louise Ehrensvard Weidermann Knapp, 2 daughters, Susan Thomas and Alexandra Knapp, 2 sons, Christopher and Nicholas Hinds. Memorial service at Calvary Church, Stonington, on Saturday 11AM. For information call Dinoto Funeral Home, Mystic, CT.
