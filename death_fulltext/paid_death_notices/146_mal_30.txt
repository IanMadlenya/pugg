
        ALTMAN-Albert A., 84, of Monroe Twp. (formerly of Brooklyn) on February 28, 1999. He earned a Juris Doctor from St. Johns University, and was a prominent Brooklyn Real Estate Attorney. He was Past President of Flatbush Real Estate Board. He sang as ''Al Albee, The Romantic Troubadour'' during the 1940's on WEVD. Surviving are his wife Diane; three sons, Kenneth, Lawrence & Sanford; a daughter Sheri, and 10 grandchildren. Services Tuesday 12:15 PM, Mt. Sinai Memorial Chapels, East Brunswick, New Jersey. Burial at Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge.
