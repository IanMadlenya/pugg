
        BOLAND - Ruth M. (nee Montgomery). A native of Dublin, Ireland. Peacefully in her sleep on November 8th. Beloved wife of the late Lt. Col. John P. Boland. Devoted mother of Eithne Boland, Ann Krupp, Eavan Hooke and Ellen Hagan. Cherished grandmother of six. Reposing at the Gannon Funeral Home, 152 East 28th St, NYC on Sunday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian burial on Monday, at 10 AM, at Our Lady of the Scapular and St. Stephen's Church. Interment U.S.M.A. at West Point.
