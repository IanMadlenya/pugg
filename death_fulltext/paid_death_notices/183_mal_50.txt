
        WEINBERG-Isaac. Born December 1, 1900, NYC, died at home in Lawrence, NY, March 28, 2002, at 101 years of age. Dear husband of Flo Kahn Weinberg, stepfather of Mike and Stanley Kahn. He was a partner with his brothers, Nathan, Reuben and Morris in the law firm of Weinberg & Weinberg, at 32 Broadway, NYC. He is survived by his nieces and nephews, Matthew Alan, Meta Weinberg Lane, Helen Weinberg Gootrad, Marion Weinberg Risman, Gladys Weinberg Post, Judith Weinberg Campbell, and Daniel Weinberg.
