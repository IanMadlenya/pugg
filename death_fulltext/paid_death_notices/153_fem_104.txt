
        SITZER-Esther H., 88. A resident of Forest Trace, Lauderhill, Florida. Died peacefully on October 8, 1999. She is mourned by all who loved her, especially Evelyn and Bob Gomer, Helen and Paul Hodys and Bill and Kathy Sitzer. She was a lifelong active member of the ADL and a past President of Victory Chapter of B'nai B'rith. Cherished by all who knew her, she will be sorely missed. Donations may be made to ADL in her memory.
