
        LUSTIG--Trudi. Died September 9, 2003, exactly 15 years to the day after her beloved husband Mike. Trudi and Mike are dancing together in heaven now. Trudi was an enormous, loving presence to all who knew her. Her sense of humor, love of fun, family and friends brought happiness to any situation. Mother, wife, grandmother, aunt, sister, friend, she will be missed by all. Lori and Morgan Hertzan
