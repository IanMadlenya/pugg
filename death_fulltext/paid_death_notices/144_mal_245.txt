
        SEALEY-Antonio. Better known as Seventeen. Died suddenly on October 2, 1998. Age 29. Revered poet, rapper and recording artist. Star performer and urbane host of the ''Flippin' the Script: Rap Meets Poetry'' reading series at the Fez and S.O.B's, 1993-1997. Beloved son of Anna Payne Bush and Stafford Sealey, stepson of Harris Bush, cousin and buddy of Kenny Johnson. In his own words, Seventeen has been ''admitted into heaven where the true angels roam/And the pain never makes it past the barrier zone.''
