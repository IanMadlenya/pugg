
        MUTTERPERL-Muriel. On October 31, 1999, after a long illness at the age of 79. Beloved wife of the late Martin; devoted mother of Donald and Nancy Mutterperl, and William and Nancy Mutterperl; adoring grandmother of John and Heather, Jeffrey, Matthew and Emily, and Adam; great-grandmother of Noah; dear sister to Shirley Jacobs and the late Janice Nick; devoted friend and companion of Harvey Silverstein. A woman of great strength and courage with boundless love for those whose lives she touched. Family will receive friends Monday, 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, Tuesday at 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
        MUTTERPERL - Muriel. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Muriel Mutterperl. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to her family. Bernard Steinweg, President David Hershberg, Secretary
        MUTTERPERL-Muriel W. Your wisdom, your stories and your presence have made a great impact on our lives. With love, your nieces, Gail, Patty, Fran, Joanne and Susan
