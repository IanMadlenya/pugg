
        RAINE--Frederick. Died suddenly on September 26, 2004, at his home in New Rochelle. Beloved husband of Mary Jenness Raine. Loving father of Spencer and Evan. Loyal friend, gifted administrator, avid sportsman, devoted Yankee fan. Fred will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved him. Service Tuesday, September 28, 2004 10 a.m. Westchester Jewish Center, Mamaroneck,NY. Donations may be sent to North Shore Long Island Jewish Foundation, 125 Community Dr., Great Neck, NY 11021
        RAINE--Frederick D. The staff of the North Shore-Long Island Jewish (LIJ) Health System is greatly saddened by the death Sunday of our friend and colleague, Frederick D. Raine, of New Rochelle, NY. Fred was a true gentleman who was beloved by all who knew him. As North Shore-LIJ's assistant vice president of managed care, Fred had a keen intellect and extensive knowledge of the complex healthcare industry, in which he worked for more than 30 years. He was widely admired and respected by all who worked with him. We will miss him greatly. We extend our most sincere condolences to his wife, Mary Jenness Raine, and his two sons, Spencer and Evan. Funeral services will be held at 10 a.m. today at the Westchester Jewish Center in Mamaroneck, followed by burial at Kensico Cemetery in Valhalla. Howard Gold Senior Vice President Michael Dowling President & CEO North Shore-LIJ Health System
