
        STAEHELIN - Marguerite M., daughter of Max Staehelin, a well known Swiss banker and lawyer, died peacefully in Basel, Switzerland on April 30, aged 93, survived by her nephews Thomas and Max Staehelin, a great nephew, a great niece and by her sister-in-law, Mrs. Helene Staehelin. A former resident of New York, she was a lifelong supporter of musicians, conductors and composers and directed The Swiss Music Library in New York. Her many friends here and abroad will miss her. Funeral services at St. Leonardkirche in Basel on Tuesday, May 9.
