
        PURCELL-Gerard W. President of Gerard W. Purcell Associates in New York. He had more than 50 years experience in the entertainment industry. Experience that encompassed an enormous variety of accomplishments. He co-produced many of the Perry Como and Red Skelton shows. Gerard Purcell was the business and personal manager for many stars and Grammy winners, such as Al Hirt and Hugo Montenegro, author Dr. Maya Angelou, songwriter Gale Garnett, poet Rod McKuen, and discovered comedian Jay Leno. He was Eddy Arnold's manager for the past 35 years. He was president of the American Conference of Personal Managers for 20 years. He produced more than 4,000 concerts. He got Perry Como to record ''Faraway Places'', ''Love Somebody'' for Doris Day, ''Java'' for Al Hirt, ''Make the World Go Away'' for Eddy Arnold, ''I Honestly Love You'' for Olivia Newton-John, etc. He is survived by his wife, actress-author Monique Van Vooren, and his son, entrepreneur Eric Purcell. Family will receive friends on Thursday 25 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Funeral Mass Friday 11 AM at St. Ignatius Loyola RC Church, 84th Street and Park Avenue.
