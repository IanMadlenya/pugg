
        ALLEN-Herbert A. We at The Presbyterian Hospital, ColumbiaPresbyterian Medical Center, deeply mourn the death of Herbert A. Allen. Through his exemplary generosity, Mr. Allen made possible the creation of the community hospital that bears his family's name, The Allen Pavilion, which benefits the residents of Washington Heights, Inwood and Riverdale, among others. We extend our condolences to his family, who may rest assured that Mr. Allen's memory will always be honored by the life-saving and -enriching services provided at The Allen Pavilion. Daniel B. Burke, Co-Chairman of the Board of Trustees John J. Mack, Co-Chairman of the Board of Trustees William T. Speck, M.D. President and CEO
