
        LOBMAN-Ethel Bloch., 75, died September 28th. She is survived by Arthur, her husband of 40 years, and her daughters Sara and Carrie. A meeting to celebrate her life & 55 years as a revolutionary and member of the Socialist Workers Party will be held on Sunday, October 10th, 1 PM, at the School of International Affairs, Columbia University, 420 West 118 Street, New York, NY. 212-399-7257
