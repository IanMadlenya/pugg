
        ANDREWS - Maryann Reeve Larkin. Longtime resident of Tudor City, teacher and Broadway and TV actress died of pneumonia on February 21 at the age of 75. She will be greatly missed by her friends, family and former students. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her name to the performing arts charity of your choice. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, February 28 at 1PM at U.N. Chapel, 777 U.N. Plaza, 44 St. and First Ave. A reception will follow.
