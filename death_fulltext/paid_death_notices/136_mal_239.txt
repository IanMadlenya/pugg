
        MARTIN-Roger Louis, Sr., of Boxford, MA. Died May 19 at the Lahey Clinic in Burlington at age 66. He was the husband of Esther M. (Stewart) Martin of Boxford, father of Dr. George Martin, D.M.D. of Lansdowne, PA., Roger Louis Martin, Jr., of High Wycombe, England, and Monique Marguerite Martin of NYC. He was also the brother of Paul Georges Martin of Brazil. A funeral Mass and burial will be Friday in Georgetown, MA. Merton E. Roberts Jr. Funeral Home, 14 Pleasant Street, Georgetown, Mass. 01833. Memorial contributions may be made to the Catholic Charities, 49 Franklin Street, Boston, MA. 02110.
