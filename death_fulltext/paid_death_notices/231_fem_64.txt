
        BERNS--Barrie, 92. Of New York City. She was the wife of ''Mike'' Bernes, beloved mother of Toni of Edwards, CO, Michael of New York City and Peter of Greenwich, Connecticut, and grandmother of Tiffany, Mathew, Sabrina and Robert. She has been the Vice President of Roburn Agencies, an international marketing company, a former columnist for the Rockaway Journal and the Nassau Herald, an early member of the Board of Lincoln Center, and the author of ''Three Out of Four Wives.'' She was the last survivor of seven sisters, and is survived also by 16 nephews and nieces and 30 grand-nephews and grandnieces.
