
        MILLER-William Harlowe. 93, of Waverly Heights, Gladwyne, PA, died on December 31, 2002. Born June 9, 1909, in Plainfield, NJ, he was a former resident of Lloyd Harbor and Oyster Bay, NY, for 60 years. He graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy in 1927, and Princeton University in 1931, where he played varsity football and crew. He served in the US Army Air Corps in WWII, retiring as a full Colonel, and was awarded the Legion of Merit. He then pursued a career with Central Hanover Bank, later Manufacturers Hanover Bank, retiring in 1974 as Senior Vice President. He was Mayor of the Village of Lloyd Harbor, NY, 1974-79 and was a Trustee of Bradford College. He is survived by his sons Ludlow, of Chestnut Hill, PA, and William H. Jr., of Norwalk, CT; a daughter Martha M. Massey, of Belmont, MA; six grandchildren, three great-grandchildren and a sister, Elizabeth Hood, of Newtown Square, PA. His wife of 63 years, Martha, died in 1997. A Memorial Service will be held at 2 PM, Tuesday, January 7, 2003 at St. Christopher's Episcopal Church, Gladwyne, PA. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Willian H. Miller '27 Wellness Fund, Phillips Exeter Academy, 20 Main Street, Exeter, NH 03833-2460.
