
        BRALOWER-William ''Hoot.'' Of Old Brookville, NY. With his usual wit and determination, celebrated his 80th birthday on April 16th. Died peacefully April 20. Devoted grandfather and father, wonderful husband and loving brother, he is survived by his wife, BeBe, sons and daughters-in-law, James and Linda, and John and Naomi, and grandchildren, Alex, Amanda, and Thomas, and brother, Stanley. Memorial service will be held on Wednesday, April 22nd at 11:30 at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York. Remembrances may be made to Saint Francis Hospital, Roslyn, New York.
