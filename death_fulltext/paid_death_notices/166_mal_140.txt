
        ROSS-Sheldon, 75. On November 12, 2000. Beloved husband of Phyllis. Loving father of Robert and Laurie. Dear brother-in-law of Jeff (Mimi) Licht, Vera Rabino and uncle of Erica Licht and Michele Kogon. Resident of Manhattan, formerly of Detroit, he taught those around him to love the arts. He leaves behind many friends and colleagues who will dearly miss his warmth, wit and kindness. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., 212288-3500, Wednesday, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow in Amenia, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Daniel Ferro Vocal Program, Inc., 575 Madison Avenue, Suite 1006, NY NY 10022 or to a charity of your choice.
