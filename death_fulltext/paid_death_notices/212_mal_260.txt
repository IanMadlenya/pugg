
        WILSON--David Anthony III. Age twenty-one, died on Wednesday, September 22, 2004, in the Adirondack Park near Wells, NY. Majoring in computer science, David was a senior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY. David attended the Episcopal School and Trinity School in New York City. After three years at Brunswick School in Greenwich, CT, he attended Gould Academy in Bethel, Maine. He graduated high school from The Pomfret School in Pomfret, CT. David was an expert skier, and an avid photographer. He enjoyed all types of music and loved movies. Dave educated himself on topics such as artificial intelligence, economics, and politics by reading, research, and debate. He was an ardent Apple Computer proponent. His favorite author was Noam Chomsky. He is survived by his parents, Ann and David A. Wilson, Jr. of New Canaan, CT, his sister, Elizabeth R. Wilson of New York City, maternal grandparents Lt. Col. George W. Gaumond, USAF (retired), and Madeline R. Gaumond of Bradenton, FL, and paternal grandfather David A. Wilson of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL. A memorial service will be held at the First Presbyterian Church in New Canaan on September 30th at 3 p.m. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to: American Civil Liberties Union (www. aclu.org) or Heifer International (www.heifer.org).
