
        STIEBEL-Eric. On September 21, 2000 at the age of 89. Beloved husband of Irene. Loving father of Gerald and his wife, Penelope. Adoring grandfather of Catherine Fiebach, Daniel & Hunter Stiebel. Services were held. Contributions in his memory may be made to UJA/Federation.
        STIEBEL-Eric. The National Antique & Art Dealers Association of America, Inc., mourns the passing of its longtime member, Eric Stiebel, father of Gerald, our past president, of Stiebel, Ltd. (formerly Rosenberg & Stiebel) and extends heartfelt condolences to his family. Andrew H. Chait, President Arlie Sulka, Secretary
