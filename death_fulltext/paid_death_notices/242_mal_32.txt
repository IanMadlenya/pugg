
        SPURR--Gregory W, Jr., 83, of Stuart, FL, previously of Short Hills, NJ, died Monday, February 26, at his home. Born in Rye, NY, Mr. Spurr was a resident of Stuart for 18 years. He received his B.A. from Yale University and his MBA from the Amos Tuck Business School. During WWII, he served as Sergeant in the US Army. Prior to retirement, Mr. Spurr was a Senior Executive Vice President for The Bank of New York, where he was employed for 38 years. Survivors include his wife Cynthia of 53 years, his children Gregory, Jeffrey, Cindy, and Pamela, his sister Jean, and five grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, the family would prefer donations in memory of Gregory Spurr Jr., to the Society for Protection of NH Forests, 54 Portsmouth St., Concord, NH 03301.
