
        WACHTEL--Norma of Suffern, NY and Boca Raton, FL. Died March 18, 2004 at the age of 74. Devoted wife of Ed. Loving mother of Alan, Jonathan, Adam, Adina, Gail, Douglas and Susan. Beloved grandmother of Jason, Matthew, Toni, Scott, Jessica, Lauren, Matthew, Emma, Erica and Nicole. Her spirit and joy were an inspiration to us all, and she will be dearly missed. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, March 21 at 1 P.M. at Hellman Memorial Chapel, 15 State Street, Spring Valley, NY (845-356-8600). In lieu of flowers, donations for leukemia and lymphoma research can be made to the Mt. Sinai School of Medicine/Janet Cuttner Research Fund, 1735 York Avenue, Suite P2, New York, New York 10128.
