
        GLEICK--Donen. 81, died December 17. He was the devoted husband of 52 years to Beth, beloved father of James, Peter, and Elizabeth, and grandfather of Daniel, Harry, Jeremy, Margaret and Julia. He was a lawyer in New York City for 50 years, most recently at Kaye, Scholer, Fierman, Hayes & Handler, where he headed the Wills and Estates Department. Born October 11, 1924, in St. Louis, he served during World War II in the Army Air Force and the Counter Intelligence Branch of the U.S. Occupation Headquarters in Vienna, graduated from Yale College (1945W), Phi Beta Kappa, and received his law degree from Yale Law School. Before moving to New York, he worked in Washington as an attorney for the Air Force and Small Business Administration. He was a passionate lover of books, a lifelong Cardinals fan, a bridge enthusiast and a birder with a life list of more than 600 species. Memorial service on Wednesday, December 21, 3:30 pm, at Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to a charity of choice.
