
        BLEIWEISS -- Ruth Marion. Died peacefully May 11, 2003 on Mother's Day and her wedding anniversary. Beloved mother of Ellen and Elizabeth. She will be missed by all whose lives she touched in her 85 years. She was married to Dr. Irwin Bleiweiss for 51 years and lived in Plainview, New York and West Palm Beach, FL. She was a wife, mother, daughter, sister, aunt, friend and singer. Though her career as an entertainer was short, her glorious voice and magnificent smile will live in our hearts forever. >>AD#
