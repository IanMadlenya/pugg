
        BEYDA-Shirley Attie, 1909-1997. Beloved matriarch, mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, leader in community service and role model. Her passing is the end of an era. She leaves us a legacy of intelligence, integrity, dignity, compassion, tolerance, grace and courage. She continues to inspire us to seek knowledge, to help others, and to love life. ''She walks in beauty.''
