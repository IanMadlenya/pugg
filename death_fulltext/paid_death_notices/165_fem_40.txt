
        HOROWITZ-Jeanette Lillian, nee Jacob, who would have been 90 on November 29, died at the Salem Lutheran Home Skilled Nursing Facility in Oakland, CA. on October 3, 2000. Mrs. Horowitz was a teacher in New York City for over 35 years and lived in the Bronx, NY, Boonton, NJ, and Walnut Creek, CA. She was predeceased by her husband of 54 years, Robert. She is survived by her son, Alexander Horowitz, MD of Livingston, NJ, her twin daughters Roslyn Kleinsinger of Oakland CA. and Paula Horowitz-Engle of Berkeley, CA, and nine grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Oakland, CA. at a later date.
