
        VARDY--Edith E. 77, died peacefully on February 10. Beloved wife of Moshe, loving mother of Joel, Iris and Michael, proud grandmother of Noah, Alex, Sofia, Andrew and David. Survived ghetto, Auschwitz, death march, slave labor. Fought for Israeli independence. Nurse, artist and speaker on the Holocaust to thousands. We will never forget.
        $(6$)VARDY--Edith Esther. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Howard A. Stecker, Sr. Rabbi Jerome Seiler, MD, President
