
        HOCHMEISTER-Mathilde J. Age 90. Of Millbrook, N.Y., formerly of Queens, died Saturday, November 23, 2002. She was one of the first female graduates of Cornell University in 1934, and worked as the Administrator of Social Services for the South Bronx. She was pre-deceased by her brother Thomas, and is survived by her sister Rose Hochmeister and her neice JoAnn VanDeCarr. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery. Inquiries to McHoul Funeral Home, 845-221-2000.
