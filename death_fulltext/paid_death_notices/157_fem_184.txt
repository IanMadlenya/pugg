
        ZOTTMAN-Roberta A., a New York native and member of the Daughters of the American Revolution, died on January 24, 2000, of a stroke in Sacramento, CA. She was 93. Mrs. Zottman received her nursing degree from Montefiore Hospital School of Nursing in New York City in 1928, and practiced for 50 years as a private duty nurse. Born in Ausable Chasm, NY, on January 5, 1907, Mrs. Zottman lived and worked in New York until 1970, when she relocated to Sacramento, CA. Mrs. Zottman is survived by a son and several grandchildren. Mrs. Zottman will be interred at the Woodstock Hill Cemetery in Woodstock, CT.
