
        McCARTHY-Virginia M., September 9th, 1997. Beloved wife of the late John E. McCarthy. Loving mother of Maureen C. McCarthy and J. Thomas McCarthy. Friends may call Friday, 2-5, 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Mass of Christian Burial Sat., September 13th, 10 AM, St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84th St. Interment will follow in Parnell, MI. She was a graduate of the Class of 1931 University of Michigan, Ann Arbor School of Architecture.
