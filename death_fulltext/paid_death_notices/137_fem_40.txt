
        LEWIS-Gertrude. The partners and staff of Lipsky, Goodkin & Company, PC, are deeply saddened at the passing of Mrs. Gertrude Lewis, beloved mother of Mrs. Janice Schneider wife of ourvery much respected and esteemed partner and friend Mr. Daniel Schneider, and grandmother of our also esteemed partner and friend, Mr. Ronald Schneider. Cherished, adored and admired mother, grandmother and great grandmother, an exemplary woman of strong character, keen mind, fortitude and determination, she leaves behind a legacy of love and a family of solid and sound foundation. She is deeply mourned and greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences to the entire bereaved family. Lipsky, Goodkin and Company, PC.
