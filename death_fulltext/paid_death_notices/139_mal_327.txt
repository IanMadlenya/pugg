
        KLEIN-Nathan. The Workmen's Circle/Arbeter Ring mourns the passing of our longtime activist, Nathan (Nat) Klein. A member of our Organization for more than 60 years, his dedication and tireless work in many leadership positions including several terms on our National Executive Board were an example for all. Our deepest condolences to his family. Mark Mlotek, President Robert Kestenbaum, Executive Director
