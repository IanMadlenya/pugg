
        ELMEZZI - Thomas. The passing of Mr. Thomas Elmezzi, the Sponsor of a new ''I Have a Dream'' Program in Ravenswood, Queens, is mourned by the ''I Have a Dream'' New York Metro Area and the national ''I Have a Dream'' Foundation. The children in his Program will benefit from his generosity for many years to come. His Fellow Sponsors, ''I Have a Dream'' NY Metro Area and nationwide
