
        SILVERMAN-Samuel. Of Tamarac, FL, on September 22, 2002. Beloved and devoted husband of Roslyn. Survived by sons Alan, Larry and Henry; daughters Patti Nevins and Lyn Silverman; sister Ceciel Platt. Mr. Silverman was C.E.O. of Petrocelli Clothes. He lived in Great Neck, NY for thirty years before relocating to FL. Adored by his six grandchildren Lea, Ilene, Jessica, Jeff, Eric and Courtney. Loved and respected by all who knew him, we will miss him greatly.
