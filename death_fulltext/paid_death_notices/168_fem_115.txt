
        GAYNOR-Geraldyne. Of Palm Beach, FL, on January 9, 2001 in her 80th year. Beloved wife of James. Cherished mother of Karen & Elliot Hershberg and Robert & Betty Ann Gaynor. Adored grandmother of Lisa & James, Julie & Jeffrey, Jill and Lynne and loving great grandmother of Samantha and Benjamin. She will be forever in our hearts. ''Grams, we will cherish all of our memories with you forever. We love you and miss you.'' A memorial service will be held for family and friends on Friday, January 12 at 10:30AM at Temple Israel, 1901 North Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, FL.
        GAYNOR-Geraldyne. The Board of Governors and Members of Fenway Golf Club record with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member, Geraldyne Gaynor, and extend our sincere condolences to her bereaved husband, James, and the members of her family. Jeffrey Citron, President Marvin Leffler, Secretary
