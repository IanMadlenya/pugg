
        KRAUTHAMER - Barbara. On February 18, 2003. Loving sister of Elissa. Cherished and adored aunt of Jonathan, Sarah, Benjamin and Anne. Great-aunt of Julia, Mariah, Sophia and Elvis. Service Friday 11:30 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. There will be a memorial held at Westbeth, 55 Bethune St. in the courtyard, 7 P.M., Saturday, February 22, 2003.
