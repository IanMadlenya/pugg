
        IMMERMAN-Isidore. Age 95, died peacefully on November 30, 2000. Loving husband of Evelyn, father of Richard and Michael, and grandfather of Tyler, Morgan and David. He will be missed. Funeral services will be held Sunday, December 3 at 11:30 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon, followed by interment at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery.
        IMMERMAN-Isidore S. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club note with sadness the passing of our esteemed life member, and extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Evelyn, and his family. Corey B. Rabin, President Ronald Stuart, Secretary
