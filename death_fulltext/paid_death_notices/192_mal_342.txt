
        CONSUEGRA - Hugo. The partners and staff of Brennan Beer Gorman/Architects (BBG) are deeply saddened by the death of Hugo Consuegra. Mr. Consuegra joined BBG in 1985 and was made a partner in 1991. A gifted architect and accomplished painter, Mr. Consuegra lent his talents to many of the firm's projects. He will be greatly missed by all who worked with him. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife and family.
