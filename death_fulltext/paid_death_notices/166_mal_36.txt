
        GRIGG-Joseph W. of Peachtree City, GA, passed away on October 29th at the age of 90. Educated at Westminster Boys School in London and at Trinity College, Cambridge, he went on to an illustrious 50-year career with United Press. Serving at various times as Bureau Manager in Berlin, London, Paris, Frankfurt and Bonn, he retired in 1982 as Chief European Correspondent. He is survived by his sister Elizabeth S. Grigg of Ypsilanti, MI, daughter Margaret Griffin of Bluffton, SC, and son Richard A. Grigg of Peachtree City, GA.
