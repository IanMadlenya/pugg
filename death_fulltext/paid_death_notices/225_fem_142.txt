
        KRULL--Sylvia R. (nee Gerber) of Boynton Beach, FL, and Englewood, NJ, on October 16. Born August 15, 1920 in Newark, NJ. Loving sister, wife, mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. Married 64 years to the late Hyman Krull. Survived by daughter Elise Herz and sons Arthur, Albert and David; grandchildren Harold, Ellen, Michael, Mark, Jeremy, Kathryn and Marissa; and great-granddaughter Erin. A caring, compassionate friend to all who knew her.
