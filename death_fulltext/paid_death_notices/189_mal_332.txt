
        BREUER - Dr. Harry Jr. (Bud). 71, of New York City and Sag Harbor, died on October 24, 2002, at NYU Medical Center. He is survived by his beloved wife Fran, his devoted daughter Gina and her husband Eric Hadley, and his cherished granddaughter Francesca. Also survived by his brothers Robert and Tony and their families. Dr. Breuer served as a clinical psychologist at Bellevue Hospital for 27 years and was a faculty member of NYU Medical Center. Friends may call at Redden Funeral Home, 325 W. 14th St, on Sunday, October 27th, 5-8 PM.
