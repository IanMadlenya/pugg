
        LEVY--Frances, on September 1, 2005 at the age of 101, of Somerville, NJ and Pompano Beach, FL. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Harry Levy and mother of Marsha and Joel Handel and the late Barbara. She will be missed and remembered by her grandchildren Beth and Armin, Libby and Matt, Marnie and Shir and Lauren and David and her great grandchildren Harrison, Sophie, Max, Kiya, Parker, Grace and Zachary, all of whose lives she touched in many ways. We will not grieve but find strength in what remains behind.
