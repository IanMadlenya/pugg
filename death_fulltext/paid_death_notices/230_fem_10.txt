
        SEIF--Molly Feller. February 27 in her 100th year peacefully at home, widow of Joseph B. Seif. Surviving are sister Gertrude Arkin, daughters Joan Seif and Sharon Silberfarb; grandsons Uriel, Daniel and Michael Levi, Jacob and Joseph Silberfarb, and nine great-grandchildren. Funeral noon today, at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st Street and Amsterdam Ave.
