
        STRACHAN - Stanley (Stan), founder and publisher of the National Mortgage News, January 7, 1997. Beloved husband of Tobyann and father of Hillary. Services Thursday, 11:15 AM of ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Diabetes Foundation or the American Kidney Foundation.
        STRACHAN - Stanley. The New York Financial Writers' Association regrets the passing of its longtime member and Vice President Stanley Strachan. The Association wishes to express its deepest sympathy to his family and friends.  Marvin Mack, President
