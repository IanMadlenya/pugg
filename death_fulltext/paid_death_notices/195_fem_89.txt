
        STEVENS-Edith S. on April 3, 2003. Lived in Laguna Beach, CA. Daughter of Helen B. Beinecke and the late Walter A. Schiffer. Survived by her husband P. Nicholas Bradley, son John W. and daughter E. Helen Marcos, beloved grandmother of John Patrick and Lillie Ann Marcos. She was a long time patron of the arts and an active philanthropist loved by those whose lives she touched. Our sorrow will transcend into eternal love.
