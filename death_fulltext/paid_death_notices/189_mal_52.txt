
        TUCK-Edward Hallam. The Board of Directors and staff of the French-American Foundation express their great sadness at the passing of their distinguished friend, Board member and former president. Few people have done more in their lives to strenghten the ties between France and the United States. He was a man of great dignity, intelligence and generosity who will be missed by all of us. We offer our deepest sympathy to Liliane and to all of his family. Michael Patterson, Chairman Anthony A. Smith, Presient
