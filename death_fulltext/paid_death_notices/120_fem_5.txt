
        WOLINS - Sarah Emily. On Sunday December 29, 1996, at her home in Sog Harbor, NY. Beloved friend of Michael Blake, devoted daughter of Joseph Wolins, of New York, adored sister of Richard Lazoor, of Tarrytown, N.Y. David Lazoor, of the Bronx, NY, & John Wolins, of Philadelphia, PA. Sarah Wollins was an accomplished actress, artist and entrepreneur. She was so beloved in Sog Harbor that numerous benefits were given for her 2 years ago to assist her in her fight with breast cancer. She touched the lives of so many. She will be sorely missed. Donations to The Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, New York, New York, will be appreciated.
