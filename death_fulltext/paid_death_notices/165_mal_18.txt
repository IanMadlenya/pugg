
        ENGLANDER - Philip. Beloved husband of Betty, loving and devoted father of Robert and Rita, cherished grandfather of Adam, Alex, Jessica, Hillary and Drew, loving and devoted father-in-law of Nancy and Jeffrey, passed quietly on September 29, 2000. Founder of the Intercollegiate Press in 1935 and Publisher until 1999. His sense of humor and devotion to his family will forever be remembered and treasured.
