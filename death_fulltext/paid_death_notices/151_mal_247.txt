
        STERNBERG-S. David, M.D. Dear husband and best friend of Shirley. Very proud and loving father of Jean (Andy), Richard (Judy). Grandfather of Michael and Emily Krakoff. Brother of Ann Zinchuk of Hyannis, Ma. and the late Leo Sternberg. Loved by his niece, nephews, family, friends, patients and collegues.
        STERNBERG-S. David. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Dr. S. David Sternberg, a cherished friend and major supporter of our mission. Dr. Sternberg left a legacy of hope for all those touched by his extraordinary generosity and concern, particularly the Jewish community on Long Island and throughout the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Shirley, his children Jean (Andy), Richard (Judy), and his grandchildren. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Arthur Katz, LI Cabinet Chair Gary Reback, LI Director Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
