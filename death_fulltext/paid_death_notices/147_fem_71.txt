
        SCHWABE-Ina (Regina) L. Kahn. Died peacefully April 5, 1999, after a long illness. Widow of Erwin S. Schwabe. Stepmother of the late Robert Schwabe. Mourned by her sister Helen D.K. Lessing, her nieces Joan C. Lessing and Pamela R.L. Friedman, her grandniece Elizabeth Lessing Friedman and her grandnephew Paul Lessing Friedman. Her memory will live on among the many friends she cultivated during a lifetime of almost eighty years during which she was involved in a wide variety of interests.
