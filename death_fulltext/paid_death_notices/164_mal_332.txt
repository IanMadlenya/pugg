
        TINKER-Robert Lee. Born October 6, 1926 in Teaneck, NJ, son of the late Teresa and Malcolm Tinker, died at home suddenly on Sunday, September 10, 2000. He is mourned by his many friends and colleagues. Funeral services will be private. A memorial will be announced.
        TINKER-Robert L. The entire New York Design Center family is saddened by the loss our dear friend and colleauge, Bob Tinker. He showed us all that professionalism and success never has to preclude kindness. James Druckman, President New York Design Center Inc.
