
        ALLEN--Marge (nee Meisel). Of Ellington, Connecticut and Palm Beach, Florida, died December 24, 2005 at the age of 79. Beloved wife of 50 years of Matthew, loving mother of Steven and mother-in-law of Gretchen and adoring grandmother of Rebecca. A native New Yorker, she worked in the television and radio industries prior to her marriage and in real estate sales with her husband afterward. She will be remembered for her sense of style, panache, wit and lifelong activity as a tennis player which originated as a teenager in Central Park. She will be deeply missed. Private services have been held. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
