
        BOCCHINO-Michael L. Firefighter and battalion aide, FDNY, Battalion 48, Engine 240. Born November 25, 1955, tragically lost in line of duty on September 11, 2001 at the World Trade Center disaster. Michael leaves behind his loving parents, Michael A. Bocchino and Lucy Bocchino, his sister, Debra Lavender, and his brother, Thomas Bocchino. A memorial mass will be held Saturday, October 13th, 2001, 2 P.M., at Immaculate Heart of Mary RC Church, 2805 Fort Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn, New York. For further information contact Blair-Mazzarella Funeral Home, 718-282-1164. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the Engine 240 Widows and Orphans Fund, C/O Engine 240 FDNY, 1307 Prospect Avenue, Brooklyn, New York 11218. God Bless America
