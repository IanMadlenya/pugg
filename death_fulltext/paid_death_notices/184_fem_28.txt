
        FISCHER-Sheila. On May 3, 2002. Beloved mother of Susan FischerDiaz and Jill Conway. Adored grandmother of Mariel Conway and Nico Diaz. Cherished sister of Helen and Alan Goodfield. ''Aunty'' of Lisa, Suzanne, Karen and Nancy. ''Granty'' to Elena, Casey and Gaylen. Loving niece of Sol Obstfeld. Memorial service 4 PM, Tuesday, at ''The Riverside,'' Amsterdam Avenue and 76th St.
