
        KLUGE--Willard G., died at home on Friday, April 20, 2007. He was 82. Born in Philadelphia on September 18, 1924, to the late Willard and Alice (Perry) Kluge, he lived in Montclair for 72 years before moving to Tewksbury 10 years ago. He was the president and CEO of the textiles firm of Artistic Identification Systems for over 50 years, retiring in 1996. He was a veteran of the United States Air Force during WWII, and trained as a navigator before the end of the war. He had a BA Degree from the Philadelphia Textile Institute, attended Montclair Academy, and graduated from the Kimball Union Academy. Mr. Kluge is survived by his wife, the former Kathleen Madigan; two sons John P. Kluge of Maryland, and Chris Kluge of Newton; four daughters, Linda Kluge of Idaho, Janet Wiggin of Califon, NJ, Barbara Hickox of Boonton Twp., and Jennifer Kluge of California; two sisters, Nancy Hall Rumery of North Carolina, and Barbara Kluge Deming of Connecticut; fourteen grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. He is also survived by his former wife, Margaret Hulst Jenkins, mother of John, Chris, Linda, Janet and Barbara. For more information contact Bailey Funeral Home, 176 Main Street, Peapack, NJ 07977. (908) 234-0590. All services are private at the request of the family. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to the charity of your choice would be appreciated by the family.
