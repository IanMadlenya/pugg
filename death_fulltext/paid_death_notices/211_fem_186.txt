
        GRANITE--Myrna Judith. On August 26. Of Ardsley, N.Y. Beloved wife of Bernie. Cherished mother, grandmother, sister and friend to all. Her wisdom and humanity touched everyone throughout her life and her passing epitomized the ultimate of dignity. She will be deeply missed by all the lives she touched. Funeral services will be held at ''Gutterman's'', 175 N. Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, N.Y. at 1PM Sunday, August 29.
