
        BERMAN - Miriam. Beloved wife of the late Samuel and Jerome B. Katz. This independent, fiesty woman, born 1900, died January 2, and will be missed by her children Jerome B. Katz, Jr. and Suzanne Geller, her daughter-in-law Louisa and son-in-law Daniel Geller, five grandchildren, seven great-grandchildren, beloved cousins, nieces and nephews. Services private.
