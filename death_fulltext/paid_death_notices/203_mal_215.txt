
        MARMOR--Judd, MD. The Trustees, Officers and Members of The American Academy of Psychoanalysis and Dynamic Psychiatry express profound sorrow for the loss of Dr. Judd Marmor, Founding Member and Past-President of the Academy and Past-President of the American Psychiatric Association. Dr. Marmor was an esteemed Life Fellow of the Academy whose contributions as a seminal thinker and compassionate, dedicated clinician and teacher influenced generations. We express our condolences to Dr. Marmor's son Michael, and to his entire family. The American Academy of Psychoanalysis and Dynamic Psychiatry Ann R. Turkel, MD President Cesar A. Alfonso, MD Secretary
