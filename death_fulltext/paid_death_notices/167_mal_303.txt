
        SILVER-Edward M., D.D.S.. Died December 20, 2000, in his 98th year in the company of his loving daughter Ellen Greenberger, sonin-law Michael Burton, and grandchildren Kari Edwards and David Silver. He practiced dentistry in Lawrence, LI, until age 85. We benefited immeasurably from his nurturance, humor, exceptional intellect, and humanitarian values. Memorial donations may be made to Hoag Hospital Foundation, Box 6100, Newport Beach, CA 92658.
        SILVER-Dr. E.M. It is with a deep sense of loss that we say ''goodbye'' to our beloved Uncle Eddie who brought love, laughter and meaning to our lives. Betty Kaye Taylor, Bob and Ellen Kaye and Steve Kaye
