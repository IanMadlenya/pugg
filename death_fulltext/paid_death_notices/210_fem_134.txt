
        TAUSEND--Marie of Encinitas, Calif passed away peacefully on July 18, 2004 at 95 years.She had a brief career in NYC theater and film in Hollywood in the 1930s, appearing in City Lights etc. In 1940, she married Francis Tausend of NYC, where she lived until 1976, when they moved to Florida then to Southern Calif. Francis passed away in 1986. She is survived by nephews Fred Tausend of Seattle, Hank Tausend of Chicago; Jeff Cooper and John Cooper, and her niece Susan Clark-Ovitt of Calif.
