
        KIRSCHNER--Ethyl Kuhl on May 27th, 2004, age 82. Wife of George, mother of Carl Kirschner and Sandy Reider. Grandmother of Emily, Jessie, Jonathan, Valerie and Amanda. Sister of Toby Zak. Born in Brooklyn, NY she graduated Hunter College and began her working career in the Brooklyn Naval Yard in 1942. Later she used her training as a teacher in the NYC school system until retiring in 1986. Those who knew her will speak of her with kindness and admiration. Funeral services are private. The family will receive friends at the home of Mr. George Kirschner on June 4th and 5th from 1 - 5pm. Bannworth Funeral Home, Elizabeth, NJ.
