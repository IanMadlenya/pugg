
        McMAHON-Stanton D. Age 82, of Jamesburg, NJ, formerly of Larchmont, NY, died on August 22, 1999. Attorney in NYC for many years. Husband of the late Katherine Beha McMahon. Survived by his daughter Mary Katherine Hawryluk, son-in-law Richard J. Hawryluk, two grandsons David and Kevin Hawryluk, brother Austin J. McMahon Jr., and sisters Mary S. and Eleanor G. McMahon. Visitation Monday, 7-8:30 PM, at A.S. Cole Funeral Home, 22 N. Main St, Cranbury, NJ. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 9:30 AM, Queenship of Mary Church, Plainsboro, NJ.
