
        MERKIN-Hermann. The National Jewish Outreach Program mourns the passing of its generous benefactor, Hermann Merkin, prominent leader of the American Jewish community, who, together with his wife, Ursula, has played a crucial role in NJOP's efforts to assure a Jewish future for American Jewry. Mr. Merkin's sons Ezra and David have also been deeply involved in NJOP's outreach efforts, demonstrating the values and heritage he transmitted to them and all his children. Sincere condolences to the family. Rabbi Ephraim Z. Buchwald, Dir.
        MERKIN - Hermann. Yeshivat Sha'alvim mourns the passing of our dear friend who personified the ideal of Torah and Derech Eretz. Heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Ulla Merkin, a woman of valor and her family. Hamakom Yenachemchem. Rav Avraham Avidan, Rosh HaYeshiva Rav Meir Schlesinger, Founder Alex Hornstein, President Werner Rosenbaum, Chairman
        MERKIN-Hermann. Hebron Yeshiva ''Knesset Israel'' Jerusalem mourns the passing of a great, outstanding Jew and a distinguished communal leader. May GD comfort his son, Ezra, a devoted patron of our Yeshiva, and the entire family in their great loss. Rabbi Isaac Hebroni, President
