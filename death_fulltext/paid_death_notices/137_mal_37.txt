
        GOULD-Eugene Emanual, MD. Passed away March 4, 1998. He was a Mensch, a book lover, a self-made man and a genius. At 19 orphaned and penniless, he put himself through college and medical school, working odd jobs. A loving father and husband, he leaves a wife Evelyn, son Claude and daughter, son-in-law and grandson, Sandy, Adam Jordan Cronin.
