
        WEILL-Lisbeth G. On Feb. 7, 1999. Devoted wife of the late Harold Weill, mother of Patricia Park of Brookline, MA., Judith Weber and Victoria Weill-Hagai of NYC, grandmother of Pamela, Mark, Eric and Lisa. Funeral services private. Donations may be made to the Children's Blood Foundation, 333 E. 38th St., NYC 10016.
        WEILL-Lizabeth. The Officers and Directors of the Children's Blood Foundation mourn the loss of their fellow Director, Lizabeth Weill. She was the widow of Harold Weill, co-founder with Dr. Carl H. Smith, of the Children's Blood Foundation in 1952. We wish to extend our sincerest sympathy to her family. John Calicchio, Chairman Margaret Hilgartner, M.D., Pres. Lawrence Bass, V.P. Susan Byrne, Executive Director
