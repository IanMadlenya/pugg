
        RICHNER-George A. Executive recruiter, retailer, institutional food executive and a longtime resident of Summit, NJ, died December 3, 2001. Beloved husband of Beulah (Bee) May Young Richner. Father of Trina Richner Lawson of NYC. Brother of William Eugene Richner of Homosassa, FL. Uncle of seven nieces and one nephew. A memorial service to be held Saturday, January 5, 2002, at 11 AM in the Chapel of Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church, 7 West 55th Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church.
