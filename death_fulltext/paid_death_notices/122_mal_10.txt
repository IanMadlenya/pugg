
        FELDBAU-Dr. Alfred E., M.D., 83, of Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of Brooklyn, N.Y., passed away on Monday, Feb. 24, 1997. Dr. Feldbau practiced family medicine in the Brooklyn area for 46 years. He is survived by his beloved wife Sophie of 58 years; son, Dr. Gary Feldbau; Daughter, Shelly Schack; 6 grandchildren; and 2 sisters; Margaret and Edith. Services and interment were held at the Star of David Memorial Chapel and Cemetery, North Lauderdale, FL.
