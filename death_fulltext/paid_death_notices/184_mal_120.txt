
        KEEGAN-Robert E. Of the Bronx, died on May 8, 2002. Robert was a former Secretary of the Committee on Character & Fitness, Appellate Division First Department. He is survived by his beloved wife, Yvonne; his children, Gary, Glenn, and Nancy; and grandchildren Stacy, Zachary, and Morgan. Visiting will be at the Flynn Memorial Home, Yonkers, NY, on Friday 2-4 and 7-9pm. Funeral Mass, St. Barnabas Church, Bronx, Saturday at 9:45am. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne NY.
