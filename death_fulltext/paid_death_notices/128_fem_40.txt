
        DOUGLAS-Vivian E., September 4, 1997 of Philadelphia, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Founder of Cultural Crossroads, Lafayette Avenue Presbyterian Church, Brooklyn, NY and active in numerous organizations such as The Links, Inc., Brooklyn, NY, and the Lawyers' Wives, Brooklyn, NY. Wife of the late Stanley Moreland Douglas, Assistant Attorney General State of New York. Survived by godchildren Sarah Taylor, Samuel Dilworth-Leslie, Colin Gray and many close friends. Graveside service to be held from Evergreen Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY on Wednesday, September 10 at 11:30 AM. Memorial contributions made in her name to AMISTDD Research Center, Tulane University, 6823 St. Charles Ave, New Orleans, LA 70118 would be appreciated. Oliver H. Bair Company, Philadelphia PA.
