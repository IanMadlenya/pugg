
        MARACHE-Nancie Allen. Age 65. Beloved wife and mother, died May 14, 1997 in Greenwich, CT. Born in Milwaukee, WS and educated at Westover and Briarcliff. She is survived by her husband Herbert W. Marache, Jr., five children Nina King, Victoria Marache, Eugenie Pavelic, Herbert Marache, III and Mark Marache, six grandchildren, her sister Jebb Allen Miller and brother Nathan R. Allen, Jr. Memorial service 2:30 P.M., Monday, May 18th, In Christ Church, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Eagle Hill School, Greenwich, Connecticut.
