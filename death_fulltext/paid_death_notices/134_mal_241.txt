
        BOWLER-William R. Age 83. Died peacefully at home on Thursday, March 19, 1998. Beloved husband of Adele. Devoted father of William, Howard, David, Carolyn and Meredyth. Loving grandfather. His warmth, generosity and insight will be deeply missed. A memorial service will be held at Wave Hill, Riverdale, NY, at 11 AM, Monday, March 30. In lieu of flowers, donations made in his memory to the Fortune Society, 39 West 19 St., New York, NY 10011, or Neighborhood Coalition for Shelter, 237 East 77 St., New York, NY 10021, would be appreciated.
