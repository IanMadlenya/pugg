
        D'ARCY--Richard A. (Dick). Of Babylon, New York on April 12. Formerly of NYC and Florida. Jerome Robbins dancer, choreographer. Broadway credits include On the Town, Brigadoon, Streets of Paris and Showgirl. Survived by his loving sister, brother, numerous nieces, nephews, grandnieces and nephews, great-grandnieces and nephews and many dear friends. Richard had a special appreciation for life and its beauty. He and his stories of Broadway life will be missed by all who knew him. Cremation private. Memorial tribute to be announced.
