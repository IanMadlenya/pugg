
        BORTZ-Bernard J. Devoted son of the late Fanny Bortz. Loving husband of the late Phyllis Elkin Bortz. Beloved father of Joyce G. Fiedler and John G. and Jean G. Bortz. Loving father-in-law of Jeffrey Fiedler and Laurie Kaplan. Devoted grandfather of Jessica and Candra Fiedler and Rebeccah and Jacob Bortz. A professional engineer with Ford, Bacon and Davis for many years and a private consultant in his later years. He was a member of Temple Israel, the Mid-Westchester Y and the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. He was an active board member of the co-op at 240 Garth Road where he resided until his death. Bernie was a caring and selfless friend, a pillar of strength to his family and a helping hand to those less fortunate. He will be sorely missed, but not forgotten. Services will be held Sunday, 12 Noon, at ''The Riverside'', 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon. Interment Beth Israel Memorial Park, Woodbridge, NJ. Shiva will be held at the Fiedler residence through Tuesday and the Bortz residence on Tuesday only. Donations may be made to the Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, NY NY 10021. (646) 227-3546.
