
        KATZ - Mildred H. Died peacefully at home on October 17, 2002, at the age of 96. Beloved wife of the late Leonard Katz. Mother of Tony Hill and Polly Hill Landess, stepmother of Laura Katz and John Katz. Loving grandmother to Simon and Elliot Hill, Joshua, Michael and Nicholas Landess and Kimberly Katz. Mrs. Katz was a merchandise manager at Bergdorf Goodman for 30 years and millinery buyer at Lord & Taylor. Active in many charities, she was a founding member of the Manhattan Chapter of the Retinitis Pigmentosa Foundation. Services at home, 1025 Fifth Avenue, Sunday, 1 PM. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to the Retinitis Pigmentosa Foundation. She will be missed for her beauty, generosity, and easy laugh.
