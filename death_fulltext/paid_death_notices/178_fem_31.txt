
        HALL-Julia Elizabeth. Passed away at her home in NYC at age 83, on October 29, 2001. Born in London within the sound of Bow Bells. Daughter of Harriett Cass Newman and Harold Newman. Longtime UN staff member, painter, photographer and world traveler. Julia is survived by her son John, her daughter Catherine, her grandchildren Andrea, Alex, Sebastian and Drew, and by her brother Harry and sister Millie. A dear friend to many, she will be deeply missed by us all. A memorial service will be held at the United Nations Chapel, at 777 UN Plaza, on November 20 at 1 PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
