
        BLAU-Bernard (Bernie), 86. Of Singer Island, FL, and New York City, died on April 21, after a brief illness in Florida. Bernie was a retired professor of English at Staten Island Community College and Hunter College. Survivors include his wife Rhoda, children Eileen, Kenneth and Donna, children-inlaw Harold & Susan, grandchildren Robert, Eric, Steven, Jennifer, Amy, Rachel and Laura, and great grandson Jacob. We loved him. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Hadassah, NYC.
