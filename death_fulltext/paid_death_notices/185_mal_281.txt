
        PALTROWITZ - M. Louis. Died in his 91st year of natural causes on June 23, 2002. He is survived by his beloved and cherished wife, Irene Paltrowitz (nee Berlin), of Elmira, NY, and Margate, FL, devoted sons and daughter, Dr. Irving Paltrowitz of Fort Lee, NJ, J. Lawrence Paltrowitz Esq. of Glen Falls, NY, and Phylis Warren of Gainesville, FL. Lou was blessed with ten wonderful grandchildren who adored him and will cherish their memories of him always.
