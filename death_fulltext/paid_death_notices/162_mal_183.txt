
        DIAMOND - J. George, M.D. Peacefully on July 16, 2000 in his 92nd year. Of Pompano Beach, FL., formerly of Plainfield and Long Branch, N.J. He will be profoundly missed by his most loving and dearly loved wife of 55 years, Evelyne (nee Stern), his loving son Dr. Mark (Leslie), his beloved daughter Darlene Kleiner (Sam), his adoring grandchildren Jeremy, Benjamin, Joshua and Arielle and his many nieces, nephews, grand nieces and grand nephews. He was legendary for his devotion to his family and his patients, and known for his great sense of humor. He was affiliated with Muhlenberg Hospital, Plainfield, N.J. for nearly 60 years. Funeral 11AM Wednesday, July 19th, Burroughs, Kohr & Dangler, 106 Main St, Madison, N.J. Memorial contributions may be made to Parkinson's Disease Foundation. He lived a long, full and happy life and now, may he rest in peace.
