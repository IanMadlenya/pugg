
        COLODNY-Dorothy S., 92. Of Longboat Key, Florida, died on March 9, 1998 in Sarasota, Florida. She was born on August 26, 1905 in Philadelphia, PA. She moved to Florida thirty years ago from Bronxville, NY. She is survived by 2 sons; Mark J. Colodny of Boca Raton, FL., and Peter I. Colodny of Port Chester, NY; 5 grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Memorial donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
