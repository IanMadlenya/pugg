
        HIRSHON-Helen Raiss. Died on October 7, 2001 at home after a long illness. She was the widow of the late John Raiss and of the late Walter Hirshon. She was born in Seattle, WA, resided in NYC for many years, and was a longtime resident of Sands Point, New York. Beloved mother of Linda de Spirlet, John Carl Raiss, and James Frederick Raiss. Beloved grandmother of Andrea Dodd Warble, Kevin K. Dodd, Elizabeth, Monica and Jennifer Raiss and Jonathan Raiss, and beloved great grandmother of Keenan Thomas and Reilly Murphy Warble. Funeral service private. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate donations sent to the Long Island Alzheimer's Association.
