
        FINBERG--Barbara D. Born February 26, 1929 in Pueblo, CO. Died Saturday, March 5, 2005 in New York City. Married to the late Alan R. Finberg on June 21, 1953, in Boston, MA. Retired from the Carnegie Corp. of New York where she was Executive Vice President. Board member of serveral foundations and not-for-profit organizations. She is survived by her brother Bob Denning and his wife Kate, and brother-in-law Donald Finberg, and his wife Hela and their children, Karen and Dana. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made to The Barbara D. and Alan R. Finberg Scholarship Fund, Stanford University, Office of Development, Francis C. Arrillaga Alumni Center, 326 Galvez St, Stanford, CA 94305-6105. Memorial service will be held at a time to be determined.
