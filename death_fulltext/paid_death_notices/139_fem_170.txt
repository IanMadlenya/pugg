
        LUZ-Jean Osmun. Died of liver cancer in her home in Bedminster, N.J. on August 9, 1998. Jean was born in New York City, lived her early life in Madison, N.J., and resided in Bedminster for the last 38 years. Jean is survived by her husband of 43 years, Carl, her daughter, Rebecca Storniolo, a sister, a brother, and 2 grandchildren. She was predeceased by her son, Carl III. Jean was the grand daughter of Mildred and James McGraw, the founder of The McGraw Hill Publishing Co, Inc.
