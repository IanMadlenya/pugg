
        TAITTONEN-Edith, September 30, 1997. A graduate of Ohio State University, a veteran of the U.S. Navy, a New Yorker since 1957, she worked for many years for the Community Service Society and for the Community Council of Greater New York, and was an active member of the Women's City Club. She is survived by her sister, Thelma Whitehead of Westland, MI, a niece and nephew, and five grand-nephews and grand-nieces. She was a good, direct and helpful person, and her family and her friends and associates mourn her. A memorial service will be held at the Women's City Club. Contributions may be made to any good cause of your choice. The Addiss Family
