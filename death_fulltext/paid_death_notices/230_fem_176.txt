
        SPATZ--Paula. 88 of Louisville, passed away peacefully on March 15. A life long learner and avid reader, she graduated college at 73. She was preceded in death by her husband Jack, son Gene and sister Minna Schielman. She is survived by daughters Amy Lowen and Cathy Spatz Widom; granddaughters Rebecca Widom, Jessica Lowen and Hannah Lowen; and sons-in-law Michael Maxfield and Gary Lowen. Burial will be at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY, Sunday, 1 PM. Donations may be made to the Jewish Community Federation of Louisville, KY.
