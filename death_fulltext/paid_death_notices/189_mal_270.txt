
        SCHOLL-Maestro Cliff. A professional musician and well-known Westchester entertainer, died quietly at home on October 20, 2002. Resting at Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY on Wednesday from 3:30PM to 7:30PM where his funeral service will be held Thursday 10:30 A.M. Interment will follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla.
