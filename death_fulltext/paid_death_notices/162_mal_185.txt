
        O'DONNELL-Joseph Patrick. Family of Joseph Patrick O'Donnell of New York City deeply mourn his passing on July 17, 2000 in Montclair, N.J. Retired NYPD Homicide Detective, USMC. Cherished father of Thomas O'Donnell of Niskayuna, N.Y., Susan MacNeil of Long Island, Peter O'Donnell of N.Y. and the late Joseph C. O'Donnell of N.Y. Beloved Grandpa to Emily Rose and Molly O'Donnell, Caitlin and Daniel MacNeil, fatherin-law of Don and Diane, cherished brother of Patrick O'Donnell of Seattle, Eileen O'Dea of Brooklyn, Frankie Brunner and Thomas O'Donnell of NYC, Julia Siegener of Montclair, N.J. and Mary McDonald of Ridgewood, N.J. Adored uncle Joe to 17 nieces and nephews. Beloved brother-in-law to Ann, Mac, Jimmy, Ray and late Joe Brunner. Best friend to Jill. Always the friend, loved by all who knew him. Your spark will always be in our hearts. Mi casa su casa. Requiescat in pace. Semper fidelis. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Av at 81 St., Wednesday and Thursday 2-5 and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 10am St. Joseph Church, 404 E. 87 St. Interment Immaculate Conception, Montclair, N.J.
