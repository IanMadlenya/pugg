
        RENNICK-Mary Ann. On June 27, peacefully at St. Vincent's Hospital. Survived and missed by niece Cecile Parker, niece Dorothy Buermann, nephew Harrie Richardson, many grand nieces and nephews, and many friends. She was employed from 1979 to 1995 by The New York Times. Special thanks to Jennifer and Sonja. A memorial service will be announced for a later date. Contributions may be made in her memory to the New York Public Library.
