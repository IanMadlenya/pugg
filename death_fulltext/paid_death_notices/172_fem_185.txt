
        SOLNICK-Dorothy. On May 21, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Abraham. Loving aunt to Stanley Guss, Allene Grognet, Joan Friedman and Jess Edwards. She died peacefully in her sleep at 107 years. She will be long remembered by her family, friends and mostly by her former students. Graveside services at 11 AM, Thursday, May 24, Mount Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, New York.
