
        JOHNSTON-J. Buckhout. The Philharmonic Symphony-Society of New York, Inc., deeply mourns the passing of J. Buckhout (''Bucky'') Johnston, a loyal friend of the Orchestra. Mr. Johnston served as a member of the Philharmonic's Board of Directors for many years, and was elected an Honorary Director of the Society in 1995, in recognition of his years of dedicated efforts on the Philharmonic's behalf. His caring and support will long continue to enrich our lives. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Emma, and their family. Kurt Masur, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Deborah Borda, Exec. Dir.
