
        BULGER-Rachel Forsen, of Mamaroneck, NY. Died January 6, 2001 of Cancer. She was 38. She is survived by her husband James C. Bulger, her parents George and Esther Forsen of Potomac, MD and her brother Richard Forsen of Herndon, VA. A memorial service will be held at Saint Elizabeth Church, 917 Montrose Road, Rockville, MD at 7pm Tuesday, January 16. A tree planting in her memory is planned for this Spring at The Rye Arts Center, Rye, NY. Memorial contributions for Rachel may be made to the Center.
