
        LIPSCOMB-Caroline Knowlton. Of Charleston, SC died April 26, 1999 at her residence. Funeral services will be held at 10:00 AM, Friday, April 30th, 1999 in St. Michael's Episcopal Church in Charleston. Burial directed by J. Henry Stuhr, Inc. will be in Elmwood Cemetery, Columbia, SC. She is survived by two daughters; Caroline L. Winn of New York City and Claudia L. Douglass of Galletin, TN.; one son: Charles Thomas Lipscomb III of Dallas, Texas; Five grandchildren; Charles Winn of Charlotte, NC, Cullen Douglass, and Andrew Douglass both of Nashville, TN, Ashley Buczik of Deleware, and William Lipscomb of NYC; one sister: Claudia K. Finlay of Columbia, SC. Memorials may be made to St. Michael's Episcopal Church, 14 St. Michael's Alley, Charleston, S.C. 29401.
