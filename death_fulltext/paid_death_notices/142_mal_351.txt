
        GREEN-Dr. Arthur H. The staff and trustees of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services mourn the passing of Dr. Arthur H. Green, psychiatrist at JBFCS' Child Development Center. Dr. Green's innovative work with abused children enriched the lives of many and made a lasting contribution to the Center. We extend our deepest sympathy to the Green family. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Seymour R. Askin, Jr., Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive Vice President, JBFCS Alice Kross Frankel, M.D., Director, CDC Robert Abramovitz, M.D., Chief Psychiatrist, JBFCS
        GREEN-Arthur H., M.D. The Board of Governors, members and staff of the East Hampton Tennis Club mourn the passing of our dear friend and longtime member, Dr. Arthur Green. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Carol and their children Susan and Jonathan. Claire L. Odell, President
