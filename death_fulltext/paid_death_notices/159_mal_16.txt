
        DREW-William T. Of Port Washington, L.I., died on February 29, 2000 after a five year residency at Ozanam Hall Nursing Home. His career spanned the Broadway Theater to niteclubs and TV. After retiring as a dancer, he continued as an entertainer and cruise director aboard several of the largest liners. He is survived by two brothers, Russell and George, and two sisters, Kate Kivlen and Mary Bertrand. A Memorial Mass will be held on April 8th at 1PM at Ozanam Hall, Bayside, Queens.
