
        RICHARDS--Benjamin T., Jr., of Darien, CT, on January 16, 2005. Husband of Margaret Olds; father of Elizabeth G. Richards and Cordelia L. Richards Richter; grandfather of Sarah E. and Cordelia L. Francis and Grace E. Richter; brother of Barbara Ann Banks, Elizabeth Egan and David Richards. Born March 20, 1927 in New York City, grew up in Chappaqua, graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy (1944) and Harvard College (1947); Navy veteran. Graduated with honors from Harvard Law School (1953), specializing in antitrust litigation. Joined Dewey Ballantine LLP, in New York (known then as ''Root, Ballantine, Harlan, Bushby & Palmer'' and after 1955, ''Dewey, Ballantine, Bushby, Palmer & Wood''). Served three years as Assistant US Attorney for the Southern District of New York. Joined Exxon (Esso) in 1963, and remained on the legal staff of the Corporation for 29 years, retiring in Dallas as Assistant General Counsel in 1992. At his request, there will be no funeral service. In lieu of flowers, his family would appreciate donations to The American Cancer Society, 372 Danbury Rd, Wilton, CT 06897 or 1-800-227-2345.
