
        TOMPKINS-Leslie Jay II. At Toronto, on Friday, May 22, 1998, at the age of 50. Grandson of the late Professor and Mrs. Leslie Jay Tompkins of New York, son of the late James B. Tompkins, and brother of the late James, Jr. Survived by his mother Patricia Tompkins, sister Teresa Tompkins, and aunt Hallie Tompkins Thomas, all of Toronto. For information contact the Humphrey Funeral Home-A.W. Miles Chapel, Toronto (800-616-3311).
