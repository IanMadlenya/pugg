
        GLICK--Howard Samuel. On September 28, 2003, age 75, of Boca Raton, FL., formerly of Norwich, CT. Beloved husband of Helen. Devoted father of Michael (Sharon) and Andrea (Seth) and the late Karol. Loving Papa of Alex and Kayla Glick and Sophia and Evan Lieberman. Cherished brother of the late Barbara Yarin (Murray). Burial will follow at New Mount Carmel Cemetery, Queens, N.Y. Memorial contributions may be made to the Karol S. Glick Fund, Beth Jacob Synagogue, Norwich, CT.
