
        WEISS--Jacob. Of New York City. Born January 1, 1917, died September 18, 2003. Beloved husband of Gertrude for 58 years. Caring father of Diane and Mark. Grandfather to Simone, uncle to Dee and Donald, and brother to the late Evelyn and Max. He will be remembered fondly by those who were customers of Clark Stores in Jackson Heights for 70 years. Missed terribly by those who had the pleasure of knowing him and most especially by his family who loved him. Services Sunday at 1 PM, Schwartz Brothers, Forest Hills, NY.
