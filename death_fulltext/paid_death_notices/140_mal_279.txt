
        GAFFNEY-Richard W. Of Brooklyn, New York, and Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. Died suddenly at home Sept. 22, 1998. An artist, educator & sportsman, he was the husband of Ann Walker Gaffney, the father of Walker Gaffney of St. Louis, and Elizabeth Gaffney of Brooklyn, & grandfather of Rynier Forrester Gaffney. A memorial service will be at 11 AM on Friday, September 25 at Grace Church Brooklyn Heights, 254 Hicks Street, Brooklyn, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Richard Gaffney Memorial Fund at Wagner College, One Campus Road, Staten Island, New York 10301.
