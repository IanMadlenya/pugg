
        PHILLIPS--Martha Jane Potter (''Marty'') of Delray Beach, FL. Marty died peacefully in the arms of her family at ''Pine Ledge'' on July 16th, 2004. She is survived by her daughter, Jane Rust Munson Harrison, her son-in-law, J.B. Harrison, and her granddaughters, Timothy Laurel Harrison and Chauncey Rachael Sherwood Harrison. A graduate of Sarah Lawrence College, Marty served our country in the Women's Airforce Service Pilots (''WASP'') in WWII, ran the Manhattan office of Steuben Glass, and chaired the Community Service Society. A memorial service will be held at 2 PM, Saturday, July 24th, at ''Pine Ledge,'' 37 Herrick Rd., S.W. Harbor, ME. An additional service will be held in Delray Beach at a later date. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the charity of your choice in Marty's name.
