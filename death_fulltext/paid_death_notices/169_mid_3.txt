
        BLATE-Howard E., 83, of Richmond, MA and Great Neck, NY. On February 8, 2001 in Sarasota, FL. Graduated Wharton School. President Brownlee Insurance Agency. Survived by his loving wife Mildred, daughter Barbara Levy and her husband Edward of Scottsdale, AZ; son Mark Blate and his wife Sharon of Sarasota, FL; a granddaughter Dara (Levy) Coope and her husband Robert of New Hyde Park, NY; two grandsons, Andrew and Micah Blate of Sarasota, FL.
