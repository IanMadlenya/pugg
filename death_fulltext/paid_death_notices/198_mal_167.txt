
        WEISBART--Wilbur, on July 17, 2003. A veteran of World War II. Loving husband of Mildred (nee Schiff). He is also survived by his beloved brother Harmon Weisbart and his wife Ina. Loved and respected uncle of Janet Weisbart, Steven Weisbart, Joanne Wohlfeld, Danny Wohlfeld and Jay Wohlfeld. Services Monday, July 21st 11AM, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street. Burial to follow at King Solomon Memorial Park in Clifton, NJ. In lieu of flowers, kindly make donations in his name to The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale, Baptist Division, 3260 Henry Hudson Parkway, Bronx, New York 10463.
        WEISBART--Wilbur. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Wilbur Weisbart our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C.
