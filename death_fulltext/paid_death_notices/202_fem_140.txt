
        deGRANDPRE--Jeanne. Baroness von Griescheim. Born June 25, 1903, in Linz, Austria. Died November 13, 2003, in New York City. She was married to Baron von Griescheim, a German diplomat who was part of the conspiracy to assassinate Hitler. Educated in Paris, France, by the Madams of the Sacred Heart, she was a linguist, speaking several languages fluently. In her youth, she was an avid equestrian, a fabulous dancer and singer. She loved the opera and classical music. An active member of the Manhattan East Chapter of the Visiting Nurse Services. Gigi also participated as a member of several clubs including but not limited to, the Circumnavigators Club, Metropolitan Club, Doubles, Dinner Dances, English Speaking Union, US Navy League and the National Women's Republican Club. Gigi loved life and people of all generations. She was a frequent world traveler and lived in Vienna, Berlin, Lima and Santiago, Chile. A love of New York made this city her home.
