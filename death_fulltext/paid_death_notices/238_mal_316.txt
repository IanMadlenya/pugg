
        HARWOOD--Daniel J. of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY, on November 22nd. Survived by his beloved wife Bernice, loving daughters Rene Gordon (Larry), Felice Spodick, and grandchildren Danna (Eric), Marc, and Chad. He was an Army Major during WWII, President of Leathercrafters Supply Company, and former President of Temple Israel, Lawrence, NY. He was a gentle and wise man beloved by all who knew him.
