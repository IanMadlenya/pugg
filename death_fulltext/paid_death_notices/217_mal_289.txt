
        MILIANS--Max Phillip. The Yeshiva University/Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary family is saddened by the passing of a cherished friend of our institution, where he and his beloved wife, Ruth, established a scholarship fund and provided generous support. Heartfelt condolences are extended to Ruth, and to his siblings, Shep Milians, Ida Goldstein, and Muriel Siegel. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Julius Berman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees
