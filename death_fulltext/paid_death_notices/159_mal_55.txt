
        DOOB - Leonard W. Sterling Professor Emeritus of the Department of Psychology at Yale University died peacefully at the age of 91 on March 30, 2000. He joined the Yale faculty in 1934 after receiving his Ph.D from Harvard. He was at Yale thereafter, except during World War II when he was with the U.S. office of War information. He was most active shortly before his death with his most recent book (Pursuing Perfection: People, Groups and Society) having been published in 1999. He was predeceased by his wife, Eveline Bates Doob, and leaves his sons, Christopher, Anthony, and Nicholas, and grandchildren, Gabriella, Joshua and Crockett. An informal gathering of family and friends will be held at the Hawley Lincoln Memorial, 493 Whitney Avenue, New Haven, Sat, April 8, at 2 PM.
