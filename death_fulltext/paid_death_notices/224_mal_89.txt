
        MILLONES--John P. Died September 2, 2005 in Riverdale, NY after a long illness. Beloved husband of Leslie. Admired teacher and mentor at Columbia Grammar & Preparatory School, where he taught Spanish and Latin and kept people laughing for 39 years. In addition to his wife, he is survived by his brother, Peter, his sisters-in-law, Deirdre and Edith, his brother-in-law, Bob Roth, and six nieces and nephews. For those who remember, one last time, ''El senor Millones es el hombre mas justo del mundo.''
