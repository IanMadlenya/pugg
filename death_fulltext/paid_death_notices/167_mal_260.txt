
        UCHITEL-David, 86, of Accord, N.Y., originally a Brooklyn native, passed away on December 19th. As a professional violist, his career was both illustrious and varied. He performed with many of the top big bands, as well as performing with the New York Metropolitan Opera for 37 years. He is survived by his wife Ernestine, son Bruce, daughter Michele, a sister Ruth Shenkman, and 4 grandchildren. He will be missed greatly by all who knew him.
