
        AWN-Elias J. Age 88, on January 5, 2001. Of Florham Park, NJ, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Beloved husband of Isabel Awn (nee Owen). Loving father of Claudette A. Maraziti of Chatham, NJ, Dr. Peter J. Awn of Manhattan, Louise E. Crowley of New Milford, CT, Richard P. Awn of Larchmont, NY. Devoted grandfather of nine grandchildren in whom his legacy lives on: Jacqueline Maraziti, Michele Maraziti, Jeremiah Crowley, Jennifer Crowley, Sarah Crowley, John Crowley, Richard Awn Jr., Susan Awn and Peter Awn. Elias (Louie) Awn and his brothers were proprietors of Awn's Specialty Shop, a fixture in the Wall Street area for over 50 years.
