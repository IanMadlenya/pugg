
        POONS--Emanuel. Born in Amsterdam, March 23, 1911. It is with profound sorrow and a deep sense of loss that we announce the death of Emanuel Poons in NYC on July 26, 2004. He is survived by his children Jacob and Henrietta Poons, and his grandsons Morgan and Mark Poons. The family wishes to express deep appreciation to Carmelita Gasmen and Elena Vi.
