
        WASHER-Helyne. The Jewish Center of Teaneck, and its constituent organizations, offer heartfelt condolences to our esteemed Rabbi Emeritus, Dr. Judah Washer, on the passing of his devoted wife, Helyne. She was Rebbitzen and leader for many decades, beloved, admired, highly respected by all. Dr. David M. Feldman, Rabbi David Waldman, President
        WASHER-Helyne. With deep sorrow, we record the passing of Helyne Washer, wife of our Rabbi Emeritus, Dr. Judah Washer, and our beloved Rebbitzen for more than 40 years. She was a constant source of inspiration, spiritual guidance, concern for others and role model to all who knew her. Sisterhood Jewish Center of Teaneck
