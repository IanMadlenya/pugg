
        DONNER-Julius. Of Boca Raton, Florida. Passed away November 19, 2001. After 40 years in the life insurance and pension business, Mr. Donner retired to Boca Raton, where he developed a passion for wildlife photography. His photography is on permanent display at Florida Atlantic University and Boca Raton Community Hospital. Mr. Donner is survived by his loving wife of 45 years, Nancy, children Lisa & Richard Goldman of Coral Springs, Florida, Eric and Robin Donner of Warren, New Jersey, Stacey & Mark Hallerman of Port Washington, New York, and six grandchildren.
