
        ATTARDI-Genevieve. Known to her many friends and family as Gina or Jean, she died on Good Friday, March 29, 2002, after a brave, intense battle with pancreatic cancer. She was the loving mother and dearest friend to her son, Charles, and also leaves behind her brother, Albert, as well as two dozen nephews and nieces. A memorial service will be held May 2, 2002, 9:45AM in Saint Paul's Chapel at Columbia University. Donations in her memory may be sent to the Visiting Nurse Service of New York-Hospice Care, 1250 Broadway, N.Y., N.Y. 10001, Cancer Care Inc., 275 7th Ave, N.Y., N.Y. 10001 and The Hirshberg Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research, 375 Homewood Road, Los Angeles, CA. 90049.
