
        SPANIERMAN-Jonathan, age 34, died on August 1, 1997 of AIDS at his home in East Hampton with his beloved wife Cheryl and his adoring mother and father Helen and Ira after a long and valiant battle. He was a musician and a composer of beautiful music that will live in our hearts forever. He is survived by his brothers David and Gavin, and his aunt Dorothy Lautman. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to the East End AIDS Wellness Project in Sag Harbor, New York 11963.
