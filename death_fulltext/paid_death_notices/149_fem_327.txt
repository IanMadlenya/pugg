
        O'BRIEN-Sister Cecelia. On June 8, 1999. She was a member of the Sisters of the Good Shepherd for over 55 years and will be greatly missed by all. She is also survived by her brother Thomas, her sister Rita, and many loving nieces and nephews. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue (at 20th St) NYC, Thursday and Friday 2-5 and 79PM. Mass of Christian Burial, 9:45 AM Saturday at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment Monday at Mt. St. Benedict's Cemetery, West Roxbury, MA. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Sisters of the Good Shepherd Ministry Fund, 8231 Don Caster Place, Jamaica, N.Y. 11432 will be appreciated.
