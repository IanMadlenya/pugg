
        ELSON-Charles. Husband of the late Diana, father of Alexandra and brother of surviving siblings Alex Elson and Elizabeth Cohen, died in Armonk on March 30th at the age of 90. A distinguished international stage and lighting designer, educator and civic leader, he will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Memorial service on Monday, April 3rd, at 11AM at the North Castle Library Theater. Memorial remembrances may be sent to the Charles Elson Fund at the Library, 19 Whippoorwill Road East, Armonk, NY 10504.
