
        HOUSE-Monty. Executive producer of entertainment and corporate events, died March 11 in Los Angeles, CA. He was 48. He is survived by wife Nancy Linari; daughter Emilia, 6; son Michael, 3; parents Carl and Lorraine House and sister Sandy David. Services are 10AM Monday, March 18 at St. Francis de Sales Church, 13360 Valley Heart Drive, Sherman Oaks, CA. Mr. House's former employer has established a trust for his children. Contributions may be made to The Emilia and Michael House Education Fund, C/O Gaytan, Kallman & Company, 125 S. Barrington Place, Los Angeles, CA 90049, attn: Joel Baumblatt.
