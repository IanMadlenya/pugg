
        ROWAN--David. A founder of the National Ski Areas Association, member US National Ski Hall of Fame, recipient of the International Skiing History Association's Lifetime Achievement Award, and onetime Aide-de-Camp to General ''Chesty'' Puller in the Korean War: died in Roxbury, CT, on June 8. He was 78. The cause of death was mesothelioma, a form of cancer. He is survived by his three children Jennifer, Olivia and Andrew, and wife Ann of 40 years and two brothers, Hamilton and Stephen Rowan. A memorial service will take place in the coming weeks in Roxbury, CT.
