
        SOSNIAK--Lauren A. Of Redwood City, CA, died January 3, 2006, after a brave fight with cancer. Lauren was a well-published and much loved Professor of Education at San Jose State University in California. She earned her Ph.D in Education at the University of Chicago. At the time of her death, Lauren was writing her latest book on Talent Development. Born in Brooklyn, Lauren was the daughter of the late Phyllis Sosniak, and the late William L. Sosniak, who lived for many years in Baldwin, NY and then Roslyn Heights, NY. She is survived by her sister, Randy, friends Roy Meyer, Pamela Wilsey, and Marilyn Chambliss, and colleagues and friends around the country. She will be dearly missed.
