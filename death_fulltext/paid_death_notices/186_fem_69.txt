
        WOLF-Margareta (Greta) nee Langer. Widow of and devoted assistant to the late Dr. Max Wolf, the well known dermatologist, died peacefully at her home on July 11, 2002. A resident of New York City since 1941, Greta was born in Vienna in 1902. Services are private. Her closest survivors, sister-in-law Mari Langer and nephew Charles Langer, will announce the date of a memorial gathering to be held in September. For further information call Frank E. Campbell at 212288-3500.
