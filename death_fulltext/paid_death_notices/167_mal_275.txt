
        COSTELLO-Edward P. A resident of Easthampton, NY died on December 20, 2000. He was 53. Mr. Costello was born on March 20, 1947 in the Bronx to Myrtle (Nelson) and the late Fabian Costello. He served in the US Armed Forces in Viet Nam. He was Senior Vice President at Barney Skanska Construction Company where he worked for the last 12 years. Mr. Costello is survived by his mother Myrtle Costello Hartmayer of the Bronx. He is also survived by a sister Cecilia Orlando and her husband Joseph of New Rochelle, and two brothers Bernard and Lawrence. Also surviving is a newphew Patrick Orlando and a niece Carol Ann Mills. Other survivors include 7 nieces and nephews. Visiting will be Friday, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Lloyd Maxcy & Sons, Beauchamp Chapel, 16 Shea Pl, New Rochelle. A funeral mass is scheduled for Saturday at 10:00 AM at Holy Family Church. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Edward Costello Scholarship Fund, Ross School, 18 Goodfriend Drive, Easthampton, NY 11937.
