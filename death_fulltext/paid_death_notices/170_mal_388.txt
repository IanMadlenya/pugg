
        WALDMAN-Edward. Real Estate Developer and Philanthropist of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of West Hartford, CT, died Friday, March 30, 2001, at the age of 76 at his home after a valiant battle with cancer. He led a full and vital life. Edward Waldman is survived by his wife of 48 years, the former Ethel Haiblum, his four children, Maryanne Waldman and her husband Tad Connelly, Andrew Waldman and his wife Ana, James Waldman, Risa Rubloff and her husband Jay and 12 grandchildren. He is also survived by his brother Abe and his sisters Irene Cooper and Frances Schulberg. The funeral and interment will be held at The Star of David Chapel and Cemetery in North Lauderdale, FL, on Sunday, April 1 at 2pm.
