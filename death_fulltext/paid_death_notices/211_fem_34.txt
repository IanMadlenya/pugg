
        FEDER--Geraldine, passed away at age 97 at her home in New York on July 19, 2004. A violin student of Leopold Auer, she concertized under the name ''Gere Cotna'' and made solo appearances with Eugene Ormandy and Ernest Ansermet. Mrs. Feder was the widow of Edgard Feder, who predeceased her in 1991. She is survived by her son, Yves Feder and her daughter-in-law, Linda Skernick, her granddaughter, Sarah Weaver and husband Michael Weaver, Connecticut residents, and by her nieces, Janine Gordon of New York City, and Judith Molle, Geri and Jeanne Steinberg of Los Angeles. Funeral arrangements are private. Donations may be made in her name to the Jacob Perlow Hospice, 1775 Broadway (Suite 300), New York, NY 10019.
