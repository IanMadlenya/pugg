
        CORWIN--June Helen, Ph.D. Born June 3, 1949. Died May 4, 2006. Psychopharmacologist. Beloved daughter of Sylvia and Leonard, adored sister of Lee, was the light of the lives of her family, friends and all who knew her. Funeral Friday, May 5, 12 noon, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 91st St.
