
        SHAW-Dr. Herbert, 76, of New York City. Died in Chapel Hill, N.C. Sunday evening. Dr. Shaw was a Dentist in New York City serving the public for 45 years. He served in the army from 1944 until 1946. Dr. Shaw is survived by his wife Eleanore of New York City, his son Gary and wife Kate of Chapel Hill, a daughter Mrs. Robin Clarke and her husband Arthur of Enfield, Conn. and four grandchildren, Thomas and William Clarke of Enfield, Conn., and Nikola and Emma Shaw of Chapel Hill. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Family requests that in lieu of flowers memorials be made to the New York Public Library.
