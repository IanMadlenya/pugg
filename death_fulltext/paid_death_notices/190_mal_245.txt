
        SPANGLER-Lee K. Age 60. Of Manhattan, on November 16th, 2002, after an extended illness. Beloved husband of Jill and devoted father of Glen, Ian and Noel. Art Director for Newsweek magazine for eighteen years. A gifted artist and painter, Lee will be remembered most of all for his kindness, his humility and his quiet strength. His warm and gentle presence will be deeply missed by all those who came to know him. A memorial service will be held at Marble Collegiate Church, 5th Ave at 29 St, Saturday, December 21st, at 2 P.M.
