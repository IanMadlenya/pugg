
        ELDER-Robert J. Passed away peacefully on October 29, 2000 at the Long Island State Veterans Home in Stony Brook, N.Y. Husband of the late Billee D. Elder, father of Mark J. Elder, father-inlaw of Stephanie C. Brand, brother of Elizabeth Nickell of Muskegon, Mich and the late Irvin Elder of Wauwetosa, Wi. Also survived by many nephews, nieces and their progeny. Born in Cement City, Mich on April 27, 1906, he received a B.S. from the University of Chicago. He moved to New York in the 1930's and served honorably in World War II in the Normandy Campaign. He was married in 1940 and widowed in 1966. All his life he worked in the financial and insurance industries. At his request, there were no services. The family will receive visitors at their home at 521 Long Beach Road, Nissequogue, N.Y. on Thursday November 2 and Friday, November 3. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to Victims Information Bureau of Suffolk.
