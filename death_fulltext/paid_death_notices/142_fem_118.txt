
        HECHT-Beatrice. Wife of recently deceased James, died Wednesday afternoon while residing at her son's home in Greenwich, Ct. Born, raised, and until recently, living on the Lower East Side of Manhattan. She is survived by her 2 sons, David Hecht and his wife Marie of Greenwich, Ct, Arnold Hecht and his wife Neva of Virginia Beach, Va. She will be sorely missed by her 7 grandchildren and 1 great grandchild. Graveside services will be Sunday, November 15, 1998 at 12:30PM at the Mt Hebron Cemetery in Flushing, NY.
