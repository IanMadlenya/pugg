
        TAHL-Marilyn Hamburger. Loving wife of Elliot, devoted mother of Nina Warfield and Joseph Tahl, wonderful daughter of Hilda and Myer Hamburger, nurturing and adoring grandmother of Erica and Jamie Warfield. She treated all people with kindness, compassion, goodness, dignity and respect. Marilyn's untimely death followed a thirteen year battle against cancer during which she showed breathtaking courage, dignity and spirit. Her family and all people whose lives she touched mourn her passing and thank God for the time we had with her. Elliot Tahl Vicki and Joseph Tahl Nina, Jack, Erica and Jamie Warfield
