
        THOMSON-Lucien, age eighty-six of New York City, died Tuesday, January 11, 2000. Survived by his first cousins Isabel D. Thomson of Atlanta, GA and Albert D. Thomson of Cotton, GA. Harpist, composer, author and teacher of the harp in New York City since 1946. Long-time member and past President of The American Harp Society, Inc. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to American Harp Society Foundation-Marcel Grandjany fund, c/o Jan Bishop, 624 Crystal Ave, Findlay, OH 45840.
