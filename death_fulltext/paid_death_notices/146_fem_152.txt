
        GELLER-Malcia. Died on March 1st, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Israel Geller, cherished mother of Martin J. Geller and Ellen H. Kamaras, loving grandmother of Joshua, Jackie, Sarah, Harrison and Logan. A true eshet chayel, a woman of valor, far beyond pearls was her value. She devoted her whole life to family and friends. We will miss her love and kindness everyday. She touched us all and her light brightened our world. Contributions may be made to the Abraham Joshua Heschel School, 314 West 91 Street, New York, NY 10024, Att: Ms. Tracy Port.
