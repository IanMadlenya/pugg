
        MALLET--Madame Andre. Born Mary de Luze, daughter of Leonie Haviland and Henri de Luze, granddaughter of Theodore Haviland and Curator and Director of Le Dois de Moutiers for 50 years, died in Le Bois des Moutiers at Varengeville - sur - Mer, France in her 99th year on March 30, 2004. A memorial service will be held at 3 PM. on Wednesday, April 14th in the American Cathedral, 23 Av George V, Paris, France.
