
        HEFFERNAN-Miriam Margaret, Ph.D.. Age 89, of Manhattan, on August 23, 2001 after a long illness. Dear daughter of the late John J. & the late Mary (nee Daly), loving sister of Dorothy (Heffernan) McVeigh of Valatie, New York (formerly of Brooklyn), the late John J. Heffernan, Jr., the late William Heffernan and the late Anne Heffernan (Sr. Mary Jean, O.P.). Devoted aunt of Andrew P. McVeigh of Barrington, IL and John J. McVeigh of Columbia, MD. Grand-aunt of Sean P. McVeigh of Columbia, MD & Doreen, Caitlin and Drew McVeigh of Barrington, IL. Caring niece of the late Sr. Miriam Margaret S.C. and the late Anna McCrae. Dr. Heffernan was retired after many years of distinguished scholarship as a Professor of English Literature at Brooklyn College of the City University of New York. Dr. Heffernan received her undergraduate training at Brooklyn College and completed graduate study at Columbia University and New York University. She returned to Brooklyn College as an instructor and spent her career in service to the institution and its undergraduate and graduate students, receiving numerous awards for teaching & research in the field of American Studies. Born and raised in the Crown Heights neighborhood of Brooklyn, Dr. Heffernan was a longtime resident of Stuyvesant Town in Manhattan. Visitation will be held at Gannon Funeral Home, 152 E. 28th St., NY, NY (212532-6579) from 2-5PM and 7-9PM on Saturday & Sunday, Aug. 25th & 26th. A Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated at Immaculate Conception R.C. Church, 414 E. 14th St., Monday, Aug. 27th at 10AM. Mass will be followed by interment services at St. John's Cemetery in Middle Village, Queens, NY. Dr. Heffernan was a loving family member, a devoted friend, and a kind and caring teacher. Her loss will be deeply felt by her family and all others who knew her.
