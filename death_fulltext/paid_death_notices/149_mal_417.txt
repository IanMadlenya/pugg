
        PROL-Julio. Foremost teacher of the Spanish classical guitar in New York, died at Lenox Hill Hosptal of a stroke on June 4. Professor Prol was born in La Coruna, Spain. He studied with Andres Segovia and many accomplished musicians of his time. He served in the U.S. Army in World War II. Because of an injury, he abandoned a concert career to teach and was beloved by his many students and proteges. He was a Fellow of the Society of the Classical Guitar since 1956. He is survived by his daughters Tani, Candela, Gizelle and his sons Antonio, Sherwell, the painter Rick Prol, and two grandchildren.
