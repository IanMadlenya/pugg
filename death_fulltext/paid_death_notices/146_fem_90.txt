
        HAMBURGER-Gisela. Age 99. ''Omi.'' Beloved mother of Ruth and son-in-law David. Grandmother of Karen, Dave, Sharyl, Gregg and Laurie, and great-grandmother of Kurt, Ben and Amanda. She escaped from Nazi Germany in 1939 to Haiti and settled in Vineland, NJ, where she guided survivors in seeking restitution. She passed away March 4, at her daughter's home. She will be missed.
