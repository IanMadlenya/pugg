
        FRANK--Rose of New York City on May 23, 2004. Beloved mother of Hettie, son-in-law Frederick Kahn, grandmother of Nicholas Kahn and beloved wife of 53 years of the late Andre. Born in Lodz, Poland, survived the Holocaust and emigrated to Israel, then to Montreal and finally to the United States in 1955. We will miss her. The Kahn-Frank Family
