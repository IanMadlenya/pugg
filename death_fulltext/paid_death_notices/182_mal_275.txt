
        SPILKE-David Herschel. Passed away peacefully in his sleep. Deeply mourned by his wife Francine, daughter Pia and son-in-law Michael Shlomo, sons Steven and Gary, and daughter-in-law Leslie, and granddaughter Holley, sister Jean Paget. Services Monday, March 18, Sinai Chapels, 16205 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, at 1 PM. Shiva will be observed at the Spilke home in Kings Point.
        SPILKE-David. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Rabbi Mordecai Waxman Steven Markowitz, President
