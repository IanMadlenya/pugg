
        SILLS-Polly Strong. At 79 on March 2, 1998. Beloved wife of Morton, mother of Rockwell and Charles Tenney. Daughter of the Society of Mayflower Decendents, Patron of the Brecht Forum, she will be in our hearts forever. Memorial service in Cold Spring Harbor, 72 Shore Rd., April 19 at noon. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Alzheimer's Association, 420 Lexington Avenue.
