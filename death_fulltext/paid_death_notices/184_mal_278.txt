
        DEUTCH-Barnet M. On May 21st, 2002. The Board of Trustees and the staff of the American Red Cross in Greater New York mourns the passing of Barnet Deutch, distinguished colleague, volunteer and friend. Barney's 59 year career with the American Red Cross included international positions in China, Burma and India and as a fund raiser for national headquarters. As Director of Financial Development for ARC/ GNY, Barney established the first planned giving program. He continued to provide invaluable expertise after his retirement. Our deepest condolences to the family. Jonathan O'Herron, Chairman Robert M. Bender, Jr., Chief Executive Officer
