
        TRUMAN--Elinor Griffenhagen, 90, on April 20, 2005, in Sarasota, Florida. Beloved wife of the late David B. Truman; mother of Edwin (Ted) Truman and his wife, Tracy P. Truman; grandmother of David R. Truman and his wife, Amy Suelzer, and of Christine J. Truman and her husband, Ian Woollett; greatgrandmother of Elena Cecilia and David James Truman. She was devoted to her family and the many communities in which she lived her long life. Contributions in lieu of flowers to Smith College, to Plymouth Harbor, Sarasota, FL, or to a charity of your choice.
