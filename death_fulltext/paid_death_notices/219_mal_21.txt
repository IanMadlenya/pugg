
        BROWN--Gordon Eames, 93, formerly of Bronxville. The relatives and friends announce with great sorrow the death of Gordon Eames Brown, husband of Loretta Patt Brown, on March 31 after a prolonged illness at the Osborn Retirement Home, 4101 Theall Road, Rye, NY 10580. He was the father of Gordon Eames Brown, Jr, and grandfather of Travis Brown, a Princeton graduate and former Executive Director of the State Communities Aid Association. He was a brilliant, caring gentleman. A memorial service to share the family's grief will be held in the near future. Donations may be made to the American Red Cross.
