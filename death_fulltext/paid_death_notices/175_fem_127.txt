
        SAKRAIDA-Mary Jo Hughes. Of Pound Ridge, N.Y. Died on August 15, 2001. She was 42 years old. She was born in Buffalo, N.Y. to Robert and Mary Sullivan Hughes. She is survived by her loving husband, Michael. Visiting hours will be Saturday and Sunday 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, N.Y. The funeral Mass will be Monday, 10 AM St. Patrick's RC Church in Bedford New York. Interment Pound Ridge Cemetery, Pound Ridge, N.Y.
