
        LANGENDORFF-Gabriella. Age 81. Peacefully in her home on April 24, 2000. It is with great sadness that we report the passing of Gabriella Langendorff. Beloved sister of Michael and sister-in-law of Gerda. Loving aunt to Karen, Jeffrey and Frank. Caring friend to all who knew her. Our dear ''Lala'' will be missed. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
