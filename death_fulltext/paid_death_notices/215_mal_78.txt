
        BECK--Franklin B. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Franklin B. Beck, a treasured friend whose dedication and generosity has helped renew Jewish life in New York and around the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to Franklin's beloved wife, Shirley; to his children; and to the entire family. Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
