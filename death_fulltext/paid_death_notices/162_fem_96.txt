
        BECKER-Adele Edelman. On July 14, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Milton Edelman and of the late Edward Becker. Cherished mother of Jim and Bob Edelman; dear mother-in-law of Diane and Carol Edelman; adored grandmother of Michael, Sara and Alexandra Edelman. Her dynamic presence and youthful spirit will always be remembered. Service Monday, July 17, 11:45 AM, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, Madison Ave and 81st St. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Manhattan Guild, 214 E. 61st St., New York, NY 10021.
        BECKER-Adele Edelman. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member, Adele Edelman Becker. Adele and her family have been members of Rodeph Sholom for four generations. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her children, James and Diane Edelman and Robert and Carol Edelman, and grandchildren, Michael, Sara and Alexandra. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
        BECKER-Adele Edelman. Manhattan Guild sadly records the passing of our beloved Adele, founder, past President and staunch supporter for over 50 years of The Manhattan Guild Children's Center, Chaim Sheba Medical Center, Israel. Our deepest sympathy to her sons Bob and Jim, and their families.
