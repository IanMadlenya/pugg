
        LAUGA -- Agnes Thenaud, MA, MSS, died May 9th 2007 after a brief illness at the age of 96. A lifelong social worker, she served as Director of Psychiatric Social Work, Albert Einstein College of Medicine 1955-1970, and then served there as Associate Director and Acting Director of Social Work. She held faculty appointments at the University of Cincinnati and Yeshiva University. A memorial gathering will be held from 11:30am-1pm Sun, 20 May, at the Regency Hotel, 540 Park Avenue, NYC. Memorial donations may be made to Fort Washington HSE Inc. 99 Fort Washington Ave. NY, NY 10032 or Smith College School for Social Work, Lilly Hall Northampton, MA 01063
