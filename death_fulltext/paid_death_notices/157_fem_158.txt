
        HAAR-Arline. The Gerrard Berman Day School of North Jersey notes with great sadness the death of Arline Haar. She and her late husband, George, have been and will continue to be an inspiration to all of us in the Jewish community. Our heartfelt sympathy and condplences to her children, grandchildren and entire family. Lawrence Traster, President Gerrard Berman Day School
