
        ENLOE-Jane, 79. Of Kennett Square, PA., died Monday, December 8, 1997 at Chester County Hospital. She was a member of the Religious Society of Friends and a graduate from Wheaton College. She is survived by her husband, Roger; two daughters, Elizabeth and Margaret McIntyre Enloe both of NYC; three brothers, Richard Dent of Schnecksville, PA, John and Walter Dent both of Allentown, PA.; two sisters, Jessie Cook of Arlington, VA., and Mary Crisp of Washington, D.C.; three grandchildren, Andrew, Laura and Sarah all of NYC; a son-in-law, Steven McIntyre of NYC. She was preceded in death by two brothers, Hal and Charles Dent of Allentown, PA. Memorial service will be announced at a later date. Contributions may be sent to American Friends Service Committee, Philadelphia, PA., or to Leonardo DaVinci Horse, Foglesville, PA.
