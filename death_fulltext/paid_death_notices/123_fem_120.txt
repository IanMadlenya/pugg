
        CHAIFETZ-Mollie Miller. Died April 14, 1997 at age 91. A student opera singer in her youth, she taught 6th grade in the New York City Schools for thirty years where she produced many musical shows in her school. She was the loving wife of the late Harry Chaifetz and was pre-deceased by her two sisters Dina Bleich and Sylvia Friedlander. She is survived by her nieces and nephews, grand nieces and grand nephews and a great grand nephew.
