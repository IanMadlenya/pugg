
        SCHLOSS-Jack. On March 3 in his 88th year in Miami, FL. Beloved husband of the late Betty. Adored father of Roslyn DePas and Michael and the late Gilbert. Dear father-in-law of Patti. Cherished grandfather of Davide and Danielle DePas. Devoted brother of Florence Friedman and the late Paul. Former architect, builder, chef and owner of the Pine Grove Hotel, Kerhonkson, N.Y. To many he was a role model and mentor. His impact on our lives will remain with us forever.
