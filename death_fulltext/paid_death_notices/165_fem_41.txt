
        LOW-Evelyn. The Board of Directors of the United Hebrew Geriatric Center mourn the passing of Mrs. Evelyn Low, beloved mother of Barbara and Robert S. Savin and Frieda and Robert Low, and widow of Max M. Low. As the esteemed Chairmen and Past Presidents of our Board, both Robert S. Savin and Max M. Low have been ardent supporters, benefactors and valued friends for many years. Among their many contributions was the endowment of the Low-Savin Residence for Seniors on our campus. Our heartfelt sympathy and prayers are extended to the beareaved family. Donald Duberstein, President Malcolm Lazarus, Chairman Rita C. Mabli, Executive Vice President LOW-Evlynne. In her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late Max. Devoted mother of Robert and Frieda, and Barbara and Robert Savin. Adored grandmother of Debi and Steve Scoggan, Scott Savin, Gary and Nancy Low, Caren and Glen Coben and Warren Low. Cherished great-grandmother of five. Service on Thursday, 11:15AM, Westchester Jewish Center, Palmer and Rockland Avenue, Mamaroneck, NY. Contributions to your favorite charity or the United Hebrew Geriatric Center. 60 Willow Dr., New Rochelle, NY. For information, call Plaza Memorial Chapel, 212-769-4400.
