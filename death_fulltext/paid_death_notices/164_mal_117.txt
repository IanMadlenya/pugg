
        MINIFIE-Jonathan. 50. English teacher at Groton School. Died of a heart attack on August 30. Son of the Rev. Benjamin and Frances Minifie, formerly of Grace Church, NYC. Graduated from Williams College, Oxford University and Harvard University Graduate School. He is survived by his wife Mary Hampson Minifie, his two sons Joseph and Samuel, his mother Frances, and brothers, the Rev. Charles, the Rev. Thomas, Edward and William, and sister Katherine.
