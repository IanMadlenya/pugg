
        JANOFSKY - Rachel. Be loved wife of the late Albert, loving mother of Dr. Elliott and Betty Janofsky and Sally and Michael Hipscher, devoted grandmother of Karen, Jenny, Steven, Joel, and Lorraine, and adored greatgrandmother of Emma Rose and Daniel. A strong and gracious woman, remembered for her great will to survive, her strength of character and spirit, always so loving, with a warm, generous, fiercely beating heart, who showed all how to live and die. Services Monday, October 16, 10 am, at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave, Brooklyn, NY. Interment following at the Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, New York.
