
        ABELES-Claus K. On Jan. 16, 1997. After a long illness at the age of 83. Beloved by his wife Marietta. Adored and cherished by his children, Suzy and Lincoln Boehm and Carol and Farrell Glasser. He will always be remembered as a special Popi to Geoff, Gaby and Greg Boehm, Lauren and Samantha Glasser and his family in Buenos Aires. He was a selfless, witty, wise, gentle and generous man. He will be in our hearts forever. Service Fri., 12:15 PM ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
