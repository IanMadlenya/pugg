
        DANA-Charles A. Jr. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of NYU Medical Center mourn the passing of our dear friend, devoted benefactor and steadfast supporter, Charles A. Dana, Jr. Charles Dana joined the NYU Medical Center Board of Trustees in 1960 and was appointed Life Trustee of Mount Sinai NYU Health in 1991. With a distinguished record of philanthropy both locally and nationally, he has been active on the Boards of the Charles A. Dana Foundation, the United Cerebral Palsy Research and Education Foundation, and the Museum of the City of New York, among others. His efforts have been matched by those of his wife, Norma Dana, who is active as a Board member and volunteer for numerous humanitarian organizations. We extend our deepest sympathy to Mr. Dana's family and loved ones. Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean NYU School of Medicine Theresa A. Bischoff, President NYU Hospitals Center Kenneth G. Langone, Chairman NYU School of Medicine Foundation Board of Trustees
        DANA-Charles A., Jr. The Hazeldon Foundation mourns the passing of Charles A. Dana, Jr., member of The Hazeldon New York Board of Trustees. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and friends.
