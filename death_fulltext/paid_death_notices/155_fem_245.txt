
        GREEN-Martha. Beloved wife of Dr. Marvin Green. Devoted mother of Dr. Geoffrey Green and wife Marcia and Arne Green and wife Fran. Also survived by sisters Florence Atlas and Esther Skolnick. Martha Green was a prominent clinical social worker. She was an authority on adolescents and children. A graduate of the Columbia School of Social Work and Hunter College, she worked for many years as district social worker at the Wantagh School System. She also worked at Queens College, the Brooklyn Bureau of Social Service, and, most recently, in private clinical practice. She was a warm, loving, compassionate, generous, and unique human being who will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved her. Services today at 1:30PM at Mount Lebanon Cemetery.
