
        CHARMATZ--William A. on September 4, 2005 died peacefully at home on West 68th St. in Manhattan. Born November 15, 1925, Bill was a commercial artist/painter/illustrator for The N.Y. Times, Sports Illustrated, Time, Inc. & Esquire. He also authored & illustrated several children's books. He is survived by his daughter Katrina Bettina Charmatz. Donations may be made to: Animal Medical Center development office, 510 E. 62nd St., NY, NY 10021. Memorial services will be held at: Riverside Memorial Chapel 76th St. & Amsterdam Ave., Thursday, September 8th @ 2 PM.
