
        AMKRAUT--Harriet (nee Potoker) of Delray Beach, FL and Bethpage, NY. Born September 11, 1916. Died of natural causes on October 24. Survived by her daughter Alynne Amkraut Krull and son-in-law Albert of Chappaqua, NY; grandchildren Jeremy and Kathryn, to whom she was devoted. She will be missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral 10:30 AM, Monday, October 27, at Bet Torah Synagogue, Mt. Kisco.
