
        BINGHAM-Mary Dunwody. Died peacefully, December 23, 2000, in her 99th year. Beloved wife of the late Arthur W. Bingham, Jr., loving mother of the late Mary B. Murray and of A. Walker Bingham III, devoted grandmother of Felicia C. Murray, Jonathan E. Murray, Arthur W. Bingham IV, Alexander D. Bingham & Nicole P. Bingham. Service at 11:30 AM, December 29, 2000, at St. James Church, Madison Avenue at 71st St. Burial in Vermont. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Smith College, Northhampton, MA.
