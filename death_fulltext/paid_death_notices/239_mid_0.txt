
        HAROUNIAN--Khorshid. We extend heartfelt condolences to our cherished and devoted friend Jack Harounian on the loss of his beloved mother. May Jack, his brothers and sisters and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem
