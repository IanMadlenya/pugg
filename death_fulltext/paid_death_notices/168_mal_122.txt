
        GLATZER-Mark. On January 8, 2001, at age 92. Beloved husband of Jewel for 60 years. Devoted father of Jeffrey and his wife Marti, Hal and his wife Bonnie, and former daughter-in-law Liz Glatzer. Adoring grandfather of Samantha and, Jessica Glatzer and Matt Murphy, and step grandchildren, Jonathan, Ginny and Liz Freirich. Born in Saarbrucken Germany, forced to emigrate to the U.S. in 1935 where he served in the U.S. Army during World War II. A retired physical therapist from the Neurological Institute of Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. Services today, 9:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in his memory to Hatzolah Volunteer Ambulance Corps.
