
        HICKEY-Lillian F. 93, on July 19, 2002. Born and lived in Brooklyn from 1909 until 1994. She had been employed as a personnel manager for General Accident Co Inc, NYC, from 1932-1974. Beloved aunt of Richard E. Hickey, Marie Breslin and Eileen Mosier, great-aunt of many, and great - great - aunt of two. Graveside service 11 AM, Monday, July 22, 2002, Cypress Hills Cemetery, Brklyn, NY. Visitation Sunday, 1-5 PM, at the O'Brien Funeral Home, Highway 35 at New Bedford Rd, Wall, NJ.
