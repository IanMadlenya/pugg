
        CAMERON--John W., died November 29, 2003, after a long illness. He attended St. John's College in Maryland and graduated from Olivet. He served in the Navy during World War II and is the author of the novel, ''The Astrologer.'' He was Eastern Bureau Chief and writer of true crime stories for Front Page and Inside Detective magazines. He is survived by his loving family: wife, Amy; three children, Margaret, William and Charles; six grandchildren, Jesse, Veronica, Olivia, Colin, Justin and Samantha, and nieces and nephews.
