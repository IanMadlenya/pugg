
        PRICE--Margie. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Margie Price, beloved mother of Steven Price and mother-in-law of Tina, treasured leaders and supporters, along with the rest of the family whose generosity and deep commitment continues to strengthen our global Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Margie's children, her grandchildren, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
