
        GELFAND--M. David. August 12, 1949 to September 25, 2005. We mourn the death of our dear friend David, loving and beloved husband of Mary, father of Katie, son of Ann Malmer. Ashton Phelps Chair and Professor of Law, Tulane University Law School. Brilliant legal scholar, inspiring teacher, defender of civil rights and liberties, passionate sailor, and lover of jazz. Extraordinary friend. We will miss him always. Joel, Michael, Terry, Meri-Jane, Christine, and Lindy
