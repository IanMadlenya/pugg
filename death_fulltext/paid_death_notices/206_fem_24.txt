
        CULLINEN--Sonia. Sonia Robbins Cullinen, sister of legendary choreographer Jerome Robbins died at the Minskoff Theater prior to the opening of ''Fiddler on the Roof.'' She was accompanied by her daughter Cydney Palencia, her son & daughterin-law Robbin & Jan Cullinen. She is also survived by her grandchildren Indre, Michael, Matthew, Marco, Reinaldo and her great grandchildren Nicholas, Lucas and Zachary. She was 91.
