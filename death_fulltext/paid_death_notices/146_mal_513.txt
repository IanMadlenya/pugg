
        McNALLY-James G. Of New Paltz, NY and Red Hook, NY, died on March 21, 1999. He is survived by his father, Edward M. and his mother, Margery of Red Hook, NY, and his brother Edward A. and sister-in-law Glenna of Ridgefield, CT, and his brother Mark of Kingston, NY. James was founder and president of Home James, a painting and contracting firm in New Paltz. We will remember our beloved brother as a multi-talented, humorous man who contributed greatly to the lives of all he touched. He had an independent spirit that inspired his friends and relatives. We will miss him deeply. Edward and Glenna McNally
