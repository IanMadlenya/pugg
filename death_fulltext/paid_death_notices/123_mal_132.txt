
        SLOANE-Howard G. It is with deep regret and a profound sense of loss that the Officers, Board of Trustees, and staff of Old Sturbridge Village in Sturbridge, MA, mark the passing of Howard G. Sloane. As a past-Chairman of our Board, Howard played a crucial and important role for Old Sturbridge Village. Howard will be remembered as an exemplary volunteer leader for his vision, dedication, and philanthropic support. He made a difference in this world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Virginia and the family. Alberta Sebolt George, Pres. Old Sturbridge Village
