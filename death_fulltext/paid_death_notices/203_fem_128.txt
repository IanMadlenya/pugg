
        CHARRY--June Boyer. Died on November 30, her 82nd birthday, in Mount Kisco, NY. As psychologist for the Greenburgh, NY School District from 1960-1980, and in private practice, she helped many children and young adults on the path to mental health. She received her MA in psychology at the University of Chicago under Carl Rodgers, and earned a Ph.D. in clinical psychology from Columbia University. She is deeply mourned by her husband, Paul, and family.
