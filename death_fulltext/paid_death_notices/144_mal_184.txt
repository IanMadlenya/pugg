
        NEURINGER-Irv, 86, of Bethlehem, NH, originally of Brooklyn on December 3, 1998. He will be deeply missed by his beloved daughter Karen, his sons Martin (Maureen) and Allen (Martha), his sister Frances Yarmish and his grandsons Jordan, Jeremy, and Christopher. He was widely known for his successful carpet business. He loved and cared for many animals, he will be remembered by all who knew and cherished him for his generosity, warmth, humor and broad smile. His love and kindness touched many lives.
