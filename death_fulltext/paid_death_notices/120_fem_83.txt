
        MAPEL - Evelyn Raines. Devoted wife of the late William Mapel died peacefully in her home in Bronxville, NY on January 11, 1997 at the age of 91. She is survived by her daughter Lynne Rhinehart of Southern Pines, NC, her son William M.R. Mapel of Darlen, CT, and three grandchildren Daniel, Susan and Stephen Mapel. Services will be private. Should friends wish contributions may be made to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St, Tuckahoe, NY 10707.
