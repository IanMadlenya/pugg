
        McCANN-Theresa Ann. Lifetime New Yorker returned to her creator in her 73rd year on Tuesday Oct. 23, 2001. Beloved daughter of the late Jane and Luke. Loving sister of James, Marilyn and Rev. Luke McCann. Her life was a triumph of loyalty & love. Family will receive friends at the Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave., Scarsdale, NY. today 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass will be held Friday at 10 AM at the Queen of Peace Chapel at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Interment to follow. In lieu of flowers, donations to a charity of your choice.
