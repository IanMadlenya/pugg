
        WIENER-Elsie. Oakland, CA. April 30, at 93. Born in Brooklyn, one year after her family immigrated from Mogilev, Russia, she lived in Brownsville with them behind her parents' candy store. Educated at City College, worked for the NYC Board of Education. Lived in Astoria and then Forest Hills. A member of the Brandeis Club, she and her husband, Philip L. Wiener, moved to CA. to be near their children and grandchildren. In the last years of her life, her wit revealed itself and kept us all in stitches. We are grateful that we had her with us and wish it had been longer. Survived by her sister, Lillian Gordon of Oakland, CA, children, Elizabeth Wiener of Oakland, Deborah Wiener, son-in-law W. Daniel Boone, grandchildren, Sarah and David of Berkeley, CA. Donations may be made in her name to VNA/Hospice Foundation, 1900 Powell St, Suite 300, Emeryville, CA. 94608.
