
        SEIDMAN - Herta L. of Manhattan, on January 3, 1997. Born Romania 1939. President of the Soros Business and Management Foundation, former Assistant Secretary of U.S. Dept. of Commerce, and former Deputy Commissioner of NY State Dept. of Commerce. Partner in life and wife of Samuel N. Seidman. She bravely fought the battle against pancreatic cancer. She had an insatiable zest for life, inspiring all who met her. Her memory can be sustained by contributing to the Pancreatic Cancer Project, c/o Dr. David Kelsen, Memorial Sloan Kettering Hospital, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021.
