
        ROSENZWEIG--Maxwell, of Coconut Creek, Florida, formerly of Bayside, New York, died peacefully on May 30, 2007. Beloved husband of Mae (Blatt) for over 60 years; father of Robin and Alex Schkrutz and Roy Rosenzweig and Deborah Kaplan; grandfather of David and Eric Schkrutz; brother of the late Martin, Gladys, and Sylvia; ''Uncle Max'' to his nieces and nephews. He graduated from City College in 1938, served in the Army Air Corps during World War II, and worked at the New York State Department of Labor for more than 30 years. His family and friends will dearly miss his good humor, wise stories, and warm presence. In lieu of flowers, send donations to the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute.
