
        GREGG-William Kirker. Of Keene Valley, NY (formerly Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ), died November 7th after a long illness. Mr. Gregg was born July 11, 1921, to Juliette (Kirker) and Otis Gregg in Hackensack, NJ. He was a World War II veteran and industrialist, manufacturing railroad cars for The Gregg Company, Brussels, Belgium. He served on the Boards of the Hackensack Hospital, YMCA, Rotary Club. After his move to the Adirondacks, he was President of the Good Samaritan Counseling Center, on the Board of Directors for the Neighborhood House and the Elizabethtown Community Hospital. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Louise (nee Lydecker); daughters, Susan, Juliette, Janet and Dorothy; granddaughters, Stephanie and Jessica DeBiasio; sons-in-law, Richard Latimer and Daniel DeBiasio; and brother, Richard. Donations may be made to the Neighborhood House, Keene Valley, New York 12943.
