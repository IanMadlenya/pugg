
        SACKS-Anne. Died October 9, 2001 at the age of 88. Widow of Louis Sacks. Mother of Henry Sacks, Rebecca Sacks Mansdorf, and Victor Sacks. Grandmother of Lucy and Nick Mansdorf. A singularly energetic, independent and modern woman, she found her calling bestowing hospitality as the proprietor of Sacks Lodge in Saugerties, NY, which she ran with enthusiasm and devotion for over 55 years. She was beautiful and had great wit, intelligence and taste. She also displayed enormous courage and determination in the face of unbearable tragedy. Donations may be made to Alzheimer's research.
