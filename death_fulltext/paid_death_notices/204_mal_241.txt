
        TRENT--Thomas Robert. On January 10, 2004, at age 84, in his Stuart, FL home. Survived by his devoted wife of 33 years, Virginia; his stepchildren, Leslie, George, Terry and Robert Davidson and four grandchildren. He graduated from Stevens Institute of Technology in 1941. In WWII, he served as Lt. Commander in the US Navy Submarine Corps aboard the USS Gudgeon, USS Thresher and USS Diablo. He served as a Trustee of the Flushing Savings Bank for over 30 years.
