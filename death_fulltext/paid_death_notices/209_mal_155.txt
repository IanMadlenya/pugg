
        ABBE--Herbert W. It is with great sadness that we, Elisabeth Abbe, Monika, Louis, Dina and Krystal Russell, Brigitte, Michael and Michelle Vignari, Herb, Sandy, Kelly and Christopher Abbe and Heather and Zachary Davonski announce the passing of Herbert W. Abbe (Sr.). Beloved husband, father, grandfather and great-grandfather. He fought a valiant and courageous battle with cancer until the very end on June 11, 2004. A Memorial Mass will be held at St. Thomas R.C. Church on Staten Island, NY, on June 15, 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, if so inclined, please make a donation to Staten Island University Hospital Hospice or the American Cancer Society. Thank you.
