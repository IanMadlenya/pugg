
        CADWELL--Franchellie Margaret. On May 25, 2003, at her home in New York. One of the first women to own a national advertising agency. Ms. Cadwell was known in the advertising world as ''Frankie''. She was an award-winning copywriter and author who was renowned for changing the way women were depicted in advertising. A memorial service will be announced at a later date by Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel.
