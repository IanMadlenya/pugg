
        MARBER--Patricia, recently of Boca Raton, Florida, died on Thursday, July 28 in Norwalk, Connecticut. Retired Investment Manager and Life Master Bridge Player, she was a tenacious competitor in all aspects of her life. She is survived by her brother William Burke, her three children, Cynthia Nikbin, Philip Marber, Michele Montano and six beautiful grandchildren. Services were held on Friday, July 29 in Redding, Connecticut. Donations in Pat's memory can be made to the Whittingham Cancer Center at Norwalk Hospital Cancer Research Program, 24 Stevens St, Norwalk, Ct. 06851.
