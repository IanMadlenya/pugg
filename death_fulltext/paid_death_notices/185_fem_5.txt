
        FRANK-Doris A. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services notes with sadness the death of Doris Frank, a devoted former trustee. Doris and her husband, Richard, Sr., were both dedicated to bettering the lives of children. Our condolences to her daughter, Linda, and her grandchildren. Jean L. Troubh, President Joseph S. Kaplan Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President and CEO
