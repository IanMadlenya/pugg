
        SNYDER-Joe Stephens. 82. Of Tenafly, N.J. Died Oct. 12, 1998. He is survived by his wife, of 52 years, Evelyn and his three children Judith Hallock, Joe Jr. and George. Four grandchildren and four stepgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 17, at the Presbyterian Church at Tenafly, N.J., at 11A.M. Donations may be made to SEED of Englewood Hospital and/or The Presbyterian Church at Tenafly.
