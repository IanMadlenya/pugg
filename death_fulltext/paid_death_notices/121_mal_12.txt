
        BARON-Benjamin Harry. Born February 16, 1909, (nee Baronofsky). Passed away on January 28, 1997. He resided in Brooklyn, NY most of his life, and West Palm Beach, Fla. He is survived by his beloved children, Ruth Herz and Carl Baron; grandchildren: Nicole, Justin and Julia; sister Mildred Halpert and brother Leon. After graduating CCNY in 1929, Ben became a dedicated foreign language teacher in the NYC High Schools and an active member of The Teachers Union. He was devoted to promoting the survival of Yiddish through his work on behalf of the Kings Highway Jewish School, had a deep love for classical music and tennis. A memorial will be held at a future date.
