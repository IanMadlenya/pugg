
        LoROCCO-Lee. Age 79, of NYC. Died on Thursday, December 2 at Cabrini Nursing Home. A tribute will be offered at 11AM on Sunday, December 12 at Andrett's Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave NYC. Ms. LoRocco owned and operated a hair salon for many years in the Gramercy Park area. Born in Jersey City, NJ, she moved to New York 50 years ago. Survinging are her son Rick and grandchildren, Danielle and Brett.
