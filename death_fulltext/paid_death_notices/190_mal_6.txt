
        RAPHAEL-Arnold M., 82. Of Boynton Beach, FL, and a 75 mile radius of New York City. Died suddenly on October 30, 2002. Loving father of Steven, Nancy and Richard, and father-in-law of Melissa and Steve Friedman and Patricia. Devoted grandfather of Amanda, Emily, Rebecca, Stephanie, David, Carolyn and Daniel. Cherished cousin of Ruth Ann and Bill. Veteran of the US Marine Corps (3rd Division). Former EVP of the Kayser-Roth Corp, President of the Interwoven and Camp Hosiery companies, Board Member of the Menswear Association, the Father's Day Council, City Innovations and a volunteer mentor in the NYC public school system. A man of infinite curiosity, passion and humor. An accomplished businessman, sportsman and sports fan. A proud Marine. A true and generous friend to all who knew him.
