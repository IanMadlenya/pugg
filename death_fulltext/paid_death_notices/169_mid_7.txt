
        TURKEL-Eric Karl, 49. Of Old Bridge, NJ, died February 23, 2001 at home after a brief illness. Musician, author, teacher and audio engineer. Survived by his grieving, much beloved wife Barbara, son Matthew, parents Dr. Herb and Dorothea, sister Judith and her partner Jennifer, father-in-law Abe Abukoff, as well as many friends and devoted students. Memorial services will be held next Saturday, March 3rd at 11AM at Trinity Evangelical Free Church, 390 Teaneck Road, Teaneck, NJ.
