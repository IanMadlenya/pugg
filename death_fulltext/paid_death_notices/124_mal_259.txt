
        DE GRASSE-Charles Ira, 74, died on Wednesday, May 21 at his late Jenkintown, PA residence. Mr. de Grasse is survived by his mother, Mildred Goulding O'Connell (Hyannis, MA) and two sisters, Eleanor de Grasse McCue (Kissimmee, FL) and Irene de Grasse Valenti (Woburn, MA), five nieces and four nephews and many, many friends. A memorial Mass will be said at Immaculate Conception Church, 604 West Avenue, Jenkintown, PA., at 11:00 AM on Saturday, May 31. No flowers, please- Fox Chase Cancer Center, 7701 Burholme Avenue, Philadelphia, PA. 19111 would welcome gifts in his name.
