
        ESKOW-Dennis. Noted expert in technology journalism, passed away at his home on October 29. Eskow began his career as a reporter for the NY Daily News, and later wrote for the Associated Press and served as science editor for Popular Mechanics. He went on to spearhead many technology publications such as InformationWeek, Datamation, and, most recently, Consumer Goods Technology and RIS News. Eskow won numerous journalism awards as Front Page, Neal Award, Computer Press Association, and the Magazine Publishers Award. He is survived by his wife, Patricia, his children, Jessica, Simon, Moriah and Rachel, two grandchildren, Evan and Ian, two brothers, Martin and Steve, and numerous friends and relatives.
