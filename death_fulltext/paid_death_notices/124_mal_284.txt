
        NEUWIRTH-Paul. 76, of Irvington, NY, on May 23, 1997, during a hospital procedure. For many years he lived a full and active life despite serious illnesses. He will be remembered as the beloved husband of Ruth, loving father of Jack and Donald, grandfather of Benjamin, brother of Deborah and Bertie. Memorial contributions may be made to the B.E.N. Scholarship Fund for graduates of Cabot School, Vermont. Date and place of service to be announced.
