
        JAFFE-Fern W. (nee West). In her 76th year. Extraordinary mother of Carl. Special & devoted friend and cousin of many throughout the country. A proud veteran and devoted social worker. She was a giving and devoted person to all, while always remaining true to her principles. While she will be greatly missed, her legacy will live on through the lives she touched. Funeral service Sunday, July 22nd, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be made in Fern's memory to Sloan-Kettering Memorial Hospital, 1275 York Avenue, N.Y., New York 10021.
