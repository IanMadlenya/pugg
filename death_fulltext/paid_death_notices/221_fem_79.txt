
        HOCHBERG--Hon. Audrey. The Fund and Committee for Modern Courts are saddened by the loss of Hon. Audrey Hochberg, who served as Vice Chair of our Board of Directors. In public and private life, Audrey Hochberg was committed to a fair and equal justice system, and was instrumental in helping Modern Court pursue court reform in New York State. Her valuable work and good humor will be missed by us all. Our deepest condolences are extended to Audrey's family and loved ones. Fern Schair, Chair Victor A. Kovner, Executive Committee Chair
