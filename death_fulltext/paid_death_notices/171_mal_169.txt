
        JACOBSON-Eugene. Of Englewood, NJ. Beloved husband of Jean nee Zuckman, devoted father of Ellen Levine and Karen Jacobson. Loving grandfather of Daniel Levine, Peter Levine, Jeffrey Bruno, and Stephen Bruno. Cherished great-grandfather of Lucas Dragon Levine. Services Friday noon, Gutterman-Musicant, 402 Park St, Hackensack, NJ.
        JACOBSON-Eugene. The Board of Trustees of The Englewood Healthcare System mourns with sorrow the loss of our distinguished friend and colleague, Eugene Jacobson. His good deeds in behalf of Englewood Hospital and Medical Center and the greater Englewood community will long be remembered. Our sympathy is extended to his beloved wife, Jean; daughters, Ellen Levine and Karen Jacobson, and their families. Andrew F. Durkin, Chairman Daniel A. Kane, President
        JACOBSON-Eugene. The Board of Governors and membership of Edgewood Country Club record with deep sorrow the death of Mr. Eugene Jacobson, an esteemed member and extend to his beloved wife, Jean and family its deepest sympathy. Arthur I. Frankel, President Brian J. Strum, Secretary
