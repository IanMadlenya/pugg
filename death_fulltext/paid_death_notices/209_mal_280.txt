
        WEST--Philip. Oboist; arranger; Professor Emeritus, Eastman School of Music; and artist/faculty, Aspen Music Festival. Died peacefully June 26th after a long illness. Survived by his beloved wife, violinist Carole Cowan; and, from his marriage to the late mezzo-soprano Jan DeGaetani, by daughter and son-inlaw, Francesca and Nicholas Watson; son, C. Mark DeGaetani; and granddaughter, Danica Watson. Contributions may be made to the Aspen Music Festival, Philip West Scholarship Fund, 2 Music School Rd., Aspen, CO 81611.
