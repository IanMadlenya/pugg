
        MAHEY--Roger M. On January 15, 2005. He was a graduate of the University of Michigan. He retired from Socony Mobil in 1985. He was predeceased by wives Constance Carlsen Mahey in 1949 and Mary Grace Jafetry Mahey in 1992. He is survived by a sister, Barbara Joan (Mahey) Swartz. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date.
