
        PELLET-Rodman. The Birch Wathen Lenox School is deeply saddened by the loss of Rodman Pellet, headmaster of the school between 1966 and 1983. Mr. Pellet's life served as an example of the values we hope to instill in our students. As a school community, we are fortunate to be the beneficiaries of his unwavering commitment to children and their accomplishments. Frank J. Carnabuci Headmaster The Birch Wathen Lenox School New York City
