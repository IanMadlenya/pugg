
        WIENER--Dr. Howard E. Of Sarasota, FL. Born October 30, 1918, formerly of Hewlett Neck NY, died at home on October 23, 2003. Beloved husband of Marilyn, adored father of Leslie Waller, Rick and Mary Wiener, John and Karen Wiener, Patty and Dave Freeman. Cherished grandfather of Jennifer, Sam, Liz, Michael, Audrey and Jake, brother of Arnold and the late Lester. Howard practiced ophthalmology for 45 years and served as Chief of Ophthalmology at St. John's Episcopal Hospital, Far Rockaway, NY. A memorial service will be held Sunday, October 26, 1 PM at Temple Sinai, 1802 Kenilworth, Sarasota, FL. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to Sarasota Memorial Hospital, or Hospice of Southwest Florida, 5955 Rand Blvd. Sarasota, FL 34238 or to a charity of your choice.
