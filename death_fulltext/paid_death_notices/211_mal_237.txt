
        ROBERTS--William ''Jack''. (Retired Naval Architect, Lloyds of London). Of West Islip, LI on August 22, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Winifred. Devoted cousin of Pauline Housden. BoydSpencer Funeral Home, 448 W. Main St., Babylon Village, LI on Friday, 7-9PM. Cremation private. His cremated remains will be cast on the sea.
