
        MOSES--Henry. The partners, associates and staff of Goldberg Weprin & Ustin, LLP express our profound sadness at the passing of our friend Henry Moses. He touched us all with his insightful, analytical mind, his devotion to his family, and his cheesecakes. This successful, gentle and caring man will be missed by all who knew him. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Ann, and his sons Henry Moses Jr., Robert Moses and their families.
