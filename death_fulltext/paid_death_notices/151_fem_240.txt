
        SLUTSKY-Emma Dressler, died at home peacefully on Saturday, August 21, 1999. She was born January 23, 1903. She was a graduate of the Juilliard School and was a member of the faculty of the Adamant Music School in Adamant, VT. where she taught piano for 30 years until her retirement in 1984. She also taught privately at her home in Brooklyn. Emma was the wife of the late Dr. Albert Slutsky. She was also predeased by their two children.
