
        KEND-David J. Of Boca Raton, FL and New York City, died on April 15. Loving husband of Joan; father of Robert and Peter; father-inlaw of Katherine and Diane and grandfather of Sydney, Alyssa and Samuel Kend. He is also survived by his sister Connie and brother-in-law Albert Eisenstat of Atherton, CA and his niece Melissa Blau and nephew Michael Eisenstat. A funeral service will be held at Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel, 7240 N. Federal Hwy, Boca Raton, at 11AM, Friday, April 18. Interment is at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, 130-04 Horace Harding Blvd, NYC at 10:30AM, Sunday, April 20. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Boca Raton Hospital for the David J. Kend Fund.
