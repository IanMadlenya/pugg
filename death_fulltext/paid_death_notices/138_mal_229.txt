
        FRY-Willie, Jr. Died of a heart attack July 10, 1998 in New York City. He was 44 years old. He played for the Pittsburgh Steelers from 1978 to 1980 when the team won back-to-back Super Bowls. Retiring from professional football, Willie worked on Wall Street until his death. Willie is survived by his father Willie Fry, Sr., his sister Linda Hullum, his nephews Albert, KK, and Earl, all from Memphis, & longtime friend Ken Hicks of New York City. A memorial service will be held Saturday, July 25, at the Convent Avenue Baptist Church, 420 West 145th Street, N.Y.C.
