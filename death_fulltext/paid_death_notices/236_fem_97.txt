
        BOEGNER -- Margaret Phipps. Died peacefully on September 16, 2006, at her house in Old Westbury, Long Island. Born in London, 1906, only daughter of John S. and Margarita Grace Phipps. Married J. Gordon Douglas Jr., 1930, and Etienne Boegner, 1950. She is survived by her loving family, her children, Dita Naylor-Leyland and J. Gordon Douglas III; three step-children, three grandchildren, Dita Amory Nickson, Nicholas Naylor-Leyland, and Michael G. Douglas; and one great-grandchild, Serena Nickson. Founder of Wakefield Bookshop. Founder, President, and Chairman of the Old Westbury Gardens. Funeral service Tuesday, September 19, 2006, at 11 am, Church of the Advent, 555 Advent Street, Westbury, NY 11568. The Old Westbury Gardens will host a centennial celebration of her life in midNovember. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Old Westbury Gardens, P.O. Box 430, Old Westbury, NY 11568. Flowers may be sent to the Church of the Advent, 555 Advent Street, Westbury, NY 11590.
