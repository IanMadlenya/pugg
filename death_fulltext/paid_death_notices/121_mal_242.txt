
        LEVINE-Ike. The Partners and staff of Stephen I. Soble and Company are in deep mourning over the loss of our long-time partner and friend, Ike Levine. He was a great leader of our firm and a close personal friend to most of our clientele over the last twenty years. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to his wife Arlene, his children Lori and Glenn, and Mindy and Randy and to his grandchildren Bryan and Michael.
