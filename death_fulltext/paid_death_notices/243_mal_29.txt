
        HEWETT--W. Walter, III, 70, passed away on March 22, 2007 after complications from surgery. A life-long resident of Augusta, GA and Manhattan, he was the son of the late Nell Pope Hewett and William Walter Hewett Sr. A graduate of Georgetown University, Mr. Hewett was cofounder of MELTAWAY, which operates private in-home nutrition and well-being seminars throughout the Southeast. In New York, he worked with Greenleigh Associates and the Brooklyn Academy of Music. He was the author of two cookbooks focusing on weight control and behavior modification. Graveside services will be held on Tuesday, April 10th, 11am at Westover Cemetery in Augusta. Contact Thomas Poteet & Son at 706-364-8484 for details.
