
        FLEISHER-Dr. Harold, 76. Of Poughkeepsie, New York. Died on August 13 of leukemia. A pioneer in computers, he retired from a thirty-seven year career at I.B.M. as an I.B.M. Fellow. Professor Emeritus of Physics, S.U.N.Y. New Paltz, he also taught at Vassar and Marist Colleges, both in Poughkeepsie. Dr. Fleisher earned his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Physics from the University of Rochester, and his Ph.D from Case Western Reserve University. He was the founding President of the Hudson Valley Technology Development Center and a Fellow of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. Active in civic and Jewish affairs, he was a past President of the Jewish Federation of Dutchess County and a Board member of Temple Beth-El in Poughkeepsie. He is survived by his wife of fifty-two years, Terry, his children, Sherry and Jonathan Woocher, Leslie and Robert Schwartz, and David Fleisher and Melissa Krantz, and his grandchildren, Meredith and Peter, Benjamin, Rebecca, Adam, Jenny, and Sara. Services to be held on Sunday, August 16 at 1:00 P.M., at Temple Beth-El, Poughkeepsie. Contributions to the American Cancer Society or the American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science. Shiva will be observed at the family's homes.
