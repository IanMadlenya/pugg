
        TIMMERMAN-Robert E. Died July 29, 1999 at the Greenwich Hospital of Alzheimer's disease. Mr. Timmerman was a long-time resident of Greenwich, Conn., but also maintained homes in Palm Beach, Fla., and Kennebunkport, Me. Mr. Timmerman was the son of the late Hearst Publisher Edward W. Timmerman and Maude Ayer Timmerman of Chicago. He is survived by his wife, Paula Reid Timmerman. Mr. Timmerman attended the Taft School and Brown University. During World War II, he was an officer in the Air Corps. During his lifetime, Mr. Timmerman had an active career in the field of advertising, holding senior positions with a number of agencies in New York City. At the time of his retirement, he was a senior vice president with Needham, Harper, Steers. In addition to his business interests, Mr. Timmerman was active in a number of social clubs including the Kennebunk River Club, Greenwich Country Club, Metropolitan Club and the Everglades Club. A private family service will be held in Rhode Island. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Alzheimer's Foundation would be preferred.
