
        WHITE-David Lee. On April 17, 1999. Of White Plains, New York, formerly of Rye. Beloved husband of Janet (Murch). Devoted father of Kristen M., Lynne J. and the late David L., Jr. Dear brother of Richard B. Also survived by a nephew, Duane S. Biasi, and several nieces. Memorial services will be held at the Rye Presbyterian Church, Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY, Thursday, April 22, 4 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Rye Presbyterian Church or New York United Hospital Medical Center, Portchester, NY. For information, William H. Graham Funeral Home, 914-967-0129.
        WHITE-David L. The Officers, Trustees & Members of American Yacht Club are deeply saddened by the loss of a distinguished leader and our very dear friend, Commodore David L.
