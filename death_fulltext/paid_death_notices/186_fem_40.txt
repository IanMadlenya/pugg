
        HOWELLS-Muriel Gurdon Seabury. On July 1, 2002, at home in Kittery Point, Maine. Beloved wife of 73 years of William White Howells, survived also by a daughter, Gurdon Metz of New York City, a son, William Dean of Washington, DC, four grandchildren, and three greatgrandchildren. Funeral services will be held at St. John's Church in Portsmouth, N.H. on July 9 at 12 noon. Contributions in her memory may be made to Strawberry Banke Museum, Box 300, Portsmouth, N.H. 03802-0300.
