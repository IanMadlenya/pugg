
        DOUBLEDAY-G. Chester. Of Gladwyne, PA, died peacefully, June 21, 2000. His wife, Mary Kelly Doubleday, predeceased him. A former vice president of IngersollRand Co., Mr. Doubleday was born in NYC, November 21, 1904. He is survived by three daughters: Patricia Irons of Vero Beach, FL, Marguerite Buck of Bernardsville, NJ, and Dorothy Massey of Santa Fe, NM, and also by eight grandchildren and twelve great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at 2:00 PM, June 27, 2000 at the Shrine of Sacred Heart, Hot Springs, VA. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Garth Newel Music Center, Box 240, Warm Springs, VA. 24484.
