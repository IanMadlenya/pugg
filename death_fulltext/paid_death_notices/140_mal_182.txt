
        RAFFERTY-Michael. New York Bus Service mourns the passing of its President, Michael Rafferty, a man whose wisdom, humility, patience, compassion and fairness knew no bounds. As a leader, mentor and friend, his loss is heartfelt by all who knew him. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his beloved wife, Phyllis & beloved son, Scott in their time of sorrow. May he rest in peace and enjoy life everlasting. Reposing Tuesday and Wednesday 3-5PM and 7-9 PM at Raymond Funeral Home, 5 East Wall Street, Norwalk, Conn. Funeral mass Thursday 11AM, St. Matthew's Church, Scribner Avenue, Norwalk, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers, the family request contributions be made to St. Jude's Childrens Research Hospital, 332 North Lauderdale Street, Memphis, Tenn. 38105.
