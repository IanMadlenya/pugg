
        BLOUSTEIN--Oscar, 90, of Scarborough, NY, died October 31. He is survived by his devoted wife Jeanne and son Laurence. Mr. Bloustein served for over 50 years as an attorney in public and private practice. He was a dedicated supporter of New York University Law School and the Edward J. Bloustein School of Public Policy and Planning at Rutgers University. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Phelps Hospice in Sleepy Hollow, NY. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, November 14 at Temple Israel of Northern Westchester in Crotonon-Hudson, NY.
