
        KAUFMAN-Dorothy, 86. Longtime resident of Larchmont, N.Y. died at her home on Thursday May 18th. Born and raised in Monessen, PA, she was the youngest of five children of Ida and Abraham Friedland. After her marriage to Jerome Kaufman on November 28, 1935, the couple moved to Washington, D.C. where they resided until 1944 when they moved to New York. The Kaufmans were among the founding families of the Larchmont Temple and she served as President of its Sisterhood. She will be remembered for her quiet strength, warmth, and generosity to both family and friends. She is survived by Jerome, her beloved husband of 64 years, her loving daughter Ronne and granddaughter Lauren of Chappaqua, N.Y. She was predeceased by her children Lynn in 1956 and Richard in 1968. Funeral services will take place Sunday, 12:30pm at Larchmont Temple with interment to follow at Mt. Hope Cemetery in Ardsley, N.Y. Memorial contributions can be made to Lynn & Richard Kaufman Scholarship Fund c/o Larchmont Temple, 75 Larchmont Avenue, Larchmont, N.Y.10538 or Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St., Tuckahoe, New York 10707.
