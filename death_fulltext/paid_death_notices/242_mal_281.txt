
        SCHWARTZ--Philip K, 93, died March 26 in Coconut Creek, FL. Highly respected in the field of advertising law, he was considered one of the leading experts on the Robinson Patman Antitrust Act. A partner for over 50 years in the New York law firm Davis and Gilbert and a graduate of John's Hopkins University and Columbia Law School. Phil was known for his integrity, loyalty, quiet dignity and sense of humor. His greatest love was his family. Predeceased by his beloved wife, Frances, he is survived by his children, Robert, Marilyn, Michael, Richie and Judy, seven grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Memorial services will be held at the Star of David Funeral Home, 7701 Bailey Road, North Ft. Lauderdale, Florida.
