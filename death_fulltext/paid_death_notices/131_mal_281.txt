
        MILLER-R. Mack 59. October 21, 1997. Operatic Tenor, Classical Actor, Producer, Director. We miss his wicked jokes, sweetness, boisterousness, great mind, energy. Survived by sister Sheila Osuch whom he loved and protected in childhood, half-sister Susan Miller, step-sister Judith Ranheim, stepbrother Stephen Miller, step-mother Ruth Miller. Upper West Side 30-year fixture who laughed and drank too hard and carried a Wagner score. Party for him on Sunday, January 25, at 2 P.M., at Dorot, 171 West 85th Street. BYOB, Food or Cash, but come. For further information, call Gene Wisoff 212-362-8550.
