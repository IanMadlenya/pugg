
        REED-Barbara Lowe ''Moppet'', internationally recognized wildlife photographer of Bedford NY, died of breast cancer Tuesday after a valiant 10-year battle. Her legion of friends and her family rejoice in her life and deeply mourn her death. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Barbara Reed Fund/St. Agnes Hospital, St. Agnes Development Dept., 172 South Broadway, White Plains, NY 10605 attn: Nadia Solovyev/2nd Flr. For further information, contact www.Legacy.com
