
        PREISLER--Simon. Died on September 11th, 2005 in Frankfurt, Germany. Born on October 10th, 1925 in Fancsicovo, Czechoslovakia. He was a survivor of Auschwitz who lived his life to the fullest. Beloved husband of Baba, adored father of Lydia Preisler Shorenstein (Douglas) and Renee Preisler Barasch (Richard) and Beloved Brother of Simcha and devoted Opa to his grandchildren, Brandon, Sandra, Danielle, Emily, Benjamin and Natalie. He will be in our hearts forever. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Hurricane Relief Fund at the UJA. Services today, 11:30AM at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel,'' 91st Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        PREISLER--Simon. We are deeply saddened to lose one of our pillars of our family. Your strength, courage, love and wisdom will be missed by all of us. You will forever live in our hearts. Our heartfelt condolences to Baba, Renee and Richard, Lydia and Doug, Emily, Benjamin, Natalie, Brandon, Sandra, and Danielle. With Love, Jane, Lowen, Matthew, Alexandra and Helen.
