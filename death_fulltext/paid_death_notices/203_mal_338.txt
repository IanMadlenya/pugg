
        RUDOLPH--Leonard H. The membership and Board of Governors of the Boca Rio Golf Club, Boca Raton, FL. announce with deep regret and sorrow the passing of our dear friend and esteemed member, Leonard H. Rudolph. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Doris and their family. Melvin S. Taub, President Boca Rio Golf Club, Inc.
