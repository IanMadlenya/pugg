
        DANGLER-Hugh Frost. Retired international marketing director for Readers Digest and a cup winning yachtsman died July 9, 2000. He was 74 and lived in New Suffolk, LI, NY. A lifelong naturalist and stimulating conversationalist, he spent his last two years working on a novel. Born in Lake Forest, IL, he graduated from Hotchkiss and Oberlin. He served in the Navy in World War II and graduated the class of '48 at Yale. He was a beloved friend to his wife Christine, dog Max, a daughter, two sons, six grandchildren and many, many friends. He will be sorely missed. Memorial services July 28 at 2 PM at St. George's Episcopal Church on Rutherford Place between 16th & 17th Sts., NYC. A reception will follow.
