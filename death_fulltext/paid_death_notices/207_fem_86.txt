
        LEWIS--Ann R. February 23, 1911-April 11, 2004, died at home surrounded by loved ones. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Devoted mother of Patricia Braverman, Susan Kominsky and mother-in-law of Norman. Cherished grandmother of Cary, Alice, Julie Reva and Andrew Lewis. Loving great-grandmother of Bryn and Finnley Wren. Dear sister of Murray Kane and the late Sybil Rose. Also survived by her loving niece and nephews. Services on Wednesday 11:30 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
