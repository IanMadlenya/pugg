
        MEISEL--Ruth, 97, of Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of Hollywood, FL and NYC, passed away on December 27, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Irving Meisel, loving mother of Mel Meisel (Carol Etkin), dear sister of Lillian Lerman and Sylvia Goldman, NY, cherished grandmother of Dr. James and Ellen Meisel and Vicki and Ron Hadar, and adored great-grandmother. Services were held in Florida. We will miss her dearly.
