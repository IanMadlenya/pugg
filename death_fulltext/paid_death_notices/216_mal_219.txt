
        SCULLY--Donal. 89, former Executive Director for North America of the Irish Export Board, died January 15, 2005. He is survived by his son Brian and his wife Margaret, his daughter Deirdre and her husband Vijay Pardal and seven grandchildren. He was predeceased by his wife Eileen and his son Paul. Visitation Wednesday, 9:30-10:30 am, at O'Connell Funeral Home in Southampton, NY. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday at 11 am, at Sacred Hearts of Jesus & Mary RC Church in Southampton.
