
        KURTYKA-Margaret (nee Christie). Of Clifton, NJ, on February 26, 1998. Born Paterson, NJ, July 12, 1913. Beloved wife for 62 years of Henry, mother of daughter Ruthanne and son-in-law Harvey Corn and grandmother of Christie, Margaret and Olivia. She was a very special person who was dearly loved and who will be sorely missed.
