
        RAME-Piry. Of Roslyn Heights, NY, died on December 8, 2000. Born in Czechoslovakia 1920. Came to US, Christmas Eve, 1934. An accomplished artist, who will live on in our hearts and in her glorious paintings. Widow of Jack Rame, mother of Rochelle Friedman, Marlyn Dorkin and Elyse Beyer. Beloved grandmother of Jordana, Alissa, Heather and Tamar Friedman; David and Joshua Dorkin; Jared and Amanda Beyer. Service at Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhassett, Sunday, December 10, 2 PM. Shiva Sunday and Monday.
