
        CURTAYNE--Grace S., age 94, of Southbury, died January 8, 2004 at East Hill Woods Health Care Center after a brief illness. She was the widow of E. Vincent Curtayne. Mrs. Curtayne was born October 5, 1909 in Brooklyn, NY, the last of ten children of the late NY Supreme Court Justice Peter P. and Edythe R. Smith. Contributions to Little Sisters of the Poor, 1365 Enfield St., Enfield, CT 06082.
        CURTAYNE -- Grace. The Community Committee of the Brooklyn Museum of Art mourns the loss of our long time devoted member.
