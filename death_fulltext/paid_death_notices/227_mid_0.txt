
        LOEB--Janet. Died on December 1, 2005. Janet was a teacher, publicist and journalist. She had a long and active life. Survivors are her brother Lawrence E. White, his wife Clarice, nephews and nieces and grandnephews. Services will be at Riverside Chapel at 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, on Sunday, December 4, 2005 at 9:45AM. Burial will be at Cedar Park Cemetery, Forest Ave, Paramus, N.J.
