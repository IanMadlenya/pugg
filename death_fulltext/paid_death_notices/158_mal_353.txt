
        SCHEER-Seymour. On March 27, 2000 in California. Beloved husband of the late Hilda S. Devoted father of J. Herbert (Ina) and Janet (Jerry) Elgarten. Cherished grandfather of Ellen (Steve) Lippa, Susan (Jeremy) Trumble, Jeffrey (Abby) and Helene (Shawn) Goldberg, and great grandfather of Robyn and Erin. A career bank executive: Deputy Superintendent of Banks (1954-1963), Executive Vice President and Director of Bank Leumi, NY (1963-1985). A compassionate, dignified, giving man, served Israel bonds, UJA, Jewish Federation, among others, with distinction. Services Thursday, 1:30 PM at ''Schwartz BrosJeffer Memorial Chapels'', 76th Road and Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
        SCHEER-Seymour. The Board of Directors and Staff of Bank Leumi USA mourn the passing of Seymour Scheer, esteemed colleague for over 30 years. His dedication and wisdom will be missed. Our sincere condolences to his family.
