
        RIZZO-Dr. Peter-Cyrus II, ''Pop,'' age 98 of Bronxville on December 3, 2001. Loving husband of the late Rose Dignan Rizzo, proud father of Thomas Dignan Rizzo, M.D. and the late Peter-Cyrus Rizzo III, M.D. Grandfather of Thomas, Jr., Peter, James, Peter-Cyrus IV, Marguerite Buttrick, Andrea Cuvelier, Kathryn, William and Francis. Great-grandfather of 18. An admired orthopedic surgeon, he served on the staffs of both The Hospital for Special Surgery and Lawrence Hospital for over 50 years and inspired both his sons and three grandsons to pursue medical careers. Visitation will be held at The Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 2 to 4 and 7 to 9PM Thursday. Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Friday, December 7 at 10AM, St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Boys and Girls Harbor, 1 East 104th Street, New York, New York 10029 and St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, New York 10708.
