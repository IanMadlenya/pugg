
        RAPHAEL--Alan H. Died peacefully at home on April 18, 2004, at age 87. Dearly beloved companion of Eleanor Hauser. Loving father of John Raphael and Cathy Raphael. Devoted husband of the late Rita S. Raphael. Whether a quip, a pun or a joke, he shared his gift of laughter with family and many friends. Donations to Cornell School of Engineering or Horace Mann will be appreciated.
        RAPHAEL--H. Alan. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Alan H. Raphael. We extend our deepest sympathy to his son, John and to the entire family. James M. Dubin, President Richard J. Bronstein, Secy
