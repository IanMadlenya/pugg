
        MURRAY-Thomas Joseph. Born September 5, 1939. Died on December 20, of cancer. Cherished husband of Ann Bacciocco. Loved by his children Tara Freeman, Jennifer Baker, Thomas Jr., Meaghan Fullerton, Matthew, Kendra and Keith, and his 14 grandchildren. Beloved brother to Angela, Patricia Blessington, and William. Thomas was Managing Director of Real Estate Syndications at GE Capital after retiring form the Chase Manhattan Bank. He was a graduate of Fordham University and Fordham Law. He raised his family in New Rochelle. Family will receive friends at John J. Fox Funeral Home in Larchmont, NY, Sunday and Monday, between 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Sts. John and Paul Church in Larchmont, NY, Tuesday, at 10 AM. Interment following at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the National Autism Society and the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
