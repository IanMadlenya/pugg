
        SHEPS--Cecil G., M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Dr. Cecil G. Sheps, who served with great distinction as the General Director of Beth Israel from 1965 through 1968. Dr. Sheps will forever be remembered for his lifelong commitment to the issues of public health policy. To his family, friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman Boards of Trustees, Continuum and Beth Israel Stanley Brezenoff, President & CEO Continuum Robert G. Newman, M.D., President Emeritus Continuum Thomas Killip, M.D. Interim President & CEO, Beth Israel
