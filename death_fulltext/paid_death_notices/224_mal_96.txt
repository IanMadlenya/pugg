
        WELLS--Stanley, 81, humanitarian, artist, designer, actor, original thinker, entrepreneur, storyteller, entertainer, and general lover of people, passed away on September 8th after a long, courageous 18-year battle with cancer. Stanley was a highly decorated veteran of WWII, having flown over 50 missions. He is survived by his wife Blanche. He was the beloved father of Mitchell, Risa and Amy Wells, beloved grandfather of Jason, Tracey, Michele, Ariana, and Luke. He was also a much loved brother, uncle, cousin, and friend.
