
        ANINAT-Raphael V. June 6, 1997 at the age of 33. He is survived by his mother Carmen Aninat Viveros, his brother Jose Antonio, his grandmother Ema Munoz de Aninat, and his friend Peter, as well as a host of other friends. He was a shining light, a radiant spirit and a joy for all who knew him. We are heartbroken. Services to be held Monday at 11 at St. Vincent's. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to your favorite charity.
