
        DOCTOROW--Eleen. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the passing of its longtime member and extends its sympathy to the family. Paul Warhit, President Evan Goodman, Rabbi
        $(6$)DOCTOROW--Elly, passed away Wednesday June 13, 2007. Born April 20, 1930 the daughter of William and Bernice Miller. Devoted wife of Donald, loving mother of Jeffrey (Tracy), David (Crystal), Jonathan (Lisa) and grandmother of Siena, Sarah and Louis. Also survived by two sisters, Deborah and Esta, and her brother and sister-inlaws, E. L. Doctorow and his wife Helen. A noon service will be held Monday June 18th at Temple Israel, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd., New Rochelle, NY, with interment to follow in The Sharon Gardens Cemetery. Services by the George T. Davis Funeral Home (914) 632-0324.
