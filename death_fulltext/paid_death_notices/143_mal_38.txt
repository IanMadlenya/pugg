
        SUSSDORF-Dieter H., Ph.D. The Board of Overseers, Faculty, and Staff of the Weill Medical College and Graduate School of Medical Sciences of Cornell University are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed colleague and friend, Dieter H. Sussdorf, Ph.D. A distinguished member of the department of microbiology, Dr. Sussdorf also served as associate and acting dean of the Graduate School of Medical Sciences. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his family. Sanford I. Weill Chairman, Board of Overseers Weill Medical College and Graduate School of Medical Sciences of Cornell University Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Dean, Weill Medical College David P. Hajjar, Ph.D. Dean, Weill Graduate School of Medical Sciences Erik Falck-Pedersen, Ph.D. Acting Chairman, Department of Microbiology
