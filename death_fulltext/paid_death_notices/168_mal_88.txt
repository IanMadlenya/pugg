
        SOLOMON-Irving. Age 86, formerly of the Bronx, died January 5. The founder of LEGI Jewelry. He had a smile for and brought a smile to all who knew him. Predeceased by his wife Evelyn. He is survived by his son Lawrence, his daughter Gail and three grandchildren Rachel, Peter and David. Services will be held at noon on Monday, January 8, from the Garlick Riverside Funeral Chapel, 1439 Unionport Rd, Bronx, NY.
