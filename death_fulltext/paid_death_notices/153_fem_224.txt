
        WOLFF-Barbara. On behalf of the people of Queens County, I extend sincere sympathy to the family, friends and colleageus of Barbara Wolff, Assistant Commissioner of the NYC Department of Business Services. Her dedication to many Queens' neighborhoods have made them better places to live, work and operate a business. All those involved in community development, neighborhood revitalization and government were privileged to have had the opportunity to know and work with her. Claire Shulman, President, Borough of Queens
        WOLFF-Barbara. The Neighborhood Development Division of the NYC Department of Business Services mourns the untimely passing of its beloved Assistant Commissioner. Deepest sympathy to her family and friends.
