
        KOTITE-Albert, 86, of Manhattan, on May 1, 2003. Loving husband for 51 years of Antoinette. Devoted father of Edward, Albert, Peter, Deborah and Toni. Beloved grandfather of Edward, Elizabeth, P.J., Christopher, Aaron, John, Harrison and Topper. A leading member of New York City's printing industry for over 62 years, he was respected and admired by all who had the privilege of working with him. His love of family, his dedication to his work and his generosity will always be remembered. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to St. David's School, 12 East 89 St., New York, NY 10128
