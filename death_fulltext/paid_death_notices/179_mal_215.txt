
        KURRELMEYER-Louis Hayner. Of Shelburne, Vermont, died in Burlington, VT, on Wednesday, December 12, 2001. He was born in Troy, NY, July 26, 1928, the son of Dr. Bernhard Kurrelmeyer and Dr. Lucy J. Hayner. A member of the New York and Washington, D.C., Bar Associations, Lou worked as a litigator in the areas of aviation law and international trade law. He served as an assistant transportation administrator for the City of New York and in 1981, was decorated Knight 1st Class Royal Swedish Order of the North Star. He retired from the firm of Winthrop, Stimpson, Putnam & Roberts in 1996. He is survived by his wife, Martina K. Kurrelmeyer; his children with Phyllis A. Damon: Ellen L. Kurrelmeyer and her husband John S. Travis of Whiting, VT, Louis H. Kurrelmeyer, Jr., and his wife Kelley of Brewster, NY, and Nancy S. Kurrelmeyer of Whiting, VT; and by his grandchildren William and Amy. Services were held on Saturday, December 15, in Burlington, VT.
