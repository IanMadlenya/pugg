
        LOTZOF - David. Beloved husband of the late Sadie. Loving father of Selwyn Lotzof and Lorraine Abramson. Cherished grandfather of Gregg, Jill, Evan, Kaylie and great grandfather of Emma and Leah.
        LOTZOF-David. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are saddened by the passing of David Lotzof. Mr. Lotzof was the beloved father of Lorraine Abramson and father-in-law of Richard Abramson, cherished friends, leaders, and major supporters of our mission, who are devoted to maintaining a strong and vital Jewish community on Long Island, in New York, and throughout the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to Lorraine and Richard, and Lorraine's brother and sister-inlaw, Selwin and Trish Lotzof, and to David's grandchildren and great - grandchildren. May his memory be a blessing. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & C.E.O. Erika Witover, L.I. Chair Stuart Tauber, LI Dir.
