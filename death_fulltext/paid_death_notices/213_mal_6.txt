
        KOOPER--Al. One of the first organizers of ''WAGRO''-Warsaw Ghetto Resistance Oraganization, died at the age of 102. During all these years, he was dedicated to the memory of our tragic past and Jewish resistance. Funeral will take place at Schwartz Bros., 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, Sunday, 11AM. Our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife Jean, and family. On behalf of his friends of WAGRO, Benjamin Meed
