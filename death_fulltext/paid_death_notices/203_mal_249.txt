
        WASHBURN--Abbott M, 88, Eisenhower appointee, Chairman of INTELSAT Conference, FCC Commissioner, General Mills Executive, died peacefully on December 11, 2003 in Washington, DC. He is survived by Wanda, his wife of 40 years, his children, Michael, Dan, and Julia and four grandchildren, Mara, Kate, Genna, and Ruby. Memorial service: All Soul's Church, Unitarian, 2835 16th St NW, Washington, DC, 11:00 AM, January 24, 2004, followed by reception. Donations: All Soul's Church Community Aid Fund and Eisenhower World Affairs Institute.
