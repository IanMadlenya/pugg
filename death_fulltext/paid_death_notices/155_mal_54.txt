
        KASSOP-Harry, Dr. On December 2, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Harriet. Devoted father of Mark Kassop and Sue London. Loving grandfather of Erika, Allison, Erin and Phillip. Dr. Kassop was a prominent physician in the Forest Hills area for more than 60 years. Funeral services Sunday, 10 AM, ''Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels,'' Queens Blvd. at 76th Road, Forest Hills. Donations may be made in his memory to a charity of your choice.
