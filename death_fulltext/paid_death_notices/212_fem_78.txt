
        D'ARCANGELO -- Marjorie Elizabeth (Foster) of Mamaroneck, NY, on September 8, 2004 at age 86. Marjorie was born in Newark, NJ on June 2, 1918 to James Edwin and Elizabeth Lavigna (Evans) Foster. Marjorie received a Bachelor of Science degree from New York University in 1942. On October 20, 1945 she married the late A. Rick D'Arcangelo, who predeceased her in February 2004. She was a wonderful mother and devoted wife whose support for her family, her husband and his career was unwavering. Marjorie is survived by her children James R. (wife Mary Ellen), Inez E. (husband Alfred Thoben), Martha A. and John P. (wife Kathleen). Also survived by nine grandchildren and one great grandchild. Marjorie is predeceased by her brother John Warren Foster (wife Jacqueline). Reposing on Saturday and Sunday from 2 to 4 & 7 to 9PM at Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 East Boston Post Rd, Mamaroneck. Funeral Mass Monday 11:00am at St. Gregory the Great Roman Catholic Church followed by interment at Greenwood Union, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions should be made to the A. Rick andMarjorie E. D'ArcangeloScholarship Fund at NYU , NYU Stern School of Business, 44 West 4th St., Ste 10-54 NY, NY 10012.
