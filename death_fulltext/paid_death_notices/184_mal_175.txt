
        ANSPACH-Ernst. The members, officers and staff of Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church give thanks to God for the life and ministry of their beloved member Ernst Anspach, who died in his home in New York City, April 27, 2002, at the age of 89, and extend our sympathies and prayers to his wife, Ruth; their children, Gretel and David, as well as the many others for whom Ernst was counselor, advocate and friend. Dr. Anspach served Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church as Ruling Elder and Trustee for more than thirty years. ''Well done, good and faithful servant! Enter the joy of your Lord.'' Memorial Service, Friday, May 17, 11 AM, at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, New York City. Contributions in Dr. Anspach's memory mory may be made to the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church.
