
        ROSENBERG-Marjorie H. (nee Marjorie Sylvia Hillson), on March 15, 1999, age 82 years. Early years in Boston area, 55 years Larchmont, NY, relocated to Columbus, Ohio, November 1998. Predeceased last month by her husband of 57 years, internationally renowned scientist, Paul Rosenberg, Ph.D. Predeceased by younger brother, Raymond Hillson. Survived by Columbus residents, daughter, Gale R. Gross; son-inlaw, William F. Gross; grandson, Joshua Samuel Gross. Boston University librarian/administrator, husband's firm 50 years. Prolific reader, eclectic interests in literature, arts, music, politics. Volunteer, extensive professional/social contacts. Funeral service Sunday, 1 PM, at the Ballard Durand Funeral Home, White Plains, NY. Interment Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Family requests donations only to Smith College, Development Office, c/o Jan, Northampton, MA 01063, or Larchmont Public Library, 121 Larchmont Ave., Larchmont, NY 10538.
