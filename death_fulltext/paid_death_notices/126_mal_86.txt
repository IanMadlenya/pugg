
        GEORGE-David L., Jr., of Hawley, PA., and formerly from Pelham, New York, died suddenly Sunday, July 6, 1997. He graduated from Lawrenceville Schools in 1964 & Denver University. He was the son of David L. and Margaret Trimpi George of Hawley, PA. In addition to his parents, he is survived by a brother William T. George of White Mills, PA. Memorial contribution may be made to Hawley Public Library, Main Avenue, Hawley, PA. 18428.
