
        EDELMAN-Esther Muskin. Of Brookline, MA, passed away surrounded by her family on February 10, 2003, and was laid to rest in Israel on February 12, 2003. Beloved wife and partner of Julian, and devoted mother and cherished friend to Elazer (Cheryl), Theodore (Jewel), Raphael (Leslie) and Daniel (Nancy). She is survived as well by 15 loving grandchildren. ''She opens her mouth with wisdom, her tongue is guided by kindness.'' Shiva will be observed at her home through Wednesday, February 19th. Contributions in her memory can be made to The Maimonides School, Brookline, MA.
