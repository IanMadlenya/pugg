
        MILLER-Milton. The Board, staff, and volunteers of the Alzheimer's Association, NYC Chapter, mourn the loss of Milton Miller, esteemed Board member, friend and family caregiver.         MILLER-Milton. The Board of Directors and staff of the Brevoort East acknowledge with great sadness the passing of Milton Miller, the Treasurer of the Cooperative for many years. His intellect, experience and guidance were greatly appreciated by the building and he will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his entire family on their tragic loss.
