
        GILLETTE--Lee, M.D. Died September 4, 2003. Graduated Columbia College P&S, Flight Surgeon in WWII at Omaha Beach on D-Day. He was a caring and compassionate General Surgeon for 40 years at 850 Park Ave, NYC. He leaves beloved wife Phyllis, three children and five grandchildren. Memorial donation to Voluntary Services, VA Hospital, 200 Springs Road, Bedford, MA. 01730.
