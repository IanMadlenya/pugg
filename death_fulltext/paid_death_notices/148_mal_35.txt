
        SOLDWEDEL-Kipp, 86. On April 30th, at Lenox Hill Hospital. Recognized as one of the country's leading maritime artists. Yale graduate, Prix de Rome Scholarship. Beloved husband of Sally Backun Soldwedel, father of Peter. Trustee, National Art Museum of Sport; member, NY Yacht Club; member, Society of Illustrators; alumnus, St. George's School, Class of '33. Memorial services on Friday, May 14, 3 PM at St. Bartholomew's, Park Ave at 50th St, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. George's School, Newport, R.I. 02840.
        SOLDWEDEL-Kipp. The Officers and members of the Society of Illustrators deeply mourn the passing of beloved member Kipp Soldwedel. He will be missed by all who knew him.
