
        BURT-Maggie Z. Horn, 105. Died on February 5th in Alexandria, VA. Born Shelbyville in East Texas in 1896, living there and Fort Worth with siblings and parents Dr. John Henry Horn and Ada Sullivan Horn. Taught piano in Fort Worth, and after 1930, in New York City. Husband, Henry Radcliffe Burt, died in 1937. She moved to the Washington area in 1976. Survivors include son Henry R. Burt of Fairfax, VA., and two grandsons, Henry and Radcliffe of Richmond.
