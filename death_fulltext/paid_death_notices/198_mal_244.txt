
        SCHWARTZ -- William. Age 87, retired aerospace engineer, Of Sunnyvale, CA., died Thursday, July 10, 2003, after a short illness. Son of Joseph and Mary Schwartz, Bill was born in Manhattan and graduated from Erasmus Hall High School and New York University, Class of 1938, with a B.S. in Engineering. He served in WWII and retired as a Lieutenant Colonel in the U.S. Air Force Reserves. He is survived by his wife of 53 years, Blanche Orlinsky Schwartz, sons Joseph and David, grandsons Brian, Jacob and Martin, and his brother Bernard. >>AD#
