
        TUCKER-Lyman B. Former ITT Executive died Oct. 26, 1998 in Sao Paolo, Brazil. He was 94. Also Managing Director, Fruehauf Trailer, S.A. and President AMF do Brazil, Lt. Col. U.S. Army, W.W. II, U.S. Military Representative, Allied Control Commission, Bucharest, Executive with ADELA, Sao Paolo. Graduated Yale 1925. Survived by second wife Ana R. Tucker & daughter Cristina Martorana of Sao Paolo, brother William E. Tucker, Old Saybrook, CT., also a son, Thomas Tucker of Broken Arrow, OK., and daughters June Lael of Boynton Beach, FL., and Jean Tomko of Hinsdale, IL.
