
        ALTMAN-Jean Weiner. Passed away in her home July 7, 1997 at the age of 69. She was an active member of her condo board and a prolific artist in various mediums. Prior to retirement, she owned a jewelery manufacturing company and was active in the professional organizations. She is survived by 2 daughters Marlene Kutler of Long Beach, New York, Risa Kaparo of Mill Valley, California, a son Mitch Altman of Nesconset, N.Y., two sisters, 2 brothers and six grandchildren, nieces, and nephews. Her warmth and good humor will be remembered and cherished by her family and friends.
