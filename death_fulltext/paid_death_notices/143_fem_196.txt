
        PAGE-Jane N. The Board of Directors and staff of the Cold Spring Harbor Whaling Museum Society are deeply saddened by the passing of Jane N. Page and appreciate her family's longtime involvement with the Museum. Together with her father, George Nichols, and her husband, Walter H. Page, both past Presidents of the Museum, she had a great interest in the Society. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to all of her family. John R. Miller III, President, Board of Directors Ann M. Gill, Executive Director Cold Spring Harbor Whaling Museum
