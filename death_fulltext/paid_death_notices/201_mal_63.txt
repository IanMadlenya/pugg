
        SHAPIRO--Dr. Philip, 69, of Frederick, MD, died Saturday, October 4th at the Frederick Memorial Hospital. He was the husband of the late Ursula Shapiro who died May 8, 2001. Dr. Shapiro attended Columbia College and graduated from NYU Medical School. He practiced internal medicine in the Frederick MD area for more than 33 years. Surviving are one brother, Dr. Herbert Shapiro and wife Geri of Scarsdale, NY, and a niece, Dr. Leslie Eber of Colorado. Stauffer Funeral Home, Frederick, MD, served the family.
