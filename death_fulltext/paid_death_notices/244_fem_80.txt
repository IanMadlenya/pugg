
        QUINN--Barbara L. (nee McAward) of Old Brookville, NY and Lakewood, NJ died on May 8, 2007. Loving wife of the late Robert John Quinn; devoted mother of Barbara Quinn McElroy, Thomas, Robert and Patrick Quinn and Suzanne Quinn Craig; loving grandmother of five; cherished sister of Mary Patricia Bell; Frances A. Costello; Sr. Nancy McAward, O.P., and Patrick J. McAward. Mrs. Quinn is a graduate of Dominican Academy in Manhattan, NY and the College of St. Elizabeth, Convent Station, NJ. Mrs. Quinn spent much of her life devoted to charitable causes. She was honored as Senior PGA Tour Volunteer of the Year in 2001. Reposing at O'Brien Funeral Home, Burnt Tavern Road at Highway 70, Brick, NJ. Visitation Thursday, May 10, 2-4pm and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian Burial, Friday, May 11, 11:00am, Sacred Heart Church, Bay Head, NJ. Burial on Saturday, May 12 at 12:30pm, Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate contributions to their parish of St. Aloysius Church, Church St., Livingston Manor, NY 12758.
