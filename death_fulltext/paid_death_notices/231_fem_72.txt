
        MOHACSY--Ildiko on April 10. Survived by her beloved son Nicholas. Adoring grandmother of April. She was affiliated with Mt. Sinai School of Medicine and The William Alanson White Institute. She was the author of many important papers on psychotherapy. Contributions in her name may be sent to The Children's Orchestra Society, 1355 Northern Blvd. Manhasset, New York 11030. Services private.
