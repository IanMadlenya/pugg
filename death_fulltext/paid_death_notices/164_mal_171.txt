
        SCHORR-Max. Beloved Palm Beach and former New York City attorney, 92, died Tuesday afternoon in Palm Beach from complications of prostate cancer. A counsel at Edwards & Angell, Max was one of Florida's oldest practicing attorneys. A graduate of Brooklyn Law School, he practiced from the early 30's until 1972 in New York City and White Plains. He is survived by two sons; Tony of Rye, New York, and Andrew of Mercer Island, Washington; 10 grandchildren; and three great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Thursday at 2PM at Temple Beth El in West Palm Beach. Contributions may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
        SCHORR-Max. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Max Schorr. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved family. Richard Bornstein, President Leon Fassler, Secretary
