
        WALTHER -- Karl Ernest. Passed away at home in Cardiff-By-The-Sea, CA, on October 13, 2005. Born in New York City on September 17, 1937, to Ernest Walther and Geraldine Gristede Walther, he is survived by his beloved wife Noemi Balinth, Ph.D. In lieu of flowers gifts may be made in his name to The New York Public Library/HSSL, Fifth Avenue and 42nd St, Room 73, NY NY 10018.
