
        VEGHTE-Mary Alice. Of New York City and Canajoharie, New York, died on April 28, 1998 at the age of 75 following a short illness. A graduate of Smith College, she was administrative officer of the tax department at Brown Brothers Harriman for forty-three years. She was an enthusiastic volunteer for many charitable organizations in the city. She was predeceased by her brother, Lewis Veghte Jr., and is survived by her niece, Elizabeth C. Veghte of Cardiff-bythe-Sea, California, and her nephew, William L. Veghte of Seattle, WA. A memorial service will be held in Canajoharie at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to Smith College or the Reformed Church of Canajoharie.
