
        BERLINER-Benjamin C. Died peacefully at home on May 23 in Bloomfield, CT after a long illness. Born on February 1, 1917 to William and Anna Berliner, he was raised in Woodmere, L.I. where he graduated from Woodmere Academy in 1935. He went on to graduate from Yale University in 1939 and Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons in 1942. He completed his residency at Cincinnati Children's Hospital and embarked upon a career in pediatrics that spanned 50 years, dedicated to caring for children in need and teaching others to do likewise. He was also a world acclaimed orchidist, avid birder, devoted environmentalist, naturalist and staunch defender of human dignity. He will be missed by all who knew him. Beloved husband of Esther Ann (Skippy); brother of William, Robert and the late Herbert; father of Frank, Charles, Benjamin and Anna; grandfather of Nicholas, Samuel, Zachary, Nathaniel, Christopher and Daniel; great grandfather of little Sasha. We love you Papa Ben. Graveside funeral will be private. A memorial service is planned and will be announced at a later time. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Orchid Conservancy of the Brooklyn Botanic Garden.
