
        LURIA--Gladys (nee Claman). Consummate New Yorker, widow of Alvord N. Luria, died peacefully in her sleep, Saturday, April 9th, 2005, shortly after her 96th birthday. She is survived by her beloved daughter Joan L. Fisher and grandchildren Carl T. Fisher, Amy Luria Nissenbaum, Robert Luria and three great-grandchildren as well as her daughter-in-law Susan Luria, wife of her late son Richard A. Luria. Funeral services will be held Monday, April 11th, at 1 pm, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Mosholu Montefiore Community Center, 3450 Dekalb Avenue, Bronx, NY 10467.
        LURIA--Gladys C. To our beloved Gaga--we will miss your benevolent heart, caring devotion and courageous spirit. With all our love -Your children, Richard and Suzi, grandchildren, Amy, Rob, Jake and Susie, and great-granchildren, Shelby, Clay and Hallie
        LURIA--Gladys C. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished longtime member, Gladys C. Luria. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C.
