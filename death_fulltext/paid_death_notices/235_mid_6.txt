
        GREENBERG--Anna. On August 26th, 2006, at the age of 99. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Sidney Greenberg. Devoted mother of Edward and his wife Carole, Elaine J. and her husband John C. May. Cherished grandmother of Jan, Theodore, Caron and Leslie. Loving great-grandmother of six girls and extended Robbins family. Funeral service Wednesday, 1 pm, at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn.
