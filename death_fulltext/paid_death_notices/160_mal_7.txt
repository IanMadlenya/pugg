
        SAIVETZ-Richard. Suddenly on Friday, April 28, of Newton, MA. Husband of Carol (Richman). Father of Aliza Saivetz of Newton, MA and Michael Saivetz of New York City. Son of Bradford and Thelma ''Temi'' (Steir) Saivetz of Quincy, MA. Brother of Ruthellen and Dr. Marc Rubin of Yardley, PA. Son-in-law of Fred and Rita Richman of Longboat Key, FL. Brother-in-law of James and Elissa Richman of New York City. Services were held at Temple Emanuel, 385 Ward St., Newton, MA on Sunday, April 30. Shiva at his late residence following interment and continuing daily 1-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Remembrances may be made to the Saivetz Endowed School Fund at Brandeis University, 415 South St., Waltham, MA 02454 or the Beaver Country Day School, 791 Hammond St., Chestnut Hill, MA 02467. Information: Levine Chapels, Brookline, MA.
