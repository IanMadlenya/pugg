
        BLUESTONE--Dr. Peter A. On July 19, after a courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. Beloved brother of Joanna. Devoted son of the late Dr. and Mrs. Aaron Bluestone. He was loved and respected by numerous relatives, friends, patients and colleagues. He devoted his life to the care of those suffering from kidney disease. The family has requested contributions to be made to Kidney and Urology Foundation of America. Funeral service 10 AM, Tuesday, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
