
        LEWIS-Beatrice. ''Omnia Ornavit Tetigit''. ''She graced everything she touched.'' A graduate of Marymount College and a Masters of Social Work at Hunter College. She was Director of Kittay House, a division of Jewish Home and Hospital, until her retirement. Her magnificent spirit lives on in her children Carole, Conrad, Barbara and Tony, her grandchildren Schuyler, Peter, Heather, Tim, Terry, Randi, Becca and her great grandchild Eli Griffin Shaw. Services Friday. June 30th, 1PM, at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
