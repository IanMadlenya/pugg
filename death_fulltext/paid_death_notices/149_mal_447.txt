
        DESSER-Maxwell. A gentleman by nature, so gracious even in receipt of things which were so plainly his due. Lt., USN Signal Corps during WWII. An artist by trade and affection, a National Academician in the National Academy of Design and a Dolphin Fellow and officer of the American Watercolor Society.
