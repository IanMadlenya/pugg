
        BOOSS-Muriel Gaukrodger., 93, a resident of Cheshire Convalescent Center, Cheshire, Conn. died on January 1, 2000. She was the widow of George F. Booss. Mrs. Booss was retired from Columbia University where she was a placement officer. She was a graduate of Barnard, a member of the Metropolitan Museum of Art and many charitable and environmental organizations. Mrs. Booss is survived by a daughter Claire Booss of Brooklyn, a son Dr. John Booss of Bethany, CT., and two grandchildren, Christine and Daniel Booss. She was predeceased by two sisters Edith Gaukrodger and Dorothy Rodger. A memorial service is planned for a future date in Brooklyn. Donations in her memory may be made to Spanish Refugee Aid, 122 East 42nd St, NY, New York 10168. For information West Haven Funeral Home at the Green, West Haven, CT.
