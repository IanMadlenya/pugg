
        SHEPARD-Sylvia Wilson. On July 3, at 90. Beloved wife of the late Lee, devoted mother of James and Grace, dear sister of Julian Wilson, loving grandmother of James Jr., Andrew and Cheryl, Peter and Elizabeth. Survived also by a host of loving friends and relatives. Her career in the NYC school system spanned 30 years as teacher and district curriculum coordinator. Following retirement she worked as an advisor for the Grolier Corporation. Private burial in Sherborn, MA. A memorial service will be scheduled at a later date.
