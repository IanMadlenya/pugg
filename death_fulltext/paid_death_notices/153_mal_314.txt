
        GIBIAN-George. 75. Unexpectedly, at home, in Ithaca, N.Y. on October 24, 1999. He was the Goldwin Smith Professor of Russian and Comparative Literature at Cornell University and a decorated veteran of W.W. II. He was born in Prague. Scholar, author, editor, translator and devoted family man, George is survived seven children, fifteen grandchildren, two brothers, and his partner Karen Brazell.
