
        LOWRY-Philip Holt. On February 28, 1998, at home in McLean, VA. He is survived by his daughters Georgia Harmony Lowry Orphan of Vienna, VA, and Marion Holt Lowry of Los Angeles, and his sister Marion L. Pennell of Washington Depot, CT. Services and interment will be private. Memorial contributions may be made to the Margaret K. Lowry Book Fund, Potomac School, McLean, VA.
