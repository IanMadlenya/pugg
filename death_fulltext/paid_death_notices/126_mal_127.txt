
        BERLINER-Rabbi Albert. On July 12, 1997. Beloved husband of Marilyn. Loving father of Atara, Shira & Gary Pickholz and Elana and Ira Tepler. Loving brother of Edith Bane and doting grandfather. He devoted his life to Jewish education and to the inculcation of Torah values. Greatly admired for his gentleness, integrity and devotion.
