
        JONES-Rev. Robert C. C.Ss.R, born December 24, 1952, age 47, died peacefully in the presence of his family on April 6, 2000. Member of the Congregation of the Most Holy Redeemer. Presently Associate Pastor of Saint Ursula Church, Parkville, MD. Arch Diocese of Baltimore and Chaplain of Notre Dame Preparatory School in Towson, MD; formerly of Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Ellicott City, MD; Saint Mary's Church Annapolis, MD; Basilica of Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Boston, MA; and Most Holy Redeemer Church, Manhattan, NY. Beloved son of Bridget Mary and the late Robert C. Jones. Devoted brother of Gerard and Nancy Jones, Debra Ryan and Patrice Halas. Loving brotherin-law of Deborah Caldwell, Robert Ryan and Walter Halas. Cherished uncle of Richard and Robert Ryan and William and David Jones.
