
        NEIMETH-Catherine. On June 21, 2001 at the age of 99. In her home in Woodmere, Long Island. Devoted wife of the late Edward. Beloved mother of Richard and the late Donald. Loving mother-inlaw of Peggy. Grandmother of Debbie, Pamela, Amy and Lisa. Great-grandmother of ten beautiful children. She was a loving and indomitable woman who fought her way through the loss of a son of 23 and three major life-threatening illnesses. Private burial.
