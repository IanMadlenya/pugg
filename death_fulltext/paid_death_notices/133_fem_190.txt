
        REED-Dr. Frances C., Ph.D. in Psychology. She leaves behind her beloved husband, Lionel C. Reed, and her four sons, Drs. Harold, Lawrence, Irving and Barry Reed. A feminist before her time, she devoted her life to the education of the slow learner and was instrumental in the development of tests used to diagnose these problems. Her family and friends sadly mourn her passing. Susan, Alex and Lacey Tisch
