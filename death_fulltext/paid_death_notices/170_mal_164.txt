
        MILLER-Jacob DDS. Born Bronx, NY, 1928. Graduated University of Pennsylvania School of Dentistry 1950. Served as Captain, U.S. Air Force 1952-1954. After 25 years in dentistry Dr. Miller took great pride in guiding his sons as Treasurer/Comptroller of Drybranch Inc. for over 20 years. Beloved husband of Florence, devoted father of Arthur, Howard and Susan, father-in-law of Sheryl, Joan and Dan. Loving grandfather of Lauren, Danny, Rachel, Benjamin, Ethan and Carly. Dear brother of Edith Geisinger. Service Wednesday, March 14, 1:45PM at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Exp, Fresh Meadows, NY.
