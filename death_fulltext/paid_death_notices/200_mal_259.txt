
        HIRSCHBERG--Robert S. On September 25. Son of the late Samuel and Betty. Survived by his sister, Judith (Seymour) Liss. His niece and nephew Jan Liss (Jim Coddington) and Jonathan (Ann) Liss. Great-nephew Jack Wolfe Liss. Friend to so many. He is also fondly remembered by Marian Gibson, missed by his loyal pal Duffy. Funeral at Central Synagogue, Lexington Ave. & 55th St., Friday, September 26 at 10 AM. For information: ''The Riverside'', 212-362-6600.
        HIRSCHBERG--Robert. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its member, Robert Hirschberg. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred Youngwood, President
