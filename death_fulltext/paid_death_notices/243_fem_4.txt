
        RAKOW--Jane. To know you was to love you. Deepest sympathy to all the family. You will be sorely missed. Elaine
        $(6$)RAKOW--Jane Zamochnick devoted wife of the late Dr. Robert B. Rakow. Adored mother of Cheryl and Leonard Spilka and Ilene and Mark Zelniker. Cherished grandmother of Andrew and Dana Schmierer, Ross and Rachael Schmierer, Lindsay and Matthew Aaronson, Jordan Zelniker and Robert Zelniker. Special great-grandmother to Brandon and Aidan Schmierer and Avery and Chase Aaronson. Caring sister of Leona and Sidney Solomon, beloved Aunt. Past President of Brooklyn/Staten Island Council B'nai Brith and B'nai Brith women, honored at the United Nations with the prestigious Jewish Women International Tikkun Olum award for outstanding service. Elected to the B'nai Brith Hall of Fame, she led an active life of fundraising and doing mitzvot. Services Sunday April 1, 2007 at 11:30 am at The North Shore Synagogue, 83 Muttontown Rd., Syosset. (www.northshoresynagogue. org). Contributions may be made to the Cystic Fibrosis Research Foundation, 425 Broad Hollow Rd., Suite 318, Mellville, NY 11747. Jane, the matriarch of our family, you will be held deeply in our hearts forever.
