
        WEISMAN-Irv. The New York Cycle Club mourns the passing of Irv Weisman, Past President and longtime club member. Irv will be remembered as an inspiration, tutor and mentor to cyclists of all levels and a tireless advocate for safe and effective cycling. Memories of his generous spirit, irrepresible wit and tireless service to the club and its members for over 40 years will be cherished by all who knew him. We will miss him dearly. Board of Directors, NYCC
