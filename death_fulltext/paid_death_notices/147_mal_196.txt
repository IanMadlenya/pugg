
        GUARINO-Lt. Col. Harold B. Of Chester, VA. On April 16, 1999. Devoted husband of Col. Judith M. Guarino. Loving father of Kenneth and Jane Guarino and Maj. Nickolas and Noel Guarino. Proud grandfather of Benjamin, Johanna and Gabriel Guarino. Beloved brother of Charles. Born in NYC in 1938 and retired from the U.S. Army after serving in an illustrious career as a Green Beret where he was the first officer to lead a Special Forces Operation in Southeast Asia. Burial with full military honors Tuesday April 27, 8:30 AM in Arlington National Cemetery.
