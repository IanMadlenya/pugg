Devoted mother of Eleanor and Walter and mother-in-law of Louis Newman and Ann Pincus. Loving grandmother of Peter, Cathy, Barbara, Andrew, Ward, Adam and Battle, and great-grandmother of Joshua, Kate, Emma, Jenny, Kate and Martha. We will all miss her.
