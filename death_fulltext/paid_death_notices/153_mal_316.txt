
        GOLDENSON-Robert M., Ph.d 91 A Memorial Service was held October 23 at Princeton University. A Westchester resident for 40 years, he was Chief Psychologist of United Cerebral Palsy of Westchester, and the author of ten books. He is survived by his wife, Irene, of Jamesburg, NJ, two sons and daughters-in-law, Daniel and Suzanne of Princeton, NJ, and Ronald and Ellen of Cohasset, MA, a brother William, and two grandsons, Andrew and Jeffrey.
