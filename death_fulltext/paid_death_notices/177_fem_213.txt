
        REISS-Clara. A patron of the arts. Died October 19, 2001 at her residence on Park Avenue. Born August 29th, 1904, married to Professor Frederick Reiss, M.D. who was a world renowned specialist in tropical dermatology. She is survived by her nieces Maria Gero and Suzanne Palocz. Service Sunday, 1 PM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
