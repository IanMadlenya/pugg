
        LICHTENBERG--Harold G. June 2, 1924 to October 30, 2005 of Woodmere, New York and Boca Raton, Florida. Devoted husband of the late Laura. Loving father of Maxine Gerson, Jeffrey, Peter (Jamie) and Susie Blumenfeld (Todd) and PopPop to Jessica, Jordan, Ryan, Brett, Nicole, Katie & Jennie. Veteran of WW II. He was the owner of Lichtenberg Buick for over forty years and past president of the 5 Boro Buick Dealers. A Queens College graduate. Devoted to family, friends, loyal employees and the St. Louis Cardinals. HGL, we will love you and miss you forever.
        LICHTENBERG -- Harold. Temple Beth El of Cedarhurst records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member, Harold Lichtenberg. We grieve with the family on their loss. Dr. Sholom Stern, Rabbi Richard Holland, President
        LICHTENBERG--Harold G. Daddy, HGL, Pop Pop, you taught us about life's most important lessons. Devotion, true love, and how to be good people. Thanks for the memories and all of your love. Susie, Todd, Nicki, Katie, and Jennie
