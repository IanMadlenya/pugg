
        MARTINSON -- Paul. The Trustees and Staff of the American Folk Art Museum record with deep sorrow the death of their dear friend, Paul Martinson, husband of the Museum's esteemed Executive Vice President, Frances Sirota Martinson. Paul's quiet dignity masked a prodigious intellect, a sure wit and an unwavering commitment to social justice. Devoted to family and friends, Paul will be remembered by us with abiding respect and affection. To Frances and the Martinson family we extend our heartfelt condolences. Ralph Esmerian, Chairman L. John Wilkerson, President Board of Trustees Gerard C. Wertkin, Director
        MARTINSON--Paul. Died Saturday morning, March 20, 2004, at the age of 96. Survived by his beloved wife Frances (Steyer Sirota) Martinson; son John Martinson and daughter-in-law Suzanne Pickett Martinson; daughter Linda Martinson Mayer and son-in-law Christopher Mayer; grandchildren Jillian, Joey and C.J. Mayer and Neo and Eco Martinson. Memorial contributions may be made to American Folk Art Museum, Manhattan School of Music, or Shakespeare & Co. of Lenox, MA. At home: Wednesday and Thursday, March 24-25, 5-8 pm.
