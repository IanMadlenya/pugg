
        POLLOCK--Rose Beverly, 85, died peacefully on July 20, 2003. She was the loving wife of the late Irving Pollock, mother of Irene Goldman and Steven Pollock, grandmother of Rebecca and Jesse Goldman and sister of Abe and Max Kramer. A modest woman, Rose, never realized how many lives she touched. Services Tuesday 11 AM at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 2nd Avenue at 10th Street, Manhattan. >>AD#
