
        GELFAND-Morris A. Beloved companion of Judith Follmann, died on October 1st, 1998 age 90. He seemed possessed of eternal youth, & we will miss him deeply. He leaves family, James Munn Gelfand, Lisa Jay Gelfand, Grant & Amy Ujifusa, Jeffrey and Karen Brooks, & grandchildren Andrew and Zachary Wright, Steven and Andrew Ujifusa, and Elizabeth and Emily Brooks. A memorial will be held at the Grolier Club, 47 East 60th Street, Wednesday, October 14 at 11:30 AM.
