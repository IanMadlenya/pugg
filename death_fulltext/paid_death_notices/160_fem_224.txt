
        LEVIT-Doris. Beloved wife of Aaron Levit, and a respected educator and Guidance Counselor in Baldwin, New York, passed away on Sunday, May 14, at her home in Boynton Beach, Florida. She is survived by four sons, Stephen, Mark, Ian and Keith, their wives Margaret, Sally, Donna and Anne, grandchildren Eric and Kevin, and her companion of many years Sidney Gardner.
