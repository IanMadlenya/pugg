
        TEITELBAUM-Robert P. On April 13th, 1998 of a heart attack. He was 68. Beloved father of Bonnie, Sheila and Barry. Cherished father-in-law to Steven, Paul and Michael. Adored ''Papa Bobby'' to grandsons Daniel and Jayson. Mr. Teitelbaum was the Sr. Managing Director at the brokerage firm of Ladenburg, Thalman. He had been with the company since 1975. He was a member of the board of Happiness is Camping, a camp for children with cancer. He was the financial advisor pro bono for the United Odd Fellow and Rebekah Home. His sense of humor and love of life will be remembered by all who knew and loved him. Contributions can be made in his memory to Happiness is Camping, 2169 Grand Concourse, Bronx, New York 10453.
        TEITELBAUM-Robert. The Officers and Directors of the United Odd Fellow and Rebekah Home offer their condolences to the family of Robert Teitelbaum of Ladenburg, Thalmann & Co. A trusted friend and advisor.
