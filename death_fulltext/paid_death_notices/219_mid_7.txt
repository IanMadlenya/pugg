
        BUCHNER -- Wolfgang. 55, died April 1, 2005. Survived by mother and stepfather, Inge and Werner Schneider, siblings Andreas Buchner, Eric Buchner and Sabine Wilms and her three children, and by his carefully tended and now forlorn garden of friends. A permanent memorial is being established at the Riverside Park 72nd Street Dog Run. Contributions to memorial appreciated. Contact Harry Franklin: 212 - 356 - 8100 or hfranklin@cirseiu.org
