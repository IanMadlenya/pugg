
        CORBETTA--Louis J. (Jerry). Of Norwalk, CT (formerly of Scarsdale, NY), died on July 30, 2004, at the age of 80. Mr. Corbetta was President of Corbetta Construction Company, a firm founded by his father and uncle in 1922. During his career, he was involved as the concrete constructor and general contractor of many projects in 20 states, Spain and Puerto Rico. Some of the projects included the Dulles Airport Terminal Building, Tappan Zee Bridge, the Union Carbide Headquarters in Danbury, CT, and both the New York State Theater and Fordham University in Lincoln Center. Before his retirement, he was active in many building organizations in the New York Metropolitan area. He attended Cornell University and Louisiana State University before serving in the US Army Corps of Engineers in WWII. He was a member of the Westchester Country Club for over 50 years and enjoyed golf, the seashore and spending time with his family. He is survived by his wife Evelyn Danks Corbetta and his five children, Robert Corbetta, Jane Yezzi, Maryann Cousins, Richard Corbetta and Allison Radin. He is also survived by eight grandchildren and two sisters, Vee Landolfe and Caryl Schiess.
