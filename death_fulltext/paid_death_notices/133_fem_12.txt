
        HELLER-Sondra, Ph.D, January 28, 1998 of Tarrytown, NY. Wife of Joel Frutkin, mother of Jenny and Jocelyn Dauphin and Jeremy Frutkin. As educator, scholar and mentor she devoted her life to children as Principal and Teacher at Yeshiva of Kings Bay in Brooklyn, Solomon Schecter High School in Brooklyn and for more than a decade dedicated her life to thousands of children as head of the N.Y. Childhood Center. In recent years she devoted her talents to the training of teachers as Coordinator of the Graduate Masters Program at Mercy College. Services private. In lieu of flowers, donations to RSHM Life Center, 32-34 Beekman Avenue, Sleepy Hollow, N.Y. 10591 would be appreciated.
