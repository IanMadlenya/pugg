
        WEISS--Albert of New York City, Quogue, and Boca Raton died suddenly of a heart attack on July 1st, 2006. Albert was married to his true love Beverly Weiss and together they built a business and a family. Their marriage was a true partnership of unflagging love and dedication to each other. Albert was devoted to daughter Karen Bander, son-in-law Neil, grandsons Gregory and Evan who lit up his life and brother-in-law Alvin. Albert was a gentle, generous and loving man, respected in his field for his knowledge, honesty and ethics. A caring and loyal friend, Albert will be deeply missed. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Wednesday July 5th, 2006 at 10:30 a.m.
