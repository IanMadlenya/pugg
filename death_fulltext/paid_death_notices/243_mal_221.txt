
        LEON--Louis, A. Devoted husband of 65 years to Gertrude (who pre-deceased him) died April 18 at the age of 92. Born and raised in Brooklyn, he attended Brooklyn college at night. During WWII he was stationed in China and was awarded the Bronze star. Brilliant and hard working, Lou rose through the civil service ranks to become Director of Dept. of Social Services in NY. He later worked at Phoenix House. Dedicated to social justice and education, he conveyed his values to his children and grandchildren. He is survived by his son Michael, daughterin-law Judith, daughter Eleanor and son-in-law Peter as well as three grandsons, Matthew, Robert, and David and his wife Amy. A funeral service will be held Sunday, April 22 at Pacific View Cemetery at Corona Del Mar, CA. Contributions in his memory can be made to the American Cancer Society.
