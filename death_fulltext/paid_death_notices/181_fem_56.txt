
        SCHWARTZ-Pauline (Bobby) in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late Ben. Devoted mother of Martin and Diana Blender, Judith and Murray Braunstein. Cherished grandmother and great grandmother. A lady of great style who loved her family, asked nothing for herself and derived great pleasure in helping and giving to others. Graveside services Tuesday, February 5, 11 AM, Mount Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, L.I. (Exit 33 Southern State Parkway).
