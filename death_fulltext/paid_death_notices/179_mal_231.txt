
        GIDDINGS-Paul G. Age 67. On October 18, 2001 after a long battle with cancer. Mr. Giddings was the first full time employee of Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette in 1959 and retired as Executive Vice President in 1975. He was formerly Vice Chair of the Studio Museum in Harlem and a consultant to the Municipal Assistance Corporation for the City of New York. He was also a jazz aficionado and, for many years, co-owner of the popular Village restaurant Zinno's, which regularly featured jazz performers. He is survived by his brother Arthur Giddings, nephews James and Robert and a devoted circle of friends. Interment took place in Oak Brook, Illinois.
