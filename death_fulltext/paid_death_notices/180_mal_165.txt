
        McCUTCHEON-Bill W., 77. A resident of Mahwah for 42 years, on January 9, 2002. Beloved husband of Anne (Routsong) McCutcheon. Loving father of Carol Crevani of Mahwah, NJ, Jay McCutcheon of Baltimore, MD., and Kenna McCutcheon Colley of Blacksburg, VA. Adored grandfather of 5 grandchildren. Brother in law of Harris Leroy and Carol Clinger. Bill was an actor all of his life, appearing in television, commercials, on Broadway and Stage Shows. Bill was well known as ''Uncle Wally'' on Sesame Street, and received a Tony Award for Anything Goes in 1988. He retired 11 years ago. Bill was a U.S. Army WWII Veteran and a recipient of the Purple Heart. A celebration of Bill's life on Saturday, January 12, 2002 at the Rosencrantz House at the Hermitage, 335N. Franklin Tpk, Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ from 1:00 till 4:00PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in Bill's name to the Actor's Fund, 729 7th Avenue, New York, New York 10019. For information contact the Van Emburgh-Sneider Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Avenue, Ramsey, New Jersey, please call 201-327-0030.
