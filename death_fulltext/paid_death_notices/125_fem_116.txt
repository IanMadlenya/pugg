
        ROSEN-Marlene. The officers, leadership, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Marlene Rosen, beloved mother of our friend and Board member, Leslie Fastenberg. Leslie and her husband, David, are active and exemplary leaders and generous supporters of UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to her children, Leslie and David, Joel Rosen and his wife Kathleen, and Norman Rosen; her sister, Beverly and her husband Stanley Weiner; her grandchildren; and the entire family. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, Exec. V.P. Arlene Wittels, Long Island Chair Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
