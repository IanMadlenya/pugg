
        WALSH--Joseph N., Jr. New York Medical College mourns the passing of Joseph N. Walsh, Jr., our esteemed colleague and friend. Mr. Walsh was a member of the Board of Trustees since 1989. During his tenure he served on the board's Executive Committee and chaired the Academic Affairs Committee. His support and counsel will be deeply missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Patricia and their children. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Rev. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett, D.Min., M.P.H. President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D. Provost and Dean School of Medicine
