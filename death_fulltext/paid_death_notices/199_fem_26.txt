
        GRABEL--Betty on August 3, 2003 at 96. Beloved mother of William and Maxine, loyal mother-in-law to Linda and Stanley. Cherished grandmother of Naomi, David, Cara, Jill and Jonathan, doting great-grandmother to Zachary, Carolyn, Benjamin, Nathaniel and Julia, and devoted wife to the late Irving for 53 years. She was greatly loved and will be sorely missed by all who knew her. Contributions may be made in her memory to Beth El Synagogue, New Rochelle, NY.
