
        STOCKHAUSEN--Thomas C. 93, on September 21, 2006 of pneumonia. Devoted husband of Anne. Loving father of Michael and Patricia. Adoring grandfather of T.J. Emmerman. Yale class of 1936. WW II veteran. Former Executive Vice President of W.R. Grace. Member of the Sands Point and Garden City Golf Clubs and University Club. His gentleness, intelligence and integrity will be missed. Services private at St. Ignatius Loyola.
