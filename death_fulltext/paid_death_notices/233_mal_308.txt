
        GOLDBERGER--Seymour. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of Fresh Meadow Country Club, record the passing of our esteemed member, Seymour Goldberger. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Selma, his sister and brother-in-law, Helen and Lawrence Doppelt, his son Marc Goldberger and the entire family.
