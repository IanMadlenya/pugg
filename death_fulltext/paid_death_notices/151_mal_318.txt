
        ORENSTEIN-Leo L., M.D. Died August 25, 1999 at age 93. Loving father of Jane and Joe Meyer, Reed and Arlene Orenstein & Dr. Mayer Liebman. Grandfather of Sam, Marnie and Ben Liebman & Jillian Orenstein. Great grandfather of Yahuda Liebman. Husband of the late Beatrice, his reason for living. Retired Professor of Clinical Psychiatry, NYU School of Medicine, Bellevue, Mount Sinai and Beth Israel hospitals for over 50 years. Lifetime Member of New York Psychoanalytic and the American Psychiatric Association. Private graveside service Mount Lebanon Cemetery, 10 AM today.
