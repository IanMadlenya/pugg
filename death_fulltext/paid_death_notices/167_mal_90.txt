
        DORE-Rev. Denis W. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former parochial vicar of St. Pius X Church, 148-10 249 St, Rosedale, on Dec. 5. Reposing in O'Reilly Funeral Home, 137-40 Brookville Blvd., Rosedale, on Thursday, December 7 from 2-4 & 7-9 PM and Friday, Dec. 8 from 2-4 PM. Vigil for the deceased at St. Pius X Church, Friday, December 8 at 8 PM. Funeral Mass Saturday, December 9 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of The Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
