
        PREM-Sybil Gertrude Nichols. On November 6, 1997 in Bridgeville, PA., at The Country Meadows Nursing and Rehabilitation Center at age 98. A long-time resident of Larchmont, N.Y., and New York, NY, she was the wife of the late F. Herbert Prem, a senior partner in the New York City admiralty law firm of Bigham, Englar, Jones & Houston, who died in 1974. For many years she had been active in fund raising activities for the New York Philharmonic Society, and had also served as Chairwoman of the Comite Maritime International Meeting in the United States. She is survived by her son, F. Herbert Prem, Jr. of New York, New York, Sharon, CT., and West Brooksville, ME., her daughter, Lois Prem Simon, of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL., five grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Services will be private.
