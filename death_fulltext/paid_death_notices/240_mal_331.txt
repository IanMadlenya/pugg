
        SILVERMAN--Jerome. It is with deep regret that DOROT announces the passing of Jerome Silverman, beloved father of our esteemed and cherished Board Co-President, Phil Silverman. Phil's devotion to his father, especially during his last years, exemplifies the highest values to which DOROT aspires. May the memory of Jerome Silverman be a blessing to Phil and his family. Paula Resnick Co-President Vivian Fenster Ehrlich Executive Director Members of the Board and Staff, DOROT, Inc.
