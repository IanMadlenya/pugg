
        BINNIAN--Jacqueline C., 83, of Greenport, L.I., on May 7, 2005. Beloved wife of the late William Binnian. Survived by her sister Anne B. Rienhoff & nephew Chadwick Pfeiffer and many cousins. Longtime resident of LLoyd Harbor, NY and Deer Isle, ME. An avid gardener ad ardent environmentalist. Memorial service in Cold Spring Harbor at a later date. Donations may be made to Nature Conservancy's Mashomac Preserve, PO Bx 850, Shelter Island, NY 11904 or St. John's Church, PO Bx 266, Cold Spring Harbor, NY 11724.
