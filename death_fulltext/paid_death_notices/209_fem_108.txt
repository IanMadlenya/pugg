
        LINDSAY--Shirley Blois died on May 1 in Jacksonville. From 1976 to 1981, she was Director of The Diller-Quaile School of Music. She is survived by her husband James Lindsay; children Susan, Ken, Pippi; six grandchildren; and one great grandchild. DillerQuaile extends its sympathy and will hold a gathering June 17 at 24 E. 95th St. at 10 AM.
