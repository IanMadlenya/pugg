
        JEWETT-Nancy R. (Tucky). Age 54, of New York City and Lyme, CT., of cancer on February 25, 1997. Wife of Jonathan. Mother of Brooke, Lindsay and Ian. Memorial Service on Saturday, March 8th at 11:30 A.M., at Old Lyme Congregational Church, Old Lyme, CT. She was actively involved with the Conservatory Garden in Central Park, and the inspiration for her family's garden in Lyme. Gifts in her memory may be made to Wave Hill Inc., 675 West 252 Street, Bronx, New York 10471, where she volunteered her time and energy for many years.
