
        EBY-Frank H. Jr, Ph.D. Of Broomall, PA, age 73, on October 19, 2000. Dr. Eby was a retired Villanova University professor. Beloved husband of 25 years to the late Kathleen (nee Toohey). He is survived by his son Frank H. Eby III (''Butch'') and his daughter, Patricia L. Seltzer and her husband Jeffrey, of Paoli, PA. Family and friends are invited to attend his funeral service on Tuesday, at 11 AM, at Spencer T. Videon, Garrett Rd, at Shadeland Ave, Drexel Hill, PA. Interment will be in Arlington Cemetery. Friends may call Monday, from 7-9 PM, and Tuesday from 10-11 AM, at the funeral home.
