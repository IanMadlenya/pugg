
        BLUESTONE--Harvey, M.D. On behalf of the members of the New York State Psychiatric Association we mourn the death of Harvey Bluestone, M.D. on March 11, 2006. Past President of the New York State Psychiatric Association, past Speaker of the Assembly of the American Psychiatric Association and past Area II Trustee to the Board of Trustees of the American Psychiatric Association. Dr. Bluestone was recognized as an outstanding clinician, educator and administrator as Professor Emeritus of Psychiatry at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and past Director of the Department of Psychiatry at Bronx-Lebanon Medical Center. We extend our sympathies to his wife, Eleanor, and their children. Barry B. Perlman, M.D., President Seth P. Stein, Esq., Executive Director
