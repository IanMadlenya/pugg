
        HEVESI--Eleanor, 86, beloved wife of the late Joseph Hevesi, died peacefully at her home in Harrison NY, January 30. She had worked both at Memorial Sloan-Kettering of Westchester and at SUNY in Purchase NY, where she won the Chancellor's Award for Excellence in Professional Service in 1979. She is survived by her loving daughters, Joelle Sander of NYC and Carla Tartaglia of Redding CT, their husbands Dr. Fred Sander and Peter Tartaglia, her beloved grandchildren Stephen Sander of NYC and Weston CT, Jason Sander and Matthew Tartaglia of NYC and Nicole Tartaglia of Danbury, CT and her cherished great-granddaughter Elizabeth Chrissa Sander. We will miss her elegance, her wit, her keen intelligence and her abiding passion for her family. A memorial gathering will be planned for the spring. Donations in her memory may be made to Hospice and Palliative Care of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601.
