
        RODOLITZ-Abraham. Beloved husband of almost 57 years to Anna. Father of Eileen, Gary, Jerry, Bruce and Scott. Fatherin-law to their respective spouses, and loving grandfather to ten grandchildren. Died suddenly on January 20. Immeasureably loved and admired by family and friends. He will be sorely missed. Funeral services conducted from ''BoulevardRiverside Chapels'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. on Friday, January 23 rd. at 9:30 A.M. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale. Family will observe shiva at the residence from Friday, January 23rd until Thursday evening January 29th.
