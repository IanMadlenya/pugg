
        CONNELLY - Eleanor Milburn. Died July 16, 1997 at 91. Survived by her husband, Albert Ray Connelly; two daughters, Mary Graff & Jean Mooney; 2 grandchildren, Alexandra and Clare Mooney, and a great-grandson Henry Nullet. Memorial service 11 AM, Monday, July 21 at St. James' Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71st Street, followed by burial at Mount Repose, Haverstraw, New York. Contributions may be made to the American Lung Association.
