
        JORDAN-June. This week June Jordan, lifelong activist, poet, writer and member of MADRE's founding Board of Directors, died. With her passing, the volume of passion in the world is diminished. In June's memory, we rededicate our own passion to building the world we dream. Staff and Board of MADRE
        JORDAN - June. Writers, teachers, and students from Teachers & Writers Collaborative (T&W) and Youth Speaks-NY mourn the loss of June Jordan-poet, activist, and educator. Her work at T&W in our early years set the tone for our continuing efforts to recognize, encourage, and nurture the ''voice of the children.''
        JORDAN-June. We celebrate the extraordinary life of our friend, and will remember always a special New Year's Eve. Your words, spirit and friendship will be with us forever. Micki and Fran
