
        HYMAN--Elaine Lubart. Beloved wife of Julian for 56 years. Mother and best friend to her children Steve, Mona and Harvey, their spouses Barbara, Michael and Lael, seven loving grandchildren, Sara, Ben, Emily, Julia, Charlie, Simone and Elliott. A sculptress, tennis player, gardener and art patron. She shaped and enhanced the lives of so many. We will all miss your advice, inspiration and love. Services Monday, 1 PM, Temple Emeth, 1666 Windsor Rd., Teaneck, NJ. For information, ''Gutterman and Musicant,'' 800-522-0588.
