
        ANTELL-Helene. Died on April 25 of complications from Alzheimer's Disease. Survived by her children Elinore and Ken, sisters Dorothy and Lillian, grandson Lee, stepgrandchildren Shawn and Brittany, nieces and nephews. A devoted wife to the late Louis and loving mother to the late Philip. Taken care of during her long struggle by three compassionate women: Sylvia, Jeannette and Blossom. A class act. She was a kind, wonderful and warm woman who was loved by all who knew her. May she rest in peace. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Caring Community Respite Program, 20 Washington Square North, New York, New York 10011.
