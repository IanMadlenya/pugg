
        EHRMAN--Elinor. 83, died Saturday, September 10, in Westport, CT. She had a long, distinguished career in marketing and public relations. Her first job after college was staff aide to the Manhattan Project. After the war, Ms. Ehrman was hired by ''Time'' magazine and prestigious public relations agencies including Burson Marsteller as Executive Vice President. Ms. Ehrman resided in Westport, CT, and Hancock, ME, and served as trustee on a number of boards including her alma mater, The College of Wooster, and Sloan-Kettering Hospital. She is survived by godchildren, Gail, Bradley and William Stevenson, Laurie Kelly and several cousins.
