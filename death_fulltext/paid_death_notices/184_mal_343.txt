
        HOLLENBACH-Frank J. On May 28, age 85, predeceased by his beloved wife, Eleanor. Survived by his loving children, Doris, Paul, John, Kenneth, and Catherine. Also survived by five cherished grandchildren and his dear sister, Mary, and brother, Bob. Frank was a decorated veteran of WW II. Visiting at the Oyster Bay Funeral Home, 261 South St., Oyster Bay, Long Island 11771 on Thursday, 7-9 PM, and Friday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. There will be a service at the First Presbyterian Church, Saturday at 10 AM. A private interment in Pennsylvania will be held at later date. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Cumberland College, 6191 College Station Drive, Williamsburg, KY. 40769-1372.
