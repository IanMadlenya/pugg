
        GAYNES-Janet Gillies. Of North White Plains, NY, on July 29, 2002. Wife of the late Erwin S. Gaynes. Dear mother of Judy Sebastian and the late Barry Gaynes. Grandmother of Michael Sullivan, Adam and Scott Sebastian. Great grandmother of Michael. Her lifelong career was in the fashion industry. For over 20 years she worked for Anne Klein. She was an original model for Robert Powers. Funeral service 12 noon Wednesday, July 31, at the Ballard - Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY. Interment Kensico Cemetery. The family will receive friends at the funeral home Wednesday, 11:30 AM until 12 noon. Memorial contributions may be sent to Alzheimer's Society for Research.
