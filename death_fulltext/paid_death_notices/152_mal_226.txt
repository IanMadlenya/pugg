
        DUNN-Francis ''Frank'' Joseph, of Topsail Beach, NC died Saturday, September 18, 1999 at his residence. Mr. Dunn was born on April 26, 1939 in Brooklyn, NY, the son of the late Deputy Inspector Frank Dunn, NYPD, and Bernadette Kearns Dunn. He was an Institutional Stock Broker in New York City for twenty-five years. He served in the Army as a 1st Lieutenant with the 106th infantry. He was a devout Catholic and was a member of the St. Mary Gate of Heaven Catholic Church. He graduated from St. John's Prep in Brooklyn, NY and went on to graduate from Villanova University. He was a member of the Downtown Athletic Club, NYC and Richmond County Country Club, Staten Island, NY. Mr. Dunn was an avid horseman and swimmer, loved Maritime History and had a great sense of humor. He was a truly good human being, adoring father and stepfather and ''the best husband in the world''. Surviving are his wife of eighteen years, Patricia McGinnis Dunn of the home; two daughters, Deirdre Dunn Haddad of Staten Island, NY and Alana Dunn of Newton, MA; two stepdaughters, Alicia Slook of San Diego, CA., and Jennifer Dames of Atlanta, GA; two step-sons, Peter Dames of Mimbres, N.M., and Mark Dames of Atlanta, GA; seven grandchildren; and one brother, William T. Dunn of Rumson, NJ. Memorial Mass will be celebrated at 10:00 A.M. Wednesday, September 22, 1999, at St. Mary Gate of Heaven Catholic Church, Surf City, with the Father Terrence Collins officiating. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to Emma Anderson Memorial Chapel, 1100 S. Anderson Blvd., Topsail Island, NC 28445 or Pender East Rescue Squad, 16660 HWY 17, Hampstead, NC 28443 or St. Mary Gate of Heaven, 420 N. Topsail Drive, Topsail Island, N.C. 28445.
