
        FAIR-Mary Katherine. Of Exeter, NH, on January 7th, formerly of Shushan, NY, and New York, NY. Beloved mother of the late Charles M. Fair III, Ellen B. Fair of New York, NY, Katherine H. Fair of Exeter, NH. Also survived by former husband, Charles M. Fair, Jr. Memorial service to be held June 15th in Cambridge, NY. Advertising copywriter and copy chief for Macy's and Bonwit Teller, New York, NY, until her retirement in 1990. Donations may be made in her memory to Seacoast Hospice, 10 Hampton Road, Exeter, NH 03833 or to The Monks of New Skete, Cambridge, NY.
