
        JOHNSON-Allan R. November 26, 1998. Beloved husband Maria, devoted father of Jane Fell, Kathy Jones, Susan Johnson & Barbara Hobin, cherished grandfather of seven and great-grandfather of four, loving brother of Patrick Johnson, cherished Godfather of Steven Toia. With Saks Fifth Avenue for thirty-six years, ten of which he spent as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, he retired in 1981 as Chairman of the store's parent company BATUS, Inc. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Tuesday, December 1, 1998, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Religious service Wednesday, December 2, St. John the Evangelist Church, 55 Street and First Ave. In lieu of flowers contributions in his name may be made to The Boys Scouts of America.
        JOHNSON-Allan. The Board of Directors and Shareholders are saddened by the passing of Allan Johnson, past President of 2 Sutton Place South. We extend our deepest sympathy to Maria and his entire family. Phyllis Luxemburg, President
