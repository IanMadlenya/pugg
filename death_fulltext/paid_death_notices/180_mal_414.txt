
        JOY-Thomas G. A Miami and New York resident died January 27, 2002. He retired from International Flavors and Fragrance as Senior Vice-President. Past member of the Regional Whist Club of NY. Thomas was on the Board of Adopt-a-Pet. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated 10:30 AM Thursday at St. Hugh Catholic Church (3460 Royal Road, Coconut Grove, FL). Friends may call from 6-8 PM Wednesday at Stanfill Funeral Home. In lieu of flowers, donation in Thomas's memory to Adopt-a-Pet (11900 SW 232 Street, Miami, FL. 33170). For more information contact Stanfill Funeral Home, 10545 South Dixie Highway, Miami, FL. 305-667-2518.
