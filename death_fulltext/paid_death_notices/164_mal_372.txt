
        MILLER-The Reverend Edward Oehler. Of Castine, Maine. On September 16th, 2000, at age 84. Distinguished Rector of St. George's Episcopal Church in NYC from 1946 to 1975. Survived by his wife, Ann Lackman Miller, his daughter, Helen H. Miller of NYC his sons, Dr. David H. Miller and Henry L. Miller, both of NYC, and the Rev. Edward O. Miller, Jr. of McLean, VA; six grandchildren, and a sister, Martha M. Burt of Marquette, MI. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Bagaduce Ambulance Corps, P.O. Box 522, Castine, ME. 04421.
