
        LOWE-Violet Lucille Smith. Beloved sister of Shirley Cooke and Hortense Thompson. Passed away October 19, 1997. She was a graduate of Hunter College, a Girl Scout leader, choir member, member of the Lions, St. Fathaith Guild, Cursillo and the Episcopal Church Women. She was a former health inspector supervisor for the New York City Department of Health. Services at the Church of St. James the Less, 107-61 166th St, Jamaica, NY 11433, Sunday, October 26, 1997, at 7 PM.
