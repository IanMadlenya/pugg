
        HIRSCHHORN--David. The Jerusalem Foundation mourns the passing of David Hirschhorn, a devoted friend and benefactor of Jerusalem whose spirit will live on in the many projects he helped to bring to life as the President of The Jacob and Hilda Blaustein Foundation. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Teddy Kollek, Founding Chair Dan Meridor, International Chair Alan G. Hassenfeld, Chairman, USA Ruth Cheshin, International President
