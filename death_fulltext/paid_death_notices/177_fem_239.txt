
        LOEW-Diane. Died October 17, 2001 in Las Vegas, NV. after a quiet, courageous battle with cancer. Age 52. Native of Reading, PA., received M.A. in English from Columbia University and Law Degree at N.Y. Law School, passed N.Y. Bar in 1990. Served as administrative assistant at Milbank, Tweed for five years. A consumate professional who tempered her keen intelligence with compassion. A lover of literature, the arts, and devoted to animals. Leaves behind her beloved partner of nine years, M. Zane Stemple, father Frank, brother Bill and many loving friends.
