
        GILMAN-Dr. Arnold Robert. Died March 28, in Hume, VA, after a long illness. A Major in the Marine Air Corps with two sets of missions in the Pacific in WWII. A veterinarian who practiced in New Jersey and Virginia. Survived by his a wife Judy, two daughters Julie and Andrea, three grandchildren Kitson, Stockard and Lauren, and a brother Dr. Manuel Gilman.
