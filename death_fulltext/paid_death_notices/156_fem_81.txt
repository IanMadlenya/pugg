
        MAX-Muriel. On January 7, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Harry. Loving mother of Alison and Lawrence. Cherished daughter of Sarah Bonnett. Dear sister of Stewart & Sally Bonnett. Devoted aunt of Laura & Jennifer Bonnett. She will be deeply missed. Service Sun. 10:45am at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in her memory may be made to the T.J. Martell Foundation.
        MAX-Muriel. The Officers and Board of Directors of the T. J. Martell Foundation for Leukemia, Cancer, and AIDS Research mourn the passing of its Executive Director, Muriel Max, who served our Foundation with distinction for 23 years. Our heartfelt sympathies go out to Muriel's children, Alison, Lawrence, and their entire family. Muriel will always be rememberd with fondness for her devotion to our Foundation and the many people who were touched by her. Frances W. Preston, President Tony Martell, Chairman Floyd Glinert, Chairman Emeritus
