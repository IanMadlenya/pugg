
        ROCHE-Thomas Kirby, of Muttontown, NY, on February 12, 1997, at the age of 79. Survived by his wife Beverly, as well as by Gary Knapp, son John, daughters Caroline dela Bretesche, Claire Ferreyrulles & Barbara Makowski. Sisters Mrs. Earl Smith and Mrs. Billy Bryant, and 10 grandchildren. Combat infantry officer in WW II, wounded, awarded Silver Star, Cruix de Guerre and Bronze Star & the Purple Heart for actions in combat. Thereafter engaged in Washington and New York in International Matters and litigation. Burial will be in Arlington National Cemetery. Memorial Service at 11 A.M., Monday, Feb. 17, Christ Church, Oyster Bay. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to a charity of your choice. For further information contact the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home Inc., 293 South St, Oyster Bay (516) 922-6700
