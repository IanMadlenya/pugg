
        REFF-Benjamin F., M.D. Feb. 2, 1998 at the age of 90. Devoted and beloved father of Albert A. Reff and Hariette Wittenberg Saturn, grandfather of Joshua Wittenberg and Jacqueline Saturn. A maverick and compassionate physician who chartered his own course and whose lust for life was an example to his family, friends and patients. Donations in his memory to the United Jewish Appeal.
