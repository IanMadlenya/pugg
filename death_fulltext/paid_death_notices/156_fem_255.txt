
        YARFITZ-Evelyn. Died January 24, 2000 in Boynton Beach, Florida. Beloved wife of Leo. Loving mother of Joan and Stuart Yarfitz and Denise Pierre. Dear motherin-law of Trudi Fajans and Marc Pierre. Treasured grandmother of Miriam Yarfitz, Daniel Fajans, Evan, Jenna and Christoph Pierre. Adored sister of Esther Block and Ralph Littman. Cherished friend of Roslyn Carlin. Evelyn was a wonderful woman in every respect; kind, honest, generous & loyal, beautiful inside and out. All who knew her were touched by her dignity and gracious presence. She will always be in our hearts.
