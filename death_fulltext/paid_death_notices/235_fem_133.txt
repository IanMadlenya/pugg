
        JEFFREY--Janice Carol died August 17 after a 21/2 year struggle with leukemia. Janice was born March 20, 1938 in Stanley, ND. Raised on a ranch she rode her pony to her one-room schoolhouse. She graduated with a degree in Religious Education from Jamestown College. She worked as a teacher until becoming a mother. After her children were grown, Janice obtained a Masters Degree from Columbia School of Social Work. She practiced many years in Park Slope with parent-child groups, families, and children. She is survived by her husband William, her sons Scott and Randal, and her beloved granddaughter Rowan Serena. Also surviving are her mother Harriet, brother Paul, and sister Bonnie. She will be greatly missed by her clients, friends, and family.
