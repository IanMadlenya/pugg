
        McCALL-Howard Weaver, Jr., of Darien, CT, died at his home on February 24, 1997. Born in Chattanooga in 1907 and graduate of the University of Virginia, he retired as President of the Chemical Bank in 1972. He is survived by his wife, Jane, three children, Nancy Watson, Howard, III, Jane Politi, and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Noroton Presbyterian Chapel, 2011 Post Rd, Darien, Fri, Feb 28, 1997, 2:30 pm. Donations may be made in his memory to The American Parkinson's Association, 1250 Hylan Blvd, Staten Island, NY 10305-1946, or Mid-Fairfield Hospice, 112 Main St, Norwalk, CT 06851.
        McCALL-Howard, Jr. We at NYU Medical Center mourn the loss of a dear friend and devoted Life Trustee & former Vice Chairman of our Board of Trustees, Howard McCall, Jr. He was an outstanding and highly respected trustee who was a remarkable individual. He was dedicated to pursue excellence in medical education, research and patient care. Always a gentleman, he convinced others by wise persuasion. His wisdom was recognized by all his colleagues and friends. His humor was unique & always contributed to the analysis of a critical situation. We will miss him. We extend our sympathy to his spouse Jane, his daughters: Jane Politi and Nancy Watson, his son, Howard III, and his four grandchildren. Laurence A. Tisch, Chairman, NYU Board of Trustees L.
