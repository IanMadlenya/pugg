
        MULLEN-Rev. Michael F., C.M. Died at Saint Catherine's Infirmary, Philadelphia, PA, on June 13, 2002. Ordained 1941 as a member of the Vincentian Community, Father Mullen served various positions at Saint John's University, Jamaica, NY, since 1947. He is survived by his sisters Rita Holston, Mary Richards and Catherine Pero, all of Woburn, MA. Viewing will be held at the Community Chapel of the Vincentian Residence at Saint John's University, 8000 Utopia Pkwy., Jamaica, NY, Monday, June 17, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10:30 AM, Our Lady of Lourdes Chapel, Saint John's University. Interment Saint Joseph's Seminary, Princeton, NJ.
