
        BARNICK-Adam. Taken prematurely at the age of 37 on May 7, 2000. Beloved husband of Pat, devoted father of Loryn and Nicole, adored son of Geraldine and Jack Barnick. Cherished brother of Robyn Ornstein and her husband, David, and Lisa Walsh and her husband, Edward. Also survived by adoring nephews Lenny and Eric, niece Katie and treasured family and close friends too numerous to mention. His love of life will live on in the hearts of all those he touched. He will be sorely missed.
