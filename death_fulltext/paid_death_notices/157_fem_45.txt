
        MORAN-Helen Gary, 93, of Manhattan, on February 1. Daughter of Hampson Gary, Ambassador to Egypt and Switzerland under President Wilson. Widow of industrialist Bernard A. Moran. Helen is survived by her sons Bernard Moran Jr., John P. Moran and Michael Moran. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave., Friday, 6-8PM. Funeral service Saturday 1PM, St. Thomas Episcopal.
