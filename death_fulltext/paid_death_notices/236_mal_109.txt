
        JONES--James M. The Browning School community notes with sadness the death of Mr. Jones, admired and respected Lower and Middle School science teacher from 1993 to 2005. He died of cancer in Fort Lauderdale, Fl. on September 3. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. At the family's request, contributions in his name may be made to: The James Jones Fund, The Browning School, 52 E. 62 St., New York, NY 10021. Stephen M. Clement, III Headmaster James S. Chanos, President Board of Trustees Sharon L. Jacob, President Parents Association
