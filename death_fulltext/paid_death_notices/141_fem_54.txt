
        GUILDEN-Tamara. On October 5, 1998. Wife of the late Ira. Mother of Pat and Richard Adler. Grandmother of Jessica Bartlett, Melanie Adler, Joanna Adler and Great-grandmother of Molly Bartlett. Services and interment private. Mrs. Guilden was an active patron of the arts including The Metropolitan Opera, The New York Philharmonic, The Sante Fe Opera, Paris Opera and Ballet, The National Actor's Theatre, The Roundabout Theatre and The Artists' Colony YADDO. She was also on the Board of Directors of the Foreign Policy Association and the National Committee for American Foreign Policy.
