
        SCHRAML - Richard. Of Smithtown, on January 13, 2006, in his 84th year. Retired Suffolk Community College Professor, a devoted family man, decorated WWII veteran and staunch supporter of environmental and conservation causes. He is survived by his wife Ann, his children Lisa and Mark, his daughter-inlaw Leila, son-in-law Jack and grandchildren Julien and Siena. Reposing at Moloney's Hauppauge Funeral Home, 840 Wheeler Road, Hauppauge, where services will be held Tuesday evening. Cremation Wednesday, 11 am, Nassau Suffolk Crematory, Lake Ronkonkoma, NY. Visiting Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. In lieu of flowers make donations to Good Shepherd Hospice or Cancer Care of Long Island.
