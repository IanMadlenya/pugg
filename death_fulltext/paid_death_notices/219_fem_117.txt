
        ADLER--Bertha. On April 9, 2005. A 1928 graduate of Hunter College, she was an NYC elementary school teacher and an active teachers union member for more than 30 years. Wife of the late Harry Adler. Mother of Ann Greenberg and the late Joel Adler. Mother - in - law of Frank Greenberg. Grandmother of David and Christopher Noll. Great-grandmother of Harrison and Alia Noll. Interment Tuesday, 11 am, Washington Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
