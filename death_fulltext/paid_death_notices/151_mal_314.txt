
        FEIDEN-Joseph. Beloved husband of the late Jewel Eliasberg Feiden. Cherished father of Margo Feiden, owner of the Margo Feiden Galleries Ltd. Died August 26, 1999 at the home of his daughter after a long illness. Brother of the late Raymond Feiden, Martin Feiden and Sally Baker. Loving father-in-law of the late Julius Cohen. He is also survived by his adored grandchildren Bambi and Jeremy, brother The Hon. Murray T. Feiden, former Brooklyn Supreme Court Judge, and loving nephews and nieces. Funeral Service will be held on Friday, 2PM at Knollwood Park Cemetery. The family wishes to thank Dr. Mark Lieb, Vena Lewis, and the Cabrini Hospice.
