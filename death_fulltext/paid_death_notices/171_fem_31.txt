
        SCHMUCKLER-Miriam, 89. Cherished wife of the late Samuel Schmuckler. Beloved mother to Judith Paradis and Alan Sears. Mrs. Schmuckler was known for her active public service and teaching career in the New Rochelle public schools. Her intelligence, determination and caring will be remembered. Donations may be sent to Hadassah. Services George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle, today, 1 PM.
