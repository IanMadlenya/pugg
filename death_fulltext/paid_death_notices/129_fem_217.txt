
        BIENENFELD-Tessie. October 24, 1997. Loving wife of the late Julius Bienenfeld. Adored mother of Stanley and Evelyn, Audrey and Max Wagner, Marilyn and Jack Roth. Devoted sister of Mildred Needle of Del Ray Beach, Florida. A gracious lady devoted to her children, her 11 grandchildren, 37 great grandchildren and her great great grandson. She will be missed by her nieces and nephews, her extended family, friends, the Jewish organizations she so generously supported and the State of Israel she loved so dearly.
