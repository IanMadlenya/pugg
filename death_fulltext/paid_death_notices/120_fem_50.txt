
        AUBRY - Leticia Costa Aubry, died peacefully on January 4, 1997, beloved wife of Stephen Aubry for 56 years, loving mother of Alan and Christian and devoted grandmother of Vanessa and Olivia. The funeral look place in St.-Prex in the presence of close family members. A mass will be celebrated in Paris, where she was born in 1912, at the Abbaye Sainte-Marie, 3, Rue de la Source, Paris 16, on January 23 at 11:45 A.M.  8, rue Forel  St-Prex, Vaud  Switzerland
