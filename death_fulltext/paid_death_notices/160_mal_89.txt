
        CHRISTIAN-John W., Jr., died Sunday, May 7th, 2000. Retired President of B. Altman's of NYC and former Executive Vice President of Thalhimer's Department Store in Richmond, VA. He is survived by his wife, Polly K. Christian, one son and daughter-in-law, John W., III and Sandra H. Christian, all of Richmond, one daughter and son-in law, Anne C. and Edward P. Collins of St. Louis, MO, one sister, Mary Ann C. Rogers of Durham, NC, four grandchildren, John W. Christian, IV, Katherine A. Christian, Kristin C. Collins and Katherine M. Collins. Memorial services will be conducted at St. Stephen's Episcopal Church in Richmond, 11 AM, Wednesday. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Stephen's Episcopal Church in lieu of flowers.
