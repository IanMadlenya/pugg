
        GINSBERG-Estelle Rae Sklower. Widow of Samuel Ginsberg, died Thursday at Beth Israel Hospital, Passaic, at the age of 104. Beloved mother of Miles David and Dulcie Freeman; grandmother of Steven, Amelia and Heidi David, and Sheila Freeman Fung, Eve Freeman Kogan, Bill Freeman and Joel Freeman; great grandmother of Julia, Lesley and Lea Fung, Rebecca and Josh Kogan, Alan and Emily Freeman, Samantha Freeman; she was ''Aunt Rae'' to scores of nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grand-nephews. Funeral services on Sunday, October 26, 10 AM, Robert Schoem Menorah Chapel, Rte. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
