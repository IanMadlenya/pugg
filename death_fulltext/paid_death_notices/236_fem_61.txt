
        MONTESANO--Irma. Of White Plains, passed away peacefully on September 8th, 2006, at her home. Irma Pierro was born in the Bronx on November 12th, 1916, and was married to the late Nicholas Montesano of White Plains. Irma taught in the White Plains public school system from 1962 to 1982. She is survived by her loving sons Roger and Neil.
