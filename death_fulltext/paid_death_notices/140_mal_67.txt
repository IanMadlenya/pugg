
        WILLIAMS-C. Dickerman. Died peacefully in his 98th year, August 12, 1998 in Hamden, Connecticut. Devoted husband of the late Virginia Fain Williams, and father of Joan Williams Farr of NYC, Honor Williams Ishida of Tokyo, and Stephen F. Williams of Washington, D.C. Memorial service at 11 A.M., October 2, Church of the Redeemer, 185 Cold Spring Street (corner of Cold Spring & Whitney Avenue), New Haven, CT. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Yale Law School.
