
        KESSLER-Martha Myiofis. Devoted wife of the late Irving. Cherished mother of Sue Feld and Margaret Danson. Dear motherin-law of Stuart Feld and Stephen Danson. Adored grandmother of Elizabeth, Peter, Christopher, and Melissa. Mourned by her sister Celia Gordon, niece and nephew, grand-niece, grand-nephew and friends. A loving, gracious, ever generous, old-fashioned, true lady. Irreplaceable to all of us who were blessed enough to have known her. Funeral services will be private. The family will be receiving on Monday, February 25, 2002, at the home of Sue and Stuart Feld, New York City, from 6 pm to 9 pm.
