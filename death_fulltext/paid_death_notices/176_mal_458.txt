
        LEWIS-Murray. Age 93. Formerly of NYC. Died September 22, 2001 Lifelong educator in NYC schools. Humanitarian and fighter for peace, for justice, and for a healthy environment. Conqueror of cancer and McCarthyism. Retired in 1973 and moved to Lenox in 1998. Survived by his wife of 71 years: Gertrude Lewis, two sons, six grandchildren, two greatgrandchildren with two more due within two months. Shalom.
