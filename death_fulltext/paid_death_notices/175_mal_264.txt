
        ROSEN-David. 87, of Lauderhill, FL, formerly of New York City. Passed away on August 24, 2001. He is survived by beloved daughter Gale Goldfarb, daughter-in-law Sally Rosen and his closest companion Arlene Shear, his three grand-children David Rosen, Nancy Goldfarb Watson and Jay Goldfarb, his two great-grandchildren Hannah and Alex Watson. He will now be joining his beloved late wife Marion and his late son Steven. In lieu of flowers, please make your donations to the National Alzheimers Association or the Muscular Dystrophy Association. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, New York, NY on Monday, August 27 at 1:15 PM.
