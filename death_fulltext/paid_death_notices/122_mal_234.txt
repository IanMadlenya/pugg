
        CONLIN-Rev. Leo E., C.S.P., on March 19, 1997, at age 81. A native of Worcester, MA., he was ordained a Paulist priest on May 11, 1946. Fr. Conlin served six years in campus ministry in TX. and MA., and 14 years in mission work in South Africa and New England. The better part of his lifetime was caring for prisoners through detention ministry in New England and San Francisco. A wake will be held at Old St. Mary's Cathedral on Friday, March 21, 1997 from 3 to 8 P.M. Mass will be at 10 A.M., on March 22, 1997, followed by interment at Holy Cross Cemetery, San Francisco.
