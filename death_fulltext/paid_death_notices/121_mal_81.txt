
        JAKUBIEL-Scott, age 35 of East Lyme, CT., and Manhattan, died Jan. 27, 1997 in Tampa, FL., at the H. Lee Moffit Cancer Center. He was a graduate of Pratt Institute & was a popular Manhattan party designer and florist. Mr. Jakubiel was preceded in death by his mother, Arline Wye, and sister, Sandra Jakubiel, both of Connecticut. He is survived by his father, Robert Jakubiel of East Lyme, CT. A memorial service will be held at a future date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, NYC 10021.
