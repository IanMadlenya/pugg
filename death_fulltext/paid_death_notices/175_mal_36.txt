
        STRAUSS-Bernard I. Age 93. Died Thursday, August 2, at home in Suffern, NY, with his family at his side. Formerly of New Rochelle, NY, his law practice, based in Manhattan, was established in the 1930's. An accomplished bridge player, he earned the rank of Life Master. Survived by his adored wife of 60 years, Rosalyn B. Strauss; daughter and son-in-law, Jill and Sidney Pryor of New City, NY; and grandson, Stefan Pryor of NYC. His beloved son, Jerrold S. Strauss predeceased him. Donations may be made to Ethical Culture Fieldston School, Annual Fund (in memory of Bernard I. Strauss, Class of 1926), 33 Central Park West, New York, NY 10023.
