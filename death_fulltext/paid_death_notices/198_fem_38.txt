
        RAHMAN-Shaista. 72 on July 1, 2003. Beloved wife of Asad ur Rahman. Mother of Zeyba, Azza and Aziz. Mother-in-law of Terrance Grace. Warm friend to the many who benefited from her wise and generous advice, her knowledgeable and witty conversation, sustained with her wonderful charm even in the time of her tribulation. Professor Emerita of Educational Services at Brooklyn College, where she taught English literature specializing in the work of Virginia Woolf. With a wide range of interests, Shaista had recently completed a book on the pioneering American woman mountain climber Annie Peck. Shaista was a lover of flowers, animals and people, a strong supporter of Doctors Without Borders and The World Wildlife Fund. Contributions to these organizations may be made in her memory.
