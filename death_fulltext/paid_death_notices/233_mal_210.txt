Paul Klotman, M.D. Chairman $(6$)GRIBETZ--Allen, M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the untimely passing of a consummate physician, colleague, teacher, and friend. Dr. Gribetz began his training at Mount Sinai in 1971 and devoted more than 35 years of service to the institution, specializing in internal medicine and pulmonary disease. He was knowledgeable, sensitive and compassionate. He was more a listener than a talker. His commitment to his patients and to the hospital will continue in the many lives he touched and through the Gribetz family legacy at Mount Sinai. He will be remembered for his outstanding clinical skills and especially for his kindness as a human being. To his adoring and adored family, we extend our sincere condolences.
