
        LUKACH-Marion Long. Died on Sept. 24th, 1998 at the of age 98. Wife of the late Arthur S. Lukach. Mother of Ellin L. Perlmutter and Arthur S. Lukach Jr. and the late Ann L. Thron. Sister of the late Louis Long Jr. and the late John E. Long. Cherished grandmother of 8 and great grandmother of four. She was a devoted volunteer and Secretary of the Board of Directors of The Visiting Nurse Service of New York, member of the Sisterhood of Central Synagogue and active in Interfaith Neighbors. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 East 70th Street, New York City, 10021. For funeral information call Frank E. Campbell at 212-288-3500.
