
        OPPENHEIMER--Edgar D. Jr. Passed away peacefully at 80 at his home in St. Petersburg, Florida, on December 5, 2005. He leaves his beloved wife of 54 years, Virginia Layton, his brother David E. Oppenheimer, his four children, Frances Oppenheimer, William L. and Barbara Oppenheimer, Carol Oppenheimer and Steve Zagerman, and Jill Oppenheimer and Alain Carriere. He also leaves ten grandchildren: Jesse, Arti, and Peter Boyd and Matthew Weigert; Michael and Claire Oppenheimer; Sophie and Willa Zagerman; and Olivier and Jean-Francois Carriere. He was preceded in death by his twin brother Frank. His parents were Dr. Edgar D. & Carolyn Weil Oppenheimer. Edgar served as a Naval Officer during World War II and remained an avid sailor all his life. He was a mechanical engineer and an expert in hightemperature welding. The family will hold a memorial service at the Academy of Senior Professionals at Eckert College (ASPEC) in St. Petersburg, Florida, on January 14, 2006. Contributions in memory should be made to the Hospice of the Florida Suncoast or the Florida Chapter of the ALS Foundation.
