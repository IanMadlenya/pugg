
        CONNOR-Ralph. Age 87. Died peacefully August 29 in Seattle, WA., surrounded by his family. He made a lasting contribution to the community of Dutchess County, N.Y., as a friend and generous supporter of Oakwood Friends School, Vassar College, Vassar Brothers Hospital, Marist College and the Town of Union Vale. At the time of his death he was an active member and Trustee of the Board of Managers of Oakwood Friends School and Member of Bulls Head Friends Meeting. He is deeply missed and lovingly remembered by Curt and Lily Beck, John and Elizabeth Daniels, Junius and Lisa Ellis, and Justin and Deborah Scheer.
