
        NACK-Saul H. American Friends of Yeshivot Bnei Akiva records with deep sorrow the passing of the beloved husband of Frieda, father of Daniel. Mr. Nack was a lifelong communal leader and activist for Jewish causes in the United States and Israel. As President of B'nai Israel Jewish Centre, he was responsible for major support of the Yeshivot Bnei Akiva network, primarily our Ulpana Tzfira. May his many deeds of chesed and tzedakah serve as sources of inspiration to his family and many friends. Our deepest condolences to his loving wife, son and grandchildren. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Services today at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, 12 noon. Marvin Bienenfeld, Chairman Joel Schreiber, Vice Chairman Dr. Irwin Kanarek, Co-President Alan Wildes, Co-President Rabbi David Warshaw, Exec. Dir.
        NACK-Saul H. Died April 9, 2001, Fort Lauderdale, FL. Beloved husband of Frieda. Loving father of Daniel and the late Dr. Emanuel Nack. President of the Bnai Israel Jewish Center Philanthropic Association, Inc. Distinguished Jurist, retired Law Assistant to the Supreme Court, Kings County, honored supporter of the Community of Even Shmuel and of Yeshiva Har Etzion Israel. Services Wednesday, May 10, 2001, 12 Noon, Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, N.Y. We mourn his loss. Bnai Israel Philanthropic Association, Inc. Rabbi Noah H. Rosenbloom, Chaplain Sidney J. Goldner, Vice President
        NACK-Saul H. Ulpana Bnei Akiva Tzfira records with deep sorrow the passing of our dear friend and long-time supporter, Mr. Saul H. Nack. As President of B'nai Israel Jewish Centre, he was responsible for major support of our scholarship fund and other special projects. We extend our deepest condolences to his beloved wife, Frieda, and son Daniel. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Moshe Klein, Rosh Ulpana Shmuel Kuperman, Director
