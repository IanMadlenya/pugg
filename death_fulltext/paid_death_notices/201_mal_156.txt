
        BAPIS--George M. On October 16, 2003, in New York City. Beloved husband of Helene and father of twin daughters Michaela and Maria. Brother of Nick (Elaine) and Jeannie (Peter) duPont. Uncle of Laura and Andrew duPont, and Michael (Eleni) and Alethia Bapis. Son of Michael and Maxine Bapis. Employed as Senior Vice President of Investments at Morgan Stanley, served as Treasurer and chaired the investment committee of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, and as Treasurer and President of the Board of Trustees of the American College of Greece. Visiting Monday, October 20, from 6 to 8 PM, at the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74th St, New York, NY 10021. Funeral services Tuesday, October 21, at 11 AM, at the Cathedral. Interment to follow at the Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity.
        BAPIS--George M. The Dean, Dr. Robert G. Stephanopoulos, and the Board of Trustees of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity in NYC, express their condolences to the family of George M. Bapis, former Trustee, Treasurer and devoted member of many years of the Cathedral. His loss is deeply felt and we shall cherish the many fond memories we have of him.
