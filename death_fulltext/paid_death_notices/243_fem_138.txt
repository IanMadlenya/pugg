
        FOX--Eileen, on April 23rd, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Francis A. Fox. She was a devoted worker and volunteer for the Roman Catholic Church and was affectionately known as the ''Mayor'' of Peter Cooper Village and Stuyvesant Town community. She will be missed by her relatives and her many dear friends. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home 353 Second Avenue (at 20th Street), NYC Wednesday 25pm and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday 10am at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment private.
