
        CARR-Thomas W., 84. Long time resident of Port Washington, NY died on January 5th in Rockville, MD. His publishing and advertising career was with Computer Decisions, Electronic Design, 50 Plus Magazines and AdWeek's Marketer's Guide to Media. He is survived by his wife, Agnes, son John and fiance, Louise, son James and wife Robin, as well as brother, Peter, from California. Services will be January 11th, at 9:30 A.M., St. Peter of Alcantara, Port Washington.
