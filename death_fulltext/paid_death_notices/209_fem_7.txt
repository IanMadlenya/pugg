
        LeVINE-LEVITAN--Zelda K. On May 30, 2004. New York City and previously St. Thomas, Virgin Islands. Beloved wife of Milton Levitan and the late Herbert S. LeVine. Loving mother of Andrea, Nancy and John and motherin-law of Lee and Marlene. Proud grandmother of Adam, David and Abby. Sister of Charlotte and Claire. Stepmother of Dan, Richard, and Robert Levitan. Her joie de vivre will be greatly missed by her family and dear friends. A memorial gathering will be held on June 4 at 10:30AM at the Harmonie Club, 4 East 60th St. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Jacob Perlow Hospice or the Cancer Center, both at Beth Israel Medical Center, 16th St at 1st Ave, New York, NY 10003.
