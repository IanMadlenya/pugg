
        BERAKHA-Esther (nee Rossi). Of Manhattan, died on December 29, 1999 just before her 89th birthday. Born in Buenos Aires. Loving wife of the late David Berakha. Mother of George Beraka, M.D., & the late Vicky Berakha. Mother-inlaw of Judy Beraka. Grandmother of Scott, David and Michael. May she rest in peace.
