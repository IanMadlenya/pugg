
        COOPER--Selwyn, 91, of Searingtown, N.Y., passed away in Boca Raton, FL. January 6, 2007. He was a genius at problem solving and at trivia and loved by all. During W.W. II he was a Naval Officer in charge of Radar on an Aircraft carrier. Survived by beloved wife, of 60 years, Hilda, loving children Andy (Kathy) Cooper, Beverly, MA., Beth Cooper Lawrence, of New York, N.Y. Amy (Robert) Zenn, Livingston, N.J., Sister, Florence Levy, Hasting-on-the-Hudson, N.Y. cherished grandchildren, Ruddick, Rachel, Ellen, Jane, Scott, many cousins, nieces and nephews and all who knew and loved him. Chapel Services Tuesday, January 9, 2007, at 12:30 pm. Riverside Nassau North, 55N. Station Plaza, Great Neck, N.Y. Information call 516-487-9600. Donations in his name to Hospice by the Sea, Boca Raton, Florida.
