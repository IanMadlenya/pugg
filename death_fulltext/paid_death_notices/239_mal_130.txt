
        ROSENBERG--Martin. Age 75. On Friday December 8th, 2006. Martin Rosenberg formally of Long Beach, New York. Beloved husband of Ilene Rosenberg. Devoted father of Michael Rosenberg of Jacksonville Florida, and Nicole Thomas of Olney Maryland. Loving grandfather of Kenneth and Samantha Rosenberg. Memorial services were held on Sunday December 10th at DanzanskyGoldberg Memorial Chapels Inc, Rockville Maryland. Family will be receiving friends Sunday through Tuesday at his late residence. Expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
