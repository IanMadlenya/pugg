
        LIEBMAN - Rebecca. Died January 29, 2003 in her 91st year. Lifelong resident of Woodmere, NY. Daughter of Fanny Saul Liebman and Morris Liebman. Beloved sister of the late Emily Greenwald Meyer and devoted aunt of the late Diana Greenwald and Alice Greenwald Ward and David Ward. Greataunt of Leda and Nat Ward. Former grade school teacher in Mineola and owner of The Uniform Shop, Woodmere. Contributions to UJA in lieu of flowers. Graveside service, 10:30 AM, February 2nd, Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, L.I.
