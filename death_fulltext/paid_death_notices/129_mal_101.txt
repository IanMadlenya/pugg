
        LANDOW-Mark H. On Sunday, September 28, 1997. Of New York, N.Y. Died at New York University Medical Center. He was the son of Rebecca and Leo Landow of Detroit, Michigan and Bronx, N.Y. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 18 at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 St, New York, N.Y. beginning at 1 PM. Memorial contributions may be made th the City College of New York Scholarship Fund.
