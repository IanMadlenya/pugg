
        REISS-Philip S. Died peacefully surrounded by his family on January 13, 2001. Beloved husband of Gloria. Loving father of Jane Reiss, Nancy Booke and Meg Reiss. Adored grandfather of Lena and Will Shefelman and Henry and Milo Booke. Dear brother of Barbara Hochfelder. Father-in-law of Nicholas Booke and Dan Shefelman. A memorial service is to be held on Monday, January 22, 2001, 10 AM, at All Souls Unitarian Church, located at 1157 Lexington Ave in NYC. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Churchill School-Philip S. Reiss Scholarship Fund, 22 East 95th St, NY, NY 10128.
        REISS-Philip S. Friends and associates from DDB Worldwide mourn the passing of Philip S. Reiss. His professionalism, expertise and guidance enhanced our lives, professionally and personally. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Gloria, his three daughters Jane, Nancy and Meg. May peace, strength and comfort be theirs in the days ahead.
