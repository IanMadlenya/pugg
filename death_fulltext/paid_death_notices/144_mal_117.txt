
        DACHS-Richard A. . Beloved son of Alan and Hilda. Brother to Carol and Enid. Brother-in-law to Robert. Nephew to Mary, Sally and Julie. Passed away suddenly Wednesday, January 6, 1999. His untimely passing left a vacant space in all our hearts. He will be sorely missed. He was a Deputy General Counsel to the New York City Council. Services will be held Sunday, January 10th, 11:15AM at Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
