
        GRAEV--Abraham(Al). passed away on June 15, 2006, at the age of 91 after a short illness. He was born in New York City on February 19, 1915, where he attended Stuyvesant High School and the Engineering School of New York University. He sang in the Children's Chorus of the New York Metropolitan Opera. He spent most of his professional career with the Eclipse-Pioneer Division of the Bendix Corporation, where he worked on a number of classified aeronautical and aerospace projects. He finished his professional career with Diagnostic Retrieval Systems. Mr. Graev is survived by his wife, the former Beatrice Ruthman, his two sons Lawrence and Bruce and their loving wives, Lorna and Nancy, and his four grandchildren, Adam, Nicole, Alyson and Stephanie. He was a wonderful and loving father and grandfather and we will all miss him. Donations can be made to the Beatrice and Abraham Graev Scholarship Fund at Cornell University, 139 East Seneca Street, Suite 400, Ithaca, NY 14850-4353.
