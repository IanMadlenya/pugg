
        LEVINE-Shirley R. On August 7, 1999 at age 71, in FL. Daughter of the late Dr. Louis and Celia Oringel of Brooklyn, Shirley graduated from Brooklyn College with B.A. and M.A. degrees. She taught for 10 years in Oceanside, LI School District in the elementary and high school. She was accredited to teach French, English, History and Math. She is survived by her husband Robert, her son Marc, a physician in Florida, daughter-in-law Robin and grandchildren Zachary and Jacob.
