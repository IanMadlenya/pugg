
        FITZPATRICK--Thomas B. M.D., Ph.D. The Department of Dermatology at New York University School of Medicine notes with sorrow the passing of Tom Fitzpatrick, Chairman-Emeritus of the Department of Dermatology at the Harvard Medical School. At Harvard and the MGH, TBF trained many dermatologic leaders throughout the world. Through their efforts his passion for cutaneous biology and his dedication to dermatology and patients with dermatologic diseases will be perpetuated. Our condolences to his wife, Bea, and his children. Irwin M. Freedberg, M.D. MacKee Professor & Chair Ronald O. Perelman Dept of Dermatology New York University School of Medicine
