
        BLUM-Morris. Died peacefully at home November 15, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Gladys B. Blum. Loving father of Nancy & Murphy Levin, Bob and Michael Blum. Devoted and cherished grandpa to Kelly, Duncan and Timothy Levin; Amanda, Jamie and Alex Blum. Cherished brother to Jackie, Mimi and Clarence. Uncle, and dear friend. Services Friday, November 17, 11 AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
        BLUM - Morris. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Morris Blum our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary

        BLUM-Morris M. Dear Moe, ''Uncle Moe'' to all his family whom he adored and who adored him. A professional, who extended his skills to the art of sculpture. A sensitive and loving person. We feel so alone without him. Mimi and Clarence
