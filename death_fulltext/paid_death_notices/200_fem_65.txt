
        de la CHAPELLE--Virginia T., 71, of Naples, FL, on September 6, 2003. Born Brooklyn, NY, raised in Manhasset, LI, graduated Mt. Holyoke College 1953. Survived by husband of 50 years, Norman, four children, Charles de la Chapelle of Barrington, IL, Anne de la Chapelle of Westport, NY, Julie de la Chapelle of Naples, and Andrew de la Chapelle of Grayslake, IL, and her eight grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Naples, 1095 Whippoorwill Lane, Naples, FL 34105.
