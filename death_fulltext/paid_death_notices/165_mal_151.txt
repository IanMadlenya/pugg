
        LEHMAN-Jack H. Jr., 82. Of Boynton Beach, Florida and Oakhurst, New Jersey. Died peacefully at home on October 10, 2000. Most loving and caring husband of Barbara. Adored father, teacher and friend of Jill L. Feist and Jack H. Lehman III. Wonderful and generous Poppop of Jeffrey and John Feist, Michael, Katherine, Skye and Lexi Lehman. Beloved brother of Babette Newman and most kind & supportive stepfather of Karen Kates. His unique personality affected all who knew him. His guidance, wisdom and courage to the end filled us with pride. We have all been blessed with his love, humor and devotion. Funeral Private. Family will be receiving friends at 222 Park Avenue, Oakhurst, NJ on Thursday, October 12th from 2-4 P.M. and from 7-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to: Hospice (VNA of Central New Jersey), 1100 Wayside Road, Tinton Falls, NJ 07712 or Monmouth Medical Center, Attn: Bridget Murphy, 300 Second Avenue, Long Branch, NJ 07740.
