
        WENDER - Dorothea Schmidt. Professor Emeritus of Classics at Wheaton College. Died January 13 in Ma. Dory will be remembered for her love of learning, humor, strong will and devotion to her daughters. A memorial will be held at the Unitarian Church in Norton, Ma. on Saturday, April 19, 2003 at 2PM. Donations in her name may be directed to the ACLU, 125 Broad Street, 18th FL., New York, N.Y. 10014.
