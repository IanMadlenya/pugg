
        ALCOTT-Fred. Age 98, on July 2, 1998. Beloved husband of Rica. Devoted father of Mimi and Virginia. Cherished grandfather of Michael, Richard, David, James & Victoria, and loving great-grandfather of Nicholas, Annie, Alice & Benjamin. He was a man of extraordinary capabilities who persevered successfully through great adversity fueled by a profound love for his family. He will live on in memory. Services on Sunday, 9:45 A.M., Campbell's Funeral Home, 81st Street and Madison Avenue.
