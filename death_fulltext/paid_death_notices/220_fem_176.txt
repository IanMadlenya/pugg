
        FERENCZ--Dolores. Born in Columbus, OH, died at her home in Southbury, CT, on May 20 at the age of 90. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Hunter College in 1935, she worked as an elementary school teacher for the NYC Public Schools from 1951 until her retirement in 1973. Devoted wife to the late Zoltan for 67 years. Cherished mother of Dr. Jonathan Ferencz and daughter - in - law Maxine, daughters Allison Hesch and the late Elizabeth Fass. Beloved grandmother to Julianne, Benjamin, Peter, Michael, Merrill, Stephanie and Armand. Great grandmother of 12, sister-in-law of Joan and aunt of Gerald and Karen. Graveside services at Beth-El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ, at 2 PM, May 22. Contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Foundation of America, 322 Eighth Avenue, 6th floor, NY, NY 10001.
