
        BEATTY-Ryan James, 18, died peacefully on the 7th of May surrounded by his family. He brought love, laughter and joy to all. Son of Marion Usdan of Summit, N.J. and Martin Beatty of Washington, N.J. A Memorial service will be held on Saturday, May 9th, 1998 at 12:00pm at the Reeves-Reed Arboretum, Hobart Avenue, Summit, N.J. (1908-273-8787). In lieu of flowers, please direct all contributions in Ryan's memory to the Matheny School, Main Street, Peapack, New Jersey 07977.
