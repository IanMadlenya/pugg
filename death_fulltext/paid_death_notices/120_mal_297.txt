
        BEER-Donald, of Princeton, New Jersey, on January 25, 1997. The many friends and neighbors of Donald Beer mourn the loss of an Olympic gold-medal winner in 1956 and carrier of the flame in 1996, ringer of the bells at Trinity Church, builder of model trains at Christmas, player of fiddles and saws, father, friend, and family man. He shall be missed. Service at Trinity Church, Princeton, N.J., 12 noon, Saturday, February 1.
