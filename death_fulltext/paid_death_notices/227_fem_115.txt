
        BENNETT--Patricia Egan. On December 14, 2005 at age 81. Wife of the late William F. Bennett. Survived by her children and their spouses, William (Constance) of Dedham, Ma., Catherine Frailey (Fred) of McLean, Va., Jeff (Kelsey) of Lebanon, NJ, Betsy Henkel (Bill) of Mason Neck, Va., and Robert (Julie) of Norwell, Ma. Cherished grandmother of 15. Graduate of St. Elizabeth College (Convent, NJ) and founder of Peapack Interiors, Inc; former resident of Morristown, NJ, Delray Beach, Fla. and Watch Hill, RI. Past Board member of Peck School and the Watch Hill Improvement Society. Devoted to her family, her many friends, and, in the good old days, her bridge games. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Peck School, 247 South Street, Morristown, N.J. 07960. Services private. A celebration of her life will be held in early summer in Watch Hill.
