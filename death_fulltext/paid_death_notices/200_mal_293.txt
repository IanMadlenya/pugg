
        ROSENTHAL--Herbert Ira. On September 26, 2003 in Palm Beach, FL, age 92. Beloved husband of Shirley Kate for 63 years. Devoted father of Judith (deceased) and Audrey Goodman (Sheldon). Loving grandfather of Victoria (Jonas). He led a successful and exciting life as an executive of MCA. His generosity of spirit was recognized by all who knew him. His kindness and humor will be remembered forever.
        ROSENTHAL--Herbert Ira. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Herbert Rosenthal, on September 26, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
