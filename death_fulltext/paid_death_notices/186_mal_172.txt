
        ROLAND-Maxwell, M.D. On July 14th at the age of 93 of Fort Lauderdale, FL. and NY. Beloved husband of Helen. Devoted father of Ronni Eron and Bruce. Cherished grandfather of Lauren. Services Wednesday 12 noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
        ROLAND-Maxwell, M.D. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of the Fresh Meadow Country Club, record the passing of Maxwell Roland, M.D., an esteemed member of our club. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Helen, and to the entire family. Robert Fortunoff, President Roger Blumencranz, Secy.
        ROLAND-Maxwell. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
