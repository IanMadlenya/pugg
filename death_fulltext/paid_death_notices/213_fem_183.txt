
        KRIENDLER--Florence Feller. On Sunday, October 24, 2004, after a long illness. Beloved widow of the late I. Robert Kriendler, adored mother of Karen Kriendler Nelson, Jeffrey Feller Kriendler and Jack Kriendler and Jack's wife Wendy. Cherished grandmother of Mark and Chris Nelson, Jenny, Dana and Catherine Kriendler, and Chris' wife Amy Rees and loving great-grandmother of Simone Rees Nelson. The family will receive relatives and friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, Tuesday, October 26, 7-9PM. Funeral at Temple EmanuEl, 5th Ave at 65th St, Wednesday, October 27 at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her name to the Acute Care Division of the Visiting Nurse Society of New York, 1250 Broadway, NY NY 10001.
        KRIENDLER--Florence Feller. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted and longtime member, Florence Feller Kriendler, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
        KRIENDLER--Florence Feller. We sadly mark the passing of the gentle matriarch of the Kriendler family. Her generous and fun-loving spirit will be missed by all who knew her. We celebrate a life well lived. Love, Dick, Anne and Bubs
