
        REINACH - Jacquelyn (nee Krasne) died peacefully on September 30 in Los Angeles, after a brief illness. Beloved wife, mother, grandmother, daughter and sister. Talented composer, writer of more than 50 books, co-creator of Sweet Pickles, music and video producer and long-time feminist. In her 70 years, she touched multitudes through her love and humanitarianism. Her work promoted health, literacy and self-esteem in young people. Her life supported love, understanding, self-knowledge and communication. Grieving family and friends will miss her deeply. Memorial service October 24 at 6PM at Church of the Advent Hope, 111 East 87 Street.
