
        BERELSON-Thelma D. On July 22, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Loving mother of Ellen and her husband Lawrence Franks and the late Philip. Dear sister of Elca Branman and sister-in-law of Elliot Berelson and his wife Suzanne. Board member of the League for the Hard of Hearing and President of The Theodore H. Barth Foundation. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Tuesday 11:30 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the League for the Hard of Hearing, 71 West 23rd Street, New York, NY 10010.
        BERELSON-Thelma D. The Board of Directors, membership and staff of the League for the Hard of Hearing mourn the death of a cherished friend and Board member. Mrs. Berelson was a woman of courage, compassion and tenacity with genuine and deep concern for the well-being of others. Dedicated mother, teacher and devoted wife of our late President Emeritus, Irving P. Berelson. We offer our deepest sympathy to her daughter Ellen, son-in-law Lawrence Franks and family. John M. Scopaz, President
