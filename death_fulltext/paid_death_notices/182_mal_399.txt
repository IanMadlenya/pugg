
        RUBINSTEIN-Ronald I., M.D., F.A.C.C., F.A.C.P., F.S.C.A.&I., F.C.C.P., F.A.H.A. 46 years old, of Ocean Township, died Wednesday, March 27th at Jersey Shore Medical Center. Dr. Rubinstein received his education at Texas Tech University School of Medicine in Lubbock, Texas. He did his Internal Medicine Internship and Residency at Mount Sinai Hospital in NYC and, his Cardiology Fellowship & Invasive and Interventional Fellowships at the Philadelphia Heart Institute, Presbyterian-University of PA Medical Center in Philadelphia. Dr. Rubinstein had a private practice in both Neptune and Marlboro Townships. He was an Interventional Cardiologist and the Director of Interventional Cardiology at Jersey Shore Medical Center, the Director of Cardiology at Bayshore Hospital and a staff Cardiologist at Community Medical Center. Dr. Rubinstein was a member of the Temple Beth Miriam, Elberon and a benefactor for the Jewish Community Center in Ocean Township. Dr. Rubinstein devoted much of his time and energy to the American Heart Association. He was the President of the American Heart Association, Southern Board and a leader in the Heart Walks and Dinner Dances for the Monmouth and Ocean Chapters. Dr. Rubinstein worked tirelessly to promote better education of Heart Disease and the need for AED's as the first chain of survival in sudden cardiac arrest. Dr. Rubinstein was also a leader in the field of Research, always striving to find safer and healthier methods to treat his Cardiac Patients. Surviving are his wife Judith Bashover Rubinstein, his three sons, Benjamin, Max and Jacob, his mother Rochelle, of New York City and 2 brothers, Todd, of the Bronx, NY and Steve, of Mamaroneck, NY. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, March 29th at 2:00 P.M. at the Temple Beth Miriam Elberon. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the American Heart Association at 2550 Highway 1, North Brunswick, NJ 08902-4301 and/or the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation.
