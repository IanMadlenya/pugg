
        SCHEINBERG-Sylvia. April 19, 2000. She was a wild spirit who touched everyone who knew her with her humor, her humanity and her song. She is the beloved mother of Linda and Tina, grandmother of Erik, Bryan, Risa, Robert and Leah, great-grandmother of Ross, Isaac, Santino, Michael, Amanda, Christina and Rachel. She joins her husband, Mickey, the love of her life, in eternal rest.
