
        MAYRSOHN--Ethel Florence Handelman, died May 4th, 2007 in Barcelona, Spain. Beloved wife of Bernard for over 60 years. Loving mother of Janet, Barbara, Mark, Andrew and daughter-in-law Kathy. Cherished grandmother of Aubreyanne and Sophia. Her extraordinary heart, charity, compassion and intelligence helped make all who knew her a better person and a better world. Her constant love, smile, warmth and companionship will be missed by all. Funeral services will be held at Temple Kol Ami, 255 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY, 10:30am, Friday, May 11th, 2007. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Tuberous Sclerosis Alliance at: tsalliance.org
