
        NAGLE-Joseph F., 89. Of Franklin Lakes, NJ, Nov. 9, 1997. Beloved husband of Lorraine (nee Negri) and loving father of Patricia Morrissey. Also survived by son-in-law Kenneth Morrissey. Adoring grandfather of six grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren. He is entombed at the Madonna Cemetery, Fort Lee, New Jersey.
