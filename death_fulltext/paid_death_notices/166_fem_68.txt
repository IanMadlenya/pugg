
        GOLDBERG-Shirley Miller. Of Fort Lee, NJ, formerly of Jericho and Brooklyn, NY, at home, surrounded by family, at peace, on November 10, 2000. For 54 years, much beloved wife of Isadore Elliott. Loving and deeply devoted to her children Sandra, Jeffrey (deceased), Neil and Howard, her son-in-law Paul, and her grandchildren John and Brian. Caring sister to Ann (deceased) and Sarah. She graduated from Thomas Jefferson High School and then worked as a legal secretary for more than 40 years, all while raising her family, keeping home and trying to help others. Services Sunday, November 12, at 11:30 AM at Gutterman-Musicant in Englewood, NJ. Graveside services at Beth Moses Cemetery in Pinelawn, NY. Shiva at home.
