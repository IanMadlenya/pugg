
        DULLEA-Jeff. A former member of the Board of Directors and Vice President of Penn South Social Services Inc., died on June 4, 2000. Jeff was an uncommon leader in the field of cooperative services. He was committed to a high level of service, care and concern for people based on cooperative principles and was a forceful advocate for progressive ideas and human needs. Our love and condolences to his wife Judith, daughter Sarah, and family. David Smith, Chairman Nat Yalowitz, President
