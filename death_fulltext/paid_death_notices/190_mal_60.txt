
        ROSENBERG - Seymour. Died on November 4. Former Major of the U.S. Army, veteran of W.W. II and former Mason. Beloved husband of the late Elsa Rosenberg, devoted father of his children Helene, Anita and Lester. Services Tuesday 10 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        ROSENBERG-Seymour. Sutton Place Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of our devoted member. Our heartfelt sympathies to the entire bereaved family. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
