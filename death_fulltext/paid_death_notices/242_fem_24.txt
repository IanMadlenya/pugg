
        REED--Amy Earl, 45, of Darien, Connecticut. Formerly of Ridgewood, NJ, died Saturday February 24, 2007. She is survived by her loving and beloved sons John and George Reed, her parents Janet and Donald Earl of Boynton Beach, Florida, her brother and his wife, John and Morgan Earl, her sisters and husbands, Donna and Stephen Hodgdon and Brenda and Michael DePaola and nieces and nephews Baker, Maxine, Tyler, Brett, Madison, Devon and Jaclyn. Amy was a graduate of Ridgewood High School and Purdue University. She was a former Board Member of the Domestic Violence Crisis Center, a supporter of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, and a member of St. Lukes Episcopal Church. She was a devoted and loving mother, loyal daughter, a steadfast and special aunt, and a good friend. She will be greatly missed. A memorial service will be celebrated at St. Lukes Episcopal Church, 1864 Post Road, Darien, CT (203655-1456) on Thursday, March 8th at 6:00 pm. The family requests donations be made to the Domestic Violence Crisis Center, 5 Eversley Ave., Norwalk, CT 06851. Private services were held under the direction of CC Van Emburgh Funeral Home of Ridgewood, NJ. (www.vanemburgh.com)
