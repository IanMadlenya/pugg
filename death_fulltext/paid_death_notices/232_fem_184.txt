
        FORSTER--Elizabeth (Betty) Graham died May 22, 2006 at her home in New York City, in her 93rd year. Predeceased by parents John R. and Grace C. (Towse) Forster, sister H. Patricia Forster, brothers Cyril, Francis R.C. Forster, MD, J. Wilfred Forster, MD, Douglas S. Forster, MD, she is survived by niece Gail (Browne) and nephews Douglas and John Forster, all of Ontario, Canada. Burial May 31, 2006 by James Reid Funeral Home, (613) 544-3411 at Cataraqui Cemetery, Kingston, Ontario.
