
        LUSCHINSKY-Heinz L., MD. On Sunday, January 21, 2001. Born in 1910 in Hamburg, Germany. Came to this country as a political refugee in 1940. Senior attending physician at French Hospital and later Roosevelt Hospital in NYC. Among the very first to introduce and practice the Lamaze Method of natural childbirth in the U.S. Survived by his wife Mildred, daughter Cathy Drennan, son Andre Ruedi and sister Lotte Lange.
