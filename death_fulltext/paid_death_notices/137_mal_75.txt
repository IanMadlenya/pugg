
        GREEN-Marshall, Ambassador. Of Washington, D.C., on Saturday, June 6, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Lispenard Green, father of Mark W. Green of Arlington, VA, Ted C. Green of Washington, D.C., and the late Brampton S. Green, brother of Clarissa Hopf and Gertrude Hammond, grandfather of Timothy A. Green. Friends may call at Joseph Gawler's Sons, 5130 Wisconsin Ave. at Harrison Street, NW, on Tuesday, from 2 to 4 PM. Memorial services will be held at St. David's Episcopal Church, 5150 Macomb Street, NW (off MacArthur Blvd.), on Wednesday, June 10, at 12 noon. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made in his name to the Japan American Society of Washington, 1020 19th St., NW, Washington, D.C. 20036 or Population Action International, 1120 19th Street, NW, Washington, D.C. 20036.
