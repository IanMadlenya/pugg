
        CIPOLLA-Charles W., on March 19, 2000 of Tenafly, NJ. Beloved father of Nina Smith of Wilmington, DE and devoted brother to Rosalie Cipolla-Beffa of Teaneck, NJ and Joseph A. Cipolla of San Diego, CA. He was a graduate of Yale University and Columbia University School of Law. He was a member of the American, New York and New Jersey Bar Associations and the Yale Club. He was an Army veteran of WW2 and served in the Army Reserve thereafter attaining the rank of Lieutenant-Colonel before retiring. There will be a memorial Mass on Sat., June 10th at 12:10PM, St. Joseph's, 371 Sixth Ave., NYC. Nina A. Smith & her Co-workers Winterthur Museum Garden & Library, Winterthur, DE
