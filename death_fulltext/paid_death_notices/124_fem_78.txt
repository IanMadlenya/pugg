
        GURWIN-Rosalind. The Officers and Directors of Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Center sadly mourn the untimely passing of Rosalind Gurwin, beloved wife of Joseph Gurwin, a cherished friend, humanitarian and esteemed supporter. Rosalind will be greatly missed. May her legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom she touched. Our profound sympathy and sincere condolences to the family. Mark L. Goldstein, President Eli S. Feldman, Executive VP Chief Executive Officer
