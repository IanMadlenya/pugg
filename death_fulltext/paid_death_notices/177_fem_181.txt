
        VARE-Beatrice. The Faculty, Advisory Council and Alumni Association of the Columbia University School of Social Work note with sadness the death of Beatrice Vare, a member of the Class of 1941. An esteemed alumna and family counselor. She was a generous supporter of the School and the University and dedicated to her profession and the work of many community welfare organizations. Our deepest condolences are extended to her family. Sheila B. Kamerman, Interim Dean
