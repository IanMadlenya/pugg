
        MURPHY--Helen Marts, 48, died May 4, 2005 in Montclair. Survived by husband Paul and children Keith and Julia. A Cornell graduate and IT Manager, she was a painter, sculptor and amateur musician. She enjoyed ballet, theater, reading and travel. A Memorial service to held at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her name to Mountainside Hospital Cancer Center in Montclair, NJ 07042.
