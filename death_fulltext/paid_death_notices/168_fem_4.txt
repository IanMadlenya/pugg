
        VAN RENSSELAER-Lillie. Age 88, died peacefully in her sleep December 30. For 55 years she lived in Locust Valley, LI, and Manhattan. She was the widow of Stephen, of the historical patroon family, beloved mother of Steve Jr. and aunt of Philip V. R. She will be greatly missed by her survivors and her friends at the Colony Club and Piping Rock. Burial will be at St. John, Lattingtown, Cold Spring Harbor, NY.
