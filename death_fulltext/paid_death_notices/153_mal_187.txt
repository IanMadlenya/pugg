
        DENNING-Peter J. On October 15, 1999. Of Jackson Heights for forty years, formerly of Chelsea. Survived by his devoted wife, Margaret (nee Clark). Mr. Denning is a veteran of foreign wars who participated in four invasions while overseas for three years. A graduated of St. Columba LaSalle Academy and Fordham University. Mr. Denning was an Inspector with the United States Treasury Department for 35 years. Reposing at Leo Kearns Funeral Home, 61-40 Woodhaven Blvd., Rego Park, Saturday and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial Monday 10:15AM at St. Joan of Arc Church followed by interment at Calvary cemetery.
