
        SUSSMAN-Samuel J., 88. Beloved husband of Ruth for 60 years. Adored father of Jeremy and Eric. Loving grandfather of Adam, Dana and Jesse. Before his retirement, he was a distinguished trial and appellate attorney. He gave many years of exemplary service to Central Synagogue in Rockville Centre. His generous spirit, kindness, leadership, wit and warmth will be sorely missed by all who loved him. Services will be held Thursday, May 13, at 2:PM at Gutterman's Inc, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, N.Y.
