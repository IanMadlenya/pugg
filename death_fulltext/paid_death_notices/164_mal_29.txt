
        CHANG-David Jih-Yuan. With great sadness, we announce the passing of David Jih-Yuan Chang on September 7, 2000, in NYC after a brief illness. An accomplished civil engineer, David designed many distinguished projects including the Crystal Cathedral in California, the majestic tented roof of the Denver International Airport, and the Newark Airport international arrivals terminal. A loving husband for over 30 years to Margarita; father to Rudolph; grandfather to Jonas; brother to Chi-Deu, Kenny, John, and Robert; son to K.; father-in-law to Jamie and brother-in-law to Ana. David also had a great love for the family's dogs, Badger and Dixie, and had countless friends. He will be sorely missed. For further information contact Beecher Funeral Home (914) 769-0001.
