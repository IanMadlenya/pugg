
        McGUIRE-Augustus S. CSP. On August 27, 2001 at age 69. Born in Nashau, NH, he was ordained a Paulist priest in 1959. His assignments included nineteen years in Toronto, Canada, seven years in Houston, TX, eleven years at Good Shepherd Parish in New York, NY, and the past six years in Clemson, SC. Fr. Gus was fluent in Spanish and Italian which served him well in his assignments. He was awarded two Master's degrees from St. Joseph's, Dunwoodie, New York. He is survived by brothers John and Robert and his sister Lucy Ann Phillips. Viewing will be at Saint Paul the Apostle Church in Seneca, SC at 6:30 p.m. on Wednesday, August 29, 2001 followed by a Mass of Christian Burial at 7:30 p.m. Burial will be at the Sparkman Hillcrest Cemetery, Northwest Highway, Dallas, TX at 1:30 p.m. on Friday, August 31, 2001.
