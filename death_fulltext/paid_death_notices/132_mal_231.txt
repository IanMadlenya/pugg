
        MARCUS-Zola Carl. Artist and educator, died on January 12, in his 83rd year. He is survived by a niece, Julie Feinsilver, now in El Salvador, and two nephews, Alan Feinsilver of Houston and Sherman Marcus of Israel. A service will be held at The Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 4 P.M., on Monday, January 19. A reception will follow at 605 Park Avenue, Apt. 4G.
