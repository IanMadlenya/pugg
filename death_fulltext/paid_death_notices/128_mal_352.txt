
        COGAN-James R., aged 69, died on September 26, 1997. Survived by his wife, Arrial, his children, Sarah E., George W. and Julia Finney, and seven grandchildren. Memorial service on Wednesday, October 1 at 4 PM at The Church of the Ascension, Fifth Avenue and 10th Street. For those who wish, contributions to Ascension Music, 12 West 11th Street, New York, NY 10011.
        COGAN-James R. The members of the boards and officers of The Bunbury Company and The Windham Foundation mourn the death of their fellow board member. Jim's service since 1972 was marked by his wry sense of humor, compassion and excellent judgment. As Chairman of the Board of Windham and Treasurer of Bunbury he was significantly responsible for their expanded resources and charitable reach. We send our heartfelt sympathies to Arrial, George, Sarah, Julia and Jim's grandchildren. The Windham Foundation by Stephan A. Morse, President The Bunbury Company by Samuel W. Lambert III, President
        COGAN-James R. The Board of Directors and Members of The Salmagundi Club deeply regret the passing of their esteemed member. Richard C. Pionk, President
