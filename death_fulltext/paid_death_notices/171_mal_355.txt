
        MILLING-Lawrence B., 75, devoted and caring husband of Jean (Claster), adoring father of Leonard and Lynn, on April 26th. He served with the US Navy during World War II. He was a retired partner at the law firm of Otterbourg, Steindler, Houston and Rosen, P.C. in New York City. Services Sunday April 29, 2001 at 11am from Wien & Wien, 129 Engle Street, Englewood, NJ. For directions: 1-800-322-0533.
        MILLING-Lawrence B. Otterbourg, Steindler, Houston & Rosen, P.C., mourns the passing of our friend and retired partner Lawrence B. Milling. Larry's integrity, intellect, investment acumen, sense of humor and his unending loyalty to the firm and its clients engendered the respect and admiration of all who knew and worked with him. He will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Jean, and his children, Lynn and Leonard. Otterbourg, Steindler, Houston & Rosen, P.C.
