
        WORTH-Irene, March 10th, 2002. Beloved sister of Carol Johnson of Santa Monica, CA and Luke Evans of Los Angeles, CA. Loving aunt of Grant Johnson, Craig Johnson, Chris Johnson and Holly Johnson. She was an internationally known actress of both stage and screen for over fifty years. She will be deeply missed by both her family and many friends throughout the world. Funeral services will be private. Memorial services are being planned for both New York and London to be announced at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Stella Adler Studio of Acting, 31 West 27th Street, New York, New York 10001.
