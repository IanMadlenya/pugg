
        AGABIAN-Louise Sophie. May 22, 1924 to August 7, 1998. Model, Artist, Interior Designer, Real Estate Broker and Business Woman. Her tremendous loss is mourned by beloved daughters Nina, Lori and Lisa; grandsons Samuel & Tristan; granddaughter Allegra; her many friends and extended family. She was married to Samuel Agabian who died in 1962, a former Executive Vice President of Sperry Rand Corporation. She touched and inspired all those who knew her and was loved for her vibrant, tenacious and indomitable spirit. Her unwavering devotion to her family and friends, especially those in need, is one of her legacies. Private service held in San Francisco August 11th. Memorial services will be held at 11AM on September 27, 1998 in New York at The Armenian Church of the Holy Martyrs, 209-15 Horace Harding Expressway, Bayside, New York 11364. 718-225-0235.
