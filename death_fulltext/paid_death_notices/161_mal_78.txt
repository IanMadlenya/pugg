        GUTHERY-Frank, F. Selfhelp Community Services mourns the passing of Frank Guthery, a long time board member who played a central role in the development of the agency. His commitment to Nazi Victims and community services helped pave the way to a more solid base for our organization serving thousands of older people. He will be remembered for his spirit, his generosity, and his willingness to help others -Our condolences to his family, of whom he was very proud.
