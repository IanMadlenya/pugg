
        OTTEN-Louis. The Board of Directors and staff of VISIONS/Services for the Blind and Visuallly Impaired records with sorrow the passing of The Honorable Louis Otten. He was a most dedicated supporter of VISIONS and Vacation Camp for the Blind and member of The Board of Directors for 60 years. Our deepest sympathy is expressed to his family. Nancy T. Jones, President Nancy D. Miller, Exec Director
