
        ROBBINS-Ann Roe, 94, widow of Leonard John Robbins, died peacefully at her home in Manhattan June 25, 2000. A '26 graduate of Smith College, she wrote cook books and garden books and ran a very successful cooking school in Manhattan, one of the first of its kind. Beloved by all, her survivors include daughters Susan Morrison, Jennifer Manocherian and Victoria Cowal, Susan's husband Sean and their children Moira, Sean and Leslie, Jennifer's husband Fred and their children Kim, John, Jed, Greg and Cara, Victoria's children Greg, Kirsten and Ali, as well as 14 great grandchildren. She will be sorely missed. The family will receive visitors at the Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 W 72 St, Tuesday June 26 from 5-6pm. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation to the charity of your choice in her name.
