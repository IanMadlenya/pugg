
        FRIEDMAN-Alvin S., 81, of Lake Worth, FL died on October 24, 1999. Born in Mt. Vernon, NY, he lived there until 1967, residing thereafter in Greenwich, CT and Somers, NY before moving to Lake Worth in 1995. He is survived by his wife Gloria, and was predeceased by his wife of 55 years, Frances Feinsod Friedman. He is also survived by three childrenRobert, Marsha Silverman and David, two brothers-Herbert and William, seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren.
