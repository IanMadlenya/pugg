
        CARLSON-Philip E. April 18, 1943December 15, 1997. We mourn the loss of a special and caring man dedicated to the arts. Formerly of East 79th Street and a graduate of Parsons School of Design, Philip and his beloved wife Jane continued the love of the arts with their store. The Carlson Collection Home and Garden Furnishings in Clinton, Connecticut. The Swann Funeral Home, 80 East Main Street, Clinton, Connecticut, for further information.
