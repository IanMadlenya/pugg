
        MARX-Arthur Jr. Age 79, of Naples, FL, a Brown University graduate, served in the U.S. Marine Corps during World War II, veteran of Iwo Jima and Saipan, he was an avid fisherman and a Specialist on the American Stock Exchange. He is survived by his wife Emilie (Hyman) Marx, a son and daughter-in-law Arthur and Molly Marx. He was predeceased by a son and daughter Alfred H. and Kathryn Marx. Loved and missed by family and friends.
