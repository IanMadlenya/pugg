
        CLYNE-Katherine E. Passed away on Sunday, May 25, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Irving M. Clyne. Devoted mother and friend of Robert. Loving sister of Marion Hirsh, Ralph Friedberg and the late Frank Friedberg. Her kindness and caring will be missed by all whom she touched. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society. Services private.
