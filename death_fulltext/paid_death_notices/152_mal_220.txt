
        ROSE-Frederick P. The Officers and Executive Committee Members of the Yale Class of 1944 on behalf of themselves and their fellow classmates mourn most deeply the passing of their distiguished friend, Fred Rose. To wife Sandra, children Jonathan, Adam and Deborah, brothers Daniel and Elihu and their families, we express our heartfelt sympathy in their untimely loss. J. Mabon Childs, Secretary Yale Class of 1944
        ROSE-Frederick Phineas. Friend and colleague for over 50 years. The fond memories of the shared time together in the woods, on golf courses, on the slopes and on the waters, and of the enlightening thoughts and ideas, and his delightful company-these all will be cherished. I will miss him. Bill Lese
