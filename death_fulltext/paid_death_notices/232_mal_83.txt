
        HOFFER--Paul B., MD. Passed away after a long and courageous battle with Parkinson's Disease. He will be remembered by family and friends as a brilliant researcher and scientist, a devoted physician, swimmer and marathoner, and above all a man of strong character and quiet strength who was selfless and humble in sharing his talents and wisdom with family, colleagues and community. He will be dearly missed by his father, Philip, daughters Marjorie, Joanne, Ilene, Suzie, and Alexandra , grandchildren Sara, Rachel, Teddy, Rosie and Ellie and brother Mark. Donations may be sent to The MJ Fox Found for Parkinson's Research, P.O. Box 4777, N Y, NY 10163. Services Thursday 11 AM at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY.
