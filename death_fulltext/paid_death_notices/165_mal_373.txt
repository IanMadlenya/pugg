
        ADAMS-David S. Passed away peacefully October 21, aged 90, in Washington, DC. Born in Edinburgh, Scotland, he joined Boosey & Hawkes Ltd., music publishers, in the 1930's, eventually heading the Toronto office, the New York office, and becoming Managing Director of the London head office in 1964 until his retirement in 1974. He was a Fellow of the Royal College of Organists, a Licentiate of the Royal Academy of Music, and a member of the Performing Rights Society, CAPAC and ASCAP. Predeceased by his wife, Doris, he is survived by two daughters, Judith Chalukoff of Virginia and Hilary Zwicky of New York City and two granddaughters, Francesca Dean and Alexandra Zwicky. Donations, if desired, to the PRS. Memorial to be announced at a later date.
