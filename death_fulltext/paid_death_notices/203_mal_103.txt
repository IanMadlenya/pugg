
        GRAHN -- Eric Lawrence Valentine, 72. Returned to his Lord on December 8, 2003 after a long battle with lung disease and cancer. Predeceased earlier this year by his wife, Juliet. Beloved father of Rebecka and Dominick Russo, Lawrence and Christine Grahn, Christina and Gregory. Loving MorFar to Justina, Maribeatta and Quinn Russo and FarFar to Corinne and Lilianna Grahn. He began his seafaring career at 15 as a galley boy and later attended the Kings Point USMM Academy. He received a commission in the US Navy as Lt. and was chief engineer on the USS ''Rankin''. Retired owner of Weber & Grahn Conditioning Corp. and W&G Service, HVAC companies serving the ''East End''. Mentor to many, generous of his time, talent and spirit to those he met along his travels, he left a unique and indelible mark on this world. His whistle and easygoing way will be missed. The family will receive friends at the WhiteVoltz Funeral Home, 364 E. Main St., Bay Shore, N.Y. Thursday and Friday, 2-5 and 7-9 pm. Funeral service on Saturday 11am at St Luke's Lutheran Church, Bay Shore. Donations in his memory can be made to the Philanthropic Arm of the American College of Chest Physicians: 330 Dundee Rd., Northbrook, IL 60062 or via the internet @ chestfoundation.org
