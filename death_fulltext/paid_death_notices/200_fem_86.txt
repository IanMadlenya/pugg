
        SAYRE--Harriet Hart, died September 6, 2003 of cancer. She was the wife of the retired Dean of the Washington National Cathedral, Rev. Francis B. Sayre. Funeral and interment will be 2:00 pm Saturday, Sept 13 in the Bethlehem Chapel of the Washington National Cathedral. A Memorial Service will be held October 4, at Grace Church, Vineyard Haven, on Martha's Vineyard. Gifts in lieu of flowers may be made to Martha's Vineyard Community Services, c/o Jan Hatchard, Director of Development, 111 Edgartown Rd, Vineyard Haven, MA 02568.
