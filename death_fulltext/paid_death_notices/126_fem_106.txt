
        NEWMAN-Muriel Orenstein, of Sunrise, FL, formerly of New York City. Died on July 12, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Stanley Newman. She will be missed by her sons Kenneth, Ralph and Glenn; daughters-in-law Michele and Anne; grandchildren Douglas, Jason, Andrew, Emily and Greg. Memorial service will be held Friday, July 18, at 12 noon at the Brooklyn Society For Ethical Culture, 53 Prospect Park West. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. John's Rehabilitation Hospital, Lauderdale Lakes, Florida.
