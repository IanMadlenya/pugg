
        THURM--Samuel, at age 88, following a long illness. Dartmouth and Columbia graduate, WWII veteran, Media Director at Young & Rubicam and Sr. VP Advertising at Lever Bros., ''Newsweek'' referred to him as ''the Branch Rickey of nighttime television,'' for his leadership in casting minorities in ad campaigns. He left Lever to head the ANA's D.C. office, and taught advertising at Florida Atlantic University until age 81. He married RuthElaine Blum in 1940. After her death in 1989 he married Arlene Bruck. He is survived by his wife, two sons, Andrew Thurm and Allen Thurm, and two granddaughters, London Thomson-Thurm and Aileen Thurm. Memorial service is planned for September in Princeton, NJ.
