
        ZWERLING-Louis. At age 88, painter, poet, world-traveler, on September 25, 1998. He will be grievously missed by his wife, Harriet, and son, Milo, and mourned by his brother, Jeff Corey of Malibu, California, sister, Edith Siegel of New York City and many friends. Our love goes with you into your dream.
