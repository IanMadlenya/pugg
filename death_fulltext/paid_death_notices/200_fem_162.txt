
        COUFOS--Ellie. Of Bay Harbor Island, FL, formerly of Jamaica, NY passed away suddenly September 20, 2003. She was the wife of the late Louis P. The family will receive friends Tuesday, September 23, 3:005:00 & 7:00-9:00PM at Forest Lawn Funeral Home, 200 West Copans Road, Pompano Beach. Funeral services will be Wednesday, 11:00AM at St. Demetrios Greek Orthodox Church, 815 NE 15th Avenue, Ft. Lauderdale, FL 33304. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests contributions be made in her memory to St. Demetrios Greek Orthodox Church, Ft. Lauderdale, FL.
