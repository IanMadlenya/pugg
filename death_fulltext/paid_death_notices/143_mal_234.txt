
        SEREGI-Bill John. On December 16, 1998. Born in Budapest, Hungary in 1903. Came to America in 1951. Master jeweler for 77 years. Beloved husband of Lily for 70 years. Devoted father of Ann and Larry. Loving grandfather of Ellie & Lewis. Also survived by nieces and nephews Veronica and Leslie Szikla; George and Georgette Dankner & their families. Services Sunday, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the B'nai Zion Foundation, 136 East 39 Street, New York City 10016.
        SEREGI-Bill John. The Partners of Rosen & Reade and Rosen Seymour Shapss Martin & Co mourn tha passing of our beloved friend of four decades. We will miss him. Our sincerest condolences to Lily, Ann, Ellie and family.
