
        RINTELS-Ruth Wyzanski. Of Teaneck, NJ, formerly of NYC and Brookline, MA, on February 1, 2000. Beloved mother of Constance Schnoll and David W. Rintels. Cherished grandmother of Suzanne Stempinski and Michael and Stephen Schnoll. Loving greatgrandmother of David and Lisa Schnoll. Devoted sister of the late Honorable Charles E. Wyzanski, Jr. Following her education at Wellesley and Simmons Colleges she spent her professional life working to help others at such organizations as CARE, The Beth Israel Hospital in Brookline and Brandeis University. For 7 years she devotedly ran the 25th reunions at Harvard College for which she was made an Honorary member of the Harvard College Class of 1932. Funeral services 10 AM Thursday ''Gutterman-Musicant'', 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ. In lieu of flowers the family request contributions to the Teaneck Volunteer Ambulance Corps or the United Jewish Appeal.
