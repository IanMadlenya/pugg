
        WHITE--Martin J., of New York City and Quogue. Beloved and devoted husband of Mary Ellen Simon, died on Sunday, August 22, 2004. Mr. White is also survived by cousins, Mary Katharine Gannon, Patricia T. Connors, Alfred J. and John J. Talley. He was graduated from Georgetown University and Harvard Law School and was longtime counsel to the Federal Aviation Agency. Upon retirement, he was cited for 41 years of service to the Federal Government. During World War II, he served in the Naval Reserve and rose to the rank of Lieutenant Commander. Interment is private with a memorial service to be held on Friday, August 27th, at 11 AM, at the Church of Immaculate Conception, 580 Main St., Quiogue Westhampton Beach, NY (631-288-1423). In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Catholic Foreign Mission Society, AKA Maryknoll, NY, NY.
