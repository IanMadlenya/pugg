
        BILLINGS-Josephine Swift. The Board of Directors and Staff of United Hospital Fund are saddened by the death of Josephine Billings. A beloved and revered member of the Fund's board for 47 years, she was an enthusiastic supporter of the Fund's mission to shape positive change in healthcare for the people of New York. Known for her sharp mind and great wit, she was an outspoken advocate for volunteers and voluntarism as well as for hospitals and quality healthcare for all, especially the most vulnerable. Her political savvy, mangement skill, and firm but gentle touch made an indelible impression on all who knew her. Howard Smith Chairman James R. Tallon Jr. President
