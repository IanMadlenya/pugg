
        MITCHELL - INNES -- Frances. Died peacefully in Cambridge, MA on February 23. The only daughter of Alexander (Sandie) Mitchell-Innes, and Anne Mitchell-Innes (nee Howard), Francie was born in Philadelphia, PA, in 1940. She will be greatly missed by her brother, Sam, and her sister, Lucy, as well as her many wonderful friends. Funeral services will be held at The Paulist Center, 5 Park Street, Boston, MA at 11 A.M. on Saturday, February 26, 2005.
