
        SCHWARTZ--Asher William. Principal in the law firm of O'Donnell and Schwartz of New York, died March 20, 2006 at his residence at The Osborn in Rye, NY. He was 94. Mr. Schwartz is survived by two sons, Alec M. Schwartz of Chicago, Fred E. Schwartz of Ithaca, NY, a daughter Susan Schwartz of San Francisco. Five grandchildren, two great grandchildren and a brother Dr. Martin F. Schwartz. Funeral services will be held at Kensico Cemetery in Valhalla, NY on March 26 at 1:30 PM. A reception at The Osborn will follow. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Legal Services of the Hudson Valley, 4 Cromwell Place, White Plains, NY 10601.
