
        PAYNTER-Grenville H. The Board of Trustees, staff and volunteers of the New York City School Volunteer Program, Inc., are saddened by the death of Grenville H. Paynter, a dedicated Trustee, and extend deepest sympathies to his family. We will miss him for his insight and sincere concern for public education. Susan Edgar, Ed.D., Executive Director Caryn Magid, Chairman, Board of Trustees The New York City School Volunteer Program, Inc.
