
        ZUCKER--Gary, MD.he entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our dear friend and former colleague, Gary Zucker, MD. Gary served with great distinction as a member of the hospital's staff in the Department of Medicine for over 50 years until his retirement in 1993. He also played a critical role in the establishment of music therapy at Beth Israel through his personal relationship with musical legend Louis Armstrong and the Louis Armstrong Educational Fund. These early efforts have led to the growth and development of the Louis Armstrong Center for Music and Medicine at Beth Israel. To all of Gary's family, friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Board of Trustees David J. Shulkin, MD President and CEO Stephen P. Haveson, MD President, Medical Board Stephen G. Baum, MD Chair, Dept. of Medicine Joanne Loewy, DA, MT-BC Director, Armstrong Center for Music and Medicine $(6$)ZUCKER--Dr. Gary.
