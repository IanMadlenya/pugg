
        AUSTIN--Richard of Short Hills died on June 26 at age 78 of mesothelioma. After serving in the Navy during WW II he received an MBA from Rutgers. He retired from a 38 year career with the Celanese Corporation as VicePresident and Controller of Celanese International. During retirement he served as the Chief Financial Officer of the National Executive Service Corps. He is survived by his wife, Dr. Grace Austin and his son Richard.
