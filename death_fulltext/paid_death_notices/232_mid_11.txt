
        LAUGHLIN--Chester Frank. Frank died in New York City on May 22, age 65. He is survived by his daughter, Beth Warner, her husband Brian and their children Brienna and Tyler in Hilliard, OH; a sister Nellie Laughlin, PhD and her children Camille and Franklin in Madison, WI, and by a loving companion, Mary Vetri. After a long career at IBM, he wrote a successful book on the history of Coca Cola and had an extended family of friends in the New York area. Service will be today 10 AM at McLaughlin & Sons Funeral Home, Third Ave. at 97th St., Brooklyn.
