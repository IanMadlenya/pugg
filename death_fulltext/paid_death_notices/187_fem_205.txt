
        KAPLAN-Hoda Jane. 57, of Brooklyn, died August 21, from injuries in a car accident. Sailor, scholar, lover of subways, she lived with integrity and a full heart. South Street Seaport volunteer, captain of The Sunnyside. She studied Greek at University of Chicago, worked for the MTA for 17 years, volunteered at a shelter for homeless women and a soup kitchen. Survived by her sister Laura Kaplan and her dear friend Jeff Megerdichian. Contributions may be made to The Seamen's Church Institute, 241 Water St., NYC 10038, a homeless women's shelter or other charity.
