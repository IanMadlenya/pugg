
        GREEN-Edson Killam. 97 years. Of Dalton, PA, and Green Valley, AZ. Died May 16, 2003. He was retired Executive Vice President, International Salt Company. Surviving: wife Genevieve Dey Green, three children, one stepdaughter, seven grandchildren, six great-grandchildren. He will long be remembered as an avid sportsman, tireless community leader, willing volunteer and good friend. Memorial services June 12, 2003, in Church of the Epiphany, Glenburn, PA. Memorial contributions to Boy Scouts of America, Northeastern PA Council, 1 Mellow Drive, Moosic, PA 18507.
