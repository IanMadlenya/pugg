
        SCHLESSINGER-Paul J.R., M.D. 77. Retired obstetrician and gynecologist, died on Monday, August, 10 of a heart attack at home in Northampton, MA. A graduate of Harvard University and Harvard Medical School, he practiced on Long Island from 1950 to 1986. At Central General Hospital in Plainview, he served as Co-Director of the OB/GYN department from 1964 to 1986, and on the Board of Directors from 1964 to 1994. He is survived by his wife, Ann, three children, five grandchildren and one great-grandchild. An informal memorial gathering will take place Sunday, August 30 at 1 PM at the offices of his former partners at 777 Old Country Rd, Plainview, LI. Interment will be private.
