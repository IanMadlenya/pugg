
        TURNHEIM-Palmer. With great sadness, the Board of Directors, volunteers and staff of United Cerebral Palsy Associations and the United Cerebral Palsy Research and Educational Foundation extend our condolences to Joy Turnheim, her family and friends on the recent death of her father, Palmer Turnheim. As a national volunteer and Board member, his years of dedication and tireless commitment to our organizations have made a positive impact in the lives of individuals with cerebral palsy and other disabilities. He will be missed. Michael H. Simonson, President Jeanette E. Harvey, Executive Director, UCPA Murray Goldstein, DO, MPH, Director UCP Research and Educational Foundation
