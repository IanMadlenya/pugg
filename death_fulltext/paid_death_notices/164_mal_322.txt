
        KIESER-Rev. Ellwood E., CSP. On September 16, 2000 in Los Angeles at age 71. Philadelphia, Pa-born, he was ordained a Paulist priest by Cardinal Spellman on 5/3/56 at Saint Paul the Apostle Church, New York City. The Hollywood priest/producer worked in film production for over forty years. Famous for promoting the gospel through television and films, he created and produced the weekly anthology series, ''Insight'', and originated the Humanitas awards for TV programs that stressed values. He was executive-producer of ''Romero,'' ''Entertaining Angels,'' and ''The Dorothy Day Story'' and dramatic specials ''We are the Children,'' and ''The Fourth Wiseman.'' There will be a wake service on Thursday, September 21 at 7:30 P.M. at Saint Paul the Apostle Church, Westwood, CA. A Mass of Christian burial will be held on Friday, September 22, at 7:30 P.M. at the same site. Burial service will be at Holy Cross Cemetery, Culver City, CA on Saturday, 9/23/00 at 11 A.M. There will be a memorial service tribute at the Writer's Guild of America West in October (date to be announced). In lieu of flowers, it is requested that donations be made to The Human Family Educational and Cultural Institute (Humanitas Prize) 17575 Pacific Coast Hwy, Pacific Palisades, CA. 90272 and The Paulist Fathers, or Catholic Relief Services.
