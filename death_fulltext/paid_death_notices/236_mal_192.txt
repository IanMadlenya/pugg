
        WATSON--John W. On September 1, 2006. Retired from NYNEX, John's civic-minded dedication included working at Keewaydin Camp in Salisbury, VT, caring for and feeding the homeless in NYC, and advocating for peace and justice. He will be missed by all who knew his generous spirit. He is survived by his wife Hanan, son Nessim, daughter-in-law Peach, and granddaughter Maya, sister Allyan, and nephew Plinio. A service celebrating John's life will be conducted at All Souls Unitarian Church, 80th and Lexington Ave, Sunday, September 24, 4 p.m. Inquiries about contributions may be directed to All Souls at 212-535-5530.
