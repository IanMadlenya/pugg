
        TOWNES-David C., age 74, on January 17 from complications from kidney failure. For over 35 years, he was a foreign tour guide, much in demand because of his proficiency in eleven languages. David's love and devotion to his friends especially those in recovery programs, will always be treasured. A service will be held on February 1 at 10:30 AM, at St. Joseph's Church, Greenwich Village, 371 Avenue of the Americas.
