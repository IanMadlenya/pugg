
        PREZZANO-Wilbur J., DDS, 90, of Vero Beach, Florida died on May 1, 2002. Loving husband of Adelaide. Devoted father of sons Dr. Peter, Vero Beach, Fl; Wilbur, Jr, Charleston, SC; Richard, West Lake Village, CA; Robert, Alpharetta, GA; Paul, Pittsford, NY, 13 grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Dr. Prezzano was the former President of the American Association of Orthodontists, American Board of Orthodontics, Dental Society of the State of NY, NY Academy of Dentistry, and the Ninth District Dental Society. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Indian River Hospital Foundation, Vero Beach, Fl 32960
