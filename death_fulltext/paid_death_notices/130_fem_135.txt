
        FELTHAM-Irene. Of Dix Hills, New York, on November 19, 1997. A brilliant and dedicated teacher of English in the New York City School System, at Martin Van Buren High School, Queens Village, New York. She was the beloved wife of John. Cherished mother of Lynne (Ralph) Stewart, Donald (Andrea) Feltham, and Laurel (Seth) Freedman. Loving grandmother of Brenna and Geoffrey (Marta) Stewart, Dr. Zenobia (Timothy) Brown, and Alexander and Andrew Freedman. Beloved great-grandmother of Milana Velasquez, Ernesto and Arthur Stewart. Reposing at Jacobsen Funeral Home, Huntington, New York, Thursday and Friday. Christian Burial Service at Sweet Hollow Presbyterian Church, Melville, New York, Saturday morning.
