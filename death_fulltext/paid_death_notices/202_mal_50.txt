
        BRODIE--Sheldon J., M.D. The faculty and staff of the NYU Medical Center Skin & Cancer Unit and the Ronald O. Perelman Department of Dermatology sadly mourn the loss of Sheldon Brodie on October 29, 2003. A wonderfully member of our faculty for over 40 years, Dr. Brodie served as Chief of the Tuesday Service for decades. Our condolences to his wife Charlotte, his children, and his grandchildren. Irwin M. Freedberg, M.D. MacKee Professor & Chair
