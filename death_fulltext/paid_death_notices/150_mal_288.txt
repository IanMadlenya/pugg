
        WYNDER - Ernst L., M.D., D.Sc.h.c., Dr.med.h.c. It is with great sadness that the staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center notes the death of their dear friend and colleague, Dr. Ernst L. Wynder. At the time of his death, Dr. Wynder was a consultant in the Department of Medicine at Memorial Hospital for Cancer and Allied Diseases. Dr. Wynder began his career at Memorial Sloan-Kettering in 1951 as a junior resident and continued his association with us as President of the American Health Foundation. Dr. Wynder will be remembered for speaking up about what he knew to be true and sounding an early alarm about the dangers of tobacco use. He is an example to all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Dr. Wynder's family and his colleagues at the Foundation. Paul A. Marks, M.D., President And the staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
        WYNDER-Ernst L., Dr. The Board of Directors, Advisory Board and Staff of The Breast Cancer Research Foundation are deeply saddened by the death of Dr. Ernst Wynder. He was a friend of the foundation, and as a brilliant cancer researcher, we have been honored to support his work. We send our heartfelt condolences to his wife Sandra & his entire family. Evelyn Lauder, President Deborah Krulewitch, Treasurer Susan Hirschhorn, Exec Director
