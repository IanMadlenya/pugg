
        SCHWARTZ -- Cynthia. 61, July 18, 2003, at Saint Vincent's Hospital. A long-time NYC School Social Worker and Family Therapist, she was a graduate of NYU, Hunter College School of Social Work, The Hunter College Post Graduate Program of Psychotherapy and The Ackerman Institute. She is survived by an aunt, Alice Grove, of Silver Spring, MD, sister, Linda Rosen-Schulte, of Calif, and nephews, Dan Rosen, of Washington, D.C., and Eric Rosen, of Calif. She will be greatly missed by family and friends. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent in her memory to SHARE, 1501 Broadway, Suite 1720, NY, NY 10036. >>AD#
