
        SHAPIAN-Betty. A public relations professional for best selling authors, Betty Shapian died of cancer on April 15, 2002, at the age of 73, at the Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles. Raised in Ridgefield, NJ, Shapian's career began in New York with Doubleday Books. In the 1960's she moved to Los Angeles where Shapian & Associates distinguished itself as a mass-media promoter of books. Shapian later pioneered satellite tours of authors. Betty's boundless wit, generosity and friendship enriched us all. She is survived by her sisters, Pearl Shapian and Rose Shapian of Ridgefield, NJ, and Victoria Leggio of Cliffside Park, NJ, and nephews Robert Leggio of Cliffside Park, NJ and William Leggio of Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be sent to the National Coalition for Cancer Survivorship at info@cansearch. org. Services will be held at George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus, NJ, on May 2, 2002, at 11 AM. For information call McCorry Brothers Funeral Home, 201-945-1220.
