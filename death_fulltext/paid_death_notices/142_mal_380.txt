
        WEISS-Joseph. Age 83. Lifetime activist for freedom and justice, human rights and equality. Struggled for tenants' rights and against the privatization of city hospitals. Tutor for Coney Island Learning Center-Adult Literacy. Beloved companion of the late Victoria O'Mara. Joe will be remembered by his children, Carol Weinshenker (Mickey), Rachel Davidsohn, Annette, and the late Marcella, his many grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Metropolitan Council on Housing or Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Services will be held 6 PM, Monday, November 30, ''Parkside'' Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn (near Avenue V). Family will be receiving visitors from 3 PM.
