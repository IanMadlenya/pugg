
        RINEHART--George Henry Doran, of Annapolis, Maryland, died Tuesday, April 10, at age 78. He is survived by his beloved wife, Anne, whom he wed in 1985; his sisters, Mary R. Huvelle and Lady Benedicta Knox; his brother, Stanley M. Rinehart III; his daughter, Deborah Rinehart Fowler; his sons, Frederick R. Rinehart II and Stephen G. Rinehart; 25 grandchildren and 12 greatgrandchildren. He is also survived by his first wife, Sharon Caulfield. George was the grandson of Mary Roberts Rinehart, the mystery writer and correspondent; and son of Stanley Marshall Rinehart, Jr., founder of Holt, Rinehart & Winston. George attended Phillips Exeter Academy and Harvard College. He worked in the publishing and editing field throughout his life and was a certified Drug and Alcohol Abuse Counselor. A memorial service will be held in Annapolis, Maryland, on a date to be announced. Donations may be made to Doctors Without Borders, 1-888392-0392 or www. doctorswithoutborders.com.
