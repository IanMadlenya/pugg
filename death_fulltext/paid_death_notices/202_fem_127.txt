
        SPITZER--Louise F. Died peacefully at home November 5, 2003. Predeceased by her husband Sidney Spitzer and survived by Karen Marshall, grandchildren, and extended family. Born in 1908, she lived a full life with a passion for the arts, a philanthropic heart and a strong love and dedication to her family and friends. Contributions in her memory can be made to a charity of one's choice.
