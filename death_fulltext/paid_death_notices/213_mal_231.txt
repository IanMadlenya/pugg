
        GEVIRMAN -- Herbert. The Chaim Sheba Medical Center and the American Friends of Sheba sorrowfully mourn the passing of Herb Gevirman, the American Friends' longtime Treasurer and Boardmember. Herb's selfless devotion and many years of service will forever be remembered. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Mati, also a cherished board member, his daughter Debbie and granddaughter Sara. Prof. Mordechai Shani, Director-General, Sheba Medical Center Leonard Shaykin, Chairman, American Friends of Sheba Lauri Novick, Exec. Dir., American Friends of Sheba
        GEVIRMAN--Herbert. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member Herbert Gevirman. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred D. Youngwood, President
