
        GELLER--Henry. Of Boca Raton, FL, died Sunday, November 27, 2005, in his home. He was born in the Bronx, NY, on July 16, 1925. He is survived by his wife, Carol Geller and his three children, Laura, Robert and Janet and his grandson, Daniel Geller. Henry Geller was not only a loving and supportive father and husband, he was a dedicated business man who worked in the men's wear industry in New York City for almost 50 years. After he moved to Florida he volunteered and donated his time to the many causes he believed in. Private funeral was held in New York City. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made in Mr. Henry Geller's memory to the American Diabetes Association.
