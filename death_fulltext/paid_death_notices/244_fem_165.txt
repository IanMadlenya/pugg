
        BURDE--Helene, on May 21, 2007, in Sarasota, FL. Born in Providence, RI, she was beautiful, gracious, loving, funny, strong and wise. Her concern was for others. She lived most of her adult life in Bridgeport, CT, and Boynton Beach, FL. She was married to Eli Burde for 69 years until his death in 2004. One of life's greatest partnerships. Mrs. Burde is survived by her devoted family, Brenda Burde and Paulette Lipton, Ronald and Sharon Burde, grandchildren, Howard and Evelyn, Brad and Jeffrey Burde, great-grandchildren, Ariel, Simone and Jed Burde, a sister Frances Robbins, nephews Victor and Richard Robbins (Lori Heller) and Albert (Sonia) Guelblum, Kobi (Polly) Kanchuk. Services Friday, May 25, at 3:00pm from The Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Rd, Fairfield, CT. Shiva Sunday, 4:00-8:00pm at Abbie Marks, 2211 Broadway, NYC, Monday, 2:00pm at Burde, 100 Ocean Pkwy, Brooklyn. Memorial contributions to American Jewish World Service, 45 W 36th St, 11th Floor, NY, NY 10018, or to The Jewish Home for the Elderly, 175 Jefferson St, Fairfield, CT 06825.
