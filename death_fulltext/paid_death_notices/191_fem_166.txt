
        KLAW-Barbara. Born Sept. 17, 1920 in New York City. Died December 14, 2002 at her home in West Cornwall, Connecticut after a long and wonderful life. Beloved wife of Spencer Klaw, mother of Joanna, Susan, Rebecca and Margaret, and grandmother of nine. A memorial gathering will be held in the spring. Contributions in her memory may be sent to the Cornwall Chronicle, 143 Cream Hill Road, West Cornwall, Connecticut 06796.
