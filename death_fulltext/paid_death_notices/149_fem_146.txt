
        DVONCH-Patricia Mathison. Retired professor and NYU department chairwoman. Her late husband, Frederick Dvonch, was musical director for Rodgers & Hammerstein productions. Surviving are a stepson, Loring Dvonch; a stepdaughter, Peggy Swingle; a sister, Mrs. William (Jane) Wieler; and a close friend, Douglas McKay. A memorial service will be held in New York City.
