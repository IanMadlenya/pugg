
        GARRETSON - TURNER -- Vir ginia Holland. 86, of Greenport, LI, on May 7, 2005. Renowned painter and needlepoint artist. Loving mother of John and James, Julia Strand and Weba Garretson. Dear sister of Julia. Cherished grandmother of Frances and Jeremy Garretson and Thomas Strand. Predeceased by her dear husbands, Summerfield Garretson in 1982, and Harold Turner in 2004. Memorial service to be announced at a later date. For information contact the Horton-Mathie Funeral Home of Greenport, LI.
