
        RUDNITSKY-Shana. National Credit Manager of Kem Manufacturing, beloved wife of Howard, retired, Senior Editor of Forbes magazine; mother of Jennifer Haftel and Daniel Rudnitsky, mother-in-law of Benjamin Haftel, loving grandmother of Jeremy, Joshua and Jack, passed away on January 11, 2003, at her home in Glen Rock, NJ. Her wit, creativity and compassion will be greatly missed by her family and friends. Services were held at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel in Paramus, NJ, on Sunday, January 12, 2003.
