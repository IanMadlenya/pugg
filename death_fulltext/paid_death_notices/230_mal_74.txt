
        LOVETT--Helen, 82, of Boynton Beach, FL. Formerly of Douglaston, NY, and Cranbury, NJ. Beloved wife of the late Martin Lovett; mother of Phyllis and Larry Mazer, and Ronald Lovett; grandmother of Erick Eidus and Zachary Mazer; sister of Ralph Stein and his wife Hannah, and the late Rubin Stein and his wife Jean. Memorial services held Monday in Florida with graveside services, 11 am, Tuesday, March 7, 2006, at King Solomon Memorial Park, Clifton, NJ. For information, Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, Boynton Beach, FL; 1-800-979-0599.
