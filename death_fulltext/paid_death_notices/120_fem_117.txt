
        SCHIEFFELIN-Annette Markoe. On January 16, 1997 in her 100th year. Of New York City and Ashville, Maine. Daughter of the late James and Annette B. Markoe. Devoted wife of the late William Jay Schieffelin, Jr. Loving mother of the late William Jay Schieffelin, III and Anne Louise Bradley. She is survived by 8 grandchildren, 15 great-grandchildren, 2 great-greatgrandchildren, a daughter-in-law Joy Schieffelin, son-in-law, Cameron Bradley and nephews John Sargent and Charles Devens. She was a member of the Colony Club, Thursday Evening Club, the Contemporary Club and the Garden Club of Maine. A memorial service to celebrate her life will be held at a later date. Burial is private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to charities of personal choice. ''She has deeply enriched our lives forever.''
