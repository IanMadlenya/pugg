
        SHAPIRO - Eitan Yitzchak. Our hearts are broken upon the untimely loss of our beloved son, brother, grandson, great-grandson, uncle, nephew, cousin and friend. He was our tower of strength and courage; our true hero. He will be sorely missed but never forgotten. The family
        SHAPIRO-Eitan Adam. The entire Jewish Theological Seminary community mourns the death of Eitan (Adam) Shapiro, 18, son of JTS Controller David Shapiro, following a courageous three-year battle with cancer. A graduate of Frisch Yeshiva High School in Paramus, NJ, with a beautiful Jewish neshama, he is also survived by his mother, Alona; sisters, Adena and Ayelet; brother, Eli; brotherin-law, Yechiel; nephew, Menahem Beryl; grand parents Malca & Howard Shapiro of Scranton and Fenya Wollman of Miami; greatgrandmother; and numerous aunts, uncles and cousins. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Chairman
