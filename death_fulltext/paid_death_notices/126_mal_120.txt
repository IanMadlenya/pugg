
        WELCH-Dr. William Joseph. On July 10, 1997. Beloved husband of Louise. Father of Anne Louise. Stepfather of Martha Welch de Llosa and Dr. Richard S. Crampton. Grandfather of Cordelia, Patricia Louise, Suzanna Louise, Thalia, Thomas William Butler and Luis Fernando. Uncle of Tony, Timothy, Martha and Victoria Welch. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Friday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral service St. Thomas Episcopal Church, 5th Avenue at 53rd Street, Saturday, July 12, 11:30 AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Physicians Home, 420 Lexington Ave., New York NY 10170.
        WELCH-Dr. William J. The directors and officers of the Emily Davie and Joseph S. Kornfeld Foundation note with deep sorrow the passing of our board member and mentor Dr. William J. Welch. Dr. Welch was a guiding light to us and to our founder, Mrs. Joseph Kornfeld. His wise counsel, clear intellect and gentle humor will be sorely missed. We extend our sympathies to Dr. Welch's wife and family. John F. White, Chairman Christopher C. Angell, President Fenella Rouse, Executive Director The Emily Davie & Joseph S. Kornfeld Foundation
