
        SILZER-Harriet Lamb at home in Bedford, NY on May 21st, 2000 after an extended illness.She was born in Brookline, Mass. the daughter of the late Pauline Ayers Lamb and Ernest Lamb. She is survived by her husband Parker W. Silzer and a brother David S. Lamb, two brothers, Ernest and Walter are deceased. Five of her six children with whom she shared great understanding and devotion were with her at her death. They are Chesley Welch, Betsy Anderson, Jennifer Clark, Christopher and Nicholas McLaren and Richard McLaren who predeceased her. She leaves 8 grandchildren Monica and Brittany McLaren, Hayden and Harriet Anderson, Rebecca and Samuel McLaren and Paxton and Chase Clark. In addition she is survived by four stepchildren to whom she was also devoted Dana, Julia, Elizabeth and Parker Silzer III. A memorial service will be held at St. Mathews Church in Bedford at 11:00AM, on Thursday, May 25th. She will be buried with her family in Bangor, Maine. In lieu of flowers please consider donations to A-Home 241 Lexington Avenue or Hospice Care 100 South Bedford Road both of Mt. Kisco, New York 10549.
