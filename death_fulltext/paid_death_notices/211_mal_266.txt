
        SHIRE--Maurice. 84, of Mt. Vernon. Beloved husband of 54 years, father, brother, grandfather, uncle, father-inlaw, cousin and friend. Born in Brooklyn to Susan and Hyman Shire in 1920, he graduated New York City College and Harvard Law School, and practiced law for over 40 years in the Empire State Building. Served in Army Air Corps, WWII, in the Pacific. Services Monday, at 1 pm, Weinstein Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave, Yonkers.
