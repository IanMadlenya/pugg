
        LEVINE-Jerome. Age 78. Died April 3, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Gladys. Devoted father of Phyllis and Charlie Ochs, Martin and Lois Levine, Lois and Mitchell Cohen, Cheryl and Ronnie Portnoy. Loving grandfather and great grandfather and loving companion of Marcia Levine. He will be sadly missed by all who knew him.
