
        KAYE-Leonard W. Age 73. On March 28, 1997 at his home in Palm City, FL., following a serious illness. A native of New York, he lived in Hartsdale, N.Y. before moving to Palm City eight years ago. He was pre-deceased by his wife Beatrice Kaye. He is survived by his children Michael, Susan, Andrew and Adam, their spouses, 8 grandchildren and his brother Frank Katz and his wife. Service on Sunday, March 30, 12 Noon, ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Those who wish may contribute to Hospice of Martin and Saint Lucy, POB 1168, Stuart, FL. 34995-1168.
