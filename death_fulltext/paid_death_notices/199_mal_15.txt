
        CURLEY--John Walton. 50, died at home in St. Louis on July 28, 2003 of lung cancer. He is survived by his children, Nina Galbraith Curley and James Ross Curley, his parents Ambassador Walter J.P. Curley and Mary Walton Curley, his brother Patrick of New York, his sister Margaret C. Wiles of North Yarmouth, ME., and his grandmother Rachel Mellon Walton of Pittsburgh. He was predeceased by his brother James. John Curley, a journalist is former City Editor of the St. Louis Post-Dispatch and previously a reporter with the Wall Street Journal. He attended the Buckley School in Manhattan, Phillips Academy in Andover, MA, the University of Grenoble in France, Yale University and Washington University in St. Louis. >>AD#
