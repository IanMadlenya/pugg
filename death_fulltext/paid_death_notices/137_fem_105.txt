
        FEINSETH-Miriam B. Passed away on June 11, 1998. Beloved mother of Debra Bodner and Ivan Feinseth, sister of Seymour Betman and mother-in-law of Andy Bodner. Director of Advertising and Communications for Hunter Douglas and a longtime member of the International Furnishings and Design Association, Miriam resided in Fort Lee, N.J. She was a congregant of the Fort Lee Jewish Community Center. Services to be held Sunday, June 14th at 1 PM. Bernheim Apter Goldsticker Suburban Funeral Chapel, 1600 Springfield Ave., Maplewood, N.J. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the American Cancer Society or the Hackensack Hospice Program in Hackensack, N.J.
