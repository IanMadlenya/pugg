
        IRELAND-R.L. III. The Board of Trustees, Trustees Emeriti and the entire Phillips Academy community in Andover, MA. express deepest sympathy at the death of R.L. Ireland III, Trustee Emeritus. A member of the Class of 1938, Tim was a Charter Trustee from 1960-1990 and founding chairman of the Andover Development Board. He will be missed.
        IRELAND-R.L. III. The Trustees of the Waterfowl Research Foundation, Inc. express their sadness upon the death of R.L. Ireland III, their friend and fellow Trustee. Tim Ireland was a knowledgeable supporter of waterfowl conservation for more than fifty years. He will be sorely missed.
