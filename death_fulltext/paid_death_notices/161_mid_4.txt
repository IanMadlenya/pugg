
        COHN-David W. June 6, 2000 at age 94. Beloved husband of Emmy-Lou for 64 years. Dearest and cherished father of Suzanne C. Simon and her husband David and the late Cecile Cohn. Loving grandfather of Deborah Wasser and her partner Susan Vaughan, David Wasser, Deirdre and Alex Silberman, and Keira and Julian Safir. Great-grandfather of Max, Savannah and Jack. He was a loving, kind and sweet man who will be missed by both his family and friends. Funeral private.
