
        LARSON-Alice. The Board of Directors, Administration and entire staff of Isabella Geriatric Center mourn the passing of Alice Larson, wife of Lawrence E. Larson, former President and Chief Executive Officer and currently emeritus Chair of of our Board. We recollect, with love and gratitude, her tireless volunteer efforts over many years which enriched the lives of Isabella's residents. She will be remembered with honor and affection by all who knew her. Richard B. Harding, Jr. Chair, Board of Directors Mark J. Kantor, President and Chief Executive Officer
