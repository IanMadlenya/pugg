
        BROMBERG--Aron A. On March 1, 2005. At home at the age of 77. A former partner, his career spanned more than 50 years with Stern Metals. Aron was a member of the 24-KT Club and sat on the boards of American ORT and NCCJ. A well-respected and much-loved member of the jewelry industry, Aron was gracious with his time and available when called upon. His many charities included ORT, NYU Tisch School of the Arts and Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS. Beloved friend to Dawn Levine, John Politi and Jeff and Joan Bernstein. Donations may be made in his name to the charities listed above. Memorial service Friday, March 18 at 11:15 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
