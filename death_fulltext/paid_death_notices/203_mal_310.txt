
        SCHNEIDER--Robert. 74, of Rye Brook, NY, on December 24. Beloved father of Gary Schneider and Linda Plank. Will be greatly missed by sister Lita, brother-in-law Bennett, brother Leonard, son-inlaw Steve, grandsons Christopher and Matthew, and nieces Karen and Diane, all the people at his barn where he rode his horse every weekend, and his coworkers at Sierra Assets, where he worked for 35 years. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mount Vernon, at 12 noon, December 30.
