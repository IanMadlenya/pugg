
        TURNESA-Michael C., 93, of Elmsford, NY. Died October 31, 2000 at Phelps Memorial Hospital, Sleepy Hollow, NY. Michael was born June 9, 1907 to Vitalie and Anna (nee Pascarella) Turnesa. Mr. Turnesa was the Golf Pro at Knollwood Country Club, Elmsford, NY from 1942 thru 1986 & the Director of Golf until present. He is predeceased by five brothers Frank, Phil, Douglas, James, Joseph Turnesa & one sister Rose Ercoli. Michael is survived by his wife Mary (nee Marinucci) Turnesa of Elmsford, NY. Two daughters Jean DeZalia of Austin, TX, Mary Ellen De Felice of Pleasantville, NY, two sons Michael of Rockville Centre, NY, James of Elmsford, NY, one brother Willie Turnesa of Elmsford, NY one sister Mildred Recchia of Elmsford NY. Grandfather of 8 great grandfather of 1. Reposing Hawthorne Funeral Home, Thursday and Friday 2-4 & 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday 10AM Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church, Elmsford, NY. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Westchester Golf Associatn Caddie Scholarship Fund, 49 Knollwood Rd., Elmsford, NY 10523 in his memory.
