
        ZIMMERMAN-Helen Blatte. On July 10, 2002, of Somers, NY, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Loving mother of Marc Blatte, David Blatte and Lisa Blatte Ennis. Grandmother of Hayley Blatte and Bryce Ennis. She was an avid tennis player and lover of classical music, mysteries, travel and a devotee of The New York Times crossword puzzles. Funeral service on Friday, July 12, at 12 Noon, Hawthorne Funeral Home, 21 Stevens Ave, Hawthorne, NY. Interment Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to UJA Planned Parenthood and Ballet Hispanico in Manhattan.
