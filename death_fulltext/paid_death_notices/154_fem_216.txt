
        KOOB-Julia Ashley (Nee Shanet). At age 85. As Julia Ashley she taught English at Woodrow Wilson Vocational High School, Erasmus High School, High School of Art and Design. A bright and warm loving person. She touched the minds and hearts of all who knew her. She was the wife of Harry, the mother of Priscilla and Joseph Erardi, Kathleen and Andrew Wittenborn. The sister of Howard and Bernice Shanet, the aunt of Laurence Shanet, the grandmother of Vincent & Deborah Erardi, Harry and Melissa Erardi, the great-grandmother of Branden and Joey Erardi. in lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate that donations be sent to The Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Medical Center, 1st Avenue at 16th Street, New York, New York 10003.
