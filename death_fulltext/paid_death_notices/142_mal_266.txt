
        CLIFFORD-Frank E. Died peacefully November 19, 1998 in Atlanta, GA. Born in Fairport, NY June 8, 1908. Resident for many years of Mountain Lakes, N.J. and Atlanta, GA. Survived by his wife Jane W. Clifford of Sebring, FL, his children Polly C. Skinner, Dean W. Clifford, Sarah C. Cook and Julie G. Clifford, numerous nieces, nephews, grandchildren and great grandchildren. Predeceased by his son David C. Clifford. He retired from E.I. duPont Nemours after 35 yrs. There will be no services. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
