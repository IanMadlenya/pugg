
        ROSS--Ann Gilmour died December 18, 2003 at Gladeview Health Care Center in Old Saybrook after a long illness. She is survived by her sister Mrs. Deane G. Marsh and her husband, the Rev. Donald Marsh of York, ME. She was the daughter of Bryant F. and Margurite Scarborough Gilmour. She was born in Brooklyn, NY, raised in Bayshore, NY, and had lived in Bellport, NY and New Canaan, CT before moving to Old Saybrook. She was a 1948 graduate of Wheaton College in Norton, MA. Mrs. Ross was a communicant of St. Ann's Episcopal Church, Old Lyme, CT. A memorial service will be held at 11:00 AM on Tuesday, December 23, 2003 in St. Ann's Church in Old Lyme. Donations may be made in her memory to St. Ann's Episcopal Church, 82 Shore Road, Old Lyme, CT 06371 or to the charity of one's choice.
