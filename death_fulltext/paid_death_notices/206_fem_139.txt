
        LINDSAY--Mary. The Board of Trustees and Staff of Theatre Development Fund mourn the passing of our admired colleague. From 1982-1998, Ms. Lindsay served as a valued trustee, and for 3 of those years she was the Vice President of TDF's Board. Her wise counsel and enthusiasm helped move the organization forward during a period of growth and change. We extend our sympathy to her family. John F. Breglio, Chairman Victoria Bailey, Exec Dir
