
        DOWNING-Dr. Edward F., neurological surgeon of Savannah, Georgia, died on December 10, 2000. A loyal alumnus and friend of the Neurological Institute of New York, Columbia University, he was revered in his community and respected nationally as an elegant and gifted neurosurgeon. Our sympathy is extended to his wife Helen and daughter Callie. He was a dear friend and will be sorely missed. The Department of Neurological Surgery, The Neurological Institute of New York, Columbia University
