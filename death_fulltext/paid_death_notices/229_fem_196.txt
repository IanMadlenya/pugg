
        FORSTER--Elizabeth E. (Lissa), 41. Died suddenly on February 20, 2006. Beloved daughter of Hamilton and Elizabeth Forster. Sister of Timothy H. Forster and Laura F. Bowman and in-laws of Diane and Peter. She was the bravest person we knew, coping with an always painful back and muscular dystrophy. She was a teacher of small children, and they adored her. Memorial service Thursday, March 2, 2006 at the Church of the Holy Trinity, 316 East 88th Street, NY, NY at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions to The Millbrook School, School Rd., Millbrook, NY 12545, or Habitat for Humanity, 121 Habitat St., Americus, GA 31709 would be appreciated.
