
        BERMAN-Sidney. 79. Of Laurelton and Lynbrook, N.Y. Died in Tamarac, FL. on October 18, 1997 after a long illness. He was the loving and cherished husband of 56 years to Helen Denowitz Berman; beloved father and father-in-law of Madelynn Berman Heintz and Nick Davy, Robert and Susan Berman and Mark and Donna Berman Saks; the adored grandfather of Jarrad, Ryan, Matthew and Sam and the devoted brother of Laura Gottlieb, Frieda Handelman and Dorothy Fried. Funeral service 2PM Tuesday, October 21, Gutterman's, Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I. Contributions may be made to The Crohn's and Colitis Foundation, 585 Stuart Ave., Garden City, N.Y. 11530 or the United Jewish Appeal.
