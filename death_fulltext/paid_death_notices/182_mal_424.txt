
        DILLON-Thomas Francis, M.D. Died March 29, 2002, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center of pneumonia and related complications of prostate cancer. He was 77. Born 1925, in Raquette Lake, NY. Graduated from Georgetown University, School of Medicine, in 1947. Served in the US Navy 1943-1951. Spent his professional career as a Professor of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Cornell Medical Center and Columbia University, College of Physicians and Surgeons. He also was the past president of the New York Obstetrical Society and the American Fertility Society. He retired in 1987 to East Hampton, NY. He was an avid golfer and fisherman. His beloved wife Jane and three children, Dr. Peter Dillon of Hershey, PA, Pamela Shea of Jackson, WY, Ann P. Dillon of St. Louis, MO, and four grandchildren survive him. A private service will be held in Raquette Lake, NY, this summer. Family and friends are making contributions to the Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center.
