
        LUKASHOK-Lillian G. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Loving mother of Martin and the late E. David. Wonderful grandmother to Bill and Janet and devoted greatgrandmother to David and Owen. Fond sister of Frances Horowitz and the late Meyer Ginsburg. Funeral service Monday, 11:15AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        LUKASHOK-Lillian. The National Women's Division of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of our dear friend, member of both our Board of Directors and New York Chapter Executive Committee, who for many years spearheaded our efforts in behalf of the College's D. Samuel Gottesman Library, where she and her late husband, Joseph, established a major reference collection in cardiology. To her son, Martin, and to her entire family, we offer our deepest condolences. Nancy Oelbaum President National Women's Division Albert Einstein College of Medicine Judy R. Rosenberg Chairperson Executive Committee
