
        BERKMAN-Lars. The entire staff of Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette mourn the passing of Mr. Lars Berkman, Senior Vice President and Director of high yield investments at DLJ Investment Management Corp. Mr. Berkman was one of the leading portfolio managers in the country, and in a short period of time made significant contributions to the growth of DLJ. He was a warm and caring colleague and will be greatly missed. A memorial service will be held on September 9, 1998 at 10:30 A.M. at King Juan Carlos I Center at NYU, 53 Washington Square South. Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette
