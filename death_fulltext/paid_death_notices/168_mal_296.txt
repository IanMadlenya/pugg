
        CHESSIN-Max, age 87, Friday, January 19, 2001, of Fairlawn, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Millie, loving father of Carolyn and her husband Wolf, Laurel and her husband David. Adored grandfather of Zachery and his wife Eileen, Joshua and his wife Mimi, Jason and Lanie. Dear brother of Lil, Betty, Louis and Marion. A graduate of Brooklyn College, he later became an outstanding chemist in the field of pharmacology. Was a captain in the US Army during World War II, and served his country with honor. His ever present good humor and love will be missed by all.
        CHESSIN-Max. Dear brother, kind and caring we will miss you. Lil, Betty, Louis and Marion.
