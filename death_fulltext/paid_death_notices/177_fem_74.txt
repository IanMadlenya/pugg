
        BERGMANN - Patricia Beverly Bull. Age 78, of Hopkinton, Massachusetts, formerly of Manhasset, NY, died Sunday in Leonard Morse Hospital, Natick, MA, as a result of injuries she sustained in an automobile accident September 21, in Wellesley, MA. Born in Brooklyn, NY, she was raised in West Orange, NJ. She married Theodore Bergmann, an advertising executive and television producer, in 1946. She resided in Manhasset from 1949 until 1976, moving to Pacific Palisades, CA. She moved to Hopkinton in 1988. She was a member of the Hopkinton Women's Club. She leaves four sons, T. Douglass Bergmann of Los Angeles, Donald G. Bergmann of Medway, MA, David L. Bergmann of Prides Crossing, MA, and J. Derek Bergmann of Hopkinton; two daughters, Laura B. Weinman of Pacific Palisades, CA, and Lisa J. Bergmann of Iowa City, IA; and a brother, William L. Bull of Florida; and eleven grandchildren. Services are pending. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Baypath Humane Society.
