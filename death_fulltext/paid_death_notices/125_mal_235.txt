
        MOSKOWITZ-David Michael, age 38 of Short Hills, NJ and Quogue, NY, suddenly while bicycling on June 21, 1997. Devoted husband of Jacqueline (nee Stevens), adoring father of Alexander and Matthew, loving son of Reba and Ted Moskowitz, beloved brother of Robert Moskowitz and Alice Freidus, cherished son-in-law of Edward and Lillian Stevens, treasured brother-in-law of Susan and Robert Tanne, Jeanette Moskowitz and Gene Freidus, adored uncle of Erica, Marc, Jill, Claire, Rachel and Joshua. Vice-President of Computer Systems at J.P. Morgan. A great friend and terrific athlete with a sharp mind and a wonderful sense of humor, he will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral services at Congregation B'nai Jeshurun, 1025 South Orange Ave., Short Hills, NJ, Tuesday at 10:15 A.M. The family will be observing shiva at the home of Susan and Robert Tanne.
        MOSKOWITZ-David. We mourn the sudden passing of our dear friend. He will be remembered for his sweetness, intelligence and wonderful sense of humor. Our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Jacqui, his adored sons, Alex and Matt and his entire family. Andrea, Jim and Alexander Wallick
