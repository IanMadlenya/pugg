
        BERGER-Samuel S. 91, died on September 11, 2001. Husband of his beloved Violet. Father of Roland, Barbara and Wendy. Grandfather of Douglas, Elizabeth, Debora, Randy, Jennifer, Suzanne, Rebecca and Alison, and eleven great-grandchildren. Former resident of New Rochelle and White Plains. Owner of First American Art Flowers. Graduate of NYU Law School Class of 1932 and MBA from Iona College. He is also survived by his sister Goldie Lowell. Graveside services at Hungarian Union Field Cemetery.
