
        WAXMAN-Vivian K. November 13, 1997, of Coconut Creek, Florida (formerly of Great Neck, New York). Beloved wife of Herbert for fifty eight years, devoted mother of daughter Jill Polymeropoulos and son Jonathon (Jon), loving grandmother of Marc, cherished sister-in-law of Juliette Sack and Cecille Ellner, David Litan and Harry Buchman. She was one of the world's special people, doer of good deeds and a blessing to all who knew her. No one who ever met her, will ever forget her. She will live forever in our hearts.
