
        MITTNACHT--Richard. Born on September 30, 1925 in Stamford, CT, died on January 1, 2007. Mr. Mittnacht is survived by his fiancee, Miss L. Kim Jones and his children, Richard A. Mittnacht, Jr. and Katherine C. Mittnacht. Educated at Kent School and Princeton University, Mr. Mittnacht started working for Johnson and Higgins on May 26, 1948. Throughout his career with Johnson and Higgins, he lent tradition, high standards of business practice, ethics and integrity to deals made and promises kept to his colleagues and clients worldwide. Mr. Mittnacht retired from Johnson and Higgins in December 1982 as Senior Vice President and Director. Mr. Mittnacht was a member of the Adios Golf Club. Contributions in his honor may be made to Palm Beach Infectious Disease Institute, Gertrude E. Skelly Charitable Foundation or Kent School. For further information, contact Erik E. Joh, Esquire at 561-276-1008.
