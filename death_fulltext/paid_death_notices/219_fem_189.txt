
        DELMAN--Geraldine Evelyn, 72, on April 26, 2005 of Boca Raton, FL. Loving wife of the late Perry Delman for over 50 years. Devoted mother of Robert and Scott. Cherished mother - in - law of Lynn. Adored grandmother of Edward, Jeffrey, Jonathan and Rebecca. She left us far too soon, and her family and friends will miss her grace, wit and kindness. Funeral services Sunset Chapel, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, New York, Friday, April 29, 2005, at 10AM. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY.
        DELMAN -- Geraldine. The Officers, Board of Directors, Members and Staff of Engineers Country Club mourn the passing of our beloved member, Geraldine Delman, on April 26, 2005, and extend our deepest sympathy to her sons Robert and Scott and their families. Les Genatt, President
