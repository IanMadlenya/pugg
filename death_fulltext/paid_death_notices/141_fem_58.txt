
        POLLACK-Mollie G., Z''L, 83, of Tamarac, FL, died in Hartford on October 3, 1998, the 14th day of Tishrei, 5759, in the middle of the high holiday season. Beloved wife, for 44 years, of the late Samuel B. Pollack who predeceased her in 1983. Survived by Ann and Edward Meyer of New York and Elliott and Eileen Pollack of Hartford and grandchildren Jill Pollack and Jessica and Mark Mindich, all of New York. She also leaves a sister Selma Katz of Delray, Florida, and brother Jack C. Groter of Boca Raton, FL. Graveside services will be at New Montefiore Cemetery on Wellwood Ave., Farmingdale, New York on Tuesday, October 6, 1998 at 2 P.M. May her memory be for a blessing.
