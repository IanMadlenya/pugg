
        DOERFLER-William G. The Stephen Wise Free Synagogue and its Women's Organization record with profound sorrow the passing of a longtime benefactor and the cherished husband of many years to Annette (Toddy), who has served with great distinction as a Board member and Officer of the Women's Organization. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Board of Trustees Women's Organization Gary Bretton-Granatoor, Rabbi Ellen Stettner, Cantor
