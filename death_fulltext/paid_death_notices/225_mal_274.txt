
        MARCHETTI--Augustus A. 89. Of Metuchen, formerly of Bellrose, Queens, died Monday in Edison, NJ. He was an Army veteran and a retired attorney for the NYC D.A.'s office and a retired U.S. Attorney. Visitation Wednesday 24 & 7-9 pm at Costello-Runyon Funeral Home, Metuchen, NJ. Services Thursday 10am at F.H. Interment in Hillside Cemetery, Metuchen.
