
        YOUNG--Patricia Burrage. Of New York City, September 3, 2005. Devoted mother of Missy Rosengarten of Carmel, N.Y., Julie O'Connor of Swampscott, MA., Steven Young of Rutland, VT. and Chris Young of Carmel, N.Y. Sister to June Jenkins and Anne Alford, both of Fort Worth, Texas; former wife of the late Robert F. Young of Hastings-On-Hudson, N.Y. Survived by 8 grandchildren, many loving nieces, nephews and countless cherished friends. Memorial Service at The Colony Club, 564 Park Avenue, New York on Monday, September 12 at 11 AM. Donations in her memory to Putnam County Humane Society, PO Box 297, Carmel, N.Y. 10512.
