
        SITT--Eddie M. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Eddie M. Sitt, whose historic and lifelong dedication to our Syrian Sephardic Campaign strengthened the Jewish community and set a standard of leadership that lives on in the efforts of his family. We extend our deepest sympathies to Eddie's beloved wife Gloria; his children Rochelle, Morris, Jeffery, and David; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
