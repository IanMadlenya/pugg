
        GARBER-Harry D. The Community Preservation Corporation mourns the passing of its long time Director and past Chairman Harry D. Garber. He was a visionary in understanding how private industry might address social problems, and helped lead our company to expand our low and moderate income housing efforts. His social judgement, commitment and friendship, which will continue to inspire us, will be sorely missed. The Board of Directors The Community Preservation Corporation John A. Somers, Chairman Michael D. Lappin, President
