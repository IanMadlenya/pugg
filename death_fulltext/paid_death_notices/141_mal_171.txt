
        SPIEGEL-Leo A., M.D.. The Officers and Members of The New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the death of their respected and beloved colleague, Dr. Leo Spiegel. In his nearly five decades as a member, Dr. Spiegel served as President of both the Institute and Society, was a training and supervising analyst in child and adult psychoanalysis and actively involved in the affairs of the Institute and Society. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his daughter, Wendy Kesselman. Eslee Samberg, MD President/Institute Lester Schwartz, MD President/Society SPIEGEL-Leo A. M.D.. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of our professor emeritus, an esteemed psychiatrist and renowned teacher who was associated with our medical school since its inception in 1955. A distinguished psychoanalytic scholar, he made major contributions to the understanding of the psychology of adolescents. We extend our deepest condolences to his daughter, Wendy Kesselman. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D., Dean
