
        PIRONE-Dr. Dominick Joseph. Of Mt. Vernon, NY, on August 23. For 25 years he was a beloved faculty member in the Biology Department of Manhattan College and the College of Mt. St. Vincent in Riverdale. Dominick is survived by his wife Joan, two sons Joseph and Robert, a sister Mary Grace Barrett, a sister-in-law & a brotherin-law, Sandra and Richard. Friends may call at the Yannantuono Funeral Home, 584 Gramatan Avenue, Mt. Vernon, NY, on Saturday and Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Ursula's Church, Monday, 10 AM.
