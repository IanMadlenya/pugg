
        McKONE -- Elvira ''Polly'' Pope. 92, born on September 27, 1911, in Manlius, NY. Passed away on March 12, 2004, at her home in Somers, NY. Polly was the daughter of the late Colonel Allan M. Pope and Elvira D. Pope. Polly is survived by her stepdaughter Darby Downey of New York City, her nephew Allan M. Pope II of Bakersfield, CA, and her niece Nancy P. Spivak of San Jose, CA. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, April 3, at 10:30 AM, at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to a charity of one's own choice.
