
        NICHOLSON--Stevens G., Jr. Graduate of Columbia University Law School, practicing attorney in NYC, resident of New Rochelle for 38 years. In Chapel Hill, NC November 14, 73 years old. Beloved husband of Ruth, beloved father of Abbye, Sherry, Jeffrey, Andrew and Keith, beloved brother of Mary, George and Christopher, beloved grandfather of Jesse, Emily, Novella, Andrew and Gloria, beloved uncle, beloved friend to so many. He leaves us all with so many joyful memories. Memorial service on Sunday, November 21, 2 PM at Community Unitarian Church, White Plains, NY. Donations may be made to NYU Brain Injury Day Program send to Dr. Ben-Yishay, 660 First Ave, 3rd Flr, NYC 10016.
