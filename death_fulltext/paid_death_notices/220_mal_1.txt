
        ANDERSON--Herman Howard (Andy), age 84, died April 14, 2005 in New York at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was a veteran of WW II stationed in the South Pacific. After discharge he returned home to finish college. He moved to New York and was employed by American Airlines until his retirement. He is survived by Donald Hess of New York, his best friend and a sister-inlaw, Anne Harris Anderson and two nephews of Decatur, Alabama.
