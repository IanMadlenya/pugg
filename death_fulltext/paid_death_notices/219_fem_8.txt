
        KRAMON--Hermine Messinger. April 1, 2005, at 83. Wife of the late Roy. Mother of Grant (and Penny), Clifford (and Sami), Glenn (and Alison), Gordon (and Heidi). Grandmother of Douglas (and Michelle), Joshua (and Susan), Caitlin. Great-grandmother of Maya. An intelligent, elegant, youthful, modest, considerate listener who knew how to make people feel good, and whose first thoughts in the morning and last at night were of her children. Service Tuesday, 1:30, Westchester Reform Temple, Scarsdale.
