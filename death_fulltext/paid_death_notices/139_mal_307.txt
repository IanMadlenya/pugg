
        POWELL-Lewis F. Jr, United States Supreme Court Justice (ret), died at his home in Richmond on Tuesday, August 25, 1998. Justice Powell was born in Suffolk, Virginia on September 19, 1907. He was the son of the late Mary Lewis Gwathmey and Louis Franklin Powell. His wife of 60 years, Josephine Rucker Powell, died in the summer of 1996. Justice Powell is survived by his children, Josephine and Richard Smith of Houston, Pendleton and Robert Bowen of Whidbey Island, Washington, Molly and Christopher Sumner of Salt Lake City, Lewis and Lisa Powell of Richmond; by grandchildren, Elliott and Denise Smith, Quincy Smith, Lycia Carmody and Rocky Fried, Nathaniel and Vickie Carmody, Ryland Sumner, Kendall Sumner, and Emily, Hannah and Lewis Powell; by his great-grandson, Zachary Smith; and by his sister Eleanor Powell Dewey of Williamsburg. Funeral services will be held at Grace Covenant Presbyterian Church, 1627 Monument Avenue, at 11:30AM on Monday, August 31st. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Lewis F. Powell Jr. Legal Services Fellowship at the University of Virginia Law School Foundation, Charlottesville, Virginia 22903.
