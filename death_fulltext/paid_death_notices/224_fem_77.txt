
        FALES--Katharine Ladd, on September 9, 2005, of Bedminster, NJ. Wife of Haliburton Fales. Mother of Nancy Fales Garrett of Delhi, NY and NY, NY, Hal Fales of Leeds, Mass, Priscilla Fales of Cambridge, Mass, Lucy Fales Evans of Denver, CO, and William E.L. Fales of Carbondale, CO. Sister of Nancy L. Crocker of New Canaan, CT, William Ladd, of Massachusetts. Grandmother of nine and great-grandmother of two. Memorial Service Wednesday, September 14, 2005 at 3PM in St. Luke's Episcopal Church, Gladstone. In lieu of flowers, donations to the St. Luke's Episcopal Church Main St., Gladstone, NJ, 07934 or the Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 101 E. 70th St., NY, NY.
        $(6$)FALES--Katharine Ladd. The Board of Directors, Clinical and Administrative Staff of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York mourn the loss of Katharine Fales, a beloved Director for 25 years and benefactor of the agency's home care and public health services. Her interest in patient care improved the health and wellbeing of countless New Yorkers. Her commitment to high quality, compassionate health care will be remembered always. We extend our profound sympathy to her family. Douglas D. Broadwater Chairman of the Board Carol Raphael President and CEO
