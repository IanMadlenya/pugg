
        MENKES-Stasia. Age 94, died at her home on August 30, 2000. She was born in Stryj, Poland. Before WW II Stasia Menkes studied at at the Sorbonne in Paris. While in Paris she befriended and became a part of the circle of the avant guard painters and sculptors such as Chagall, Lipchitz, Soutine, Pasein, Zadkin and Sigmund Menkes. She fell in love with the painter Sigmund Menkes whom she marrried. She and her husband Sigmund Menkes escaped the Nazis and immigrated to the USA. Stasia Menkes was a striking beauty. She became the model for many of her husband's paintings. She was a talented fashion designer. She was a generous hostess. To her last days she displayed her sharpness, intelligence, and awareness over her circumstances. She is survived by her nephew Prof. Michele Jamiolkowski, his wife, Jadzia and their two daughters, Caterina and Maddalena. A private memorial service by the surviving family members and friends to follow.
