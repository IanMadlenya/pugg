
        SURY--Stanley J., 80, a resident of Ormond Beach, FL. and former longtime resident of the Town of Rye, NY died suddenly at his home on April 19, 2003. He was born and raised in Port Chester, NY and graduated from Port Chester High School in 1940. He was a veteran of World War II, serving in the United States Army in Panama. He was a longtime member of the Washington Engine & Hose Co. #
