
        PLITT-George. 87, pioneer developer of the cat litter and wild bird food industries, died on February 16 in West Palm Beach, FL. His wife, Rose, children, Arthur and Miriam, Arlene and Richard Kossoff, Jane Plitt and James Bruen, grandchildren, Hugh, Seth, Drew, Laura, Brett and Beth and great-grandchild, Dean mourn the loss of their unique patriarch. Funeral service Thursday, February 20th, at 11 AM, ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
