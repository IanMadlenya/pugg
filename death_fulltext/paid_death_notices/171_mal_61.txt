
        McKIERNAN-Rev. Raymond D. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the parochial vicar of Holy Name Church, 245 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn, on April 1. Reposing at Smith Funeral Home, 248 Prospect Park West, Wednesday April 4 from 2 to 5PM. Mass of Transferral on Wednesday April 4 at 7:30PM. Funeral Mass on Thursday April 5 at 10:30AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
