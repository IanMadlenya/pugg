
        KRUMBEIN-Aaron D. The First Chairman of the Association of Orthodox Jewish Scientists (AOJS) Board of Governors, passed away in Shaare Zedek Hospital in Jerusalem, Israel on July 10, 1998. He was a graduate student in physics at New York University and a former Nahal Soreg Nuclear Research Center physicist. Born on April 6, 1921, Aaron was a valuable member of every community in which he resided over his 77 year lifespan.
