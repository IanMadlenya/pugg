
        NORDLINGER-Alan. 87. Died Sunday, April 18, 1999. Leaves behind his wife, Annetta, and her family; his children, Steve, Joan and James; their spouses, Carol, Steve and Michelle; his grandchildren, Alejandro, Jacob and Samuel; and a niece, Yvonne, and her husband, Charles Cannon. Respected attorney and partner at Guggenheimer & Untermeyer, valued for his integrity and insight. Actively participated in the New York Real Estate Board and the New York Bar Association. Past President of the Shorefront YM-YWHA of Brighton-Manhattan Beach. Past Vice President, Board of Education, East Williston School District, and Past President of North Shore Community Arts Center. A veteran of World War II. With love. We are proud of you.
