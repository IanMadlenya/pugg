
        GELB--Lillian Berger. Passed in Chicago, July 16, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Harold. Loving mother of Steven. Mother-in-law of Rita. Proud grandmother of Alexis. Her family and friends will always remember her unique wisdom and insights, and most of all, her wonderful personality and enthusiastic approach to life. Expressions of sympathy may be donated to the Council for Jewish Elderly for Adult Day Services, Attn: Judy Siegel, 3003 West Touhy Avenue, Chicago, IL. 60645. Services on Friday, July 22, 2:15 P.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
