
        RELIN--Irene. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind are deeply saddened by the death of Irene Relin, a member of The Guild's Board of Trustees, former President of the Women's Division, longtime supporter and frequent volunteer. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Pauline Raiff, Bd. Chairman Alan R. Morse, Pres. & CEO
