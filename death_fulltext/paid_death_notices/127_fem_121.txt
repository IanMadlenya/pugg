
        WERNER-Alexandra Kliatshco, August 18, 1997, age 97. Beloved wife of the late Abraham Werner. Survived by her children, Julia and Peter Stern of Newtown, CT., Gordon Werner, Santa Barbara, CA., and daughter-in-law Marie Anne Werner, grandchildren: Joe, Rachel and Bill Stern, Gordon and Duncan Werner, and her devoted friend Lottye Johnson. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York Times Neediest Cases, PO Box 5193, GPO New York, New York 10087.
