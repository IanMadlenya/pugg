
        BOWERSOCK - Christine Rose (nee Welch), Sept. 1, 1999 at the age of 33 years. Beloved wife of the late Jeffrey M. Bowersock. Dear daughter of Kate Welch (fiance Stan Kuil) and Gene Welch. Dear sister of Steve, Owen, Dean, Gena, Mike, Erin, Eamon, and Tom Welch. Granddaughter of Marie Welch and J. Vincent Dunn. Daughter-in-law of Wallace and Kathleen Bowersock. Funeral Services will be Monday at 10:30 A.M. at The Victory Memorial Chapel at St. John's Military Academy in Delafield, Wisconsin. Friends may call on Sunday from 4 to 8 P.M. at the funeral home and on Monday at The Victory Memorial Chapel, from 9:30 A.M. until the time of service.
