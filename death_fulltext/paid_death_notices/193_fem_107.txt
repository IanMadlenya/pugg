
        KOBART-Ruth. Singer and actress, who performed on Broadway, opera, theatre, films and TV, died December 14, 2002 in San Francisco. Early on, she sang mezzo soprano roles with The Lemonade, NBC, NYC Operas. On Broadway, her performance in ''A Funny Thing Happened On The Way To The Forum'' was Tony-nominated. She also played Miss Jones in both the Broadway musical and film of ''How To Success In Business Without Even Trying.'' A founding member of San Francisco's American Conservatory Theatre, she worked for many years in the California theatre, film and TV community. She is survived by a brother, Howard, of Minneapolis, and nieces and nephews. A public memorial will be held at The Geary Theatre in San Francisco on Sunday, March 9, 2003 at 7 P.M.
