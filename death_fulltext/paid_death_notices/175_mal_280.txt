
        HENRY-Carl. Americans for a Safe Israel/AFSI mourns the passing of our esteemed boardmember. He served as a colonel in the 80th Infantry Division of Patton's Third Army, graduated from Harvard, and created a successful business. His later years were marked by his devotion to Torah study and to Israel's survival. AFSI is proud to have had such a distinguished and ardent supporter. Herbert Zweibon, Chairman, AFSI
        HENRY-Carl. The National Jewish Outreach Program (NJOP) mourns the passing of its devoted friend and supporter, Carl (Chaim) Henry. A very successful businessman, Carl devoted himself to Torah in his later years and became an eloquent spokesman for the Jewish people and NJOP. Condolences to the entire family. Yehi Zichro Baruch. Rabbi Ephraim Z. Buchwald, Dir.
