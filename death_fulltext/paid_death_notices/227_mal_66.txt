
        MULLEN--Benjamin Joseph, 95, on December 3, 2005. Husband of the late Catherine. Father of Doran, Joseph, Michael and Thomas. He was a resident of Palm Beach, FL for30 years, where he was an active member of the Pundits, the Beach Club, the Old Guard, the English-speaking Union, and the Society of the Four Arts. Also mourned by daughters - in - law Nancy (Sheridan), Nancy (Donnoli), and grandchildren Christopher, Stephen, Katherine, and Samantha. Funeral services at St. Mary's Church, Winchester, MA, December 10th. Memorial service in Palm Beach at later date. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Edward's Church -- Benjamin Mullen Fund, 144 N. County Rd., Palm Beach, FL 33480.
