
        LIPKIN-Robert H., on November 14, 2000, when Robert lost his courageous battle with cancer. Robert is survived by his wife Ellen, his daughter Beth, and his brothers Warren and Perry and their families. Prior to his illness, Robert was involved in corporate business finance and consulting in the New York area for his entire career. He was a Vietnam veteran and took pride in having served his country. He was much loved and will be missed by many. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, November 16, 1 PM, at ''Gutterman's Inc'', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, L.I. Shiva will be observed at the Lipkin home.
