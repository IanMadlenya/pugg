
        BRONSON-Emerick R. Died on Oct. 15, at home, in Sag Harbor, NY, of a heart attack. Graduate of the Merchant Marine Academy, served in WW II. His career as an internatinal fashion photographer was based in Paris and NY. Predeceased by his wife Arden Catlin. Survived by his sisters Frances Fulton, Anna Luciana and Mary Schafer. Also survived by a number of nieces and nephews. Remembered with much affection by his many friends who shared his wide interests. Services were held and his ashes will be buried in the family plot in Pittsburgh, PA.
