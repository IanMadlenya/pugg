
        ARMSTRONG - John K. The Governors, medical staff and administration of Lawrence Hospital are deeply saddened by the death of our Legal Counsel, John K. Armstrong. He was a trusted advisor and friend of the Hospital. His sound guidance and intelligence, combined with his understanding and concern for the health care in our community were deeply valued and he will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Mieke and his entire family. Roger G. Dvorak, President George E. Austin, Chairman, Board of Governors
