
        CRIPPEN--Davis A. Died suddenly on September 2 at the age of 75. He is survived by his wife Cynthia, his sons Alex and Tom, and his grandchildren Daniel and Eli. A service will be held at 3 PM on Saturday, September 17 at Christ Church, 416 Valentine Avenue, Sparkill, NY. He loved books and the opera. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Piermont Library Building Fund, 153 Hudson Terrace, Piermont, NY 10968, or the the Metropolitan Opera in NYC.
