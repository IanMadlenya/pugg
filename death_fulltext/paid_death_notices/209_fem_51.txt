
        MARMELSTEIN -- Gladys (Serulneck) of Natick, MA formerly of Brooklyn, NY entered into rest June 6, 2004. Dear mother of Barbara & Susan. Loving sister of Harry Serulneck of Framingham, MA, Kenneth Serulneck of Staten Island, NY & Jacqueline Schoenbloom of NYC. Cherished aunt of many nieces and nephews. She was a special friend to many. Graveside services will be held Wednesday, June 9 at 1:00 pm at Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Memorial observance will be at the home of Fern Serrell through Wednesday evening only. Expressions of sympathy in her memory may be made to the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, 220 N. Main St., Natick, MA 01760. For information Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, Brookline, MA.
