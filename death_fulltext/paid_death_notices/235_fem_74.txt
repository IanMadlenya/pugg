
        SAPHIRE--Rose died peacefully on August 7th in Palm Harbor, Fl; 4 months short of her 100th birthday. Born December 9th, 1906 in Bayonne, NJ, daughter of the late Reuben and Freda Rockoff, eldest sister of her late siblings Louis, Abe and Ruth, beloved wife of the late E. William, adored mother of Larry and Phyllis (Alman), wonderful grandmother and greatgrandmother of 17 children, she will be sorely missed and fondly remembered.
