
        BELSON-Kathy Plaszner. On November 26, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Michael, cherished mother of Jaclyn and Jonathan. Devoted daughter of Ervin and Terry Plaszner. Adored sister of Suzanne Weinstein. Beloved niece of Michael Plassner. Loving daughter-in-law of Jerome and Maxine Belson. Services Friday 11AM, from Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 West 83 St, NY, NY.
        BELSON-Kathy Plaszner. Congregation Rodeph Sholom and Rodeph Sholom School mourn the passing of our beloved, long-term member, Kathy Plaszner Belson. We will always remember her passionate love for her family, abiding Jewish commitment, and indefatigable courage. We send deepest condolences to her children, Jaclyn and Jonathan, her parents, Ervin and Therese, her sister Suzanne, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Jack B. Levitt, President Irwin Shlacter, Headmaster
        BELSON-Kathy (Plaszner). A gracious and talented lady who knew far too much pain in her eight year struggle with cancer. We are everlastingly grateful to her friends and associates for their caring devotion and love to Kathy and her daughter Jackie and son Jonathan. May Kathy finally experience the peace and comfort that was denied to her in these last pain filled months. Maxine and Jerome Belson.
