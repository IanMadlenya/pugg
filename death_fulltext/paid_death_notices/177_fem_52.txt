
        STAVELY-Patricia Weiland. Age 55, suddenly on October 5, 2001, in Cambridge, MA. Adoring, caring mother of Jonathan. Deeply loved, greatly respected daughter of Sue and Ed Weiland. Loving sister and sister-in-law of Nancy and Tom Carpenter. Special friend of Carmen. Beloved by her greataunt, aunts, uncles, cousins and friends. We shall always remember her wonderful way with words, her creative style, quick wit and her easy laugh. God grant you peace our darling Pat.
