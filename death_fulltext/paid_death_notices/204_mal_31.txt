
        CAREY--James J., 62, of Princeton, NJ, died January 1, 2004 at his residence surrounded by his family. Senior Vice President of Citigroup where he worked for 43 years. Survived by his wife Jacqueline, mother Wilma, sister Linda, daughter Heather, step-daughter Christine and husband David, step-sons Patrick and wife Kristin and Lowell. Donations to Healing the Children of Greater Philadelphia Area or Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
