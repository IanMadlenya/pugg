
        ROEDER--Eileen. In her 83rd year on October 27, 2006 of Glen Head formerly of Roslyn Heights, NY. Beloved wife of the late William A. Roeder. Loving mother of Jane Roeder, John Roeder (Michelle), Mary Anne Roeder, Nancy Zolezzi (Joe) and Carol Jahnke (Dudley). Cherished grandmother of 12 grandchildren. Devoted aunt, sister-in-law and friend. Eileen was a respected colleague to many in the L.I. real estate community. Her generous spirit and warm hugs will be missed by all. Visitation will be at the Weigand Bros. Funeral Home, 49 Hillside Avenue, Williston Park, NY Sunday and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 10:00, at St. Aidan's Church. Interment at Nassau Knolls Cemetery.
