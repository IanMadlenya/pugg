
        RAMCHANDANI-Atma. The family of Atma C. Ramchandani celebrates his life and deeply mourns his passing on January 10, 1999 at age 95. Beloved husband of the late Savitri and dear father of Sunny and Prem. Grandfather of Ariel and Talia. He will be long remembered for his contribution to the building of the Institute of Electrical and Telecommunication Engineers and the introduction of TV into India through his leadership at All India Radio. He lived a full life and he will be fondly remembered by all who knew him.
