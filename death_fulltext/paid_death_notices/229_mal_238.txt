
        SAKOFSKY - Murray. Of Brooklyn, NY, died peacefully in Fort Lauderdale, FL, on Friday, February 17, at age 91. He is survived by his wife of 63 years, Isabelle, his children Judi (Alan) Englander, Richard (Sharon) Sakofsky and by his grandchildren, Jason, Andrew and Amanda Englander, and Evan and Lauren Sakofsky. He was a gentle and kind man who will be missed by all.
