
        MATIS-Rosalie Metzger. Of NY, died peacefully on February 18, 2002 at age 78. Beloved wife of the late Jacob David Matis, beloved and devoted mother of Nancy Dreyfuss, loving and adored grandmother of Jessica Matis and Leah Dreyfuss. She is also survived by a son, George Matis. You fought a valiant battle and are now at peace. We love you and we will miss your strength. Services Wednesday 11:15 AM, February 20 at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
