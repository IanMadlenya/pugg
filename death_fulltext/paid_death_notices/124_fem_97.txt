
        ROSS-Annette Burr. Died at the age of 102 in Sarasota, Florida, on May 11. Formerly of Westport, CT. and NYC. Widow of Gustave Ross. Survived by her daughter, Sheilah Gignoux, son, Christopher, granddaughters, Amanda Cross, Lisa Pownall-Gray, and Victoria Green; grandson Nicholas Ross and six great grandchildren. Service in Sarasota on November 9.
