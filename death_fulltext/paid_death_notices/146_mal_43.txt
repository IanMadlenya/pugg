
        GOLDWATER-John. The members of the Board of Directors of the New York Society for the Deaf mourn the passing of our dear friend and former president, John Goldwater, who provided us with the benefits of his leadership and counsel for over 30 years. His memory and legacy will always form a part of the fabric of the Society. To his wife, Gloria, his sons and grandchildren, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Stephen E. Adler, Chairman Paul L. Kohnstamm, President Pearl I. Johnson, Exec. Director
        GOLDWATER-John. The ADL Women's Division extends its heartfelt sympathies to our Honorary Chair, Gloria Goldwater, on the death of her beloved husband. Audrey Magid, Chair Rhoda W. Korman, Director
