
        SHAPIRO-Lillian. The Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union mourns the loss of Lillian Shapiro, beloved mother of our President, Lenore Miller, and Barbara Sachs; beloved grandmother of Jessica Miller and Richard Sachs. She was a great friend of our Union and we will miss her. Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union, AFL-CIO, CLC Stuart Appelbaum, Secretary-Treasurer
