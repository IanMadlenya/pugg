
        KONOPKO--Lea. The Board of the John F. Kennedy, Jr. Institute for Worker Education expresses our condolences on the passing of Lea Konopko, devoted and loving mother of Deborah Konopko, a key participant in the founding of the Institute. Lea Konopko's continued triumphs over adversity were an inspiration to all who knew her. We extend our sympathy to the entire Konopko family.
