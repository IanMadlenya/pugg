
        BERKON--Samuel F., 101 years (3/3/1905 -6/12/2006). The rock and patriarch of our close family. Beloved husband of the late Sara. Father of Martin, Marilyn and Frederick. Grandfather of Jonathan and Benjamin. Daughtersin-law, Eileen and Susan. Famed New York City trial lawyer for over half a century, he was a man of extraordinary intelligence, wit, spirit, compassion and generosity. He was deeply loved by all of us and we will miss you so much Dad. You will be in our hearts forever. Services, Thursday, June 15, 11:15am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street, Amsterdam Avenue, Manhattan.
