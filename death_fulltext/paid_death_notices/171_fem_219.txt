
        ROGOW-Helen. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) mourn the passing of Helen Rogow of Boca Raton, Florida, and Avon, Conn, an Honorary Member of the Technion International Board of Governors, a Life Trustee of the National Board of Regents, and a member of the Palm Beach Board of Directors. With her late husband Louis, she was a Technion Guardian, having reached the highest level of commitment to the Technion, Israel and the ATS. They supported undergraduate life, teaching and research at the Technion through establishment of the Helen and Louis B. RogowPalm Beach Dormitory, the Louis B. and Helen Rogow Fellowship Fund and the Louis Rogow Aeronautical Research Center. Our sincere condolences go to her sister Sylvia Bean, nephews Bruce Rogow and Gary Greenberg and the entire Rogow Family. American Society For TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Lawrence S. Jackier, President Irving A. Shephard, Chair Melvyn H. Bloom, Executive Vice President Paul Wieseneck, President Palm Beach Chapter
