
        SOBEL-Irene (Rusty). Of Woodmere, New York. Died on March 3. Beloved wife the late Paul Sobel. Loving mother of Barbara Goetz Gelb (Joe) and Richard Sobel. Adored grandmother of Andrew Goetz, Lucy Leung, Jane Goetz and Lindsay Sobel. Irene was a career woman most of her life and a community volunteer in her later years. She was strong, feisty and beautiful throughout. Graveside services on March 5 at 10:15am at Mt. Lebanon cemetery. For directions to the cemetery please call Joseph Gelb at 516374-1387.
