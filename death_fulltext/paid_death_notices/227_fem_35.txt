
        LITTENBERG--Roslyn Halper. Of Manhattan, NY. Died December 4, 2005 at age 88. Beloved wife of the late Eli, devoted mother of Ronnie and Laurence, grandmother of Liza, Michael, and Jeff. Mourned also by sister Naomi, brothers Norman and Ted, sisters-in-law, Roz and Carol, son-in-law Phil, daughter-in-law Marcia, loving nieces and nephews, and dear friends. Social worker, feminist, counselor to all who knew her, she will be deeply missed. Visiting Tuesday 7-9 PM and services Wednesday 1PM, both at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
