
        SELTZER--David J. Died suddenly on May 23, 2004. Beloved husband and partner of Nancy Derene Seltzer. Devoted father of James and Peter. Devoted son of Philip Seltzer and the late Rose Seltzer. Brother of Eric. David was a former Broadway producer, Republican advisor, and successful real estate developer. Funeral Services will be held in Philadelphia.
        SELTZER--David. The Board of Trustees, faculty, staff and students of Metropolitan College of New York mourn the loss of David Seltzer, a member of our Board. We will miss his valuable counsel and support in our efforts to transform lives through education. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Nancy, and his family. John S. Rodgers, Chairman of the Board Stephen R. Greenwald, President Metropolitan College of NY
