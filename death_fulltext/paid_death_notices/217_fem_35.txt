
        BRIEGER--Anny (nee Birnbaum). Peacefully in her 96th year at her home in Manhattan. Adored wife of the late Clarence. Beloved twin sister of the late Edith Oblatt and late brother - in - law Dr. George Oblatt. Born in Antwerp, Belgium. Recording Secretary of her Barnard College class of 1928. Active member of Temple Israel. Member since 1941 and longtime summer weekend resident of the Fairview Country Club where she and her late husband were frequently champion golfers. Longstanding involvement with and support for the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Mount Sinai Medical Center. Her extended family of several generations and her many friends will deeply miss her wisdom, intellect and active energy. Services on Friday, February 4th at 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
        BRIEGER--Anny. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member, Anny Brieger. Anny had been a member since 1941. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to her family. Earle Yaffa, President Dale Winston, Secretary
