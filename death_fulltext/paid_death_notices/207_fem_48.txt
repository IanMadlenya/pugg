
        LAMSTEIN--Pauline, 94, of Lauderhill, FL, passed away on Tuesday, April 6, 2004. She was the beloved wife of the late Dr. Jay Lamstein. A wonderful and loving mother to Allan (Susan) and the late Judith (Leonard) Charney. Warm and cherished grandmother to Joanne, Cathy and Johanna. Devoted sister to Shirley (Jerry) Friedman and the late Leroy Scheiner and Frances Levy. Adored aunt of many loving nieces and nephews. A most treasured friend to many. The memory of this most gracious and wonderful lady will warm the hearts of her loving family and friends forever and ever.
