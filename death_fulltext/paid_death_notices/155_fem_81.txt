
        BROWN-Marion Rhodes. Of Ardsley-on-Hudson, died November 30, 1999 at her home. She was born April 8, 1909 in New York City. She received her B.A. from Barnard College in 1930 and then taught history at Walton High School. She married the late John Albert Brown in 1943. She obtained her Ph.D. at Teachers College, Columbia University in 1958 and became a Professor of Educational Psychology at the City College of New York. She was dedicated in volunteering time and energy to the World Education Fellowship, the American Association of University Women and the United Nations. She cared about issues of protecting the environment and working for peace among all people of the world. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Val and David Brown-Tillotson of Anchorage, Alaska. Memorial service will be Saturday, December 11 at 12 noon at Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Avenue (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY.
