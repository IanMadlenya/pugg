
        GORBATY-Aaron, Rabbi. Of Baltimore, MD. Formerly of Boro Park, Brooklyn. The Board of Trustees and Members of Temple Emanu-El of Boro Park mourn the loss of our dear friend and Rabbi Emeritus. Rabbi Gorbaty was highly esteemed for the level of Talmudic Scholarship he possessed, and loved for his warmth, humility and sense of humor. His memory, and the impact he made upon all our lives will remain with us always. We exted our heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Temple Emanu-El of Boro Park Allen M. Shapiro, President
