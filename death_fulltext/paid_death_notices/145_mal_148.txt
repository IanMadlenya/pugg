
        AUERBACH-Harry M., 88, on February 9, 1999. Adored and adoring husband of Gladys for 58 years. Loving father of Kenneth & Susan Auerbach & Jane & Neal Gelfand. Proud grandfather of Philip, Peter, Alexandra and Laura. Cherished brother of Helene Feuereisen. His strength and wisdom illuminated our lives.
