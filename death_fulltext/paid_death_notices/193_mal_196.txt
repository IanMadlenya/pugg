
        ESKAY - Richard Spencer. Beloved husband of Marion Miller. Loving father of Marjorie, Linda, and Julie; grandfather to seven grandsons. President of RSK Associates, a representative of Specialty Planners; Producer, with his wife, of Pathways to Children's Literature. Proud and active alumnus of Cornell where he met Marion and shared 50 years of marriage. Bringing joy and humor to others, his spirit remains in us all.
