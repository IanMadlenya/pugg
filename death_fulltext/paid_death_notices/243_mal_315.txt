
        GURIAN--Elias on April 25, 2007 in his 91st year. Devoted and beloved husband and friend of Lil (Leah) for 65 years. Father of Stephen, Bonny and Marvin Rosen, Donald and Karen Gurian. Grandfather of Alyson and Steven Justman, Melanie and Robert Gallucci, Justin Gurian, Ariel Gurian. Greatgrandfather to Jordyn, Alexa and Tyler. Founder of Gurian Fabrics, Inc. Inventor of the Fabrak. Served with the US Air Force during WWII. He was a member of The American Arbitration Association and was active in many Philanthropies including UJA/ Federation, Lubavitch Youth, State of Israel Bonds. Former Chairman of the Board of Congregation Beth Shalom. Long time resident of Lawrence, NY, and then Boca Raton, FL. He will be missed by the numerous people whose lives he enhanced by virtue of his great sense of humor, his kindness and his loving generosity. Graveside service at Old Montefiore Cemetery in Springfield Gardens, Queens, at 2pm on Friday, April 27. He wanted to be remembered, not mourned, since he had a most satisfying life.
