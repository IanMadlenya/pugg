
        PUTNAM-William A. III. Age 78, of Falmouth, MA, died of Alzheimer's disease on March 22, at the Cape Cod Hospital. He was the husband of Felicity N. (Koller) Putnam. Mr. Putnam was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, and spent his summers in the Adirondacks, where his family had a home in Ausable Club, NY. William attended the Berkshire School in Massachusetts and Cornell University in New York. He served in the US Army during the Korean War and then moved to Falmouth. In 1955 her opened Putnam Book and Music Shop in Falmouth. He owned and operated the business until his retirement in 1976. William was past president of Falmouth Rotary and was an avid golfer. He was a pioneer of Falmouth recycling. Besides his wife, Mr. Putnam is survived by his stepson John Rumery of Malden, MA, and his stepdaughter Sarah Heighton of Amherst, New Hampshire, and two grandchildren. A private memorial service will be held at a later date. William will be dearly missed by his family and friends. For information contact Chapman Cole and Gleason Funeral Home, Falmouth.
