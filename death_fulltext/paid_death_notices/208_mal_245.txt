
        RAK--Mayer. On May 20, 2004, in his 93rd year. Devoted husband of the late Guta, beloved father of Eda. Deeply mourned by his son-in-law Richard Neugebauer, granddaughters Sabina and Lila, family and friends. He was a respected Yiddish journalist and a commited socialist whose life and work embodied the values and ideals of the Eastern European Jewish world of his birth. Services Sunday, May 23, 10 a.m., Plaza Community Chapel, 91 Street and Amsterdam Ave.
