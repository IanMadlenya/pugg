
        MORRIS--Aaron. Passed away on January 29th, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Norma for 57 years. Loving father of Hal and his wife Lorie and the late Robert. Devoted grandfather of Rebeca. Brother of Paul and the late Simon. Aaron served as a Major in the US Army during WWII. Aaron will forever be ''39''.
