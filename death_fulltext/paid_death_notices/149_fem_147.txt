
        GALANTY-Lee, on June 15, 1999, died peacefully at the Jewish Home and Hospital where she was cared for by a devoted staff. Beloved wife of the late Sol Galanty. Dear mother of Helene Sencer and devoted mother-in-law of the late Wally Sencer. Adored sister of Bernice and Irving Weinflasch and the late Pearl Kornblum. She was a cherished aunt and had a devoted and loving family and friends and companion, Nana. Graveside service today, June 17, 12:30PM, at Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
