
        HEIT-Albert. Distinguished entertainment attorney, on November 21, 2000. A 1934 graduate of Cornell Law School and an avid alumni supporter, Mr. Heit handled business affairs for film producer Sam Spiegel for 35 years. He is survived by his beloved wife, Natalie, brother, Murray, his niece, Berit, nephew, Jay. Donations can be sent to The South Salem Library, South Salem, NY. Services Friday, 1 PM November 24 at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave
