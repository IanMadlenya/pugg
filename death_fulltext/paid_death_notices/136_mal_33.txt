
        ULANSKY-Gene. Graduate of Columbia University, Ph.D. Stanford University, writer, editor, teacher, counselor, advisor, mentor. Active in Crossroads Africa. Died Thursday, April 23, 1998 in his Berkeley, CA home at age sixty. Survived by loving wife Susan Clark, daughter Kate Ulansky, sister Nina Kohnstamm, brothers & sisters-in-law, nieces & nephews and thousands of beloved students. Donations in his memory to ''Kate's College Fund'', C/O Barbara Clark, 2928 Pine Street, Berkeley, CA. 94705.
