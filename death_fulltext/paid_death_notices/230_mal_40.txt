
        WHITE--William R. Mutual of America, its Board of Directors, together with all of its officers and employees, mourn the passing of our former Senior Vice President, William R. White. For close to twenty years he helped shape the image of the Company by developing communications and presentation policies adhered to till this day. We will miss his friendly laugh and his insightful opinions. Thomas J. Moran Chairman of the Board, President and CEO Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
