
        COCKCROFT-Eva S. Muralist, painter and writer, with more than thirty murals in New York, New Jersey, California, Nicaragua and Berlin, died on April 1, in Los Angeles, of breast cancer, at age 62. The co-author of ''Towards a People's Art,'' her writing also appeared in ''Art Forum'' and ''Art in America''. Eva is survived by her father, Dr. Otto Sperling; three sons, Ben, Eric and Peter; her companion Bruce Snyder; and seven grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Social and Public Art Resource Center, 685 Venice Boulevard, Venice, CA, on May 1, 1999. For information call 323-256-1853.
