
        McGUINNESS-Edward. Peacefully on August 4, 2001. Beloved husband of Soledad and the late Dorothy. Loving father of Patrick, Susan, Doreen, Edward and the late Thomas. Dear stepfather of Ralph. Cherished grandfather of 10. Edward, a proud veteran of WWII, was a Sergeant in the U.S. Marine Corps. Among his many decorations, he received the Purple Heart for injuries sustained on the battlefield. He worked as a Representative of the NY Typographical Union #
