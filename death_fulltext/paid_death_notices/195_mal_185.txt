
        SCHOENWETTER-Sumner Edward, on April 15th, of lymphoma. Husband of Susan Lehman, brother of Cynthia Sobel, brother-in-law of Gerald Sobel, uncle to Adam and Melissa Sobel. Artist, musician and eternal scholar. As Sumner Crane, his composition ''Jon Gavanti'' and Mars group's recordings stand as monuments of No-Wave music. Service Monday, April 21, 4-6 PM at Redden Funeral Home, 325 West 14 Street.
