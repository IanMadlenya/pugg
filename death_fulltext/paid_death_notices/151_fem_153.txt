
        LANDAU-Beverly Hirsch. Of New York City and Southampton, New York. Passed away peacefully on August 17, 1999. Fashion Buyer, Theatrical Producer, Literary Agent, and most recently a Real Estate Broker. She will be deeply missed by her three children Todd, Joan Ogilvie and Larry, her mother Irene Hirsch Levine and sister Eleanor H. Goldner, seven grandchildren, and hundreds who were truly her friend. A very special lady to all who knew and loved her. Memorial services Friday, August 20th. from 2pm-3pm, followed by a reception from 3pm5pm at The Chapel, at 777 United Nations Plaza, (44 St & 1st Ave.).
