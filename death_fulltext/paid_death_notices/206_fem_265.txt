
        MALICK--Blossom Katz. Of Boynton Beach, FL died from injuries sustained in an automobile accident on March 15. Formerly of Woodmere, NY, she is survived by her wonderful husband Bill, beloved son and daughter-in-law Alan and Judy Katz, treasured grandsons Steven and Randy of Princeton Junction, NJ. She is also survived by her brother and sister-in-law Merv and Lucille Shorr of Howard Beach, NY as well as nieces Elaine Miller, Diane Farino, nephew Steven Shorr and four grand-nephews. She joins in heaven her late husband Dr. Sidney Katz and daughter Wendy Katz.
