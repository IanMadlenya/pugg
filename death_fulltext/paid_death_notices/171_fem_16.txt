
        FRIEDMAN-Reginia H., M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni record with sorrow the untimely death of Dr. Reginia Friedman, School of Medicine graduate of the Class of 1983. An outstanding young physician, she will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to the entire family. Avi Barbasch, M.D., President Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D. Vice Pres. Maria L. Padilla, M.D. Secretary William G. Stein, M.D. Treasurer
