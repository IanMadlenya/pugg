
        HEROLD--Bernard. On November 5, 2005, at age 84. Loving husband of Tressa. Devoted father of Paula and Larry and daughter-in-law Michelle. Adoring grandfather of Spencer. He founded the NYSE firm of Bernard Herold and Company Inc, and never stopped going to work every day. He was a born teacher. At the seminars he conducted for CCNY graduate students he would be enthusiastically applauded. He treasured the thank you letters they wrote. He could sell enthusiasm. He had a zest for life and lived it to the fullest. He lived large. He liked to have fun. Services will be on Monday, November 7, at 1 pm, at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Cedar Park, and Beth El Cemetaries, Westwood, NJ. Donations in his name can be made to the City College of New York.
