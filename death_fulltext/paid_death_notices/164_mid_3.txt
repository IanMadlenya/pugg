
        MINTKESKI-Jessie Cleverdon. Of Manhasset, NY, born 1914 in New York City, died on August 30, 2000. Beloved wife of Eugene Mintkeski. Loving mother of Walter Mintkeski and his wife Vicki. Devoted grandmother of Tyler and Charles. Mrs. Mintkeski graduated from Wellesley College in 1935 and is a member of Wellesley on Long Island. Contributions in her memory may be made to Wellesley College, Wellesley, Mass. Private memorial service was held at the family home.
