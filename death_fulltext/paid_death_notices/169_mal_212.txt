
        ENNIS-Dr. Charles E. Music Professor Emeritus of Long Island University, former Organist and Choir Director of All Angels Episcopal Church, N.Y. and Peekskill Presbyterian Church. Died February 18, 2001 after a brief illness. He leaves his beloved wife Agnes, daughter Susan Compton, grandchildren Caitlin and Gavin, brother Fowler Ennis, family and friends. Services and burial in Vandalia, MO. Remember him for the joy of singing he brought to so many.
