
        GROSS-Jeffrey Shaine, who departed this life on May 1st, in his 53rd year in Indianapolis, Indiana. Father of Shaine, Shannon and Sarah. Son of Jack Gross and the late Rita B. Gross. Brother of Stephen Gross, and Deborah Green. A memorial service will be announced at a later date at Kol Ami Temple, 252 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, New York 10606.
