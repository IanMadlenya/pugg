
        RUDNER-Gladys Muriel, on August 29, 2001, at 86 years. Beloved and loving wife of Albert for 66 years. Cherished mother of Jane and Reese Sherman and Judy and Jerry Kessel. Adored grandmother of Cathy and Jon Fiebach, Lynn M. Sherman, Danny and Kirstin Stiebel and Susan and Les Morgenstein. Dear great-grandmother of Charles, Joshua, Aidan, James and Matthew. ''To live in the heart is never to be forgotten.'' Graveside service on Sunday, September 2, at 11AM, at Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, L.I. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        RUDNER-Gladys Muriel. Congretation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Gladys Muriel Rudner. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
