
        LINCOLN-Peter G., Clinical psychologist in private practice in Manhattan and active member of the New York State Psychological Assn. Formerly with St. LukesRoosevelt Hospital Center and Lincoln Institute for Psychotherapy. Graduate of Princeton University, Class of 1965, and recipient of a doctorate in clinical psychology from NYU Graduate School of Arts and Science. Father of Justin Lincoln, Sloatsburg, NY, and Kara Lincoln, Seattle, WA; son of Jerome and Ray Lincoln, Elkins Park, PA; brother of Joseph Lincoln, Penn Valley, PA and Lisa Lincoln, NYC; life partner of Mary McCorry, NYC. Predeceased by his son, Kai Lincoln. We mourn his passing on August 6, 2000. All who knew him are invited to a memorial service that will be held on Saturday, September 9, 2000 at 1:00 P.M. in the auditorium of The New York Society for Ethical Culture at 2 West 64th Street. Memorial contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave., NY, NY 10021, toward the devel. of a hospice for its long-term patients.
