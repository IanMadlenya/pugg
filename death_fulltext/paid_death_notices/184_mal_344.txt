
        KELLY-Francis Brandt. Of Beverly Hills, Florida. Born in New York City died on Saturday, May 18, 2002. Former employee of The New York Times until 1989. He served in the United States Army Air Force during the Korean War. Survivors are his wife Ann, three daughters, Judith Kelly, Barbara Braithwaite and Muriel KellyCharboneau and granddaughter, Ariana Braithwaite.
