
        MERSON-Beatrice (Billie), 80 years old, died February 26, 1997two days short of 60th wedding anniversary. Formerly of Bronxville, New York, she recently moved to Goshen, NY. Former proprieter of BAM Liquors of Mt. Vernon, NY, she is survived by husband, Carl, two daughters, Ann Bialek and Miriam Shapiro, 11 grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by her son, Robert. Funeral services at Riverside Chapel, Mount Vernon, New York, on February 27 at 1 PM.
