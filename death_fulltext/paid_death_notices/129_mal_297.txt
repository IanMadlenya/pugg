
        LIBERMAN-George, M.D., 91, passed away Friday, October 24, 1997, in Bal Harbour, FL. A dedicated physician, he practiced family medicine in Brooklyn from 1930 to 1989. He was elected ''Family Doctor of the Year'' in 1985. He was the beloved husband of Pearl. Cherished father of Ellen (David) Goldschild and Dr. David (Marie) Liberman. Loving grandfather of Michael and Noah Liberman. ''We will miss you always.'' Expressions of sympathy may be made in Dr. Liberman's name to the Wein Center at Mount Sinai Miami Beach, FL, or Catholic Hospice, Miami, FL. There will be a visitation at Riverside Memorial Chapel (Brooklyn) on Monday, October 27, 1997, from 7-9 PM. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, October 28, 1997, at 12 noon (at the chapel). Interment will follow at the family plot in Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. For further information contact Riverside Memorial Chapels, 312 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn, NY, 718-633-0400.
