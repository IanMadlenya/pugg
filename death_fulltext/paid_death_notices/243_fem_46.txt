
        KEHOE--Veronica Lally, 64, peacefully at home after a two year illness she bore with great grace, strength and dignity and at the same moment the Easter Vigil service at St. Ignatius Loyola Church she loved so much for so many years was beginning. She is survived by her loving husband, John P. (Jack) Kehoe, her devoted sister, Maureen Lally, her beloved daughter, Allise Dickson, Allises husband, Bradley, and her adored granddaughter of three months, Lilly Grace, for whose birth and first months of magical life God spared her to enjoy, and by Jacks four children and five grandchildren. An alumna of the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, summa cum laude graduate at Fordham Universitys College at Lincoln Center and a member of the WFUV-FM Advisory Board, Veronica, an AFTRA and SAG member, actor and theatrical producer, for 25 years supported and encouraged new American plays and playwrights through the activities of her not-for-profit Gypsy Road Company in association with 33 of the leading writing programs among Americas colleges and an Advisory Board chaired originally by Ms. Helen Hayes. Family visiting at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue (at East 81st Street), New York, NY 10028, (212) 288-3500. Monday, April 9th, 7 9 PM, Tuesday April 10th, 2 5 PM and 7 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Saint Ignatius Loyola RC Church, 980 Park Avenue (East 84th Street), Wednesday April 11th, 10:00 AM. Internment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In Lieu of flowers, the family welcomes donations to Fordham University Theater Department, or the Marian Francis Harriott S.C. Scholarship at College of Mount Saint Vincent.
