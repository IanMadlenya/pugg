
        LATEINER -- Col. Robert, M.D. In his 96th year. Beloved husband of Dorothy (nee Kasenetz) for 66 years. Cherished father of Lloyd Lateiner, M.D. and Lynne Manning (Burton). Loving grandfather of Patrick, Mitchel and Dagny Maidman and Eve and Robert Lateiner. Adoring great-grandfather of Allison, Beatrice, Harrison, Julia and Jonathan Maidman. Services Tuesday, May 17 at 10:00 A.M. at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY. Shiva at the Manning home, Manhattan, Wednesday and Thursday May 18th & 19th 4-8 PM.
        LATEINER--Col. Robert, M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved father of our friend and supporter, Lynne Manning, an honorary president of both Einstein's National Women's Division and its New York Chapter. Dr. Lateiner also was the grandfather of Mitchel & Arlene Maidman, who serve, respectively, on the boards of our Men's Division and the New York Chapter of our Women's Division. To Lynne and to the entire family, we offer our sincere condolences. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Albert Einstein College of Medicine Ira M.
