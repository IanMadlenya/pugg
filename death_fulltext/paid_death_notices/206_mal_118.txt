
        KLEIN--Jack. 95, recently of Amityville, died peacefully at his home with his children at his side. Devoted father to Joan Tell, Neal and wife Cyla, his grandchildren David Reader, Cheryl and Don Freund, Jeffrey and Cara, Michael, Jonathan and Alan, and his great-grandchildren, Jacqui, Kelly, Stephen, Phoebe and Ross. He lived a life devoted to his family, giving help to whoever needed it. First in his family to graduate from City College at night, a proud CPA. He touched and shaped all our lives.
