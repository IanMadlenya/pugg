
        RAINEY--John Crews. Architect, died August 10, in NYC, at age 69. He is survived by his wife Mary Jane Norcross, brother-in-law John Norcross of Chicago, and step-daughters Cynthia Penney and Virginia Tougas of NYC. There will be no memorial service. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Northwestern University, the National High School Institute, 617 Noyes St, Evanston, IL 60208.
