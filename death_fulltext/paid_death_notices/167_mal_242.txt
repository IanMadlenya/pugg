
        MANZELLA-Onofrio H. (Freddie). On December 14, 2000. Predeceased by his first wife, Mary Litrell. Survived by second wife, Christiane, and daughter Lenore and her husband William Frick. Mr. Manzella enjoyed a successful career as a violinist and conductor. He toured in the United States and South America. He was Concertmaster and Associate Conductor for many Broadway shows. Service Tuesday at 11:00 AM at St. Clement's Church, 423 West 46 St. Donations to the American Heart Association would be appreciated.
