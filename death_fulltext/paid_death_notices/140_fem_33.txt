
        DANIELL-Tatiana (Tania) Long. In Ottawa, on Friday, September 4, 1998. Widow of Francis Raymond Daniell, New York Times correspondent for 38 years in the U.S., Britain, Europe and Canada, deceased in 1969. Mother of Robert M. Gray, deceased in 1981. Herself, a newspaper correspondent first with the New York Herald Tribune, then for 25 years with the New York Times. Interment of cremated remains will be beside her husband in the Daniell family plot in the East Lawn Cemetery, East Haven, CT, USA.
