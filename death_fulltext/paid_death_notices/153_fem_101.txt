
        FISCH-Miriam. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Miriam Fisch matriarch of her family, mother of Elizabeth Wilf and mother-in-law of our dedicated Treasurer Joseph Wilf, founders and scholarship benefactors of the Rabbi Arthur Schneier Park East Day School. To Elizabeth, her brother Erwin, children, grandchildren and great grandchildren, our deepest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
