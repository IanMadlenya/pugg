
        JESSE-Mary Jane. The Physicians and Staff of the Department of Pediatrics, Columbia University, College of Physicians & Surgeons and the Babies Hospital Alumni Association wish to express our heartfelt sorrow and condolences to the family of Mary Jane Jesse, MD. Following a successful career on Madison Avenue, Dr. Jesse graduated from College of Physicians & Surgeons and completed her residency and a fellowship in cardiology from Babies Hospital. She was awarded the Babies Hospital Distinguished Alumnus Award in 1992. Mary Jane's brilliance has stimulated and brought pleasure to those of her friends from Babies Hospital who had the pleasure of being her friend and colleague. John M. Driscoll, Jr., MD Reuben S. Carpentier Professor Chairman, Dept. of Pediatrics Dorothy Levine, MD President Babies Hospital Alumni Assoc.
