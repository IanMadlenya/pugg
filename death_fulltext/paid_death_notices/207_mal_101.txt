
        WEITZNER--Morton Leonard. Passed away suddenly April 7, 2004. Adored husband of Carol R. Weitzner, loving father of Cherie Weitzner Acierno and Gregg Harrison Weitzner, beloved grandfather of Jane Celine Acierno, devoted brother of Dorothea M. Weitzner, dear father-in-law of Lou Acierno, loving brother-in-law of Judy and Gary Richter, and dear uncle of Stacy Richter Mercugliano and Wendy Samuels. Mr. Weitzner attended the University of Pennsylvania and graduated from New York University. He was the owner and President of Henry M. Weitzner Construction and Lehigh Management Corporation and the holder of substanial real estate equities. He served on the Board of Directors of Congregation Ansche Chesed, the Board of Columbia Grammar and Preparatory School, and on the Board of the 510 Park Avenue Cooperative. He was a founding member of Rolling Hills Country Club in Wilton, Connecticut, where he had an 8 handicap. For visiting hours friends may call Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St., (212)-288-3500. Services Sunday, April 11th, 11 AM at Temple Emanu-El Fifth Ave., at 65th St. Entombment at Ferncliff Mausoleum, Hartsdale, NY.
        WEITZNER--Morton L. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Morton Leonard Weitzner. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
        WEITZNER--Morton. Rolling Hills Country Club expresses its deepest sympathy to the Weitzner family as we mourn the loss of one of our beloved founding members.
