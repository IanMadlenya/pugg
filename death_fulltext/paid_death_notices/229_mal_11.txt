
        PAIK--Nam June. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art notes with great sadness the passing of Nam June Paik. A beloved artist in our Permanent Collection, Nam June was a foremost pioneer of video art and remained one of the medium's most influential figures throughout his long and prolific career. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to his family and friends. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Robert J. Hurst, President Adam D. Weinberg, Director and the entire staff of the Whitney Museum
        $(6$)PAIK--Nam June. EAI is deeply saddened at the loss of Nam June Paik, who held a special place in the history of art and in our hearts. His vision and legacy will live on in the art he created and the people he inspired. Our thoughts are with his wife, Shigeko Kubota.
