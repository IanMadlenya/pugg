
        SCHLANGER-Esther B., 91, died peacefully in her home on March 17, 2001. ''Petey'' was an exceptional teacher of biology at many New York City high schools. After retirement from the classroom, she worked regularly with birds at the American Museum of Natural History and was an active member of the League of Women Voters. She leaves her son Jeff, daughter-in-law Anne, grandsons Raphael and Jordan, great-grandchildren Mikolo and Zoe, and nieces and nephews around the country, along with friends and colleagues grateful to have shared her vivid clarity, throrough care for everything in life and her sustaining love.
