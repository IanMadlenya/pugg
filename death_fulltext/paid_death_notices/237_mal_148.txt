
        FARBER--Saul J., M.D. The Bellevue family mourns the loss of Dr. Saul Farber. His dedication to our historic instutition was legendary. He was an architect in the making of a doctor who understood that education is the hallmark of clinical excellence. Generations of physicians gained from his leadership as they learned the art of healing at Bellevue. The Administrative and Clinical Staff of Bellevue Hospital Center
