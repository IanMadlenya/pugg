
        THOMPSON-Joseph J. Of Rockville Centre, New York on March 23, 2002, in his 85th year. Beloved husband of Geraldine M. (Gerry) for sixty years. Loving father of Justin, Kevin, Dennis, Rory and Joanne. Beloved fatherin-law of Bonnie, Kathy, Ronnie, Pattie & Roger. Dear grandfather of fourteen grandchildren and two great grandchildren. He was employed at The New York Times from June 1, 1947 to April 1, 1985. Past Captain of the Fire Police Squad, and a 48 year member of the Eureka Hook, Ladder and Bucket Co. #
