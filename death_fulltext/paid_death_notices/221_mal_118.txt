
        COOPER--Norman S., MD. Died June 11, 2005, at age 84. He is survived by wife Evelyn & son Jonathan, and his sister Rita with her spouse Stanley. He was a devoted father and husband as well as a conscientious member of the community. Dr. Cooper spent many years as a Professor of Pathology at NYU School of Medicine and was also the Director of Pathology at the VA Hospital on 23rd St. Funeral services on Tuesday, June 14 at 10 AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
