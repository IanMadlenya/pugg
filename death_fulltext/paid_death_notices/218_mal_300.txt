
        HERGEN--James G. One of the greatest men of The Greatest Generation on March 24, 2005. Loving husband of the late Audrey. Devoted father of his daughters Ellen and Tracey, and his sons Jim, George, Bob and Jeff. Beloved grandfather of Adrienne, Drew, James, Connor, Audrey, Kathryn and Nicole. His gentle and generous life was an inspiration to all who knew him. Born in 1917, he came of age in the Depression, served as an officer in the U.S. Army during WW II, retired as Vice President of NBC and as a senior executive of Ringling Bros. Circus World in FL. We shall not see his like again. Visiting Monday 2-5 & 7-9 PM at Denis S. O'Connor Funeral Home, 91-05 Beach Channel Drive, Rockaway Beach, New York. Funeral Mass 9:45 AM at St. Francis De Sales Church. Interment Calverton National Cemetery.
