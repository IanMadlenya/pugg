
        VELLA-Doris W. On June 16, 1998 of Rockville Centre, New York. The first woman from the Village of Rockville Centre, New York to volunteer for the U.S. Army. During World War II she attained the rank of corporal. Beloved wife of Armand J. Vella. Dear mother of Andrea V. Brennan (John), James Vella (Kathleen), Lisa V. Iantosca (Alan), & the late Gary. Cherished sister of Lenore Goldsmith. Devoted grandmother of Matthew (Christine), Emily, Mark, Matthew, Armand, Charlie, Billy. Fond great grandmother of Rebecca. Reposing at Macken Mortuary, Inc, 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Centre, New York, today 2-4, 7-9 P.M. Funeral Service Thursday 11 A.M., at St. Agnes Cathedral, Rockville Centre, NY. Interment Cemetery of the Holy Rood, Westbury, New York. Should friends desire contributions may be sent to Ronald McDonald House of Long Island 267-07, 76 Avenue, New Hyde Park, New York 11040.
