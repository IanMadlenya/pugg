
        RUBENSTEIN-Dorothy F. State of Israel Bonds mourns the passing of Dorothy F. Rubenstein of Columbus, Ohio, a devoted leader and a passionate supporter of Israel. She was a member of the National Executive Board of Israel Bonds, a past chair of the Central Ohio Israel Bonds Women's Division & recipient of the prestigious Golda Meir Award. Her untiring work for Bonds helped to raise millions of dollars in investment capital to build Israel's economy. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband, Rabbi Samuel Rubenstein, rabbi emeritus at Agudas Achim Synagogue in Columbus, Ohio, and to her entire family. Gideon Patt, President & CEO Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board Irwin Hochberg, National Campaign Chairman Bobbie Goldstein, National Associate Campaign Chairman Jay Schottenstein, Central Ohio General Chairman
