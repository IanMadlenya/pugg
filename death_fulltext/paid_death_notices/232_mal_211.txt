
        GRAZIO--Alan Roy. On May 16, 2006, after a courageous fight against lung cancer, Alan Grazio, aged 66, passed away peacefully in his home in Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Everyone who knew Alan knew him to be kind and giving with a great sense of humor and an infectious laugh. He enjoyed life to the fullest and his passing will leave a large void in the hearts of the family members and friends whose lives he touched. Alan's memory will be long cherished by his wife Joan, his mother Sue, his daughter Lisa & her husband John, his daughter Diana, his grandchildren Evan and Alexandra, his sister Cecily, his niece and nephews, aunts and uncles, cousins and many friends. A memorial service celebrating Alan's life will be held at 11:00 AM, on Wednesday, June 7, 2006, at Saint Matthew the Apostle Parish, 335 Dover Chester Road, Randolph, NJ 07869, 973584-1101. Those unable to attend may send condolences to the grazios@adelphia.net to be shared with the rest of Alan's family. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests a donation to Hospice of Palm Beach County or to The Sylvester Cancer Center at the University of Miami. For more information contact Aycock Funeral Home 1112 Military Trail, Jupiter, Florida 561-746-5124.
