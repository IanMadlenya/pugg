
        LEVITT-David. After a courageous stuggle for health, beloved husband for 60 years of Norma Uttal Levitt and loving father of Sally Steinberg, Nancy Hoffman and Andrew Levitt. Devoted grandfather to Noah M. and Jonah A. Steinberg, Rebecca HoffmanGreenwald, Mia and Elizabeth Levitt. Also mourned by son-in-law Peter Greenwald, daughter-in-law Peggy Whalen-Levitt and sister Dorothy Beskind and her family. Services at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Rd., Great Neck, NY at 1:00 PM Sunday. Contributions to be made to Temple Beth-El or The American Cancer Society.
        LEVITT-David. The Board of Directors and staff of the New York City Division mourn the passing of our dear friend and volunteer, David Levitt. Through his financial generosity and his special gifts of time, creativity, wisdom and guidance, he made significant contributions to our fight against cancer. He will be deeply missed and live in our hearts forever. Ralph Destino, Chairman Michael A. Paglia, MD, President George E. Barker, Executive VP American Cancer Society, New York City Division, Inc
        LEVITT-David M. The leadership of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations profoundly mourns the death of a faithful Honorary Life Trustee. David served the cause of Reform Judaism with great dedication for more than four decades and chaired many of our critical committees. We extend deepest sympathy to his loving wife, Norma, his children, grandchildren and all his loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President
        LEVITT-David M . Temple BethEl of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed long time member and honorary trustee. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
