
        HIMOFF-Ick Thornton, Dr., D.D.S. 94, on May 15, 2001. Cherished husband of 57 years of Helen and their devoted children, Eve-Ellen and James Starkman, Aviva and Gregory Himoff. Beloved grandfather of Amy and Wendy Starkman, Arielle and Dana Himoff. Loving brother of the late Mildred Lewis, Dudley and Robert, and adored uncle and grand uncle to many nieces and nephews. A graduate of Pennsylvania State University and the University of Pennsylvania Dental School, he practiced dentistry in White Plains for 50 years. A W.W. II veteran leaves behind a legacy of wonderful memories for his family and friends. Embraced forever by all who were privileged to share in the life of a man for all seasons. Services will be held on Thursday, May 17 at 10:30 AM at Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, NY 10606. Calling hours at 50 Winslow Road will be Thursday 7-9 PM, Friday 15 PM, Saturday 8-10 PM, and Sunday in NYC at 114 East 72nd Street from 2-6 PM. Contributions in his memory may be sent to Congregation Kol Ami.
        HIMOFF-Dr. Ick T. The Officers, Board of Governors and members of Metropolis Country club mourn the passing of our esteemed member and extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Helen, his daughter Eve, and his family. Corey B. Rabin, President Ronald M. Stuart, Secretary
