
        BERMAN-Philip I. The Jerusalem Foundation mourns the passing of Philip I. Berman, and extends its condolences to his family. Together with his beloved wife, Muriel, they were wise and passionate philanthropists who greatly enriched the city of Jerusalem with important gifts of outdoor sculpture. Our memories of him will forever be a blessing. Teddy Kollek, Chair-Int'l Council Alvin H. Einbender, Nat'l Chair. Michael Neiditch, Nat'l President Mendel Kaplan, Int'l Chair. Ruth Cheshin, Int'l President
