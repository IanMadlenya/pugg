
        STERN--Milton, O.D., died on May 17 at age 93. Devoted husband of Frances E. Stern, loving father of Judith Stern Torres and Daniel E. Stern, and grandfather of Julie S. Signor (nee Stern), Joel A. Torres, and Anne F. Stevens (nee Stern). Dr. Stern completed his education at Columbia University, and for over 35 years he maintained one of the largest individual optometric practices in New York City. He provided optometric care and eyewear to employees of the Long Island and Pennsylvania Railroads, as well as to many employees of the main branch of the New York City Post Office. He also had numerous patients in the entertainment and sports world. To his family and friends, Dr. Stern was the fixing daddy, as well as an avid sailor and expert gardener. He will be long remembered for his kindness, generosity, and endless good humor. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to Old Westbury Gardens, where he volunteered for many years.
