
        FISCHER-Pauline, 98. Devoted wife of the late Jack; loving mother of Bill and Stuart, Iris (Stanley) Aronson and Carol and Howard Schleien; loving sister of Esther Nathanson; adoring grandmother of ten grandchildren and fifteen great grandchildren. Pauline Fischer, along with her husband Jack, ran the famous Little Oriental Restaurant in Brooklyn. Services on Sunday, 12 Noon, ''Parkside'' Chapels, at 66th Avenue and Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
