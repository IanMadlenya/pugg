
        FRENCH - Raymond. Passed away suddenly on Wednesday, February 20th, 2002, in Jupiter Island, Florida. Born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, he attended Yale University and was graduated in the class of 1946 from the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy. He was the former President and CEO of Canal Randolph Corporation and a former Director of Lincoln National Life Insurance Co., Downstate Medical, Dollar Drydock Bank, American States Insurance Co's., Blue Ridge Real Estate Co., C.W. Post College and Hofstra University. He is survived by his wife, Joan Foy French and three daughters, Wendy Nolan of Manhattan, Christina Porter of Locust Valley, New York, and Pamela French of Chatam, New York. A memorial service will be held on Long Island in May. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Saint John of Lattingtown, Long Island.
