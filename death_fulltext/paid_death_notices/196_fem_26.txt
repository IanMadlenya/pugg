
        SCHREIBER-Stacey Kaplan. Age 39. On April 28, 2003, after a courageous battle with cancer. Graduate of Emory and formerly a Vice President at Citibank. Beloved wife of Gary. Fond daughter-in-law of Mel and Lynn Schreiber. Her extended family and countless friends mourn her untimely passing and she will be deeply missed. Contributions to any cancer fund or Temple Chaverim, Plainview, New York.
