
        LANDAU-Valerie. Born in Budapest, Hungary in 1915, died on December 29, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Abraham. Loving mother of Adrienne, Jack and Elaine. Dear sister of Olga Polgar. She was the brightest light of all of our lives, guiding us with her positive loving nature and inspiration. She is a true angel. Service Wednesday, December 30, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the General Scholarship Fund at Fashion Institute of Technology, or to the Bronx County Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Children.
