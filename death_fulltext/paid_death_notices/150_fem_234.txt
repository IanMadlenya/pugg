
        COWEN-Maria Szeni, July 27, 1999. Beloved wife of Leon. Devoted stepmother of Ron and loving sister of Elizabeth Moesser. Recognized abstract expressionist painter for half a century. Memorial service on Sunday, August 8, at Oakland Memorial Park, Monmouth Junction, NJ. Contributions in her memory may be made to the United Jewish Appeal, 130 East 59 St., New York, NY 10022.
