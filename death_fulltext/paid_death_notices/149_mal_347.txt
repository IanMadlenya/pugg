
        PAGLIARO-Frank J. Of Mt. Kisco on June 19, at 99, died at home after a short illness. He was a leader of the New York construction industry for more than 50 years. A resident of Mt. Kisco, he was a longtime member of its Beautification Committee. He is survived by his wife of 53 years, the former Anita Tabacchi; a sister, Mary Petrillo of Bronxville; two sons, Frank, Jr. of Burlingame, CA, and Leonard of Seattle, WA; four daughters, Anita and Carla of Livingston, MT, and Alix Barthelmes and Lisa Selz of Manhattan; six grandchildren and a great-granddaughter. A mass will be held at 10 AM Tuesday, June 22 at St. Francis of Assisi, Mt. Kisco. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Mt. Kisco Beautification Committee or to Hospice of Nothern Westchester.
