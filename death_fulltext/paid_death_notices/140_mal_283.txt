
        KOLACK-Sol. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the death of Sol Kolack, retired Director of ADL's Regional Office in Boston. From 1947 to 1983 Sol led the fight against anti-Semitism, racism and bigotry in New England and implemented educational programs to promote tolerance and understanding. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Shirley, his children Richard and Nancy, and the family. Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
