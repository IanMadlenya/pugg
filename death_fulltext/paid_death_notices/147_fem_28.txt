
        NEUMAN-Halina. On April 2, 1999. Loving mother of the late Jola Hoffman. Cherished grandmother of Kenneth, Marc, David and Estes Hoffman. Great-grandmother of Jay, Leah, Phoebe, Stephen and Michael. Halina was a Holocaust survivor, pianist and teacher. She will be greatly missed. Graveside services Sunday, 1:30 PM at Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, NJ.
