
        MORRIS -- Evelyn (nee Charleston). Died October 4, 2004 at home at the age of 93 after a long, valiant battle with cancer. Beloved wife of the late Perry Morris. She was born in Brooklyn on December 16, 1910. Survived by her sons, Dave and Steve, and daughter-in-law, Jill; three grandchildren, two great-grandchildren, one step great-grandson and her older sister, Gert Lee. One tough and loving old lady, Evelyn will be missed by all who knew her. Services were private.
