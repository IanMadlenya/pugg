
        SMITH-Rica (nee Martens). Actress who appeared in such feature films as Living in Oblivion, Welcome to the Dollhouse and Box of Moonlight, and such Broadway shows as The Shrike and Laura, died August 11, 1999. She was the beloved mother of Laurel Smith and loving sister of Fred Martens, Evelyn Ladick and Lotus Murphy. She was the greatest mother a daughter could ask for and a loyal and compassionate friend to those she loved. A memorial service will be held at MCC Theater, 120 West 28th St., August 26, 7 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the American Lung Association, 1740 Broadway, NYC 10019.
