
        GROLLMAN-Lorraine., 70, on January 26th, 2000 at her home in Setauket, NY. Beloved wife of Dr. Arthur Grollman. Devoted mother of Nancy Lubin, Donna Goldman and Madelon Finkel. Cherished grandmother of Jesse, Rachel, Wendy Goldman, Alexandra Sopko & Rebecca Finkel. Loving sister of Phyllis Wendorf and treasured sister and sister-in-law of Arline and Marvin Lubin. Lorraine's interests were wide and varied: she was an active participant in artistic and community affairs and an intrepid outdoors woman, having trekked in recent years in the Himalayas, Andes and Karakoram. She was a truly unique person who will be sorely missed by all who had the privilege of knowing her. We celebrate her life and deeply mourn her passing.
