
        POWER--Roger J., 75, peacefully at his home on January 26th in Moorestown, NJ. Navy veteran. Seton Hall alumnus. NYC newspaper ad sales executive, Sawyer Ferguson Walker, Million Markets and Newspapers First. Avid reader and sports fan. Active participant in swimming, tennis, golf and strength training. Beloved husband of Patricia (Degnen), father of Tracy, Roger, Meaghan, Timothy, Kate, grandfather of seven, brother of Ann Allen. Memorial service February 7, 10 AM, Our Lady of Good Counsel Church, Moorestown, NJ.
