
        KOPELMAN-Igor, MD. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Igor Kopelman, MD, who was an attending physician in the Department of Surgery at Beth Israel's Singer Division for seventeen years. To his wife, his entire family, and to all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, MD President & CEO Anthony C.
