
        BERNSTEIN-Seymour R.. Formerly a long-time resident of North Bellmore, New York. Died on December 27th, 1999 in Boca Raton, Florida. A veteran of W.W. II, he had been a manufacturing furrier and salesman in retail pharmaceuticals until his retirement in 1982. He is survived by his loving wife Ruth, his children, Jess, Ellen, Robert, Kenneth, and Matthew and his grandchildren, Daniel, Joanna, Jade, Jared, Cassie, Chad, and Max. We will all miss this loving, generous man.
