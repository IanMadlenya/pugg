
        KENNY-Stephen J. of Briarcliff Manor died early Friday morning. Born and raised in New York City, he was graduated from Manhattan College in 1952, entered the U.S. Army, and served in Korea. In 1954 he began his career in the Municipal Bond industry with C.J. Devine & Co, later absorbed by Merrill Lynch, where he remained until 1979. He then joined Goldman Sachs & Co where, as Vice-President at various times, he managed trading, underwriting/syndication, and sales. Mr. Kenny was an active member of the Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board and an officer and Board member of the Municipal Bond Club of NY. After retiring in 1989, he served as a volunteer in the E.S.L. program, teaching English to inmates at Sing Sing Prison. Married in 1956 to the former Anne M. O'Brien, he is survived by his widow and their children and children-in-law: Suzanne and Jeff Mickelson of Wellesley, Ma, Stephen and Sheila Kenny of Glen Rock, NJ., Michael & Lauren of Rye, NY, Marie and John Foley of Norwich, Ct and Christine of Boston, Ma. He leaves eleven grandchildren, Stephen, Daniel, Benjamin, Patrick, Conor, Christopher, Allison, Anne, Maeve, Ryan and Timothy. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Be a Student's Friend fund of the Inner City Scholarship Fund, 1011 First Ave, Suite 1400, New York, NY 10022, or at their website: www.innercityss.org. Visiting Waterbury & Kelly Funeral Home, Briarcliff Manor on Saturday & Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass, Church of the Magdalene, Pocantico Hills on Monday at 10:30AM. Interment private.
