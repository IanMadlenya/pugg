
        IRWIN-Barbara L.M. Age 85, died December 7, at home in Larchmont, NY. Loving and beloved of Wallace Irwin Jr., her husband; William W. Irwin, her son; Victoria Woolley of Minneapolis, her daughter; Benjamin Woolley, her grandson; Philip Sprott of Thame, Oxfordshire, England, her brother. Gifted in arts and crafts, avid tennis player, able and devoted supporter of Planned Parenthood. Twelve years a stroke survivor, she bore her disability with courage and grace. Her kindness, charm and humor earned her countless friends who will keep her in their hearts.
