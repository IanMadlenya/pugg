
        BLOCK--Leonard N. (December 21, 1911-November 9, 2005). Survived by Adele Block, wife of 69 years, children, Peggy Danziger (Dick) and Thomas Block (Marilyn Friedman); grandchildren, Michael Danzinger (Betsy), Katie Danziger (Steve Horowitz) and Jonathan and Alison Block; great-grandchildren, James, Robby, Charlie and Teddy Danziger and Jake and Jilly Horowitz. Services Sunday, November 13, 11 am, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th St, NYC. $(6$)BLOCK--Leonard. The Board and staff of Lincoln Center Theater are deeply saddened by the death of Leonard Block, who passed away peacefully on November 9. Beloved husband of founding Board member Adele Block, Leonard was a staunch and generous advocate of the theater, passionate in his determination to see the theater reestablished, artistically distinguished and organizationally sound. We extend our condolences and love to Peggy, Tom and the entire Block family. John B. Beinecke, Chairman John Chalsty, President Andre Bishop Artistic Director Bernard Gersten Executive Producer $(6$)BLOCK -- Leonard.
