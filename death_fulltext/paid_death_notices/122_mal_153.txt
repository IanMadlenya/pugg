
        COLETTI-PERUCCA-Sonio Edward, died Sunday, March 9, of cancer. Age 77. Beloved husband of Gertrude. Loving father of Onorio and Marc Andrea, and stepfather of Jackie Drexel. He is survived by six grandchildren. He was born in New York City, November 12, 1919. He served with distinction as an officer in the American Army. Funeral Friday at 11 AM at St. Paul the Apostle Church, Route 107, Brookville. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Cancer Center at Glen Cove Hospital.
