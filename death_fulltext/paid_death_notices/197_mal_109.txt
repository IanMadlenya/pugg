
        HADIKS--Dean, 50, died suddenly June 8, 2003. A loving and passionate husband to Cheryl, father to Ari, brother to Milton and son to Themis Revis. Dean was a dedicated psychologist who worked as the director of psychology at New York Methodist Hospital in Brooklyn and also maintained a private practice. Dean was a thinker, a poet and an adventurer. He will be greatly missed by his family, friends, co-workers and patients. The family will be home on Thursday evening June 12 and his life will be celebrated at the Hadiks' home on Sunday, June 15 at 4 p.m. >>AD#
