
        NICHOLAS-Frances. Graveside services for Frances Nicholas will be held at 1 P.M. Sunday, March 23, at Beth David Cemetery, 300 Elmont Rd., in Elmont, Long Island. The long time teacher (retired) at PS 209 in Queens died in Cleveland on March 15, at age 90. She is survived by two sisters, three children; six grandchildren, and four great grandchildren.
