
        HANF--Lotte Wallach, of Cornwall, CT, died on April 22. Daughter of the late Moritz and Meta Wallach of Munich, widow of the late Erich Hanf, she is predeceased by her daughter Brigitte Hanf-Enos. She is survived by her daughter Catherine Hanf Noren, her granddaughter Eve HanfEnos, her sister Annelise Rosenberg, of Roslyn, Long Island and her brother Fred Wallace of Sarasota, FL. She led a long, vigorous and much admired life, and died peacefully at age 95. Rest in peace, Lotte, knowing that your work is done.
