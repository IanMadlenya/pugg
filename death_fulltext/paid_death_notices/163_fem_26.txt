
        DAVIS-Beatrice, 96, devoted wife of the late Sam, beloved mother of Malcolm and Ilene, George, Theodore and Cindee, adored grandmother & loving greatgrandmother, sister of Edith Epstein. Bea was a wonderful and giving mother and person. She was full of fun, an active Red Cross volunteer during World War II at St. Albans and North Shore Veterans Hospitals. Active in UJA, Hadassah, Zionist Organization and Hebrew Institute of Israel. Her memory will live in our hearts forever. Services Thursday, August 3, 12:45 at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam.
