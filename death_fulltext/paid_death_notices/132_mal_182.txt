
        COHEN-Louis John, 61. January 13, 1998. ''Home is the fisherman, home to the sea.'' Dear husband of Susan. Devoted father of Judd and Donna, Kenneth and Jacqueline. Adored grandpa of Erica, Brandon and Daniel. He was a graduate of Woodmere High School and Boston University. As an insurance broker, he spent 37 years with Leon Rosenblatt Inc., where his last position was President. He will be sorrowfully missed by all who knew him. In lieu of flowers, Louis would request that donations be made to the Send-A-Kid Fishing Program C/O The Long Island Fisherman, 14 Ramsey Road, Shirley, NY 11967. Services to be held Friday, January 16 at 12:30PM, BoulevardRiverside Park West Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York.
