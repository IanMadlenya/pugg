
        KADANOFF-Irving. Age 92, passed away on Friday, June 29, 2001, in Fort Lauderdale. He is remembered as the beloved husband of 67 years of Gertrude, sister of Bea Shapiro, dear father of Philip and Robert, father-in-law of Eileen and Carol, adoring grandfather of Peri, Jacqueline, Paula, David, Allison and Lawrence, and great-grandfather of Jake. Memorial services will be held in NYC, at Riverside Chapel, 76th St, on Monday, July 2, at 11:15 AM.
