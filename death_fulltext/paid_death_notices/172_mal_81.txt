
        BELL-Gordon Knox, Jr., 99, died May 4, 2001 in Raleigh, NC. Born in New York City 1902 to Gordon Knox and Marlan Mason Crafts Bell. Graduate Groton, Harvard, with a Masters from Columbia. Veteran of the 7th Regiment, member of S. Nicholas Society, Union Club, Society of Colonial Wars and Holland Lodge No. 8. Predeceased by his wife, Virginia Ferguson Bell, survived by his daughters; Helen Fedema, Barbara Bell, Mary Bell White and grandsons, Gordon and Elliot White. For information Mitchell Funeral Home, 600 St. Mary's St., Raleigh, NC.
