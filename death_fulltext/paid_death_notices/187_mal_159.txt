
        REICH-Isidore Alan, peacefully at his home in Boca Raton, Florida, on August 13, 2002. Formerly of New York City and Long Island, New York. Track star and graduate of NYU Class of 1932. The first registered physical therapist in the State of NY. One of the first members of Temple Emanuel in Lynbrook, NY. The loving and faithful husband for 59 years to the late Ida Sarah. Cherished father of Evelyn Reich and her partner Ira Kaskel and Marti Reich and her husband Jeffrey L. Glatzer. Beloved brother-in-law to Abraham and Rose Reigrod. Devoted grandfather to Robert Kaskel, Daniel and Susan Kaskel, Deborah and Randy Frimet, Jonathan Freirich and his wife Ginny Reel and Elizabeth Freirich. He is also survived by six great-grandchildren. Contributions in his memory can be made to Alzheimers Association or Hospice By The Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Rd, Boca Raton FL 33486-3395. Shiva will be held at the home of Marti Reich and Jeffrey Glatzer on Monday and Tuesday, August 19 and 20, from 5PM to 9PM. For further information: Gutterman Warheit Chapel, Boca Raton, FL: 800-992-9262.
