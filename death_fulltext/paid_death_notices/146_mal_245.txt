
        WEISMAN-Herman L. The Zionist Organization of America mourns the passing of our esteemed past National President (1970-'74). He served the cause of Israel and Zionism with distinction and he will be sorely missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Jeannette, and to his bereaved family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Morton A. Klein, National Pres. Michael Orbach, Chmn. of the Bd
