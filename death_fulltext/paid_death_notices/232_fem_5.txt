
        MAYERSON--Florence. Of Dayton, OH, died April 28, 2006, at 85, after a valiant six year battle with cancer. Beloved wife of Manuel D. Mayerson, she is also survived by children Harvey Chaim Mayerson and Lynn Mayerson Berger of Israel and Sandra Mayerson of New York, as well as six grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Family requests donations to Hadassah, 5045 N. Main St., Dayton, OH 45415.
