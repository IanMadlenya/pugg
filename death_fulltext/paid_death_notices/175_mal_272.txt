
        SHULSKY-Rubin. Passed on August 25, 2001. Beloved father of Rena, husband of the late Marie Shulsky and son of the late Sarah and Louis Shulsky. He was a man of unique wisdom, intelligence, wit, compassion, grace and culture. An original mind and an original life, wonderfully creative, he was loved and appreciated by those who had the good fortune to know him. Service at Fifth Avenue Synagogue, 5 East 62nd St, today at 12 noon.
        SHULSKY-Rubin. The employees of Shire Realty/1220 Broadway LLC mourn the loss of Rubin Shulsky, who passed away Saturday, August 25, 2001. He was a true friend to everyone. His generosity and his sense of humor, wit, kind heart, sensitivity and lively intelligence made him an extraordinary and rare employer. His legacy will live on and he will be sorely missed by his employees. Our deepest sympathy and condolences to his daughter Rena M. Shulsky.
        SHULSKY-Rubin. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its dedicated trustee, beloved father of its devoted member, Rena Shulsky, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth Rabbi Ira L. Rennert Chairman J. Ezra Merkin President
