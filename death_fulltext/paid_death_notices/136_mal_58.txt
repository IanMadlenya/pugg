
        GOLDSTEIN-Abraham. On May 5, 1998. Beloved husband of Mildred. Loving father of Fredricka, Karen, Louis, David. Dear brother of Henriette Goldberg. Adoring grandfather of Samuel, Stephen, Brian, Ann, Susan, Anthony, Daniella, Amanda and Joseph. Great grandfather of Alexandra. Chairman and leader of Society of Jewish Science. Lover of the people and land of Israel where he lived for 20 years. A generous hand to those in need and those he loved. Service Thursday 11 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his memory to Society of Jewish Science, 109 East 39 Street, New York, New York 10016.
