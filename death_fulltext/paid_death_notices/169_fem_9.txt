
        APPEL-Gloria W. Suddenly on January 31. Remembered with love and joy by her children: Carley Broder, Michael Broder and Rita Rubin, Charles Broder and Celeste Hoppey-Broder, and Betsy Broder and David Wentworth. Proud grandmother of Elexa, Ethan, Hannah and Aliza Broder and Ariel and Caroline Wentworth. Widow of Ed Appel, she was devoted to Eddie's children: Marianne Berson, Michaele and Thad Keyes, and Diane and John McCann; their children Deborah Parnes, Brad Berson, Rachel and Gabe Keyes, and Kevin and Ian McCann. President of the Louis and Harold Price Foundation and the Price Institute for Entrepreneurial Studies, which she served for over 20 years, a trustee of Babson College and Medical Development for Israel and advisor to Ewing and Marion Kauffman Foundation. Gloria also leaves countless cherished and loyal friends. Services Sunday, 1:15 Temple Shaaray Tefila, 79th and 2nd. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Price-Babson Fellows Program at Babson College or Medical Development for Israel.
        APPEL-Gloria W.
