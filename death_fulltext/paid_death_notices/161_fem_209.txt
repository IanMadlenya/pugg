
        HALPERN-Ruth. On June 24, of Great Neck. Beloved wife of the late Jesse. Devoted mother of Marc Halpern, Andrea Naft and Joel Halpern. Cherished grandmother and great-grandmother. Memorial services were held Sunday. Contributions may be made to the Lighthouse for the Blind.
        HALPERN-Ruth. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
