
        BROUT-Betty Lea. On November 8, 1999. Dear sister of Judith Friedman and Robert Brout. Sister-inlaw of Sandor Friedman. Betty was a social worker and writer; she dedicated her life to the principles of humanism and ethical culture. She has served on the Boards of the New York Ethical Culture Society and the American Humanist Association. We all remember her for her goodness of heart. Service Thursday 12:00 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Avenue.
