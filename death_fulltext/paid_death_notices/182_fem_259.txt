
        CORNING-Ursula. The entire Board of Trustees and the Blythedale Children's Hospital community mourns the loss of our dear friend and long-standing Board member, Ursula Corning. Ursula was a devoted supporter of the work of the hospital for over 40 years. She cared greatly about children and their rehabilitation. We send deep and sincere condolences to her entire family. Laurence Cohen, Chair of the Board of Trustees Larry Levine, President and CEO Joelle Mast, PhD., M.D., Chief Medical Officer
        CORNING-Ursula. Opera Orchestra notes with great sadness the death of Ursula Corning. Miss Corning was a great lover of opera and a generous supporter of music. Admired and respected by many, she will be remembered with affection and respect by all. Opera Orchestra of New York
