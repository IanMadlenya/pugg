
        GALE--Jean (nee Edelmuth), died October 17, age 97. Beloved wife of the late William Gale, dear mother of Judith Bloom Shaw and Robert, Eleanor Bloom Surkis and Michael, Rose-Lynn Gale Sokol and Daniel. Loving grandmother of David, Andrew, William, John, Charles, Sarah, and Lizzy. Devoted sister-inlaw of Ruth Gale. Mrs. Gale, with her husband, was the proprietor of Jean Gale, Inc at 535 Madison Avenue until she retired in 1980. Burial was in Boca Raton, Florida.
