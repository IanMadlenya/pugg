
        GOODMAN-Leon M. On Saturday December 13, 1997 at his home in New York. Born January 6, 1908 in Philadelphia. Beloved husband of the late Minnie Gabriel Goodman and the late Gertrude Morgenstern Greenberg. Devoted father of David and Judith and dear grandfather of Joseph, Jeannette, Laura and Eve. The last surviving original member of the American Trotskyist movement. Interment private. Contributions may be made to the Workmen's Circle Home and Geriatric Center, 3155 Grace Avenue, Bronx, NY 10469.
