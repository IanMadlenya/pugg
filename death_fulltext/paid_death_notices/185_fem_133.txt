
        UNTERMEYER-Sophie G. 92, of New York City, passed away Saturday, June 14, after a long illness. Aunt of Charles S. and Randolph Guggenheimer Jr., and sister-in-law of Elinor C. Guggenheimer. Daughter of the late Charles and Minnie Guggenheimer, sister of the late Randolph Guggenheimer. She excelled in many sports, mainly golf, in which she won numerous tournaments including the prestigious Women's MGA Championship. She ran the Lewisohn Stadium Concerts following the death of her mother, the legendary ''Minnie,'' and was an honorary member of the Board of Directors of the New York Philharmonic. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York Philharmonic, Avery Fisher Hall, 10 Lincoln Center Plaza, New York, NY 10023.
