
        PUTNAM-Dorothy Von Elm. Of East Hampton, NY on December 2, 1999 at age 87. Beloved wife of the late John L. Putnam. Mother of John L. Putnam, Jr. of Riverside CT and the late Nancy de la Vega of Madrid Spain. Also survived by daughter-in-law Andrea Putnam, granddaughters, Wendy Putnam and Jennifer Putnam, both of Greenwich CT, and brother Charles A. Von Elm, of Forest Hills NY. Interment will be private. Memorial service to be scheduled. Donations in her memory may be made to St. Luke's Church, East Hampton, NY 11937.
