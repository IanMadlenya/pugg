
        KOBELL-Mary. On July 14, 1998, of Belle Harbor, NY and East Brunswick, NJ. Beloved wife of Irving Kobell, mother of Adrianne and Michael Eisner and Gerald and Helen Kobell. Grandmother of Wendy Levenkron, Lori and Todd Eisner, Deena, Rona, Aliza, and Daniel Kobell, great grandmother of Matthew Jason Levenkron. A gifted and dedicated teacher and Jewish educator, the whole community was her classroom. Shiva at the Eisner residence. Contributions in her memory may be made to the East Brunswick Jewish Center or Hadassah.
