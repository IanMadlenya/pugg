
        BAUR-Louise Chase. The Board of Trustees and Staff at the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our dear friend, Louise Chase Baur, the wife of the late John I.H. Baur, who was director of The Whitney Museum. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to her children, Susan, Arthur and Jean and to the entire family. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Joel S. Ehrenkranz, President Maxwell L. Anderson, Director
