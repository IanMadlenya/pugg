
        KIMMEL-Harry, M.D. Beloved husband of Tanya. Loving father of Dr. Marina Kimmel and Dr. Michael Finkelstein, Dr. Barbara and Eric Cherches, and Andrew and Robin Kimmel. Cherished grandfather of Evan, Rachel, Joanna, Carly, Brian, Cody-Rose, Austin, Jesse and Bri. Loving brother of Beatrice Sebastian of Paris, France. Physician of pediatric medicine for more than 50 years, first in Romania and then in New York City for over thirty years. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in memory of Dr. Harry Kimmel to the Pediatric Department at St. Lukes Roosevelt Hospital. Services Sunday 12:30PM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        KIMMEL-Harry, M.D. The administration and medical staff of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center join with the Department of Pediatrics in mourning the death of our esteemed colleague and friend, Dr. Harry Kimmel. A longtime member of the pediatrics department, Dr. Kimmel was beloved by his patients and friends. He will be missed. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family and loved ones.
