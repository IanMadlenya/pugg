
        EARLY--Virginia B. Wife of Albert D. Early, on May 4, 2005. Originally of New Vernon, NJ, she lived in Sarasota, FL since 1980. She is survived by a son, A. Blakeman Early, three grandchildren: Alexandra B. Early, G. Blake Hargrave, Megan B. McIntyre; and three great-grandchildren; Mac McIntyre, Sean Hargrave, and Calum McIntyre. Her family celebrates her grace, strength, and love of life. Donations in her memory may be made to the Salvation Army.
