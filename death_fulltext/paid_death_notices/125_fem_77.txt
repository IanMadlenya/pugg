
        EINHORN-Carolyn Beitman, widow of Benjamin Einhorn, died yesterday at her home. She recently celebrated her 89th birthday. Mrs. Einhorn is lovingly remembered by her daughters Ellen Einhorn Messing & Susan Einhorn Whyman. She took pride in her six grandchildren: Robin, Cathy, Nancy, Bill, Jennifer and Wendi and her six great grandchildren: Julie, Emily, Jared, Jamie, Benjamin and Samantha. She will be missed by her caring companions: Eileen, Grace, Alice, Lorraine, Sheila and Theresa. A funeral service will be held Friday, June 13, 11:30am at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations to the Milton B. Rosenbluth Foundation, 912 5th Avenue, New York City 10021 or a charity of choice.
