
        WEISS--Martin, 76, of White Plains, NY, suddenly on August 16th, twenty-nine days after the passing of his dear wife Elaine, and after fifty years of marriage. Survived by his three sons, David, Robert and Fred, three loving daughters-in-law and three grandsons. Services Thursday, August 19th, 12 Noon, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY 10552. Contributions may be made to American Diabetes Association.
