
        PETRIE-Helen Hurst. Long time resident of Summit, New Jersey, died after a short illness, December 4, 2002 in Kingston - on - Thames, En gland while visiting her family. The wife of the late James A. Petrie Jr., formerly of Montclair and Summit, beloved mother of James and Bruce, grandmother of Samantha, George and Charles, she is survived by her sister Claire Hurst Smith, of Miami, Florida as well as eight nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be announced. Donations in her memory may be sent to the Overlook Hospital, Cancer Research, Summit, New Jersey.
