
        O'DONNELL -- Lillian. On April 2nd, 2005. Beloved wife of the late J. Leonard O'Donnell. Her career started in the theater and evolved into being an international syndicated mystery writer. A lifetime member of the West Side Tennis Club for 50 years, a member of the 7th Regiment Tennis Club, and served as a council member of St. Vincent Ferrer Church. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, April 4th, 3-5 and 7-9 pm. Funeral Mass at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave at 66 St, Tuesday, 10 am. Interment to follow at Maple Grove Cemetery.
