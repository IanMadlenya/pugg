
        NISSELSON-William J. On Tuesday, June 19, 2001 at age 56. Chief Operating Officer of Sound One, Inc., Bill Nisselson was a major figure in the New York post-production film world. Widely admired for his support of the independent film community, his warmth, generosity and humor touched the lives of many in and beyond the film industry. He is survived by his wife Susan Diederich, his mother Herta Danis, his sister Jane Assimakopoulos, his step-daughter Alana Vidal and his nieces Anna Papaioannou and Daphne Assimakopoulos. Services at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 Street, New York, N.Y. on Thursday, June 28, 2001 at 6:00PM. Donations in his memory may be made to the Independent Feature Project at 104 West 29th Street, New York, New York 10001.
