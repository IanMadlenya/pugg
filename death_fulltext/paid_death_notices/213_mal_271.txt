
        REMPELL--Joseph, 92 died on October 20, 2004 at his home in White Plains, NY. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his wife Grace, his children Steven and Marcia Rempell, David and Donna Rempell, Lisa and Howard Klein and Susan and David Roth and seven grandchildren. He is also survived by his brother Irving. He was devoted to his family. We will all miss him. Mr. Rempell will be remembered for his dedication to progressive causes. He owned a number of dry cleaning stores and made sure that they were union shops. He supported through action and financial contributions many anti-poverty programs. His concern for others had a great impact on the rest of his family.
