
        NAIDAMAST - Daniel, 80 years old, a Great Neck resident, passed away quietly in his sleep at the Nesconset Nursing Home on August 22, 2002 following a long illness. Professionally, Mr. Naidamast was Aerospace Engineer and worked for several companies in the metropolitan New York area including Kollsman Electronics and the Loral Corporation. It was at Loral where he was engaged in testing components for the radar warning receiver, which alerts F-15 fighter pilots to incoming surface-toair missiles. A 47 year resident of Great Neck, Mr. Naidamast was active with the Nassau County Council Boy Scouts of America for more than three and a half decades. He initially served as the Scoutmaster of Troop 446, which met at the Baker Hill School in Great Neck and later served as a Neighborhood Commissioner, responsible for working with adult leaders from numerous troops in the county area. He also was an elected member of the Sagamores, an honorary group of adult scout leaders. Mr. Naidamast was born and raised in the Bronx and graduated from Evander Childs High School. He later earned a Bachelor of Science in Engineering and a Masters in Business Administration from the City College of New York. Mr. Naidamast is survived by his wife Nille, his daughter Elion, his son Edward and four grandchildren, Adam, Samuel and David Austin and Ilana D'vorah Naidamast. Services Friday 12:30 PM at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
