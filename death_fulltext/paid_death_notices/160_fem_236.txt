
        HALPRIN-Maxine (nee Rickard), age 72, of Hoboken, NJ, formerly of Juno Beach and Miami, San Antonio and New York City, died May 27, 2000. Daughter of the late George ''Tex'' Rickard, the boxing promoter and founder of the New York Rangers Hockey Club, who built the Madison Square Garden building that stood at Eighth Ave. and 50th St. in NYC. Devoted mother of Edwin Rickard Goodfellow of San Antonio, TX, Joan Christine Halprin of Beaufort, SC and Joseph Rickard Halprin of Hoboken, NJ. Her smile, friendship and love will be missed by all. Visitation on Tuesday from 7-9PM at Lawton-Turso Funeral Home, 631 Washington St., Hoboken. Funeral Wednesday. Mass 10AM at Sts. Peter and Paul R.C. Church, 4th and Hudson Sts. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY. Contributions to Alzheimer's Association or American Heart Association greatly appreciated.
