
        HEYMAN - Stanley, 83, passed away January 10, 2006. Loving husband of Eleanor and father of three. A WWII veteran he owned Lichter's, a chain of stores that clothed women and children in Rockland County for over four decades. He was know for his warmth and kindness and impish sense of humor. He is survived by his children, Ida, Alison and Philip and his grandson Elliot. Services at 1 pm January 12, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave at 91st St. We love and miss you Dad.
