
        FORD-Norbert Martin. Died unexpectedly in Dublin on Sunday, Sept. 10, 2000. Dearly beloved husband of Ludlow. Father of Andrew and Carol and grandfather of Jared, Martin, Robert, Geoffrey and Anne. Mr. Ford was a businessman who designed and manufactured mens and womens apparel. He was an Infantry Captain in the U.S. Army in World War II. He served in Europe until 1946. May he rest in peace. The funeral will take place in Dublin on Friday, September 15 followed by cremation. In lieu of flowers, a donation to the Arthritis Foundation would be appreciated.
