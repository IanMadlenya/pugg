
        CALVI-Gino. of East Rutherford, New Jersey was a victim of the World Trade Center Attack on September 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Christine (nee Wagner) Calvi. Cherished son of Mario and Emma (nee Mormone) Calvi. Loving brother of Gabriella Rooney and her husband Joseph and Alex Calvi and his fiance Rebecca Bellini. Dear uncle of Lucas. Memorial Service, Saturday, October 27, 2001 at 11 AM, at St. Joseph's Church, 120 Hoboken Rd., East Rutherford, NJ. Memorial donations may be made to the Gino Calvi Foundation Scholarship, University of Buffalo Foundation, P.O.
