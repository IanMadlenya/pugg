
        TRENT--Bertram J., died March 28, 2007 at the age of 88 of lung cancer. He was a resident of Scarsdale, NY and Palm Beach, FL. He leaves behind his loving wife, Geraldine, of 60 years, his two cherished sons, Bruce and Michael, Michael's spouse Laura, two grandsons, Daniel and Jason. Educated at Yale University and University of Virginia, a veteran of WWII, he served in the army 1943-1946 at Shemya in the Aleutian Islands. He was stalwart, courageous, a loving husband and father. He will be missed greatly. Services will be held Friday, 10 am at the ''Chapel in the Woods'' at Kol Ami Temple, White Plains, NY.
