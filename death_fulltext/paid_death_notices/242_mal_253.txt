
        PUNAK--Randy M., 53 of NYC died March 5, 2007 in Paris, France. Born in Milwaukee, graduated UWM with B.A. including studies at Alberts Ludwig Universitat in Freiburg, West Germany in German Literature. Enjoyed a career of 19 years as in International Flight Attendant with American Airlines. He leaves behind his loving partner Dan Pierce of 30 years. A memorial service will be held at Middle Collegiate Church on 2nd Avenue, Monday, March 26, at 6:30 pm. In lieu of flowers a donation to: www.wingsfoundation.org may be made.
