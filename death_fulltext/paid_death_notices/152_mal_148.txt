
        LYNCH-Douglas Charles. On September 10, in Westchester, NY, in his 85th year. Formerly of New York City, he was a senior executive with Phelps Dodge, Lynch Corporation, RCA and other international companies, and an author published in three languages. The son of an American Army Officer, he was born in the Phillipines, and served as advisor to the President of the Phillipines after World War II. He was a wise and unforgettable man who will be deeply missed by his wife Carolyn, his children Victoria and Clifford, and his grandchildren Chloe and Luke, and by his many friends. According to his wishes, no funeral service will be held.
