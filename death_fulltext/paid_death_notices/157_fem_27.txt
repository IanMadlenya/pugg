
        ROSEN-Edith Hodes, 78. Passed away peacefully at her home in Key Biscayne, FL, on February 2, 2000. She is survived by her daughters, Margery Hadar of Manhattan and Victoria Simons of Key Biscayne, her son-in-law, Dr. Robert Simons, her grandchildren, Eric (Allison) Hadar and Joshua Hadar, Matthew Simons and Ilana Simons, and her great granddaughter, Anna Joe Hadar. She is also survived by her brother, Stuart T. Hodes. She was an elegant woman who shared her great strength and wisdom with her children and grandchildren. She was much loved and will be sorely missed by all who knew her. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Florida Philharmonic, 3401 Northwest 9th Ave., Ft. Lauderdale, FL 33309, (305)-476-1234.
