
        HUMM-Harold Judson. Marine Scientist. February 26, 1912-December 11, 2000. Marine scientist, professor and a leading authority on algae, marine bacteriology and land plan ecology and taxonomy. At least 11 new species of marine algae, diatoms and invertebrate animals were named in his honor. First resident: Director of the Duke Marine Laboratory, Beaufort, NC (1948-49); Founding Director of the Oceanographic Insitute of Florida State University (194954); Visiting Scientist Woods Hole Oceanographic Institute (1959); Jacques Loeb Associate in Marine Biology, Rockefeller Institute (1959-60); and University of South Florida Founding Director of Institute of Marine Science (1967-74).
