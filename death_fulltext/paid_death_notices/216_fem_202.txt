
        SHORE--Irene Freeman, age 97, on January 19, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Josselyn Shore for 72 years. Cherished mother of Wini and Mike Freund, and Toni and Freeman Shore. Adored grandmother of Jenny and Mike Pardee, Mia and Ramsey Walker, and Julie and Peter Strauss. Loving greatgrandmother of Ben, Jake, Samantha, Abby, Charlie and Katie. Dear sister of the late Anne Bartner, Sol and Gus Freeman. Her generous spirit and great beauty were an inspiration to all. Services Friday, January 21st at 12:30 at Temple Beth-El of Great Neck. Contributions in her memory to Temple Beth-El.
        SHORE--Irene. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of Fresh Meadow Country Club, record the passing of our member Irene Shore, wife of Josselyn Shore (d'2000). We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her son Freeman Shore, a former member, and to her entire family. Mark Claster, President Roger Blumencranz, Secy
        SHORE--Irene. Temple BethEl of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of Irene Shore, wife of our former Temple President, the late Josselyn Shore. She brought great warmth & love to our congregation. We shall miss her. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
