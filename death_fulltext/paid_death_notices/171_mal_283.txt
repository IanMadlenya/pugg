
        KAHN-Frank J., Ph.D., J.D. Professor, Lehman College, on April 20, 2001. Dr. Kahn is the beloved husband of the late Abby Pearlman Kahn, loving father of Dr. Julie Kahn and Leora Kahn. Dear brother of Vida Peskay. Dr. Kahn taught communications at the Speech and Theater Department of the Lehman College. He was a devoted father, caring professor and had a passion for jazz music. He will be missed by all. Funeral today, noon, EdwardsDowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY. Memorials to the Multiple Sclerosis Society appreciated.
