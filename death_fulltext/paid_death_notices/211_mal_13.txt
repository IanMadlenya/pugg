
        RIDALL--Edmund W., Jr., Esq. On Monday, July 26, 2004, after a brief battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Gloria Slattery Ridall, and the late Mitzi Braun Ridall and Carol Smale Ridall. Father of Kathryn Ridall, Linda Villa, Edmund W. Ridall, III, Pauline H. Smale and Charlotte Reid. Also survived by six grandchildren and one great-grandson. Ed proudly served his country during WWII as a bombardier in the US Army Air Corps. Friends will be received on Tuesday, August 3, in the Parlor of Shadyside Presbyterian Church one hour prior to a 1 PM Memorial Service in the Main Sanctuary. Remembrances may be made to the Culver Military Academy, 1300 Academy Rd., 132 Alumni Office, Culver, IN 46511. John A.
