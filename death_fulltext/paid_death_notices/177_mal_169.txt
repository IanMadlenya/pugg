
        SCHWARTZ-Samuel E., MD. Retired New York family physician, died on October 6th in Phoenix, Arizona. Dr. Schwartz was born on September 3rd, 1910, in New York City. He was a graduate of City College of New York in 1931 and New York University School of Medicine in 1938. He interned at Willard Parker and later served in the United States Army during WW II. He practiced for over 40 years in Jackson Heights before retiring and moving to Scottsdale, Arizona in 1983. He is survived by his wife Lee A. Schwartz, children Judy Warner, Stanley Schwartz and Nanci Ryder, 4 grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. He was interred at Mount Sinai Mortuary in Los Angeles.
