
        KOLB-Libby. The Aderet Chapter of EMUNAH of America and its national officers mourn the tragic passing of Libby Kolb, age 37, past Chapter President. With her magnetism, talents and vision, she built EMUNAH in Teaneck on behalf of Israel's children. She will be missed by everyone she touched. Our sincerest condolences to Eli, Miriam, Moshe, Yitzie, Yaacov and her sisters Shelly and Judy. May the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rhonda Avner, Rochel Feld, Janis Klein Aderet Chapter Presidium Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld, Nat'l Pres.
