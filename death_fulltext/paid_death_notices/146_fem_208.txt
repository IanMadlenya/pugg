
        WIMMER-Alice. Died March 17, aged 95, in her Berkeley, CA home. Born in Vienna, she lived in New York from 1940 to 1977. Widow of Hugo, Alice is survived by her daughter Gertrude Weil of Berkeley, son Harry Wimmer and his wife Shirley Givens of NYC, four grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. WYOGE-Irving/d. Retired pharmacist. Died on March 19, 1999 at the age of 95. Beloved husband of 61 years of Esther. Devoted father of Dr. Norman and Felice Wyloge, Madi and Bruce Alter, Diane and Gregg Borak. Adored grandfather of Jonathan and Marci, Daniel, Nicole and Jonathan, Ilana, Jarrod, Shane, Cody and great grandfather of Jordan and Zachary. A quiet, gentle, scholarly man who was loved by all. Services today, 12 noon, Riverside Memorial Chapels, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon.
