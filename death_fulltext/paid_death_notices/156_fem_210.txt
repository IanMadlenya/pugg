
        WEISS-Darcia. Died peacefully on January 4, 2000. She was the daughter of the late Cecelia Garblik and Isidore Weiss. Darcia will be remembered for her love of music, gardens, birds, travel, and for the courage she showed through years of illness. A memorial meeting of friends is planned for the Spring.
