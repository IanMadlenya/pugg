
        KONDRITZER--Gerald Robert, died peacefully at home on October 14, 2006, in the company of his beloved wife, Christy Wilkinson. He was born on July 13, 1947, in Baltimore, Maryland, and was an alumnus of Columbia University and Marietta College. His distinguished career in finance included positions as Vice President and Treasurer at Chiquita Brands International and Assistant Treasurer at Amerada Hess Corporation and The Seagram Company, Ltd. Gerry will be remembered for his intelligence, energy, zest for life and sense of humor, and for his appreciation and support of the arts, interest in history and enthusiasm for New York City. He was greatly loved and will be deeply missed.
