
        ROSENBERG -- Samuel L. Born on June 21, 1909 in Odessa, Ukraine. Emigrated to the United States in 1911 with his parents and three sisters, Ann Weitzenberg (died 1998), Eva Blackman and Marcia Hoffman. Preceded in death by wife of 67 years, Natalie ''Netty'' Rosenberg (nee Baer). Survived by three children Morton (Linda), Harvey (Naomi), and Joel. Five grandchildren and twenty great-grandchildren also survive. Previously employed by Astoria Federal Bank in Forest Hills. Service to be held Sunday November 13, 2005 at 1:00 PM at Schwartz Brothers - Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. Grandson Rabbi Jonathan Rosenberg officiating.
