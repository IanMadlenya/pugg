
        EBSTEL-John. Dedicated husband of his late wife Jean. As a member of the Photo League, John's clarity of vision and technical expertise shaped the work of many young photographers. The highlight of his career was his role as the exclusive photographer for Louis Kahn's architectural works. Born on Bastille Day, John strove to integrate his esthetic vision with his commitment to social equality. John and Jean were founding member of the Hillman Cooperatives on the Lower East Side. John's vitality and tenacious spirit will be missed by all who knew him.
