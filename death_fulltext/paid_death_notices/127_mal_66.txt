
        FARRELL-Paul V., at age 81 on August 6th. A resident of Barre, MA., and formerly of Oceanside, NY. He was predeceased by his wife, Maude Whitbread Farrell. He is survived by his wife, Theodora Cogley Farrell; his children: Barbara McTiernan, Paul, Mary, Rita and Peter Farrell; his sons and daughters-in-law Charles E. McTiernan, Jr., Denise Rinaldo, Michael Broad and Gene Harkless, and eight grandchildren. He also leaves five stepchildren and many other relatives and friends who loved and cherished him. During World War II, he served in the U.S. 3rd Army in England, France and Germany. After the War, he became a busines journalist. He was Editor of Purchasing magazine until 1968 and was associated with the National Association of Purchasing Management until his retirement in 1980. He served on the Boards of Directors of Commonweal magazine and of the Oceanside Free Public Library. He will always be remembered for his vast knowledge, his loyalty to friends and family, and his keen sense of humor. Friends may call at Macken's Mortuary, 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Centre, NY Friday, 2 to 4 PM and 7 to 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Ignatius Martyr Church, Long Beach, NY, Saturday, Aug 9, 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to the Commonweal Endowment, 475 Riverside Drive, New York, N.Y. 10115.
