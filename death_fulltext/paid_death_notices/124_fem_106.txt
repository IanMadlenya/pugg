
        KESTIN-Ethel. The Officers and Directors of Metropolitan Jewish Health System sadly mourn the untimely passing of Ethel Kestin, beloved and esteemed former Board member of Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Center. Her involvement with MJGC dates back, along with her late husband Samuel, more than half a century to the Brooklyn Hebrew Home and Hospital for the Aged, the Town League and other Affiliates. A devoted friend and ardent supporter, Ethel will be greatly missed. May her legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom she touched. Our profound sympathy and sincere condolences to the family. Mark L. Goldstein, President Eli S. Feldman, Executive VP Chief Executive Officer
        KESTIN - Ethel. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Ethel Kestin. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        KESTIN-Ethel F. The LIL of the Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Center is grieved at the loss of Ethel, a devoted Board member and avid worker. Our condolences to her family. Bea Teitelbaum, President Arlene Glass, Treasurer
        KESTIN-Ethel F. We're saddened at the loss of our dear and loving friend. We'll remember her love for art, for people and for life, and for being a wonderful woman. Bea and Bill
