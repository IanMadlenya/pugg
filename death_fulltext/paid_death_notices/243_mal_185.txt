
        HOSTETTER--Donald. Presbyterian pastor, church executive, and legendary pioneer in camping passed away peacefully on Easter Day, April 8, 2007. He is survived by his wife Joan, his sons Mark and Jonathan, five grandchildren, three stepchildren, and five stepgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held at First Presbyterian Church, Fifth Avenue and 11th Street, Saturday April 28, 2007 at 2 PM. Memorial gifts may be made to PCUSA Hostetter Fund at the Presbyterian Foundation, PO Box 406863, Atlanta, GA 30384-6863.
