
        WOLLY--Seymour, 86, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Farmingdale and Huntington, NY, and Rockville, MD, died September 3, 2004. Beloved husband for 62 years of Ida (Gillman), father of Michael and Marcia, grandfather of Peter, Mark, and Brian, father-in-law of Valentine (Nazarian), and uncle of Bruce Gillman. Sy was a graduate of NYU, a veteran of the Air Force in WWII and the Korean War, proprietor of Wolly's Hardware in Farmingdale, NY, and a volunteer at the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center in Commack, NY, and the Morse Geriatric Center of West Palm Beach, FL. An avid golfer, he was one of the first caddies at Bethpage Golf Course. He will be in our memories forever. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America, Greater Washington D.C. Chapter, 108 N. Alfred Street, Alexandria, VA 22314. Funeral service will be held September 7 at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 21 E. Deer Park Road, Dix Hills, NY. Call 631-499-6060 for time. Interment Huntington Jewish Center Cemetery.
