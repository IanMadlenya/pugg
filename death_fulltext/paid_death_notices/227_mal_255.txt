
        LEVINE--Robert. Died on December 17 after a long illness, with his wife, artist Emma Amos, and children, Nick and India, at his side. Born in Brooklyn in 1929, he attended Brooklyn Technical H.S. and the Cooper Union School of Engineering, was a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of NYU in 1951, and served in the Army. He was an international and N. American racing champion in the Flying Dutchman Junior Class in the 1960s and 70s, an avid photographer and editor, and an early computer enthusiast and consultant. He began his career as an advertising writer and retired as director of marketing for Standard Motor Products.
