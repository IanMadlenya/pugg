
        GORMAN--Althea Robinson Richards. Of Sewickley, PA. and Delray, FL died peacefully at her home on March 5, 2006. Wife of the late C. Snowdon Richards & the late Paul A. Gorman. She was predeceased by three of her four children and her parents William Christopher Robinson and Mary Laughlin Robinson. Survived by her daughter Althea Richards of Virginia; 11 grandchildren and 18 greatgrandchildren. For information: Richard D. Cole Funeral Home, Inc., Sewickley, PA.
