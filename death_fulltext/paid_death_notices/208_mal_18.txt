
        MORRISON--John C. A longtime resident of Water Mill, L.I., died on April 16 at his home in Naples, FL. A graduate of the Cranwell Prep School and Georgetown University, he served in the Marine Corps as a 1st Lt. during the Korean War. In Water Mill, he involved himself in various local charities and tirelessly chaired fundraising events. He served on the board of East End Hospice for many years and will be remembered for his many acts of kindness by both friends and associates. A funeral service will be held Saturday, May 8, at 11AM, at the Queen of the Holy Rosary Church in Bridgehampton. Contribu tions may be made to East End Hospice.
