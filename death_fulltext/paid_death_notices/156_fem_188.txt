
        HORNICK-Lita. On January 21, 2000 at age 72. Academic scholar receiving her Ph.D in English Literature from Columbia in 1958. Publisher of Kulchur Magazine, sponsored 20 years of poetry reading at the Museum of Modern Art. Patron and collector of contemporary paintings and sculpture. Beloved wife of tha late Morton J. Hornick. Loving mother of Louis and Blake Hornick and mother-in-law to Wendy and Liz. Cherished grandmother of Tripp and Matthew. Intermnet private.
