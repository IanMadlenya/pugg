
        SHEEHAN-John J. Of Point Lookout, LI, on May 2, 2002, in his 91st year. Son of the late Thomas and Teresa (nee Dolphin). Predeceased by brothers Thomas, William, Vincent, Paul and Conrad and cousin Helen Holland. Survived by his brothers and sister, Sr. Teresa, Society of Helpers, Charles, and Rev. Lawrence Sheehan, SJ, and sisters-in-law Anna and Margaret. Devoted uncle to 50 nieces and nephews. Graduate Manhattan College (1934), Fordham Law (1937). Retired Major, U.S. Army, World War II veteran, founder of Sheehan and Sheehan, labor law practice. Founding member of the Association of Catholic Trade Unionists. Reposing Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 2515 North Jerusalem Rd., East Meadow. Funeral Mass Tuesday morning Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal R.C. Church, Point Lookout. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Family will receive friends Sunday and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM.
