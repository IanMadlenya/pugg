
        OHRENSTEIN-Pauline. Of Delray Beach, Fla. died at her home on Wednesday, February 23, 2000 at the age of 87. She was pre-deceased by her husband Louis in 1992. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law Carole and Ken Mersel of New City, N.Y. and her sister Mary Karmazin of Hallandale, Fla. She was also survived by her 3 granddaughters Fran, Jill and Mindy. For more information contact Hellman Memorial Chapels, 15 State Street, Spring Valley, N.Y. 10977. 914-356-8600.
