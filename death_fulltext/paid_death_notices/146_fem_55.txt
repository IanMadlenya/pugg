
        LOWE-Mary Ruth Johnson. It is with deep sorrow that the family of Mary Ruth Johnson Lowe announces her death on Saturday, February 27th. A memorial service will be held on Friday, March 5th at 3 P.M., in the Nave of the Riverside Church, 120th Street and Riverside Drive.
        LOWE-Mary Johnson. We mourn the death of our beloved friend and colleague, Judge Mary Johnson Lowe who has been so close to our hearts for so many years. The Raskin Family
