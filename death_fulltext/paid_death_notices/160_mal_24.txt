
        TROY-Sidney. The Labor Zionist Alliance extends condolences to Shula and family on Sid's passing. A life-long Labor Zionist, he was a leader in Habonim, led American Aliya to Kibbutz, and was Executive Director of the Habonim Camping Association. He was a founder of the American Habonim Association which mobilize alumni in support of Habonim youth. His early leadership of the Habonim Dror Foundation was critical. He was a member of the LZA National Executive Committee. He will be missed. Jeffry Mallow, President Stephane Acel, Exec. Director
