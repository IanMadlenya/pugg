
        BERNSTEIN-Maurice. On February 10, 2000 in Keene, N.H., formerly of NYC. Loving father and fatherin-law of Peggy and Bill Heyman. Beloved grandfather of Lynn and Chris Hansen, Mark Heyman and Jay Heyman. Dear great-grandfather of Laura and Kyle Hansen. Raised in the Hebrew Orphan Asylum, he rose to become the HOA's Director until its closing in 1941. He had a distinguished career as a social worker retiring as Senior Consultant and Director of Community Planning from the Council of Jewish Federations and Welfare Funds. A memorial service will be held in NYC on March 13, 2000 at the 92nd St. YM-YWHA at 10:00 AM in the Weill Art Gallery.
