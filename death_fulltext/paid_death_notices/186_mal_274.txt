
        DERISE-Joseph P., 76. Arranger, songwriter, recording artist & jazz pianist in the New York Cabaret scene, formerlly of New York City, died on July 23, 2002, in Connecticut. Survived by his children James Derise of Yonkers and Fay Richardson of Poughkeepsie, two grandchildren, a sister Vivian Hackett of Nyack, a brother Alex Desiderato of Spring Valley, and a host of extended family and friends. A memorial mass will be held Thursday, August 1, 11am, at Holy Rosary Church, Fr. Salemi Dr., Ansonia, CT.
