
        MEYER-Eva. Young Israel of the West Side mourns the passing of Mrs. Eva Meyer, a distinguished founding member. She was the daughter of the late renowned Rabbi Dr. Jacob Hoffman and a descendant of Torah giants. We extend condolences to her husband Manfred and son David. May the Comforter of Zion console the entire family. Emanuel Gettinger, Rabbi Sheldon Fine, President
