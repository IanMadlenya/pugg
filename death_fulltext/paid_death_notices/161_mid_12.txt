
        BOTWINICK-Benjamin . With head bowed, we express our deep sorrow over the loss of Benjamin Botwinick, the father of Elaine Wolfensohn, staunch supporter of Israel's Open University and member of the Founders Society of the American Friends. We convey our condolences to the Botwinick and Wolfensohn families. May his memory be for a blessing. Irving M. Rosenbaum, National Chairman Ingeborg Rennert, NY Chair Eric G. Heffler, Executive V.P. American Friends of The Open University of Israel BOTWINICK - Benjamin. The American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra Board of Directors and staff wish to express our heartfelt condolences to Board Vice President Elaine Wolfensohn on the passing of her father. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Zubin Mehta and Itzhak Perlman, Co-Chairmen Herman S. Sandler, President Al Schussler, President Emeritus Suzanne K. Ponsot, Exec Director
