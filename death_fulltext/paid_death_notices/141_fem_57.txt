
        MILLS-Grace S. Wife of Ralph J. Mills of Baton Rouge, LA and formerly of Chappaqua, NY, passed away on September 29. She leaves a daughter, Amy Levert of Baton Rouge, a son, Philip Rodger of Charlotte, NC, a step-daughter, Leslie Ann Santos of Randolph, NJ, and four grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, please consider a donation to the Humane Society of Broward County, FL. or The Salvation Army.
