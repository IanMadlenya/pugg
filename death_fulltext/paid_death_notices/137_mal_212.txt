
        RINGLAND-Byron F., 63, of New York City, died Thursday, June 4, in his apartment of a sudden heart attack. He was born in Des Moines, IA, son the late John Ringland and Helen Forbes. He is survived by his brother, Jack F. Ringland of Hilton Head, SC, his nephews, John F. Ringland and Craig O. Ringland, and his two Alaskan Husky dogs, Alaska and Osok. A memorial service will be held at 6 PM, Thursday, June 25, at St. Malachy's, The Actor's Chapel, at 239 West 49 St., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Blue Hill Troupe, Ltd., on their 75th Anniversary, PO Box 6800, Yorkville Station, New York, NY 10128.
