
        GROSS--Gloria Lipson, of Great Neck, New York on November 19, 2006. Wife of Robert Gross, mother of Donald, Kenneth, and Jeffrey Gross and Betsy Hurowitz, sister of Joy Beal, grandmother of Lisa Gross and Noah Levine, mother-in-law of Su Kwak, Don Hurowitz, Julie Breskin. Community leader, Women's Right's Advocate, public television producer, award winning mediator, master gardener, world traveler, loving wife, mother, mother-in-law and grandmother, ready and loyal friend, life long devotee of New York City. She will be missed by all who knew her for her style, intelligence, independent and adventurous spirit and love of life. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be sent to the Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
