
        EBERHART--Pamela S. 59, of Greenwich, CT, died Monday after a lengthy illness. Born June 30, 1945 in Bethlehem, PA, Mrs. Eberhart was the daughter of the late Mr. & Mrs. James L. Shearer of Bethlehem, PA. She was the wife of the late Mr. Walter M. Eberhart, Jr. She is survived by her two daughters Julie Eberhart and Caroline Eberhart Grubb, a granddaughter, two sisters, four nieces and four nephews. A memorial service will be held at 3pm on Friday at Christ Church, Greenwich, CT.
