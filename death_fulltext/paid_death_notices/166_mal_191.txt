
        FLASH-Arthur G. 86, of Hartsdale, New York. Died November 16. Beloved longtime companion of Mary W. Kaufman. Husband of the late Hope K. (Billie) Flash. Brother of the late Malcolm Flash. He is survived by nephews, Michael and Douglas Flash. Arthur graduated from Princeton University and served his country during W.W.II in the China-Burma theatre. A trusted stockbroker, Arthur was formerly a member of Quaker Ridge Country Club in Scarsdale. No services.
