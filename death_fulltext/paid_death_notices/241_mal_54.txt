
        RIVERA--Gilbert, The Trustees, Staff and Auxiliary of Brooklyn Botanic Garden mourn the passing of our dear friend and Trustee Gilbert Rivera for the past nine years, Gilbert brought his broad experience and good humor to bear on the Garden's affairs. His love of the Garden and of Brooklyn and it's diverse communities will be deeply missed. We send our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Erminia, and to his family. Earl D. Weiner, Board Chairman Scot Medbury, President Constance Thatcher, Auxilary President
        $(6$)RIVERA--Gilbert The New York City Department of Small Business Services Minority- and WomenOwned Business Enterprise Advisory Board mourns the loss of our esteemed friend and advisor, Gilbert Rivera. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his entire family. Robert W. Walsh, Commissioner Heyward B. Davenport, Chair
        $(6$)RIVERA--Gilbert. The Partners of Pryor, Cashman, Sherman & Flynn LLP mourn the passing of Gilbert Rivera, one of the great citizens of Brooklyn and this nation.
