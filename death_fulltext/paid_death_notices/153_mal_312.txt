
        GABLEMAN-Arthur. Age 87, of Sunrise, Fla. formerly of Flushing, NY on October 23, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia. Devoted father of David, Paul and Bruce. Loving father-in-law of Marge, Joan & Grace. Cherished grandfather of Glenn, Gregg, Amy and Keith. His kindness and sense of humor will remain everlasting memories with all who knew and loved him.
