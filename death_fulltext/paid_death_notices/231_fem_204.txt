
        RIBADENEYRA -- Virginia Gable, 78, formerly of New York City and Jacksonville, Florida, died Friday, April 21, 2006, in Shelby, North Carolina. She was preceded in death by her husband John Anthony Ribadeneyra. Born October 30, 1927 in Altoona, Pennsylvania, Ginny graduated from Vassar College and subsequently moved to New York City where she worked for Life Magazine. She is survived by a son, Dr. Michael Ribadeneyra of Shelby; a daughter, Dr. Elizabeth Bozeman of Winston-Salem; and four grandchildren. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to the Vassar College, 124 Raymond Avenue, Poughkeepsie, New York 12604.
