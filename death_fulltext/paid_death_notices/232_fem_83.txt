
        KUSHNER--Celia. 79, passed away on May 10, 2006, in her home after a long valiant struggle with cancer. Beloved wife of Milton for 60 years. Loving mother of Claudia Wolloch and Ron Kushner. Adored grandmother of Adam, Benjamin and Zachary. Cherished sister of Harold Kfare. Mother - in - law of Elaine and Norbert. She lived her life as an artist and touched with beauty and grace the lives of so many. She will live in our hearts forever.
