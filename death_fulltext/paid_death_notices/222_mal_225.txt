
        RINGLER--Isidore. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the husband of our member, Miriam Ringler, father of Rachel Ringler and father-in-law of Yossi Siegel. May their entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion & Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Joseph S. Kaplan, President Paulette Papilsky, Sisterhood
        RINGLER -- Isidore (Izzy). Born February 27, 1922. Adoring husband of Miriam, proud father of Cheryl (Steven), Louise (Mitchell) and Rachel (Yossi). Cherished grandfather of Adam (Melissa), Joshua (Penny) & Sara Schildkraut, Sarene, Ariel and Matthew Leeds, Alana, Sam and Talia Siegel, Howard (Jaime) and Paul Jeruchimowitz. Greatgrandfather of Madeline, Eli, Jonah, Caylie and Natalie. Survived by sisters Esther Preisler, Trude Vick & Goldie Bagatelle. Izzy served with pride and distinction with The Ozarks 102nd Infantry Division in WWII. Like his name, Yitzhak, he had an easy laugh. His wife, children, grandchildren and greatgrandchildren were his whole world and his legacy. Funeral Friday 10 AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 91st Street.
