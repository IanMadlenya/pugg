
        BACHMAN--Lewis M. Friend and colleague for over 30 years. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Ann, their children Howard, Terri and Gary and their progeny. Services on July 6 at 1:30 pm Robert Schoem Funeral Home, Paramus, NJ. Davida and Alvin Deutsch
