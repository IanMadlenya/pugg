
        POMERANCE-Norman, 72, former book publishing executive and engineer, on November 23, 1998. Husband of Paula Diamond and the late Carol Joan Raynor. Services at 10:45 AM on Friday, November 27 at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to the American Diabetes Association. Friends are welcome at the family residence on Friday from 4-7 P.M., and on Saturday from 1-4 P.M.
