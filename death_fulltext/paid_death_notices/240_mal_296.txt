
        MANERI--K. Peter. A Memorial Mass will be held for Mr. Maneri at Holy Family Church in New Rochelle, N.Y. on Saturday, Jan. 27, at 11 a.m. Mr. Maneri died of cancer on Jan. 15 in Tulsa. Born in 1945, Keith Peter Maneri was raised on Staten Island and graduated from Xaverian High School in Brooklyn. Mr. Maneri entered Iona College in New Rochelle, N.Y., in 1963, beginning a 44-year association with Iona that would culminate in service as a member of the colleges Board of Trustees. Mr. Maneri enjoyed an extensive career in public relations, holding managerial positions of increasing responsibility with Iona, Dunn & Bradstreet Corporation, Texaco Inc. in Harrison and London, and New York-based Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide, and Computer Sciences Corporation of California. Mr. Maneri is survived by his loving wife, Janet, his son, Keith, 21, his daughter, Leigh Ann, 17, sisters, Eileen Marinakis, andKathleen Mead and a brother Dennis. Mr. Maneri lived inManhattan beach, Calif. and his body was interred in Los Angeles. The Memorial Mass will be followed immediately by a reception at Iona's Spellman hall. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the christian Brothers Foundation, 145 Huguenot St., Rm. 402, New RoChelle, N.Y. 10804.
