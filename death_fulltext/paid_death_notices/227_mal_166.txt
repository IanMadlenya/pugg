
        FRIEDMAN--Sanford, 79. On December 13, 2005. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Betty; his three children, Steven, Kathy and Richard; and his brother, Arnold, as well as his four grandchildren, Chanel, Kristina, Alex and Andy. He lived in Boca Raton, FL following his retirement from his position as a textile industry executive in 1989. A great husband, father and grandfather, and a dedicated family man who will be deeply missed. The family will be available for visitation from Saturday through Tuesday, December 17-20 at 87 Centre Street, Woodmere, NY for those wishing to share their memories of a life well lived. Funeral services will be held on Monday, December 19 at 11:30 AM at The Boulevard Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY (516295-3100). Donations can be made in Sandy's name to the American Cancer Society.
