
        GOLDSMITH--Lawrence C., 87, of Fairfax, VT, died at home March 7. Artist, author of ''Watercolor Bold and Free,'' and watercolor teacher to hundreds, he is survived by his wife Lynda, his sister Helen Grunebaum of NYC, his sister Lucille Roland of Abington, PA, his son Paul, daughter-in-law Peta, granddaughters Columbine, Tamsin, Alice and Rebecca, all of Calabasas, CA, and many devoted nephews and nieces. A Memorial gathering will be announced at a future date.
