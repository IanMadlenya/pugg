
        WARMS - Dorothy Glenz. Age 90. Died peacefully at home November 26, 2002. Survived by her daughters, Lisbeth Orgel (Charles) and Miggie Warms, and by her granddaughter, Sara A. Orgel, and grandson, Daniel Richard Orgel. Born July 31, 1912, Dorothy Warms was a graduate of Hunter College H.S. and of Barnard College. Her work at Rehabilitation International now RI and later at United Cerebral Palsy of NYC, took her all over the world, and she leaves behind friends in many countries. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to RI or UCP.
