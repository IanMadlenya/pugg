
        JAKOBOVICS-Helene. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of or our esteemed, longtime member Helene Jakobovics, beloved mother of our member Alphonse Jakobovics, and of Sylvain (Evelyn) Jakobovics and Elizabeth Oestericher. May her entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
