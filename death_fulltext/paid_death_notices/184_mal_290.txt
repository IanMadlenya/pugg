
        ATTRIDE-Roy Ronald Buxton, Jr., 59, died peacefully but unexpectedly on May 16, 2002. He was born in New York City on November 24, 1942 to parents Roy Ronald Buxton Attride, Sr. and Anne Earle Attride of Cold Spring Harbor, NY. Mr. Attride is survived by his family and loved ones. A memorial service to celebrate the life of Mr. Attride will be held in Raleigh, NC on June 22, 2002.
