
        PENNYBACKER-Jean Rodney Kintner. Died July 9, 1999 after a long and productive life. Active in politics, social causes and environmental change, Jean was a tenacious Democrat. Married to Robert Kintner, President of NBC and White House Counsel, and later to Miles Pennybacker. Jean served as Board Member for The Museum of Modern Art, The U.S. Peace Corp, Channel Thirteen, and Save The Children. She is survived by her three children, Susan Kintner, of Westport, CT, Michael Kintner, of Suffield, CT, and Jeffrey Kintner, of Pfafftown, N.C., and five grandchildren. To celebrate her life, her family invites all who knew her to a prayer service and reception at Christ and Holy Trinity Church, in Westport, CT, on Sunday July 18, 1999, at 3 P.M. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that contributions be made to The Jean Pennybacker Memorial Fund, Save The Children, 54 Wilton Road, Westport, CT. 06880.
