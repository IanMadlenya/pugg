
        LIPNICK-Daniel. Age 85, on August 11, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Gloria Lipnick. His strength, vigor and unfailing good humor will be sadly missed by his children, Iris Convissar and Bruce Lipnick, their spouses Michael Convissar and Eunyce Lipnick, grandchildren Glenna Cohen, Beth Maycon, Amanda Lipnick and Olivia Lipnick, and great-grandchildren Emily and Adam Cohen, and Hannah Maycon. Also survived by sisters Helen Sakol, Ruth Greenwald, brothers Marvin and Jerry Lipnick, countless relatives and friends. Funeral services 11:30 AM, Wednesday, August 15, at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn. Interment at Old Montefiore Cemetery, Springfield Blvd, Queens.
