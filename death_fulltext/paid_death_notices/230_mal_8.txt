
        PERRY--Donald Dunham, 83, of Milford, NJ, and North Truro, MA, on February 23, 2006. Harvard graduate, US Army Air Corps WWII, retired research chemist. Survived by wife of 48 years, Helen (Knubel), sons Thomas (Judy) of Easley, SC, Andrew of Newton, MA, and David of Santa Cruz, CA, and granddaughter, Anya. Memorial service March 25, 1 PM, First United Church of Christ in Milford, where he was a deacon and sang in the choir.
