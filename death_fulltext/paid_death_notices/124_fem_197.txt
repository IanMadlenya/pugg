
        KIRBY-Joyce A., 80, died at home, May 27, 1997. Ms. Kirby was born in New Rochelle, N.Y. She received a Master's Degree in Social Work from Columbia University. She had a long, and distinguished career as a Social Worker in New York City, the Virgin Islands and Florida. She was a beloved supervisor and mentor to several generations of clinicians. She is survived by a niece Susan Pinco of New Jersey. Moss-Feaster Funeral Homes Clearwater, Florida.
