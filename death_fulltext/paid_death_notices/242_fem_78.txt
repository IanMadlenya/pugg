
        BADER--Amanda, age 51, died February 28, 2007 in Ocala, FL from injuries sustained while doing what she loved best, riding her horse in an Eventing competition. She leaves a very large hole in the lives of her husband, Philippe Steinmann, her parents Bob and Helen Bader, her sister Stephanie and her sisterin-law Cathy as well as innumerable friends and relatives. Contributions may be made to the Amanda Bader Fund at Winslow Therapeutic Center, 328 Route 17A, Warwick, NY 10990.
