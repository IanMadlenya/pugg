
        LEVINE--Burton. Of Rye Brook, NY. Died May 24, 2005, at age 81. Born March 28, 1924, in Brooklyn to Julius and Rose (Kornberg) Levine. He served in the US Navy during WWII. Burton was the owner of The Village Shoe Store in Scarsdale, NY. He was a member of Willow Ridge Country Club, a Hadassah Associate and a volunteer at White Plains Hospital. Survived by his wife Diane (Kalmenson), children Jay (wife Patricia) Levine, Donald (wife Patricia) Levine and Gwen (husband Mark) Parnett, twin granddaughters Natalie and Nicole Levine and a sister Alma Shore (husband Hyman). Funeral service Friday, 12 noon Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, NY, 914-381-1809 followed by interment at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery. Donations to the charity of your choice are requested.
