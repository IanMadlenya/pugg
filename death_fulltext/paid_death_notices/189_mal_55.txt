
        HARVEY - Gerald Joseph. July 27, 1925-October 2, 2002. We mourn the loss of our loving husband, father, grandfather and brother. While we are deeply saddened, we are relieved that he no longer suffers. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, October 6, 2002, 9:45 A.M. at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
