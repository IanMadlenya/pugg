
        MILLMAN--Neil H. The partners and staff of Hecht and Company, P.C. are deeply saddened by the passing of Neil H. Millman, Esq. Neil was associated with our firm for over 30 years and played an integral part of its success. He was not only a brilliant tax advisor but also a person who cared deeply about the well being of his clients. He leaves a void which will not be easily filled. Our sincerest condolences are extended to his wife Susan, to his son Matthew and to the other members of his family. Donald Hecht and Michael Hecht
