
        KLEIN-Steve. The staff, students and families of PS 85X mourn the untimely passing of Steve Klein, teacher and guidance counselor at our school for 30 years. His caring and professionalisn have enriched the lives of generations of students. He will be profoundly missed by the entire PS 85 community. We extend deepest sympathy to his wife, Barbara, and to his children, Wade and Abby.
