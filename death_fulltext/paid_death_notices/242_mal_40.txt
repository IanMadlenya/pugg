
        COX--Denton Sayer, M.D. of Manhattan 79, died March 3, 2007. Son of Doctor and Mrs. William Wheeler Cox of Montclair, NJ. A graduate of Yale University and Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons. He was honored by and a member of numerous Medical and Scientific Associations and Societies. He was Associate Professor at Cornell University Medical College and Associate Attending Physician at NY Hospital. He is survived by his devoted family; sister Patricia Cox Mansfield her husband Peter, their children Peter, Geoffrey, Timothy, Pamela, their spouses and eight great nieces and nephews. Gifts in his memory may be sent to The Denton Sayer Cox Professorship, Development Office, NY Presbyterian Hospital Weill Cornell Medical Center, 525 East 68th St. NY, NY 10021.
