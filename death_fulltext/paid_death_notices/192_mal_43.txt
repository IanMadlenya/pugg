
        WOOD-Daniel P., 88. Of Midlothian, Virginia, formerly of Delray Beach, Florida and Locust Valley, New York died January 1, 2003. He was preceded in death by his wife Edna M. Wood. Survivors include two sons, Donald C. Wood and his wife Janet and Roy G. Wood and his wife Nancy and four granddaughters, Erin Wood, Lauren Wood, Leigh Anna Wood and Adrianne Wood. A memorial service will be held 3:30 PM Monday, Woodlake United Methodist Church, Midlothian, Virginia. Inurnment private. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to your favorite charity.
