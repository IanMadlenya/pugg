
        JONES-Rees. Died peacefully on April 27, 1999, at Beth Israel Hospital. Survived by his wife Betty, family members and many friends. He enjoyed life fully with his love of music, travel, lawn bowling, and the company of friends and family. Memorial service will be held at First Presbyterian Church, 12th St, and Fifth Ave., Manhattan. Sunday, May 16th, 1999, at 2:30pm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to First Presbyterian Church Music Fund, 12 W. 12 St., NY, NY 10011, would be appreciated.
