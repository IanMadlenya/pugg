
        GOVE-Wayne D. Of Mount Kisco, N.Y. (Reader's Digest Promotion Director). Mr. Gove joined Reader's Digest in 1967. He rose to the rank of Promotion Director and was responsible for creating the famed digest style which helped turn the small magazine into a publishing giant. He is known throughout the industry as one of the truly great promotion writers of his time. In addition to his wife Patricia and his mother Rose of Saint Paul, MN., he is survived by his children Nancy Lynch, Judith Quinones and John W. Gove. Also survived by six grandchildren. Visitation 5-9 P.M. Thursday and Friday at the Cassidy-Flynn Funeral Home, Mount Kisco. Services are at 10 A.M., Saturday, Mount Kisco Presbyterian Church.
