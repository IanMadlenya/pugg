
        SCHWARTZ-Stanley, of Lawrence, NY, and Boca Raton, Fla. On December 7, 2000. He was a longtime executive of Friendship Dairies, an avid golfer, and dedicated philanthropist, serving on the Board of Directors of Temple Beth-El of Long Beach, the Peninsula Counseling Center, and the Long Beach Medical Center. Beloved husband of Helen. Loving father of Lawrence (Lois) Schwartz, Judy (David) Feldman and Gail (Robert) Lutolf. Devoted grandfather of Jenny and Joshua Schwartz and Pamela Lutolf. Dear brother of Phyllis Farber. His family and friends will miss him every day, every way. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI, 11:30 AM, Sunday.
