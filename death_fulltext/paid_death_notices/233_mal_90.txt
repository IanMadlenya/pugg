
        LAZARUS--The Honorable Eli, 87 years old, died on June 7, 2006. Retired Judge of The Criminal Court of The City of New York. Retired Deputy Chief Inspector of The New York City Police Department. President of The Captains Endowment Association for eight years. He earned four university degrees including a BA from CCNY in 1938 and was elected Phi Beta Kappa, a Master's Degree in Education in French, an LLB from Brooklyn Law School in 1947 and a JSD from Brooklyn Law School in 1949. He also served in the Army Air Corps in Hawaii during World War II. He is survived by his beloved wife of 65 years, Bernice (nee Shapiro). He was the devoted father of George and Shelly Lazarus and Robert and Jill Lazarus and a cherished grandfather of Theodore, Sa mantha, Benjamin, Anna and Eli. Services Friday 1PM at ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opp. LIRR), Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Society of Honorary Police Surgeons Scholarship Fund, 133 East 73 Street New York, NY 10021.
