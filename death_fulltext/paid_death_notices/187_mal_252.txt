
        O'BRIEN - Kevin Michael. With sadness we announce the accidental passing of Michael, 37, on Monday, August 19. He will be deeply missed by his mother, Tiiu, his father, Denis; his sister, brother-inlaw and nephews, Jennifer, Dwight, Drew and Jayden; his brother, sister-in-law and niece, Connor, Louise Anne and Mikaela; and his brother John; and his many friends. A celebration in his memory will be held on Tuesday, August 27, between 6 & 8 PM, at Bloom Ballroom, 48 West 23rd Street. A donation in his name to Habitat for Humanity, 334 Furman Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201, would be appreciated. Michael will be remembered for his dedication to Habitat for Humanity, his generosity, kindness, wit, gentle manner and his love for music.
