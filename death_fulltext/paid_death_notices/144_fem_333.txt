
        TERWILLIGER - Roberta. Of Bronxville, NY. Died January 29 at age 91. Devoted wife of the late Charles Terwilliger and beloved mother of Anne T. Olsen of Laramie, WY, and Robert Terwilliger of Coconut Grove, FL. An active volunteer in Planned Parenthood, Radcliffe in Westchester, the Urban League, the United Negro College Fund, and the Girl Scouts. Services private. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions to a local Planned Parenthood center or any of the charities she supported so tirelessly.
