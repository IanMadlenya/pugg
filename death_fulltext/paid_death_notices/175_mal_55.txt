
        BURTIS-Theodore A. III. Of Brooklyn, NY, on August 1, 2001, after a long illness. A gifted and public spirited architect, and founding partner of Buttrick, White & Burtis. Beloved husband of Judy and loving father of Theo. In addition to his wife and son, Mr. Burtis is survived by his father, Theodore A. Burtis, II, and two sisters, Barbara Shipps and Pamela Bartholomew. A memorial service will be held on August 15, 2001 at 11:15 AM in the Fordham University Church at the Rose Hill campus in the Bronx. Additional information, Whitting Funeral Home, Glen Head, New York, (516) 671-0807.
