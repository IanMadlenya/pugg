
        FUQUA-Stephen O. Jr, 88, retired General US Army, of Cornwall-onHudson NY died at Westchester Medical Center April 28, 1999. He is survived by his wife Frances Hathaway Fuqua at home, four sons and one daughter, four stepchildren. Funeral service will be at Old Cadet Chapel U.S. Military Academy at West Point NY 1:30pm May 3, burial West Point NY.
