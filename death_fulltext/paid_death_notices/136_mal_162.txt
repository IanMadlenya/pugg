
        ROTHENBERG-Richard. May 15, 1997 (9 Iyar 5757). Beloved Stuyvesant HS alumnus, teacher, and Math Department Chairman. Dr. Rothenberg influenced thousands of students and scores of teachers with his scholarship, award-winning teaching ability, guidance, and warmheartedness. His gentlemanly, humane nature lives on in those who had the privilege of knowing him.
