
        CONNELLY-Kathryn L., 80. Of Vero Beach, FL. formerly of Brookville, NY died September 10, 2001. Mrs. Connelly was one of the founders of The School of the Holy Child, Old Westbury, New York; and a former member of Nassau Country Club, Glen Cove, New York. Survivors include three sons, William P. Connelly, Jr. of New York City; Peter J. Connelly of Sebastian, FL and Brian G. Connelly of Cardiff By The Sea, CA and a daughter, Elizabeth K. Connelly of New York City. She was predeased by her husband, William P. Connelly. For information Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach
