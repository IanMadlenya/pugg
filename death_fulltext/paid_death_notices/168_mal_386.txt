
        TRENT-Philip, 93. Died peacefully in his bed at The Actors' Fund Home on January 24, 2001. Devoted and beloved father of Peter Trent, father-in-law of May Wong Trent, grandfather of Christopher and Mary Helen, husband of the late Isabel Carson and the late Estelle Fohrmeister. His kind and loving presence will be missed by all whose lives he touched. Reposing at Wien & Wien, 402 Park St (corner of Park and Passaic), Hackensack, NJ (1-800-322-0533) from 3-5PM Friday, January 26th with memorial service at 4:30PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to The Actors' Fund Home, 155-175 West Hudson Ave, Englewood, New Jersey 07631.
