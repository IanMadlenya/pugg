
        KAPLAN-Lester. On Feb. 4, 1997, of cancer. Cherished husband of Gladys Kaplan, beloved father of Jeffrey and Scott Kaplan, and daughter-in-law Debra Kaplan. Adored grandfather of Seth and Alexandra Kaplan, beloved brother and brother-in-law of Helen and Ben Lenetsky. In his 72nd year. The world has been a better place for having had such a truly good soul inhabit it. His presence will be sorely missed, but his memory will always linger in the hearts of the many who loved him. In lieu of gifts, donations to the cancer organization of your choice, in his memory, will be appreciated. Services will be held ''Wien & Wien'', 129 Engle Street, Englewood, New Jersey, on Thursday at noon.
