
        DUDINE--William F., Jr. 75, died December 27th at Thomas Jefferson University Hospital in Philadelphia of complications from a stroke. He is survived by his wife, Jean Phillips Worley; his children Mary Dudine Kyle and William F. Dudine III, both of Brooklyn, and Jane Dudine of Southampton, five grandchildren, two sisters, and three stepchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife, Frances Confort Dudine, in 1996. Bill was born in Jasper, Indiana, graduatedmagna cum laude from University of Notre Dame in 1953 and received a J.D. from Cornell University Law School in 1960. He was a lieutenant in the U.S. Navy. He was a partner at the law firm Darby and Darby in Manhattan where he practiced patent and trademark law for 37 years. For many years he lived in Eastern Long Island and was a board member of Concerned Citizens of Montauk and The Group for the South Fork. In later years he was active in the Central Baptist Church of Wayne, PA. He volunteered in many ways at the Crosslands Retirement Community where he lived with his wife. Memorial services will be held at Central Baptist Church in Wayne, PA at 2:00 PM on January 13, 2007 and at Crosslands Retirement Community, Kennett Square, PA at 4:00 PM January 14, 2007. Memorial gifts may be made to Central Baptist Church, P.O. Box 309, Wayne, PA 19087 or to the KendalCrosslands Child Development Scholarship, P.O. Box 100, Kennett Square, PA 19348, attn. Denise Deaver.
