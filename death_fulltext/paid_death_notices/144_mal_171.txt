
        PUSHMAN-Armand. January 8, 1999, age 98. Beloved son of the late Hovsep and the late Hulia Pushman, devoted brother of the late Arsene Pushman. He was the last surviving son of the celebrated American Armenian artist Hovsep Pushman. Armand and his brother were partners in Pushman & Company dealing in fine oriental rugs. Armand was a talented musician having studied the viola in Paris with Maurice Vieux in his youth and enjoyed playing chamber music to the present. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call Sunday and Monday 2-5 and 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Service Tuesday, January 12th, 1 PM, at The Armenian Evangelical Church, 152 East 34 St, NYC. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Juilliard School Armenian Scholarship Fund.
