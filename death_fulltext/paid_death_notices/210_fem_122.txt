
        McHUGH--Ann. The members of the Board of Directors of Helping Hands: Monkey Helpers for the Disabled are deeply saddened by the passing of Ann McHugh. We send our heartfelt condolences to the McHugh family. She was a superlative and generous friend of Helping Hands who made our existence possible. She will be greatly missed and always remembered. We will celebrate her life through our continued work. Robert Stern, Chair, Board of Directors Judi Zazula, Exec. Dir. Helping Hands: Monkey Helpers for the Disabled
