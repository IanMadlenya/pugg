
        McMENAMIN -- Joan Stitt. The trustees, faculty, and staff of Poly Prep Country Day School mourn the passing of Joan Stitt McMenamin, headmistress emerita of Nightingale-Bamford School, friend to Poly Prep, and visionary educator. We extend our condolences to her brother, her sister, and to the entire Nightingale-Bamford community. Donald Carswell, Chairman David Harman, Headmaster Poly Prep Country Day School, Brooklyn, NY.
        McMENAMIN -- Joan Stitt. The Friends of the Amherst College Library are saddened by the death of this loyal supporter and daughter of the first Chairman of the group. Sam Ellenport, Chairman
