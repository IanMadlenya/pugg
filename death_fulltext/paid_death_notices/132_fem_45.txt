
        MOKARZEL-Yemna Helen. Died January 2, 1998. Daughter of Salloum and Helen Kalil Mokarzel. She is survived by her sisters Mary Mokarzel and Alice Jaoudi, and many loving nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her sisters Rose Tanous and Lila Hatab. She attended Columbia University School of Journalism; she served for a time as Lebanon correspondent for the Lebanese American Journal (the English version of Al Hoda Daily, established 1898). Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, 9:30 AM, St. Vincent Ferrer, Lexington and 66th. Donations may be made in her name to Catholic Near East Welfare, 1011 First Ave, NYC.
