
        CUMMINGS-Paul. 64. On February 7, of complications following a stroke. Former curator of Drawings at the Whitney Museum of American Art; past president of the Drawing Society. Beloved brother of Rita L. Yankasammy; brother-in-law of Sam; uncle of Devicka and Krishna of Garden Grove, CA. Donations may be made in his memory to The Library Fellows of the Whitney Museum, 945 Madison Ave, New York, NY 10021, or to the Poetry Project at St. Marks Church, 131 E. 10th St, New York, NY 10003. A memorial service will be announced at a future date.
