
        NEWMAN-Donald H. April 13, 1928-March 22, 2002. His loss is mourned by his wife Francoise, his daughters Hope, Hillary, Robin and Vanessa, his grandchild Isabelle, and his three sons-in-law. Memorial will be held April 15, at St. Paul's Chapel, Columbia University, from 2-3 PM, followed by a gathering in the Chapel's Red Room. The 117th St. & Amsterdam Avenue gate will be opened for access. In lieu of flowers please make a contribution to the Central Park Conservancy.
