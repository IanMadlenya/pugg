
        BRODY-Adele Cecile. A remarkable woman, vivacious, intelligent and witty, generous and concerned, brave and beautiful, memorable to all that met her. Her many friends will miss her companionship. Wife of the late Andrew Brody for 49 years. Mother of Cora B. Marcus and Ellen K. Brody. Grandmother of Ezra A. Marcus, Joseph W. Marcus and Amy M. Savath, who will always feel the loss but will cherish the years and memories. She made a difference. Memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel at 11 AM, Wednesday, September 12.
