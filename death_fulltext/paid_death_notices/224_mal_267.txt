
        VISLOCKY--Nicholas W. The Board of Trustees, officers and staff of The Green-Wood Cemetery mourn the loss of our dear friend and Superintendent, Nicholas W. Vislocky. With passion, commitment and good humor he served Green-Wood for more than 34 years, five of those years as its Superintendent. His many contributions to the beauty of the Cemetery and his abiding love of GreenWood will be deeply missed. We extend our condolences to his wife, Marie, and to his children, Lisa and Nicholas. The Board of Trustees of The Green-Wood Cemetery
