
        BRODKIN--Sylvia Z. 89, April 21, after a long illness. B.A. Brooklyn College, M.A. NYU. She taught English at West Hempstead High School and was chair, English Department 15 years before retiring. She co-authored texts and anthologies on literature. In her retirement she lectured at the Institute for Continuing Education at Palm Beach Community College. Survived by husband Irving, sons Marc (Antoinette) and Joel, granddaughter Mary, step-grandchildren Kris and Adam Rainey, and step-great-granddaughter Emma. She will be remembered for her uncommon courage, generosity and optimism.
