
        RUBIN--Sam. Died in Palm Beach, FL. on February 13, 2006. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Devoted father of Phyllis Leighton. Grandfather of Greg and David Leighton. Great-grandfather of Zachary, Mitchell, Jennifer and Allison. Sam was a legend in both the toy industry and in the thoroughbred racing world. Sam and Dorothy were the owners of John Henry, a champion race horse, in the early 1980's. An incredibly warm and loving man, Sam has left his footprint on our planet. He will always be in our hearts.
