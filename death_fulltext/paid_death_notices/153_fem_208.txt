
        ZIEGLER-Barbara Sutro. Of New York City, on October 21, 1999 at the age of 91. Widow of Dr. James E. Ziegler, Jr. She will be greatly missed by her brother-in-law, Karl Ziegler, her nieces, Lady Barbara Bowen Larcom and Mary Sutro Callender, her nephews, Dr. Michael Sedgwick Sutro and Denis O'Sullivan Sutro, her many loving relatives and friends. An accomplished photographer and artist, Mrs. Ziegler was a lifelong and generous patron of the arts. In lieu of flowers, a contribution may be made to the New York Philharmonic or your favorite charity.
