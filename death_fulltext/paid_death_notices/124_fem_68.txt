
        BLINKEN-Ethel H., died in Palm Beach, Florida, after a long illness on May 7. She was born in New York City in 1903. In 1924, she married Maurice H. Blinken who died in 1986. She is survived by her sons, Ambassador Donald Blinken of Budapest, Robert Blinken of Bedford Hills and Ambassador Alan Blinken of Brussels and nine grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Interment will be private.
