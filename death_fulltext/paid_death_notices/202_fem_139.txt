
        CAIOLA--Hedwig Birkenfeld, born February 21, 1925 in New York. A resident of New York, Geneva and Rome, she died peacefully on November 13th in Rome. A loving wife and mother, she is survived by husband Dr. Nicola Caiola; children Amelia, Sara and Francesco; granddaughter Chiara; and sons-in law Laurence Ross and Chandran Madhu. Memorial gifts may be made to the Alzheimer's Association of New York, 360 Lexington Avenue, NY, NY 10017. The family will also receive friends in New York at a later date.
