
        PSAROUDIS - Jovanka, passed quietly at her beloved ''home with a view'' in Northport, New York, on May 24, 2000. Jovanka was a breeder, exhibitor and fancier of Scottish Terriers. Loving wife of Nicholas. Her funeral service will be held at noon on Tuesday, May 30, 2000, at St. Paraskevi G.O. Church, Greenlawn, New York. Her interment will be at the Cedar Grove Cemetery, Flushing, NY. For information contact The Nolan & Taylor-Howe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Avenue, Northport, NY.
