
        GARFINKLE-Jerome. Died on May 4, 2000, in Great Neck, NY. Born in Riga, Latvia, on March 10, 1906. Beloved husband of Raye Gewirtz Garfinkle for 66 years, father of Helen Garfinkle of Tucson, AZ, and Ann Garfinkle-Brent of Washington, DC, grandfather of Duncan, David and William Brent, and great-grandfather of Malcolm and Eli Brent, of Silver Spring, MD. Jerome led a full and satisfying life, filled with love. He founded Youthful Fashions, worked as a manufacturer of women's dresses until he was 88 years old. Fishing and gardening were his passions with many neighbors and friends enjoying his fresh fish and tomatoes. He was a mensch. Services Monday, 9:45 AM, at Temple Beth-El of Great Neck, 5 Old Mill Rd. Interment Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
