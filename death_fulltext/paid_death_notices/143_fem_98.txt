
        SLOTKIN-Sarah A. Artist and teacher. Beloved and loving wife of Aaron and mother of Deborah Orin and Mark. Died December 9 at age 86. She loved life and she painted with joy. We loved her and we will miss her. Services Sunday, December 13, at 12 noon, Gramercy Memorial Chapel, 152 2nd Avenue, Manhattan.
        SLOTKIN-Sarah Weingart. The Board of Directors of the Scholarship and Welfare Fund of the Alumni Association of Hunter College mourns the death of Sarah Weingart Slotkin, a director of the Fund more than 24 years. Her generosity, wise counsel and dedication on behalf of the students of her alma mater will long be remembered. Our deep sympathy is extended to her family. Joan M. Lewis, President Scholarship and Welfare Fund of the Alumni Association of Hunter College
