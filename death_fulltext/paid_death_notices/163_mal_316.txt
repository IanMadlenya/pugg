
        WEISSMAN-Howard Stanford, 81. Died August 16 in Durham, N.C. A native of New York City, he relocated in Durham in 1994 from Vieques, P.R. Mr. Weissman was a publicist in NY. Mr. Weissman is survived by three children, six grandchildren. Memorials to the Jewish Family Service, 3700 Lyckan Pkwy, Durham, N.C. 27707.
