
        NECKMAN - Arnold Samuel. Passed away at his home in Hollywood, FL January 1, 2000 shortly after his 67th birthday. Husband to his great love of 50 years Shelley. Beloved father of Chuck, Steven and Beth. Loving grandfather of Andrew, David and Allison. He will surely be missed. Arnold was born in the Bronx, educated at the Peddie School and Tufts Univ. He established himself as a builder and contractor in the northeast and in Florida before entering the retail business with his son. After many years on the water, he became an avid golfer. Of his many successes, he was proudest of his children and gained his greatest joy from his family and close friends. He will be remembered by all as a gentleman and a gentle man.
