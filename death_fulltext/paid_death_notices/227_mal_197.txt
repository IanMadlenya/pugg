
        GROSS--Robert. December 17, 2005, at the age of 78, loving husband of Gloria for 54 years, devoted father of Donald, Kenneth, Jeffrey, Elizabeth, grandfather of Lisa and Noah, son of George and Anna Gross, brother of Alice Gross Newhouse and Gerald Gross, father-in-law of Su Kwak and Don Hurowitz. Bachelor of Civil Engineering, Cornell University, 1949. Masters in History, Long Island University, 2001. Real estate developer, business executive, civic leader, WWII Navy veteran, historian, enthusiastic traveler, outdoorsman, and photographer, he will be greatly missed by all who knew him for his kindness, wisdom, humor and intellectual curiosity. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Tuesday, 12:30 pm. Interment Salem Fields Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society.
