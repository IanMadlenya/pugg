
        KING-Sid. Died on Monday, On December 18, 2000. Beloved husband of Clara Greenspan King. Father of Jerry King and Sheila Wank. Grandfather of Matthew and Jonathan Wank, Steffi King Kitzes and Patti King. Great grandfather of Jordan Spencer Wank. He was a former teacher at the Franklin School, Director of Student Activities at the Borough of Manhattan Community College and the owner and director of Camp Cherokee, Beach Lake, PA. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to City College of New York Athletic Program, C/O The Office of Development, Convent Ave at 138 Street, Shepard Hall, Room 154, New York, N.Y. 10031.
