
        DONENFELD - Israel James. Composer, pianist and teacher of music, in his 82nd year, on January 19, 1999. Beloved husband of Anne; devoted father of Ken, Howard and Jane; adored grandpa of Elissa and Jon, Gil and Ron, Shalom and Romi. Funeral on Friday, January 22 at 10:45 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Joint Passover Association, UJA.
