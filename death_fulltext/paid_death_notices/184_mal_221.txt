
        FALLIS-George A. Of Valley Stream, NY on May 11, 2002 at the age of 90. He was an Archon of the Greek Orthodox Church of America and an incredibly loving and generous man to all who knew him. Survived by his wife of 64 years, Penelope, sister Marika Doukas, daughter Sophia Drakoulias and her husband Dean, son Tom Fallis and his wife Peggy and four grandsons, Peter and George Drakoulias and George and Dean Fallis. Donations in his memory can be made to either Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, 20 Field Ave, Hicksville, NY 11801. 516433-4522 or The TORCH Program, 80 8th Ave, NYC 10011. 212-929-9573 www.thetorchprogram.org
