
        KELEN-Cantor Tibor. The Rabbi, Officers, Board of Trustess and members of Temple Beth El of Cedarhurst express their deepest sympathy on the tragic passing of its beloved Hazzan. His genuine piety and humility, combined with his extraordinarily powerful voice, inspired our congregation to pray with greater devotion. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Sholom Stern, Rabbi Richard Holland, President
