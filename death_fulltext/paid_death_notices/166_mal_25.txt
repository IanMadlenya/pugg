
        GROSS-Bernard L. On October 31 in Stamford, CT. Father of William and adoring Pop-Pop to Katherine. Born Lion Gross in Brooklyn, N.Y. in 1915. He always lived up to that name. A business executive who took on his most cherished roleProfessor-in his later years. His knowledge, strength and unwavering high standards influenced many young minds. We'll miss his hearty laugh and his roar. Goodbye, Old Lion.
