
        HENDRIX-Ellis H. Services for Ellis H. Hendrix, 81, of Lexington, S.C. will be held at 2:00 p.m. Saturday at St. John's Lutheran Church with burial in Arlington National Cemetery. Caughman - Harman Funeral Homes, Lexington Chapel is in charge. Memorials may be made to the Church. Mr. Hendrix served in the U.S. Navy during World War II and received the Bronze Star for Meritorious Service. He worked on the White House Staff as Personal Physical Therapist for Presidents Kennedy and Johnson. He worked for Dr. Hans Kraus in New York City, retiring in 1990.
