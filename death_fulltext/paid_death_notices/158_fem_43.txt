
        BERNSTEIN-Berta. Beloved wife of William. Passed away on the 28th of Adar, 5760. Loving mother of Henny and Rabbi Pynchas Brener, Evelyn and Manny Gross, Nathan and Rachel Bernstein and Dr. Celia Bernstein Waldman. Cherished grandmother and great-grandmother. Funeral services were held Sunday March 5, 2000, and interment is in Jerusalem. Shiva services beginning Tuesday, March 7.
        BERNSTEIN-Berta. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the mother of its devoted members Mrs. Evelyn Gross and Dr. Celia Berstein Waldman. May her entire family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
