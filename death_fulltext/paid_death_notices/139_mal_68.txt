
        KIRKPATRICK-Patrick ''Kirk.'' On Aug. 3. A veteran executive of the Cruise and Travel industries. From 1975-1983, Kirk was General Manager of Norwegian America Line's North American operations. In 1985 he became Executive Director of Loews Corporations' Grande Collection of Hotels. In 1987 he was named President, Society Expeditions Cruises, Seattle. He remained an active industry consultant, developing plans for a new concept in cruise sales. Family and friends from around the world greatly mourn his passing.
