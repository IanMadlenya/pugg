
        HEMION-Malcolm Land. 73, of Point Pleasant Beach, NJ, formerly of Verona, NJ, New York City, and Mantoloking, NJ, died at Brick Hospital, March 5. Predeceased by his beloved wife of 39 years, Judith Ann (Hawie) Hemion. Surviving are his son, Eric Hemion, and his brother and sister-in-law, Dwight and Kit Hemion. A memorial service is being planned. For details please visit www.erichemion.com/machemion. For more information contact Van Hise and Callagan Funeral Home, 732-892-0100.
