
        CUTLER--Claire Mintz, attorney at law, died peacefully on August 12, 2005 at her Manhattan home after a long illness. She was 86 years of age. A graduate of Hunter College, Class of 1939 and Brooklyn Law School, Class of 1942. She was the beloved wife and law partner of the late Max Cutler, with whom she worked side by side for nearly half a century. She is survived by her two sons Bill and John. A family graveside service was held August 15.
