
        ROSS--Alexander A., 95, of West Orange, NJ died peacefully on June 9. Beloved husband of the late Matilda (Blumenthal), father of the late Daniel and grandfather of the late Alan, he is survived by his son Michael, daughter - in - law Leslie, daughter Elizabeth and grandchildren Christopher and Jana. Alex graduated from NY University in 1929 and for many years was President of Comet Chemical Co of Newark, retiring in 1982, and a member of the American Chemical Society. His philanthropic activities included participation in the United Jewish Appeal, The Jewish Community Centers of Essex County and Score (Service Core of Retired Executives). A graveside service Sunday, June 12 at 11:00 A.M. at King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that contributions be made to the Alan G. Ross Memorial Poetry Mentoring Project, Administered by The Alliance for Young Artists and Writers, 568 Broadway, NY, NY 10012.
