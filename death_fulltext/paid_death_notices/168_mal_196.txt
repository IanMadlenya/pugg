
        SHIFREN-Herman. Died January 11, 2001. Loving father of Jonathan and James. Devoted husband to Lori Shifren. He was a veteran, having served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. Graduate of Temple University and Columbia University School of Public Health. He worked sucessfully for nearly 40 years in the field of medical and pharmaceutical advertising, retiring from a position as director of advertising copy at Ayerst Laboratories, a division of American Home Products Corporation. His greatest joy was his family and his three grandchildren Andrew, Emma and Daniel. He will never be forgotten.
