
        ELKIN-Rosalind. On March 6, 2000. Died suddenly at home. She was a social worker for many years for the City of New York and later with private social welfare agencies, including most recently Stanton House. She was loved and respected by all who knew her and a joy to those whose lives she touched. She will be deeply missed. Survived by brother, Robert Elkin. Graveside service Sunday, 1:30 PM, at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI. For further information please call ''The Riverside'' 212-362-6600.
