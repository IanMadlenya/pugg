
        CIMBRECK-Doris Bade, 85. of Greenville, SC, widow of John Cimbreck, died Wednesday, May 5, 1999. Born in Brooklyn, NY, daughter of the late John Henry and Henrietta Wehmann Bade, she was a graduate of Katherine Gibbs School in New York City. Before her retirement, she was secretary manager for Lybran Ross Brothers & Montgomery, accountants, of New York City, and at one time was secretary for the NY State Society of Accountants. Surviving one brother, John Bade, and his wife, Dorothy, of 3368 Hollywood Avenue, Medford, OR 97501. Services were held Friday, May 7, in Greenville, SC. For information Mackey Mortuary, Greenville, SC.
