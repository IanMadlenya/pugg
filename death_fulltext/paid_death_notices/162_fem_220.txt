
        MEYEROVITZ-Marguerite. Died aged 90 in Colmar France July 19th. Widow of Jan Meyerovitz, musicologist and composer who deceased on December 1998, she was an organist and a professor of French musical diction at the Juilliard School of Lincoln Center. She was also a French diction coach at the Glyndebourne Festival Opera, the MET, and the Summer Vocal Institute (AIMS) in Graz (Austria)
