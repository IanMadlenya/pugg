
        SCOTT-George H., 94. On March 24, peacefully at home in Florida. Adored and cherished husband of Hazel (Leichter) and the late Thelma (Lippman). Loving father of Jane Lande and Nancy and husband Arthur Stampleman. Stepfather of Beth Sommer and Anthony Leichter. Proud and loving grandfather and great-grandfather. Loved and admired by his brothers Nathan and Dr. Benjamin Scott and his many in-laws, nieces and nephews. Funeral services at Paperman and Sons, Montreal, Quebec, 1-800-515-7101. In lieu of flowers, a donation would be appreciated to Mt. Sinai-CVI in honor of, Dr. Valentin Fuster, Mt. Sinai Medical Center, 1 Gustave Levy Place, New York, New York 10029, or Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407.
        SCOTT-George. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, George H. Scott. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Paul M. Weltz, President Burton J. Esrig, Secretary
