
        LORBER--Dr. Jerome W. Of Lake Worth, FL. Husband of the late Ruth Lorber, peacefully passed away on July 24 at the age of 89. In addition to being a dentist, Jerry was most widely known as the owner and director of CAMP RANGER, which was founded by his father in 1926. Throughout his long life he served in the Army as a dentist in World War II, raised great sums of money for UJA and Israel Bonds, was active at the Israel Center of Hillcrest Manor and was a role model to all who knew him. He was admired by his family and will be forever missed by his children Susan and Matthew, Sandy and Sheldon, his devoted grandchildren Eileen and Lowell, Alan and Sandi, David and Alison, Debbie and Eric, and his great grandchildren Kyle, Jake, Casey, Joshua and brand new Jed, who will be told countless wonderful stories about their special and generous great grandfather. Shiva will be at the home of Susan and Matthew Liebman, 101-06 67th Drive, Apt 5A, Forest Hills, on Monday, 7-10 PM, Tues 11 AM-4 PM and 7-10 PM. Donations may be made to Alzheimer's Association and United Jewish Appeal. >>AD#
