
        EISENSTAT - Bonnie. The Trustees, Dean, Faculty, students, and staff of New York Law School express our sorrow for the great loss to our community of Bonnie Eisenstat, beloved and esteemed first-year student. We offer our deepest condolences and sympathies to her husband, Mark, and daughters Stephanie and Nicole, and to all her family and many friends. Arthur N. Abbey, Chairman, Board of Trustees Richard A. Matasar, Dean and President
