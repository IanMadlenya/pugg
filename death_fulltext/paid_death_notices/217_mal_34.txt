
        LIPPIN--Warren G. Age 82. He left us to join his beloved wife Elaine on Sunday, January 30, 2005, while living in Coconut Creek, FLorida. Mr. Lippin was born in Brooklyn, NY and resided in Tarrytown, NY, Hemlock Farms, Pennsylvania, and Coconut Creek. Loving father to Doreen, Janet, and Howard, he was predeceased by his wife, his brothers Robert and Leon, and his sister Blanche. Mr. Lippin served in the US Army during WW II and fought in the Battle of the Bulge with the famed 35th Infantry Division. Mr. Lippin was recognized as a marksman, and was awarded the Good Conduct Medal, the Combat Infantryman's Badge, and the Bronze Star. A gifted businessman, Warren will be remembered for his contributions to the fertilize industry, and his relationships with his friends and associates. He will be deeply missed by all who ever knew him. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, February 4, 2005 at 12 noon at the Riverside Memorial Chapel located at 21 W. Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY, 914-664-6800. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the National Foundation for Facial Reconstruction, 317 East 34th Street, New York, New York 10016, phone (212) 263-6656.
