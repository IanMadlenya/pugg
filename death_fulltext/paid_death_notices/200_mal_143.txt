
        PLOTKIN--Samuel, on September 11, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Betty. Loving father of Marc and the late Diane. Cherished grandfather and great-grandfather. For service information, contact ''The Riverside'' 212-362-6600.
        PLOTKIN--Hon. Samuel. The Board of Trustees, Administration, Medical Staff and employees of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center express great sorrow on the passing of Samuel Plotkin, devoted Vice Chairman Emeritus of the Board of Trustees and Chairman of the Associate Board for 23 years. His extraordinary contribution as a major benefactor for four decades of hands -on dedicated service to this institution will always be remembered by his colleagues and those whose lives were touched by his compassion and caring. To his son Mark, and grandchildren, we offer our profound and sincere condolences. May the many memories we all share of this dear friend help comfort them in their grief. Alvin I. Kahn, MD, FACP, Chairman, Board of Trustees David I. Schachne, Chairman, Executive Committee David P. Rosen, President and CEO Richard J. Fogler, MD Sr. Vice President and CMO
