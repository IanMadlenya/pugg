
        GOODMAN-Cynthia (Harkaby) born January 10, 1913 in New York City. Beloved wife of the late Arthur Goodman. Resided in Greenwich Village. Founder of The Gotham Chapter of B'Nai-Brith, an active member for thirty years. She leaves behind her nephews Warren and Stuart Vogel, her great nieces Melissa, Amy, Kimberly and great nephew Jonathan. Services on Thursday 10AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
