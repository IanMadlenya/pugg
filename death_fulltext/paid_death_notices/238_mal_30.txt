
        McGEADY--Paul J. The Board of Directors and staff of Morality in Media, Inc., mourn the passing of our General Counsel and the Director of our National Obscenity Law Center. Paul led MIM's legal efforts to enact, defend and enforce obscenity and indecency laws for 27 years. His strength and expertise in this difficult endeavor will be greatly missed by all who labor in this important cause. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. There will be a Funeral Mass on Saturday, November 4 at 9:30AM, at the Holy Family Church, 28 Brookline Avenue, Nutley, NJ 07110. At the family's request, in lieu of flowers, Memorial Donations may be made to: Morality in Media, Inc., 475 Riverside Dr., New York, NY 10015
