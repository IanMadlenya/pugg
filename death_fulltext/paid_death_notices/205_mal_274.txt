
        ECKER--Joseph, M.D. Beloved and devoted husband of Eva. Devoted brother-inlaw of Gerson and Judith Leiber. Dr. Ecker was a kind, compassionate physician. A man of great intellect and courage. He always had a warm and friendly smile and enriched the lives of all of us who were fortunate to know him. He will be missed by his family and friends.
