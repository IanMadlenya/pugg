
        LIPMAN--Jesse B. Died June 14, 2005 at the age of 61 after living with chronic lymphocytic leukemia for 13 years. Beloved husband of Carolee, father of Dr. Hannah Lipman, Allison and Tony Bastos, Jennifer and Glen Katz. Brother of Barry Lipman, adoring grandfather of Rebecca, Jake, Michael and Blake. Always generous with his love, Jesse is loved by many. In lieu of flowers donations to be made care of Dr. Kanti Rai, Leukemia Research Foundation, Long Island Jewish Medical Center, New Hyde Park, NY.
