
        HO-Betty Jane, died October 24, 1997, at Mary Manning Walsh, Manhattan, age 73, following a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Norman Ho, she is survived by her daughter Dr. Ann Ho of Manhattan, her granddaughter Cheryl Andrews of Tarrytown; her brother Russell F. Anderson of Manhattan; her nephew Jon R. Anderson of Gettysburg, PA., and niece Bonnie R. Anderson of Delavan, WI.
