
        EASTWOOD-W. Norman. Died peacefully at age 94. He was born in Ashton-under-Lyme, England and came to Sand Key, Clearwater in 1974. He retired as Vice President of Merritt-Chapman & Scott of New York after a long construction management career. He was Assistant construction Superintendent of the Empire State Building and supervised construction projects in Iran, Turkey, the north of Canada, Niagara Falls and elsewhere around the world. He was an Associate member of Church of the Isles, Indian Rocks Beach and a member of Marble Collegiate Church, New York City, He was a member of Egypt Temple Shrine, Tampa and past President of Holiday Isles Shrine Club, and past Master Tabernacle Lodge and past President of the Men's League of Marble Collegiate Church, both of New York and past President of Sand Key Club Condominium Association. He is survived by his wife Alma of 46 years; a son, Norman of Palos Verde, Ca., a daughter, Diane Plotkin of Huntington Beach, Ca. several grandchildren, great-grandchildren, nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held at 10 am, Saturday, September 23, 2000 at Church of the Isles, 2408 Bay Blvd., Indian Rocks Beach. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Shriners Hospitals for Children, 12502 North Pine Drive, Tampa, FL. 33612-9499.
