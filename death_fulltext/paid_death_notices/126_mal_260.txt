
        INSELBUCH-Samson, 93, on July 23, 1997, the beloved husband of Frada, his ''Sunny'', father of Elihu and Harriet, and grandfather of Adam, Frank and Lenora, and Jessica and Jan. He was in every way a man of honor and a gentleman. Services on Friday, 12 noon, at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
