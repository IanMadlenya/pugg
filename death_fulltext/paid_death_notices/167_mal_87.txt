
        BREITBART-Dr. George Jay, age 81 of Manhattan. Died on December 5, 2000 at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital. Dr. Breitbart received his undergraduate degree from NYU, his medical degree from Middlesex University in Massachusetts, and became a member of the Medical Society of the County of NY. He was a poet, inventor, writer and sculptor-truly a renaissance man. He is survived by his sons John Breitbart and Douglas Breitbart, and sisters Pauline Ross and Frances Friedman. The family is receiving on Sunday, December 10th, from 12PM-6PM at the home of Douglas and Cheryl Breitbart, Ridgewood, NJ. Memorial services are being planned for January 2001. He will be dearly missed by his family, friends and patients.
