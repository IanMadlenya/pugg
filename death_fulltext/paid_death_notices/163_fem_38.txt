
        ZINSSER-Barbara L. (Mrs. Hans). The Trustees of Robert College of Istanbul honor the life of their friend and colleague, Barbara Zinsser, and express their sadness at her death. As an active member of the Board for 45 years her insightful mind and her willingness to approach all problems in a constructive way provided invaluable guidance to the College and the Board. She will be greatly missed. The Trustees extend their deepest condolences to her daughter, Leslie Armstrong, and her family.
