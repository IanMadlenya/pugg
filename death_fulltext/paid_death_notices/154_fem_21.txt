
        POMERANTZ-Gerta. On November 2, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Freddy Pomerantz. Dear mother of Leslie Fay Pomerantz and John and Laura Pomerantz. Adored grandmother of Andrew and Lynn Gross, Frederick (Ricky) Coff, Andrea and Matthew Lustig, Susan and Kevin Davis, and Marnie. Great-grandmother of Kristen, Matthew, Nicholas, Michael, Anna and Zachary. You were the matriarch of our family, and now that you are gone, a part of who we are and from where we have come, leaves us, too. You are in our hearts. Rest in peace. Services Thursday, 9:30 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        POMERANTZ-Gerta. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Gerta Pomerantz. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        PREISER-Alex. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Alex Preiser, associated with The Times from December 31, 1954, until retiring in January 6, 1997.
