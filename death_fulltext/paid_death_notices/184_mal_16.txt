
        LOOKER-Charles. The Officers, Board of Directors and Staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind, are deeply saddened by the death of Charles Looker, a former member of the Board of Directors and longtime supporter and friend of The Guild. His valued counsel as a Director and Trustee will be missed by all who knew him. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the Looker family. Joseph S. Mishkin, Chairman Pauline Raiff, Chairman-Elect Alan R. Morse, President & CEO
