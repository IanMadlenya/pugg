
        RAYNOR--Arnold, 75 years old. Formerly of New York, N.Y., Hemlock Farms, PA, and Great Neck, N.Y., died January 27, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Sandra, father of Daniel (Susan) and Robert (Kathleen), brother of Myron, grandfather of Devon, Zachary, Samantha and Lauren. Graduated from City College of New York in 1950 and Yale Law School in 1953 and practiced real estate law for over 40 years. Arnold will be remembered for his kindness, generosity, impeccable integrity, keen intellect, and uncanny sense of humor. Funeral services will be held Friday, January 30, 2004, at 11:30AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (91st St) , New York and interment will be at Sharon Gardens, 273 Lakeview Avenue, Valhalla, N.Y. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Jewish Federation of Greater Philadelphia, 2100 Arch Street, Philadelphia, PA 19103
