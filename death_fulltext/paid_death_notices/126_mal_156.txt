
        JACOBSEN-John Warburton, entered into rest July 12 in Shelton, CT. Born on March 12, 1912, in Montreal, he was a graduate of the Royal Military College and McGill University in Canada. In his life, he was a world traveler, engineer and successful businessman. John was dearly loved by his family and friends and will be sorely missed. He is survived by a sister Marion O'Dea of Reston, VA and his daughter Carol Jacobsen Poland of Shelton, CT. Friends are invited to come to the home of his daughter from 4 to 6 P.M. this evening. John will be buried at Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, New York at 2 PM on Friday. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Humane Society.
