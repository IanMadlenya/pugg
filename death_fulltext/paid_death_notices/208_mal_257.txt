
        KOTZ--Richard Paul, 63, passed away peacefully after a long and courageous battle with lymphoma on May 15th, 2004. Formerly of Wilton, CT, Basking Ridge, NJ and Manhattan, he relocated to Bonita Springs, FL in 1999. He was a Wealth Management Advisor at Merrill Lynch after a long career as a senior executive at corporations such as American Express and Chase Manhattan Bank. He is survived by his wife of nine years, Joan Strand Kotz; father, Paul Kotz, of Lindenhurst, IL; son, David (Lisa), of Far Hills, NJ; daughter, Julie Richie (Chris), of Dallas; brother, Phillip Kotz, and sister, Carol Hays; and six grandchildren. He was predeceased by his wife, Susan Jacobs Kotz. A family memorial will take place in August in Gloucester, MA. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 3350 Woods Edge Circle, Suite 102, Bonita Springs, FL 34134.
