
        COLLINS-Dr. John J. Died at his home in Blauvelt, NY, on June 16, 2001, at the age of 92. Born in NYC in 1909, Dr. Collins served as a professor of history at Fordham University for 40 years and was also a consultant to numerous independent tanker unions for more than 50 years. From 1950 until the early 1970's, Dr. Collins owned and operated Birch Brook Day Camp in Palisades, NY. He is survived by his daughters Suzanne Collins Broidrick, Jeanne Collins Donovan and Anne Collins, and his grandchildren Emily Broidrick, Michael, Maura and John Donovan, and Jesse and Jenny Medvene-Collins. His wife, Agnes Gerrity Collins, died in 1963, and his daughter Claire in 1994. He was also predeceased by his brother William. Friends may call at the Moritz Funeral Home, 98 Route 303, Tappan, NY, on Tuesday, June 19, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday, June 20, at 10 AM, at St. Catharine's Church, 523 Western Highway, Blauvelt, NY. Burial to follow at Maryrest Cemetery, Darlington (Mahwah), NJ. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Fordham University, 441 E. Fordham Rd., Bronx, NY 10451, or a charity of one's choice.
