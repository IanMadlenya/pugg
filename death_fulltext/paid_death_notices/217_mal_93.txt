
        MAURER--Jack. Of Manhattan and Quiogue, died peacefully at home on Sunday. Husband of Rona, cherished father of Lisa, Justine and Nicole. Loving brother of Ed, Rose and Shirley. Beloved grandfather of Allegra and Lucas. Cared for devotedly by Mona Tay. He was President and Publisher of Indicator Research Group, acted as financial advisor and manager to many prominent figures in fashion and the arts, and was a generous philanthropist and benefactor through the Maurer Family Foundation. Jack was a distinguished member of the Friars and University Clubs. He was a U.S. army captain who served with valor in the Aleutians during World War II. Jack had a lifelong passion for tennis and backgammon, and cherished greatly his many friendships. Services Tuesday, February 8 at 11:30 A.M., at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Friars Foundation, 57 East 55th Street, NY, NY 10022.
