
        GASSEL--Virginia S. Age 70, of Chicago, IL, beloved wife of the late Stuart P. Gassel, loving mother of Virginia S. Gassel, Jr. and James A. Gassel, and grandmother of Grace M. Gassel, passed away on April 2. Virginia was a philanthropist and humanitarian. She was passionate about her family, friends and the arts. She contributed her time to various organizations such as the Handel and Haydn Society, The Chicago Symphony as well as several Museums. Virginia's Memorial Service will be held on Monday, April 24 at 11:00AM at the Fourth Presbyterian Church on Michigan Avenue in Chicago. In lieu of flowers, please forward donations to either of the following organizations: Chicago Humanities Festival - Virginia Gassel Memorial Fund 500 North Dearborn #
