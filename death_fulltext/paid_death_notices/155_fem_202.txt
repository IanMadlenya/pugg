
        DOHRENWEND-Constance Wilckes. Died December 18, 1999 at the age of 93 at the Osborn Retirement Community, Rye, NY. Devoted wife for 57 years of the late Otto E. Dohrenwend. Caring mother of Renata D. Gallagher of New York City, James W. (Sandra) of Denville, NJ, Robert C. (Patricia) of Bronxville, NY, Paul B. (Robyn) of Newtown, CT and the late Claire Christine Dohrenwend. Loving sister of Irma W. Parady of Bloomfield, CT., and Renata W. Harrison of Stevenson, MD., and the late Ruth W. McCarthy of Toronto. She is also survived by 15 grandchildren, 18 great grandchildren and eight nieces and nephews. A long time resident of Scarsdale, Connie was an active member of the PTA, League of Women Voters, The Scarsdale Creche Committee, Youth Counseling League (as a Board member), The Scarsdale Women's Republican Club (past President) and the Women of St. James (past President). Services at St. James the Less Episcopal Church, Scarsdale, Wednesday December 22, 3PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Miriam Osborn Memorial Home Assoc, 101 Theall Road, Rye, NY 10580 or a charity of your choice.
