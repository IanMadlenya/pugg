
        ESSEX-Marie L. Age 64, of Trumbull, CT, died on Friday, July 27, 2001. Mrs. Essex, a well recognized fashion illustrator and fashion educator, was a member of the Parsons Fashion Design Department over 40 years as a fulltime faculty, Associate Chair for 10 years, and its Chair from 1996-2000. Survivors include her beloved husband, Jack T. Essex, a son, Harry Essex of NYC, a sister, Vivian Ascenzi and her husband Orlando of Bedford Hills, NY, several nieces and nephews. Donations to Mercy Corps International or Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center. Viewing at the Abriola Parkview Funeral Home, 419 White Plains Rd., Trumbull, CT, Monday, 4-8 PM. Funeral Mass Christ the King Church, Trumbull, CT, 10 AM, Tuesday. Interment St. Francis Cemetery, Mt. Kisco, NY, 1 PM, Tuesday.
