
        GITHENS-William French. A pioneer in producing and popularizing newsreels, died of complications following a stroke. He is survived by his stepson John Lawrence Githens, his stepdaughter Marianne Githens and his grandson Jonathan Githens-Mazer. Memorial service will be held at Vinson Hall, Virginia, and burial at Saint Mary's Cemetery in New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Nothern Virginia Hospice, Arlington Virginia.
