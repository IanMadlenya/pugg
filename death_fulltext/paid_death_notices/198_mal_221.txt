
        WALL--F. William (Bill), died on July 22nd, 2003 of complications resulting from cancer surgery. Mr. Wall was born on December 30th, 1919 and is survived by his beloved wife of 57 years, Mary, and three devoted children, Linda (John) Schieffelin of Jamestown, RI, William (Sarah) of Maui, HI, and Helene (Paul) Fagan of Southampton, NY, seven grandchildren, and his brother, Michael Wall of Malvern, PA. A graduate of St. George's School, he attended Yale '43 & served as a USAF bombardier in WWII. He was a member of the Yale Club of NYC, Beaver Dam Winter Sports Club and the Southampton Bathing Corp. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, August 2nd, at St. Andrew's Dune Church, Southampton, at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. John's Episcopal Church, PO Box 5068, Southampton, NY 11969 would be appreciated. >>AD#
