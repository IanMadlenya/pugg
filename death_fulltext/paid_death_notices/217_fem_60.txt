
        DELANO--Elizabeth Washburn. 89, resident of York, ME, on February 2, 2005. She was the widow of Warren Delano. Born in New York City, she graduated from The Brearley School and Bryn Mawr College, Class of 1937. She is survived by her children, Sara Delano of Winchester, MA, Warren Delano of New Canaan, CT, and Lyman Delano of Greenwich, CT; and eight grandchildren. Services will be private, in accordance with her wishes. Gifts may be made in her memory to the York Hospital, 15 Hospital Drive, York, ME 03909; The Music Hall, 28 Chestnut St, Portsmouth, NH 03801; or the charity of one's choice.
