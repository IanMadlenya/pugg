
        FLANAGAN -- Dennis. The Board of Trustees of the New York Hall of Science notes with sorrow the passing of our founding Trustee and long-time friend, Dennis Flanagan. His lifelong commitment to the public understanding of science inspired and represented our mission and his grand wit animated our work together. His memory burns bright and his work goes on here at the New York Hall of Science. Our affection and condolences to his wife, Barbara, and his family. Ivan G. Seidenberg Chairman Seth H. Dubin, Esq. President Emeritus Alan J. Friedman, PhD Director & CEO
