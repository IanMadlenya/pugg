
        BAXTER--Katherine D. On January 19, 2005 in Peterborough, NH of natural causes at age 86. She is survived by her husband of 65 years, Richard S., her son David A. Baxter of Crested Butte, CO, her three daughters, Mildred B. Perkins of Topsfield, Massachusetts, Katherine R. Torrance of Keene, NY, Hilary Jenkins of Kent's Store, VA; ten grandchildren and 8 great-grandchildren. Her parents were the late Louis and Antoinette Irving of Southampton, New York. Burial will be private. Instead of flowers, donations may be made to the Eastern Long Island Audubon Society, Box 206, East Quogue, New York 11942.
