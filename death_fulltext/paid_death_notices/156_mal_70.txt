
        RACHLIN-Carl. The N.Y. Civil Liberties Union mourns the passing of Carl Rachlin, former Board Member and Director Emeritus. Carl was an effective and strong advocate for civil liberties and for principles of equality, especially racial equality. We will miss him. Our condolences to his wife Helen, their daughters Margaret and Ruth and his entire family. Steven J. Hyman, President Norman Siegel, Exec. Director
