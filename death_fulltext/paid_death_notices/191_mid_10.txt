
        SCHULMAN-Norman. In his 87th year in Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, on December 20th. Beloved husband of Florence. Devoted father of Nancy Bellovin and her husband Dennis, and Leslie Fishoff and her husband Scott. Cherished grandfather of Hal, Emily, Alex and Zach. A generous, vivacious and compassionate man, dedicated to family and friends. He will be deeply missed and remain forever in the hearts of those who loved him.
