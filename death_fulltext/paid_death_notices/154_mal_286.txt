
        SCHWARTZ-Harry, 99, of Hollywood, Florida, formerly of NYC. Passed away November 22, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Lillian. Loving father of Arnold and Eileen Schwartz and Ruth and David Schwab. Loving and much loved Grandpa Harry to his five grandchildren, their spouses, and his twelve great-grandchildren. Harry was raised at the Brooklyn Hebrew Orphan Asylum. He became the youngest CPA in NY and founded his own firm, and later became an Advisory Partner at the firm which is now Deloitte & Touche. Levitt Weinstein Funeral Home, (800-343-5400).
