
        LAUB-Ernest. Died December 17, 1997. Beloved husband of Roslyn. Devoted father of Alane and Linda. Father-in-law of Neal Gartenberg. Grandfather of Adam and Scott. Your love, kindness, gentle wit and wisdom got us all through the ''trials and tribulations'' of life. You will be sorely missed and never forgotten. Services held at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, New York. Call 516-921-5757. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society in his name.
        LAUB-Ernest. The Board of Directors and membership of Glen Head Country Club announce with profound sorrow the untimely passing of our esteemed member, Ernest Laub. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Martin Scheman, President
