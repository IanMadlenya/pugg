
        VAN BRUNT--Mary Harrsen on July 30, 2004, in her 90th year. Formerly of Essex Fells, NJ, she was a resident of Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, NJ. She was the widow of Arthur H. Van Brunt, Jr. and leaves her children Carol V. Rasic and Arthur H. Van Brunt, III and grandchildren Timothy Rasic, Carolyn Rasic and Kristina Van Brunt. Services will be private.
