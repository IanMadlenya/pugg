
        POTTER--Martin William, on February 2, 2005, of Harrington Park, NJ. Retired NYPD Det. 1st Grade. Former Asst. DA of Queens, NY. Retired Attorney for the Legal Aide Society in Queens County. First Union President of the Legal Aide Society of NYC. Member of the NY State Bar Association and NYPD Detective Endowment Association. Graduate of St. John's Law School. Admitted to the Bar before the US Supreme Court, NY Appellate Court and the Eastern and Southern Districts of NY. Beloved husband of Regina (nee Fuhrmann). Devoted father of Joel E., Michael M., Martin Jr., Patricia and Christine. Dear brother of Ellen Camerieri. Dear grandfather of 13 grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife Alice (nee Murphy) and his son Christopher John. Funeral Mass Saturday 10AM at Our Lady of Victories RC Church, Harrington Park, NJ. Burial to follow at Rockland Cemetery, Sparkill, NY. Visiting hours Thursday 7-9PM and Friday 2-4 & 7-9PM at the Moritz Funeral Home, 348 Closter Dock Rd, Closter, NJ. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to the Harrington Park Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Kline St, Harrington Park, NJ 07640 or the Bergen Community Healthcare Hospice, 400 Old Hook Road, Westwood, NJ 07675.
