
        JANOWITZ-Ira. Of Potomac, MD, age 44. We are deeply saddened by the tragic, sudden death of our dearest friend, Ira, beloved husband of Donna and father to Adam and Lindsay. A boy of the Bronx with a special four years at the University of Rochester, he will always be remembered as the warmest of friends and the warmest of people. Our heartfelt condolences to his family whose loss we share deeply. Ira's warmth, humor and friendship will always be rememberd by those whose lives he touched. Services were held January 9, 2000. Bradley A. Radwaner, M.D. Mary Radwaner, Jeff Koval Joel Rosenberg, M.D. Alan Hartman, M.D. Paul Anisman, M.D.
