
        EARLE--Henry Dr., Optometrist formerly of Maplewood, N.J. died November 6, 2006. A visionary and inventor, he was a pioneer in developing plastic lenses. He was described as ''a unique blend of optical expert, technician, psychologist and astute businessman'' (optical Management March 1984). Survived by Bernice, his devoted wife of 50 years, he was a loving father to Judy Lind (Dennis), beloved grandfather to Mimi and Martin Lind (Deidre) and adoring great-grandfather to Rachel and Garrett Lind. Services were held in Los Angeles.
