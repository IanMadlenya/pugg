
        BERLOW--Richard J. Adored husband of Joyce, cherished father to Jody and Nancy, doting grandfather and greatgrandfather, loving and admired uncle to an extended family of nieces and nephews, died at age 85, on October 2, 2004, after an extended hospitalization. His generosity of spirit and kindness of heart characterized all of his business and personal relationships. A decorated World War II combat pilot, he was a pioneer in the aviation insurance field. A superb athlete and expert teacher, he successfully imparted his knowledge, values and interests to three generations. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him. Services at Gutterman-Muscicant, 402 Park St, Hackensack, NJ, October 4, at 11:30 am.
