
        TRUSSELL-Ray E. Greater New York Hospital Association mourns the passing of Ray E. Trussell, M.D. In addition to serving as former Commissioner of Hospitals in New York City and former General Director of Beth Israel Medical Center in Manhattan, Dr. Trussell was also a dedicated Board Member and leader of Greater New York Hospital Association. He will be remembered for his outstanding contributions to the field of health care in NY. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Elizabeth D. Trussell, M.D., his entire family and many friends. Mark J. Mundy, Chairman Kenneth A. Raske, President Greater N.Y. Hospital Association
