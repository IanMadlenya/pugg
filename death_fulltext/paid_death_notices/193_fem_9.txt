
        DIAMOND - Irene. The Trustees, faculty and staff of New School University mourn with deep sadness the death of Irene Diamond, a dedicated and strong supporter of our educational mission. She was a visionary philanthropist and a staunch leader in creating opportunities for society's underrepresented groups. Irene Diamond's ongoing commitment to expand educational opportunities to diverse and academically gifted individuals secured and enriched the future of a generation of our students. We have lost an esteemed friend. Philip Scaturro, Chairman Board of Trustees Bob Kerrey, President New School University
