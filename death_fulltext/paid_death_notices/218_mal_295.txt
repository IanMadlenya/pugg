
        DeVITO--Joseph D., 80, of Spring Hill, FL passed away March 23, 2005. He was born in Brooklyn, NY. He served in the Navy during WW II and the Korean War. He worked for 30 years and was co-owner of Danross Agency, NY, NY. He is survived by his wife Mary; three sons Robert, Joseph, Thomas residing in CT and NY; brother Tom and sister Rose; five grandchildren. Friends may call at the Clair S. Bartholomew Funeral Home, Bellmore, NY, Monday, March 28, 2-4 & 7-9:30.
