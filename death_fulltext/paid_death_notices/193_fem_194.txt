
        TINGUE-Marjorie Cooper. Of Essex, CT, died February 22, 2003, in Middletown, CT. Wife of the late William M. Tingue and mother of the late Marjorie C. Tingue. Survived by her son William J. Tingue, three grandchildren, Margret, David, and Christopher, and three great - grandchildren. Memorial service at the First Congregational Church, Essex, CT, on Tuesday, February 25, 2003, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Middlesex Hospital or the First Congregational Church.
