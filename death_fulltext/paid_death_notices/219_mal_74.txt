
        KANE -- Howard L. The trustees, directors, staff, participants and beneficiaries of the 1199 SEIU Benefit and Pension Funds mourn the loss of our valued actuary and advisor, Howard L. Kane. Howard served the Funds for more than 30 years with indispensable expertise that helped us improve the quality of life for our members. His contributions have ensured our ability to provide health care and pension benefits today and in the future. We offer our deepest sympathy to Sandra, Wendy and the Kane family.
