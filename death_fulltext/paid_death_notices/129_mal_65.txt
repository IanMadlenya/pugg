
        MURPHY-Patrick Joseph, formerly of Valley Stream, NY, died Friday, October 3, 1997. He was a graduate of CCNY, a Golden Gloves boxer, and served during W.W. II as a Marine Corps fighter pilot. He was the first Commander of the Catholic War Veterans in LIC. During Mr. Murphy's career he was a NYC Police detective, FBI Special Agent, Deputy Commissioner of Investigations for NYC, and the President of Proudfoot Reports. He is survived by his wife Mary Elizabeth Murphy. Memorial donations in Mr. Murphy's name may be made to Precious Blood of Christ Church Building Fund, 455 Waverly Shell Rd., Pawleys Island, S.C. 29585.
