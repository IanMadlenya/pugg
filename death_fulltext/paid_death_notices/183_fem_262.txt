
        SALZBERG - Harriet. Beloved daughter of the late Dr. Max and Regina Salzberg. Loving sister of Marlene Feldman; sister-in-law of Milton Feldman, and cherished aunt of Mark and Amy, Michael and A.J., and Matthew and Mara. Passed away in her 57th year on April 25, 2002. She will be missed by her sisters and brothers at the Sanford Avenue House in Flushing, Queens. No longer will she hear the voices. Finally, peace will be hers eternally. Graveside burial was at Mount Zion Cemetery, Maspeth, Queens, on Friday, April 26. Shiva will be observed at the Feldman home in Pine Brook, NJ.
