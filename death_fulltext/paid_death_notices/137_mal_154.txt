
        LEVINE-Louis. Died on June 13, 1998 at age 95 in his home in Palm Beach, FL. Loving husband of the late Mae Bruckman Levine for 61 years. Cherished by his daughter, Susan Raanan, and beloved by his grandchildren Matthew and Victoria and his son-in-law Robert Fleischer. A gentle and sensitive man of considerable artistic talent. We will you very much.
