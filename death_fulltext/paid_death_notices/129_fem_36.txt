
        TANNER-Pauline Steinlauf, after a long illness, cherished wife of the late Irving; loved by children Ben and Ellen Tanner Farkas, and Nicki and Harold Tanner; and grandchildren Esta and Paul Farkas, Gary and Lynn Farkas Schwartz, Peggy and David Tanner; Catherine Allegra and James Tanner, Kent and Karen Tanner Allen; and proud great-grandmother of 11. Her intelligence, love of, and loyalty to, family strengthened and enhanced our lives. Expressions of remembrance may be made to the Hadassah Hospital in Jerusalem or to the Pauline and Irving Tanner Scholarship Fund at Cornell University. Funeral services will be on Sunday, October 5, at 1:00 P.M., at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, New York.
        TANNER-Pauline Steinlauf. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Pauline Steinlauf Tanner, beloved mother of Harold Tanner, an extraordinary leader and philanthropist in our community and throughout the United States, and mother-in-law of Nicki Tanner, chair of our Capital Development Fund. Both are cherished friends of UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec.
