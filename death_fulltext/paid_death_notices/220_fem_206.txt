
        RUBIN--Jessie. The faculty and staff of the Charles C. Harris Skin and Cancer Pavilion, Ronald O. Perelman Department of Dermatology, NYU School of Medicine record with great sorrow the death of Jessie Rubin, MD, Associate Professor of Dermatology on May 23, 2005. A graduate of the Dermatology Residency Program at NYU and a member of our volunteer staff, she served our department for over 55 years. To her children, all her family and friends, we extend our sympathy. Nicholas A. Soter, MD Interim Chairman Irwin M. Freedberg, MD George Miller MacKee, Prof. Dept. of Dermatology NYU School of Medicine
