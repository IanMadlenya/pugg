
        FREEMAN-Frank. Of New York City and Point O' Woods, Fire Island, age 77, on May 24, at home. Beloved husband of Beverly, loving father of Howard and James, and daughters-in-law Karen and Rachel. Also survived by his stepmother Mary Freeman, brothers George and Richard and sister Mary Catherine Rockey. He was a retired advertising vice-president. A memorial service will be held at The Church At Point O' Woods on Saturday, June 20 at 3:30 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Salvation Army would be appreciated.
