
        JULIBER-I. Gerard. 85. Died peacefully after a hard-fought battle. Beloved and devoted husband of Roslyn. Loving and supportive father of Ilene and Stan (Barshay), Lois and Jon (Adams). Grandfather of Larry and Lisa, Scott and Melissa, and Missy, great-grandfather of Jennifer, Emily and Seth. Retired Senior Vice President of Revlon. He taught us the meaning of family, loyalty, courage and love. We will miss him dearly, and cherish his memory. Funeral Monday at Gutterman's, Woodbury, New York.
