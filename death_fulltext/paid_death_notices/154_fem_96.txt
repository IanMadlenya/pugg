
        FRIEDMAN-Toby Nevins. Of Boynton Beach, FL; formerly of Cedarhurst, NY, and White Meadow Lake, NJ; passed away November 11, 1999. Woman of valor. Beloved wife of Harold K. for 53 years. Loving mother of Lydia (Jon) Jacobs and Nancy Friedman (Steve Posner). Proud grandmother of Scott, Jolie, Jay, Melanie, Jared, and Daniel. She will always be in our thoughts and hearts.
