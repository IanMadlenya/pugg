
        ANYAEGBUNAM - Akolisa M., M.D. The entire Albert Einstein College of Medicine community mourns the untimely passing of our distinguished Professor of Obstetrics, Gynecology and Women's Health and Clinical Chairman of Obstetrics, Gynecology and Women's Health at Jacobi Medical Center. A dedicated and caring physician, an outstanding teacher, a prolific author in the scientific literature, and an inspiring leader in the medical community, he exemplified all that is most highly revered in our profession. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D., Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
        ANYAEGBUNAM-Dr. Akolisa. It is with the greatest sense of sorrow and loss, both for the patients we serve and ourselves, that we, on behalf of the physicians and staff of Jacobi Hospital and North Central Bronx Hosptial, note the passing of Dr. Akolisa Anyaegbunam.
