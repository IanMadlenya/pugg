
        SODDEN--Rabbi Jacob. The Van Cortlandt Jewish Center mourns the passing of their esteemed Rabbi Jacob Sodden. He was our Rabbi and spiritual leader for 60 years. He will be remembered and missed by the entire Van Cortlandt family. He was a kind, compassionate man who cared very much about his congregation. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Rebbitzen Jean Sodden, sons Rabbi Mayer, Aaron and daughter Helene, also three grandchildren, three great - grandchildren, four brothers and six sisters. Van Cortlandt Jewish Center
