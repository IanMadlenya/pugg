
        O'BRIEN-Robert, 60, of Little Egg Harbor, New Jersey, died Friday at Atlantic City Medical Center Mainland, Poamona. Mr. O'Brien worked for the New York Times for over twenty-nine years. He was an Advertising Makeup Assistant Manager. Mr. O'Brien was a veteran of the Marine Corps. He was born in Jersey City and lived most of his life in Monmouth County. Survived by his wife Barbara, son Robert Michael, and step-children Rebecca and David Byrnes. His remains will be donated to Robert Wood Johnson Medical School, Piscataway, N.J.
        O'BRIEN-Robert. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Robert O'Brien, associated with The Times since 1967.
