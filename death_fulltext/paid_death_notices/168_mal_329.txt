
        ROCKWELL-Charles Embree. 84, lifelong resident of Smithtown, on January 19, 2001. A pioneer in aviation, he was a board member of the Bank of Smithtown, the L.I. Museums, and the Smithtown Historical Society. Beloved father of Robin Markle Rockwell, Joan Lawrence Rockwell, Charles Embree Rockwell Jr., and Steven Arthur Rockwell. Also survived by nine grandchildren. Burial is private. A memorial service will be held Monday, 2 PM, at the First Presbyterian Church of Smithtown. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Smithtown Historical Society, PO Box 69, Smithtown, NY 11787 would be appreciated.
