
        ZONERAICH-Dr. Samuel. Born November 4, 1921, passed away on May 13, 2000. Cardiologist at Long Island Jewish Hospital, Flushing Hospital and The New York Hospital in Queens. He dedicated his life to medicine and the field of Cardiology. He is survived by his brother Bruno and his family. The funeral will take place Tuesday, May 16, at 1:30 PM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY.
