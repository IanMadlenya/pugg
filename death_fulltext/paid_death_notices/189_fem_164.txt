
        KETT-Eileen. On October 21. Native of Snugboro, Castlebar, County Mayo, Ireland. Beloved wife of the late Martin. Loving mother of Frances McKenna and Eileen Caulfield. Loving mother-inlaw of Richard V. McKenna and the late William Caulfield. Cherished grandmother of Richard McKenna, Susan McKenna, Kett McKenna, Thomas McKenna, Caroline Caulfield, Frances Caulfield Porraro and Christopher Caulfield. Loving grandmother-inlaw of Siobhan, Sheila, Kathy, Matthew and Dana, and great-grandmother of Richard, Caitlin, Kett, Troy, Meaghan, Caitlin, Sara and Mia. Devoted sister of the late John McGough, the late Miles McGough and the late Annie Kilcoyne. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Wednesday, October 23, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, October 24, at 10 A.M., at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84th Street.
