
        DWECK--Morris. On May 20, 2005. Beloved husband of Nina. Loving father of Robbie and his wife Karen Kisslinger and Laura and her husband John Aden. Adoring grandfather of Emma and Ava Dweck and Paula and Charles Aden. Dear brother of Henry. After more than 40 years with the State of Israel Bonds, he retired as the Executive Director of Public Relations. Throughout his life, he was deeply committed to the struggle for social justice and passed his passion onto his children & grandchildren. A memorial service is planned at a future date.
