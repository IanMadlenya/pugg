
        PERLMAN--Irving B., 87. Passed away November 16, 2005 at his home in Floral Park, N.Y. Beloved husband of Ruth, father and father-inlaw of Marjorie and Stephen Shoenholz, grandfather of Jeffrey and Ellyn Guttman and Amy Guttman. Former owner of M. Perlman, Inc. Office Supplies of New York City. Services 10AM, Friday, November 18 at ''RiversideNassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York. Interment at Union Fields Cemetery, Queens, N.Y.
