
        TROY-Joseph F. Rev. CSP. On August 8, 2001 at age 93. Born in Dobbs Ferry, NY, he was ordained a Paulist priest on December 22, 1934 at the Lateran Basilica, Rome, Italy. During his sixty-seven years of priestly service, Fr. Troy served at parishes in Portland, OR; New York, NY; Austin, TX; San Francisco, CA, and Boston, MA. Under his guidance Saint Philip Neri Church was built in Portland and a church, rectory and school at Saint Austin's in Austin. A wake will be held on Friday, August 10, 2001 from 2 to 8 P.M. at Saint Paul the Apostle rectory, 415 West 59th Street, New York, NY. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10:00 A.M. on Saturday, August 11, 2001 at Saint Paul's Church. Interment will be at Mount Paul Cemetery, Oak Ridge, NJ.
