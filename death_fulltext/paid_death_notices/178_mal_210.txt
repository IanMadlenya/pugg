
        SHULMAN-Max L. The American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science mourns the passing of Major Max L. Shulman, dedicated ACWIS board member; President's Circle member; benefactor of the Joe and Celia Weinstein Scholarship; & avid supporter of the Weizmann Institute. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife Sylvia Weinstein Shulman, son Lloyd Shulman, daughter Gail Koster, and the entire family. Robert H. Asher, Chairman, American Committee Albert Willner, M.D., President Martin Kraar, Exec. V.P. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Prof. Haim Harari, President
