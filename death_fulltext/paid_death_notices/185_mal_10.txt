
        GRAHAM-Francis D., 89. Died on May 24, 2002. Of Walnut Creek, CA, previously of Cedar Grove and West Orange, NJ. He is survived by his wife of sixty-three years, Rosalind (Hausthor); his daughter Susan Graham Harrison of Orinda, CA; his son Kenneth R. Graham of Berkeley Heights, NJ; his grandchildren, Melinda Graham, Laura Graham and Craig A. Harrison; his sister, Ruth G. Shriro of Walnut Creek, CA, and many nieces and nephews. He graduated from Columbia University, and worked for many years as a mechanical engineer and management consultant. Memorial contributions may be made to John Muir Foundation, 1400 Treat Blvd, Walnut Creek, CA 94596.
