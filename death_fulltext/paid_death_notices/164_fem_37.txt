Survived by her sons, Kenneth (Phyllis), Richard (Elise); grandchildren Joann, Marc, Dara, Samantha; greatgranchild JJ. Services will be on Sunday, September 10th, 12 noon, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Union Fields.
