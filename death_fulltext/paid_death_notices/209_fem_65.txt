
        BAER--Marion C., on June 9, 2004. Beloved sister of Joseph (Geraldine), Jane (William) and the late Eleanor (Frank). Devoted aunt of ten nieces and nephews. She also leaves behind many caring friends. Long time employee of B. Altman and Company. Friends may call at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue, New York City, Saturday and Sunday, 2-5 and 79pm. Mass of Christian burial Monday, June 14, at 10am at Epiphany Church, 22nd Street and Second Avenue, NYC.
        BAER -- Marion C. The Trustees and the staff of the Altman Foundation mourn the death of our beloved Secretary and friend who served from 1970 to 1995. She was also the Executive Secretary to the President of B. Altman & Company for 31 years. Her loyalty, her high standards and her sense of humor have influenced generations of colleagues and friends. Even a ''man on a galloping horse'' would notice the hole she has left in our hearts. We extend our sympathy to her brother, sister, and her many nieces, nephews and grand nieces and nephews. Jane B. O'Connell, President Karen L. Rosa, Vice Pres. & Exec Dir
