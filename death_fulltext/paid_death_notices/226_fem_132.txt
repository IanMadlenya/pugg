
        SILBERLING--Diane. The faculty and staff of the Charles C. Harris Skin and Cancer Unit, Department of Dermatology, NYU School of Medicine record with sadness the death of Diane Silberling. Diane served our department loyally for over 30 years working with our past three Chairmen. Her caring and enthusiasm are missed. We extend our sympathy to her entire family. Nicholas A. Soter, MD Interim-Chair NYU Department of Dermatology
