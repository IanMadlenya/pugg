
        DORFMAN--Carl. The Board of Directors, staff and members of the Y express profound sorrow from the passing of our dear friend and Honorary Director, Carl Dorfman. Carl served as a Director and Officer for three decades with dedication and untiring effort. His devotion to his late wife, Libby was a model to all. Our deepest sympathies to his family. Jeffrey M. Loewy, President Martin G. Englisher Executive V.P.
