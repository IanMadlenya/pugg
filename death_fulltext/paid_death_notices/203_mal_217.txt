
        POLLOCK -- Eugene D. Passed away December 14, 2003 in Aventura, FL, at age 91. Beloved husband of the late Josephine, loving father of Wendy Pollock, Hillel and Beth Pollock, grandfather of Lara and Marc, uncle of Dennis and June Pollack. Former executive of National United Jewish Appeal, serving over 35 years. Mentor and friend to many. Known for his compassion, relentless intellect and search for adventure.
