        SCHRAGIS-Fannie (nee Hurwitz). Of Peekskill, N.Y. On December 28, 1999. Age 95. A loving, intelligent, unique woman of the 20th century. Beloved wife for 61 years of the late Jacob Schragis. Loving mother of Shirley and Seymour Gordon, Alvin and Carole Schragis & Rae & Bert Messenger. Devoted sister of Dr. Maurice Hurwitz and Harry Harwood & the late Mollie Goldstein and Ida Bloom. Inspiring aunt, grandmother of eight and great-grandmother of 12. What a privilege it was to have our Fannie, our mother, our Bubbie, whose wisdom, wit and love will continue to illuminate the lives of so many. Services at 12 noon on Friday, December 31 at the First Hebrew Congregation, East Main Street (Route 6), Peekskill. Shiva will be observed at the home of Rae & Bert Messenger beginning Saturday evening January 1, 2000. Contributions in her memory may be sent to the Peekskill Field Library, The Hudson Valley Hospital Center or the First Hebrew Congregation of Peekskill.
        SCHRAGIS-Fannie. The end of the last chapter of a beautiful book. She was a special lady. Love, Karen and Marty Berger
