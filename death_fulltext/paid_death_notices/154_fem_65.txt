
        VOGEL-Ruth. (nee Epstein). Died peacefully November 7, 1999 at the age of 82 at her home in Boynton Beach, FL. Formerly of New York City, Chatham, N.Y. and Freeport, L.I. Beloved wife of Dr. George for 56 years. Devoted mother of Dr. Robert and Ronald. Loving grandmother of Daria, Evan, Geoffrey and Alec. Dedicated volunteer of the Jewish Board of Social Children's Services. Graveside service to be held 11AM, Wednesday at Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, N.Y. Donations in Ruth's name may be made to the Fund for Blood and Cancer Research, Weill Medical College of Cornell University, C/O Dr. Mark Pasmantier, 407 East 70 Street, N.Y., N.Y. 10021.
