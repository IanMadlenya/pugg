
        GRAY-John, M.D. THe New York Medical Group P.C., the Board of Directors and the entire medical and administrative staff mourn the loss of John Gray, M.D. on Tuesday, October 28, 1997. Dr. Gray was affiliated with the New York Medical Group P.C. since October, 1982. He was a member of the Board of Directors until his untimely passing. His numerous friends and admirers within the health care community also join in sympathy with his beloved family. He won the respect and admiration of all those who came within his circle, and will be greatly missed. Moshe Labi, M.D., Medical Director New York Medical Group PC
