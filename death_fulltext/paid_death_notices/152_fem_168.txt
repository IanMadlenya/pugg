
        RABINEAU-Ruth. At age 80, on September 17th, 1999. Beloved wife of the Westchester architect, Eli Rabineau, devoted mother of Susan Wilmarth-Rabineau and Phyllis Rabineau, loving motherin-law of John Alderson and loving grandmother of Isaac Alderson of Chicago, Illinois. Ruth was a highly respected interior designer and gifted painter. She worked as an interior designer in New York City, as well as with her husband on numerous architectural projects in Westchester County. Her character was absolutely flawless and her loving, tender and sensitive personality was cherished by all who knew her. A funeral service will be held at Bet Torah Synagogue, 60 Smith Avenue, Mount Kisco, NY, on Tuesday, September 21, at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made in Ruth's name to the Museum of Modern Art, New York City.
