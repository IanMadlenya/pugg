
        VAN NOSTRAND-William P. of Bronxville, N.Y., age 79. Retired Executive Search Consultant, died August 15, 1999 after a long illness. He attended The Taft School in Watertown, CT and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Cambridge MA, class of 1942. He was a member of the Holland Society of New York. He served as a officer in the U.S. Navy during World War II in both the European and Pacific Theaters. He leaves his wife Jacqueline and son William, Jr. The funeral and interment will be private.
