
        LUERY-Robert Leslie, 79, of Laguna Beach, CA., on February 10, 1997 at South Beach Hospital. Father of Andrea Fenton, Michael and Russell Luery. Former husband of Anita Meyers Luery. Predeceased by his daughter Roberta Jeanne Luery. Retired Property Manager of W.M. Capital Management whose holdings include the Empire State Building. Funeral Services will be held on Friday Feb. 14th in Temple Sinai, 458 Lakeside Drive, Stamford, Ct. Burial will follow in Fairfield Memorial Park, Stamford. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Temple Sinai or to the Roberta Jeanne Luery Memorial Fund for Study Abroad, Attention: Jack Pirozzi, Box 1228, 1 Brookings Drive, Washington University, St. Louis, MO. 63130. For information Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, Stamford, Connecticut.
