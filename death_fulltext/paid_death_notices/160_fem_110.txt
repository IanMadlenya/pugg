
        HANSEN-Isabelle M. (nee Dillinger). Age 74, of New York City. Died at Beth Israel Medical Center Sunday, May 7, 2000. She resided in the New York area her whole life. Wife of the late Martin C. Hansen & daughter of the late Edward G. Dillinger. Survived by her mother Elvira M. Dillinger (nee Gilkinson), her brother and his wife Edward and Eleanor Dillinger, her niece & her husband Candie and William Fuller and their children Melinda and Kelsey Fuller. Burial will be handled by Frank E. Campbell, and will be private.
