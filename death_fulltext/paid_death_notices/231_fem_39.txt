
        LEE--Frances P. Died on April 5 at her home with family and friends by her side. A survivor of numerous health challenges since childhood and a pioneering open-heart surgery patient in 1970, she maintained her grace and beauty to the very end. She is predeceased by her parents, Albert and Barbara Parillo and by her former husband Warren B. Lee with whom she raised their two children, Beck and Barbara Lee. With her brother Albert M. Parillo she co-founded a New Yorkbased advertising agency, Par Communications Ltd. All who knew her, including her many friends in the Upper West Side building she called home for over 45 years, celebrate her indomitable will to live. May she savor her hardearned and richly deserved rest after a long and eventful journey. For information: Sisk Brothers Funeral Home, Hamden, CT.
