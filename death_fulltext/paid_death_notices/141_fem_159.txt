
        DELGADO deTorres-Mercedes Salvador. Born December 28, 1907, Madrid, Spain. Died July 2, 1998, at Beth Israel Hospital, New York. Widow of Demetrio Delgado deTorres. Pre-deceased by her eldest son Juan. She is survived by her three children, Ines, Pablo, and Miguel, and five grandchildren, Maud Kozodoy, Fergus Kinnell, Lena Delgado deTorres, Christian and Andreas Delgado deTorres. In lieu of flowers, donations by friends may be made in her memory to: Spanish Refugee Aid, A Division of International Rescue Committee, 122 East 42 Street, New York, New York 10168-1289.
