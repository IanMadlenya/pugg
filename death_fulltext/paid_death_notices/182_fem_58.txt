
        LINCOLN-Anne. 94. Born May 13, 1907, Birmingham, AL. Died March 2, 2002, Delray Beach, FL. Beloved wife of the late Sol, mother of Rowena (Charles) and Bruce (Elaine), grandmother of Linda (Alice), David (Cassie), Melissa (Joe), and Lori (Alan), and greatgrandmother of Corey, Jennifer, Grant, Grace, Jessica, and Sara. She will be deeply missed by all.
