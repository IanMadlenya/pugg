
        KRIVITSKY--Maria (nee Martins) of New York City and Water Mill, NY. On July 28, 2006. Beloved wife of David. Maria was born June 11, 1933 in Bristol, RI. After graduating St. Mary's Seminary as class valedictorian, she graduated Albertus Magnus College in 1954. To hone her administrative and secretarial skills she enrolled at the Katherine Gibbs School in Boston in a special course for women college graduates. She spent most of her business career at the B. Altman Company. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10:30am St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 65 East 89th St, NYC. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the Animal Rescue Fund of the Hamptons (ARF), PO Box 901, Wainscott, NY 11975.
