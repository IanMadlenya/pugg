
        GREISMAN-Leah D. On September 21, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Jacob Greisman. Loving mother of Dr. Stewart G. Greisman and Lois C. Greisman. Adored grandmother of Laura, Jill and Jack. Mother-in-law of Dr. Lisa Babitz. Sister of the late Juliet Zekster, aunt of Elizabeth Girardat and Gregory Zekster, loyal alumna of Hunter College. Services Sunday, September 23, 1:15 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Scholarship and Welfare Fund of Hunter College, 695 Park Avenue, Room E 1314, NYC 10021.
        GREISMAN-Leah D. We note with deep sorrow the passing of a Past President of the Scholarship and Welfare Fund of the Alumni Association of Hunter College. She leaves for us all a shining example of dedication to the Fund, to Hunter and her class and to CUNY. Our condolences to her family. Hadassah Winer Gold, President
