
        JAKOVICH--Michael Steven, 43, of Boca Raton. Loving husband of Elena; father to Nathan, Stefen and Alex; brother to Robert; son to Elaine Brooks, and mourned by former wife, Jody, and all those students whose lives he touched and changed forever. He trusted and believed in everyone. Principal, teacher, humanitarian and idealist, Michael's wonderful sense of humor, his poems and love will never be forgotten. ''His heart was too big for this world.'' Graveside service 10:30 AM, Sunday, August 31, at the Beth-El Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey.
