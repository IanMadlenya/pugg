
        RAGLAND - Lawrence Carey ''Larry.'' Died January 24, 2002, in New York City, New York. He was a vocalist/impressionist/come dian; and commercial lines underwriter for S & M Kline Company. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, February 5, 2002, at 11 AM, at St. James Episcopal Church, Portsmouth, Virginia, by Reverend John O. Agbaje. Services by Corprew Funeral Home, Portsmouth, Virginia.
