
        SCHWARTZ -- Sidney. Born Zembrov, Poland 1908. Died April 26. Age 95. Preceded in death by wife, Lois. Survived by son Ralph, brother Isidore, two grandchildren, nieces and nephews. Longtime member Local 6 HERE; tireless fighter for rights of working people; was progressive activist from 1930s until death. In retirement he fought for preservation of Medicare and social security, wws active in Union Pensioners Society and National Council of Senior Citizens. Service at Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam, Friday 11:30 AM.
