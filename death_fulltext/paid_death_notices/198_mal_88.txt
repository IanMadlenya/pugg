
        AVALONE--Anthony I. Age 52. Died July 4, 2003. He is survived by his wife Nancy R.S. Kramer. HBO Editor for 15 years. Received 1999 International Monitors Award. Member of the Radio and TV Broadcast Engineers Union, Local 1212, IBEW. Previously married to Rebecca Koborie, who perished at the WTC on September 11. His memorial service to be held Monday, July 14, 2003, 6PM at the Cutting Room, 19 W. 24 St, N.Y.C. Donations in lieu of flowers to The Yankees Foundation, Yankee Stadium, Bronx, N.Y. 10451. Contact Michael Hegarty Funeral Home, Old Bridge, N.J. for information. >>AD#
