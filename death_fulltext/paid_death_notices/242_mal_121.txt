
        STEIN--Irwin L., on March 12, 2007 at age 83. Beloved husband of Claire of 62 years, devoted father of David (Karen) and Roni (David). Cherished grandfather of Rebecca, Richard (Pamela), Deborah and Andrew (Jaclyn) and great-grandfather of Mara. A kind and gentle man. 60 year member of the Goodfellowship Corset Club. Life Master bridge player, avid fisherman and great story teller. He will be lovingly remembered and deeply missed. Donations may be made to Hadassah or Sons of Israel Nyack.
