
        FRAENKEL-Cecele Ross. Of Purchase, NY, formerly of Rye, NY, and New Orleans, LA, died on March 25, 2000. Devoted wife of Francis. Loving mother of Sallie and Julie. Adored grandmother of Emily, Melanie and Max. Beloved sister of Paula. Cecele's memory will be cherished by all of those whose lives she touched. A leader within the Jewish community, Cecele was a dedicated supporter of UJA and Rye Community Synagogue. Funeral services are 10 AM, Monday, March 27, at Rye Community Synagogue. Donations to charity in lieu of flowers.
        FRAENKEL-Cecele. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Cecele Fraenkel, wife of Francis, and mother of Sally and Julie. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to her family. Bernard Steinweg, President David Hershberg, Secretary
