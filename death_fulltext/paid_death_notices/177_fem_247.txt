
        GORMAN-Martha. Died October 24th, 2001 at the age of 87. Following the performing career under the stage name of Marny Frances, she was the beloved wife of the late David Gorman. She was the loving mother of Gail Furman and mother-in-law of Jay Furman; devoted grandmother of Jason and Jesse Furman, Ariela Dubler, and Eve Gerber; and adoring great grandmother of Ilan David Dubler-Furman. She is survived as well by her devoted caregivers, Sonia Reid and Beverly Golding. We all loved her very much, and she will be missed by all who knew her. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue on Sunday, October 28th at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may made to Planned Parenthood of NYC, 810 Seventh Avenue, New York, New York 10019.
