
        SWARTZ--Paul Edgar. On Sunday, May 23, 2004, at the age of 96, at his residence in Riverdale. Beloved husband of the late Edith G. (nee Williams) Swartz. Surviving are his sister Ruth M. Bankert of Hanover, PA, numerous nieces, nephews and many friends. He was a retired senior partner of Satterlee, Stephens, Burke & Burke LLP and a respected tax attorney. Funeral service Wednesday, June 2, at 11 AM, from the Riverdale Presbyterian Church, 4763 Henry Hudson Parkway, Riverdale, NY. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery. For more information, call Riverdale-on-Hudson Funeral Home at 718-884-6100.
