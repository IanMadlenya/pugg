
        POLLINS-Martin S. Retired radio, television and entertainment executive. Died on October 28, 1999 at the age of 80 in New York City. Martin was born in Manhattan and served in the Navy during WWII. He was a senior executive in spot sales for ABC and NBC, Vice President of Distribution for Paramount Pictures and President of Gannett Productions in New York City. He is survived by his wife Judith, sister Elinor Ellis, daughters Terri Carr and Phyllis Maguire, three grandchildren, Lindsay and Morgan Maguire, and Ryan Carr. A memorial service will be held at ''The Riverside'', Sunday, October 31st, 11:15AM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Make a Wish Foundation, 85 Old Shore Road, Port Washington, New York 11050.
