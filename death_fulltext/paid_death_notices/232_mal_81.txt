
        GERHOLD - Paul. Passed away peacefully at home on May 6, 2006. Paul was a leader in market research for over 60 years. He started his career at the Chicago Mail Order Company in 1938, held top media and research jobs at Lord and Thomas, Foote Cone & Belding, J. Walter Thompson, and then served as president of Advertising Research Foundation. He was elected to the Market Research Council Hall of Fame. He shared 62 years with his beloved wife Connie, who died last year. He is survived by three sons and their families: Paul Jr. and Claudette, Timothy and Annie, and Mark and Carolyn. A memorial service will be held on May 12, 2006, 11:00AM at the Larchmont Ave. Presbyterian Church, Larchmont, NY.
