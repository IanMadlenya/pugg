
        KRAMER--Lola. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of the Jewish Federation of Palm Beach County mark with great sadness the passing of Lola Kramer, a pillar of Jewish life in the greater Palm Beaches. Her unwavering support of the Jewish people and our community will forever serve as a valued inspiration and example to all who knew and loved her and for generations to come. We extend our deepest sympathies to her family. Judith A. Levy, President Jeffrey L. Klein, CEO
        KRAMER--Lola. The Partners of Katten Muchin Rosenman mourn the passing of our longtime friend and client Lola Kramer and offer our sincere condolences to her husband Saul, her children, Robert Kramer and Kathie Kramer Rudy and her grandchildren, David, Nancie, James, Charles, Lesley and Michael.
