
        SAPERIA-Irving. The trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services are saddened by the passing of Irving Saperia, the father of our colleague and friend Phillip A. Saperia, executive director of the Coalition of Voluntary Mental Health Agencies. We express our deepest sympathies to his wife, Pearl; his daughters, Judy Bohrer and Barbara Zwart; his sons, Bruce and Phil; his grandchildren; and other members of his family. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive Vice President Paul Levine, CSW, Associate Executive V.P.
        SAPERIA-Irving. The Board of Directors and The Staff of The Coalition of Voluntary Mental Health Agencies, deeply mourn the death of Irving Saperia, father of Phillip A. Saperia, Executive Director of The Coalition. We extend our deepest sympathy to his mother, Pearl, brother, Bruce, sisters, Barbara and Judy, and to Phillip's niece, Danielle, and nephews, Adam and Jason. Funeral services will be held on Monday January 15, 2001, at 10 A.M. at The LevineBriss Funeral Home, 84 Mazzeo Drive, Randolph, MA. The family will sit shiva after the funeral services at Trilling House, 640 North Main Street, Randolph, MA.
