
        BRADLEY-John Francis II. Passed to Heaven with our Lord Jesus Christ, December 31, 1952. Loving husband of Florence Duddy Bradley, deceased 1970. Survived by his son John Francis Bradley III, John and Virginia Connolly (Bradley), James and Edda Bradley, and many grandchildren, grandnieces and grandnephews. May he rest in peace in the company of the Lord. Amen.
