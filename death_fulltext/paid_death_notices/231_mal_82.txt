
        FOYE--Harold J., CSP. Age 90, died Monday, April 4, at Marian Manor, South Boston. Father Foye was ordained in 1946 at St. Paul the Apostle Church in New York City. For sixty years he served Catholic communities from Boston to Johannesburg, from Toronto to San Francisco as a campus minister and parish priest. He was also an effective preacher of missions and retreats in many parts of North America. A vigil service will be held on Monday, April 10 from 4 to 6 PM at the Paulist Center, 5 Park St, Boston, MA. Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated Tuesday at 10AM and burial will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery, W. Roxbury, MA.
