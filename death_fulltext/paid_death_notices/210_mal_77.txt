
        WOLFE--Brian, suddenly on July 5th, 2004 at age 29. Born in Yonkers October 10, 1974 to William and Patricia (nee McDermott) Wolfe. He was former sales manager of Pace BMW and currently a Futures Trader. Brian was an avid sailor and motorcycle enthusiast. Survived by his father William Wolfe and stepmother Eileen Hawe, brother Michael and wife Kerri Wolfe, Brian's love, Aimee Leddy, nieces Shannon and Kristen Wolfe. He is predeceased by his mother Patricia. Visitation Thursday 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 at Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 East Boston Post Rd., Mamaroneck, New York. For more information 914698-5968, coxeandgrazia no.com followed by interment at Gate of Heaven.
