
        CUNNINGHAM-James F. of Port Jefferson Station, L.I., formerly of Bay Shore, L.I. and Astoria, Queens. Dedicated service to the Marine Corps. Proud retiree of the NYC Fire Department and Air France. James was a marathon runner, world traveler who joyfully enriched the lives of all who crossed his path. Devoted husband of the late Theresa M. Cunningham. Loving father of Debra T. (Donald C.) Elliott, Daniel J. (Debra) Cunningham, James J. (Christine) Cunningham and Michael R. (Ellie M.) Cunningham. Cherished grandfather of 11 and great grandfather of two. Fond brother of Joseph Cunningham and Doris D'eufemia. Dear companion of Dorothy Nevins. Reposing at The Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Hwy., (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Cswy). Funeral Prayer Service of Christian Burial, Thursday 10:00 AM at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, Long Island. Interment Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton, L.I. Visiting Tuesday and Wednesday 24:30 and 7-9:30 PM.
