
        SIMPSON-James Randolph, 80, of Houston, TX and Bay Head, NJ on June 7, 1997. Long time employee of W.R. Grace Co. and Occidental Petroleum Co. He is survived by his wife, five daughters, four grandchildren, a brother, a sister, four step-children and four stepgrandchildren. The Funeral Service will be held at The Bay Head Chapel, Bay Head, NJ, Wednesday at 11:00 A.M. Interment will follow in Greenwood, Brielle, NJ. Friends are invited to visit with the family on Tuesday evening 5-7 P.M., at the Neary-Quinn Funeral Home, 39 South Street, Manasquan, New Jersey. Contributions may be made to a charity of your choice.
