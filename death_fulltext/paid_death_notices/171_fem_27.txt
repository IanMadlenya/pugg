
        FENIMORE-Ruth Thomson. August 17, 1920 to April 2, 2001. Ruth was a strong, kind, selfless woman. She worked for many years to nurture and provide for her 4 loving daughters, Christina, Cynthia, Patricia, and Janet. She was loved by all her family, and cherished by her friends. She was adored by her grandchildren, Sarah, Ben, David, Emily, Lindsay, and Freda. All of us will miss Mona.
