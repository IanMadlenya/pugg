
        ANTOSOFSKY-Lisa. 37, of Boulder, CO, daughter of Rabbi Marvin Antosofsky and Ruth Seif, after a courageous ten year battle with breast cancer. A talented musician, counselor, teacher of Judaism and spirituality, she brought joy to all with her magnetic personality and exhuberant vitality. She strove always to live life to its fullest, seeking truth, beauty and countless ways to repair the world. She will be sorely missed by all, especially her companion Donna, her family David and Diana, Mark and Barbara, Nomi and Joe, Michael and Joel, Leah, Julia and Anna, Loretta and Norman and their families.
