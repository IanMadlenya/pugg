
        COLON--Jose M., M.D., on September 26, 2006. Loving son of Susana, dear brother of Rafael, Vivian and Susie, loving nephew of Nydia Pesquera. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Dr. Colon was a Doctor of Medicine, New York University School of Medicine. Among his credentials, he was also Assistant Professor, Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, New Jersey School of Medicine, Attending Physician, Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, UMDNJ-University Hospital, Assistant Attending Physician, Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Hackensack University Medical Center and University Reproductive Associate, Hasbrouck Heights, NJ. Services to be announced at a later date.
