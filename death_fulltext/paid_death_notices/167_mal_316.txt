
        HILAL-Sadek K. The Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, The College of Physicians and Surgeons, The Department of Radiology and their Alumni Associations, mourn the passing of Dr. Sadek K. Hilal. Dr. Hilal was a worldrenowned neuroradiologist, inventor, and Professor Emeritus, who developed magnetic resonance imaging at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. He was instrumental in the establishment of the Hatch MRI Center at CPMC Neurological Institute. We extend our condolences to his wife Cynthia and their children, Peter, Diane, Paul and Philip. Herbert Pardes, M.D. President and CEO NY Presbyterian Medical Center Thomas Q. Morris, M.D. and David Hirsh, Ph.D. Interim Deans, College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University Philip O. Alderson, M.D. Chairman, Department of Radiology, College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University
