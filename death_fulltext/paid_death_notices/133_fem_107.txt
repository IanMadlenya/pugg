
        del BALSO-Tobi Rosen. Of Riverdale. The best friend we ever had. Died February 10, 1998. Much too soon, after a long illness of cancer and multiple sclerosis. She was a teacher and counselor who changed the lives of everyone she met. She is survived by her husband Paul, father George E. Rosen, nieces Jill and Tina, nephew Eric and all her friends. Services will be held Friday, 12:30 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
