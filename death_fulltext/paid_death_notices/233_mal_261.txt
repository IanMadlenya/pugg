
        DARLING--George F., 70, of Providence RI and formerly of East 77th Street, New York City, died June 16. Mr. Darling was the vice president of Regatta Sport in New York City. He was a former member of the New York Athletic Club and the Broadway Theatre League. He leaves a sister, nieces and a nephew. The funeral and burial was in Providence, RI. Donations in his memory may be made to Hospice Outreach, 502 Bedford Street, Fall River, MA 02720.
