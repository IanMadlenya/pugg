
        FOLEY-Archibald Richard, M.D. On February 12, 1997. Survived by his sister, Edna Foley Gannon, and many nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. Visitation at the Abbey (Campbell Bldg) 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Friday 5-7PM. Mass of Resurrection at the Church of Our Saviour, Park Avenue at 38th Street, Saturday 10:30 AM. Burial will be in Kingston, Ontario, Canada. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Fountain House or a charity of your choice.
        FOLEY-Archibald R. Arch served and shared our community for over thirty-three years. His sensitivity, insight and good humor enriched the lives of all who were privileged to meet and work with him, and we will miss him greatly. We send our sympathy to his family for his tragic and untimely passing. The members, staff and Board of Directors of Fountain House
