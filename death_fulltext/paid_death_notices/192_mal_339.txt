
        BROWNING-John. Manhattan School of Music is saddened by the loss of former faculty member, John Browning. One of America's most eminent pianists, he was a member of The School's piano faculty from 1980 to 1986. His intellect and vigor enriched the lives of his many students. We extend our most heartfelt sympathy to his family. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
        BROWNING-John. In eternal and loving memory of John Browning and Tyler. Daniel and Barbara Liberman
