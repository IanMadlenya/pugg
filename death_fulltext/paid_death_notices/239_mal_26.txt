
        GALLETTA--Claudia Black, 90, of Ansonia CT, formerly of Old Westbury, NY. Born October 3, 1916 died on November 24, 2006. Beloved wife of Mario (Mal) Galletta, Loving mother of Malcolm and his wife Claire, and Claudia Mirante. Loving grandmother of Arthur Mirante, Claudia O'Regan, Malcolm and Danielle Galletta and greatgrandmother of six greatgrandchildren. Funeral mass was held at St. Brigid's Church, Westbury, NY. Interment at Holy Rood Cemetery. Leo F. Kearns Funeral Home, 718-441-3300.
