
        MOSS--Dr. Doris R. Loving wife of the late Ira. Cherished mother of Rabbi Steven and Judy, Marjorie and Emil Sobylak. Devoted grandmother of Karl, Anya, Eli and the late Paul. Among her many accomplishments she was the Director for Adult Education for the City of New York, aide to US Congressmen and founder of the Museum of the Rockaways. She will be missed by her family, friends and community. She and dad are together once again. Funeral services will be held Sunday, 1:15 PM, at the Sinai Chapels, Fresh Meadows, New York.
