
        BELL--Philip, of Rockville Centre, NY, died peacefully early Friday at South Nassau Communities Hospital. Beloved and devoted husband of Gladys; dedicated father of Jack and Robert; cherished grandfather who worshiped Jesse, Samantha and Sadie; dear father-in-law to Theresa and Teresa. Your love, smile, gentle nature, sense of humor and kindness will be dearly missed by all. He proudly served his country in the Pacific during World War II. A memorial service will be held at 1 pm tomorrow, May 20, 2007, at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY, with burial to follow at Mount Lebanon Cemetery in Glendale, Queens.
