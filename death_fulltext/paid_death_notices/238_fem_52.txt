
        FRESSOLA--Elena, died at her home in Edgewater, NJ, last month shortly before her 85th birthday. Her love will be greatly missed by all who knew her. She was always a caring aunt, great-aunt, and great-great-aunt. Her funeral was held at Our Lady of Pompei Church in Greenwich Village, where she was born. Donations in her memory should be made to Sacred Heart Monastery, POB 900, Hales Corners, WI 53130.
