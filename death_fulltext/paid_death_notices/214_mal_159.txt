
        SCHWAB--Betty B. 78, passed away peacefully November 13, after a five-year, courageous battle with ovarian cancer. Beloved wife of Martin for 55 years. Adored mother of Jeffrey and Magdi, Sally and Sam Honig, Steven and Susan, sister of Elizabeth and Richard Fuld. Devoted grandmother of Jay, Katie, Julie, Adam, Alex and step-grandchildren Szonja and Krista. We will miss you, Mom. Services Wednesday, November 17, at 11 am, at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave East, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to charity of choice.
        SCHWAB -- Betty B. The Board of Directors and Staff of The Nathan Miller Center for Nursing Care extend our condolences to our longtime Board Member, Martin Schwab and family on the death of his wife Betty.
