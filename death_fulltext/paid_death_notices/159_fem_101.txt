
        SEILER-Pauline R. Age 93, of Rockville, Maryland, beloved wife of the late Max W. Seiler, died April 10, 2000. She is survived by her loving children, Ilene Gruenfeld, and sons, Leonard, and Dr. Jerome Seiler, and daughter-inlaws, Elaine and Harriet, son-inlaw, Ernest, and adoring fourteen grandchildren, and loving four great grandchildren. Interment will be on Thursday, April 13th, at 2 P.M. at Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, New Jersey at the intersections of Route 1 and The Garden State Parkway.
