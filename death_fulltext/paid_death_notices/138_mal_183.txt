
        LEIR-Henry. Bar-Ilan University in Israel and its American Friends mourn with great sorrow the passing of beloved friend and benefactor, Henry Leir. Through his commitment to our university, he made a significant contribution to the advancement of higher education and the strengthening of scholarly excellence and Jewish heritage. We extend heartfelt condolences to his sister, Hedy Wegier, and his entire family. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman Chancellor, Bar-Ilan University Melvin Stein, President, American Friends of Bar-Ilan Brig. Gen. (Res.) Yehuda Halevy Executive Vice President, Bar-Ilan University

        LEIR-Henry J. The Board of Trustees, Overseers, President, Administration, Faculty and Staff of Tufts University mourn the passing of philanthropist and visionary Henry J. Leir. His dedication to education and international nutrition which he shared with his friend and former Tufts President Jean Mayer have left a legacy that will improve the lives of future generations.
