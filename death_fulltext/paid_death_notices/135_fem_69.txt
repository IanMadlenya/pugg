
        FELDMAN - Gwendolyn. (Nee Kieve). Of Tenafly, NJ on April 9, 1998 following a valiant battle with ovarian cancer. Beloved wife of Bud (Edgar J.) for 45 years. Dear mother of Amy Feldman & Nena Colligan. Adored grandmother of Ted, Will and Kieve Colligan. She died as she lived, with dignity and grace, surrounded by her family. There will be a service at Wien & Wien, 129 Engle Street, Englewood, New Jersey, on Monday, April 13 at 11:30AM. Visiting at the chapel 10:30AM before funeral. Contributions to the National Ovarian Cancer Coalition, P.O. Box 4472, Boca Raton, FL 33472, in her memory would be appreciated.
