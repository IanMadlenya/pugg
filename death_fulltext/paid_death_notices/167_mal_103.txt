
        CARR-Robert S., 57, of Stamford, CT, an executive of Citigroup for 19 years, passed away suddenly on December 5. Beloved husband and best friend of Joan. Loving and devoted son of Elsie Peterson Carr and Harry F. Carr of Wilmington, DE. Brother of the late Thomas Carr. Bob was an exceptional man who made a difference in the lives of many. He was quiet, unselfish and faced life with humor and a loving heart which will live on in us forever. Bob really loved his family, his friends, his home, his job and his life with Joan. As a managing director of Citigroup's Audit and Risk Review he was thoughtful, supportive, and one who made things happen. He is a shining example to us all of leadership and in caring for his staff and others. A memorial service will be held at Leo P. Gallagher Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St., Stamford, CT Saturday, December 9, at 1:00PM. The family will receive guests at the funeral home on Saturday from 11:30 AM until the time of service. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be sent to The University of New Mexico-Anderson School, Albuquerque, NM 87131 or Stamford Hospital, Shelburne Road, Stamford, CT 06902.
        CARR-Robert. We will so miss Bob's friendship, humor, warmth and wisdom. To his beloved wife, Joan, and Elsie, his cherished mother, we extend our love and deepest sympathy. Bob will live forever in our hearts. Valerie and Jim O'Halpin
