
        RATTINER-A. Alan. Of Montauk, NY, and Pompano Beach, FL, on November 8, 1997, at the age of 86. Beloved husband of Jeanette, loving father of Daniel and Nancy, cherished grandfather of Maya, Adam, David and Gabriel, Andrew and Eric. Services Monday, 11 AM, at the Jewish Center of the Hamptons in East Hampton.
        RATTINER-Alan. The Officers and Trustees of the Jewish Center of the Hamptons mourn the passing of Alan Rattiner, a devoted member of our Congregation and extend our condolences to his beloved wife Jen and his family.
