
        TORTORELLA-Carmela B. (Camille), on January 11th, 2001. Devoted wife of the late Salvatore. Cherished mother of Michael. Adored mother-in-law of Andrea. Fond grandmother of the late Matthew Tortorella. Camille graduated from New York University in 1940 and held a Master's Degree in Education from Columbia University. She was a special education teacher for the New York City School System. Viewing at the David J. Hodder & Son Funeral Home, 899 McLean Ave. (near Kimball Ave.), Yonkers, NY, Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 9:45 AM, St. Barnabas Church. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery.
