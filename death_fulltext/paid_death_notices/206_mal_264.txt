
        NEW--Herman. It is with heartfelt sorrow that we record the passing of Herman New, beloved father of Michael New, Executive Director of the Sephardic Nursing & Rehabilitation Center in Brooklyn, New York, and sister, Margaret Yosub of Petach Tikvah, Israel. Herman was a dear resident of the Sephardic home for 7 years. He was a loving husband to Sarah, of blessed memory; father, father-in-law, brother, grandfather and great-grandfather. We extend our sincere condolences to the entire family. Funeral and Shiva will be held in Israel. Executive Officers Board of Directors, and Staff of Sephardic Home
