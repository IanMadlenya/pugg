
        GREENBERG-William (Bill). 77 years, of Margate, FL, formerly of Merrick, NY died Friday, July 25, 1997. Beloved husband of Ruth; father of Steve Greenberg and Caryn Chechanover; father-in-law of Paul Chechanover; brother of Ada Michel. Bill was an educator and a veteran of both World War II and Korea. Golf was his passion. He will be missed by his many relatives and friends.
