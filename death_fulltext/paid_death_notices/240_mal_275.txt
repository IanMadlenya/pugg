
        KARNAL--George J. Passed away peacefully in Burlingame, CA, on January 20, 2007, at age 92. Husband of Audrey Karnal; father of Karen and George Kingston and the late Craig Karnal; grandfather, uncle and cousin to many. Born in Brooklyn, NY, and raised in New Jersey. In 1947, after he was married in San Francisco, he and Audrey moved to New York and then Connecticut. Moved back to San Francisco in 1975 after George retired as National Sales Manager for Phillip Morris Co. (Altria). He was an honorary life member of the American Red Cross, and a volunteer for the NY Jewish Federation. Services by Sinai Memorial Chapel, 415 921-3636
