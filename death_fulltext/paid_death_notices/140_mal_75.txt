
        DETELS-Michael. Of Point Lookout, LI, died suddenly at the age of 63 on Thursday, September 3rd, at Long Beach Medical Center, Long Beach, NY. He is survived by his mother, Janice Crooker Detels, his beloved wife, Lucy (Nee Hitt) Detels, his three children Michael Clayton, Holly and Brett. Proud grandfather of Megan. Dear brother of Martin Paul, Peter, Roger and Terry. A memorial service to be held at The Point Lookout Community Church, Freeport Avenue, Point Lookout, Long Island, New York, Saturday, September 19, 1998 at 11 A.M.
