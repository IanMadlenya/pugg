
        PIKE-Timothy C. Died Wednesday, March 27, 2002 at Cabrini Hospice. He was a graduate of Manhattan College and New York University. He was a participating associate of Jaros, Baum & Bolles. He is survived by his parents Leroy and Nancy Pike, brothers William and David, and sister Dr. Anne Pike-Tay, niece Hannah Tay and nephew Brendan Tay. Memorial mass will be April 26, 2002, 11 AM at St. Francis Xavier Church, 46 West 16th St. Memorial contributions to Cabrini Hospice, 227 E. 19th St., NYC 10003.
