
        SABRIN-George J., M.D. 86, of Manhasset and Westhampton Beach died peacefully March 31. He is survived by his loving wife of 58 years, Alma; his children Russell of Atlanta, GA, Douglas of Manhasset, Amy of Washington, D.C., and Pamela of NYC; grandchildren Adam, Michael and Lucy; and sister Alice Gordon of Great Neck. Dr. Sabrin was a retired General Practitioner who served his patients for over 50 years. Born in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, he attended Boy's High School and Columbia College. He earned his medical degree from the University of Edinburgh, College of Physicians & Surgeons and completed his residency at Mt. Sinai Hospital. During WWII, Dr. Sabrin served as a Major in the Air Force, stationed in the South Pacific. Dr. Sabrin played semi-pro baseball in the UK with the Wembley Lions and was a founding member of the Aspatuck Tennis Club of Westhampton Beach. Services were held April 2, Sinai Chapels, Fresh Meadows. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Mt. Sinai Alumni, Box 1015, 1 Gustav L. Levy Pl., NY, NY 10029.
