
        ABOFF-Celia. Died in Ft. Lauderdale on July 4, 2002 at the age of 87. Beloved mother of Bruce and sister-in-law of Tess; cherished aunt of Stanley, Nancy, Stephen, and David, and great aunt of Jill and Michelle, Alexandra and Peter, Randy and Allison, and David; adoring great-great aunt of Taylor, Nicholas, Tyler and Emily, and Monica; special friend to Debbie, Ellie, John, and Henny, and good friend to so many others. She was greatly loved and admired, and she will be greatly missed. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York Public Library where she enjoyed her many years as a volunteer.
