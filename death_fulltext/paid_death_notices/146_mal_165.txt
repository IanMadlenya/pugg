
        DORRANCE-Dick. Accomplished writer, advertising executive, Dartmouth graduate '36, died February 19th at home in Goiania, Brazil. He was 85. During his long professional career, he was Director of Promotion for the CBS Radio Stations, founder of the first FM broadcasters trade organization and principal of Dorrance and Company, advertising and trade promotion specialists. He is survived by his wife Abadia, a son, daughter-in-law, daughter, and four granddaughters.
