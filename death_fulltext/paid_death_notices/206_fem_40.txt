
        ROTHSCHILD--Maurine. The Trustees and staff of The Museum of Modern Art are saddened by the death of Maurine Rothschild, longtime member of the Trustee Committee on Museum Archives, Library and Research and founding member of The Library Council. Maurine's generosity to and faithful participation in these committee's events always ensured a lively and stimulating discussion and celebration of the Museum's research resources. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her husband Bob & extended family. Glenn D. Lowry, Director Agnes Gund, President Emerita Barbara Jakobson, Chair, Trustee Committee on Museum Archives, Library & Research Milan R. Hughston, Chief of Library & Museum Archives The Museum of Modern Art, New York
