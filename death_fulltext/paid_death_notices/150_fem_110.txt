
        PEDRO-Georgie V. Passed in her 95th year, of natural causes, on July 2, 1999. Beloved wife of George, loving mother of Laura and Donald. Adoring grandmother of Kevin, Marla, Paul, Laura, Donna, Donald, Bruce and Patty. Adored great-grandmother of Stephen, Elisabeth, Lindsay and Laura. We forever love and forever remember. Till we meet again. Interment was held on Tuesday, July 6 at Woodlawn Cemetery.
