
        ROBERTS-Rhoda. On October 27, 2002 at age 88. Beloved wife of the late Maxwell B. Roberts (deceased September 11, 1988). Loving mother of John J. Roberts and Jean Colonomos. Devoted grandmother of Benjamin Roberts, Sarah Nayer, and Sarah Colonomos and great-grandmother of Danielle Roberts, Emily Roberts and Rachel Nayer. Interment private.
