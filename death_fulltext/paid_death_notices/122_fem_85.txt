        LANDE-Helen. Hadassah, WZOA mourns the passing of an extraordinary Hadassah leader and benefactor, Helen Lande. Devoted to Hadassah for over half a century, Mrs. Lande founded the Palm Beach Chapter and the very successful annual Angel of Mercy event. An ardent Zionist, she was very generous in her support of the Hadassah Medical Oganization in Jerusalem, and inspired many others with her devotion. Her leadership and dedication will be missed. May her memory be for a blessing and may the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marlene E. Post National President Beth Wohlgelernter National Executive Director
        LANDE-Helen. The Board of Governors of the Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member and dear friend, Helen Lande, and offers its heartfelt sympathy to her family and friends. A. Ephraim Diamond, Pres. Robert S. Puder, Secy.
