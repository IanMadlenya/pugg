
        SAXE--Eleanor (nee Ginsburg). Beloved wife of Ben Saxe. Cherished mother of Emmett and his wife Sharon. Sister of Rachel Weisfuse and the late Moe Ginsburg. After a long illness, she passed away peacefully Monday, November 8, 2004. She was loved and respected by family and friends. An ardent lover of Israel and the Jewish people, her life exemplified humility and acts of loving kindness. May her memory be a source of blessing. Shiva will be observed through Sunday night at the Saxe residence, 215 Walker Place, West Hempstead, N.Y. May her family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
