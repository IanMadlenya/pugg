
        O'CONNOR-Ann E. Age 83, died on March 25, 2002. Of Naples, FL and Manchester, VT. Beloved daughter of the late Randal and Florence Burns Borough. Granddaughter of William J. Burns, founder of the William J. Burns International Detective Agency Inc, which became Burns International Security Services, Inc. Beloved wife of the late John D. ''Pete'' O'Connor. Dearest sister of the late Joan Goldsborough and Randal Borough. Survived and mourned by nieces Patricia and Mary Anne, nephews Jack, Randy, and Randal, nine great nieces and nephews and three greatgreat nieces and nephews, sisterin-laws Eileen and Sarah, and nieces-in-law Maureen and Shelby. Funeral Mass at St. Theresa's RC Church, Briarcliff, New York, on Wednesday, April 3, 9:45 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the International Myeloma Foundation, 2129 Stanley Hills Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90046.
