
        FLEISCHMANN-Stephen Gardner. Died peacefully in his sleep on Wednesday, October 17th. The cause was a heart attack. Born on May 24th, 1953 in New York City, son of the late Nancy M. Schmidt and the late Peter F. Fleischmann, and stepson of Jeanne C. Fleischmann and the late Benno C. Schmidt. Survived by his loving sister Ruth G. Fleischmann, his brother James R. Fleischmann, his stepmother Jeanne C. Fleischmann, and his stepbrother David Wilson. He will be dearly missed by his Schmidt stepbrothers, Benno, Ralph, John and Bill, and their families and by his many friends. Devoted and beloved uncle to Kate, Nancy, Ruth and Russell Planitzer. Stephen graduated from the Buckley School in New York City, the Hotchkiss School and Yale University, Class of 1975. Stephen had worked at The New Yorker magazine, founded by his grandfather Raoul H. Fleischmann. Other pursuits included medical research with Dr. Samuel Perry at New York Hospital, real estate development and private investing. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to his local fire house, Engine 22, Ladder 13, 159 East 85th Street, New York, NY 10028, or the Society of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York City, 10021. The service will be at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue at 73rd Street at 11 AM, Tuesday, October 23rd.
