
        KRAMER-David T. Died suddenly on May 17, 2002 at age 51. Of Silver Spring, MD, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Beloved husband of Karen, loving father of Miriam Kramer and Aaron Levy, Danny and Miri Kramer, and Adina L. Kramer. Adoring grandfather of Kayla and Rochel Kramer. Devoted son of Milton and Etta Kramer, cherished brother of Roberta Sigall and Allen Kramer. Funeral services were held on May 19 in Silver Spring, MD. Shiva will take place in Silver Spring, MD and will also take place beginning Wednesday afternoon in Woodmere, NY.
        KRAMER-David. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the brother of its devoted member, Roberta Sigall. May his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
