
        PAPRIN--Maurice S. Passed away on November 25, 2005. Former president of Associated Builders and Owners of Greater New York, where he led the Association in the development of the residential matrix of this great city. He was concerned with that which would benefit our great city. Our sympathies are extended to his wife, Josephine, and his children Seth, Yale, Judith and Frederick. Jerome Belson, President Michael Kerr and Arnold Sollar, Co-Chairmen Nick LaPorte Associated Builders & Owners of Greater New York
        PAPRIN--Maurice. The Board of Directors of The City College Fund extends its deepest sympathy to the family of our long-time Board member. As a devoted alumnus and supporter of public higher education, he was a ''sturdy son'' of The City College and will be missed by all of us. Herbert Robinson, Chair Frank J. Sciame, President
