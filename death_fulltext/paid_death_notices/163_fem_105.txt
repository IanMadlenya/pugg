
        LOCKWOOD - Henrietta. The Board, Staff, and Friends of The New York Botanical Garden are deeply saddened by the loss of their colleague Netta Lockwood. A member of the Board through five decades and devoted to the Native Plant Garden, Netta was a masterful gardener, passionate conservationist and a great friend of public gardens. We shall miss her wit, energy and inspiration. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Wilson Nolen, Chairman Gregory Long, President
