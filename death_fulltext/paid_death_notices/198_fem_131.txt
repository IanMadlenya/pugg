
        TURECK--Rosalyn. The family of Rosalyn Tureck mourns the passing of a beloved sister, aunt and greataunt whose international renown as a pianist and harpsichordist was attributed to her lifelong devotion to the work of Johann Sebastian Bach. The family wishes to express appreciation to her many colleagues and friends who comforted her during her illness. Sonya Tureck Goldsmith Dr. Alan Bramowitz Dr. Stewart Bramson Nancy Bramson Emily, Melissa and Julie Bramowit
