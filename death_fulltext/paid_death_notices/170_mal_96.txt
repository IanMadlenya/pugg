
        SHAPIRO-Samuel. Entered into rest on March 8, of Palm Beach, Florida, formerly of Swampscott, Massachusetts. Beloved husband of Bernice (Stein) Shapiro and the late Mae Rita (Sternburg) Shapiro. Devoted father of Randi L. Cohen of Marblehead, MA., Cindi M. Shapiro of Beverly, MA., and Donald R. Shapiro of Weston, MA. Loving brother of Anne Bornstein of Brookline, MA., & the late Arthur Shapiro. Dear grandfather of 10 grandchildren. Services at Stanetsky-Hymanson Memorial Chapels, 10 Vinnin St. Salem, MA on Friday, March 9 at 12:00 Noon. Memorial observance Friday afternoon and Saturday evening at the home of Randi L. Cohen of Marblehead, MA. Expressions of Sympathy in his memory may be donated to the Good Samaritan Hospital Critical Care Unit of Palm Beach, FL., Massachusetts General Hospital Cardiology Dept., Boston, MA or Beth Israel Deaconness Hospital of Boston, MA or the Dana Farber Cancer Institute, Boston, MA. or the Jewish Rehabilitation Center of Swampscott, MA.
        SHAPIRO-Samuel N. The Palm Beach Country CLub expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member, Samuel N. Shapiro and extends sincere sympathy to his family and friends. David S. Mack, President Marshall L. Hess, Secretary
