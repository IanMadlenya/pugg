
        ROSSITER--Frances Allen: on May 17, 2006, Age 98, died peacefully at Fox Hill Village Westwood, MA. Formerly of Huntington, L.I., widow of the late Arthur W. Rossiter, Jr. Survived by sons, Edward, Allen, and Wickes and their wives, nine grandchildren and six great grandchildren. Private service to be held at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to St. Johns Church, Cold Spring Harbor, NY
