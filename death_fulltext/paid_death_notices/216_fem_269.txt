
        DALEY--Mary, (nee Collins), 96, of Bronxville, died on January 25, 2005. She was born May 20, 1908 in Bangor, Maine. Beloved wife of the late Thomas V. Daley who was the former Executive Vice President of N.Y. Telephone. Cherished mother of Elizabeth Gillis Daley, Lucinda Daley Finnegan, Thomas V. Daley, Jr. and Cornelius James Daley. Devoted grandmother of six and great grandmother of three.
