
        TANNER-Pauline Steinlauf, after a long illness, cherished wife of the late Irving; loved by children Ben and Ellen Tanner Farkas, and Nicki and Harold Tanner; and grandchildren Esta and Paul Farkas, Gary and Lynn Farkas Schwartz, Peggy and David Tanner; Catherine Allegra and James Tanner, Kent and Karen Tanner Allen; and proud great-grandmother of 11. Her intelligence, love of, and loyalty to, family strengthened and enhanced our lives. Expressions of remembrance may be made to the Hadassah Hospital in Jerusalem or to the Pauline and Irving Tanner Scholarship Fund at Cornell University. Funeral services will be Sunday, October 5, at 1:00 PM at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, New York.
        TANNER-Pauline S. The officers, members and staff of the American Jewish Committee are deeply saddened by the death of Pauline Tanner, mother of Harold Tanner, immediate past chair of AJC's Board of Trustees, and a longtime and deeply devoted member of our Board of Governors and National Leadership Council. We send our most heartfelt condolences to Harold, his wife Nicki, their children, and the rest of the grieving family. Robert S. Rifkind, President David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
