
        STARR-George, 90, of Summit on April 30. Beloved husband of the late Edna. Devoted father and father-in-law of Robby and Bob Friedland and Jennifer and Dave Friedland. Beloved grandfather of Daniel Friedland and Samantha Friedland Yucel and her husband Recai. He devoted his life to the struggle for equality for all and will be missed. Contributions can be sent to The Southern Poverty Law Center, C.A.S.A. of Morris and Sussex Counties or the Food Bank of Monmouth and Ocean Counties.
