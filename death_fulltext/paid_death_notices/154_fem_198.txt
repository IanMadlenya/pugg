
        HARRIS-Ethel Rosenfield. Lifelong Chicago resident. Died age 97, November 25, 1999. Mrs. Harris was a life Board Member of Michael Reese Hospital & a member of the Hospital's Women's Board. Charitable interests were Jewish organizations and The Art Institute of Chicago. She is survived by three daughters: Jeanne Hansell of Washington, D.C.; Mary Marks of Chicago, IL and Helen Brandt of Sante Fe, NM; their spouses; 7 grandchildren and seven greatgrandchildren. Contributions in her memory may be sent to: The Jewish Federation of Metropolitan Chicago, 1 South Franklin Street, Chicago, Illinois 60606 or Horizon Hospice, 833 West Chicago Avenue, Chicago, IL. 60622.
