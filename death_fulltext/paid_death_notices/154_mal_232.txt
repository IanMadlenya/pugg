
        BIONDO-Frank X., Ph.D.., of Oyster Bay, NY, on November 18, 1999 at the age of 72. Beloved husband of Arlene. Devoted father of Catherine. Dear brother of Dr. Vincent (Mary) Biondo, May G. Biondo, and Josephine Rizzuti. Brother-inlaw of Arthur N. (Lynn) Boulanger. Uncle of Maryann Montalbano, and Maria Biondo. Visiting at the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home, Inc., 293 South Street, Oyster Bay, Saturday & Sunday 3-5, 7-9 p.m. Funeral Mass Monday St. Dominic's R.C. Chapel 9:30 a.m. Interment Pinelawn Memorial Park. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his name to Jamaica Hospital Medical Center, 8900 Van Wyck Expressway, Jamaica, NY.
        BIONDO-Francis X., Ph.D. The Board of Trustees and staff of Jamaica Hospital Medical Center note with profound sorrow the passing of Francis X. Biondo, PhD. He served on the Board since 1986, most recently as Secretary. Frank was a true champion for this hospital and the community and his enthusiasm, spirit and wisdom will never be duplicated. Our condolences to his wife Arlene and daughter Catherine. Ann B. Dufficy, Chairman David P. Rosen, Pres & CEO
