
        ZAKIN--Albert. The 92nd Street Y Board and staff mourn the passing of Albert Zakin, adored father of Board member Nancy Lane and father-in-law of our esteemed colleague Barbi Zakin. Our hearts and prayers are with them, their spouses Jeffrey and Kenny and the entire Zakin and Lane families. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Fredric Mack, President Michael Goldstein, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
