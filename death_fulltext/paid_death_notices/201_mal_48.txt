
        EYER--George A. Jr. Died at his NYC residence on October 2, 2003. He was 89 years old and died of natural causes. Mr. Eyer attended the Groton School and Yale University, and had a distinguished naval career in WWII. He was a candidate for Congress in 1958. In recent years Mr. Eyer divided his time between his apartment on Park Avenue and his farm in Pawling, NY. He leaves behind his wife of many years, Lisa Auchincloss Eyer, Diana Bohn of McLean, VA, Lisa Ryan of East Hampton, NY, and NYC, Alexandra EyerMartinez of Gold Beach, OR, as well as six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, October 8th, 3:30 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Cha pel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the NYC Fire Department Widows and Orphans Fund.
