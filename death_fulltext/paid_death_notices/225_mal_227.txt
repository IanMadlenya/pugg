
        CURRAN--Lt. James. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend, James Curran, retired FDNY lieutenant and president of the New York Firefighters Burn Center Foundation, where, for nearly 30 years, he gave his tireless energy and support to the Hearst Burn Center at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital/Weill Cornell Medical Center. A man of wisdom, strength, compassion, and generosity, Jimmy Curran was a true hero, whose commitment to the Burn Center helped countless adults and children who suffered devastating burn-related injuries. He was beloved by all and his spirit will forever remain in our hearts. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the Curran family.
