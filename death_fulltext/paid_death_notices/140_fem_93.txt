
        LEWIN-Frances (Frolich). Beloved wife, mother and grandmother. Member of the Hunter College Alumni Hall of Fame along with classmate Bella Abzug. For 20 years she was Commissioner of the Broadway show League giving entertainment each week to 400 members of the Broadway theatre community. After graduating from Hunter, class of 1942, she worked at the Brooklyn Navy Yard on various aircraft carriers. She is survived by her husband Richard (Dick), three children, Jill Chesler of CA., Richard E. Lewin II and Jay Lewin of New York and five granddaughters. Services will be private. Contributions may be made in her name to the Hunter College Scholarship and Welfare Fund.
