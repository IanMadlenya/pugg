
        KOLTON--Hilary, 53, died peacefully on September 29th. After a long illness, she passed away at home in the presence of her family. Beloved wife of Robert, mother of Jordan and Joanna, and daughter of Julius Cohen, she is predeceased by her mother, Rosalyn Cohen, and her brother, Lee Cohen. She will be remembered as a youthful, vibrant, beautiful woman who took tremendous strength and joy from her many wonderful friends and her close and loving family. Funeral will be held at 11:30, Wednesday morning at Congregation B'nai Yisrael, 2 Banksville Rd, Armonk NY. In lieu of flowers, a donation in her memory may be made to The Institute for Neurosciences at Northern Westchester Hospital Center, 400 East Main St, Mt. Kisco, NY 10549.
        KOLTON--Hilary. We at Congregation B'nai Yisrael mourn the passing of our friend and member, Hilary Kolton. We will miss her warmth and love of life. We share our sadness with Bob, her husband, and children, Jordan and Joanna. Barby K. Siegel, President Rabbi Douglas E. Krantz Rabbi Seth M. Limmer Cantor Miriam Eskenasy
