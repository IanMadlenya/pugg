
        McBane - Day (Bonynge), 88, of Durham, NJ, died Jan. 5, 1997 in NH. Born Aug. 22, 1908, Hoboken, NJ, daughter of Albert W. and Claribel (Messiter) Bonynge. Resided in Bronxville, NY 1965-85. Member of Bronxville Dutch Reform Church , Women's Club and Music Club in Yonkers. Sruvived by her daughter, Claribel McBone and family, and a brother, Albert W. Bonynge Jr. of Doylestown, PA. Burial at a later date in Mountain Grove Memorial Cemetery, Hulett's Landing, NY. Memorials may be made to Lake George Assn. Lake George, NY  12845.
