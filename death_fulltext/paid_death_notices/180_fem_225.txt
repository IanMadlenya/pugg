
        WEISS-Gustava, 91. On December 24, 2001. Widow of Edward Weiss, pianist, and mother of Angela Migliorini, Gustava Weiss (nee Kirchberg) had been a highly-acclaimed coloratura soprano. More recently she taught singing and was a judge for The International Vocal Competitions of The National Arts Club, The Liederkranz Foundation, and The National Federation of Music Clubs. A Memorial Service will be held 6 P.M., Thursday, January 24th at Holy Trinity Lutheran Church, 65th Street and Central Park West in Manhattan.
