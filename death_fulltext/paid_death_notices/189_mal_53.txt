
        ANNENBERG-Walter H. The Chapin School Community is deeply saddened by the death of Walter H. Annenberg, a Chapin grandfather and our school's friend and most generous benefactor. His many gifts to us include our spacious and beautiful library multimedia center, named in his honor. We extend our profound sympathy to his wife Leonore Annenberg, his step-daughter Elizabeth Kabler, Chapin Trustee, her daughter Elizabeth, Class of 2001 and the entire Annenberg family. Ella M. Foshay, Pres. of the Board of Trustees Cera B. Robbins, Trustee Emerita Sandra J. Theunick, Head of School
        ANNENBERG-Walter. The Peddie School is deeply saddened by the death of Walter H. Annenberg, our beloved alumnus, benefactor and friend. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Lee, the Annenberg family, and our friends at the Annenberg Foundation. His loyalty and generosity to his alma mater were unparalleled. Countless Peddie students are grateful for the education he made possible. Finn M. W. Caspersen Chairman of the Board John Green, Head of School The Peddie Community
        ANNENBERG-Walter. The Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art, Cornell University, mourns the passing of Walter Annenberg, a major figure in American philanthropy. His generosity has transformed many institutions, including ours, and we shall always be deeply grateful to him. We extend our sympathy to his family.
