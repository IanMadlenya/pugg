
        SHAPIRO-Arnold B. Of Mamaroneck, NY on May 10, 2002. He was born on May 30, 1917 in Waterbury, Connecticut to Isaac Aaron and Hattie (nee Goldberg) Shapiro. He is survived by his loving wife Claire of 48 years, his cherished daughter Meryl Ann Rogers (husband Michael) of Valley Stream and his adored grandson Benjamin. He was also the devoted brother of Lauretta Schneiderman and the late Evelyn Reinish, David & Myron. Funeral service was held on May 13, 2002 at the Zion Memorial Chapel of Westchester, NY, 914-381-1809.
