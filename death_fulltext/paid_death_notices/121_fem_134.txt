
        STANG-Bertha Tilzer, a native of Staten Island, died of cancer on Feb. 19, 1997 at Montefiore Hospital in the Bronx. She was 90. Mrs. Stang's late brother was Ira L. Tilzer. Mrs. Stang is survived by her nieces Louise Tilzer Greenfield of Manhattan, Leslie Tilzer Krasny of San Anselmo, CA., and three great-nieces, Lauren and Alexa Krasny and Jessica Greenfield. The funeral was held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, NYC on Feb. 20.
