
        CLARKE--Beaufort James Barron. The Empire State Pride Agenda Board and Staff mourns the loss of our Board member, supporter and good friend, Beau Clarke, who passed away April 29, 2007. Our sincere condolences go to his family and many close friends. Beau was well known for his philanthropy to the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender community. His dedication, passion, energy and friendship will be greatly missed.
