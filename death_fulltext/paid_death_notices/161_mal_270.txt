
        McGANN-Dr. Charles James. Died at his home early morning June 21, 2000 at age 69. A dedicated psychoanalyst, he cared deeply for his patients, friends and family. He lived strongly committed to his principles. His three children, Nadine, John and Vanessa and his wife Helen grieve for him. McGANN-Charles James. The members of the New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of their longtime colleague, Dr. Charles James McGann (Jim). He was dedicated to psychoanalysis and devoted to the Society. We extend our condolences to his wife, Mrs. Helen McGann, and to his children, Nadine, John and Vanessa. He will be deeply missed. Friends and family will be receiving at 137 Duane St., Apt. 5D, from 4-8 PM, on Friday, June 23, 2000. Contributions can be sent to the James Mara Center for Lung Disease, St. Lukes-Roosevelt Hospital Center, 1000 10th Ave., New York, New York 10019. Eslee Samberg, M.D. President, Institute Albert M. Sax, M.D. President, Society
