
        DEIRO-Pietro (Lee), Jr. On June 19, 1999, at age 85. Founder of Pietro Deiro Publications. He pioneered and convinced popular songwriters to compose easy arrangements for the accordion. He was responsible for the development of the accordionist Tops in Pops, as well as composing and publishing accordion music himself. He was the President of the American Accordionist Association. Beloved husband of the late Ines Deiro. Loving father of Sandra L. Cattani and the late Peter Deiro. Devoted grandfather of Andrea, Christopher and Jessica. Dear brother of Blanche Pancotti. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Monday, June 21, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass at St. Thomas More Church, 65 East 89 St., Tuesday, 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Peter Deiro Scholarship Fund at the Rice Catholic School.
