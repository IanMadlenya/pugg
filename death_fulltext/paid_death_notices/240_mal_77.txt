        $(6$)KRESTON--Martin H., 75, died January 5th of lung cancer. Adored husband of Judie, and an extraordinarily loving father of Rebecca and Mark, and step-father to Russell, Mark and Douglas; father-inlaw to Kim, Andrew, Lisa, Elle and Meryl; grandfather of Ryan, Connor, Caroline, Zachary, Isabella, Matthew, Michael, Rachel, Tyler and dear brother of Joyce. He had a long and illustrious career in the advertising world, most notably at Doyle Dane Bernbach and at USA Today. He will be forever remembered as a courageous, elegant man whose love of life and all things New York was equaled only by his great love of family. His warmth, charm, integrity and rich appetite for learning enriched the lives of all he touched. The world is a lesser place without him. Funeral Services Sunday, January 7th, 11:45 am at The Riverside, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions may be made to the James F. Holland Research Fund c/o Dr. James Holland, Mt. Sinai Hospital, 1 Gustave Levy Pl., Box 1129, NYC ,10029.
