
        BUCK-Polly Stone, widow of Yale Provost Norman S. Buck, died on March 23 at age 102. She was a longtime resident of New Haven, CT, and served for 17 years as the Master's wife of Yale's Branford College. She was the author of four books and the recipient of the Yale Medal and the Agnes Scott College Outstanding Alumna Award. She is survived by two daughters, three grandsons and one great-granddaughter. Private committal at a later date.
