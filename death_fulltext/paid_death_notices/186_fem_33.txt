
        SPARKS-Lois V. President of Sparks Systems, died June 26, 2002, of cancer. Her upbeat attitude, contagious enthusiasm, and gift for friendship were all legendary. Her determined spirit as a nine year cancer survivor inspired many patients and friends around the world. Survived by parents, Caroline and Chester Sparks, sister, Caroline, nephews, Bill and Chris Stanfield, niece, Jeanne Crago, great nephews, David and Andrew, and great niece, Emily. Memorial Service, All Angels Church, 251 West 80 St, NYC, July 8th at 4 PM. Memorials to Adopt A Bench, Central Park Conservancy, Lois Sparks Fund.
