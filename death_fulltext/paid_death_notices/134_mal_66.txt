
        ZUPA-Vito M. On March 6, 1998. Devoted father of Victor. Loving father-in-law of Mary. Dear grandfather of Evelyn and Paul Shyposh, Ann and Mary Beth. Also surviving are many family members and his longtime beloved friend Janina Haba. Mass of Christian Burial was celebrated today at 10 A.M., at Our Lady of Solace Church and entombment followed at St. Raymond's Cemetery. The family has requested that donations be made in the memory of Vito to Visiting Nurse Services of New York, 981 Morris Park Avenue, Bronx, New York 10462.
