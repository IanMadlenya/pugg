
        RYAN-Thomas Alfred, Jr., 88. Died April 1, 2002. A native New Yorker. Former president of William L. Marshall, Ltd. Expert in exotic veneers, lifelong baseball fan, fought Alzheimer's for thirteen years. Tom is survived by his beloved wife Claire, three great children and eight wonderful grandchildren. A Memorial Mass was held April 6th at Our Lady of the Pillar in Half Moon Bay, CA.
