
        HOLMES--Brother Edmund, O.S.F. Died on Friday, December 9, 2005. Brother was born on August 11, 1908. He was a Fransciscan Brother of Brooklyn from 1929 until his death. Brother was a professor at St. Francis College, Brooklyn Heights, NY, for many years and a counselor at Camp Albernia in Centerport, NY, for over 50 years. Brother Edmund is survived by his sister Mary Bickford, brother Michael Holmes, three nieces, Barbara Tuck of Great Neck, NY, Virginia Fahey of Syosset, NY, and Maureen Lawrence. Wake will be held at St. Francis Monastery at Mount Albernia, 103 Prospect Rd., Centerport, NY, Monday, December 12, and Tuesday, December 13, from 2-4 and 7-9 pm. A Mass of Christian Burial will be offered on Wednesday, December 14, 10:30 am, at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs R.C. Church, Prospect Rd, Centerport, NY. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Wesbury, NY.
