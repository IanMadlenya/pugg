
        ZAKARIA--Rafiq. Scholar, politician, philanthropist, humanist and lover of life. The American friends and admirers of Rafiq Zakaria mourn the passing of a towering figure and send their condolences to his beloved wife Fatma, his children Tasnim, Mansour, Arshad, and Fareed, and their families. From Mumbai to Aurangabad to Delhi to London to New York and beyond, his example will shine for all who knew him or were touched by his wisdom, humanity, and great works.
