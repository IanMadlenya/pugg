
        SHAPIRO--Ida, 93, formerly of Beechhurst, Queens. Died on June 13. A graduate of Hunter College, she retired in 1976 after a career as teacher and administrator at Bayside High School and Martin Van Buren High School. Pre-deceased by husband Irwin D. Shapiro, she is survived by sons Jonathan of West Newton, MA. and Ethan of Rockaway, N.J., brother Leon Smith of Monroe Township, N.J., three grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
