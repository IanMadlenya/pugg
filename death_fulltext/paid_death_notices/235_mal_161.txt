
        NOLAN--Joseph of New Canaan, CT, Charlestown RI, and Key Largo, FL, died on August 14th, 2006 of prostate cancer. He was 60 years old. Joe was born in Lewisburg, PA in 1946. He graduated from Villanova University in 1968 and received his MBA from Boston College in 1973. Joe served in the United States National Guard from 1968-1974. A resident of Chappaqua, NY for 25 years, he moved to New Canaan in 2000. Joe worked for Pfizer Inc. for 33 years. He held many positions in marketing and sales. He retired in April 2006 as Vice President of Sales Operations, U.S. Pharmaceuticals. He was predeceased by his parents, Martin J. and Mary J. Nolan and his sister, Sally Nolan Schwarz. Joe is survived by his wife of 36 years, Kathleen Lane Nolan of New Canaan and his daughters Elizabeth Anne Nolan of Boston, MA and Katherine Nolan Joyce of Jamaica, VT. Also surviving are his brothers Richard Nolan of Claremont, NH and his sister, Marilyn Nolan Baker of Falls Church, VA. A mass of Christian burial will be celebrated at St. Aloysius Church, New Canaan on Friday, August 18, 2006 at 10:00 am. The burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Prostate Cancer Diagnostic Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021 or to Norwalk Hospital, 24 Stevens Street, Norwalk, CT 06850.
