
        FRIEDHOFF-Arnold J., 77, died Wednesday. Son of the late Abraham and Stella Beerman Friedhoff. Beloved husband of 55 years to Frances Wolfe Friedhoff. Devoted father of Lawrence T. Friedhoff and his wife Beth, Nancy Friedhoff Walsh and her husband Dan and Richard M. Friedhoff. Loving brother of Hulda Friedhoff Edelstein and her husband Robert. Cherished grandfather of Emily, Jane and Sarah Friedhoff. Uncle of Michael Wolfe, Teri Edelstein and Andrew Blair. Memorial service Sunday, February 25, 4PM at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Family and friends may call from 3-4PM. Memorial contributions may be made to The Stella and Abraham Friedhoff Foundation.
        FRIEDHOFF-Arnold, M.D. The members of the NYU-Bellevue Psychiatric Society mourn the loss of their esteemed colleague and offer deepest condolences to his family. Dr. Friedhoff was a towering figure in the field of psychiatric research and made lasting contributions of immeasurable help to those suffering from mental illness. He was a dedicated psychiatrist who will be missed by all those privileged to have been his patients, friends and colleagues. Benjamin J. Sadock, M.D., President
