
        ALPERT-Betty Langberg. On October 17, 1999 at age 94. Beloved wife of the late Harry Alpert. She is survived by her children Deborah and Martin Fishbein of Philadelphia, PA., her nieces and nephews Paula and Ephraim Biran, Arnold and Dagnija Langberg plus many great and great greats. Mrs. Alpert was an executive of the Lion Ribbon Company of NYC and NJ from 1920 to 1985. She will be remembered by all who knew her and worked with her as a caring and giving person, always cheerful and positive. Graveside services Wednesday 11:00 AM at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Those desiring transportation contact Frank E. Campbell at 212288-3500. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Children's Defense Fund, 25 E Street, N.W., Washington, DC 20001.
