
        KONHEIM -- Estelle (nee Mitchell). 82, passed away at her home after a brief illness on June 17 with her family by her side. Beloved wife to the late Ralph Konheim and sister of the late Jean Mitchell. Estelle was the cherished mother of Patricia Heffron, Nancy Greene and Ann Polansky. Loving mother-in-law to Victor Greene and Robert Polansky. She was adored by her grandchildren Samuel, Jerome, Joshua, Christopher, Jill, Tom, Pamela and Shannon. Great grandmother to their four children Nicholas, Jack, Estelle and Henry. Estelle was also devoted to her sister-in-law Doris Rubin and her late husband Harry and the late Jean and Norton Tannenbaum. Estelle was recognized by her many friends and family members for her grace, elegance and style. Estelle will be mourned by all who knew her and her memory will remain forever in our hearts. Services Wednesday, June 18, 11:30 AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65 St.
        KONHEIM--Estelle. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Estelle Konheim, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Dr. David M. Posner, Senior Rabbi Dr. Ronald B.
