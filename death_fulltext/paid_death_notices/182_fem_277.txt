
        ASCH-Florence M. On Wednesday, March 27th, 2002, at the age of 92, at The Quadrangle Health Center in Haverford, PA. Wife of the late Solomon E. Asch, and mother of the late Peter Asch. She is survived by her grandchildren Eric J. Asch of Los Angeles, CA, David Asch and Laurie Adler Asch of Silver Spring, MD, great-grandson Peter Adler Asch of Silver Spring, and daughter-in-law Rita Asch of Princeton, NJ. Born in New York City, she lived for many years in Swarthmore, PA, and Princeton, New Jersey, before moving to The Quadrangle Retirement Community in 1990. Service to be held in the near future.
