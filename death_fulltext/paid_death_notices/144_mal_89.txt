
        NEUFELD-Abraham. Of Hewlett Harbor, New York, veteran of World War II, active in many humanitarian causes. President of LI Committee of Soviet Jewry. Beloved husband of Helen, loving father of Jeffrey, Pam, and Robert. Cherished grandfather. We will miss you. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI. 11:30 AM Wednesday.
        NEUFELD-Abraham. Five Towns Jewish Council records the untimely death of our esteemed and illustrious past president Abe Neufeld. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Lester Henner, President Gail Blaustein, Bd Chairman
        NEUFELD-Abraham. The Hewlett-East Rockaway Jewish Centre mourns the loss of its esteemed member, Abraham Neufeld, and extends heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Helen, and the entire family. Rabbi Stanley Platek Dr. Norman Doctor, Pres.
