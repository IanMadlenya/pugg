
        GRIMM--Elaine, PhD. The William Alanson White Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of Elaine Grimm, PhD. An esteemed graduate, member of the Society Board, faculty member and a treasured colleague. Known for her generosity of spirit and for her sage, direct and straightforward manner, Elaine served successfully in many arenas with plucky vitality. She was much valued and will be sorely missed. Joerg Bose, MD, Institute Director Gurmeet Kanwal, MD Society President
