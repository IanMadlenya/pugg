
        FOX--Regina Reiss, 94, of Lakewood, NJ and West Palm Beach, FL, died January 14 in West Palm and interred January 16 in Lakewood. Born in Tarnapol, Ukraine, she came to Brooklyn, in 1937, moved to Lakewood in 1953 and retired to Florida. Predeceased by beloved husband Murray and devoted son Chester, sons Stanley, Fred, and grandchildren: Gregory, Jeremy, Joshua, and Emily survive her. Her love, optimism, energy and generosity are profoundly missed. Contributions to Hadassah, 50 W 58th St, NY, NY 10019.
