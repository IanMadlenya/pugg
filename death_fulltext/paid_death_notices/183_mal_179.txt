
        TUSTIN-Whitney. Died February 18, 2002, age 90, in Denver, CO. Formerly of Muttontown and Jericho, L.I. Survived by his beloved wife, Gilda; three stepchildren: Susan Iadone and Karen Shapiro of NYC and Michael Shapiro of Houston, TX; & a brother, Wayne, of Santa Barbara. Principal oboe, NYC Opera, 1949-1966. Also held principal positions in other orchestras throughout the United States. He will be remembered by his colleagues across the country, his friends, and by the many people who studied oboe with him.
