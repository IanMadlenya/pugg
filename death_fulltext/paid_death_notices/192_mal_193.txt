
        DWORKEN-Rabbi Steven. We mourn the untimely death of our colleague and friend, devoted Executive Vice President of the Rabbinical Council of America, whose energies and skills were dedicated to bringing benefit and caring to rabbis and their families, whose passion was teaching Torah and who labored for klal Yisrael. We extend our condolences to his loving wife, Susan, to his family, and to all who were touched by his caring and friendship. The memory of his life is a blessing for his life was a blessing. Rabbi Reuven Hammer, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Executive Vice President The Rabbinical Assembly
        DWORKEN-Rabbi Steven. Maimonides School, Brookline, MA, mourns the tragic passing of its distinguished graduate, Rabbi Steven Dworken, Executive Vice President, Rabbinical Council of America. Rabbi Dworken's tireless leadership and personal warmth will serve as an inspiration for generations of our students. May his family be comforted together with the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Bernard Kosowsky, Bd Ch Abraham Levovitz, Pres.
