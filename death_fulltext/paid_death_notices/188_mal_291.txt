
        BACHRACH-Robert Lee. On Friday, September 20, 2002, after a long illness, at age 75. Beloved husband of Marjorie Baker Bachrach. Adored father of Jeff and the late Clifford Lee Bachrach and father-in-law of Stacey Roth Bachrach. Devoted brother of John D. Bachrach and Ellin B. Gordon. Also, adored grandfather of Adam and Eric and devoted brother-inlaw of Deborah and Robert W. Bloch, Baron J. Gordon and Mary Max Bachrach. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to East Hill Woods Memorial Fund, 611 East Hill Road, Southbury, CT. 06488, or a charity of your choice.
