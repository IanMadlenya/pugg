
        HIRSCHFELD--Zipora. The entire St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital Center (SLR) family joins with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Zipora Hirschfeld, beloved mother of our dear friend and colleague, Elie Hirschfeld, a member of the Continuum Board of Trustees. The hospital is forever grateful to Mrs. Hirschfeld and the entire Hirschfeld family for its generosity and support over many years. To Elie, the entire Hirschfeld family, and all of his mother's loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Boards of Trustees Continuum and SLR Stanley Brezenoff President and CEO Continuum Richard F. Daines, MD President and CEO SLR
