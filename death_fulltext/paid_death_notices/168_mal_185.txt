
        ORSHAN-H. Allen, Dr. Age 75, will be buried in a private ceremony in Ft. Myers, Florida. He passed away on January 7, 2001 after a long struggle with Parkinson's Disease. After a fulfilling career in public education, he retired in 1979 as a New York City high school principal and moved to Shokan, NY. A World War II veteran, he is survived by his wife Heidi. His daughters Malka and Wendy, of New York City and his sister Sybil Lee of Chicago. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research, 800-708-7644, or Hope Hospice Care, 941-482-4673.
