
        NAVARRETE -- Ignacio, 92, died at home in Oakland, CA. Born in Havana, Cuba, formerly of Staten Island and Greenwich Village. Studied at Columbia University School of Architecture. Worked for the Office of Franciscan Art and Architecture, and for the Port Authority. A life centered on his family and his profession. Survived by Irma, wife of 50 years, daughter Christine and son Ignacio, daughter-in-law Hester, and grandsons Andy and Paul. Memorial donations to Belen High School in Miami, FL, or to the Avery Library at Columbia. Memorial service in New York to be announced. For details, call (510) 642-2108.
