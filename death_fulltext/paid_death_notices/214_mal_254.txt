
        APPLEBAUM -- Robert W. (Rob). Of New York and Easthampton died on November 25th, 2004 at 52. Father to Theo, 2, and Riley, 4 months. Husband to Lei, son of Eleanor Applebaum and the late Herbert Applebaum, brother to Janet and Walter Drucker. He is also survived by a loving, extended family. In his lifetime, he had been a mediator, Real Estate Manager, writer, teacher and photographer. He enjoyed the friendships of many, gave generously and took in heartily. His ebullience and magnitism, his zest for life will stay with those close to him. He will continue to live on in the hearts of family and friends.
