
        WHITE--Edith Seema. Died April 29, 2006, in Brooklyn, NY. She had just recently celebrated her 90th birthday. She was the loving mother of Jonathan and Edwina White, loving sister of Gertrude Spector, loving aunt of Lori Spector and Phoebe Sue Collina, loving grandmother of Jennifer and Sarah White, loving mother - in - law of Diane Klein-White, and greataunt of Kris Rutman and Joseph Collina. She will be deeply missed by her family, extended family and many friends. Her kindness and loving nature will be with us for all of our lives.
