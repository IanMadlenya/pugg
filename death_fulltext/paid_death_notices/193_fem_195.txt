
        BAM-Sheila Catherine, 48. Of Stamford, CT, passed away peacefully on Sunday, February 23, 2003, surrounded by family and friends. She is survived by her husband, Kenneth Kosakoff, their daughter, April, her parents, Margaret and Foster, her siblings, Carol and Eric and so many others whom she touched with her strong and giving heart. We will miss her dearly. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to The Stamford Hospital, in her name.
