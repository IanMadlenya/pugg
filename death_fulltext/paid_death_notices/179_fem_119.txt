
        FLANAGAN-Rose Kosoff. Died on December 16, 2001. Loving wife of the late Samuel Kosoff and the late George Flanagan. Beloved mother of Lydia Schwarz (Joel), Susan Kosoff (Paul), David Kosoff (Sheila); devoted grandmother of Melissa, Jeffrey, Mark, Jordan, Zora, and Langston, and proud great-grandmother of Samantha and Adam. She will be deeply missed by her loving family and friends and always remembered for her smile, style and spirit. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cancer Care Inc., 275 7th Ave., New York, New York 10001.
