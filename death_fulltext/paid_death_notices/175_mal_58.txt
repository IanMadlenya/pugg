
        KARETZKY-Harry. Former First Deputy Director of the Office of Municipal Labor Relations of New York City, passed away August 5, 2001. Predeceased by his wife Lillian. He is survived by his children Elisabeth Karetzky and Stephen Karetzky and his grandchildren Benjamin F. Klein and Nikolai A. Klein. Devoted public servant, good husband, excellent father, loving grandfather, he will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Services were held at Sinai Chapels.
