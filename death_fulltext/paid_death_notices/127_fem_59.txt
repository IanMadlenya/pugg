
        LOPATA-Frances. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of our dear friend and Benefactor, who established an academic chair in liver disease research in memory of her late husband, Herman. She leaves a legacy of compassion and caring that exemplifies the true spirit of humanitarianism that is at the heart of our institution. To her children, Peggy, Roy and Edward, and to the entire family, we offer our heartfelt sympathy. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers, Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura, Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
