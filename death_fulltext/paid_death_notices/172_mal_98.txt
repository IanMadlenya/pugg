
        KRESSEL-Herman A. (Lefty). 85. Died on May 5, 2001 in Buffalo, N.Y., his home after many years in Hewlett, N.Y. and St. Petersburg, FL. Mr. Kressel was born in NYC, 1915 and had a BS degree from Brooklyn College and a Master's Degree from NYU. He was a Master Plumber who became a vocational high school teacher in NYC and later, entered school administration and became original Director of the NYC Manpower Development and Training Program. He is predeceased by his wife Louise Alexander Kressel and is survived by his daughters Alexandra K. Sussman, Esquire, Patricia Duffner, MD, and her husband John, two grandchildren Michael and Jeremy Sussman, and his sister Frances Smith. He will be missed.
