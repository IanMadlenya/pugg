
        ANDERSON-Paul J. Physician, journal editor, educator. Died peacefully at home in New York on Tuesday August 11, 1998 after a brief battle with cancer. His energy, intelligence, creativity, charm and wit will be remembered by his children and the friends, colleagues, students and countless others whose lives he touched. Survived by sons Jon, Colin and Kjell, and daughter Rebecca, all of New York. Donations in his memory may be made to the World Monuments Fund, 949 Park Av, NY, NY 10028. A memorial service is planned for September in New York City, notice forthcoming.
