
        GREENWALD - Sarah Kate (Vicki), died March 6 after a long battle with breast cancer. Service on Sunday at 10 A.M., in the main santuary of Park Avenue Synagogue, 87 St. and Madison Avenue. Interment at Sharon Gardens. Family and friends are welcome at Debby's house after service and welcome at Wendy's on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday.
        GREENWALD-Vicki. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband, Dr. Stanley Greenwald, and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Alan Levine, Ch. of the Bd. Robert P. Antler, President
