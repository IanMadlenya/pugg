
        SCHIFFER-Mortimer. Age 86. On December 19, 1998. A pioneer of play group psychotherapy for children for the Jewish Board of Guardians and NYC Board of Education. Survived by his wife Esther, 2 sons, Charles Schiffer, M.D., Bloomfield, MI and Paul Schiffer, Los Angeles, and 3 grandchildren. Memorial service January 24, Brooklyn Ethical Culture Society, 53 Prospect Park West, 2:30 PM.
