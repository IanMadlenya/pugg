
        PECKHAM-Janet G., Chairman and C.E.O. of Peckham Industries of White Plains, N.Y., died on Sunday, March 21, 1999 at her home in Chappaqua, N.Y. An active civil leader in the community for over thirty years, a life-long resident of Westchester County, Mrs. Peckkam was the widow of John S. Peckham. She is survived by her three children, daughter Katherine Moreau and sons John and Douglas Peckham, a sister Joan MacArthur and eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Presbyterian Church of Mount Kisco at 4:00 pm on Wednesday, March 24th. The family has requested that donations be made to The Sloane Kettering Memorial Cancer Research Fund or the Westchester Council for the Arts in lieu of flowers.
        PECKHAM-Janet G. The Westchester Community Foundation extends deepest sympathies to the family of our Board Member, Janet G. Peckham. Janet was admired by all for her intelligence, graciousness and commitment to the community. We are greatly saddened and will miss her.
