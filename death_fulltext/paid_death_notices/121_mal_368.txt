
        KRUG-Paul L., 83, of Boynton Beach, Florida, passed away on February 25, 1997. Mr. Krug was an executive with the Krug Baking Company of New York and H.D. Catty Company in Connecticut. He was a graduate of Canterbury School, New Milford, CT and Yale University 1936. Mr. Krug is survived by his beloved wife, Ella Galvin Krug; loving father of Pamela K. Boalt, Karen Krug, Sueanne Miller, Judith McLean, Paul Jr., John and Peter; fifteen grandchildren and five great grandchildren. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Maryknoll Fathers, Maryknoll, NY 10545 or to Alzheimer's Research.
