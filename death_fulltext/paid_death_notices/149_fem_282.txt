
        KOTLUS-Thelma (''Teddi''). 81, of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, on June 25. She was greatly admired by everyone who knew her. She was the wife of the late Jacob Kotlus. Beloved mother of Susan & Allan Retzky, and Ellen Kotlus & Max Gates. Grandmother of Andrea, Deborah & Bob Shaul, Maia & Aaron Kotlus-Gates.
