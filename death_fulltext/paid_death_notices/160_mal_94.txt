
        ENGEL-Joseph G. Of Princeton, New Jersey. Died peacefully on May 7, 2000 at age 83 after a long illness. Survived by his wife of 52 years, Jane D. Engel of Princeton, NJ. Also survived by his sons, William V. Engel of Basking Ridge, NJ, Richard F. Engel of West Windsor, NJ, and Robert J. Engel of Wayland, MA, his daughters-inlaw, Brenda F. Engel and Patricia H. Engel, his five grandchildren, John, Robert, Bill, Betsy, and Libby, his brother Edward G. Engel and his sister, Mary E. Flannery. Funeral services and interment private. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, May 21, 2000 at 1:30 P.M. at The Pingry School, Martinsville Road, Bernards Township, NJ. Memorial donations may be made to Alzheimer's Association, Attention: Acknowledgements Dept., Suite 1000, 919 N. Michigan Ave., Chicago, IL 60611 or The Pingry School, P.O. Box 366, Martinsville, N.J. 08836. For information August F. Schmidt Memorial Funeral Home, Elizabeth, New Jersey.
