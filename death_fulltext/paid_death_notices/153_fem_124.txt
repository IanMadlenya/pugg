
        WHEELOCK-Elizabeth T., died October 12 at her home in New Canaan, Conn. Widow of Morgan Dix Wheelock, she was also predeceased by her first husband, J. Foster Bowers, and second husband, J. Paul Stabler, both of whom died of cancer. She is survived by two daughters, Beverly Bowers Jennings and May Bowers Morris; four grandchildren, Justine Gregg Barkstrom, Kimberly A. Jennings, Cary L. Jennings and Dr. David Gregg IV. Also a stepdaughter, Eileen S. Buckley; two step-grandchildren, Linda and Mark Buckley; one great-grandson, Jackson W. Barkstrom; and one brother, Major General Charles S. Thompson. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM Friday, October 15, 1999 at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, New Canaan. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, East 68th St., New York, NY 10021 or to St. Mark's Episcopal Church, Oenoke Ridge, New Canaan, CT. 06840.
