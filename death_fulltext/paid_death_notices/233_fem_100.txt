
        FRANC--Helen M. Died peacefully on June 14 at the age of 98. Worked for the Museum of Modern Art as an Editorial Assistant until her reitrement in 1972. Published extensively in the art world, and is best known for ''An Invitation to See'' which is still used as a guide to the works of MOMA. She had numerous philanthropic interests and volunteered her time to further human rights and the welfare of children. She is survived by her nieces, Anne Morgan, and Marie Ruth Willcox and their children and grandchildren.
