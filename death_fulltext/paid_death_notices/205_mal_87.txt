
        GILLIES--James Perry, Jr. Age 87, of Fairfield, CT. and Wellfleet, MA, retired President of Warnaco International, died Monday, February 2, 2004. Graduate of Yale University, Class of 1938. Survived by his wife of 63 years, Aline Paige Cornwall Gillies, his children, Paige C. Gillies, Gail G. Smith, J. Perry Gillies and his wife Valerie, and five grandchildren. A memorial service Wednesday, February 11th, 2PM, Greenfield Hill Congregational Church, Fairfield, CT. Inquiries: The Spear Funeral Home of Fairfield, www.spearfuneralhome.com
