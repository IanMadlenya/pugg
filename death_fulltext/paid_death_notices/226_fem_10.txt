
        BOURNE--Beebe, on November 1, 2005. Beloved daughter of the late Saul and the late Bonnie Bourne. Survived by her loving Marco Berrocal. She was a renowned independent music publisher of Bourne Company. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St, Thursday, November 3, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Service Friday, November 4, 2PM at St. Jean Baptiste Church, 184 E. 76 St. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Lighthouse International Music School, Sol & Lillian Goldman Building, 111 East 59th Street, New York, New York 10022.
        BOURNE--Beebe. The New York Chapter of the Association of Independent Music Publishers mourns the passing of our Executive Director, Ms. Beebe Bourne. Her dedicated leadership to our Board and to the members of our organization was inspirational to us all. She set a high standard that we will find difficult to follow. The entire music publishing community will surely miss her knowledge of and devotion to our industry. Directors, New York Chapter AIMP
        BOURNE--Beebe. We are heartbroken to have lost a dear friend and treasured colleague. Beebe was a mentor, leader, and lovable spirit with a tremendous heart. She will be missed. Jennifer Bilfield, President Boosey & Hawkes
