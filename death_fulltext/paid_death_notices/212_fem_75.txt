
        SHELKIN--Lisa B., 43, of Old Tappan, NJ, formerly Demarast, NJ on Wednesday, September 8, 2004. She is survived by her husband Philip Lenger, her son Sam, her mother Muriel, grandmother Tillie, her two brothers Scott and Chris, and her three nieces. She had been Managing Editor of ''Self,'' ''Woman,'' ''Vogue,'' and ''Ladies Home Journal.'' She had owned Matilda's Garden in Nyack, NY. Donations may be made in her memory to the Brain Tumor Center at Duke, DUMC, Box 3624, Durham, NC 27710.
