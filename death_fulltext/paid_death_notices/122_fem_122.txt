
        KLABER-Linda. On March 15, in Miami, FL, at age 57. She lived in Boca Raton, FL. Ms. Klaber worked in advertising for over 30 years. Before moving to Florida, she lived in California for over 10 years, where she leaves many friends. She was born in Brooklyn, NY, and was the only child of Frederick and Shirley Klaber. Ms. Klaber is survived by her daughter Lisa Fegan and her grandchildren Justin and Allison, as well as many other relatives and friends. Memorial services to be held at a later date.
