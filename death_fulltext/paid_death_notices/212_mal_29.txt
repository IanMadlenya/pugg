
        COHEN--James. Of Richmond, VA. Born January 26, 1916. Husband to the late Ruth Newman Cohen. Father to Karl and Penny Cohen, Marcia Penn and Leivy Smolar, Jeffrey and Linda Cohen. Blessed with 18 adoring grandchildren and 14 loving great-grandchildren. Along with his late brothers, Ben and David Cohen, and late sister Stella Tobin, founded retail chain John's Bargain Stores. In his role as Executive VP he helped steer the retail giant to success in the 1940's-1960's. He'll long be remembered for his generosity, good humor and love of all children. In lieu of remembrances, the family asks that donations be made to Bon Scoures Hospice, 8580 Magellan Parkway, Richmond, VA 23227. Services today, 1 PM, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY.
