
        FRUCHTMAN-Arthur, age 66. Of Montville, N.J. Succumbed after his courageous 19 month battle with leukemia. He was an inspiration to all and his great sense of humor and kindness will always be remembered and cherished. Beloved husband of Paula (nee Ehrlich). Beloved father of Beth (Jess) Rosenthal, Dr. William (Beth), and Jason (Ellen). Beloved sonin-law of Sylvia Ehrlich. Adored grandfather to Jacob, Jonah, Julia and Carly. Much loved brother of Irene (Howard) Stein, Dr. Bernard (Hansi) and pre-deceased Carl (Janet Moss). For eight years prior to his retirement, he served as President, General Counsel and Secretary of Ingersoll Dresser Pump Company. He was a former member of the Board of Directors of the Lake Hiawatha Jewish Center, as well as a member of the Pine Brook Jewish Center in Pine Brook, N.J. Memorial service will be held at Bernheim Apter Kreitzman Funeral Home, 68 Old Short Hills Road, Livingston, N.J. 07039 on Thursday morning January 2, 2003 at 10:30AM. Interment will be at Mount Hebron Cemetery. Donations may be made in his honor to Dr. Mark Weiss Research Fund, Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York NY 10021 or a charity of choice.
