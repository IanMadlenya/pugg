
        KATZ-Solomon. Died August 9, 2001 at Mount Sinai Hospital at the age of 86 after a long illness. Beloved father of Louise and Richard. Also survived by his brother Phillip & his wife Frances. He worked for the IRS 57 years and was president of the Manhattan Chapter of the IRS Employee Union. His kindness will be missed. Services Friday, August 10, 1 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in his memory may be made to Cancer Care.
