
        SHAPIRO-Marjorie Kemble. She passed away peacefully on November 8, 2002, after a courageous struggle with brain cancer. Marjorie was the loving wife of the late Jerome Shapiro and a loving mother to Jeffrey, Jill, and E. Noach (Eric). She was a proud grandmother to Daniel, Laurie, Samuel, Eliana, Ziva, and Eran. We will all miss her. She was a good friend to many and a joy to those around her, radiating her love of music and life. A Memorial Service will be held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, New York, New York on Sunday, November 17th at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers please donate to the American Brain Tumor Association, 2720 River Rd, Des Plaines, IL 60018.
