
        MILLER--Gail L., of Mamaroneck, New York, died peacefully in Greenwich Hospital on May 15th, 2005, after a struggle with emphysema. Loving mother of Matthew Miller, beloved mother-in-law of Marnie Miller, adoring grandmother of Lindsay, Katie, and Isabelle. She was Director of Nursing, Department of Nuclear Medicine, at Montefiore Center for over twenty-three years. A private burial took place at The Greenwood Union Cemetery in Rye, New York, on Wednesday, May 18. A Memorial Service will take place Sunday, May 22nd, at 4 PM, at St. John's Episcopal Church, 4 Fountain Square, Larchmont, New York. In lieu of flowers, please send a donation made out to: The Children's Hospital at Montefiore and sent to: The Children's Hospital at Montefiore, attn: Development Office/Nancy Riedel, 111 East 210th Street, Bronx, New York 10467.
