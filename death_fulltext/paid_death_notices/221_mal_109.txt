
        TEDORI--Fred Sr., 78, died Sunday, May 30, 2005 in Dana Point, California after fighting valiantly with Parkinson's disease and cancer. Born in New York City on December 27, 1927, he was the son of Italian immigrants and an example of the American dream. Despite a challenging childhood, he graduated valedictorian from Stuyvesant High School in New York City and then went on to serve his country in World War II. After the war, he put himself through college at Pace University and embarked upon a successful career. He is survived by his loving wife Ronette and their six children, Fred Tedori, Jr. and wife Robin, Monica Tedori, Linda Tedor Whitman and husband Michael, Letetia Tedori Callinan and husband James; Joseph Tedori; and Randy Tedori Sanson and husband Michael. He will be remembered always by his eighteen grandchildren. He is also survived by his brothers and sisters, Anna Gattulo, Frank Tedori, Elizabeth Montane, and Albert Tedori. A celebration of his life was held June 1, 2005 at eleven thirty A.M. at St. Edward the Confessor Catholic Church, Dana Point, CA.
