
        STEVENSON-John R. The Council on Ocean Law notes with sadness the death of its longtime Board Member, John R. Stevenson, on October 25, in Fort Worth, Texas. Ambassador Stevenson was the first Chairman of the American delegation to The Law of the Sea Conference and will be missed by professional colleagues in that endeavor around the world who respected his leadership and dedication. We extend our sympathy to his wife, Ruth, and to his children and grandchildren. Elliot L. Richardson, Chairman Christian A. Herter, Jr. Margaret L. Tomlinson, Vice-Chairmen
