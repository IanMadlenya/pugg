
        TYRRELL-Annabel K. Died on Wednesday, April 15, at Oysterponds Manor, Orient, NY. She was born in 1911 to Takeji and Mary Kusanobu in New York City. A 1984 graduate of Smith College, she also studied at the Sorbonne in Paris. Before joining the staff of the United Nations Secretariat she was an editor at Oxford University Press, New York. She married Arthur Clifford Tyrrell in 1953. They lived in New York City, except for four years in Sydney, Australia, in the mid-1960's when Arthur represented the United Nations there. After his retirement they moved to Orient, New York. Arthur died in 1988. A memorial service in Orient will be announced later.
