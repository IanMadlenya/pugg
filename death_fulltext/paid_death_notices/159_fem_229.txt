
        HUBBARD-Mildred W. of Riverhead, on April 26, 2000, age 94. Loving wife of the late Seth A. Hubbard. Cherished sister of Peggy Snow, of Waterford, Conn. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. On June 21, 1995, on her 90th Birthday, the Riverhead Bridge Club proclaimed this day to be Mildred Hubbard Day. Mildred was a member of the Riverhead Bridge Club for 40 years. Monies raised by the Bridge Club were donated to Central Suffolk Hospital. She was also an Officer of the Hospital Auxillary, Member of the Riverhead Garden Club and Riverhead Women's Club. Reposing Reginald H. Tuthill Funeral Home, Inc. 406 East Main St., Riverhead, NY, Friday, April 28, 2000, 2-4 & 7-9 p.m. Celebration of the Liturgy on Saturday, April 29, 2000, 9:30 a.m. St John the Evangelist R.C. Church, Riverhead, NY. Interment to follow in St. John's Cemetery, Riverhead, NY. Contributions may be made to Central Suffolk Hospital or The Hubbard Park.
