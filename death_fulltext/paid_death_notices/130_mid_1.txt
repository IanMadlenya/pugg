        LITTLE-John Watson II. The Society of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center expresses its sincerest sympathy to our dear colleague, Jean Remmel Little, Past President of The Society, upon the death of her husband, John Watson Little II. Both Mr. and Mrs. Little have been staunch friends and supporters of the critical work of Memorial Sloan-Kettering. He will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Mrs. Coleman P. Burke President The Society of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
