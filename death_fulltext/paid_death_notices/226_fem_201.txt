
        SULZBERGER--Maye (nee Dorfman). Died Saturday night, November 26th, with her daughter and grandson at her bedside. She is survived by her daughter, Susan Sulzberger Rolfe, grandson Myron Sulzberger (Mickey) Rolfe, granddaughters Joanne (Jody) Quinn and her husband Roger, and Penny R. Sullivan and her husband Tim. Also survived by three great grandsons, Billy and Teddy Quinn, and Tommy Sullivan. Reunited at last with her adored late husband, Edward, from whom she was widowed for 17 years. The daughter of the late Louis and Anna Dorfman, and predeceased by her sister, Paula Bulm, Maye is also survived by her sister, Charlotte Rosenfield, and by nieces and nephews, and by many friends and bridge partners in New York City. She was a member of the Regency Whist Club, the Harmonie Club and the Quaker Ridge Country Club. Our family is indebted to Sophia Sanchez for her steadfast care and love for 30 years, and, more recently, to the four wonderful nurses who tended to Maye as if she was their own. We will receive friends at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street on Tuesday from 6-9 P.M. with services Wednesday, 11;30 A.M. Interment private. Family will be at Susan Rolfe's home on Thursday from 2-7. Memorial contributions, if desired, to Mount Sinai Medical Center, attention Dr. Alvin Teirstein, 1 Gustav Levy Place, NY, NY 10029. She was fierce and brave, a boss, and we loved her.
        $(6$)SULZBERGER--Maye. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our longtime and cherished member, Maye Sulzberger. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. George Asch, Sec'y.
