
        DiSALVO -- Nicholas A., D.D.S., Ph.D., Professor Emeritus of Dentistry within the Section of Growth and Development, Division of Orthodontics, at the Columbia University College of Dental Medicine, passed away on June 8, 2007. Dr. DiSalvo received his D.D.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Columbia University in 1945 and 1952, respectively. Dr. DiSalvo was first appointed as a fellow in 1947 and became Assistant Professor of Dentistry in 1951. In 1958 he was promoted to the rank of Professor of Dentistry and was appointed as Director of the Division of Orthodontics. He served in that capacity for many years until his retirement in 1987. Dr. DiSalvo was active in organized dentistry and orthodontics, and chaired and served on many committees of the Northeastern Society of Orthodontists. He was President of the Society in 1973-74. He was also a long-time member of the Edward H. Angle Society of Orthodontists, Eastern Component and served as its President in 1973 and 1974. Our thoughts and prayers are extended to his wife Pauline, sons Donald and Alan, and to members of his extended family. A service was held on June 11, 2007 at the Coffey Funeral Home, Tarrytown, NY, which was followed by interment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. Ira B. Lamster, D.D.S., M.M.Sc., Dean Thomas J. Cangialosi, D.D.S., Director, Division of Orthodontics Columbia University College of Dental Medicine
