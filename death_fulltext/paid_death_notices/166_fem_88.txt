
        McLAUGHLIN-Rita C. On November 12, in New York City at the age of 87. Daughter of the late Thomas P. and Mary G. of Jersey City. Rita worked in the Personnel Dept of Metropolitan Life Insurance Co. for 43 years and retired in March, 1976. She was a member of the Immaculate Conception Church for over 50 years. She was predeceased by her two brothers, Edmund and his wife Anna and Thomas F. and his wife, Mary, her sister, Helen McGuire and her husband, Edward, and her niece Mary Jane Ciotta. Rita was survived by her nephew, Thomas P. and four nieces, Maureen Gonzalez, Ellen McGuire, Patricia Reihman, and Rita MacIndoe. She was also survived by 16 grand nieces and grand nephews and nine great grand nieces and grand nephews. Visiting at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave at 20th St, NYC, Wednesday, 2-4:30 PM and 79 PM. A Mass of Christian burial Immaculate Conception Church, 11 AM Thursday. Interment Holy Name Cemetery, Jersey City, N.J.
