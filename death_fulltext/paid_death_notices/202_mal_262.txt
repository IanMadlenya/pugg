
        SPELKE--Henry. Of Delray, FL, formerly of Stamford, CT. Died Monday, November 24, 2003 in his 95th year. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his wife, Judy, sons, Robert and Clifford, daughter-in-law Leslie, and grandchildren, Craig, Samantha, Jenna, and Brandon, who will remember him lovingly. Funeral services at Congregation Agudath Sholom, 301 Strawberry Hill Avenue, Stamford, CT 06902, Friday, November 28th at 11 A.M.
