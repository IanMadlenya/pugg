
        GRIEFF-Ruth M. On February 25, 2003 at the age of 90. Beloved aunt of Sandra Cohen of Suffern, NY and Annette Kaufman of Boca Raton, FL. Loving great aunt and greatgreat aunt. She was born on August 8, 1912 in Little Falls, NY to Sam and Selina Grieff. She was the sister of the late Celia Land. Donations may be made to the Little Falls Library, 10 Waverly Place, Little Falls, NY 13365. For further information contact Hellman Memorial Chapels, 845-356-8600.
