
        ACTON--Annie M., 88, November 5, 2005. Born in Castlebar, Co. Mayo IR, she was a resident of Norwood, Bx. for many years. Member of the League of the Sacred Heart at St. Margaret Mary Parish for 37 years. She worked at the United Nations as a Housekeeping Associate for 30 years. Daughter of the late John J. Kelly & Myria (nee Fadden). Beloved wife of 54 years to the late Patrick Acton. Dear sister of Winnie & Theresa. Loving mother of Joe, Michael, and the late Myria, Peter & John-Terrence. Visiting Tuesday 6-9 & Wed 2-5 & 7-9 P.M. at McKeon Funeral Home, 3129 Perry Avenue, Bronx. Funeral Mass on Thursday at 10 A.M. at St. Margaret Mary Church. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Margaret Mary Church in memory of Mrs. Acton.
