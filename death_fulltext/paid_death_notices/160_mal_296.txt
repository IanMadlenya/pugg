
        SPIEGEL-Leon. Passed away peacefully on Tuesday 23rd May 2000 in Scottsdale, Arizona. Aged 74 years. He is survived and greatly missed by his wife Amanda, her son Brad and family, his daughters Leslie and husband Bob, Cheryl and her son Justin and friend Lee John Glatz of Tuscon. Funeral will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations to be made to an animal welfare organization would be in harmony with his wishes. Those we love can never really leave us.
