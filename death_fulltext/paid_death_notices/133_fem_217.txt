
        MAY-Fay Sucherman. Died in Eugene, Oregon, on February 21, 1998, at the age of 88. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Beloved wife of the late Irving May. Devoted mother of Sondra May Steinman, of Fairfax, CA, Maurice May, of Needham, MA, and Glenn May, of Eugene, OR. Grandmother of eight and great-grandmother of six. Mrs. May was an accomplished actress and supporter of Jewish causes. Contributions in her memory may be made to the May Scholarship Fund, Hebrew Rehabilitation Center for Aged, 1200 Centre St, Boston, MA 02131
