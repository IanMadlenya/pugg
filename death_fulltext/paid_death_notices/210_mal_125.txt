
        UNGER--Arthur, 80, of New York City, suddenly in Paris, France on July 9, 2004. Longtime movie and TV critic for the Christian Science Monitor, publisher of Datebook and Beatles Magazines, and distinguished alumnus of the University of Missouri School of Journalism, where his collection of interviews with entertainment notables is housed. He served as an Army cryptographer in the Pacific Theater in WWII. Survived by his life partner of 45 years, Raul Nunez, his beloved sister Rosalind Ferber of Southbury, CT, brother Dr. Sanford Unger of Sedona, AZ, brother-in-law, sister-in-law, cousins, nephews and nieces.
