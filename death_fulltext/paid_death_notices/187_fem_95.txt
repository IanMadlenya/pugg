
        ALPER-Ethyl. On August 13, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Sigfried Somers. Devoted mother of Owen Hill Alper & Harriet A. Otto. Cherished grandmother of David Hill Alper, Ellyn Schoen, Regan Otto Schroeder, Tiffany A. Otto and Dylan R. Otto and great-grandmother of Justine Bernhardt, Jason Schoen and Evan Alper. She was an accomplished attorney in the metropolitan area, past-president of The American Institute of Decorators and was involved with numerous projects with the International Executive Service Corps. Service, Thursday, August 15, 2002, 11:30 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The American Alzheimer's Foundation.
