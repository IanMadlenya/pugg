
        MOSKIN-Doris Bloch, 75, resident of Hartsdale, NY, beloved mother of Mark, David and Nancy and grandmother of Isaac, Lillian and Mae Rose, died suddenly July 24th at White Plains Hospital. Born in Stuttgart, Germany, she was brought to England and then to the United States as a child. She graduated from Queens College. Served as Chairman of the Board of the Scarsdale (NY) Adult School. Also survived by her former husband, J. Robert Moskin. Graveside service at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Ardsley, NY, at 2 PM Sunday.
