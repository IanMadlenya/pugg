
        COLEMAN-Martin S. On May 24, 2000, at age 86. Former President and Chairman of the Board of the Mosler Safe Company. Survived by Janet, his wife of 60 years; his children Ann and Fred Lynn, John and Linda Coleman, Nancy and Matthew Mintzis; his grandsons Jeff Lynn and Julian Mintzis; his nephews James Coleman, Robert and William Perper; his niece Victoria Lasdon Rose and his sisterin-law Dorie Coleman. He was the brother of the late Julian Coleman and Janet Perper. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Saturday 7-9PM with service Sunday, 1:30PM. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to New York University Medical Center, 550 1st Avenue, New York, New York 10016.
        COLEMAN-Martin S. in sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished member, Martin S. Coleman. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
