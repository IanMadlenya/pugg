
        WALKER--Daniel. February 1, 1909--October 8, 2003, born in DeWitt, VA, parents Albert and Rosa Walker. Survivors, Audrey, Cooper, Felton, Albertine, Vicie, and Carla Walker, Edward Earl and Charles Earl Harris. He lived on Lenox Avenue for over 50 years. He attended the A. Phillips Randolph Senior Center for many years. He was buried in Dallas, TX, on October 14, 2003.
