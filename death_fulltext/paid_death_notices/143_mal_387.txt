
        KAYNIS-Samuel A., died peacefully on December 27, 1998 at the age of 96. Husband for sixty-seven years of the late Daisy B. Kaynis, father of Katherine Goldsmith and Michael D. Kaynis, father-in-law of Sabine Kaynis and Clifford H. Goldsmith, grandfather of Alexandra Goldsmith Fallon. A fine craftsman, with a clear and creative mind to the very end, he was admired and loved by all who knew him. A memorial service will be held in the near future.
