
        STRONG-Elizabeth S. (White-Leary) of Locust Valley, NY and Vero Beach, Fla. On December 16, 1999. Owner of the former Mill Pond House, Oyster Bay, with the late Ann Gilbert, Wellesley College, Class of 1941. Beloved wife of Frederick S. Strong III. She is also survived by her devoted daughter Blaine White Tavares, three step sons, Charles, Frederick, Kevin Leary, and ten grandchildren. Service will be held at St. John's of Lattingtown, Mon 11am. Memorial donations may be made to American Cancer Society .
