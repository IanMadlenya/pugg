
        STEIN--Philip. Born September 14, 1983, died September 5, 2003. His life was tragically ended in an automobile accident. Beloved son of Jane and Edward, brother of Frannie, grandson of Alvin and Dorothy Schwartz, both deceased, Getrude and Philip Stein, both deceased, nephew of Tom and Lonnie Schwartz, Amy and Chuck Spielman, Marty and Bert Katz, and Karen Stein, cousin of Ilyse, Adam, Laura, Eric, Jenny, Julie, Rachel, Pam, Stephanie and David, and grand-nephew of Ellen Spear Smith and Henry Yusen. Funeral was held Sunday, Shiva will be at the Stein's home until Friday sundown. Philip was and is deeply loved by his family and friends.
