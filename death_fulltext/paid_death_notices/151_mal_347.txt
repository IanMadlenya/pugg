
        LIEBMANN-Fred L., of Ossining, died August 27. Beloved husband of the late Vivian (Grau). Devoted son of the late Leon and Bella Liebmann. Beloved uncle. He was an architect and past President of the NY Society of Architects. He completed numerous commercial projects in Manhattan and throughout the metropolitan area. He leaves a large body of residential work throughout Westchester. He gave generously to Brandeis University, Teatown Lake Reservation and raised money for the United Jewish Appeal among others. Graveside services at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, on Monday, August 30, at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Teatown Lake Reservation, 1600 Spring Valley Road, Ossining, 10562. For information, call Stanetsky Chapels, Brookline, MA (800) 842-4280.
