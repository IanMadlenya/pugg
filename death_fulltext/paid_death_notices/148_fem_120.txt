
        LOEWENSTEIN-Regina. Passed away May 16th. Predeceased by her loving and caring parents Frieda and Solomon Loewenstein and adored sister Norma Drabkin. Interment private. A memorial service will be held at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 Street, on May 20 at 11:00 AM.
