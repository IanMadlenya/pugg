
        SCHWARTZBERG - Jack. Of Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of Brooklyn, New York, died on Sunday, March 7th, 1999 at the age of 95. He is survived by his son Stanley (Mary Lou), grandchildren Mark (Sasha), Laury (Andrew), David, and Sarah (Kenneth); five great-grandchildren, many relatives and friends. A generous man who enriched the lives of all those who knew him. He will be greatly missed.
