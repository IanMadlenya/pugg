
        DAVIS - Veronica, died peacefully at home in Irvington, New York on April 23, 2003 after a long illness. She was the much loved sister of Father Bernard J. Campbell, CSP; Mrs. Margaretta Bruegger of Snowmass, CO. and Mrs. Ellen Appelle of Irvington, New York. She was the beloved aunt of Nicole, Amanda, Michael, and Matthew Appelle and John Francis and Retta Bruegger. Ronnie was a graduate of Cornell University, Catholic University and Georgetown University and a dedicated and caring professional who touched many thousands of lives over a thirty year career as a nurse practitioner and midwife. She practiced her profession at the Navaho Reservation near Gallup, New Mexico, Washington, D.C., Portland, Maine and New York City. Her last place of employment was North Central Bronx Hospital where she was most esteemed for her consummate skill and kindness. Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated on Saturday, April 26, 10:00AM, Transfiguration Church, N. Broadway, Tarrytown, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorials to Phelps Hospice, Sleepy Hollow, NY appreciated.
