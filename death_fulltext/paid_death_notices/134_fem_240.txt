
        TWIGG-SMITH-Laila. The Board of Trustees and staff at the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our beloved friend and member of the Museum's National and Painting & Sculpture Committees, Laila Twigg-Smith. She will be warmly remembered for her steadfast support of American artists and her dedication to furthering the welfare and progress of contemporary American art. We extend our deepest condolences to her son, Cade. Flora M. Biddle, Honorary Chairman Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Gilbert C. Maurer, President David A. Ross, Director
        TWIGG - SMITH - Laila. The Trustees and staff of the New Museum of Contemporary Art mourn the passing of Trustee Laila Twigg-Smith. Ms. Twigg-Smith was a source of inspiration and good humor to all who had the pleasure of working with her. She believed deeply that art has the power to change people's lives and that artists should be supported and nurtured in their work. She will be greatly missed. Our deepest condolences to her entire family. Henry Luce III, President Saul Dennison, Sr. Vice President Marcia Tucker, Director
        TWIGG-SMITH-Laila. Art lover, art patroness, and friend, par excellence: thank you for sharing ten extraordinary years. Cade and Janet: there aren't words to thank you for your care of Laila. Christopher English Walling
