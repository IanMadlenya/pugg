
        GREW--Henry Sturgis, of New York City and West Palm Beach, Florida, died suddenly on May 25th, 2007 in Florida. He had lived in Boston and Newport, Rhode Island. He is survived by a sister, Selina Grew Rossiter of Lincoln, Massachusetts, and two brothers, Joseph Clark Grew of Boston and Robert Sturgis Grew of Raleigh, North Carolina; as well as five nieces, four nephews, six grandnieces and one grandnephew. A memorial Service will be held on August 4, at 11 am, at St. Mary's By-The-Sea, Northeast Harbor, Maine. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Dublin School, Inc., Dublin, New Hampshire.
