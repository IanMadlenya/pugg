
        ADAMS--Brockman ''Brock.'' (77) of Stevensville, MD, a Representative and a Senator from Washington, died at his home on Friday, September 10, 2004. Survived by his beloved wife, Elizabeth ''Betty'' Scott Adams, father of, Scott Adams of Jacksonville, FL, Dean Adams of Roswell, GA, Kokie Adams of Seattle, WA and Aleen Adams of Seattle, WA; sister, Phyllis Hayes of Issaquah, WA; grandfather of seven and uncle of two. Memorial services will be held on Wednesday, September 15, 2004 at 1PM at the Christ Episcopal Church, 830 Romancoke Rd, Stevensville, MD 21666. Interment will be private. Memorial contribution gifts may be made to: Hospice of Queen Anne's, Inc., 300 Del Rhodes Ave., Queenstown, MD 21658 or University of Washington Libraries Brock Adams Papers, Box 352900 Seattle, Washington 98195-2900. Arrangements made by Fellows, Helfenbein and Newnam Funeral Home, P. A., Chester, MD. See www.fhnfuneralhome.com.
