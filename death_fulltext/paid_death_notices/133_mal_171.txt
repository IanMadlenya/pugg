
        LURIE-Harold. Professor Emeritus, Caltech University. Aeronautical/nuclear engineer, lawyer, pilot, adventurer, mentor. A gentle man. Harold passed away peacefully in his 78th year. Beloved father of Diana Lurie of Missoula, Montana and David Lurie of Boston, Massachusetts. Cherished companion of Bobbie Altman. Donations in his memory may be made to Hospice of Saddleback Valley, 24022 Calle de la Plata, Suite 500, Laguna Hills, CA 92653
