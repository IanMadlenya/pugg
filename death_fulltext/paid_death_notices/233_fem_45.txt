        $(6$)SOKOL--Margaret. The Faculty of Arts and Science at NYU mourns the loss of our friend and benefactor, Margaret Sokol. Margaret was a spirited member of the Board of Overseers and a thoughtful patron of Arts and Science, supporting a wide range of initiatives including undergraduate scholarships, post doctoral fellowships, the Margaret and Herman Sokol Faculty Award in the Sciences and the Margaret and Herman Sokol Chair in Chemistry. We fondly remember Margaret's deep personal interest in our students and faculty and her great pride in their accomplishments. She will be missed. We offer our deep sympathies to her friends and family. Richard M. Foley, Anne and Joel Ehrenkranz Dean of the Faculty of Arts and Science, NYU Peter Lennie, Dean for Science
