
        De SERIO-Francis William. On January 25, 2002, of Rosemont, PA, formerly of Hornell, NY. Mr. De Serio was retired from the Defense Department in 1984 and served as President of Essex Research Corporation since 1987. He was the beloved husband of Charlotte Heuer De Serio. Like a father to Robert Allen Watson and William Russell Watson. Brother of Donald A. De Serio and Clara Beritela. Also survived by numerous nieces, nephews and their children. Funeral Mass Tuesday January 29, 2 PM, St. John Vianney Church, Youngsford and Conshohocken State Rds., Gladwyne, PA. Interment West Laurel Hill Cemetery, Bala Cynwyd, PA. Relatives and friends are invited to his visitation Monday evening, 7 to 9 PM, at The McConaghy Funeral Home, 328 West Lancaster Ave., Ardmore, PA. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to Bryn Mawr Hospital, 138 S. Bryn Mawr Ave, Bryn Mawr, PA 19010.
