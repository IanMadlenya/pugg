
        STRAHL--Phyllis. (Brous). Of Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY passed away on October 27, 2005. She is survived by her loving husband of 40 years, Norman Strahl, devoted mother to her children and their spouses Matthew and DeeAnn Strahl, and Leslie and Lee Droppelman, beloved Nana to Elias Strahl. She will be fondly remembered for her dedication to family and friends. Services to be held at Menorah Chapels, 12:00 PM, October 30, at 9321 Memorial Park Rd, Palm Beach, FL.
