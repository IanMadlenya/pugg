
        GRAHAM-Irvin. Songwriter, died Dec. 1, 2001. Married to the late Lillian Graham for 30 years. Devoted brother of Cecile Abraham. Irvin had his first hit at 15 when he wrote ''You Better Go Now'' which Imogene Coca sang in ''New Faces.'' He wrote for the ''Show of Shows'' starring Coca and Sid Caesar. Also wrote for the Jane Frohman Show and he & his co-writers wrote ''I Believe'' for her which has become a standard all over the world sung by singers from Elvis Presley, Frankie Laine, to Barbra Streisand on her latest album. He had many devoted and loving friends who will miss him always.
