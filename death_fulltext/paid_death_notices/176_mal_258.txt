
        MILLER-Edwin Haviland, Professor Emeritus of English at New York University died September 17th at age 83 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Rosalind, father of Pamela Miller Ness, father-in-law of Paul M. Ness. He was a distinguished scholar of American Literature, publishing numerous biographies and critical studies of Whitman, Melville and Hawthorne. He received a B.A. in English from Lehigh University in 1940, an M.A. from Pennsylvania State University in 1942, and a Ph.D. from Harvard University in 1951. A memorial service is planned for a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to the New York Public Library, Berg Collection, 42nd St. & 5th Avenue, New York, NY 10018, or the Allentown Art Museum, 5th and Court St, Allentown, PA 18105.
