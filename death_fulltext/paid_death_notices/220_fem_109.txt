
        LIPPMAN--Doris. The officers and members of the Board of Governors of the Jewish Home at Rockleigh, Russberrie Home for Jewish Living, note with profound sorrow the passing of Doris Lippman, wife of our Board member, William Lippman, and mother of our Board member, Howard Lippman. The Lippman family have been longtime friends and supporters of the Jewish Home. Doris was a special person who, along with Bill, exhibited deep commitment to humanitarian, Jewish and community causes. She will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to her beloved husband Bill, her children, Howard and Sherri Lippman, Debbie and Stuart Himmelfarb, her grandchildren and her entire family. May her name be for a blessing. Maggie Kaplen, President Charles P. Berkowitz, Executive Vice President
        LIPPMAN--Doris. Temple Sinai of Bergen County deely mourns the passing of our member, Doris Lippman, wife of William Lippman. Mother and mother-in-Law of Deborah and Stuart Himmelfarb, and Howard and Sherri Lippman. Condolences are extended to the entire family. Dr. Bruce S. Block, Rabbi George Bean, President
        LIPPMAN--Doris. Dear Dor, I cannot imagine not having you in my life. You were a true friend in my darkest hour and in every other way. Thursday evenings will never be the same. I shall never forget you. Lorraine Z.
