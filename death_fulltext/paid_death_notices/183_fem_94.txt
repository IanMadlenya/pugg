
        DREIZEN-Roslyn. On April 9, 2002. Passed away peacefully at home in her 97th year. Beloved wife of the late Solomon Dreizen. Adored mother of Paul, and Alan and Carol. Loving grandmother of Stefanie. Cherished aunt of Martin and Shirley, great aunt of Herbert, Harvey, Kenneth, Stephen, Lisa and Mitchell. Alert, caring and cheerful to the end, a wonderful person adored by all who knew her. President of the City of Hope, Lincoln Center Chapter, for many years. Services Friday, 11:30AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Faculty and Friends, NYU School of Medicine.
