
        GARREN--Dr. Mitchel G. A memorial service for Dr. Mitchel Garren will be held on Sunday, January 14, at 2:00pm at the Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 St., New York, NY. Born October 31, 1912, he died December 13, 2006. After serving in World War II, he married Betty Aronson (now deceased). A graduate of NYU Medical School, Dr. Garren practiced medicine for over 50 years. He is survived by his brother, Daniel (Beulah) of Boca Raton, FL, two sons, Scott (Heather) and Brent (Wilma), four grandchildren, five great-grandchildren, two nephews and his second wife, Rosemary. A fund in Dr. Garren's name has been established at the Arthritis Foundation, www.arthritis.org.
