
        WEITZ-Dr. Leo. Devoted father of Barbara Pollard; loving grandfather of Laura Masurovsky and Jonathan Pollard; delighted greatgrandfather of Alex, Adam, Jason, Benjamin and Danielle. An avid proponent of public education, Dr. Weitz served as Principal of James Monroe High School in the Bronx, and Lafayette High School in Brooklyn. He created the Core Curriculum and helped to develop the plans for John Dewey High School in Brooklyn. He went on to establish the Graduate Program in Administration and Supervision at Pace Unviversity in New York City. He loved people, books and teaching, in that order.
