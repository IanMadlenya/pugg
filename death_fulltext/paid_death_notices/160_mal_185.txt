
        LEWIS-Rabbi Theodore N. Died on May 15, 2000. Beloved father and teacher, grandfather and great grandfather, mourned by his family. Remembered with love by the thousands and thousands of men and women of four generations to whom he taught Torah and Yiddishkeit and the love of Ha - Shem, Zeicher Tsaddik L'Brocha: The memory of the righteous man is a blessing.
