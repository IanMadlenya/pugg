
        HEHRE-Edward J., M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is saddened by the death of the founding chairman and professor emeritus of its Department of Microbiology and Immunology. A distinguished researcher, world-renowned for his outstanding work with carbohydrate enzymology, his colleagues and students remember him fondly as a gentle mentor and tireless academic scholar. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Hehre family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
