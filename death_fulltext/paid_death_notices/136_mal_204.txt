
        SHERER-Rabbi Moshe. Outstanding leader of Torah Jewry whose essence reflected a boundless commitment to Klal Yisrael. He creatively united the Yeshiva and Chassidic worlds into a force for public advocacy, the dissemination of Torah and the defense of Jewish rights. A steadfast proponant of the Torah value of Jewish unity, he was an articulate and skilled spokesman for the American Orthodox community. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Orthodox Union Mandell I. Ganchrow, M.D., Pres. Marcel Weber, Ch. Bd of Directors Dr. Marcos Katz, Ch., Bd of Gov. Rabbi Raphael B. Butler, Exec V.P. Rabbi Pinchas Stolper, Sr. Exec
        SHERER-Moshe. The World Jewish Congress mourns the passing of Rabbi Moshe Sherer, a leader of the Jewish people, righteous among men. We extend our condolences to the family, to World Agudath Israel and to the Jewish people as a whole. Rabbi Sherer leaves a vacuum impossible to fill. Hamakom yenachem etchem toch shaar avelei Zion Jerushalayim. Edgar M. Bronfman, President Israel Singer, Secretary General
        SHERER-Moshe. The Conference on Jewish Material Claims Against Germany records its profound sorrow on the passing of one of its Vice Presidents, Rabbi Moshe Sherer. We will deeply miss the warmth, constructive insight and spirit of cooperation which he brought to the Conference and to all his endeavors. Dr. Israel Miller, President Mr. Saul Kagan, Executive V.P.
        SHERER-Rabbi Moshe. Together with all of Klal Yisroel, we mourn the passing of a great Torah leader. May the family, together with the entire nation, be comforted amongst the mourners of Tziyon and Yerushalayim. Kehillas Bais Yisroel, Minneapolis, Minnesota
        SHERER-Rabbi Moshe. We mourn Rabbi Moshe Sherer, a veritable institution and role model of a consummate Manhig B'Yisrael. We are impoverished by his loss. Rabbinical Council of America Rabbi Jacob Rubenstein, Pres. Rabbi Steven Dworken, Exec VP
