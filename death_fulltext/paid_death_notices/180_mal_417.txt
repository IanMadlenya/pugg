
        McCOLLUM-H.W. The Board of Directors and Officers of Amerada Hess Corporation express their deep sorrow and sense of loss on the death of their esteemed colleague and close friend, H.W. McCollum. Mr. McCollum's leadership as Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Board of Directors of Amerada Hess and his advice and guidance as a Director of Amerada Hess from 1969 to 1998 were instrumental in the growth of the Corporation. His contributions and wise counsel have been of great value and will be missed. We express our deepest sympathy to his children and grandchildren.
