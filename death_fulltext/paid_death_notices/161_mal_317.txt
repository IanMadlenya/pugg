
        KOLLIGS-George W., 71, of Chester, New York. Retired pressman for New York Times, on June 26th at his residence, son of the late Robert & Emily Boucher Kolligs, loving husband of Lois, (nee McComiskey), devoted father of Dennis & Wayne Kolligs. Beloved brother of Robert Kolligs & Evelyn Brandafino & loving grandfather of four. Visitation Thursday, June 29, 2-4 & 7-9 PM at Smith, Seaman & Quackenbush Funeral Home, 117 Maple Avenue, Monroe, New York. Funeral Friday, June 30th, at 10 AM at the Funeral Home.
