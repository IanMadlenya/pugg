
        KIELY--Francis G., Jr., 77, died Monday, June 19, 2006, at Moses Ludington Hospital, Ticonderoga. Born November 18, 1928, in Brooklyn, he was the son of the late Frances G. & Mary (Fitzsimmons) Kiely. Mr. Kiely served during World War II as a paratrooper in the United Stares Army. He was a retired pressman with the New York Times after 40 years of service. Mr. Kiely was a member of the Veterans of Foreign Wars and he enjoyed crossword puzzles, horse racing and fishing. Mr. Kiely vacationed in Adirondack for 15 years and moved with his wife permanently 6 years ago from Floral Park. He will be remembered as a loving husband, father and grandfather. Mr. Kiely was predeceased by two brothers, James and John Kiely, and one sister, Gloria LaVelle. Survivors include his wife of 54 years, Joan Christmann Kiely of Adirondack whom he married December 19, 1951; one son, Matthew Kiely of Adirondack; four daughters, Patricia (Robert) Smith of Floral Park, Karen (Robert) Tooher of Guilderland, Jean (Edward) Luna of Dix HIlls, and Ellen (Thomas) Eckl of Rotterdam; also 12 grandchildren. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 11 A.M. Friday, June 23, at Our Lady of Lourdes Catholic Church, Schroon Lake, with the Rev. William G. Muench officiating. Interment will be at the Adirondack Cemetery, Adirondack. Relatives and friends may call from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 P.M. Thursday, June 23, at Barton-McDermott Funeral Hoem, 9 Pine St., Chestertown.
