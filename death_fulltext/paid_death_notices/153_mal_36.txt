
        HIGHBERGER-John K., Reverend. On October 2, 1999, of Ramsey, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Helen Stewart Foster Highberger (1993). Devoted father of Judith S. Highberger of Ramsey and William F. Highberger of Pacific Palisades, CA. Loving grandfather of Helen Barbara Highberger and Anna Mary Highberger both of Pacific Palisades, CA. Born in Connellsville, PA, Rev. Highberger left Waymart, PA, and was installed the Ramsey pastor on May 11, 1945. He brought with him a wealth of talent and experience. He received his bachelor's degree from Washington and Jefferson College, his bachelor and master's in theology from Princeton Theological Seminary. Rev. Highberger was Pastor of the First Presbyterian Church, Ramsey, from 1945 to 1973, and Pastor Emeritus since then, and also Assistant Minister at the Saddle River Reformed Church, Upper Saddle River, from 1974 to 1976. Ecumenism was always at the core of Rev. Highberger's ministry. When the Jewish community organized in Ramsey, they worshipped in the Presbyterian Church. He was especially known for working ecumenically within Ramsey. A funeral service will be held on Tuesday, October 5, 1999, at 10 AM, at the First Presbyterian Church, Ramsey. Visitation Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Van Emburgh Sneider Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Avenue, Ramsey. Interment Princeton Cemetery, Princeton, NJ. Donations to the Ramsey Ambulance Corps will be appreciated.
