
        SHERMAN -- Robert (Bob). Age 74, died peacefully of a stroke on Sunday, January 25th. Survived by beloved wife Mimi, devoted children Ricky (Alisa), Carol (Deivy) and Bruce (Lori). Grandfather to Ryen, Brett, Ilana, Alex, Brandon and Jamie. Bob was an avid tennis player and always viewed his children and grandchildren as his greatest life accomplishment. Services at 10AM, on Friday, January 30th, at ''BoulevardRiverside'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island.
