
        PREGER--Rachel. On August 8, 2003 in her 96th year. Survived by her adoring children, Barbara and Lou Kahn, Teaneck, NJ, Mickey and Judy Preger, Camden, SC, grandchildren and great-grandson. Devoted wife of the late Paul D. Preger. Teacher, mentor. Woman of great strength, integrity and generosity; who shared her love and wisdom with so many. Contributions to the Arthritis Foundation, 122 E. 42nd St., NY, NY 10168.
