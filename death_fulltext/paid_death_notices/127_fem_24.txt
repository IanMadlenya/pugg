
        RYBCZYNSKI-Wanda, M.D. Age 48. On August 3, 1997, in Berlin, Germany. Beloved wife of Zbigniew, an award-winning cinematographer and HDTV pioneer. Dearly remembered by her son Pawel, her brother-in-law Witold and his wife Sandy. She was a devoted wife, instrumental in her huband's success, a loving mother, a sympathetic friend and spiritual scholar. Throughout her life, even during times of suffering and pain, she maintained a deep love of life and all living things, her family, her friends and her animals. She was truly an angel. Funeral services are being held on Wednesday, August 7, in Berlin. A memorial service is planned for a future date in NYC.
