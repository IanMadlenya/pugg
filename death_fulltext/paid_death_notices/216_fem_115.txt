
        BLANCATO--Nancy Benedict, on January 12, in Rye, NY. Beloved wife of Dr. Louis S. Blancato. wonderful mother to Louis Jr., Robert, John and Amy, and grandmother to Celia and Carly. She was a friend to many. She enjoyed two careers, nursing and wife, mother and grandmother. She was a woman of warmth, intellect, with a wonderful sense of humor. The Blancato family thanks the dedicated staff of the Osborn Pavilion. Service on Saturday at 1:30PM at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, 20 Cedar St., Bronxville. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to Hospice and Palliative Care of Westchester.
