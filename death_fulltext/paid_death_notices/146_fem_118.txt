
        FROST-Linda. Of Great Neck, NY and Williams Island, FL. Passed away March 4, 1999 at age 58 after a long illness. Beloved wife for thirty years of Jerry Frost. She will always be remembered for her beauty, grace and compassion. Services will be held Friday, March 12, 1999 at 12:00 noon at Riverside Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York, 516-487-9600.
