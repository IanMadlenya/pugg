
        TISHMAN -- Alan. The trustees and staff of the New York City Police Foundation are deeply saddened by the passing of Alan Tishman. He was a staunch supporter of our work for many years along with his wife Peggy. Alan's active participation had a significant impact on our work, and we will indeed miss him. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to our dedicated Board member, Peggy, and the entire Tishman family. George D. Benjamin, Chairman Pamela D. Delaney, President
