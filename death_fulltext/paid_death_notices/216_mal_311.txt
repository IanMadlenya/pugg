
        WEINSTEIN--Alvin, 79, Old Westbury, NY. Beloved husband of Joan for 54 years, died January 20, 2005 in Juno Beach, Florida. Loving father of Michael, Marc, Abbe, Peter, David and Jonathan. Grandfather of Jennifer, Melissa, Nikko, Max, Adam, Matthew, Jack, Jasmine, Jules and Noah. Alvin will be missed for his generosity, honesty and devotion to his wife & family. Alvin created Concord Fabrics, a textiles and fashion business that has thrived for many years. He was a passionate advocate for American manufacturing and generously supported his community with philanthropy & unwavering commitment. A funeral service will be held for Alvin in Florida, 11 AM Tuesday, January 25, at I.J. Morris Funeral Directors, 4714 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach, FL. A graveside ceremony will be held in New York, 11 AM on Thursday, January 27, at the Beth David Cemetery, 300 Elmont Road, Elmont, New York.
