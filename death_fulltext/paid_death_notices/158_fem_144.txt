
        KATZ-Rose S. Passed away peacefully at home on March 9. Beloved wife of the late Seymour. Devoted mother of Steven and Susan, and Herbert and Linda. Cherished grandmother of Naomi and Aviva, and Jonathan, David and Marlee. Dear sister of Jack Sutter. Revered aunt of many. A true Aishet Chayil and loyal daughter of her people. A pioneer in the movement to free Soviet Jewry. The matriarch of our family.
