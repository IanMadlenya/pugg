
        ROTHSTEIN-Nathaniel, 89. On November 29 at the Bethel Health Care Center, formerly of Southbury, CT and Harrison, New York. Beloved husband of Jane Rothstein of Stamford, CT, cherished father of Amy (Peter Salerno) and Julie Ruff (Leslie), adored grandfather of Daniel and Emily Ruff, devoted brother of Beverly Kahn and the late Edna Valenstein, retired lawyer, director emeritus of Visiting Nurse Service of Connecticut. He graced everyone who knew him with his wit, intelligence, generosity and originality. Memorial service to be held at a later date.
