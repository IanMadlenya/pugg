
        ADLER-Kurt A., MD, Phd. The Advanced Institute for Analytic Psychotherapy and the Advanced Center for Psychotherapy note with sadness the passing of our esteemed colleague, Dr. Kurt Adler. For many years he was President and Dean of Psychiatric Studies of the Institute and Medical Director of the Advanced Center. He was a teacher, a healer and a wise mentor. He was loved and will be missed. Our condolences to Tanya and his family.
