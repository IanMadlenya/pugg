
        PLUTCHIK--Robert, Ph.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is deeply saddened by the passing of its professor emeritus of psychiatry and behavioral sciences. A world-renowned methodologist and national expert in the study of emotions, he joined the Einstein faculty in 1971, serving as director of the Program Development and Clinical Research Unit for the Department of Psychiatry. A research mentor to generations of students, he was much beloved and will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Plutchik family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
