
        CARLTON-Margaret Gillies, 94. February 21, 1998. Wife of the late Winslow Carlton, mother of Mary G. Swope, Ann W. Carlton, and Rhona Carlton-Foss and grandmother of Tim and Ian Swope and Margaret Carlton-Foss. Funeral Services 3:30 PM, Saturday, Feb. 28, Church of the Messiah, Church St., Woodshole, MA. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Henry Street Settlement.
        CARLTON-Margaret. It is with great sorrow that we note the passing of Margaret Carlton. She was a longtime Trustee and supporter of The Henry Street Settlement. Our deepest sympathy to the family. The Board and Staff of The Henry Street Settlement
