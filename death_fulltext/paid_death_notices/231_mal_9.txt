
        FARRELL--Matthew J., Esq. Passed away on March 24, 2006, at age 35. He is survived by his wife Anastasia, mother Elizabeth, father Matthew, sisters Kelly and Kimberly, brothers-in-law, nieces, nephew and many dear friends. Matthew touched so many people's lives with his special charm, endless generosity and contagious charisma. He was larger than life and his warm heart and beautiful soul will live on forever.
