
        ZELTNER--Harold, 93, formerly of New York City, on January 6, 2004. For many years he was NY district sales manager for Metro-GoldwynMayer. He is survived by his sons, Ted Zeltner, M.D., of Albany, NY, and Craig of Wayne, NJ; sisters, Hortense Paster of Maryland, and Francis Hammer of Pennsylvania, and two grandsons. Graveside services Thursday, January 8, 1pm, at Menorah Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. Donations, in lieu of flowers, to ECLC Foundation for Child Handicaps, Chatham, NJ. Information Jewish Memorial Chapel, 973-779-3048.
