
        SCHNEIDER--Leon D. Died on March 2, 2005 at the age of 71. A passionate New Yorker, Mr. Schneider was a graduate of Tilden High School, Columbia University and N.Y.U. Law School. A practicing member of the New York bar for more than 47 years, Mr. Schneider began his legal career with the Waterfront Commission and then with the N.Y.C. Housing and Redevelopment Board. He authored ''A Guide to the Court System'' under the aegis of the Institute for Judicial Administration. He was a long term President of the Gustave Hartman Y in Rockaway Beach, Queens. Mr. Schneider is survived by his children Daniel, William and Elizabeth and by his grandchildren, Taryn, Dean, Max, Tyler, Cole and Preston. Graveside services Sunday March 7th, 11:00 A.M. at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale. Donations in his memory may be made to ''Roy Reuther Senior Center'' 718-471-3110, in lieu of flowers.
