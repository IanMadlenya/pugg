
        PALTER--Harriet R. Passed on September 1, at age 80, after a courageous four-and-ahalf year battle with lung cancer. She is survived by her husband Buddy, after a beautiful life together of 59 years and seven months, and her two sons, Scott and Steven, also her brother and sister-inlaw, Marvin and Faye Rubin. Services private, family only.
