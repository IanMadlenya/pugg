
        SILBERMAN--Dr. Elliot J., 75, passed away on January 24 after more than 4 years of the most courageous and valiant battle with pancreatic cancer. Beloved husband of 40 years to Dr. Rosanne K., devoted dad to Michelle and Dr. Clark Hubbard and Dr. Marc and Amy Silberman. Adored grandpa to Rebecca, Jaclyn, and Max. Loving brother to Barbara Cohan. Cherished Uncle Joey and Dr. Joe to all the children whose smiles he cared for as their pedodontist. His inspiration and gentle goodness will live on in our hearts forever. Services will be held at 1:30pm, January 27 at Congregation Neve Shalom, 250 Grove Avenue, Metuchen, NJ.
