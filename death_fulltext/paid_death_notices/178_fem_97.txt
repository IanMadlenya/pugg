
        VANDER POEL-Dorothy Marlatt. On Tuesday, November 6, 2001 at her residence in Washington, DC. Beloved wife of Halsted Billings Vander Poel; devoted mother of Jane Stuart Lenzetti, Virginia Stuart Muse, Deborah Billings Jorgensen and Halsted Billings Vander Poel II; sister of Constance Huested of California. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Hospice of Washington, 3720 Upton Street, NW, Washington, DC 20016, Attn: Development Department. Please reference ''in memory of Dorothy Vander Poel.''
