
        OPPENHEIM-Henry. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Henry Oppenheim, a dear friend of the Jewish community and beloved father of Steven, a valued leader and distinguished member of our Board of Directors. We extend our sincerest condolences to his sons, Steven and Richard; and to his grandchildren, Donald, Allyson, Laura, Michael, and Daniel. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
        OPPENHEIM-Henry. The Simon Wiesenthal Center extends its sincerest condolences to our trustee, Steve Oppenheim, on the death of his father. May the entire Oppenheim family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and know of no further sorrow for a long time. Simon Wiesenthal Center Rabbi Marvin Hier Dean and Founder Nelson Peltz, Co-Chairman Marc A. Utay, Chairman New York Executive Board Rhonda Barad, Eastern Director
