
        HEFFNER-Marilyn Jeanne Hood, 75. Of Irvington, New York, on June 24, 2002. Dearly cherished wife of Lee and beloved mother of Kristen, Lincoln & Amy. Grandmother of Claire, David, Melissa, Jesse, Kelby and Tanner and mother-in-law of Patty and Bill. We celebrate her life and the joy, love and strength that were an inspiration to all who knew her. She devoted herself to her family, church and community and her love and care touched and enriched the lives of so many. She will stay in our hearts & prayers forever. Memorial services will be held at the Irvington Presbyterian Church, Irvington, NY, Monday July 1, 11:00 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Memorial Garden of the Irvington Presbyterian Church or Doctors without Borders.
