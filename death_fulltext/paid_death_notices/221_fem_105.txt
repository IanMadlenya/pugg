
        FEINBERG--Barbara J. on June 6, 2005, at the age of 56. Barbara was a beloved mother, sister and daughter to her family, as well as a scholar, an athlete and caring, compassionate friend to all who knew her. A woman of remarkable independence, intelligence, beauty and strength, she practiced as a pharmacist before obtaining her MBA from the University of California at Berkeley. She returned to Columbia University to pursue her dream of obtaining a PhD in Social and Organizational Psychology, which she continued to work on through her illness. She is survived by her son Raefer, father Herbert, mother Gloria, brother Larry, sister-in-law Cindy, and sister Susan.
