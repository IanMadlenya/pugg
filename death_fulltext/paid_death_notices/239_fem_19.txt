
        WIEN--Ruth (Kupper), Passed away November 26, 2006. Ruth was the widow of the late Lawrence A. Wien, a prominent New York Attorney. She is survived by Mr. Wien's daughters Isabel and Peter Malkin and their beloved children and grandchildren, Enid (Dinny) and Lester Morse and their beloved children and grandchildren, and many adored friends in New York. In Florida she is survived by daughter Cookie Wynne, granddaughters Leslie Wynne and Jodie (Steven) Streeter, two great - grandchildren Aidan and Ava. Services were held November 30, 2006 in Miami Beach, Florida.
