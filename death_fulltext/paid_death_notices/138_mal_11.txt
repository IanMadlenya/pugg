
        BROWN-Ralph Sharp. The Dean and Faculty of Yale Law School mark with sadness the passing, on Wednesday, June 17, 1998, of Ralph S. Brown, Simeon E. Baldwin Professor Emeritus of Law and Frank E. Taplin Professorial Lecturer in Law, longtime friend and beloved colleague. He will be warmly remembered for over four decades of devoted teaching and service at Yale Law School; and for his scholarly contributions to the law. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. A memorial service for Professor Brown will be held at the Yale University Press, 302 Temple Street, New Haven, CT., Sunday, September 27, 1998, at 5 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to The Ralph S. Brown Fund for Student Needs at The Yale Law School Fund, PO Box 208341, New Haven, CT. 06520-8341. Anthony T. Kronman, Dean Yale Law School
