
        KATSIKARIS--Joseph Theodore, ''Bobby'', died in his sleep at his home on St. Croix on January 4th, 2007. He touched the lives of so many people. He will be sadly missed. He was the manager of Club Thrush from 1969 until 1973. Any friends, patrons or co-workers (espically bouncers) who would like to attend a memorial service please call Emily at 212-628-4316 or Dee at 607-775-1999. Bobby was predeceased by his father, Theodore Katsikaris, and his wife, Nanci (Francisco) Katsikaris. He is survived by his mother, Lillian (Vanasse) Katsikaris (Ottawa, Canada). Daughter, Mary (Katsikaris) Hornick. Grandchildren, Kristina Katsikaris and Joseph Hornick (Jerome, PA). Brothers; Ron (Toronto, Canada), Wayne (Hull, Quebec, Canada), George (California), and his Sister, Helen (Katsikaris) Beck (Texas). His Aunts, Helen Farnsworth, Mary and Ann Katsikaris, and other friends, cousins and relatives too numerous to mention. Donations in Bobby's Memory may be made to A.I.D.S. Research or St. Jude's Childrens Hospital.
