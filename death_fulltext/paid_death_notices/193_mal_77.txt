
        BOFSHEVER-Bernard. Of Tamarac, FL, died on February 7, 2003, after a valiant struggle with cancer. Born on August 25, 1919, in Brooklyn, NY, Mr. Bofshever served as a captain in the US Army during W.W. II. Upon his return he founded the Metroplitan Lamp Co, retiring in 1988, after 35 years serving as its president. He is survived by his loving wife Caroline, his daughter Marilyn, his son Michael, his grandchildren Jessica, Katie, James and Elizabeth, and his brother Ralph. Services will be held on Sunday, February 9, 2:15 PM, at the Star of David Chapel, North Lauderdale, FL.
