
        HESSE--Elizabeth Blass. On January 1, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Stanley W. Hesse. Sister of the late Julian Blass. Devoted mother of Barbara (Dr. Theodore M.) Tabachnick and William B. (Jennifer) Hesse. Sister-in-law of the late Norma and Lewis Raabin. Much loved by her grandchildren Alan (Kate) Tabachnick, James Tabachnick, Michael (Sarah) Tabachnick, Stacy and Scott Hesse. Also survived by three greatgrandchildren, nieces, nephews and dear friends. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Monday, January 3rd, 12-1 PM with graveside service to follow at Temple Israel Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Jewish Guild for the Blind or Search and Care.
        HESSE--Elizabeth. The Board of Directors, Officers and staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind mourn the death of Elizabeth Hesse, a Trustee, former President of the Women's Division and longtime supporter. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Pauline Raiff, Bd. Chairman Alan R. Morse, Pres. and CEO
