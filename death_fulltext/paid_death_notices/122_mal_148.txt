
        BALDWIN-David. Raconteur, actor, painter, beloved son of Berdis Emma Baldwin and brother of the late distinguished writer James Baldwin, died March 10th, 1997. His survivors include a son, Daniel, his wife, Marion Russell Baldwin, two brothers George and Wilmer, five sisters: Barbara Jamison, Gloria Karefa-Smart, Ruth Crumm, Elizabeth Dingle, and Paula Whaley, and a host of nieces, nephews, and friends. Services will be held at the Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine, 112th Street & Amsterdam Avenue, NYC, 10:30 A.M., Monday, March 17th. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests a contribution to your favorite charity.
