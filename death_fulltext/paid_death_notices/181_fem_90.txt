
        LEVINE-Rose (nee Goodman). Age 93, on January 27, 2002. Predeceased by adored husband Isidore in 1972. Beloved mother of Frances and Marian, dear motherin-law, loving grandmother and great-grandmother. Executive secretary for Jewish National Fund until her retirement in 1988, she was an accomplished folk dancer with a passion for the arts, nature and world travel. Her generous heart, radiant smile, wisdom and courageous spirit will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. ''Dance on a gentle wind''.
