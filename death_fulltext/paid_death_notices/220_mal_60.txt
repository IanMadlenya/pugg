
        BILECK--Marvin (b. 1920). With sadness we mark the death of Buddy on April 29. His remarkable, energetic and generous spirit lives on through his art, his books and the many lives that he so deeply touched. Our condolences to his wife Emily. A reception will be held at the gallery for the opening of his exhibition on Saturday, May 14, at 1 PM. Phil Alexandre Alexandre Gallery, New York www.alexandregallery.com
