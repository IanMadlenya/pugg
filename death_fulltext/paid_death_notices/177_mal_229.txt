
        ROSENBLOOM-Ben. The Board of Directors and Staff of the Jewish Outreach Institute in New York, mourn the passing of Ben Rosenbloom, loving father of Howard Rosenbloom a generous benefactor of JOI, & grandfather of Keith Rosenbloom, esteemed member of our Board. Through his life he taught the values that live on through those who knew and loved him, to whom we express our deepest sympathy: to Keith and Howard and all the members of the family. Terrence A. Elkes, President Dr. Kerry M. Olitzky, Exec Dir Dr. Egon Mayer, Founding Dir
