
        RODAL--Alexander. 91 years old, died peacefully on March 28, 2007. Husband for 50 years of the late Mina. Father of Gail Stadler (Jeff), Dr. Stewart (Eva). Grandfather of Joshua, David (Jill), Alison, and Carolyn. Beloved uncle of Steve and Orna, friend of Esther. Holocaust survivor. For 60 years a well lived life. He will be missed. Services were held privately.
