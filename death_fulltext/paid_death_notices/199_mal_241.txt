
        WALSH--Thomas Hammill, of Pelham, NY, died August 21, 2003. He is survived by his wife Mary McCauley Walsh, his children Thomas H. Walsh Jr, his wife Lynne, their son Christopher; Paul M. Walsh, his wife Cindy, their children Laura and David; Kathleen M. Walsh and Nicholas F. Walsh, his sisters Elizabeth, Rosemary & Grail Kearney. His entire professional career was in publishing. During WWII, he served as a Marine pilot in the Pacific and served during the Korean conflict. He retired as a Major. Friends may call at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Avenue, Pelham, NY on Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial at St. Catharine's Church on Monday, 10AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in his memory to All Hollows High School, 111 East 164th Street, Bronx, NY 10452.
