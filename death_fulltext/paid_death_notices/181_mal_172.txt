
        MARRITT-Dr. Samuel of Somers, NY, died on Wednesday, February 13, 2002 at White Plains Hospital in White Plains, NY. He was 90. Dr. Marritt was a Doctor of Medicine in Brooklyn and at Brooklyn Jewish Hospital. Dr. Marritt was born January 25, 1912 in New York City to Louis and Anna Marotznik Polansky. Dr. Marritt studied medicine in Scotland and graduated from St. Andrew's Medical School in 1936. Dr. Marritt lived in Somers, NY for the past four years. Before that he lived in Carmel, New York. He is survived by his wife Miriam Marritt, daughter Anne Schwartz and husband Bennett, son Dr. Emanuel Marritt & his wife Ellen, Naomi Nomikos and husband James, grandchildren Joseph and Mathew Schwartz, Allison Mazer, Eric Splarer and Phoebe Marritt. Services will be held at Temple Beth Shalom of Mahopac, New York at 11AM Thursday, February 14, 2002. Interment will follow at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Friends may send memorial donations to Temple Beth Shalom, 760 Rt. 6, Mahopac, New York 10541. Information: Cargain Funeral Homes Inc, 418 Route 6, Mahopac, New York.
