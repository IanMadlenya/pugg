
        BRAILOVE-Morris. Devoted husband of Sylvia. Died peacefully on February 21st at age 96. Cherished father of Michael and Jane Brailove Rutkoff and beloved grandfather of Adam, Rachel, Joshua and Rebekah. A native of Woodbine, NJ and graduate of Temple University Dental School, he practiced in Elizabeth for 50 years before moving to Monroe Township. His life was devoted to caring for family and friends. Memorial service to be announced.
