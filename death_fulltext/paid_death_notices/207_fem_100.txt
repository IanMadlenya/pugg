
        JOSEPHS--Miriam. The officers and staff of State of Israel Bonds deeply mourn the passing of Miriam Josephs of New Jersey, inspiring and devoted community leader and passionate supporter of Israel. A founding member of the Bergen County Israel Bonds Women's Division, she served as local chairman and as a member of the division's National Board of Directors. Our heartfelt condolences to her son, Neil, and her daughters, Debra Josephs and Barbara Josephs Cohen, and the entire family. May they be consoled among the mourners of Zion and may her memory be for a blessing. Joshua Matza President & CEO Burton P. Resnick Chairman of the Board Bobbie Goldstein Nat'l Campaign Chairman Warren Feldman Campaign Co-Chairman Bergen County Bernie Koster Campaign Co-Chairman Bergen County
