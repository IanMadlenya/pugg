
        GARVEY-John P., 71, New York City, died February 27, 1998, emphysema. USNR, 1944-1946, graduate of Yale University, 1952, BA. Survived by his children, John P., Jr., Lee (Lili) G. Carmel, Nathalie (Mimi) G. Mascolo, six grandchildren, and his sister, Natalie Scott Martin. Service will be private.
