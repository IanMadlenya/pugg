
        GINSBURG-Lillian. (aka Gainsburgh) Age 90. On March 17, 1997 after a short illness and after a long and fruitful life. Beloved sister of Ruth Harris of Toms River, N.J. Much loved aunt of ten nieces and nephews and great aunt of many grand nieces and grand nephews. Details about her future memorial service can be had by inquiring at her former address.
