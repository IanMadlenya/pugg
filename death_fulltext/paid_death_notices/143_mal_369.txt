
        FINKEL-Saul. The Young Israel of West Hempstead has suffered a great loss with the passing of Saul Finkel on Tuesday, December 29th, 1998. Mr. Finkel was a beloved member and an individual devoted to his Jewish heritage. He is survived by his wife Muriel and two sons, and is mourned by the entire community. Rabbi Yehuda Kelemer Mark Nathanson, President
