
        ADELMAN-Helen. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Helen Adelman, beloved mother of our esteemed member Stanley Adelman. Mr. Adelman is chair of the Data Processing Committee. Our deepest sympathies go to Stanley, his wife, Evelyn, and their sons, Eric, William, and Jason. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair and President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
