
        WILLIAMS-Craig Miller. Born in Mobile, AL, died in New York City on March 17, 1998. His laughter which rang like wind chimes and his smile as bright as the Noonday sun will live forever in our hearts. He is missed by wife Jacqueline, sister Martha, son Craig, Jr., mom Roberta, brother Mack, aunt Mary, co-workers of The New York Times, many relatives and friends in New York and Alabama
