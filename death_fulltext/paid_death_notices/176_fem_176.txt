
        JACOBS-WELSH-Deborah Ann. July 20, 1952-September 11, 2001. We remember our beloved Deborah Ann Jacobs-Welsh, who lost her life tragically while on duty on United Airlines Flight 93 on September 11, 2001. Deborah, of suburban Philadelphia, is survived by her husband Patrick Charles Welsh, her mother Lillian McBride Jacobs, brothers James, Kevin and Bryan, sister Eileen Jacobs Brady, their spouses, and nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her father, George William Jacobs and her brother, Bill Jacobs. A Memorial Mass will be held at St. Paul the Apostle R.C. Church, 9th Avenue at 59th St., New York City, on Saturday, September 22, 2001, at 7 PM. Memorial contributions may be made in her name to Catholic Charities, USA -Disaster Relief, P.O. Box 25168, Alexandria, VA. 22313.
