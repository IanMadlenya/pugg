
        BOWER--Nancy Milender, of Cambridge, MA, longtime resident of Lincoln, MA, died peacefully on September 19, 2006. She is survived by her husband Joseph Lyon Bower, her children Jonathan Bower and Deborah Brodheim and their children Eliza and Aaron Kano-Bower, and Adam, Hannah, and Max Brodheim, as well as her father Louis Milender. A great lover of music, dance, and the peace of Martha's Vineyard, she struggled successfully with illness for more than a decade. The funeral will be at Temple Israel, 477 Longwood Avenue, Boston, MA, on Thursday, September 21, at 1:30 pm. Parking available on The Riverway. The family will receive friends that evening from 5-8pm. A memorial service will be held later. Remembrances may be made to the New England Conservatory of Music, (Joseph & Nancy Bower Family Fund), 290 Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA 02115. Levine Chapels, Brookline, MA 617-277-8300
