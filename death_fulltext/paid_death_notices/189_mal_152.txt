
        OLDEN-Bert, 85. Of New York City, died in his home of a heart attack on October 10, 2002. His humor, generosity, integrity and adventuresome spirit made him a unique and specially loved man. His loss is mourned deeply by his daughters, Melanie and Dany, his relatives in California, and the friends who were also his beloved family. A memorial will be held on Monday, October 14 at Riverside Memorial, 76 St. and Amsterdam Avenue at 1:00 PM.
