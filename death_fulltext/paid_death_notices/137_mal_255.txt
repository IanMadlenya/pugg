
        FREEHILL-Paul. Suddenly on June 24th, 1998. Beloved son of Alice and James, dear brother of Laurene, Patrice, Allison, Bob, Janet, Joseph, Mary Lou, and the late Jimmy. Devoted grandson of Marion, loving uncle of Amanda, Neil, Jayne, Lynn, Jamie and Catherine. Survived by numerous aunts, uncles, in-laws, cousins, and dedicated friends. Committed and loyal employee of Audrey Cohen College. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, 81st St, Friday 2-5, and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass at St. Monica's Church, 413 East 79 St, Saturday 10AM. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. A memorial scholarship will be formed in his memory at the college.
