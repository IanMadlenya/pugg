
        STERN-Joan Shirley Engel. With deep sadness we record Joan's passing in her home on January 18th, in her 79th year, after a long illness. Loving wife of 40 years of Irving Stern; beloved mother of Anthony Marc Benjamin & Marian Lisa Lasday. Joan was a creative, vivacious, passionate woman, a loving and devoted friend who will be missed by all who knew her. She is survived by her husband, her son and daughter, her nephew Robert Engel, and her son-in-law David Lasday. Religious services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Tuesday, January 19th at 12 Noon.
