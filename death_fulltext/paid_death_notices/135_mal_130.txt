
        REILLY-Peter M. J. Age 84, of Singer Island, FL, formerly of Manhattan and Islip, NY, died of heart disease April 11, 1998. Husband of Valerie Clayton Reilly and the late Maureen McManus Reilly. He is survived by his children Peter M. J. Reilly, Jr., of Islip, NY, Mary Jo Essaye of Vero Beach, FL, Robert Reilly of Los Angeles, CA, and twelve grandchildren.
