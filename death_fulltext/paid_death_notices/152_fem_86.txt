
        FURTH-Dora. On September 8, 1999. One of the early female graduates of NYU Law School; an associate in the Estates and Trusts Department of Fried, Frank, Harris, Shiver and Jacobson from 1950 to 1984; after her retirement from that firm, associated with the Law Offices of Paul J. Schrag and Raymond V.J. Schrag for over 12 years. Perfectionist, consummate draftsperson, excellent teacher and dedicated counsellor. Many young attorneys benefited under her tutorlage and from her expertise.
