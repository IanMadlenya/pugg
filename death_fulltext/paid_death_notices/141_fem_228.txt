
        CALDERONE-Mary S. The faculty, students, alumni and staff of the Joseph L. Mailman School of Public Health of Columbia University mourn the loss of its distinguished alumna, Mary S. Calderone, MD, MS '42. A true visionary, co-founder of SIECUS, and indefatigable leader in sexual responsibility, Dr. Calderone has left a remarkable legacy to the health of the public. To her daughter, Francesca Calderone-Steichen and the entire family, we extend our deepest sympathy. Allan Rosenfield, MD Dean Joseph L. Mailman School of Public Health Columbia University
        CALDERONE-Dr. Mary S. The Board of Directors and staff of SIECUS mourn the passing of Mary S. Calderone, our co-founder and President, 1964-1982. The family requests that, in lieu of flowers, donations be made to SIECUS, 130 W. 42 St., New York, NY 10036. Debra W. Haffner, President SIECUS
        CALDERONE-Dr. Mary Steichen. The Planned Parenthood Federation of America family holds dear our memory of the life and work of our colleague, Dr. Mary Steichen Calderone, who taught us that accepting and understanding our human sexuality is essential for health and happiness. We celebrate Mary for her wise, brave and tireless efforts to protect our children from the dangers of sexual ignorance. We offer her family and friends our best wishes for happy and proud memories. Sharon Allison, Chairperson Gloria Feldt, President Planned Parenthood Federation of America
