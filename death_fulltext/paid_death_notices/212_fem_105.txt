
        CLAUS--Ruby M. Carlsbad, CA. Died on September 12, 2004 at Scripps Hospital in Encinitas California after a brief illness. She was 93 years of age. A long time resident of Chappaqua, NY, Mrs. Claus was the wife of Carl Claus (deceased) for 66 years. She was the daughter of the late Orton H. and Lois Miller (nee Parker) of Barberton, Ohio. She attended Mount Union College in Alliance, Ohio where she was a member of the Delta Delta Delta Sorority. In addition Mrs. Claus was a member of the Daughters of the American Revolution, an honorary member of the Whippoorwill Country Club in Armonk, NY, and a former member of the Junior League of Augusta, GA. She is survived by her son Carl M. Claus and two grandchildren Lois F. Claus and Carl R. Claus. Interment will be private. The family requests that, in lieu of flowers, memorial contributions be made to Scripps Hospital, 354 Santa Fe Drive, Encinitas, CA 92024.
