
        FEINMAN-Max L. On December 2, 1999 at age 94. Long-time physician, surgeon and teacher. He graduated from Long Island College of Medicine and served as a Captain in the U.S. Army during WWII; Chief of Surgery at Lefferts General Hospital, affiliated with Wycoff Heights Hospital and Doctors Hospital (Beth Israel Medical Center North), faculty member at the New School for Social Research and author of ''Protect Your Heart''. Long-time member of Brooklyn Jewish Center. Beloved husband of Sylvia (nee Mann). Loving father of Jeffrey and Richard. Devoted grandfather of Jessica and Robin. Services were private.
