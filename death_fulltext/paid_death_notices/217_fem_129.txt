
        WHITE -- Lillian Adelaide Windsor, passed away January 14, 2005 in Seattle, WA. Born in Brooklyn NY, October 7, 1906, she married Lindsay Hardy White in 1932. A graduate of Erasmus Hall HS and Teachers College-Columbia Univ., Lillian retired from the NYC school system in 1965. She lived and taught in Africa and visited Asia, Canada and the West Indies. Lillian dedicated her life to civic and volunteer responsibilities; her legacy continued when she settled in Seattle. She is survived by her daughter Linda Wilson, Seattle; son Lindsay Eric White, NYC; three grandchildren, Lindsay Wilson, Laurie Wilson & Eric Vishnu White, and four great-grandchildren. Memorial services will be held in NYC on February 19th, 3 PM, at The Riverside Church. In lieu of flowers, donations to Save the Children, 54 Wilton Road, Westport, CT. 06880 in memory of Lillian White.
