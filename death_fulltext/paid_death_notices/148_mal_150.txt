
        SALEEBY-James T., husband of Evelyn E. (nee Tahan); loving brother of Samir, June Pearson, & Patsy Stein. It is with deep sorrow that we announce the untimely death of James T. Saleeby, born in South Carolina, raised in Lebanon and later in Bay Ridge where he was a renowned figure. He will be missed by all who had contact with him. Viewing will be at McLaughlin & Sons, 9620 Third Avenue, Brooklyn, New York, on Sunday May 16, 1999 from 2 PM to 5 PM and 7 PM to 9 PM. Service will be at The Church of the Virgin Mary at 216 8th Ave. Brooklyn, NY Monday, May 17, 1999 at 10 AM. May he rest in peace.
