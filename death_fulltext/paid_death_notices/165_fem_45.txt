
        RODGERS-Terry (Lila) of Terry Rodgers Antique and Fine Jewelry of New York City passed away on Tuesday Morning, October 3, at approximately 10:30 AM, on a bright, beautiful, sunny day. Her adoring and adored husband Jesse was at her side. She is also survived by her beloved daughter Melody, grandson Cameron and son-in-law John. Terry, a remarkable woman, will be greatly missed by her loving family, cherished friends, fellow members of F.A., and all who had the great fortune to have known her. The Rodgers family resides in Englewood, New Jersey, and services will be conducted at Gutterman's Funeral Home in Hackensack, NJ at 12 noon on Thursday, October 5. Terry is now in magnificent company in her rightful place at the head of the Round Table.
