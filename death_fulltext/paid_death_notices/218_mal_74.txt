
        RAIMONDO -- Charles ''Chick,'' builder, businessman, civic leader, of Fort Lee and recently of Closter, at Englewood Hospital after a short illness. He was 72. Mr. Raimondo is survived by his wife Janice of fifty years and four children: Jacey of Wyckoff, Charles Jr. of Manhattan, Shari of Fort Lee, and Nancy of Closter. Also seven grandchildren: Madeline, Emily, Tyler, Charles, Eric, Gaia and Carlo, and two brothers: Frank of Tenafly and Arthur Pastore of Cape Cod. In 1955 Chick joined his father Carmelo in the family construction business founded in 1923 and grew it to one of the leading general construction companies in the New York-New Jersey Metropolitan area. Raimondo Construction was rated by Engineering News Record as one of the Top Ten builders of retail space in America serving a broad list of national retail clients. Funeral from the A.K. Macagna Funeral Home, 495 Anderson Ave., Cliffside Park, NJ, on Monday, March 7, 2005 at 9 AM, thence to Madonna RC Church, Fort Lee, NJ, where the Funeral Mass will be offered at 10 AM. Interment at Madonna Cemetery, Fort Lee. Visiting hours Saturday 7-9 PM and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM.
