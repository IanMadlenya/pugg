
        ROBERTSON--James Oliver. Beloved husband of Janet Cohen, loving father and grandfather, died at home in Hampton, CT, April 21, age 72 after a long battle with leukemia. Historian, poet, painter and author. Born in Fargo, North Dakota. A.B., A.M., Ph.D, Harvard. UConn history Professor until 1993. He loved his family, he loved to teach and he loved Hampton.
