
        MENDELSOHN-Lore (Hiller). 70, of New York City, on November 14th, 1999, after a short illness. Beloved wife of Paul and mother of Teri and Eric. Lore died in her home with her family at her side. We will miss her enthusiastic engagement with the world and the energy she brought to everything she set her hand to. In lieu of flowers, please send a donation to Learning Leaders, 352 Park Avenue South, NY, NY 10010, or Donation Department, American Cancer Society, 19 West 56 Street, New York, New York 10019-3984.
