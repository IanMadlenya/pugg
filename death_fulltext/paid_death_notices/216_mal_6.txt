
        SUSSMAN--David, CPA, 91 years old, of NYC and Woodridge, NY, on December 30th. He was preceded by his loving wife of 54 years, Doris Steinberg Sussman, and is survived by his children, Allan Sussman of Stamford, CT, Elaine Shafran of Buffalo Grove, IL, and five grandchildren. At his retirement he was senior partner of the accounting firm of Baumgarten and Sussman. Services Monday, 10 AM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Ave.
