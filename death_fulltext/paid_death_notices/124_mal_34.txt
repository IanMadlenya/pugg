
        WARSHAWSKY-Roy I. age 81; beloved husband of Sarita, nee Sherman; loving father of Ilene (Michael) Shaw and Carol (Michael Steinberg) Warshawsky; proud grandfather of Caroline and Elizabeth Shaw, Daniel, Andrew and James Steinberg; fond brother of Lowell Warshawsky, dear uncle of many nieces and nephews; devoted son of the late Nellie and Israel Warshawsky.
