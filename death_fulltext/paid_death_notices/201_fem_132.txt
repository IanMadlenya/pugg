
        SIEGEL--Ruth E. (nee Emden). Age 84, of Eastchester, died on October 23. Beloved wife of Frank C. Siegel. Devoted mother of Barbara, Jill and Monica. Cherished grandmother of Lisa, Jay, Jordan, Matthew, Jesse, Leigh, Josh and Allison. She was active in the community. Friends may gather at Westchester Funeral Home Inc., Eastchester on Sunday, October 26, starting at 12 Noon. Service at the funeral home at 1PM followed by interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla. Contributions may be made to the Eastchester Beautification Foundation or to The Association for Macular Diseases.
