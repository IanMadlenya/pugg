
        SONNENSCHEIN-Edith H. ''Lene''. On January 8, 1999 after a long life of 97 years. Beloved wife of the late Hans. Dear sister of the late Greta Kramer. Survived by a cousin, Francis Gordon of London, England. Cherished lifelong friend of the Wells and Weinreich families. Donations in her memory to Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York. Private services.
