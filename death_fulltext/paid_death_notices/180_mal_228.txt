
        EBERHARD-Leslie, 50. Died of complications from cancer, in Los Angeles, California on Saturday. He was a noted TV and theatrical writer. He wrote for many Saturday morning TV series, including Saved By The Bell, USA High and Malibu, CA. With collaborator David Levy, he wrote many theatrical musicals such as ''Jolson'', ''All Dressed Up'', ''Hey Ma'' and ''Jeckyl and Hyde'', (not the Broadway version), which is currently touring. He is survived by his mother, Theresa Eberhard of St. Louis, his sister, Joanne Kalemis and his longtime partner, actor Loren Freeman. A service will be held in St. Louis this week, and an additional memorial will be held in Los Angeles.
