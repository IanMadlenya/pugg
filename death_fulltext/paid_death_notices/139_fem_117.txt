
        UGELOW-Martha (Mae) (nee Tannenbaum), 87, beloved wife of the late Philip Ugelow, died Saturday, August 8, 1998 at home in Bloomfield, CT. Born in Poland, she immigrated to New York City where she resided until moving to Connecticut in 1997. She was the mother of Judith Ugelow Satlof, of West Hartford, CT., and Richard Ugelow, of Washington, DC, and the grandmother of Mark, Joshua & Phillip Satlof, Stewart and Philip Ugelow. Interment was in New Jersey. Memorial donations may be made to Fort Tryon Jewish Center, 524 Fort Washington Avenue, New York, NY 10033.
