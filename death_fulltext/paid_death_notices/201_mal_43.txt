
        ROSCHKO -- Joseph. Union Temple of Brooklyn mourns the loss of its beloved Honorary President, Joseph Roschko. For half a century, Joe was our trusted counselor, advisor and friend. He was a learned and devoted Jew. Our hearts are with his wife Zeva, and the entire Roschko family. Dr. Linda Henry Goodman Rabbi Dr. A. Stanley Dreyfus Rabbi Emeritus Mike Baron, President
