
        MORLEY--Margot Z. Of Larchmont, NY and Delray Beach, FL. on October 25, 2005 after a long illness at age 88. Born Marguerite Madeline Zimmer in Pittsburgh, PA to Ben and Nellie Zimmer who emigrated from Luxembourg to the US in 1913. Mrs. Morley lived in Larchmont for 83 years during which time she graduated from Rye Country Day School, class of 1934 and Briarcliff Junior College, class of 1936. Wife of the late James T. ''Bud'' Morley (1913-1998). Survived by Karen Brennan of Salt Lake City, UT, Mary Pat Ware (Peter) of Tucson, AZ, William J. Morley (Suzanne) of Larchmont, NY and James T. Morley, Jr. (Marcia) of Newtown, CT, 11 grandchildren and 7 great grandchildren. Mrs. Morley was an active member of the Ladies of Charity, sponsored by the Archdiocese of NY. Visiting Thursday 3-8pm at John J. Fox Funeral Home, Inc. in Larchmont. Mass Friday 10:30am at St. Augustine Church in Larchmont. Interment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Augustine Church in Larchmont or the Padre Pio Shelter for Homeless Men c/o Fr. Benedict Groeschel, Box 55, Larchmont, NY 10538.
