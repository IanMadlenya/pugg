
        MILLMAN--Denise (nee Fruchtman), 60, on Monday January 29, 2007 at Mt. Sinai Hospital. She was a resident of Teaneck, NJ, and is survived by daughter Natasha Millman Fitterman and sonin-law David, brother Irving Fruchtman, sister Joyce Kupfer and predeceased by brother Jack Fruchtman. Services were held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel on January 31, 2007. Burial was at Old Montefiore Cemetery in Springfield Gardens, NY.
