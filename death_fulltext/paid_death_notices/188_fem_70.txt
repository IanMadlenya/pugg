
        SILVERSTEIN - Gloria. On September 5. Beloved wife of the late George Silverstein. Devoted sister, aunt and friend to many. Gloria was a former model and entertainer who was devoted to many charitable organizations. She was most proud of her work for The Lighthouse and the Sutton area community. Family will receive friends on Tuesday, 2-4PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Funeral services Wednesday, 1PM at Frank E. Campbell.
