
        BAILEY--John M. Former Chief State's Attorney for the State of Connecticut. Husband of Elizabeth D. Bailey. Passed away peacefully at home Monday, September 22nd. Survivors include his two sons, John and Brian, his three sisters, Barbara, Louise and Judith. Friends may call at Ahern Funeral Home, 180 Farmington Ave., Hartford, CT, Wednesday, 4-8 PM. Funeral Mass on Thursday, 10 AM, Cathedral of St. Joseph, Hartford, CT.
