
        MORRISON-Ian A. Age 77, died November 16, in Bradenton, FL. Former Dean of Men, Wagner College, Staten Island, NY. Retired CEO and President of GreerWoodycrest Children's Services, Millbrook, NY. World War II veteran, ex-POW. Survived by wife Naida, son Craig W. of Sarasota, FL, daughter Sheila N. Jackson of New Braunfels, TX, and three grandchildren. Career devoted to work on behalf of children; loved and admired by all who knew him.
