
        SMEAL-Mary Jean. The National Center for Disability Services mourns the passing of Mary Jean Smeal, a much respected supporter of programs for people with disabilities. Her efforts leave a lasting legacy that will improve the lives of countless future generations. The National Center for Disability Services expresses their heartfelt condolences to her husband Frank, her children, grandchildren, family and friends. Edmund L. Cortez, President and CEO and the Board of Directors, National Center for Disability Services
