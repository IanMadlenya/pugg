
        CASSEL--Milton Edward. 95 years old, died September 4, 2004, in New York. Milton was a distinguished musician and a graduate of Juilliard in January of 1929. Mr. Cassel had been President of the Rita Allen Foundation for many years and had been a strong advocate for medical research. Milton was a former member of the New York Stock Exchange. Mr. Cassel is survived by his wife Lucette Cassel.
