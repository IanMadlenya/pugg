
        FINGEROTH - Ethan Shepard. Suddenly on July 13, 1997. Beloved husband of Barbara. Devoted father of Stephanie, Justin and Michael. Loving son of Blanche and the late Cantor Robert. Dear brother of James and Patricia and Daniel and Jaclyn. Loving uncle of Robert. Dear brother-in-law of Arlene Gebber. The sweetest, nicest, most wonderful person. He was there when we needed him. He inspired us all and he will be sorely missed. Services Wednesday, 12 noon, Temple Beth Shalom, 760 Route 6, Mahopac, New York. For information call Plaza Memorial Chapel at 212-769-4400.
        FINGEROTH-Ethan. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Ethan Fingeroth, beloved brother of our devoted members James and Patricia Fingeroth. Sincere condolences to the entire family.
