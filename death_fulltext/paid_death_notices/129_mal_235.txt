
        GOIZUETA-Roberto C. The leadership and Entertainment, Media & Communications division of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Roberto C. Goizueta, chairman and chief executive of the Coca-Cola Company. His fierce dedication to the company strengthened its global dominance, making it one of the most successful firms in corporate history. Less known are his many educational and philanthropic contributions. His wit, warmth, loyalty and kindness will be missed by his employees, friends, and family. To his wife, Olga, his two children and his eight grandchildren, we send our heartfelt condolences. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Edward Bleier & Marty Pompadur, Co-Chairs, Entertainment, Media & Communications Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
