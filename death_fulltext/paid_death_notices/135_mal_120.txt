        RUBACK-Robert I. The Pace University community mourns the passing of Robert I. Ruback, General Counsel and University Secretary, who passed away suddenly on April 10. Bob served Pace with distinction for over five decades, beginning as an adjunct professor, building the Department of Taxation and serving as its chair for over thirty years. He was named University Counsel in 1975. Our condolences are extended to his wife, Ruth, his daughters, Karen Merson and Marsha Ruback, granddaughters Erica and Dana and his son-in-law Mark A. Merson. Services will be held at noon on Monday, April 13, Gutterman's, Inc. 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Ruth and Robert Ruback Scholarship Fund Pace University. Carl H. Pforzheimer III, Chairman, Board of Trustees Patricia O. Ewers, President
