
        FOWLER-Dorothy Ganfield. The History Department of Hunter College regrets the death of Dorothy Ganfield Fowler, our colleague and friend. A respected scholar in United States history whose books include studies of the Postmasters General, sensorship in the post office and the First Presbyterian Church of New York. A teacher who inspired two generations of students, the Chair of the History Department, an administrator of rare widsom and skill, she was a member of the Hunter faculty for over 40 years.
