
        KATZ-Rabbi Aaron. Yeshiva University and its affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary mourn the passing of a highly respected rabbinic scholar, a dayan in Jerusalem. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Chana; children; brothers, Dr. Abraham Katsh, Rabbi Dr. Leon Katz-dedicated alumnus and honorary degree recipient who is a YU Guardian and founder of the Rabbi Dr. and Mrs. Leon Katz Professorship in Rabbinics at RIETS-and Rabbi Dr. Michael Katz-devoted alumnus and rosh yeshiva at RIETS-grandchildren, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees Judah Feinerman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees
