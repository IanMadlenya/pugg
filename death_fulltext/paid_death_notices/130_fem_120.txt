
        SEVASTOPOULO-Elisabeth. Died on Thursday, November 18 at the home of her daughter and son-in-law in Mauchline, Ayrshire, Scotland. She was born Princess Cantacuzene, Countess Speransky in 1911 in St. Petersburg, Russia. Elisabeth Sevastopoulo is survived by her son, Dimitri Sevastopoulo, her daughter, Viscountess Weir, two grandsons, Andrew Weir and Marc Sevastopoulo, and her brother, Prince Michel Cantacuzene, Count Speransky. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, December 4th at 6:30 PM at St. Nicholas Cathedral, 17 East 97th Street in Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to: The Tolstoy Foundation, 104 Lake Road, Valley Cottage, NY 10989.
