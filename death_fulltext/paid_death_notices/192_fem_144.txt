
        ARMOUR-Alexandra Lester. Beloved daughter of Julia Henry Armour of Coconut Grove, FL died in Honolulu, HI. on January 4, 2003, aged 56 after a long illness. She is also survived by her brother Philip D. Armour III of Kaneohe, HI, her sister Julia Armour Walker of Washington, CT, four nephews, one niece, two great nephews and one great niece. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Muscular Dystrophy Association, P.O. Box 78342, Phoenix, AZ 85062-8342 or on line at www.mdausa.org
