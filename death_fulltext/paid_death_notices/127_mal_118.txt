
        ANDREI-Jean, 71, on August 11, after a long and valiant struggle with cancer. Beloved lifelong companion of William C. Brown, he is also survived by his mother, Josette Andrei, of Nice, France. The lives of his many friends will be forever diminished by the loss of this bright, talented, generous spirit. Donations to the American Cancer Society or Cabrini Hospice Care in lieu of flowers.
