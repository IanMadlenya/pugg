
        TRAU--Peggy. Peacefully, at age 93, on Saturday, August 6. Widow of Fillip. Beloved aunt of Fritzi and Herbert Owens and Ilse and Curtis Hecht. She was a great aunt to Robert, Ruth and Mark Owens, as well as Richard Hecht and their children. A Holocaust survivor from Poland, she escaped to America with her adored sister Lorca and started a new life. She never forgot, nor let us forget. She was a dedicated supporter of many charities. She was loved and will be missed. Donations in her memory may be made to Hadassah.
