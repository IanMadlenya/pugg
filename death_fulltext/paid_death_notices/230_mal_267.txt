
        COLEY - Mortimer, 97. Peacefully at his home in Bronxville, on March 19, 2006. Loving husband of Esther Gussaroff Coley. Father of Dorothy Coley. Grandfather of Lara Antonucci, great grandfather of four. He will be sorely missed by all. Graveside service at Mount Hope Cemetery, Hastings, on Thursday, March 23 at 1 PM.
