
        CARPINO-Dr. Joseph J. Died August 4, 1998. Emeritus Professor of Philosophy, Saint Francis College, Brooklyn, New York, where he taught for over thirty-five years. He was a master of dialogues and wit, gratefully remembered by his colleagues and students for his devotion to helping them say and write what they really meant. Frank J. Macchiarola, Pres. Kevin C. Fogarty, Chairman
