
        KIRSCHNER-Roselyn. 78, of Coconut Creek, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, passed away on June 13th, 2002 after a prolonged illness. She will always be remembered as a paragon of strength, grace, and love. She is survived by her adoring husband, Dr. Stephen A. Kirschner; loving children: Mitchell Kirschner, Susan Shank, Joan Kirschner; cherished grandchildren: Britt, Dara, Jill, Daniel, Tracy, and Eric. Chapel services on Sunday, June 16, 2002 at 12:45 PM, Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, 5808 West Atlantic Avenue, Delray Beach, FL. Interment at Eternal Light Cemetery, Boynton Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, donations to Pancreatic Cancer Action Network.
