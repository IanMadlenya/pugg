
        SMALL-I. Maxwell. Died on December 15 at the age of 91. A practicing lawyer until the age of 90, Max is survived by his loving wife Norma, devoted children Jeffrey Small and Rise Dimson, grandchildren Denise and Yoav Rekem, Lara and Aviv Lawrence, Stuart and Susan Dimson, David and Alyssa Small, and great-grandchildren Shash, Oak, Tali, Tsivi, Yonatan and Itai. He is also survived by his sister Edith Berman and brothers Joe, Hy and Charles Small. He will always be remembered for his devotion to his family and the beautiful music he brought into all of our lives. We will miss him. Services today at 12 noon at the ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel,'' corner of 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
