
        ADLER-Dr. Alexandra. Died on January 4, 2001 at age 99. She was a brilliant researcher, neurologist & psychiatrist, and the daughter of Alfred Adler, one of the founders of modern psychiatry. She was one of the first women neurologists at Harvard Medical School. Beloved aunt of Margot Adler and great-aunt of Alexander Gliedman-Adler. We will miss your astute clinical wisdom, your deep sweetness and love and your pithy one-liners.
