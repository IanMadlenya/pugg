
        RAICER--Julius B., 87. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, friend and colleague, Julius died peacefully at his home in Morristown, NJ on January 9, 2007, surrounded by family. Julius was a WWII Veteran, President of Dover Furniture, partner in Whippany Manor, and active in commercial real estate development. Julius is survived by sons Robert and Alan, daughter Leslie, and grandchildren, Mei-An, Geoffrey, and Alex. His wife of 56 years, Helen DeLello Raicer, died in 2002. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Julius B. Raicer Memorial Fund, c/o Leslie Raicer, 13 Atilda Ave., Dobbs Ferry, NY 10522
