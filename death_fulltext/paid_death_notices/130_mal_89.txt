
        CONNOLLY-Kevin Joseph, 63, husband of Elizabeth Fraser Connolly, of Fairfield, CT., died Thursday, November 6, 1997, in Bridgeport, CT. Born in NYC, Mr. Connolly was a graduate of Fordham University and the Bernard Baruch School of Business. He was a former resident of Shrewsbury, NJ prior to relocating to Fairfield 14 years ago. He was a V.P. of Human Resources for Tetley/Allied North America prior to his retirement. In addition to his wife, he is survived by one son, Dr. Kevin J. Connolly, Jr. of Newbury, VT; two daughters, Elizabeth Dell of Fair Haven, NJ and Suzanne Lynch of Marietta, GA; three sisters, Anne Schrager of Rensselaerville, NY, Mary Burnett of New Hyde Park, NY and Joan Loughran of Queens Village, NY and six grandchildren. A Mass will take place on Monday at St. Pius X Church. Friends may call on Sunday from 2-4 and 6-8 p.m. at the Shaughnessey Funeral Home, 50 Reef Road, Fairfield, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to Catholic Relief Services, P.O. Box 17090, Baltimore, MD. 21203-7090.
