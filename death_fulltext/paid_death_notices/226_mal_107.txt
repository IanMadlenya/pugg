
        THOMPSON--Gerard ''Gerry'', 87. Of Hilton Head Island, S.C., died November 1. He served in World War II in the Army Air Force. He was formerly Executive Vice President in charge of Financial Services for Wall Street Division of Chemical Bank. Survived by his wife Barbara; children Gerard, Jr., Philip, Molly, Melanie Fauchet and Bethlyn Proft; and 21 grandchildren. Funeral services will be November 11 at 12 noon at Providence Presbyterian Church, Hilton Head Isl., SC. Memorials to The Hilton Head Island Military Veterans Coalition, c/o Community Foundation of the Lowcountry, Inc. or a charity of your choice. The Island Funeral Home for information.
