
        KLAR--Henry. The Brooklyn Law School community mourns the loss of Henry Klar, Class of 1938, an esteemed and devoted alumnus of the Law School, whose generous support helped establish the Klar Family Reading Lounge. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Gloria, his son, Steven, also a graduate of the Law School, his sons Lester and Ronald, and his brother, Edward. Joan G. Wexler, Dean Stuart Subotnick, Chairman of the Board of Trustees
