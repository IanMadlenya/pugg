
        HADLEY-Philip N. Of Danbury, CT., died June 20, 2001 at age of 73. Devoted husband of the late Flora Werner Hadley. His warmth, grace and generosity will long be remembered by family & friends. Services at the Hull Funeral Home of Danbury, CT. at 3 P.M., on Sunday, June 24th. Interment is at 2 PM Monday, June 25th, Pine Grove Cemetery, Templeton, MA. He will be missed.
