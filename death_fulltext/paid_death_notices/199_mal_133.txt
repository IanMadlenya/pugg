
        SLOAN--Walter Forbes, age 91, died on August 5, 2003 of cancer. He retired as General Tax Counsel of Caltex in 1977. Beloved husband of Margaret O'Brien Sloan for 64 years, loving father of Tina Sloan McPherson, John Sloan, and the late Margo Sloan Pearson. He leaves six grandchildren. Born in Niles, Michigan, he was a graduate of the University of Illinois, and of Harvard Law and Business Schools. He served in World War II as a Lieutenant in the US Naval Reserve in the Mediterranean. He lived for 65 years in Bronxville, NY and was a member of the Bronxville Field Club and The Siwanoy Country Club where he served on the Board of Governors and as Secretary of the Board. A Memorial service will be held on Monday, August 18 at 11:00 AM in the Chapel at The Osborne, 101 Theall Road, Rye, NY.
