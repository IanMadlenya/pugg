
        PITKIEWICZ-Rev. Vincent F. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the Pastor Emeritus of St. Joseph's Church, 108-43 Sutphin Blvd., Jamaica, on Aug 2. Reposing at Leahy-McDonald Funeral Home, 111-02 Atlantic Ave., Richmond Hill, Monday, Aug 4, from 2 to 4:30 and 7 to 9pm, Tuesday, Aug. 5, from 2 to 4:30pm. Mass of Transferral in St. Joseph's Church at 7:30pm. Funeral Mass Wednesday, Aug. 6, at 10:30am. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
