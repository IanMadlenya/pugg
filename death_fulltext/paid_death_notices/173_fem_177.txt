
        SLOAN-Madeline D. (''Dolores''). Of Pelham, NY on June 14. Born in Brooklyn on June 16, 1925, she married Edward P. Sloan (d. 1970) in 1949 and lovingly raised their children, Edward, Peter, Pamela and Laura in St. Catherine of Genoa RC parish. She moved to Pelham in 1989 where, as ''Grammy'', she happily cared for her grandchildren and their friends. She is survived by her children, their spouses and nine grandchildren who ask that anyone wishing to honor her life make a contribution to Angela House, Inc. 3323 Steuben Ave, Bronx, NY 10467, a home for single mothers and their children.
