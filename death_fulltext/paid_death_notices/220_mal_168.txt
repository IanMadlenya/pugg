
        RABSTENEK -- Tom. The Manhattan Soccer Club mourns the passing of our founding President and esteemed colleague, Tom Rabstenek. Tom inspired us to establish a developmental soccer program that benefits thousands of children. His acumen and sage counsel will be missed. Our heartfelt sympathy and love go out to his wife, Diane, his children, Thomas and Christine, and his father, Thomas.
