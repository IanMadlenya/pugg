
        GOTTLIEB-Leo. Died April 11, 2001 at 84 years of age in Deerfield Beach, FL following surgery for colon cancer. Funeral was April 15th at Star of David Cemetery in N. Lauderdale, FL. His life was one of fearless fun, loved by all, now it's done. He is survived by his sons, percussionist Gordon, pianist Jay, his first wife, Florence Sloan, his cousin Norman, his 2nd. wife of 41 years, Edith, her children Linda, Steven & Maris, and their children.
