
        RITTS-Herb. The Board of Directors and the staff of the American Foundation for AIDS Research (amfAR) mourn the loss of our valued friend and supporter Herb Ritts. This internationally acclaimed photographer with the unique ability to capture the human spirit leaves us with enduring images of style and simplicity. We at amfAR greatly appreciated his special talent and valued his generosity over the years in the fight against AIDS. We express our condolences to his partner Erik Hyman and to his entire family. Mathilde Krim, Ph.D., Founding Chairman and Chairman of the Board Jerome J. Radwin, CEO Board of Directors and Staff amfAR
