
        FORREST-Rochelle. Age 80. Passed away suddenly on August 3rd in Deerfield Beach, Florida (formerly of White Plains). She was the beloved wife of the late and cherished Norman; devoted mother of Robert and Juliet and dear sister of Muriel. A New York memorial service is planned some time in September. Her luminous spirit will shine forever in our hearts and in the minds of all who knew her.
