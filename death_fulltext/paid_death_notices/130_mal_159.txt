
        BATIUCHOK-William, M.D. On November 14, 1997. Born May 26, 1920. Beloved and devoted husband of Margaret. Loving father of John and his wife Louisa, Margaret and Susan. Devoted grandfather of Daniel and Beth. Graduate of Columbia College '41, Cornell University Medical College '44, Emeritus Director Thoracic Surgery of Wyckoff Heights Hospital and Flushing Hospital. Secretary, Board of Trustees Wyckoff Heights Hospital; past President of Medical Board Flushing Hospital; past President and current Member, Board of Trustees, Garden School. Reposing in Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd, Flushing. Visiting hours Sunday and Monday 2-5 and 7-10 PM. Service Monday, 7:30 PM. Funeral Tuesday, 10 AM. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery. Donations to Wyckoff Heights Hospital, Flushing Hospital, Columbia College '41 or Garden School.
