
        KATCHER--Arthur. Died February 20, 2003, one year ago, at the age of 96. Devoted husband for 65 years to his wife Evelyn, and caring father to his children, grandchildren, and great - grandchildren. Manufacturer of paper boxes for 80 years, he was president of Paragon Paper Box Corp. On this first anniversary we all remember you. His widow resides at 70-25 Yellowstone Blvd, Forest Hills NY 11375
