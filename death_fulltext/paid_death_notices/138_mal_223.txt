
        KIVLEHAN-Denis, 66. Of Orangeburg, died on July 19, 1998 at his home. Mr. Kivlehan was born on July 4th, 1932 in Tubbercurry, County Sligo, Ireland, to Thomas and Bridget Kivlehan. He was married to Brigid (Della) Oates. A resident of Orangeburg since 1962, he formerly lived in the Bronx. Mr. Kivlehan formerly worked as a national school teacher in Ireland from 1954 to 1962; he was a loan department clerk for Morgan Guarantee Trust Company in New York from 1962 to 1987 and worked as a paper assembler for the New York Times in Edison, NJ, from 1987 to 1997. He was a parishioner of St. Catharine's R.C. Church in Blauvelt. Survived by his wife Brigid (Della), daughters Maureen Labarbera of Waldorf, MD., and Patricia Dougherty of Glenside, PA., and his son Thomas of Hellertown, PA.; sisters Theresa Davey of Blauvelt and Margaret Kelly of the Bronx; a brother Martin of Blauvelt, and three grandchildren. Wake today, 2-4, 7-9PM, Moritz Funeral Home, Tappan, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday 10AM, St. Catherine's R.C. Church, Blauvelt. Burial follows in St. Anthony's Cemetery, Nanuet. Memorial donations may be made to the United Hospice of Rockland, New York.
