
        MAHONY--Grace M. (nee Sullivan). Died peacefully on September 6, 2003, after a short illness at Lenox Hill Hospital. Age 95. Beloved wife of the late Paul A. Mahony. Devoted mother of Paul A. Mahony, his wife, Mary, Sheila A. Mahony, her husband Charles Riggs, Robert E. Mahony, his wife Christina, and the late Peter Mahony. Loving grandmother of Patrick Mahony, Matthew Mahony, Megan McFarlin and Nora Mahony, Charles Riggs III and Julia Riggs Shultis, and great-grandmother of eight. Family will receive friends Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 10 A.M. at St. Ignatius Loyola R.C. Church, Park Ave at 84th St. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
