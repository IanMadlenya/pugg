
        STRANGE-Randall S., 48. Of Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, formerly of New York City and Virginia, died on Thursday, September 24, 1998, after a long illness. Employed as Senior Litigation Counsel for W.R. Grace and Co., and graduate of the University of Virginia Law School. Devoted companion to the late Hugh Docker. Survived by his parents, Forrest and Ruth Strange; sister, Debra Seale; nephew, Damien Seale; and his close friends, Larry Smart, Lance Shaw, and Jim Weingarten. Burial will be in Topeka, Kansas.
