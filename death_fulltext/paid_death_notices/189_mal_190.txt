
        PHILLIPS-Ronald H. Of New Orleans, LA. Formerly of New York City, died on October 10, 2002. He was 70 years old. Ron was the General Manager of the Fountain Bay Club in New Orleans and an executive at CBS, ITC and Columbia Screen Gems in New York City. He is survived by his children Christopher and Pamela. We will miss his humor, his love and his smile. At Ron's request there will be no services but donations can be made to The American Diabetes Association.
