
        SCHULTHEIS-Dr. Daniel. of Manhasset and Shelter Island, NY. on May 16, 2000. Pioneer in the field of Dermatology. Beloved husband of Anne (nee Prager). Devoted father of George, Hilary, Lesley, Jennifer and John. Loving fatherin-law of Ronald and grandfather of Zachary and Valyrea. Dear brother of Mary Louise Gallo and Jeannie Brechter. Dr. Schultheis, a graduate of the Lawrenceville School, Cornell University, and the Long Island College of Medicine. He was on the staff of Flushing Hospital and St. Francis Hospital. He served his country as Captain and Flight Surgeon from 1948-1950. Dr. Schultheis was an avid sailor, member and Fleet Surgeon of Manhasset Bay Yacht Club, member of Shelter Island Yacht Club and Royal Bermuda Yacht Club. He will be greatly missed by his loving family, friends, patients, and colleagues. Memorial services to be announced.
