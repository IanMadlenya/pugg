
        FASTENBERG-Vera, age 91. Beloved daughter of the late Dorrothy and Hyman Fastenberg. Sister of Eleanor Miller, Arnold, Irwin, Sanford and the late Joseph Forster. For 40 years a teacher at PS 48, the Bronx. Vera also had a profound influence on her siblings, nieces and nephews, all of whom she loved dearly. Graveside services Wednesday, November 19, 11am at Mt. Hebron Cemetery.
