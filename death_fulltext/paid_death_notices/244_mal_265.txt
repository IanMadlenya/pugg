
        BILLIK--Gene, of Livingston, NJ, and Boca Raton, FL, died at home on May 29, 2007, after a five year battle with cancer. Devoted husband of the late Marilyn Billik. Born January 1, 1927 in Flushing, NY, attended Storm King School and Brown University. He began his long career in the spirits industry with Popper Morson and later became a VP at William Grant & Sons. He is survived by son Buzz (Beth) Billik, daughter Nancy (Ken) Weinrib and grandsons, Britton, Cameron and Cole Billik, and Craig, Doug and Miles Weinrib. Funeral Service will be held at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ on May 31st at 2:30pm. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (Fred's Team), PO Box 27106, NY, NY 10087.
