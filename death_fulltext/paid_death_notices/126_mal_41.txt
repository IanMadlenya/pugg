
        SCHWARTZ-Robert S., of New York City, died on June 28, 1997 at age 69 due to complications following heart surgery after a long and valiant struggle. Devoted husband of Mary. Father of David and Matthew. Beloved family man, grandfather of three and friend to all. He leaves a large hole in the lives of everyone he knew. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to the Zoot Sims Scholarship Fund at The New School 66 West 12th Street, New York, NY would be appreciated. Services were private and a Memorial service will be held at a later date. J.S. Waterman & Sons-EastmanWaring Funeral Service, Boston, Massachusetts.
