
        FOX--Ralph, age 87. Passed away March 24, 2007. Born and raised in NY, NY. He lived in Monroe Township, NJ, for the last 15 years. Ralph is survived by his loving wife Dinah of 60 years, a daughter Stephanie Morris (Robert) of NY, NY; two sons, Daniel (Elaine) of Rockville, MD, and Lawrence (Laura) of Pleasantville, NY; five grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. Graveside services will be held Monday, March 26 at 1 pm, New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Donations may be made to The Challah Fund Foundation, 205 Easton Ave, New Brunswick, NJ 08901.
