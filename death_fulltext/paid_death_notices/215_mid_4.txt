
        BRIZELL -- Murray J. ''Breeze'' On December 3, 2004. Beloved husband of Harriet. Devoted father of Hillary Brizell-DeLise and her husband Joseph, and Vicki Dellon and her husband Scott. Cherished grandfather of Allison DeLise, David Dellon, Jonathan DeLise and Kate Dellon. A decorated veteran of WW II who will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Graveside service Sunday, December 5th, 10:30AM at United Hebrew Cemetery, Staten Isand N.Y. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
