
        EPSTEIN-Louis H. Of Plantation, FL. Died peacefully on Monday, September 25, 2000 at the age of 93. Beloved husband of the late Ruth Jacobs. Loving father of Myrna Algus and son-in-law Dr. Seymour Algus of Rockville Centre, N.Y. Loving grandfather of Dr. Jane Algus, and Dr. Mitchell Algus and wife Dyanne Maue. Anavid sportsman, Mr. Epstein continued to play racquetball well intohis 93rd year. He was much loved and will be greatly missed. Funeral services will be held at Gutterman's Funeral Home, Rockville Center, N.Y., Wednesday, September 27th, 12 Noon. The family will observe shiva at the home of Dr. and Mrs. Seymour Algus, 39 Milburn St, Rockville Centre, N.Y. Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Sunday and Monday.
