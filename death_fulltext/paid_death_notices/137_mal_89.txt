
        ROSENBACH-Simon. Of Rego Park, NY, Tuesday, June 9, 1998. Husband and best friend of Naomi Rosenbach. Father of Willa Rosenbach Morris and Dean Rosenbach, father-in-law of Eric and Ellen, grandfather of Jason and Eden Morris and Jillian and Marshall Rosenbach. He is also survived by four brothers. An auctioneer who never stopped working, Shim was larger than life, warm, funny, devoted, unforgettable, and loved by all who knew him. His memory will keep the world vibrant. Funeral Wednesday, 2PM, at Parkside, 66th Avenue and Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
