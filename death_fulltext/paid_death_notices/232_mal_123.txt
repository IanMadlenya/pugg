
        STORCH--Allen. Age 85, of Palm Coast, FL. A wonderful husband of 60 years and a devoted and caring father. In a life of accomplishment, he was most proud of his children and grandchildren. He was President of his class at St. John's, and remained a keen supporter of that institution long after graduation. During the Second World War he served as a naval officer in the South Pacific. He was captain of a Subchaser and later served as captain of a Destroyer Escort until April, 1946. Should friends desire, contributions may be made to Disabled War Veterans. Service will be private.
