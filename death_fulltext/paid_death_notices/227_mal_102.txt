
        KORYN--Andrew, M.D. Age 74, on December 2 in La Jolla, CA. Born 1931 in Amsterdam. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Devoted and loving father of Ramona and Daniel. Dear brother of Ted Koryn. He was very much loved by his family and friends. Prior to retirement he practiced medicine in La Jolla, CA. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him.
