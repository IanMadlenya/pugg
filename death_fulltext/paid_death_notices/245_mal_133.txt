
        WEBER--Martin J. died at home on June 9, 2007 at the age of 102. Born in New York City on March 7, 1905, son of Rebecca and Philip Weiner. He is survived by Naomi, his wife of 48 years, his children Ellen, Carl, and Joy Weber and Marilyn Marsden, 10 grandchildren, and 15 greatgrandchildren. His first wife Natalie pre-deceased him in 1956. He was well known in the field of graphic arts as an innovator and pioneer
