
        SINGER-Morris. Age 89. Beloved husband of the late Thelma Keller Singer. Devoted father of Charles and Peter, grandfather of Mark, Todd, Guy and Samantha. Loving son of Hyman and Fanny. Brother of Ann and the late Molly, Harry, Irving and Leonard. Caring and loyal to countless friends, he inspired affection in all who met him. He will be deeply missed but lovingly remembered.
