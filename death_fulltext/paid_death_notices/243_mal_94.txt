
        VOLK--Herbert, M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is saddened by the passing of Dr. Herbert Volk, a member of Einstein's founding faculty in the Department of Surgery. He was a valued member of the Einstein community from 1955 to 1991. We extend our condolences to his wife, Irma, and the entire Volk family. Allen M. Spiegel, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
