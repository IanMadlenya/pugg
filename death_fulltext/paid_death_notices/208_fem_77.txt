
        ZUKERMAN--Jennie. Age 97. Passed away in her sleep after a short illness at home in North Miami Beach on May 8, 2004. She now will join her husband of 45 years, William, who predeceased her. A woman of grace passed away with those who loved her around her. A family encompassing five generations will miss one that they loved greatly, a wife, a mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, and great-greatgrandmother. The family will have a graveside service at Cedar Park, 1 pm, Monday, May 10. Shiva will be at 64 East 86th St, starting Tuesday, May 11.
