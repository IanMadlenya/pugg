
        BALLOCH-Mary Chase (Maisie). 80, died January 24, 2000. Beloved wife of (the late) Tony Balloch, who died in 1998. She is survived by her brothers: Seymour Howard of Paris, France and Macauley Howard of Washington, D.C; by her children: Patricia Tuff of Atlanta, GA, Joy Ahrens of Yarmouth, ME, Howard Balloch of Beijing, China, and Hugh Balloch of Darien, CT; sixteen grandchildren and one great-grandchild. A memorial service will be held this summer in Jamestown, RI. Donations may be made in her memory to the Alzheimer's Association, 163 Lancaster Street, Suite 160B, Portland, ME 04101.
