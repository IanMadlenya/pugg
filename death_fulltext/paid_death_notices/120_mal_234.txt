
        BERGER-Melvin. A pillar of strength and courage, a professor of understanding and kindness, a poet of love and compassion. Beloved father, husband, son and friend. Died January 20, at Long Island Jewish Hospital, after valiantly fighting a two year battle with cancer. He is survived by his loving wife, Amy, cherished children, Joshua and Matthew, his brothers, Michael and Howard, his ''sister'' Jane, his mother, Vita and his mother-in-law, Sylvia Sakelik. He will be mourned by a great number of family and friends who loved and adored him. Memorial services will be held at Port Jewish Center, Port Washington, NY, at 1:00 P.M., Wednesday, January 22. Contributions in his memory can be made to Home Away From Home, 601 Brookside Drive #
