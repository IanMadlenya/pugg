
        BENNETTE-George Gilbert. 70, on November 8, at his home in Fairlee, VT. Husband of Shirley Hersom, father of Stephen Page and Matthew Gilbert, and brother of Elizabeth Shackelford. Concert pianist and longtime Director of Arts and Leisure Education at the Lighthouse International, Manhattan. Memorial donations may be made to Guiding Eyes for the Blind, 611 Granite Springs Road, Yorktown Heights, NY 10598, or to a charity of your choice.
