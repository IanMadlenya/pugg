
        DEMBER-Jules J. Of Massapequa and Amityville. Devoted husband of the late, Muriel ''Mimi''. Loving father of Stuart, Anne Ryan, John and Allyne, Peggy and Gary Zucker, Larry and Jennifer. Cherished grandfather of Robert, Philip, Ian, Jasmine, Rebecca, Ryan and Liam. Adored brother of Phyllis and Abby Katzman. A proud veteran of World War II. He devoted 40 years to Slomin's Oil Company. He enjoyed the adventures of world travel with his wife. Services will be held Thursday, 11:30AM at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah. Memorial donations may be made to the Cancer Center at St. Francis Hospital, Trenton, NJ.
