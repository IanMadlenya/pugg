
        BRUST-Saul. Died Sept. 4, 1997. Beloved brother of Mary Ann Frieder and Norman Brust. Brother-in-law of Robert Frieder, uncle of Mark Frieder, Laura Darnell, Gina Frieder and Eric Darnell. Great-uncle of Lisa Frieder, Stephen Frieder, Rex Darnell, & Leah Darnell.''A truly gentle man.''
        BRUST-Saul. On September 4, 1997. A publisher when it was an occupation for gentlemen. A private man with a wide circle of friends who will remember him with great affection. ''Remembered laughter, remembered tears.''
