
        LYNTON--Harold S., 97, on June 11. Mourned by his wife Hattie, sons Stephen and Dick, granddaughters, and family. Formerly husband of the late Edith F. Lynton. Father of the late Andrew. Former partner in the law firm of Shea & Gould; Of Counsel to Dornbush Schaeffer Strongin & Venaglia. Memorial services to be arranged.
