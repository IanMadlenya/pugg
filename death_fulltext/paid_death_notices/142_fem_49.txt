
        MOORE-Louise Lane. Born May 29, 1898. Died peacefully September 9, 1998. She was married to the late Harry A. Moore. She was a past President of Phi Mu Fraternity, active in AAUW and Marble Church. Memorial service at Marble Church, 29th St and 5th Ave, New York City, November 7, 1998, 12:30PM. Contributions to Phi Mu Foundation or Marble Church.
