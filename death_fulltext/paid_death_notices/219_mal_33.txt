
        BASSUK--Sidney. 91, of Great Neck, NY, passed away peacefully Saturday night, April 2, 2005. He was surrounded by his loving family. Sid was a longtime CPA and business advisor, confidante, advisor and friend to many. Devoted husband of Selma for 40 years. Beloved father of Richard and Eslyn, Joan and Len, Howard and Sarah, Michael and Naomi, Roy and Nancy, and Paul and Liz. Loving ''Papa'' to Jon, Matt, Jeff, Sara, Adam, Seema, Doug and Ben. Dear brother of Irving, Edward, Arnold and the late Albert. Sid was an Executive Board member of the Pride of Judea Community Services and generous benefactor to many charities. Funeral services will be held at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY, on Monday, April 4, at 12 noon. Contributions in Sid's memory can be made to Memorial Sloane - Kettering Cancer Center or the JBFCS Pride of Judea. He was dearly loved and will be deeply missed.
        BASSUK--Sidney. With a sense of deep loss and profound sorrow, the Pride of Judea Community Services Division of Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services mourns the passing of Sidney Bassuk. A lifelong supporter of Pride of Judea and a member of Pride's Board for more than 20 years, Sidney's leadership and commitment to the agency's well being will be missed by all. Our love to his beloved Selma and the entire family. Jean L. Troubh President, JBFCS Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Exec. VP & CEO, JBFCS Jeffrey Bernstein Chair, Pride of Judea Division Shoshana Garber, Director
