
        WARD-Virginia B. Retired Manager for American Airlines died March 16, 1998 in Sloan-Kettering, NYC. Her career with American began in 1944. She trained reservations agents when computers were introduced. She retired in 1974. She graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.A. in Music in 1934. She founded a preschool with her brother MacKenzie in Chicago in 1934. She was born in Chicago March 22, 1913. Survivors include sister, Barbara Pare of Chicago and ten nieces and nephews. Family and friends will gather at Davis Pk., Fire Is. to celebrate Ginny's life in June.
