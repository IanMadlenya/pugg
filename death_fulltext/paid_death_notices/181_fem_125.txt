
        NEFF-Mary Alice. Age 88. Died February 4, 2002 in Manhattan, where she had resided since 1939, after a long illness. Survived by her husband, Richard B. Neff, and son, Richard B. Neff, Jr., of Palm Beach. Burial to be in Athens, GA. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Children's Health Service, 690 Amsterdam Avenue, New York, N.Y. 10025 would be appreciated.
