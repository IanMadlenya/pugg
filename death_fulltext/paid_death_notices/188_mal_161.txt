
        LUCKOM-Ben. 91. Died on September 14, 2002. Devoted to his family, he was the beloved husband for 61 years of Edith who died just one month ago; adored father of Bram Luckom and Fran Luckom - Nurnberg; dear brother of Irving Luckom; loved father-in-law of Debbie Luckom & Ronald Nurnberg; and cherished grandfather of Becky Mobly, Carolyn and Peter Nurnberg. You will be sorely missed. The remembrance of your comforting way and love will remain in our hearts forever. Funeral Services Wednesday, September 18th at 12:30PM at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave.
