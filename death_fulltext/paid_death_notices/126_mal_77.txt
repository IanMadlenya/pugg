
        LONG-Robert Lancaster. June 30, 1997, age 91. Resident of Manchester Center, VT., and formerly Rochester, N.Y. Loving husband of Mary Franchot Gorsline, who predeceased him in 1995. Survived by sons, Robert L., Jr. of Corrales, NM, and William Gorsline Long of Bronxville, NY, five grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
