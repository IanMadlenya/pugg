
        SCHULMAN -- Harold L. Passed away suddenly at home, in NYC, on Friday, November 26. He is deeply mourned by his beloved wife, whom he adored, Martha (nee Schier), and sons Michael and Robert. He is affectionately remembered by his friends and by his colleagues in the men's wear industry. His gentleness and love of life will always be with us. A private service will be held in celebration of his life.
