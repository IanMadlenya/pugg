
        HALPER--Arthur A. on January 4, 2005, age 90. A gentle soul and generous spirit has passed. Forsaking the limelight, he held the light so that others might shine. Loving husband of Evelyn, devoted father of Bonnie Cunningham (Joe) and Dee Fogel (Mark), adored grandfather of Jessica, Gaynor, J.D., Sofia and Rafael. Professor of economics, Brooklyn College; CPA. A memorial to be held in June.
