
        EPSTEIN-Kenneth J. After a courageous fight. Beloved husband of Doreen; devoted father of Karen, Ivy and Amy; loving grandfather of Emily and Tracy. His humor and sweetness will be sorely missed. Service Sunday, 12 noon at ''Gramercy Park Chapel'' 2nd Avenue between 9th and 10th Streets. Contributions may be made in his memory, to Gilda's Club, Houston Street.
