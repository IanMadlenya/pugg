
        LOUDON-Malcolm John, Wing Commander, DFC, on 9/20/97 in the UK at age 81. RAF, Battle of Britain, WWII 1935-1946. Mentioned in dispatches for distinguished service. POW at Stalag Luft III from 1944 to 1945. He moved to USA in 1946, where he married and had two children. He returned to the U.K. in 1988. He will be missed greatly by his two daughters, Gay Wells Loudon-King and Serena Ford Loudon, as well as by his friends.
