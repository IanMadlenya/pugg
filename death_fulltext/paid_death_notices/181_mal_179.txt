
        SHTULL-Jacob, Rabbi. We record with sorrow the passing of our revered colleague and friend, Rabbi Emeritus of Congregation Shaarey Tikvah, Beachwood, OH, whose community he served for 35 years. Our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Rita, to their family and to all who were blessed by his rabbinate. Rabbi Reuven Hammer, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP The Rabbinical Assembly
