
        DUBIN--Harold. Born March 26th, 1930, died peacefully at his home in New York City on May 21. Born in Boston, Harold was the youngest son of the late John and Mildred Dubin, brother to Maurice (Silver Spring, MD) and Joseph (La Jolla, CA). Harold was a graduate of Boston College and the Harvard Business School. He was a lifetime executive of Mobil Oil and a long time resident of New York City. With him at his time of death were his brother Maurice Dubin and nephew Ben Dubin. For more information: Beth Abraham Memorial Chapel.
