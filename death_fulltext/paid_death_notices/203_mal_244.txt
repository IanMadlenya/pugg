
        LOWENSTEIN--Julius, on December 17, 2003; born in Germany, October 16, 1914; came to America in 1937; served in the army from 1942 to 1946; owner of Midtown Packing since 1946. Survived by his beautiful wife Ruth of 62 years; two daughters, Marilyn Blumenthal (married to the late Stephen Blumenthal) and Vivian Daniel (married to Fred Daniel); a sister Selma; five married grandchildren, nine great-grandchildren. He was very charitable and loved by many. He will be missed.
