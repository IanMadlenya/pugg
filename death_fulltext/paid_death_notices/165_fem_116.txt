
        CAPLAN-Deborah. Age 69, died in NYC on October 8. The wife of H. Lawrence King and the mother of Aaron and Leah Caplan, she is survived by them and by her brothers, Thomas, Jonathan, and Timothy Frank, and numerous nephews and nieces. A distinguished teacher and practitioner of physical therapy and the Alexander Technique, author of numerous articles, a book ''Back Trouble'', and the video tape ''Solutions for Back Trouble.'' Burial Thursday, October 12, 1:30 PM, at Mt Pleasant Cemetery, Valhalla, N.Y. Memorial services same day at 5 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St at Amsterdam Ave.
