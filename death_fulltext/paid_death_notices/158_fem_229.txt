
        HOUCK-Louise J. Booth. On March 25, 2000, suddenly at home in Sarasota, FL after a long life of giving friendship and joy to others. Born November 7, 1911, she was a graduate of Carnegie Institute of Technology and loving wife of the late Howard W. Booth and the late Harold E. Houck. She is survived by Howard W. Booth Jr. of Groton, CT, William R. Booth of Toronto, Canada, granddaughters Teresa Bridgeman and Barbara Clay; grandsons Douglas and David Booth and great-grandchildren Michael, Ryan, Emily and Brandon. A memorial service will be held at Bay Village of Sarasota, 8400 Vamo Road on Wednesday, March 29 at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests a donation to the charity of your choice.
