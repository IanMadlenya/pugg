
        HOCTOR--Danny. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Danny Hoctor, on July 14, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Thursday, July 17, at 10 AM, at St. Luke's Church, Franklin Turnpike, Waldwick, NJ.
