
        FRIEDERICH-Peter A., age 71, on February 21, 2000, in a motorscooter accident in Lighthouse Point, Fla. Loving father of Cindy, Peter and Thomas; cherished grandfather of Brandon and Brittany; adored brother of Terre and Barbara; a favorite uncle to many nieces and nephews; an army veteran, he was a retired VP of RCA. He joins his soul mate Lenore in heaven. We miss you Dad.
