
        REDMOND-Frances Jones. On September 11, 2001 at the Osborn retirement home in Rye, NY. She was 101 years old. Born on June 25, 1900 in Brooklyn, NY she was the daughter of the late Sally and Matthew Carpenter Jones and sister of the late Matthew Carpenter Jones, Jr. Mrs. Redmond graduated from the Packer Collegiate Institute in Brooklyn and was a long time member of the Junior League of Brooklyn and the Colony Club in Manhattan. She maintained summer residences for many years in Bellport, New York and, later, in Westhampton Beach, New York. As well as her parents and brother she was predeceased by her husband, Cyril J. Redmond and a daughter, Barbara Redmond Ryan of New York. She is survived by a daughter, Jean Dalzell Cushing of Riverside, CT, five grandchildren, Deborah Zinn Prouty and Kathryn Zinn Cerussi of Riverside, CT, Sally Ryan Shaw of Hamilton, MA, Allan A. Ryan IV of Palm Beach, FL and Melissa Ryan Kaiser of New York, NY. She also leaves eleven greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be scheduled at a later date.
