
        DEMAS-Anthony. Of New York City and Madison, CT was lost in the World Trade Center tragedy. Born in Suffern, NY and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Mr. Demas was a graduate of Pratt Institute and earned a PMD from the Harvard University School of Business. Mr. Demas served his country as a First Lieutenant in the U.S. Army. At the time of the tragedy, he held the position of Managing Director at AON Corporation. Previously, he was a Managing Director of Marsh & McLennan, where he worked for thirty-one years. Mr. Demas is survived by his wife, Violetta Veras Demas, and their twin eight-year-old sons, Nicholas and Andrew. In addition, he is survived by his mother, Athanassia Demas; sister, Eve Zelle; father-inlaw, James Veras and his wife, Betty Aspasia; sister-in-law, Lydia Veras, brother-in-law, George Veras and his wife, Kim; two nieces and four nephews. A loving memorial service for Anthony Demas will be held at the Church of Our Saviour, Rye, NY on Saturday, September 22 at 2:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers, please send memorial contributions to: The Annunciation Greek Orthodox Church, Attention: Anthony Demas Memorial Fund, 302 West 91st Street, New York, New York 10024.
