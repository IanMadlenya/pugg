
        SAGOR-Irving. Of Portland, OR, formerly of Larchmont, NY, at age 81. Loving husband of Harriet. Devoted father of Robert and Richard. Proud grandfather of Emily, Eli, Ellisa and Emma. Built successful CPA practice in NYC. Founding Director and Financial Officer of Sony Corporation of America. Longtime trustee of Larchmont Temple and former member of Fairview Country Club. Irving's intellect, wit and generosity greatly enriched the lives of everyone whose path he crossed. We are all better people for having known him.
