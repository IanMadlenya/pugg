
        SUGAAR-Stephen, M.D., 90. Of Canaan, New York, died April 18, 2002 at Berkshire Medical Center, Pittsfield, MA. Born on April 20, 1911 in Budapest, Hungary the son of Vilmos and Jenny Spitz Sugaar, he graduated from medical school in Hungary and then worked at the Pasteur Institute of medical research in Paris. Upon immigrating to the United States in 1939 he served his medical residency at Beth Israel Hospital in New York City. He was a Medical Pathologist and retired in 1979 from Brooklyn Veterans Administration Medical Center in NYC. He has been a resident of Canaan since 1974. He leaves his wife, the former Herthe Maria Meyer, whom he married in New York City in 1948.
