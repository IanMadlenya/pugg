
        LAVENTURE-Barbara, 89 years of age, died on August 23, in the Dobbs Ferry Hospital. She was a resident of Irvington-on-Hudson for 58 years. The wife of William Laventure, who died in 1984, she is survived by her son, Hugh Hardy, from her first marriage, to Gelston Hardy. Also survived by her grandchildren, Sebastian and Penelope Hardy. A birthday celebration in her honor will be announced.
