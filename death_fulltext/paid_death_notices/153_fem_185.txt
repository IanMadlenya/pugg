
        HARMON-Nora Alkalay Schueller Born Nagy Bisce, Hungary 1909, died New York City October 9, 1999. A beautiful, courageous, strong woman, she lived and died on her own terms. She will be remembered by all who knew her for her charm, intelligence and beauty. A Memorial will be held on Saturday, October 30th at 12:30PM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue. Servus, Nora. Lilot, Christopher, Nicholas, Keith
