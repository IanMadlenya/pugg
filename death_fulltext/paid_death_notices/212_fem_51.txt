
        KOSS--Suzanne Helein. September 3, 2004. Professor Emerita Queens College, C.U.N.Y. Beloved wife of Murray. Cherished sister-in-law of Rosalyn and Albert Meisler. Devoted aunt of Laura, Janice, Rachel and Melissa. Her many friends and relatives in Belgium, France and the United States will miss her love, her loyalty, her wit, her kindness, her goodness. There will be a private memorial service. Contributions in her memory to a charity of your choice.
