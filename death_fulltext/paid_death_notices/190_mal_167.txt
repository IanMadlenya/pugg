
        COBURN-Ralph G., 93. Of Walpole formerly of Bedford, NY died November 8 of heart failure. Retired Rear Admiral, USNR. A memorial service will be held Saturday, November 23 at 11 o'clock AM at Grace Episcopal Church, 150 Chapel St., Norwood, MA. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to Women of Means, Inc., 148 Linden St., Suite 104A, Wellesley, MA 02482-a non-profit organization that provides free medical care for homeless women and children of Boston, MA.
