
        PANZIRER--Leo E. Age 95, died peacefully at home on Friday, June 10, 2005. Beloved husband of Zelda (nee Silberstein). Adored and loving father of Bethany Gilboard (Bruce) and loving ''Papa Leo'' of Andrew and Sam. A practicing attorney for 60 years, he was greatly loved and admired by all who had the pleasure of knowing him and he will be greatly missed by all. Services 10 AM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to American Society for Technion.
        PANZIRER -- Leo. Temple Shaaray Tefila notes with great sorrow the death of our longtime member, Leo Panzirer. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Zelda, and his daughter Bethany and her family. May all who loved him be comforted among the mourners of our people in Zion and Jerusalem Rabbi Jonathan A. Stein Michael H. Laufer, Pres.
