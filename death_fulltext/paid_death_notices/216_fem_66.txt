
        KNIGHT -- Adelaide Clarke. Died on January 3, 2005 at the age of 88. Survived by her son, George, grandchildren Glen, Jim, great-grandchildren Adelaide, Charlie and sister Elizabeth Dillon. She graduated from the Chapin School. Her hobbies included tennis, golf, bowling and ballroom dancing. She will be missed by her family and her many devoted friends to whom she had opened her heart. A memorial service will be held Saturday January 15 at 1:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Salvation Army and Red Cross Blood Bank.
