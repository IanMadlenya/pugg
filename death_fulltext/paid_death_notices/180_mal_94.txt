
        MILLER-J. Wade, Jr. Of New York City and Ogunquit, ME. Died December 13, 2001. Born in Pittsburgh, PA, he graduated from Washington and Jefferson College where he was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. He went on to earn a Ph.D. from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, graduating from the Sloane School of Management. During W.W. II, he served as an officer in the U.S. Navy in the Pacific. He enjoyed a distinguished career in management and human resources including the positions of Corporate Vice President of B.F. Goodrich and Executive Vice President of Organization Resources Counselors. During his career, he served as a member of the Business Roundtable. He was a longtime member of the Vestry of Trinity Episcopal Church in Boston, MA. He is survived by his wife Margaret, daughters Susan Werthan of Bethesda, Maryland and Janice Thorpe of Montclair, N.J., and grandsons David and Benjamin Werthan. Donations in his memory may be made to the J. Wade Miller Scholarship Fund, Washington and Jefferson College, Washington, PA. 15301. A memorial service will be held in Maine at a later date.
