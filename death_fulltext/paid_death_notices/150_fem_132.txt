
        SPELLMAN-Violet (Ibi). Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member, beloved wife of our devoted Gabbai and Trustee Mr. Henry Eric Spellman, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi, David Yagoda, Chairman, Dr. William Schwartz, President
        SPELLMAN-Violette (Iby). We extend our sincerest condolences to Mr. Henry (Erik) Spellman on the loss of his beloved wife. A woman of valor who treasured her family and friends, Iby was always there to lend a helping hand. She was truly loved by all and will be greatly missed. As Golden founders, the Spellmans have made a vital contribution to the people of Israel. May Henry, their daughter Ildiko Rosenfeld, a founder in her own right, her sister Martha Lunzer, brother Tibor Herzog and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med Ctr. in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, Pres Stuart Tauber, Exec Dir
