
        WALLACE-Walter (Wally). Well loved theater booster and community activist died Tuesday, September 7 at his sister's home in Bronxville, NY. Wally was a proud member of the gay community & active in Greenwich Village and Chelsea. Wally was born May 12, 1938 in Cresskill, NJ. A gathering for friends and family will be held at 9A.M. Saturday, September 11 at the Cresskill Congregational Church, 85 Union Avenue, Cresskill, NJ. Phone 201-568-0608. The gathering will be followed by a funeral service at 10A.M. In lieu of flowers contributions to Broadway Cares, Equity Fights AIDS and the American Heart Association are appreciated.
