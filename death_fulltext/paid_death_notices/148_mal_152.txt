
        TUGWOOD-Reverend James C. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of Our Lady of the Snows Church, 258-15 80th Avenue, Floral Park, on May 13. Reposing at Our Lady of Snows Chapel, Saturday, May 15, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 P.M. and on Sunday, May 16, from 2 to 5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Sunday, May 16, at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass Monday, May 17, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop Of Brooklyn.
