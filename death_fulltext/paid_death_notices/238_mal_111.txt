
        SCICCHITANO--Anthony. On November 7, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Rae. Loving father of Josephine Taglianetti, Rita Sacchi and Phyllis Serino. Cherished grandfather of Terrence Anthony, Alexandra, Clio, Laura and Philip. A true American success story, coming to this country from his hometown of Calabria, Italy as a young teen, Tony became a successful entrepreneur in the food and restaurant business, building the well known brand of A&S Pork Stores. He was a pioneer in the neighborhood of Park Slope, Brooklyn, instrumental in developing and revitalizing the community along what is now Fifth Avenue. The stretch of sidewalk which he patrolled on a daily basis, greeting neighbors and helping residents, will always seem a little emptier. For his generosity and vision, he received honorary degrees from the Berkeley Carroll School and Brooklyn College. Reposing at Andrew Torregrossa & Sons Funeral Home, 1305 79 Street, Brooklyn, NY. Visitation Thursday 12-4 P.M. and 7-10 P.M. Funeral Mass Friday 9:30 A.M. at St. Bernadette R.C. Church. Entombment Greenwood Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, family is requesting donations be made to cardiac center of New York Methodist Hospital, Attention: Development Office, 506 Sixth Street, Brooklyn, NY 11215
