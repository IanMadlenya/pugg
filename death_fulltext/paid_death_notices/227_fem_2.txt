
        SHERMAN--Guelda Kirkwood. Of Scarsdale, N.Y. Died November 16. Mrs. Sherman was a graduate of the Eastman School of Music where she honed her skills as a professional cellist. Her natural love of music blossomed into a rewarding career which filled her life with beauty. She is survived by her husband Bertram, son Spencer of Burlington, Vt and daughter Heather of Marco Island, Fl & grandchildren Natalie & Nicole. We will always love her as her memory will live in our hearts forever.
