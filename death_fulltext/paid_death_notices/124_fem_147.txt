
        KATZENSTEIN-Margaret Rothschild. Slept away in her 101st year on May 16, 1997. She was a remarkable person and was a beloved mother of Lee Louis of Hastings On Hudson, NY, Alex Katzenstein of Greensboro, N.C., Charles J. Katzenstein, Jr. of Gastonia, N.C. Her loving grandchildren are: Sandra Pomeroy, Diane Louis McClintock, David M. Louis, Lisa and Ruth Katzenstein, Steven Katzenstein, Jake Rothschild and Judith K. Berlin. She had two great grandchildren and four great, great grandchildren. A Memorial Service was held on May 18th. Gifts in her memory may be sent to the Society for Ethical Culture, 4450 Fieldston Road, Bronx, N.Y. 10471 or The Riverdale Mental Health Association, 5676 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, New York 10471.
