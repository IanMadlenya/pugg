
        KROTOSKY--Ruth (Wilk), 81, formerly from Brooklyn, NY, died at home peacefully in her sleep on February 17, 2007. A resident of Boca Raton, FL since the death of her beloved husband Jerry, in 1988, she is survived by her daughter Michaele Wilk Houston and son-in-law Bob Frymire, her son Steven Kaye and daughter-in-law Jo Kail, as well as her treasured grandson, Seth Kaye. She was a teacher, a Master teacher and a guidance counselor for the NYC Board of Eduication until her retirement in 1986. She leaves a legacy of grateful students and a lifetime of good work. Graveside service at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, Wednesday, February 21, 2007 at 11:30am. The family requests gifts in memory be made to the Rebecca Leah Berger Tuberous Sclerosis Foundation, PO Box 811805, Boca Raton, FL 33481.
