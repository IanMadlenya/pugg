
        SHORE-Josselyn M. May 7, 2000, peacefully at age 96. Beloved husband of Irene Freeman Shore for 71 years. Cherished father of Winifred and Mike Freund and Freeman and Toni Shore. Adored grandfather of Jennifer and Michael Pardee, Mia and Ramsey Walker and Peter and Julie Strauss. Loving Pop-Pop of Benjamin and Jacob Pardee, Samantha and Abigail Walker, and Charles Strauss. Dear brother of the late Jessie Kramer, Sanford, Beatrice and Frederick Shore. He was larger than life and will live in our hearts forever. Services will be at 1 PM, Tuesday, May 9, at Temple Beth-El, Old Mill Rd., Great Neck. Family will be at 3 Davis Rd., Port Washington, Tuesday and Thursday from 5-8 PM and Wednesday, 530 East 86th St., NYC, from 5-8 PM. Contributions may be made to the Josselyn M. Shore Memorial Fund at Temple Beth-El.
        SHORE-Josselyn. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, Members and staff of the Fresh Meadow Country Club record the passing of Josselyn Shore, our esteemed member.         SHORE-Josselyn. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member and beloved former President. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
