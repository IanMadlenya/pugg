
        O'CONNOR -- Mary Wistar Morris. 102, of Mt. Kisco, NY, died peacefully at her home on July 13. She was born in Hartford, CT and attended Miss Porter's School, Farmington, CT. Among her community interests were the District Nursing Association and the Bedford Garden Club of which she was a president. She was the wife of Robert Barnard O'Connor, who died in 1993, and is survived by her sons Robert B. Jr. of Mountain Home, TX and Anthony M. of New York City, four grandsons Robert B., III, Clint H., Anthony M., Jr. and Edward R. and two greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. Mark's Church, Mt. Kisco, NY on Saturday, August 14 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Mark's Church, Mt. Kisco, NY 10549.
