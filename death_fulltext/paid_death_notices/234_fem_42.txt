
        GROGAN--Mary Jane, 84, of Brookhill Road, Sugarloaf, formerly of Eastchester, NY, passed away Friday morning. Born in Brooklyn, NY, July 17, 1921, she was the daughter of the late J. James and Jane Francis (Mellon) Shea. She was preceded in death, in addition to her parents and siblings, by her husband William Joseph Grogan. Surviving are a daughter Mary Breslin and her husband Robert, Sugarloaf, PA; a son John Grogan and his wife Ann, Cumberland Foreside, ME; and six grandchildren. A Memorial Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Saturday, July 15, 2006, at 11 AM, in St. John Bosco RC Church, Conyngham, PA.
