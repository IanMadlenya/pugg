
        SIGAL-Harriet F. On May 3, 2002 in Sarasota, FL. Concert and operatic mezzo-soprano. High school choral director and music teacher in New York City schools. Beloved wife of the late Alvin I. Sigal. She will be deeply missed by her children Leah and husband Mark Spitzer, Joshua & partner Howard Haskin, Melanie and husband Douglas Gordin, & grandchildren Rebecca, Max, Jacob, Zoe, and Isaac. Remembered by many for her devotion, excellence as a performer and teacher, unique spirit, and volunteer work. Private family memorial service.
