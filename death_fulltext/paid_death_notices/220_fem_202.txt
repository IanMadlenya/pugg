
        FRANKENBERGER -- Ghis laine of Paris, France and longtime resident of Rye, NY, died of cancer on May 23, 2005. She was 58. She is survived by her husband Burkhard and children Caroline and Oliver. A brief memorial service will be held 5PM on Thursday, May 26, 2005 at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, NY. Friends may send memorial donations to St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital, 1000 Tenth Ave., New York, NY 10019.
