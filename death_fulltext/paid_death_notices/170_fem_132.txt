
        KISSEBERTH-Marie Ann (nee Gagin). On Thursday, March 15, 2001. Of Berkeley Heights, N.J. Wife of the late Irving M. Kisseberth. Mother of Joanne Heller, Leslie Ann Hauspurg and her husband Thomas, and the late James Kisseberth. Grandmother of ten grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Relatives and friends may attend the funeral mass at Church of the Little Flower, Berkeley Heights on Monday, March 19, at 11:15AM. Visitation at Brough Funeral Home, 535 Springfield Avenue, Summit from 4 to 6PM Sunday. Interment in St. Teresa's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society or the Alzheimer's Fund.
