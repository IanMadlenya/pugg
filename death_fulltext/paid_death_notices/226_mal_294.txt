
        BERGER--Bernard. On November 24, 2005, two weeks after his 88th birthday, died at the Dallas Home for Jewish Aged. Born and raised on the Lower East Side, he studied finance at NYU before being drafted into the Army for W.W. II. Afterwards, he joined his father and brother George at J. Berger & Sons, 54 Orchard St. Marrying the late Doris Gelman, they resided and raised a family in Brooklyn. At the age of 60, retired to Lake Worth, FL. Survived by sons, Ira of Heath, TX, and Stuart of Jericho, NY; grandchildren Jeffery, Jaime, George and Jaclyn. Services private later on this week. In lieu of flowers, donations to Alzheimer's Foundation.
