
        TIGER-Elinor (nee Hamburg). Aged 89, died December 31, 1997 at her home in West Orange, New Jersey. BA Wellesley College 1930; MS NYU 1932. Married to Howard Lang Tiger in 1932. Survived by a sister, Joan H. Weisl of NYC; two daughters: Madeline Tiger of Montclair, NJ and Barbara Tiger Rubens of Santa Monica, CA.; seven grandchildren and seven greatgrandchildren. Private interment. No flowers. Donations may be sent to the UJA, Wellesley College, The Ethical Culture Society, or Planned Parenthood of NJ.
