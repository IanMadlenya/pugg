
        MILANO-Daniel Adam. Age 26, of NYC, on November 28, after a valiant and determined six year battle with cancer. Loving husband of Laura Aguilar. Beloved son of Joan and Andrew. Cherished brother of Eric and Amy. He was a graduate of Brown University and a neurobiologic researcher at the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University. Funeral services at Bet Am Shalom Synagogue, White Plains, NY, Sunday, November 30, 1997, at 11 AM. Donations may be made to Ronald McDonald House in memory of Daniel Milano.
