
        MARCUS-David. Died on September 28th, 1997. Beloved father of Adam David and Uncle of Carol Landsman. Services today at 10AM, Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, New York. Graduated from the University of Kentucky and Cornell Law School and practiced law with distinction for forty-seven years. Formerly Law Secretary to Irving Lehman, Chief Judge of the State of New York and Assistant Attorney General of New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Cornell Law School or U.J.A. in his memory.
