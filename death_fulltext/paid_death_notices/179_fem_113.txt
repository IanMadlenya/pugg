
        PORZIO-Linda. Age 51. Passed away on December 14, 2001 after a long and courageous battle with Multiple Sclerosis. Linda touched and loved everyone with whom she came in contact and she bore her illness without complaint. She is survived by her husband Richard. Service Friday, December 21, 11AM at St. John the Martyr RC Church, 250 East 72nd Street, NYC.
