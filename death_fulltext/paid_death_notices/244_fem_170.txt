
        PALMER--Mary, died May 19 after a short illness. She was retired from Fairchild Publication, where she was executive secretary to the editor and also a columnist who reported on the Men's Ware Trade. She is survived by two sisters, Cynthia Gray of North Carolina, and Suzanne Coppel of New York City, and three nephews James Breeding of Somerville, NJ, Andrew Breeding of Boston, MA, and William Breeding of Champagne, IL. The funeral will be at Redden's Funeral Home on Friday May 25, 11am-1pm.
