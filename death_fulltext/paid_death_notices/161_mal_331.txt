
        KERTZNER-Leonard H., M.D. 82, on June 28, 2000. Passed away after a long illness in the loving company of his family. Loving father, husband, grandfather, and uncle. Prominent physician in the community. He was a Pulitzer Scholar, served as Chief of Internal Medicine at Southside Hospital, Bayshore, NY for 28 years, and served as a Member of The Stonybrook Roundtable. His humor, devotion and loyalty will be deeply missed. He is survived by his wife, Judith, sons, Richard, Robert, Ronald, and Daniel, brothers, Bert and Stanley, four grandchildren, and eight nieces and nephews. Funeral service at White-Voltz Funeral Home, 364 East Main Street, Bayshore, NY, at 11 A.M. Friday June 30th.
