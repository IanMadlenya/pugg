
        MEADE-George F. A resident of Scarborough, NY, died August 1, 2002. He was 82. He worked as a production and distribution manager for Time Inc. in New York City for over 40 years. He is survived by his wife Jeanne, and his four children and nine grandchildren. Services were held in Briarcliff Manor, New York by the Waterbury & Kelly Funeral Home.
