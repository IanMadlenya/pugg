
        STREET--Ann. The Board of Directors of the James Lenox House Association mourns the death of longtime Board member, Ann Street, on February 7. She served devotedly on many committees including Executive, Admission, Library and Stewardship Committees. We will miss her spirit, elegance, generosity and devotion to independence in senior living. A memorial service will take place at 11 AM on February 24, Brick Church, Park Avenue and 91 Street. Paul J. Massey Jr., President Friedhilde Milburn, Exec. Dir.
