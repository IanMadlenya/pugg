
        LEWIS--Charles Elliott. Age 71. Of Boca Raton, FL., formerly, a long-time resident of Bronxville, N.Y., passed away peacefully after a long illness on Sunday, January 4, 2004. Adored and beloved husband, soulmate and friend of Christine B. Lewis. Devoted and loving father of Charles E. Lewis, Jr. and Dr. Catherine F. Lewis. Loving brother of Maryann Wolter. Admiring father-in-law of Dean Hamilton. Adoring grandfather of Colin Lewis Hamilton. Born in Youngstown, OH. Charles served in the Air Force and held an MBA from Case Western University and an LLB from Yale Law School where he was on the Law Review. He retired in 1998, having spent his legal career as an attorney at Wilkie Farr, Gulf & Western, and F.D.I.C. Charles was one of a kind, an extraordinary man of great character, inner strength and spirit. His learned advice, legal and business knowledge, worldly views and witty personality will sorely be missed by his family and all who knew him. May he rest in peace. A memorial service will be held Saturday, January 17, 2004, 1PM, Christ Church, Bronxville, N.Y. Memorial donations may be made to Yale Law School, POB 208341, New Haven, CT. 06520-8341.
