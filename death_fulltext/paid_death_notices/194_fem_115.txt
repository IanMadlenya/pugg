
        STEENKEN-Mary Frances Milmoe. 90, of Richmond, VA, formerly of Florida, died Saturday, March 15, 2003. Mrs. Steenken was born in New York and received her undergraduate and Masters degrees from St. Joseph's College for Women in New York, and was a teacher and counselor at Lynbrook High School. Funeral Mass and interment will be in Richmond. For information, Bliley Funeral Homes, Richmond, VA; 804-355-3800.
