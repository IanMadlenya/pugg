
        NORMAN--Dr. Alex. The Board of Trustees and the Medical Staff of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute record with deep sorrow the passing of one of our world-renowned physicians. As a former Chairman of the HJD Department of Radiology, founder of the International Skeletal Society and author of the first textbook on bone radiology, he will be remembered as an eminent physician and an outstanding teacher. We express our sincere sympathy to his wife Muriel and the family. John N. Kastanis, President & CEO William A. Perlmuth, Chairman of the Board
