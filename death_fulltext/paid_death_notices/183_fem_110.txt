
        SENFT-Rhoda F. Devoted wife of Edward Senft for over 60 years, died this morning after a long illness at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was a well known fashion and photographic model who appeared on the covers of several national magazines. Surviving are her husband Edward Senft, her sister Gloria Kay Levinson, her brother and sister-in-law Bill and Peggy Diamant, her grandson Paul Gutman and many loving nieces and nephews. She will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved her. Visitation will be at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street from 7-9 PM on Thursday, April 11th. Funeral services will be at Frank E. Campbell on Friday, April 12th at 11:30 AM.
