
        BACON-Charlotte Robb. Of Batavia, Illinois, formerly of Gilbertsville and East Aurora, New York. Passed away on January 11, 2000. Pre-deceased by her beloved husband, John A. Bacon. Loving mother of John A. Bacon Jr. of Wayne, IL and James E. Bacon of Redding, CT. Survived also by devoted daughters-in-law, Elisabeth, Caroline and Edith; by five grandchildren; and by five great-grandchildren. Memorial eucharistic service was held at St. Mark's Church, Geneva, IL on Saturday January 15. Contributions in her memory to the John A. and Charlotte R. Bacon Scholarship Fund, Middlesex School, Lowell Rd., Concord, MA 01742 would be appreciated.
