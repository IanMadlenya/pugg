
        LEWBELL--Elaine D. (nee Drucker). On Wednesday, December 28th Elaine passed away peacefully at the age of 95. Beloved wife of the late Kenneth Lewbell. Loving mother of Carol and Gary. Cherished grandmother of Erik, Christopher and Julia. She lived her life with grace and style and always did it her way. Elaine will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral service Monday, January 2, 2006, 11:30 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. Interment to follow at Hungarian Union Field Cemetery.
