
        FAYSON-Viola M. Born July 26, 1946 in White Castle, LA, died June 7, 2002 at St. Luke's Hospital in NY. Survived by her companion Herman Bryant, daughter Bonnie Slater, stepmother Edna L. Bell, 5 brothers: Raymond Lewis, Lloyd Jr., Gregory, Eric, and Frank, three sisters: Vonola Avist, Katherine Davis and Vanessa Baptiste. Preceded in death by father Lloyd Bell, Sr./Ned Tucker, mother Margie Hughes Tucker Skinner, sister Lois Ann Skinner. She was a member of the Eastern Star Masonic Organization. Memorial service will be held on Friday, June 21st at 2 P.M. at the Metropolitan Baptist Church, 151 West 128th Street.
