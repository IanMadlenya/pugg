
        McCARRON-Virginia. Born October 19, 1916, died May 2, 2002. She was predeceased by her parents, Michael T. McCarron and Julia Byrnes, a sister, Helyn, and two brothers, Michael and Warren. Survivors include two nephews, Michael McCarron and Mark McCarron, and a niece, Tara Owens. Viewing hours are Sunday 2-5 and 7-9 PM and Monday at 9 AM at Fox Funeral Home, 9807 Ascan Avenue, Forest Hills, NY. Burial at Calvary Cemetery on Monday following the viewing.
