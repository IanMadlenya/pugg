
        BLUESTONE-Sally. November 13, 1998 after a very brief illness. One month before her 96th birthday. Beloved sister, aunt, great-aunt and great great-aunt. Devoted daughter of the late Fannie and Joseph Bluestone. Generous, caring, loyal and gifted lady. Will be remembered for a lifetime of hard work, commitment to family and Judaism. Life Member and Past President of Hatikvah Chapter of Hadassah. She was A Presence. She will be missed. Service Sunday, November 15. Family to meet at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn at 1 P.M. and proceed to New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, N.Y. for graveside service. Shiva, 1718 Quentin Road, Brooklyn, N.Y.
