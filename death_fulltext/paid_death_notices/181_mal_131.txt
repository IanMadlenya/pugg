
        THOMAS-Archimedes K., M.D. Of Boxford, Massachusetts. passed away on January 22, 2002. He was a graduate of Stuyvesant High School Class of 1970, S.U.N.Y. Stony Brook and S.U.N.Y. Buffalo School of Medicine. He is survived by his wife Susan, his sons Andrew and Matthew, his parents Lester and Eina & his sisters Florentine, Cricita, Calena and Marcia. Memorial donations may be made to Stuyvesant High School.
