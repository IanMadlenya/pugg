
        BARCHILON--Jose, M.D. The members of The New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of their longtime colleague, Dr. Jose Barchilon. He was a beloved teacher and friend to many of our members during their psychiatric training and beyond. He was dedicated to psychoanalysis and will be deeply missed. We extend our condolences to his entire family. Paul Rosenbaum, M.D., President, Institute Leon Kupferstein, M.D., President, Society
        BARCHILON--Jose, M.D. On August 6. The world will move on, without him, to our great loss, but we his friends will miss him forever.
