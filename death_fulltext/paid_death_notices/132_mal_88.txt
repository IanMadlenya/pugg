
        DISTEFANO-Pat, 43. From complications of a brain tumor, January 1st at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. Mr. Distefano worked for the last four years of his life as a casting director for Hopkins Smith Barden Casting. Before this he worked as an actor, appearing in such plays as ''Blue Dahlia'' and ''Search And Destroy'' in L.A., and also as a producer for the Pacific Repertory Theater. He is survived by his parents, Pat and Antoinette of Las Vegas, and his sister, Linda Lackey of Los Angeles. A memorial service will be held on Friday, January 9th, at 4:00 P.M., at St. Monica's Church in Santa Monica (701 California Avenue).
