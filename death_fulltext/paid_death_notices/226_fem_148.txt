
        ROSENTHAL -- Ann Edith, (nee Prince). Of Boca Raton, FL, died on November 15, 2005. She remained vital and beautiful until her passing at the age of 95. Beloved wife of the late Edward H. Rosenthal, M.D. and mother of the late Nelson I. Rosenthal. She is survived by her children Alan David Rosenthal, M.D. and Enid Feldman Rosenthal; granddaughters Nina Michelle Fusillo and Amy Ilyse Rosenthal, grandson-in-law, Michael Fusillo, Ph.D., and great grandson Nathaniel James Fusillo. Her commitment to the community included membership on the board of the March of Dimes of Greater New York and a former president of the Brooklyn chapter of the National Council of Jewish Women. Her journey was long and varied but always traveled with elegance and a graceful expansion of her horizons. She will be deeply missed. Service on Sunday, November 20, at 11:30AM, at the ''Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave, New York City. Contributions may be made to the March of Dimes of Greater New York.
