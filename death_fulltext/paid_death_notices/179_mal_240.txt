
        ANDREWS-Martin. 62, filmmaker, lighting designer and gaffer, died at home in Hoboken, NJ, last Friday of cancer. Location technical director on ''Woodstock,'' Marty most recently lit the Christmas scenes in ''Riding in Cars With Boys.'' Born in NYC, he crewed for Pomfret School and the Univ. of Penn. A passionate sailor, Marty divided his time between homes in Hoboken and Wiscasset, ME. He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth and sons Hilary, Alexander and Frederick. Friends and family will gather at the Elks Club, 1003 Washington St, Hoboken, on Sunday, December 23, 2-5 PM. Donations may be made in his name to the Wilderness Society.
