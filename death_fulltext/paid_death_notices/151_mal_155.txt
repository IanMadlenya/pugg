
        BUBIS-Ignatz. The World Jewish Congress mourns the passing of Ignatz Bubis, courageous head of Germany's Jewish community and President of The European Jewish Congress. The Jewish world has lost a great champion of human rights who embodied the Jewish experience from the depth of the Holocaust to the renaissance of Jewish identity and peoplehood. May all who loved and admired him be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Edgar M. Bronfman, President Israel Singer, Secretary General Evelyn Sommer, Chairman American Section Elan Steinberg, Executive Director
        BUBIS-Ignatz. The Conference on Jewish Material Claims Against Germany is deeply saddend by the loss of our esteemed Board Member and Vice President. He was a great Jewish leader of German and European Jewry who valiantly fought for justice and restitution for Holocaust survivors. His tireless efforts to educate the German people about the Shoah will be felt for many generations. We extend our sincere and heartfelt condolences to his family and the Jewish Community of Germany. Rabbi Israel Miller, President Gideon Taylor, Exec. VP Saul Kagan, Exec. VP Emeritus
        BUBIS-Ignatz. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of Ignatz Bubis, head of the German Jewish community. Mr. Bubis, a Holocaust survivor, was an international figure and a moral authority. His visionary leadership will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Ida, and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
