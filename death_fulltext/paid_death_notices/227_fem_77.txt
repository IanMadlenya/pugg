
        BOTTA--Elizabeth Ann (aka Betty Santoro). Of Bayside, NY, on December 10, 2005. Beloved daughter of Madeline and John. Betty was a loving inspiration to both her family and friends. A pioneer in the gay rights movement, she helped organize the 1979 March on Washington and gave the keynote speech. Her years of advocacy before the New York City's Council also helped spur passage of an anti-discrimination bill for lesbians and gay men.
