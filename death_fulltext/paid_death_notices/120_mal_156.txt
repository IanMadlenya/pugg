
        FROST-Frederick William, age 85, of Thompson, Connecticut, died on January 15, 1997. Son of the late Mr. and Mrs. F.W. Frost of San Francisco, California; husband of Joan Alice; father of Christopher and Jeremy. A graduate of Union College, Schenectady, New York in 1937, he was a Vice President and Manager, Television Art and Production Department, Young & Rubicam, Inc., NY. He retired as Administrative Manager of the N.Y. office. Funeral arrangements are private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Dr. C.W. Lowman Fund or the Crippled Children's Guild, C/O Los Angeles Orthopedic Hospital, 2400 South Flower Street, Los Angeles, California 90016.
