
        GETZ-Ruth (nee Mehlman). Of White Plains, NY, died peacefully at home on March 7, 2002, at the age of 89. Beloved wife and best friend of the late George Getz. Adoring and adored mother of Nancy and Mitchell Wollman and Marcia and Donald Gerber. Cherished grandmother of Jeffrey, Richard, Daniel, Susan & Andrew. Loving great grandmother of Kaidy, Erika, Ross, Joanna, Caroline & Tayler. Devoted sister of Gladys Aronson and Bernice Zerwitz. She will live in our hearts forever. Services private.
