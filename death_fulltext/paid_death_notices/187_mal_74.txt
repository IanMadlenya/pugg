
        GOODALE-Peter Perkins, painter and sculptor, died at home on August 1, at the age of 46. The cause was pancreatic cancer. Peter was educated at the Middlesex School in Concord, Mass and at Harvard University. He was represented by the Jack Tilton Gallery in New York and had also shown at the Nielsen Gallery in Boston. He is survived by his wife Susan, his sons Thomas and Joseph, his mother Irene, his brothers Nathaniel and Wing, and his siters Kate, Olivia, and Ava.
