
        CASEY-Sophia B. On October 5, 2000, of Roslyn Harbor. Beloved wife of the late William J. Casey, former Director of Central Intelligence. Loving mother of Bernadette Casey Smith and her husband Owen T. Smith. Dear sister of Lillian Kurz and Joseph Kurz. Loving aunt of Noel Glenn, Carol Murray, Thomas Langan and many loving grand-nieces and nephews. Family will receive friends at the Casey residency (Mayknoll) 12 Glenwood Road, Roslyn Harbor, Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 79PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Tuesday, 11:00 AM, St. Mary's RC Church, Roslyn Harbor. Interment, Holy Rood Cemetery. She was a Dame of the Order of St. Gregory and the Order of Holy Sepulchre.
