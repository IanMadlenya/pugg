
        LINDSAY--Elizabeth Austin, May 15, 1923-September 13, 2004. Libby dedicated her life to children, teaching at Potomac School, McLean, VA: East Woods School, Oyster Bay, NY; and serving as Director of the East Harlem Tutorial Program, NYC. Her joie de vivre will be missed by her four children, nine grandchildren and extended community. Memorial service at Grace Church, Millbrook, NY, November 26th 4:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, memorials to East Harlem Tutorial Program, 2050 2nd Avenue, NYC, New York 10029.
