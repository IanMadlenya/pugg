
        KUSEL-Stanley P., 83, of Virginia Beach, VA, died February 1, 1996, in a local hospital. Mr. Kusel was a native of New York, NY. Survivors include his wife, Eleanor Kahn Kusel of Virginia Beach, a daughter, Barbara K. Fried of Virginia Beach, a son, Donald R. Kusel of Pleasantville, NY, and his four grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. A graveside service will be conducted at 1 PM, Monday, February 3, 1997, in Forest Lawn Cemetery, Norfolk by Rabbi Elliott Marmon. For information, contact H.D. Oliver Funeral Apartments, Laskin Road Chapel.
