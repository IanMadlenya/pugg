
        WEINSTEIN -- Kenneth D., died of cancer on May 10, 2005. Born July 20, 1952 in NYC. Predeceased by father Edward G. Weinstein and brother Peter Weinstein. Survived by wife Libby, sons Matthew (Elan), Taylor and Timothy, granddaughter Kylee, all of Colorado; mother Barbara of NYC and sister Ellen Adnopoz of Mt. Kisco, NY. He graduated from Scarsdale HS in 1970, from Colgate Univ. in 1974 and received an MBA from Columbia Univ. in 1977. Kenneth was a stockbroker for Smith Barney for 20 years. A contribution in his memory may be made to Pikes Peak Hospice Foundation, 825 E. Pikes Peak Ave. #
