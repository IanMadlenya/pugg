
        BRENNAN - Maurice Anthony. Passed away peacefully on May 9, 1998, at 91. Beloved husband of the late Emily Humphrey Brennan. Cherished father to Laraine. Father-in-law to Steven and beloved grandfather to Catherine, Tanya and Erin. Formerly of Little Neck, NY, he was born in Limerick, Ireland, and became a U.S. citizen in 1929. A Consolidated Edison employee for 47 years, he was a recipient of a medal from the National Safety Council for meritorious service and a founding member of the original Irish Theater Company that produced Sean O'Casey's plays on the site that is now Lincoln Center. An accomplished artist, a lover of Irish history and a scholar of nature, his gentle spirit and great wisdom will always guide us. We will miss you Pom Pom.
