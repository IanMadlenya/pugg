
        PEARL--Harry, 92, November 20th. Naval Research Scientist, innovator, world traveler, dancer, punster. Last of the Pearl siblings: Phil, Bill, Sally, Nellie, and Ethel. Honored uncle of Nella, Sylvia, Sam, Felice, Arnold, and David. Loved and cherished by the late Kaye and Anna. He took great pleasure in life and gave it in return.
