
        REISS-Shlomo. Age 79, on November 15, 2000, after a long illness. Former Vice President of the Society of Flavor Chemists, he was a leader in his profession. His was one of the finest noses in the industry. Devoted husband to Judith. Loving father of Nira, Jacob, Tallia and Michal, and caring grandfather to Ayelet, Annette, Annie, Adam and Jenna. The family wishes to express their appreciation to Laurel for her service, devotion and care. He was buried in Haifa, Israel. His sense of humor and companionship will be greatly missed.
