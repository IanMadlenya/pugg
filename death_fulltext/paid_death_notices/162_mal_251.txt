
        GENDIN-Stephen. POZ magazine mourns the loss of co-founder, Contributing Editor, AIDS activist, colleague, lover and friend Stephen Gendin. His unapologetic candor, courage and determination inspired all who knew him. Community leaders, loved ones and the public will gather to celebrate the meaningfulness of his life Thursday, July 27th (tomorrow), 11:30 AM at the Lesbian Gay Community Center, 1 Little West 12th. St., NY, NY.
        GENDIN-Stephen. Stephen was our friend, our hero, our inspiration. He gave hope-and life-to thousands. We are profoundly saddened by his passing. -The staff and clients of Community Prescription Service. Act Up! Fight Back! Fight Aids!
