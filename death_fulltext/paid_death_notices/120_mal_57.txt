
        KARBAN - Elias, on January 5, 1997. The partners, associates and staff of Arenson, Dittmar & karban mourn the passing of our dear friend and esteemed senior partner, Elias Karban. His wise counsel, strength of character and genuine humanity will be long remembered and missed. Mr. Karban had distinguished legal andmilitary careers. He served his country with honor in World War II and the Korean War, rising to the rank of Major. He was actively engaged in the practice of law and lectured widely on the law of estates at the Practicing Law Institute, bar associations, United Jewish Appeal and the Roman Catholic Diocese of Brooklyn. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, children, grandchildren and brother. Funeral services will be conducted at I.J. Morris, Inc. 46 Greenwich St. Hempstead, NY on Tuesday, January 7, 1996 at noon.
        KARBAN - Elias. The Shelter Rock Jewish Center, Roslyn, N.Y. sadly records the death of longtime member and extends condolences to the family. May God comfort and console them.
