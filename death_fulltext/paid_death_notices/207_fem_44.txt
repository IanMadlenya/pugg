
        SHAWN -- Catherine (aka Schwartz), on April 5, 2004 in Manhattan. Was the creator of GO2BUILDINGS.COM website and environmental clearing house bringing contractors, architects and suppliers together to build environmentally friendly homes and offices. Graduate of Parsons School of Design and Bennington College. In a prior career studied under Lee Strassberg and was a member of Actors Studio.
