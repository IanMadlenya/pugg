
        SEFERT-Dennis H., 56. Died on Monday, March 22, 1999 after a long fought battle with cancer. He spent thirteen years in the Bond Department of Solomon Bros. in New York and Cleveland. In 1981, Mr. Sefert left Solomon to become involved in the commodity futures business in Chicago. Mr. Sefert died peacefully surrounded by family. He is survived by his wife Olivia, his sister Beth and his children Geoffrey, Wendy, Ashley and Jennifer. Memorial service Friday, March 26th, 1 PM, at Saint Chrysostom's Episcopal Church, 1424 N. Dearborn, Chicago. IL.
