
        ROGERS-Judith Sanden. Died on November 28, 2001, after a courageous battle with cancer. She was the headmistress of Saint Joseph's Episcopal School and president of the Schoolhouse Children's Museum, both in Boynton Beach, FL. She is survived by her daughter Jennifer N. Rogers, and brother Kenneth Sanden, both of New York City. Contributions in her memory may be made to Saint Joseph's School Endowment Fund, 3300B S. Seacrest Boulevard, Boynton Beach, FL. 33435.
