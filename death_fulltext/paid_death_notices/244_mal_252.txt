
        HARTNETT--William J., on May 26, 2007, at age 87. Beloved husband of Eleanor Joan (nee Dowd). Loving father of Pat, Jane and William Jr., and father-in-law of Jim and Tom. Adored grandfather of Kerri, Kelly, Jimmy, Megan and Chris. Dear brother of the late Joseph, Margaret and Eleanor. Decorated Veteran of WWII (USMC) and the NYC Police Department. Always faithful. Friends may call at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue, Tuesday 2-5PM and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Immaculate Conception Church, 10AM, 414 East 14th Street, NYC. Internment to follow at St. Mary's Cemetery Yonkers, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Family Circle at Mary Manning Walsh Nursing Home, 1339 York Ave., NY, NY 10021.
