
        MORRIS-Kathleen E. Age 77. Of Dayton, OH, died Sunday, Feb. 28. Formerly a resident of Toledo, OH and an employee of Owen Illinois Corp. Born in the Bronx, NY. She was a graduate of St. Nicholas of Tolentine High School, a Member of the Church of the Incarnation Parish in Centerville, OH and was a volunteer for Incarnation Parish organizations and for Kettering Hospital. Preceded in death by her father and mother, Walter Elliott and Kathleen E. (Eagan) Morris; sisters, Anne M. Morris and Mrs. Edward J. McHugh of Washington Township, OH. Graveside services will be held in Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, New York, Saturday, March 6 at 11:00 A.M. For information call Routsong Funeral Home, Dayton, Ohio.
