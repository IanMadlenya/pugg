
        FLACHBARTH - Virginia Bush Slook. Died May 15, 2001 in Parkersburg, West Va. of congested heart failure. Burial will be in Philadelphia. She was a direct descendant of Richard Peters, Secretary of War under the Articles of Confederation and a member of the Continental Congress. Richard Peters was appointed U.S. District Judge for Pennsylvania by his good friend George Washington. Virginia Flachbarth is survived by her husband Charles Thomas Flachbarth, her son Charles Thomas Pawling Flachbarth of Alexandria, Virginia, and various nephews and nieces.
