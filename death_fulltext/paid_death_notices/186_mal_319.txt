
        LAGUARDIA-Kendrick M. Age 33 of Orlando. Passed away July 26, 2002 in Orlando. He was born December 10, 1968 in Nyack, NY and has resided in Orlando the past eight years. He attended Florida State University and was a member of Kappa Alpha Fraternity. Ken was a homebuilder and enjoyed airboating and skiing. He is survived by his wife Niki and son Kendrick R, parents John and Lynn Laguardia of Orlando, his sister, Day Gigliotti (John) and nieces Annie & Grayson of Winter Park; paternal uncle, Arnold Laguardia (Susan) of Del Mar, CA; maternal aunt Kendra Merrill (David), cousins Seth and Nathan Merrill all of Burlington, VT.
