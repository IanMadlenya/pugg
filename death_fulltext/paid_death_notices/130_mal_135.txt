
        ROSS-Bernard Charles. On November 10, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Estelle Ross. A kind and loving man who savored life until the end, he is survived by his children, Jane and Gerry Siegel and Larry and Lynn Ross. Donations can be sent to the Daughter of Israel in West Orange, N.J. Mourning period will be observed at the home of Jane and Gerry Siegel, Wednesday and Thursday, 3-8 PM.
