
        COLES-Mary Drake. Watercolor artist and long-time resident of Edgartown, MA, on Sunday, December 13, 1998, at age 95. Graduate of Smith College, Class of 1924, Ms. Coles painted throughout her life despite loss of her sight in mid 1950's. Works exhibited in group shows at Metropolitan Museum of Art and other galleries. Memorial service planned for next spring on Martha's Vineyard. Donations in her memory may be made to The Seeing Eye, P.O. Box 375, Morristown, NJ 07963.
