
        MEEHAN-John Thomas (Jack) of Dublin, Ireland and formerly Brooklyn, New York, May 20, 2000 (suddenly) dearly beloved husband of Therese. Sadly missed by his brother James; dear friend of Fr. Patrick, P.J., Robert and Aidan. Removal from Fanagans Funeral Home, Lr. Main St. Dundrum, Dublin to the Church of the Miraculous Medal, Bird Avenue arriving at 5:30 o'clock this (Tuesday) evening. Funeral tomorrow (Wednesday) after 10:00 o'clock Mass to Shanganagh Cemetery, Shankill, Co. Dublin. May he rest in peace.
