
        HALKIN--Cyrille R. M.D., 84, amazing mother of Rose and Scott Schwartz, and Scott's partner, Dr. Mary Belmont. Beloved pediatrician and spiritual counselor to generations of New Yorkers. Graduate of Hunter High School, University of Michigan, and NY Medical College (1945), past president of the Alumni Association and Professor of Pediatrics at NY Medical College, Vice President and attending physician at Flower and Fifth Avenue Hospital, and Attending at Lenox Hill Hospital. She forged inroads for professional women, practicing her grace, elegance, and biting humor, while raising a family. Francophile, art lover, gourmet, political analyst, humorist, and high priestess of the art of joie de vivre. Funeral services were held on Sunday February 4, 2007.
