
        GINNA--Margaret Williams, died May 7 at Southampton Hospital, age 80. Survived by her husband, Robert Emmett Ginna, Jr., her son, Peter St. John Ginna, daughter, Mary F. (Molly) Ginna, and two grandchildren, Henry W. and Katharine W. Ginna. Funeral service at Christ Episcopal Church, Sag Harbor, 11AM, Saturday, May 22. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to Southampton Hospital, 240 Meeting House Lane, Southampton, NY 11968.
