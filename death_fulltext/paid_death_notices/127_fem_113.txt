        HARRIS-Mary Jeanne. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Administration of the Hospital for Special Surgery mourn with profound sorrow the death of Mary Jeanne Harris, wife of our Chairman Emeritus, Henry U. Harris, Jr., who joined the Hospital Board in 1961 and served with distinction as Chairman from 1972 to 1988. Mrs. Harris' deep devotion to her family, her inner strengths and gentle kindness combined to make her an inspiration to all who knew her and lent invaluable support to her husband's dedicated efforts in leading the Hospital toward the fulfillment of its mission of service. She will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to Mr. Harris, sons Henry III, Jonathan and Peter and to the entire Harris family. Richard L. Menschel Winfield P. Jones Co-Chairmen of the Board of Trustees John R. Ahearn John R. Reynolds Co-Chief Executive Officers Russell F. Warren, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief Philip D. Wilson, Jr., M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief Emeritus
        HARRIS-Mary Jeanne. Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory mourns the loss of their dear friend and patron Mary Jeanne Harris. A trustee of the Laboratory's Board of Trustees from 1982 - 1988 and a dedicated director of the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory Association since 1980, Mrs. Harris was an ardent and enthusiastic patron of basic cancer research as well as science education. The Laboratory will always be in her debt. On behalf of the trustees, scientists and staff, our deepest condolences go to her husband and our friend, Henry U. Harris, Jr.
