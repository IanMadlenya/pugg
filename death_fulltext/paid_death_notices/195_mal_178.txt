
        SHAIN-Charles. The trustees and officers of Connecticut College offer their heartfelt condolences to the family of President Emeritus Charles Shain, who died April 13 in Maine. President Shain served as a much beloved president from 1962 to 1974, and his visionary leadership continues to shine in the College's achievements and aspirations. Duncan N. Dayton, Chair of the Board of Trustees Carolyn Holleran, Vice Chair Barbara Shattuck Kohn, Vice Chair Norman Fainstein, President
