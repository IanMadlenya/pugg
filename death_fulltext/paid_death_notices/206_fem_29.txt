
        SOLOMON--Ruth K. Beloved mother of Susan Solomon, mother-in-law of Paul Goldberger, grandmother of Adam Hirsh and Ben and Alex Goldberger, great grandmother of Thibeaux Hirsh, died on March 2nd. She cherished all of life's gifts, from Mozart to Scrabble, and she inspired us as she met every challenge with courage and love. Services 10AM, Thursday, March 4, at Riverside Funeral Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. at 76 St., with burial to follow at Shaarey Pardes Cemetery, East Hampton.
