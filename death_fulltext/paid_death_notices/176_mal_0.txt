
        DERTOUZOS - Michael. Philips Research joins the faculty and students of the Laboratory for Computer Science at MIT in mourning the passing of Michael Dertouzos, Director since 1974. We extend our condolences to the family of Professor Dertouzos. Philips Research was pleased to have joined the Oxygen Alliance at MIT to share Professor Dertouzos' goal of a radical shift toward human centric computing. We rejoice in the knowledge that his work will continue at MIT. Ad Huijser CEO, Philips Research Barry Singer Managing Director, Philips Research USA
