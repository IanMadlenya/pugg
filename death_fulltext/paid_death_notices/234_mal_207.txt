
        COLLIGAN--Rev.Msgr. Joseph F. (Pastor Emeritus of Our Lady Queen of Martyrs R.C. Church, Centerport) Suddenly on July 17th, 2006. Beloved son of the late William and Florence (Thompson) Colligan. Dear brother of the late William J., Jr. and the late Charles A. Fond brotherin-law of Josephine Colligan, loving uncle of Mary Beth Woods, Patricia Moran, Jo Ann O'Hagen, Kathleen Gunderman, Jane Wilkins, William J., Steven and Paul Colligan. Beloved grand uncle of eighteen and great grand uncle of four. Reposing Community Room at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Centerport, Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9PM and Thursday 2-5 PM. Mass of Transferral Thursday 8PM. Funeral Mass Friday, 11AM Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, with Bishop William F. Murphy, Celebrant, interment Mount St. Marys Cemetery, Flushing, in lieu of flowers donations in his memory to Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Parish Outreach, 53 Prospect Road, Centerport, NY 11721 appreciated.
