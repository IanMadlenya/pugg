
        RABBINO-Mitchell. Central Synagogue of New York mourns with great sadness the passing of our esteemed and beloved Past President Mitch Rabbino. With a great love of Judaism, he led our congregation with dignity and vision. Mitch was a learned man with a fierce loyalty and dedication to our synagogue. Our clergy, leadership, and entire membership will remember Mitch with deep affection and extend condolences to Skit, their children Alissa, David and Hal, and their families. May his memory be a blessing. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Samuel Wasserman, Pres
