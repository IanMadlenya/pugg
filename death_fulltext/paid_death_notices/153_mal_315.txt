
        GOLDBLATT-Jacob, 92, of Hollywood, FL passed away Monday, October 25, 1999. Formerly of Great Neck, NY and Passaic, NJ He was the beloved husband of Lillian Leibman Goldblatt, who passed away July 16, 1998. Mr. Goldblatt is survived by his devoted son, Mark and daughter-in-law, Ann & cherished grandson, Max, all of Los Angeles, CA. & brother, Herman Goldblatt of Clifton, NJ. Graveside services will be held Wednesday, Oct. 27, 1999 in New Mount Carmel Cemetery, Queens, NY at 11:30 AM. Information: Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, Hollywood, FL 954-921-1776.
