
        SAMUELS-Robert P. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Robert Samuels on Thursday, April 5, 2001. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        SAMUELS-Robert. Our heartfelt condolences to Shirley, Laurie, Steven and Janet and all the grandchildren on the passing of their beloved Bob.         SAMUELS-Robert. The members, Board of Governors, and staff of Pine Hollow Country Club mourn the passing of our longtime member and good friend. We extend our sympathies to his wife, Shirley, and the entire family. The Board of Governors Pine Hollow Country Club
