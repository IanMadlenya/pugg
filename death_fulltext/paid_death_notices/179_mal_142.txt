
        KELLY-Edward F. Died peacefully at home December 9th, at age 78, after a courageous three year battle with cancer. For 50 years he was the loving husband of Jeanne Bradley. Devoted father of Marie Frances Burns, Kate Kelly Smith, Edward F. Jr., and T. Bradley. Adoring grandfather of Talley, Peter, Eddie, Isabel, Grace, Sam, Davis and Jack. He graduated from Cornell University, class of '44. During W.W. II, he was Aide-deCamp to General James F. Collins in the Pacific Theater. He worked in the advertising industry for over 30 years. An avid golfer he was a member of the U.S. Seniors' Golf Association and the Father/Son Golfing Organization. His courage, spirit and good humor will live on in the hearts of his children and grandchildren. He will always be remembered for his love of family and friends. Funeral mass will be celebrated on Wednesday, December 19, at 10AM, at St. Michael the Archangel, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT. The memorial mass will be Friday, January 4, at 10AM, at Holy Cross Church, Vero Beach, FL. Contributions in his name may be made to the VNA Hospice of Indian River County, 1111 36th Street, Vero Beach, Florida 32960.
