
        ROSENBERG -- Ross. Pre viously of NYC, passed away on May 19 in Philadelphia, PA. Beloved husband of Lucille, devoted father of Ellen Rosenberg-Caserta and Liz Rosenberg, and loving grandfather to Vanessa, Adam and Eli. He was a graduate of R.P.I., a WW II Demolition and Engineering Veteran, a chemical engineer, an amateur magician and he helped to build the second largest tool manufacturing company in the U.S. He was a dedicated family man, generous and loving. He will be deeply missed. >>AD#
