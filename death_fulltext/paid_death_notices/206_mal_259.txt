
        GROSSMAN -- Herbert. On March 19, 2004. Beloved husband of Linda Grossman. Dear son of the late Isidore and Celia Grossman. Cherished brother of Dr. Edward and Madeleine Grossman and the late Solomon Grossman and sister-in-law Shirley Grossman and brother-in-law Neal Goodless. Adored uncle.         GROSSMAN--Herbert. The Board of Governors and Members of Fenway Golf Club record with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member, Herbert Grossman, and extend to the members of his family our sincere condolences. Bruce D. Frank, President Roger Eichel, Secretary
