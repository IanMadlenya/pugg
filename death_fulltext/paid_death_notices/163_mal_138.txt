
        SACHDEV-Ved, M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hosptial mourns the passing of our long-standing and beloved colleague. Dr. Sachdev began his prominent career in neurosurgery and neuroanatomy at Mount Sinai in 1970. He dedicated himself to the teaching and practice of medicine and was highly regarded for his clinical and academic skills. He will always be remembered for his commitment to his patients and to the institution as well as for his kind and gentle nature. Our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Stanley Tuhrim, M.D., President Terry Weill, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
