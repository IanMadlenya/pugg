
        BLAKE-Loretta (Petty) Nelson. On March 5, in Fallbrook, CA. Petty was a stylist and reporter at LIFE magazine, among other positions. Beloved mother of Casey Nelson Blake, mother-in-law of Arlene Shaner, stepmother of Christina Blake Oliver, grandmother of Hannah and Laura Blake, and aunt of Kathy Beal, Elizabeth Ininns, William Kamberg, and their children. Petty's humor, generosity, and love of beauty will be remembered by all who knew her. In lieu of flowers, donations to Fallbrook Land Conservancy, POB 2701, Fallbrook, CA. 92088.
