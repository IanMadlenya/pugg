
        RUTLEDGE-John W., Sr. A resident of New Canaan for over 30 years. Died Sunday, July 30, 2000 at Norwalk Hospital. He was the husband of Mary Jo McKinley Rutledge. Born on March 12, 1923, in Eureka, California, he was the son of the late William E. & Ellen Jordan Rutledge. He was the former President of the Bulova Watch Company, in New York City, and was the Senior Vice President and Director of the Xerox Corporation in Stamford, CT. Mr. Rutledge was a graduate of Northwestern University, with a Bachelor of Science degree in 1943, and a Master's in Business from Harvard University. He later earned a Master's in Arts Degree in Archaeology from Yale University in 1985. He was a Veteran of WWII serving in the United States Navy, with rank of Lieutenant JG serving aboard the USS Missouri. In addition to his wife Mary Jo, of New Canaan, Jack is survived by two daughters Ellen Valenti, and Amy Jebrine of Stamford, one son John W. Rutledge Jr. of Darien, one sister Katherine Van Slambrouck, of San Jose California, and two brothers, Edward J. Rutledge, of Glenview, Illinois, and Robert J. Rutledge of Williamsport, PA. Three grandchildren Courtney W. Rutledge, Megan D. Rutledge, and Olivia M. Jebrine also survive. He was predeceased by his brother Paul J. Rutledge. A memorial Mass of Christian Burial will be held in the St. Aloysius R.C. Church in New Canaan 11:00AM on Friday August 4, 2000. There will be no calling Hours and interment will be private. The family has requested in lieu of flowers, donations be considered to the New Canaan Library, 151 Main Street, New Canaan or the New Canaan Nature Center, 144 Oenoke Ridge Road, New Canaan, Connecticut 06840. The F. Hoyt Funeral Home, 199 Main Street, New Canaan is handling the funeral services.
