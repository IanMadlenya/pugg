
        TRASK-Frederick K., Jr. The boards, faculty and staff of The New York Hospital and Cornell University Medical College are deeply saddened by the passing of esteemed New York Hospital Life Governor Frederick K. Trask, Jr. Mr. Trask joined the Board of Governors of The Society of the New York Hospital in 1954, and rendered distinguished service as president from 1961 to 1965. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his wife and family. John T. McGillicuddy Chairman, Board of Governors The New York Hospital David B. Skinner, MD President and CEO The New York Hospital Sanford I. Weill Chairman, Board of Overseers Cornell University Medical College Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., MD Stephen and Suzanne Weiss Dean Cornell University Medical College
