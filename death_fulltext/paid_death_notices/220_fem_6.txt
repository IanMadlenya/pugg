
        ROSENTHAL--Freda. Died April 28, in her 98th year. Beloved wife of the late Irving R. Devoted mother of Dr. and Mrs. Stuart Rosenthal and Judge and Mrs. Louis Rosenthal. Loving grandmother of Dr. Seth Rosenthal, Suzanne (Ryan) Brackley, Mr. and Mrs. John Hoffman, Joshua Rosenthal and Justin Rosenthal. Great grandmother. Services Monday, May 2, 11:45 AM, I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn.
