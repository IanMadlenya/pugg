
        TERLIZZI-Alfred. With profound sadness, The Morchand Center for Clinical Competence of The Mount Sinai School of Medicine mourns the loss of a dear friend, Alfred Terlizzi. Alfred was a compassionate man with a heart as big and warm as his sense of humor. He will be greatly missed. Our sincere sympathy to his wife LuAnne, sons Michael and Nicholas, brothers Nicholas and Donald, sister Aldine, and his two beloved granddaughters, Brianna and Gabriella. Mark H. Swartz, M.D., Professor of Medicine and Medical Education Director, The Morchand Center
        TERLIZZI-Alfred S. It is with great sadness that the Board of Directors and staff of Career Transition For Dancers acknowledge the untimely passing of a long time advisor, supporter, and close friend Alfred Terlizzi. As President of Capezio Ballet Makers Inc., Mr. Terlizzi was a source of great encouragement for our efforts to serve the needs of dancers who are actively pursuing new directions for their professional life. His interest and inspiration will be missed by all. We extend our sympathies to his family, friends, and colleagues around the world. Cynthia Gregory, Chair Career Transition For Dancers
        TERLIZZI-Alfred. His family, the entire dance world and the Capezio, Ballet Makers family has suffered a great loss.
