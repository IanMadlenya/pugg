
        AUSTIN--Joan Hanley (nee Neary) passed away August 24th after a long struggle with Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis. She is survived by her husband, Francis M. Austin, Jr., her son, John S. Hanley, his wife Mary Ellen and their sons Ian and Ryan, a sister Mary Neary, a brother Thomas Neary and a number of nieces and nephews. She retired from New York Telephone where she was Executive -Assistant to a number of senior managers. Mrs. Austin was active as an officer of the Sutton area community and as a volunteer at PS 59 where she helped children for whom English was a second language. Funeral arrangements are private.
