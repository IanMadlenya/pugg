
        WEST-Evelyn M. Died at home on February 28, 2002. A long time member of MENSA and INTERTEL, she graduated with a B.A. and a Phi Beta Kappa from Mount Holyoke and an M.A. in experimental psychology from Columbia University. Her career was long and varied, including teaching, mediation and social work as well as administrative assignments in several City agencies. She headed a foundation for the promotion of badminton and organized consciousness-raising groups for NOW. She was a poet and a ballroom dancer. She is survived by a wide circle of friends who loved her. Funeral services will be held on Monday, March 4 at 12:00 noon at the Fairhaven Funeral Home, 1045 Fulton Street, Brooklyn.
