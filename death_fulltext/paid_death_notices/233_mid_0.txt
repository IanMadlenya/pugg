
        BASS--Esther. On April 27, 2006 at age 98. Widow of Benjamin Bass, founder of the Strand Bookstore in NYC. She worked as a bookkeeper and was active in B'nai B'rith, the civil rights movement and the Women's Strike for Peace, during the 1960's and 1970's. Survivors include her daughter and son-in-law, Eleanor and Warren Allen, grandsons, Daniel, Aaron and Zachary, stepdaughter Dorothy Bass and stepson Fred Bass and his wife Pat and granddaughter Nancy Bass Wyden. Memorial service will be held on June 11 at 11AM at New Mt. Carmel Cemetery. Contributions may be made to All Souls Unitarian Church, 1300 Gotham St., Watertown, NY 13601 or the American Maple Museum, POB 81, Croghan, NY 13327.
