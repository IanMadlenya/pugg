
        FRANKEL-Irving. On September 20, 2002, 75, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, lost his three and a half year battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Miriam. Cherished father of Sheryl, Larry and Marc. Father-in-law to Faye and Laurie. Devoted ''Popi'' to Stefani, Steven, Corey and Brian. His brilliant mind, quick wit and generosity of spirit will be sorely missed by all. Services to be held on Monday, September 23, at 12 noon, at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made by calling 1-800-IFANGEL.
