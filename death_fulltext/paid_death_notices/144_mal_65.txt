
        LYSOHIR-Walter. Died January 1, 1999 of Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of Queens, New York. Beloved husband of Betty, loving father of Steven and Bonnie, adored grandfather of Jack, Charlotte, Keith and Wallis. Cherished brother and uncle. Born July 10, 1917 in New York City, he was a pilot in WWII where he commanded a B-29 Bomber in the Pacific. He made his living in the brewing business until his retirement in 1983. An outgoing and charismatic friend legendary for his humor and honesty, equally well known for his passion for sports, the cinema and operatic tenors. He died of a massive heart attack after years of heroic prevention. Services to be announced. Donations in Walters name may be made to the heart fund.
