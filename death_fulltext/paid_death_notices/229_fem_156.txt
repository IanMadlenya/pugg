
        BERS--Mary. 91, cherished wife of the late Lipman Bers, died peacefully. A compassionate teacher, staunch liberal and enduring source of strength for her family. Survived by her children Ruth (Bob) Shapiro, Victor (Susan) Bers, her grandchildren, Ann (Dov) Scherzer and Naomi (Bruce) Langer, Alice (John) Baick and Diane (Jason) Malkin, her great-grandchildren Michael and Sarah Baick, Alexandra Malkin, her greatgrandchildren Jonathan, Julia and Gabriel Scherzer, Kayla and Sarina Langer, her brother Harry (Muschie) Kagan, her niece Debra (Luke) Birkeland, her nephew Mark (Lori) Kagan, and her great-nephew Benjamin Birkeland.
