
        ROMAN-Joseph. Suddenly on May 15, 2001 at age 67. Son of the late Dyonizy and Bronislawa Roman, brother of the late Daniel J. Roman. Survived by his sister Theresa R. Lynch and husband James, two nieces, and a nephew. A noted Interior Designer, Mr. Roman was a longtime resident of New York City and East Hampton. He attended the New York School of Interior Design and for many years was a member of the American Society of Interior Designers. Mr. Roman was also a Board Member of the Pebble Hill Plantation Museum in Thomasville, Georgia. Funeral Mass at Church of the Holy Family, 315 East 47th St., 6PM Friday, May 18th. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the New York School of Interior Design.
