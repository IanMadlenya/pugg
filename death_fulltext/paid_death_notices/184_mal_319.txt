
        HOROWITZ-Rabbi Philip. Beloved husband, father, brother, Saba (grandfather), rabbi, teacher, friend, died on Sunday, May 26th, at the age of 80. Brother of Marion Fahn. Father of Ilana Horowitz Ratner, Dr. Bethamie Horowitz, Daurielle Horowitz-Leopold. Father-in-law of Charles Ratner, Dr. Barry Holtz, David Leopold. Grandfather of Jessica, Josh, Sophia, Elan, Adam, Kevin (Alisa), Rachel (Richard), Jon (Meg). Born in Vienna, raised in New York City, he served as a rabbi and teacher in Cleveland for forty years. He had a profound knowledge of Jewish sources, a prodigious memory and a great love of Israel and the Hebrew language. May his memory be a blessing.
