
        ELLMAN-Sidney. On January 3, 2002. Forever loved by daughter Joyce, son Michael and his wife Sue Ann, grandchildren Jared, Courtney and Chelsea. Sidney was a devoted husband of the late Jeanette and a loving father and grandfather, who served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. His love, understanding and guidance will be missed by all who knew him. Funeral services Sunday, January 6th at 12 noon, from Garlick-Riverside Chapel, 1439 Unionport Road, Bronx, NY.
