
        LUNDBERG--Kathleen (Kelly), 66, of Yarmouthport, MA died September 23, 2003. Wife of Olle I. Lundberg. Mother of Steven, Karen, and Andrew Patterson. Also survived by two brothers, six sisters and many nieces and nephews. She lived life to the fullest and loved to travel. She had great style and grace. She was a staunch Democrat who loved to discuss politics. She was forever the Irish woman. Me morial donations to the Hospice Foundation of Cape Cod, 270 Communication Way, Hyannis, MA 02601. For information Hallett Funeral Home, South Yarmouth, MA.
