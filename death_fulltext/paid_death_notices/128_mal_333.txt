
        KAYNARD-Samuel M., J.D., L.L.M. Hofstra University is profoundly saddened by the death of Samuel M. Kaynard, Professor of Law at the Hofstra University School of Law. Professor Kaynard taught at the School of Law for eleven years. He received his B.A. from College of the City of New York, his J.D. from New York University School of Law, and his L.L.M. from Georgetown University. A nationally recognized expert and frequent speaker in the field of labor law and labor relations, Professor Kaynard was a revered and respected teacher. He is survived by his wife Ann, his son Alan and daughter-in-law, Joyce; his daughter Meryl and son-in-law Gerry Wall; and three granddaughters. In tribute to the memory of Professor Kaynard, all Hofstra University flags have been lowered to half-staff. James M. Shuart, President Helene Fortunoff, Chairperson Board of Trustees Hofstra University
