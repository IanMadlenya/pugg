
        PARKER-Maynard. Age 58, died of complications of leukemia on October 16. As reporter, editor, friend, father and husband, he was the best of the best. A memorial service will be held at 3 P.M., October 22, at the Church of the Heavenly Rest at Fifth Avenue and 90th Street. In lieu of flowers please make contributions in his memory to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Ave, New York City 10021.
