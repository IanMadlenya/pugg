
        CORSON-Harriette. On January 5, 2001, of Pompano Beach, FL. Formerly of Great Neck, NY, and New York City. Beloved wife of the late Nat Corson. Loving mother of the late Robert Corson and of Lee Corson. Mother-in-law of Jill. Grandmother of Wendy, David, Michael and Nancy. Greatgrandmother of Alex and Brody. Friend to all. Her spirit, strength and grace were inspirational. She will be dearly missed. Service Monday, Riverside Memorial, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
