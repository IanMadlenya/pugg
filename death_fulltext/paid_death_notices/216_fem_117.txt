
        GEORGE--Regina. A loving daughter and sister, Regina George passed away on January 9, 2005. She is survived by her parents, Thomas and Aleyamma, her older sister Christina, and brother-in-law Jaison. In addition, she leaves behind many aunts, uncles, cousins and countless friends. She graduated from NYU in June 2002 with a Bachelor's degree in psychology. Regina enjoyed her work as an EMT in NYC and was actively pursuing a career in medicine where she would continue doing what she loved by helping others. Although Regina was only 24 years old, she has touched many lives. Her infectious laughter and smile will always remain in our hearts.
