
        GUIDA-Jennie. On January 27, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Antonio. Loving mother to Elizabeth McIntyre and Anthony Guida. Devoted grandmother to Suzanne Loomis, Kristen and Meghan Guida and great-grandmother to Stephen, Jenny and Billy Loomis. Mrs. Guida will always be remembered for her volunteer work at Frances Schrevier Nursing Home where she was instrumental in creating a Gift Shop she volunteered at for approximately 45 years. Reposing Wednesday 8:30-9:30 AM at Riverdale-on-Hudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Ave., Bronx, NY. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 10AM at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery. Gifts in her memory may be made to D'youville Manor, 1750 Quarry Rd, Yardley, PA. 19067.
