
        LEVY-Jim. Died January 1, 2000, at age 69 in Raleigh, NC. Loving father of Dr. Laurance Levy and his wife Vera of East Hampton, CT. Loving grandfather of Alexis, Marissa, and Bethany. Beloved brother of Dr. Roger Levy and his wife Judy of NYC and cherished uncle of Dr. Clifford Levy and his wife Nalda of Hopkinton, NH, and Elizabeth Greenfeld and her husband Dr. Jonathan of Tucson, AZ. Formerly of New York, he is also remembered by friends and hunting and fishing buddies in New York and North Carolina. Funeral service Wednesday, January 5, 12 noon at Frank Campbell's Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave., NYC.
