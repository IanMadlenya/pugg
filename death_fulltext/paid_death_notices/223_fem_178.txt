
        NEAHER -- Catherine King. June 13, 1915-August 19, 2005. Widow of U.S. District Judge Edward R. Neaher (Eastern District), sister of Ann and Eugene King, mother of Nancy (James Maas), Rosemary, Virginia (Stuart Pape) and Edward Jr. (Francesca Mengarelli). She also leaves eight grandchildren. A practicing attorney, she worked for the U.S. Social Security Appeals Division before her retirement in 1983. Graveside service to be held on Monday, August 29, 11 AM, Holy Rood Cemetery, Old Country Road, Westbury, NY. Memorial donations may be sent to St. John's University Law School.
