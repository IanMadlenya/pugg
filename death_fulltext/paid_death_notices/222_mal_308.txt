
        BENENSON--Edward H. Benenson Capital Partners mourns the loss of our distinguished cousin, partner and friend, Edward H. Benenson. We will miss his intelligence and sense of humor. We have over 60 years of business and friendship together to remember and cherish. We will always remember a man who was greatly dedicated to his family, business and philanthropy. Those of us who know how special he was share the sense of loss with his family, friends and associates. Our deepest sympathy to the entire Benenson family. Bill Benenson Fred Benenson Lawrence B.
