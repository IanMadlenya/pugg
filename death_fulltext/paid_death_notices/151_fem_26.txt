
        RICHMAN-Pauline Frances (Paula, to the world), on August 2nd, 1999 at age 94. She left us as she lived, peacefully and with dignity. Services Wednesday, August 4th, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. The family will receive guests prior to the service from noon till 1:15 PM.
