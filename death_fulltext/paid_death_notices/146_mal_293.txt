
        ROSEN-Benjamin. Deceased February 21, 1999. He was 104 years old. His survivors are a brother, Stanley B. Rosen, a sister, Claire Flohr, a niece, a grandnephew, a daughter, Claire Weiss, three grandsons and six great-grandchildren. He was a violinist, music teacher, a poet, and operated a music store until his 100th birthday. He was noted for the rarity of the music he sold. He was honored by Jascha Heifetz for his contribution to music. He was a good friend and loved by many.
