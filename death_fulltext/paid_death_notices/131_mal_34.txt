
        GILBERT-Paul L., M.D. The physicians and staff of Mount Sinai Faculty Practice Associates mourn the loss of Dr. Paul Gilbert, our colleague and friend. He will be missed by his many patients and by all of us who worked with him and cherished his wise counsel, his gentle irreverence and his genuine warmth. Our deepest condolences to his wife, Dr. Harriet Gilbert and to his entire family. Kristjan T. Ragnarsson, M.D. Chair, Board of Governors Burton P. Drayer, M.D. Vice Chair, Board of Governors Sloane Elman, Executive Director Mount Sinai School of Medicine Faculty Practice Associates.
