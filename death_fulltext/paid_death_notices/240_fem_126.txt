
        SKLAR - JACKSON--Isabel, died suddenly at her home in Alpine, New Jersey, on January 11, 2007. Isabel was a New York and New Jersey psychotherapist. She is survived by her loving husband, Fred Jackson, her brothers, Jay and Michael Sklar, grandchildren, nephews, nieces, and scores of friends. Service Sunday 2pm at ''The Riverside'', 76 and Amsterdam NYC. $(6$)SKLAR - JACKSON -- Isabel. Isabel brought us together with her capacity for love, for support. Her intensity in friendship presented each of us with more than we could have asked. Graceful in her own pain, honest about her challenges, she needed few words to open the right doors. She was at the center of our connections: her love is returned always. Friends of Isabel Sklar-Jackson
