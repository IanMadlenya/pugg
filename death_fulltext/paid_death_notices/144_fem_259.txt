
        KESTENBERG-Dr. Judith. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University notes with profound sorrow the passing of Dr. Judith Kestenberg. Her world renowned collection of interviews of child survivors of the Holocaust is proudly housed at Tel Aviv University's Wiener Library. Our heartfelt condolences to the family. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Alan L. Aufzien, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Robert J. Topchik, Pres. Stephen Lecker, Exec. V.P.
