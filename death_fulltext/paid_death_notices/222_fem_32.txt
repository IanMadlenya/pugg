
        KEMPNER--Nan. The Society of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center mourns the passing of our longtime Benefit Advisory Council member. Nan's imaginative and spirited leadership helped guide The Society's successful fundraising events for over thirty years. Nan touched many hearts and minds with her unremitting efforts to improve patient care for people with cancer and to advance cancer research. She will be greatly missed and we offer our sincerest condolences to her family. Mrs. Bijan Safai, President The Society of MSKCC
