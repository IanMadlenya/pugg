
        PERLESS-Meyer, C.P.A. 101, of Greenwich, CT, Bal Harbour, FL, and Manhattan Beach, NY, died January 29, 2001. After practicing accounting for seventy years, he retired at age 92. Predeceased by his dear wife, Ethel Glassman. He is survived by his loving son Robert and daughter-in-law Ellen, of Greenwich, CT.
