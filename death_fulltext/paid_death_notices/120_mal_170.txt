
        COHEN-George M. Devoted husband of Susan and loving father of Josh, Andrew, Greg and Allison. He was a senior parnter in the international law firm of Cleary Gottlieb Steen and Hamilton. He graduated form Northwestern University in 1957 and Yale Law School in 1960. George was a wonderful friend and valued colleague to all who knew him. Funeral services will be held today, at 11:15 AM, at Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, New York, 914-949-4717. Shiva will be observed at the Cohen home in Scarsdale through Sunday. Contributions in George's memory may be made to the Yale Law School Fund or Northwestern University.
        COHEN-George M. Congregation Kol Ami Jewish Community Center of White Plains records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member and conveys condolences to his bereaved family. Shira Milgrom, Rabbi Mark A. Hershey, President
