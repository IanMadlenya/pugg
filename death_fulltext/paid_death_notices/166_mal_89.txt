
        PAIGE-William C. Born January 3, 1922-died November 7, 2000 of emphysema at his home in Florida. Mr. Paige was Director of Advertising and Public Relations for the French House of Revillon Freres, in charge of fur parfums and wholesale divisions throughout the United States in the 1950s and 60s. He later became the Executive Vice President of the Ritter Fur Company. Mr. William B. Hoover, a friend, said memorial services will be held later.
