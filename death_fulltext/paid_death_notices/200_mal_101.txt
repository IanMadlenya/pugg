
        HARRIS--Harold, of Roslyn Heights, NY, on September 7, 2003. Former political columnist for three New York City newspapers and former assistant press secretary to Governors Rockefeller and Wilson. Past president of The Inner Circle. Beloved husband of Gertrude. Loving father of Sharon Dannis and Wayne Harris. Dear brother of Ruben J. Harris. Devoted grandfather of five. Services Tuesday 12:15pm at Sinai Chapels, 162-50 Horace Harding Expswy, Fresh Meadows, NY.
        HARRIS -- Harold. Temple Beth Sholom, Roslyn Heights, NY, mourns the passing of our beloved member Harold Harris. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife & family. Rabbi Alan B. Lucas Dr. Steven Zeldis, President
