
        WOLLHEIM-Norbert. The Officers and Members of the Kindertransport Association mourn the passing of Norbert Wollheim who escorted many of us on the rescue trips from Nazi Germany to England. We extend our sincere sympathy to his family. Kurt Goldberger, President
        WOLLHEIM-Norbert. A true hero of the survivors and my comrade for half a century. Your fight is over. Ours will continue as long as we live. Ernest W. Michel
