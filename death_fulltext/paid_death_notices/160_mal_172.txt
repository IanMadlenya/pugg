
        SANDERS-Morton, 79, formerly of Manhattan, died May 5 in Toms River, NJ. He worked as an architectural designer creating Morton Sanders/Space Planning and Design, retiring in 1983. He played drums and shekere on Sundays in Central Park for about 25 years; moved to NJ in 1997. Survived by his wife of 31 years, Eleanore and son Mark of Manhattan.
