
        TARTELL-Adam Ethan. Age 44. Pediatric dentist, died suddenly of a heart attack on August 1,2000. Dr. Tartell graudated from West Hempstead High School, Cobleskill College, Hofstra University, NYU College of Dentistry and in 1985 the Eastman Dental School, University of Rochester. He practiced Pedodontia and resided at 142 Flower Avenue West, Watertown, NY. He is survived by his wife, Sharon and children, Benjamin, Zachary and Clair, his parents, Dr. Robert and Mrs. Lottie Tartell, West Hempstead, and two brothers, Ross, Wilton, Conn. and Marc, Scarsdale, New York.
