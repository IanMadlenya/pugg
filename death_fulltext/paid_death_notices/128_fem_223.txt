        JASPAN-Dr. Jeanne Rafsky. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a distinguished physician and highly respected member of the community, beloved wife of Norman Jaspan and mother of Michael (and Terry) Jaspan and Ronald (and Michelle) Jaspan. Dr. Jeanne Jaspan was a granddaughter of the late Harry Fischel, a founder of Yeshiva University. The Harry and Jane Fischel Foundation-of which Michael Jaspan is executive director and Norman and Ronald Jaspan are members-established the Harry Fischel School for Higher Jewish Studies, the summer program of our Bernard Revel Graduate School. We extend our heartfelt condolences as well to grandchildren and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        JASPAN-Dr. Jeanne. The Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Dr. Jeanne Jaspan, beloved wife of Norman and mother of Michael and Terry and Ronald and Michelle and adoring grandmother.
