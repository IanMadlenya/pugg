
        SALTEN--David G. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of David G. Salten, an esteemed leader of the Jewish community whose service & commitment as executive director of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies from 1965-1969 strengthened our ability to serve those in need. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
