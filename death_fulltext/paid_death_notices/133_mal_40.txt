
        MILLS-Sanford. Feb. 1, 1998, beloved husband of the late Shirley (Levin) Mills. Residence 69 Boon Street, Narragansett, R.I. Dear father of Richard Mills, Hungary and Boston, MA., Dr. Keith Mills, Philadelphia, PA., Chana Smith, Brooklyn, NY, Ide Mills-Ratinetz, NYC and Joan Sullivan, Mystic, CT.
