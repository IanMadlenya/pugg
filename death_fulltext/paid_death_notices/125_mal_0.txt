
        LEHMANN-Manfred, Dr. Ohr Torah Institutions Of Israel records with sorrow the passing of Dr. Manfred Lehman. Scholarly intellectual of Judaic texts and passionate champion of his peoples rights to their homeland. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Shlomo Riskin, Dean Fred Ehrman, President
        LEHMANN-Manfred. We are stunned and saddened to learn of the passing of our beloved friend and teacher. He was a giant as a committed Jew; a highly respected scholar, newspaper columnist and author of a new book just released. He was involved in every aspect of Jewish life in the U.S., in Israel and around the world. Condolences to his wife Anne, children and grandchildren who loved him with all their hearts. May his family sustain strength in the memory of his tremendous achievements and his endless love for his family. Esther and Harry Walker
        LEHMANN-Manfred, Dr. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of a generous benefactor and esteemed scholar, the beloved father of our devoted parent Mrs. Karen Lehmann Eisner. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Lehmann and to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Principal David Kahn, Chairman Board of Trustees
        LEHMANN - Manfred. Lincoln Square Synagogue mourns the passing of Manfred Lehmann, beloved father of our devoted member and Governor, Karen Lehmann Eisner. A remarkable and generous man was lost to the, Jewish people. May the bereaved family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Richard Kestenbaum, President
