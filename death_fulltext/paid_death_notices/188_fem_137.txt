
        ARADER-Margaret (nee Byrne). Of Malvern, PA, formerly of Chappaqua, NY, on September 17, 2002. Contributions may be made to either Country Day School of the Sacred Heart, 480 Bryn Mawr Ave, Bryn Mawr, PA 19010; or St. Ann's Memorial Fund, 502 South Main St, Phoenixville, PA 19460.
