
        LEVENTHAL--Bessie. The Board of Trustees of The ALS Association National Office and Greater New York Chapter are deeply saddened by the loss of a good friend, Bessie Leventhal. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her daughter, Madelon Rand, her son-in-law Larry Rand, her grandchildren Amy, Bari, and Adam; Allan, Joshua and Emily. Bessie and her family have contributed greatly to advancing ALS research and finding a cure for ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease). Allen Finkelstein Alan Griffith Dorine Gordon Jacqueline Reinhard
