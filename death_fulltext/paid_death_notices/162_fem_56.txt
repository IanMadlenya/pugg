
        MILLER-Claire Strauss, of Chappaqua, NY, died on June 22, after a short illness at the age of 85. Born in Atlanta, GA., daughter of Oscar Richard Strauss and Rubye Rich Strauss, she graduated from Sarah Lawrence College in 1936 and received an MA in Dance Education from Columbia Teachers College. Mrs. Miller taught modern dance for over 50 years. She was a founder and leading member of the Unitarian Universalist Fellowship of No. Westchester. She was predeceased by one and one-half years by her husband of 50 years, Donald H. Miller, Jr. A previous marriage to Pettus Kaufman ended in divorce. She is survived by six children, Lindsey Itkin of NYC, Bruce Miller of Yorktown Heights, NY, Geoff Kaufman of New London, CT, Sheila Miller Bernson of Beirut, Lebanon, Meredith Davis of Mount Kisco, NY, and Dr. Donald H. Miller of Andover, Mass; by nine grandchildren and one great-grandchild. She is also survived by a niece, Margaret Strauss Weiller of Atlanta, Ga., and a nephew, Oscar Richard Strauss IV of Seattle, Wash. Memorial services will be held at 4 PM on Saturday, July 22, at the Unitarian Universalist Fellowship of No. Westchester in Mount Kisco, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Unitarian Universalist Fellowship of No. Westchester, POB 298, Mt. Kisco, NY 10549, or The Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Ave. Montgomery, AL 36104, or to Sarah Lawrence College, 1 Meadway, Bronxville, NY 10507.
