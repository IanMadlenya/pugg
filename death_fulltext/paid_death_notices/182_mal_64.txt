
        STUART-Francis H. Died in New York on March 3, age 76. Born in Cincinnati, OH, and grew up in the suburb of Glendale, Francis was the son of Archibald and Frances Stuart Marquette. He served in the Army in World War II and graduated from the School of Journalism at the University of Missouri. He lived in New York for the last 50 years, working as a reporter for the Daily News Record. He is survived by his brother Archibald Stuart of West Hartford, CT. His ashes will be buried next to his parents in Cincinnati. There will be a memorial service in New York at a later date.
