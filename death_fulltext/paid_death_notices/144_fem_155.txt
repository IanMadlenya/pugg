
        MILLER-Ruth. The family of Ruth Miller celebrates her life and mourns her passing on January 13, 1999. The loving wife of Alvin (deceased). The beloved mother of Carole, Arthur, and Michael. Mother-in-law of Laurence and Robin. The very proud and loving grandmother of Adam, Andrew, Alex and Zachary. She touched the lives of everyone who knew her with love, warmth, humor and friendship. We will all miss her dearly. Her passing leaves a void that will never be filled. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Eden II School for Autistic Children, 150 Granite Ave., Staten Island, NY 10303 in her name.
