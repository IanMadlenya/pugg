
        HALL-Edward J. The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at State University of New York-Health Sicence Center at Brooklyn was very saddened to hear about the death of Dr. J. Edward Hall. Dr. Hall was an active member of the faculty in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for 19 years during the period 1952-1971. Dr. Hall was appointed to the faculty in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology as a Clinical Assistant Professor in 1952, he was promoted to the level of an Associate Professor in 1954 and as a Professor in 1962. Dr. Hall served as Chief of Service at King's County Hospital Center during the period 1969-1970. He was appointed an Emeritus Professor in 1971. Dr. Hall made enormous contributions to the field of obstetrics and gynecology on a national and international level. He was an excellent teacher to the medical students and residents in the department. The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at State University of New York-Health Science Center at Brooklyn and King's County Hospital Center extends its deepest sympathy to the family.
