
        GIBELL-Jeanette. March 17, 1998, in Stamford, CT. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Loving sister of the late Joseph Yarchower. Cherished aunt of Harriet and Stan Simon and Karil Daniels, devoted great-aunt of Emily and Jessica. Proud member of the Monderer/ Ebner family. Born and educated in Bronx, NY, Jeanette enjoyed a long and satisfying career as Assistant Controller of the SSC & B/Lintas Advertising Agency until her retirement. Pride in her professional accomplishments, independence and a love of family and travel were an inspiration to all who knew her. Her sweet and loving nature will be deeply missed. Graveside services Friday, March 20, 11 A.M., Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
