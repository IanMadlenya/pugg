
        COHEN-Lilian S. Formerly of Long Beach, New York, died at age 102 in Stamford, Connecticut. Survived by her daughter Paula Manaly, grandchildren Elizabeth Breyer, Charles Freedgood, Elisse Ely and Marcia Ely, and seven great-grandchildren. Predeceased by husband Albert and daughter Frances Freedgood. Her joys were family, teaching, & creating art. Graveside services were private. Memorials may be made to Temple Sinai Prayerbook Fund or VNS-Hospice Care.
