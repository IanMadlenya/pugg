
        GETRAER-Joseph, Esq. Beth Israel Synagogue of Westport/Norwalk mourns the untimely passing of its beloved member of the Board, Joseph Getraer, Esq. of Greens Farms, Connecticut. A pillar of the Jewish Community and a leading philanthropist supporting our synagogue's Aleph Bet Preschool and Hebrew School, he will be sorely missed by his many friends within our growing congregation. Our condolences are extended to his devoted wife, June, and to his father-in-law, Gary Shulman, and to his son, Andrew, who serves with distinction as the Hillel Director of Rutgers University and to daughter-in-law, Jean, and grandchildren, Sandy, Benjamin, Alexander and Lindsay. Rabbi Yehoshua S. Hecht Spiritual Leader David Beck, M.D. President, Beth Israel Synagogue
        GETRAER-Joseph L. The partners of Katten Muchin Zavis Rosenman, formerly Rosenman & Colin, mourn the untimely death of their beloved partner, Joseph L. Getraer. Joe was a man for all seasons and a life force of the Firm who will sorely be missed. We are all the richer for his remarkable legal and leadership skills, and we will profit for years to come from his example. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his wife, June, his son, Andrew, and the entire Getraer family.
        GETRAER-Joseph L. The Board of Directors and all the associates of The Tiffen Company LLC mourn the loss of their attorney, confidant and friend, Joseph L. Getraer. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to his family.
