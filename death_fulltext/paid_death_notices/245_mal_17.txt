
        HANAUER--Bernard E., died peacefully with his family around him after a brief illness, May 30, 2007 at age 99. Beloved husband of Lilo (nee Growald) for 53 years. He will be sadly missed by daughters Elaine Hanauer Ravich and Linda Hanauer, son-in-law William Ravich and granddaughters Madeline Ravich and Emily Ravich. Donations can be made to YM & YWHA of Washington Heights and Inwood Senior Center, 54 Nagle Ave., NY, NY 10040.
