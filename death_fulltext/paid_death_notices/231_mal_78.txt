
        COHEN--Martin, D.D.S,, 74. Passed on April 8, 2006, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. He died at home with his wife and children by his side. Martin is survived by his wife Grace Oshin, his two sons Matthew and Steven, and his eight grandchildren. Those who knew him will never forget his kindness, wisdom, support and humor. He will be missed dearly. Services will be held Monday at 10 am, Riverside Chapel, 21 West Broadway, Mt. Vernon, NY; 914-664-6800.
