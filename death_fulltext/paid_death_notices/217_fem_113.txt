
        LUTZKY -- Leah (Rabino vich), D.C. (chiropractor). On February 12, 2005. Wife of the late Albert Lutzky. Mother of Harriet Lutzky, Susan Schmerler and Daniel Lutzky. Grandmother of Deborah and Todd Schmerler, Raymond and Andrew Lutzky. Great-grandmother of Samuel, Lucas and Nicholas Eade, Allyson and Nathaniel Schmerler. Sister of Jacob Rabinovich and Esther Horne. A vibrant, generous, inspiring and indomitable spirit, loved by all who knew her. We mourn our loss. Service to be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 76th St, on Sunday, February 20, 2005, at 2 pm.
