
        LEDERMAN-Eugene. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Eugene Lederman, beloved husband of Muriel and father of Mark Lederman. Mark, his wife, Carol, their daughters, Amy Verschleiser and her husband, Jeffrey Verschleiser, and Judy Lederman Kloner and her husband, Kenneth Kloner, are cherished friends of UJA-Federation, & devoted leaders of Jewish & humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mrs. Lederman & the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        LEDERMAN-Eugene. Eugene, maybe I let you down. If I had not given up gin you might still be around to kibbitz. Sylvia and I miss that and so much more. Muriel, we love you and wish you strength. Sylvia & Lew Lubitz Sara & Mort Richter
