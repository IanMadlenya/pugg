
        O'BRIEN-Katherine L. Died on February 5, in Philadelphia at age 90. Born Ellisburg, New York, on Oct. 22, 1907 and graduate of Wells College, Aurora, N.Y. She was a longtime resident of New York City before she moved to Cathedral Village, Philadelphia in 1990. She loved opera, traveling, and for many years was a devoted staff member of the New York Public Library. Kay O'Brien was the first Staten Island Borough Coordinator, founded Donnell Library Center and at retirement was Chief of Mid Manhattan Library. Funeral service Monday 11 A.M. St. Mary's Church, Cathedral Village, Philadelphia, PA (Tele. 215-482-6300). A memorial service to be held at the Library is being planned.
