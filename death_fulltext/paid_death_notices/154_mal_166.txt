
        WINEBURGH-Joel T. Age 58, of New City, NY, passed away November 11, 1999. Loving husband of Marjorie, father of Steven and Diana, Susan, and Amy, adoring grandfather of Henry, devoted son of Edith and the late Samuel Wineburgh, brother of Michael and Melinda, and cherished friend. He graduated from Syracuse University and Thunderbird AGSIM, and served as a 1st Lieutenant in the U.S. Army. He was a Senior Vice President of Cometals, Inc. Funeral services today, Hellman Memorial Chapel, Spring Valley, NY, at 1 PM. Memorial contributions may be made to Hadassah. Joel's energy and enthusiasm will be missed by family and friends around the world
