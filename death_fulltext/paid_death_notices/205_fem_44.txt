
        MELNICK -- Evelyn. We mourn her death at age 95. Devoted wife of the late Julius Melnick. She is survived by her children Michael and Barbara Segal, her brother Leonard Rockower, five grandchildren, five greatgrandchildren and many nieces and nephews. She was our beloved matriarch and role model, a wise, generous, loving woman. Services Thursday 1PM at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam.
