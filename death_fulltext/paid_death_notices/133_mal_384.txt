
        WU-William Iu-Liang, age 85, of Westport, CT., died Wednesday, February 25, 1998 in CT. Survivors include his loving wife, Jeannine; his son, Norman Wu, of Orlinda, CA; his daughter Suzanne WuBliss, of Seattle, WA; four grandchildren; Lori and William Wu, and Michael and Erica Bliss; and two nieces; Linda Wu, and Lorraine Wu-Chen. Funeral services were held privately by his family.
