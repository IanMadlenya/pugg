
        LEVY-Samuel. Born October 30, 1906. Died on December 24, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Anne, devoted father of Mark, loving brother to Rose & the late Henry, Miriam and Herman, cherished uncle and friend. He worked for the Wall St. firm of Abraham and Company from January 1924 until January 1974 and was one of its most loyal and respected employees. He was a member of the Brooklyn Jewish Center in the 1950s, 1960s and 1970s. He was also a member of the Lake Hopatcong Jewish Community Center from 1954 until his death, where he served as Financial Secretary from 1954 until 1970. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, December 26, 11:30AM, at Boulevard Chapels, 1895 Flatbush Avenue in Brooklyn. Anyone wishing to make a donation in his memory may contribute to the Lake Hopatcong Jewish Community Center or to the charity of one's choosing.
