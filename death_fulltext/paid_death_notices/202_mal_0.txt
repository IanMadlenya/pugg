
        DAL MOLIN--Armando, 86, Italian-born inventor of the music typewriter, musician, engineer, family man, and avid skier. His business, Music Typographers, of Oyster Bay, NY, engraved most of the music published in the 60's and 70's, earning him numerous Paul Revere Awards and recognition in the Groves Dictionary. He is survived by his wife, Elisabeth, of Shelburne, VT, and their three children and seven grandchildren.
