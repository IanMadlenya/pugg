
        KAPLAN--Yetta W. Died in her Florida home on November 10, 2005. Wife of the late Harry V. She leaves her daughter Linda Weis, son Gerald and his wife Jerilyn, grandchildren David, Adam, Joshua and Jonathan Weis and Evan, Joy and Dana Kaplan and four great-grandchildren. A longtime resident of Sea Gate, N.Y., Yetta met Harry when they were both 14 years old and recently celebrated 72 glorious years of marriage. She was a school teacher at P.S. 188 in Coney Island for 25 years. Family was everything to Yetta, her favorite saying ''May our children have happy parents.'' Funeral private
