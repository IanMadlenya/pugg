
        PEGRISH-Nima, Dr. The Solomon Schechter School of Westchester community mourns the death of Dr. Nima Pegrish, beloved & treasured member of our faculty for 22 years. Dr. Pegrish imbued a generation of students with a commitment to Jewish learning & a passionate love of the Jewish people and the State of Israel. She will be missed by all of us who considered ourselves to be her students. We offer our condolences to Joel, Nurit, and Dafna. May the memory of this Jewish teacher be a lasting blessing to our community. M. Mac Schwebel, Chairman, Board of Trustees James M. Dubin, Vice Chairman Dr. Elliot Spiegel, Headmaster
