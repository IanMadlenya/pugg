
        ROSS-Benjamin (Ben). Of Boca Raton, FL formerly of Great Neck, NY. Died peacefully on November 12. Husband of the late Ruth, loving father of Barbara Block and Kathy Adams, father-in-law of Seymour Block and Eric Adams, grandfather of Robert, Stacy, Alexandra, Todd and Joseph, and great grandfather of Samantha, Rachel and Evan. He was president of Ross Watch Case Company, a consultant to the jewelry industry, member of the Manufacturing Jewelers Association and the 24-Karat Club. Contributions to the charity of your choice.
