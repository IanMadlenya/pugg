
        HABER-Ruth. Age 89. Beloved wife of the late George. Adored sister of Irwin Resnick of San Diego, CA, and Bernard Resnick of Miami, FL. Died after a long battle with Alzheimers on February 16, 2002. Loved by many nieces, nephews and friends and admired for her long successful career with I. Magnin. She will always be remembered for her chic style and love of travel and adventure. Interment will be private. Donations may be made to the Alzheimers Foundation.
