
        GITTERMAN - Sidney, 84 passed away September 30, 2002. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on March 16, 1918. He was a WWII Veteran, a watchmaker and 25 year teacher, Assistant Principal at PS4, Bronx. He was married to the late Esther Jacobowitz Gitterman in 1950. He loved art, writing, music and hiking. Final years in Huron Woods Alzheimer Home, Ypsilanti, MI. Beloved father of Dena and David. Grandfather of Bill, Adam and Elizabeth. Brother of Yetta, Irene, Molly, Jack and the late Murray and Irving.
