
        MORRISON-Victor I. Jr., by his own hand on August 18th, 2000 in Old Lyme, Connecticut. Born Montecito, California on July 8, 1924 to Col. Victor I. Morrison and Nancy Park Morrison. Predeceased by a sister, Patricia, a brother, Park, and his first wife and mother of his children, Betsy Holmes McLeod of Pittsburgh. Survived by his son Victor III of Hinesburg, Vermont and family Patty Dunn, Park and Zelie Dunn-Morrison; by his son, Park of Rockport, Maine and family, Melissa Chapman, Storrey and Sarah; by his daughter, Susan of Alberquerque, New Mexico and his beloved wife Elsie Paulson Chapman Morrison.
