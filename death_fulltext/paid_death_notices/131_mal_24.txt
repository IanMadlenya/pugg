
        SAYDAH-Edward K. Husband of Lyra (nee Brzell), died Monday, December 1, 1997. A retired importer of embroidered linens, he moved from Brooklyn to Southbury, CT in 1981. Survived also by a son Charles E. Saydah of Monsey, NY, a daughter Marie L. Sherer of Middlebury, CT, a sister Lillian Saydah of New York and six grandchildren.
