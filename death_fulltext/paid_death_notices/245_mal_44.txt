
        McCORMICK--Dr. Charles G., a psychologist in private practice who taught at The New School for Social Research in New York City for 47 years, died May 11th, at age 93 at his home in Manhattan. He was born in New York City. He is survived by his wife, Ella Hansen McCormick of Manhattan; a son, James of Winston-Salem, NC, and a daughter, Margot of Syracuse, NY.
