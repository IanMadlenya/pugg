
        SHAPIRO-Muriel. Age 85. On December 29, 2002. Wife of the late David Shapiro. Mother of Ann Leda and Lee Harris. Mother-in-law of Gloria Barczak. Grandmother of Zachary. An exceptionally creative woman, poet, artist, humanitarian, involved in social and political activities. She will be dearly missed by family and many friends. Memorial at Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 Street, N.Y. Saturday, January 25th, at 1:30 P.M.
