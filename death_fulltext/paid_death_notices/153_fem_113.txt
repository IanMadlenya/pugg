
        SANDLER-Phyllis. A loving and caring person who touched many lives and was an inspiration to all. Beloved wife of Eli, devoted mother of Dr. Dennis, cherished aunt, great aunt and great-great aunt. Services at Boulevard-Riverside, 1895 Flatbush Avenue at Avenue L, Brooklyn, today at 11:30 AM. She will be missed.
        SANDLER-Phyllis. Congregation Tifereth Israel of Mapleton Park records with profound sorrow th passing of the wife of our President, Elliot Sandler and extends sincere condolences to the bereaved family. Milton Fox, Secretary-Treasurer
