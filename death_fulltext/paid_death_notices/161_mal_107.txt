
        JENKS-William Reid, 52, died after a long illness at his home on June 1, 2000. Mr. Jenks was born on January 18, 1948 in Waterbury, Ct. the son of Aldro and Jessica (Reid) Jenks. He was employed for many years as a stagehand, and was a member of the I.A.T.S.E., Local 74, of New Haven, Ct. for over twenty years. He is survived by his wife, Robin Cocking of Castleton, Vt., his mother, Jessica Wyckoff of Stuart, Fl., his brother, Aldro Jenks of Woodbury, Ct., and several nieces & nephews. He was predeceased by his father, Aldro Jenks, and his sister, Barbara Jenks Theiner.
