
        HOLZMAN-Seymour, Ph.D. Died March 18, 2002 at age 78 after a long illness. He is survived by Rosalie, his beloved wife of 35 years. He was a practicing clinical psychologist, earned his Ph.D. at University College, London, and did advanced work in psychotherapy and psychoanalysis at Postgraduate Center for Mental Health in New York City. He will be missed by many who knew him as a caring and supportive gentleman. A celebration of his life will be held at a future date. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Melvin D. Yahr Parkinson's Research Fund, Box 1137, Five East 98 Street, New York City 10029.
