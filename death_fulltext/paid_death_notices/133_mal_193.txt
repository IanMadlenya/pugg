
        MACMILLAN - Charles James Barr of Tallahassee, Fl. age 62. February 11, 1998 of Cancer. Survived by his wife Joan. Daughter Ann, son and daughter-in-law Tyler and Jill. Granddaughter Lindsay. He taught Philosophy of Education at Florida State University, specializing in the study of Ludwig Wittgenstein. BA and PhD Cornell M.A. Colgate. Memorial contributions to The Isabel Peard Scholarship Fund, Day Hall, Cornell University, Ithaca, N.Y.14850.
