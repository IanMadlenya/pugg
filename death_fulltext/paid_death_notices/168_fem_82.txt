
        SCHWARZ-Meta Cecile. Passed away on January 2, 2001 at the age of 97. She was the devoted wife of her late husband Francois L. Schwarz and is survived by her nephew, Herbert Orth, her cousin, Henrietta Cavallo, a host of friends and admirers and the many people who have been the recipients of her generosity and good will. Her personal history is a reflection of the time period in which and where she lived during her lifetime. She was a grand lady in the fullest sense of those words and she will be sorely missed by all those who had the good fortune to have known her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St., Monday, January 8, 4-7PM. Services to be held at the funeral home on Tuesday, January 9, 2001 at 2PM. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to charities of choice.
