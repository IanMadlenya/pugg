
        LOWRY-Donald I. Died June 17, 1999, in Camden, ME, residing in Cincinatti for many years before retiring as a Senior Vice President for Proctor & Gamble, and moving to Naples, FL. He leaves his wife, Barbara, two sons, Peter H., and Samuel A., three daughters, Pamela L., of Boston, MA, Sarah L. Fasoldt, of Camden, and Amy L. Poole, of Chicago. Also survived by a brother, Ralph A., of Charlottesville, VA., and a sister, Dorothy MacDonald, of Louisville, KY. He served in the U.S. Navy during W.W. II, graduated from Harvard College in 1941, where he was a John Harvard Fellow.
