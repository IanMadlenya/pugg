
        USSAK--Robert. Of Edison on Saturday, May 6, 2006, age 89. Loving husband of the late Eileen Ussak; devoted father of Michael of Edison, NJ and the late Richard Ussak; father-in-law of AnnMarie Ussak of Edison; cherished grandfather of Marianne and Amanda; beloved uncle of Celia, Lillian and Judith; and survived by his extended family in England. Direct cremation services have been handled by the Gosselin Funeral Home, Edison, NJ. Donations in his loving memory can be made to the Clara Barton First Aid Squad, 1079 Amboy Avenue, Edison, NJ 08837, or to the Sharing Network, 841 Mountain Ave, Springfield, NJ 07081.
