
        DARCY--Natalie. July 29, 1909-May 18, 2006. Dr. Darcy, the only girl of thirteen children born to Maud Horgan Darcy and Michael Darcy, was a psychologist and teacher at Brooklyn College and Fordham University for many years before retirement in 1972. She was a devoted parishoner and first chair person of the Parish Council at St. Joseph's Church in Greenwich Village, was a member of the Third Order of St. Francis, and of the Ladies of the Holy Sepulcher. Her body will lie in state at St. Joseph's Church, 371 6th Ave., between 8th St. and Waverly Place on Monday evening at 7PM, May 22, 2006. There will be a Funeral Mass at St. Joseph's on Tuesday, May 23 at 10AM followed by burial in St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, Queens. In lieu of flowers, contributions are suggested to St. Joseph's Church in her memory.
