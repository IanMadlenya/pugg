
        WEISENFELD--Karole W., 64, passed away June 30th, 2006, after a courageous fight with ovarian cancer. She graduated Mills College, BA and Columbia University, MA. She taught in NYC elementary schools; she enjoyed tennis, entertaining her friends and making beautiful collages, but her first love was her family. She dedicated her life to her husband, Roy, and her sons, Robert, Lee andMitchell (Tracy). She will be deeply missed by them and her sister, Judge Sandy Karlan (Joe Lupo), her mother, Lucille Karlan, grandchildren, Ryan and Samantha, and all of her relatives and friends. Contributions in her name may be made to Share, 1501 Broadway, Suite. 704A, New York, NY 10036.
