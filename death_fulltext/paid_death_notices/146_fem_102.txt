
        BASS-Anne Loewy. Died peacefully at home on March 5, 1999. Cherished mother of Rena Bass Forman and son-in-law Dr. Scott Forman. Adoring grandmother of Leila and Zaria. Anne was a survivor whose existence was challenged many times during her lifetime. She will always be remembered by her family and friends for her strength and courage. May peace, love and light be with her now and always. Graveside service at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY, on March 10 at 1 PM.
