
        HESS-Barbara F. (Chiefy). On November 6 after a long and courageous battle at the age of 77. Beloved wife of ''Tommy'', loving mother of John Newburger, Susan Newburger Karchmer and her husband Michael Karchmer and Billy Straus and his wife Lynne Weinstein. Devoted step-mother of Peter Hess and Debra Kenyon, Tom Hess and Georgianna Dwight and Jill Hess and James Wollum. Adored grandmother of Ian and Emma Straus. Step-grandmother of Rachel and Sara Hess, Carly and Camey Hess and Allie and Jamie Wollum. The family will be receiving at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St, Wednesday, November 8, from 4:30-7:30PM. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell on Friday November 10 at 11:30AM followed by a reception at the Cosmopolitan Club, 122 E. 66 St. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Society of Memorial Sloan Kettering, where Barbara served on the Ladies Board for many years or to Women in Need, 115 W. 31 St.
