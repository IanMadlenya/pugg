
        FAHY--Edward P., on January 24. Age 92. Husband of the late Constance. Father of Peter Fahy, Barbara Hayslip, Patricia Greenleaf and Ann Scozzafava. Loving grandfather of nine. He also leaves his long-time devoted partner, Theresa Klappenberger. Born in Brooklyn, he attended the US Coast Guard Academy and Fordham University. He had a 32 year career with the NYPD and retired as a captain in 1973. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Good Counsel, E. 90th St., January 27, 11 AM. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn.
