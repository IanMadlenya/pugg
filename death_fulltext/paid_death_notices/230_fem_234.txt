
        GREENHOUSE--Aurora de la Luz Fernandez y Menendez 94, died peacefully on March 25, at her home in Wellfleet, MA. Beloved wife of Bernard, devoted mother of Nancy Carter Menendez and Elena Delbanco (Nicholas), grandmother of Jonathan (Susan), Francesca (Nicholas), and Andrea (Alex), great grandmother of Christopher and Steven, and sister of Byron. Her extended family and extraordinary circle of friends share our grief. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to VNA Assoc of Cape Cod, 255 Independence Dr, Hyannis, MA 02601.
