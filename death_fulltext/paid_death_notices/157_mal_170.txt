
        QUINN-John Dillon, 87, of New Canaan, CT., founder of Quinn Construction Group, Inc., died February 13. He is survived by his wife, Agnes and their 10 children: John, Jr., Gerald, Elizabeth, Matthew, Barbara, Karen, Virginia, Regina, Kevin, and Dennis. Visiting will be 4:00-8:00 p.m. on February 16 at Hoyt's Funeral Home and an 11:00 a.m. funeral Mass February 17 at St. Aloysius Church. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to: The Friends of St. Aloysius School, P.O. Box 1342, New Canaan, CT 06840
