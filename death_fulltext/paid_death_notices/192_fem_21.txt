
        LEOPOLD-Jutta. The members of the Quadrille Committees are deeply saddened by the untimely passing of their dear friend and Committee member, Jutta Leopold. Particularly her spirited advice and fine humor will be missed at our meetings. We are fortunate to have known her. Our deepest sympathy to her husband Kurt and her daughter Michaela. Heide Herz, Co-Chairman The Quadrille Committees
