
        SULLIVAN--Brother J. Stephen, F.S.C. The Manhattan College Alumni Society mourns the passing of Brother J. Stephen Sullivan, F.S.C., the College's 17th President, on January 9th. His support for the Manhattan College Alumni Society generated new strength and accomplishments for the society as the colleges graduates increased significantly in number and geographical diversity. On behalf of all Manhattan College graduates, the society marks Brother Sullivan's passing with sadness. James Smith President Manhattan College Alumni Society
