
        GOODMAN-Melvin H. Beloved husband to Rosamond for 62 years, cherished father of Judith, Robert and Leonard, father-in-law of Martin and Jody, adored grandfather of Harrison, Julia and Steven, passed away on March 19, 2001. An owner of New Jerseybased Goodman's Furniture. Loved for his kindness, respected for his intellect, admired for his humor and his gentle nature. You will be in our lives forever.
