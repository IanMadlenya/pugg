
        LEBENBERG-Gerald. 57. Of Thiells, NY, died of a heart attack on Sunday, July 18, 1999. He is survived by his loving wife, Flo, three devoted daughters, Margot, Michelle and Ivette, two sons-in-law, a grandson, and his sister and brother-in-law, Estelle and Carl Fryburg. Gerald was loved and admired by all and will be greatly missed. The funeral will be held on Tuesday, July 20, 1999 at Hellman's Funeral Parlor in Spring Valley, NY. Family will sit shiva at his home in Theills, NY. Donations can be sent to the Jewish Federation in Gerald's name.
