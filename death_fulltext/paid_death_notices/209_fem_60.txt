
        WIGUTOFF--Frances, of Ft. Lauderdale, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Beloved wife of the late Albert Wigutoff and daughter of the late Morris Nasin. Died on June 7, 2004, age 83. Survived by son Edwin (Sharon) and daughter Laura Tarlowe (Anthony), grandchildren Lisa, Jason and the late Dana and two great-grandchildren, Jared and Jillian. She will be missed and loved always. Services private.
