
        RAPAPORT--Harold, April 11, 1922-April 25, 2007, 85. Beloved father of Hannah and Martin, father-in-law of Baba Salia and Carol Rapaport, adored grandfather of Rachel and Mariama Salia and Molly, Owen, Emmett, and Maggie Rapaport. Beloved brother-in-law of Florence, brother of Marlene, uncle of David, Lise, Daniel and Ellen. Summa cum laude graduate University of Pittsburgh, Brooklyn Polytechnic. US Navy WW II. GI Bill instrumental in his education. Accepted Fellow on Atomic Energy Commission 1949. RCA, ITT, and General Instrument Executive Vice President Rapaport Association helped companies achieve their goals. His amazing creative and analytic intellect was far reaching. Harold generously supported many organizations, individuals, and his family over the course of his lifetime, consistently putting others needs before his own. He was always there for people. His ability to listen carefully, explore the micro and macro pieces of a problem, and come up with wonderfully creative solutions was unparalleled. Harold will be greatly missed by his family and friends. Memorial May 1st, 11:30, Frank Patti & Kenneth Mikatarian F.D., Ft. Lee, NJ.
