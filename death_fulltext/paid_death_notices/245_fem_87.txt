
        SULLIVAN--Amy P., 54, of 43 Coult Lane, Old Lyme, CT died peacefully at her home June 10, 2007 surrounded by her family after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Wife of Bruce R. Josephy. Mother of Dan W. Sullivan. Born in Newark, NJ on March 31, 1953; daughter of Catherine Zizza Sullivan of West Long Branch, NJ and the Daniel Sullivan; daughterin-law of Joyce White Josephy of West Haven, CT. Amy loved to spend long hours working in her garden, and took a special pleasure in visiting the seaside where she grew up in New Jersey. She was an exceptionally strong woman who lived, and in the end passed, on her own terms. Her life was a beautiful one, and her memory will live forever in the hearts and minds of the many people who have been touched by her love. Private services with a memorial to be announced. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that memorial contributions be made to the Eugene O'Neill Theater Center, 305 Great Neck Rd., Waterford, CT 06485 or the Father Michael J. McGivney Center for Cancer Care, 1450 Chapel St., New Haven, CT 06511. The North Haven Funeral Home, 46 Washington Avenue, North Haven, CT has been entrusted with her services.
