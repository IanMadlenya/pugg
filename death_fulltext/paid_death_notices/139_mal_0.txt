
        GREATREX-Niles T. 72. Died July 28, 1998 in Cortland, NY. He was born January 5, 1926 in New York City, NY the son of William and Irene Reilly Greatrex and had been a resident of Syracuse since 1980 and previously resided in New York City. He was a past member of St. Paul's Catholic Church in New York City. Prior to his retirement in 1969, he was an Addressograph Operator at the New York Daily News for 15 years. He was a veteran of WWII, serving with the United States Navy from 1944 to 1946. Surviving are one brother; John J. Greatrex of Syracuse, NY, one sister; Emily Bostick of Dewitt, NY. He was predeceased by one brother; William Greatrex, and two sisters; Margaret Clarkin and Susan Rotavera. Donald L. Barber Funeral Home, 5016 North Main St, Homer, NY.
