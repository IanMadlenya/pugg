
        LACHTERMAN - Yetta Bessie. Born 1909. Died August 10, 2000, in Chicago, IL. Wife of the late Max. Mother of Paul (and Alita) of Park Ridge, IL, William (and Teddy) of Tulsa, OK. Sister of late Ida Landau and late Anna Brenner. Also survived by Selma and Saul Landsberg, of Tucson, AZ, and Danny and Goldy Landau of NYC. Graveside services Tuesday, 1:30 PM, at Beth David Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, you may donate to your favorite charity in her honor.
