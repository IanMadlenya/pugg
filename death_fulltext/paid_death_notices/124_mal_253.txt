
        MAHLKE-Philip A., of Bricktown, formerly of Flushing, N.Y. on May 16, 1997. He was employed by N.Y.C. Police Dept for 40 years, Chief Clerk for 5 years before retiring in 1972. Beloved husband of the late Esther. Devoted father of Philip R. and Linda Steel. Brother of Raymond and Elsa Morgan. Loving grandfather of 7. Great grandfather of 7. Funeral services were held at the Silverton Memorial Funeral Home, 2482 Church Road, Toms River, New Jersey.
