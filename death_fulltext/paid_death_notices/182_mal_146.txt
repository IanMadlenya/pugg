
        BUCHBINDER-Dr. Edward H. March 7, in Boca Raton, FL. Born in Bayonne, NJ, 1911. Last surviving and youngest of 11 children born to the late Meyer and Emma Esther Buchbinder from whom he and his siblings learned devotion to family, charity and religion. A distinguished graduate of Ohio College of Podiatric medicine. Survived by his caring wife Faye, two sons and their spouses and five grandchildren. Especially loved and respected by his 23 nieces and nephews and their spouses.
