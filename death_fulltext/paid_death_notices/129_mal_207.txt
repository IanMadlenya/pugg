
        TWERSKY-Yitzchak, Rabbi. The leadership and staff of UJAFederation mourn the passing of Rabbi Yitzchak Twersky, cherished father, beloved husband, renowned scholar, eminent educator. His life enriched those around him with his gentleness and knowledge. To his beloved wife, Atarah, to his daughter, Rebbitzin Tzipporah Rosenblatt, his son-in-law, Jonathan Rosenblatt, rabbi of the Riverdale Jewish Center, and his grandchildren, Yehuda, Tonya, Shmuel and Rivka, and the entire Twersky/Rosenblatt families, we send our deepest sympathies. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        TWERSKY - Professor Isadore. The American Academy for Jewish Research mourns the loss of its distinguished Fellow, Professor Isadore Twersky of Harvard University. Professor Twersky was a major researcher in medieval Jewish law and thought, trained gifted graduate students during his long tenure at Harvard University, and provided leadership for the young field of Jewish studies, both nationally and internationally. His legacy will enrich our field and organization for decades to come. Professor Robert Chazan, Pres. Professor Alfred Ivry, Secy.
