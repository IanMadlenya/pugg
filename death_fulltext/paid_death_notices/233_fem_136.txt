
        DULMAN--Dorothy, age 77, loving wife of the late Howard Dulman, mother of David and Laura Dulman, Abby and Barry Simon, Melissa and Troy Hangen, passed away June 23, 2006 in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. Adored Nanny to Aaron, Rebecca, Annie, Michael, Spencer, Julie and Claudia. Interment at Mt. Hope Cemetery, June 26, 10:30 AM. Lifelong New Yorker and part time Floridian, longtime member of Brae Burn Country Club in Purchase, NY. An accomplished artist and sculptor, she enjoyed many trips overseas and especially loved Italy.
