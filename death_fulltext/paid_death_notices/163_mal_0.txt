
        CONSIDINE-John J., Reverend Monsignor. The Department of Education of the Archdiocese of New York recalls with gratitude and high esteem the steadfast leadership of Monsignor John J. Considine in his role as President of the Association of Catholic Schools. Monsignor Considine brought wisdom and pastoral insight to the collective bargaining process. He provided guidance, direction and contemplation which form part of his legacy to all of our schools. We extend our sympathy to his sister, Veronica Carey and his family. Rev. Msgr. Thomas J. Bergin Vicar for Education The Archdiocese of New York
