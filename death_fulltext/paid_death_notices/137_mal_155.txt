
        LISTFIELD-Leonard M. Died in London on June 11, 1998. Loving husband to Helen for forty-seven years. Devoted father to Andrew and Emily. Cherished father-in-law to George. Adoring and adored granddaddy to Sasha. He will be deeply missed for his wit, wisdom, humor and loyalty by friends and family. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Saint Thomas Hospital, Mead Ward, Lambeth Palace Road, London SE1, 7EH, C/O Dr. Richard Leach.
