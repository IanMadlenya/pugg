
        KAUFMAN--Naida R. Born August 27, 1926, in Birmingham, England; died October 4, 2004, after a long battle with cancer. Her loving husband, Dr. Robert Kaufman predeceased her. She is survived by her two sons, Colin and Chris, their wives Irene and Barbara and her three grandchildren, Elizabeth, Molly and Natalie as well as her brothers Colin and Christopher Rose. A memorial service will be held in the near future. Should you wish to do so, the family would appreciate a donation to the American Cancer Society in lieu of flowers.
