
        COHEN-Paul. 1927 to 2000. A man with an acute sense of social justice and a lifelong commitment to living his ideals, stalwart of the union and civil rights movements, a talented amateur photographer, a vital repository of New York history, and raconteur extraordinaire. His loss will be deeply felt by his many family members and friends. Pre-deceased by wife Claire and son Michael. Survived by sisters Ethel and Gerti, partner and sole mate Helen, childhood friend Danny, loving daughter and son-in-law Debby and Joe. Graveside service at Beth Moses Cemetery, 1pm Monday, February 28th
