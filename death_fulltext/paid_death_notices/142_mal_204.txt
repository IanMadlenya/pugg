
        LOBEL-Sheldon. On November 15, 1998. Beloved husband of Barbara. Devoted father of Debra Lobel, Lisa Samuels and Mark Lobel. Loving father-in-law of Dr. Mitchell Samuels and Dr. Melissa Cohen. Adored and adoring grandfather of Cara, Dana and Michael Samuels. Dear brother of Millicent Haber and the late Irv Lobel. CoFounder of Lobel Chemical Corporation. His indomitable spirit and incredible courage throughout his epic struggle against cancer was truly inspiring. Services Wednesday, November 18th, 11:30AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. Interment Mount Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He will always be loved and missed.
