
        HEWITT-Mrs. Martha, 93. Longtime resident of Denver, NY, died November 4, 1998. Mrs. Hewitt was born Martha Mary Sutter, 152 W. 52 St., NYC, on Oct. 8, 1905. For over 40 years Mrs. Hewitt and her late husband, John B. Hewitt, Sr., ran the Catskills vacation boarding house, Crystal Spring Farm in Denver, NY. She is survived by her younger son, Joseph B. Hewitt, nine grandchildren, 13 great grandchildren, and countless dear friends. Visitation will be Friday, November 6 at the Sacred Heart Catholic Church, Margaretville, New York. Funeral November 7.
