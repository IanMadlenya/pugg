
        CORNELL-Frida. Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., expresses its deep sorrow at the passing of Frida Cornell, beloved wife of the late Ernest, devoted mother of Alan, loving grandmother of Lauren, Michael and his wife Kimberly and cherished great-grandmother of Chloe. As a Guardian of Tomorrow, Frida was a generous benefactor of the Hadassah Medical Organization. She was devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her legacy will live on through her good name, and she will be missed be all who knew her. Bonnie Lipton, President
