
        MILLS-Yvonne B. A Palm Beach, FL, resident, died November 22, 1997, at the age of 94. Predeceased by her husband, Harry C. Mills, she is survived by two daughters, Isabelle Harbolick of Vienna, VA, and Marie-Louise Mills of Lucca, Italy; two sons Arthur B. Kelly of Boca Raton, FL, and Peter E. Kelly of West Chester, OH. Funeral Mass 11 AM, November 26, at St. Edward Catholic Church, Palm Beach, FL. Entombment service 9 AM, November 28, at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
