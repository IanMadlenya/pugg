
        SPERLING-Alexander. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our devoted member Alexander Sperling, beloved husband of Mura and father of Carol Sperling and Nora Coblence. Deepest sympathy to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
        SPERLING-Alexander S. Passed away peacefully at his residence on September 14, 1999. Survived by his beloved wife, Mura, two daughters, three grandchildren who love him very dearly. Services will be held on Wednesday September 15th, 1 P.M at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
