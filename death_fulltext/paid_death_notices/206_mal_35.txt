
        BRODY -- Richard Samuel, Ed.D. Devoted and beloved husband of Karen (nee Slavin). Loving father of Rachel and Daniel, father-in-law of Michael and adoring grandfather of baby Alexandra. Dear brother of Miriam and brother-in-law of Isaac, Larry and Leslie. Beloved son of the late Louis & Anna Brody and son-in-law of Edward & the late Nettie Slavin. Guide and mentor to many students of Baruch College. All will miss his wise, kind and gentle nature. Memorial service March 4 at 12 noon, Brooklyn Heights Synagogue, 131 Remsen Street, Brooklyn, New York 11201. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his honor to Brooklyn Heights Synagogue Pre-School at above address.
