
        FRANKLIN -- Phyllis. The Board and staff of the American Council of Learned Societies mourn the passing of Phyllis Franklin, former Executive Director of the Modern Language Association, former member of the Council's Board and long-time leader of and friend to the members of its Conference of Administrative Officers. We extend our sympathy to her family and share the sadness of all who knew her. Pauline YU, President American Council of Learned Societies
