
        MANDELBLATT--Adam, of Manhasset at 83 years. Holocaust survivor and business owner in the garment industry for over 45 years. He is survived by his devoted wife Irene, his loving children, Beverly and Alan Berson, Steven and Lauren Mandel, and his adoring grandchildren; Jason, Jennifer, Whitney, Jared, and Evan. He will always be remembered as a unique, loving, and generous man. Services will take place Wednesday at 11:15 am at Sinai Chapels of Fresh Meadows, New York. Interment to follow at New Montefiore Cemetery.
