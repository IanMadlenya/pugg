
        D'AMICO-Mabel. In Amagansett, NY on November 22, 1998. The Board of Trustees of The Victor D'Amico Institute of Art/The Art Barge mourn the loss of Mabel D'Amico. We will sadly miss her inspiration and creative energy. Burial service at Cedar Lawn cemetery, Easthampton, NY on November 28, 11am. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Art Barge, PO Box 1266, Amagansett, NY 11930. C. Kohan, President of the Board of Trustees, The Art Barge
