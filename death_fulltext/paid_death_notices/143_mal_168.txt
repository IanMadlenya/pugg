
        VOGEL-Oscar M. On December 4th, 1998. Beloved husband of Eleanor, dear father of Gerald, Robert and Kathleen Mullins. Loving grandfather of six. Survived by two sisters, predeceased by three brothers and one sister. He was in the Navy for four years and served in World War II. He was an employee of the New York Times for thirty-one years.
        VOGEL-Oscar M. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Oscar M. Vogel, associated with The Times since 1959 until retiring in 1990.
