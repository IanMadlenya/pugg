
        NOONEY-Edward, J. Passed away December 26, 2001. He is survived by his wife Edna, his sister Gladys, brother-in-law Kennith. Brother of the late George. He is also survived by his sister-in-law Mary, several nephews and nieces. He worked in the newspaper industry for forty-five years, thirty-five of them with The New York Times. Services will be held at The Stafford Funeral Home, Lake George, New York on December 28, 2001. Burial will be held at the Pine View Cemetery Queensbury, New York, on December 29.
