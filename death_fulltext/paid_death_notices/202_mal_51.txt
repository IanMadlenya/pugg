
        FORREST--Richard E. October 25. Devoted husband of Judy. Father of Kathleen Forrest Shun and husband Adam, and Susan Forrest-Reynolds. Beloved grandfather of Nicholas and Lucia. Citizen of New York City. At his request a remembrance of Richard will be held at his home on December 7, from 4 to 7 PM. Donations in Richard's name may be made to the Hudson River Foundation or Scenic Hudson. ''Phlebas the Phoenician, a fortnight dead...''
