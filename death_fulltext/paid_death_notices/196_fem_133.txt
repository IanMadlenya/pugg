
        RICHMAN-Gloria. The family of Gloria Richman, wife of the late Dr. Benjamin Richman, mourns the loss of their beloved mother, grandmother and great-grandmother, who passed away on May 15, 2003. She is survived by her children, Lorna Bodian and Ronald Richman, and their spouses Eugene and Rona, six grandchildren and ten great-grandchildren. May her courage and zest for life live on in those of us who loved her. Services will be held at Temple Beth-El, Monday, May 19, at 11:30 AM, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, NY.
