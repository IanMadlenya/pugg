
        BLOS-Betsy Thomas. Our beloved gift from God, returned to God, May 20, 2000. She will be eternally missed. Born in York, SC, Betsy was one of six children of Dunnie and Elsie Thomas. Betsy moved to New York City after receiving her Bachelor's Degree from the University of South Carolina. She received her master's and Ph.D from New York University and a M.S.W. from Yeshiva University. Betsy was married to Peter Blos, renowned adolescent Psychoanalyst, now deceased. Betsy taught in elementary schools and later joined the Institute for Developmental studies at N.Y.U. There she taught preschool in the research, experimental classes that later lead to the development of the Head Start Programs. She also served as a teacher trainer for Head Start and Follow Through Programs throughout the country and the Virgin Islands. Later in her career Betsy became a Psychoanalyst after completing her psychoanalytic training at the New York Freudian Society. Betsy is survived by her brother, Dr. Benjamin Thomas, and her sister, Wilma Florence and her devoted niece, Lea and other nieces, nephews and extended family.
