
        HIRSCHHORN-Ellen S. of West Orange, NJ, died February 1, 2000 after a short illness. Age 76, she was a Founding Member and Vice-President of the Jewish Historical Society of MetroWest and a co-President of the Essex County Chapter of the Brandeis National Women's Committee. She served as a Board Member of the Daughters of Israel Geriatric Center in West Orange and the United Jewish Federation of MetroWest. She is survived by her beloved husband of 52 years, Isidor S. Hirschhorn, her son, Robert, her daughter, Susan Hirschhorn and son-in-law, Arthur Klebanoff, two grandsons, Alexander and Jonathan Klebanoff and her mother, Anna Stein. Funeral services will be held at Temple Sharey TefiloIsrael, South Orange, NJ, on Friday, February 4, 2000 at 10:30 AM. The family has requested that in lieu of flowers, contributions be made in her memory to either the Brandeis National Women's Committee c/o Sylvia Baron, 806 Morris Turnpike, Short Hills, NJ 07078 or the Jewish Historical Society of MetroWest, 901 Route 10 East, Whippany, NJ 07981
