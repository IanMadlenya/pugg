
        LEAHY-Dorothy M., 83, of West Palm Beach, Fla., passed away February 4, 2001. She was an interior decorator who graduated from Pratt University in Brooklyn, NY. She attended Bethesda by the Sea Episcopal Church in Palm Beach, Fla. Graveside services will be held Tuesday, February 27, 1 PM, at Hillcrest Memorial Park, West Palm Beach, Fla.
