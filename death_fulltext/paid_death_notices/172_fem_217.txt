
        LEFCOURT-Rachel. Died peacefully on May 25, 2001, a day before her 91st birthday. Wife of the late Albert Lefcourt, an optomotrist, who died in 1998. Survived by her stepchildren Robert and Gerald and her nephew, Ian Ehrlich. Grandmother of Jenny, Daniel, Jeffrey and Karen. Graveside service at Mt. Zion Cemetery, LIC, NY, at 2 PM, on Tuesday, May 29.
