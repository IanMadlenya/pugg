
        HOLLAND--Frank L., 79, of West Orange, NJ and Wilmington, VT, died in an airplane crash in Hamptonburgh, NY on December 14, 2006. He was predeceased by his wife of 42 years, Arlene Holland. Surviving are his daughter Lysa Holland and her husband Tom McNichol, son Robert Holland and his wife Michelle Holland, daughter Nancy Holland and her husband Rick Waxman as well as six grandchildren. The family has requested that any donations be made to The Valerie Fund at www.thevaleriefund.org
