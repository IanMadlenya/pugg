
        WEINDLING-Leon David. Of Manhattan. On May 8, at 78 years of age. Beloved husband of Sonia Weindling. Devoted father of Suzanne Burakoff and Steven Weindling and father-in-law of Dr. Steven Burakoff. Adored grandfather of Alexis Weindling Burakoff and brother of Irene Probstein and Irwin Weindling. He was the President of London Star, Ltd. A compassionate and generous man who enhanced the lives of his family, friends and colleagues, he served as an officer in both the Belgian and US armies during WW II. A beneficent supporter of many charities and a devoted friend to Israel. He will be missed by everyone he touched. Services will be held Monday, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th and Amsterdam.
