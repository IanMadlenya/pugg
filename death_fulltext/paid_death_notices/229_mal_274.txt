
        AMES--Lincoln, 73. Of Montclair, NJ, and Little Compton, RI, died suddenly from a stroke on Wednesday, February 22. Beloved husband of Aubin Zabriskie Ames; devoted father of Hyla Ames Bauer, Mark Zabriskie Ames, and David Wyllys Ames; loving grandfather of Liesel Bauer, Clara Bauer, and Anna Morgan Ames; and dear brother of Hyla Ames Troxell and Theodore Ames. Mr. Ames was a graduate of the Hill School, Yale University, and Harvard Business School, and served as an Air Intelligence Officer in the U.S. Navy. He was a Director of Blyth & Co. Inc., and finished his career as Executive Vice President of Dean Witter Reynolds, Inc., serving on its Board of Directors and Executive Committee. He was also a Director of Montclair Savings Bank. At the time of his death he was majority owner and Chairman of Hammer Manufacturing Co. in Linden, NJ. A memorial service will be held Saturday, February 25, at 2:00 P.M. at Central Presbyterian Church, 46 Park St., Montclair, NJ. The family requests that in lieu of flowers memorial donations may be sent to The HillSchool, 717 E.
