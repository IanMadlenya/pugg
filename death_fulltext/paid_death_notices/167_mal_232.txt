
        TARG-David. On December 16, 2000, at age 89. Beloved husband of Pauline, to whom he was married for 58 years. Devoted uncle of Richard Engelman, Robert Targ, Louis, Nora, and Amelia Peck, and Patricia Nocella. Born on October 24, 1911, in Brooklyn, NY, he was a graduate of the Brooklyn Law School, and worked for the Lehigh Railroad in the years leading up to and during World War II. After the war, he established a law office in Manhattan, and was actively engaged in the practice of law for 45 years. Committed to the philosophy of Henry George, he taught at the Henry George School for nearly five decades. Services will be held Wednesday at 11:30 AM, at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
