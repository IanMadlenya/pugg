
        MULLY-George, suddenly and unexpectedly at home on Easter Monday, at the age of 73, much loved husband of Ann Mully. Loving stepfather of Max Born (Sue Saunders), Sebastian Born (Sue Thompson) and Georgina Born (Andrew Barry). Grandfather of Kip, Lily, Rosie, Theo and Clara. He will be greatly missed by all of his family, friends and co-workers. ''He was a man, take him for all in all, we shall not look upon his like again.'' Friends may visit at the Central Chapel of Hulse, Playfair & McGarry, 315 McLeod Street, on Thursday, April 8, from 7-9 PM, and Friday, April 9, from 2-4 PM. Reception to follow at the Hulse, Playfair & McGarry Resource and Reception Center, 328 McLeod Street. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Canadian Cooperative Association would be appreciated by the family.
