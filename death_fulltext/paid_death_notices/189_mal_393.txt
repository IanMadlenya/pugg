
        MEYER-Joseph H., 82. Of Stuart, FL., and Port Ewen, NY. Died on October 30, 2002. Beloved husband of 58 years to Anne, father of Joseph, William, Joanne, Margaret and Laura. Grandfather of four, and brother of three sisters Maxine Greene, his twin, Jeanne Shinefield and Carol Meyer (deceased). Veteran of the U.S. Army Air Corps, President of Richelieu Pearls Corp., Board Member and Alumnus of Alfred University, Board President of South Nassau Communities Hospital, Member of Young Presidents Organization (YPO).
