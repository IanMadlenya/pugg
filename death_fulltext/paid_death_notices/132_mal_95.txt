
        SHEREFF-Henry David. In loving memory of Henry David Shereff. We regret to announce the death of Henry David Shereff, January 6, 1998 at 78 years old. Born May 25, 1919, son of Solomon and Leah. Beloved husband of Meta. Beloved father of Stephen, Sidney and Charles. Beloved brother-inlaw to Anna, Shirley and Rose. Beloved father-in-law to Sharon. Beloved grandfather of Rachel. He graduated from Townsend Harris High School and CCNY. He served in the USAAF during W.W. II and was honorably discharged with the rank of Master sergeant. After the war he went to Columbia University Law School and graduated in 1947. He was accepted to the NY Bar in 1948. He worked as a Staff Attorney to the NYS Revision Commission. He then joined Berlack, Israels & Liberman where he became a managing partner and stayed until 1971 when he founded his own firm Shereff, Friedman, Hoffman & Goodman. He worked there specializing in corporate tax and agricultural cooperative law. He wrote numerous articles and three books for the Practicing Law Institute. He wrote one law textbook for the ALI of the American Bar Association. He reestablished Townsend Harris High School for which Queens Borough President Claire Schulman declared April 26, 1991 Henry D. Shereff day. He received the Founders Award from the Townsend Harris High School Alumni Association on June 23, 1988. He was also on the Board of Directors of the Columbia University Law School alumni Association. In his spare time he enjoyed deep sea fishing, handball, jogging and oil painting. His great spirit and love of life will be deeply missed and remembered by everyone who knew and loved him. There will be a service at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial chapels at 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, New York, Thursday, January 8, 1998 at 1 P.M., followed by burial at Mount Hebron Cemetery in Flushing, Queens.
