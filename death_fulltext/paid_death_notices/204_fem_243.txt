
        MEIKLEJOHN--Cornelia Billard, died January 17th in Winter Park, FL after a brief illness. She was a woman before her time, educated at Smith and Harvard Graduate School of Architecture. A champion of literacy and the arts, she was ever involved as a philanthropist and volunteer. She is survived by her son, Pierre Billard, and his wife, Rebecca, of Pulaski, TN and her niece Chris Everett of Missoula, MT. Memorials may be sent in her name to National Public Radio.
