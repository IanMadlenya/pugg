
        GILLMAN-Beatrice, 89. On May 28th. She leaves three daughters, Justine, Stephanie and Cathy, her late son Michael and husband Dr. Jac. Gillman, 5 grandchildren, two great grandchildren, sister Ruth and brother Abe. A former NYC special ed teacher, she brought love, laughter and life to all who knew her. Bea's presence inspired those at Daughters of Miriam. She was proud of her accomplishments from President of the Tenants Association to Editor of their newsletter. A true lexicographer, Bea brought her love of learning to all and nobody did the Sunday New York Times crossword puzzle (in pen) with greater speed.
