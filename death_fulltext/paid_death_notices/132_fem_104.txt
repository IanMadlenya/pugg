
        SHELDON-Edna Ether. 1898-1998. Longtime resident of New York City. Collector of postcards and Americana. Died at her daughter Edna's home in San Diego on Thursday, January 8. She is survived by her daughter Edna V. Deodato, and her son Ronald L. Wohleking, of El Paso, and 13 grandchildren. As matriarch of her family, her legacy lives on. She will be interred next to her late husband, Bert Temple Sheldon.
