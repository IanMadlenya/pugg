
        LURIE-Arnold David. A memorial service will take place on Sunday, April 16, 2000 at 2 PM at Teeters' Funeral Home in Hawley, PA for Dr. Arnold David Lurie, of Lake Ariel, PA, formerly of Baldwin, NY, who died April 5, 2000. His wife, Kay Elizabeth Nord Lurie, survives. In lieu of flowers the family asks that memorial donations be made to VNA Hospice of Lackawanna County, 301 Delaware Ave., Olyphant, PA 18447.
