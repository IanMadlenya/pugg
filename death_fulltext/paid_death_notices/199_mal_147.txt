
        SMIRNOFF--Alexander G. Director of Telecommunications and Broadcasting at the Empire State Building. Died August 11, 2003. Born 1940 was 62 years old, wife Susan, one son, two daughters, two grandchildren, mother and sister. Funeral services, 10 AM, Saturday, August 16 at Noroton Presbyterian Church, Post Road, Darien, CT. Mr. Smirnoff was responsible for the 15 television stations and 20 radio stations broadcasting from The Empire State Building. He joined The Empire State Building in 1991. Helmsley-Spear, Inc. and Empire State Building Company LLC mourn the passing of Alexander G. Smirnoff. He will be greatly missed by his friends, colleagues and the community.
        SMIRNOFF--Alexander G. of Darien, CT on August 11, 2003. Husband of Suzanne Waters Smirnoff; father of Anastasia Arevalo, George A. Smirnoff and Christina Smirnoff, son of Nina Smirnoff & grandfather of two. A Memorial Service will be held at the Noroton Presbyterian Church, Darien, CT on Saturday, August 16, 2003 at 10:00 AM.
