
        SIMMONS--Jack L., 86, of New York, NY, passed away on June 7, 2006, in Gainesville, FL. Born in Montgomery, AL, and later moving to New York. Mr. Simmons worked for International Longevity Center. He received his Master's degree from Fordam University and also served in the U.S. Navy. Mr. Simmons volunteered and donated to New York museums, AIDS Network, and S.A.G.E. He contributed to many charities and local causes that were near and dear to his heart in the greater New York area. He was a lover and participant in theater, opera, and the arts. He is survived by six nieces, his loving family and friends. Information, Forest Meadows Funeral Home, Gainesville, FL.
