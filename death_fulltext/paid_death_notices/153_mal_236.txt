
        ROGOVIN-Jack. Age 87, passed away on October 17, 1999 in Hollywood, Florida due to complications of pneumonia and a heart attack. Longtime teacher of French, Spanish & Russian and Guidance Counselor at Tilden High School in Brooklyn. Active in the Teachers Union. He served in the Army Signal Corps, South Pacific during W.W.II. Devoted husband of the late Gussie whom he visited faithfully every day for more than a dozen years after she contracted Alzheimers. Survived by numerous loving nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers please make contributions to the Alzheimers Disease and Related Disease Association of Broward County, FL (He was President of the Fort Lauderdale chapter for many years.) Services 11AM Thurs, New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, L.I., NY.
