
        PELL--Stuyvesant B., died June 3, 2007, at his home in Princeton, NJ, at age 75. Predeceased by his wife Patricia, and sister Melissa Pell Thomson, he was the son of the late Edith Minturn Bonsal Pell and The Reverend Walden Pell II, and is survived by his daughters Alison and Sarah; grandsons James, Sam and Grayson, and his sister Mary Leigh Whitmer. The service will be held Thursday, June 7th, at 3pm at Trinity Church, 33 Mercer Street, Princeton, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made to Fort Ticonderoga Association, Inc., Box 390, Ticonderoga, NY 12883 or Trinity Church, Prinecton, NJ 08540.
