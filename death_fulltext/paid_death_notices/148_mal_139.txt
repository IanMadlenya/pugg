
        MICHELL-Jacques Dr.. On Wenesday, May 12, 1999. Loving husband of Irina, father of Monica and grandfather of Isabella. Dr. Michell was a dedicated internist in private practice in New Rochelle for thirty years, and on the staff of the Sound Shore Medical Center. He faced life with courage and humor and enriched the lives of all who knew him. Visiting will be tonight from 7-9 PM, at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 LeCount Place, New Rochelle, New York. Service is on Saturday, 1 PM, at the funeral home chapel. Cremation will follow at the Ferncliff Crematory.
