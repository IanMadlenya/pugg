
        LEKAREW--Leonard. 74, of Oakland Park, FL, formerly of St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands, and Glen Head, NY, passed away August 20th, 2005. Beloved lifetime companion of 40 years to George Moskowitz. Cherished brother of Gerald and Gladys Lekarew. Adored uncle of Carol and Mark. He was a distinguished attorney and served on the boards of the Hebrew Congregation of St. Thomas and Congregation Etz Chaim in Florida. Memorial services in Florida. In lieu of flowers, donations to Mt. Sinai Comprehensive Care Center, 4306 Alton Road, Miami Beach, FL 33140.
