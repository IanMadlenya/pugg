
        JOHNSON-Laura S. May 12, 2002. Beloved wife of the late F. Raymond Johnson, former President of Saks Fifth Avenue, NYC. Loving stepmother of Patrick Johnson.         JOHNSON-Laura. We mourn the passing of a New York legend, Laura Johnson. A woman of great style, wit and charm, a treasured friend who will never be forgotten. She will be deeply missed.
