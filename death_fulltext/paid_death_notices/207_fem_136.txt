
        GLOTZER--Diana (nee Sorkin). Peacefully passed away in her sleep on April 17, 2004. Married to George for 67 years, she was an adoring mother to Sandy and Alan, grandmother of Kathy, Andy, Emily and Owen and greatgrandmother to Lydia, Zachary, Sophie and Macy. She treasured the in-laws they brought into her family--Michael, Susan, Karen and Duane and her nieces, nephews and cousins. She loved friends, of whom she had many. She was fighting societal injustice up to her last day.
