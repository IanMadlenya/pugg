
        DAVIS--Theodore (Teddy), died peacefully on November 10th, 2004 in Delray Beach, FL at age 68. Beloved husband of Cyndee, loving father to his son and daughter-in-law, Michael and Jennifer, and doting father to his daughter, Jennifer. Brother and brother-in-law of Malcolm, Ilene and George Davis. Generous uncle to all his nieces and nephews. He was a cheerful, warm and loving human being. He will be greatly missed by all who knew him.
