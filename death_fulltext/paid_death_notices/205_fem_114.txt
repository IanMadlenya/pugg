
        FREED--Nettie M. Died February 13, 2004, age 96. Beloved wife of the late Leo Freed, her husband of 53 years. Devoted mother of Jeanie Pelzman and Judith Evans. Loving grandmother of Bonnie Burns, Betsy Ross, Debbie Jelinek, Andrew Evans and Lauren Evans. She is also survived by seven great-grandchildren and one great-great grandson. Mrs. Freed was the past president of the Westchester Division of the National Jewish Hospital of Denver and worked for the Counseling Center for Reform Judaism. Following a private funeral service, the family will sit at the Evans home in Greenwich, CT, on Sunday from 4-8 p.m. She did it her way.
