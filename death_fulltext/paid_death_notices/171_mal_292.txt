
        RUDOLPH-Theodore. On April 18th, 2001. A former employee of The New York Times, having retired in 1964. He served with the Army Air Corps Intelligence Division in World War II as an instructor and intelligence person in the War Room. After his retirement, he moved to Chatham, MA. He is survived by two cousins, Donald Tyler of Tryon, N.C., and Phyllis Coe of Norwood, MA. Interment in National Cemetery in Bourne, MA. A memorial service will be held in May.
