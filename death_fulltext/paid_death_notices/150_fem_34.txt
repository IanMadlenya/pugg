
        CASANAVE-Carolyn J. On June 23rd, 1999, at home surrounded by family and friends after a six year battle with breast cancer. Veteran actress of musical comedy in New York and Los Angeles. She will be remembered by all for her incredible courage, talent, love and generosity. She is survived by her beloved husband, George, parents Robert and Alice Thomson, brother Scott Thomson and sister Arlene Calkins.
