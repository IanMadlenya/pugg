
        DRUSS-Sylvia Schofler, 101 years, September 24, 1997 in Riverdale, NY. Beloved wife of the late Louis A. Devoted mother of Viola Rosenheck and the late Adelle Wolff. Dear mother in law of Alfred R. Wolff, and the late Dr. Kenneth Rosenheck. Adored grandmother of six grandchildren and sixteen great grandchildren. She was an active volunteer in several organizations in White Plains, NY throughout her lifetime with special interests in the Red Cross, Education and Nursing Homes. Services will be private. For those who wish, contributions may be made to the White Plains Beautification Foundation, Inc., PO Box 1614, White Plains, NY 10602 or to your favorite charity.
