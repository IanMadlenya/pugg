
        ORLECK-Joseph P. The family of Joseph Orleck mourns the passing of a unique and generous spirit. Semi-pro baseball and basketball player, an early force at Billboard Magazine, in 1940 he founded Cashbox, and built it into an internationally respected music magazine. Beloved uncle, grandpa, brother-in-law, ingenious storyteller, wry wit. He took care of the whole family. Funeral services to be held Friday, Feb. 7, 11AM, at Mount Carmel Cemetery, 83-45 Cypress Hills St., Glendale, NY.
