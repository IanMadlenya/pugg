
        McEWAN-Ruth Juliet Bell. Died peacefully March 12, 2002 in Montecito, CA. Lived most of her life in New York City and Darien, CT. Daughter of Marcus L. and Ruth B. Bell. Attended Brearley and Rosemary Hall, & graduated from Vassar in 1934. Married in 1937, Ruth was the devoted wife of the late Allerton J. McEwan. Loving mother of Anthony and Michael, and grandmother of Jessica and Matthew. She will be interred next to Allerton in Darien. We have been blessed by her intelligence, elegance, wit and knowledge, and will miss her deeply.
