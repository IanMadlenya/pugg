
        SCHWARTZ--Gabriel B. Age 78, after a courageous battle with cancer. PFC in US Army, fought in the Battle of the Bulge, awarded a Purple heart. Graduate of the Harvard Law School. Assistant U.S. attorney in the criminal division, Southern District of NY. Adored husband of Nora, devoted father of William James, Margaret Ann and the late Elizabeth Jane. Father-in-law of Priscilla Lundin and David Felsenthal. Beloved grandfather of Zoe and Ethan Schwartz and Eve, Ned and Daniel Felsenthal. Dear brother of Nathaniel and Ammiel Schwartz. Funeral services will be held on Friday, October 15, 9:45 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapels, Amsterdam Avenue and 76 St, NYC.
        SCHWARTZ--Gabriel B. His partners and colleagues at Hahn & Hessen, where Gabriel B. Schwartz spent the last 29 years of his professional life, extend their most heartfelt condolences to Nora and to Gabe's entire family. Gabe's inspiring skills as advocate, counselor, mentor and friend, together with his wisdom, humor and memorable ability to ''sum up'' virtually anything, will be sorely missed.
        SCHWARTZ--Gabriel. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our long time member, Gabriel Schwartz, the beloved husband of Nora, and father to William and Margaret and the late Elizabeth. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the entire family. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
