
        MANDEL--Johnny, 90, of Miami Beach, FL, and formerly of Manhattan, died November 16, 2006 at Douglas Gardens. Mr. Mandel was a detective for the NYPD for 20 years and was President Emeritus of the Retired Detectives Association. He also coached the 1960 US Olympic Wrestling Team. Of all his accomplishments, he was most proud of being a Detective in the NYPD, the greatest organization in the world. He is survived by his son, John Mandel Jr., of Amenia, NY; his daughter, Joan Mandel of Tamarac, FL; seven grandchildren and five great - grandchildren. Inter ment was in St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village. Services were entrusted to Allen Funeral Home, Millbrook, NY.
