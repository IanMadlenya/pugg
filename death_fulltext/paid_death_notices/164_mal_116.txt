
        LEFOR-Michael William. Of Storrs, CT, died Sunday, September 3, 2000, at Hartford Hospital after a brief illness. He is survived by his brother Guy Lefor of Storrs, CT, and several cousins. Born April 1, 1943, and a graduate in botany of Hobart College and the University of Connecticut, he recently retired from the faculty of the Geography Department of the University. At the time of his death, he was President of Aton Forest, Inc., an environmental research facility in Norfolk, CT, and a consultant on coastal wetlands to the Connecticut Department of Environmental Protection. He was a longtime member of St. Mark's Episcopal Chapel at the University of Connecticut, a member of its choir, and the maintainer of its pipe organ and was a member of the American Guild of Organists. For information, contact St. Mark's Chapel in Storrs (860429-2647).
