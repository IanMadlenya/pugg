
        KOBRE--Theodore A., 73, of White Plains, NY, on January 4th, 2004 after a serious illness. He was loved by many and will be missed by all. Services private.
        KOBRE--Theodore A. The Board of Directors and Members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Theodore A. Kobre, whose many years of friendship, kindness and enthusiastic spirit will always be remembered. Steven M. Slaven, President Thomas W. Brock, Secretary
