
        WILSON-Jeffrey. The Jewish Braille Institute of America (JBI) is deeply saddened by the passing of a valued Trustee of our Institute, Jeffrey Wilson. His keen sense of philanthropy and Jewish commitment helped strengthen our agency's mission to enable the visually impaired and blind to have access to their tradition. Our heartfelt thoughts and condolences are with his family during this time of sorrow. We pray that his memory will endure as a continuing blessing and inspiration to the entire family. Barbara B. Friedman, President Dr. Ellen Isler, Executive V.P.
        WILSON-Jeffrey. Beloved husband of Christina. Adoring father of Sammy and Jacob. Son of Merle and the late Irving. Brother of Lauren, Billy and Elizabeth, uncle and devoted friend to many. Our deepest condolences to our entire family. We will miss you and always remember the wonderful family times we all shared. Aunt Fran and Uncle Herb
