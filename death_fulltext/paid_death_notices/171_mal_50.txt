
        ADELSOHN-Yale. It is with profound sorrow that Kingsbrook Jewish Medical Center/Rutland Nursing Home, its Board of Trustees, Medical/Dental Staff and all personnel mourn the passing of Yale Adelsohn, our esteemed Board Member and Vice Chairman of Rutland Nursing Home. Sincere sympathies to his sons and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. David Minkin, President Emeritus Stanley Mandel, Chairman, KJMC Board of Trustees Richard Holland, Chairman, Rutland Nursing Home Linda Brady, MD, President & CEO
