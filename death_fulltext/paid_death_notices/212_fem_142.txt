
        GOLDBERG--Ellen Xandria. Passed away September 18. She was 58 years old. Beloved mother of Laura Goldberg Ortiz and Julie Goldberg. Loving grandmother of Drew and Jordan Ortiz. Cherished sister of Richard Ivan Gussaroff. Daughter of the late Jennie Ehrlich Gussaroff and George Gussaroff. Dear friend of Elaine Buchsbaum, Joan Swillings and Ruth Brown. Ellen had an irrepressible spirit and zest for life and a love above all for her family. In lieu of flowers, donations to the MS Society would be appreciated by the family. Services Sunday, September 19, 1 pm, at The Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
