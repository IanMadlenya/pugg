
        GIDRO-FRANK-Lothar, M.D. The New York State Psychiatric Institute and the Department of Psychiatry at Columbia University join in mourning the passing of a dear colleague, Dr. Lothar GidroFrank. He started his career at the Institute in 1949 and was Clinical Director from 1973 to 1977. He will be missed by all who had the pleasure of working with him. We extend condolences to his family. John Oldham, M.D. NY State Psychiatric Institute and Columbia University
