
        FLYNN--James Morgan Jr. 62, of Montauk, NY, and Palm Beach, FL, passed away on June 14, 2006. Jim was born in Boston, MA, and graduated from Boston Latin School and St. Michael's College in Vermont. He is survived by his wife Valerie Kelly; his son James Morgan Flynn III, his sister Marilyn, brothers Richard, Charles and Thomas, their wives, and nine nieces and nephews. Please make donations in Jim's name to Renal Transplant Fund, Dept. of Surgery, Columbia University/NY Presbyterian Hospital, 630 West 168th St. NY, NY 10032; attn Mark Hardy, M.D.
