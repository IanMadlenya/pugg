
        KAMINER-Peter H., August 17, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Marie Scott Kaminer, devoted father of Stevenson Scott Kaminer. Service Thursday, August 20th, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Yale Law School Fund. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
        KAMINER-Peter H. The Justices of the New York State Supreme Court, Appellate Division, First Department, mark with sorrow the passing of a great friend of the Court. As Chairman of the Special Masters and Vice-Chairman of the Committee on Character and Fitness, he served the Court with distinction. His dedication and wisdom will serve as his legacy to the Court. Alfred D. Lerner, Presiding Justice
