
        COLLIER -- Carol Cheston. Daughter of Daniel and Anna Cheston and wife to George Collier and Donald M. Schliemann. Carol Collier died at the age of 80, Sunday, January 11 of natural causes. A 1946 graduate and faculty member of Columbia University Presbyterian Hospital School of Nursing. She is survived by her three children Donna Weber of Sinking Spring, PA., Todd H. Schliemann of New York, N.Y., Elise Frigon of Longview, TX, her brother Jon Cheston of Belfast, Me. and her four grandchildren.
