
        CHALIAPIN--Helen (Helcia), age 98, formerly of Easton, CT and Marion, MA, died on March 6, 2007 at the Hebrew Senior Life Center in Roslindale, MA. Helcia was the beloved wife of portrait painter and Time Magazine cover artist Boris Chaliapin, daughterin-law of Russian operatic bass Feodor Chaliapin, and sister-in-law of actor Feodor Chaliapin Jr. She is survived by her brother, Morris Davidson, her sister-in-law Marina Chaliapin Freddi, her stepgrandson Christopher Murphy, her nieces and nephews, Rema Sessler, Maggie Salvatore and Michael Davidson, as well as great-nieces and nephews, Adlyn Loewenthal, David Schwartz, Eric Schwartz, Joshua Sessler, Nina Englander, Tracy McIntosh, Dassia Posner, Gabriella Salvatore, and many greatgreat-nieces and nephews. Helicia's dry humor and wit, her kindness and grace, as well as her unending beauty will be deeply missed by all. A memorial service will be held privately. In lieu of flowers, memorials can be sent to the American Heritage Center at the University of Wyoming for the ongoing care and digitization of the finding aid for the Boris Chaliapin collection.
