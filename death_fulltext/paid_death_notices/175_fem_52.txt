
        SHERMAN-Jay Susan. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Newton, MA, and NYC, on August 4. Beloved wife of Philip Abrams. Devoted sister of John F. Sherman III. Services at the Sherman Family Mausoleum of Temple Israel of Boston Cemetery on August 7, at 12 noon. Jay Susan Sherman received a Masters of Art and Masters of Philosophy from Columbia University. She was retired editor for Harry N. Abrams, Inc. of New York, where she was picture editor for numerous books, including John Pile's ''Interior Design'' and the illustrated version of Daniel Boorstin's ''The Discovers.''
