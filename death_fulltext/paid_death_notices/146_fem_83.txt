
        GERRY-Marjorie Kane. Died on March 4 at the age of 89 following a short illness at Delhi, N.Y. Widow of the late Elbridge T. Gerry who died on February 26. She is survived by two sons Elbridge T. Jr. of Locust Valley, N.Y. and Peter G. of Hopewell, N.J., a daughter Marjorie G. Ryland of Beverly, MA., a sister Patricia Kane Leonard of Chesiers, Switzerland, eleven grandchildren, two step-grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. A joint memorial service for she and her husband of 67 years will be held on Wednesday, March 10, 1999 at Trinity Church, Wall St. and Broadway, NYC, at 10:30AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Lake Delaware Boys Camp or The O'Connor Hospital, Delhi, NY.
