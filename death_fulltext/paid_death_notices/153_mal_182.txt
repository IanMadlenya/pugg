
        BAKER-Crowell, 68 years old of Lawrence, L.I. October 14, 1999. Survived by his wife, the Rev. Edyth K. Baker; a son L. Crowell Baker; a daughter Janann Telitz and a granddaughter Leigh Telitz. Memorial service Monday, October 18, 11AM, Trinity-St. John's Episcopal Church, 1142 Broadway, Hewlett, NY (516) 374-1415. Interment private. In lieu of flowers memorial gifts may be sent to Trinity-St. John's Church or to Family Consultation Service, 216-10 Jamaica Av, Queens Village, 11428
        BAKER-Crowell. The Lawrence Association is deeply saddened by the loss of our past president, Crowell Baker, who served with distinction. For over 30 years he was an active member of the Lawrence Association, winning the respect, admiration, and friendship of our members and village residents. His dedication, righteousness, wisdom, and pleasant personality will be greatly missed. We offer our deepest condolences to his wife and family.
        BAKER-Crowell. The Board of Governors and the membership of the Cedarhurst Yacht Club mourns the loss of our former Commodore and Governor at Large. The devotion, leaderhsip and energy he provided to the Club and his friendship will always be remembered and treasured. Our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Deedee and his family. Jeffrey P. Nelson, Commodore
        BAKER-Crowell. The Mayor, Board of Trustees and Staff of the Village of Lawrence, mourn the death of Trustee Crowell Baker. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife and family. Mayor Lawrence A. Blatte and the Board of Trustees Village of Lawrence, NY
