
        A'HEARN-Ruth A. (nee Donovan) On May 4, 1998 in her 98th year. Of Garden City, N.Y. Formerly of East Atlantic Beach, N.Y. Beloved wife of the late Frank B. A'Hearn. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Avenue at 12th Street, Garden City, Wednesday 7-9 P.M., and Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial, 10 AM, Friday St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Garden City. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, New York.
