
        SCHLUGER-Joseph, M.D. The Board of Directors, volunteers and staff of the New York Heart Association mourn the passing of Joseph Schluger, M.D., former Board President. Through his dedication and commitment, he helped establish the New York Heart Association as a major force in addressing the health needs of all New Yorkers, particularly in the area of emergency cardiovascular care. We extend our sincere condolences to Charlotte and his entire family. Steven W. Duff Jonathan L. Halperin, M.D. Morgan B. Stark Michael L. Weamer
        SCHLUGER-Joseph. The Isabel O'Neil Studio Workshop For The Art Of The Painted Finish has lost a great, good friend, and talented colleague. Joe had the eye of an artist and the care and patience of master craftsman. His work was admired by the most discerning. He earned his Journeyman Award in 1999. Joe will be greatly missed but his good example lives on.
