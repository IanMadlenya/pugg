
        LUGO-Margaret A. Age 76 years, of NYC, NY on Sunday October 10, 1999 at her son's residence in Palermo, NJ. She was a veteran of WWII, having served in the U.S. Army Air Corps as a Sergeant. She was an Administrator for the YWCA in New York City, and a patron of the arts. She is survived by her son and daughter-in-law, John F. and Jane Lugo of Palermo NJ, and three grandchildren Stephanie, Jessica and Deanna. Her services will be private at the convenience of her family.
