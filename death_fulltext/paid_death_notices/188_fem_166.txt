
        SLATER-Shirley Weisenfeld, 78. On September 24, 2002, in Oklahoma City, after a long illness. She grew up in Brooklyn, where she raised her children and worked as a physician. Devoted wife of Stanley for 51 years. Loving mother of Leonard and Michael, and grandmother of Elana, Benjamin, Wallis, Reuben and Maxine. Services Thursday at noon, at Weinstein - Garlick - Kirschenbaum Chapels, Coney Island Ave. and Avenue H, Brooklyn.
