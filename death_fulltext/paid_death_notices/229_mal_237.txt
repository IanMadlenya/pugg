
        OLSEN--Theodore James. Died February 15. Born on September 8, 1929, in Manchester, IN, to Howard and Dorothy (Scull) Olsen. Graduated with distinction from Indiana University in 1951 and with MBA in 1954. Corporate VP at Olin Corp and Pitney Bowes, member of Executive Committee at Touche Ross, and VP of the National Executive Service Corps. Member of the Conference Board's Organization and Personnel Advisory Boards, the Personnel Roundtable and New York Committee for the US Civil Rights Commission. Survived by his wife Elsa Bautista-Olsen, daughters, Sandra Allen and Karen Appleton, and grandchildren, Brett Pollo, Kimberly Pollo and Avery Appleton.
