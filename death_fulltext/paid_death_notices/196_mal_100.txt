
        LENNARD-Jules. With profound sorrow, the Board of Governors and members of Dellwood Country Club, New City, NY, record the passing of our esteemed and longtime member. Since 1960 he was a loyal and dedicated supporter of Dellwood, serving as president, a founder of Dellwood Foundation, Board and committee member. A true gentleman who'll be sorely missed but never forgotten as we remember him fondly. Jerrold Sholtz, Pres. Grace Friedman, Secy.
        LENNARD-Jules. The Board of Directors of the Dellwood Foundation, the charity arm of Dellwood Country Club, notes with sadness the passing of our beloved founder and past chairman, Jules Lennard. He was an inspiration to all.
