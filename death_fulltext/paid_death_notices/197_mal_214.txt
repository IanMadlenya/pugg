
        BERNARD--Andre. On May 23, 2003, at age 78. Renowned afternoon music host for 25 years on WNYC Radio. Masterful teacher at New York University for over 30 years--a profound influence in the modern dance community. Wonderful, gentle, wise man with a powerful voice and astute knowledge of the human body. His teachings touched deeply. Service Thursday, 3 PM, at St. Marks Church-in-the-Bowery, East 10th Street and 2nd Avenue. >>AD#
