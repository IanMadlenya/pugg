
        LIPSON-Herbert J., M.D. 86. Formerly of Baldwin, L.I., New York died Saturday, Oct. 16, 1999 in El Paso, Texas. Graveside Services were held at Fort Bliss National Cemetery, El Paso, Texas on October 18, 1999. Survivors include his wife, Genevieve, his sons Stuart of Miami, Florida and Michael of Phoenix, Arizona and 3 grandchildren. He was a retired Lt. Colonel in the Air Force, where he served as a medical doctor for four years during World War II. Dr. Lipson practiced medicine in Baldwin, Long Island for thirty-five years and in El Paso, Texas for ten years. He will be missed by all.
