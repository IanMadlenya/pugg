
        RAHIM--Shaikh Arif. Age 60, died in his sleep at home on July 18th, 2004. Appropriately, his last night was spent entertaining his friends with his delicious cooking, fine wine and conversation. His sons Omar and Hasan, daughter-in-law Neesha and grandson Shyam will be holding an evening of remembrance & Quranic recitation on Tuesday, July 20th at home at 20 E. 118th St at 7PM. Those who wish to celebrate his life are welcome.
