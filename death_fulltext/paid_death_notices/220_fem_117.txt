
        PLATNICK--Bernice (Bunny). From Brooklyn, NY. She passed away peacefully Wednesday, May 11 at Haven Hospice. Daughter of predeceased Elsie, Robert and brother Leonard. Bunnie's life was full of friends and family. She traveled extensively, as a buyer of handbags and leather goods, throughout the world. After a very successful business career, she retired but continued to enjoy her worldwide traveling. Bunny will be missed greatly as she was dearly loved. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to: Haven Hospice, Maryanne Pavillion, c/o The JFK Medical Center Foundation, 80 James Street, Edison, NJ 08818-9972
