
        WOOLNER-James L., Jr. Age 48. Died July 12, 1998 at St. Vincent's Hospital, New York City. A graduate of Scarsdale High School, Washington University of St. Louis and the University of Chicago Law School, he was Assistant General Counsel of Prudential Enterprise Legal Services at the corporate headquarters in Newark, NJ. He was the beloved eldest son of James and Trudy Woolner, loving brother of Dan Woolner and Nison Wheatly of Edward, CO, Steven and Alison Woolner of Sharon, MA and closest friends Rosalind Pachter of Chicago and Robert Moysan of NY. Funeral will be Tuesday, July 14, at 1 PM at Temple Emanuel of Westchester, Kenilworth and Westchester Ave., Rye, NY.
