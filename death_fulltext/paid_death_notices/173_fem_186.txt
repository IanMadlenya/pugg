
        WILSON-Cynthia, 48. Of Manhattan, accomplished singer, on June 17, 2001. Majoring in music and minoring in writing at Sarah Lawrence while also studying at Juilliard, she got her BA degree, was a runner-up at The NYC Division of Miss America Competition, and went on to appear in musicals on & off Broadway. She was writing a musical comedy before succumbing to cancer. Daughter of the late C. Philip Wilson, M.D., she is survived by her mother, Christine, brothers, Scott and Marc, nephew, Eric, niece, Julia, & loving cousins, aunts, and uncles. A Memorial Service will be held at The Congregational Church in Old Lyme, CT, Saturday, July 14 at 3 P.M. Contributions in her memory may be sent to The American Cancer Society.
