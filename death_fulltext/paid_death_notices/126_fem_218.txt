
        STEVENSON - Robin Broude, passed into the light, July 24, 1997 at 4:48 PM. Beloved wife of Bruce, sister of Susan, daughter of Bernard and Martha. Formerly a singer, she was a member of MWA and The National Arts Club. Her book Switchback, written with coauthor Tom Bade, is a time travel fantasy love story. All those who survive her are sustained by the power of her love.
