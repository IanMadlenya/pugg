
        KLEIN--Hortense Ciner. On November 15, 2003, at age 89. Beloved wife of the late Daniel J. Klein. Loving mother of Carolyne and Edward Levy and Robert and Susan Klein. Adoring grandmother of Jim and Sigal Levy, Matthew and Sharon Levy, Andrew and Alyssa Klein and Jeffrey Klein. Cherished great-grandmother of Danny and Lauren Levy and Olivia and Ellie Klein. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, November 17th, 11:30 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        KLEIN--Hortense Ciner. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Hortense Ciner Klein, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
        KLEIN--Hortense. Dear Hortense, you were an unparalleled role model for all your grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. We will miss you dearly. With deep love, Andrew and Alyssa Klein Jeffrey Klein Jimmy and Sigal Levy Matthew and Sharon Levy
        KLEIN--Hortense Ciner. The Parisier family extends deepest condolences to Bob, Susan and the beautifully devoted family of Hortense Klein. As close friends, we feel your loss and mourn with you. Elaine and Simon Parisier
