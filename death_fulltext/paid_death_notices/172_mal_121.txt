
        KATZMAN-Irving M. The partners and staff of Lipsky, Goodkin & Company, PC, deeply mourn the passing of Irving M. Katzman, the father of our esteemed partner, Stuart Katzman. The warmth, affection, compassion, love and understanding, so prominently displayed by Stuart, his brother, sister, and their families, is the foundation created by their father and will be his legacy to them forever. To Ruth, his wife of 53 years, his children, Stuart, Judy, Cindy, Fred and Randi, his grandchildren, Jenna, Zachary, Joseph and Gregg, his brother, Jerome, his sister, Diane Kahn, our heartfelt condolences.
