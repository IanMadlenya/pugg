
        RAINE-Harry G., 83, husband of Eliane, father of William and Henry Raine, died December 23, 1996, in South Charleston, Ohio, where he was interred. A memorial mass will celebrated at the Church of Saint Bartholomew, 6900 River Road, Bethesda, MD, Saturday, January 25, 1997 at 11 AM. Flowers and charitable contributions are respectfully discouraged.
