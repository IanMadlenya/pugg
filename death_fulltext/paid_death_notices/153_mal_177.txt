
        KORON-Hyman. Formerly of Great Neck. August 9, peacefully in San Francisco. Beloved father of Judith Hoffmann and Barry Koron and father-in-law of Kate Tompkins. Adoring grandfather of Rose Madelin Koron. Loving husband of the late Edith Schultz Koron. Loved by extended family, friends and co-workers. He spoke five languages and never said a harsh word in any. Interment 10:30 A.M. Friday, October 15 at New Montefiore Cemetery. Memorial service 1:00 p.m. at the Great Neck Library, 169 Bayview Ave. Contributions in his name may be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
