
        SIMON-Hyman G. 86 years, died on December 26, 1997. Beloved husband of Phyllis (nee Lieber). Devoted and proud father of Kenneth, Geoffrey and Robert, and father-in-law of Andi, Andrea and Teresa. Cherished and proud grandfather of Jamie and Daniel, Spencer and Hannah, and Jacqueline and Katherine. Loving brother-in-law of Stephen and Connie Lieber. He lived his life with remarkable courage and will remain an inspiration to all who knew and loved him. Phyllis and Hy's marriage will remain an example of love, honor and devotion. Services Sunday, 11 AM, at ''Riverside-Nasau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Interment at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
