
        IRISH--Dorothy (nee Louis). Peacefully, August 27 in Walnut Creek, CA. Born January 13, 1911. Graduated from the Lincoln School in 1928 and awarded a BFA summa cum laude from NYU in 1932. Married to John Stedman Irish in 1936, she was a Long Island resident until 1976. Dorothy was a talented painter and a lifetime member of the National Assoc. of Women Artists. After her husband's death she moved closer to her family in California. She is survived by her daughters Leslie Haines and Lynda Hay, in addition to three granddaughters and two great grandchildren.
