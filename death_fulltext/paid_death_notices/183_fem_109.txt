
        SALZBANK-Claire. Boys Town Jerusalem mourns the passing of a cherished member of its extended family, who together with her beloved husband Julius, was a Chai Founder of the school. The Salzbanks' valued friendship spans over two decades and their generous support has helped Boys Town fulfill its mission of turning young boys from limited backgrounds into young men with limitless futures. We extend heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Moshe Linchner, Dean Raphael Benaroya, Chairman Josh S. Weston, Honorary Chairman Michael J. Scharf, President Rabbi Ronald Gray, Exec. V.P.
