
        GELLER--Morton J., Dr., of Kings Point, L.I., retired pediatrician, passed away on July 28, 2003 at the age of 86. He enjoyed fifty years of practice as a pediatrician in Flushing, New York. Dr. Geller is survived by his loving wife, Ruth, sister, Thelma Weinberg, daughters, Jill Lenchner and Karen Kneller, and four grandsons.
        GELLER--Morton, M.D. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of Fresh Meadow Country Club record the passing of Dr. Morton Geller, an esteemed member of our Club. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Ruth and to the entire family. Robert Fortunoff, Pres. Roger Blumencranz, Secy. >>AD#
