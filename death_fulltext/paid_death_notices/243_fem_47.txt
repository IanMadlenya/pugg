
        KOCH--Natalie, our beautiful, funny, loving daughter, sister, granddaughter, godchild, cousin and friend of many died unexpectedly on April 5, 2007 at the age of 18. Natalie was presently a senior at Westhill High School in Stamford, CT. She was an artistic, social and loving girl. She is survived by her parents Henrik and Annette Koch and one brother Oliver Koch, all of Stamford. Calling hours will be held Wednesday, April 11th from 4-8 pm at Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 2900 Summer Street, Stamford, CT with a service at 7 pm at the funeral home. A burial will take place in Denmark. In lieu of flowers, the family ask you to make donations in Natalie Koch's memory to the LMG Liberation House, 159 Colonial Road, Stamford, CT 06906 or 119 Main St., Stamford, CT 06901.
