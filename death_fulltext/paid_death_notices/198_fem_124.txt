
        RINDLAUB--Edythe V. Of Saddle River, NJ, died on July 12, 2003, two days short of her 92nd birthday. A former teacher, she was the widow of John W. ''Will'' Rindlaub; mother of Diane, John and Neil; the aunt of Elaine and Robert; grandmother of seven; and great-grandmother of one. She was very active in her community and in the Unitarian Society of Ridgewood, NJ, and the Ridgewood Country Club. Donations in her memory may be sent to the Green Meadow School, 307 Hungry Hollow Rd., Chestnut Ridge, NY 10977, attention: Tony Cerone. >>AD#
