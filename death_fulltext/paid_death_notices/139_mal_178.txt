
        HARDIN-John A. Former President and Chairman of the Board of Mutual Broadcasting Corp. Died on August 1, 1998, at Mountainside Hospital, Montclair, New Jersey, after a brief illness. He was a ardent woodsman and fly fisherman and was a guide in Algonquin Park in Ontario, Canada, as a young man. Prior to becoming president of Mutual Broadcasting, Mr. Hardin was an accountant and financial consultant in New York City. he was born in 1911, in Montclair, New Jersey, he is survived by his wife, the former Edith Kaletsch, whom he married in 1937. A private memorial service was held.
