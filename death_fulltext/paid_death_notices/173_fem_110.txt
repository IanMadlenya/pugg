
        MOSSHOLDER-Terry, 53, passed away of cancer on May 6. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, June 14 at 4 PM at the Park Avenue United Methodist Church on 106 East 86th St. A reception will occur at the church following the Memorial Service. Ms. Mossholder was a resident of New York City, where she was employed at American Express Tax and Business Services as a financial proofreader. She is survived by a sister, Judith Mossholder, of Chicago, IL. The interment will take place at the Masonic Cemetery in Des Moines, IA.
