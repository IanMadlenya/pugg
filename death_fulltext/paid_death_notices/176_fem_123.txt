
        KLITZMAN-Karen Joyce, 38. Beloved twin sister of Donna, sister of Robert and Susan, daughter of Joan and the late Joseph and aunt of Joseph Elliott, died while working at e-Speed, a division of Cantor Fitzgerald, on the 105th floor of the World Trade Center, Tower One, Tuesday, September 11th, 2001, when the building was attacked. She was working in research on commodities futures. A graduate of Princeton University, Class of 1984 and Columbia University SIPA. She had previously been the Vice President of Research at New York Mercantile Exchange. Memorial Service will be held at the Stephen Wise Free Synagogue at 30 West 68th Street in Manhattan at 7:30 P.M. on Sunday, September 16th.
        KLITZMAN-Karen Joyce. In loving memory of Karen, daughter of Joan Klitzman, Executive Board member of Park River Independent Democrats, who died while working on the 105th floor of the World Trade Center Tower 1, Tuesday, September 11, 2001. Our deepest sympathy is extended to Joan and her family over the tragic loss of their beloved daughter and sister. With all our love, P.R.I.D.
