
        ARNOLD--Ruth Rabinoff Born April 6, 1918, died December 3, 2006, from complications from pneumonia. Head of the Reference Library at Jersey City State Teachers College for 24 years. Widow of the artist Dean Arnold, mother of Susan Ariel and Jennifer Robin. She was predeceased by another daughter, Sophie Miranda. Graduate of Antioch College, Masters Degrees from New York University and Columbia University. She was a woman ahead of her times. A memorial service to be announced at a later date.
