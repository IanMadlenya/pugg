
        LIEBERMAN--Irwin H. Died September 25, 2005 after a long illness. He is survived by brother Allan, children David, Audrey and Vincent Ibelli; two grandchildren Taylor and Jared. Also survived by Lucy B. Lieberman. He was buried in New Montefiore Cemetery. Irwin received his law degree and served in WW II 20th Division Air Force. He worked for the NYC Housing Authority in Forest Hills. Services were held on September 26, 2005.
