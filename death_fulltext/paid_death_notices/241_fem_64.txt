
        de KAY--Barbara Scott. Beloved wife of the late, Ormonde de Kay, mother of Thomas de Kay, Lenore Madeleine and Philip Roosevelt, sister of Lael French Scott, grandmother of Maxwell, Daniel and Margaret Roosevelt. Born in Seattle on August 15, 1928, she set out for New York City at 20 and made it her home for life. She worked in radio, early television and publishing and later helped Mr. de Kay, a writer, with research. She died in New York on February 9. A memorial service will be held at the Church of the Heavenly Rest on February 22 at 5 pm. In lieu of flowers, donations are encouraged to the Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center, P.O. Box 27106, New York, NY 10087.
