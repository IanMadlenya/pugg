
        DWYER - Andrew Knox. Born June 21, 1982, passed away suddenly on Friday, January 17, 2003. Beloved and adored son of Andrew and Cindy Dwyer of Hobe Sound, FL, and Bedford, NY, and brother of Nancy and Elly. A sophomore at Yale University, Andrew was a graduate of Rippowam Cisqua School and The Hotchkiss School. Grandson of the late Martin Dwyer and Mary Dwyer of Hobe Sound, FL, and the late Alex Kroll and Nancy and Red Gordon of Williamstown, MA. In addition to the immediate family, he is survived by many loving aunts, uncles and cousins. A Memorial Service will be held Tuesday, January 21, 11 AM, at St. Matthew's Episcopal Church in Bedford, NY. In lieu of flowers, please direct contriubtions to The Hotchkiss School, Lakeville, CT.
