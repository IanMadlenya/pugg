
        ESIBILL-Nancy L. Ph.D, CRC. The faculty and staff of the New York University Rehabilitation Counseling Program mourn the loss of their friend. As a faculty member since 1974 & Program Chairperson since 1986, she was valued for her academic leadership, the currency of her teaching, and her untiring service to doctoral candidates. Nancy earned a M.A. degree in rehabilitation counseling at Hunter College and a Ph.D degree, also in rehabilitation counseling, at NYU. She was a active member of the vocational rehabilitation community and touched the lives of countless students, colleagues and consumers. She faced her own disabilities with resolute bravery and unfailing humor and was a model for all persons with disabilities for whom she was always a strong advocate.
