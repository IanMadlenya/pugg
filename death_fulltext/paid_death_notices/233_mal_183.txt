
        STARK--Stephen Henry ,a financial consultant who managed the New York office of L.F. Rothschild & Co. and later held a number of other senior positions in the financial services industry, including, most recently, Senior Vice President at RBC Dain Rauscher, died on June 14, 2006 in New York City. He was 72. A Yale graduate and 50-year member of the New York Athletic Club, Mr. Stark's passion for his work and dedication to his clients remained constant during his nearly five decades in the industry. He continued to be actively engaged in his profession at the time of his death. Mr. Stark's professional success was mirrored in his personal life, where his grateful, loving family and many friends basked in his warm smile, sharp humor and generous nature. He is survived by his wife of 48 years, Joan, his three children, Anthony (and wife Debbie), Sanford (and wife Miriam), and Victoria (and husband Richard), and grandchildren Gigi, Anthony, Alessandra, Angelica, Ella, Stephen, Josephine, Ryan and Sarah. He will be sorely missed but not forgotten by everyone who knew and loved him. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The DOE Fund, 232 East 84th Street, New York, New York 10028 (212-628-5207). Services will be private.
