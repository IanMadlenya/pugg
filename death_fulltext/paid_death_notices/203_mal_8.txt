
        FACTOR--Morris, M.D. Loving and devoted husband to Elaine. Cherished father of David and Dayle and adoring grandfather of Claire Rebecca. He enjoyed an active life including extensive traveling, music, theatre, photography and driving his Model 'A' Fords. In his practice of psychoanalysis for over 50 years, he was a compassionate and deeply concerned physician. He touched many lives and he will be deeply missed. Services Tuesday 11:30AM at Riverside-Nassau North chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, N.Y.
