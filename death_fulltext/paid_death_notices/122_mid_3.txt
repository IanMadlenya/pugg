
        GORDON-Helena F. (Freeman), Wednesday, March 5, of Newton, MA. Beloved wife of the late Jacob. Loving mother of Patricia C. Gordon of Boston, MA and Hiram Gordon and his wife Barbara of New York City. Adored grandmother of Jared and Elisabeth. Services at the Levine Chapel, 470 Harvard St., Brookline, MA, today, at 10 AM. Memorial observance through Sunday afternoon at her late residence. Remembrances may be made to Hebrew College, 43 Hawes St., Brookline, MA 02146.
