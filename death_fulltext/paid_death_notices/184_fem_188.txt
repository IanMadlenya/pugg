
        KAVOURAS-Harriet. Of Whitestone, NY, died on May 24, 2002, at the age of 57. She was an incredibly generous woman to all who knew her. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. Catherine's and George Greek Orthodox Church, 30-11 30th Drive, Astoria, NY 11103; or the Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation, 3 Forest St., New Canaan, CT 06840.
