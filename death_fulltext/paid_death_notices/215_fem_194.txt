
        KAHN--Dr. David, died December 27, 2004, at age 92, after a rich and wonderful life. Devoted husband of Carrol, beloved father of Wendy Kahn (Martin Burns) and Susan (Jeffrey) Stern, cherished grandfather of Jacob Burns, Michael and Peter Stern. Caring physician, dedicated teacher and leader of the community, his wisdom, compassion and kindness will serve forever as a loving memorial. May his soul be bound up in eternal peace. Contributions may be made to UJAFederation of New York.
        KAHN--Dr. David. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Dr. David Kahn, cherished husband of Carrol, and father of Susie K. Stern, our treasured and beloved board chair. At this difficult time, we express our great affection to Susie for her graceful leadership, boundless energy, and the myriad ways that she defines what it means to build an inspired Jewish community here in New York and worldwide. We understand that it is David and Carrol's remarkable legacy of compassionate service to community & the Jewish people that has served as a driving force & a role model for Susie and her family. We extend our deepest sympathies to Carrol; to daughter Susie and her husband, Jeffrey M. Stern; their sons, Michael and Peter; to daughter Wendy Kahn, her husband Marty Burns and their son, Jacob; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        KAHN--Dr. David. Israel Policy Forum mourns the loss of Dr. David Kahn, beloved father of our Vice President, Susan Stern. We are deeply saddened and our condolences and prayers go out to Susie and her family.
