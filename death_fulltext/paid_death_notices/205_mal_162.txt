
        MESSING--William L., 90, on February 9, 2004 in Los Gatos, California. Loved and survived by Norma, wife of 63 years; children Joan, Margie and Gary and their spouses; five grandchildren and their partners; and one greatgrandchild. Graduated New York City College, 1933 and Columbia Law School, 1936. Extraordinary 55 year legal career in government and private practice. Esteemed for his integrity, intellect and compassion. Deeply committed to social justice. Gifts may be sent to the ACLU.
