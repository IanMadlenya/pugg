
        SOFFES--Eliot. Age 51, died suddenly on January 11, 2005. A devoted husband to Dr. Diane Goldkopf and loving father to his children, Jake and Sarah. His creative talent brought him respect in the architectural community. He will be greatly missed by his business associates, friends and family.
        SOFFES--Eliot. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the untimely death of Eliot Soffes, our former school architect, Rodeph Sholom School parent and member. Our heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife, Diane Goldkopf, their children, Jake and Sarah, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D. Senior Rabbi Irwin Shlachter, Headmaster Nancy Solomon, President
