
        GRABER-Edward A., M.D.. The faculty and staff of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology of Weill Medical College of Cornell University are saddened by the passing of our colleague and friend, Edward A. Graber, M.D., Professor Emeritus of Clinical Obstetrics and Gynecology. Dr. Graber, a member of our faculty since 1973, was especially noted for his devotion to clinical excellence and his encyclopedic knowledge of the medical literature, which he imparted to medical students, residents, and the obstetricgynecologic staff. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Sylvia, and his children, Fredric and Elizabeth. In accordance with the family's wishes, memorial gifts may be sent to Weill Medical College of Cornell University, Dept. of OB/GYN Education Fund, 525 East 68th St., NYC 10021. Weill Medical College of Cornell Univ. Dept. of Obstetrics & Gynecology Frank A. Chervenak, M.D. Acting Chairman; Professor of Obstetrics & Gynecology William J. Ledger, M.D. Immediate Past Chairman; Given Foundation Professor Emeritus of Obstetrics and Gynecology
