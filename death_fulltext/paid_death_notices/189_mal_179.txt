
        ABELES-James David. Age 86 of Basking Ridge, NJ on October 9, 2002. Beloved husband of Mary Elizabeth (Campbell) Abeles, devoted father of Elizabeth Kirkland Heald, grandfather of two and step-grandfather of four. He was predeceased by first wife Elizabeth Brunet Abeles and his son James Brunet Abeles. A Memorial Service will be held at 11:00 AM on Wednesday, October 16, 2002 in St. Bernards Episcopal Church, Claremont Road, Bernardsville. Memorial contributions can be made to the College of the Atlantic, Eden Avenue, Bar Harbor, ME 04609.
