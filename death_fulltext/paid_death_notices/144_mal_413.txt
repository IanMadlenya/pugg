
        SANDERS-Milton. Died January 22, 1999. Attorney and Supreme Court Judge, State of New York. Beloved husband of Ruth (Amsterdam). Devoted father of Elaine Brodsky, Alison Lichstrahl and father-in-law of Dr. Alan Lichstrahl. Dear broher of Fred and Louis. Cherished grandfather. Loving great-grandfather. Friend of many, he will be deeply missed by all. For more information, Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 212-477-6334.
