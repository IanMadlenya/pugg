
        BURKE--Thomas C. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of Thomas C. Burke, a former Officer of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Alfred E. Smith, IV, President Brian G. Andersson, Historian $(6$)BURKE--Thomas C. The Board and Staff of Catholic Guardian Society and Home Bureau note with great sadness the death of Thomas C. Burke. As a former chair of the Board of Catholic Home Bureau, Mr. Burke extended his personal devotion to his own family to countless other children and families served by our programs. We wish to convey our deepest sympathy to Janet, to our Board member, Michael Burke and to his entire family. Julia V. Shea, John Tricamo Co-Chairs of the Board John Frein, Executive Director
