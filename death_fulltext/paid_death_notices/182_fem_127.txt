
        DEGEN-Beatrice. Beatrice Bertha Blaustein Degen, age 90, died Sunday, March 10, 2002, after a brief illness. A graduate of Brooklyn College and Columbia University, she was an office manager for many years in Brooklyn College English Department. Wife of the late Harry Degen. She is survived by sisters Henrietta Honeyman, Marjorie Lubell, Clarisse Ruchames, brother Elliot Blaustein, and numerous nieces and nephews. Funeral arrangements are private.
