
        SKALET - ROBINSON - Selma (nee Hurwitz), 91, of Bal Harbour, FL., and White Plains, NY, on April 13. Survived by Ira and Ellie Skalet, John H. and Barbara Skalet, grandchildren Paul and Francine Skalet, Joanne and Ken Sarama, Howard and Kelly Skalet, Adam and Kalli Skalet and six great - grandchildren.
