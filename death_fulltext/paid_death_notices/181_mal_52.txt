
        BIRNBAUM-Harris. Beloved husband of Muriel. Devoted father of Richard and Barry. Cherished grandfather of Ronald, Alice and Charles. Revered uncle of Tully Safian, Sara Ann Wallerstein, Susan Gamza and Marilyn Hookman, and loving great-uncle to their children. Distinguished member of the New York State Bar for over 60 years. Dedicated counselor to all of his clients. He was one of a kind. He will always be missed. Funeral was held Sunday, 12 noon, at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapel.
