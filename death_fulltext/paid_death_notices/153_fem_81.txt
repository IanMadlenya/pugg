
        TURKEWITZ-Grace. Shelter Rock Jewish Center, Roslyn, New York, deeply mourns the loss of its past President of Sisterhood, and longstanding and devoted member of its Board of Trustees. A heroic stuggle for life has ended. To her husband Bob and her family, our sincerest condolences. Rabbi Myron M. Fenster Laurence A. Feldman, President
        TURKEWITZ-Grace. Of Roslyn, NY. Died at Memorial Hospital in NYC after a six year battle with cancer. Known alternately as Mom Chemdah, and Bubbie, she continued to pursue every activity with her family, enormous network of friends, and synagogue. She enjoyed life to its fullest, from beginning to end, entertaining, traveling, volunteering, organizing, and acting as family matriarch. Beloved wife of 48 years to her high school sweetheart, Bob. Devoted mother of four sons, enthusiastic grandmother to eight, loving sister and aunt to seven. Former grade school teacher. She made a lasting impact on all who were fortunate enough to be touched by her. The funeral will be held at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Funeral Home on Queens Blvd at 76 Road, Forest Hills, NY at 9:00 A.M. on Sunday, October 10. The family will sit shivah at their home from Sunday, October 10 through Friday afternoon, October 16.
