
        HUSTING-Eugene E. (Gene). Of Locust Valley, Long Island, died peacefully on November 20, 1997. Retired Vice President of the First National Bank of Boston, early aviation and antique car historian. Survived by his wife, Betty, and daughters Hillary C. Vander Stucken and Alison H. Zetterquist. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Hartford Heritage Auto Museum, 147 North Rural Street, Hartford, WI. 53027. There will be no service.
