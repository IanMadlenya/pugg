
        MARUSI-Gene A., 80, a long-time resident of Manhasset, New York died Sunday, June 11, 2000. He was born in Glen Ridge, NJ in 1920. He graduated Lehigh University as a Metallurgical Engineer. Gene served as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Army in Germany during W.W. II. He founded O-DEE Equipment Corp. and Marusi Equipment Corp. serving the New York construction industry since the mid40's. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Silvia Marusi, his children Victoria and Ted Butler, Lawrence and Sandra Marusi, Christina and John Rand, Robert and Beth Marusi, and his brother Augustine Marusi. He is also survived by eight grandchildren. Visiting hours will be held at Fairchild sons Funeral Home, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, New York, on Tuesday, June 13, 2000, from 3-5 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. Mass will be held at 10:45 A.M. on Wednesday, June 14, 2000 at St. Mary's Catholic Church, Northern Blvd., Manhasset, New York. Donations can be made to: North Shore University Hospital, c/o Volunteer Office, Stroke Club, 300 Community Drive, Manhasset, New York.
