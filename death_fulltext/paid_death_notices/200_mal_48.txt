
        FALK--George. September 3, 2003, age 89, Boca Raton, Florida. Beloved husband of Betty, devoted father of Kenneth (Barbara), Theodore (Ginnie), Stephan. Cherished grandfather of Andrew (Amy), David (Melissa), Jenn ifer (Jose), Jeffrey (Melissa), Lindsey, Amy, Daniel and great grandfather of five. Services Sunday, at 2:00 PM at Guttermans, Woodbury, New York.
