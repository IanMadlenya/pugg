
        PEARL-Leon. Son of Benzion and Helen Pearl. Husband of Roslyn for 46 years; father of Janet, Barry, Kenneth and Carl; grandfather of Seth and Stephen; Professor Emeritus of Philosophy at Hofstra University, died on June 26, at St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn, NY. He was 77. Dr. Pearl was born in Poland, served in the U.S. Armed Forces in World War II, and held the Ph.D. in Philosophy from New York University and the J.D. from Hofstra Law School. He joined the Hofstra Philosophy Department in 1960, serving as Chair from 1989 to 1992. He authored two books, ''Four Philosophical Problems,'' and ''Descartes,'' as well as numerous articles in prestigious journals. He will be missed by family, friends and colleagues.
