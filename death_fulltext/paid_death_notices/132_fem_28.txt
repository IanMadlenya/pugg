
        FIGMAN-Anne Behrens. On December 30, 1997 in her 91st year. Beloved wife of Dr. Louis H. Figman. Cherished mother of Dr. Myles Behrens and Susan Borkow and dear mother-in-law of Marsha Behrens and Dr. Stephen Borkow. Loving grandmother of Allan and Renee Borkow, Adam Behrens and Michael Borkow and delighted great-grandmother of Robert Benjamin Borkow. Honored stepgrandmother of Dr. Cary, Dean and Alexandra and Michelle Siegel and step-mother of Dr. Robert and Nomi Figman, Holly and Roy Whitlow, and Dr. Howard Figman and step-grandmother of their children. Her memory will be a blessing.
