
        WEINSTOCK-Frances K., 103. On September 20, 1998. Beloved wife of the late George J., devoted mother of Blossom Landsman, Arthur Weinstock, his wife Betty, & the late Rita Weinstock. Loving grandmother of Peggy Kerner & Carol Landsman. Also survived by great granddaughters Erica and Julie. Nieces and nephews all of whom she loved and who loved her. A special woman of caring, concern, wisdom and generosity. Services on Wednesday, at 1:00 P.M., at ''Gutterman's Inc'', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York.
