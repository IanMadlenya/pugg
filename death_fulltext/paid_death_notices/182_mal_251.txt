
        HASBROUCK-Brigadier General Sherman V. Age 103, on March 13, 2002, of Stone Ridge, NY, died at the L.I. State Veterans Home at Stony Brook. 1920 Graduate of US Military Academy, West Point, and oldest living graduate. Survived by his wife, Clara R. Morthland Hasbrouck, four stepchildren, nine step-grandchildren. Services will be at the US Military Academy Cemetery, West Point, June 18, 2002, at 10 AM. Memorials to Friends of the Castle Endowment Fund, Frost Valley YMCA, 298 Claremont Ave., Montclair, NJ 07042. For more info call George Moylan Funeral Home, 845658-3139.
