
        GAGE-Edwin ''Peter'', realtor, passed away at Mary Manning Walsh Home in New York City on April 7, 1997 after a long illness. Husband of Suzanne Reade Gage. Father of Linda O'Connor and Thomas Gage and beloved ''Pop'' of Andrew, Jessica, Peter and Laura. Contributions may be made to the Monmouth medical Center. Funeral services private.
        GAGE-Edwin. Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mr. Edwin Gage. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
