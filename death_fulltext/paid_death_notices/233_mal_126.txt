
        DUMA--Michael George. On Sunday, June 11, 2006 at the age of 88. He is survived by his wife of 59 years, Katherine Palam Duma, son and his wife, Dr. & Mrs. Christopher Duma of Newport Beach, and three grandchildren, Chace, Matthew and William Duma. A viewing will be held Friday from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Ave (at 20 St), NYC. Funeral Mass Saturday, 10 AM, at the Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment St. John's Cemetery.
