
        STEPHENS-Roy, M.D. We are saddened by the loss of our esteemed friend and colleague whose devotion to, and leadership of our society were an inspiration to all. Roy's boundless energy, enthusiasm and humor were greatly valued by all his colleagues and patients. We will sorely miss our friend whose contributions to this society were invaluable. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Barbara and entire family. The Long Island Dermatological Society
