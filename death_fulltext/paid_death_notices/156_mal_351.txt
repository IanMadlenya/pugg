
        FISHER-Charles R. Rev. 79 of Wells, ME, formerly of Hackensack, NJ, died Sunday in ME. He ministered at Christ Church in Hackensack, NJ from 1955 to 1982, and started the Holley Child Care and Development Center. Husband to Georgie Maxine Tyler Fisher, father to Stephen Fisher and Elizabeth Fisher, and three grandchildren survive. Funeral 1:30 PM Wednesday at St. David's Church, Kennebunk, ME. Memorials to Holley Child Care and Development Center, 360 Union St., Hackensack, NJ, 07601 or to General Theological Seminary, New York, NY. Information: Bibber Memorial Chapel, Kennebunk, ME.
