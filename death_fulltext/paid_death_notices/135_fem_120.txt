
        FRIEDMAN-Rae (Licht). On April 15. Beloved wife of the late Jack. Loving mother of Mark (Robin) Friedman and Eve Fox. Adored grandmother of Andrew (Nicky) and Alyson Fox, Hallie and John Friedman. Her tireless devotion to her family will be sorely missed. Funeral services at Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Sunday at noon. Contributions in her memory may be made to League for the Hard of Hearing.
