
        BURGESS-Louis. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Brookdale University Hospital & Medical Center mourn the loss of Louis Burgess, Chairman of Community Board 17; a great humanitarian, a devoted friend of the hospital and a pillar in the community. Our sincere sympathy to his family. Edwin L. Schulman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Frank J. Maddalena, President and CEO Alvin I. Kahn, MD, Sr. Vice President Medical Affairs
