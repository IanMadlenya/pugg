
        OBERMAN-Esther K. On April 14, 2002, after a valiant struggle against cancer. Beloved wife of Dr. Jacob Oberman. Loving mother of Elissa and Jonathan and mother-in-law of Monique Breindel and Mary Lou Keyes. Cherished and adoring grandmother to Hannah, Naomi and Sarah Rose. Devoted sister of Arthur Kaplan and Hon. Louis I. Kaplan. She died as she lived, with dignity, humor and great love for her family, friends and life. Services Tuesday, 12:45pm, at ''The Riverside'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        OBERMAN-Esther K. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband Dr. Jacob L. Oberman, her brothers Arthur Kaplan and Judge Louis I. Kaplan and the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
