
        ABRAHAM-Jacob W. On Mar. 25, 2002. Beloved husband of Regina A. Abraham. Devoted son of the late Louis & Esther (Weisner). Former Justice of The Supreme Court, New York County. Past President, Bankruptcy Lawyers Bar Association. New York State Labor Department Special Referee. For over twenty-five years, Judge Abraham led The Federal Republican Club in Manhattan. A native New Yorker, he was a proud graduate of Seward Park High School, and Brooklyn Law School. A practicing attorney for seventy years, he was a respected and revered advisor and friend. Services are at Plaza Chapels, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, New York, NY, March 26, 2002 at noon.
        ABRAHAM-Jacob W., Honorable. Beloved lifetime friend, godfather to Charles Joshua. Forever in our hearts. Adele, Charles, Clara Unterberg The Rana Family
