
        FEIGENBAUM--Irving Leonard., Died at 85 years and 11 months. Passed away, on Thursday, January 18th, 2007. He left behind a wife Phyllis Roth-Feigendaum, a stepson Richard A. Roth, stepdaughter Charlene Curtiss-Roth and two grandchildren; Ingrid and Alexander. He did Nuclear physics research at Brookhaven National Laboratory for 50 years. Services were private.
