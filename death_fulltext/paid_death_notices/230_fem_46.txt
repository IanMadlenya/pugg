
        MUCCIA--Anna. Died peacefully in Vero Beach, FL on February 11, 2006. Anna was born in NYC on February 22, 1910. Beloved wife of the late Carrol A. Muccia. She is survived by her devoted children, Carrol A. Muccia Jr., Diana Muccia and Peter Muccia. Loving grandmother of Elizabeth Pyle, Timothy Muccia, Anne Droesch, David Muccia and Bianca Muccia. Dearest nanny to 12 great-grandchildren. Funeral Mass will be celebrated on Wednesday March 8, 10 AM at the Church of the Resurrection, Rye, NY. Burial at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Hawthorne, NY.
