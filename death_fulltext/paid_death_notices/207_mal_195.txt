
        CURTIS--Edward S., 78, of Boynton Beach, Florida died on April 8, 2004 at his home after a long struggle with Cancer. Born May 1, 1925 in New York City, he was the son of the late David and Sadie Cohen. He was the husband of Harriet Hanft Curtis, who died in 1995. Retired, he was a dentist in private practice in Schenectady, New York. He served in the Navy in WWII, and in the Air Force National Guard thereafter. Surviving are dearest friend Renee Telsey, of Boynton Beach, Florida; sister Carol Schulman and her husband Norman Schulman, of Great Neck, New York; son Charlie Curtis and his wife Betty Curtis, and their daughter, Paige, of Winchester, Virginia; and son Doug Curtis and his wife Marjorie, of Sykesville, Maryland. A service will be held in Schenectady, New York at the Temple Gates of Heaven Cemetery on April 23, 2004 at 2PM.
