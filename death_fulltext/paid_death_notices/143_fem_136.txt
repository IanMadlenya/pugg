
        STRAUSS-Annette. The Board of Directors and staff of Phoenix House mourn the passing of Annette Strauss. A woman of spirit, strength, and commitment, she greatly enriched the nation and the Dallas community. A founding member of our Board of Directors, she cared deeply about the welfare of youth and, as a passionate advocate, strengthened our organization and advanced our efforts in Texas. Charles A. Heimbold, Jr., Chairman of the Board, Phoenix House Foundation Burton M. Tansky, Chairman of the Board Phoenix House of Texas Mitchell S. Rosenthal, M.D. President
