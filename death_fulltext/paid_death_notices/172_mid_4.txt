
        FORD-Sister Barbara Ann. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, died on May 5, 2001. Dear daughter of Dorothy and the late John Ford. Beloved sister of Dorothy, John and his wife, Alice Casey. Loving aunt of Sean. Bobbie died in Guatemala after 20 years of service to the people she loved so dearly. Reposing at the Convent of Mount St. Vincent on Wednesday from 1-8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday 10 am in the Chapel at Mount Saint Vincent with interment following at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, NY 10471. For information F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc., Yonkers, New York.
