
        FRANK-Philip J. Of New York, On April 25th. Phil is survived by his beloved wife Leylah, sons Robert, Gerard and David, their mother Shirley and his cherished grandchildren Nathan, Lindsey and Penelope. Phil graduated from West Point Military Academy and retired from the U.S. Army as brevetted full colonel. Phil had a distinguished career in the U.S. Army followed as an investment broker. Phil demonstrated his belief in the American tradition of charitable giving by the many organizations he supported. At present, per Phil's request, a private family service will be held and he desired that all friends and family say Kaddish, or recite a psalm or other reading in praise of God.
