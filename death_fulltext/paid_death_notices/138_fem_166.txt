
        QUINTON-Ella (nee Lauterstein). Died peacefully at age 102 in Florida. She was a loving mother, grandmother and great grandmother, and a brave and uniquely indomitable spirit. Services today, July 26, 1998, 2:30 PM at Gutterman's Inc. (Parkside Building), 9860 Queen's Blvd (corner 66th Avenue), Rego Park, New York.
