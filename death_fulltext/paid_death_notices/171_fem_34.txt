
        SPIRER-Mae. Age 85. Died in Portland, ME. on April 2, 2001. Caring wife of the late William Spirer. Loving mother of Kenneth and the late James Spirer. Adoring mother-in-law of Joan Leitzer. Beloved grandmother of Laren, Bill and Katie. She is also survived by her brother Irving Golfman of Miami, FL. Funeral services will be private. She loved family and friends unconditionally, and they loved her in return. Contributions in her memory may be made to the James Spirer Scholarship Fund, Saint John's College, POB 2800, Annapolis, MD. 21404. Messages of condolences may be sent to 18 Neal St, Portland, ME 04102. Inquiries Hay & Peabody Funeral Home, Portland, ME.
