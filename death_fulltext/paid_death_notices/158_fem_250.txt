
        TIGHE-Frances M. Of Pound Ridge, NY. Died March 29, 2000. She was 78 years old. Mrs. Tighe was born January 28, 1922 in Yonkers, NY to Stanley and Sarah Moffat. Beloved mother of Lorna de Zengotita and Laurence Tighe. Loving grandmother of five. Service Saturday St. Matthews Episcopal Church, Bedford, NY.
