
        SCAL-Sadie. Formerly of Great Neck, LI, on January 1, 2000. She loved and was loved by her late husband Jacob, her son Donald and her daughter Jane Roberts, grandchildren Andrew, Edward, Peter, Jan, Ellen and Nancy, and eight great-grandchildren. At age 101, her life spanned three centuries with sweet disposition, family love and helpfulness. Services Tuesday, January 4, 12 noon, at Temple Beth-El, Great Neck, LI.
        SCAL-Sadie. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
