
        MAJOR-Claude, 39, of Manhattan and St. Eustache, Quebec, died Monday, February 24, 1997 after a brief illness. During the 1980's, Mr. Major was national sales manager for Nicole Farhi, an English sportswear company. A native of St. Eustache, Quebec, Mr. Major graduated from the University of Quebec at Montreal with a degree in French Literature. He later attended The Fashion Institute of Technology in New York. He is survived by his mother, Carmen Major, of St. Eustache, Quebec, a brother, Michel Major, his sisterin-law Sylvie Girard, and two nephews, Marc-Andre and David Major of St. Eustache, Quebec.
