
        UMANOFF--Rose, 85 years old of Rockville Centre, N.Y. Died on September 3. Loving mother of Jane and Dan. Devoted grandmother of Becky, Lila, Zak and Nicole. Adoring great-grandmother of Max and dear mother-in-law of Bo. Rose was a positive, enthusiastic and youthful presence with her family and friends. Her love of tennis, gardening, antiquing and creating beautiful crafts was boundless. Rose was loved by many and will be missed by all. Contributions may be made to Planned Parenthood.
