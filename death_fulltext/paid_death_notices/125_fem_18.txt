
        SPILLANE-Laura Elizabeth Carmody, of Charlotte, North Carolina, formerly of Freeport and Cold Spring Hills, Long Island, on May 27, 1997. Mrs. Spillane is survived by her children, Claire Wilfert and Vivian McCarthy, both of Charlotte, Linda Steinmetz of Abbeville, SC, and John Spillane of Wilmington, NC; a sister, Barbara Hall of Lancaster, PA; 2 brothers, Charles Carmody of Freeport, L.I. and George Carmody of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. She is also survived by five grandchildren and 4 great-grandchildren. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, New York, Tuesday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral service Wednesday at the funeral home. Interment in Cedar Grove Cemetery.
