
        SPIELVOGEL-Roslyn Bremer. On August 31. Deeply loved and devoted mother of David, Rachel and Paul. Beloved grandmother of Daniel, Carly and Jake. Cherished sister of the late Arnold, Sidney and Florence Bremer. She enriched and enhanced the lives of others with her beauty, warmth, intellect and wit. Though gone from our sight, she will forever live in our hearts. Donations may be made to the UJA. Service Tuesday, 1 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
