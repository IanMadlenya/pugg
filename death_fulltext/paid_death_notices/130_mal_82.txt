
        REIS-Rabbi Paul S. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague, age 50. After ordination, he served as director of the Hillel Foundations at W. Virginia University and North Carolina, as rabbi and educator in Pittsburgh, and since 1983 as rabbi of Congregation Beth Israel in Flint, MI. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his widow, Sara; mother, Edith; brother, Irwin and to his sons, Ezra, Ari and Efrem, and all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi David L. Lieber, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec VP The Rabbinical Assembly
