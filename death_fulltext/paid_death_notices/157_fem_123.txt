
        SETTEL-Sylvia, 86. Died February 11th. in Syracuse, NY. Born in NYC. She studied at the NY School of Design and the Art Students League. She was an independent artist who had exhibits at the Donnell Art Library in NYC, the Harriman Library and the Hudson River Museum. Several select pieces of her work are on permanent exhibit at Syracuse University School of Information Studies. She was a member of the National Association of Artists. Her husband Harold died in 1972. Survived by daughter Barbara Settel of Lafayette, NY, and son William of Elkton, MD, six grandchildren. Contributions to ALS research.
