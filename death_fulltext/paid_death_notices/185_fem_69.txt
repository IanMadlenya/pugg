
        FELDER-Betty Rice. A longtime resident of East Hampton, NY, died peacefully in her sleep Sunday at home. She was 91. Although she had grown increasingly frail in recent months, she was blessed with good health. Her children were with her at the time of her death. She is survived by her daughter Bettina, and by her son Wilson and daughter-in-law Laura, and their children William and Julia. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to the Girls Vacation Fund, Inc, 370 Lexington Ave, NY, NY 10017.
