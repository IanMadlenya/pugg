
        WADE-Robert P. On January 31. Beloved husband of Deborah E. Cohen, devoted father of Aryeh Cohen-Wade and Samuel J. Cohen-Wade.         WADE-Robert. It is with great sorrow that we note the passing of former colleague, Robert Wade. For twelve years he demonstrated his devotion to Hebrew University as Director of Public Relations and Communications at American Friends of The Hebrew University. On behalf of AFHU, we extend our condolences to his sons Samuel and Aryeh Cohen-Wade and the entire family. American Friends of The Hebrew University Adam B. Kahan, Executive Vice President
