
        SITT--Eddie M. The Sephardic Food Fund mourns with sadness and sorrow the passing of Eddie M. Sitt, the father of our founder, president, and friend, David Sitt. Our condolences are extended to Mrs. Gloria Sitt and her children, Rochelle, Morris, Jeffrey, David and their families, as well as to his sister, Mrs. Renee Sutton. May Eddie's legacy of kindness, impeccable ethics, and community service continue to provide comfort and guidance to his family for generations to come. Rabbi Raymond Sultan Elliot Sutton Jack Schweky Charlie Anteby
