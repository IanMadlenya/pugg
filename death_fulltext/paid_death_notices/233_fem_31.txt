
        KALLENBACH--Susan Fran. Died on May 26, 2006 in New York City after a prolonged battle with cancer. Ms. Kallenbach graduated from Douglass College and received a Master's in Library Science from Drexel University. Ms. Kallenbach was a librarian and held management positions at the Van Pelt Library of the University of Pennsylvania and Bobst Library of New York University. She retired from NYU in 2000. Ms. Kallenbach is survived by her daughter Lisa and son Charles, and four grandchildren. A memorial service in her honor will be announced and held in the Fall. Memorial contributions can be made to the Weill Medical College of Cornell University, Division of Geriatrics.
