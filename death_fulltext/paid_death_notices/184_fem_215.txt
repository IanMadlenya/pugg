
        MERVIS-Gertrude (1915-2002); retired employee of Grossinger's Resort. Sister of Ruth Roper and the late Irwin and Florence, and sister-in-law of Jack Roper and Molly Mervis. Fondly remembered by her nieces, Laurie and Jamie Roper, and her nephews, James and Richard Mervis. Rest in peace, darling.
