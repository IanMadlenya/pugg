
        SHAPIRO-Harvey R. On October 22, in Santa Monica, CA. Writer, publisher, political historian, activist, a founder of the Teachers' Freedom Party, Citizens for Local Democracy, and acclaimed political journal ''The Public Life,'' his major works are ''The Bureaucratic State'' and ''Democracy n America: a Political History of United States.'' H.R. Shapiro is survived by his son, David, and sister, Judy. Memorial service to be held 1 PM, October 28, at Workmen's Circle Cemetery, Saddlebrook, NJ. For information call 800-525-3834. ''The vanguard is the person who I always wanted to be.'' HRS
