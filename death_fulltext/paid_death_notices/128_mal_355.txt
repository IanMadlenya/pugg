
        HICKS-Everett, age 74, of Port Washington, L.I., on September 28, 1997. Beloved father of Mark Hicks of Naples, Fla., Jeffrey Hicks of No. Brookfield, Mass., and Lindsay Reeves of Chapel Hill, N.C. Devoted grandfather of seven grandchildren. Also survived by sisters, Grace Johns and Helen Coulter and a brother, Harry Hicks. A Memorial Service will be held on Wednesday, October 1st at 3 P.M. at the Community Reformed Church, Manhasset, NY. Interment is private. Memorial Contributions may be made in his name to: The Reformed Church in America, P.O. Box 19381, Newark, N.J. 07195-1938.
