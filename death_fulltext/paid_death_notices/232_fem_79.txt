
        DOYEL--Rowena Diggs. On May 5, at 84 in New York City. Dedicated nurse and civic activist. As owner of the oldest dwelling in Chelsea's Historic District, she was passionate and outspoken about the neighborhood and was President of the Council of Chelsea Block Associations. Rowena is survived by her devoted husband John, daughter Lesley, son-in-law Nick Fritsch and granddaughter Nora.
