
        BELL-Wilmot Kennedy, died on Wednesday, February 9, 2000, at Somers Manor, Somers, NY. The Pleasantville native was 97. Long active in village activities, he was a Captain of the Volunteer Fire Department Patrol Company, President of the University Club, a vestry man at St. John's Episcopal Church, and a tireless fund raiser for the Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts. A graduate of The Salisbury School (1921) and Williams College (1925), he retired from Metropolitan Life Insurance Company in 1967. His wife, Mildred Kelly Bell predeceased him in death in 1976. He is survived by his daughter Nina Bell Allen of Tucson, AZ, a retired assistant managing editor of Readers Digest. Funeral service will be held on Saturday, March 25, 2000, at 10:00 A.M. at St. John's Episcopal Church, Pleasantville, NY. Burial will follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, New York. In lieu of flowers donations to the Nantucket Conservation Foundation, Box 13, Nantucket, MA. 02554 will be appreciated. For Information: Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, New York.
