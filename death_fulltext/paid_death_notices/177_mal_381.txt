
        MARCUS-Robert P., life-long love, companion, and best friend of his wife Betty G. Marcus; beloved son of the late Libby P. Marcus and Bernard K. Marcus; caring and devoted son-in-law of the late Ziuta Gerstenzang Akston and Leo Gerstenzang; proud and loving father of his daughter, Lauren Marcus Hughes; adoring grandfather of Christopher Robert Marcus Hughes and Meghan Ruth Marcus Hughes and father of Gary P. Marcus. He enriched the lives of all those he met and all those who called him friend, for he was a kind, caring, wise and gentle man. He was a natural leader who spent a lifetime quietly and selflessly helping others and improving the lives of untold numbers of people. He will be remembered by all those he touched forever and ever. A Memorial Service will be held at 11:00 AM on Sunday, October 28, 2001 at Ferncliff Cemetery, 280 Secor Rd., Hartsdale, NY. For information, contact Quattlebaum Funeral Home at 561-832-5171.
