
        SHAPIRO--Adrian, 81, died Monday, January 24 in his home in Forest Hills, NY. A man of tremendous spirit, Adrian valiantly battled MS for over 50 years. He was well known and respected in the publishing field having worked for Crown Publishers for over 40 years, retiring in 1985 as the Vice President for Manufacturing and Production. He is survived by Beverly, his loving wife of 52 years, his brother David, sister Estelle, children Richard and Peter, daughters-in-law Denise and Laura, grandchildren Jennifer and Andrew. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The National MS Society or The United Spinal Association. Services Wednesday 11:30 AM at Schwartz - Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd, 76th Road, Forest Hills, NY.
