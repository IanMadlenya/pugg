
        CAVERS -- John Arthur, passed away on June 15, 2005. He was a wonderful father and husband and a brilliant teacher who faithfully served his community and country. He was a Renaissance man and a citizen of the world. He fought in World War II. Afterwards, he taught Latin, French, English, and math to high school students. Later, he became a Fulbright Scholar. John had an uncanny ability to make his lessons vivid and enduring for his students. He imbued his students with a love of learning.
