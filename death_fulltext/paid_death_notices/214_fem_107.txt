
        ASHBURN--Jean (Lang), age 95, formerly of Brooks School, North Andover, MA, died Sunday November 14 at Fox Hill Village, Westwood, MA. Mrs. Ashburn was preceded in death by her husband, Frank D. Ashburn. Survivors include her sisters Agnes Reynolds of Charleston, SC, Helen Kelly of Indian Harbour Beach, FL, and many loving nieces and nephews. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. For information: Holden, Dunnirvine & Lawler Funeral Home, Westwood, MA.
