
        WIENER--Leo. May 14th, 2005, at 80. Beloved husband of his adored Naomi. Devoted brother of Sol. Loving father to Lewis, Lynne, Michael, Priscilla, Robert. Adored grandfather, uncle, cousin, father-in-law. Cherished friend to all blessed to knew him. A true renaissance man who loved fishing, travel, folk dancing, reading, skiing, golf. Graduated Syracuse University School of Forestry. Founder Wiener & Crowley Wholesale Lumber. Services May 17th at 2:30 pm, at Riverside Nassau-North, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Contributions may be made to Workman's Circle and Yiddish Book Club.
