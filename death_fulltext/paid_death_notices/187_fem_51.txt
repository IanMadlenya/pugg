
        SHIH-Daphne Bayne Hellman. Passed away peacefully on August 4, 2002, at age 86. Famed jazz harpist of Hellman's Angels. Beloved mother of Daisy Hellman Paradis, Digger St. John and the late Sandy Bull. Dear motherin-law of David Paradis, Mona St. John and Candy Bull. Cherished grandmother of Sasha and Alain Paradis, and Davina-Nicole, BrittanyKara and Bayne-Alexander St. John, and Cassandra, Jesse and Jackson Bull. Dear sister of the late Elizabeth Shields. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Thursday, August 8, 11:30 AM. Interment private.
        SHIH-Daphne Hellman. The Jazz Ministry of Saint Peter's Church deeply mourns the passing of our dear friend, Daphne Hellman Shih. Her great talent and personal warmth will be sorely missed, but her light will shine forever in our hearts. The People of Saint Peter's
