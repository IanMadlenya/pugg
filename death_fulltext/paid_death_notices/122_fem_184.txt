
        DOMINGO-Isabel. The owners, management and employees of Answer America, all of whom loved and admired her, are deeply saddened by the passing of Isabel Domingo. She will be sorely missed and fondly remembered. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to her husband, David; her daughter, Sarah, and to all her family. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, New York 10601.
