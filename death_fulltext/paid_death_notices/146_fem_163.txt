
        HORNSTEIN-Eva. Born November 2nd, 1911. Died March 14, 1999 in Fort Lauderdale, FL. Devoted wife of Hyman Hornstein. Cherished mother of Barbara Kass and Richard Hornstein, son-in-law & daughter-in-law, Herbert Kass & Marie Hornstein. Survived by dear sister & brother, Gertrude Hornstein & Louis Shapiro, loving grandchildren, Jennifer Kass, Deborah Halperin and Emily and Joseph Hornstein. Her loss is deeply mourned by all. Services Tuesday, March 16th, at 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
