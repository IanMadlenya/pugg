
        DINERMAN-Irving Paul. Beloved, generous and kind husband, father and grandfather. Survived by his wife Shirley (nee Zax), children Jane, Barry and Richard, grandchildren Catherine, Brian, Kate, Shannon, Danny and Anna. He was an attorney and founding partner of Dinerman, Bergam and Dinerman of 170 Broadway, NY, NY. Jewish war veteran of WWII. Graduate of New York University, undergraduate and law school in 1951. Funeral service Monday, September 17, 2001, 9:15 A.M. Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, New York (opposite Kings Plaza).
