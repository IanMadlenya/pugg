
        HOYT--Elizabeth Sayer. Died peacefully on February 5th, in Ponte Vedra, FL. at age 91. Formerly of Oyster Bay, NY. She is the widow of Everett W. Hoyt. She is survived by her three sons, Charles E. of Washington, DC., Anthony S. of New York City, and William W. of Hamilton, MA. She also has nine grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. A private Memorial Service will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church in Cold Spring Harbor, NY. at 11 AM on Saturday, February 14th.
