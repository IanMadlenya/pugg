
        LUCK-David J. L. The Rockefeller University community mourns with abiding sadness the loss of our distinguished colleague and beloved friend David J.L. Luck. Dr. Luck, Alfred E. Mirsky Professor & Vice President for Academic Affairs at the Rockefeller University, was a pioneer in the field of cell biology. He will long be remembered for his deep commitment and selfless dedication to the university. He was a wise counsellor, a dedicated teacher and a good and caring friend to old and young alike. Dr. Luck will be profoundly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Torsten Wiesel, President Richard M. Furlaud, Chairman, Board of Trustees David Rockefeller, Honorary Chairman Board of Trustees
