
        HOLLISTER-Leo E., M.D. 80, Professor Emeritus of Psychiatry and Pharmacology at the University of Texas Medical School at Houston, passed away December 19, 2000. He was also a professor Emeritus of Medicine, Psychiatry and Pharmacology at Stanford University School of Medicine. Notes and remembrances may be sent to Harris County Psychiatric Center, 2800 South MacGregor Way, Rm 3A31, Houston, TX 77021.
