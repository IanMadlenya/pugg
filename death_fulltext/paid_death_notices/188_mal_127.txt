
        ADLER-George R. The Faculty and Staff of The MarieJosee and Henry R. Kravis Center for Cardiovascular Health and The Zena and Michael A. Wiener Cardiovascular Institute of Mount Sinai Medical Center are deeply saddened by the passing of George R. Adler. A thoughtful and compassionate benefactor, George shared our special commitment to improving the lives of those suffering from cardiovascular disease. He will be remembered fondly for his kind and generous support. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Marion, and to the entire family. Valentin Fuster, M.D., Ph.D. Richard Gorlin, M.D./Heart Research Foundation Professor Cardiology Director The Marie-Josee and Henry R. Kravis Center for Cardiovascular Health The Zena and Michael A. Wiener Cardiovascular Institute The Mount Sinai Medical Center
