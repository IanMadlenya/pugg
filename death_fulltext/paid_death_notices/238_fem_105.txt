
        RAPPAPORT--Frances (nee Schwartz), age 89, formerly of Rockville Centre, NY, died at home in Coconut Creek, Fl, on November 17, 2006. Predeceased by her husband, Al, son Robert and brothers, Ned Shaw and Irving Schwartz. Survived by sons Mort (Karen Bonn), Steven (Judith Garson) and Donald; grandchildren Marcy, Mandy (O'Hara) and Peter; and great grandchildren Sam, Oliver, Pauline and Luke. Private funeral will be held in NYC.
