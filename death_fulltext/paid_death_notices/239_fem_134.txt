
        SOLOMON--Jeannette. The Tenn South Social Services, Inc. NORC program, Tenn South program for seniors, honor Jeannette Solomon for a life well-lived and filled with care and concern for others. She offered continued support for our seniors at Tenn South Co-op. We offer our condolences to her children, Richard and Peter, and her grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. Nat Yalowitz, President David Smith, Chairman Nancy Spannbauer, Director
