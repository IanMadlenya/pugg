
        KLEIN--Sadie, died peacefully at home on January 24, 2008 at age 101. Devoted wife of Emmanuel (deceased). Cherished mother of Norma and Victor (both deceased) and mother-in-law of Dorothy. Beloved grandmother of Jennifer, Katie, and Emily. Adoring great-grandmother of Ezekiel. A passionate activist, a lover of literature, a ferocious player of tennis and Anagrams, a witty correspondent, Sadie will be deeply missed by her family and her many friends of all ages. A memorial reception will be held at her apartment from 59pm on Friday, February 1.
