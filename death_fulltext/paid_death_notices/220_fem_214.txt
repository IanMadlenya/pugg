
        LOVINGER--Rose, 102, NYC, died May 26. Born in Philadelphia, she worked for Girard Bank & Trust Co, Philadelphia, then as executive secretary for The Bobbs Merrill Publishing House, NYC, for many years. She devoted many hours helping those in need including reading at The Jewish Guild for the Blind. Survived by husband of 53 years, Monroe. Funeral service 1:15 PM Sunday at Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah New Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. For information contact Freeman Funeral Home, Manalapan, NJ.
