
        MINES-Fay Shirley. Of West Orange, N.J. Formerly of West Hartford, CT. Died at the age of 95 on October 24, 2002. The family of Fay Mines mourns her passing. She was the beloved wife of the late Abraham Mines. Mother of Herbert Mines, Patricia Veit and the late Jeffrey Mines; mother-in-law of Barbara Mines, Walter Veit and Barbara Mines; grandmother of Roberta Birmingham, Jacqueline Veit, Catherine Veit, Susan Mines, Deborah Forman, Jonathan Mines and Richard Mines; great-grandmother of Laicy Mines, Larry Mines, Victoria Birmingham and David Birmingham. She had a long and full life, respected and loved by all. She will be greatly missed. The family requests donations be made to Hadassah, 50 W. 58 St, New York, N.Y. 10019, or The American Jewish Committee, 165 East 56 Street, New York, N.Y. 10022. Funeral services will be held at Weinstein Funeral Home in Hartford on Sunday, October 27, 2002 at 1:30 P.M.
