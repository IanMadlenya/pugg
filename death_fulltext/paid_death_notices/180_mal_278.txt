
        THOMSEN-Basil Max. Died January 15, 2002, after a courageous struggle to recover following open heart surgery. Beloved husband of Barbara, devoted father of Kim and Jon, loving brother of Phil and Doug and their wives Claire and Flora. His kindness, humor and generosity of spirit will be remembered by all who knew him. He will be missed in our hearts forever. A Memorial Service will be held on Sunday, January 20th at 1:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
