
        TURKEWITZ - Doris. Died January 26, 2006 at age 77. Daughter of the late Manny and Madeline Turkewitz. Dear devoted cousin and friend to many. Her life was a courageous battle. She will be missed by all who were close to her. We thank Inga, her caregiving companion, for all her love and devotion. A memorial serivce will be held in the spring.
