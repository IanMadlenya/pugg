
        VALDES-Esperanza Victoria. On September 26, 1997. Beloved mother of Dr. Lesley Rankin-Hill; sister Mauricio C. Valdes. Respected & loved New York City teacher at Brandeis High School & JHS 43. Her caring, devotion, insight and wisdom touched the lives of countless people young and old. Her passing leaves a void in the universe. Donations in her memory may be made to The Jacob Perlow Hospice, Beth Israel Medical Center South, NY, NY 10003.
