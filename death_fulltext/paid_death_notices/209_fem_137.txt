
        MEEHAN--Mother Mary Bernadette, O.P. At Rosary Hill Home in Hawthorne, NY, on June 18, 2004. Born in Philadelphia, PA, Mother made her first Profession on September 14, 1932 and was assigned to Rosary Hill Home in Hawthorne. On March 2, 1958 she was appointed Superior of Rosary Hill Home. Mother was appointed Superior of Sacred Heart Home in Philadelphia on March 9, 1973 and was elected Mother General February 23, 1981. She is survived by many nieces, nephews, grand nieces and grand nephews. Mother Bernadette will be reposing in the Chapel of Our Lady of the Rosary from 3:00 to 9:00 PM on Sunday. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Monday at 10:00 AM. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
