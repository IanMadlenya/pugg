
        SCHUMAN-Daniel G. Of Martha's Vineyard, MA, and Stuart, FL, entered into rest November 24, 2002. Age 86, retired chairman of Bausch and Lomb Co. Beloved husband of Jacquelyne. Devoted father of sons Joel of Saugerties, NY, and Craig of Overland Park, KS; his daughter, Meredith, of Raleigh, NC; and nine grandchildren. Graveside service today, 11 AM, Abel's Hill Cemetery, Chilmark, MA. Donations may be made to the charity of one's choice.
