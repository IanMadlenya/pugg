
        MARSTON-Albert O. 76, of New York City, peacefully on November 10. Beloved husband of Mary V. Hebb. Fond brother of Liane. Devoted father of Douglas, Jeffrey, Julie and Wendy. Loving grandfather of five. Retired litigator, former Assistant Attorney General in New York City for many years. He lost his devoted first wife, Margrit J. Neubauer, in 1981. He will be deeply missed by all who knew his sharp mind, deep voice of wisdom and wonderful sense of humor.
