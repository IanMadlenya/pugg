
        WOLFE--Maynard Frank, on October 15th in his 77th year. Author, photojournalist, industrial designer, contract photographer, for ''Time,'' ''Life,'' ''Newsweek'' and other noted publications. He covered three wars, The White House, many outstanding motion pictures and much fast-breaking news of the 20th century. Based in Hong Kong, he founded a successful design company and went on to become President of Rube Goldberg, Inc., in New York. Survived by a daughter Jessica, a son Alexander and brother Richard. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
