
        KEHOE--Margaret, nee McMahon, on August 21, 2003. Loving wife of the late Joseph. Devoted mother of Joseph, Jr. Beloved sister of Sr. Marcella of London, England. Cherished grandmother of Stefanie and Karen. Born in County Claire, Ireland, was well versed in, and a passionate devotee of, Irish history and culture. A Memorial Mass in her memory will be celebrated at St. John Nepomucene Church, 66th St and 1st Avenue, Manhattan on Wednesday, September 3 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory can be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
