
        DUNN-Eleanor Mary (nee Brady). Daughter of the late Mary and Arthur Diamond Dunn. Granddaughter of Judge John J. Brady. Died Monday, November 11, 2002 at the Westhampton Care Facility. She was 97. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated at a later date. Inquires to the R.J. O'Shea Funeral Home, Hampton Bays, New York.
