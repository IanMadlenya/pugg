
        ANNUNZIATA-Dorothy ''Dolly'' (nee Antos). Passed away peacefully in her sleep after a long illness of emphysema on Sept. 17, 1999. Dear wife of the late Anthony Annunziata. Loving mother of Beverly Wolcson & loving motherin-law of Mickey Wolcson. Devoted sister of Lois and Timothy. Cherished grandmother (Bocky) of Jessica, Nicole and Anthony Wolcson. Beloved and devoted family member to numerous nieces, nephews, grand nieces, grand nephews and cousins. Dolly was a retired employee of American Airlines & was known for her spirited personality and love of travel. She will be deeply missed by her family and her many, many friends who loved her. She will always be remembered. Interment will be private. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. Memorial donations can be made in her memory to the American Lung Association.
