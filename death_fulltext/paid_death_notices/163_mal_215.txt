
        COHEN-Eli. Died August 17, Age 88. Retired from the National Committee on Employment of Youth and New York City Board of Education. Survived by his wife, Selma and five children; Katey York, Ellen Lerner, Bob Cohen, Peter and Marc Rosenbaum; and grandchildren Sasha, Nicholas, Timothy, Jessye, Sophia and Samantha. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
