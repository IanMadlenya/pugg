
        PFLUEGER--Kathleen Isabel Powers (Kiyi). On March 30, after a long illness, the wife of the late Edward M. Pflueger. Together they amassed one of the finest privately held 16th and 17th century faience and 18th century porcelain collections now bequeathed to the Boston Museum of Fine Arts. Kiyi was on the Board of the East Side Settlement House and was one of the founding members of the Winter Antiques Show. Survived by her loving sister, Mrs. Hans A. Frech, of New York City and many nieces and nephews. Funeral will be held at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue and 71st Street, at 2pm, Friday, April 7th, 2006. Burial immediately following at Woodlawn Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name to St. James Episcopal Church, 865 Madison Avenue, NY, NY 10021, or the East Side Settlement House, 337 Alexander Avenue, Bronx, NY 10454.
