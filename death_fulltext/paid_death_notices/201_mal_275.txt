
        WEINTRAUB -- Morris. The Solomon Schechter School of Westchester notes with deep sadness the death of our former trustee, Morris ''Tiny'' Weintraub, husband of our esteemed friend Selma, and grandfather of alumni Neil and Scott Kaufman, Heather Stein, Jennifer, Rebecca and Lexie Weintraub. Tiny was a vital force for many years in the development of this institution. His energy, wisdow and generosity were an example for our community in the formative years of this school. Tiny Weintraub's memory will be a blessing to the children of this school for generations to come. M. Mac Schwebel, Chairman, Board of Trustees James M. Dubin, Vice Chairman Dr. Elliot Spiegel, Headmaster
        WEINTRAUB -- Morris. We mourn the death and loss of our dear friend, Morris Weintraub, beloved husband of Selma and cherished father of our distinguished colleague, Connee Kaufman.
