
        SPECHT-Lisa M. A memorial service will be held for Lisa M. Specht, artist extraordinaire, compassionate teacher and a dear friend. We hope that you can join her family and friends in honoring her: Saturday, March 31, 2001 at 12 noon at the Art Students League of New York, 215 West 57 St., N Y, NY 10019. Service will be held in the Gallery on the 2nd floor.
