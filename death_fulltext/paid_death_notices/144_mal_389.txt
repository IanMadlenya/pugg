
        LOWENHERZ-Alfred, died on January 21. He leaves a loving sister Lisel, son Jack and daughterin-law Lila, and grandchildren Mark and Jennifer. An expert in photography and its history, he will be missed by his many camera comrades. At his wish, there will be no services.
        LOWENHERZ-Alfred. The Camera Club of NY mourns the passing of a wonderful friend. A distinguished Officer and Board member, an outstanding photographer and photographic equipment designer. The Alfred Lowenherz Exhibition Gallery is named in honor of his many achievements and years of service. Jerry Sheik, President
