
        KRAYTON--S. Jack. Of Lake Peekskill, NY, died at home on May 9, 2004. He was a Veteran of WWII and a pharmacist for 37 years. Jack was also a multi-talented artist who worked in many media particularly the creation of custom stained glass pieces. His creativity merged with his generosity as he graciously bestowed his artwork on family and friends all of whom cherished his gift. Jack is survived by his wife of 57 years Lillian Shine, their daughters Sheryl (Marc) Saidel of Somers, NY, and Marcia (Ron) Goldstein of Brynmawr, PA, grandchildren Jenna (Michael) Lebowich, Eric (Tahl) Saidel, Daria (Andrew) Prusky and Jesse Goldstein, beloved greatgrandchildren Eliana Saidel, Marina Lebowich, Alex Prusky and Sara Saidel, sisters Rachelle Band and Frieda Isaacs and many nieces and nephews. We will all miss Jack's vision, his talent, his ''can-do'' attitude and his willingness to help all he knew. A funeral service will be held on Tuesday, May 11, 2004, 12 noon at Weinstein Memorial Chapel Inc., 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, New York, 914-793-3800.
