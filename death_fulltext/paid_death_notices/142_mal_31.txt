
        NIMMO-Richard F. ''Dick'' 82, of NYC, formerly of Stamford, CT. Passed away peacefully Sunday, November 1, 1998 at the Actor's Home in Englewood, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Joan (nee Etchinson). Loving father of Robert of River Vale, NJ, Joan of West New York, NJ and the late Richard F., II. Cherished grandfather of Diana and Richard. Dear father-inlaw of Elyn, dear brother of the late William. Dick retired in 1997, he was a 51 year member of Theatrical Protective Union, Local One I.A.T.S.E. He began his career in the infancy of television and served, with distinction, as Television Business Manager for Local One for 26 years. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Richard F. Nimmo Scholarship Fund, c/o Local One, I.A.T.S.E., 320 West 46 Street, NY, NY 10036. Family will receive friends Tuesday 2-5 and 7-9 at Buckley Funeral Home, 445 West 43 Street, NYC. Service Wednesday 10AM followed by interment at Fairfield Memorial Park, Stamford, CT.
