
        PRICE--Margery. Born on March 21, 1934 in the Bronx and died peacefully at home after a long and courageous battle with cancer on October 19, 2006. Even while struggling with her own illness, she continued to volunteer at the Mt. Sinai Breast House Resource Program offering care and hope to countless cancer patients. She will be remembered by everyone who knew her for her kindess and incredible spirit for life. She is survived by her loving children, Eileen Price Farbman and Steven Price, and their spouses Steven Farbman and Tina Price. She was a loving and loved grammy to her four treasured grandchildren Allie, Leo, Lucy and Kyle. She is also survived by her former husband Robert Price. Funeral services will be held on October 20, 2006 at 9:45am at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91 St., NY. Donations can be made to Mt. Sinai Breast House Resource Program, 16 E. 98 St., 1B, NY, NY 10029.
