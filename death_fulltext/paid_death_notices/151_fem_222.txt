
        DAVIS-Maude Bouvier. Died New York City, Friday, August 20, 1999. Beloved wife of the late John E. Davis. Cherished mother of Maude Sergant Davis and John Hagy Davis. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to Cabrini Hospice, 227 E. 19 St., NYC 10003.
