
        KASHINS--Laura, died peacefully Saturday January 13th at the age of 1031/2. Predeceased by husbands Herman Kashins and Michael Reiburn. Survived by daughter Barbara Moore, grand daughters Robin and Rebecca Moore, great-grandaughter Irene MacDonald. Donations can be made in her memory to the Metropolitan Museum, where she was a dedicated volunteer until she was 100; or Channel 13, her other volunteer job into her 90s. Burial private. Her family will celebrate her life with an informal reception on Saturday January 20. Information 212-463-7348 or 212-564-5989.
