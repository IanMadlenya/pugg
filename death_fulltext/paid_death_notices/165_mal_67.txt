
        OKIN-Robert (Bob). Bob passed away on October 3, 2000. Born to Deborah and Harry Okin on February 20, 1917 in Bronx, NY, he was a graduate of Ohio University and St. John's School of Law. Bob was a founding member of the Atlantis Yacht Club in Sea Bright, NJ and a former Executive Vice President and Board member of Faberge. Father of Kenneth and Dennis Okin, beloved grandfather of Peter, Elizabeth, Samuel and Catherine Okin, Bob was preceded in death by his loving wife Babette Slotkin Okin. Business advisor and friend for over fifty years to George Barrie. A loving friend, father and grandfather who will be greatly missed by all. Graveside services will be held Friday, October 6, 2000, 11AM at Riverside Cemetery in Saddle Brook, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The American Diabetes Association. For information, please call Louis Suburban Chapels (201) 791-0015.
