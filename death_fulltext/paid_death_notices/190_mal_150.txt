
        BERNARD-David S., 75. Of Hartsdale, NY. Died on November 11, 2002. He was born on June 19, 1927 in New York City. David was President of D.S.B. Associates in New York City retiring in 1997. Mr. Bernard was a Docent at Hudson River Museum, Yonkers, NY and Volunteer at Family Services of Westchester in White Plains, NY. David is predeceased by his wife Mary (nee Beck) Bernard in 1994. He is survived by his daughter Beth Reiman of Hawthorne, NY, and son Edward Bernard of Concord, MA, one brother Fred Bernard of Forty-Forts, PA. and four grandchildren. Funeral service Friday, November 15, 2002, Ferncliff Cemetery Chapel, Hartsdale, NY, 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to Hudson River Museum, 511 Warburton Avenue, Yonkers, NY 10701 in his memory would be appreciated.
