
        PEVERLEY-Ralph W. Of Litchfield, CT, formerly of Greenwich, died Tuesday at Charlotte Hungerford Hospital in Torrington, CT. Mr. Peverley was born in New York, NY, son of the late Ralph S. and Julia (Barnhill) Peverley. He was predeceased by his wife, E. Mary Peverley, and is survived by his daughter Susan P. Vasileff and son-in-law Nicholas L. Vasileff of Roxbury, CT., and a sister Juliet Peverley of Alton, NH. Memorial contributions may be made to Greenwich Hospital Auxiliary, Perryridge Rd., Greenwich, CT 06830
