
        ALLALOUF-Sam, 73. Of NYC. Died Saturday, July 7, 2001, after weeks of complications following heart surgery. Beloved brother of Pauline and Maury (and wife Jenny) and uncle to Robert, Felice, Gary and Francine. Retired Public Relations Officer and News Editor, formerly of Roosevelt Hospital and St. Mary's Hospital, Hoboken, among others. He will be missed by his family and friends. Memorial service will be held at 11:30 AM, on Tuesday, July 10, at Nieberg's Midwood Chapel, Inc, 1625 Coney Island Ave (corner Ave M), Brooklyn; 718-377-2700.
