
        STRAUSS-Isidore. Died November 7, 1998, at age 86. Beloved father, grandfather, husband, companion, brother and friend. Dearly loved by all whose lives he touched. We all cherish his memory and know he will be in our hearts forever. Poppa, we miss you and love you. Services today, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
