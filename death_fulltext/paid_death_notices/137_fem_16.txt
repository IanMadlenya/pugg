
        CLARE-Barbara K. Died on Saturday, May 30, 1998 from complications of cancer, which she had fought with courage, determination and humor. She was the widow of N. Holmes Clare. She is survived by her three children Marcia C. Lucas of Charleston, SC, Christina C. Evans and N. Holmes Clare Jr, both of New York and six grandchildren. Friends may call Wednesday 5-7pm at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Funeral Mass Thursday 10am at St. Thomas Moore, 65 E. 89 St.
