
        STARKEY--Agnes. 99. On July 21 of complications from a fall in her Amityville home. Widow of former State Supreme Court Justice John R. Starkey. Mother of John Richard of Manhattan, James G.-a Supreme Court Justice in Brooklyn like his father, and Mary Jane Canaan of Port Townsend, WA. She leaves seven grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren. Viewing Sunday, 7:00-9:00 p.m. and Monday 2:00-4:30, 7:00-9:30 at D'Andrea Bros. Funeral Home, 99 Oak Street, Copiague. Funeral Mass will be 9:30 A.M. St. Martin of Tours Church, Amityville. Burial St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village.
