
        CANTARELLA-Francesco. The trustees and staff of NYC Police Foundation note with sorrow the passing of trustee, Francesco. We express our most profound condolences to his wife, Marcia, and to the members of his family. Mr. Cantarella served on the board wholeheartedly and with great pride. His concern for the well-being of all New Yorkers was deeply appreciated. We will miss him. George D. Benjamin, Chairman Pamela D. Delaney, President
