
        HURLEY-Miriam. Of Boston and Chilmark, Marthas Vineyard (formerly of Weston) on Thursday, February 1, 2001. Miriam Greene Hurley, age 90. Wife of the late Donald J. Hurley. Mother of Cornelia Hurley McPeek of Chestnut Hill, Donald Hurley, Jr. of Lloyd Harbor, NY, Stephen Nash Hurley of Boston and Rosamond Page Shugrue of Boston. Grandmother of several grandchildren. There will be a private family Mass. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, March 9, 2001 at the Memorial Church of Harvard University, Harvard Yard, Cambridge at 3:00 P.M. Relatives and friends are respectfully invited to attend. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Miriam Greene Hurley Memorial Fund at the Martha's Vineyard Garden Club, RFD 319X, Vineyard Haven, MA 02568. For information please contact J.S. WatermanEastman-Waring Funeral Services, Boston, MA. (617)536-4110.
