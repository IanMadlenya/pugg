
        LOGAN-Catherine Maria Sr. Sister Catherine Maria Logan, beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, died Thursday, March 15, 2001. Survived by her nephews. Reposing at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark Street, Yonkers, NY on Sunday from 1 to 8 p.m. Mass of Christian Burial Monday 9:30a.m. at the convent with interment following at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, NY 10471
