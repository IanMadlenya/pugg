
        JACKSON-William E. The Board of Trustees, Executive Director and staff of the National Foundation for Facial Reconstruction wish to express their sympathy and their condolences to Nancy Jackson and her daughters on the passing of William E. Jackson. He was a long time member of the NFFR Board and his wisdom and counsel were greatly respected and appreciated. J.P. Hoguet, Chairman John R. Gordon, President
        JACKSON-William E. The Board of Governors, the members and the staff of the River Club of New York mourn the passing of our President, William E. Jackson, Esq. His outstanding leadership as a Governor since 1964 and as President for the pass 22 years earned him the respect and greatest affection of all. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his wife Nancy Jackson and their daughters. The Board of Governors
