
        BROOKS--Henrietta, beloved mother of daughter Rhoda Linda Brooks & son Kenneth Ian Brooks. Beloved aunt of niece Karen Richman. Henrietta died peacefully in the arms of her beloved on Wednesday, May 18th at noon at the age of 88. A beautiful and gracious woman, she brought joy and happiness to all who crossed her path. She will be missed. Services Sunday, 12:45 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in her name may be made to NYU Medical Center.
