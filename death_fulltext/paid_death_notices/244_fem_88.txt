
        SWANTON--Eileen D., 96, died on Thursday May 10, 2007 at Franciscan Oaks Health Center in Denville, NJ. She is survived by her children and their spouses Patricia and Thomas G. Noonan of Hamburg, NJ and Norman F. and Elise Swanton of Greenwich, CT, nine grandchildren and twenty-five great-grandchildren. All services are private, donations being accepted to Franciscan Oaks, 19 Pocono Rd., Denville, NJ 07834.
