
        BASRI-Dr. Albert S. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a longtime friend, esteemed pathologist and valued member of the Lakewood, NJ, Jewish community and Iraqi Jewish community. He and his wife, Annette Iny Basri, highly respected member of YU's Sephardic Council of Overseers, provided scholarship aid for Sephardic students at Yeshiva College, Stern College for Women, and YU High Schools, and supported projects of the YU Women's Organization and YU Museum. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Annette; children, Dr. Raymond (and Ava) Basri, Dr. William Ezra (and Nancy) Basri, and Carole Basri; grandchildren; and the entire family. Min hashamayim tenuhamu. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman Board of Trustees
