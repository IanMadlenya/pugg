
        SPRITZER-David Joseph, Rabbi. Age 61. Died in Hemlock Farms, Lords Valley, PA, December 21, 2001, of cancer. Rabbi Spritzer was a beloved husband, father and grandfather. He leaves behind six children, seven grandchildren, and his devoted wife Cynthia Winograd Spritzer. Rabbi Spritzer was born in Mt. Vernon, New York. A graduate of Stanford University and the Jewish Theological Seminary, he had a Master's degree in Education and Counseling from California State University. Rabbi Spritzer served in congregations Peru, California, and Israel. His last congregation was at Temple Israel in Stroudsburg, PA. He served in the Israeli army and was the founder of two Conservative congregations in Israel. He also served as the president of the Rabbinical Assembly in Israel. Rabbi Spritzer, to all those who knew him well, was an inspiring teacher and was described as a '' sweet man'' who was known for his kind and caring relationships with people. Rabbi Spritzer will be buried in Jerusalem. Donations in his memory can be made to Temple Israel in Stroudsburg PA.
        SPRITZER-Rabbi David J. We mourn with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. He served pulpits in California, Israel and Lima, Peru. He was founding Rabbi of Congregation Hod V'Hadar in Kfar Saba. At the time of his death, he was the Rabbi of Temple Israel in Stroudsburg, PA. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Cynthia Winograd Spritzer, children, Alisa Spritzer, Tamar Sapir, Davidi Winograd, Doni Shefer, Shira Kabik, and Havi Nakar, sister Rozy Badichi, and to all who were touched through his Rabbinate. Rabbi Vernon H. Kurtz, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec VP The Rabbinical Assembly
