
        KILSTEIN-Rita (nee Meyer). On November 20, 1997. Devoted wife of the late Dr. Richard I. Kilstein. After a long illness in Tuscaloosa, AL. Beloved mother of daughter Star (Stan) Bloom and the Rev. Dr. Bonnie Ring. Adored grandmother of Tara and Ashley Bloom. She was a longtime resident of New York City until her retirement to Laguna Hills, CA. Well known in the furniture industry as an officer of Wycombe Meyer Furniture Co. Surviving family include Sydney, Theodore R. Meyer, Norman and Constance Sadek. Contributions may be made to breast cancer.
