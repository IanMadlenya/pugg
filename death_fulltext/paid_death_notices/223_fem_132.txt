
        ROTHSTEIN--Gertrude. In Miami, FL., on August 17, 2005. Widow of Manuel Rothstein. Beloved mother of Jerry Rothstein (Cynthia) and Judy Weinstein (Arthur). Also survived by sister Hilda Soule, brother Marvin Buxbaum, her grandchildren Stephen and Marc Weinstein and Michael, Lori and Meryl Rothstein and her adored nieces and nephews. Her energy, positive attitude, strength of character, loyalty and clearly stated strong opinions (as well as her great cooking) made her memorable to all who knew her. If you desire, contributions to HADASSAH or your favorite charity. Services Friday 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
