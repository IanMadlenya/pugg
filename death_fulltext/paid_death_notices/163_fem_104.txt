
        KIVY-Isabel. Died peacefully in her sleep at home on Saturday, August 12, 2000, at the age of 100. Wife of the late Julius Kivy. Mother of Dr. Peter Kivy and Dr. Elaine Genkins. Grandmother of Karen Fairbank, Esq, Steven Genkins, MD, and Amy Genkins, Esq. Great-grandmother of David and Sarah Fairbank, Daniel and Julian Genkins and Gillian and Jesse Horwitz. Mrs. Kivy was a strong, capable and talented woman. She devoted her life to the welfare of her family. Mrs. Kivy, an honor graduate of Hunter High School, highly valued intellectual endeavors and was strongly committed to the education of her children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. A dancer in her youth and an artist throughout her life, Isabel Kivy was admired by all who knew her and will be missed by everyone whose life she touched.
