
        SHAPIRO--Hyman R., formerly of Lake Worth, Florida, died peacefully on March 29, 2007 at the age of 98. Survived by his daughter, Leila, and her husband, Robert Rubler, grandchildren; Leslie and Neil and Neil's wife Allison; greatgrandchildren Madeleine and Olivia, and his sister Ruth Helfand. Interment in Florida beside Selma, his beloved wife, who predeceased him.
