
        MULLALLY-Dr. Joseph Patrick Michael. On March 22, 1999. Beloved husband of Mary Agnes (nee Galligan). Devoted father of Mary Ann Rose, Joan Scales, Noreen McGuire, Kathleen Mullally and Virginia Hallinan. Dearest brother of Sr. Marie Annunciata of the Sisters of St. Joseph of Brentwood. Loving grandfather of ten grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. He was born in Kilross, County Tipperary, Ireland on March 17, 1913. He received his Doctorate in Philosophy from Columbia University in 1944. As a lover of all things Irish, he taught at the University of Notre Dame from 1944 to 1950. He then taught at Queens College until his retirement in the late 1970's. Visiting hours Tuesday 7-9PM and Wednesday 3-5 and 7-9PM at Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday 10AM. St. Mary's R.C. Church, 1300 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset.
