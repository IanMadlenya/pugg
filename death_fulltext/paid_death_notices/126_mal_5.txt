
        HOPKINS-Gordon A. Age 85 of Whitestone, New York died on June 29, 1997. He was born in Newton, MA., and was in the publishing business in Boston at the Boston Transcript and with Lothrop, Lee and Shepard. His 35-year association with Doubleday in sales and the editorial office in New York and Maine was interrupted by World War II and he retired from the firm in 1967. His wife of 45 years, Myrtle Fredenburg, died in 1980. He is survived by his second wife, Helen, and a beloved niece, Joyce Moisan. There will be no services. Donations, if you wish, to the Whitestone Branch of the Queensboro Library.
