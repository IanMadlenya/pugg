
        KAHN-Stephen, 83. Of Scarsdale, NY, died January 29, 2003. Beloved husband of Eva. Father of Wendy, Jeffrey, and James. Grandfather of six. President of Hofor Tobacco, veteran, ardent historian, linguist, mineralogist. He will be dearly missed. Service 11 AM Friday, Congregation Kol Ami, White Plains, NY.
        KAHN-Stephen M. Congregation Kol Ami records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member and conveys sincere condolences to his bereaved family. Rabbi Tom Weiner Rabbi Shira Milgrom Ken Simon, President
