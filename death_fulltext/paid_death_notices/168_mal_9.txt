
        BETTMANN-James J. Of Williston, VT, died peacefully on Saturday, December 30, 2000; beloved husband of Beverley, devoted father of Jessica Pack and Joen Bettmann. Alumnus of Columbia Grammer School 1936, and Cornell University 1940, also member of the Herringbone Ski Club. He was predeceased by his brother Bob on December 24, 2000.
