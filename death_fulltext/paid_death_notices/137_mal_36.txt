
        DOMENITZ-Steven, 43, on June 3, 1998. Adored and adoring husband of Nancy; beloved son of Irwin and Alyce, and grandson of Lucille Moskowitz; treasured brother of Janet and Louise; cherished stepfather of Oren and Eitan Atias. He also leaves his loving niece and nephews, brothers and sisters-inlaw, mother and father-in-law, and numerous aunts, uncles, and cousins. Died after a courageous 18month battle against a cancerous brain tumor. His sense of humor, musical spirit, warmth and strength will be profoundly missed.
