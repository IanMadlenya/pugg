
        MEISTER -- Bertram, M.D. Respected Yonkers Dermatologist for over 41 years and World War II veteran died peacefully at home on July 24, surrounded by his loving wife Arlene, adoring daughters Lynn and Barbara, devoted sons-in-law Seth and Jean-Marc, and cherished grandchildren Stephanie, David, Hannah, Brooke, and Kate. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mt. Vernon on Wednesday 11:30. Donations to the American Cancer Society, 6725 Lyons Street, PO Box 7 East Syracuse, NY 13057, would be appreciated.
