
        LUGER--Dr. Daniel on May 23 in Florida after a long illness. He leaves a loving and devoted family-his cherished wife, Anita, his children Dr. Jay Luger, Dr. Steven Luger, Richard Luger, Susan Luger and his dear brother Dr. Norton Luger. His grandchildren, all 10 of them, were the delight of his life and they of his. They are Dr. Karyn Kolman, Michael Kolman and eight Lugers--Rachel, Rena, Sara, Judith, Adam, Sarielle, Benjamin and Zekiel. Formerly a resident of Westchester. Dr. Luger was Clinical Professor of Surgery at Mt. Sinai Medical Center. >>AD#
