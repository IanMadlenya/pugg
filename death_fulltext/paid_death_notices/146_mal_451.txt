
        TILLES-Roy E. Jr., 82, of Rye, New York, passed away March 22, 1999, after a short illness. Devoted and beloved husband of Doris. Devoted father of Roy E. III, of Rye, NY, and Thomas N., of Bethel, CT, and daughters-in-law, Barbara and Cynthia. Loving grandfather of Amy, Scott, Bradley, and Kerrie. Survived by his sister Carol T. Shore, of Palm Beach, FL. Born May 18, 1916, in St. Louis, MO, and raised in New Rochelle, NY. Graduated from The Peddie School in Heightstown, NJ, in 1933, and Amherst College in 1937. Attended Harvard Business School, where he was an Assistant Coach for The Harvard College Freshman Football Team. Following service in The United States Navy as a Lieutenant J.G. (1943-1946), he embarked on a business career with Gotham Hosiery. This was followed by a long and successful career as a respected stockbroker with Merrill Lynch in New York City and until recently with Tucker Anthony in Stamford, CT. Avid golfer and member for over 60 years of The Quaker Ridge Golf Club in Scarsdale, NY. 25 year member of The New York State Seniors and The Westchester Seniors Golf Association. Friends may call Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY. Funeral services and interment private. Memorial contributions may be made to Amherst College, Amherst, MA 01002.
