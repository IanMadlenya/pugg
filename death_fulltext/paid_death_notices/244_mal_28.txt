
        POSTON--Tom. The American Academy of Dramatic Arts mourns the loss of its beloved alumnus Tom Poston, AADA Class of 1947, a virtuoso performer whose comedic talents charmed audiences for 60 years. The Academy communities in New York and Los Angeles extend their sincere condolences to his widow, Susanne Pleshette, and his three children, Francesca, Jason and Hudson. Samuel Davis, Chairman of the Board Roger Croucher, President and CEO James Warwick, President, Los Angeles Division
