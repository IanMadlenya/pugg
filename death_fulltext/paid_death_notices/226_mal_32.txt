
        BAKER--Dr. William O. The Board of Trustees and staff of The Fund for New Jersey mourn the passing of our dear friend and esteemed colleague, Dr. William O. Baker. A Trustee for 30 years and then Emeritus, it was our privilege to share responsibility with him for the stewardship of the Foundation. His wisdom, breadth of experience and unique insight into the dynamics of our society were enormous assets to us. We extend our sincerest sympathy to his son Joseph and family. Leonard Lieberman Chairman Joseph C. Cornwall Chairman Emeritus Mark M. Murphy, President
        BAKER--Dr. William O. The Directors, Officers and staff of General American Investors Company, Inc. are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed colleague, Bill Baker. A Director for 13 years and a Director Emeritus since 1996, Bill served the Company with great distinction and dedication, providing us with the benefit of his keen intellect, broad knowledge and wise counsel. We extend our condolences to the members of his family. Lawrence B. Buttenwieser Chairman, Board of Directors
