
        BERLAND-Karen Ina, PhD, 49. Died unexpectedly Saturday in Colorado. She was the beloved daughter of Max and Lillian (Graf) Berland of Southbury, Connecticut. She was a clinical and school Psychologist in the Denver school system and in her own private practice. Graveside service will be held Mount ArArat Cemetery, Farmingdale, Long Island, today, August 5, 1 PM. Shiva will be held in her parents' home at 654B Heritage Village this week.
