
        FREEMAN--Gladys W. Our grandmother and greatgrandmother who shared her wisdom, humor and love throughout her 103 years. Cousin to Capone gangster, ''Potatoes'' Kaufman, Leopold-Loeb trial witness and a University of Chicago graduate. A truly remarkable person whom we will remember and miss. Funeral services Thursday, 10:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to Doctors Without Borders, 1 - 888 392-0392.
