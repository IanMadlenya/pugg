
        RABKIN--Jacob. LexisNexis Matthew Bender mourns the death of one of its most distinguished and longstanding authors, Jacob Rabkin, on July 1, 2003. Together with his co-author Mark H. Johnson, Mr. Rabkin wrote three major legal publications for Matthew Bender, including Rabkin and Johnson, ''Current Legal Forms with Tax Analysis.'' Mr. Rabkin's passing is a great loss to Matthew Bender, LexisNexis, and the entire legal community. We extend our sincere condolences to Mrs. Dorothy Rabkin and her family. >>AD#
