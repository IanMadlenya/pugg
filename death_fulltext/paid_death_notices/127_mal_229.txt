
        KEEN-Joseph, 89, of N. Miami Beach. August 21, 1997, after long illness. Husband of the late Eva. Father of Fred (Whitney) of Tenafly, NJ, Bob (Tamara Browning) of Teaneck, NJ. Grandfather of Michael (Carolyn) Keen, Erica (Greg) Keen Thomas, Leila, Juliana, Vanessa and Amanda Keen. Great-grandfather of William and Allison Keen. Also survived by his sisters, Rose Vitale Weber, Dorothy Finkel (Murray) and Ethel Slobes and numerous nieces and nephews. Founder and retired Chairman of Repeat-O-Type Manufacturing Co, Inc., of Wayne, NJ. Interment private. For biography, see www.repeatotype.com
