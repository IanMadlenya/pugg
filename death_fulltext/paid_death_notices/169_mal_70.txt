
        OBERWAGER-Karl. Beloved husband, left us on Feb. 4, one month after his hundredth birthday. Survived by his loving and devoted wife, Susie; his family in Israel and Mexico, and numerous friends who all loved him. My life is diminished without him. Yours always, Susie and his poodle, Giselle. Funeral service on Wednesday, February 7, 10 AM, at Riverside Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. & 76 St.
