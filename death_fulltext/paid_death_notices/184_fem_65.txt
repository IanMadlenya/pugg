
        LYONS-Beverly. Beloved mother and wife peacefully on Sunday May 5, 2002. Born in Brooklyn on April 16, 1923. Survived by loving husband Dr. Alvin V. Lyons, children Jo-Lynne and husband John Seeger, Richard Green, Daniel Lyons and partner Stanley Cushing, Deborah Lyons and partner Midge Zarling and grandchildren Avery, Emerson, Hanna and Nathaniel. Remembered my many for her smile and good humor, excellence as a dental hygienist, her volunteer work and love of swimming and the ocean. Services will be held at 10AM, May 9th in Chula Vista, CA. Condolences in the form of charitable contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Assn.
