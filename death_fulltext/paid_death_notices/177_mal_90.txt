
        SHAPIRO-Jerome G., 77. Died Tuesday, October 2, 2001 after complications from a stroke and Alzheimer's disease. He practiced law in New York City for over 45 years with the firm of Hughes Hubbard & Reed, becoming its Chairman from 1975-1989. Survived by his wife of 42 years, Marjorie Mackay Shapiro, three children, Jeffrey Kemble, Jill Dara and Eric Noach, and six grandchildren. He is also mourned by his beloved sister, Norma Kleiner and many nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue and West 91st Street on Sunday, October 7th at 3 PM. Burial service will be at Abel's Hill Cemetery in Chilmark, MA at 4 PM on Thursday, October 4, 2001. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his honor to the Alzheimer's Association of New York, 360 Lexington Avenue, New York, NY 10017.
