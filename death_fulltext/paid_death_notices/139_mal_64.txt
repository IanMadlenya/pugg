
        TIERS-Lindley W. Devoted husband of the late Sarah Morgan Gardner Tiers. Passed away on July 29, 1998. He was a nationally ranked tennis player in the late 1930's. During his career with CitiBank, he was posted to China, India and Singapore. Memorial contributions may be made to the Sarah Gardner Tiers Scholarship Foundation Inc., 1211 Park Ave., NY, NY 10128.
