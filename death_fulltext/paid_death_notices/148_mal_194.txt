
        BERNFELD-Alan L. Of Hallandale, FL. Died May 18, 1999. A dear, sweet, gentle man. Loving husband of Lillian. Father of Bruce and Fran. Father-in-law of Eileen. Grandfather of Susannah. Brother-in-law, uncle, great uncle and friend. His 90 years were filled with abounding love and deep devotion to and from his family and friends. He loved life, work and people. His laughter and humor will be missed and always remembered. Graveside service 1:30PM Wednesday, May 19th at Menorah Gardens, 21100 West Griffin Road, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33332.
