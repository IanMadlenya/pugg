
        MONDSHEIN-Esther Belle. 98-1/2 years young. Beloved wife of the late Frank. Loving mother of Marian Edson, Harriet Dicker and son-in-law Arnold Edson. Adored grandmother of Andrea, Richard, Steven, Jennifer, Donna and Debbie and seven great grandchildren. Former Manager of the Consulate of Afghanistan for thirty years, Belle was an outstanding and unusual human being. We love her and we will all miss her greatly. Funeral service at Zion Memorial Home, 767 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, New York, Sunday, April 20th, at 12 Noon.
