
        POKART--Joseph. Age 95, died May 4, 2005 on the birthday of his beloved late wife of 60 years, Billie Pokart. Loving father of Steve and Sam; father-in-law of Nurit. Grandfather of Rebekah, Tom and Sarah. Salutatorian St. John's Law School, 1934; distinguished lawyer everafter. Will be cherished always by all who knew him. Services Sunday May 8, at Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 7205 West Atlantic Ave, Delray Beach, FL. Call 561496-7550 for time of service.
