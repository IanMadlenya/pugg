
        SCHOOLSKY-Robert. November 5, 1998. Wine writer, lecturer and consultant. Former Vice Chairman of the ''Wine Media Guild'' and a member of Burgundy's Compagnon de Beaujolais. Survived by his sister Esther and brother-in-law Harvey Kircsh, nephews Steven and Jonathan, grandniece Ellie, and his beloved longtime companion Bernice (Bunny) Blaine. Memorial services to be held in Denver, CO, at a later date to be announced. Donations in Robert's memory may be made to the American Lung Association.
