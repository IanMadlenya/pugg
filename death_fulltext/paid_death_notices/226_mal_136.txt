
        KASDAN--John Martin, 66. Of New York City died in Boston on Wednesday. Born and raised in NYC, he practiced law, computer programming and was a professor at Columbia University School of Law for many years. He graduated Harvard in 1960, has a Master's in mathematics from Yale, PhD in mathematics from UCLA, post graduate work in mathematics from Cambridge in England, law degree from UCLA. He was a man of many interest, ballet, theater, scuba diving, squash, bridge, marathon runner, animal lover, and world traveler. Survived by his companion of 22 years, Lillian Meyer, step daughters, Jenny Meyer of Brooklyn, Margot Hughes-Lopez of San Francisco, granddaughters, Maya and Nicole Lopez, and many relatives and friends. Services 1 PM Friday, Bloomfield-Cooper Jewish Chapels, Ocean Township, NJ.
