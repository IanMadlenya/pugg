
        KNOX-Joan Darrach Fox, 65. Of Monticello, FL., died Thursday, March 12. A native of NYC and former resident of Sarasota, FL., and Greenwich, CT. She was a watercolor artist whose work has been displayed nationally and internationally. She was a humanitarian and environmentalist. She is survived by a son, Robert Knox of Monticello, FL, a daughter Ellen Knox Ward, of Saudi Arabia, a brother Kerry Fox of Fullerton, CA., and two grandchildren. Memorial services will be announced and held in May in Florida.
