
        REINER--Dr. Leopold, on March 7, 2004, at the age of 93. Beloved husband of the late Lillian Reiner. Brother of Bella Federman. Loving aunt and great aunt of Lydia, James, Robert, and Laurie Nolan; Doris, Stephen and Donald Young; David Siegel, Janet Kaplan, Ruth Bletzinger, Michael Federman, and Irith Landau. He was born in Leipsig, Germany and attended the Universities of Frieberg and Leipsig. He attended the University of Vienna and received his medical degree from Rothschild Hospital in Vienna. He was Chief of Pathology at Beth Israel Hospital in Boston as well as at Bronx Lebanon Hospital in New York. He was Professor of Pathology at Harvard Medical School and Albert Einstein College of Medicine. Graveside services, Tuesday, March 9, 2004, at 1:30 PM, Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
