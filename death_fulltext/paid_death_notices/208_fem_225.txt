
        SCHRAGER--Anne Connolly Carroll. Born in New York City in 1919, died Thursday at St. Peter's Hospital in Albany, NY after a brief illness. She graduated from Barnard College in 1941, and was a member of the staff of the American Embassy in Rome between 1945 and 1950. She worked for the New York State Department of Labor during the 1970's and 80's, and retired to Rensselaerville, NY in 1987. She had been married to Victor Leland Schrager of New York City and London, who predeceased her. She is survived by her son, Victor, his wife, Mame and her grandson Victor McClellan Schrager. Donations in lieu of flowers may be sent to St. David's School in New York City.
