
        OHTA-Yoshi (Tiny). Of NYC and Tokyo passed away on September 13, 1998. The devoted wife of the late Teruo Ohta. An inspirational mother to Yasuteru and Akiko Ohta and Mme. Hiroko Yukawa and exceptional mother-in-law to Chie Ohta. Also loving grandmother of Motoyasu and Motoyoshi Yukawa and Teruo and Hikari Ohta. She was a patron of the Arts and an especially devoted fan of The Metropolitan Opera and The St. Petersburg Opera. She lived with love and compassion until the very last of her life. We will miss her presence forever. A private service will be held at a later date in Tokyo.
