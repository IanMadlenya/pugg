
        SCHWARTZ-Lester. Died on June 18, 2000. Beloved husband of Mara, devoted father of Ralph, Victoria, Ted, Nancy and brother of Martin. Adoring grandfather of Elana, Jonathan and Jack. A dedicated psychoanalyst, deeply devoted to his patients, family and friends. Services-The SAJ, 15 W. 86th St., June 20, 9:30 AM. Donations-The New York Psychoanalytic Institute.
        SCHWARTZ-Lester, M.D. The members of The New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of their esteemed and beloved colleague, Dr. Lester Schwartz. He was a remarkable clinician and educator, who trained and guided many psychoanalysts. His integrity and capacity for insight was treasured by his peers and his students. As Past President of The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Chairman of many committees, his contributions have left an indelible mark on our institution and society. Our loss is considerable and he will be deeply missed. We extend our condolences to his wife, Mrs. Mara Schwartz, and to his family. Contributions in his name may be sent to The New York Psychoanalytic Society at 247 East 82nd Street, New York City, N.Y. 10028. Eslee Samberg, M.D. President, Institute Albert M. Sax, M.D. President, Society
