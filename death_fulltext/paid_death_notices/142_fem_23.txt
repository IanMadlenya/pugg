
        WILKES-Margaret, 48. Of NYC and Greensboro, N.C., on October 17, 1998 from ovarian cancer. She led a long and successful career as a director in apparel and advertising. She is survived by husband, Allen Holt Gwyn, her son Stephen Denslow of Sandusky, OH., and brothers, John Wilkes of NYC and Timothy Wilkes of Milan, OH.
