
        SKLAR-Mildred Robbins. Died peacefully at age 89 after a long illness on June 27, 1997. Loving mother of Jay & Ellen Sklar, Isabel Sklar Jackson and Fred Jackson. Beloved grandmother of Elizabeth and Deborah; devoted great grandmother of Suzanne, Jennifer & Alexander. Loved & respected by many family members, former students & friends for her courage, curiosity, devotion to health care reform and her active pursuit of life. Contributions in her memory can be made to the Lincoln Guild Outreach Program, 303 West 66th Street, New York City. Services are on Sunday at 11 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
