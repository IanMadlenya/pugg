
        MURRAY-Edward Mitchell. Of Crosslands, Kennett Square, PA (formerly of Port Washington and Flushing, NY), on Tuesday, March 13, 2001 in his 89th year. Husband of Gainor Lowry Murray; father of Margaret Murray, Robert Murray and the late William King Murray, grandfather of Katherine Meadows Murray. Memorial services will be held 2 P.M. on Saturday, March 17, 2001 at Crosslands, Rt. 926, Kennett Square, PA. Memorial contributions in his name may be made to Episcopal Relief and Development Fund, 815 2nd Ave., New York, New York 10017.
