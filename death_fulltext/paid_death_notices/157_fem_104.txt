
        MORRISON-Pauline O'Gorman. Wife of the late Barclay Morrison, formerly of New Vernon, NJ and Hilton Head, SC died February 9th in Greenwich, CT after a long illness. She is survived by her daughters Pauline Morrison of Seattle, WA, Elizabeth Morrison Baker of Katonah, NY, Barbara Morrison Missiras of Moorhead, MN, Carol Morrison Shiels of Kaufman, TX and her daughter-inlaw Mrs. Barclay Morrison, Jr. of Villanova, PA. She is also survived by her two sisters, Mrs. Willard W. Dixon of West Caldwell, NJ and Mrs. John Karlin of Locust, NJ, her cousin Mrs. Ralph T. Crane of Quogue, NY and her sister-in-law Miss Carol Morrison of Montauk, NY. She leaves behind eight grandchildren and one great granddaughter. A memorial service will be held at The Church of Christ the King in New Vernon, NJ on Friday, March 3rd at 11 am. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Capt.
