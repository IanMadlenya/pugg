
        STAVIS-Bernice Coe, 81. On April 24, 2001. She is survived by her husband, playwright Barrie Stavis, daughter Jane, son Alex, and sister Doris. A pioneer in the television distribution business, she founded Coe Film Associates in 1970, influencing programming and mentoring a generation of women TV executives. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Bernice Coe Stavis Scholarship Fund at Vassar College (124 Raymond Avenue, Poughkeepsie, NY 12604). A memorial service to be announced.
        STAVIS-Bernice Coe. Thank you for all those wonderful, unforgettable days. Gerry Gewirtz Friedman
