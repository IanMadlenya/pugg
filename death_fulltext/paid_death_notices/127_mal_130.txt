
        PETERMAN-Dr. Richard Lee. Age 74 of Madison, NJ. Died August 8, 1997 at the Lutheran Home of Moorestown, NJ. He is survived by his wife Betty Shogren Peterman of Madison, NJ, two sons, Richard Lee, Jr. and David D. a daughter, Rebecca Johnsen, a brother Earl Linwood and seven grandchildren. A Eucharistic Memorial Service will be held on August 20, 1997 at 8:00PM at St. John's Lutheran Church, 587 Springfield Avenue, Summit, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made to the Rev. Dr. Richard Lee Peterman Memorial Evangelical Lutheran Church in America Foundation, 8765 West Higgins Road, Chicago, IL 60631-4179, in lieu of flowers.
