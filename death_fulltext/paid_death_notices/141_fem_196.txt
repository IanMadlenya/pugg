
        ANDERSON-Dr. Margaret McKee, 90, died October 19, 1998, in Carthage, IL. Preceded in death by husband Orrin E. Anderson in 1972. She was graduated from Bryn Mawr College in 1928 and from Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons in 1932. She spent most of her life in Riverdale, NY, where she practiced pediatric medicine for 32 years with offices in Manhattan and Riverdale, and was affiliated with St. Luke's Hospital, New York City. In 1971 she retired to the family home in Carthage, IL. Surviving are two daughters Laura Anderson of St. Louis, MO and Martha Lou Anderson of Carthage, IL, and a niece, Edythe McKee of Stamford, CN.
