
        GOSSETT--O. Milton, 80. Milt worked his way up the advertising industry ladder reaching Chairman and CEO of Compton Advertising and later CEO of Saatchi & Saatchi. While he was very successful in his career, his most favorite things to do were fishing and gardening. He leaves loving wife Anna, five children, Susanne, Michael, Thomas, Lorraine and James, and 11 grandchildren.
