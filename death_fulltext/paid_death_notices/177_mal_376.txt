
        GLOSTER-Charles D. Architect, 79, died October 21, 2001. Beloved husband of Muriel (Fried) for more than 59 years. Devoted father of Robert Gloster and Susan D'Ver. Adoring grandfather of Ethan D'Ver and Talia Eshel Gloster. Dear brother of Helen Richards and the late Amos E. Gloster. He filled our lives with love and laughter.
