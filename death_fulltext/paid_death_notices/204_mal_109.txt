
        von ARTENS -- Peter (1937-2003). The staff at Wally Findlay Galleries deeply mourns the death of their dear friend and esteemed artist on December 31, 2003. Peter's extraordinary ability to create a masterpiece of realism awarded him recognition in global art circles. His recently published book celebrates his unparalleled accomplishments amongst the Latin American Masters. Our deepest sympathy to his devoted partner Victor Losito. James R. Borynack, Chairman and CEO
