
        STEINBAUM-Dr. Paul S. Died August 16, at his home in Morristown, NJ, at age 85, after suffering a stroke in July. A family physician for fifty years, he practiced in Leonia and Ortley Beach, NJ, before retiring in 1994. During World War II, he was captain of the 726th Army Medical Company and also served in France. An ardent tennis player, skier, sailor, swimmer and everyone's handyman, he shared his enthusiasms with an extended family and friends of all ages. Survived by a sister, Evelyn Zinader Baum of Bayonne. Beloved by his wife of fifty-eight years, Esther Rosenberg Steinbaum, his children, Robert and Rosemary Steinbaum of Montclair, Jonnet and Peter Abeles of New York, and four grandsons, Marshall and Elliot Steinbaum, and Oren and Ethan Abeles. Funeral service Friday, August 18, at noon at the Dangler Funeral Home, 600 Speedwell Avenue, Morris Plains. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Congregation Ahavas Sholom, 145 Broadway, Newark, NJ 07104 or to the Freylinghuysen Arboretum, 53 East Hanover Avenue, Morristown, NJ 07960.
