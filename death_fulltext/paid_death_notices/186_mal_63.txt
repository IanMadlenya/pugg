
        BYER-Samuel. President of the Board of Directors of the Workmen's Circle MultiCare Center since 1987. He will always be remembered for the strength of his leadership. He was truly a man of greatness with a vision for the future of the Workmen's Circle MultiCare Center. His extraordinary dedication and tireless efforts turned that vision into reality. His commitment to the frail and elderly residents knew no bounds. He will be missed by all who had the privilege to know and work with him. Our heartfelt condolences to his loving family. Board of Directors David Londin, Exec Dir Judith Fenster, Asst Exec Dir Residents and Staff of the Home
