
        LEHMAN-Franz J. Age 85, of the Lower East Side, NYC. Died on June 24, after being struck by an automobile on May 30. Survived by his wife of 54 years Rosel, a niece, nephew and many friends. He will be sorely missed. Contributions in his name may be made to Metropolitan Council on Housing, 64 Fulton St., NYC 10038. There will be a memorial in the near future.
