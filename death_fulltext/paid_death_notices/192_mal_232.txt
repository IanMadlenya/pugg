
        PALEY-Jess. Native New York, born 1919. Died January 14. Cinematographer with a fine and precise eye, he worked on many documentaries, commercials and films. Complicated brilliance, deep tenderness, a difficult lover of the world. Survived by his children, who learned the value of music from him: Nora and Danny Paley, daughter-in-law Debbie Paley, grandchildren Laura Manya Paley, Zamir Zenya Paley, Sienna Jenya Paley; dear friend Ruth Gray and former wife and friend Grace Paley.
