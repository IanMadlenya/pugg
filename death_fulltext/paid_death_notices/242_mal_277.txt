
        PARKER--Keith E., importer and seller of art works, passed away peacefully at Cabrini Hospice on March 20, after much suffering. He is survived by his partner of 20 years, Brian Richardson. He was born in Perth, Western Australia, to Ernest (deceased) and Peggy Parker and had six siblings, Ray, Maree, Julie, Jenny, Greg and Dennis. He is survived by a large extended family. His life was celebrated at a gathering of family and friends at their home on March 25. He was an inspiration to us all in his battle and will be greatly missed by all his family and friends. We will remember him always.
