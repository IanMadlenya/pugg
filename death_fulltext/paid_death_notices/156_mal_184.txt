
        WARSHAW - Milton. American Friends of Yeshivot Bnei Akiva records with deep sorrow the passing of Milton Warshaw, father of our Executive Director, Rabbi David Warshaw. We extend deepest condolences to his wife, Hermia, sons David and Abba (and Susan), and grandchidren Jonathan, Seth and Rachel. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marvin Bienenfeld, Chairman Joel Schreiber, Vice Chairman Dr. Irwin Kanarek, Co-President Alan Wildes, Co-President
