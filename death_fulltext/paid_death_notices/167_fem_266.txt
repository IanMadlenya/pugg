
        BRIEFER-Ruth (Meyer). Died Friday, Dec. 22 in her 94th year. Born in New York City, she spent her last 30 years in Winter Park, FL, where she and her late husband, Frederick Briefer, were active in the Unitarian Church and OCCA. She is survived by her son Charles Briefer, her sister, Florine Bleyer, and nieces and nephews.
