
        HIRSH-Margaret. Devoted wife of Kurt, beloved mother of Helen and Richard, cherished and adored grandmother of Daniel, Evelyn, Stephen, and David, trusted friend and confidante, passed away Feburary 28, age 85. A partner in life and in business with Kurt, she will endure as a loving inspiration and role model to family and friends. Her perseverance, compassion, and grace will be remembered by those who loved her. In lieu of flowers, donations can be sent to the American Lung Association. Services Monday, March 3, 11:30 AM, Riverside-Nassau North Chapel, Great Neck, NY.
