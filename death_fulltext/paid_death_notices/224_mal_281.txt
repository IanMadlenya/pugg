
        SMITH--Martin. The New York Philharmonic mourns the passing, on September 24, of Martin Smith, 58, who joined the Orchestra in 1974 as Associate Principal Horn and became Acting Principal Horn in 1979. Mr. Smith began studying the horn at age 12, and received bachelor's and master's degrees from The Juilliard School. At his death he was co-principal horn of the Pittsburgh Symphony, which he joined in 1980. We extend condolences to his wife, Carrie, daughter Jennifer, son Michael, and family. Lorin Maazel, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Zarin Mehta, Pres./Exec. Dir. Members of the Staff
