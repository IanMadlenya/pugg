
        KOVACH--Carole. Age 81, died February 8, after a short illness. Devoted wife of the late John. Loving mother of Gail. Sister of the late Harriet Frohman. Adoring aunt to Ed, Scott and Dara Frohman, Sallee, Faran and Jayme Cohen. A compassionate, generous and loving person who will be sorely missed by all who knew her.
