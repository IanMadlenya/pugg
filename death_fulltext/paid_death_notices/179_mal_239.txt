
        WOLFSON-Nathaniel, DDS. Died after nearly 102 years of a happy and healthy life. Husband of the late Beatrice. Father of Estarose Wolfson and Manfred Mohr, Milton Wolfson and Patty Burrows. Grandfather of Jody and Edward, Tracy and Scott, Jessica, Jordan. Great-grandfather of Maximiliana, Henri and Hana. We will remember him always for his warmth, kindness, generosity of spirit and extraordinary wit. Services Wednesday 11:30 AM, Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam. Shiva Wednesday and Thursday at the home of Estarose Wolfson.
