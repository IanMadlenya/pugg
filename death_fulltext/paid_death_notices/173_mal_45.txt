
        PETZINER-Norman, 91, died suddenly, in his Delray Beach, FL home on June 2. He had been a long time resident of Westchester County, NY. Born in NYC, he was a graduate of Peter Stuyvesant High School and Columbia University School of Pharmacy. He had been a founding member and violionist in the Philharmonic Symphony of Westchester, the Yonkers Philharmonic Orchestra and the Deerfield, FL. Symphony. He is survived by his wife of 66 years, Helen, two daughters, Terre Wallach and Barbara Carman, six grandchildren, six great-grandchildren, a brother, Milton, and sister, Mildred Firestone. The service is to be held at The Riverside in Mt. Vernon, NY at 21 Broad St. Wednesday, June 6, at 12 noon.
