
        HARRIS-Pauline S. (Sang) of Nashua, NH. Entered into rest, March 22, 2000 formerly of New York City and Lowell, MA., aged 102 years. She was the wife of the late Maurice E. Harris; loving mother of the late Richard Harris, and grandmother of Richard Harris of IN and Mark and Shelli Harris of NH; sister of Alfred Sang, of NY. Funeral Service Sun. at 10am at the Morse-Bayliss Funeral Home, 122 Princeton Blvd., Lowell, MA., Friends invited. Please omit flowers. Friends who wish may make Memorial contributions in her name to the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Museum, 100 Raoul Wallenberg Pl., SW, Washington, D.C. 20024-2150. Interment in Temple Beth El Cemetery. The Memorial observance will be private.
