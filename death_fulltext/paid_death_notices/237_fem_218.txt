
        RILANDER--Beverly. Age 89, of Rockville Centre, NY, passed away on October 26 of natural causes. Beloved wife of the late Arthur for 68 years. Loving mother of Harvey (Lynda) and Kenneth (Nancy) and cherished grandmother of Adam and Allison. She will be remembered as a devoted mother and wife who always wanted the best for her family. Graveside service at New Montefiore Cemetery at 11 AM Monday.
