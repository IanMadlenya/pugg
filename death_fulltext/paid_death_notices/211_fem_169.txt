
        NGO--Ignacia U., M.D. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our dear friend and beloved colleague, Ignacia U. Ngo, M.D., a member of the faculty in the Dept. of Anesthesiology for 28 years. ''Iggy,'' as she was affectionately known at SLR, will be remembered by all whose lives were touched by her constant dedication to the highest quality medical care. To her family, friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. Richard F. Daines, M.D. President and CEO Carl W. Braun, M.D. President, Medical Board Daniel M. Thys, M.D. Chairman of Anesthesiology
