
        SETON--Fenmore R., 85, of Hamden, CT. Beloved husband of Phyllis, devoted father of Diana Wakerley, loving grandfather of Christopher and James Adams. Died on Memorial Day, May 26, 2003. Fen was a decorated WW II Air Force Major, the founder and CEO of Seton Name Plate Corp., past President of Rehabilitation International, a civic leader and philanthropist, who for more than 60 years dedicated himself to improving the lives of others. Contributions may be made to The Rehabilitation Center, 95 Hamilton St, New Haven, CT. 06511, or The Clifford Beers Clinic, 93 Edwards St, New Haven, CT. 06511. >>AD#
