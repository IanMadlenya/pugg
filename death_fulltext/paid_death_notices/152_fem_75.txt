
        GILLESPIE-Ruth Reed, 86. Died on Sunday, September 5 at her home in NYC. Former head librarian at Collegiate School and Alumni President and Trustee of the Chapin School, survived by her daughter, Jane Brown Grimes of New York and Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania and her son, Samuel H. Gillespie, III of Washington, D.C., as well as seven grandchildren and her three sisters, Julia Reed Palmer of New York, Mary Reed Shepard of St. Paul, Minn. and Joan Reed Hudson of Southwest Harbor, Me. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Collegiate School library, 370 West End Avenue, NY, NY 10024. Services Friday September 10, 1999 at 10 A.M. at The Church Of The Holy Trinity, 316 East 88th Street.
