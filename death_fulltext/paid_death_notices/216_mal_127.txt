
        COLM--Werner. On January 3, 2005 in his 97th year. Predeceased by his wife Helen. He is survived by his niece Hella R. Adler of Philadelphia. Born in Germany, he lived for many years in Shanghi, moving to New York where he was a metal trader until his retirement. Full of energy until his death, after a brief illness. His spirit will be remembered by his many friends. Ralph A. Gant, Long-time friend, colleague, executor
