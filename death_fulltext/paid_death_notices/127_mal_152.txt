
        GIOVANNETTI-Robert J. Passed away August 13, 1997. Survived by his wife Gale F; children Joseph D. and Mary Elizabeth Giovannetti. Parents Daniel and Mary Giovannetti. Also many aunts, uncles and cousins. He was a partner of the law firm of Jackson, Lewis, Schnitzler & Krupman. The partners, associates and staff of Jackson, Lewis, Schnitzler & Krupman mourn the passing of our partner Robert J . Giovannetti. His ability, skill and mentoring will be missed by all. We shall miss his friendship even more. Our prayers and thoughts are extended to his parents and family; Gale, joseph and Mary Elizabeth. Mr. Giovannetti was a member of the American Bar Assoc., California Bar Assoc., New York Bar Assoc, Westchester Bar Assoc. and the Columbia Law School Alumni Assoc. Funeral Mass Friday, 10 AM at Holy family Church, South Pasadena, Calif. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Diabetes Assoc, The Juvenile Diabetes Assoc or R.O.P.A.
