
        MACKTA-Regina Katz, 85, of Midlothian, VA, formerly of New York City, died January 15, 1997. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Leo Mackta, DDS, retired; son Fred Mackta of Richmond, VA, daughter Betsy Sue Scott of Great Falls, VA., brother Milton Kaye of New York City. Also five grandchildren and 1 great grandchild. The family will receive friends at her residence in Midlothian. Information: Woody Funeral Home, Midlothian. Memorial contributions may be made to your favorite charity.
