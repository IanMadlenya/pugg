
        EUSTACE-Edward Patrick. On Friday, January 9. Age 74. Of Montvale, NJ. Born to the late Patrick and Mary Eustace of New York City. Husband of Catherine Eustace, 39 years. Father of Edward, Stephen, James, Mary Catherine, and John. Father-in-law of Emmy, Maura, Cheryl and Edward Johnson. Grandfather of Ana, Colin and Christopher. Brother of Robert J. Eustace and Mary Eustace Leonard. Combat veteran World War II, serving in the U.S. Army Air Corps as a member of the U.S. 8th Air Force. He was awarded five Air Medals for courageous service in bomber operations over occupied Europe in 1944-45. 1948 graduate of Manhattan College. Served in the Banking Department of the State of New York for 35 years. Retired from the position of Deputy Superintendent of Foreign Banks for the State of New York and was widely recognized for his major contribution to a sound international banking community in the State. Former Grand Knight, Knights of Columbus Council 4486, Park Ridge, NJ. Viewing at Becker Funeral Home, Westwood, NJ, 7-9 PM, Monday, and 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM, Tuesday. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Mercy Church, Park Ridge, NJ, 9:30 AM, on Wednesday. Memorial contributions may be made to Tomorrow's Children Fund, 30 Prospect Ave., Hackensack, NJ, or the Parkinson's Disease Foundation.
