
        FELDBLUM - Professor Meyer S. The Bernard Revel Graduate School of Jewish Studies of Yeshiva University mourns a former colleague and alumnus. A distinguished scholar, he served as Revel's University Professor of Talmudic Literature. Heartfelt condolences to his wife, Dr. Ayala Feldblum, his children, and all the members of his family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Arthur Hyman, BRGS Dean Mordecai D. Katz, Chairman BRGS Board
