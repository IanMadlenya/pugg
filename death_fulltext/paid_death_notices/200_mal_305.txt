
        LOBENFELD--Sam. On September 27th, 2003 at the age of 89 in the home of his daughter in Brooklyn, N.Y. Beloved husband of the late Ruth Rifkin Lobenfeld. He is survived by his daughter Frann Giambanco and his son Eric Lobenfeld, their spouses Glenn and Trish, grandchildren Jessica and her husband Keith Oechsner, Claire and Margot, and great granddaughter, Dylan Annice. He was an Army Captain in WWII, a POW and received the Purple Heart in 1945. A generous, quiet man, he will be greatly missed by his family. A funeral service will be held at 10AM Tuesday, September 30, 2003 at I.J. Morris, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Bklyn, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Metropolitan Hospice of Greater New York.
