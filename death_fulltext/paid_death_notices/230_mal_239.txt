
        UNGERS--Simon, 1957-2006. Architect and artist, passed away on Monday, March 6, in Cologne, Germany after a long illness. He was probably best known for the critically acclaimed ''T-House'' in Upstate New York. He was internationally known and respected not only as an architect, but also as an artist who maintained a lifelong investigation into abstraction, reversal and the void. Simon is survived by his wife, Janet O'Hair, his parents, Liselotte and Mathias Ungers, sisters, Sibylle Rowan and Sophia Ungers. A memorial service will be held at the AIA, Center for Architecture, 536 LaGuardia Place, NYC, Wednesday, March 22, at 6 PM.
