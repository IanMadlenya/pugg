
        WOLF-Lona B. Born June 15, 1910, died peacefully at home on January 31, 2001. Beloved sister of Emma C. Lynaugh, Frances M. Deegan and Harry J. Wolf. Devoted sister-in-law of Marie Giblin Wolf, Marie Frey Wolf and Agnes Morr Wolf. Adored ''Auntie'' to her many nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grand-nephews that survive her. Loyal friend to all. We will greatly miss this generous and selfless lady who gave so much to others throughout her lifetime. Reposing at Thomas C. Montera Funeral Home, 1848 Westchester Ave., Bronx, NY, Saturday and Sunday, 2:00-5:00 and 7:00-9:00 PM. Funeral Mass Monday 10:00 AM, Blessed Sacrament Church, Bronx, NY. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Cathedral High School Scholarship Fund, 350 E. 56 St., New York, NY 10022-4199.
