
        HANSEN--Tara. With profound sorrow, the Horace Mann School mourns the tragic passing of Mr. Tara Hansen '70, devoted husband of Anuradha and beloved and cherished father of Amar '06 & Tarun '03. The family's role model and inspiration in life, his sudden death is a great loss to his family and Horace Mann. His passion, loyalty and belief in Horace Mann's work and mission will never be forgotten. Our thoughts and heartfelt condolences are with the family, Anuradha, Amar & Tarun, during this time of deep sadness. Dr. Thomas M. Kelly Head of School Robert J. Katz '65 Chair, Board of Trustees
