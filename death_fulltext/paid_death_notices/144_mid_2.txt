
        TURNHEIM-Palmer. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board and members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City express our heartfelt condolences to Joy Turnheim on the untimely passing of her father, Palmer Turnheim. He was a devoted friend and staunch supporter for UCP over many years and an advocate for children and adults with cerebral palsy and related disabilities. We share her grief. Leo Hausman, President Edward R. Matthews, Exec. Dir. Ronald J.H. Napal, Director of Development UCP/New York City
