
        OISLANDER-William B. Dr. 87, passed away Monday, May 14, 2001 in Lake Worth, FL. He was a highly respected and dearly loved General Surgeon who practiced in Lindenhurst, NY and NYC for over 50 years. He is survived by his loving wife of 54 years, Lillian, devoted daughters, Meredith Oislander, Marjorie Krause, and cherished grandchildren Lindsay and Allison. Graveside service will be held Wednesday May 16 at 2:30pm at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, New York. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
