
        KASSAN--Sandra. Of Westport, CT, died Saturday night, September 25, 2004, at her home on her 58th birthday. She is survived by her husband, Alan Kassan, her sons, Matthew and Andrew, three grandchildren Madison, Taylor and Sydney, her brother, Mark, and her sister, Joan. She will be remembered as a loving wife, mother, sister, friend and lover of life. Donations can be made on her behalf to The Lustgarten Foundation For Pancreatic Cancer Research at 1111 Stewart Avenue, Bethpage, NY, 11714, Phone: 516-803-2304.
