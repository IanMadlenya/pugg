
        BAER-Ralph S. Died at home in Yonkers, NY, on February 11th, 2000, at the age of 85. Beloved husband of Dorothy nee Griesheimer. Devoted father of Denise Resnik, grandfather of Andrew and James Resnik, and brother of Lisa Messinger. He was an active volunteer for the New York Public Library. The family will receive friends at his home. His love and grace will be long remembered.
