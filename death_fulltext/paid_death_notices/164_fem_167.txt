
        TROWBRIDGE-Lee Claire Doran. Of Bedford, NY. Died at home on September 13, 2000. She attended Briarcliff Junior College and a graduate of Tufts University. She has lived in Bedford, NY, since 1979 and previously before that in Bronxville, NY; Los Angeles, CA; Chicago, IL; Cambridge, MA; and Wellesley Hills, MA. She is survived by her loving husband Calvin Durand Trowbridge, Jr., and her children Calvin Durand Trowbridge III, Lee Crane Trowbridge Gwyn, David Kenyon Webster Trowbridge and Nina Ripley Trowbridge. She is also survived by her grandchildren Alexandra Hayley Trowbridge and Catherine Durand Trowbridge. She is also survived by her loving parents Mr. and Mrs. Arthur Clements Doran and five siblings. Lee will be remembered for her love of her husband, children, grandchildren and friends, her sense of humor, her love of tennis and broad interest. Memorial service at St. John's Parish in South Salem, NY, 11 AM, Tuesday, September 19. Reception at Bedford Golf and Tennis Club, Bedford, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, Dr. Jackie Bromberg Research Fund, Breast Cancer Clinic, 64th Street, General Post Office, PO Box 26352, NY, NY 10087-6352 or St. John's Parish, The Lee Claire Doran Trowbridge Memorial Fund, PO Box 394, South Salem, NY 10590 or Veteran's of Foreign Wars, Post 2285, 433 White Plains Rd., Eastchester, NY 10709.
