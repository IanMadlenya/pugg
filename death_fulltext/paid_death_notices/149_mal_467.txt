
        BIGGS-Walter J. 78, on June 9, 1999. Of respiratory failure, after a brief illness. Founder of Hospital Publications and publisher of Medical Aspects of Human Sexuality and Hospital Medicine. Served honorably as a United State Naval Officer during World War II, graduate of St. John's University. Survived by his son Walter Biggs Jr., his daughters, Barbara Schade and Joanne Biggs, and his granddaughter Carlota Biggs-Rubio.
