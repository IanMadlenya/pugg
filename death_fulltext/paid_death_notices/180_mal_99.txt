
        ROSENSTOCK-Jerome, 77. Of Boca Raton, FL, died on the sea that he loved on January 3rd, 2002. He is the son of the late Louis and Anna Rosenstock of Ellenville, NY. He is survived by his adoring children, Karen and Linda, his two loving grandchildren, Jared and Jordana, his brothers and sisters, nieces and nephews, cousins and many devoted friends and coworkers. Jerry was President of General Sportwear Co., Inc. of Ellenville, NY and NYC until his retirement in 1985. Donations can be made to Congregation Ezrath Israel Synagogue, Ellenville, NY. For funeral information, call Joseph N. Garlick Funeral Home, 845-794-7474.
