
        MASCOLO-Alfred E. Emeritus Dean of Instructional Services and Professor of Business at Kingsborough Community College, The City University of New York. The adminstration, faculty, staff and students of Kingsborough Community College mourn the passing of a most valued colleague and friend on Sunday, July 5. Dean Mascolo joined Kingsborough on October 15, 1971, directing admissions, registration, and the computer center until his retirement in 1987. He was affiliated with a number of professional societies, including the Catholic Teachers Association, American Association of Collegiate Registrars and Officers of Admissions, and Schoolmen's Post 543, American Legion. Before coming to Kingsborough, Mr. Mascolo spent five years at Borough of Manhattan Community College, holding the posts of Dean of Institutional Services, Dean of Administration and Professor of Business Administration. His career in education began at New York City Community College, where he served as Associate Dean of Administration from 1950-1966. Mr. Mascolo was educated at New York University, where he earned a Bachelor of Science in 1961 and a Master of Arts in 1966. His dedication and professionalism were an inspiration to many members of the Kingsborough community. His warmth, his positive outlook, and his honesty will be greatly missed. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family.
