
        RANGHELLI--Christopher. 43, on June 29, 2006. Beloved son of Joseph and Joan Ranghelli, brother of David and Lisa Ranghelli, brotherin-law to Esther Duran and Winton Pitcoff, and uncle of Miranda, Marcelo, and Noah. A dedicated employee at The Brooklyn Bureau of Community Services. Chris was a sensitive, caring soul, and will be greatly missed by all whose lives he touched.
