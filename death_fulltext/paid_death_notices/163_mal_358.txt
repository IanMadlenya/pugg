
        SALOP-Arnold M., M.D. The Board of Directors, Medical Staff, Administration, hospital staff, friends and community of Phelps Memorial Hospital Center mourns the passing of one of its most distinguished physicians, Dr. Arnold M. Salop. An Internist who specialized in Cardiology, Dr. Salop was a highly skilled and gifted physician. In addition to his clinical practice at Phelps, Dr. Salop served the hospital in many important roles. He was Chief of Electrocardiology and established one of the first comprehensive diagnostic cardiology laboratories in Westchester. Dr. Salop also helped found the Intensive Care Units at Phelps. He served as Director of the Department of Medicine and President of the Medical Staff. He later served as the hospital's Medical Director and after his retirement from clinical practice was elected to the Board of Directors, where he chaired the Membership and Development Committee and cochaired the hospital's successful capital campaign. He was always an advocate of high quality patient care and professional excellence. We extend our deepest condolences to his devoted wife Mary Ellen, his children Andrea, Evan and Holly, and the entire Salop family. Funeral services will be held at Temple Israel of Northern Westchester in Croton-On-Hudson, NY on Thursday, August 31st at 9AM. Following the interment, there will be a reception at the James House on the Phelps campus in Sleepy Hollow, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to The Arnold M. Salop Intensive Care Unit at Phelps Memorial Hospital Center. Henry Hilken, Chairman, Board of Directors Keith F. Safian, President and Chief Executive Officer Kenneth C. Kaplan, M.D., President of the Medical Staff
