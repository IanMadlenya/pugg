
        WIZEMAN-John William (Bill) III, 69, of Hunter, New York, and formerly of Richmond Hill, New York, died Sat., January 2, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Mary Agnes (nee Murray). Beloved father of John William IV, Robert, Elizabeth, Phillip, Stephen, Marguerite, Michael, Matthew, Dennis and James. Loving grandfather of Beth, Caroline, Erin, Rebecca, John William Wizeman V, Kristen, Katherine and Julia. He was a former Vice President of Prudential Securities. Bill was a veteran and had received his B.A. in Economics from Hofstra University. He was loved by all who had the privilege of knowing him. Reposing at the Galante Funeral Home, 2800 Morris Avenue, Union, NJ. Visitation Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass Wednesday 10:30 A.M., at the Holy Spirit R.C. Church, Union, N.J. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery, Kew Gardens, Queens, New York.
