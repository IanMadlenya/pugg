
        RAMER-Marvin M., 81, July 18th in Boca Raton. Beloved husband of Stella, father of Joan and Frances Ramer, Karen Sinnreich, grandfather of Helene, Michael, Kristina and Lauren. A 1940 graduate, he was very active in R.P.I. alumni affairs. He retired as Vice Chairman of the Lummus Company. A memorial service at 1 PM, September 9th, the UN Chapel, 1st Avenue at 44th St. Donations may be made to Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY 12180.
