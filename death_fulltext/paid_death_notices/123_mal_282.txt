
        BABSON-Donald P., of Wellesley, Massachusetts; on April 21, 1997. He was 73. Husband of the late Susan (Averill) Babson. He is survived by 5 children: Averill Babson & her husband Gregory E. Sohns of Darien, CT; Paul Talbot Babson, II of Needham, MA; James A. Babson and his wife Asoka Bandarage of South Hadley, MA; Deborah Babson of Jaffrey, N.H. & Richard Babson of Boston, MA; three granddaughters: Olivia Louise Sohns, Antonia Averill Sohns and Juliet Susan Babson of Darien, CT; and one grandson, Maithri Bandarage Babson of South Hadley, MA. The family request that no flowers be sent. Contributions in his name may be made to the institution or charity that best represents your affiliation with Donald P. Babson. A memorial service in celebration of his life will commence at 7:30PM on Friday, May 30, in Knight Auditorium on the Babson College Campus. Mr. Babson was Chairman of Babson-United Investment Advisors, Inc. as well as Chairman and President of United Business Service. For information call J.S. Waterman and Sons-Waring Funeral Home, Wellesley, Massachusetts (617-235-4110).
