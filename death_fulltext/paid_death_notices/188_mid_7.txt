Loving grandmother of David, Daniel and Fara (David) Berman; great-grandmother of Jacob, Joshua and baby girl Berman. Service Monday, 9:30 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        DESKIN-Claire. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale deeply mourn the death of Claire Deskin, beloved mother of our dear friend, Michael Steinhardt. The Hebrew Home was privileged to have Claire live with us for over three years, where she was a prominent and beloved member of our community. With deep affection and profound sadness, we extend our condolences to Michael, Judy and the entire Steinhardt family. The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Burton P. Resnick, Chairman, Building Committee Daniel Reingold, Executive Vice President
