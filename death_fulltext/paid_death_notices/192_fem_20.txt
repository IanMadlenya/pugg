
        JAINSCHIGG - Janet Gem mell, 94. Of Chester Village West, Chester, CT., former resident of Darien, CT., where she was a leader of historic preservation efforts and Red Cross fundraising activities died December 30. She served as Chairman of Volunteers, Darien Chapter of American Red Cross, and was past president of the Darien Historical Society. Memorial service January 25, 11AM at Emmanuel Episcopal Church in Killingworth, CT. For info The Robinson, Wright & Weymer Funeral Home, 34 Main Street, Centerbrook, CT.
