
        BLAKE--Peter, 86, on December 5th, 2006. Architect, critic, editor, teacher. He is survived by his sister, Madi Lanier, daughter, Christina Oliver, son, Casey Nelson Blake, five grandchildren, six great-grandchildren, one nephew and one niece. A memorial service will be held in the New Year. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made in his memory to the Department of Architecture and Design, MOMA, 11 West 53rd St., New York, NY 10019.
