
        HIGINBOTHAM-Paul. The family and friends of pianist, G. Paul Higinbotham, are deeply saddened by his death on May 10, 1999. Born in 1920 in Victor, NY, Paul received a Bachelor of Music from Oberlin Conservatory of Music in 1949 and toured Europe with the Mozart Trio in the 1950's. Later he was a ballet rehearsal pianist and vocal coach/accompanist in Dallas, Washington, D.C., and NYC. Interment: Boughton Hill Cemetery, Victor, NY. Memorial service in NYC, June 19, 1 P.M., at Rutgers Presbyterian Church.
