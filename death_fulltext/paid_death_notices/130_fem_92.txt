
        LEIBNER-Eleanor. November 13, 1997. Beloved wife of Sol for fiftynine years. Devoted mother of Jerry and Denise Leibner and Richard and Carole Leibner. The most loving and adored grandmother of Adam and his wife Amy, Matthew, Jonathan, David, Elizabeth, Evan and Jordan. Dear sister of Allen and Irving Zelon. Tireless and committed founder, worker and officer for Congregation Beth Shalom of Kings Bay and their sisterhood, officer and board member of the Brooklyn Woman's Division of UJA. She gave of herself to all who knew her. She denied herself but was extraordinarily charitable. She was an unselfish woman with a strong moral code that she always tried to pass along to her children and grandchildren. She will be sorely missed by her family, many friends, and the community. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions in her memory to Brooklyn Woman's Division of UJA. Visitation Saturday evening, 7:309:30pm. Service 11AM on Sunday, November 16, Nieberg Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, (corner Avenue M). For directions call 718-377-2700.
