
        LEESON-Laura. On October 28, 1997. Aunt Laura to three generations of Schrader, Levkov and Kaufman children. Beloved and tolerated for her independence and outspokeness. A woman and artist, much ahead of her time. Still youthful and flirtatious at 99. Things will not be the same without her. Services on Wednesday 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
