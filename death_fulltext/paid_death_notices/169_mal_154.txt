
        JUDSON-Paul. Died February 9, 2001 in Pinehurst, NC. He attended Pascedina Playhouse, had a long and outstanding career both as singer and actor. Toured with Spike Jones Band. Later years he became a teaching golf pro in Manhattan. He sang in many night clubs in the US and he was seen in many television commercials. Survived by daughter Terri Milligan of Oakdale, NY; sister Genevieve Chorba, and two grandchildren. Memorials may be made to a favorite charity. For information Powell in Southern Pines, N.C., call 1-800-765-0630.
