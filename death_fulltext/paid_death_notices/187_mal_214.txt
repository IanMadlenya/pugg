
        SCHER - Robert. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia; adored father of Ellen and Moshe; devoted grandpa to Avi and Ricky; loving brother, uncle and friend to all. Bob was a gentleman & a scholar, a kind, generous, compassionate man. In his quiet, humble way, he taught us how to love life in spite of adversity. A passionate Zionist, he has left a legacy for generations to come. He is at peace now, with his beloved Sylvia. Funeral services 10:15 AM at Beth El Synagogue. Shiva will be observed at the home of his daughter. Contributions to UJA-Federation in Bob's memory would be appreciated.
        SCHER-Robert. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Robert Scher, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Mr. Scher & his late wife, Sylvia, expressed their deep commitment to the Jewish community and other humanitarian causes through their exceptional support of UJAFederation & its beneficiary agencies. His daughter, Ellen, continues her family's legacy of caring for those in need through her active involvement in the Westchester Women's Campaign. We extend our sincerest condolences to Robert's daughter, Ellen, and her husband, Moshe; to his two grandchildren, Avi and Ricky; and to the entire Scher family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & C.E.O.
        SCHER-Robert. We mourn the loss of our beloved president & co-founder after a long and valiant struggle against cancer. A gentle giant of the textile industry, his reputation for honesty and kindness reached across the globe. Every employee became his family. He was a father to us all. We will cherish his memory. He is irreplaceable in our hearts. The Employees of Scher Fabrics, Inc.
