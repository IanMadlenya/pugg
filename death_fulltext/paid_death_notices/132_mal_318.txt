
        SCHNEIDER-William Eitel. 88 Died Friday, January 23, 1998. A native of Bronx, NY, he had resided in Westport, CT for more than 40 years prior to moving to Virginia. Mr. Schneider retired in 1984 as Vice President of Business Affairs for the television and radio departments at Benton & Bowles Advertising Agency in New York, NY. He volunteered at the Carver Center for 10 years as a tutor and mentor. He was preceded in death by his wife, Charlotte M. Schneider. Survivors include two daughters, Erica Porter of Newport News and Debby Schneider of Chicago, Ill; his son Christopher Schneider of Englewood, CO; his sister, August Severin of Bronxville, N.Y.; two grandchildren, Melissa Porter and Jason Rafferty; and numerous nieces and nephews. A private memorial service will be held at a later date in Westport, CT. For information Peninsula Funeral Home, Newport News.
