
        BLUMENKRANTZ-Steven J. 52, of the town of Maryland, NY. Died Tuesday, April 20 of an apparent heart attack. Beloved husband of Carole. Father of Katie. Son of Renee and the late Harris. Brother of David. He practiced law in New York City and Oneonta, NY. He was an Assistant Professor at the State University at Oneonta.
