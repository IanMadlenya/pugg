        DELSENER-Ackley Vincent. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of Ackley Vincent Delsener, beloved father of Ron Delsener. We extend condolences to Ron and his family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Cantor Debra Stein-Davidson Donald Zucker, President
