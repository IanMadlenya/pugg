
        RITTS-Herb. The Bard College community mourns the passing of Herb Ritts, '75, an outstanding alumnus, whose talent, skill, and artistry, as well as his empathy and great personal warmth, led him to the top of his field. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. He will sorely missed. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman
