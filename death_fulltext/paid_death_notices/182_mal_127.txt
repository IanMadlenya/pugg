
        KANNOF-Benjamin. The board and staff of the National Foundation for Jewish Culture record with sorrow the passing of Benjamin Kannof, beloved father of our distinguished board member and chair of the Alan King Award Dinner, Alan Kannof. Condolences to Alan's wife Ellen Umansky, to his sisters Lisa Kannof and Janet Grobard, his brother-in-law Mark Kaiserman and his sons Abraham, Ezra and Seth. May his memory be for a blessing. James August, President Lynn Korda Kroll, Chair Richard A. Siegel, Exec. Dir.
