
        AMKRAUT-William. Age 83, of Delray Beach, FL and Bethpage, NY. Born October 28, 1915, died of natural causes. Survived by his loving wife of 57 years, Harriet Potoker Amkraut, and his brother, Jacob Amkraut; daughter Alynne Amkraut Krull and son-in-law Albert of Chappaqua, NY; grandchildren Jeremy and Kathryn, to whom he was devoted. Worked for United States Weather Service for more than 35 years, when he retired as the Chief Accountant of the Eastern Region. A man of great humor and gentle demeanor, he will be missed by all who knew him and loved him. Funeral, 11 AM, February 5, Bet Torah Synagogue, Mt. Kisco, NY.
