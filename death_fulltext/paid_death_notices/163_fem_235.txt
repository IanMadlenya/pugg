
        SANDQUIST-Fannie W. Widow of Gustave. Sister of the late Arthur W. Wallander (former NYC Police Commissioner). Beloved aunt of Henrietta McFee, Rosalind Renouard, Barbara Spencer and Arthur Wallander, Jr. Died in her 102nd year on August 28 in Lenox Hill Hospital after a short illness. Her indomitable spirit and love of life brought joy and happiness to all she touched. A viewing followed by a religious service will be held on Saturday, Sept. 2 at 11 AM at the Chas. Peter Nagel Funeral Home, 352 East 87 Street (1st and 2nd Avenues), New York City.
