
        DUBOW-Arthur M. Of East Hampton, NY and Patagonia, AZ, on December 3 at age 66. Beloved husband of Barbara Shattuck Dubow and devoted father of Alexandra and Charles and grandfather of William. A graduate of Harvard College and Harvard Law School, Mr. Dubow became a venture capitalist and pursued an active and rewarding career investing in South Korea, as well as in the oil and gas business, the publishing industry and film production. He was a member of the Harvard Club of New York, as well as Boston's Somerset Club and Tavern Club. His great passions were education, international affairs and Bernese Mountain Dogs. He was a man who was loved by many and will be deeply missed.
