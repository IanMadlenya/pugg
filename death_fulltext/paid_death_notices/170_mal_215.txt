
        GOLDSTEIN-Bernard, Esq. Suddenly, on March 17, 2001. Retired tax attorney for the IRS, Adjunct Professor of tax accounting at Pace University and Baruch College. Beloved husband of Pat Goldstein. Dear brother of Sanford R. Goldstein. Services Wednesday, March 21, 2001, 4 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name to ADL.
