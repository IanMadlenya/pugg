
        SONNENKLAR - Norman, M.D. The Medical Board of Elmhurst Hospital Center and the entire medical staff mourn the loss of Norman Sonnenklar, M.D. We express our sincere condolences to his wife, Susan, and sons, Eric and Alan, and their families. Until his retirement, Dr. Sonnenklar was the Director of the Department of Anesthesiology at Elmhurst Hospital Center, and Assistant Dean for Faculty Practice at Mount Sinai School of Medicine; he was also Past President of the Medical Board. During his many years of service at Elmhurst, Dr. Sonnenklar was a dedicated physician to his patients as well as a devoted teacher to his students and residents. After his retirement, Dr. Sonnenklar continued to offer his advice on a regular basis. We will miss our beloved colleague, teacher and friend.
