
        BERTENTHAL-Irving. Age 93. Father of Muriel Weisel, Doris Sklar and husband Samuel, grandchildren and great grandchildren. Beloved by all. Shiva will be observed through Sunday morning March 29th.
        BERTENTHAL-Irving. The Kingsbridge Center of Israel records with sorrow the passing of Irving Bertenthal, a distinguished Founder and President of our synagogue. May his loving family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Harvey Crupar
