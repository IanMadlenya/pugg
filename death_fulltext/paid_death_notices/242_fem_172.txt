
        CALAGNA--Caterina ''Aunt Tina'', died Wednesday March 21, 2007 at her home in NYC. She was born in Corleone, Sicily on February 16, 1909. She is survived by her nephew Tom, his wife Judith and their children Vanessa and Jesse. She was ''Aunt Tina'' to countless others whom she loved and loved her. She was sent by God on a mission to feed all who came her way, her mission was accomplished. Mass will be held at St. Gregory's Church on 90th Street between Amsterdam and Columbus Ave., Monday March 26, at 11am.
