
        DIETZ-Lew, 90, in Rockport, Maine April 26, 1997. Best known as the author along with Harry Goodridge of ''A Seal Called Andre''. Lew wrote twenty books and hundreds of magazine pieces, almost all of them drawn from Maine life in a sixty year writing career. His wife Denny Winters, a noted painter, died in 1985. A memorial service is planned later in the summer. Laite & Pratt Funeral Home, Camden, Maine 1-800494-3365.
