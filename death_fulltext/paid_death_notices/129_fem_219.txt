
        FEINBERG-Charlotte. Age 82. Died October 26 after suffering from Alzheimer's Disease for several years. She lived for many years in Des Moines, Chicago, New York City and Florida. She is survived by her devoted husband Milton, loving daughters Susan Farber Ganz and Linda Graev and three grandchildren Adam Graev, Nicole Graev and Samantha Ganz Panzier. Her love and caring will always be missed. She has found the peace she deserved. Funeral services will be held at 10AM Tuesday at Lillis Funeral Home, 58 Bridge Street, New Milford, CT.
