
        MOSCOVICI--Willy G., M.D. On October 18, 2005. We mourn the death of Dr. Willy Moscovici an outstanding physician, an innovator in his field and fully dedicated to his patients. He was loved by his family, friends, and his patients. He will be missed by all. He leaves behind his wife Elisa, daughter Rodica and her husband Sergio, granddaughters, Alexandra, Karen, and Andrea, and their husbands, Graham, Jeff, and Sal, and three great-grandchildren. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell - the Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, (81st Street), Thursday 11:30 A.M.
