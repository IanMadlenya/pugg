
        GOLDBERG-Paul, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Paul Goldberg. Dr Goldberg was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Urology for many years. He also did his internship and residency at Maimonides. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. S. Farber, M.D., Pres. H. Lehman, M.D., Pres. Elect. I. Mayer, M.D., Secy. W. Wexelman, M.D., Treas.
