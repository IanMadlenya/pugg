
        STURGIS--Nancy Dahn. Born July 7, 1924, passed away at age 81 on Thursday, December 15, 2005 in Manhattan where she had lived her entire life. She leaves behind two daughters, Lisa Sturgis of Stamford, CT and Victoria Sturgis Neely of Manhattan, two grandchildren, Henry and Carolina Neely of Manhattan, and her sister Barbara Dahn of Pasadena, CA.
