
        SILVERSTEIN--Joseph. Died August 29 at the age of 93 in Jupiter, Florida. He was one of Wall Street's greatest arbitrageurs, most prominently with Ladenburg, Thallman & Co. Joe and his previously deceased wife, Bea, formerly lived in Harrison, NY and in Palm Beach, Florida. He was a loving husband, father, nephew, cousin and friend. A great golfer and a fine person. He is survived by his only son, Mark, of Lee, New Hampshire and numerous cousins, and will be remembered for a long and good life. May he rest in peace. The Leet and Hamburg Families
