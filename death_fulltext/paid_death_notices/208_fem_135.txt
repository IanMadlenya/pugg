
        STRAUSS--Lillian B. Of North Haven, CT, on May 16, 2004, at the young age of 99. Devoted wife of the late Isadore G. Strauss, she was also the much loved and loving mother of Glenna Brody, grandmother of Ira, great-grandmother of Annabelle, Caroline, Samuel and William Brody, all of New York, and sister of Samuel Simons, of New Haven. Daughter of the late Louis and Anna Simons, and sister of the late Ruth Prager and Nathan Simons. Graveside services Monday, 3 pm, at Mishkan Israel Cemetery, Whalley Ave, in New Haven, CT.
