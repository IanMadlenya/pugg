
        HELLINGER-Jim. On December 27, 1999, at age 84. Husband of Eleanor; father of Doug, Steve, John, and Bob; grandfather of Danny, Sam, Abigail, and Laura. Retired President of Anglo-African Shipping Co. of N.Y.; Harvard Law School Class of 1937; CCNY Class of 1934; Townsend Harris Class of 1930. He was one of a kind, and will be sorely missed. Please consider donations to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, New York 11590.
