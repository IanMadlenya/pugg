
        TARLOW-William F., 87, formerly of Roslyn, NY, at home, Sunday, in Somers, NY, following a long illness. Husband of Glenys, father of Reuven, Lawrence, Lee Miller, and the late Andrew; grandfather of Jeffrey, Rachel, Henry, Yosef Yitzhak, Chana, & Shneur Zalman; brother of Jerome, Elayne, & the late Gertrude. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice Care of Westchester and Putnam, Inc., 100 South Bedford Road, Mount Kisco, New York 10549.
