
        SCOTT-Dr. Paul H. On August 4, 1920-April 10th, 1999. Devoted husband of the late Dr. Dorothy Gregg; cherished father of Kim Alzerreca and Gregg Scott; loving grandfather of Xoli Redmond and Sophia Alzerreca. Compassionate dentist, outstanding sportsman and a heck of a dancer. Longtime mover and shaker at the New York Athletic Club, Chairman of Boxing, Chairman of Personal Defense and Chairman of Ballroom Dance. He will be missed by countless people whose lives he touched. Memorial service to be held at the New York Athletic Club, Tuesday June 1st, at 6PM.
