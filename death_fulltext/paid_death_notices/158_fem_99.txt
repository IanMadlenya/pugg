
        MORRELL-Joan Vaughan, on March 7, 2000. Wife of James F. Morrell, M.D. of Blue Bell, PA. She was born in Brooklyn, graduated from Erasmus Hall and the College of New Rochelle. Long time resident of Garden City. Survived by son James G., of Garden City, daughters Margot of New York City, Alison Thompson of Fort Washington, PA and Jeanne Harrell of Mt. Plea sant, SC and six grandchildren. There will be a memorial service at the Nassau Academy of Medicine 1200 Stewart Avenue, Garden City, NY at 1:00 PM on Saturday, April 8, 2000. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations to the College of New Rochelle, New Rochelle, NY 10805, to the Interfaith Nutrition Network, 148 Front Street, Hempstead, NY 11550, or to a charity of your choice.
