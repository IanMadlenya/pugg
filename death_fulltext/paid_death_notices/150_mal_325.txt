
        COHEN-Eugene J., M.D. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of New York Presbyterian Hospital and Weill Medical College of Cornell University are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Dr. Eugene J. Cohen. A leader in the field of endocrinology, Dr. Cohen's distinguished career spanned over 60 years. His commitment and dedication resulted in advances in medical research, education and patient care. We join with all those who knew him in mourning his loss and extend our deepest condolences to his family. Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Stephen & Suzanne Weiss Dean Weill Medical College of Cornell University David B. Skinner, M.D. Vice Chairman, President & CEO New York Presbyterian Hospital
        COHEN-Milton, MD. Died on July 19, 1999. Devoted husband of Francine. Beloved father of Paul and Lisa. Cherished grandfather of Brian, Jacob and Hillary. Loving brother of Elaine and Raymond. Services Wednesday, 10:00 am at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
