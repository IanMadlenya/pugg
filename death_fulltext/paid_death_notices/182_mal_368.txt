
        KELLY-Dr. Chester Scott. Died Friday, March 22, 2002, Louisville, KY. He was 70 years old. Born in Hazard, KY, son of Chester and Rachel Kelly. Graduated Phi Beta Kappa and valedictorian of Hampden-Sydney College, class of 1953. Served as Lieutenant in US Navy, earned MA in International Relations at the University of Pennsylvania and a PhD from Teachers College of Columbia University. Fulbright Scholar in Paris 1957-59. He co-directed the first Peace Corps Training Program for Liberia. Served as education coordinator in Lagos, Nigeria. While at Columbia he organized and recruited 40 to 150 teachers per year for East African schools and universities. Employed as an Africanist and senior political risk analyst for Mobil Oil. He is survived by his sister, Kitty Galloway of Louisville, KY, her children Dayna Payton, Louisville and Britton Galloway, New Orleans. A graveside service was in Lancaster, KY on Monday, March 25, 2002. Memorial gifts to Broadway Baptist Church, 4000 Brownsboro Road, Louisville, KY 40207, or charity of choice.
