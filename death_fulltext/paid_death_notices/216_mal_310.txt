
        THURSTON--Paul William, 84, longtime resident of New York City, died on January 16, 2005 in Lebanon, NJ due to complications from Alzheimer's disease. He was raised in Millburn, NJ, the son of the late Elbert Wren Thurston and Myrtle Thurston Snelling (nee Farrell). He graduated from Michigan State University and served as a lieutenant in the US Navy during WWII. He had a long career in the Public Relations field. Predeceased by his brother, Elbert Thurston, and sister-inlaw, Adele Smith Thurston, Paul is survived by his niece, Elizabeth Thurston Hogan of Hampton, NJ, nephews, James Thurston of Condon, MT and Robert Thurston of Plainfield, NJ, and grand nieces and nephews. Funeral services private. God bless!
