
        LAREDO--Ruth. Manhattan School of Music deeply mourns the passing of Ruth Laredo, a distinguished member of the piano faculty since 1992. Her artistry, intellect, energy and compassion have had an enormous impact on generations of pianists. As a colleague and friend she will be sorely missed by all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her daughter Jennifer and to her family. David A. Rahm, Chairman Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
