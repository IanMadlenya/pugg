
        SCHMIED--Ernest. 80, of Woodside, NY, died January 25, of heart failure in Puerto Rico. His loving wife Marian and son Robert will miss him deeply. He was born in Vienna, Austria. As an electrical and consulting engineer, he designed and directed the power installations of many notable structures in the U.S. and abroad, including Lincoln Center's Met. He was an enlightened man whose deep passion for justice for the underprivileged will burn forever in our hearts and minds.
