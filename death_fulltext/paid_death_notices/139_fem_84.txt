
        COX-Oden McKay, 73. Of Weekapaug, RI & Short Hills, New Jersey. Died Friday, Aug. 7 at the Westerly Hospital, Westerly, R.I. She was the widow of the late Joseph E. Cox, Jr. She is survived by her son Ned Cox of London and her daughter Deni Cox Catullo of Weekapaug as well as 2 grandsons Roy & Christopher Catullo of Weekapaug. A Memorial Service will be held in the Weekapaug Chapel on August 18th at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Weekapaug Foundation for Conservation, The Weekapaug Chapel Society, Christ Church of Short Hills, N.J. or New Eyes for the Needy, Millburn, N.J.
