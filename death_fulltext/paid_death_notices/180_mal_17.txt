
        HETTENA-Ran. Died December 30th, 2001 of cancer at age 78 in New York City. President of Maritime Overseas Corporation, Director of Overseas Shipholding Group of New York. Former Chairman of GARD Insurance of Norway. Beloved husband of Shirley; devoted father of Avi and Seth; loving grandfather of Joshua, Daniel, David, Alexandra and Gabrielle; adoring brother of Dina Recanati. Burial services were held privately. A memorial service will take place at noon on January 30th, 2002 at Temple Israel, 112 East 75th Street in NYC.
        HETTENA-Ran. The Board of Directors, Senior Management and Staff of Overseas Shipholding Group, Inc., express their deep sadness upon the death of Ran Hettena, a Director and guiding spirit of our Company since its inception. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Shirley, their sons, Avi and Seth, to his sister, Diane Recanati and to their families and all who loved him. Ran Hettena was a key figure in the growth and success of OSG. We were privileged to have the benefit of his keen intellect and sound judgement, as well as his warm friendship and strength of character. Throughout his career, Ran's expertise in the bulk shipping business was widely recognized in governmental and industry circles both within the U.S. and the international shipping communities, and his views were frequently sought on critical issues. We will sorely miss him. Overseas Shipholding Group, Inc Morton P. Hyman Chairman and Chief Exec. Officer
