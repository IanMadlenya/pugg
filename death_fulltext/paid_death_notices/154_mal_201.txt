
        MUNSON-Nicholas, NVVF. Born April 19, 1911 in Williamsport, PA, passed away Monday November 15, 1999. Yale Graduate Class of 1933 and veteran of World War II. Predeceased by his wife, Helen, in 1991, and one of his four sons, Edgar Munson, in 1997. Survived by sons Nicholas, William, and Thomas, daughter Monie Munson, and Sheilah Taylor, also survived by numerous grandchildren and great grandchildren. Memorial service Saturday, November 20, at 11 A.M. at The First Presbyterian Church in Allegany, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to The First Presbyterian Church or The Adirondack Presbyterian Church, Keese Mills Road, Paul Smith, New York 12970.
