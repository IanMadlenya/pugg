
        HEINEMAN-Ruth (Kress). Age 75. She passed away peacefully in her sleep at home after a long illness born with courage and dignity. She is survived by her loving husband Bernard (Jack) Heineman, Jr. Her devoted children Deborah Heineman (Scaglione) and Ralph Scaglione, Matthew and Katherine (Phillips) Heineman and Stephen and Katherine (Lingg) Heineman and by her adoring grandchildren Joshua, Benjamin, Nicholas, Emily, Tyler and Scott. She is also survived by her brothers George and Kenneth and her sister Nancy Armstrong and many nieces and nephews. She will be greatly missed by her brothers-in-law William Heineman, and his family, and Andrew Heineman. The family will receive friends at The Frank E. Campbell Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Sunday March 8 from 6-9 PM and on Monday March 9 from 6-9 PM. The funeral service will be held Tuesday, March 10 at 4 PM at The First Presbyterian Church, 12th St and 5th Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The College of Wooster, Wooster, OH, The Public Education Association, 39 W. 32nd Street, New York City or the charity of your choice.
