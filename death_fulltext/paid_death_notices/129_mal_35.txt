
        MIHAI-Eugen, Fr., 84, of St. Mary's Romanian Byzantine Rite Catholic Mission, Astoria, New York. A veteran of W.W. II and 12 years of communist prisons, at age 75 he risked jail by returning to Romania to be ordained, May 18, 1988 in the still banned church. He served well, was loved by all and will be missed.
