
        O'SULLIVAN-John J. Age 60, on September 1st. He received his Ph.D. in American History from Columbia University and has taught at Florida Atlantic University in Boca Raton, FL, since 1971. His most recent book was ''We Have Just Begun to Not Fight,'' about conscientious objectors during WWII. He is survived by his loving wife Marjorie, his treasured children John and wife Josefina, Michael and wife Allison, Shelagh, and Daniel; cherished grandchildren John Joseph, Isabel, Lacey, and Bailey; and his niece and nephew Patty Mele and Jimmy Keogh. Donations may be made to Miami Children's Hospital Foundation (Hugs & Kisses), attn Lily Mirete, 3000 S.W. 67th Ave, Miami, FL 33155.
