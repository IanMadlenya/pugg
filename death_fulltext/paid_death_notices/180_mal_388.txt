
        RODRIGUEZ-Harold J. Age 81, January 26, 2002. Beloved husband of Joan. Devoted father of Pam, Lee, Hal, Joanne and Nancy. Cherished grandfather of Kate, Colin, Christine and Laura Stirrat, Rebecca, Ashley and Tracy Schneider, Christopher and Anne Rodriguez and Alexander and Brian Black. A distinguished executive in the shipping industry for over 50 years, who was President of Rodriguez Sons Company, Inc. He will be greatly missed by family, friends, colleagues and business associates and will always be in our hearts. Contributions in his memory should be sent to Bridgeport Hospital, 267 Grant St, Bridgeport, CT 06610. A funeral mass will be held Wednesday, 11 AM, at St. Michael's Church, 469 North St., Greenwich, CT 06830. Burial will be at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
