
        HERR-Kate Pritchett. Died peacefully June 29, 2002, in Houston, TX, at age 98. A friend, a loving mother, and family matriarch, we will miss her. Son, George Pritchett Herr, and daughter-in-law, Eleanor A. Herr, of Playa del Rey, CA, and four generations survive her. Memorial service at University Place Retirement Community, Houston, TX, July 13, 2002. Donations to Christ United Methodist Church.
