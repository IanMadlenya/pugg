
        LAURO-Sophie M. On Sunday April 19th. Cherished sister of Dr. Louis Lauro, the late Raphael Lauro, Sebastian Lauro and Helen Lauro Greco. Beloved Aunt Sophie of her nieces, nephews, and especially Vicki. Honorably discharged as First Sergeant after serving her country in W.W. II in the United States Womens Army Corps (1943-1953). Department Manager at the Navy Exchange, Naples, Italy until retirement to New York in 1986. Services at A. Ribustello & Son, 811 Morris Park Avenue, Bronx, New York, 11 A.M. on Thursday, April 23rd. We will remember your kindness and generosity, Sophie, and keep you in our hearts forever.
