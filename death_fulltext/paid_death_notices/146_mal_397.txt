
        EISENBUD-Jule. M.D.. 90. Died on March 10th, 1999. A native New Yorker, Dr. Eisenbud attended Columbia University and The New York Psychoanalytic Institute. He moved to Denver in 1950, became its first psychoanalyist, and taught at the University of Colorado Medical Center. His wife Molly died in 1995. He is survived by three children, five grandchildren, and a great-grandson.
