
        KAY-Ina. Hadassah The Womens Zionist Organization of American Inc mourns the passing of Ina Kay. Beloved wife of Jack, devoted mother of Shelley Kay and Lauren and Richard Pollin, and loving grandmother of Sander and Kennis Haskins, Alyssa Perper and Julie Zitin. As a Guardian of Tomorrow, Ina was a generous benefactor of Hadassah. She was devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her legacy will live on through the Ina and Jack Kay Hospice and the visionary projects she supported at the Hadassah medical organization. May her memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, President
        KAY-Ina. The Board of Governors and members of the Falls Country Club, Lake Worth, FL, acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Ina Kay. We extend our deepest sympathy to her beloved husband, Jack and their family. Elliott Solomon, President
