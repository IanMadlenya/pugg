
        KURTZ--Norman. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Norman Kurtz, beloved husband of Honey Kurtz, a cherished leader of the New York Jewish community whose generosity and dedication strengthens our ability to care for those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Honey, to her daughters, Lori and Stephanie; and her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
