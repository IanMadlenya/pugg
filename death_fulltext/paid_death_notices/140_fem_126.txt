
        BERNSTEIN-Dr. Eva (Lichtenfeld). Sept 17, 1998. Survived by her nephews, Dr. Peter Lichtenfeld of Great Barrington, MA, Yoram Sadeh-Lichtenfeld of Israel; niece, Hanna Dubin of Westchester, NY; Sister-In-Law Leonia Field of NYC; several great nieces & nephews. Wife of the late Werner Bernstein. Dr. Bernstein was one of the first women Dentists in the U.S. and a supporter of the Arts. A memorial service and interment were held in Rochester, NY.
