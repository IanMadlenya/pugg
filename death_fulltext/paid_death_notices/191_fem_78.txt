
        BASSETT-Helen R., 71. Died December 5, 2002 in Kingston, NY. Formerly of Manhattan and New Rochelle, she taught English at New Rochelle High School and Isaac Young Junior High School. Sister of Peg Tears and Suzanne Campbell. A celebration of her life will be conducted on her birthday in the spring. For info contact Simpson-Gaus Funeral Home, Kingston, NY. Memorials to the Lance Armstrong Foundation, P.O. Box 161150, Austin, TX. 78716, or The Crawford Lynbrook Animal Hospital, 690 Merrick Rd., Lynbrook, NY 11563.
