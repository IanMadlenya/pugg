
        REHFUSS-Amy C., 86. Died Saturday, September 30 in Concord, NH. She taught in the bible department at the Masters School, Dobbs Ferry, NY from 1941 to 1967. From 1967 to 1980 she was the academic dean at Ethel Walker School, Simsbury, CT. She was on the Board of Directors of the Northfield League. There are no immediate survivors. A memorial service will be held at The First Church of Christ Congregational in Farmington, CT at a later date. Memorial donations may be made to the scholarship fund at the Masters School, Dobbs Ferry, NY 10522 or to the Ethel Walker School, Simsbury, CT 06070. For info Bennett Funeral Home, Concord, N.H.
