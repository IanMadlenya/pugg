
        KANTERMAN-Edgar Allen. Devoted and loving husband of Phyllis. Dear brother-in-law of Jerry Schwartz. Loving uncle of Anne Levitan and Fran Wheeler. Also survived by four great-nieces and nephews. He was a greatly admired dentist in Bayside, Queens, since 1949. Graveside services Sunday 11:30 A.M., at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York.
