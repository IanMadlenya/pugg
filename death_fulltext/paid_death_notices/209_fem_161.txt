
        BERNARDO--Lillian (Brown), 88, on June 21, 2004. Deeply loved wife of Joseph, mother of Steven (Leslie), Sheldon (Carol), grandmother of Ian, sister of Dora Weinberg, sister-in-law, aunt, friend. Lil embraced life with open arms. She lived each moment to the fullest--whether as teacher to thousands of children, reader and leader of a book club, traveler, playgoer, charity worker for OTR. She was a generous, shining presence for all privileged to know her. Funeral Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave., Yonkers, Wednesday 10:00AM. Shiva at the home of Steven and Leslie Bernardo from Wednesday 2PM to Friday 6PM.
