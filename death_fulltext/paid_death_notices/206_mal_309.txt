
        TACKEL--Julius Jack on March 22, 2004. Beloved husband of Muriel. Loving and devoted father of Phyllis and Martin (Abbe) Tackel. Loving grandfather of David. Loved and admired by all who knew him. Services Wednesday, March 24, 12 noon, Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave., Yonkers, NY
