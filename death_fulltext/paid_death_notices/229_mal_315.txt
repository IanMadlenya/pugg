
        KOHLOFF--Roland L. 71. On February 24, 2006. Beloved husband of Janet (nee Unger), loving father to Jami (Mark) Grassi and Steven, grandfather of Cassandra and Gianna, brother of Cindi and George. For 32 years, he was principal timpanist in the New York Philharmonic, and 16 years prior as Principal timpanist San Franciso Symphony. A professor at Juilliard School, many of his students have become accomplished and famous timpanists throughout the musical community. Roland was a stong advocate for the mentally ill. Funeral 11 am, Thursday, Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, Dobbs Ferry (Saw Mill Exit 17). In lieu of flowers, memorials to NAMI, www.nami.org.
