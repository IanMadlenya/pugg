
        BARNES-Frank P. Husband of Dorothy. Son of the late Doris Plummer Barnes, former Senator, U.S. Territory, Alaska, and the late Frank Scott Barnes, proprietor Salmon Canneries in Alaska, passed away February 21, 1997. Father of Margaret A. Barnes of NYC. He was a graduate of Stanford University School of Engineering. He was a resident of NYC for the past 34 years. Mr. Barnes was a senior vice president of telecommunications for ITT of New York and retired in 1979. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
