
        MILANO-Robert J., who passed away January 29 at the age of 87 was a self-made man with a passion for public service and educational opportunity for all Americans. Kind, generous, canny and tireless, he was a rare human being who inspired and energized every organization he served. The members of the Board of Trustees of New School University convey their heartfelt condolences to his wife, Sidonia, and son, Robert Scott. A memorial service will take place a New School University in March. We have lost a priceless friend. John L. Tishman, Chairman of the Board New School University Philip Scaturro, Chancellor Edward J. Blakely, Dean Robert J. Milano Graduate School of Management and Urban Policy
