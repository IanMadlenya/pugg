
        BAUM-Jess, 74. Of Lake Worth, Florida, passed away August 7, 2000. Born in Brooklyn, NY, Mr. Baum graduated from New York University ''Summa Cum Laude''. He had served in the United States Armed Forces as Master Sargeant, serving his country in WWII. He had later worked in sales in the fashion industry and coaching little league. He was known as ''the greatest'' by all of his family and friends, who will miss him dearly. Mr. Baum is survived by his wife of 50 years, Lila Koreff Baum, and his son, Jonathan, also of Lake Worth, Florida. The funeral services will be in private. The funeral services are entrusted to Dorsey Funeral Home, 3525 South Congress Avenue, Lake Worth, Florida 33461.
