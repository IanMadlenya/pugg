
        ROTHENBERG-Aaron. 85. Of Bal Harbour, FL died April 25, 2000. He founded Sylvette Shops in 1935 together with his wife Sylvia. Born on the Lower East Side he lived in Jersey City before retiring to Florida. Surviving are his sister Rose Feldman, 2 daughters Lois Weiss of Tenafly, NJ, Trisha Roth of Beverly Hills, CA, a son Kenneth of Tenafly, 11 grandchildren and 5 great grandchildren. Memorial services 3 PM Sunday, April 30, 2000, at Wien & Wien Memorial Chapel, 129 Engel St., Englewood, NJ. For information: RiversideGordon, 800-759-1151.
