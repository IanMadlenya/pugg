
        GREGORY-Julian A. Of Wilton, Connecticut died September 16. He was a graduate of Princeton University, Class of 1935 and University of Michigan Law School, Class of 1938. Son of the late Julian A. Gregory and Virginia Evans Gregory, he is survived by his sister, Virginia Perry, his wife Margaret, son Julian A. Gregory, Jr., daughter-in-law Molly, and five grandchildren. He was a former Selectman of the Town of Wilton and founding partner of the New York City and Wilton law firm of Gregory and Adams. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, September 19 at 2:00 P.M. at the Wilton Congregational Church, Route 33, Wilton, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be sent to Princeton University or the Wilton Library Building Fund.
