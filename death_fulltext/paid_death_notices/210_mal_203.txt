
        DILLON--Milton, M.D., 90 years old, died on July 20, 2004. Devoted husband of Celia (nee Marcus). Beloved father of Robert and Phyllis and Donald and Louise. Cherished grandfather of Alexander, Samuel and Hannah. Respected OB/GYN, Navy Lt. in WWII. Loved and honored by those who knew him. Memorial services Friday 11AM, at ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
