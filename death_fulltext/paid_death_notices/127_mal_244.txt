
        CONNORS-Francis X. Died on August 22, 1997, at Hilton Head Island, SC. He is survived by his devoted wife Mary Twigg Connors, his loving daughter Janet C. Parry of Summit, NJ, and two grandchildren Thomas and Clare Parry. His first wife Janet Murphy Connors died in 1960. He practiced law in NYC for 40 years, specializing in trademark law. He was a member of the Order of the Holy Sepulchre. A funeral mass will be celebrated on Monday, August 25, at 11 AM, at Holy Family Catholic Church, Hilton Head Island, SC. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Renovation Fund at Holy Family Church, 24 Pope Ave, Hilton Head Island, SC 29928. For information contact the Island Funeral Home, Hilton Head Island.
