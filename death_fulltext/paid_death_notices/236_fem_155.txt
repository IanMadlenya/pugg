        $(6$)SCHER--Pearl Crystal. Born February 24, 1915 in Brooklyn. Pearl gave of herself wholeheartedly as a teacher, assistant principal, and principal in the New York City school system, as a marine during World War II, and, in retirement, as a politician and activist. She served on the town board in Somers, New York, and--after moving to Battery Park City at the age of 85--on Community Board 1 in lower Manhattan. Pearl leaves behind many who admire and love her for her passion, her spirit, and her inspiring example. Donations should be made to Americans United for Separation of Church and State. Services Friday 9:30am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
