
        DEUTSCH-Dr. Alex. On November 24, at the age of 84 he passed peacefully in his sleep. Beloved husband of the late Terry (nee Crystal), devoted and beloved father to Beth, Stella, Les, Philip, and adored grandfather of Jon, David, and Jason. He is survived by his sisters Lee and Anne. He loved life, his family and gave his wonderful sense of humor to all he touched. His inner strength was an inspiration to many. He will be missed. Memorial service will be held at New Montefiore Cemetery on Sunday, November 30 at 11:30 A.M.
