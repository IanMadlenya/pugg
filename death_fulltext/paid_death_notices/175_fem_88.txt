
        STEINMAN-Lillian May. Age 91. Died Friday, August 10, 2001. Graduate from New Rochelle High School in 1925, as valedictorian at age 15. Yearbook quote ''Her working ability has given her fame, though she is a scholar, she's a sport just the same.'' She later attended Wellesley and Barnard colleges. Loving wife of the late Theodore C. Steinman. Devoted mother to Paul (Lynn) and Charles Steinman. Grandmother to Nancy Steinman Banz and Jill A. Dyche. Great-grandmother to Sedona Steinman Banz. A graveside service will be held Monday, August 13, 2001, at 11 AM, at the Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Barnard College Annual Fund, 3009 Broadway, NYC 10027-9960.
