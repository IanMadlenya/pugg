
        FULLER-Emily Clarkson Hurry. Age 89 passed away quietly after a long illness, on January 30, 2000 at home in Oyster Bay, New York. Beloved wife of the late Stephen Dow Fuller, devoted mother of Emily Fuller Kingston of New York City, Ann B. Fuller of Big Horn, Wyoming, and Stephen W. Fuller of Joplin, Missouri. Six grandchildren, Samuel Kingston, Sage and Aeron Daly, Cary, Jane and Emily Fuller also survived her. She attended Skidmore College, was a member of the National Society of Colonial Dames, The Descendants of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence, The Daughters of the Cincinnati, and for many years, a member of the Colony Club of New York. The funeral service will be Thursday, February 3 at Christ Episcopal Church, East Main Street, Oyster Bay, New York. Friends may call at the Oyster Bay Funeral Home, 261 South Street, Oyster Bay. For hours call 516-922-7442. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of Mrs. Fuller may be sent to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, NY 11590.
