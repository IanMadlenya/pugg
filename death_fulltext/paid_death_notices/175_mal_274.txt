
        BANDLER-Richard V., 85. On August 25, 2001, in Boston, MA. Formerly of Riverdale, NY. Retired Associate Regional Administrator, U.S. Securities & Exchange Commission, New York, and an associate of Fried, Frank, Harris, Shriver, and Jacobson. Active lay leader in Jewish education. Devoted husband of the late Jeannette (Finkelstein). Beloved father of Michael & Leah Bandler of Silver Spring, MD, and Naomi & Michael Nadelberg of Newton, MA. Cherished grandfather of Jonathan, Jeremy, Daniel, Sara, Ari, Amanda, Oshi, Brianna, and Stacy. Dear brother of the late Samson. Graveside services were held Monday in New Jersey. Shiva will be observed at his children's homes. Donations in Richard's memory may be made to the Richard V. Bandler Education Fund of the Board of Jewish Education, 426 West 58th Street, New York, NY 10019, or to the Alumni Association Fund of New York University Law School.
        BANDLER-Richard. The Officers and Staff of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York mourn the passing of Mr. Richard Bandler, who served with distinction on our Board of Directors for more than a quarter century. May the Almighty console the Bandler family among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Avrom I. Doft, President Chaim Lauer, Exec VP Philip Schatten, Board Chair
