
        PEARSE-Robert Gardner, 86. Of Delray Beach, Fla., formerly of Rumson, N.J., and St. Croix, USVI, died Monday, March 22nd, 1999 in Delray Beach. Mr. Pearse's wife of 58 years, Virginia Rogers Pearse, died in 1997. He is survived by three daughters, Penelope P. Houghton of Rumson and Delray Beach, Stephanie P. Hurtt of Navesink, and Roberta Pearse-Drance of Pennington, NJ, and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. George's-by-the-River, Rumson, on Thursday, April 1, at 11 AM. Contributions may be made to the Oncological Nursing Continuing Education Scholarship Fund at Riverview Medical Center, which was established in memory of Mrs. Pearse, at Riverview Foundation, One Riverview Plaza, Red Bank, New Jersey 07701.
