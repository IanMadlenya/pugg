
        MOSENKIS -- Matthew, 94, died June 28. Jewish educator with Board of Jewish Education of N.Y.C. Marshalliah, Herzliah Teachers Institute, Histadrut Ivrit, an editor of Shviley Hachinuch. Survived by his wife Rebekah, sons Robert Yehudah (Naida), Daniel (Sharon), grandchildren and great grandchildren. Funeral today, 12 noon, at Weinstein - Garlick - Kirschen baum, 1153 Coney Island Avenue at Ave. H, Brooklyn.
