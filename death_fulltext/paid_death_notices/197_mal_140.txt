
        SKLAR--Leo J., M.D. Died peacefully with devoted family at his side on June 12, 2003. Beloved husband and best friend of Rita Gruber Sklar. Loving father of Susan and Fred Drath, Peter and Joyce, and Patty and Steven Greenstein. Cherished Poppi of Caroline and David Perel, Matthew and Rachel, Vanessa, Gabrielle, Keith and Michele. Graduate of the University of Maryland and Georgetown Medical School. A Major in the U.S. Army Medical Corp. during WWII in the Pacific Theatre. Received Bronze Star with clusters and other honors. Practiced internal medicine for forty years in Lawrence, N.Y. Leo will be remembered as a gentle and kind man. He was loved, respected and admired by all who knew him, personally and professionally. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. 11:30AM Friday.
