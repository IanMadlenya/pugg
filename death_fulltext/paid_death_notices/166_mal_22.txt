
        FEINER-Benjamin. Of Malverne, NY and Delray Beach, FL, passed away on Thursday, November 2, 2000. Devoted husband of Jeanne for 69 years, beloved father of Irwin and Fred. His loving memory lives in our dreams and lives. We will miss him more with each passing day. Services Gutterman's, Rockville Centre, Friday at noon.
