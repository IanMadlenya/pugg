
        NEUBAUER--Harold Bradlee. Died peacefully April 20, 2005. Beloved uncle, brother and friend. He is survived by his sisters Jane Gay Neubauer of Providence, RI, and Brenda Neubauer Straus of New York City and Palm Beach Gardens, FL, and by his many loving nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. His generosity and humor will be missed by all who knew and loved him.
