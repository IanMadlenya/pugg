
        BLICK--Roslyn. On March 12, 2005. Passed away peacefully at home. Adoring and beloved mother of Marcy and Roberta. A wonderful woman born in the Bronx where she lived a full and happy life. Devoted employee of Columbia University Medical Center for over 35 years. She will be dearly missed and never forgotten. In lieu of flowers a contribution to a charity of your choice would be appreciated. Services Monday, March 14, at 11 am, at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
