
        EIDELMAN-Yosef Dr. The Faculty, Residents and Staff at the Institute of Reconstructive Plastic Surgery at the NYU Medical Center wish to express their sincere sorrow at the passing of their friend and colleague Yosef Eidelman, M.D. On his death at age 36 years on May 30th, he was near the completion of his extensive training in general surgery, plastic surgery and microsurgery. He was a devoted father, son, and brother, a generous friend and diligent and gifted surgeon. His presence will be missed by his many friends at the Institute. We would like to express our sincerest condolences to his entire family.
