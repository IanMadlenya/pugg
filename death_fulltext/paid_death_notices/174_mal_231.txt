
        BRENNAN-Frances M., 80. Fanny died at New York Weill Cornell Center on July 22, 2001. Wife of the late Hank Brennan. Survived by son Christopher and his wife Ellen of Feasterville, PA, son Richard and his wife Elizabeth of New York City, and sister Lee M. Wrenn of Orcas Island, WA. The family wishes to extend its profound gratitude to Fanny's friends, physicians, nurses and attendants for their love and care. Funeral services will be private. A memorial service will be planned at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The American Cancer Society, 19 W. 56th St, New York, NY 10019-3984.
