
        BRENGLE-William C. The Board of Trustees, Headmaster, Faculty an Staff of East Woods School mourn the passing of our former Headmaster William C. Brengle, who enthusiastically served the school from 1966 to 1973. His wisdom and dedication to East Woods will not be forgotten. We extend our deepest sympathy to Mrs. Brengle and his entire family. Howard B. Grace, President James E. Ferrer, Headmaster
