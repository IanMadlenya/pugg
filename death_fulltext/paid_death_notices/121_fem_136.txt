
        BAKER-Helen E., 64, of Bethany, PA, on February 21, 1997 at her home. Survived by husband, Alexander J. Baker, son, Alexander J. Baker of Philadelphia, daughters, Cecilia Brennan of Westwood, NJ and Cathlin Baker of NYC. Employed with Lucent Technologies, she received a B.A. from Manhattanville College, an M.A. from NYU and a Ph.D. from Rutgers University. She was a harpsichordist and pianist and a music professor. Memorial service at 2 PM on February 23 in Bethany, PA. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Riverside Church/Emancipation from Poverty Program, 490 Riverside Drive, NY, NY 10027.
