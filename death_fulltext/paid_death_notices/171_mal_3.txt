
        BRUNO-Howard. 86. Of Scarsdale, NY. Formerly of New Rochelle, NY. Died peacefully on Thursday, March 29th at his winter home in Palm Beach Gardens, FL after a long illness. Loving husband for 54 years to Margery (Doernberg) Bruno. Born June 4, 1914 in New York son of Edwin and Hettie (Ollendorf) Bruno. He was predeceased by his daughter Barbara Ann Bruno and his brother William Seligman Bruno. He is survived by his wife Margery, his two sons and their wives David Edwin Bruno and Susan Lipper and Robert Howard Bruno and Susan (Dostie) Bruno and his sister Dorothy Peisinger. He was a long standing member of The Old Guard of White Plains. Funeral services will be held at the Chapel in the Woods, Congregation Kol Ami, Soundview Avenue, White Plains, NY, Monday April 2 at 1 PM. Memorial contributions to hospice or charity of your choice.
