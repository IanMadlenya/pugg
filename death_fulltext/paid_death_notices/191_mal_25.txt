
        LEWISTON-Robert S. At age 93. Beloved husband of the late Josephine, devoted father of Janet L. Markel and Margaret Goodman. Adored grandfather of Heather Markel and Deborah Meister. Cherished father-in-law of William J. Cardew. Retired Lieutenant Colonel of U.S. Army Reserve and former Assistant Commissioner with the N.Y. State Department of Taxation and Finance. Active in Senior Personnel Placement Bureau. He is loved and missed by all.
