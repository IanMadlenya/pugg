
        SAUL--Eli E. On December 7, 2005 at age 92. Beloved husband of the late Helen. Devoted father of Dr. Barry Saul, Cheri Saul, and father-in-law of Ellen Saul. Proud grandfather of Dr. Karen Miller (Howard), Dr. Michael Saul, and Lauren Gidseg (Dr. David). Adored great-grandfather of Parker, Harris, and Grant Miller. He was a skilled machinist for the New York Times and a dedicated leader for the Boy Scouts of America. He excelled at woodworking, photography, and creating novelty chocolates. He leaves the legacy of a curious nature, sharp mind, and strong work ethic. A special life has passed from our sight but never from our hearts. Services Sunday, December 11 at 1 P.M. at BoulevardRiverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY.
