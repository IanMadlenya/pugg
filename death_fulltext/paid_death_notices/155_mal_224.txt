
        SHERMAN-Martin. 83, of Forest Hills, died on December 16 at Cabrini Medical Center in Manhattan. Mr. Sherman was the owner of ''Glass Art'', located on East 75th Street in Manhattan, which he established in 1967. Mr. Sherman was well known to many in the antiques and interior decoration professions for his mastery in creating and repairing Venetianstyle cut-glass and mirrors. He came to the United States in 1967 with his wife Piry, who died in 1998, and their two daughters. He is survived by his daughters, Erika Safran, of New York, and Carmelita Watkinson, of Rockville, MD, and 5 grandchildren. Services today 12:30 at Schwartz Brothers Memorial Chapel, Forest Hills.
