
        FRIEDMAN--Gerald J., M.D. The Beth Israel Medical Center family and its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, are saddened by the death of our dear colleague and generous supporter, Gerald J. Friedman, M.D. Dr. Friedman was a dedicated member of the Beth Israel medical staff for more than 40 years. He served in several leadership positions during his long association with the hospital, including tenure as Chief of Diabetes and Metabolism and two terms as President of the Medical Board. His generous philanthropic support named The Gerald J. Friedman Chair in Endocrinology and Metabolism. In addition, the Conference Center at Beth Israel's Phillips Ambulatory Care Center and the medical intensive care unit at the hospital's main Petrie Division are named for Dr. Friedman and his wife. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire Friedman family over the death of this fine man. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Boards of Trustees Beth Israel and Continuum Stanley Brezenoff, President and CEO Continuum Thomas Killip, M.D. Interim President and CEO Beth Israel Robert G.
