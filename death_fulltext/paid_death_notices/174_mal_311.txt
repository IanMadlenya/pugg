
        GAVER-James H. Of Secaucus, NJ, on Thursday, July 26, 2001. Mr. Gaver was a 1963 graduate of Yale University and was employed by the Federal Reserve Bank, New York, NY, from 1967 to 1997, where he rose to the position of Vice President. Beloved brother of Dean A. and Charles Gaver Jr. Loving uncle of three nieces and two nephews. The family will receive their friends at the Vander Plaat Colonial Home, Inc., 13-31 Saddle River Rd., Fair Lawn, NJ, Tuesday, July 31, 2001, 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers donations to Yale University would be appreciated.
