
        McCABE-James Mason ''Jay''. 61, noted Wall Street analyst died Thursday, January 11, 2001, in Hackensack, NJ, from pulmonary failure following a stem-cell transplant. A lifelong resident of Little Silver, NJ, Mr. McCabe held degrees from the Canterbury School, Princeton University and an MBA from Columbia University. Beloved husband of Marie Louise ''Mimi'' to whom he was married for 39 years, devoted father of Marie, James and his wife Kelli, Christopher, Matthew and Andrew, loving grandfather of James and Sam, cherished brother of Peter and loyal friend. Companies that he was associated with during his career included Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette, Prudential Securities, Nomura Securities and Tiedemann Boltres Partners. For information contact John E. Day Funeral Home, Red Bank, NJ.
