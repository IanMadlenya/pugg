
        PAULSEN--Albert, died April 25, in Los Angeles at 78 years old. Veteran actor appeared in over 20 films and 50 TV shows. Beloved brother of Alfred, he is survived by brother Juan, sister-in-law Jacqueline, devoted nephews William & John and nieces Theodora & Julia. Private memorial service will be held at the Actors Studio in Los Angeles Friday, April 30.
