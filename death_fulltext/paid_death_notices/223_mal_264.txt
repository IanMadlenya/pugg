
        KATZ--Robert H. (Bob). Of NY and Easthampton, died peacefully at age 85 in Easthampton. Loving husband for 45 years of Rita Kupsick Katz. Adored father of Toni Fillet and Jody and Giulio Martini. Loving ''Poppy'' to Gabriella and Francesca Martini. He is also survived by two sisters. Bob attended the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania where he majored in Economics and received a Bachelor of Science. During WWII, he was a Lieutenant in the US Navy on USS Carmick and the USS Macomb, serving in the AsiaticPacific Theater, EuropeanAfrican Theater, and won a WWII Victory Medal. Visting Tuesday, August 30, 6-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, Wednesday at 11:30 AM.
