
        NEWMAN-Debra S. tragically in St. Louis on October 1, 2002. Struck down in the prime of her life, Debra touched the lives of all she came in contact with. Born on June 13, 1980 to her proud and loving parents, Howard and Ann (Solomon) Newman. Debra graduated from Ethical Culture School in Riverdale, Hastings - on - Hudson High School, Cornell University, School of Industrial Relations and Washington University Law School, in St. Louis. Debra leaves behind her loving parents, loving grandfather Louis Solomon and dear aunt Sharon Solomon. Funeral Thursday, October 3, 2002, 1:00PM Temple Beth Shalom, 740 Broadway, Hastings-onHudson, NY. Memorials can be made to the Debra S. Newman Memorial Fund in care of Temple Beth Shalom.
