
        WILKINSON-Irma. Died April 23, 1997. The Board of Trustees and the Staff of the Brooklyn Museum of Art record with deep sorrow the passing of Irma Wilkinson. A collector and museum professional, she was a good friend and important supporter of the Brooklyn Museum of Art until her death. She was married to the late Charles K. Wilkinson, who was from 1969 to 1974 the Museum's Chief Curator and Curator of the Department of Middle Eastern Art for which he formed the core of the Islamic collection.
