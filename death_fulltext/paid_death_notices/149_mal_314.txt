
        SZABO-Tibor, Dr. Died June 18th in Sarasota, FL. Loving and devoted husband of Ruth Simon Szabo. Loving brother of Martha Koyacs. Uncle of Andrea and Dr. Karoly Koyacs. Dr. Szabo was Director of Animal Research at Mt. Sinai Hospital in New York City until 1989. He also received a Master of Public Health at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. In lieu of flowers, donations accepted for the Sarasota Ballet Ambassadors.
