
        GIBSON-Irene Ann, Ph.D. On June 9, at home in her 93rd year. Born in Oswego, NY, Ms. Gibson received her degree from Columbia University Teachers College and was a long time supervisor with the NYC Board of Education. A student and collector of American Indian culture and artifacts she lived in Boulder, CO for a number of years, and served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. She was a 40 year resident of Stuyvesant Town and parishoner of Immaculate Conception Church (414 East 14th St., NYC 10009) where gifts in her memory may be sent. A memorial is being planned.
