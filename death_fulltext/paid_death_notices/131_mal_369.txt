
        EITEN-David. The National Realty Club notes with sorrow the passing of David Eiten, a past president and cherished member. Mr. Eiten was an accomplished raconteur and story teller and his presence will be missed at our luncheons and gatherings. Sincere condolences to his family. Thomas K. Graf, President Stephen D. Barkin, Membership Chair Jerrold Fuchs, Program Chair Anne Teshima, Secretary
