
        ANDERSON-Rose Mary, of Summitville, NY, a retired Manager for Anderson Handprints in Middletown, died Tuesday, Jan. 21, at her home in Summitville. She was 70 years old. The daughter of the late Theodore and Margaret Feeney Zellers, she was born September 17, 1926 in Toledo, OH. Survivors include her husband, Robert S. Anderson at home. There will be no visitation. Cremation was held at the convenience of the family.
