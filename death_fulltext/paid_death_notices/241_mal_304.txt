        BERGER--Robert, Armin. Robert A. Berger, M.D., 75, beloved husband, father, grandfather, doctor and friend, died on February 18 after complications from a fall from his horse while fox hunting. He is survived by his loving wife, Alta; his devoted children, Laura, Alexander and Melanie; and his adored grandchildren, Sage, Andrew, Cedar and Rowan. He was preceded in death by his brother Phillip and sisters Nancy, Janet, and Norma. His death is an indescribable loss for us, his family. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell funeral parlor in Manhattan on February 28 at 11:30am. In lieu of flowers, please consider a donation in his name to the Department of Dermatology at Mt. Sinai Hospital.
