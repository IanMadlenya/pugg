
        LOWRY-Warren. The Trustees and Staff of The Museum of Modern Art extend our deepest sympathies to Glenn Lowry on the sudden and tragic loss of his father. Warren's compassion, his keen intelligence, and his boundless energy live on in Glenn and in Glenn's children. Glenn and his family are in our thoughts during this sad time. David Rockefeller, Chairman Emeritus Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President The Museum of Modern Art, NY
        LOWRY-Warren M. On behalf of the members of The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art, we express our deep sympathy on learning of the sudden death of Glenn Lowry's father, Warren. To Glenn, his wife Susan, his mother Laure, his sister Vicky and his grandchildren, we send our warmest thoughts at this time of great sadness. Jo Carole Lauder, President Brian Urquhart, Chairman The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art, NY
