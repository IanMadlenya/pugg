
        ABOLAFIA-Lee, 84, after a long and courageous struggle with M.S. Beautiful and cherished wife of the late Ralph. Loving mother of the late Rochelle, and Sondra Blank. Mother-in-law to Benjamin Blank. Adored grandmother of Stacy Lee, Dean, and Steve. Greatgrandmother to Rachel, and Jacob Raphael. Her charm, beauty and wit will be missed by all who knew her.
