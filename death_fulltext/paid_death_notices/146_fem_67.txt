
        PLEISSNER-Marion W.G., 97, of Manchester, Vermont, died Sunday, February 28, 1999 at her home in Manchester. A memorial celebration of Mrs. Pleissner's life will be held on Saturday March 6, 1999 at 11am at the First Congregational Church of Manchester in the small chapel. Committal will be private. There are to be no calling hours. For those who wish, memorial gifts in Mrs. Pleissner's memory may be made to the Manchester Rescue Squad or the Southern Vermont Art Center, c/o Brewster Funeral Service, PO Box 885, Manchester CTR, VT 05255.
