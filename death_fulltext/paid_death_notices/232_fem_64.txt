
        NADOLNEY - Bernice, 80. Died at her home in Greenwich Village on May 6. A graduate of NYU, she taught public school in the Bronx and English in Japan (1955-1957) where she developed an enduring love of Japanese culture. A MENSA member, great wit and devotee of the the arts, she leaves a niece, nephew, cousins and many friends. We will meet at Beth Abraham Chapel, 199 Bleecker St., 10:30 AM, Friday, May 12, before a service at New Montefiore Cemetery
