
        MORRISON--John J., of Sarasota, Florida and Madison, NJ on Saturday, March 17, 2007 after a short illness. He was 74 years old. Born in Staten Island, Mr. Morrison lived in Summit for 30 years, before moving to Florida and Madison in 2000. He oversaw the International Treasury Department at Pfizer, Inc. for 30 years, retiring as the Assistant Corporate Treasurer in 1994. Mr. Morrison graduated from St. Peters College in Jersey City in 1954 with a Bachelor's degree. He also earned an MBA from Wagner College in 1968. He was a member of Canoe Brook Country Club in Summit, NJ, as well as the Meadows Country Club in Sarasota, FL. Mr. Morrison served in the U.S. Army from 1954-1956. He is survived by his wife Mary (Graham) Morrison of Sarasota, Florida and Madison, NJ; his daughter Katherine Holland and her husband Craig of New Providence; and his sons Thomas Morrison and his wife Allison of Hoboken; and James Morrison and his wife Jennifer of Hoboken. He is also survived by his grandchildren: Cameron Holland and Eva Morrison. A funeral service for Mr. Morrison will be held on Thursday, March 22,2007 at 8:30 am at Burroughs, Kohr & Dangler Funeral Home, Inc., 106 Main Street, NJ. A funeral mass will follow at 9:30 am at Corpus Christi Church, 234 Southern Boulevard, Chatham, NJ. Interment will be at St. Teresa Cemetery in Summit, NJ. Visiting will be on Wednesday, March 21, 2007 from 2-4 and 7-9 pm at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, donations in Mr. Morrison's name can be made to: Tidwell Hospice, 5955 Rand Blvd., Sarasota, Florida, 34238 or Community Foodbank of NJ, 31 Evans Temple Road, Hillside, NJ 07205.
