
        SINNOTT--Edward C., MD. Of Somers, NY and Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Roslyn and Old Westbury, NY died on April 9, 2004. A graduate of NY Medical College, he served in the U.S. Navy and then as an attending anesthesiologist at Meadowbrook Hospital in East Meadow, NY. He was Chairman of the Department of Anesthesiology and the Executive Committee of the Medical Staff at Nassau County Medical Center. He was past President of the New York State Society of Anesthesiologists and of the Nassau Academy of Medicine. Served as a Trustee of the National Board for Respiratory Care and was actively involved on the New York State Board for Professional Medical Conduct. He was born February 17, 1928 to the late J. Paul and Helen Cranwell Sinnott. He was predeceased by his loving wife, Lydia (Trini) Fitzpatrick Sinnott. He is survived by his son Edward, Jr. of Mt. Kisco, NY, daughter-in-law Eileen and three grandchildren, Daniel, Edward and William. Also survived by his brother, J. Paul, Jr. of Katonah, NY. Reposing at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY on Friday from 2-4 and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian Burial at the St. Francis Chapel at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY on Saturday, 10:30am. Interment will follow at the family mausoleum at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
