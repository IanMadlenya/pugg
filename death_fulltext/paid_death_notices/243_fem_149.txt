
        HART--Kitty Carlisle. The Aston Magna Foundation for Music mourns the passing of Kitty Carlisle Hart. For the past 25 years, she gave generously of her time, spirit and energy, both in New York and Great Barrington. She will be deeply missed. Lee M. Elman and the Board of Directors of Aston Magna $(6$)HART--Kitty Carlisle. The Board of Trustees and Staff of Theatre Development Fund mourn the passing of our admired colleague and a great advocate for the arts. From 1970 -1976, Mrs. Hart served as a valued trustee prior to her appointment to the New York State Council on the Arts. Her wise counsel and enthusiasm helped move TDF forward during a period of growth and change. We extend our sympathies to her family. David D. Holbrook, Chairman Victoria Bailey, Exec Dir.
