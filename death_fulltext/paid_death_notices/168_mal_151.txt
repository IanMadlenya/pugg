
        RUDD-Wallace (Wally). Prolific inventor and founder of Thermatool Corporation. Died December 26, 2000. Father of Robert Rudd of Rye, N.Y. and Nancy Nottermann of Vermont; three grandchildren also survive. He was a recipient of the 1986 N.Y. Patent, Trademark and Copywrite Law Association Inventor Award and was a graduate of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. A Memorial Gathering will be held at noon on Saturday, January 13th in the New Canaan Historical Society, 13 Oenoke Ridge, New Canaan, Ct. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the New Canaan Historical Society or Americares Foundation, 161 Cherry Street, New Canaan, CT. 06840.
