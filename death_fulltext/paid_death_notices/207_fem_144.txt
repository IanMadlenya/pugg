
        BREITZER--Dora Margolis. Died on April 19, 2004. She was 89 years old. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Bernard Breitzer. Sister of Rebecca Fink. Cherished mother of Sheila Leitner and Dr. Gerard Breitzer and the late Dr. Robert Breitzer. Devoted mother-in-law of Art Leitner and Dr. Susan Frank. Loving grandmother of Dr. Jonathan Breitzer and Frith Gowan, Joshua & Rebekah Breitzer, David, Brian, and Jonathan Leitner. Great-grandmother of Sophie Rose. Contributions may be made to the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center, Commack, New York 11725.
