
        FIGATNER-Herman. Born on October 27, 1916, died February 7, 2001 in New Rochelle, New York. Beloved husband of Betsy. Father of Steven and Nancy. Grandfather of Julia, Jamal, Sarah and Dustin. Executive for 45 years at George Braziller Publishers. Concerned community member, he showed the world grace, integrity and compassion, always with a smile. Private memorial planned.
