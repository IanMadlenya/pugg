
        LAWRY-Sylvia. The Corinne Goldsmith Dickinson Center for Multiple Sclerosis at the Mount Sinai School of Medicine is deeply saddened by the passing of Sylvia Lawry. Mrs. Lawry was the undisputed leader and pioneer in thefight against multiple sclerosis. We shall all miss her greatly. Fred Lublin, Director George J. Gillespie, III and Clifford H. Goldsmith Co-Founders
