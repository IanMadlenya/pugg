
        PLISKIN-Robert. On September 26, 2000. Died peacefully at home surrounded by a loving family. Beloved husband to Caroline. Devoted father to Arlene, Patricia and the late Pamela. Adored grandfather to Stephanie, Mindy, Tracy, Joshua, Jamie, Alysha, Jason and Melissa. Dear brother to Irving and Mort. We mourn the loss of a committed, energetic, generous human being & a friend to many. He fought a long & courageous battle. He will be sorely missed and affectionately remembered. Service Thursday, September 28th, 1:15PM, Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave., NYC.
        PLISKIN-Robert. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member Friar Robert Pliskin on September 26, 2000. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. A memorial service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street, 1:15 PM, Thursday, September 28. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        PLISKIN-Robert. We are deeply saddened by the loss of Bob, a wonderful, kind and caring friend. We and all who knew him will miss his unique good nature. Our sincere condolences to his loving wife, Caroline, & his entire family. Ellin and Leo Salon
        PLISKIN-Robert. The American Watch Association mourns the passing of Robert Pliskin, our former Chairman and outstanding leader of the American watch industry. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family.
