
        FERENBACH-Hannah Smedley Powers. Age 85, died June 1, 2001, at Evergreen Woods in North Branford, CT. Born in Conyngham, PA, on December 27, 1915. Preceded in death by her husband Carl Ferenbach. Survived by son Carl Ferenbach and wife Judy, her daughter Polly Ferenbach Franchot, two granddaughters, Jennifer and Alexandra and great-granddaughter, Elizabeth. Memorial service Wednesday, June 6, 11 AM at St. John's On The Mountain in Bernardsville, NJ. In lieu of flowers memorials preferred to the Alzheimers Association.
