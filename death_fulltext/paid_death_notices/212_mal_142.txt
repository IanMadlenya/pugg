
        COHN--Richard J., on September 15, 2004. Beloved husband of Leita Hesen. Devoted brother of Adelaide Peters. Loving uncle of Tom and Gerry Peters. A graduate of Lawrence High School and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. A longtime member of the American Stock Exchange and options trader for Susquehanna. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St., Saturday, September 18, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Services Sunday, September 19, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to SloanKettering Hospital.
