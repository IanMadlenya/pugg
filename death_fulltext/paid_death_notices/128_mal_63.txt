
        McGEE-Rev. Thomas F. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicar of St. Francis de Sales Church, Belle Harbor, on Sept. 3. Reposing in Evergreen Funeral Home, 131 Nassau Ave., Friday, Sept. 5, 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Friday, Sept. 5 at 7:30 P.M. in St. Cecilia Church, 84 Herbert St., Brooklyn. Funeral Mass, Saturday, Sept. 6 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
