
        GARTENBERG-Anne (nee Stassi). On February 3rd, at 62 years. Beloved wife of Seymour Gartenberg. Loving mother of Leslie Mlawski, Karen Gartenberg and Mark Gartenberg. Devoted grandmother of Shana Mlawski, Ariele Mlawski and Justin Gartenberg. Cherished sister of John Stassi and Margaret Stassi. Adored daughter of the late Margherita and Joseph Stassi. Services were held at Temple Sinai of Long Island, February 6, 1998. Contributions to Brooklyn Hospice or Temple Sinai of Long Island. Her loving and caring spirit will light up our lives forever.
