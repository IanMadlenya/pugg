
        WETZLER-Hedwig Seewald, 95, died July 27 after a long illness endured with courage. Widow of Max Wetzler. Sister of the late Ruth Messer, Lothar, Fred and Hans Seewald. Survived by sons, Elliot, Gerald, Peter and Leonard; brother Ernest J. Seewald; sister Edith S. Ratner. She will be dearly missed by all who knew her.
