
        COHEN-Helen Young Of New York City. Died on June 24, 1999. Survived by her husband of 59 years, Elliot; brothers, Gilbert and Judson Young, of South Bend, IN; numerous nephews and nieces, great nephews and nieces, and 12 great-great nephews and nieces. Interment was June 28 in South Bend. A graduate of St. Joseph's College for Women, Brooklyn, NY, and a co-sponsor and supporter of the Elliot E. and Helen Y. Cohen Forum Endowment at the College of William and Mary. A memorial service will be held in New York at a later date.
