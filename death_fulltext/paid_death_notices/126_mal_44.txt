
        MORRIS-Jonathan Scott. On June 23, 1997. Memorial services were held on June 25, 1997, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. The family of Jonathan Scott Morris has requested that any donations made in his honor be made to the Jonathan Morris Jerusalem Middle East Peace Theatre Foundation. Contributions should be sent to Dr. Robert H. Morris, 1040 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10028.
