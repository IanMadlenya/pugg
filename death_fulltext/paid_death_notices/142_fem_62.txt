
        MILLER-Jeanne White. Resident of West Palm Beach, FL, wife of the late Andrew O. Miller, died on October 30, 1998, at the age of 77. She was born in Boston, MA, daughter of the late Loring and Arlene White, of Cohasset and Palm Beach. She was a wonderful mother, a loving wife, a beautiful person, an artist and an accomplished athlete who excelled at tennis and golf. She is survived by her children Constance M. Bergh of MA, Deborah M. Lalor of VA, Jeffrey C. Miller of CT, Dwight A. Miller of MO, and Bradford P. Miller of FL, her brothers William and Frederick White and sister Anne Bedford, nine grandchildren, and numerous nephews and nieces. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, November 22, 1998, at St. John's Episcopal Church in Cold Spring Harbor, NY at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the Arthritis Foundation, 1330 W. Peach Tree St, Atlanta, GA 30309.
