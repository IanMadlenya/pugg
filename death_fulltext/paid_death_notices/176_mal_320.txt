A memorial service will be held Saturday, September 22, 5:00 PM at the Harmonie Club, 4 East 60th St. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Andrew Golkin Scholarship Fund at SEO, care of Sponsors for Educational Opportunities, 23 Gramercy Park South, New York, NY 10003.
        GOLKIN-Andrew, H. His dazzling blue eyes, joyful smile and generous heart will warm and lift our spirits forever. Dear Golkin and Cohen families, we share your sorrow and loss. Susan, Fred, Stephanie and Andrea Friedman
