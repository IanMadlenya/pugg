
        KATZ-Martin, 73, a private investor, passed away October 18 following a fall at his home the preceding week. Until several years ago, Mr. Katz was president of PS Products, Inc., a specialty printer. An alumnus of The Townsend Harris School and Rutgers University, he is survived by his fiancee/ companion of seven years, Pearl Rind Schoenberg, a brother and sister-in-law, Edward C. Katz and Candace Leeds. Mr. Katz served in the U.S. Army occupation forces in Japan following World War II (1946-1947). Mr. Katz maintained a life-long interest in military history. He was also a member of the Metropolitan Opera Guild and gave liberally to various animal charities. Mr. Katz enjoyed his retirement, spending many pleasant hours with friends at the Colonial Heights Tennis Club. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, October 28, 2001 at 1:00 pm at the Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave., Yonkers. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to your favorite charity in his memory.
