
        HARTMAN-Elliott. 67, died on July 28, at the home of his sister, Lynn Hartman Alpert, wife of State Supreme Court Justice Bruce D. Alpert. Mr. Hartman was very devoted to Lynn, his brother Richard, his nephew Michael Alpert, his niece Lauren Alpert Grossman, her husband Craig, and his cousin Monte Green and his wife Anne. Funeral services will be held at 12 noon, Tuesday, July 30, at Gutterman's Funeral Home in Rockville Centre.
