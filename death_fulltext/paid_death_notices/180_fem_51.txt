
        COLVIN-Elinor. On Sunday, December 30, 2001. Born on February 24, 1944. Beloved wife of Stephen B. Colvin, M.D., devoted, loving & caring mother of Laurel Tanier, RN, NP, and Heather Colvin, M.D., cherished grandmother of Julia, beloved sister of Arlene Cheiken and daughter of the late Naomi and Jack Greneman. Mrs. Colvin was a graduate of Washington Square College of Arts and Science of New York University and a former elementary school teacher in New York City. Contributions in her memory can be made to either the Elinor Colvin Memorial Scholarship at New York University School of Medicine, C/O Stephen B. Colvin, M.D., NYU Medical Center, 530 First Avenue, Suite 9V, NY, NY 10016, or to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, 30 West 26th Street, New York, New York 10010.
