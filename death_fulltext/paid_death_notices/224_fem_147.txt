
        DUNLAVY--Francine Alessi, 95, died 29 January 2005. A talented linguist and teacher, she was preceded in death by her husband, Alfred R. Dunlavy, and is survived by a daughter and son-in-law, Patricia and Peter Valenti; grandson, Marco Valenti; granddaughter and grandson-in-law, Christine and Justin Shreve. Burial service 22 September at Calvary Cemetery, Queens, NY, at 10:00 am.
