
        PULLANO-Joyce, M.D. New York Community Hospital mourns the sudden and unexpected loss of our dear friend and colleague Dr. Joyce Pullano who served as our President of the Medical Board. The entire hospital family will always remember Dr. Pullano for her eighteen years of devotion to our patients, fellow employees, staff and the hospital. We remember her for her compassion, courage and loyalty. Our heartfelt condolences to her family. Lin H. Mo, President & CEO George Weinberger, President, Board of Trustees
        PULLANO-Joyce, M.D. The members of the Medical Staff of New York Community Hospital mourn the loss of their friend and colleague, Dr. Joyce Pullano, President of the Medical Board, and extend sincere sympathy and condolences to the family. Alan Sonsky, M.D. Vice President, Medical Board
