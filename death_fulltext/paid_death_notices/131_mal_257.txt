
        BARRETT-Paul H. Of New Caanan, CT. On December 12, 1997, in Tibet, while on a two month volunteer trip with the International Executive Corp to help reform the Chinese banking system. He was formerly with Bankers Trust and Bank Leu. Survived by his wife May Chang Barrett, daughter Anne Catherine Barrett and brothers Noel and Peter. Funeral service Saturday, December 27, at 3PM, St. Mark's Church, Oenoke Ridge Rd, New Caanan. Contributions may be made to the Multiple Sclerosis Society, 733 Third Ave, NYC 10017, C/O Sylvia Lawry.
