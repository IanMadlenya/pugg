
        SHAW--Grace of Scarsdale passed away on November 23 of complications of breast cancer. Grace was predeceased by her husband Herbert F. Shaw. She is survived by her son Brandon H. Shaw of Tampa, Florida. Grace worked for several decades as a book publishing editor in New York City at publishing houses such as Grosset & Dunlap and Bobbs-Merrill, often as managing editor or publisher. A memorial service will be held at the Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave, Scarsdale, NY 10583 today at 6PM.
