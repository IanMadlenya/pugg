
        OSMAN-Sam. Passed away February 15, 2000. Founder and owner of New York's legendary store, Job Lot/Pushcart. Beloved husband of Eve. Adored and cherished by his loving extended family: Robbie, Donny, Janna and Daniel Osman; Peter, Joan, Vicki, Rachel and Andrew Bergang; Mimi, Nancy and Marc Hirschmann. He was a man of dignity and integrity and will be deeply missed.
