
        LUPO-Adela, 65, of Commack, NY. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Died January 30, 2000 of pancreatic cancer. She is survived by her only child, Jennifer, her son-in-law, Jose, two sisters, Carmen and Henrietta, a niece, Denise, a greatnephew, Vincent, and her two cats, Oscar, too and Felix. Services will be held at the Commack Abbey, 96 Commack Road, Commack, New York on February 1, 2000 from 2PM to 5PM and 7PM to 10PM. Interment on February 2, 2000 at Pinelawn Memorial Park, Farmingdale, NY. 90294. Memorial donations may be made to the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research, PO Box 2141 New York, NY 10116.
