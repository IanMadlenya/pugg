
        MARTIN--Peter, of New York City, who for over thirty years developed the skills of young musicians in the Freeport School System and brought the joy of music to countless students, died on February 15, 2007, after a 10 year-long battle with cancer. Trained in music education at SUNY Potsdam and awarded a Masters in Conducting from Julliard. Peter is survived by his wife; Susan A. Kotlier, M.D. Peter was the devoted son of Paula Martin, a cherished son-in-law, brother-inlaw, uncle and cousin, and a loved and respected friend and colleague. Services at Temple Israel, 477 Longwood Ave., Boston, MA, Sunday, February 18, 2007 at 11am. Burial at the B'nai Lodge Cemetery, Worcester, MA. Shiva will be observed at the home of Jonathan Kotlier. Donations may be made to Memorial Sloan Kettering, Julliard, or a charity of your choice. We mourn the passing of this gentle and generous soul. He will be sorely missed.
