
        KRIDEL-Carol H., 78. September 8, at home. She was the widow of William J. Kridel. She is survived by her son, William J. Kridel, Jr, of London and New York, and by her daughter, Linda K. A memorial service will be held at Campbell's Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue (at 81st Street), Sunday, September 13 at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Mt. Sinai Medical Center, Palliative Care Program, 1 Gustave L. Levy Place, New York, NY 10029, or to The Collegiate School, 370 West End Avenue, New York, NY 10024.
