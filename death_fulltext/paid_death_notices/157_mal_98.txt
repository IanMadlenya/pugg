
        HAMILTON-Thomas Parker, Jr., Dr. Died February 8, 2000. Retired surgeon. Resided Tenafly, N.J. He is survived by his wife Ruth, sons Thomas P. Hamilton, III of Seminole, Fla., Lawrence R. Hamilton of Saratoga Springs, N.Y. and his daughter Virginia H. Idyll of Toronto, Ca; granddaughters Holly Boldrin, Kimberly Hamilton, Parker B. Hamilton, Candy R. Hamilton. Additional survivors are stepdaughter Betsey Colby, Wayland, Ma., step-son Ned Taft of Wellesley, Ma. and step-grandchildren Kate, Charles and Caroline Colby and Betsey and Ben Taft. Dr. Hamilton was a graduate of Mercersburg Academy, Princeton University and Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. Dr. Hamilton was a surgeon with the US Army Medical Corp. His unit landed on Omaha Beach on D-Day. As a Major, he was awarded the Bronze Star for heroic service. Contribution in his memory may be made to the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, 6931 Arlington Road, Bethesda, Maryland 20814. A private memorial service will be held in May.
