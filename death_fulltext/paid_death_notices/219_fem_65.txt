
        REDDICK -- Jeanne. (stage name Jeanne Shepherd) died on her 84th birthday, March 16, 2005. Born in Verona, NJ, she graduated Bucknell College in 1943, and commenced her acting career on tour in ''My Sister Eileen''. Two Moss Hart productions followed, along with a lifetime friendship with his wife, Kitty Carlisle. After playing Laura in the first ''Glass Menagerie'' she landed contract with Warner Bros., starring in ''Adventures of Don Juan'' with Errol Flynn. She married Bill Reddick in 1952. They worked in television for many years. A career in California real estate followed until ill health intervened. A truly regal, sensitive, charming, witty lady has left our stage.
