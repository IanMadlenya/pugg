
        ELLIS-Esta B. Formerly of Mt Vernon, NY, on June 16, 1997 at 94. She served on the National Board of Hadassah and for 16 years represented Hadassah at the United Nations. She was also active in her community. She is survived by her daughter Patricia Stone, grandchildren Susan and Robert and great-grandson Jeffrey. In accordance with her wishes she was cremated after a small family service and her ashes scattered at sea. Contributions in her name can be sent to Hadassah.
