
        CHEN-Una Y.T., 87. Granddaughter of Tan Chun-Lin, Governor General of Guang Dong and Guang Xi Provinces, daughter of Tan Yen-Kai, Chairman and First Prime Minister of Nationalist China, died peacefully while visiting her son, Harold Chen in Salt Lake City, Utah. Mrs. Chen, beloved wife of Kuan-Cheng, a graduate of Newnham College, Cambridge University, taught English in China and Linguistics at Georgetown and Johns Hopkins Universities before retiring to New York City. In addition to her son, Mrs. Chen is survived by daughters, Elaine Chen-Nash of Virginia, Tina ChenJosephson of Manhattan, Sana Chen-Powell of Hawaii and seven grandchildren. Service is at 11:30AM, Sat., Aug. 19, Campbell Funeral Home, Madison at 81 St.
