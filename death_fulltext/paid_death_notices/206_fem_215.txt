
        FREEMAN--Eva, age 102, passed away at home March 22 surrounded by her devoted relatives. A magnificent matriarch. A native of Hamburg who fled Nazi Germany with her husband, Dr. Ernest Freeman, and five children who survive her: Mrs. Jenny Michael (Kenneth), Rabbi Chaim Freeman (Bitya), Rabbi Alfred Freeman (Shoshana), Dr. Ruth Freeman Lewis (Robert) and Mrs. Rachel Steiner (Mark). She leaves 26 grandchildren, over 100 great-grandchildren and three great great-grandchildren. Burial in Israel, March 23. Shiva will be observed 251 West 87th Street, Apt 22.
