
        RIGHTHAND-Herbert, CLU. Died January 12, 2002 in Walnut Creek, CA at age 89. Born April 1st, 1912 in NY. Herb graduated from Boys High in Brooklyn and attended Syracuse University. He served in the army during World War II, was honorably discharged and decorated. Mr. Righthand was well known and respected in the insurance industry. He was a CLU and a Leaders Round Table Agent with Conn.Mutual-Mass Mutual during a 50-year career in insurance. Herb also served as an auxiliary Nassau County Police officer. He enjoyed his life, loved his family as they loved him and lived a happy and admirable life. He is survived by his wife of 63 years, Bernice, their two children, Penny and Scott, & three grandchildren.
