
        WEISSBURG-Martin. The Cooper Union Alumni Association mourns the death of distinguished Cooper Union alumnus, Martin Weissburg, Electrical Engineering 1929, and President of the Alumni Association 1964-1965. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Mildred. An active and devoted Cooper alumnus & friend, Martin Weissburg will be missed. Marilyn Hoffner, President, Cooper Union Alumni Association
