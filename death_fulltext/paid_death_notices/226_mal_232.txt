
        KOLKER--Leon (Lee), 95. Survived by loving wife of 37 years, Betty Lee March Kolker; previously married to the late Myrtle Picker Kolker. Father of Anne and Thomas, grandfather of Joanne and Amy, stepfather to Ward, Tom and Bill Blum, and children. 1931 graduate of MIT. Owner of Kolker Chemical of Newark, NJ and Houston, TX. Trustee of Elmira College. Honorary Consul General of The Gambia, 1973-1986. Services private. In lieu of flowers please send donations in his name to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021.
