
        ROSA-Elisa Cristina. Age 38 of Maplewood, New Jersey, passed away July 17, 2002 at New York Presbyterian Hospital following a long and courageous fight against cancer. Beloved wife of Joseph P. Kirk Jr. She is also survived by her mother Ute W. Rosa, father Edgar P. Rosa, grandmother Olga Wagner, brother Marcelo Rosa and his family. Cristina was born in Sao Paulo, Brazil on June 14, 1964. She attended the University of Kansas and completed her studies at Syracuse University. She worked as a textile design artist at Brunschwig & Fils in Manhattan. A memorial service will be held on August 24, 2002, 10:00AM at the First Presbyterian Church, 124 Henry Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to either: Brooklyn Botanic Garden, 1000 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, New York; or Cancer Care, 275 Seventh Avenue, New York, New York 10001.
