
        LINKOFF-Helen Harvey, 81. On November 17th, 1997, from complications from a car accident. Loving mother of James, Sarah and Frank, mother-in-law of Ted, adored grandmother of Jason, Sarah, Raimy, Daniel, Thomas, Benjamin and Henry. Beloved partner of Marion Matera. Was a notable theatrical literary agent happily retired to her home nestled in between her apple orchard and garden in New Milford, CT.
