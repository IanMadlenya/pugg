
        SONDERLING - Arthur, C.P.A. Died peacefully at home on September 2, 2000. Loving husband of Lillian, cherished father of Edward and Harlan, beloved father-in-law and grandfather. A gentleman and a lifelong New Yorker, born August 18, 1914, graduate of Dewitt Clinton High School and NYU, 1935. Defended his country, 1941-1945, in North Africa as Staff Sergeant in the U.S. Army. Compassionate and wise, a scholar and a lover of life, language, and people. Trusted and esteemed by all who knew him. We miss you, Dad. Gather in Manhattan after funeral Wednesday, 4-8 PM. Call 212-308-5033. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33486.
