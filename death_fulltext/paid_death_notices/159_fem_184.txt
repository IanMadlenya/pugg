
        MAGIDOFF-Ruth. Passed away April 20, 2000. Widow of Davis. Survived by loving daughter, Natalie Sheffler, and son-in-law, Frank; adoring son, Alan Magidoff, and daughter-in-law, Iris; 7 grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren.
