
        NEUWIRTH--Richard. Testrite Instrument Co., Inc., mourns the loss of Richard Neuwirth. Testrite was founded by Richard's father, Herman Neuwirth in 1919. Everyone at Testrite knew Dick to be a kind and caring individual. Dick is survived by his wife Eileen, daughter Shelby Richardson, son-in-law Doug Richardson and grandson Dylan Ryan Richardson. Funeral will be at Gutterman and Musicant, Hackensack, NJ, 11:30am Sunday.
