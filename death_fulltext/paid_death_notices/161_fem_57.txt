
        FINE-Rose. Veteran school teacher for TV and film children in the '60s, '70s and '80s, died at her Studio City, California home on June 5, 2000. She is survived by her husband, Sidney Fine, to whom she was married for 75 years; a daughter, Judith L. Simcoe, five grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Her students included Ron Howard, Annette Funicello, Bill Mumy, Jodie Foster, the Lennon Sisters, Michael Jackson, Janet Jackson, Kevin Corcoran, and other members of the Jackson and Corcoran families. Mrs. Fine traveled worldwide with Michael and the other Jacksons on their performance tours. She was 94.
