
        COOK-Benjamin A. On March 16, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Fay Moss Cook. Brother of Edy Barron of Pembrook Pines, FL, brother of the late Joseph and Jack. Uncle, great-uncle and great-great-uncle. He lived his life the way he wanted to and his trademark was eloquence. WWII veteran, North African Campaign, and member of the Masons. Longtime resident and merchant of Lynbrook, LI. Graveside service today, 2:30 PM, at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
