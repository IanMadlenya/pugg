
        KAPP -- Frederic C. Died March 13th, 2004. The officers, directors and employees of New York Design Center, Inc. join with his family in mourning the passing of our friend and colleague, Fred Kapp, a director of our company for over 20 years. He was a fine human being whose presence will be sorely missed. A memorial service will be held at the St. Simons Island Presbyterian Church today, March 16th. For details, please contact The Edo Miller and Sons Funeral Home in Brunswick, GA, 912265-3636. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Hospice of the Golden Isles, The American Cancer Society or The St. Simons Presbyterian Church Building Fund, whose addresses can be obtained from the funeral home.
