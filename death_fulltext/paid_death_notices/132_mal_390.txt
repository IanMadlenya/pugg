
        MURPHY-Dr. Joseph S. District Council 37, AFSCME, AFL-CIO and its constituent locals mourn the untimely loss of Dr. Joseph S. Murphy, former Chancellor of the City University of New York and a strong advocate for working people's education. Our members who work for the City University system had great respect for his stewardship as Chancellor. His leadership in fighting for higher educational opportunities for working men and women and his knowledge of and respect for the labor movement is deeply appreciated by the Board, the staff and the members of our union. Our condolences to his family and many friends. Stanley Hill, Executive Director Victor Guadalupe, President Helen Greene, Secretary Robert F. Myers, Jr., Treasurer
