
        FINK-Manny, passed away October 25, 1998, survived by loving wife Claudette, adoring sons Henri B. and David B., broken-hearted mother Anna and caring brother David. Former Managing and Director of Oppenheimer & Co. and private entrepreneur. The sun will never shine as brightly for us again. Services Tuesday, 9:45AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        FINK-Manny. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Manny Fink, a generous supporter and valued member of our Wall Street Division. His compassion and concern will live on through his sons, who have embraced their family's devotion to Jewish causes. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife, Claudette, and his two sons, Henry and David. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        FINK-Manny. Sutton Place Synagogue records with deep sorrow the loss of our longtime member and benefactor. Our sympathies to his devoted wife Claudette, adored children Henri and David, beloved mother Anna and the entire bereaved family. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
        FINK-Manny. Wall Street Synagogue mourns the loss of a devoted friend, officer and benefactor. We extend sincere condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Meyer Hager
        FINK-Manny. The Commission mourns the death of a very special man, husband and father and extends sympathy to the entire Fink family. Your friends will always be there for all of you.
