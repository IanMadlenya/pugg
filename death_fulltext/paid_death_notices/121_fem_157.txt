
        SWAN-Lila. On February 21. Passed away peacefully after a long illness. Loving wife of Kingsley Swan, will be remembered with love by her step-daughter Suzette McGouth, her nephew Bonzo King and cousin John Baker. Beloved daughter of the late Philip and Stella King. She will be deeply missed by her wide circle of friends. She touched many lives in an unforgettable way. Services at The Unitarian Church Of All Souls, 1157 Lexington Avenue at 80th Street, Thursday, February 27, 11 AM. Family will receive friends at her home after the service.
