
        OPPENHEIM-Selma. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation note with sorrow the passing of Selma Oppenheim. Her son, Steven, is an outstanding member of our Board of Directors and a leader in our Business and Finance Division. He served as vice president of the Educational Alliance, a UJA-Federation beneficiary agency. The entire Jewish community is indebted to the family's dedication and support, and express condolences to Steven, Mrs. Oppenheim's daughter-inlaw, Lucille, and her grandchildren, Laura, Michael and Daniel. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
