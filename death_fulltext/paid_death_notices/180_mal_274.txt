
        KANTO-John M. Died on January 7, 2002 at age 57 after a long and courageous struggle with emphysema. Son of Florene and the late John U. Kanto, John was born in Carlsbad, NM in 1944. In 1967, after attending Texas Western University in El Paso, John came to New York and joined Citibank as a Teller. He rose steadily through the ranks at Citibank until his retirement in 1992 when he was a Vice President and Chief of Staff to the Chief Auditor of Citicorp worldwide. John was a volunteer in numerous organizations, including the Community Health Project and the AIDS Memorial at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine. He was preceded in death by his good friend Jim Zitlow and his companion Jonathan Sand. He leaves his mother and many friends with memories of his generous spirit and quick wit. Contributions in John's name may be made to the Lambda Legal Defense and Education Fund, the ACLU Lesbian and Gay Rights Project or St Luke's Roosevelt Hospital. A Memorial Service to celebrate John's life will be held in St. Luke's Chapel in the Cathedral of St. John the Divine, 1047 Amsterdam Ave at W. 110 St., on Saturday, February 16, at 1 PM.
