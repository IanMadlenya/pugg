
        MENDECKI--Jozef. Ph.D. December 4th, 2006. Dr. Mendecki was born in Poland and came to the U.S. as a distinguished Fullbright Fellow. In 1971, he joined the Department of Radiation Oncology at Montefiore Medical Center, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, as a research scientist in the field of cancer therapy development. Dr. Mendecki established joint research programs with the Departments of Pathology and Urology, and was instrumental in the discovery of how temperature affects cancer cell killing by radiation. He was a pioneer in hyperthermia research, developing experimental models and technologies to increase temperature within tumors. He was a man of many talents and abilities, always ready to assist others, friends and strangers alike. Dr . Jozef Mendecki was loved and valued by all who knew him, and will be greatly missed.
