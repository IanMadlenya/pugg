
        BLOOM-Samuel M. MD.. On June 25, 1999. Beloved husband of Zita. Loving father of Lloyd and Betty. Dear grandfather of Michael and Benjamin Berson and Henny Bloom. BLOOM-Samuel M., M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of The Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our long-standing colleague and Associate Clinical Professor Emeritus in the Department of Otolaryngology. Dr.Bloom completed his residency in 1939. From 1941 to 1946 he served as a member of the United States Medical Corps and was honored with a Bronze Star for his outstanding service. He returned to Mount Sinai where he became active in clinical practice and teaching. He will be fondly remembered for his courage as well as his warm and gentle humanity. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Stanley Tuhrim, M.D., President Terry Weill, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Admin BLOOM-Samuel M.D. Congregation Or Zarua mourns the death of our member, Samuel Bloom. May He who consoles the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem bring comfort to his family. Dr. Harlan J. Wechsler, Rabbi Alisa Doctoroff, President
