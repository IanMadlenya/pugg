
        FRIEDMAN - Dr. Ronald Marvin. Of Riverdale, NY, born April 26, 1930, in Brooklyn, NY, beloved husband, father and grandfather, accomplished molecular biologist. Horace Mann graduate 1950, B.S. in Zoology and Chemistry Columbia University 1960, M.S. Cardiovascular Physiology N.Y.U. 1967, Ph.D. Cell Biology N.Y.U. 1977, passed away March 6. To be laid to rest at Woodlawn Cemetery March 10, at 10 AM. He will be greatly missed.
