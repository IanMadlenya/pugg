
        RIKER-Anne T. ''Nancy'' (Cox). 91, of Weston, MA, formerly of NYC, July 21, 2002. Wife of the late Samuel Riker who died in 1970. She is survived by her daughter Anne T. Buttrick of Concord, MA and sons, Samuel of Tuckers Town, Bermuda and Robert T. of Sarasota, FL. She also leaves her sister Frances B. Cox of Sarasota, FL, eight grandchildren and ten greatgrandchildren. A celebration of her life will be held in late September in Concord followed by burial in Bermuda. Gifts in her memory may be made to the Massachusetts Audubon Society, 208 South Great Road, Lincoln, MA 01773.
