
        BREED-Helen Illick. On January 4. She was the daughter of Joseph Simon Illick and Della May Brotzman. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Henry E. Breed II, three children Henry E. Breed, III , Joseph I. Breed and Brenda B. Shapiro, a brother Ira E. Illick, and a sister Anna May Langlois. Funeral service Tues, 4pm from the Gilead Lutheran Church, Troy, NY. Friends may call at the Bryce Funeral Home Inc., Troy, NY on Monday 3-5 & 7-9pm. Interment Oakwood Cemetery in Syracuse. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Illick Music Fund, Office of Development, 200 Crouse College, Syracuse University, Syracuse, NY 13244
