
        WALDMAN-Joseph, M.D. Of Philadelphia, PA, on October 9, 1999 at age 93. Husband of Lillian (nee Cades). Father of Julia Waldman Rasch, Julian David Waldman and Joanna Waldman. Brother of Ann Feldman. Also survived by 7 grandchildren and 1 great-grandchild.
