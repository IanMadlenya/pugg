Beloved wife of Robert; mother of David and Michael; sister of Barbara and Philip Lichtenstein and Caryl Yanow and William Grueskin; aunt of Sarah Lichtenstein; niece of Robert and Sandra Levine and Mildred and Marvin Wallach; daughter of the late Mitchell and Elaine Yanow.
