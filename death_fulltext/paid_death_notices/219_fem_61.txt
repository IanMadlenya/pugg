
        GOLDSMITH--Elsa M. World renowned Artist of ''Joan of Arc'' and ''Walking Happy'' peacefully passed away April 8, 2005. Beloved mother of Cris and Jo Ann Goldsmith. Her stories and art contributions will be greatly missed. Services Sunday, April 10, 3PM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza ''opposite the LIRR'', Great Neck, NY.
