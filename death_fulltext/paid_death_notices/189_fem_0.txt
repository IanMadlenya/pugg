
        BLICKSTEIN-Florence. Died September 29, 2002. She was of Spring Glen, NY and the retired co-owner and operator of the Homowack Lodge. She is survived by her two daughters, Karen and Ellen, and her three loving grandchildren: Michael, Ann and Maia. Funeral services will be held today at Congregation Ezrath Israel Synagogue, Ellenville, N.Y. at 2 P.M. Burial at Ezrath Israel Cemetery, Wawarsing.
