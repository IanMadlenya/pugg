
        BAUMAN-Zaphrirah J. Died at home July 1, 2001 in her 84th year. A devoted teacher & courageous union activist, she was also a lifelong advocate for tenants rights & other progressive political causes. She is survived by her husband Joseph Bauman; her brother Joseph Kremen; her sons Ellis and Richard Bauman; grandsons Mark and Andrew Bauman; granddaughters Gretchen and Rachael Stoeltje; great-grandchildren Matthew, Morgan, Madisen Bauman, Alexandra Bauman & Calvin and Nicholas Anderson. We will miss her loving heart and her indomitable spirit. A memorial service will be announced later in the Fall.
