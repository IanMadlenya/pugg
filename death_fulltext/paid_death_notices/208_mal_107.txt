
        BOSTELMAN--William T. Of Ringwood, NJ, on May 5, 2004. Loving husband of Leta (nee Buchanan). Father of Patricia, John and his wife Emily. He was President of Bostelman Assoc., a public relations firm in New York City. He served as a Lieutenant, US Navy, WWII in the Asiatic Pacific Theater earning seven battle stars. Memorial service Wednesday, May 12, 11 am, at St. Paul's Episcopal Church, Englewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations to Ringwood Public Library, Ringwood, NJ 07456, please. Directions visit www.barrettfuneralhome.net.
