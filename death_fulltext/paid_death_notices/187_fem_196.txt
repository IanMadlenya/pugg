
        BERENS-Mary. On August 27, 2002. She would have said she was 59. Beloved wife of Abe for 64 years, treasured mother of Sandy and Marcia; Susan and Alan; Bob; and Arlene. Grandmother of Andy and Frances; Lisa and Andrew; Carolyn; Jennifer and Evan; Jeff and Kathy. Greatgrandmother of Connor, Alyssa, Gabrielle and Emma. Sister of Phil and Lillian; Jules and Edith; Ida and Lou; Harry and Ida; Ben and Judy; Jeanette and Oscar. She will be missed dearly by hundreds of nieces, nephews and cousins. Services Thursday, August 29, 12 noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza (opposite the LIRR), Great Neck, NY, followed by interment at Mt. Ararat Cemetery.
        BERENS-Mary. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of the Fresh Meadow Country Club, record the passing of Mary Berens, wife of our esteemed member Abraham Berens. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Robert Fortunoff, President Roger Blumencranz, Secy.
