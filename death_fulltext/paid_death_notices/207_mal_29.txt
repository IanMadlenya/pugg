
        McREYNOLDS--Charles B., 87, of Newport News, VA, passed away Wednesday, March 31, 2004. Chuck was an architect with Becket International for over 40 years serving as the Director of the Los Angeles and New York offices and later President of Becket International. He is survived by his wife, Virginia McReynolds, and two daughters, Linda Little and Kimberly Merton. Visitation and Mass Sunday, April 4, at 5:30PM. For information: Peninsula Funeral Home, Newport News, VA.
