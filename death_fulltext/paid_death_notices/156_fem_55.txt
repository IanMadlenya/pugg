
        BROCKMAN-Roberta, M.D.. The Board of Trustees, medical staff and administration of the Hospital for Special Surgery mourn with profound sorrow the death of Roberta Brockman, M.D. As a resident and a member of the Attending Staff at HSS, she provided the very finest in orthopedic care to the hospital's patients. She will be greatly missed by all fortunate enough to have known her. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her mother Mary, her sisters Marilyn and Nancy, her brother James, and to her entire family. Richard L. Menschel, Chairman Aldo Papone, Co-Chairman Board of Trustees Russell F. Warren, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief John R. Reynolds, President and Chief Executive Officer
