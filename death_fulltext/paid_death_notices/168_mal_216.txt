
        WEINBERG-Honorable Sanford I. Acting Village Justice of Kings Point died suddenly on January 13, 2001. Beloved husband of Clara. Loving father of Elliot and Eric. Dear brother of Anita Sperber of Boston. He was a partner in the law firm of Batzer & Weinberg, P.C. in Rockville Centre and was of counsel to Mullooly, Jeffrey, Rooney & Flynn in Glen Cove. He served as an Acting Justice in the Village of Kings Point for the past 15 years and was a United States Standing Bankruptcy Trustee. Sandy was the former President of the L.I. Regional Board Anti Defamation League and active in the Republican Party, serving as former Zone Leader of Great Neck. He was also a former Assistant Town Attorney for the Town of North Hempstead. For over 25 years he was an active member of Temple Israel in Great Neck and was a former Trustee of the Mens Club, and former member of the Couples Club. He graduated with Honors from Suffolk University Law School in Boston and was a founding member of its Alumni Association, New York Chapter. Funeral service will be held at Riverside Nassau North Chapels at 10AM Wednesday, January 17 at 55 North Station Plaza in Great Neck, NY. Intermet at Mount Hebron Cemetery in Queens.
        WEINBERG-Sanford I., 53, passed away on Saturday, January 13, 2001. Loving husband to Clara, father of Elliot and Eric. A wonderful person leaving us all far too soon -we will miss you forever. Lori, Michael, Daniel, Brian.
