
        LINCER-William. The Juilliard School mourns the death of William Lincer distinguished member of the faculty since 1969 and one of the most prominent and respected violists of this century. At Juilliard he developed an extraordinarily strong class of violists who went on to become professionally active in solo, chamber and orchestral settings. All the members of the Juilliard community extend deepest condolences to his family. Mary Rodgers Guettel Chairman of the Board Joseph W. Polisi President The Juilliard School
