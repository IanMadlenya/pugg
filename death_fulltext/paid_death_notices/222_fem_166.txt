
        BREKSTONE--Candida Miele. 93, on July 27, 2005. Graduate of NYU School of Law and a successful real estate broker, she proudly owned her own agency, Hudson Valley Realty in Dobbs Ferry for over 40 years. Survived by daughter Barbara Carmenini of Dobbs Ferry, two grandchildren Candace Martin of Bronxville and Christopher Carmenini of Dobbs Ferry, two great grandchildren Lilly and Charlotte Martin, her brother Stephen Miele and sister Yolanda Flanagan. Her beloved husband Stephen died in 1999. Memorial Mass 9:30AM Monday, August 1, Our Lady of Pompeii Church, Dobbs Ferry. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Lung Association.
