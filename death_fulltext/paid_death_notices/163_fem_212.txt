
        HANSON-Muriel R. Died August 23, 2000 in Chatham, MA., at the age of 83. Born on July 7, 1917, she graduated from Vassar College in 1938. Her first marriage to Roberto Rendueles Alsina ended in divorce. She later married Donald P. Hanson, founder and publisher of Woman's Day magazine. After her marriage, she wrote a regular monthly column for Woman's Day and, for several years, served as a Features editor of the magazine. An active member of the Congregational Church, Mrs Hanson wrote a published history of the First Congregational Church of Ridgefield, Connecticut. For many years, she also served as a trustee of the Hammond Museum in North Salem, N.Y. She is survived by two sons, Lars P. Hanson and Donald P. Hanson Jr., a daughter Wendy Gimbel, a sister Margaret Milbrath and seven grandchildren.
