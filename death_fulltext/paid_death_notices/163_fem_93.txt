
        FONDILLER-Elizabeth. 6/29/26-8/ 11/00. Her words, her deeds and her choice of profession reflected her lifelong dedication to helping others achieve their goals and pursue their dreams. She began her career as an artist and art teacher. Her thirst for knowledge led her on to degrees in occupational therapy and social work. She was a woman of great strength, creativity, compassion, and intellectual depth and diversity. She leaves behind a loving daughter, Jill, many friends and relatives, and her infinite collection of chatchkes. She will be greatly missed by everyone whose life she touched. Betty, we love you. Funeral services will be held at Plaza Funeral Home, 91st and Amsterdam, Sunday, August 13th at 2:30PM.
