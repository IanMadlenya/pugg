
        TURI-Charles S., Born in 1916 in Szolnok, Hungary. Son of Kalman and Francesca Turi. Graduate of Pasman University with a Doctorate in Law. He left Hungary in 1939 with his brother George to avoid the Holocaust. During World War II, he served in the American Army. Upon his return to Hungary in 1945, he met his wife Ava and was married in 1946. In 1947 he formed the Charles Turi Jewelry Company, a manufacturer of fine jewelry that supplied the premier American retailers with European fashion and quality throughout his tenure. His company continues to operate successfully to this date. He is survived by his sons, George and Andrew, their wives, Susan and Judith, and grandchildren: Beth, Michael, Karen, Nicholas and Caitlin. His humanity, grace and spirit enriched all of his family and many dear friends. He will be missed by all. Service Sunday 11:00 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
