
        BLAKE-Melvin, D.D.S.. On March 14, 1999, at the age of 71, after a wonderful life that enriched all those who knew him. Mel was a superbly skilled specialist in both oral surgery and oral pathology and was an avid collector of fine art. A bright light has gone out. The family will miss his warmth and gentle manner. Memorial service Sunday, April 11, 3 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street.
