
        RABINOR--George, of Boca Raton, FL and formerly of Long Island, NY. He passed away peacefully at the age of 91 on July 22, 2003. Beloved husband of Terry Rabinor. Devoted father of Judith C. Kirschner, Arnold J. Rabinor, and Joan E. Rabinor, and devoted stepfather of Jennifer A. Serito. He is also survived by eight grandchildren and four great grandchildren. After graduating from C.C.N.Y. as a Phi Beta Kappa, he taught high school English and Speech for 13 yrs. He was admitted to the NY State Bar in 1934. Mr. Rabinor was an active developer of rental housing; he built a total of 528 single-family homes & 2044 apartments in NYC, NY State, PA, and FL. Services to be held Fri. at Sinai Memorial Chapel, Delray Bch., FL. >>AD#
