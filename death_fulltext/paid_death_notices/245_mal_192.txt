
        LEE--Joel S., distinguished scholar and brilliant Professor of Law, died April 15th 2007, at age 93. Following valiant service in the US Army in WWII, he earned his law degree at New York University. Satisfying his lifelong ambition to teach law, he joined the faculty at New York Law School. For 44 years he devoted his outstanding academic skills to the legal education of thousands of students. Many of his students became judges and successful attorneys. He is survived by his cousin, A.H. Lewis, and his devoted nurse and friend Patricia Standen. Contributions may be made to The New York Times Neediest Cases.
