
        SILVER--Melba. The adored and cherished mother of Ann, Erik, Owen and Adam. Melba loved and was loved by sister Lois, daughter-in-law Carlotta, and grandson Eli. Melba passed away in Boca Raton, FL. on March 1, 2004. She was a 50-year resident of Rye, N.Y., devoting many years of volunteer service to the City as President of the Rye Library and Chair of the Rye Planning Commission. In Boca Raton, she served as an active member of Planned Parenthood of South Palm Beach and Broward Counties, President of the Boca Raton Symphony and Chair of the Florida Philharmonic's Boca Raton Board of Directors. Melba was an active and dedicated alumnus of Cornell University, Class of 1947. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in memory of Melba Silver to Planned Parenthood of South Palm Beach and Broward Counties, 455 NW 35th St., Boca Raton, FL. 33431.
