
        VOGEL--George, DDS. Of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of New York, on November 30, 2004 at the age of 88. Beloved husband of Jean Stern Vogel and the late Ruth Vogel, father of Dr. Robert Vogel of NYC and Ronald Vogel of Bethsda, MD, father-in-law of Andrea and Eileen Vogel, loving grandfather of Daria, Evan, Geoffrey and Alec and brother of Estelle, Jeannette and Lewis. We will always remember him as a moral, ethical & righteous man. In lieu of flowers contributions to the American Cancer Society.
