
        KLEBANOW--Hy. August 14, 1915 to June 1, 2006. Gentleman, friend, mentor, a distinguished artist and founder of graphic arts company Kleb Associates, Inc., serving national accounts, he encouraged others to appreciate the joy of art and the art of seeing. Survived by a son and daughter, four grandchildren, two great-grandchildren. Private service for family, followed by exhibition of paintings and reception Sunday, June 4, 12:30 P.M., 500 N. Columbus Avenue, Mt. Vernon.
