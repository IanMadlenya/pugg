
        MISHKIN--Sara B. Cherished wife of Irving. Beloved mother of Barry and Marypat, Jeffrey and Rochelle, Debra and Gabriel Sganga and six grandchildren. Of all her accomplishments she was most dedicated to Hadassah where she was a member of the National Board for many years until health failed. Family visitation at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY on Tuesday evening, August 26, 7-9 PM. Services at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Valhalla, Wednesday, August 27 at 11 AM.
        MISHKIN--Sara. The National Board of Hadassah, The Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., mourns the passing of Sara Mishkin, our esteemed colleague. Our heartfelt condolences go to the entire family. May her memory be for a blessing. June Walker, Nat'l President
        MISHKIN--Sara. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member Sara Mishkin. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest sympathy to her husband Irving, her son and daughter-in law Barry and Mary Pat and her grandchildren Nina and Zoe. Jacqueline Bellsey, President Dale Winston, Secretary
