
        GREENBERG-Shirley (nee Levine). Of East Meadow, N.Y. Beloved wife of Leon for 53 years. Loving mother of Edward & Richard. Devoted sister to Ruth Ciline. Adored grandmother of Jonathan. Great friend and cherished mother-in-law of Janet. She will be remembered for her loving kindness, great pride in her family, a wonderful sense of humor. She will be missed by all. Services were held Wednesday, June 26th from the ''Riverside Chapel'', Amsterdam Avenue and West 76th Street, New York City.
