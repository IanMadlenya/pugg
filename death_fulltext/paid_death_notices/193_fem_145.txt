
        JOFFE-Grace Frankel, 92. Died peacefully February 17, 2003 in New Rochelle. Born in Manhattan, she lived in Flushing until 2001. Loving and adored sister, aunt and great-aunt. Predeceased by husbands William Springman and Isaac Joffe as well as two brothers and four sisters. Secretary at Bryant High School, Long Island City, for many years. Memorial service will be held in New Rochelle at a later date.
