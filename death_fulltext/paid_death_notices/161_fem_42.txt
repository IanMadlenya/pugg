
        KATZMAN-Karen E. The law firm of Kaye, Scholer, Fierman, Hays & Handler, LLP, mourns the untimely loss of their partner, colleague and friend, Karen E. Katzman, who died on Saturday, June 3, 2000. Ms. Katzman began her career at Kaye, Scholer in 1974 after graduating from the Harvard Law School. She was named a partner in the New York Litigation Department in 1983. In addition to the many contributions she made to the firm, Ms. Katzman was an active member and supporter of the Association of the Bar of the City of New York's Committee on Women and the Law and NOW, the National Organization of Women. The firm extends its deepest sympathies to the family and friends of Ms. Katzman. She will be missed.
        KATZMAN-Karen. The Board and Staff of NOW Legal Defense and Education Fund mourn the loss of our very good friend Karen Katzman. She brought gusto, a brilliant legal mind, commitment, creativity and great good humor to her work with NOW LDEF. Her generous and unflagging support for women's rights will be missed as much as her friendship. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family.
