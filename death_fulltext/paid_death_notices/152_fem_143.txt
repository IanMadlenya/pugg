
        LEVINE-Barbara Ann, of New City, NY, age 55, on September 17, 1999, after a long and courageous battle with ovarian cancer. Beloved wife of Larry; devoted mother of Robin, Jeffrey and Brian; cherished mother-in-law of Paul and Samantha; adored ''Baba'' of Jonah and Matthew; daughter of Estelle and the late Jack Vinikoor; and sister of Mark and Joel Vinikoor. An artist, an arts educator, Barbara loved her family, friends and work. She shared her knowledge and talent with boundless energy, enthusiasm and passion. Services will be held Sunday, September 19, at 10 AM, at Hellman Memorial Chapel, Spring Valley, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to SHARE, 1501 Broadway, Ste 1720, NY, NY 10036, would be greatly appreciated.
