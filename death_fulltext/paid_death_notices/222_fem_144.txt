
        FAYMAN--Edythe. Died peacefully, July 24, 2005. She was 87. Beloved wife of the late Leo, devoted mother of Stanley (Dianne), Elaine (Martin), cherished grandmother of Noah, Shayna (Brett). Past President of Sisterhood of Golden Lakes Temple and honored volunteer at the Jewish Community Center. Funeral service at 1:45 PM, Tuesday, July 26th, at Menorah Gardens, West Palm Beach, FL.
