
        PRATT-Richardson, Jr. Board and staff of the Federation of Protestant Welfare Agencies, Inc. mourn the loss of our Board member, friend and supporter, Richardson Pratt, Jr. He served on our Board for 29 years. We will miss his extraordinary leadership, generosity and unwavering commitment to the well-being of children and families. Jerry will remain in our hearts and in our thughts. He was very gentle and always willing to do his part to advance the mission of the federation. We wish to express our deepest condolences to his wife Jan and the rest of the family.
