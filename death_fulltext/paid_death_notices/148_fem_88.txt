
        LANCHESTER-Elizabeth Ann, 82, formerly of New York City and London, England, on May 11, 1999. Wife of the late Pilot Officer Henry Robert Lanchester, who was killed in action in 1941. She is survived by a son, Robert Lanchester, of Princeton, NJ, a daughter Christine Deirdre Champagne, of Littleton, NH, six grandchildren and a great grandson. Funeral service 4 P.M. Sunday May 16, 1999 at Trinity Church, Princeton, NJ.
