
        CHIA-Dewey Teh-huai, 86. New York University Medical Center extends its sincerest condolences to our esteemed colleague and trustee, Pei-yuan Chia, on the passing of his father. Born in Shanghai, China, in 1912, Dewey Teh-huai Chia came to the United States in 1956, where he established an international shipping & trading business. We also extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Kitty Shun-hua; daughters, Pei-hsin and Pei-loh; daughter-inlaw, Frances; sons-in-law, David Gedalecia & Adrian Lo, and seven grandchildren. Noel L. Cohen, M.D. Interim Dean and Provost NYU Medical Center
