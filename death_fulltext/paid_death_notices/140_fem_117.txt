
        NADOOLMAN LYONS-Julia. Age 40. Died Monday. She was born July 13, 1958 in Manhattan, New York. A certified public accountant, she was a 14 year resident of North Las Vegas. She is survived by her husband Keith of North Las Vegas, parents Jesse and Beatrice Nadoolman of New York City, and sister Joanna Nadoolman of Kerhonkson, NY. Memorial services will be 3:30PM Tuesday in Bunkers Eden Vale Mortuary. Memorial donations can be made to the American Heart Association or Vietnam Veterans of America, Chapter 17, Las Vegas, NV 89030.
