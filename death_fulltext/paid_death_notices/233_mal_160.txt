
        MORRIS--Joseph. Long Island resident and Palm Beach snowbird. Father, husband, attorney, judge, Republican, Bayshore Jewish Center President, Cold Spring Valley Tennis Club founder, Union College and NYU Law School graduate. He loved tennis, bridge, his sons Jeff and Bob, wife Ethel now deceased, and sister Sylvia. He liked everyone he met and always kept a song in his heart along with a package of Sweet & Low in his wallet.
