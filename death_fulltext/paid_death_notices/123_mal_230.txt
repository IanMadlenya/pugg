
        McBEAN-Peter. It is with great sadness that we acknowledge the death of Peter McBean, late of San Francisco and Newport, Knight of Grace in The Priory in the United States of The Most Venerable Order of the Hospital of St. John of Jerusalem, awarded by Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II. Peter was a good friend, loyal supporter and Governor of The American Society for almost forty years. We shall miss him. To his wife Nancy, and his daughters Judith, Sheila, and Edith we send our condolences and very best wishes. John R. Drexel, IV, K. St. J., Prior G. William Haas, K. St. J., Chancellor Don W. Lundquist, K. St. J., Secretary
