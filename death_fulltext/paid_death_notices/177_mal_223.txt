
        JACOBSON-Harold G., M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of our Distinguished Professor Emeritus of Radiology, who served our medical school with great devotion since 1963. He was chairman of our Department of Radiology from 1972 to 1985, and was renowned worldwide for his many contributions to the advancement of radiology. In 1985, he was designated as a ''Living Legend'' in the field by the American College of Radiology. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz, Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
