
        BLUM-Ray. Died Sunday, March 25, age 97. A gifted and creative handworker, homemaker, musician and gardener. Widow of Joseph G., beloved mother of Robert Peter. She is survived by her loving daughter Judy, cherished grandchildren Jennifer, Lesley and Joey, and daughter-in-law Franny. Memorial contributions may be made to the New York Botanical Gardens, 200th St. and Kazimiroff Blvd., Bronx, NY 10458, attention: Dr. Gregory Long.
