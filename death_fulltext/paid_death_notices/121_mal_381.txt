
        VAN DYKE-Frank. The Board of Directors and staff of Citizens' Committee for Children of New York mourn the passing of former Board Member and member Frank Van Dyke. Mr. Van Dyke's dedication to children and the issues they face in NYC made him an especially valuable and impassioned advocate on their behalf. Edythe First, Chairman Martha Olson, President Gail Nayowith, Exec. Director
