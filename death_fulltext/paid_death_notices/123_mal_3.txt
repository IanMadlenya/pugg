
        ROTH-Murray. On March 30, 1997. Loving brother of Lou Roth and Evelyn Fishman. Uncle of Marvin, Fran, Nelson, Nancy and Judy Roth, Neal and Enid Fishman and Charles Goodstein and numerous grand and great nephews and nieces and friends. He set the standard for all of us to strive for. Formerly with the Internal Revenue Service and a well known tax and corporate attorney. Services Wednesday, 12:30PM, ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions may be made in his memory to his favorite charity, The Sylvia Roth Memorial Scholarship Fund at Stern College for Women.
        ROTH-Murray. The Brooklyn Law School community notes with sadness the passing of Murray Roth, a member of the Class of 1960 and a devoted alumnus. His unflagging spirit and good cheer will be missed. We extend our condolences to his family. Paul Windels, Jr. Chmn. of the Board of Trustees Joan G. Wexler, Dean
        ROTH-Murray. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi Hermann Merkin, Founding Pres. David Yagoda, Chairman William Schwartz, President
        ROTH-Murray. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our devoted member Murray Roth. Sincere condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
        ROTH-Murray. The lawyers and staff of Maguire, Kehl, & Nealon, LLP, note the passing of our dear friend, who enriched our lives as a member of our extended professional family for many years. As a lawyer of distinction and conviction, and as a warm and generous person, we will miss him.
        ROTH-Murray. Men's Club of Park East Synagogue is saddened by the passing of our devoted Board Member, Murray Roth. The entire Board extends its condolences to the bereaved family. Allan Goldberg, President Herman Hochberg, Chairman
