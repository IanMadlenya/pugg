
        LISTFIELD--Helen, 80, of Boynton Beach, FL, died on March 6th, 2007. Beloved wife of 60 years to Walter. Loving mother of the late Michael, Jamie and Patrice, Amy and Ron, devoted grandmother of Adam, Amanda, and Matthew, dear sister of Joan. Helen was a beautiful lady who dedicated her life to her family and was a wonderful friend. She will be missed by all. Memorial donations to American Cancer Society or City of Hope.
