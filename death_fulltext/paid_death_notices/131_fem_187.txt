
        LINZER-Estelle. The Board of Directors and Staff of the United Nations Association of the U.S.A. are deeply saddened at the passing of Estelle Linzer, a Vice Chair of the Association. Ms. Linzer was a longtime leader of our Association who worked with Eleanor Roosevelt to build public support for the United Nations. Her lively intelligence and commitment to our cause will be sorely missed. We extend very sincere condolences to her family and friends. Alvin P. Adams, President UNA-USA
