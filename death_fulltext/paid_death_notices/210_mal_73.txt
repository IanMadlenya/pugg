
        LEOPOLD--Edward Philip, born May 15, 1913, died peacefully July 5, 2004. Beloved husband of Margaret. Devoted father of Jackie (Norman), Leslie (Arthur), and Paul (Karen). Adored grandfather of Jonathan, Karen, Stacy and Erica. Poppy instinctively knew how to fix, mend, and solve everything. He was precise and meticulous and moral to the core. His sharp and insightful wisdom will be missed by all and will be in our hearts forever. Memorial services Thursday, July 8, at 10 AM, at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills.
