
        SACHS--Charles. With a deep sense of sadness we extend our sincere condolences to the family of Charles Sachs who passed away May 7th. A devoted husband, father and dear friend to all. He was Secretary of the Union Mutual Benefit Association for 20 years and Trustee of the Allied Educational Foundation for a decade. His devotion to improve the life of every person with whom he came in contact will be remembered. Union Mutual Benefit Association Allied Educational Foundation
