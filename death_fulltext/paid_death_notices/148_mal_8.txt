
        DENSON-Lawrence J., M.D. Beloved son of the late Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Denson. Beloved brother of Ruth Denson Cohen, Harold Denson, and the late Allan I. Denson. Services to be held at Gutterman-Musicant Funeral Home, Hackensack, NJ, Monday, April 3, at 10:30 AM. Interment at Riverside Cemetery, Saddle Brook, NJ.
        DENSON-Lawrence J., M.D. The Trustees, Board of Advisors and staff of the Frances L. and Edwin L. Cummings Memorial Fund are saddened by the death of Dr. Lawrence J. Denson, our beloved and highly esteemed friend. We are grateful for his astute and conscientious leadership as founding Chairman of our Board of Advisors since 1983. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family.
