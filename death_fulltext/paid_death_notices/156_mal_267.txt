
        WATERS-Joseph. To Our ''Grandpa Joe'', the expanse of your warmth, empathy and tenacity nurtured us from birth into adulthood. The sweetness of your smile and the power of your goodness provided a dazzling model for all that one would hope to be. We constantly felt valued and loved by you. Thank you for that gift of love. We will hold it inside our hearts and throughout our generations. Forever Love, Your Grandchildren Stephanie, Michael, Haley, Gregg, Courtney and Jamie
        WATERS-Joseph, of Verona, NJ, on Sunday, Jan. 16, at the age of 82. Beloved husband of Ruth (nee Ostroff). Devoted father of Susan Simon of Montclair, NJ and Valerie Granet of Madison, NJ. Dear brother of Paul Waters and Sally Entin, both of Verona, NJ. Loving grandfather of five grandchildren. Services at Jewish Memorial Chapel, 841 Allwood Rd., Clifton, NJ on Wednesday at 12:00 P.M.
        WATERS-Joseph. It is with extreme sorrow that we mourn the passing of Joe Waters, one of the nicest, most decent, honorable men on this earth. It was a privelege to know him and to know Joe was to love him. We've been together for more than fifty years. We miss him already. Our deepest sympathies to his wife, Ruth, his children, Susan, Valerie, Joel and Roger and his very special grandchildren. Hoberman, Miller, Goldstein and Lesser
        WATERS-Joseph. The members, Board of Governors and officers of the Preakness Hills Country Club mourn the loss of a good friend and fellow member of long standing and extend their deepest sympathies to his wife, Ruth, and the family. Melvin Fand, President
