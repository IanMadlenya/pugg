
        MESSINGER-Alex. The Board of Governors and members of Brae Burn Country Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Alex Messinger. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife, Deana, and his entire family. Leon Fassler, President Monty Davis, Secretary
        MESSINGER-Alex. Of Palm Beach, FL, and Larchmont, NY. Beloved wife of Deana. Devoted father of Lawrence and Richard. Loving father-in-law of Bonnie and Janice. Brother of Bernice Rosenzweig, Sylvia Purisch and Eddie Messinger. Special grandfather of David, Melisa, Robert and Scott. This proud and honorable man will be missed by all who knew him. Services will be held Tuesday, 12 noon at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY.
