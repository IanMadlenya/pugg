
        ZUSS-Michael E. Beloved husband, father, and educator died November 7, age 82. Dr. Zuss received his Ph.D. in philosophy at the Graduate Center, CUNY, at the age of 70, and was a member of the Phi Beta Kappa Society. An active scholar of religious and classical philosophy, he was devoted to the advancement of knowledge and his many students. He is survived by his wife, Ruth and his sons, Robert and Mark.
