
        GOMEZ-REINOSO-Teofilo I. August 4, 2000. A native of Cuba, he was both a noted lawyer and judge. A graduate of New York University, where he received his masters degree in philosophy. He is survived by his brother Manuel Gomez-Reinoso. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday, August 7, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, August 8, 10 AM, St. Thomas More Church, East 89th St, NYC. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
