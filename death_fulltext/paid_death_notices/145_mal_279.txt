
        BYRNE-Leo Gerald, 79, of Tucson, passed away February 23, 1999. Survived by wife, Antoinette Marie; parents, Patrick and Elizabeth; daughters, Geraldine DiazPeterson, Elizabeth Keswick and Kathleen Azad; son, Michael Byrne. Also survived by nine grandchildren. 4th degree Knights of Columbus, Mr. Byrne attended Fordham University in New York and later attained his Master's Degree in Theology in 1987 at St. Vincent de Paul Seminary in Florida. He will be greatly missed.
