
        TAFFEL-Alexander. ''A man beloved, a man elect of men.'' peacfully at home, on January 19, 1997. Principal of the Bronx High School of Science 1958-1978. Adored husband of Mildred Gottlieb Taffel, dear brother of Bess Taffel Boyle, Dr. Max Taffel and the late Dr. Abram Taffel. Loving uncle to his many nieces and nephews, grandnieces and grand nephews; to his friends and family, a treasure. All will miss him grievously. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 81st and Madison, Sunday, February 2, 1997, at 2:30PM. In lieu of flowers donate to a charity of your choice.
