
        PSYHOS -- Helen, 90, on March 22, 2004 in California. Helen and her husband Sarantos owned Windsor Florists in Manhattan until 1980 when they retired to Florida. Preceded in death by her husband and son Sarantos, Jr., she is survived by daughter Elenitsa Fields, granddaughter Marika Rice, sister Evelyn Patsalos and many nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Riverdale Funeral Home, 5044 Broadway, Tuesday & Wednesday, 2-4 & 7-9 PM. Services at Greek Orthodox Cathedral, 319 E. 74th St., Thursday, April 1 at 10 AM.
