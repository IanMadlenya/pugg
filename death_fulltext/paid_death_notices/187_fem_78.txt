
        MILLER-Rosalind S, retired professor of social work at Columbia University, died peacefully at home on August 8th at age 79. Devoted wife of the late Edwin Haviland Miller, beloved mother of Pamela Miller Ness, mother-in-law of Paul Marc Ness and cousin of Bernard D. Tunik. She received a BA in English from Pennsylvania State University, MA in English from Columbia University and MSW from Simmons College School of Social Work. For many years she was active as a member of The Board of Trustess of the Allentown Art Museum. Burial was private. A memorial is planned for a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Allentown Art Museum, 5th & Court Street, Allentown, PA 18105.
