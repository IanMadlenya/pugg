
        BENDER--Dr. Israel B. The Manhattan School of Music is saddened by the loss of Dr. Israel B. Bender, beloved father of Susan Bender Blumstein, the school's Vice President External Affairs. Manhattan School of Music is very grateful to Dr. Bender for his generous support of its annual interdisciplinary symposia on historically significant composers, and for his lifetime commitment to music and the arts. We extend our most sincere and heartfelt condolences to Susan and all members of the Bender family. Marta Istomin, President David A. Rahm, Chairman Board of Trustees >>AD#
