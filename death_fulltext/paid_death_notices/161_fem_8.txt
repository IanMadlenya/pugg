
        RICHARDS-Betty Jenney. Widow of Richard Draper Richards. Passed away peacefully at the age of 91 after several years of declining health. Mother of James, Anthony, and Frederick Ayer. Stepmother of Toni King and Tristram Fearon. Grandmother of Jenney Petrikin, Peter and James Ayer, Elizabeth Lee, Andrew, Alexander and Estelle Ayer. Step-grandmother of Clarence H. King, III, Toni King Callahan, Jed and Trevor Fearon and eleven great-grandchildren. Services will be held on Thursday, June 8 at 10:30AM at St. Luke's Church, Easthampton, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory to St. Luke's Church, Easthampton.
