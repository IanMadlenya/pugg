
        SOKOLOW - Joseph. The Yeshiva University family is grieved at the passing of a distinguished alumnus, a highly regarded business professional, and Jewish communal leader who served as President of the Yeshiva College Alumni for many years. Heartfelt condolences are extended to his beloved wife, Hannah, and to their children, Judith and Dr. Moshe Sokolow, the Fanya Gottesfeld Heller Professor of Jewish Education, Zipporah and Leslie Friedman, and Nechama Sokolow, and to his grandchildren. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry Weiss, Chairman, Board of Trustees
