
        GOLDRING-Charles. The officers and members of the National Commission and staff of the AntiDefamation League mourn the passing of Charles Goldring, 90, of Los Angeles, a great national leader and longtime devoted supporter. It was his foresight and determination that inspired, initiated and built ADL's Planned Giving program. Charlie was a man of warmth, wit and wisdom and he will be sorely missed. We extend out heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Leona, and the entire family. David H. Strassler National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
