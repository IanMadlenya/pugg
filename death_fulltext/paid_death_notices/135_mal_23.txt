
        BONNER-James C. Age 50, of Fairfield, CT, died on April 2, 1998 in New York Hospital, New York City. Beloved husband of Susan Whidden Bonner and loving father of Christopher and Ellie Bonner. Brother to Peter Bonner of Swansea, MA, Nancy Ceccarelli of Marshfield, MA, Genny Pearce of Fairfield, CT., Susan Paden of Newark, DE, Sarah O'Neil of St. Simons, GA., and Margaret Bonner of Dallas, TX. Mr. Bonner was President and Senior Partner of Area Marketing Associates in New York City. A Memorial Service will be held on Monday, April 6, 1998 at 11 AM at Norfield Congregational Church, 64 Norfield Road, Weston, CT. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made in his memory to: The Fund for Blood and Cancer Research, New York Hospital, 525 East 68th Street, New York, New York 10021.
