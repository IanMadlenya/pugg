
        FLYNN-Mary E. Died January 26, 2001, age 93. A native and real New Yorker. Beloved sister of the late John, James and Daniel. Beloved aunt of Patricia, John and James. Mass of the Resurrection at the Church of St. Paul the Apostle, 415 W. 59 St., NYC, on Tuesday, January 30 at 10 AM. Interment to follow at Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent in her name to St. Paul's Church.
