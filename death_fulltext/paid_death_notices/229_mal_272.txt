
        STILLMAN--Israel H. In his 93rd year. Adoring husband and soulmate of Leona, married for 66 wonderful years. Loving father of Celine and Pascal and Tom and Kathy. Grandfather of very special granddaughter Isabelle. A man of compassion, generosity and humor. Always true to himself, he lived an enviable life to the very end. We will miss him. Donations to: Jewish Guild for Blind. Memorial service: Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave at 91 St, Thursday February 23 at 12:15PM.
