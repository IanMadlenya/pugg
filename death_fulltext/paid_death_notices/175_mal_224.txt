
        HALKEDIS-Theodore. August 21, 2001. Devoted son of the late John and the late Simela Halkedis. Beloved husband of Aristea. Devoted brother of Elpis Kyriazis and Haralambos Halkedis. Loving uncle of Arthur and John Kyriazis, Semele, John and Anne Halkedis. Born in Philadelphia and graduated from Pennsylvania State University and later served in the U.S. Army. He spent his lifetime career in the international shipping industry. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st. St. Thursday, August 23, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral service Friday, August 24, 12 noon at the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74 St. between First and Second Avenues. The family will accept flowers at Frank E. Campbell or contributions to a charity of your choice.
