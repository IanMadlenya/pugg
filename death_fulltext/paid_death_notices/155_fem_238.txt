
        MITCHELL-Frances Eliot Heiner, 69. Beloved sister, cousin and friend. Alumna of Chapin and St. Timothy's Schools and Barnard College. Longtime member and sometime president of the Snarks theater club for women. Mourned by her brother and sister-in-law Dennis and Helena Heiner, cousin Gordon Heiner, other cousins and many friends. Buried from Our Saviour Church. Blessed are the peacemakers: for they shall be called the children of God.
