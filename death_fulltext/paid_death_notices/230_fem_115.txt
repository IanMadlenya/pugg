
        COHAN -- Blanche Billow. March 10, 2006, two days after her 93rd birthday. She stayed for the party and now has joined her beloved husband Leo. Dear sister of Selma and Muriel. Loving mother of Mike and Carol, Susan and Ken, grandmother of Edward and Patrice, Peter and Melissa, Sandy and Rob. Greatgrandmother of Lee, Olivia, Luke, Hadara and Molly. Private service and interment March 12. Donations to Temple Shaaray Tefila, 250 East 79 St, NYC; Jewish Museum of NY; and NARAL.
