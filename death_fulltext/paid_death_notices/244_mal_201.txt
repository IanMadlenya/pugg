
        ZICKEL--Colonel Lewis L., died May 13, 2007, 80 years old. He was a Korean War hero and an alumnus of The U.S. Military Academy at West Point. He spent many years devoting his time and skills as a Structural and Architectural Engineer to many projects at the Academy. He was buried at West Point on May 15, 2007. He is survived by his brother, Robert, his wife, Jael, four children and 12 grandchildren.
