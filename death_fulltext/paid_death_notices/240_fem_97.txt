
        LEICHTLING--Selma Roslyn, wife of the late Dr. Julius J. Leichtling, passed away peacefully at her home. Graduating from Vassar College, she became a life long devotee of the theatre as a casting agent, producer and executive administrator of the Actors Studio, New York, in its heyday. She will be remembered as ever vivacious, outspoken and elegant by her sisters, Edith Greenfield and Deanie Gordon, her children Dr. Jonathan Leichtling and Jane Ives and her very beloved grandchildren, Justine, Noah, Zachary and Jake. Services Sunday 11:30 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
