
        MURAD-Rachel. Beloved mother of six; cherished grandmother of 11 and adored great-grandmother of 20. Passed away on April 20, 2000. Rachel was born in 1906 in Baghdad and immigrated to NYC in 1946. She was married for 74 years to Albert Murad who preceded her in death in 1996. Her memory will be cherished and her legacy to all who knew her was her strength of character and desire for all to be well. Donations in Rachel's memory would be appreciated to the Albert Murad Educational Fund, Park Avenue Synagogue, 50 E. 87th St, NY, NY 10128.
