
        DONOVAN-Joan D., 76. Died September 17, in Scottsdale, AZ. She was preceded in death by her beloved husband of 47 years, Dr. Raymond J. She was born in Queens, NY, graduated from Woodhaven High School, the College of New Rochelle, and received her Master's Degree from Columbia University. She was a teacher in the NYC school system and later an office manager in her husband's medical practice. She also served as a Eucharistic Minister at The Franciscan Renewal Center in Paradise Valley. She is survived by three loving sons, Raymond G., Thomas C., Gerard J., and five grandchildren. Graveside memorial service to be held at the Most Holy Trinity Cemetery in East Hampton, LI, at 11 AM, on Thursday, October 10, 2002.
