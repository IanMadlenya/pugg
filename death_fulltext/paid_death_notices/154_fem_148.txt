
        WEBSTER-Frances Brown. On Nov. 11 at Kendal at Hanover in Hanover, NH, of a pulmonary embolism. Beloved wife of the late Beveridge Webster, pianist and teacher at the Julliard School of Music, who died June 30, 1999. Survived by their daughter Wendy Webster Ardizzone and son Michael Webster, her sister Charlotte Wheeler, five nieces and nephews, and six grandchildren. Mrs. Webster was a cellist, pianist, violist and singer and taught piano for many years. She was principal cellist of the Westchester Symphony and participated in chamber music programs at Bennington College, VT and Mohonk, NY. Born on Jan. 24, 1915 in Riverhead, NY, Mrs. Webster received degrees from Oberlin College and the Oberlin Conservatory of Music and her Master's from Radcliffe. Her family will miss her loving and caring heart and her wonderful smile and sense of humor. They wish to express their deepest gratitude to the medical staff and caregivers at Kendal who have given Franny such loving care since her stroke on Nov. 30, 1997. A memorial service will be held at Kendal on Sunday, Dec. 12 at 3:00pm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Joy of Music Program, 112 Morningside Rd., Worcester, MA 01602, a non-profit community music school founded and directed by the Websters' daughter.
