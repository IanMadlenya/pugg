
        KIRSHEN-Philip H. On March 24, 2001. Devoted husband of Irene. Devoted father of Nina and Janice and grandfather of five. Services private.
        KIRSHEN-Philip. The Auxiliary of Montefiore Medical Center is saddened by the passing of Philip Kirshen, a devoted member. Our deepest sympathy goes to his wife Irene, who has headed our I and R service for over 30 years, and to his daughters Nina and Janice, and the grandchildren.
