
        GURSEL-Erol, M.D. On July 20, 2001. He was born in Istanbul, Turkey, June 20, 1931. He grew up in Turkey and came to the U.S. in 1961. He attended medical school in Istanbul and continued his medical education in New York. He completed great contributions to medicine. He is survived by his loving wife Sevgi, son Ali, daughter Sumru, son-in-law Ahmet, daughter-in-law Diane, grandson Malik and granddaughter Sevgi. He was a giving and devoted person to all. While he will be greatly missed his legacy will live on through the lives he touched. A memorial service was held at Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, White Plains, New York, on July 22.
