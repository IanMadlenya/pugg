
        GREENE-Henry Dr. Renaissance man thrived unchallenge. Hank Green (Dr. Henry Greene), internationally known artist and performer, died of prostate cancer. He served with distinction during WWII as Commander of an LCT landing craft. Hank earned a doctorate in education and served as assistant principal at Tilden High School for 36 years. He was truly a renaissance man. He was a highly acclaimed square dance caller, professional singer and band leader. Hank was also a published poet, artist and sculptor, whose works were displayed for years in well known art galleries. He was an outstanding athlete, excelling at basketball, baseball and tennis and rode a unique ''Side By Side'' bicycle with his wife Charlotte throughout Europe. Most recently, Hank achieved success as a professional magician performing on such prestigious cruise lines as the QEII. Hank is survived by Charlotte, his wife of 58 years, and two sons, Gregory Greene and Dr. Richard Greene. In lieu of flowers, donations can be sent to a research foundation dedicated to the treatment and eventual cure of prostate cancer-addressed to: Dr. Cy Stein, Columbia University, 630 W. 168 St, New York, NY 10032
