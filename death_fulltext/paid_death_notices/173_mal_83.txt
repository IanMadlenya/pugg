
        LIEGEY-Jean Richard. 72, died June 3, 2001. Born in Far Rockaway, NY, formerly of Stamford, CT, and a resident of Plantation, FL. He is survived by his wife of 49 years, Angela, and their four children, four grandchildren, and many brothers and sisters. A Funeral Mass was held on June 6, 2001, at St. Gregory Church in Plantation. A memorial service will be held in New York City in the fall.
