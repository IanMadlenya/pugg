
        LIEBERMAN--Fred, died at the Maine Medical Center on the morning of January 27, 2007. He will be remembered for his unwavering love and commitment to his two children. He was their best friend, their coach, and beloved father. He is survived by his mother Helen. Services Tuesday, 12pm, at ''Gutterman's Inc'' 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre. Interment at Mount Ararat Cemetery.
