
        CARRINGTON--Dr. John F. Died on May 11, 2006. Graduate of The College of the Holy Cross, Class of 1959. Dr. Carrington practiced orthopedic surgery in Brooklyn, NY, until his retirement. He is survived by his wife Barbara, five children and two grandchildren. Visitation is Monday, 6-9 pm, Tuesday 2-5 and 6-9 pm, at St. Gertrude RC Church, Rockaway, NY. Mass of Resurrection, Wednesday, 10:30 am. Interment to follow at Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY. For more information contact Dennis S. O'Connor Funeral Home at 718-634-4011. In lieu of flowers please send donations to ''The Scholarship Student Fund,'' The College of the Holy Cross, Worcester, MA.
