
        HOLMES-John Greer. Died at his home in East Lyme, CT, Sunday morning, January 19. He was 84 and died peacefully in his sleep, said Lyn Austin of Music-Theatre Group. Mr. Holmes was Chairman of the Board of Music-Theatre Group for several years. He represented inspired leadership and will be deeply missed, said Ms. Austin. Mr. Holmes is survived by his wife Ruth Lord, his four children and nine grandchildren.
