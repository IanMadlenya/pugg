Funeral Service on Monday, at 10 A.M., ''Plaza Memorial Chapel'', 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        TEMEL-Max. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Max Temel, a courageous survivor of the Holocaust and Riga ghetto. His beloved son, Charles Temel, an outstanding leader in the Jewish Community, is a board member of the Jewish Community Relations Council of New York, a UJA-Federation beneficiary, and a former chair of UJA-Federation's Manhattan Division and Wall Street Planning Board. We send our deepest condolences to Charles; his wife, Judy Wesalo Temel; and the entire family. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P. Jeffrey R. Solomon, C.O.O.
