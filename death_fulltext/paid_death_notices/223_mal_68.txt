
        ROSENZWEIG--Ely. 88, beloved husband of the late Barbara, devoted father of Sue, Larry, Charles, Ellen and Jodi, cherished grandfather of Amy, Mike, Brad, Katie, Matthew, Alex, Jackie, Daniel and Ben, great-grandfather of Ian and loving brother-inlaw and uncle, died on August 5, 2005.
