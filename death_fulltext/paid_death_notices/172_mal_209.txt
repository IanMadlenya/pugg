
        HACHMANN-Beverly K. On Wednesday, May 16th, 2001, of Solomons, Md. Beloved husband of Patricia P. Hachmann of 53 years, and loving father of Joanne P. Evans, John, Jeffrey, and Janet Parker. He is also survived by nine grandchildren and two step-grandchildren. He was a retired Director of Labor Relations for Chiquita International and a World War II veteran, serving in the U.S. Army. All services were private. Contributions can be made in his memory to a charity of your choice.
