
        DOBO-Francis Ferenc (Frank). Passed away peacefully on Wednesday, June 3, succumbing in his 90th year to a protracted illness. With him at the time in his Manhattan apartment was his wife and loving companion of 56 years, Bita Dobo (nee Sulzberger). In addition, he is survived by his son Michael of Culver City, CA. Memorial Service is pending.
