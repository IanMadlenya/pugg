
        WALDRON-Robert E. Age 67. On Nov. 15th, 1998. CEO of Waldron Associates Ltd. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Father of Eric, Clifford, Robert E. Jr. and Susanne. Grandfather of six. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, on Thursday from 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. with service Friday, at 11:30 A.M. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        WALDRON-Robert E. The Board of Directors of the Sovereign Apartments deeply regrets the loss of its colleague, fellow board member and friend and expresses its sincerest condolences to his wife, children and family. The Board shall sorely miss his counsel. Donald C. Moss, President
