
        BERMAN-Eunice T. Beloved wife of Ronald Berman, daughter of Walter Soffer, loving mother of Theodore and Erica, died on March 9, 2000 after a long battle with breast cancer. Eunice was a Managing Director and group head of Taxable Credit Research at J.P. Morgan Securities. During her 30 year Wall Street career, she touched the hearts of everyone who knew her and influenced hundreds of people in the financial services, electric utility, telephone, and natural gas industries. Eunice was a Chartered Financial Analyst and a member of the New York Society of Security Analysts, the Association for Investment Management and Research, the Financial Women's Association, the Wall Street Utility Group, and the Fixed Income Analysts Society, of which she was a founding member, and a member of Congregation Sons of Israel and Barnert Temple. She provided an example of excellence, both professionally and personally, and inspired countless numbers of people to achieve their ultimate potential. The funeral will be held on Sunday, March 12, 2000 at ''The Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 180 West 76th Street, New York at 10A.M. Shiva will be observed on Sunday, 7-9P.M. and Monday through Thursday, 2-9P.M. at her residence, 150 West 56 St., apartment 6703, New York. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Eunice Foundation, C/O Ronald Berman, 150 West 56 Street, New York, NY 10019.
