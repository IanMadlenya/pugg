
        SCHNEIDER-Irien (nee Knapp) parishioner of the Church of the Incarnation on Monday, June 2, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Mark Lockyer. Dear sister of Cathryn Henderer of Paramus, NJ, Mary Tietgen of Chester, NY and John Knapp of Moonachie, NJ. Much loved aunt and great aunt to 25 nieces and nephews. She was a lifetime resident of New York City. Before retiring in 1988, she was a loan officer with Citibank, Madison Avenue and 42nd Street, New York City. She was a member of the Union Club of New York City. Service at 8:30 P.M. on Wednesday at Vander Platt Memorial Home, S113 Farview Avenue, Paramus. Family will receive their friends Wednesday 78:30 p.m. Internment Forty Fort Cemetery, Forty Fort, PA, 12:00 p.m. Thursday.
