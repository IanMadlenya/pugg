
        WIENER--Sidney, on August 27th, 2004. Beloved husband of Shirley. Loving father of Helen Beth Friedlander, Alan Wiener, Bunny Schorr and Kenneth. Adored grandfather of Robert, Deborah, Justin, Jeremy, David, Deborah Anne, Brian, Amy and Kevin. Former National President of Bnai Zion. May his memory be for a blessing. Services today, 12 noon, at ''BoulevardRiverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI.
        WIENER--Sidney. Bnai Zion mourns the loss of our past President. An extraordinary man whose selflessness and kind heart helped to write a glorious chapter in the history of Bnai Zion and the State of Israel. During his lifetime he helped to build and strengthen vital institutions that aided the rebuilding of the Jewish State. He served as a beacon of hope and an inspiration to thousands of people throughout the world. His overwhelming generosity will benefit the people of Israel for generations to come. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, children, brother, grandchildren and other members of the family. Michael J. Lazar National President George W.
