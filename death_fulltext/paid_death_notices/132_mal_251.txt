
        O'LEARY-Rev. William A. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church, 94-40 188th St, Richmond Hill, January 17. Reposing LeahyMcDonald Funeral Home, 111-02 Atlantic Avenue, Richmond Hill, Tuesday, January 20, from 2 to 4:30 and 7 to 9 PM. Funeral Mass in St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church, Wednesday, Jan. 21, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev Thomas V.
