
        RUBIN-Maxwell A. On July 4, 1997, age 86, after a brief illness. Beloved husband and best friend of Hyacinth. Devoted father of Janet and Lois. Loving grandfather of Heather and Dustin. Cherished brother of Rose Krefetz. Services Sunday, July 6, at noon, at the Riverside Nassau Chapel in Great Neck. He lived his life with dignity and love, and will be missed.
