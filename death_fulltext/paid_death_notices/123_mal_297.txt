
        DEFEO-Philip L. On April 25, 1997 after a long illness. Dearest husband of Genevieve B. (McTague/ Carr). Beloved son of the late Philip L. and Anna K. (Schlott). Respected and beloved uncle, friend and benefactor to Teresa M. McTague and Philip Stahlman and their family and Thomas J. McTague. Served proudly in the U.S. Navy in WWII. Salesman extraordinaire for twenty-seven years for American Bank Note Company. Phil was a mentor to the younger salesmen who worked with him and was respected by his colleagues. He was a handsome, charming and intelligent man who was admired by all who knew him. He endured his long illness with grace, dignity and humor. It was a pleasure and honor to know him. He will be missed but not forgotten. Visiting Saturday and Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, McLaughlin & Sons, Third Avenue at 97 Street, Brooklyn. Interment Monday, 10:30AM, Maple Grove Cemetery.
