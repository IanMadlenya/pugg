
        WEINBERG-Samuel. Of Dobbs Ferry, died on July 30th. Retired publisher for H.B.J. Publishing. Survived by his loving wife Muriel, and devoted children Beth Rose of Boston, MA., and Adam Weinberg of Dobbs Ferry. Service 12 noon today Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave. Dobbs Ferry (exit 17 Saw Mill Parkway).
