
        SHIFRIN-Herbert E. On May 19, 1998. Beloved husband of Norma. Loving father of Roy and David Shifrin and Laura Tarlowe (Paul). Brother of Beatrice Saltzman. Adoring grandfather of William Shifrin and Stephanie and Ezra Tarlowe. Services today, 12:30 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        SHIFRIN-Herbert. The Board of Directors, Artists and Staff of The Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center are deeply saddened by the passing of Herbert Shifrin, beloved father of Artistic Director David Shifrin. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to David, his mother, Norma, his brother, Roy, and his sister, Laura. Our thoughts and prayers are with you. Donaldson C. Pillsbury, Chairman Mrs. John D. Coffin, President Jacqueline M. Taylor, Exec. Dir.
