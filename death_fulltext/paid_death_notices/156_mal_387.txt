
        KRUMHOLZ-George. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society express profound regret at the loss of George Krumholz of Miami. As Technion Guardians, he and his wife, Hannah, demonstrated the highest level of commitment to the Technion, the American Technion Society and Israel. They supported the Advanced Microscopy Laboratory as well as scholarships, faculty recruitment programs and medical engineering projects. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Hannah and the Krumholz family. American Society for TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Lawrence S. Jackier, President Irving A. Shepard, Chairman of the Board Melvyn H. Bloom, Executive VP Sam B. Topf, Chairman, Southern Region Jack Bellock, Chairman, Miami Board Jonathan I. Kislak, President, Miami Chapter
