
        BECK-Dorothy Fahs, Dr., Sociologist and Humanitarian. Retired Research Director of the Family Service Association of America, died May 5 at 94. She was widely admired for her insightful research work on family issues and for her broad commitment to the advancement of social justice. Readers may wish to consult ''Who's Who in America'' for more detail. A native of New York City, Dr. Beck lived most of her life in Manhattan. In 1989 she moved to a Pennsylvania retirement community called Crosslands. Dorothy Beck was co-founder of the Fahs/ Beck Fund for Research and Experimentation at the New York Community Trust, 2 Park Avenue, New York City, NY 10016. Donations to the fund are welcome.
