
        GOLDSTONE-Harriet Bloch. Died on January 18 (Shevat 11). Dearly beloved wife of 54 years of Abraham, dearest twin sister of Charlotte Schecter, devoted mother of David and the late Susan Goldstone, and dearest mother-in-law of Dorothy Gilbert Goldstone and cherished grandmother of Joseph, Daniel and Jennifer Goldstone. Her goodness, love, devotion, generosity and talent brought comfort and cheer to all who knew her. Funeral services Monday, January 20, 11 AM, at the Sherman Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn.
