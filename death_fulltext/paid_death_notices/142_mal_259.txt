
        SALTZMAN-Julius. Of Fort Lauderdale, FL and N.Y.C. Much loved brother, brother-in-law, uncle, and great-uncle, passed away peacefully on November 28 at age 81 to join his beloved companion, the late Moe Fine, his cherished sister, the late Helen Pepose, and his dear brother, the late Murray Saltzman, after a brave battle with melanoma at the home of his niece, Maura Copeland and her husband, Paul of Swampscott, MA. Also survived by his brother, Irving Saltzman, and sisters-in-law Rae and Beatrice; nephews, Alan Saltzman, Robert Saltzman and wife Kathy, Jay Pepose and wife, Susan Feigenbaum, and niece, Arlene Berkman and husband Milton. He was adored by ten grandnieces and grand-nephews; Lysa, Lauryn, Seth, David, Marnine, Daniel, Maxwell, Samuel, Morissa, and Harry. He will be greatly missed. Services at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Sunday, November 22 at 12 P.M. Most appreciated donations in his memory to the FineSaltzman Nursing Fellowship, c/o Jimmie Holland, M.D., MemorialSloan Kettering, 275 York Avenue, New York, N.Y. 10021.
