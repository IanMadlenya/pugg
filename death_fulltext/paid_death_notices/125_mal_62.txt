
        LIVANOS-George P. The trustees and staff of the Seamen's Church Institute note with deep sorrow the death of George P. Livanos following a long illness. Mr. Livanos was an esteemed member of the board of trustees for twelve years. He served on the executive committee, the finance committee, and the Center for Seafarers Rights advisory committee with distinction. Mr. Livanos received the prestigious Silver Bell Award in 1987 in recognition of his contributions to the international maritime community. He was a true friend to seafarers.
