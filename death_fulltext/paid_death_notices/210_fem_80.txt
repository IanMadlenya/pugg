
        JOHNS--Hazel T., 84, of New York City, died Friday, July 9, 2004 at Roosevelt Hospital New York City following a long illness. She had lived in New York City since September 1962. Born in Rangoon, Burma she was the daughter of the late Alfred Stanley and Grace Victoria Beggs Johns. From 1972 to 1985 she worked directly with the United Nations as a Consultant For Development For The United Church Board For World Ministries located in Cleveland, Ohio. Previously she was a staff member for the Commission for Social Action for The United Church of Christ for nine years. She was a member of Riverside Church, New York City. Surviving are two sisters, Daphne married to Trevor Chipchase of St. Albans Herts, England and Bernice married to Peter Turner of Cornwall, England. A Memorial Service will be held at Grace Chapel of The Riverside Church, 490 Riverside Dr, New York, New York on Friday, July 23, 2004 at 11:00 AM.
