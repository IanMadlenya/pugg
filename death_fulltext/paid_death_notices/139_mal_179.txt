
        HATTORI-Tsugio. The Reece Galleries, Inc. deeply mourns the passing of our beloved artist and dearest friend, Tsugio Hattori, who died suddenly on August 10, 1998. He was forty-six years old. We have been privileged to exclusively represent Mr. Hattori for the past fourteen wonder-filled years and feel an overwhelming personal loss. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Hattori family and friends.
