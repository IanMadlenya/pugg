
        DOLINKO--Marvin. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Marvin Dolinko, beloved father of Nancy Berkowitz, and father-in-law to David Berkowitz, devoted friends whose dedication strengthens our efforts on behalf of the Jewish community worldwide. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Marvin's wife Phyllis; his children: Nancy and David, Carol and Gary, Diane and Steve; his grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
