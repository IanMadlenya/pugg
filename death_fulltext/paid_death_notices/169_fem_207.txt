
        PRICE-Dorothy Hurt. Died on February 22nd after a long illness. She was a resident of Palm Beach, Southampton and New York City. Her three husbands, Malcolm Meacham, Edgar Eyre and Hickman Price, Jr. all predeceased her. She drove an ambulance in New York as a volunteer during World War II, and was president of the Village Improvement Society in Southampton. She was the last surviving founding member of the Bath and Tennis Club and a member of the Everglades Club. She had an identical twin, Margaret, whom she survived. She is survived by a daughter, Marjorie Meacham Meek, a granddaughter, Marina Dunn Nelson and four grandsons, Arvid, Eric and Malcolm Nelson and Jonathan Dunn. There will be a small private reception at her home in Palm Beach later in the spring.
