
        DUNNE-Marie P. Age 92 on June 5, 2002 of Locust Valley, New York. Beloved mother of Robert C. Dunne. Fond sister of Jean Eakins. Also survived by two nieces and one nephew. Retired librarian at the Locust Valley High School for 16 years. Memorial service to be held Tuesday 11AM, June 11 at St. John's of Lattingtown Church. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Locust Valley Library in her name would be appreciated. For information call Roslyn Heights Funeral Home at 516621-4545.
