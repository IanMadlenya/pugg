
        BLUMENTHAL--Lenore Curtis Deutscher of Palm Harbor, FL, died March 15, after a long illness. Born in Brooklyn, NY, she worked for many years for the NYC Health Department, retiring as the Director of the Health Research Training Program. She is survived by two sons, Dr. Edward Deutscher (Nahid) of Safety Harbor, FL, and Dr. Matthew Deutscher (Nancy) of Parkland, FL; a sister, Dr. Bernice Bauman of Mount Vernon, NY, a brother, Dr. Joel Curtis of NYC; her companion, Howard Itzkowitz; and five grandchildren.
