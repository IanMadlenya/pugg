
        FRIEDMAN--Maurice. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Maurice Friedman, beloved father of Stephen Friedman and father-in-law of Barbara Friedman, treasured leaders of the Jewish community. Maurice's benevolent legacy continues through the efforts of Stephen and Barbara, who have devoted themselves selflessly to education, the arts, and Jewish community projects. We extend our deepest sympathies to his cherished wife Hilda; to Stephen and Barbara; and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        FRIEDMAN--Maurice. The Board, Faculty and Staff of CLAL--the National Jewish Center for Learning and Leadership extend deepest condolences to Barbara Friedman, our esteemed past chairman, and her husband, Stephen, on the loss of his father, Maurice. Beloved by everyone and a pillar of the community, his spirit and kindness will live on through his wonderful family. May his memory be a blessing for his wife and all his family, and for everyone who knew him. Thomas O. Katz, Chairman Rabbi Irwin Kula, President Donna M. Rosenthal Executive Vice Chairman Rabbi Brad Hirschfield Vice President
        FRIEDMAN -- Maurice. He brew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion records with profound sorrow the death of Maurice Friedman, beloved father of Stephen Friedman and father-in-law of our Vice Chair of the Board of Governors, Barbara Friedman. Mr. Friedman was also the father of our devoted New York Overseer, Phyllis F. Perkins. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family whose loss we share. May they find comfort together with all those who mourn for Zion & Jerusalem. Rabbi David Ellenson President Burton Lehman Chair of the Board
