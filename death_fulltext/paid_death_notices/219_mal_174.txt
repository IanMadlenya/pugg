
        MATHIEU--Charles L. Jr., 85, of New York City, Southampton and Palm Beach, FL, died April 6, 2005. Born in Flushing, Queens, raised on Long Island, he attended Columbia University. Following Pearl Harbor, he joined the Marines, fighting at Guadacanal, later being discharged as a Captain. He returned to New York City to form his own financial public relations firm. He retired in 1972 to Salt Lake City, then to Palm Beach, FL. He is survived by his brother James of Monti Locarno, Switzerland; a daughter Jessica M. Tuthill of Sag Harbor, NY; a son Fred of Union City, NJ, and four grandchildren. A celebration of his life was held Monday, April 11, in Palm Beach, FL.
