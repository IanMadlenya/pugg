
        FIDLER--Leo, died of heart failure October 24, 2006, in Rome, celebrating his 93rd birthday. Beloved husband of the late Helen Fidler, inspirational father of Susan Smith and the late Bonny Robbins. He will be missed by his loving daughter, in-laws, 14 grandchildren, 12 great grandchildren, 23 nieces and nephews. His caring, optimism, wit and spirit will be remembered forever by all those he touched. A tribute to his life will be held on Sunday, 2:00PM at Pleasantville Community Synagogue, 219 Bedford Road, Pleasantville, N.Y. Donations may be made: The Norton Art Museum, West Palm beach, Florida or the American Cancer Society.
