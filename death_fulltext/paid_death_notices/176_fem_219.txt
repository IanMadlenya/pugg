
        DIAMOND-Renee Judith, passed away September 24, 2001, of natural causes. Cherished daughter of the late Irving and Doris Diamond, loving sister of Barbara L. Diamond and Robert. She was an inspiration to all who knew her, with great spirit, determination, fortitude and conviction. She will be in our hearts forever. Services Friday, 11:30AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations to American Cancer Society.
