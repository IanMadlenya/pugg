
        HOLLOWAY-Gail Fiske, 66. Of Buck Hill Falls, PA and formerly of Manhattan, died March 26. Predeceased by her husband, Edward Holloway, Jr. and son ''Quint''. She was a graduate of the Chapin School and Columbia University, & a member of DAR. Survived by her mother, Ruth (Winckelmann) Fiske, daughter Hope Holloway, and sister Jane Fiske McHugh. Funeral on Saturday, March 30, at noon from Canadensis United Methodist Church, Canadensis, PA. Viewing from 11AM-Noon at the church. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to the Chapin School, 100 East End Ave., NY NY.
