
        KING-Charles R. October 22, 2000 at the age of 86. Devoted husband of the late Betty S. King. Father of Michael (wife Claire) of Carlisle, MA, and Jonathan (wife Kathleen) of Englewood, NJ. Grandfather of Adam, Brian, Gabrielle and Benjamin. Also survived by his brother Roger (wife Louise) of Port Chester, NY, and his former wife Maxine of Topsham, ME. Stepfather to Carla Vozios and Paula Lonborg and step grandfather to John Vozios, Anna and David Lonborg. Throughout his life, Charles' natural curiosity and intellectual energy drove him to pursue many interests. In recent years Charles devoted time and passion as a volunteer at the American Museum of Natural History. As a tour guide he thrived on the discovery and sharing of new knowledge. Contributions in his memory may be sent to the American Museum of Natural History, Volunteer Services, 79 Central Park West, New York, NY 10024-5192, or to a charity of your choice. Interment private.
