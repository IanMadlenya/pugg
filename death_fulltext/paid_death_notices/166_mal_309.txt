
        DAWID-Heinz. Died November 25, 2000. He was a founding member of a well known noted intellectual property law firm, Weiss, Dawid, Fross, Zelnick and Lehrman, now known as Fross, Zelnick, Lehrman and Zissu. He was a world traveler. Survivors include his wife Jeanette of Oneonta, NY, his children, Anne Dawid of Portland, OR, Marcia Dawid Boshnack and her husband, Mark, of Franklin, NY, and Irvin Dawid of Palo Alto, CA, his grandchildren, Samantha, Benjamin, Isaiah and the late Michael, and several relatives in Europe. Private service will be held today and a memorial service will be held at a later date.
