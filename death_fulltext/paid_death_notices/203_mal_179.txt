
        VALKENIER--Robert W. Of NYC and New Milford, CT. Died peacefully at home in NYC, on December 12, 2003, two days before his 79th birthday. An editor retired from the Council on Foreign Relations, Rob is survived by his wife Elizabeth, his daughter Lisa, his brother Paul, and many friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the New Milford Public Library, 24 Main St, New Milford, CT 06776. Memorial service to be announced.
