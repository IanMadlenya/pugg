
        SCHNEE--Alexander (Lex). Passed away on July 4, 2003. A graduate of Dartmouth College, he also attended the London School of Economics and the National War College, Washington DC. Schnee spent 40 years with the Department of State, including 25 years in the Foreign Service and 15 years in Congressional Relations. During WWII he was a First Lieutenant in the U.S. Marine Corps. He is predeceased by his wife, Helen Louise (Lala) and his sister, Amy Goldberg. He is survived by his sister, Hedwig Goldstein and his daughters, Susan Carol Holt and Alix Sandra Schnee (Giordano) and families. Interment services for Lex and Lala will be held at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY on November 9, 2003.
