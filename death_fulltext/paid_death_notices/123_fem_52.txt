
        ALBERIO deCELLARIO-Raquel. Of New York City & Buenos Aires. On April 5, 1997, on the eve of her 81st year. Loving wife of the late Alberto Cellario and devoted mother of Raquel, Maria Ines and Maria Rosa and their husbands, Humberto, Anthony, and Vincent. Beloved grandmother and great grandmother of Marisa, Alberto, Mariana, Nicholas, Rafe, Santiago, Juan, Carlos, and Sebastian. The family will receive friends and well wishers at home April 8 and 9 from 6 to 9 P.M. A funeral mass will be celebrated Saturday, April 12, 2 P.M. at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, 869 Lexington Avenue. Her garden will forever grow.
