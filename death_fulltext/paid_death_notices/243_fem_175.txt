
        LEYNSE--Judith Light, 68, died in her sleep on April 15th at her home in New York. Her life was full of hope, love and purpose, and she will be greatly missed by all who knew her. She is survived by her sons, James and Andrew Leynse; their loving wives, Wendy Leynse and Mary Bacon; and two grandchildren, whom she adored. Donations in her name can be made to Primary Stages Co., Temple Emanu-El or the Ronald McDonald House. Services Monday, April 30th, 2pm at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
