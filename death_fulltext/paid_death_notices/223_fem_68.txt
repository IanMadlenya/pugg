
        GEFFIN--Wendy. August 6, 2005 following surgery. Born May 13, 1960, raised Flushing, Queens, graduated SUNY Stony Brook, she made a career in information and media technology. Survived by parents Corine Petry, Gerry Geffin, siblings Trudy Hetzel, Paul S. Levine, partner Philip Thurston, nieces & nephews Samantha, Max, Jacob, Alice, Charles and loving family and friends. Services are at King David Cemetery, Putnam Valley, NY today 2 PM. Contributions to Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation.
