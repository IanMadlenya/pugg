
        ZINBARG-Esther M. Of New Milford, NJ, formerly of Manhattan, died on December 2, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Irving Payson Zinbarg. Devoted mother of Eugene D. (Roberta) and Steven (Nitza). Loving grandmother of Jeffrey, Richard, Robert and Trisha and great-grandmother of five. Services will be held at ''Wein & Wein,'' 129 Engle St, Englewood, NJ, on Monday, December 4, 11 AM.
        ZINBARG-Esther. The New York Metropolitan Region of The United Synagogue of Conservative Judaism mourns the untimely passing of the beloved mother of our Past President Eugene Zinbarg. May her memory be for a blessing. Marsha Shlefstein, President Bruce F. Greenfield, Exec Dir
