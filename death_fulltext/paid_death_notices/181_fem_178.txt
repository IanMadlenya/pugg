
        MOSKOWITZ-Gertrude. Beloved wife of the late Solomon. Died peacefully on February 16, 2002, at age 97 in Stamford, CT. She is survived by a son, Mark Moskowitz, a daughter and son-in-law, Judith & Ira Ellenthal, five grandchildren, three granddaughters-in-law, six great-grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. A service will be held today at 11:30 A.M. at Gutterman's Inc., Rockville Centre, L.I., with burial to follow at New Montefiore Cemetery.
