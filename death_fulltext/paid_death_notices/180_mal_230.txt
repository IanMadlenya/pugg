
        GARLOCK-John ''Ted''. Of Hartsdale, NY on Sunday January 13, 2002, age 80. Beloved husband of Leila Goldstein Garlock. Loving father to his daughters Susan, Linda, and Nancy. Cherished grandfather of Jill, David, Hannah, Rachel and John. Memorial service Tuesday January 15, 1:30PM at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to W. G. A. Caddy Scholarship Fund, 49 Knollwood Road, Elmsford, New York 10523.
        GARLOCK-John H., Jr. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, John H. Garlock, Jr. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Leila, and to the entire family. James M. Dubin, President Richard J. Bronstein, Secretary
