
        AXELROD-Ruth. 88, died peacfully May 30th. Daughter of the late Dr. Benjamin and Augusta Axelrod. Sister of the late Kermit Axel. Aunt of Jane Bobrick, Blair Axel and Carol Axel. Great aunt of Benjamin, Rachel and Nathaniel Bobrick; Matthew, Sara and Kermit Akiva Axel and Gemma Weiner. She will be remembered for her gentleness. Burial June 2, Mt. Zion Cemetery.
