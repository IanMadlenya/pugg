
        BROWN-David. Former resident of Metuchen and Manalapan, NJ. Born on March 22nd, 1913. Died on April 28, 2002. Longtime employee of The State of New Jersey Department of Labor. Appreciated in death by his wife of 55 years, Adele, and survived by his companion, Anne Rosnow, children, Susan and husband David, Barry and wife Terri, and five grandchildren.
