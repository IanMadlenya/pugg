
        HANRAHAN-G. Michael. Founder and senior partner in the Pelham law firm of Hanrahan & Hanrahan, died Sunday, May 4, 1997, at his home in Somers, NY. He was 85. In addition to his wife, Florence, he is survived by four daughters, Florie H. Welch, Ellen H. Allen, Nancy H. McKinney, and Kathleen J. Hanrahan; three sons, Michael G., George A., & William F., and thirteen grandchildren. Visiting hours today at the Pelham Funeral Home. Funeral service at 10 A.M., on Wednesday, in St. Catharine's Church in Pelham.
