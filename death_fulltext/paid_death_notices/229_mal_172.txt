
        REBHUN--Dr. Lionel Earl. Of Boca Raton, FL. formerly of Lake Success, NY passed away February 10 after a long illness. Dr. Rebhun practiced Dentistry in Flushing, NY. He graduated with honors from Columbia University Dental School in 1948. Dr. Rebhun was a member of William Jarvie Society and OKU. Beloved husband of Pearl. Father of Laurence, Kit, Andrew and Dr's Donald and Francine Rebhun. Grandfather of Caroline, Chad, Carl, Nicholas and Coby. Loving brother of Elsa Cohen and Allan. He will be interred at Beth Moses Cemetery on Long Island. Shiva will be at his residence in Florida. Donations can be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
