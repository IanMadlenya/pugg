
        GALLANT-Arthur. 74, of Harrison, NY, on November 25, 2000. The son of the late Earl and Essie Gallant. Beloved husband of Lois (nee Shapiro). Devoted father of Debra and Joel Binstok, Stephen and Melissa Gallant. Cherished grandfather of Danielle, Michael, Madison, Sarah and Emily. Loving brother of Elaine Roth and brothers-in-law Stanton Roth and Edward Shapiro. Arthur was an inspiration to all his friends and to those who worked with him. Services will be today, November 27, 2 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Donations to the Jewish Guild for the Blind.
        GALLANT-Arthur. He was a man of valor and compassion, intelligent, kind and above all, courageous. His love for Lois, his children and grandchildren knew no bounds. He will be missed, long remembered and loved by all who knew him. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Lois, his children Debra and Joel Binstok, Melissa and Stephen Gallant and his five beautiful grandchildren. Elaine, Stanton and Glenn Roth Laurie, Roddy and Evan Austin
        GALLANT-Arthur. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Arthur Gallant. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to his wife Lois and family. Bernard Steinweg, President Leonard Golub, Secretary
