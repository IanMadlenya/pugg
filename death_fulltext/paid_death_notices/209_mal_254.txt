
        FRUMKIN -- Benjamin. 93, died June 24, 2004 after a nice, long life enriched by the love of his wife Sunny, his children Barbara, Neal, Yvette and their spouses, grandchildren Kera, Alena, Ann, Jesse, Lori, Matthew, Joanna and one great-granchild Sara, as well as many other relatives and friends. A wonderful, strong and gentle, caring man devoted to family and social issues, he showed his integrity, intelligence and humor throughout his life. A constant fighter for progressive ideas, he worked for a world of justice and peace without oppression or exploitation. His values live on in us.
