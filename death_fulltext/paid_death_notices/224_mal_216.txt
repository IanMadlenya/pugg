
        MOGAN--Harry. Of White Plains, N.Y. Died on Monday, September 19, 2005. He was 85 years old. Harry was born on February 6, 1920 in Vienna, Austria, and served in the U.S. Army overseas during World War II. Beloved husband of Marietta, loving father of James and Dr. Glen Mogan and Lynn Cesaria, dear father-in-law of Claire and Diane, caring grandfather of Jacqueline, Jeffrey; Grant, Craig, Douglas; Bradley, Caroline and Jesse. Great-grandfather of Isaac. Service Wednesday September 21, 2005, 11AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations may be made in the name of Harry Mogan to UJA.
