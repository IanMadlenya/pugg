
        VUJNOVICH-Mirjana (nee Lazich), 91, on April 19, 2003. Beloved wife for 62 years of George, mother of Xenia Wilkinson, grandmother of Julia. Her loss is deeply felt by family, friends, church and by the many refugees from her native Yugoslavia that she helped in America. Wake at Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison, April 23, 7-9PM. Funeral at St. Sava Serbian Orthodox Cathedral, 15 West 25th, April 24, 1 PM.
