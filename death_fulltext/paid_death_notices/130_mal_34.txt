
        KASINETZ-Zev, Rabbi. 95. One of the early organizers of Lubavitch in the United States. Later appointed by the Lubavitcher Rebbe to International Board of Agudas Chasidei Chabad-Lubavitch. Was interred Friday near Rebbe's Ohel. Husband of the late Ethel. Father of Moshe A. Kasinetz, Rabbi Suburban Torah Center, Livingston, New Jersey and wife of Sarah. Father of Devorah, wife of Rabbi Yehuda Krinsky. To all the above and his scores of grandchildren and great-grandchildren our heartfelt condolences. May the Almighty comfort you amongst all mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Lubavitch World Headquarters
