
        PAINE--Peter S. The Board of Trustees and staff of The Nature Conservancy of New York State note with sadness the death yesterday of Peter S. Paine in Willsboro, NY. He was born in the Adirondacks where he and his family created an extraordinary legacy as advocates for its conservation and for the people who live there. We are grateful for their leadership in a community that extends from the high peaks to New York City and around the world. Thomas S. Middleton Chairman Henry Tepper, State Director
