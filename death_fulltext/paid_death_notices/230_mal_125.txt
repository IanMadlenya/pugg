
        MINKOWITZ--Stanley, MD. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center is saddened by the death of Dr. Stanley Minkowitz. Dr. Minkowitz had been associated with Maimonides Medical Center for many years. He was Director of Pathology from 1981 through 1994. He was instrumental in upgrading the Pathology Department and continuing an approved residency program. After leaving Maimonides he went into private practice with his son. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. Ira Mayer, MD, President Warren Wexelman, MD, President-Elect Steven Farber, MD, Secretary-Treasurer
