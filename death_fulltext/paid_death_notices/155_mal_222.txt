Services at 10 A.M., on Sunday, December 19, Schwartz Brothers Chapel, Forest Hills. Private burial. Contributions in lieu of flowers to P.S. 175 School Library, 64-35 102 Street, Rego Park, New York 11374, Attention: Pearl Schulson.
