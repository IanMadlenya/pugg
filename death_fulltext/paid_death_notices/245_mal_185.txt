
        SPARKMAN--John C., died May 13, 2007, at his home on Groton Long Point, CT. He is survived by his wife, Patricia, his daughter Susan Sparkman-Sperandio, his sons John Jr. and Paul, and three grandsons. He was a partner at Carter, Ledyard & Milburn. A memorial will be held September 6, at The New York Yacht Club.
