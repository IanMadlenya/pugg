
        ZUCKERMAN -- Musia. Passed away peacefully on October 31. Beloved wife of Martin. Loving mother of David. Devoted daughter of the late Joseph and Lea Dimentstein. Cherished niece of Anna Caitak and Masha Sommer and cousin of Doris Martinazzi. Dedicated psychotherapist. Holocaust survivor. Her gentle ways will be missed. Services at ''The Riverside,'' 76th and Amsterdam Avenue, Sunday, 11:45 a.m. Shiva will be observed at the Zuckerman residence.
