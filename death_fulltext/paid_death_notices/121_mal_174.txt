
        KRAUS-George E., 88, of Scottsdale, AZ, formerly of New York, died February 13. Beloved husband of Bobbi, cherished father and father-in-law to Marlene and Steve Morris of New York and Leslie Kraus and Susan Rosenthal Kraus of Minnesota. Loving grandfather of David and Gary Morris and Erica Rosenthal Kraus. His goodness, wisdom and sense of humor will be forever appreciated. Contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
