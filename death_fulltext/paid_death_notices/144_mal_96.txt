
        VISITACION-Florendo M. January 4, 1999 at the age of 88. Father of Edward, Laura, and Bladimir; grandfather of Cari, Joshua, Julie, and Dylan; father-in-law of John and Beverly. A tenth degree black belt and W.W. II Veteran, Grand Master ''Prof. Vee'' was the Founder of Vee Jitsu (''The System of Systems''), a form of self-defense unique in its incorporation of diverse techniques drawn from various martial arts. His decades of teaching enriched the lives of generations of martial arts students throughout the United States and in other parts of the world, and the valuable legacy of his art and philosphy lives on in us all.
