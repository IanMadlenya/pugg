
        SCHWIMMER--Beatrice Osgood. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died November 7, 2004 in Santa Monica, CA at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Bertram. Survived by her son Arthur and daughter-in-law Arlene and grandchildren Elinor and David. Retired concert pianist and piano teacher, proud and loving mother and grandmother, intellectually curious and filled with wonder at the world she experienced for nearly a century, she was cherished by her family and friends, and will forever remain in our hearts.
