
        GREENE-Eugene S. Age 85. Died June 16, 1998. A long time resident of Stuyvesant Town, and more recently the Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale. Devoted husband of the late Joan Blumenfeld Greene. Father of Marc Greene and Sue Ruskin-Mayher. Grandfather of Nicole Adele and Joanna Ilene Ruskin. He will be missed by all. A memorial service will be held today, 5:15PM at the Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale, 5901 Palisade Avenue, Bronx, NY 10471. Memorial contributions may be made to the Hebrew Home.
