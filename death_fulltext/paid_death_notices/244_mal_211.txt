
        REILLY--Martin W., 69, retired writer. Beloved husband of Dinah; father of Colin and Maura; brother of Mary; dear uncle of several nieces and nephews, died on May 20th of cancer. He was predeceased by brothers John, James, Gerard and Francis. His intelligence, dry wit and kindness made his company a delight to all whom he touched. Friends received at Collins Funeral Home in Norwalk, CT, on May 24th, 5-8pm. A mass of Christian Burial will take place at Our Lady of Fatima Church, Wilton, CT, May 25th at 10:30 am.
