
        SCHWARTZ-The Honorable Allen G., 68, of Rye, NY, died March 22, 2003. Beloved husband of Joan. Loving father of David, Rachel & Deborah and John Meer. Cherished brother of Robert. Respected Federal Judge and former Corporation Counsel, City of New York. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation or the International Myeloma Foundation.
        SCHWARTZ-Allen G. The NYC Law Department expresses its profound sympathy on the untimely passing of Allen G. Schwartz, who served with great distinction as the City's Corporation Counsel from 1978 through 1981. Allen was an outstanding jurist, advocate and advisor. His extraordinary leadership led to a modernized Law Department in which professionalism and meritocracy became the standard. Our deepest sympathy to Allen's wife, Joan, and his family. Michael A. Cardozo, NYC Corporation Counsel
