
        DeJOY-Gary. Born June 1, 1958. Died of complications from AIDS, April 25, 1997, in Miami, FL. Beloved life partner of Paul Kevin Cline. Beloved son of Joseph C. and the late Edna White DeJoy. Beloved brother and friend to Michael J., Joanne and Robert C. DeJoy. Beloved of friends in New York, Florida and Bucks County, PA. He enriched their lives with a spirit of kindness and generosity they will always treasure. Good night, sweet prince.
