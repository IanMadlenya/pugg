
        PHILIPPS--Helen. On January 30, 2004 at the age of 96. Beloved wife of the late Arthur. Loving mother of Peter and mother-in-law of Nancy. Devoted grandmother of Robert (Mari), Rippy (Phyllis), Michael (Shari) and Suzanne (Giuseppe). Adoring greatgrandmother of Mark, Harrison, Adam, Paige, Laine and Danielle. She was the matriarch of a family that loved and cherished her. She will be missed by all.
