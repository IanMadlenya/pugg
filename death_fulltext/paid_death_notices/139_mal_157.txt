
        FLEISHER-Harold, Ph.D. The Covenant Foundation mourns the passing of Dr. Harold Fleisher, father-in-law of Dr. Jonathan S. Woocher, a founding member of our Board of Directors. A man of keen intellect and ready wit, Hal Fleisher will be missed by all who knew him. Our condolences to Jon, Sherry, Terri, Meredith and the rest of his devoted family. Susan Crown, President Eli N. Evans, Chairman Judith Ginsberg, Exec. Director
