
        GRIGGS--Marc Michael. August 15, 1921-March 5, 2006. He was a Vice-President of Batten, Barton, Durstine and Osborn, advertising agency in New York. He graduated from the Hotchkiss School in 1940 and was Secretary of the Hotchkiss Alumni Association. He was a graduate of Williams College where he was President of the Class of 1944, President of the Alumni Association, member of the Executive Council, Class Secretary, I. F. C. Secretary, Kappa Alpha Fraternity, and President of Gargoyle, an Honorary Society. At Williams he played baseball, was College Champion of the squash team, and Commodore of the Yacht Club. From 1944 until 1946 he was with the 2nd U.S. Marine Division and was honorably discharged with the rank of Captain, U.S.M.C.R. He was on the Board of Governors of the Williams Club, New York City and Secretary-Treasurer of the Williams Association of Fairfield County. A long time resident of Greenwich, Connecticut, he was a sailing instructor at Indian Harbor Yacht Club, served on the Board of Governors and Chairman of the American Red Cross and the Board of the Field Club of Greenwich. Donations may be made to the Williams College Scholarship Fund if desired.
