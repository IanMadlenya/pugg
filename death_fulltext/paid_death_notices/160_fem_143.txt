
        McDONALD-Kathryn A. The Board of Directors and Staff of Lawyers For Children, Inc. notes with deep sadness the death of our friend and colleague Kay McDonald. As one of the founding directors of Lawyers For Children, she generously shared her wisdom, dedication and enthusiasm with us all. She will forever remain an inspiration to those who had the great pleasure of her friendship and counsel. We extend our sincere condolences to her beloved daughter, Ann.
