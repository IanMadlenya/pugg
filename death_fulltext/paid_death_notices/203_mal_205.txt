
        KAUFMAN--Charles. August 11, 1910--December 15, 2003. Beloved husband of Blanche. Devoted father of Isabel and Steven. Treasured father-inlaw of Michael and Aviva. Cherished grandfather of Andrew, Sivia, Lauren and Seth. Charlie will be sadly missed but lovingly remembered by all of his family and friends, as he brightened so many lives during his wonderful life.
