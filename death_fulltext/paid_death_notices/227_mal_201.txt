
        MARCUS--Michael. 86, Lieutenant WWII, sales engineer, accomplished actor. Survived by wife Nell, son Stephen and wife Eileen, son Charles and wife Cherie, grandchildren Benjamin and Hannah, and stepdaughter Gwyn and husband Stan Jones. Loving and beloved, he did not ''go gentle into that good night.'' Contributions in his memory may be made to the Visiting Nurse Service of New York and Partners in Care.
