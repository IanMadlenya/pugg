
        MARTHELEUR-Didier. In memory of our beloved Didier, who passed away on Sunday, July 27. We will cherish the love and joy that he brought into our lives. He leaves behind his son Sam, his wife Shannon, his parents Nicole and Andre, his sisters and brother, Astrid, Jean and Yaan, his grandmother Paulette, his uncles Sylvain and Jacques, and many other family and friends who will miss him dearly. Friends who wish to join the family in remembering Didier are invited to the Plaza Memorial at 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 11am on Tuesday, July 29th.
