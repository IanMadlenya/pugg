
        RUBIN--Irving A. The National Board of Trustees, Founders, and staff of the Crohn's & Colitis Foundation of America mourn the loss of our dedicated Chairperson Emeritus and dear friend, Irving A. Rubin. As our second National President, Irving played a crucial role in the development and success of the Foundation. He was an inspiring leader, highly respected among physician and lay leadership. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his entire family. Irwin and Suzanne Rosenthal William and Shelby Modell Henry D. Janowitz, M.D. National Co-Founders Eugene A. Kestenbaum Chair, Board of Trustees Rodger L. DeRose President & CEO
