
        GERSTEN-Esther B. Died peacefully on August 28, 1997 at the age of 99. Beloved mother of Louis, devoted sister of Kyver Blumstein, dear mother-in-law of Bess, cherished sister-in-law of Esther, fondly remembered aunt. Wife of her dearest late husband, Alfred, with whom she would wish, in the words of the poet, Robert Browning, to finally: ''O thou soul of my soul/I shall clasp thee again/ and with God be the rest.'' Service on Sunday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
