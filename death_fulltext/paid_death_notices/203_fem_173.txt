
        PATT -- Edith. Bnai Zion mourns the loss of its distinguished member, Edith Patt, wife of our former National President, Raymond Patt. A social worker by profession, she spent a lifetime serving the blind and multiply handicapped. We extend our sincere condolences to Raymond, son David and Mary Jo, grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. Michael J. Lazar National President George W.
