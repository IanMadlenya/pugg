
        WILSON-C. Philip. The Officers and Faculty of the NYU Psychoanalytic Institute and the Officers and Members of the Psychoanalytic Association of New York mourn the passing of our esteemed and beloved colleague Dr. C. Philip Wilson. His contributions to psychosomatic medicine are memorable. He was an inspiration and we will miss him deeply. We extend our condolences to his family. Dr. Michael Singer, Director NYU Psychoanalytic Institute and Dr. Leonard Barkin, President, PANY
