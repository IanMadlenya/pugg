
        NORTHROP-Johnston Filmer. Of Greenwich, CT and Nantucket, MA on September 19, 2002. After graduating from Yale in 1943, he served with distinction in WWII as head of a bomb disposal unit for the USNR in the Pacific. After the war he joined the CIA, serving in the US and Germany. He was then recruited into investment banking, first with JH Whitney, followed by Electric Bond and Share, Merrill Lynch, Security Pacific and Manufacters Hanover Trust, from which he retired in 1986. He is survived by his beloved wife, Lucile Gray Northrop, her two daughters and three grandchildren and, from his marriage to the late Abby Willard Northrop, a daughter Abby Willard Northrop of Washington, DC and a son Johnston Willard Northrop of Bedminster, NJ and three grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 11:30 AM on September 25, 2002 at St. Matthew's Church in Beford, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be sent to the Nantucket Conservation Foundation, Inc., PO Box 13, Nantucket, MA 02554.
