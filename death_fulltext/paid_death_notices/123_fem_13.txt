
        MORGAN - BRENNER - Elizabeth R., Dr. Born in Roumania 1907. Died on March 31, 1997 after a long illness. She was educated in prestigious unversities in France, Switzerland and the United States. She was known to support many charities in the United States and Israel. Beloved by her late husband Dr. Herman Brenner, several of her surviving cousins, many, many friends. She will be remembered as a generous, loyal person, dedicated to family and friends & highly esteemed by her patients. Services ''The Riverside'', 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave., Friday 11 A.M.
