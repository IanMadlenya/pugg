
        PILLING-Charles W. On August 12. Minister of Music at Central Presbyterian Church and Director of The Central City Chorus, NYC. Survived by his partner Leonard DeCicco, his parents Jane and Alan Pilling, his brother Jack and sister-in-law Anne, his nephew Luke and niece Kris. Interment will be in Storm Lake, Iowa. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Central City Chorus or The Central Presbyterian Church Organ Restoration Fund, both at 593 Park Avenue, NY, NY 10021.
