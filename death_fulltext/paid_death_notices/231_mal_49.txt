
        WILLIAMS--Frederick Boyd. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Rockefeller Foundation mourn the passing of Rev. Canon Frederick Boyd Williams, our beloved trustee. Canon Williams was a source of wise counsel, inspiration and strong leadership for our Foundation as for so many others, both in the Harlem community and around the world. He will be profoundly missed. James F. Orr, III, Chair Judith Rodin, President
