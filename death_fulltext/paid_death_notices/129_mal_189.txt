
        HOFFMAN-Jerome Jay, M.D. Died October 14, 1997. Beloved husband of Joan (Osias) Hoffman. Loving father of Elizabeth and Patricia. Adoring grandfather of Jeffrey and Rachel Rothberg, and devoted brother of Elliot. Dr. Hoffman, an obstetrician and gynecologist, was a member of the Beth Israel Hospital Staff since 1950 and was much loved by all his patients. Funeral service 10:45 A.M., Friday October 17, ''The Riverside'', 76th Street at Amsterdam Avenue. Interment to follow at King David Cemetery, Putnam Valley, N.Y. In lieu of flowers please contact family for memorial trust address.
