
        MOSHER--James W. Lawyer, 65, died May 31, 2006 in Manhattan. His practice was in the theater and music industry. Prior to forming his own firm, he had been a partner in Zissu, Stein, Bergman, Couture and Mosher. A memorial is planned for June 23, 2006 at the Princeton Club in New York, 15 West 43rd Street at 5:00 PM. Memorial gifts in his name may be made to the ACLU.
