
        COVER--Franklin. 77, died peacefully at the Actors Fund Home on February 3rd 2006. An actor whose career spanned six decades, he created countless roles in regional theatre, Broadway, television and film and is best known for playing Tom Willis on The Jeffersons. His talent, infectious humor and his generous loving spirit will linger in our memories and our hearts. He is survived by his wife Mary, his children Bradford & Susan and his grandson Maxwell. Services will be held Tuesday, February 14th at 11AM at The Church of the Resurrection, 115 E. 74 St. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Actors Fund of America.
