
        ROSENTHAL--Hilda R. Died on Thanksgiving Day, surrounded by her loving family. She was a very special lady who will be missed by all who were lucky enough to know her. Beloved and devoted wife of Louis. Mother of Bernice and Adella. Sister of Helen Zirilstein and aunt of Joan Zirilstein. Services Sunday, November 30th at 10:15 AM at Clark Associates Funeral Home, 4 Woodsbridge Road, Katonah, NY. Contributions may be made to the Katonah Museum of Art.
