
        WEISSBERG-Josef, M.D. The members of the New York State Psychoanalytic Coordinating Committee wish to express our deep personal and professional loss with the death of our colleague, member and friend, Dr. Josef Weissberg. For over 15 years his wisdom, sense of proportion and compassionate interest in his profession helped guide us in our work. We will miss him deeply. New York State Psychoanalytic Coordinating Committee
