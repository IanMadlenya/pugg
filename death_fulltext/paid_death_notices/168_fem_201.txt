
        GALLO-Dolores Daniele. Dolores Gallo of Briarcliff Manor, NY passed away at her home in Singer Island, FL, surrounded by her family on December 27, 2000 after a prolonged illness. Dolores Gallo leaves to cherish her memory her devoted husband of 47 years, Thomas J. Gallo, her mother, Louise Daniele, her son, Thomas, her daughters, Pia, Deborah, Lisette, Elyse, Toni and ten grandchildren. A Memorial Mass will be held at the RC Church of St. Augustine, Ossining, NY on Saturday, January 27, at 12:30. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of Dolores Gallo may be made to: The Helen and Harry Gray Cancer Center Institute, Good Samaritan Medical Center, 1309 N. Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, FL 33401. Attn. Sue Nelson, Director, Oncology, for Dolores Gallo.
