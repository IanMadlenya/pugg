
        WHITE-James Joseph. Of Pleasantville, N.Y. and Spanish Point, Ireland. Died on Wednesday at University Hospital in Galway. He is survived by his wife, Josephine and his four children, James, Jr., Karen, John, and Maryann; his sisters, Ann Brown and Mary O'Connor; and his stepchildren, William and Kathleen. There will be a wake on Sunday from 2PM-4PM and 7PM-9PM at the William Graham Funeral Home in Rye, N.Y. and a funeral mass at 10:30AM on Monday at the Resurrection Church, also in Rye. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the American Heart Foundation.
