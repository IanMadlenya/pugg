
        HUGHES--Mary E. A Greenwich resident passed away at Greenwich Hospital on Thursday, April 1, 2004, due to heart failure, she was 84. Born in NYC on February 14, 1920, she was the daughter of the late James and Catherine O'Neil Ward. Upon graduation from Julia Richmond High School, she worked at Macy's Herald Square, where whe was named Miss Macy. She is survived by her husband Patrick J. Hughes, of Greenwich, her son Thomas J. Hughes, of Greenwich, her daughter Kathleen M. Chiaravalle, of Bronxville, NY, two sisters, Jeanette Grisoli of Baywater, NY, and Esther Ward of Brooklyn, NY. Additionally, six grandchildren, one great-granddaughter and several nieces and nephews survive her. Her two brothers and two sisters predeceased her, as well as her daughter Patricia Ann Hughes, who passed away in 1971. Calling hours will be held at the Leo P. Gallagher and Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, on Friday, April 2, 2004, from 2 PM to 4 PM, and from 6 PM to 9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will take place at St. Mary's Roman Catholic Church, 178 Greenwich Ave, on Saturday, April 3, 2004, at 10 AM. Burial will be held privately. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, C/O Doris Tulcin, 245 Main St, Suite 520, White Plains, New York 10606.
        HUGHES--Mary. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the passing of Mary Hughes, mother of fellow member and member of the Board of Governors, Tom Hughes. We extend our sincere condolences and heartfelt sympathy to Tom, his wife, Linda, and their daughters, Catherine and Mallory, along with the rest of Mary's family. Robert E.
