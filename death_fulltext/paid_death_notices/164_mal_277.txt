
        RASKIND-David, M.D. Dr. David Raskind died September 13 at the age of 100. He is survived by his children, Dr. Josephine Raskind VonHippel and Dr. Renee Richards and two brothers, Ben Raskind and Albert Raskind as well as three grandchildren David & James VonHippel & Nicholas Raskind, and three great-grandchildren. Services are being held at Schwartz Brothers Funeral Home, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills NY at 10 AM Friday September 15. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to U.S. Tennis Center, Super Seniors Division.
