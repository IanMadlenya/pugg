
        SCHORR-Max. Beloved Palm Beach and former New York City attorney, 92, died Tuesday afternoon in Palm Beach from complications of prostate cancer. A counsel at Edwards & Angell, Max was one of Florida's oldest practicing attorneys. A graduate of Brooklyn Law School, he practiced from the early 30's until 1972 in New York City and White Plains. He is survived by two sons; Tony of Rye, New York, and Andrew of Mercer Island, Washington; 10 grandchildren; and three great-grandchildren. Contributions may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
