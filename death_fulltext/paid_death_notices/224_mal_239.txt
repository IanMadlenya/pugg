
        STUTZ--Ronald S., 70, of Miami, Fla., Bayside, N.Y. and Roxbury, N.Y. Died peacefully on September 20, 2005 in Miami. Retired from teaching at Jamaica High School. He will be sorely missed by his loving wife Linda and by many cousins and friends. Graveside services on Sunday, September 25 at 3PM at Congregation B'nai Israel Cemetery in Fleischmanns, N.Y. Contributions may be made in his memory to Congregation B'nai Israel, Fleischmanns, N.Y. 12430.
