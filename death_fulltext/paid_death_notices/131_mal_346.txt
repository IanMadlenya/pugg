
        WALKER-Robert B., Jr. Born January 16, 1927. Died December 23, 1997. He was an Administrator for IBM in New York City. Loving companion of Kenneth Fabbrini. Beloved brother of William J. Walker and James J. Walker. Visitation will be Sunday, December 28 from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at The Donovan Funeral Home, 82 So. Church St., Goshen, NY. Services will be on Monday, December 29 at 10 AM at St. James Episcopal Church, Goshen, NY. Interment will be in the Wallkill Cemetery in the town of Wallkill, New York.
