
        SAGER-Michael, 43, son of the late Jacqueline Perlman Sager and the late Jacob Sager died March 16, 2000 at Kingston Hospital. Formerly of NYC, Michael was a graduate of Bates College and had attended law school in California. He worked as an intern at the Ulster County District Attorney's Office in 1986 and at Columbia Law School as a research assistant. Survivors include an aunt, Beverly Perlman of Woodstock, an uncle Stuart Pelham of Florida, cousins Andrew and Lisa Perlman of NYC and Ilene Conte of Florida.
