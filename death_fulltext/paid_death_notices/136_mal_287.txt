
        GERARD-Julian, eighty. Died peacefully at his home in Palm Beach, FL on May 25, 1998. He will be sorely missed by his family and many friends there, in New York, N.Y. & Redding, CT where he also maintained homes. In accordance with his wishes, there will be no memorial service and the interment will be private in New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the American Cancer Society will be appreciated.
