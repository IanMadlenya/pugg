
        DUSSEAU-John L. On January 9, 1999, resident of Waverly Heights in Gladwyne, PA, age 86 years, retired Vice President and Editor-inChief of W.B. Saunders Company, Inc. Pre-deceased by his wife of 41 years, Audrey Augustine Dusseau, and by his second wife of 14 years, the former Sheila Sloane. Survived by his friend, Edna Cathcart. Mr. Dusseau requested that no formal service mark his passing. Memorial gifts may be sent to the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, 2150 Towne Centre Place, Suite 120, Anaheim, CA 92806, or to Haverford College, c/o Development Office, 370 Lancaster Avenue, Haverford, PA 19041
