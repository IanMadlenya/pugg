
        SAUNDERS-Dero A. We mourn the loss of our distinguished life member. Writer, scholar, journalist and gentleman, Dero was wonderful company and a great conversationalist. His courtliness elevated the atmosphere of every hall he walked into. Our deepest sympathies to his wife, Beatrice, their son, Richard, and the family. A memorial service will be held at The Players, 16 Gramercy Park, on Thursday, June 13, at 5:30 PM. The Players
