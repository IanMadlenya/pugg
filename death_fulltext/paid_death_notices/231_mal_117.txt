
        KELLOGG--Hon. Francis L. The Board of Trustees and Officers of the National Committee on American Foreign Policy offer their profound condolences to the family of their fellow Trustee, Fran Kellogg. We mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague and former President who was such an integral part of the National Committee for so many years. His gracious nature and generosity will be greatly missed. The National Committee on American Foreign Policy William J. Flynn, Chairman George D. Schwab, President
