
        SCOTT-Owen Milton, 72. On December 5, 1997. Retired architectural graphic designer with Port Authority of New York and New Jersey. Born November 22, 1925, in Cozad, NE. He served in U.S. Navy during World War II, received his BA from University of Nebraska, and his MA from Cranbrook Academy. Survived by his sister, Donna Swezy of Albuquerque; nephews Scott and Grant Swezy; niece Anne Mallory; and his companion of many years, Anthony H. Holten. Honoring Owen's request, there will be no services.
