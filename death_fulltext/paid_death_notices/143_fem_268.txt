
        THOLFSEN-Rachel Alexander, died on December 26, 1998 in her 98th year. She was a graduate of Hunter College, and a dedicated teacher of foreign languages at James Madison High School and a member of the Teachers' Union. Widow of the late Erling Tholfsen, loving mother and grandmother to Judy and Helman Brook, Paul and Jean Tholfsen, Michael, Joshua, Jonathan, Daniel and Amy. She died peacfully after a long and happy life.
