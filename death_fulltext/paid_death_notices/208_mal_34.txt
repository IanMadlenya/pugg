
        KLEIN--Jerome E. Beloved husband of Jean Elise Klein. Loving father of Robert Klein and Judith and her husband Richard Frimer. Devoted grandfather of Devon Klein and Danielle and Kayla Frimer. Jerome was a founding member of The Public Relations Society of America. He was former Director of Public Relations for Lane Bryant. Jerome was also a Manager at EAB Bank. He was a published writer. Funeral services Monday, May 3, 2004, 10:45 am, at Temple Beth Abraham, 25 Leroy Ave, Tarrytown, NY. Interment to follow at Union Fields Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY. For information, Hellman-Garlick Memorial Chapel, 1300 Pleasantville Rd, Briarcliff Manor, NY 10510.
