
        CASE-Vincent T., Reverend Monsignor. Pastor of the Immaculate Conception and Assumption Churches, the Catholic Community of Tuckahoe, died on June 13, 1999 at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx after a long illness. He was born in New York City on May 4, 1932, son of Lillian Marinelli and Philip P. Case. He attended P.S. 61 and 64 in Manhattan and Cardinal Hayes High School, Cathedral Prep, Cathedral College and St. Joseph's Seminary in Yonkers. He received a Master's Degree in Pastoral Counseling from Iona College after his ordination. He was ordained on May 31, 1958 in St. Patrick's Cathedral by Cardinal Francis Spellman and celebrated his first Mass the next day at the Church of St. Brigid in lower Manhattan. He served at St. Joseph's Church in Spring Valley from 1958 to 1964 and Corpus Christi Church in New York City from 1964 to 1976. He was appointed administrator of Our Lady of Grace Church, the Bronx in October 1976 and was appointed pastor there in 1981 and served until 1993 when he was appointed pastor of St. Bartholomew's Church, Yonkers, NY. On September 14, 1997, he became pastor of the Immaculate Conception and Assumption Churches in Tuckahoe, New York. Monsignor Case is the beloved brother of Paul Case of Hudson, New Hampshire and Lillian Guilbert of Larchmont, New York. He is the cherished uncle of Carol Ann Gelfer, Germaine Sullivan, Philip Case, Maureen Grier, Vincent Case and James Case. He is also survived by nine grand nieces and nephews.
