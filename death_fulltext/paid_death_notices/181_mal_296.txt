
        OLIN-Philip E. The Board of Directors, Staff and people served by Queens Cerebral Palsy mourn the passing of Philip Olin. Phil served our agency for almost 25 years as a member of the Board of Directors, as a tireless fund raiser, and following his retirement, as a volunteer in Queens CP's Work Training Center. He earned the admiration, respect, and love of all who worked with him. Phil's spirit will always be part of our work at QCP. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the Olin family. Bruce A. Hall President, Board of Directors Charles Houston Executive Director
        OLIN-Philip E. The Zoning Advisory Council mourns the loss of a public servant who served the Building Department and our industry with dedication and distinction. He will be sorely missed. Sheldon Lobel, President
