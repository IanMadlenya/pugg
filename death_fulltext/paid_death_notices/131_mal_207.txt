
        KENT-Nelson. Age 93. Beloved husband of Elsie and loving father of Alexandra Selesnick and Nelson Kent, Jr. Proud grandfather of Joshua, Benjamin and Jordan Selesnick.         KENT-Nelson. The Point O'Woods Art Association and the Point O'Woods Historicial Society mourn the passing of their founder. Our thoughts are with his wife Elsie and their two children.
