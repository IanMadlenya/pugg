
        HILLMAN-Bill. Leader, colleague, and friend, former President of the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists and Chairman of the Trustees of the AFTRA Health & Retirement Funds. His vision and selfless dedication were instrumental in uniting both management and labor in creating, safeguarding and perpetuating outstanding health and retirement benefits for thousands of his colleagues and other performers and broadcasters who work in the television, radio and the recording industries. Our loss is great, his contributions were immense. American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (AFL-CIO) AFTRA Health & Retirement Fund
