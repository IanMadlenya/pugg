
        BERNARD-Eileen. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Eileen Bernard, a cherished friend of the Jewish community who will be greatly missed. We extend our sincerest condolences to her daughter, Migs Woodside; her son, Lewis W. Bernard; and her grandchildren. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
