
        MURPHY-Catherine Olive Laurel (Cowen), 79, of Truro, MA., in Hyannis, MA., on Thursday, June 11th, 1998. Wife of the late Adrian M. Murphy. Mother of Serre A. Murphy of Rockledge, Florida. Step-mother of Lydia Savage of Near Pineswick, Gloucester, England. Aunt of Robert Stephens of Tyngsboro, MA. Grandmother of Brendan Murphy, Laura Savage, Rebecca Savage, and Belinda Savage. A Funeral service will be held in Christian Union Church, Truro, MA., Wednesday, June 17, 1998 at 2:00 P.M. Friends may call at Nickerson Funeral Home, Main Street, Wellfleet, MA., Tuesday, June 16, 1998 from 7:00 to 9:00 PM and at the church from 1:00 to 2:00 PM on Wednesday. Interment will be in Congregational Cemetery, Truro. Donations in her memory may be made to the charity of one's choice.
