
        NEWHOUSE - Russell Conwell. Age 91 of Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, NJ, died at home Thursday. A native of Clyde, OH, Mr. Newhouse was awarded the Bachelor of Electrical Engineering and Master of Science in Electrical Engineering degrees from Ohio State University. Mr. Newhouse made notable contributions to defense electronic systems, television, radar, computers, navigation aids and air traffic control systems. Husband of the late Clara Lucile Scovell, Mr. Newhouse is survived by his wife, the former Jane Davies, his son Alan, three grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
