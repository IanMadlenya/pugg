
        ASENCIO-Adelaida (nee Sanchez). A strong and courageous woman passed away September 28, 1997 after a long illness. Beloved wife of Juan (Eliseo). Devoted mother of Dr. Eliseo Asencio and Dr. Marysol Asencio. Adored aunt of Myra Gonzalez and husband Jose. Adopted mother of Dr. Margaret Hynes, Fabio Silva and Dr. Pedro de Armas. Loving sister of Leonardo, Herberto, Nereida, Gilberto, Juan and Lidia. Also survived by aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, cousins and friends who deeply cared for her. In lieu of flowers a contribution may be made in her name to the American Diabetes Association, 149 Madison Avenue, 7th Floor, NYC 10016 for research. The funeral will be held in Miami, FL.
        ASENCIO-Adelaida. The Faculty and Staff of The Columbia School of Public Health, The Division of Sociomedical Sciences and The Center for Applied Public Health mourn the loss of Adelaida Asencio, mother of our colleague Dr. Marysol Asencio. Mrs. Asencio died on September 29, 1997 at the age of 68. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the family and friends. Surviving are her husband of 50 years, Eliseo Asencio, daughter Dr. Marysol Asencio, and son, Dr. Eliseo Asencio. A funeral and burial in Miami, Florida has been arranged. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to The American Diabetes Association, 149 Madison Avenue, 7th Flr, NY, NY 10016 earmarked for research.
