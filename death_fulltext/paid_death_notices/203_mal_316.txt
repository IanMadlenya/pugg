
        FEILER--Alfred W. Died on December 27, 2003 in Durham, NC at the age of 90. Passionate, intellectual, witty rapporteur and political critic. Ali will be missed by friends and family. Former resident of Berlin, Paris, London, New York and Chapel Hill. Ali is survived by his wife of 68 years, Lily, his two daughters, Ylana (Miller) and Mirriam (Rosen), their husbands Martin and Howard, and his grandchildren, Joshua Miller and and Zinaida Miller, Benjamin Rosen and Gabriel Rosen. The funeral will take place on Wednesday, December 31, at 10AM at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'' 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
