
        TUNICK--Mildred. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of an extraordinary lady who along with her late beloved husband Stanley were Founders of the College and helped advanced research in Ophthalmology. We extend our deepest condolences to her sons Andrew and Richard and to the entire family. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry Weiss, Chairman Board of Trustees Albert Einstein College of Medicine Ira M. Millstein, Chairperson Board of Overseers Allen M. Spiegel, MD The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean
        $(6$)TUNICK--Mildred S., 98, of Rye, NY, formerly of Palm Beach, FL and Rockville Centre, NY, on May 31. Beloved wife of the late Stanley, devoted mother of Andrew and Ana of New York and Richard and Carole of Scarsdale. Adored grandmother of Sara, Robert, Rebecca, Erica and David. An extraordinary woman who was a source of strength, wisdom and comfort to her family and all those privileged to have known her. Blessed with a remarkable intellect, constant curiosity, and unfailing optimism, hers was a life welllived.
