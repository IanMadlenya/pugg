
        GLEBERMAN--Miriam Rivkin, on November 22, 2003, at age 80. Beloved wife of J. Harvey (deceased). Loving mother of Joan, Ellen and Joseph. Grandmother of Matthew, Benjamin, Samantha, Sarah, Margaret and Charles. Also Linda Pyo Cavalier. Sister of Roslyn Attinson and Edith Susskind of Staten Island. Graduate of New York University. Founded J.H. Harvey retail furniture chain together with late husband. One of the first female directors of National Home Furnishing Association and founder, past president and chair of its Interior Design Society. Served on JCC of MidWestchester board. Funeral service Tuesday, November 25, 2003, 1:30 PM, Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY. For information, Hellman Garlick Memorial Chapels, 1300 Pleasantville Road, Briarcliff Manor, NY 10510, 914-762-5501.
        GLEBERMAN -- Mimi. The members and staff of Elmwood Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed member and friend, Mimi Gleberman, beloved wife of late Past President, Harvey Gleberman. We wish to express our deepest sympathies to her family.
        GLEBERMAN--Miriam. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its devoted, longtime member and extends sympathy to her bereaved family. Amy Lemle, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi Jack Stern, Rabbi Emeritus
