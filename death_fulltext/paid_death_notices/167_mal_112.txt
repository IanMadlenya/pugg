
        SCHWARTZ-Stanley. The Board of Trustees of Long Beach Medical Center records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague, Stanley Schwartz. For almost 30 years, he helped nurture the growth of our Medical Center. His wisdom, warmth and caring spirit will be sorely missed by all. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his beloved wife, Helen, their children Gail and Bob Lutolf, Larry and Lois, and Judyth and Dave Feldman, and their grandchildren. Alfred I. Osterland Chairman Bernard Kennedy President Douglas L. Melzer Chief Executive Officer
