
        GROVES-E. Calvin, 75. Died unexpectedly on December 13 in NYC. Born in Lynn, MA, he attended schools in Salem, graduating 1943 from Salem High School. A 1951 graduate of Dartmouth College, he then received a diploma from the School of the Louvre in Paris. He served the navy in WWII. He is survived by his sister, Jane Dickinson, Danvers, MA. A Memorial Service will be held on Saturday, January 13, 2PM at Church of the Good Shepherd, 236 East 31st St, NYC 10016. Donations may be made in his name to the Church.
