
        FINK-Stanley. The Board of Directors, the management and the staff of The Legal Aid Society mourn the death of Stanley Fink, our distinguished colleague and dedicated long time supporter who served as a Vice President and as a member of the Board of Directors. We express our sincere condolences to his family. Archibald R. Murray, Chair. Michael J. Chepiga, Pres. Daniel L. Greenberg, Exec. Dir. and Attorney-in-Chief
