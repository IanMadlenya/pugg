
        GROSSMAN-William J. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of William J. Grossman, a generous supporter and cherished friend who was deeply committed to the Jewish community and humanitarian causes. A longtime contributor to UJA-Federation through the Isidore Grossman Foundation, he inspired others to support our efforts by helping to establish the Philanthropic 50 group. We extend our sincerest condolences to his brother, Irwin; his daughter, Laurie Gioia; his sons John and Robert; and his grandchildren. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
