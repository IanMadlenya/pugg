
        SLUTSKY--Julius. Ellenville, NY. Retired owner and operator of the Nevele Country Club, passed away on Friday, April 7. He was 94. He is survived by his three sons, Jeffrey, Richard and David, and his daughters-in-law, Lynn, Robin and Natalie, his eight grandchildren, and one greatgrandchild, and many nieces and nephews. Funeral services today, April 9, at 1 pm, in Congregation Ezrath Israel Synagogue, Ellenville, NY. Burial in Ezrath Israel Cemetery, Wawarsing.
