
        KOSMACHER-Harry A. Patriarch, businessman, and community leader, peacefully at 96, August 30, 1997. Beloved husband of Estelle. Devoted father of Lois and the late Kenneth. He will be dearly missed by daughter-in -law Mildred Richter, grandchildren Karen Lazar, Steven, Richard and Jeffrey Kosmacher, great granddaughters Kassie, Isabella and Sophie, family and friends. Services will be private.
