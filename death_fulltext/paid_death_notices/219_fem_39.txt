
        FREEMAN--Susan Lee of Florida and formerly of Rye Brook, NY. Died April 5, 2005 at age 59. Born August 22, 1945 in the Bronx to Benjamin and Frances (Preizer) Phau. Survived by her daughter Alex Freeman Osz (husband Charly), her mother Frances Phau and brother Donald Phau. Predeceased by her husband Arnold Freeman. Funeral Service Friday 12 noon at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 E Boston Post Road in Mamaroneck 914-381-1809. Followed by an interment at Sharon Gardens Cemetery.
