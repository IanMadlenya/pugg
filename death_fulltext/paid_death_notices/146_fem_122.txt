
        SELEY-Charlotte A. March 9, 1999. Loving mother of Elisabeth, John, and Carolyn, son-in-law David, daughter-in-law Liz. Cherished sister of Helen. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions in her name to the charity of your choice.
