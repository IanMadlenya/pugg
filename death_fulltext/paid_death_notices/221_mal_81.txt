
        STUECKLEN--Karl W. A free-spirit artist, painter of landscapes and portraits and illustrator of cooking and gardening books, died at his home in Sandgate, Vermont on April 24, 2005, after a long illness. He was 64. There will be a Memorial Service and celebration of his life at the Sandgate Town Hall on Saturday, June 11 at 1:00 PM. Memorial donations can can made to the Wurslin Stuecklen Scholarship Fund in Sandgate, VT. Contact: Jayne Feinberg Stuecklen at 802-375-8088 or email; jayne@stuecklenstudios.com
