
        WATERSTON-Harry. On December 5, 2001, at the age of 92. Longtime Riverdale resident, Harry was a versatile and prolific artist specializing in watercolor and best known for his sketches of Riverdale landmarks and scenic views. He leaves a legacy through his paintings and commitment to the Jewish community. His life is celebrated and remembered by his brother, Nat Waterston, and his nieces and nephews and their families.
