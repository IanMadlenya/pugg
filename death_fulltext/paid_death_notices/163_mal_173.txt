
        KIRSHENBAUM-A. Hyman, M.D. On August 14, 2000 at the age of 75. Beloved husband of Judith Iseson Kirshenbaum and devoted father of Wendy Lipstadt (Nat) & Barry Kirshenbaum (Corinne Benchimol, M.D.). Proud grandfather of Adam and Jordan Lipstadt and Matthew and twins, Lauren and Stephanie Kirshenbaum. For many years was associated in the Department of Neuro Surgery at Beth Israel Hospital. Through his skills as a surgeon he brought healing to thousands. Modesty was his mark, but his service and devotion to friends and community brought him honor, repute and love. He was the rock of our family, our center of gravity. Without him, we are bereft. His life, and our memories will be the inheritance of his children's children. Funeral services at Lincoln Square Synagogue on Wednesday, August 16 at 1 PM. Shiva will be observed at home.
        KIRSHENBAUM-A. Hyman, MD. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with great sadness the passing of our colleague, A. Hyman Kirshenbaum, MD, who was an esteemed member of the Department of Surgery at Beth Israel for almost forty years. To his wife, his entire family, and to all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink, MD President and CEO Moses Nussbaum, MD Chairman, Department of Surgery Henry S. Singman, MD President, Medical Board Robert L. Salant, MD President, Doctors Alumni Assoc.
