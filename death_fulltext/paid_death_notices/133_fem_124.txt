
        ABRAMOWICZ-Lea Mescherowsky. Died February 14, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Mendel; cherished mother of George and Myriam; sister of Alexander and the late Shoura and Bernard; grandmother of Maxine; aunt of Nadine, Carmen, Janet, Alice, Alexa, Gregor and Dolly. Born March 1, 1906, a refugee from the Russian pogroms in 1917 and a hidden Jew in Belgium during WW II, Lea was the inspiration both for the film ''As If It Were Yesterday,'' directed by her daughter, concerning Belgian resistance and for The Hidden Child Foundation (consisting of international groups of survivors who were hidden as children). Funeral services Tuesday, February 17, 12 noon, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Congregation Bnai Jeshurun Judith Bernstein Lunch Program, 100A West 89 St., New York, NY 10024.
