
        FINKELSTEIN--Sandra. It is with great sadness that we mourn the death of Sandra Finkelstein, esteemed member of Ridgeway Country Club for 25 years. Loving wife, mother, grandmother and dear friend. She lived a full life and touched many hearts. She will truly be missed by all who knew her. Our deepest condolences are with her friends and family. With loving thoughts, The Board and Staff of Ridgeway Country Club.
