
        GREENAWALT-Martha Sloan. Died March 17, 1999 at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Kenneth and mother of William, Kent and Kim Greenawalt, and Ann Abernethy, with 16 grandchildren and seven great grandchildren. Outstanding civic and community leader throughout her life. Service will be held at 3PM on Sunday, March 21, and reception will follow, at the Scarsdale Congregational Church, 1 Heathcote Road (at White Plains Post Road), Scarsdale, NY.
