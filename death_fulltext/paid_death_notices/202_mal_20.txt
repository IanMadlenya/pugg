
        BROWN--J. B. Of Darien, CT, passed away unexpectedly on November 1, 2003, in Darien, CT. Born May 12, 1966, in Birmingham, MI, he was the son of Vern Brown and Sheila Henderson Brown of Wilmington, NC. Mr. Brown was a principal partner of Hynes, Himmelreich, Glennon & Company of Darien. J.B. was an active member of the Darien Fire Department and will receive the 2003 Rookie of the Year Award posthumously. He was also active with Big Brothers/Big Sisters of America and was a coach for Darien Junior Flag Football. He is survived by his wife, Megan Axthelm Brown of Darien, and his two children, Matthew and Mary. He is also survived by his sister Heather Brown of Wilmington, NC, and his mother-in-law Jill Axthelm of New York City. Friends may call at the Lawrence Funeral Home, 2119 Post Road, Darien, CT, on Tuesday, November 4, 2003, from 5:00 to 9:00 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 374 Middlesex Road, Darien, on Wednesday, November 5, 2003, at 10 AM. Interment is private. Memorial donations may be sent to the Matthew and Mary Brown College Education Fund, c/o Hynes, Himmelreich, Glennon & Company, 30 Old Kings Highway South, P.O. Box 4004, Darien, CT 06820.
