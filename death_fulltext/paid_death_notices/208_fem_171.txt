
        WEISS--Dorrie. Much loved longtime English teacher at Bronx H.S. of Science. Later she became deeply involved in the NGO disarmament community at the UN, traveling the world to promote peace, and also co-edited the ''Metaphors Dictionary''. Married to Victor Weiss for 38 years. She is survived by many friends, her loving family, and the family of her husband Ben Baker. Donations may be sent to ECAAR (ecaar.org) or NGO Committee on Disarmament, Peace and Security (disarm.igc.org). Services on Sunday, May 23 at 11 AM at Plaza Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (91 St).
