
        BALSAMO--Ralph. A former partner at Eisner LLP, died February 1, while in hospice. A memorial service for Ralph will be held on Monday, February 27, at 5:30 PM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue in New York City. Ralph was a determined, passionate man who will be remembered for his dedication to task, and will be missed by all who knew him. Ralph is survived by his long time companion Betty Morolla.
