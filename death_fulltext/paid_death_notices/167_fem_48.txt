
        JUNGREISZ-Rebbetzin Miriam. Revered mother of esteemed Rebbetzin Esther Jungries, founder of Hineni organization. Known to all as ''Mama'', her smile, her love, and great caring acts of chesed will be missed not only by her beloved children, grandchildren and great grandchildren but by thousands of people whose lives she has touched. The Jewish community has lost a true tzaddekes. Our families are honored to have known and been loved by her. May she find eternal rest in the Garden of Eden among the righteous of Zion and Jerusalem. Levine, Pilevsky and Leifer Family.
