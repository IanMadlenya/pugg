
        GOODAVAGE-Evelyn. A radiant beacon of joy, laughter and love. Adored by all who knew her. Worked with United Artists V.P. Max Youngstein for many years during the Golden Age of Hollywood. Left N.Y. for 12 years of country life and raising her children in Maine, then brought her elegance and grace back to N.Y. Memorial: L.I. Marriott, Uniondale, N.Y. May 9th, 11:00 AM.
