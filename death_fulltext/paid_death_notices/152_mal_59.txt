
        LUKASH-Alvin. 81, died Saturday, September 4, 1999 in Pompano Beach, FL. Born in New York City June 12, 1918, he was the son of the late Samuel and Bessie Lukash. He leaves his much loved children, Seth Lukash of Westport, and Laura Lukash Knee and her husband Thomas of Avon. Poppy Al also leaves his adored grandson, Samuel Knee. He also leaves behind his brother and sister-inlaw, Dr. Leslie and Gladys Lukash of NY, his nieces and nephew. Alvin was predeceased by his wives Selina in 1958 and Mildred in 1998. He also leaves behind his companion Bobbi Cohen who brought great joy and love to his last year. Alvin graduated from City College of NY. He started a company call Hi-G, Inc., in an old army barracks in Windsor Locks, CT that grew and prospered to a place on the American Stock Exchange. His passing leaves an empty place in our lives and in our hearts. We are all, family and friends far better for having known him. Donations can be made in his memory to the University of Connecticut Childrens Cancer Fund or charity of the donors choice. Funeral services will be Thursday, September 9 at 1:00 PM at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd., at 76 Road, Forest Hills, NY. Burial will be after service at Mt. Hebron Cemetery.
