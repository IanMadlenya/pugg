
        TOTH-Alexander, M.D., D.D.S. Alexander Toth, 81, died on September 2, 1997. Dr. Toth was born October 22, 1915 in Koronco, Hungary. He moved to the United States with his family from Gyor, Hungary in 1956. For the better part of his professional career he practiced dentistry in New Brunswick, New Jersey. Dr. Toth retired to Sarasota, Florida in 1989. Loving survivors include his wife Anna; three children: Adrian, Lollia, Zoltan; and five grandchildren. Also, his soul brother Aurel and family and former wife Aranka. Funeral services were private. A memorial mass will be held at a future date which will be announced. The family respectfully requests any donations to be made to: Kossuth Club Scholarship Fund, Hungarian American Cultural Association, Incorporated, P.O. Box 19774, Sarasota, Florida 34276.
