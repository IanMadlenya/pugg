
        GINSBERG-Ida Robbins. On April 13, 2003, in NYC. Beloved wife of the late Mitchell I. Ginsberg. She is survived by a sister, Anne Cheslow of Sierra Madre, CA, and six nieces and nephews. She will always be remembered for her warm smile and cheerful presence. The kindness and friendliness that she radiated will continue to inspire those who have been touched by her. Ida Ginsberg was loved by all who knew her and she will be sorely missed. Memorial service on Sunday, April 27, at 2 PM, The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Ida R. and Mitchell I. Ginsberg Social Policy Fund at Columbia University School of Social Work, 622 West 113th St, NYC 10025.
