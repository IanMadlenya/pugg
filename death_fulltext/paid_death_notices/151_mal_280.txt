
        DIAMOND-Chaim. On August 24, 1999. A truly unique individual who touched everyone he met in a very special way. Blessed with a brilliant mind, great love of Judiasm and old world charm, he will forever be missed by his loving family and friends. Funeral service today, 12 Noon at Sutton Place Synagogue, 225 East 51 St, N.Y.
        DIAMOND-Chaim. Sutton Place Synagogue records with deep sorrow the loss of our distinguished and revered longtime member. Our condolences to the bereaved family. We were enriched by his learning and friendship. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
