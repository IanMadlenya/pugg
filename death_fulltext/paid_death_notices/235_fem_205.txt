
        TABLER--Phyllis Baker. Died at age 92 on August 22 in Glen Cove Hospital. Mrs. Tabler, a 1936 graduate of Wellesley College and longtime resident of Upper Brookville, served for many years as President of the Long Island Kennel Club and as a volunteer at Glen Cove Hospital. Mrs. Tabler's husband, the architect William B. Tabler died in 2004. She is survived by a son William B. Jr., a daughter Judith, and three grandchildren. Services private.
