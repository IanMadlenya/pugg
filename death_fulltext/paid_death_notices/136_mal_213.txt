
        OELLERICH-John. Age 60, of Old Saybrook, CT. died suddenly on Monday, May 18, 1998 at his home. He was a partner in the consulting firm of M&O. He was the husband of Noel Sagarese Oellerich. He leaves 2 daughters, Kim Oellerich and Kelley Watson. Friends may call at the Swan Funeral Home in Old Saybrook, on Thursday, from 3-5 P.M.
