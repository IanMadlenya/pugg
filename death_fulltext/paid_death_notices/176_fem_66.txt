
        FLIEG-Esther. On September 6, 2001 in Houston, TX. Beloved wife of the late Leo Flieg. Cherished mother of Augusta Levine and her husband, Sidney. Loving grandmother of Leslie. Esther Flieg was a longtime resident of New York City and a devoted member of the Brotherhood Synagogue. She worked for many years for the Jewish Labor Committee. Esther will be greatly missed by her family and friends. Service on Sunday, September 9, 10:00 AM at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Ave., NYC. Interment to follow at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery. Contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
