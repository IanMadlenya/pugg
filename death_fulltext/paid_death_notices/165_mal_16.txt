Mass of Christian Burial on Wednesday, at 10 AM, from Sacred Heart Church, Monroe. Entombment will be in Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY. Contributions may be made to Hospice of Orange, 800 Stony Brook Court, Newburgh, NY 12550.
