
        NEWDOW--Ira. September 19, 2006 in Rockleigh, N.J. Beloved husband and partner of Rosalyn for 57 years. Devoted father of Mark(wife Terry), Michael and Julie, loving grandfather of Adam, Emily, Adriana, Glen, Joshua and Tamara; cherished brother of Miriam(Elliot). Extraordinary man, brilliant engineer and real estate developer and lover of music, he earned a Master's of electrical engineering from NYU. His individuality, wit and sense of humor will be missed by family and friends. His wisdom, intelligence, humility and determination will be treasured and never forgotten.
