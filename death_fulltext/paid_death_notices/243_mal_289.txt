
        FRIEDSON--Dr. Bernard, 89, of Jerusalem, formerly of Saratoga Springs, NY, and Delray Beach, FL, passed away on April 24, 2007. A decorated veteran of WWII, he participated in the liberation of Dachau. Upon his return, he married the love of his life, Ruth Kopolsky. In addition to practicing medicine, he served on the Saratoga Board of Education and was an active philanthropist for Jewish causes. Dr. Friedson is survived by his beloved wife and his children, Arlene Saslow of Jerusalem; Michael (Felice) Friedson of Jerusalem; and Arthur S. Friedson of Chicago. The family can be contacted through friedsonfamily@yahoo.com.
