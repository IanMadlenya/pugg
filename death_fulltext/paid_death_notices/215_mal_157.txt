
        LaWARE--John P., age 76, of Sea Island, GA, former Governor of the Federal Reserve Board, died December 13, 2004. Surviving are his children, J. Kevin LaWare, Appleton, WI and Margaret LaWare, Lynn, MA, grandsons John David LaWare and William Powels LaWare, Brentwood, TN. Funeral 11:00 AM Friday at Frederica Presbyterian Church, St. Simons Island, GA. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Neurofibromatosis, Inc, New England, 9 Bedford St, Burlington, MA 01803. Edo Miller and Sons Funeral Home, Brunswick, GA.
