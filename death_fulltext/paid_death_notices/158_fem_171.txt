
        WILBERT-Wendla. Died at home March 15 after a long illness. She was a graduate of Colby Sawyer College and Boston University. She received a Master's degree in social work from New York University. She had previously been employed as an Editorial Assistant and worked as a social worker for Beth Israel and Roosevelt Hospitals. She was an active volunteer at The International Club and United Nations where she taught English as a second language. She was also a member of the Art Students League. She is survived by her father, Theodore R. Wilbert of Key Largo, FL, her two sisters; Cornelia Grondahl of Lebanon, NH and Alexandra Fleischman of Northborough, MA and Southampton, NY. She also leaves several nieces and nephews. Services at Frank E. Campbell,1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday, March 20th, 11:30 AM.
