
        BURNS--Catherine T. (nee McGarr) died on August 26th, 2005 in Brooklyn, NY. Mrs. Burns was born on September 7th, 1916 in Manhattan, NY and is predeceased by her beloved mother Mary Rooney McGarr and father William J. McGarr and her brothers William McGarr, Joseph McGarr, Father Charles Holland CSSR and Gerard Holland. She is survived by her brother, James C. McGarr, Father Jerome Holland CSSR, Kathleen Holland and by her four beloved children: Bernadette Burns Hollis, James J. Burns, Thomas P. Burns, William J. Burns and eight grandchildren. For information McLaughlin Funeral Home, 9620 Third Avenue, Brooklyn, NY and interment at Cavalry Cemetery, Queens, NY.
