
        ZEKELMAN--Wanda Ruth. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community of Windsor, Ontario, Canada, who, with her late husband, Harry Zekelman, was a YU Guardian. Heartfelt condolences are extended to her children, Alan (and Lori) Zekelman, a Yeshiva College Fellow and active leader on the Yeshiva College Board of Directors, Barry (and Stephanie) Zekelman, and Clayton Zekelman, and to her grandchildren. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Dr. Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman, Board of Trustees Joshua L. Muss, Chairman, YC Board of Directors
