
        WILLOWS-Judy. The Board of Directors, Advisory Board and staff of the Art Omi International Arts Center mourn the passing of our beloved board member and founder of the Music Omi Int'l Musicians' Residency Program, Judy Willows. Judy's zest for life and love of the arts inspired all who knew and loved her. Our sincerest condolences to her husband, Francis Greenburger, her children, her parents and her brother Ross. John and Linda Cross and the Art Omi Boar
