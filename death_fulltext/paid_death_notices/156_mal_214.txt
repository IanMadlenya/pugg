
        THOMAS-George M. Died January 10, 2000. Age 84, of New York and Longboat Key, FL. Devoted husband of Bernice. Loving father of Joan Ellen Thomas and Philip Shale Thomas of New York and Dr. Edward Seth Thomas of Rhode Island. Adored grandfather of four and brother of Reuben Thomas of Englewood, NJ. Graduated City College of NY and New York University Law School 1939. Member of the NY Bar Association since 1941, the Washington DC Bar Association since 1943 and the American Bar Association since 1946. A principal of Susan Thomas Inc., a womens wear apparel company. Subsequently in private law practice and involved in philanthropy through a family charitable foundation and New York Community Trust. Admitted to the Senior Lawyers Division of the Supreme Court in 1997. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Thursday, January 13 at 11:30AM.
        THOMAS-George. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the death of George Thomas, a devoted supporter whose generosity allows us to fulfill our mission on behalf of the Jewish community at home, in Israel, and throughout the world. We extend our sincere sympathies to his beloved wife, Bernice, and his daughter, Joan, and all the members of the family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO Arnold Gerson, Exec. Director, Annual Campaign
