
        GLATZER - Ruth. Passed away on November 4, 2005 in her 97th year. She was the beloved wife of the late Reuben Glatzer. She is survived by her sons, Sanford B. of Larchmont, NY, and Kenneth D. of Paramus, NJ, and daughtersin-law, Tania and Phyllis. She was the adoring and adored grandmother of Jocelyn (and her husband Jeff Spagat), Lawrence, Lauren (and her fiance, Jeremy Parker), Jason and Lisa. Her enduring optimism, generosity, love and concern for all will never be forgotten. Funeral services Sunday, November 6 at 12 Noon at Gutterman Musicant, 402 Park Avenue, Hackensack, NJ, 800-522-0588.
