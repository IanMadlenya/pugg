
        SHAPIRO--Helen Lewin, age 96, passed away peacefully July 17. Widow of Herman Shapiro. Cherished cousin of the Weinstein, Baron and Baskin families. She was an honored and esteemed teacher at Music and Art High School from its inception to her retirement. Memorial service to be announced. For further information contact Margot Baron or Stephen Weinstein. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent in her memory to the charity of your choice. >>AD#
