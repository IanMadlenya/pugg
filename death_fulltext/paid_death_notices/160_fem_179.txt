
        FLEHINGER-SCHULTZ-Dr. Betty Flehinger-Schultz, a Biostatistician of 37 years for IBM, died on May 21, 2000 at the Sound Shore Medical Center. She was 78. She obtained her Ph.D. in applied mathematics. She is the beloved wife of William Schultz. Also survived by her children, Merritt Flehinger, Lois LaPadula, Joan Ammon, Harley Schultz and Cindy Katzoff, her grand children, Margo, Lisa, Michael, Susan, Jonas, Laura, Shaina, Karen & Meira, and one great-granddaughter, Julie. Funeral Service will be held today 11am at Zion Memorial Chapel 785 E. Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, 914-381-1809, followed by entombment at Ferncliff Cemetery.
