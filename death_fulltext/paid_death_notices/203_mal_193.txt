
        HARFIELD--Henry. A memorial service will be held at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 90th St. and 5th Ave. at 10:30 AM on Thursday, December 18, 2003. Died on September 13, 2003 in New York City at age 89. Beloved father of Susan Harfield Leness Peck. Survived also by grandchildren Amanda Leness Gubbin, Susan Leness Gilbert, Anthony Harfield Leness, Sarah Harfield Piel and Katharine Harfield. Greatgrandfather of Henry Maxwell Gilbert, Eliza Leness Gilbert and Griffin Piel McAlinden. Predeceased in 1999 by his wife of 59 years, Marion Bussang Harfield and in 1996 by his daughter, Ellen Harfield Piel. Yale Class of '34 and Columbia Law School Class of '37. At his death he was of counsel to Shearman & Sterling. Memorial donations may be made to Yale University, Class of '34, P.O. Box 2038, New Haven, CT 06521 or to East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978.
