
        AMES-James Barr. Of Lincoln, Massachusetts. On September 8, 2000 at age 89. Distinguished lawyer with the Boston firm of Ropes & Gray, president of the Boston Atheneaum, treasurer of the Boston Museum of Fine Arts, and president of the Massachusetts Historical Society. Survived by his wife Suzannah Ayer Ames; his sister Phyllis Cox of South Brooksville, Maine; his sons Richard and Charles Ames, both of Brookline, Massachusetts; and seven grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Memorial Church in Cambridge on Friday, October 13, at 3:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Ames-Wollan Scholarship Fund at Harvard College.
