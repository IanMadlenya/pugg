
        HUNTER-William F., age 64, of Bardonia, NY, formerly of New City, NY, passed away on Thursday, April 27, 2000 at Our Lady of Mercy Hospital after a long battle with cancer. He was born in Brooklyn, the son of William and Ellen (nee Corcoran) Hunter. He married Elenore Cherry in 1959 in Astoria, NY. He worked for the New York City Fire Department for 25 years. He was appointed in 1959 and retired as Deputy Chief in 1984. He was past-President and Chief's Representative of the New York City Uniformed Fire Officers Association. He is survived by his wife and his children, Ellen and her husband Pat Braff, Kathleen and her husband Peter Cassidy, and Maureen Strysko, two brothers Robert and Thomas, and sister Eileen Hughe, and five grandchildren. Visiting Saturday and Sunday 2-4PM and 7-9PM at the Michael J. Higgins Funeral Service, 321 S. Main St., New City, NY. A Mass of Christian burial will be held on Monday, 10AM, St. Francis of Assisi Church in West Nyack, with burial in St. Anthony's Cemetery, Nanuet, NY.
