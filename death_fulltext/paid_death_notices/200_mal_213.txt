
        PURSEL--Stewart E., M.D. September 27, 1930-September 18, 2003. Survived by wife Mary Louise, son Tom, daughter Linda, six grandchildren and sister Joan. Graduated Mercersburg Academy '48, Princeton University '52 and Yale Medical School '56. Chief of Thoracic Surgery at four Detroit hospitals including William Beaumont, where he passed of cancer. A man of integrity, compassion and a skilled surgeon, he will be cherished always.
