
        WAGER--Walter H. Author, raconteur, father and grandfather. Columbia B.A., Harvard J.D., Northwestern L.L.M., Fulbright fellow at Sorbonne, aviation advisor to Israeli government, U.N. editor, wrote for Edward R. Murrow, ''Playbill'' editor-in-chief, Press Chief at ASCAP, Juilliard, U. Bridgeport, author of more than 30 books. Survived by wife Winifred, daughter Lisa (Bob Liff) and granddaughters Eleni and Clara. His style, wit and knowledge are already missed. Service today 11 am, ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
