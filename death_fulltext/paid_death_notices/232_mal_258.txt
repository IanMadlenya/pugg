
        RIESS--Jonathan B., 58. On May 17, 2006. Darling beloved of Beth Eline. Adored father of Marine Corporal Christopher Morgan-Riess. Cherished brother of Claudia Wasserman and brother-inlaw Robert, uncle of Eric and Lise. Grand-uncle of Emily, Brian, Danielle and Gabrielle. As Professor of Art History at the University of Cincinatti, Jonathan also served terms as the Director of the School of Art and Graduate and Undergraduate Advisor in Art History. He authored books, essays and reviews, primarily in his field of concentration, the Italian Renaissance. Jonathan's passion for teaching was transformed by his loving students into a profound appreciation of pure art and academic research. He will be deeply missed. Private services.
