
        ZASLAVSKY--Sam, on February 28, age 93, in Boston. A life-long New Yorker and activist for freedom and equality. Electrical engineer; mathematics teacher at Baruch College. Survived by sons Thomas and Alan and grandchildren Clara Zaslavsky Correia and David, Sarah, and Katherine Zaslavsky. His wife of 63 years, Claudia, predeceased him by one year.
