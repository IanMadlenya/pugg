
        SHERR-Jean F. On July 19, 1999. Age 79. Beloved wife of Abraham I. Sherr. Loving mother of Rita, Barbara and Alan. Service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday, July 21 at 2PM. Interment to follow at Salem Field Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        SHERR-Jean. The Helen Keller Manhattan League records with sorrow the passing of Jean Sherr, devoted wife of Abraham Sherr and esteemed supporter of the League. Helen Keller Services for the Blind
        SHERR-Jean F. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband, our honorary president and benefactor of The Sherr Institute of Adult Judaic Studies, Abraham I. Sherr, and to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H.
