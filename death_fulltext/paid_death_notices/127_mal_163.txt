
        MOSBACHER-Emil Jr. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Administration of the Hospital for Special Surgery mourn with profound sorrow the death of Emil Mosbacher, Jr., a devoted friend and benefactor of the Hospital and husband of Patricia Mosbacher whose many years of outstanding service on Special Surgery's Board is inspirational. We extend our deepest sympathy to our dedicated Trustee, sons Emil III, R. Bruce and John and to the entire Mosbacher family. Richard L. Menschel Winfield P. Jones Co-Chairmen of the Board of Trustees John R. Ahearn John R. Reynolds Co-Chief Executive Officers Russell F. Warren, MD, Surgeon-in-Chief
        MOSBACHER-Emil, Jr. (Bus). The Trustees, Members and Volunteers of the South Street Seaport Museum mourn the passing of Emil (Bus) Mosbacher, Jr. Loving husband of our esteemed Trustee Patricia and valued friend of the Museum. A true gentleman, a yachtsman who enjoyed the admiration and respect of his peers, a distinguished and patriotic American & a champion of maritime history, Bus Mosbacher will be sorely missed. Our condolences to Pat and the entire family. Peter A. Aron, Chairman Peter Neill, President
        MOSBACHER-Emil, Jr. The Pilgrims of the United States mourn the death of their distinguished Executive Committee Member and extend their deepest sympathies to his wife Patricia and their sons. Henry Luce III, President Mark Dixon, Secretary Eben Pyne, Treasurer
