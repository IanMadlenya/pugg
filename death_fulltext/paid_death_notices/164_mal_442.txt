
        STIASSNI-Joseph E. Passed away suddenly but peacefully on September 26, 2000. Loving husband of Joy, devoted father of Michael Stiassni, Sylvia Frelinghuysen, and Nicholas Stiassni. Also survived by eight grandchildren and three great grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at 11 A.M. Saturday September 30, 2000 at Saint Bernard's Church, Mine Mount Rd, Bernardsville, NJ. He will be missed and remembered by all.
