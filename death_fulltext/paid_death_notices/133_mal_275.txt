
        CLEAVER-George H. Formerly of Huntington, New York and prior to that, Washington, D.C., died on February 14 at a nursing home in Amherst, Massachusetts after a long illnes. He was 80 years old. He is survived by his wife Claudia Jones Cleaver of Amherst, Massachusetts, his five children, Kevin Cleaver of Bethesda, Maryland, Claudia Cleaver of Petaluma, California, Carole Cleaver of New York, N.Y., Cynthia Margiotta of Gillette, Wyoming, and Jacqueline Cleaver of Richmond, Virginia, and six grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at Leeds Church in Markham, Virginia on Thursday, February 19 at 2:00PM.
