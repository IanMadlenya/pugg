
        BROADUS-Anne Bowen, on July 18, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Carlisle Broadus. Devoted mother of Anne Elizabeth Holding of Lafayette, California and Margaret Carlisle Saling of New York City. Cherished grandmother of Amanda Carlisle Holding, Katherine Sinclair Holding, Joseph Carlisle Saling and John Pritchard Saling. Mrs. Broadus graduated from Sweet Briar College (VA) and received her Master's degree in Social Work from Columbia University. She was active in many charities in NYC, among them the Society of The Third Street Music School Settlement, of which she was an Honorary Director. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, July 21, at 4:30 PM in the Chapel of the Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church, 7 West 55 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Society of the Third Street Music School Settlement, 235 East 11 Street, NY NY 10003.
