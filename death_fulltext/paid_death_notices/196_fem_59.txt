
        BAKER-Katharine J., 98, died on April 29, 2003, at her home in Englewood, NJ. Katharine Baker was born in Cleveland, Ohio on July 1, 1904, daughter of Arthur Bacon and Ida Irwin Jones. She earned degrees in biochemistry from Vassar College and Radcliffe College. During World War II, she participated in the Manhattan Project. Thereafter, she worked in basic medical research at the College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University. She is survived by two children and eight grandchildren. The memorial service will be held at the First Presbyterian Church in Englewood at 10 AM on May 22. Memorial gifts may be made to the Katharine Jones Baker Scholarship Fund, Vassar College, Poughkeepsie, New York; or to the Dr. Thomas Quinlan Morris Scholarship Fund, Columbia University, New York.
