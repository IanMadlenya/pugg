
        ROSARIO - SIEVERT - Heather, Ph.d. Died peacefully at home on October 30, 2000 after a courageous battle against lung cancer. Professor of English at Hostos Community College of CUNY, translator and poet. Loving wife of William Sievert. Devoted mother Liana Rosario and her husband David Aronson & Claudia Rosario Hammond. Cherished grandmother of Hailey London Aronson. Sister of Audrey Novoa. Services today, 3 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions in her name to Save the Children Foundation.
