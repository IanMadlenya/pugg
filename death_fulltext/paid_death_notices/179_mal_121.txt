
        BROWN-Seymour W. On December 9, 2001. Former President of Michael Baker Jr. of NY and Seymour W. Brown & Associates. Advisor to Admiral Hyman Rickover in the design of environmental systems for the U.S.N. Nautilus, the nation's first nuclear submarine. A pioneer in the commercial use of waste heat in power plants to produce electricity, known as Cogeneration. A recipient of many honors and awards for his contributions. Beloved husband of Ruth. Loving father of Barbara Hawthorn. Devoted grandfather of James. Dear brother of Bernard. Services Wednesday, 12:45 P.M. at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
