
        NIKOLIS-Gary P., M.D. On April 19, 1997, at the age of 44. He is survived by his mother Artie, brothers Bill and Phillip, sister Anna, nieces and nephews and many friends. Knowles, Inc, Funeral Home, 128 Main St, Port Washington, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions to GMHC, 129 West 20th St, New York, NY 10011, would be greatly appreciated.
