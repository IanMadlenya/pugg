
        KATZMAN--Sara. On February 12, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Devoted mother of Beverly Katzman and Shelley Katzman Selig; adored grandmother of Matthew Selig and Jane Selig Goldstein, loving great grandmother of Andrew and Emily Goldstein. She is also survived by her good friend Rose Idemne. Her love and generous spirit will be greatly missed.
