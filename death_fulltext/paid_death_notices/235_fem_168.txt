
        CHAITKIN--Minnie. Of Manalapan, N.J. died on August 24, 2006. Born on May 17, 1914 in New York City. Beloved mother, grandmother and friend. She lived the last 7 years in New Jersey to be closer to family, but New York City was always her home. Her generosity, wit and humor will be missed. She is survived by her daughter Marcia Rosen and grandchildren Marc and Staci Rosen.
