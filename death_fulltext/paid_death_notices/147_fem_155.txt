
        GOLDSCHMIDT - Florence G. Born in Brooklyn, New York on July 15, 1918 passed away in Dallas, Texas on April 26, 1999. She is survived by her loving husband, Arthur; daughter and son-in-law Karen and Norman Lerner; daughter and son-in-law Joan and Dr. Alan Weinstock; and grandchildren Rachel, Elie and husband Naama, Yosef, Devora & Chana. Services will be held at 10:00 on Wednesday, April 28, at Shermans Flatbush M.C., 1283 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn. A shiva will be held at the Lerners residence.
