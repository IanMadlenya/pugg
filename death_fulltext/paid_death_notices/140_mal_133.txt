
        ARNOW-Peter (Meyer) died peacefully in his sleep on September 8, age 84 years. He was a poet, dreamer, and scientist who always loved to laugh. He was a devoted husband to Fran, loving father to Julie, and a playful, if sometimes forgetful, grandfather to Zoe, Sophia, and Navah. Graveside services were held at Newton Cemetery, Newton, MA., September 10. He will be dearly missed.
