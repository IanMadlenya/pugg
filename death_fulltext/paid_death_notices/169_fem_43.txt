
        ADOLF-Rosalie K. Gentle, generous and gracious lady. Devoted wife of the late Norman L. Adolf. Caring mother of Benine Adolf and Sally Laurent (Neil). Grandmother of Nate and Ben. Longtime active member of National Council of Jewish Women; volunteer at LIJ Hospital; graduate and benefactor of Hunter College; devotee of art, music and theater. Graveside services Monday, 1:30 PM, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, NY. Contributions to National Council of Jewish Women or your favorite charity in lieu of flowers.
        ADOLF-Rosalie K. Devoted volunteer and enthusiastic supporter of Council Senior Center, respected Board member of NCJW New York Section, Rosalie will be remembered for her commitment and incisive thinking. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, February 14, at 2:30 PM, at 820 2nd Avenue, New York City. Rita Fishman, President Helen C. Heller, Exec. Dir.
        ADOLF-Rosalie K. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Rosalie Adolf. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
