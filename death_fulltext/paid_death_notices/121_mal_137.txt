
        LEVINE-Irving R., age 73, on February 9, 1997. Mr. Levine was an advertising and production director for Revlon and Torlow advertising agency for the past 25 years. He was a member of the Dellwood Country Club, New City, NY for over 40 years. He is survived by two brothers, Murray and Saul, five nieces and 12 great-nieces and nephews. He was also pre-deceased by a brother, Martin and a nephew, Dr. Richard L. Levine. Services will be held today, 1:30 PM at Hellman Memorial Chapels, 15 State Street, Spring Valley, NY.
