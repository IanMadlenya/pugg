
        JACOBY--Henry J., 87, of New York City on September 15, 2004. Beloved husband of Doris Goodman Jacoby; brother of Gertrude Handelman and Leah Berton; father of Ruth Ellen Jacoby Barnert, Karen Jacoby Zovic, and Jean Jacoby Bentley; grandfather of Matthew Jacoby Barnert, Michelle Celeste Zovic, Nicholas Henry Zovic, Barritt Wolff Jacoby Bentley, and Emma Rose Bentley Jacoby. Of complications from a heart attack after a brief illness. Retired President and Chairman of the Board of Grow Tunneling Corporation, Honorary Vice President and Trustee of Congregation Rodeph Sholom, former Chairman of the Board, Walden School, former President of the Anthropology Museum of the People of New York. We will always love and miss him. Funeral services will be held on Monday 11:30 AM at Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 W. 83 St., NYC. In lieu of flowers the family requests that contributions be made to the Nature Conservancy.
