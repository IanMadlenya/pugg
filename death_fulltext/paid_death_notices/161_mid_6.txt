
        WORKMAN-Bernard. At the age of 96. Beloved husband of the late Jeanette. Devoted father of Peter and the late David. Loving fatherin-law of Carolan and Sue. Cherished grandfather of Betsy, Cindy, Elizabeth and her husband Mark, Katherine and her husband Gary, and great-grandfather of Jack. Services Monday, 11:30 AM, at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Rd., Great Neck, NY.
        WORKMAN-Bernard. June 10, 2000. The Board of Governors and the Members of Glen Oaks Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their esteemed longtime member of 62 years, Bernard, and extend their heartfelt sympathy to his family. Board of Governors Richard A. Horowitz, President
        WORKMAN - Bernard. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathies to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
