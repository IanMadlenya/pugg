
        McCREERY--Robert L. On June 8, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Saunders O'Keefe. Loving father of Marina McCreery. Devoted brother of the late William C. McCreery, Gladys McCreery, Beatrice Flynn and Florence Rick. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Graduate of Fordham University and St. John's Law School. He was a U.S. Attorney for the Treasury Department. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Friday 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial Saturday, June 12th, 10AM at The Church of St. Thomas More, 65 E. 89th St., NYC.
