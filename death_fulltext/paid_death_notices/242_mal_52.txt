
        SWEENEY--CHARLES (Chuck) F., on March 1st, 2007, at his home in Philadelphia, PA. Beloved husband and best friend of Nina. Devoted brother of Margaret Schneider. Also survived by nieces and nephews. He was in the Army Medical Corp in WWII. He was a song writer and composer. He was recognized for such memorable songs as: Here I am in Love Again, How Blue, Blossom, We Got Us and the Hauntingly Beautiful Lullaby, Stars. These songs and many others were performed by Buddy Greco, Julius LaRosa, The Mills Brothers, Tony Martin, and many others. Along with Moose Charlap, he wrote The King and Mrs. Candle for television. Interment private. Family will receive friends at Affinia Gardens Hotel, 215 E 64th St, New York, NY, on Thursday, March 8, from 4:306:30pm.
