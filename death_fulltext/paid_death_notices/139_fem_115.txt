
        ROSENBERG-Louise Marie, 63. Died peacefully Tuesday in her Wilton home, surrounded by family. With husband Peter, she transformed their business Vallin Galleries into a renowned Asian art gallery. Louise was an inspirational, nurturing mother and friend. She created a loving family & gracious home with beautiful gardens. She is survived by her husband, six children Robert, Jean, Michael, Thomas, Simon and Nicolas, five grandchildren, and sister Mary.
