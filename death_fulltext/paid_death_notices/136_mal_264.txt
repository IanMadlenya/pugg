
        CARBONI-Daniel. Of NYC, formerly of Teaneck, NJ, on May 19, 1998. Beloved son of Francesco and Susan (Gordon) Carboni. Dear brother of Peter. Loving grandson of Alice Gordon. Cherished nephew of Martin Gordon. Dan did stand-up comedy in various clubs in NY; he also interned at Politically Incorrect and the Conan O'Brien shows. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, May 26, 1998, at 7:30 PM, at the Volk-Leber Funeral Home, Teaneck, NJ. The family requests donatons be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave., NYC, in Dan's memory.
