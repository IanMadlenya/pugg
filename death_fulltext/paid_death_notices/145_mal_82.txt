
        ROSSI-Neil J. On February 4, 1999. Survived by his loving wife, Mina M. Rossi. Dear father of Neil Rossi and Diane Taylor. Devoted grandfather of Christopher and Matthew Rossi, Nicholas and Megan Taylor. Dear father-in-law of Paul Taylor and Jody Rossi. Served with the US Navy in the Pacific Theater during World War II. He was a graphic artist in his civilian life. Reposing at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue (at 20 St.), NYC, today 2-5 and 7-9 PM, services at 4:30 PM. Interment Monday at Calverton National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to the American Cancer Society would be gratefully appreciated.
