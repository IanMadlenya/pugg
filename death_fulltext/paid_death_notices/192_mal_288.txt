
        LIPSON-Milton ''Mitch.'' On January 22, very nearly 90 years old. A man who lived fully in his times serving in the administration of Fiorello LaGuardia, in the U.S. Secret Service, in private legal practice, as Commissioner of Investigation of Nassau County, vice president for security of American Express, Assistant District Attorney of Nassau County and village judge of Sea Cliff. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Broader Horizon Fund of the Sea Cliff Public Library. Clara, Eden and Merek
