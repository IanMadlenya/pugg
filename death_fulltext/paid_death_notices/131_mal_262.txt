
        NICCOLI-Reverend Andrew. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicar of Most Precious Blood Church, 32-23 36th St., Long Island City, On December 18. Reposing in Thomas M. Quinn & Sons Funeral Home, 32-20 Broadway, Long Island City, Sunday, Dec. 21, 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Sunday, Dec. 21, 7:30 P.M., Most Precious Blood Church. Funeral Mass, Monday, December 22, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
