
        SIEGEL-George. Died March 3 after a long illness at age 76. Professor of Russian Literature and Doctor of Philosophy. Loving companion to Faye Mittelman. Devoted brother and brother-in-law to Jesse and Wilma Siegel. Proud uncle to Hillary Levin and Nancy Siegel. The Siegel family loses by his passing, love, intellect, caring and good counsel that is irreplaceable. Funeral services were held at Frank E. Campbell.
