
        WINCIG--Marie Grant departed peacefully on Sunday September 17th, after a long illness. Loving wife of Bernard Wincig and beloved mother of Wendy Burke Svarre, Jonathan W. Burke, and loving stepmother of Owen Wincig, and Lauren Kline. Devoted grandmother of Grant and Caroline Svarre, Jack and Olivia Burke, Kyle, Ryan and Tyler Wincig, Jacqueline, Rebecca and Danielle Kline. Marie retired from Bloomingdale's as the Fashion Director of Women's Accessories, after 25 years with the company. Her passion for her career was only surpassed by her love for her husband, her children, her grandchildren and all the lives that she touched. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to Alzheimer's Association at www.alz.org or 800-272-3900. Funeral will be held at 11:30 a.m. on Wednesday, September 20th, at Frank E. Campbell, 81st and Madison Ave.
