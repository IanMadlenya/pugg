
        VAN NORDEN-Montagnie on Tuesday, October 1, 2002. Cherished husband for 52 years of the former Gwendolyn Olson (Joy). Devoted father of Montagnie Van Norden, Jr., of Concord, MA, Adam Van Norden of Reno, NV, Kristina Van Norden Anderson of Cincinnati, OH and Juliana Van Norden Wynne of Westport, CT. Beloved and caring grandfather of Christopher and Katherine Van Norden, Emilia and Benjamin Anderson, and Henry and Grace Wynne. He was born in New York City March 26, 1923, the son of the late Warner Montagnie Van Norden and the late Grace Talcott Van Norden and brother of the late James Talcott Van Norden. He was a graduate and trustee of the Browning School and a graduate of Princeton class of '45. He served in WW II first in the 463rd parachute battalion and was then assigned to the 101st Airborne in the Battle of the Bulge at Bastogne. He joined Merrill Lynch in 1949 and retired in 1990. His interests were varied, ranging from history, poetry and mathematics to hunting and fishing throughout the world. He lived his entire life in New York but maintained homes in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida and ''Sand Pond'' in Willowemoc, New York. A Memorial Service in celebration of his life will be held on Thursday, October 17 at 3PM at St. Vincent Ferrer 66th Street and Lexington Avenue. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his memory to the Rogosin Institute, 505 East 70th Street, NYC 10021 (attn: Carolyn Diehl) or to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461.
