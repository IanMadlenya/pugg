
        ZEIDMAN--David S. The partners and staff of Zeidman, Lackowitz, Prisand & Co., LLP are deeply saddened by the loss of our beloved founding partner, David S. Zeidman, CPA. He was patient and kind and always smiled. His leadership, skill and subtle humor will be missed by all. We have established a college scholarship in David's name. Please send any donations to the Baruch College Fund, David S. Zeidman Scholarship, C/O Baruch College, One Baruch College Way, Box A1603, New York, N.Y. 10010.
