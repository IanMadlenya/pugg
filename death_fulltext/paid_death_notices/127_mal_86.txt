
        PRAGER-David A. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of David A. Prager, friend and longtime supporter of the Whitney's many programs. He will be warmly remembered for his steadfast support of American artists and his dedication to furthering the welfare and progress of contemporary American art. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Annabelle, and his family. Flora M. Biddle, Hon. Chairman Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Gilbert C. Maurer, President David A. Ross, Director
