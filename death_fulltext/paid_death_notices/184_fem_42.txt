
        DEVERY-H. Patrick. On May 3, 2002, of Ridge (former 45 year resident of Seaford). Well known in the shipping industry for over 50 years, WWII U.S. Navy veteran. Beloved husband of 52 years to Dorothea C. Devoted father of William P. and his wife Jacqueline, the late Katherine and her husband John, Patricia and her husband Gary and Maureen and her husband Tommy. Loving son of Florence. Dear brother of Rosemary Morris, Joseph P., Florence Mower and the late Andrew. Cherished grandfather of Joanna, John, David, Michael, Thomas, Kathryn and Christopher. Also survived by many loving nieces and nephews. Reposing at the Charles G. Schmitt Funeral Home, 3863 Merrick Rd, Seaford (2 traffic lights East of Rt. 135). Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, 10 AM, at St. William the Abbot R.C. Church. Interment Calverton National Cemetery. Hours Monday and Tuesday 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 P.M. Donations to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, Long Island Chapter, 555 Broad Hollow Rd, Suite 403 Melville, NY 11747-5001; or St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis, TN 38105-1905, would be appreciated.
