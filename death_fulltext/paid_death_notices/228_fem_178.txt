
        GREEN--Gilberta A. January 18, 2006. Formerly of Mt. Vernon, NY and NYC. She is survived by many nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. Ms. Green received her bachelors of History at Columbia University. She was instrumental in the development of telecommunications/patents for IT & T between Europe and the US following World War II, during which time she traveled extensively. A burial will be held in Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY on Saturday, January 28 at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
