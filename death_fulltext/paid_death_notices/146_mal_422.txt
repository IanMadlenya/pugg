
        BRANDT-Irving. Age 85. Of West Palm Beach, FL. Passed away March 22, 1999. He is survived by his loving wife of 53 years, Rhoda and by his devoted and loving children Francine (Stanley Goldstein), Fort Lee, N.J., Warren (Barbara Brandt), Stamford, CT. and by the love of his life grandson Jeffrey L. Brandt. Also survived by devoted sisters Florence Brodish and Gertrude Brand of Forest Hills, N.Y. He was associated with the New York City School System for 33 years. Services at Riverside Chapel, 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC, 12N Wednesday, March 24th. Contributions to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Bch, FL. 33407.
