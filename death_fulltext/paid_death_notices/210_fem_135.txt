
        TOMPKINS--Rebecca Crane, 89, died in Belfast, ME, of congestive heart failure. She had recently transferred from her home on Islesboro, built by her grandmother in 1899. Born in New York City on May 5, 1915, Mrs. Tompkins was the only child of Rebecca Riggs Crane and Clinton H. Crane, the noted yacht designer. She attended Chapin School and the first class of Bennington College for Women. She was an accomplished sailor and golfer, who in recent years lived in Cotton Bay, the Bahamas and Islesboro. Mrs. Tompkins leaves two daughters, Lela Truslow of Cold Spring Harbor, NY, and Susan Humphrey of Brooks, ME; three grandchildren and seven greatgrandchildren. Burial will be private.
