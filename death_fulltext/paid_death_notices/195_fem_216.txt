
        GOTTBETTER-Falene Simet. A courageous, elegant and radiant soul was lost to the world on April 28, 2003. Devoted wife of Paul. Steadfast sister to Linda Klein and like a sister to Sydell. Proud mother of Franklyn and Adam. Loving mother-in-law to Tracy and Lori. Adoring and generous grandmother to her greatest little treasures Taylor, Sophia, Rebecca and Max. Falene fought the challenges that life threw at her with dignity and poise. She was an example to all those who were fortunate enough to know her. Her deep and long lasting friendships are a testament to her sincerity and innate ability to bring people together. Her uncompromising optimism, coupled with her love of beauty and fashion brought her great pleasure and success with her recent Doncaster clothing business. Her beauty and grace will not be forgotten. Services Wednesday, April 30, 2003, 10:00AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St, NYC. The family will sit shiva in Westchester at Paul Gottbetter's home on April 30 and May 1,3,4,5. Also, the family will sit shiva in NYC at Adam Gottbetter's home on May 2.
        GOTTBETTER-Falene. So precious, so loved, revered beyond words. Our profound sympathies to her desolate and devoted family. Those fortunate to have had Falene in their lives remember her fortitude, her grace, her spirit. All that is good, all that is beautiful epitomizes Falene. We cherish her memory and will hold her dear in our hearts forever. Her Godparents, Flo and Irving Rubinstein
