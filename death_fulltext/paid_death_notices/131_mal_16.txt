
        FRIEND-Sidney L. The Board of Trustees, President David C. Chang, Chancellor George Bugliarello and the entire Polytechnic University community offer our deepest condolences to Trustee William L. Friend, Director of Bechtel Group Inc., over the passing of his father, Sidney L. Friend. Mr. Friend died at his home in Overland Park, Kansas, after a brief illness. We also express our heartfelt sympathy to the Friend family during their time of grief.
