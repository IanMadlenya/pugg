
        MARIAM-Rudolph K. ''Ralph.'' Of Kew Gardens, NY, on May 31, 2002, at the age of 90. Devoted husband of Lisa Mariam for 60 years. Beloved father of Rita Golden and her husband, Steven, of Scarsdale, NY, and Tom Mariam and his wife, Alyce, of Rye Brook, NY. Proud grandfather of Meredith and Alissa Golden and Michael Mariam. Treasured brother of Hortense Lewin. Born and educated in Germany, a U.S. citizen since 1938, and a founding member of Congregation Habonim, his kindness and compassion will be missed by all. Services will be held 11 o'clock today at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, Queens Blvd. at 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY.
