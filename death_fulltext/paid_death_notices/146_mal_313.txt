
        BLOCK-Morris, MD. Age 96. Beloved husband of Helen M. Block. Survived by brother Sidney (Martha), nephews Jospeh (Sue), Lewis Buchman, grand nephews Mark, Seth, Andrew and Michael Buchman. Comm. USNR 1942-1946 Dr. of Medicine N.Y.U., 1927, Attending Physician Bellevue Hospital 1931, Associate Physician at Bellevue, Associate Professor of Clinical Medicine N.Y.U. College of Medicine, Associate Visiting Physician N.Y.U. Hospital, Consultant U.S. Naval Hospital, St. Albans, Attending Physician V.A. Hospital, certified by Board of Internal Medicine, Fellow of N.Y. Academy of Medicine, Fellow of the American College of Physicians, Fellow of the American Trudeau Society and Fellow of the N.Y. Trudeau Society, Alpha Omega Alpha, coauthor of five published papers on Pneumococcal Pneumonia. Funeral Services at Bayside Cemetery, 80-35 Pitkin Ave., Ozone Park, NY, Thursday, March 18, 1999 at 1:30 P.M. Contributions may be made in his memory to the N.Y.U. School of Medicine.
        BLOCK-Morris MD. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Men's Club mourn the passing of the beloved brother of their devoted member Dr. Sidney Block and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family.
