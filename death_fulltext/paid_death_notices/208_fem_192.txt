
        ROTH--Florence, 95. Widow of Sol who died in 1949. Beloved by her children, Ellen and Paul Roth, Barbara and Jon Roth, and Judy & Howard Berkowitz. Loving grandmother of Stefanie & David, Jessica and Ben, and Sandra and Jamie. Adoring great grandmother of Amelia and Isabel. She lived her life nobly with grace and dignity, fighting with all her strength to make a good life for her children. They are her legacy. Funeral service Tuesday, 12:30 PM, from ''Westchester Reform Temple'', 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY
        ROTH--Florence. The Board and staff of the Center for Educational Innovation-Public Education Association mourn the death of Florence Roth, a woman of great dignity and beloved mother of our chairman Judy Roth Berkowitz. We extend our deepest sympathy to Judy & Howard and to the entire Berkowitz and Roth families. Seymour Fliegel, President
        ROTH--Florence. Florence always told us how lucky she was to have the family that she had. It was one of the few things she had exactly backwards. Sara and Mort Richter
