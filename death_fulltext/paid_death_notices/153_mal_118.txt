
        SCHECHTER - Marshall. The Board of Directors and the Staff of The Evan B. Donaldson Adoption Institute extend their heartfelt condolences to the Schechter family upon the loss of Dr. Marshall D. Schechter. Dr. Schechter was an exceptional professional who contributed profoundly to the advancement of our understanding of adoption and a warm and generous gentleman who graced our lives. He provided leadership and guidance to the Institute in immeasurable ways. He will be deeply missed. Curtis R. Welling President of the Board Madelyn Freundlich Executive Director
