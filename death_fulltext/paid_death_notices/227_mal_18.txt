
        SKALET--Joseph. On November 30, 2005, age 95. Husband of the late Lucile (nee Adels). Father of Steven (wife Linda) Skalet and Barbara Strutin. Brother of Helen Stein and the late Philip Skalet. Grandfather of four, great-grandfather of two. Service Sunday 11:30AM at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck. Interment Mt. Pleasant Cemetery.
        $(6$)SKALET--Joseph. The Board of Governors and the Members of Fenway Golf Club record with profound sorrow the passing of Joseph Skalet, a member of the club for 53 years. We extend our sincere condolences to his family and friends. Bruce D. Frank, President Roger Eichel, Secretary
        $(6$)SKALET--Joseph. We are terribly saddened by your leaving us orphaned. Endless troubles are the price we must pay for the gift of life. We join Beverly, Barbara and Steven in mourning. Good night, sweet prince. Lillian and Lewis Mittman
