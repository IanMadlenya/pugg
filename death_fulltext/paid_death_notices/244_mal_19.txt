
        JACOB--James Palmer, educator on three continents and former admissions director for the Collegiate School, died early Saturday, April 21, in his home surrounded by his family. He was 82 years old. Mr. Jacob is survived by his wife Mary Louise, his children Abigail, John, Bill and Sarah, his sister Betsy, and grandchildren Sarah, Chelsea, Breck, Acadia and Henry. A memorial service will be held on Monday, May 7th, at 4:00 pm at the West End Collegiate Church on West End and 77th Street, with a reception to follow in the Alumni Gymnasium of the Collegiate School. A short graveside service and interment will take place on Tuesday, May 8th at 11:00 am, at the Warwick Cemetery in Warwick, New York. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made to the Columbia University Memory Research Disorder Group. Checks should be made out to ''Trustees of Columbia University'' with a memo notation ''In memory of James Jacob for Memory Disorder Research''. Checks should be mailed to: The Taub Institute, Physicians & Surgeons, Box 16, 630 West 168th Street, NY, NY 10032.
