
        RUVOLO-Charles Sr., M.D. The Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart and the Medical Staff of Cabrini Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend who served our institution for more than 35 years. Dr. Ruvolo was a dedicated physician who always showed sincere concern for his patients and for Cabrini Medical Center. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife and family. Maurizio Daliana, MD President, Medical Staff Morton Davidson, MD Medical Director Jeffrey Frerichs, President Cabrini Medical Center
