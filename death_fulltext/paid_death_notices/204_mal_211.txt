
        NAGLER--Simon H., M.D. died on January 7, in New York. Dr. Joseph English, Professor and Chairman of the Department of Psychiatry, New York Medical College and the Psychoanalytic Faculty of the Psychoanalytic Institute of New York Medical College mourn the passing of their esteemed colleague, Professor Emeritus of the Department of Psychiatry and former Director of their Psychoanalytic Institute. He was a loved teacher, an inspiring mentor, an outstanding clinician and a caring friend. We express our heartfelt condolences and sincerest sympathy to his wife of 64 years, Thelma, his children and grandchildren.
