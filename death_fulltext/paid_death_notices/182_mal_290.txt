
        WEINGARTEN-Fred P. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Fred P. Weingarten, on March 15. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Tuesday, March 19, 9:30 AM at Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, LI. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
