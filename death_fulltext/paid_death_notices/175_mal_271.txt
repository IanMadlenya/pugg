
        KOLODNY-Martin. Of Riverdale, died peacefully at home on August 25, 2001, after a long battle with lung cancer. He will be forever missed and remembered by his wife Sandra, sons Richard and David, and grandchildren Steven, Lauren and Jonathan. Dear brother of Ruth Levy-Stroh. He was a graduate of Stuyvesant High School and City College and became a Certified Public Accountant. He was a managing partner of Edward Isaacs and Co. and its successor RSM McGladrey, Inc. Services Tuesday, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. We love you.
