
        TRAISTER--Phyllis Kaplan. August 7, 1911-August 26, 2005. She was the wife of the late William Kaplan and the late Paul Traister. Loving mother of Edythe Goldberger and Abraham Kaplan. Mother-in-law to Helene Kaye Kaplan and Fred Goldberger. Grandmother of Helen, Andrew, Mitchell and Jordan. Great grandmother to Alex, Ben and Zachary. She will be missed.
