
        BLOCK-Dr. James D.C. Died August 18, 2000. Beloved husband of Dr. Angela B. Dellepiane; dear brother of Gloria Putnam; loving uncle of Nelson, Mary Helen, and Catherine. He is also survived by his grandnieces and grandnephews, and his cousins Bruce and Barbara, Edward and Wilmot and their children. He brightened the lives of all his relatives, friends and colleagues. He leaves a deep void in our lives, as well as in the lives of his relatives and friends in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
