
        SCHOENBERG-Mel. Members of Pictorial Photographers of America mourn with sorrow the passing of our treasurer and beloved longtime member. He was an avid photographer who gave enthusiastic support and friendship to all in the club. One of of his prize winning pictures will be shown at the Hammond Museum, North Salem, NY, starting Friday evening, November 15. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Lea, his children Doug and Erica, and his entire family. The Board of Directors Pictorial Photographers of America, New York
