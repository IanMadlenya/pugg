
        KUMRO-Arthur C. Age 74. On Tuesday, August 29th, 2000, of Arlington, VA. Beloved husband of 48 years of the late M. Louise Kumro. Loving father of Kathy L. Contributions may be made in his memory to the American Heart Association, PO Box 5216, Glen Allen, VA 23058.
