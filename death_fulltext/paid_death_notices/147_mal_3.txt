
        RICCI-John. Professor Emeritus, NYU, died March 30, age 92. For all his 43 years as a faculty member, he was a great teacher and scientist. He was the pre-eminent authority on phase rule and heterogeneous equilibrium. Consultant Oak Ridge National Lab. Article Encyclopaedia Britannica. Highest honor NYU Alumni Assn. Especially remembered for great integrity, clear thinking and warm personality. Met wife Ada Juliani in Italy. She and brother Mario survive. Ricci and Juliani families will be at Flowers Funeral Home, 714 Yonkers Ave., Thursday and Friday, 2-4 and 7-9. Instead of flowers, send contribution to J.E. Ricci Memorial Fund, NYU.
