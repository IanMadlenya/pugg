
        NEWMAN-Eileen Halleran. Died peacefully at home on December 21, 1999 at age 86. Beloved mother of John E. Murphy, Sheila M. Stuart, Karen M. Miller and the late Peter Murphy. Cherished grandmother of James, Craig, Lauren, Jill and Michael, and loving aunt of numerous nieces and nephews. Last of her generation, she will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may call Wednesday, December 22, 1999, 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Mass of Christian Burial is on Thursday, December 23, 10AM at St. John the Martyr Church, East 72 Street between 2nd & 3rd Avenue.
