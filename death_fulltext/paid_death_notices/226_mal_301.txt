
        OBOLER -- Hon. Alan D., known to his friends as ''El Zorro Gris''--a man among men, a friend among friends. A beloved husband, father, grandfather, father - in - law, brother, brother-in-law, uncle, great-uncle, and friend-loved in eternity. Veteran, lawyer, judge and our Hero-lost his battle on Thanksgiving Day, November 24th. He moved mountains for our family and friends, as a universal humanitarian, creating families with lots of little ''Alans.'' Alabama U. and New York Law alumnus and professor; member of Cornell Club. All the people who love you will cherish your life's journey. Be at peace with your beloved brother, Leonard. Visitation Sunday, 7-9 pm and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 pm, with service Tuesday, 11:30 am, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. Entombment to follow at Westchester Hills Cemetery.
