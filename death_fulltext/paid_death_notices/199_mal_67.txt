
        LUPU--Jack, 96, on August 5, 2003. Co-proprietor of Lupu's Dress Shop in Brooklyn before retiring to Florida. Beloved husband of the late Hattie Lupu, loving brother of Laura Stern and Rusty Remin, and uncle of Diane and Robert Remin, Chip and Jerry Lupu, and the late Sandy Stern. We will all miss his warmth and his incredible stories. Services Friday 11:30AM at Mount Ararat in Farmingdale.
