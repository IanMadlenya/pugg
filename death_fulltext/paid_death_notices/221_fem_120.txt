
        KINGKADE--Mary Jane, 82, died May 26. Dr. Kingkade, former Chair of Chemistry, Hunter College, and Associate Dean of CUNY, administered CUNY's Baccalaureate and Overseas Study Programs. She also developed a public high school program that enabled disadvantaged students to enter and succeed in colleges with pre - medical and other science programs. In recent years she and her husband, Bill, were residents of the Quadrangle, Haverford, PA. She is survived by her son, a grandson, two sisters, and several nieces and nephews.
