
        SISK--Robert J. Of Greenwich, died on August 8, 2004. Former Chairman of the Wall Street law firm of Hughes Hubbard & Reed, he was one of the country's most esteemed trial lawyers. He is survived by his wife Sandra, his children Julie, Robert, Kathryn Tucker, Laura, Philip and John, by Sandra's son Howard Clark, III, four grandchildren and by two brothers Alvin and Sidney Sisk. A memorial service will be held at 1:30pm on August 23rd at the Sisk home in Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations can be made to Yale Medical Oncology Dept, 333 Cedar St, P.O.
