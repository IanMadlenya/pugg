
        BAUDRY-Paul. On July 4, 1999 in his 104th year. Husband of the late Fanny Baudry. Survived by his son Dr. Francis Baudry, his daughter-in-law Christianne Baudry, his three grandchildren Isabelle, Alain and Dominique, and five great grandchildren. His courage, realism, stoicism and great dignity in the last years were an inspiration to all who knew him.
