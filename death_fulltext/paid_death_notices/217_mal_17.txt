
        FRIEDMAN--Jacob. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Jacob Friedman, who together with his beloved wife, Beulah, left an indelible mark on the New York Jewish community through their lifelong generosity and devotion. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
