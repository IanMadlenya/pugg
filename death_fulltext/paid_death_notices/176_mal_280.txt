
        CHUSE-Robert A. Age 93. Of Leonia, N.J. Beloved husband of Eve. Loving brother of Bette Chuse Silverman (Rockville Centre, New York), Lillian Chuse Wurzberg (Long Beach, N.Y.) and Emanuel Chuse (Upper Darby, PA.). Cherished grandfather of Rebecca P. Chuse (Denver, CO.). He was the author of the book, ''Pressure Vessels, The ASME Code Simplified'', published by McGraw-Hill, and was a member of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers. Services at Wien & Wien Memorial Chapels, 402 Park St., Hacksensack, N.J., Thursday, September 20th at 1:30 P.M.
