
        ALEXANDER - George B. Found dead in his New York City home on January 24. An Air Force Veteran and retired professor, he earned his B.A., M.A. and Ph.D. from NYU, and M.A. from Stanford. A deep love of music and literature helped sustain him in a 17 year battle with multiple myeloma. His great courage is remembered by his sister Evon, brother Kyle and his nieces and nephew. After a long struggle he is at peace.
