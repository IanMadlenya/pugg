
        SCHERAGO-Earl J. passed away May 8th after a short illness. Beloved husband of Marsha. Loving father of Gina (James), Susanne (Frank), Jacque (Kirk) and Darrin (Mindy). Adoring grandfather of Caitlin, Devin, Casey and Jena and a devoted brother of Emily. A graduate of Cornell University. President of Scherago Associates, an advertising and marketing company for scientific associations. Earl was an active member of the American Association for Clinical Chemistry and board member of the Van Slyke Society. He held memberships at Turnberry Isle and Crestmont Country Clubs. His loves in life were his family and golf. He will be greatly missed. Funeral services Wednesday, May 10th 11:30AM at Bernheim-ApterGoldsticker Suburban Funeral Chapel 1600 Springfield Avenue, Maplewood, New Jersey.
