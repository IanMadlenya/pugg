
        LEHMAN - Aileen Tarrow. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Aileen Tarrow Lehman, beloved mother of Mark Lehman, a cherished and active friend of UJA-Federation. Mark and his wife, Diane, are devoted to helping those in need and supporting the work of UJA-Federation. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Mark and Diane; to their children, David and Abigail; and to the entire Lehman family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
