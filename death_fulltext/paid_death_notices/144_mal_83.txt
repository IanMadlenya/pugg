
        FRAMPTON-Dr. Merle E. The Board of Trustees of The New York Institute For Special Education (founded in 1831 as The New York Institute For The Education Of The Blind) records with deep regret the death of one of its former Directors and nationally-renowned figure in the field of education and rehabilitation of the visually handicapped. Our sympathy is extended to his family. Gordon C. Dewey, President, Board of Trustees Robert L. Guarino, Ph.D Executive Director
