
        FOSTER--Robert C., age 77, resident of Bergenfield, New Jersey, on September 25, 2003. Devoted father of Robert A. Foster, Victoria Dziezawiec and Marjorie Foster; grandfather of ten. Also survived by his sister Margie Zaremba. He was a decorated World War II veteran with two Purple Hearts, a member of VFW Post 6467 in Bergenfield, NJ. A printer for the New York Times for over 35 years. Buried at the New Jersey Veterans Memorial Cemetery, Arneytown, New Jersey.
