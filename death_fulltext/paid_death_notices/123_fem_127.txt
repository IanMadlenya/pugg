
        OSTERWEIL-Joyce (nee Skarbnik), 63, of River Vale, New Jersey, on April 15, 1997. Beloved mother of Marla and mother-inlaw of Geoff Hewitt. Cherished Nana of Brent and Eric of Westfield, New Jersey. Loving sister of Florence Kent, Jack Skarbnik and the late Howard Skarbnik, and adored by her nieces & nephews. She will always be remembered and deeply missed by her many friends, colleagues and her family. While she faced much adversity, she never lost her ability to look forward to a new day. She was an accomplished poet and a gifted writer. Funeral service on Friday, April 18, at 1PM at Louis Chapel, Fairlawn, NJ, (800-525-3834).
