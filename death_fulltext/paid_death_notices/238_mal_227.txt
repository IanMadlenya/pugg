
        LEVINE--Jack, 64, of NY, on November 10th, 2006 after a long and valiant fight against cancer. A lawyer who devoted his Philadelphia career to social justice, he practiced most recently at Kasowitz, Benson, Torres and Friedman. Son of the late Cecile and Herman Levine. Survived by his wife Molly Fowler, daughters Macklin and Carson, twin sister Jill (Ted) Rosen as well as many cousins and friends. Contributions may be made to a trust being established for his children's education.
        $(6$)LEVINE--Morris M., of Scarsdale, NY and Fort Myers, FL, died on Monday November 13, 2006 from complications resulting from knee surgery. Morris, an engineer who in 1969 invented the unique micro circuitry chip that powered the first digital watch. Beloved husband of Barbara and cherished father to Stephen (Diane) and Lori (Janusz) and grandchildren Justin, Jessica, Andrew and Jared. Services Sunday 10AM at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I.
