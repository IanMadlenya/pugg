
        POLLAK--Helen Field. Postgraduate Center for Mental Health notes with profound sorrow the loss of Helen Field Pollak, wife of our esteemed Trustee, Alan H. Pollak. Helen, who passed away on November 12, was a great friend of the agency who donated much time and energy to help us. We will miss her advice and support. We express our heartfelt sympathy to her husband and children. Aniello A. Bianco, Chairman Dr. Jacob Barak, President and CEO
