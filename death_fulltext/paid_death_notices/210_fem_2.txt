
        BLOCH--Joyce K. Beloved woman of eloquence, empathy, dignity, wisdom, humor, and integrity, with a great love for the arts, died June 27, 2004. With an MA from Teachers College Columbia University, Joyce was a gifted and passionate lifelong educator. As a speech therapist, she was a pioneer in her teaching of and advocacy on behalf of children. Her altruism and dedication to fairness and equality continue to inspire. She is survived by her daughters, Leslie and Kate Bloch, and their spouses, as well as by her grandchildren, Laura, Ben, David and Julia. Joyce was predeceased by her husband, Professor Herman D. Bloch. She will be deeply missed.
