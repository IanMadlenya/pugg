
        FEIT-Dov. 76, of Lawrence, NY, on April 18, 2003. Beloved husband of Anna, father of Mark, Sharon and David. Loving grandfather of seven. Caring brother and father-inlaw. A true military hero of Israel and a proud businessman. His enduring legacy lives on forever. We will all miss him greatly. Services Monday, 11:30 a.m., Boulevard Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY.
        FEIT - Dov. Congregation Beth Sholom records the passing of our esteemed member Dov Feit. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Kenneth N. Hain Dr. Gilbert Klaperman, Rabbi Emeritus Danny Hiller, President Gary Miller, Chairman of the Board
