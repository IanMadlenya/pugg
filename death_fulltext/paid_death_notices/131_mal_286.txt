
        STIX-Edgar. Died at home December 16. Loving and beloved husband of Carol, devoted father of Allen, Wayne, Roger and Gail. Father-in-law of Robert Way and Jane Share. Life-long resident of Scarsdale, NY, served in Submarine Force in World War II, dedicated his life to family and close friends to whom he was a wise, generous and dependable mainstay. Memorial Services were held on December 19, 1997. Donations may be made to Planned Parenthood of Westchester, 11 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, New York 10532 or The Cancer Research Fund, 675 Third Avenue, New York, New York 10017.
