
        NOLAN-Raymond F., 87, of Glen Rock, formerly of Ridgewood, on March 12, 2000. Born in Nyack, NY, Mr. Nolan had been a resident of Glen Rock for over 30 years. Prior to his retirement in 1977, Mr. Nolan had been an Assistant Treasurer with Texaco in N.Y. City. He is survived by his loving wife Mary C. of Glen Rock, a daughter Kathleen Nolan Taylor of Dover, NH, and two sons, Raymond F. Nolan III of Manchester, NH and Douglas G. Thompson of Nevada. Friends may call on Thursday March 16 from 2-4 & 7-9 at the CC Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 306 East Ridgewood Ave., Ridgewood, NJ 07450. A funeral service will be held on Friday March 17 at 11 AM at the Community Church of Glen rock with interment to follow at the Oak Hill Cemetery in Nyack. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory can be made to the Christian Home for Children, 275 State St., Hackensack, NJ 07601-5512.
