
        HARRITON--Patricia A. (nee Daly). At home April 3, age 71. Born in Brooklyn, lived in Manhattan for 40 years, summered in East Hampton for 30 of those years. Worked on Wall Street in the Municipal Bond business. When husband Paul retired from his law practice, they became full time residents of East Hampton. Mrs. Harriton filled her time volunteering for many local charitable organizations. Survived by many cousins, sister-in-law Natalie Becker, many special nieces, nephews, grand-nieces and grand - nephews. Visiting hours Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at Yardley & Pino Funeral Home, 94 Pantigo Road, East Hampton.
