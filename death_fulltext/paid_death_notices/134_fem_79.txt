
        DOUBILET-Florence Baerwald ''Bobbie''. Died peacefully in her home in Elberon, New Jersey, in her 82nd year, after a brave battle with cancer. She will be missed by her family and friends and the entire golfing community. She is survived by her daughter, Jane Kramer, and her son David Doubilet and his wife Anne. Also her son-inlaw Jose Alves and her granddaughters Rain Kramer and Emily Doubilet, her sister, Pauline Falk and her many nieces and nephews. Services will be held at Temple Beth Miriam, Elberon, NJ today 10:30AM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Camp Oakhurst, New York Services for the Handicapped, 853 Broadway Suite 605, New York, N.Y. 10003.
        DOUBILET-Florence B. The Board and staff of the New York Service for the Handicapped/ Camp Oakhurst are deeply saddened at the death of Florence Doubilet, a board member for many years. Her interest in the welfare of disabled children found expression in her loyal support of Camp Oakhurst. To her family go our heartfelt condolences. Marilyn Friedman, President Marvin A. Raps, Executive Director
        DOUBILET-Bobbie. The Board of Trustees and members of the Ocean Beach Club mourn the loss of dear friend Bobbie Doubilet. Her unique style and energy will be missed by her beach club family. Sandy Paskin, President Laurie Lowenstein, Secretary
        DOUBILET-Bobbie. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mrs. Bobbie Doubilet. We offer our heartfelt condolences to her family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
