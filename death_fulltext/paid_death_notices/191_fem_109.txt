
        LENESS - Christine Gibbs. Died peacefully at home on December 9 in her 97th year. Wife of the late George J. Leness. She is survived by a sister Marion Galland, three sons, John, George and Anthony, six grandchildren, and seven great grandchildren. She was a graduate of Smith College and a member of the Colony Club of New York. She was associated with the Grosvenor Neighborhood House for many years, including serving as its president for two terms. A memorial service will be held this summer in Quogue, New York, at the Church of the Atonement.
