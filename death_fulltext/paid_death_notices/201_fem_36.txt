
        DRABKIN -- Sylvia. Born March 5, 1933, New York City, N.Y., passed away Tuesday morning October 7, 2003 following a prolonged illness. Sylvia graduated from Columbia University and the University of Wisconsin earning a Bachelor of Arts and a Master of Arts degree. Sylvia was a long term substitute teacher in a New York City school system, sang opera with the New York City Opera and an accomplished pianist. She is survived by her brother Dr. Bernard Drabkin of Gaithersburg, Maryland, five nieces and nephews and six grand nieces and nephews.
