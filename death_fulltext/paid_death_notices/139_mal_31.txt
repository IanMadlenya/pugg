
        BERALL-Nathan. Of Duxbury, Massachusetts, on August 1, 1998, age 94. Husband of the late Terese (Levine) Berall, father of Jonathan Berall of Brooklyn Heights, NY & Stephen Berall & his wife Ellen Erdwein of Duxbury. Grandfather of Jeffrey & David Berall, both of Duxbury. Also survived by a sister, Sylvia Byalin of Staten Island, NY. Funeral services were private. A memorial gathering will be held on Tuesday, August 4 at the home of Stephen Berall & Ellen Erdwein, 254 Elm Street, Duxbury, from 7-9 PM. For information, Shepherd Funeral Home, Kingston, MA.
