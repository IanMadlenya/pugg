
        HAZEN--Jonathan Lindenfeld. On August 26, 2005, age 43. Beloved husband of Ann Donaldson Hazen, devoted son of Kathleen Dark. Founder and 1st violinist of the Mariani String Quartet; music producer for Telly award-winning documentary ''Death in America.'' He faced his long and debilitating illness with courage, humor and grace. He will be greatly missed by all whose lives he touched. Memorial service at 4PM on Saturday, October 29, 2005, at The Church of the Incarnation, 35th & Madison Avenue, New York City.
