
        O'BRIEN-Eleanor E. December 18, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Jack O'Brien (former Senior Editor of Newsweek Magazine). Loving mother of John and his wife Bea Jae, James and his wife Trang, David and his wife Carol, Peter and his wife Carol, Eleanor Carsey and her husband Kevin, Kerry and his wife Fran and Kevin and his wife Michelle. Cherished grandmother of 24 and greatgrandmother of 11. Beloved sister of Joseph Walsh. An incredible lady whose love touched both her family and friends. A courageous lady whose light continues to shine. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday and Thursday 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, December 21, 10AM at Corpus Christi Church, 529 W. 121 Street. In her memory the family requests that all say a special prayer for the children of the world.
