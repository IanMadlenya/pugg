
        AUGUST--Jesse. 75, of Hobe Sound, FL, passed away Friday, January 23, 2004, at his residence. Born in China, Jesse has resided in Hobe Sound for the past six years, moving here from New York. Jesse was a self employed pearl importer and exporter all over the world prior to retiring. He was a U.S. Army Veteran and he was a member of Temple Emanuel, New York City. Jesse was an active member of Hobe Sound Golf Club, Hobe Sound, FL, he was an avid golfer, bridge player and loved photography. Jesse was well loved by everyone who met him or knew him. Jesse loved his pets. He was the son of the late Edward and Rebecca Slutsky and was predeceased by his son Stefan Slutsky. Surviving are his wife Buzzy August of Hobe Sound, FL, two daughters Shari Lanzarone of Wakefield, MA, and Susan Taplansky of upstate New York, and his two stepdaughters Diane Dann of Chappaqua, NY, and Margie Satell of Simsbury, CT. Jesse is also survived by nine grandchildren. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to a charity of one's choice in his memory. Burial will be in Baron Hirsch Cemetery, Staten Island, NY. For further information please call Plaza Jewish Community Chapel at 212769-4400.
        AUGUST--Jesse. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Jesse August. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Secy.
