
        BIRNBAUM -- Dr. Harvey. Passed away after a long and valiant fight with diabetes and coronary disease at the age of 79. He is survived by his wife Elaine, daughters and sons-in-law Shelly & Mark, Karen & Don Chapman and grandchildren Kerri, Samantha and Josh. He served in the US Navy during WWII. In 1952, Harvey founded the Parent-Child-Adult Guidance Center which today is the Brooklyn Center for Psychotherapy. In 1988, the Center expanded to include New Directions Outpatient Alcohol and Substance Abuse Treatment Program. He was full of foresight and innovation. He was a trusted advisor, a skilled and analytic psychologist, and a respected administrator. He will be greatly missed by all those whose lives he touched.
