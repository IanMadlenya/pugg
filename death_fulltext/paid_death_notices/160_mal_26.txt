
        WILDGRUBER-Rev. Francis C. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of Father Wildgruber on April 30. Reposing at Beney Funeral Home, 79 Berry Hill Road, Syosset, on Tuesday, May 2, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 10 P.M. Funeral Mass on Wednesday, May 3, at St. Edward The Confessor Church, 205 Jackson Ave., Syosset at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
