
        FRIEDMAN-Rayminnie Marks, July 13, 1999. Age 84. Beloved wife of the late Chester S. Friedman. Loving mother of Joseph Friedman & Rita Aron. Adoring grandmother of Jonathan and Timothy Aron and Michael and David Friedman. Dedicated volunteer to many charities and past president of The Women's Auxiliary of Temple Emanu-El. Funeral service will be held on Friday, July 16th, 10AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Womens Auxiliary or The Sunday Lunch Program of Temple Emanu-El, 1 East 65th Street, New York, N.Y. 10021.
        FRIEDMAN-Rayminnie. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation Emanu-El expresses great sorrow at the death of our esteemed Honorary President, Rayminnie Friedman. For more than three decades she served us with extraordinary dedication. She was a caring teacher and mentor to her colleagues and will be missed. Our condolences to her children and grandchildren. Bunny Barb, President Sivia Brodsky, Recording Sec'y
        FRIEDMAN-Rayminnie. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Rayminnie Friedman, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
