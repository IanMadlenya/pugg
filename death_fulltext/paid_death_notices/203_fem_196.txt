
        FAIRBANKS--Mary Herron. Died December 19, 2003. Wife of the late William E., mother of Paul (deceased), Mary Fairbanks Bennett, William and Richard Fairbanks; sister of Helen, Paul (deceased) and Charles (deceased). Former director of public information at the New Jersey Dept. of Higher Education, former chair New York State Hospital Review & Planning Council. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Nature Conservancy, 4245 North Fairfax Drive, Suite 100, Arlington, VA 22203.
