
        INTURRISI-Louis. The members of the Department of Pharmacology, Cornell University Medical College, express our heartfelt condolences to our dear colleague, Dr. Charles Inturrisi, on the death of his beloved brother, Louis. Louis' writings gave pleasure to all of us and we will miss them. We grieve together with the rest of the Inturrisi family and send them all our sympathies. Dr. Lorraine Gudas, Chairperson
