
        GORDON-Jo Ellen. On July 1, 1997, age 48, after a prolonged illness. Loving sister of Andrea Cooper, Michele Gordon and Steven Gordon. Dear sister-in-law of Barry Cooper, cherished aunt of Elizabeth, David, Adam and Brett. Extraordinary friend. Service Thursday, 11:15 am at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave.
        GORDON-Jo Ellen. The Barnard College community is deeply saddened by the death of Jo Ellen Gordon, Class of 1971, and longtime learning specialist in the Barnard Office of Disability Services. Jo Ellen's generosity of spirit, her concern for others, and her vast expertise in the field of learning disabilities made her a most respected colleague and a valued mentor and friend to many students and staff members. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family. Judith Shapiro, President and Dorothy Denburg, Dean of the College Barnard College
