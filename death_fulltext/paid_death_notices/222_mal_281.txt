
        RUSKAY--Joseph A., on July 24, 2005 in his 95th year in NYC. Beloved husband of 57 years of Margot. Devoted father of David and Deborah. Loving grandfather of Morgan, Meagan & Benjamin. He was a highly successful attorney, specializing in anti-trust law, with a lifetime commitment to and passion for democratic and liberal political causes. He was also a member of the Board of Americans for Democratic Action. In his memory, donations may be made to: JCC, Manhattan, 336 Amsterdam Avenue, NY, NY 10023. Services will be private.
