
        KWITKO-Irving D., age 87, born in Russia and resided for many years in Brooklyn and North Miami Beach, Florida with his late beloved wife Sally. Survived by daughter Gloria Bell and son-inlaw Donald of Palm Beach Garden, Florida and son Edward and daughter-in-law Carla of Melville, Long Island. Loving grandfather of Amy, Brian, David and Seth and four great grandchildren. He was a founding director of Jenapo Federal Credit Union in Brooklyn. He will be missed by all for his kindness and charity.
