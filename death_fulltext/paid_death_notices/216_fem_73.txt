
        CAUMAN--Rebecca Lowenstein. The Columbia University School of Social Work and its Alumni Association are deeply saddened at the death of Rebecca Cauman, Class of 1941. A dedicated social worker and devoted alumna, she was a past officer of the Association and a member of its Hall of Fame. A friend to many, she will be long remembered for her caring nature and zest for life. Sincere condolences are extended to her family. Jeanette Takamura, Dean
