
        SONN-William Louis. The Board of Directors and staff of The Rent Stabilization Association mourn the loss of William Sonn, an Executive Committee member of the Board of the RSA. Mr. Sonn was forever attentive to industry concerns as well as to public needs. Mr. Sonn died very much as he lived, caring for the safety and well being of others even in his last heroic moments. He will be missed as a respected leader in the housing community. Our deepest condolences to the entire Sonn family. Joseph Strasburg, President Arnold Goldstein, Chairman
