
        SINGER-Vivian B. Of Rego Park, NY died in her home on January 9, 2002 after battling a long illness with leukemia. Born in 1924, Vivian was a devoted wife to her husband Eugene, a caring mother to her family and a giving and sharing woman to her friends and community. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, at 11:30AM at Parkside Memorial Chapel, Rego Park, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made to the Cancer Research and Treatment Fund, 1440 York Avenue, NY, NY, 10021.
