
        BARSTOW-Richard I. who, for 50 years, practiced medicine from his office in Norfolk, CT, died peacefully October 19, 1999 in Middlebury, VT, surrounded by his family. He was 93. Born in 1906, he opened his medical practice in Norfolk in 1936, retiring in 1986. A memorial service will be held Saturday, October 30, 11 AM, Church of Christ Congregational, Norfolk, CT.
