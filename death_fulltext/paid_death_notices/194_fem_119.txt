
        GROSS-Florence L. Peacefully, at home, on March 16, 2003, at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Alfred Gross. Loving mother of Peter M. and Jane A. Gross. Grandmother of David, Linda and James, and great-grandmother of two. Services private.
        GROSS-Florence L. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Florence L. Gross. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
        GROSS-Florence. The partners and staff of Weiser, LLP mourn the loss of Florence Gross. Our deep condolences to her family.
