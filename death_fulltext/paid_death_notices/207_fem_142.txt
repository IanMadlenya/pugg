
        SOLOW--Rita Newman. Was passionate about her family, her friends, politics, the environment, culture, education and ideas. A lifelong political activist, she taught English at Adelphi University. She died suddenly in Sag Harbor on April 13, 2004. The family requests that those wishing to remmber Rita send contributions to: Peconic Bay Keeper, Box 1308, Riverhead, NY 11901.
