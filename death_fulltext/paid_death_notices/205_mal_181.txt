
        LOVE--Dr. Sidney Irwin, 82. Psychoanalyst, psychologist, educator and renowned family therapist. Dr. Love, author of numerous papers on psychoanalysis and family treatment, died on February 11 in New York City. He was born February 12, 1922, in the Bronx. He received a Purple Heart serving in WWII. He then received a Master's Degree in social work from Columbia University, a certification as a psychologist and psychoanalyst, and received a doctorate in psychoanalysis. He was also co-founder of the Riverdale Seminars in Modern Analytic Psychotherapy. His contributions to the theory and understanding of modern analytic family therapy are immense. He will be missed by all who knew him. He is survived by his beloved wife Shirley, his beloved daughters, Carolyn Bersak and Jeanine Dropkin, four grandchildren and two sonsin-law. Services Sunday, February 15, 11:30 AM, at the Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
