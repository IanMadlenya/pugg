
        RHEA-Alexander Dodson, III. Mr. Rhea of New York City and The Greenbrier, West Virginia, died in New York City Friday, September 1, 2000. He was born in Whitney, Texas. He is survived by his wife, Suzanne Menocal Rhea. He is also survived by his sister, Dorothy Rhea of Fort Worth, Texas. Mr. Rhea graduated from Princeton University. During World War II, he served with the United States Navy with rank of Commander in the North Atlantic and Pacific Theaters. His career with General Motors Overseas Operations covered assignments in the New York office, Mexico, South America, Europe and Australia. He was Chairman and Managing Director of Vauxhall Motors, Limited in the United Kingdom and Chairman of the General Motors Corporation European Advisory Council in London.
