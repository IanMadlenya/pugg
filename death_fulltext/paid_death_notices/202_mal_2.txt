
        MENDLOWITZ--Milton, M.D. October 30, 2003. Dearest husband of Muriel, nee Chertoff. Devoted father of Judith Chertoff Berman, Susan Burnette and Benjamin Mendlowitz. Adored grandfather of nine and great-grandfather. He was a Professor Emeritus at Mount Sinai Hospital where he was affiliated for 65 years as a renouned researcher, teacher and attending physician. Memorial service to be announced at a later date.
