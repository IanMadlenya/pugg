
        MARASCIULLO--Joan M. died on October 21, 2006 at age 94 in Hospice of Palm Beach in Atlantis, FL, where she resided after her retirement. She began her career in hospital administration and was employed by the VA and Mt. Sinai hospitals. She was also employed in the press secretary's office in the White House and as an administrative assistant to Nelson Rockefeller. She was the Director of the US Peace Corps volunteers in Jamaica, West Indies and Rio de Janeiro, Brazil; Director of the Federal OEO in Nassau and Suffolk Counties on Long Island and Asst. Dean of Adm. in the School of Social Work at SUNY Stony Brook. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at St. Matthew's R.C. Church November 10, 2006 , 9:45 AM.
