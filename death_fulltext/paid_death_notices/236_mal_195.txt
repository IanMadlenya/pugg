
        HARL--Sidney W. The Board of Directors of the William S. Paley Foundation is deeply saddened by the passing of Sidney W. Harl, a dedicated and longtime Director and officer. For over 50 years our colleague was a trusted personal and financial advisor to the late William S. Paley and the Paley Foundation. His insights, wisdom and friendship will be sorely missed. We extend our deepest condolences to his family, Kenneth Harl, and Karen, Danny, Melissa and Jessica Cicale. Dr. Henry A. Kissinger, Chairman Patrick S. Gallagher George J. Gillespie III Daniel L. Mosley William C. Paley
