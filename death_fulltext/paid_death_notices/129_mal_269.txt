
        SINGER-Max, M.D. On October 24, 1997 of Stamford, CT. Formerly of New Rochelle and White Plains, NY. Beloved husband of the late Belle P. Singer and Norma Monoson Singer. Devoted father of Elliot Roy Singer, M.D. of Chappaqua, NY, Marcia Rogull of Rye Brook, NY and Roberta Aviram of Kings Point, NY. Dear grandfather of Elan and Kai Singer, Joshua and Sarah Rogull and Jesse, Simona and Armond Aviram. He was a life member of the Medical Society State of New York, a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons, and attending Opthalmologist Emeritus of New York Eye and Ear Infirmary. Services will be held Sunday October 26, 11:30 AM at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd. Interment West Ridge Lawn Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. Contributions may be made to UJA-Federation.
