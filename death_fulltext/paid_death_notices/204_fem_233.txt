
        LEBER--Edith. On January 22, 2004. Faithful wife of the late Leopold. Beloved mother of George and Bernice and mother-in-law of Yvonne and David Rosenberg. Cherished grandmother of Andrew and Melissa Leber and Aaron Rosenberg. Devoted aunt of Mark and Vivian and children. Dear sister-in-law of Dr. Arthur Leber. Holocaust survivor, devoted to Jewish causes, lover of the arts and her family. Services Sunday 9:45 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations in her memory may be made to The Englewood Hospital Medical Center-Nursing Dept or The US Holocaust Museum. Washington D.C.
