
        WOLLMAN--Raye. Born in NY and died in Hollywood, FL at the age of 99. Beloved wife of the late Max B. Wollman. Devoted mother of Barbara Geist, Mitchell Wollman and Betty Usdan. Loving motherin-law of Nancy Wollman and Herman Geist. Grandmother of nine and great-grandmother of 18. She had a great zest for life and lived it to the fullest, sharing her good fortune with others by immersing herself in charitable activities. She will live in our hearts and memories forever. Graveside service, Mt. Eden Cemetery, Mt. Pleasant, NY, 11:30 Friday, January 6.
