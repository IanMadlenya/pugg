
        SATZ--Charlotte of Woodmere, New York and Lake Worth, Florida taken in her 90th year on October 20th. Beloved wife of the late Leonard Satz, devoted mother of Helene Stein and Bettyann Glasser, sister of Mildred Soloway, Florence Lehrer and the late Bernard Zimmerman. Grandmother of Hal Stein, Adrienne Glasser, Jennifer Barmash and Stephanie Callini. Loved by all. ''Our sweet embraceable you''.
