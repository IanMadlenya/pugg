
        CREMIN--Daniel, native of Brosna, Co. Kerry, Ireland. Passed away peacefully on December 18, 2003. Survived by his loving wife Johanna and children Mary, Elizabeth, Caroline and William. Loving grandfather of Johanna. Cherished father-in-law of Christopher and Monnel. Beloved brother of Nora Mary, Shiela, Theresa, Margaret, Michael and Jeremiah. Visitation Saturday 7-9:30 PM and Sunday 2-5 & 7-9:30 PM at Frederick Funeral Home of Flushing, Northern Blvd at 193rd Street. Funeral Mass Monday 9:45 AM at St. Lukes R.C. Church, 16-34 Clintonville Street, Whitestone, New York. Interment in Flushing Cemetery.
