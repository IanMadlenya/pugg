
        BRESLER-Aaron David. Age 73. Retired Director of Antenna Systems Divsion for AIL Systems, Inc. Dr. Bresler was a Life Fellow of the IEEE. He was a graduate of CCNY cum laude in 1944 and received his MEE and DEE from Polytechnic Institute of Brooklyn. Beloved husband of Marjorie (Denker Wiener); loving father of Emily and Tom Kuhny, Susan Bresler, Edie and Kevin Bresler O'Malley and Stewart and Sagit Bresler; caring step-father of Susan and Carol Wiener; cherished grandfather of Tamir, Aviv and Natalie; brother of Helen and Jesse Drogin and Martin and Shirley Bresler. Funeral service Monday, 10 AM, at Gutterman's, Inc, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, Long Island.
