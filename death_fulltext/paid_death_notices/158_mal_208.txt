
        LAIRD-Walter J., Jr. Of Chadds Ford, Pa. on March 15, Age 73. Beloved husband of Antonia Bissell. Devoted father of David, Ian, Philip, Jay, Emily and Stephen. Adored ''Big Bear'' by his nine grandchildren. Senior Vice President of Morgan Stanley Dean Witter. Former Chairman of the Board of Winterthur Museum and Board Member of Tower Hill School, St. Andrews School, the Delaware Trust Company and the Catholic Diocese of Delaware. President of the Wilmington Club. Graduate of Princeton and M.I.T. Services will be held at Christ Church, Christiana Hundred, Wilmington, DE on Saturday, March 18th at 1:30pm. In lieu of flowers contributions to Winterthur Museum, Tower Hill School, or St. Andrews School.
