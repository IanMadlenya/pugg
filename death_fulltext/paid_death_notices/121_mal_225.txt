
        ILUTOVICH-Leon. The Federation of Polish Jews mourns the passing of its Vice President. A leading Zionist personality who devoted all his talents to the Jewish people and to the land of Israel. A survivor of the Holocaust, he was a shining example of the glorious Polish Jewry. Our condolences to his wife Renia and family. Kalman Sultanik, President Jechiel Dobekirer, Vice President Sam Bloch, Treasurer Dr. Solomon Goldman, Executive Secretary
