
        QUINN-Margaret Mary. January 25th, 1999. Beloved daughter of Donald & Margaret Quinn. Loving sister of Diane, Eileen, Mary, Denis and James. Devoted aunt of ten nieces, three nephews and one grandniece. She was the Principal at St. Vincent Ferrer High School, NYC. Friends may call at the Abbey Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue, NYC Wednesday and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, January 29, 10:30AM at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Avenue at 66th Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Margaret M. Quinn Scholarship Fund at St. Vincent Ferrer High School, 151 East 65 Street, NYC 10021.
