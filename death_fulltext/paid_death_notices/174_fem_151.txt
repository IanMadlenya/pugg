
        ZALK-Sue Rosenberg. The Board of Directors and staff of the Feminist Press at CUNY mourn the sudden loss of our colleague, collaborator and friend, Sue Rosenberg Zalk. Sue served for a decade as a member & secretary of our board and chaired our transition and search committees. Her energy, intelligence, leadership, and stalwart support have made an incalculable contribution to our work and will be deeply missed.
        ZALK-Dr. Sue Rosenberg. The Division on Women's Issues and the Division on Social Issues of the New York State Psychological Association are saddened by the untimely loss of Dr. Sue Rosenberg Zalk. She was an ardent and dedicated feminist and an inspiration to her colleagues. Her strength and love of life will be remembered by all.
