
        MOORE-Stratford E. At age 38. Beloved son of Dolorita and James J. Moore, brother of James and Edmund and brother-in-law of Margaret. Died March 30 in his family's home in Hampton Bays, NY, from a sudden heart attack. Stratford, a kind, gentle and loving person, will be missed by his uncles and aunts, Mr. and Mrs. William D. Powers, Mrs. Julia Wallace, Mr. and Mrs. Stratford C. Wallace and Mr. and Mrs. William F. Wallace, and his many cousins. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave. at 84th St., Monday, 10:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Hampton Bays Beautification Society or the Riverhead Train Museum.
