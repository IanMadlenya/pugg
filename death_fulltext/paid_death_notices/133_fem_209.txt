
        MILBANK-Rose. The Board of Governors, Trustees, staff and members of Boys & Girls Clubs of America wish to express their profound sorrow over the psssing of Mrs. Rose Milbank, the wife of Jeremiah Milbank, our dear friend and former President and Chairman. Along with her husband Jerry, ''Posy'' Milbank demonstrated a lifelong concern for America's children, working tirelessly on their behalf, both on the national and local level. We will miss her greatly. Boys & Girls Clubs of America Rick Goings, Chairman Roxanne Spillett, President
