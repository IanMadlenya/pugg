
        KIRBY-Seymour D. Age 66. Of Brooklyn, New York. Passed away January 6, 2001 after a long illness. Mr. Kirby worked in The New York Times press room for thirty years. He is survived by his wife Roslyn, brother Philip, nephew Hyde Kirby, niece Robin Kane, and their families.
        KIRBY-Seymour D. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Seymour D. Kirby, associated with The Times from 1951 until 1984.
