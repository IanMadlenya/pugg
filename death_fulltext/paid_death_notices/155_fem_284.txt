
        MOONEY-Martha Bowers Doherty. of Floral Park, NY at age 85. Beloved wife of the late Vincent J. Mooney. She is survived by her sons Vincent Jr., Christopher, Roger, Walter and Neil and by her daughters Martha Waltien and Katherine Pryor as well as 11 grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. A graduate of Emmanuel College in Boston (class of '35) who also studied at Radcliffe College, Martha founded the St. Thomas More Bookstore in Harvard Square. She is past president of the Emmanuel Club of Long Island and of the Radcliffe Club of Long Island, and was a member of the Board of the West Nassau Mental Health Clinic. Visiting hours at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, Floral Park, on Thursday, December 30 from 2-5 & 7-9:30 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Victory Church, Floral Park, Friday December 31st at 9:30 AM. Burial at Holy Rood Cemetery. Contributions in memory of Martha may be made to the Monastery of the Carmelite Sisters, 1000 Lincoln Street, Santa Clara, CA. 94050-5285.
