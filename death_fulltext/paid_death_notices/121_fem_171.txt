
        SALLICK - Marion Marshall. Peacefully at home on February 21. Widow of Myron A. Sallick, M.D. Survived by her children, Joy Rogers and Richard Sallick, six grandchildren, five great grandchildren, sister and brother-in-law, Margaret and Arthur Lycett, Jr., sister-in-law, Alice Marshall, and dear friend Anne Jacobs. Originally from Bridgetown, Nova Scotia, a graduate of Mount Sinai School of Nursing, long time member of the National Arts Club and the Gramercy Park Neighborhood Assn. She was loved by all who knew her and is greatly missed.
