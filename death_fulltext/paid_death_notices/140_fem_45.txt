
        HAMMOND-Marion French, 90. On September 7, 1998, at Hightstown, New Jersey. She was the widow of Neill P. Overman, Ernest C. Johnson Jr., and Samuel Hammond. She is survived by her son Neill P. Overman Jr. of Stockton, N.J., her daughter Anne Overman Bunn of Short Hills, NJ, her sister Emily French Hazen, and several stepchildren, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. A private graveside service will be held on Saturday, September 19.
