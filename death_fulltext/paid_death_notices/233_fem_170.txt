
        MACKSOUD--Doris J., of Waterville, Maine (formerly of Mamaroneck, New York) died on June 23. Born Doris Deyhle March 15, 1924 in Buffalo, New York. Married George E. Macksoud in 1953 (died March 1993). She is survived by her daughter Marcia Parmenter of Aurora, Co, her sons Mark Macksoud and wife Julie of Waterville, Keith Macksoud and wife Therese Balduzzi of Larchmont, NY rk and five grandsons, Micah and wife Christa, Mason and Marcus Parmenter, Gregory and Kevin Macksoud. She loved New York and will be missed.
