
        FADER-Jutta. The staff of the Kings County District Attorney's Office mourns with deep sorrow the untimely passing of Jutta Fader, beloved wife of David Fader, Executive Director of Fiscal and Human Resouces. In addition to her husband, Jutta is survived by her mother, Herta Schliewert, her sister, Barbara, and partner, Ulla Scholtes. David's friends and colleagues send their heartfelt condolences to him and the entire family. We know she will be truly missed. Charles J. Hynes, District Attorney and staff of the District Attorney's Office
