
        LEIBMAN--Frances, died on Friday, February 2 2007, at her home in Manhattan after a long illness. She was born in Manhattan on March 27, 1915, and attended the Fieldston School and Wheaton College in Norton, MA. The daughter of the late Morris and Irma Moskin, Frances was married in 1937 to Dr. Milton Newman who died 16 months later. In 1946 she married Sidney Leibman, and they had 56 years of a loving married life together before he died in 2002. She was the caring sister of the late Donald Moskin and of Robert Moskin. She is also survived by her sisters-in-law Marietta Moskin and Lynn Goldberg; nieces and nephews James, Linda, Mark, David, Camela, and Nancy Moskin, and grand nephew and nieces Isaac, Lillian, and Mae. A service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 12:30 PM on Sunday, February 4, followed by burial in Westchester Hills Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Frances' memory to the Joslin Diabetic Center, 1 Joslin Place, Boston, MA 02215.
