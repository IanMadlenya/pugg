
        URMAN-Felix, 84. Beloved husband for 30 years of Doris and the late Michelle. Devoted father of Dorothy and Howard Denberg, Mark Urman, Deborah Davis. Dearest stepfather of Ann and Abe Oster, Harry and Cammy Weisleder, adored and adoring Poppy of Michelle, Elizabeth, Abigail, Oliver, Cleo, Avi, Jane, Seth, Dina, Abby and Lexy. He was a strong and a proud Holocaust survivor and the sweetest and greatest man. Services Friday, 11 AM, ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'' 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        URMAN-Felix. The Trustees and Staff of the Museum of Jewish Heritage-A Living Memorial to the Holocaust mourn the passing of Felix Urman, beloved stepfather of our Trustee Ann Oster. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Ann and family. Robert M. Morgenthau, Chairman David G. Marwell, Director
