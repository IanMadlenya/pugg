
        KEYES--Marie A. (Bonanno). Of Bayside, NY, Saturday, May 20. Loving mother of Louise Rutkay & Gregory Isola. Cherished grandmother of Andrew Isola, David, Scott and Caitlin Rutkay. Mrs. Keyes worked for the NYC Board of Education for 25 years. Upon retirement, she volunteered at Old Westbury Gardens, and was Treasurer of the NY Microscopical Society. Memorial celebration to be announced. Condolences to Louise, 973-379-3259 or Gregory, 973-283-2650.
