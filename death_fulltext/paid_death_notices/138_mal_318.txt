
        LATSEY-Van Peter. A prominent gynecologist/obstetrician passed away on July 28, 1998. He is remembered as a loving husband, a kind gentleman and a mentor and generous friend to many. His wife, Angelika, and nephews and nieces, in Athens, Washington, Boston and New York survive him. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel 81st St. and Madison Ave., New York on July 30th from 6PM-9PM. Funeral services will be held at the Greek Orthodox Archdiocesan Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74th Street, New York City, on July 31st at 10AM. Interment Pinelawn Memorial Park. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Michael's Home for the Aged, 3 Lehman Terrace, Yonkers, NY 10705, in memory of Dr. Latsey, will be appreciated.
