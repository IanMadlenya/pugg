
        HECHT-Arlene (nee Shotz). Wife of George Hecht, of San Diego, on September 16, 2002. With her husband she owned the Buyways Gallery for over 20 years, first in Greenwich Village, later in Sarasota. In addition to her husband, she is survived by her two sons, Donald Rosenberg and Fred Rosenberg, both of NYC, and two grandchildren.
