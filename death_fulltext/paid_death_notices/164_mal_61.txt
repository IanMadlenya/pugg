
        MAUKSCH-George S. On August 20, 2000, died suddenly of a heart attack while vacationing in the south of France. He would have turned 81 on August 31. He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Mary, his daughters Judith and Jane, their husbands Richard Cotter and Bill Perri, and four grandchildren, Christopher and Jason Cotter, and Justin and Michelle Perri. Born in Vienna, Austria, he came to the United States in 1938. A veteran of WWII, he graduated from Brooklyn Polytechnic and went on to work for Western Electric, AT&T Long Lines, and the ITT Corporation. In 1976, he founded H&M Automation Systems. He will be remembered for his sense of humor, his great charm and for his love of the good things in life.
