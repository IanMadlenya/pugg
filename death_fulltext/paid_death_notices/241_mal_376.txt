
        LANDAU--Siegfried. The Jewish Theological Seminary mourns the passing of Professor Siegfried Landau and his wife Irene Gabriel. Professor Landau was one of the original teachers and mentors to many of the first students at the H.L. Miller Cantorial School and College of Jewish Music. May their family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Arnold Eisen, Chancellor-elect Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
