
        LITWIN-Joan of Little Silver, on Feb 17, 1997. Beloved wife of Gordon N. Litwin. Devoted mother of Richard, Abby Mooney and Peter. Dear sister of Harriet Goldsmith. Loving mother-in-law of Michael Mooney and Christina Litwin, nee Berti, and adored grandmother of Matthew, Andrew, Sophie and Perry. Memorial service at Monmouth Reform Temple on Hance Road in Tinton Falls, NJ on Sunday, Feb 23, 1997 at 3:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Valerie Fund's Children's Cancer Center at Monmouth Medical Center. Her family will be at home for visitors in Little Silver on Wednesday and Thursday evening from 7-9 PM.
        LITWIN-Joan W. The Trustees of the Monmouth Park Charity Fund are deeply saddened at the passing of our President, Joan W. Litwin. All those who served with her will miss her intelligence, leadership and concern for those in need in Monmouth County. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her loving family. Lois P. Broder, Vice President Elmer Godwin, Secretary
        LITWIN-Joan. The Board of Trustees and membership of the Ocean Beach Club mourn the tragic loss of long-time friend, Joan Litwin. Her life was distinguished by courage and social consciousness. She will be greatly missed. Andrew Arno, President Laurie Lowenstein, Secretary
