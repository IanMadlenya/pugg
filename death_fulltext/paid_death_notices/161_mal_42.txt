
        HUDIS-Dr. Morris Mac, of Monroe Township, NJ, formerly of Yonkers and Irvington, NY. Died peacefully after a long struggle on June 3, 2000. Son of the late Ida and Rafael Hudis. Born in the Bronx, NY. Beloved husband for 53 years of Dr. Ann Hudis, devoted father of Dr. Paula Hudis (Dr. Ira Fink) of Berkely, CA, Iris Hudis of New York City and Dr. Stephen (Merrye) of Princeton, NJ, and adored grandfather of Loel and Suzanne. He graduated from CCNY in 1938,and from NYU Dental School in the Class of 1942. Served from 1941 to 1968 in the U.S. Navy and Naval Reserve, retiring with the rank of Lt. Commander. For 42 years he taught Prosthodontics to many students at NYU Dental School where he was a Professor. A Fellow of the International College of Dentists (FICD), the American College of Dentists (FACD), and the American Academy of Oral Medicine. He lectured internationally and authored numerous articles in the field of Prosthodontics, including a textbook entitled ''Dental Laboratory Prosthodontics.'' He was a leader of the Men's Club at the Jewish Community Center of Tarrytown. He will be missed by everyone who knew and loved him. Graveside services are Monday 2 PM, at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. The period of mourning will be observed at the Hudis residence at (The Ponds) in Monroe Township, NJ, on Monday and Tuesday, and at the Hudis Residence in Princeton on Wednesday.
