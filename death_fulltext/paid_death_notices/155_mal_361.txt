
        WAMPLER-Melvin H., Reverend, Patriarch and devoted servant of Our Lord, formerly of The Bronx, NY and Groceclose, VA. He pastored the Pilgrim United Church of Christ in the Bronx, NY for 39 years and is Pastor Emeritus. Survived by his wife, Emily Sperry Wampler, one son Stanley E. and Raphaela Wampler, and two grandchildren, Matthew F. & Jessica L. Wampler, all of St. Peters, MO. Contributions to the Church or Synagogue of one's choice.
