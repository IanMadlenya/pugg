
        TANG--Dennis Thompson, 52, beloved husband of Paula J. Leicht, of Elizabethtown, PA. Died October 11, 2003. Formerly of Tenafly, NJ., he was the son of Dr. Yushang Tang of Tenafly, NJ., and the late Clifton C. Tang. He is also survived by a brother, Stanley Tang, of Swathmore, PA., and eight nieces and nephews. Contributions may be made in his memory to Adenoid Cystic Carcinoma Organization, ACC Restricted Funds at NORD, P.O. Box 15482, San Diego, CA. 92175-5482 or to Amnesty International USA, 322 Eighth Avenue, New York, New York 10001.
