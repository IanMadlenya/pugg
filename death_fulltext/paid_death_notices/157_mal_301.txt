
        HEWITT-John Hamilton, Jr. Died at home on the Upper West Side of Manhattan on Friday afternoon on February 25, 2000. He is survived by Vivian Davidson Hewitt, his wife of 50 years; his son, Dr. John Hamilton Hewitt III; daughter in-law, Marie Anne Mallonga Hewitt; granddaughters, Leigh Ann and Marivien of Hagerstown, MD; brother in-law Earl Davidson of Waldo, FL; and nieces, Joyce, Natalie and Elena of Fort Washington, MD. A memorial sevice will be held on Saturday, April 15, 2000, at 11:00 AM, at St. Philip's Church, 204 West 134th Street (Manhattan). In lieu of flowers contributions to St. Philip's Church would be appreciated.
