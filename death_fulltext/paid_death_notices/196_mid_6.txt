
        PARNES-Arlene. Died May 8, in Orlando. Born February 1, 1923, in Brooklyn, NY, to Paul and Fay Parnes. She was a gifted artist and photographer. She was also active in JGS of Orlando. She is survived by sons, Michael Groberg, his wife Sandi and their daughters, Tami Hinkley and Shira; and Bruce Groberg, his wife Nelie, their daughter Frances and son Robert. Donations to Jewish Family Services of Orlando.
