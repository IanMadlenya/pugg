
        TINDEL-Seymour, M.D., J.D. 70 years old. Of Kings Point, NY. On November 8, 1998. Beloved husband of Barbara, proud and loving father of Michael and Nathaniel, and cherished brother of Martin. Dr. Tindel practiced medicine and surgery in the Bronx for over 30 years and taught at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. A renaissance man, he became a lawyer in 1989. Loved by family, friends, and patients alike, he will be truly missed by all those fortunate enough to have made his acquaintance. Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, NY, November 10, 1998, at 10:00 AM.
