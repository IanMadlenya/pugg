
        KUPERBERG--David S. 85, of Freehold, NJ (formerly of Brooklyn, NY) on November 20, 2005. He was predeceased by his wife of 61 years Etta Kuperberg. Surviving are two sons, Mitchell Kuperberg and Nelson Kuperberg; a daughter, Roberta Rosenfeld; two sisters, Florence Friedman and Beatrice Montross; two grandchildren; and six great grandchildren. Memorial donations may be made to Deborah Hospital, Browns Mills, NJ. Funeral services will be held at the Higgins Memorial Home, Freehold, NJ on Tuesday, November 22, at 9AM.
