
        MONTAG-May, died peacefully on January 11, at 98 years. Beloved sister of the late Miriam Tannenbaum and Jack Montag, and devoted sister-in-law of Ann Montag. Adored aunt of Toba and Mickey Smith and Jill and Elliot Goldman, and cherished greataunt of Marc and Laurie Smith, Benjamin and Allysa Goldman, Rebecca and Alon Vogel, Liz Goldman and Mike Gibbons, Meredith and Andrew Barbour, and Cathy Goldman. She loved her greatgrandnieces and nephews, Jake, Jason, Aren, Danielle and Justin, and was the ultimate example of all that was kind and good. Graveside services Thursday, January 13, 2000.
