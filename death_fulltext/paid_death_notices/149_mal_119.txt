
        SONNENREICH-Joseph I., Col. The Members of the Board of Directors of the New York Society for the Deaf mourn the passing of our dear friend and former President, Colonel Joseph I. Sonnenreich. His leadership, his wisdom and his commitment to service will be greatly missed by us all. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his wife Rena and to his entire family. Stephen E. Adler, Chairman Paul L. Kohnstamm, President Pearl I. Johnson, Exec. Dir.
        SONNENREICH-Joseph I. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of Joseph I. Sonnenreich, a longtime activist and supporter of the League. Joe served as Chairman of ADL's New York Regional Board and was a member of our National Commission. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Rena and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
        SONNENREICH-Joseph I. The Board of Governors and members of the League of Advertising Agencies are deeply saddened by the death of our past-president, dear friend and colleague, Joe Sonnenreich. Heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife Rena. Mary Boland, Exec. Director League of Advertising Agencies
