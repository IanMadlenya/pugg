
        COHEN-Leonard. Of Riverdale, NY and Boca Raton, FL died on October 26, 2002 from cancer. Born December 25, 1915 in New York City, the second born son of Mary and Irving Cohen. Loving father of Mark Cohen & Lori Klein and grandfather of Erica & Jenna. Dearly beloved by Joyce Horn, the DeGuires & David Bailey and all others who truly knew him. Retired former dentist, practicing in Manhattan and a strong supporter of the state of Israel. Funeral service will be held on Tuesday, October 29 at Forest Lawn, 200 W. Copans Road, Pompano Beach, FL. at 10AM. Interment will be on Wednesday October 30 at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-onthe-Hudson, NY 914-478-1855.
