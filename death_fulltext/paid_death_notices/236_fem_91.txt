
        GROSSMAN -- Marilyn (Klein). Of Somers, died at age 76, on September 15, 2006, following a brief and courageous battle with cancer. Marilyn is survived by her husband Dave, children, Barbara Grossman (Jay Berger), Stephen Grossman (Sally Hellerman), Jeffrey Grossman, grandchildren, Alex, Benjamin, Sophia, Kyra, sister, Gloriane Klein Gamzon and adopted sister, Bella Pasternak. She will remain in our hearts and will be missed and loved forever. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY.
