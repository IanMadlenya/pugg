
        YOUDELMAN - Beatrice. On March 20, 1998, originally of NYC. Cherished and devoted mother of Carol and Barry Widensky and the late Matthew. Widow of Herbert Youdelman. Adored grandmother of Chad, Bradley and Ian. Terrific aunt and friend. Loved by all who were fortunate enough to know her in her lifetime. Her memory will inspire others to count their blessings, not their misfortunes.
