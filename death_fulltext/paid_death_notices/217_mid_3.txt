
        GUILFOYLE--Shirley Anne. Of NYC. Beloved wife of the late John W. Guilfoyle, International Telephone & Telegraph executive. Died at home on Thursday, February 17, 2005 at age 81. Born in Chicago, IL, she and her late husband traveled extensively throughout the world. Loving mother of Patricia (Sandy) Guilfoyle and grandmother of Dr. Rebecca Goldstein, Aaron John Guilfoyle and his wife Liz, and the late Jennifer Goldstein. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street Sunday February 20, 4-7 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Monday 10 A.M. at St. Catherine of Siena Church, 411 E. 68th St. Interment of Christ the King Cemetery, Franklin Lakes, New Jersey.
