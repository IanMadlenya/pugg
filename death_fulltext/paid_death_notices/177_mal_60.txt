
        SAVAS-Anthony. Of Astoria, NY, tragically on September 11, 2001 at the World Trade Center. Tony was a construction inspector for the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey working on the 88th floor of the North Tower. Tony saw front line action during the Korean War and helped save lives there and during the 1993 bombing of the Twin Towers. He loved $2 cigars, dancing, his country and his family. Family and friends will remember Tony for his tremendous wit, boundless sense of humor and joy of life. His children will always admire him for his devotion, example of fairness and unparalleled perseverance. Tony was a committed husband, devoted father and proud grandfather. He is survived by his wife Phaedra, his children Sandra Savas, Christina Christman, and John Savas; daughter-in-law Bernadine Savas and son-in-law Robert Christman; and grandchildren Jessica and John. Reposing: Basis Funeral Home, 27-12 23rd Ave., Astoria, NY, Friday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral liturgy, St. Demetrios G.O. Cathedral, 30-11 30th Drive, Astoria, Saturday 10:15 AM. in lieu of flowers donations to the American Red Cross and Salvation Army would be greatly appreciated.
