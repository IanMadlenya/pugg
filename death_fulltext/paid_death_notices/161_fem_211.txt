
        KRAMER-Rose G. (nee Bernstein). Last of the Bernstein clan. 97 years young. Passed away Sunday, June 25, 2000 after a brief illness. Wife of the late William C., mother of J. Laurence (Laddie) and Minnette, Dr. Marc B. and Diane; grandmother of David and Ronni, Bari and Peter, Penny and Josh; great-grandmother of Matthew, Erica, Brooke, Lindsay and William C. She gave of herself as the matriarch of her extended family and served as a dedicated volunteer into her nineties. Services Tuesday, June 27, 10 AM, Sinai Chapels, Horace Harding Expwy., 162nd St., Fresh Meadows, NY.
