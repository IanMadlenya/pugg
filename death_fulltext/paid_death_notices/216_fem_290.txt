
        DIEDERICH--Margaret Ross. Of New York City, formerly of Sneden's Landing, Palisades, NY, on January 23, 2005, at age 79. Margaret was an extraordinarily gifted, world class harpist and seven time recipient of the ''Most Valuable Player'' award from N.A.R.A.S. She was also blessed with an incredible sense of humor and was a tremendously generous, giving, caring and compassionate person. Predeceased by her husband, William Hunt (Chappy) Diederich, she is survived by her half-sister, Hannah Harris, three children, Susan, Lynn and Michael, and his wife Elizabeth, and two granddaughters, Alana and Mikayla. A memorial service will be held in the spring. Donations in her memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
