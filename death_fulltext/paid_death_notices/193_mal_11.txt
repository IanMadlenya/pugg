
        CLARKE-Joseph P. 86, died at home on January 31, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Eileen Williams Clarke, devoted father of Eileen Guiney, Mary Anne Korsiak, Joseph M., Peter and Kevin. He is survived by three brothers, Rev. Thomas Clarke, S.J., Charles Clarke and Raymond Clarke. A veteran of World War II, Mr. Clarke was an active leader in the building and construction industry for over 60 years. He was President of the New York Roofing Co., and past President of the Building Trades Employers Association. He was chairman of Local 8 Board of Trustees. Mr. Clarke's long career was marked by volunteer service to many organizations. He served on the Board of Directors of Calvary Hospital, The Catholic Medical Center of Brooklyn and Queens, and St. Johns Medical Center. An active member of Sacred Heart Parish, Mr. Clarke was also a longtime advisor to the Archdiocese of New York and the Diocese of Brooklyn and Queens. He was an honoree of the Cardinal's Committee of the Laity. Family will receive friends at Francis X. Hatton Funeral Home, 36-46 Bell Blvd, Bayside, NY, on Sunday and Monday from 2-5 PM, and 7-9 PM. Mass of the Resurrection, Tuesday 11:30 AM, Sacred Heart Parish, Bayside, NY. Interment at Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations to Calvary Hospital Hospice Fund.
