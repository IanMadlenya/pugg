
        ATWOOD--Margaret Parkhurst, of Lake Wales, Florida died on January 31, 2007. She was the daughter of Clarence D. Buckpitt and Ada May Argue. She was a graduate of the National Cathedral School. She was the beloved wife of the late Rawson Atwood, devoted mother of Joan A. Kunzelmann of New York and John R. Atwood of Newcastle, ME, grandmother of five and great-grandmother of three. Services are at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Lake Wales, FL on Monday, February 5, 2007 at 10:00 AM with interment in Vermont at a later date. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to National Cathedral School, Mount Saint Albins, Washington D.C. 20016 or Church of the Good Shepherd, 221 South Fourth Street, Lake Wales, FL 33853.
