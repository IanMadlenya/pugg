
        GRUNTFEST-Irving James, Ph.D. November 1, 1999, in West Palm Beach, FL, age 82. Mourned by special friend Lillian Baker of West Palm Beach, son David of Arlington, VA, daughter Eve Gruntfest of Fort Collins, CO, sister Lillian Cote of West Lafayette, IN. He was a research chemist. Contributions may be sent to Brown University Annual Fund, Providence, RI 02912.
