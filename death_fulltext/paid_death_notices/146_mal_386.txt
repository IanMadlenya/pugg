
        SCHER-Lewis J. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Fresh Meadows, New York. Died on March 13, 1999. A memorial service was held in Florida. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Palm Beach County Florida Library System. Beloved husband of Marion; devoted father of Vicki and Seth; loving grandfather of Hilary; much loved brother of Jack & Belle; and treasured uncle of Paul. ''His life was gentle, and the elements so mixed in him, that nature might stand up and say to all the world, 'This was a man.'''
