
        FOSCATO--Marie Owen. Of Dolphin Green, Port Washington, NY, formerly of Manhasset, NY, passed away on September 30, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Donald A. Foscato, Sr. Loving mother of Donald and wife Linda, New Canaan, CT; Peter and wife Agatha, Fairfield, PA; Barbara Foscato, Holden, MA; Marie Jo Spinella and husband, Philip, Port Washington, NY. She is survived by her cherished 14 grandchildren and 15 great grandchildren and sister Gertrude Ryan. Friends may call at the Fairchild Chapel, Manhasset, Sat. 7-9, Sun., 2-4 & 7-9. Mass of Resurrection, St. Peter of Alcantara, Port Washington, NY Mon. 9:30am. Interment, Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY. In lieu of flowers remember Maryknoll Fathers, Box 305, Maryknoll NY 10545 c/o Rev. William J. Galvin.
