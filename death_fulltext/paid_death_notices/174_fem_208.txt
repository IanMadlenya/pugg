
        MARTIN-Lucille Staebler. 98, died as gracefully as she lived, at home, on July 24, 2001. Wife of the late Murray Martin. Artist, actress, teacher, beloved mother and grandmother of Jack and Lucy Martin Gianino, Bruce and Wilhelmina Martin Eaken, and of Gemina, Antonia, Gian-Murray, Amanda and Webb. Funeral Services Monday, July 30 at 4 P.M. at Saint Bartholomew's Church, 50th Street & Park Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Saint Bartholomew's Church.
