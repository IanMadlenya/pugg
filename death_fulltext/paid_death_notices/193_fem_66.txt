
        BUCKSTEIN-Rochelle J., 61. Of Boca Raton, formerly of New York, passed away Thursday, February 6, 2003. Loving daughter and daughter-in-law. Beloved wife of Mark, devoted mother of Michael and Robin (Michael) Stier. Joyous grandmother of Jason, Jared and Talia. Loved by her extended family of brothers, sisters-in -law, nephews and nieces. As the wind beneath the wings of all who knew her she will always be remembered and cherished as a loving and caring friend. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Hospice By The Sea, Boca Raton, where she spent many days caring for the infirm and where she spent her last peaceful days with us, will be appreciated. Funeral services will be tomorrow, February 9, 2003, at Levitt Weinstein Memorial Chapel, Coconut Creek, Fl. at 9:30AM. Interment to follow at Eternal Light Memorial Gardens, Boynton Beach.
