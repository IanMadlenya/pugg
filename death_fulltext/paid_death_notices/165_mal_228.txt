
        LAVITT-Sanford. The American Camping Association-NY Section is deeply saddened by the loss of Sandy Lavitt, a leader & dedicated supporter of S.C.O.P.E. (Summer Camp Opportunities Provide an Edge), a program that sends inner-city children to summer camp. Sandy was a unique individual whose tireless efforts touched the lives of many children. We will miss his friendship, support and love of life. We extend our deepest sympathy to Arlene, Keith, Jason, Lisa and Michelle. Barry Royce, President ACA-NY Marc Honigfeld, Chairperson of S.C.O.P.E.
