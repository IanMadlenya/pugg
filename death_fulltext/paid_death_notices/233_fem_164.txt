
        Ressler--Elizabeth S. Of Quogue, New York died on June 26 after a long battle with lung cancer. She was born in Englewood, New Jersey on April 2, 1920, the daughter of Helen and Oswald Kirkby. She grew up in Englewood and traveled extensively with her parents. She graduated from Warrenton Country Day School in Warrenton, Virginia and attended Briarcliff Junior College. On September 12, 1941, she married David B. Ressler with whom she had four sons. She was a leader in many community activities in Englewood. She and David moved to Quogue when he retired and she was active in and co-president of the Westhampton Garden Club. She is survived by her husband and sons David B. Jr., Harold K., Richard S. and Stewart K. Ressler. A memorial service will be held at a future date. Donations in her memory may be made to East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978
