        MATSIL-Chana (Annette) The Village Temple notes with great sorrow the passing on Sunday of Chana (Annette) Roth Matsil, longtime Trustee and pillar of the Congregation. Our deepest sympathies to her husband Milton, her mother Pauline Povill, and her children and granddaughter. Funeral services at 11:30 AM, May 23, The Riverside Chapel.
