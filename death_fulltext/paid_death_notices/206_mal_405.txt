
        STEIN--Dr. Myron, a pioneer in asthma and pulmonary embolism research, died from pancreatic cancer Monday at his home in Beverly Hills. ''Mike'' graduated the Boston Latin school in 1942, Dartmouth College in 1946 and Tufts University School of Medicine in 1952. His wife, Dr. Pauline Alpert Stein, died in 1992. He leaves behind his wife Alice and his daughters Lisa, Susan, Amy and Laurie as well as seven grandchildren. Services will be held at Mt. Sinai in Los Angeles.
