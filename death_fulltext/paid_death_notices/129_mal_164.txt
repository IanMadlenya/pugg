
        RISING-Albert E., Jr., on October 11, at his home in Brightwaters, New York. Consulting Metallurgist and Geologist, Dr. of Engineering. He graduated Cum Laude from Princeton University, 1939 and Yale University in 1942. Lt. in US Navy, 1942-1946. Member Sigma XI Society. He is survived by his wife of 53 years, four children and their spouses, eight grandchildren, one of which is married. Family & friends are welcome at his home October 17, 1997 from 2 till 5 P.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the L.I. Maritime Museum, P.O. Box 184, West Sayville, N.Y. 11796, or North Shore University Hospital at Glen Cove, St. Andrews Lane, Glen Cove, New York 11542. Care entrusted to the George C. White Funeral Home, Bay Shore, New York.
