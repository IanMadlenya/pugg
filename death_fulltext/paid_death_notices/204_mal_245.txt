
        DINGLE--Derek, born in London, England, died on Sunday, January 11, 2004 in NYC at the age of 66. Derek was in the Royal Air Force based in Hong Kong. He moved to Canada in the early sixties where he worked as an engineer. In the late sixties Derek moved to New York City. In New York, Derek's reputation as a premier sleight of hand artist spread, he changed professions and became a professional magician. In a short time he became known as a world class Close up magic performer. His two books, Dingle's Deceptions and The Complete Works of Derek Dingle, became instant best sellers and are considered classics in the art of Close up magic. In the early seventies Derek cofounded The Magic Agency with his partner of 25 years, Shelley Carroll. The New York company is devoted to providing great entertainment. Derek constantly performed and lectured on magic around the world. He is survived by Shelley Carroll of New York, his son Steven P. of London, England, his brother Roy and sister-in-law Elaine. A memorial service will be held in his memory on Sunday, February 8, 2004 at Ryan's Daughter, 350 E. 85th St., NYC, from 2-5 PM. If planning to attend, please RSVP to derek@palookaproductions.com.
