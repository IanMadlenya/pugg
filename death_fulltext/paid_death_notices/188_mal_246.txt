
        SOPHER-Sassoon. Wednesday, September 18, 2002 in Geneva, Switzerland. Mr. Sopher was born in Basrah, Iraq, on May 25, 1906. He was the devoted husband of Rachel for 64 years. He was the devoted father and father-in-law of Frederic and his wife Joyce and of Jamil and his wife Lynn, and the devoted grandfather of Margaret, Peter and Philip.
