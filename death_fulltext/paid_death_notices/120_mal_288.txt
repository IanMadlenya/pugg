
        FREEMAN-Benjamin Walter, at age 86 on January 25, 1997 in Margate, FL after a long illness. Beloved husband of Claire for 57 beautiful years, father of Debra and Barry Goldman, and Lynn and David Pravda, doting and loving grandfather to Lisa, Heather, and William Goldman, and Jacqueline and Douglas Pravda, brother of Paul Freeman and the late Lilyan Donner Kaplan. A kind and generous man who will be missed by all who knew him personally, as an attorney or in business (Atlas Corrugated Case Co.). Services at Riverside, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY today at 1:15 PM. In lieu of flowers donations to the American Parkinson's Disease Association or the charity of your choice will be appreciated.
