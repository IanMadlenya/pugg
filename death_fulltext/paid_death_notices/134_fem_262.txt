
        WHITNEY-Betsey Cushing. We mourn the death of our beloved mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother, Betsey Cushing Whitney, who died on March 25, 1998. Her sense of humor, her determination and her courage will be with us always. Our thanks to all who have communicated their love and support. A memorial service to celebrate her life will be held on April 25 at 11AM, Christ Church, Northern Boulevard, Manhasset. In lieu of flowers, contributions to North Shore University Hospital and New York Hospital.
        WHITNEY-Betsey Cushing. The Trustees and Staff of The Museum of Modern Art and Members of its International Council are deeply saddened by the passing of Honorary Trustee Betsey Whitney, a longtime friend of the Museum and a 40-year member of the Council. She and her husband John Hay Whitney were thoughtful and devoted patrons of the Museum as well as generous benefactors and donors. To her daughters Kate and Sara and to her grandchildren and greatgrandchildren, we send our warmest and most heartfelt sympathy. David Rockefeller, Chairman Emeritus Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Musuem of Modern Art, NY Sir Brian Urquhart, Chairman Jo Carole Lauder, President The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art, NY
        WHITNEY-Betsey. The Sankofa Circle expresses its profound sorrow on the passing of the beloved mother of Kate Whitney. We are thankful for the time she spent with us. Lucia Jack, President Joanne Toran
