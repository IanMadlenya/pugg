
        MANDEL--Esther, June 9, 2005, 83 years old. Born in Poland, she came to America at age seven and grew up in NYC. A graduate of Hunter College, she went on to become an executive buyer for Lerner Shops Corp. for over 30 years. A passionate patron of the arts and avid traveler, whe was a woman of singular intelligence and independence. Married to Albert for 57 years. A very loving mother, she is survived by children Lisa, Robert and Karen and sisters Ellen and Elsa. A proud and devoted grandmother of Matthew, Eva, Sam, Julia, Rebecca & Hannah. Services are at 10 A.M. Sunday, Adath Israel, Lawrenceville, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Princeton Child Development Institute.
