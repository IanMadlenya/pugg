
        WOLF-Maggie. Age 45. Beloved wife of P. Geoffrey Lowney, mother of Sam. Marketing Vice President, Polaroid. Graduate of Greenwich High School 1973, Yale 1977, Columbia Business School 1984. She was a delight to everyone lucky enough to know her. Services June 12, 2 PM, at First Parish Unitarian Universalist Church, Concord, MA. Memorials to Wellness Community, 1320 Center St., Suite 305, Newton, MA 02459.
