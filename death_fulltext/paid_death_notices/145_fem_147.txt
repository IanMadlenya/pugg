
        MAZEN-Henrietta. Of Scarsdale, N.Y. On February 16, 1999, at age 90, died peacefully at home. Wife of the late Ben Mazen. Survived by her brother Bernard Stegman, her children Sam Mazen and Marjorie Smith and her grandchildren Ben and Jessica Smith. Service at Garlick's, 1439 Unionport Road, Bronx, N.Y., on Friday, February 19 at 11 A.M. Call 914-365-2358 or 914-472-8716 for information.
