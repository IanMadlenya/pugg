
        CRUIKSHANK--Marianne, of New York, NY, died peacefully in her sleep after a battle with pancreatic cancer on Tuesday, August 17, 2004. Devoted mother to son Doug (Lisa), daughter Christina and loving grandmother of Lindsey and Robbie. Widow of Robert L. Cruikshank, who was killed in the World Trade Center on 9/11. Private family interment. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Barbara Ziegler Palliative Care Educational Fund at Memorial Sloan - Kettering Hospital in New York .
