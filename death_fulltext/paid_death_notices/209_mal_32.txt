
        SALTZER -- Paul, passed away on May 31, 2004. Age 87. Survived by his beloved wife of 60 years, Mildred (Wurman), daughters Susan Saltzer-Drucker (Daniel) and Sandra Saltzer - Duzak (James), grandchildren, Joshua Drucker (Kristin Martin) and Matana Drucker, sister Irene, several nieces and nephews. Paul was a graduate of Baruch School of Business, 1939, and a C.P.A. He was a Captain in the U.S. Army during WW II. He retired from Witco Chemical Corp in 1981, where he was Corporate Vice President and Secretary of the Corporation for many years. Paul was an accomplished violinist and an avid golfer and a sports enthusiast. He will be greatly missed by his family and all who knew him. Funeral service will be Thursday, June 3, at 1:00PM at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers. Donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
