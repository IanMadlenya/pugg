
        KELLY--Charles B. On August 8, 2003, at St. Vincent's Hospital. Son of the late Michael and Frances Schaid Kelly. Esteemed uncle of Maureen Adelman, Patricia Schmidt and John Kelly and their families. Decorated WWII veteran. Charles is survived by Larry Kiss, his life partner of almost 30 years. Visitation Redden's Funeral Home, 325 West 14th Street, NY, NY, on Monday, August 11, from 4-8 PM. Funeral Mass on August 12, at 10 AM, Church of St. Francis Xavier, 46 W. 16th Street. A contribution in his honor can be made to St. Francis Xavier Restoration Fund, 55 W. 15th Street, NY, NY 10011.
