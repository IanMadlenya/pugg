
        WEINMAN-David, 73. Of Jericho, NY, died on April 8. A graduate of CCNY and NYU Law School, he retired from the Dime Savings Bank of New York in 1993. Beloved husband of Sydelle (Chippie). Loving father of Robert and Christine Weinman. Proud grandfather of Rachel and Jonathan. Dearest son-in-law of Lena Klein and brother-in-law of Edythe Nelson and Natalie Roberts. Loving uncle and great uncle. A kind and loving friend who made a difference to all who knew him. We are proud and blessed to have had him in our lives.
