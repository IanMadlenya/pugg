
        WEISS-Mollie G., 92. Died Monday at Nathan Adelson Hospice. She was born September 29, 1908 in Brooklyn, NY. A homemaker, she was a three year resident of Las Vegas and a member of Free Sons of Israel, F.D.R. Chapter, B'nai B'rith and Forest Hills Jewish Center. She is survived by her daughters Barbara Chozahinoff of NY, and Las Vegas, Eilene Weiss of West Hollywood, CA, son Monroe of New York City, sisters Mary Smookler of Torrance, CA., and Sylvia Ross of New York City, brother Julius Marmor of Long Island, NY and four grandchildren Dan, Sharon, Sasha and Piper. Services will be at 1:00 PM today at the Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Blvd. (76th Road), Forest Hills, NY. The family request memorial donations to be made to Nathan Adelson Hospice, 4141 Swenson Street, Las Vegas, NV. 89119.
