
        JAMESON-Edwin C. Jr. In Hamble, England. Of West Palm Beach, FL., at the age of 88, on September 28, 2002. Son of the late Mary G. and Edwin C. Jameson of New York and Sharon, CT. Survived by his sister Mary J.P. Moore and nephews Samuel F. Posey and Nicholas J. Moore, all of Sharon, CT., and David W. Moore of Woodbridge, CT. Memorial service will take place at Christ Church, Sharon, CT. Thursday, October 24, 2002 at 11AM with interment following at the Sharon Cemetery.
