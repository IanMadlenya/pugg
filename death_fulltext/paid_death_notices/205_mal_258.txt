
        GAITHER--Gant. The Board of Trustees of the Princess Grace Foundation - USA mourns with great sorrow the passing of founding Trustee and beloved friend, Gant Gaither. His artistic approach to life and work was an inspiration to all who knew him. Our condolences go out to the family and friends who will miss him dearly. Memorial service will be held at the Episcopal Church of St. Paul in the Desert, 125 W. El Alemeda, Palm Springs, CA, on Saturday, February 28, at 11 AM; reception to follow. Condolences/flowers C/O Jack Parks at Wiefels & Son, 690 Vella Rd, Palm Springs, CA; 760-327-1257.
