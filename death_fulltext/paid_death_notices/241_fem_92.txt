
        GREEN--Florence Miron. Jerry, Helaine and Judy sadly announce the death of Flossie, Smith College 1926, on February 7th, at age 100. Born June 1, 1906 in Elizabeth, wife of Max (2001); grandmother of Laurie, Andrea, Serena, Claudia and Adriana; greatgrandmother of six. Her love of books, joy of nature, and avid interest in people and the world around her will enrich our lives forever.
