
        LURIE-Jack. Beloved husband of Marjorie. Loving father of Ellen and Brian Steklof, Ed and April Lurie, Bill and Susan Lurie. Cherished grandfather of Lisa and Eric Steklof, Elizabeth, Daniel, Jonathan and Korina Lurie. Mr. Lurie was the CEO of the Republic Lens Company in Englewood, New Jersey. He was a graduate of Cornell University. Services were held on April 14, 2000.
