
        BERNHEIMER-Charles. Ph.D. February 21, 1998 at age 55 in Berkeley, CA. Professor of French and former Chair of the Comparative Literature Program at The University of Pennsylvania, devoted teacher, esteemed colleague and friend, beloved brother, son and husband. Authored or edited five scholarly books and numerous articles. Charlie loved to read and write, was passionately interested in the exchange of ideas and an enthusiastic traveler. Survived by his wife Olga Matich, sister, Celia Bernheimer, and mother Gladys Berhemimer. Son of the late Richard Bernheimer. Memorial to be held March 23 at The University of Pennsylvania. Contributions in his name can be made to the American Cancer Society for Research on Pancreatic Cancer.
