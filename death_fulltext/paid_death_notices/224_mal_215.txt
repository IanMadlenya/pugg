
        McQUILLER--Charles Leroy. Better known as ''Pop'', died July 21, 2005. Born in Lancaster, SC on December 31, 1923 to a large family. Son of William and Anne McQuiller. Married to Evelyn Ford McQuiller for 55 years. Father of three sons and grandfather of nine. Served in the US Navy and was a dedicated employee of the New York Times for 33 years. After retirement he enjoyed his later years surrounded by his loving family members and good friends. He brought laughter and smiles to everyone who knew him. He had such a giving soul and a big heart full of love for his family, friends and fellow man.
