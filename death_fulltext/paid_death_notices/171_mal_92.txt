
        SHAFER-Dr. Donald. The Board of Directors and Medical Staff of Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital express deep sorrow at the passing of Donald Shafer, MD. Dr. Shafer, an esteemed member of our medical staff since 1948 and Surgeon Director Emeritus, served with great distinction as Chairman of the Department of Ophthalmology from 1973-77. A pioneer in the field of Vitreo Retinal surgery, a respected teacher, researcher and staunch patient advocate, he was dedicated to the highest standards of medicine. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman Board of Trustees Philip P. Rosenthal, Executive Director Steven Fochios, President Board of Surgeon Directors Jack M. Dodick, Chairman Dept of Ophthalmology
