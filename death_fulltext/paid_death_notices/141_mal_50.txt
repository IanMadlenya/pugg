
        ROSE-Dr. Sydney J. 82. Died on October 3, 1998, in Winchester, MA. Beloved husband for 55 years of Hester. Dear father of Linda Portnay and Jeffrey Rose. Devoted grandfather of Rebecca and Ted Portnay, and Jennifer and Joshua Rose. He practiced family medicine in Flushing, Queens. His kindness and generous spirit will be missed by many. Memorial donations may be made to Camp Sussex, Inc., 110-11 Queens Blvd., Apt. 1BB, Forest Hills, NY 11375.
