
        MANTON--Honorable. Thomas J. on July 22, 2006. U.S. Congressman (7th CD Ret.), Chairman, Queens County Democratic Organization, New York City Councilman (Ret.), District Leader , Anoroc Democratic Club, Inc., Veteran, U.S. Marine Corp (Sgt.), Retired NYPD, Partner in the Law Firm of Manton, Sweeney, Gallo, Reich & Bolz. Cherished grandfather of Marc, Nicole and Danielle Laurent and Liam Henry Manton. Dearest nephew of Della Moore. Also survived by many loving cousins, colleagues and friends. Reposing at Edward D. In lieu of flowers donations to Long Island City Roots Memorial Community Garden, 47-07 30th Place, LIC, NY 11101 or St. John's Prep Scholarship Endowment Fund, 21-21 Crescent St, Astoria, NY 11105 would be deeply appreciated.
