
        SILAS-Metropolitan. A 40-Day Memorial Service for His Excellency Metropolitan Silas, former Greek Orthodox Bishop of the Diocese of New Jersey, will be held Saturday, January 20 at 11:00 a.m. at the Church of Our Saviour, Rye, NY. Born December 27, 1919 on the Greek Island of Corfu, he was a distinguished priest and hierarch of the Greek Orthodox Church in America for 35 years and also served with distinction as a teacher and later president of Hellenic College/Holy Cross School of Theology. Archdiocese Missions Chairman, member of the Appeal of Conscience, beloved by thousands of Orthodox faithful, he will be remembered as a model of benevolence, kindness and reconciliation. ''Memory Eternal''.
