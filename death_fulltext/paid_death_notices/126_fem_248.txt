
        ANECKER-Carole. Of Bronxville and Riverdale, New York. Died after a long illness July 27. Born May 11, 1925 to Charles and Celia Hynes Anecker. She was a real estate broker for the architect and builder Henry Kibel. Survived by son Robert Louis Zervas, twin daughters Maureen Militana Scheublein and Kathleen Militana Busa, both of Bronxville, NY, and 3 grandchildren. Memorial service Monday, August 11, 7:15 P.M. at St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville. In lieu of flowers donations to Bronxville School Foundation for Special Education, 177 Pondfield Road, Bronxville, New York 10708.
