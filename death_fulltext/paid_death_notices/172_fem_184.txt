
        SMALL-Roberta A. Of Clinton Corners, New York, & Manhattan. Devoted mother, mother-in-law, grandmother, sister and aunt, died on May 21, 2001 from complications due to cancer. An advertising executive before retiring to the country life where she became active in local politics and assumed a seat on her town Board of Appeals. Her life was guided by her strength of character, love for family and pride in community. Her last months were defined by bravery, humor and a continued concern for others. She will be deeply missed.
