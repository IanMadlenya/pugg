
        FRIEDEL--Dora (Dorothy). Devoted wife of the late Irving Friedel, mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, sister, aunt and great-aunt. She is survived by her children Celia and Charles Reiner, Walter and Dorothy Friedel, brother Bernard Fox, grandchildren Michael, Eric, Rose, Donald and Connie Reiner, Evan, Erika, Jay, Laura and Gregory Friedel, great grandchildren Daniel and Nicole Reiner and Alexander Friedel and many loving nieces and nephews. Her strength and wisdom will be missed by all who enjoyed her friendship and generosity and by the family who cherished her for all of her 94 years. Services 12 noon, Sunday, April 11, Riverside Chapels, Mt. Vernon, NY.
