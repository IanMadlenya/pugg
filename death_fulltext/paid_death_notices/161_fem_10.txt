
        BARTLE-Annette Gruber (Hania). Died May 25. A dear friend, gifted artist, writer, journalist, world traveler, teacher, linguist, community volunteer, and lover of nature, astronomy, and animals. She will be remembered with respect and love and greatly missed by Halinka, Sally, Janet and Jerry, Joan, John, Gunther, Anne, Wanda, Katy, Rose, Maureen, Kathleen, and Tony.
