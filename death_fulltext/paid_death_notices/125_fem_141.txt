
        WARDWELL-Lelia M., 90, June 11, 1997, peacefully at home. Wife of the late Edward R. Wardwell, mother of Lelia W. Seidner, Allen Wardwell, Helen W. DuBois. Also survived by her sister-in-law Clarissa Wardwell Pell, 9 grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Memorial service Wednesday, June 18, 11am, Church of the Epiphany, E. 74 St & York Ave. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Church of the Epiphany or the the New York Philharmonic.
