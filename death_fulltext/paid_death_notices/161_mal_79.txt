
        HYMAN-Morris D. Born May 6, 1914 in Philadelphia. Died June 6, 2000 in Little Neck, N.Y. A loving man with a big heart and a ready hug, he was cherished by his wife of 56 years Beatrice Lehman Hyman, sons Charles (Elizabeth Greenfield) and Paul, his grandchildren Claire, Catherine and Gregory, late brother Benjamin and late sister Pearl, nieces and nephews and a multititude of friends.
