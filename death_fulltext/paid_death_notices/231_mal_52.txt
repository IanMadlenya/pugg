
        GEEN--William J. A former longtime resident of Pelham Manor and member & Elder of The Huguenot Memorial Presbyterian Church, passed away on April 1, 2006 after a long illness. Born May 30, 1930, Bill was the son of the late William John and Annie (Jones) Geen. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Ruth Parker Geen, and his five children: Barbara LeMaster, David, Elizabeth Gallagher, Robert and Christopher. Bill was an alumnus of Drexel University, and the University of Pennsylvania law school. He was partner of Chadbourne & Parke, LLP from 1971-95, where he was said to combine ''meticulous fairness, intellectual honesty and the fearlessness of a pirate.'' Upon retirement, Bill and Ruth moved to Easton, MD, where he enjoyed sailing, skiing, trumpet and piano playing. Bill's greatest joy was his 12 grandchildren: Kate, Sam, Tom, Rachel, Josh, Liza, Anne, Peter, William, Lucy, Georgia, and Henry. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, April 8 at 1:00PM at the family home in Easton. For information, call Liz Gallagher at 410.822.1257.
