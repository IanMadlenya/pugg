
        WILLENS-Roslyn (Breitman). Widow of the late Jules, died June 17 1998. Bronx born and co-founder co-operator of Manhattan's popular Caffe Cielo. She was among the first woman salespersons in the highly competitive garment industry. Your pals and patrons adore you Roz-and ever will. Funeral service 11AM, Sunday, June 21, Riverside Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street.
