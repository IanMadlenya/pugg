
        WYLIE-Joan E.A. of Rye and Port Chester, NY. Died March 15, 1997. Artist, poet and sailor. She was 79. Widow of Amos K. Wylie & J. Norman Anderson. Survived by J. Norman A. Wylie, Joan W. Minich, Winifred W. Walden and five grandchildren. Memorial 4/12/97 Rye Presbyterian Church. Donations to the Hemlock Society.
