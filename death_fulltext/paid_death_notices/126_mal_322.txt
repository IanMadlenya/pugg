
        STONE-Leo, M.D. Died July 29, 1997, in his 93rd year. Beloved husband and best friend of Marta. A loving brother, father, grandfather and great grandfather. He will be greatly missed. He was a kind and gentle spirit who touched us all. His memory will be cherished by family, friends, colleagues and patients forever. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue on Friday, August 1st, at 3:30 P.M.
        STONE-Leo, M.D. The officers and members of The New York Psychoanalytic Society & Institute express their profound sorrow at the death of their esteemed and beloved colleague and member Dr. Leo Stone. Dr. Stone deeply influenced generations of students and made lasting contributions to the field of psychoanalysis. He was an innovative clinician and uniquely helpful to his patients. Above all he will be remembered and missed as an extraordinary human being. Our deepest sympathy to his wife, Marta. Memorial services will be held on Friday, August 1, 3:30 P.M., at Campbell's, 81st Street and Madison Avenue. Lester Schwartz, M.D., Pres/Soc. Edward Hartmann, M.D., Pres/Inst
