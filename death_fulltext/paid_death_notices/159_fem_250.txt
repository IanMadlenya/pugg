
        STEIN-Charlotte. On Thursday, April 27, 2000. The former President of Na'amat USA and a member of the Board of the National Council of Jewish Women. She will be missed by her daughters Norma Dryce and Vivian Finkelstein, and her six grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. A service will be held at Mt. Moriah Cemetery in Fairview, NJ, on Sunday, April 30, 12 noon. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in her name to either the American Alzheimer's Association, 919 North Michigan Ave, Suite 1100, Chicago, IL 60611; or Na'amat USA, 350 5th Avenue, Suite 4700, NYC 10118.
