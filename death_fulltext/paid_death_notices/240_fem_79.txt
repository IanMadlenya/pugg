
        FEIWEL--Sadie. The congregation of The East 55th Street Conservative Synagogue expresses its condolences to the family of Sadie Feiwel, a true ''eshes chayiel''. A long-time active member and Trustee of our congregation, Sadie brought unique grace to our synagogue. She was esteemed and loved by all. May her family be comforted among the mourners of Israel.
        $(6$)FEIWEL--Sadie; beloved wife of the late Samuel and dear mother of Herbert (Harriet), Victor (Susan), and Rachel Shapiro; adored grandmother of Heidi, Andrew, Nancy, Laura, Cheryl and Robert; cherished great-grandmother of Rae, Leo, Stephanie, William, Max and Ilana. Services Wednesday, Jan. 10th, 12:15 PM at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
