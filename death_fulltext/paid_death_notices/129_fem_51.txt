
        FRANKFURTER-Estelle S. The Board and Staff of the American Associates, Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, mourn the passing of Estelle Frankfurter of New York, a longtime, generous supporter and friend of Ben-Gurion University. We extend our sincere and heartfelt condolences to her family. Kenneth L. Tucker, Pres. AABGU Vered Mallon Greater N.Y. Regional Dir. Lis Gaines N.Y. Regional Chairperson
