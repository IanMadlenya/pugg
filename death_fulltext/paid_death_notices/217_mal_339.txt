
        OCKRIN--John, 88, of Ithaca but forever of New York, the city he loved, died February 21, 2005 predeceased by his wife Olga. Lifelong learner, lover of books, photographer of life in the city, compassionate father who gave his children a sense of possibility. Survived by daughter Diana Ozolins and son, John; eight grandchildren; three great grandchildren who consider him an inspiration.
