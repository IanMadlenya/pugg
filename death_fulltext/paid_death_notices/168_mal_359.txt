
        MITCHELL-William A. (Bill). Beloved husband of Tittica Roberts Mitchell, died peacefully on Sunday morning, January 21, 2001, at Grant Medical Center in Columbus, Ohio. Adoring father of Mark Stuart Mitchell (Sue) and Carol Mitchell Cuoco (Michael) and worshipping grandfather of Kelly, Brian, Katie, and Erik Mitchell and Megan and Daniel Cuoco, he is also survived by much loved sister Doris (Tom) France, sister-in-law Jean Mitchell and many nieces, nephews, grand-nieces and nephews. Preceded in death by his parents, brother Jack and his big old ginger cat, Jebby. Born September 25, 1927, to William C. and Margaret H. Mitchell in Plain City, OH, Mr. Mitchell was a retired partner of Coopers & Lybrand in New York City, served in the U.S. Air Force, was a graduate of The Ohio State University (and member of the OSU Marching Band), and also a member of Beta Gamma Sigma, Beta Alpha Psi, Masonic Lodge of Hackensack, New Jersey, Ridgewood Country Club (New Jersey), TBITL and was a really nifty guy whom everyone loved. A memorial service will be held at 12 Noon on Saturday, January 27, 2001 at the German Village Meeting Haus, 588 S. Third Street in Columbus. No flowers, please. If you so wish, contributions may be made to the William A. Mitchell Memorial Fund at Grant Medical Center, 111 S. Grant Avenue, Suite 350, Columbus, OH 43215-1898. The family will receive friends at the memorial service.
