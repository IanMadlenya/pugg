
        ROTHSTEIN-Sidney N., MD, 83. Passed away on August 13, 1998, from complications of cancer. Born in Warsaw, Poland, he escaped the Nazi onslaught and arrived in America in 1941. He enlisted in the U.S. Army in 1944, and served with distinction, earning a Bronze Star and Purple Heart. In 1946 he opened a solo practice of medicine in the 5 Towns, which he maintained for 50 years. His family loved him dearly. His patients adored his sense of humor, his bedside manner and his clinical skills. We will all miss him dearly. He is survived by Sophie, his wife of almost 59 years; his son, Edward and his family-wife, Ellen and children, Aaron and Emily; his son, Jeffrey; & his brother, Joshua. Funeral services will be held at 12:15 PM on Sunday, August 16th, at Temple Israel in Lawrence. In lieu of any gifts the family suggests that you send a donation to the charity of your choice.
