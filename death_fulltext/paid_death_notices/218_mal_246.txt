
        LINOWITZ--Sol. The Jewish Theological Seminary community mourns the death of Sol Linowitz, 91, a dedicated friend of JTS and former Chairman of its Board of Trustees. A U.S. Ambassador to the Organization of American States, Mr. Linowitz was a world-renowned lawyer and businessman, committed humanitarian and generous philanthropist. Ambassador Linowitz is also remembered for his unstinting efforts on behalf of his Conservative synagogue, the total Jewish community in Washington and Judaism, worldwide. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
        LINOWITZ--Sol M. The American Jewish Committee notes with profound sorrow the death of Ambassador Sol Linowitz, an esteemed AJC officer and beloved friend. As a diplomat, lawyer and business executive, Sol's breadth of expertise and experience in world affairs was remarkable. He served America under three presidents, working, among other issues of global consequence, to find a peaceful resolution to the Middle East conflict. His presence and wise counsel will be sorely missed. We send heartfelt condolences to his wife, Evelyn, his daughters, grandchildren and the rest of his grieving family. E. Robert Goodkind, Pres. Harold Tanner, Hon. Pres. David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
