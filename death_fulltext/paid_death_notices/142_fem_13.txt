
        SALOMON-Rosalind. Died peacefully on October 30, 1998 at the age of 93. She was the beloved wife of the late Gustav Salomon, M.D. The loving mother of Alice and Seymour Rothman and Caryl and George Bernstein. Grandmother of Ellen and Richard Passov, Mark and Phyllis Terry, Alexander Rothman and Nina Sayer, Catherine and David Brous, Jr., and greatgrandmother of Gus Passov and Noah Rothman. Services were held on Sunday.
