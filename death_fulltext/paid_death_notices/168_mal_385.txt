
        TAI-Yuan Shing. The American Heart Association in New York City mourns the passing of Mr. Yuan Tai. Mr. Tai and his family have been longtime friends of our mission. Over the years, their generosity has supported our heart disease and stroke programs to save lives from heart disease and stroke and sponsored the research of young medical scientists. We extend our warmest condolences to Mr. Tai's wife, Jane, their family and loved ones. Steven W. Duff, Chairman of the Board Jonathan L. Halperin, M.D., President Michael L. Weamer, Executive GP
