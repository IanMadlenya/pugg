
        NADORF--Ben. 1919-2006. An icon of the boxing world has died. Ben Nadorf, 87, passed away on June 1, 2006, in Palm Beach Gardens, Fla. A WWII veteran, Mr. Nadorf was President of Everlast Sports Manufacturing Corp. from 1957 to 2000. He made Everlast the national and international brand of boxing; boxing gloves and trunks were worn by many world champions. A loving and generous husband, brother, father and grandfather, he is survived by his devoted wife Sue, brother Louis, son and daughter-in-law Wayne Eugene Jacob and Dulce, grandson Joshua David, daughter and son-in-law Alexis Nadorf Teplitz and Paul Teplitz, and grandchildren Lita Arielle and Spencer Wayne. Services for Mr. Nadorf will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY on Monday, June 5 at 9:30am. He will be buried at Sharon Gardens in Valhalla, NY next to his first wife Leona Yvette and son Wayne Alan. In lieu of flowers please make donations to: Rabbi Emeritus Discretionary Fund, Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd., New Rochelle, NY 10804. (914) 235-1800.
