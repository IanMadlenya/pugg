
        FRYER-William S. It is with deep sorrow that we record the passing January 11, 1999 of our devoted member and past president (1997) of the General Society of Mechanics and Tradesmen of the City of New York. William served on various committees over the years and has been a member of the Society since 1983. Sincere condolences are sent to his family and the membership will miss him. Matthew V. McFarland, President Daniel R. Stimson, Executive Director
