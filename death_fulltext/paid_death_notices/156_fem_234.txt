
        MORRISON-Eleanor B. The Teachers College, Columbia University community mourns the loss of Eleanor B. Morrison, Associate Professor of Speech Pathology and beloved teacher and mentor of students who served the College for 31 years. Her work on the pathology and therapy for adults and children with organic speech problems and initiatives in remedial speech and hearing programs in the New York City public schools are enduring legacies that we will celebrate in her memory. The Faculty and her students will miss her. John Saxman, Chair, Dept. of Biobehavioral Studies
