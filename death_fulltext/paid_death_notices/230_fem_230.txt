
        KEARNS--Patricia Anne. The Boys Scouts of America, Greater New York Councils, acknowledge the passing of Patricia Anne Kearns, and extends its sincere sympathy to her family and friends. Our thoughts and prayers are with all of her loved ones, particularly her husband, William M. Kearns, Jr., a long-time friend, supporter, and Vice President of our Board of Directors. John F. McGillicuddy, President John C. Whitehead, Chairman Charles E. Rosser, Chief Executive Officer
