
        IMPERATORE - Andrew. North Shore Child & Family Guidance Center honors the memory of Andrew Imperatore, Board Member and Past President of the Association from 1981 through 1983. Mr. Imperatore shepherded the Center as it grew and purchased its headquarters building in Roslyn Heights. He is remembered as a gifted, exciting, innovative, and energetic advocate for children, youth and families everywhere. We offer this tribute to a most beloved friend. Marion S. Levine, ACSW Executive Director Sandra Garfunkel President
