
        ITZEL-Josephine M. Age 91, Died April 15, 1997. Daughter of Pauline and Carl Itzel. A Bronx resident her entire life, for over 35 years she taught swimming in New York City public schools, retiring from Walton High School in 1962. She is survived by a sister and brotherin-law Pauline and Frank Kolinek of Freedom, New Hampshire. Burial in Woodlawn cemetary at a later date.
