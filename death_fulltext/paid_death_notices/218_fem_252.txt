
        SHORT--Elinor Monell, died peacefully March 28, 2005 at the age of 99 in Richmond, Virginia having previously lived for many years in Allendale, New Jersey. She was predeceased by her beloved husband of 41 years, William M. Short and her sister, Dorothy M. Short. She is survived by her daughter, Caroline N. Beebe and son-in-law, John H. Beebe, Jr. of Richmond and her son R. Carter N. Short and daughter-in-law, Ann K. Short of Allendale. She is also survived by six grandchildren, John H. Beebe, 3rd and Anne M. Beebe of Chicago, Blair B. Smith and her husband, Harvard, of Richmond, William H. Beebe of Aspen, CO, Ann C. Short of New York City, Caroline S. Beisswanger and her husband, William, of Charolottesville, VA and four great-grandchildren. In addition, she is survived by her brother, Theodore Monell of High Falls, New York. A memorial service will be held in Richmond at a future date. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Church of the Epiphany, Episcopal 55 George Street, Allendale, NJ 07401
