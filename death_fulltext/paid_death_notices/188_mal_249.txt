
        ZACK-David. 85. Of New York City and Princeton, NJ, September 22, 2002. Beloved husband of 60 years of the late Beatrice Bodin; devoted father of Brian G. (Virginia August); Donald B. (Ellen Okun); beloved grandfather of Rebecca, Ian and Daniel Zack and Rachel, Elizabeth and Andrew Zack. Retired partner David Berdon & Co., CPAs; past president, New York State Society of CPAs. Interment private.
        ZACK-David. The partners and staff of Berdon LLP mourn the September 22 passing of good friend and valued colleague, David Zack, of Manhattan. David retired as Comanaging Partner of the CPA firm in 1987. Those who worked with David regarded him as a keen and insightful adviser. Friends and co-workers alike, remember him fondly as a gentleman. Our heartfelt condolences go to his family, friends, and to all those who will miss his presence in their lives.
