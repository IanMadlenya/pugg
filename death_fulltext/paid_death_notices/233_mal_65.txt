
        GOODMAN--Barbara F.The Board and Staff of Citizens for NYC mourn the untimely passing of the beloved wife of our Board member Senator Roy Goodman. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the Senator and his family in this difficult time. Osborn Elliott, Founding Chairman Henry Cornell, Chairman Peter H. Kostmayer, Pres. GOODMAN--Barbara/d. Died June 2. Beloved mother of Claire, Leslie, Randy and grandmother of Ricky, Danny, Nicole, Erika, Thomas and Kaitlyn and mother-inlaw of Rusty and Molly. Adored wife of 50 of the happiest years of marriage to Senator Roy Goodman. Sister of John Furrer. Funeral services Wednesday, June 7 at 11 AM, Carnegie Hall, 57th Street and 7th Avenue. Condolence visitation at Harvard Club, 27 West 44th Street, on Tuesday, June 6, from 7-9 PM, and Wednesday, June 7, from 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Barbara Goodman Fund at Teachers College, 525 W. 120 St, NYC 10027-6696. GOODMAN--Barbara/d. Appeal of Conscience Foundation extends deepest sympathy to Senator Roy Goodman, advisory council trustee, on the loss of his beloved Barbara, devoted partner in public life and philanthropy. Condolences to their children and grandchildren. Rabbi Arthur Schneier President Arch. Khajag Barsamian Rev. Joseph A. O'Hare, S.J. Vice Presidents
