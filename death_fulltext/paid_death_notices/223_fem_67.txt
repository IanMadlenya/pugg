
        WINGATE--Adele, 78, passed away on Friday, August 5 following a nine year battle with cancer. The gallantry with which Adele fought this disease was an inspiration to all. The enormity of the loss of this good, kind and gracious lady cannot be expressed in words. Adele is survived by Bill & Susan, her loving and devoted husband and daughter, by her step-daughter Jeri and her family including grandchild Daniel, and countless friends and admirers in Ocean Beach, Fire Island, the National Council of Jewish Women, and elsewhere. Services will be held Tuesday, August 9 at 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation 341 West 38th Street, New York, New York 10018.
        WINGATE--Adele. NCJWNY Section mourns the passing of our courageous Board member and past officer. Our condolences to her husband Bill, daughter Susan and entire family. Natalie Katz, President Judith R. Golub, Exec. Dir.
        WINGATE--Adele. The Board of Trustees of the Fire Island Synagogue mourns the passing of Adele Wingate. Our deepest sympathies to Bill and Susan. Shaul Magid, Rabbi Harry Trattner, Cantor
