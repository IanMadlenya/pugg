
        ALBERT-Sondra R. Beloved daughter of the late Bernard and Lily Albert. Sister of Sigmund and Edward Albert (Brenda Krepol). Cherished friend of Joan Srager and Sally Dorst. Passed away peacefully January 31 after a long illness. Services will be held Thursday, February 6, 10 AM, at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in her memory to the Jerome Robbins Foundation, 18 West 21st Street, NY, NY 10010.
