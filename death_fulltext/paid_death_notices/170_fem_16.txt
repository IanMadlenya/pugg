
        MANN-Frances. 83, died February 27, 2001. Temple Gates of Zion of Valley Stream is deeply saddened and mourns the passing of Frances, a longtime friend and a guiding force of the Temple. Wife of the late Cantor Emeritus David Mann and mother to her late son Daniel. Our deepest sympathy to her surviving son Zachary Mann, daughter Thelma Mann and her husband Albert Nicolas and their children Daniel, Alexa and Jenny. All donations in memory may be made to the Danny Mann Memorial Fund, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Yeshiva University.
