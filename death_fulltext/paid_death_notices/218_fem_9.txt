
        HOLLAND--Geraldine Mary on February 28, 2005 of Garden City, NY and Breezy Point, after a long battle with ovarian cancer. Cherished wife of Michael J. Holland. Adored mother of Mark, Caroline, Christopher and Alexandra. Beloved sister of Kathleen & Denise Leonard. Also survived by numerous cousons, nieces & nephews. Graduate of St. John's University (1971) and Fordham University School of Education (1991). Her beauty, warmth, courage and dignity will never be forgotten. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave. at 12th Street, Garden City, 2-4 and 7-9 PM Wednesday and Thursday. Mass of Christian burial St. Joseph's Church, Garden City, Friday 10 AM.
