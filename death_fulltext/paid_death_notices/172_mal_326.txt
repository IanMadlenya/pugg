
        BOTARELLI-Angelo. Age 84. Immigrated from Viterbo, Italy in 1953 on the Andrea Doria. Owner of Angelo's Diamond Touch Barber Shop for 46 years in Boro Park, Brooklyn. A true gentleman. He is survived by his wife Erika and his two children, Anna and Marco. His daughter-in-law Antoinette and two wonderful grandsons Fabio and Stefan who will always remember his passion for life. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Rosalia's R.C. Church, Brooklyn Friday 9:30AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Churchill School, 212-722-0610 would be appreciated.
