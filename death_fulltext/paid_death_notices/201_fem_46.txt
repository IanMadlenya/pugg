
        STEINER--Elizabeth (Betty) Guggenheimer, of Great Neck, NY on October 9, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Malcolm Steiner, mother of the late Susan (Betty) Steiner. Survived by her loving son Lawrence and daughter-inlaw Nanci, and grandchildren Jennifer and Jeffrey. The family will be at home Friday, following a private memorial service. Contributions may be made to the Service Guild of Long Island Jewish Medical Center.
        STEINER -- Elizabeth. The Service Guild of Long Island Jewish Medical Center mourns the passing of founding member, Betty Steiner, former President of the Guild and longtime Chairman of the Gift Shop. We extend condolences to the family. Donations to the Guild in her memory would be appreciated. Isabel R. Albom, President
        STEINER--Betty. North Shore Country Club records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Betty Steiner. We extend our deepest condolences to her family and friends. Peter J. Baum, Pres. Stuart Bednoff, Secy.
