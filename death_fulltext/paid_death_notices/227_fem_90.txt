
        SOYER--Jane. The Board and staff of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged note with profound sorrow the passing of Jane Soyer, wife of David Soyer, JASA's founding Director of Community Services. Jane was a kind and gentle soul and a devoted wife, mother and grandmother. She will be missed by all whose lives she touched. We extend our condolences to her husband David, her children, and her grandson. David M. Warren, President Aileen Gitelson, CEO
