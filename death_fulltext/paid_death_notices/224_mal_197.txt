
        SHIMM--Melvin G. Professor Emeritus at Duke University Law School, died September 17, 2005 in Durham, NC at the age of 79. Survivors include his wife Cynia Brown Shimm, M.D., sons David (Carmen) of Charleston, WV, Jon (Judith) of Pittsburgh and grandsons Abe and Alex of Pittsburgh and brother Robert of New York City. For information contact the Riverside Memorial Chapel at 212-362-6600.
