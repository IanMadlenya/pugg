
        RAPPAPORT-Herbert. On April 29, 1999 at age 86. A gentle man and a gentle soul, admired for his keen intelligence and quick wit. He was devoted to his late wife, Eleanor, and dearly loved by his daughters, Judy Tobey and Ruthann Lehrer. He will be missed by his grandchildren, Jackie, Emily, Deborah, Daniel, Ben and Rachel, and his five great-grandchildren. Services at ''Riverside'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, Sunday at 11:45.
