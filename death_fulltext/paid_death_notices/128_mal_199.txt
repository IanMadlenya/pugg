
        HUNT-Bro. Gregory, FSC. Age 71. Assistant to the President of Manhattan College for Alumni Affairs. Devoted son of the late Patrick and May Regan Hunt. Beloved brother of Marion Cahill and the late Thomas Hunt. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. The wake will be held in the Rotunda, Memorial Hall, at Manhattan College campus on Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM and Wednesday, 2-5 and 78 PM. Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated at The Chapel of DeLaSalle and his Brothers Wednesday evening, 8 PM. Interment will take place on Thursday, September 18, from the Christian Brothers Center, 4415 Post Rd, Bronx, NY, leaving at 10 AM, for the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
