
        SCHAEFLER-Gertrude H. Died at home on August 29, 2000 in her 97th year, after a brief illness. Beloved wife of the late Leon Schaefler. Devoted mother of Roberta S. Adler-Lazar, and Kathryn S. Pershan. Fond mother-inlaw of Richard H. Pershan and Nat Lazar. Loving grandmother of six grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Graveside service at Riverside Cemetary, Saddlebrook, NJ, on Thursday August 31, 2000, at 11:00. The family will be at Kathryn's home, 1435 Lexington Avenue, on Thursday evening, August 31st, from 5:00 to 7:00 to receive callers. Instead of flower, please make a contribution to your favorite charity.
        SCHAEFLER-Gertrude H. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Gertrude H. Schaefler our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
