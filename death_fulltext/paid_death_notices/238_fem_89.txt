
        BOSWORTH--Anne. The faculty, students, staff, trustees, and alumni of Saint Ann's School mourn the passing of our beloved friend and colleague Annie Bosworth on November 15, 2006. Founder of the Saint Ann's School Library, Annie inspired forty years of students and teachers with her integrity and dedication, enriching our lives with her fierce commitment to intellectual excellence and her boundless love of books and learning. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, December 10 at 4:30 p.m. at the Church of St. Ann and the Holy Trinity, corner of Clinton and Montague Streets, Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Saint Ann's School Library 129 Pierrepont Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
