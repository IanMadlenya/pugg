
        SCHWARTZ-Lawrence. Of Coronado, CA. On March 31, 1998 after a valiant struggle. He will be greatly missed by his loving children, Ellen Schwartz, Amy and David Kriss, Jeffrey and Lynne Schwartz. Cherished grandfather of Mariel, Carly, Joshua, Danielle and Courtney. Beloved companion of Gail Kempt whose devotion during his last few months was immeasurable, He will live in all of our hearts forever. Services will be held at the Westchester Jewish Center, Rockland and Palmer Avenues, Mamaroneck on April 4 at 11:15 AM, followed by interment at Sharon Gardens. For further information contact Zion Memorial Chapel of Westchester.
