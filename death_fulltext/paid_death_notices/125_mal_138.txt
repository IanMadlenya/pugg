
        CONGER-David Boyd, born on 12/ 17/44 in Hudson, Ohio and died on 6/10/97 at home in Dallas of kidney cancer. He worked for HoechstCelanese for 21 years in New York City, Chatham, NJ and Dallas, TX. Dave is survived by his wife, Sue, and daughter, Katie. The family will receive friends at the funeral home Thursday, June 12, from 7 to 9 PM. Sparkman-Dickey Funeral Home, 17501 N. Dallas Tollway, Dallas, Texas 75287.
