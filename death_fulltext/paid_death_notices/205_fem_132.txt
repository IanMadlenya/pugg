
        MIROTZNIK -- Rosalyn. Passed away on February 15 after a long illness, her family at her side. Beloved wife of Bernard for 54 years, mother of Carol, Charles and Michael, mother-in-law of Jacqueline, grandmother of Bari and Alexa. Devoted friend, confidante and teacher at C.S. 47 in the Bronx for 25 years. What she said of her mother also applies to her-''Her beauty was exceeded only by her goodness.'' She is missed and will forever be in our hearts.
