
        ROCKEFELLER-Rodman C. The Phelps Memorial Hospital community mourns the passing of Rodman C. Rockefeller, who was a dedicated friend and counselor to the Hospital for many years. Mr. Rockefeller was a member of the Hospital's Board of Directors throughout the 1990's, serving on the Planning and Nominating Committees. His broad experience in the fields of finance and real estate brought invaluable expertise to the Board at a time when the Hospital faced important decisions regarding its expansion and growth. Mr. Rockefeller will long be remembered at Phelps for his brilliant insight and his selfless devotion to the community and, especially, to Phelps Memorial Hospital Center. James C.
