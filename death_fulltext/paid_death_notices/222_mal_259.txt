
        YOUNG--Dr. Kevin Scott. Died peacefully in his sleep on July 23 at his home in Easthampton, NY, surrounded by his family and friends: he was 43 years old. Dr. Young, a graduate of Colby College and NYU Medical School, practiced internal medicine in NYC for the last 15 years. He is survived by his loving partner George Laskaris, their dogs Bailey and Brady, parents Linda and Robert Young, brother and sister-inlaw Peter and Naomi, and their children Luci, Kofi and Emmett. Kevin was a passionate physician, gardener, chef, artist and friend. He will be dearly missed by his family, friends and patients. Private services will be held in Easthampton.
