
        SENTNOR-Marvin M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Marvin Sentnor. Dr. Sentnor was a physician with the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for over 30 years. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Jacob Shani, M.D., Pres. David Feldman, M.D., Pres. Elect Sal DeGliuomini, M.D., Secy. Steven Farber, M.D. Treasurer
