
        FEIGELSON-Charles, M.D. On March 24th. Age 71. A psychoanalyst known for his sensitivity to the emotional problems of children. He had been regarded as one of the most gifted psychoanalysts of his generation. Famous among his friends for his sunny ironic wit, his personal sweetness, and superb intellect. Survived by his wife Carolyn, his sons Daniel, Thomas, Jeremy, Paul, his daughter Suzanne, his brothers Harvey and Eugene and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in June. Donations in Charlie's memory made be made to: The Bridge, 712 Park Street, Charlottesville, VA. 22901.
