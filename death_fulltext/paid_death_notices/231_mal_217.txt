
        LEVITAN--Milton. Born in Brooklyn, NY, August 21, 1909. Died peacefully at his home at the age of 96 on April 19, 2006. Son of Daniel Levitan and Anna Gluck; brother to Sidney and Sylvia; loving father to Dan, Richard and Robert; grandfather to Matthew, Jessica, Elana and Olivia; and beloved father-in-law to Diane Edbril and Stacey Winston Levitan. Devoted husband to Minna Osinoff Levitan for 33 years, who died in 1984. Remarried to Zelda Levine, who died in 2004. Graduate of Boys High School, City College and Brooklyn Law School. A member of the New York City Bar Association for 75 years. Started his distinguished legal career as a clerk for I. Gainsburg, his mentor and role model. He became a partner in Gainsburg, Gottlieb, Levitan and Cole, where he worked for more than 55 years practicing corporate and estate law. Trusted counselor and loyal friend to his clients, many for decades. Served in the U.S. Army Air Corps during WWII in Egypt. Longtime member of Temple Emanu-El of the City of New York and the City Athletic Club. He was an eternal optimist, a gentle and kind man who loved life and New York City. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., on Friday, April 21st at 1:30PM. Interment to follow at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Queens, New York.
