
        McMENAMIN -- Joan Stitt. The Directors of The Clark Foundation note with profound sorrow the passing of Joan Stitt McMenamin, a beloved and esteemed educator who was a member of our Board of Directors for the past 25 years. Her passion in advocating opportunity for the education and training of those in need was resolute, and her wise counsel and guidance served our mission well. Joan will be deeply missed and our sympathies are extended to her family and to all those whom she has inspired over the course of her distinguished life. Jane Forbes Clark, President The Clark Foundation
        McMENAMIN--Joan. The InterSchool Orchestras of NY mourn the passing of Joan McMenamin, longtime friend and former trustee. We will miss her wisdom, common sense and love of children. Annabelle Prager, Founder Ann McKinney, Exec. Dir.
