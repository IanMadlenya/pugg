
        KINNE-Evamaria, Prof. Dr. nee Saffran. A great heart full of joy, love and caring has stopped beating. Although it is incomprehensible to all of us, we must announce that Prof. Dr. Evamaria Kinne nee Saffran passed away on December 6, 2002 at the age of 61. Her family is grateful for the sympathy of their friends in this time of deep mourning. Rolf, Daniel and Lynda Kinne
