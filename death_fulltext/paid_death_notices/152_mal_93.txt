
        GOODWIN - Charles Lawrence, passed away on September 1, at age 77. He was the president of Forest Board Paper Group Inc. He leaves a wife, Clara Goodwin; three children, William Goodwin, James Goodwin and Julie Goodwin Biniaris; son-in-law, Chris Biniaris. daughter-in-law, Angela Goodwin; grandchildren, Daniel and Marina, and Christine Benzinger, and her husband, Richard; two great-grandchildren, Matthew and Michael. Services at Austin Knowles Funeral Home 128 Main St. Port Washington, NY.
