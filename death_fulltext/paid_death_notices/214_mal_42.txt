
        KIRSHNER--Herbert J. Died peacefully on November 2, 2004 at the age of 86 at his home with his family by his side. Married for 61 years to his late beloved wife, Natalie (Bailey). Devoted and loving father of Patricia Lowy, Kathy Matson, Peter Kirshner and Jon (Cathy) Kirshner. Adored grandfather to Michael (Shannon) Rusoff, Andrew Rusoff, Jessica Kirshner and Jordan Kirshner. Brother of Alvin and a dear friend of Bob Feuerman. He was an exceptional man who lived life with integrity, values, strength, grace and passion. He will be missed by all. Services on Thursday, November 4, 2004 at 11 AM at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
