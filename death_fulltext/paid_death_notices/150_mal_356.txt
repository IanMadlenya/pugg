
        ADAMS-Anthony Lane, 54, of Greenwich, CT, died Wednesday at Greenwich Hospital. Born in Salt Lake City, Utah, Mr. Adams was the son of Elaine Evans Adams and the late Lane Webster Adams. He was the husband of Louise Rambo Adams. Mr. Adams was a Managing Director Investment Counsel for New Bridge Partners, LLC. He was a Graduate of St. Paul's School in Concord, NH, and Harvard University. He was a member of the Lane W. Adams Achievement Award in Care Giving, Harvard Medical School Neurology Dept., a Board Member of the New York Racket & Tennis Club, Board Member of the Brook Club in NYC, National Golf Links of America in Southampton, NY, and the Round Hill Club. In addition to his wife and mother of Greenwich, Mr. Adams is survived by a son Anthony Lane Adams, a daughter Isabelle Adams, both of Greenwich and a sister Victoria Adams Quan of New York, NY. Services will be announced at a later date. Memorial contributions may be made to Quan Fellowship in Neurobiology at Harvard Medical School, c/o Mary Beth Pearlberg, Harvard Medical School, 25 Shattuck St., Bldg. A, Boston, MA 02115.
        ADAMS-Anthony Lane. The Lane W. Adams Awards Committee of the American Cancer Society mourns the loss of an extraordinary man whose wisdom guided the awards program founded by his father. Our deepest sympathies to his entire family.
