
        WIESNER-Louis A. The Board of Directors and the staff of the International Rescue Committee note with great sorrow the passing of our dear retired colleague, Louis A. Wiesner. As a member of our Board and Executive Committee, Lou made extraordinary contributions to the IRC and the cause of refugees. We have been inspired by his selfless leadership, unwavering commitment and strong passion for helping the world's most vulnerable people. On behalf of the Board and staff, we extend our condolences to his wife, Elizabeth; his children, Jonathan, Liz, Meg and Andrew; his grandchildren, Britta and Spencer; his daughterin-law, Mary Ann, and his son-in-law, Dave. Winston Lord and James C. Strickler, M.D., Co-Chairmen of the Board
