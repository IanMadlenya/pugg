
        ROSENBLAUM--Alan H. Family and friends of Alan Rosenbaum mourn his passing on July 27 at his home in Charlottesville, VA. He was 70 years old. The son of Murray and Rose Rosenbaum. Alan was born in Brooklyn and attended Far Rockaway High School and later graduated from Carnegie Mellon University. A lifelong printer, Alan was also an avid reader and woodworker. He is survived by two sons: Steven Rosenbaum of Manhattan, NY and Roger Rosenbaum of Saugerties, NY, two grandsons Max and Murray Rosenbaum and sister Sue Briansky of Needham, MA.
