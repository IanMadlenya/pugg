
        GREENFIELD-Eva Miller. On October 8, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Loving mother of Eleanor & Henry Lowet, & Susan and Marvin Shapiro.         GREENFIELD-Eva. The East 55th Street Conservative Synagogue sorrowfully records the loss of its esteemed and beloved Board Trustee and past Recording Secretary of our congregation.
