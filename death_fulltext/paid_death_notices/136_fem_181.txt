
        WALLICK-Corinne, of Manhasset, NY and Boca Raton, FL. Beloved wife of Sanders (Sandy) Wallick. Died suddenly on May 27. A loving mother of Steven and Don Schatzberg, a gentle, generous lady who gave of herself to the many causes she supported. Survived by her sister, Sue and Ted Kaskel, brother, George (Shirley) Loeb. Stepmother of Jimmy and Andrea Wallick, and Michelle & Steven Williams, sister-in-law Rhoda Lohre, and many nieces, nephews and grand-nieces and nephews. Services on Friday, May 29 at Riverside Nassau, 12 Noon.
