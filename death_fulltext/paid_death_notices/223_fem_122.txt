
        MITCHELL--Sarah ''Sally'', M.D. Age 83. Died August 10, 2005 after a brief illness. Cum Laude graduate of Bryn Mawr. Graduate Cornell Medical School, third in class, 1947. Worked for New York Hospital, Westchester Division, Westchester County Department of Mental Health. Survived by husband of 48 years Rowland L. Mitchell, Jr., Ph.D., one son and two granddaughters. Memorial service Saturday, September 17th, 3PM Scarsdale Congregational Church, Scarsdale, N.Y. We will miss her.
