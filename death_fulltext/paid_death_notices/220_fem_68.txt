
        JONES--Jennie Rothman, 95, of Beachwood, Ohio. Formerly of Detroit and Sarasota. Former National Chair of the Womens Division of United Jewish Appeal in 1967. Served on National Boards of Hadassah and Girl Scouts. She was the beloved wife of the late Dr. Harry L. Jones, mother of Richard and Jayne Jones and the late Ellen Kushen, five grandchildren, 10 great grandchildren. Services and interment held in Detroit.
