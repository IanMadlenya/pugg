
        GOLDSTEIN-Murray. The Officers and Members of the New York Joint Board, Unite express deep sense of loss at the death of Murray Goldstein, former Manager of the New York Joint Board, ACWA, ACTWU, Unite. He served as Vice President of ACTWU, Vice President of the Sidney Hillman Health Center, Director of the Amalgamated Bank of New York, and Director of the Amalgamated Life Insurance Company. He also served as Chairman of the Workmens Circle, the National Board of Histadrud, American Labor ORT and the Jewish Labor Committee. Murray retired from the New York Joint Board in 1981. His kindness and inspiration will be deeply missed. Our sympathies are extended to his wife Gladyce, his children Bruce, Donald, Stanley and Robert, their spouses, his seven grandchildren, and two sisters.
