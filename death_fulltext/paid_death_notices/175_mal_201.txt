
        CALISCH-Budd, 76, died at his home in Manhattan, August 3, 2001 after a lengthy illness. He was diagnosed with throat cancer in 1993. Budd Calisch was a prominent New York public relations and event planning specialist. His company represented many wellknown brands and companies and organized some of the city's major charity events. Mr. Calisch was born and raised in Toledo, Ohio. He lived in Paris during the early 1950's before moving to New York in 1955. He will long be remembered and missed by his many friends.
