
        FINCH-Christi, 54, on March 14, 2000, after a long illness. Beloved mother of Pilar; devoted daughter of Janice & George Kangas of Lincoln City, Oregon; cherished sister of Kathryn Amrani, Sheila Coventry and Sharon Borgford, all of the Portland area. Memorial services were held on March 17 in Lincoln City, Oregon; a New York memorial service will be held at The Cathedral of St. John the Divine, Amsterdam Avenue and 110th Street on Saturday, April 1 at 4:30 PM. Christi's luminous spirit forever will remain in the hearts of her family, friends and colleagues at Word of Mouth.
