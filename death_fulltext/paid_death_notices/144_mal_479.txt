
        BESTERMAN-Jack. Died on January 25, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Claire. Devoted father of Catherine (Michael). Also survived by many nieces, nephews, cousins and friends. Jack was a survivor of the Holocaust leaving behind his wife and son, mother, father and several siblings. Retired printer of The New York Times, which he loved. He was also a member of the New York Typographical Union and several Jewish organizations. Published poet and editor of several community newspapers including The New York Times, Sunshine/Pensioners and The Big Six Towers Reporter. Burial and services were held at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to a charity of your choice or the planting of trees in Israel would be greatly appreciated.
        BESTERMAN-Jack. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Jack Besterman, associated with The Times from 1954, until retiring in 1982.
