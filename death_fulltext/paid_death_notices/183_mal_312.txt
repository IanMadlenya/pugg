
        HERMAN-John R., M.D. On April 22nd, 2002 in Washington, DC at the age of 86. Formerly of NYC and Hewlett, NY. Husband of the late Marion Seamon Herman. Survived by his three children, Thomas, Elizabeth and Susan, five grandchildren and his brother, George. Educated at Dartmouth College and Cornell School of Medicine. Taught at Albert Einstein College of Medicine, practiced urology in Manhattan and served at the Northport Veterans Administration Hospital. Memorial service will be held at 11 AM on Friday, May 24th, at the United Nations Chapel, 44th St. and 1st Ave. Donations may be made to the American Urological Association History Fund, 1120 N. Charles St., Baltimore, MD 21201. Joseph Gawler's Sons Funeral Home
