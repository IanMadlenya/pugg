
        BECK--James H. The Board of Directors, officers, and members of ArtWatch International, Inc., are greatly saddened by the passing of our founder and president, Professor James H. Beck, whose dedication to artists and their work, and whose perseverance in preserving the world's great works of art will long be remembered. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, Darma, and the entire Beck family. Board of Directors, Artwatch, International
