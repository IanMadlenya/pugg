
        TAYLOR--Shelda, 97, on June 17, 2003. Teacher, Headmistress youth theatre and arts director over 73-year career. From a one room school on Saskatchewan plains to Settlement Houses (Toronto and NYC), Bronx YMHA, McGuffey School (Ohio), K-12 programs at Riverside Church, The (Trevor) Day School of Church of the Heavenly Rest, and the Cathedral School at St. John the Divine, where she also founded the ACT youth program. Private reading tutor, 1975-1997. Passionate birder. Daughters Alison Lavin and Leslie Hefner. Husband Brodie died in 1982. Memorial service in Fall. For info: hefnerk@aol.com >>AD#
