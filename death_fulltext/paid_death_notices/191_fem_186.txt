
        ALBERTS-Cheri. Park East Synagogue mourns the untimely passing of our devoted member and former parent in our Day and Religious Schools, Cheri Alberts, beloved wife of Dr. Richard and mother of Jackson, Marguerite and Henry. Shocked and saddened, we fondly recall her warmth, creativity, and generosity of spirit in service to her family, community and synagogue. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
