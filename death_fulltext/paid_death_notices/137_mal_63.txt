
        POTTER-Harry R., MD. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Harry R. Potter, MD. An attending physician at Roosevelt Hospital for nearly five decades, he was a beloved and revered colleague in our Psychiatry Department who upheld our finest traditions of medical excellence. He will be sorely missed. To his wife Catharine, his children Andrew, John, and Carol, and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Wendy Z. Goldstein Chief Operating Officer Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
