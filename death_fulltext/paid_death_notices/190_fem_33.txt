
        MUNNELL-Frances Clark. November 1. Survived by her loved and loving husband Equinn W. Munnell, M.D., Emeritus Professor Columbia Prestyterian Medical Center. Her children are Thomas C. Munnell, Boston, Catherine M. Smith, Rome, Italy, Elizabeth H. Munnell, Boston. Seven grandchildren, three great - grandchildren. Born 1915, in Korea of American southern presbyterian missionary parents. Mt. Holyoke B.A. Past President PTA. Presbyterian Hospital GYN Social Services. Trustee Gramercy Park, National Arts Club. Memorial services Saturday, November 9, 4 PM, Calvary Gramercy Church, 20th St, Park Avenue South.
