
        POVERENY--Rachel, 92. Beloved aunt, great-aunt, greatgreat-aunt, cousin and friend. Former teacher and guidance counselor at Bronx High School of Science. Profound influence on both children and adults, whom she nurtured intellectually and infused with a joy of life in all forms. Memorial Service November 16, 2003, Society for Advancement of Judaism, 15 West 86 St, NY, NY at 3:30pm.
