
        MAURER-James L., age 65, of Lakewood, NJ, died Thursday at his home. Mr. Maurer worked for the F.W. Woolworth Co. of New York as a Senior Toy Buyer for 46 years, retiring in 1997. He then worked for Imaginarium of North Jersey as a Senior Toy Buyer. Mr. Maurer was a founding member and past president of the Agnes Trapp Memorial Association, which was started 45 years ago in memory of his grandmother. He was a communicant of St. Joseph R.C. Church. Born in North Bergen, NJ he lived in Princeton Junction, NJ before moving to Lakewood 1998. Surviving are his wife Margaret Ann Maurer, son David Maurer of Hamilton, NJ. Sons & daughters-in-law: James J. & MaryAnn Maurer of Ramsey, NJ, John M. & Diana Maurer of Ewing, NJ, Paul T. & Julia Maurer of Hamilton, NJ, Daniel E. & Lori Maurer of Robbinsville, NJ. Brothers & sisters-inlaw: Joseph & Carol Maurer of Pocono Pines, PA, Vincent & MaryLou Maurer of Beachwood, NJ, Eugene & Betsy Maurer of Cranford, NJ, Robert & Mary Maurer of Metuchen, NJ, Thomas & Sharon Maurer of Middletown, NY. Sister & brother-in-law: Marion & Robert Forenza of Pocono Lakes, PA. Seven grandchildren: Lindsay, Stacy, Hunter, McKenzie, Ashley, Caroline & Kelsey Maurer. Visitation will be held Saturday 7-9 and Sunday 2-4 & 7-9, at the Kedz Funeral Home, 1123 Hooper Ave. Toms River. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated 10:00am Monday at St. Joseph R.C. Church, Hooper Ave. Toms River. Interment will follow at Holy Cross Cemetery, N. Arlington, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Jude Childrens Hospital, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis TN 38105, in his name would be appreciated.
