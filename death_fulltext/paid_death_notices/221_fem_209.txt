
        SOKOL--Irene M., of New York City, formerly of Morristown, New Jersey, died quietly on June 28, at SloanKettering Hospital. Loving sister of Helen Lefko and Florentine; dear aunt of Lauren, Janet and Frank; grandaunt of Lindsey, Jeffrey, Christopher and Charles. A graduate of Hunter College, Bryn Mawr and the University of Warsaw, where she received a Doctorate in Eastern European Studies. She taught at Fairleigh Dickinson U., Madison Campus, and was an active member of the Zonta International, serving as Governor of District 3. She will be deeply missed. Funeral Mass will be Saturday, July 2, at 9 AM, at St. Adalbert's R.C. Church, Elmhurst, NY.
