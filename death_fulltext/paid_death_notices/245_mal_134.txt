
        MARASHINSKY--Julius, World War II Veteran, of East Rockaway, New York, died June 10. Survived by wife, Myra, of 57 years, he will be remembered with love, and affection by her and children Amy Sophia and Andrew Marashinsky, Amanda Lundberg, son-in-law, Marcel, and grandchildren Charlotte and Jem, and by a grateful country, in whose service he earned a Purple Heart.
