
        NAGLER--Simon H., M.D., died on January 7, in New York. Born in November, 1910, he is survived by his devoted wife of 64 years, Thelma, two loving children, Harris and Lauren, his daughterin-law Freema, and two granddaughters Arielle and Gabrielle. Services will be held Friday, January 9, at 11:15AM, at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        NAGLER--Simon, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Simon Nagler, M.D., beloved father of our dear friend and colleague, Harris Nagler, M.D., Chairman of Urology at Beth Israel. To Harris, the entire Nagler family and to all who loved him, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Morton P.
