
        RUSS-Isidore. 89, died unexpectedly on August 8, 1998, in Madison, WI. Beloved husband of the late Jeanette Deutscher Russ and Alice Kimmel Russ. Adored father of Rosalyn Hershkowitz and Myrna Russ and grandfather of Elaine Yasuna and Debra Hershkowitz. For over 35 years, he was an outstanding music teacher at the High School of Music and Art in Manhattan. He profoundly influenced many generations of students and is remembered with much affection. Funeral services Schwartz Bros., Queens Blvd. and 76th Road, Forest Hills, NY, Tuesday, August 11, at 12 noon.
