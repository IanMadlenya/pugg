
        OLDENBURG-Lisa Turnure on April 17, 1998, at home. Deeply beloved wife of Richard E. Oldenburg and cherished daughter of Mrs. David A. Turnure. She was blessed with many friends she loved who loved her in return. Memorial service at St. Thomas Church, Fifth Avenue and 53rd Street, at 11 AM on Friday, May 1. Given her love for books, gifts in her memory in lieu of flowers would be gratefully received by the Amagansett Free Library, Main Street, Amagansett, LI.
        OLDENBURG-Lisa Turnure. The Trustees and Staff of The Museum of Modern Art and members of its International Council are deeply saddened by the passing of Lisa Oldenburg. She was a beloved friend and traveling companion, whose profound intelligence, passion for history and the arts, sharp wit, and indomitable spirit enriched the lives of those fortunate enough to have known her. Lisa shared with her husband Dick, the Museum's long-time Director, cherished and abiding friendships with Trustees, Council members, and staff. To Dick, her mother Mrs. David Turnure, and her nephew and nieces, we extend our most heartfelt sympathy. David Rockefeller, Chairman Emeritus Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, NY Sir Brian Urquhart, Chairman Jo Carole Lauder, President The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art, NY
