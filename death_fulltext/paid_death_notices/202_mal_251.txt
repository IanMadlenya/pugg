
        POOLE--Henry P. 40 year resident of Malverne, suddenly on November 22, 2003. Beloved husband of 47 years to Jacqueline. Loving father of Margaret, Nancy Parker (Terrence), Jacqueline Zerilli (Steven), Patricia, Michael J. (Andrea), and Daniel P. (Tara). Dear grandfather of Ryan, Janine, Joseph, Brooke, Jacqueline, Jessica, James, Christopher and Kelly. Cherished brother of Sr. M. Julianna Poole, S.S.N.D. Longtime parishioner of Our Lady of Lourdes R.C. Church, Malverne. Henry was the retired Executive Vice President and General Counsel of the New York Stock Exchange Inc. (1973-1993). Prior thereto he was Vice President and General Counsel of Merrill Lynch. He was a graduate of St. John's University and St. John's University School of Law (1957). Family will receive friends Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Flinch & Bruns Funeral Home, Inc., 34 Hempstead Avenue (corner Peninsula Blvd), Lynbrook. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10:30 AM, Our Lady of Lourdes R.C. Church, Malverne. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury.
