
        HOROWITZ BATZ-Muriel. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Muriel Horowitz Batz, a very generous supporter who embraced Tzedakah through her passionate commitment to the Jewish community. She was a Lion of Judah contributor, and a beloved leader in our Long Island Women's Campaign. She will be greatly missed. We extend our sincerest condolences to her son, Sanford, and Robert Batz. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Diane Wohl, Chair, Long Island Women's Campaign Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
