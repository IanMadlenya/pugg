
        RACKMILL--Judith. On February 15, 2006 at the age of 76. Beloved wife of the late Lloyd. Devoted sister of Martin Taffet and Rosalyn Perlmutter. Loving sister-inlaw to Carol and Nathan. Cherished aunt of Jordan, David, Jacqueline, Andrea and David. She retired as New York regional administrator of The Federal Administration On Aging. Services on Sunday 10AM at Gutterman's Inc, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY.
