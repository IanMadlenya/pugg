
        METCALF-Thomas Newell, Jr, 75, of South Dartmouth, Massachusetts, died Thursday, March 5, 1998, in Beaufort Memorial Hospital, Beaufort, South Carolina. Mr. Metcalf was born June 10, 1922, in Brookline, Massachusetts to the late Thomas Newell Metcalf and Elizabeth Paine Metcalf. He was a 1940 graduate of Groton School. He graduated in 1944 from Harvard University and in 1950 Mr. Metcalf graduated from Harvard Law School. While at Harvard, he was President of the Spee Club. From 1951 until 1969 he was a Foreign Service Officer for the Department of State. He became an investment and financial advisor at Charles A. Collins, Inc in Boston, Massachusetts. He was a US Army veteran. Surviving are his wife, Patricia Thompson Metcalf, South Dartmouth, Massachusetts; Three sons; Thomas N. Metcalf III, Fairport, New York, Jonathan M. Metcalf, Boston, Massachusetts; Patrick R. Metcalf, Memphis, Tennessee; One brother; Robert T.P. Metcalf, Beverly Farms, Massachusetts; Two sisters; Joan Lee, Boston, Massachusetts; Diana Stainow, London, England; Six granchildren. Memorial Services will be at 2:00 PM Thursday, March 12 in the Church of the Redeemer, 379 Hammond Street, Chestnut Hill, Massachusetts. Those wishing may make Memorial contributions to The Thomas N. Metcalf, Jr. Fund c/o Brooks School, 1160 Great Pond Road, North Andover, Massachusetts 01845 or Beaufort Memorial Hospital, Post Office Box 1068, Beaufort, South Carolina 29901. Copeland Funeral Home of Beaufort, South Carolina is in charge of arrangements.
