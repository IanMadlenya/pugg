
        LINZ-Andrew Melchior. 76, on November 12, 2000 after a long illness. Son of the late Rudolph and Ida Linz, beloved husband of Brigitte Loewy Linz, devoted father of Peter Erwin Linz and Andrea Linz Wyckoff. Dear father-in-law of Ann Secord Linz and Scott Allen Wyckoff. Proud and adoring grandfather of Andrew David Linz, Thomas Gregory Linz and Erica Louise Wyckoff. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him. Funeral services will be held on Friday, November 17 at 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 Street. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Linz Memorial Fund for the Gift of Life Program, New York Rotary Foundation, 28 W. 44th St., Suite 1508, NY, NY 10036
