
        BOWDEN--Mary Virginia. Died April 17 at the Sequoias in San Francisco. Born in Pittsburgh in 1914, she was an economist and Asst. VP at New York Life and first woman president of the Chatham College Board of Trustees. She held a B.A. and Honorary L.L.D. from Chatham, and M.A. from Penn State. Memorials may be made to Chatham College, Woodland Rd, Pittsburgh, PA 15232.
