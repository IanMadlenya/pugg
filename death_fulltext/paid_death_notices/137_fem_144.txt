
        STEVENS-Nancy Turner Ford, 92. Died June 10, 1998. She led an active, creative, philanthropic life. She held careers in radio, fashion & advertising. Formerly married to Howeth T. Ford and John E. Stevens. Survived by daughter Cornelia Coles and grandson Charles F. Coles, Jr. and many nieces and nephews. Memorial services Brick Presbyterian Church, Wednesday June 17, 12:30 PM, 91st & Park Avenue, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Brick Church or Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center.
