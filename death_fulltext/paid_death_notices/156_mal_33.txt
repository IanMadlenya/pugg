
        CHASER-Conrad. The Officers and Employees of G & G are deeply shocked by the sudden passing of our Plant Manager, Director and dear friend. Connie was a loyal Executive Officer for over fifty years and served us in many capacities with manifold talents. His loss will be irreparable and the warmth of his personality will be missed by all who knew him. Our deep condolences to his beloved wife Betty, his devoted daughters Paula and Lisa, his dear family and his many loving friends. G & G Instrument Corp. Ira M. Levy, Pres.
