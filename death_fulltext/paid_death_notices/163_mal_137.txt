
        MAHONEY-David J., III. 40, of Royal Palm Beach, Florida, passed away suddenly on August 10, 2000, at his home. David, originally of New York City and Bridgehampton, NY, was an area resident since 1992. He was the owner and CEO of Flips, Inc., a local internet service providing company. He leaves his wife, Patricia M. Mahoney, Wellington, FL; three children, David J. (D.J.) Mahoney, Casey Mahoney and Dillon Mahoney, all of Wellington, FL; sister, Barbara Ann Mahoney, Water Mill, NY; aunt and uncle, Helene and Robert Mahoney, Bridgehampton, NY; stepmother, Hildegarde Mahoney, Palm Beach, FL, and Lausanne, Switzerland; two stepbrothers, Arthur (Monique) Merrill, Muttontown, NY, Robert (Christina) Merrill, Locust Valley, NY; five nephews and three nieces. Funeral services will be held in Florida. For further information contact Tillman Funeral Home, West Palm Beach.
