
        GARCY -- Nellie (Nelson). Passed away Wednesday, January 12th, 2005 peacefully at home in her 97th year. Beloved wife of the late William Garcy. Adoring mother of the late Deanne Garcy. Treasured aunt and great-aunt to Lance and Marilyn Garcy, Allison and Erika Garcy, Phillip and Barbara Phillips, and Jane Garcy Benninson. She leaves behind many loving and devoted friends. Nellie was a true woman of substance, dignity and grace. Her words of wisdom, her love of life and nature will always be remembered. Her kindness and warmth will touch us forever and she will continue living in our hearts everyday. Memorial service private.
