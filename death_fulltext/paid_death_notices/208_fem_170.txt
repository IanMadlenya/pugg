
        MANLEY--Marguerite, beloved wife of her late husband, Otto, mother of Edward, John and Frances and daughters-in-law Maya and Susanne, grandmother of Alan, Audrey and Thomas Fritton, Jr., Cynthia and Thomas Baldwin, Catherine, Chris and Sarah, and greatgrandmother of Esme Fritton, we mourn your passing. Your pure and generous spirit believed in nothing but the good in each person you met and in each cause you supported. You lived a life of grace and unconditional love. A memorial service will be held at the Hitchcock Church in Scarsdale, NY, on Saturday, May 22, at 11:00 AM, followed by a private family burial. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Camphill Village USA, Copake, New York 12516, or Making Headway Foundation, 115 King St., Chappaqua, New York 10514.
