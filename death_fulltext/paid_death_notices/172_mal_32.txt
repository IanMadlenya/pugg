
        HOFFBERG-William Emannuel, 77. Engineer, President and coowner of AIW-Alton Iron Works, Windsor, Connecticut, a specialty military parts manufacturing company originally founded by his father in New York in 1930. Mr. Hoffberg was born in The Bronx, New York in 1923, graduated Brooklyn Polytechnic in 1941 and was awarded a degree in mechanical engineering from Pratt Institute in 1947. He served in the Navy during both World War II and the Korean Conflict. He is survived by his wife of over fifty years, Rina R. Hoffberg, two sons, three grandsons, a sister and a brother. Services will be held on Thursday, May 3, 2001 at 12:30 PM, at Schwartz-Jeffer, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, New York with burial to follow at Mt. Zion Cemetery in Long Island City.
