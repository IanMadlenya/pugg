        KLEIN--Frances H., passed away peacefully at home in NYC October 13, 2005. Raised in Monticello, NY, Frances's family owned Skliars Hotel. Graduate of Hunter College Class of 1938. Dedicated employee of UJA Federation for over 20 years. Founding member and literary editor of QUEST. Published playwright and short story writer. Survived by cherished brother, Albert (Ann), adoring nieces and nephews, Elizabeth, Debra, Kenneth and Paul. Services Sunday, October 16, 2005, 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
