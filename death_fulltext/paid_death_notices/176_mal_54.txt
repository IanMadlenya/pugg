
        KALATSKY-Morton. The Board of Directors, Trustees, Officers, staff and members of the Yad Avraham Institute mourn the passing of Morton Kalatsky, father of our cherished Dean and teacher, Rabbi Yosef Kalatsky. We express our profound sympathy to his wife, Jennie, & the entire Kalatsky family. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yad Avraham Institute Ira Rennert, Trustee Louis Glick, Trustee David Lobel, President
        KALATSKY-Morton. We express our heartfelt condolences to Jennie, Herbert, Shimon and Rabbi Yosef Kalatsky on the loss of their beloved husband and father Morton Kalatsky. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. David and Judith Lobel Richard and Julie Lobel Robert Lobel, Esther Lobel
