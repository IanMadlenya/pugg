
        RUSSO--Serafina, 92, died peacefully at home, January 28, 2004. Known as Sally and Sadie to friends and family. Beloved wife of the late Nicholas L. Russo, M.D., mother of Maria T. Russo and D. Nicholas Russo; sister of Philomena DiIorio. Retired kindergarten teacher of PS 32, Queens. Her unwavering positivity for humankind and its creative works and her deep respect for the gifts of nature were hallmarks of her persona. Contributions in her memory may be made to any charity reflecting these values. A memorial gathering will be held in the spring.
