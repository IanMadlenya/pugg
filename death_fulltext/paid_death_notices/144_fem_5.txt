
        POLLACK-Rae. Passed away peacefully December 28, 1998 at the age of 89. Loving mother of Leslie and Yvonne Pollack and the late Susan Pollack. Devoted grandmother of Jonathan Pollack, Jennifer Reiner and Fredrica Pollack. Proud great-grandmother of Annie Reiner. Beloved wife of the late Martin Pollack, the late Samuel T. Cohn, and the late Arnold A. Freedman. Adored sister of the late Bill Fischer and the late Murry Fischer. Service Sunday January 3, 1999, 9:15AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla NY, after which the family will be receiving visitors until 9PM at their home. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the Katonah Museum of Art, Route 22 at Jay St, Katonah NY 10536 or the American Jewish Historical Society, 2 Thornton Rd, Waltham MA 02453 or the charity of your choice.
        POLLACK-Rae. The leadership & staff of UJA-Federation of New York in Westchester record with sorrow the passing of Rae Pollack, beloved mother of our devoted donor Leslie Pollack. An active philanthropist throughout her long life, Rae Pollack's charitable legacy is being carried on. May her memory be for a blessing. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair, James S. Tisch, Pres., Barry J. Effron, Chair, Westchester General Campaign, Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
