
        WILLIAMS--Bruce D. Of Hackensack, NJ, February 21 at Englewood Hospital. Born June 15, 1932, Washington, DC. Raised in the Midwest, graduated Harvard 1954. Stanford MBA 1956. Spent 30 years as a corporate financial officer in industries including petroleum, heavy machinery, and M&A. Board member of Englewood Hospital from 1976-2002. Board member of Greater Bergen County YMCA for 20 years, 3 as President. Mr. Williams is survived by his wife, Deirdre, by his daughters, Katie Fahs of Atlanta and Nicole Dietrich of NYC, and four grandchildren. Service at First Presbyterian Church, 150 E. Palisades Ave, Englewood, 11AM Monday, February 27. Memorials to the Harvard Class of '54, 124 Mt. Auburn Street, Cambridge MA 02138 or Englewood Hospital, 350 Engle Street, Englewood, NJ 07631.
