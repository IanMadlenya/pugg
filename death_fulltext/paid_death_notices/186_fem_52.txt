
        GLASHOW-Judith Magill. Of Saddle River, NJ, and Boca Raton, FL. She was a graduate of Skidmore College in Saratoga Springs, NY. She is survived by her husband Dr. Jules, daughter Jamie Pardell, sons Dr. Jonathan and Jeffrey, grandchildren Logan and Connor Pardell. Services are Thursday, 11 A.M. at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Route 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
        GLASHOW-Judith. The Board of Governors and membership of Edgewood Country Club record with deep sorrow the death of Mrs. Judith Glashow, an esteemed member & extend to her beloved husband Jules and family its deepest sympathy. Kenneth Apfel, President Sidney Gottlieb, Secretary
