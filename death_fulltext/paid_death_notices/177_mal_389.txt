
        SCHWEIKERT-Louis. On October 25, 2001, at age 88. Born in Orange, NJ, Lou spent over 50 years at E.B. Myrowitz in New York City as an obstetrician. His clients included members of the White House, Hollywood celebrities and public officials. Lou's greatest enjoyment was oil painting, travel, golfing and fishing with his friends in New York and West Palm Beach. He leaves behind his beautiful wife Marcia, daughter Mary Lou Perkins, sister Irene Tempel, grandson James Perkins and many nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Sunday, October 28th, 2-4 and 6-8 PM. Funeral Mass at Blessed Sacrament Church, Roseland, NJ, Monday, 10 AM. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, East Hanover, NJ. Please omit flowers.
