The Headmaster, Trustees, Faculty, Staff, Boys, Alumni and Parents of Saint David's School, New York City mourn the passing of a dear friend, former Board President, Trustee from 1972 to 1986 and Honorary Trustee thereafter. Tom's tremendous support and dedication to the School for more than three decades is truly appreciated. Our prayers are with his wife, Janet, four sons, Thomas Jr., 1973, Michael, 1974, James, 1976, Peter, 1978, and three daughters, Janet, Anne and Susan at this difficult time.
