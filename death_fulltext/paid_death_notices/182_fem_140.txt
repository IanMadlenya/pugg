
        SCHULTZ-Carol. On March 10, 2002. A graduate of The High School of Music and Art with degrees from Long Island University & Fairleigh Dickinson University. Carol worked as a Psychologist in the Rutherford New Jersey School System and maintained a practice in New York where she was trained at the New York Center for Psychoanalytic Training. An amateur violinist and music lover Carol played in the Bergen Philharmonic for almost forty years. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Devoted mother of Peter, Jill and Frances. Dear sister of Joy Stiller and Marion Dragoon. Cherished grandmother of Samantha, Rebecca and Isaac. Services on Tuesday 1 PM at The ''Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
