
        RESNICK-Arthur, MD, 82. Died July 24 at Monmouth Medical Center. He was a specialist in oral rehabilitation. He obtained his BS in Pharmacy from St. John's in 1939; DDS from University of Pennsylvania in 1943; lectured at St. John's College of Pharmacy from 1948 - 1964; Associate Clinical Professor at The College of Dentistry, New York City; Co-founder, Fellow of NYU Academy of Oral Rehabilitation; Captain in U.S. Dental Corps, Master of Academy of General Dentistry. Member; Military Surgeons USA; Royal Society of Health; International College of Dentists. Surviving are his wife, Vicki Trop Resnick, daughter Jill, sister Ruth, grandchildren and great-grandchildren.
