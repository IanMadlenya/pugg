
        SCHNEITZER-Mildred. Died February 20, 2002. Beloved wife of 50 years to Herbert, who predeceased her. Dear mother of Leila Schneitzer Wilner. Mother-in-law of Gregg Wilner. Cherished grandma of Jordan and Harris Wilner. Services Friday 10 AM at I.J. Morris, 1895 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn. Interment to follow, New Montefiore Cemetery. Donations in her memory to the Poverty Fund, UJA Federation, 130 East 59 St., New York, NY 10022. May she rest in peace.
