
        KAMMERMAN--Beatrice Barnstone. Passed away on August 4, 2006 on her 91st birthday. She was predeceased by her ''Prince,'' Oscar, in 1979. Beatrice will be remembered lovingly by her children John, Lesley, Mickey and Steve, her grandchildren Joshua, Kathy, James, Rachel, Nicholas and Suzanne, her great-grandchildren Sophia, Lucas, Julian and Justin. Devoted sister of Willis Barnstone, Billie and Al Eiger, Diana and Herb Warner, the late Howard Barnstone and the late Freddie and Henry Weissbrodt. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, August 8th at 10:00 a.m. at Schwartz Brothers - Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, NY, 718-263-7600
