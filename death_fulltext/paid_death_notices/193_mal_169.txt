
        LAPLACE-Maurice L. On February 13, he passed away at the age of 85. He was the loving and devoted husband of Dorothy, his wife of fiftyone years. He was an adoring and caring father of Karen, Robin and Lisa and a proud and doting grandfather of Jennifer, Athina, Alex, Lauren and Sophia. He was survived by his cherished sister and brother, Frances and Saniel. He was predeceased by his dear sisters and brothers: Helen, Ruth, Mildred, Alma, Oscar and Noa. He was a partner of L.J. & M. Laplace Chemicals. As a WW II veteran of the U.S. Army, he was decorated with the American Defense Ribbon, American Theater Ribbon, the European-African-Middle Eastern Ribbon, the Good Conduct Medal and the Victory Medal. We will remember his generosity of spirit and love for his family. He will be in our hearts forever. Relatives and friends are invited to services on Sunday, February 16 at 12:30PM at Gutterman Musicant on the corner of Park St. and Passaic St., Hackensack, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Hepatobiliary Research (Fund Number 205355), Box E, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
