
        SILZER--Florence Joan Windatt. Died peacefully on August 13th, 2004. Joan was born in Winnipeg, Canada, the daughter of Dorothy A. and Richard D. Windatt and grew up in Plainfield, NJ where she was married and started a family. In 1966, they moved to the Fairfield-Westport CT area. She is survived by her brother Richard A. Windatt, her children Parker, Dana, Julia and Elizabeth, and her grandchildren. Her quick smile, bright laugh and unfailing generous spirit will be missed by family and friends. A memorial service will be held on August 27th, 2004 at 11 A.M. in the Trinity Episcopal Church, 651 Pequot Road, Southport, CT.
