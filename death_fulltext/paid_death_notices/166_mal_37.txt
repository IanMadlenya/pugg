
        PHILLIPS-Nicholas R., Sr. Age 64, of Wyckoff, NJ, died Thursday, November 2, 2000 at the Valley Hospital, Ridgewood. Born in NY, NY, a Wyckoff resident for the past 27 years. Nicholas was the owner and president of Wexkill Inc. of Wyckoff and a former money manager at Kalb Voorhis & Company, NY, NY and a former partner at Fahnestock. Member of St. Elizabeth's RC Church, Wyckoff, an Army veteran and former member of the Ridgewood Country Club and NY Athletic Club. Beloved husband of Eileen (nee Weber). Father of Eileen Marie Phillips of Wyckoff; Nicole Ann Phillips of Virginia Beach, VA; Nicholas R. Jr. of Dublin, Ireland; Deirdre Ann and John Paul Phillips of Wyckoff. Brother of Robert E. of NY, NY. Predeceased by his brothers Lawrence E. Jr., William T. and Richard. Visiting at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Ave., Wyckoff on Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Funeral Mass, St. Elizabeth's RC Church, 700 Wyckoff Ave., Wyckoff, Monday 10 am. Interment Christ The King Cemetery, Franklin Lakes. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Wyckoff Volunteer Ambulance Corps, PO Box 214, Wyckoff, NJ 07481.
