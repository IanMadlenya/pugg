
        GALLAGHER -- Reverend William Patrick, 64, died October 13, 2003. Born June 9, 1939. Predeceased by his parents Francis D. and E. Rose (McGovern) Gallagher and his stepmother Kay Gallagher. Survived by a twin brother, John, an aunt, Ann Sheridan of Rutland, VT, several nieces, one nephew, many cousins. Following cremation, a Liturgy of Christian burial will take place at Our Lady of the Snows, Woodstock, VT on Saturday, October 18, at 11 AM. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in memory of Fr. Gallagher to Tepeyac Association of NY, 251 W. 14 St, NYC 10011.
