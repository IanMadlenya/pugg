
        KOFFLER--Anne (nee Greenberg) died on October 23 at home in the company of her husband Nicholas, her great love of 48 years. Anne was born July 17, 1936 in Shanghai, China. Anne dearly loved her three children Stephen, Pamela, and Richard and their spouses Marcella, Russell, and Lauren. She was a wonderful grandmother to seven grandchildren, and caring aunt to Kenneth, Andrea, and Jennifer, and their families. She a devoted daughter to Frances. She lived life with passion and verve, taking nothing for granted. We will remember her intelligence, beauty, grace, kindness, charm, and fabulous sense of style. Goodbye Anne. Donations to the American Cancer Society.
