
        RABIN-Leah. The Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations expresses condolences on the passing of Leah Rabin, who worked closely with her husband, former Prime Minister Yitzchak Rabin, on behalf of the Jewish people and the State of Israel. We pray that the family will be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Malcolm Hoenlein, Executive Vice Chairman
