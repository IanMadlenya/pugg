
        GOODMAN--Lorraine R., 78. Once of Brooklyn and Sleepy Hollow, NY and Cambridge, Massachusetts, died June 16 at her Bedford, MA home with her husband, William, her children, Jonas and Sara, and her niece, Laurie Miller, at her side. Mother, grandmother of Alex and Ben, sister of Blossom and Roz, teacher, retired Bolt, Baranek & Newman administrator, seasoned reading for the Blind and Dyslexic volunteer, devoted gardener, patient birder and good neighbor. Her unfailing intelligence and grand humane competence served all who knew her well. She was much loved.
