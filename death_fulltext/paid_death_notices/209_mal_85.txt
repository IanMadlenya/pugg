
        SCHWARTZ -- Edward R. Passed away peacefully at home at age 93. Alumnus of University of Pennsylvania, Class of '31. Beloved husband and best friend for 63 years to June. Loving father of Ann Barish and son-in-law to Keith. Devoted grandfather to Christoper Edward Barish. He will always be remembered for his galantry, cheerfulness, warmth and love of friends. His terrific attitude towards life made him extraodinary. Services private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Lenox Hill Hospital or charity of your choice.
