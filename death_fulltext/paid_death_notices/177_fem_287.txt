
        SCHWARTZ-Ethel Gold. Died October 26, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Charles I. Schwartz, M.D., devoted mother of Judy and Richard Berg, adoring grandmother of Erica Lynn and David Joshua (of beloved memory), beloved sister of Jessie Handler and the late Florence Anzel, Jeanette Gold and Mayer Gold. She was a gentle and wise soul who enriched the lives of all she met. We were all blessed to have shared our lives with her. She knew it was always five o'clock somewhere. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made to the David Joshua Berg Nursery School at Temple Beth Torah, 35 Bagatelle Rd, Melville, NY 11747; or Cope Foundation, PO Box 1251, Melville, NY 11747.
