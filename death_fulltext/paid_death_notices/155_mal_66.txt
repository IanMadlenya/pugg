
        HARRIS-Martin. March 13, 1933December 1, 1999. Adored husband of Boots (Lauren). Cherished dad of Michael, Clint, Rex, Saroya & Eytan Chissick. Playful Grandpa Marty to Patrick, Brooke, Richard, Daphna, Avital and Zev Shmvel. Cherished son of Josephine and the late Samuel. World's best friend, pilot, motorcycle rider, restaurateur, son, husband, grandfather. A kind, gentle, caring man who touched all he met with love and humor. Marty always had time to help a friend and counsel those in need! Marty would have loved any contributions to be made to Sloan-Kettering Memorial Hospital, NYC. Funeral services were held on Friday, December 3.
