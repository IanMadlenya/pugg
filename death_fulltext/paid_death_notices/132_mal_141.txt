
        KIRSTEN-Grace Elish. On January 8, 1998 in her 98th year. Psychotherapist, lecturer and author. Loving mother of Dr. Maurice E. Sherman and his wife Jane. Adored grandmother of Susan (Les) Morgenstein and Lynn M. Sherman. Great grandmother of Charles. Cherished sister of the late May Markewich, the late Dewey Elish and the late Karl Elish. The family is very grateful to Dr. Stanley Mirsky for his many years of devoted care. Contributions can be made in Grace's name to Temple Beth Emeth Shaari Zedek, 83 Marlborough Road, Brooklyn, NY 11226.
