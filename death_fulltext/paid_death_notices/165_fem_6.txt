
        OSTUW-Gladys (nee Kalman). On September 16, 2000 in Hollywood, FL. Formerly of Woodmere, N.Y. Devoted wife of the late Jack Ostuw. Loved and respected by her son and daughterin-law, Richard and Lois, her sister and brother-in-law, Shirley and David Chernow and her friends. Her love of the arts led to her participation in the Five Towns Music and Art Foundation. She was in at the inception of the organization, served as Vice President, Secretary and Treasurer for many years. She will be remembered for her many attributes, her sense of humor and especially her integrity.
