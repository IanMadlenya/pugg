
        VASA-Ralph L., O.D., Captain USN, Ret. passed away suddenly May 14, 2000. Beloved husband, father and grandfather, he was predeceased by first wife Irene E. Jarvis Vasa, second wife Natalie Flatow Vasa, and brother Arnold Vasa. He is survived by brother and sister-in-law, Harold and Mildred Vasa of Coram, New York, daughters and sons-in-law Sandra Vasa-Sideris and Tony Sideris of Marietta, Georgia, Kathy and Wendell Thomas of Powell, Tennessee, Robin L. Vasa and Stephen Smith of Washington, D.C., Natalie B. Vasa and David Moelling of Simsbury, Connecticut, by son and daughter-in-law Ben Ralph and Mary Lynne Vasa of Rockford, Illinois, and by grandchildren George Sideris, Matt and Craige Thomas, Leah Moelling, and Ivan and Katrina Vasa, and by several nieces and nephews. He was a member of the Mason's Waukegan Lodge No. 78, East End Temple, and active in civic and charitable organizations. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to your favorite charitable organization. Services at Fort Myer Chapel, May 24, 11 AM, Rabbi Deborah Hirsch officiating. Interment at Arlington National Cemetery.
