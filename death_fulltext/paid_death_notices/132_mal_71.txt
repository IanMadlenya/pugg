
        HIGGINS-Joseph Hanlon. Passed away January 1, 1998, in Laguna Hills, CA. He was 80. Mr. Higgins was a Credit Manager for Dun & Bradstreet for many years. He is survived by his wife Jeanette of 25 years, one son, Roger Higgins, daughters, Carol (Michael) Tampa, Donna (Joe) Licari, Diane (Stan) Kosek, Betty (Jeff) Lane, niece Joette Higgins and nephew Duane Garrett. Memorial services will be Thursday, January 8, 1998, 11 AM, the Pacific View Mortuary in Newport Beach, California.
