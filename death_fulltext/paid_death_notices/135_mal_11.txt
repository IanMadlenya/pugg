
        BRAND-Joseph A. Of New York and Deal, N.J. On March 31, 1998. A 1941 graduate of University of Virginia. Distinguished U.S. Naval Officer World War II and former President of Federated Brokerage Group. Beloved husband of Ina. Devoted father of Peter and Rena Sue, brother of Bill Brand. Friends may call Thursday, 2-6 P.M., Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Services Friday, April 3, 12 noon at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave. at 65th St. Interment private.
        BRAND-Joseph A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Joseph A. Brand. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        BRAND-Joseph A. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation EmanuEl sorrowfully records the death of the beloved husband of our esteemed Past President Ina Brand. We extend sincere condolences to the family. Eve Haberman, President Sivia Brodsky, Rec. Secretary
        BRAND-Joseph A. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mr. Joseph A. Brand. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Alan S. Berger, President Eric B. Sambol, Secretary
