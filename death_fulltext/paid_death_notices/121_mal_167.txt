
        BLAGDEN-Crawford, on February 10, 1997 age 84. A decorated Veteran of WWII. He was awarded the Silver Star, the Bronze Star and a Purple Heart. Husband of the late Mary K. Blagden. Father of Crawford Blagden, Jr, Elizabeth B. Strawbridge, Frederic K. Blagden, Samuel P. Blagden, nine grandchildren, five great grandchildren. Interment private. Memorial service will be at St. Marys in Tuxedo, Tuxedo Park, NY, April 4, 1997 twelve o'clock (noon). In lieu of flowers contributions to the Ruffed Grouse Society would be appreciated.
