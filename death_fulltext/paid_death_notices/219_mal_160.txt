
        KAHN--Charles S. On April 14, 2005, in West Palm Beach, FL, at age 88. Chuck was married for 56 years to his beloved wife, Shirley, who predeceased him by eleven months. He is survived by his loving daughter Marguerite and his devoted son Gene and his wife Connie. He was the proud grandfather of Amanda and Jodi. His family and friends will miss his wit, compassion and friendship, and will always be motivated by his courage. Gifts in his memory may be sent to The Heart Institute at JFK Medical Center, 5301 South Congress Avenue, Atlantis, FL 33462. Services 11:30 am, Sunday, April 17th, at The Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
