
        SUGGS-Dorothy. Died May 19, 2000. Age 53. At her home in Melbourne, Florida. Mrs. Suggs was born in New York City and taught at Marshall Elementary School in Hempstead, New York until 1995. She is survived by her husband of 34 years Eldridge Suggs III, her son Neal of London, her daughter Michele of New York City, her stepsons Eldridge Suggs IV of Atlanta and Timothy Suggs of Los Angeles and her brothers Paul Atkins and his wife Elaine of Philadelphia, Marc Atkins and his wife Mary Lou Stewart of Chicago & Carl Atkins and his wife Lucinda of Rockville Centre. She will also be mourned by her three grandchildren Sheldon, Zachary and Zaria and her nephews and nieces Daniel and Michelle Atkins & their children Eve and Michael, Jennifer and Robert Armstrong, Lee Atkins & Christopher Atkins.
