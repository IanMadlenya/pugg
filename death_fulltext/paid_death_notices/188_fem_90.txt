
        HABACHY-Melek M. (nee Dimyan). On September 10, 2002. Beloved wife of Nazeeh Saba Habachy, cherished mother of Tiya Saba Habachy and Dora Gamila Habachy, and loving sister of Amin, Wafik and Magid Dimyan. Born in Egypt, Melek graduated from the American University in Cairo. She came to the United States in 1972 when she married and made her life in NYC. She worked at the United Nations for 12 years until her passing. Funeral services will be held Friday, September 13, 11AM in the Chapel of Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church, 7 West 55th St. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent in her name to the American Cancer Society or the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals. Burial will take place in Egypt.
