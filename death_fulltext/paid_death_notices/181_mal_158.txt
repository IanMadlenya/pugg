
        FRISCH-Robert E. Born August 18, 1925, died on February 5, 2002. He was a graduate of Harvard College and Harvard Law and a partner at Rogers and Wells. He is survived by his wife, Dolly Hemphill and children, Katharine, Robert, Peter, and ten grandchildren. Memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Rye, New York, on March 9 at 11 AM.
