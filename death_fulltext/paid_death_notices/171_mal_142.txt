
        FEIGELSON-Philip. Of Tenafly, NJ, born in Corona, Queens in 1925, passed away on April 9, 2001. From 1954 through 1998 he was Professor of Biochemistry in the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University. He is survived by Muriel Feigelson, his wife since 1947, daughter Janet Feigelson of Mendon, NY, son Eric Feigelson of State College, PA, and 5 grandchildren. A memorial gathering will be held in Tenafly on Saturday April 14th.
