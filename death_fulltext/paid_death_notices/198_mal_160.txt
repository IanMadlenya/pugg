
        HONT--Michael. At age 76, on July 17. Husband to Vera, father to Jeanette and Kathy, father-in-law to Gary and Howie, grandfather to Tommy, Brian, Samantha, Jonathan and Allison. Dear friend to many. Partner in Asiel & Co. for nearly 30 years. Active in countless organizations. Highly respected in business and greatly admired in life. He was a man who truly lived life to its fullest and whose greatest treasure was his family. We could not be more proud of the man he was. He will live forever in our hearts. Funeral Monday, 9:45 AM, Parkside Memorial Chapel, Rego Park, NY. >>AD#
