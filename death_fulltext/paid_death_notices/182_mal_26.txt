
        CHARNOFF-Stanley, M.D. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the death of Dr. Stanley Charnoff. Dr. Charnoff was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Medicine for many years. He will be sorely missed by all his friends, patients and colleagues. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Steven Farber, M.D., Pres Herbert Lehman, M.D., Pres Elect Ira Mayer, M.D., Secretary Amin Hakim, M.D., Treasurer
        CHARNOFF-Stanley. The physicians and staff of Cross County Medical extend its heartfelt condolences to Dr. Judah A. Charnoff upon the passing of his father, Dr. Stanley Charnoff. May the entire Charnoff family be comforted and know of no further sorrows. Daniel Zanger, M.D. Howard C. Eisenstein, M.D.
