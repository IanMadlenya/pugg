
        WIDES-Ruth. Passed away on August 10, 2001. Much adored and loved wife of Melvin Wides of Boca Raton, FL, for 60 marvelous years. She was the mother of Susan Rosenblum and Steven Wides, the grandmother of Michael and Darren Rosenblum, and Sean and Bradley Wides, and the greatgrandmother of Rachel and Brooke Rosenblum. Always elegant and lively, she was a devoted wife, sister, mother and mother-inlaw, grandmother, and greatgrandmother who will always be in the loving thoughts and cherished memories of her family and friends. Service will be held at Gutterman's of Rockville Centre, NY, on Monday, August 13th, 12 Noon.
