
        TAUBMAN--Sylvia. Beloved grandmother, mother, wife, sister and friend to all she knew, passed away at her home on Thursday, July 17. Her dedication to family, support for her friends, unflagging energy and total selflessness influenced all who were fortunate to know her. A proud sweet woman, who worked as a bookkeeper and premium buyer and was involved in charitable activities, she will be greatly missed but never forgotten. Graveside services will be held at King Solomon Memorial Park, Clifton, NJ, Sunday, July 20 at 11 AM. >>AD#
