
        LOWE-Jacques. Prominent photographer, died peacefully at his home in Manhattan, on May 12, 2001, at the age of 71. Beloved father of Jacques (Jamie) Lowe, Jr., Victoria Allen, Alexander (Sacha) Joseph Vinten Lowe, Thomasina Raissa Vinten Lowe, and Kristina Rebecca ThorhaugeLowe of London. A memorial service is planned for the Fall. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to the American Cancer Society.
