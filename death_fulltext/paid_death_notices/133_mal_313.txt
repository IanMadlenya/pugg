
        FOSS-Joseph W., 87, of Albuquerque, NM. Died February 16, 1998. He is survived by his daughter, Frances Lee Neufeld, of Albuquerque, and grandson, Christopher Blake, of Los Angeles. Mr. Foss was President and CEO of General Tire International and Chairman of the Board of Inverness Investment Corp. of New York and Geneva, retiring in 1975. Memorial services have taken place in Albuquerque.
