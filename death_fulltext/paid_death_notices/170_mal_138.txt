
        KLEIN-Arthur. Age 87, March 10, in Boynton Beach, FL. Cherished husband of Muriel. Dear father of Jeffrey (Sherry) Klein, Barbara (Ken) Scherick and Mitchell (Miriam) Klein. Proud grandfather of Russell Klein, Gabrielle Roth and Rachel Klein; Adam, Scott and Heather Scherick; and Daniel, Samantha and Douglas Klein. A man of principle and wit, he was the founder of Gloss-Flo Corporation in 1940 and served as its president until his retirement in 1988. Services 1 PM, Tuesday, March 13, at Gutterman's of Woodbury. For further information call Howard Kotkin at 516-921-5757.
