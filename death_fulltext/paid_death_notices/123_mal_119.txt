
        WIENER-George. Died peacefully April 8, 1997, in Encino, California. Born in New York City February 25, 1901, he was 96 years old and lived through every decade of this century. He was a music publisher, a Brill building original and one of the colorful music men who made up the Tin Pan Alley scene. His card playing skills were legendary. Devoted husband to the late Evelyn, he was a loving father, grandfather and great-grandfather. He leaves his sons, Robert and Stuart; daughters-in-law, Claire and Marlene; grandchildren, Richard, Michael, Allen, Wendy, Jill, Joshua and Katie; and great-granddaughter, Jamie. Services will be held April 11, 1997 in Los Angeles.
