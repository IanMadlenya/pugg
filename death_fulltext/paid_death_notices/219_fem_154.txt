
        LINDER--Sylvia, 87, died on April 20, 2005. Loving wife and best friend to Ira for 65 years. Beloved mother of Theodore, Andrew and Arthur. Loving mother-in-law of Arlene and V.J. Cherished grandmother of Jonathan, Eric, Jacob and Dana. Loving sister of Rose Ullman and the late Jack Radin and Saul Radin. Adored sister-in-law of Ruby Radin, the late Jack Ullman and Ethel Radin. Aunt of Amy, Ellen, Geri and Theodore. Even in her waning days she was courageous, strong and dignified. Her warm heart, sense of humor and vivacious spirit will be missed forever. Services are today, 12 noon, ''Gutterman's'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I., phone 516-921-5757.
