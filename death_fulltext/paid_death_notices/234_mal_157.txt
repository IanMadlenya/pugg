
        FEINER--Michael, 68, on July 4th, 2006. Beloved husband of Susan Blond. Loving father of Sharon LItvack and Uri Feiner. Proud grandfather of Lior and Shira LItvack. An immigration attorney, he served the Jewish-Israeli community and had served as Chair of the AILA. He was recently honored by West Side Kollel and Ohav Shalom for his contributions to the Jewish community. He was buried in Jerusalem.
