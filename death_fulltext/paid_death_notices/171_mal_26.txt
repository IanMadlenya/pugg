
        GREENWALD-Irving. Of New York City and Coconut Creek, FL. Beloved husband of Maralyn. Father of Adrienne Youngstein and Wayne Greenwald. Grandfather of Margot and Eli and family to Steve Gruberg and Lily Kaplan. Died on March 30, 2001. He was a man whose warmth and love of life endeared him to all who knew him. We will all remember and miss him. Shiva observed at W. Greenwald, 370 1st Ave., NY.
