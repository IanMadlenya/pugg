
        MESHNICK-Martha. 81, on October 5. Loving mother to Ellen and Steve, and mother-in-law to Paul Immerman and Mary McKinney. Donations may be made to the Don Monti Cancer Center, North Shore University Hospital, 300 Community Dr., Manhasset, NY 11030. Services Sunday, 10 AM, Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
