
        DAVIDOFF--Howard M., 50, of Port Washington and Southold, NY, died Tuesday, November 14, 2006. Best friend, lifetime love, devoted husband to Lisa (Klein), adored and cherished by his three children: Melanie, Owen and Gabrie. Beloved son of Robert and Esther, son-in-law of Felice and Steve Klein, caring brother and uncle to Bari (Amanda, Allison and Adam) Weintraub, Joanne and Dan (Jesse) Free, Larry and Lisa (Benjamin, Hannah, Michael) Davidoff, Michael (Zachary) Klein. He was a loyal, supportive and compassionate friend well-loved by all who knew him. Funeral services Thursday, November 16th at 10AM, at The Community Synagogue, Port Washington, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Howard's three favorite charities: P.Y.A., Community Chest of Port Washington, and The Port Washington Public Library.
