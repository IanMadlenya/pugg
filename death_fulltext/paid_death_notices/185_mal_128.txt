
        BILDNER-Dr. Bertram S. The Executive Board and the membership of the Bronx County Dental Society mourns the passing of Dr. Bertram S. Bildner, honored colleague, dedicated official and beloved friend. The wisdom, energy, commitment and sense of perspective of our devoted Founder and Past President of the New York State Dental Association will be sorely missed. His warmth and sincerity endeared him to all. Dr. Robert S. Yeshion Executive Director Bronx County Dental Society
