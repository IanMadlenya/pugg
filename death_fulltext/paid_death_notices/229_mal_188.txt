
        ZUCKER--Seymour, M.D. Age 97. Husband of the late Cynthia Prince. Died peacefully February 13, 2006. Longtime staff member and teacher at Beth Israel Hospital. His loves were his family and medicine. He will be sorely missed by his children, Jane and Jonathan, his grandchildren, Amanda and Jeremy, and his many friends. Services private. Memorial Service to be held at a future date. Contributions may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice.
        $(6$)ZUCKER -- Seymour, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians and staff in the Department of Medicine in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and former colleague, Seymour Zucker, M.D., a member of the Beth Israel medical staff from 1964 until his retirement in 1993. Dr. Zucker will forever be remembered as an outstanding clinician beloved by his patients and greatly respected by all who worked with him. To his son, John Zucker, M.D., his daughter, Jane Zucker, his grandchildren, and all who loved him, we extend heartfelt condolences. David J. Shulkin, M.D., President and CEO Stephen P.
