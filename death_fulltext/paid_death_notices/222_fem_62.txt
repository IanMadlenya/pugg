
        GROBERG--Marion Shulman Died Saturday night in NYC after a brief illness. Born 1913 in Russia, daughter of Faya Woolman Shulman and Joseph Lieb Shulman. Wife of the late Robert L. Groberg. Beloved stepmother of Bruce, Nelie, Michael, Sandi; grandmother of Frances, Robert, Tami, Shira. Graveside service Tuesday, 2PM at Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx.
        GROBERG--Marion. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its member, Marion Groberg. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred D. Youngwood, Pres.
