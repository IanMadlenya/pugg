
        TONKENS--Rosalie Robbins. Native New Yorker. Died February 4, 2005. Devoted mother of Jane Heller Gerard of New York, and Nancy Lerner of Providence, RI. Loving grandmother of Andrew Heller, Irving Fain and Jacob Fain. She is survived by her sister Ruth Ozarow and her brother Daniel Robbins both of Ithica, NY. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions can be made in her name to the Jewish Home for the Elderly, 175 Jefferson St, Fairfield, CT 06825.
