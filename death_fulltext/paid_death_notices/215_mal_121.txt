
        KERN--Edward, 79 of New York and Nantucket. Beloved by all who knew him, died suddenly on November 26th. He was an admired Senior Editor/Writer on Life Magazine, noted for massive series on ancient Greece and Rome, etc. A Navy Veteran, he was educated in France, Germany and England, graduated from Exeter Academy and Harvard ''cum laude''. He is survived by his first wife Dolores Nygren and their son David, his second wife Banita, his sister-in-law Anne, nieces Wendy and Cynthia, nephew John Christopher and nine grand nieces and nephews.
