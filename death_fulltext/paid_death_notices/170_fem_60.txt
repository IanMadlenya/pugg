
        AMES-Helen, 91. On February 26, 2001, known as Helen Alexander, ''Nightingale of The Airwaves'' during Major Bowes 1930s Capital Family Hour. Helen will be remembered for her soprano voice and quick wit. An icon of The Upper West Side's Ansonia, she was a true free thinker, and her zest for life and opera sparkled brightly to the end. Daughter of Henry Alexander, Asst DA of NY, 50 years a wife to Tom Ames (died 1988), she was a beloved mother to Frank, Joyce, and Tony (died 1955), and adored mother-in-law to Eddie & grandmother to Richard. A gathering will be held at The Riverside Memorial Chapel on March 12th from 4 to 8 P.M.
