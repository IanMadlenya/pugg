
        KANNOF-Benjamin P. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Benjamin P. Kannof, beloved father of Alan Kannof, a dear friend of the Jewish community, who helps improve the lives of others through his exceptional commitment to UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to Alan and his wife, Dr. Ellen M. Umansky; to Mr. Kannof's eight grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren; and to the entire Kannof family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
