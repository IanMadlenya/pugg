
        DUBNO--Peter. On October 30, 2006 at the age of 90. Beloved husband of I. Marguerite. Survived by his stepsons, Douglas & Kenneth; his children Karen, Lorna, Peter & Elyse; and six grandchildren. Formerly of NYC; psychologist, Professor Emeritus NYU Stern School of Business. A memorial service will be held Saturday, November 4, 2-4 PM at his home in Carmel, NY.
