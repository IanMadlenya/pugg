
        RONAN--Joseph, 85, of Somers, NY, formerly of Brooklyn. Died on April 15 at Putnam Hospital. Former Sales Manager for Texaco, Inc. in Purchase, N.Y. Graduate of Brooklyn College. Beloved husband of Jeanne and the late Ann Dixon Ronan. Loving father of Monica Ronan, Mark Ronan and his wife Maureen, Maura Olson and her husband Lawrence. Devoted grandfather of six. Loving brother of Loretto Keck and the late Edward, Richard and Mary Ronan. Calling hours at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, New York, Saturday 79PM, Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Croton Falls, N.Y. Monday at 11:30AM. Interment St. Joseph's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Bishop Loughlin High School.
