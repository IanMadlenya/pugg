
        MILLER--Bernard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Bernard Miller, a renowned business leader and cherished friend of the New York Jewish community. Bernard, together with his brothers Saul and George Miller, was an esteemed leader whose dedication and commitment, exemplified by his boundless generosity, enriched millions of lives around the globe. We offer our deepest sympathies to Saul and George; to Bernard's beloved wife, Harriet; to his children, Robert and Steven; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
