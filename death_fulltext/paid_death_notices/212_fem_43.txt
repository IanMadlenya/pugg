
        BLUMENFELD--Violet. On August 31, 2004. Family and friends deeply mourn her passing. She is survived by her nieces, Frances, Gail, Lora Ann, and nephew Peter, as well as her great and great-greatnieces and nephews. For years she was the kind, patient, and elegant manager of the Swoboda School of Ballet in NYC. Her second career as a travel agent for Ports of Call and Travel Bureau, Inc, took her around the world, making friends wherever she went. Services Tuesday, Spetember 7, at 11:30 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
