
        STYLES-Arthur Dennis. Age 85, of Sebring, FL. Died August 5, 1999. Born in Montreal, moving to Sebring in 1998. He was an investment banker for Kidder Peabody in Boston. He is survived by his wife Mary Alice Styles & daughter Susan Tuttle both of Sebring, two grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Preceded in death by son Arthur Dennis Styles Jr. Services August 9, 8:30 AM, St. Michaels RC Church, Wauchula, FL.
