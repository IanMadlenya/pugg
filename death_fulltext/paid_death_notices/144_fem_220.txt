
        BEREZIN-Mae L. Of Aventura, Florida, formerly of NYC. Cherished and beloved aunt, great-aunt & sister. A lifelong friend to many. Mae moved to Florida after a successful career in the fashion industry, where she was president of Mae Littell Fashions. Mae was a remarkable woman who gave generously to many charities, including Hadassah, the Jewish Home & Hospital for the Aged at Douglas Gardens, American Friends of Beit Issie Shapiro, Miami Jewish Federation, among others. Mae was loved by all who knew her & will be greatly missed. Services will be held on Friday, January 22, at 11:15 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 180 West 76th Street, with burial to follow at Mount Ararat Cemetery in Farmingdale, New York.
