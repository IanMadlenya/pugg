
        BUKIET--Belle. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of the beloved wife of Al Bukiet, who, with him and his late brother, Joseph (and Rose) Bukiet, are YU Guardians and together dedicated the Bukiet Family Holocaust Collection of Yeshiva College in remembrance of the Holocaust in which their family perished. Condolences are extended to Al, to her children, Bruce (and Gail) Bukiet, Caryn Bukiet, and Michael (and Karen) Bukiet; to her brothers George (and Bernice) Glatter and Herb (and Lynne) Glatter, and to her nine grandchildren. May the entire Bukiet family find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry Weiss, Chairman, Board of Trustees
