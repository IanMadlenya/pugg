
        BRAM-Morris E. (Monty). Monty passed away peacefully in his sleep on Tuesday April 16. Beloved husband of M.D. (Lita Taracido). He is survived by his son Jason and daughter-in-law Sarah; granddaughter Emily; stepchildren David and Juli; stepgrandchildren Martine and Gabriela and many cousins, nephews and nieces. A loving friend to many, he will be remembered for his boundless kindness to all. Monty has left a mark in many lives. He will be missed. Funeral services 10AM today at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, (corner of 91st St) New York, NY.
