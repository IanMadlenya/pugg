
        KRATENSTEIN-Helen. On November 20 at Albany, NY. Wife of the late Meyer Kratenstein. Mother of Rhoda Kratenstein of Schenectady, NY and Selig Kratenstein and his wife Robin of North Woodmere, NY. Sister of William Bellinger of Roswell, GA. Grandmother of Matthew and Andrew. Services 1:00 PM, November 22 at the Old Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, NY. Contributions to the United Jewish Appeal. The period of mourning will be held at the residence of Mr. and Mrs. Selig Kratenstein. For information call the Levine Memorial Chapel, Albany, New York at 800-427-0280.
