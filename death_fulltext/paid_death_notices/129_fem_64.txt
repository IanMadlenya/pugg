
        RYAN-Marion M. Of Bronxville, N.Y., October 7, 1997. Dear sister of Eileen M. and Anne P. Ryan. Served for over forty years at Newtown High School in Elmhurst, Queens in the varied roles of teacher, teacher counselor, dean, cooperative course administrator and assistant principal in charge of guidance. When she retired in October 1980 she left behind a strong guidance department as her legacy. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville 2-4 and 7-9 PM Thursday. Mass of Christian burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville on Friday, October 10 at 10AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her name to the Maryknoll Fathers or Maryknoll Sisters, Ossining, New York 10545.
