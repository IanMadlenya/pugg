
        SEIDENBERG - Gloria H. (nee Shine). On January 4, 2003, after a long and courageous battle with CLL. Beloved wife of the late Jerry, loving mother of Jane Seidenberg and Susan (Rick) Adler, devoted grandmother of Jacob and Leah Adler, caring sister of Roberta Kunin and Warren Shine. Teacher, librarian, friend to all children, and a true believer in human kindness. She will always be missed and remembered with love. Contributions in Gloria's memory to the Gloria Seidenberg Memorial Fund for Children's Services, Rockville Centre Public Library, 221 North Village Ave, RVC, NY 11570. The family wil be at Gloria's this week. Memorial Service to be announced at a later date.
