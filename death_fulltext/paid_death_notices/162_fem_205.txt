
        HELLMAN-Aviva died peacefully in her sleep on July 26th, after a long illness. She will be buried in Jerusalem. The widow of Yehuda Hellman, the Executive Vice Chairman of The Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations; Mrs. Hellman was a prolific novelist and author of six best sellers. She is survived by her longtime companion, Paul Janco, her daughter, DorLee and son-in-law Pine Lebensart and her son and daughter-in-law Linda Hellman. She was the adored grandmother of Daniella, Emanual, Leora, Sarah and Joshua.
