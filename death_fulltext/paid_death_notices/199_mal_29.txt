
        SELOVER--John Lewis of Boston on August 1, 2003; husband of Elizabeth Bliss Selover. Mr. Selover was a member of the Christian Science Board of Directors and Manager of the Christian Science Publishing Society. He is lovingly remembered by his wife Elizabeth; his children Alison and Alexander; his grandchildren Jesse and Gabriella and his brother William C. Selover. A commemorative to honor his life will be held at a later date. For further information, contact J. S. Waterman & Sons, 617536-4110. >>AD#
