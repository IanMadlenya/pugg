
        POWELL-Cecile V.K. Of McLean, VA, and late of Bayside, NY, on March 28, 2002, at age 94 of natural causes. Beloved wife of the late J. Richard Powell; loving and devoted mother of J. Richard Powell Jr. of McLean, VA. She was preceded in death by her daughter Madeline Harriet Martin. She is also survived by nine grandchildren and 17 great-grandchildren. Memorial services will be held at All Saints' Episcopal Church in Bayside at 10:30 AM, July 20. Inurnment will be in the family gravesite. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Daughters of the American Revolution, 1776 D St. NW, Washington, DC 20006.
