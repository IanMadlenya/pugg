
        CURTIS-Thomas E. Real estate developer, banker, and founder of the Curtis Supermarket chain, died Friday of a stroke. Born in Boston, Mr. Curtis developed more than two million square feet of commercial space in Southeastern Massachusetts. He was a community leader and contributed greatly to a variety of charities. After losing his eldest son Tom Jr., an AllAmerican sailor at Georgetown University, Tom Sr. became active in promoting the GU Sailing Team with grants, gifts and scholarships. He also established an educational scholarship at St. Anselms College, the alma mater of his eldest daughter Kathleen, who died in 1993. Tom and his wife Marjorie resided in Palm Beach Gardens, FL, and summered in Hingham, MA. Beloved husband of Marjorie E. (Pearce) Curtis. Loving father of Brian P. Curtis of Hingham, MA, Elizabeth A. Curtis of New York, NY, Dianne M. Curtis of Hingham, Sarah C. Hanlon of Hingham, and Jonathan P. Curtis of Hingham. Devoted brother of Mary, Richard, David, Alan, Jean, and Dorothy. Loving ''papa'' of 14 grandchildren. Funeral Mass in St. Paul's Church, Hingham, Wednesday, August 16, at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Lincoln Sailing Center, P.O. Box 492, Hingham, MA 02043.
