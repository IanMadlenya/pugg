
        TWETI-Harriette S. Died suddenly on August 2, 2000 in Teaneck, New Jersey of a heart attack. Beloved mother of Mira, Nathan and Caleb; beloved wife of the late Chaim Tweti; beloved daughter of the late Bertha Griffel. Her free spirit, love of life, the arts and her commitment to matchmaking will be missed by all her knew her. Funeral services were held on Thursday August 3, 2000 at The Riverside Memorial Chapel in Manhattan. For more information regarding Shiva, call 212-362-6600. Interred at Cedar Park Cemetery, Forest Avenue, Paramus, New Jersey.
