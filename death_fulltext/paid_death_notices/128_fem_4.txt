
        JENNISON-Jane Lowe. Departed peacefully August 26 at her home in Taftsville, VT., at 79. Formerly of Manhattan and Westport, Conn. She is survived by her husband Peter S. Jennison, a son Andrew & two grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 4, 3 P.M., at St. James Episcopal Church, Woodstock, Vermont. Remembrance contributions may be made to the Norman Williams Public Library, Woodstock, Vermont.
