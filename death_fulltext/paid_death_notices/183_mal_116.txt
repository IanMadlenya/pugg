
        RESNICK-Abraham. The clergy, trustees, officers and membership of the East 55th Street Conservative Synagogue sorrowfully record the loss of our dedicated, esteemed and beloved Corresponding Secretary. He faithfully served our Congregation with dedication and devotion for over half a century. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Sarah, his children, and grandson. He will be sorely missed. Reuven Siegel, Rabbi Frank Mayo, President Lawrence Levine, Chairman of the Board
