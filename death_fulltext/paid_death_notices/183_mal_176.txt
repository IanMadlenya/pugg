
        MILLER-Neal. The Board and membership of the Association for Applied Psychophysiology and Biofeedback mourn the loss of our esteemed past president, Dr. Neal Miller. Dr. Miller was a noted scientist whose research pioneered the field of Biofeedback. His work lives on but we will miss his guidance and grace. We extend heartfelt sympathies to his family. Paul Lehrer, Ph.D., President
