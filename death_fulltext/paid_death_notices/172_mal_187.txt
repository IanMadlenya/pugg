
        MEYERS-Daniel. We mourn the passing of our inspiration, Daniel Meyers, 89, on May 16, 2001. Mr. Meyers suffered from Parkinson's Disease. He is survived by his wife, Florence, his children, Gail Meyers, Lynn Broide & Jonathan. He is also survived by his grandchildren Nina Martin and Binyomin Broide, his brother Hillel and sisters Miriam Kahn, Ruth Belilos and Esther Cohen, as well as numerous nieces and nephews. We will miss him a lot. Services will be held in Israel. The Family and Employees of A. Meyers & Sons Corp.
        MEYERS-Daniel. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its affiliates mourn the passing of the beloved brother of our devoted member, Miriam Kahn. We extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Rabbi Joshua Lookstein Chaim Edelstein, President
