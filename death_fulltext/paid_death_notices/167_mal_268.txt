
        ORNSTEIN-Albert. On December 19, 2000. Beloved husband of Miriam, dear father of Steve and the late Dooley and Robert, and father-in-law of Joan, Sy, and Nancy. Cherished grandfather of Nancy, Richard, Jeorjie, Jodi, Kenny, Jim, Gus, Rosie, Nina, and great grandfather of Jordan. ''Bronco'', we love you and miss you already. Services Friday, December 22, 2000, 11:45 A.M. at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to The Dooley Ornstein Reisman, Robert Ornstein, Lt. Adolph Ornstein Scholarship Fund, Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y.
        ORNSTEIN-Albert. The Trustees and staff of World Learning and the National Directors of Federation EIL are deeply saddened at the passing of our devoted friend, enthusiastic supporter and beloved member of The Experiment in International Living family. There was no volunteer more tireless, nor advocate more passionate about international education and the importance of working for a more just and peaceful world. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his dear wife and co-recipient of The Experiment Citation, Miriam, and his entire family. David A. Murdoch, Chair James A. Cramer, President World Learning Robin Bitters, Director Federation EIL
