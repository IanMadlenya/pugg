
        CAMUNAS-MONGE-Jorge. Died in Santurce, PR, March 9, 2001. Born February 21, 1915. Graduate of the University of Puerto Rico, a CPA, he was Vice President of Corona Brewing Corp., where he worked from 1940-1982. He was President of the San Juan Lions Club, Governor of Lions District 51 and International Director of this organization (1961 - 1963). He served for many years in The Chamber of Commerce and was named Director Emeritus. He is survived by his wife Alida Muniz de Camunas and a son, Jorge Camunas, MD. He will be viewed at Puerto Rico Memorial today. Family requests donations to Stella Maris Church or Manuel Fernandez Juncos Childrens Home in San Juan in lieu of flowers.
