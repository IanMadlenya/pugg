
        WEINBERG-Samuel. Peacefully, in his sleep on October 1, 2000. Born March 28, 1908, son of Bertha Katz and Morris Weinberg. Attorney-at-law, senior partner at Sperry, Weinberg & Cohen. Loving husband of the late Mildred Wolton Weinberg. Beloved father of Ladd and the late Michael. Caring father-in-law of Catherine. Adored grandfather of Gillian Ladd and Joanna Weinberg. Devoted brother of Sophie Karp and dear uncle of Marilyn Silverman and Martin Karp. Fond friend of Carol Cohen. We are all richer for having shared his warmth and long life. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81st St. on Tuesday, October 3, 7:00-9:00 PM. Services to be held Wednesday, October 4, 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation, 381 Park Ave. South, New York, NY 10016. WEINBERG-Samuel. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Samuel Weinberg. We extend our deepest sympathy to the entire family. James M. Dubin, President Martin Lowy, Secretary
