
        HATCH-Luzie. On September 16, 2001 at the age of 89. Ms. Hatch was born in Germany on May 9, 1912. Dedicated employee and officer of the American Jewish Committee. Ms. Hatch, a Holocaust survivor, emigrated to the United States in 1938. She was the proud recipient of the Order of Merit of the Federal Republic of Germany which was bestowed upon her September 24, 1992 in recognition of her relentless pursuit to ensure that this atrocity would never recur. Interment private. Donations to the American Jewish Committee, 165 East 56th St., NYC 10022 would be appreciated.
