
        PHELAN-Robert Thomas, 87, died on April 24, 2000 suddenly at his home in Delray Beach, FL. Cherished husband of 47 years of Mimi Morgan Phelan, beloved father of James J. II (Kathy), Jody Phelan Cooney, Gerard T., Robert M. and fond grandfather of Andrew G. and John M. II. He was a devoted son of the Church, a loyal graduate of Georgetown University C. '35 and a proud Officer in the United States Navy serving in WWII. Mass of the Resurrection, Friday, April 28th at 10:30AM, Church of St. Vincent Ferrer, Delray Beach and Monday May 1st at 10:00AM, Church of the Immaculate Conception, Westhampton Beach. Interment private. Please omit flowers; Masses preferred.
