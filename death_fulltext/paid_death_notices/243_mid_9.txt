
        ROBERTS--Joseph L., Jr., age 87, of Sewickley, PA, died on April 15, 2007, of pancreatic cancer. Survived by his wife, Helen Shaw Roberts: daughters Melinda R. O'Rourke and her husband Terrence, of Sewickley Heights, PA, and New York City, Jean S. Roberts of North Carolina, and the Rev. Kyoki Roberts of Sewickley, PA. A grandson and two great-grandsons, Joseph, David and Tristan Eliason of Omaha, NE. Memorial service, Saturday, 1 pm, April 21, 2007 at St. Stephen's Church, Sewickley, PA. Contributions may be made to Hillman Cancer Center, 5150 Centre Ave., Pittsburgh, PA 15232
