
        BRAUMAN-Lillian (nee Heyman). Passed away June 24 at her home in Anchorage, Alaska. She was born in Jersey City, New Jersey, February 14, 1911 and lived most of her life in the New Jersey, New York metropolitan area. She is the beloved mother of Patricia B. Wolf, mother-in-law of Aron S. Wolf, M.D., dear grandmother of Jonathan Paul Wolf, Lisa WolfWendel and Laurie Beth Wolf, and great-grandmother of Alexandre Paul Wolf. She is preceded in death by her parents Irving and Ella Y. Heyman, husband Leonard Paul Brauman, her son Paul Brauman, and her sister Adelade H. Davidson. Funeral arrangements are scheduled for Tuesday, June 30. For further information call Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Anchorage Museum Association, 121 W. Seventh Ave., Anchorage, AK 99501.
