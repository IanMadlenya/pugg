
        FREUDENBERGER-Herbert J. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the death of Herbert J. Freudenberger, beloved father of Marc J. Freud, vice-chair of our Real Estate Executives Committee, and father-inlaw of Nikki Freud, Director of Strategic Initiatives. A renowned psychoanalyst and prolific writer, he had a lasting influence on his profession which remains as his permanent legacy. We extend our sincerest condolences to Marc and Nikki; to his wife, Arlene; daughter Lisa Freudenberger Roth and her husband Theodore; daughter Lori Freudenberger; and all the members of the family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Andrew J. Singer, Chair, Real Estate Div John S. Ruskay, Executive VP
