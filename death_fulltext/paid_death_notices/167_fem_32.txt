
        STEWART-Margot, on November 27, 2000, in NYC. Loving mother of Brandon, dearest sister of Gerda Loffler and Gunther Endres. Born June 24, 1927, in Nuremberg, Germany. Painter, mosaic muralist and charter student of the Norfolk School of Art of Yale University. She had numerous shows, including solo exhibits at the John B. Myers Gallery. Known for her kindness to her fellow artists, she was an assistant to Charles Egan when his gallery showed artists who became the forefront of Abstract Expressionism. Her love of nature and animals inspired her to create beautiful visions for those she touched. A memorial service will be announced.
