
        PEARSON--Samuel Malcolm. 92, a founding father of the Dreyfus Fund, passed away on June 27, 2005. His survivors are his sons Matthew and Roger, daughter-in-laws Ronnie and Jenny, and grandchildren Danny, Ryan, Michael, Olivia, Adam and Ian. A graveside service will be held at 1PM on June 29 at Temple Sholom Cemetery, Memory Lane, Greenwich, CT. The family will sit Shiva after the service until 3 PM, July 1st at 327 Woodlands Road, Harrison, N.Y. 10528.
