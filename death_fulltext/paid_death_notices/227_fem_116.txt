
        HARRIS--Nancy Ottis. White Bear Lake, MN, age 75, on December 11, 2005. Graduate of Summit School in St. Paul, Mills College, and ColumbiaPresbyterian School of Nursing. With her intelligence, great good sense and grounded personality, Nancy could be counted on completely by those close to her. She gave very generously of herself to her friends, family and community throughout her lifetime, and she will be missed greatly by all who knew her.
