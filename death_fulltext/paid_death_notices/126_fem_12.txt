
        GINTELL-Anne Leerburger, 92, of New York City on July 1, 1997. Wonderful mother of John W. Gintell of Cambridge, MA, beloved widow of Syl W. Gintell. Contributions may be made in her memory to the American Heart Association. At her request, her ashes will be scattered at sea by the Neptune Society. A memorial service will be held in the future.
