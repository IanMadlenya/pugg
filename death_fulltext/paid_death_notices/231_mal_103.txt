
        BECKER--Thomas W. Of Scarsdale passed away on April 9, 2006. Mr. Becker worked for many years as a trader on the Commodities Exchange. He is survived by his loving wife Patricia, his cherished son Michael Thomas, his sister and brother-inlaw Elizabeth and John McNulty, his sister-in-law and brother-in-law Joan and Don Roberts, his sister-in-law Lucy Becker, his cousins Brother Joseph Cussen, Mary and James Long, Mary and William Prattella, John and Carol Nardo. He will be greatly missed by his nieces and nephews Kathleen (Daniel) Kaiser, Donald Roberts, Michael (Doni) Roberts, Patricia (Christopher) Millington, Richard Roberts, John Roberts, Lee (Sara) Becker, Joey Becker, members of the Cussen family in Chile and his many, many friends. Mr. Becker is predeceased by his brother Joseph and his sister Priscilla. Friends may visit the Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue Scarsdale, NY (914) 725-1137 today Tuesday from 2-4 & 7-9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10AM on Wednesday at St. Pius X Church in Scarsdale. Interment will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
