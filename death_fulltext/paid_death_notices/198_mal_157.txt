
        ANDROMIDAS -- Steven Christopher. Also known as Chris Andromidas, a composer for film and television whose credits include more than 350 films, died on Friday July 11, 2003 at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, NY. He was 50. Chris was born on March 2, 1953 in Mineola, L.I. and resided in Ossining. Chris is survived by his wife, Christina Muller, formerly of Ellenville, NY and one brother, Charles (Rocky) Andromidas of San Francisco, CA. He is predeceased by his father Charles J. Andromidas, his mother Katherine (Notar), and his younger brother Thomas. A private memorial will be held at the convenience of the family. In lieu of flowers, donations in memory of Chris to ALCASE, Unit 78, POB 4800, Portland, OR. 97208-4800. >>AD#
