
        WITTENSTEIN-Sari. Age 73, on Sunday, January 26th, in Boca Raton, FL. Predeceased by husband, Robert P. Wittenstein, and survived by her sons, Ira and Andrew; a daughter, Victoria, and four grandchildren. Formerly of New York City and Westport, CT., she was a long-time supporter of the arts and performed in several local and regional theater groups. Services Thursday, January 30th, at 11:00 AM, from Abraham L. Green & Son, 88 Beach Rd., Fairfield, CT. Memorial contributions to Parkinson's Disease Foundation, or United Jewish Appeal.
