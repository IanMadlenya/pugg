
        ROTHBAUM-Minnie. February 9, 1998, 83 years old. Beloved wife of the late Harry Rothbaum. She is survived by her four children, Michael Rothbaum, Ronnie Storch, Jane Levin, Arnold Rothbaum, and eight grandchildren. Services today at 1283 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn. Her beauty and kindness will be sorely missed by all.
