
        POPISH-Samuel. On August 23, 1999. Age 87. Beloved husband of Pearl. Loving father of Paul and Diane. Adoring grandfather of Stephen, Elena, Daniel, Thea and Hannah. Great-grandfather of Logan. He is also survived by his brothers Harry, Abraham and his sister Jean. He was a Workers Compensation Attorney for many years and president of the Workers Compensation Bar Association. He was respected by his peers and his clients. We celebrate his memory. Graveside services will be held today at Sharon Garden Cemetery, Valhalla, NY at 11AM. For information, Jewish Memorial Chapel, Yonkers, NY.
