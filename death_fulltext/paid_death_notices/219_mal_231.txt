
        ULICH--George F. Was called home to his eternal rest on April 19, 2005. He died at the Homles Regional Medical Center in Melbourne, Fl. He was born in the Bronx, NY on January 29, 1924 to Anna (nee Dehn) and Adam Ulich. He graduated from Herman Ridder Jr. High School, James Monroe High School and Pace College. He was a veteran of the 104th Timberwolf Infantry Division and saw combat in Europe during WWII. He received the bronze star for heroic acheivement. His business career was spent largely at the Williamsburg Savings Bank in Brooklyn where he became President and CEO. He retired in 1987 after 46 years. Mr Ulich served on various boards including Hellen Keller Services for the Blind, The Wartburg Home in Mt Vernon and San Simeon on the Sound in Greenport, NY. He is survived by his wife of 49 years Eleanor (nee Bauer), 2 children and 4 grandchildren. His daughter Christa Lorraine and her children Andrew Paul, Caroline Grace and Christopher Adam of New Hyde Park. His son Glenn Adam, daughter-in-law Sara Marsden and their daughter Gwendolyn Grace of Richmond, VA. He and his family lived in Brookville, NY for many years and he and his wife moved to Manhasset Hills in 2004. He was a summer resident of East Marion, NY since 1969. In 1992 he started to winter in Vero Beach, Fl. He was a member of Good Shepherd Lutheran Church in Plainveiw, NY where the services will be held on Saturday, April 23, 2005 at 10:00 AM. He was an associate member of St. Peter's Lutheran Church in Greenport, NY and Our Saviour Lutheran Church in Vero Beach, Fl. Burial will be in Orient Cemetery, Orient, NY. Funeral services will be held at R. Stutzmann & Son Funeral Home, 2000 Hillside Ave, New Hyde Park, NY. Visiting Friday, 2:00-4:00 PM and 7:009:00 PM. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made to Hellen Keller Services for the Blind, Good Shepherd Lutheran Church, St. Peter's Lutheran Church or a charity of your choice.
