
        CHIU - Patty. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center community mourns the untimely passing of fourth-year College of Medicine student, Patty Chiu. Our deepest sympathies are extended to the entire Chiu family, to Mr. Scott Duong, and to her many friends. Those wishing to honorher memory may do so by contributing to Doctors Without Borders. John C. LaRosa, M.D., Pres.
