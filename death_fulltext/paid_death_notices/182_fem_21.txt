
        CRAIGIE-Nancy-Ray. Of Mountainside NJ on Wednesday, February 27, 2002. Wife of the late Hugh A.; mother of Jeff and Mrs. Lisa Chiramonte; sister of Mernie Richardson, Carol Bickford, Lucy Anderson and Ruth Maynerd. Survived by 3 grandchildren. Mrs. Craigie was one of the editors for Stratameyer Syndicate, publishers of the Hardy Boy Books in NYC. She later worked for United Jersey Bank in Mountainside, NJ. Memorial services were held on Saturday in Mountainside. For more information contact Madison Memorial Home in Madison, NJ.
