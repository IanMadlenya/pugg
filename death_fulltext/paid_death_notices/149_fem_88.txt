
        CHEN-Selena Shiao-Fu Chiao. Died June 9, 1999. Beloved wife of Sidney Shin-Yee; dear mother of Wendy, Daryl & Kenneth; devoted daughter, sister, and friend. For 24 years, she worked tirelessly as a physician and director of the Medical Clinic at Mary Immaculate Hospital in Jamaica, New York. She loved to help others, and her infinite kindness and generosity will be remembered forever. We will always be thinking of her. A memorial service will be held at 10:00 AM on Saturday, June 19, at Fairchild Funeral Home, 1201 Franklin Avenue, Garden City, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, the family will be accepting contributions for a fund dedicated to a college in Sansi Province, China.
