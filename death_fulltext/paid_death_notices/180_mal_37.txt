
        MASIUS-William S. Age 89, on December 23, 2001. Survived by his beloved wife Barbara, daughter Carol Zuckerman, son-in-law Stuart Zuckerman, and granddaughter Kate Zuckerman. Also survived by son John and grandchildren Hannah, Max and Sam of Los Angeles. Private services were held. Donations may be made to the New York League for the Hard of Hearing, 71 West 23rd St., NY or to the University of Pennsylvania, 3451 Walnut Street, Philadelphia.
        MASIUS-William S. The Board of Directors and members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed Golden Member, William S. Masius, whose many years of friendship, kindness and devotion to the club will always be remembered. We send our condolences to his wife, Barbara and his children. Beth Z. Post, President Roger H. Tulcin, Secretary
