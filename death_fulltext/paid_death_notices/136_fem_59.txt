
        DE BARBIERI - Evelyn nee Roberts. At Montreal on May 3, 1998 at the age of 75 years, Evelyn born in Montreal, Quebec passed away in the presence of her children. Daughter of the late Leontine Seguin and the late Albert Roberts. Wife of the late Lt. Col. Thomas F. De Barbieri. She is survived by her children Frederick Leontine and Victoria Marie. Cherished sister of Albert (New York) and Bob (Georgia). A memorial mass will take place at 10AM, Saturday, July 4, 1998 at the church of the Ascension of Our Lord in Westmount, Quebec. In lieu of flowers donations to the Victorian Order of Nurses of Montreal or the Regroupement de solidarite avec les Autochtones/Solidarity with the Native Peoples would be appreciated.
