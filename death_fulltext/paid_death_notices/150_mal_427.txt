
        McCAFFREY-Thomas J., Rev. On July 27, 1999. Ordained for the people of New York on March 1, 1947. Born on March 1, 1922. The son of Felix & Agnes Maloney McCaffrey. He fulfilled his ministry at St. Mary's Church in Yonkers, St. Pius X Church and Our Lady of Fatima Church in Scarsdale. He was a teacher at Archbishop Stepinac High School for over 30 years teaching American history and religion. He also taught the use of sign language, often celebrating Mass using signing. He is the brother of John & Rita McCaffrey of West Palm Beach, uncle of Kevin and Karen McCaffrey and great-uncle of Tara McCaffrey. Family will receive friends at the Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, New York on Thursday, 2-4 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial will take place on Friday at 10 AM at Our Lady of Fatima Church in Scarsdale. In lieu of flowers donations to the Society for the Propagation of the Faith, 1011 First Avenue, New York City 10022 will be appreciated.
