
        SPIRA-Molly. Died peacefully on November 3, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Bernhard Spira. Loving mother of Steven and Gale, Robert and Naomi. Dear sister of Eva. Adoring grandmother of Jennifer and David, David and Michele, Avi, Daniele, Etan and Benjamin. Great-grandmother to Max, Lily, Sarah-Golda and Yaakov. Funeral service will be held at 11:15AM at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York City.
        SPIRA-Molly. Lincoln Square Synagogue mourns the passing of Molly Spira, beloved mother of our Honorary President and dedicated Trustee, Steven Spira. A woman of great strength and nobility, who devoted herself to her family. May her memory serve as a blessing for all those who cared for her and loved her in life. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Richard Kestenbaum, President
