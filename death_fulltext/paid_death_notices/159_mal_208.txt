
        GOODMAN-Lawrence Eugene. Of Saint Paul, Minnesota. Born in Manhattan, only son of the late Commissioner and Mrs. Joseph Goodman. Died on April 17, 2000, age 80, in College Station, Texas. 1st Lt. USS Missouri, U.S. Navy, World War II. Record Professor of Civil Engineering at the University of Minnesota. Member of the American Society of Civil Engineers. Beloved husband of Katherine. Dear father of Jennifer, Jeanne & Alice, & their husbands Jeff Wallock, Charles Herzog and Geoffrey Hill. Adored grandfather of Joscelyn and Linnea Herzog, and Alberta Hill. Loved and respected teacher and colleague of many scores of engineers around the world. His life was a blessing to all who knew him.
