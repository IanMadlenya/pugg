
        HUNTING--Roger Bryant. Died in Richmond VA on June 3rd at the age of 85. He had previously lived in New York for 45 years where he practiced law with Greenbaum Wolff & Ernst and retired in 1992 after 8 years of service as a New York City Civil Court Judge. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, and brother, he is survived by Betty Ann Grove Hunting, his wife of 35 years, daughter Barbara H. Bean, and son-inlaw Bruce W. Bean of East Lansing Michigan, and grandchildren Austin and Ashley Bean. He graduated from the University of Vermont and Columbia Law School, and served in the US army Air Corp on Guam during World War II. Former secretary of The Players. Memorial service arrangements are pending. Burial will be in New Marlborough, Mass.
