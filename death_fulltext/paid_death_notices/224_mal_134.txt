
        HEINZEN -- Bruce Robert, MD. Of Plandome, New York. Husband of Clare Heinzen, father of Jerome, Barbara, Thomas and Nancy, died at home 11 September 2005. He was an attending surgeon when North Shore Community Hospital opened in 1953 in Manhasset and continued there in private practice until he retired in 1989. Best remembered as a dedicated teacher and doctor, Bruce Heinzen inspired many with his plain honesty and personal care. A service will be held at home on Saturday, 17 September 2005. Please ring the family for details on 516627-1527. Memorial gifts to Adventures in Learning, High Street, Manhasset 11030, tel: (516) 365-7131.
