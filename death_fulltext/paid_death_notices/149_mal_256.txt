
        GITLIN-Buz (Alan). Of Roslyn Heights, N.Y. Died at 52, after a long illness. Devoted son of Rose and the late Mac of Great Neck. He was the precious husband of Jane Samuels for almost 30 years and the adored and adoring father of Nikki. Beloved brother of Bruce Gitlin (Carol Schrager) and Susan Cherlin (Dr. Edward) and favorite uncle of many nieces and nephews. Funeral Friday, June 18th, 12 Noon at ''Gutterman's'' Woodbury, L.I. Contribution to N.S.U.H. Nephrology Division, 100 Community Drive, Great Neck, NY 11021. His love and humor will forever be remembered.
