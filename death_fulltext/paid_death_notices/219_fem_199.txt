
        McCARTEN--Dorothy ''Gloria''. Of Pawtucket, RI died April 26, 2005. She was the wife of Louis Andrew Cuvillier McCarten to whom she had been married for over 49 years. Born in Manhattan, she was a daughter of the late Philip and Marguerite (Thornton) Kearns. She was a graduate of Mount Saint Ursula, Bedford Park, NY and of Manhattanville College of the Sacred Heart, Purchase, NY and received an M.B.A. degree from the New York University Graduate School of Business Administration. She was a real estate agent for many years, retiring in 1996. She was a member of the RI Short Story Club, RI Preservation Society, University Club, and RI Country Club. She was an artist, writer and lover of life. Besides her husband, she is survived by one daughter, Dorothy Gloria McCarten of Norwalk, CT; three sons Louis McCarten II of Glendale, CA, Charles R. McCarten of Pawtucket, RI and Christopher McCarten and his wife Mary Jo of Fall River, MA; two sisters Audrey Kelly of Scarsdale, NY, Marguerite Perrin of Larchmont, NY; and three grandchildren, Samuel, Nicholas and Alexis. A funeral Mass will be held in Cathedral of St. Patrick, 51st Street, New York, NY on Saturday at 10 a.m. Burial will be in St. Raymond's Cemetery, Bronx, NY. Cheetham Mortuary, Inc., 1012 Newport Ave., Pawtucket for information.
