
        GINZLER-Robert M., 83, of Morris Plains, NJ, died on Tuesday, December 15, 1998. He was a CPA and WWII Veteran of the South Pacific campaign. Born in NYC in 1915, he graduated from Townsend Harris H.S. and City College, both of NY. His wife Alice died six years previously. He is survived by a son, Edward, of St. Louis, MO., a daughter Elinor, of Silver Spring, MD. and two grandsons, Benjamin and Daniel. Private memorial will be held at a later date.
