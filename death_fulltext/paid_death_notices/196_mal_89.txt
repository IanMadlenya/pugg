
        STEINBERG-Elias. Died at 85 on May 8, 2003 in Nashville. Beloved father of Ronnie and Arthur, brother of Leo, grandfather of Jennifer Levine, David and Peter Steinberg, and Brian and Elizabeth Ames. He was a decorated test-pilot during WW II. He was the manager of the family bakery, and a golfer in his retirement. Funeral services Sunday at noon at Schwartz Bros. in Forest Hills, and burial will be in Beth David Cemetery in Elmont. We loved him dearly, he made a difference in our lives, and he will be missed.
