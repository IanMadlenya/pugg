
        BALET--John W. 98, a longtime resident of Pelham, NY, died Monday, June 9. He graduated from Columbia College in 1925 and from Columbia's School of Engineering in 1927. He retired from Con Edison after 35 years in 1969. John Balet leaves behind four children, twelve grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. He is predeceased by his wife, the former Kathleen Giblin. The wake will be at the Pelham Funeral Home Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, with a funeral on Thursday at St. Catherine's in Pelham, NY. Contributions may made to the Church of the Holy Name, 207 West 96 St, New York City. >>AD#
