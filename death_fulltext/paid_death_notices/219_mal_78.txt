
        SCHWARTZ--Ozzie, on April 7, 2005, age 74. He spent the past 40 years coaching children on the sports fields of Central Park and Randalls Island. His legacy will live on in the young lives he enriched. Ozzie was a man of honor and dignity, who made a lasting impression on those who knew him. His wealth was his friendship and love. We celebrate his life. He was the best. The most beloved husband and best friend of Susan. Devoted father of Warren and step-father of Marc Oseroff. Services Sunday, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
