
        WHITE--James. Died unexpectedly on March 24 in Fishkill, NY. Born in Concord, NH on August 21, 1955, to Webster and Shirley White. As Captain with Glorious Food of NYC, James served many of their distinquished clients. He performed with Winged Victory Singers and Lori Shayne Company. Member of Actors Equity and SAG.
