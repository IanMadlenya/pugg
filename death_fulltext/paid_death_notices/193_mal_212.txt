
        POLITE-L. John Jr. 81. A longtime resident of Saddle River, N.J and Jupiter, Florida died Sunday, February 9, from complications of surgery. Born on April 30,1921 in McKeesport, Pennsylvania, Mr. Polite attended Aspinwall H.S. and went on to graduate from Williams College in 1942. Thereafter, he became a commissioned officer of the U.S. Navy and served aboard the USS Minneapolis as well as the USS Bremerton. Enjoying a distinguished business career, Mr. Polite was the President of the Essex Chemical Corporation from 1962 to 1988 and served as a director for numerous corporations and non profit organizations. He will be remembered most for his generosity and giant heart. He is survived by previous wife Marilyn Bewie, first wife Katherine Loresch, their sons John (Tuxedo, N.Y.), Daniel (Hillsborough, CA) and David (Carlton, OR), as well as two granddaughters, his brother Willis (Belvedere, CA), and his sister Barbara Grant (Lincoln, NE). A memorial service will be held on February 22 from 4-6:00 P.M. at the Chemist Club, 40 West 45th Street in New York City. Those wishing to do so may make a memorial donation to the National Organization for Alzheimer's Disease, 919 North Michigan Avenue, Chicago, IL. 60611.
