
        SINGH-Sina. Born May 1, 1955, died April 5, 1997. Raised in India, lived in NYC since 1975. Cherished son of Ram and Nancy Singh. Beloved brother of Mike and Raj. Loving brother-in-law of Joanna, uncle of Jacob and Kiva. A magical artist of brilliance and grace. He lived for painting, music, cooking, laughter and giving. He greeted neighbors from his stoop with tea and dazzling wit. The world's most wonderful guy, we love you, we will miss you.
