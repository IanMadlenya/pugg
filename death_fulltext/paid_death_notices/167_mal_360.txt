
        SULLIVAN-The Rev. Daniel J. of the Vincentian Community at St. John's University on December 26, 2000. Father was an English Professor at St. John's and Niagara University and also served as Associate Dean of Notre Dame College, Staten Island. Father is survived by his cousins Mary Duigan & Joseph Sullivan. Family & Religious Order will receive friends at the Vincentian Residence of St. John's University 8000 Utopia Pkwy, Jamaica, NY Thursday 3-5 & 7-9 P.M. Mass will be celebrated at the Residence Friday 10:30 A.M. Interment St. Joseph's Plainsboro, NJ.
