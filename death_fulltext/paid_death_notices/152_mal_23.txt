
        ROYCE-Milton E. Age 83, of Glen Cove, NY, August 30, 1999. Loving husband of Dolores. Father of Stephen Royce, Kyle Larsen and Tracey Stoever; grandfather of Gregory, Allen and Lisa Royce, Eric Riddle Larsen, Alex Larsen and Cady Ruth Stoever; loving brother of Lynn Goldberg. Memorial service 11:30 AM, Friday at Whitting Funeral Home, Glen Head, NY. He will always be warmly remembered in our minds and our hearts as a guiding light and inspiration. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to charities sponsoring heart and cancer objectives.
