
        LITCHMORE-Myrtle Olive. Passed on January 20th, 2003. Myrtle was an elegant, generous, loving, kind and private lady whose life achievements will continue to live on in the hearts of all those lives she touched. Myrtle is survived by one brother, Aston, and two sisters, Dasey and Lucibell, and her two children, Barbara and Alvin. Grandchildren include Alycia, Alston and Alleah; great grandchildren include Ajeya and Alakai. Services were held on Friday, January 24th at the Greater Allen Cathedral of New York, Jamaica, NY. The family requests, in lieu of flowers, donations in her memory be made to Calvary Hospital, Bronx, NY.
