
        CARR-Paul E., Sr. Former Executive Vice President Goodall Fabrics. Died March 4, 2002 in Danbury, CT at the age of 98. Husband of the late Mildred Brenner Carr. Beloved father of Paul Jr., Nancy Carr, Joan Eggert and Virginia Kops. Devoted grandfather of Ambrose Carr III, Paul Carr III, Christopher, Carol, Charlotte and Celeste Eggert, George, Gregory and Alexandra Kops, Paula Cummings, Margot Carr and Marette Winchester. Also five great grandchildren. Memorial services will be announced. Contributions to Favorite Charity in his memory would be appreciated. Cornell Memorial Home, Danbury, CT.
