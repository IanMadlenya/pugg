
        SARNOFF-Robert William. Age 78, of NYC, died Feb. 22, at Lenox Hill Hospital after a long illness. Beloved husband of Anna Moffo, brother of Edward and Thomas, father of Rosita Sarnoff, Serena Benenson and Claudia Parrot; grandfather of Adam, Toby and Felicia Jeanne Benenson. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, Feb. 26, at 11:30 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave and 65th St. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to the American Cancer Society, 19 W. 56th St, NYC 10019.
        SARNOFF-Robert W. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member and Officer with the title of Prior, Friar Robert W. Sarnoff, on Saturday, February 22. His strength, spirit, devotion and dedication have guided us over the last few years. He will be sorely missed and forever be in our hearts. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Temple Emanu-El on 5th Avenue at 65th St. on Wednesday, February 26, at 11:30 AM. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
        SARNOFF-Robert William. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York, records the passing of our distinguished member, Robert W. Sarnoff. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualites of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
