
        KRAMER-Rose. Age 91, on December 27. Of Astoria, New York, and Far Rockaway, NY. Wife for 64 years of Samuel Kramer, of blessed memory; devoted member of Congregation Beth Jacob. Beloved mother of Marlene and Lionel Press and Eileen Barron; sister of Elaine & Lester Horowitz and Irving and Ruth Pross; grandmother of Deborah, Mimi, Sharon, Jacob, Sharon, Laura, & Charles. Great grandmother of Sarah, Adam, David, Rachel, Mitchel, Jason, Sandra, Rachel, and Hood. Her memory is an honor to her family.
