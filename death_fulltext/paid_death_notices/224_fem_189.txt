
        BOOKSON--Paul. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Honorable Paul Bookson, beloved father of Shoshana Bookson, a treasured friend and leader whose devoted services as a chair of our Trial Lawyer's Group profoundly impacts millions of lives in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Shoshana and her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
