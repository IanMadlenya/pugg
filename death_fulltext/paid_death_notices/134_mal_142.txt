
        BUTGER-Milton. The Board of Trustees of YAI National Institute for People with Disabilities is greatly saddened by the passing of Milton Butger. As a distinguished member of our Board, he was a tireless and passionate advocate on behalf of people with disabilities. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his entire family.
