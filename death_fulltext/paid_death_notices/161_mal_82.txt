
        MULQUEEN-Rev. John C. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of retired parochial vicar of St. Elizabeth Church, 94-20 85th St., Ozone Park, on June 3. Reposing in Turner Funeral Home, 14360 Spring Hill Drive, Spring Hill, Florida on Wednesday, June 7, 2 to 4, 6 to 8 p.m., and Thursday, June 8, at St. Joan Of Arc Church, 13485 Spring Hill Drive, Spring Hill, Fl, 5 to 7:25 p.m. Vigil Mass/Service on Thursday, June 8, at 7:30 to 9 p.m. Funeral Mass Friday, June 9, at 10:30 a.m. Memorial Mass on Wednesday, June 21, at St. Elizabeth Church, 94-20 85th St., Ozone Park, at 7:30 p.m. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society.
