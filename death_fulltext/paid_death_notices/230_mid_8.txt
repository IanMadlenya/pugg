
        FALKOW--Michele G. The New York University School of Law community is deeply saddened by the loss of Michele G. Falkow, class of 1985 and the beloved wife of our trustee Lewis Steinberg. An accomplished and dynamic individual, Michele was an example of kindness and courage. She will be greatly missed. Our thoughts and prayers are with Lew and his family. Lester Pollack, Chair of the Board, Richard Revesz, Dean, NYU School of Law
