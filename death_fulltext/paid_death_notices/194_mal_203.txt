
        MEIER-August, historian, on March 19, 2003. Born April 30, 1923. Teacher and scholar whose research and writing shaped the field of AfricanAmerican history. Dearly beloved brother of Paul and Louise, cherished uncle of Diane, Karen, and Joan. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture, 515 Malcom X Blvd, NYC 10037. A memorial service will be held in NYC in several months.
