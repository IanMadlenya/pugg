
        PADDOCK-Sandy (nee Carol Sandra Jenkins). Died February 10 at her home in Shoreham. A longtime Glen Cove resident, she was the wife of Bob and the mother of Bruce and Patricia. She was also godmother to thousands of pets, as the proprietor of the Animal Inn, a boarding kennel in Glen Cove. The Paddocks will celebrate Sandy's life on Sunday, March 4, at 2 PM, at Harrison House, Dosoris Lane, Glen Cove. Donations may be sent to the James A. Baker Institute for Animal Health, New York State College of Veterinary Medicine, Cornell University, Ithaca, New York 14853.
