
        KOSH-Ruth, 87, of Key Biscayne, FL, passed away October 5, 1997. Ruth had made her home in South Florida for the past 21 years coming from NYC. Ruth was born in New York City and raised in the Seagate Section of Brooklyn. She attended Hunter College. She, with her late husband David of 64 years was his helpmate with his activities at Temple Beth Emeth of Brooklyn, City College of NY and Jewish Federation. Ruth was a great supporter of the Arts, Miami City Ballet, Bass Museum and Florida Philharmonic. Beloved wife of the late David Kosh, devoted mother of Joyce (Dr. Gerard) Kaiser, loving sister of Roselle Schreiber, adored grandmother of Beth (Shai) Gold, Jordan (Elizabeth) Kaiser, Dr. Charles (Hope) Kaiser, dear great-grandmother of Benjamin Gold, Sasha Gold, and Joshua Kaiser. Funeral services will be held 9:30 AM Wednesday, October 8, 1997 at Temple Israel of Greater Miami, 137 NE 19th Street, Miami, FL, with interment to follow at Mt. Nebo Memorial Park. In lieu of flowers, donations in memory may be made to the Friends of the Bass Museum, 2121 Park Avenue, Miami Beach, FL 33139, or the Miami City Ballet, 905 Lincoln Road, Miami Beach, FL 33139. For further information contact Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 1717 S.W. 37th Avenue, Miami, Florida, 305-535-4626.
