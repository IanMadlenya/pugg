
        ROSEN-Joshua Benjamin. The leadership of UJA-Federation of New York offers its sincere condolences to Rachel Freedman, cochair of our White Plains Women's Campaign, and her husband, Daniel Rosen, on the tragic and untimely passing of their infant son, Joshua Benjamin Rosen. We share their sadness, though we cannot begin to know their pain. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Joan Ginsburg, Chair Westchester Women's Campaign Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
