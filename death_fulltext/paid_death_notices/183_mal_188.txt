
        MARX-Leonard Maximilian. Born October 4, 1904 in New York City, died peacefully at home on April 12, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Virginia Wise Marx. Brother of the late Emily Marx House. Loving father of Sylvia & Leonard Marx Jr. of Greenwich, CT, the late Ellen Marx, Mary Lynn and Tony Bianco of Los Angeles, CA, and Jennifer and Bud Gruenberg of Scarsdale, NY. A 1925 graduate of Yale University, Mr. Marx was president of Marx Realty & Improvement Company and chairman of Merchants National Properties. He is also survived by six grandchildren: Richard Marx, Nancy Marx Better, Ned Solot, Claire Solot, Wendy Gruenberg and Jon Gruenberg, and by seven great - grandchildren: Carolyn, Kaitlin, David, Charlie, Sarah, Griffin and Ava Tess. Services will be held at the Marx family home in Scarsdale, New York, Tuesday, April 16, at 2:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Virginia Marx Children's Center at Westchester Community College, Valhalla, NY or White Plains Hospital Medical Center, Post Rd., White Plains, NY.
        MARX-Leonard M. The Westchester Community College Board of Trustees and the Foundation are deeply saddened by the loss of our longtime friend, Mr. Leonard M. Marx. Along with his late wife Virginia, he made a significant difference in the quality of life at the College by supporting The Virginia Marx Children's Center. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his son, Leonard Marx, Jr., his daughters Jennifer Gruenberg and Mary Lynn Bianco, their spouses, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren.
