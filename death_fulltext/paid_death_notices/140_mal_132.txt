
        SHAPIRO-George M. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family notes with sorrow the passing of our friend, George M. Shapiro. As past President of the Baron Edmond Rothschild Foundation, George was an important philanthropic supporter of the Medical Center. We will miss his encouragement and are grateful for his steadfast commitment to numerous innovative health care programs at Beth Israel. To his wife Rita, his daughter Karen, and all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, M.D. Pres. and CEO.
