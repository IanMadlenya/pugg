
        MINKEL-Helen S. On December 17, 1999. Helen S. Minkel of Milton and Centerville, MA, died peacefully at her home surrounded by her family. She is survived by her beloved husband, Dr. Herbert P. Minkel, her cherished children, Herbert P. of Boston and New York; Mary Ellen Burke of Atherton, CA; Ann Corkery of Salisbury, CT; Jean Minkel of New Windsor, NY, and Maura Frakleton of Arlington, MA, and her adored grandchildren Kate and Ellen, Molly, Ned and Hannah, and Sean and Sam. She was a great lady whose true joy in life was the happiness of others. Visiting hours will be on Sunday, December 19, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Dolan Funeral Home, 1140 Washington St, Dorchester, MA, and a Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on December 20, at 11 AM, at St. Elizabeth's Church, Milton, MA. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to Regis College, Weston, MA.
