
        SIMON--Rabbi Joshua R. Passed away on August 7, 2005. Beloved husband of Dr. Miriam Bensimhon. Devoted father of Dylan Rafael and Marley Reuven. Loving son of Rabbi Matthew and Sara Rubinow Simon. Dear brother of Ethan (Sandy Katz) and Elizabeth (LT COL Jeffrey, USAF) Cowan. Services Tuesday 1 PM at New Mt. Carmel Cemetery, Glendale, New York. Donations may be made to the Tikvah Special Needs Program at Camp Ramah C/O B'nai Israel Congregation, 6301 Montrose Road, Rockville, MD. 20852.
        SIMON--Rabbi Joshua. The Board and Membership of Congregation Ezrath Israel The Actors' Temple-mourn the passing of their beloved Rabbi, Joshua Simon. During his four years of spiritual leadership, he brought music, warmth and openness to our synagogue. He will be profoundly missed. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife Miriam, his twin sons Dylan and Marley, his parents, Matthew and Sarah, his siblings Betsy and Ethan, and the entire Simon and Bensimhon families. There will be a gravesite funeral at New Mount Carmel Cemetery on Tuesday at 1 PM.
