
        MERRILL-Vernon L. Of Locust Valley, NY, beloved wife of Robert G. Merrill, died on July 13th after a long battle with breast cancer. She was 72. Mrs. Merrill is survived by six children: Lynn Gray of Locust Valley; Gail Merrill of New Canaan, CT.; Edmund Merrill and Douglas Merrill, both of Denver, CO.; Signa Herman of St. Louis, MO.; and Nina Ogan of Denver. She is survived by 8 grandchildren, a stepgrandchild, her sister, Signa L. Mills and her brother, Edmund C. Lynch, Jr. Mrs. Merrill was the daughter of the late Mr. and Mrs. Edmund C. Lynch. Mr. Lynch was a founder of Merrill Lynch & Co. Mrs. Merrill was an accomplished athlete; she excelled in tennis and croquet. Active in many educational endeavors, she served on the boards of trustees of several schools and colleges. In addition, she was active in the North Country Garden Club and was the current president of the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory Associates. The burial is private. A memorial service will be held at 11:00 A.M., on Saturday, July 18th at St. Paul the Apostle Church, Cedar Swamp Road in Brookville, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory Associates, P.O. Box 100, Cold Spring Harbor, New York 11724, would be appreciated.
        MERRILL-Vernon L. Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory's Board of Trustees, its Association, administration and staff extend deepest sympathy to the family of Vernon Merrill. Mrs. Merrill, president of the CSHL Association and member of the Board of Trustees, was an active and generous supporter of the Laboratory since 1985. She was an ardent proponent of the Laboratory's initiative to establish on-site child care, and she served on the Building, Development and Executive Committees for the Board of Trustees. Mrs. Merrill and her enthusiastic involvement will be deeply missed.
