
        MEADE-Reverend Garvey G. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the parochial vicar of Good Shepherd Church, 1950 Batchelder Street, Brooklyn, on November 13, 1999. Reposing in Marine Park Funeral Home, 3024 Quentin Road, Brooklyn, Wednesday, November 17, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 10 p.m., and Thursday, November 18, from 2 to 5 p.m. Mass of Transferral at Good Sheperd Church, on Thursday, November 18, at 7:30 p.m. Funeral Mass, Friday, November 19, at 10:30 a.m. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
