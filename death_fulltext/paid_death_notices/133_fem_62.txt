
        ECKHARD-Marie T., Dr. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY, has died after a short illness in Wilmington, DE. Feb. 3, 1998. A retired professor of American History and Women's Studies at Pratt Institute, she was a faculty member 1967-1992. She was awarded Distinguished Professor 1990-91 and received Emeritus status in 1992. Devoted to her profession, Dr. Eckhard enjoyed and maintained respect and admiration from her professional colleagues and students.
