
        SONENFELD-Sol, died August 12, 1997, at the age of 88. Devoted husband of Bea for 55 years. Cherished father of Roberta and her husband Bob; adored and adoring grandfather of Taryn. Dear Dad, my intense sense of loss is softened only by the rich legacy and wonderful memories you've given me. I will continually strive to do the same for Taryn. You'll always be my hero. Love, Roberta. Contributions may be made to YIVO Institute for Jewish Research or the National Yiddish Book Center.
