
        SHAPIRO-Ethne Davies. Age 74. On January 8, 2001. Beloved wife of Robert for forty years. Loving mother of Richard and his wife Renee. Loving stepmother of Susan, Meryl, Paula and Roy. Loving step grandmother of Jason, Joshua, Micah and Noah. Beauty, Fashion and Home Sewing Editor of Good Housekeeping, United Kingdom. Devoted volunteer at Lenox Hill Hospital. Other volunteer work included literacy and reviewing literature for children. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Thursday, January 11, 2001, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Interment private.
