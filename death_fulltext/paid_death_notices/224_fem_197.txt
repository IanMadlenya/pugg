
        URMAN--Doris. Of Ft. Lee, NJ. Wife of the late Felix Urman and Solomon Weisleder. Mother of Ann Oster and Harry Weisleder. Sister of Rifka Goldstein. Grandmother of ten grandchildren. Services Thursday, 9:30 AM at ''Wien & Wien'' 402 Park St, Hackensack, NJ.
        $(6$)URMAN--Doris. The Trustees and Staff of the Museum of Jewish Heritage-A Living Memorial to the Holocaust mourn the passing of Doris Urman, beloved mother of Trustee Ann Oster. A devoted mother and grandmother, she was steadfast in her devotion to her family and to our mission. Our heartfelt condolences to Ann, her children, Avi and Jane, and her entire family. Robert M. Morgenthau, Chairman David G. Marwell, Director
