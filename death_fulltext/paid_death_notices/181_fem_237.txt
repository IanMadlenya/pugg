
        FITZPATRICK-Nancy. Tudor City Greens records with sorrow the loss of their president, Nancy Sue Elizabeth Marie Fitzpatrick, on Sunday, February 24. Nancy, a member of the Greens' Board of Directors since 1988, served as their president for the past 12 years. She was responsible for the transformation of the Tudor City parks into the wonderful oases that they are today. Nancy will be grievously missed. A tribute to Nancy will be held in the parks in the springtime, date to be announced. Board of Directors Tudor City Greens, Inc.
