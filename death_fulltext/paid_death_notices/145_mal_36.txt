
        BERAKHA-David, February 2, 1999. Husband of Esther. Father of George J. Beraka, M.D., and his wife, Judy, and the late Victoria. Grandfather of Scott, David and Michael. Born in Beirut, Lebanon, immigrated to Buenos Aires as a young man and later settled in the United States, becoming a senior vice president at Republic National Bank. He will be deeply missed by his family and friends. Service Thursday, February 4, 5:30 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Interment private.
