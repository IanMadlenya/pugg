
        GOLDSTEIN-Hartley J., 83, died May 5, 2003. Born in New York City, July 12, 1920. Attended Columbia Grammar School and a graduate of the University of Michigan, 1941. LCDR U.S. Navy until 1945. Former President of Scout Tex, Inc. Beloved husband of Ellen, cherished father of Hartley Jr., Patricia Tarr and Barbara Washkowitz. Loving grandfather to Jennifer and Jeff Tarr Jr., Emily, Andrew and Peter Washkowitz. Devoted father-in-law to Jeff Tarr and Alan Washkowitz. Hartley possessed an unusual flair for living and treasured his friends and family. He always had a story to tell accompanied by a great sense of humor. All who knew him will mourn his passing. Services Wednesday, 11:30AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        GOLDSTEIN-Hartley J. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Hartley J. Goldstein. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner, Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
