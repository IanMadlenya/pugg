
        THORSON--William L., aged 69, a retired IBM executive of Chappaqua, NY died on Sunday, April 1, 2007 after a long courageous struggle with Parkinson's Disease. He served in the US Army and joined IBM where he held positions in marketing. He devoted himself to raising funds for research on Parkinson's Disease and supporting those with the disease. He is survived by his wife Susan, daughter Jennifer and brother Robert. A memorial service will be held at Scarborough Presbyterian Church, Route 9, Scarborough, NY on April 14 at 11:00 am. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations to The Parkinson's Unity Walk. 1-866-789-9255 www.unitywalk.org
