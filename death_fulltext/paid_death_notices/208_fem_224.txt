
        ROTH--Florence. 95, Died May 24, 2004. Widow of Sol who died in 1949. Beloved by her children, Ellen and Paul Roth, Barbara and Jon Roth and Judy and Howard Berkowitz. Loving grandmother of Stefanie and David, Roger and Jenny Lyn, Jessica and Ben and Sandra and Jamie. Adoring great-grandmother of Amelia and Isabel. She lived her life nobly with grace and dignity, fighting with all her strength to make a good life for her children. They are her legacy.
        ROTH--Florence . The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of Florence Roth, beloved mother of our dear friend Judy Berkowitz, wife of past Honorary National Chair, Howard Berkowitz. Florence's dedication to family and community is her legacy. We extend our sincere condolences to Judy, Howard and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser, National Chair Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
