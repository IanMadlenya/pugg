
        MOSKOWITZ--Ruth. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ruth Moskowitz, beloved mother of Harold Moskowitz, an esteemed leader whose deep historic commitment and passionate concern for our General Insurance Division strengthens our ability to serve those in need. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Harold and his wife, Carole, and his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec.
