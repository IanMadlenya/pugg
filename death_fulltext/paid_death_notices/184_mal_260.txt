
        LORD-Walter, 84. Author of historical books, including ''A Night to Remember'' and ''Day of Infamy.'' Died May 19, after suffering for many years from Parkinson's. No immediate survivors, but a host of devoted friends. Memorial gathering at 12, Monday, June 10, at The New-York Historical Society.
        LORD-Walter. The Trustees and Staff of the New-York Historical Society mourn the passing of their great friend and Trustee Emeritus. Historian, author, and beloved colleague, his contributions to this institution and to the field of history will long be remembered. Kenneth T. Jackson, President Nancy Newcomb, Chairman
        LORD-Walter. The Trustees and staff of The New York Society Library are deeply saddened by the death of our colleague and friend. A Trustee for thirty-nine years, his humor and intelligence enlightened our deliberations. We will miss his lively manner, warmth and sound judgement.
