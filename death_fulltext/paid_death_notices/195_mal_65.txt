
        HERSCHMAN - David Wil liam, born NYC, January 6, 1968, passed away peacefully, April 3, 2003 in San Francisco after a fierce struggle with melanoma. A UC Berkeley graduate, he traveled extensively and excelled as an athlete. A successful entrepreneur, by 1999 he created the world's leading online gaming site, Prize Central, sold to Vivendi-Universal in April 2000. David is survived by his beloved wife, Dr. Michelle Birch, His parents, Lee and Stanley Herschman, sister, Jill Herschman, uncle Arthur Salzfass, and scores of close friends and relatives.
