
        DUNN-Margaret Freyn ''Maggie''. Cruciverbalist, musician, generous volunteer died September 25, 2001 in Shelburne, VT at age 85. Formerly of New Rochelle, NY. Predeceased by husband Thomas Mansfield Dunn. Survived by her children Thomas Freyn Dunn of Boston, MA and Sarah Mansfield Hoag (Sally) and her husband John W. Hoag of Toronto, Canada.
