
        DICKERSON-O. Bruce, M.D. Of New Canaan, CT, a leader in occupational medicine, on August 28, 2000. Survivors include his mother Juanita Baird, his wife Mimi Haggerty Dickerson, a son John Bruce Dickerson, a brother Lt. Colonel I. Wayne Dickerson and a sister Rita Shalton. Memorial service September 1, 11AM St Mark's Church, Route 124, New Canaan, CT. Contributions may be made to the Bruce Dickerson M.D. Fund at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021 or the Wittingham Cancer Center, c/o Norwalk Hospital, Norwalk, CT. 06850.
