
        McGILL--Douglas B. A Mayo Clinic physician, educator, and administrator since 1958 and a former president of the American Gastroenterological Association, he died on February 21 of pneumonia, at age 74. He is survived by his wife, Jean Perkins McGill, four children, and three grandchildren. In lieu of flowers donations are suggested in his name to the Mayo Fund for GI Research and Education.
