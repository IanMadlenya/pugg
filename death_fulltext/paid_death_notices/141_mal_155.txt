
        CATES-Joseph. October 10, 1998. Faithful husband of Joan BrandtCates. Loving father of Phillip, Valerie, Phoebe and Alexandra Cates and stepfather of Wilson Brandt. Adoring grandfather of Owen Joseph and Greta Simone Kline. Devoted brother of Gil Cates and Kit Adler. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday, October 15, 6-9PM with service Thursday 11:30AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the Friars Foundation, Inc., 57 E. 55 St, NY, NY 10022.
        CATES-Joseph. The Winston Preparatory School joins in mourning the loss of Joseph Cates, founding parent and Board member of this singular school for young adolescents who learn differently. Without his caring and extraordinary financial support, Winston could not have opened its doors eighteen years ago. We extend our sympathies to his children and other family members.
        CATES-Joseph. The Board of Directors of Variety-The Children's Charity mourn the loss of our terrific director and long time friend. We extend our condolences to his family. ''Cousin'' Bruce Morrow, President Fred Friedman, Chrmn. Emeritus Bonnie Ben Pilar, Chairman Leslye Schneider, Exec. Director
