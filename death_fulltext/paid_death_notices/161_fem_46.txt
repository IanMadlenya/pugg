
        NIEPORENT-Sybil Trent. On June 5, 2000. Devoted wife of the late Andrew Nieporent. Loving mother of Tracy and Drew. Wonderful grandmother to Matthew, Robert, Andrew and Gabrielle. Devoted friend to so many people who she touched with her warmth, laughter and compassion. Her performances on ''Let's Pretend'' and many other radio programs brought joy to millions. Private interment at Ferncliff Cemetery. A memorial service to celebrate her life is planned.
