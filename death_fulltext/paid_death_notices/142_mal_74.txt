
        HOLMES-Walter S., Jr. Financier, age 79, retired Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of CIT Financial Corporation, died on November 3, 1998, at Delray Beach, FL, where he retired in 1984. Holmes also served as corporate trustee of Lehigh University, Bethlehem, PA from 1974-1994, when he was named a Trustee Emeritus. Mr. Holmes is survived by his wife of 57 years, Jean Pringle, his two sons, Stephen of Ross, CA and Richard of Las Vegas, NV, and his two granddaughters, Lesley and Laura, both of San Francisco, CA. A private family service will be held. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Lehigh University as additions to the HolmesPringle Memorial Fund, which provides financial aid to Lehigh Business School Students.
