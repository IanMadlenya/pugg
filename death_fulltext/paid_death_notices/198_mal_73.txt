
        MACE--Frank Lawrence, 80, of Boca Raton, FL and Somers, NY. Died on July 6, 2003 at home in Somers. Beloved husband of Valerie, loving father of Frank Lawrence Mace, Jr., Sarah Mace, Christina Mace Turner and the late Kent Mace. Father-in-law of Alex Turner. Devoted grandfather of Oona Kent Mace Turner. He was born on December 31, 1922 in Norwalk, CT. He graduated from Dartmouth College in 1945 and from the Amos Tuck School of Business Administration in 1948. He served six years in the U.S. Marine Corps during World War II and the Korean War and retired with the rank of Major in 1952. He then spent an active career in the securities industry. Frank was a man of unique charm, humor and true kindness. He was a lifelong athlete, champion squash player, devoted family man and excellent friend. Memorial service at the Waccabuc Country Club, Mead Street, Waccabuc, NY, on July 10th, 2003 at 11 A.M. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Amnesty International USA, Inc., 322 8th Ave, New York, New York, 10001. >>AD#
