
        FRITZ--Barry Edward. 60, beloved son, father, grandfather and brother, died on April 14, at home in Miami Beach, after a courageous 12 year battle with prostate cancer. Barry and his late father Herbert, from Westfield, NJ, enjoyed a successful career in retailing. He will always be remembered for his passion for life, friendship and generosity. He is survived by his mother, Annette Fritz, two daughters Allison Fritz Feuer and Nicole Fritz McFarland, two grandsons, Miles and Benjamin Feuer, and brother Ken Fritz. Contributions can be made to The Foundation for Cancer Research and Education, P.O. Box 50, Free Union, VA 29940; or Dr. Bild Animal Hospital, 2500 NW 79th St, Miami Beach, FL 33147.
