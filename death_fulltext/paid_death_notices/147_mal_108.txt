
        ABU-HAKIMA-Ahmad Mustafa. Professor, scholar, historian and world authority on Eastern Arabia, born August 28, 1923, in Abbasiyya, Palestine, died April 9, 1998, in Ottawa, Canada. A truly contented man, wherever he traversed he brought optimism and love to the lives of many, many people around the world. Always remembered in our hearts, thoughts and prayers.
