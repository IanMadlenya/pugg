
        BILDNER-Benjamin. The Graduate School and University Center of The City University of New York mourns the passing of Benjamin Bildner, father of Albert Bildner of the Graduate Center Foundation's Board of Trustees and longtime member of the Graduate Center's Board of Visitors. Benjamin Bildner led a long, distinguished and productive life, in which he generously shared his self-made success through his support of educational institutions and other philanthropic causes. We extend our deepest sympathy to Albert and his wife, Lin, and the entire family. Frances Degen Horowitz, President Julien J. Studley, Chairman Graduate Center Foundation Bernd Brecher, Executive Director Graduate Center Foundation
        BILDNER-Benjamin. The Officers and Board of the American Associates, Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, are deeply saddened by the death of Benjamin Bildner. Beloved husband of Ruth Bildner, devoted father of Albert Bildner, and father-in-law of Lin Illusorio Bildner. Ben-Gurion University is most grateful for his generous support and extends sincere condolences to his entire family. Kenneth L. Tucker, President Harold L. Oshry, Chairman of the Board Kenneth M. Farber, Executive Vice President
