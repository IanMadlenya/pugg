
        CHARNEY--Sol, Ed.D. Of Yonkers, NY, died on January 8, 2005, at the age of 79 after a long illness. Devoted husband of the late Maxine Charney. Beloved father of Dr. Richard Charney of Briarcliff Manor, NY, Dr. Robert Charney of Springfield, NJ, and father-in-law of Stacy. Cherished grandfather of Molly and Laura. Devoted brother of Hy Charney of Rye Brook, NY, and Evelyn Kantor of Riverdale, NY. A dedicated, devoted educator of thousands of students during his 45 year tenure as a science teacher and administrator in the New Rochelle School District. His love of science lives on through the success of those he inspired. Graveside services New Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Iselin, NJ, Monday, January 10, 2005, at 11:30 am. Shiva will be observed at the home of Dr. Richard Charney.
