
        GOLDER-Morris T. (Teddy). On September 11, 2001, in Florida. Graduate of NYU and Captain in the US Army. Chancellor of Knights of Pythias. President and owner of T.P. Industries. He was loved and cherished by his family. He is survived by his beloved wife Louise, children Bennett, Mark and Betsy; grandchildren, who loved him so much, Bradley, Bennett, Michael and Lindsey. Funeral was held.
