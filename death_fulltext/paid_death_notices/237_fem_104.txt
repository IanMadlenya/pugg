
        LIPMAN--Betty Jayne, 86, of Roslyn Heights, died Saturday October 14th, 2006. Wife of Stanley Lipman for 64 years. Mother of Leslie Principe and Ric Lipman. Grandmother of Michael Principe and Robert Principe. Greatgrandmother of Mira and Jesse and Ray Principe. She was a Certified Braille Teacher and a choreographer for community musical productions. Services at 1:30pm, Monday October 16th at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
        $(6$)LIPMAN--Betty Jayne. The members of Shelter Rock Tennis Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Betty Jayne Lipman. She was a friend and will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to her husband and his entire family.
