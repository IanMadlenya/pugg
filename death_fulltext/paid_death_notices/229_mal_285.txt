
        LUFRANO--Carmine (Carm). Age 89, died on February 20 after a long struggle with Alzheimer's Disease. He is survived by his wife of 58 years, Joan (nee O'Brien), his sister Marie Maier, his brothers Louis (Rosemary) and Edward (Anne), sisters-in-law Rose O'Brien, Anne Kenny, Marie King and Jeanne Lufrano. He was adored by numerous nieces and nephews. Carm was born in New York City and was a lifelong resident. He served in WWII with the U.S. Americal Division in the South Pacific and was decorated for bravery on Guadalcanal. He spent his working career in the textile industry. His charm and wit will be missed by all who knew him. Abiding by his wishes there will be no service.
