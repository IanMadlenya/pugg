
        SILFEN-Mildred. The Board of Trustees and the entire Ethical Culture Fieldston School community note with sadness the death of Mildred Silfen, former Fieldston Lower faculty member who taught from 1961 until her retirement from the School in 1986. Loyal colleague and dear friend, Milli's generosity, wisdom, and affectionate humor will be remembered always. Sara E. Nathan and S. Donald Sussman Frederick C. Calder Helen Halverson Ethical Culture Fieldston School
