
        MOSBACH-Erwin H. Died Aug. 1 after a long illness. He is survived by his loving wife, Marianne, and by his son, Peter, and his grandchildren, David and Michael. He was formerly a biochemist at Beth Israel Medical Center. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
        MOSBACH-Erwin, Ph.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins the physicians and staff of our Department of Surgery in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague, Erwin Mosbach, Ph.D., former Director of the Lipid Research Laboratory and the recipient of numerous research grants from the National Institute of Health. To his wife, Marianne, his son, Peter, and the entire Mosbach family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Robert G.
