
        MEINWALD-Rose. Age 89. On March 6, 2000. Loving mother of Linda Gold and Barbara Meinwald. Beloved grandmother of Adam, Michael and Steven Gold. She will always be remembered for her loyalty to her numerous lifelong friends and family, her intuition, sense of humor, sweet beauty and love of music, theatre and dance. Graveside services today, Tuesday, 2PM, at Mount Lebanon Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Parker Jewish Geriatric Institute.
