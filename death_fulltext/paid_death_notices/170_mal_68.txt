
        BERGER-Robert S. Of Brooklyn, N.Y. Died on Wednesday, March 7, 2001 at age 91. Mr. Berger graduated from the City College of New York class of 1930. He went on to do graduate work at the University of Berlin and Yale University. He had a long career in the New York City school system as a teacher and administrator. During World War II he served with the Department of the Navy. Mr. Berger is survived by his devoted wife of 64 years, Mrs. Gertrude M. Berger of Brooklyn, N.Y., a brother Mr. Jack Berger of Long Island, N.Y., two sons, Dr. Avram S. Berger of West Simsbury, CT, Professor Selman A. Berger of Staten Island, N.Y., four grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. His intellectual prowess and creativity will be acutely missed. Funeral services will take place today, Thursday, March 8, 2001 at the the Menorah Chapels at 1:45PM, 2145 Richmond Ave., Staten Island, N.Y.
