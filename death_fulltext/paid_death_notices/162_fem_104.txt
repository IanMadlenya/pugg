
        BERGMANN-Michael D. Longtime resident of Larchmont, NY, died July 12. Ms. Bergmann was a successful real estate entrepreneur and president and owner of Condo Experts in Larchmont. She is survived by her brother Roy, his wife Janie, niece Hillary, nephew Erik, her dear friend Barbara De Maria and the family of her longtime friend Phyllis Bers, also of Larchmont (Phillip and Mozart Kaufman, John and Mary Bers and Ed Rosenfeld.) She will be remembered for her quick wit, independent thinking, passion for living and dazzling blue eyes. A memorial will be held on Friday, August 18, at 11 AM, at St. Augustine's Church in Larchmont. Friends may send memorial donations to Planned Parenthood or to Jansen Memorial Hospice (Tuckahoe, NY).
