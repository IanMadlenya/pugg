
        SCOURBY-Lula. 88, of Manhattan. Born at home in Brooklyn, June 12, 1912. Died September 2, 2000, at Lenox Hill Hospital. A longtime independent art dealer and interior designer, she earned a B.S. from Columbia University School of Business. Survived by niece Alexandra Scourby Mackler, great-niece Amanda Mackler, numerous loving cousins, nieces, nephews, and devoted friends who will miss her vast wisdom and humor. Predeceased by sister Mary and brothers Nicholas and Alexander Scourby. Visitation Tuesday, 24 PM and 7-9 PM, at Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 1st Avenue. Funeral service noon Wednesday, at Holy Trinity Cathedral, 319 East 74th St. 212-288-3215. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Holy Trinity Cathedral.
