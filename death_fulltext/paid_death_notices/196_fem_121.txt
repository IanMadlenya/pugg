
        SCHUETZ-Mona Wolcott, 82, of Middletown, NJ died May 15, 2003. Mona graduated from Elmira College and received her Master's degree as a dietician. She devoted much of her time to volunteer work in various charitable organizations and was President of the Family and Children's Service, Long Branch. She is survived by her husband, son, daughter, four grandchildren and a sister.
