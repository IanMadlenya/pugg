
        SMITH--Marion J. E. In her 102nd year, on June 17, 2006, of Roslyn Heights, NY. Beloved sister of Helen Emmerich and aunt and great-aunt to many. Lifelong member and past president of the Soroptimist International Club of New York. Marion also founded and served as president for 35 years of Marioness, Inc., of New York, a company she established in the Depression years.
