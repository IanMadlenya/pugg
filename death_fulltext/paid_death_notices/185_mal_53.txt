
        FOSTER-E. Stephen. November 22, 1963-June 3, 2002. A Southern gentleman and a dedicated Brooklynite, Stephen was an ebullient human being with extraordinary political knowledge, an unmatchable sense of humor, and true joy in life. Stephen was Director of Government Affairs at Brooklyn Public Library for many years and, earlier, Borough President Howard Golden's representative to the Library Board. He loved the Library and we loved working with him. We will sorely miss him. From his colleagues and friends at BPL
