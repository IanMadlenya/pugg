
        WINSTON-RUBENSTEIN-Alice, of NYC, on Sunday, January 3rd, 1999, of cancer. Master drama teacher of the Stella Adler Theatre School. She is survived by her husband, Boris Rubenstein, son, Stephen Rubenstein, and two sisters, Irene Wyman of Boston and Fay Rosen Bain of NYC. Services today, 10 AM, Congregation Ramat Orah, 550 W. 110th St., NYC.
