
        GERSON--Micki. We are saddened by the untimely passing of Micki, only 26 years of age, on May 13th, 2005. Beloved daughter of Sharon Gerson Farley and Steven Gerson, devoted step-daughter of Bob Farley and Beth Gerson, dear granddaughter of Miriam Levy and loving sister of Carly. Micki attended the Dalton School and was a graduate of the University of Miami. She will be deeply missed by all her family and friends. Services will be held at the Plaza Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, Monday, May 16th, at 2 pm.
