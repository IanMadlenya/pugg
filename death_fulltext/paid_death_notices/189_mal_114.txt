
        PARKINSON-Samuel Dexter. Age 80, of Lyme, CT, and formerly of Laurel Hollow, New York, died October 7th 2002. He was born August 7, 1922 in Stockton, California. His wife of 48 years Ann, children, Elizabeth Terry Smith and husband Brant, of Essex, CT, Louisa Evans and husband Robert of Fishers Island, NY, Andrew Parkinson & wife Elizabeth of Winnetka, IL., and Thomas Parkinson & wife Jennifer of Winnetka, IL, 11 grandchildren and his two sisters Nancy Brennan and Jean Carey, survive him. Services will be held on Friday, October 11th, at 11 A.M., St. Ann's Episcopal Church in Old Lyme, Connecticut. He was greatly loved and will be sorely missed.
