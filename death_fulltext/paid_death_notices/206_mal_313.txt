
        GORELICK -- Sol (Sholem). Died March 23. Beloved husband of Mildred. Father of David and Deborah Zimmer. Grandfather of nine. Greatgrandfather of ''Nes''. A man of exceptional wisdom, a staunch Union Man, beloved by all who knew him and worked with him for causes that would better humanity and the world. He leaves a void that can't be filled. Donations may be made to any hospice.
