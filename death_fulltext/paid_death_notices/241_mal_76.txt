
        HOCKER--Lon Jr., of Woods Hole MA, a lawyer and WWII Navy commander, at home on January 31, at 96. Survivors include: his wife of nearly 70 years, Esther Sands Hocker; a daughter, Priscilla Hocker Claman, of Weston MA; a son, Lon Hocker III, of Hawaii; five grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren. Memorial service at Church of the Messiah Episcopal, 22 Church St, Woods Hole, Saturday, February 10 at 4 PM. Relatives and friends kindly invited. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to American Red Cross, Cape Cod and Islands Chapter, 286 South Street, Hyannis MA 02601, 866-762-1928.
