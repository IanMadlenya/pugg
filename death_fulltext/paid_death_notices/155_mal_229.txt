
        NAST-Thomas D. Of Rye, NY, died on December 16,. 1999 at the age of 85 years. Husband of Janet Baldwin Nast. Father of Lawrence D., Thomas B., Cynthia C. & Tricia Nast Beran. Grandfather of seven. Member of Sunningdale Country Club. Also a member of World Presidents' Organization (Metro Chapter). Memorial service Sunday, 2 PM, at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Avenue, White Plains, New York.
        NAST-Thomas D. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Thomas D. Nast. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Janet, and to the entire family. William Ettelson, President Michael Roth, Secretary
