
        FUNK--Alice Lowenbraun. 69, of Westbury, on Saturday, January 21, 2006. Alice was a retired elementary school teacher in Brentwood. Her second and favorite career was helping to raise her two grandchildren, Zack and Alexander. She had just celebrated her 50th anniversary with her husband Alan, and was the mother of Andy and Lisa Ann. Alice was always smiling, so remember her with your own smile. Funeral is 12 noon, Sunday, January 22, at North Shore Synagogue in Syosset. Donations to North Shore Synagogue Feed the Needy Fund or any charity that feeds the hungry.
