
        EMORY-Katherine Riegel, aged 88, resident of Southport, CT, and Brooklin, ME, and formerly of New York City on April 5. Beloved wife of the late German H.H. Emory. Survived by three daughters, Katherine E. Stookey, Leila E. Baldwin, and Lucy E. Ambach, and a son, Benjamin R. Emory. Grandmother of fourteen and great grandmother of fourteen. Funeral Services will take place April 10 at 11 a.m. at Trinity Episcopal Church in Southport. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in her memory to Children's Aid Society, 105 East 22nd Street, New York, NY 10010 or to Family Services Woodfield, Inc., 475 Clinton Avenue, Bridgeport, CT 06605.
