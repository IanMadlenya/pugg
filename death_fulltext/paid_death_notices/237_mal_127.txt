
        DUGGAN--Terry. 52, of NYC, died peacefully October 8, 2006 of kidney cancer. Nobody fought harder. He is survived by his lifetime partner of 28 years Michael Rosen, his brother Michael Duggan of Sarasota, FL, and his sister Therese Steuart of Port Charlotte, FL. His death will leave a deep hole in the hearts of his co-workers at Ovation Travel and especially his aunt, nieces and nephew and his beloved extended family. Donations can be made in his name to the Kidney Cancer Association.
