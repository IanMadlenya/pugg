
        GLATZER-Rabbi Joseph. Congregation Beth Sholom of Lawrence records with profound sorrow the passing in Israel of our revered and beloved Shamash for over 45 years. After miraculously surviving the Shoah, he devoted his life to serving his people. He will be remembered as an extraordinary scholar, a gentle teacher and a loving friend to every member of our congregation. Rabbi Kenneth N. Hain Dr. Gilbert Klaperman Rabbi Emeritus Danny Hiller, President
