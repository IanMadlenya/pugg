
        OSHINS-Susan Reingold. Cherished wife of the late Alfred Beno Oshins, devoted mother of E.J., Jo Ann, & son-in-law Ray, treasured sister of Barbara Diamond, Joyce Baumgarten, and the late Ruth Joseph, loving aunt, and loyal friend. Our 'SuSu'. A New Yorkers' New Yorker. Shared her energy, verve, and civic pride with The Hunger Project, Manhattan Theater Club, n.g.o. at the UN, School Volunteers and Abzug campaigns. Her optimism & courage inspired and guided all. She will always be a sparkling presence in our universe.
