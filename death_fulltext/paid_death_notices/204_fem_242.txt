
        LOWENTHAL--Lillian Plane, January 22, 2004. Beloved wife of the late William. Devoted mother of Mark Lowenthal, mother-in-law of Ilene, loving grandmother of Neil and Elena Lowenthal, Linda and Robert Tolstoi, and Roger Lowenthal. Greatgrandmother of Michael Tolstoi. Sister of the late Lewis Plane, Rose Kline Tobias, and Jack Plane. With her wisdom she helped many in her long life. We will remember her with love and affection. Private funeral services were held Friday in Trevose, PA.
