
        JASSEN--Mollie. On Thursday, October 21, 2004, at St. Peter's Hospital, Albany. Born in Brooklyn the daughter of the late Adolph and Anna Altman Rausch. Graduate of Jamaica Teachers College and taught at Elementary School PS 181 for many years. She was the wife of the late Bernard Jassen and is survived by a daughter, Susan Luntz of Glenmont, NY; a son, Harvey Jassen of Brooklyn, NY. Graveside service Sunday, October 24, 1:30 PM at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, NY. Donations to the Alzheimer's Ass'n.
