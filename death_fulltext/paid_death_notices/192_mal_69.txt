
        SUNA-Joel. Of Malverne, NY. On January 4, 2003 in his home in his 93rd year. Beloved husband of Eleanor Torge Suna and father to Linda Moliver, Nada Mazurek and Carla Suna Nolte. Dear father-in-law to Gary, Bob and Chris. Loving grandfather of seven. Devoted brother to Gladys, Rosalie, Frances and Leonard. Beloved and cherished by many other friends and family. He was a successful jeweler for over 60 years. He was a veteran of the Polish and US Armies. Funeral services will be Monday, January 6th, at Gutterman's in Rockville Centre at 10 AM. Donations may be made to the Comprehensive Community Hospice, 516586-1511, or a charity of your choice. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery.
