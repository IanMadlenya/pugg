
        NELKIN-Julian. 85. Lake Worth Fl., on November 30, 1999. Formerly of Great Neck. Devoted and loving husband of Rae. Caring father of Susan Lee, Alan and Henry, and father-in-law of Marianne and Janice. Wonderful grandfather of David and Kelli, Jonathan, Emily and Dylan. Brother of Mildred Grumet. Brother-in-law of Esther, Louis and Claire, Irving and Rhoda, Buzzy and Roy. Dear uncle and great uncle, friend to many. Above all, he was a mensch who will be missed by all he touched. Memorial contributions to Hospice of Palm Beach County, Inc., 5300 East Ave, West Palm Beach Fl 33407.
