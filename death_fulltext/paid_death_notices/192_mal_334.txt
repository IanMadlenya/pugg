
        SORIN-Murray. Age 94, of Palm Beach, FL, and formerly of New York City, died January 25, 2003. An airconditioning pioneer in New York City, Sorin was a Brooklyn Law School graduate, held a seat on the NYSE, and served in WWII. He is survived by his beloved wife Sascha Newman Sorin, his sons John Sorin and William Sorin, and three grandchildren. Private memorial service.
