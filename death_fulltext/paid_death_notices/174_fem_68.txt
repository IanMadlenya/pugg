
        HARNDEN-Ida. Dancer. Devoted wife of the late artist William Hershel Harnden. Loving mother of Bonnie and Danny. Will finally play The Palace. Cherished sister of Pauline Bloom and affectionate aunt to many nieces and nephews. She was a tireless senior advocate, loved and admired by all who knew her. Friends may call at Riverside Chapel 180 W. 76 St., NYC, Tuesday July 10th 7pm-9pm. Services Wednesday 11:15am.
