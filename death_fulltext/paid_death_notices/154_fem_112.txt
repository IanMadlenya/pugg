
        LEMKAU-Janet L., 84, of Essex, Connecticut, formerly of Plandome, NY, died on November 10, 1999, in Essex. She was born in Syracuse, NY, on March 20, 1915, the daughter of Arthur W. and Adena Phillips Leasby. She was the widow of Hudson B. Lemkau, Sr.; the mother of Arthur Lemkau of Port Washington, NY, Hudson B. Lemkau, Jr., of Hillsboro, NH, and Alison Vaughn of Old Lyme, Conn.; & the grandmother of 7. Funeral services will be private. Donations may be made in her memory to the Essex Meadows Employee Scholarship Fund, 30 Bokum Road, Essex, Conn. 06426. For more information, contact the Fulton-Theroux Funeral Home, Beckwith Lane, Old Lyme.
