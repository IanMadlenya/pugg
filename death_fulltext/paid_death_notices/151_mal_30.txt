
        RICHARDSON-Donald, John. 66 years, died July 24, Salt Lake City, Utah. He was born in Syracuse, New York. Attended Duke University and Wharton Business School at University of Pennsylvania. He was a U.S. Marine Corp veteran and commanded an artilery battery. He was the owner of several businesses, vice-president of several others. Survived by sons, Donald J. Richardson II, Pleasant Grove, UT, David Richardson, Tampa, FL and Dirk Richardson, Amherst, NH, daughter Debbie Osborn, Sydney, Australia. Funeral will be held Wednesday, August 4, 11:00 AM, The John C. Tindall Funeral Home, Syracuse, NY. Burial, Oakwood Cemetery.
