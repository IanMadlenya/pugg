
        BAUMGARTNER-Frances. On September 20, 1997 after a long illness. She was a dedicated volunteer for over 30 years at The Lighthouse for the Blind, New York City and also was employed by Rubin Donnoley Corp. of New York City. Beloved sister of Edith O. Katz of Sarasota, Florida and Lillian Kiesler of New York City. Also survived by many nieces, nephews and cousins. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, 1PM at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Avenue (9th and 10th Streets). Contributions in her memory may be made to: The Lighthouse for the Blind or Cabrini Hospice of New York City.
