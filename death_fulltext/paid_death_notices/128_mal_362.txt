
        STYLES-James G., Chappaqua, N.Y., September 28, 1997. Son of Patricia Styles. Husband of Ann M. (nee Lacoseglio). Father of twin sons, Gordon and Connor. Brother of Michael and Roger Styles and Kathleen Nehus. Resting Beecher Funeral home, Pleasantville, where family will receive friends Wednesday 7-9 and Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Burial of the Dead: Rite II, Church of St. Mary the Virgin, Saturday 11 A.M. Interment will follow in Churchyard. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Teatown Lake Reservation or the Church of St. Mary The Virgin in his name would be appreciated.
        STYLES-Jim. It is with extreme sorrow that we mourn the untimely death of our friend and neighbor, Jim Styles. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his loving wife Ann and children Connor and Gordon. We cannot express the horror and pain we feel for you. Elyse and Michael Fischman
