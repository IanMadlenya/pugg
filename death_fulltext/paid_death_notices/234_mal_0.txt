
        COHEN--Robert Marks, 78, passed away on June 29th, 2006. Cherished husband of Zipporah Cohen, devoted father of Margot Cohen, beloved brother of Michael Marks Cohen. Raised in Mt Vernon, NY he served more than four decades as a sales executive at W. Braun Company. Aficionado of cruise ships and classical music. Funeral services Sunday, July 2nd at 11:00am at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society. Shiva will be observed Sunday through Tuesday.
