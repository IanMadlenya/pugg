
        LEWISOHN--Florence Davis. October 13, 1911-April 12, 2005. Journalist and historian. In the 1930's when female journalists were few and far between she worked on The Oregonian, San Francisco Chronicle and CBS; during WWII she was the OWI's Far East editor. Florence went on to chronicle the arts, culture and history of the West Indies in 8 volumes. She and her late husband Walter P. Lewisohn, spent more than 30 years collecting, chronicling, preserving and promoting the arts, culture and history of their beloved West Indies. Florence is survived by her sons Loren, Leonard and his wife Jane. Service will be held on April 18, 11 AM, Russell and Gooch Funeral Chapel, 270 Miller Ave., Mill Valley, CA 94941. 415-388-1573. Cremation to follow. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hospice of Marin, 17 E. Sir Francis Drake Blvd, Larkspur, CA 94939.
