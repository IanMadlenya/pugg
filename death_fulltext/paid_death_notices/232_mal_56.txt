
        SCHOTLAND--Dr. Richard M. Professor Emeritus of Atmospheric Physics at the University of Arizona died April 24, 2006 after a gallant battle with Multiple Myeloma. Dr. Schotland was born February 11, 1927 in Irvington, NJ. He earned a doctorate in meteorology at MIT in 1952. A Professor of Meteorology at NYU Uptown from 1952 to 1973, he went on to become a Professor of Atmospheric Physics at the University of Arizona. Dr. Schotland was a pioneer in laser radar research. At his request no memorial services were held. He is survived by his wife Jane, son Robert, daughter Dana and three grandchildren.
