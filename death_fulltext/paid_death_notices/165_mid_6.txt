
        DONGHIA-Carmella, mother of the late Angelo Donghia, passed away Friday, October 27, 2000. Born on October 3, 1913 in Tyler, Pennsylvania, Mrs. Donghia is survived by a brother, James Ferraccio and two sisters, Amelia Krotzer and Pompey Sciullo. She moved to NYC in 1968 to assist her son in his design endeavors and remained a mentor to Angelo Donghia throughout his career. Funeral services will take place at Saint Vincent Ferrer Church located at 869 Lexington Avenue on Monday, October 30, 2000 at 10:30AM. In lieu of flowers the family has requested that donations be made to Saint Vincents Hospital Comprehensive Breast Center.
