
        HOFFMAN-Lillian. On January 5, 2001, at age of 93. Beloved wife of the late Irving Hoffman. Devoted mother of Donna (Hoffman) Bilgrei and Michael Bilgrei and Ellen (Hoffman) Lieberman and Robert Lieberman. Loving sister of Adeline Bodner and adoring grandmother to Jeremy, Allyson, Rachel and Joshua. Dedicated NYC school teacher for 25 years and world traveler. The family mourns the loss of our wise, witty, and brilliant tower of strength. She will be greatly missed. May she rest in peace. Funeral on Sunday, January 7, 1:30 PM at Schwartz Bros.Jeffer Memorial Chapel, Queens Blvd. and 76th Rd., Forest Hills.
