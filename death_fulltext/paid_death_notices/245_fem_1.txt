
        MESSELOFF--Lillian Vivian Levinson, wife of the late Charles R. Messeloff M.D., died peacefully on May 30, 2007. She was a devoted mom to Alice Fraenkel and Reva Greenberg, a loving motherin-law, supportive and concerned grandma and sweet, caring great-grandma. Her family will miss her. October 1908-May 2007.
