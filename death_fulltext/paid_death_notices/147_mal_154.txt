
        REISS-George. Died suddenly at the age of 78 on Saturday, April 17th, 1999. Loving father of Johnny. Dearest life's partner of Rena. Devoted brother-in-law of Gordon and of Louise. Beloved husband of the late Helga and brother of the late Eric. Born in Vienna, he came to the U.S. in 1939. He was a World War II veteran, a husband, a father, a lawyer, and a gentleman who graced all those who knew him with his friendship and kindness. A memorial service will be held today at 11 AM at the Park West Riverside Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, New York.
