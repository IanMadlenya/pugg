
        CHIA-Dewey Teh-huai. Age 86, on August 18th at Greenwich Hospital. Born in Shanghai, China. He came to the United States in 1956 where he established an oceanic shipping and international trading business. He is survived by his wife, Kitty Shun-hua, son Pei-yuan, daughters Pei-hsin and Pei-loh, daughter-in-law Frances, sons-inlaw David Gedalecia and Adrian Lo, and seven grandchildren.
