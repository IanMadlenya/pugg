
        RUTHIZER-Harold. On July 17, 1999, at age 90. Formerly of Roosevelt, NY, and for the last 16 years of Coconut Creek, FL. Beloved husband of the late Ruth. Devoted and loved father of Marilyn and Allen Greenberg, Jeffrey and Monica Ruthizer, Grant and Mimi Ruthizer and Theodore and Jane Ruthizer. Cherished grandfather of Dr.'s William and Hilary Greenberg, Elizabeth and Alan Cohen, Caroline and Mitchell Nussbaum, Jessica and Robin Ruthizer, Joshua and Alexander Ruthizer. Affectionately known as ''Poppy'' to his five great-grandchildren, Jonathan, Rachel and Jordana Cohen and Robert and David Greenberg. Adored friend and companion of Bella Grossman. From 1944 to 1980 Harold was the owner of ''Roosevelt Hardware'' on Nassau Road, Roosevelt, LI. Funeral service on Tuesday, July 20, at 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        RUTHIZER-Harold. The Kefalidis and Golden families offer their deepest sympathy to their true friend and colleague Grant Ruthizer and his entire family on the passing of their beloved father, grandfather and great-grandfather. Our hearts and prayers are with you in your time of sadness.
