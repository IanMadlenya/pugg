
        MAIER-Mary Richmond. Wife of the late John Maier, M.D., died on November 7th in Ithaca, NY, age 85. She is survived by two sons, Hugh P. of Portland, OR., and John R. of West Hartford, CT, one daughter, Jan of Manhattan, and three granddaughters. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cornell Medical College, C/O Director of Operations, Office of Development, Cornell Medical College, 1300 York Avenue, Box 123, New York, NY 10021.
