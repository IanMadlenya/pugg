
        KING--John F. The Professional Advisors Council of the Calvary Fund mourns with sadness the loss of our esteemed member and colleague, John F. King. His commitment to the mission of Calvary Hospital as well as his strategic counsel and dedicated participation were instrumental in the formation and growth of the Professional Advisors Council. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife and the entire family. Thomas J. Fahey, Jr., M.D., Chairman, Calvary Hospital Board of Directors Richard E. Meyer, Chairman, Calvary Fund Board of Directors Frank A. Calamari, President, Calvary Hospital/ Calvary Fund Gerald C. Tobin, Esq., Chairman, Calvary Fund Professional Advisors Council Vincent J.
