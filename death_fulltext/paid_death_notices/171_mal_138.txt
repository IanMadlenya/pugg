
        BOTTONE-Alphonse P. Passed away Sunday April 8, 2001. Age 73. Passed away after a short battle with cancer. Survived by his mother Catherine Bottone, age 96, and his sister Angela Evangelista, brother-in-law Sandy Evangelista, niece Susan Zwick and nephews John and Matthew Evangelista, and two grand-nieces and one grand-nephew. Services in Forest Lawn, Hollywood Hills, California on Friday, April 13, at 2:30PM.
