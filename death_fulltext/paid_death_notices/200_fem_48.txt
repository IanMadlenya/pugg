
        FOCHIOS--Mary L., age 77, formerly of Tuxedo Park and the Upper West Side, died September 2, 2003 after a prolonged illness. Born in Charleston, West Virginia to Landon Rodgers Jones and Mary Arthur. She attended the University of Maryland and received her Master's Degree from Columbia University Teachers College. She taught English for many years at the High School of Art and Design in NYC and was also a social worker and writer. She is fondly remembered for her culinary specialties. Survived by her brother, Landon, daughter Andrea, son Steven, daughters-in-law Martha and Gretl, and grandchildren, Evan and Stephen.
