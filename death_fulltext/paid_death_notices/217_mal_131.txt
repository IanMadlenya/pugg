
        BERGER -- Alexander, 83, died February 9, 2005 with his wife and children at his side. Beloved husband of Phyllis, loved father of Peter and Marsha, brother of Norman. Writer, accountant, concerned citizen of the world, veteran of WWII, a critical thinker with an inquiring mind. Funeral service 11 AM today at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, Manhattan. Donations to the Hospice by the Sea, Boca Raton, FL.
