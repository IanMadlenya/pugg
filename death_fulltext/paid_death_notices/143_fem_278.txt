
        USADI-Kathleen, age 57, died Monday, December 28, 1998, in Brooklyn. Wife of Bob, mother of Douglas and Ben, sister of Patty Lollis. Her life was rich with feelings for growing things, painting, listening, talking, information, friends. She loved working with children. She was not a simple person. Contributions to Sixth Avenue & Fifteenth Street Community Garden, 565 6th Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11215 would be welcome.
