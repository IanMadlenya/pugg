
        CAINE-David, 79. Of Boca Raton, FL, died on March 19th, 2003. Formerly of Riverdale, NY, retired owner of Churchill Furniture Rental Company and Caine Brothers Furniture, Inc. He is survived by his loving wife, Lillian, daughters Nancy and Claudia, son-in-law Arnie, grandchildren, Matthew, Jaime, and Michael Floyd. Services to be held on Friday in North Lauderdale, FL.
