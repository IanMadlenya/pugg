
        NESTANPOWER-Marion M. Died December 1, 1998, 82 years old. Beloved wife of Samuel and sister of Herman Masin. Adored mother of Louise and Nathaniel Yohalem, Stephen and Ruth Nestanpower, cherished grandmother of Jennifer Yohalem, Eve Yohalem and Nicholas Polsky. Ecstatic greatgrandmother of Joseph Yohalem Polsky. A pioneering reading teacher for 25 years, Mary insured that no child left her classroom without being able to read. She worked tirelessly on behalf of her community, serving as President of B'nai Brith Redemption Chapter 275. Mary was the heart and bedrock of all who knew her. Funeral Wednesday, December 2, 1:15PM, Riverside Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to B'nai Brith and The Children's Home in Israel.
        NESTANPOWER - Marion. The Riverdale Jewish Center extends profound condolences to Sam Nestanpower on the loss of his wife. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
