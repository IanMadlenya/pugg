
        ROTHENBUECHER-Beatrice M., age 93, died December 30th in Manhattan. She had a career in documentary film making and public information. In the early sixties, she became an associate of the Film and Broadcasting Commission of the National Council of Churches of Christ. For the past years she has been passionately committed to the completion of her documentary film about the life and times of Dietrich Bonhoeffer. She is survived by her daughter, Beatrice, two granddaughters, Gita and Maya, seven great-grandchildren, Olivia, Judson, Julia, Lauren, Celeste, Samuel and Chelsea. Memorial service will be held January 12, at 1PM, Madison Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Ave
