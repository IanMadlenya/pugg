
        BLADES-William B., III, a native of New Bern, NC, died peacefully at his home on October 6, 1997 at the age of 75; he spent most of his adult life in New York. He was a graduate of the University of North Carolina and the American Institute of International Commerce. He was employed by the American Express Co. and subsequently retired from Scandinavian Airlines. Memorial gifts may be made to the Metropolitan Museum of Art, Development Office, 1000 Fifth Avenue, New York, New York 10028.
