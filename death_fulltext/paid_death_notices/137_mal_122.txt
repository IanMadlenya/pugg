
        BROOKS-James R. On June 13, 1998. Beloved husband of Frances Forsdick. Loving father of Lawrence, Jonathan, Carolyn and Penelope. Cherished grandfather of Timothy, Rebecca and Jeremy. A distinguished lawyer, diplomat and economist, he worked for the United Nations and governments around the world to promote economic development. Beginning with the US Treasury Department during WW II, he helped implement the Marshall Plan in Europe and post-war recovery in Greece. He was Deputy Head of the United Nations Civilian Operations in the post-colonial Congo and advised the governments of Bolivia and Afghanistan, finally retiring to private practice from the the US State Department. He will be deeply missed by his family and his friends thoughout the world.
