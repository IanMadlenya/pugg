
        GREENE--Marian Schomer. 83, passed away after a brief illness. Beloved wife of Stanley for 62 years. Loving mother of Peter, Benjamin and Andrew. Devoted sister of Jesse and Morton. Admired for her career in social work in service to the aged. Director of Nassau County Office of Senior Citizens' Affairs and of Jewish Association of Services for the aged. Graduate of Hunter Hight School, Hunter College 1943, and ColuMbia University School of Social Work. She will be missed.
