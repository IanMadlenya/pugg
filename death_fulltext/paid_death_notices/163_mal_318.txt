
        FARRELLY-Reverend Thomas J. Son of Thomas and Josephine Farrelly, loving brother of Ann Morris and uncle of Robert, JoAnn, Eileen and Maureen, grand-uncle of Justine and Erin. Ordained priest of the Archdiocese of New York in 1961, went to the Lord on August 25, 2000. He served as Associate Pastor of St. Gregory the Great Parish and Pastor of Sacred Heart Parish in Manhattan, and Blessed Sacrament Parish in the Bronx. He devoted himself to the pastoral and social needs of all members of the communities where he served and in a special way to the Spanish-speaking communities. Wake at Montera Funeral Home, 1848 Westchester Ave., Bronx, on Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Blessed Sacrament Church on Monday, 2-5 PM. Mass of the Holy Eucharist on Monday, at 7:30 PM. Funeral at Blessed Sacrament Church on Tuesday, 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, please donate to Blessed Sacrament Parish.
