
        SPODHEIM - Renee. Died January 31, 2003 at her residence in New York; daughter of Frederic and Regina Spodheim. She was born in Bacau, Romania and became a U.S. citizen in 1947. During WWII she was a translator for the Office of War Information. She studied at the Sorbonne and Columbia University; she taught at Columbia, The University of Havana, Cuba, and Fieldston School, holding the last post until she retired. She was also a literary agent and translator. She is survived by her sister Hilda DeBacker of Charleston, S.C. The burial will be private.
