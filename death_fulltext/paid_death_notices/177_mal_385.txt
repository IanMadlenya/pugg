
        PAZ-Peter. Photographer. 64. He has died in Nice, France, after a lengthy illness. Known mainly for his corporate annual report and advertising photography, he was winner of the Clio Award for the Otis Annual Report. Represented by the Image Bank, his clients included, among other multi-nationals, Monsanto, Exxon, McCormick and Aerospatial. Mr. Paz also traveled extensively on assignment for the Jordanian government, NBC News, and Business Week. In his earlier career as a professional dancer under the name Yigal Paz, he danced with the Anna Sokolow dance company and the Batsheva de Rothschild company. He is survived by his daughter Lyrissa, his ex-wife Luba Paz, and his companion of many years in Nice, Beverly Pimsleur.
