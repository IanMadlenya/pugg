
        SAUL--Joseph E. The Trustees, faculty, and staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of Joseph E. Saul, an outstanding philanthropist. Mr. Saul's personal commitment to Mount Sinai included generous support for the Campaign for Excellence and Alzheimers research. His loyalty to Mount Sinai will long be remembered. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Norma; his son Andrew, an esteemed Trustee; and the entire Saul family. Peter W. May, Chairman, Boards of Trustees, The Mount Sinai Medical Center Kenneth L. Davis, M.D., President and CEO, The Mount Sinai Medical Center
