
        BERNSTEIN-Stella, 97. Hollywood, FL. Formerly of West Hartford, on February 5, 2002. An extraordinary life has ended and we are all poorer for it. Devoted wife of the late Sidney, adored mother of Dick and Sonny, Mark & Arlene, David and Patty; grandchildren Lori, Robert, Jeffrey, Michael, Emily and Brad; and four great grandchildren. Also survived by her cherished sister-in-law Ethel Goodman. Graveside service on Friday, February 8th, 10:30AM Beth El Cemetery, Avon, CT.
