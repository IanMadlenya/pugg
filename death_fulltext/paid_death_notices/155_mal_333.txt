
        HAYNES-John M., Ph.D., of Northport, NY, on December 22, 1999, of leukemia. Survived by Gretchen, his daughters, Karen and Julia, and grandchildren, Charlotte and Oliver. John was the founding president of the Academy of Family Mediators and an international trainer of mediators and court personnel. He was also a prolific author of journal articles and three books. A memorial service is to be held on January 16 at 2:00 PM at Westbury Friends Meeting, Westbury, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to a memorial scholarship fund at The Union Institute in Cincinnati, Ohio.
