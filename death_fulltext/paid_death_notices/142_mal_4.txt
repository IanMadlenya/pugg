
        DiGIACINTO-Rose. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians and staff of the Hyman-Newman Institute for neurology and neurosurgery (INN) in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Rose DiGiacinto, beloved mother of George V. DiGiacinto, M.D., Director of the Division of Neurosurgery, at our partner institution, St Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center. To Dr. G. DiGiacinto, his entire family and all of his mother's loved ones, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink, MD, President and CEO Fred J. Epstein, MD, Co-Chairman Department of Neurosurgery and Director INN Eugene S. Flamm, MD, Co-Chairman Department of Neurosurgery
