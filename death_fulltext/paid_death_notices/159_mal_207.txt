
        WARREN-The Reverend William E. entered eternal life on April 18, 2000. Father Warren was pastor of St. Margaret of Cortona Church in the Bronx since July 1991. Beloved son of Mariana (nee Harkins) and the late William E. Warren, Sr. He attended St. Philip Neri Grammar School and Archbishop Stepinac High School. He entered St. Bonaventure University in 1956 and graduated with a degree of Political Science in 1960. In 1964 he entered St. Joseph's Seminary, Dunwoodie. He was ordained to the Sacred Priesthood on May 31, 1969 in St. Patrick's Cathedral by His Eminence Terrence Cardinal Cooke. The family will receive friends and parishioners Thursday and Friday 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. at Riverdale - on - Hudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Avenue (at West 261st Street), Bronx. Funeral Liturgy of the Word will be celebrated by Bishop James F. McCarthy on Saturday 10 A.M. at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated Monday 11 A.M. at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Margaret of Cortona School, Archbishop Stepinac High School or St. Bonaventure University would be deeply appreciated.
