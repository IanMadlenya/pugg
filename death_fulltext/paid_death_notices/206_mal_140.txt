
        SUTHERLAND--Dr. George L. Died March 10, 2004. He was 81. Research chemist and community leader. George had a long and successful career with American Cyanamid Company, retiring as VP Director of Research and Development, Lederle Labs. George was very active in the Rockland County community. He served on various boards, including Ramapo College, The Salvation Army, Good Samaritan Hospital, Association for the Visually Impaired and Helen Hayes Theatre. He was past President of Rockland United Way. Survived by his devoted wife Carol and a large, loving family. A Memorial Service will be held at Wanamaker & Carlough, Suffern, New York at 2 PM on March 14th.
