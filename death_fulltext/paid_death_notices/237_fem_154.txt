
        PEARCE--Sally. Passed away peacefully at home in her 92nd year on October 18, 2006. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Henry Pearce. Loving mother of Dr. David B. Pearce and Linda and Dr. Norton Rosensweig. Adored ''Nanna'' of her grandchildren J.P.
