
        WEBER--Julian L. 77, of New York City, on August 20, 2006. Beloved husband of Idelle, father of Todd and Suzanne, father-in-law of Elisabetta and Chris, grandfather of Bianca, Julia and Carlo. Brother of Vivian and Marilyn. Lawyer and former president of The National Lampoon. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the American Lung Association or Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America. His family and friends will miss his humor, intellect and generosity. A private memorial service will be held at a later date.
