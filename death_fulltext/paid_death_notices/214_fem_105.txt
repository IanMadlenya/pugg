
        RAABIN--Norma Hesse. 97, died peacefully on November 13, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Lewis D. Raabin. Loving mother of Edie and Eddie Scheurer. Adored grandmother of Lynne and Andrew. Cherished great-grandmother of Sarah and Noah. Devoted sister-in-law of Elizabeth Hesse. Predeceased by her brother Stanley Hesse. Caring aunt, cousin, teacher and friend. Her warmth, charm and grace endeared her to all whose lives she touched. She was devoted to community and charitable causes. She was a trustee of the Jewish Guild for the Blind and a member of the Board of the Women's Auxiliary of Temple Emanu-El. She was active in English in Action through the English Speaking Union. She cared deeply for her students. Family will receive at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81st St, Monday, November 15th, 6:30-9:00 pm, with services Tuesday, 11:30 am. Gifts to the Jewish Guild for the Blind, Women's Auxiliary Temple Emanu-El, or Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation would be greatly appreciated.
        RAABIN--Norma. Congregation Emanu-El, of the City of New York, expresses to the family of our devoted member, Norma Raabin, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Dr. David M. Posner Senior Rabbi Dr. Ronald B. Sobel Senior Rabbi Emeritus Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
        RAABIN--Norma. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation Emanu-El note sadly the death of our cherished and dedicated Board member, Norma Raabin. Jocelyn F. Chait, Pres.
