
        SCORSESE - Catherine, (nee Capa), on January 6th at Tisch Hospital, NYC. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Adored mother of Frank and Martin. Affectionate sister of Joseph Cappa and Millie Loacano and dear friend of the Guidetti family. Reposing at the Church Center of the United Nations, 44th Street and 1st Avenue, NYC on Tuesday, 2-4 PM and 6-8 PM and Wed. at Colonial Funeral Home 2819 Hylan Blvd. S.I. from 4-8 PM.         SCORSESE - Catherine. The Tisch School of the Arts at New York University deeply mourns the passing of Catherine Scorsese, mother of alumnus and dear friend, Martin Scorsese.
