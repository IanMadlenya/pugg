
        OBERKOTTER-Paul. Age 93, Mar. 16 suddenly at his apartment in Manhattan. Survived by his daughter Mildred L. Oberkotter of Palo Alto, CA; his daughter-in-law, Joyce Oberkotter widow of his son Robert of Newport, NH; three grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. Burial will be private and a memorial service will be scheduled at a later date. For those caring to contribute in his memory it is suggested that they consider the Alexander Graham Bell Assn for the Deaf, 3417 Volta Place, Wash., D.C. 20007; any of the Option Oral Deaf Schools or a charity of their own choosing.
