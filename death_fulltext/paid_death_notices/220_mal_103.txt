
        AKRONGOLD--Rochelle. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rochelle Akrongold, beloved mother of Barry and Bruce Akrongold, cherished friends whose generosity and commitment enriches Jewish life in New York and strengthens our ability to serve those in need. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Barry; to Bruce and his wife, Elisa; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
