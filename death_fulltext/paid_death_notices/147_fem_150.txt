
        ROTHSTEIN-Louise C. Of Brooklyn, NY, died an untimely death in Tampa, FL, on April 24, 1999. Survived by her mother Rose Rothstein, sister and brother-in-law Jane and Jonathan Bell, nephews and niece Stephen, Marc and Jennifer; daughter of the late Ira Rothstein. Served as Deputy Director of Personnel for the Board of Education, City of New York. Funeral service Wednesday, April 28, at 9:30 AM, ''Boulevard-Riverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI.
