
        FIELD-Dorothy M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Dorothy M. Field. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        FIELD-Dorothy M. Age 88, died September 18, 1999, peacefully, in New York City. Beloved wife of Nathaniel Field. Adored mother of Robert Fisher, devoted mother-inlaw of Janet and cherished grandmother of Morgan. Services Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, Tuesday morning. For details, contact chapel.
