
        FRIEDMAN--Jack N. On behalf of the Board of Directors, volunteers, consumers and families of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City, we offer our heartfelt condolences to the family of Jack N. Friedman. Jack was a long time friend and supporter who never said no but gave generously whenever asked. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Lorraine, to his daughter Lynne, sons Mitchell and Freddy, and to the entire Friedman and Hammerschlag families. Martin C. Hausman, President Edward R. Matthews, Executive Director Ronald J. H. Napal, Chief Development Officer
