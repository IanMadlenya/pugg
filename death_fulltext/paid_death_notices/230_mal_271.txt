
        KRONENBERG - Jack. Passed away peacefully on Friday, March 17, 2006 at age 87. Husband for 51 years to the late Evelyn; father of Scott Kronenberg (Cecilia) of Exeter, CA and Noah Kronenberg of North Bergan, NJ; grandfather of Hilary, Louis, Sara and Jeremy; brother of Betty Balsam (Barret) of Pembroke Pines, FL. A native of New York. A graduate from Boys High School and Brooklyn College class of 1939. He was involved in men's clothing manufacturing for 40 years. Funeral Services were held in Santa Rosa, California. Donations may be made to the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society.
