
        FATER-Joseph J. On March 23, 1997, after a long illness. Beloved husband of the late Theresa. Loving father of daughter Patricia and son-in-law James. Cherished grandfather of Dana. He was a graduate of Dartmouth College, Retired Col. Marine Corp., Managing Director for 35 years of the Building Contractor's Association, Inc., Director for 30 years of Brooklyn Federal Savings Bank, Knight of Malta, and Vice President of Cardinal's Committee of the Laity. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be sent to New York Foundling Hospital and Diabetic Research and Education Foundation. Memorial service on Monday, April 7, 1997, at 11 A.M., at St. Joseph's R.C. Church in Bronxille, New York.
