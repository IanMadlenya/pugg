
        BROD--Albert T. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a loyal supporter of Jewish education. He and his wife, Lois, are YU Guardians who established the Albert T. and Lois Brod Scholarship and the Albert T. and Ruth Hagy Brod Scholarship at Yeshiva University. Our heartfelt condolences to Lois and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman Board of Trustees
