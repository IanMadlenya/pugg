
        FELDMAN--Herman. August 14, 1906--February 8, 2006. Spent many rewarding, happy years as Principal of Frederick Douglas and Horace Greeley JHS. Loved by friends, family, teachers, students and staff. Died at home with his family living independently on his own terms.
