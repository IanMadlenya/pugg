
        BLOCK-Harris. The partners and staff of Lipsky, Goodkin & Company, PC, deeply mourn the passing on January 24, 2001 of Harris Block. A valued & respected client for many years, he was admired, honored and held in high esteem by every member of our firm. A man of integrity, highly intelligent, generous, charitable, possessing all the qualities of just being good, he will be missed but always remembered. Our deep condolences to his family.
