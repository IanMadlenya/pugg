
        KUSHNER-Joseph M., 93. Died at his home in Bay Harbor Islands, Florida on August 26th, 2001. Formerly a leading real estate and insurance broker in the Bay Ridge section of Brooklyn, New York. He is survived by his wife of 71 years Lillian; children James and Mary Kushner of Salt Lake City, Utah and Barbara and Samuel Cantor of Provincetown, MA; granddaughters Cathy, Constance, Jane and Nina; great-grandchildren: Sunny, Michael, Gregory and James. Burial services in New York will be private.
