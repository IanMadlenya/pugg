
        GRAU--Katherine Mackay O'Brien. February 21, 1926August 8, 2006, died peacefully on Tuesday, August 8, 2006, in California. Loved and cherished mother of Tara, Alice and Fritz. Grandmother of Cameron, Katherine & Charles Smith and Chancey & Tyler Martin. Daughter of the late Katherine Mackay O'Brien Hawkins and Judge Kenneth J. O'Brien. Sister of the late Marie Louise O'Brien Wiegand and Morgan J. O'Brien. Also survived by her niece Marie Louise Wiegand Williams and nephews George & Michael Wiegand and Morgan & Kenneth O'Brien. Contributions may be made to St. Mary's in the Mountains, PO Box 510, Virginia City, NV 89440, where she will be buried.
