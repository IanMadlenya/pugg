
        FINKLE--Nettie S., passed peacefully on May 1st, 2007, shortly before her 100th birthday. Devoted wife of the late David Finkle. Nettie is survived by her children Marc and Sara Finkle, Ann Miller, Patty and Mark Klein, 14 grandchildren, 27 great grandchildren, two greatgreat-grandchildren. An outstanding member of the Palm Beach community, Nettie served on many local committees and was honored by several charitable organizations. She was dearly loved by her family and by a multitude of friends. Nettie will be missed and remembered for many years to come. Memorial Service to be held Thursday, May 3rd, 11am, Temple Emmanuel, Palm Beach, FL. Interment is private.
