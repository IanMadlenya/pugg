
        PINES-Kermit, M.D. The faculty and staff of Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons and NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague Dr. Kermit Pines, who passed away Sunday, June 10, 2001. A highly regarded educator, researcher, and clinician, Dr. Pines dedicated his entire medical career to Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center and its community, serving both the university and hospital in a variety of appointments for over 40 years. He will be sincerely missed by the students, patients, and colleagues whose lives he enriched through his expertise. We will cherish his memory, and promise to continue the legacy of medical excellence that he embodied. We extend our deepest sympathies to the Pines family. Gerald D.
