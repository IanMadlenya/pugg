
        GENDEBIEN-Albert W. Lafayette College mourns the passing of Albert W. Gendebien, Class of 1934, history professor emeritus and archivist emeritus, on March 8, 2002, at age 88. We wish to express our deepest condolences to his wife, Martha, and family. A memorial service will be held at 2 PM, Saturday, March 16, in Colton Chapel on campus.
