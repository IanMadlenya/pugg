
        WALSH-John Perry. Died on Saturday evening, September 5, after a long and valiant battle with cancer at Memorial Sloan-Kettering in New York. A graduate of Yale, Class of 1950, a Captain in the United States Marine Corps, Mr. Walsh was President in the family apparel business, Florence Walsh, Inc., until his retirement in 1984. Beloved husband of Margo Grant Walsh. He is survived by his sister Elizabeth Walsh Sisk, one nephew and three nieces. Friends may call Tuesday, September 8, from 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Funeral mass will be Wednesday, September 9, 9:30 AM, at St. John the Evangelist Roman Catholic Church at 55th Street and 1st Ave., New York City. Interment will be private in the family plot in St. Joseph's Cemetery, Rhinecliff, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to The Solid Tumor Group, Memorial Sloan-Kettering, in the name of John Perry Walsh.
