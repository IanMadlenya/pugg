
        LEE-David. The Board and staff of the Joint Passover Assoc. of the City of New York mourns the passing of David Lee, beloved spouse of our long time Board member, Gertrude Lee. Gert's devotion and energy was directed toward caring for David in recent years, but she never forgot her ties to JPA and found time to continue her fund raising efforts on behalf of needy Jews. To Gert and to her family, we offer our love and deepest sympathy. Michael Stoler, President
        LEE-David. The Village Temple mourns the passing of its long time member, David Lee. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Gertrude and the entire family. Rabbi Dennis N. Math Beverly Haynes, David R. Marcus, Co-Presidents
