
        TULCHIN--Stanley''Ted''. A man of vision and integrity. Theater producer. Founder of STA Intl; Maidstone Productions; and Continental Bank of Garden City. Brother to Norman and the late Gertrude Rosenfeld. Adored husband of Patsy. Devoted father of Jeffrey, Jill and Steven. Proud grandfather to Gregory, Jessica and Sara. Loved by all who knew him. He will be greatly missed. Memorial service Thursday (12/22) at 10:30 at Community Reform Temple in Westbury, NY. Donations may be made to New Dramatists.
