
        DILWORTH-J. Richardson. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Community Service Society of New York deeply regret the death of their Honorary Life Trustee, J. Richardson Dilworth. In his fourteen years of devoted service to CSS, he held many offices including President and Vice Chairman of the Board. His work on eight board committees illuminated both his fund-raising skills and dedication to improving the lives of poor New Yorkers served by CSS. Our heartfelt condolences to his family and the uncounted friends he helped in a lifetime of philanthropy. David R. Jones, Esq., President Stephen R. Aiello, Ph.D., Chair
