
        WHEELER-Mark. We deeply mourn the untimely death of Mark C. Wheeler, Jr., former member of the Leadership Committee of the Lincoln Center Consolidated Corporate Fund and former Chairman of the Lincoln Center Business Council. Mark provided dynamic leadership to the Council during its critical formative stages. We will miss his vibrant spirit and wise counsel. Our most sincere condolences to Mark's family. Beverly Sills, Chairman, Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts Nathan Leventhal, President, Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts Alair A. Townsend, Chair, Leadership Committee, Consolidated Corporate Fund Donald J. Gibson, Chairman, Lincoln Center Business Council
