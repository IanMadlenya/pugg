
        BECKER-Harriet J. Of New Rochelle, N.Y. On November 18, 1997. She brightened many peoples lives with her caring and compassion. She lived with elegance and dignity. In addition to raising a family, she was active in volunteer work, including fund raising for cancer research, transcribing books into Braille and acting as an advocate for battered women in the courts. She was a wonderful wife, mother, grandmother and friend. Her husband, Howard Becker, preceded her in death, but she will be sorely missed by her son Mark, daughter Susan, son-inlaw Jim, grandchildren Jonathan and Kate and many others. Contributions may be made to Hospice of Boulder County, 2825 Marine Street, Boulder, CO. 80303.
