
        CROSS - Jackson, in his 90th year, passed away in Vero Beach, FL. December 29, 1996. Son of the late Dr. and Mrs. Frank Bethel Cross. Beloved husband of Anne Meyer Cross. Devoted father of Mitzi Scrivan, Susan Benoit and Alice Dorrance. Loving grandfather of Kate, Andrew, Tessa, Cyrus, Sam and Anne. Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY and former resident of Fairfield, CT. Graduate of The University of the South, Class of 1930. Memorial service 4 PM, Friday, Jan 3, 1997, First Presbyterian Church, Vero Beach. In Lieu of flowers contributions in his memory are welcome to The University of the South, Sewanee, TN. 37383.
