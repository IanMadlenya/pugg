
        GELOFSKY-Eve. Beloved wife of the late Abe Gelofsky. Died on November 23, 1997 at the age of 88. She and her husband were the proprietors of Jelof's in Newark and Millburn, NJ. She is survived by her grand nieces Margot and Karen Lovett, both of California. Graveside services will be held at United Hebrew Cemetery, Staten Island on November 25, at 2 PM.
