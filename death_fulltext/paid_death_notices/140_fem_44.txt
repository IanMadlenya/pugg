
        EISENDRATH-Barbara T. Age 75. Died at home, September 7, 1998. Wife of the late David B. Eisendrath. Survived by her brother, Richard Forrest and sister-in-law Judith, their two daughters and two nephews. A true New Yorker, Barbara Eisendrath shared her intellectual enthusiasms and her joie de vivre with many friends, old and new. She will be remembered for her generosity, her wit, her interest in life, and her green thumb. Donations in her memory may be made to the ASPCA.
