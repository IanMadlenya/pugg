
        COHEN--Harris. Of Great Neck, NY, died on the morning of July 13, 2004 at the age of 85. After proudly serving his country in World War II, he began a long and successful career as businessman, CPA, and treasurer. He was a self-made man who attributed his success to hard work, unshakable integrity, loyalty and to his partnership with the love of his life, his wife of 56 years, Adele. He is survived by Adele, his sons Gary and Seth (Lynn), devoted sister Shirley, granddaughters Molly and Emily; nieces Ginny (Morton), Sheila (Bernie), Shanti, and Dr. Mindy; and loving great-nieces and nephews. His love and devotion to his family will be missed but never forgotten. Services Wednesday, 12:30 PM at Temple Emanuel, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck.
