
        McCARTHY -- Lawrence F. The Partners and staff of Lipsky, Goodkin & Company, PC, deeply mourn the passing of Lawrence F. McCarthy, the brother of our esteemed partner, William J. McCarthy. We have great admiration & respect for Bill & his family. Over the years of our association with Bill, we observed the love, warmth, dedication and strong feelings the McCarthy family has for each other. We are moved by their loss & offer our heartfelt condolences to the family of Lawrence F. McCarthy.
