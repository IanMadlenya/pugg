
        TEPPER-Regina, of Yonkers on May 28, 1997. In 1995 her life story was chronicled in the off-Broadway play, ''Coming Through'' which described her experiences at Ellis Island and her ensuing life. Devoted wife of the late Harry Tepper. Loving father of Bobbi and her husband Bruce Fabricant of Ardsley; Roy and his wife Annie Tepper of Greenwich, CT. Dear grandmother of Robin, Vicki, Meri and Karen. Service 10 AM today at Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Avenue (Exit 17 Saw Mill Parkway) Dobbs Ferry, NY. Contributions to the American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Pl., White Plains, NY 10601, appreciated.
