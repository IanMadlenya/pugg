
        ORDOVER-Alfred. 75. Died June 20 at his home in Saugerties, NY. Beloved husband of Mary Neill Ordover, father of Sarah and Andrew Ordover, step-father of Josh and David Touster and Cynthia Hendricks. Loving grandfather of Rachel and August Stolba. Retired Vice President of NBC, an avid and astute collector of modern art, and a dedicated supporter of new artists. A memorial service at the O.K. Harris Gallery is planned for the fall. For information call Mary Neill Ordover 845-247-9312.
