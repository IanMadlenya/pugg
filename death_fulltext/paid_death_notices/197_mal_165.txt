
        BERCIK -- The Honorable Steven J., former Union County, NJ, judge, Waterfront Commissioner for New Jersey and ex-Mayor of Elizbeth, died Saturday, June 14, 2003. He was elected Mayor of Elizabeth from 1956-64 and established the Elizabeth Human Relations Commission. Appointed by Gov. Richard Hughes for the post of New Jersey Waterfront Commissioner of New York Harbor from 1966-71. Appointed in 1972 as judge to the Juvenile and Domestics Court of Union County, NJ, serving as Presiding Judge from 1977-88, when he retired. A member of the National Council of Juvenile Court Judges, NJ State Juvenile Court Judges Association, the Judicial College, the New Jersey State Bar Association, the Union County Bar Association, the Elizabeth Knights of Columbus, the Young Democrats of Union County and the St. Patrick's High Shcool Hall of Fame, Elizabeth, NJ. Surviving are his wife of 60 years, Catherine V. (Clark); father of Steven J. Jr., Dr. Michael, Dr. Robert J., Dr. Kevin, Dr. Richard, Anne Marie Bercik, Ph.D., Patricia Kirkowski, Mary Ellen Bercik-Nies and Jean O'Toole. Also survived by 23 grandchildren. Services will be Wednesday, June 18, at 9:30 AM, from the Leonard Home for Funerals, 242 W. Jersey St, Elizabeth, followed by a 10 AM Funeral Mass at St. Genevieve's R.C. Church, Elizabeth. Interment St. Mary's Cemetery, Berkeley Heights, NJ. Viewing Monday, 7-9 PM, and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Jude's Children's Hospital or St. Patrick's Church, Elizabeth, NJ, would be appreciated. >>AD#
