
        CATTON-Eddie. The Officers and Board of Trustees of the Magen David Yeshivah, Isaac Shalom Elementary School and Celia Esses High School, mourn the untimely passing of Eddie Catton, a devoted, generous and highly respected friend of the Yeshivah. We extend our sincere sympathies to his wife, Frieda, daughters, Rochelle Catton and Melanie Shalam, son, Sammy Catton, parents, Rae and Sam Catton, and brother, Harry Catton. Our thoughts and prayers are with the entire Catton family at this difficult time. Eddie J. Esses Steven D. Shamah Co-Presidents
