
        MURRAY-Nancy Mann. Died peacefully at home in her sleep on September 18, 1999. She is survived by her beloved husband, Edwards B. Murray; two daughters, Bettina Pyles Nelson of NY, and Courtney Pyles Kahn of San Francisco; a grandson, Alexander Pyles Nelson of St. Andrews, Scotland; and a brother, William H. Mann of Richmond, VA. She was the daughter of the late Dr. and Mrs. Herbert Mann of Richmond. A memorial service will be held at St. James' Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue and 71st Street, on Tuesday, September 21, at 2 PM. Burial will be in Richmond at Holly Wood Cemetery on Wednesday. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. James' Church Spring Festival, 865 Madison Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
