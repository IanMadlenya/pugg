Also loved by her six grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. Funeral services Saturday 12 noon at Hanne mann Funeral Home, 88 South Broadway, Nyack. Burial Oak Hill Cemetery, Nyack. Visiting hours Friday at the funeral home at 3-5 and 7-9 P.M.
