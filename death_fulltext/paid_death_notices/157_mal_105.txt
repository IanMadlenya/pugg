
        WASSER-Rabbi Max Raphael. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in 1944, he served several pulpits including congregation B'rith Shalom in Bethlehem and Congregation of Mercy and Truth in Pottstown, PA. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Judith, children, David, Deborah, and Shari, siblings, Rose, Leah and Sam and to all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Seymour L. Essrog, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP The Rabbinical Assembly
