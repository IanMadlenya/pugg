
        MACKAY--Gwen. The members of the Board of Trustees of Old Westbury Gardens express sadness at the loss of our longtime and dear friend, Gwen Mackay, who helped form the Gardens in its earliest years and continued to serve on our Board until her death. We wish to offer sympathy to her family and friends. Peggie P. Boegner, Chairman Emerita Mary S. Phipps, Chairman Carol E. Large, President
