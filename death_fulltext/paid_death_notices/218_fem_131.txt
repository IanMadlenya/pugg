
        CAMPBELL--Mary L., of New York, NY died March 11, 2005. She was a teacher of vocal music, string instruments and orchestra at the Milton School in Rye, NY for 32 years. She was predeceased by her brother, Dr. Ivor E. Campbell of New Albany, OH, and is survived by her sister, Ruth C. Tuomi of Arlington Heights, IL, her brother, Dr. Edwin Campbell of New York, nieces, Dr. Donna T. Beck of Champaign, IL and Rev. Mary T. Hammond of Oberlin, OH, and many great nieces and nephews.
