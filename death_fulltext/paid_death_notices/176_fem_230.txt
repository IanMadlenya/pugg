
        PAYER-Lynn. The Alumni and Faculty of the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism pay tribute to Lynn Payer, Class of 1969, for her journalistic contributions to understanding healthcare and medicine in our society. We will miss her quiet, dedicated, involvement in our lives. Lynn's sister, Cheryl Payer, and friends will gather Saturday, September 29, 48pm, at the home of Barbara Fletcher, 720 West 181 St, Apt 67. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Palisades Nature Association, PO Box 155, Alpine, NJ 07620-0155. Tom Goldstein, Dean
