
        JAROS-Elsie H. Age 92. On March 2, 2000. Loving and beloved wife, mother, grandmother, sister, aunt and great aunt. Elsie was the widow of Murry H. Levine, M.D. She later married John J. Jaros. Her children are Elizabeth Levine Jaros Biers (Martin H. Biers, M.D.) and Edwin J. Jaros (Sheila Greene); grandchildren, Eric L. (Carolyn R.), Carl D. Biers (Nina Browne) and John M. Biers. Cynthia Gordon is her sister as was the late Mildred (Mimi) Falk. Elsie Jaros held a Masters Degree in Sociology from Columbia University Teachers College. She was a brave and loving person and a friend to many. Service Sunday, 1PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        JAROS-Elsie. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Elsie Jaros. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
