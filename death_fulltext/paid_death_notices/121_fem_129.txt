
        CASE-Peggy K., age 73, of N. Branford, CT. Beloved wife of George, died Thurs. Feb. 6 at CT Hospice. Mother of Carole Spadaro and Marcia Case, grandmother of Lisa Gallina, niece of Lorraine Brown, she was a graduate of Rhodes college & had MSW from NYU. Retired psychiatric social worker and accomplished violinist, she was a past president of Junior League of Westchester-on-Hudson, she produced Westchester Heritage map of Indian & colonial settlement and authored definitive monograph on Albert Bierstadt & his Tarrytown estate. Memorial Service 1:30 PM, Saturday, Feb 22, Irvington Presbyterian Church. Memorial contributions may be made to OPUS, 101 N. Central Avenue, Hartsdale, NY 10530.
