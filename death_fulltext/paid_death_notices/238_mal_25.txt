
        GEERTZ--Clifford, 80, died October 30, 2006 of complications following heart surgery. Beloved husband of Karen I. Blu; father of Erika Reading and Benjamin; grandfather of Andrea and Elena. Also survived by his first wife Hildred Geertz. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Institute for Advanced Study, School of Social Science, Einstein Drive, Princeton, NJ 08540. Details of a memorial service at the Institute will be announced.
