
        BERNSTEIN - William. Deepest sympathy to the Bernstein family. A Silver Founder with his late wife Berta, William raised his children and lived his life according to the highest values of Jewish tradition and ethics. May the children, Dr. Celia Bernstein Waldman, Nathan Bernstein, Evelyn Gross & Henny Brener, a member of the International Board of Governors, be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem, along with the entire family. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med. Cntr. in Jerusalem
        BERNSTEIN-William. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the father of its devoted members Evelyn Gross and Dr. Celia Waldman. May his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
