
        WHITEHEAD-Edward R., Jr., 88, of West Allenhurst, NJ died Dec. 12, 2000. He was the sixth oldest and 60 year member of the NY Stock Exchange. He graduated Horace Mann Prep School & was a 1935 graduate of Brown University. He was a member of Sigma Chi and an award-winnng athlete. Surviving are two daughters Dee Ann McCarthy & Nancy Zielinski, three granddaughters Holly, Vanessa and Veronica. Services will be in the Buckley Funeral Home, 509 Second Ave., Asbury Park, NJ on Saturday at 12 noon.
