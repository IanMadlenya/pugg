
        MARTOCCIA-Carmela R. (nee Neglia) Age 89. Devoted wife of the late Arthur R. Martoccia, on February 20, 2000. Dear mother of Linda Centurrino and Elaine Pantaleo. Cherished mother-in-law of Robert Centurrino and Robert Pantaleo. Loving grandmother of Brian, Laura, Janine and Kevin. Caring sister of Josephine Neglia. Former department store buyer, dedicated volunteer at Grace Church, Grace Church School and New York Eye and Ear Hospital. She will forever be missed by family and friends. Vistitors may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., Thursday 2-4pm & 7-9pm. Services at Grace Church, Broadway at 10th St., Friday 11am. Entombment Woodlawn Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Melissa Neier Memorial Fund, P.O. Box 333, Montville, N.J. 07045.
