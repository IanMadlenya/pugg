
        ADLER -- Goldie. Loving mother to Eddie. Beloved and devoted sister and sister-inlaw to Jean and Eugen Gluck. Cherished aunt and greataunt. Admired and respected by hundreds of employees at E. Gluck Corporation. From the ashes of the Holocaust, Goldie created a beautiful life filled with warmth and dignity. She loved to give to charity and was a staunch supporter of many just causes for Israel and the United States, appearing at countless rallies to make her causes and opinions heard. She often pushed others to do more and get involved. Goldie Adler set a standard that future generations can aspire to. She will be sorely missed by all whose lives she touched. Funeral services are Monday, January 5, 10:30 am, at the Parkside Funeral Home in Rego Park, Queens.
