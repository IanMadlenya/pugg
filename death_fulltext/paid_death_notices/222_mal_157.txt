
        THAL--Rudolf. Age 83. Of Englewood Cliffs, N.J. and formerly of Bernkastel-Kues, Germany, on July 14, 2005. Loving and devoted husband of Ruth Thal. Dear brother of Hilda Herbst of Boca Raton, FL. Beloved father and father-in-law to Susan Thal and Elliot of Teaneck, N.J. and Gary Thal and Kathy Hoffstedder of Demarest, N.J. Adoring and doting grandfather to Joshua, Gabrielle, Adam, David and Jonathon. Loving uncle to Leonard and Roberta Herbst of Pikesville, MD.and Alwin and Heidi Herbst of Boca Raton, FL., and cherished by a host of relations and friends. Rudy and his family escaped from Nazi Germany to become a successful entrepreneur in the United States, which he was also most proud to serve in the U.S. Army during WWII. Co-founder of E&T Plastic Mfg., Co., Inc., in Long Island City, his many accomplishments in business over the past 50 years are non the less surpassed by the deep and everlasting love of his family and friends and the admiration, esteem and respect of his business associates. We will always remember and dearly miss his passion and boundless enthusiasm for life. Funeral service Sunday, July 17th, 11:30AM at GuttermanMusicant, 402 Park St., Hackensack, N.J. In lieu of flowers, the family request that contributions be made in Rudy's memory to UJA Federation of Northern N.J., P.O. Box 4176, River Edge, N.J. 07661.
