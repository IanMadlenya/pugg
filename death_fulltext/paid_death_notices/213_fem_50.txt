
        MOROSANI -- Virginia War rington. 93 widow of Remy Edmond Morosani, died at her home in Litchfield, CT., Friday, October 1, 2004. She is survived by four children, George W. Morosani, Reto W. Morosani, Roopa Morosani, and John W. Morosani; eleven grandchildren, and eleven great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by two daughters, Mrs. Virginia M. Smith, Dr. Francesca M. Thompson, and a stepson, Remy E. Morosani. A funeral service will be held on Saturday, October 16, 2004 at 11 AM, St. Michael's Church, Litchfield, CT.
