
        MULLAN--William Richard, 70, died on February 5th in Tucson, AZ. A former advertising executive with Ted Bates & Co. and Ally & Gargano, his warmth and provocative humor continue to live in the memories of those who knew him. Despite many personal struggles, he generously and indiscriminately reached out to help others. He is survived by his brother Robert Mullan; his daughter Alexandra Jain; his son Peter Mullan; his grandchildren, Nicolas and Katherine Jain; and his friends. Memorial service will be held Tuesday, February 22, at 2:00 at St. James Church, 865 Madison Ave, NYC. Donations in his memory may be made to The Doe Fund, 232 East 84th St., NYC 10028.
