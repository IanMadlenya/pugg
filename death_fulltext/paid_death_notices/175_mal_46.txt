
        EDELSTEIN-Herman. In Montreal, on Wednesday, August 1, 2001 after a prolonged illness. Beloved husband of the late Esther, cherished father of Dr. Martin of Great Neck and Leonard of Montreal. Devoted father-in-law of Judy and Debbie and grandfather to Jeremy, Joshua, Justin, Jonathan and Andrea. His warmth, charm and love of life will be missed by all who knew him.
