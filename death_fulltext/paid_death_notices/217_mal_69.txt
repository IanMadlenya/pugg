
        PRENNER--Edward J., M.D. Died January 30, 2005 after a long battle with cancer. Services were held for the family on February 1st in Forest Hills where he practiced as an Ophthalmologist for many years. He will be sorely missed by his family and his friends. He was a devoted father to his daughters Emily and Elizabeth, and his grandchildren Simon, Caitlin, Alex and Olivia. He is also survived by his former wife and the mother of his children, Ruth Heiferman Prenner. Contributions in his memory can be made to The Pancreatic Research Fund, Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, P.O. Box 27106, NYC 10087
