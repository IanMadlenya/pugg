
        MODELL-Jill S. On July 25, 2000 at age 55, after a courageous struggle, from breast cancer. Passed away peacefully at home with her family at her side. Her tireless determination, optimism, love of people and life will be with us always. She is survived by her husband Jerry of 34 years, her mother Arleen Meyers, her children Jonathan and Samantha, her brothers Michael and Donald Ostrower. Founder and President of Fiber-Seal of Long Island. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Susan G. Komen New York Race for the Cure, 341 W. 38 St., 10th Flr, N.Y., N.Y. 10018. Funeral services will be held at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Tpk, Woodbury, L.I. Friday, July 28, 2000 at 12 Noon.
        MODELL-Jill. Our very dearest lifelong friend. You were a woman of courage, style, humor and determination. We love you sweetheart and will miss you more than imaginable. We join with Jerry, Samantha and Jonathan, and the rest of your family and your huge circle of friends in mourning the loss of a most special person. Bon and Ba
