
        GOLDMAN--Ira J. Age 82. Beloved husband for 62 years of Marylin, father of Alan, grandfather of Melissa Oldman, proud great-grandfather of Lucas and Aurora, dear brother to Lowell and Linda Grossman. A Marine who served in World War II and the Korean conflict, he was owner and president of A-A Ambulance & Oxygen Co. in The Bronx. Graveside service Friday at 11AM at King David Memorial Gardens, Shrub Oak, NY. In lieu of flowers please send a memorial contribution to The Kiwanis Club of Greenacres City of Florida (club key K12656), or American Heart Association. Shiva observed at the home of Alan Goldman.
