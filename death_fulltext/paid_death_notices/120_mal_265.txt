
        POMERANZ-Jonathan N. Husband of Marilyn, father of Jacques and Frank. Grandfather of Jeffrey and Sharon. Died on January 21, 1997 and was 88 years old. Funeral Services Friday, Jan. 24, 11 AM, Reconstructionist Synagogue of the North Shore, Plandome, N.Y. Nat lived in Hollywood, Fla. after moving from Baldwin, NY. He was an active member of the Baldwin Jewish Center. He was a Vice President of Roberts Company, a textile machinery manufacturer headquartered in Sanford, NC. He led a full life, was loved, and will be missed greatly.
