
        MEREDITH-Morley. Age 77, Metropolitan Opera baritone for over 30 years, formerly of Scarsdale, NY died of cancer at Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center (Florida) on February 3, 2000. Born in Winnipeg, Canada, graduated from University of Manitoba before serving in the Canadian Navy. Preceded in death by Erlene, his wife of 48 years. Survived by his sister, Cecille Feldman, and her husband, Herman, of Minneapolis, MN, his aunt, and many loving nieces, nephews, cousins and friends. Burial private. Memorial service at a later date.
        MEREDITH-Morley. We mourn the loss of our dear friend whose glorious baritone voice brightened our lives from the stage of the Metropolitan Opera to many happy family gatherings at home. We shall miss his humor, his great presence and his beautiful music. Eugene & Emily Grant & Family
