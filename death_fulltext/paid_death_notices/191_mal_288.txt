
        SACKS-Roger, D.D.S. A man of honor, great courage and dignity. He was a graduate of Stuyvesant High School 1954, Columbia College 1958, and Columbia College of Dentistry 1962. He was a published cartoonist who brought his wit with him wherever he went. He leaves his beloved wife Arlene, his cherished son Lt. Philip Sacks, his wonderful nephew Ethan (Masako) Sacks. Loving brother of the late Dr. Elliott Sacks. Dear son of the late Philip and Naomi Sacks.
