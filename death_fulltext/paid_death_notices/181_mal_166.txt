
        SINDEBAND-Seymour J. On February 1, 2002. Age 85. Of Pound Ridge, N.Y. Husband of the late Elizabeth Bennett. Survived by son Markham L., daughter-in-law Jacqueline, daughter Caroline Ricker, brother Allan L., eight grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Veteran U.S. Navy, 6 years of active service, W.W. II. An engineer, inventor and entrepreneur. He was a distinguished Engineering graduate of Columbia University and winner of the Egelston Medal for Distinguished Engineering Achievements and the Columbia University John Jay Award for Distinguished Professional Achievement. His family and friends hearts are filled with pride, and all whose paths have crossed with his feel privileged to have known him. Service will be held at the Pound Ridge Community Church, 3 Pound Ridge Road, Pound Ridge, N.Y. 2 PM, Saturday, February 16th. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society in his name.
