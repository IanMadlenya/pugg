
        SCHMIER-Ann. Died Thursday May 8, of natural causes at the age of 89. She was the daughter of Asaac and Rose Revkin Mintzer. Mrs. Schmier was a principal in the NYC school system. She is survived by a brother, George Mintzer of San Francisco, her daughter, Frances Lourie of Stamford, CT., and two grandchildren. She was predeceased by her husband Maurice Schmier, a chiropractor.
