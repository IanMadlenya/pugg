        $(6$)VIETOR--Anna Glen Butler (Mrs. Alexander Orr Vietor). The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society records with sorrow the death of Anna Glen Butler Vietor on December 20, 2005. A member of the Society since 1986, Mrs. Vietor was a Life Member and Trustee Emerita of the Society. She served with distinction on the Board of Trustees from 1987-1996 and was elected a Fellow of the Society, F.G.B.S., in 1993. Mrs. Vietor was a generous benefactor of the Society. Through her efforts, the former Auditorium was renovated to provide the Society with a multi-purpose event space. Henry C.B. Lindh Chairman of the Board William Potter Johns President
        $(6$)VIETOR--Anna Glen Butler. Society of Daughters of Holland Dames express our deep sorrow at the death of our very dear friend, member and past Directress General, Mrs. Alexander O. Vietor. Anna Glen was our Directress General from 1996 through 2001. Her abundant kindness, integrity, and gracious elegance set a standard for our Society. We will greatly miss Anna Glen.
