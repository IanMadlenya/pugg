
        WALKER--Fanny. At 97. January 12, in Manhattan. Beloved mother of Judith Walsh; mother-in-law of Jeremy Walsh; grandmother of Jonathan and Nathaniel, dear sister of the late Tess Blay, Anne Murray and Al Schuss; sister-in-law of Ira Murray, aunt and great aunt of many deeply devoted nephews and nieces. Fanny's intelligence, wit, zest for life and love of family and friends was an inspiration to all who knew her.
