
        MORAN-Frank R., 84. On June 27, 2002, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Ridgewood, NJ. Beloved husband of Joan and loving father of Joan Trout, Frank Jr., Douglas, Marybeth Wooters, Paul, and Thomas. Adoring grandfather of 15. Born in Brooklyn, NY, loyal Alumnus of Fordham University, most honorably served as an officer in the U.S. Navy during W.W. II. In 1956 he founded Moran Shipping Corp. and remained president until his retirement in 1986. He was an avid golfer and member of Ridgewood Country Club and Arcola Country Club. The family will receive callers at Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Ave, Ridgewood, NJ from 2PM-4PM & 7PM-9PM Sunday, June 30. A Funeral Mass will take place at 1PM on July 1 at Mt. Carmel Church, Ridgewood, NJ, to celebrate his life. Interment to follow at St. Luke's Cemetery, Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ. Donations in his memory may be made to St. Vincent Ferrer School, Delray Beach, FL. 33483.
