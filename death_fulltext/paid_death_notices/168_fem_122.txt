
        CAHN-Nettie. Of New York City and Palm Beach, Florida. Formerly of Great Neck, NY on January 9, 2001. Treasured wife of the late Ernest Cahn. Adored mother of Carol Weltz and Mitchel and Lesley Cahn. Loving grandmother of Catherine Weltz, Heather Solomon and Blaire Cahn. Beloved sister of Shirley and Kermit Gordon, the late Miriam Pearlman, and sisterin-law of Eva Kahn. Nettie was a life member of the Women's Division of the American Society for Technion, helping to establish the Nettie Cahn Scholarship as well as the Pearl S. Milch Chair in Biomedical Engineering and Life Sciences. She was also a life member of the National Women's Committee of Brandeis University. She was an active volunteer at the Guild for the Blind and especially enjoyed participating in their annual Posh Sale. In addition to a wonderful sense of humor she was a lover of beauty and style. She was devoted to theater, ballet, opera, travel and her family. She was the quintessential New Yorker and she absolutely adored Central Park. Nettie was very loved and will be deeply missed. Service Sunday, 9:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County and Technion University.
        CAHN-Nettie. In loving memory of our Nana. So many beautiful memories to cherish. We love you, you will always remain in our hearts. Carol, Catherine & Gautier
