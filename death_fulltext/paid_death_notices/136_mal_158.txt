
        SCHEEL-Ted. Caricaturist-Cartoonist, Author, former member St. Louis Cycling Club. On May 8, 1998. Age 99. In New York City. Adored husband of Lita, beloved father of Eugene and family. Cherished uncle of Tinker. His wit, gentleness, modesty and encouragement of the talents of others will forever enrich the lives of those he touched.
