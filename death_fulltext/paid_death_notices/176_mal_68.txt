
        BOOZAN-Charles W., M.D. Age 82. Died peacefully at home in Sea Girt, N.J. on September 5, 2001. A graduate of Princeton University and Cornell Medical College, he practiced ophthalmology for 40 years in Elizabeth, N.J. He was an inspiration to his three sons who all attended Princeton and became physicians. He is survived by his wife Mona, sons William, John and James, sister Doris Lynch and seven adoring grandchildren. A funeral mass will be said Saturday, September 8th 9:30AM at Saint Mark's Church in Sea Girt, N.J.
