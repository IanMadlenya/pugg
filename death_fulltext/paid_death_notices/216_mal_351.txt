
        STOKES--Peter, M.D. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Dr. Peter Stokes, professor emeritus in psychiatry at Weill Cornell Medical College, where he also received his medical degree. A pioneer in the field of psychopharmacology, he was a highly revered clinician at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital/Weill Cornell Medical Center. Dr. Stokes was known for his extraordinary grace and depth-of-knowledge. We will miss his uncommon intellect and friendship. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire Stokes family.
