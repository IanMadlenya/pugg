
        JONES--Thomas W. CSP. Age 51, died Tuesday, January 16, in Los Angeles. Father Jones was ordained in 1982 at St. Paul the Apostle Church in New York City. Fr. Jones served at St. Nicholas Parish in North Pole Alaska, as UCLA director of campus ministry and as the Paulist Fathers First Consultor and Vice President of the community. His last assignment was as pastor of St. Paul the Apostle Church in Los Angeles. Viewing will be at St. Paul the Apostle Church, 10750 Ohio Ave, in Los Angeles, Monday, January 22, 3:00pm to 7:30pm; Tuesday, January 23, 3:00pm to 7:15pm; with Mass of Christian Burial following at 7:30pm. Burial will be at Holy Cross Cemetery in Culver City, CA, at 10:30am, Wednesday, January 24.
