
        FALLON-Robert R. on April 11, 1997. Educated Michigan State University, Cranbrook Bloomfield Hills, MI, Art Student's League. Served in Submarine Service. Career began J.L. Hudson, later graphic designer for Vogue, Sperry Hutchinson, IBM, American Express. A prolific painter, his work appeared in New York and East Hampton. The world will be a little less without him.
