
        KELLEY-Florence M. Prominent retired New York City Judge. May 3, 1997, age 85. Of Little Compton, RI, formerly Manhattan, New York City, NY. Wife of David S. Worgan, retired Executive Assistant D.A. of New York County. Sister of Augustus M. Kelley and Nicholas Kelley, Jr. Her funeral services and burial will be private. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Little Compton Nursing Association, P.O. Box 545, Little Compton, RI 02837.
