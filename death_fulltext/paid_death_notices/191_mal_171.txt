
        RUDINGER-Ernest. Age 90 of Ithaca formerly of Armonk New York. Passed away peacefully on Tuesday, November 26, 2002. Born in Vienna, Austria. Emigrated to USA in 1938. Was in the 10th Mountain Division of the US Army. In 1946 he married Gerda Knegtmans of the Netherlands. Memorial Service January 3, 2003 in Ithaca NY. For information call his daughter Elizabeth Salon at 607-277-2201.
