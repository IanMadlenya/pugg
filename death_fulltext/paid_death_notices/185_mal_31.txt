
        DEROW-Dr. Joshua Reynolds. Born November 3, 1911, New York City, died June 1, 2002, Hastingson-Hudson, in his home. Survived by his wife Dr. Elisabeth F. Derow-Turnauer, his daughter Deborah Bruenell (Craig), grandsons Brad and Saul Bruenell, brother Bernard S. Derow, and nieces, a nephew and many cousins. Graduated from Boston University Medical College 1937, Colonel U.S. Army Retired, Brooklyn V.A. Department of Radiology 1956-1972. Memorial serice to be announced.
