
        FANNING--Margaret Frueauff, actress and director, known on the Broadway Stage as Margaret Perry. Daughter of Antoinette Perry and Frank Frueauff. Born in Denver in 1913 she passed away at home in Colorado at age 94. Survived by sons and daughter and numerous grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. She had a great run and will be deeply missed. Cards only, care of Salt Works Ranch, P.O. Box 26, Hartsel, CO, 80449.
