
        KRAININ-David A., 89, died Sat., July 12, Boca Raton, FL. Beloved husband, father and brother, he is survived by his wife of sixty-three years, Anne Wineblatt Krainin, sons Julian & Lewis and families, and brother Dr. Philip Krainin. Memorial service 10 A.M., Sunday, July 20, at Temple Beth El, Boca Raton; interment September 7, at Hillbrook Acres, Naples, Maine.
