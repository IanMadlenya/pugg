
        WILSON--Derek R. Beloved son and brother, died after a seven year battle with cancer on May 23, 2005 at his Gretna, LA home. He was 38 years old. He was a graduate of St. Stanislaus High School in Bay St. Louis, MS and received his degree from the University of Mississippi in Oxford, MS. He resided in Pass Christian, MS where he owned a small business prior to his illness. Derek loved his family, fishing, his friends and the New Orleans Saints. Mr. Wilson was a member of Kappa Alpha Fraternity and Pass Road Baptist Church in Gulfport, MS. Throughout his illness, Derek continued to counsel and help others who shared his rare disease. Survivors include his parents, A.W. (Buddy) and Joy Wilson of Gretna, LA and a sister, Cynthia (Missy) Wilson, of New York, NY and his dog Zoe. He was preceded in death by his grandparents, Alfred and Geneva Bendily and Lee Roy and Cecil Forehand Wilson. Relatives and friends are invited to attend the Funeral Service at Lake Lawn Metairie Funeral Home, 5100 Pontchartrain Blvd. (in Metairie Cemetery) on Thursday, May 26, 2005 at 2 P.M. Interment will follow in All Saints Mausoleum in Metairie Cemetery. Visitation will be at 12:30 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Survivor's Retreat Inc., c/o Dr. Gurtler, 3939 Houma Blvd., Ste. 6, Metairie, LA 70006.
