
        APAT--Lillian (nee Packer). On January 19, 2005 at the age of 97. Beloved mother of Claire A. Simkins and Lewis Apat. Also survived by her daughter-in-law Ann; four grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Pre-deceased by her devoted husband Abraham and eldest grandson. Graveside services Monday January 24, 2005, 11AM, Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, N.Y. Donations to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
