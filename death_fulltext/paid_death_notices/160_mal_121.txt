
        FITZPATRICK-Eugene Vincent. Age 73, of Jacksonville, FL, died peacefully in his sleep at home on Monday, May 8, 2000. He leaves Mildred Libath Fitzpatrick, his dear wife and best friend of 50 years; his six devoted children and their spouses and 12 adoring grandchildren. A native New Yorker, Gene Fitzpatrick retired from CSX Corp. in Jacksonville, FL after a long and dedicated career. He was the President of the New York Traffic Club in 1980. The family will be receiving friends at Lieber Funeral Home in Valley Stream, on Thursday, May 11, from 7:00-9:00 PM. On Friday, May 12 at 9:45 AM, Father Tim Hirten will say the Funeral Mass at Holy Name of Mary Church in Valley Stream where Gene and Mildred were married 50 years ago.
