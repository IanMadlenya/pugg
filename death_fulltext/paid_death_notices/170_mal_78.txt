
        KOHL-Paul. Loving husband of the late Rhoda Kohl. Father of Ken and Vicki. Proud grandfather of Lindsay, Stacey and Spencer and father-in-law of Myrna and Peter. Died as a result of a stroke at Palm Beach Gardens, Florida on March 6, 2001. Mr. Kohl spent almost 50 years as a general contractor in New York managing the family operated Herbert Construction Company. As a member of the ''Greatest Generation'', Master Sargent Kohl served proudly in the U.S. Army, participating in the envasion and liberation of France. Mr, Kohl's charitable works included support of numerous organizations serving the Jewish and general commmunities. Funeral services will be held at Gutterman's Funeral Chapel, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, Long Island Friday March 9 at 10AM. Interment at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pine Lawm, Long Iland. In lieu of flowers, a donation to a charity of your choice would be appreciated.
