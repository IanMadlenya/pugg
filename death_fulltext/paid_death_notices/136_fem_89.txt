
        RAINES-Barbara G., PhD. Died peacefully on May 14 at the age of 89. She is survived by her two children Martha Raines Burt and Richard C. Raines, her loving sister Mildred (Mimi) Adler, and her brother Bernard Goldberg. Dr. Raines served as the first woman appointed Leader by the Society for Ethical Culture in the 20th Century and helped to found several Societies for Ethical Culture in California. She was also Professor Emeritus at Antioch College, where she taught at its Columbia, MD. campus until her retirement. A memorial service will be held June 7, 1998 at 3PM at the Brooklyn Society for Ethical Culture, 53 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn.
