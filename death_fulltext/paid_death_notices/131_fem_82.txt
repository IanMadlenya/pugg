
        ROSE-Faye. December 4, 1901December 7, 1997. Faye was loved by everyone for her warm generous personality. She was a musical talent who loved to entertain. We will all miss her. Beloved wife of the late Philip. Devoted mother of Allan and Jeanette Zimmerman. Dear mother-in-law of Howard Zimmerman. Loving sister of Hyman and Morris Schneider and Esther Davis. Cherished grandmother of Briana, Patricia, Marisa, David, Jonathan, David and two great-grandchildren. Also survived by nieces and nephews. Services Tuesday 10:45AM, ''The Riverside'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
