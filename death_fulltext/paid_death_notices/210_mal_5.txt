
        KETTANEH--C. Anthony. A private investor and international businessman, died of heart failure in Geneva, Switzerland on June 28, 2004 at the age of 67. He is survived by his wife Ruth, his mother Mary, his daughter Leigh and granddaughter Michele as well as his sisters Joyce Longworth and Lorna Kettaneh and a brother Edward. He also leaves numerous nieces and cousins. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. on Friday July 2 at 3:30PM. Family will be receiving friends at the Chapel on Friday at 2:30PM. Donations in his honor may be made to the National Kidney Foundation, 30 East 33 St, Suite 1100, NY, NY 10016 or www.kidney.org
