
        STAPLETON -- Margaret Alice. A Memorial Service will be held for Alice, age 76, widow of Lawrence V. Stapleton, both longtime residents of NYC, Quogue and Westhampton. Alice was born in NYC on December 2, 1928. She had begun her career as a dancer in both Las Vegas and NYC; with her husband, co-founded the Mallet Club in Westhampton Beach before moving to Jeddah and Madrid with Larry, an advertising and airline executive. After 20 years abroad they retired to Quogue where Alice was a library volunteer. Alice, most recently of Mystic, CT, died on August 15th. There are no immediate survivors. A Memorial will be held at St. Mark's Church on Main Street in Westhampton Beach, NY on October 25th at 1 PM (631-288-2111).
