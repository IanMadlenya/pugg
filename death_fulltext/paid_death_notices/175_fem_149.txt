
        STUYCK-Alida J. 94, formerly of Stamford, CT, died August 18, 2001. She was predeceased by her husband George, a son Jan-Pieter, a brother Pieter Groen, and a sister Johanna Groen. She leaves two daughters, Johanna S. Gould and L. Marietta Avery, a son Joris, seven grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Funeral arrangements are private. Memorial contributions may be made to the U.S. Fund for UNICEF, attn: Tribute Program, 333 E. 38th St, NYC 10016.
