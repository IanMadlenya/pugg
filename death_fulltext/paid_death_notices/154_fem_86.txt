
        PINCHAS-Marcia Cooper. on November 11, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Raphael, devoted mother of Daniel and David, loving grandmother of Marisa. She was a United Nations staff member for 35 years. She will be deeply missed by all whose lives she touched. Service today at 9:30AM at the ''Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Leukemia Society of America.
        PINCHAS-Marcia C. The Sisterhood of the Spanish and Portuguese Synagogue mourns the loss of our devoted Vice President and Recording Secretary and extends condolences to her bereaved family. Carla Schein, President
        PINCHAS-Marcia C. Congregation Shearith Israel, the Spanish and Portuguese Synagogue, mourns the loss of its devoted member and extends its deepest sympathy to her family. Alvin Deutsch, Parnas
