
        KELLOGG-Marguerite Adelaide Graham. Passed from this world on December 30, 2001, at age 83. A memorial will be held in the coming months. Widow of William Bradford Kellogg, she is mourned by her daughters, Ellen KelloggEvans and Riley Kellogg, her sisters, Louise Cook and Ruth Macleay, her nieces, nephews, cousins and friends. Poet, mother, friend, teacher, sister, wife, daughter, aunt, lover of learning, her light brightened each life it touched. Sing now, Mama, and together we will try to carry your beacon.
