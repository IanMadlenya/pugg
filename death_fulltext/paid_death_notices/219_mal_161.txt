
        MASRI -- Saleh. American Friends of The Hebrew University and The Hebrew University of Jerusalem mourn the loss of Saleh Masri, a Guardian of the Hebrew University and a dedicated friend of Israel and its people. His years of leadership and support provided vital scholarships for deserving Hebrew University students and created an enduring legacy. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. AFHU George A. Schieren President Ira Lee Sorkin Chairman of the Board Peter T. Willner National Executive Director The Hebrew University Prof. Menachem Magidor President
