
        ALDRICH-Susan Cutler. Of Barrytown, NY, died at home at Rokeby, September 9, 1998, in her 85th year. Widow of Richard Chanler Aldrich, daughter of the late John Wilson and Rosalind Fish Cutler. Survived by her children Richard and John Winthrop Aldrich, both of Barrytown, NY, and Rosalind Michahelles of Cambridge, MA; five beloved granddaughters; her brother John Wilson Cutler; and her sisters Patricia Rosalind Warner and Judith Bayard Shinkle. The funeral will be held at the Church of St John the Evangelist, Barrytown, New York at twelve noon, on Monday, September 14th. In place of flowers please make donations to a local animal shelter or a charity of your choice.
