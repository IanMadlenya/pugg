
        MELNIKER--S. William. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of it's longtime member, S. William Melniker. We extend heart felt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Howard F. Sharfstein, President
        MELNIKER--S. William, passed away after a long illness. Beloved brother of Dorothy Adlman, twin brother of Harry Melniker and brotherin-law of Margot. Cherished uncle of Linda Snyder, Jeffrey Adlman, Jill and Larry Melniker. Devoted granduncle and great-granduncle. Bill spent his life in the furniture industry. He was a great lover of music and enjoyed playing the organ and piano and was a devotee of the opera. A life-long mason, he was a Right Worshipful and achieved the 32nd degree. He was also an active Shriner. In his retirement, he gave much time to cheritable endeavours and was Chairman of the Masonic Brotherhood Fund. Services will be held Thursday, February 22 at 1pm at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Masonic Brotherhood Fund 71 W 23rd St, room 1629, New York, NY 10001 or your favorite charity would be appreciated.
        MELNIKER--S.William. The Board of Directors and the entire ''family'' of The New York Design Center f/k/a The New York Furniture Exchange mourn the passing of Bill Melniker, long a fixture in the Furniture Industry and our community. He will be greatly missed. Our condolences to his brother Harry and the rest of the family. Jim Druckman, President Dan Farr, Managing Director
