
        MEYERHOFF-Inge (nee Zuber). Wife of Erich, mother of Tina & Claude, grandmother of Aviva and Yardena, sister of Ines and Anke, cousin of John Eden, MD, stepdaughter of Christiana, and kind friend to many, died suddenly of cardiac arrest on Sept. 9, 2002. She had many talents, and always kept her good heart, even in the face of difficulties. She was beloved by all who knew her.
