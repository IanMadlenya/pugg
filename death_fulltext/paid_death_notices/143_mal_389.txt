
        SATOW-Jed. On December 29, 1998. Beloved son of Phillip and Donna Satow. Loving brother of Michael and Julie, and sister-inlaw Barbara. Dear uncle of Max. Cherished grandson of Shirley Pechter & Sarah Satow. Services Thursday, 11:30 A.M., at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        SATOW-Jed. The Columbia College Alumni Association extends its deepest sympathy to our president, Phillip M. Satow and his family on the loss of son Jed David. To his parents Phillip and Donna, his sister Julie, his brother Michael, his sister-in-law Barbara Marden, his nephew Max, his grandmother Sylvia Satow and the Pechter family, we pray that you receive comfort and peace at this anguished moment of your lives. Contributions may be made to The Hun School of Princeton, 176 Edgerstoune Road, Princeton, New Jersey 08540. Your Friends & Colleagues at the Columbia College Alumni Association
        SATOW-Jed. Central Synagogue notes with profound sorrow the tremendous loss suffered by our dear members, the Satow family. We extend heartfelt sympathy and prayers to Jed's parents, Donna and Phillip, and to his brother and sister, Michael and Julie. May his light continue to shine and his memory forever be a blessing. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Martin I. Klein, President
