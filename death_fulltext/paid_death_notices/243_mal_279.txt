
        SMITH--Frank. The East End Hospice Board of Directors, staff and volunteers note with great sadness the death of Frank Smith. Frank was a loyal supporter and a devoted and valued friend. We are truly grateful to have had his gentle counsel. We extend our deepest sympathy to his partner, East End Hospice Board Member Emeritus Norman Leavitt and his many friends. We shall miss him greatly. Priscilla Ruffin, Pres. & CEO Michael Pitcher, Chairman, Board of Directors East End Hospice, Westhampton Beach, NY $(6$)SMITH--Frank, died peacefully at home, age 80 on April 23, 2007. He was born in New Orleans and grew up in Hialeah, FL. Frank served in the Air Force in WWII. He graduated from Pratt Institute after studying fashion design. For 32 years, he was the chief designer for Evan Picone. After retiring, he devoted his energy and imagination to his beautiful garden in Remsenburg, NY. He is remembered by his many friends and his partner, Norman Leavitt.
