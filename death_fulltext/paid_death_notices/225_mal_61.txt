
        RUANE--William J. The New School, including Mannes and Lang Colleges, deeply mourn the passing of BillRuane, a most caring friend who combined deep understanding of our missions with extraordinary generosity. Memories of his charm, elegance, and wit will warm our lives for a longtime to come. We extend our deepest sympathies to his widow, Joy, and to his family. Philip Scaturro, Chair, Board of Trustees Bob Kerrey, President, The New School Joel Lester, Dean, Mannes College The New School for Music Jonathan Veitch, Dean, Eugene Lang College The New School for Liberal Arts
