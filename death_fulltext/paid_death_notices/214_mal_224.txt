
        LEVINE--Irving H. Congregation Rodeph Sholom is deeply saddened by the death of Irving H. Levine. Irving was a devoted husband to Lillian and beloved father of Robert N. Levine, our Senior Rabbi, and Scott and Sheree Levine. He is also survived by his daughters-in-law, Gina Stahl Levine and Alice Geller, his brother Aaron and sister Charlotte Rome and his seven grandchildren, Judah, Ezra, Maya, Kate, Sarah, William and David. The funeral will be held in Springfield, MA on November 24 at 1PM at the Harold R. Ascher & Son Memorial Chapel. There will be a memorial service at Rodeph Sholom on Wednesday, December 1 at 5:45PM. We extend our deepest condolences to the entire family. Clergy, Board of Trustees, Staff and Members of Congregation Rodeph Sholom
