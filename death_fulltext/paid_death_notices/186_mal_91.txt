
        WAREHAM-Simon. 82, a longtime resident of the Hudson River Valley and decorated WWII Naval Officer, died July 2, after a long battle with myasthenia gravis. Predeceased by his wife of 48 years, Barbara. Survived by second wife, Joan Searle, daughter Barbara Kavanagh of Greenwich, CT, son Raymond of Manhattan, and six granddaughters. Services Wednesday, July 10, 10 AM, St. Mary's Episcopal Church, Scarborough, NY. Donations to the Myasthenia Gravis Foundation welcome in lieu of flowers, 5841 Cedar Lake Road, Suite 204, Minneapolis, MN 55416. His guidance, love, humor and strength will be missed forever.
