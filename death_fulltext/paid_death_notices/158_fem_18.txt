
        EDMISTON-Essie Green, art dealer to premier Black artists, and founding owner of Essie Green Galleries in Harlem, NY, on February 26, 2000. She leaves her loving husband, Sherman K. Edmiston, Jr., her devoted mother, Mary L. Green, two stepchildren, three grandchildren, seven sisters and brothers and other relatives. A memorial service will be held Saturday, March 4, 7 PM at the SGI Culture Center, 7 E 15 St. Man.
