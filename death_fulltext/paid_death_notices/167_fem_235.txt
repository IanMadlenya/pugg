
        PERLMAN-Rosalind. On December 25, 2000. Of East Hampton and New York City. Widow of the late Milton Perlman. Mother of Susan Ann Perlman of Paris, France, and Robert Anthony Perlman, mother ter-in-law of Carol Jolles Perlman, and grandmother of Jonathan Lee Perlman, all of New York City. Sister of Harold William Solomon and sister-in-law of Marilyn H. Solomon of Nashua, N.H. Her love of literature, art, and the theatre will be remembered by all who knew her. Donations in her memory may be made to the Ladies Village Improvement Society of East Hampton (LVIS), or to the charity of your choice.
