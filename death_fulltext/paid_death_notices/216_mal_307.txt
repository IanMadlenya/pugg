
        RIDLEY--Rev. Harold E. On January 19, 2005. Reverend Harold E. ''Hap'' Ridley, S.J. President of Loyola College in Maryland, beloved son of Doris (nee Corcoran) Ridley of Manasquan, NJ, and the late Harold E. Ridley of Bernardsville, NJ and Sr. Judith Ridley of Philadelphia. He is also survived by several nieces & nephews. Fr. Ridley will lie in state at the Loyola College Alumni Memorial Chapel on Sunday, January 23, from 1:30 to 5:30 PM. A Mass of Remembrance will be offered on Monday at 12:10 PM, in the Reitz Arena at Loyola College. Fr. Ridley will again lie in state at the Loyola College Alumni Memorial Chapel on Monday from 5:00 to 9:00 PM, during which a Christian Wake Service will be held at 7:00 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be offered on Tuesday at 11:00 AM, at the Cathedral of Mary Our Queen. Interment will be private at the Shrine of Our Lady of Martyrs Cemetery in Auriesville, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations are requested to Loyola College in Maryland for a memorial Scholarship Fund honoring Father Ridley. For information, contact Mitchell-Wiedefeld Funeral Home, Inc.
