Services Friday, 10AM at Gutterman's Inc., 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, L.I. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her name may be made to the Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, N.Y. 11590.
