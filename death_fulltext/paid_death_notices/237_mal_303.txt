
        CLANCY--Reverend Monsignor Thomas C. Peacefully returned to the Lord on October 28, 2006. Born on March 14, 1920 to William Clancy and Elizabeth (nee Corrigan). Ordained to the Holy Priesthood on April 3, 1945. After many years of faithful service to the Diocese of Brooklyn, he was appointed as Pastor of the parish of St. Bernard in Bergen Beach and served until his retirement. Dear brother of the late William, Brother Bartholomew OFM ''Jack'' and Ambrose. Survived by his sister Marie Cummings, his sister-in-law Margaret Clancy as well as many nieces, nephews and cousins. Reposing in the Blessed Sacrament Chapel of St. Bernard's Church, Tuesday 7-9 PM, Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9 PM, Thursday 2-5 PM. Solemn Mass of Transferral Thursday, 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass Friday, 10:30 AM. Interment to follow at Calvary Cemetery. For information contact: The Marine Park Funeral Home, 718-339-8900.
