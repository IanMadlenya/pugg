
        TIMPSON-Hilles Morris, on April 23, 2000, after a long illness. Mrs. Timpson was born in Quogue in 1907, the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Stuyvesant Fish Morris. She was a direct descendant of Martin Van Buren, 8th President of the United States, and of Lewis Morris, a signer of the Declaration of Independence. A permanent resident of Southampton since 1978, she became a member of the summer community in the 1920's. Mrs. Timpson committed a great deal of her time and resources to the well being of children and animals and she served for many years as a board member and was a pastpresident of the Southampton Fresh Air Home. Mrs. Timpson was married to L. Gordon Hamersley who died in 1942. She was also married to George L. Bartlett and Robert C. L. Timpson who are deceased. Pre-deceased by her son, James Hooker Hamersley, she is survived by the children of her first marriage, L. Gordon Hamersley, Jr., Stuyvesant M. Hamersley, and Hilles Martin. She also leaves nine grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. Graveside services and interment in Quogue will be private. Those who wish to do so may make memorial contributions to the Southampton Fresh Air Home or to the Animal Rescue Fund.
