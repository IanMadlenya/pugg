
        MANN-Ivin. On October 8, 2002. Former President of the Board of Trustees of Fountain House, Inc., having served as a Trustee for over 50 years. He is survived by his sons Henry and Peter, his grandchildren Jennifer and David, and his loving companion of many years, Elaine Felsher. Contributions in his memory may be made to Fountain House Inc., 425 West 47th St., NYC, 10036. Memorial service will be announced at a later date.
