
        SPILKA -- Louise Alayne (Schaffer), 66, died October 7, 2003, surrounded by her loving family, after a brief, brave battle with cancer. She lived a rich, full life. With Bill, her husband of 47 years, she shared laughter, music, world-wide travel and pride in their sons Jesse and Theo, grandchildren Molly, Stephen, Marc and Christine, and daughter-in-law Susan. With a BA and MPH from Hunter College and School of Health Sciences, she had a long career in health education and administration. She was also a tireless tenant activist. There will be a memorial service. Donations to Bide-aWee or The Humane Society are requested.
