
        BALDWIN-William H. At the age of 82 on December 23, 2002. Dearly loved and loving husband, father, brother and granddad. Survived by his wife Margot, sons Glenn and Mark, brothers Charles and Paul (Billingham), sister Elizabeth Rowland, grandchildren Matthew and Dana and a host of friends. The family will receive friends Thursday, January 16, 5-8 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street with a memorial service, Friday, January 17, at 4 PM, at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the organization of your choice. To die but not to perish is to be eternally present.
