
        OLIN--Gerald D. The North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System mourns the passing of Gerald D. Olin of North Hills, NY and Longboat Key, FL, a life trustee and generous benefactor for more than three decades. A World War II Army veteran and clothing industry executive, Mr. Olin was elected to the board of North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset in 1971 and actively supported a range of initiatives, including the hospital's emergency department expansion, and cardiology and pediatric hematology programs. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family, including his wife Susanne Cohen, his son Daniel and his daughter Laura. Roy J. Zuckerberg, Chairman Michael Dowling President & CEO North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System
        OLIN--Gerald D. Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory's Board of Trustees, its Association, administration and staff extend deepest sympathy to the family and friends of Gerald D. Olin. Mr. Olin served as a director of the CSHLA from 1995 through 1997 and, together with his wife Sue, was a generous supporter of research, education and cultural programs at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Mr. Olin will be deeply missed by all who knew him.
        OLIN--Gerald. Temple Sinai of Roslyn records with profound sorrow the death of our devoted member. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Rabbi Michael A. White Carol Weinstock, President
