
        STERN-Marvin, M.D. The NYU - Bellevue Psychiatric Society notes, with deepest regret, the death of one their founding members, Marvin Stern, M.D., on May 13th. Dr. Stern was Professor Emeritus of Psychiatry at the NYU School of Medicine and was associated with Bellevue Hospital for over 65 years. He was a distinguished educator and clinician who taught generations of psychiatrists, all of whom valued him as an esteemed and inspirational teacher. The members and officers of the Society extend their deepest sympathies to the bereaved family. Benjamin J. Sadock, M.D. President, NYU-Bellevue Psychiatric Society
