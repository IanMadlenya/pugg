
        LAWRENCE-Janet Walling. Age 82, of Wilton, CT, died peacefully on March 26. Mrs. Lawrence was predeceased by her husband, John Townsend Lawrence, former President of William Morrow & Co., publishers. A woman of uncommon spirit, Mrs. Lawrence is survived by Mrs. George W. Carmany III of Boston, MA; Ms. Deborah Lawrence-Riddell, of Amherst, MA; John Townsend Lawrence Jr., of Fort Collins, CO, and six grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held on Monday, March 31 at St. Matthew's Church, Wilton, CT, at 2 PM.
