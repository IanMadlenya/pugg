
        WEISSMAN-Hope, 58. Wesleyan University Associate Professor of Letters and of Women's Studies, died July 24, 2002. Survived by her mother, Sarah Weissman of Monroe Township, NJ and brothers Robert of Randolph, N.J., and Arthur of Falls Church, VA. Memorial service is planned for the fall at Wesleyan University. Donations may be made to Wesleyan University, attention Hope Weissman Memorial Scholarship Fund, C/O University Relations, 318 High St., Middletown, CT. 06459.
