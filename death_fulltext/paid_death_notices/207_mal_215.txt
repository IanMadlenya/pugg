
        MASSET--Andrew H. 88, a 30year Ford Motor International executive, died February 4 in Guadalajara, Mexico. He was president of Delta Kappa of Delta Tau Delta fraternity and part of the Class of '38 at Duke. He was predeceased by wife Lee and daughter Melanie, and is survived by daughters Valerie, Hilary, Stephanie, son Andrew and eight grandchildren.
