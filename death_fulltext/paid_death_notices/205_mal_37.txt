
        PODUSKA--Howard J., 83, died on Monday, February 2, 2004 at his home in Middletown. After over 40 years of employment, he retired as the President of the Bank of New York in NYC. He was also on the Board of Directors of the Sterling Bank of NYC and the Glenview State Bank of Illinois. Visitation will be held on Wednesday from 2-4 and 7-9pm at the John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Ave, Red Bank. Funeral services will begin at 10:30am on Thursday at the funeral home. Entombment will follow at the Fair View Cemetery Mausoleum, Middletown. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that memorial contributions be made in his name to Meridian Hospice, 615 Hope Road, 2nd Floor, Eatontown, NJ 07724.
