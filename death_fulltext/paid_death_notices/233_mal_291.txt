
        LOEW--Elliott, 89 years old, of Lake Worth, Florida. Formerly of Great Neck, New York. Died June 23, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia Loew, father of Judith, Jonathon, and Patricia, grandfather of nine and great grandfather of four. He lived a long and successful life, and he will be greatly missed. Funeral to be held today at I.J. MorrIs in Hempstead, New York at 10 AM.
