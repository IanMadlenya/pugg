
        WATTS-Edward Francis, Senior. Born October 4, 1916, died March 14, 2000. Retired from the Greenpoint Savings Bank after more than 50 years. Husband of the late Paula. He is survived by his children Edward and Gladys, daughterin-law Linda and granddaughter Brooke. Friends may call at Guidos Funeral Home, 440 Clinton St, Brooklyn, Thursday 2-5 & 7-9PM. Funeral service Friday, March 17, 10AM, at the First Unitarian Church, 50 Monroe Pl, Brooklyn. Interment Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn.
