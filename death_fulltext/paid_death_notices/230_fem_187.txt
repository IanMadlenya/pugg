
        WERNE--Irene Garrow, M.D. A retired pathologist and dedicated peace activist, died in her own home of natural causes at age 91 on March 17, 2006. She was the beloved wife of the late Dr. Jacob Werne and is survived by a sister, Ms. Eleanore Nyilas, her two daughters, Dr. Joellen Werne and Naomi Werne, Esq. (retired), and a son, Dr. Carl Werne, as well as four grandchildren, Joanna and Emily Werne and Laura and Jacob Smolowe.
