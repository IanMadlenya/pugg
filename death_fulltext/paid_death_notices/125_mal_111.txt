
        FORRESTER-Robert R. Jr., 87, of Gulfstream, FL, former President of the Wilcolator Co. of Elizabeth, NJ, died June 7, at his residence. Survivors, include his wife, Elena; three sons: Robert, George and Peter; seven grandchildren and six great grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at 11 A.M., Thursday, June 12, at St. Paul's Episcopal Church, Delray Beach, FL. For more information contact Aycock Funeral Home, Jupiter, Florida, (561-746-5124).
