
        MURRAY--James W., 80, On October 28, 2005, of Ramsey, NJ. Beloved husband of Dolores (nee O'Connor) Murray for 51 years. Loving father of Anne, Ellen, Kathy and James. Adored grandfather of Brian Jr., Matthew, Chelsea, Leeland, Madeline and Colleen. Dear brother of Marie Riley. James was a US Army WWII veteran and employed by Pfizer, Inc. in NYC for 40 years, retiring in 1990. He was a parishioner of St. Paul R.C. Church in Ramsey. Visitation will be held on Sunday, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Van Emburgh-Sneider Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Ave, Ramsey. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated on Monday, October 31, 2005, at 10 AM, at St. Paul R.C. Church in Ramsey. Interment to follow at Maryrest Cemetery in Mahwah. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association NYC Chapter, 360 Lexington Ave, 5th Floor, NY, NY 10017.
