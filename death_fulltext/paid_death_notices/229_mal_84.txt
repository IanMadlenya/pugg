
        FISHMAN - Bernard. The Jewish Theological Seminary community mourns the death of Bernard Fishman of Philadelphia, a former member of JTS's Board of Trustees. A leading philanthropist and longtime friend of JTS, Bernie was highly respected in the business and Jewish community for his integrity and warmth. He was honored by JTS in 2004 with the Chancellor's Medal in appreciation of his unswerving support and generosity, which enabled the institution to further its mission of enhancing Jewish life in America, Israel, and beyond. May all his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
