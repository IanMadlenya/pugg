
        LEDY--William A. Beloved husband of the late Susan. Adored father of David and Pia Ledy and Nancy LedyGurren. Devoted grandfather to Jordan and Cameron Ledy. A real estate investor and businessman, he will be remembered most for his warmth, humor and his unabiding love and respect for people of all ages. There could be no more caring husband, father, brother, uncle and friend. His smile warmed the hearts of many. Funeral services at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, NY, on Tuesday, October 26, at 2 pm. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate a contribution to The Make a Wish Foundation or The Sephardic Home, www.sephardichome. org. Shiva will be observed at his daughter's home on Tuesday and Wednesday and at the home of his son on Thursday and Friday until 5 pm.
