
        BECKMAN--Arnold O, Ph.D. Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory's Board of Trustees, administration and staff extend deepest sympathy to the family and friends of Arnold O. Beckman, Ph.D. Dr. Beckman, through the Arnold and Mabel Beckman Foundation, was a generous supporter of the research, infrastructure, educational initiatives and programs of Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory for nearly two decades. Dr. Beckman's lead gift in 1987 led to the establishment of a neuroscience program at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory and created a center for neuroscience which today bears his name. As a scientist, entrepreneur, philanthropist and friend, Dr. Beckman will be deeply missed by all who knew him. James D. Watson, Chancellor William R. Miller, Chairman Bruce Stillman, President & Chief Executive Officer W. Dillaway Ayres Chief Operating Officer
