
        JEFFERS-Monsignor Vincent W. Former Director of the Propagation of the Faith and retired Pastor of Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor, died April 19, 2002. Visiting hours at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Avenue, Pelham, on Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church on Thursday 10 A.M. Interment Holy Mount Cemetery, Eastchester, NY. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to the Propagation of the Faith, 1011 1st Avenue, Room 170, New York, New York 10022.
