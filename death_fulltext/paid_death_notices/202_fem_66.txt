
        MITCHELL--Valerie (Nield). 67, died at home in Yarmouth Port, MA, Thursday, October 30, after a three year battle with cancer. Born in New York City, she attended Nightingale-Bamford School, was presented at the Tuxedo Autum Ball and graduated from Bennett Junior College. She worked for the Shubert Theatre Organization, Playwright Howard Teichman and the late David Suskind. She is survived by her husband Garry, her daughter Heather Mitchell of Los Angeles and her brother Ogden Nield of Fort Lauderdale. Memorial donations may be made to the Chatham Drama Guild, P.O. Box 92, 134 Krowell Road, Chatham, MA 02633.
