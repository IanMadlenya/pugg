
        TOFFEL-Harry, 91, of Lawrence, NY and Palm Beach, FL. passed away on May 1, 2003. Preceded in death by his beloved wife Hattie, he is survived by his daughter Sandra Kaiser, grandsons Andrew and Kenneth, great-grandchildren Adam, Steven, Jordan, Margot and Katie and sister Yetta Epstein. A leader in the garment industry he was extremely philanthropic and devoted to his family. He will be greatly missed by all who knew him.
