
        WILLIAMS-Roger E., Esq., 65, of Black Point, CT, on June 4, 1997. Beloved father of Jocelyn and Karen. Graduate of Dartmouth College and Yale Law School. Served three years in U.S. Navy. Trustee of Grace Church School 1974-1983. Retired from the NY law firm Townley & Updike. A memorial mass, Wednesday, June 11, 11 AM, St. Joseph's Church, Greenwich Village. Memorial contributions may be made in his name to Dartmouth College.
