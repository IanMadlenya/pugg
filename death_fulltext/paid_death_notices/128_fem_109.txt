
        DIETHELM-Margaret W., 90, of Delray Beach, FL., and formerly of Locust Valley, Long Island, NY, passed away Sunday, September 7, 1997. Mrs. Diethelm is the daughter of the late Arthur E. and Florence C. Whitney of New York. She is survived by a daughter, Margaret D. Foster of Honolulu, HI.; and a sister, Helen W. Brown of Huntington, Long Island, New York. Funeral services were held September 10, 1997 in Delray Beach, Florida. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
