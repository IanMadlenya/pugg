
        BENJAMIN-Roy A. 81. Died at his home in White Plains, N.Y., on January 15, 1998, after a long and valiant fight against cancer. Survived by his wife of 50 years, Betty (Heilbrun) Benjamin; his children, Nancy Benjamin, Kate Spector, and Chip Benjamin; his sons-and daughter-in-law Herbert Nolan, Arnold Spector, and Laurie Benjamin and five grandchildren. Also surviving are a brother George Benjamin and a sister, Margaret Rothschild, both of FL. Chairman of the Benjamin Company, a book publishing/marketing organiza tion, he was also an avid golfer and author of two golf books. Memorial services will be held on Monday, January 19 at 11 AM at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Avenue East, Rye, 914-967-4382. Contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021 or a charity of your choice.
        BENJAMIN-Roy. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow, the passing of our beloved long-time member, Roy Benjamin. He was a very active member, serving several terms on the Board of Governors and had been Chairman of both the Green and Golf Committees and the Club's Historian. Roy was the Club's Golf Champion a total of 13 times in his 53 years as a member. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to Betty and his family. Andrew J. Frankel, President Judith Weil, Secretary
