
        LOWENGARD-Jerome Harry Claster, 77. Of West Hartford, CT, on October 17, 2002. He leaves his wife of 51 years, Elaine Title Lowengard, his children, Mary and Sarah of NYC, Henry and wife Nancy Graham of Kingston, NY, Benjamin and wife Susan Cohen of Watertown, MA, Alexander of West Hartford, and Jeremiah and wife Sheila of South Portland, ME, and six grandchildren. Memorial service Monday, October 21, 3 PM, Congregation Beth Israel, 701 Farmington Ave., West Hartford. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Endowment Foundation of the Greater Hartford Jewish Federation, Hartford Stage Co., or Connecticut Forest and Parks Association.
