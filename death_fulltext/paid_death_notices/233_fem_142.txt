
        KOSSOVER - Ruth. Passed away at home surrounded by her loved ones. Beloved wife of the late Harold. Devoted mother of Steven and Andrew. Cherished grandmother of Noah, Lindsey, Jacqueline, Lauren and Jared. Former President of Julia Bernstein League of Free Nurses, former Board member of Parker Jewish Geriatrics at Long Island Jewish Hospital, former board member of North Shore League for Parent Education, and retired New York City school teacher. Services Sunday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
