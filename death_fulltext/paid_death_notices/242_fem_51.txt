
        LESSALL--Frances Schlanger. Beloved wife of the late George Lessall, loving mother of Kenneth Lessall and Bonnie Bach (Gilbert), adoring grandma of Matthew Lessall, Nancy Bach, and Amy Markman (John), left us on March 4th at the age of 95. She was the consummate mother, only caring about her family and their well-being. Always there, always loving, she will always be missed and in our hearts. Love, Ken, Bonnie, Gil, Matthew, Nancy, Amy, John
