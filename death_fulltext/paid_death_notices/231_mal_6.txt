
        BERLER - Alfred. Beloved husband, father, brother, father - in - law, grandfather, great-grandfather and uncle, born in Vienna, Austria, on May 7, 1911, died March 31, 2006, at age 94, in Queens, NY, where he lived for 54 years. He is survived by his sons, Herbert and Ronald, his daughter-in-law Jesslynn, his grandchildren Mark and Royce, his great-grandchildren Matthew and Allison, and several nephews and nieces.
