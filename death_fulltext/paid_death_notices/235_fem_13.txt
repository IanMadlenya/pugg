
        APPELBAUM--Gertrude Helen. passed away on July 17. She was born February 20, 1908 to Celia Cohen and David Appelbaum and is survived by her sister Sylvia and brother Burton. As casting director in the Broadway theater, where Gertrude was known as Apple, she had an eye for young, untried talent, many of whom obtained their first acting Jobs through her. Her major discovery was the late Walter Matthau. Messages of condolence may be sent to 55 Central Park West (5G), New York, N.Y. 10023.
