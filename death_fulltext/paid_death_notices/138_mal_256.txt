
        WRIGHT-Garland. The acting company, artistic and administrative staffs, and Board of Trustees of The Shakespeare Theatre in Washington, D.C. mourn the loss of their beloved colleague, Garland Wright. One of the most brilliant theatre directors of his generation, Garland will be sorely missed and remembered always. Michael Kahn, Artistic Dir. Sam Sweet, Managing Dir. Lawrence A. Hough, Chairman, Board of Trustees The Shakespeare Theatre
        WRIGHT-Garland. The Juilliard Community is deeply saddened by the passing of Garland Wright, an extraordinary artist and teacher. His presence as a director and member of the faculty had an enormously positive impact on the entire institution. He will be deeply missed by us all. Mary Rodgers Guettel, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
        WRIGHT-Garland. The Board of Directors, Administration, Alumni & extended family of The Acting Company are deeply saddened by the loss of a cherished friend and brilliant colleague. Margot Harley, Producing Director Edgar Lansbury, Chairman of the Board
        WRIGHT-Garland. Michael Kahn and the faculty, staff and students of The Juilliard Drama Division mourn the untimely death of our beloved friend and treasured colleague. We extend our love and deepest sympathy to Cynthia, Jack, John and Sari.
        WRIGHT-Garland. New York Theatre Workshop mourns the loss of a visionary director, an inspiring colleague and a beloved friend. We will remember his tremendous spirit always.
