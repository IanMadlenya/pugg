
        KONHEIM-Natalie B. Of Longboat Key, Florida. Died March 7, 1999 at the age of 81. Natalie lived in Woodmere, New York, until ten years ago when she moved to Longboat Key. She is survived by her husband, Norris Konheim, to whom she was married for 60 years. She is also survived by her daughter, Patty Goldenberg of Newton, MA., and her son-in-law, Dr. Don Goldenberg, and her grandchildren Wendy Hagen of Shelburne, VT, and Julie Goldenberg of Portland, OR. She is also survived by Kenneth Robinson and Alice Robinson of New York City, the children of Natalie and Norris' other daughter, Nancy K. Robinson, who died in 1994. Natalie was also the great-grandmother of Michael and Julia Hagen, the children of Thom and Wendy Hagen. Condolence calls can be made March 8-11 at the Konheim's apartment on Longboat Key at 4430 Exeter Drive, Apt. M107, (941) 383-0095. In lieu of flowers, donations should be made to Hospice of Southwest Florida, 5955 Rand Blvd, Sarasota, FL 34238.
