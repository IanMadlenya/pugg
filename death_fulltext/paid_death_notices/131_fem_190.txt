
        WALDRON-Sybil Kane Jay. Died Dec. 19, 1997 after a long illness. Wife of William A. Waldron, mother of Sybil Baldwin, Maisie Houghton, Elizabeth Kinnicutt, grandmother of Sarah and Benjamin Baldwin and James and Nina Houghton. She was predeceased by her first husband, Francis P. Kinnicutt. Memorial service at Christ Church, Cambridge, MA, on Saturday, January 24, 1998 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to The Brearley School, 610 East 83rd Street, New York City 10028-7988 or to Shady Hill School, Coolidge Hill, Cambridge, MA 02138-5598.
