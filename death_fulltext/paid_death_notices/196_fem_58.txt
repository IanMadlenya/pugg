
        STEINER - Jeanette. Died May 5, 2003 in Jerusalem, Israel after a long illness. Beloved wife of Dr. Alexander Steiner. Devoted mother to Hagai and step-mother to Dan, Nava and Miriam. Loving grandmother to Bar and Shir. Cherished sister of Alice Geisler and aunt of Robert Geisler. She was a very talented artist and a loyal friend. She will be greatly missed.
