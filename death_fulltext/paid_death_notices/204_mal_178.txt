
        OLIN--Gerald. The Long Island Head Injury Association expresses deep sorrow at the passing of their beloved founding member and secretary. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Susanne and the entire family. Stuart A. Gleiber, Pres Leonard Feinstein, Vice Pres. Charles Wolf, Executive Dir.
        OLIN--Gerald. North Shore Country Club records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Gerald Olin. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Peter J. Baum, President Stuart Bednoff, Secretary
        OLIN--Gerald D. Of North Hills, NY, and Longboat Key, FL. Died peacefully on January 10, at age 77. Beloved husband of Susanne Cohen for 53 years. World War II veteran, graduate of Brown University 1948, and respected executive in mens clothing industry. Father to Daniel, Laura and the late Michael. Father-in-law to Melissa, Trevor, and Jill. Grandpa to Andrew, Chelsea, Heather and Daniel. Donations in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or to your favorite charity. Funeral Monday, January 12, 1:30 pm, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck.
