
        WARREN-William C. The Officers, Directors, and Staff of Sterling Bancorp and Sterling National Bank mourn the passing of their long time friend, advisor and colleague, who served as a Director with great integrity and distinction for many years. His distinguished and exceptional career spanned the worlds of academia, law, and business. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Louis J. Cappelli, Chairman John C. Millman, President
        WARREN-William C. The Columbia Law School faculty and staff mourn the passing of their extraordinary leader and benefactor, Dean William C. Warren. As dean from 1952 until 1970 and as a trusted advisor until his passing, he brought the school into the modern age and made possible the school's continued success. We are thankful for his inspired leadership and support spanning more than five decades. Dean David W. Leebron and the Columbia Law School community
