
        ANCONA-Victor. On February 10, peacefully, at New York Hospital, in his 86th year. Husband of Ruth Ancona, father of Barry Ancona and Ronnie Ancona, father-in-law of Shannon Carr and Steven Cole, grandfather of Polly Ancona Carr and David Ancona-Cole. Artist and student of the arts. Graduate of Erasmus Hall High School and Parsons School of Design. Commercial artist, advertising executive, video art critic, civic volunteer. No services, at his request.
