
        FOX--Professor Seymour, 77, died July 10th, in Jerusalem, Israel. Beloved husband of Sue, father of David, Eytan, Danny and family. A cherished leader in outstanding educational initiatives in the United States, Israel, and worldwide. Including his positions as: Director of camps Ramah, Dean of the Teachers Institute of the Jewish Theological Seminary, Head of the Hebrew University School of Education, Director of the Melton Center for Jewish Education, founder of the Jerusalem Fellows and President of the Mandel Institute Worldwide. A dear friend, outstanding human being and great educator. He will be sorely missed. Donna and Stanley Batkin
