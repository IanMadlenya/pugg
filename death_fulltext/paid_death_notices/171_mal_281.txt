
        CHISHOLM-William H., 83. Of Greenwich died on April 18, at home. Survived by his wife Edith ''Peggy'' Chisholm, two daughters Barbara C. Young of Montrose, CO, Margo J. Chisholm of Carbondale, CO and a granddauther, he was predeceased by his first wife Alice Chisholm. Services will be held at Christ Church, 254 East Putnam Ave. Greenwich, CT 06830 on April 24, at 2:00 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Paul's School, Concord, NY 03301, Yale University Class of 1940 Fund, New Haven, CT. 06520 or Christ Church.
