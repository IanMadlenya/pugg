
        LISZ-Gary Kent. Of New York City. On August 11, 1998. He was 44 years old. He was a painter, award-winning costume designer, & professor of Design at Parsons & The Fashion Institute of Technology. Mr. Lisz is survived by his parents, Dorothy & Leonard Lisz of Romney, West Virginia, his nephew, Paul Rodriguez of Washington, D.C., his companion Kenneth Daigle of Manhattan & countless collaborators and loving friends. A funeral service for family and close friends will be held Monday, August 17th, at 7 P.M. at the Fourth Universalist Society, at Central Park West and 76th Street. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions in his name can be made to The Hetrick-Martin Institute, 2 Astor Place, New York City, 10003.
