
        KLINGER-Tathiana S. (Tomi). Died Tuesday, June 12, 2001 in Concord, NH. Devoted wife of Dr. Leo Klinger for 61 years. She is survived by her husband, her children and their spouses: Ann and Dana Turner of Austell, Ga., Charles and Carol Klinger of Honeoye Falls, NY and Bernard and Eda Klinger of White Plains, N.Y. Beloved grandmother of Maria and Daniel Barr, David Follman and Michael and Rebecca Klinger. Born on June 8, 1913 in New York City to Boris and Celia Shapiro. She graduated from NYU with a degree in economics. From 1940 to 1972 she lived in Rochester, NH serving on the School Board, Business and Professional Women, the Visiting Nurse Board and many civic organizations. Known to many as ''Ma Klinger'' she delighted in her hobbies of quilting, knitting, needle crafts and baking ''kurls.'' She will be greatly missed. Funeral service at Temple Beth Jacob, Concord, NH on Thursday, June 14th, at 2 PM.
