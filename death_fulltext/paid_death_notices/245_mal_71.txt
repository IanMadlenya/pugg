
        SZLACHTER--Morris. Congregation Rodeph Sholom is deeply saddened bythe death of Morris Szlachter, widower of Frida Szlachter and the beloved father of Irwin Shlacter, our longtime past headmaster. He will be deeply missed by his family. We extend our deepest condolences to Irwin and Renee, their children, Adam, Zimra, Jordan and Rayna, and all their dear ones. The Clergy, the Board of Trustees and the Rodeph Sholom Day School
