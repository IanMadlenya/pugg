
        HENDERSON--Gregory M., 51, of Palm Beach, FL, died Wednesday, June 18, at home. Born in South Orange, NJ, he lived in Rumson for most of his life. Prior to his retirement in March 2000, he was a Senior Vice President of Henderson Brothers, Inc. as well as a member of the New York Stock Exchange for twenty-three years. He graduated from Christian Brothers Academy in 1970 and Villanova University in 1974. At the time of his death he was a partner at Hook, Line and Sinker Restaurant and Chief Financial Officer of H2O Velocity Athletic Wear. He was a member of The Breakers Hotel and Club, Palm Beach Yacht Club, Navesink Country Club and a former member of Monmouth Beach Bath and Tennis Club. He was a communicant of Saint Edwards Church, Palm Beach, FL. His brother Charles F. Henderson III predeceased him. He is survived by his loved and loving daughters, Katherine, Lauren and Christin; his former wife, Janet Henderson of Rumson; his parents Mary Louise and Charles F. Henderson of Palm Beach, FL; his three brothers, David of Rumson, Guy of Palm Beach FL, and Jerome of Middletown, NJ, and one sister, Mary Louise Clarke of Short Hills, NJ, and many nieces and nephews. Visitation will be held from 25PM and 7-9PM Monday at John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Ave., Red Bank, NJ. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10:30AM, Tuesday, at Holy Cross Church, Rumson. Interment will be held at Mount Olivet Cemetery, Middletown, NJ. Memorial donations may be made in his memory to Christian Brothers Academy, 850 Newman Springs Road, Lincroft, New Jersey 07738. >>AD#
