
        BEGUN-Benson H. On September 8, 1997. Beloved husband of Angela, loving son of Ruth and the late Harry. Cherished father of Harold and Ellen, Matthew and Elizabeth, Daniel and devoted brother of Marcia, beloved cousin of Martin Begun. All mourn his passing after an arduous and difficult illness. Fortified with extraordinary determination to survive, blessed with an intellect and winning personality he strove and achieved excellence in his professional life and nurtured a loving family. A graduate of Columbia College and Columbia Law School where he was awarded the high honor of being an Harlan Fiske Stone Scholar and an Editor of the Law Review. His career in entertainment law was highlighted by his association with olumbia Pictures, Warner-Amex and the Fox Film Corp. Service to be held Wednesday 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the Leukemia Society of America, 600 3rd Avenue, NYC. His spirit will remain in our hearts forever.
