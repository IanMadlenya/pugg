
        ALPERN-Dwight K. Chemist, electrical engineer, metallurgist, mineralogist, writer, educator. Born on August 21, 1903; died on August 10th, 1999. Son of Morris Israel Alpern and Anna Trenner Alpern. Survived by his sons John, Andrew and Jerrold, and his grandchildren, Alison and Adam. His wife Grace Michelman Alpern died in 1990.
