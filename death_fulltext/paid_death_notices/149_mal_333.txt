
        BLOOMBERG-Hal. Of Boynton Beach, Fla. Died June 17 at age 82. Beloved husband of Louise Austin and cherished father of Michael N. Bloomberg. Devoted brother of Leon and Miriam Bloomberg of Oakland, Ca. Dearest stepfather of Jonathan Austin (Mary Anne Mazzocco) and Benjamin Austin. Hal was a P.E. who retired from The Communications Division of the NYC Police Dept. Graveside services were held June 20, 1999 in Woodbridge, N.J. He will be greatly missed by his extended family and his many friends.
