
        BROILES--Melvyn. The Juilliard School is deeply saddened by the death of Melvyn Broiles, who was a member of the trumpet faculty from 1971 to the present. With dedication and great professionalism, he was an important performer and teacher for generations of brass players. We express our condolences to his wife and family. He will be greatly missed. Joseph W. Polisi, President Stephen Clapp, Dean
        BROILES--Melvyn. The Metropolitan Opera mourns the death of our dear colleague and former principal trumpet, Melvyn Broiles. We will miss his friendship and good humor as well as his artistry, and we extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Terri, and his daughter, Karen. Joseph Volpe General Manager James Levine Artistic Director
