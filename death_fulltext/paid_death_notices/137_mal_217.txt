
        AUSTIN-Richard. On June 20, 1998, following an extended illness. Cherished husband of Carolyn for 50 years. Beloved father of Leonard and Deborah Austin, Andrew Austin and Michael Sonberg. Devoted grandfather to Shaun and Beth. Brother of Sanford Ostrofsky. Retired Hicksville High School business teacher. A warm, wise, witty and wonderful man who will be greatly missed. May his memory be for a blessing.
