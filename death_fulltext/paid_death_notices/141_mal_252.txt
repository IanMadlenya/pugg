
        LAPKIN-Nathaniel. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Mount Sinai School of Medicine and the Mount Sinai-NYU Medical Center and Health System deeply mourn the passing of Nathaniel Lapkin, a generous long-time supporter and Sponsor of Mount Sinai School of Medicine, as well as a benefactor of The Mount Sinai Hospital for many years. Mr. Lapkin supported a broad range of research, educational and clinical activities, as well as assistance for indigent patients. He will be remembered most fondly, with gratitude for his far reaching philanthropy. We extend deepest sympathy to his daughter Frances, his son, Mitchell, to his grandchildren and to the entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, MD, President Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President Theresa A. Bischoff, Executive Vice President
