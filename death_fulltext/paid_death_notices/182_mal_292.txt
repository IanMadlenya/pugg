
        GATELY-Martin J., MD. On March 18, 2002 of Manhasset, NY. Beloved husband of the late Mary Patricia. Loving father of Tricia & John Rowe, Martin & Mary Ellen Gately, Kevin and Terry Gately, Dr. Michael & Sherry Gately, Rosemary & Peter O'Neill, Timothy & Charleen Gately. Also survived by eighteen grandchildren and ten great-grandchildren. Doctor Gately was a proud New York City Fireman for 13 years.He was an Ensign in the Pacific Theatre during WW II. He was a physician in private practice in Levittown for 47 years and retired as a Police Surgeon in Nassau County. He was very involved in Fordham Prep and the MaryKnoll Missions in Guatemala. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel Tuesday and Wednesday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Thursday 10:00 AM at St. Mary's RC Church in Manhasset. Interment Nassau Knolls Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory to Fordham Prep or MaryKnoll Missions would be appreciated.
