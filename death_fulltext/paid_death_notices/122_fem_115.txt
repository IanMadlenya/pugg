
        STONE-Louise Hawley Higgins. Peacefully, at the Balmoral Club, Toronto on March 9, 1997, in her 94th year. Beloved wife of the late Louis Higgins and the late Edgar Stone. Dear daughter of the late Fred and Miriam Hawley. Sister of the late William Hawley. Stepmother of Richard Stone and Elizabeth Elliott. Aunt of Louise, Kay, Helen, Evelyn and the late Fred. Louise was a longtime patron of the Royal Ontario Museum, serving on the Board of Directors and especially the Bishop White Committee which she chaired for many years, establishing the Far Eastern Endowment Fund. In 1996, she created the Louise Hawley Stone Chair of Far Eastern Art. Private service was held followed by cremation. Memorial donations may be made to Far Eastern Endowment Fund. The Bishop White Committee, The Royal Ontario Museum, 100 Queen's Park Crescent, Toronto, Ontario M5S 2C6.
