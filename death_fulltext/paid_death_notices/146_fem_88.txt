
        CHECHANOVER-Sue Carol. Of Petaluma, CA, passed away on February 22, 1999. Beloved wife of Victor, mother of Robert, Paul and Michael, mother-in-law of Janine, Caryn and Vivian, grandmother of Elizabeth, Lauren, Peter and Julianne. She was very active in her community dedicating her life to helping those in need. The world was her home, and she was concerned about everyone she met, making friends everywhere. She gave everything and took nothing in return. Make a donation to your favorite charity. Goodbye Ma, we will miss your kind heart and gentle soul. Your loving sons.
