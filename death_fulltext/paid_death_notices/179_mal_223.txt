
        KAHN-Peter S. Longtime resident of Scarsdale, NY, died December 14, 2001 in New Milford, CT at the home of his daughter, Alexandra McBrian. At his side, was his loving wife of 61 years, Lida Huelsman Kahn. He is survived by his wife Lida, his sister, Yereth Knowles of Alexandria, VA, and his 3 children, Alexandra McBrien of New Milford, CT, Patrina Ulmer of Eugene, OR, and Gretchen Espe of DeMoines, IA, as well as his six wonderful grandchildren.
