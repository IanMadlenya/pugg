
        KALLMAN - Jonathan. Of Highland Park, NJ. Wife Amy, children Natasha and Joseph, parents Audrey and the late David, and sisters Sandy and Evie mourn the loss of Jonathan. Mr. Kallman was associate director of market regulation at the Security and Exchange Commission in Washington, DC., instrumental in developing T+3, and a principal architect of SEC oversight program changes in response to the 1987 market break. In 1996 Mr. Kallman joined Morgan, Lewis, and Bockus where he practiced securities law. Interment is at Beth Israel Cemetery in Woodbridge, New Jersey.
