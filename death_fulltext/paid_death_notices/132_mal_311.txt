
        LEAVEY-John G., a retired IBM executive, died on January 21. Mr. Leavey was 74 and died of pneumonia He joined IBM in 1957 and spent over 34 years as a special activities executive. He is survived by his wife Lorraine, his sons James, John and William, and his five grandchildren, Christopher, Michael, Patrick, Noel and Owen.
