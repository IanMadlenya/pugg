
        KESSLER--Louis B. Founder and President of the New York Reptile Co, Inc., a ladies handbag manufacturing company and resident of 150 East 61 St., NYC, died in NYC on July 5, 2004. Funeral services will be held Thursday, July 8, 2004 at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel located at 630 Amsterdam Ave, NYC at 11:30AM. Mr. Kessler leaves behind his companion Ms. Fermina Bravo, a niece Ms. Janis Sexton of Gainesville, FL., and three first cousins: Frida Neumark of New Jersey and Lillian Bandel of California and Thelma Surfer of Delray Beach, FL.
