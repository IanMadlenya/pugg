
        BAKER-J. Edward, 64. Of Ridgefield, CT, August 13, 2001 after a battle with cancer. Retired VP of Natural Resources at Lazard Freres & Co. of NYC. Well known within the oil and gas industry. Graduate of Oklahoma University & a member of Phi Kappa Sigma. Survived by his wife, Ann (Dixon) Baker, a son, Jeff Baker of Danbury, CT, a daughter, Sherry Gervais of Ridgefield, CT, a sister, Shirley Rossiter of Tulsa, OK., and four grandchildren. Funeral services, on Friday, at 11 A.M., at First Congregational Church, Ridgefield, CT. Interment private. The family will receive friends following the service. In lieu of flowers, contributions to your favorite charity would be appreciated.
