
        BURNS--Denis H. Suddenly on November 27, 2004. Beloved husband of Virginia Hacket Burns. Devoted father of Ward and Hugh (Molly). Brother of Priscilla Cunningham and Frances Clough. Proud graduate of Choate School, Princeton University Class of 1955 and Harvard University Class of 1961. Denis enjoyed a long and remarkable career in banking for Citibank and Chase. Now he leaves behind a legacy of joy and kindness toward his family and wide circle of friends. Visiting at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Tuesday 2-4 and 68PM. Funeral mass at St. John The Evangelist Church, 1st Ave at 55th St, Wednesday 10:30AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
