
        LEVIEN-Augusta. Born September 15, 1905, died January 25, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Eddie, loving mother of Joy and Jimmy and his wife, Arlene, proud grandmother of Jason and Stacey and her husband, Chris Ciauri, and great-grandmother of Sabrina, dear sister of Edward Ritz and his wife, Kitty, and Evelyn Wolf, and devoted aunt to many nieces and nephews. ''Augie'' was an extraordinary and beautiful woman, inside and out, who saw only the good in all people and never turned anyone away who needed a home or help. She will be greatly missed by all those whose lives she touched with her unbounded generosity, amazing vibrancy and exceptional kindness. Service Monday, January 28th, 1 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
