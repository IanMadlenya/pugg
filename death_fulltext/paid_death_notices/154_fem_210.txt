
        KAUFMAN-Rita. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of its devoted alumni parent and grandparent and longtime benefactor, the beloved mother of our cherished alumni and supporters, the late Judy Hurwich, Gerald S. Kaufman, Belda Lindenbaum and Carol Newman. The Kaufman Auditorium in our Upper School, together with her many expressions of love for the children of Ramaz and the School itself will render her memory eternal in our midst. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein, Principal David N. Kahn Chairman, Board of Trustees
