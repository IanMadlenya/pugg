
        LEWIS-Minna Margulies. Of Fifth Ave., NY on May 7, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Duffy. Devoted mother of Donald Lewis, Jane Rosenberg and son-in-law, Joel Rosenberg. Loving grandmother of Jennifer and Dawn Rosenberg. Her charm, wit, beauty and zest for life made a lasting impression on all who knew her. To know her was to love her. Services Monday, 1 PM at Riverside Cemetery, Lodi, New Jersey.
