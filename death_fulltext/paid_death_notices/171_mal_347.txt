
        PENKRAT-Peter Alexis. Formerly of Franklin Lakes, N.J. and Nyack, N.Y., died on Friday, April 20, 2001 of cancer. He was 69. An HVAC Designer Engineer, he was a pioneer in air conditioning design. Among the many examples of his creative & innovative work in Manhattan are buildings like the Guggenheim Museum, the Council on Foreign Relations and Gracie Mansion. He was greatly loved and will be missed by family and friends. Donations to the Tolstoy Foundation, 104 Lake Road, Valley Cottage, New York 10989.
