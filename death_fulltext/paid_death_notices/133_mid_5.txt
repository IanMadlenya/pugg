
        MILBANK-Posy D. The Board of Trustees, staff and consumers of ICD-International Center for the Disabled note with deep sadness the passing of Posy (Rose), beloved wife of our Honorary President, Jeremiah Milbank, Jr. Gracious, warm-hearted and generous, Posy Milbank shared in Jeremiah Milbank's commitment to ICD and his support of its mission. She will be greatly missed by all who knew her, and we offer our deepest sympathy to Jeremiah Milbank, Jr. and to all members of the family. Harry G. Hohn Chairman of the Board of Trustees ICD-International Center for the Disabled
