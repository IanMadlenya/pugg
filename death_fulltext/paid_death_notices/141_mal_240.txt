
        STAHL-Earl (Buster). Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the passing of Earl Stahl, beloved husband of Shirley and devoted father of Gina and Rabbi Robert Levine, Trudi and Neal Epstein, Eric and Elizabeth Stahl and Marcia and Joseph Rosen. May his memory be a blessing. The funeral will be held in Springfield, Ma. on October 22. Shiva will be observed at the Stahl home, 88 Briarcliff Road, Long Meadow, Ma. and will continue in NYC at Rodeph Sholom on Monday evening October 26 from 6:30 to 8:30PM. We extend our deepest condolences to the entire family. Clergy, Board of Trustees, Staff and Members of Congregation Rodeph Sholom
