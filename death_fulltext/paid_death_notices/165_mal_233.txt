
        SOLOMON-Nathaniel H. October 16th. at Beth Israel Hospital, NYC. Devoted husband of 59-1/2 yrs of Miriam. Beloved father of Shelah and Charles. Loving grandfather of Samuel, Alexa and Molly. His great passion was the practice of pediatrics which he devoted 53 years. He served in General Patton's Third Army in WWII when they marched into Germany. He was a Captain in the Medical Corp, decorated for bravery with a Bronze Star. Member of Queens Pediatric Society. He was a pediatric staff member at Long Island Jewish Hospital, Beth Israel Hospital NY and the New York Hospital of Queens. His optimism was extraordinary and he will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Services 2PM Wednesday at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Blvd at 76 Road, Forest Hills, New York. SOLOMON-Nathaniel H., M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians and staff of the Milton and Bernice Stern Department of Pediatrics in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and former colleague, Nathaniel H. Solomon, M.D. Beginning with his residency in 1937, Dr. Solomon served the patients of Beth Israel with great distinction for more than 50 years, retiring in 1992. To his beloved wife, Miriam, his daughter Shelah, the entire Solomon family and all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, M.D. President and CEO Henry S. Singman, M.D. President, Medical Board Andrew P. Mezey, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Pediatrics Robert L. Salant, M.D. President, Doctors Alumni Assoc.
