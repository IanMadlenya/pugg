
        BLAU-Gina. Known professionally as Shrimathi Gina, outstanding East Indian dancer of NYC, age 92, on October 23rd. Survived by loving sisters, Jeanette Silverman and Gladys Satzman. Service held at home of Ann Aviles, her niece. Memorial Sunday, November 8th, 4PM, at Gallery 678, 678 Broadway (3rd Street) 2nd Floor.
