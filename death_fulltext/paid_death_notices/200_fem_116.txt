
        MENAKER--Esther. Ph.D. 95. Died peacefully in her home on August 20, 2003. She is mourned by her family, as well as by a wide circle of friends, colleagues, students and former patients. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM on Saturday, October 25, 2003, at the New-York Historical Society, 2 West 77th St., New York, NY.
