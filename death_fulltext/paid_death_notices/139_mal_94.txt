
        LAWRENCE-Bernard A. Son of Ada and Morris Cohen; died at home in Elizaville, NY on August 7, 1998 and is survived by his brother Matthew and his beloved family of friends. Bernie was a successful, accomplished commercial photographer in Manhattan. After he retired from commercial photography, he devoted himself to teaching photography at the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan. He was a wood turner and a maker of fine wooden furniture and objects of art. Bernie was creative, talented and skilled in so many wonderful ways. Bernie loved to fish in the wilderness and he loved the Indian and Eskimo peoples. And Bernie, lest we never forget your gourmet cooking, and all our fun dining together. We love you, Bernie. You will always be with us. Graveside service Sunday, August 9 at 11AM at Riverside Cemetery in Saddle Brook, New Jersey.
