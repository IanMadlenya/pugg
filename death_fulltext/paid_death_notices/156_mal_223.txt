
        ROSE-O. Alan. The Lenox Hill Hospital, Department of Medicine mourns the loss if its esteemed colleaque O. Alan Rose, MD, a member of the Department since 1941. Dr. Rose served as the Chief of the Cardiac Clinic for years, was a clinical investigator of cardiac drug therapy and an active member of our teaching faculty. A gifted and compassionate physician, his devotion to his many patients was unwavering. He leaves behind a legacy of service to this Department and its programs in patient care and medical education. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Frances B.Rose, MD, and his family. Michael S. Bruno, MD Director/Medicine Ira R. Hoffman, MD Associate Director/Medicine
