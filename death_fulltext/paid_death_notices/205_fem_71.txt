
        WURMFELD -- Esther Wit zling, 89, died peacefully at home Friday, February 6th. Devoted widow of Charles J. Beloved mother of Sanford and the late Michael. Cherished mother-in-law of Rella and Hope. Adored grandma of Charles, Eden, Jeremy, Treva, Jason and Justin. A lifelong New Yorker, graduated Hunter High '31, Hunter College '35. A much appreciated NYC public school teacher. She made her silver screen debut in the 2002 film, ''Kissing Jessica Stein.'' Her life inspired love, knowledge, and laughter and will be celebrated at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91 St., Monday, February 9th, 12 noon. The family will also be receiving visitors at 18 Warren St., Tuesday, February 10th, 6:30-9:30 PM.
