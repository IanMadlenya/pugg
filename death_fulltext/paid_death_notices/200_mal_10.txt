
        SILBERMAN--Sidney J. The Officers, Board, staff and students of Columbia/Barnard Hillel offer condolences to the family of our devoted Board member. Sidney was a passionate advocate for Columbia Jewish life for three decades. A Founder of our Kraft Center, he cared deeply for his Alma Mater and Hillel. His gift of a Torah in honor of Rabbi Sheer will be read for generations. Our sympathies to Judy, Uri and children, his loving companion, Helene Fagin, and the entire family. Ruth E. Horowitz Richard E. Kobrin Co-Presidents Earle W. Kazis Chairman of the Board Arthur Ochs Sulzberger Chairman, Exec. Committee Joanne Ben-Avi Director of Development Charles H.
