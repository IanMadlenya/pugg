
        PATTERSON -- Ellmore C. The Boards of Overseers and Managers and staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center and Boards of Managers of Memorial Hospital for Cancer and Allied Diseases and of the SloanKettering Institute for Cancer Research record with sorrow the passing of Ellmore (Pat) C. Patterson, Member Emeritus of the Center's Board of Overseers. Mr. Patterson began his service on Memorial's governing body in 1951, when he was elected to the Board of Managers of Memorial Hospital, and stayed active until his retirement in 1993. He served as Treasurer of Memorial Hospital, as Chairman of the Finance Committee and of the Investment Committee for many years. Upon his retirement, he was named to the Board of Overseers Emeriti. Mr. Patterson will be remembered for his advice and leadership in establishing important programs to ensure the financial base of the institution. Mr. Patterson was a valued friend, colleague, and enthusiastic supporter of Memorial Sloan-Kettering's mission, and we extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. James D. Robinson III Honorary Chairman Douglas A. Warner III Chairman Richard I. Beattie Vice Chairman Louis V.
