
        KREMENTZ-Robert Middleton, formerly of New Vernon, NJ, died November 15, 1997, at Hilton Head Island, S.C. He retired in 1974 as Chairman of Krementz & Co in Newark, NJ. He was a member of the New York Yacht Club and the Cruising Club of America. A memorial service will be held at First Presbyterian Church of New Vernon, 4:00 PM, Tuesday, November 25. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to The Newark Museum. Attn: Mary Sue Sweeney Price, 49 Washington Street, Newark, NJ 07101.
