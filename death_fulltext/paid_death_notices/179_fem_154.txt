
        HART-Joan Johnson. Daughter of Richard O. Johnson and Jean F. Holt. Born February 28, 1918 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Wife of the late Thornley W. Hart. Beloved mother of Richard M. (Susan), Thornley A. Hart (Barbara), Angela H. Morris and her grandchildren, Nina, Edward Morris, Richard A., Nicholas, Kelsey and Gaelen Hart, and Thornley F. Hart. Died peacefully at home December 21. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021.
