
        SPIELMAN-Gerald M., M.D. It is with great sadness that Congregation Beth Elohim mourns the sudden passing of its recent president, Gerald Spielman. For five years, ''Doc Jerry'' led the congregation with dedication and caring. His commitment to Jewish learning was an inspiration to all. As a pediatrician, he was devoted to his patients and to his profession. We extend our deepest sympathies to Phyllis, Jocelyn and Loren as well as to his parents and brothers. We have lost a dear and wonderful friend. Services Wednesday, August 18, 11AM at Congregation Beth Elohim, 8th Avenue and Garfield Place, Brooklyn NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Congregation's Kadima Fund. Gerald I. Weider, Rabbi Paul Levy, President
        SPIELMAN-Gerald, M.D. The Department of Pediatrics at New York Presbyterian Hospital-Weill Medical College of Cornell University was saddened to learn of the untimely death of Dr. Spielman. He has been a valued member of our Department since 1966 serving as an intern, resident, chief resident, fellow and faculty member. He was highly respected and admired by students, residents and attendings. We extend our condolences to his wife, Phyllis, and to his children, Loren and Jocelin. We will miss his thoughtful and warm presence and support. Maria I. New, M.D., Chairman Department of Pediatrics
