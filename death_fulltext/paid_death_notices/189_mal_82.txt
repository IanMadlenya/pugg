
        FLANAGAN-John D. Born in Washington D.C. on September 21, 1918. Died peacefully at home on October 5, 2002. Attended college at Rutgers University, veteran of WWII 4th Armored Division and retired Chairman of the Board of Authenticolor, Inc. Survived by his wife Kathleen, his children Christopher and Karen and four grandchildren. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Monday, 7-9 PM, and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Avenue at 66th Street, Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment private.
