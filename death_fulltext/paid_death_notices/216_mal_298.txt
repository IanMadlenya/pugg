
        HOFFMAN--Jerome, 80, of Port Washington, NY, suddenly at home, on Sunday, January 16. Born 1924 in Manhattan, to the late Lewis and Lillian Hoffman. World War II veteran, devoted bridge player, retired EDP manager, Forbes Inc. Mourned by his wife Judith, sons John of Mountain View, CA, Matt of Walpole, MA, Dan of Victoria, British Columbia, and Gabriel of Kettering, OH, and seven grandchildren. Interment was at Nassau Knolls Cemetery. Contributions in his name to the Salvation Army or Brave Hearts of St. Francis Hospital, Roslyn, NY, would be greatly appreciated.
