
        SAXON-Florence (nee Bloom). Age 81. Of North Miami Beach, FL. on August 30, 2001. Beloved and loving wife of Bernard Saxon for 61 years. Cherished mother & mother-in-law of Michele Saxon of Manhattan and Stephanie and Sam Levine of Aventura, Florida. Devoted grandmother of Erica and Andrea Levine. Until her retirement, she taught Home Economics at The Wheatley School in Old Westbury, New York. Services were held on September 2, 2001. ''...a lovely light.''
