
        KISTER-Dr. Sven J. The members of the Department of Surgery of the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University and the Surgical Service of the Presbyterian Hospital mourn the passing of our long term esteemed colleague and friend. He spent all his professional life at our institution and leaves a host of peers, students, patients and friends who benefited from his personal and compassionate care of patients, his respected surgical skills and his loyalty and support of his colleagues. Our deepest sympathy goes to his wife Christina, his children Karl, and Karin, his brother Sigurd and his grandchildren Katherine and Elizabeth. Eric A. Rose, MD, Chairman and members of the Dept. of Surgery Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center
