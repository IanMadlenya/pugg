
        WERBIN - Rhea. Passed away August 27. At the age of nine, after the violent deaths of her mother and siblings in the pogroms, she escaped Russia with her sister, Gussie. Arriving in the U.S., she lived with friends and also confronted language barriers. Despite these obstacles and an era that was not conducive she became a successful businesswoman and manufacturer while raising two children alone. After running ''Baby Headwear'' for many years, she established a plant in the Dominican Republic. Upon retiring she worked for the NYC Dept. of Social Services assisting the elderly.
