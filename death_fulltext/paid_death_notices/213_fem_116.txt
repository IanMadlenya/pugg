
        BRITTON--Edna, 94, on October 13. Beloved daughter of the late Alfred and Emily Britton of Bronxville, and sister of the late A. Dudley Britton, Jr. of Pleasantville, after a long illness at the Brandywine Nursing Home in Briarcliff Manor, NY. Miss Britton is survived by her nephew Peter Britton, of Pleasantville. Donations may be made in her name to NRDC.
