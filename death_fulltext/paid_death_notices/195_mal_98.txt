
        WOLLENBERGER - Gerard, 71, on April 8, 2003, after a sudden short illness. Distinguished member of the New York bar for more than four decades. Survived by his brother, Lutz Wollenberger, his sister-in-law, Eva Wollenberger, his nephew, Neal Wollenberger (Ruby) and his nieces, Tina Wollenberger Calderon (Rafael) and Linda Wollenberger. Graveside service at 1 PM Friday, April 11, 2003 at Old Montefiore Cemetery, St. Albans, Queens, NY (718-528-1700).
