
        HERZOG-Grace Feder. On Thursday, November 26, 1998, Grace Feder Herzog, beloved wife of the late Sol A. Herzog, extraordinary mother of Richard and Robert Herzog, devoted aunt to Bruce and Madeleine Sidelnik, loving mother-in-law to Barbara and Margot, adoring grandmother to Jeffrey Herzog, Kari AlperovitzBichell, Stuart Hunt, Stephanie Hunt Ma, great-aunt to Katharine Sidelnik, great-grandmother to Hendrik Moes Ma. She encompassed all the dimensions of love. Services will be Monday, November 30, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave., NYC. Memorial contributions may be made to the Muhlenberg Branch of the New York Public Library through the Library Friends Office, 212-930-0778.
