
        STAHL-Pearl. The Board of Directors and staff of the Jewish Foundation for the Righteous mourn the passing of Pearl Stahl, one of our great stalwart volunteers and the mother of our Executive Director Stanlee Stahl. Pearl worked tirelesly to improve the world in which we live, and we will miss her smile, widsom and boundless energy. Harvey Schulweis, Chairman Paul Goldberger, President, Jewish Foundation of the Righteous
