
        ARTSIS-Mildred Storch. Of Great Neck, N.Y. and Boynton Beach, FL. Died November 17, 1997. Loving and devoted wife of the late Jerome M. Artsis; beloved mother of Leslie Artsis Adams of West Palm Beach, FL., and Steven M. Artsis of New York City. Adored grandmother of Michael Artsis. Caring sister and sister-in-law of Clifford and Doris Storch of Lake Worth, FL., and Shirley and Arnold Friedman of Roslyn, N.Y. Favorite aunt to Marilyn and Bob Schoenberger; Adele and Neil Bayer; Dana and Ira Effron; Cherie and Bruce Bramley; Loren and Robert Gelber; Jeffrey and Joan Grossman; Stephanie and Kenny Lichtman; Sally Storch Cooney; and Sam and Robin Storch. Mother-inlaw to Doris Mele, Glenda Artsis, and Kathryn Scott. Devoted fiancee to Alan Brandt of Great Neck, N.Y. and Boca Raton, FL. Gracious and elegant, this beautiful dignified lady inspired us all through her great courage, sense of humor and zest for life. Considerate, kind and generous, she was always there for her family and friends. A contributor to multiple charities, she served as a past-president of Womens American Ort, Great Neck Chapter. Mildred, we will remember and miss you forever. You will always be in our hearts. Funeral services will take place Wednesday, November 19, 1997, at 9:30 AM, at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, N.Y. A memorial service to be announced, will be held at Temple Emanuel in Palm Beach, FL. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
        ARTSIS-Mildred. We share with Shirley and Clifford the loss of their cherished sister and our dear friend. Jean and Jack, Charlotte and Norty
        ARTSIS-Mildred. Mildred dear, Blanchie and I loved you. Leo Hausman
