
        ADAMS-Winifred Beach. Born June 3, 1908 died February 6, 2002. Great-granddaughter of Alfred Ely Beach, inventor of the first New York subway. Devoted and loving mother and friend. She had a special excitement and love of life that was hers alone. Precious memories will linger with all who knew her. She was preceded in death by her husbands, Leonard Martin and Walter Adams, grandson Scott Campagna, and greatgrandson Kenneth Fahle. Survived by her daughter Winifred Mae Campagna and son Stan Martin. Grandchildren include Frank Campagna, Leonard Martin and Wendy Fahle, and great-grandchildren Frank A. and Amber Campagna, Scott and Erin Fahle, Perry and Eric Martin. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., Saturday, February 9th, 2-5 and 7-9PM and Sunday 7-9 P.M. with service Sunday 2 PM.
