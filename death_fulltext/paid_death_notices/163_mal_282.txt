
        CLARK-Brother Bartholomew Albert, F.S.C., 80, of De La Salle Hall, Lincroft, NJ on Aug. 24, 2000. Brother Albert was the founder of Theta Alpha Kappa-the National Honor Society of Religious Studies/Theology and served as President from 1977-2000. His assignments included De La Salle Institute, St. Bernard's School, Bishop Loughlin High School, De La Salle College, and Manhattan College. He is survived by a sister, Dorothy McCarthy and was predeceased by a brother, John. Calling hours will be held at De La Salle Hall, Lincroft, NJ on Friday from 2:004:00 and 6:30 to 7:30 PM. The Funeral Liturgy will be celebrated at De La Salle Hall on Friday at 8:00 PM. A Committal service will be held on Saturday at 10:30 AM at De La Salle Hall followed by interment at St. Gabriel's Cemetery, Malboro, NJ. Memorial donations to the De La Salle Nursing Development, care of De La Salle Hall, 810 Newman Springs Rd., Lincroft, NJ 07738 would be appreciated.
