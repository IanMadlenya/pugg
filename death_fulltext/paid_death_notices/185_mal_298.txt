
        DRAKE-Frederick Charles II. Was born in Taft, CA on January 28, 1958. In 1993 he founded the Rancho de la Luna recording studio in Joshua Tree, CA. A resident of Houston for many years, Fred is survived by his beloved mother and father, Josephine and Charles, his sisters Melanie and Deidre, his nieces Taylor, Jordan and Kennedy, and his horse and dear companion Kashmir. On Thursday June 20, Fred died as he lived-with grace and dignity, surrounded by friends in his home in Joshua Tree.
