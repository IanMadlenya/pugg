
        NIE-Ming (Yo). We mourn his sudden passing on January 21st. Beloved husband of Sim Seto and father of sons Nicholas, Norman, Herman. Born Oct. 7, 1924 in Canton China, W.W. II survivor, came to U.S. in 1952 to fulfill dreams of family, academics, career. Artistically gifted, yet a modest, kind hearted, private man. Always made time to serve his community. He will be greatly missed. Services at Cheung Sang Funeral, 22 Mulberry St, NYC, 10013, on January 29, 3-7pm & January 30, 10am
