
        VOGEL - Charles. Beloved husband of the late Esther Vogel. Father, grandfather, brother and friend. Died suddenly in the company of friends, April 22, 2003. He is survived by three sons and spouses. Jeffrey and Brenda, Carl and Valarie, Peter and Maureen. Grandchildren Michael, Lucas, Sarah, Zachary, Jordan, Alexis and Michael. His vision of social and economic justice will continue to inspire us. Donations in his memory can be made to radio station WBAI-FM.
