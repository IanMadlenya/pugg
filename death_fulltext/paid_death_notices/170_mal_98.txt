
        STRACKA-Peter Jr. Of Somers, N.Y. and Bonita Springs, FL passed away on March 7 in Florida. He had been a longtime resident of Sleepy Hollow and Ardsley. He was born in Johnson City, NY to Peter and Helen Stracka on May 16, 1934 and was a graduate of Cornell University where he was a member of Theta Xi Fraternity. He later served in the U.S. Army in Germany. He was an owner and president of Baitinger Electric Co., Inc. and had also been employed by Graybar Electric Co. and Kennedy Electric Co., all of New York City. He was a member of the New York Building Congress, the Independent Electrical Contractors Assn., the Eastern Electrical Wholesalers Assn. and the Jovian Electric Club. At the time of his death he was a member of Heritage Hills Country Club and Spanish Wells Country Club. Previously he had been a member of Sleepy Hollow Country Club and Leewood Golf Club. He is survived by his wife, the former Joan (Joby) Kelly and two children, Peter Stracka III of Hawthorne and Kristin Stracka McSpedon of Ridgefield, CT, two grandchildren, Ryan and Julia McSpedon and a sister, Dorothy Rosinsky of Binghamton, NY. Friends may call at the Dwyer Funeral Home, Tarrytown on Sunday, March 11 from 2-4 pm and 79pm. A funeral Mass will be held at Transfiguration Church, Tarrytown at 11:30 AM on Monday, March 12 followed by interment at Sleepy Hollow Cemetery. In lieu of flowers the family has requested donations to the HCM Program of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital c/o Mark Sherrid, M.D., 1000 10th Avenue, Room 3B30, New York, New York 10019.
