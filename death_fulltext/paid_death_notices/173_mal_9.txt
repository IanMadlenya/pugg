
        SILVIAN-Slomo. Born in Romania, died peacefully May 31, 2001. Loving husband of Cindy. Devoted brother to Butsa and her huband Herman, who reside in Israel. Also devoted uncle and great-uncle. He was very instrumental in forming an adult home association. He was an ardent supporter and generous benefactor for many years for organizations in America and Israel. We extend our deepest and most sincere sympathy at the loss of a man who was a caring friend to all. Everyone who knew him was blessed with his warmth and affection. He will be greatly missed. Funeral service will be held at Gutterman's, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, Sunday, June 3rd at 11 AM.
