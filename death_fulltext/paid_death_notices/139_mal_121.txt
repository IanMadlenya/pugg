
        QUINN-Paddy. On August 8, 1998. It is a sad day with the passing of Paddy Quinn for Sheila and all those who knew and loved him. He will be so missed forever in our hearts. Funeral mass at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84th Street, Wednesday, 10 A.M. Burial to follow at St. Raymond's Cemetery, Bronx, New York.
