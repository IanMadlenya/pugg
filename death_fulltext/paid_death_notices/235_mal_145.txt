
        WIENER--Marvin, 70, passed away Saturday, August 12. He is survived by his beloved wife Barbara, brother-in-law Dick Richards, and his wife Hilke Richards, his niece and three nephews, his cousins Elinor and Doreen. He was Founder, Chairman, and CEO of Marvin Wiener LTD for over 40 years. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the Queens Center for Progress 81-15 164th St, Jamaica, NY 11432, 718-380-3000. Funeral services Tuesday August 15, 9:30am, Schwartz Brothers Funeral Home 114-03 Queens BlvD, corner of 76th Rd, Forest Hills, NY.
