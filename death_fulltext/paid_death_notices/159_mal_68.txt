
        BOTTS-John C. of St. Petersburg, FL and formerly of Bronxville, NY, on March 26, 2000. Husband of Priscilla F. Botts for 60 years, and father of John, Ted, and Priscilla Ditchfield. Grandfather of six and great-grandfather of one. John spent thirty years in New York City as advertising and marketing executive and was a champion squash racquets player at The Princeton Club of NYC. He was an active community volunteer with SCORE and St. Anthony's Hospital in St. Petersburg. He was a true gentleman who will be missed.
