
        ELLIOTT-Barbara B. The Board of Directors of the Indpendence Savings Bank extend their deepest sympathy to fellow director Donald H. Elliott and his family on the loss his wife Barbara Burton Elliott. This remarkable woman will be remembered for her love of her family, her commitment to the education of the young people of Brooklyn through Il Piccolo Teatro dell Opera and her enduring friendship with the Indpendence family. We will miss her. Board of Directors, Independence Savings Bank
        ELLIOTT-Barbara. The Mary McDowell Center for Learning extends its condolences to our Trustee and friend, Donald Elliott on the death of his beloved wife. Edward G. Doty, Clerk, Board of Trustees Debbie Zlotowitz, Head of School
