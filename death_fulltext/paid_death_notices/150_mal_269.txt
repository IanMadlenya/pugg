
        DUNIETZ-Joseph. Holocaust survivor and hotelier is being mourned by the extended Dunietz family. Born March 20, 1917, Joseph is survived by his beloved wife, Edith, and his two children, Jack Dunietz and Gail Maloff. He was known affectionately as ''Papa'' to his four loving grandchildren, Jared, Jason, Joshua & Alana. He is also survived by numerous brothers, sisters, nieces & nephews. Funeral services will be held on Friday morning, July 16th at 10AM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St., NYC.
        DUNIETZ-Joseph. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member, beloved husband of Edith, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi, David Yagoda, Chairman, Dr. William Schwartz, President
        DUNIETZ-Joseph. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Men's Club mourn the passing of the beloved father of their devoted member Dr. Jack Dunietz and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family.
