
        KLEIN--Howard B. Age 78, died December 27 after an accident Christmas day while skiing in his beloved New Hampshire. He is survived by devoted wife of 55 years, the artist Marion, their children Amanda, Matthew and Josh, his sister Lila and his sons' wives Dr. Debra Adler-Klein and Beatrice Weinberger and eight grandchildren, Max and Alice Gottesman, Josh, Rachel, Samantha, Will, Theo and Augusta Klein. Funeral was private. Contributions can be made in memory of Howard to: Stuyvesant/CCNY Fund at The City College of NY, 138th St. & Convent Ave, S154, NYC, 10031.
