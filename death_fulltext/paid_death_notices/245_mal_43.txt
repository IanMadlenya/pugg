
        WEINSTEIN--George W., M.D. 72. Formerly the Jane McDermott Shott Professor and Chair, Department of Ophthalmology, Robert C. Byrd Health Sciences Center, West Virginia University, Professor and Chair, Division of Ophthalmology, University of Texas Health Sciences Center, San Antonio, Texas. He was a past president of the American Academy of Ophthalmology, former Regent of the American College of Surgeons and faculty member of the Wilmer Eye Institute, Johns Hopkins University. He is survived by his wife, Sheila, his son and daughter-in-law Bruce and Kristen, his daughters, Elizabeth and Rachel, two grandchildren, Carlee and Sammy, a sister, Barbara and brother-in-law, Daniel. Donations may be made in his memory to the Neurosciences Center for Dementia Research, Mayo Clinic Development Office, 4500 San Pablo Road, Jacksonville, FL, 32224.
