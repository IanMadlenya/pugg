
        DOHERTY--Marna O'Mara. Age 53, on November 11, 2006 after a courageous battle against colon cancer. Known as Dia, she is survived by husband Regis Blodgett, sons Justin, Morgan and Jared. Siblings Lisa Greene, Meghan, Sarah and Gregory Doherty. Predeceased by brother Sean. She radiated joy with humor and love to all who knew her.
