
        LEE-John F., in his eighty-seventh year on June 1, 2000, of Westbury, NY formerly of Manhattan. Retired from New York Life Insurance Co. Beloved son of the late Patrick J. and Annie Lee. Adoring husband of the late Helen K. Lee. Devoted father of Dorrit, Barbara, and Cathleen. Proud grandfather of Joshua. Dearest brother of the late Daniel, Cathleen, George T., and Anne C. Lee. Cherished brother-in-law of Florence Lee. Loving Uncle of George T., Patrick, Stephen, and Anne Lee. Fond cousin of Patrick Lee of Castleconnell, Ireland, and Sr. Eileen Lee of Murroe, Ireland. Reposing at Donohue Funeral Home, 293 Castle Avenue, Westbury Sunday between the hours of 2-4 & 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial on Monday 10 AM. Interment Mount St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, New York.
