
        PITTMAN--Sample Noel. Of New York, NY. Died Tuesday, October 5, after a long illness. Noted collector of slavery artifacts, counselor and professor at Borough of Manhattan Community College, he was 79. Beloved father of Nicole, Sample Jr., Ava and Nicholas. Husband of Carol. Former husband of Vivian. Services at 4 pm, viewing 10 am-4 pm, on Wednesday, October 13, at Benta's Funeral Home, 630 St Nicholas Ave, at 141st St.
