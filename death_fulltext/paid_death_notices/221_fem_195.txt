
        BREININ--Rose-Helen, 77, of New York City, died June 25. Beloved wife for 58 years of Dr. Goodwin Breinin. Loving mother of Bartley Breinin & Constance Paton. Devoted mother-in-law of Rachel Breinin and Nigel Paton. Adored grandmother of Alexander, Sarah, Caroline and Emma. Art historian and collector, her volunteer work at The Metropolitan Museum of Art contributed to numerous exhibitions. Funeral services on Thursday, June 30, 11:30AM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to The Metropolitan Museum of Art, Department of Drawings and Prints. Please indicate that donations are for acquisition of prints.
