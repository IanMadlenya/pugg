
        OKINAKA -- Arthur Junichi. Died on April 21, 2005. He was the widower of Gladys J. Okinaka and is survived by his two daughters Alison Joan Okinaka and Leslie Okinaka Juan. He was brother to Richard Okinaka and grandfather of three, Corey Arthur Juan, Alexander Mills Juan and Avery Katherine Juan. He received his medical degree from the University of Chicago in 1954 and was an Associate Professor of Surgery at Weill Medical College of Cornell University and a practicing thoracic surgeon at New York Presbysterian Hospital from 1961 through 1989. He was a dedicated physician and a devoted husband and father. He will always be remembered for his intellect, his drive and his tremendous sense of humor. We will miss him.
