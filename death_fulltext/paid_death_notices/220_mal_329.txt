
        PERRIN--Forrest G. Pianist, musical director, and society orchestra leader, died of pneumonia on Friday, May 27th, 2005, in New York City at the age of 88. He is survived by his loving wife Lesley Davison Perrin; two children, Wendy Perrin Baker and Christopher Scott Perrin; two grandchildren, Charlie Baker and Douglas Baker; and sister Margaret Perrin of Atlanta, his former two-piano-team partner on ABC Radio's Piano Playhouse. Everybody loved him for his kindness, good nature, quick wit, and wonderful talent. Contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
