
        LEEF-Sylvia Tonner. Who lived to do for others. Died in her sleep on April 23, 2000. She was 93. Beloved wife of the late Freddie Leef. Loving mother of Ellen Leef and Michael Sherrow and the late Nettie Leef Mack. Doting Nana of Rachel and Gaby. Graveside service on Tuesday, 10:30AM at Mount Hebron Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory to New York Alzheimer's Association of Brookdale Center for Aging. Family will be receiving at Ellen and Michael's until Wednesday evening.
