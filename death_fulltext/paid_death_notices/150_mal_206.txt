
        SEIBERT-William L. Of Miami, Florida, formerly of Bronxville, New York, died on July 8, 1999 in Charlottesville, VA. Born June 27, 1905 in Brooklyn, NY. He was the son of the late William Hill Seibert and Bernice Eugenia Higbe. He was preceded in death by his wife Marjorie Fell Seibert. Mr. Seibert was a graduate of St. Paul's School in Garden City, NY, Princeton University and Fordham Law School. He was a member of the Sons of the Revolution; the Princeton Club; past president of St. Andrews Golf Club and past president of the US Men's Curling Assoc. He was a member of the 7th Regiment National Guard NYC. Survivors include a son William Hill Seibert II and his wife, Joyce Rockhill Seibert of Miami, Florida, two stepchildren Marcia Darlington of Short Hills, NY, and Dr. Harrison McMichael of Paoli, PA, grandchildren William Lincoln Seibert II and his wife Teresa Steele Seibert of Oregon City, OR, Kimberly Ariane Seibert of New York, NY and Chantal Seibert of Boston, MA, and two great grandchildren Arionne Marie Seibert and Alexander Lincoln Seibert of Oregon City, OR. Interment occurred at Friends Cemetery in Old Westbury, Long Island, New York.
