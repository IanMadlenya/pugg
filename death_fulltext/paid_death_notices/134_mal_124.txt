
        BOUDREAU - Laurence Allan Giles. Of Ridgefield, CT formerly of Greenwich Village, New York at the age of 62. Survived by his wife; Carol (nee Emerling) Boudreau. He was the son in law of the late Ernest and Helen Louise Emerling of Great Neck, New York, and Dayton, Ohio. A memorial mass of Christian Burial will take place Saturday at 12 noon in St. Mary's R.C. Church, Ridgefield, Conn. Contributions in Mr. Boudreau's memory may be made to the Ridgefield Library, 472 Main Street, Ridgefield, CT. 06877.
