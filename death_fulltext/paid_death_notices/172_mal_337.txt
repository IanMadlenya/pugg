
        STANGER-Wesley A. Jr. On Wednesday, May 23, 2001, age 92 of Summit, NJ. Husband of Natalie Williams Stanger and the late Sarah McKee Stanger. Father of William M. Stanger of Summit, NJ and Robert A. Stanger of Little Silver, NJ. Brother of Rhys L. Stanger of Jacksonville, Fl. Survived by 7 grandchildren and 7 great grandchildren. Relatives and friends are kindly invited to attend the memorial service at the Brough Funeral Home, 535 Springfield Ave (corner of Morris Ave), Summit, NJ on Saturday, June 16th at 3:00 PM. Please omit flowers, but a donation to the University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, in his memory would be appreciated.
