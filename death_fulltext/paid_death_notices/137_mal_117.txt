        GIBBS-Stanley L., D.D.S. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center deeply mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague, Stanley L. Gibbs, D.D.S., Assistant Clinical Professor of Dentistry and Chief of Orthodontics. Recognized for both his clinical skills and compassion for patients during nearly five decades of active practice, Dr. Gibbs was also a gifted teacher who contributed significantly to the growth and development of Mount Sinai's School of Dentistry. He will be remembered most fondly and he will be sorely missed. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife, Emily, his daughter, Betsy, his sons, Alan M. Gibbs, D.D.S. and Eric P. Gibbs, D.D.S., who serve on our faculty, and to the entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, MD, President Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President Alan L. Schiller, M.D. President, Medical Board
        GIBBS-Dr. Stanley. The Department of Dentistry of the Mount Sinai Medical Center mourns the untimely passing of our long-time colleague and dear friend. He played a key role as Chief of Orthodontics in the successful growth of our department. His cheerful presence and wise counsel will be missed by all of us, and especially by his students. Our deepest sympathy to his family. Jack Klatell Professor and Chairman
