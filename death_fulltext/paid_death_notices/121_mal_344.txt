
        SHANKER-Albert. The Board of Directors of the Health Insurance Plan of Greater New York mourn the loss of Albert Shanker, president of the American Federation of Teachers. Our heartfelt condolences go to his family and colleagues in recognition of his many years of educational leadership in the local, national and international arenas. Anthony L. Watson President & CEO
        SHANKER-Albert. The United College Employees of FIT deeply mourn the passing of our friend and leader, Albert Shanker. Al Shanker had been at the forefront of improving the quality of education in the U.S. Under Al's leadership, the quality of life of all teachers has improved. We will miss him profoundly.
