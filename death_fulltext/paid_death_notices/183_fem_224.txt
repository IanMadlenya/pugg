
        RICHARDSON - Dolores Hoyle, formerly of New York City, died on April 20 after a brief illness. Born in 1914, Mrs. Richardson was formerly a landscape architect with the NYC Department of Parks, a member of the ASLA and a parishioner of St. James Church. She is survived by a daughter, Pilar Schmidt, and two grandchildren. A family memorial service is planned. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Francis Hospital, 114 Woodland Street, Hartford, Connecticut 06105.
