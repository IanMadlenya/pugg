
        SCHEIN-Rudolph. On June 23 1997. Born in Vienna, Austria in 1913, came to the US in 1946. Devoted husband of the late Beatrice Schein. Beloved uncle of Sam and Wini Schein, the late Lilian Schein, Susan and Izhak Ifrach, Jay and Cheryl Perlstein. Dear brother-inlaw of Selma and the late Arthur Perlstein. Southern sales representative for Weiner and Bauer for over 35 years. Good friend to many, loved by all who knew him.
