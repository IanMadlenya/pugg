
        SEIDER-Julia L. in her 92nd year passed away of natural causes on November 15, 1999 at Hospice By The Sea in Boca Raton, FL. The widow of Morris Seider, she was the cherished mother of Norman Seider and Paula Seider Solomon and the beloved grandmother of Mitchell Seider, Shari Jacobson, Phillip Manketo and Jason Manketo and the great grandmother of William and Charles Seider. She was a distinguished attorney for 60 years, a true pioneer for women in the legal profession in Suffolk county, where she served as the President of the County's Bar Association. She was a brave and beautiful lady who will live in the hearts of her loved ones forever. Contributions may be made to Hospice By The Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL 33486. Service Thursday November 18, 1999 10:00 A.M. Gutterman's Inc. 8800 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY, (516-921-5757).
