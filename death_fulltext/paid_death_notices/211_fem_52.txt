
        PRIESTLEY--Justine Tyrrell Smadbeck. We mourn the passing of Justine Priestley. Her devotion to the welfare of children and to the dignity of all people inspired all who were privileged to know her. Our condolences to her husband Bob and to her children Arthur, Louis, David and Paul Smadbeck. Virginia Sloane, Peter Sloane, Gail Meyers
