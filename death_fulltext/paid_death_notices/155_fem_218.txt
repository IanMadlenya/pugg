
        KRAMER-Irwin Hamilton. On December 20, 1999. Beloved husband of Terry Allen, father and grandfather. Funeral Thursday, December 23, 10 A.M., at Temple EmanuEl, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
        KRAMER-Irwin. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of New York-Presbyterian Hospital, Joan and Sanford I. Weill Medical College of Cornell University, and Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons extend our deepest sympathies to Hospital Trustee Terry Allen Kramer on the death of her beloved husband, Irwin Kramer. Through their exceptional generosity, the Kramer and Allen families have been among the major benefactors of the Columbia Presbyterian and Weill Cornell centers of New York Presbyterian Hospital.
