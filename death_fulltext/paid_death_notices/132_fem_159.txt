
        ABRAMS-Joan Walker. Beloved wife of George. Adored mother of Noel Gahagan Walker and Brooks Walker, and Cherished sister of Ninalee Allen Craig. Died in an automobile accident January 14. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory to your favorite charity would be deeply appreciated by the family. Joan, an expert in Asian art, was known for her generosity, spirit and originality. She never touched anything or anyone without making it or them better. She will be sorely missed.
