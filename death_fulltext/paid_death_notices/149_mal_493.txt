        EVANS-Francis J., Msgr. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of Our Lady of Grace Church, 100-05 159th Ave., Howard Beach, on June 28. Reposing in Casey & Porrello Funeral Home, 100-18 159th Ave., Howard Beach, on Tuesday, June 29, from 7 to 9:30 PM and Wednesday, June 30, from 2 to 4 PM. Mass of Transferral on Wednesday at 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Grace Church on Thursday, July 1, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V.
