
        BEZBORODKO-David. We mourn the passing of our beloved Silver Founder, David Bezborodko. Longtime generous supporters of the Medical Center, David and his late wife, Sarah, personified the values of tzedakah and chesed. May the warm memories of this special individual bring solace to his children, grandchildren, greatgrandchildren and brother and may the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med. Ctr. in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, Pres. Sidney Adler, Treas. Lee Weinbach, Pres., Nat'l Women's Div. Stuart Tauber, Exec. Dir.
        BEZBORODKO-David. The Riverdale Jewish Center extends condolences to Joseph Bezborodko and Denise Verstandig on the loss of their father. May the family be conforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
