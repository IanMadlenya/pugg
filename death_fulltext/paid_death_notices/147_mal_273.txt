
        HAHN-Martin I. The New York Power Squadron announces with sadness the death on April 25th of Past Commander Martin I. Hahn in his 92nd year. Commander Hahn, who had been a member of the New York Squadron for 62 years, will be missed for his leadership and warmth. A memorial service was held Wednesday, April 28 at the Riverside Memorial Chapel. Cdr. Sidney White Cdr.-Elect David Zeller
