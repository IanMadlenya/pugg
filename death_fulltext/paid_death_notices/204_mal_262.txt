
        TURNER--Joel M., 80, died January 15, 2004 after a prolonged illness. Born in New York City but a long time resident of Virginia Beach. He was the son of the late Albert Turner and the Late Pauline Turner and brother of the late Erwin Turner. He is survived by his loving wife, Jackie R. Turner, beloved sons, Paul and Win Turner, cherished daughter, Kim Vaadia and husband Boaz; his brother Robert Turner and wife Rosemary, four adored grandchildren: Stephanie, Andrew, Rebecca, Sara and great-grandson, Connor. He was President of Old Dominion Dairy and Oxford International Financial Corp. and a member of the Cavalier Golf and Yacht Club.
