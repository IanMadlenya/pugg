
        MEIKLE-Thomas H., Jr., MD. The Faculty and Staff of Columbia School of Public Health and the Center for Applied Public Health mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend, Thomas H. Meikle, MD. Dr. Meikle's contribution to training for minority health professionals made a lasting impression on the health of the nation. Dr. Meikle, who both at Cornell and more recently at the Josiah Macy Jr. Foundation, has been a strong advocate for improved health of all people. He will be greatly missed and we send our sincere condolences to his wife, Jacqueline Winterkorn, MD, and children, David and Sarah Meikle, and Elizabeth and Margaret Winterkorn. Allan Rosenfield, MD, Dean Cheryl Healton, DrPH, Div. Head, Sociomedical Sciences Columbia School of Public Health
