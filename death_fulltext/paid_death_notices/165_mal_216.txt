
        SCHLACHET-Peter J. The Eastern Group Psychotherapy Society (EGPS) mourns the death of their esteemed colleague and friend. Dr. Schlachet served as a leader of our professional community. He was a Past President (1989-1991), Book Review Editor and Editor of the EGPS professional journal, GROUP (1986-1997). Dr. Schlachet presented numerous workshops, institutes and papers at EGPS conferences. His gentleness, charm, integrity and wisdom will be missed by all. Felicia Einhorn, DCSW, CGP President Jeffrey Kleinberg, PhD. President-Elect Board of Directors, EGPS
