
        HEGG-The Rev. David P. II. Beloved S.V.P. of the Church Pension Fund and Director of Pastoral Care & Education, January 24, during heart surgery. His outstanding ministry had a profound influence on the vitality of the Episcopal Church and the lives of active and retired clergy around the country. Funeral services held January 29, at St. Peter's, Morristown, NJ, where he is buried with his daughter Kimberly, who predeceased him. He is survived by his dear wife Judith, sons David and Eric and their wives, and four grandchildren. He will be deeply missed by all at C.P.F. and the whole church. The staff and Trustees of the Church Pension Fund
