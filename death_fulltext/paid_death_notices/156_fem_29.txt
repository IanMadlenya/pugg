
        LEVINSON-Sylvia Landy. Age 77. On January 3, 2000. Adored wife for 52 years of Alfred Levinson, mother of Laurie, Marjorie, John, and William, grandmother of Olivia, Cecily, Marisa, Julia, and Daniel. For 15 years, devoted volunteer to In-Touch Networks and the Jewish Guild for the Blind-Like her mother before her, an exemplary soul-Donations in her memory may be made to Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Medical Center. Services Wednesday, January 5th, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
