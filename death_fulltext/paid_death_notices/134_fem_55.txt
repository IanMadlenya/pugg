
        MARKHAM-Linda (Conant). 64, of Barnstable, MA, died March 5 of lung cancer. Born in NYC, she lived with her lifelong friend and husband Emerson in Chappaqua and Bronxville, NY before moving to Cape Cod in 1994. Also surviving are her two children, Leslie and Jeffrey and three grandchildren, Cyrus Brown and True and Finlay Markham. Visiting hours Sunday, March 8, 3-6 PM, Doane, Beal & Ames Funeral Home, 160 W. Main St., Hyannis. Memorial service St. Mary's Church, Barnstable, Saturday, March 21, 3:30 PM. Donations to Friends of Sturgis Library, P.O. Box 606, Barnstable, MA 02630.
