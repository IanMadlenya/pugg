
        BENNETT--John J. Jr. The partners and staff of Brauner Baron Rosenzweig and Klein, LLP are deeply saddened by the loss of our very dear friend and colleague, John J. Bennett, who was counsel to our firm for nearly two decades. Jack was a kind and dignified presence in our office, always ready with the word of comfort or advice. We will miss his friendship, wise counsel and gentle wit and extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Angela (Jean) and to their children and grandson.
