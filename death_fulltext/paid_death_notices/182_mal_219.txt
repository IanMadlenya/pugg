
        GOLD-August. On March 12, 2002 at age 91. Educator, mentor and inspiration to an extended global family. Teacher in NYC high schools and facilities planner at the NYC Board of Education for five decades. Community activist, preservationist, environmentalist, he devoted his later life to the development of affordable housing in Westchester County. Survived by Lillian, his wife of 67 years, his children Carol and Paul, and grandson Erik. Donations may be made to Interfaith Council for Action, Inc., P.O. Box 775, Ossining, New York 10562.
