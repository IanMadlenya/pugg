
        FUREY--Bertram J. Age 84, of Jupiter, Florida, died on Wednesday, January 28, 2004. Bert attended St. Lawrence University before joining the Royal Canadian Air Force and then the U.S. Air Corps as a bomber pilot. He was shot down while on a night mission and spent 22 months as a prisoner of war in Germany. He retired as an executive of Conde Nast publications. Bert was predeceased by his daughter Constance and by two brothers and one sister. He is survived by his loving wife of 58 years, Marcelle, and by daughters Laurette (Drew) Giles, Georgette Furey (Roel) van der Vossen, Marcelle Furey and son Bertram, Jr. (Melina). He is also survived by seven grandchildren and one sister and one brother. To express condolences please make donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407.
