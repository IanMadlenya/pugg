
        RIEGER--Tina. The Board of Trustees of the Mint Theater mourn the loss of their fellow Trustee Tina Rieger, whose time with them was, like her life, all too short, but filled with generosity and thoughtfulness. We extend our deepest condolences to Howard and the family. Geoffrey Chinn, Chairman Jonathan Bank, Artistic Director $(6$)RIEGER--Tina Susan. The Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations mourns the passing of Tina Susan Rieger, beloved wife of our esteemed colleague and friend Howard Rieger, President and CEO of UJC, who worked together on behalf of the Jewish people and the State of Israel. We pray the Rieger family will be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Harold Tanner, Chairman Malcolm Hoenlein, Executive Vice Chairman
