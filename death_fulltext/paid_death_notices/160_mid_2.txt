
        HOROWITZ-Louis L. Died on April 28, 2000 in Aventura, FL. Beloved husband of Claire. Beloved father of Marcia Hochberg & Barbara Greenblatt. Dear father-inlaw of Dr. Fred Hochberg and Philip Greenblatt. Cherished grandfather of Jeffrey and David Greenblatt and Ian Hochberg, Elizabeth Hochberg and her husband Michael Schloff.
