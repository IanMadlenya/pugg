
        FRIEDMAN--Si, 77, died peacefully November 3, 2006 after a long illness. He was an award winning package designer with his own NYC firm for 28 years, and an artist his whole life. He is survived by his wife, Margaret Schuette, son, Mark Friedman, and his pride and joy, grandson James Friedman; brother, Ron Friedman, sisters Doris Slutsky, Shirley Lister, and Ann Race; he was predeceased by daughter Nina Ramsey in 2001. A memorial will be held Sunday December 3 at 12 noon at the Harmonie Club, 4 E. 60th St, NYC. Contributions in his name may be sent to Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Art, 128 N. Broad St, Philadelphia, PA 19102, Attention: Alumni
