
        SITT-Jack. The officers and Board of Trustees of the Magen David Yeshiva (Isaac Shalom Elementary School) and the Magen David Yeshiva (Celia Esses High School) mourn the passing of our devoted and highly respected friend. His generosity helped further the education and preserve the heritage of the Sephardic Jewish world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Sitt family. Our thoughts and prayers are with you at this difficult time. Eddie J. Esses and Steven D. Shamah, Co-Presidents
