
        NELSON-Rabbi Zev K. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in 1939, he served Beth Jacob Congregation in Norwich, CT, and in 1947 assumed the pulpit of Temple Emeth in Newton, MA, becoming rabbi emeritus in 1977. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his children Avi, and Dr. Yona NelsonShulman and Dr. Deena Nelson, and all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi David L. Lieber, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP, The Rabbinical Assembly
