
        PRAGER-Ruth. A very special person passed away today, May 1. She was a loving and caring wife to her late husband Jack. A unique mother to her son Michael. She had been a devoted dutiful daughter and an extraordinary care giving niece. Her greatest pleasure came from her decades long lasting friendships. Ruth's friends meant the world to her, she will truly be missed. Graveside service today, 11 AM at the Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. For information call Schwartz Bros., 718-263-7600.
