
        ELDER-Barbara Jean (Hawkins). January 4, 2003. 73 years of age, of Manhattan. Originally from Portland, OR. A veteran of the Korean War, US Army Nurse Corps. She is survived by her husband of 45 years George, her children Scott, Janet and John and two grandchildren, William and Sierra. There will be a memorial service Sunday, January 12, 4 PM at First Presbyterian Church, Fifth Avenue at 12th St, NYC.
