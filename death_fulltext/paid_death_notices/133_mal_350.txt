
        MILLER-Theodore R. The staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center notes with sorrow the passing of their dear colleague Dr. Theodore Miller, a pioneering surgical oncologist. Dr. Miller joined the staff of Memorial Hospital for Cancer and Allied Diseases in 1947. From 1964 to 1972 he was Chief of the Orthopaedic Service. At the time of his death, Dr. Miller was an Emeritus staff member. Dr. Miller's contributions to treatment and patient care will serve generations of physicians. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Dr. Miller's family. The Staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
