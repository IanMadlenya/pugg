
        COHEN--Dr. Herbert G. The New York Academy of Medicine records with sorrow the death of Dr. Herbert G. Cohen, a devoted Fellow of the Academy for 54 years. A physician of broad interests, active intellect and deep compassion, he concerned himself with the welfare of the sick, the affairs of the Academy of Medicine, and the health of his profession across a long and distinguished career. Jack D. Barchas, M.D. Chairman, Board of Trustees Jeremiah A. Barondess, M.D., President
