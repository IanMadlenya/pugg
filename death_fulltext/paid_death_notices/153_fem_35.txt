
        GOLDSTEIN-Rose. Age 96, in Delray Beach, FL. Beloved wife of the late Jonathan Goldstein, loving mother of Marsha Ozer and mother-in-law of Martin Ozer, devoted mother of Ivy and Craig Zuckerman, Tracey and Scott Schweber, Jennifer and Michael Korten and Jeffrey Ozer. Proud great-grandmother of Sam, Jake, Emily and Henry. Her family will cherish the memory of a warm, sweet and compassionate person who leaves a legacy of love, caring and family devotion.
