
        WEIL--Stephen, an internationally recognized leader and mentor in the field of museum management, died on Tuesday, August 9, 2005, at the Washington Home Hospice. He is survived by his wife, Wendy Luke, and by children Rachel Weil of Ithaca, NY, David Weil of Providence, RI, and Michael Weil of Brooklyn, New York. Steve also leaves four grandchildren: Ariel, Yoni, Tamar, and Benjamin. A memorial service will be scheduled in September. Contributions may be made in Steve's name to one of the following: Washington Home Hospice, Inpatient Unit, 3720 Upton St, NW, Washington, DC 20016; or American Association of Museums, 1575 Eye St, NW, Suite 400, Washington, DC 20005; or Fund for Johns Hopkins Medicine, 100 N. Charles Street, Suite 440, Baltimore, MD. 21201.
