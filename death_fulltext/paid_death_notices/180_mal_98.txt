
        NIMITZ-Chester W., Rear Admiral, age 86, and his wife, Joan Labern Nimitz, age 89, of Needham and Wellfleet, MA, and formerly of Boca Grande, FL, by mutual consent on January 2, 2002 at their home in Needham.
