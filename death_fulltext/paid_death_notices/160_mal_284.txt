
        WINKLER-John, 91, of Rossmoor, Monroe Twp., NJ, passed away Sunday at home. Formerly of Brooklyn and Manhattan. Beloved by his wife of 59 years, Marjorie Mills Winkler; his daughter Nancy Winkler Naftulin; his son-in-law, Lawrence Jeffrey Naftulin; and his granddaughter, Julia McLeod Naftulin. Private interment at Greenwood Cemetery. Memorial Service will be held on Friday, June 16, 2000 noon at the Rossmoor Meeting House, Monroe Twp., NJ
