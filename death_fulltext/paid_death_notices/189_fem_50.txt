
        KARTEN-Julia. The Officers and Board of the American Society for Yad Vashem mourn the passing of Julia Karten. Julia, with her late husband Isidore Karten, Vice Chairman of the American Society, survived as partisans and became leaders in Holocaust Remembrance. They were Benefactors twice at Yad Vashem, of the Valley of the Communities, and the Partisans Panorama. Condolences to their children, Berne and James Bookhamer, Marcia and Ya'akov Toledano and Harry and Danielle Karten, and grandchildren. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Eli Zborowski, Chairman Joseph Wilf, Sam Skura Vice Chairmen American Society for Yad Vashem
