
        STERN-Sanford. On October 9, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Marcia. Loving father of Lisa, Adam and Geoffrey, and father-inlaw of Linda Block and James Ricciardi. Adored son of Laura Stern and the late Jerry Stern, and sonin-law of Betty Fox Maged and Myron Maged. Cherished brotherin-law of Jerry and Marcia Fox, and uncle of Barbara Fox, Jonathan and Kristen Fox, and Erika and Rick Eden. A charming man of incisive intelligence and subtle wit. With his wife, Marcia, he ran Lupu's in Brooklyn with great integrity and style. He loved laughter, good food and his home by the sea. Services Tuesday, October 12, at 12 noon, at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn, NY. Memorials to Cancer Care, Inc., 1180 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY 10036.
