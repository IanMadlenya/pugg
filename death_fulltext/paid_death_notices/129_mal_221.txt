
        GOLDBLATT-Jacob Aaron, 71, of Manasota Key, Florida, died Sunday, October 19 in Englewood Community Hospital. He was the most adored and loved husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his wife Barbara Goldblatt; children Paul Goldblatt, Laura Goldblatt, Tracy Edwards, and Seth Katz; seven grandchildren, all from Mahopac, New York. There will be no services. For information Farley Funeral Home and Crematory, Englewood, FL. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Heart Association, 122 East 42nd St, New York City, NY 10168.
