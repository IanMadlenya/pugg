
        O'BRIEN--Martin G. Of Roxbury, CT, died July 22, 2005. Born in Nyack, NY, March 30, 1957, Martin had a distinguished career as an international advisor to the hotel industry. He was President and CEO of The Page Group, a hotel management firm. His most recent projects included Brazilian Court, Palm Beach, FL, Traymore, Miami Beach, FL; and Georgian Terrace, Atlanta, GA. Martin is survived by his partner of 10 years G. Blane Withers, his mother Eunice Dube Yaffa of Palm Beach, FL, his brother and sister, Mitchell O'Brien of Nyack, NY, and Maris O'Brien Elder of Louisville, KY.
