
        BOLGER--Francis J, 78, actor, director, teacher, and activist, died on June 5, 2006. Mr. Bolger appeared in television, film, and New York theater, receiving great critical acclaim for his performance in the Broadway revival of The Hasty Heart. In Los Angeles, he founded the Group Theater West and served as Associate Professor of Advanced Acting at the Pasadena Playhouse. Mr. Bolger became a leading AIDS activist in Los Angeles, receiving awards from the City of West Hollywood and from AIDS Project Los Angeles for his work. Mr. Bolger was also an active volunteer for the Actors' Fund of America. A memorial service will be held at the Church of St. Paul the Apostle, 2pm, Saturday July 15th.
