
        FALCO--Salvatore V. Resident of NYC, NY passed away on October 18, 2004. Dear husband of Martha Falco; Con Edison retiree. Survived by a devoted stepson and his wife Robert and Marlene Stacer Sr. and a step grandson and his wife Robert and Sandra Stacer Jr. Also survived by a brother and his wife Dominick and Jane Falco, brother Anthony Falco, and five nieces and nephews. Mr. Falco retired from the Surrogates Court, Probate Dept., State of New York. He was a member and former trustee of St. John's Lodge #
