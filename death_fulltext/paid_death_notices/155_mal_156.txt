
        MAIDMAN-William. 95, of Hollywood, FL; formerly of NYC and Great Neck, NY. On Saturday, December 11. Beloved husband of Ada and of the late Aida; father of Richard (Gail), David (Nancy) and Bonnie; cherished grandfather of Patrick (Jacqueline), Mitchel (Arlene), Gregory, Dagny, Randi (Howard) and Wendy; adored great-grandfather of Allison, Beatrice, Harrison and Julia; revered by Evan and Starr and Mark, grandchildren and son by marriage; brother of the late Irving, Moe, Samuel and Lea. Former member of Glen Oaks Country Club, distinguished member of the real estate community, revered and loved by all who were blessed by his long life, humor and sagacity. Services Sunday 1PM at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue and 65th, NYC. Interment at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Queens.
        MAIDMAN-William. Peacefully and comfortably at home in his 96th year following the giving (with his goodbyes) of his blessings gathered to the custody of our maker, our much loved husband and father. So is a good life rewarded. Truly the beauty of his life lives amongst us-a living benediction. Ada and Bonnie Richard and Gail David and Nancy
