
        LEET-Glen F. The Board, staff and members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City note the passing of Glen Leet, beloved husband and partner of Mildren Robbins Leet. Mrs. Leet is a founder of UCP and a persuasive advocate for the rights of individuals with disabilities. In recent years, she and Mr. Leet have committed to the wonderful work of the Trickle Up Program. However, her efforts for UCP will always be cherished. Jack Hausman, Founder Leo Hausman, President UCP/NYC
        LEET-Glen F. I extend my deep condolences to Millie Robbins Leet on the passing of her beloved husband Glen. My late wife Ethel and I always cherished Millie's friendship. Jack Hausman
