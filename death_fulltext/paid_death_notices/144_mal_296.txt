
        BINDER-Harry. Former President of Jaffie Mechanical, Inc. World War II Veteran of Army Engineering Corps-European and Pacific Theatres died suddenly of a heart attack in his home in Glen Cove, N.Y. January 18, 1999. Harry is survived by his wife Nanette, his daughter Dr. Karen BinderBrynes, his son Andrew Binder, his sister Shirley Rosen, brother Sol Binder and granddaughters Elana and Lily Brynes. Services today 1PM at ''Gutterman's'', 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I. Please direct all contributions to American Heart Association.
