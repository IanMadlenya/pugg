
        DUGGAN -- John Charles ''Jack'', 75. Of Boca Raton, Florida, died April 22, 2006. Son of the late Charles and Rose Duggan, Irish immigrants. Devoted husband of Carolyn Mitchell Duggan. Loving father of Charles and John. Father-in-law of Julia Bator. Grandfather of Owen Fox and John Fineas. Brother of Rosemary Collier, Kathleen Gallagher, Kay McGrath and Eileen Napolitano. Graduate of Cardinal Hayes High School, Fordham University and Columbia Law School. Member of the New York Bar Association. He began his legal career in admiralty at Kirlin, Campbell and Keating. He worked with the Office of Equal Opportunity and served on the Stichman Commission. He was Senior International Counsel and Assistant General Counsel at W.R. Grace & Co., retiring in 1995. He was a Commander of Company D-8 of the Pershing Rifles and an Officer in the U.S.A.F. He was a remarkable and beloved gentleman. Friends may call Wednesday, April 26 at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Mass Thursday, April 27 at 10 A.M. at St. Joseph's Church, 87th St (between 1st and York Avenues). In lieu of flowers donations to the American Cancer Society would be appreciated.
