
        SCHOENBERG-Robert E.. 80, of Boca Raton, FL; formerly of Fair Lawn, NJ. Passed away at home on the evening of January 10th. He is survived by his wife of 57 years, Evelyn Lapp Schoenberg; and three sons, Donald of South Toms River, NJ, Stephen of Boca Raton, FL, and Loren of Riverdale, NY. He is also lovingly remembered by his four grandchildren Rebecca, David, Brian, and Katherine. The people who knew him best will always remember him as a loving, caring, gentle man who would not compromise his principles and his beliefs that all people deserve to be treated fairly and without prejudice. Services were held on January 13 in Boca Raton, FL. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to Hospice By-The-Sea, 1531 W. Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL 33431.
