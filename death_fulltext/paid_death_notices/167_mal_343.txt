
        RUBIN-Leonard G. A liaison executive for the nongovernmental organization of the United Nations, former marketing executive and faculty member of NYU School of Continuing Education and F.I.T. He died on December 26, 2000. He was 86, a resident of New York City. He was the son of the late Dr. George Rubin and Erma Weill Rubin. Beloved father of Caryl Lee Rubin Fisher, grandfather of Charles Fisher, brother of Cissy Haas and the late Maurice Rubin, brother-in-law of Albert Haas, Caryl and Robert Magnus, uncle of Michael Haas, Mimi Kucherlapati, Renee Rubin, Jon Rubin and Ronald Rubin. Donations can be made to any U.N. related charity.
