
        KAUFMAN-Victor Charles, 82. Retired Detective/Lieutenant NYPD. Former Com. Officer, Brooklyn South Homicide Squad. USMC, WWII, serving Pacific area and China. Saint John's University Law School, received an LLB. Founder and President of NY Lie Detection Labs. Charter Member of American Polygraph Association, honored with Lifetime Membership Award. On behalf of his profession, he gave many TV interviews. Surviving are his beloved wife, Beverly Hosier Draffin, his son Alfred Kaufman, daughter, Claudette Randazzo, grandsons, John and Robert Randazzo, and step daughters, Rayne Mayer, and Dawne Hentrich. Reposing at Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court Street, Brooklyn. Visiting from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Wednesday and Thursday. Service to be held Thursday evening 7:30 P.M. with the Reverend Niles Blatz, of Grace Church, Brooklyn Heights, in attendance. Interment in the Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
