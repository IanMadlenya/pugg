
        O'LOUGHLIN-Jean M. (Gallant). Of Floral Park, NY, died September 1, 1998. She was predeceased by her husband, Joseph M. O'Loughlin, also of Floral Park. She is survived by five children, Anne, Jean, Noreen, Joellyn and Martin; four grandchildren; and six siblings, Marie Johnson, Francis Gallant, Raymond Gallant, Robert Gallant, Kevin Gallant and James Gallant. Wake at Dalton Funeral Home, Floral Park, New York, Wednesday, September 2. Funeral mass at Our Lady of Victory R.C. Church, Thursday, September 3, at 10:30 A.M. Family requests donations made to Covenant House, New York, in lieu of flowers.
