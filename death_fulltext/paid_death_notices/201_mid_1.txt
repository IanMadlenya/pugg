
        DeVITO--Mary, a Rye resident, died on October 15, 2003 at her home. She was 85. Born in Pittsburgh, PA to Alexander & Mary (Cardillo) Tedesco. Mary worked as a seamtress in the ILGWU. Loving wife of the late Daniel G. Devito. He passed away in 1979. She is the cherished mother of Daniel A. DeVito of Rye and his wife Gina. Devoted grandmother of three. She is predeceased by three sisters, Virginia, Margaret & Nancy. Visitation Thursday 2-4 & 7-9 PM at Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 E. Boston Post Rd. 914-698-5968. Funeral Mass Friday 10:30 AM at Resurrection Church in Rye followed by interment at St. Raymonds Cemetery. Donations are requested to the American Cancer Society.
