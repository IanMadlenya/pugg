
        DALY--Daniel R., 78, former resident of Baldwin, NY, died Sunday, November 9 in Gardner, MA following a long illness. He was the oldest son of Daniel E. and Mary (Murphy) Daly's seven children. A lifelong educator, Mr. Daly taught hundreds of students during his 36-year career as a teacher. He taught English at Lawrence High School, Cedarhurst, NY on Long Island from 1951-68. Mr. Daly received his Bachelor's degree in English and History in 1950 from the University of Massachusetts-Amherst. He received his Master's degree in Education from Harvard University's School of Education, Cambridge, MA in 1951. Mr. Daly, affectionately known as ''Digger,'' saw active duty in the Pacific Theater of World War II as a United States Marine. He leaves his wife of 52 years, Ellen E. (Crahan) Daly of Gardner, three sons, Brian, Daniel, and Christopher, one daughter, Ellen Bell, and five grandchildren. The Funeral Mass will be held Thursday, November 13 at 10:00 A.M. at Holy Spirit Church, 50 Lovewell Street, Gardner.
