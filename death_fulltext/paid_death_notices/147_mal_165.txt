
        ROSS-George. March 1, Palm Beach, FL. Survived by wife, Bette Flamm Ross, and children, John Ross and Susan Gardner Brooks. His career as a producer, publicist and writer included a stint on the NY World-Telegram, a 1944 play, ''Sophie'', WW II USO shows, and as a publicist for many major Broadway shows. Posthumously he will appear on the A&E May 10th ''Barbara Hutton Story.''
