
        MARCUS-Helen. Of Fort Lauderdale, passed away peacefully on Sunday, April 6, 1997. Mrs. Marcus made her home here for the past 22 years, coming from Teaneck, NJ. A devoted wife of the late Herbert Marcus, M.D., she was a beloved mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, sister and friend. ''Amah,'' as Helen was known to all, was mother of Robin (Thomas) Costa, Robert Rein and Barbara (John) Hedman, and mother to Martha (Ron) Krauss, and the late Leslie Marcus. Her sister Alva Scharf, friend Shirley Raybin and sister-in-law Edna Silverman will always remember their dear Helen. She was the adored grandmother of Mark (Aurlie), Debbie (Derek), David, Rachel, Adam and Rebecca. Loving great-grandmother of Liav and Elital, Zachary and Jacob, and caring aunt to her numerous nieces and nephews. She was a member of the I Can Cope Group at Holy Cross Hospital for many years. Aside from being a businesswoman, her primary devotion was to her family. She took pride in their many achievements and loved them all dearly. She will be greatly missed by all. A truly selfless person, when Amah died, everyone cried. Services will be held on Tuesday, April 8, 1997, at 2 PM, at The Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapels, Tamarac, Florida, 954523-5801. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation to the charity of your choice.
