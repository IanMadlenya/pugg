
        COOPER-Muriel Gurdon Kiessling Died at the Honey Hill Rest Home in Norwalk, Connecticut, Sunday, April 5, 1998. She was a resident of New Canaan, Connecticut. Mrs. Cooper was the daughter of the late Calvin Kiessling and Grace Saltonstall Kiessling of New Canaan. She is survived by two sons, Richard Saltonstall Fay of New York City and William Bayard Cooper of Phoenix, AZ, as well as two grandchildren, Lisa and Keith Fay. Mrs. Cooper was active for many years as a volunteer with the New Canaan Historical Society, the Red Cross, Silver Hill Hospital & Inter-Church Driving Service. There will be no funeral service.
