
        BOICHIS-Havim, M.D. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University is profoundly saddened by the passing of Havim Boichis, Professor and Past Dean of the Tel Aviv University Sackler School of Medicine and the incumbent of the University's Lea & Arie PickelPaamoni Chair in Pediatric Nephrology. His colleagues, friends & students, world-wide, will cherish the memory of this remarkable man of great warmth and compassion. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Yola, sons Eli and Ron, and their families. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Bernard Rapoport, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Robert J. Topchik, Pres. Jules Love, Exec. Vice Pres.
