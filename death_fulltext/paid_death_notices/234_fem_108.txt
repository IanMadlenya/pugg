
        LUTZKY--Florence. Passed away peacefully in her 95th year. Beloved sister of Stanley Mitchell and cousin and friend of Anne Tanzman. She will be remembered for her strong will and determination, for having worked as a bookkeeper in the family business for 50 years, and for her devotion and generosity to her family, especially to her cherished nieces and nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews, and great grandniece and great grandnephews. We will truly miss her. Service on Thursday, July 20th at 11 a.m. at Plaza Community Chapel on Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St.
