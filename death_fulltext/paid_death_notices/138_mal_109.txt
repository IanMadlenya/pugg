
        LYNAUGH-George Louis, 58. Died July 9 at his home in Milan, N.Y., after a long and courageous battle with lung cancer. George was executive vice president of McCann Erickson, culminating a lifelong career in advertising. After retirement he was owner and operator of the Hyde Park Antique Center, his labor of love. He is survived by his mother Nancy, brothers Leonard & Louis, sister Eileen Durst and six dear nephews. He will be sorely missed by his many friends and beloved animals. In lieu of flowers donations made to cancer research or an animal care organization would please him. For information Dapson-Chestney Funeral Home, Rhinebeck, New York 914-876-6000.
