
        URKEN--Jerome A., of Langhorne, Pennsylvania, formerly of Bordentown, NJ, passed away on February 2, 2007. Jerome was the loving husband of Marilyn Urken, an adoring father of Joan Zimmett (Mark), Gail Geronemus (Roy), and Mark Urken (Laura), a proud grandfather of Nora Zimmett Kravis (Randy) and Lili Zimmett Evan and Greg Geronemus, and Gabrielle, Grant and Hannah Urken. He was also the cherished stepfather to Lisa Steinberg (Larry) and Marla Sabl, and the beloved step-grandfather to Carl and Rachel Steinberg. Jerome Urken attended the University of Pennsylvania and was a graduate of its School of Dental Medicine. After graduation, he served as a dentist in the United States Military during World War II. Subsequently, he practiced dentistry in Bordentown, NJ for over 40 years. A leader in the community, Jerome Urken acted as the President of the Bordentown, NJ Rotary Club, held a seat on the board of Doane Academy-St. Mary's Hall, and received the Alumni Award of Merit for his distinguished work on behalf of the Penn School of Dental Medicine. Most importantly, Jerome A. Urken was loved and admired by everyone who had the privilege of knowing him. The funeral will be held on Sunday, February 4, 2007 at 11 am at Orland's Ewing Memorial Chapel, which is located at 1534 Pennington Road, Trenton, NJ. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to The Lilian and Benjamin Hertzberg Palliative Care Institute, One Gustave L. Levy Place, Box 1070, New York, NY 10029.
