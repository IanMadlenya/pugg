
        SORKEN-Fran. Age 57. Died at her home in Yorktown Heights, NY, on Saturday, October 10. She was the loving wife of Edward, beloved mother of Elizabeth and Jonathan, dear sister of Natalie Hannon, Margaret Rodkin, and Ronald Rodkin. Funeral Services were held on Sunday, October 11, at the Yorktown Jewish Center, Yorktown Heights, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Phelps Hospice at Phelps Memorial Hospital, Sleepy Hollow, NY, The Yorktown Volunteer Ambulance Corp, and The American Cancer Society.
