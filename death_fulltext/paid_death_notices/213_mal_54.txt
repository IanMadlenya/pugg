
        CANNER--Daniel Leon, 53, of New York City, formerly of Teaneck, NJ, on September 13, 2004. Beloved husband of Adrianne Kamsler, loving father of Jonah, Zoe, and Benno, devoted son to Frida and Isidor Canner, caring brother to Judith Moss. A cofounder of Tangent Graphics, Inc., Danny was an unpublished writer who delighted in his family, friends and the world around him.
