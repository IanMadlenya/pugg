
        PAPPELIS-Ruth. December 31, 2001. Loving cousin of Wendy Wall Shabsis and Nicolas Wolfers MacLean. A graduate of Barnard College and later became director of volunteers in healthcare services for many years. She was devoted to the humane care and rescue of animals. Friends may call Friday, January 4, 7-9 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Service Saturday 11 A.M. at All Souls Unitarian Church, Lexington Avenue at 80th St, NYC. Contributions in her memory may be made to an animal charity of your choice.
