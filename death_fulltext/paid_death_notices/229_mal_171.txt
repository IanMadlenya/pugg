
        LERMAN--Morris (Murray). Of Lake Worth, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY on February 10, 2006. Beloved husband of 55 years to Blanche (Useloff) Lerman. Loving father of Roberta and Alan Herman and Andy and Judi Lerman. Proud grandparent of Jaime and Daniel Herman and David and Adam Lerman. Brother of Saul Lerman. Retired Chief Executive of a manufacturing firm, Murray served in the Army during WWII. He delighted in his family and friends, went out of his way to make others happy, and was a terrific golfer and pool player. He will be missed deeply by all who knew him. Services Wednesday, February 15 at Gutterman's in Rockville Centre, 516-764-9400. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to the Alzheimer's Association.
