
        KAUFMAN--Ruth. The officers and staff of the Village Copier note with great sorrow the untimely passing of our dear friend, colleague and mentor whose passion for excellence, wisdom with people and boundless energy made her loved by us all and a cornerstone of our success. Our deepest condolences to her husband Bob, her son Monte, and her granddaughters Lindsay and Kyra. Myron Schonfeld, President
