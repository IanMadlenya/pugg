
        TIROLA-Elvira Angela. On May 16, 1998 at the age of 88. She was born in New York City. Retired as an Executive Secretary for New York County Medical Society after 43 years. She was a member of the American Association of Medical Executives and Emeritus since 1979. She is survived by her niece Angela Krauss, brother Vincent Tirola, nephew Vincent Tirola, great nephews Ian Lawson and Douglas Tirola. Reposing Thursday 2-4 & 7-9 PM at Riverdale-onHudson Funeral Home. Mass Friday 10AM, at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment St. Mary's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Margaret of Cortona Church or The American Cancer Society would be appreciated.
