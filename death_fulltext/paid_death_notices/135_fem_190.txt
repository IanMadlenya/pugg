
        AUSTERN-Hazel. Passed away Friday, April 24, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Elliott Austern, devoted mother of Steven, Susan (Michael) Korngold, Faye (Steven) Krawitz and Wendy (Uri) Ottensoser, dear sister of Hugh Greenberg and sister-in-law of Beverly Greenberg, loving grandmother of Jonathan, Jason, Shari, Talia, Brian and Elyssa. Shiva observed until Friday A.M.
        AUSTERN-Hazel. Congregation Beth Sholom records the passing of our esteemed member, Hazel Austern. May the family be conforted among the mourners of Zion. Rabbi Kenneth Hain Dr. Gilbert Klaperman, Rabbi Emeritus Howard Wolf, President Lester Henner, Chairman of the Board
        AUSTERN-Hazel. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Hazel Austern, beloved sister of our devoted members and benefactors, Hugh and Sisterhood President Beverly Greenberg. Sincere condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
        AUSTERN-Hazel. Sisterhood of Park East Synagogue expresses its deepest sympathy to our President, Beverly Greenberg, her husband Hugh and the entire family on the loss of their beloved Hazel Austern. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Polly Etkind Hochberg, Chairman
        AUSTERN-Hazel. Men's Club of Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Hazel Austern, beloved sister of our devoted board member Hugh Greenberg. May her memory be for a blessing. Dr. Allan Goldberg, Pres. Herman Hochberg, Chairman
