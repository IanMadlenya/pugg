
        RIVERS-Larry. The Board of Governors of The National Arts Club expresses it profound sorrow at the passing of valued member Larry Rivers. We will treasure the memory of his frequent visits to our historic clubhouse and the performances there with his band. He was a wonderful, supportive friend and contributor. We offer our sympathy and condolences to all his family. Aldon James, President
