
        ROBERTIELLO-Richard C. On August 30, 2002. Beloved husband of Terril Gagnier. Father of Robert. Brother of Charles Robertiello. He was a noted psychiatrist in the metropolitan area for over a half century and author of many books. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call Wednesday, September 4, 2 to 5, and 7 to 9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, New York City, with service Thursday, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
