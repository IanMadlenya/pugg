
        LOEB-Charles. Died peacefully at his home on Monday afternoon, October 21. He was born in NYC but lived from 1943 to 1988 in Bradley Beach, NJ, before returning to his birthplace. He was chairman of the Awards Committee and a civilian procurement officer for the Research and Development Laboratories at the U.S. Signal Corps in Fort Monmouth, NJ for 20 years. Survivors include his loving wife Janet, his brother-in-law Lawrence White and his wife Clarice, sister-in-law Sylvia Eisenberg of San Francisco, a sister-inlaw Miriam Levine of Boynton Beach, FL., and many nieces & nephews. Asbury Park Press please note.
