
        GOTTESMAN-Natalie (Nibs) Sabin. Of Miami Beach. Passed away January 19, 1997. Most loved and loving mother of Barbara and Stephen Miller and Charles and Merrill Gottesman. Devoted daughter of Emma and the late Irving Sabsevitz. The adored grandmother of Erin Miller and Melinda and Caroline Gottesman. She was the beloved sister of Arnold and Lite Sabin and the late Judith Sabin Meisel. A loving aunt and great-aunt. Services will be today, Tuesday, January 21, 1 PM graveside Mount Nebo Cemetery, Miami. For additional information Riverside-Gordon, West Palm Beach, Fla. 561-683-8676. Donations in her memory may be made to Mount Sinai Medical Center (Miami) or the Miami Heart Institute.
