
        GALLENT--Martin. Devoted husband of Aline; loving father of Amy (Randy), Judith (Scott) and Gregory (Janet). Doting grandfather of Lauren, Eli, Benjamin, Stephanie and Julia. Died unexpectedly July 16th. Former Vice Chairman, New York City Planning Commission, former Chairman, Community Board 3 (Queens) and Past President of the Jewish Center of Jackson Heights, Jackson Heights-Elmhurst Kehillah, Flushing Meadow Corona Park Development Corporation. Services Wednesday at Schwartz Bros 76 Road and Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, N.Y. $(6$)GALLENT--Martin. The Jewish Center of Jackson Heights mourns the loss of its past president. May his memory remain for a blessing. David Kent, Rabbi Michael Schwartz, Cantor Steve Knobel, President
