        BAUM--Frederick. The Boards of Directors and staff of Selfhelp Community Services and United Help are saddened by the loss of our esteemed former Board member, Frederick A. Baum. For nearly thirty years, Mr. Baum's service was distinguished by his unwavering commitment to Selfhelp's mission, especially our programs for victims of Nazi persecution. We extend our deepest sympathy to his son, current Selfhelp Board member Dennis Baum, and the entire family.
