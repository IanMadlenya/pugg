
        KEMPINSKI--Theodore B., Jr. 62, of Manhattan died February 27, 2007, after a courageous battle with Lung Cancer. He was a beloved son of Jean Di Maio Kempinski, and the late Theodore B. Kempinski, Sr. The adored and adoring father Francesca Kempinski, the devoted brother of the late Sherry Jean Buonasera (Dr. John Buonasera). He was a loving uncle and granduncle. Loved by many, he leaves behind relatives, friends and colleagues who will always remember his good nature, charm and quick wit. A Mass of the Resurrection will be held at The Epiphany Roman Catholic Church on 2nd Avenue between 21st and 22nd Streets on Tuesday, March 6, 2007 at 11 am.
