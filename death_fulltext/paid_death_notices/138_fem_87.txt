
        GOLDRING-Helen. Beloved wife of the late Dr. William Goldring. Mother of Dr. Roberta Goldring Coles and the late Nancy Goldring. Grandmother of Anthony, John and Dr. Nancy Coles Goldstein. Great-grandmother of eight. A 1928 graduate of NYU Law School and a source of strength for 92 years for all who knew her. Services Monday, 1:30 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Family will receive at home Monday, 5-9 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to Dr. William Goldring Memorial Fund, Department of Medicine, NYU Medical Center, 550 1st Ave., NY, NY 10016 would be appreciated.
