
        KLAFTER-Corinne K. November 13, 1997, of Great Neck, Long Island. Beloved wife of Horace. Loving mother of Dr. Lois and Jerry Snyder, David Klafter, Esq. & Nancy Kestenbaum, Esq; Rabbi Beth Klafter and Rabbi Jonathan Hecht. In lieu of flowers or other gifts kindly direct contributions to Temple Beth El of Great Neck or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
        KLAFTER-Corinne. Temple BethEl of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its long time member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
