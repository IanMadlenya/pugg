
        ZALAR-Charles, 90, of Ghent, NY, died June 16th, 2000 at his home. Former diplomat to the Kingdom of Yugoslavia and also National Science Foundation Director. Survived by son, Gregory L. and daughter-in-law, Patricia of NYC, sister, Elka Kosir of Slovenia, and grandson, Andrej of NYC. Funeral service private. McDonald Funeral Home, Hudson, NY.
