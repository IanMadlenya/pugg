
        SAYRE--Nancy Bond, 77, of Delray Beach, FL, Chatham, MA, and a longtime resident of Rumson, NJ, passed away on November 17, 2006. Mrs. Sayre is survived by her husband of 55 years, Stuart Sayre and by her children, Libby, Stephen and Casey and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, May 19, 2007, at 11:00 am, at St. George's-by-the-River Episcopal Church in Rumson, NJ.
