
        EPSTEIN-Milton. 83. Died on December 5, 1998, to the deep sorrow of his wife Etta, children Barbara, Alan, Eugene and Ann; grandchildren Jules, Hannah, Nicholas, Clara & Lily; sister, brother, nieces, nephews and many friends. He was loved and cherished for his sense of humor and sound judgment. Many considered him their best friend and he will be remembered for his loyalty and generosity. Donations in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
