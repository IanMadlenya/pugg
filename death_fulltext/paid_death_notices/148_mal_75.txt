
        HESLIN-James. Dr. James Heslin, former director of the New York Historical Society, passed away on April 30th at St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital, New York. Dr. Heslin was the husband of the late Phyllis Heslin and brother of the late Daniel and Helen Heslin. He is survived by his sister Mary Ryan of North Cambridge, MA. and brother William Heslin of Atlanta, GA. He leaves five nieces and nephews and twelve grand nieces and nephews. Dr. Heslin received his Doctorate at Columbia University. Services were private.
