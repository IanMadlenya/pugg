
        O'GARA-William F.. On September 28, 1999. Born in NYC, October 31, 1933 and served in the U.S. Navy from January 1957 until 1959 when he was discharged with honors for a military related disability. During Mr. O'Gara's life he spent time in NYC and was currently a resident of West Palm Beach. Mr. O'Gara is survived by his daughter Maureen. A graveside service is planned for Friday, October 8 at Calverton National Cemetery. For further information please call F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc. Funeral Home at 888-828-1800.
