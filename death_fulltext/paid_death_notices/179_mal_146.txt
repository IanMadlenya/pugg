
        ROSEN-Leonard K. Age 87, passed away on Tuesday, December 11, 2001. He is survived by his wife of almost 63 years, Thelma J. Rosen. He is also survived by his daughter, Gail F. Berman and a daughter in California, five grandchildren, Alan, Matthew, Marion and Kevin, and a granddaughter. Mr. Rosen was an active member of the Bronx community for over 40 years from real estate to construction to civic volunteering. He will be missed by everyone who knew him as ''Lenny-Mr. Boston Road''. Services at 1 PM on Thursday, December 13th at GarlickRiverside Memorial Chapel, 1439 Unionport Rd, Bronx, NY. Interment Beth David Cemetery. In lieu of flowers or fruit baskets, contributions to your charity of choice.
