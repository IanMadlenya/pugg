
        DOHERTY-John J. Of Hartsdale, NY, an employee of AON Risk Services of NYC, missing since September 11, 2001 in the tragedy of the World Trade Center. He was the dear husband of Mary Birde Doherty & the loving father of two daughters, Barbara and Maureen. Beloved uncle of Cathy Hammacher, Anne Rauh and Jennifer Foley and many other loving nieces and nephews. He is predeceased by his sister, Irene Billings. John dearly touched the lives of all in his family and they all will miss him. A Mass of the Resurrection will be held on Wednesday, September 26 at 10 A.M. at Sacred Heart Church, 10 Lawton Ave. (at Central Avenue), Hartsdale, NY. Memorial donations in John's honor may be made to The Ursuline School, 1354 North Ave., New Rochelle, NY 10804, or Special Needs Advocacy Committee, C/O White Plains Senior Center, 65 Mitchell Place, White Plains, NY 10601, ATT: Jane Russo. His family and friends will gather in commemoration and celebration of John's life on Tuesday, September 25 from 2-4 & 7-9 P.M. at F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc., 732 Yonkers, Avenue, Yonkers, New York.
