
        NORDLINGER--Mildred. On Friday, November 28, 2003, at age 88. Abounding in an infinity of energy and ingenuity, Milly was the first woman member of the Automotive Advertising Council. A creative and inventive toy and gift advertising executive, she later began her own agency. Focused her energies after retirement on issues that she had always found most important. As Chapter President of NOW and an avid worker for equal rights, pro-choice and healthcare for all, Milly fought passionately. Admired friend to so many. Much loved partner of William Silverman. Loving sister of Jay. Beloved Mom to Steve, Joan and James. Mother-in-law of Carol, Steven and Michelle. Adored grandmother of Alex, Jacob and Sam. We miss you.
