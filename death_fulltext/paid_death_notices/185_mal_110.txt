
        RISTINE-J. Rolland. 70, an architect of many talents and wide interests, died on May 30, 2002, of cancer at Mt. Sinai Hospital in NYC. He is survived by a daughter, Sarah E.; a son, Peter J.; their mother, Sue Peterson Ristine; a half-sister, Dollie Cring; a niece, Diana Mars; a nephew, Rollie Hudson; a companion, Susan Bresler, and other devoted friends and associates across the country. All mourn the loss of this gentle, wise, playful spirit. Plans are underway for a celebration of his life later this year.
