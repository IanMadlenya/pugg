
        AVEDON-Samson. Age 89, formerly of New York City and now of Hollywood and Miami, FL. died February 16, 1998. He was the husband of the late Pauline Grossberg Avedon and cousin of Dru and Harvey Avedon of New York. Samson Avedon was the Comptroller of the Gillman Paper Co. of New York City until his retirement 30 years ago. Information, contact Panciera Memorial Home, 4200 Hollywood Boulvard, Hollywood, Florida 33021; 954-989-9900.
