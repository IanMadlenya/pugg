
        PLOTKIN-Harry Morris. On Friday, January 30, 1998 at his residence. Husband of the late Esther Plotkin; father of Ira Lev Plotkin (Kay); Judith Deborah Wilkenfeld (Jonathan); brother of Melvin Plotkin (Esther); Norman Plotkin (Rosalind) and Lena Gordon. Also survived by his grandchildren, Noah Benjamin Plotkin, Joshua Robert Plotkin, Aria Micha Wilkenfeld (Melissa); Daniela Lisa Wiggins (Marcus) and Gilad Joseph Wilkenfeld. Funeral services will be held Sunday, February 1 at 1:30 P.M., at Adas Israel Congregation, 2850 Quebec Street, N.W., Washington, DC 20008. Interment Judean Memorial Gardens. The family will observe Shiva Sunday through Thursday evenings, 7 P.M. and Monday through Friday mornings, 7:30 A.M., at the home of Harry Plotkin. Contributions may be made to The Charles E. Smith Jewish Day School, City Lights or Jewish Primary Day School at Adas Israel.
