
        GELLMAN--Jerome I., 79, on June 14 of Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, former longtime resident of Scarsdale, New York. Mourned by Florence, his wife of 55 years. Loving father of Elyse (Bill), Stephen (Wendy) and Ken (Melissa). Joyful and doting grandfather of Amanda, Lindsay, David, Alexa, Reese, Paige and Nathan. Devoted brother to Elaine and Melvin. Partner in Tucker, Gellman & Mulderig. Of Counsel to Cowan, Liebowitz and Latman. A man of great warmth, charm and humor. His family and friends will miss him dearly. Funeral on June 18, 2007, at 12:00 noon at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY.
