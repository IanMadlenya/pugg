
        SEIFTER-Dr. Eli. The Department of Surgery at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine extends its deepest, heartfelt condolences to Dr. Sam Seifter, brother, and the entire family of Dr. Eli Seifter, Professor Emeritus of Surgery and Biochemistry, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, who died in Cleveland, Ohio on October 3rd, 2001. Dr. Seifter was an extremely valuable, inspiring member of our faculty, who made a number of important original, creative discoveries in oncology, tissue repair, and metabolism. He was a dedicated, charismatic teacher, a generous, unselfish colleague, and an effective bridge between basic science and clinical medicine and surgery. Dr. T.S. Ravikumar, Professor and University Chairman, Department of Surgery Dr. Stanley M. Levenson, University Professor Emeritus Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Bronx, New York
