        QUITTMAN-Herman Z. The Officers, The Board of Directors, and The National Membership of The America - Israel Friendship League, deeply mourn the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague, Herman Z. Quittman, the moving spirit behind the founding of our organization and its Executive Vice President Emeritus. His life and efforts selflessly dedicated to the cause of the Jewish people and The State of Israel, as well as to his beloved organization, Bnai Zion, have been an inspiration to us all. Our profound condolences to his wife, Claire, his sister, Serene, to his sons, Michael and Stephen, and to their families. Kenneth J. Bialkin, Chairman of the Board Mortimer B. Zuckerman, President Elana Artman, Executive Vice President Stanley A. Urman, Senior Executive Vice President
        QUITTMAN-Herman Z. Bnai Zion mourns the loss of our Executive Vice President Emeritus. An extraordinary man whose selflessness and kind heart helped to write a glorious chapter in the history of Bnai Zion and the State of Israel. During his lifetime, he helped to build and strengthen vital institutions that aided the rebuilding of the Jewish State. Herman served as a beacon of hope and an inspiration to thousands of people throughout the world. His overwhelming generosity will benefit the people of Israel for generations to come. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, Claire, his sons, Stephen and Michael, his sister, Serene, and the other members of the family. Hon. Alan G. Hevesi, President Mel Parness, Exec.
