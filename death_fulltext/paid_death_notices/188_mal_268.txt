
        VALLELY, JR. - Wilfred (Bud) F. On September 25, 2002. Survived by his wife, Marnie, sons, Wilfred (Val), Scott, Mark and Todd and daughter, Victoria (Tori) Cox, his sister, Mitzi Fidao, and seventeen grandchildren. Bud was a distinguished executive in the paper industry for 50 years and ultimately Chairman of the Board of A.T. Clayton & Co., Inc. Relatives and friends are invited to a funeral mass on Saturday, September 28, at 10:00 AM at St. Michael's Church, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT. The family respectfully requests, in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to Myelo Dysplastic Syndrome Foundation, Inc., P.O. Box 353, 36 Front Street, Crosswicks, NJ 08515. Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home.
