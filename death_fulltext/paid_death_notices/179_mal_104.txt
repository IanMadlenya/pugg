
        MAPPLETHORPE - Harry. The Robert Mapplethorpe Foundation, Inc. notes with sadness the death of Harry Mapplethorpe on November 30, 2001, the father of Robert Mapplethorpe, the Foundation's founder, who predeceased him on March 9, 1989. Our sympathy is extended to the families of Robert Mapplethorpe's siblings, Nancy Rooney, Susan Schneider, James Mapplethorpe, and Edward Mapplethorpe. Michael Ward Stout, President
