
        CARELLI-Gabor. The Feszek Klub of New York mourns the passing of its long time President, Gabor Carelli who led our club with enthusiasm, energy and devotion. His spirited leadership made The Feszek Klub the cultural home of the Hungarian Community of New York. He was esteemed and beloved by all. Service Sunday, 2:45PM at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street.
