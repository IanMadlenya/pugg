
        CARRELL-Russell. The Board of Managers of East Side House Settlement expresses its deep sympathy at the death of Mr. Russell Carrell. As long-time manager of the Winter Antiques Show until his retirement, Mr. Carrell was instrumental to the Show's success. We are grateful for his many contributions. David L. Duffy, President East Side House Bd of Managers John A. Sanchez, Exec Director East Side House Settlement
