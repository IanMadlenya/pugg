
        RABHAN--Dr. Nathan Benami, 67. On February 7, 2006. Beloved husband of Jane Sonnenschein Rabhan for 43 years. Adored father of Rabbi David, Laura, and Rachel. Loving grandfather to six. Dear brother to Debora Hynda of Tel Aviv, Israel and Charlotte, N.C. Revered brother-in-law to Marc Sonnenschein and Edward Pizer. For 45 years he was devoted to the care and well-being of his patients who will never forget his dedication. Shiva is being observed at 115 Central Park West until Tuesday.
