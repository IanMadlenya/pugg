
        GREENBERG-Rebecca. Died on August 5, at home in Springfield, Mass., after a brief illness. Age 86. A lifelong social justice activist and teacher, she joined the Peace Corps at age 80. She was the widow of Bernard Greenberg, a negotiator for the United Steelworkers of America. She is survived by her children, Jean Stange of Denver, Colo., Linda Stone Davidoff of New York City, Richard of Springfield, Mass., and Adria Lique of Hopewell, N.J., 13 grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
