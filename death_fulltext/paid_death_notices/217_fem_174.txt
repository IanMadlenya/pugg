
        BRODSKY--Sarah (Hart). A longtime resident of Bronxville, N.Y., died February 5, 2005 at Lawrence Hospital. She was 68. Mrs. Brodsky was a trained concert pianist who graduated from the Yale School of Music and had studied piano and flute in Vienna. She was born in Philadelphia and grew up in Princeton, New Jersey. She was predeceased by her husband, Murray Brodsky. She is survived by her brother Brandon Hart and several nieces and nephews.
