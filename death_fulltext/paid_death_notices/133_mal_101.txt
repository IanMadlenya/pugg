
        GALUTEN-Murry (Morris). On February 6, at the age of 85, in Weston, FL. Much beloved husband of 58 years of Iris. Beloved father of Jeffrey and his wife Patricia, Alan and his wife Melanie, and adored grandfather to Jill, David, Jason and Samuel Noah. He leaves behind his brother Robert and his sister Fannie Schifrin. Past President of 69th Infantry Division Associaton. Service will be held Monday, noon, Riverside-Gordon, Tamarac, FL. Interment Wednesday, 11 AM, Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Queens, NY.
