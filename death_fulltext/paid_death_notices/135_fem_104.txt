
        WEAVER-Laura Ann. The Board, staff and members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City extend our profound condolences to Board Member John Weaver and to the Weaver family on the death of Laura Ann Weaver, who resided at UCP's Castleton residence and was a participant in our day treatment program on Staten Island. Laura Ann is missed by all who knew her. Leo Hausman, President Jack Hausman, Founder Edward R. Matthews, Exec. Dir. UCP of New York City, Inc.
