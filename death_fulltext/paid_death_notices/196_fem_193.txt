
        MARSHALL--Susan Nelson of La Jolla, CA died on May 18 at at the age of 63 after a brief illness. She was the daughter of the late Richard and Helen Nelson of Short Hills, NJ. She was a graduate of the Beard School and Wheelock College. Mrs. Marshall formerly resided in Short Hills and Darien, CT. While in NJ she served on the Boards of the Junior League of the Oranges and Short Hills and Children's Aid and Adoption Society. In CT she served as president of the Episcopal Church Women for St. Luke's Parish, Darien High School PTA and the Darien United Way and Community Council. She also served as an officer of the Darien Council of School Parents, the Lockwood - Mathews Mansion Museum of Norwalk, the Junior League of Stamford-Norwalk as well as other school organizations. In San Diego Susie was involved with the La Jolla Newcomers Club. She leaves her husband of 42 years, Bill, of La Jolla; a daughter, Jill Oppedisano of Santa Monica, CA and a son, James Marshall of Boulder, CO. A reception to celebrate Susie's life will be held at the Lockwood - Mathews Man sion Museum in Norwalk, CT on Saturday, June 14 at 4:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Susie's name to LockwoodMathews Mansion Museum, 295 West Avenue, Norwalk, CT. 06850. >>AD#
