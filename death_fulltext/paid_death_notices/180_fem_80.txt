
        WYMAN - Elizabeth Minnerly ''Betsy.'' Of Savannah, GA, died January 4, 2002, age 70, after a long illness. Loving wife, mother, friend. She is survived by her husband Thomas Hunt Wyman; a daughter Elizabeth Wyman Cordel; six sons, Winthrop Knowlton Jr., Christopher Knowlton, Oliver Knowlton, Peter Wyman, Michael Wyman, and Thomas Hunt Wyman Jr.; and 11 grandchildren. She will be deeply missed. A family graveside service will be held in North Egremont, MA, at a future date.
