
        FEINBERG--Fred (Chic). The employees of Tiger Button Co. Inc. offer our sincere condolences to our esteemed president Bernie Feinberg and his family on the loss of his dear brother Fred. Bernie and Fred founded Tiger Button 37 years ago and Fred was active until his retirement in 1996. Funeral service will take place in Florida on Sunday. We offer condolences to Fred's wife Iris, daughter Ellen, her husband Jaime and grandchildren Justin and Jason.
