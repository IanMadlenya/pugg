
        KAUFMAN-Lilli. The Jewish Community Center of Harrison announces with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed Founding Member. We extend deepest sympathy to her children Steven, Amy, and the entire family. Alfredo F. Borodowski, Rabbi Norton D. Shargel, Rabbi Emeritus Steven Chill, President Deborah Schiff, Chairman
        KAUFMAN-Lillian Petoff, 84. Died peacefully at home on August 14th, 2002. Beloved wife of the late David K. Kaufman, beloved mother of Steven and Amy Kaufman Goott, mother-in-law of Judy Kaufman and Gene Goott, grandmother of Sarah, Laura, Sharon and David. She was first and foremost a loving wife and mother. She was generous to friends, family and community. She and David were founding members fo the Jewish Community Center of Harrison and she devoted herself to many philanthropic causes including the Harrison Public Library. She will always be missed. Services will be held 11 AM, Friday, August 16th, at the Jewish Community Center of Harrison, Union Avenue, Harrison, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Jewish Community Center of Harrison or the Harrison Public Library.
