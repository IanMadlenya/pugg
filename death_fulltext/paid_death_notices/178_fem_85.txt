
        WERNER-Rosalind. On Tuesday, November 6, 2001, Rosalind Werner passed away after a heroic 4-1/2 year battle with pancreatic cancer. She is survived by her loving husband, Jules, sisters Gertrude Entin and Anne Zucker, children Toby Kaulkin, Linda Diaz, Roger Werner, Matthew Schneider and Kenneth Werner, 10 grandchildren and 3 great grandchildren. Rosalind loved her life, her family, her many good friends and the many causes and social events through which she shined. She died surrounded by family at her daughter's home, content with all the wonderful years with which she was blessed. Funeral services on Friday, November 9th, 10 AM at Temple Beth El of Great Neck, Old Mill Road, Great Neck, N.Y. Interment will follow at Beth Moses Cemetery, Farmingdale, L.I.
        WERNER - Rosalind. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
