
        WELLS--Viola. Known as Dotty, on October 11, 2005, of New York City, Scarsdale and Brant Lake, New York. Beloved wife of the late Jay Wells. Loving and beloved mother of Richard J. Wells, Katherine J. Wells and Elizabeth Wells Elkin. Loving and beloved grandmother of Laura, Benjamin, Joshua, Katy and Jocelyn, and greatgrandmother to Riley. Artist and homemaker, she was buried by her family on October 14, 2005.
