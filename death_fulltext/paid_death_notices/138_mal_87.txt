
        MAZZEO-Joseph A. On July 6, 1998. Beloved husband of Lucy (Welch). Also survived by his sisters, Mary Rinaldi, Mae Scolaro and Lauretta Korothy, and uncle of many nieces and nephews. Avalon Foundation Professor Emeritus in the Humanities of the Department of English and Comparative Literature at Columbia University. Memorial service to be held at Columbia University at a future date.
        MAZZEO-Joseph A. The members of the Department of English and Comparative Literature of Columbia University mourn the passing of their esteemed former chairman and admired colleague.
