
        BLACK-Arnold. Musician, composer/violinist, Founder and Artistic Director of Mohawk Trail Concerts Chamber Music Series in Western Massachusetts died suddenly and peacefully at his home Charlemont, MA, June 24, aged 77. Beloved husband of Ruth, adored father of Hilary, he was predeceased by two sons, Sandy and Tony. He leaves countless colleagues, music lovers, neighbors and friends saddened but cherishing gratefully his abiding generosity, warmth and irrepressible humor. A memorial service will be held at The Federated Church, Charlemont, July 3rd at 3:00 PM. Gifts in his memory may be made to the Mohawk Trail Concerts, Shelburne, Falls, MA 01370.
