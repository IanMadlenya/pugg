
        SCHAEFER-George, M.D. Age 84, died peacefully at home on Sept. 27. Beloved husband of Shirley, father of Jay and Michael, grandfather of Daniel and Julia, brother of Minnie Reiter. He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from NYU and Cornell University Medical College in 1937, and was an Attending Physician and on the faculty of Cornell-New York Hospital from 1951 to 1978. He moved to San Diego, was appointed Director of Residency Teaching Program in Obstetrics at Mercy Hospital. He was one of the foremost authorities on tuberculosis in OB/GYN. Author of many articles and books, including ''The Expectant Father.'' Rememberd with love and admiration.
