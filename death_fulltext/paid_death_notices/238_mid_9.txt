
        GAM--Kenneth L., 79. Passed away after a valiant battle with cancer on Friday, November 17th. Loving father of Laurie Gam and her partner, Gail Amato, and of the late Robin Arcati. Previously married to Elayne Gam for 32 years. He is also survived by sister Harriet Leeman and granddaughter Amanda Arcati. Devoted companion to Blossom Warren and her wonderful family. We will all miss his wit and wisdom.
