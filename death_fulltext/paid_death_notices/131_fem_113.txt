
        OTTINGER-Louise L., 98. Died peacefully in her Hampshire House apartment on December 12, 1997. Major philanthropist and political activist for progressive social change causes, particularly for homeless and poor people in New York City. Curious about life, caring friend, involved citizen, proud mother, unconditionally loving and supportive grandmother and great grandmother, whose elegance, laughter and charm will always be remembered and cherished. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in the name of Louise Ottinger to Planned Parenthood of New York City, Development Office, 26 Bleecker Street, New York, New York 10012.
