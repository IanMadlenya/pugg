
        LAUB--Benjamin. On October 6, 2004 at age 100. As designer and manufacturer for Variety Frocks Maternity Wear for over 50 years, his company was the first to produce maternity dresses on a large scale. Beloved husband of the late Grace. Devoted father of Arlene and Madelon and father-in-law of Taylor and Kit. Loving brother of Sam and Bob. Services on Sunday 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
