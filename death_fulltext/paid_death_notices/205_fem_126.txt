
        MILLER--Rachel (Ray). 96, died peacefully at home on Saturday, February 14. Born in Manhattan on December 20, 1907, she was predeceased by her beloved husband, Dr. Benjamin J. Miller in 1995, after 66 years of marriage. She is survived by her daughter Isabelle Miller Hyman, her son Albert A. Miller, her devoted son-in-law and daughter-in-law Jerome E. Hyman and Sally Frawley Miller, and by her loving grandchildren Joseph Barnett Miller and Anne Rose Miller. A beautiful, compassionate woman who wished above all to make others happy and comfortable, she was loved and admired by everyone who knew her in private life and in her long career at Bergdorf Goodman. After a long, busy life, rest in peace now, dearest Ray. Services private.
