
        HURWITZ-Bertha (Schneider) on January 10, 1999, late of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY and Revere, MA. Beloved aunt of Bernard and Esther Schneider. A graduate of Hunter College and Columbia University, she served her community as a family social worker and her country as a Field Director with the Red Cross in Australia during World War II. A true woman of valor, who enjoyed and cherished life's most precious gifts, she will be remembered with love by her family and by all who knew her and were enriched by her. Services at the Schlossberg & Solomon Memorial Chapel, Canton, MA., on Wednesday, January 13 at 12:30 P.M. Expressions of sympathy may be donated to the Jewish National Fund.
