
        SCHEIBER-Donald Z. Died January 9th. President, The Bart Group of Port Washington. Past president, The Queens Symphony Orchestra. He had been V.P. of Student Affairs, St. John's Univ. & Adjunct Assoc. Professor of Administration & Economics. A memorial service will be held Friday, January 19th, 4 p.m. in the Jeanne Rimsky Theater in Port Washington. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Indiana University Foundation, Little 500, P.O. Box 500 Bloomington, IN 47402.
