
        SESKIS--Eleanor. On August 31, 2004. Died peacefully at home after a long, valiant endurance of Alzheimers disease surrounded by her loving family and friends. Devoted wife of John Y. Loving mother of Jane Soudavar. Beloved grandmother of Annabelle Soudavar. Sister of the late Herbert Diamond. Daughter of the late Sadie and Max Diamond. Her love of fun and her always pragmatic loyal and positive approach to life has been an inspiration to all her family. She will be deeply missed forever. Service Wednesday September 1, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
        SESKIS--Eleanor. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Eleanor Seskis. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memories bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
