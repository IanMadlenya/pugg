
        STEWART - Faith Severance Hackl. Age 93, died on March 27, 2002. She resided in Princeton, NJ. Daughter of H. Craig Severance, architect of 40 Wall Street which competed with the Chrysler Building for the tallest skyscraper in New York. She was married to George F. Hackl, Jr. former governor and vice chairman of the New York Stock Exchange. After his death in 1974, she married James P. Stewart, president of DeLaval Co. and trustee of Cornell University, Rider College and Pace University. The author Elizabeth Rodewald wrote of her, ''there is a kind of inexhaustible elegance about you that trails clouds of glory wherever you go.'' She is survived by daughter Faith Hackl Ward, son George C.S. Hackl, six grandchildren and five great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Trinity Church, Princeton, N.J. on May 23rd, 11 A.M. Memorial gifts may be sent to Princeton Project 55, 12 Stockton St., Princeton, NJ 08540.
