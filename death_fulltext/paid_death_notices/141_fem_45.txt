
        DILBERT-Ida. Died October 3, 1998, in Great Neck, NY. Beloved mother of Phyllis Turin and Ann Sandler and dear mother-in-law of Michael Turin and the late Melvin Sandler. Devoted sister of Esther Bernstein and the late Rose Schatner. She was a loving grandmother and great-grandmother. We will remember her as a true lady. Graveside services will be held Tuesday, October 6, at 11:30, Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
        DILBERT-Ida. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
