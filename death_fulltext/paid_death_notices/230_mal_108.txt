
        SIMONS--Gertrude (Moriarity), 89. Of Tamarac, FL (formerly of Great Neck, NY) died Monday, March 6, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Simons of Superior Printing Ink Company. Devoted mother of Jeffrey Simons and his wife Denise of Boca Raton, FL; Ellen Simons of Brookline, MA; Joanne Simons Fahey Turner of Wellington, FL, and the late Michael Simons. Loving Grandmother of Roseanne, Angela, Michael, Brian, Andrew, and Jennifer. Dear great-grandmother of eight. Dear sister of William Moriarity and Doris Gace. Visitation and services will be at the Hawthorne Funeral Home, NY (www. Hawthornefuneralhome. com) on Saturday, March 11, 2006 at 1PM. Burial will follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to The American Red Cross or the Salvation Army.
