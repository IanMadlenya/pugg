
        SCULLY-Leonard Tyson, Colonel, U.S.A. Died October 17, 1997 at his home in Pound Ridge, New York. He is survived by his wife Jean Scully. Colonel Scully was formerly President of Excelsior Income Shares, Inc. and a retired Officer of United States Trust Company of New York. He served on the Boards of a number of charitable organizations. Funeral services are private. Burial will be at Arlington National Cemetery.
