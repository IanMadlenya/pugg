
        GUNDERSEN - Harriet Adams. Died on July 1, 2000, at the age of 97 in Hanover, New Hampshire. Widow of Sven M. Gundersen, M.D., a renowned diagnostician and much loved doctor at Mary Hitchcock Memorial Hospital in Hanover, who died two years ago. She is the daughter of Professor John Chester Adams of Yale University and Elizabeth Munger Adams, and mother of Karen Gundersen Lerner of New York and Pamela Gundersen Miller of Lexington, Kentucky. Loving grandmother of Erik, Alexander and Karen Miller, and great-grandmother of Erika. Cherished by her family and multitude of friends for her lively personality and volunteer services, especially her work for Planned Parenthood of Northern New England. A memorial service is planned for August 5th, at her beloved summer home in Hancock Point on the coast of Maine. We wish her smooth sailing.
