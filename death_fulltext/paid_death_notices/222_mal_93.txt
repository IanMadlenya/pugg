
        ALLIS--Anthony Arthur. Died Tuesday, July 5, 2005, at age 83. He will always be remembered for his positive spirit and the ''light'' he brought to every gathering. He will be missed dearly by his family and friends. He is survived by his devoted wife of 57 years, Christine G. Allis, and his loving children, Beatrice C. AllisMinion of Boca Raton, Barbara A. Allis, M.D., of Lloyd Harbor, NY, and Anthony J. Allis of Bronx, NY, his three grandchildren, Kathryn, Anthony and John Robert, and his brother, Arthur Allis of Fort Worth, TX. Memorial service will be held in August, in Long Island, NY.
