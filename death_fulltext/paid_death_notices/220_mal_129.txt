
        LACEY--Robert Irving, of Tamarac, FL, formerly of Great Neck, NY passed away peacefully at home on May 12, 2005 at the age of 94. Most beloved husband for 70 years of Edith Gordon; adored father of Carol & Edward Sacks, Joan & Ronald Rothaus; most loved grandfather of Jonathan & Barrie Sacks, Bonnie & Robert Moses, Robin Sacks, Rod & Samantha Rothaus, and Merryl Rothaus; most special GG Pa of Evan, Andrew, Ariella, Elijah, and Sadie Rose. Also survived by three devoted brothers-inlaw Julie Pepper, Robert Gordon and Victor Gordon and many loving nieces and nephews. He was a graduate of NYU School of Commerce and Brooklyn Law School and co-founder of Gordon-Lacey Chemical Products Company, Inc. He was honored by UJAFederation for his tireless dedication to worldwide Jewry. He will live and be loved forever in all of our hearts. Graveside services, Sunday May 15 at 1PM, Beth Moses Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Contributions may be made in his memory to UJC of Broward County, 5890 S. Pine Island Road, Davie, FL 33328, or a charity of your choice.
        LACEY -- Robert. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of our longtime Temple member, Robert Lacey. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
