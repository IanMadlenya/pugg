
        WRIGHTSON--George D. Jr. 83, Longboat Key, FL. Died May 25 at Sarasota Memorial Hospital. Leaves his wife, Patricia, son, George III, daughters, Mary Boothby and Ann Wrightson. A graduate of Hotchkiss School, Class of '39, attended Williams College, and served in the artillery of the U.S. Army for 35 months. Grew up on Shippan Point, Stamford, CT. Predeceased by his first wife, Harriette. For many years he lived in the Hanover, NH area where he was actively involved in his community and St. Thomas Episcopal Church. Memorial service May 28, 2004 11 AM All Angels Episcopal Church, Longboat Key, FL. A celebration of his life will be held at a future date at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, Hanover, NH. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Hotchkiss School, All Angels Episcopal Church, Longboat Key or St. Thomas Church, Hanover, NH.
