
        GANT-Bettie Alexander, 46. Died peacefully in her sleep at her home in Manhattan last weekend. ''Alex'' Gant was the building editor of ''This Old House'' magazine. Donations in her name may be made to the American Diabetes Association, PO Box 2680, North Canton, OH 44720. Funeral services will be held on September 20th, at 2 p.m., at the Church of the Holy Comforter in Burlington, NC. A memorial service will take place at 4 P.M. on October 1, at Christ & St. Stephen's Church, 120 West 69th Street in Manhattan.
