
        HERMALYN-Isabelle Lee. Of Fort Lee, New Jersey, formerly of Teaneck and the Bronx. Devoted wife of Sol. Loving mother of three and adored grandmother of five. A truly grand lady who was everyone's friend, Isabelle had the unique talent to make all who met her feel happy and important. Longtime volunteer in numerous charitable causes, including Women's American ORT, Cancer Care Inc., Starfish, and 15 years in Holy Name Hospital. From singer to homemaker to bank manager, she lived her life to the fullest. She will be greatly missed. Services Thursday, 1:30 P.M., at ''Gutterman-Musicant,'' 402 Park Street, Hackensack, New Jersey.
