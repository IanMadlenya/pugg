
        BASSEN-Frances, 91. On November 14, 2002. Beloved mother of Sally Morse and Nancy Mayer, devoted wife of Frank Bassen, cherished grandmother of Carl and Daniel Mayer, adored greatgrandmother of Samuel and Ruby Mayer. She was a woman of great beauty, dignity and artistic achievement who was always gracious and generous to friends and family. She will be deeply missed. Services Friday, November 22, 11 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, New York City.
        BASSEN - Frances. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member, Frances Bassen. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest sympathy to her husband Frank and to all of their family. Jacqueline Bellsey, President Dale Winston, Secretary
