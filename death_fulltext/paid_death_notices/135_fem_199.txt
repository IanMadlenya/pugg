
        ABRAMSON-Dr. Belle. On April 27, 1998. Beloved daughter and sister. Service Wednesday 1:30 P.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        ABRAMSON-Belle, Dr. The Board of Directors of Surprise Lake Camp expresses deep sorrow at the loss of its long time colleague and supporter. Her dedication to the needs of children at Surprise Lake Camp for over forty years is an inspiration to us all. Laurence H. Rubinstein, President Jordan Dale, Executive Director
