
        WINTER--Philip L., Esq. Formerly of Larchmont, N.Y. passed away Tuesday, June 24, 2003 in Jensen Beach, FL. He was predeceased by his wife Sarah ''Scat'' Winter and their daughter Jeanie. He is survived by two grandchildren, his stepson and two nieces and nephews. He graduated Harvard College in 1944 and Harvard Law School in 1949, served in the U.S. Army as a Japanese interpreter during WWII, and practiced entertainment law in New York City before moving his practice to Larchmont, N.Y. and Estes Park, CO. Funeral services and interment will be held in Estes Park, CO. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Hospice Care.
