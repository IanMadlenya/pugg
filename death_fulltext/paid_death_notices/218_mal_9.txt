
        KISSIN--Benjamin, M.D. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center community mourns the passing of Benjamin Kissin, M.D., Professor Emeritus of Psychiatry. His many contributions to the study and treatment of alcohol and substance abuse will long be valued and remembered. We extend our condolences to his family, colleagues and friends. John C. LaRosa, M.D., President Eugene B. Feigelson, M.D., Senior V.P. and Dean of the College of Medicine. Stephen Goldfinger, M.D., Chairman of Psychiatry
