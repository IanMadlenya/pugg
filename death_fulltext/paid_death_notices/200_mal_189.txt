
        GANS--Harold David. Beloved and adored husband of Jeanne for 61 years. Cherished father of Ellen (Bruce) Aldewereld and Barbara (Nat) Russo. Devoted grandfather of Lisa (Thomas) Krommenhoek and Melissa (Andrew) Moss. Dear brother of Felicia Price and the late May Miller. Passed away peacefully at home in his 94th year on Wednesday, September 17, 2003.
