Services Monday, September 28, 11:30 AM, Riverside Memorial, 76th and Amsterdam.
        BAUER--Stanley, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our good friend and distinguished colleague, Dr. Stanley Bauer, formerly Associate Chairman of the Department of Diagnostic Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at Beth Israel. Dr. Bauer was widely recognized for his work in improving techniques for the collection, reporting and analyzing of data in hospital laboratories. A prolific researcher and speaker, he was also known for his dedication to training pathology residents. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family.
