
        TRESNOWSKE-John. 79, died at his home after a long illness on Tuesday, November 7, 2000. Beloved husband of Valerie, loving father of Charles and Richard. He served in the Armed Forces in Europe from 1942 to 1945. He practiced law for many years and served under three Mayors. He was our guiding light and will be missed by all. Contributions in his memory may be sent to his alma mater Brooklyn Law School.
