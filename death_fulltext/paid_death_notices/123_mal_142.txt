
        ABRAHAMS - Howard Phineas. March 19, 1997. Advertising and promotion executive. Born in Yonkers, NY, July 12, 1904. Education: Cornell University, University of Florida and Cumberland University. Pre-deceased by his son Allen E. Abrahams, Ph.D. Survived by his wife Florence Blostein Abrahams, daughter Elinor Seaman, four grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren.
