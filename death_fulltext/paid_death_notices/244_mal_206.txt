
        SELONICK--James B., 81, died peacefully at his Cincinnati home, May 18, 2007. Beloved husband of Peggy Frieder Selonick, father of Ellen, James and William; grandfather of seven treasured grandchildren. Born in New York, graduate of Townsend Harris High School; Ensign in the US Navy during WWII. Received a law degree from the University of Cincinnati and pursued a distinguished career in real estate ending as Senior Vice President for Federated Department Stores. Active in civic affairs throughout his life and a vocal participant in local development issues. Memorial contributions should be made to Congregation Beth Adam or Cincinnati Symphony Orchestra.
