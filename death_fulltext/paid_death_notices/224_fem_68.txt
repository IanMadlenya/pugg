
        TRAUTMAN--Andrea Dominique Hohlwein, died suddenly at age 44 on September 6, 2005. Beloved wife of Gregory. Devoted mother of Emily Helen, Natalie Siena and Juliet Gabriella. Loving daughter of Hans and Kathryn Hohlwein. Beloved sister of Reinhard Hohlwein and Laura Hohlwein. She received her doctorate in literature from the University of British Columbia. Friends may call Friday, September 16th, 6-8 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Service Saturday, September 17th, 9:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell. Interment Oak Cemetery, Sag Harbor, NY.
