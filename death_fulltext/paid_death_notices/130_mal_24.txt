
        WASSERMAN-John M. On October 30, 1997. Beloved husband of 65 years of Miriam (Wertheimer) Wasserman. Devoted father of Ann Kiggen and Jane Ferrar, grandfather of Andrew, Carolyn, Linda, Derek and Jessica. Great grandfather of Aaron, Daniel, Peter, Jessica, Danielle, Michael and Quinn. Former Senior Partner Asiel & Co., former National Governor National Association of Securities Dealers, former Governor American Stock Exchange, former longtime Trustee Altro Health and Rehabilitation Services. Age 94. Memorial service at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, Madison Ave. and 81st St, Wednesday, November 5, at 12 PM. Friends may call from 11 AM until time of service. Contributions may be made in his memory to charity of one's choice.
