
        FRANKEL--Norma, age 74, died on April 26, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Abraham Frankel, daughter of the late Harry and Helen Frank, mother of David, Ross, Harriet, and Charles, and daughters-in-law Lynn and Caren and son-in-law Marc. Loving and adoring grandmother of Deborah, Hilary, Melissa, Nina, Bradley, Emily and Leah. Graduate of NYU at age 19, she returned to school for a Masters degree in Library Sciences and served as medical librarian at LIJ Hospital for more than 30 years. Lover of literature, bridge and the NY Mets (she had no choice). Funeral Sunday, April 29 at 9am, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', 55 N. Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Catharine Margaret Pasmantier Fund, c/o Mark Pasmantier, M.D., 407 E. 70th St., NY, NY 10021.
