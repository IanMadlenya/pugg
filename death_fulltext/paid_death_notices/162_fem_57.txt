
        PERKINS-Sheila R. Of Milton, Massachusetts, June 23. Sheila Redmond Perkins, widow of Malcolm D. Perkins, mother of Malcolm Jr. of Seattle, Sara of New York, Samuel of Milton, and William of Baltimore. Sister of Sylvie Griffiths, Cynthia Mead, and the late Joan Read. She is also survived by three daughters-in-law and seven grandchildren. Memorial service at First Parish Unitarian Church, 535 Canton Ave. in Milton on Saturday, July 15 at 2 P.M. followed by a reception at the Perkins residence. Donations may be made in Sheila's memory to The Walters Art Gallery, Baltimore, The Conservation Law Foundation, or The Natural Resources Defense Council.
