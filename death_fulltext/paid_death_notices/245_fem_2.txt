
        SPIGNER--Christine E. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Queens Library mourn the passing of a dear friend and colleague. She was a tireless advocate for literacy and strengthened Queens Library with her leadership. She served as a library Trustee from 1980 until her death and was Board President in 1992 and 1993. She will be deeply missed. Our deepest condolences to her husband Hon. Archie Spigner and their whole family. Leonard T. D'Amico, Pres., Queens Library Board Thomas W. Galante, Library Director and the Board of Trustees
