
        JOSEPH-Richard W. Community Service Providers of New York/ NY State CSP's (LIAAC, ACSWNY, ASC, ACNENY, SIATF, BAS, STAP, ACR, ACQC, BATF, ARC, and AIDS Rochester) mourn the loss of Richard W. Joseph who died on Sunday, December 5th, 1999. Richard spent the last six years as Executive Director of ACNENY. Under his leadership, he doubled both the budget and the staff of the agency. He also served as an officer of the CSP Association/NY State CSP's. He will be remembered for his diplomatic skills and his gentle manner.
