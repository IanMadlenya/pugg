
        SHAVER--Alice Crafts. Died peacefully November 26 in Oberlin, Ohio, age 89. Loved for her trenchant wit, her curiosity and her gift for friendship, she was a model of imaginative service to others. Wife of the late Chester Linn Shaver. Survived by a son, Phillip A. Shaver of Princeton, and daughter Annie ShaverCrandell of New York City, three grandchildren and eight nieces and nephews.
