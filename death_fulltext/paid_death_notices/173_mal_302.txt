
        SULLIVAN-Raymond Peter, Jr. Died peacefully on June 27, 2001 at the age of 87 at his home in Scottsdale, Arizona. Beloved husband of the late Catherine McDonnell Sullivan, he is survived by his sister Katherine Meehan, his children R. Peter Sullivan III, Maureen Brookman, Sheila, Kevin, Karen and Sean Sullivan. Also by his grandsons David, Jonathan and Luke Singer and many nieces and nephews. Visitation at the O'Connell Funeral Home, 30 Little Plains Road, Southampton, NY Friday from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. There will be a Funeral Mass on Saturday at 10:00 A.M. at the Sacred Hearts of Jesus and Mary Church, 168 Hill Street, Southampton, NY.
