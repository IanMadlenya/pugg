
        STRASBURG-Samuel. Of Lake Worth, FL and formerly of Yonkers, NY on Wednesday, January 17, 2001, shortly before his 96th birthday. Beloved husband of the late Irene, loving father of Philip and his wife, Roberta. Dearest grandfather of Donald Strasburg, Lauren and Scott Freifeld, doting great-grandfather of Jared Freifeld and devoted brother of Frances, Jeanette and the late Rose and Lily. Funeral service will be held Sunday, January 21, at 11:30 AM at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave, Yonkers, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Ave, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
