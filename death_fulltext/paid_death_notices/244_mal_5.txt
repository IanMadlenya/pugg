
        TORRES--Angel. July 9, 1962 April 21, 2007. Born in Brooklyn, NY, resided in Tamarac, Florida. Passed away at the age of 44, in Fort Lauderdale, FL after a long and valiant struggle against HIV/AIDS. Beloved son of the late Angel Torres Sr., Angel leaves behind his mother Rafaela, brother Rudy, sister Judy and his loving companion and friend of 25 years, Ernest Grossman.
