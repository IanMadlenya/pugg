
        USSAK-Eileen. Seventy-eight, of West Davenport, NY. Died on Wed., March 11, 1998 at the A.O. Fox Memorial Hospital, Oneonta, NY. She was born Dec. 24, 1919 in Norwich England, the daughter of Max and Louise (Lang) Brenner. Eileen married Robert Ussak Feb. 4, 1945, in Norwich England. She is survived by her husband of 53 years, Robert Ussak of West Davenport, 2 sons and a daughterin-law, Richard Ussak of Tucson AZ, and Michael and AnnMarie Ussak of Edison, New Jersey.
