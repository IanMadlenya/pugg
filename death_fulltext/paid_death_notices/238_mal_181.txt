
        KOFFLER--Joseph H., died at age 85 on November 13, 2006. Born in Stamford, CT. Professor at New York Law School for over 50 years. Beloved by his family for his loyalty and wit. Survived by daughter Lynne Koffler, sisters Anne Roth, Edythe Jarvik, Frances Goldstein, wife Monica B. Koffler, sister-inlaw Carolyn Koffler, nieces, nephews and dear friends. Predeceased by brother Arthur Koffler. Funeral services will be held at Leo P. Gallagher and Son, 2900 Summer St., Stamford, CT on Thursday at 3pm. In lieu of flowers, donations are directed to the Professor Koffler Scholarship at New York Law School or the Parkinson's Foundation.
