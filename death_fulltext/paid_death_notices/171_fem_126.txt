
        PEPPER-Anita Golden, Dr. In loving memory of our wife, mother, dearest friend, teacher, who died April 7, 2001 at her home in Montague, MA on her 46th wedding anniversary at age 73. Professor, passionate advocate for peace, social justice, healthcare for all, the special concerns of mothers and children; she delighted also in the domestic arts of fine cooking and baking, knitting and quilting, gardening. A special voice for peace and justice in the Middle East. Gifted teacher, inspired and inspiring human being. Burial was at Old South Cemetery, Montague, MA Memorial Celebration on Sunday, May 13, 11AM at National Yiddish Book Center, Hampshire College, Amherst, MA. Max, Sara, Thomas
