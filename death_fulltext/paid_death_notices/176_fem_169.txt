
        SELIGSON-Lilian Yohalem. Age 101. Died at home, September 19th. Widow of Harold P. Seligson, Esq. Mother of Carl H. and the late Paul Y. Seligson. Mother-in-law of Brenda and Bonnie. Grandmother of Susan and Greg Pattenaude, Judy and Tim Keller, Barbara and Bob Zweig, James Lieberman, Nina and Ari Priven and Eric Drath. Great grandmother of Kate and Andrew Pattenaude, Emily Zweig, Rachel, Regan and Sadie Keller, and Aaron and Mariel Priven. Services private. Contributions in her memory may be made to Doctors without Borders, PO Box 2247, New York, New York 10116.
