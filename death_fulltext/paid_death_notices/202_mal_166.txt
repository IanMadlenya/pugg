
        BERSOHN--Richard. Died on November 18, 2003. He is survived by his beloved and devoted wife Nehama, his children Malcolm Mark, David, Rina and Leora, his grandchildren Daniel and Rebecca, his daughter-in-law Shelley Shapiro and son-in-law Adam Spiewak, his twin brother Malcolm and his sister Barbara Elkin, his sisters-in-law JoAnn Bersohn, Miriam Rezler and Sara Rezler. Richard was the Higgins Professor of Natural Science at Columbia University. He will be sadly missed.
