
        KAUFMANN-Herbert O. 90, on April 12, after a brief illness. Decorated veteran of World War II. A devoted husband and father, he will be held in loving memory by his wife Edith Kaufmann, his daughters Joan Bloom and Margret Kaufmann-Pankov, their husbands Richard Bloom and Gradimir Pankov, his sister Charlotte Thyssen, his brother Rudolf Kaufman, and a large circle of family and friends.
