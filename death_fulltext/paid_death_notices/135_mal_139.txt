
        GEER-Dr. E. Throop Jr. (Bob). Age 79, of Riverside, CT, died April 12, 1998. Husband of Viola C. Geer. He is survived by one daughter, Suzanne C. Geer, and three sons, John C. Geer, E. Throop Geer III, and Christopher M. Geer. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, April 18 at 2 PM, St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Riverside, Connecticut. Donations in his memory may be made to St. Paul's Church, 200 Riverside Avenue, Riverside, CT. 06878, or Williams College, C/O Alumni Office, Class of 1941, Williamstown, MA. 01267.
