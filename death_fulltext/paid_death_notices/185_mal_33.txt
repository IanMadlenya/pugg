
        GATES-James Benedict. 64, on June 3, 2002. It is with deepest sadness that we announce the passing of James Benedict Gates. He leaves his sister Patricia Gates and his nephew Joseph Gates of Massachusetts. He also leaves devoted, close friends, Ben Wichman, Ursula Meyer, Jeannie Michaels and Laurie Winfrey. A Memorial Mass for James will be announced later this month in The New York Times.
