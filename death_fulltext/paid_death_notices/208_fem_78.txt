
        BELL--Polly Putney, 65, beloved wife of Frank S. Bell Jr. a resident of Williamstown, Massachusetts and Palm Beach, FL died Friday May 7, 2004 at her residence in Williamstown. An outdoor memorial service will be held at their residence at 255 Hancock Road Williamstown, MA Saturday, May 15, 2004 at noon. A reception will follow. In lieu of flowers contributions in memory of Polly may be made to Miss Hall's School through the office of the George M. Hopkins Funeral Home, 61-67 Spring Street, Williamstown, MA. 01267.
