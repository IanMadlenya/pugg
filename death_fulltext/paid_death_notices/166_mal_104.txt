
        WEISER-Harry. At 95, of Rego Park, NY, died peacefully at home November 8. Beloved husband of Rose (deceased); loving father of Alan and his wife, Ruth, and Eleanor (deceased); devoted and cherished grandfather of David, Debra and Diane, and beloved uncle. He will be missed by all who knew him. He was a gentle man who held family above all else. Services will be held at Gutterman's, Rockville Centre, Friday, November 10, at Noon.
