
        SILVERMAN--Bernard. Central Synagogue mourns our longtime member, Bernie Silverman. He demonstrated his support of the Jewish people and of our Congregation with profound commitment, resolute vision and a deep love of the values of Jewish life. We are especially grateful for his work on behalf of Brotherhood and as founder of the Jethro program. He will be missed. Rabbi Peter J. Rubinstein Howard Sharfstein, President $(6$)SILVERMAN--Bernard (''Bernie''). Jewish lay leader and wood worker. Born in Zastavna, Bukovina, on May 3, 1920; died in New York City on September 9, 2006. Beloved husband of Naomi, father of Moses, Emily, Daniel, Matthew, Betty Robbins and Keith Mostov and grandfather of Benjamin, Rachel, Ariella, Zeke, Andre and Raphael. Funeral services will be held at Central Synagogue, 123 E. 55th Street at Lexington Avenue, New York City, on Monday, September 11, 2006, at 12 noon. $(6$)SILVERMAN--Bernard.
