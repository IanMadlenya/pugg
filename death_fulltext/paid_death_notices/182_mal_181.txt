
        JEFFCOAT-Albert E. (Jeff). The staff, board and artists of the Manhattan Theatre Club are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed Founder and Chairman Emeritus, A.E. ''Jeff'' Jeffcoat. Jeff was instrumental in the early growth of MTC and served as its Chairman from 1970 to 1982. MTC is grateful for his leadership, dedication and thoughtfulness. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Jan, children Thomas, Peter, John, Ross, Lindsay and Catherine and to their four grandchildren. Lynne Meadow, Artistic Director Barry Grove, Executive Producer Peter J. Solomon, Chairman
