
        ARONSON-Rudolph. On Nov. 9, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Julia. Devoted father of Raymond and father-in-law of Linda. Cherished grandfather of Philip and Deborah, Deborah, Suzanne and Sarah. Dearest great-grandfather of Ethan and Julia. Rudolph was a graduate of the Cooper Union, Class of '29 of which he remained an active alumnus. He was also a founder, director, officer and shareholder of Walter Balfour and Co., Inc. and an original member of the Hollis Hills Jewish Center. Services Thursday, 9:30 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, New York. Interment at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the Cooper Union, 30 Cooper Square, 8th Floor, New York City 10003.
