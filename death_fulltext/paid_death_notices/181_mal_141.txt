
        SCHNEIDER-Bernard, M.D. 89, of Yonkers, NY, died suddenly of cardiac causes on Saturday, February 9, 2002. He was a highly respected, admired and skilled family practitioner to thousands of families in the Bronx for over 50 years. A devoted and loving husband, father and grandfather, a generous and caring friend. He will always be in our hearts and minds. He is survived by his devoted wife Magda, three loving daughters and sonsin-law Susan and Dennis Spencer of Woodbridge, CT, Beth and Gordon Weingarth of Woodbridge, CT, and Karen and Michael Kimball of North Haven, CT, and four adoring grandchildren, Andrea Spencer, Joanna Spencer, Michael Weingarth and Caroline Kimball. Funeral services will take place Tuesday, February 12, at 12 noon at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mount Vernon, NY. Donations in his memory in the spirit of his caring can be made to the Epilepsy Foundation, 4351 Garden City Dr., Landover, MD 20785, (800) 332-1000.
