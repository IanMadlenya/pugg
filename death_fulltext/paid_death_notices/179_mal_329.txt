
        RUSSELL-Edwin F., 87. Of New York, died peacefully December 22, at his home in Florida. He had a life-long career in journalism with Newhouse Newspapers. He was president of Metrosuburbia Inc. and former publisher of the Harrisburg Patriot-News and ''Vogue'' magazine. Admired and beloved husband of Cynthia Cary Russell; devoted father to Serena Balfour, Consuela Judson, Alexandra Birch and Jacqueline Williams. Caring stepfather to Peter, Abby and Guy Van Pelt. Loving grandfather to Morgan, Lucinda, Lily and Alastair, Alex, Nick and Ian; Gene, Harry and Philip. Memorial services are pending. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Fresh Air Fund.
