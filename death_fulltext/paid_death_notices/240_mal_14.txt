
        ROGIN--Richard, died January 1st, 2007, age 74; cherished wife of Abigail Thomas; beloved father of Sally Rogin Dreslin, Katie Rogin, Frank Rogin; loving brother of Gilbert Rogin; much loved stepfather of Sarah Waddell, Jennifer Waddell, Ralph Waddell and Catherine Luttinger. Delighted grandfather of Nora Dreslin and Mateo Rogin. He was a writer and a runner. ''A Finnish proverb goes,strong sisu (guts) will help get a person even through a gray rock. Now I chant'sis-u sis-u' inside my head as I lug myself up those weary hills toward the end of a long run.''--Richard Rogin. Darling man, we miss you already.
