
        BELL-Thomas A. Of Vero Beach, FL, died Friday, January 5, 2001, at his residence. Born in West New York, he lived in Jersey City before moving to Vero Beach, FL. Mr. Bell was the International Director of Public Relations for Trans World Airlines, Paris, France. Mr. Bell was a former parishioner of Our Lady of Mercy R.C. Church, Jersey City. Surviving is his wife Marie (DeSimone) Cancro Bell. Two daughters, Jacqueline Sidoti of Rockville Centre, NY, and Priscilla Urban of Fayetteville, AK. A son, Anthony Cancro of Brick, NJ. Also surviving are two grandchildren. He was predecesed by his first wife Edna in 1989, and a sister, Dorothy. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 9:30 AM, at Church of the Nativity, 315 Prospect St, Midland Park, NJ. Interment George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus. Visiting Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Olthuis Funeral Home, 159 Godwin Ave, Midland Park, NJ. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to one's favorite charity.
