
        NETCHINSKY--Rhoda (Levin), 84, artist and political activist, member of the Art Students League, on May 22 in Brooklyn. Deeply loved by friends and family: her sister, Mimi Leipzig, daughters, Jill Netchinsky - Toussaint and Suzy D'Angelo, grandchildren, Joseph, Jennifer, Antoinette and Jonah, and three great - granddaughters. Funeral services 10 AM, Wednesday, May 25, Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court St., Brooklyn (718-875-1640).
