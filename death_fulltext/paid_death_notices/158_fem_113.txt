
        SCHOENWIESNER - Gertrude. The Officers and Staff of Local 1102 RWDSU and UFCW mourn the loss of a dedicated trade unionist and a loyal and trusted friend. ''Trudy'' was loved and respected by her members and coworkers for her honesty, integrity and sheer hard work. Trudy's devotion to the cause of working people has improved the lives of everyone she has touched. She will be greatly missed. We extend our sincere condolences to her family and friends. Frank S. Bail, President
