
        GOLDSTEIN-Irving, Queens College class of 1960, beloved member of the Board of Trustees of the Queens College Foundation since 1986. He served as Chairman of the Foundation from 1987 to 1991, when he became Chairman Emeritus. He was a devoted supporter of his alma mater, giving his leadership, remarkable energies and profound insight to ambitious projects to advance the institution. He was the architect of today's Foundation, and his visionary guidance has been an inspiration to all of his fellow trustees. Irving Goldstein was an irreplaceable mentor whose wise counsel will be greatly missed. The Queens College Foundation, Inc.
        GOLDSTEIN-Irving. IDT Corporation mourns the loss of Irving Goldstein, a dear friend and distinguished member of its board of directors. Prior to joining the IDT board, Irving Goldstein was a former CEO of INTELSAT, a commercial consortium of more than 140 countries that own and operate a global system of satellites. Before that Mr. Goldstein served as Chairman and CEO of the Communications Satellite Corporation (CMSAT). The IDT Family offer their sincere condolences to the Goldstein Family.
