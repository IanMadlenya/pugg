
        WARRICK--Ruth. Died Saturday, January 15th, in her Manhattan home of complications from pneumonia. She was 88. She made her film debut in the Classic Citizen Kane and received a Lifetime Achievement Emmy Award in May 2004 for her work on All My Children. In 1983 she was awarded the first National Arts in Education Award and served as a Dropout Prevention consultant to President Kennedy. Certified as a licensed teacher from Unity Church, Ms. Warrick's indomitable spirit and zest for life touched all who knew her. She's survived by her children Karen Langenwalter, Jon Rolf, Robert McNamara, her grandson Erik Rolf and six great grandchildren. A Public Memorial Service celebrating her Life and Career will be announced at a later date.
