
        ROOSEVELT-Theodore III. Of Beaumont at Bryn Mawr, PA. Died on Wednesday,,May 2, 2001. Husband of the late Anne Mason Babcock Roosevelt and is survived by his son Theodore Roosevelt IV, daughter-in-law Constance Rogers Roosevelt and grandson Theodore Roosevelt V. The service and interment will be private. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be to the Wilderness Society, 1615 M Street NW, Washington, D.C. 20036.
