
        SHAPIRO-Ivan. The New York Society for Ethical Culture records with sorrow the death of their former President, Trustee and member on November 4, 1998. Mr. Shapiro was a much respected lawyer and civil libertarian. We extend our sympathy to his wife Maria and his family. A memorial service will be held November 16, 11AM at The Society, 2 W. 64 St. Dr. Judith D. Wallach, Pres. Dr. Bob Kaufmann, Interim Senior Leader Trustees and Staff of The New York Society for Ethical Culture
