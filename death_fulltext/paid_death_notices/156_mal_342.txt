
        ROSENBERG-Alfred A./;d Passed away January 20 at age 84, after a spirited 12-year fight against illness. Loving and devoted husband and partner of Judy R. Rosenberg. Confidant, mentor and pal to numerous nieces, nephews, grandnieces, grandnephews and cousins. Brother of Lillian Sherman, Joseph Rosenberg, and the late Sylvia Ginsberg, Simon and Louis Rosenberg. True friend to many, both within and outside his beloved practice of law, which was centered on creditors' rights and Federal Bankruptcy Court in Brooklyn. He inspired all with his intelligence, sensitivity and courage. As humanitarians and philanthropists, he and Judy have been lifelong supporters of medical research, the performing arts, and education, especially in the establishment of scholarship funds. Contributions may be made to Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University, Jack and Pearl Resnick Campus, Room B803, Center for Genetic and Translational Medicine, 1300 Morris Park Avenue, Bronx, NY 10461. Services on Sunday, January 23, 12:30PM, at The Riverside, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Family is at home Monday and Tuesday, 3-6 and 7-9PM.
        ROSENBERG-Alfred. The Board of Trustees, The Council of Conservators and Staff of The New York Public Library extend their condolences to Judy Rosenberg on the death of her beloved husband, Alfred. Mr. and Mrs. Rosenberg have been great friends of the Library for over 25 years, particularly in their commitment to The New York Public Library for the Performing Arts and the Conservators program. We extend deepest sympathy to the family. Samuel C. Butler, Chair Paul LeClerc, President The New York Public Library Astor, Lenox and Tilden Foundations
