
        BENVENISTE-Sylvia. Age 81, on June 16, 2002, after complications from a stroke. Beloved mother of Richard; grandmother of Jonathan, Danielle, Elizabeth and Olivia; cherished mother-in-law of Donna Grell. Preceded in death by her husband, Isaac; daughter, Lorraine; brothers, Milton, Paul and Alexander, and sister, Leah. Funeral service will be held at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I., at 12:30 PM, on Wednesday, June 19, 516-295-3100. Family will receive guests at the home of Steven and Sherri Davis, 588 Lakeview Ave., Rockville Centre, L.I., 5-7 PM, June 19; and at Richard and Donna's home in Washington, D.C., on Thursday, June 20, where shiva service will begin at 7 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Religious Action Center for Reform Judaism, 2027 Massachusetts Ave, NW, Washington, DC 20036.
