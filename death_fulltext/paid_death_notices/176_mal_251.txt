
        EISENSTADT-Betty. 91 years old. Widow of Benjamin Eisenstadt, longtime Trustee and benefactor of Maimonides Medical Center, who served as Secretary, Vice Chairman and Chairman during his 20 year tenure. Among Maimonides' major facilities, a testament to their generosity, are the Eisenstadt Administration Building and the Gellman Pavilion, named in memory of Dr. Abraham Gellman, Mrs. Eisenstadt's brother. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Eisenstadt's children: Marvin, Ira, Gladys, Ellen, and the entire Eisenstadt family. Martin D. Payson, Chairman, Board of Trustees Stanley Brezenoff, President and CEO Maimonides Medical Center
        EISENSTADT-Betty. Eisenstadt, Vice Chairman and Officer, Board of Directors. Sincere condolences are extended to the entire Eisenstadt family who are all significant participants in the growth and development of our Hospital Center. Joel A.
