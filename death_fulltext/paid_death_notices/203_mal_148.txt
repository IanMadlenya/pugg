
        GLENN--William Love, husband, father, grandfather, uncle, dear friend, retired company president and avid sailor, died unexpectedly in the early morning hours of Sunday, December 7, in St. Petersburg, Florida. He was 59. In addition to his wife, Maxine, and son, William, Mr. Glenn is survived by three grandchildren, Claire Elise, John Alden Grice, and Emma Grace Glenn; two brothers, Joseph Wooderson ''Woody'' Glenn III and Lawrence ''Larry'' Randolph Glenn; and a sister, Sarah Glenn Meyer. A public viewing will be held at DeVine Funeral Home, 293 South Street, Oyster Bay, Long Island, NY, from 2 p.m. to 5 p.m. and 7 p.m. to 9 p.m. on Friday, December 12. A public funeral service will be held at Christ Episcopal Church, 61 East Main Street, Oyster Bay, NY, on Saturday, December 13 at 9:30 a.m. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the ''Will Glenn fund for the love of sailing'' c/o Oyster Bay Sailing Foundation, PO Box 720 Oyster Bay, NY 11771.
