
        RYAN-Winston Durrell. On Dec. 26, 2002 in Callahan, FL. Graduate of the New York State Maritime Academy. He also served in the US Navy during the Korean Conflict. Beloved father of Brooke Byrd & Clifford Ryan. Loving brother of Nancy Rain and Mary Krepela. Also, survived by four grandchildren and one great grandchild. There will be a graveside service Friday, January 3, 2003, 11:00AM at the Nassau Knolls Cemetery, Port Washington, New York.
