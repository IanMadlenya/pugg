
        STRUTHERS-Harvey J. In Brunswick, ME. November 13, 2000, age 83, born in Minneapolis, MN, died from Parkinson's Disease. Formerly of Darien, CT, and Naples, FL, an executive of 40 years with CBS. He is survived by his wife, Helen Gould Struthers of Brunswick, sons Harvey J. Struthers, Jr., of Chicago and Thomas G, Struthers of Freeport, a sister, Martha S. Farley of Evanston, IL. Also survived by three grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Services will be private in Yarmouth, Maine with interment in Minneapolis, Minn. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to Struthers Parkinson Center, 6701 Country Club Dr., Golden Valley, MN 55427 or Courage Center, 3915 Golden Valley Rd., Minneapolis, MN 55422. Services by Lindquist Funeral Home, One Mayberry Lane, Yarmouth, ME.
