
        PHILIPS-Lee. Television director. Passed away peacefully in his home in Brentwood, CA, from complications due to Parkinson's disease March 3, 1999. He was 72 years old. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in his name to the National Parkinson Foundation, Inc., 1501 NW 9th Ave., Miami, FL 33136-1494. He is survived by his two daughters, Caitlin Philips and Julie Philips.
