
        BLAKE-Esther. Born 7/12/1898, died 10/11/97. Loving wife of the late George Blake, she lived in Manhattan Beach, Brooklyn until 1985, when she moved to Pound Ridge. She is survived by her children Olive Colon of VT, Marshall Blake of CT, Steven Blake of Pound Ridge, NY, eight grandchildren and five great-grandchildren, as well as her sisters Lola Farer and Grace Cooper. Donations in her name can be made to Habitat for Humanity, PO Box 351, White Plains, NY 10603.
