
        KESSELBRENNER-Israel., M.D. November 27. Beloved husband of the late Ida. He is survived by sons Michael and Dan, daughters-inlaw Doree and Sarah, and grandchildren, Kiersten, Jeffrey, Joseph, David, Sarah, Louis, Joel. Retired director of Manhattan State Hospital & Clinical Professor at Columbia Medical School. He will be missed. Services, Sunday November 30, 1:00 PM, Menorah Chapel, Union, NJ. Interment, Beth-El Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the organization which aided his escape from Nazi Europe, The American Friends Service Committee, 1501 Cherry Street, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania 19103. Shiva will be observed, from Sunday-Thursday, 58 East Sherbrooke Parkway, Livingston, New Jersey.
