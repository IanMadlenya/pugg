
        STERNBERG-Marcia, 65, of Lawrenceville, died Wednesday at home. Daughter of the late Harry Tartalsky, she is survived by her husband John Sternberg, her mother, Florence Ruben Tartalsky of Asbury Park, two sons Jim Sternberg of Mill Valley, California and Jeffrey Sternberg of Wilmington, Delaware and a daughter Hilary Gilbert of Nashua, New Hampshire. Also surviving are five grandchildren. The funeral will be held on Friday at 1 PM at Poulson & Van Hise Funeral Directors 650 Lawrence Road, Lawrenceville with Rabbi Morton Rosenthal officiating. Burial will be in Fountain Lawn Memorial Park in Ewing.
