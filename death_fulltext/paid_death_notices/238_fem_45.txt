        $(6$)BIEDERMAN--Esther. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home at Riverdale deeply mourn the passing of Esther Biederman, beloved wife of our esteemed Board member Morris E. Biederman. The Biedermans have been devoted and loyal friends of the Hebrew Home, helping to make it so special. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Morris, Dr. Neil Greenberg and the entire Biederman family and hope they will be comforted by fond memories. The Hebrew Home at Riverdale Jeffrey S. Maurer, Chairman Daniel Reingold, President and CEO
