
        KRAMER--Arthur, age 78, of Boynton Beach, FL, died Tuesday, November 28, 2006; after a long illness. A Korean War veteran, he retired as Deputy Director of the New York Housing Authority after 36 years of service. Survived by his wife Selma (nee Cohn); two children, Michael (Leslie Adams) Kramer of Lando Lakes, FL, and Tina (James) Rokuson of Randolph NJ; loving grandfather to Nicolette, Amy, Marissa and Jodi; mother-in-law, Miriam Cohn and sisters Rose Schwartz and Beatrice Garabedian. Memorial service and interment will be Friday in Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. Services conducted by Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel. (800)94-Shalom
