
        LISSNER-Louise A., (nee Auf der Heide), lifelong of West New York, New Jersey, on Monday, July 28, 1997, devoted mother of Carolyn L. Heveran, loving grandmother of Elizabeth McSwain, Henry George Ottley, and James Henry Ottley, cherished great-grandmother of 4. Daughter of the late Henry George Auf der Heide, and niece of the late New Jersey Congressman, Oscar L. Auf der Heide. Louise was a beloved and active member of the West New York community for 97 years. She ran for election as councilperson of West New York, in 1962. In 1964, Louise served as President of the West New York Library Board. A religious prayer service will be held at McCorry Brothers Funeral Home, 780 Anderson Avenue, Cliffside Park, on Wednesday, July 30, at 8:00 P.M. Funeral is private. Visiting hours on Wed. from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Memorial donations to: UNITY, 1901 NW Blue Parkway, Unity Village, MO. 64065-0001, would be appreciated.
