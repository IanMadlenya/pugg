
        FINEGOLD-Rochelle Levinson. Died July 10, 1998, age 73, after an illness sudden in onset and brief in duration. ''A lifetime is like a flash of lightning in the sky, rushing by, like a torrent down a steep mountain.'' A lifelong teacher and lover of learning, her generosity, intelligence, and integrity have touched many. Wife of the late William Finegold, she is survived by their son Jonathan and his fiancee Marcy. Service 1 PM Monday, July 13, at Sinai Chapel in Queens. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to Hadassah.
