
        FLOYD-Andrew Leslie, Ph.D. Economist, Travel Consultant. May 22, 2001. Born Laszlo Endre Fenyo; Szeged, Hungary, Sept 5, 1920. Beloved husband of Elizabeth McFall Floyd, loving father of Laszlo and Istvan Fenyo, loving grandfather of Zsolt Fenyo. Andrew cherished his family, his many friends and his garden. He loved to travel, and his sudden death in Paris came after a very happy fortnight, spent wandering through Brittany with his wife. Mount Hope Cemetery, HastingsOn-Hudson, N.Y.
