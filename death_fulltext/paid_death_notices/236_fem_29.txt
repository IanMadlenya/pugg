
        KELSEY--Judith Ann (Pazolt, Antonetti). Of Melrose, MA, formerly of San Juan, PR, passed away peacefully on August 31, 2006, after a brief illness. Judy was the beloved wife of the late Donald P. Kelsey and loving mother of Salvador William Antonetti (Rhonda) of Charleston, SC, and David Vicente Antonetti (Kimberlee) of Orlando, FL. She was also the cherished grandmother of Christina Antonetti. Donations in Judy's memory may be made to Hallmark Health Hospice. Visit www.gatelyfh.com
