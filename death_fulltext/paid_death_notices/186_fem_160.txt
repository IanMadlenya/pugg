
        DIMARZIO-Alice Gertrude Allen RN. Born on 14th December, 1907 died on Thursday, July 25th in Plymouth, Massachusetts. She was the wife of Dr. James Lawrence Flynn of Boston, MA and Rafael F. DiMarzio of Plymouth, MA. She was a 1929 graduate of the Mercy Hospital Nursing School in Springfield, MA. She leaves two children, Alice and James and grandchildren Patricia and Christina Tscharskyj. Donations to be made to the Food Allergy Initiative, 625 Madison Ave, 4th Fl, New York, NY 10022
