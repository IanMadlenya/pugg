        POLLACK--Marsha. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University and its National Women's Division are deeply grieved at the passing of our dear and caring friend, whose compassionate and dedicated leadership will be sorely missed. Marsha served with distinction as a Vice President of the National Board of the Women's Division and as President of its New Jersey Chapter. With her husband, Leon, she was a major supporter of the Gruss Magnetic Resonance Research Center on the Einstein campus. To Leon, to their daughters, Andrea Gabay and Robin Perlman, and to the entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Yeshiva University Richard M.
