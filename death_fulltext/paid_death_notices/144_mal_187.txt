
        RITZENBERG-Milton. 85. Of Washington, DC. Builder and Breeder of thoroughbred race horses, passed away on Friday, January 8, 1999. He was a graduate of Rensselaer College. Mr. Ritzenberg was past president of the Virginia Thoroughbred Association and board member of the Morven Park Equine Center. He raced extensively in England as well as in the United States. He was also a patron of the arts and affiliated with the National Gallery of Art in Washington, DC and the Virginia Museum of Art in Richmond. He is survived by his beloved wife, Grace, daughter Toni and sister Adelle (Bert) Tracy. He was the brother of the late Saul Ritzenberg. Also survived by numerous nieces and nephews. A private interment was held on Monday, January 11, 1999. Memorial Contributions may be made to the Washington Home Hospice, 3720 Upton Street, NW, Washington, DC 20016
