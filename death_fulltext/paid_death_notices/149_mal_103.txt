
        BERNHEIM-Philip G., 88. Survived by beloved wife Gioia, children Thomas and Lynn Reese, brother Richard, three grandchildren, five great-grandchildren. Died June 4, 1999 in Tequesta, FL. Also resident of Red Bank, N.J. and formerly NYC. He graduated from Wisconsin, Phi Beta Kappa and was V.P. of R. Neumann Co., an avid golfer and loved by all who knew him.
