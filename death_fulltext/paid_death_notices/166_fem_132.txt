
        KAPLAN-Dorothy Pearl nee Goldblatt. 86. Formerly of Florida, recently of Lakewood, New Jersey, died on November 14. She worked for the New York State Department of Labor for over 35 years, retiring in 1970. Her husband, Morris Henry Kaplan, died in 1989. He was associated with the New York City Mayor's Office during the Wagner and Lindsay administrations. Her sister and brotherin-law Anne and Jacob Lubitz predeceased her. Surviving are sister and brother-in-law Selma and Emanuel Gershkowitz and nieces and nephews: Lucille and Michael Weiss, Rosalyn Lubitz, Allen and Mary Gershkowitz, and Fran and Rick Goldberg. Services were held on November 16.
