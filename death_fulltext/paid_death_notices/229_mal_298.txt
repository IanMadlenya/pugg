
        CURTIS--Frederick S., formerly Fritz Idstein, of Mannheim and Krefeld, Germany, passed away peacefully on February 21, 2006, at Mary Manning Walsh Home in New York shortly before his 96th birthday. He was the dearly beloved and loving husband of Ursel (nee Blumenheim) for 45 years. Cherished father of Vera Ginsberg and her companion Marsha Genard; adoring Opa of Jason and Stephanie; devoted brother of Kurt (Lisa); dear uncle of Linda (Robert) Sonderman; loving cousin of Evelyn (Joel) Ullman and their children Steven, Caren (Robert) Reich and Randie (Tony) Paterno. His humor was irrepressible to the end. Private graveside service was held at Cedar Park Cemetery.
