
        CANTOR-Dorothy Van Brink, 77. Of Greenwich, Ct and Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, passed away Saturday, Oct. 14. Mrs. Cantor, a native of New York City, she was active in volunteer work in Greenwich, CT. She was an avid reader who also traveled the world extensively. She is survived by her husband of nearly 60 years, Samuel C. Cantor, retired Executive Vice President of Mutual Life Insurance Company of New York, two daughters Judy C. Stone of Rockaway, NJ and Barbara C. Palm of Ponte Vedra, FL, & three grandchildren, Jeremy Stone, and Alicia and Michael Palm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be sent to The Greenwich Library, Putnam Ave., Greenwich, Ct 06830.
