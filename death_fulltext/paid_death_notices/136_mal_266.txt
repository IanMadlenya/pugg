
        HALPERN-Milton. May 21, 1906May 16, 1998. The Halpern family cherishes the memory of this passionate participant in a turbulent century, whose monumental intellect, brilliant musicianship, and political acumen inspired so many. His daughters Lucy Johns and Ellen Kauffman, their children, spouses, aunts, and generations of cousins celebrate Milton's extraordinary life and mourn our loss.
