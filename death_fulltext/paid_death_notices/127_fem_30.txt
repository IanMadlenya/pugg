
        EIDELMAN WACHS-Dr. Barbara. We express our deepest sympathy on the untimely passing of Dr. Barbara Eidelman Wachs in Israel while attempting to rescue a drowning child. Dr. Wachs was a source of great pride to her family and will be sorely missed. We extend sincerest condolences to her husband, Saul, children, parents, brothers and their families, Aaron and Sherry Eidelman, supporters of Shaare Zedek medical Center and Prof. Arthur Eidelman, Director of the Neonatology Department at Shaare Zedek Medical Center and his wife Arleen. May the family be comforted amongst the the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and may her memory be a blessing. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chairperson of the Board Menno Ratzker, President
