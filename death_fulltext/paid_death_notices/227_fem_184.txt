
        WOLINSKY--Dr. Gloria F. Died peacefully at home in New York City, surrounded by her family on December 15, 2005. Dr. Wolinsky was a Professor Emeritus at Hunter College of the City of New York and a former Chairperson of the Education Foundation. Beloved wife of the late Marvin Wolinsky, loving mother of Dr. Philip Wolinsky, devoted sister of Grace Weiner, also aunt and great aunt. She gave generously to her family, charity and friends throughout her life. Her strength of character and strength during her struggle with her illness were inspirational. This extraordinary woman who was always caring and charitable will be greatly missed by all who knew her. Contributions may be made to a charity of your choice.
