
        SHIPMAN -- Pauline (nee Tourre) of Manhattan, formerly of Muskegon, MI, on March 10, 2006. She was active in Civic Theatre playing the leading roles in ''The Night of January 16th'' and ''The Man Who Came to Dinner''. Founding partner in Tourre's Soda Grille until her marriage to George E. Shipman III. After his death, she owned and managed Shipman Industries. She retired from Time, Inc. where she had worked for SI, TIME and People magazines. She also appeared in TV commercials and print ads, and was a member of SAG and AFTRA.
