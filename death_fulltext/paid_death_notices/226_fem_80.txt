
        GREENWALD--Carrie Weisberg, died peacefully in the Bronx, NY at 95 years. A social worker and for more than 50 years, a teacher in New York City schools, she remained independent through her final days. She was a loving wife to Herbert Greenwald, loving mother to John and Mark, loving grandmother to Joshua & Rachel. A great lover of the arts, poetry, ideas and nature, she was an engaging and strong willed spirit to the end. A private service will be held.
