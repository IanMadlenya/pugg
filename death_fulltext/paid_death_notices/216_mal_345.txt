
        PECK--Malvin. Congregation Rodeph Sholom notes with sorrow the passing of our longtime, devoted Honorary Trustee and congregant for almost 50 years, Malvin Peck, beloved husband of Joyce Peck. Mal was also a co-President of our Brotherhood and served as President of the Metropolitan Council on the NFTB. Our deepest condolences to his wife, his son Charles and his wife, Bonnie, and his granddaughters Teren and Emily. We will miss his devotion. Robert N. Levine, DD, Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
