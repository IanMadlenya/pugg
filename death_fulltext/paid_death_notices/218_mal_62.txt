
        ENGEL--Morris. Age 86, on March 5, 2005. Filmmaker and photographer died peacefully in his sleep at home surrounded by friends and family. He is remembered as a kind and compassionate person. Beloved father to Andy (Magda), Mary (Darryl), grandfather to Zachary, brother to Pearl and Helen. Until the end, Morris, who always welcomed people with his firm handshake, lived by the motto ''the older I get the better it gets.'' Proud WWII Veteran. He leaves behind a wonderful legacy of photographs and films for all to enjoy. A Memorial Service is being planned.
