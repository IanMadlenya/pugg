
        SILVER-Julius. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Julius Silver. Known for his generosity and devotion to the New York Jewish community, Julius contributed to UJA-Federation through the Jurodin Fund. He will be missed by the entire UJA-Federation family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
