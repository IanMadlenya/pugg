
        LIPPINCOTT--Joan B., on June 20, 2003, of cancer at age 70. Widow of David, mother of Christopher, and grandmother of David S. Sister of Carol Ely and Betty Rank. Mrs. Lippincott was a graduate of Smith College ('51) and the Masters School ('47). She had been a member of the New York Junior League, the Cosmopolitan Club, the City Gardens Club, and the Colonial Dames. She will be deeply missed by all those whose lives she touched. A memorial service will be held at the St. James Church, Madison Ave. at 71st St., Wednesday, July 2nd at 2pm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the St. James Church, 865 Maidson Ave., NY NY 10021 or the American Cancer Society. >>AD#
