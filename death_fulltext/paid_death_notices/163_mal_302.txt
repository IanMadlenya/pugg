
        BAYER-Jack. A philanthropist & devoted father and grandfather, died Wednesday at Mt. Sinai Hospital. Born in Burlington, VT on December 22, 1907, he married Anne Pilver Bayer in 1936. The two lived in Claremont, NH during the 58 years of their loving marriage. Mr. Bayer owned a number of stores in the New England area and was a tireless fundraiser for UJA. He is survived by his children: Marilyn Fassberg, Deborah Paley, Michael Bayer and Susan Bayer Fishman, eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. He will be missed by all those he supported in his lifetime.
