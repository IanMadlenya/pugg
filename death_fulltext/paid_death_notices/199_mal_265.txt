
        LEVINE--Rose. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of a woman of compassion and grace, the beloved mother of Hirschell E. Levine, an esteemed member of our Board of Overseers whose extraordinary commitment to biomedical research has helped to advance many programs at Einstein. To Mr. Levine, the recipient of an honorary degree from Yeshiva University; his wife, Deanna; their children, grandchildren and the entire family, we extend our heartfelt condolences.
