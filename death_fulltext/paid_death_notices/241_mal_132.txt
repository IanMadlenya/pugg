
        KRET--Jacob. The Jewish Theological Seminary mourns the passing of Rabbi Jacob Kret, a beloved tutor, spiritual guide, and mentor to many students, now rabbis, for a quarter of a century. His kindness, wisdom, and menshlikhkayt will be missed. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Arnold Eisen, Chancellor-elect Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
