
        MEYERS-Aaron (Archie). Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member, Archie Meyers. Archie and his beloved wife, Corinne, have been members of Rodeph Sholom for over 50 years. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his wife and daughters, Sherri and Carole, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
