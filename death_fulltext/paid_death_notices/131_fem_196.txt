
        MILES-Frances. 76. Formerly of Manhasset, New York, widow of Edward F. Miles Jr., died of cancer December 22, 1997. She was mother to Mary Jackson, of Raleigh, NC, Edward F. Miles of Alexandria, VA, the late Francine Hyland of Manhasset, NY, Michele Dunn of Wilton, CT, and Christine Miles of Southampton, NY. She had 13 granchildren, and seven great grandchildren. Funeral Mass at St. Mary's RC Church, Manhasset, NY on January 6, 1998 at 9:45AM. Interment will follow at Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn.
