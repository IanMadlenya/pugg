
        LOOKER-Charles. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Charles Looker, a cherished friend, exemplary leader, and lifetime trustee of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies. We extend our deepest sympathies to Charles' sons, Andrew and Thomas; to his dear companion, Miriam Horowitz; and to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
