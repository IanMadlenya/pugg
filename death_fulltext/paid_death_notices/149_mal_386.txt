
        HILLER-Ira, on June 23, 1999, at age 58. We deeply mourn the untimely death of our dear friend, after a heroic battle with cancer. Son of the late Edith and Morris Hiller, brother of the late Arnold Hiller, former partner of Yohalem, Gillman and Co. He will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved him. Services today, 10AM at ''The Riverside,'' Amsterdam Ave. and 76th St. Donations to the American Cancer Society.
