
        SCHNEBERG-Esther. Deceased October 16th, died as she wished, at her new home in Portland, OR. She was a valiant survivor of cancer, but she could shadowbox no longer. She will be remembered as a champion of the disenfranchised-a teacher and guidance counselor at Thomas Jefferson High School. The funeral service will take place at Parkside Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Ave. in Brooklyn on Sunday, October 21st, at 11:30 AM, 718-338-1500.
