
        BOBERG--Regina L., age 92, of Urbana died on Wednesday, September 15, 2004. She was born January 29, 1912 in Pittsburgh, PA to August and Magdalena (Farbacher) Eichner. She is survived by her daughter Therese (Edward) Young of Urbana; son Edward (Phyllis) Boberg of London, OH. Preceded in death by her parents, and her husband, Gerard C. Boberg. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Monday, September 20, 2004 in St. Mary's Church. Burial will be in Holy Sepulchre Cemetery in Rochester, NY.
