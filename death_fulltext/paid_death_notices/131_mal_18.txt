
        GOLDSTEIN-Marvin. The Officers and Board of the American Friends of the Hebrew University deeply mourn the passing of Dr. Marvin C. Goldstein of Atlanta. Dr. Goldstein was an honorary fellow of the University and a founder of the Hebrew University-Hadassah School of Dental Medicine. His contributions will benefit many generations of students and researchers. We express our most sincere condolences to his wife Rita and his entire family. Keith L. Sachs President Lawrence Newman Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan Executive Vice President
