
        HACKETT-John E. Suddenly, on Friday, April 2nd, 1999. John E. Hackett, beloved husband of Colette Lavoie. Born on May 15, 1943, Mr. Hackett was the son of Marielle Teryazos nee Gohier and of the late Guy Hackett. He was the father of Sophie, Guy and Alexandre whose mother is Lalage Hackett nee Wright. He is also mourned by his brother Robert (Susana Abreu) whose daughter is Nadia Hackett. Mr. Hackett is resting at the Desourdy Wilson Funeral Home, 4 Vale Perkins, Mansonville, Quebec, J0E 1X0, 450-263-1212, fax 450-263-9557. Visitation will be held on Friday, April 9, 1999 from 7-10 PM. Funeral service will be held on Saturday, April 10, 1999 at 2 PM from St. Cajetan Catholic Church in Mansonville followed by the interment at St. Cajetan Catholic Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory can be made to the Heart and Stroke Foundation of Sherbrooke, 2630 King Ouest, Sherbrooke, Quebec, J1J 2H1 or to the University Geriatric Institute Foundation of Sherbrooke, 1036 Belvedere South, Sherbrooke, Quebec, QC J1H 4C4 would be gratefully appreciated by the family. Please take note: Saturday, the day of the funeral, the Funeral Home will be opening at noon.
