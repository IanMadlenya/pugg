
        WEIL--James We mourn the passing of our dear friend Jimmy Weil. He filled our lives with warmth and happiness. Our sympathy goes out to his wife Gloria and their children. We will all miss him. June and Gene Siegel
        $(6$)WEIL--James L. Passed away on July 7. He will be dearly missed by his wife of 53 years, Gloria; children Tony, Peter and Jennifer; daughter - in - law Lynne; grandchildren Melanie & Daniel and their mother Elsie; sister Pauline Schwartz and his niece Judith SchwartzEprile and nephew Frederick Schwartz and their families. Born in New York City on June 15, 1929, Weil attended the Fieldston School and received a BA from the University of Chicago. He had a distinguished career as a poet and publisher and touched many lives with his kindness. Services Monday 10AM Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the Hospice and Palliative Care of Westchester or University of Chicago College Fund.
