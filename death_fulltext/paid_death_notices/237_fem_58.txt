
        TAUSSIG--Cecilia F., 68, of New York City, died Saturday, October 7, 2006, at Lenox Hill Hospital of emphysema. Blessed with a sharp wit, a sense of humor, and great curiosity, Celia was a loyal tennis fan, loved musical theater and word puzzles, read voraciously, but most of all, valued her friends. Donations in her memory can be made to the Memorial SloanKettering Guttman Diagnostic Center, 55 Fifth Ave., New York, NY 10003, or for emphysema research to the American Lung Association, 61 Broadway, New York, New York 10006.
