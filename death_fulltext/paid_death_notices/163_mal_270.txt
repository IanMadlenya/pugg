
        MAVETY-George W. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member Friar George Mavety on August 19, 2000. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. A memorial service will be held Thursday, August 24th, 3PM at Saint Peter's Lutheran Church, 619 Lexington Ave at 54 Street, NYC. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
