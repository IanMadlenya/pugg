
        DeBLASIO-Jo. A painter, known by the name of Jo Warner, died on October 25, 1999 from complications of bone marrow cancer (multiple myeloma). Raised in Idaho Falls, and a graduate of the University of Colorado and the Art Students League of New York, she had many one woman exhibitions during her career at galleries including the Camino and Phoenix in New York. Two of her oil paintings were included in the Museum of Modern Art's Circulating Exhibition from 1961 to 1963 and were viewed throughout the U.S. and Europe. She was married for 29 years to Peter E. DeBlasio, and she was the very loving mother of Caralee DeBlasio of Manhattan and Alessandra DeBlasio of Washington, DC.
