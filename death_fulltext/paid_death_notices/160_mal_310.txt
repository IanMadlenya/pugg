
        DiGIORGIO-Joseph. On May 26, of New York City. Beloved brother of Helen DiLello and Salvator DiGiorgio; loving uncle of John, Jon, JoAnn, Christine and Nicole. Joe, whose work was viewed nationally, devoted his life to painting American landscapes. Reposing at Cusimano and Russo, 2005 West 6th St., Brooklyn, Monday and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-10 PM. Funeral Mass 9:45 AM on Wednesday, St. Finbar R.C. Church.
