
        NELSON-Leah (nee Borowitz). On May 6, 1997, at age 94. Beloved wife, for 66 years, of the late Murray. Devoted mother of Melvin J. Nelson and Paula, and of the late Stanley I. Nelson. Cherished grandmother of Wendy, Lisa, John and Jane Nelson, and Mark and Christopher Nelson. Loving great grandmother of Carly, Adam, Lindsay and Annie, and Noah Nelson. Adored aunt and cousin. Her passing signals the end of an era. She was truly unique, and will be dearly missed. Interment private.
