
        MATHEZ-Helen Juliette. The Youth Foundation notes with sorrow the passing of Helen Mathez, its former long-time Executive Administrator. She will be missed by her friends at the Foundation and the many students she helped over the years. A. David Russell, Esq., President Mrs. Donald M. Liddell, Vice President S.
