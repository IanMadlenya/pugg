
        NUTKU--Gail Kohns. On April 2, 2005. Born in NYC August 3, 1933. Survived by her three children, Rana, Kerim, and Suzan; five grandchildren, Sophie, Annie, Mimi, Jalia, and Imara; brother, Lee; sister, Ann; three nephews, Clyde, Peter, and Daniel; and niece, Sarah. Daughter of the late Paul L. Kohns and Katharine Bach Kohns, Gail attended Rye Country Day School, Cranbrook School, Oberlin College, and The Katherine Gibbs School. She was an administrator and comptroller for ALAH Architects, and will be remembered by her family and friends for her love of travel, languages, music, books, and philanthropy.
