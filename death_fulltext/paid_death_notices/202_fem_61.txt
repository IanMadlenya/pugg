
        BAKER -- Kathleen O'Neil. Passed away peacefully November 7, in Halifax, Nova Scotia. She was predeceased by her beloved husband Sylvester S. Baker. She is grieved by her daughter Nancy Baker Weeren, son-in-law Donald, and family. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Smith College, she had a distinguished career in the New York City public school system as an inspiring teacher and later administrator at Julia Richman High School. She will be deeply missed by all who were touched by her caring, courage and joie de vivre.
