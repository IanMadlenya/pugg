
        RAND--Sidney, M.D., 81 years old, after several years of illness, passed away on January 10, 2007. He was the beloved husband of Marcia and loving father to David, Jonathan, and Sara. He served in the U.S. Navy in WWII, then attended medical school in Switzerland. He practiced otolaryngology for 40 years in Elmhurst, NY. He was a kind, compassionate man, who cared very much for his family, patients, and friends. He will be missed by all who knew him.
