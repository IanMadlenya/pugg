
        BILDNER-Bertram S., D.D.S. Of Bedford, N.Y., past president of the New York State Dental Association, on June 4, 2002 at age 69. Dr. Bildner, a graduate of New York University College of Dentistry, was a periodontist in private practice in the Bronx, a clinical professor at Albert Einstein College of Medicine and Dentistry and Executive Director of the Bronx County Dental Society. He was a member of the New York State Society of Periodontology, the American College of Dentists, the International College of Dentists and the Pierre Fauchard Academy. Dr. Bildner was the recipient of the New York State Dental Association Distinguished Service Award in recognition of his service to organized dentistry.
