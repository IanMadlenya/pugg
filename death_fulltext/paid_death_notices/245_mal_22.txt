
        CHAMBERS--Bruce William, Ph.D., nationally recognized historian of 19th and 20th century American art died Saturday, May 26 at the age of 65, after a long illness. A memorial service will be held at Trinity Episcopal Church on the Green in New Haven, Connecticut at 11:00 am on Saturday, June 9th. A celebratory luncheon will be held immediately following the church undercroft. Contributions in his name may be made to Trinity Episcopal Church, 129 Church Street, Suite 705, New Haven, CT 06510. Services are with Hawley Lincoln Memorial, 493 Whitney Avenue.
