
        HELLMAN-Jack. On August 7, 2001. Beloved husband of Anne. Devoted father of Renee, Ronald and Barbara and father-in-law of Norman, Sheri and Lenard. Loving and proud grandfather of Suzanne, Andrew, Michelle, Jeffrey and Sarah. He dedicated his life to his family. He gave unconditionally to his friends, his profession and to his heritage. His passion for life has been a source of strength for all of us and he will be deeply missed. Services Thursday 11AM at ''Little Neck Jewish Center'', 4910 Little Neck Parkway, Little Neck, New York.
