
        LUSTBADER-Morris S. ''Bud,'' died peacefully in New York on June 20th, 2000 at the age of 92. Son of the late Samuel and Sarah Lustbader, for many years a civil engineer in this city, Bud was educated at Exeter and Yale, served proudly in the Seabees on Guadalcanal in World War II and became a commander USNR, married the late Marion Lustbader, and is now mourned by his daughter, Susan Friedman, his sister, Ruth L. Israel, his beloved friend, Joan Strausberg, & his many nieces, nephews and friends. A private memorial service will be held Monday, June 26th. Contributions may be made to Central Synagogue, 123 East 55th Street, New York City, for its Caring Committee.
