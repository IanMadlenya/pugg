
        GORDON--Lynne Lewin died peacefully at home November 26th after a short illness. Loving wife of the late Al Gordon, wonderful mother to Diane and Ronni Gordon, mother-in-law to David Koven and devoted sister to Margaret Borofsky and sister-inlaw to William Grossman. Beloved grandmother of Ben, Lily, Joe, Katie and Sam and aunt of six nieces and nephews and 15 great-nieces and nephews. Served her county during World War II testing trucks and instruments. Owned Lynne's Specialty Shop and was a successful jewelry designer and talented artist. Her humor, strength and warmth made her a friend to many who will miss her grace and beauty. Services Thursday 3 pm at The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam. Contributions to Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60th Street, New York, NY 10022
