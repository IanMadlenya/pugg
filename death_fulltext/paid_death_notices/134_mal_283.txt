
        CRUPE-Santo W. Esq., March 25, 1998. Beloved husband of Isabelle (nee Tuzio). Devoted father of Dorothy Tomasulo, William Santo and Peter James. Loving grandfather of Christine Ayers, William Santo Jr. and Elizabeth Crupe, and Stephen and Anthony Tomasulo. Born in Albany, New York, he was a graduate of Columbia College and St. John's Law School. He practiced law in New York City for almost 50 years. An Army veteran of W.W. II, he served as a military governor in Italy, attaining the rank of captain. He also served as a judge advocate in the U.S. Army Reserves, retiring as lieutenant colonel in 1964. Reposing at Ralph Aievoli & Son, Inc., 1275 65 Street, Brooklyn, New York.
