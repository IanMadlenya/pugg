
        MEADE--Reverend James C. on January 26, 2005. Jim was ordained a White Father of Africa in 1951 in Canada. Jim spent over seventeen years in Mwanza Africa. Jim was awarded the Distinguished Flying Cross, while on a bombing mission over Germany during WW II. He also spent twenty-three months in Stalag Luft III. Dear brother of Jane Maloney & Thomas Meade. Cherished brother-inlaw of Richard Prince and John Macklin. Also survived by many nieces & nephews. Friends may call Thursday 25 & 7-9 P.M. at Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221st Street, Queens Village, N.Y. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, 10:30 A.M. at Queen of Peace Residence. Interment Long Island National Cemetery. In lieu of Flowers, donations to Queen of Peace Residence would be appreciated. For information Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home. New Hyde Park, NY.
