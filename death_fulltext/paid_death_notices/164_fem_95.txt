
        WILLKIE-Arlinda F. Born July 31, 1946; died on Sept 7, 2000 from breast cancer. She was courageous, graceful, loving and beloved. We thank her friends, caretakers, nurses and doctors. She is survived by her family including her daughter, Julia T. Miranda; her brother and sister-in-law Frederick & Ida; her sister Julia; her brother Hall; Tom Craveiro; Riccardo Salmona; and her pug Peg. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the Benheim Cancer Center or the Red Cross of Greenwich, CT. Service Monday, September 11 at 4 PM, First Congregational Church Old Greenwich, CT.
        WILLKIE-Arlinda F. The chairmen, officers, directors, brokers and employees of Brown Harris Stevens mourn the passing of Arlinda F. Willkie, sister of Julia F. Willkie & Hall F. Willkie, Executive Vice Presidents of Brown Harris Stevens. We extend our utmost sympathies to Julia, Hall and Arlinda's daughter, Julia Miranda, and the entire Willkie family.
