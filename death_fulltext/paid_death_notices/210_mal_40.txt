
        LANE--William Hustace Jr. 96, died July 3, 2004, in Mt. Pleasant, SC. For information, contact J. Henry Stuhr, Inc., Mount Pleasant Chapel. The former Comptroller of Columbia University is predeceased by his wife Elizabeth Nesbitt Lane, his parents, William H. and Josephine Bassett Lane, and brother John B. Lane. Surviving are children William H. Lane III of Los Altos Hills, CA, and Susan Lane Henderlite of Mt. Pleasant, SC; four grandchildren; five great-grandchildren; and three brothers. Interment in Darien, CT, at a later date.
