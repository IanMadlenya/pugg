
        LEE-Linda C., 34. While attending a conference at the World Trade Center in New York City, September 11, 2001. Beloved daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Myong H. Lee of Silver Spring, Maryland, and sister of Eddie S. Lee. Cherished by her many friends. Linda was a 1990 graduate of the University of Maryland where she earned a dual Bachelors of Science degree in Finance and Zoology. She also received a law degree from Fordham University in 1994. Linda lived for a year in Japan where she taught English to businessmen & students, and studied Japanese. She was also an accomplished classical pianist. She joined Jennison Associates in March of this year as Senior Associate, Corporate Administration and had previously worked at Morgan Stanley and Chase Manhattan Bank. Linda was a vivacious, caring and remarkable person who made an impact on many people. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her. Services to be held at the Park Avenue Christian Church, 1010 Park Avenue (at East 85th St), November 3rd at Noon.
