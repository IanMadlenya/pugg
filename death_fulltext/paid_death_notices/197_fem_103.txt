
        LEVINSON--Rose, 95, in Tamarac, FL, on June 5th, 2003. Formerly of NYC. Proud first generation American who lived the 20th century. Adored, loving and devoted mother to Marcy and to Elyse Mintz. Caring grandmother to Janna and Claudia and great-grandmother to Emily, Jared and Tauby. One of the first women to be licensed in the State of New York as an insurance adjuster in the early 1950s. Active in the Fulton Senior Citizen Center in Chelsea and in local Board of Elections. Traveled in the US, Canada and Mexico as well as in the Caribbean, Europe and Southeast Asia. She loved New York and Florida and was loved by her friends. ''Live life to the fullest, then put it away.'' >>AD#
