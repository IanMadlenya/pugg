        KRONENGOLD - Marjorie. DOROT mourns the loss of our peerless woman of valor. Marjorie set new standards of excellence for the agency through her meticulous program preparation, and added a grace note of beauty through her exquisite eye. Her kindness and heartfelt empathy for the elderly continue to infuse DOROT's work, and we will carry her in our hearts always. Deepest condolences to her beloved Toby, and to her father, Harold Franks. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Vivian Fenster Ehrlich, Executive Director Joel D. Kazis, President
        KRONENGOLD - Marjorie. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member Marjorie Kronengold. We will miss her warmth and caring, her selfless devotion to family, friends and community. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her beloved husband, Toby, and her loving father, Harold Franks, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
