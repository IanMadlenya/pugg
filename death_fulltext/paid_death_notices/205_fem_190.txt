
        ALDEN -- Barbara, beloved mother of Christopher, David and Jennifer, passed away on February 11, 2004. She was greatly loved by her late husband, playwright Jerome Alden. As a creative, special education teacher at the Reece School and August Aichhorn Center, she changed many lives for the better. As a dancer, she performed in the original production of ''Annie Get your Gun'' & ''On the Town.'' Family and friends will miss her young spirit and loving attention.
