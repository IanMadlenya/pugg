
        ROSEN--Lawrence. August 11, 1925--October 16, 2005. A patent attorney in Manhattan for forty years and an ardent patron of the arts, he traveled extensively and lived in Greenwich Village, London, and Tokyo. In 1992, he retired to Del Mar, California. Survived by brother Fred Rosen, sister Sue Graybar, and families. His humor and generosity of heart will be with us forever. Graveside Service, Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Iselin, NJ, November 27, 2005.
