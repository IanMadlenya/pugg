
        DuBRIN-Harry L. Jr. Age 81. Of Altamont and NYC died Friday, Oct. 11, 2002 in Albany. He was the Managing Director of Pension Assets for Metropolitan Life Insurance Company & former Assistant Director and Legal Counsel for New York State Teachers' Retirement System. He is survived by his wife, Carolyn DuBrin & family. A memorial service Sat., Oct. 19th, 2PM at the Altamont Reformed Church, Altamont, N.Y.
