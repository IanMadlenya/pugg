
        TUCKERMAN-David, 75. On June 11 of cancer, at the Beth Israel Hospice. He loved music, food and wine. He was independent in mind and spirit; he charmed all who encountered him. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to Young Concert Artists, Inc, 250 West 57th St, Suite 1222, New York, NY 10019. He is survived by his wife Anne, his children Laurette and Claire, sister Ruth Wolfson, brothers Richard and Bert, nieces, nephews, and three grandchildren.
