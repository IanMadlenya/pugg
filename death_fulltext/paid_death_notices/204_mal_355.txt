
        STREBEL -- Ralph F., Jr. Ph.D., 80, of Canastota, NY, passed away following a lengthy illness on January 23, 2004 at his home with his family by his side. He is survived by his wife, Christine, by his four children, Frederick, Stephen, Deborah and Christina from his prior marriage to Melitta, and by their nine grandchildren Christopher, Matthew, Katelin, Sarah, James, Danielle, Katarina, Nicholas and Matthew. Funeral services will be held at 7PM Tuesday, January 27, preceded by calling hours from 57PM at the J. Homer Ball Funeral Home, Inc., 201 James St, Canastota, NY. Contributions in Ralph's memory may be made to Ralph F. Strebel Foundation for Comparative Medicine, New York Medical College, Office of University Development, Administration Bldg, Valhalla, NY 10595. 914-594-4550
