
        WELLINGTON--Herbert G. Jr. of Locust Valley, NY, on August 15, 2005, age 85. WWII Veteran, beloved husband of Patricia. Father of Charles, James and William. Grandfather of Robin, Michael, James and Emily. Service and interment private.
        WELLINGTON--Herbert G., Jr. The Board of Directors, Officers and employees of H.G. Wellington & Co. Inc. mourn the passing of our Chairman. We send our heartfelt condolences to his family.
