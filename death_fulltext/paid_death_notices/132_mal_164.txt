
        GRAHAM-Canon John Webb, E.D., Q.C., B.A., LI.B., D.S.Litt., U.E. Peacefully at home, on Friday, January 9, 1998 in his 86th year. Beloved husband of Natasha. Loving father of Ann Grahman Calderisi and Ted Rogers. Caring fatherin-law of Loretta Rogers. Dear brother of Betty C. Graham. Cherished grandfather of Lisa, Edward, Melinda and Martha Rogers and Rafe Calderisi. Great-grandfather of Chloe Rogers. Step-father of Norman Nicholas Campbell and Tanya O'Neil. Predeceased by his wife Velma Rogers Graham. A true gentleman with a distinguished service in the law, business, the military, his church and public service. The family will receive friends at the Humphrey Funeral Home-A.W. Miles Chapel, 1403 Bayview Avenue, Toronto, from 3-5 P.M. and 7-9 PM on Sunday, January 11, 1998. Funeral service Monday, January 12, 1998, 11 o'clock Grace Church On-TheHill, 300 Lonsdale Road (bet. Ave Road and Spadina Road) Toronto. . Interment at Mount Pleasant Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to The John Webb Graham Library, Trinity College, Univ. of Toronto would be appreciated.
