
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. 86, died April 21, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Marian and devoted father of Barry, Loren and Lisa and loving grandfather of Marc, David, Sarah, Tal, Elan, Tova and Benjamin. He was CEO of Spellman High Voltage Electronics; past chair for North Shore Campaign UJA; member of the Boards of Trustees Long Island Jewish Medical Center and Worcester Polytechnic Institute and President of Gurwin Geriatric Foundation. Funeral services were held on April 22nd.
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of S. Merrill Skeist, whose enthusiastic leadership in the New York Jewish community, including his service as founding president of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Foundation, strengthened and enriched our efforts to care for those in need. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his beloved children, Barry, Loren, and Lisa, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
