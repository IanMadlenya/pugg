
        BREEN-Gerard (Jerry). On July 31, 2000, peacefully at home, surrounded by his family. Beloved husband of Bonnie (Grisar) and loving and devoted father to his children Emelia Gabrielle & Dylan Gerard. Loving son of Marie V. Breen and the late Daniel J. Breen. Son-in-law of Ben & Vivian Grisar. Faithful and grateful brother of John (Jack), Daniel, Kevin, Tommy, Kim and Anne. He was loved by his nieces and nephews, Derek, Sean, Collin, Danny, Michael, Shannon, Kristen, Kevin, & Sammy Breen, Kathleen, Daniel and Stephen Reiser, Jenna and P.J. Grisar and Allison Mikuliwich. He is also survived by his aunts, Catherine Giltenan, Ann Murphy, Rose Malone, and uncle John Malone and many cousins. Gerard was a graduate of SUNY at Oneonta. He received his Juris Doctor degree from Brooklyn Law School in 1984 and his LLM from Pace University and was an attorney for the New York City Police Department. Jerry faced his illness and untimely death with the same courage that he had when he pursued his passion for rock climbing. His life has been an inspiration to all who had the privilege of knowing him. His memory will live in our hearts forver. Reposing at Weigand-Pensa Funeral Home, 2283 Grand Ave, Baldwin. Visiting Wednesday and Thursday, 2-4 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass Friday at St. Christopher's R.C. Church, Baldwin, 10:30 AM. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery.
