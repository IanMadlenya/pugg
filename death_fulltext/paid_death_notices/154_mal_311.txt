
        REED-Donald Bailey. Of New Cannan, CT, and Isle of Palms, SC, died in Charleston, SC, November 26. Until his retirement he was President of Alice Mills, Inc, in NYC, previously with Cannon Mills and Deering-Milliken. A member of the New York Board of Trade, was active in the ''Made in America'' campaign. A graduate of Syracuse, flag officer of the Stamford Yacht Club and the Corinthian Club. Interment Thursday morning at Hoyt Funeral Home of New Cannan, CT.
