
        JONES--William Creed, died peacefully in New York City on March 29, 2004 after a brief illness. He was the devoted husband of the late Genevieve and the loving father of Thomas Lee Jones of Manhattan and of the late William Philip Jones of Wisconsin. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the American History Fund of the Acorn Foundation, 620 Park Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
