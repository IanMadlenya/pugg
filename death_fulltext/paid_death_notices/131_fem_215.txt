
        ADELMAN-Eva. The National Officers, the National Boards and the entire EMUNAH of America family deeply mourns the passing of its Honorary National President, Eva Adelman. She was the founder and first president of World EMUNAH. As a visonary and dynamic leader, she brought our organization to new heights, constantly inspiring our leaders and members. Eva was a woman of great warmth and wisdom and will be sorely missed. She lived in Israel for several decades. The funeral is being held today in Israel. We extend condolences to her beloved husband, Milton, her children, grandchildren and sister, Leah Gross. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld, National President
