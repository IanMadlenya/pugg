
        PERLMAN-Julian. The Board of Trustees and staff of The Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of Julian Perlman, a longtime member and enthusiastic supporter of the Museum. Together with his wife, the late Jane Perlman, and as President of The Norman and Rosita Winston Foundation, he was a true friend and a generous benefactor. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family and friends. He will be missed by all. Whitney Museum Of American Art Flora M. Biddle, Honorary Chairman Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Gilbert C. Maurer, President David A. Ross, Alice Pratt Brown Director
        PERLMAN-Julian. The City College of New York mourns the passing of Julian Perlman, Director of the Norman & Rosita Winston Foundation, Inc. Mr. Perlman was a strong supporter of public higher education and a firm friend of City College. On behalf of the students, faculty, staff and alumni of CCNY, I wish to extend my condolences to his family. Yolanda T. Moses, President The City College of N.Y./CUNY
