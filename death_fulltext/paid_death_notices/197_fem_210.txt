
        BRUSS--Annela. 93, died evening of June 26, 2003, at home. Born 1910, Warsaw, Poland. Lived through WWII in Warsaw and other Polish cities. Survived the German death machine onslaught in Poland. Defender of her faith, a fighter, close to God. Survived by her son Bernard Bruss. Burial at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY, Sunday, June 29, 2003. >>AD#
