
        HUTZLER--Lilo. January 14, after an extended illness which she endured with dignity and courage. Beloved wife of Harry. Devoted mother of Ronald and Michelle. Proud mother-in-law of Jay Monitz. Adored grandmother of Gary and Trevor Monitz. She will be remembered by her many friends for her indomitable spirit and love of life. Services Sunday 10:30am at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Rd, Scarsdale, NY.
        HUTZLER--Lilo. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its longtime, devoted member and extends sympathy to her bereaved family. Amy Lemle, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi Jack Stern, Rabbi Emeritus
