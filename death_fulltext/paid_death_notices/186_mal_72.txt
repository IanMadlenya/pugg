
        KEMPER-James S. Jr. The Board of Governors, Trustees, staff and members of Boys & Girls Clubs of America wish to express their profound sorrow over the passing of Mr. James S. Kemper Jr., member of our Board of Governors and a lifelong advocate for children. Mr. Kemper devoted 40 years of service to Boys & Girls Clubs; our Midwest headquarters, the Kemper Training and Service Center, is named in his honor. A recipient of the Herbert Hoover Humanitarian Award from Boys & Girls Clubs of America for his passionate commitment to America's youth, Mr. Kemper's positive influence will be felt for generations to come. We will miss him greatly. Boys & Girls Clubs of America Arnold I. Burns, Chairman Roxanne Spillett, President
