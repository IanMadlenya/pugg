
        KOEPPEL-Alfred J. Of New York City and Boca Raton, FL passed away on January 19, 2001 after a lengthy fight against cancer. Loving husband of Ruth, devoted father of Lynne and Caleb, predeceased by Sherri, Lesley and David and Sarah and Robert Cohn. Adoring grandfather of Melissa, Kyle, Allison, Max, Sam, Emily, Noah, Adam and Claire. Beloved son-in-law of Mildred Rubenstein, survived by his brother Bevin and sisters Geraldine Adler, Louise Feldman and Grace Gold. Funeral services will be held on Sunday January 21, 2001 at 10:00AM at Congregation Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65 St. Contributions to Trinity College c/o Development Office, Summit St, Hartford, CT 06106 or to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, New York, NY 10021 attention Dr. David Pfister to be used for research into head and neck cancers would be appreciated greatly.
