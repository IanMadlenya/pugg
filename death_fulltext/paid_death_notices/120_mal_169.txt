
        BARTHOLOMEO-Walter E. Regis High School mourns the loss of Walter E. Bartholomeo, affectionately known as ''The Boss''. Walter passed away on January 1, 1997, after serving the Regis community as Superintendent of Maintenance for 39 years. A memorial Mass will be celebrated at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue and East 84th Street, on Tuesday, January 21 at 2 P.M. All alumni, parents and friends of Regis are welcome. Walter has left an indelible impression on Regis. He will forever be remembered as a loyal and true friend. May Walter rest in peace.
