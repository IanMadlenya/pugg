
        ZIMMERMAN--Edward J.P. A former tax advisor to the Rockefeller family, died March 6, of heart failure, at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center near his home in New York City. He was 86 years old. A graduate of Harvard College and the University of Virginia Law School, he was a tax specialist associated with the Milbank firm and Rockefeller Family & Associates in Manhattan. He is survived by his wife, the former Starr Quigg, of New York, six children, twelve grandchildren, a brother, Peter, of Winchester, MA., and a sister, Mary, of Austin, Texas. A funeral will be held 11 A.M., March 10 at the Holy Trinity Episcopal Church in Pawling, New York.
