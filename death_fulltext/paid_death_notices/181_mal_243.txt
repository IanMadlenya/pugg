
        HAINES-G. Roger, 91. Of Greenwich, CT, died on February 19, a native of London, he spent his insurance career with Phoenix Assurance Co. and later Continental Insurance Co. He retired in 1976 as Vice President of Continental and President and director of Phoenix London Group. During his career he was based in London, Peru, San Francisco, Chicago, New York and spent time in more than 40 countries. He continued with extensive arbitration, consulting, and volunteer work following his retirement. He received the 1995 Greenwich Time Community Leader Award. A Memorial service will be held February 23, at 11 A.M. at First Presbyterian Church of Greenwich. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Call-ARide or TechnoServe.
