
        BELL-James Milton, MD, LSAPA, FACP, Colonel-M.C., U.S.A., retired, of Hudsonview, Old Post Road North, Croton on Hudson, on March 9th, son of the late Lucy H. Barnes-Bell and Charles E. Bell; beloved brother of Jacqueline B. Moorhead, June B. Brown, Lucy B. Powell, Mattie B. Bell and Randolph Bell; devoted uncle of James J. Moorhead, Jr. and brother-in-law of Rudolph W. Powell. Graduate of Meharry Medical College and Karl Menninger School of Psychiatry. Member of the Rotary and numerous other associations. Past-Commander at the Kelly Army Hospital at West Point. He was Chief of Psychiatry, Director of Clinical Services at the Berkshire Farm Center and Services for Youth, in Canaan, NY. He was the recipient of numerous awards, the latest being the Good Samaritan Award from the Parsons Child and Family Center. In recognition for his outstanding service and dedication to the Berkshire Farm Center and Services for Youth, a facility was named the James M. Bell Training Institute in his honor. Viewing 9-10 AM, funeral 10 AM, Thursday, March 13th at the Immaculate Conception Church, Route 20, New Lebanon, NY; interment Maple Grove Cemetery, Kew Gardens, NY. For more information contact Greaves Memorial Funeral Services, 718-712-1900.
