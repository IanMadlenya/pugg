
        HARRIS--Philip E., passed from this life on March 21st at the age of 91. He was a community leader and entrepreneur who lived in Scranton, PA, and Miami Beach, FL. He will be remembered for his courage in the face of adversity, his extraordinary resilience and a tremendous capacity to embrace life. He is survived by his children, Stewart (Gloria) Harris, Judy (Stuart) Poloner, Suri Harris and Malki (Gary) Gartenberg. A daughter, Susan, died in 1995. Mr. Harris' surviving siblings are Samuel and David Harris, Shirley Gross, Muriel Davidowitz and Malka Stern. In addition, he is survived by 18 grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. Funeral services and burial will be in Scranton, PA.
