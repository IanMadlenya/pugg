
        LEIR-Henry J. The Officers and Staff of American ORT mourn the passing of our friend and major supporter, Henry J. Leir. His dedication to the Jewish people, children and education is his legacy to the students of ORT. We extend heartfelt condolences to his sister, Hedy Weiger and his family.
