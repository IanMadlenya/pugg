
        CLIFFORD--Charles Blumberg, DDS. Passed away Sunday, June 25. He leaves a heartbroken Judi, his wife of 47 years. He was dearest Daddy to his beloved daughters Mindy and Amy; loving father-in-law to Andrew & Scott; and adored Poppy to Sydney, Melissa & Zach. Cliff was a vibrant, young man and a dear loving friend to so many. It is a great loss to all who knew him. Services to be held Thursday, June 29, 11 A.M. Gutterman's Memorial Chapel, 8000 Jericho Turnpike in Woodbury.
