
        MILLER-Harold E. On August 11, 2001, in his 85th year. Dearly beloved husband of Florence, his wife of 62 years. Also survived by his loving son John. Served in WWII in the U.S. Navy from 1943-1946, on the Aircraft Carrier USS Nehenta Bay. He retired from J.P. Morgan Company in 1981. He was a member of the First Presbyterian Church for many years where he served as a deacon and church elder. For nearly 20 years he was President and Treasurer of Fredericka House, a church camp for inner city children. Visitation on Monday at Harmon Funeral Home, at 571 Forest Avenue, Staten Island, New York and on Tuesday, at the Alexander Chapel at First Presbyterian at 12th Street and Fifth Avenue. Funeral on Wednesday, August 15, at 11 AM, at the church. Graveside services at Moravian Cemetery on Staten Island. Harold was deeply loved and will be sorely missed by his many friends and extended family. In lieu of flowers donations would be appreciated to the Music Fund at First Presbyterian Church at 12th St, NY, NY 10011.
