
        LaBEET-Olga Jarvis. 76 died in New Rochelle, NY. Harlem native and longtime resident of New Rochelle, she was the retired principal of NYC P.S. 134, and the Martha Neilson School. Co-Founder and Co-Director of Samaritan Women's After School Program, located at La Hermosa Church inNYC. Left to mourn her loss are her children, Bruce Labeet and Mona Lisa Crump; her mother, Ida Lake Jarvis; grandchildren Nuru Hunter, Charles Witherspoon, and a multitude of close relatives and friends. Services will be held Sunday, March 11, 6PM at New York S.D.A. Church located at 145-51 West 110th Street, NYC.
