
        FALB-Harriet M. Died July 14. There is a great loss of a lifelong campaigner for human rights and world peace. Much loved by her family and friends, who will have a memorial in celebration of her life at Long Beach Terrace, 430 Shore Road, Long Beach, at 1 PM, on Thursday, July 18. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Sloan-Kettering, or Amnesty International.
