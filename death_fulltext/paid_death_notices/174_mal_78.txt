
        BUTLER-Gerald L. Of Florham Park, NJ, former Director of Labor Relations for Allied-Signal, on July 4. Beloved husband of Nancy, devoted father of Gerald Jr., Jeffrey and Michelle, brother of Marie Lauria and William. 41 year career in labor relations, recognized for his integrity, compassion and fairness. Born Newark 1933, Sergeant, US Marine Corps, 1959 graduate Seton Hall, Nutley resident before moving to Florham Park. Mass: Monday, 11 AM, St. Vincent's, Madison, NJ. Visitation: Sunday, 2-4, 7-9, Burroughs, Kohr, Dangler, Madison. Contributions to American Diabetes Association Research Foundation.
