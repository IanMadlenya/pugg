
        COLEMAN--Joan Donovan, on February 7, 2007. Nurse, author and Lady of Charity. Beloved wife of the late John A. Coleman Jr. Loving mother of John A. Coleman III. Family will receive friends on Friday, 5-8 pm at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:30 am at Mary Manning Walsh, 1339 York Ave. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Mary Manning Walsh Home.
        $(6$)COLEMAN--Joan. St. Vincent's Hospital Manhattan, the Sisters of Charity, the Auxiliary and the St. Vincent's Hospital School of Nursing Alumnae Association mourn the passing of Joan Donovan Coleman. Mrs. Coleman, a 1950 graduate of St. Vincent's School of Nursing, with her husband John Coleman Jr., was a longtime benefactor of the hospital. Mrs. Coleman's son, John Coleman III, is a member of the hospital's Advisory Council and continues the family's commitment to our work. Joan Coleman's spirit and generosity were a treasured part of St. Vincent's for many years. We extend our deepest sympathy to this very special family. Alfred E. Smith IV, Chairman and the Board of Trustees of Saint Vincent Catholic Medical Centers
