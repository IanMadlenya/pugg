
        HAWKINS-Louis Golson ''Goldie''. Mr. Hawkins, noted pianist and restauranteur in Ocean Beach and New York City, died Thursday, March 16, 2000 in West Palm Beach, FL. Goldie retired to Palm Beach 25 years ago where he continued to entertain for 20 more years at various private functions. Known and loved by thousands, Goldie is survived by first cousins, Anne Hawkins MacMillan of Tampa, FL, formerly from West Palm Beach, Francis Golson of Gray, GA, Ruth Helms of Huntsvuille, AL, Carolyn Sallee of Sanford, FL, Marie Cheyne of Richmond, VA., and Jim McGrath who was his friend and caretaker.
