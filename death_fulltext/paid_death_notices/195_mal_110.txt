
        LIEBLING-Walter. On April 8, 2003, at the age of 90. Born in Vienna, Austria, graduate of the University of Vienna Medical School, he immigrated to the United States in 1938 and served during World War II with the US Army Medical Corps in Europe and the Philippines. After the war he set up a private medical practice in New York City and retired in 1986. He is survived by Margaret, his wife of 53 years, sister Alice, children Lawrence, Charles, and Anne, and grandchildren Emily, Peter, Christopher, and Erika. A Memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions may be sent to St. Lukes Roosevelt Hospital Center, Development Department, 55 West 57 Street, 5th Floor, NY, NY 10019 or the American Leprosy Foundation, 11600 Nebel St., Rockville, MD 20852, Ref: Lepers of Cebu, Philippines.
