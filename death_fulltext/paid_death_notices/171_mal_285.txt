
        LEVY-Isadore. Of Aventura, FL & Long Beach, NY. Passed away April 17. Beloved husband of Rosalind. Adored father of Richard (George Ann) and Bruce (Linda), and adoring grandfather of Emily, Kevin and Ben. Also survived by sister Blanche Tempkin and an extended family including many nieces, nephews and cousins. A loving, kind, charitable, generous and gentle man. Enormously devoted to his immediate and extended family and caring of his friends, he will be deeply missed by all who knew him. A Funeral Service at Gutterman's Funeral Home, Rockville Centre, NY on Sunday, April 22 at 10AM. Memorial contributions may be made to the Bessie Tatiner Children's Toy Fund at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
