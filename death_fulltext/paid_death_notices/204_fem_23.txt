
        BARZILAY--Helly. March 31, 1923-December 30, 2003. Beloved wife of Isaac Barzilay, beloved mother of Joshua and Sharona, beloved grandmother of Benjamin and Rebecca Graff and Ruth and Simon Barzilay, beloved sister of Erika Lorch, and beloved aunt of many. She devoted her life to doing good deeds. May her memory be for a blessing.
