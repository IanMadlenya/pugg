
        JACKSON--Irma Evelyn. February 26, 1911 - December 8, 2003. Treasured wife of Roberts Brock Jackson. Daughter of Pearl Radlow and Abraham Lowenstein of Boston; sister of the late Carolyn Lewis and Bernice Factor. Graduated from Teacher's College in 1929. Received a Master's degree in Directing and Acting from Yale Drama School, 1940. Toured the South Pacific with the USO as Manager and actress of six girl troupe. Survived by her amazing husband of 54 years. Beloved by grandnephew, David Raymond Lewis and grandniece, Kimberly Lewis Pratt, and great-grandnephews, Douglas and Paul. With love from all of your friends at 161 West 75th.
