
        KAHN-Robert I. The Central Conference of American Rabbis records with deep sorrow the death of Rabbi Robert I. Kahn, our cherished past President, who for 58 years served Congregation Emanu El of Houston as founding Rabbi and Rabbi Emeritus. He was renowned as a learned teacher, eloquent preacher, innovative educator, Jewish and civic leader. Our hearts go out to Rozelle, his life partner for 62 years and to his children, Alfred, Edward and Sharon. His memory will be a blessing to many. Rabbi Martin S. Weiner, Pres Rabbi Paul J. Menitoff, Executive Vice President
