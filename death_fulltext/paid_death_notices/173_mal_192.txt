
        DUKE-James (Jim). Of Diamond Grange, Portadown and Clonskeagh. June 15th, 2001. Retired Principal of St. Andrew's College. Sadly missed by his beloved wife Veronica, sister Karleen and a wide circle of friends. Funeral Service this Monday morning at 11 o'clock in Sandford Road Church Of Ireland followed by cremation immediately afterwards at Mountjerome Crematorium, Dublin, Ireland.
