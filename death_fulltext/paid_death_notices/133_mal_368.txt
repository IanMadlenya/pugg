
        HIRSCHEL-Louis. Died February 25, 1998 in Sharon, CT, at the age of 83 years young. His brother Arthur Hirschel of Florida survives him. Additionally, his daughters Yvette and her husband David Smith of River Vale, N.J., Myrnaand Nat Yagoda of Fort Lee, N.J., and his son Marc and his wife Paula Hirschel of Sharon, CT. Lou also leaves behind his grandchildren Craig, Todd, Danielle and Seth, and his very best friend Mona King Myerson of Woodlands, Florida. After immigrating from Poland in 1929, Lou helped established Batterman and Hirschel Caterers of Rego Park, Queens and Yonkers, N.Y. He touched many people's lives with both his personality and his tremendous sense of humor and will be greatly missed by all. Graveside services will be held Friday, February 27, 1998 at Mount Hebron Cemetery in Flushing, New York at 10:30 A.M.
