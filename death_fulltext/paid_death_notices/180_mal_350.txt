
        DAVIDSON-Herbert S. Passed away on January 24 at age 79. Beloved husband of Jan and the late Grace, devoted father to Ken and Willa and Andrew Epstein, adored and loving grandfather of Michael, dearest brother of Robert and Esther Davidoff, and much loved brother-in-law to Richard and Lynn Hyman, Diane and the late Martin Hyman, caring son-in-law of Billie Hyman. A leading innovator in the electronic distribution industry and the founder and CEO of Milgray Electronics, Inc. Passionately devoted to his family and friends, a generous and caring man with a wonderful sense of humor. He will be so greatly missed by all those whose lives he touched. Services on Sunday, January 27, at 1 PM. Gutterman's Parkside, Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, 718-284-1500.
