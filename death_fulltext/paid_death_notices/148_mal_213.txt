
        ARONS-George. Died Thursday, May 20 in Stamford, CT. He was 67 years old. Funeral services will be held Sunday, May 23 at 12:30 P.M. at the Congregation Agudath Sholom, 301 Strawberry Hill Avenue, Stamford, CT. Burial will follow at the Agudath Sholom Cemetery in Stamford. He is survived by his wife, three sons, and seven grandchildren. Donations can be made to the Congregation Agudath Sholom Hebrew School or the Bennett Cancer Center.
