
        WELLINGTON-Thomas Dutton. 78. Of Princeton, NJ. Died July 19, 2000. Survived by his wife Margaret, daughters Margaret Constantine, Irene Wellington and Sarah Wellington, son Peter Wellington and five grandchildren. Also stepchildren John, Sarah, Thomas and Fairlie Myers. A service will be held on Tuesday, July 25, Princeton University Chapel at 4PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Habitat for Humanity or Doctors Without Borders.
