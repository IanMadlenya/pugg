
        ORDAN-Bert. Bnai Zion Foundation notes with sorrow the untimely passing of our Vice President and former Board Chairman Bert Ordan. A devoted Zionist, he and his beloved wife Elaine are long time leaders of our organization. He shall be sorely missed. Our heartfelt sympathies to his widow, children and grandchildren. Hon. Alan G.
