
        ZOSS-Betty Hurwich. A resident of Falmouth, died February 2, at the Royal Megansett Nursing and Retirement Home in North Falmouth, MA. She was 83. Mrs. Zoss was born in South Bend, IN, and was a longtime resident of Martha's Vineyard, MA, and Berkeley Heights, NJ. A graduate of the University of Chicago, she was an accomplished photographer and writer. Mrs. Zoss is survived by her children, Roger M. Zoss of Ferndale, CA; Joel R. Zoss of Conway, MA; and Hope R. Zoss of Philadelphia, PA; and three grandchildren. Memorial arrangements are pending. Donations may be sent to the Dora Hershenow Music Fund, Temple Beth-El, 305 West Madison St., South Bend, IN.
