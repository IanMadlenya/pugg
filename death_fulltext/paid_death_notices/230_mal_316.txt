
        WOLF--Raymond Joseph. 85, died of bone cancer on March 22nd, 2006 at his home in Palm Beach, FL. He was the husband of Sylvia (Adams) Wolf. They were married for 60 years. Mr. Wolf was born on February 21, 1921, in Brooklyn, NY. He resided in Old Westbury, NY, New York City and Palm Beach. Mr. Wolf was the Founder of Rapid Park Industries, a leading New York City parking company. He was a WWII U.S. Army veteran and was among the troops in Okinawa. He was an avid golfer and life member of the Old Westbury Golf and Country Club. Mr. Wolf was also a member of The Breakers Club and served on the Board of Governors for the Palm Beach Opera House. He was a generous supporter of the UJA Foundation, HomeSafe of Palm Beach and Palm Beach AIDS Foundation. In addition to his loving wife, survivors include his four children and their spouses, Jeffrey and Jean Wolf of Steamboat Springs, CO, Brandon and Joelle Wolfe of Santa Barbara, CA, Kevin and Pam Wolf of New York City, NY and Pamela Wolf and Paul Boritzer of New York City, NY, ten grandchildren and a sister, Marion Rossin of Boca Raton, FL. The funeral and burial were held on Friday, March 24, at the Menorah Gardens and Funeral Chapel in West Palm Beach. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach.
