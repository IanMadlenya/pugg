
        BROOKS -- Donald. The Trustees, faculty and staff of The New School and the Board of Governors of Parsons The New School for Design express their sadness at the passing of Donald Brooks, distinguished fashion designer, Parsons alumnus, Honorary Board member, Honorary Degree recipient, and mentor to students. As a Designer Critic in the Fashion Design program for 40 years, Mr. Brooks played a direct and meaningful role in shaping the education and professional direction for scores of students. He was awarded the Parsons Medal for Distinguished Achievement in 1974 and an Honorary Doctorate in Fine Arts in 1985, and, in 2003, his work was celebrated in an exhibition. Though saddened by his passing, individuals throughout the Parsons community will continue to feel the influence of his work and be inspired by his accomplshments and his lifelong commitment to excellence in design. Philip Scaturro, Chair, Board of Trustees The New School Bob Kerrey, President, The New School George Walker, Chair, Board of Governors Parsons The New School for Design Paul Goldberger, Dean, Parsons The New School for Design
