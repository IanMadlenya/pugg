
        LUBLIN - Emily Langley. Of Brookline, MA, age 84, on April 28. Beloved mother of Langley Danowitz and Alice Morris, former wife of Gerson Lublin, all of NYC, and grandmother of Allison Deaver of Whitefish, MT. Emmy, always a free spirit, served as fashion editor, community volunteer and medical abstractor. She walked miles daily in devotion to Friends of the Muddy River, Victorian Society and Society of Architectural Historians. Born in Milwaukee, raised in Minneapolis, yet treasured every word in the NY Times and the New Yorker. Gathering planned May 13, at J. S. Waterman & Sons, Boston. Thank you, Mom, from your ''people''.
