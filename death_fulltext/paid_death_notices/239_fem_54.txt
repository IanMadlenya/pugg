
        ELLENBOGEN--May (nee Kaplan), 89, on December 6, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Abraham, devoted mother of Irene and Ira, Sima and Zane, Joe and Ellen, doting grandmother of Nathan, Hallie and Kobe. Poet, story teller, weaver of images, true friend who lived life to the fullest. Shining star who will be missed by all lucky enough to have been touched by her on her journey through life. She will forever be remembered.
