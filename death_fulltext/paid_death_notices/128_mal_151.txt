
        BERNTSEN-Carl A., of 355 Grace Church Street, Rye, N.Y. Died after a short illness, September 9, 1997. Survived by his widow the former Anne Rice, two sons Carl, III and Thomas S., a daughter Elise B. Stanbury and 4 grandchildren. Predeceased by his son Douglas E., Professor Emeritus in Medicine, Cornell Medical Center, New York Hospital and Physician Emeritus in Medicine at Hospital for Special Surgery. Service 4PM Saturday, September 13th, Christ Church, Rye, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions to United Hospital Hospice, 406 Boston Post Road, Port Chester, NY 10573.
