
        WENDLINGER--Robert I. (April 11, 1922-April 27, 2007). Award-winning newspaper photographer, nominated for PulitzerPrize in photography, WWII veteran. Will be remembered lovingly by his companion Shirley Kurtzberg, his children Patti and Steve Rosen, Ronald and Elaine Wendlinger, his sister Marilyn W. Goldberg, his grandchildren Kara Rosen, Amanda Wendlinger, and Chelsea Wendlinger, and by his step-daughters and their families. Bob will be missed by family and friends and by Duke. Internment will be at Sharon Gardens. Donations may be made to the Lymphatic Research Foundation.
