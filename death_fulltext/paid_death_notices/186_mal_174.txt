
        VAGT-Henry Tyrrell Jr. On July 14, 2002, of Scarsdale and Eagle Bay, NY. Born in Brooklyn on April 18, 1927, son of the late Veronica Maxwell Vagt and Henry Tyrrell Vagt. Graduate of Brooklyn Preparatory School, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute B.E.E. and Polytechnic University M.E.E. Survived by his wife of fifty years, Miriam Smith Vagt, and their children, Kathleen, Janet Vagt Scully, Thomas, Kevin, Mary Lauren Vagt Smith, and grandsons, Hunter and Maxwell Vagt. Friends may call at the Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, NY, on Wednesday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be held at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church on Thursday, 9:45 AM. Interment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
