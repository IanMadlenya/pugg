
        SHIPLEY-Linwood Parks. Of Hightstown, New Jersey. Formerly of Summit, N.J. On January 26, 1999. Husband of the late Emily Shipley (nee Herzog). Father of L. Parks Shipley, Jr. of Saint Michaels, MD., Frederick H. Shipley of Ripley, W.VA., Walter V. Shipley of Summit, N.J. and Emily Shipley Davis of Ottowa, Canada. Also survived by ten grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. A funeral service will be held on Saturday, January 30, 1999 at 11AM from Central Presbyterian Church, Summit, N.J. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name may be made to Young Life, Up With People or The American Cancer Society.
