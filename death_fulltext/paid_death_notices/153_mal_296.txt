
        SOD-Louis M., M.D. Died October 16, 1999 at the age of 73 due to diabetes and Alzheimer's disease. He is survived by his wife, Cynthia; his son and daughter-in-law, Paul and Matty; his son, Eric; his daughter, Ruth; six grandchildren, Yanky, Yossi, Batsheva, Baila, Rafi and Zev; his siter and brother-in-law, Pauline & Gilbert Levin of Norfolk, VA. Dr. Sod was raised in Norfolk, VA. and resided in Bergen County, NJ. He was Chief of Radiology at various hospitals including Byrd S. Coller Memorial Hospital, St. Claire's Hospital in Denville, NJ., and a professor of radiology at New York Medical College.
