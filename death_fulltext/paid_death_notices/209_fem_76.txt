
        CALIENDO--Edith. On June 10, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Frank. Devoted mother of Judy Friedman and mother-in-law of Alfred Friedman. Cherished grandmother of Jason. Funeral services were held Saturday at the Chas. Peter Nagel Funeral Home with burial in St. Raymond's Cemetery. The family would appreciate donations in Edith's memory to the Alzheimer's Association of New York City, 360 Lexington Ave., NY, NY 10017. Edith was a kind and loving person who will be missed by all who knew her.
