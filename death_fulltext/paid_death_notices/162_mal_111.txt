
        ROCKWELL-John Marshall. Of Spring Lake Heights, NJ, on July 8, 2000, aged 87. Born in Chicago, Illinois on June 14, 1913. He was a creative entrepreneur, founding and heading Rockwell Products Corporation until its sale in 1968. An avid pianist, he often entertained family and friends with spontaneous performances. He is survived by his devoted wife, Irene, his beloved sons, Alan, Andy, Sam and David, his grandson Sam and brother Dick. He will be dearly missed by all who had the great pleasure to know him. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM on Friday, July 28 at The Church of Saint Rose, 603 Seventh Ave, Belmar, NJ. Donations in his memory may be made to Citymeals on Wheels, 355 Lexington Ave, Floor 3, New York, NY 10017.
