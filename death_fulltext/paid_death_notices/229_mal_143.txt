
        EASON--Charles Francis, Sr. On January 21, 2006 at age 93 in Bethesda, MD. Born April 7, 1912 in Brooklyn, NY to Charles Aloysius Eason and Katherine Marie Larkin. He was predeceased in 2005 by Eleanor Butler Eason, his wife of 54 years. He leaves 4 sons, Charles Francis Eason, Jr. of Washington, DC, David Butler Eason of Chevy Chase, MD, Paul Bauer Eason of Owings, MD and Peter Larkin Eason of Kensington, MD.
