
        McDONALD-John D. On December 23, 1998, age 92, of respiratory failure. Husband of artist Dorothy Eisner McDonald, who died in 1984; father of Joan McD. Miller and Christie McDonald; father-inlaw of Richard Miller and Michael Rosengarten; grandfather of Lucas Miller and Adam and Jacob Vance. He was author of books on the subjects of fly fishing, game theory, and business, and served as editor of ''My Years With General Motors'' by Alfred P. Sloan, Jr. He was a writer and editor at Fortune Magazine 1945 to 1971. A memorial service will be held in February.
