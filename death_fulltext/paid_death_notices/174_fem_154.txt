
        GRAHAM-Katharine. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board and Members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City note the death of our dedicated friend and generous supporter, Kay Graham. Her vision and civic endeavors were an inspiration to us all. In 1993, UCP was proud to bestow the Annual Humanitarian Award upon her. The many individuals at UCP/NYC who have benefited from her generosity join us in extending our heartfelt condolences to her family. Leo Hausman, Chairman Martin C. Hausman, President UCP/NYC
        GRAHAM-Katharine. The Board of Directors, Faculty and Staff of the New York University Child Study Center note with deep sympathy the passing of Katharine Graham, mother and mother-inlaw of our board member Stephen and Cathy Graham. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Stephen, Cathy and the entire Graham family. Harold S. Koplewicz, MD, Dir. Brooke Garber Neidich, Chairman
        GRAHAM-Katharine. The Musicians, Trustees and Staff of the Marlboro Music School and Festival extend our sympathy and affection to her family. Her generosity and devotion to Marlboro, its musicians and the Serkin family will long be remembered. Stephen Stamas, Chairman Board of Trustees
