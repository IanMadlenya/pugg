
        WAGNER-Edward Willett, 77. Of Lexington, MA. passed away on December 7 of complications from Alzheimer's disease. He was a professor of Korean Studies at Harvard University and founder of the Korea Institute. He is survived by his wife, Namhi Kim Wagner, a brother John Wagner, two sons from his first marriage, Robert Camner and J. Christopher Wagner and three stepdaughters, Yunghi Choi, Sokhi Choi Wagner and Sanghi Choi Wagner. A memorial service will be held in April, 2002 at Harvard University.
