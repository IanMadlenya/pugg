
        MACEWICZ - Robert, ''Airport Bob'' died December 21, 1996 at his home at the former Santa Rosa Air Center. He is survived by his son, Matthew and stepson, Sean. Brothers John of Arizona and Walter of New Jersey. He was born on 8/9/43. Bob was a member of the military and served in Korea. An avid reader and a lover of flight, he leaves many friends, all who will always remember his generous heart.
