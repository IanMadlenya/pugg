
        KENLER--Muriel died March 9, 2006, in New Jersey, of heart failure. She was born December 25, 1915, in New York. She was a graduate of Delphi University and Columbia University. She is survived by her companion of 37 years, Rafael Colon of New Jersey; her brother, Adrian Marcuse of Florida; her grandson, James Kenler; her nieces, Elizabeth Marcuse, Nancy Marshall, and Sally Crawford; and her daughterin-law, Susan Kenler. She was preceded in death by her son, John Kenler.
