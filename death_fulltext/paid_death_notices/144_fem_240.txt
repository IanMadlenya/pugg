
        TOWNSEND-Mary Elizabeth, 61. Of Hopewell Township, died Friday, January 15 at the Medical Center of Princeton. Born in Kingston, NY, she was a resident of Hopewell Township, NJ. A graduate of Manhattanville College, she entered the Society of the Sacred Heart of Jesus in 1960. During her career, she was Head of the Middle School at Stuart Country Day School, Princeton, and at Greenwich, CT School of the Sacred Heart. In 1989 she retired from the Society of the Sacred Heart and in 1990, married Charles C. Townsend Jr. She was a former trustee of the Stony Brook Millstone Watershed Association and a member of the Garden Club of Princeton. She was a member of Trinity Church of Princeton. Daughter of the late Reynolds B. & Margaret M. Carr, she is survived by her husband, Charles C. Townsend, Jr., two step-children, brother Dr. Arthur G. Carr II of Hurley, NY and five nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers, her family requests contributions to the Society of the Sacred Heart, NY Province for the benefit of Sprout Creek Farm, 4 Lauer, Poughkeepsie, NY 12603. Interment is private. A Memorial service will be held Saturday, January 30 at 2:30 pm at the Stuart Country Day School of the Sacred Heart, 1200 Stuart Road, Princeton, NJ 08540. For information, Cromwell Memorial Home, 71 East Prospect St., Hopewell, NJ
