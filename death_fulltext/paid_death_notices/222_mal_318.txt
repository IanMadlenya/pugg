
        BERGER--Mark H. 77, of New York, N.Y., suddenly on July 27, 2005. Loving husband of 51 years of Margaret, beloved father of Joshua (Leeza) and David (Tracey), and wonderful grandfather of Tashi and Noah. He was an alumnus of Polytechnic University (1947) and of Harvard Law School (1950). Services at Riverside Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. on Sunday, July 31, 11:30am. Donations in lieu of flowers to East 55 St. Conservative Synagogue, 308 E. 55 St., N.Y., N.Y. 10022.
        BERGER -- Mark H. The Brooklyn Law School community deeply mourns the loss of our friend, Mark H. Berger, the husband of our beloved colleague, Professor Margaret Berger. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Margaret, his children Joshua and David, their wives Leeza and Tracey, and his grandchildren Tashi and Noah. Stuart Subotnick, Chairman, Board of Trustees Joan G. Wexler, Dean
