
        O'BRIEN-Margaret M. Passed away after brief illness on March 12, 1997. Daughter of the late William C. and Margaret Tully O'Brien and sister of the late John P., she is survived by Jeanne P. O'Brien of Tampa and Msgr. William B. O'Brien of New York. A long-time Scarsdale resident, she pioneered early childhood education, after receiving a Master's in Education at Columbia University, at renowned Children's Corner Nursery School in Scarsdale. Viewing at Bennett Funeral Home in Scarsdale, Thursday 7-9 PM and Friday 2-4 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Resurrection on Saturday morning at 11 AM at Our Lady of Fatima Church in Scarsdale.
