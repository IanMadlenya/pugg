
        FRANK-Walter Nils, 91. Died Tuesday at Kendal at Hanover, NH. He was born April 22, 1908 in Brooklyn, NY, the son of Louis and Selma (Persson) Frank. He attended Brooklyn schools and entered New York University in 1924. He was married on April 22, 1931 to Ruth Carlson. Mr. Frank had been a member of the New York Stock Exchange since 1937 and served as chairman of the Board of Governors from 1965-1967. He was predeceased by his wife in 1998 and by his son, Walter Nils Frank, Jr., in 1995. He is survived by his daughter, Judith Frank Ketterer of Lyme, NH; his sister, Elsa Hintze of Hanover; six grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Calling services will be held from 2-4 and 7-9 PM Friday at Rand-Wilson Funeral Home at 11-1/2 School Street, Hanover. Funeral services will be held at 1 PM Saturday in the Rand-Wilson Funeral Home. Burial will follow at Pine Knoll Cemetery. Contributions in Mr. Frank's memory may be made to the Cadbury Fund at Kendal, 80 Lyme Road, Kendal at Hanover, Hanover, NH 03755 or to the Children's Hospital at Dartmouth (CHAD), c/o Dartmouth-Hitchcock Medical Center, One Medical Center Drive, Lebanon, NH 03756.
        FRANK-Walter N. The Board of Directors, members and employees of the New York Stock Exchange, Inc. mourn with deep sorrow the passing of a dear friend and esteemed colleague, Walter N. Frank, Chairman of the Exchange from 1965 to 1967 and a member of the Exchange since 1937. His vision, leadership, and dedication to the Exchange and the securities industry will be greatly missed. Richard A. Grasso Chairman and Chief Executive Officer New York Stock Exchange, Inc.
