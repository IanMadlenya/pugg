
        HATFIELD--Robert S. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and Weill Cornell Medical College mourn the passing of our dear friend and esteemed Hospital Trustee Robert S. Hatfield. A business leader and a man of great personal warmth, Mr. Hatfield served as President of the Board of Governors of The Society of The New York Hospital from 1981 to 1987. He retired in 1987, but remained a member of the Board of Trustees of the merged entity, NewYork - Presbyterian Hospital, until the time of his death. Mr. Hatfield's commitment to corporate citizenship was demonstrated by his long years of devoted service to enhancing health care and higher education. He leaves an outstanding legacy of leadership and dedication, and will be greatly missed. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. John J. Mack Chairman, Board of Trustees NY-Presbyterian Hospital Dr. Herbert Pardes President and CEO NY-Presbyterian Hospital Sanford I. Weill, Chairman Board of Overseers Weill Cornell Medical College Dr. Antonio M. Gotto, Jr. Dean Weill Cornell Medical College
