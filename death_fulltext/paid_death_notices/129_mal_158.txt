
        GREENE-Orville N., 88. Of Manhattan. Suddenly, October 12, 1997. Beloved husband of Maxine Greene. Devoted father of Timothy, Elizabeth and the late Jonathan. Father-in-law of JoAnn and Paula. Loving grandfather of Dan and Chris. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81 st. 11:30AM Wednesday, October 15. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Epilepsy Foundation of America, Landover, Md. or the Neurology Department, Mayo Foundation, Scottsdale, Ariz. in memory of Jonathan Greene.
