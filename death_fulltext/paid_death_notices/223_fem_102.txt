
        TISHMAN--Suzanne. Suzanne Weisberg Tishman passed away on August 8, 2005 after a prolonged illness. She is survived by her husband, John L. Tishman, and their children, Daniel R. Tishman and Katherine T. Blacklock, Daniel's wife Sheryl, as well as three beloved grandchildren, Joshua and Gabriel Tishman, and Kyle Blacklock. She is also survived by her loving sister, Dorothy Lyon. Services were held privately. Contributions in her memory to the Archaeology Department at The American Museum of Natural History, 81st St and Central Park West, N.Y., N.Y. 10024 would have been meaningful to her and greatly appreciated by her family.
