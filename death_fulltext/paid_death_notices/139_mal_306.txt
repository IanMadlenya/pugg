
        LUNN-Harry, Jr. The membership of the Association of International Photography Art Dealers, Inc mourns the loss of a founder and respected colleague. As a member of the Board, Harry's experience and guidance were significant to the growth of AIPAD. We will miss his wisdom, humor, and generosity of spirit. Our deepest condolences to Harry's family. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, October 6, 1998, 5:30PM at St. Bartholomew's Episcopal Church, 109 East 50th Street at Park Avenue. Robert Klein, President
