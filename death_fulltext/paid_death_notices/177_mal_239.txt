
        KORN-Frank. Died peacefully on October 15, 2001 at the age of 88 at his home, with his family by his side. Beloved husband of Chizuko & the late Marian. Devoted father of Evelyn Radin, Carol Korn-Bronson and Susie Korn and father-inlaw of Harley Radin and John Bronson. Adored grandfather of Jason Radin. Treasured brother of Eva Danton and brother-in-law of the late Eugene Danton. We will feel his loss and celebrate his generosity, devotion and love for life, knowledge and beauty forever. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, October 16, from 6 to 8PM, with services on Wednesday, October 17, at 9:30AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to The Frank Korn Scholarship Fund, C/O The San Francisco School, 300 Gaven Street, San Francisco, CA. 94134.
        KORN-Frank. The officers and employees of Hugo Neu Corporation and the John L. Neu family are deeply saddened by the passing of Frank Korn, a true gentleman, brilliant colleague and Renaissance man. Frank served the company and the Neu family as a senior executive and advisor for over half a century and was instrumental in establishing and maintaining the company's preeminent position as a supplier to the steel industry in the Far East. We will greatly miss his wise counsel, keen intellect, sense of humor, love of music and art, humanity and friendship. Our sincere condolences to all of the members of the Korn family, who mean so much to us. John L. Neu, CEO
