
        HUSIN--Bernice Spitalny, on her 96th birthday, November 23rd, 2006, peacefully at home. Devoted wife of the late Irvin Husin, loving mother of Carol and Margo and son-in-laws, David and Tony, adored by granddaughters Rachel and Abigail and greatgranddaughter Ilana. Service Sunday November 26th, 11 am at Temple Israel of Jamaica, Queens, New York.
