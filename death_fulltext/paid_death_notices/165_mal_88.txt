
        DUTKA-Leonard M. Of Pound Ridge, N.Y. and Carlsbad, Calif. Died on October 4. Retired President of Dynarex Corp and former President of IPCO Hospital Supply. Beloved husband of Charlotte. Devoted father of Elaine, Mark and Richard. Loving father-in-law of Irene. Doting grandfather of Annie B. and Zoe C. Brother of Seymour and Robert. Fighting against insurmountable odds in his battle with Lou Gehrig's Disease, he displayed the courage, selflessness and dignity that typified his entire life. A devout humanist, family man, proponent of social justice, our ''Rock'' and inspiration, he will be sorely missed. Contributions to the ALS Association 818-880-9007 or the Rick Dutka Memorial Fund c/o the Tides Foundation 415561-6400. Services on Sunday at 10:30AM at the Jewish Family Congregation, South Salem, N.Y. 914-763-3028. Interment immediately following at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, N.Y.
