
        ROSENBLUM--Beatrice. Stamford, CT, formerly of Middletown, NY. Beatrice Rosenblum, a columnist on antiques and collectibles for the Times Herald Record and author of several books on antiques, died Thursday, October 19, 2006, at Stamford Hospital. She was 93. She was a longtime resident of Middletown, NY. The beloved widow of Martin Rosenblum, mother of Constance and James Rosenblum, and grandmother of Sarah Oliva Geller, Nina Rosenblum and Victoria Rosenblum. Services will be private. Memorial contributions in Beatrice's name may be made to Friends of Hillhold, Orange County Parks Department, 211 Route 216, Montgomery, NY 12549.
