
        FRANK-Aaron S., at his home in Vero Beach, Florida on October 8, 1997. Formerly of Phila., PA, Loveladies Harbour, NJ and New Hope, PA. He served in the United States Army from 1941 through 1945. He was associated with the Na-Lor Mfg. Co. of Phila. and the Forum Sportwear Co. of NYC for most of his life, serving as CEO and President of both companies. He is survived by his wife, Susan Wardick Frank; his 3 daughters: Lisa Baladad of Deerfield, Il, Donna Riskin of Eddyville, OR, Marcy Patrick of Valdez, AK; his sister Phyllis Brooks of Vero Beach, FL, and his three grandchildren: Addi, Austin and Jennifer. The family suggests that donations be made to their favorite charitable organization.
