
        TOTTENHAM-Dinah L. Died Nov. 14, 1997 in London, England after a long illness. She is survived by her daughters Philippa Gerard and Amanda Outerbridge, and by five grandchildren: Ashley, Nina, Wylie, Robin and Ben. Until recently, she resided in New York City where she was the Executive Director of the Parkinson's Disease Foundation from 1978 to 1996. Donations may be made to the Dinah Tottenham Memorial Fund, C/O The Parkinson's Disease Foundation, Columbia University Medical Center, 710 West 168 Street, New York, New York 10032.
