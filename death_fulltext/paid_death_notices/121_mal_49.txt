
        KAUFMAN-Louis H. On February 2, at the age of 84. A lifelong New York City resident. Graduate of New York University School of Law and founding partner of the legal firm of Schneider, Kaufman & Sherman, P.C. He is survived by his beloved companion Liselotte (Judy) Langholtz, daughter Jaymee Brandt, grandchildren Adam and Keri Brandt, and sister Gussie. A memorial service will be held at 2 PM, on Wednesday, February 5, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the National Asphia Association, PO Box 1887, Murray Hill Station, New York, NY, 10156.
