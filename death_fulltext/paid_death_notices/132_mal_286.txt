
        ROMANOWSKI-Rev. Francis C. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Stanislaus Bishop & Martyr Church, 88-10 102nd Avenue, Ozone Park, on Jan. 20. Reposing in James & Stephen Romanelli Funeral Home, 89-01 Rockaway Blvd., Ozone Park, Thursday, Jan. 22, 2-10 P.M. and Friday, Jan. 23, 25 P.M. Mass of Transferral Friday, Jan. 23 at 7:30 P.M., St. Stanislaus Bishop & Martyr Church. Funeral Mass Saturday, Jan. 24, at 10 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
