
        FLESCH-Marjorie (nee Denzer). 87, of Rye, N.Y. Formerly of Scarsdale, on March 4, 2002. Loving wife of the late Richard C., beloved mother of Barbara Resnek (Frank) and Joan Flesch. Devoted grandmother of Lisa Resnek Wyett (Chris) and Paul Resnek, proud great grandmother of Henry and Jonah Wyett, adored (aunt Marjorie) to nieces and nephews. Member of Brown University (Pembroke) Class of '36. Generous, caring and spirited, she will remain in our hearts forever. Funeral Thursday, March 7, 1 PM at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, Maple Ave at South Broadway, White Plains, N.Y. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Brown University, Class of '36, UJA Federation or Brandeis University.
