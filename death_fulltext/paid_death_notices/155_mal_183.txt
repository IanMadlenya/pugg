
        HALE-John P., Esq. The Board of Trustees, the priests, religious and staff of The Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of New York record with heartfelt sympathy the death of our dear friend, John P. Hale, Esq. He served as special counsel to Catholic Charities for child care & as a valued member of the Archdiocesan Legal Advisory Committee. We treasure the memory of his strong faith, his love for the poor & needy, and all those served by Catholic Charities, his quiet graciousness, his wisdom and advice. We thank God for his life with us, and we express our sincere condolences to his family whom he loved so dearly. John Cardinal O'Connor, President Rev. Msgr. James J. Murray, Secy
        HALE-John P. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of John P. Hale, a Life Member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Timothy G. Reynolds, Pres. Henry F. White, Jr., Historian
