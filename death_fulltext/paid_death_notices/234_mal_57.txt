
        BERMAN--Bennett. Of Palm Beach, FL, formerly of New York City, died on July 2, 2006 at his home. He was 91 years of age. He was a successful entrepreneur and a generous philanthropist. He leaves his wife, Marilyn, children Jeffrey and Helaine, and grandchildren Justin and Jordan. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., on Friday, July 7, 2006, at 11:30 AM. Interment Montefiore Cemetery. $(6$)BERMAN--Bennett M. On behalf of the Board of Directors, staff, volunteers, friends and supporters of the Marilyn & Stanley M. Katz Seniors Campus, we mourn the passing of our esteemed benefactor, colleague and friend, Bennett M. Berman. As a past President of the Board of Directors, his vision and support of the Joseph L. Morse Geriatric Center has served generations of seniors throughout Palm Beach County for more than two decades. He will be sorely missed. Heartfelt condolences to his wife Marilyn Zelnick-Berman, a past President of the Morse Women's Auxiliary, and his entire family. E. Drew Gackenheimer, Executive Vice President Arthur S. Loring, President Marilyn & Stanley M. Katz Seniors Campus
