
        VARE-Beatrice. On October 12, 2001, at age 83. Beloved wife of the late Louis A. Rosenblum M.D. and Arthur Vare. Loving mother of Margaret Hammerschlag, M.D. and Elizabeth Rosenblum. Adoring grandmother of Zoe Hammerschlag. Devoted daughter of the late Charles H. and Rose Breitbart. Dear sister of Hannah Froelich and Marilyn Goodman. Services Monday, October 15th, 11:30 AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to The American Lung Association of New York, 432 Park Avenue South, New York, New York 10016.
        VARE - Beatrice. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Beatrice Vare. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
