
        WERKSTELL--Leslie J. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Leslie J. Werkstell, a treasured friend of the New York Jewish community who strengthened our ability to care for those in need through his dedication and support. We extend our deepest sympathies to Les' beloved wife, Lisa, his sons, Joshua and Justin, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
