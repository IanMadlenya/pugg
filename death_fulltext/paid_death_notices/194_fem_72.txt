
        SHAPIRO - Toni Mendez. Died March 9, 2003 at home at age 94, after a distinguished career as a dancer, choreographer, Rockette and literary agent extraordinaire. A woman who wore many hats. Survived by her niece Cynthia Weil Mann and her nephew Richard Weil. Visiting from Noon to 1 PM on Thursday, March 13 at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Avenue and 81st St. Interment private. A memorial service is planned for the Spring.
        SHAPIRO - Toni Mendez. Congregation Emanu-El of teh City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Toni Mendez Shapiro. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
