
        HITCHCOCK--Mari Watts, 97, prolific painter and muralist showed in London and America and had a retrospective of her work at the New Jersey State Museum. She left the earth from her home, on April 17, where she was surrounded by her three children. She was looking forward to her next adventure. Mother, grandmother, great grandmother and friend, Mari loved life with a passion and inspired people of all ages to live their dream. She was dearly loved by all who knew her. There will be a celebration of her life in October of 2007.
