
        NARDELLI--Vito. 94 years old, left us on February 22, 2007 to unite with his parents and his son Camillo Nardelli Esq. who predeceased him in 1987. Survived by his loving wife of 74 years, Palma, his children, the Honorable Eugene Nardelli, Laura Nardelli, his beloved daughter who took care of him for so many years and Vito Nardelli Esq. He was the proud grandfather of five and great-grandfather of three. He emigrated from Italy 68 years ago with a dream and little more. He opened his own green grocery working seven days a week. He lived a life of faith, strong family values, and total sacrifice. Intensely focused on the importance of education for his children, he dedicated his life completely to achieving this goal. He remains loved and respected by all. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street on Sunday from 2-5 and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian burial at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84 Street Monday at 10am. Interment Gates of Heaven cemetery.
