
        WAINER-Shirley, 86. Of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, passed away suddenly on September 6, 2001. Survived by her son, Paul Feinstein, & his wife Martha; daughter, Lorna Sarrel & her husband Philip; grandchildren Emily & Samantha Feinstein, Laura Robertson, Marc Sarrel and Jennifer Voelker; and great-grandchild, Evan Voelker. She survived three husbands, Bertram Feinstein, Louis Rudolph, and Harry Wainer. She was a truly exceptional and beloved woman who made important contributions to her community, particularly in her later years in Florida. She was an indefatigable fundraiser for charities, especially Jewish Federation. She served as campaign chair for the Woodlands Women's Division, was President and a director of the Women's Division of Greater Fort Lauderdale from 1994 to 1996 and was honored by them for her outstanding work. She was on the Board of Directors of Jewish Federation, Greater Fort Lauderdale and then the newly formed Broward County Board. She helped to establish Friends For Life in the Woodlands community. Funeral service on Sunday, September 9th, 12:45PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. 212-362-6600. Interment Mt. Lebanon Cemetery.
