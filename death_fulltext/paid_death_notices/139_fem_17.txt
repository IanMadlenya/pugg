
        GALMITZ-Ida, 101, died on August 1 peacefully surrounded by her loved ones. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Preceded in death by her husband, Aaron. Mother of Alice Meltzer and Arnold (Ellen). Grandmother of Ellen November (Leon), Curtis Meltzer (Rhonda), Paul and Ruth Galmitz. Greatgrandmother of Kara and Daniel November, and Jacob and Joshua Meltzer. She was a retired NYC schoolteacher. Graveside services 10 AM, August 3, at Star of David Memorial Chapel, 7701 Bailey Road, North Lauderdale, FL 33068. Contributions can be made in her name to Hamilton - Madison House, 50 Madison St., New York, NY 10038.
