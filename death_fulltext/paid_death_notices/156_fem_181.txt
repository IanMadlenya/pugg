
        HARTFIELD-Joan (nee Flesch). Died January 13 in Chicago at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Otto Hartfield, she is survived by her son Robert, a Chicago resident, four granddaughters, Claire, Lisa, Karen and Lynn Hartfield, and seven great - grandchildren. Daughter of the esteemed violinist, the late Carl Flesch, she was the sister of businessman and biographer Carl F. Flesch, who currently resides in London.
