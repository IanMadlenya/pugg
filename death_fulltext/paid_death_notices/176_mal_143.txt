
        STOLLER-Elias R., M.D. On September 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Beatrice and the late Ruth. Loving father of Robert and Philip. Dear brother of Sydney and the late Norman.         STOLLER-Dr. Elias R. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of Dr. Elias R. Stoller, a longtime ADL leader, supporter and friend. The professional medical services and advice he provided to ADL's staff and leadership were exceptional and greatly appreciated. We extend our condolences to his wife Beatrice, sons Robert and Philip, brother Sidney and the entire family. Glen A. Tobias National Chairman Abraham H.
