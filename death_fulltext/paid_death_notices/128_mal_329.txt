
        ECKSTAT-Charles, 89, on Sept. 22. A language teacher in the NYC school system for 30 years, active in the labor movement, a loyal union member. Survived by his beloved wife of 61 years, Jenny Eckstat, his daughter Elsa Meyers, his son-in-law Ronald R. Meyers. By his grandchildren Daniel Meyers and Lisa Reznik Meyers, Seth Meyers and Eloise Flood Meyers, and his great-granddaughter Miranda Colette Meyers. Also survived by his sisters Mae Anderman, Lillian White and Beatrice Bienstock.
