
        ATLAS-Anthony H., 68. Died December 11, 2001 at his home in Rego Park, NY. Beloved husband of Elena, father of Seth and Cliff, father-in-law of Clare and Laura. Loving grandfather of Emily, Elizabeth and Benjamin. Beloved brother of Jeffrey. Tony was a dedicated attorney, public servant, and civic leader, council to the law firm of Jackson Lewis Schnitzler & Krupman, and chairman of Community Board 6Q. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Alliance of Queens Artists. Graveside service, Wednesday 2 PM, Mount Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, New York.
