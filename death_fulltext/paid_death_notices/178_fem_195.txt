
        SHERMAN-Beulah. Born and lived her entire life of 82 years in Lawrence. Her father was a founder and past President of Shaary Tefila. As well, her active life included the synagogue and she was a long time participant as a volunteer in Peninsula General Hospital. Devoted wife of the late Martin. Adored mother of Michael. Cherished sister of Jenny Finkelstein. Passed away peacefully. She loved to travel, attend theater and always looked very glamorous & elegant as an original Lawrence lady. Graveside services 10:30 AM, Mt. Lebanon Cemetery.
        SHERMAN-Beulah. Mom, thank you for all your encouragement in letting me be who I am. I will miss all our good times together. You were my best friend. Love, Michael.
