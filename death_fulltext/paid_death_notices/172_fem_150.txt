
        BURDING-Margaret A. (Peg), 57. Died in Orlando, FL May 15, 2001, after a short illness. Originally from Bronxville, New York, she graduated from Colby College. She was a Sales Promotion Specialist spending most of her career in the New York metropolitan area with consumer product companies, Sales Promotion agencies and 16 years with T.J. Lipton. She joined Tupperware Inc. in Orlando in 1994, followed by Try-Foods, and subsequently ran her own sales promotion agency. She was predeceased by her husband, Frank O'Connor and is survived by her sister Barbara B. Kemp of New York City, a brother William D. Burding of Scottsdale, Arizona, four nieces and nephews, eight grandnieces and nephews and her fiance James P. McCaffrey of Orlando. A Memorial service will be held at the Dutch Reformed Church, Pondfield Road, Bronxville, New York at 4:00 P.M. on June 15, 2001. In lieu of flowers her family request that contributions made to Leukemia Research, C/O of the Society of Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1233 York Ave., N Y, New York 10021.
