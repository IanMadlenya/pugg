
        BURRIESCI-Anthony John. Ninety-three, died on April 1, 2002. Beloved husband of Bernice Bakke Burriesci. Loving father of Robert and his wife Karen, David, Donald and his wife Janice. Cherished grandfather of Robert, Lauren, Andrea and Kevin. He was an advertising executive, a community leader, & a good friend. The family will receive friends Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Westchester Funeral Home, Inc., Eastchester. A service will be held on Thursday April 4 at 12 noon in the Chapel at the Wartburg Home in Mount Vernon. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers friends who wish may contribute to Catholic Charities of NY, 1011 1st Ave, NY, NY, 10022-4134.
