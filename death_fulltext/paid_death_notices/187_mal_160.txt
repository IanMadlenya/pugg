
        SPENCER-Judson. The New York Design Center family mourns the loss of our friend and colleague, Judson Spencer. His courage, fortitude and ever-positive outlook on life and our industry served as a fine example. Contributions in his memory may be made to New York Presbyterian Hospital, the American Institute for Cancer Research or a charity of your choice. Memorial gathering at NYDC, 200 Lexington Ave, N.Y., September 18th, 1PM. James P. Druckman, Pres., Daniel M. Farr, Director New York Design Center, Inc.
