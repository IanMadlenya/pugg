
        SWEET - Emanuel (''Manny''). The Board of Governors and members of Hampshire Country Club are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed member Manny Sweet. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his dear wife, June Sweet, and to all his family. Hampshire Country Club Board of Governors Norman Zirkin, President
        SWEET-Emanuel (Manny). On August 14, 2000, at the age of 75. Beloved husband of June. Loving father of Evan Forster and David Thomas, and Barbara and Neil Forster. Adoring grandfather of Julia and Michael. Wonderful uncle of Beverly and Bruce Lee, Sharon Donnelly, Ronnie Siegel, Cindy and Martin Siegel, and Michael and Maritza Siegel. He was ''Sweet Manny'' to all who knew him. Service on Tuesday, 1:30 PM at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel,'' 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, New York.
