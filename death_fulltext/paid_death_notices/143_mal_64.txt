
        PRESTON-Robert B. Of New York City and Key Biscayne, Fla. Died on December 1, 1998, in New York City. Born on April 23, 1935, in Washington D.C. He graduated from Cornell University ('56) and The University of Virginia Law School ('59). He was an international corporate attorney for over 30 years. Mr. Preston is survived by his wife Margarita, son Robert Francis, daughter Vivian Marie, sister Barbara-Jean, and grandchildren William, Kelsey, Alyssa and Tyler. A memorial Mass will be held at St. Stephen of Hungary Church, 414 E. 82 St., NYC, on December 12th. 11am. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to a cancer related foundation.
