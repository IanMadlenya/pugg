
        MELE-Peter A., M.D. Of Tenafly, NJ, a prominent Englewood, NJ, surgeon died on Friday, March 6, 1998, of heart failure after a prolonged illness. He was 69. Husband of Paola Maranesi Mele. Carmel RC Church, Tenafly, NJ, on Wednesday at 11 AM. Visiting at Riewerts Memorial Home, 187 South Washington Ave, Bergenfield, NJ, on Monday and Tuesday, 2-4 and 6:30-9:30 PM.
