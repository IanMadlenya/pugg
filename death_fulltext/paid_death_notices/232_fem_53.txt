
        KNOBLER--Selma (Nee Frankel). Died May 9 at the age of 84 after a long struggle with Alzheimer's. A lifetime educator, she taught nursery school at Downtown Commu nity School and received a Master's Degree from the Bank Street College of Edu cation, where she later joined the faculty and taught early childhood education. She was adored by her many students and advisees. She is mourned by her son, Peter, daughter, Joanna (Posy), her brother, Edgar Frankel, daughter-in-law, Jane Dissin Knobler, son-in-law, Ira Ka waller, and grandchildren, Daniel Knobler and Ben and Zoe Kawaller. Donations should go to Bank Street Col lege of Education and/or Or ganizations actively opposing Bush Administration Policies. A memorial service will be announced.
