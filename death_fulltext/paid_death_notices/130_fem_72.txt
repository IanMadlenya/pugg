
        SAYERS-Lillian. Beloved wife of Theodore, loving mother of Barbara and Mark, cherished grandmother of Lauren & Nichole Braun, surviving sister May Green, of Los Angeles. Lillian Sayers was a pioneer in the Internet apparel industry and held over ten patents. She was the Chief Designer for Exquisite Form Industries, Bali Bras, and Lillyette. Mrs. Sayers worked for the Community AIDS Society and The 62nd Street Association. Lillian was a unique and vibrant woman and will be missed for her extraordinary inspiration, creativity, hospitality and generosity to her family and friends. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, Nov. 13 at 12:00 Temple Emanuel, 65th Street and Fifth Avenue in the Beth El Chapel. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Lung Cancer Research Fund at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, NYC.
        SAYERS - Lillian. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Lillian Sayers, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        SAYERS-Lillian. A cherished friend of many years will be missed deeply. Lilian, Milton, Randy and Barbara
