
        BALABAN - Sigmund. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Devoted father of Aileen Silver, Mark and Barry Balaban. Cherished grandfather of Alanna and Beth Silver, Scott and Brian Balaban. Loving brother of Frieda Ashare and Reuben Balaban. He was the founder of Sigmund Balaban and Co., CPA's, a retired army captain during World War II and an avid outdoorsman. He will be deeply missed by all those who knew and loved him. Services Monday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck.
        BALABAN-Sigmund. A gentleman, a scholar and a mensch in the truest sense. Sig was a dear friend and advisor to our family for over 40 years. We are very grateful for his good advice and counsel. His loss will be deeply felt. Very sincere condolences to his dear wife of 56 years, Sylvia, Mark and Shari, and the entire Balaban family. We all hope for happier days to come. The Schwalbe Family
