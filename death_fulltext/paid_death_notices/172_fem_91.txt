
        BARTOK-LeAnn. Noted pioneering conceptual visionary artist, painter, film maker, sculptor, poet, actress, passed away on Wednesday, May 9, at her art studio in NYC. Born August 1, 1937, in Martin's Ferry, OH. Graduated from Mercy Hospital School of Nursing, Pittsburh, PA. Actress in early 1960's Japanese films. Creator of ''SKYWORKS'' conceptual art projects in 1970's. Recipient of Senator William Proxmire's ''Golden Fleece'' Award for controversial NEA grant. Participant in numerous national and international art exhibitions. Devoted member of the Baha'i community. Survived by daughter Shari, sons Mark, Dennis, Jayce and granddaughter Claire.
