
        BARNETT-Minna. Died June 17, 2001. She was a former New York City school teacher and served in the Office of Education Liaison during the Lindsay administration. She is survived by Molly and Irwin Feigin; Elaine Rubin; Malcolm, Judy and Jen Barnett and Radha and Izare Mohammed. Contributions to Hunter College, AJC or Women's City Club.
