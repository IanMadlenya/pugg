
        OLIVER-Ward L., M.D. 100. Of Cobleskill, New York, died on Friday, August 16, 2002. Dr. Oliver practiced medicine for more than 60 years, 43 of which were spent in practice with his wife, Dr. Virginia Loucks Oliver. He is survived by his wife, five children, and ten grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the Cobleskill United Methodist Church on August 20th, 2002, at 11 A.M.
