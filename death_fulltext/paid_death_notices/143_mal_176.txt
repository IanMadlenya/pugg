
        DEGENSTEIN-Lester E. Died at home on Saturday, December 12, at the age of 95. He is survived by his loving wife Lila, his son Lee, and daughter-in-law Dr. Joan Kent. He was the devoted grandfather of David Robert Degenstein and Michael Kent Degenstein. He was the brother of the late Charles B. Degenstein and Anita Degenstein. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 81st and Madison, on Tuesday, December 15, at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the American Cancer Society or Lenox Hill Hospital.
        DEGENSTEIN-Lester E. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Lester E. Degenstein our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
