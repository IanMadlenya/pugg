
        KAPLAN-Nettie. It is with profound sorrow that the Village Temple of NYC marks the passing of its longtime member and past president Mrs. Nettie W. Kaplan. Her legacy will live with us forever. Funeral services will be held Thursday, July 8, 1999 at 10:45 A.M. at the Village Temple, 33 East 12 Street, New York, New York. Village Temple Board of Trustees E. Krugman, M. Rindler, Co-Presidents
        KAPLAN-Nettie. The Sisterhood of the Village Temple sadly bids goodbye to our friend of over forty years, Mrs. Nettie W. Kaplan. Her energy and kindness will be missed.
