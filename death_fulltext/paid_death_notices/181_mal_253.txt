
        MADOS-John. On February 19, 2002. Beloved husband of Suzanne Mados, loving brother of Harriet Nichols of Boca Raton, Florida. Family will receive friends on Friday, from 5-7:30 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Funeral services private.
        MADOS-John. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Mount Sinai School of Medicine and The Mount Sinai Hospital mourn the passing of John Mados, a generous benefactor. Mr. Mados, together with his wife, Suzanne, were outstanding supporters of research and medical education, and their commitment to improve the quality of patient care will long be remembered. We extend our sincere sympathy to Suzanne Mados, and the entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman of the Board The Mount Sinai Medical Center Nathan Kase, Interim President and CEO The Mount Sinai Medical Center and Interim Dean, Mount Sinai School of Medicine Barry R.
