
        LAVAY--Victor Herbert, CEO and co-founder of Vending Times, died August 11th, 2003 after a brief illness. He was 78. Mr. Lavay served in the U.S. Navy during WWII. After attending college under the GI Bill, he pursued a career in advertising, leading him to the trade journalism field. In 1962, he became part owner of a corporation established to publish Vending Times which first appeared in that year. In the early 1990s, he acquired full interest. Mr. Lavay is survived by his wife of 59 years, Fay, and five daughters. Services today, 2 PM, at Gutterman's Chapel, Rockville Centre, NY.
