
        BASS-Dorothy. On March 28, 2002 in her 100th year. Beloved wife of the late Nat Bass. Dear mother of the late Donald Bass. Cherished grandmother and great grandmother. Services Sunday, March 31st 9:30 AM at Congregation Emanu-El, New York City.
        BASS-Dorothy. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Dorothy Bass, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
