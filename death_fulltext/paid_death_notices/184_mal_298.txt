
        REEVES-Jeneil. The Board and Staff of the Greater New York Councils, Boy Scouts of America, extend their deepest sympathy to Ed Reeves and his family on the loss of his mother, Jeneil Reeves. Mrs. Reeves and her family have been longtime benefactors of the Boy Scouts. The John E. Reeves Cub World at Camp Alpine, named in honor of Jeneil's husband, serves as a living legacy of the family's commitment to the children of New York City. John F. McGillicuddy, Council President Daniel R. Gasparo, Scout Exec.
