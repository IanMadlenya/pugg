
        ARMSTRONG-Mary W. At age 87, died in Topsham, ME, on July 23, 2001. After graduating from Mt. Holyoke, Mary served 20 years in the Navy. Lt. Commander (Ret.) Armstrong spent the next two decades in the State Department. Mary and the stories about her travels will be sorely missed by her sister, Lucy Bailey, and her nieces, Kathy Armstrong, Susan and Elizabeth Bailey, as well as nephew, John Bailey.
