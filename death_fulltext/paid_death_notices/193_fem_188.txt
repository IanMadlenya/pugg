
        YERMAN-Sybil Sherri Berg. 84, on February 21, 2003 in New York City. Died at home surrounded by her family. Cherished wife of Elliott, devoted mother of Leslie, Douglas and Marcia. Adored and most special grandmother of Philip Samuel Dubow. She will be remembered for her wit and her devotion to her family. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Monday, February 24, at 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to the Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Ave., Montgomery, AL 36104.
        YERMAN-Sybil. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member, Sybil Yerman. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Samuel Wasserman, President
