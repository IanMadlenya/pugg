
        CHALFIN--Samuel, founder and former president of Invincible IPF furniture in Paterson, New Jersey died December 28th in Boca Raton, Florida. He was 93 years old. He is survived by his wife Rhoda of 62 years in Boca Raton, FL, his children Vicky Monaloy, Ruth Chasolen, Larry Chalfin, and his six grandchildren. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, December 30th, 1:30 PM at Robert Schoem Memorial Chapel, 150 Rt 4 East Paramus, NJ. In lieu of flowers, the family has requested a donation to be made to the American Cancer Society.
