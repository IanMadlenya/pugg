
        KINGSBURY -- Oliver. Died August 1, 2005 at age 103 in Williamstown, MA. His wife, Margaret Smith Kingsbury, predeceased him in 1994. He is survived by his sons James Kingsbury of Greenport, N.Y., Robert Kingsbury of Williamstown, MA., daughter Tirzah Kingsbury of Danbury, CT., seven grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. A private graveside service will be held at the Hector Union Cemetery, Burdett, N.Y. on Friday, August 5, 4PM. Contribution to Hitchcock Presbyterian Church, Scarsdale, N.Y. or The Church at Point O' Woods, Point O' Woods, N.Y.
