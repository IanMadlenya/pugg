
        MARYLES-Avi. The Board of Directors of the Zamir Choral Foundation expresses its heartfelt sorrow upon the untimely passing of Avi Maryles, beloved brother of its members Matthew J. Expressions of condolence to his beloved wife Daisy, their cherished daughter Miriam, dear brothers Norman and Josh Maryles, and the entire family, which mourns so profoundly the loss of this unique man. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Matthew Lazar Founder and Director Marsha Bryan Edelman President
        MARYLES-Avi.
