
        HANLES-Evelyn Jorrisch. One of a kind daughter, sister, wife, aunt, grandmother, cousin and friend. Best friend and devoted wife of the late Lester Hanles. Loving and much loved aunt of Barbara and Judith Jorrisch and families. Adoring grandmother of H. Scott and Eva Harrison and delirious greatgrandmother. She also leaves devoted cousin Betty Perlis, and family and friends who loved and laughed together through good times and bad. Memorial service in New York at a later date.
