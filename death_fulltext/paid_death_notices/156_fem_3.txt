
        McCRORY-Sylvia (nee Hogben). of Pound Ridge, NY; born London, England. She was the daughter of Lancelot Hogben and Enid Charles. She will be remembered as a loving Grandmother, Mother and Wife. Breeder of Liafail Irish Setters. She volunteered at Pound Ridge, Hiram Halle Memorial Library. She is survived by her husband Wallace W. McCrory, M.D., Chief of Pediatrics, NY Hospital, Cornell Medical Center (Emeritus); her children Pamela Sylvia McCrory of Lawrenceville, NJ, Michael Wallace Lancelot McCrory of Tucson, AZ and Christen Claire Gilley of Streetsboro, OH; her two brothers, Adrian Hogben and Dr. David Hogben; and cherished grandchildren James, David, Wendy, Holly and Andrew. Calling hours 2-4PM Monday, Jan. 3rd at the Cassidy-Flynn Funeral Home, Mt. Kisco, NY. Friends may send memorial donations to the American Friends Service Committee. Please omit flowers.
