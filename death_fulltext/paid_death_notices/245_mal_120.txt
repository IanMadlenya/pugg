
        DRUCKER--Milton (Mike), 85, Ed.D., died peacefully June 9, 2007. Founder and former Dean of the Marine Education Program at Kingsborough Community College. Previously served as Deputy to the Chancellor for Legislative Affairs of CUNY and executive director of the office of Queens District Attorney. Played a significant role in Queens politics throughout the 1960s and 1970s. His devoted family deeply mourns the passing of this remarkable man, possessed of extraordinary talents, infectious energy, and piercing wit. Services June 11, 12:00 noon, at Guttermans Funeral Home, 175 N. Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY.
