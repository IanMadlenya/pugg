
        GROSE-John Lewis. Of Locust Valley, NY, on October 25, 2001 at age 88. Loving husband of the late Barbara Houston. He is survived by a son, Peter Lewis, a daughter, Judith Grose-Johnson, by nine grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. A second son, John Francis, passed away in October 1999. Service will be held at 11 AM on Saturday, November 24th, at All Saints Episcopal Church in Great Neck.
