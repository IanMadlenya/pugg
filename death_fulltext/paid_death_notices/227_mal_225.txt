
        COSGRIFF -- Stuart, M.D. NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and Columbia University Medical Center mourn the passing of our respected friend and colleague, Dr. Stuart W. Cosgriff, Consultant Emeritus at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and Clinical Professor Emeritus in Medicine at Columbia University's College of Physicians and Surgeons. Dr. Cosgriff, 88, was renowned for his role in clinical anticoagulation, a therapy used to treat vascular disorders. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Dr. Cosgriff's five children, 10 grandchildren, and six greatgrandchildren. Dr. Herbert Pardes President and CEO NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital Dr. Gerald D. Fischbach Executive Vice President and Dean Columbia University Medical Center
