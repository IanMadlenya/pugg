
        KEELAN--Brian. In Cornwall, England while on holiday, Brian Keelan, beloved husband of Araceli, father of Geordie & Thomas, and brother of Rory & Aidan, died suddenly in the early hours of Saturday morning the 6th August 2005 from complications arising from leukaemia. An inspired man whose many friends around the world will miss him greatly. A funeral service for family and friends will be held at the Church of the Immaculate Conception, Farm Street, London W1 on Tuesday 16th August at 10:30 A.M. and a memorial service will be held at a later date. No flowers please but donations will be gratefully received for The Gambian Schooling Fund and sent to J H Kenyon Funeral Directors, 74 Rochester Row, London SW1P 1JU, Tel. +44 (0)20 7834 4624.
