
        McINERNEY - Denis, died peacefully at home on January 31, 2006. Beloved husband of Irene Murphy McInerney. Devoted father of Kathleen O'Hare, Denis J. McInerney and Maura Romano. He is also survived by eight grandchildren. The family will receive friends at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York on Thursday from 7-9 p.m., and Friday from 2-4 and 7-9 p.m. Mass of Christian Burial will be on Saturday at 10 a.m. at the Church of the Resurrection, 910 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York. His interment will follow at the Greenwood Union Cemetery, Rye, New York. In lieu of flowers, the McInerney family requests that any donations be made to the McInerney Scholarship for Public Service, Fordham Law School, 140 West 62nd Street, New York, New York 10023.
        $(6$)McINERNEY--Denis. Fordham University School of Law records with profound sorrow the passing of Denis McInerney '51, an outstanding lawyer and advocate, a distinguished and devoted graduate, a talented adjunct faculty member, a past president of the Fordham Law Alumni Association, and of the School's Board of Visitors. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. William M. Treanor, Dean
