
        DUFFY-Laurence E. Of Red Hook, NY; formerly of Manhattan. Died on March 15, 2000. Beloved companion of Valerie Roberts; devoted father of Krishni Kiffa and adoring grandfather of Sydnie Hyams. Also survived by a brother, Maurice (Mike) Duffy; a sister, Sheila Welsh; Corey and Meghan Roberts; several nieces and nephews and a son in-law, Tor Hyams. His adventurous and inquisitive spirit will be remembered and cherished by his loving family and loyal friends. A memorial service will be held at a later date in Cold Spring, NY.
