
        GOLDMAN--Helen. Born November 2, 1913, dies December 10, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Hans, devoted mother to Hank (Joan), proud grandmother of David and Jennifer (Jeff Wetzler) and adoring great-grandmother of Jacob. Her unique history took her from Czechoslovakia to Vienna to Sosua, Dominican Republic and the Bronx and Riverdale. She will be remembered by those who love her forever.
