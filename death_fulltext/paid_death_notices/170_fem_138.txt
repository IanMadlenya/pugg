
        TULCHIN-Yetta. On March 15, 2001. Graduate of the Jewish Theological Seminary, former Hadassah chapter president and esteemed teacher. Beloved wife of the late Louis. Mourned by daughters Bayla and Judith and her husband Richard. Adoring grandmother and great grandmother. Services will be held at Gutterman's Funeral Home in Woodbury, NY, 2 PM, Sunday, March 18. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society, the Alzheimer's Association and Haddassah.
