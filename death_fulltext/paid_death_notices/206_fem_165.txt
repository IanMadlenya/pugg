
        STRAUSS--Florence. Age 85. Formerly of South Fallsburg, N.Y. Passed on to a more peaceful place March 14, 2004 in Delray Beach, FL. She is survived by her companion Aaron Denenburg, sons Dr. Russell (Myrna) and Dr. Teddy (Maddy), five grandchildren, six great grandchildren. Her passion for life and ability to overcome all obstacles leaves a clear path for all of us to follow. GG-Flo, you will never be forgotten.
