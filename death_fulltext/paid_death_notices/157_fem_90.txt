
        KOENIG-Stella A. On February 7, 2000. Age 99. Adored and adoring wife of Mortimer H., mother of the late Dr. M. Glenn Koenig. Beloved grandmother of Mark A. and Scott R. Koenig. Also survived by Constance R. Koenig McIntyre, the remarkable widow of Glenn, and her husband Tom McIntyre; greatgrandchildren Alexandra M. and Samuel M.; Mark's wife Debra; and many nieces, nephews and friends galore. Stella, dear Stella rest in peace. Service Thursday, February 10 at 12 noon at The Riverside, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory to M. Glenn Koenig Fund at Vanderbilt University, Nashville, Tenn.
