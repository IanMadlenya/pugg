
        SHARPE-Dr. Lawrence. Died from cancer Thursday, October 19, 2000 at the McKeene Pavillion, Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. Dr. Sharpe was born in London on December 25, 1930. He completed his medical training at University College Hospital, London, 1961. His first academic position in the United States was as an Instructor in Psychiatry at Johns Hopkins Hospital in Baltimore, Maryland. He was an Assistant Professor of Psychiatry at State University New York & became an Associate Professor of Clinical Psychiatry at the University College of Physicians and Surgeons in 1967. Dr. Sharpe joined the Biometrics Research Department of the New York State Institute in 1967. As a Research Psychiatrist he worked on the NIMH US/UK Diagnosis of Mental Disorders Project for many years. He joined the Department of Research Assessment and Training in 1980 and was involved in all of their projects until the last week of his life. He is survived by Robert Roy Nicholson, friend; his brother Robert Arden and wife Joy; his niece Suzy; his nephew Matthew and his wife Alex and their daughter, his great niece, Talullah. A memorial service will be held to celebrate his life on November 4 at 2:00 PM at the Church of Incarnation, 209 Madison Avenue, New York, New York 10016 (at 35th Street). In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy may be sent to the American Cancer Society.
