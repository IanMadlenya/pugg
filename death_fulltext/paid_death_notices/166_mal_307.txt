
        CLARK-Francis P. 81, of Harwich, MA, and formerly of Briarcliff Manor, NY, in Plymouth, MA, November 24. Husband of Lenore (Riemann) Clark of Harwich; father of Peter F. Clark of Wilmington, DE, Christine Morrison of Charlottesville, VA, and Ann Clark Tucker of Pittsfield, MA; brother of William Clark of Candlewood Lake, CT, and Helen Thiemann of Westport, CT. He is also survived by four grandchildren. He was V.P. of Robert Reis & Co. in NYC, where he worked for 40 years. Memorial donations may be made to Jordan Hospital Capitol Campaign Fund, 275 Sandwich Rd., Plymouth, MA 02360. For additional information and family guest book go to www.legacy.com.
