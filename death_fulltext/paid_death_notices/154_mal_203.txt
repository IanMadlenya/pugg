
        PODESTA-Frederick J. On November 15, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Lillian (nee Daly). Fred Podesta has been associated with Madison Square Garden for more than 25 years in executive capacities as Vice President, Publicity, Advertising & Promotion Director. He was also General Manager of the New York Knickerbockers and on the Board of Governors of the National Basketball Association. Reposing at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 West 72 Street, NYC, Wednesday 7-9 PM, Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass, Blessed Sacrament R.C. Church, Friday 10 AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the John V. Mara Cancer Center at St. Vincent's Hospital.
