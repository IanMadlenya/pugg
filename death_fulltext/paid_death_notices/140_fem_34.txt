
        HARNICK-Rhoda. Died September 5, 1998, Pompano Beach, FL. Formerly of Flushing, NY. Beloved wife for 57 years of Leon. Mother of Arlene (Michael) Goodman, Dr. Paul (Katherine) Harnick and Janet (Robert) Rosenthal. Beloved grandmother of Elyse, Michael, Corey, Marc, Daniel, Alex, Paige, Remy and Chica. Former teacher and guidance counselor at Jamaica High School, most recently serving as President of the City College Alumni Association, Florida Chapter. She will be missed by all those who knew her. Donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
