
        RESNICK-Louis, 89. Of Ellenville, New York, died March 26 in Miami, FL. Louis founded Channel Master Corporation with his brothers Harry and Joe. The company started with the first pre-assembled antennas in the country, which grew into a giant industrial complex in his hometown of Ellenville, employing over 1000 people. Lou's benevolence knew no boundaries, endowing his resources on a myriad of humanitarian causes in the area. He lived a life of personal fulfillment. He is survived by his wife Mildred, his daughters, Myrna Jargowsky, and Sherry Ronan, and Michele Cohen. His brother Harry and his sister, Paula Rosenblum his three sons-in-law, and his 11 precious grandchildren and two great-grandchildren, as well as nieces, nephews, and countless friends. He was predeceased by his brother Former Congressman Joseph Y. Resnick and his sister Arlene Rosenblatt. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, March 31, 2002 at 1 PM in Cong. Ezrath Israel Synagogue, Ellenville, NY and burial will be at the Cong. Cemetery in Wawarsing, NY. Donations can be made to the Ellenville Regional Hosp., Route 209, Ellenville, NY 12428, or Cong. Ezrath Israel Syn. Rabbi Eisner Square, Ellenville, NY 12428.
