
        PAPPAS -- Douglas Reed. Passed away on May 20th, 2004 while hiking in Big Bend National Park in Texas. He was 42 years old. Mr. Pappas was a lawyer with Mintz and Gold LLC in Manhattan. He was also a member of the Society for American Baseball Research. Mr. Pappas is survived by his mother Carolyn Reed Pappas and his stepmother Karen Pappas. Visitation at Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, NY 10583 on Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial Monday June 7th, 2004 at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, 10:45AM. Interment Holy Mount Cemetery.
