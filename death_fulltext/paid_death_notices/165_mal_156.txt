
        STEELE-Ken. The faculty and staff of the New York State Psychiatric Institute mourn the loss of Ken Steele, a devoted friend and advocate of the mentally ill. As a member of our Institutional Review Board and as a guest lecturer, he vigorously represented the patient's perspective to us. Students from all disciplines benefited greatly from his fund of knowledge. We will miss him. John Oldham, M.D. NY State Psychiatric Institute
        STEELE-Ken. The Mental Health Association of New York City deeply mourns the passing of this tireless leader and advocate for people living with mental illness. Ken's accomplishments will be long remembered and his friendship greatly missed by the many individuals whose lives he touched. He was an inspiration to us all. Giselle Stolper, Executive Director
        STEELE-Ken. Friends and colleagues from the Mental Health Association in New York State (MHANYS) mourn the loss and celebrate the life and work of our board member, Ken Steele. His tireless work has made the world a better place for people living with mental illness.
