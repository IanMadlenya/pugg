
        SHERMAN-Victor. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Victor Sherman, a dedicated leader and valued member of the Executive Committee of our Plumbing and Heating Division. We extend our sincerest condolences to his beloved wife, Glenda; his daughters Beth and Nancy; his two grandchildren; and the entire family. James S.
