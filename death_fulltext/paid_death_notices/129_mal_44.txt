
        FREIMAN-Robert, died September 3. Born in New York. Artist who studied at The Art Students' League, The National Academy of Design, Parsons School of Art, Pratt Institute, Ecole des Beaux Arts, Fontain Bleau, where he was awarded first prize by The French Republic. He was a member of The American Water Color Society, and Who's Who in American Art. Bob will be missed by many. Dr. and Mrs. Hugh Barber Molly and Bill Reed
