
        SENNISH-Robert. 78, Professor Emeritus of Humanities, SUNY/Maritime College, died at his home in Piermont, NY, June 5, 2002. He is survived by his wife Peggy Fox, his daughters Bronwen and Maggie Sennish, a son Alexandre DeWitt, a brother George Sennish, and a grandson Nicholas Albert. A memorial will be held July 20, 4 PM, at The Reformed Church of Piermont.
