
        SCHWARZ-Lotte. The American Friends of Bar-Ilan University in Israel mourn the passing of the beloved mother of our esteemed benefactor Miguel Schwarz. We extend to Miguel and all his loved ones our sincerest condolences. His philanthropies were a source of pride to her even as he continues to inspire all who know him. May all the mourners derive solace from cherished memories. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, Chancellor, Bar-Ilan University Prof Moshe Kaveh, President, Bar-Ilan University Charles Dimston, Chairman, American Board of Trustees Melvin Stein, President American Friends Brig. Gen. (Ret) Yehuda Halevy, Executive Vice President Bar-Ilan University
