
        WALKER-James Wear, of Hobe Sound Fla., formerly of Oyster Bay, New York, died on August 30 after suffering a heart attack. Son of the founder of the international Walker Cup competition, he was a lifelong golfer and devoted family man. Husband of the late Sara Mitchell Walker. Survived by brother Louis of Hobe Sound, daughter Hilary Walker Gilmore of Ore., stepdaughter Sheila O'Keefe Todd of Colo., stepson John O'Keefe of MA.; grandchildren: William Walker Todd, James Bridger Todd, Whitney DeForest Todd, James Tucker Gilmore and Eliza Fahy Johnstone Gilmore; one great-granddaughter, many nieces and nephews. Family services will be held September 5 in Kennebunkport, Maine.
