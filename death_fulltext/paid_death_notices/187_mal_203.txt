
        EIBERSON-M. Arthur. Former Supervising Judge of Nassau County District Court. Passed away Sunday, August 18, 2002. He leaves behind his loving wife Freda, daughters Debra Gordon and Francine Capicchioni and their husbands Stephen and Edward. Cherished grandchildren Jeffrey, Daniel, Amy, George, Scott and sister Norma Hillman. Services are today 11 A.M. from Temple Israel, Riverside Boulevard, Long Beach, New York.
