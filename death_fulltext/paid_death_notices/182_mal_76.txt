
        GRANDIN-John L. Of Chestnut Hill, February 25, 2002. Officer of The Gillette Company. Beloved husband of the late Susanne P. W. Grandin and loving father of John L., III and his wife Anne of Chestnut Hill, Edward W. of Jamaica Plain and Preston E. and his wife Susan of Chestnut Hill. Also survived by five grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Northfield Mount Hermon School, Northfield, MA 01360. Late WW II U.S. Navy Reserve Lt. Commander. For information, call J.S.
