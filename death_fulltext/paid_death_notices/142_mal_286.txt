
        FOWKES-Robert Allen, Ph.D. The St. David's Society of the State of New York records with profound sorrow the death of its much beloved past president (1955-1958), Professor Robert A. Fowkes. Born in Harrison, NY, on April 7, 1913, he married Angela Vescio in Yonkers, NY on June 17, 1939. He died in Valhalla, NY, on November 18, 1998. Interment will be in Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings, NY. To his wife, Angela, his sons, Robert, Jr., Richard, and William, his grandchildren and great-grandchildren, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Mary Lynne Bird, President James W. Thomas, Secretary
