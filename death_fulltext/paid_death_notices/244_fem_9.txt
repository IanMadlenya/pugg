
        CARPENTER--Marjorie J., ''Bonnie'', on April 29, 2007, at the age of 63, of esophageal cancer. Lifetime resident of Bay Shore, NY and Fair Harbor, Fire Island. Devoted wife Phil, Jr., and loving mother of Jennifer, Philip III., Elizabeth, Susan Moore and her husband Tony. Also survived by her cherished sister Susanne Potter and her husband Robert. Bonnie graduated from the University of Miami with a BS/RN in 1964 and worked as a nurse at Southside Hospital for 16 years. She was an active member of the Dollars and Sense Investment Club, a dedicated volunteer for both Hospice and Planned Parenthood and a frequent wildlife rescuer. Bonnie shared with her family and friends a love of gardening, nature and all living creatures. She possessed an indomitable spirit and joie de vivre. The enthusiasm and passion with which she embraced life will never be forgotten. We will always honor her memory as a true survivor, having overcome both pancreatic cancer in 1983 and breast cancer in 1997. Memorial Visitation, Wednesday 7 pm -9 pm at The Fredrick J. Chapel & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Causeway). Memorial Service Thursday, 10 am at the Funeral Home. Reception to follow at Captain Bill's Restaurant, Bay Shore, NY.
