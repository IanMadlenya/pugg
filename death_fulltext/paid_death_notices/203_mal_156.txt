
        BUIRSKI -- Physil ''Fishie.'' Age 95, died December 13, in Hallandale, FL. Born 1908, in Capetown, South Africa. Received an MBA from Columbia University. Businessman in Johannesburg and NYC. Married 65 years to the late Pearl. Ever-present father of Peter and Cathy, devoted grandfather of Max and Diana, caring brother of Abe and the late Margot Barkham and Pauline Forbath. The Buirski family has lost its patriarch. We were lucky to have Fishie for so long but will miss him deeply. ''Tot siens.''
