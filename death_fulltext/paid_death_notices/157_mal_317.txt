
        PARKER-Herbert M., DDS. Died on February 19; he was 87. He practiced dentistry in NYC, and taught at NYU Dental School until 1992. He published articles in prosthodontics and was elected a fellow to a number of dental societies. He was married to Dorothy R. Parker for over 50 years. They lived in Scarsdale for more than 35 years where they were involved in local activities. He is survived by a daughter, Elissa, and a son, Cary. He is missed by colleagues, friends, and family.
