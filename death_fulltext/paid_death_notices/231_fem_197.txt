
        ZUCKERMAN--Muriel. Died on April 26, 2006 at the age of 83. She worked many years for the Youth Consultation Service. Muriel is survived by her husband Sam, daughter Judith (Joseph) McGreal, sons David (Ferne) and Michael, grandchildren Mark, Lynn and Daniel, sister Doris Dreyer and four nieces. Services today at 12 noon at Bet Am Shalom Synagogue, 295 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, NY.
