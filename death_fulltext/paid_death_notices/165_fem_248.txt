        GLICKMAN-Doris. The Board, staff, and orchestra members of the New York Chamber Symphony mourn the passing of Doris Glickman, wife of Loren Glickman, our founding orchestra contractor and mother of our Board Member, Ron Glickman. Doris attended many of our concerts and events-we will miss her. Our deepest sympathy to Loren, Ron and the entire Glickman family. Dr. Ernest Kafka, Chairman Jeffrey Klein, Chairman Emeritus Gerard Schwarz, Music Director Omus Hirshbein, Executive V.P.
        GLICKMAN-Doris. The New York Choral Society mourns the death of Doris Glickman, beloved wife of Loren Glickman and loyal friend of the New York Choral Society for over 30 years. We will miss her warm friendship and lively spirit. John Daly Goodwin, Music Director Robert DeCormier, Music Director Emeritus Stephen M. Weitzman, MD, Chairman James Hagen, President
        GLICKMAN-Doris. We will miss our beloved vibrant friend who lived so fully and cared so much. Miriam and Yiannis
