
        MAHON--Catherine Barry. On March 13, 2005 in her 99th year of life. Beloved wife of the late Thomas. Loving mother of Cornelia Mahon & Maureen Kelly. Also survived by four grandchildren and four great-grandchildren, as well as several nieces and nephews. Her wisdom, her joyous, generous, steadfast spirit gave light to all our lives. We shall miss her. Visitation Friday, 9-9:30 AM at Mary Manning Walsh Home (1339 York Ave, NYC). Mass of Christian Burial, on Friday at 9:30 A.M., at Walsh Home. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory to Walsh Home.
