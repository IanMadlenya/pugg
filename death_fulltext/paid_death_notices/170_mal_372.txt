
        GORDON-Otto Robert. On March 23rd, 2001. A retired orthopedic surgeon who practiced in Hungary and New York. A cherished husband, father, stepfather and grandfather who will be missed deeply. He touched countless lives through his work, humor, intelligence and love for life. His work with disabled children always held a special place in his heart. Memorial contributions may be sent to Alexander Ability-School for Disabled Children, Chandler, AZ or Shriners Hospitals for Children, Tampa, FL.
