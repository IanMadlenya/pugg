
        KAPLAN--Ellen. The trustees and staff of the 1199SEIU Benefit and Pension Funds mourn the loss of our dear friend and colleague Ellen Kaplan, Chief Pension Officer. During her six years with the Funds, Ellen was deeply committed to improving the lives of our members and retirees. Her fighting spirit, integrity and dedication will be deeply missed by those of us who had the honor of working with her. We offer our deepest sympathy to her husband Eddie, her children Melissa and Brian & her grandchildren Rebecca, Kevin and Zoe
