
        GOLDMAN - Florence Dreizen. Age 84, of Atlanta, GA, and formerly of New Rochelle, NY, on February 3, 2001. Florence enjoyed floral arranging and won several blue ribbons for her accomplishments. Survived by her husband Harold J. Goldman, her children Nancy E. Levy and her husband Paul, Dr. Robert Goldman and his wife Toby; beloved grandchildren from Nancy, Glen Levy, Cheryl and her husband Brian, Harris, and one greatgrandchild, Maxwell Harris; from Robert, Hana, Avi, Aaron and Nathaniel. She is also survived by one sister, Ann, and brother-in-law Abe Bress. Services will be today, 10:30 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Mt. Eden Cemetery. Donations may be made to Hadassah or New Atlanta Jewish Community High School, 2012 Womack Rd., Dunwoody, GA 30338.
