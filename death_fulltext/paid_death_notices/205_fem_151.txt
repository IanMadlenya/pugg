
        KETOVER--Cele, 93, former New York City restauranteur, passed away with grace and dignity on February 18th in Boca Raton, FL where she had retired after a lifelong love affair with New York. She was a beloved wife, mother, grandmother, greatgrandmother and friend who now joins her eldest son and husband. Her wit, her love and her indomitable spirit will be deeply missed by all who truly loved her. Memorial service will be held at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, Sunday, 10:30AM.
