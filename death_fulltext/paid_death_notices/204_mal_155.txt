
        BERGER -- Seymour. Died January 9th at age 80. Dearly beloved husband, soulmate and friend, at my side and in my heart and mind for twenty-five years. I mourn your death deeply. Jan
        BERGER--Seymour. On January 9, 2004. Adored husband of Jan, beloved brother and pal of Marilyn and Don Hewitt and of the late Adele Satz and Florence Globus; fun-loving uncle of David, Joanne and James Wallenstein. Sy's hearty laughter will echo in our ears; his love for us and our love for him will be forever with us.
