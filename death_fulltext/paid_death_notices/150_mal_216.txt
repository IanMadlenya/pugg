
        FRIEDLANDER-Michael J. 61, loving husband of Michele Trufant Friedlander, father of John Friedlander, brother of Marilyn Cohen, died July 8, 1999, in Fairfax Hospital, Fairfax, VA, after a brief illness. A true horseman and a passionate supporter of 3-day eventing, Michael encouraged many riders and horses at all levels of the sport. He graduated from Deerfield Academy and Princeton University and was a skilled commodities trader from early childhood, ultimately founding and chairing M.J. Friedlander Co., Inc. He will forever be missed for his consummate style, joyful wit and tremendous love of life. Donations may be made to favored charities. Following Michael's wishes, there will be no services.
