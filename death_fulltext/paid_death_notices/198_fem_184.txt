
        MESSINESI -- Despina (nee Plakias), of New York City and Millerton, NY, on July 22, 2003. At Vogue magazine, from 1941 until her retirement in 1993, she worked as a fashion writer and editor, as American Vogue's representative in Paris, immediately following WWII until the early 1950's, and as the magazine's travel editor for over 20 years. Survived by her nephew, Nick Plakias, cousin Dora Messare, sister-in-law Mrs. John Plakias, and loving friends. She will be deeply missed. A memorial service is planned for September. >>AD#
