
        MALLOY--Joseph D. On September 1, 2003, in Huntington, LI. Survived by his wife Vivian and children David, Barbara, Cathy and Steven. Also grandfather of Jessica and Sarah Meg. Mourned by all including his sisters Madeline Anbinder and Tobene Rosenthal, his brother Howard Malloy and sister-in-law Susan Malloy. Joe's professional life involved submarine simulator design and production as President of Hydrosystems. He will be remembered for his personal qualities, faithfulness to family and friends, and a great sense of fun.
