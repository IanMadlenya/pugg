
        ZACK-Beatrice Bodin, 86, of NYC. Died on March 17, 2001 in Baltimore, MD. For 60 years, beloved wife of David Zack. Devoted mother of Dr. Brian Zack (and wife Virginia August) of Princeton, NJ and of Dr. Donald Zack (and wife Dr. Ellen Okun) of Baltimore, MD. Cherished grandmother of Rebecca, Ian and Daniel Zack of Princeton, and of Rachel, Elizabeth and Andrew Zack of Baltimore. Private memorial service. She will be deeply missed.
