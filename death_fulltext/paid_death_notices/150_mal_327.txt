
        FAUS-Norton T. Of Boca Raton, FL and Darien, CT on July 19, 1999. Husband of June Northrop Faus. Graduate Stanford University in 1931 and received his MBA from Harvard in 1933. Retired Presiding Partner of MacKay-Shields Economics, Inc in 1973. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. Interment private.
