
        SCHLOESSINGER - Elsbeth. On February 25, 2001, at home. A woman of valor. Born in Frankfurt, Germany in 1912 to Alice and Leo Goldschmidt. Her sister, Eva Mela survives. Beloved wife of the late Willi Schloessinger, cherished mother of Fred and Kathleen Schloessinger, and Joan and Mark Chesler. Adored grandmother of Naomi Chesler and Deborah Char. Elsbeth was a special friend to many people. Her compassion, generosity, intellect, wit, elegance, grace, warmth, and love enriched us all. Services Tuesday 1:30 P.M. at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
        SCHLOESSINGER-Elsbeth. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Mordecai Waxman, Rabbi George N. Abrahams, President
