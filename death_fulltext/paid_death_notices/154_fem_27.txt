
        BLITZER-Florence Kaiden. On November 3, 1999. Beloved wife of Arnold. Loving aunt, great aunt and dear sister-in-law. Florence was a master reading teacher for the New York City Board of Education and for Hunter College. She will be profoundly missed. Service Friday, 10AM at the ''Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        BLITZER-Florence. Sutton Place Synagogue records with sorrow the loss of our longtime member and Patron. Our sympathies to her devoted husband, Arnold, and the bereaved family. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
