
        STARFIELD-Harry. Died peacefully in his bed on April 20 at the age of 93. He was a well beloved teacher of Spanish at Tilden High School before his retirement. He spent many happy summers in Schroon Lake, NY. He is survived by his sisters, Esther Siegel, Phoebe Gozan and Florence Surovell. He is also survived by three generations of adoring nieces and nephews.
