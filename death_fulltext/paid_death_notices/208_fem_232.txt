
        RAE--Barbara. 76, of Edgewater, FL, passed away on Thursday, May 20, 2004. Born in Perth Amboy, NJ, she and her family lived in Ho-HoKus, NJ for many years before moving to Florida in 1974. Barbara is predeceased by her husband John ''Jack'' Rae, and is survived by sons Tom Rae and John Rae Jr., daughters Robin Rae Infante and Kori Rae, sisters Lucille Wiley and Anita Zeidman.
