
        MASLIANSKY-Joseph. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Joseph Masliansky, a cherished colleague and dear friend who served as director of our Council of Organizations from 1973 until his retirement in 1988. As an exceptional professional who was deeply devoted to Israel, he enhanced our efforts through his unwavering commitment and effective leadership. We extend our sincerest condolences to his beloved wife, Tsippora; to his children Nechama, Ronnie, Moshe, and Avi; to his grandchildren; and to his entire family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair and President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
