
        BREITMAN--Jeffrey K. Died April 26, 2007 of heart failure at age 49. Most dearly beloved son of Ruth and Robert Friedman and the late Harold Breitman. Loved and adored by his sister Joanne Breitman Singer and her husband Eric. Brother Martin Friedman (Toinette), his nieces and his sister, the late Anne Friedman. All who knew Jeff respected and thought highly of him. He was intelligent, caring, loving and kind. A fine gentleman. He will be missed.
