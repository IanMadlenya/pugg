
        PERRY-Richard S. Of Baldwin, NY, husband of the late Ruth Carolyn Richards, father of the late Judith Ann Perry, on Saturday, April 3, 1999. Mr. Perry was a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Wharton School Class of 1937. A World War II Navy veteran, he was president of William Iselin & Co., New York, NY. He is survived by his son, Richard S. Perry, and grandsons, Petty Officer 3rd USN Christopher T. Perry and Schuyler N. Perry of Sandy Hook, Conn. Reposing at WeigandPensa Funeral Home, 2283 Grand Ave., Baldwin. Visiting hours Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Services Wednesday, 11 AM. Cremation private.
