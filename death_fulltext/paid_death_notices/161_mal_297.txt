
        MOSELEY-Ronald G. Born January 10, 1935. Passed away after a long illness on June 24, 2000 in New York City. He is survived by his beloved children, Nicole, Tash and Ron Jr.; his stepchildren, Kwabana and Biko; his grandchildren, Jackson and Malloy; his brothers, James and Glenn; his sister, Lenore and his ex-wives, Louise, Katharine and Afi. A memorial service will be held at Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 117 West 72nd St., Wednesday, June 28, at 11 AM.
