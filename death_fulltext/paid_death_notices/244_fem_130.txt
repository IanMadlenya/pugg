
        STRASSMAN--Natalie Krieger on May 14, 2007, at 96 years young. Beloved wife of the late Solomon, devoted daughter and sister, wonderful mother, adored grandmother and great-grandmother. Survived by daughters and their husbands Judy and Eric Herbst, Toby and Harold Weinberger, grandchildren and their spouses James and Erica Weinberger, Karen Weinberger, Elisabeth and Emmanuel Buzay, Andrea and Jacques Paul, Seth Herbst, and greatgrandsons Eli and Nate Weinberger. She spent the last years of her life surrounded by the family she loved and that loved her so.
