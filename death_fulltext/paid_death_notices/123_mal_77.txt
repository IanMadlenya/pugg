
        HECKSCHER-August. Was a valiant and inspirational member of the New School community, serving with great devotion for 41 years as a Trustee and Life Trustee, as Chair of the Board of Trustees, Chair of the Board of Governors of Parsons School of Design, and member of the Board of Governors of Eugene Lang College and Mannes College of Music. He believed deeply in the mission of higher education and in the responsibility of each individual for the environment of our cities. The Board extends its heartfelt condolences to his wife Claude, their sons Stephen, Philip and Charles, and their 6 grandchildren. John L. Tishman, Chair, Board of Trustees Jonathan F. Fanton, President New School for Social Research
