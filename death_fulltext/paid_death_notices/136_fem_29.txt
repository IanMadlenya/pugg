
        WOLFF-Anne K. In loving memory of Anne K. Wolff. Anne was a wonderful grandmother and mother, cherished and treasured for her infinite wisdom & unending love, kindness and indomitable spirit. She will be dearly missed & remembered forever by Janine, Steven, Nancy and Bradford. Her caring nature brought joy to everyone. These memories of her will always remain in our hearts and souls from this day forward to eternity & beyond. We will always luminate this eternal light of Anne's love for us and for all who were touched by her life. May her memorial remain forever more.
