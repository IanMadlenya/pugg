
        ALLEN--Lewis. The Actors' Fund of America mourns the death of Lewis M. Allen. Lew was a dedicated trustee of the Actors' Fund and the many people helped by the fund are eternally grateful for his efforts in improving the lives of all entertainment professionals. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his wife Jay Presson Allen and daughter Brooke. Tom Dillon, President Joe Benincasa, Exec Dir.
