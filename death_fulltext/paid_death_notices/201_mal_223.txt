
        PURNELL--Richard I. Of Locust Valley and Fishers Island, N.Y. On October 21, 2003, age 85. Beloved husband of Marguerite H., cherished father of Marguerite W. and Peter F. (Suzanne) and proud grandfather of Jimmy, Teddy, Bobby and Henry. Survived by many nieces, nephews and cousins. Visitation will be at Dodge-Thomas Funeral Home, Glen Cove, N.Y. on Monday, October 27th from 4-7PM and Tuesday, October 28th from 9AM10AM. A funeral service will be held on Tuesday, October 28th at 11AM at St. Johns of Lattingtown. Private burial to be held at Fishers Island. In lieu of flowers donations in Richard I. Purnell's name may be made to Princeton University Class of 1940. Memorial Scholarship Fund, Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center or The Kent School.
        PURNELL--Richard I. The Women's Committee of the Central Park Conservancy acknowledges with great sadness the passing of Richard I. Purnell. We offer deepest sympathy to his wife, Marguerite Purnell, co-founder and second president of the Women's Committee and trustee of the Conservancy, and to the entire Purnell family. Maggie, with the support of her husband, has been instrumental in the restoration of Central Park over the past 20 years. Mr. Purnell will be remembered for his gentleness, kindness, sense of humor and generosity. Women's Committee Board Central Park Conservancy
