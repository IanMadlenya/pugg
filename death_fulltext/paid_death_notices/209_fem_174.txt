
        YOUNG--Diane. Died on June 22, 2004. Beloved wife of Irwin. Devoted mother of Nancy Young, M.D. and Linda Young and mother-in-law of Mitch Marinello. Cherished grandmother of Samantha, Lindsey and Michelle. Loving sister of Arthur Nalven. She was an officer and director of Du Art Film and Video Laboratories, in New York City. Services Thursday, 11 AM, at The Community Synagogue of Sands Point, 150 Middle Neck Rd, Pt Washington, NY.
        YOUNG--Diane. North Shore Country Club records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Diane Young. We extend our deepest condolences to her family. Peter J. Baum, President Stuart Bednoff, Secretary
