
        PERLOW--George J., age 84. Beloved brother of Diane Hempel and loving uncle of Miranda/Toby Hempel and Peter Hempel. Dear friends who mourn his loss along with legal and accounting colleagues. Mr. Perlow was a member of the Bar of the United States Supreme Court, Finance Chairman of North Shore Towers Corp, Little Neck, NY, was appointed to the Legislative Advisory Committee of The Assembly of NY. Service Thursday, May 27, 2004, 11AM at Mount Lebanon Cemetery and Sanctuary, 78th St, Myrtle Avenue near Forest Park, Brooklyn, New York. Thank you for your love, life and your joy and integrity.
