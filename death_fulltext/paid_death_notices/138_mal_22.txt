
        BAMBERY-Richard John, Sr. Of Garden City, New York. Peacefully passed into eternal rest on July 1, 1998. Loving father of Richard, Jr., Michael, Robert and the late Michelle. Grandfather of seven. Forever in the heart of Agnes Napolitano. Born in Saint Paul, MN. Pilot. Major, Eighth Air Force, W.W. II, National Director of Marketing for Admiral and Panasonic Corporations. Member of the Garden City Golf Club. At his request, his body has been donated to Stony Brook Anatomical Department. Memorial Mass to be held at Saint Joseph's Church, Garden City, July 9th, at 10 A.M.
