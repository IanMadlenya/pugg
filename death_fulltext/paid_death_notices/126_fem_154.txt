
        HEISLER-Florence A. Westbury Friends School Board mourns the passing of Florence Heisler, Ph.D, dedicated and long term supporter of the school. She died in her Bentley Village retirement home in Florida on June 3, 1997. She was born on April 3, 1909 and was a member of the Religious Society of Friends (Quakers). She received her Ph.D. in Psychology from New York University. Her deep interest and support of the Westbury Friends School was a great source of strength to us. We are grateful for her wisdom and generosity of her spirit. A memorial service will be held at Westbury Friends Meeting House in Westbury, NY on Sunday, September 28, 1997, at 4 P.M. Contributions may be made in Florence Heisler's name to the Westbury Friends School, 550 Post Avenue, Westbury, New York 11590.
