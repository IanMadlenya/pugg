
        THADDEUS-Janice Farrar. On December 23, 2001, of a stroke in Cambridge, MA. Beloved wife of Patrick and mother of Eva and Michael, she is also survived by a brother, Curtis Farrar, and a sister, Alison Wilson. As a teacher of English literature for over forty years at Barnard and Harvard, she touched and challenged thousands of students. A memorial celebration will be held later this winter. Contributions can be sent to: Doctors Without Borders, 6 E. 39th St. 8th floor, New York, NY 10016.
