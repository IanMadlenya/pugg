
        BRODY-Claire. October 25, 2002, died peacefully in her sleep in Providence, Rhode Island. Widow of Steve Brody. True friend of Jean Kalmanoff and Elaine Jupiter. Survived by her daughter Jill, her son-in-law Herb Emers and her loving grandchildren Jenny, Nola and David. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Wildlife Conservation Society, 2300 Southern Boulevard, Bronx, NY 10460. Memorial service will be held at a later date.
