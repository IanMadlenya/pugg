
        HAYES-Father Thomas L. Director of the Episcopal Peace Fellowship during the Vietnam War. He participated in the draft card burning in Sheep's Meadow in Central Park in 1967. He served as minister to the American draft resisters in Sweden. Funeral services today, 4 PM, at St. Paul's Episcopal Church, Windsor, VT. Blessed are the peacemakers. Norma Becker, Vietnam Peace Parade Committee
