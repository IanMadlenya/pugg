
        O'GORMAN-Emma Louisa (Crawford), in her eighty-fourth year. Beloved wife of the late Edward M. O'Gorman, devoted mother of Kathleen, Charlotte, Elizabeth and Martha. Loving daughter of the late James and Anne Kerr Crawford. Cherished grandmother of eight. A memorial service will be held Saturday, March 8 at 11:00 AM at St. Mary'sin-Tuxedo, Tuxedo Park, N.Y. Contributions to the Tuxedo Park School, Tuxedo Park, NY 10987 or the Tuxedo Volunteer Ambulance Corps would be appreciated.
