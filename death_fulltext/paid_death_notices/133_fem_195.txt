
        HUBBELL-Dorothy Twyeffort. A woman of elegant strength, died February 23, 1998 in Falmouth, Me. She was 89. Born in Paris, to American parents, Josephine and Louis Twyeffort, she lived there until she graduated from Vassar in 1930. A year later she married William Bradford Hubbell and moved to Bedford, N.Y. Captain W.B. Hubbell died in 1943, serving in the Army Air Corps in Alaska. She lived on Mianus River Road in Bedford for 63 years and retired to Me. in 1995. She is survived by their sons William B. Hubbell, Jr. of Cumberland Foreside, Me., John T. Hubbell of Asheville, NC., daughter Anne H. French of Bedford, NY and by 8 grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Mrs. Hubbell was an Elder of the Bedford Presbyterian Church as well as a devoted supporter of the Mianus Gorge Preserve. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be sent to the Mianus Gorge Preserve, 167 Mianus River Road, Bedford, New York. 10506. A memorial service will be held in the spring in Bedford when the lilacs are in bloom.
