
        LUKE-William Guthrie, Jr., 80, of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL., passed away Monday, June 9, 1997 at his residence. He was born January 3, 1917 in Luke, MD. Mr. Luke served as a Lieutenant in the USNR during WWII. He was a graduate of the Lawrenceville School and Cornell University. He is survived by his wife, Marjorie, his son, William G. III; his two daughters, Sandra L. Stephenson and Barbara L. Bareham; his sister, Marion Nagele; eight grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Northeast Florida, Jacksonville, FL. or The Lawrenceville School, Lawrenceville, NJ.
