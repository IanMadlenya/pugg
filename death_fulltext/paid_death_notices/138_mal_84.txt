
        KNUDSEN-Semon E. The Board of Governors, Trustees and staff of Boys & Girls Clubs of America (B&GCA) wish to express their sorrow over the recent passing of Mr. Semon E. Knudsen, a B&GCA trustee for nearly 40 years. His lifelong concern for America's children was personified by his tireless work on their behalf, both on the national and local level. We will miss him dearly. Boys & Girls Clubs of America Rick Goings, Chairman Roxanne Spillett, President
