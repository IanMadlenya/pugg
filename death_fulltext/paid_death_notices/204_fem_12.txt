
        SCHREIBER -- Maurine K. Age 97. Beloved wife of the late Isadore Raiff and the late E. Martie Schreiber. Loving mother of Frederic and Pauline Raiff. Cherished grandmother, great-grandmother and aunt. Services private.
        SCHREIBER -- Maurine K. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Maurine K. Schreiber our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi Dr. David M. Posner, Senior Rabbi Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Senior Rabbi Emeritus Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Secty
        SCHREIBER--Maurine K. The Board of Directors, Officers and Staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind note with sadness the death of Maurine Schreiber, beloved motherin-law of our esteemed Board Chairman, Pauline Raiff.
