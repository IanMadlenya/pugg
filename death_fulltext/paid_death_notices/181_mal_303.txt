
        LANGTON-Michael J., 59. Of Bahrain, beloved husband of Irene ''Gay'' (Maluski) Langton, died Friday (February 22, 2002) in Bahrain. Born August 12, 1942, in Baltimore, MD, son of the late Harold ''Irish'' and Dorothea (Griffith) Langton. He had lived in Matawan, NJ and St. Augustine, FL., and was a resident of Bahrain for the past eight years. Mr. Langton received his Master's in Business Administration from Fordham University and at the time of his death was Director of Bahrain Institute of Banking and Finance (BIBF). He had previously been Director of American Institute of Banking in New York City, Executive Officer of New York State Bankers Assoc., Executive fficer of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. Mr Langton was a member of the Salmaniya Rotary Club, Chairman of the Board of the American Mission Hospital, founder and chairman of AAB/AMH Island Classic Golf Tournament, member of Bahrain Bankers Society, Founder of the TradeQuest Program for high school students. Besides his wife he is survived by a son, 3 daughters, 4 brothers, 4 sisters, and 2 grandchildren. A mass of Christian Burial will be held Saturday (March 2) at 11:00 AM in St. Dunstan's Church, Manchester Road, Glastonbury. Burial will be at the convenience of the family. Friends may call at the Mulryan Funeral Home, 725 Hebron Avenue, Glastonbury, Thursday from 5-8 PM and Friday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made to the Fordham Football Recruiting Fund, c/o Michael Fazio, Development Office, Fordham University, 113 West 60th Street, New York, New York 10023-7471.
