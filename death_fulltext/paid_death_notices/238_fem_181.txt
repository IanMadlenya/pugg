
        HUFFORD--Susan. The Board of Directors and staff of The ALS Association Greater New York Chapter and The National Board of Trustees mourn the loss of our esteemed board member Susan Hufford. Susan was a passionate advocate for people with ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease). As President of Michael Zaslow's ZazAngels, she worked tirelessly to advance the search for a cure. She was a champion in the fight against ALS and will be missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her devoted daughter, Marika, her friends and extended family.
        HUFFORD--Susan, Born December 15, 1938. Died November 28, 2006. Actor, author, psychotherapist, President of ZazAngels, member of Board of Trustees ALS Association, Hodel in the original production of Fiddler on the Roof. Among her many books were ''Going All The Way'', ''Reflections'', ''Miracles'', ''Not That Man Any More''. Beloved mother to Marika Zaslow, devoted wife to the late Michael Zaslow, loving mother to the late Helena Zaslow, cherished friend to many. Spirited enthusiast of life. Memorial service Friday, December 1, 1:15 pm, Riverside Memorial, Amsterdam Avenue between 75th and 76th Street. Contributions to ZazAngels c/o ALS Association, 116 John Street, Suite 1304, New York, NY 10038
