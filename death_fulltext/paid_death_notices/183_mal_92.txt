
        TOBELMAN - William Thomas. Died Thursday, March 28, 2002 at his home in Orford, NH. He was born June 10, 1928 in South Orange, NJ and is survived by his wife Ella St. Amand Tobelman, son William T. II, daughter Margaret J. and two grandsons. He, a member of the class of 1950 at Brown University and Psi Upsilon, served as 1st Lt. in the U.S. Army. At retirement, he was the Advertising and Sales Promotion Manager for Sargent and Co. in New Haven and lived in Clinton, CT.
