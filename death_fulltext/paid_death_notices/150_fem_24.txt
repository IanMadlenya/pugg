
        McVEY-Elizabeth, 87. Of Scottsdale, AZ died July 1, 1999. Born in New York, she worked for Metropolitan Insurance Co. in New York City. Survived by sisters, Frances A. and Jane Mary of Scottsdale, AZ; brothers, William G., New York; James, New Jersey; Robert, Scottsdale, AZ; four nephews, four nieces, three great-nieces. Mass Tuesday 10 AM Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Scottsdale. Interment St. Charles Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY. Contributions to favorite charity.
