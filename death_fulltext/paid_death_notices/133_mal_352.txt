
        ROBBINS-Barnard H., M.D. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Barnard H. Robbins, M.D., devoted husband of the late Beatrice Goldstein Robbins. His compassion and concern for those less fortunate will live on in the generous gifts he created for their benefit through UJA-Federation, and Beth Israel Medical Center, a UJA-Federation beneficiary. We extend our sincerest condolences to the entire family. Services 1 P.M., Wednesday, February 25, at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        ROBBINS-Bernard, M.D. Beloved husband of the late Beatrice. Loving brother of Dr. Edward Robbins. Services 1 P.M., Wednesday at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        ROBBINS-Barnard, M.D. (Robbie). Neighbor, friend, fisherman. With sadness upon the loss of a founding congregant whose prayers, support, confidence, Levihood and warm smile we will truly miss. May his soul find peace and eternal rest along with all the mourners of Israel and Jerusalem. The Fire Island Minyan
