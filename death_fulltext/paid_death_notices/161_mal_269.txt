
        LUBIN-Emanuel. The Benenson Capital Company mourns the loss of our Distinguished friend and trusted advisor, Manny Lubin. We will miss his advice, intelligence, counsel and wit. We have over fifty years of business and friendship together to remember and cherish. We will always remember a man who was greatly dedicated to his family and business. Those of us who knew how special he was know the sense of loss that is shared by his family, friends andhis associates. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Evelyn, his daughter Helen, his son-in-law Robert, and his grandchildren Caroline and David.
