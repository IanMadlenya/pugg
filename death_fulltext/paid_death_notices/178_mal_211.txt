
        STAMBAUGH-Randolph U. On October 26, 2001 at age 90. Former president of B. Altman & Co., New York City. Survived by his wife, Mary Clark Stambaugh; his two daughters, Anne Satterthwaite (Henry), and Dana Semeraro (Richard); three grandchildren, Dana Amy Jonson, Randolph Jonson, and Kenneth Semeraro. Also survived by his sister, Mary Shafer. He was predeceased by his wife of 50 years, Virginia Wiggins Stambaugh in 1987.
