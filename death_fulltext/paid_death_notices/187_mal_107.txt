
        KAVANAGH-Roger Pierce Jr. 84, died at his home in Greensboro, NC, on July 25th, 2002. 1953 co-founder Kavanagh-Smith and Co., 1967 cofounder Westminster Homes. Board of Directors Greensboro Urban Ministry, founder low income housing fund for Greensboro Community Foundation. Survived by his wife Elizabeth G. Kavanagh and two sons, John Kavanagh and his wife Ellen of Greensboro, and Roger Kavanagh III and his wife Danielle of New Orleans. Also survived by five grandchildren, Mary Ellen and Elizabeth Anne Kavanagh of Greensboro, Zachary, Dylan and Sophie Kavanagh of New Orleans, and a sister Mary Louise Dyer of Carmel, CA.
