
        BEREANO-Leon. Beloved husband of Beatrice for 60 years. Devoted father of Philip and Bruce. Loving grandfather of Joshua and Bryon. Services Thursday, December 18, at The Riverdale Temple, West 246 Street and Independence Avenue.
        BEREANO-Leon P. The Rabbi, Cantor, Officers, Executive Board of Trustees and members of Riverdale Temple mourn the passing of our beloved Leon P. Bereano. A devoted long-standing member, he served as Trustee, was Chairman of High Holy Day Arrangements Committee, served on the Journal Committee, and worked ardently for our bazaars. He will be missed. Sincere and heartfelt condolences to his wife Bea and his sons Bruce and Dr. Philip.
