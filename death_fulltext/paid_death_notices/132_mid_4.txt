
        GUILDEN-Louise. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the death of Louise Guilden. We extend our sincere sympathy to her son Paul, a true and valued friend of the Home and the entire Guilden family. We hope that your loving memories of Louise will ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec. V.P.
        GUILDEN-Louise. The Board Members of the US Wheelchair Sports Fund extend our sympathy to our fellow board member, Paul Guilden, on the loss of his mother Louise, who passed away on January 15, 1998 at the age of 100. Richard Traum, President
