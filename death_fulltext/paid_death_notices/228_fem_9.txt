
        GETZLER--Eva. December 28, in her 100th year. Wife of the late Charles. Mother of Abe and Shelly (Horowitz). Cherished grandmother of Renee, Marian, Joel, Robin, Amy, Allen. Beloved greatgrandmother of 14. Greatgreat-grandmother of two. A wonderful, remarkable woman. Many good memories and her legacy of wisdom and love will help ease the pain of our loss.
