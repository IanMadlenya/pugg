
        WADDELL--Harry Lee, of Yachats, OR (formerly of NYC, Port Jervis and Garden City), on June 25. A newspaper and magazine editor and executive, he worked for the Buffalo Evening News, McGraw-Hill, Doubleday & Co., and Simmons-Boardman. In 1983 he retired to Port Jervis with his second wife Helene. He moved to Yachats in 1996 to be near his daughters: Nancy of Whidbey Island, WA; Carol of Malibu, CA; Jessica of Yachats; three grandchildren, one great-grandchild. His inquisitive spirit and concern for community and young people will be missed by those who knew him. Memorials to Hospice of Orange County or Alzheimer's Assoc. >>AD#
