
        ZIMBERG-Stuart. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed Member Friar Stuart Zimberg on November 19th. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Thursday, November 23, at 10AM, Boulevard Riverside, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York, 516-295-3101. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
