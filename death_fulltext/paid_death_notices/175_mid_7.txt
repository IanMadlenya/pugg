
        MENDELSON-Matilda. The entire Congregation Beth Sholom family mourns the passing of Matilda Mendelson, mother of our beloved Cantor Solomon Mendelson, his brother Cantor Jacob Mendelson, and his sisters Betty Busch and Shirley Wendroff. Her loving kindness and compassion were felt by all. We send our love to her family. Ellyn Kreitman, President
