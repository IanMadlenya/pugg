
        UCHIN--Jerome F., D.D.S. on November 11, 2003. Of Boca Raton, FL., formerly of Highland Park and Princeton, N.J. A graduate of the NYU School of Dentistry (1942) and practiced in the New Brunswick area. Loving husband of Dorothy to whom he was married 58 years. Adored father of Larry Uchin and Meryl Schultz. Cherished grandfather, beloved brother.
