
        NOUN-Louise Rosenfield. 94 of Des Moines, Iowa died August 23, 2002. Memorial Services will be 11 a.m., Saturday September 14, 2002 at the Iowa State Historical Building in Des Moines. Louise was a life long Feminist, Philanthropist, Author and Social activist. Her grandson survives her: Jason Flora of Fairfield, IA. She was a member of the Harvard Club and had close ties to the Ms. Foundation. Memorial contributions are acceptable to: The Chrysalis Foundation, 3101 Ingersoll Ave., Des Moines, IA 50312, The Iowa Civil Liberties Union, 446 Insurance Exchange Building, Suite 901, Des Moines, IA 50309 or the Iowa Women's Archives at University of Iowa Libraries, Iowa City, IA 52242. For information, contact Dunn's Funeral Home 515-244-2121.
