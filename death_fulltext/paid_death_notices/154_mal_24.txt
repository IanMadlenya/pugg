
        PRICE-Samson D. Died November 2, 1999 in Greenville, SC. He was 96 years old. He is survived by his wife, Ruth; his sons, Kenneth, Edward and Arthur; his grandchildren Giselle, Andrew and Stephen; and his daughters-in law, Graciela, Patricia and Sandra. He was a pioneer of the frozen fruit industry. He was a founder of Chill-Ripe Fruit, Inc., Frosted Fruit Products, Inc., Mid-Valley Products, Inc, and Empacadora Santa Clara, S.A. He was devoted to his family, his church and his industry.
