
        PANKEN-Irving, 78, Director of Organization, Professional Staff Congress-CUNY and lifetime Social Democrat, died on April 13 after a lengthy illness. Beloved husband of Shirley Panken, devoted father of Ted Panken, doting grandfather of Angelina, loving brother of Mildred Diller. Irving began life as a legendary soapbox orator in the Bronx; he brought clarity, vision and sweet spirit to the many worlds he intersected with and enriched.
