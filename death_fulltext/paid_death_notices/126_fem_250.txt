
        KNOWLES-Corinne ''Renee,'' widow of Kenneth E. Knowles Jr., passed away on July 29, 1997. She was 77 years of age. She is survived by her daughter, Nancy J. Knowles; son, Kenneth E. Knowles III, and two grandchildren, William and Eliza. A private memorial service will be held at Christ Church in Oyster Bay. Contributions, in lieu of flowers, should be made to the North Shore Animal League.
