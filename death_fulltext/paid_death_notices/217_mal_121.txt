
        MILLER--Harold. Beloved by his wife Lillian, his son Brian, his daughter-in-law Deirdre, his grandchildren Gabrielle, Kayla, Shaanan and Carmela. Longtime resident of Jackson Heights. Graduate of the University of Michigan with degrees in Chemical and Mathematical Engineering. Served in W.W. II and the Merchant Marines. A manufacturer of stuffed toys with factories in Brooklyn, Long Island City and Korea. He was an active member as vice president in the the Jewish Center of Jackson Heights. Shiva at 86-56 Clio Street, Holliswood, N.Y.
