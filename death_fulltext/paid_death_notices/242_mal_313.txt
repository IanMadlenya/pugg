
        SATZ--Marvin (Bud), 85, after a valiant battle against Melanoma on March 30. Adored and adoring husband of Rita for 64 years. Deeply loved and loving father of Richard, Tina, Ginnie, Andy and Kathleen. Very special grandfather of Rebekah, Jed and Virginia, Brian, Elizabeth, Meghan and Grace. Bud loved his life, his family, his friends at play and at work, and they loved him. Interment private. Donations may be made to American Skin Association, 346 Park Ave. S., NY 10010.
