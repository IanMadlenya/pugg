
        LONG-Jay Nicholas. Passed away on September 18, 1997. Beloved husband of Maria, cherished father of Angelique and her fiance, Hampton Peterson, and of Christopher and his wife Christianna and adoring grandfather of Nicholas and Alexander Long.
