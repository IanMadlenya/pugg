
        CARTER-Mary L. Age 96, of Brick, NJ, on March 5, 2001. She was employed as a secretary for Archdiocese of NY for 40 years until her retirement in 1969. Surviving are her brother William Carter of Brick, NJ. Relatives and friends are kindly invited to attend the funeral services from ''The Family Owned'' Carmona-Bolen Home for Funerals, 412 Main St., Toms River, NJ, on Monday at 9 AM, followed by a funeral liturgy at St. Joseph RC Church, Toms River, at 10 AM. Burial will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
