
        WHITWELL-Joseph E. Jr., long time leader of General Cigar Co., dies at the age of 82. Born in St. Paul, MN, he was educated at Northwestern University. As a Captain in the Army Air Corps, Mr. Whitwell was awarded the ''Air Medal'' as squadron commander in the battle of Kasserine Pass. Joining General Cigar Co. in 1948, he rose through the ranks and became President of the then parent company, Culbro Corporation. He retired in 1987 as President and Chief Operating Officer of Culbro. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Josephine Whitwell, 1 daughter, 1 son, 6 grandsons and 1 great granddaughter.
