
        GINSBURG-Sara. Died at home in New York City on May 24, 1997. Retired Assistant Professor in the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. Served there from 1954 until 1985 in the Departments of Biochemistry and Neurology. Born in St. Petersburg, Russia in 1908. Sister of the late Victoria Front. Received her Ph.D. in organic chemistry from the Univertsity of Berlin. At Columbia, was instrumental in designing numerous drugs, including one still used in overcoming poisoning caused by war gases and insecticides. Her body was given to Columbia for anatomical study. No funeral or memorial service. Any gifts in her memory may be made to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, Inc., 710 West 168th Street, Box F, New York, New York 10032.
