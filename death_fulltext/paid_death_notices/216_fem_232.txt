
        LASKY--Jerome M., on January 15, at 83. Cherished husband of Lila for 59 years. Devoted father of Beth, Ronni and her husband, Bill Metzger. Adoring ''Papa'' to Scott. Loving brother of Harvey. World War II officer, brilliant attorney, and former judge of the Nassau County District Court. We will all miss Jerry, a wise and caring ''gentle giant.'' Memorial gathering in the spring.
