
        SCHWARTZ - Lawrence, age 77. Beloved and cherished husband of Sandy. Adored father of Jill, David and Marc. Loving brother of Sidney, June and Harold. Died suddenly January 1, 1997 in Sun City Center, FL. A resident of Floral Park, NY for more than 44 years, he was a proud veteran of 39 months of overseas combat service in World War II. He was a teacher who loved his work, his students, many friends, in-laws, nieces and nephews and always, above all, his family. Services Sunday January 5, 1997, 12 noon at Parkside, 98-60 Queens Blvd.
