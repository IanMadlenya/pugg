
        GAY-William (Bill). On February 2, 2001. Loving husband of Elizabeth, son of the late Frank and Rose O'Brien Gay. He was born on July 12, 1904, in Hudson Falls, NY. He was a teacher and swimming instructor for the NYC Department of Education. He was also the swimming coach for the NYAC Saturday Morning Boys. Visiting hours 2-5 and 7-9, Monday, at Nagel Funeral Home, 352 East 87th St., NYC. Funeral mass Tuesday, 10 AM, at the Holy Name of Jesus Church, 96th St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, send donations in his memory to the Catholic Relief Services or a charity of your choice.
