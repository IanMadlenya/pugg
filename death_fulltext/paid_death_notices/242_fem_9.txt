
        ZLOTNIK--Rosalie. On February 28, 2007, Rosalie Zlotnik, formerly of Rockville Centre, died peacefully at home. She was the loving wife of 60 years to Meyer, devoted mother of Marc and wife Joan and Ellen and husband Matthew, and adored grandmother of Julie, Jessica, Sarah and Deborah. Rosalie was a beloved teacher, known for her warmth and smile. The funeral will be at Temple Emanuel, in Kensington, Maryland, on March 2, at 11:00 am. Donations may be made to the Jewish Federation or the American Diabetes Association.
