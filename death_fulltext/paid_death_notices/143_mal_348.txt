
        ELBIRT-Jack. State of Israel Bonds mourns the passing of a devoted leader, the longtime President of the Federation of Wolynian Jews of America. For over 32 years Jack ran successful State of Israel Bonds functions, raising millions of dollars in investment capital to help build Israel's economy. His dedication to Israel and the Jewish people inspired all who knew him. We extend our condolences to his wife, Laura, and the entire family. Gideon Patt, President Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board Irwin Hochberg, National Campaign Chairman
