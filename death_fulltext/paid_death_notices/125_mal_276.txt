
        TENNENBAUM-Felix. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Elmwood Country Club mourn the passing its esteemed member, Felix Tennenbaum. Felix was a low-key individual, who believed in dedicating himself to his business and to his devoted wife, Evelyn; these were his two true loves in life. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, Evelyn and to his family. Lawrence Itkin, President
