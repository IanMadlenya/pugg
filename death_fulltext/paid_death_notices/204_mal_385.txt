
        DEVINE--Joseph Dudley, 77, on January 28, 2004. Beloved father of Christina Devine of Tucson, AZ, Sheila Devine of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico and Joseph Leighton Devine of Amelia Island, FL. Devoted brother of the late Sheila Suarez of Litchfield, Ct. A U.S. Naval veteran of World War II Iwo Jima Campaign, member of Division 29 of the Ancient Order of Hibernians, Yale graduate of 1951 and UVA Law Class of 1954. The family will receive friends at the Chas. Peter Nagel Funeral Home, 352 E. 87 St, NYC on Friday and Saturday 2-5 and 7-9 and on Sunday from 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial Our Lady of Good Counsel, 230 E. 90 St on Monday, 11AM. Interment at Forest Lawn Cemetery, Buffalo, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory can be made to Cabrini Hospice, 227 E. 19 St. NYC.
