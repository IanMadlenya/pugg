
        PALSGROVE-James III. June 23, 1998. Beloved husband of Phyllis L. Palsgrove. Loving father of Lincoln Palsgrove IV. Devoted brother of Barbara C. Palsgrove. He was Music Director of St. Mary the Virgin Anglican Church and Director of Music at St. Thomas More Catholic Church. Mass of Christian Burial Monday, June 29, 2 PM, St. Ignatius Loyola Church, 980 Park Avenue at 84th Street.
