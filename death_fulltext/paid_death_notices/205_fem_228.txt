        WALDMAN -- Blanche. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Blanche Waldman, beloved wife of the late Lester Waldman and mother of Michael J. Waldman. Lester was the former executive director and board president of the New York Society for the Deaf, a beneficiary of UJAFederation. Blanche's son, Michael, is the current board president of NYSD. We extend our deepest sympathies to Michael and his wife, Rebecca, and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        WALDMAN -- Blanche. The officers & staff of the AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of our dear friend and longtime colleague, Blanche Waldman. For 50 years, Blanche served ADL in her position as Director of Special Gifts, and after her retirement in 1994 as a volunteer to our Development Division. Blanche and her late husband Lester, also a League professional, were committed to ensuring ADL's vitality. We will miss her sage advice and quick wit. We extend our sincere condolences to her son Michael and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser, National Chair Abraham H. Foxman National Director
