
        WEINSTEIN--Sylvia, of Queens, NY, died on October 13, 2004, at the age of 83. Beloved wife of the late Morris Weinstein, devoted mother of Mark H. Weinstein of Ardsley, NY, and Jane H. Gelman of Bethesda, MD, and mother-in-law of Fran Weinstein and Bob Gelman. Cherished grandmother of Lauren and Eric Weinstein, and David Gelman. Loving sister to Dorothy Bennett of Greenwich, CT, and Florence Caplan of Miami, FL. Known for her unwavering love of family and mastery of NYT crossword puzzles, Sylvia will be remembered with love always. A memorial service will be held on Friday, October 15, 9:45 AM, at Parkside Chapel, 98-60 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, NY, 718-896-9000. Burial will be held at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Lighthouse International (www.lighthouse.org).
