
        LIVSEY-Dail Cameron. 64. Sarasota, FL. Died July 21, 2002. Born October 6, 1937 in New York City. Dail grew up in Bedford, NY and was educated at Rippowam Cisqua, Miss Hall's School and Vassar College. An accomplished art historian, Dail served as a docent at The Art Museum of Ontario in Toronto, The Metropolitan Museum of Art and its branch, The Cloisters in New York, The Art Institute of Chicago and The Ringling Museum of Art in Sarasota. She is survived by her husband of 44 years, Brian, daughter Alexandra of San Francisco, son Philip, daughter-in-law Camille, grandchildren Allison and Philip, Jr. of Avon, CT and sister Elaine Hinkle of Naples, FL and her family. A Mass will be held on August 5 at 10:30 AM at St. Martha's Catholic Church, 200 North Orange (at Fruitville) Sarasota, FL. Memorial donations may be made to The Ringling Museum Docent Memorial Fund, 5401 Bayshore Rd, Sarasota, FL 34243.
