
        CHESTER - Lenore. The Boards of Directors and staff of Citizens Union & Citizens Union Foundation mourn the passing of Lenore Chester. She was associate director of the organization for many years, the founding editor of the award winning publication Searchlight on the City Council. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Ogden Lewis, Chair CU Robert Abrams, Pres. CUF Linda Stone Davidoff, Executive Director
        CHESTER-Lenore. Our former President, inspirational leader, civic conscience, devoted to family and friends. She will be missed. The League of Women Voters of the City of NY
