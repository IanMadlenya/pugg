
        GOTTSEGEN--Rita (Gillette), died suddenly on July 16, 2005, her 78th birthday, in Rye, NY. Survived by her loving husband of 54 years, Dr. Robert Gottsegen. Cherished mother of Dr. David Gottsegen (Shelly), Daniel Gottsegen (Margaret Jackson) and Jonathan Gottsegen (Mindy). Devoted grandmother of Jessica, Liza, Samuel, Benjamin, Kate and Gardner. Dear sister of Dr. Burton Gillette (Marcia). A chemist who taught science at the Brearley School in Manhattan for 20 years. She will always be remembered for her great sensitivity, curiosity about the world and her passion for people. Memorial service Wednesday, 2PM at Zion Memorial Chapel in Mamaroneck, 914-381-1809. Donations in her memory may be sent to Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Avenue, Montgomery, AL 36104 or The Anti-Defamation League of B'nai B'rith.
