
        GALLANT-Arthur. 74, of Harrison, NY, on November 25, 2000. The son of the late Earl and Essie Gallant. Beloved husband of Lois (nee Shapiro). Devoted father of Debra and Joel Binstok, Stephen and Melissa Gallant. Cherished grandfather of Danielle, Michael, Madison, Sarah and Emily. Loving brother of Elaine Roth and brothers-in-law Stanton Roth and Edward Shapiro. Arthur was an inspiration to all his friends and to those who worked with him. Services were held Monday. Donations to the Jewish Guild for the Blind. For information contact Riverside Memorial Chapel, 914-664-6800.
