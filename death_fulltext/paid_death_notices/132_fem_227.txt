
        HEIN-Barbara Betty. Of Thornwood, New York. On January 25, 1998. Beloved and devoted wife of Harold for fifty years. Cherished and loving mother of Richard, Robert, Steven and Loren and mother-in-law of Julia and Michelle. Adored and adoring grandmother of Emily. Her warmth, intelligence and incomparable courage will be remembered by all who knew her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street on Tuesday between 7 and 9 P.M. Services will be held there on Wednesday at 1:30 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the Dr. M. Heany Research Group, at The Sloan-Kettering Memorial Hospital.
