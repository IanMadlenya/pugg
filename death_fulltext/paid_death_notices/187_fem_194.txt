
        WINTER-Jessica. 81, passed away on Sunday, August 25, 2002, following a short illness. Beloved wife and best friend of Martin E. Winter. Devoted aunt of Marvin and Diane Bernstein, Philip and Beverly Sklover, William and Sharon Friedheim, William and Oda Friedheim, Drs. Lee and Margarita Winter and Randy Winter. Great-aunt of David and Susan Bernstein, Alanna, Samuel and Joseph Sklover, Lisa Blaudoin and Gregory Friedheim. Sister-in-law of Joan and Eugene Winter and Anne Friedheim. Loving godmother to David and Jennifer Kouvant. Lifelong friend of Vivian Kouvant, Sylvia and Austin Fried and Regina Gelford. She had a long and distinguished career on Wall Street and recently retired after 20 years from the firm Ehrenkrantz, King and Nussbaum. Funeral services Wednesday, August 28, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 81st. St. and Madison Ave. Donations in honor of Jessica may be made to Variety-The Children's Charity.
        WINTER-Jessica. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its member, Jessica Winter. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Samuel Wasserman, Pres.
