
        NOYES -- Carol Rothschild. The President and Board of Trustees of Vassar College note with deepest sorrow the passing of Carol Rothschild Noyes, Class of 1939, a loyal and devoted supporter of the College who served as a trustee, director of the Alumnae Association and the founding co-chair of the Friends of the Art Gallery. Three of her children attended Vassar: Allison Bradford of the Class of 1968, Deborah Bradford of the Class of 1972, and David Bradford of the Class of 1974. We extend our condolences to them and to the rest of the family. Frances D. Fergusson President Richard Van Demark Chair, Board of Trustees
