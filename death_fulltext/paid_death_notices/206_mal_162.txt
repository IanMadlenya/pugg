
        BELL--Harold ''Harry.'' Of Boca Raton, FL, and Fort Lee, NJ, age 73, died Saturday, March 13, 2004, at Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital in New York City. Harold is survived by his wife Sandra, son Allan, daughter-in-law Dale, granddaughter Brittany and grandson Brandon. Harold grew up in Everett, MA, and graduated from Bentley College. He was President of Harry Bell Sales Co., Inc., manufacturers representives in the hardware industry, for more than 30 years until his recent retirement. Service Monday, 2 pm, at The Sanctuary of Abraham & Sarah at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Donations in his memory may be made to Solomon Schechter Day School of Bergen County, 275 McKinley Avenue, New Millford, NJ 07646.
