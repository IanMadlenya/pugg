
        SCHUTZER-Seymour, M.D. The American Diabetes Association, New York State Affiliate, Inc., notes with sorrow the passing of Dr. Seymour Schutzer, a member of the New York City Chapter Board. Dr. Schutzer was devoted to caring for people with diabetes and spent time during his retirement volunteering with the Diabetes Information Program. He will be missed by all who knew him. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Margaret Spencer, Chair of the Board Harvey Katzeff, M.D., President of the Board Staff, NY Downstate Region
