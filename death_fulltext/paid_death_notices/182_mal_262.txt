
        SHERMAN-Murray, 92. Attorney, March 7, in Plantation, FL. In W.W. II Capt. Murray served in USAAF in Europe as an Intelligence Officer. He managed a realty corp. in NYC in '50s and '60s and moved to FL in '73 with wife Essie, who died in '77. Survived by brother, Sidney of Merritt Island, FL and two sisters, Toby Taylor of FL and Pearl Goldfarb of New Jersey. Services were held.
