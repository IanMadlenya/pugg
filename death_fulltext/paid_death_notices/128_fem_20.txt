
        GREENE-Lucy, died August 31 at the age of 77 after a brief illness. The family wishes to express their great sorrow at her passing. She is survived by her husband, Thomas LoMonaco, her two children, Joshua Greene and Deborah Bershatsky, her daughter-in-law Judy Greene, her son-in-law Charles Bershatsky, and four grandchildren, Rachel, Sarah, Lydia and Ezra. She will be very much missed by all of us.
