
        KATZ-Blanche Kassin. On Wednesday, November 21, suddenly at home in Brooklyn at age 93. Adored wife of the late Dr. Alexander A. Katz, mother of Vera Pavone of Brooklyn, Laura Strowe of Ann Arbor, MI and the late Paul Katz, grandmother of Chris and Joey Pavone and Anna Strowe. Hunter College, high school English teacher. She will be remembered for her wide-ranging interests, prodigious memory, political acumen, literary knowledge, and her ceaseless efforts to bring about world peace and social justice. A memorial gathering will be held on Saturday afternoon January 5th from 2:00 to 5:00 PM at Vera's home.
