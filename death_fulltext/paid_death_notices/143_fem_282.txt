
        KAUFMAN-Beryl Berger. Born February 27, 1902. Died December 29, 1998. Adored wife of the late Emanuel Kaufman. Beloved mother and mother-in-law of Jay and Anita. Cherished grandmother of Nina, Laurance and John. Beryl was a woman of great strength and determination who delighted in life. She will be sorely missed and fondly remembered. Graveside services will be private.
