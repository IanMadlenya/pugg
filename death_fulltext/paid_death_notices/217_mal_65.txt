
        LAVERY--Dr. Gerard B. On January 21, 2005. Professor of Classics for 35 years at the College of the Holy Cross in Worcester, MA. Gerry was a graduate of Regis High School in New York and received his Master's and Ph.D from Fordham University. Beloved brother of Mary Lavery and the late Alice Lavery and Peggy Bischoff. Loving uncle and great uncle to many. Cremation was private. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated on Sunday, February 20, 2005 at 11 A.M. at Blessed Sacrament Church, 551 Pleasant Street, Worcester, MA.
