
        IRITANI-Chika A., of New York City and Saugerties, NY on April 15, 1997. Beloved daughter of Amy and the late James Iritani of Rye Brook, NY and devoted sister and sister-in-law of James and Dottie Iritani of Markham, Virginia. She was Assistant Manager, Grants Administration and Operations for The Ford Foundation. Family and friends will celebrate her life at a memorial service at the United Nations Chapel, 777 UN Plaza on April 28, 1997 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her name to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021 or The Audubon Society, 700 Broadway, New York, NY 10003.
