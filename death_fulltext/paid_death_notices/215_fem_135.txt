
        BEGLEY--Frances. On December 18, 2004, two days short of her 94th birthday. Widow of Dr. Edward D. Begley. Beloved mother of Louis Begley, grandmother of Peter, Adam and Amey, and greatgrandmother of Nicholas, Julia and Jacob. Died in her sleep at her home. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St., Tuesday, December 21, 11:30 am. Burial private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the 92nd Street YMHA.
