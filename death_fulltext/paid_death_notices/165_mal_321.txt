
        RICH-Martin, 95. Conductor. Died October 23, 2000 peacefully after massive stroke at Roosevelt Hospital. Cherished husband of Maria. Beloved father of Monica Sturm, father-in-law of David and grandfather of Edward and Brendan, brother of Faya Rose and Harriet Appleman. Services private. A Memorial Service will be announced at a later date.
        RICH-Martin. Beloved brother to Faya and Harriet, uncle to Vicki, great uncle to Leslie and Stacy. A great musician who through his care and concern saved his family from the Holocaust. We shall sorely miss you.
