
        MEROLLA-Robert. The staff of the New York Road Runners Club mourn the passing of our esteemed and beloved colleague, Bob Merolla, aka ''Papa Grunt.'' He was a key member of the organizing committee of the New York City Marathon for 18 years. His gentle ways and humor will be greatly missed. He will never be forgotten. Wake will be held at Perazzo Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., NYC, on Monday, March 17 2-5pm and 7-9:30pm. Funeral services Tuesday, March 18, 9:30am at Our Lady of Pompeii Church, 25 Carmine Street, NYC.
