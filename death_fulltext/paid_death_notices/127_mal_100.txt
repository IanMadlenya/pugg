
        SHEA-Peter Lane, of Locust Valley, LI, died suddenly and peacefully at his home in Northeast Harbor, ME, on August 9th. He was 64. He is survived by his wife, Nancy Sage Shea, his sisters, Edwina Millington and Betty Rouss, and his four sons, Chris, Andy, Ed and Jamie Shea. He is the grandfather of Phelan, Nicholas, Elyssa, Brody, Spencer, Reid and Natalie Shea. A private family service will be held on Tuesday. A memorial service will be held later in September.
