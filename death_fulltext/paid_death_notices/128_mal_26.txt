
        MEYER-Victor B., Ph.D., 76, organic chemist, on August 31st. We have lost a brilliant mind tragically destroyed by Alzheimer's disease. In profound sorrow: his beloved wife of fifty years, Evelyn; cherished sons, Dan and Jonathan; devoted sister and brother-in-law, Gloria and Benjamin Rothberg. His passion for learning lives. Memorial service at a later date. Plant a perennial in his memory.
