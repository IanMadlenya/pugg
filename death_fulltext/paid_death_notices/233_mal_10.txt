
        GRUENBERGER--Leslie. On May 30, 2006. Beloved husband for over 70 years of Olga, deceased October 4, 2005; father of Peter and Joyce; grandfather of Karen, Richard, Laurie, Lynn and Jon; great grandfather of Brian, Ethan, Jared, Kyra, Maya and Olivia; and brother of Shani and Aranka. He was a gentle lion of a man who shouldered the burdens of generations with a ready quip and guided his family with a keen light. We shall miss you Dad. Funeral service and interment will be held June 1 at New Montefiore Cemetery, L.I. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Mount Sinai Hospital, Visiting Doctors Program, 1 Gustave Levy Pl., NY, NY 10029.
