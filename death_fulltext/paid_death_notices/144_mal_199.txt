
        KOCHER-Eric passed away peacefully at home at age 86 on January 2, 1999. Beloved husband of Margaret Helburn Kocher, devoted and gentle father of Eric Glenn of Atlanta, GA, Terry of Anchorage, AL, Christopher of San Francisco, CA and Debra of Tenafly, NJ. Grandfather to four grandsons and three granddaughters. A memorial service is planned for the spring. Donations may be made in his name to The Eugene O'Neill Center, New York.
