
        STEINER--Fred, died peacefully in his sleep on July 4th at the age of 86. Fred will be remembered by his family and his many friends and associates for his tireless dedication to his business, his generosity and unceasing effort in his charitable works and his love and devotion to family. His grandchildren were his pride and joy. He is survived by his wife Thelma, daughter Mimi Cohen, son-in-law David Cohen and grandsons Scott and Russell.
