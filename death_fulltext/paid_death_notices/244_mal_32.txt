
        TODMAN--Howard F., television producer of famed game show production company Goodson - Todman Produc tions, died on May 2, 2007. Beloved father, grandfather and great-grandfather. Always a true gentleman who is survived by his four daughters, Raleigh Weiss Todman and Myka Weiss Todman of New York, Leslee Todman of California and Kathie Poses of Florida.
