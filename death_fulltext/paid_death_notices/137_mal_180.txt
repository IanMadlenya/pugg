
        CAREY-Edwin J. Age 74 of Wyckoff died Wednesday, June 17, 1998 at New York University Hospital. Born in New York he was a resident of Wyckoff since 1960. He attended Manhattan College from 1941-1943 and received an LL.B in 1951 from St. John's University Law School. He was admitted to practice before the Supreme Court of the U.S., the U.S. District Courts for the Southern and Eastern districts of N.Y. and the N.Y. State Court of Appeals. Mr. Carey was a partner at the law firm of Hill, Rivkins and Hayden LLP before his retirement in 1988. He was a member of the Maritime Law Association of the U.S. and served as President from 1972-1974. He was also a Titular Member of the Comite Maritime International. He was an avid golfer and a member of Arcola Country Club, Paramus. A parishioner of St. Elizabeth R.C. Church, where he served as a former Lector and coach of the Boy's CYO Basketball Team. He was a WWII Army veteran. Surviving are his wife Marian (nee Burke) Carey, a daughter, Brianne Wright of Allendale, a son, Christopher of New Orleans. Also surviving are four grandchildren, Christopher, Brigid, Colleen, and Katherine. Funeral services Saturday 10:15AM from the Vander Plaat Funeral Home 257 Godwin Ave, Wyckoff, NJ. Funeral Mass 11AM St. Elizabeth's RC Church 700 Wyckoff Ave, Wyckoff, NJ. Interment Maryrest Cemetery, Mahwah.
