
        KAUFMAN-Rose. The officers, Board of Trustees, and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services note with deep sorrow the lost of Rose Kaufman, beloved mother of Rabbi Yechiel Kaufman, the esteemed associate director of our Division for Developmental Disabilities. We extend our sincere condolences to Rabbi Kaufman and his entire family. Seymour R. Askin, Jr., President Joseph S. Kaplan President-Elect Fredric W. Yerman Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President Paul Levine, Associate Executive Vice President
