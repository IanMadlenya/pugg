
        DREXLER--Leonard A. Long time resident of Greenwich Village, died on April 21, 2004, following an extended illness. Born August 26, 1917 in Brooklyn, New York, he was a graduate of Harvard Law School and practiced law in New York City for fifty years. Interment was at Beth David Cemetery on Sunday April 25, 2004. Surviving are his brother, Gene Drexler, of Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, two nieces, Frances and Joanne, and their children, and a newphew, Robert, & his children.
