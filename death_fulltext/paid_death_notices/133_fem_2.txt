
        CROSS-Patricia T. Ann Arbor, Michigan. Age 77, died January 26, 11:45 P.M. at home. A resident of Ann Arbor since 1949. She was born May 21, 1920 in New York City, the daughter of Harold and Grace G. Townsend. She was a graduate of The Brearley School and Vassar College. On March 22, 1946 she married Dr. Thomas N. Cross. He pre-deceased her in December, 1996. She is survived by their children John (Jean) Cross, Chappaqua, N.Y.; Katherine C. Reece, Kennebunkport, ME; Peter R. Cross, Ann Arbor, MI; three grandchildren; and a sister, Nancy T. Haynes, Naples, FL. Cremation has taken place. In lieu of a formal service, contributions may be made to Therapy Dogs, Int'l and Ann Arbor Hospice.
