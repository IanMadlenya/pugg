
        BAER-Gertrude (Verina). Originally from Kentucky. Accomplished and talented pianist. Performed at Manhattan Savings Banks and verious hotels and supper clubs. Survived by a niece, nephew and dear friends. Leaves a legacy of her creative works in the archives of the University of Kentucky, Louisville, KY. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., today 2-5, 7-9 PM. Funeral service Tuesday 11:30 AM. Interment to follow Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
