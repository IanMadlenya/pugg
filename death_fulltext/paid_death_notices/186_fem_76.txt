
        FUNSTON-Elizabeth Kennedy. Widow of G. Keith Funston, former President of Trinity College of Hartford, CT., and The New York Stock Exchange, died at Hope Hospice in Fort Meyers, FL, on June 15, 2002. Betty Funston, the daughter of John Joseph & Marguerite Scott Kennedy, of Pittsburgh, PA., was born on September 12, 1912. After being educated at The Westover School, in Middlebury, CT, and Brilliamont, Lausanne, Switzerland, she made her debut in Pittsburgh, and was married to Keith Funston in 1939. A dedicated horticulturalist, she was a member of The Greenwich Garden Club, and served on The Board of The Pirate Playhouse Theater on Sanibel Island, FL. A devoted wife, mother, and grandmother, her survivors are a son, G. Keith Funston, Jr., of Sudbury, MA, two daughters, Marguerite Funston Thatcher, of Greenwich, CT, and Gail Funston Wasson, of Lebanon, NH, and eight grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at Christ Church in Greenwich, CT. on Friday, September 6, 2002, at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Salvation Army, HealthPark Medical Center, or Hope Hospice, of Fort Meyers, FL.
