
        RODENKIRCHEN-Eleanora V., 77, formerly of Nutley, New Jersey and Warrington, PA. Died at home February 28. She was the widow of Robert P. Rodenkirchen, ex-Governor of The New York Athletic Club. She is survived by four children, ten grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. The family will recieve friends on Saturday, March 8, at 10 AM, at The Union Funeral Home, 1600 Stuyvesant Avenue, Union, NJ. Entombment immediately following at Hollywood Memorial Park, 1500 Stuyvesant Avenue, Union, NJ. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to The Robert P. Rodenkirchen Track and Field Memorial Fund, C/O Laurine Kohanik, 27 Cypress Road, Burlington, N.J. 08016.
