
        KATZ--Rosalie. On March 7, 2005 at age 62. Our mother was and always will be our rock and our anchor. She filled our lives with love and laughter. She embodied the highest standards of compassion, generosity, and integrity. Her courage, wisdom, and insight will continue to guide our lives and the generations to come. She was the loving wife of the late Daniel; cherished mother of Wendy Katz, Meredith Gantcher, and Gary Katz; adored grandmother of Danielle Gantscher and Yaira, Gidon, and Matan Katz. Services Thursday, March 10, 11 A.M. at Temple Israel of Great Neck, 108 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, NY. Contributions in her memory should be directed to The Rosalie Katz Internship for Leadership at the Jewish Theological Seminary or to The National Marrow Donor Program, New York.
        KATZ--Rosalie. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and former Vice President and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Howard A. Stecker, Sr. Rabbi Sidney Freedman, President
