
        BEDERSON-William. Of Lake Worth, Florida, passed away on Tuesday, June 1, 1999. Originally from New York, he was a longtime resident of The Fountains of Lake Worth and Boca Lago in Boca Raton, FL. He is survived by and will be fondly remembered by his dear companion Florence Dornfeld; stepsons Sandy and Steven Lipson; many grandchildren, great-grandchildren & nieces. He will be laid to rest beside his late wife, Anita Lipson Bederson, Wednesday, June 2, 1999 at Mt. Nebo Cemetery, Miami, FL.
