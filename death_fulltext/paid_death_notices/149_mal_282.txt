
        LYNN-Marshall. On June 16, 1999. Beloved father of Vanessa, son of Verda, husband of Sylvia, brother of Sandra. Served our nation valiantly in Vietnam. Attended Duke University and Northern Valley High School, Demarest, NJ, where in 1960 he was a member of the mile relay team that won the Penn Relays.
