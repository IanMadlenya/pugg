
        MARSHALL-John C. Of Bronxville, NY, passed away peacefully on February 8, 2003. He attended the College of William and Mary and was a graduate of Swarthmore College Class of 1948. He spent most of his career at American International Group, retiring as a Managing Director in their Global Investment Group. Beloved husband of Barbara Dexter. Devoted father of Candace D., John C. Jr., and James Steuart. Cherished grandfather of Jack, Page, Sarah and Katie. He was a Member of the Reformed Church in Bronxville and a longtime member of the Shenorock Shore Club in Rye, NY. An avid sailor and music enthusiast, he was a wonderful, kind and honorable man, loved by all who knew him. We love you, Dad, and will miss you always. A private memorial service for the immediate family will be held at a later date.
