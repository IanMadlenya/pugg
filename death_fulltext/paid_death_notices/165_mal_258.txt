
        EDLICH-Theodore, Jr. M.D. The Daughters of Mary and the medical staff of Saint Clare's Hospital and Health Center mourn the loss of our esteemed collegue and friend who served our institution for many years. Dr. Edlich was a dedicated physician who always showed sincere concern for his patients and for St. Clare's Hospital and Health Center. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his family. Theodore Flogaites M.D, President, Medical Staff Paul Marino, Medical Director James A. Rutherford, Pres/CEO St. Clare's Hospital and Health Center
