
        SWANANDER-Gary A. The Board of Governors, the membership and staff of the Atlantic Golf Club are saddened by the passing of our fellow member and past Board Member, Gary Swanander. Gary, a charter member of the Club, served on the Board enthusiastically for five years. He spent many days at the Club and enjoyed the game to its fullest. We will miss him and cherish his memory. We offer our sincere condolences to his children, Andrea, Gary Jr., Andrew and Michael.
