
        PITNEY--Duncan. On December 28 at home. An artist and raconteur. Graduate of Aiken Preparatory School, Milton Academy and Princeton '43. He served in the Army Air Corps during World War II, flying aviation gas from India to China. He was a member of the River, Brook and Somerset Hills Country Clubs. He served on the NJ Water Policy & Supply Council and was State Treasurer for the NJ Republican Party. He leaves his wife of 42 years, Kathleen Troast, sons John and Christopher, stepsons Trygve and Erik Sletteland, stepdaughters Sigrid Franzblau, Karen Jackson and Wendy Macbeth. A daughter, Jenifer Lyle, predeceased him. At his request there will be no service.
