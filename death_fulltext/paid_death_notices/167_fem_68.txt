
        PFEFFER-Hope Turkel, formerly of New York City and Scarsdale, NY, Norwalk and Fairfield, CT, passed away in Palm Beach, FL on December 3rd. She was retired from Saks Fifth Avenue, Palm Beach, and was previously employed at Razook's in Southport and Martha West in New Canaan. She was the loving wife of the late Leo Pfeffer, and was also predeceased by her beloved seven siblings, including Sonya T. Bernen, who passed away on November 14. She leaves two adoring sons and their wives, Ronald A. and Janet M. Pfeffer, of Southington, CT and Paul I. Pfeffer and Nyomi Guberman-Pfeffer of Stratford, CT, five grandchildren, Carolyn, Allison, and Joseph Pfeffer of Southington, Jason and Matthew Guberman-Pfeffer of Stratford, one sister-in-law, Mildred Turkel of Raleigh, NC, and dear nieces, nephews, and cousins, including Sonya Stall of Palm Beach and Saratoga Springs, NY. There will be a Memorial Service on Sunday, Dec. 10th at 10:00 A.M. at the Abraham L. Green Funeral Home, 88 Beach Road, Fairfield, CT. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, Inc., 5300 East Ave., West Palm Beach, FL. 33407.
