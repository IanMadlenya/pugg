
        COWAN-Judith Amster. 61, beloved wife of Lawrence Cowan and wonderful mother to Andrew and Jennifer, died at home in Los Angeles on July 18, 1997. Born in Brooklyn to Philip and Mildred Loeb Amster, Judy graduated from Vassar College in 1956. Judy is also survived by her dear cousins Jane, Beth and Ruth. Services will be held at 12 noon, today, July 20, at Hillside Memorial Park in Los Angeles. Contributions may be made to the Gilda Radner Ovarian Cancer Detection Program at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, or to Vassar.
