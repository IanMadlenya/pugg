
        GOROVOY - Hyman, of Brooklyn, NY on Sunday April 27, 2003. As a very young child Hy immigrated from Poland with his family to America and has lived in Brooklyn ever since. An Army Veteran and Philanthropist along with his brothers, he built many apartment complexes throughout Northern, NJ and until his recent retirement continued to own and operate several of them. Beloved husband of Annette for 63 years. Devoted father of Murray. Loving grandfather of Ivy Gantert and Eric Gorovoy, and great-grandfather of Benjamin and Samuel Gantert. Services today 11:30am at Parkside Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn.
