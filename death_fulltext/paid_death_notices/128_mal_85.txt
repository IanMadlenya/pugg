
        SHERWIN-Irving. Died Sept 2. A renaissance man, he was a believer in the quest for loving, learning and living. Always in pursuit of personal and relational excellence. He found beauty and joy in all of life. He realized a life full of experiences only a few could imagine. A traveler in both time and space, he touched those he met with insight and enlightenment. His accomplishments were many, he was a Mensa, a philanthropist, a Mason and a successful businessman. He owned the world renowned Sheru. His wisdom and love will be greatly missed by those who knew him and those who will never have the opportunity to meet him. He is survived by his children Marny and Joe Nedlin, and Steven and Dana Sherwin; his grandchildren Richard Nedlin, and Brian and Kevin Sherwin; and companion Barthy.
