
        CAMERON-Hope.(Bulkeley) Widow of Billy Friedberg, veteran actor of stage, film and TV, died November 18 at the age of 78, after a heroic fight against cancer. She played with Lee. J. Cobb, in ''Death of a Salesman'', on Broadaway for it's 96 week run, and continued acting up to this year in a performance at the Actors Studio. Survived by her family, friends, and her family of 500 fellow members of the Actors Studio. Memorial service at the Actors Studio, 432 West 44th St., on Friday, December 4th at 2PM.
