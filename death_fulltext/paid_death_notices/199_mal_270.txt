
        GARTLAND--John J. The trustees, faculty, staff and students of The Culinary Institute of America (CIA) pay tribute to the life and philanthropy of John J. Gartland, Honorary Trustee. As senior partner at Corbally, Gartland & Rappleyea, Mr. Gartland was instrumental in the college's move to Hyde Park in 1972. As president of the James J. McCann Foundation, he supported the CIA, funding student facilities and scholarships. Jack Gartland was a leader who truly impacted the quality of life in the Hudson Valley. Our deepest sympathies are extended to Mr. Gartland's family, friends and colleagues.
