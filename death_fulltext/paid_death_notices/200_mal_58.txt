
        TOPAL--Donald on September 3, 2003. Loving husband and companion of fifty-three years to Lois. Devoted father of Robert and the late Laurie. Father-in-law of Laurie, John and Atsuko. A proud grandfather of Jesse and Dylan, Ashley and Jeffrey, and Thomas and Christina. Two brothers and their loving wives also survive. Loyal friend of many for many years. He will be missed by everyone who knew and loved him. Funeral service Sunday 11 AM at Schwartz Brothers Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. Interment at the Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, New York.
