
        LAVIN-Nick (Norman). After a courageous battle with pancreatic cancer, Nick passed away peacefully on February 27, 2002 at his home in Larchmont, NY. Beloved husband of Monica, devoted father of Sondra and David, and loving brother of Michael. Contributions in Nick's memory may be made to the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research, 1111 Stewart Avenue, Bethpage, New York 11714. A Funeral Service will be held on Sunday 1:15 P.M. at John J. Fox Funeral Home in Larchmont, New York.
