
        SABETTA-Victoria, Devoted wife of the late John A. Sabetta. Beloved mother of Lorelei Jackson and John Sabetta. Cherished grandmother of Kate And Chris Jackson and Giancarlo Sabetta. Died April 3, 2000 in West Reading PA. Born in 1918 in W. VA. She lived for more than 50 years in Bensonhurst, Brooklyn until relocating to PA. in 1992. She is also survived by a sister, Lena King of Danbury, CT., and a brother Frank Pobega of Morgantown, W. VA. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday, April 5 at 10:15AM at St. Rosalia's Catholic Church, 14 Avenue and 63 Street, Brooklyn. Friends and family will be received immediately before the service on Wednesday, April 5 from 8AM to 10AM at the Aievoli Funeral Home, 1275 65th Street, Brooklyn. Her boundless generosity and love of family and friends will be remembered always by those who loved her.
