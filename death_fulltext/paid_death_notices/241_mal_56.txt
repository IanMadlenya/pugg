
        SOMMER--Robert Charles, born Bronx, NY, June 24, 1926, died New York, NY, January 30, 2007. Bob was a notorious Upper West Side curmudgeon who graduated from Townsend Harris High School, CUNY (BS) and the Henry Krumb School of Mines at Columbia University (MS). He served in the US Army during WWII and was a scout during the Battle of the Bulge. An iconoclastic political thinker, Bob was the last of the Stalinists West of Amsterdam Avenue and an ardent supporter of WBAI. He is survived by his wife of 38 years, Susan (Suki) Thiemann Sommer, and his brother Alfred.
