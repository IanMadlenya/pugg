
        MILLER-Lindley G., President of Miller-Davison & Co. of Locust Valley and retired Village Justice of Upper Brookville, died Saturday, March 16, 2002, after a brief illness. Beloved husband of the former Patricia Cromwell and devoted father of Barbara C. Miller, Alexandra M. Cooper, Patricia R. Miller, Carley M. Sullivan and L. Garrison Miller Jr. Father-in-law of A. Gary Sullivan and Carrie Barr Miller. Proud grandfather of Grant Cooper, Kimberly Spallone, Courtney Sullivan, Pepperrell Sullivan and Charles Miller. Also, survived by brothers J. Norris Miller, Edward M. Miller and sister Anne Miller Mason. Predeceased by his sister Jane Benziger and brother William W. Miller. Judge Miller graduated from Princeton University in 1943 and the University of Virginia Law School in 1949. During W.W. II, he served as a flight engineer in the United States Air Force on a B-24 in the 451st Bomber Group. He flew 39 missions, earning a D.F.C. Air Medal, three Clusters, a Purple Heart and the Presidential Citation. Judge Miller was elected Village Justice of Upper Brookville, New York in 1958, a position he held until he retired in 2001. A memorial service is scheduled for 10:30 AM on Friday, March 22nd at the St. Dominic's RC Church in Oyster Bay, New York. In lieu of flowers, a donation in his memory would be greatly appreciated to the North Shore University Hospital at Glen Cove, C/O the Emergency Department Campaign or to the Upper Brookville Village Court.
