
        KASNER--Robert, 53, Circulation Director and a S.V.P. for Village Voice Media, died March 26 at NYU Hospital. The cause of death was complications from liver cancer. He lived in Brooklyn with his wife, Patricia, and is survived by his son, parents and siblings. He was an affable man who enjoyed the written word and a good cigar. He was a wonderful husband and father who lived life to the fullest and will be remembered for his wit and kind smile. A memorial service will be held 11am Friday, April 1, at Christ Church, 326 Clinton St., Brooklyn, NY. Donations can be made to Community Funds, Inc.--The Robert A. Kasner Fund. Send to: 24 Warren Pl., Brooklyn, NY 11201.
