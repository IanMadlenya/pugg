
        MILES-Martha H., ''Mattie''. Died peacefully on March 5, 2002 after a brief illness. Deeply mourned by her siblings, Hildegard Lynn, Werner Maas, Dr. Rudolf Maas, their wives, as aunt by her nephews and nieces, and as sister-inlaw by Dr. Peter Lynn, and as stepmother by Asherah Cinnamon & her family. Mattie was formerly Head Dietician at Sloan-Kettering Hospital. She loved to sculpt and paint, & had a great zest for living. We ask that you remember her with the love she radiated.
