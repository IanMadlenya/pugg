
        STERNAU--Phyllis M. (nee Mayer), died peacefully on June 12, 2004 at her home in Maryland, at the age of 93. Now she is with her adored husband, Howard, and much loved son, Philip, both of whom pre-deceased her. Phyllis is survived by her beloved daughter Sally S. Shubin, son-in-law Elliot Shubin, nephew Henry P. Baer, and dear friends, Barbara, Alfredo and Sarah - Elizabeth Fonts. A memorial service will be held at Collington Continuing Life Care Community, Mitchellville, MD, on Tuesday June 29, 2004 at 11am. In lieu of flowers, please send a donation to The Adoption & Children's Services Program of Family Services of Westchester, 1 Gateway Plaza, Portchester, NY 10573 or Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Avenue East, Rye, NY 10580.
