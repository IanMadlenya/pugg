
        SINGER-Monroe Schuyler, 72. A lifelong New Yorker, Monty graduated from Harvard College in 1947 and Yale Law School in 1950. He began his career at Shearman & Sterling, became a partner at Berlack, Israels & Liberman, and then spent over thirty years as Assistant Secretary and Vice President of Interpublic Group of Companies. He was a founder and vice president of the West Brooklyn Independent Democrats. An indefatigable traveler and railroad buff, he rode trains on every continent. He was the beloved father of Andrew Benjamin and former husband of Ann Singer. There will be a memorial gathering of friends and relatives on Sunday, November 21.
