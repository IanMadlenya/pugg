
        RADDING-George S. On August 23rd, 1999, at the age of 83. He loved and was devoted to his wife of fifty-eight years, Jean, his sons, Ronald and Rory, his daughters-inlaw, Jin Suk and Nina, his grandchildren, Ryan and Christopher, and his art. Witty, charming, young in spirit, and a friend to all. A true gentleman. A good man.
