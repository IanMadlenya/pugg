
        GILLEN-Rev. Francis W. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the parochial vicar of Our Lady of Guadalupe Church, 7201 15th Ave., Brooklyn, November 9. Reposing in Our Lady of Guadalupe Chapel, Tuesday, Nov. 11, 2-5 and 7-9:30 P.M. and Wednesday, November 12, 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Wednesday, Nov. 12 at 7:30pm. in Our Lady of Guadalupe Church. Funeral Mass Thursday, November 13 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
