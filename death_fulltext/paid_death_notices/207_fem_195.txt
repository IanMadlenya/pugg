
        SELTER--Helen. Of Suffern, NY, formerly of Long Beach, NY, passed away on April 24, 2004, at age 87. Beloved wife of the late Isidore. Devoted mother of Eileen and Paul Josephart and Joel and Beth Selter. Adored grandmother of Jill and Adam Josephart, Yael, Eitan, Aliza, Isaac and Isabelle Selter. Cherished sister of Ralphine Kresky. A feisty, loveable woman whose greatest joy and devotion were to her children, grandchildren, family and friends. A true matriarch, she will be missed terribly and remembered always. Services Monday, 9 am, at Hellman's, 15 State St, Spring Valley.
