
        MAGOWAN-Stephen Charles. On December 20, 2001, from complications of kidney cancer. Born in New York City on May 7, 1944, the fourth son of Robert Anderson Magowan and Doris Merrill Magowan. Educated at St. Bernard's School, St. Mark's School and Yale. Playwright, actor, pianist, Stephen Magowan was gifted with a unique sense of humor, personal fortitude, and an abundant capacity for enduring friendship. He is survived by his brothers Robin, Merrill, Peter and Mark, and his friend Antonio Jorge da Silva. Memorial contributions may be made to the Rogosin Institute, 505 East 70th St, NY, NY 10021, attn: Dr. John Wang.
