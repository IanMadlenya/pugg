
        READ-David Haxton Carswell, D.D. Senior Minister of Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church from 1956 until his retirement December 31, 1989, and Pastor Emeritus until his death January 7, 2001. The death of the Rev. Dr. David H.C. Read is mourned by the members, friends, officers and staff of the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, who extend their deepest sympathies to David's wife Pat Gilbert-Read and their son Rory. A memorial service of Witness to the Resurrection and Thanksgiving for His Life and Ministry will be held Tuesday afternoon, January 23, at 5:00 PM in the Church Sanctuary located at Madison Avenue and 73rd Street, New York City. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the David H.C. Read Preacher/Scholar Fund or the David H.C. Read Memorial Chapel Fund, both of Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue, NYC 10021.
