
        HAUSBERG--Dr. Jack, 89 of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Scarsdale, NY, passed away on June 11, 2004. Survived by his devoted wife, Sylvia; his loving children, Randi (Michael), Patrice (Blaine), Jill (David), and Jimmy (Linda); step-children, Steve, Margery, and Wendy (Neal); his 15 grandchildren; three greatgrandchildren and his sister, Ida Theobald. Services will be Monday, June 14, 2004, at 11 AM, at Westchester Reform Temple in Scarsdale, NY.
