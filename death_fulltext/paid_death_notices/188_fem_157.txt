
        LORENZ-Barbara S., 71. Died Tuesday, September 17, in the hospital, near her summer home in Maine. She was a life long resident in Manhattan. She is survived by her only child, Anne E. Lorenz. Her sister Eleanor S. Graf, & her brother-in-law Karl; also by her two nieces, Angelia, Kathleen, and her nephew and godson, Frederick B. Graf. According to her wishes, no funeral service will be held.
