
        FIPPINGER - Grace J. passed away peacefully on Monday, January 6 after a prolonged illness. Daughter of the late Fred and Johanna Fippinger, Grace is survived by her sister, Irene Bastiano and many friends. A resident of Woodbury, NY, graduate of Hicksville High School and St. Lawrence University, Grace retired from NYNEX as Vice President, Secretary and Treasurer after 42 years of service. She had served as a director of Connecitcut Mutual Life Insurance and Paramount Communications Inc. and continued to serve on the boards of Pfizer Inc. and The Bear Steams Companies, Inc. Calling hours are on Friday, January 10 from 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Henry J. Stock Funeral Home, Inc. located at 132 Newbridge Road, Hicksville, NY. Mass of Christian Burial and Memorial Serve will be doncuted on Saturday, January 11 at 11:15 AM at Holy Name of Jesus RCC. In lieu of flowers, kindly make donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
