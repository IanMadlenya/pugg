
        McGUIRK-Rev. Raymond A. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicar of Holy Innocents Church, 279 East 17 St, Brooklyn, on November 29. Reposing in McLaughlin Funeral Home, 9620 3rd Ave, Brooklyn, on Friday, December 1 from 2-5 PM. Mass of Transferral in Holy Innocents Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass on Saturday December 2, 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of The Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
