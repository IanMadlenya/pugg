
        UPTON--John Davis (Jack), of Southport, CT died January 15, 2005. Husband of both the late Kate Hurd Rennell, the great-great-granddaughter of P.T. Barnum & Eleanor Bingham Walker. Survived by two daughters, Mary D. Upton of Bristol, RI, and Elinor Biggs and husband Jim of Greens Farms, CT, three step-children, six step-grandchildren, and four step-greatgrandchildren. He graduated from the Hotchkiss School and Yale University, served in the US Army as a Captain during World War II in Italy and Africa, and was awarded the Bronze Star Medal with Oak Leaf Cluster. Mr. Upton spent his advertising career with N.W. Ayer, retiring as Vice Chairman in 1972. For service information and memorial contributions contact the Spear Funeral Home, Fairfield, CT 203-259-0824.
