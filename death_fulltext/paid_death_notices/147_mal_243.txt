
        STALLINGS-Roger W., 87, died Sunday, April 25, 1999 at the Teresian House in Albany. He was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri and was a longtime resident of Rye, NY. A graduate of St. Louis University with honors, he served as a Lieutenant in the Navy during World War II. He continued to serve in the United States Naval Reserve. After more than 40 years of service, he retired as a financial executive with Mobil Oil Corportation. He was also a member of the National Association of Accountants. Roger was a life member of the Masonic Temple of Webster Groves, MO. and a member of the Coveleigh Club in Rye. He enjoyed golf, badminton, tennis and racquetball. He is survived by his wife, Helen Stallings; his daughter Pamela R. Brondo and son-in-law Edwin T. Brondo of Albany; two grandchildren, Kristen L. Brondo of NYC and Edwin T. Brondo Jr. of Albany. He was predeceased by his brother Donald Stallings and his sister Lucille Stallings. Funeral services will be private. There will be no calling hours. Information: New Comer-Cannon Family Funeral Home 343 New Karner Road, Albany, New York.
