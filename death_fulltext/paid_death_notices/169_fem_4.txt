
        KASS-Elaine G., M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our long-standing colleague. Dr. Kass began her affiliation with Mount Sinai in 1959, when she began her residency in the Department of Pediatrics. She was a highly respected teacher and clinician and will be remembered as a dedicated practitioner. We extend our sincere condolences to her family. Anthony Squire, M.D., President Vicki Lopachin, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
        KASS-MESNICK-Elaine G., M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni express profound sorrow at the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend. She will be missed. Our deepest condolences are extented to her family. Avi Barbasch, M.D., President Jeffrey S. Freed, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria L. Padilla, M.D., Secretary William G. Stein, M.D., Treasurer
