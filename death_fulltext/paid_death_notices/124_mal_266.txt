
        SHERIN-Arthur H., Jr. of Hopewell Junction, died May 22, 1997. He was a retired advertising sales executive for CBS Publishing and a former elder of the Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church. Beloved husband of Flora DeBenedetto Sherin and father of Suzanne Lopiano, Deborah Pell and Bonnie Kollmann. For information call McHoul Funeral Home, Rte. 82, Hopewell Junction, New York.
