
        PASS-Sheldon, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Sheldon Pass. Dr. Pass was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Ophthalmology serving as the Attending in Charge for many years. He will be sorely missed by all his colleagues, friends and patients. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Warren Wexelman, M.D., Pres. Jacob Shani, M.D., Pres. Elect David Feldman, M.D. Secretary Salvatore DeGliuomini, M.D., Treasurer
