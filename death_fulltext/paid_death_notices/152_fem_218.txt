        KOVAK-Ellen. The staff of Kovak-Likly Communications is deeply saddened by the loss of our dear colleague and friend, Ellen Kovak, a woman of great character and intelligence, engaging wit and warmth, exuberant spirit and exceptional talent as a public relations executive. Her leadership, insight and inspiration enriched our lives. We thank God for her life and extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, Stan, and daughter, Janet.
