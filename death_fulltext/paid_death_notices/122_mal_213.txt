
        JORDAN-Alphonse, Dr. The Department of Medicine is saddened by the death of our long time colleague, Dr. Alphonse Jordan. An expert in cardiac catheterization, Dr. Jordan was a member of our faculty since 1956 and rose to the rank of Associate Professor of Medicine at Albert Einstein College of Medicine. As such, he trained a generation of cardiologists and improved the lives of countless patients. Board certified in Cardiology, he was a member of the Alpha Omega Alpha medical honor society. He was active in numerous organizations, including the National Medical Association, and he received the Walter Booker Award of the Association of Black Cardiologists. We express condolences to his family. James Scheuer, M.D. Chairman Milford Fulop, M.D. Vice Chairman J. Anthony Ware, M.D. Director of Cardiology Edmund H. Sonnenblick, M.D. Distinguished Professor of Cardioogy
