
        ZIMMER-Fred. Died peacefully on Nov. 2, 1998. Devoted husband of Anna ''Honey''; beloved father of Dr. Harold Zimmer and Phyllis Arn Zimmer of Bellevue, WA.; Sarah Chapman and Sam Chapman of Greenbrae, CA.; and Bonnie Zimmer and Jim Ptacek of Cambridge, MA. Proud grandfather of Allegra and Sabina Chapman and Alex Ptacek Zimmer. Cherished brother of Marilyn Heischober and Rosalie Soladar. His sense of humor and love of nature created his second career as a craftsman and was the source of much joy to all. Contributions to a charity of your choice or The Rice Public Library, Kittery, ME. 03904. Service will be held Thursday, 1 PM, from Kirschenbaum, 1700 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, New York.
        ZIMMER-Fred. The Board of Overseers and Staff of the Brookdale Center on Aging of Hunter College grieve with our beloved Honey and all of the Zimmers' as they mourn the loss of their family's patriarch.
