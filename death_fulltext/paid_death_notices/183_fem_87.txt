
        BOWIE-Barbara Jean. On April 6th, 2002. She served for the U.S. Navy from 1956 to 1960 and the Army National Guard from 1976 to 1993. She was also employed by the City of New York Housing Authority for the past 36 years. Loving mother of Alexsandra Bowie West, mother-in-law of Derrick Logan West. Devoted grandmother of Javin Lee Alexander and Jayden Logan Avery. Also survived by many loving relatives and friends. Family will receive friends Thursday, 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Funeral Mass Friday, 11 AM, at Our Lady of Good Counsel R.C. Church, 230 East 90th Street.
