
        HILLS-Robert C. Died peacefully on January 26, 1998 at the age of 89 at his home in Metairie, Louisiana. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Dorothy Fleury Hills and his children, Anne Hills of Metairie, John F. Hills of New Orleans, LA, Thomas C. Hills of Secaucus, NJ and a grandson, Robert F. Hills of New Orleans. A successful businessman, devoted husband and loving father, his wisdom and wonderful spirit will be greatly missed. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Tulane Center for Clinical Effectiveness & Prevention, Tulane Medical Center, Box TW34, 1430 Tulane Ave., New Orleans, LA 70112
