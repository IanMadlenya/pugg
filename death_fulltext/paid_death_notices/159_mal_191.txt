
        KEANE-Gilbert Harrison. On April 16, 2000. Loving husband to Hannah for fifty-nine years. He sowed many gardens and likewise, abundant wisdom and humor in the hearts of his many friends. Gentle, cherished father and father-in-law to Susan and Dean Avery, devoted grandfather to Jennifer and Phoebe. His compassionate and courageous spirit endures forever. Contributions in his memory to Lighthouse of Broward County, 650 North Andrews Avenue, Fort Lauderdale, FL. 33311.
