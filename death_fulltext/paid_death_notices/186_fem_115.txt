
        MILLARD-Liselotte. July 15, 2002 in New York. We mourn the passing of Liselotte after a long and courageous fight. Her will to live has been an inspiration to us all. George and Marsha Crawford, stepdaughter; Dini, William and Charles von Mueffling, stepchildren; and Tatiana, step granddaughter. Services will be held Saturday, July 20th at Saint Andrew's Dune Church in Southampton at 12 Noon.
