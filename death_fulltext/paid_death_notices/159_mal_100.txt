
        CLAY-William Law Jr., retired attorney, died March 27 at St. Catherine of Siena Medical Center in Smithtown, NY. He was 93. Mr. Clay practiced law for 41 years, and was employed as a corporate attorney for General Electric from 1948 until retiring in 1971. He lived in the Village of Nissequogue where he served as Village Justice from 1988 to 1991. Survivors include his wife, Helen LeRoy Clay of Nissequogue; and two children, William Law Clay III of Staten Island, New York, and George Miller Clay of San Diego, California.
