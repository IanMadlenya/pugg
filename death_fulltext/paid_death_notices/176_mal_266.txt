
        RICHNER-Robert, 79. Of Lawrence, NY and Boca Raton, FL, died Tuesday, Sept.18, 2001 after an extended illness. Loving husband for 51 years of his beloved wife Edith. A loving father and devoted grandfather, he is also survived by his children, Clifford of Manhattan, Stuart & Nancy of Rockville Centre, NY, and Donald & Carole of Stamford, CT. and grandchildren, Zachary, Gregory, Lucas, Kate and Willie. Together with his wife, he was the founder and longtime publisher of Richner Communications, publishers of the Nassau Herald and the Herald Community Newspaper group of L.I. Funeral Thursday 12:30 P.M., Temple Israel, 140 Central Ave, Lawrence, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Five Towns Community Chest.
