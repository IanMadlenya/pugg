
        STOCK--Ruth, (nee Zwiren), age 102, formerly of Brooklyn passed away January 31, 2005 in Cincinnati. Adored wife of the late Morris A. Stock; beloved mother of Judith Shapiro of Cincinnati & Larry Stock of Pacific Grove, CA; motherin-law of Dr. Herbert Shapiro of Cincinnati & Barbara Stock of Saratoga, CA; devoted grandmother of Dr. Mark S. Shapiro of San Antonio, TX, Nina Shapiro of Seattle, WA, David Stock, Victoria Stock & Stacy Stock Griffiths of Saratoga, CA; great-grandmother of Sophia, Tessa & Max. Memorial services will be held in Cincinnati, Monday February 21. Interment will be with her late husband at Temple Beth El Gardens, in Davie, FL.
