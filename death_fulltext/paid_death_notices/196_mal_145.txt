
        PALEY-Jack. On May 13, 2003. Loving husband and best friend of Sylvia Schultz Paley for 65 years. Brother of Reba Windreich. Father of Anita and Howard Orlin, Kenneth and Jian Paley. Greatly admired and beloved grandfather of Jonathan and Mary Orlin, Liz Orlin and John Ifcher, and Janet Orlin. Greatgrandfather of Jonah Paley Ifcher. A graduate of NYU, a founder of District 65, Executive Officer of the Retail Wholesale and Department Store Union, an official of the AFL-CIO, trustee and founder of the District 65 Pension and Security Fund and long-time fighter for social justice. He worked to improve conditions and wages for the working person. His life made a difference.
