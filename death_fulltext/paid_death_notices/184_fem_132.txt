
        STEIN-Adele Miller. Always 29, died on Mother's Day, May 12, 2002 after enjoying herself at a bar mitzvah. Beloved wife of the late William A. Stein. Incredible mother of Dr. Dodie Stein and the late Mark Scheinman Stein. She never chose her friends by their weight or their age. Donations may be made to the Mark Scheinman Stein Camp Scholarship Fund at Eisner Camp-UAHC, 633 Third Avenue, New York City 10017.
