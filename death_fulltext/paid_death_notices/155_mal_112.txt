
        SWANSON-Robert A. The Boards of Overseers and Managers and the staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering note with sadness the death of Robert A. Swanson, our friend and colleague. Mr. Swanson joined the Board of Overseers of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in March of 1998. His potential to contribute to the mission of Memorial Sloan-Kettering will be sorely missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the Swanson family. Laurance S. Rockefeller, Honorary Co-Chairman James D. Robinson III, Honorary Co-Chairman Douglas A. Warner III, Chairman H. Virgil Sherrill, Vice Chairman Richard L. Gelb, Vice Chairman Paul A. Marks, M.D., President, Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
