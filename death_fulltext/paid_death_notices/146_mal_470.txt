
        OSSERMAN-Richard A.., Esq. Of Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of New York City and Stamford, CT. Died peacefully at home on March 24, 1999, in his 68th year after 2-1/2 years of heroic battle with cancer. He was deeply loved by his family, friends and colleagues. A lawyer for over 40 years, Richard loved his profession and was devoted to his clients. He was a gentleman and mensch in the truest sense, and believed he was a very lucky man, as he lived all his dreams, traveled the world and experienced life to the fullest. He was a brilliant man with an open mind and heart who constantly reinvented himself and adapted to life's changes and challenges. He will be very much missed by his loving wife Malka, and his devoted sons Eric, Joel, Justin and Jordan, and his two stepsons Steve and Robert Chait. He will also be missed by his daughter-in-law Linda and four grandchildren, Harris, Amy, Matthew and Gwen, as well as his brother George and wife Zan D. Gallaway, and his nephews and nieces. He will be missed and remembered fondly by everyone who was touched by him. The family asks that gifts in memory of Richard A. Osserman be made out to the Richard Osserman Memorial Fund and sent to Hobart and William Smith Colleges, Geneva, New York 14456. Services will be Sunday, March 28, 1999, at 3:30 PM, at the Star of David Cemetery and Funeral Home, 7701 Bailey Road, North Lauderdale, Florida 33068, phone 954-722-9000.
