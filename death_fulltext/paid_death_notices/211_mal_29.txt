
        KOLB--Larry of White Plains on August 1, 2004 at age 72. Born December 5, 1931 in New York City to Andrew and Rose (Goldberg) Kolb. Survived by his loving wife Ilona (Brandvain) Kolb, loving children Claude Kolb, Rhonda Casson (husband Gary), Elissa Kramer (husband Dr. Albert) and Sherri Friedman (husband Stuart) cherished grandchildren Ryan, Michael, Trevor, Nicole, Kyle, Corey, Lindsay and Robbie and a caring sister Stevie Ross. Also survived by his devoted in-laws Dr. Simon and Ruth Brandvain. Services today 1 PM at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Rd. Mamaroneck, NY 914-381-1809 followed by interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Memorial donations are requested for the benefit of Pediatric Leukemia Research to the Hassenfeld Childrens Cancer Center C/O Trish Lollo, 317 East 34th St. 8th Floor, New York, New York 10016.
