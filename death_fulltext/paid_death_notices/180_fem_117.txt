
        LANDAU-Marianne. December 31, 2001. Escaping Nazi Germany, she found profession and family in New York. Wife of Herbie, mother of Pamela, mourner for both, she leaves vivid memories to grandson Julian; cousins Herbert, Robert, and their families; niece Deborah; cousin and friend Gaby; and many other relatives and friends. Services Sunday, January 13, 10 AM, Schwartz Bros. Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills.
