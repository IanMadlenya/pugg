
        CURRY--Anthony Xavier, 67, of Charlotte, NC, died peacefully Wednesday, May 30, 2007. He was born October 31, 1939, in Brooklyn, NY. Mr. Curry was a Consultant with ADP and was the President and Founder of Buttonwood Business Systems, which he led for ten years. A longtime resident of Long Island, NY. Tony had resided in Charlotte since 1997. Funeral service at 2:00 pm, Tuesday, June 5, 2007 in the J.B. Tallent Funeral Service Chapel, 1937 Sharon Amith Road, Charlotte. Tony is survived by his loving wife of 42 years, Barbara Anne Curry; a daughter, Diane Ruggiero and husband, Chuck, of Charlotte; a son, Glenn Curry and wife, Theresa, and only granddaughter, Laura Jean Curry, all of Long Island, NY; and his beloved sister, Alyce Curry, of Charlotte, NC. Memorials may be made to Hospice & Palliative Care Charlotte Region, 1420 East Seventh Street, Charlotte, NC 28204. Online condolences may be shared at www. tallentfuneralservice.com.
