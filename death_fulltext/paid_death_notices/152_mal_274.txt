
        BELLER - Abraham Benjamin. Passed away on September 10th. He was 67 years old. Abe was a brilliant teacher who taught for many years at the High School of Music and Art in Manhattan and at Somerset County College in New Jersey. He was also an inspiring High School administrator and an educational leader in developing innovative school programs in the arts throughout the state of New Jersey. He was a loving husband, father and grandfather, and a devoted friend. Abe's wonderful sense of humor, his zest for life, and his endless and empathic support of all who had the good fortune to know him left an enduring impression that Abe was a very special human being. He will be sorely missed by the many who loved him. He leaves his wife, Mickey; two children, Heidi and Rick, and five grandchildren. Ernest Brahm Murray Howard Michael Marks Irwin Natov
