
        REDO--Dr. S. Frank. On July 4, 2006. Husband to Maria (nee Lappano) for 58 years. Father to Philip and Martha. Father-in-law to Jenette Survived by his sisters, Carmela Massaro and Louise Massian. Healer and poet. A brilliant, gentle soul who made the world a better place. Born December 28, 1920 in Brooklyn. Chief of Pediatric Surgery, New York Hospital Cornell Medical Center 1960 to 1995. Summer resident of Truro, MA where a memorial service is planned.
