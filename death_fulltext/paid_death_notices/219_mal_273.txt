
        COURTIS--James G., Sr., age 79, of Oakland, New Jersey. On April 23, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Jettie D. Courtis (1976), dear father of James G. Courtis Jr. of Lakewood, Colorado and Stefanie Beach of Florida, New York. Loving companion of Althea Terman. Dear brother of George Courtis of Totowa, NJ. Cherished grandfather of four. Mr. Courtis was a truck driver for the New York Times newspaper. He was a U.S. Army Veteran. Mr. Courtis also belonged to the American Legion, The Power Squadron and the Wyckoff Lodge #
