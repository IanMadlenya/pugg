
        HORWITZ-George. 80 of Boca Raton, Florida, formerly of Rochester New York, on June 27, 2000. Beloved husband of Joan (Kubie) Horwitz; Loving father of John and Joanne Horwitz, and stepfather of Donald, David and Kenneth Kubie; Cherished grandfather of 10; Dear brother of Arthur, James and the late Samter Horwitz. He served as chairman of The National Clothing Company. Following his retirement, he became president of the local chapter of Planned Parenthood. Private family services.
