
        WOLF-Benedict. On July 23, 1999 in his 94th year. Adored husband of the late Miriam J. Wolf. Beloved father of Dr. Deborah W. Tanzer. Cherished grandfather of David, Kenneth & Charles Tanzer. Loving brother-in-law of Gertrude J. Bonime. Fond uncle and great uncle. He was a child of his century and inspired a generation of caring and commitment to social justice. Services Sunday, July 25th, 10:45AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        WOLF-Benedict. The partners, associates and staff of Wolf Popper LLP mourn the passing of our esteemed founding partner, mentor and friend, Benedict Wolf. Benedict Wolf was a founder of Wolf Popper LLP (formerly Wolf Popper Ross Wolf & Jones) in 1945. His wisdom guided the firm for more than 50 years. Mr. Wolf was an innovator in the field of securities class actions. Prior to founding Wolf Popper LLP, Mr. Wolf was the first Secretary and Chief Trial Examiner of the National Labor Relations Board. He was married to his beloved Miriam for more than 65 years until her passing in 1995. Mr. Wolf embodied the best qualities of the legal profession: a keen mind, compassion and commitment. Benedict Wolf was a gentleman and a scholar. We will remember him always with great fondness. The Partners of Wolf Popper LLP
