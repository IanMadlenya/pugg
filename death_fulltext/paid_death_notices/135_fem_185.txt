
        KESSLER-Mae. On April 24, 1998. Beloved wife of Norman for 56 years. Devoted mother of Ellen and Keith. Dear sister of Mildred and Sylvia and sister-in-law of Jean, Marion, Sam, Sara and Arthur. Services at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, on Wednesday, April 29, at 9:45 AM. She will live forever in the hearts of all who were touched by her warmth, her caring and her joy of life.
