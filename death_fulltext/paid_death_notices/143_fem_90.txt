
        EDELSON-Jean. On Saturday, December 5, 1998, peacefully at her home in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late H. Edward Edelson, dear sister of Lillian de Burgos, loving mother of Robert and loving mother-in-law of his wife Jill, loving mother of Alan and loving mother-in-law of his wife Carol; cherished and much beloved grandmother of Lynne, David, Richard, and Helena; loving great-grandmother of Rachael, Esther, and Devorah-Leah; dear sister-in-law of Lila Malsman and brother-in-law of William Edelson. She is best commemorated by the gift of love in the same copious measure that she herself gave to all those dear to her.
