
        BREINDEL-Joseph H., M.D. Born 1909, in Zablotow, Poland, and graduated medical school in Italy, then immigrated to the US and established a private practice in Obstetrics and Gynecology for over 50 years until his retirement in 1989. Full attending at Beth Israel for many years and former Chief of Obstetrics at New York Infirmary. A leader in Obstetrics, beloved by his patients and adored by his family, Dr. Breindel is survived by his wife Sonia, his daughter Dr. Monique Breindel, son-in-law Jonathan Oberman and his adoring grandchildren Hannah, Naomi and Sarah Rose. Predeceased by his son Eric. Services Monday, 12:45, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave.
