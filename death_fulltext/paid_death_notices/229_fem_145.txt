
        BENINGSON--Renee. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Renee Beningson, whose deep commitment to our Women's Campaign strengthened the lives of millions in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to her beloved husband, Bob; her daughter, Susan; her daughter, Robin, and her husband, Sal; and the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
