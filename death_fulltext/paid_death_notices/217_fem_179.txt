
        HAMMELBURGER--Ann, 87, of Boca Raton, Florida and Roslyn, New York, died at home on February 16 surrounded by her family. She was the beloved wife of Lou for 58 wonderful years, cherished mother of Marcia Cohen (Leonard) and Florie Wachtenheim (Robert). ''Maw Maw'' was adored by all her grandchildren, Jason and Jodie, Scott and Lauren, Jeffrey, Andrew & Gregory. We are forever enriched by the unconditional love and undying support she gave us. She will live in our hearts forever. Rest peacefully.
