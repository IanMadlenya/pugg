
        KAGGEN-Elias D.O., M.D. Of Brooklyn, NY, died at his home on December 17, 2001, at the age of 85. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia Muntner Kaggen and the late Rissel Kaggen, he is survived by his daughters, Lois Kaggen McCann and Marilyn Kaggen Fried. His grandson, Daniel Kaggen Fried and his sons-in-law, Michael McCann and Stephen Fried. He received a D.O. from the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine in 1938 and a M.D. from the Chicago College of Medicine in 1947. A practicing family physician until his death, he spent his 63 year career caring for his patients, utilizing osteopathic and allopathic approaches and life-saving diagonistic skills. He will be remembered for his love of and dedication to his family and his work. In lieu of flowers remembrances may be sent to City Harvest, 575 8th Avenue, 4th floor, NY, NY 10018-3011.
