
        KATZ-Irving. Born May 22, 1913, died November 22 at age 85. Retired from the U.N. since 1974. He is well remembered for excellence in linguistics and his vast knowledge of history. He is missed by his wife of 51 years, Elaine, son Robert, daughter-in-law Florence, and two granddaughters, Laura and Alison. Services today, 10 AM ''Sinai Chapels'', 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York.
