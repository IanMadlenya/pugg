
        OSBORNE-Msgr. William J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired pastor emeritus of St. Fidelis Church, 12306 14th Ave., College Point on April 2. Reposing in James Stout Funeral Home, 1339 College Point Blvd., College Point, Saturday April 4, from 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. and Sunday, April 5, from 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Sunday, April 5 at 7:30 P.M. in St. Fidelis Church. Funeral Mass, Monday, April 6, at 10:30 A.M. in St. Fidelis Church. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
