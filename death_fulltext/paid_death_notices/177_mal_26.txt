
        SIMON-Alan M. Age 69, died at his home in Manhattan, on September 29, 2001. Beloved husband of Toni Lautman Simon. Loving father of Andrew and Leslie Simon and father-in-law of Nancy Simon and Marc Bender. Devoted grandfather of Allison and Samantha Simon, and Arlo and Philip Bender Simon. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, 6-8 PM, with service Tuesday, 11:30 AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Cancer Society, The Horace Mann School or Yale University.
        SIMON-Alan. The Board of Governors, members and staff of the Real Estate Board of New York mourn the passing of Alan Simon. Alan was an active member of REBNY for many years, serving with distinction as chairman of its Admissions Committee, directing the tennis tournament at the board's golf and tennis outings, and participating on several commercial real estate committees. Alan was known in the industry as an individual of the highest integrity and character. We express our deepest sympathy to his wife, Toni, children Andrew and Leslie, grandchildren, and the entire Simon family. Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Steven Spinola, President
