
        HUBLITZ-W. Charles. 89. Passed away Thursday, February 6, 2003, at the Village Nursing Home in Manhattan. Born February 15, 1913, Charles was raised in the Bronx. A longtime employee of Georg Jensen Silversmiths, he rose to the position of Vice President. He later worked for the Lauffer Co. of New York in the capacity of Executive Vice President. At both companies he developed fine China, crystal, blown glass, ironware, stoneware and flatware, working with fine designers and artisans around the world. His life partner, Charles Miata; Philip Norman Hublitz, Barbara Reark and Jennifer Getchell survive him.
