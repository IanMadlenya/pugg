
        BERGER-Leo, Captain. With great sorrow, Dr. Allan Abramson and the Department of Otolaryngology of Long Island Jewish Medical Center acknowledge the passing of Captain Leo Berger, a dear friend and great benefactor whose tireless support of the clinical and academic goals of the Department of Otolaryngology for the past twenty-eight years has made a positive difference in the lives of countless people. His presence will be deeply missed. Dr. Allan Abramson, Chairman Department of Otolaryngology L.I. Jewish Medical Center
