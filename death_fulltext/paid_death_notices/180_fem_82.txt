
        RESNIK-Ruth H. On December 31, 2001, in Brooklyn, NY. Widow of the late Dr. Ernest Resnik, daughter of the late Rose and Samuel Duberstein, sister of the late Norman Duberstein. Survived by her brother Abner Duberstein, his wife and children, Frances, Ronald and David; by her niece, Ellen Nagler of Santa Barbara, CA; by grandniece Hilary and grand-nephew Adam, cousins and friends. Ruth was an artist of great talent, and a beautiful woman in body, mind and spirit. The family is grateful to Claudia Williams for the tender care and companionship she gave our ''Honey'' during her last years. Services private.
