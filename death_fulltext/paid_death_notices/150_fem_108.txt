
        JONAS-Ilse. Age 91, died July 8 in Pittsburgh, PA. Beloved wife of the late Fred Jonas, devoted mother of Jeffrey and Carla. She worked tirelessly for the National Council of Jewish Women and Camp Louemma. She will be missed by all those she touched. Services Sunday, 11AM, Schwartz Bros Funeral Home, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. For information 718-263-7600. Donations to the Alzheimer's Association.
