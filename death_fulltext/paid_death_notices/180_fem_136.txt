
        MURPHY-Mary Ellen (Mickey) Green. 84, retired journalist, died January 10, at Long Island College Hospital after a brief illness. Wife of the late Mark Murphy, mother of Timothy Murphy and Sarah Murphy Proudlock, grandmother of Robin, Samantha, Mark, Lucero, Lee and great-grandmother of Justin, Reggie and Keegan. A passionate proponent of the New York harbor and waterfront, Mickey will be deeply missed by her many friends and family. Donations may be made to Bargemusic, 2 Old Fulton St., Brooklyn 11201. Memorial service at Bargemusic, Saturday, January 19, 1PM.
