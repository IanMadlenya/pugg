
        BAXT--George. 80, an acclaimed screenwriter and mystery novelist, most widely known for his Pharaoh Love detective series, died in New York on June 28 from complications following heart surgery. An uniquely talented man, George was the son of the late Lena and Sam Baxt, brother of Juliette Terman, the late Estelle Brickel and the late Morris Baxt. He was the devoted and beloved uncle of Stephen and Gail Brickel, Susan and Ron Goldstein, the late Barbara Kam, Elliot and Marilyn Terman and Fred Terman. Funeral arrangements private. Memorial service at later date. >>AD#
