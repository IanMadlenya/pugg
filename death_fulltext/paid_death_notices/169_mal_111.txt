
        RYAN-Daniel. Age 70 of Wayne, NJ on February 4th 2001. Retired New York Times Paperhandler, formerly of Touraafulla, County Limerick, Ireland. Beloved husband of Mary Ryan of Wayne. Loving father of Daniel J.A., John J. and Gina M. Daniel is predeceased by his daughter Regina (1968). Dear brother of Michael, Sean, Timothy and his late brother Patrick (1972). Funeral Mass Thursday, 10: AM at St. Joseph's R.C. Church in Oradell. Interment is at the Mary-Rest Cemetery in Mahwah, New Jersey. Family will receive friends at the Beaugard Funeral Home, 869 Kinderkamack Road, River-Edge, New Jersey, Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers donations in Daniel's memory are requested to the Hospice of New Jersey @ 400 Broad Acres, Bloomfield, N.J. 07003.
