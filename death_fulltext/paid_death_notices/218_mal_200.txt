
        FREUND--Richard. Of Malverne, New York on March 16, 2005. Husband of the late Esta Freund. Father of Alice, Robert and Charles, and proud grandfather of Alex, Elizabeth, Harrison, Amy, Andrew, Clifford and Evelyn. A graduate of Columbia Law School and an expert in entertainment and labor law, he worked in labor relations at ABC for 30 years. He rose to become Vice President of Labor Relations for Capital Cities/ABC Inc., where he retired in 1987. We will deeply miss his kindness, humor and care for others. Services at 2 P.M. March 18th at I.J. Morris Inc., 46 Greenwich Street, Hempstead, NY. Interment at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale.
