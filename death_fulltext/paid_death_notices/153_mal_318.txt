
        LEFFELL-Rabbi A. Bernard. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague who was founding rabbi of Congregation Shaare Zedek in Montreal from his ordination in 1954 until his retirement four years ago. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Freda, sons Jonathan, Daniel, David and Michael, mother, Marion and to all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Seymour L. Essrog, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP The Rabbinical Assembly
