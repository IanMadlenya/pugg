
        PIKE-William. The members of the New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of our colleague, Dr. William Pike, a member for nearly 50 years. We extend our sincere condolences to his family members: Peri, Steven, Paula, Seth and Alden Pike. His wife, Mrs. Edith Pike (deceased) participated with him in activities of the society. Albert M. Sax, M.D., President/Society Eslee Samberg, M.D., President/Institute
