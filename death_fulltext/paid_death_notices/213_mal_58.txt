
        GREENSTEIN--Samuel. Retired Justice of the Supreme Court of the State of New York. Beloved father of Betty and George Farkas, and Gerald and Robin Greenstein. Beloved Pappa of Michael, Randi, Jason and Bari, and great grandfather of Isabella. Elected in 1980, he personified the essence of his campaign slogan in both his professional and personal life: He was a ''Mensch on the Bench'' and a Mensch in life. Contributions, in lieu of flowers, to the American Heart Association. Services at Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1895 Flatbush Avenue (at Avenue L), Brooklyn, today at 1PM.
