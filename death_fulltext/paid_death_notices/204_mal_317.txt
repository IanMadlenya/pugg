
        FISHMAN -- Irving. 85, of Riverdale, NY, died January 22, 2004. Beloved husband of Frances, father of Phyllis Angelo and husband Roger and Paul Fishman and wife Elizabeth, grandfather of Gary, Lauren husband Glenn, Leah, Meyer, and Edwin, brother of Estelle Dichter. Veteran of WWII and the Manhattan Project, founding member of the Riverdale Jewish Center, Founder and President of Maritime Power Corporation of New York and Jersey City, NJ. His caring, generous, playful soul is alive in all who loved him. In lieu of flowers make a donation to your favorite charity.
