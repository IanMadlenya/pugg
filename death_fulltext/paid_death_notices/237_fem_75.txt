
        KENT--Elsie F. On October 10, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Nelson Kent. Loving mother of Alexandra Kent and Nelson Kent; cherished grandmother of Joshua, Ben, Jordan and Ada, devoted sister of the late Gloria Gersh. Born in 1921, Elsie was raised in New Jersey and attended Barnard College and lived her life in New York City. She worked for the United Nations School, but her true love was painting landscapes. A lover of art and music, she traveled often to Europe with her family. Memorial service Monday, October 16th, 11am at the Brick Church, Park Ave at 91 Street.
