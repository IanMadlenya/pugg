
        OTTAWAY-James Haller Sr.. The trustees and administration of Bard College mourn the passing James Ottaway, Sr., a distinguished and active member of the Hudson Valley community and father of Bard College trustee James H. Ottaway, Jr. His deep commitments to journalism and to civic duty are evident throughout the region, and his generosity and kindness will be remembered and cherished far into the future. We send our deepest condolences to his wife, Ruth Blackburne Ottaway, his daughter Ruth Ottaway Sherer, his sons James H. Ottaway Jr. and David Blackburne Ottaway and the rest of his family. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman, Board of Trustees
