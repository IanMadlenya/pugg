
        GARRETT-Irving ''Gosie''. Age 76, longtime resident of Bayside, Queens, and recently of Hallendale, FL, died August 3, 2002, after a courageous battle with congestive heart failure. He died at home surrounded by his loved ones. Gosie owned restaurants and loved life. He is survived by his wife Harriet; his daughters Randi Freedman and Debbie Garrett; his son-in-law Robert Freedman; two grandchildren, Max and Katie Freedman; and his brother Steve. Donations can be made to Hospice Care of Northern Westchester. Funeral services 10 AM, Tuesday, August 6, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon.
