
        BONESTEEL-Louise. Born March 22, 1918 in Watervliet, N.Y. Died of cancer November 11, 2000 at her home in Washington, D.C. Faithful helper and friend of the late Esther L. Moscow for fifty years. Miss Bonesteel is mourned by all the members of the Heimann and Holm families. Contributions in her name would be welcomed at The Salvation Army.
