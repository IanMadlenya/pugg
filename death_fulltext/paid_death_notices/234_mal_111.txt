
        FRANKEL--Howard J. D.D.S. 83, July 1St, formerly of New Rochelle, NY, devoted husband and best friend of 56 years to Janice. Survived by son Jeffrey, daughter-in-law Gwen, granddaughter Holli and adored daughter Heidi Shore. In addition to a private practice in New York City, he held the rank of Assistant Professor of Crown and Bridge at NYU School of Dentistry. Donations to: Alzheimer's Society.
