
        JOSEPHBERG-Sarah Greenblatt. The Officers and Board of Directors of the America-Israel Friendship League extend our sincerest condolences to our friend and colleague Ira Greenblatt on the passing of his beloved mother, Mrs. Sarah (Syd) Greenblatt Josephberg. Syd was a longtime friend and supporter of the AIFL. We will cherish her memory. Kenneth J. Bialkin, Chairman Mortimer B. Zuckerman, Pres Ilana Artman, Executive V.P.
