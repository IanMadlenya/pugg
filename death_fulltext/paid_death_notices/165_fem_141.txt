        SIEGFRIED-Gladys. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Gladys Siegfried, mother of our devoted Trustee, Robert, and his brother, Jonathan. With them and all their loved ones we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of her life will endure forever in the hearts of those whose lives she touched. Dr. Ronald B.
