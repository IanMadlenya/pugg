
        ROSS--Joan A. A long time resident of Mount Vernon, NY, died on October 13, 2004, after a long battle with colon cancer. Joan was born in New York City on December 4, 1928, the daughter of William and Lena Flug. She earned a bachelors degree from Hunter College. She married Richard C. Ross on September 7, 1952 and moved to and resided in Mount Vernon continuously until her death. Beside her husband her survivors include a daughter, Marcia S. Ross-Rayside, of Studio City, CA, two sons, William N. Ross, of St. Louis, MO, and Andrew D. Ross, of Marina Del Rey, CA, and two grandchildren, Alice Rose RossRayside, and Michael N. Ross. The funeral will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt Vernon, NY on Friday, October 15, 2004 at 1 P.M. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate a donation to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main Street, Tuckahoe, NY 10707
