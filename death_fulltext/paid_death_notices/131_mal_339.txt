
        JOSEPH-Michael, Jr. 84 died Thurs. at home. Husband of Marion Leeds Joseph. Father of Patricia Ann Joseph and Linda Jane Gregg. Friends may call from 3-8 p.m. at his home, 50 Penn Blvd., Scarsdale, NY. Funeral services will be held at 11:00 a.m. Mon. in the Fred D. Knapp & Son FuneralHome, 267 Greenwich Ave., Greenwich, CT. Interment will follow in Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Parkinson Society.
