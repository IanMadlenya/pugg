
        MILLER--Jeanette Doerfler, passed away Wednesday, May 4, 2005, at the age of 83, in West Palm Beach, FL. Wife of Jerry Miller, now deceased, for 61 years. A former educator in the New York City school system, she leaves her sons, Andrew and his wife, Gail; Richard and his wife, Debbie; grandchildren, Evan Labb and his wife, Jody; Jarrod Miller and Darren Miller, and great-grandchild, Elijah. Her ever-present smile, outgoing personality and upbeat attitude towards life, even in the face of adversity, will always be remembered. Funeral services Sunday, May 8, 11 AM, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., New York, NY.
