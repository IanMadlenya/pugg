
        FAIRLY-Gene. A memorial service Saturday, March 17, 4 PM, at the Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 St. Gene, 75, died November 25, 2000. A staunch friend and beloved mentor to many, Gene led healing meditation sessions for the seriously ill. He was also a video disc publisher, a writer, a translator, and a Renaissance man.
