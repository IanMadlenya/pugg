
        SHOMER-Robert R. V.M.D. The Jewish Braille Institute of America mourns the passing of a long-time and dedicated Trustee, Dr. Robert R. Shomer. As a devoted friend of the blind and visually impaired he will be missed by those whose lives were enriched by his deep caring. May his memory serve as a blessing and his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Barbara B. Friedman, President Gerald M. Kass, Executive Vice President
