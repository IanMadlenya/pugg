
        LAHN-Philip M. On February 15, 2002 age 100. Predeceased by his dear wife Jean. Survived by his beloved & loving daughter Marion; son Daniel and his wife Stefanie Weldon; grandchildren Hilary and Simon; & many nieces & nephews. Services were Monday, February 18, 2002, 9:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
