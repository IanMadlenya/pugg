
        SHERMAN--Michael A. Died on February 5, 2005. Beloved and devoted husband for 61 years to Shirley (nee Kirschenbaum). Deeply cherished and loved father of Lynne and Susan. Adored grandfather of Joshua and Lauren. Survived by his brother George and his close cousin Bob Napack. Predeceased by his brothers Edward and Bernard. To all of those he knew, he will be remembered for his loving and gentle nature, his goodness of spirit and his never ending curiosity about life. He will be greatly missed by all of the people whose lives he touched. Services Tuesday, February 8, 11 am, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 91st, NYC.
