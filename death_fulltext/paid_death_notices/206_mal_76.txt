
        SINGER--Rabbi Dr. Joseph I. Beloved husband of the late Rebecca Heller Singer. Loving father of Alexander T., Linda Singer and Vivian S. Singer. Adoring grandfather of Glen Eric and Jason Evan. Devoted brother of the late Sophie Lewis. Rabbi of Manhattan Beach Jewish Center since 1947, and now Rabbi Emeritus since 1997. Author of the books Margin for Living and Margin of Triumph. Editor, 11 volumes of the Sermon Manuel of Rabbinical Council of America. Editor, Mesoreth Ha Tanach, five volumes of his father-in-law Rabbi Chiam Heller. Services today, 11 AM, at the Manhattan Beach Jewish Center, 60 West End Ave, Brooklyn, NY. For more information, contact Sherman Flatbush Memorial Chapel; (718) 377-7300.
