
        MILBAUER--Richard S. on November 13, 2004. Born January 8, 1927, in Brooklyn, NY, son of Philip and Jean (Bloom) Milbauer. He received his Master's in Economics from the University of Wisconsin and began his career in radio at WHA in Madison and went on to produce news and training films. Husband of Mickey, father of Jean (Bill Knapp) Milbauer, Marian (Laura Orlando) Milbauer and Deborah (Corey Dolgon) Milbauer. Grandfather of Samantha, Gillian, Bryan, Eliza, Ally, Zachary and Bailey. His brother David died in 1962. Services were Monday. Memorials to Woodlands Temple, 50 Worthington Road, White Plains, NY 10607.
