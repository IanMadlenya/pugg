
        GOLDFINGER CRINGAN-Bonnie Jill, 46, died on August 9th, 2001 in California after a valiant four year battle with breast cancer. Formerly of Rye Brook, NY. She is survived by her beloved sons Jonathan and Jacob, husband Gary, cherised sister Beth Goldfinger Signer and husband Jeffrey, stepbrother Michael Harris and wife Susan, step-sister Lisa Harris, devoted mother Barbara Shifrin, father Irwin (Bud) Goldfinger and step-mother Marcia. She also leaves adored niece Rebecca and step-nieces Marina and Alaina. Donations may be made to Gilda's Club of Westchester, 80 Maple Avenue, White Plains, NY 10601.
