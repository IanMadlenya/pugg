
        STAPEN--Manuel, MD, age 92, of New York, NY. It is with great sorrow that we announce the passing of this prominant psychoanalyst who died peacefully in his sleep on May 25, 2007. Born in Brooklyn, NY on August 19, 1914 , he served as a medical officer during WWII. After further training he practiced in Manhattan, and was a member of the New York Psychoanalytic Institute. Dr. Stapen will be remembered for his kindness, generosity, and intellectual curiosity. He was a devotee of the Opera, Synphony, and Ballet, and had a great interest in art. He is survived by his devoted wife Tatiana, and her daughters Elena and Karen; by his former wife Bonnie, and beloved daughters Anita (Richard) and Nancy (Ron); and grandchildren Rachel, David, Sarah, and Julia. He will also be mourned by brothers Milton and Irving, and sister Annabelle, as well as numerous neices and nephews. Funeral to be held at Schwartz Brothers -Jeffer Memorial Chapel on Tuesday, May 29, at 1:30 pm.
