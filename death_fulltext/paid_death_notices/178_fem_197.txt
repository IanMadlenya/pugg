
        THORBECKE - HOCHWALD - G. Jeanette, MD, died November 16, 2001. The trustees, faculty and staff of NYU School of Medicine mourn the tragic loss of our friend and colleague, G. Jeanette Thorbecke-Hochwald, MD, Professor of Pathology. A distinguished member of the Department of Pathology for more than 40 years, Dr. Thorbecke - Hochwald achieved international recognition for her original contributions to immunology, including serving as President of the American Association of Immunologists. She was an outstanding scientist, a prolific author, a committed teacher, and a very active member of the School's academic community. Dr. Thorbecke-Hochwald will be missed by the many people, students and colleagues whose lives she touched. We extend our deepest condolences to her husband Gerald, a senior member of the faculty at the School, and her family. Kenneth G. Langone, Chairman NYU School of Medicine Foundation Board of Trustees Robert M. Glickman, MD, Dean, NYU School of Medicine Vittorio Defendi, MD, Chairman Department of Pathology NYU School of Medicine
