
        MOZES-Jean-Max, Chief Financial Officer of Czarnikow-Rionda, graduate of the Sorbonne in Paris, France, died on Swissair Flight 111 on September 2, 1998, at the age of 51. Jean-Max leaves his wife, Annie, his three children, Marina, Nathan and Julie, and his step-son, Jean-Marie Paras and wife Guillemette, and their daughter, Lucile. He is also survived by his mother, Betty Mozes of Cannes, France, and his sister, Danielle Berthoud & her family of Aix-en-Provence, France. He is missed by his many friends. A memorial service will be held on Friday, September 11 at 10:00 A.M., at Congregation Rodeph Sholom in New York, 7 West 83rd Street, Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Community High School Emergency Fund, Fleet Bank, C/O John Rowland, Branch Manager, 85 Jefferson Avenue, Westwood, New Jersey 07675.
