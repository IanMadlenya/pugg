
        LOTOCKI de VELIGOST-Brigitte. 7 April 1919 to 10 January 2002. After a lengthy illness, our beloved Brigitte Lotocki de Veligost has departed this earth, but her spirit lives. She is predeceased by her husband, Borys, and her daughter, Marina. She is survived by her son, Alexander; daughter-in-law, Susan, & granddaughter, Isabelle. Requiescat in Pace. Donations may be made in her memory to: The Breast Cancer Research Foundation or Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center.
