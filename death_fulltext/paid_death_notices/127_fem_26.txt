
        THOMAS-Joycelyn. The Board of Trustees, staff, participants and families of the League Treatment Center mourn the death of our beloved friend and colleague, Joycelyn Thomas. Joycelyn has been the nurse at CFCS for five years. She will be remembered as an inspiration to her friends and colleagues. A Memorial Service will be held August 6 at Frank J. Barone Funeral Home, 4502 Avenue D in Brooklyn. Viewing 4-7 PM. Service at 7:30 PM. We send our condolences to her devoted family and friends.
