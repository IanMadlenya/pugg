
        ABRAMSON-Annette. The Officers, Trustees and staff of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations note with profound sadness the death of a dear friend and colleague. Annette was a dedicated and talented copy editor who served The Union and UAHC Press for over 30 years with rare and precious devotion. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her children Mark and Linda, her five grandchildren and all her loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President Rabbi Alexander M. Schindler, President Emeritus
