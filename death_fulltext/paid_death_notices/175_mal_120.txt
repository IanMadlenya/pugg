
        GRONER-Oscar. The Hillel family mourns the loss of former International Director Rabbi Oscar Groner who touched lives and helped shape the movement during his 30 year Hillel career. He is survived by wife Judyth; siblings Ruth Rosenbaum, Sarah Barach, Irwin and Julius; children Jonathan, Judith and Jeremy; step-children Joshua and Benjamin; and 11 grandchildren. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Charles Newman, Chairman Hillel Board of Directors Richard M. Joel International Director
