
        DONNER-Isaac Cohen. Born Thanksgiving Day, 1899, in NYC. Was married 60 years to Ray Breakstone Donner. He is survived by daughters Rose-Anne Donner Colt and Judith Donner Hancock. He was a graduate of NY Law School, an attorney, and a founding member of the National Lawyers Guild. Active in liberal and democratic politics. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, March 10th at 12:45 PM. Contributions to the National Lawyers Guild and NY Law School.
