
        NAPARSTEK-Samuel S.G. With great sorrow we note the passing of beloved husband and father May 15, 2002. Graduated in 1939 from Brooklyn College and 1943 from Columbia University School of Business. Army Air Force Service 1943-1946. Interment Arlington National Cemetery. Highlights of his career include Field Director United Jewish Appeal, Associate Director, CedarsSinai Medical Center, VP of Development, Salk Institute for Biological Studies, Director of Development, Cardinal Stritch University. Survivors include his wife Mary, daughter Lydia and sister Helen.
