
        CUTLER--Ethel R., Ph.D., 93, died peacefully in her sleep at home on Saturday, March 31st. Dr. Cutler was an art historian, artist, and university professor who had been retired for many years. She is survived by her loving sister, Dr. Rhoda Cutler, a clinical psychologist. Graveside services were held on Sunday, April 1st, at Beth Moses Cemetery. Contributions can be made to the Park Avenue Synagogue, 50 East 87th Street, New York City.
