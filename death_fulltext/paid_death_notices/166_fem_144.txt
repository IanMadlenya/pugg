
        BAUDOIN-Suzanne. Thanks to Scandinavia notes with sadness the passing of Suzanne Baudoin, beloved wife of our late board member and vigorous champion of Thanks to Scandinavia's scholarship fund dedicated to the nations of Denmark, Finland, Norway and Sweden in appreciation of the historic heroism of their people, governments and churches in rescuing thousands of their Jewish neighbors during WW II. Our heartfelt sympathy to her children Patsy, Jean-Yves, Marina and Bettina and their families. Victor Borge, National Chairman Richard Netter, President
