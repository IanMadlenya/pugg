
        LEIR-Henry J. The officers, board & staff of the American Technion Society express profound regret at the loss of Henry Leir of New York City, a philanthropist and major benefactor of many Jewish causes. As a supporter of the American Technion Society, he demonstrated great vision and commitment to the Technion and to Israel. His support will help ensure the university's continued excellence and vital contributions to Israel and the world. American Society for TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Irving A. Shepard, President Ben Z. Sosewitz, Chairman of the Board Melvyn H. Bloom, Exec. VP Cindy Sipkin, NY Metropolitan Region Chair Technion-Israel Institute of Technology Prof. Zehev Tadmor, President
        LEIR-Henry. The world-wide family of the ''Hospital With A Heart'' mourns the loss of Henry J. Leir, a devoted friend and patron. His generous support helped the Medical Center to develop an international reputation for combining state-of-the-art medical technology and treatment with compassionate care. For this the people of Jerusalem will forever be grateful. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Menno Ratzker, President Erica Jesselsonh, Chair, Stuart Tauber, Executive Director
        LEIR-Henry J. The Board and staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum mourn the loss of our long time friend and generous supporter. We extend condolences to his family and friends. Teddy Kollek, President-IM Judy Steinhardt, President-AFIM
