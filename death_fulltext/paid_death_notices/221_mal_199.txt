
        WIMPFHEIMER--Ernst. We offer our Kos Tanchumim to the entire Wimpfheimer family. A Silver Founder of the Medical Center together with his late wife Putti, Ernst was a generous, modest man whose open hand epitomized the highest standards of Jewish tradition. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem
