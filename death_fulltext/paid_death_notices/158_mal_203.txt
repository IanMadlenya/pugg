
        SALUSKY-Robert in Orinda on March 14th at 84 years of age. A native of Montreal, Canada, lived in New York as a young man and then in Southern California for over 50 years. An optometrist for 37 years, a member of the American Optometric Association and avid gardener. Loving father of Ida, Anna, Sheppard, Monica (John Sutherland); devoted grandfather of Erica and Alexis Sutherland and leaves his former wife & friend Cacilda Salusky. Services were held under the direction of Sinai Memorial Chapel, Lafayette, CA. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Jewish Family & Children's Services of the Greater East Bay, 2484 Shattuck Ave., Suite 210, Berkeley, CA 94704.
