
        MacKINNON-Alan D., May 24, at age 49. He devoted his adult years to helping handicapped children participate more fully in life. His gentle warmth and professional skill touched many lives and will be fondly remembered. Son of the late Gladys and Gladstone MacKinnon of Hyde Park in Boston. Survived by siblings, Barbara Kenney of Columbia Falls, Mont., Donald MacKinnon of Billerica, MA., Nancy MacKinnon of Boston, and David MacKinnon of Framingham, Mass., and by life partner, John Hetland of Manhattan. A memorial gathering of family and friends will be arranged.
