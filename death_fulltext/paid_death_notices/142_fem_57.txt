
        GOLDFINCH-Elizabeth Canales. On November 2, 1998. Beloved mother Anne Goldfinch Locascio. Cherished grandmother of Lisa & Julia Locascio. She was a noted performer with the Robert Shaw Chorale whose career spanned both Europe and the United States. Mrs. Goldfinch was a distinguished member of one of the founding families of South Texas. A memorial service will be held in South Carolina at a later date.
