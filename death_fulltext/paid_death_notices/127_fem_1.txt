
        FLEISCHNER-Jeannette E. The Teachers College, Columbia University community mourns the loss of its dear friend and colleague, Jeannette E. Fleischner, professor of education. We will miss her greatly. Her more than 25 years of counsel, service, and leadership to the College and her distinguished contributions to the research and teaching of children with disabilities are an enduring legacy that we will celebrate in memorial of her. Arthur Levine, President
