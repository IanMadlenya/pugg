
        BRENNER-Frederick. Age 85, on April 25, 2003, of Greensboro, NC, formerly of New York City. Beloved husband (for 56 years) of Suzette Eisenberg Brenner; cherished father of Jayne Rande Gottlieb and Linda Joy Betheil; adored grandfather (''Poppi'') of Lauren and Melissa Betheil; father-in-law of Ed Gottlieb and Leslie Betheil; brother of Howard Brenner; brother-in-law of Helen Brenner, dear uncle of Jill Browne, Janet Brenner Maltby, Gary, Roy, and Robin Graham. Born on October 4, 1917, in Passaic, NJ, Mr. Brenner served in the U.S. Army during World War II. He was President of Brenzac Fibers until his retirement in the mid 1980's. Graveside service Sunday, April 27, at 11 AM, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, NY.
        BRENNER - Frederick. We mourn the loss of our dear friend with whom we spent so many wonderful times. Our love to Sue and the family. Sallie and Steve
