
        PRINCIPE-Thomas. The management and staff of North Bay Cadillac, Hempstead Jaguar and North Shore Infiniti mourn the death of their friend and colleague Thomas Principe. Tom brought a rich automotive heritage with him when he joined our firm over 15 years ago. His dedication to his customers and perseverance through times of challenge are an example for all. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Jane and his beautiful children Jennifer, Meghan, Victoria, John, and Christina.
