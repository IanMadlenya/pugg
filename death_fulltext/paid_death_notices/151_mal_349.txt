
        ANSORGE-Robert. Of New York City, Southbury, Ct. and Longboat Key, Fl., died August 29 at his home in Connecticut. Beloved husband of June (Leder), devoted father of Andrew and Laura (Koopman) Ansorge and Sally and Michael Mulvey and loving grandfather of Alexandra Pearl Mulvey. A gathering of friends to celebrate his life will be held Sunday, September 12 from 2 to 4PM at our Connecticut home. Donations may be sent to Visiting Nurse Service Hospice, Southbury, Ct.
