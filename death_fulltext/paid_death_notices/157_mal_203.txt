        LIFSCHUTZ-Ezekiel (1902-2000). The Board and staff of the YIVO Institute for Jewish Research mourn the passing of our friend and former Chief Archivist, Ezekiel Lifschutz, a distinguished member of our staff from 1954 to 1973. His work as an archivist, historian and teacher is an example to us all. May his memory be for a blessing. Bruce Slovin, Chairman Carl J. Rheins, Executive Director Marek Web, Chief Archivist
