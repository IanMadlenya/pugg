
        HALLE-Dr. Robert. Of London, England on September 20. Born Nuremberg, Germany, 1914. Loving and devoted husband of Edna. Beloved father of Margaret Sinclair (Walter), David Halle (Louise Mirrer), and Judith Afia (David). Doting and cherished grandfather to Julian, Debra, Emma, Philip, Carla, Malcolm, Joel and Jenny. Proud great-grandfather and friend to many. He will be remembered as a brilliant and caring man who took pride in the achievements of his children and grandchildren, and who was always available to help those in need.
