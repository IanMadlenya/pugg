
        MYRON-Joshua. Beloved husband of the late Sybil Myron. Cherished and devoted father of Naomi Scheuer. Loving grandfather of Marc Lubin, Eve Lubin, Abigail Scheuer and their spouses Robin Lubin, C.R. Jeffs and Ateatema. Passed on after 102 years spanning three centuries on two continents. Born in Rishon le Zion in todays Israel, he spent a lifetime of commitment to Israel and Jewish causes serving with heroism in the Jewish Legion during World War I. In 1920 he came to America to study, then married and became a pharmacist. Residing in Suffern, NY since 1938, he will be greatly missed by his family and his community. Services will be held at 12:30 on Sunday, June 11, at Congregation Sons of Israel, at 34 Montebello Road in Suffern. In lieu of flowers contributions in his honor may be sent to Congregation Sons of Israel.
