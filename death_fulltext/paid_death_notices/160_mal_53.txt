
        THOMPSON-Peter, one of New York's leading acting teachers, died April 24th at New York Hospital of complications following heart surgery. Mr. Thompson taught at Circle in the Square, NYU, and the Michael Howard Studios. As an actor, he appeared opposite Jane Alexander, Eva LeGallienne, Sada Thompson and Moses Gunn. He directed plays in New York and numerous regional theaters. Mr. Thompson was born in Carnegie, OK on March 28th, 1935. He received his BFA and MFA from the University of Oklahoma. He is survived by his partner, Bob Cosenza, and his brother, William Thompson of Oklahoma City. A memorial service will be held at the Michael Howard Studios, 152 West 25th St. on Friday, May 19th, at 3 PM.
