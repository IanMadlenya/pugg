
        GORDEVITCH--Igor, Publisher and resident of Pawling, NY, suddenly on March 24, 2004. He began his career as a reporter covering the Vatican and European affairs for the International Herald Tribune. He became Editor and later Publisher at Vision Inc., a publisher of Latin American business magazines. He was Publisher at Gruhner and Jahr USA for GEO magazine. He created and published IMPACT, US Investment Report and ECO, magazines on international business, investment and ecology. He is survived by his wife, Carin Gordevitch, his children, Alexandra Gordevitch Rota, Tatiana Gordevitch, Christopher D. Kirsten, Michelle K. Krepak and five grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations to Convent Novo Diveeno, 100 Smith Rd., Nanuet, NY 10954.
