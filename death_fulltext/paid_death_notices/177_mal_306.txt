
        JONES-Christopher D. 53. Of Huntington, NY, on September 11, 2001, at The World Trade Center. He worked at Cantor Fitzgerald where he was a partner. Born and raised in England, he graduated from Cambridge University, Fitzwilliam College in 1969. Beloved husband of thirty-one years of Susan, and loving father of Sarah and Lucy. A Memorial Service will be held at Old First Presbyterian Church, 125 Main Street, Huntington, NY, on Saturday October 27th at 2 P.M.
