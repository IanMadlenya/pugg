
        BOLOCAN--Dr. Hyam. The Department of Psychiatry of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and Weill Medical College of Cornell University regrets the passing of our esteemed and beloved faculty member, Dr. Hyam Bolocan, a much-loved teacher, an influential mentor, and a loyal sweet friend. He added immeasurably to our patients' care and our students' wisdom. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Dr. Jack D. Barchas Chairman Dr. Philip J. Wilner Medical Director
