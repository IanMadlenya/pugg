
        COWITT-Sylvia (Herschenhous). Of West Palm Beach, FL, on September 14, 2001. Born September 1, 1910, in New York City. Beloved wife of the late Jack Cowitt. Devoted mother of Bette Blankfein (Richard) and Dr. Owen Cowitt (Robin). Grandmother of Eric and Roger, Jeffrey and Hayley, and great-grandmother of Jason. Survived by her sisters Sally and Jean and her brother David. She will be forever remembered with love and admiration by her family and friends. The funeral took place on Sunday, September 16 at Gutterman's, Woodbury, L.I.
