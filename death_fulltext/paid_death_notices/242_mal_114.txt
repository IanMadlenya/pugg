
        KASINITZ--Julius On March 10, 2007 at age 79. Husband of Margaret Kasinitz of 50 years, father of Philip and Barry Kasinitz. Born in Brooklyn, he was the former CEO of Time Square stores and former president of Almay stores and Two Guys. Prior to that he was for many years Vice President of Caldor and had worked for S.Kliens. Private service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel at 1pm, Monday March 12th. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the American Heart Association.
