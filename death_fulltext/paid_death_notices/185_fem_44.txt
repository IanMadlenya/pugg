
        STEWART-Mary Buchanan. Of New York City, New York, died May 24, 2002 at home. She was 92 years of age. Born in New Brunswick, Canada. She was the daughter of the late Adam & Isabel (Grattan) Stewart. Mary worked for the General Foods Corporation for 38 years. She retired in 1975. She is survived by one sister Miss Elizabeth J. Stewart of New Brunswick, Canada, several nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held at a later date in Canada.
