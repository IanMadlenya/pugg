
        BRAUN-Shirley W. Daughter of the late Dean K. and Lucy T. Worcester, of New York City, died in Cambridge, MA, Thursday, June 20, 2002. Sister of Susan W. Light of Suffield, CT, and of the late D.K. Worcester Jr. and Lucy W. Chauncey. Predeceased by her husband, Dr. Edmund M. Braun, but survived by many nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held in the fall in New York City. Memorial gifts may be made to The Rector's Discretionary Fund, Church of the Epiphany, 1393 York Ave, New York, NY 10021.
