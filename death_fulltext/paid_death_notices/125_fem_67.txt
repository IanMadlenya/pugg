
        MARKMAN-Charlotte. June 9, 1997. She is survived by her husband Abe, her son Michael, her daughter-in-law Cassandra and grandson Tariq, aunt Ethel Montgomery and cousin Nan McCune and a host of dear and devoted relatives and friends. Charlotte was beloved on the Lower East Side and was a life long fighter for justice. Services Thursday, June 12, 1997, at 10 AM, at St. Augustine's Church, 290 Henry Street, NYC.
