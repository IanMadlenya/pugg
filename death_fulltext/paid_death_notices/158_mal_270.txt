
        HANFLING-Nathan. Congregation Machane Chodosh mourns the passing of its beloved member Nathan Hanfling, who was, and always will be remembered as a man of piety and goodness, modesty and generosity. We express our deepest sympathy to his bereaved wife, Felicia, and to his family. May Hashem grant them an abundance of comfort. Manfred Gans, Rabbi Herbert Jaffe, President
