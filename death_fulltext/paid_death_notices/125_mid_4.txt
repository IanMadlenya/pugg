
        ROSEN-Marlene. Died on Wednesday, June 11 in Sun City, Arizona. Beloved mother of Leslie and her husband David Fastenberg, Joel Rosen and his wife Kathleen, and Norman Rosen. Services on Friday, June 13, at 1 P.M., at Gutterman in Woodbury, New York. Memorial service on Sunday, June 21 at Congregation Beth Am, Solana Beach, California.
