
        HENZEL-Posie. Founder of Galleria La Serra in the D&D Building in NYC, died April 9. She was known internationally for her watercolor botanicals, a mainstay in her gallery, along with antique european prints. Her spunk, charm and love will be sorely missed in the interior design community and by her three children and sister. A memorial mass is set for 10am April 21, Our Lady's Chapel, St Patrick's Cathedral, NYC
