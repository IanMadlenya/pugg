
        LERNER - Alfred. The Boards, Faculty, and Staff of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital, Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons, and Weill Cornell Medical College mourn the passing of Alfred Lerner, a member since 1998 of the Hospital's Board of Trustees. Mr. Lerner, a uniquely brilliant and generous man with broad perspective and insight, was a prominent businessman and valued friend. We extend our sincere condolences to his family.
