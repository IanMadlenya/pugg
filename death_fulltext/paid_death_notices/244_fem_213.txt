
        PLASSE--Sherley. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Howard A. Stecker, Sr. Rabbi Hon. Richard Kestenbaum, President $(6$)PLASSE--Sherley Pearl (Puner), 89 years. Beloved wife, sister, cousin, mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother, died peacefully on May 27. Sherley was a leader in her community, serving as President of the Great Neck Chapter of Hadassah and President of the Temple Israel of Great Neck Sisterhood. She was also devoted to the work of American Professors for Peace in the Middle East. Above all, Sherley was the loving matriarch of the family, reaching out to each generation with her elegant, generous heart. She was beloved by all whose lives she touched. Funeral services will be held at 12 noon on Tuesday May 29 at the Riverside Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hadassah College Jerusalem care of Hadassah 50 West 58th Street, New York, NY 10019. $(6$)PLASSE--Sherley. The Partners and Staff of Labaton Sucharow & Rudoff LLP express their deepest sympathy to their Partner Jonathan Plasse on the passing of his mother, Sherley Plasse, and we extend our condolences to her sons Terry and Andrew, her grandchildren and greatgrandchildren.
