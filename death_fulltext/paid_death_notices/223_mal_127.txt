
        BUCHBAND--G. Peter, 76, of Port Washington and Southampton, LI, died on August 12, 2005 of complications from multiple myeloma. Devoted husband of 43 years of Wally Goldstein Buchband. Loving father of Richard (Betsy) and William (Jean). Adoring grandfather of Hannah, Celia, Ethan, Mia and Drew. Born in Vienna and educated at Columbia and Purdue, Peter had a long career as a systems engineer, consultant, and cofounder of the Garden City Group, Melville, LI. He will be remembered as a generous husband, father, friend, mentor, business leader and golf partner. Services are on Sunday, August 14 at 11AM at The Reconstructionist Synagogue of the North Shore, 1001 Plandome Road, Plandome, LI. Contributions may be made in Peter's name to the Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation, 51 Locust Ave, New Canaan, CT. 06840.
        BUCHBAND--G. Peter . The Garden City Group Inc. deeply mourns the passing of Peter Buchband. Peter was a founder of our company and served with distinction as its first President. Peter was universally admired for his leadership, foresight and wisdom. Peter will always be remembered as a caring and generous friend who was deeply concerned about the welfare of all our employees. He will be greatly missed. Our thoughts, prayers and deepest sympathies are with his wife Wally, children and grandchildren. Michael J. Sherin, Chairman David Isaac, President Neil Zola, E.V.P. & C.O.O.
        BUCHBAND--Peter. The Noyac Golf Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member, Peter Buchband, and extends sincere sympathy to his wife Wally, and other members of his family. Ted Ronick, President Richard Mathew, Secretary
