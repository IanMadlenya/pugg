
        MAXON--Yetta. Dearly loved wife, mother, grandmother, great - grandmother, and mother-in-law. Died on October 8, 2004, at age 89, after a long struggle with Alzheimer's disease. Born and raised and lived almost all her life in Port Chester, New York. She was the salutatorian of her graduating class at Port Chester High School and a life member of Hadassah. She spent years in business and then devoted herself to her passion in life--her family. She was a vibrant, caring woman who will be remembered for her kindness and generosity to all. She could not do enough for others. She leaves her husband of 66 years, Ralph, of Boynton Beach, Florida, son, Andrew and wife Carol, of Stamford, Connecticut, daughters, Barbara Benerofe of Purchase and Ilene Finder and husband Ken, of Rye Brook, grandchildren, Jodi Gubner, Jeffrey Maxon, Jamie Cohen, Craig Benerofe, Samantha Benerofe, Gary Benerofe, Barry Finder and Marc Finder and great-grandchildren Jacob, Sydney and Gabrielle. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 919 N. Michigan Avenue, Suite 1100, Chicago, Illinois 60611-1676. Funeral will be held on Sunday, October 10, at 10:30 am, at Congregation Knesses Tifereth Israel, 575 King Street, Port Chester, NY.
