
        CORY--David. 76, of New Rochelle, NY. Passed away peacefully June 30, 2004. Dear husband and lover to Sally A. Cory, father to Leslie and Robert Cory, Lucy Landesman Halperin, Paul and Peter Landesman. Beloved fatherin-law and grandfather. President and Founder of Tee Pee Olives Inc., Italica Imports and the H.B. Hunter Company. A world renowned expert on Spanish olives and Mediterranean olive oil. A benefactor to numerous charities and a generous and caring mentor to his employees. ''Un fuerte abrazo'' from his family and friends. Funeral service 3PM, Thursday July 1st, Sharon Gardens Cemetery Valhalla, NY. For information 914-723-6600
