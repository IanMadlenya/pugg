
        BERGER-Chaya Rifka. The Officers and members of the New Synagogue of Fort Lee mourn the loss of our beloved rabbi's mother. Born in 1911 in Jerusalem, she will be interred there alongside her beloved husband, Rabbi Baruch Berger. She was from two of the oldest and most prestigious families in Jerusalem, the Eisenbachs and the Hamburgers. Survived by her children, Miriam and Louis Fruchter, Rabbi Meir and Miriam, Samuel and Ronnee, Isaac, Cantor Leon and Francine, Rabbi Moshe and Karen, eight grandchldren and six great grandchildren. Donations may be made to the New Synagogue of Fort Lee, 1585 Center Ave., Fort Lee, NJ 07024, or JOY, Jewish Outreach and Intervention.
