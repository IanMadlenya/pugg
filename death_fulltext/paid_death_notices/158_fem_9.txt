
        FUCHS-Rhoda, died peacefully on March 1st. Beloved wife of Lazar. Loving mother of Linda and Yehuda Isaacs, Alan and Joan Fuchs, Tzivey and Arthur Friedman, Rachel and Abe Abittan.
