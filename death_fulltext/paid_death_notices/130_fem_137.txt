
        MCGUIRE-Lillian J. On November 18, 1997. Wife of the late Harold F. McGuire. Mother of Harold and Bartlett McGuire. Grandmother of Christopher, Katherine and Daniel. Sister of Mrs. Johnson D. Hill, Jr. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street, Saturday, November 22 at 11:30 A.M. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to The Visiting Nurse Service of New York.
