
        APPEL-Gloria W. The Board of Trustees, the Officers and Staff of Babson College mourn the passing of a distinguished member of our Board. Gloria Appel will be remembered for her spontaneous initiative and thoughtful insight in moving the Women's Leadership Program forward, and for the generosity of the Price Institute in support of Babson's Entrepreneurial Studies program. Our deepest condolences are extended to her family and friends. Brian M. Barefoot, Chairman of the Board of Trustees
