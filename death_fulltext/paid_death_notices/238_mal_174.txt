
        MIRA-Y-LOPEZ--Rafael. The Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation mourns the untimely passing of Rafael Mira-y-Lopez, M.D., Ph.D., Associate Professor of Medicine at Mount Sinai School of Medicine and an esteemed member of the Foundation's Institute Without Walls. A renowned scientist at the peak of his career developing animal models for breast cancer, his humanistic qualities will be forever remembered. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his family. Michael Nierenberg, Chairman of the Board of Directors Samuel Waxman, M.D., Scientific Director Merle Duskin Kailas, Executive Director
