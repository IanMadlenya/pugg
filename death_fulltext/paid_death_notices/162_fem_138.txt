
        EGOLF-Suzanne (nee Bragg). 61, of Tucson, AZ. Died on July 14, 2000 of an accidental drowning. Born and raised in Harrison, New York, graduated Harrison High School 1956, Northwestern University 1960. Survived by husband James Egolf, daughter Lisa Kraas, son Kevin Kraas and her brothers, Spike and Mike Bragg.
