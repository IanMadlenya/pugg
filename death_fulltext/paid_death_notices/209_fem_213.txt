
        GLATTSTEIN--Hanna. December 24, 1924-June 26, 2004. The Young Israel of Brooklyn mourns the passing of our last Secretary - Treasurer. Hanna Z'L survived Aushwitz, remained dedicated to Torah education, and was an ILGWU union activist. Our heartfelt condolences go out to her son, grandchildren, family and friends in the Williamsburg community. Teeyah Zicrah Baruch. Bernard Weinberger, Rabbi
