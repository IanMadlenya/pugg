
        LANGA-Harry, Dr. His family, many friends and colleagues mourn the passing of Dr. Harry Langa on Wednesday, September 29. He was the source and recipient of much love from his beloved wife Evelyn as father, brother, grandfather and great-grandfather. He is survived by his loving wife Evelyn, his children Robert, Roz, Steven; his step-children Ira and Michael Rubin; grandfather of Jeffrey, Kenneth, Laurie, Michael, Danny, Beth, Joel, Jocelyn, Nicole and Jared, his great-grandchildren, and his sister Nell Seifer. He was the author of the book ''Relative Analgesia in Dental Practice'' and a teacher and lecturer all over the world. Funeral service will be at the Schwartz Brothers Funeral Home, Queens Blvd and 76th Road, Sunday, October 3, 9:30AM. All donations are to go the the Dr. Harry Langa Scholarship Fund at the N.Y. University Dental School. 345 E. 24 ST. ATT: Suzanne Ryer
