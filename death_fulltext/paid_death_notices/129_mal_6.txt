
        NADAL-Carlos G., at Southampton, N.Y., on September 28, 1997. Mr. Nadal was born in New YorK City on January 29, 1912, and was highly decorated during his service with the U.S. Marine Corps in World War II. In 1946 he founded Nadal & Baxendale, Inc., a general contracting firm, and was active in construction in New York City, Florida and the Cayman Islands. He retired to Southampton in 1977, where he served on the Southampton Village Zoning Board of Appeals & the Southampton Town Open Space Advisory Committee, in addition to doing volunteer work at the Southampton Hospital. He is survived by three sons, Gerrit L. Lansing of Greenwich, CT.; David W. Nadal of Southampton, N.Y.; and Dr. Evan C. Nadal of East Hampton, N.Y.; and a brother, Gordon Nadal Morford of Palm Beach, FL. A memorial service will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church in Southampton, Saturday, October 4th, at 11:00 A.M. Interment will be private.
