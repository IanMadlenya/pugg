
        SALTON--Justine Augusta. The Horace Mann School is deeply saddened by the sudden death of Justine Salton '90. Her kindness, her sense of humor and thoughtfulness toward her friends and Horace Mann School are acknowledged by all. Our heartfelt condolences go to her husband, Raphael Allison, her mother, Edith Salton, her sister, Gillian Salton '86, her brother, William Salton, and the rest of her family. She will be greatly missed. Dr. Thomas M. Kelly Head of School Robert J. Katz '65 Chair, Board of Trustees
