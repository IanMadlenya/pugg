
        DEAULT -- Mary Metcalf, Longmont, CO, died May 20th, 2005. Graduate of University of Colorado, Boulder, Phi Beta Kappa, Chinese Studies; Master's Library Science. Wife of Monty Deault, mother of Matthew and Mark. Beloved daughter of Manton and Tray Metcalf. Beloved sister of Teresa Metcalf, Lili Perez, John Metcalf and the late Manton Metcalf. She will be greatly missed. Memorial service to be held at Chautauqua Park, Boulder, CO, 5 PM on May 30th.
