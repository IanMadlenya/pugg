
        WEIXEL--David, 91, on December 31, 2006. Beloved husband of his late wife, Lola. Loving father of Miriam and Eugene (Marbella); grandfather of Joanna, Rose and Goldie (Chad); step-grandfather of Yorknewsa and Zadaai; brother, uncle, greatuncle and friend to many. A soldier during WWII, he was an infantryman on the Anzio Beachhead and later worked in combat zones as a reconnaissance car crewman, making topographical sketches of terrain and using his knowledge of French, Italian and German to interrogate prisoners. In civilian life, he was a film editor, artist and sculptor. We will miss this brave and gentle man. Memorial service to be announced.
