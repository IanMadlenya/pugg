
        DALRYMPLE-Jean. The Board of Directors of the American Theatre Wing is deeply saddened by the death of their cherished friend and beloved, long-time officer. After an association of 58 years, her words of knowledge and wisdom sadly are no longer heard. Among her other contributions, she was well known as a comoderator on the Wing's long-running ''Working In The Theatre'' Seminars. She will be remembered for her wit, insights and love of theatre. Isabelle Stevenson Chairwoman of the Board American Theatre Wing Roy A. Somlyo President
