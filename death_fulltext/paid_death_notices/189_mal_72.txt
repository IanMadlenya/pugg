
        SCHILLER-Edward H., 80. Of Sarasota, FL, formerly of Roslyn Heights, Long Island, and Santa Barbara, CA., died in Sarasota. Boy's High, WWII Trailblazers, consummate teacher, (Professor of History, Nassau and Santa Barbara Community Colleges) with a passion for the ''world of ideas,'' social justice, humanistic values, a man full of love, warmth and wit. Wife of 47 years, Roselle Lind, died 1991 in Santa Barbara. Survived by second wife, Evvie Gross. A loving husband, father, grandfather, brother, and devoted friend, he is deeply missed.
