
        BROMBERG - Rabbi Ken neth. We mourn with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. After serving as chaplain in the US Air Force, he served several rabbinic and educational positions before becoming rabbi of Congregation Beth Shalom in Clearwater, FL where he retired as rabbi emeritus in 1995. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Johanna, children, Naomi Bar-Yam, Efrem, and Hillel, and to all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Reuven Hammer, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Executive Vice President The Rabbinical Assembly
