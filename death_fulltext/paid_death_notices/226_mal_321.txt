
        BLACK--Donald Miller. Passed away on Nov. 6, 2005 in Redding, CT. Born Denison, TX. April 18, 1922. Husband of Elizabeth for 58 years. Graduate of RPI (BS 1942), recruited to the Manhattan Project from MIT to which he returned after W.W. II (PhD 1947 and Sigma Xi Honorary Society); then embarked on a 40 year career in the chemical industry. Survived by his wife, sister Barbara Boyd, sons Donald and Thomas, daughters Holly Grillo and Barbara Quinn, nephew John Boyd, niece Nina Mahaffey, nine grandchildren and one great grandchild.
