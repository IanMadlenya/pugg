
        MALONEY-William Gerard. Investment banker, philanthropist of New Canaan, CT died on May 5, 2003. Survived by two sons, William J. Maloney of Ft. Meyers, FL and Stephen G. Maloney of Stamford, CT, a daughter, Elizabeth A. Coletti of Newtown, CT and five grandchildren. He was predeceased by his wife Katherine Kenney Maloney and his brother Howard. Funeral Mass Thursday, May 8, 2003, St. Aloysius Church, Cherry St., New Canaan, CT. Interment, Lakeview Cemetery. Visitation 4 to 8 PM Wednesday (this evening) in the Hoyt Funeral Home, 199 Main St., New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to St. Camillus Health Care Center, 494 Elm Street, Stamford, CT. 06902.
        MALONEY-William G. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of William Maloney, former member of the Board of Trustees. Consumate Christian gentleman, passionate about the mission of the College, Bill brought his faith, wisdom and generosity to the College and we are stronger for it. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D., President Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU, Chair, Bd of Trustees
