
        STARKEY-George W.B., MD, 81, a resident of Brookline, MA and Norfolk, CT, died February 5 in Boston. He was a graduate of Trinity College and Harvard Medical School. A thoracic surgeon, he was Associate Clinical Professor of Surgery at Harvard Medical School. In WWII, he served as a battalion surgeon. He leaves his wife Lois (MacMurray); two daughters, Joan S. Palmer and Alison Starkey Morozovsky; son, Hugh Starkey; and five grandchildren. Memorial service 3pm Feb 18, in Memorial Church at Harvard University, Cambridge, MA; reception to follow at the Harvard Faculty Club. Donations made to the Grace B. Starkey Scholarship Fund, C/O Trinity College, 300 Summit Street, Hartford, CT. 06106.
