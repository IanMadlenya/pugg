
        SIMON-Marion, 63. Of Riverdale, N.Y. On October 8, 1999 after three years of struggling against Melanoma. Devoted sister of Gabrielle Lefer of New York and adoring aunt to Michael Lefer. Dedicated and loyal friend. Retired teacher of 25 years in the Yonkers public school system. Painter of great integrity and depth. We shall remember her for her humanity and expansiveness of spirit. She shall be with us always. Services at Mount Hope Cemetery, Oct.12th.L
