
        ZELNER--Burton L. On January 13 at 84. After a courageous struggle with Parkinson's Disease. Beloved Husband of 56 years to Shirley. Loving Father to Barbara, Carole and her husband Seth Margolis, and Michael. Adoring grandfather to Jack and Margie. Dear brother of Audrey. World War II veteran. Devoted and loving career in Advertising and Publishing. Memorial Contributions may be made to Parkinson's Disease Foundation. Funeral Service Sunday, 12:30 PM Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Rd, Scarsdale, NY
