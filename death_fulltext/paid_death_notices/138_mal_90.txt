
        SCHUCK-George Jacob, Jr. The students, faculty, administration and staff of the Harry Van Arsdale, Jr. School of Labor Studies of Empire State College, SUNY mourn the loss of George Schuck, Jr., a staunch supporter and friend of labor education and Chairman of the Joint Industry Board of the Electrical Industry since July of 1986. His advice, good counsel, warmth and genuine interest in working students, and his expertise in the electrical industry will be missed by all who benefitted from his substantive support to workers and worker education. We extend our sympathies to his wife Jean and his entire family. Dr. Jane Altes, President, Empire State College, SUNY. Dr. Marie Wittek, Dean, The Harry Van Arsdale Jr. School of Labor Studies.
