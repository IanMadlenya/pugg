
        BRAVERMAN-Donald P. On March 20, 1999. Loving and devoted husband of Meryl. Loving son of Edna and the late Theodore. Nephew of Lillian Berkal and Dr. Lawrence Ames. Brother of Arline and Irwin. Beloved father of Peter, Jill and Andrew, Rhonda and Arnie, and Mark and Barbara. Loving and proud grandfather of Sandy, Felice, Melissa and Marc. He was the Vice President of Salomon Smith Barney. He served proudly in the Korean War and was Lieutenant J.G., serving on the North Wind. He will be missed by all who loved him. Funeral service 12:00, Monday, at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        BRAVERMAN - Donald. Sutton Place Synagogue extends profound sympathies to Meryl Braverman on the loss of her beloved husband and our longtime member. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
