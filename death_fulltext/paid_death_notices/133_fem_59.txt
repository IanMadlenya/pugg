
        DAVIDSON-Alice, (nee Gordon) 86, mother of CTG Ahmanson Theatre and Mark Taper Forum Artistic Director/Producer Gordon Davidson, passed away January 28, 1998, in Culver City, CA, of a heart attack. Formerly of Brooklyn, New York. The beloved wife of the late Professor Jo ''Skipper'' Davidson, she is survived by loving sons (in addition to Gordon) Michael and Robert, daughters-inlaw Judi, Karen and Maureen, as well as grandchildren Adam, Rachel, Jesse and Kate. Donations may be made to the Jo Davidson Memorial Fund, C/O Brooklyn College Foundation, Inc., or to Center Theatre Group of Los Angeles.
