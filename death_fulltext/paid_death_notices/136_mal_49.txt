
        OECHLER-Charles. On April 29, 1998, after a short illness. He is survived by a brother, Willima F. Oechler of Cincinnati, a sister-inlaw Convere Oechler of New York City, two nephews, Hugh McA. Oechler of Cambridge, MA., and Henry J. Oechler, Jr. of Princeton, New Jersey, & a niece, Katherine O. Whitbeck of New York City. A memorial service will be held at the Brick Presbyterian Church at 4P.M., on Friday, May 8.
