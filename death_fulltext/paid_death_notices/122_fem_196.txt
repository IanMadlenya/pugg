
        ECHLIN-Letitia, on March 1, aged 85 years, in Mexico. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Francis A. Echlin. Loving mother of Vals Osborne of New York, Diana Gillam of London, and Johanna Echlin Bastian of Montreal. Cherished grandmother of Tony, Alessandra, Letitia, Luke, Jane, and Jason. A Memorial Fund has been established in her name, in support of theatre arts and environmental conservation. (212-727-7861).
