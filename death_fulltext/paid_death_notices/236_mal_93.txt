
        PETERFREUND - Stanley. Died September 7, 2006 at the age of 80. Beloved husband of Eve for 57 years; loving father of Nancy, Lynn (Nick) and Alan (Cathy); grandfather of Rebecca, Sean, Ezra, and Sam; and brother of Sally. His work over five decades as a management consultant had a significant impact on a generation of employees by focusing on the quality of work life.
