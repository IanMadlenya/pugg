
        CASTELLI-Leo. The Trustees and staff of The Museum of Modern Art, are deeply saddened by the death of long-time friend and Honorary Trustee, Leo Castelli. His commitment to an extraordinary group of contemporary artists, fostered through his gallery, is legendary and secured his unique place as an art dealer of great vision, taste, and humanity. We will long remember his warmth and generosity, and we offer our profound sympathy to his family. Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, NY
        CASTELLI-Leo. On the passing of Leo Castelli, the Trustees and Staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art express our sympathy to his family and our enormous gratitude for his passionate support of American art and artists. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Joel S. Ehrenkranz, President Maxwell L. Anderson, Director
        CASTELLI-Leo. The Trustees and staff of The Aldrich Museum of Contemporary Art celebrate the life of Leo Castelli, a cultural icon and true friend of contemporary art. In 1995, he was the first recipient of the Museum's Larry Award honoring his lifetime of distinguished service to contemporary art.
