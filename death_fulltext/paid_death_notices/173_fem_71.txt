
        SYLVESTER-Clara (nee Condeles). On June 5, 2001. Survived by her beloved husband Al and devoted sister Yorka C. Linakis retired Justice New York State Supreme Court. Clara was a remarkable lady whose quiet elegance, dry wit and warm spirit will always be remembered. She was a devout member of St. Demetrios G.O. Church, Jamaica, N.Y. and Leadership 100 of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocese. Reposing at the Frederick Funeral Home of Flushing, Northern Blvd at 193rd. St. Visiting hours Thursday 2-5 & 7-10pm. Funeral service Friday St. Demetrios G.O. Church Jamaica at 11:30am. Contributions may be made to The St. Demetrios G.O. Church, Jamaica, N.Y. or St. Michaels Home for the Aged, Yonkers, New York.
