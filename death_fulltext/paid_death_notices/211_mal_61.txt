
        LEONG--Kit, on Saturday, July 31, 2004 at Raritan Bay Medical Center, Old Bridge, NJ, in his 81st year. Beloved husband of Ajah, devoted father of Alawiah Yap and Lynn Leong Little. Loved and missed by grandchildren Sharon Aeria, Jeffrey Yap, Jennifer Yap, Frankie Yap, greatgranddaughter Madeline Yap, sons-in-law Vernon Yap and Michael Little, and granddaughter-in-law Michelle Yap. ''Break, break, break on thy cold gray crags, O Sea!/But oh for the touch of a vanished hand/And the sound of a voice that will never come back to me.''
