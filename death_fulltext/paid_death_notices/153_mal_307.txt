
        SUMERGRADE-Saul R. On October 23, 1999, after a brief illness, at the age of 93. Beloved husband of Alice, devoted father of Nancy and Donald Mutterperl, and Michael and Patti Sumergrade; adoring grandfather of John and Heather Mutterperl, Jeffrey Mutterperl, Jane and Jim Webster, and Jenny Sumergrade; dear brother of the late Herman, Harry and Byrde. He always had a twinkle in his eye and a smile for his loving family and friends. Family will receive friends Tuesday, 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, Wednesday, 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions to Helen Keller Services for the Blind, or your favorite charity.
        SUMERGRADE-Saul. My deepest sympathy to his wife, Alice, and the rest of his family. He was a wonderful man and will be greatly missed. Sadly, Page Morton Black
