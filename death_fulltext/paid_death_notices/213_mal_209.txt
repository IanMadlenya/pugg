
        CHOLST--Shelly, Dr. Humanist, psychiatrist, poet and artist, passed away peacefully on October 18, 2004. Surviving him are his daughter Ina and his two sons David and Tony. Also surviving are a brother Dr. Mortimer Cholst and two sisters Paula Levitt and Betty Cholst. He also leaves seven grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. Funeral service Wednesday, October 20 at 10AM at Plaza Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., at 91st St., Manhattan.
