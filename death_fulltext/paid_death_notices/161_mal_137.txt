
        HARLEY-Donald J. On June 11, 2000. Retired City Marshal. Life Member of the Society of The Friendly Sons of Saint Patrick. Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Breezy Point Surf Club. Beloved husband of Mary R. (nee Tighe). Cherished uncle. Funeral Wed., 9:15AM from the Marine Park Funeral Home, 3024 Quentin Road, Bklyn. Mass Resurrection R.C. Church, 10:15AM. Interment Calverton National Cemetery.
        HARLEY-Donald J. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York records with profound sorrow the death of Donald J. Harley, a Life Member of the Society, and former Chairman of the Board of Stewards and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Timothy G. Reynolds President Henry F. White Jr. Historian
