
        ENGEL-David E. age 71, died in Boston of cancer. Former President of the Pittsburgh Board of Education. A professor emeritus of the University of Pittsburgh. He was ordained to the ministry of the Presbyterian Church, USA. Burial will be in Southwest Harbor, Maine. A memorial service in Heinz Chapel, U. of P., Monday, December 20 at 11 a.m. For information Fernald Funeral Chapel, Mt. Desert, ME.
