
        LOW-Arthur J. On November 12, 2001. Beloved husband of Marjorie. Loving father of Peter and Peggy Low, and Babette R. Low, C.S.W., and Alan Lintz. Adoring grandfather of Amy and David Chasen and John Low. Retired long-time member of the New York Stock Exchange and pasthistorian of its Buttonwood Club. Services are today 12:45 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. You will be in our hearts forever.
        LOW-Arthur J. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Arthur J. Low our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        LOW-Arthur J. The offices and members of the Buttonwood Club of the NYSE, with deep sorrow, mourn the passing of Arthur Low, a dear friend, a guide, and our longtime Club Historian.
