
        CARTY--Thomas F. on January 6, 2004 at the age of 85. Dear brother to Joseph and the best friend he ever had. Thomas was a proud W.W. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. For information, contact Riverdale-on-Hudson Funeral Home, Riverdale, NY.
