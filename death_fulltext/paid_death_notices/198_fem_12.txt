
        COHEN -- Hermaine Kurtz, age 82, of Edina, formerly of New York City, died June 28, 2003. Preceded in death by parents, Irwin and Josephine (Herzbrun) Kurtz; husband, New York State Supreme Court Justice, Charles H. Cohen; sisters, Babette Kurtz and Eve Kurtz Rackow. Survived by sons, Jonathan (Barbara) of Prior Lake, MN and Harlan (Patricia Salas) of Washington, DC; grand daughters Ilana, Aliza and Julia. ''Hummy'' was a loving and generous wife, mother and grandmother, a world traveler, a gourmet cook, an expert needlewoman and a loyal friend to many. She will be greatly missed. >>AD#
