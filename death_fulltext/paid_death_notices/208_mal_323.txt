
        COFFEE--Donn. Suddenly, at his home, on May 24. Beloved husband of Toni, adored and adoring father of Peter, Susan, and Eve (Jeffers) and grandfather of six. Also survived by his five brothers and sisters. Next to his family, he loved Columbia best; contributions to the Columbia College Fund would be welcome. Memorial service Thursday, June 3 at 2 P.M. at St. Paul's Chapel, Columbia University; reception in Van Am Quadrangle.
