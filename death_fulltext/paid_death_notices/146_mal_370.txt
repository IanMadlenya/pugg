
        KINIGSBERG-Leo. Age 90, died on March 17. Beloved husband of the late Billie Isabelle and devoted father of Renee Sacks and Frederick Kinigsberg. Loving grandfather of Justin, Allison, Mark, Lara, Eric and Cory. Father-in-law to Jerry Greenberg and Wendy Kinigsberg. Good friend and companion to Nettie Lostfogel. Funeral services today at 12:30pm at Boulevard Riverside Chapel, 312 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn, New York. Born in Lodz, Poland in 1908, he retired from a successful leather manufacturing business at the age of 84. With his family, he shared the lessons of his lifes' challenges with great dignity. He will always be remembered for his courage and his love.
