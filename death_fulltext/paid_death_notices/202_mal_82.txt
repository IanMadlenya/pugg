
        COOPER--Peter B., on November 9, 2003 in Upper Montclair, NJ. He served the community as a Judge of the Superior Court of New Jersey, practicing lawyer, Councilman and Mayor of the Township of Livingston, NJ. Memorial Service Tuesday, November 11, 2003 at Temple Emanuel, Livingston, NJ at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations will be accepted by the Essex County Bar Association, in order to establish a fund in Judge Cooper's name to promote compassion and equity in probate practice. Please make donations to the ''Peter Cooper Trust Account''. Bernheim Apter Kreitzman Suburban Funeral Chapel, Livingston, NJ.
