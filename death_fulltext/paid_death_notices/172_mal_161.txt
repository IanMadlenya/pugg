
        SUSSMAN-Ralph M. M.D. Former Chairman of the Department of Cardiology at Doctor's Hospital. He served the Manhattan community as a caring physician for 60 years. Beloved husband of Gertrude and the late Frances Goldberg. Devoted father of Elizabeth Anne Socolow and the late Victoria Sussman and step-children, Elizabeth Stevens and Henry Gottlieb. Loving grandfather of seven grandchildren. Services Tuesday 12 noon at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice, Beth Israel Medical Center, 1st Ave at 16th St., NY, NY 10003.
        SUSSMAN-Ralph M., M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our dear friend and devoted colleague, Ralph M. Sussman, M.D., former Chief of Cardiology at Doctors Hospital (which became a part of Beth Israel in 1987 and now serves as The Medical Center's Singer Division), and a long time member of The Hospital's Medical Staff. He provided excellent clinical care to the people of New York for more than 60 years. To his wife, Gertrude, his family, and all who loved him, we extend heartfelt condolences. Morton P.
