
        PERKEL-Mickey. Beloved father, grandfather, great-grandfather. Died June 16. He was 76 years old. He loved his family and friends and they loved him. He cared, devoting much time and energy to many social causes throughout his life. He was a veteran of World War II and a prisoner of war for two years. In recent years he was very active in the Brooklyn Key Chapter of the American Ex-Prisoners of War. He was greatly loved and missed by many, especially his children, Amy McCarthy and Charles Perkel, and grandchildren Rachel and Sarah, Josh, Maggie and Ben.
