
        WINSTON-Sharon. Age 72, of Ridgewood, NJ, on August 8, 2001, of neurological complications of Alzheimer's disease. Beloved mother of Doug, Randy and Matt; Beloved grandmother of Dustin, Jenna & Paige. Sharon graduated Summa Cum Laude from William Paterson in 1967, later obtaining her Master's from NYU. She served with distinction as an English teacher at Paramus High School from 1967-1994 and at the International School in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia 1978-'79, 1983-'84. Funeral August 12 at ''GuttermanMusicant'' Hackensack, NJ at 2PM
