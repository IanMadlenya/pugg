
        HARRISON-Milton S. A resident of Briarcliff Manor, died December 30th at the age of 94. Predeceased by his first wife Joan, his second wife Marjorie and his third wife Barbara. Survivors include two sons Michael and Joseph Harrison, his step-children Peter and Ellen Abrams, Emily Loeb and Nancy Novick, a grandson Nathan Harrison and a brother Eugene Bablove. He was a retired White Plains attorney. Graveside service at Westchester Hills Cemetery. Call Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 914-949-0566 for details.
        HARRISON-Milton. Congregation Kol Ami records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member, Milton Harrison, and conveys sincere condolences to his bereaved family. Rabbi Thomas K. Weiner Rabbi Shira Milgrom Cantor Annie Bornstein Ken Simon, President
