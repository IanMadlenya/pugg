
        GRAY--Mary Washburn. Age 89, of New York City, died on April 27, 2006. Survived by her sons, Stephen V. Gray and Samuel O. Gray, and her daughter, Pauline Gray, and by five grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Mother of David Augustus Clarkson Gray, who died in 1975. She was the daughter of Pauline Clarkson Washburn and Frank B. Washburn and a graduate of The Brearley School (where she later taught) and Radcliffe College ('37) (summa cum laude). She was the wife of John Davis Gray, an attorney, who died in 1991. There will be a private memorial service set for late summer in Tivoli, NY.
