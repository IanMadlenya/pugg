
        KALIK--Edward L., 79, died September 22. The World War II veteran was born and raised in New York. Since 1966, he lived in St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands. He is survived by his wife, Margaret; daughters, Barbara Kalik and Linda Spaisman; sister, Irene Zeidman; stepdaughter Kathy Lindsay; stepson James Curran; grandchildren Amy and Mark Spaisman; step-grandchildren Sean and Ryan Curran; sister-in-law, Joanne Kalik and many other relatives and friends. He was preceded in death by his brother, Irwin Kalik. Services will be at 2:45 P.M. Friday, September 30 at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah at Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, N.J.
