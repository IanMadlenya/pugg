
        ZEILER--Miriam (Micky), on October 21, 2004, at age 91. Beloved wife of the late Norman Zeiler. Loving mother of Nancy Coplon and John Zeiler. Adored grandmother of Benjamin Zeiler Coplon and Jonathan David Coplon. Dear aunt of Eve France and Joan Minchen. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81 Street, Sunday 11:30am. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Police Athletic League or M.S. Society.
        ZEILER--Miriam. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Miriam Zeiler. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
