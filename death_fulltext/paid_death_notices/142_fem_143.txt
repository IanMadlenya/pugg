
        URSTADT-Claire Jordan, 96, a long-time resident of Mamaroneck, New York, on November 14th, 1998. Widow of the late Charles G. Urstadt and beloved mother of Charles J. Urstadt of Bronxville, NY and J. Jeffrey Urstadt of New Canaan, CT. She was the grandmother of Charles D. Urstadt, Catherine U. Biddle, Bryant E. Urstadt, J. Jordan Urstadt and Elizabeth C. Urstadt, and greatgrandmother of Elinor and Dana Biddle. Interment will be private. Contributions in her memory may be made to Dartmouth College, Hanover, New Hampshire.
