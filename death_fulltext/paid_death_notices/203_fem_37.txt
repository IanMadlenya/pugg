
        SULLIVAN--Kathleen V. (nee Dwyer), of Riverdale, died peacefully at her home on December 5, 2003. She was born in Stone Park, Co. Roscommon, Ireland, one of four sisters and a brother, all but one of whom (Annie Mahon of Emlanagree, Donamon, Co. Roscommon, Ireland) have preceded her in death. Beloved wife of the late Patrick J. Sullivan who was born in Gorth, Eyeries, Co. Cork, Ireland. Cherished mother of three grateful daughters: Sr. Kathleen Sullivan of Blauvelt, NY, Josephine Sullivan of Riverdale and Maureen Hawkins of Seattle, WA. Beloved grandmother of Quinn and Nora Hawkins and dear mother-in-law to David Hawkins. She also leaves behind many nieces and nephews in the U.S. and Ireland. She will be greatly missed and always remembered lovingly. Reposing Sunday and Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, at Riverdaleon-Hudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Ave., Bronx. Mass of Christian Burial 9:45 AM, Tuesday, at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment Gate of Heaven. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be made to Dominican College, Orangeburg, NY.
