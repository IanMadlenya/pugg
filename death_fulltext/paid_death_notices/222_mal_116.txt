
        FREYTAG--Richard A. Citicorp Banker, of Wilmington, DE, age 71, died on July 4, 2005. He retired in 1996 as President and CEO of Citicorp Banking Corporation, New Castle, DE after 34 years service with the bank and he retired from the U.S. Air Force with the rank of Major General after 38 years service. Survivors include his beloved wife, Pamela; son, Richard; daughter, Bliss Smith; 2 grandchildren; brother, Donald; sister, Dotti Howe. Friends and associates are invited to attend a military funeral service at 3PM, July 19 at Bolling Air Force Base, Chapel #
