
        ANSCHER-Bernard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are saddened by the passing of Bernard Anscher, beloved father of our dear friend Marlene Herring. Marlene is a committed member of UJA-Federation's Manhattan Women's Campaign and serves on the Women's Campaign Major Gifts Cabinet. Marlene's generosity and leadership touch countless people throughout the Jewish community. We extend heartfelt sympathies to Marlene and her husband, Paul, their children, and the entire Anscher family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay Exec. V.P. and C.E.O. Joan Ginsburg Women's Campaign Chair
