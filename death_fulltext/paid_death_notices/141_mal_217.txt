
        GREATHOUSE-Timothy D. Age 48, at his home in Manhattan on October 18, 1998 of AIDS related complications. An artist, gallery owner and graphic designer. He owned and ran art galleries (Oggi Domani and Greathouse) in the East Village during the 1980s. More recently, he worked as a commercial graphic designer in the fine arts and advertising worlds. He also spent hundreds of hours providing pro bono services to various not-for-profit organizations. He is survived by his life partner Jay L. Katz, his mother Elsie Greathouse, his brother Mark Greathouse and a close group of loving friends. A celebration of his life will be held at Feature, 76 Greene Street, 2nd floor, NYC on November 7, 1998 from 6:30 to 8:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Body Positive, 19 Fulton St., Suite 308B, NYC 10038
