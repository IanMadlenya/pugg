
        WAINE-Edith Sutro, 93, died March 4, 2001 in Stamford, CT. Born Manila, PI to Anna Sutro & Maurice F. Loewenstein. Attended Ethical Culture School and Vassar College. Widow of Hans Waine, MD. Lifelong association with the Ntnl. Student Fed. In 1990, received the Award of Merit from the Ntnl. Council for International Visitors of the USIA. She and her husband of 68 years were active sailors and horticulturists. A summer memorial service to be at Salters Point, MA. Survived by sons Martin, Kenneth and Peter. Susan, their daughter, died in 1996.
