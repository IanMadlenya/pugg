
        GORDON--Monte J., formerly of NYC, and of Henderson, NV, on Friday, March 30, 2007 at age 83. A retired Research Director of Financial Securities and a U.S. Air Force WWII veteran. Funeral services are pending in San Diego, CA. Survivors include his wife, Arline Gordon of Henderson, NV, his daughters Dale Gordon of Las Vegas, NV and Margery (Richard) Winters of Orlando, FL, four grandchildren, Joshua, Brandy and Jeffrey Bell of Las Vegas, NV and Andrew Winters of Orlando, FL. Contributions to your favorite charity.
