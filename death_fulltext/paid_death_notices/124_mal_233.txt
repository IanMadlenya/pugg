
        WEISSGLASS-Bennett. Died on May 11, 1997 at age 73. A physician specializing in internal medicine. He lived and practiced in Morristown, New Jersey since 1955. Born in Brooklyn he graduated Stamford University and the University of Oregon Medical School. He completed his residencies and internship at Coney Hospital in Brooklyn and Bellevue Hospital in New York City. A veteran of the US Army he served in WWII and the Korean War. He was the devoted husband of Lela and cherished father of Susan, Leslie and Jennie. He is also survived by a sister Charlotte and seven grandchildren.
