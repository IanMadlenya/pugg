
        MEYERS--Samuel. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Samuel Meyers, beloved father of Robert Meyers, whose generosity and enthusiastic leadership strengthens lives and enriches the Jewish community in New York, in Israel, and throughout the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to Bob and his wife, Harriet, and to his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
