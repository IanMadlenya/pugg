
        ROSEN-Robert M. Died Monday, March 24, 2003 at home in Rock Hill, NY. He was 64. Long time attorney in Monticello in firm of Rosen Law Office. He is survived by his wife of 37 years, Nathana Mintz Rosen; his three sons Erik, Jeremiah and Lucas; his brother and sister-in-law William and Lois Rosen; and his brother-in-law Ward Mintz and his partner Floyd Lattin; and nieces, nephews, cousins and countless friends. A remembrance of Bob's life will be held at 2PM on Wednesday, March 26 at Bernie's Holiday Restaurant in Rock Hill. Contributions may be made to the Sullivan County United Way or a charity of choice.
