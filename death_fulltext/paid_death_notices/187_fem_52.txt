
        CONRAD TOURETZ-Lillian. Died at home August 6, 2002, surrounded by her children and grandson, at peace with a beautiful smile. Psychotherapist, mother of David and Donna, grandmother of Zakary, sister of Gladys, Irene, Mae, beloved aunt, cherished member of lifelong community of friends. Predeceased by second husband Arthur, by parents Rose and Philip, friend to first husband Martin. First woman union president, led march for racial integration in '40's, decoded for Gen'l Grove on Manhattan Project. Great woman of pizzaz with brilliant mind, vital spirit, empathic heart, touched the depths of many. Service on Friday, August 9th, 11 AM, Riverside, 21 Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, 914-664-6800.
