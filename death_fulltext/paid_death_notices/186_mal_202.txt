
        KROTKI-Carl, 87. Beloved husband of the late Shirley, father of Saul Krotki and Isaac and Judith Putterman, devoted grandfather of Amanda Krotki, Chad and Jessica Haller, and Karen Putterman, great-grandfather of Leila. CPA, attorney, controller of Permag Corporation, and passionate bibliophile. Past president of the NY Mineralogical Club and for 14 years treasurer of the Eastern Federation of Gem and Mineral Societies. His library was a landmark resource for mineralogists the world over. Shiva through Wednesday at the Putterman home.
