
        MUGLER--Richard C. Jr. At home on October 8, 2005, following a lengthy battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Suzanne Alsop for 45 years. Survived by their children Jennifer Mugler Peterson, Meredith Mugler Bowman, Kimberly Mugler Esson M.D., Richard C. Mugler III and 8 grandchildren. Memorials may be made in his memory to the Austerlitz Cemetery Association or the Austerlitz Historical Society, both in Austerlitz, N.Y., 12017.
