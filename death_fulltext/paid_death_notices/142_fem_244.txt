
        VUILLET-Frances Jeanne. Died November 21 at Geer Nursing and Rehabilitation Center in Canaan, Connecticut. She was 91 years old. Born in Jersey City, New Jersey, Mrs. Vuillet lived for many years in London and Paris with her late husband, Samuel Vuillet. Since 1960, she had resided in Locust Valley and then Glen Cove, New York. Survivors include a daughter, Jeanne Poole of Huntington, NY, son, Robert Vuillet of Stanfordville, NY, six grandchildren and thirteen great-grandchildren. The family will hold a memorial service at Woodlawn Cemetery in December. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Geer Nursing and Rehabilitation, Canaan, Connecticut.
