
        JOLLS--Frederick Keith. Better known as ''Keith'' passed away Sunday, March 20, 2005, of heart failure at Holy Cross Hospital in Fort Lauderdale, FL. Keith graduated from Clarkson University and recieved his Master's degree from Columbia University. He was a former partner at Vollmer Associates and worked at Louis Berger International, Inc. Most recently he was the owner of Jolls Engineering in Fort Lauderdale, FL. He is survived by his wife, Ann, and his sons, Chris and Curtis. At Keith's request there will be no service. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: The American Heart Association.
