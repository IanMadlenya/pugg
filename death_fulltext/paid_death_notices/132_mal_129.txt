
        OBERRENDER-Girard, M.D. The Lenox Hill Hospital Alumni Association deeply regrets the passing of Girard Oberrender, M.D., esteemed member of the Department of Otolaryngology since January 1, 1921. He has remained on the Honorary Staff to the present time. He died on January 7, 1998 at the age of 104 and is survived by his son and daughterin-law, Girard and Marty Oberrender. Dr. Oberrender received the Distinguished Alumnus Award in 1983 from the LHH Alumni Association. He was a dedicated physician, teacher and mentor. He will be deeply missed by his family, friends and colleagues. Peter Bruno, M.D., President LHH Alumni Association Peter Baiocco, M.D., Secretary LHH Alumni Association
