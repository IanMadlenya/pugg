
        CROUTER -- Agathe. The Hewitt School community mourns the loss of Agathe K. Crouter, who served Hewitt as its head of school from 1980-1990. Agathe's inspirational and dedicated leadership influenced the growth and development of The Hewitt School in innumerable ways. We extend our deepest condolences to Agathe's husband, Hank and to her family. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, June 18 at 4 PM at The Hewitt School, 45 E. 75th St. Linda M.
