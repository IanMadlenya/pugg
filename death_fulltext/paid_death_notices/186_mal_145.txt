
        BERKE-Arnold H. Age 83, on July 11, of Floral Park, NY, of a heart attack while doing what he loved for over 40 years, playing tennis. Beloved husband of the late Rose. Cherished friend and father to Sandy, Barbara and husbands Don and Michael. Proud and adoring grandfather to Rebecca. Loving brother of Morty and Estelle, and their families. His life was filled with devotion, caring and unselfish love, leaving a void in the hearts of his family forever. Services Sunday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Association or the American Diabetes Foundation.
        BERKE-Arnold. The members of Shelter Rock Tennis Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Arnold Berke. He was a friend to all and will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to his entire family.
