
        NADEL--Charlotte F. Died peacefully on November 13 in her 100th year. She was the wife of the late Benjamin Nadel and is survived by her sons, Lawrence, Norman and Joshua, by six grandchildren, Charles, Brian, Melissa, Victoria, Dan and Etyan, and seven great-grandchildren, Emily, Graham, Nicole, Peter, Jamie, Brendan and Lauren. She brought brightness to all she encountered and her gentle dignity will be missed. Services 10AM Sunday, at Sinai Chapel, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, Queens.
