
        GOLDWEITZ-Saul. Of Chestnut Hill, Massachusetts, on July 3, 1998. Born in New York City in 1920, he graduated from Townsend-Harris and the College of the City of New York, and earned his MBA from Harvard University, with distinction. He served as Lt. Commander in the U.S. Navy during World War II. He retired from Cahners Publishing Company in 1986 having served as President and CEO. He is survived by his children, Mark of Boston, MA, Dr. Jonathan of Stamford, CT, Elissa Baly of Washington, DC and Julie of Boston, MA, his grandchildren, Daniel, David, Benjamin, Marissa and Jared, his brother Joseph of Montville, NJ and his former wife, Harriet Carlton Goldweitz. Services Sunday at 11:00 AM at Temple Reyim, 1860 Washington Street, Newton, MA. Interment Monday at 12:30 PM at Cedar Park, Paramus, NJ. Shiva at the home of his brother. Memorial observance in Chestnut Hill, MA., Tuesday through Thursday. Donations in his memory to Temple Reyim. For further information, call Stanetsky Chapels, 800-842-4280.
