
        SAMUELS-Helen Peck. Died peacefully at home on February 16, at age 97. She was predeceased by her beloved husband Barney Peck, and loving husband Jules Samuels. She was the devoted mother of Alice P. Soll, Joan Betty Schwarz, Stephen M. Peck and Larry Samuels. She had a special relationship with each of her children's spouses. She adored and was adored by her 8 grandchildren and their spouses. Her ten great grandchildren brought immense joy to her. We will miss the embrace of her enduring love, her boundless energy, her clear thinking, her good taste, and the music and adventure she brought to our lives. Services private.
        SAMUELS-Helen Peck. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Helen Peck Samuels, beloved mother of Stephen M. Peck, and mother-in-law of Judith Stern Peck. Stephen, as past vice chair of the Board of Directors, and Judith, as past chair, have provided inspiring leadership, insightful vision, and generous support to our global Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Stephen and Judith and to Mrs. Samuels' entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
