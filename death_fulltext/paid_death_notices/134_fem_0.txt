
        AGREE-Betty On February 25, 1998, in her 74th year. Daughter of the late Isadore and Mary Agree. Dear sister of George E. and Lee Agree, of Washington D.C. Beloved aunt of Andrew, Robert and Jonathan. Betty will be lovingly remembered by her many friends, colleagues and relatives. Memorial services Monday, March 2nd, 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
