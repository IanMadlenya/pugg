
        GALEF -- James M. The Board of Trustees, Headmaster, faculty, staff & the Collegiate School community mourn the loss of devoted alumnus and former Trustee James M. Galef '74. Mr. Galef served the School as a longtime member of the Alumni Council and as a Trustee from 1996 to 2002. His stewardship and counsel were deeply valued and much appreciated. The Collegiate community extends sincere condolences to his wife, Kirsten, his children and the entire Galef family. David B. Forer, President, Board of Trustees Kerry P. Brennan, Headmaster
