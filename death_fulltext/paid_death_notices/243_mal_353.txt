
        GARY--Raymond Bedros, Age 78 died peacefully on Saturday, April 28, 2007 after a short illness. He was the beloved husband of Prudence Gibb Gary for 36 years and the adored father of their daughter Amelia Mai Gary. He is also survived by his brother Captain Stanley Platt Gary and his family. B.A. Yale University 1950, M.A. Harvard University Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, 1951. U.S. Navy 1951-1954, Morgan Stanley 1955, Partner 1964, Advisory Director 1983. Raymond was on the Board of Directors of the Third Street Music School Settlement and Stone Oil Corporation. There will be a service held at the Union Chapel, Fishers Island, N.Y. at 12:30pm on Friday, May 4, 2007 and a private family burial. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Third Street Music School Settlement, 235 East 11th Street, New York 10003 would be appreciated by the family.
