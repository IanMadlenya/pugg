
        EPSTEIN -- Miriam (nee Drossner), 88. Of Floral Park, NY, died on January 1, 2006, of a stroke. Adored grandmother of Caroline Epstein Lynn and Daniel Epstein Lynn. Devoted mother of the late Allen and the late Joseph. Beloved wife of the late Oscar. Dear sister to the late Jacob. Dear sister-in-law of Muriel. Cherished aunt of Marjorie, Michael, Robbie and the late Jay. Loving mother-in-law of Gabriella and Wendy. After graduating form Temple University, she taught physics and later volunteered as a docent at the American Museum of Natural History. She was buried in Mt. Carmel Cemetery, Glendale, NY, on January 2, 2006. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions to the American Museum of Natural History, Central Park West at 79th Street, New York, New York 10024.
