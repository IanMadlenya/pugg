
        SMITH-Frank MacGregor, Jr. Born May 28, 1927 in Indianapolis, ID. Died April 2, 1998 in North Palm Beach, Florida. after a long illness. Son of Frank and Frieda Boes Smith, dedicated and loving husband to Abby, devoted father to Frank M. Smith III, Braddock M. Smith, Thaddeus M. Smith, Nathaniel M. Smith, and Caleb M. Smith. Contributor to the golden days of Broadcasting, the world of sports, and his community. Memorial service at the Greenville Community Church, Scarsdale, New York, on April 8, 1998 at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Frank Smith Memorial Fund at Deerfield Academy, Deerfield, MA. 01342.
