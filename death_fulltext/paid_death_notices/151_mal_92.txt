
        BRUMER-Milton. Died August 5, 1999, in Boca Raton, FL, at the age of 97. He was an adoring husband for more than 70 years to his beloved late wife, Mary. He leaves his daughters Barbara Brumer of Boca Raton and Carolyn Graham of Hartsdale, NY, son-in-law Jack Graham, granddaughters Elizabeth Schnee and Emily Schnee and their husbands, John Rearick and Carlos Toruno, and his greatgrandsons Joey and Willy Rearick and Benjamin and Camilo Toruno. He was an eminent civil engineer and a mighty presence in his family. He will be oft remembered and much missed.
