
        DUEBLER-Josephine. Died on March 20, 2003. Beloved ''Joey,'' was beautiful and kind and good. Born in Antigo, WI, she grew up in Shawano, WI. She was a graduate of Ms. Brown's School in Milwaukee, and in 1945 she came to New York City where she was first employed by the Borden Co, and later by the Rockefeller Brothers Fund. She is survived by her sister Clarice Wolfgram, two brothers Francis and Frederick, her niece Martha Kleemann and by her loving, lifelong friend Dorothy Livermore.
