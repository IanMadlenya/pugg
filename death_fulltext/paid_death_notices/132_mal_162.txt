
        FRIEDMAN-Hyman R. Age 93. Beloved father of Michael and Carole. Adored grandfather of Debbie and Mark Cooper, Lisa and Tom Steigelman, Jon and Sherri Friedman. Admired great-grandfather of Bradley, Adam and Zachary Cooper. A father's father, a husband's husband, a lawyer's lawyer for almost 70 years, a self made man of deep integrity and wisdom and infinite love who placed his family before himself. He taught by example and we are all the better for having known him. We shall miss him and remember him always. The family requests that visiting with the mourners will take place Monday through Thursday at 7 PM, at 150 Central Park South.
        FRIEDMAN-Hyman R. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member and father of its devoted member, Mr. Michael Friedman, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family.
