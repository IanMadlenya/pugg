
        GOODMAN-Mollie. The Israel Education Fund mourn the passing of Mollie Goodman, mother of Charles H. Goodman, Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Jewish Agency for Israel, UJA National Vice Chairman and former member of the UJA Board of Trustees, and past President of CJF. Our condolences to Charles and his family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jane F. Sherman, Chairman, Israel Education Fund Avrum Burg, Chairman, JAFI Executive Yitzchak Shavit, Vice President, Israel Education Fund
