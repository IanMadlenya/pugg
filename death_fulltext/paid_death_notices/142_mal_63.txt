
        ISAACSON-Robert L. On Nov. 5, 1998 at home in Manhattan. He was an urbane figure well-known to enthusiasts of 19th-century academic painting. A Requiem Mass will be held at Saint Ignatius Loyola on Park Avenue, November 10th at 11:00 AM. Donations in his memory may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Medical Center, 1st Avenue at 16th Street, NY, NY 10003.
