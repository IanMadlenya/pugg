
        FEIN-Mitchel. On March 23, 2001 at age 90. A realtor for 40 years with the former Walter Scott & Company. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia Fein. Loving father of Mindy Leenen and father-in-law of Frans. Adoring grandfather of Sarah, David and Peter. Graveside services private.
        FEIN-Mitchel. The Officers, National Board of Directors and chapter members of ARMDIAmerican Red Magen David for Israel note with deepest sadness the passing of Mitchel Fein, beloved husband of the late Sylvia Fein. He was the past National President and dedicated member of the ARMDI Board of Directors for more than 40 years. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his daughter Mindy and son-in-law Dr. Frans Leenen and his grandchildren Sarah, David and Peter. May beautiful memories bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert L. Sadoff, MD, National President Benjamin Saxe, Excecutive Vice President
