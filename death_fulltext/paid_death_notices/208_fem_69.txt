
        DRUCE--Olga, age 92, died of natural causes on April 18th, 2004. Producer/Director of ''Captain Video'' on TV; ''House of Mystery'' and ''Superman'' on the radio. Broadway actress in ''Judgement Day'', ''Time of Your Life'' and ''The Eternal Road''. She was Chairman of the Drama Group at the Analytical Psychology Club in NYC. She is survived by a sister, niece, great nieces and nephews, cousins and beloved friends.
