
        HORNBLOWER -- Marne Lloyd-Smith, 85, born in New York City, died in Washington, DC, on March 30, 2004. Survived by her husband, George Marshall Hornblower, of Washington, her daughters, Marjorie Johnson of Golden, Colorado, Virginia Lawrence of New York City, and Nancy Rice of Coronado, California, her son, Jonathan Hornblower of Corpus Christi, TX, eleven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Huron Mountain Wildlife Foundation, Big Bay, MI. 49808, would be appreciated.
