
        BERND-Addie, 79. On July 23, 2001. Former owner of Tip Top Printing in Washington Heights. Survived by his wife, Lisa, his daughters, Paulette and Janet, his sons-in-law, Steven and Marc, and his grandchildren, Alex, David, Rebecca and Scott. We will miss his warmth, humor and love. Services today at 1 PM at Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, New York. In lieu of flowers donations to the Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale.
