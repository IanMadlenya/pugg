
        LICHTENSTEIN--Stephen G., 64, of Virginia Beach, VA, died Saturday, August 5, 2006. He is survived by his wife Pamela, son Jonathan, and sister Ilynne. He was a writer, pilot, and training consultant for AT&T, the EDO Corporation, and the U.S. Navy, and co-owner of Asteriques jewelry store. A remembrance gathering will be held at a later date.
