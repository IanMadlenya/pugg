
        MELIK--Soss E., on October 11, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Lois May Radley Melik. Graveside services were held at the Miami Cemetery, Waynesville, OH on Friday, October 24, 2003. Mr. Melik was born on August 2, 1914 in Kislovodsk, Russia, of Armenian parents. He was predeceased by his father, the late Efrem Melik Shah-Nazaroff, his mother Maria Avanesov, and his brother Zaven. He was a world-renowned artist whose many portraits are exhibited at the National Portrait Gallery at the Smithsonian Institute in Washington, DC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Armenian National Science and Educational Fund, FAR, 630 2nd Ave., NY, NY 10016.
