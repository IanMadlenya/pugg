
        CAMPO-J. William (''Bill''). Died peacefully at age 81 in Jupiter, FL. Beloved husband of Dorothy (''Dodie''), adored father of Priscilla (Michael Press), Jack (Betsy), Mary Kay (Chet Cobb), Didi, Joanie (Chris Carter), Tom and nine grandchildren, Michael and Dodie Press, Katie, Gigi and William Campo, Sally, Katherine and Julia Cobb and Reilly Carter, he also is survived by his loving sister, Mary Fleming (Jim). Bill was thoroughly devoted to his family. An innovative leader of the insurance industry, he served as Chairman of Ter Bush and Powell and Deputy Chairman of Jardine Insurance Brokers, Inc. A man of tremendous integrity, compassion, humor and courage, he was adored by many and his passing leaves a hole in all our hearts. Visiting hours Tuesday April 23rd, 2 to 5PM and 7 to 9PM, at the Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, CT. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held 10AM, Wednesday, April 24, at St. Michael the Archangel, 469 North St., Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Franciscan Missions, Knights of Malta or Jupiter Medical Center, Jupiter, FL.
