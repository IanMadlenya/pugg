
        SCHWARTZ--Irving.Synagogue for the Arts regrets the passing of longtime member and past President Irving Schwartz. May his memory be a blessing. Jonathan Glass, Rabbi Fabian Friedland, Pres.
        SCHWARTZ--Irving. 96, July 23rd, beloved husband of 41 years to Libby, and the late Lee, devoted father of Gloria and Alan, and the late Carol, stepfather of Anne, Sandy, Dan, and Alan, seven grandchildren and fifteen great grandchildren. Founder in 1930 of Schwartz Electric Company that serviced commercial, cultural and institutional businesses in NYC until his retirement at the age of 90. Founder, Treasurer and President of The Civic Center Synagogue where he remained a generous benefactor throughout his life.
