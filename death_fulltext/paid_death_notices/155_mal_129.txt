
        MARBUT-Terrell Clifford. Of Sands Point, New York. Died December 8, 1999 at age 91. Devoted husband of Katharine Lott Marbut, father of Katharine Marbut Ullman and Clifford Daniel Marbut, grandfather of Laura Ullman Schwartz, Susan Katharine Ullman, Valerie Ullman Taylor and Frank Clifford Ullman and great grandfather of five. Upon graduation from Georgia Tech, he joined General Motors where he was a valued employee until retirement in 1973. He lived a wonderful life and loved his family and his home. We will miss him greatly.
