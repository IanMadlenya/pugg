
        JACOBSON -- Rita. Beloved wife of the late David, mother of Paula and Mark, grandmother of Rae, Rosalie and Billy, mother-in-law of Nancy and good friend to many. Passed away January 2, 2005, at age 84, after a valiant struggle with cancer. A proud New Yorker and union member she graduated from New York University in 1940 and taught in New York City Public Schools for 30 years. An unforgettable personality and strongly beneficial influence on her many friends and relatives, she will be missed. A funeral/memorial service will be held January 4, 2005, 12:15 pm, at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, Queens, NY. All invited. In lieu of flowers donations can be sent to the American Cancer Society.
