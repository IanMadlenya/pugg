
        SHATTAN--Boaz M., D.D.S. 84, died on May 9, after fighting coronary disease and in grief at the sudden death of his beloved wife Mimi. Devoted father of Boaz Jr. (Buzz) and Thomas, adoring father-in-law of Kate Shattan and loving grandfather. Bo was a dedicated and revered oral surgeon who practiced for almost 60 years and a distinguished faculty member of Columbia Dental School. His kindness and gentle soul touched everyone who met him. Services private. The family will receive visitors today through Wednesday.
