
        FEIN-Bernard. Born July 11, 1932, died April 22, 2000. Beloved husband and best friend of Carol (Rosenberg) Fein. Devoted son of Mollie Fein. Loving father of Neil and Michele (Fein) Weinman and dear son-in-law Mark, and like a second father to Jacky Fein. Adored ''PA'' to granddaughter Alyssa Joelle Weinman. Caring brother-in-law to Phyllis and Stephen Stolar and loving uncle to their children. A graduate of City College of New York and Brooklyn Law School. Recipient of the 1972 Good Scout Award and the 1986 Silver Beaver Award of the Greater New York Council of Boy Scouts of America-Construction Industry Division. He was affiliated for 43 years with the Rudin Management Company as a Vice President. He was a Hadassah Associate. Donations in his memory may be made to the Fair Lawn Chapter of Hadassah, 10-10 Norma Ave, Fair Lawn, NJ 07410, designated for Melanoma Research at Hadassah Hospital Jerusalem.
