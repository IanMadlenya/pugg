
        SMITH-Patricia Grantier. Peacefully passed away March 5th,2000 at Northern Westchester Hospital. Her endearing strength, guidance, love of random acts of kindness to all will forever be cherished by all who survived her. Lovingly and proudly we say good-bye, wishing her eternal peace. Life will continue, but only with her in our hearts. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. In memory and in lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the First Presbysterian Church of Katonah, 31 Bedford Rd, Katonah, N.Y. 10536.
