
        LEVENTHAL--Rose. Age 90 In Delray Beach, FL. on December 19, 2003. Beloved, beautiful wife of Jack. Dearest mother of Peter, Joan (Wynn) and Robin. Adored grandmother of Andrew (Nada) and Alexander Wynn, and Gabriella Leventhal-Posner. Honored mother-in-law of Richard Wynn. Great grandmother of Alissar. Her beauty, honesty, valor and intelligence will live in us always. Contributions in her memory will be appreciated by Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33486.
