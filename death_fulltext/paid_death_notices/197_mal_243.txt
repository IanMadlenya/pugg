
        HELFANT--Gerson, peacefully, on June 24. A graduate of Erasmus Hall High School, attended Syracuse University. A self-made success as CEO of Bennett Utilities, served as president of Westhampton Beach Rotary, Commodore of Westhampton Yacht Squadron. Survived by his wife Judith, daughters Ida Shuve, Lisa Turpin, brother Mike, three grandsons and a great grandson. Visitation Thurs, 7-9pm, Sinnickson's Moriches Funeral Home, Centre Moriches, NY.
