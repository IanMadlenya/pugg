
        TRILLON-Yves M. Age 47. Of Brooklyn, N.Y. Beloved son of Jean and Janine Trillon. Loving brother of Marc Trillon and Annick Jemfer. Died at home after a long illness, May 13, 2001. Born in France, he lived in New York for eighteen years. He worked as an architect for several City agencies in the Bronx and, in his latest position, at the N.Y.C.H.A. His family in France and loved ones here wish to thank all of his friends and colleagues whose prayers and support carried him through his most difficult last years. Contributions in his name may be made to Cancer Care and the American Cancer Society. A memorial services is planned for a later date.
