
        JAFFE-Trude Michel. On August 31, 1998 after a short illness. Wife of the late Herman J. Jaffe who died January 23, 1998. Senior Editor, crossword puzzles, Los Angeles Times Syndicate. She will be missed and fondly remembered by her family, which includes Herman's, and by her many friends. A memorial service will be held in New York at a later date. Donations in her memory made be made to American Ballet Theatre, 890 Broadway, NY, NY 10003.
