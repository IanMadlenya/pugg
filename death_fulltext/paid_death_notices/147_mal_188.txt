
        BROWN-Harvey 86, died April 22, 1999. Devoted husband of Ida. Beloved father of Susan and Michael. Caring grandfather. A life-long resident of New York's garment district. He was dedicated to his family and proud of their achievements, especially the 1985 Nobel Prize awarded to his son. Memorial service at the ''Riverside'' Sunday April 25 at 9:45am. Burial at Mt Ararat Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
