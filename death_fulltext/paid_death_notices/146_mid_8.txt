
        RUBEN-Sadie. The President and Board of Trustees of Montefiore Medical Center are deeply saddened by the death of Sadie Ruben, beloved mother of Robert Ruben, M.D., director of Montefiore's Robert Ruben, M.D., Communications Disorders Institute, distinguished University Professor at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and former Chairman of the Department of Otolaryngology at Montefiore and the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. We extend our sincere condolences to her son and her entire family. Spencer Foreman, M.D., President Jay B. Langner, Chairman, Board of Trustees Montefiore Medical Center
        RUBEN-Sadie. The faculty, administration and staff of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine extend heartfelt condolences to our distinguished faculty member and long-time chairman of the Department of Otolaryngology, Robert J. Ruben, M.D., on the loss of his dear mother, Sadie. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D., Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
