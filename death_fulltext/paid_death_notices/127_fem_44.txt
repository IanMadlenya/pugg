
        ROSE-Lillian Koslan, on August 6, 1997. She was a secondary school teacher and District Supervisor of Libraries in District 29 Queens. She was married to Rudolph Koslan for forty-nine years and is survived by Bernard Rose, husband of ten years. Mother of Martha Rich. Grandmother of four. Sister of Hilda Kirsch and Selma Rudnick. Services on Sunday, 1 P.M., at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel in Forest Hills.
