
        GOLDSTEIN-Joyce. Former Head Nurse at the New York Times newspaper (retired 1980) passed away at the Wood River Retirement Community in Bensalem, PA on August 19, 1997. Mrs. Goldstein was 76. Wife of the late David Goldstein, also retired (1969) from the New York Times newspaper. Mrs. Goldstein was President of the National Association of Industrial Nurses. She was the daughter of John and Edna (nee Nave) Nelson. Joyce is survived by three brothers, Retired Col. Carl A. Nelson of Colorado Springs, CO; John W. Nelson, Jr., of Mountaintop, PA; and Frank L. Nelson of Emporia, KS. Also several nieces and nephews. Tomlinson Funeral Home, Bensalem, PA.
