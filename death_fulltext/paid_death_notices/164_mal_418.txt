
        HASSENFELD-Harold I. The American Jewish Committee mourns the death of Harold Hassenfeld, a dear friend, devoted leader and generous benefactor. Harold served AJC with distinction as former Honorary Chairman of our Palm Beach Campaign and, along with his wife Rita Dee, as members of our National Leadership Council. We were proud to have honored them both with our National Human Relations Award. We extend our deepest sympathy to Rita Dee, their children, grandchildren and entire family. Bruce M. Ramer, President David A. Harris, Exec Director Jaime Kelstein, Development Dir
        HASSENFELD-Harold D. was a wonderful man and a fighter to the very end. He was a wonderful husband, father and grandfather and had a business instinct second to none. He was totally devoted to his family, his employees and his friends. The partners at Baer Marks & Upham join us in offering our sincere condolences to his entire family. Suzanne and Donald J. Bezahler
        HASSENFELD-Harold I. The Board of Governors at the Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member and dear friend, Harold I. Hassenfeld, and offers it heartfelt sympathy to his family and friends. David Mack, President Marshall Hess, Secretary
