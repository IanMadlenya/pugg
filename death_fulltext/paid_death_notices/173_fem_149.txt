
        MORRIS-Eleonor. A pioneer social worker who retired from the U.S. Administration on Aging in 1980, passed away June 1, 2001, from complications following surgery. She was 87. A celebration of her life will be held Wednesday, June 27, at the Laura Parsons Pratt Conference Center, 281 Park Ave. So., at 22nd St., from 4-7 PM.
