
        AMES-Ann F. Of New York, NY. Died on November 23, 2000 at the age of 96. Mrs. Ames was the wife of the late Irving M. Ames. She is survived by her daughter Susan Ames. Mrs. Ames was awarded a Juris Doctor degree from Cleveland-Marshall College of Law at Cleveland State University, a member of the Class of 1928, where contributions in the memory of Mrs. Ames will be accepted. Funeral services will be private.
