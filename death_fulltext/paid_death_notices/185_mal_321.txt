
        EDELMAN-Dr. Morton H. Of NYC, June 27, 2002, at age 88. Cherished husband of the late Hilda Rachel Wyner Edelman. Beloved father of Diana E. E. Kleiner, Robert G. Edelman, and Nancy Salzman. Adored grandfather of Alex Kleiner and Daniel Salzman. Dear brother of Albert Edelman and Daniel J. Edelman. He received BA and MD degrees from Johns Hopkins University. During WW II, he served as a Major in the Philippines and New Guinea, specializing in diagnosing and treating tropical diseases, and later published his research in leading medical journals. He practiced internal medicine in NY and was Attending Physician at Mt Sinai Hospital. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Johns Hopkins Medical School or Visiting Nurse Service of NY-Hospice Care.
