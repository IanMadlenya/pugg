
        FRIEL-James Edward, 75. Died August 28, 1998. A Naples, Florida resident since 1983, coming from West Babylon, N.Y. Mr. Friel was a W.W. II veteran & worked for the New York Times as a dispatcher for 30 plus years. He is survived by his loving wife of 55 years, Beverly E. Friel, children Carol (James) Leuschner, James E. Friel, Steven (Laurie) Friel, Gerard (Maria) Friel, Lori (Martin) Gensler. Grandchildren Loren, Kelly, L.J., Chris, Keri Jean, Adam, Erica, Tara, Jesse, Gillian, Tyler & Brendon. Sister, Gertrude Wise. A Memorial Mass will be held on Thursday, September 3, 1998 at 3 P.M., at St. Peter's in Naples, Florida. Memorial contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 919 North Michigan Avenue, Suite 1000, Chicago, Illinois 60611. Phone 800-272-3900.
