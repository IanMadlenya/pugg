
        YORKOWITZ-Isaac. On October 24, 1997. Beloved companion of Rita Wernick and dearly loved by all of her family. Warm, caring and a good friend to so many. Loving brother of Geri Singerman and Millie York. A wonderful uncle to all of his nieces and nephews. Everyone who knew him will miss him. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory can be made to UJA Federation. Services Sunday, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
