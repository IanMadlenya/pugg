
        GARDINER-Hilliard, of Kew Gardens, Queens, died Sunday, May 23, 1999. He is survived by a nephew and a niece. Member of Yale Alumni Club of Queens. Graduate of Yale Law School, 1949. Hilliard will be sadly missed by the counselors, friends and students whom he interviewed for admission to Yale. In lieu of a scheduled memorial, contributions may be made to AHRC, 200 Park Avenue South, NY, NY, 10003, in the name of Madeline Rosenberg.
