
        NEWMAN-Ida. The President of Baruch College and the Trustees of the Baruch College Fund extend their heartfelt sympathy to our friend and colleague William Newman and his family on the death of his mother, Ida. Matriarch of a remarkable family, Ida Newman was a strong supporter of education and culture and of the Hebrew language. Hers was a long and productive life and she passed her generosity and vision on to her sons. Edward V. Regan, President, Baruch College Lawrence Zicklin, President, The Baruch College Fund
        NEWMAN-Ida. The Partners of Hofheimer Gartlir & Gross wish to express their sincerest sympathies to William and Melvin and the Newman family upon the loss of their beloved mother.
