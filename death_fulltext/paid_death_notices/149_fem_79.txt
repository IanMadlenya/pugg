
        KAHN-Jan Jaffe. Died June 10 at 2:45AM of multiple myeloma. With her were her husband Ron and her daughters Laura and Sasha. She was a screenwriter, playwright and novelist of extraordinary sensitivities. Graveside services Sunday, June 13th, 1 PM at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to The Myeloma Foundation.
