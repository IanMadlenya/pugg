
        HEE--Dickson. The Chinatown Health Clinic Foundation expresses its deepest condolences to the family of Dickson Hee. He will be remembered for his dedication to our community and to public service. Mr. Hee served as the Board Chair of the Charles B. Wang Community Health Center. A member of the NY Federal Reserve Bank's Business Advisory Committee, he was newly appointed to the US Small Business Administration's Region II Regulatory Fairness Board. We mourn the loss of our trusted friend. Raymond Fong, MD Samuel Yeh, MD Donald Armstrong, MD Pyser Edelsack Sandra Lee Kawano Marie Lam Joanne M.J. Quan Irene Chu Jane T. Eng, Esq. >>AD#
