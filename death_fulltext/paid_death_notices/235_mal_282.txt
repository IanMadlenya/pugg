
        WILSON--Richard J. Born in 1926 in New York City. After serving in the USMC, he joined the New York Times in 1950, where he worked for the Postal Department for 42 years during the reign of three Publishers until his retirement in 1991. Richie passed away on April 19, 2006 after a brief illness. An artistic, affectionate and caring husband, father, brother, grandfather, uncle, mentor and friend was quietly taken from us. Gone but never forgotten...Richie will be sadly missed by all who knew him
