
        DAVIS--Betsy. The Trustees and staff of the Madison Square Park Conservancy express their sorrow over the sudden loss of our friend, Betsy Davis. Betsy's contagious enthusiasm, her passion and vivacious personality will be remembered by all of us. Betsy's many contributions to Madison Square Park will live on forever and provide pleasure to so many. Our condolences to Betsy's family. George Trapp, Chairman Deborah Landau, President John Herrold, Park Manager
