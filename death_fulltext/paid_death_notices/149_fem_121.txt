
        ST. LOUIS-Marie. Of New York, passed away June 10, 1999 after a brave struggle with cancer. She is survived by her son Marc St.Louis, her daughter-in-law Patrice Rushen and grandson Cameron, and her brother Jack Barrow. Born in Boston on April 5, 1928, attended Girls' High School and Simmons College. She worked for 37 years for Festival Productions, Inc., the company who created the Newport Jazz Festival among other major international jazz festivals. Funeral service will be held on Wednesday, June 16 at 1:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., N.Y., N.Y. Burial is private in Great Barrington, MA. Donations in the name of Marie St. Louis may be made to the American Cancer Society.
