
        BERNARD-Bobby. Of New York City and Margate, NJ. Died after a lengthy illness on November 22, in New York City. He was born in New York City and a graduate of Boston University and Emerson College. He was a theatrical manager for most of his life and retired eight years ago. He lived in Lowell, Mass for many years before moving to New York City in 1949. He was a summer resident of Margate, NJ for the past 40 years. He is survived by his wife, Rosita, his son, David of Jackson, NJ and four grandchildren. Funeral services will be Tuesday, 12 noon, at the John Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Ave., NYC. Interment New Mt. Carmel Cemetery, Bklyn, NY.
