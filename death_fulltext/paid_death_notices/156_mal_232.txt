
        HAUSER-John T. (J. Engelberg). 88, died January 12, 2000, after a long illness. Very beloved husband and dearest friend of Lee, his wife of 56 years. Born in Austria. Came to the U.S. in 1938. Served four years in an intelligence unit of the U.S. Army during World War II. Conducted a printing business, Hauser Printing Co., on Spring Street in New York City for 46 years. Surviving besides his wife are grieving close in-laws and nieces and nephews, and deeply saddened associates and friends who will not forget his kindness and help when needed. Rest in peace, great mensch.
