
        HALBERT-Jean, 82. Off Broadway producer died on Sunday, August 18, 2002 in Greenwich Village. Born in Rahway, N.J., she grew up in Staten Island & was educated at Wagner College. Halbert worked for many years in the medical profession including being a supervisor at Roosevelt Hospital. In the 60's she moved to Greenwich Village and began her career in the theatre. She was instrumental along with partners, Lawrence Goossen and Jeff Wachtel in bringing David Mamet's early works to Off Broadway. She worked at numerous theatres including long stints at the Cherry Lane Theatre, Circle in the Square and the American Place Theatre. She is survived by a brother Neal of TX., a sister Midge of PA, a daughter Sally of NYC, three sons David, Stephen and Peter all of CA. In lieu of flowers send donations in her name to The American Lung Association.
