
        RICHARDSON-Lee D., 73. Died on Saturday at his home. Beloved husband of the late Elane Richardson. Cherished father of Rachel and Jonathan. Devoted brother of Dolores Levine. Services will be held on Wednesday, October 6, 1:30 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the Actor's Fund, 729 7th Avenue, 10th floor, New York, New York 10019.
        RICHARDSON-Lee. We mourn the loss of our fellow Player. A great actor and a true man of the Theatre, he embodied the traditions and the joy of a profession that he loved so much. His presence will be sorely missed. Our deepest sympathy to his family. The Players
