
        LINDENBAUM-John, MD. The members of the Department of Medicine, Harlem Hospital Center, mourn the passing of Dr. John Lidenbaum, one of its most revered colleagues. Dr. Lindenbaum served as Associate Director of Medicine and as Chief of Hemotology-Oncology at Harlem Hosptial frrm 1969 to 1986. For years, this gifted and generous clinician, teacher, and researcher served as a model for many young physicians. Through his example we learned the importance of being knowledgeable, skillful, and always caring. Alfred R. Ashford, MD Associate Director of Medicine and Chief of Hemotology-Onocology Charles K. Francis, MD Director of Medicine Department of Medicine, Harlem Hospital Center
