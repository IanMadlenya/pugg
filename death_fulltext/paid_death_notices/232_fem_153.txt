
        RUBINSTEIN-WEBER--Ruth, 86. Died on Monday, May 22, 2006 after a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Norman Rubinstein & the late Jack Weber, sister of Doris Selub and the late Norman Burtman, devoted mother of William S. Rubinstein (Sheryl) & Alan F. Rubinstein (Susan), grandmother of Mark, Andrew, Michael and Natasha, and great-grandmother of Emily and Nicholas. Funeral service on Wednesday, May 24, at 11 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 55 No. Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York. Gifts in her memory may be made to The Alzheimer's Foundation of America, 322 8th Ave., 6th Flr., New York, New York 10001.
