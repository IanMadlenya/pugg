
        YEH-Chen Shin. At age 80 on February 21, 1999. Beloved husband of Nancy (nee Huang) for 49 years. Loving father of Logos, Ruby, father-in-law of Margaret and uncle of cherished niece, Julie. Graduate of Sun Yat-Sen/Huachung University with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and of North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC with a Masters of Science in Nuclear Physics. Worked as a nuclear physicist and electronics engineer at Curtiss-Wright, Princeton, Atomic Power Development Associates, Detroit and Burns & Roe, Oradell with publications/contributions resulting in a listing within Marquis Who's Who in Frontier Science and Technology. A caring, kind and wise man already missed by his family, friends and others he touched in his daily life. Services will be held at the Bradley-Braviak Funeral Home, 49 Whippany Road, Whippany, New Jersey, on Saturday, February 27, 1999. Friends may call from 2-4 PM.
