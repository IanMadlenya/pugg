
        KOEGEL-George J., 71. Of New Canaan, CT., died on Wednesday, January 14th at his home. He was formerly of Garden City, L.I. Retired Divisional Manager of Malden Mills and Sales Manager with J.C. Penney of Texas and New York. Beloved husband of Rita (nee Wulforst). Loving father of Diane Nizlek, Susan Needle, Sandra Heelan and Nancy Koegel. Grandfather to six grandchildren. Graveside services Saturday at 12:30PM in Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to The New Canaan Volunteer Ambulance Corp, South Avenue, New Canaan, CT 06840.
