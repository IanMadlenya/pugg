
        FELTHAM--Reginald. Completed his journey in passing on March 23, 2006. Reg was a world-class swimmer in the 1940's shattering NYC and national breast stroke records throughout his high school and college years. Later, Reg was Director of Special Education for B.O.C.E.S. II on Long Island--selected ''Educator of the Year'' in 1976. Reggie will be remembered for his devotion to family, his commitment to quality education for everyone, his adventurous spirit, his work hard/play hard mentality and his awesome spoon playing. Reg is survived by his loving wife, Mary, seven adoring children & eighteen cherished grandchildren. Reginald Feltham wanted us all to live, love, laugh and be happy!
