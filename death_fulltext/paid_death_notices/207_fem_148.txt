
        ROTHSCHILD -- Tirzah. Passed away peacefully in her own apartment in Upper Manhattan on Sunday, April 18, 2004, after a short illness. Beloved daughter of the late Dr. Leo and Paula Rothschild, sister of Dr. Karlo Rothschild and the late Hilde Straus and sister-in-law of Walter Straus and Frances Rothschild. She was buried in Jerusalem. For over 50 years she was teacher/principal at Yeshiva Rabbi S.R. Hirsch in New York City & was respected and admired as a ''Teacher's Teacher.'' She devoted much time and boundless energy to volunteer work, charity and to helping others. Her family, friends and countless students will always be grateful to her.
