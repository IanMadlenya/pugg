
        KAPNICK-Oscar. Died at age 84 on March 16, 1997. Beloved husband and dearest friend of Syd (Sylvia) Kapnick, devoted and loving father of Steven Kapnick, the late Jo Anne Kapnick and the late Susan Kapnick Bart. Devoted and loving father-in-law of Joan Kapnick and Jacob Bart. Adored and cherished grandfather of Joshua and Madelyn Bart. He was a warm-hearted and generous man, deeply loved and respected by all those who knew him. His many charitable activities and contributions included long-time support for Sound Shore Medical Center, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, United Hebrew Geriatric Center, The Guidance Center, and Temple Israel of New Rochelle, of which he was a trustee. Services will be held at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pine Brook Boulevard, New Rochelle, NY on Tuesday, March 18 at 10am. Shiva will be observed from TuesdayThursday at the Bart residence, 19 Abbey Close, Scarsdale, NY.
        KAPNICK-Oscar. The Board of Governors and members of Fenway Golf Club record with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member Oscar Kapnick, and extend to his bereaved wife Sylvia and the members of his family our sincere condolences. Clifford Friedricks, President Jeffrey I. Citron, Secretary
        KAPNICK-Oscar. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its honorary trustee and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. A stalwart member and supporter. May his name ever be for a blessing. Dr. Jeffrey Stier, President Dr. Amiel Wohl, Rabbi
