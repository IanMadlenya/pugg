
        RIBEIRO -- Ludgero. Also known as N'Koumba Karamoka. Died on Friday, September 24. Born March 21, 1945 in Lisbon, Portugal. Activist, bookseller, scholar, conversationalist, and affectionate friend of all who knew him. He will be missed by his family and neighbors in Brooklyn Heights and beyond. Survived by his beloved companion and comrade of 16 years, Deirdre Mullane. Services Tuesday, September 28, 1:30pm at Weinstein Garlick Kirschenbaum Chapels, 1153 Coney Island Ave, Brooklyn, 718-377-4848.
