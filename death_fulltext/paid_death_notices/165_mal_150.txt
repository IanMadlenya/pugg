
        HORNE-Marc Bruce, 42 yrs., of Jericho on October 8, 2000. Beloved husband and soulmate of Fran Kiperman-Horne for 7 years. Loving father of Jason, Jennifer, and Christopher. Survived by his adoring mother Zelda; his father Harvey and stepmother Minerva; and by Beth, Sol, Neil, Troy and Amanda Kiperman. His untimely passing has left an incredible void in the lives of all he touched in his personal life and in his career as a detective for the New York State Attorney General's Office, Special Task Force. Services will be held at Gutterman's Thursday, October 12, 10 AM, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY. In lieu of flowers/ baskets donations may be made to, American Heart Association.
