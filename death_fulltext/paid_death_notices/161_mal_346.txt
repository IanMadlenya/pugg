
        PAVLAKIS-Jim. The Estee Lauder Companies mourns the untimely passing of Jim Pavlakis, Vice President, Business Operations, Aveda, who died suddenly Wednedsay, June 28, at his home in New York. ''Jim was a valued colleague and friend to everyone,'' stated Jeanette S. Wagner, Vice Chairman, The Estee Lauder Companies, Inc.. ''He was a warmhearted person, a first rate Manager and was instrumental in developing Aveda into a truly global brand. We will miss him greatly,'' she concluded. An 18-year Estee Lauder veteran, Mr. Pavlakis, age 55, worked in several brands, including Aramis and Prescriptives, before joining the corporate human resources department in 1995 as a senior executive overseeing financial and administrative areas. He assumed his most recent position in 1998 and oversaw finance, human resources, manufacturing, legal, packaging, information services and quality assurance for Aveda at the Blaine, Minnesota offices.
