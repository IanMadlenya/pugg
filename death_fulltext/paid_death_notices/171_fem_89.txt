
        SCIUTTI-Grazella. Peacefully after a short illness, Graziella Sciutti died on Monday 9 April 2001 in Geneva. Funeral to take place at 14.00 hrs. Tuesday, 17 April at La Chapelle Catholic Italienne, 15 Rue de la Mairie, Geneva. Followed by interment at the Cemetery of Chene-Bougeries. No flowers, but donations in her memory to the RCM Graziella Sciutti Opera Scholarship Fund, C/O The Development Department, Royal College of Music, Prince Consort Road, London SW7 2BS.
