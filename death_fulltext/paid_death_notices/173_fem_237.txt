
        PERLMAN-Nancy Denton. On June 25th, 2001 in the comfort of the home in which she grew up. She shined a special light on everyone and all those who knew her were touched by her special warmth. Keep the warmth with you for it is the essence of Nancy and her gift to those she leaves behind. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. She was blessed and will be missed.
