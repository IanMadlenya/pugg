
        MURRAY--Margaret Arnette (Maggie), on June 4, 2007. Beloved wife of Stephen Botkin; devoted mother of Hannah; loving daughter of the late Arthur and the late Charleen Murray; loving niece of Margaret Paris and Frank Murray. A native New Yorker was educated at the Marymount School and earned her B.A. at Middlebury College. Her ardent love of writing and reading lead to a twenty year career in academic publishing. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Friday, June 8, 7-9pm. Mass of Christian burial Saturday, 10am, St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84th Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Marymount School, in her name, at 1026 5th Avenue, NYC 10028.
