
        McKEON-Daniel Manning. The Lincoln Hall Board of Managers notes with deep regret the passing of Daniel Manning McKeon who faithfully served on the Board over 50 years. Mr. McKeon was Secretary of the Board of Managers and served on the Executive, Finance and Buildings & Grounds Committees. His expertise in finance and advocacy of the youth in Lincoln Hall's care made him an invaluable asset to the agency. The Board and staff extend sincere condolences to Mr. McKeon's family on their loss. James G. Nugent, President Douglas W. Wyatt, Vice President & Lincoln Hall Board of Managers
