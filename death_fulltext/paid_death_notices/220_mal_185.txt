
        SCHINDLER -- Jack, D.D.S., died Sunday, May 15th, peacefully at age 76 at his daughter's home in Mamaroneck with his family at his side. Gloria's beloved husband of 51 years, caring son of Robert and Fanny, loving father of Susan and Steven, adoring grandfather of Alex, Adam, Emma and Sarah, and gracious father-in-law of Susan and Alan. He will be forever in our hearts. Services Tuesday, 11:15 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Jack's memory to ''The Belsky Fund, AMNH'' and sent to Dr. George Harlow, EPS, American Museum of Natural History, CPW at 79th St., NYC 10024.
