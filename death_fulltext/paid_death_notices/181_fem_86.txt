
        FUCHS-Stella (Weinreb), 80. Died on February 5th, 2002 at Sarasota Memorial Hospital. Formerly of Miami Beach, FL and Forest Hills, New York. Beloved wife of the late Eugene Fuchs. Mother of Vivian (Vicki) Fuchs of Centerville, MA, & Monica Kramer & her husband Barry of Sarasota, FL. Loving grandmother of Michael and Ariel Caplan of Sarasota, FL. Sister of Gertrude Herz of Alberquerque, NM. Aunt of Cary Herz of Alberquerque, N.M. Funeral was held on Friday, February 8th at Mount Hebron Cemetery. Expressions of sympathy for Stella may be made to Jewish Family Services. She will be missed.
