
        GAHAN--Sylvia Barnett, on February 26, 2004, at age 98. Much loved widow of Edward Francis Gahan; mother of Antony Gahan; grandmother of Rebecca Fletcher, Victoria Cliche and Christopher Gahan; great grandmother of Rachel, Leila Rose, Stanley and Spencer. Sylvia was the last surviving child of Theresa and Jacob Barnett. She loved people, was a fine teacher and always concerned with the betterment of society. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, from 11AM on Sunday with services at 12 noon.
