
        KISSINGER-Paula. The Board of Directors, Residents and Staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with profound sorrow the death of Paula Kissinger, a very special lady and cherished member of The Hebrew Home's extended family. Mrs. Kissinger's warmth, kindness and vivacious spirit will be missed by all of us who came to know her. We extend our sincere sympathy to her sons Henry and Walter, her grandchildren and great-grandchildren. We hope that your loving memories of times together will ease your sorrow during these days of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, Pres. Bernard Goldberg, Chairman, Board of Directors Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman, Board of Directors Daniel A. Reingold, Executive Vice Pres.
