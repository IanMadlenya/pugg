
        ZENKE-Henry Christian II. Of Greensboro, NC, died at his home on Saturday, June 17, 2000. Funeral services will be held at 2 PM, Tuesday, June 20, at Holy Trinity Episcopal Church, 607 North Greene St. in Greensboro, NC. Interment will be in Green Hill Cemetery. Mr. Zenke is survived by his wife of 48 years, Virginia Ford Zenke; a daughter, Virginia Hawthorne Zenke and a son, Henry Christian Zenke III, all of Greensboro. Memorials, in lieu of flowers, may be made to Holy Trinity Episcopal Church, 607 North Green St., Greensboro, NC 27401 or Preservation GreensboroBlandwood Mansion, 447 W. Washington St., Greensboro, NC 27401. For information, Forbis & Dick, North Elm Street, Greensboro.
