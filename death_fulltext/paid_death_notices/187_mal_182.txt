
        DELL'AMICO-Vando. The Board, Officers, Trustees and Staff of United Seamen's Service mourn the loss of former Executive Director, Vando Dell'Amico, a dedicated social servant and friend to America's seafarers. We will remember Dell's commitment to his mission, his long friendship and his professionalism. Our sincerest sympathy to his wife, Frances, son, Michael, daughter, Elisa, and other family and friends. Roger Korner, Executive Director Tal Simpkins, President
