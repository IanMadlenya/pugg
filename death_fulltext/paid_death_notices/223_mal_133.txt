
        GOTSIS--Constantine A., M.D. The staff and patients of the Division of Substance Abuse, Department of Psychiatry & Behavioral Sciences of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine, are deeply saddened by the loss of our former medical director and a founding member of our Division, Constantine A. Gotsis, M.D. Dr. Gotsis, a quintessential physician, helped establish the model of sensitive, dignified, patient - centered care that has stood as the foundation of our medical services for over 30 years. His principles and wisdom set the highest of standards in the addiction treatment field. He never lost sight of the humanity and dignity of persons in his care. We will miss him greatly and extend our deepest sympathy to his family. T. Byram Karasu, M.D., Silverman Professor & the University Chairman, Department of Psychiatry & Behavioral Sciences Ira J. Marion, Executive Director, Division of Substance Abuse Patricia Sanders Romano, Executive Administrator, Department of Psychiatry & Behavioral Sciences Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
        GOTSIS--Constantine A., M.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of the former medical director of its Division of Substance Abuse in the Department of Psychiatry and Behavorial Sciences.
