
        BRUSTEIN--Abraham B., on May 20th, 2005 in Eatontown, NJ. He was a dentist, a graduate of NYU Dental School, Brooklyn College and a WWII veteran of the U.S. Army. An avid artist, photographer and golfer, he practice dentistry for 40 years with his partner and brother, Milton Brustein. He will be sadly missed by his wife, Lillian, sons, Fred, Jeff, Scott, daughters-in-law, Marsha, Ina, Lenore, grandchildren, Adam, Rebecca, Josh and his fiance, Miriah.
