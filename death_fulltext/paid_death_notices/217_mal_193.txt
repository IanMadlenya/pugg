
        TORNABENE -- Frank. Co founder of Scafa-Tornabene Art Publishing, died February 9, 2005, in Tucson, AZ, at age 80. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Lyn Tornabene; his grandson Luke; his sister Ninfa Leddo. His daughter Wendy Lyn predeceased him. Mr. Tornabene served in the South Pacific in WWII. He and his family lived in Greenwich, CT, for 30 years before he retired to Tucson. He was a published author. For information, Bring's Broadway Chapel, 6910 E. Broadway, Tucson, AZ.
