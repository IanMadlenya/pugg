
        FISHER-Bennett Lawson, 58, of Stamford, CT on September 11, 2001 at the World Trade Center. He is survived by his wife of 32 years, Susan Huntington Fisher, their children, Louisa Huntington Fisher and James Bruen Fisher, his mother, Elsie Lawson Fisher, two brothers and four sisters. A senior vice president of Fiduciary Trust Company International, Ben contributed considerable time to numerous non profit organizations. A memorial service is October 1 at 11 AM at St. Barnabas Church, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made in Ben's memory to the Pomfret School, Pomfret, CT or Save the Greenwich Lighthouse Fund, Let There Be Light, Inc., 21 W. Putnam Ave, Greenwich, CT 06830
