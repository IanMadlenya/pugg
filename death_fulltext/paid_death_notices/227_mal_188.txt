
        ZUCKERMAN--Sidney, M.D., 87. On December 14 in Boca Raton, Fl. Beloved husband of Irene, his wife and partner for 60 years. Born in New York City, he graduated from Stuyvesant High School, Columbia College and New York Medical College. A Captain in the Army in World War II, he was a prominent allergist in Forest Hills and Manhasset, where he lived for 40 years. Former Medical Director of Unisys and a ''Jack of all trades'', he enjoyed gardening, carpentry, electronics, bridge and baking his famous apple pies. He is survived by devoted daughters Elaine, Laurie and Amy, sons-in-law Paul, Steve and Jeff, grandchildren Noah, Josh and Christine, Jillian, Ashley, Adam and Jason. He is predeceased by son, Jed. Memorial service will be held at 3PM at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 7240 North Federal Highway, Boca Raton, Fl.
