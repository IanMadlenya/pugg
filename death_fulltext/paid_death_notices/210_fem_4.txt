
        KURTZ--Marlene. Beloved daughter of Herman Ratner and stepdaughter of Julie Ratner, dear mother of Jeffrey, sister of Neil and sisterin-law of Doris, died on Wednesday, July 30, 2004, after a long and heroic struggle with cancer. She was 59. A teacher of Spanish at the Princeton Day School, she was devoted to her students and admired as an expert on the folk arts of Mexico. Funeral Friday, July 2, at 1 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, NY.
