
        LINDEN-Leonard J., 86, died Tuesday, August 10, 1999 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Bella L. Linden. Father of Dr. Peter J. Linden of New York and Nantucket; Paul R. Linden of Columbus, Ohio; & Leonard J. Linden of Ridgewood, New Jersey. Grandfather of Noah, Joshua and Peter Linden, as well as Steven, Joshua, Hope & Debra Perleman. Brother of Jerome Linden of Palm Springs, CA. Services Sunday, August 15th, 2PM at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, donations to the scholarship fund of The Manhattan School of Music would be greatly appreciated. For information: ''Gutterman-Musicant,'' 1-800522-0588.
        LINDEN-Leonard. We share the pain of the loss suffered by Bella Linden and other members of the Linden families at the passing of a devoted and gentle man. He always gave richly of his common sense and affectionate devotion. We know the memories of the good years will help during this difficult time. With much love, Linda and Isaac Stern
        LINDEN-Leonard. The Board of Trustees and Staff of Carnegie Hall mourn the passing of Leonard Linden, beloved husband of Bella Linden, longtime Trustee and dear friend of Carnegie Hall. Bella's devotion to her late husband is an inspiration to us all. We extend our deepest sympathies to Bella and her family. Isaac Stern, President Sanford I. Weill, Chairman
        LINDEN-Leonard. We at ICM Artists Ltd join his wife Stella and the family in mourning the passing of a respected friend. We are with you at this difficult time. Lee Lamont David Foster Steward Warkow Byron Gustafson
