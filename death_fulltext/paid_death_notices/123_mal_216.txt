
        HERZOG-Chaim. The American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science notes with sorrow the passing of a dynamic diplomat and statesman, who served his country and his people with unsurpassed devotion. We extend heartfelt condolences to the entire Herzog family. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Haim Harari, President S. Donald Sussman, Chairman American Committee Robert G. Begam, President Fran Ginsburg, Executive Director
        HERZOG-Chaim. The Officers and Board of the American Associates, Ben-Gurion University of the Negev mourn the loss of our dear friend, President Chaim Herzog. The Chaim Herzog Center for Middle East Studies and Diplomacy recently established at BenGurion University will carry on the pursuit of the dreams of this soldier for peace. Kenneth L. Tucker, President Harold L. Oshry Chairman of the Board
