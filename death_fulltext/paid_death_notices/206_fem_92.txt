
        BELOFF--Ruth (Kahn), age 93, mother of Susan Kahn Gersten of NYC, died on March 6th in Chapel Hill, NC. She was born in New York City and lived in Denver, CO for over thirty years. She was the wife of Maxwell Kahn and Samuel Beloff. She is also survived by a son, Charles Kahn, a daughter, Vicki Kahn Ross, four grandchildren and a great-grandson.
