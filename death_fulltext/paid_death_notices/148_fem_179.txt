
        RABINOWITZ-Clara. Trained first as a social worker and then as a psychologist, devoted much of her life to community service, including service at the local & national levels. She died in her home on May 23, 1999. She is survived by a sister, Zelda Meranze & a brother, Albert Rabinowitz, eleven nieces and nephews, 26 grandnieces and nephews in addition to several great-grandnephews and great grandnieces. She will be interred today at the Woodbine Brotherhood Cemetery in Woodbine, NJ. A memorial service is planned for a later date. Contributions may be made to Psychologists for Social Responsibility and Doctors Without Borders.
