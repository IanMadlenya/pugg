
        FEINBERG-Boris. Of Scarsdale, NY on January 5, 1999. Beloved husband of Sylvia Schwartz Feinberg. Father of Cynthia Jacobson, Renee Feinberg and Henry Feinberg. Father-in-law of Paul Jacobson and Janet Feinberg. Grandfather of Adam and Steven Jacobson, Sheelah Feinberg and Hannah, Rachel and Samuel Feinberg. He was the president of Consolidated Terminal Warehouses and was active in Manhattan commercial real estate. Donations in memory of Mr. Feinberg may be made to the Phelps Memorial Hospital, Mental Heath Clinic, Sleepy Hollow, NY.
