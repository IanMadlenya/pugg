
        DE GUNZBURG -- Baron Alain. The Trustees and staff of The Jewish Museum mourn the passing of Baron Alain de Gunzburg, father of our Trustee Charles de Gunzburg and brother-in-law of our Trustee Andrea Bronfman. We extend our deepest condolences to Charles, his wife Nathalie, and to the entire de Gunzburg and Bronfman families. Susan Lytle Lipton, Chairman John L. Vogelstein, President Joan Rosenbaum, Director
