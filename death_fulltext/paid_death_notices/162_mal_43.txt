        SCHWARTZ-Harold, D.D.S. We deeply mourn the passing of our cherished friend and mentor, Harold Schwartz. A person of exceptional achievement and insight, Harold inspired his collegaues to greater excellence in dentistry. He was a legendary mentor, role model, and practitioner. His wisdom and inspiration will be greatly missed. We extend our most profound sympathy to his wife, Joyce, and to his entire family. Prosthodontic Associates Drs.
