
        McCANN-Michael F. Age 59, of Lake Worth, passed away on September 16, 2002. He had lived in Florida since 1999 coming from NY, and was past president of I.Q. Manufacturing after a long career in retail with Saks Fifth Avenue in NY, CA. and FL. Preceded in death by his parents Michael and Modean McCann. Survivors include his partner in life, David Eckis of Lake Worth, FL; sister, Jacqueline (John) Leppanen of Midland, MI.; brothers, Raymond (Faye) McCann of West Bloomfield, MI., and Patrick McCann of Midland, MI. A Memorial Service will be held on Saturday, October 5, 2002 at 10 AM at St. Matthew Roman Catholic Church. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made in Michael's name to a charity of choice. For information E. Earl Smith & Son Funeral Home and Cremation Chapel, 1032 No Dixie Highway, Lake Worth, FL. 33460.
