
        TOWNE-Augur. Of 74 East Avenue, Westerly, RI. and formerly of NYC and Stonington, Conn. died Wednesday, March 3, 1999 at his home. Born in Evanston, Ill. on May 15, 1913. Son of the late John D.C. and Jane Holabird Towne. Mr. Towne was an architect for New York Life. Survived by two nieces Diana Towne Knox of Greensboro, N.C. and Daphne Towne Witsell of Hobe Sound, Fla. Funeral services will be held at the convenience of the family. For more information The BucklerJohnston Funeral Home, 121 Main Street, Westerly, R.I.
