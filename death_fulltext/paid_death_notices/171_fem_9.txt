
        SHEA-Margaret. Of Somers, NY, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died on March 30, 2001. Mrs. Shea lived in Somers, NY, for the past two years, and before that she lived in Brooklyn, NY, for 67 years. She is survived by her sons Michael, Robert, John and Timothy, and also survived by her sisters Barbara, Eileen and brother Martin, along with seven grandchildren. She is predeceased by her husband Cornelius, sister Mary Agnes and mother and father Bridget and Martin Murphy. Visiting hours will be held at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. The funeral mass will be Tuesday, 10 AM, at St. Joseph's Church in Croton Falls, with interment at St. John's Cemetery in Queens.
