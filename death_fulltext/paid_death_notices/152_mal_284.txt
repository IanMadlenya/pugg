
        BONIME-Stanley, at the age of 75, of Scottsdale, AZ, formerly of Eastchester, NY. Beloved husband of Arlene. Devoted father of Erica, and Jonathan and Heather. Adoring grandfather of Lauren and Joshua. He was a graduate of NYU and NYU Dental School and had his practice in Manhattan for 46 years. He was a lifetime member of The Free Masons. Service, Wednesday, 10 AM at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to, The American Heart Association or The Scottsdale Healthcare Foundation.
