
        LONG-Evelyn Lehman. The Board of Trustees, the Board of Directors and the staff of the YWCA of the City of New York are deeply saddened at the loss of their longtime colleague and friend. As board member, President from 1982 to 1986, and Trustee in the years since, Evelyn was a gifted leader with a deep commitment to the women and girls of the YWCA/NYC, and she will be deeply missed by all of us. Rae Linefsky, Exec. Dir./CEO Constance A. Tate, President
