
        KELLY-Mrs. Thomas A. (nee June Hess), age 92, died on Thursday, May 18 from complications from a stroke in Croton, NY. Daughter of Mr. & Mrs. Harry Bellas Hess of New York City and Huntington, LI, she attended the Spence School and studied history and literature at Columbia University. She was married to Thomas A. Kelly and is survived by their five children, Barbara Johnson and Maureen Congdon of East Hampton, NY, Carolyn Kelly of Newfoundland, PA, Linda Hodgden of Enid, OK and Thomas Kelly, Jr. of Croton, NY. She is also survived by ten grandchildren, 12 great grandchildren and one great great grandchild. Funeral services will be held at St. Luke's Episcopal Church in East Hampton, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her name to the Music and Art Programs of Guild Hall in East Hampton, NY.
