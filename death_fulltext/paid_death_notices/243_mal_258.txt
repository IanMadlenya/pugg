
        HEARN--Thomas Michael Jr., of Hobe Sound, FL, and Upper Largo, Fife, Scotland, died on April 21, 2007, at the age of 82. He is survived by his wife, Helen; children, Thomas, Peter, David, Gail, and Edward; and six grandchildren. Following Naval service in the Pacific in World War II he returned to Yale and graduated in the Class of 1948. He was President of G.L. Hodson & Son, Inc., Reinsurance Intermediaries, from 1977 until his retirement in 1986 and served as a director of R.K. Carvill (Int'l Holdings) Ltd., Hamilton, Bermuda from 1990-2000. There will be a Memorial Mass at 10:30 AM, Tuesday, April 24, 2007 at St. Christopher Catholic Church, Hobe Sound, FL. Contributions may be made to: Jupiter Medical Center Foundation, 1210 South Old Dixie 01Highway, Jupiter, FL 33458.
