
        VANDERLIP-Carole Ann (previously Cam Newell, nee McGillicuddy), sculptor and author, died September 19th at her home in Chelsea, England of brain cancer. She was well loved by her husband Kelvin, who can answer all inquiries at (011) 4420 7581-8344. A memorial service will be held on October 20th at 5:30 P.M. at Our Lady of Pompeii, Carmine Street. Family flowers only, please.
