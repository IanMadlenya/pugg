
        GIBELMAN--Margaret. Wurzweiler School of Social Work of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of a great colleague, scholar, author, researcher, mentor, and leader in the social work field. She was Professor of Social Work and Director of Wurzweiler's Doctoral Program. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her sister, Susan J. Kleszewski, and family. Sheldon R.
