
        LaVIALE--Elaine. On July 11 in her hundredth year. Formerly of NYC, late of Sherman, CT. Devoted wife of the late Roger LaViale; adored mother of the late Roger LaViale Jr. Survived by daughters Colette (Mrs. Charles W.) Russell and Yvonne LaViale, both of Manhattan. Loving grandmother of eight; great grandmother of eleven; and great-great grandmother of one. Visitation July 14, 910:30AM, Horn & Thomes Funeral Home, Pawling, NY (845-855-3550). Funeral 11AM, Christ Church, Quaker Hill, Pawling NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Christ Church in memory of Mrs. LaViale.
