
        STERN--George, age 91, died on January 23, 2008, 15 days after the death of Mollie, his beloved wife of 67 years. He enjoyed a 50 year career as a metallurgist and engineer. His family, the clarinet, tennis and the Mamaroneck community nourished his profound humanity. Survived by children Susan Dalsimer, Eric Stern and Joan Stern, and grandchildren Sophie and Sam Dalsimer.
