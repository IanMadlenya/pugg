
        SCHIRMER-Reverend Thomas E., Cleveland native, died New York City May 27th, 2001 after a brief illness. He was 59 years old. Father Schirmer was the Rector of All Saints Episcopal Church in Parma before taking over as Priest in Charge at St. Andrews Church in Astoria, New York. Noted for his commitment to serving the poor, he was dubbed ''The gallant radical'' in a New York Times article profiling him in 1998. He is survived by his longtime companion, Paco Schiraldi of New York City, as well as three brothers Charles Schirmer of Lexington, Kentucky; Robert Schirmer of Liverpool, New York; and William Schirmer of Ashland, Ohio. A sister, Margaret Schirmer, lives in Phoenix, Arizona. A Funeral Mass will be held on Saturday, September 15, 2001 at All Saints Episcopal Church, Cleveland, OH. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: Gods Love We Deliver, 166 Ave of the Americas, New York, NY 10013.
