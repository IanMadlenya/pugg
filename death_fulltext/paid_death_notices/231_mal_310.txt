
        MIAO--Tung. April 26 in Princeton, NJ, at age 90. Beloved husband of Mouchi and father of four and grandfather of nine. He was a diplomat for the Republic of China in South Africa, went to the US to study, and worked 28 years as a translator for the United Nations Secretariat. A memorial service to be held at a later date. Graveside funeral service May 1, 11 AM, Kingston Presbyterian Church cemetery, NJ. In lieu of flowers, please donate to a favorite charity in his memory.
