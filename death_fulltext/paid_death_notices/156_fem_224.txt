
        ALLEN-Irma. Born in Brooklyn, NY in 1929, passed away at age 70 at her home in Tomkins Cove, NY. She is survived by her children, Joe and Nancy of Tomkins Cove, New York, Stu and Kerri of New City, NY; brothers Jerry Ivers and Allen Isacowitz, both of Florida; 5 grandchildren, Christine, Michael, Ashley, Erik and Carleigh; and one great-grandson, Cameron. Contributions to United Hospice of Rockland County and Rockland County Meals on Wheels. Services are Thursday, 12 noon, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
