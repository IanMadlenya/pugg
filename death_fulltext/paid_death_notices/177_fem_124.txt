
        GREENBERG-Barbara (Smedley), R.N. Age 59, of Stratford, Connecticut, and New York City, beloved wife of attorney Stephen H. Greenberg, died Friday, October 12, 2001, in New York Presbyterian Hospital. Mrs. Greenberg was born in Mt. Vernon, NY, January 25, 1942, daughter of the late Edward and Isabelle Smedley. She was raised in Stratford and was a graduate of Stratford High School. Her late grandfather was the noted painter, William Thomas Smedley. Survivors in addition to her husband include her aunts and cousins. Friends are invited to meet at Our Lady of Peace Church in Stratford on Monday, October 15, 2001, at 9:30 AM for a Mass of Christian Burial. Interment will follow in Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Calling hours wil be Sunday from 4 PM to 8 PM in the William R. McDonald Funeral Home, 2591 Main Street, Stratford, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Barbara's memory to Our Lady of Peace Church, 230 Park Blvd., Stratford, CT 06615.
