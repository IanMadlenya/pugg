
        ADLER-Ruth C. On December 25, 1998. Beloved wife of the late David F. Adler. Loving mother of Richard C. Adler and mother-inlaw of Andrea K. Nordell. Adoring grandmother of Jared N. Adler. Devoted aunt of Frederic and Henry Tausend. Mrs. Adler was a lifelong member of Central Synagogue. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday, December 28 at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Central Synagogue Restoration Fund.
