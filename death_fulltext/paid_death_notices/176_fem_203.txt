
        McGARRY - NOACK - Katherine ''Katie''. Of Hoboken, N.J. (formerly of North Merrick, N.Y.). Tragically on September 11, 2001 in the World Trade Center in her 31st year. Katie attended Sacred Heart School in North Merrick, graduated from Sacred Heart Academy, Hempstead in 1988. She attended St. Bonaventure College, Olean, N.Y. before graduating from St. John's University in 1992. Beloved and devoted wife of Bradley Noack. Cherished daughter of Kathryn and Everett McGarry. Loving sister of Marianne Burke and her husband Joseph, Deborah Brink and her husband Michael, Patrick McGarry, and Ellen Macri and her husband Joseph. Dear daughter-in-law of Jeanette and Robert Noack of Sidney, Australia. Fond sister-in-law of Jennilyn Noack and her husband Justin Sommers and Darren Noack and his wife Alison. Loving aunt of Kevin, Kelly and Colleen Burke, Nicholas Brink, Daniel and Ryan Macri, Edward and Darcy Noack. Katie was a proud godmother to Daniel Macri. Memorial visitation Thursday, September 27, 2001 Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 2515 North Jerusalem Rd (Exit 25N SS Parkway), East Meadow. Memorial Mass Friday, September 28, 2001 1PM at Sacred Heart Roman Catholic Church, North Merrick. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Autism Research Institute, 4182 Adams Avenue, San Diego, California 92116.
