
        CAMPBELL--Charles J., March 1, at 80. Ophthalmologist. Emeritus Professor of Ophthalmology, Columbia University and Director, Edward S. Harkness Eye Institute, Presbyterian Hospital, New York. Survived by his loving and devoted wife Mary Catherine, his children Dr. Catherine M. Campbell, Barbara I. Campbell-Kopec, and Charles A. Campbell III, his sister Mary Ann Campbell. Interment private. Funeral service to be March 7th at the Alpine Community Church at 11:00 am. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Alpine Community Church, One Old Dock Road, Alpine, NJ 07620, The Garden State Academy of Music, 420 Valley Brook Avenue, Lyndhurst, NJ 07071 or the VNA & Hospice Foundation, 1110 35th Lane, Vero Beach, Florida 32960.
