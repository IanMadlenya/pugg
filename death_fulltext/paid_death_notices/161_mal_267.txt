
        LEW-Anna Jacob. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community, beloved mother of Marvin E. Jacob, Board member of our affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, who, with his wife, Atara, established RIETS' Shalom Yaakov Jacob Scholarship. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
