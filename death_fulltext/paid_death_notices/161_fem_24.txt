
        SPRINGER-Sylvia, age 95, on June 1, 2000, in the aftermath of a broken hip operation. The beloved mother of Julie Springer, Dr. Philip Springer and the late Anthony Springer, Esq. She was a fine pianist in her earlier years, a student of the renowned pianist Clarence Adler. Her noble nature was incapbale of a small or mean gesture. For 25 years she was owner with daughter Julie of the popular Greenwich Village toy shop ''The Gingerbread House''. She spared no inch of courage in helping her children through life. She was the widow of the late M. P. Springer, Esq. Graveside service Sunday 1:30PM at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farnmingdale, NY.
