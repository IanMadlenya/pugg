
        GREENWALD-Anna. Of Miami Beach, Fla. at age 101. Beloved mother of the late Jerry and Dotty Ross, Gill and Ethel Ross and George and Billie Ross. Devoted sister of Bea Hyman. Cherished grandmother of Rick and Iris Ross, David and Cathy Jensen, Vicki Ross, Tom and Stephanie Suskin and Nanci Ross. Adored great grandmother of Michael and Amy, Barrie, Erica and Emily. Funeral Services at graveside Wednesday, February 18th at Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, N.J. She will forever be in the hearts of all whose lives she touched.
