Also surviving, sister, Carol Lusthaus (husband Steven), sister-in-law, Harriet Marshall (husband Calvin), nephews Adam and Jason and niece, Marisa. Devoted grandmother of Matthew, Melissa, Michelle, Jeffrey, Jennifer, Nicole, Alex, Julia and Richard, ''her angels''. Funeral service today 1PM at Zion Memorial Chapel in Mamaroneck 914 - 381 - 1809. www.zionmemorialchapel. com. Interment Sharon Gardens.
