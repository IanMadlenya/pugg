
        SCHISGALL-James (Jim). Truly a remarkable, intelligent and funny guy. Died at age 67 on September 10, 2001, after a lengthy and brave battle with cancer for seven years. Beloved and dedicated husband of Beth. Devoted and loving father of Stuart, Alex and Robbie. Son of the late Lillian and Oscar Shisgall, brother of the late Dr. Richard Schisgall. A wonderful friend and uncle to all those he cared for and will be missed by all whose lives he touched. Service Friday 1 PM, Temple Chaverim, Plainview.
