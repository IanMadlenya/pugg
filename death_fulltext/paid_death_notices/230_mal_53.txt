
        FORD--Joseph M., MD. 86. On February 16, 2006 at his home in Sagaponack, NY. Retired Attending Surgeon and former Chairman, Department of Thoracic Surgery, Roosevelt Hospital. Survived by his wife Barbara White Ford, his daughter Sara Eliot Ford of Bridgehampton, NY and his son John Ford of New York City. Predeceased in 1999 by his first wife Mary Eliot Ford. A memorial will be held at a later date.
