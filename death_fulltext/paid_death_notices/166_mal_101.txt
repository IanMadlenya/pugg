
        LADOUSIER-Roderick G. On November 6, 2000 in St. Louis, MO after a long illness. He was 77. Beloved brother and uncle, Roderick Ladousier is survived by his sister, Hazel Mitchell of San Francisco, CA, sister-in-law, Joanne Ladousier of Grand Rapids, MI and many blessed nephews and nieces. He graduated from Ohio University with a business degree in 1948. He retired as Comptroller of the American Lung Association after 15 years. Long time resident of Manhattan, a memorial gathering will be scheduled at a later date. Funeral service will take place at St. John Vianney Church, 4101 Clyde Park Ave., SW, in Wyoming, MI at 1:00 pm on November 18th.
