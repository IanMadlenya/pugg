
        ALBAM-Manny. The Manhattan School of Music is saddened by the death of renowned composer, arranger, conducter and jazz faculty member Manny Albam. Since 1993 Manhattan School musicians have been privileged to share the insight, humor, passion and humanity that made Mr. Albam's presence in the jazz world so remarkable. As a colleague and friend he will be missed by all. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
