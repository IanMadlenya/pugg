
        MAYER--Dolf 1918 - 2006 Scarsdale NY. Beloved husband of the late Jill Russek, father of Valerie (Tony Torcia), grandfather of Anthony Tiliacos, Danielle & Adam Torcia. Born Germany, educated France, Holland & Scotland. Served in US Army, translator at liberation of Buchenwald & Nuremberg Trials. Principal in Mayer Cos. A generous gentleman who lived life on his own terms. Contributions to Scleroderma Federation, Teaneck NJ.
