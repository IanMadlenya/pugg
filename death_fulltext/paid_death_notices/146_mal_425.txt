
        CROHN-David. Died in Israel, his home, March 19, age 77. Survived by his wife of 50 years, the former Annette Lash, three daughters Ariella Yosefi of Herzlia, Yael Duhl of Jerusalem, Aviva Plaut, of Magnolia, KY, and eight grandchildren. Our beloved husband, father and grandfather has left us forever. He will be sorely missed by his family and many friends.
