
        FREUNDLICH - Roza (nee Lobatto). Passed away on August 2, 2002 at her home in New Rochelle, N.Y. She was born in Amsterdam on June 25, 1907 and was predeceased by her husband Fred Freundlich and a sister in Holland. She is survived by her brother Fred Lobatto of New York City and her nieces Suzanne Neulander of Wheeling, Illinois and Barbara Taylor of Hamburg, New York. She is also survived by several other nieces and nephews. Her kind and devoted friends Bobbie and Dave Abrahams of New Rochelle and her many friends.
