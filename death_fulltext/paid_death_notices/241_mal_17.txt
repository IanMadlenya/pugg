
        NADASI--John Ivan, died January 31, 2007 at age 73. Born May 15, 1933 in Zagreb, Yugoslavia, to Desider and Ljerka (nee Hirschl) Nadasi. John served his country in Kiel, Germany, with the US Army, who liberated him. John was the co-founder of the Blue Star Youth Soccer Club, and worked as a Ship Broker for Universal Transport Corporation for 50 years. Survived by his loving children David (wife Rose Anne), Michael, and daughter Barbara (Jeff) Levy, and cherished grandchildren Meredith, Scott, Paul, Eric, and Alex. Predeceased by a grandchild Stephen. Services were handled by Zion Memorial Chapel in Mamaroneck 914381-1809.
