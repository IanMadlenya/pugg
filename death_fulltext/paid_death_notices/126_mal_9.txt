
        KIRSCHENBAUM-Richard. The Jewish Community Center of Harrison announces with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed Founding Member.         KIRSCHENBAUM - Richard. Of Harrison, NY and Palm Beach on June 30. Loving husband of the late Elaine. Devoted father of Charles and Penny. Survived by grandchildren Josh and Matthew and brother Henry. Graveside service Wednesday, 1 PM, Riverville cemetery, Glenville, Conn.
