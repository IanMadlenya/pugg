
        FREIDENBERG--Edgar I., Jr. 91, Scarsdale, NY. Beloved husband of Jeanne Freund & the late Margaret Watters. Father of Robert, Ken and Janet. Grandfather of Jacob and Hannah. Stepfather of Joseph and Janice Meyer, step-grandfather of Alex and Daniel. President Brittany Fabrics, WWII veteran, avid golfer, kind thoughtful man and known workaholic who will be missed by all who loved him. Memorial service 2:30 PM, Sunday, January 29 at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave, East Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to White Plains Hospital Center, Development Office.
