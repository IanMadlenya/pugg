
        GOLD-Abraham (Abe). Beloved husband of Honey. Devoted and caring stepfather of Steven Hirschberg, Rada Salomon, Peggy Noonan Foster & husband Dennis Foster. Cherished grandfather of Bran Noonan. Loving brother of the late Bill, Rose Rose, Rickey Lieberman and Sylvia Farber. Mentor and friend to many. His company, A. Gold Fabric was honored by the tie industry with its Achievement Award. He served with distinction as a Colonel in the ETO during World War II. Family receiving at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Sunday 7-9PM with services Monday 11:30AM. Contributions to N.C.J.W. New York Section, 9 E. 69 St., NYC 10021 or the charity of your choice would be appreciated.
        GOLD-Abraham. Congregration Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Abraham Gold. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
