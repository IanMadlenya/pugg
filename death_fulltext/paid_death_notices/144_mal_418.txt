
        FIELDS-Ernest W. Of Morristown, New Jersey, formerly of Chatham, NJ on January 22, 1999. Husband of Maxine (Rabe) Fields. Father of Frederick of San Francisco. Step-father of Stephen Smythe of Ohio & Susan Schreiber of NJ. Brother of Ronsia of Cupertino, CA. Grandfather of Peter of San Francisco and Alexander of New York City. Step-grandfather of Lance and David Smythe of Maine, and Brian and Kevin O'Dea of Illinois. He is also survived by one step great grandson. A memorial service will be held at the Stanley Congregational Church, Chatham, NJ, Monday, February 1, 1999 at 11 AM. Those who which may contribute in his memory to the Ernest W. Fields and Friends Scholarship Fund at Lincoln Memorial University, Cumberland Gap Parkway, Harrogate, TN. 37752. For further information contact the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, Chatham, N.J.
