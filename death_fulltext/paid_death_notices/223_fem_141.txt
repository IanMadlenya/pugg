
        MAHIN -- Stephanie Benet. Daughter of the late Stephen Vincent and Rosemary Carr Benet, died August 19, at her home, 55 Cleveland St., Arlington, MA 02474. She attended the Brearley School in New York City, Swarthmore College, and was a graduate of the Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital School of Nursing in New York. She was the mother of eight children and had six grandchildren. She is also survived by a brother, Thomas Benet, of San Francisco. Her sister, Rachel Lewis, predeceased her. For information contact Keefe Funeral Home, Arlington, MA; 781648-5544. Contributions may be made to Edmundite Fathers and Brothers, PO Box 399, Mystic, CT 06355.
