
        HOLESHA -- Winifred M. ''Winnie'', 57, of Palm Harbor, Florida, passed away peacefully on Good Friday, April 6, 2007 at Hospice House Woodside, Hospice of the Florida Suncoast, in Pinellas Park, FL. Originally from the Chicago area, she was an Advanced Nurse Practitioner, with 27 years of RN experience including Loyola University Medical Center, UIC Medical Center, West Suburban Hospital, Alexian Brothers Medical Center, Mount Sinai Hospital, and most recent at Tampa General Hospital. She is a graduate of Trinity High School, River Forest, Elmhurst College and Loyola University's Masters of Science in Nursing. Winnie is the beloved wife of Jim; loving mother of Jamie Ann and Jennifer M. Holesha; cherished daugher of Concetta (the late Joseph) Farley; dear sister of Joseph (Susan) Farley; fond aunt of Andrew and Juliana Farley; daugherin-law of Bernice Holesha; sister-in-law of Sheryl (the late William). Winnie was cremated and her ashes scattered on the waters of the Gulf of Mexico by her family. Memorial Visitation Saturday, April 14, from 9:00 a.m. until time of funeral service 11:30 a.m. at Gibbons Funeral Home, 134 South York Road, (North of Saint Charles Road) Elmhurst, Illinois. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to any of the following: Susan G. Komen for the Cure, the Moffitt Cancer Foundation (Tampa), or the Hospice of the Florida Suncoast.
