
        ROSS-Joshua. Dr. Joshua Felix Ross died peacefully in his sleep on April 20 at home in Boca Raton, FL. Born in NYC in 1917, he was the beloved husband of Grayce; brother of Raymond; father of Mark, David and Robert; father-inlaw to Roni, Margaret and Donna, and grandfather to Lindsay, Emily, and Colin. He was a gentle, loving, and thoughtful man whose life was devoted to family and friends. His passing will be mourned by all who knew and loved him. Funeral services will be held at Temple Beth El in Boca Raton, Thursday, April 22 at 3PM.
