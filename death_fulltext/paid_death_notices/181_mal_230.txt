
        NABATOFF-Dr. Robert A. Beloved husband of Joan. Dear father of Diane, Ross and Richard. Loving grandfather of Alison, Andrew and Nathan. Devoted brother of Gene Norman, Sylvia Mandelbaum and the late Ruth Lichtenstein. Services on Friday. February 22, 12 noon at Temple Emanu-El Fifth Avenue at 65th St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Mount Sinai Hospital in his honor, where he spent fifty years of service.
        NABATOFF-Dr. Robert A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with profound sorrow the passing of Dr. Robert A. Nabatoff. A brilliant physician, he served his patients with extraordinary sensitivity that reflected his reverence for life. Equally, did he enjoy the admiration and respect of his medical colleagues. With his beloved Joan, and their children, we mourn his death while giving thanks for the glory of his life. His memory will endure as a magnificent blessing. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Senior Rabbi Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
