
        STARK-Arthur. Arbitrator, died suddenly at his home Tuesday, December 3, 2002 at the age of 83. Mr. Stark was a native New Yorker. He is survivied by his wife Dorothy of 47 years, four children, four grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be announced.
        STARK-Arthur. Labor arbitration has lost a beloved giant, colleague and friend. His innate sense of fairness, analytical ability and humanity made him a model. 1977 National President and member since 1950, he had extraordinary wit and charm. Mattye M. Gandel, Nat'l Academy of Arbitrators Region 2 Chair
