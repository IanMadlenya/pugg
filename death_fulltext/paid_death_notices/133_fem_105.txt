
        ROSENSON-Marguerite Bailey. February 8, 1998. Age 97. Beloved mother of Barbara Bailey Bedevian and mother-in-law of A. Jack Bedevian. Former editor and publisher of the Harlem Valley Times in Amenia, N.Y. and member of the Board of Visitors of the New York State Commission of Mental Hygiene. Her courageous spirit touched all who knew her.
