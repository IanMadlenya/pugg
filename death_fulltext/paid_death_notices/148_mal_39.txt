
        DEAVERS-Karl A. The board of Trustees, Headmaster, Faculty, Staff, and the Collegiate School community mourn the loss of former Trustee Karl A. Deavers. Mr. Deavers, an Elder of the Collegiate Church, served the school as a Trustee from 1987 to 1993. As an officer of the Board, his guidance and wise counsel were highly valued. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Kerry, his children, and the entire Deavers family. Robert Hollander, '51, President Of The Board Of Trustees Jacob A. Dresden, Headmaster
