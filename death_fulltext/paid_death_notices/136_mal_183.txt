
        SALAMON - Alexander ''Aba.'' Age 90, born in Kolysvar, Hungary. Lived in Israel, NY and West Palm Beach. Lived a full life and enjoyed every moment of it. His joie di vivre will always be an inspiration to his family: son Itamar and his wife Linda, grandchildren Peggy, Tommy, Betsy and her husband Michael. He leaves behind his loving companion Sally Epstein. The family expresses its gratitude for the devoted care he received in his final years from Martha Flores Lopez.
