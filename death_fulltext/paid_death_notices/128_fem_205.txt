
        RATNER-Goldie. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Goldie Ratner, beloved mother of Shelly Ratner, who has been a dedicated leader and generous supporter of UJA-Federation as Co-Chair of our Apparel Division and as the former Captain of our Vanguard Solicitors Club. To Mrs. Ratner's beloved husband, Milton, and her sons, Shelly and Kenny, we extend our sincerest sympathies. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
