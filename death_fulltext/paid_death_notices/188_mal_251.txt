
        HERZIG-William F., M.D. Loving husband, father, grandfather, great grandfather. Survived by his loving wife, Rita Wynne Herzig; son Dr. Steven Herzig and his wife, Dr. Cathy; daughter Karen Herzig Sherman and her husband, Howard; grandchildren Melissa, Robin, Amanda, Shoshana and Jeremy; and great grandchildren Samantha and Alison. He was born September 27, 1909. He was a graduate of the University of Vienna Medical School in Austria, 1938 and a gastroenterologist for 50 years at Beth Israel Medical Center. He will be remembered as a man of integrity, a brilliant and caring doctor who was always available to help those in need. Services Wednesday, 12 noon, Plaza Memorial 91st and Amsterdam Avenue.
