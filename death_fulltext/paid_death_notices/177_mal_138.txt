
        PALMADESSA-John J. On October 8, 2001 at age 73. Loving husband of Mary, and devoted father to Danielle and Douglas. Grandfather to Douglas Jr. Brother-inlaw to Anthony Aglione who was his ''brother at heart'' and many devoted nieces and nephews. Korean War Veteran, self-made entrepreneur and inventor. Founder and owner of Red Ball Interior Demolition. Resident of New York City and Palm Beach, FL. Former resident of Ho-Ho-Kus and Spring Lake, NJ. He was a giant of a man in his achievements. His generosity, caring, and compassion will be in our hearts always. John has inspired many pople and was greatly admired. He will be missed by all who have crossed his path. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Tuesday and Wednesday, 2-5 and 7-9 pm. For further information regarding Funeral Mass, call Frank E. Campbell, 212-288-3500.
