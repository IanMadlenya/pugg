
        THEISEN--Vincent A. (Ty), 91, a prominent attorney in Wilmington, DE, died suddenly on April 17, 2005. Ty graduated from Colgate University and Harvard Law School. He is survived by his wife, Mary Duggan Jornlin; three daughters, Deborah Clough, The Villages, FL, Pamela Morss, Manchester, MA, and Barbara McGonagle, Ft. Myers, FL, 5 grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren.
