
        BROAS-Donald S. On November 22, 2000 at age 60. Beloved husband of Betty Jane Langer-Broas. Devoted father of Nancy Gray, Donald Jr., Kimberley and Gregory. Cherished grandfather of Hayley Gray. Former president Excelcare Health System Inc, executive vice president Interfaith Medical Center, chief executive officer Health Care Development International, Inc., president Hospital for Special Surgery, trustee Springfield College and Medic Alert Foundation. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Sunday 6-9 PM, with service Monday 3:30 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
