
        WEINSTEIN-Max J., M.D. On February 3, 1998. The last of seven children, at the age of 92. Adored by his patients. Beloved husband of the late Esther, devoted father to his son Kenneth who followed in his footsteps, his daughter Marcia who passed away in 1958, and his daughter-in-law Frida. Services private. Contributions in his name can be made to Isabella Geriatric Center, 515 Audubon Avenue, New York, New York 10040.
