
        GOODMAN--Mildred Noskin. Beloved mother of Jennifer, wife of Peter Marco, and Eileen, wife of Richard Ekstract; devoted grandparent to Mitchell, Darryl and Salma Marco and Janet, Michael and Steven Ekstract. Mildred will be remembered for her lifetime commitment to Hadassah, her endless cheerful demeanor and long list of friends. Mildred was always a comfort to those who knew her and sought nothing more than the happiness of friends and family. Her deep and abiding humanity will never be forgotten.
