
        GREEN-Bertha K. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of one of its most devoted and distinguished members. She graced our community for decades with her warmth, optimism and love of Judaism. We extend our condolences to her illustrious son, Mr. Morris Green, Honorary Chairman of our Board of Trustees, and to her entire family. May they all be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
