
        POPKIN-George. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of George Popkin, beloved longtime leader and generous supporter. George was an Honorary Life Member of our National Commission and blessed our Palm Beach Regional Board with his warmth, wit and wisdom. His sage counsel, active participation and loving personality will be greatly missed. We extend our profound condolences to the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
