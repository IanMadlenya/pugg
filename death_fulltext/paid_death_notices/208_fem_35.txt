
        GROSS--Sydelle (Windt) List, 88, of Manhattan on May 4, 2004. Devoted wife of the late Martin J. List and the late Sidney L. Gross and devoted mother to the late Lawrence I. List. Sydelle's whole life was dedicated to her family. She is deeply loved and will always be admired by her surviving family, brother, Robert S. Windt of Brooklyn, daughter-in-law Bobye G. List, granddaughters Dr. Deborah G. List and Mali List Mayer and sons-in-law, William Mayer and David Schwartz, all of New York City. Funeral service will be held on Thursday, May 6, 1:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., NYC. The family suggests contributions in memory of Sydelle can be made to the List Family Fund for Jewish Culture in memory of Lawrence List at the Brooklyn Heights Synagogue, 131 Remsen St., Brooklyn, New York 11201.
