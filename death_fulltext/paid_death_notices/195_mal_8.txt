
        AEZEN - Joseph, passed away at his home on Sunday, March 23, 2003. He is survived by his friend of 20 years, Elaine Rosomoff; the mother of his children, Mollie Aezen, daughter Samantha Harris Aezen and son Peter Michael Aezen. He was delighted to learn from his friend Elaine Rosomoff that the Rittenhouse Square flower bed that he had enjoyed for much of his life was to be dedicated in his memory by longtime friend Ralph Lauren. He will be deeply missed.
