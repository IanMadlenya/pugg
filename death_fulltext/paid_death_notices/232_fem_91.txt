
        DRUMMER--Pamela Ruth, loving daughter, sister and aunt, died on May 7th of injuries suffered at her home in Redwood City, CA. She was 55. Pam was the beloved daughter of Mickey and Juanita Drummer, the much loved sister of Carol Sattler and Ken Drummer, and sister-in-law to Ted Sattler and Nan Drummer. She is also survived by her nephews, Jamie, Greg and Zack, and nieces, Lindsay and Stephanie. She will be missed by all who knew and loved her. Donations may be made in her name to the Santa Clara Valley Medical Center/Burn Center Trust Fund at 751 South Bascom Avenue, San Jose, CA 95128, Attn: Jill Sprol.
