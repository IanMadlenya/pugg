
        SHEA-Joseph, 76. Of Spring Lake Hts, NJ, formerly of NYC, on April 9, 2001. Mr. Shea was former owner and CEO of Harbor Hill Company in NYC. Predeceased by his brother Raymond. Beloved husband of Kathleen. Dear father of Joseph, Thomas, Frances, Daniel and Kathleen Munson; brother of John and Ellen Hunt. Dear grandfather of 13. Visitation Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 at the Meehan Funeral Home, 555 Warren Avenue, Spring Lake Hts, NJ. A Blessing will be held on Thursday, 9:30AM, St. Mark's R.C. Church, Sea Girt, NJ. Burial, St. Catharine's Cemetery in Wall, NJ. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Parkinsons Fund, C/O Robert Wood Johnson School of Medicine, 97 Paterson Street, New Brunswick, NJ 08901.
