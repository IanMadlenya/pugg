
        PLAUT--Robert J., of White Plains, formerly of Larchmont, NY, died Monday, February 9, 2004. He was 84. Son of the late Ralph and Gertrude Plaut. Loving husband of Patricia Maimin Plaut. Adoring father to Betsy Lublin. Cherished grandfather to Jennifer and Debbie. Brother of Edward Plaut and deceased brother Ralph, Jr. Funeral service Wednesday at 12 noon at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave at South Broadway, White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions in his memory may be made to the White Plains Hospital, Davis Ave. at Post Rd, White Plains, NY 10601 or the charity of your choice.
