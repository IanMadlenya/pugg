
        BREALEY - John. Died peacefully at New York Hospital on December 19th, 2002, after a long illness, aged 77. Paintings conservator and an Englishman in New York. Much loved father of Nick and grandfather of Sam, and will be sadly missed by his family and friends. Funeral service will take place at Frank Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, on Monday, December 30th, at 3:30 PM. Donations may be made to the American Stroke Association, 122 East 42nd Street, New York, NY 10017.
