
        BAROUCH -- Dennis Alan, Ph.D. Passed away September 24, 2004 in Riverdale, NY at age 52. Graduate of Columbia University, Ph.D. in Psychology from Yeshiva University. Loving father of Oren, and Jessie, beloved son of the late Herman N. and Edna Barouch. Devoted brother of Norman, (Laurie) and Andi (Steve) uncle to Jason, Ricky, Nicole and Ben, stepson of Gloria and loving companion to Beth. Funeral service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, New York City, on Sunday, September 26 at 11:15 AM. Dennis will be missed by all who knew him.
