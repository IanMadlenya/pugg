
        KATZ--Murray S. On October 18, 2005 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Natalie. Survived by numerous nieces, nephews, great nieces and nephews and cousins. He had a large giving and loving heart. Services Thursday, October 20, 10 AM at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, NY, NY. Contributions to American ORT, 817 Broadway, NY, NY 10003, or NCJW New York Section, 820 2nd Avenue, NY, NY 10017.
        $(6$)KATZ--Murray S. The American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra Board of Directors and staff mourn the passing of Murray Katz. Murray's sense of humor, love of music, photography, traveling and Israel was unparalleled. His commitment and generosity will contribute to the future of the IPO for generations to come. We offer our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Natalie. We shall always remember Murray from the Berkshires to Buckingham Palace! Zubin Mehta and Itzhak Perlman, Co-Chairmen Lynn Syms, President David A. Hirsch, Charles I. Petschek, Elaine Wolfensohn, Vice Presidents Morton S. Ackerman, Treasurer Suzanne K.
