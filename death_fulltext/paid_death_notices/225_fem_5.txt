
        ASSUMMA--Clara M. A former resident of Eastchester, NY, died peacefully with her children by her side, on September 29, 2005. Born in Queens, NY, on July 9, 1926, to Joseph and Rosalia Cannata. Devoted wife of the late Daniel for 46 years (April 17, 1995). Beloved mother of Daniel of Somers, NY, Frank of Old Chatham, NY, Joanna of Pottersville, NJ, Peter of Chestnut Ridge, NY, and John of Waccabuc, NY. Caring sister of Antoinette Cannata, Bronx, NY, and predeceased brothers Placido and Salvatore. Cherished grandmother of 15, great-grandmother of one. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated, 10 AM, on Tuesday, Immaculate Heart of Mary, in Scarsdale, NY. Burial will follow, Mount Calvary Cemetery, White Plains, NY. Visiting Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Donations may be made in Clara M. Assumma's memory to WGA Caddie Scholarship Fund, c/o Assumma-Shankey Funeral Home, 34 North Summit Street, Pearl River, NY 10965. Assumma-Shankey Funeral Home, Pearl River, NY: 845735-4849.
