
        CASEY--Richard Conway, honorable March 22, 2007, beloved father of Richard and his wife Michelle, cherished grandfather of Allison. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. A graduate of Holy Cross and Georgetown Law School who later served as a federal judge for the Southern District of New York. Friends may call Sunday 7-9 and Monday 2-5 and 7-9 at Frank E. Campbell 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Massive Christian burial Tuesday 10am St. Patricks Cathedral 5th Ave. at 50th St NYC. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Guiding Eyes for the Blind, Yorktown Heights, NY.
