
        McVAY--Rosanne (nee Magdol) Passed peacefully, October 26th, after a long life that included a vibrant career in publishing at The New Yorker and Collier's. Rosanne survived two husbands and three brothers, is survived by nieces Lynn Magdol and Carole Aronson, nephews Joseph Magdol and Michael Magdol, and by many, many friends who have delighted in her persistent elan and inspired by her dedication to living life to its very fullest.
