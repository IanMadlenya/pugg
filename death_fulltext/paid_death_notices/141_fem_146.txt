
        ZIMMERMAN-Honora. On October 10, 1998. Beloved mother of Daniel and Matthew. Cherished grandmother to Natalie. Loving daughter of Mildred and the late Edwin Coane. Dear sister of Linda Copeland and Ethan Coane. Services Thursday, 5 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. The warmth, wit, wisdom and generosity that Honey brought to our lives will be forever cherished and honored in the memories of her family and friends.
