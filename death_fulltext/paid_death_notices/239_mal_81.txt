
        KOCH--Robert P., 62, lost a year-long battle with cancer and passed away with his family by his side on December 2nd, 2006. Resident of Westbury, NY and Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Survived by Jeanne, his wife of 6 years, his children Bernadine (John), Christine (David), Kevin (Pamela), and Daniel (Jennifer), his stepchildren Hayley (Marc), and Rene', his grandchildren Abigail, Jack, Benjamin and Madison, his brothers George (Maryann) and Richard (Linda) and his loyal companion, Gracie. Son of the late George and Mary Koch/Baggs. Obtained a Criminal Justice Degree from John Jay College. Joined the NYC Transit Police in 1965 and achieved the rank of captain at the age of 27. Former President of the Superior Officers' Council and the Captains Endowment Association. Received his Juris Doctor Law Degree from St. John's University and was a principal and General Counsel of a dental insurance company. At the time of his death he was a practicing attorney, a retired NYC Police Captain and a member of the Board of Zoning Appeals in the Incorporated Village of Westbury. A funeral mass will be held at Our Lady of Hope In Carle Place, NY on Saturday at 10am.
