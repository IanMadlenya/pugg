
        WIDDER-Herman. Of New York City and Montauk, Long Island. On April 16 at age 83. President of Widder Bros., Inc. and the Widder Foundation. Survived by Ruth Laurette, Lynnette and Thilo Widder, & Kolbein Finsnes. Memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue, on Thursday, April 19th, at 11:15 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to The New York Times Neediest Cases.
        WIDDER-Herman. The Board, staff and musicians of the Orchestra of St. Luke's note with great sorrow the passing of Herman Widder, and extend our heartfelt condolences to our valued Board member Ruth Widder and her family.
