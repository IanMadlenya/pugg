
        SHIPLEY-Madeleine S. Beloved wife, mother, mother-in-law, grandmother, friend, Realtor nonpareil and fifth-generation New Yorker died November 4, 1998 at her home, of complications from emphysema. She will be sorely missed by all those whose lives she touched so profoundly. A memorial service will be held November 14th, 1PM at the Church of the Heavenly Rest. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Madeleine's name to the Hassenfeld Children's Center for Cancer and Blood Disorders, c/o NYU Medical, 317 East 34 St, NY NY 10016.
