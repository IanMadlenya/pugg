
        AKHTAR-M. Akbar, of Manhattan, died suddenly Sunday, March 30. He was a vice president at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, and an economist who was widely known and respected. A warm, kind man, he will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Condolences to his son Erik, who lives in Manhattan, and to his mother and sister, who live in Pakistan. Services will be held on Thursday, April 3, 1:30PM, Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue (81st Street). In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made in Akbar's memory to St. Hilda's and St. Hugh's School, 619 West 114th Street, N.Y., N.Y. 10025.
