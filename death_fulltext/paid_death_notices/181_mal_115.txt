
        BACKER-Bernard. Born July 18, 1918. Died on February 7, 2002. Former Senior Vice President, The Segal Company. Former President, the Workmen's Circle. Lifelong social democrat and fighter for social justice. Cherished husband of Henrietta. Father of Louise Uphoff, Jo Laird & Richard Backer. Eight grandchildren, one great-grandchild. In lieu of flowers, donations to: DSA, 180 Varick St., NY, NY 10014, or NJ Geriatric Center of the Workmen's Circle, 225 W. Jersey St., Elizabeth, NJ 07207.
        BACKER-Bernard. The Workmen's Circle/Arbeter Ring mourns the passing of our former President and longtime activist, Bernard Backer. A member of our organization for 60 years, his deep dedicaton to our work in the Cultural Jewish and Social Action arenas were constants in his many leadership positions. He was tireless in his devotion to our goals and ideals, and will serve as an example for generations to come. Our deepest condolences to his family. Martin Krupnick, President Robert Kestenbaum Executive Director
