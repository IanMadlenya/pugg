
        TASCH-Ralph Joshua, 86, of Palm Beach, formerly of New York City, passed away November 1, 1998. He served his country during World War II, then started a long career as CEO of Vita Food Products. He is survived by his three daughters, Wendy, Melissa and Amanda, with two loving grandchildren. Services will be Wednesday, November 4, 1 PM, graveside, at Mount Carmel Cemetery. For further information call Riverside Gordon, West Palm Bch., Fla.
