
        GILBERT--Leonard, age 78, died on July 27, 2006 at his home in Great Neck after a long and valiant battle with cancer, with his daughter Geri Gilbert and his loving partner Lorraine Lester by his side. He is also survived by his brother Daniel and his son Steven. A native New Yorker and a devoted sports fan whose heart belonged to the New York Mets, he loved travel, jazz, theater and most of all, being with his family and friends. His humor, generosity and big hearted presence will be greatly missed. A memorial service will be held at 9 AM on August 5th in Great Neck, contact family.
