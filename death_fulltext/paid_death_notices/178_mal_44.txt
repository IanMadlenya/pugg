
        BROWN - Captain Patrick J. FDNY. Heroically died in the line of duty at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. A 21 year veteran of the FDNY. Proud Captain of Ladder 3, former Captain of 69 Engine, former Lieutenant of 28 Truck and former firefighter of Rescue 2, Rescue 1 and 26 Truck. Patrick was one of the most highly decorated active members of the FDNY. He was also a decorated Marine in Vietnam, and award winner of the Silver Star. Patrick also received the Holy Name Society Man of the Year Award. Patrick is survived by his father John (Retired FBI) & his mother, the late Ruth. Loving brother of Michael Brown, M.D., former Firefighter of Engine 37 and his wife Janet, his sister Carolyn Negron and her husband Hector. Memorial visitation will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday, November 7th and Thursday November 8th, 2 to 4 and 7 to 9PM. Memorial Mass Friday, 2PM, at St. Patrick's Cathedral, 5th Avenue at 51st Street. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to Ladder 3 Assistance Fund, 108 East 18th Street, New York, N.Y. 10003.
