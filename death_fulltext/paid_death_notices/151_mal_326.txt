
        BERLOWE-Jay B., MD. A long time resident of Cold Spring Harbor, NY, passed away on August 12, 1999 at the age of 72. A graduate of Allegheny College, he completed medical studies at SUNY Downstate in 1952. After completing a residency and fellowship in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry at Kings County Medical Center, he began his private practice on Long Island, joining the staff at Huntington Hospital in 1965. In 1988, he was elected as Fellow Member of The American Psychiatric Association. He continued to practice full-time up until his death. Dr. Berlowe is survived by his wife, Nina, and by his two sons, Andrew and Matthew. He was buried at the Mt. Pleasant Cemetery in Westchester on August 20. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be sent to Allegheny College, Meadville, PA 16335
