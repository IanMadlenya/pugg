
        ELIASBERG-Harold. 88, a longtime resident of Sands Point, died in Phoenix, AZ, on January 10, 2003. He is survived by his wife Svea (Singer), his daughter Peggy (Cochran), his grandson Christopher (Cochran) and his brother Jay. He received a Bachelor of Arts degree from Cornell University in 1934, was a member of the New York Stock Exchange for 65 years and served as a Lieutenant - Commander in the US Navy in World War II.
