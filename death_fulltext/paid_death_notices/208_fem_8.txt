
        ROONEY--Marguerite. The Directors, Trustees and Staff of the Edmund Niles Huyck Preserve mourn the loss of our Chairman, Marge Rooney. Marge was an extraordinary woman and inspired us all with her selfless commitment to the Preserve and our community. Her humor and generous heart will always be with us. We extend our most sincere condolences to the Rooney family. Laura Carter, President
