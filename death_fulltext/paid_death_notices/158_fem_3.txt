
        SHULTZ-Florence Reiser. Died peacefully on February 27, 2000, at the age of ninety-two. She was the wife of the late Irving Shultz, sister of the late Helen Sheller, and aunt of the late Ed Sheller. She is survived by her brother-in-law, Robert Sheller, and her nieces and nephews, Elly, John, Daniel, and Kate Rufer, and Alex and Chris Sheller. She will be deeply missed by those who loved her.
