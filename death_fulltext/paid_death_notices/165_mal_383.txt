
        MORFOGEN-James. Age 53, of Fort Lee, NJ, formerly of NYC, passed away at his sister's home in Glen Rock, NJ, on Saturday, October 28, 2000. Beloved and cherished son of Christine (nee Christakos) and the late James G. Morfogen. Cherished brother of Elaine and George Morfogen. Loving uncle of Kristine and Alexander John Morfogen. Adored by his aunts, uncles, cousins and friends. James was an assistant manager at Colony Records, Inc, NYC, from 1993 to present. Prior to that he was employed by Capital Cities/ ABC, Inc, as the supervisor at ABC Music Library in NYC from 1986 to 1993. All funeral services private. Interment George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus, NJ. For information contact Vander Plaat Colonial Home, Inc, Fair Lawn, NJ.
