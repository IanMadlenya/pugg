
        CROWLEY--John S. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, staff and volunteers of Thirteen/WNET New York, we express our sorrow at the passing of John S. Crowley. A former chairman of Thirteen's Board of Trustees, he made great contributions to the health and welfare of people around the world through his charitable work with the American Foundation for the Blind and Helen Keller International. We extend our condolences to his wife and family. Steven Rattner Chairman William F. Baker President
