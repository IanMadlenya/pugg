
        ROSENSTONE--Howard. The Officers, Board of Directors and Staff of Dramatists Play Service mourn the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague. Howard was Vice President and a member of the Dramatist Play Service Board of Directors for over 10 years. Everyone will miss his love of the theater and enthusiam and excitement for his clients. Our deep sympathy goes to his partners, Ron and Phyllis, his companion John and sister Elaine. Dramatists Play Service Stephen Sultan, President
