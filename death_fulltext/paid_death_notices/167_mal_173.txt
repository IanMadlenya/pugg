
        SIRULNICK-Dr. Ralph. Age 93. Died on December 2nd in Boca Raton, FL. His sons, Sid and Leon, four grandchildren, 2 great-grandchildren and two sisters have lost a uniquely courageous, intellectually inquisitive, funny and loving leader of our family. And he voted correctly! Formerly practiced podiatry in New York City and lived in Brooklyn until 1982.
