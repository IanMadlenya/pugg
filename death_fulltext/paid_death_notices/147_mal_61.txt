
        McGRATH-Albert Nicholson. On April 4th, 1999. Beloved son of the late Lillian Nicholson McGrath and John Francis McGrath, M.D. Beloved brother of Edward H., S.J., of Orissa, India, Robert W., M.D., of Georgetown, SC and the late Lillian Ryan and John F., Jr., M.D. Also survived by ten nieces and nephews and twenty grand-nieces and nephews. Founder and President of Friends of LaGuardia Place, a community organization that created a park in the Village in honor of the late Mayor of New York, Fiorello LaGuardia. Reposing at Perazzo Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., NYC, Tuesday, 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 9:30 AM, St. Joseph's R.C. Church. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to Friends of LaGuardia Place, 532 LaGuardia Place, Box 260, NY NY 10012, would be appreciated.
