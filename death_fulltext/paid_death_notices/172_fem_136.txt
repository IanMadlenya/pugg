
        McMAHON-Marie (nee Stringer. Of Bayonne, NJ, on May 13, 2001. Beloved wife of William, devoted mother of Eileen Marie and William Jr. Born in Jersey City, she worked for Merrill Lynch before marrying her husband, a sales and marketing executive. At the time of her death, Mrs. McMahon was the Treasurer of her daughter's agency, Eileen McMahon & Co., representing internationally renowned artists, including Ronald Searle, Gerald Scarfe, Andre Francois, Clare Mackie and the estate of Edward Gorey. Her constant pride in and support of her family is a true comfort to us during this last hard passage. Funeral arrangements were private, with burial in Dublin, Ireland.
