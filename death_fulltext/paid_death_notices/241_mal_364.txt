
        WASSERMAN--Bernard H. D.D.S., 85, of Somers, NY. Noted periodontist, inventor, Renaissance man. For 53 years, the beloved husband of the late Evelyn Chodosh. Adored by his loving children Alan (Susan), Steven (Judith), Nancy (John), Neil (Andrea), and grandchildren Michael, Matthew, Daniel, Brianna, and Evan, and devoted wife Marlene Jaul. He was an extraordinary man who will be sorely missed by all whose lives he touched. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate donations to: SLE Lupus Foundation, 330 7th Avenue, New York, NY, 10001.
