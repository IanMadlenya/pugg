
        ZACHTER-Leo. The Officers, Members of the Board and Administration of OHEL, Bais Ezra and the Lifetime Care Foundation for the Jewish Disabled mourn the passing of the beloved father of Mel Zachter, devoted Chairman of OHEL's Executive Committee and Treasurer, and selfless and giving friend to the children and families who turn to us in their greatest times of distress and need. Leo was the patriarch of a family renowned for its generosity and concern for the welfare of the Jewish community around the world. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Moishe Hellman, OHEL Pres. David Jacobson, Bais Ezra Pres. Ronny Hersh, Lifetime Care Pres. Moshe Zakheim, Chairman of the Board David Mandel, CEO
        ZACHTER-Leo. On behalf of our Partners and Associates, we are saddened to learn of the loss of Leo Zachter, father of our Executive Partner Melvin Zachter. Leo Zachter was very proud of the accomplishments of his family and their devotion to many charitable causes. He was beloved by all who came in contact with him. He had an enduring smile and a special personality. We express condolences to Mel and his brothers Albe, Sidney, daughters-in-law and grandchildren. May the family be granted comfort and consolation with all of those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Loeb & Troper
