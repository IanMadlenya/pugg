
        SCAVONE--Kathleen C. Age 70, died peacefully on Saturday, October 9, 2004, at the Valley Hospital in Ridgewood. Kathleen was born in Brooklyn, NY, on June 6, 1934. She was a resident of Waldwick for the past 43 years. Kathleen was a member of the New York Stock Exchange, and a parishioner of the Church of the Nativity in Midland Park. Surviving are her husband of 47 years, Robert A. Scavone, her children, Robert A. Jr. of Franklin Lakes, Stephen M. and Edward J., both of Mahway, Thomas E. of Wyckoff, James J. of Ramsey, and Kathleen Scavone of Manasquan, 13 grandchildren, her brother Michael Carraher of Freehold, and her sister Alice Doody of Staten Island, NY. Funeral Mass will be held on Wednesday, 10:30 am, at the Church of the Nativity, 315 Prospect St, Midland Park. Interment will follow in St. Luke's Cemetery in HoHoKus. Visiting is Monday and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Ave, Wyckoff, NJ. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the Robert and Audrey Luckow Pavilion, 1 Valley Health Plaza, Paramus, NJ 07652. www.vanderplaatfuneralhome.com
