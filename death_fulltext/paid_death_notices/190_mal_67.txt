
        LONDON-Carl. Of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Staten Island, NY, passed away yesterday, November 5, 2002, at the age of 73. Loving husband to Mildred; beloved father to daughter, Sherri, and her husband Michael & son, David, and his wife Jennifer; and devoted grandfather to Lauren, Emily, Jack, Caroline, and Brett. Carl worked for over 40 years at Wallachs Men's Clothing store where he was known as ''Mr. 46th St.'' He was an avid tennis player and an active member of the Brownsville Boys Club. Funeral to be held Thursday, November 7, at 12 PM at Gutterman's in Woodbury, NY. Burial following services at Beth Moses Cemetery.
