
        NATHANS--Barbara Lange. Died on June 19, 2006 at her home in Coronado, California. She was 74 years old and died of Melanoma. A weaver and bead artist, she was the wife of Robert Nathans, sister of Judy Lange Topkis, and mother of Mark, Leah, Jessie, Sam, and Amy. She was also the grandmother of nine grandchildren. Barbara was born on April 26, 1932 in Wilmington, DE. She received her BA from The University of Delaware. Barbara was an accomplished textile and bead artist. She lived in Bellport, NY, and later moved to Coronado, CA. A Memorial Service is planned for the fall. Donations may be made to The San Diego Creative Weavers Guild, or The Skin Cancer Foundation.
