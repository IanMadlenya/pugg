
        HARMAN-Donn E. On Friday, April 4, 1997 after a long illness. Born January 27, 1917 the son of Henry Harman and Beth Soper Harman in Summit, New Jersey. In the early '50's he wrote plays and television shows with George Batson. He join the Katz Agency in the early '50's and became Financial Director of Katz, and later was Controller of Media Payment of the Katz Agency. After his retirement, he co-founded Tollett and Harman Autographs with his companion Robert Tollett, retiring from that when he became ill. He is survived by brother Henry Harman, sister-in-law Irene Harman, a cousin Gordon Genung and his companion of forty-five years Robert Tollett.
