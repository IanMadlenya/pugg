
        NEWMAN-Ida. Beloved wife of the late Morris B. Newman and mother of William (Anita), the late Joseph (Rosalyn) and Melvin (Carol). A devotee of Hebrew language and culture, she lived a long and fruitful life. She was a patron of Jewish education and pursued knowledge until her final days. She will be greatly missed by her children and her many grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Funeral services Tuesday, 1:15 at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        NEWMAN-Ida. The Histadruth Ivrith of America sorrowfully notes the death of Ida Newman, widow of Morris Newman and mother of William and Melvin Newman. The Newman family have been longtime generous supporters of our activities in behalf of the Hebrew language, creativity and culture in the United States. We extend deep sympathy to her bereaved family. Miriam Ostow, President
