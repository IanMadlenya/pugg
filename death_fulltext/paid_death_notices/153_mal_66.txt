
        STRESEMANN-Joachim. Age 92. Died on October 3, 1999 in New York City. He was the son of Gustav Stresemann, who served as Chancellor and Foreign Minister of Germany during the Weimar Republic, and Kaete Stresemann. After studying law and economics in Germany and England he moved to New York where he was with Chase Manhattan Bank. An excellent pianist, he continued his interest in music and art and served on the Boards of the Bruno Walter Foundation, the Cosmopolitan Arts Foundation and others, a generous friend to many. He is survived by the family of his older brother, Wolfgang Stresemann, who was the General Manager of the Berlin Philharmonics for 22 years and died in 1998 in Berlin, Germany.
