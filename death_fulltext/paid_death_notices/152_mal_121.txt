
        BRUKENFELD-M. Ronald Died peacefully the evening of September 7 at the age of 86. He was the beloved son of the late Morris and Sarah Brukenfeld and brother of the late Carl Brukenfeld, Frances Englander and Edith Hirsch. He spent his working life in the investment community, a Member of the New York Stock Exchange from 1935 to 1980 and was a former Governor of the NYSE. He continued to pursue his interest in the world of investments, going to the office past the age of 84, until the onset of his long illness. Throughout many years of his life, he had a great interest in Mt. Sinai Hospital and was a Trustee since 1961. Though diverted from his original plan to teach English literature, which he did at Columbia, he maintained throughout his life an avid interest in the written word, in books, the theatre and sports. He is survived by his longtime loving companion, Annette Edwards. He is also survived by his brother-in-law, Chester Hirsch. He was the cherished uncle of Edward and DiAnne Hirsch, John and Elizabeth Hirsch, Richard Hirsch, Dick and Mimi Brukenfeld, Jane Holzer, Betty Jane and Alan Goldboro, and was the adoring great uncle of Douglas, Karen, Elisa, Nancy, Catherine, Mark, Bruce, Carla and Rustin. A service will be held at Frank E. Campbell's at 81 Street and Madison Avenue on Monday, September 13 at 11:30AM. Interment to follow At Union Field Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to Mt. Sinai and/or Columbia College, Carl M. Brukenfeld Memorial Scholarships Fund. A man of great wit, charm and vibrant personalitiy, he will be greatly missed.
