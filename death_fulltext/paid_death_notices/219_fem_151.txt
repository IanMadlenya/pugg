
        EATON--Justine A., 93, of New York, and Dublin, NH. Died peacefully at home, April 20th. Widow of Fredrick M. Eaton, a partner at Shearman & Sterling. Mother of Justine E. Auchincloss, Frederick A. Eaton, and the late John M. Eaton. Sister of Irene Grant and the late Martha Blagden. Attended the Chapin School and the Foxcroft School. Grandmother of ten, great-grandmother of eight, she was an inspiration to her entire extended family and friends. Services will be held at St. James Episcopal Church in New York on Tuesday, April 26th at 11am, and in Dublin, NH at a later date.
