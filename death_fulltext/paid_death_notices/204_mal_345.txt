
        DOMAN--Nicholas R., 90, slipped peacefully away Sunday morning, January 25. Husband of Judith Nicely Doman, father of Daniel, Alexander, stepfather of Lisa, grandfather of Amanda and Joshua, stepgrandfather of four. Born in Budapest, educated in Hungary and at the University of Colorado. Served in the U.S. Army and the O.S.S. Assistant prosecutor Nuremberg trials 1945-1946. A brilliant lawyer, he worked tirelessly on the restitution of properties seized during the Nazi and Communist periods. He spoke five languages, had the map of every major city in his head, loved tennis, bridge, travel and his house in Shelter Island. But above all, he loved his family who will keep him in their hearts forever. Memorial service St Mary's Church, Shelter Island 3 PM February 6.
