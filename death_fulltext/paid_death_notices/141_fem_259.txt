
        WADDELL-Jeanette Connolly. Died November 28. A resident of Manhattan, Mrs. Waddell was a business woman who was active in many Catholic charities. She is survived by her sister, Frances McGorry of Wilmington, N.C., and by five nieces and nephews. Memorial mass is Saturday at 2PM at St. John the Martyr Catholic Church, 72nd St and 2nd Ave, NYC. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Ave., NY 10021.
