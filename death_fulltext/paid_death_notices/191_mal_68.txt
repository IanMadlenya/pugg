
        SHAW-Arvell. We mourn the passing of Arvell Shaw, a fine bassist and a great friend and colleague of Louis Armstrong for over 25 years. Our condolences to his family and to his many fans all over the world. Shaw's last appearance with Armstrong was with the Louis Armstrong All Stars in the film High Society with Bing Crosby, Frank Sinatra and Grace Kelly. Marty Napoleon, Pianist Joe Muranyi, Clarinetist Oscar Cohen Associated Booking David Gold The Louis Armstrong Educational Foundation
