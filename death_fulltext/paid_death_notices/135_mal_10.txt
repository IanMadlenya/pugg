
        BASS-Martin. On March 20, 1998, of cancer, at age 75. Beloved husband of Dell, father of Karen J. Fletcher, grandfather of Jason and Frank, brother of Philip, uncle of Judith and Marcy. An unconventional man, he left a successful career in TV commercial production with partner J. Sedelmaier in Chicago to become a fruit farmer. For 17 years, Market Master of Benton Harbor Michigan Wholesale Produce Market. Contributions may be made to Ralph DiGia Fund for Peace and Justice, 532 LaGuardia Place, NY, NY 10012.
