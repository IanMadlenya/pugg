
        HASKELL-Florence, 86, on February 16, 2002. Beloved sister, aunt and friend. Lifelong champion of the disabled, despite her own disability. We celebrate her love, courage, wise counsel and wit. She is survived by sisters Esther, Ruth; brother Leon; nieces Donna, Linda, Hope, Bonnie; nephew Kenneth; grandnieces Julie, Katla; grandnephews Brian, Tryggvi, Jonah, Matthew. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Penn South Program for Seniors or Disabled in Action.
