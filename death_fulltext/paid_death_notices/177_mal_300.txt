
        IRWIN-Charles Jr. October 17, 2001. Major USMC (retired). Beloved husband of Jane Darlington Irwin. Devoted father of Charles D. Irwin and his wife Dona. Cherished grandfather of Carter, Robert and Roger. He graduated from Schreiner Military Academy & Southern Methodist University, class of 1942. Decorated Combat Marine Corps Officer in the Pacific Theatre during World War II and The Korean Conflict. Partner of the Wall St. firm W.C. Langley Co. An ardent student of history and a friend to all who knew him. Service on Tuesday, October 23rd, 10 AM at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, 5th Avenue and 53rd St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Marine Corps. Scholarship Fund, P.O. Box 3008, Princeton, New Jersey 08543, or to St. Thomas Episcopal Church.
