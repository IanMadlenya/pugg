
        GOLDBERG-Norman. Died at home in West Windsor, N.J. on Tuesday July 10, 2001, after a lengthy battle with cancer. He was 74 years old. Norman was a successful business executive, working 25 years at Amerada Hess Corporation. Upon retirement, he was coowner and creator of Photo Plus, a retail film processing business in Plainsboro. He is survived by sons Mark and Larry, as well as many family members and devoted friends.
