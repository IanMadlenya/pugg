
        TURTLE-Norman, 81. Of Rockville Centre, passed away March 19, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Shirley. He is survived by his dearest friend, Jean. Loving father of John and Randee, Jeri & Andrew, and Randy. Cherished grandfather of four. He was an active volunteer at Long Island Jewish Hospital, and an ardent supporter of the State of Israel. Services on Thursday, 12 noon at Gutterman's Inc., 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY.
        TURTLE-Norman. The officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of The Muttontown Club mourn the passing of our beloved member, Norman Turtle, and extend our deepest sympathy to his son Jon, daughters Jeri, Randi and their families. Steven Goldstein, President
