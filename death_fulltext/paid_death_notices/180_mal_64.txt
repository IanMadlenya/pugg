
        STOLNITZ-George J. Age 81 on December 18, 2001 in Bloomington, Indiana. He was an international expert on demographic trends, consultant to the United Nations, and a past president of the Population Association of America. Stolnitz graduated from the City College of NY and earned his PhD from Princeton. He joined the Indiana University Department of Economics in 1956. He is survived by wife Monique Stolnitz, daughters Cindy Heaton, Dr. Wendy Silverstein and Dia Stolnitz and brother Daniel Stolnitz.
