
        GOLDBERG - TROTTA - Wendy Iris. On May 7, 1999. 50 years old. Beloved wife to Nicholas. Devoted sister of Rosalind and Robert Danor. A prominent female business entrepreneur, she owned American Data Mart, a securities firm handling McGraw Hill, American Express and hundreds of other firms. A lover of harness racing, she owned world champion Imperfection and many others. Graveside services today, 1 PM, at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, NY. Her warmth and dedication to her family will be sorely missed by all who knew her.
