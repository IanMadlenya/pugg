
        PETERS-Philip Homer, 81, died suddenly at his home on August 10, 1997. The former EVP of John Hancock Mutual Life Insurance Co was committed to the greater Boston community and to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Memorial services will be held Sunday, September 21, 1997 at 2 PM at the Wellesley Hills Congregational Church. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the MIT Class of '37 Scholarship Fund in memory of Philip H. Peters.
