
        BALLEN--Doris Miller. Died peacefully at her home on November 22, 2005 at the age of 83. Beloved wife of Frederick. Loving mother and mother-in-law of Marjorie and Eric Whitman, Edward and Sharon Ballen, Ram Ballen and Anne Black-Ballen and Kenneth Ballen. Devoted grandmother of Andrew Whitman, Lauren Ordower, Joshua and Rachel Ballen, Nicholas and Cecilia Ballen and great-grandmother of Nathan, Sam and Ellie Whitman and Sadie Ordower. She had boundless energy and dedication to charitable causes. Her enduring love and loyalty to her husband, children, grandchildren, great-grandchildren and friends is her legacy. Funeral services will be held Friday, 12 noon, at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. Interment Mt. Pleasant Cemetery. Donations in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        $(6$)BALLEN--Doris. The Board of Directors and members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Doris Ballen. Our heartfelt condolences to Fred, Marjorie and Eric Whitman, Lauren and William Ordower and the entire family. Steven M. Slaven, President Thomas W. Brock, Secretary
