
        MACE--Joyce Amster, 79. Of Aptos, CA., formerly of Rockville Centre, N.Y., died peacefully at her home on February 28, 2006. Loving and cherished wife of David Mace. Devoted and adored mother of Carol and Jon Mace. Loving grandmother of Harrison, Rebecca, Jackson and Jamie Mace, and David & Jane Pincus. Loving sister of Richard Amster. Mama, we all love you.
