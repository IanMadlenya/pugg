
        LITTWIN-Phyllis Duke of Tenafly, NJ. Died in her sleep, Monday, October 19 unexpectedly. Wife of Dr. Stuart Littwin. Mother of Carolyn Fischer, and Charles Littwin. Grandmother of Rachel and Jack. Sister of Herbert Duke. Daughterin-law of Ruth Littwin. Member of Temple Emeth Congregation in Teaneck, NJ. A memorial service will be held at Temple Emeth, 11AM, Wednesday, October 21.
        LITTWIN-Phyllis. Alpine Country Club, its Officers, Board of Governors and Members deeply mourn the untimely passing of their fellow Member Phyllis Littwin and offer their deepest sympathy to her husband Dr. Stuart and the entire Littwin family. Alpine Country Club Michael Bergstein, President
