
        PALOZZOLO -- Edna. The Board of Trustees, medical staff, and administration of Maimonides Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend, Edna Palozzolo. We gratefully recognize her 39 years of devoted service to the hospital, culminating in her role as President of the Maimonides Research and Development Foundation. In this role, Mrs. Palozzolo was a tireless advocate for the research activities of the Foundation. The entire Maimonides family wants also to extend our deepest sympathy to her loving husband Jack and the Palozzolo family. Martin D. Payson, Chairman Board of Trustees Pamela S. Brier, Pres. & CEO Maimonides Medical Center Marvin Eisenstadt, Chairman Maimonides Research and Development Foundation
