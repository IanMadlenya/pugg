
        WEINBERG-Irving N. Washington Heights Congregation is deeply saddened at the loss of their esteemed Rabbi Emeritus, Rabbi Irving N. Weinberg. He was the former principal of the Yeshiva Rabbi Moses Soloveichik and in the past years the active Rabbi of the Congreation Hechal Moshe, Natanya, Israel. His memory will be cherished forever. Ellis Bloch, Rabbi Aaron Bullman, President
