
        MACKAY-Emily. On March 10, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Richard Mackay. Devoted and loving mother of Bonnie Mackay-Eisenhardt. Respected and admired retired teacher in Patchogue, N.Y. of emotionally disturbed and memtally challenged children. She is survived by her brother Warner Lowe, sister Virginia Graham and their children. Emily was loved by all. Visitation Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Krtil Funeral Home; Yorkville Funeral Service, Inc., 1297 1st Ave, NYC. Funeral service Thursday, 11AM at Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Ave, NYC. Interment Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn, N.Y. Contributions can be made in her memory to Mary Manning Walsh Home and the Museum of Modern Art.
