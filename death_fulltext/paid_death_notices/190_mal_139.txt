
        GOLDMAN-Steven A., 58. Of Saugerties, NY and formerly of West Nyack died November 10, 2002. Founder of Goldman Machinery Dynamics Corp. in West Nyack, he was a Professional Engineer. He authored and lectured on vibration analysis. Husband of Rosemary, father of Daniel and Lizabeth Goldman; son of Joseph & Shirley, brother of Robert and Scott and Gail Yeager. Funeral services on Wednesday at 11:30 A.M. at Simpson - Gaus Funeral Home. Kingston, New York. Contributions to Hospice Foundation, Inc., 34 Broadway, Kingston, NY 12401.
