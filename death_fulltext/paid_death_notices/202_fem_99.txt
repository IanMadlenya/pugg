
        DOMBROFF--Rita. Wife of the late Joseph Dombroff. After 104 wonderful years passed from a short illness November 11. She was much loved. A vibrant woman, a student and teacher seeking truth. An inspiration to all who knew her. She is survived by great-nieces Deanna Morton, Wendy Sternberg and a host of young family members and dedicated friends. A memorial service will be held on Friday, November 14 at 4PM at The Atria, 333 W. 86 St, NYC. Donation in Rita's memory may be made to Ananda Ashram, Monroe, N.Y. or a charity of your choice.
