
        CURRY--Lorraine Vocco, on February 7, 2007 in Palm Beach, Florida. Born in Chicago, Illinois, November 15, 1918. Adored wife of Bernard for 59 years. Beloved mother of Catherine Curry, grandmother of Elizabeth Curry Gil and mother-in-law of Andres V. Gil, all of New York City. Treasured friend of so many. Lorraine lived most of her life in Manhattan and Forest Hills, NY, and the last 26 years in Palm Beach. She was a graduate of Trinity College in Washington, D.C., a founding member of the Forest Hills Village Improvement Society and a long-time member of the Board of the Rehabilitation Center for Children and Adults in Palm Beach. Campbell Funeral Home, 81st St. and Madison Avenue, New York City, on Monday, February 12th, from 2:00 to 4:00 PM and 7:00 to 9:00 PM. A memorial Mass will be held on Saturday, February 10th at 10:00 AM at St. Edwards Catholic Church, 144 North County Road, Palm Beach. A funeral Mass will be held on Tuesday, February 13th at 10:00 AM at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, 980 Park Avenue at 84th Street, New York City, with a private burial to follow. In lieu of flowers, donations may be given in Lorraines memory to the Rehabilitation Center for Children and Adults, 300 Royal Palm Way, Palm Beach, Florida 33480.
