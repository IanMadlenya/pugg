
        KOCH--Selma. The Board of Directors and staff of VISIONS/Services for the Blind and Visually Impaired records with deep sorrow the passing of Selma Koch. She was a most dedicated supporter of VISIONS and Vacation Camp for the Blind, president of the Board of Directors from 1949 to 1953, and a member of the VISIONS Board for over 60 years. Our heartfelt sympathy is expressed to her sons, David and Peter, who are Board members of VISIONS, and her entire family. Nancy T. Jones, President Nancy D. Miller, Exec. Dir. >>AD#
