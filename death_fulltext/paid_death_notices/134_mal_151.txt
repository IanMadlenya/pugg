
        CHAMBERLIN-Robert H., 93. Of Ghent, NY. Died Tuesday, March 10, 1998. Born in Dayton, OH. He was a graduate and devoted alumnus of Williams College. He was a longtime resident of Scarsdale, NY active in civic, church affairs and community theater groups and a retired manager of Brown Brothers Harriman & Company. He is survived by his wife Anita of Ghent, NY; his children Robert, Ann, Jean and Thomas; 11 grand children & 2 great-grandchildren. A private memorial service will be held at the convenience of the family. Charitable contributions may be made to the Chatham Rescue Squad, 11 Moore Avenue, Chatham, New York 12037.
