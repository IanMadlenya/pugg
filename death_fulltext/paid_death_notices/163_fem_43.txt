
        SMITHERS-Eleanor Vey Thibaut, 90. Of Hot Springs, Va. Died Tuesday, August 1 at her residence. She was born October 25, 1909, Brooklyn, N.Y., the daughter of Richard E. and Helen Cranford Thibaut. She was preceded in death by her husband Charles Francis Smithers. She is survived by a daughter Mary Vey Smithers of Antiqua, Guatamala and Hot Springs, a niece Jacqueline Eubanks of Stuart, Fla, a stepson Charles F. Smithers, Jr. of New York, three grandchildren, two great-grandchildren. A service will be held Sunday, 4PM August 6 at her residence. A private interment will be in Woodlawn Cemetery in New York. Memorial contributions may be made to the Brooklyn Botanic Gardens, 1000 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11225. For more information call McLaughlin Funeral Home at 540-839-2961.
