
        KOWALL--Bonnie Crenshaw, 83, on November 8 in Kirkland, WA. Beloved wife of the late Konstant, devoted mother of Christina Edwards and grandmother of Jennifer Heiszler, and delighted greatgrandmother of Elke, all of Carnation, WA. She will be remembered by all her family and many friends in Washington DC, McLean And Great Falls, VA, and on Bainbridge and Shelter Islands. Rest in peace.
