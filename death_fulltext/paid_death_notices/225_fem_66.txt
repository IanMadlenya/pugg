
        ZOREK--Elizabeth M. Daughter of the late Emmanuel and Josephine Perl. Loving wife of the late John. Devoted mother of Jeffrey and his wife Janet. Beloved grandmother of Samuel and Sophia. Dear aunt of Robert and Laurie and their children Alison and Liza. Born in Vienna on April 29, 1919, and a resident of many countries, Lisl was a lover of life who cherished her famly, dear friends, travel, Fire Island and her bridge games. She will be missed terribly by all. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. (81st Street), Wednesday, 9:30 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Self Help, 520 8th Avenue, New York, NY 10018.
