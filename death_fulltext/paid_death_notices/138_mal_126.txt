
        ROSENBERG-Arthur. Born on February 6th, 1915 in Brooklyn, New York. Died on July 8th, 1998. Predeceased by his beloved wife of 58 years, Helen M. Rosenberg. Adoring father of Susan Rose and Leslie Nagy. Devoted grandfather of Julie Weiner, Carrie Pillar, Alissa and Pamela Nagy, whom he referred to as ''his girls''. Father-inlaw to Allan Ghitterman & Colonel William Nagy, USAR Ret. Arthur attended Cornell University and started an insurance underwriting corporatiion in New York City, Kaye Agency, Inc. After nearly 60 years in the insurance industry, Arthur retired, and in 1996 moved to Santa Barbara, CA. Prior to moving to Santa Barbara, Arthur had lived in New York City and Pound Ridge, NY, where he and Helen were long time members of Rockrimmon Country Club. Arthur died peacefully at home, as he wished, surrounded by his family. Services will be held in California. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to your favorite charity.
