
        OSSER--Edna (nee Meisel). On April 2, 2005, age 85. Service on Tuesday, 12 noon, at the Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, NY. Interment at Sharon Gardens.
        OSSER--Edna. The Jewish Community Center of Harrison announces with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed Founding Member. We extend deepest sympathy to her husband, Abe, her children, Jonathan, David, and to the entire family. Norton D. Shargel, Rabbi Emeritus Sarah A. Zemel, Hazzan Robert M. Schack, President
