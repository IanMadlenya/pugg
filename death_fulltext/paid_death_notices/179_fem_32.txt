
        LEWIS-Grace Pierson on December 4, 2001 in Rye, NY (formerly of Short Hills, NJ and Westhampton Beach, NY), 103. Born April 7, 1898 in Brooklyn, NY, she was the daughter of the late Lewis E. and Blanche Thorne Pierson. Beloved by all. She is survived by her sons, Stanley P. Lewis of Westport, CT, John T. Lewis of Vero Beach, FL, Lewis P. Lewis of New York City, 14 grandchildren and 19 greatgrandchildren. Memorial service will be held Tuesday, December 18, at 10 AM, The Church of the Immaculate Conception, Westhampton Beach, New York.
