
        DOBKIN--Paul S., 71, of New York City and Washington, CT. Died on February 8, 2007. Son of the late Celia and Herman J. Dobkin. Much loved husband of Josephine and loving and proud father to his son, Matthew. Dear brother of Suzanne Barnett. He will be remembered by treasured cousins and friends. A graduate of Horace Mann, the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, and New York University School of Law. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street, NYC, Tuesday, February 13, at 4pm. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to the Steep Rock Association: PO Box 279, Washington Depot, CT 06794 or to Continuum Hospice Care, 1775 Broadway, NY, NY 10019.
