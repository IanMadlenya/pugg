
        WECHSLER-Dr. Herman, on May 18, 2000, at the age of 90. Beloved husband of Rosalind. Devoted father of Michael and Joyce, and Robert and Sally. Proud grandfather of Rabbi Deborah, Steven, Stuart and Stacey, Daniel, Lisa, Jay, Jordan, and Matthew. Past President of the Genesis Hebrew Center, Founding member of the Albert Einstein College Hospital, dedicated physician, and decorated Captain in the United States Army, 1941-1945. Service Sunday, May 21, 1:30pm, at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 21 W. Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Rabbinical College of the Jewish Theological Seminary.
