
        COHEN-Abraham, Dr. On December 11, 1999, at the age of 82. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Devoted father of Mark and Jill, and Bobby and Louise. Proud and adoring grandfather of Seth, Sarah and David. He served his country in the US Navy. A dedicated educator with the NYC Public Schools for over 35 years. A respected teacher, Principal and District Superintendent. A kind and gentle man who will be missed by all. Service Monday, 11 AM, at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel,'' 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY.
