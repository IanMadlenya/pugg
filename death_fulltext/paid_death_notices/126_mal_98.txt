
        DUNNE-Anthony Thomas (Tony). Of Dublin, July 4, 1997 at St. Vincent's Private Hospital. Aged 35 years, treasured husband of Marian. Late of Centre Re (Dublin), Finpro Bowring (London), Guy Carpenter (New York) and Prudential (Dublin). Interment took place in Dunshaughlin Cemetery, County Meath on Tuesday, July 8, 1997. He lived and died with courage and dignity. 'Carpe Diem.'
