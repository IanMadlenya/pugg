
        ROCHE--Mary Alice. On July 29, 2004 in Caldwell, NJ. Born in Ohio in 1917, Ms. Roche moved to Caldwell in 1945 where she opened a horticultural photography studio with her husband. In 1971 she helped establish the Sensory Awareness Foundation and served as Managing Secretary and Editor of its publications for twenty years, while conducting Sensory Awareness workshops. Ms. Roche became a Director of The Lifwynn Foundation in 1993 and was instrumental in integrating its human relations research program and Sensory Awareness. A memorial service is planned for October in NYC.
