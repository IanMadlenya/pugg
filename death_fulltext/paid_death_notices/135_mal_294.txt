
        MEAD-Robert H. After an extended illness. Cherished husband of Henrietta Mills. Beloved father of Robert, Slade and Winter, and grandfather of six young children. A graduate of Yale University Class of 1954; teacher, coach and counselor at Salisbury School for 25 years; longterm President of the Hartley Corporation Charitable Foundation, and a civic leader in his hometown of Norfolk, CT. A memorial service will be held at 3PM on Saturday, May 2 at Church of Christ Congregational, Norfolk.
