
        BUSCHMANN - Lucille A. Loving widow of the Honorable Albert H. Buschmann Sr, Justice of the New York State Supreme Court, died December 27, 2002. Beloved mother of Mary Lu Prescott, Albert Buschmann, Angele Gilroy, Dina Buschmann and Lois Buschmann. Adored grandmother of five. Loving sisterin-law of Regina Grange and many nieces and nephews. A funeral mass will be held at 10:00 AM Monday, January 6, at Saint James Roman Catholic Church, 80 Hicksville Road, Seaford, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hospice of the Panhandle, 122 Waverly Court, Martinsburg, WV 25401. For info. Jefferson Chapel Funeral Home at 304-725-5321.
