
        MARTON--Richard, M.D. The Board of Trustees, Department of Medicine and the entire staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express their deep sorrow at the death of Richard Marton, M.D., a distinguished member of our staff for over 54 years. Dr. Marton was a dedicated physician who was greatly admired by his patients and colleagues. The Lenox Hill Hospital family honors his memory and mourns his loss. We extend our sincere sympathies to the Marton family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George President and CEO James J. Richter, M.D., Ph.D. President, Medical Board Robert A. Phillips, M.D., Ph.D., Chairman, Department of Medicine
