
        BOTTOMLEY-Hilda Ridgway. Of London, England, died October 14, 1999 in New Castle, NSW, Australia. Mother of eight, grandmother of 26 and great-grandmother of 34. She was a loving, proud and brave woman who raised a wonderful family despite the perils of the London blitz in World War II. We love you, Nan, and will always be very proud that you were part of our heritage. Love, The St. John and Speakman Families
