
        HANNA-Valerie J., 57. Senior Vice President for Technology for Marsh & McLennan, died at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. She is survived by her husband of 29 years Glenn Hughes of Freeville, New York, daughters Lydia Robertson and Denene Ray, foster son Victor Arencibia, mother Joan Haviland, stepmother Mary Hanna, brother Sherman Hanna, sisters Kathleen Hanna and Barbara Hanna and three granddaughters. She was preceded in death by her father Patrick Hanna and son Joel Robertson. Memorial services will be at 10AM September 29 at 295 13th St., Brooklyn and 1 PM September 30 at The Unitarian Church of Ithaca, New York.
