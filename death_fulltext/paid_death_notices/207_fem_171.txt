
        BROOK--Susan, 63, died on April 22, 2004 following a recent accident. She is survived by devoted husband Michael, loving daughter Kira, stepchildren Mandy Shear and Scott Brook and Julie and Daniel Brook; adoring grandchildren Zev and Isaiah, mother-in-law Anne Brook and special friend Kelwin Coleman. Susan was a multifaceted woman, an occupational therapist, clinical social worker, psychoanalyst, artist and craftsperson. She touched many lives with her persistence and courage in the face of physical obstacles. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Avenue on Monday, April 26 at 11:15 AM.
