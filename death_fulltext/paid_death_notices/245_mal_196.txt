
        SHIMBERG--James Heiman, 84, of Tampa, FL, formerly of New York, dies June 15, 2007. He is survived by his beloved wife of fifty years, Amy Gross Shimberg; his five children and their spouses: James, Jr. (Elizabeth); Nancy (Dr. Edward Paikoff); Robert (Michele); Richard (Dena) and Janet (Andrew Knust); fifteen grandchildren; brother Mandell ''Hinks'' Shimberg (Elaine) and numerous nieces and nephews. Born in Syracuse, New York, WWII Airforce veteran and practiced law in New York from 1949 until 1958. Mr. Shimberg was a prominent real estate developer and philanthropic community leader in Florida.
