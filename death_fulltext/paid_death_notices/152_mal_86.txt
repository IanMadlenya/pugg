        WISNIEWSKI-Henry, Dr. The Department of Pathology at SUNY Downstate Medical Center, Brooklyn mourns the loss of our distinguished colleague and friend, Dr. Henry Wisniewski. A past president of the American Association of Neuropathologists, Dr. Wisniewski was internationally recognized for his many seminal contributions to our understanding of human neurological disorders. He will long be remembered for his tireless energy, enthusiasm, good humor and passion for neuropathology. We send our heartfelt condolences to his wife, our colleague in the Department of Neurology at Downstate, Dr. Krystyna Wisniewski, and his son, Dr. Thomas Wisniewski. Suzanne S. Mirra, M.D., Chair, Department of Pathology
