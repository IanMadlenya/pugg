
        KRAMARIC-Peter S., 67, formerly of New York City and Wilton, CT, died Wednesday, May 21, 1997 at Wake Robin surrounded by his family following a courageous battle with cancer. He was born April 29, 1930 in Ljubljana, Slovenia, the son of Stefan and Ana (Vidic) Kramaric. He was raised in Slovenia and was a graduate of the University of Ljubljana Law School in 1954. After serving two years as a political prisoner in Yugoslavia, Peter escaped Communist rule to arrive in the United States on May 25, 1956, owning $70.00. He graduated from Yale Law School in 1960. He was an International Attorney, having worked for Union Carbide Corp., General Foods, Corp., and most recently American Home Products Corp. He retired in 1993 and moved to Colchester, Vermont to be near his daughter, Karen Dean, and her family. Mr. Kramaric was a member of The American Bar Association, New York Bar Association and the Yale Club of New York City. He was also a member of The Ethan Allen Club of Burlington, VT. He attended the College Street Congregational Church in Burlington. He is survived by his devoted daughter, Karen Dean and her husband, Laurence, of Essex; grandson, Andrew Peter Dean, of Essex; two sisters, Polona Mauer of Utah and Marijana Polak of Slovenia; nieces and nephews. He was predeceased by his wife, Susan L. Kramaric, in 1993. A memorial service will be held in the College Street Congregational Church in Burlington on Monday, May 26, 1997 at 11AM. Burial will take place in Shelburne, VT at the convenience of the family. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be made to Hospice of the Champlain Valley, 25 Prim Road, Colchester, VT 05446 or to The American Cancer Society, Vermont Division, 13 Loomis Street, Montpelier, VT 05602. For information contact Corbin and Palmer Funeral Home, 71 S. Union St., Burlington, VT.
