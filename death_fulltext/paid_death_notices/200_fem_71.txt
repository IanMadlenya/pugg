
        KEELER--Ruth, age 94, of North Salem, NY, died September 1, 2003 at her home, Keeler Homestead. Daughter of Floyd and Romola (Lyon) Keeler. Miss Keeler was predeceased by her sister, Kathryn Sherrill, and is survived by her nephews, Leicester and Christopher Sherrill and their families, a first cousin of Margaret Lyon. A Memorial Service will he held at St. James Episcopal Church in North Salem at 3pm on Friday, September 12, 2003. Beecher Funeral Home (845) 279-3615.
