
        HURFORD-John B. On April 3, 2000, beloved husband of Hilge Drueke. Devoted father of Jennifer, loving nephew of Jessica Simon. He was a prominent philanthopist and international investment adviser. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street Sunday April 9th, 3-6 and 7-9 P.M. Service Monday April 10th, 10 A.M. at Saint James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71st Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Haberford College, 370 Lancaster Avenue, Haberford, PA 19041 or to Southampton Hospital, 240 Meeting House Lane, Southampton, NY 11968.
        HURFORD-John B. The Board of Directors, staff, and associates of the EastWest Institute wish to express our deep sorrow and sense of loss at the passing of John B. Hurford. He was a distinguished member of our Board and served on the Institutional Advancement Committee for many years. We will always remember his insight and kindness and great support of our Institute. Our condolences to Hilge and Jennifer and to all his family and friends.
        HURFORD-John B. The Trustees, Fellows and staff of The National Humanities Center wish to express their sorrow at the loss of a wise and committed servant of the Center and its mission. We will long remember and benefit from his valuable counsel and generosity as a member of The Board of Trustees from 1993 to 1999. Our deepest condolences to Hilge and Jennifer.
        HURFORD-John B. The Board of Governors and Staff of the Foreign Policy Association wish to express their profound sorrow at the loss of their colleague and friend, loving husband of Hilge and devoted father of Jennifer, whose ability to be a wise and caring listener will be sorely missed.
