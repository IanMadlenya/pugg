
        RIECKER--Charles. The Board of Directors and the singers of the Schuyler Foundation for Career Bridges mourn the tragic loss of our beloved friend and member, Charles Riecker. Charlie has been a vital part of our foundation from its inception. His mentoring, caring, and love shown to all of us will be deeply missed. We extend our deepest sympathies to his loving wife, Wally. David Schuyler Bender
