
        WOLFF-Johanna R., 93, of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, formerly of NYC, died on February 19, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Fred G. Wolff. Loving mother of Barbara Graham of Scottsdale, AZ, and cherished grandmother of Lynne Goldstein of Essexville, MI., and Ellen Congiundi of Houston, TX. She is survived by five wonderful, great grandchildren: Lauren, Joshua, Samuel, Allison and Lisa. All those who were fortunate to have known her will always remember and miss her.
