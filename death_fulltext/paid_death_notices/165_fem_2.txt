
        de RICHEMOND-Cynthia Micheline. In Cannes. Professor RobertEmile Bical has the painful task of announcing the death of his dearest wife, Cynthia Micheline de Richemond alias ''Petit Bozel''. Free France Voluntary Conscript Medal, former B.C.R.A. Officer. Her death occurred peacefully in Cannes on September 23, 2000 in her 76th year, after a long and painful illness. According to her wishes, her ashes were scattered in the creek at Port la Galere, in the grounds of Chateau Banquet in Geneva and at BICAL Mill (Cantal county). May her Soul Rest in Peace.
