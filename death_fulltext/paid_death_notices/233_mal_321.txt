
        BERK--Irving A. The Community Preservation Corporation mourns , with deep sadness, the passing of Irving Berk. He was a pioneer in the field of trade schools as the founder of the Berk Trade and Business School. His work to create non-degree programs created employment opportunities for thousands of working people. In addition to being a pioneer in education, Irving was also a good and caring friend who will be sorely missed. Michael D. Lappin, President and CEO, The Community Preservation Corporation
