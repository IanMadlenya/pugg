
        LOTH-Clifford Bernard. In his 95th year, beloved husband of the late Sally Renner Loth, devoted father of Louis Renner Loth and Suzanne Loth Oliver, loving grandfather of Shereen, Traci and Marni Loth and Brian and David Zeiden. Always proud that he was among the founding families of Reform Judaism in Cincinnati, OH. He and his dear Sally continued the Reform tradition through service and membership in New York and Los Angeles. Service and interment were in Los Angeles, CA. For information, R.L. Malinow-Glasband-Weinstein, 800-300-0223.
