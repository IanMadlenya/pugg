
        WOLFF--Sandford I. (Bud). The National Office of the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists, the 70,000 member union of professional performers and broadcasters, mourns the passing of our colleague, mentor and friend who lead this union for 17 years as its chief executive. Sincere condolences to his beloved wife of 37 years, Ann Barry, a former dancer and AFTRA member.
