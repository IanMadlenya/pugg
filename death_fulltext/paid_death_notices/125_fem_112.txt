
        LAFONTANT - MANKARIOUS Jewel. The Board of Directors of Equitable Life notes with sadness the passing of former Board member, Jewel Lafontant-Mankarious. During her twelve year tenure on the Board, she served with distinction, imparting outstanding counsel, guidance and wisdom to the management of Equitable LIfe. Throughout her life, she was known as an excellent public servant and an individual of great dignity, intelligence and warmth. We extend our sincerest condolences to her husband Naguib S. Mankarious and to her family. Joseph J. Melone, Chairman of the Board The Equitable Life Assurance Society
