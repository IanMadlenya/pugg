
        COHEN--Ann F. Died on Thursday, September 22, 2005 A graduate of City College of New York, she was an accountant as well an active volunteer in many civic and Judaic organizations. Her strength, warmth, wisdom and humor will be deeply missed by her daughter, sonin-law and grandchildren as well as a devoted extended family and many lifelong friends. Services Sunday, September 25th at 10:30 AM at Plaza Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. and 91st St. in New York. Shiva at 245 East 80th Street, NY. Memorial contributions to Solomon Schechter Day School, 26 Buena Vista Road, West Hartford, CT 06107.
