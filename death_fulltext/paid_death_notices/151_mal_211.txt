
        WERNER-Welles. 80, of New York, died August 16, 1999 in Seattle, WA. He was born September 4, 1918 in Brooklyn, NY. He worked as a commercial interior designer for over 40 years in the New York area. Welles is survived by his daughters, April and Nancy Werner, and his sister, Estelle Kellerman. He was preceded in death by his wife of 55 years, Carolyn, in 1997. In keeping with his wishes, no services will be held. Memorials may be made to the Alzheimers Association, 12721 30th Avenue NE, Seattle, WA 98125.
