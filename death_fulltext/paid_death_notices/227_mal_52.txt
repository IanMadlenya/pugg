
        MURPHY--John M. Of Breezy Point, NY, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, and Rockville Centre, on December 4, 2005, at the age of 84. Born April 18, 1921, John was the youngest of four boys. He was a decorated veteran of WWII serving in Europe. Founder and President of East America, Inc., a commodity trading firm in New York. He was a fabulous cook, master storyteller and a devoted husband and father. With his loving wife Grace, he enjoyed his retirement on the beaches of Breezy Point and Hutchinson Island, FL. His warmth, wit and sage counsel will be profoundly missed. In addition to his wife Grace, John is survived by his beloved children: Kathleen (Spangler), Michael, James, John, Robert and the late Charles, with whom he now rests. He will also be deeply missed by his fourteen adoring grandchildren. Reposing at Denis S. O'Connor, Inc., 9105 Beach Channel Drive, Rockaway Beach, NY. Funeral Mass at St. Thomas More Church, Breezy Point, on Friday at 11:30 AM. Interment St. John's Cemetery. Visiting Thursday, 2-5 and 7-10 PM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Covenant House, PO Box JAF 2973, New York, NY 10116-2973.
