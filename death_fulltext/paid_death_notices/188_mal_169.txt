
        COHEN-Abraham. On September 17th, 2002 at age 89. Beloved husband of the late Ann. Cherished father of Sharon and Eileen and sons-inlaw Mickey and Bob. Adored grandfather of Matthew and Renata, Jonathan, Jeremy and Jessica, Rachel and Rob. Founding member of Lake Success Jewish Center and devoted volunteer of the Gift of Life, he will remain in our hearts forever. Funeral services today at Lake Success Jewish Center on Long Island at 11 AM. Interment to follow at the New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York.
