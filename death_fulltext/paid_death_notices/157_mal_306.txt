
        SELIGSON-Jack M. Age 86, longtime Harrison, NY, resident passed away in Boynton Beach, FL, on February 25, 2000. Survived by wife, Lillian, and two sons, Captain Harold E. Seligson, USN, Retired (wife Marilyn) in Virginia and Dr. Charles D. Seligson (wife Elizabeth) in New Jersey. He leaves seven grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren.
