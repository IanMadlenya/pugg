
        PALMER--Marcia S. Elegant; strong; composed; stunning; sharp; exquisite; sound; mighty; refined. March 8, 2004. Wife of the late Bernard Palmer; Mother of Robert and Sandee Palmer, Diane and Buddy Flateman, Mickey and Diane Palmer; Grandmother of Cary and Lori, Mindy, Jennie, Sadie, and Dana; loving and loved aunt; great friend to many. For 86 years, the world has been blessed with her grace, wisdom and quiet power. We will go on breathing the air that she made sweet. Service 11 AM, Thursday at Plaza Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St.
