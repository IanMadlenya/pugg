
        UZIELLI-Philip A. It is with deep regret that we announce the death of Philip A. Uzielli on August 25, 2001 in Florence, Italy. His family and friends mourn their loss on his sudden passing and will miss his warmth, humor and humanity. ''Always keep them laughing when you say goodbye.''         UZIELLI-Philip A. The partners and staff of Tocqueville Asset Management mourn the passing of Philip A. Uzielli, a long time friend of our firm and a former trustee of the Tocqueville Trust. We will miss his advice, contagious good humor and incessant curiosity for others.
