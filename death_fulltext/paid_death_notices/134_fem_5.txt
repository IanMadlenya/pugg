
        DALY-Katharine (nee Boston). Formerly of New York City, died at La Jolla, CA., on February 23, 1998 in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late J. Holmes Daly and adored mother of Pamela Daly Vose. She was the daughter of the late Charles A. Boston and Ethel Lyon Boston of New York. Her father was president of the American Bar Association and an early partner of Hornblower & Boston, now Willkie Farr & Gallagher. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Chapin School, 100 East End Avenue, NYC 10028.
