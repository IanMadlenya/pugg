
        COSTELLO-Robert B. On August 5, 2000, in Boston, Massachusetts. A renowned and admired editor, writer, and lexicographer, known for his brilliance, wit, and sensitive soul, Bob touched many and will be deeply missed and remembered by all, especially his loving daughter Kate C. Boyle, his devoted sister Barbara LeRoy and brother John J. Costello, Jr. and nieces and nephews Betsy, John, Elizabeth, James, and Richard, and longtime companion Carl A. Rossi. Contributions in his name may be made to the American Cancer Society, 1-800-227-2345.
