
        ANTINE-Anthony. Of Fort Lee, NJ, passed away on Sunday July 14, 2002. He is survived by Ho Shin, Lorraine, his sister, and beloved family and friends. An internationally renowned designer for over twenty years with commercial and private accomplishments including The Point Hotel, Saranac Lake, NY, and Hart Mountain Industries, and private residences of Robert Redford, Mountain House Atom, Japan, Neo Classical House, Tokyo, and a private yacht in Monte Carlo. Mr. Antine has been a frequent participant in the Kips Bay Show House, included Small Wonders Auction, as well as, Abigail Adams Museum Vingnette. Mr. Antine has achieved eight cover features and has been published in leading design publications including Interior Design, Design Time, House Beautiful, The New York Times, British H & G, and French Elle Decor. He also appeared nationally on The Today Show, CNN Style, and Regis and Cathy Lee Show. Mr. Antine was trained as Couturier at The Fashion Institute of Technology, and created wardrobes for many of the most fashionable and discriminating women in the world, such as, Barbra Streisand, and Betsy Palmer. Family will receive friends at The A.K. Macagna Funeral Home, 495 Anderson Avenue, Cliffside Park, NJ, on Wednesday, July 17 from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Interment on Thursday at 11:30 A.M. at Fairview Cemetery, Fairview, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory are requested to: American Diabetes Association Memorial Honor Program-Call Center, PO Box 2680, North Canton, OH 44720.
