
        FLYNN-Elizabeth. On January 31, 2002, in her 93rd year. Beloved wife of the late Judge John Flynn. Loving mother of Frederick, John, Elizabeth, & Valentina Abrahams. She is also survived by ten grand and nine great-grandchildren. Reposing at Fives Smithtown Funeral Home, 31 Landing Ave., Smithtown. Visiting Sunday, 2-4 & 7-9 P.M. Mass Monday at 9:45 A.M. St. Patricks R.C. Church. Interment in Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Salesian Missions, 2 Lefevre Lane, New Rochelle, New York 10801.
