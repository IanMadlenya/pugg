
        KUPERMAN-Abraham S., MD. Age 66 of Manhattan, died December 4, 2000, in Boston after a short illness. Pulmonologist on the faculty of Albert Einstein College of Medicine, affiliated with Westchester Square Hospital, who had an active clinical practice in the Bronx for 27 years. Born in Capetown, South Africa, in 1934 he made Aliyah to Israel in 1955 and moved to the United States in 1963. He is survived by his sons, Gilad and Alan, both of Boston, his exwife Carmel Kuperman of Manhattan, his brother Israel of Capetown, his sister Miriam Rosenberg of Sydney, Australia, and his friend Anita Notrica of Manhattan. Funeral at the Edwards Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Avenue (Exit 17 Sawmill), Dobbs Ferry, New York, December 5, at 1 PM. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY, Shiva TuesdayThursday 6-9PM at 40 E. 84th St, Apt 14A and Sunday 2-5PM at 303 West 66th Street, Apt 5AE.
