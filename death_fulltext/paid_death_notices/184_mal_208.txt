
        ALTMAN-Jack. 87. Passed away May 17, 2002. A devoted husband and father, Jack cherished family above all else. For over 60 years, he worked as a journeyman plumber. Among the many structures that bear his signature is the Technology Building at CCNY built in the 1960s. He excelled in handball and swimming in his early years. In his later years, he was very involved in his synagogue. He was married for over forty years to the late Sarah. He is survived by his children Anita and Stan, daughterin-law Claire, grandchildren Sascha, Jeffrey and David, sisters Etta and Esta and brother Bob. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, May 19th at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (near 91st Street) at 11 A.M. Contributions can be made to the Jack and Sarah Fund at FJC, 130 East 59 Street, New York, New York 10022.
