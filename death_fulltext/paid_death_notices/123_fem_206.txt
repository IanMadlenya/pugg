
        FINS-Ruth Lovett. It is with deep sorrow that the Rabbis, Trustees, Administrators, and members of Congregation Emanu-El record the passing of Ruth L. Fins, beloved wife of Hank, never-to-beforgotten mother of Dr. Joseph J. Fins and Robin L. Fins, and cherished mother-in-law of our colleague, Rabbi Amy B. Ehrlich. We share with them the grief of sorrow and the pain of bereavement. It is our prayer that the loving memories she bequeaths will bring comfort to their hearts and to the souls of all whose lives she touched. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Senior Rabbi David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        FINS-Ruth. Central Synagogue of Nassau County sorrowfully announces the passing of its longtime member and member of its board of trustees and extends to her beloved family its sincerest sympathy. Rabbi Julian I. Cook Les Gilbert, President
