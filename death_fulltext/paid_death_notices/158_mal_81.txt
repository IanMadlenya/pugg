
        ROEGNER-George Peter, 67, died at home in Vero Beach, Florida, on March 4, 2000. Born in Flushing, NY, Mr. Roegner graduated from Pratt Institute in 1954. His distinguished career as an industrial designer began at General Motors. He later held positions at Raymond Loewy, Westinghouse, RCA and Lenox China, before founding and serving as President of Curtis Hanson Meade, a consultant firm designing and marketing consumer electronics products in the United States, Europe and Asia. Mr. Roegner is survived by his wife, the former Jane Lamphear Kramer, and sons, George Curtis Roegner, John Hanson Roegner and Nicholas Meade Roegner.
