He is survived by his loving wife Shirley, his children Marilyn and Richard, his son-in-law Frank; his grandchildren Ian, Adam, Brian, Joshua and Ryan, and his great grandchildren Ilana, Isaac, Samantha, Abigail, Naomi. He will be greatly missed, but remembered in our hearts always.
