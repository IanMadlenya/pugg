
        MOORE-Philip Walsh. Age 81, died in Ponte Vedra Beach, FL. July 18, 2001 after a short illness. He is survived by his devoted wife, Phaedra; daughter Jourdan Houston, (Colorado); son Thomas Moore, (Miami); son Ned Moore, (Virginia); six grandchildren; and sister Louise Immer of Thun, Switzerland. He was preceded in death by his son, Philip Moore, Jr. A private family service is planned at the Moore mausoleum in Burmont, Pennsylvania.
