
        PALIN--Caryl. Ohr Torah Stone Institutions of Israel and especially the children of our Efrat Nursery School mourn with sorrow the passing of Caryl Palin, beloved wife, life partner to Mickey, a philanthropist and humanitarian in her own right. May the Almighty comfort my very special, intimate friend Mickey, their beloved son Dean and all of those who mourn the death of such a special, elegant and gracious woman. Shlomo Riskin, Chancellor and Dean, Ohr Torah Stone Institutions of Israel, Honorary Rabbi of Lone Star Boat Club
