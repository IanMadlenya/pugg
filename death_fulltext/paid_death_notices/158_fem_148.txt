
        SONDHEIM-Evelyn. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, deeply mourns the death of Evelyn Sondheim, our beloved colleague. A Hadassah Founder, she served with great distinction as a National Vice President, National Secretary, Chair of the Jewish National Fund, and as a member of the Honorary Counsel, among other distinguished committees. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. May her memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, National President Dr. Laura S. Schor, National Executive Director
