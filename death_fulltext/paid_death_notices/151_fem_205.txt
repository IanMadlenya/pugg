
        JENKS-Elisabeth R., M.D. Died of cancer at Calvary Hospital, Bronx, August 12. Born 1929, Cambridge, Mass. Attended Concord Academy, Swarthmore College, and Albert Einstein School of Medicine. Practiced psychiatry and was on the staff of Gouverneur Hospital. Wife of the late Emery I. Valyi of Katonah, NY. She leaves a sister, Anne L. Jenks, Cambridge. Funeral service at All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Avenue at East 80th Street, August 26 at 4PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Nature Conservancy, Arlington, VA. or the National Audubon Society, New York NY.
