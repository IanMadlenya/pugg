
        RIMLER-Moe. Of Manhattan. On February 8, 2001. Peacefully at age 80, after a long and happy life. Proprietor of Moe & Phil Clothiers, on New York's vanishing Lower East Side. Loved and beloved husband of Ruth for 60 years. Extraordinary father to George, Marsha and Phyllis. Outstanding father-inlaw to Anita and Howard. Cherished and loving Grandpa to Lee, Melanie and her husband Gary, and Nina. Devoted brother to the late Albert. He was one-of-a-kind and will truly be missed. Services 10AM Sunday, Gramercy Chapel, 2nd Ave and 9th Street, New York. Donations to Simon Wisenthal Center, 9760 West Pico Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA. 90035.
