
        SOROKO--Rose Frank, on March 5, 2006, at 92. Beloved mother of Doris, Pearl, Susan and Jacqueline. Also survived by devoted brothers Jack and Leonard, seven adoring grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Her sweetness will always be remembered. Predeceased by husband Mark D. Soroko. Donations may be made to City of Hope, 3333 W. Commercial Blvd, Suite 106, Ft Lauderdale, FL 33309.
