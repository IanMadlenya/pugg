
        TARLOW-Ricki. September 26, 1999. Devoted mother of Bill Schacter of Newton, Massachusetts, Peg Ryan of Plainville, Massachusetts, and Wendy Chava Schacter of West Fork, Arkansas. Loving grandmother of four grandchildren. She was a copy editor for Time, Inc. for more than 20 years. Memorial service at the U.N. Chapel, 777 United Nations Plaza (1st Ave/44th. St.) on Wednesday at 2:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to the Center of Complex Systems, Brandeis University, 415 South Street, MS 126, Waltham, MA. 02454.
