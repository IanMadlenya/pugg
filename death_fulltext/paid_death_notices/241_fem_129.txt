
        SIMON--Frances (nee Hammerman), left us on February 15, 2007 at the age of 94. Beloved wife of Bernard ''Buzzy'' Simon. She leaves behind her devoted son Elliot and wife Maxine, grandchildren Joy, Marni and Greg, Gerry and JoanMarie, Lewis and Caroline, Beth and Dominic, 11 great-grandchildren and many caring nieces and nephews. A quiet woman who showed her love and concern for all. She will be missed.
