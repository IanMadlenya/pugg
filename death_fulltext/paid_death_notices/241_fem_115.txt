
        KALVIN--Florence I. A long time resident of White Plains, NY, passed away on February 11, 2007, in Lincolnshire, IL. Partner/Owner of Instant Replay. Harrison, NY, from 1975-2000. Beloved wife of the late Harold, attorney, brother of Dr. Ira Cohen, loving mother of Richard and Dr. Douglas Kalvin, cherished grandmother of Ashley, Hannah and Harris, loving aunt of Amy and Bonnie Cohen. We miss you and love you. Rest in peace in the arms of your loving husband, Harold. Services Friday 1pm at Cedar Park Cemetery, Emerson, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations in her name to Hadasah or National Parkinson's Research Foundation.
