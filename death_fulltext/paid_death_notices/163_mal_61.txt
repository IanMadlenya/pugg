
        RIBNER-Herbert, M.D. Rabbi, Neurologist, Author. Died July 16 in Jerusalem. Survived by wife Betty; children Dr. Hillel (Elizabeth), Eytan (Irene) and Dr. Dena (Mark); grandchildren Lisa, Danielle, David, Avigayil, Yehudit, Nathaniel, Maya, Boaz & Aderet. Healer of souls and of illness, who with his heroics in World War II, late-life entry into Medicine and Aliyah to Israel taught us that all dreams can be achieved during one's lifetime. May his Memory be for a Blessing.
