
        KOPPELMAN-Sarah Levy. Of Tenafly, NJ. Five days shy of her 70th birthday. Died after a valiant eight year battle with ovarian cancer on July 13. Beloved mother of Lisa Tames, Suzanne Koppelman and Janet Koppelman. Devoted grandmother to Emma Tames, Jacqueline Tames, and Jenna Katz. A dedicated nurse for 30 years at Englewood Hospital, she was truly unique and we will miss her. We were honored to have her as our mother.
