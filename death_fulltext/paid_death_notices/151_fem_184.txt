
        BUTTERWEICH-Frances Lewis Levin. On August 20, 1999 in her 97th year. Beloved mother of Eleanor Spector (Edward) and Dr. Alfred C. Levin (Corinne). Cherished grandmother of Nancy Ryder (Peter), Jane Froehlich (William), Alfred C. Jr., Robert, Charles and Michael Levin. Loving sister of Serena Freund, George Lewis and the late Merwin, Seymour and Herman Lewis. Also survived by many greatgrandchildren. Private services were held in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.
