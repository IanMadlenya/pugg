
        AMORY-Charles M., a WWII hero, died in his 86th year in Palm Beach, Florida on May 11, 2002. He is survived by his wife Julia Aspuru Amory, his two sons Minot and Peter, and five grandchildren. Services will be held on May 15 at 11am, at Bethesda by-the-Sea, Palm Beach. Donations may be given to the Diabetes Foundation of Palm Beach, Florida.
