
        FOWLER-Phyllis Birrell Franke. Died Jan. 8, 2002. She graduated from Wells College, Aurora, NY and received her MSW from NYU, School of Social Work. She married a Navy pilot, Harding Hall Fowler in 1943. He died in 1985. They resided in Dobbs Ferry, NY, Troy, NY, Larchmont, NY and Tega Cay, SC. In 1988, Mrs. Fowler moved to the Pines Retirement Home in Davidson, N.C. She is survived by her son, Russell; daughter, Elizabeth Horton; three grandchildren, Patrick, Ryan and Kaitlyn; & her sisters, Anne Ulinski and Patricia Kouns.
