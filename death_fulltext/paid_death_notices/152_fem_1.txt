
        BESS-Barbara E., M.D. New York Medical College was deeply saddened to learn of the death of our distinquished alumna, Barbara E. Bess, MD '65, who for many years was an integral part of the College's psychiatry residency training program at our University Hospital, Metropolitan Hospital Center. Dr. Bess, professor of clinical psychiatry and valued member of the faculty, was a wonderful teacher, much beloved by residents and staff. We extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D., Provost and Dean, School of Medicine Joseph T. English, M.D., Chairman, Dept. of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences Richard K. Stone, M.D., Senior Associate Dean and Medical Director, Metropolitan Hospital Center Ronnie G. Swift, M.D., Associate Professor of Clinical Psychiatry and Chief of Psychiatry, Metropolitan Hospital Center
