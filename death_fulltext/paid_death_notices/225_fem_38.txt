
        KAY--Helen Dorothy. Died on October 4, 2005 at the age of 103. Before marrying Irving Kay on October 3, 1926 she wrote and acted in plays and short stories and received professional offers. As a conscientious wife and mother of two sons she still became an outstanding handball player and worker in a war time naval hospital. Afterwards, she was a braillist, cosmetologist and ceramist. She is survived by two sons Mike and Eddie, four grandchildren, seven great-grandchildren, ten nieces and nephews and Vladimir Fortunny. Eddie is the former secretary-treasurer of 1199 Union. Helen had a lifetime commitment to work for the betterment of all people. Her family has been fortunate to have Helen's quiet determination and loving care. Viewing on Saturday, October 8, 7:30PM and funeral services, Sunday, October 9 at 10AM. Nieberg Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Ave, Brooklyn.
