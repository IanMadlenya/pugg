
        WARD-Laura Alison. On Oct. 14, 1997, after a long illness. Survived by her mother, Frances, sisters, Claudia and Susan, as well as their families who all loved her very dearly. There will be a short service at the Greenwood Cemetery and Crematory, Fifth Avenue and 25 Street in Brooklyn, Thursday, October 16, 11 AM for family and friends wishing to attend. In lieu of flowers donations to the Breast Cancer Fund or Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth Israel Medical Center would be appreciated.
