
        FRIEDLER - Sylvia. Died April 11, 2006 peacefully of natural causes in New Rochelle, NY at age 99. The loving wife of the late Reverend Saul Friedler, she will be missed by her family and community. The family will not be receiving visitors and donations can be sent to: Beth El Synagogue, Saul & Sylvia Friedler Scholarship Fund, North Ave. & Northfield Rd, New Rochelle, NY 10804.
