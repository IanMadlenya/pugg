
        GROTHEER--George J. 86, died December 17. A 50 year resident of Katonah, he is survived by his loving wife Selma, his three children, Alan, Laurence and Lisa and six grandchildren. He was predeceased by his sister Louise and his eldest son George Jr. His successful career with Mobil Oil Corp. was followed by many happy years in retirement. A loving man with a gentle spirit, he cherished his family and the life they shared. A service is planned for 2PM December 21 at the Lutheran Church in Mount Kisco, NY. Contributions may be made to the church music fund or Mount Kisco Hospice.
