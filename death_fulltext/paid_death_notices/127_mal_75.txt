
        HILL-Frederick, Reverend. Retired Rector of St. Michael's Church, New York City. Longtime Associate at St. James, Madison Avenue, suddenly and peacefully at home in Goshen, N.Y., August 2, 1997. He was 67. Survived by James Kennedy, many relatives and friends. Services Saturday, August 9, at 1 PM at St. Michael's, 225 W. 99 St, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Michael's Church would be appreciated.
