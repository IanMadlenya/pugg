
        LEUBSDORF-Bertha Boschwitz. The Board of Directors of the Scholarship and Welfare Fund of the Alumni Association of Hunter College mourns the death of Bertha Boschwitz Leubsdorf, a past president of the fund. Bertha Leubsdorf brought outstanding leadership and generosity to the fund during her many years of service. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. She will be missed by the entire Hunter College community. Grace Schut, President Scholarship and Welfare Fund
