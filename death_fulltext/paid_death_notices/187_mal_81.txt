
        MOSSAVAR - RAHMANI Shahin. Much beloved son, brother, brother-in-law and uncle and a cherished friend to so many passed away on August 3, 2002 at the age of 48 of heart failure while jogging in Central Park. Following a career in journalism, he earned a Ph.D. in international relations from the University of Pennsylvania and became a founding member and principal of UAS Asset Management in New York City. A gentle soul, he was fiercely devoted to his family, friends, work and sports and gave generously of himself to all. A celebration of his life will be held Friday, August 9, 2002 at the Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave, at 1:30 PM followed by burial services.
