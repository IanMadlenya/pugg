
        SHAPIRO--Arnold, 90 years old, passed away on June 15th, 2007. Beloved husband for 20 years of Muriel Abramson Shapiro. He was predeceased by his first wife, the late Dorothy Milton Shapiro. Beloved father of Douglas and Cynthia Shapiro and Marjorie Shapiro, and the late Joel Shapiro. Beloved father by marriage of Patricia and Alan B. Abramson. Beloved grandfather of Mark and Bridget Shapiro, Michelle Shapiro, Dr. Seth and Luisa Narins, Sara Narins, Josh Narins, Adam Abramson and Joshua Abramson. Graduate of Dartmouth College, class of 1937. While at his family business, Maryland Cup, he participated in the invention of the first machine-made ice cream cones and the ''Bendy Straw.'' $(6$)SHAPIRO--Arnold. Your presence in our lives for the last 20 years was a blessing. Your love for us, and ours for you was perfect. Your sweetness and kindness never faltered. You will live in our hearts forever. Alan, Patricia, Adam and Joshua Abramson
