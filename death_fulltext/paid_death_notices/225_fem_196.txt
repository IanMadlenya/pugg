
        RUBIN--Ruth A. (nee Goldman). Beloved wife of the late Louis M. Cherished mother of Richard and of daughter-in-law Susan. Devoted grandmother of Karen and Jill. In addition to her family, Ruth's activities for National Council of Jewish Women will long be remembered. She taught us how to age. Funeral will be held Sunday, noon, at Schwartz Bros., 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills.
