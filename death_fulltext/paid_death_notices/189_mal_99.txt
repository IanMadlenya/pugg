
        PRENNER-Jack. On October 7, 2002, age 94. Dear uncle of Edward Prenner and loving great-uncle of Emily and Elizabeth. An extraordinary musician and a link to the great days of the Broadway musical theatre, where for many years he was the coach and rehearsal pianist for Rodgers, Hart, Hammerstein, and many others.
