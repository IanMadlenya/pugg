
        McBRIDE-Dorothy Morante. Of Rye, New York, died June 21, 2000 at her home. She was 81. Born August 30, 1918 in New York City to John & Margaret Perdisatt Burke. Beloved wife of the late Harry T. McBride and the late Edward A. Morante. Devoted mother of Edward Morante of Palm Desert, CA, Barbara Starke of Raleigh, NC, Pamela Doherty of Rye Brook, N.Y., and Colin McBride of New York City. Cherished grandmother of Stephen, David and Mark Morante, Jennifer Peach & Alexander Starke, Paul Doherty and Cami & Terrence McBride. Loving sister to Patrick Burke of New York City. Predeceased by brothers John J. & William. Her friendship, joy for life, courage and style will be missed by her relatives and friends. For information please call Coxe and Graziano Funeral Home, 914-698-5968.
