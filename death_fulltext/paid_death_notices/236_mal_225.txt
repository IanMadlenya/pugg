        ROSENBERG--Michael J. The members of the NYU Leonard N. Stern School of Business community mourn the passing of their dear friend and alumnus, Michael J. Rosenberg. An NYU alumni Trustee, past President of the NYU Alumni Association and former President of the Stern Graduate Alumni Association, Michael took a leadership role in numerous NYU organizations. He was a generous supporter of the School, and his warmth, energy, and dedication to the School will be sorely missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Kathleen, and his entire family.
        ROSENBERG--Michael J. Founding Trustee who served for 28 years as Treasurer of The Town Hall, cherished friend to his colleagues on the Board and a man of great integrity and loyalty whose acumen, sincerity and willingness to play a key role in the restoration of The Town Hall will be remembered always. Our deepest sympathy to Kathleen, Emily and Ann and to the entire family. For The Town Hall Board Marvin Leffler, President
