
        SCHIFFER-Morton A., M.D. The staff of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology of the New York Methodist Hospital mourns the loss of our longtime friend and colleague, Mort Schiffer. An outstanding and compassionate clinician, a superb teacher, a strong advocate for the underserved and a role model for all who follow in his field, he will be sorely missed. Our condolences to his wife Ruth and all of his family. Richard H. Schwarz, M.D. Chairman
