
        FRIEDLANDER--Gordon D. Died peacefully at home in Tarrytown, NY on September 9, 2003 at age 83. He is survived by his beloved wife Florence Covino and many loving nieces, nephews, grandnieces, grandnephews and very dear cousins in Germany. He was the son of the late eminent American sculptor Leo Friedlander. Mr. Friedlander was a professional engineer who studied at RPI and the University of Michigan. He had experience as a structural and project engineer and was engaged in the design and construction of fossil fuel and hydro-electric power plants. He worked for many of the leading engineering firms in New York and Toronto. He had several consulting assignments involving MIT, The Univeristy of VA and the Research Institute of America and lectured at several major universities. He was a senior editor of the IEE SPECTRUM of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers and later served as generation editor of the magazine Electrical World. Mr. Friedlander's many accomplishments earned him a place in Who's Who In The East. He was a kind and gifted man who enjoyed many friendships here and abroad and who enriched the lives of all who knew him. He will be missed forever. A memorial service will be held at the Community Unitarian Church of White Plains NY on Sunday September 21 at 2:30 PM. Donations in his honor may be made to the Community Unitarian Church, New York Cornell Hospital or a charity of your choice.
