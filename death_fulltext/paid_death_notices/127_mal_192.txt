
        TODD-David. On August 12, 1997. In Lebanon, N.H., of injuries following an automobile accident. An inspired teacher of organic chemistry and research chemist for 30 years at Worcester Polytechnic Institute and at other colleges and research centers, graduate of Swarthmore College, B.A. and Harvard Ph.D, public spirited volunteer in many fields. His family, Constance, Rebecca, Laurence, Julia, Jenny, Christopher, Alden, and Mary salute his memory.
