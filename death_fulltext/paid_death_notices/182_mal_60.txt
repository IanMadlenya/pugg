
        ROSS-Harvey. Passed away Mon, March 4, 2002 in Aventura, FL. Son of immigrant parents, he started early learning the watch business and grew it into an international company. Very well loved here and abroad. A supporter of synagogues & other charities, member of Town Club and City Athletic clubs. Loving brother, husband, father, uncle and son. Services at Lincoln Square Synagogue, Mar. 6, 11 AM, 70th St & Amsterdam Ave.
        ROSS-Harvey. Lincoln Square Synagogue records with sorrow the passing of our longtime member, Mr. Harvey Ross. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Ken Cappell, President
