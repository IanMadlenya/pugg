
        CANDIDO--Salvatore Frank. Of Pelham, NY. Loving husband, father, grandfather, uncle and friend died on May 16, 2006 at Memorial SloanKettering Hospital after a courageous battle with cancer. In 1975, along with his brother Anthony, Sal founded Manhattan Information Systems, one of the largest privately owned computer networking companies in Manhattan. Sal held positions with the Pelham Civic Association, St. Joseph's Church Holy Name Society and the Feeney Park Boys Club. He is survived by his loving wife of 50 years, Elaine; his children Larry and daughter-in-law Annette; son Sal; grandsons Zachary, Nicholas and Dylan of whom he was very proud; his brother Anthony of Purchase, NY; his sisters Antoinette Friscia and Rose Roos of New Rochelle and many loving nieces and nephews. Friends may gather at Pelham Funeral Home on Wednesday and Thursday, May 17 and 18. In lieu of flowers, please make any donations to St. Joseph's Church, 280 Washington Avenue, New Rochelle, New York 10801. Sal is very dearly loved and he will be deeply missed but he will be an unforgettable part of our lives forever.
