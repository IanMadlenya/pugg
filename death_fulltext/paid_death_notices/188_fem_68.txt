
        KLEIN-Florence. 90, passed away August 31, 2002, in Boca Raton, FL. She was the mother of Sandy (Sara Ann) Kane, of Boynton Beach, FL, and Herbert Klein of Aspen, CO. Florence, the daughter of David and Dora Pollock, was born and raised in the Bronx, NY. She was married to Jack Klein, of Jersey City, NJ, who preceeded her in death. Florence and Jack made their home on Teaneck, NJ, where they ran the Plaza Youth Center Clothing Store for over 30 years. Florence was active in community and Jewish organizations and was a life-long member of Hadassah, and member of the National Council of Jewish Women. She was an accomplished concert violinist, an avid reader, and speaker on Jewish history and contemporary issues. She is survived by her son-in-law, Stephen Kane, of Boynton Beach, FL., her daughter-in-law, Marsha Klein, of Aspen, CO, and her grandsons, Matthew Kane, Daniel Kane, and Evan Klein. Contributions in her honor may be made to your local chapter of Hadassah.
