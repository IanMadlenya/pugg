
        GRIGG-Dr. Ernest C. II. Beloved father and grandfather. Died early Tuesday morning at the age of 89. He had been battling cancer for more than 10 years. He is survived by his three grandchildren, Rhema, Christa and Ernie. Dr. Grigg was a career diplomat for the US Mission to the United Nations and held many posts with Save the Children including Director for Africa, Middleast and Europe in the 1970's and Executive Vice President in the 1980's. Dr. Grigg was a true gentleman in every sense of the word and will be greatly missed by all who knew him. A memorial service will be held Friday, August 27 at 9AM at St. john's Church, 55 Street and 1st Avenue in Manhattan.
