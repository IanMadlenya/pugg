
        LUDEMAN-Patricia M. ''Trisha''. On March 14, 1999. Wife of the late Louis. Beloved and dedicated mother of Christopher, Louis, Gerard and Sue Ann Haesloop. Cherished daughter of the late Elizabeth and Harry Hickey, former Treasurer of the Brooklyn Dodgers. Dear sister of Betty and Dr. Herbert Fett and the late Virginia and John Garvin. Devoted and endearing grandmother of Bryan and Owen, whom she adored. Fond mother-in-law of E. Gordon Haesloop. Trisha leaves many family members and dear friends who will remember and love her always. Visiting on Tuesday and Wednesday, from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M., Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 Fourth Avenue, Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial, on Thursday, 10 A.M., St. Anselms Church.
