
        MEYER-Herbert E. Age 86. Died of cancer March 3, 1999 in New York. He was a lover of music and a passionate Chef. Following a career trading commodities, he created the food company, Gala Delicacies, and befriended Chefs from Chinatown to the Waldorf. Since the death of his wife, Marjorie in 1986, he was a flaneur in N.Y., the delight of dinner parties as host and guest. He leaves three children: John A. of Manhattan, Priscilla A. of Middletown, Ct., Christopher A. of Lexington, Ma., and two granddaughters: Rachel Trousdale and Miranda Meyer. A Musico-Epicurean celebration will be held in May.
