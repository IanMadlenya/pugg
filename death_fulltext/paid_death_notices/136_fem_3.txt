
        SCUDDER-Louise Fry. Of Gulfstream, FL. and Lake Placid, New York. Died on April 28 at age 78 following a courageous battle with cancer. She was an enthusiastic volunteer for many charitable organizations. She was the beloved wife of Edward W. Scudder. Devoted mother of Katherine (Tiballi), Mary Gale, Edward and Robert. Survivors also include eight grandchildren. A memorial service in Lake Placid will be announced. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering or the University of South Carolina.
