
        DODGE-Bruce M. Passed away peacefully at his home in Rancho Mirage, California, on April 28, 1998. He was born in New York City, on December 18, 1920. Memorial contributions may be made in his memory to the American Cancer Society or the charity of your choice. For information Palm Springs Mortuary in Cathedral City, CA.
