
        PASEKA -- Margaret Clara (nee Meszaros). Born May 23, 1932, died November 13, 2003, in St. Thomas, U.S.V.I. Adventuress, world traveler, brilliant, independent, fearless, fun loving, caring and beautiful raconteur extraordinaire. Devoted school teacher at P.S. 221 in Douglaston. Loving mother of Kyle, Lawton, Kurt and Brett. Adoring grandmother of Katherine, Cody, Quin and Dylan. She will be missed by all. We love her so.
