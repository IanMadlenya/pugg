
        HORNSTEIN-Gertrude. Of Hollywood, FL, formerly of Merrick, LI. Passed away March 23, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Moses. Devoted mother of Larry and Francine and Judy and Gene Goldman. Cherished grandmother of Meryl and Mark Nachbar, David Hornstein, Pam and Danny Fried, Jeff and Laurie Goldberger, Michael and Robin Goldman. Most special and adored great-grandma to Perry, Sydney, Cody, Kyle, Jamie and Jared. She was their best friend. Special friend to Eunice Mendes. Devoted sister of Eve Ferster, Ann Holtzer, Jean Tierney, Helen and Philip Schectman, Roslyn and the late Ira Schectman. Services Tuesday 1 PM, 3/25/97, at the Riverside Nassau-North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Dysautonomia Foundation, 20 E. 46 St, NY NY 10017, or the Long Island Hearing and Speech Society, 430 Lakeville Rd, New Hyde Park, NY 11042.
