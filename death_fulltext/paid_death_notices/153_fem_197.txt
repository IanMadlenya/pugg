
        OLIVER-Rose. On October 20, 1999, of heart disease, at her residence in Peterborough, NH. She was a clinical psychologist with a Ph.D. from CUNY. For a number of years she was associated with the Institude for Rational-Emotive Therapy, in addition to her private practice. Survivors are her daughter Theresa Oliver, son John and wife Priscilla, grandchildren Peter and wife Julie, Anna, Christopher, Eric, Heather, great-grandchildren Cole and Clay, sister Anna London, and many nieces and nephews. Services at ''The Riverside'', Sunday, October 24, 9:45AM, at Amsterdam Ave. and 76th Street.
