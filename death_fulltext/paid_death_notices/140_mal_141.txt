
        WEINSTEIN-Edwin, M.D. The Department of Neurology of the Mt. Sinai School of Medicine mourns the passing of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Edwin Weinstein, Professor Emeritus of Neurology. He was a gentleman and a scholar, a wonderful teacher and a gentle man who made a major contribution to communication disorders and authored many works including a major text on Woodrow Wilson. We extend our sincere condolences to his entire family.
