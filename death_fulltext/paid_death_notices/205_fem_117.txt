
        KONOVE--Jill F., Ed.D. 56, died February 3, 2004. Adored daughter of Paulina and Robert Konove, dear sister of Ronald (Kay) Konove, aunt of Elissa, Jonathan and Andrew, niece of Carl (Edith), cousin of Dr. Joseph Markenson, Susan, Paul, Natalia Begun family, Lillian Sobol family and Sally Kash. Special thanks to Dr. Markenson, S. Jerome Feinberg family, and Keith Olsen, best friends, for their unwavering assistance. Jill's selfless love for the helpless and oppressed (human and animal) filled her entire life. Interment private.
