
        McCARTHY-Robert G. 33, died tragically on September 11, 2001, as a result of the World Trade Center disaster. He was employed on the 104th floor by Cantor Fitzgerald. He is survived by his loving wife, Annie Rosenfeld McCarthy, his four-week old son Shane Robert, his parents Robert and Patricia McCarthy, a sister Mary Jean O'Leary, and a brother Patrick James McCarthy. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday, September 26, 2001, at 10:15 AM, at St. Gregory Barbarigo Church, Garnerville, New York. The family will receive friends at St. Gregory's Church on Monday, September 24, and Tuesday, September 25, from 3-8 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Robert G. McCarthy Memorial Fund, PMB 871, 222 Rte 9W, Haverstraw, NY 10927. Higgins Funeral Home, Stony Point, NY.
