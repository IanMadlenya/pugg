
        BERKULE--Leon, age 93, of Scarsdale, New York, died Saturday, April 7, 2007 at St. Vincent's Hospital. He is predeceased by his wife, Mildred Berkule. He was a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, admitted to the Bar in 1945. He is survived by his three children, Barbara (Sheldon) Cohen, Lynne (Stephen) Stavrides and Steven Berkule.
