
        DEL MONTE--LeRoy, 82, on May 23, 2006 in Norwalk, CT after a long illness. Survived by sister Ruth Del Monte and companion Irena Dizer. Roy was a successful private investor. He attended Syracuse University, leaving to serve in the Pacific theater in WWII. Roy was married to the late Helen Del Monte, editor at McCall's, for over 30 years. He participated in and donated generously to New York faith community feeding and homeless programs and Westport charities. Roy was a sailor and avid winter sportsman and devoted friend to Irena and Stafford. Friends and family will dearly miss him.
