
        HORN-Miriam. Age 90, died in her home on Sunday, September 24, 2000, after a long and full life. Devoted wife of the late Dr. George. Loving mother to Elayne and Barbara. Eldest of the four Levine sisters, Esther, Sarah and Alice. Matriarch to three generations of the Levine and Horn families, and lifelong friend to many. She will be remembered in our hearts. Services Tuesday, 12 noon, at ''Gutterman's, Inc.,'' 175 Long Beach Rd., Rockville Centre, LI. Interment Mt. Ararat Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to the St. Vincent's Palliative Care Program, c/o Kathleen Perry, 153 West 11th St., NYC 10011, or Mercy Hospice, 1220 Front St., Uniondale, NY 11553.
