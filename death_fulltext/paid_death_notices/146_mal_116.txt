
        HITCHINGS-Thomas E. On March 2, 1999. Cherished and adored father of Andrew, beloved husband of Katherine, loving brother of Laramie and loving brother-in-law of Robert Brown. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Friday, March 5 and Saturday March 6 from 2-5 and 7-9PM.         HITCHINGS-Thomas E. The Facts On File News Services family mourn the death of their visionary leader, publisher Thomas E. Hitchings, a renaissance man, whose creative energy transformed FOF. He served as our staunch advocate, enlightened teacher and dearest friend. We send our deepest condolences to his beloved wife, Katherine and adored, cherished son, Andrew.
