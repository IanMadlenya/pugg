
        HITZIG-Isabelle Koehler of Bright Waters, NY, on April 16, 2002. She was 76 years old and died of lung cancer. Beloved wife of the late Dr. William Hitzig and mother of Carolyn Dahab and the late Douglas Hitzig. Grandmother of Evelyn and William Dahab. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Unitarian Universalist Society of South Suffolk, 28 Brentwood Rd, Bayshore, NY 11706.
