
        McCARTNEY-Linda. The Board members & staff of The American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers are deeply saddened by the passing of Linda McCartney, a wonderful songwriter, photographer and humanitarian. Our most heartfelt condolences to her husband, Paul McCartney, their children, Linda's brother, John Eastman, a member of the ASCAP Board, and the entire McCartney-Eastman family. Marilyn Bergman, President & Chairman, ASCAP
