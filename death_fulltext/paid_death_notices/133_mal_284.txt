
        SIMELS-Irving William, 85. Son of Louis and Lee. Mount Vernon and Bronx, dentist for over 45 years, beloved husband of Lilyan, father of Toba Stutz, and Robert Simels, grandfather of Edward, Jennifer and Rebecca Stutz, and Alexandra Simels, and his brother Raymond. Brother-in-law to Ruth Kaslof, Uncle to Tina Harriet and Lisa. Former Lieutenant in U.S. Army during W.W. II and a Freemason Lodge Mt. Masada. Stuyvesant H.S. Arista Hon. society, NYU. Case Western Reserve. Shiva observed at 21 East Ridge Road in Waccabuc. Funeral to be held on Friday, February 20 at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Broad Street, Mount Vernon.
