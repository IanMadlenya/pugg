
        MOUNTY-Robert. On August 6, 2000. Father of Dr. Judith L. Mounty (Robert B.) Weinstock, Ann Mounty (Steven Simon), Ian Mounty and Jennifer Mounty. Brother of Seymour Mounty, Bernard Mounty and Shirley Mounty. Grandfather of Matthew, Luda & Hannah Jo, also by two nephews and two great nieces. Relatives and friends are invited to services Wednesday, 10:00 AM at Riverside Chapels, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave., NYC. Interment Mt. Jacob Cemetery, Collingdale, PA. Family will return to his late residence. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of the donor's choice.
        MOUNTY-Robert. On August 6, 2000. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn th loss of their esteemed longtime Member and former Board Member Robert Mounty. He was fierce in his dedication and commitment to the Club and his loyalty and personal guidance over the years were an invaluable asset to all the Friars. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, New York City, on Wednesday, August 9, at 10:00 A.M. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Sally Jessy Raphael, Prior David B. Cornstein, Treasurer Stewie Stone, Scribe Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
