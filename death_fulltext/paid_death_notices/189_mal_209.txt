
        MARTENS-Frederick W. Jr., MD. Passed away October 16th in his NYC home. Born in 1925 in Whitestone, NY, he served in the Naval Air Corps in 1944-45. He graduated from Cornell Medical School in 1957, joined the OB/GYN practice of Martens, Ryan, Steadman, and became a full professor of Cornell in 1985. He served on the vestry of St. Bartholomew's Church and was Commodore of Larchmont Yacht Club at the time of his death. His wife of 54 years Joan, his sons Frederick William III and David Byron; his daughters-in-law Muriel Anderson and Julie Ann; and five grandchildren will miss his strength, courage, wisdom and guidance. A Memorial service will be held at 2 PM October 21st at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave and 50th St. Donations may be made in lieu of flowers to the Dept. of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Cornell, Attn: Dr. F. Chervenak, MD, Rm J130, 525 East 68th Street, New York, NY 10021.
        MARTENS - Frederick W., M.D. The Faculty and Staff of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology of New York-Presbyterian Hospital and Weill Cornell Medical College deeply mourn the passing of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Frederick W. Martens. Dr. Martens entered the Medical College as a student in 1953, and, except for a year's internship at another hospital, was affiliated with our institution until his death. An Attending Gynecologist and Clinical Professor of Ob/Gyn, he had a thriving private practice, and won the loyalty and gratitude of his many patients. A yachtsman, and vestryman of St. Bartholomew's Church, he was the devoted husband to Joan, father to David and Gibb, and grandfather to five. A memorial service will be held at St. Bartholomew's Church, 50th Street and Park Avenue, on Monday, Oct. 21, at 2 P.M. Donations may be made in his name to the Dept. of Ob/ Gyn, New York Weill Cornell Medical Center, 525 East 68th Street, J-130, NYC 10021. Frank A. Chervenak, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Obstetrics & Gynecology
