
        MELLECKER--John B., 93, on February 5, 2005. Born in Anamosa, Iowa. Attended Iowa State, served in U.S. Army, 1942-1946. Chemical engineer, and editor. Past historian of Chemists Club. Survived by his wife, Dorcas Fick-Mellecker, son, John B. Mellecker, Jr., of Holden, MA, daughter, Judith Parker, of West Cornwall, CT., four grandchildren, and five great grandchildren. Reception for family and friends on Friday February 11, at 2:30 P.M. at The Chemists Club, 40 West 45th Street.
