
        BREUER-Jacob. Of Jerusalem, Israel, passed away peacefully on March 15th, at age 83. A gentle man, well known for his great intelligence and sense of humor, he was married for 22 beautiful years to his beloved Judy. Loving son of the late Rika & Rav. Dr. Joseph Breuer; adored brother of Edith Silverman, Meta Bechhofer, Sophie Gutmann, and the late Marc Breuer, Hanna Schwalbe, Rosy Bondi & Samson Breuer. A wonderful uncle to twenty-one nephews and nieces. He will be sorely missed. Shiva at 50 Overlook Terrace, NYC, Apt 1A, until Thursday morning.
