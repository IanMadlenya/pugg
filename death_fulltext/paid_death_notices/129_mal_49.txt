        MARGETT-Hon. Charles. On October 2, 1997. The Board of Governors and the Members of Glen Oaks Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their esteemed member of 57 years and past President, and wish to extend their heartfelt sympathy to his wife Doris and his family. His wisdom and guidance will remain with us always. Board of Governors Richard A. Horowitz, Pres.
