
        SEWALL-Harold Marsh, 71, of New York and Brunswick, Maine, on April 9. He graduated from Bowdoin College in 1951. Mr. Sewall was a retired investment banker and commercial real estate agent in New York. He served in the Marine Corps and the Army in Korea. Surviving are three sisters, Nancy Sewall Brett, Cynthia Sewall Janeway Coates and Sallie Lane Sewall Comey, & his faithful friend, Margery Rice. Donations may be made to the Maine Maritime Museum, 243 Washington Street, Bath, Maine 04530. Service Wednesday, April 12, 11 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
