
        YEARWOOD-Anne. January 20, 1905-August 2, 1999. Teacher extraordinaire and all-around wonderful human being. She will be missed by Camille Johnson and family, many relatives and friends, and by her ''children,'' pupils of P.S. 54 and P.S. 20, Brooklyn. Services were held; a memorial will be held in the fall. 718-463-8687 for details.
