
        HAAS-Col. Harry H., died peacefully at home in Victoria July 24, Born March 19, 1900 in Union City, NJ. Graduated from United States Military Academy at West Point in 1922. Married Hazel Newkirk in 1923. After retiring from the Military in 1955 he joined Houdaille Industries and was appointed vice pres. in 1957 retiring in 1963. Survived by his daughter, Jacquelyn (Kenneth) Gibbs; two grandchildren, Karen and Donald; great grandchild, Benjamin, of Victoria, B.C. Funeral services will be held at U.S. Military Academy West Point Thurs, July 31st 1:30PM.
