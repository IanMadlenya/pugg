
        COLEMAN-Francis T., 80. Of Bronxville, NY and formerly of Glenville, NY died March 7, 2002. Former Labor Relations executive with Atlantic Richfield. Proud member of the 8th Air Force Historical Society and a supporter of the Friends of the Old Dutch Burying Ground in Sleepy Hollow. Visitation at Coffey Funeral Home, 91 N. Broadway, Tarrytown, NY on Saturday (today) 7-9 PM and Sunday 2-4 PM only. Funeral Mass Mon. 11:00 Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church, Elmsford. Interment Sleepy Hollow Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name to the Friends of the Old Dutch Burying Grounds, 42 North Broadway, Tarrytown, NY 10591, would be appreciated.
