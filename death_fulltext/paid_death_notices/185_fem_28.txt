
        LEWIS-Flora. The Trustees, alumni, residents and staff of International House note with sorrow the passing of distinguished alumna Flora Lewis, 1990 Recipient of the Harry Edmonds Award for Outstanding Lifetime Achievement. Her extraordinary career in journalism constitutes a major legacy in the area of international understanding, and our deepest sympathy is conveyed to her family. Paul A. Volcker, Chairman Donald L. Cuneo, President
