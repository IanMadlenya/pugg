
        JOHNSON-Robert P. Died January 26th, 2003; retired United States Army Colonel, member of the West Point Class of 1955, President of the Lee-Grant Chapter of the West Point Alumni Association, survived by his wife, Mary Alice, his daughter, Dr. Valerie Tyson and son Major Stephen Johnson, his grandchildren Justin and Claire Tyson, his sister Helen T. Lowe, his son-in-law Dr. Daryl Tyson and daughter-in-law Sabina Johnson. Memorial ceremony and interment will take place on Monday, February 3, 2003, at 1:30PM, at The National Cemetery at the United States Military Academy at West Point, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to the Robert P. Johnson Scholarship Fund at All Hallows High School, 111 East 164th St, Bronx, NY 10452
