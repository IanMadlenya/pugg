
        HUTNER - Batsheva. Distin guished Aishes Chaver of Rabbi Yehoshua Hutner, Director and guiding force of Yad Horav Herzog in Jerusalem for half a century. In her lifetime as a devoted wife and dedicated servant of her people, she was an inspiring model of scholarship and benevolence, warmth and gentility, and love for Israel, the People of Israel and all mankind. May her memory be a blessing and may her soul be bound up in the bond of eternal life. Rabbi Gilbert Klaperman Chairman, American Board
