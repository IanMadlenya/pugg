
        BRODEY-Stanley Carl, 81. Awardwinning water colorist, former longtime resident of Woodmere, LI. Beloved brother of Jesse, Dr. Marvin, Elaine Brodey, and the late Dr. Adrian. Kind and loving uncle to seven nieces and nephews and ten grand-nieces. Died April 17 at Lenox Hill Hospital. His wit and personality spread joy to all who knew him. Services 11:30AM, Friday, April 19th, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
