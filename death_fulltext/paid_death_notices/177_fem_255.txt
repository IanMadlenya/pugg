
        BLANC-Nancy Scott. On October 21, 2001. Beloved wife of Robert S. Blanc Jr., devoted mother of Anne D'Olier, and Scottie Harrison, stepmother of Robert Blanc III and Roger Blanc, loving grandmother of Sam, Nicko, Matt, Alex, David, Gregory, Cynthia, Christopher and Melissa. Ms. Blanc, a descendant of Nathaniel Heaton who came to Boston in 1632, was a graduate of Wheaton College and Columbia Teachers College. She had a lifelong appreciation of fine arts and poetry. She will be greatly missed by the Manhasset Friend's Meeting, of which she she was an active member for over forty years. Services Sunday, Oct. 28, 11 AM at Manhasset Friends Meeting House. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Friend's Service Committee.
