
        SCHREIBER - Evelyn Morgen stern, November 16, 2001. Graduate of Brooklyn College, Valedictorian and Propellea Society, with MA. in Geology. National President of Amit Women 1973-1975. Wife of the late Louis J., mother of Saul, Phoenix, AZ; Honey Levinson, West Hempstead; Avram, New York; Simeon, Bethesda, MD. Grandmother of eleven and great grandmother of twenty-one. She was unique in her devotion to her family, faith, Israel and her people. She changed the lives of all who knew her and their world is diminished by her loss. Contributions in her memory may be made to Amit Women, 817 Broadway, New York, NY 10003.
