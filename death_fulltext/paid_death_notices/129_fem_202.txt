
        TISCHLER-Esther. Of Somerset, New Jersey, formerly of NYC, died October 22, 1997. She retired from the U.S. Corps. of Engineers after 35 years. She is survived by her sisters Ann Newman and Ruth Piltz Bell and many nieces and nephews. Graveside services are Friday, King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. The family will receive friends on Saturday and Sunday at the home of Arlyne and Barry Zalaznick, Scarsdale, New York.
