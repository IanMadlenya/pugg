
        LICHTENSTEIN--Dr. Lawrence, on June 9, 2007. Beloved husband of the late Irene (nee Sonnenschein). Loving father of Loan Tucker and Robert Lichtenstein. Fond father-in-law of Alan Tucker. Cherished grandfather of Jennifer, Josh, Seth and Jamie. A prominent dental practitioner for 50 years and an esteemed member of the faculty at The Bronx Lebanon Hospital. He will be greatly missed.
