
        HEYMAN--Milton. Age 84, on December 11, 2005. Most beloved husband of Ruth for 591/2 years. Adored father of Neil and Janna Heyman, Sharon Heyman and Barry Zitser, Nancy and Russell Wohl. Loving grandfather of David, Larry, Jamie, Rochelle, Brad, Taryn and Samantha. He leaves behind his dear sister Florence, many loving family members and friends. Milton grew up in New York, served in Army Intelligence in WWII and had a long and distinguished career as an Ernest & Young partner. We will always cherish his unselfish love and generosity, his sense of fairness and justice, and most of all his devotion to his family. We love you and we'll miss you.
