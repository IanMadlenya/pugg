
        KRIDEL-Carol H., 78. September 8, at home. She was the widow of William J. Kridel. She is survived by her son, William J. Kridel, Jr, of London and New York, and by her daughter, Linda K. Smith, of Beloit, Wisconsin, and by three grandchildren. Burial will be private. A memorial service will be held at Campbell's Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue (at 81st Street), Sunday, September 13 at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Mt. Sinai Medical Center, Palliative Care Program, 1 Gustave L. Levy Place, New York, NY 10029, or to The Collegiate School, 370 West End Avenue, New York, NY 10024.
        KRIDEL-Carol H. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of Carol H. Kridel, widow of our most distinguished Trustee, William J. Kridel and daughter of our late, long-time Trustee, Hortense Hirsch. The Kridel and Hirsch families supported and helped guide our institution over many years, and we extend our sincere condolences to her son William, Jr., to her daughter Linda K. Smith, her grandchildren, and the entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, MD, President Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President
