
        KARP-H. Jack. On August 15, 2001. Beloved husband of Rose Karp. Loving father of Richard Reed & Dr. Marvin Karp. Adoring grandfather of Stacey & Courtney Karp. Dear brother of Arthur and Milton Karp and the late Samuel Karp, Dr. Lewis Park and Linda Reiss. Funeral service on Sunday, August 19th, 1:30PM at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street. Interment New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York.
        KARP-H. Jack. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, H. Jack Karp, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
