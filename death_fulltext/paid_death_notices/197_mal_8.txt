
        LOVELESS--Robert F. May 20, in Las Vegas, formerly of Staten Island. 84 years. Distinguished career with Illinois Power, Stone & Webster, EBASCO and Pandick Press. Bob was an expert in public utility finance and leaves his wife, Faith Loveless, and a sister in Illinois. He will be greatly missed by all who loved him and enjoyed working with him.
