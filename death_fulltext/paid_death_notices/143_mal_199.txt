
        HIGGINBOTHAM-A. Leon, Jr.  The partners, associates and staff of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison express profound sorrow at the passing of our Senior Counsel, Judge A. Leon Higginbotham, Jr., a great lawyer, wise jurist, dedicated public servant, admired colleague and beloved friend. Judge Higginbotham will long be remembered for his deep commitment to social causes, his wise counsel and his many accomplishments. His death is a profound loss to all who knew and loved him and to the nation at large. We extend our deepest sympathies and condolences to his wife, Evelyn, his sons, Stephen and Kenneth, his daughters, Nia and Karen, and all the other members of his family.
        HIGGINBOTHAM-A. Leon, Jr. The New York Times Company, its Board of Directors and its employees note with sadness the death of A. Leon Higginbotham, Jr., a member of our board since 1993. We will miss Judge Higginbotham's formidable intelligence, sound judgement and unfailing integrity. We will remember fondly his wit, warmth and humanity. We express our heartfelt condolences to his family. Arthur Sulzberger, Jr., Chairman The New York Times Company
