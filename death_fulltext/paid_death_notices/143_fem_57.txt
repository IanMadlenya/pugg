
        BUNCE-Hazel W., 94, of New York City on December 2, 1998. Formerly of Brooklyn, Miss Bunce was a long time resident of Peter Cooper Village. The only child of William H. and Martha W. Bunce, Miss Bunce worked as an Executive Secretary to the President of the Lehigh Valley Railroad for most of her adult life. After her retirement in 1962, she served as an Executrix for Estates and Co-Trustee of a number of Trusts until very recently. Funeral services will be held at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Avenue, (at 20th St) NYC on December 8, 1998, at 11:30AM. Interment Greenwood Cemetery Brooklyn, New York.
