
        RENNERT-Joseph, Prof. The Faculty and Staff of the Chemistry Department of The City College of New York deeply regret the loss of our longtime friend and colleague, Prof. Joseph Rennert. At this very difficult time, we extend our deepest condolences and sympathy to Helen Rennert, his devoted and loving wife, to his children, Michael and Karen, and to the entire Rennert family and friends. Prof. Rennert's warmth, caring, and good humor will always be remembered by his colleagues and students.
