
        LUSARDI-James P. Lafayette College mourns the passing of James P. Lusardi, class of 1955, Francis A. March Professor Emeritus of English, on November 10, 2002, at age 71. We express our deepest condolences to his wife, Marcia, and family. A memorial service will be held at 2pm Sunday, November 17, in Colton Chapel on campus.
