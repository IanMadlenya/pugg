
        CHEROUNY-Janet Little. 88. Died in Chatham, MA., on October 25. She was the widow of Arthur Stratton Cherouny who died in 1995. She is survived by two sons; Robert S. Cherouny of Round Hill, VA., Richard M. Cherouny of Canton, CT., a daughter, Cynthia C. Yeaw of Chatham, MA., a brother Robert F. Little of Laurel Hollow, NY, eight grandchildren and nine great grandchildren. A funeral service will be held at The Battel Chapel in Norfolk, CT, 10 AM, Nov. 1. Memorial contributions may be made to: Norfolk Public Library, Box 605 Norfolk, CT. 06058.
