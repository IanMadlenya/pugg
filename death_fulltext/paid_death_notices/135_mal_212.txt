
        LEE-David Henry. On April 19, 1998, in his 89th year. Long time resident of Greenwich Village. Survived by his beloved wife Gertrude, devoted daughters Adrienne (Ronald) Stone, Karen (David) Kaufman and Margaret (Steven) Lowell, adored grandchildren Anthony Balsley, Julianne Stone, Jeffrey (Pamela) Goldstein, Tanya Balsley and Michael Kaufman, loving sister Bess (Morris) Silverman. Services Wednesday April 22, 1998 The Village Temple, 33 E. 12 St, NYC at 1PM. Contributions in his memory can be made to The Joint Passover Association, addressed to The Gertrude G. Lee Foundation, C/O The Joint Passover Association, 210 West 91 Street, New York, N.Y. 10024.
