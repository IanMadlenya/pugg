
        SMOLIAR-Esther. June 29, 2001, in Prairie Village, Kansas. Formerly of Peter Cooper Village in Manhattan and Clearbrook in New Jersey. Beloved wife of the late Milton Smoliar. She will be remembered fondly by her son and daughter-in-law, Burton and Barbara Smoliar, her grandchildren, Dr. Laura Smoliar Arbore and David Smoliar, and her great-granddaughter Sara Ann Smoliar.
