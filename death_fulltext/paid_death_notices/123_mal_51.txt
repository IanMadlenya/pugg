
        PARSLY-Lawrence. Died peacefully on April 4, 1997. Loving husband of 55 years to Beverly, adored father of Joan and Michael Sherman and Gail and Robert Parsly, cherished by grandchildren Hope and Richard Sherman and David and Lauren Parsly. Also survived by sister Ruth Robinowitz and brother-in-law Gerald Schnitzer. His undying love and fierce devotion to his family have been an inspiration to us all. We will revere and remember the incredible courage, strength and determination that guided his life through each and every turn forever. Services Sunday, 12 noon, The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam Ave.
