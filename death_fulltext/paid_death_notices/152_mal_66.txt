
        BARENHOLTZ-Steven Howard, 44, of Ossining, NY died suddenly on August 29 at his home. Born in New York City, he was an author, lecturer and consultant to the restaurant industry. Son of Evelyn Barenholtz of Syracuse, NY and the late Morton Barenholtz, he is also survived by brothers Jan of Southport, CT., and Paul of New York City, nieces Jeanne and Madeleine and nephews Daniel and David. Services were at Beth David in Elmont, New York.
