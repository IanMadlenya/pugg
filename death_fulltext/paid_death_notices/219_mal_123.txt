
        WARNER--Robert C. Professor Emeritus, Molecular Biology and Biochemistry, School of Biological Sciences, University of California at Irvine, died March 25th, 2005. Beloved husband of Estelle; father of Peter, Jisho and Victoria and stepfather of Ruth Anderson. He did his undergraduate work at California Institute of Technology, received his PhD from NYU in 1941 and was Professor of Biochemistry at NYU Medical School from 1946 until 1969, and Chairman of the Department and Professor of Molecular Biology and Biochemistry at Irvine beginning in 1969. He did pioneering work in the mechanisms of genetic recombination.
