
        ROBERTSON--Elizabeth King, 53. Lizzy died October 12, 2005 from cancer, peacefully in her sleep at her home in Alameda, California. She is survived by many loving and devoted relatives, including her husband James Robertson, parents Henry L. King and Mary S. King, stepmother Margaret G. King, siblings Matthew, Katie, Andy & Ellie, stepbrothers Michael and Joseph, and eleven nieces and nephews. Lizzy will be remembered for her warmth, generosity of spirit and sense of humor. She will be greatly missed by her family and many friends. A private memorial service will be scheduled in the future.
