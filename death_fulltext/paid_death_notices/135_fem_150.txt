
        LUKOMNIK-Joanne. The students, alumni, faculty and staff of the Columbia School of Public Health mourn the passing of our dedicated faculty member. A longtime advocate for public health issues and leader in the American Public Health Association, she will be missed. To her husband, Len Rodberg, children Josie and Simon and her many friends, we extend our deepest sympathy. Allan Rosenfield, MD, Dean Columbia School of Public Health
        LUKOMNIK-Joanne, M.D. The staff, faculty and alumni of the Residency Program in Social Medicine of Montefiore Medical Center mourn the death of a special friend and esteemed colleague. Dr. Lukomnik has been an inspiring leader and advocate for public health and community health centers locally and nationally for the past 20 years. Her expansive knowledge and tireless activism will be greatly missed.
