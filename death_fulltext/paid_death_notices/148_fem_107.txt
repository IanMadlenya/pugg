
        EVERETT-Lee Kaye. 68, of New York City, NY, on May 14, 1999. Loving mother of Karen Millet of Hilton Head, SC, and Anthony Everett of Wayland, MA. She is also survived by her five grandchildren. Ms. Everett had been a fashion executive at Vanity Fair Mills, Hoechst Fibers, Bonwit Teller, and Women's Wear Daily. She was President and CEO of her own consulting company, Corporate Accessories. In addition, she was involved in many charitable works, notably Childville. In lieu of flowers her children request donations be made to The Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
