
        SHEA-James Donald, age 72, of Bethpage, Long Island, and Shelter Island, New York. Formerly of Albertson, died May 11, 2000 after a long battle with cancer. Born October 25, 1927 to James Francis Shea & Frances Fitzgerald Shea of Brooklyn, NY. Survived by his loving wife Ruth Theobald Shea, pre-deceased by his beloved wife Margaret M. Shea, his five children, Deirdre Fonseca, James, Brian, Christopher & Gavin, their spouses Martin, Maria, Patricia, Suzanne and Mary, and twelve grandchildren. Also survived by his sisters Clair Marie Dolan and Marian Jelinek and brothers Kevin and Gerard. Predeceased by his brother Geoffrey. Reposing after 2PM today, Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 2515 No. Jerusalem Road, (Exit 25N SS Pkwy), East Meadow. Funeral Mass Monday, 9AM at St. James RC Church, Seaford LI. Interment Our Lady of the Isle Cemetery in Shelter Island, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to Calvary Fund, 1740 Eastchester Rd., Bronx, NY 10461, The American Cancer Society, 839 Stewart Ave, Garden City, New York 11530 or Our Lady of the Isle Church, PO Box 3027, Shelter Island Heights, N.Y. 11965. Family will receive friends Saturday and Sunday 2-5 and 7-10 P.M.
