
        GROHS-Polly Hart. Of Sarasota Fla. formerly of Cleveland, Ohio and Scarsdale, NY. Died February 12, 2001 at age 87. Beloved wife of Edward M. for 59 years. Dear sister of Elaine H. Neuburger and the late Louis M. Hart. Remembered with love by her nephew and niece, Frederic Neuburger and Joan N. Savitt. She will be missed.
