
        FASTENBERG-Vera. The National Commission and staff of the Anti-Defamation League mourn the passing of Vera Fastenberg, beloved sister of our General Counsel, Arnold Forster. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Arnold and his family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Howard P. Berkowitz, Nat'l Chmn. Abraham H. Foxman, Nat'l Dir.
