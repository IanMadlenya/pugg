
        FRIES--Cynthia M. on July 1, 2003 at Franciscan Oaks, Denville, New Jersey. Longtime resident of Montclair, NJ. Daughter of Colonel and Mrs. Claude S. Fries. Col. Fries was a veteran of the Spanish American War and WW I. She is survived and missed by her sister-in-law and three nephews and several grand nephews and grand nieces and a friend of many years.
