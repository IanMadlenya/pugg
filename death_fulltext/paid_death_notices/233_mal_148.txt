
        LESSER--Saal D. (1924 - 2006). Died at peace of Alzheimer 's Disease on June 13, 2006. He was ever the adoring husband of Deborah (Silverman), doting father of Marc and Elizabeth Lesser, Robin and Chris Cordima, and Nina and Todd Insler and proud grandfather of Danielle, Madeleine, Matthew, Gabriel and Ethan. He served as Director of the Encampment for Citizenship, District Director of the Office of Economic Opportunity under Sargent Shriver and as the Executive Director the N.Y. State Division of Human Rights. Through his consulting firms , Saal Lesser Associates and EEO Compliance Services, he was a respected advisor and tireless champion of equal rights. He served as President of the YM-YWHA of Southern Westchester and as Civil Service Commissioner of the City of Mount Vernon. He was an honored athlete and a true friend. Contributions in his memory would be appreciated by The Southern Poverty Law Center or by Jansen Hospice, Tuckahoe, N.Y. His family and friends will gather to remember him on Sunday, June 18, 2006 at noon at Riverside Chapel 21W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon, N. Y.
