
        HOWE-Marion Chait. On April 2, 1997. Devoted and loving sister of Allan Chait, beloved sister-in-law of Jocelyn Chait, adored aunt of Andrew and Steven Chait and Madeleine Brand, caring stepmother of David Howe and her step-grandchildren. Friends may call Saturday evening 7 to 9 PM. Services Sunday, April 6, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions may be made to The Philanthropic Fund of Temple Emanu-El, 1 E. 65 St, NYC 10021 or Channel 13.
        HOWE-Marion Chait. The Officers, Board and the Members of the National Antique & Art Dealers Association of America, Inc., are deeply saddened by the loss of our long-time member who was a sister of our past President Allan Chait, and aunt of our Secretary Andrew Chait. Gracious in spirit, boundless in energy, she will be greatly missed. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her family. Anthony Victoria, President
