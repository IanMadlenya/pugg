
        GOLDSTONE-Clarice. Died October 27, 2001. Loving wife for 48 years of Abner Goldstone, who died in 1993. Loving and devoted mother and grandmother. She is survived by her son Abner (Sandy) and her granddaughter Leah. Clarice lived a full life and will be missed by her many friends. She did not wish to have a memorial service.
