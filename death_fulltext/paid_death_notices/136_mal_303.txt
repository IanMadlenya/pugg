
        GIMBEL-Nicholas Steiner. Died May 25, 1998. Cherished husband, father, brother, grandfather, uncle and friend. His wife, Amy, his children, Elizabeth, Victoria and Nicholas, his brothers, Tom and Stinor, and his grandchildren, Beatrice, Chloe, Emma, Amos, Rebecca, Isabella, Eliza and Anne, mourn his loss. Graduate of Fessenden, Hotchkiss, Yale, and the University of Pennsylvania Medical School. His life as a surgeon and teacher was distinguished by compassion and insight. Memorial service will be on June 19th.
