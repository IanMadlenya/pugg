
        HESS - Leonard J. On January 1, 1997, in his 91st year. passed away in Hollywood, Flordia. Beloved husband of the late Ruth Sloan Hess and the late Carol Hirschfeld Hess. Devoted father of Stephen R. and Katherine Ann. Loving brother of the late Shirley Freydberg. Service Sunday, January 5, 1997, 10 AM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Jewish Guild for the Blind, 15 West 65 Street, New York, New York 10023.
        HESS, Leonard. Temple Shaaray-Tefila records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member. Heartfelt condolences are extended to Katherine and Stephen and the entire family.  Rabbi Harvey M. Tattelbaum  Ellen R. Saxl, President
