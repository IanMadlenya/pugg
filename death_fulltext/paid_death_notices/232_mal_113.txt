
        GORDON--Louis H. A memorial service for Louis H. Gordon, 90, of Millbrook, NY, will be held at The Unitarian Fellowship of Poughkeepsie on May 20, 2006 at 1 P.M. Louis Gordon died on March 25, 2006. A veteran of The Abraham Lincoln Brigade in the Spanish Civil War. He also served in the U.S. Army in World War II. He was a Union Officer for more than 40 years with The United Paperworkers International Union. His loss is mourned by his family, friends, and the Officers and Members of The Steelworkers Local 4-107, Brooklyn, NY. For more information call: 718-522-7272.
