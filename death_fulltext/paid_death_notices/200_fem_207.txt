
        van VALKENBURG--Arlene. 65, of New York City, passed away September 27, at her residence after a long illness. Born July 25, 1938, Ms. van Valkenburg grew up in Larchmont, NY, the beloved daughter of the late George B. and Catherine. A graduate of Mamaroneck High School and St. Michael's College of the University of Toronto, Arlene was retired from Martin H. Bauman and Associates in New York City. She is survived by two brothers, George B. Jr. of Orange Park, FL, and John R. of South Salem, NY, and two sisters, Joan Platz of Charleston, WV, and Anne Fletcher of Skaneateles, NY. Also surviving are her sisters-in-law Ellie and Lucille van Valkenburg and her brother-in-law Theodore A. Platz Jr., as well as 13 nieces and nephews and 16 great-nieces and nephews. Friends may call at the Gannon Funeral Home, 152 East 28th St., New York City, on Sunday and Monday between the hours of 7 and 9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Francis of Assissi Chapel at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY, on Tuesday, September 30, at noon. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests that contributions be made to Visiting Nurse Services of New YorkHospice Care, 1250 Broadway, New York, NY 10001, Miracle House, 560 West 43rd St., New York City 10036 or Emunah Services, Inc., 1371 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn, NY 11230.
