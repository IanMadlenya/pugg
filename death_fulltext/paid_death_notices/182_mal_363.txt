
        BORENSTEIN-Samuel. The Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach records with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved and esteemed member, Samuel Borenstein. He is fondly remembered for over 50 years of support and leadership, including service as President from 1975 to 1985. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and know no further sorrow.
        BORENSTEIN - Samuel. Palm Beach Orthodox Synagogue mourns the passing of its beloved and esteemed Board Member. His memory will serve as an inspiration to all who knew him. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Rabbi Moshe E. Scheiner, Michael C. Gelfand, M.D., President
        BORENSTEIN-Samuel. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi Ira Leon Rennert, Chairman J. Ezra Merkin, President
