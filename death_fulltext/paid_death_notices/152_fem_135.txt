
        MAHLSTEDT-Ruth H. McCarthy. Of Somers, formerly a 20 year Chappaqua resident passed away September 13, 1999. She was the widow of John Henry Mahlstedt, the devoted mother of John, Daniel and Peter. Loving mother-inlaw of Carol and Susan and the spirited, cherished grandmother of Lindsay, Lauren, Kristen, Sarah, Katie and John. Mrs. Mahlstedt was the former church secretary, and Elder of the Pleasantville Presbyterian Church. Her family will receive friends Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Beecher Funeral Home in Pleasantville, NY. Service Friday 10 AM Pleasantville Presbyterian Church. Interment Kensico Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to the Pleasantville Presbyterian Church or the American Heart Association in her name would be appreciated.
