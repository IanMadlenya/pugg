
        DiFRANCESCO-John, M.D. The Ophthalmology Department of Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center of New York notes with sadness the death of John DiFrancesco, M.D. An esteemed physician and active staff member for over 35 years, his clinical acumen, surgical skills and practical good judgment earned him the respect of his patients and colleagues. His gentle, compassionate manner and warm smile won him their affection. He will be greatly missed. Robert A. D'Amico, M.D., Chairman, Dept of Ophthalmology
