
        BRAUNSTEIN -- Charles Stuart. With great sadness, the family of Charles Braunstein announces his death on March 24, 2004 in Scottsdale, AZ. His warmth, humor and caring will be remembered and missed by family and friends and especially by his wife, Ruth and sisters, Phyllis Margolin and Edith Magaziner. Donations may be made to Hospice of the Valley, 1510 East Flower St, Phoenix, AZ, 85014 or Self-Help for Hard of Hearing People (SHHH), 7910 Woodmont Ave, Suite 1200, Bethesda, MD. 20814.
