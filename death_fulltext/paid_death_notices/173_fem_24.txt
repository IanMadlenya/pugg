
        BLOOM-Joanne Sobel. Age 56. Passed away May 29, 2001. Devoted wife of Robert Bloom, adored daughter of Sylvia and the late Oscar Sobel, beloved sister of Barbara and brother-in-law of Mark Lichtenstein, loving mother to Darren, Brandon and Justin Mallis, caring grandmother of Ethan Greenberg. Survived by many dear relatives, inlaws and friends. Laid to rest June 1 at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Remembered for her creativity and kindness. She will be missed by all and remains in our hearts forever. Donations to American Heart Association would be appreciated.
