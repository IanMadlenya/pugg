
        ACKERMAN-Kenneth P. 92, on June 10, 2000. Lost his valiant battle with Parkinson's disease. Long time member Real Estate Board of New York. Survived by his life partner Russ Webster; sister-in-law Mary of Chambersburg, PA; nieces and nephews cherished since their infancy. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Tuesday, June 13, 4 PM. To know him was to love him. Ask anybody.
