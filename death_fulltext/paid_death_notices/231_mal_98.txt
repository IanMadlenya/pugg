
        PLUMMER -- Samuel Craig IV. Of Croton, died early Friday morning, April 7. Never a complainer, he bore his illnesses with extraordinary dignity and patience. Born in Chicago on December 12, 1934, he was the son of Samuel Craig III and Anne Elizabeth Blanchard Plummer. A retired textbook editor and writer, Sam is survived by his wife Vera, daughter Elizabeth Repoli and husband Andrew of New York City and son William of Shanghai. Friends may call at the Edward F. Carter Funeral Home, 41 Grand St, in Croton, on Wednesday, April 12, from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9.
