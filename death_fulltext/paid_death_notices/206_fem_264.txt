
        LEVIN--Fanny. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of a long-time friend and supporter of medical research and education programs at our institution. With her late husband, Milton, who was an esteemed member of our Board of Overseers, she was among the earliest members of our Society of Founders and a University Guardian. An ardent supporter of our cancer research programs, Mrs. Levin, along with her son, James, served on our Cancer Research Advisory Board. To James, to her daughter, Gene Heller, and to the entire family, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman Board of Trustees Albert Einstein College of Medicine Robert A. Belfer, Chairperson Board of Overseers Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Marilyn Katz, Chairperson Cancer Research Advisory Board
        LEVIN--Fanny. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Fanny Levin, beloved mother of Gene Heller and James Levin, cherished friends of the New York Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
