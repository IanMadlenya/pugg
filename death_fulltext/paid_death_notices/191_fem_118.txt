
        YOUNG-Ruth. Born July 13, 1905 in Lakewood, New Jersey. Passed away in Rockville, MD on December 12, 2002. She is survived by her devoted son Robert, adoring grandchildren Melissa, Debbie, Michael and Danny and loving great-granddaughter Emily. We will all cherish her memory. Services will be held at Danzansky-Goldberg Memorial Chapel in Rockville on Sunday, December 15.
