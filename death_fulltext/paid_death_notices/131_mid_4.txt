
        DELIBERT-Anne Lash. The New York Society for Ethical Culture records with deepest sorrow the death of our devoted member on November 27 in her 87th year. Her husband Joseph C. Delibert was the former president of the New York Society for Ethical Culture. She is survived by her husband, Joseph C. Delibert, by their son Steven, his wife Kris Reed, and their daughters Harsha and Himu; by their son Arthur, his wife Anne, and their daughter Adrienne; and by a step grandson Jeremy Blake. Don Johnson, Senior Leader Judy Wallach, President Trustees and Staff of the New York Society for Ethical Culture
