
        POCKER-M. Peabody. Passed away November 14, 2001 following a long illness. Beloved by all who knew him. He is survived by his soulmate and life-long companion Lily Pocker and his grieving family, Richard and Beth Pocker of Pound Ridge, N.Y., Audrey and Marvin Pocker of New Rochelle, N.Y., Sam Pocker of Stamford, CT. and a sister, Roxie Azzoni of New Milford, CT. The family wishes to thank Dr. Sean Bell of Greenwich, CT. for his friendship and support during this difficult time. Blessed with every virtue and grace, loving and courageous, he was and shall remain the standard for a champion. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be set to Adopt-aDog Inc. of Greenwich, CT.
