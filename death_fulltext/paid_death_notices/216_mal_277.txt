
        RICHARDS--John Elliott. 58, of Palm Beach Gardens, FL, on January 4, 2005. Beloved husband to Karen Moran Richards. Adoring father to John Patrick, son of Raymond G. Richards and Catherine Gannon. Born in Bronxville, NY, raised in Larchmont, NY. Attended Iona Elementary and Prep, graduated from Mamaroneck High School. Received a BBA from the University of Miami; did post - graduate work at George Washington University in Washington, DC. Served in the U.S. Air Force from 1969 to 1972. Owner of Richards-McLaughlin & Co., a publishers' representative firm, has represented many major publications some of which are the AARP Group, Kiplinger, ABA Journal, Sports Illustrated, Cosmopolitan, Time Inc. and many others. He is survived by a brother, R. Gill Richards of Stuart, FL, sisters Marion Hagerty of Larchmont, NY and Margaret Golden of Dallas. He was uncle to 15 nieces and nephews, and granduncle to 11. In lieu of flowers please send donations to Maryknoll Father and Brothers, 914-941-7590.
