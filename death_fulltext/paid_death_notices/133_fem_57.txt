
        ROGIN-Lillian (nee Ruderman). On February 5, 1998, at home, aged 98. Devoted wife of the late Robert, beloved mother of Gilbert and Richard, dear mother-in-law of Abigail, loving grandmother of Kate, Frank and Sally, and devoted aunt to Carolyn Korshin, Juliet Bernstein and Lucy Pearce. Her family will always cherish her fond recollections of her theatrical career when, under the stage name of Billie Rudell she acted with the Triangle Players, the Provincetown Players, and on Broadway in a David Belasco production, and with Alfred Lunt in Eugene O'Neill's ''Marco Millions,'' produced by The Theatre Guild.
