
        SETON-Stanley, DDS. On January 22nd. Adored husband of Adele, cherished uncle of Tony, Jennie, Nora, Maggie, Julie, Wendy, Richard, Simone, Joel, Beverly, Charles and Lois. Loved by a multitude of friends. Served in the U.S. Army in WW II and as a Captain in the Air Force in the Korean War. Was a clinical instructor in oral surgery at Polyclinic Hospital. His work was his life. He was considered the best by the best. Memorial service on Wednesday, January 23rd, at 3:30PM at the Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 4717 Okeechobee Boulevard, West Palm Beach. Graveside service Friday, January 25th, at 1:00PM, at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York.
        SETON-Dr. Stanley. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Dr. Stanley Seton. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Adele, and their family. Richard J. Bornstein, President Leon Fassler, Secretary
        SETON-Stanley Dr. A most devoted friend, we will greatly miss that warm loving smile, and his very special laughter. Selma and Marty
