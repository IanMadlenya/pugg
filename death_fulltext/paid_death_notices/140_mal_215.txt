
        CLARK-Edward G. Age 74. Died suddenly on September 15th, at Saltaire, Fire Island. He was a former reporter and editor for the Associated Press. His longtime companion Suzanne Sames and his many, many friends in New York, Green Valley, AZ, and Madison, WI., treasured his wit and devoted friendship, and he will never be replaced in our hearts. Survived by his brother Robert and 11 nieces and nephews in Madison, WI. Funeral Mass and burial Monday, September 21 in Madison
