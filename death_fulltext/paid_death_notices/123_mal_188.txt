
        BRAZIER-John B. (Jack), age 51, died on April 16, 1997 in Meriden, CT, after a long battle with cancer. He was the beloved husband of Leslie Clauson Brazier, and was a sales rep for World of Golf, NYC. He was born in Bridgeport, CT., and was a resident most of his life. Services will take place on Friday at 11:00 AM in St. Thomas Church, Fairfield, CT. Burial will be in Lakeview Cemetery, Bridgeport. Donations may be made to the William S. Brazier Fund or the Sarah E. Brazier Fund, C/O Lafayette American Bank, 1643 Post Road, Fairfield, CT, 06430, Attn: Dee Wust-Forman, Community Banking Officer.
