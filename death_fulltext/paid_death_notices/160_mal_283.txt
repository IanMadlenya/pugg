
        SASSONE-Frank Paul, Brother, C.F.C., age 66, died suddenly on May 22 in Lima, Peru. Predeceased by his beloved parents Frank and Elizabeth Sassone, his sister Marcellus, and brother-inlaw John Jarkowsky, he is survived by his brother Joseph of Huntington Bay, NY, his sisters, Sr. Bernadette, D.W., of Brooklyn, NY, and Mary Jane Jarkowsky, Warwick, RI, his brother John Gerard of Floral Park, NY, his devoted sisters-in-law Victoria and Patricia, a host of loving nieces, nephews, & in-laws from New York, Rhode Island, and Arizona, and affectionate cousins from Cape Breton, Nova Scotia. He is mourned by a legion of beloved and loyal friends and associates. He leaves behind a legacy of love for the Congregation of Christian Brothers, devotion to the cause for canonization of their founder Blessed Edmund Rice and the missionary work in Peru, most recently fostering the Religious Education of children with handicapping conditions. Calling hours are Thurs, May 25 from 2-4PM & 7-9PM and Friday 2-4PM only at St. Joseph's Care Center, 30 Montgomery Circle, New Rochelle, NY 10804. Funeral Mass will be offered Fri. 7:30PM at Blessed Sacrament Church, 15 Shea Pl., New Rochelle, NY. Interment will take place Sat. 11AM at Christian Brothers Cemetery, West Park, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions to Christian Brothers Foundation Peruvian Mission or St. Jospeh's Care Center.
