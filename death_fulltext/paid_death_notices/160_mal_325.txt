
        SHELTON-Edward D. May 26, 2000 in Troy, MI. Age 77. Greatly loved by his wife Ginny and his daughter Samantha. Mr. Shelton loved the theatre and was a Broadway stage manager/director for 20 years. Memorial service today in Bloomfield Hills, MI. Memorials to University of Michigan, Department of Theatre & Drama. Call A.J. Desmond & Sons Funeral Home 248-362-2500 for info.
