
        WILVERS-Robert W., 65. Died at home, December 10, 1997. Loving father of Roberta Wilvers of New York City and Tracy Cassidy of Framingham, Massachusetts. Loving owner of Henrietta Wilvers and beloved grandfather of Aidan Cassidy. His death is a tremendous loss to all of us. Memorial service Saturday, December 20, 11 AM, at Unitarian Universalist Society on Picketts Ridge Road, West Redding, Connecticut.
