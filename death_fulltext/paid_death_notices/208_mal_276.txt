
        BRUEN--Edward F.L. The Board of Directors of Youth Foundation, Inc. notes with profound sorrow the passing of its distinguished Director Emeritus, Edward F.L. Bruen on May 23, 2004. He was the Foundation's long serving Vice President in which capacity he gave unstintingly of his time and energy. His selfless dedication to the Foundation gave added luster to the term ''volunteer'' while his great kindness gave added luster to the term ''gentleman''. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Robert W. Radsch, President Pamela S. Fulweiler, Vice President Guy N. Robinson, Secretary S. Scott Nicholls, Jr., Treasurer
        BRUEN--Edward F.L. The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society notes with sadness the death of Edward Fiedler Livingston Bruen, F.G.B.S., Trustee Emeritus, on May 23, 2004 in Oyster Bay, New York. He served as Trustee, Secretary, President and Chairman of the Heraldry Committee during his many years of devoted service to the Society. The Officers, Board and Staff extend their condolences to his family. Henry C.B. Lindh, President
