
        GILMAN--Arline, 75 years old, died on February 22, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Seymour ''Sy'' Antor. Sister of the late Ira Gilman. Dearest aunt of Jodi Gilman and Jeff and Alysa Gilman and sisterin-law of Della Gilman. Also survived by a grand nephew Adam & grand niece Kaitlyn. Graveside services Sunday 11AM at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, N.Y. Donations in her memory may be made to Temple Beth El of Great Neck or the Cerebral Palsy Association.
