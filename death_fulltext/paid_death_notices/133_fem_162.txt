
        DEANIN-Grace Gordon, 73. Of New York City, died on February 19, 1998 after a long illness. Dr. Deanin, Professor Emeritus at the University of New Mexico, School of Medicine, designated member of The Hall of Fame of Hunter College, author of many scientific papers, is survived by her husband Malcolm, her 2 daughters Nancy and Alice, and three grandchildren. Donations in Dr. Deanin name may be made to organizations of your choice. A Gathering celebrating her life and work will be announced soon.
