
        NUNES-Donald. Died in Bath, England, on Tuesday, November 11, after courageous battle with cancer. Son of Kenneth and Helen Nunes, husband of Ann Biddick Nunes, father of Becky and Jonathan. Don was inspirational, much loved and irreplaceable. A celebration of his life will take place in Lacock Church, Wiltshire, at 2 PM on Monday, November 24. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Don Nunes Memorial Scholarship Fund, c/o Advanced Studies in England, 2 Pierrepont St., Bath BA1 1LB, England.
