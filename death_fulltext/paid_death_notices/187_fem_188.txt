
        ALLEN-Harold. Of Great Neck on August 27, 2002, age 92. Beloved husband of Ruth. Cherished father of Janet and Joe Dippell and Joy and David Campbell. Grandfather of Dawn Dippell, Allen Dippell and Marjorie Mannix and her husband John. Great grandfather of Michael, Evan and Megan Mannix. Brother of Marjorie Bisgood. Former Partner of Allen & Company Investment Bankers and later a member of the NYSE. Served as trustee of Ithaca College & Associate Trustee of North Shore University Hospital. Visiting hours Wednesday 3-5pm at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard Manhasset. Interment 10am Thursday, All Saints Cemetery, Great Neck. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Cardiology Division of North Shore University Hospital.
