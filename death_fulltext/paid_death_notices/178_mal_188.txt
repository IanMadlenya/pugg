
        McCRACKEN-John E. The Amateur Comedy Club records with sorrow the passing on November 4th of its esteemed Retired Member. Elected in 1951, John was a gentleman and loyal member of the Club, and will be missed. Our sincere condolences to his family. There will be a memorial service at Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church on November 15 at 11 AM. George S. Coyne II, President
