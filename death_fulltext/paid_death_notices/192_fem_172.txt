Nieces Laura McSweeney of West Granby, Connecticut and Sarah Chadwick of Schnectady, New York and cousins Frank Stanley of Canton, Connecticut and Courtney Stanley Brady of Edgartown, Massachussets. Donations in her memory may be made to the Order of St. Anne, 25 Hillside Ave, Arlington, Massachussets, 02476-5816. For information the Richard Davis Funeral Homes of Plymouth and Manomet.
