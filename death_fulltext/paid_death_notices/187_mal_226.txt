
        FARRAH-Jere Tiffin. Age 57, died suddenly of a heart attack, August 18, 2002. Beloved son of Mrs. Jere T. Farrah Sr, uncle of Tiffin. Funeral held in South Carolina. Esteemed member of the staff at The Cathedral Church of St John the Divine where a memorial service is planned. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
