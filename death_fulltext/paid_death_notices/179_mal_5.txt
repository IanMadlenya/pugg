
        VITOLO-Ralph Francis, 59. Of Lido Beach, NY, died on November 17 in Pattaya, Thailand. Cause of death was heart failure. He is survived by Theresa Vitolo, mother Frances of Fort Lauderdale, FL, sister Ann Carol Caponi of Greenwich Village, NY and niece Meeso and nephew Jared. Mr. Vitolo had a 23 year career in New York City's Government, Computer and Data Communications Service Agency. He retired as Commissioner of User Services, which provided Telecommunications Services to over 40 City Agencies. Avid reader, traveler, and lover of life, he will be dearly missed by family, friends, and golf buddies. Memorial services to be held Monday, December 3, 2001 at The First Presbyterian Church in the City of New York, Fifth Avenue, Eleventh and Twelfth Streets, 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to The Tiger Woods Foundation at 4281 Katella Ave., Ste 111, Los Alamitos, CA 90720, would be appreciated.
