
        PRICE--Margery. The Jewish Women's Foundation of New York is gravely saddened by Margery Price's death. She was an early member of the Foundation. Her concern for Jewish women and girls will continue to inspire our work. Our deepest sympathies are extended to her entire family including her daughter,Eileen Farbman and her daughterin-law, Tina Price, both JWFNY members. Betsy Landis, President Sherri Greenbach, Exec. Dir.
