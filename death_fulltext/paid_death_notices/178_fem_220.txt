
        BERGER-Annette. 77, of Fort Lee and formerly Teaneck, NJ, died Nov. 19, 2001. Annette awaited her childhood sweetheart's return from World War II to become a loving wife to Leon Berger for 56 years and a devoted mother to Douglas and the late Andrew Berger. She is also survived by brother, Albert Richmond and sister, Sylvia Pierce. Annette participated in the struggle for human rights. She was the administrator for her family's business, Creston Optical in NY. A talented sculptor, Annette loved theater and the arts. She enjoyed life and will be forever remembered. Services will be held noon on Sunday, November 25th at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway, Fair Lawn, NJ.
