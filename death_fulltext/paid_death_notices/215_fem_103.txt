
        SOFFER--Madeline Magzis, 63. On December 11, 2004 in Norwalk, Ct. from sequelae of Diabetes. She worked at the Norwalk Public Library and is survived by her husband of 36 years, Richard, their children Philip of Berkeley, and Abigail, of San Francisco, her brother Michael of New York City and her sister, Laura, of Concord, N.H. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
