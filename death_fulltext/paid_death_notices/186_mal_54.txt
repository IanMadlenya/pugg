
        BERLIN-Emanuel. Passed away on June 26th, 2002, at the age of 81. He is survived by Doris, his beloved wife of fifty-three years, his children, Michael and Patricia to whom he was devoted, his adored grandson, Alexander, his sister, Rosetta, and other family members. Emanuel was self employed in the pharmaceutical industry and a law school graduate with a passion for justice. He was a devoted family man, who enjoyed life and lived it to the fullest. We will miss you. Service will be held on Sunday, July 7 at 12 noon at the family home.
