
        CHASE-Paul Jerome. Of Mill Neck, NY died at home in Palm Beach, FL on February 7, 2003. He is survived by his wife, Florence McKim Chase, his daughters, Mrs. Edward Culver of Wayland, MA, Mrs. Richard I. Flintoft of New York City, a son Brewster Chase of Ithaca, NY, two granddaughters Ann Prentice and Jennings Lee Culver, four step-daughters, Mrs. John P. Havens, Mrs. Newell B. Woodworth III, Heidi L. Doubleday and Mrs. Carl W. Timpson III. He is survived by his brothers, George E. Chase of Atlanta, GA, Jerome K. Chase of Wayland, MA, Peter Chase of Santa Fe, NM. He was predeceased by Dr. Henry A. Chase of New York City and his former wife, Mary Brewster Jennings of Glen Head, Long Island, NY. He graduated from Choate School, Yale University, Class of 1939 and the University of Virginia Law School in 1942. He was a partner at Casey, Beinecke and Chase and a founding partner at Olwine Connelly, Chase, O'Donnell and Weyher and later of council to Breed, Abbott and Morgan. A memorial service will be held at 11:30AM on Tuesday, February 18, 2003 at Bethesda By the Sea Episcopal Church, Palm Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the Strang Cancer Prevention Center at 428 E. 72 St, New York, NY 10021 or Choate-Rosemary Hall, 333 Christian Street, Wallingford, CT. 06492.
