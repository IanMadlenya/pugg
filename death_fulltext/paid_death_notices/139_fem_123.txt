
        JOVANOVIC-Cornelia Judith (Judy) Laurentia. In Windsor, Ontario (formerly of Bailey's Bay, Bermuda). On August 14, 1998 in her 40th year. Beloved wife of George and loving mother of Thea and Nicholas. Pre-deceased by her father, the late Antonie Thyssen (1985).
