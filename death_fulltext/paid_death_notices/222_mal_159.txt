
        BAKER--William Graham, Jr., 86, of Sanibel, Florida, died on July 9, 2005. He was a retired Vice President of the F.W. Woolworth Co. He is survived by his wife, Janice, daughters, Linda, Kim, Cindy, Leigh, sons William III, Robert, nine grandchildren, two great grandchildren, sisters Margaret Weber and Viann McGarry.
