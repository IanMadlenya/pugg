
        HART-Parker Thompson. On Wednesday, October 15, 1997 Parker Thompson Hart of Washington, D.C. Beloved husband of Jane S. Hart. Father of Margaret Hart Espey of Lafayette, CA and Judith Hart Halsema of Karachi, Pakisan. Grandfather of John and Audrey Espey and Petra and Rachael Halsema. He is also survived by 1 nephew, 4 nieces and many grandnephews and nieces. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. Interment private. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made in his name to the Middle East Institute or Bread for the City. Joseph Gawler's Sons, Inc.
