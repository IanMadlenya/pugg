
        WILSON-Arthur R. Died at his home July 22, 1999. Mr. Wilson was a Phi Beta Capa graduate of Dartmouth College and served in the Marine Corps during the Second World War. He went on to become a founder of the DR Group where he became a recognized leader in the Direct Marketing Industry. He will be missed both by his family and his many friends. There will be no memorial service. Contributions may be made in his memory to: God's Love We Deliver, 166 Avenue of the Americas, NY, NY 10013.
