
        BAYLOS--Eugene. Born November 16, 1906, died peacefully today, January 10, 2005. Devoted husband of Cyrile and loving uncle. Longtime member of the Friars Club and known as ''the Comedian's Comedian.'' Services Tuesday, 2 P.M., at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        BAYLOS--Gene. The officers, governors and members of the Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their beloved and esteemed member and Honorary Officer Gene Baylos. Gene was the consummate court jester who gave the gift of laughter. He was a master craftsman of humor who brought the Friars Club to hilarious heights during his Roast and Dinner appearances. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel Tuesday, January 11 at 2 PM at 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
