
        FONTANALS--George, on June 1, 2005, of Old Chatham, NY, on his 70th birthday. Beloved husband of Jane Friend, loving father of George, Jr. of East Hampton, NY, Jennifer Fontanals Woolford of Wilmette, IL, their mother Mary Ann Fontanals; cherished grandfather of Aubrey and Cooper Woolford, and stepfather of Karen Hermanson, Gerard Friend, Julie Smith and Ted Friend. He ran his own swimwear co., Gabar, Inc. in NYC and will be missed by his friends in Old Chatham NY, Saltaire, Fire Island, and St. Kitts, WI. A memorial Mass will take place Monday, June 6 at 11AM at St. James Church, Chatham, NY.
