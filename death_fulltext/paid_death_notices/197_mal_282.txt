
        KUNSTADTER--John W., on June 18, 2003 at age 75, philanthropist, physicist and international banker, died peacefully at home. Beloved husband of Geraldine Kunstadter. Loving father of John Jr., Lisa and Chris Kunstadter and Elizabeth von Habsburg. Devoted grandfather of eight. Dear brother of Peter Kunstadter. Interment private. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions in his memory may be made to Bargemusic, Fulton Ferry Landing, Brooklyn, NY 11201. >>AD#
