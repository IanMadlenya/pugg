
        RODGERS -- James Beall, M.D. Died in Englewood, NJ on March 9, 2005. He was educated at the University of Virginia where he received his M.D.. He completed his surgical training in New York at Columbia University and Bellevue Hospitals. He was a senior attending surgeon at St. Luke's - Roosevelt Hospital and was in private practice in New York until his retirement in 1991. He was a nationally recognized watercolorist and a signature member of various watercolor societies. He is survived by his wife, Anne, and three children: David, Alan, and Alice RodgersGraves. Services will be held at the Church of the Atonement in Tenafly, NJ on Monday, March 14 at 4:00PM. Contributions may be made in his name to the University of Virginia Medical Alumni Foundation, P.O Box 800776, Charlottesville, VA 22908.
