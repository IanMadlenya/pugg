
        PRENDERGAST-Kevin J. On September 9, 2000, after a long illness. Tax attorney, specializing in executive compensation. For most of his career, practiced law with the firm of Patterson, Belknap, Webb & Tyler, where he was described by a partner as a person of emense integrity and ability. Beloved husband of Elizabeth. Donations in his memory may be made to Washington University in support of lung transplant research through Randy Farmer, Medical Alumni & Development Programs, Washington University, Campus Box 8509, 4444 Forest Park Ave, Saint Louis, MO. 63108.
