
        DESIBIA-Edward J., 82. Of 2 South View Drive, Keene, NH died Tuesday at Lee Memorial Hospital in Fort Myers, FL. He was born in New York City, August 12, 1915, the son of John and Grace (Antonucci) Desibia, was raised there and then lived most of his life in River Edge, NJ before moving to Pompton Lakes, NJ for several years & Keene in 1991. Mr. Desibia was employed from 1945 to 1980 as a chauffeur for the New York Times. He was an active member of St. Peters Church in River Edge and was a fourth degree Knights of Columbus. He was a US Marine veteran of World War II serving in the South Pacific and Okinawa. Survivors include his wife of 51 years, Anita (Cipriani) Desibia of Keene, one son, John E. Desibia of Warwick, NY, two daughters, Deborah A. McMahon of Walpole and Phyllis Thrower of Belleview, NE, one sister, Mary Gangi of Bronx, NY, ten grandchildren, seven great grandchildren, nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be conducted at a later date. Burial will be in Friedsam Cemetery in Chesterfield, NH. There will be no calling hours. Contributions may be made in his memory to the NH Heart Fund, 20 Merrimack St, Suite 1, Manchester, NH 03101.
