
        PORTER-Robert C., age 85, died of heart failure on March 12. He is survived by his wife of 56 years, Elizabeth W. Porter, two children, and two grandchildren. He was raised in Philadelphia, spent most of his life in Summit and Chatham, NJ, and summered in South Harpswell, Maine. He graduated from Bowdoin College and the University of Pennsylvania Law School. He began his long career of legal and investment work in New York at Cravath, Swaine and Moore, was Vice President at Chemical Bank & Trust, and head of investment banking at Shearson, Hamill & Co. He became partner and later President, Chairman and Director of F. Eberstadt & Co. and Eberstadt Fund Management, Inc. Before his retirement in 1987, he was President and then Chairman of Chemical Fund, Inc. and on the Board of Directors of the Alliance Fund. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests contributions to the Lost Tree Charitable Foundation, North Palm Beach, FL, 33408. Memorial services will be held at the Lost Tree Chapel, North Palm Beach on March 24. For information contact Wm. N. Howard Funeral Home, North Palm Beach, FL.
