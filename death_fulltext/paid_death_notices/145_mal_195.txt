
        FEINBERG-Milt, 87, formerly of Manhattan and Lauderhill, FL. died from pneumonia on February 14th. He was the Executive Vice President of National Screen Service, an advertising company for major motion picture studios until his retirement in 1976. His wife Charlotte died in 1997. He is survived by two daughters, Susan Farber Ganz of Washington, CT., Linda Graev of NYC and 3 grandchildren, Adam and Nicole Graev and Samantha Ganz Panzier.
