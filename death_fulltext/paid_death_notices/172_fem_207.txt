
        FELDMAN-Doris, 75. Of Boca Raton, FL., died suddenly Wednesday, May 23. Born August 1, 1925, Rockaway, NY. Doris graduated Columbia University as a dental hygienist. She married Hilliard (Hilly) Feldman of Philadelphia, December 15, 1946. Raising her family in Philadelphia, she returned to New York in 1969. Active in Hadassah, she enjoyed the New York art scene. Upon the death of her beloved husband of 39 years in 1986, Doris relocated to Boca Raton. A lover of the arts, Doris was a docent at the Boca Raton Museum of Art. She lived life to the fullest with a passion for boating, travel, dancing, theater, ballet & the arts. Energetic & generous, she had a great sense of humor and keen appreciation for the outrageous and absurd. A wonderful, devoted, loving mother to her daughters, Ellen Goodgold and Nancy Feldman-Saylor, she will be cherished by them always. She was adored by her grandson, Jason Hilliard Goodgold, and sons-in law, Ken Goodgold & John Saylor. Her beloved brother, Harold Schwartz, DDS, predeceased her June 30, 2000. She is survived by sisters-in-law and brother-in-law and their families. We will celebrate the memory of Doris Sunday, June 10, 1:00 PM, at La Fontana, 2003 North Ocean Blvd., Boca Raton. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in memory of Doris Feldman, to the Art Education Program, Boca Raton Museum of Art, 501 Plaza Real, Boca Raton, FL. 33432.
