
        MOLLIN-Lorraine Hoffman. Died on August 22 of complications following a brain aneurysm at age 67. She is survived by her devoted husband, Howard, beloved sons, Steven and Jeffrey, and brother, Marvin. Mrs. Mollin attended Brooklyn College and had been a longtime employee of the Brooklyn Developmental Center. Burial was at Wellwood Cemetery in Farmingdale, Long Island in the presence of her many friends, relatives and neighbors.
