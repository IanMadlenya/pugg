
        KALKSTEIN-Beatrice M. Age 91. February 8, 1997 in Stamford, CT. An actress for fifty years on Broadway, radio soap operas and TV, monologist and story-teller in New York and Stamford, for which she was Honored by the State of Connecticut in October 1991. Survived by sons Shawn of Stamford, Michael of Los Gatos, CA and Peter of New York, six grandchildren and various nieces and nephews.
