
        BRANDT-George W. Sr. of New York City died February 5, 2000 at age 85. He is survived by his wife, Lillian Sampson (Fuhrtz), two children, The Rev. Canon George W. Memorial service Tuesday, February 29th at Noon, at St. Michael's Episcopal Church, Amsterdam Ave. at 99th Street. Interment private.
