
        BERNSTEIN-Sylvia Harris. On January 27, in Mexico, at 92. NYC advertising executive-one of the first women to hold such position. Widow of the writer Hillel Bernstein; mother of Mary Bernstein and grandmother of Jessica Bernstein-Wax, both of San Francisco; sister of the late Jed Harris, the late Mildred Harris, Florence Levine of Los Angeles, and Saul Howitt of New York. She will be greatly missed.
