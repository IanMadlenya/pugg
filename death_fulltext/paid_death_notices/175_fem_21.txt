
        SHANAHAN-Anita, 79. Of St. Petersburg, FL, formerly of Flushing, NY, died July 27th, 2001. Born in Bronx, NY, she was an insurance adjuster for Home Life in Manhattan. Survivors include a sister, Patricia Shanahan, a companion, Eloise Cooke, 4 nieces and nephews and six grand-nieces and nephews. Memorial service Friday, 10 AM, at St. Bede's Episcopal Church, St. Petersburg. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations to St. Bede's Episcopal Church, 2500-16th St. North, St. Petersburg, FL 33704.
