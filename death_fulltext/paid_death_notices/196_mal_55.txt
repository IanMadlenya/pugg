
        WEISBERG - Laurence, 50, died March 13, 2003. Surrealist poet, occasional resident of New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Oaxaca and Sulawesi. Published works include ''The Glacial Blush,'' ''Phantomatic.'' Uncompromising poet of life, his conversation and knowledge were luminous, generous, changing the course of innumerable lives.
