
        PYERON-Joseph J., Sr. Died November 9, 1998 in his sleep after a full day of work. Husband of the late Tessie and survived by his two brothers, Stan and John and his two children, Ruth and Joseph, Jr. He served as a marine in WWII and was a machinist for a defense contractor by career. Viewing will be held at the Dalton Funeral Home Friday evening and funeral on Saturday morning. Donations in his memory may be sent to Bellerose Baptist Church, 240-20 Braddock Avenue, Bellerose, NY 11426.
