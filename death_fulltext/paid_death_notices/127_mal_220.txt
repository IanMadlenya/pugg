
        ZWIRN-Irving. Age 87, on July 29, 1997, Moravia, N.Y. Esteemed attorney for over 60 years. Syracuse University and St. John's Law School, World War II veteran, former Police Justice. Beloved husband of Pola Lewin for 57 years, cherished father of David, Richard and Diana. Adored grandfather of Jonathan. A kind, generous, gentle man with a keen sense of humor. He leaves a special legacy and will be greatly missed.
