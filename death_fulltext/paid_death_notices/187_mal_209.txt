
        LONGCOPE-Edwin. A preeminent maritime lawyer who handled several of the most significant marine disaster case of the 20th century, died in New York August 12, 2002 from complications following hip surgery, at the age of 92. Mr. Longcope was awarded the French Legion of Honor in 1969. Interment will be private & a gathering of remembrance will be held at his beloved University Club at a later time.
