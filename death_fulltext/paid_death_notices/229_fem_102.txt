
        POST--Yetta Hecht. Passed away February 10, at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Sam. Survived by her children, Ina Goldberg (the late Arthur), Gerald and Susan Post, grandchildren, Lawrence Goldberg, Amy and Robert Tardif, Neal Post, Dina and David Nass, great-grandchildren, Adam and Ian Tardif, Tyler, Sophie and Abigail Nass, her brothers and sisters-inlaw, Morris and Gilda Hecht, and Reuben and Leah Hecht, and many cousins, nieces and nephews. Services Monday, February 13, 11:30 AM, at Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn.
