
        STERNBERG -- Samuel, of Brooklyn, NY, passed away on Thursday, March 25th, 2004 at the age of 81. Beloved and devoted husband of his wife of 57 years, Beverly (nee Tartell), cherished father of Wilma, Marcia, and Stuart, adored grandfather of Marissa, Morgen, Sandy, Jake, Natalie, and Ella, loving fatherin-law of Michael, Lisa, and Charles. Mr. Sternberg graduated from Yeshiva of Flatbush in 1939, as part of its first graduating class. He was a proud WWII veteran, having served in the 3rd Army Resupply Squadron of the US Army Air Corps which supplied General George Patton's 3rd Army on its march through Europe. He was an officer of the Seaview Jewish Center and owned the Pillow Shop in Brooklyn, NY. All of us will miss him deeply for his support, wisdom, sense of humor and zest for life. He had a love for sports, movies, and good food, but most importantly, for good friends and good times. He was always so very proud of his whole family. Memorial services will be held at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd. at 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY, on Sunday, March 28th, 2004 at 11 AM. Interment will follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, charitable donations may be made in his memory to the NYU Cardiovascular Center, c/o Dr. William Cole, 530 1st Ave., NY NY 10016.
