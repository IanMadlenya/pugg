
        RUSKAY-Shira. The administration, faculty, students and boards of the Jewish Theological Seminary record with sorrow the death of Shira Ruskay, beloved wife, mother, daughter and sister. Our hearts go out to her husband, our friend and former vice chancellor John Ruskay; her parents, JTS alumnus Rabbi Sidney and Hilda Greenberg; and her entire family on the loss of this exemplary woman. May her memory be for a blessing. Ismar Schorsch, chancellor Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
        RUSKAY-Shira. The officers, Board of Trustees, and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services extends sincere condolences to our colleague and friend, Dr. John Ruskay, COO of UJA-Federation, on the loss of his beloved wife, Shira. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Seymour R. Askin, Jr, Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President
        RUSKAY-Shira. Jewish Life Network mourns the untimely death of a loving and courageous woman, Shira Ruskay, beloved wife of our cherished friend and colleague, John Ruskay. May her entire family be comforted with the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Michael Steinhardt, Chairman Rabbi Irving Greenberg, President Jonathan J. Greenberg, Exec. Dir.
