
        ADLER--Beniek. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Beniek Adler, beloved father of Sol Adler, a treasured leader whose visionary work as executive director of the 92nd Street YM-YWHA has reinvigorated and enriched the New York Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Sol and his wife, Debbie, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
