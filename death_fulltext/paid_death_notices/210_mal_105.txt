
        ATLAS--Howard T. NABETCWA and its membership mourn the passing of Howard T. Atlas on July 8, 2004. Howie's long years of dedicated service in a number of leadership positions in our Union improved the working lives of our members and served as an inspiration to all of us. He will be missed. We extend our sincere sympathy to his wife Ruth and his entire family. John S. Clark, President, James C. Joyce, Vice President NABET-CWA Sector Executive Council
