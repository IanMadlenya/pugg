
        RANGELL--Evelyn. 87, of Bloomingburg, NY, adored wife of Meyer Rangell, died July 31. Loved and respected by all, Evelyn was an inspired teacher. BA from Brooklyn College and MA from the University of Rhode Island. Besides her loving husband she leaves three children Mark, Laura, and Elizabeth, and five grandchildren Nathaniel, Gina, Rosa, Eric and Allison. She was also the ''mother'' and grandmother of Annie, Danielle and Lee, and the cherished great-grandmother of her precious Ryan, Rachel and Benjamin. Gracious, kind, loving and generous, her beautiful presence was central to all our lives. Meyer. Services private.
