
        MORRIS -- Norman David. July 15, age 94. Devoted husband of the late Sylvia, with whom he shared 65 wonderful years. Beloved father of Barbara (Kuperman) and Fredric and father-in-law of Leonard and Marcia. Cherished by his North American and South American grandchildren. Loving brother-inlaw of Emil and Rima Kustin. Norman's full life was enriched by love shared with Terry in his last years. His affection, loyalty, quick mind and jovial spirit will be missed and always remembered.
