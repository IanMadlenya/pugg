
        AMOROSI--Lenora Toland. After a courageous battle with pulmonary fibrosis, ''Mimi'' died on September 12, 2003 at home in McLean, VA. She was the cherished wife of Dr. Leo D. Amorosi and beloved mother of ''Muffin'' Lynham, Maureen Cook, Leo, Edmund, Mark and Brian Amorosi and adored grandmother of eight. She will be sorely missed by her family and friends, as well as the many clients she served so well at Miller Real Estate in Washington, D.C. and Maryland. We, too, will miss you, Mimi. The Amorosi, Bennett and O'Brien families
