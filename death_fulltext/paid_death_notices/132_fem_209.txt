
        HUTCHINGS-Sarah Willim. In her 59th year. Beloved wife of Neil of Paget Parish, Bermuda, mother of Carroll, and step-mother of Nathaniel and Nancy Hutchings. Also survived by her daughter-in-law Rachel, brother John Stone Willim II and his wife Louise in Florida; sister Mrs. Melissa Trask and her husband Fritz of Denver, CO. A memorial service will be held at Christ Presbyterian Church, Warwick, Bermuda, Wednesday, January 28, 4 PM.
