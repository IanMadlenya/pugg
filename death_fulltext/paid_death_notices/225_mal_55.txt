
        KATZ--Dr. Robert. 72 years old died October 5, 2005 at home. Beloved husband and sweetheart of Marilyn for over fifty years. Devoted father of Karin, Daniel and Jordan. Cherished grandfather of Lily and Sam. He practiced medicine in New York City after graduating from Stuyvesant High School, New York University, and the University of Zurich. He and his wife were founders of the Hampton Synagogue in Westhampton Beach and members of the Park Avenue Synagogue. He was a man of principals, devoted to his family, with a wonderful sense of humor. He was a generous man and had a very caring heart. His family and friends will miss him dearly. Services on Friday October 7th at 11 AM at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st and Amsterdam Avenue.
