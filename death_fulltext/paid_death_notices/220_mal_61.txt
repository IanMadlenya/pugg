
        BLAKE--Frederick H. Jr., on May 3, 2005. Husband of the late Lois Catherine Robinson. Previously deceased are his parents, Helen Avery and Frederick H. Blake. Also deceased are his sister, Avery B. Walker, and two brothers, Charles A. Blake and Dexter B. Blake. Surviving are four generations of nieces and nephews. Services will be private. Those who wish may contribute in his memory to the New Vernon First Aid Squad or the New Vernon Volunteer Fire Department, both located at PO Box 143 Village Road, New Vernon, New Jersey 07976.
