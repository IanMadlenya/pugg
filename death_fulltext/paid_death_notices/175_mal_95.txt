
        PIOMBINI-Monsignor Mario G. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Rosalia-Regina Pacis Church, Brooklyn, on August 8, 2001. Reposing at St. Rosalia, 14th Ave & 63rd St., Saturday, August 11th from 1-4 and 6-9PM and Sunday August 12th from 1-4PM. Mass of Transferral at Regina Pacis, 12th Av and 60th St at 7PM. Funeral Mass at Regina Pacis on Monday, August 13th at 10:30AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V.
