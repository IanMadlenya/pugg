
        BROOMER-Judge Alan. After a long illness, retired Judge Alan Broomer succumbed to cancer yesterday. His brilliant judicial career in Kings County Superior Court spanned over a decade. He began his legal career in the Manhattan District Attorney's office, rising to the position of Bureau Chief before his appointment to the bench by Mayor Koch. He is survived by a sister Doris Kaplan and a brother Brian Broomer, both of Long Island as well as loving in-laws and nieces and nephews. A great man has passedlet it be noted.
