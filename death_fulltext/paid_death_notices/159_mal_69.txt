
        CARPENTER-Kevin. February 18, 2000. Suddenly at Seatowers, Madeira Beach, FL. Beloved son of Elizabeth. Devoted husband to Valerie. Kevin was a linguist, a teacher & a writer, with two Master's from NYU where he was a Ford Fellow and a research assistant to the Director of the Center for International Studies 1969-72. A Federal civil rights investigator 1978-84. Sadly missed by all who knew him for his great intellect & warm loving personality, but most of all by his mother, wife, Aunt Pat, Uncle Ray, Aunt Gurli.
