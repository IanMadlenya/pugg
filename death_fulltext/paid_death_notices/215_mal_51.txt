
        CHEN -- David T.Y. Died peacefully at home on November 27, 2004. He was 84. Born in Shanghai, China, he was a longtime resident of Great Neck, NY. He received his BA at Chien Tung University in Shanghai and his MBA at Wharton School of Business, University of Pennsylvania, in 1951. He is survived by his wife, Evelyn H. Chen, sons, Stephen and Timothy, and daughter, Esther. A memorial service will be held December 11 at 10:30 AM at the Community Church of Great Neck, 2 Stoner Ave., Great Neck, NY. Flowers omitted. Memorial contributions may be made to the AHRC, 189 Wheatley Rd., Brookville, NY 11545.
