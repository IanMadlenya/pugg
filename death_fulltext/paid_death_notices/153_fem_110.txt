
        ERENBERG-Lillian. Beloved wife and life partner for 60 years of the late Benjamin. Devoted mother of the late Elissa Erenberg and Joel Erenberg. Loving grandmother of the late Marc Barron Erenberg, Robin Hilary and Michael Marc Erenberg. Dear sister of Betty Beatty, the late Morris and Harry and the late Bella, Renee & Annie. From her youth she championed causes to improve and protect the children of the world as well as being a tireless worker on behalf of Jewish health, welfare & secular. Service 10AM Wednesday at ''Wien & Wien'', 402 Park Street, Hackensack, New Jersey. Memorial contributions in memory of Lillian Erenberg may be directed to the Trauma Education and Injury Prevention Fund at Hackensack University Medical Center, Attention: John LoCurto, M.D., 30 Prospect Ave., Hackensack, N.J. 07601.
