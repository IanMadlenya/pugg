
        CONNORTON-Grace Guerin. Died peacefully at home at age 91 on November 24. Beloved wife of the late Dr. John V. Connorton. Loving mother of John V. Connorton, Jr., Mary Connorton FitzGerald, Margaret Connorton Reilly, the late Kevin J. Connorton, M.D., Jane Connorton Honig and Peter Connorton. She is also survived by 13 grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. Family will receive friends Tuesday, 5-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Funeral services Wednesday 9 A.M. at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 65 East 89th Street. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Regis High School, 55 East 84th Street, New York, NY 10028. Please note that the place of service has changed from Saint Vincent's to Saint Thomas More.
