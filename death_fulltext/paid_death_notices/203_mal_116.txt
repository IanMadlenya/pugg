
        GENNARELLI--Valentino J., 94, of Dallas, TX and Yorktown Heights, NY, died December 7, 2003. Born in Casalciprano, Italy, he immigrated to New York in 1929 and married Mary LeChase in 1935. They had two sons, Charles and Louis, respectively from Greenwich, CT and Dallas, TX. In 1948 Gennarelli, started a business in New York that designed and manufactured clerical robes and vestments for the Catholic Church. He would become one of the largest suppliers of garments in North America with a reputation for quality that extended to the Vatican. A mass in his honor was performed at St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic Church by his Eminence Bishop Edwin Broderick of New York.
