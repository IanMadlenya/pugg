
        ROMERO-Joseph James, age 86, March 16, 2000. Beloved husband of Louise. Loving father of Joseph, Lois, Fay Ellen, Mary, Gerard, Margaret, Dolores, Carolyn, Robert and Janet. Oldest brother of George, Ann, Veronica, Martha and of the late Winifred, Margaret and William. Beloved grandfather of 24 grandchildren. As a young man, Joseph helped his family survive the Great Depression and joined the Civilian Conservation Corp. in Idaho. During World War II, he worked in the Brooklyn Navy Yard as a welder and worked on the USS Missouri. He retired in 1988 after working 40 years for the ILA/CIO Union in various capacities. Visiting at Perry's Funeral Home, 118 Union Avenue, Lynbrook, NY, Saturday and Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass Monday, 10:30 AM, St. Raymond's, Atlantic Ave., East Rockaway. NY
