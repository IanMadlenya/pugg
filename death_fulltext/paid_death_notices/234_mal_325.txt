
        GIARDINO--Edward P., M.D. 90, died Peacefully at home with his children on July 22, 2006. He is survived by a brother, three daughters, two sons-in-law and three grandchildren. He will be missed by all who knew him and remembered always as a great man, a compassionate physician and a dear friend. A wake is to be held Sunday, July 30th, 2006 at Vernon C. Wagner's Funeral Home in Hicksville, New York. The interment is on Monday, July 31st, 2006 at Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, New York.
