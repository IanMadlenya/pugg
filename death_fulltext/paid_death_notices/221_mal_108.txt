
        SQUITIERI--Alan. Of Gainesville, Florida died Friday morning at his home. He was 92. Mr. Squitieri was born in New York City. He began his career as a violinist for the Columbia Broadcasting System. In 1976, he moved to Gainesville where he developed the Oaks Mall. His career in real estate spanned over 55 years. He was a generous contributor to innumerable charities, including the Center for Performing Arts, the University of Florida and the Children's Home Society. He was a parishioner of St. Patrick's Church. Survivors include his wife, Carol Squitieri, his sister Rose, his sons Dennis, Sandy, Rick and Chip and 11 grandchildren.
