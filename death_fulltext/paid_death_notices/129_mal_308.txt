
        GOLDBLATT-Leonard, 82. Died October 26 in Kingston, New York. A well-known teacher and lecturer on art, he was the originator of the lecture series ''Art on Rye'' at Nassau Community College. Surviving are his wife Evelyn and sons, Stephen, Paul and David. A memorial service will be held Tuesday 1 PM at the Dapson-Chestney Funeral Home, Rhinebeck, New York.
