
        WILLCUTS-Mildred G. Of New York City. After attending the University of Wisconsin, she worked for the National Labor Relations Board in Washington, D.C. and Minneapolis. In 1947, she came to New York and became legal secretary and office manager for Lawrence R. Condon and, more recently, for Milton Maurer. For many years she was an active member & Treasurer of the Pilot Club of New York. She is survived by her sister Katherine Chase, Sea Cliff, L.I., nieces Kathleen Starr, Middlebury. VT., and Nancy Rouisse, North Hero, VT., and their families. Memorial service Saturday, May 13th, 2PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, N.Y. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Pilot Club of New York , C/O Pamela Domestico, 110-11 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, New York 11375.
