
        CALISHER -- Helen Farrell. Passed away peacefully on October 10, 2005 after a long illness. The devoted and loving mother of Douglass, she is survived by him, her loving niece Marilyn Smith Church of Boston, Ma., and nephews Douglas E. Golde of NYC and Michael E. Smith of Charlotte, N.C. A private service will be held Monday, October 17, 2005 at the Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 352 E. 87 St., NYC 10128. Interment will be in Lutheran Cemetery. To commemorate the life of this wonderful woman, who shall remain forever in our hearts. Donations may be made to the Special Olympics, 1133 19th St. NW, Washington, DC 20036.
