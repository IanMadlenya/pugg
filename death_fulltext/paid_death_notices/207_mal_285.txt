
        SAMUELSON--Carl A. Of Delray Beach, FL and Loch Sheldrake, NY, passed away April 20, 2004. He was the owner of Stagedoor Manor Performing Arts Training Center founded in 1974 by him and his late wife Elsie. He leaves behind his devoted wife Camilla Samuelson, two children Debra Samuelson of NY, Cynthia Samuelson of Lexington, MA and son-inlaw, Jonathan Samen. His beloved grandchildren Joel, Hayley and Maggie Samen, two sisters Ruth Weisberg and Harriet Blank, brother-inlaw Chuck Blank, numerous loving nieces and nephews, devoted cousins and his special pride, the Stagedoor family and all of his friends. He lived a full life and touched everyone he knew and will be greatly missed by all. Visitation at Zion Funeral Home, 785 East Boston Post Rd., Mamaroneck, NY on Saturday, April 24, between the hours of 6 and 8 PM. Funeral services will be held Sunday, April 25, 2004, 10:00 AM at Community Synagogue in Rye NY. Contributions may be made to The Make - A - Wish Foundation. Condolence flowers may be sent to Zion Funeral Home.
