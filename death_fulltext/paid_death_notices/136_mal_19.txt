
        CHISOLM-Lawrence Washington. Died April 29 of cancer, at his home in Buffalo. Born February 7, 1929, New York City. Son of Ruth Anderton and William Garnett Chisolm. Graduated St. Mark's Academy and Princeton, class of 1950. Served as Lieutenant, U.S. Navy, 1950-53. Ph.D. 1957 Yale, where he taught until 1968. Established American Studies Department at the State University of New York at Buffalo, where he taught for 30 years. A warm and generous man and a gifted teacher, he is survived by his wife, Patricia Shelly; five daughters from a prior marriage to the late Elizabeth Richards Chisolm: Anne Huntington, Ruth Hamilton, Elizabeth Palmer, Susan Richards and Sarah Edings; and nine loving grandchildren.
