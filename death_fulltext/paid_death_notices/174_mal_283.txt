
        ROSENTHAL-E.M. The officers and Board of Trustees of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations profoundly mourn the death of a cherished friend and colleague. An honorary Vice Chairman, Manny served our sacred cause for many years, always with caring concern and dedication to the great ideals of our faith and our people. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his loving wife, Roslyn, his children and all his loved ones. Russell Silverman, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President
