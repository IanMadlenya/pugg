
        ABRAMSON--Lillian. 89, on March 27, 2004. Surrounded by her husband of 67 years, Dr. Abraham E. Abramson and her four children and their families. She is also survived by nine grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. A cultured woman of strong intellectual beliefs, she will be sorely missed for her joie de vivre. Services will be held on Monday, March 29, at 1 PM, at Jewish Memorial Chapel, 841 Allwood Road, Clifton, NJ. (jewishmemorialchapel.org) Donations may be made in her memory to Cantor's Music Fund, c/o Temple Ner Tamid, 936 Broad Street, Bloomfield, NJ 07003.
