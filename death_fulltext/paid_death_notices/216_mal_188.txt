
        BODDIGER--George C. The Board of Directors, Volunteers and Staff of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society express deep sorrow on the passing of George C. Boddiger who died January 10, at the age of 87. Mr Boddiger was a member of the Society's National Board of Directors from 1966 to 2001. He also served as a Trustee of the National Capital Chapter and as a member and President of the Multiple Sclerosis International Federation, which awarded him humanitarian and lifetime achievement awards. Mr. Boddiger was the former President and CEO of Equitable Life Insurance Company and retired in 1982 after 43 years in the life insurance industry. He is survived by his wife Wilma; daughters Nancy Estrada, Jean Johnstone and Kathryn Jones, and three grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made in his name to the NMSS National Capital Chapter, Washington, D.C. or the Multiple Sclerosis International Federation, London, England. We extend our sincere sympathy to his entire family. Edward A. Kangas, Chairman of the Board Joyce Nelson, President and CEO
