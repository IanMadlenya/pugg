
        MCNEILLY SR-Henry Branham. Of the Town of Newburgh and Southold, Long Island, who was the President of Micron Clean Uniform Service Incorporated of Newburgh, died Monday, March 2, 1998, at St. Lukes Hospital, Newburgh. He was 61. Survivors include his wife Barbara Ranges McNeilly at home, son Henry B. McNeilly, Jr. of Briarcliff Manor, two daughters, Caroline Boyar of Scarsdale, and Mary Gwynne McNeilly of Manhattan and one brother Custis Wright McNeilly of Newburgh. He is also survived by several nieces and nephews. Visitation will be from 2-4 and 7-9 p.m. at the Coloni Funeral Home, Rt. 9W, New Windsor, New York on Wednesday, March 4, 1998. A funeral mass will be celebrated at St. Patrick's Church, Grand Street, Newburgh on Thursday, March 5, 1998 at 10:00 A.M.
