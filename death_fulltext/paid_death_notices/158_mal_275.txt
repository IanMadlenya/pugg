
        ROCHE-Edward P. Born July 11, 1928 in the Bronx, NY. He grew up in Saint Margaret's Parish in Riverdale and graduated from Manhattan Prep. Mr. Roche served as a paratrooper with the 188th Parachute Infantry Regiment of the US Army, receiving an honorable discharge. Mr. Roche attended Manhattan College. He worked in the sales department of Lindenmeyer Paper Corporation for over forty years. Upon retirement in 1993, Mr. Roche moved from Scarsdale, NY to Spring Lake, NJ. He had been a summer resident of Spring Lake since 1970. Mr. Roche is survived by his wife Carol, sons Edward and Kevin, daughter-in-law Phyllis, two sisters, Patricia Cheetham and Helen Fries, brother James Roche, and numerous nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Spring Lake First Aid Squad. Visitation Thursday, 2-4 and 7-9 at Meehan Funeral Home, Spring Lake Heights, NJ. Funeral mass 11:00AM, Friday, St. Catharine's R.C. Church. Burial at St. Anne's Cemetery, Wall, New Jersey.
