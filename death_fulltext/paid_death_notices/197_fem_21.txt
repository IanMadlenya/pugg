
        MINICHIELLO--Sophie. Age 86, on May 31, 2003. Born in Manhattan. Graduated St. John's University School of Pharmacy in 1939. Resided in Sheepshead Bay. Beloved wife of the late Frank. Survived by her children Frances, Joseph, George, Marie, Ann and the late Rita, and their spouses, Linda, George and Michael, and seven grandchildren. Her brother Tony Mysak and cousin Basil Mysak.
