
        CITRIN-Philip. Age 86, of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Glen Cove, NY, on November 27, 1999. Excellent husband to his beloved wife, Evelyn. Devoted father to Barbara and Jack Gattuso, Ava and Michael Dvoretz, Niles and Nancy Citrin. Adored Pop Pop of Suzanne, Brian, Steven, Todd, Jodi, Elise and Mark.         CITRIN-Philip. Dearest Phil. To my beloved husband you will forever be in my heart. Until we meet again. Always and all ways. Your Evelyn
