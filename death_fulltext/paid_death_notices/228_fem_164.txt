
        KRAFT--Ruth (nee Jaffee). Age 94, died peacefully in Edison, NJ on Wednesday January 18, 2006. She was a long time resident of Washington Heights, NY. Beloved wife of the late Lionel Kraft, mother of Burton (Andrea), grandmother of Francine Esposito (James), Lisa Luttrell (John) and Michael Kraft, great-grandmother of AJ, Cori and Alexandria. Funeral Friday, January 20th at 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. The family will be sitting shiva at the Burton residence from Sunday thru Thursday from 12 pm to 9 pm.
