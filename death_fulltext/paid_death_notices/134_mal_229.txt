
        WILLIAMS-Craig. The Systems and Technology Department of The New York Times extends their deepest sympathy to Craig Williams' family on his passing. Always a cheerful friend and coworker who was optimistic and courageous, no matter how serious his ailments were. We think of Craig with tender memories and his parting leaves a void in our hearts. All of us in the Systems and Technology Department will miss his bright smile and enchanting laughter.
