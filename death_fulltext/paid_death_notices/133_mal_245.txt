
        LEBENS-Henry J. Age 84, of Ft. Lee, NJ and Pompano Beach, FL, passed away after a short debilitating illness. Beloved husband of Roslyn for 56 years. Loving father of Leslie Davies and Donna Epstein. Devoted father-in-law of Michael Davies and Dr. Mitchell Epstein. Cherished grandfather of Jessica Pell, Lauren and Matthew Epstein. He loved life and lived it to the fullest. We will miss our Henny and his incredible spirit.
