
        ADAMS--Eddie. On Thursday, October 21, family and friends will gather at The Cathedral Church of Saint John the Divine at 10 A.M. to remember and celebrate the life of Pulitzer Prize-winning photographer Eddie Adams, who died September 19 of Lou Gehrig's disease. During his career, Adams won more than 500 awards, including the Pulitzer Prize in 1969. He covered 13 wars and photographed seven U.S. presidents, 65 heads of state, and scores of top celebrities.
