
        DUNNINGTON-Walter G. Jr. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of the New York Presbyterian Hospital and the Joan and Sanford I. Weill Medical College and Graduate School of Medical Sciences of Cornell University are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and esteemed Trustee, Walter G. Dunnington. Following in a tradition of distinguished service set by his father, the late Walter Grey Dunnington, Sr., he served on the Board of the Hospital for over 37 years, lending his support and leadership to a wide variety of Board endeavors that have benefitted patient care, research and medical education. We will miss his wisdom and insight. Our deepest condolences are extended to the Dunnington family. New York Presbyterian Hospital Daniel B.
