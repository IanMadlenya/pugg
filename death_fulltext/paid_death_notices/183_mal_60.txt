
        TALMAGE-Robert V. Producer, director, choreographer, Robert Talmage passed away March 25, 2002 at the Cedars Sinai Medical Hospital in Los Angeles due to complications from his battle with cancer. We deeply mourn his passing and know that he is in a better place. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. A Memorial Service will be held for family & friends at 2201 Maravilla Drive, Los Angeles at 4 P.M., on April 13th. Please come join us celebrate his incredible life.
