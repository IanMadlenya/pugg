        LEWIS--Sherman R. With great sorrow, Lehman Brothers mourns the passing of our trusted and respected colleague and dear friend, of over 30 years, Sherman R. Lewis, Jr. Mr. Lewis held a number of senior positions at Lehman Brothers, including Vice Chairman and Director of the firm. A beloved friend and partner, he carried with him, in both his career and his life, the highest level of professionalism, loyalty, ethics, generosity, and, above all kindness. His keen intellect and sage counsel will always be remembered by his clients, colleagues, and friends. A remarkable man, he served as an inspiring teacher, mentor, humanitarian, and role model. He will be profoundly missed by all who had the privilege to have known him. We extend our deepest sympathy and prayers to his wife Dorothy, his four children, and two granddaughters. Richard S. Fuld, Jr., Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Joseph M. Gregory, Chief Operating Officer Bradley H. Jack, Chief Operating Officer
