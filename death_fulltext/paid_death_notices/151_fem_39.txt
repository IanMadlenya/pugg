
        RADTKE-Janel Marie. Born in Milwaukee, WI. July 8, 1952 passed away August 3, 1999 in Nyack, NY. Beloved daughter of Florence and the late Joseph, sister of Jeffrey & his wife Diane, and life partner of Sherri Eisenpress. Founder and partner in Radiant Communications, a public relations firm dedicated to helping non-profits and foundations communicate more effectively. Our Janel, a writer and teacher is best remembered for her love of nature, animals, the ocean and beauty. Her friends will continue to be inspired by her strong sense of spirituality. A memorial service will be held on Thursday at 11AM at the Hannenmann Funeral Home, Inc. of Nyack. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Nature Conservancy, Girls Incorporated, or The Humane Society.
