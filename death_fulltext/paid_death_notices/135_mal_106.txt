
        BENIVEGNA-Dr. Salvatore L. On April 9, 1998 after a long illness. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, brother, uncle, friend. He grew up and lived most of his life in Bklyn, NY where he practiced medicine as a general practitioner and surgeon. During World War II he served in the army medical corps at front line field hospitals in the Pacific. His intelligence, compassion and generosity to his family, friends and patients, inspired their loyalty, love and respect. A funeral mass will be held at St. Peter's Church, Danbury, CT, April 14, 1998 at 10:30AM. Contact Green Funeral Home 203-748-2131 for more information.
