
        POPPER-Robert L. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of the Federation of Protestant Welfare Agencies, Inc. mourn the loss of Robert L. Popper, former Board member and cherished friend. Mr. Popper served on our Board for 24 years, and was an avid supporter of our efforts to assist disenfranchised groups, particularly the elderly. Our profound condolences go to his wife of 30 years, Ellen Goldberg Popper, and his many nieces and nephews. Rev. Dr. Herbert Anderson, Board Chair Megan E. McLaughlin, Executive Director/CEO
