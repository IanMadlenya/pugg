
        WASSERMAN-Milton. 71, after a courageous fight against cancer. Founder and President of MERJ Insurance Brokerage. Adored husband and life partner of Rhoda (Rosner) for forty-eight years. Loving father of Susan (Sanders), Jeffrey (Wasserman) and Debi (Snyder). Cherished grandfather of Adam, Ashley, Emmanuel, Jessica, Stephen and Jasmine. Devoted brother of Stanley and his late sister Sylvia (Singman). His caring and generous presence will be forever missed by his family and friends. His heroic ability to conquer the everyday battles of life serve as an example of ''menschlichkeit'' for all of us. Funeral services Sunday, 10:30 AM, Parkside Funeral Chapel, Queens Blvd. 718-896-9000. Interment to follow.
        WASSERMAN-Milton. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Sisterhood mourn the passing of the beloved father of their devoted member Susan Sanders and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Fred Distenfeld, President
