
        PIERCE-C. Eliot, 84. Of Northeast Harbor, Maine. Died on Saturday, June 12th. in Bar Harbor, Maine. He is survived by his beloved wife of 58 years, Dora Redway Pierce of Northeast Harbor and by his son Charles E. Pierce Jr. of Manhattan and Salt Point, NY, his daughter Edith P. Murphy of Rockland, ME., his daughter Joan P. Uraneck of South Portland, ME, his son Benjamin R. Pierce of Devon, PA. and seven grandchildren. A private family service will be held in Northeast Harbor on Saturday, June 19th. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Maine Community Foundation/ Watershed Project, 245 East Main Street, Ellsworth, ME. 04605.
