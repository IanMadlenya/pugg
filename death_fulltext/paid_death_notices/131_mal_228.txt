
        LOFRESE-Raymond. The Officers, Executive Board, and Members of The Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York, Inc, mourns the passing of our member Raymond LoFrese, of Olympic Plumbing and Heating Corp. Mr. LoFrese, over the years, made a valuable contribution to the plumbing industry. Our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Pauline, and all his bereaved family. Association of Contracting Plumbers of The City of New York Ralph J. DeMartino, President John O'Donnell, Exec Vice President
