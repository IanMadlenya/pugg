
        SILLS-Ruth Fife. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ruth Fife Sills. Our sympathies are extended to her husband, Nathaniel L. Sills, and her children, Lawrence and Susan, Arthur and Sarah, Peter and Susan, and to her brother, Bernard Fife. The Sills and Fife families have always been heartfelt supporters of UJA-Federation. We offer our deepest condolences to the entire family and all those whose lives she has enriched with her presence. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec.
