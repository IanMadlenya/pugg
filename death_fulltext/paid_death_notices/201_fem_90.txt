
        TECOTZKY--Sylvia. Beloved wife of Mel. Devoted mother of Nan and Mark. Loving grandmother of Ethan, Julia, Sam, Henry and Alice. Dear sister of Dorothy Altschuler and Edy Comis. She exuded sweetness and fortitude, goodness and dignity. She withheld judgement, coveted little, shared what she had, and loved her family deeply and dearly. She is greatly missed. Services 11:15 AM, Sunday at ''the Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        TECOTZKY -- Sylvia. The community of West End Synagogue mourns the loss of this elegant, though diminutive, gracious lady who often suprised us with a sly wink of humor. We will miss her.
