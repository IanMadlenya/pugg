
        BROWN - Maurice. The Board and staff of the Greater New York Chapter, Crohn's & Colitis Foundation of America acknowledges with profound sadness the passing of Maurice Brown, beloved father-in-law of our esteemed president Nancy Brown, father of Howard Brown and grandfather of Board member Michele Sweetwood. We extend our deepest sympathies to the Brown and Sweetwood families and hope that they derive comfort in knowing that they are in our hearts and prayers. Judy Saphir, Chairman Suzanne and Irwin Rosenthal, CCFA Co-Founders Shelby and William Modell, CCFA Co-Founders Joan Telley, Executive Director
