
        SHINE--Warren S., of Putnam Valley, NY, died peacefully on February 18, 2004. Survived by his daughter, Elizabeth Shine McDevitt; grandson, Liam Warren McDevitt; companion, Connie Gruber; sister, Roberta Kunin, of Madison, WI, and nieces and nephews. Services private. In lieu of flowers, donations to Memorial SloanKettering would be appreciated. He served as a lieutenant in the U.S. Navy during the Korean War aboard USS Des Moines; he retired as a senior tax partner from Ernst & Young in the late 1980's. He was a board member of many charitable organizations and foundations, including President of Stanley M. Isaacs Neighborhood Center. He was a kind and loving man of many interests.
        SHINE--Warren S. The David Dibner Family and the Dibner Fund are deeply saddened by the death of Warren Shine. He was a friend of many decades, a wise counselor and he will be sorely missed. Our profound condolences to his loved ones. David Dibner, President The Dibner Fund Board
