
        BREE--Warren H. 74, of West Palm Beach, FL, beloved husband of Violet Bree, died on August 16, 2005. Mr. Bree was the Chief Investment Officer and a Senior Portfolio Manager of WHB/Wolverine Asset Management, Inc., a registered investment advisory firm that he founded in New York City in 1990. He is also survived by sisters, Corinne Levinsohn of Tenafly, NJ, and Barbara Bree Shaab of Chesterbrook, PA, a brother Arthur Bree of San Francisco, CA, two daughters, Nancy Bree Babyak of Weston, CT, Jill Bree of New Haven, CT, a son Adam Bree of New York City; a stepson Roger Corneretto of Alexandria, VA, two grandchildren; and several nieces and nephews. Services August 18, 2005 at 2:00 PM from The Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Road Fairfield, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to Pancreatic Cancer Action Network, Inc. (PanCan), 2141 Rosecrans Avenue, Suite 7000, El Segundo, CA 90245.
