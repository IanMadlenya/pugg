
        JONAS--Florence. Born February 25, 1908, died August 30, 2003. Daughter of Jacob and Leah Kyzor. Married to Harold Jonas (deceased) 1933-1946. Loving mother of Steven and grandmother of Jacob and Lillian, all of Port Jefferson, NY. Active all of her life in the arts and politics, translator of the first biography of Prokofiev and several other important books on Russian music, and lifelong fighter for progressive political causes in our country.
