
        WARREN-Ilse. Beloved wife, mother and grandmother, Ilse died peacefully on September 28, 2000 after a courageous battle with cancer. She leaves her devoted son and daughter-in-law, Steven and Judith Katten, her cherished grandson, Daniel, and her dear sister and best friend, Irene (Reni) Rendsburg. A woman of grace and beauty, Ilse will be missed by all who knew her. Services at ''The Riverside,'' on Monday, October 2nd, at 12:45 P.M.
