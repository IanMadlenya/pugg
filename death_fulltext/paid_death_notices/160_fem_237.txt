
        KENEALLY-Catherine E., 76, of Moriches, N.Y. on Sunday, May 28 in her home. She is survived by her beloved husband of 56 years, Dr. Francis J. Keneally, her devoted daughters, Betty Anne Conneally, Kathy Leber and Karen Devine, her devoted sons, Dr. Francis J. Jr ''Ken'', Timothy, Michael, Gerard and Joseph. Mrs. Keneally is also survived by her sisters, June Meyer and Pat Fisher, her 25 grandchildren and one great grandchild, her daughters-inlaw, Pat Keneally, Mary Ann Keneally, Sheila Keneally, Patricia Keneally and Mary Keneally and her sons-in-law, Bill Leber and Christopher Conneally. A Liturgy of Christian burial will be offered on Thursday, June 1, at 10:00 AM at St. Lawrence Roman Catholic Church, Main St., Chester N.J. The visitation will be on Tuesday, May 30 from 7-9 PM and again on Wednesday, May 31 from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the William J. Leber Funeral Home, 15 Furnace Rd, Chester, N.J. 07930, 908-879-3090. The interment will take place at Calvary Cemetery in Woodside, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Holy Rosary Roman Catholic Church, 41 Windermere Ave, Greenwood Lake, N.Y. 10925.
