
        DALL-Mark Healey. Of Leeds Mass and Sedona Ariz. Died December 28, 2000 in his 86th year. Husband of Pauline Kingsland Dall, father of Hester, Whitney, Eliza and Sara. Also survived by nine grandchildren. Services private. Interment Mount Auburn Cemetery, Brookline Mass. in June. Memorial donations may be made to the Alaska Natury Conservancy, 421 W. 1st St, Anchorage, Ak. 49501.
