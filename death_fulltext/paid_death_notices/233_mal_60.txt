
        CASSON--Phillip. The Officers, Committees and staff of the Pawling Mountain Club , Pawling, N.Y. mourns the loss of one of its founding members Dr. Phillip Casson an avid sportsman and dear friend. Sympathies to his wife Rosemary, daughters Nicole, Victoria and Lindsy. He will be truly missed but never forgotten. Dr. Philip C. Bonanno John M. B. O'Connor.
