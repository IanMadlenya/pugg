
        LEWIS -- Estelle (Gottlieb). Died August 3, 2004. Born in Brooklyn in 1905, in her late teens and early twenties, she performed in Broadway musicals. She married the late Joseph Lewis, residing in Philadelphia until her passing at age 99. She had one child, the late Bernard (Buddy). She leaves behind her daughterin-law, Miriam (Mickie), four grandchildren, and six great grandchildren, sisters, Jessie and Shirley, brother, Murray, and brother-in-law, Danny. Her devotion to family was unparalleled. Shirley and Danny Rubin Barbara Miller and family
