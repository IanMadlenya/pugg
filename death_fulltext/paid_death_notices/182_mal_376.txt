
        CUTLER-E. Newton Jr. Of Bryn Mawr, PA. and Edgartown, MA. On March 24th, 2002. Age 86. He graduated from St. Paul's SchoolConcord, N.H. in 1933 and from Princeton University in 1937. He served in W.W. II as a Lieutenant in the United States Marine Corps. He spent over 35 years with Citibank in New York and left as Sr. Vice President to become Chairman of Horizon Bancorp, Morristown, N.J. until his retirement in 1979. In addition to his wife of 64 years, Beverly, he is survived by his two sons Earle N. Cutler III & Michael W. Cutler, 2 daughters, Carolyn C. Goodman and Allison C. Fox, 11 grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM on April 2 at St. David's Episcopal Church-Chapel, 763 Valley Forge Road, Wayne, PA. and a similar service will be held this summer in Edgartown, MA. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made in his memory to St. Paul's School, 325 Pleasant St, Concord, N.H. 03301 or the Vineyard Sailing Foundation, PO Box 319, Vineyard Haven, MA. 02568. Stuard Funeral Home, Ardmore, PA.
