
        STROSS--R. Marshall. Retired director of news and information for the Lutheran Church in America, died on March 18, 2006, at the age of 88, at home in Manhasset, NY. Survived by his wife, Suzanne, three sons, four grandchildren and one great-grandchild. He was predeceased by his first wife Christine. Funeral services 11 am, Tuesday, March 21, at Christ Lutheran Church, 47-17 Glenwood St, Little Neck, NY 11362. Visiting 2-5 and 7-9, Monday, March 20, at Fairchild Sons, 1570 Northern Blvd, Manhasset. Donations to Wabash College-Alumni Office, POB 352, Crawfordsville, IN 47933-0352.
