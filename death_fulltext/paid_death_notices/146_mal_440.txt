
        CAMERON-William B. On March 22, age 49 in Southampton, Long Island, of lung cancer. Loving son of Jane B. Cameron and devoted brother of Laurie C. Carson. Friends may call on Thursday, March 25, 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM at the J.M. O'Connell Funeral Home, 30 Little Plains Road, Southampton. A funeral service will be held on Friday, March 26 at 1 PM, St. John's Episcopal Church, Southampton. His battle against his disease was a source of inspiration to all who knew him. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Society of Memorial SloanKettering at 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021 or East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978-7048.
