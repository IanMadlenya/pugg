
        SCHNELL-Eugene Abraham. A resident of Longboat Key, FL., formerly of New York City and Tyringham, MA, died March 8 in Sarasota. He was born May 2, 1922 in New York City. Survivors include his wife Ina Spelke, and three children who reside in New York City, a son Eric, and two daughters Vicki S. Rosen and Nancy from a previous marriage; a sister Millicent Golding of Sun City, AZ; a brother Seymour Schnell, & five nieces & nephews, all of New York City. Services in Sarasota were private. Memorial donations may be made to the Robert S. Peabody Museum of Archaeology, Phillips Academy, Andover, MA 01810, or to the Florida West Coast Symphony, 709 North Tamiami Trail, Sarasota, FL 34236.
