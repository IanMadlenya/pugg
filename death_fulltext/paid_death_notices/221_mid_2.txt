        FORSTER--May. The Officers, National Commission and staff of the Anti-Defamation League mourn the passing of May Forster, beloved wife of our colleague and friend, Arnold. May was Arnold's life partner, by his side throughout his ADL career of more than six decades, as he led the fight against anti-semitism, for civil rights and for security for the State of Israel and the Jewish people. May was a member of the ADL family and we will miss her bright smile, good humor and intelligence. We extend our deepest condolences to Arnold, their daughter Janie and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser National Chair Abraham H. Foxman National Director
