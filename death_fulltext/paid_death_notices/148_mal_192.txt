
        SHANLEY - Brother Matthew (John), F.S.C.. Age 74, of Lincroft, New Jersey, died on Saturday, the feast day of St. John Baptist De La Salle, founder of the Brothers of the Christian Schools, at De La Salle Hall, Lincroft. His teaching assignments included St. Cecilia's, Sacred Heart, St. Jerome's and Holy Name Schools, St. Peter's, Cardinal Spellman and St. Raymond's High Schools. He was a dedicated & knowledgeable devotee of the tenor John McCormick. Even after suffering a severe stroke at the end of 1981, which effected his memory, he managed to retain much information about Mr. McCormick. Through friends and associates he managed to continue his interest in the tenor and impart some of his knowledge about the man. The wake will be held at De La Salle Hall, Lincroft on Tuesday from 7-9 P.M. and Wednesday 2-4 and 6:30 to 7:30 P.M. The Funeral Liturgy will be celebrated on Wednesday at 7:30 P.M. at De La Salle Hall. The interment will be held at St. Gabriel's Cemetery, Marlboro, on Thursday leaving from De La Salle Hall at 10:00 A.M. Memorial donations to De La Salle Nursing Development, 810 Newman Springs Rd, Lincroft, N.J. 07738 would be appreciated.
