
        SCHLANG-Edwin A. Died May 25, 2001. Services will be held on Tuesday, May 29, at 10 AM, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Boulevard (76th Road), Forest Hills, NY, followed by burial at Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, LI. Ed graduated from MIT at age 21 with a B.S. in Chemical Engineering. He eventually went on to become the president of his own plastics extrusion business, Vanguard Extruders Inc, which he successfully ran for over 25 years. He enjoyed life to the fullest and was an avid golfer, tennis player, horseback rider and scuba diver. He is survived by children Barbara, Carin and Jordan, and the love of his life, wife Andrea (Randee).
