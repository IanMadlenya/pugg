
        BAIR-Elizabeth Morris. March 27, after a long illness. Born July 3, 1911 in Kuling, China. Beloved widow of George Marjoribanks and John C. Bair. She leaves a brother, DuBois Morris of Weston, CT. and a sister Eleanor Morrison of Edinburgh, Scotland. She was also a sister of the late John McLean Morris of New Haven Ct. Member of the Brick Presbyterian Church, the Lotos Club and the Junior League. Board member of the Keimei Foundation. She leaves 11 devoted nieces and nephews. Services at the Brick Presbyterian Church, Park Ave at 91 St, Friday, April 14, 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Brick Church Memorial Fund.
