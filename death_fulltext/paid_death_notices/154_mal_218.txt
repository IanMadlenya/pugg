
        KOEVARY-Theodore., peacefully on November 16. Loving husband of Lilla. Devoted father of Richard and the late George. Adored father-in-law, grandfather, brother and uncle. A pillar of strength, he dazzled us with his intellect, amused us with his stories, and inspired us with his piety and kindness. Contributions in his memory to The Elmhurst Jewish Center, 37-53 90 St, Jackson Hts, NY 11372
