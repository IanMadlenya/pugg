
        SHAVIRO--Sol. The Trustees and staff of The Metropolitan Museum of Art mourn the loss of Sol Shaviro who was a dedicated member and participant in this institution formore than 30 years. He was elected a Trustee representing the Borough of the Bronx in 1972, and a Trustee Emeritus in 1981, and for many years served on committees to advise such areas as education and community relations. He cared passionately about the Museum and the vistors who come to experience it, and his lively acuman will be greatly missed. We extend our condolences to his wife Frieda and to the entire family. James R. Houghton, Chairman Philippe de Montebello, Director Emily K. Rafferty, President
