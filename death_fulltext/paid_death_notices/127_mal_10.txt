
        TOMCHIN-Mark L. Died July 25 at his home in New York City. He was 56. The cause was Lou Gehrig's disease. During his 30-year career as an executive recruiter, he developed the industry's first specialized practice in the hightechnology arena. Since 1982 he was president of his own firm, Mark L. Tomchin & Associates, Inc. He is survived by his loving wife and companion of 19 years, Barbara Anderson-Tomchin. Memorial service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, NYC, Monday, Aug. 4, at 6PM. Memorial gifts, marked ''for research only'' to The ALS Association, 21021 Ventura Blvd., Suite 321, Woodland Hills, CA 91364.
