
        COMMERFORD-Sr. Rose Mary (formerly Sister Mary Raymond). Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity. Social worker, administrator, teacher, and former counselor for the Sisters of Charity. On November 8, 1997. Survived by cousins. Reposing at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark St., Yonkers, NY, on Sunday, 4-8 PM, and Monday 1-8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10:30 AM, at St. Mary's Church. Interment at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Ave., Bronx, NY 10471.
        COMMERFORD-Sister Rose Mary. The New York Foundling Board of Trustees and the Foundling community express their sorrow at the passing of Sister Rose Mary Commerford. Her longtime commitment and dedication to the Foundling's mission to children and families have been an inspiration to all. She will be missed. We extend our sympathy to the Sisters of Charity, her cousins, and her many friends. Sister Carol Barnes, Chair, Board of Trustees Michael Garber Executive Director
