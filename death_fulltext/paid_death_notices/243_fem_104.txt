
        GRAND--Bessie, 95, died surrounded by loved ones at home in Manhattan on Saturday, April 14th. ''Bubbie'', as she was known, was adored and revered by her family, friends and caregivers. A painter, baker and world traveler, Bubbie will be remembered always for her wisdom, humor, impeccability and strength. A service in her memory will be held today at 1 pm, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, NY, NY.
