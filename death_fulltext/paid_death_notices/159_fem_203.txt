
        KISSANE-Lucille Paolillo. April 21, 2000, at age 48. Vice President of St. Francis Hospital, Roslyn, NY. Beloved wife of Robert Kissane & mother of Evan Danaher. Loving daughter of Ciro and the late Lucille Paolillo and sister of Robert and Gary Paolillo. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81 St, Tuesday, 25 & 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave, at 84 St, Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the St. Francis Hospital Foundation, Development Office, 100 Port Washington Blvd, Roslyn, N.Y. 11576, or Women At Risk, Breast Surgery Service, Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, 161 Fort Washington Ave., Suite 1011, New York, New York 10032.
        KISSANE-Lucille. You brought us together and will forever be the heart of our circle of kindred spirits. The memories of our dinners, our visits to GK, your laughter and your stories, the way a call from you could brighten any day, will be with us always. We will try to live life with a passion, in tribute to our cherished friend. Caryn, Debbie, Kathy and Martha
        KISSANE-Lucille. The Advisory Council of Women At Risk is deeply saddened by the passing of one of its founding members. She will always be remembered for her vision and dedication to the success of the WAR Program. The WAR Advisory Council
