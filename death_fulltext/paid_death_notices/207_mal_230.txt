
        RYAN--Honorable John J. 92, formerly of Brooklyn on April 16, 2004. He served as a Judge of the New York Criminal Court, New York State Court of Claims and the New York Supreme Court. Beloved husband of the late Eileen (Keegan) and the late Capitola (Murphy); father of Bob, John P., and Mary Beth; father-in-law of Christine and Mary Ryan and Henry Sholar; grandfather of Tim, Elizabeth, Brendan, Rob, Jason, Sarah and the late Pierce J. Mass 10 a.m. Monday at Holy Trinity Church, Westfield. Burial in Fairview Cemetery.
