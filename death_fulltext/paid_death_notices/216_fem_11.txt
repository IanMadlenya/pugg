
        LAMPRECHT--Judith Heltman. Of Delmar, NY. On December 24, 2004, at age 86. Judy used her dramatic talents and experience from Syracuse University to direct community theater throughout her life. Judy is survived by her husband, Joseph Lamprecht Jr.; a sister, Rebecca Heltman Griffiths; her son, Jeremy Lamprecht; her daughter, Cynthia Lamprecht Wetzell; six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in her name to your charity of choice.
