
        DANSKER-Shirley. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted, longtime member, Shirley Dansker, widow of Raphael Dansker. Shirley was an active member in our temple community and was also a past President of Sisterhood. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her daughter, Leslie Toepfer, and her sons, Steven and Bruce, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
