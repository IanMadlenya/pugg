
        REED-Dr. Frances C., Ed.D. Leaves behind her beloved husband Lionel and her four sons, Drs. Harold, Lawrence, Irving and Barry Reed. Daughter-in-laws Arlene and Jill, sisters Anna Yager and Pearl Brod. Grandchildren Staci, Michael, Joshua, Dahlia and Ilana. She devoted her life to the education of slow learners and was instrumental in the development of tests used to diagnose their problems. She will be missed. A graveside service will be held Sunday, March 1 in Miami, FL. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the National Center for Learning Disabilities Inc., 381 Park Ave. South, New York City 10016.
