
        WILSON--John F., 84, of NYC died of lung cancer on October 8, 2006. He was a graduate of Horace Mann School and Brown University, and was a United States Navy Officer in the Pacific during World War II. Following the war, he was in the Insurance business and was a member of The Million Dollar Round Table. In the 1960's he served as chairman of the Brown University Alumni Admissions Committee in New York City. Known to his friends a ''Cliff'', he was a man of integrity, conviction, and high principles who believed deeply in the spirit as well as the letter of the law. Devoted brother, loving son, loyal friend, he leaves his sister Edith W. Wolf and brother-in-law Richard of Coral Gables, FL, and longtime friends for whose love and support his family is profoundly grateful. Services Thursday at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave at 11:30 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to a charity of your choice.
        $(6$)WILSON--John F. Temple Shaaray Tefila records with profound sorrow the death of our cherished member. Heartfelt condolences are extended to his sister, Edith, and her loved ones. May his memory be for blessing. Rabbi Jonathan A. Stein Lois S. Nyren, President
