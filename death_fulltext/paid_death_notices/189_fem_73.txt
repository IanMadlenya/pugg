
        BATES - Victoria Freling huysen. Of Brielle, NJ, died at her residence on Wednesday, October 9, 2002. Widow of John Glenville Bates Jr. Mother of Emily Bates Douglas of Stanford, CT, Anita B. Glasson of Pine Beach, NJ, Joseph S.F. Bates of Parkdale, OR, and the late John G. Bates III. Sister of Joseph S. Frelinghuysen Jr. of Far Hills, NJ, and Emily B. McFarland of Basking Ridge, NJ. Grandmother of the late Boulton Frelinghuysen Bates, also survived by 11 grandchildren and 14 great-grandchildren. The funeral service will be held on Tuesday, October 15, 2002, at 11 A.M. in St. Bernard's Episcopal Church, 88 Claremont Rd, Bernardsville, NJ. Interment in St. Bernard's Cemetery. Relatives and friends may call at the Layton Funeral Home, 475 Main St, Bedminster, NJ, on Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Memorial gifts may be made to the charity of one's choice.
