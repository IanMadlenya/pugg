
        SILHA-Otto. City Innovation mourns the death of its Founder and Chairman, on September 11, in Minneapolis, MN. He had the vision, enthusiasm and determination of a true leader. He cared about the futures of people in our cities: their education, work, daily lives and opportunities for growth. The newspaper industry honored him with many leadership posts. Numerous philanthropic groups benefited from his energy and dedication. All of us in City Innovation have lost one of our most cherished friends. Alfred Eisenpreis, Vice Chairman Donna Anderson, President City Innovation
