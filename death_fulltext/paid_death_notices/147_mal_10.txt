
        FRANK-Herbert P. On March 31, 1999 at the age of 89. Beloved husband of the late Gwen, loving father of Ellen and Lee. Cherished grandfather of Lisa, Wendy, John, Stephen and Julie and great grandfather of Carly. President of Washington Beef Company, Inc. since 1940, founded by his father in 1904. Funeral service at Temple Shaaray Tefila, 79th Street and 2nd Ave on Friday, April 2nd, 11:30am.
        FRANK-Herbert. Temple Shaaray Tefila records with profound sorrow the passing of our longtime member. Our heartfelt condolences to all his family and loved ones. Rabbi Harvey M. Tattelbaum Dr. Ellen R. Saxl, President
