
        MILLER-Michael G. Left us in his 91st year. Survived by his wife Sophie, devoted son-in-law Allan Turits, grandchildren Mark, his wife Kathy, and Andrew; great grandchildren Jack and Jesse, and brothers Rubin and Seymour. He will now join his recently deceased daughter Joan, his deceased wife Helen, his parents Yetta and Joseph, his sister Fannie Friedman and his brother Abraham. Mike became a CPA in 1932 and was retired from the firm of Hoberman-Miller & Co., CPA's, NYC. Rest in peace. Funeral service Sunday, June 14, 10:30 AM at ''Gutterman - Musicant'' corner Passaic and Park Sts., Hackensack, NJ.
