
        WEISHAR-Jessie. In her 90th year. Loving and devoted wife of the late Irving L. Loving and spirited mother to Joseph, Rochelle Krieg (deceased), Maxine and Robert Sokoloff. Adoring grandmother to Margo and Peter; Harold and Steven; Sharon, Diann, and Steven. Proud great grandmother to eight children. Dear sister to Louis, Isadore (deceased), Jack, Fay, and Esta. Her generous, enabling life has given joyful inspirational memories to all whom she has touched. She kept her counsel, stayed true to her loved ones, and was a model who taught us all how to love. She will be sorely missed. Service Thurs. March 23, 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St & Amsterdam Ave.
