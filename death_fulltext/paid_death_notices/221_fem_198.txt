
        GLADSTONE -- Dorothy Bruckner. Age 82. Passed on peacefully June 27th, 2005. Loving mother of Lynn Weisman and the late Andrew Gladstone. Mother-in-law of Leonard and Marta. Devoted Grandmother to Samantha, Jacob, Mateo, Violeta, Vicki, Mark, Laurie and Roz. Great Dorothy to Charlotte, Natalie, and Niko. Caring cousin, discerning friend, predeceased by her husband, Matthew, in 1963. A lover of family, fauna and flora. Teacher, pursuer of knowledge both Jewish and secular.
