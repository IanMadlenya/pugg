
        LEVINE-Florence (nee Levitas). In her 91st year. Devoted wife of the late Charles Levine, daughter of the late Gerson and Yetta Levitas, beloved mother of Abraham (Toddie) and Trudy, Benita and Murray Gross, Leon and Sybil and Jack and Frances. Adored grandmother of fourteen and great grandmother of fifteen. Hunter College Class of 1928. A woman of great dignity, inordinate courage and uncompromising integrity, who inspired all who knew her. She will be forever in our hearts. Donations to AMIT Women would be appreciated.
