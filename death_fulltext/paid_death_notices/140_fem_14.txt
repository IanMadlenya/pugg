
        WALLACE-Gertrude de Lancey. Mother of Peter de L. and Helen W. Wallace, grandmother of Campbell Lloyd-, Peter Woodbridge, and Cornelia S. Wallace, died at home on September 1. Funeral service will be at St. Bartholomew's chapel, 50th Street & Park Avenue Friday, September 4, at 10:30 AM. Donations in her memory may be made to the Animal Medical Center, 510 East 62nd Street, New York, N.Y. 10021.
