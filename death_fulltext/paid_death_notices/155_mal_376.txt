
        TERRY-Robert Lee. Born June 20, 1919 in Princeton, N.J. and was brought up in Princeton. He attended Groton School & Princeton University. He had homes in both Princeton, N.J. and Palm Beach, FL. During his fifty year tenure with Florida Public Utilities, he served as Chairman of the Board, CEO and, most recently, Chairman of the Executive Committee. He is a member of the Society of the Four Arts, the Bath and Tennis and Everglades Clubs in Palm Beach and the Bedensbrook Club in Princeton. Widowed by his wife of 50 years, Ellen McHenry Bruce, he is survived by his children Ellen Terry Benoit, Robert Lee Terry, Jr., Prescott leBreton Terry and grandchildren Turner Alexander and Whitmore McHenry Benoit, Heather Bruce, Jesse Lee, Prescott leBreton, Jr. and Andrew Herpel Terry. Contributions in lieu of flowers to either the Cancer Institute at Good Samaritan Medical Center, 1309 North Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, Florida 33401 or Bethesda-by-the-Sea Church, 141 South County Road, Palm Beach, Florida 33480.
