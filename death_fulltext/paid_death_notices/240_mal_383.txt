
        AUGUSTINE--Kermit. The Board of Directors, participants, staff, and friends of AHRC NYC sadly express their sorrow at the passing of Mr. Kermit Augustine, who for 16 years served on the Board of Directors of the Association. In addition, Mr. Augustine served as the Chairperson of our Brooklyn Borough Committee for many years, acting as a liaison for that community, as well as on numerous other committees. The funeral will be held at Our Lady of Victory Church at 583 Throop Avenue, Brooklyn, New York, on Thursday, February 1 at 10:00am. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. We will miss his gentle, supportive presence. Laura Kennedy, President. Michael Goldfarb, Executive Director.
