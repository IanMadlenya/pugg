
        DROOZ-Richard, M.D. Passed on Friday, January 24th. Survived by his beloved wife, Miriam. Dr. Drooz, psychoanalyst and psychiatrist, will be remembered for his brilliance as an academician, clinician, teacher, mentor, and for his kind, generous spirit. He will be greatly missed. Neither he nor his words of wisdom will ever be forgotten. Marla F. Wald, M.D. Chevy Chase, Md.
