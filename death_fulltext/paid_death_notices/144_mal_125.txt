
        MANHEIM-Paul E.. The Board of Trustees and the Staff of the Brooklyn Museum of Art record with deep sorrow, the passing of collector and longtime friend of the BMA, Paul E. Manheim, and extend condolences to his wife Simone and to his children Anthony Manheim, Martha Green and Emily Goldman. Paul Manheim served as a Trustee from 1969 until 1982 when he joined the Advisory Board where he served until his death. Robert S. Rubin, Chairman
