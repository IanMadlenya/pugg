
        JOSEPH-Irving, 75, noted pianist, conductor and musical director, died suddenly on Tuesday, March 28, after a brief illness. Funeral services will be held Friday, March 31, at noon, at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. He is survived by his beloved wife, Phyllis Dolgin, his brother Dr. Samuel Joseph, and numerous loving nieces, nephews, friends and relatives.
