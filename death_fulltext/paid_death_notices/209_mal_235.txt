
        DORF--Charles B. June 21, 2004. He will be missed by his many friends and relatives in the New York and Palm City, FL areas. Mr. Dorf was the devoted husband of Marilou, the caring father of Robert and James and daughter-inlaw Jodie. He shared with his family his love of travel, the arts, sports and New York City. Mr. Dorf had a long and distinguished career as a customs broker and freight forwarder, but particularly enjoyed his participation and accomplishments with eleemosynary institutions. A private service will be held at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY. Donations in lieu of flowers can be sent in his name to Colgate University, Development Office, 13 Oak Drive, Hamilton, NY 13346.
