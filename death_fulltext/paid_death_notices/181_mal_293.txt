
        LITTRELL-James Franklin, 74. Of Quogue, NY, formerly of Kansas City, MO, died February 19, 2002 after a long illness. Jim is survived by his devoted wife of 29 years, Mary Lou; loving sister Patty and her two children Cynthia and Preston; four loving children Cindy, Jeff, Vicki, and Jill; one grandson, four granddaughters, and one great-granddaughter. Jim will be sorely missed by all who knew him. The family requests that contributions be made in Jim's memory to Alzheimer's Association.
