
        GORDON--George, age 91, on July 9, 2005. Husband of the late Elsie Wexler Gordon. Father of Judith and Henry. Grandfather of Jason and Evan Brown, Lily, Paul and Maya Gordon. Graduate of Columbia Law School, he had a 45-year career with West Publishing Co. as an editor of Corpus Juris Secundum. Longtime resident of Roslyn, NY, he had vacation homes in Mahopac, NY and Delray Beach, FL. He loved reading, walking and tennis.
