
        HARBUS--Sarah. On July 2, 2005. Loving wife of Meyer, mother of Richard, Lorna, and Fredric, grandmother and great-grandmother. Sarah was was a wonderful homemaker, and a lover of music, art, theater, dance, books and travel; she joined her husband in the fight for social justice. She was warm and kind always and had many friends. Sarah is now with her dearest Meyer forever. We will miss her and Dad very much. Services Wednesday, 11:30 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
