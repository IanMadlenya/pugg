
        SAVITSKY--Elias, M.D. The Board of Trustees, management and staff of the Jewish Home & Hospital Lifecare System mourn the passing of Dr. Elias Savitsky, a respected geriatric psychiatrist, who served at the System's Bronx division for 45 years. He will be missed by those who were privileged to know and learn from him. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Fred M. Lowenfels Chairman Jewish Home & Hospital Lifecare System Board Audrey Weiner, DSW President and CEO
