
        LEVINE-Phyllis E. Age 73, of Hallandale, formerly of NYC, passed away Thursday, December 14, 2000. She was the beloved wife of the late Phillip, loving mother of Michael (Harriet) Levine and Karen (Allen) Saperstein. Cherished grandmother of Paige, Lara and Rikki Saperstein. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Leukemia Society. Service will be held December 19, 2000, 10 AM, at Blasberg Rubin Zilbert Memorial Chapels, 720 71st St, Miami Beach, FL 33141; 305-865-2353.
