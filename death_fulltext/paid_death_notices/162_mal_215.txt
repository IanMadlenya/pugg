
        SPERDAKOS-George Athanasios. Died July 21, at age 68 at Bridgehampton, New York. A native of Montreal, he resided in Toronto, Ontario. He was a respected actor in Canada and in the U.S. His final stage performance was in ''The Diary of Anne Frank'' at Stratford, Ontario.
