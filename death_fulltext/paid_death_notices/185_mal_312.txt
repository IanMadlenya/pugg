
        RAUCH-Eduardo. We mourn the loss of our dear friend Eduardo Rauch. Compassionate lover of nature and humanity, activist, peace-loving Zionist, scholar, teacher, intellectual, poetic soul, truthteller, Edy was an irreplaceable treasure. Love and condolences to his wife Chicky, his father Nathan and his brother Claudio. May his memory be a blessing.
