
        UNGER-Bernard. On March 23, 1999, in Washington, DC. Beloved husband of Mary. Dear brother of Dr. Allen and Beth. Devoted uncle of Allison and Eric, David and Bradley. A retired U.S. Environmental Protection Agency personnel classifier, Bernie was a distinguished authority on personnel management and lectured widely in this field. He was a brilliant and kind man and will be sorely missed. Inurnment at Arlington National Cemetery.
