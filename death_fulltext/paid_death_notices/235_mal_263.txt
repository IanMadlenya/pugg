
        SHICHMAN--David, formerly of Great Neck, NY, died peacefully at his home in Delray Beach, FL on August 17 in his 83rd year. Beloved husband of Marion (nee Soupios) Shichman and proud and devoted father of Michael (Helen), Karen and Janet; beloved step-father of Sandy and Charles Soupios. Cherished grandfather of David Nathan Shichman and Jake and Ryan Crawford. David was a gentle, thoughtful and selfless soul who devoted his life to his family and friends. While we all grieved his loss, we are grateful for the 83 years of his love and friendship. The family has requested that in lieu of flowers any and all donations be made to The Smile Train, Dept.
