
        KIERNAN-Richard Rowen. On January 22, 1998. Husband of Greta and friend and companion of Judy Frisch. Father of Ann, Mark, Paul, John, James Kiernan and Clare Lafferty. Grandfather of ten. Brother of David and Jerry. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Saturday and Sunday 2-5 P.M. Funeral mass at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84th Street, on Monday at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Renal Dialysis Unit of Lenox Hill Hospital.
        KIERNAN-Richard R. The executives and employees of Grey Advertising mourn the death of Dick Kiernan, retired Executive Vice President/Director of Creative Services. He was an outstanding copywriter and inspiring creative leader during his thirty year association with Grey. Dick's good-natured wit, intelligence and ability to craft a well-turned phrase will always be part of our agency's heritage. His many good friends will miss him.
