
        ZARCHIN-Dr. Jules. Formerly of Paterson, NJ, and Venice, Florida, died at West Boca Medical Center, Boca Raton, Florida, on November 21, 2002. Dr. Zarchin is survived by his wife of sixtythree years, Sylvia (Katz), three daughters, Gail Ehrens Weisberger, Pat Cirkus, and Biddy Fein, as well as six grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Memorial donations would be appreciated to the Jewish Community Center of Venice, Florida.
