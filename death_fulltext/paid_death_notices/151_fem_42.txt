
        GILMAN-Sylvia P. On August 4, 1999, at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Loving mother of the late Howard and Charles Jr. She is survived by members of the Gilman, Phillips and Marcus families. Services and interment private.
        GILMAN-Sylvia Phillips. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University mourn the passing of our respected and honored friend, Sylvia Phillips Gilman. Recipient of Doctor Philosophiae Honoris Causa, she was a generous and steadfast supporter of the University and its Faculty of Humanities. Our deep condolences to her family. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Alan L. Aufzien, Chairman Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Robert J. Topchik, President Stephen Lecker, Exec. V.P.
