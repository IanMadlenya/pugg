
        BALL--Dr. Eugene R., died April 25, 2007, age 84. He had a private practice in dentistry and was a Associate Attending Dental Surgeon at St. Luke's Hospital. He served as a US Navy officer in the Pacific during World War II. He was a Fellow of the American College of Dentists. He was a member of the Racquet and Tennis Club, the Tuxedo Club, the Society of Mayflower Decendents, the Military Order of Foreign Wars and the Society of Colonial Wars. He is survived by his wife Sally Ball and his children, Perry Ball and Martha Ball Heppenheimer, his grandchildren, Geoffrey Heppenheimer, Elizabeth Heppenheimer, Katherine Ball and Victoria Ball. Funeral Service will be held at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 5th Ave. at 90th Street at 3:30 pm May 3rd. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Church of the Heavenly Rest.
