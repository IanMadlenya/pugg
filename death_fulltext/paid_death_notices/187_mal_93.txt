
        ALPERT-Richard B., Attorney. Age 49. Died in NYC, August 9th, 2002 after a hard fought battle with pancreatic cancer. Beloved son of Erwin and Florence (Sterling) Alpert of Hamden, CT. Cherished brother of Daniel Alpert (Partner, Rabbi Eric Weiss) of San Francisco, CA. Devoted friend of Eduardo Sese of NYC. A graduate of Rutgers University and the New York Law School, Mr. Alpert was Deputy District Director of E.E.O.C., New York District Office. He was a valiant defender of human rights and social justice. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, August 11th, at 11:45 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th St., NYC. Graveside service at Workmen's Circle Cemetery, Farwell St., W. Haven, CT, at 3:30 PM. Period of mourning will be private. Memorial contributions may be made to Jacob Perlow Hospice, Beth Israel Medical Center, 1st Ave. at 16th St., NY, NY 10003, or to St. Vincent Supportive Care Program, St. Vincents Hospital, 153 W. 11th St., NY, NY 10011.
