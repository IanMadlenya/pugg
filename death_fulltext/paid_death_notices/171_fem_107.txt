
        KRAMER-Frances. Of New York City on April 11, 2001. Beloved sister of Beatrice Spielman of Lewisburg, PA., Jerome Kramer and Elaine Rexdale of Hackensack, N.J. and Sydelle and Fred Klinek of Yonkers, N.Y. Her concern for others and her wonderful sense of humor will be missed by her family, her numerous devoted nieces, nephews, their children and her friends. Rest in peace, Fran. Memorial Service April 19. 4pm, Central Synagogue, 123 E. 55 St., NYC.
        KRAMER-Frances. The Board of Directors, Scientific Advisory Committee and the Fellows of Irvington Institute for Immunological Research are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and benefactor. Ms. Kramer's incredible vision and dedication to basic research was the cornerstone of Irvington's Fellowship Program. She was instrumental in establising The Eugene V. Weissman Memorial Research Fellowship at Irvington Institute. We will always remember her wonderful generosity and friendship. Patricia W. Chadwick, President Mrs. John J. Veronis, Chairman, Board of Directors
