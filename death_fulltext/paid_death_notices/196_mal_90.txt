
        WEINKRANTZ - Murray H. Deceased on May 8th, 2003. Beloved husband of Gloria. Father to Susan Hill & David Weinkrantz. Proud grandfather to Danielle. Ex-G.I., WWII, founder Westchester United Cerebral Palsy. Graveside services Sunday, 11 AM, Mt. Eden Cemetery, Valhalla, New York.
        WEINKRANTZ-Murray. We mourn the passing of Murray Weinkrantz, founding Board Member of United Cerebral Palsy of Westchester. Mr Weinkrantz was a devoted champion of the rights of people with developmental disabilities. He will be greatly missed by individuals with disabilities and their families, the staff and fellow Board Members. Richard Osterer, Board Pres. UCP of Westchester
