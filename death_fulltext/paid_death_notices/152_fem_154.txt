
        THORNE-Leslie Lovejoy. Leslie Lovejoy Thorne of Greenwich died on September 16, 1999, at her home. Mrs. Thorne headed Homeworks, a decorating firm. A member of the Junior League of New York and of the National Society for Colonial Dames in the State of New York. Mrs. Thorne is survived by her husband Edwin Thorne; two daughters Allison Prouty and Leslie Lee, both of Greenwich; one son Grant Simmons III of Ohio; a brother Stuart Lovejoy of Pennsylvania; three grandchildren Thomas Lee, Leslie Lee and Annabel Prouty and six step-children. A memorial service will be held at the Round Hill Community Church on Wednesday, September 22, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made in her memory to the Garden Education Center, the Leslie Thorne Fund of the Boys and Girls Club, or to the Round Hill Community Church, all of Greenwich, CT.
