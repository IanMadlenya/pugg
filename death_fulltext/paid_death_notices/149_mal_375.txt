        STAVISKY-Leonard P. The City College of New York mourns the passing of New York State Senator Leonard P. Stavisky, a graduate of the Class of 1945. Senator Stavisky was a strong supporter of public higher education and a loyal friend of his Alma Mater. On behalf of the City College community I wish to extend my condolences to his family. Yolanda T. Moses, President The City College of New York/ CUNY
