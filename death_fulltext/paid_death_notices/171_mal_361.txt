
        COLIN-Seymour L. Born N.Y., N.Y. March 6, 1913. Died February 26, 2001. President, N.Y. State Trial Lawyers Association, 1971-1972. President, Metropolitan Trial Lawyers Association, 1981-1983. Member of New York State Bar Association, served on Executive Commitee of Trial Lawyers Section. Also served as Chairman of Committee on State Legislation, New York County Lawyers Association and as member of Committee on Courts of Superior Jurisdication, Association of the Bar of the City of New York. Was a member of the First Judicial Panel in New York County, and lectured for the Practicing Law Institute. He will be sorely missed by his widow Sue and his family and his friends.
