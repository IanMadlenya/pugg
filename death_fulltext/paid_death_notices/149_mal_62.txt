
        REISMAN-Philip H., Jr. TV and screen writer of Larchmont, NY. Died June 1st. at Sound Shore Medical Center, New Rochelle. He was 82. Husband of Anna T. Reisman and father of Christopher, Peter, Philip III, the Rev. Catherine T.R. MacDonald and Irene M. Biada. Memorial service 10:30 a.m. Saturday at Larchmont Avenue Church, 60 Forest Park Avenue, Larchmont, NY. Contributions may be sent to the church.
