
        WANDERER--Kenneth Herbert. Died on January 6, 2004, age 80. Survived by his wife Joan; father to Kathryn, Thomas, Diane and Elaine; grandfather to Cynthia, Kyra, Christopher, Emily, Meghan, Miriam, Gregory, Alexander, Sara and Benjamin; great grandfather to Gabrielle. Ken Wanderer was an inspiration, a man of deep faith and a steward of great gifts to his community, church and above all to his family. Viewing: Fox Funeral Home, 2080 Boston Post Road, Larchmont, NY, January 8, 2-4PM and 7-9PM. Service: Larchmont Avenue Church, January 9, 11 AM.
