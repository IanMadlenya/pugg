
        SILVERSTEIN--Don, 71, of New York City, formerly of Detroit, MI. Died January 30th, 2004. Mr. Silverstein is survived by his wife Sakiko Silverstein, his sister Roberta Cohen, and his nephews Dale and Warren Cohen. Mr. Silverstein was a contemporary painter and illustrator of children's books. He began his career in commercial illustration at the Society of Arts and Crafts in Detroit. After two years of service in the U.S. Army, he settled in London and did freelance work. When he finally began to concentrate on fine arts, he had his works displayed at the Detroit Institute of Arts, among other venues. A collection of Mr. Silverstein's art work can be found on the web at www.donsilverstein.com.
