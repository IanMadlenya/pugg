
        FRIEDLANDER-Royal. On October 30, 2000, at Northwest Hospital, Seattle. Husband of the late Mary. Survived by sons Bernard and Joel, daughter-in-law, Jill, and grandson, Max. Recepient of the Bronze Star in WWII. Longtime resident of Westchester County. A printer, teacher of printing, volunteer, storyteller. Spent the last five years of his life as a resident of Seattle. Everyones ''Favorite Uncle''. He is dearly missed by many. Interment at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings, NY.
