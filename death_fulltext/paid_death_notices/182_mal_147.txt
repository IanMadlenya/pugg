
        FOLGER-Samuel Reid. On March 6, 2002, at the age of 81 at his home in Doylestown, PA. Born in Brooklyn, raised in Summit, NJ, graduated from Princeton University in 1941. Served as a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy and was manager of Systems Engineering for General Electric. His loving spirit, depth of character and sense of humor touched us all. He is survived by his four children, Susan, Peter, Jane and Ann, and three grandchildren. Memorial service will be held 2 PM, March 23, at Lenape Valley Presbyterian Church, Rte. 202 and Ute Road, New Britian, PA, 215-345-1099. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the National Parkinson Foundation, 1501 N.W. 9th Avenue, Miami, FL 33136.
