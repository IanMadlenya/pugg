
        BODENHEIMER-Estelle Louise. Of Sarasota, FL, formerly of NYC and Akron, OH, on August 23, 2001, age 75. Dearly beloved wife of Richard B. (Dick), retired editor of ''Chief-Leader.'' Daughter of the late Sallie and Dr. Phillip Hose. Sister of the late Lieut. Leonard Hose, USN, lost in WW II. She was graduate of University of Michigan Music School, sang with school's symphony orchestra; also pianist, violinist. Interment Maimonides Elmont (NY) Cemetery on August 27th. Services private.
