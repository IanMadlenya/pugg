
        CAWLEY-Wayne W., 84, of Palm Beach, FL, died of cancer on February 23, 2002. Born in Johnson City, NY, November 23, 1917, Mr. Cawley resided in Endicott, NY before moving to Palm Beach County, FL. A family man and entrepreneur, his career included dairy business, electronic plants, and realty investment/mortgage brokerage establishments. Mr. Cawley is survived by his wife, Charlotte Jane, a son, Jeffrey W. Cawley of Raleigh, NC, and four daughters, Lucinda L. Cawley of Syracuse, NY, Janice M. Price of Port St. Lucie, FL, Grace Cawley Rhine of Winter Springs, FL, & Virginia J. Berland of Brandon, FL.
