
        COHN-Myron J. On July 23, 1999 at Albany, NY. Practicing attorney for more than 50 years in Schenectady, NY. He is survived by his wife Elaine, two sons, John Myers & his wife Lucy, of Chicago and Bill Myers of Ballston Lake, NY. Brother of Morris Marshall Cohn of Schenectady, NY and Rita Clayman, also of Schenectady. A brother, Milton Cohn, M.D. and a sister, Elizabeth Weisberg, predeceased him. Grandfather of Alexander, Jack and Caroline Myers. Services at Congregation Beth Emeth, Albany, NY on Sunday, July 25, at 2 P.M. Contributions to a charity of one's choice. For more information, contact Levine Memorial Chapel, 518-438-1002.
