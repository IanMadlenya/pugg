
        BAUM--Frederick. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Frederick Baum, whose enthusiastic leadership in our Long Island Campaign and as a board member of Selfhelp Community Services enriched and strengthened our efforts to care for Nazi Victims throughout New York. We extend our deepest sympathies to Frederick's beloved wife, Ilse; to their children, Barbara and Dennis; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        BAUM -- Frederick. Paster nak, Baum and Company, Inc. notes with sadness the passing of our Founder and former President, Fred Baum. He was our inspiration and the model of a gentleman, conducting himself always with dignity and determination. Our deepest sympathies to Ilse and the children.
        BAUM -- Frederick. North Shore Country Club records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Frederick Baum. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Ilse, and his family. Howard S. Jacobs, President Sandra J. Ash, Secretary
        BAUM--Frederick. My best friend who I immigrated with nearly 70 years ago has died. I will never forget the friendship that we had and I am sorry that he is no longer with us. John Slade Bear, Stearns & Co. Inc.
