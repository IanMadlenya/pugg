
        ROSEN-Marvin D., DDS. Suddenly on January 31, 2000. Of San Bernardino, Calif. Age 75. Only son of the late Drs. James Ralph and Martha Gluck Rosen. Veteran World War II and Korea. Prestigious oral surgeon, accomplished musician, Marvin will be greatly missed by his aunts, numerous cousins and friends. Services were held Mt. Hebron cemetery, Flushing.
