
        LEE-SMITH-Hughie. The Board and staff of The Studio Museum in Harlem mourn the loss of the outstanding painter, Hughie LeeSmith. Mr. Lee-Smith was a painter of consistent excellence and sustained commitment to Modernism. His exhibition at The Studio Museum in Harlem in 1973 provided New York audiences with a rare and important look at this significant artist. His work is in the permanent collection of the museum and other institutions around the country. He will be deeply missed. Our condolences to his family and friends. George L. Knox, Chairman Kinshasha H. Conwill, Director
        LEE-SMITH-Hughie. The members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and long time friend, Hughie Lee-Smith, and send condolences to his family.
