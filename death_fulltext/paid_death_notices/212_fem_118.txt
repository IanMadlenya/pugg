
        WAGNER--Julia A. Of New York City, N.Y. Passed from this life on September 13, 2004 at the age of 80. She was a native of Alexandria, VA., born there on February 15, 1924 to the late Luigi and Domenica Coppa. She is a graduate of George Washington High School and George Washington University. After marrying the love of her life, the late Edward A. Wagner, she pursued her career as his Assistant Editor of Horoscope Magazine for Dell Publishing Company. After her retirement, she continued to visit her family in Virginia. She is survived by her loving sister, Lucy C. Strobel and her brothers-in-law, Frank O. Strobel and Mack Bishop. She leaves behind five devoted nieces and three grand nieces and seven grand nephews. Family will receive friends at Demaines Funeral Home, 520 South Washington St., Alexandria, VA. September 18, 6-8 PM and September 19, 2-4 PM and 6-8PM. Funeral Mass will be celebrated at St. Rita's Catholic Church, Alexandria, at 10 AM, September 20, 2004. Interment will follow at Mt. Comfort Cemetery. Donations in Julia's memory may be made to Cabrini Hospice, 227 East 19 Street, New York City, N.Y. 10013.
