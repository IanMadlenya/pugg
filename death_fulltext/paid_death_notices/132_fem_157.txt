
        SCHUYLER-Pearl. Suddenly on January 17, 1998. Beloved wife of Irving. Loving mother of Sandra Schuyler Jaffee and Harvey Schuyler. Dear mother-in-law of Carl Jaffee and cherished grandmother of Evan Jaffee. Private private.
        SCHUYLER-Pearl. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Pearl Schuyler. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        SCHUYLER-Pearl. We mourn, along with your beloved Irving, your loving children Harvey and Sandra, your dear son-in-law Carl, and your adored grandson Evan. We will always miss you, Pearl dear. Rest in peace. Helene and Irving Schwartz
