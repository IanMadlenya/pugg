
        PEZENIK-Alfred Joel, Esq. In his 80th year. Dearly beloved husband of Dorothy. Dear uncle of David and Steven of New York, and Lisa and Wendy World of Toronto. Funeral service Thursday, 12 Noon at ''Gutterman's Inc.'' 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, Long Island. Interment Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, New York.
