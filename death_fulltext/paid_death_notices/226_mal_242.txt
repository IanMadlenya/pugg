
        WARD--Paul L. Sarah Lawrence College mourns the death of its fifth president Paul Langdon Ward, who led the liberal arts college from 1960-1965 during a period of growth, while strengthening and preserving the College's commitment to rigorous, individualized education. We extend our sympathies to his wife, Catherine Wakefield Ward, his children and grandchildren. Michele Tolela Myers President Barbara Kaplan Dean of the College
