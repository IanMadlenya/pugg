
        VILLAMENA--Dr. Anthony. Died on March 6, 2006 after a brief battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Cean Tillett Villamena, adored father of Vincenzo and Nicola, and dear brother of Dr. Diodato Villamena and wife, Karen. A dedicated psychiatrist, he was a fellow of the American Psychiatric Association, and past president of the Psychiatric Society of Westchester. He was a graduate of Brooklyn Friends School, New York University and New York Medical School. With a passion to help others, Tony was president of the Parish Council at Holy Family Church, he built homes in the U.S. and Mexico with Habitat for Humanity, and spread his love to everyone he touched. He will be deeply missed. Visitation on Thursday, 2-4 & 7-9 at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 Lecount Place, New Rochelle. Mass will be celebrated Friday, 9:30 AM Holy Family Church. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
