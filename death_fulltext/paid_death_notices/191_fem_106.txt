
        FEINBERG - Lillie Wise. Passed away on December 10, 2002 in Palm Beach, Fl. at age 94. Formerly a resident of NYC, originally from Savannah, Georgia. Beloved wife of the late Meyer Feinberg. Loving mother of Helen Weinstein and mother-in-law of Larry Weinstein. Dearest grandmother of Lisa Lynds, Ilene Weinstein and Jaimie Gironda and great-grandmother of Abigail, Elizabeth and Spencer. She had a wonderful life and will be missed by all. Rest in peace. Services Friday, 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
