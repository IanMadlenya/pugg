
        GORDON--Robert E., MD. The faculty of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology of New York Medical College and the administration of the Medical College, note with great sadness the death of Robert E. Gordon, M.D. We offer our deepest sympathies to his family. Dr. Gordon was a remarkable clinician, a superb teacher, a truly talented surgeon and a loyal faculty member. Bob was also my best friend. James R. Jones, M.D. Professor Emeritus Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, New York Medical College
