
        SPIVAK-Harold P. Died January 12, 1999. Devoted husband of Ruth Spivak, loving father of Steven and Barbara Spivak, father-in-law of Sandy and Howard, devoted grandfather of Lisa, Eric, Katie, Adam and Elizabeth, dear brother of Pearl Levy & Evelyn Weingold and the late Ruth, Dorothy and Murray. Memorial services will be held at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street at Amsterdam Avenue, N.Y., NY, Thursday, January 14, 1999 at 12:30 PM. The family will be receiving visitors at their home, 205 3rd Ave, Friday 1-6 & Saturday 1-5. Family members and friends wishing to make a donation in memory of Harold P. Spivak are requested to send their contribution to the Richard F. Walsh/Alfred W. Di Tolla Foundation, 1515 Broadway, Suite 601, New York, New York 10036, 212-730-1770.

        SPIVAK-Harold P. Spivak, Lipton, Watanabe, Spivak & Moss mourns the loss of its senior partner and leader. His extraordinary warmth, decency, brilliance and sage counsel have inspired us all. General Counsel to the International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees for a half century, respected advisor to his many clients in the entertainment industry, and fighter for the rights of working men and women. We will miss him dearly. Spivak, Lipton, Watanabe, Spivak & Moss
        SPIVAK-Harold. The Trustees and participants of the Welfare, Pension and Annuity Fund of Local One, I.A.T.S.E. are deeply saddened by the passing of our most respected and dearly beloved cocounsel, Harold Spivak of the firm, Spivak, Watanabe, Spivak and Moss.
        SPIVAK-Harold P. Fund Counsel for over 40 years, Harold was a gentleman and a friend who will be sadly missed by everyone. The Trustees Make-up Artist and Hair Stylist Local 798 I.A.T.S.E.
