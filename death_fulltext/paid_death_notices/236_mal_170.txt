
        STEVENS--Oswald, age 62, known as Ozzie to his family and many friends, passed away on Sunday, September 10, 2006 after a brief illness. Ozzie was the beloved husband of Teresa and a loving and devoted father to his son Anil, his daughter Sunita and her husband Christopher Meyers. Ozzie was owner and chef-proprietor of Pandit India Restaurant on both the East and West sides of Manhattan for over 20 years. He nourished us with his wonderful food, his laughter and endless happy memories at his restaurants, as well as at his home. Ozzie will be dearly missed by his family and friends who will always cherishhis smile that lit up a room, his love of life, his good humor and his generous spirit. To know Ozzie was to love Ozzie. We are blessed to have had him in our lives and we will hold his spirit close in our hearts. He was reposed at Edward D. Lynch Funeral Home, 4307 Queens Boulevard, Sunnyside, NY 11104; (718) 784-1525. Visitations were held on Wednesday September 13 and Thursday September 14; 2-5 pm and 79:30 pm; Funeral Mass to be held today, September 15, 2006 at 10 am at St. Raphael's Church, 3520 Greenpoint Avenue, Long Island City, NY 11101; (718) 729 8957. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. Raphael's Church in memory of Ozzie Stevens.
