
        KAGAN-Hildegard (nee Wallach). 93. On October 11, 1997 in Geneva, Switzerland. Devoted wife of the late Illi Kagan. She is survived by her brother, Helmuth Wallach, her two children, Tanya Josefowitz and Vladimir Kagan, six granchildren and ten great-grandchildren. She had a wonderful, long life and will be missed. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Breast Cancer Research. Services will held in Geneva, Switzerland.
