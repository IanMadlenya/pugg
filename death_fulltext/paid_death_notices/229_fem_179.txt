
        GLASSMAN--Sylvia (Goldsmith). Beloved mother of Milton, Sheldon, Stanley (deceased), Jerome, Deborah, Seth, and Paul, and grandmother of Jed, Gregory, Daniel, Leah, and Zachary, left us on February 17. Extremely ill for years, she fought back time and again to share her life with her children and friends. Her love and kindness will be remembered always. Burial was February 19, at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont.
