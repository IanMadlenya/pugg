
        BECKER-Nathaniel. Of Hollywood, FL. Died in his 94th year, on June 13, 1998. Beloved husband for 69 years to his sweetheart and dancing partner Selma. Loving father to Harriet and Ira Teper, Joel and Dorothy Becker and Richard Becker. Cherished grandfather to Melissa Teper, Laurie Becker and Paul Berkelhammer, Wendy and Michael Zucker and Jeffrey and Joanne Levine. Loving great-grandfather to Jennie and Bryan Levine and Joshua and Sarah Zucker. Caring brother-inlaw to Pearl Kohnop. A testament to this thoughtful and generous man lives in the warm memories his many nieces, nephews, friends and acquaintances in New York and Florida will hold in their hearts. Services at I. J. Morris, Deer Park Ave, Dix Hills, NY, 12:15 PM, Monday, June 15.
