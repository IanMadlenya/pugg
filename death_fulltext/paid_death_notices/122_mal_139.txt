
        HAMILTON-Dr. Francis J. The faculty and staff of the New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center, Department of Psychiatry note with profound sorrow the passing of Dr. Francis J. Hamilton, former Medical Director of the Westchester Division from 1965 until his retirement in 1973, and respected member of the faculty of the Payne Whitney Clinic for many years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his sons, Dr. Frank Hamilton, Jr. and Timothy Hamilton and their entire family. A graduate of St. Joseph's College and Jefferson Medical School in Philadelphia, Dr. Hamilton completed his medical training as a resident at The New York Hospital. Following his service in the Navy during W.W. II, he returned to the New York Hospital where he held a number of positions, including Director of the Payne Whitney Clinic Outpatient Department, prior to his appointment as the Medical Director of the Westchester Division in White Plains. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Francis J. Hamilton Memorial Fund, c/o The New York Hospital, Department of Psychiatry, 525 East 68 Street, NY, NY 10021. Jack D. Barchas, M.D. Professor and Chair, Department of Psychiatry Gary L. Tischler, M.D. Professor and Exec Vice Chair Department of Psychiatry
