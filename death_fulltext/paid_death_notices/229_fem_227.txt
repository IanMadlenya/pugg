
        BIRNBAUM--Lillian ''Goodie'' Goode, 90. On February 26, 2006 of Pancreatic Cancer. A memorial service will be held at 11:30 AM on Wednesday, March 1 at Riverside Chapel, 180 W. 76th St. Born in 1915 in Rochester, NY, she came to NYC to attend Beth Israel Hospital School of Nursing, earning her RN. Moved to Rockville Centre in 1952 with her husband Bernard Birnbaum and their three children. After her husband's death in 1970, she moved to Greenwich Village. She then worked for ten years at a Beth Israel Methadone Clinic. Survivors include her son and daughter-in-law Alan and Janet Blake, daughter Carol Brody, son and daughter-in-law Peter and Carol Lynn Birnbaum and grandsons Randall and Evan Blake. Contributions may be made to the Ida & Samuel Goode Family Fund at The Jewish Home of Rochester or The Village Temple Soup Kitchen.
