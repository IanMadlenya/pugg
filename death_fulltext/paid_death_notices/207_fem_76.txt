
        SCHAFFER--Helen. May 14, 1916-April 9, 2004, loving mother, wife, grandmother, great - grandmother and friend to so many, died at home after a long illness, surrounded by family. Preceded in death by her husband Michael, her ten siblings, and grandson David. She is survived by her daughter Wendy Appel of Boston, MA, and son Peter Schaffer of Oklahoma City, OK; three grandchildren; and one great-grandson. Services were held Sunday, April 11. at Temple Israel of New Rochelle. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to Lung Failure Fund of New York-Presbyterian Hospital, c/o Byron Thomashow, M.D., 161 Fort Washington Ave, NYC 10032.
