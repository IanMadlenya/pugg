
        ISAACSON-Judge Oscar, 87. Died on August 11, Lauderhill, Florida. Previously of Teaneck, NJ. Chief Administrative Law Judge in TN, NC, FL. His brilliance, caring and humor remembered especially by beloved wife of 61 years Betty, daughter Anita (Carl) Jacobs of Teaneck, grandchildren Eliezer and Abraham Jacobs and Debra Isaacson and Evelyn, wife of late son Louis Isaacson.
