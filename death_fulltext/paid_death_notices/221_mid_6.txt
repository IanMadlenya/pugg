
        LIEBERMAN--Marcia. The Yeshiva University family expresses sorrow on the passing of the beloved mother of Senator Joseph Lieberman, a YU honorary alumnus. An Aishet Chayil, she embodied the values for which he stands. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman Board of Trustees
