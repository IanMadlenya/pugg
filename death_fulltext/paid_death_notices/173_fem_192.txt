
        REISFELD-Joan H. Of Marco Island, FL, passed away on June 20th, 2001. Born in Astoria, Queens to Estelle and William Streim, Joan was a graduate of New York University. She was a wonderful wife for 54 years to Mort, a caring and loving mother to her children, Rona and Donald, & grandmother to Daryl, Lauren and Sean. Joan was a social worker, teacher, swimming instructor & volunteer for the Nassau Center for Emotionally Disturbed Children and Temple Emanu-El of New York. The family suggests that donations be made in her memory to the American Juvenile Diabetes Association. Funeral services held today at 1:45 PM at Gutterman and Musicant, Hackensack, NJ.
