
        DE LUNA-George C. Beloved husband of Barbara. Cherished father of George, Robert, and John. Devoted son of Dorothea. Loving brother of Eugene. A career banking executive, George de Luna was revered internationally for his insightful analyses and accurate predictions in foreign currency exchange. Over 30 years he served as vice president at Citibank, Security Pacific and Irving Trust, working in New York, London and Toronto. A graduate of Colby College, he held degrees in psychology, math and physics. Epitomizing integrity and honesty, he was 66 when he died on July 6 following a battle with cancer. He will be sorely missed. In lieu of flowers please send donations to prostate cancer research, Medical Oncology, Columbia Presbyterian.
