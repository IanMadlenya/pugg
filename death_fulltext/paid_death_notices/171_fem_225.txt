
        SLATTERY-Annabelle. William Doyle Galleries is deeply saddened by the loss of our beloved Annabelle Slattery, a member of our family for over 25 years. Annabelle's warm greetings and professional advice set the standard for service to thousands of our clients. We will forever miss the wise counsel and gentle grace that Annabelle generously shared with us until her 96th year. Kathleen M. Doyle Chairman/C.E.O. Doyle New York
