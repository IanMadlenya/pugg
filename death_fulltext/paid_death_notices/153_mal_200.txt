
        JACOBSON - Nathan Hanford, D.D.S. Age 89. First dentist in Elmont, L.I. Son of Dora Menken and Harry Jacobson. Brother of the late Eugene Debs Jacobson and Sylvia Feldhammer. Husband of Roslyn (nee Sank) and father of Eric, Neil, Donna and Mark. Grandfather of Alexander, William, James, Cecelia, Max and Allie Rose. Sports enthusiast, poet, celebrator of life. He was loved and will be remembered.
