
        MANN--Dr. Sidney. 75, of Old Tappan, NJ, formerly of Fair Lawn, NJ, on September 20, 2003, following a brief but courageous battle with cancer. An orthodontist for four decades, born in the Bronx and graduated from New York University with a Bachelor of Arts in 1950. He graduated NYU School of Dentistry in 1954 and received his certificate in orthodontics from the State University of Iowa in 1957. Dr. Mann treated over 5,000 patients throughout the course of his career. Survived by his wife of 46 years, Harriet (nee Griss), children Steven and his wife Susan, and Linda Spelling and her husband Ian; grandchildren Justin, Max and Jamie, mother Mary Mann, sister Racelle Hill; nephews Larry, Sam and Ted; and niece Susan. The family will receive visitors at the Mann residence in Old Tappan through Friday. Donations in the name of Dr. Sidney Mann to Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, attn: Gall Bladder Research.
