
        BERNSTEIN - Seymour Harold. Died on March 6, 1999, at the age of 87. Beloved husband for fiftytwo years of Esther Teper Bernstein. Devoted father of Carol Weinstein, Geri Freund and Alan Bernstein. Adored grandfather of Matanya, Noa, Merav, Eliya and Benjamin. Father-in-law of Carol Bowen and Gilad Freund. Brother of the late Pearl Max, the late I. Maurice Bernstein, Ira Bernstein, Hilda Morris and Betty Bernstein. The ''Chief'' to Alex Burger. Funeral will be held at 12 noon on March 9, 1999, at The Riverside. May his strength, faith, generosity and love be a blessing to us all.
