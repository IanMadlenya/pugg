
        MILLS-John Porter. Died at home in NY on July 10, 1998 at age 94. Born in Brooklyn, NY, he was a graduate of Wesleyan University and had a long career as a mathematician with The Equitable. Husband of the late Sherring Mills. Survived by his daughter, Nancy Gilbert, brother Robert, grandchildren Roger, Elizabeth and Scott, and great-grandson Nicholas. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Church of St Paul and St Andrew, West End Avenue at 86 Street, NY 10024 where a memorial service will be held Tuesday, July 14 at 6:30PM.
