
        SILVERMAN-Beverly R. (nee Roth). At age 84, passed away peacefully at home on Mother's Day, May 12, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Buddy Silverman. Devoted mother of William M. Silverman and Lynn G. Silverman, and Bruce G. Silverman and Rita M. Silverman. Cherished grandmother of Kim Lindsay, John and Isabelle Bodmer Silverman, Scott M. Silverman and Max D. Silverman. Adored great-grandmother of Sophie Anna Silverman. Funeral services are private. Contributions in her memory may be made to your favorite charity.
        SILVERMAN-Beverly. We deeply mourn the passing of Beverly Silverman, the mother of William M. Silverman, our esteemed partner. Our thoughts and sympathies are with Bill and his wife, Lynn, and their children, Kim, Scott, John and his wife Isabelle and their daughter Sophie, and with Bill's brother Bruce and his wife, Rita, and their son Max. Beverly Silverman was a bright and extraordinary woman who adored and was adored by her entire family. Otterbourg, Steindler, Houston & Rosen, P.C.
        SILVERMAN-Beverly R. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Beverly R. Silverman. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
