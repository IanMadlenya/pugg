
        PRIVITERA-Msgr. Santi J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Bernadette's Church, 8201 13th Ave., Brooklyn, on September 4. Reposing at Torregrossa Funeral Home, 1305 79th St., Brooklyn, Wednesday, September 6, from 2 to 5 p.m. Mass of Transferral at St. Bernadette's Church, at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass on Thursday, September 7, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
