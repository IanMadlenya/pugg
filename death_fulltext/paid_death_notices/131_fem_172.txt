
        DONNELLY-Loretto G. Age 90, formerly of NYC, died December 19, 1997 in Elmira, NY. She is survived by her sister, Anne Maloney of Elmira; several nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by three sisters and brother, Msgr. Thomas J. Donnelly. She was a graduate of the College of Mt. St. Vincent. She was a superintendent for the Board of Education for the Physically Handicapped. Funeral services will be held at 11:00 AM in the Church of Transfiguration, Tarrytown, NY.
