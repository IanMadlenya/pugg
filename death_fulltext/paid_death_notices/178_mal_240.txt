
        ADELHART-Andrew J. It is with deep sorrow and affection that the General Society of Mechanics and Tradesmen of the City of New York records the passing on November 8, 2001, of our member and Past President (1977), Andrew J. Adelhart. For many years he served the Society in numerous capacities, most notably as Chairman of the Library Committee. Andrew's father was also associated with the Society and graduated from the Mechanics Institute. Sincere condlences are sent to his wife and family. A memorial service was held on Tuesday, November 13, 2001.
