
        WEIL-John A. Died May 29, 2000 in Fort Lauderdale, FL, of an embolism. He is survived by brother Kenneth M. Weil of Stamford, CT, sister Anne W. Weinberg of Cleveland, OH. He graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy and Yale University with a degree in economics. He served on the Atomic Bomb Test Bikini atoll. He served in public relations all of his life. We will miss him.
