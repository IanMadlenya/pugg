
        SCHANK-Patricia Ann Smith. Widow of Sherwood Schank, died of natural causes at her home in New York on February 22. Daughter of Anne and Vincent Smith, she is survived by her first cousin Constance Meaney. Mrs. Schank was active for many years on the Boards of Soldiers', Sailors', Marines' & Airmen's Club and Elder Craftsmen. Services at 4 PM, Saturday, March 3, St. George's Chapel, Stuyvesant Square. Donations in her memory may be made to these organizations.
        SCHANK-Patricia S. Active and esteemed member of the Board of Soldiers', Sailors', Marines' and Airmen's Club, died in her sleep on Thursday night, 22 February, 2001. Her tireless efforts for the House Committee of the SSMAC to serve the US Military, veterans and their families, set an example for us all. Memorial services: 4 PM, Saturday, 3 March, 2001, at St. George's Chapel, Stuyvesant Square, between 16th and 17th Streets, east of Third Avenue, New York. R.I.P. Ivan Obolensky, Chairman
