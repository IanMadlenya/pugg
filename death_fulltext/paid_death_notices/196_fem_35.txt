
        SCHLANSKY - Helen (nee Walder). On May 4. Beloved wife of the late Bernard Schlansky. Loving mother of Mitchell and Mark Schlansky and Barbara Schaffer. Contributions may be made to The National Yiddish Book Center, 1021 West Street, Amherst, MA 01002.
