
        CLEAVER-John P., 79, died on December 11, 1998 at home in Little Compton, R.I. following a brief illness. He leaves his wife of 52 years, Elizabeth (Ruth) Cleaver; three children, three grandchildren, a brother, William Cleaver and seven nieces and nephews. A Memorial service will be held at 12:00, January 9, 1999 at the United Congregational Church in Little Compton, Rhode Island. Contributions in his memory may be sent to Princeton-Blairstown Center, The Armory, Princeton University, Princeton, NJ 08544, or The Little Compton Agricultural Conservancy Trust, Town Hall, Little Compton, R.I. 02837. For information, The Potter Funeral Home, 81 Reed Road, Westport, Mass.
