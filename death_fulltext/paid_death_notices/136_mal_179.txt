
        MAXWELL-Michael Phillip. A customs attorney, died of a heart attack on May 9, 1998, in Los Angeles, CA, at the age of 48. He will be sorely missed by his family and many friends. He received his B.A. degree from Memphis State University, a law degree from Memphis State University and a Master of Law degree from the University of Pennsylvania. He practiced law in New York City and Los Angeles. He is survived by his parents Mike and Irene Maxwell of FL; a sister Amanda Maxwell, of Miami; and a brother James, of New York City. Services were held at Hillside Memorial Park in Los Angeles, CA, on Thursday, May 14, 1998. Memorial donations may be made to either a scholarship fund established in the name of Michael P. Maxwell by Women in International Trade, Inc., P.O. Box 3972, Barranca Parkway, Suite J200, Irvine, CA 92604, (310) 522-0200 ext 340, or to the American Heart Association, (800) 242-8721.
        MAXWELL-Michael. The partners, associates and staff of Grunfeld, Desiderio, Lebowitz and Silverman note with sorrow the untimely passing in Los Angeles on May 9 of their colleague and friend, Michael Maxwell, and extend heartfelt condolences to his entire family. Michael was an esteemed member of the customs and international trade bar whose contributions to the firm's practice will be sorely missed. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Heart Association, 1055 Wilshire Blvd., Suite 900, Los Angeles, CA. 90017-2400.
