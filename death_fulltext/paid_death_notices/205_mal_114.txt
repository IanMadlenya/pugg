
        HARRIS--Leonard A. 92, died Friday, in Norwalk, CT. Mr. Harris was a retired editor of the Norwalk Hour and served the City of Norwalk as a fire commissioner, a shellfish commissioner and Second Taxing District Commissioner. He also was a member of the Planning Commission and advisory panels for the Coastal Area Management and the Enterprise Zone. Mr. Harris served twice as Commodore and was a life member of The South Norwalk Boat Club. He was predeceased by his first wife, Jeanette Fainer Harris, and his second wife Elizabeth Rairden Harris. Surviving are three stepsons, Richard Ramsey of Norwalk, CT, Donald Ramsey of Newtown, CT, and Douglas Ramsey of Stratford, CT.
