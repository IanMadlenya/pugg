
        STRAUS-William. Age 96, of Lauderhill, Florida, passed away July 30, 1998. Mr. Strauss is survived by his loving wife, Celia Topf Straus; devoted son, Robert H. Straus acting Justice New York State Supreme Court, adored daughter-in-law Bobbi Straus and cherished grandson Matthew Robert Straus. Funeral services will be held today, Friday July 31 at 11 AM, Washington Cemetery, Brooklyn, New York. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Sarah L. Straus Alumni Scholarship Fund, c/o Seward Park High School, 350 Grand Street, New York, NY 10002
