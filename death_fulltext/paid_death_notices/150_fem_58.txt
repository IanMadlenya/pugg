
        DAVIS-Thelma (Tukel). On July 5, 1999 at age 72 after a long illness at her home in Merrick, N.Y. Cherished mother of Erica Tukel Wax of Chicago and David Tukel. Beloved wife of the late Robert Tukel and loving wife of Henry Davis. Devoted grandmother of Rachel and Benjamin Wax. Born in Brooklyn, N.Y. Youngest of six children of Philip and Ida Gellman. Former officer of Kadillac Mortgage Banking Corporation. Services Tuesday, 1PM, at ''Gutterman's Inc.'', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville, Centre, L.I. Interment at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, L.I.
