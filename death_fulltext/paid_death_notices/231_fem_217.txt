
        KAMEN--Anne P. 89. Beloved widow of Paul Kamen (WWII casualty) and controller at Proskauer Rose Goetz and Mendelsohn, until 1981, died on Wednesday, April 26, 2006. Mrs. Kamen spent much of her life doing things she enjoyed, including her career and traveling around the world. Anne was a role model both professionally and personally. She had a powerful effect on everyone she met. She was a kind and caring woman who will be missed by all who knew her. Funeral service at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, today, at 2 PM.
