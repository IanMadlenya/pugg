
        MENDELSON--Jerry. At 79, December 28, 2005. He was the son of Sadie and Irving Mendelson. His father remarried Marjorie Weiss when he was 12. In 1953, Jerry married Marilyn, they had three children, Jim, Jane and Susan. He had a vital career in the business of women's shoes. In addition to his wife and children, he leaves behind his brother Jon and seven grandchildren.
