
        O'NEIL-James Martin. 71. Of New York City on May 1, 1999. Beloved husband of Anne Kennedy O'Neil. Devoted father of Anne Ocampo of New York City, and Martin O'Neil of Hong Kong. Loving grandfather of Molly, Julie, James, Mariko, and Anna. Mass of Christian Burial will be held at the Church of St. Thomas More, 65 East 89 St., Wednesday, May 5, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Church of St. Thomas More would be greatly appreciated.
        O'NEIL-James M. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York records with profound sorrow the death of James M. O'Neil, a member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. William J. Cronin, President Robert J. Reilly, Historian
