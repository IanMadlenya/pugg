
        WITKOWSKI-Dr. Francis Xavier. Noted researcher and staff cardiologist at the University of Alberta Hospital. Passed away on December 17, 1999 at the age of 52 years. He is survived by his wife, Pat, three daughters Chloe, Elizabeth and Olivia, his loving mother Helen J. Witkowski of Atlanta, GA., one sister Mary Lou Dykes of Atlanta, GA., two nephews David and his wife Vicki Dykes of Marietta, GA., Eric and his wife Amy Dykes of Lawrenceville, GA. He was pre-deceased by his father Francis V. Witkowski in 1975. Funeral service will be held at 3 P.M., on Wednesday, December 22 at The Chapel of Howard M. McBride Westlawn, 16310 Stony Plain Road, Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. Interment at Westlawn Memorial Gardens.
