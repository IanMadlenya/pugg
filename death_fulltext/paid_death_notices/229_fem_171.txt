
        GREINER--Victoria. February 18, 2006. Beloved mother of Stephen (Marilyn) and the late Luise Greiner. Widow of Louis and longtime adored companion of the late Massimo Yezzi. Sister of Ann and Stephen. Loving grandmother of Lauren, Alison and Elizabeth. A woman of strong will and determination, Victoria worked until the age of 85. All who were fortunate enough to know her were struck by her intelligence and kind, gentle, patient nature. She will be forever loved and deeply missed.
