
        GOLDSMITH-Theresa K. On June 10, 1998. It is with great sadness and loss her family mourns a devoted and much loved woman. Wife of the late Irvin J., mother of James & Kathleen, grandmother of David, Dawn, Jonathan and Robert, and great-grandmother of David Jr. Beloved sisted of Gertrude Kastoff. Aunt of Stephen and Peter Kastoff. Theresa resided in both Riverdale and Mahopac, NY. She was a congregant of Temple Beth Shalom, Mahopac, NY. Services to be held 11AM June 12, 1998. Joseph Smith Funeral Home, 692 Route 6, Mahopac, New York.
