
        BAKER-Penny B., Salisbury, CT, 58, mother, housewife, and photo stylist in her husband's illustration business, died Monday, March 31, 2003 at New York University Tisch Hospital after a long battle with the disease of scleroderma (CREST). She was the wife of Joe Baker of Salisbury, CT and mother of Stephen Koshland also of Salisbury, CT. Mrs. Baker was born November 4, 1944 in Manhattan, NY. Daughter of Gwen (Gaillard) Bloomingdale of Nantucket, MA and the late Lymans G. Bloomingdale of Manhattan, NY. Great-granddaughter of the late Lymans G. Bloomingdale, the founder of Bloomingdale's Department Store. After attending Grace Church School in New York and Nantucket High School she graduated from Mills College. She taught nursery school at Little Scholar School in Lakeville, CT. In addition to her husband, son and mother, she is survived by two sisters, Jennifer Nelson of Milton, MA., and Billie Logan of Reno, NV., and several nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Critical Care East ICU, New York University Tisch Hospital, 560 First Ave., New York, NY 10016, Attention Judy Dillworth. For information Newkirk-Palmer Funeral Home, 118 Main St., Canaan, CT 06018. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, April 5, at the funeral home 1 PM.
