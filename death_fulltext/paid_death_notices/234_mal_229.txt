
        WEINER--Howard A., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Department of Psychiatry of Lenox Hill Hospital express profound sorrow at the passing of Howard A. Weiner, M.D., a distinguished psychiatrist on our staff for over 40 years. Dr. Weiner was an outstanding physician, esteemed colleague and highly regarded supervisor of medical students and residents. He will be sorely missed by patients, colleagues and friends. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO Chitranjan Ranawat, M.D. President, Medical Board Molly Poag, M.D. Acting Chair, Department of Psychiatry
