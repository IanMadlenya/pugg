
        HOCHSTIM - Theodore S. (Ted). Passed away suddenly on April 29, 2003 at home in Dallas. He is survived by his wife, Iva, children, David and Michelle Hochstim, Betsy and Rick Fijolek, Nancy and Michael Goldberg and seven grandchildren. Born July 7, 1927, attended Townsend Harris, graduated from Horace Mann, Washington & Lee and Cornell Law School. He served on the boards of the AFA, NPR Foundation and Macerich Company, Brown Brothers Harriman Trust of Texas. Services Thursday, May 1 in Dallas.
