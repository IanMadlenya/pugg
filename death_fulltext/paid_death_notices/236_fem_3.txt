
        RUSH--Adele. It is with great sorrow that we announce the passing of Adele Rush, the matriarch of the Rush family. She was a stalwart for family values as well as an early pioneer for Hadassah, Zionism, and the state of our homeland Israel. Alvin and Marilyn, Jeffrey and Loni, her eight grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren are proud that she still remembered every name of these 28 family members. Services at Gutterman's, Woodbury, LI Sunday 10am.
        $(6$)RUSH--Adele. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Adele Rush, beloved mother of Alvin Rush and mother-inlaw of Marilyn Rush, cherished leaders of the New York Jewish community who strengthen our ability to serve those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Alvin and Marilyn, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
