
        ZIRINSKY--Helen Gelsey. 83, after a long period of illness. She was a Brooklyn girl with great charm and enthusiasm for life. She was a beautiful, outspoken woman with a big heart who always lived her life to the fullest. Helen was a devoted daughter and a great friend to many. She gave her all for her four children. She is survived by her brother Dan Gelsey (and Myrna), her sons John (and Olga) and Bill (and Ruth), and five grandchildren: Sara, Hanna, Jacob, Juliana and Leela. Viewing on Monday, 7-9 pm, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. Service Tuesday, at 10 am, at Frank E. Campbell.
