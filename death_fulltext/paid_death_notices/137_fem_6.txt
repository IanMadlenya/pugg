
        BARON-Carol S. Born September 26, 1912 in New York City passed away May 30, 1998 in Los Angeles where she resided for nearly fifty years. A Smith College graduate, she was a secretary at CBS Television for more than twenty years until her retirement in 1977. During her career, she worked on such programs as Playhouse 90, The Danny Kaye Show, The Judy Garland Show and The Eve Arden Show. She is survived by her son, Roger (wife Margi) of Northbrook, IL. and grandsons Jeffrey (wife Pamela) and Gregory. Graveside services and interment will be in the family plot at Salem Fields Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
