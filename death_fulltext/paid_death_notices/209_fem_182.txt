
        GAINES--Sophie (nee Golden). Of Brooklyn, NY, Hewlett, LI and Pompano Beach, FL on June 25 of ovarian cancer at age 84. Loving and loved wife of Michael (deceased). Mother of Barbara and Ira. Grandmother of Andrew. Mother-in-law of Aari. She will be missed by all those whose lives she touched. We will miss her sense of humor, her generosity, her crazy devotion to her family. Funeral service: Sunday, June 27, 10 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 331 Amsterdam Ave. Shiva will be sat at Barbara and Aari's home, Monday, June 28 through Wednesday, June 30, 3:30 to 9:00 PM. Contributions in Sophie's memory may be made to: Congregation Beth Simchat Torah (CBST), 57 Bethune St., NY, NY 10014.
