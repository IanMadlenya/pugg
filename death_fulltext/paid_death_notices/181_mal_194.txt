
        TELCHIN-Joseph G. On February 5, 2002, age 92. Retired attorney in NY, Washington DC and Sarasota, FL. Also a founder and general counsel of Countrywide and Statewide Insurance Companies. He is survived by his daughter, Esther Baker Fassler of Sarasota, his granddaughter, Andrea Glantz Kas, and great-granddaughter, Griffin Elizabeth Kas of Michigan.
