
        GELLMAN - Zelda. Passed away peacefully at home on April 13, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Norman. Loving and devoted mother of Lance & Caren. Adored grandmother of Melissa, Glenn, Spencer and Kimberly. A woman with unmistakable flair, a passion & zest for life, a tremendous sense of humor and artistic creativity, Zelda was in the truest sense one-of-a-kind. She touched many with her gifts and will be missed by all. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to JAFCO. For further information please call ''Gutterman-Musicant'', Hackensack, New Jersey.
