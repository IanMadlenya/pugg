
        ELTING-Faye. 87. Passed away on January 23, 2001, after a brief illness. Loving mother of Lois and Eve. Grandmother of David and Eric. Former wife of L.M. Elting, MD. Born in Bayonne, NJ, attended Staten Island School of Nursing and resided in Teaneck, NJ, for over 50 years. She was an active member of the Womens Guild of the Teaneck Symphony/Bergen Philharmonic. She will be remembered fondly for her interests in music, nature, gardening; for her loyalty to friends and family and caretaker; and her ever ready eagerness to engage in lively political discourse. Donations to Paul Taylor Dance Foundation, 552 Broadway, NY, NY 10012. Memorial services to be held March 18.
