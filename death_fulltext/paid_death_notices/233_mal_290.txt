
        KEILLOR--Ronald Woodhull, 82, of Essex, CT, died June 23, 2006 at Middlesex Hospital Hospice Unit in Middletown. He is survived by his loving wife Wilda (Heath) Keillor, his four children, Frederick M. Keillor of Martha's Vineyard, MA, Dana Davison (Keillor-Fitton) of Essex, CT, Jay D. Keillor of Monroe, CT, Ronald W. Keillor Jr. of Kerhonkson, NY, his son-in-law Geoffrey Fitton, 11 grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. He served honorably in the USGC during WWII and was the former Chief of the East Williston, NY Volunteer Fire Dept. Graveside services will be held at Wading River Cemetery, Wading River, L.I. on Wednesday, June 28, 2006 at 10 A.M. Memorial service to follow at 4 P.M. at the First Congregational Church, Methodist Hill, Essex, CT. Donations in his memory may be made to the First Congregational Church in Essex. For information contact Robinson, Wright & Weymer Funeral Home, Centerbrook, CT.
