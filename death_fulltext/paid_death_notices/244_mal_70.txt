
        HARRIS--Arthur Joseph, of Larchmont and most recently of Brooklyn, died May 4, 2007, age 28. Son of Myra (Levine) Harris and the late Jonathan Harris. Graduate of Columbia in 2001, Arthur was the Press Secretary for Andrew Cuomo. Survived by his mother, Myra, brother, Robbie, and many loving friends and relatives. Donations can be sent to a charity of your choice. Services were conducted by Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809.
