
        FELMAN--Rabbi Meir, 93. On July 27. Beloved father of Yehudi, Naomi and Shevi and 9 grandchildren.Formerly Rabbi of Judea Center in Brooklyn, NY. Major supporter of Yeshiva University, N.Y. , and Mevareset Zion Yeshiva in Israel. Funeral on Sunday, July 30 at Shomrei Hadas Chapel, 3803 14th Ave, Brooklyn, N.Y. at 2:00 P.M. Burial in Israel on Monday, July 31. May all his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
        $(6$)FELMAN--Rabbi Meir . Yeshiva University and the Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary (RIETS) family are saddened by the passing of a distinguished rabbi and community leader, husband of the late Tova Felman, father of Shevi (and Milton) Cohen, Naomi (and Dr. Norman) Bloom, Dr. Yehudi M. (and Brenda) Felman. A four-time alumnus of Yeshiva University, recipient of an honorary degree from YU, and former President of RIETS' Rabbinic Alumni, Rabbi Felman and his wife were YU Guardians, established a scholarship at RIETS, and dedicated a classroom in Zysman Hall. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the children and grandchildren. May they all be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Rabbi Julius Berman Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees
