
        SCHUSTER-Wally. Of New York and Las Vegas. Retired music publisher. Dearest son of the late songwriter Ira Schuster and Minnie Schuster. Beloved husband of Elaine. Devoted father of Peter, Jason and Michael. Loving grandfather of Maisie. Adored brother of Phoebe & Leonard Lewin. Cherished uncle of John & Lynn and Jamie and Lee Lewin. Dear brother-in-law of Jackie & Gary Barnett. Fond cousin and friend of many. At the time of his death, he was a member of ASCAP Songwriters Guild of America and the Academy of Popular Music. Services were held in California on June 3,1997. You will never be forgotten.
