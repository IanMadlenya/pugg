
        KULCSAR-John J., M.D. On April 18, 2000. Past President of the Medical Board and Senior Attending Physician at Cabrini Medical Center, mentor, colleague and friend to past and present members of the medical and nursing staff. Devoted friend of the Sisters of the Sacred Heart. Beloved husband of Esperanza (nee Tavarro). Devoted father of Roy. Loving father-inlaw of Karen (nee Talay). Cherished grandfather of Christopher, Spencer and Justin. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Avenue (at 20th St), NYC, Thursday and Friday, 2-5, 7-9PM. Funeral service, Saturday 9AM at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
        KULCSAR-John, M.D. The Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart and the medical staff of Cabrini Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague, past Medical Staff President, and friend. Dr. Kulcsar, who served our institution for more than 50 years was a dedicated physician who always showed sincere concern for his patients and for Cabrini Medical Center. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife and family. Maurizio Daliana, M.D. President, Medical Staff Morton Davidson, M.D. Medical Director Raymond LaRaja, M.D., Director of Surgery Jeffrey Frerichs, President Cabrini Medical Center
