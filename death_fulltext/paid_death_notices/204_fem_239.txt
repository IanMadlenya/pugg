
        KAHN--Elizabeth Marie Heslin, 86, of Mamaroneck and Palm Beach, died Saturday January 17, 2004 in NYC. Widow of Irving Berlin Kahn, she was born December 24, 1917 in NYC, daughter of the late Matthew and Elizabeth (Gabor) Heslin. She is survived by her two daughters Ruth Kahn of Brooklyn and Jean Kahn of NYC. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her name to the charity of your choice dealing with Alzheimers, Parkinsons, or animal safety and rescue.
