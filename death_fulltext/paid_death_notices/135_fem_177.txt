
        COONEY-Margaret B. Formerly of Summit, New Jersey, died on April 16th, 1998 in Palm Beach Gardens, FL. She was the beloved wife of the late Vincent T. Cooney, and the loving mother of Mary Ellen Bowes of Mountainside, NJ, Regina Payne of Jupiter, FL, and Clare Ford of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Until shortly before her death, Margaret was energetic and healthy, enjoying her poodle, Marco Polo, her water aerobics, and her love of reading.
