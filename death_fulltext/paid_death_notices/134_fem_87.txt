
        WHITE-Elizabeth M., 83. Of Hilton Head Island and formerly of Darien died on Saturday, March 7th, at the Hilton Head Medical Center and Clinics. She was born in Pittsburgh, PA and was the daughter of the late J.D. and Elizabeth Schillenberg McGinnis. She moved to Hilton Head Island in 1997 from Darien, CT. She retired as President of Leahy Archives in New York. She was active in national archive associations. She was a member of Weeburn Country Club in Darien and Country Club of Hilton Head. She was a previous owner of the world famous yacht ''Bolero'' which held the record for the Bermuda Race for many years. She is survived by her husband of 33 years, Charles M. White and a brother, Joseph McGinnis of Bradenton, FL. Her first husband Emmett Leahy died in 1964. Services will be held later in Darien, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Heart Association. For more information, contact the Island Funeral Home of Hilton Head Island, S.C.
