
        FREIBERGER-Margaret Flora Forsyth. Age 84, of Holmes Beach, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died March 29, in Bradenton, FL. She was a retired office manager fo the Ruben H. Donnelly Co. Widow of Edward Freiberger. She is survived by a son, Peter of Santa Monica, CA., and daughters Jeanne T. Roche of Holmes Beach, FL., and Roberta Lewis of Wooster, MA, four grandchildren and two great-granddaughters. Memorial service will be Sunday, April 13, 9:00 A.M., at The First Presbyterian Church, 124 Henry Street, Brooklyn, New York.
