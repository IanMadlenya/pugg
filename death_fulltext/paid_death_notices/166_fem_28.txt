
        FEIDELSEIT-Lilli (nee Bernstein). Age 93 of Brooklyn, passed away on November 2nd. Daughter of Sarah Ehrenreich (1875-1957) and Harris J. Bernstein (1871-1920). Sister of Michael (1899-1981), Murray (1903-31), and Celia (1909-17). Loving wife of her childhood sweetheart, Harry (d. 1979), proprietor of the former Prospect Pharmacy on Prospect Park SW. Mother of Jay Fields (1930-76). Survived by her loving daughter Marilyn Landau, her grandchildren, Melissa and Brian, and her great-grandchildren, David and Amelia Strauss. She will be sorely missed by her family, and her many friends from the park on East 18th Street.
