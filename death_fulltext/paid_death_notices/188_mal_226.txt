
        WIESNER-Louis A. Passed away September 20, 2002, at the age of 86, in the company of his family. Loving husband of Elizabeth, father to Jonathan, Liz, Meg, and Andrew, grandfather to Britta and Spencer, and father-in-law to Mary Ann and Dave. He will be remembered as a committed humanitarian who worked tirelessly on behalf of refugees around the world for the past 35 years, first as a Career Officer with the Department of State, and then later as a member of the Board and Executive Committee of the International Rescue Committee. A service will be held at Christ Church in North Conway, NH. Donations in his memory can be made to the International Rescue Committee, 122 East 42nd St, NY, NY 10168, www.theirc.org.
