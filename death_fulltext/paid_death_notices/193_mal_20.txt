
        KREIDMAN-Julius. Age 86. Beloved husband of Eleanor, whose loyalty and caring during his lifetime and in his time of need was instinctive, died Sunday, January 26, 2003 at the Hudson View Nursing Home. He leaves his loving daughter, Tina Kelly and her husband, Peter and was predeceased by his beloved son, Richard Kreidman. He is also survived by two step daughters, Cindy and Carol and three grandchildren, Sarah, Russell and Christian whom he loved dearly. Donations may be made to the Leukemia Society, or to the Alzheimers Association.
