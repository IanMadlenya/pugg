
        SHULMAN-Alan, 87. Composer and cellist, died peacefully July 10, 2002 in Hudson, NY. Survivors include Jay Shulman of Claverack, NY; Laurie Shulman of Dallas, TX; Marc Shulman of Manhattan; and Lisa Shulman. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Violoncello Society, 340 W. 55 St., NY NY 10019, or Maverick Concerts, P.O. Box 102, Woodstock, NY 12498.
