
        ZINTL - BRYANT--Leola. A longtime resident of Bronxville, NY, Leola died of cancer on Friday, April 27th. She was the widow of Robert T. Zintl, and Francis L. Bryant Jr., mother to Colby and Anna Zintl, sister to Lisa Burrowes, and gleeful grandmother of Joseph, India and Jack Markus. Services will be at Christ Church in Bronxville on Friday, May 4th at 1pm. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Robert and Leola Zintl Memorial Fund at the University of Pennsylvania. Checks payable to ''Trustees of the University of Pennsylvania'', School of the Arts and Sciences; 3440 Market Street, Suite 300; Philadelphia, PA 191043325. Attention: Meaghan Hogan.
