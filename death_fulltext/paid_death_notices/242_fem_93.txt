
        RICHARD--Jane S., 87, of Vero Beach, FL and York Harbor, ME, formerly of Brookville, NY died on Friday, March 9th, 2007 at Indian River Medical Center in Vero Beach. A loving wife, mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother, she is survived by her beloved husband of 65 years, H. Van B. Richard, her daughter Phyllis Fritts, sonin-law Guy A. Fritts, daughter-in-law Virginia R. Richard, five grandchildren: James and Garret Fritts; Zachary, Travis and Cecily Richard and one great-grandson, Kai Fritts. She was predeceased by her son Peter L. Richard. A graduate of The Ethel Walker School in Simsbury, CT she was a talented interior decorator, passionate gardener and loyal friend. A Memorial Service is planned in the Chapel of Trinity Episcopal Church, 2365 Pine Avenue, Vero Beach, FL 32960 on Saturday, March 17th at 3:30 pm. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Alzheimer/Parkinson Association of Indian River County, 2501 27th Avenue, Vero Beach, FL 32960.
