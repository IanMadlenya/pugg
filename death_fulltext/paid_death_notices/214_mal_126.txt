
        LEVY--Rabbi Theodore S., beloved & cherished husband of Ina Rae Burdman Levy; devoted and adoring father of Seth, Dr. Cynthia and Jonathan and his wife Karen. Proud grandfather of Jacob, Sarah, Leah, Dori and Zachary Levy. Loving brother of Fredda Levy Stan and brother-in-law of Ethan Stang, B. Richard and Babette Burdman and Marsha Burdman, Rabbi Emeritus of Temple Society of Concord, Syracuse, N.Y. Passed away peacefully at his home on Hilton Head Island, S.C. on November 11, 2004. Funeral services will be at 2 PM Friday, November 12th at Congregation Beth Yam, Hilton Head Island. A graveside burial service will be held at 2 PM Sunday at Coitsville Cemetery in Youngstown, Ohio conducted by Rabbi Frank Muller and Rabbi Joseph Shoenberger. Memorial contributions may be made to The Rabbi Theodore S. Levy Endowed Fellowship at The American Jewish Archives, Hebrew Union College, 3101 Clifton Avenue, Cincinnati, OH 45220 or the College of Charleston, Jewish Studies Program c/o Dr. Martin Perlmutter, 96 Wentworth Street, Charleston, S.C. 29424.
