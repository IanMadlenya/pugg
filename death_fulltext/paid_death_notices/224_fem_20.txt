
        LEVITAN - Geordie. Died peacefully September, 3, 2005, surrounded by her family after a long, courageous battle with cancer. She was the beloved daughter of George and Florence Levitan, mother of Brooke, partner of Warren and sister of Carol, Barbara and Joanne. She was a friend to too many to name, and loved by all who knew her. She will be greatly missed for her beauty, generosity, spirit, wit, fearlessness and honesty. Her service will be held on Tuesday, September 6, 2005, at 10 am, at John J. Fox Funeral Home, 2080 Boston Post Rd, Larchmont, NY 10538.
