
        MARLOW--Marilyn E. 75, of NYC, died on August 25th, 2003. Beloved daughter of Clifford and Ella Marlow of Waterloo, IA. B.A. University of Northern Iowa, M.A. English, University of Michigan. Executive Vice President, Curtis Brown Ltd. literary agency. She devoted her career to children's authors. Dear sister of William Marlow of Arlington, VA, and Joan Foley of Schoolcraft, MI. Devoted aunt of 11 nieces and nephews and 23 grandnieces and nephews. Memorial Mass at St. Mary's Catholic Church, Waterloo, IA, on September 20, 10:30 A.M. A memorial service will be held at a later date in NYC. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations can be made to Greene Public Library, Juvenile Department, 231 Traer Street West, Greene, IA 50636.
