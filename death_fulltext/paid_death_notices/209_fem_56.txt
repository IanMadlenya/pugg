
        GALLAGHER -- Mary Ann (nee O'Shea). Wife of 57 years to the late Thomas F. Gallagher, died on June 6 at her home in New Canaan, CT. Her three children were at her side. She graduated from Hunter College in 1939 and worked as a bacteriologist in Walter Reed Army Hospital during WW II. She is survived by her three children: Paul of Easton CT; Joan of New Canaan CT; Peter of NYC; and grandchildren Sarah, Timothy, Katherine, Caroline, James and Kathryn. Wake Hoyt Funeral Home, 190 Main St, New Canaan, CT, Wednesday June 9, 4-8pm. Funeral Mass at St. Aloysius R.C. Church, New Canaan, Thursday June 10, at 10am. Donations in her memory may be made to Alzheimers Association, NYC Chapter, 360 Lexington Ave, NY 10017.
