
        DREIFUSS -- Robert Leon. Swiss born, dynamic, entrepreneur. Attended the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania and was the President of R.L. Dreifuss, Inc. Mr. Dreifuss served as a member of the Community Board 8 in NYC. He loved his family which included his three daughters, Danielle Butin, Marguerite Gelfman, Suzanne Dreifuss, son-in-laws, Mitchell Butin and Peter Gelfman, his sisters Juliette Braunschweig and Lis Gaines, his grandchildren Sam, Caroline, Justine, Max and Sally, and his cherished nieces and nephews. He lived his life to the fullest and will be missed by many. Services were held Sunday at Frank E. Campbell, with burial at Sharon Gardens.
