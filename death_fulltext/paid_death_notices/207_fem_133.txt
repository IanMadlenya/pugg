
        VOGEL--Sandy, formerly of Manhattan, died Friday, April 2nd, at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, NY following open heart surgery. For many years an executive producer of TV commercials, she retired in 1998 to her home in Montauk, NY. Devoted sister, beloved aunt and great-aunt, world traveler and friend to many. A celebration of her life will be held Friday, April 23rd, at the Wainwright House, Rye, NY, at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her name to Concerned Citizens of Montauk, PO Box 915, Montauk, NY 11954.
