
        KING-Donald Nelson. Age 66. Of Mantoloking, NJ, passed away Thursday, September 14, in Darby, Montana. Donald was a graduate of Columbia University. He served as Managing Director for J. P. Morgan Investments, and Managing Director for Trust Company of West. He was a member of the Bay Head Yacht Club, a Korean War veteran and an avid fisherman. Surviving include his wife Patricia; daughters Christina McCarthy, Dorothy H. King and Karen P. Moran; granddaughter Katie Magee; mother Alfhild King; two brothers and one sister.
