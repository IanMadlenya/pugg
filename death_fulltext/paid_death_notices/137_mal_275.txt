
        FRIEDMAN-Chester Sydney. On June 26, 1998 in his 91st year. Loving husband of Rayminnie. Beloved brother of Gertrude Glaubman. Adored father of Joseph and Rita Aron. Beloved grandfather of Michael and David Friedman and Jonathan and Timothy Aron. Graveside service June 28th, 1998. Contributions to the Women's Auxiliary of Temple Emanu-El.
        FRIEDMAN-Chester. The Officers and members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of their dear friend and fellow member, Chester Friedman. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Rayminnie, to his children and other members of his family. Robert K. Low, President
