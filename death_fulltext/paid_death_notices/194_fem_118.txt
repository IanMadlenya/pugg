
        GOLDEN-Inez Ruth. 87. Formerly of New York, died March 1, 2003 in Phoenix, AZ. A warm and caring wife of 60 years to the late Jack, mother, grandmother and great grandmother, her love of life was inspiring and contagious. Her daughter Olivia Cupp, her son, Ken Golden, grandchildren Roger, Melissa and Jared, and two great-grandchildren Haley and Isabella, survive her. Burial was in the Phoenix Memorial Park Cemetery.
