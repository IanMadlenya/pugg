
        STRAUSS - Annette Greenfield. The staff at Sotheby's is deeply saddened by the loss of The Honorable Annette Greenfield Strauss, the mother of our dear colleague, Nancy Strauss Halbreich. Mrs. Strauss was the first woman elected mayor of Dallas, she served two terms from 1987-1991. As an active member of hte city's cultural and political affairs, her efforts earned Dallas the distinction of Best City for Business'' in 1989. Mrs. Strauss is survived by her husband of fifty-two years and their two daughters. The family asks that memorials be directed to Family Gateway, TACA and the DAllas Symphony Association.
