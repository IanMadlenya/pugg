
        GREGORY-Chon, 73, Chappaqua, NY. Beloved father of Susan, Judith and Pamela and loving grandfather of Patrick, Molly, Dorothy and Solomon, died peacefully on Sunday, December 31, 2000. His cherished wife Dorothy died on December 9, 2000. A memorial service to honor Chon and Dorothy will be held on February 17, 2001 at 1 PM at the Community Center in Chappaqua, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in memory of Dorothy and Chon Gregory to Doctors Without Borders, 6 East 39 St, 8 Floor, NY, New York 10016, 1-888-392-0392.
