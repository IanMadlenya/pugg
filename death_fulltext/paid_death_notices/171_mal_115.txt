
        SECUNDA-Louis, C.P.A. At rest in Palo Alto, CA. April 3, 2001. Beloved husband of 58 yrs to the Late Bernice Secunda. He leaves behind daughter Judy (Larry) William, son Al Secunda, 2 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. A native and 92 year resident of New York City, he was an alumnus of the Brooklyn Hebrew Orphan Asylum. (323) 654-8821 for info.
