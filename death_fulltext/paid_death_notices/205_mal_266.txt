
        SCHWEITZER--Paul, 96, beloved and wise New York City teacher and Fordham professor, died peacefully Wednesday at his home in Pelham, N.Y. His wife Eloise, seven children and 13 grandchildren rejoice in his superbly-lived life, and give thanks for his wisdom, love and generosity of spirit. Visitation Pelham Funeral Home, Thursday and Friday 2-4 and 7-9. Funeral Mass Saturday, February 21 at 10AM at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham. N.Y.
