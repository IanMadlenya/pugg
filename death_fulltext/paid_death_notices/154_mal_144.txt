
        BLISH-Sylvester, age 82, died Thursday, November 11, 1999 at Stamford Hospital. Born October 1, 1917, he was predeceased by his wife Marite Orteig Blish, and survived by his children Marguerite Coffey of Bridgwater, CT, Suzanne Sclafani and Sharon LaGreca of Darien, CT, Cindy Philpott of Holmdel, NJ, Paul Blish of Norwark and Nanette Blish of Rowayton, CT, 14 grandchildren, a greatgrandson, and sisters Kathrene Blish Houlihan and Elizabeth Blish. A Lt. Commander in the U.S. Navy during WWII, he was retired from Domtar Inc. Mass of Christian burial today 10:00 at St. Leo's Church, Stamford, CT. Information: Leo P. Gallagher and Son Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St., Stamford, CT.
