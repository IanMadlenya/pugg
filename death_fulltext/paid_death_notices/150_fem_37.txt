
        KURTZ-Mary Kathryn Lynch Devoted wife of the late Maestro Efrem Kurtz, passed away after a long illness at her home in London on June 27th, 1999. Mrs. Kurtz, originally from Houston, TX. was a loving aunt to her two nephews, John Wyatt and Richard Wyatt, and to her other nephews, nieces, grand-nieces and grand-nephews. Through her passionate involvement in the arts, she enriched the lives of so many. An extraordinary lady, a thoughtful and caring friend, she will always live in our memory.
