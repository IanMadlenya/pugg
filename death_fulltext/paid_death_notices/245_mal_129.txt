
        FEDER--Walter, M.D. The Yeshiva University/Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary family mourns the passing of a distinguished cardiologist, admired scholar of rabbinics and Jewish History, who served on the Board of Directors of the Bernard Revel Graduate School of Jewish Studies, and with his wife, Cecile, was a YU Guardian. Heartfelt condolences are extended to Cecile Feder, a former academic leader of Stern College for Women; to his children: Martin (and Danielle) Feder, Roslyn (and Marvin) Lipsky, and Carole (and Noach) Simon; to his cherished siblings: Doris Feder Getzler; Yeshiva College Board member, Rabbi Aaron (and Shirley) Feder; and to his beloved grandchildren. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor and Rosh HaYeshiva Morry Weiss, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Julius Berman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees Mordecai Katz, Chairman BRGS Board of Directors
