
        SNYDER--Dr. Arthur I. A memorial service for Dr. Arthur I. Snyder will be held at 2:30 pm, on Tuesday, April 13, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st Street.
        SNYDER--Arthur, M.D. The Medical Board of the New York Presbyterian Hospital on behalf of the physicians and staff of the Hospital is saddened by the loss of Arthur Snyder, M.D. He earned both his A.B. and M.D. at Columbia and joined the faculty of the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons in the division of Rheumatology in 1979. He was a true friend, a wonderful colleague and will be missed by all of us as well as the many patients he cared for over 46 years in practice as an internist and rheumatologist. Memorial Services are to be held Tuesday, April 13 at 2:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. Donations in lieu of flowers may be sent to the Arthritis Foundation, New York Chapter, 122 E. 42nd St., 18th Flr, NY, NY 10168.
