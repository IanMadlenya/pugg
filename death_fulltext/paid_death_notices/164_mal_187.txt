
        ROSENBERG-Philip. Died on September 4, 2000, at age 79. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Devoted father of son, Eric, and his wife, Linda, son, Douglas, and daughter, Amy. Loving grandfather of Kyle. Dear brother and cherished uncle and grand-uncle of Diane and Nicole. He was a graduate of New York University. As a parishioner of St. Helen's R.C. Church in Howard Beach, he was a proud and active member of the Holy Name Society. Mr Rosenberg was co-founder and former president of BPC Management Corp. Mass of the Resurrection today, 9:45 AM, at St. Helen's R.C. Church in Howard Beach, Queens. Interment St. Charles Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. It was Philip's wish that memorial contributions be made to St. Helen's R.C. Church, PC Support Group, 157-10 83rd St., Howard Beach, NY 11414.
