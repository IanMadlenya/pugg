
        UNGER--Edith (nee Protter). Beloved wife of the late Sidney B. Unger, passed away quietly on June 14, 2004 at her home in Queens, NY. Devoted daughter of Helena and Ignaz Protter, loving sister of Eric Protter, cherished mother of the late Philip S. Unger, and Carol Wolfson. Adoring grandmother of Jennifer Unger (Jeff Hamilton), Merie (Scott Mefferd), Rachel and Leah Wolfson, proud great grandmother of Mariah and Cassadie Mefferd. Dear aunt of Barbara (Frank Simon), Philip M. Unger, Lisa (Duke Brannock), and Arpine (Andrew Stein). Loving sister-inlaw of Rhoda and Melvin Unger. Mrs. Unger attended Hunter College and pursued a career in textile and jewelry design. In lieu of flowers, contact Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel: 718 263-7600. Funeral services Wednesday 12:30 P.M. at Schwartz Brothers, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
