
        POLLET-Benjamin. Died January 12. Beloved husband of Estelle (nee Kubrin) for 61 years. Loving father of Michael and Marian Sue and grandfather of Steven, Merryl, Erik, Alison and Jeremy. Sixty plus years as a caring, dedicated family physician to generations in the Ridgewood and Flatbush sections of Brooklyn. He will long be remembered for his care and skill and his devotion to the healing of thousands.
