
        NEVAI - Bela B. founder of NEVCO, died at 88, Dec. 6th, 1996 in New York City. After ''retiring'' in 1972 he became an investor and philanthropist. When asked his profession he jokingly called himself ''just a golfer.'' Bill Nevai was loved and cherished by all for his wide interests and great knowledge. His wit and good humor brought fun into many lives. Bill Nevai's wife and business partner of 60 years, Clara, will miss him most. He is survived by his sons, Andras and Peter De, grandson, Nandor, sister lionka Sole and all the members of their families. He will be remembered forever.
