
        PATTULLO-G. Robson ''Rob'' died peacefully on February 3, 2000 in New York City. Cherished friend and husband of Susan. Beloved father of Claire. Devoted son of Elizabeth and Pat Pattullo, brother of Betsy, Ann, Greg and Ronald. Uncle of Jess, Gabe, Alissa and Sasha. Associated for nearly 25 years with ABC News. A joyous light in the lives of all who knew him. Funeral services will be held in Winchester, MA. A memorial celebration in New York City will follow. Donations in Rob's memory may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Easthester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
        PATTULLO - George Robson. Your joy, kindness, and love are part of us. T.T.F.N. Ann, Ronald, Alissa, Sasha
