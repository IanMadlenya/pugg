
        GREENBERG-Herbert. Of Great Neck, New York on Saturday, July 25, 1998. Beloved husband of Rosalie ''Mickey'' Brown; loving father of Edward ''Woody'', Betsy Feinberg (Robert), Amy Poster (Robert), and Susan Piper (Gordon); adored grandfather of Cara Feinberg, Guy Greenberg, and Joanna, Tracy and Michael Piper; devoted son of the late Charles and Jennie Greenberg; brother of Gladys Goldstein (Murray), the late Leo Greenberg, and the late Rose Rabbino; loving uncle and dear friend. Former Chairman and CEO of Charles Greenberg & Sons, Inc.; Captain in the U.S. Army during World War II; dedicated and active leader of many philanthropic and community organizations. Funeral will be held at Temple Beth El of Great Neck, NY on Monday, July 27 at 1:30 PM.
        GREENBERG-Herbert. The Flag Officers, Board of Governors and the entire membership of the Knickerbocker Yacht Club mourn the passing of their Past Commodore and lifelong friend, Herbert Greenberg. Herb was in every sense a true gentleman, dedicated member and accomplished yachtsman. He will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mickey and the entire Greenberg family. Jonathan N. Helfat, Commodore Knickerbocker Yacht Club
        GREENBERG-Herbert. Service Guild joins our beloved member Mickey Greenberg in mourning the loss of her dear husband Herbert. Our hearts reach out to the entire family. Lynn Halperin, Co-President Linda Stillman, Co-President
