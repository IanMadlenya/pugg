
        LAWRENCE--Florence W. On March 23. Adored wife of both the late Lewis R. Lawrence, M.D., and the late Samuel Rosenfeld, M.D., and cherished sister of the late Paul E. Wilson, M.D. Treasured by her children, Lynn and Jeffrey and their spouses Michael and Glenda. Beloved by all who met her for her gentle, sweet way, her immeasurable kindness and caring. Her generosity and goodness knew no bounds. A women of tremendous courage, who faced life's adversities head on with resilience and acceptance. We are grateful for the time we had her but her loss leaves a forever hole in all our hearts. Services Sunday, 1:00 PM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 N. Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
