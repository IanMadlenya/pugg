
        MURPHY-Alvina E. October 24, 2002, at age 97. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Willis A. Murphy. She was a nurse, advisor and compassionate person who cared about all living things. Her friends celebrate her long life and mourn their loss. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, October 29, 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, October 30, 11 AM, at The Church of St. Ignatius Loyola, Park Avenue at 84th Street, NYC.
