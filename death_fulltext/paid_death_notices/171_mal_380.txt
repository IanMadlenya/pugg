
        SWIRSKY-Philip. Monroe Township, NJ, 87, Friday, at the New Jersey Veterans Memorial Home at Menlo Park, NJ. Born in Poland, he resided in Brooklyn, NY, before moving to Bayonne, NJ. In the late 1970s he and his wife moved to the Clearbrook Community where he currently resided. Mr. Swirsky had a distinguished career in the U.S. Army, where he served for over 20 years. Formerly employed as a quality control engineer in the fashion industry. A lifetime member of the Retired Officers Association and the Reserve Officers Association of the United States, he dedicated himself to serving his Jewish community and was actively involved in supporting the activities of B'nai B'rith, the AntiDefamation League and Beth El Synagogue of East Windsor. Survived by his wife of 53 years, Jessie Leibowitz Swirsky of Clearbrook; son and daughter-in-law, Daniel and Nancy Swirsky of Princeton Junction; brother and sister-in-law, Sheldon and Linda Swirsky of Cedarhurst, NY; and sister, Irene Lipschitz of Delray Beach, FL. For information contact A.S. Cole Funeral Home, 22 No. Main St., Cranbury, NJ, 609395-0770. Interment Washington Cemetery, Monmouth Jct., NJ.
