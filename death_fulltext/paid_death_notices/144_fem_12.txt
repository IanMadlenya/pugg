
        SPIGLAND-Sheilah. The Auxiliary of Montefiore Medical Center is deeply saddened by the passing of its member, Sheilah Spigland. Sheilah was a dedicated volunteer who worked in the gift shop as well as in the library. She gave unstintingly of her time in the library and donated many, many books. We will miss her energy, devotion and her very warm and friendly manner. Our deepest sympathy to her beloved husband Dr. Ilya Spigland, her two daughters Nitsana and Karen and her son Ethan. Auxiliary of Montefiore Medical Center Ruth Saporta, President Mary Goldsmith, Secretary
