
        BAUM-Leuba. Beloved wife of the late Ralph. Devoted mother of the late Carol. Dear sister of Si Pavelle. Cherished aunt of many nieces and nephews. Co-founder with her husband, Ralph, of the Judaica Museum at the Hebrew Home in Riverdale, N.Y. Service today, 12 Noon at Hunter Cemetery. Memorial service Wednesday, August 27, at the Judaica Museum.
        BAUM-Leuba. The Boards of Directors of the Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale and The Judaica Museum mourn with sorrow the death of Leuba Baum. We extend our sincerest sympathies to her brother Si Pazelle, nieces and nephews, and in blessed memory of her late daughter Carol Gitta Baum-Jarrett. Leuba's vision and intelligence guided our staff since she-along with her late husband Ralph-founded the museum in 1982. She was loved and will be missed. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel Reingold, Executive Vice President Karen S. Franklin, Director, The Judaica Museum
