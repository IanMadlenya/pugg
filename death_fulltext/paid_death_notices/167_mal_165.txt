
        KEENAN-Thomas F. The Officers, staff and members of the Newspaper Guild of New York mourn the passing of Tom Keenan, who served as a champion of working people during his many years of involvement in our Union. In addition to working as a Local Representative, Tom served seven terms as Guild Chairperson at The New York Times Unit with great determination and integrity. His wide smile and friendly handshake will be terribly missed by all those who were honored to know him. Barry F. Lipton, President William J. O'Meara, Secy/Treas. Peter Szekely, Local Chairperson
