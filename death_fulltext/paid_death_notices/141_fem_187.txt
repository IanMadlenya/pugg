
        SCHNITZLER-Lucille H. Died on October 20, 1998 at the age of 92. Beloved daughter of the late Harry and Bertha Wilkus. Wife of the late Laurence Schnitzler who she married on October 20, 1929. She was the dear sister of the late Doris Miller, Ruth Rosenweig, Teddy and William Wilkus. Survived by her daughter Barbara Goldman (Mark), two grandchildren, 4 great grandchildren. Services will be 12:30 Wednesday, October 21st, Beth El Synagogue, New Rochelle.
