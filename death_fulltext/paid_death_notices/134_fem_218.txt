
        HEIDEN - Stella Goldberger. Passed away on March 22, 1998. Born June 21, 1903. Married to the late Dr. Lionel Heiden. Beloved ''Aunt'' Stella to dozens, piano and music teacher to hundreds, degrees from Damrosch-Juilliard and Columbia University. Taught at Horace Mann, Julia Richman, and Washington Irving. Avid sailor and swimmer. Board member of Bloomingdale House of Music, Secretary of the UFT Retired Teachers Chapter for 25 years. Funeral service at Gramercy Chapel, 1 PM on March 24th. Donations to Bloomingdale House of Music. We will miss her.
