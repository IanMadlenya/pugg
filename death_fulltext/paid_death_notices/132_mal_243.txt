
        SCHREIBMAN-Alex a.k.a. Allan Small. 87, died Saturday in his home at Boca Raton, FL. He was a noted pianist, composer, arranger and music teacher. He is survived by his wife, Lucille and three children. Graveside service will be held Tuesday, January 20, at New Montefiore Cemetery in Pinelawn, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to a charity of your choice. For information call I.J. Morris Funeral Home, Inc., 1-800624-8282.
