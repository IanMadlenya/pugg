
        KRAMER-Sam. The Knitgoods Workers Union Local 155, UNITE! sadly acknowledges the passing of a founding member of our Local. He was a long time labor activist, an advocate for social causes, and a fighter for workers rights. He served for many years as a Board Member of our Union. He will be sorely missed. With expression of sympathy and understanding to the family, we deeply mourn his passing. Joseph Lombardo Manager-Secretary Knitgoods Workers Union Local 155, UNITE!
