
        ZIEGLER--Theodore E. The General Society of Mechanics & Tradesmen of the City of New York mourns the passing of our Past President (1984) who joined the Society in 1978 and served on several committees from 1978 through 1994. He was an alumni of the Mechanics Institute. He is survived by his children: Richard, John and Linda Ziegler, and Virginia Burt, and Dolores Mayo, and eight grandchildren. The thoughts and prayers of his Society brothers are with his family and friends. Viewing: Anderson and Campbell Funeral Home, Manchester, NJ on Thursday, September 23rd, 2-4 and 5-7 P.M. Funeral will be at 10 A.M. Friday, September 24th, at St. John's R.C. Church in Lakehurst, NJ.
