
        NEWCOMBE-Richard. 58, of Germantown, MD, on March 9, 2000. Beloved of his wife Anne of 37 years, daughter Ellen, grandchildren Joshua and Adrianna of Pittsburgh, PA, his brothers Peter of Parrish, FL, and Christopher of Wynnewood, PA and their families, and friends. Dr. Newcombe, a geographer, worked as an operations research analyst for the U.S. Department of Energy, an energy analyst at Brookhaven National Laboratory (NY) and in private consulting in Maryland and Illinois. A memorial service will be held at M. H. Barber Funeral Home, 21525 Laytonsville Rd., Laytonsville, MD, Saturday, March 18, 2:00 PM.
