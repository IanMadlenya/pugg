
        NELSON-Peter Dowd. On January 2, 2001. Beloved and best friend of Bette. Loving and devoted father of Michael. Father-in-law of Jennifer. Adoring grandfather of Jake and Katharine. Close brother of Robert and John and sisters-inlaw Lyntha and Eileen. Commercial real estate broker, executive director at Cushman & Wakefield for over 39 years. Active member of the Board of the Greater New York Councils Boy Scouts of America with over 25 years of service. He was a friend to all who met him; a gentle and caring man. He will be profoundly missed. Memorial service on January 8th at 11 AM, Middle Collegiate Church, 50 East 7th Street, NYC.
        NELSON-Peter D. The Board and staff of the Greater New York Councils, Boy Scouts of America extend their deepest sympathy to the family of our longtime Director, Peter D. Nelson. For over twenty-five years, Peter was dedicated to creating opportunities for young men and women through scouting programs in New York City. Our thoughts and prayers go to his best friend Bette, his son and daughter-in-law Michael and Jennifer, his beloved grandchildren and to all of his colleagues at Cushman & Wakefield. John F.
