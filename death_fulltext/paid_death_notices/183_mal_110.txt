
        GORMAN-Jack. Passed away at 87 years of age on April 5, 2002, in Boca Raton, FL, formerly of NYC. Born in Brooklyn, NY, to the late Anna and Wolf Goichman, brother of Aaron, and the late Mac, Al and Rose. Devoted father to Linda and Doug Weintraub, adoring and proud grandfather to Abby and Richard, his ''favorite grandchildren.'' Husband of his dear, late Ethel, the late Muriel and loving companion to Henrietta Wiener. Jack was a dear friend to so many. As a partner and senior vice president of La Salle Industries, one of the largest lithography companies in NYC, he was an extraordinary businessman. He retired at the age of 80, making his home in Boca Raton, FL, where he played golf, bridge, and had a multitude of new and old friends. Having been the UJA Man of the Year in the printing industry, he continued his philantrophy by donating to many diverse charities. Jack loved his life and we loved him so dearly. Services 11:15 AM, on Wednesday, April 10, 2002, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave and 76th St, NYC. Contributions to the charity of your choice would be appreciated. A memorial service will be held in Florida at a later date to celebrate Jack's incredible life.
