
        KARTEN--Morton K. Innovative marketeer and industrialist from Manhattan Beach, NY, died at his Boca Raton, FL home January 30, 2006. Beloved by so many for his unique spirit, intellect and wit; generous with his wisdom and kindness. Deeply committed to service of country through the Army Counter Intelligence Corps during and after WWII. For decades, embraced Haiti as his home, its culture and artwork his passion, building business in support of the Caribbean Basin Initiative his life's work. His love carries forth in all whose lives he's touched, especially his adoring wife Susan, his devoted son James and wife Cynthia, and his loving niece Margo, nephew Harris and their families. His vision and guidance live on in those he has inspired and encouraged over the course of a long and gratifying life.
