
        KANTROWITZ--Marilyn C., died peacefully at home on August 31, 2003. Beloved wife of Ira; mother of Jeremy; mother-in-law of Sandra; grandmother of Elizabeth and Thomas; sister of Ann Bailey Pinkham; aunt of David, Gregory, Sarah and Matthew Johnston, Lucy Frank and David Kane. Her remarkable beauty, grace, generosity and strength will be profoundly missed by her family and friends.
