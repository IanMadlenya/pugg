
        LEEDS-Richard L. Passed away May 11, 2001 in Camp Hill, PA. He was 88 and a former resident of Bronxville, N.Y. Survived by sons Richard L., Jr. of Mechanicsburg, PA and Thomas F. of Medfield, MA., daughter Linda L. Scott of San Juan Bautista, CA., seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren. He attended Yale University and was a graduate of the Univerity of Arizona. He was the devoted husband of the late Mary Louise Gilchrist Leeds, a member of the Reformed Church of Bronxville and retired as Publisher for Blessings Corporation. Services private. Donations may be made to the Multiple Sclerosis Society of Westchester County.
