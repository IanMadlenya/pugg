
        JAMES-Henry Jr. Librarian Emeritus of Sweet Briar, Virginia, died January 21 in North Branford, CT. He graduated from Yale University in 1940 and received his Master's degrees from Stanford and Columbia Universities. In retirement he was an alumni activist for Yale University and a docent at the Yale Center for British Art. He is survived by a daughter Emily Jones of Newtown, CT., and brothers Daniel and Philip James, both of New York City.
