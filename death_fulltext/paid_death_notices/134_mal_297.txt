
        KEAVY-William T., M.D. The Lenox Hill Hospital family expresses it sorrow at the passing of William T. Keavy, M.D., a distinguished member of our Plastic Surgery staff for twenty-seven years. Dr. Keavy was nationally and internationally renowned for his exceptional skill as a plastic and reconstructive surgeon. Throughout his remarkable career, his deep commitment to his patients and to Lenox Hill Hospital was unwavering. A devoted & gifted educator who took great pleasure in sharing his knowledge and love of surgery with young physicians, Dr. Keavy impressed all who knew him with his kindness, compassion and integrity. Lenox Hill Hospital honors the memory of this truly remarkable physician who exemplified the highest standards of his profession. We extend our deep sympathies to his family and friends. James S. Marcus, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George President and CEO Stephen A. Obstbaum, M.D., President, Medical Board Norman H. Schulman, M.D. Chief, Section of Plastic Surgery
