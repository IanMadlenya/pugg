
        ROSS-Libbie. Of Merrick, NY, October 19, 2002, at age 89. Beloved wife of Jesse, devoted mother of David (Carol) Ross and Laura (Jeffrey) Blumenfeld, adored grandmother of Peter (Maria Grazia) and Jeremy Ross, Jennifer (Graham) Schwarz and Joshua Blumenfeld. Loving sister to Nettie Schnure, and loving aunt to many neices and nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. Her sweetness, wisdom, and warmth were treasured by all who knew and loved her, and she will be deeply missed.
