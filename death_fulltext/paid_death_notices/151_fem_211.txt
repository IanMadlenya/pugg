
        WAYSEK-Helene C., Dr. Hofstra University is profoundly saddened by the death of Dr. Helene C. Waysek, Associate Professor of Comparative Literature & Language and Coordinator of the University Honors Program. Dr. Waysek, a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Douglass College, received an M.A. and Ph.D. from Columbia University. She was an active member of the Hofstra faculty for 36 years, teaching Latin, Greek and comparative literature and serving as Chair of Comparative Literature & Languages for 16 years. In addition to her duties as coordinator of the University Honors Program she served as secretary of the Hofstra Chapter of Phi Beta Kappa. A viewing will be held at the Allwood Funeral Home, 660 Allwood Ave, Clifton, NJ Wednesday, August 25, 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM. For information call 973-773-6666. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Dr. Helene C. Waysek Memorial Scholarship Fund can be sent to the Development Office, 101 Hofstra University, Hempstead, NY 11549. In tribute to the memory of Dr. Waysek all Hofstra University flags have been lowered to half staff. James M. Shuart, President Helene Fortunoff, Chairperson, Board of Trustees Hofstra University
