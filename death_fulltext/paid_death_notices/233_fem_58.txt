
        DOWD--Margaret E., (nee Slattery), 67, on June 12, of Brooklyn Heights, Remsenburg and Hutchinson Island. Beloved wife of the Hon. Joseph J. Dowd. Dearest friend and mentor of John & Patrick McConnell. Loving grandmother of Michael, Patrick and Daniel. A woman of unsurpassed wisdom, grace and ineffable beauty, she will be missed beyond words. Visitation, Wednesday, June 14, 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Follett & Werner Funeral Home, 60 Mill Road, Westhampton Beach. Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated Thursday, June 15, 10 AM at the Church of the Immaculate Conception, 580 Main Street, Westhampton Beach. The family respectfully requests that contributions in her memory be made to St. Vincent's Services, Inc. 66 Boerum Pl, Bklyn, NY 11201.
