
        DENTON-Robert W. Death at age 87 came February 18, 2002 to Robert W. Denton, who owned and managed General Motors automobile dealerships, including a Cadillac/Olds dealership in Kingston, New York. He leaves his widow, Dacia, and eight children. Memorials: to Manhattanville College, or the Convent of the Sacred Heart, 91st Street.
