
        LEESON-Laura. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center (BIMC) family joins with the leadership of its parent corporation Greater Metropolitan Health Systems (GMHS), Inc., in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Laura Leeson, beloved sister of our dear friend and benefactor Abe Schrader, a member of the Beth Israel Board of Trustees for more than twentyfive years. To Abe, the Schrader and Leeson families and all who loved her, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Boards of Trustees, GMHS and BIMC Robert G. Newman, M.D., President, GMHS Matthew E. Sink, M.D., President BIMC
