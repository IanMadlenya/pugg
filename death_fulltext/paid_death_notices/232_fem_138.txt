
        VORSANGER - SAUL - Helen. We mourn the loss of Helen Vorsanger-Saul, age 51, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. She is survived by her devoted husband Mitchell, loving daughters Lauren and Stacy, her mother Anne Vorsanger, brother Dr. Gary Vorsanger (Debbie), her cousins Dr. Jeffrey Vorsanger, Mrs. Nancy Musat, and aunt and uncle Eric and Frances Vorsanger. All donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
