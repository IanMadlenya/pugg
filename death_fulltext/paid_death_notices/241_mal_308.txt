
        LEVINE--Harold. Exempli fied the true response to the Rabbinic saying ''What is the best way to lead your life? Have a good heart''. Worked untiringly for Jewish causes, his Synagogue and Federation, and dedicated to making the world a better place for all. Committed to the Boy Scouts and served his country in Vietnam. His spirit has imbued all of us with a sense of love and affection that will remain grounded in this world for all time. Deepest sympathies to his wife Carol, children Rachel and Eric, and Levine Family. Andrew, Dorothy, Zoe, JoBeth and Tanner Tananbaum
