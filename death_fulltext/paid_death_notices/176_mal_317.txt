
        FRASER-Richard K., 32, missing and presumed dead in the World Trade Center attack. Survived by his loving wife, Suzanne, and beautiful son, Aidan C., age 21 months; also by his parents Charles E. and Kathryn F. Fraser; his brother Leland B. and sister Margaret A. Aaron. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, 980 Park Avenue, Manhattan, at 1:30 PM, Saturday, September 22. In lieu of flowers, donations made to the Aidan C. Fraser Medical & Educational Fund will be greatly appreciated.
