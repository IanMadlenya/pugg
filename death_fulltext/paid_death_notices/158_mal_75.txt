
        HICKEY-Robert, F. Esquire on Mar. 4, 2000. Native of the Bronx & a resident of Quogue, N.Y. former Bronx Assistant District Attorney, Human Rights Commissioner and a member of the University Glee Club. Beloved son of the late Frank J. and Anna C. (nee Maginnis). Loving brother of Kathleen P. O'Brien and the late Anne M. Keegan. Fond brother-in-law of Thomas C. O'Brien. He was also a loving uncle and grand uncle. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday 10 AM at the Frances Schervier Home, 2975 Independence Ave., Bronx, New York. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
