
        SILMAN-Anna. On October 8, 1998. Beloved wife of George, mother of Trudie and Jeffrey, mother-in-law of Lewis. Lifelong, devoted member of Hadassah and holder of many local and regional offices. New York City teacher and guidance counselor, she provided inspiration and love for all whose lives she touched. Services will be held 10AM Sunday at the Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave., Yonkers.
