
        APSEL--Abe. Of Boca Raton, FL, died peacefully, on February 1st, at the age of 89. Beloved husband and devoted caregiver for the late Ruth, who battled ALS. Cherished father of Dorine Gordon, and Linda and Nick Faraci. Adored grandfather of Felicia and David. He will be greatly missed by his loving family and all who experienced his generosity of spirit and were warmed by his wonderful smile. Contributions in his memory may be made to The ALS Association, 116 John St, NYC 10038.
