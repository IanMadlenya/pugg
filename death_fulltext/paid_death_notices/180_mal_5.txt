
        ROISTACHER-Herbert D. Died Sunday, December 30, 2001, at age 84 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Ruth. Devoted father of Nancy and Wayne Merkelson and Kenneth and Shelley Roistacher. Cherished grandfather of Randy and Jeremy Merkelson & Andrew and Daniel Roistacher. Dear brother-in-law of Alvin and Francine Lashinsky and Edna Roistacher, and dear uncle and friend. Respected attorney, formerly Chief Clerk of the New York State Supreme Court, First Judicial District, Criminal Division, Manhattan and the Bronx. New York Yankees baseball fan, attended, watched or heard over 8,000 games. Services were held on Monday at Sinai Chapels, Queens. The family will be sitting at Hollis Hills home. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital and Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
