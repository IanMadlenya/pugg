
        GIOELI--Rose. On August 23, 2003. Loving aunt of Catherine F. Gioeli, A. Casey Gioeli, Daniel G. Gioeli and his wife, Debra. Great aunt of Cole and Grace. Graduate of Hunter College, Rose worked with Morris Abrams at Paul, Weiss, LLP, as a legal secretary for many years. She was an avid world traveler and devotee to the arts. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street Thursday, August 28, fr 7-9 P.M. and Friday, August 29, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, August 30th at 10 A.M. at Saint James R.C. Church, 23 Oliver Street (enter on James Street between Madison and Pearl Streets). Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to: The Lower East Side Tenement Museum, 90 Orchard Street, NY, NY 10002 or to Seaman Church Institute, 241 Water Street, New York, New York 10038.
