
        BECKER-Marilyn. Beloved wife, mother, daughter, friend, community activist, writer, therapist and teacher, died peacefully on November 24, 1999, surrounded by her husband, Jack Kobliska, and daughters, Rosey and Annie. Marilyn was an exceptional person who is deeply loved. We will remember her insightful counsel, kind words and the ways in which she inspired us to challenge complacency and convention. A remembrance will be held Saturday, December 11, at 10:30 AM. For more information, 212-982-5650.
