
        KORR--Rhoda Ellis. 88. Dr. Rhoda Ellis Korr, a former professor at Brooklyn College for 35 years, died November 14, in Sun City, AZ. Dr. Ellis earned her Ph.D. from NYU in 1944. She was active in the Beth Emeth Congregation, won awards for her work with the UJA-Federation of Greater Phoenix, and was a Charter Member of Sun City Hadassah and ORT. She is survived by daughter Joan Shrednick, sister Natalie Friedman and grandson Jason Shrednick (and wife Jacqueline). Rhoda was loved and respected by all who knew her and will be sorely missed.
