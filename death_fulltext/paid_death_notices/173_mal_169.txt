
        AMSTERDAM-Albert J., 79. Of Key West, and Alexandria Bay, NY; formerly Syracuse, NY, born Philadelphia, PA. Son of Florence and Jules. Attended Columbia High School, Maplewood, NJ. and the U of Virginia. U.S. Army Air Corp; businessman Amsterdam Brothers; Amsterdam Orchards; Titan Tug and Towing; founder and operator of Curry Mansion Inn in Key West. Predeceased by his son Bruce. He is survived by his wife, Edith Lehr Amsterdam; daughter Carol Fornari, Brooklyn; Patsy Amsterdam and son Philip Amsterdam, Syracuse, NY; six grandchildren. Brother of Stanley Amsterdam and Shirley Notkin of West Orange, NJ. Donations to Key West Symphony, P.O. Box 774, Key West, FL. 33040.
