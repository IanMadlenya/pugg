
        ROMAGNA - Victor A. The Trustees of the South Street Seaport Museum mourn the passing of founding Board member, Victor A. Romagna of Annapolis, MD. As friend, yachtsman, and exemplar of values associated with the sea, Victor was without equal and will be sorely missed. Our deepest sympathies to Louise, his wife of 48 years and to his children and grandchildren. Peter A. Aron, Co-Chairman David A. Olsen, Co-Chairman Peter Neill, President
