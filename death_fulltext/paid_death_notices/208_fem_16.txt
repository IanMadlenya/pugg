
        BAKER--Enid H., 90. Of Sea Cliff, N.Y. On May 2, 2004. Beloved mother of Charles E. & the late Valerie D. Wonderful and graceful friend whose greatest joy was her children, family and friends for whom she had great compassion and love. Memorial contributions may be made in her memory to the NAACP Legal Defense and Education Fund, Inc., 99 Hudson St, New York, N.Y. 10013. Memorial service to be announced.
