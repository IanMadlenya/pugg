
        TORREY-Owen Cates Jr, at Norwalk, CT., on February 13, 2001. Born October 31, 1925, in New York, NY, son of Owen Cates Torrey and Emily Gertrude McChesney Torrey. Graduate of St. Paul's School 1943, Harvard University AB 1947 and Columbia University Law School JD, 1951. Admiralty lawyer and sailmaker. Served in the U.S. Army, 63rd Armored Infantry Batallion, 1944-1945 in the Ardennnes and Rhineland campaigns. Winner of the Bronze Medal (Yachting-Swallow Class) in the 1948 Olympic Games (XIVth Olympiad), London, 1948. Member of American Yacht Club, New York Yacht Club, Storm Trysail Club, and life member of ISCYRA; President of the Yacht Racing Association of Long Island Sound 1992-1994. Lifelong involvement in yacht racing as a competitor (including two America's Cup campaigns), racing handicapper, race committee member and U.S. sailing judge. Survived by his wife Allegra Mertz Torrey, South Norwalk, CT. and four children of a previous marriage: Emily Southard Torrey, Jackson, WY; Ellen McChesney Torrey, Elkton, MD.; Margery Torrey Godwin, Pine Mountain, GA.; and Owen Cates Torrey III, Houston, TX. Funeral service to be held at the American Yacht Club, Milton Point, Rye, NY, on Friday, February 16, 2001, at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers, donation to New York's Aquarium (Wildlife Conservation Society) 2300 Southern Blvd, Bronx, NY 10460-1099.
