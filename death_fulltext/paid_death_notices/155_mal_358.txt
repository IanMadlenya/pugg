
        JAFFIN-George. The Board of the Jewish Campus Life Fund of Columbia University/Barnard College mourns the passing of our good friend and esteemed Board Member George Jaffin. Our deepest sympathy to his wife Janet, their children, David, Doris and Lois and entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Prof. Robert Pollack, President Earle W. Kazis, Chairman of the Board Arthur Ochs Sulzberger Chairman, Executive Committee Rabbi Charles Sheer
        JAFFIN-George M. We extend heartfelt condolences to Janet Jaffin, David Jaffin, Lois Levine and Doris Greenberg, on the passing of their beloved husband and father. A Silver Founder of the Medical Center, together with his wife, Janet, he exemplified the highest ethics & morals of Jewish tradition. May the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med. Ctr. in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, Pres.
        JAFFIN-George M. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of our longtime supporter George M. Jaffin. We extend our condolences to his wife, Janet, and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
