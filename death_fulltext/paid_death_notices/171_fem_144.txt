
        TOBIAS-Mollie. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Mollie Tobias, beloved mother-in-law of Lynn Tobias, a dear friend who offers invaluable leadership as a member of our Board of Directors and as chair of the Manhattan Division of our Women's Campaign. We extend our sincerest condolences to Lynn and her husband, Glen, and to the entire family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair and President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Sandra F. Cahn, Chair, Women's Campaign
