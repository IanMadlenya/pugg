
        O'BRIEN-William Jourdan of Quiogue, NY, 65, passed away on August 18, 2001 at Central Suffolk Hospital. Son of the late Henry L. and Natalie Jourdan O'Brien, he is survived by his wife, Verity Voight O'Brien, his sister Natalie O'Brien Conklin and stepchildren Elizabeth Webb Rowe, David, Peter and Burton Webb, 11 grandchildren and aide Robert deYoung. He was a graduate of Buckley and Hotchkiss Schools and Cornell University, B.A. and LL.B. In respect of his wishes, there will be no service. Memorial contributions may be made to the Henry L. O'Brien Jr. Scholarship Fund, office of Cornell University, 55 Brown Road, Ithaca, NY 14850 or to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society.
