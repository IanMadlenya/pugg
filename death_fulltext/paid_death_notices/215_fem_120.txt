
        KING--Gioconda. The Trustees and staff of The Metropolitan Museum of Art mourn the passing of Gioconda King, a generous benefactor and member of the Chairman's Council, whose interest in and benevolence toward the museum spanned many years. Her graciousness and her love of the institution will be greatly missed. James R. Houghton, Chairman Philippe de Montebello, Director David E. McKinney, President
