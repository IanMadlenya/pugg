
        PATTERSON-Mary Marvin Breckinridge. On December 11, 2002 at her residence in Washington, D.C. Widow of Jefferson Patterson. Mother of Patricia M. Patterson and grandmother of Ian Patterson. Predeceased by her son Mark J. Patterson. A memorial service will be held at the National Cathedral on January 9, 2003 at 3 P.M. Contributions may be made to the contributor's choice.
