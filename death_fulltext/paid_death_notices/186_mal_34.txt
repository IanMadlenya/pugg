        WEISEN-Stephen. The partners and staff of Berdon LLP mourn the July 1st passing of our colleague, Stephen Weisen of Great Neck. A partner with the firm, Stephen was admired for his devotion to his family and friends, his keen insights and professionalism, and for his courage in facing a difficult illness. Our deepest sympathies go out to his wife Barbara, his children Daniel and Carly, and to family members, friends & business associates who will miss his intelligence and good cheer. He leaves behind many strong & fond memories.
