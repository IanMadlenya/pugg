
        BRISK-Seymour H. Of Longmeadow, MA, and Boynton Beach, FL, 85 years young, died January 18. Loving and much loved husband of Harriet, proud and cherished father of Gayle & Roger Bronstein, Sheryl & Ben Schugar, and Susan & Danny Monosson. Adored grandfather of Lauryn & Jordan Bronstein, Eric Schneider, and Sam, Jeremy & Zachary Monosson. Dear brother-in-law of Harvey & Paula Mandel. Services Tuesday, 1 PM, Temple Beth El, Springfield, MA. A special man, a friend to all. We will miss him so much.
