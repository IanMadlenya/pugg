
        McMANUS-James J. March 26, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Eileen T. McManus. Loving father of Eileen and her husband Rick Wolfman, Patricia and her husband Mark Matthews, James and his wife Kathleen, and Brian and his wife Rosemary. Cherished grandfather of eleven. Devoted brother of Marie Plante, the late Helen McCarthy & the late Peter. His entire life was spent as the proud and devoted proprietor of Peter McManus Cafe in Chelsea, a New York City landmark for 70 years. The Cafe was chosen for numerous movies and television locals. A decorated veteran of W.W. II and the recipient of two Purple Hearts. A gifted and unique individual who will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St Thursday, 7-9PM and Friday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, March 31st, 10AM at Saint Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave at 84 St, NYC. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
