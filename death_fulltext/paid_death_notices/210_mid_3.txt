
        DOBRINSKY--Lily. The entire Yeshiva University family is deeply saddened by the passing in Montreal of a highly admired and respected member of the community, at 94, the beloved mother of our Vice President for University Affairs, Rabbi Dr. Herbert C. (and Dina) Dobrinsky and Sharon (and Louis) Goelman. She was the cherished grandmother of five and great grandmother of 13 devoted great grandchildren. The funeral took place on Monday, July 4th in Montreal. Rabbi Dobrinsky will return to complete the Shiva at his home in Riverdale, NY from Thursday morning until after morning services on Sunday. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman Board of Trustees
