
        KENNEY-Jeanne. On January 8th, 2002 after her courageous battle. She was a lifelong resident of Inwood/Good Shepherd Parish. Jeanne was the inspiration and strength for her beloved daughters, Patricia Fallon and Jeanne Marie Dillman. Loving sister of Mary & Robert. Cherished sisterin-law of Paula & adored motherin-law of Michael Fallon. Devoted grandmother of the late Angela and baby-to-be. Jeanne will be greatly missed by her endless number of family and friends whose needs so often came first. She will continue to live on in the hearts of all who knew and loved her. Family will receive friends at Riverdale Funeral Home, 5044 Broadway at 214th Street, NYC, on Thursday and Friday, 2-5 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial at Good Shepherd R.C. Church, Saturday, 10 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Make A Wish Foundation of Metro NY, 85 Old Shore Road, Pt. Washington, New York 11050.
