
        COHEN -- Dr. Ernest, 78, passed away peacefully on January 14, 2004, surrounded by his family. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Julie, children Paul, Beth, Larry, Anne and Andrew, granddaughter Ariel, brother Howard, sister-in-law Joy, and niece Cynthia. Dr. Cohen also leaves behind many devoted patients from his psychiatric practice in Pompton Plains, NJ, and, since 1983, in South Florida. He served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. A funeral service will be held at 1:00PM today at Star of David Chapel, 7701 Bailey Road, North Lauderdale, Florida. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in his memory to the Chicago Medical School (class of 1953), c/o the Development Office, 3333 Green Bay Road, North Chicago, Illinois 60064 or Friends of Mostly Mozart, c/o Lincoln Center, 70 Lincoln Center Plaza, New York, New York 10023, www.lincolncenter.org
