
        SOLOVITZ-Samuel L., age 71, passed away on Tuesday, January 12, 1999. Mr. Solovitz moved to Houston in 1984, after retiring from the New York Times Newspaper after 40 years. Surviving him are, niece, Brenda Polunsky and Husband Richard; nephew, Lewis Solovitz and wife Mona; great-nieces, Anna Polunsky, Michelle and Amanda Solovitz; and a great-nephew, Jeremy Polunsky. Services and interment were held Sunday, January 17, 1999, in Clifton, New Jersey.
