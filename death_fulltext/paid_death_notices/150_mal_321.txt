
        TORTORELLA-Salvatore L. On July 17, 1999. Beloved husband of Camille. Devoted father of Michael. Decorated Army veteran of World War II. After a distinguished career in Army counterintelligence, he retired a Major in 1965. Honored teacher for the Theodore Roosevelt H.S., Bronx, N.Y., for some 15 years, member of the Knights of Columbus, Wakefield Council no. 2922 and the Retired Officers Association. Viewing Tuesday 2 to 4 and 7 to 9PM at the David J. Hodder and Son Funeral Home, 899 McLean Avenue (near Kimball Avenue), Yonkers, N.Y. Mass Wednesday, St. Barnabas Church, 9:45AM. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery.
