
        KUHN-Dr. Charles III. Age 69, December 29. Professor Emeritus of Pathology, Brown University, retired Chief of Pathology, Memorial Hospital of Rhode Island. Husband of Nobuko (Obayashi) Kuhn, father of Heather, Holly, and the late Carla Kuhn, brother of Sally Kuhn. Memorial service is on Wednesday, 12 noon, Monahan, Drabble & Sherman, 230 Waterman St, Providence. Contributions in his memory: Brown University, Pathobiology Graduate Program, Charles Kuhn Award, C/O Dr. A.B. Kane, Box G-B5, Brown University, Providence, Rhode Island 02912.
