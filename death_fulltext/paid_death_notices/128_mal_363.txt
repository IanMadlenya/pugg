
        SWIG-Richard. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Richard Swig of San Francisco, a champion of the Jewish community and fervent supporter of Israel, who will be greatly missed. Mr. Swig emulated the extraordinary generosity and humanitarian concerns of his family, who have been cherished friends of the federation system in New York and San Francisco for four generations. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Swig family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        SWIG-Richard. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of Richard Swig of San Francisco, Honorary Life Member of our National Commission, member of our San Francisco Regional Board, longtime ADL supporter and an outstanding community leader and philanthropist. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife and the entire Swig family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director Barbara Bergen, San Francisco Regional Director
