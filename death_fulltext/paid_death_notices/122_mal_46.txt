
        KLEINFELD-Morris, M.D. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center expresses with sorrow the death of Dr. Morris Kleinfeld. Dr. Kleinfeld was associated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Medicine for over fifty years. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. David Eddi, M.D., President Warren Wexelman, M.D. President Elect Jacob Shani, M.D., Secretary David Feldman, M.D, Treasurer
