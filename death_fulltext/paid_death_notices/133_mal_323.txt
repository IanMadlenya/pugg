
        ZEIDMAN-Eugene M. Age 93, on February 17, 1998, peacefully at home in Birmingham, Alabama. Attorney and long time leader of Birmingham Jewish community. His wife of 57 years, Ida Fisher Zeidman, and grandson, John Fisher Zeidman predeceased him. Survived by daughter, Adele Z. Silver of Cleveland, Ohio, son Philip F. Zeidman of Washington, DC, five grandchildren, Jonathan (Melissa), Michael (Deborah), and Sarah Silver and Betsy and Jennifer Zeidman and two great-grandchildren. Memorial gifts may be made to Zeidman Family Center for Chinese Studies at Sidwell Friends School, Washington, DC or Daniel Jeremy Silver Fellowship, Center for Jewish Studies, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA.
