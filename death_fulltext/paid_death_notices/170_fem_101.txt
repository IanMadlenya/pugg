
        GOTTFRIED-Vera G. Formerly of Hallandale, FL, and Buffalo, NY, died peacefully on March 13, 2001, four days after her ninetyfourth birthday. Loving & devoted mother of Judith Murray Agisim, and son-in-law Leslie, cherished grandmother of Taylor, pre-deceased by her husband Charles Gottfried. She will be greatly missed. Funeral services will be held at Elmlawn Mausoleum, Buffalo, NY, at 1 PM on Thursday March 15, 2001. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate donations to The National Emphysema Foundation, 15 Stevens Street, Norwalk, CT 06856.
