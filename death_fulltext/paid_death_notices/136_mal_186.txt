
        WASSNER-Abraham. Died May 16, 1998, due to complications of lymphoma. He was the devoted husband of his late wife Clara, loving father of Joan and Steven, and doting grandfather of Regina, Ari and Nancy. A man of immense ability, a linguist par excellence, and an interpreter and translator in over 25 languages. He initially served as supervisory interpreter for the INS in NYC, and after his retirment, as an invaluable resource and friend to immigration professionals thoughout NY and beyond. Respected by all who met him, loved by all who knew him. Funeral services Monday, at Parkside Chapel, Queens Blvd.
