
        HERCULES--Dellora. Died on Thursday, September 30 after a brief illness. She was an educator, a New York City public school Principal, devoted wife of Frank and mother of Eric. Beloved aunt of Raymond John and Armando Howard, grandmother of John and Abenaa Hayes. Memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 16 at 12 noon at All Saints Episcopal Church, 230 E. 60 St.
