
        BRANDT-Henry. On September 30, 2000, at age 77. Beloved husband of Roxanne. Loving father of Pamela Jackson and Jonathan Brandt and father-in-law of Thomas Jackson. Adored grandfather of Natalie. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, 7-9 PM, with service Tuesday, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Riverside Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to the National Parkinson Foundation, Inc, 1501 N.W. 9th Ave, Bob Hope Road, Miami, FL 33136-1494.
        BRANDT-Henry. Founder and first President of the World Federalist Association-New York Chapter. Our profound appreciation for his many years of dedication to the cause of World Peace through World Laws and for his perseverance, patience, good humor and humanity. With thanks, Your Board
