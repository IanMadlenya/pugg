
        STANNARD-Thelma Nuthall, 82. Died Sunday, August 22, 1999 at Forrestall Skilled Nursing and Rehab Center in Princeton, NJ. Born in Richmond Surrey, England, she immigrated to New York City some 50 years ago. She worked for British Airways in New York for approximately 30 years and was display manager for the U.S. and Canada at her retirement in 1977. She was preceded in death by her husband Donald and a brother and two sisters. Surviving are a sister Molly B. Harding of Richmond Surrey, England, a brother, John Nuthall of Twickenham, England, a nephew Christopher Nuthall and his wife Jane of Brevard, North Carolina and several nieces and nephews in England. Visitation is from 7 to 9 P.M. Thursday at the Papavero Funeral Home, 72-27 Grand Avenue, Maspeth, NY (1-718-651-3535). Funeral Services are Friday at 10:00 A.M. at the funeral home with burial in Maple Grove Cemetery, Kew Gardens, NY.
