
        ABRAMSON-Jacob. Age 91. Of Rye Brook, N.Y. He operated his small, ethical pharmacy in Port Chester, New York. for forty years, later serving for twenty years as Chief Pharmacist of The New York Hospital, Westchester Division. He was married to the late Reva Kurman and is survived by four children: Richard Abramson, Diane Abramson, Daniel Abramson and Debra Lewis, four grandchildren: Katherine, Ethan, Catherine and Caroline, 2 brothers: David and Robert, and an adoring family of nieces, nephews and cousins. His radiant warmth will reside in our hearts forever.
