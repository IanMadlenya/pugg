
        SALPETER-Burton F. M.D. died Tuesday, December 18, after 58 years of family practice. Graduate of Far Rockaway High School, University of Virginia and Georgetown Medical School; he served as Director of Family Practice at Peninsula Hospital Center. Accomplishments ranged from tennis junior finalist at Forest Hills, World War II Battalion Surgeon under General Patton, and President of Rockaway Rotary. Beloved husband of Lucy for 53 years, father of Ann Schockett and Steven Salpeter, grandfather of Debra, Erica, Paul and Jeffrey. Services are scheduled for Friday, December 21, at ''Riverside-Boulevard'' Chapel, Hewlett, L.I. Interment at Old Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, NY.
        SALPETER-Burton F., MD. The Board of Directors, Administration, Medical Board and entire staff of Peninsula Hospital Center are deeply saddened by the passing of Burton F. Salpeter, MD, Benefactor, past President of the Medical Board and Medical Staff member for over fifty years.
