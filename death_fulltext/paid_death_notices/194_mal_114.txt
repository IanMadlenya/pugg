
        GOODKIND-Walter Larney. Age 94. Of NYC. Passed away February 22, 2003. Was a talent agent for Universal Pictures, a personal manager and literary agent with his wife Karen for classical singers and musicians including William Warfield and Leontyne Price. Survived by his son, daughter-in-law and two grandchildren. A memorial service will be held March 15. Call 908-725-8875.
