
        RICH--Daniel E., 31. Of New York, NY on November 16, 2005 after a long and courageous fight against all odds. Beloved son of Bruce and Judy Rich. Adored brother of Stacey, loving nephew and cousin, and devoted friend. Honors graduate of Cornell University and graduate of Emory University School of Law. Donations in his memory may be made to the Daniel E. Rich Fund, C/O Dr. Lisa De Angelis, Chair, Dept. of Neurology, Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, West 76th St. at Amsterdam Ave. on Sunday, November 20 at 11:45 AM.
        $(6$)RICH--Daniel. Temple Shaaray Tefila records with profound sorrow the death of our cherished member.
