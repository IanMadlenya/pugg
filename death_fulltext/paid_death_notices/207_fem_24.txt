
        HAGERTY--Lisa B. 71. On March 22, 2004. Preceded in her death by her husband, Sheward Hagerty. Attended the Brearley School, Milton Academy and Bryn Mawr College. Survived by her sons, Sheward, Sean and Sandford, as well as her four grandsons. Her radiance, generosity and optimism will be missed. Memorial pending.
