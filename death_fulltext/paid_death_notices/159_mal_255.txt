
        LAWS-Wyatt, M.D., age 85, on April 22, 2000, peacefully at home in Cudjoe Key, FL. Husband of Brenda of Cudgoe Key and Plattsburgh, NY. Father of Wendy Riggs of Bronxville, NY and grandfather of Jennifer Riggs Vetter of Oakland, CA., and Sarah Riggs of Ann Arbor, MI. B.A. and M.D. from McGill University, RCAF 1940-1945, ophthalmologist in Montreal, Canada and later Plattsburgh, NY. Retired 1978 and moved to Florida. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice of the Florida Keys, 1319 William Street, Key West, FL 33040.
