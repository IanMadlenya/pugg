
        NUSSDORF-Bernard. Died March 18, 1999. Beloved husband of Ruth. Father of Stephen, Glen and Arlene. Adored father-in-law of Alicia, Claudine and Steve. Grandfather of Marni, Zachary, Jacob, Aaron, Julia, Ethan and Madeline. Brother of Matilda, Natalie and Herbert. Everyone's special friend.
