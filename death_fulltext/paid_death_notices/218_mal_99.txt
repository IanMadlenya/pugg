
        FISHER--Max. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum mourns the loss of Max Fisher. A Museum Founder, his early generosity was instrumental in creating the institution. His philanthropy and leadership were marked by an extraordinary vision. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Marjorie, and his entire family. Fred S. Zeidman, Chair Ruth B. Mandel, Vice Chair Sara J. Bloomfield, Director
