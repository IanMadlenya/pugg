
        GLYNN-Thomas B., age 96, died Saturday, January 27th, 2001 at the Norwalk Hospital. Born January 1st, 1905 in Boston, Massachusetts. He lived in Greens Farms, CT. for over 70 years. He graduated from Harvard University in 1929. Mr. Glynn was a lifelong horseman and polo enthusiast. He is survived by his daughter, Sanford Glynn, of Boykin, SC, his son, Michael Glynn, of NYC, and granddaughter, Shannon Woolfe, of Hillsborough, NC. He was predeceased by his wife of 55 years, the former Mabel Jennings. Services will be held Friday, February 2, at 11 AM, St. Thomas Aquinas Church, 1719 Post Road, Fairfield, Conn. In lieu of flowers, donations in Mr. Glynn's memory may be made to the Thoroughbred Retirement Foundation, 450 Shrewsbury Plaza, Suite 351, Shrewsbury, New Jersey 07702.
