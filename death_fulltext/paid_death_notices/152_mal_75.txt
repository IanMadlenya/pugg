
        MOSS-David. 73, Of Fresh Meadows NY. He was an off broadway pioneer. He produced two Off Broadway shows (Revivals of Tobacco Road and Enemy of the People-Provincetown playhouse). As an actor he appeared in numerous off broadway, summer stock, and channel thirteen productions. He was the drama coach at Forest Hills High School. He will be dearly missed by his wife, June Moss and his son, Geoffrey Moss-a professor of Sociology at SUNY-Old Westbury.
