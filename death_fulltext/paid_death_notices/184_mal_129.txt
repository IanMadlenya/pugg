
        LOHF-Kenneth A. The Trustees and staff of The Museum of Modern Art mourn the loss of a respected member of the Museum's Trustee Committee on Museum, Library, and Research as well as its Library Council. Mr. Lohf, who was a distinguished librarian, scholar and author, will be deeply missed by all of his friends at the Museum and by the many other institutions who had the benefit of his involvement. Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, NY
