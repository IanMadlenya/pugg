
        GORDON--Monte J., formerly of NYC, and of Henderson, NV, on Friday, March 30, 2007 at age 83. A retired Research Director of Financial Securities and a U.S. Air Force WWII veteran. Funeral services are pending in San Diego, CA. Survivors include his wife of 61 years, Arline Gordon, devoted father of Dale Gordon and Margery (Richard) Winters and loving grandfather of Joshua, Brandy and Jeffrey Bell and Andy Winters. Services are private. Contributions are to your favorite charity.
