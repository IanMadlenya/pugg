
        APPELL-Daniel T. Of Lexington, MA, formerly of Scarsdale, NY. Died Sunday, June 7, at the age of 98, in Lexington, MA. Beloved father of Jane Appell of Lincoln, MA. Grandfather of two. Loving uncle, granduncle & great-granduncle, especially loved by grandniece and great-grandniece. Memorial service to be held Sunday, June 21, 2 PM, at Brookhaven, 1010 Waltham Street, Lexington, MA. Friends & family are invited to the home of Jane Appell and Steven Shapse, from 3:30 to 9 PM, following the service. Donations to be made to Hospice Care, Inc., 41 Montvale Avenue, Stoneham, MA 02180, or the American Diabetes Association, 2 Mount Royal Avenue, Marlborough, MA 01752.
        APPELL-Daniel T. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed life member, and extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Murray B. Hirsch, Pres. Howard Ecker, Secretary
