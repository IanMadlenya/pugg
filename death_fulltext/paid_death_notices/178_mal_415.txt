
        BELL-Stanley P., age 69. Awardwinning sailor died on Tuesday, November 27, 2001. 1953 graduate of Dartmouth College, former President of Executive Health Examiners. A sailor all his life, he was Chairman of the Winner Race Committee at the Larchmont Yacht Club where he was a life member. He is survived by his wife Cynthia W. Bell of Mattapoisett, MA, two sons Stephen of Amherst, NY, Stuart of Princeton, NJ and six grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at St. John's Church, Larchmont, 11 AM, December 1st. In lieu of flowers donations to the Dartmouth College Alumni Fund Class of '53 Scholarship, Hanover, NH 03755.
