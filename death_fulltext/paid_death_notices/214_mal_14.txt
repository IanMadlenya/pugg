
        BURFORD--Barry Frederick. Age 28 of Wellington, FL passed away on October 30th. A graduate of U.C.F., in 2000, and resided most recently in Astoria, New York. He is survived by his parents Jerry and Colleen; brother Clayton, all of Wellington, FL, his sister Sheila of New York and the love of his life Christine Janes of Astoria, New York. A visitation will be held on Wednesday, November 3, 2004 from 6-9 P.M. with a service at 8:30 P.M. at Palms West Funeral Home.
