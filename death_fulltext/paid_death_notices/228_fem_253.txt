        $(6$)STILLMAN -- Eugenia Wat ters (Mrs. P. Gordon B. Stillman). The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society notes with sorrow the death of Eugenia W. Stillman at her home in New York on Friday, January 27, 2006. Mrs. Stillman was a loyal friend and benefactor of the Society and a member of the Library Committee for many years. Her late husband was a Trustee of the Society from 1990 to 1995 and Librarian from 1992 to 1995. Her son, Waddell W. Stillman, is currently a Trustee and Vice-Chairman. The Trustees and Staff extend their deep sympathy to the family of this beloved lady. Henry C.B. Lindh, Chairman William P. Johns, President
        $(6$)STILLMAN--Eugenia Waddell Watters. The National Society of Colonial Dames in the State of New York records with deep regret the death of its distinguished Past President, former Board member, former Vice President of The National Society of The Colonial Dames of America, and former Chairman of the Dumbarton House Board. She will be greatly missed. Anne F. Crawford, President
        $(6$)STILLMAN--Eugenia W. The New York Altar Guild notes with deep sorrow the passing of its longtime Board member and past President. ''Eugie'' was the spark and the life of the Altar Guild for many years and her wise counsel and good humor will be sorely missed. Our warmest sympathies are extended to her family. Mai D. Harper, President
