
        RAUE--Philip Baldwin, 86, passed away July 19. Long time resident of Pelham Manor, NY and South Newbury, VT, he is survived by his wife, Jeanne Lungren Raue, sons David and Gregory, their wives and 5 grandchildren. After Yale Law School he joined Donovan, Leisure, Newton and Irvine and retired as Assistant General Counsel of Mobil Chemical. He was an avid outdoorsman with interests in hiking, skiing, biking and rowing. He will be remembered and sorely missed by all who knew him for his intellect, wit, grace, charm, style and optimism. Memorial service details will be available later.
