
        PAPAMARKOU - Alecko. The Board of Trustees and the staff of the Central Park Conservancy mourn the passing of Alecko Papamarkou, a very generous friend to Central Park. In loving memory of his mother Margot, Alecko recently endowed the Pond landscape, located in the southeastern section of Central Park. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family, friends and colleagues. Ara M. Millstein, Chairman Karen H. Putnam, President
