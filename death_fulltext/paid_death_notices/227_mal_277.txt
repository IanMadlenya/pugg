
        COLIN--George. Of Delray Beach, Florida passed away on December 24, 2005. He was the beloved husband of Yetta Colin, father of Jay Barry Colin of Montecito, CA and Delray Beach, FL, Larry H. Colin of Atlanta, GA, and adoring grandfather of six grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren. He lived his life helping all those who knew him. He proudly served our Country in the Merchant Marines during WWII. He was a retired Chairman of Colin Service Systems, Inc. one of the largest privately-held Facility Service providers that was recently acquired by ABM Service Industries. George served as president of Congregation Emanu-El of Yonkers, NY. He was past president of the Yonkers Chamber of Commerce, and a Board Member in the Westchester County Association. He actively supported many Jewish and non-sectarian organizations in both Westchester County and the Palm Beach County. It was as if he were a good deed in motion. A true gentleman, George blazed paths for better understanding and harmony among people--we all benefit from his having been with us. The Colin Family invites his many friends, and admirers to celebrate the 86 years that he was with us. Memorial Services were held at the Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel on Tuesday December 27. He will be interred at Mount Hope Cemetery located in Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, this Thursday at 10:30a.m. Donations in his memory can be made to the American Cancer Society.
