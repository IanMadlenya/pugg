
        SKLAR-Ann Skolnick. Born West Haven, CT, 1907, died May 21, 2002, Queens, NY. Married to Philip Sklar (d. 1956), Toscaninni's principal bassist with the NBC Symphony. Worked for the Queens Public Library. Graduate of Ann Arbor University, MI, and Columbia University, NY. Beloved by her children, Arle Sklar-Weinstein of Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, and Martin Sklar of Bloomfield, NJ. Daughter, Sondra Lamberton-James, predeceased her in 1997. A family service will be held at the Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, NJ, June 3 at 1 PM.
