
        HILL-Elena Karam. On March 30, 2001 in San Francisco, CA, at 92. She was a member of the Group Theater in the 1930's and an actress on Broadway and in films for 40 years. Wife of John W. Hill, founder of Hill & Knowlton Inc. public relations firm. Survived by daughter Alexandra Karam of San Francisco, seven grandchildren and nine great grandchildren.
