
        GELLER-Fred H. age 72 November 29, 2000. Beloved husband of Maxine Udell Geller. Beloved father of Hillary and Michael Ellowitz, Liz and Laurence Geller. Loving grandfather of Melissa, Jacob, Jessica. Loving brother of Lynda and David Finkelstein, Honey and Arnold Geller, Elaine and Irwin Geller. May his soul rest in peace.
        GELLER-Fred. The Board of Governors and Members of Inwood Country Club record with sorrow the passing of Fred Geller. Beloved husband of Maxine. We extend deepest sympathy to the family. Edwin R. Stein, President Allan S. Sexter, Secretary
        GELLER-Fred. The Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed member and Trustee, Fred Geller, husband of Maxine Geller. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and know no further sorrow.
