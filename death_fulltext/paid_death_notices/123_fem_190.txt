
        WITTFOGEL-Esther S. (Esther S. Goldfrank). Anthropologist, author and editor. At Sarah Neuman Nursing Home, Mamaroneck, N.Y., on April 23, 1997. Twelve days short of her 101st birthday. Widow of Karl A. Wittfogel (1988) and Walter S. Goldfrank (1935). Survived by daughter Susan G. Lennhoff, stepson Thomas Goldfrank, step grandchildren Sandra G. Zolna and John, Walter, David, Frederick and Edward Goldfrank, and 11 step great grandchildren. At her request, there will be no funeral or memorial preservation.
