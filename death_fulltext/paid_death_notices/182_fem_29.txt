
        HALPER-Beatrice T. Beloved wife of the late Oscar for 45 years. Cherished mother of Harlan. Loving mother-in-law of Marlina. Adored grandmother of Rachel and Jennifer. Reveered sister of the late Sam, Mina, Lea, Harry and Moe. Passed away on March 1, in Yonkers, NY, at the age of 81. She loved her family, her friends (whom she considered as close as family), the arts, and all that NYC had to offer. A strong supporter of Judaism, she was a proud member of Temple Israel of The City of New York since 1967. She loved life and she lived it with class, grace, and style. For all who knew her she will be dearly missed, always loved and never forgotten. Services will be held on Wednesday, March 6, at 12 Noon at ''Gutterman's Inc'', 175 Long Beach Rd, Rockville Centre, L.I. Donations may be made in her memory to The National MS Society or Temple Israel of The City of NY, 112 East 75th St, NY, NY 10021-3297.
