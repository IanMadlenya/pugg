
        LEVIN-Jacob A. ''Yankel'', age 90, died peacefully on February 11, 1999. Heroic Holocaust survivor. Devoted husband of Eda to whom he was married for 50 years. Beloved father and father-in-law of Dr. Sol Levin and his wife JoAnne; Elise and her husband Dr. Stephen Shapiro. Adored grandfather of Gregory and Daniel Shapiro, Jared and Samantha Levin. Services at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, 12:15pm, Sunday, February 14, 1999. Donations in his honor may be made to the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Museum in Washington, DC.
