
        LOIZEAUX-Daniel Clarke. Age 83 of Greenwich, CT. retired Art Director and Trustee of Loizeaux Publishers, on July 27 in Lancaster, PA. His wife, Marion Davies of England, predeceased him in 1995. He is survived by his sister, Marie Loizeaux and brother, Elie, both of Lancaster; three daughters, Katharine Loizeaux of Kent, CT, Esther den Breems of West Redding, CT and Marion Loizeaux of Greenwich, CT; and five grandchildren. Funeral on Saturday, August 2 at 10:00am, White Plains Gospel Chapel, North Broadway and McBride Avenue, White Plains, NY
