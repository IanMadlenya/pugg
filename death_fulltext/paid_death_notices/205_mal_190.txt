
        FITZGIBBON--John P., died peacefully on February 6, 2004 in Tucson, Arizona at age 84. He is survived by his wife, Sophie Breckenridge FitzGibbon; his sister, Pat Bueler; his children, Timmie Saltzman, Mary Walsh, and John Jr., James, Bonnie, Susan, and Brian FitzGibbon; 11 grandchildren and one greatgranddaughter. John was the son of James E. FitzGibbon and Margaret P. Byrne, and was predeceased by his sister, Ky Nuland. He was born in New York City.
