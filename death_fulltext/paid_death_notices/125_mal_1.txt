
        MISHKIN-Robert. Born on June 17, 1916, died May 30, 1997. Beloved husband of Sylvia and father of Marc. Adored uncle of Ruby, to three generations of nieces and nephews. Practicing attorney for 45 years and vice president of Kravet Fabrics Inc. Youngest and last of six brothers, sons of Max and Mary Mishkin. Services Monday, 1:00 PM, ''Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapel'', 76th Road and Queens Blvd., Forest Hills.
        MISHKIN-Robert (Ruby). A man for all seasons, who inspired us through his dedication, concern, patriotism and charity. We mourn his passing and will continue to live our lives according to these virtues. A.L.N.N.
