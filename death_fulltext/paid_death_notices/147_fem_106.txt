
        LEVINE-Celia. April 1, 1907-April 10, 1999. Rest in peace, Aunt Celia. Predeceased by her adored husband, Louis Levine; devoted daughter of the late Esther and Benjamin Schreiber; loving sister of Sidney Schreiber of Chicago, the late Shirley Seiler and Gladys Braun of New York; caring aunt of Betsy Katz, Phyllis Leventhal, Jane Saltoun, Eliot Schreiber and Judy Teller. Great-aunt, in every way, of nine. Funeral today, Riverside Memorial Chapel.
