
        SALLOVITZ--Myron Morris, April 21, 1927--April 4, 2006. Rancho Palos Verdes, CA, originally of New York. Beloved husband of Elinore (nee Kass). Survived by his sister Rhoda Rosenfeld, his daughters, Joy Firshein, Karen Sallovitz, Linda Sallovitz, granddaughters Jessica and Jenna Firshein. Myron was a WWII Jewish War Veteran and a lifetime member of the Boy Scouts of America. Loving and devoted husband, father, grandfather and friend. He will be in our hearts forever.
