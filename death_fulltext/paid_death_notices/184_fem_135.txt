
        CRANE-Sylvia E. Of Princeton, NJ, and Woods Hole, MA, died on Friday. She was the wife of John Crane who died in 1982; and since then companion to Henry Hemmendinger. She left two sons, Charles and Thomas, and four grandchildren. She was the author of ''White Silence'' and ''Czechoslovakia: Anvil of the Cold War'', and was Vice Chairman of the Committee to Abolish HUAC. The funeral will be private.
