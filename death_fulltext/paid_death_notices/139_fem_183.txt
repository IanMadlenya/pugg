
        BEER-Florence Fay. Beloved wife of Walter E. Beer, Jr. for 68 years. Loving mother to John and David. Devoted grandmother to Thomas, Amy, Jeffrey, Elizabeth, Andrew and to her great-grandchildren Tea, Nuala and India. Florence worked for many years for the Public Education Association and Vocational Advisory Service of New York and various organizations in Brooklyn Heights where they lived for 35 years. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Keene Valley Neighborhood House, Keene Valley, N.Y. 12943. A memorial service is planned.
