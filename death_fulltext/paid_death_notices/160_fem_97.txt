
        WHITE-Helaine Shari. On May 9, 2000 at age 33. Taken from us all too soon. Beloved daughter of Donald and Rhoda White. Devoted and cherished sister of Renee and Hillari and their husbands, Ronnie Spiegel and Peter Boritz. Adored aunt of Samantha and Brittany Reeps and Marc Spiegel. Treasured granddaughter of Minnie Namanworth. Loving niece of Adele Berman and Arthur White. Loved by cousins and friends. Her wit, courage, beauty, humor and love will remain with us forever. All who she touched will remember her. Services today, 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory to the Diabetes Research Institute, 133 Spruce Street, Cedarhurst, New York 11516.
