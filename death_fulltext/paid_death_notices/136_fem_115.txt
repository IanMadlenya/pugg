
        MEDINE-Edith D., 89, Arlington, VA., formerly of West Palm Beach. Survived by her beloved husband William; children Amy Stein, Laurie and Art Alexander, grandchildren Eric, Tony, George and Mary Stein, Paul, Stacia, Peter and Nancy Saperstone, great grandchildren Amanda, Zachary and Emily. Edith, and her husband, Bill, were the Founding Directors of Camp Echo Lake, Warrensburg, New York, 1946-1964. Her warmth and vivacity set a tone for her campers and the two generations that follow.
