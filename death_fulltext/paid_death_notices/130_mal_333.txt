
        GREEN-Thomas M. III. Headmaster, died peacefully at home in Savannah, GA, Thanksgiving Day. Born Baltimore, MD, 1922. For 16 years was headmaster of the Peck School in Morristown, NJ. He is survived by his wife, Dulcinea Lee Green; two brothers, Douglas Sadtler Green and John McEldowney Green; two sons, Thomas M. Green IV and Jonathan Wales Green; four stepchildren, Nichol duPont Conway, Peter Rhett duPont, Alicia Lee duPont and Scott Townsend duPont; and four grandchildren.
