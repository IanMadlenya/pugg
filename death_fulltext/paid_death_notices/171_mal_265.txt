
        PENTZ-Herbert C. A sixth generation American, all of whose paternal ancestors since 1710 were born, lived and died in New York City, died at the age of 98 years, a 56 year resident of Pelham, New York. He graduated from Columbia College and Columbia Law School in New York City, to both of which he made substantial yearly contributions including a college student scholarship fund & practiced law in New York City for sixty years. He leaves a daughter, Coralee Pentz Noble, six grandchildren and a great grandchild.
