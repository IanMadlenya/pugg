
        WEISS--Mandel (Manny). 96. Brilliant and loving died March 4, 2005 in Miami, FL. Formerly of Long Beach, NY. He was CEO of Rheingold Breweries. Preceded in death by wife Sari. Survived by daughter Diane and husband Maurice Mufson, M.D., grandchildren Michael (Dorie) Mufson, Karen (Greg) Kleckner, Pamela Mufson & three great-grandchildren.
