
        CENSOR--Jacques. The Board of Trustees of Yeshiva University and Boards of Directors of its Stern College for Women and Bernard Revel Graduate School of Jewish Studies mourn the passing of the beloved father of Dr. Monique C. (and Mordecai D.) Katz, vice chairman of the Stern College Board and University Benefactor with her husband (a YU trustee and chairman of the Revel Board), and Walter (and Linda) Censor, and adoring grandfather and great-grandfather. Our sincerest condolences to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Marjorie Diener Blenden Chairman, SCW Board of Directors Arthur Hyman, Dean BRGS
