
        CURTIN-Murray. On May 3, 2000, at age 66, after a short illness. Graduate of Hamilton College, Heidelberg University and Oxford University. He would want to be remembered as former Editor-inChief and Publisher of the Random House College Department, literary agent and ''Village type.'' Survived by his twin sister Ann Curtin, loving daughters Arielle Curtin and Becky Curtin, grandchildren Chadeayne and Rees Curtin-De Tar. No service will be held. Donations in his name to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, Palliative Care Center would be appreciated. Rest in peace Dad, we love you.
