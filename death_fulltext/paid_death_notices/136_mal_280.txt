
        OGORZALY - Stanley Francis, M.D. of North Palm Beach, Florida on May 21, 1998. He was Chief of Cardiology and Co-Chairman of Intensive Care Unit, St. John's Riverside Hospital, Yonkers, New York. Graduate of Fordham University & Georgetown University receiving his medical degree. Survived by sister-in-law Jewel C. Ogorzaly, 7 nieces & nephews. Funeral Mass in North Palm Beach. Donations to the American Heart Association, 2701 North Australian Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407
