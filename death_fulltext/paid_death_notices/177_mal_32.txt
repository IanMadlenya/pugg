
        ASHTON-Thomas J., 21. Of Woodside, NY, died tragically and innocently on September 11, 2001, while working on the 95th floor of One World Trade Center. Precious and cherished son of John and Kathleen Ashton. Beloved brother of Colleen & Mary Ashton. Loving boyfriend of Jacqueline Crilley. Dedicated and loving grandson of Lewis & Kathleen Worth, Virginia and the late John Ashton and great-grandmother Agnes Carroll. Tommy's life and memory will also be cherished by a large, loving extended family and dear friends. Tommy lived his entire short life in St. Sebastian's parish in Woodside, Queens, where he attended St. Sebastian's School. A 1997 graduate of Archbishop Molloy High School, Briarwood, Tommy excelled as a competitive swimmer and was captain of both Molloy's swim team and the Flushing Flyer's swim team. Tommy attended St. Francis College in Brooklyn as a political science major. He was an IBEW Local 3 apprentice electrician who admired the beauty, majesty and purpose of the World Trade Center towers. A gift to family and friends, Tommy was a loving, kind, deeply introspective and spiritual person who enjoyed relaxing weekends in the mountains, fun evenings with family and friends and Mets games. Family and friends will honor Tommy's memory at a Memorial Wake on Thursday, October 4, and Friday, Oct. 5, at Kennedy-Roth Funeral Home 41-45 58th Street, Woodside, Queens (from 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM). A Memorial Mass will be held on Saturday, October 6, at 10:30AM at St. Sebastian's Church, 58th St and Roosevelt Avenue, Woodside. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made payable to: Archbishop Molloy High School, 83-53 Manton Street, Briarwood, NY 11435 (please indicate on check: for The Thomas Ashton Memorial Fund).
