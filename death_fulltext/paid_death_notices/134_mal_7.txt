
        HAMMETT-John A. Retired Vice President of Douglas Gibbons, Holliday & Ives. On February 27, 1998, after a long illness. His many friends mourn the passing of this kind and generous man. Memorial mass 10 AM, Tuesday, March 3 at Saint John the Evangelist Church, 1st Ave. and 55 St., NY. Private interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
