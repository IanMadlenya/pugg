
        DONAT-Leona. Survivor of the Warsaw Ghetto, Majdanek and Auschwitz. Beloved wife of the late Alexander, loving mother of William & his wife Ellen, devoted sister-in-law of Maria Liberman. Adored grandmother of Jocelyn, Ted and his wife Avril, Gregory and his wife Suzanne and greatgrandmother of Annalisa, Caroline, Lindsay and Madeleine. She imparted her strength and wisdom to us. The family gatherings where she reigned shall be missed. Private graveside service.
