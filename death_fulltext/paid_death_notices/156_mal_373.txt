
        RIATTI-Dr. Pierluigi. Died Monday, January 24 in Tahiti where he was vacationing. He was 74. He was an Attending Anesthesiologist at United Hospital in Port Chester NY where he served as Chairman of the Department from 1974-1986. Dr. Riatti was predeceased by his beloved wife Doris O'Donnell Riatti and is survived by three daughters, Lisa Gatti of Greenwich, CT, Suzanne Dudley and Julia Riatti of Dallas, TX, a son David Riatti of Dallas, TX and his beloved fiancee, Marguerite Sargeant.
