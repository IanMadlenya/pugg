
        LUNDBERG-Helen., 83. Died on Thursday, January 28, 1999 at her home in Briarcliff Manor, NY. Longtime resident of Briarcliff Manor. She was born in Manhattan and had been a longtime member of the Junior League. She was the wife of the late Nils A. Lundberg, whom she married on January 16, 1937. She is survived by her daughter Linda Mosbarger of Green Valley, AZ, and her sons Jon Lundberg of Briarcliff Manor and Anders Lundberg of Birmingham, MI. She is also survived by her seven grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Visiting hours are on Sunday, January 31, from 2-4 and 7-9PM at the Waterbury and Kelly Funeral Home, Briarcliff Manor. Services will be at 10AM on Monday at the funeral home with a private interment at Kensico Cemetery.
