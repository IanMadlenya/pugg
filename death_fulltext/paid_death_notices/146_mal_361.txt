
        BIJUR-Herbert I. Passed away peacefully on March 17, 1999 at age 88. A Haverford graduate with a successful career in retailing and publishing culminating in the position of President, McCalls Pattern Company. Avid orchid grower, active at the New York Botanical Garden and Bartlett Arboretum, Stamford, CT, flutist, fisherman & Mark Twain collector. Enriched the lives of family, friends and community. Survived by his wife, Marion, children Peter, Pricilla and Polly, and eight grandchildren. Services will be held privately. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to a charity of one's choice.
