
        ROSS-Frank, Sr. The Metropolitan Council on Jewish Poverty mourns the untimely loss of our dear friend and supporter Frank Ross, Sr., Co-chair of HRH Construction. Frank was a pioneer in construction and the development of housing for the elderly poor. Our condolences to his loving family. We will miss him. Merryl H. Tisch, President Joseph C. Shenker, President-Elect William E. Rapfogel, CEO Rabbi David Cohen, Senior Executive Consultant
