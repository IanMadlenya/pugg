
        BECKOFF-Samuel, former chairman of the English Department at Music & Art High School, died Friday at the age of eighty-five. Dr. Beckoff taught at City College and Yeshiva University. He was the author of several textbooks, two novels and a family memoir. He had taught and chaired the English Department at Long Island City High School and taught at Queens Vocational High School. He is survived by two daughters Laurie Wellman and Jill Nagy; four grandchildren and a great-granddaughter. His wife, the former Beatrice Ehrlich, predeceased him. Funeral services will be private. Those who wish to, may make donations to Alumni and Friends of LaGuardia High School of the Arts, PO Box 231485, New York, New York 10023-1479. For further information, contact Bryce Funeral Home Inc., Troy, New York, 518-272-7281.
