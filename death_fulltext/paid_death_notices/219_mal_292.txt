
        JETTMAR--Emil F., 85, of Sherman, CT, died April 21, 2005. Son of Frantiska and Bedrich. Survived by wife, Eleanore; children, Charles & Barbara; sisters-in-law, Mary and Barbara, and the late Bedrich Jettmar II. He served as a B-25 pilot in 487th Bombardment Squadron of 12th US Army Air Force in WWII. Graduated from Columbia University and worked as a commercial architect.
