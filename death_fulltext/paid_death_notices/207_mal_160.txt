
        BAILEY--Elden. The New York Philharmonic mourns with deep sadness the passing, on April 13th, of Elden ''Buster'' Bailey, a proud member of the percussion section from 1949-91. A Jazz Musician, teacher, author, and Juilliard graduate, he charmed colleagues with his ready smile and home movies of Philharmonic tours, now housed in the Archives. We extend condolences to his wife, Barbara, and family. Lorin Maazel, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Zarin Mehta, Exec Director Members of the Staff
        BAILEY--Elden. The Juilliard School is deeply saddened by the death of Elden ''Buster'' Bailey. As a distinguished member of our percussion department, he helped educate new generations of percussionists around the world. We extend our condolences to his entire family. Joseph W. Polisi, President The Juilliard School
