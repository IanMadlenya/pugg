
        METH--Arnold David, 83, of Boca Raton, FL, died suddenly on January 31, 2007. Beloved husband of Elaine, loving father of Richard Meth (Melissa) and Deborah Fleitz (David) and the late Stephen Meth (Dorothy). Dear grandfather of Michelle, Rachel and Justin. Also survived by stepdaughters Bonnie Olguin (Jerome) and Laura Stobbe (Jan) and their children. WWII vet, valued friend of many, he will be missed.
