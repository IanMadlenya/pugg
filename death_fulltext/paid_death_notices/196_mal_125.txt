
        WITTER-William., age 73, of New York, NY died Sunday, May 11, 2003, at his home after a short illness. Born in San Francisco, California, Mr. Witter has been a New York resident since 1953. He was the principal of William D. Witter, Inc., an investment counseling firm that he founded 26 years ago. He is survived by his wife, Inger Kullberg Witter, their two children, Michael and Christina, of New York City, and his sister, Ann Witter, of San Francisco, CA. He is also survived by his former wife, Susan Rentschler Witter, of New York City, and their four children, Virginia Woods, of Altamont, NY; Elizabeth Tacy Mayer, of Los Altos, CA; Sidney Daire, of New York City; and William Perkins Witter, of Boulder, CO. He is survived by nine grandchildren. Memorial services will be held at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71st Street, New York City, on Tuesday May 20, 2003, at 2:00 PM. An informal reception in the offices of William D. Witter, Inc. 51st floor, Citicorp Center, 153 East 53rd Street, will follow these services
