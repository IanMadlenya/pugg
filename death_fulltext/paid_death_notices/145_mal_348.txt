
        PECKELIS-Arthur. Aka Artie Peck and Doc. Passed away on February 15, 1999. Born in Brooklyn, NY. He was for many years in the garment industry. He was a generous and well loved man who had many devoted friends especially the police officers of the Midtown South Precinct. Mr. Pecklis is survived by his Aunts Dr. Edith Sands of NYC and Lillian Clausen of Brooklyn as well as his cousin Masia Massett of London, England and Dr. Robert Abeloff of Beverly Hills, California.
