
        CLAYBURGH-Albert H., died peacefully on February 6, 1997 at St. Luke's Hospital. He is survived by his lover, companion, wife Sandra Church, three children Corlies Clayburgh, Jill Rabe, and James Clayburgh, eight grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. A memorial gathering will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Princeton University would be appreciated.
