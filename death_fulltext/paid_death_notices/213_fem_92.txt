
        BEREZUK--Marilyn M., 76 years old, died October 5, 2004 after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Her loving husband of 56 years, Harry Berezuk, her daughter, Corinne M. Berezuk, and her sonin-law, Michael A. Stieber, survive her. A memorial mass to celebrate her life and her strength will be held at the Church of the Assumption, 55 Cranberry St, Brooklyn Heights, NY at 11AM Monday, October 18, 2004. In lieu of flowers, those wishing to join the family to advance cancer care research, please forward contributions in Mrs. Berezuk's memory to Dr. Philip A. Paty, Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NY, 10021.
