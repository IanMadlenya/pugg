
        ABRAMSON--Doris Waters. Of Boise, Idaho, formerly of Somers and Pleasantville, NY, passed away peacefully on January 18. She was 90. She was predeceased in 1992 by Arnold, her husband of 57 years, and is survived by a daughter, Laurie Pulice and her husband, Vincent, of Boise, Idaho; a son, Peter Abramson and his wife, Kathy, of Bedford, NY; a brother, Morton Waters of Goshen, NY; a grandson, David Abramson and his wife, Amy Liebesman, of Larchmont, NY and two greatgrandchildren, Simon and Jillian Abramson. Interment will be at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY, on Thursday, January 27, at 1:00PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Doctors Without Borders or your local Humane Society.
