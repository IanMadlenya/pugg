
        SCHOENFELD--Alice Forgosh on July 20th quietly in her sleep after a 100 year and 3 month life. Wife of the late Edward Schoenfeld. She will be remembered as the most loving mother, grandmother, and great grandmother; by her sons Norman and Lester, daughters-in-law Michele and linda, grandchildren Steven, Susie, Jen, and Dana, and great grandchildren Jacob, and Mathew. Services at Kol AMi, 252 Soundview Avenue, White Plains, Sunday 11am.
