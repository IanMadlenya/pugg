
        GELLER--George, 91, of Scarsdale, NY, died May 1, 2004. ''Uncle George'', Owner of Camp Winston, Sackett Lake, NY, Developer in Westchester. Husband of the late Fay, father of Michael and Sandra, grandfather of Wendy, Bob, Lori and Jackie, great-grandfather of Alessandra, Rachel and Jake. He will always be in our hearts.
