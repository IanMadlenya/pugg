
        LUNDELL-Kert. The Board of Trustees and membership of Ensemble Studio Theatre mourn the loss of our great friend and Trustee, Kert Lundell. A distinguished designer and long-time supporter of new theatre and design talent, he will be sorely missed. We send our deepest sympathy to his wife Bretta, his son Eric and daughter, Kate. Curt Dempster, Artistic Director Daniel Selznick, Board Chairman
