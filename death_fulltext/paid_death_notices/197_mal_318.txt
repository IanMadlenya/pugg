
        NONAS--Dr. Gerson. On June 28, 2003, at age 90. A dedicated internist of exceptional talent, beloved by his patients for his thoroughness and kindness, who right up to the day of his retirement at age 88, was still making house calls. Survived by his daughters Barbara Nonas and her husband Robert Hunter, Elisabeth Nonas and her partner Nancy K. Bereano, his grandson Luke Nonas Hunter, his sisters Betty Lash and Miriam Blechner, and brother Elliott. Also survived by hislongtime companion Stephanie Kaufman. Predeceased by his wife Marjorie Crystal Nonas. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
