
        MALLEY--Terence, 67, died July 8, 2004, in Tacoma, WA. Professor Emeritus Long Island University, Brooklyn Campus, he was the author of ''Richard Brautigan,'' a critical study; a poet and a baseball scholar. He will be greatly missed by friends and his surviving family, including his wife Kathleen, niece Alexandra Lynch, and nephews Robert and Geoffrey Lynch.
