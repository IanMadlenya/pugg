
        HANDLER--Lillian Nerenberg. Born Newark, N.J., March 3, 1912. Wife of the late Sol Handler, longtime resident of Englewood, N.J. and Hollywood, FL. Beloved wife & mother. Died January 30, 2005 peacefully at home. Survived by her loving daughter and only child, Joy. Her laughter and love will be sorely missed. Contributions to Hospice By The Sea, 1531 W. Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33486.
