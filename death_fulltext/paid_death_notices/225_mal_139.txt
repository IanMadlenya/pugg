
        WALLACE--James J., 84, of NYC and Hillsdale, New York. Died October 11, 2005. A man of great wit who lived life to its fullest. Jim was happily married to the late Coreen Coleman for 58 years. After serving in the navy in WWII, he had a long and distinguished career in the textile industry both in the USA and abroad. He was chairman of Vicars Cross (USA) Ltd and CEO of St. James Traders. He is survived by hischildren Frank, Jay, Coreen and Ellen and grandchildren Christopher, Patrick, Freya, Molly, Gretchen, Kirsten, Jessica, Oliver and three great grandchildren Anne, Elizabeth and Katherine. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, on Thursday, 2-4 and 6-8PM. Funeral services Friday, 10AM, at The Church of Heavenly Rest, 90th Street and Fifth Avenue
