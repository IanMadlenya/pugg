
        MECKLER-Herman L., died 9 May, in Longboat Key, Florida. He is survived by his wife of 63 years, Lillian; his children, Nancy and Alan, and six grandchildren. Born in Cleveland, he lived most of his working life in New York and London. He was the founder and CEO of Leaseplan International. Memorial service 11 May, 3:30 PM, at Temple Beth Israel, Bay Isle Drive, Longboat Key, Fla. Memorial donations may be made to Sarasota Memorial Hospital Century Foundation, 1838 Waldemere Street, Sarasota, FL 34239. For more information contact Toale Brothers Funeral Home, Colonial Chapel, 941-955-4171.
