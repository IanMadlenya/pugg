
        LaPORTE--James A. Died May 26. Born November 11, 1928 in Kankakee, IL. LaPorte was vice president of Lionel D. Edie Co. and Edie International. He also worked for Merrill Lynch and Abel/Noser. James was a proud member of the Explorers Club, the Yacht Club and the New York University Club. Per his wishes there is no service. His ashes will be scattered over the waters of Bimini. James is survived by his brother Thomas, his daughter Denise and his beloved granddaughters JoEsther and Margaret.
