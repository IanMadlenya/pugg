
        MILLER-Elliot H., Sculptor and art therapist, died in New York City on April 30, at 60, of complications from heart surgery. Elliot maintained a studio in Brooklyn and his work appeared in 50 exhibitions worldwide during a 40 year career. His kind and gentle nature was most evident in his work with developmentally disabled adults at the League Center in Brooklyn. He is survived by his wife Joanne of Brooklyn, his sister Karen and niece Lisa of Miami, and many devoted friends.
