
        GOLDBERG--Bill. The members of the Diamond Dealers Club mourn the passing of Bill Goldberg, one of our industry's giants. A DDC member for nearly half a century, Mr. Goldberg was a President and Honorary President of our Club. A trusted colleague, and successful businessman, Bill's commitment to the highest standards of integrity and leadership will be sorely missed by all who knew him. His dynamic guidance of our industry is irreplaceable. Our sincerest condolences to his wife Mrs. Lili Goldberg, and to the Goldberg family. Jacob Banda, President
        GOLDBERG--William. The Gesher Foundation mourns the passing of its esteemed and beloved friend, a true Jewish visionary. Bill understood Israel's core values and acted to buttress them. His steadfast principles, guidance and unique style will be sorely missed. May Lili, his children and grandchildren be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Matthew J. Maryles, Chairman Dr. Daniel Tropper, President and Founder
