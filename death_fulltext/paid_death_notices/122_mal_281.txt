
        ROBINSON-Joseph R. Age 85, of Margate, FL. Mr. Robinson died Saturday evening at his home in Florida. For many years, a Superintendent of Electricians at Arc Electric of NYC. He is survived by his wife Ettie, his brother Harold, his daughter Iris Leopold, and her husband Robert, his son Gary and his wife Natalie, five grandchildren, Rebecca, Deborah, and Simon Leopold, and Aileen and Rima Robinson, and a greatgrandson, Zev Leopold. A graveside service will be held on Monday, March 24 at 2:30 PM, at King Solomon Cemetery in Clifton, NJ. For more information, call Bernheim-Apter-Goldsticker Chapel, 201-763-3505.
