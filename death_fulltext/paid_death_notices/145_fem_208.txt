
        PERKIN-Carol. Daughter of George and Ann Perkin of West Orange, NJ, died Tuesday morning. Her loved ones will miss her extraordinary grace, her courage, and her laughter. Ms. Perkin, 44, was a reporter for the Associated Press and a valued writer at ABC's ''Good Morning America''. A Memorial Service will be held tonight, February 25 from 7-9PM at the Quinn-Hopping Funeral Home in Livingston, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations can be sent to the American Cancer Society/Breast Cancer Research or The West Orange Public Library.
