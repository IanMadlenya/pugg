
        SILVERSTONE-Rose Orliansky of Providence, RI, Jamaica Estates, NY and Orient, L.I. died November 21 just short of her 90th birthday. Rose had been a librarian at Queens College, James Monroe High School and Forest Hills High School. Beloved wife of the late Max, beloved mother of Vida Wagner, and David Silverstone, grandmother of five, great-grandmother of two. Graveside services New Montefiore Cemetery, L.I., on Sunday, November 23, at 1 PM. For information 1-800-382-4145.
