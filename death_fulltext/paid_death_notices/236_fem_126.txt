
        WALSH--Ann H. On September 20, 2006 of Garden City, New York and Stuart, Florida. Devoted wife of the late Edward. Loving mother of Edward, Patricia and Timothy and mother-in-law of Alice and Marie. Devoted grandmother of Peter, Stephen, Bridget and Connor. Graduate of The Mary Louis Academy and St. John's University. A Golfer ahead of her time. Her family, golf and the Lord were her passions. Twelve time Golf Club champion of the Cherry Valley Club, also Golf Champion of the Mariner Sands Country Club. A long time member of St. Joseph's Church and St. Christopher's Church. Her humor, class, grace and spirit will be missed by her loved ones and friends. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave. at 12th Street, Garden City 2-4 and 7-9 PM Friday. Mass of Christian burial St. Joseph's Church, 9:30 AM Saturday. Interment Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing.
