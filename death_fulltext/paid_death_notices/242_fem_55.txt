
        WERTHEIM--Arlene (nee Person), 75, previously of Monsey, NY. Died of cancer at her home in Lake Worth, FL, on March 5, 2007. A loving and caring wife, mother, and grandmother. Beloved wife of 54 years to John. Cherished by her children Joshua, Sara, and David, and grandchildren Marc, Melissa, Sydney, and Stephanie. Loving mother-inlaw to Armand, Deborah, and the late Linda. A blessing and joy to all who knew her heart, humor, intelligence, kindness and love. Services to be held Thursday March 8, 1pm at Hellman Memorial Chapel, 15 State St., Spring Valley, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County or The American Cancer Society.
