
        KERR--Louise H., Formerly of Shelter Island and Peconic Landing, Greenport, L.I. on September 29, 2004 in her 85th year. Former Vice-President, Citibank, New York City. Former Director, Cleveland H. Dodge Foundation and former member and trustee Charlotte Palmer Phillips Foundation, both in New York City. Survived by her cousin William Weiss of Paramus, NJ and Naples, Fl. Memorial services took place at the Shelter Island Presbyterian Church on October 4th. For information HortonMathie Funeral Home of Greenport, LI.
