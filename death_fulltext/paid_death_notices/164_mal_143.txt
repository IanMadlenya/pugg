
        BRIGGS-Taylor R., Esq. The New York Law School community is deeply saddened by the death of Taylor R. Briggs, Esq., former Chairman of the Board of Trustees and a true friend of New York Law School. Taylor Briggs played a vital role in supporting the School's mission in legal education and ensuring its future. He will be greatly missed for his friendship and kindness and for his invaluable contribution to the School as a Trustee and as a member of the Advisor Board of the Center for New York City Law.
