
        ROSS-Chester. The Board of Directors and Staff of the New York Association for New Americans, Inc. (NYANA) note with sadness the passing of Chester Ross, beloved husband of Blanche Ross, esteemed Honorary Board Member of NYANA, and past-President of the Agency. On behalf of the NYANA family, we extend our heartfelt condolences to Blanche and to her daughter, Dorie. Laura G. Ross, President Mark Handelman, Exec. V.P.
