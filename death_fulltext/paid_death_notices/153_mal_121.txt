
        SMITH-Edmund Dutilh. Of New York City. Died on October 6 after a long illness. He was born on March 3, 1916. He is survived by his wife Renee, a brother George, two nephews Douglas and Gordon, two nieces Mandy and Kim. He was an honorary member of the Amateur Comedy Club in New York City. He will be buried in the cemetery of the Church of the Redeemer in Bryn Mawr, Pa.
