
        MORRIS--David, 65, Died June 20 at his home in Essex, CT. Son of Perry and Evelyn Morris of Great Neck, New York and brother of Stephen Morris of Santa Barbara, CA. Devoted surrogate grandfather of Matthew Michael Fabiani. Retired soldier and policeman, member of Troop F, 196th Light Infantry Brigade, Vietnam. Historian, supporter of children's causes and events. Dave will be missed by his many friends in Old Saybrook, Essex, and Westport, CT. Memorial service, Old Saybrook Point Pavillion, Old Saybrook, CT, Thursday, September 8, 2005 at 9:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, make contributions to American Red Cross or American Cancer Society in Dave's name.
