
        FLEMING-Josephine. Widow of John Fleming. Mother of Joan and Carol. Died March 20, 1998 in Phoenix, Arizona. Well known throughout the rare book community from her early days at the Rosenbach Company, to her many activities with her husband John, the legendary successor to Dr. A.S.W. Rosenbach, and lately as a Trustee of the Rosenbach Museum and Library, she was also a fierce competitor on the bowling alley, at the bridge table and on the tennis court. She leaves, as well, her grandchildren Holly Johnson, Heather Meyer and Alexander Cremin, six great grandchildren and her many friends in Larchmont.
