
        ORLAND--Helen Kurtz. Born in New York, July 25, 1918, passed away in Los Angeles, Ca., April 20, 2004. She graduated Hunter College and received a Master's degree from Columbia University Teachers College. She survived her parents Ray and Aaron Kurtz and her brother Lieutenant Commander George Kurtz. She is survived by her beloved husband of 62 years Paul Orland, her adoring children Laura Adler (Franklin) and Malcolm Orland (Andrea). She will be deeply missed by her four grandchildren Amy and Gabrielle Adler, Daniel and Adam Orland. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation to the charity of your choice.
