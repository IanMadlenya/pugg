
        ABBELL-Joseph J. The board of overseers of the Library of the Jewish Theological Seminary records with profound sorrow the death of Joseph J. Abbell. Beloved father of longtime board member, Louise A. Holland, he was a staunch supporter of J.T.S. and its library. May his entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Ellen Kapito, Chairwoman Library Board of Overseers Rabbi Mayer Rabinowitz Librarian
