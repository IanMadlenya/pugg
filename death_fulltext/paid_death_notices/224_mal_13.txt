
        ROTTENBERG--Ira M. age 75 of Paramus, N.J. raised in Manhattan, on September 1, 2005 at his home with his family at his side. Survived by his loving wife of 49 years, Enid (nee Bergel) of Providence, R.I., his children and their spouses, Mark and Elise Rottenberg, Hank and Ilene Rottenberg, and Beth and Scott Rappoport, his brother and sister-in-law, Alan and Barbara Rottenberg, and seven adored grandchildren, Jason, Haley, Rachel, Matthew, Jackie, David and Jared, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. He was a graduate of Stuyvesant H.S. and Lowell (Mass) Textile Institute, a Lieutenant in the U.S. Coast Guard, who served in the Korean War, was a Past Commander of Post 669 of the Jewish War Veterans, was a longtime devoted member of the Jewish Community Center of Paramus and spent his life working in the textile industry.
