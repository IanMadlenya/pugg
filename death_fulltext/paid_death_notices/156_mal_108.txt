
        LOUCHHEIM-William S. Sr. The President, Chancellor, Boards of Governors and Overseers, faculty, administration and students of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion record with profound sorrow the death of William S. Louchheim Sr., a member of the Los Angles Board of Overseers for decades. A recipient of the Doctor of Humane Letters degree, honoris causa from the College-Institute, Mr. Louchheim was honored for his singular role in the founding and development of the Los Angeles School and for his devotion to Jewish values and ideals. The Jerome H. Louchheim School of Judaic Studies, named in memory of his father, personifies Mr. Louchheim's lifelong commitment to Jewish education and Jewish continuity. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family whose loss we share. May the family find comfort together with all those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman, Pres. Dr. Alfred Gottschalk, Chancellor Dr. Lewis M. Barth, Dean Los Angeles School Mr. Burton Lehman, Chair Board of Governors
