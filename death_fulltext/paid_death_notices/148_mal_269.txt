
        PIECH-Peter M. 80, of Boca Raton, FL, died Tuesday, May 25, 1999 at the Boca Raton Community Hospital. Mr. Piech was a member of The Producers Guild of America in California, N.A.T.A.S., and was the Executive Producer of the Rocky & Bullwinkle Show, Underdog, and Judge Roy Bean. He is survived by his wife Olga and two children, Pam Sampson and Peter, both of NJ, and four grandchildren. Services will be held in Boca Raton, FL. For information contact, The Babione Funeral Home, Boca Raton, FL.
