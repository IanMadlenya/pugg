
        SCHLESINGER--Arthur M., Jr., 89. Husband of Alexandra, father of Stephen, Katharine, Christina, Andrew, Robert and Peter; adoring grandfather of Hugh, Sarah and Chun. Professor and student of history, distinguished author, public servant, activist and incandescent personality, he will be greatly missed. Memorial services will be at the Cooper Union on Cooper Square at 10:30am on April 23. Charitable donations in his name can be directed to the Arthur and Elizabeth Schlesinger Library, Radcliffe Institute, 10 Garden Street, Cambridge, MA 02138 (should include ''Arthur Schlesinger memorial''); or to the Arthur M. Schlesinger, Jr. Award in American History at the Franklin and Eleanor Roosevelt Institute, 4079 Albany Post Road, Hyde Park, NY 12538.
