
        ADLER-Herbert . Died on Wednesday, January 6, 1999 in Naples, FL, having taken a very difficult path with great courage. Herbert was the only son of Lothar and Ellen Adler, and dearest friend and beloved husband of Susan. He was husband in first marriage to Elin and father of Eric and Marc. Funeral service will take place at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, New York on Sunday, January 10, 1999 at 11:30 A.M.
