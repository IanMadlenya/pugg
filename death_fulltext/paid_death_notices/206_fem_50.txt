
        SOLOMON--Ruth K. Beloved mother of Susan Solomon, mother-in-law of Paul Goldberger, grandmother of Adam Hirsh and Ben and Alex Goldberger, great grandmother of Thibeaux Hirsh, died on March 2nd. She cherished all of life's gifts, from Mozart to Scrabble, and she inspired us as she met every challenge with courage and love. In lieu of flowers her family would be grateful for contributions in her memory to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
