
        BUCHIN--John D. Age 50, on November 11, 2005, in Los Angeles, CA, after a long, courageous battle against lymphoma. Beloved son of Jean and the late Irving, dear brother of Peter and brotherin-law of Marilyn, cherished uncle of Benjamin. Graduate of the Horace Mann School; B.A. University of Pennsylvania; M.A. Northwestern University. He was a television reporter and journalist in NYC, and later a marketing director for corporations. A talented and creative individual who was well-loved by many people. His courage in the face of illness was an inspiration to us all. He will be missed dearly. Contributions may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
