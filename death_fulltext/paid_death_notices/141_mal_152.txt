
        SANDLER-Joseph J. International Universities Press mourns the death of our esteemed author, Joseph J. Sandler, and extends heartfelt condolences to his wife, Anne-Marie Sandler and his family. He was a noted psychoanalyst with an extensive international following and a prolific writer in the field of psychoanalysis. Among his 11 books he published with International Universities Press are ''Freud's Models of the Mind, Analysis of Defense'', and ''The Patient and the Analyst''. He will be deeply missed by all.
