
        ADLER--Benjamin H., DPM. Age 93, of Whitestone, NY. Husband of the late Anne Greenfield Adler. Loving father of Charles and Peggy, Elliot and Helen. Devoted grandfather of Rachel and Julian. For 50 years he cared for his patients with creativity and dedication. He had the ability to see the excellence in all experience. His optimism, enthusiasm and gratitude for the blessings of life will continue to inspire us. Services today, 5 P.M., at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, NY.
