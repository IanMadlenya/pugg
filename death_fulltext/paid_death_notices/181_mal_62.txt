
        ZSEDENYI-Karoly. Died peacefully at home on February 2, at the age of 91. Principal dancer and ballet master at the Opera of Budapest and ballet master and choreographer at the Antwerp and Brussels Operas. Director of Zsedenyi Ballet Company and School in Santiago, Chili. Beloved ballet maestro to his students in New York. Visitation Tuesday, February 5, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, New York. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
