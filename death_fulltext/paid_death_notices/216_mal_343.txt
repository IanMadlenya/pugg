
        McCORMACK--George H., Jr., M.D. On January 25. Son of George H. and Natalie McCormack, brother of Elizabeth J. McCormack Aron, loving companion of Mary Murray, father of Jane (William Lauck), Hugh, George (Corinne), Julia, step-father of Lisa (Philip Crawford), grandfather of Adam, and step-grandfather of John and Kingsley Crawford. Trusted physician & valued teacher, graduate of Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons, Captain U.S. Army. Dr. McCormack retired after more than 50 years in private practice in New York City. He was an exceptional clinician and was widely recognized for his friendly and gentle approach to his patients whom he treated as equals with compassion and expertise. He was a skillful sailor, photographer, and a reader who loved to share his passion for literature with everyone he touched. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., Wednesday, Jan. 26, 2:00-4:00 and 6:008:00 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Ignatius Loyola, 980 Park Ave., Thursday, Jan. 27 at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the 'Robert A. Savitt and George H. McCormack Award', c/o Development Office, College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University, or The Visiting Nurse Service of New York Hospice Care.
