
        LASKEY-Sybil. Loving wife of J. Larry Laskey, devoted mother of Amy, Tom, mother-in-law of Debra Rothschild and sister of Martin Tomberg. She will be warmly remembered for her culinary skills, keen mind and vibrant personality. She reveled in the accomplishments not only of her children but also of her nephews and nieces. Services August 3, 12:30 PM, at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to your favorite charity.
