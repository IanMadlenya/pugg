
        ZOBEL-Isador. 89, passed away June 13, 1998. Survivors, children, Dr. Judith Zobel Feigin and husband Dr. Ralph D. Feigin, Joyce Zobel Opper and husband Edward Opper. Eight grandchildren; three great-grandchildren. He was an attorney and outstanding educator who for many years served as principal in several New York City schools where he introduced model educational programs that have been emulated nationally. Funeral services 1 PM, Tuesday, in Houston, TX, Levy Funeral Directors, 4525 Bissonnet, Bellaire, TX 77401. Call 713-660-6633 for information.
