
        MEYER-Fred A. On May 16, 2003 at age 80. Beloved husband of Ruth L. Meyer and the late Ruth E. Meyer. Loving father of Clifford and loving stepfather to Jonathan and David Leason and father-in-law of Alle Hall, Anna and Wendy. Adoring grandfather to Jordan, Justin, Jessica, Melanie and Morgan Leason. Dear brother to Eric and the late John. Mr. Meyer served in the 10th Mountain Division and in Counterintelligence during World War II. He retired as an investment adviser. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Sunday, May 18th 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Mt. Neboh Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
