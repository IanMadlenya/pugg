
        SIDERS-Irving. The Board members of The Association for the Help of Retarded Children-New York League, mourn the passing of Irving Siders, beloved husband of our Board member Pat. We extend our heartfelt sympathy and love to Pat and members of his bereaved family. Caroline Hirsch President The Association for the Help of Retarded Children
        SIDERS-Irving. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Irving Siders, on October 13, 1999. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec Director
        SIDERS-Irving. We join the theatre community in mourning the loss of our friend and colleague and extend our deepest sympathy to Pat and the family. Martin Richards, Sam Crothers The Producer Circle Co.
