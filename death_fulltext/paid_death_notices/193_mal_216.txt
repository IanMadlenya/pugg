
        YOUNG-Daniel M. of Atlanta GA at the age of 85. Former resident of NYC. Graduate of Rutgers University. Distinguished Pilot in the 384th bomber group, captain of the ''Damned Yankee'' B-17 in W.W. II, Daniel completed 23 missions over enemy occupied Europe. Retired 1971. A small business owner most of his life, at 75 he again became distinguished as a leading senior citizen employee in Atlanta in 1990. He is survived by his beloved wife Madeleine. His sons, Daniel and Robert, daughter in law Ingeborg. He will be missed.
