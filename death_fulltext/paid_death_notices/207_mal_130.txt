
        BUCHHOLTZ--Samuel Sidney. Passed away on April 11, 2004, after a valiant struggle. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Norma and is survived by his children Michael (Mary Uricchio) Buchholtz, Sheryl B. Rosenfield and Sheila B. Scharfman. Adored by his grandchildren Melissa, Daniel, Jessica, Charles, Alex, Emily, Warren and Nora. Loving brother of Stanley and Pearl. A brilliant legal scholar and teacher to many. His legacy of high moral character, scholarship, love of Judaism and kindness to all will endure forever. Services will be held Tuesday, April 13, 12:30 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions may be made to the Jewish National Fund.
