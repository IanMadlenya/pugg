
        MARTIN--Clifford James, 86, of Fredericksburg, VA died Monday, September 29, 2003. Born 1917 in Jersey City, N.J. He was a U.S. Army World War II veteran having served in the European Theatre and was the circulation manager for the Stars and Stripes Newspaper. Mr. Martin worked for the Journal American from 1945-1962 and then became The New York Times Director of Circulation from 1962-1985. Survivors include his wife of 59 years, Phyllis Martin; two daughters, Kathryn Wilewski and her husband, Henry; Bonnie Kurz and her husband, Jack; seven grandchildren and six great grandchildren. A funeral mass will be held at 11 A.M. Thursday, October 2, at St. Mary Catholic Church, Fredericksburg. Burial will be in Quantico National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers memorials may be made to the Mary Washington Hospice, 2300 Fall Hill Avenue, Suite 210, Fredericksburg, VA. 22401. On-line guest book convenantfuneralservice.com
