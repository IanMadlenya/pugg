
        MacISAAC - Brittan Campbell. Died on October 12th in Hopewell Junction, NY. Survivors include his three children, Gregory, Christine and Douglas, his former wife Virginia Cornell MacIsaac and two stepdaughters Holly Berkley and Heather Smith MacIsaac. Mr. MacIsaac was President of Building Systems Incorporated in Pittsburgh, PA, served in the US Navy Air Corps in World War II and was educated at Northwestern University. Memorial services Saturday October 27th in Hopewell Junction, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to The American Heart Association, New York City would be appreciated.
