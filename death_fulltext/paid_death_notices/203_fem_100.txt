
        RILEY--Mary Frances Kiernan, 78, of Bronxville, New York, on December 14, 2003. Predeceased by husband Timbes Riley (1959), longtime companion Raymond D. Von Frank (2001), and father John M. Kiernan Sr. Survived by her mother Agnes Kiernan. Loving mother of Lynn (Richard) Riley Feinson, of New York City, Brian (Maureen) Riley of Tampa, Florida, and Christopher (Karen) Riley, Esq., of Montebello, NY. Grandmother of Tim Riley Jr., John Patrick Riley, Margaret Mary Riley of Tampa & Katie Riley of Montebello. Mary Riley was a graduate of Columbia University (MLS), Fordham University and the College of New Rochelle. During World War II, she was a licensed pilot and a draftswoman for Sikorsky Aircraft. She was later employed by Fordham University for 36 years and retired as Director of Libraries for both the Rose Hill and Lincoln Center campuses. Mary was a longtime member and former president of the Bronxville Women's Club, an avid golfer, gardener and skier, and a parishioner of St. Joseph's Roman Catholic Church for over 40 years. She was ever loyal to her faith and to her family. Viewing will be in McGrath's Funeral Home, Bronxville, New York 2-4 and 7-9PM Thursday on December 18 with a funeral Mass at St. Joseph's Church in Bronxville on December 19 at 10 A.M. Burial will be in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, New York.
