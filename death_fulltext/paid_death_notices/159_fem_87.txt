
        KUTTNER-Phyllis. A loving and generous woman, died peacefully on april 9th at age 80. Married for over 50 years to Louis-David Kuttner, who preceded her in death; beloved mother of Robin Gausebeck, doting grandmother to Barbara and Daniel Fellars. She spent her years as a tireless working Mom, with a career as a NYC school teacher, who also found time to involve herself in her daughters school and in community affairs. In retirement, she was an active leader in the 5 Towns Senior Center. Phyllis will be deeply missed by the many people whose lives she touched. She requested that donations in her memory be sent to the Hospice Care Network.
