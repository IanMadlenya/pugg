
        LESSER-Frank. Commissioner of the Great Neck Water Pollution Control District, Democratic State Committeeman, died on September 4, 1997. He will be greatly missed by his beloved wife Deena, children Steven, Laura and Julie, seven adoring grandchildren, sister Carolyn Frucht, brother Stephen Marlowe. Services Sunday, 11 AM, Reconstructionist Synagogue, Plandome Rd., Manhasset. Memorial donations maybe sent to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, Project Ezra, or the Great Neck United Community Fund.
