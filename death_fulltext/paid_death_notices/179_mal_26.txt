
        BUCHNER-Marvin. At age 72. Loving husband of Phyllis. Adored father of Alan and Karen Buchner and Caryn and Tim Coville. Cherished grandfather of Jason, Ryan, Brian and Robbie. Devoted brother of Bunny, Doris, Alvin, Marty and the late Bernie. Dear uncle and friend. Respected attorney who loved boating, fishing, family and friends. Graduate of Catawba College and NYU Law School. Generous, witty and kind-hearted, a very special person who was one in a million. He will be truly missed by many. Services today, 12 noon, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I.
