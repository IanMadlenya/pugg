
        MARX-Vita Ortman Weiss. She died at home on October 27, after a long illness. Devoted wife of the late Ralph Marx. She is survived by her daughter Ellen Ash, her stepdaughters Alice Weafer, and Nancy Bocian & six grandchildren. She devoted her life to insuring affordable housing for low and middle income people working for the N.Y.C. Department of City Planning, The Lenox Hill Neighborhood Association, The Legislative Drafting Research Fund at Columbia Law School and HUD.
