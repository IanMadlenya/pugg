
        SHEARN-William G., 72. Of Sarasota, FL on March 19, 2002. Graduate of the Cornell School of Hotel Administration. Survived by one brother Gregory of Greentown, PA., and nieces Marcella Dietz of McLean, VA and Claudia O'Donnell of Winchester, MA. Predeceased by his sister Marcella Shearn Hanlon. Interment private Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Memorial Mass Friday, April 5, 2002, 11 AM Church of St. Matthew, R.C., Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. Memorials to Cornell School of Hotel Administration or the American Cancer Society appreciated.
