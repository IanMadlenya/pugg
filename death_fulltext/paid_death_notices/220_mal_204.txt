
        O'HARA--Barney. Died early Wednesday morning in peace and at home surrounded by his wife of 50 years, Ruth, his two sons, Steven and Jonathan, and his daughter, Sharon. He was a man much beloved by family and friends, one who touched many lives with his kindness and generosity. Barney was truly a man for all seasons. Ceremony Friday, May 20th, 9:30AM at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave., followed by burial in Sag Harbor at the Independent Jewish Cemetery.
