
        SIBINGA--Maarten Smit, M.D. Age 76 of Media, PA on November 16, 2003 from respiratory failure after a long illness. Dr. Sibinga, born in the Netherlands, was a pediatrician who specialized in gastroenterology and practiced for many years at St. Christopher's Hospital for Children in Philadelphia. He was also a dedicated amateur violinist and chamber musician. He is survived by his loving family, wife of 47 years, Cecilia (nee Bradbeer) children, Michael, Nicholas, Erica Sibinga, six grandchildren, twin brother, Joost and sister, Marijke both of the Netherlands. There will be a memorial meeting, Saturday, November 29, 2003 at Media Friends Meeting, 125 West Third St, Media, 11AM. Followed by a reception. The family will also be available prior to the meeting from 10:30AM at the Meetinghouse. Burial private. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Pendle Hill, Wallingford, PA.
