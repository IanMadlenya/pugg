
        SENFELD - S. William. The trustees, faculty, staff, and alumni/ ae of Bard College mourn the passing of dear friend and devoted alumnus, S. William Senfeld '62. Bill, who received the Bard medal in 1996, was a loyal alumnus whose energetic involvement within the Bard community will be sorely missed. Our deepest condolences to his family and many friends. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman, Board of Trustees Michael DeWitt, President, Bard-St. Stephens Alumni/ae Association
