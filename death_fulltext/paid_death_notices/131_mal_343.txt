
        ROSE-William B. Age 72. Of Mahwah, died Thursday, December 25, 1997, at his residence. Born in Staten Island, he lived in Franklin Lakes before moving to Mahwah eleven years ago. Mr. Rose was the Director of Distribution and Traffic for Witco Chemical Corp of Greenwich, Connecticut before his retirement in 1990. He was a World War II Navy Veteran. He was a member and past President of the Traffic Club of New York, and a member of the National Defense Executive Reserve, U.S. Department of Transportation. Member of Grace United Methodist Church, Wyckoff. Surviving are his wife, Elizabeth, nee Longair, one son, William B. Jr. of Martinsville, New Jersey, two daughters, Barbara Rose Anderson of Chevy Chase, Maryland and Beth Anne Coolidge of North Andover, Massachusetts, four grandchildren. Memorial services, Saturday, January 10, 1998, 1:30PM, from Grace United Methodist Church, 555 Russell Avenue, Wyckoff, New Jersey. Private burial was held at Moravian Cemetery, Staten Island. In lieu of flowers, donations to Mahwah Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Company 1, 258 Franklin Turnpike, Mahwah, New Jersey, 07430 or American Cancer Society, New Jersey Division, Bergen Unit, 20 Mercer Street, Hackensack, New Jersey, 07601. The Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Avenue, Wyckoff, New Jersey.
