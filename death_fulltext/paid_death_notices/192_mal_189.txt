
        SOLTA-Jacques, 85. Died January 13, 2003. Loving companion of the late Ruth Shapiro and son of the late Deborah Soltanitzsky. He is survived by his daughters, Nadya and Dana, grandson, Leland, sons-in-law, Dr. Barton Hoexter and Owen Wild. For over fifty years he was a member of the American Stock Exchange. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him. Services will be held Sunday, January 19th, 2003 at 11:30 AM at Riverside Memorial, Amsterdam and West 76th Street. Dad, forever rest in peace.
