
        MIDDENDORF-Henry S. Jr. Sons of the Revolution in the State of New York notes with sorrow the death of Henry S. Middendorf, Jr., a life member and past president of this society. He served with devotion on the Board of Managers and held many other vital commitee positions. Our heartfelt condolences to his family. Laurence S. Simpson, D.D.S., Pres.
        MIDDENDORF-Henry Stump Jr. The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society notes with sadness the death of its former president, Henry S. Middendorf, Jr., trustee of the Society 1970-1998 and president 1989-1998. He will be remembered for his dedication, loyalty and generosity. Walter Wilmerding, President
