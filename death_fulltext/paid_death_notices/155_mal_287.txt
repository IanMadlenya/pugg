
        ALLEN-Abraham. Born May 28, 1909, Brooklyn, NY, died peacefully at home in Manh., December 21. Abe was a wonderful artist and tireless supporter of just causes. He was a founder and first director of public information for Albert Einstein College of Medicine. Abe was an inexhaustible source of support, humor and strength to his family and large circle of friends. He was the beloved husband of the late Ruth Allen and companion for many years of the late Rose Kane, both of whom he heroically saw through long debilitating illnesses. He is survived by his son Philip Allen, his daughter-in-law Laura, grandson Daniel, and sister Miriam Lipton. He was loved immeasurably and will be painfully missed. Services today, 9:30AM, ''Riverside'', 76th and Amsterdam.
