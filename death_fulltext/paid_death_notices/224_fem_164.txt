
        FUTTER--Victor. The President and Board of Trustees of Barnard College are saddened to learn of the death of Victor Futter, esteemed attorney and law professor, father of former Barnard President and Trustee, Ellen Futter. We extend our deepest sympathies to Ellen and her family. Anna Quindlen, Chair of the Board Judith Shapiro, President
        FUTTER -- Victor. The Trustees and the entire community of the American Museum of Natural History are greatly saddened by the death of Victor Futter, father of Ellen V. Futter, our beloved and inspiring president. We express deepest sympathies to all her family on their loss. Lewis W. Bernard, Chairman
