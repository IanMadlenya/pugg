
        GURNEY-Peter. Of Darien, CT. Died on Sunday, April 1 at his home after a long illness. He is survived by his wife, Susan; his three children, Christie Rawlings of Lakeville, Connecticut; Magie Gurney of Darien; Christopher Gurney of Clearwater, FL; five grandchildren; and his brother, George of Arlington, VA. Mr. Gurney worked at Bankers Trust for more than forty years. In lieu of flowers, contributions may bemade in memory of Peter Gurney to The Richard C. Gurney Scholarship Fund at The Hotchkiss School, PO Box 800, Lakeville, CT. 06039. A memorial service will be held at the end of April.
