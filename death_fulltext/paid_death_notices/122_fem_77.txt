
        FOX-Helen Goldstein. Of Delray Beach, Florida, passed away March 7, 1997. She is survived by her loving children Sheila (Herman) Eidmann and Stuart (Annette) Goldstein, cherished grandchildren Jennifer and Steven Goldstein and adored Missy and Gabriel. Donations in her memory to the Simon Wiesenthal Holocaust Center, 100 Raoul Wallenberg Place SW, Washington, DC 20024, would be appreciated.
