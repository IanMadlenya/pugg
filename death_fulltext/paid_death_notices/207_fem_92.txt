
        LAZARUS--Elizabeth June. Died peacefully on Sunday, April 11th, 2004 at White Plains Hospital, at the age of 83. Mrs. Lazarus was born on June 25, 1920 in Elm Creek, Nebraska. She was the youngest, and final survivor, of the seven children of Cyrena Harper and Joseph Henry Stallbories. After attending Nebraska Wesleyan University on a full scholarship, June came to New York City and began a business career. She married Stanley Lazarus in New York City, who predeceased her in 1965. They had one child, a daughter, Marian Lewis of White Plains, who survives. Mrs. Lazarus was formerly a proprietor of both Larchmont Healthmart and Greenwich Healthmart. She was a lovely woman who will be sadly missed. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory to Sarah Neuman Center for Healthcare and Rehabilitation, in Mamaroneck, or to White Plains Hospital, would be appreciated. For more information contact Zion Memorial Chapel in Mamaroneck, 914-381-1809.
