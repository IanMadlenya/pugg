
        SAVIO-S. Charles. On August 22, 1998. Mr. Savio, a graduate of Fordham University School of Pharmacy, was the executive director of the New York State Pharmaceutical Association and was president of the Italian Pharmaceutical Society. He had been the proprietor of the St. George Pharmacy in the St. George Hotel for many years as well as pharmacies in the Carlyle and Beekman hotels. He is survived by his nephew Alfred B. DelBello, former Westchester County Executive and New York State Lieut. Gov.; nieces Bettina Filardi & Annmarie Carlo; 11 grand nieces & nephews. Friends may call Wednesday, 5-9 PM at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Funeral mass Thursday, 1 PM at Holy Family Church, 315 East 47 St. Interment will follow in Calvary Cemetery, Queens. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Rd., Bronx, NY 10461
