
        MELAMED-Feiga. Husband Mr. Ghers Melamed, daughters Assia and Jeannine, and grandson David announce with great sorrow the untimely death of Feiga Melamed, born Bales Kogan, that occurred during the night of November 25-26. She was 92 years old. She lies now in peace for eternity. The funeral will take place on December 3, in the cemetery of Montparnasse at 3, Blvd. Edgar Quinet, Paris 14th Arr.
