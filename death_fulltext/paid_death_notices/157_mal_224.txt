
        ALLEN-Ronald R., Jr. Of New York City, died February 18, while vacationing in Miami Beach, FL. He is survived by his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Ronald R. Allen, Sr. of Knoxville, TN; his brother Rick; his sister Jennifer; and his companion Connie Jones. The family will receive friends on Tuesday, February 22, at 6 PM, at Rose Mortuary on Broadway in Knoxville, with the funeral service following at 8 PM. He was loved deeply and will be missed greatly.
