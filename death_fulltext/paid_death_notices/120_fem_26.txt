
        KANE - Janet (nee Pompan), 75, December 27, 1996. She died after a long and courageous battle with emphysema. Beloved mother of Alison and mother-in-law of Marc Zarowin. Loving aunt of Barbara Elberger and Susan Hallowell, and cousin of Robert Gardiner. She attended Smith College, and earned a BA and a PhD in applied psychology from NYU. She taught psychology prior to obtaining her LLB from Columbia Law School in 1952. For many years she was a practicing attorney, most recently a partner at Phillips, Nizer. Services on Sunday, January 12, 2 PM, at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 2nd Ave. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the National Jewish Medical and Research Center, 1400 Jackson Street, Denver, CO 80206.
