
        CARR-Helen K. Died peacefully on January 1, 2002. Beloved wife of Leonard, loving mother of Eugene and Anita. She wil be deeply missed by all of her friends and family. Her generosity, courage, sophistication and beautiful smile will be an inspiration for us all. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 St, on Thursday 1:15 PM. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions should be made to The Alumni and Friends of LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts (Helen Carr Scholarship Fund), or High Five Tickets to the Arts, or The Aspen Music Festival and School.
