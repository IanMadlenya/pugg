
        DAVID--Katherine Anne Brodie. We announce with great sorrow the death of Katherine Anne Brodie David on November 5, 2004, at the age of 59. She is survived by her husband Keith David, her daughter Elizabeth (Bicky) Brodie David and her brother Michael Brodie. Kate was the gradute of Smith College and, before her marriage and treasured motherhood, a lexicographer editing dictionaries at Macmillan. A celebration of Kate's life will be held at the Church of the Heavenly Rest at Fifth Avenue and 90th Street, at 4 pm, on November 11. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Have A Chance, Inc., 26 Schoolhouse Lane, Morristown, NJ 07960.
