
        SOBOTKA-Renato. Of Kew Gardens and Southampton, NY. Died on March 14 at the age of 87. The former director of the NYC Housing Authority's Design Department. He was the solid foundation on which our family was built and the moral and ethical compass to which we all turned in times of need. He is survived by his beloved wife, Ann, his children Michael and Barbara, and his grandchildren, Eric, Kathy and Davy. The family will receive visitors on Friday 1-9 P.M. at Michael's house in Jamaica Estates.
