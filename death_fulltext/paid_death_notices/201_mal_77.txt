
        GOLDEN -- Julius. Passed away peacefully on October 8th, 2 days before his 80th birthday. Respected and loved by Mimi, his wife of 53 years; his sons Lester, Paul and Richard and their wives Gabriella, Alaine and Tessa; grandchildren Sarah, Jeremy, Ariel and Kate Celia; his sister Lucy Stiglitz and her children Phyllis and Mitchell. His loyalty and affection will live on in us always. Service will be on Friday, October 10th at 11:00 AM at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Donations may be made to the Simon Wiesenthal Center.
