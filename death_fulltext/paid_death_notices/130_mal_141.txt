
        JACOBY-John Z. III, M.D. On November 9, 1997, at age forty. We mourn with overwhelming sadness the untimely passing of our beloved friend and most esteemed colleague. As Medical Advisor for over a decade to the Young Adult Program of the Cystic Fibrosis Association of Greater New York Dr. Jack Jacoby worked endlessly to advance the life, hope and horizons of the growing CF young adult population even as he too battled the ravages of this relentless disease. With his caring, compassion and dedicated medical expertise he imparted extraordinary strength and courage as he ministered to the cystic fibrosis young and their families. He was truly one of the Lord's chosen messengers. The beauty of his love will live in our hearts. The Cystic Fibrosis Association extends profound sympathies to his beloved family, his parents John and Joanne Jacoby, brother Jeff, sister-in-law Mary Jean and their adored children. Phyllis L. Kossoff, President Jerome Hershman, Exec. V.P.
