
        STAVROPOULOS - Ethel. Age 89 of Mendham, NJ (formerly of Jamaica Estates, NY and owner of Hillside Florist Inc.) on Sunday July 21, 2002. Wife of the late Gus. Mother of Sylvia Christakos and her husband, Manny of Mendham and the late Rev. Germanos Stavropoulos, former beloved Chancellor of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocese in New York City. Grandmother of Dean Andreas and Athena Christakos. Funeral service will be held on Saturday, July 27, at 9:00 AM at Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, 250 Gallows Hill Road, Westfield, NJ. Interment Mt. Olivet Cemetery, Maspeth, NY. Friends may call at the Bailey Funeral Home Inc., 8 Hilltop Road, Mendham NJ, on Friday 2-4, 7-9PM.
