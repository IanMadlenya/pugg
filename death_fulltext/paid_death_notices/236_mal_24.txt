
        PARKER--William Howard, beloved father of Cody, son of David and Diane, brother of Pam, passed away on August 12, 2006 in Florida. Will was a talented musician, receiving his Masters from Julliard in 1978. He performed internationally with the Metropolitan Brass Quartet, the NY Philharmonic, numerous Broadway shows and orchestras. We mourn his passing and pray for his comfort in eternal light. The memorial service will be Monday, September 25, 2006 at 7:30 pm at St. Paul & St. Andrew Methodist Church, West End Ave at 86th St. It is requested that in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the Church.
