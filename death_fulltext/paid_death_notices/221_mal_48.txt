
        ZIPSER--Herman Benjamin. Died June 4, 2005, at the age of 95. Beloved husband of the late Eleanor, loving father and father-in-law of Marcia and Robert Hefter and Myra and Jack Korman, adored grandfather of Michele and Victor Seitles and Jason Hefter, doting great-grandfather of Madeline and Mia Seitles, devoted brother of Philip Zipser and Sylvia Schur. Herman Zipser was a graduate of St. John's Law School and for many years a prominent and respected attorney in New York City. He will be sorely missed. Funeral and services will be held at Schwartz Bros.Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 11403 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, on Monday, June 6, 2005, at noon. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Temple Israel of Riverhead, NY, or the American Cancer Society.
