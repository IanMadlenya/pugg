
        HALL-Carol Benedict, of Mantoloking, NJ and Vero Beach, FL died at 75 in Boulder, CO on November 1. Rodney Dennis Hall, Jr, her husband of 39 years, died in 1982. She is survived by two daughters Louise Allen Grauer and Judith Hall Dominguez; four grandchildren, and a sister Anne B. Swain. Services will be held at 11 AM on November 8 in St. Simon-By-the-Sea, Mantoloking, NJ. Contributions in her memory can be made to Hospice of Boulder County, 2825 Marine Street, Boulder, CO 80303; Kent Place School, 42 Norwood Ave, Summit, NJ 07902, and Vassar College, Box 159, Poughkeepsie, NY 12604.
