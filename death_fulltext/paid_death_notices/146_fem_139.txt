
        HOLMES-Sallie, Ackley, 69 of Greenwich Village, died Sunday, March 7 at NYU Hospital. She was born in Philadelphia, PA, April 17, 1929 and resided in Jenkinstown, PA until 1950 when she moved to NY. She attended Smith College, and the Latin American Institute. She retired from Shearson Lehman and devoted much of her time during retirement to enjoying blues and jazz music. She leaves her best friend John Rodriguez, two sisters Elizabeth H. Osth of Palm Harbor, Fl and Jaquelin H. Anderson of East Granby, CT, a niece and three nephews. She will be buried at Trinity Church Cemetery and Mausoleum. A memorial service will be held at the convenience of her family.
