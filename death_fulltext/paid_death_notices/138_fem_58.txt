
        HARTZ-Elizabeth K. Age 90, in Bernardsville, NJ, on Monday, July 6, 1998. Wife of the late Raymond E. Hartz. Mother of Elizabeth H. Forster of New York City and John C. Hartz of Basking Ridge, NJ. Also predeceased by son, Raymond E. Hartz, Jr. Grandmother of six. A memorial service will be held on Monday, July 13 at 11 AM at St. Bernard's Episcopal Church, Bernardsville, NJ. In her memory donations may be made to the Visiting Nurse Association of Somerset Hills, PO Box 478, 219 Main Street, Gladstone, NJ 07934
