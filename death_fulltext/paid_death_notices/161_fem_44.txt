
        MACLEAN-Barbara Barondess. Died May 31 at Beth Israel Hospital. Born, Brooklyn 07/04/08. Mrs. MacLean was a Broadway and Hollywood actor. The Barbara Barondess Foundation will continue it's work in her name. Mrs. MacLean leaves no survivors. Memorial will take place Monday, June 12th at 6:30 PM, 225 E. 57th St.
