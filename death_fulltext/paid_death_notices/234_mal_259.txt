
        ROBINSON--Rabbi Michael A., Temple Israel of Northern Westchester mourns the loss of its deeply respected Rabbi Emeritus Michael A. Robinson, activist in defense of freedom, advocate in the cause of peace. His voice may be stilled, but his message will always sound in our hearts and minds. Funeral services will be held July 23 in California. Contributions in Rabbi Robinson's memory may be made to The Michael A. Robinson Memorial Fund, Congregation Shomrei Torah, 1717 Yuluya Avenue, Santa Rosa, CA 95405 or to the Joel Robinson Social Action Fund, Temple Israel Of Northern Westchester, Glengary Road, Croton-on-Hudson, NY 10520.
