
        ELLEN--Leslie. Most wonderful wife, mother and grandmother, died with dignity and grace at home with her loved ones around her on November 20, 2004, after a long and courageous battle. She was a beautiful, brilliantly gifted and loving human being. Now she's gone to the big dance class in the sky. She will be deeply missed by all of us. Survived by her adoring family: husband of 42 years Max, daughter Caroline, son Daniel, son - in - law Rick, daughter - in - law Antonella, grandchildren Max and Lucinda, brother Peter and sister Gail. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Wednesday, November 24, at 2 pm.
