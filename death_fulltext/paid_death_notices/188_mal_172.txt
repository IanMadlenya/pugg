
        FLYER-Gregory, 78. Died Saturday in Scottsdale, AZ. Born July 6, 1924 in Brooklyn. World War II vet. He owned San Filippo Liquor Store in Bensonhurst. Survived by children Eric, Glenn, Marvin, Ilana; sister Xenia Fane; three grandchildren. Services today at Green Acres Mortuary Scottsdale, AZ.
