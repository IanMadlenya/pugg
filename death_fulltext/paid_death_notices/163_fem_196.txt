
        SCHWEBEL-Devora Ruth Denenholz. We mark on the 22nd of August the first year since the sudden passing of Devora Ruth Denenholz Schwebel, beloved wife of Solomon, youngest of the Denenholz Ten who knew her as Dovey : Her sisters and brothers Alma, Amelia, Grace, the late Marshall, Daniel, Richard, Jessica, Reginald, & Victor. She is survived by Solomon, Robert, Paul, and her grandchildren Kate, Laura, Lillian, and Ella. She is irreplaceable, her memory cherished. She lives on in those she loved. AVANTI
