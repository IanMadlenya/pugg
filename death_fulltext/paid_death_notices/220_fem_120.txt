
        STIGLITZ -- Charlotte nee Fishman, 90, passed away May 6, 2005. Cherished wife of the late Nathaniel; devoted mother of Joseph (Anya Schiffrin) and Eloise (Alan Breus) Stiglitz. Teacher at Purdue University Calument Campus until age 80. She will be missed for her warm smile and giving heart. In lieu of flowers, offerings appreciated to the Nat & Charlotte Stiglitz Purdue Calument Scholarship Fund, 155 Washington St., Apt. 2406, Jersey City, NJ 07302. The family will receive friends at the Stiglitz residence, 305 Riverside Drive, Apt. 8B, NYC, Sunday, 2-8PM. (212-316-5990).
