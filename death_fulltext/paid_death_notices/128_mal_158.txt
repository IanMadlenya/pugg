
        KAHN-Stanley M. Died September 7, 1997. He is survived by his wife Ethel Gruber Kahn, his daughter Holli Kahn Gersh and husband Edward Gersh, son, Robert Kahn and his wife Susan, daughter Cynthia Kahn Abrams; grandchildren, Heather Greenberg, Harrison and Ashley Kahn, and Maya Abrams. Mr. Kahn built Holly Hills Homes on Long Island. He raised thoroughbred horses in Chatham, New York. He later retired to Boynton Beach, Florida.
