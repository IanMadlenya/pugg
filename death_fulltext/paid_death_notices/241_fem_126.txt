
        HOLSTEIN--Lisa, born August 21, 1955, passed away on February 9th, 2007 after a courageous battle with cancer. Daughter of Cecelia Elaine Kussner of Rye, NY and Philip Mazur Holstein Jr. of Aspen, CO. Lisa graduated with honors from Rye; received her B.S. with distinction from the University of Michigan and law degree, cum laude, from Syracuse University. She was counsel at Proskauer Rose & Schulte Roth Zabel; Partner at Brown Raysman Millstein Felder & Steiner LLP. Lisa leaves behind Raymond Elders, their eight year old daughter, Samantha Elders and stepdaughter Kathryn Elders; mother and father; sister Karen Holstein and nephews Ari Holstein and Ryan Malpass of Dobbs Ferry. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Gilda's Club of Westchester, 80 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY 10605 or Hospice Palliative Care of Westchester.
