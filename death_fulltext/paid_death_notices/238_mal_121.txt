
        ANDREWS--Benny. The Board of Trustees and the Staff of The Studio Museum in Harlem are deeply saddened by the passing of artist Benny Andrews. An internationally recognized painter, printmaker, illustrator, passionate cultural leader and arts advocate, Benny was a revered member of The SMH family who will be greatly missed by us all. We send our heartfelt condolences to his family. Raymond J. McGuire, Chairman Thelma Golden, Director
