
        MILNER-Jean. Died at her home at age 91 shortly following the passing of her beloved husband, Jimmy. Beloved mother of Mary Wittner and Robert Milner and mother-law of Saralee Milner and Andrew Wittner. Cherished grandmother of Diane Wittner and Jon Acheson, Pamela and Doug D'Alexander, and Steve and Jane Wittner. Adored great grandmother of Gabriel, Baird, Dominic, Steven and Beth. She died as she lived, with humor, strength and fully possessed of her extraordinary intellect.
