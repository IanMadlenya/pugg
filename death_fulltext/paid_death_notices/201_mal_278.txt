
        CALABRESI--Paul, 73, of Barrington, RI, died peacefully on October 25, 2003 after a courageous battle with cancer, the disease of which he cured so many. He was Professor of Medicine and Chairman Emeritus of the Brown University Department of Medicine and the First Chairman of the National Cancer Advisory Board. He is survived by his wife of 49 years, Celia, three children, Steven, Janice and Peter, eight grandchildren and a brother, Guido. A funeral service will be held on Saturday, November 1 at 10:30am in the First Unitarian Church of Providence, 1 Benevolent St, Providence. Memorial contributions may be made to the Calabresi Professorship at Rhode Island Hospital Foundation to support an endowed chair at Brown University, c/o Lifespan Development Office, PO Box H, Providence, RI 02901 or to the Yale Cancer Center, Paul Calabresi Lectureship, Office of Medical Development, PO Box 7611, New Haven, CT 06519-1714.
