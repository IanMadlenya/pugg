
        KUPFER -- Adele, beloved wife of Dr. Sherman Kupfer, passed away on July 2, 2003. Besides her husband of 52 years she is survived by her three children, Marcia, Joel and Kenneth Kupfer, and six grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (between 90th & 91st Street) on July 4th at 10 AM.
        KUPFER--Adele. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband Dr. Sherman Kupfer and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln Senior Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft President >>AD#
