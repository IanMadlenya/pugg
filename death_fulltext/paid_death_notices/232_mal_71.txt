
        ITKIN -Sam, 92 The last of the Itkin brothers, died on May 6, 2006, one day after his 92nd birthday, in Pompano Beach, Florida. Beloved husband of 60 years to the late Edythe, he leaves sons Larry and David, daughters-in-law Cheryl and Valerie, grandchildren Nicole and Stephanie, Ben Sawyer, husband of Nicole, and great grandson, Everett. Inter ment at Mount Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY on Tuesday, May 9t at 11AM.
