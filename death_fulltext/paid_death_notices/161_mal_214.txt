
        BISGYER-Melvin, died June 14th, 2000 in Portland, OR. Retired mortgage broker and Adjunct Professor of Real Estate at New York University. Board member of Community Access. Beloved brother to Janice Grossman and Justin Bisgyer. Beloved grandfather to Madeline Bisgyer, Philip Avallone, Elizabeth Avallone. Adored father of Marcia Bisgyer, Ann Bisgyer and Susan Bisgyer, father-in-law of Albert Avallone and John Wolz, and great friend to Barbara Cohn, and Ken and Betty Wolz. He will be remembered for his charm and good nature. His family and the New York real estate community will deeply miss him. The family suggests contributions to the Mel Bisgyer Memorial Fund, Community Access, 666 Broadway, 3rd Floor, NY NY 10012.
