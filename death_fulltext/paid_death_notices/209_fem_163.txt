
        MILLER--Sondra. Died at home June 20. She is survived by her husband William Arp, son Christopher, stepdaughter Rachel Ramstad. She is also survived by her beloved sister Helene Miller, niece Reyna Rosenshein, and brother-in-law Thomas Arp. A native of Brooklyn, she attended Erasmus Hall High School, Brooklyn College and received her Master's degree at Pratt Institute. She was an interior designer with the firm of William Cohen Architect. She was also a teacher, author, knitwear designer and clothing designer for Elie Tahari, Oleg Cassini, Chabot Freres and others. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Research, 1111 Stewart Ave, Bethpage, New York 11714.
