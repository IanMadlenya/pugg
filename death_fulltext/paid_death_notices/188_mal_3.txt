
        BRECHER-H. Reggie. 97 years young, passed away on August 28, 2002. Devoted son, lifelong member of the Masons, past Master of the Nathan Strauss Lodge, President of the Bon Vivants Social Club of New Rochelle, NY. Survived by his beloved sister Jeanne Gadd of Plantation, FL, newphews Samuel Gadd of Chino Hills, CA, and Melvin Gadd of Cambridge, MA, and their families, numerous grandnieces, a nephew and a great-grandniece. Lived life fully and was a source of joy to all his family and friends as he danced his way through life. Private burial at Cedar Park Cemetery. Memorial service to be held in October, 2002.
