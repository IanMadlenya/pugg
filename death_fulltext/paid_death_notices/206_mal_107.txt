
        O'HANLON--Thomas J. Died peacefully at his home in Greenwich, CT on March 8 after a brave battle with cancer. His beloved daughter Isolde O'Hanlon of Manhattan, his long time companion Jean Conwell and her children Jennifer, Kelly and Brian and his former wife Grainne O'Hanlon, survive him. A Memorial Mass will be held on Saturday, March 27, at 1:30 PM at St. Michael the Archangel Roman Catholic Church, 469 North St., Greenwich, CT. Donations in his memory may be made to the Friends of the Greenwich Library, 101 W. Putnam Ave., Greenwich, CT. 06830.
