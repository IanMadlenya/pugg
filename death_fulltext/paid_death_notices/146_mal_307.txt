
        POLLACK-Alexander. On March 13, 1999, at the age of 75. Beloved husband to Violet. Devoted father to Nancy, Michael and Anne. Caring father-in-law to Bob and Barbara. Adored grandfather of Leslie, Eric, Lucy and Ava. Loving son of Frank and the late Lillian. Loyal brother, brother-in-law, uncle, granduncle, great-granduncle and friend to all who knew him. A kind, wonderful man, he brought joy and love to his family and friends with his friendly face and bright smile. The songs he sang will echo in our hearts. His passion and charm will be greatly missed. Services Monday, 10 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck.
