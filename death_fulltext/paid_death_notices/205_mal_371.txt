
        WOLF--Semon II. 74, died Monday, February 23, 2004, at home. Born in New York City he graduated from Horace Mann High School in 1947. He graduated from the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania, where he received a bachelor's degree in science and economics in 1951. He was a Second Lieutenant in the Air Force. He was a stockbroker for Prudential Securities before retiring in 1985. He lived in Ireland for 18 years where he was Master of Foxhounds with the Black and Tan Scarteen Hunt, before returning to the United States in March 2001. Surviving are his wife Beverly, daughter and son-inlaw Lori and Kevin Lane, son Leonard Wolf, three grandsons, Avalon Wolf, Max and Jake Lane and his sister Sue Schloss.
