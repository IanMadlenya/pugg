
        GORDON--Alice, 85, of Forest Hills, NY, on March 7, 2005. Loving wife of the late Arthur Gordon, mother of Eric M. Gordon, of Palo Alto, and Nancy R. Gordon, of Anchorage. Proud grandmother of Jacob A. Gordon, of Los Angeles, and Kerin A. Gordon, of Boston, and eldest sister of Edith Jaffe, Doris Kahme, and newspaper columnist and author Art Buchwald. Alice was a wellspring of kindness, love, and empathy. An energetic, committed community volunteer for many years, and a wonderful, nurturing mother, grandmother, sister, and friend. Her generous, caring, indominable spirit and warmth will be sorely missed by all who knew her. Service Thursday at 10 A.M. at Schwartz BrosJeffer Memorial Chapel 11403, Queens Boulevard, 76th Road, Forest Hills, NY.
