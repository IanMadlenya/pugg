
        WARMBOLD--Grace, the former Grace Staub of Miami, Florida left this world to be with the Lord on July 11, 2003 after a long battle with cancer. She was sixty-five years old. Grace Warmbold was born and raised in New York City and educated in Florida. She is a graduate of the University of Miami and former President of Delta Gamma sorority. After graduating from college, Grace studied painting and textile design at New York City's world renowned ''Art Students League'' Institute. She began her professional career as a freelance artist, initially designing textiles for the home furnishings industry. In addition, she pursued interest in painting one-of-a-kind art on fabrics as well as oils, water colors and acrylic which were displayed in galleries in New York and Southampton, Long Island. In 1990, Grace and her family relocated from New York City to Florida. Grace's artwork then reflected the Florida life-style with emphasis on golf course scenes and signature holes. She leaves with loving memories her husband, Herman Warmbold of Miami, daughter Kimberly of New York City, a brother, nieces and nephews. A memorial gathering will be held on July 17, 2003 in New York City at the Essex House. Interment and a memorial service will follow at Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, NY on July 24, 2003 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society. >>AD#
