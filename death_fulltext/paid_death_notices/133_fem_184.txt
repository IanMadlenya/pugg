
        BROGAN-Peggy Dunn, writer, teacher, educational consultant on February 15 in Ishpiming, MI after a long illness. Born Margaret Dunn in Chisholm, MN. on July 9, 1918. Graduate of Northwestern University. Teacher at Home School in New York City. Predeceased by husband Whit Brogan and brother John Dunn but leaves behind thousands of children who were touched by her work.
