
        BARTOLOMEO-Emily. Of Eastchester, New York: formerly of The Bronx. Born on July 8, 1924; died on January 10, 2002. Beloved wife of the late John. Devoted mother of John, Evelyn and Charles and mother-in-law of Rosemary. Cherished grandmother of Amelia and Grace. Devoted sister of Louise Dentato. Dear friend to many. To have known Emily was to have loved her. The family will receive friends on Saturday 7-9 & Sunday 2-5 & 7-9 PM at F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc., 726 Morris Park Avenue, Bronx, NY. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Clare's Church, Rhinelander Avenue, Bronx, NY on Monday 9:45 AM followed by a graveside service at Mount Hope Cemetery in Westchester.
