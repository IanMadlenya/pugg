
        DISKIN-Thomas A., 87, of Westhampton Beach, NY, and Haverford, PA, on March 23, 2001. Beloved husband of Mary. Devoted father of Thomas A. Diskin Jr. of Dover, NH, Mary Freeman of San Diego, CA, Anne Smalley of McLean, VA, Peter Diskin of Havertown, PA. Brother of Frances Dullea, New Milford, NJ. Also survived by eight grandchildren and one great-grandchild. As a Senior Partner of DeWitt, Nast & Diskin, he practiced publishing law in New York City for many years. Visitation Monday, 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM at Follett & Werner Funeral Home, Westhampton Beach, NY. Mass will be celebrated on Tuesday morning at Immaculate Conception Church, Westhampton Beach.
