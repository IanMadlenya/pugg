
        KARELITZ-Ethel R. The North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System mourns the passing of Ethel R. Karelitz, a founding board member of Long Island Jewish (LIJ) Medical Center and a Life Trustee of the North Shore-LIJ Health System. Mrs. Karelitz was also instrumental in the creation of the LIJ Service Guild and devoted much of her time and energy to that hospital. She was the widow of LIJ's first chief of pediatrics, Samuel Karelitz, MD. Our heartfelt condolences to her family and friends. Roy J. Zuckerberg, Chairman Michael J. Dowling President & CEO North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System
