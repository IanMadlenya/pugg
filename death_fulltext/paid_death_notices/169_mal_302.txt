
        FORNARI-Dr. Harry D. The American Fats and Oils Association mourns the death of Dr. Harry D. Fornari, its founding member, two-time President, and longserving Executive Director. For almost forty years, his vision served our Association as a bright beacon, lighting its growth. He will be missed for his commitment to principle, the soundness of his judgment, and, most importantly, the warmth of his friendship. American Fats and Oils Assn., Inc David Italiaander, Pres. Walter J. Karpinski, Vice. Pres. Nicholas B. Sander, Vice Pres. Ralph Joel, Treasurer Eric H. Jackson, Secretary David E. Rothschild, Exec. Dir. Bardin Levavy, General Counsel
