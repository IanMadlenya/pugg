
        LOFTUS--Robert McNichol, age 73, of Glen Rock, NJ, died on Monday, June 14th, 2004. He was the beloved husband of Margery, cherished father of Kimberley (Joseph) Carroll, Karen Loftus and John (Linda) Loftus and adored grandfather of Patrick and Maggie Carroll and Brendan and Riley Loftus. Friends may call at Feeney's Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ, on Friday, 4 to 8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial on Saturday at 11AM at St. Catharine's Church, Glen Rock, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be sent to Foundation of UMDNJ-Parkinson Fund, 1 World's Fair Dr, Suite 2100, Somerset, NJ 08873 or Christian Health Care Center, 301 Sicomac Avenue, Wyckoff, NJ 07481.
