
        KANE-Bernard, 72. Of Delray Beach, Fla., and Ardsley, NY. Survived by his beloved wife, son, daughter and four grandchildren. A leader in the field of elevator modernization and computerization. Co-founder of New York Elevator and Computerized Elevator Control. An accomplished aviator and enthusiastic volunteer pilot for Angel Flight Northeast. Services at Westchester Riverside Memorial, Mount Vernon, Noon, Friday.
        KANE-Bernard. Delaire Country Club announces with deep sorrow the passing of their member, Bernard Kane and extends their deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Barbara, and his devoted family. Robert Goldberg, Pres.
