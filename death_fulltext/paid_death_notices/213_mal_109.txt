
        JASON--Bernard A. LTC. USAR. Ret. Died October 8. Attended the American University in Paris. Graduated from George Washington University. Practiced accounting as a CPA for 40 years. Beloved husband to Barbara and Edith, deceased. Adored father and grandfather to Jack, Debra, Steven, Sheldon, Fred, Jonathan, Daniel, Jamie and Alex. He will be remembered in our hearts always. Funeral services this morning at 10 am, Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, NY.
        JASON--Bernard. The Board of Governors and members of Willow Ridge Country Club sadly note the passing of Bernard Jason, a distinguished member of this club. Our deepest condolences are offerred to his wife Barbara and their entire family. Brad Mattes, President Board of Governors
