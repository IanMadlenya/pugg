
        FINE-Herman J. Of Bronx, New York, beloved husband of Esther (Goldstein) Fine, died on January 14, 2002. He was born in New York, June 14, 1913. He was a longtime member of Beth Shraga Synagogue. He attended Yeshiva D'Harlem and Yeshiva Yitzhak Elchanan High School. He then went to City College where he received a B.A. and an M.S. in Education. He subsequently earned an M.A. degree in Semitic Studies from Columbia University. He also completed a course of study at the Jewish Theological Seminary. Besides his beloved wife, he is survived by his loving daughters and sons-in-law, Serena and Michael English of New York, Rhoda and Todd Gladstone of Connecticut and sister Diane Kassner of the Bronx. He is also survived by his loving grandchildren, Rebecca & Daniel English, Debbie & Beth Gladstone and many nieces and nephews. Funeral services were held at the Louis Hirsch & Sons Funeral Home, 1225 Jerome Avenue in the Bronx with burial at Beth El Cemetery in Paramus, NJ. A memorial period will be observed at his home at 185 E. 206 Street in the Bronx until Monday January 21, 2002. Donations in his memory to Memorial Sloan-Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
