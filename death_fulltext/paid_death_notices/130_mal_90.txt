
        LEFCOURT-Michael. Died on November 6th, 1997. Adoring husband of Carol Blumenson Lefcourt. Devoted father of Jill Lander and Peter and Jack Lefcourt. Cherished grandfather of Jennifer, Allison, Scott and Nancy. Art Director at Dell Publishing until his retirement in 1988 to Boca Raton, FL. A very special man of humor and presence who will be greatly missed. Services private in Florida. Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel, 1-800-992-9262.
