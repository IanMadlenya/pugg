
        WOOLF-Leopold. Of Boynton Beach, FL and Somers, NY died July 24 at age 77, after a heroic effort to recover from complications following a kidney transplant. Born in Vienna, Austria, emigrated to the US in 1939. He attended Columbia University and University of Chicago. He served in the U.S. Army's ski troops, and was wounded in Italy. A jeweler's apprentice in Vienna, he became a Master Jeweler and Appraiser, until his retirement in 1994. Known as ''Bob,'' he leaves behind his adoring wife of 52 years, Dorrit, their two sons, Richard of Scarsdale, NY and Matthew of Manhattan, daughter-in-law Jayne and son-inlaw Steven, as well as grandchildren Joshua, Jennifer and Andrew.
