
        PETERS-Betty Martone. Died peacefully on May 18, 1998, at age 84. Devoted & patient wife of the late Leone J. Peters; beloved mother of Paul Leone Peters and Gail Peters Beitz; adored grandmother of Adam & Jaime Peters, and Bradley and Drew Beitz; cherished mother-in-law of Kenneth Beitz; dear sister of Loretta Goldman; and a great, elegant lady who was loved and respected by all her family and friends. A generous and independent woman with great spirit, she gave her family the gift of unconditional love, and we shall miss her so very much. Memorial service will be held at 11 A.M., on Thursday, May 21, 1998, at Christ Episcopal Church in Manhasset, New York. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to this church would be greatly appreciated by her family.
