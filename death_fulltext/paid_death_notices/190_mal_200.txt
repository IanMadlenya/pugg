
        FRIEDMAN-Charles. Passed away on October 10th, 2002, at age 84. A fifty-one year employee of The New York Times. He started as a messenger boy and advanced to a copy editor position in various departments, ending his career in the Sports Department where he enjoyed writing most about tennis. His other passion was opera; he sometimes serenaded his coworkers. He is survived by his wife of fifty-two years, Paulina, his daughter Belita, and his son Neil.
        FRIEDMAN - Charles. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Charles Friedman, associated with The Times from 1937 until retiring in 1986.
