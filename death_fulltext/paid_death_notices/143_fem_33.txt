
        ABEL-Theodora Mead, widow of Theodore F. Abel, died December 2, of a heart attack at Hartwood, NY. She is survived by three children, Peter, Caroline Lalire, and Zita Emerson; her beloved cousin, Robert G. Cleveland; ten grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. She was recipient of the American Psychological Association's 1997 Gold Medal Award for Life Achievement in the Practice of Psychology. She will be sorely missed by the many who loved her. Services will be at Elsinore Hartwood Club, Port Jervis, New York, on December 5, at 3 PM. Contributions, in lieu of flowers, may be made to The Humane Society or the World Wildlife Fund.
