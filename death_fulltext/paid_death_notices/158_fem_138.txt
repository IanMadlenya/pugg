
        VENTURELLI-Helen. The Police and Fire Line of Duty Widows of NYC, Inc. records with sorrow the passing of our co-founder, Helen Venturelli. She served as our President with the utmost dedication and her wisdom, leadership and wit will be sorely missed by the members of the Board and the many members throughout the country. To her bereaved family, we extend heartfelt condolences. Louise Crane, Vice President Janet Moran, Treasurer Shirley Johnson, Secretary
