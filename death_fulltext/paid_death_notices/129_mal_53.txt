
        SCHWARCZ-Ernest. The faculty and students of the Jewish Studies Program at Queens College, CUNY, mourn the tragic death of Dean and Professor Emeritus Ernest Schwarcz. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his wife, Marta. A memorial service will be held on Monday, October 6, 1997 at 12:30 pm in the LeFrak Concert Hall at the College. Thomas E.
