
        ALBERT-Elihu Dale, 87. Of White Plains. Devoted father and grandfather; 1934 graduate of Wesleyan University, admitted to College of Physicians and Surgeons, but of necessity took a position as research chemist, completed a MA at Columbia, wrote textbooks. During WWII, he had a maritime commission, developing coatings for lighter, faster ships. In 1951, he married the love of his life, Rose Perlstein, who predeceased him in 1981. For many years he was Executive V.P. at Merkin Paint Company. Until he was 84, he was in charge of new accounts at CSS. A man of high intelligence and rare integrity, he had a rich sense of humor. Compassionate and generous, he took great care of his family and made friends where ever he went.
