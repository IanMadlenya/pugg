
        CHANOVER-Rabbi Hyman. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in 1945, he distinguished himself in the field of Jewish education and authored many books for children. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Channa, children Leonard and David and all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Seymour L. Essrog, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec VP The Rabbinical Assembly
