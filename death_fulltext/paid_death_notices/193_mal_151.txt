
        LEVINE-Jack, M.D., 88. Of NYC, died peacefully at home on February 11, 2003. Cherished husband of the late Esther (Quitt), adored father and father-in-law of Michael and Janet, Carol and David, Susan and Malcolm, Jed and Jerry, loving Poppy to 13 grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Beloved by his brother and sister-in-law Danny and Annette and many nieces, nephews and cousins. Lovingly cared for by Rosa Bradley. Decorated WWII veteran, retired chairman of orthopedic surgery, Brookdale Hospital Medical Center, researcher, inventor, teacher, avid tennis player, and world traveler. MEDICO/ CARE physician in Afghanistan, Indonesia and other remote locations. He loved good food and good times with his family and many friends. He lived life to the fullest and will be missed. Memorial service Sunday, February 16, 2003, 1:30 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St and Amsterdam. Memorial donations may be made to Alzheimer's Association, NYC 360 Lexington Ave, NYC 10017 or Pediatric Orthopedic Fund of NY Orthopedic Hospital, 3959 Bway 8 North, NYC 10032.
        LEVINE-Jack, MD. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center record with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Jack Levine, Chairman Emeritus of the Department of Orthopedics. As a mentor of many of our physicians, Dr. Levine was affiliated with Brookdale for over five decades. Our sincere condolences to his children, grandchildren, greatgrandchildren and the entire family. Alvin I. Kahn, MD, FACP Chairman, Board of Trustees David P. Rosen, Pres. & CEO Richard J. Fogler, MD Chief Medical Officer Joel Teicher, MD Chairman, Orthopedics
