
        HOMANS--Marian Bennett. Born June 17, 1908, died May 27, 2003, at her home in Englewood, NJ, after a long illness. She died peacefully and with dignity. Beloved wife of the late Eugene V. Homans. Loving mother of Helen Homans Thompson of New York City. She is also survived by her sister Helen Bennett Jackson; a granddaughter, Bettina Thompson Stern; three greatgrandsons; a nephew, John Gillespie Jackson III; two grandnieces, Helen Davidson Tapper and Marian Davidson Rouse; and a grandnephew, Matthew Halstead Davidson. She was also the devoted aunt of the late Marian Jackson Davidson. Contributions would be appreciated for the Alzheimer's Association, 225 North Michigan Ave., Suite 1700, Chicago, IL 60601-7633, the American Red Cross National Headquarters, 431 18th St., NW, Washington, DC 20006, Cancer Research Fund of the Damon Runyon-Walter Winchell Foundation, 675 3rd Ave., New York, NY 10017. >>AD#
