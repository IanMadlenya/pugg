
        CASTELLI-Gladys nee Fox, 97. Of Dallastown, PA, formerly of NYC, died on October 26th, 2001 at her residence in Pennsylvania. Wife of the late Albert Castelli. She worked as a switchboard operator for the Ritz-Carlton Hotel in NY until she retired at age 85. Funeral service will be held today, at 11 AM, in Dallastown, PA.
