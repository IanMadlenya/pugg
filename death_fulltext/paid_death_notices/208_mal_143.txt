
        NORTON--Paul Allen. The Boy Scouts of America, Greater New York Councils acknowledges the passing of Paul Allen Norton, and extends its sincere sympathy to his son Richard, his daughter Carolyn and his many friends. Mr. Norton's leadership in the insurance industry was well known. His support for his church and his community as well as his efforts on behalf of boy scouting made a postive difference. The Greater New York Councils pay tribute to Mr. Norton as a wonderful supporter and a true friend. He will be missed. John F. McGillicuddy, President Charles E. Rosser, CEO
