
        SWAN-Louis. Of Freeland, WA, and formerly of Old Bethpage, Long Island on October 15, 2000 at the age of 46. Devoted and adored wife of Todd, cherished daughter of Mildred and the late Abraham Goldstein of New York City. Beloved sister of Fredricka Stillman, Karen Ferber and David Goldstein. Gifted Landscape Architect and lover of nature who brought passion to all of her interests and loves. A brave and courageous soul who had a strong sense of right, a loving compassion for those in need, and who left a positive mark on all she met.
