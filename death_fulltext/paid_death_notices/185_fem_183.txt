
        JOHNSON - Elizabeth Taft. Died on June 19 in Delray Beach. She was the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Walbridge S. Taft of New York. She leaves a daughter, Libby Lourie of Seattle; a sister, Lucie Bard of Locust Valley; and a step-son, David Johnson of New York. She was also a sister of the late Henry W. Taft of Camden, ME. She graduated from Miss Porter's School and Columbia University. Formerly married to the late Leon Lourie, Edward Chaplin, Peter Campbell and Gerald Johnson. Funeral Saturday, June 29, 11AM at the River Bend Cemetery in Westerly, R.I. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Westerly Public Library or Delray Beach Public Library would be appreciated.
