
        DRATT--Charles, on February 2, 2007. Born April 2, 1918, he was a long time resident of Riverdale and beloved husband of Beatrice Dratt for 64 years. He is also survived by three children, Arnold, Sherry and Jude, his grandchildren, Tara, Andrew and Jessica, and his great-graandchildren, Jolie, Jacob and Noah. He was a WWII US Army veteran and a Bronze Star recipient and a well known executive in the men's apparel industry in New York. Services Tuesday 11:30 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to The Hebrew Home for the Aged Development Office, 5901 Palisade Avenue, Bronx, NY 10463.
