
        MICHELSON-Horace. The Board of Directors and 12,000 employees of The Chubb Corporation mourn the passing of Horace Michelson, the beloved husband of G.G. Michelson, a retired member of Chubb's Board of Directors. In the course of Mrs. Michelson's many years of valued service and counsel to Chubb, we came to know and admire this remarkable man and we will miss him. We extend our condolences to the entire Michelson family. Dean R. O'Hare Chairman and CEO The Chubb Corporation
