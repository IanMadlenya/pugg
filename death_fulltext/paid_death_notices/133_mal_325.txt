
        GINSBERG-Louis. Cherished son, deeply caring brother, and dear uncle. Devoted husband of the late Lillian Rosenblatt Ginsberg and the late Renee Haskin Ginsberg. Adored and adoring father of the late Bernice Ginsberg Segal. Beloved and loving grandfather of Elizabeth and Daniel, and granddaughter-in-law Laurie. Delighted great-grandfather of Hannah and Nathan. He was an inspiring mathematics teacher in Brooklyn public high schools for 40 years, and a generous and profoundly considerate human being. Contributions may be made to the Bernice G. Segal Summer Research Internships Fund at Barnard College or to the Carroll Center for the Blind in Newton, MA.
