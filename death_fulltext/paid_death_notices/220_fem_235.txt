
        ARMSTRONG -- Sheila H. Died at home on Saturday, May 28, at age 93 after a long illness. Devoted wife of the late Edward, and beloved mother of Edward, John, and Sheila A. Hoerle. Adored grandmother of Jeffrey, Scott, Alex, Ian, and Anne. Great grandmother of William, Elizabeth, Nicholas and Katharine. Sheila was a past President of the Women's National Republican Club, and of the board of the Knickerbocker Greys. Her indomitable spirit will long be remembered by her family and friends. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the John G. Armstrong '03 and Edward M. Armstrong '04 Scholarship, Princeton University, PO Box 140, Princeton, NJ 08544.
