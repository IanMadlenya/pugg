
        WHYTE-William H. (Holly), Jr. Friends of William H. Whyte are invited to join his family in a service of thanks for his life and presence among us. Service will be at 10:30 AM, Tuesday, January 19, at the Church of the Epiphany, 1393 York Ave. at 74th St. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be sent to the Municipal Art Society in Holly's name.
