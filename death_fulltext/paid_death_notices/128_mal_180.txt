
        STUBER-Florian, died September 11, 1997, of complications due to AIDS. Columbia B.A. and Ph.D. Professor at F.I.T., scholar, playwright, devoted teacher. Mourned by many friends in NYC. Survived by parents, Mr. and Mrs. Florian Stuber, of Buffalo, two brothers and a sister, nieces and nephews. Predeceased by his partner of many years, Irving Clark Piper. For information on the memorial service, call 212-662-4929. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to God's Love We Deliver.
