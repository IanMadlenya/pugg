
        JOHNSON - Pauline. Beloved mother, grandmother and friend, Pauline Johnson was born Jan. 3, 1917, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, and died on August 10, 1998, in New York City. She is survived by her two loving daughters and sons-in-law, Leslie Radziemski and Michael Radziemski of New York City and Jennifer Barraza and Fred Barraza of Silver City, New Mexico. She has three grandchildren: Scott and Luis Barraza and Lily Bea Radziemski.
