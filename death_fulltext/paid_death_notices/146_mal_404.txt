
        KATZENBACH-L.. Emery, 83. Of Waccabuc, N.Y. and Westport, MA.. An investment banker and former Chairman of White Weld & Company of N.Y. On March 18, 1999 at home. Beloved husband of Marley (nee Marseilles). Loving father to Mrs. Nicholas Niles, Jr. of Southport, CT., Mrs. William A. Barker of Dublin, N.H., and Christian D. Katzenbach of Irasburg, VT. Six grandchildren also survive. He was predeceased by a son, L. Emery Katzenbach, Jr. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, March 24 at 11 A.M. in the chapel of the United Congregational Church of New Canaan, 23 Park Street, New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Atlantic Salmon Federation (U.S.) PO Box 429, St. Andrew's New Brunswick, Canada EOG-2XO or St. Johnsbury Athenaeum, 30 Main St., St. Johnsbury, VT 05819.
