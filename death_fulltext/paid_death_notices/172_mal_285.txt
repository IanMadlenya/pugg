
        BOND-Simon (Sasha). On May 25, 2001, at the age of 104. Husband of the late Tekla Bond, father of Leo Bond, uncle of Julien Peskin and close friend of Eugenie Fromer and Irving Schneider. He was a Talmudic scholar, newspaperman, banker and real estate investor. Mr. Bond was a substantial supporter of Yeshiva University, The Weizmann Institute, Technion and UJA-Federation. Funeral services Wednesday, May 30, 10:00 AM at Riverside Memorial Chepel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street.
