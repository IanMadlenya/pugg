
        WEXLER-William A. B'nai B'rith International mourns the death of Dr. William A. Wexler, international president from 1965-1971 in Israel-where he had lived for many years-on November 30. A past chairman of the Conference of Presidents of Major Jewish American Organizations, member of the United Jewish Appeal National campaign cabinet, former Savannah, GA alderman and civil rights activist, Bill Wexler's example will continue to inspire us all. We extend our condolences to his family and friends. May his memory be a blessing always. Richard D. Heideman, Int'l President of B'nai B'rith Daniel S. Mariaschin Executive Vice President Arie Bar Zion, President of B'nai B'rith Israel Pnina Bor, International Vice President Naor Yerushalmi, Chairman B'nai B'rith World Cntr-Jerusalem Alan Schneider, Director B'nai B'rith World Cntr-Jerusalem
