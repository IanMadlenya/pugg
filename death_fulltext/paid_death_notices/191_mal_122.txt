
        BUTERBAUGH - George Henry, 99. Of Salisbury, December 8th in Salisbury, CT. He was the husband of Lucille (Egelhoff) Buterbaugh. He worked at Charles Platt in NYC and was an associate member of Eggers & Higgins as well as Harry T. Lindberg in NYC for many years. A memorial service will be held on Friday, December 13, 2002 at 11AM at the Noble Horizons Chapel, 17 Cobble Rd., Salisbury, CT. The Kenny Funeral Homes, 41 Main Street, Sharon, CT.
