
        WEINBERG--Herschel. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Yaakov Y. Kermaier, Rabbi Ira Leon Rennert, Chairman J. Ezra Merkin, President
        $(6$)WEINBERG--Herschel M., on December 26. Devoted son to the late Jacob and Gertrude. Beloved brother to Rhea Schonzeit. Loving uncle to Suzanne and Brad Feinberg, Andrew and Ronnie Schonzeit. Fun loving great-uncle to Gabriel, Jeremy, Yael and Jacob. Herschel attended Yeshiva of Flatbush, Brooklyn College and Harvard Law School. His warmth and compassion knew no boundaries. His keen sense of humor made us all laugh. He was philanthropic and successful in many arenas, and unconditionally devoted to Israel. His ethics and honesty were known to all. He was a trusted advisor and mentor to clients, family and friends alike. He will be greatly missed. Services Thursday, 9:45am, at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam.
