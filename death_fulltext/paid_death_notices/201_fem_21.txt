
        SMITH--Phyllis W. On October 3, 2003 at age 83 in Albany, NY. Widow of the late John K. Weiss and the late Walter W. Smith. She is survived by her brother Roger Warner, her children Karen Weiss, Robin Weiss, Peter Weiss and Jenifer Weiss, her stepchildren Terence Smith and Catherine Smith O'Maera, daughters-in-law Beth Ruland, Ruth Tobiassen and Elli Sher, 10 grandchildren, five great-grandchildren and her dear friend Peggy Slocum. Her son Kim Weiss predeceased her. A memorial service will be held in Albany at a later date.
