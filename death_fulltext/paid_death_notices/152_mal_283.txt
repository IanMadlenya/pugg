
        BONDARIN-Avrum. The Board, staff and members of the North American Federation of Temple Brotherhoods and the Jewish Chautauqua Society, mourn the untimely death of Av Bondarin, former executive director, in Lantana, FL. Av devoted 35 years of his life to the Reform Movement. He is survived by his loving wife Mary, daughter Lisa, son-in-law John and grandchildren Katrina and Mathew. May his memory be for a blessing. Stephen K. Breslauer, NFTB President; Irving B. Shnaider, JCS Chancellor; Douglas E. Barden, Executive Director.
