
        McLAUGHLIN-Edward J. On January 9, 2000, at age 51. Beloved son of Edward and Kathleen. Devoted brother of Maureen Villaverde and her husband William. Proud uncle of Claire and Sara. Fordham Prep '65 and Fordham University '69. Attained the rank of Major in New York State Supreme Court. Former Franciscan Friar. Affiliate to Franciscan Order of Holy Name Province. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Monday and Tuesday, 2-9 PM. Funeral Mass at St. Stephen of Hungary Church, 414 East 82nd St, Wednesday, 11 AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to Fordham Prep or St. Francis of Assisi Church.
