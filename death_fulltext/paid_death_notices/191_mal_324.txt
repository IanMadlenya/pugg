
        BARASH-David. December 28, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Muriel. Devoted father of Laura Goodfriend and Stephanie Becker. Cherished grandfather of Scott Becker, Lisa and Galen Giebler, Lawrence and Jennifer Goodfriend, Amy and Jon Nussbaum. Proud great-grandfather of Cadie Mae. Loving companion of Sis Goldstein. He was a CPA, partner of Barash, Friedman, Friedberg and Adasko, avid golfer and Chairman Emeritus of the Corinthian. He was loved and will be missed by all who knew him. Services Tuesday, 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
        BARASH-David. The Board of Governors and Membership of Ridgeway Country Club offer their deepest condolences to the family of David Barash upon his passing. Peter Arest, President
