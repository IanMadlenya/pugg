
        FONER-Lorraine, 79. Died on April 22nd, 2002. Beloved wife for 54 years of Henry; caring mother of Rachel and of the late Diane; loving grandmother of Michael and Justin; devoted sister of Elaine Seiff, Barry Lieberman and Leon Lieberman. She fashioned a life of concern for her fellow humans and has left an indelible mark on all who have been privileged to know her. Her memory will be treasured. Friends are invited to share in remembering her on Friday, April 26th, at 1 PM, at the Norman L. Jeffer Community Chapels, 47th St. and Ft. Hamilton Pkwy., Brooklyn, 718-853-4000.
