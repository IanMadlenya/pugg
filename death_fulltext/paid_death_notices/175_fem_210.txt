
        RUSH-Mary. On August 5, of Alzheimer's. ''Mollie'' nee Mary Stevens Conover, married Richard Rush in 1928 and moved to St. Paul's School, Concord, NH, where her husband ''Speedy'' taught. They spent summers in New Milford, CT, and retired there in '65. She is survived by her children, who will be forever grateful for the way she transformed their lives: Tom Rush, Wilson, WY; Bob Rush and Susan Rush, New Milford, CT; and five grandchildren.
