
        SCHLENGER-Ruth (nee Warner), 102. Of Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of South Orange and East Orange, NJ. Died on February 11, 2001, after a long, beautiful life. Beloved wife of the late Herman Schlenger for 67 years. Devoted mother of Donald, Judith Heyman and Marjorie Weil and dear mother-in-law of Linda Schlenger, Martin Weil and the late Kenneth Heyman. Adored grandmother of Nancy Doernberg (Richard), Carol Schlenger, John Heyman (Nita), Kenneth Heyman Jr. (Mimi), Patricia Heyman, Peter Weil (Pamela) and David Weil (Nancy). Also survived by 13 great-grandchildren and one great-great-grandchild. As the last survivor of the large Warner family of Newark, NJ, she will be missed by countless nieces and nephews. Funeral services private. Contributions in her memory to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Rd., Boca Raton, FL, would be appreciated.
