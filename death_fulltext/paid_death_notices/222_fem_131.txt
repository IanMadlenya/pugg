
        SIMON--Melinda Sue McVey, 42, Plantation, FL, danced her way through life across the U.S. and internationally. Raised in New York City on Riverside Drive, she studied with the famous ballet master, George Chaffe, who helped her launch her career, which she continued until her marriage to Joshua Simon, Florida pastry entrepreneur. The cause of death was breast cancer, which she fought bravely for nearly five years. Melinda Sue died Tuesday, July 19, 2005. She is survived by husband Joshua, and their daughter Alexandra of Plantation, FL; in New York City by her parents Mr. and Mrs. William J. McVey, Jr., and her four brothers and sisters. A memorial service will be held NYC at later date.
