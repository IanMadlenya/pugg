
        ZORN-Milton J. August 21, 2000. Beloved husband and best friend of Norma for 48 years. Most loving father of Amy, the late Janet, and son-in-law Steven. Happiest grandfather of Jonah. Dear uncle and brother-in-law, kind friend and colleague of greatest integrity. We are all blessed to have known and loved you. We will not be the same without you. Funeral services Wednesday, 12:00 noon at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale. Contributions to the New York Chapter of the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation are appreciated.
        ZORN-Milton J. The New York Chapter of the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation International mourns with deep sorrow the death of Milton J. Zorn, beloved husband of Norma, devoted father of Amy and the late Janet, and cherished grandfather. Milton was a founder and past President of the NY Chapter and was currently serving as Vice Chairman of the Board of Chancellors. Milton was a dear friend and wise counselor to many and was always there when needed. We will miss him forever. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the bereaved family. Lawrence B. Sorrel, President New York Chapter
        ZORN-Milton. The Board of Governors and members of Braeburn Country Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Milton Zorn. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Norma and his entire family. Len Shendell, President Judith Tarter, Secretary
