
        RANDOLPH--Francis F., Jr. Age 78 of New York City and Ridgefield, Connecticut, died on May 1, 2006. Survived by his wife, Catherine; his daughters, Sarah T. Randolph and Anne P. Randolph; his stepdaughter, Leslie Patel; his son-in-law, Sanjay Patel; and their daughters, Alisha and India; his sister, Elizabeth Cole; and his brother, Dr. Peter B. Randolph. He was the son of Francis F. Randolph and Sarah B. Randolph. He was a graduate of The Hotchkiss School, Yale College, and the Columbia Law School. He served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. Frank felt privileged in his professional life to have been a partner in the law firm Cravath, Swaine & Moore and, later, a Vice Chairman of Cablevision Systems Corporation. A memorial service will be set for late summer. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the NYPH Patient Database Fund, c/o Dr. Joseph Hayes, Box 18, New YorkPresbyterian Hospital/Weill Cornell Medical Center, 525 East 68th Street, New York, NY 10021.
