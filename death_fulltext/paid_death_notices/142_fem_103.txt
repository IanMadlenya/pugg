
        WALSH-Florence L. (Meg). It is wih great sadness that the Board, Staff and friends of Jersey Battered Women's Services, Morris Plains, NJ, mourn the sudden and untimely passing of Meg Walsh, our Director, and her infant son, Dixon. Early on, Meg showed a keen interest in and commitment to helping the victims of domestic violence. She will be sorely missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband, Mark, son, Carter and family. Baxter W. Graham, President Deborah A. Callahan, Exec. Dir.
