
        MUTH-Arthur Edward, 75, former president and co-owner of Petry Television Company, New York, NY, died March 1 at his home in Indian River Shores, FL. He was a native of Yonkers and attended Cornell and graduated from Rutgers University. He was a member of the University Club in New York, a Platform Tennis National Champion, and member of Fox Meadow Tennis Club, Scarsdale.
