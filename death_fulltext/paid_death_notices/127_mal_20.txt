
        NICHOLSON-Rev. George M. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of St. John Vianney Church, Flushing, July 31. Reposing St. John Vianney Parish Center, 140-10 34th Ave., Saturday, Aug. 2, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM, Sunday, August 3, 4-6 PM. Mass of Transferral Sunday, Aug. 3 at 7:30 P.M., St. John Vianney Church. Funeral Mass Monday, August 4, 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
