
        BENDEWALD-Mervin H. November 13, 2001. Born in eastern Montana to a ranching/homesteading family 68 years ago. Merv settled in the West Village after college and two years in the army. For nearly thirty-five years, he has been a mainstay to the Hudson Street community with a variety of enterprises including the current western wear store Whiskey Dust. Abe Beame called him ''the Mayor of Hudson Street.'' He will be deeply missed by his wife Hollie, children Daniel and Andrea, and his four sisters & two brothers. Services will be held Saturday November 17 at The Greenwich Village Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker Street at 11:30 AM. Contributions may be made to The Cardiovascular Institute in memory of Mervin Bendewald, The Mt. Sinai Medical Center, Box 1030, One Gustave L. Levy Place, New York, NY 10029.
