
        TRUPIN-Daniel Eugene. July 13, 1903-August 15, 1999. Worked in license department of New York, Jewish Community House of Bensonhurst. Moved to Berkeley 1972. Active in Gray Panthers. Published poetry, ''I Knock At Every Door'' (Agada Press). Survived by children, Bob and Joella, grandchildren, Naomi, Reevan and Johannah, and great-grandchildren, Lauren and Colleen. Preceded in death by his wife, Sophie, 1997. Contributions to Trupin Fund, Berkeley Gray Panthers, 1403 Addison St, Berkeley, CA 94702. Memorial in Berkeley, October 10.
