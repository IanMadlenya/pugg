
        SPENCER-Peter Harvey. Died on Feb. 15, at his home in Telluride, CO. Born January 18, 1939 in Brooklyn, he was a graduate of Yale University, manufacturer of children's clothing, former Mayor of Telluride, & professional writer. Loving husband of Rebecca Parmer Spencer; brother of Nancy Munno; devoted father of Meghan Jane, Scott & partner Sara Silton, Deborah S. Jacobs and husband Andrew, & Bonnie Sarah Spencer; grandfather to Seth Edward Jacobs. Services are to be held in Telluride on February 18.
