
        HULLEY-Roger M. Bloom. 41, of Gardiner, NY, formerly of Ridgewood, NJ, died on Monday Aug. 11th of AIDS-related PML. A graduate of Brown University and Columbia Law School, Roger was an assoc. attorney at Skadden Arps in NYC. As a long-term AIDS survivor, he was active in his community, leading buddy teams for ARCS, a local AIDS organization. Survivors include his partner Jeffrey Bright, mother, Terry Hope Hulley, of Weston, CT, sister, Marcia Hulley, of Brookline, MA, and nephews, Willie and Peter. Donations may be sent to The Roger M. Bloom Hulley Memorial Fund, PO Box 399, Gardiner, NY 12525.
