
        SOMACH--Martin, age 75, died July 14, 2004 at home in Great Neck, NY. For almost two years he put up a gutsy fight against pancreatic cancer. Dear husband of Barbara, loving father of Cara (Robert), Brian (Joy) and Gary (deceased). Proud and boastful grandfather of Samantha, Zachary, Joshua and Dillon. Marty will be remembered for his infectious laugh, ingratiating smile and overwhelming enthusiasm for life. He was passionate about tennis, bridge, lunch with guys and sunsets in Siesta Key, FL. He was one of the old time retailers on the Lower East Side and in the Bronx, owning and operating Somach's for 55 years. Marty touched many lives. He will be greatly missed. Funeral services Sunday at 12:30PM, Temple Emanuel, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck. Contributions may be made in Marty's memory to The Dr. Howard Bruckner Research Fund, Dept of Medical Oncology, 150 55th St, Brooklyn, NY 11220.
        SOMACH--Martin. A terrific friend, with special warmth and a strong sense of self. Some of our most golden moments were shared with Marty and Barbara. He fought to the end to stay with his loved ones. We'll all miss him. Joan and Al Weinstein
        SOMACH--Martin. The members of Shelter Rock Tennis Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Martin Somach. He was a friend to all and will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to his wife and to his entire family.
