
        BASS - Catherine Hawley. Died in Cambridge, MA, October 25th, aged 27, of manicdepressive illness. Katie graduated summa cum laude from St. Paul's School in 1993 and was a member of the Princeton Class of 1999. She is survived by her parents, George S. Bass and Jane L. Bass, her siblings Margot and Steedman, and grandmother Jane H. Liddell. A memorial service will be held Friday, November 8th at 11:00 AM, Church of the Heavenly Rest, 5th Ave and 90th St. Rememberances to Trees NY or Cheetah Conservation Fund.
