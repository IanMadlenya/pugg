
        ZELTZER--Pearl. The National Foundation for Jewish Culture extends its sincere condolences to George Mike Zeltzer, founder of the NFJC, and to his family on the passing of his beloved wife Pearl. Pearl was a visionary patron of the arts and an avid supporter of dance. Her legacy lives on in her contributions to the Jewish community. Carol B. Spinner, President Charlotte Newberger, Chairperson Elise M. Bernhardt, Executive Director
