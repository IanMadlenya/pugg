
        AVERSA-Pauline (nee Weinberg). March 18, 1911-November 13, 1999. Cherished wife, mother, grandmother and friend. Devoted to her family, late husband Rafael; children Gina Aversa, Hugo Kierszenbaum, M.D. and Susan Wein; granchildren Heidi Wein and Samantha Kierszenbaum. Former social worker, teacher and retailer; Hunter College Class of 1931. Active in her younger years in organizations devoted to peace, civil rights and education; formidable card player. An extraordinary human being: compassionate, feisty, wise and courageous. She will be greatly missed by all.
