        TUCKER-Herman. Temple Israel Center of White Plains, NY, mourns the passing of Herman Tucker, beloved father of our esteemed rabbi, Gordon Tucker. Mr. Tucker was a special presence in our community and we will greatly miss his warmth and humor. It has been our pleasure to share in his enormous pride in his family. Our condolences go to Sylvia Rosencranz, Rabbi Tucker and Amy Cohn, Toby Tucker Hecht and Barry Hecht, and grandchildren Ethan and Ariela, Becky, Micah, Spencer and Jonathan. May his memory endure as a blessing for all who knew him. David Glasser, President
        TUCKER-Herman. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the death of Herman Tucker, father of our dear friend and colleague, Rabbi Gordon Tucker. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Rabbi Tucker and his wife, Amy Cohn, his sister and brother-in-law, Toby Tucker Hecht and Barry Hecht, and the entire Tucker and Hecht families upon their sad loss.
