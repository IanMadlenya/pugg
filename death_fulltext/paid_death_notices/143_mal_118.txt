
        FOX-William T. On December 6, 1998. Beloved husband of Bonnie, loving father of Mona, dear brother of John Taussig. Memorial service Thursday, December 10, 11:30 AM at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65 St. Interment to follow at Salem Fields Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to the William T. Fox Scholarship Fund Trust Account at Bank of Coronado, 1190 Orange Ave., Coronado, CA 92118. Mr. Fox was a grandson of William Fox, founder of Fox Film Corporation, was on the Board of Directors of the William and Eva Fox Foundation, which offers fellowships to graduate students in theater at such universities as Yale, NYU, Juilliard and Guild Hall and also on the Board of Directors for the New York Virtuosi Chamber Orchestra based in Manhattan.
