
        BUSH--Helen Stokes Merrill. Peacefully, after a brief illness, in her ninety-ninth year. Daughter of I.N. Phelps Stokes, widow of Edwin K. Merrill and Donald F. Bush. Mother of Edith M. Smart, the late Edwin K. Merrill, Jr., Penelope M. Brouder and Newton P.S. Merrill. Survived by numerous descendants. Funeral service 11AM, Friday, August 13, St. Matthew's Church, Bedford, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions please to St. Matthew's Church, PO Box 293, Bedford, New York 10506.
