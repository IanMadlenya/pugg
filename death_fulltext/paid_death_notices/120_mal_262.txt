
        MEYER-William R., 47. Author and film historian, of natural causes at his home in Staten Island. Graduated 1967 from Pascack Hills H.S., Montvale, NJ; The New School for Social Research, BA, 1974; NY Univ., MA, 1976. Books include ''The Making of the Great Western'', ''The Warner Bros. Directors'' and ''The Film Buff's Catalog''. For information call A. Azzara Funeral Home, Inc., Staten Island, New York.
