
        BAXTER-The Hon. Kay, O.D. Consul General of Jamaica. The officers, directors and friends of American Friends of Jamaica offer sincere condolences to family and government of Jamaica in the untimely passing of Kay Baxter, who gave creativity and excellence to her diplomatic life. She will be missed by all who knew her. Hon. Glen A. Holden, Chairman Anne F. Sabo, Secretary A.F.J.
