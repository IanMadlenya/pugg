
        SHAPIRO--Dr. Robert S. Passed away Tuesday, July 5, 2005. He is survived by his beloved wife, Melba. Devoted father of Mark (Roberta) and Jonathan (Elaine). Cherished grandfather of Rebecca, Sara, Samuel, Jessica and Lara. Loving brother of Dr. Theodore (Joan) Shapiro. He also leaves many loving nieces and nephews and a wide circle of dear friends. Dr. Shapiro was a psychiatrist/psychoanalyst in New York City for over 50 years. He was a graduate of Washington University where he received his B.A. and M.D. He also graduated from Columbia Psychoanalytic Clinic, where he supervised candidates and residents. A memorial service is scheduled for Friday 11 AM at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 Lecount Place, New Rochelle. Family will receive guests from 10 AM.
