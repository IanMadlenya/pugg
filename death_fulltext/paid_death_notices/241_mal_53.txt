
        REHAGE--Kenneth J., 96, of New Brunswick, NJ, Professor Emeritus of Education at the University of Chicago, died January 31, 2007. He will be deeply missed by his wife Laurel; his children Joan, David, and Larry; his five grandchildren and one great-grandchild; and all who were lucky enough to have known him. Ken had two careers: professor of education at the University of Chicago and secretary of the National Society for the Study of Education, a post from which he retired at age 90. A memorial service was held February 3, 2007. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions in his name may be made to the National Society for the Study of Education, College of Education (M/ C147), University of Illinois at Chicago. 1040 W. Harrison Street, Chicago, IL 60607-7133.
