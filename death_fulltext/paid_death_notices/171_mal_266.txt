
        SCHOENBERG-Mitchell. Died on April 18th, 2001, at age 89. Beloved husband of Beatrice. Loving father of the late Dr. Bruce S. Schoenberg. Devoted grandfather of Ian and Claire. Cherished brother of Marguerite Davidow. Was for many years Senior Chemical Consultant and Chief Executive, Sales, for the Diamond Shamrock Chemical Company. He was respected for his honesty and integrity, and will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Graveside services on Sunday, April 22, at 1 PM, at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Iselin, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Bruce S. Schoenberg Award Fund, American Academy of Neurology, Education and Research Foundation, 1080 Montreal Avenue, St. Paul, MN 55116-2325.
