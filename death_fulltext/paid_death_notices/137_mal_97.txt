
        EISENMAN-Henry (Hank). The Board of Governors, members and staff of Greater New York Hospital Association mourn the loss of Hank Eisenman, beloved father of our esteemed colleague and friend, Dana Eisenman Sherwin. Mr. Eisenman was a major contributor to New York's health care industry, having served as an executive at Empire Blue Cross and Blue Shield for many years. A man of warmth and intellect, he will be missed by many. We extend our sincere condolences to the entire Eisenman and Sherwin families. Mark J. Mundy, Chairman Kenneth E. Raske, President Greater NY Hospital Association
