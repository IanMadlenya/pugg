
        VOORHEES-Lois (nee Gaw), age 86, on January 20, 1997, in Harleysville, PA. Wife of the late G. Coerte Voorhees. She is survived by her son G. Stephen and Pieter Beekman Voorhees and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, February 9, 1997 at 2 p.m. St. Christopher's Episcopal Church, Gladwyne, PA. Contributions in her memory may be made to the G.C. Voorhees Memorial Faculty Enhancement Fund, St. Andrew's School, Middletown, DE 19709.
