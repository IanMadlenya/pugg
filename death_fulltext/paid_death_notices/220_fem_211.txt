
        SELSKY--Tobe (nee Lichtenthal) died peacefully age 93 on May 23. Predeceased by husband Maurice in 1974. Mother to Susan and son-inlaw Dr. Solomon Liebowitz, Edward and daughter-in-law Barbara. Grandmother to Nancy, Samantha and Neal Kaufman. Great-grandmother to Madeline Lily. Tobe loved her dearest companions--angels sent from Heaven--Valentina Horowitz, Tamara, Marie, Nana, Medea and Nino. May Tobe be greeted by her parents and husband in Heaven. In our memories always.
