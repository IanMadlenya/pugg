
        RECCO-Salvatore. Of Lansdale, formerly of New Britain Township, PA, died Tuesday, October 24, 2000 at his home; he was 74 years old. He was the devoted husband of Joan Kotchie Recco. Born in Queens, NY he was the son of the late Salvatore and Mary Picano Recco. Mr. Recco lived in New Britain Township for over 20 years. He was the owner and mechanic of a floor covering business for many years in Queens, NY. He also worked for the Byrne Stain Glass Co., in Doylestown.
