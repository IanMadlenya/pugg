
        WEINBERG--Emmeline Epstein. ''Em'' of Pipersville PA, formerly of New York, NY, died Thursday, May 4, 2006 after a protracted illness. She was 92. Beloved wife of the late Eugene Weinberg. Mother of Michael and the late Alex. Grandmother of Mia Weinberg Clinton and the late Michael Weinberg Jr. Greatgrandmother of Michael III, Amber, Keegan and Devin. Sister of Rose Frish. Relatives and friends are invited to her memorial service at 11AM on Wednesday, June 28, 2006 at the family residence at 6312 Carversville Rd., Pipersville, PA. Friends are requested to make donations to their favorite charity.
