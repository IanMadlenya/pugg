
        PASTORIZA--Nancy King. A 31-year resident of Bronxville, NY, died on Thursday, April 6, in Bellevue, WA at the age of 80. Born in Bakersfield, CA, Nancy graduated from University of Califorinia at Berkeley, Class of 1947. She was a reporter at Life Magazine in New York City, retiring to marry Hugh G. Pastoriza in 1961. She is greatly missed by her three children: Lucie, Carolyn and Hugh, and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Reform Church in Bronxville at 11 AM on Saturday, April 29.
