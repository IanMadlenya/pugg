
        BISTRITZKY-Schifra. We mourn the loss of a devoted friend and a kind and compassionate person, Mrs. Schifra Bistritzky. As a Founder of the Medical Center, she played a vital role in helping the mission of the ''Hospital with a Heart'' to heal the sick and ease the pain of the people of Jerusalem and Israel. We extend our sincerest condolences to her sons, Leibel and Alexander, and her daughters, Mrs. Rilka Rhein and Mrs. Bella Katzenstein. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Mrs. Erica Jesselson, Chairperson of the Board Menno Ratzker, President Sidney Adler, Treasurer Dr. Stuart Tauber, Exec. Dir.
