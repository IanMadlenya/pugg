
        REGA-John A., Colonel, U.S. Army, Retired. Of Old Tappan, New Jersey, formerly of Silver Spring, MD, and Huntsville, Alabama, on April 2, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Mary G. (nee Palombo). Cherished father of Mary L. Zaks (Arthur), Judith D. D'Ambrosio (Anthony) & Jane L. Rega. Loving grandfather of Joseph M. D'Ambrosio (Trudi), Zosia, Karen and Laura Zaks, & great-grandfather of Anthony Jacob D'Ambrosio. Born in Valle Agricola, Italy, in 1905, Colonel Rega came to this country as a young boy. He entered military service in 1922, serving for thirty years in the U.S. Army. He is a veteran of World War II and the Korean War. He worked for the state of Connecticut, Office of Civil Defense and for NASA at the Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, AL. A Christian Mass and military burial will be held at Arlington National Cemetery at 12:45 PM, on May 12, 1999. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Colonel Rega's memory to the Arthritis Foundation or the St. Jude's Children Hospital in Memphis, TN.
