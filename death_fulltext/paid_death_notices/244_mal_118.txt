
        LANDOR--John Henry M.D., Ph.B. Beloved husband of Mary Gibbons, died January 4th, 2007 at home in Monterey, MA. Professor of general surgery and practicing surgeon, he had a long and productive career which included extensive research in gastrointestinal physiology. Survived by six children from his first marriage, two stepchildren, four grandchildren and five step-grandchildren. Memorial service on August 4th, 2007 in Monterey, MA. Contributions can be made in his memory to the Massachusetts Audubon Society.
