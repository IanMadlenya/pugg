
        LANDIS-William B. Aged 84, of New York City, on July 6, 2002. Retired senior tax partner of Mudge, Rose, Guthrie, Alexander and Ferdon, and member of Tax Forum. Survived by Elizabeth, his wife of 54 years, his brother F. Parks Landis and his family. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Center for Constitutional Rights or Cornell University Law School.
