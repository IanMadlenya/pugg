
        ONDERDONK -- John C. ''Jack.'' Of Chestnut Ridge, NY, died Friday, June 25, 2004, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in Manhattan. Born in Englewood, NJ, on July 20, 1931, Jack was an AllCounty basketball player at Dwight Morrow High School before attending Duke University and serving in the United States Navy. He married Elizabeth Anne Schilling in 1961, and started a family in River Edge, NJ. He lived in Upper Saddle River, NJ, for 14 years before moving to Chestnut Ridge, NY. An insurance broker, Jack was a cofounder of Dunlop, Onderdonk and Wilson, Inc, of Manhattan, a top fund raiser and a member of the WalkAmerica Executive Committee for the March of Dimes, an avid golfer, and a devoted family man. Survived by beloved wife Elizabeth and five children, John of Hoboken, NJ, Paul of Wyckoff, NJ, Amy of Boston, MA, Beth of Hoboken, NJ, and Chris of Boston, MA, and siblings Sue Bach of Chapel Hill, NC, and Fred of Amagansett, NY. Also survived by six loving grandchildren, three daughters-inlaw, Trang, Maria and Colleen, and son-in-law Kevin. Predeceased by his sister Beth Walker Shapleigh of Vero Beach, FL. Visiting Monday, 7-9 pm, and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Ave, Wyckoff, NJ 07481. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 11 am, at St. Elizabeth's RC Church, 700 Wyckoff Ave, Wyckoff. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in Jack's name to the Jack Onderdonk Memorial Fund at Green Meadow Waldorf School, Chestnut Ridge, NY, or the March of Dimes of Greater New York.
