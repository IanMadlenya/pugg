
        LIPTON--Henry. On September 27, 2004 at the age of 69. Beloved husband of 45 years to Marion. Loving father of Dana and Joanna and fatherin-law of Americo. Cherished grandfather of Victoria Rose. Devoted brother of Marjorie Navarro. Supervisor of School Psychologists in District 22, Brooklyn, for over 30 years. Devoted to his family and profession. He will be missed by family and colleagues alike. Services Wednesday 1 PM at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave. (Ave. J), Brooklyn, NY.
