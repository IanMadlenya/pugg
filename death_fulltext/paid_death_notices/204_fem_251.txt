
        WEINGARTEN--Doris Dunn. 76, of New York City and Hancock, NY, passed away peacefully on January 16th, 2004. A graduate of Skidmore College (Class of 1948), she was a committed NYC art teacher, a gifted interior designer, and a lover of the arts and travel. Married to the late Aaron Weingarten, sister of Irving Dunn, M.D., Nicole Dunn and Sylvia Dunn, loving aunt and great-aunt to many nieces and nephews. Memorial service will be on Saturday, February 7th at 2PM. Please contact the family for details. In lieu of flowers, do nations to the Central Park Conservancy Memorial Gifts, 14 East 60th St, NY NY 10022 would be appreciated.
