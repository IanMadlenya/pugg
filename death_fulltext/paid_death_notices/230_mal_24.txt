
        ROSENTHAL--Maurice Milton (Sharkey). WWII veteran, passed away peacefully on March 1, 2006. Former resident of Trenton, NJ and Boca Raton, FL. He is survived by his beloved wife, Beverly, sister, Dorothy Saaz, sons, Andrew and Robert, daughter, Jane Schorr, son-in-law, Marc Schorr, and cherished grandchildren, Seth and Courtney Schorr. Services will be held at Temple Beth El, Boca Raton, Friday, March 3, 12 noon.
