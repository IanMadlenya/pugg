
        ROSENFELD--Naomi E. Of New York City and Quiogue, LI, died on April 19, 2006, after a valiant fight against leukemia. Following a distinguished career in investment banking and investor relations, she immersed herself in the world of music and opera, becoming a member of the Board of the Metropolitan Opera Club and Chair of its Program Committee. She is survived by her husband Steven, her daughters Kathryn and Elizabeth, her sister Daniela Winkler Martin, and a large circle of friends, all of whom will miss her terribly. Funeral services will be held on Monday, April 24 at 11:30 a.m. at the Beth-El Chapel of Temple Emanuel, Fifth Avenue and 65th Street. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Naomi's memory to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, designated to support the research of Dr. Marc Heaney, and/or City of Hope Medical Center, Duarte, CA, designated to support the work of Dr. Stephen J. Forman.
