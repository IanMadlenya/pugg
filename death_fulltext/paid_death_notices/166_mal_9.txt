
        WALKER-Robert W. July 17, 1923October 30, 2000. Beloved husband of Georgina (Rena), loving father of Gail (Nicholas) Patitucci, Lynn (Michael) Kern, Robert (Nancy) Walker and Coreen (Wayne) Sumple. Devoted grandfather of Krista, Scott, Jennifer, Heather, Kimberly, Robbie, Colin, Tyler, Kyle and Kelsey. Loving greatgrandfather to Drew, Tyler, Daniel, David and Rebecca. Raised in Greenwich Village and graduated from the High School of Commerce. Three years in Naval service in China during WWII. Retired from the textile industry as Executive Vice President and Member of the Board of Directors, Stonecutter Mills. Formerly a member at North Jersey and Ridgewood Country Clubs. Always devoted in his faith, Bob was a former member of Advent Lutheran Church in Wyckoff, New Jersey, where he served as Church Council President. Retired to Palm City, Florida where he was a member of Immanuel Lutheran Church and Harbour Ridge Golf and Country Club. A celebration of his life will be held on November 1 at 11:00 AM, Immanuel Lutheran Church, 2655 Immanuel Drive, Palm City 34990. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be given to the Music Fund at Immanuel Lutheran Church or to the Salvation Army.
