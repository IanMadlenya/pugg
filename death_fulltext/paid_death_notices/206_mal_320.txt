
        SANTANGELO--Francis R. of Boynton Beach, Florida and Harrison, New York died of lung cancer on Monday, March 22, 2004 at Good Samaritan Hospital in Palm Beach, Florida. He was 71. Mr. Santangelo founded Santangelo and Company Specialist Firm on the American Stock Exchange until 1987. He continued to trade and was a consultant until his death. He was born in 1932 to the Honorable Robert V. and Juliette Santangelo. He graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 1953 and later earned a law degree from St. John's University and his graduate degree from New York University. He served in the United States Marine Corps during the Korean War and earned the rank of First Lieutenant. He is survived by his wife Barbara Taccetta and four children, Toni Archibald, Robert Santangelo, Linda Santangelo and Dina Pfohl, eight grandchildren and brother Robert V. Santangelo, Jr. He will be remembered for his love of family, friends and great zest for life, along with his passion for thoroughbred racing, boating, fine art and his retreat on Hen Island. Family will receive friends on Friday, March 26, 2004 from 2-4PM and 7-9PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, New York. Funeral mass is 10AM on Saturday, March 27, 2004 at the Church of the Resurrection, 910 Boston Post Rd., Rye, NY. Interment will follow at Greenwood Union Cemetery, Rye, New York. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Lung Cancer Research Fund, C/O Dr. Mark Kris, Memorial Sloan-Kettering, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
