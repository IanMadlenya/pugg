
        GAMPERT--Stewart Allan. Formerly of Tenafly, NJ, passed away on October 16, 2004 in Boca Raton, FL. Beloved husband of Louise (Rothstein). Adored father of Richard Gampert of NYC, and Caryll Greene and her husband of Steven of Massachusetts. Devoted uncle of Lynn, Elizabeth, Richard, Ellen and Samantha. He was the son of the late Louis and Estelle Gampert, and devoted brother to his late sister Caryll. His dedication and love to his family, friends and colleagues will be greatly missed. Family will recieve friends Tuesday, 6-9 pm, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Funeral services Wednesday, 11:30 am, at Frank E. Campbell. Interment to follow at Maimonides Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Huntington Disease Society of America.
