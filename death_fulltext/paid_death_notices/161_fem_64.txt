
        LANDEGGER-Cecilia. June 5, 2000. Beloved mother of Christine Mroczek, Cary Landegger, Claudia Malaya, Celia Landegger and the late Carl Michael Landegger. Grandmother of ten. Devoted sister of the late Elena Ugarte. She was the daughter of the late Joaquin Elizalde who was the Philippine ambassador to the United States and Foreign Secretary. She will be deeply missed. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street Monday, June 12, 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, June 13, 10AM at the Church of the Epiphany, 239 E. 21 St, NYC. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
