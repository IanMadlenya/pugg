
        HAYWARD-Howard Boynton. 85, of Vero Beach, FL, died May 26, 2001, at Indian River Estates Medical Center. Mr. Hayward was born February 21, 1916, in Brooklyn, NY, resided in Scarsdale, NY, for 60 years. He was raised in Bronxville, NY. He was a graduate of the Lawrenceville School in Lawrenceville, NJ, and a World War II veteran. He spent most of his career in advertising, largely with Rubin H. Donnely, and was a member of the Squadron A Club in Manhattan, NY. Survivors include his wife of 59 years, Margaret Hayward of Vero Beach; two daughters, Deanne Moynahan and Brooke Morell both of Rye, NY; and seven grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Rye. Friends may make contributions to St. Jude Childrens Research Hospital, PO Box 50, Memphis, TN 38101-9929, in memory of Mr. Hayward. Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach.
