
        MOSKOWITZ--Ruth B. Suddenly on May 12. Beloved wife of the late Louis. Adored mother of Seymour and Paul. Cherished mother-in-law of Susan.         MOSKOWITZ--Ruth. The Hewlett-East Rockaway Jewish Centre mourns the loss of its esteemed member, Ruth Moskowitz, and extends heartfelt sympathy to her sons, Paul and Sy, and the entire family. Rabbi Stanley Platek Marc Goldberg, President
