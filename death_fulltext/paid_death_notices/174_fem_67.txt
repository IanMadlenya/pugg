
        FRESE-Elizabeth B. Of Jamestown, R.I., died July 6, 2001. She was 72. Born in NYC, daughter of the late Gwendolin Evans and A. Prentiss Butler of Scarsdale, NY. She was employed by Bank of Newport from 1975 until 1995. Wife of the late Calvin W. Survived by four children, Lynda A. Frese, Calvin W. Frese Jr., Catherine E. IanFrese and Robert P. Frese. Sister Ann B. Brown and 6 grandchildren. Service at Saint Matthew's Episcopal Church in Jamestown, R.I., on July 13th, 2001 at 11:00am.
