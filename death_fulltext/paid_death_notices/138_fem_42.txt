
        LANGNER-Rose (nee Moskowitz) died on Sunday, July 5, 1998. She was a dedicated teacher at P.S. 39 of Brooklyn for over twenty-five years. She will be mourned by her mother, Golda Moskowitz, by her husband Saul, children Elisheva, Sury (Cheryl) and Dovid Hager; grandson Charlie, sister Hannah Lindenblatt, and brothers Dr. George, Rabbi Mendy, Moshe, Dr. Shuli and Avi Moskowitz. She will be missed by all who were privileged to have known her.
