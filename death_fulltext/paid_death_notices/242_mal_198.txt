
        SCHWARTZ--Dr. Alfred H., D.D.S., Died March 20th, after a long illness, at age 88. Beloved husband, family member, colleague and friend. Associate professor of oral and maxillofacial surgery at NYU College of Dentistry. Member of numerous professional organizations. He was recognized for 35 years of service by NYU. Past executive board member of Kappa Chapter (Columbia Teachers College) Kappa Delta Pi Honor Society. Friend of the Jewish Museum and member of the Gothic Lodge of Masons. He spent a lifetime of dedicated service to humanity. Services Thursday, 12:45 pm, at The Riverside 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
