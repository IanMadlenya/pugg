
        BURRIS-H. Barry. On May 10, 2002, at age 72. Beloved by all. Father of David Burris, Joel Burris, and Ellen Burris Kramer. Husband of Rita Burris. Father-in-law of Susan Burris, Pam Esterman, and Andrew Kramer. Devoted grandfather of Rachel, Sarah, Justin, Paige, Joshua, Emily and Daniel. Retired partner of Pricewaterhouse Coopers LLP. Barry lived his life to the fullest, including his love of sailing and his family. He will be dearly missed by his family and all who knew him. Contributions may be made to The Starlight Children's Foundation, 1560 Broadway N.Y, N.Y. 10036. Funeral service 11:15 a.m, Sunday, May 12th at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
