
        TAMBINI-Louis A. Died on September 5, 1999 at age 70 after a long illness. He was pre-deceased by his first wife Elma (nee Calvi). He is survived by his wife Phyllis Shepard Tambini, a beloved daughter, Amy McMillan Tambini, a stepson Bruce Shepard Newman and a cousin Anna Sterlini. He was a graduate of Cardinal Hayes and St. John's University. A much loved educator he was the Athletic Director of the Loyola School, Manhattan for 30 years and of the St. Bernard School in Manhattan for 15 years. At Loyola he coached many outstanding basketball teams and was cited by the Daily News as the ''Winningest High School Coach.'' He also scouted college players for the NY Nets and Knicks. Calling hours are from 7-9PM at the Frank E. Campbell funeral chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Thursday, September 9. A memorial mass will be held on Friday, September 10, at 1PM at St. Ignatius Church, Park Avenue at 84 Street. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY
