
        CARLSON -- Eileen Evers, Esq., 79, of Carefree, AZ passed away December 11, 2005. Mrs. Carlson was retired chief legal counsel for the Securities & Exchange Commission, the Legal Aid Society of New York and also served as CEO of the Arthur J. Evers Corporation. She was a member of Our Lady of Joy Catholic Church in Carefree and the Circumnavigators Club. Her husband Robert preceded her in death in January of 2005. Memorial Services will be held at 10:00 AM, Saturday, December 17, 2005 at Our Lady of Joy Catholic Church, 36811 N. Pima Road, Carefree, AZ. For info. Messinger Pinnacle Peak Mortuary.
