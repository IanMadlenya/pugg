
        GRISWOLD--John S. The Officers, Trustees and staff of the Children's Aid Society mourn the passing of former Chairman and President, John S. Griswold. His 50 plus years as officer, trustee and chairman emeritus of Children's Aid were a tribute to his leadership. His enlightened presence and thoughtful determination helped to grow CAS to become one of the largest youth-serving agencies in the country. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and his many colleagues and friends. Edgar Koerner, Chairman Angela Diaz, M.D., M.Ph., President Philip Coltoff, CEO C. Warren Moses, Executive Director
        GRISWOLD--John Sr. The Board of Governors, Trustees, staff and members of Boys & Girls Clubs of America wish to express their profound sorrow over the passing of Mr. John Griswold Sr., a Life Member National Trustee, friend and a great advocate for America's children. Mr. Griswold gave 64 years of active service to the Boys & Girls Club cause, and was part of our national leadership since 1949. always willing to take on new challenges, he was an inspiration to generations of board members. We will miss him greatly. Boys & Girls Clubs Anne Szostak, Chairman Roxanne Spillett, President
