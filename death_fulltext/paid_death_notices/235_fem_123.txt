
        ASPINWALL--Valerie on August 17, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Everett Aspinwall. Loving daughter of the late Gerson & Vivian Reichman. Caring sister of Richard and the late Gar Reichman. Adoring aunt of Greg, Arlyn, Nathaniel, Benjamin and Amanda. Valerie was born and raised in NY. She has spent the past 35 years in Palm Beach, Florida. In 1972 she and her late husband owned and operated WPBR Radio Station until 1987. It was during these years they hosted the popular radio programs ''Palm Beach AM'' and ''Luncheon at the Colony.'' She then owned and operated Aspinwall Communications. She was the grand benefactor for the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society in Palm Beach. She was voted women of the year for The City of Hope Foundation. She also sat on the board as Vice President of the Altschul Foundation. Services will be held Monday at 10 AM the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY 10580. Interment will be held Wednesday 11 AM at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, 1311 Mamaroneck Ave., White Plains, NY 10605, Reichman Memorial Fund Development Dept.
