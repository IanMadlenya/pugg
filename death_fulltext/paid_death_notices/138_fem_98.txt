
        YUSEN-Muriel (nee Spear). Passed away of breast cancer on July 12, 1998. Beloved wife of Henry. Loving mother of Andrew and Darlene Yusen and Susan and Jay Perry. Incredible grandmother of Max Perry, Matthew and Kyle Yusen. Dear sister of Ellen Spear Smith. Services Tuesday, 1:30 PM at Temple Beth El, Old Mill Road, Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Auxiliary of North Shore University Hospital at Manhasset or Temple Beth El of Great Neck would be appreciated.
        YUSEN-Muriel Spear. Dear, sweet sister-in-law, aunt, great aunt, best friend-we love her and will miss her always. Alvin, Jane, Amy, Tom, Ed, Chuck, Lonie, Frannie, Philip, Laura, Eric, Jenny, Ilyse and Adam
        YUSEN-Muriel. The entire Auxiliary mourns the loss of a dear and good friend. A loyal and active volunteer for twenty-five years, Muriel (Mickey) served the Auxiliary in a variety of leadership positions. She could be relied on to take on any task with responsibility, enthusiasm and sensitivity. Throughout the years, Mickey Yusen was Chairman of a number of auxialiary/volunteer services, an officer of the Auxiliary Executive Board and especially, a loyal and dedicated friend. We will all miss Mickey, and join together in expressing our deepest sympathy to her family. Hilary Chilton, President Auxiliary North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset
        YUSEN-MURIEL. Temple BethEl of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its long-time member and immediate past president of our sisterhood. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
