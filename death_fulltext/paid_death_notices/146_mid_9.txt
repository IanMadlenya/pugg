
        FARROW-Florence. Died February 23, 1999. She was born on January 29, 1906 in St. Louis, MO, the daughter of a well-known radiologist Edward Skinner and his wife Florence Skinner. Her husband, John Farrow was the Chief of Breast Service at Memorial SloanKettering. Dr. and Mrs Farrow had no children. Mrs. Farrow is survived by a cousin Mrs. Betty Schroeder of Mission Viejo, CA. Burial took place at Evergreen Cemetery. A memorial service will be held Thursday. March 25, 1999 at 4PM at the Church of Heavenly Rest at 5th Ave. and 90th St, NYC. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Breast and Cancer Reserach Foundation, 1735 York Avenue, NY, NY 10128.
