
        DuLONG-Raoul. The Chorus of The Metropolitan Opera is deeply saddened at the death of our friend and colleague. His voice was silenced too early. Visitation Redden Funeral Home, 325 West 14 Street, NYC Thursday, 7-9PM. Interment private.
        DuLONG-Raoul, 40, of New York City, died July 29, 1999. Dear friend and colleague, member of the Metropolitan Opera Chorus, world traveler, lover of languages, cultures, films and his music. He will be dearly missed by all who loved him. May his soul be in restful peace and surrounded by the light, sunshine and love he so dearly longed for in life.
