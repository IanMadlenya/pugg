
        KELSON-Theodore Thomas. The son of Daisy Kelson Lockley of Baltimore, Md. Died suddenly on June 11, 2000 in Goldwater Hospital in Long Island, New York. He leaves to mourn a son, Theodore Kelson Williams of New York, a mother Daisy Kelson Lockley, six sisters, Doris, Gloria, Eleanor, Alice, Roxann and Hazel, four brothers, Godfrey, Herman, James and Carl all of Baltimore.
        KELSON-Theodore. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Theodore Kelson, associated with The Times from 1981 until retiring in 1992.
