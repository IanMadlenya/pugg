
        GARSON-Inez. Artist, art historian, died in Seattle, September 17. 83. Beloved mother, grandmother, great-grandmother. Born in New York City, she is survived by her three children, Rachel Garson of Seattle, Adam Perl of Ithaca, NY, Joshua Perl of New York City; five grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. She worked at the Museum of Modern Art, directed the White Art Museum at Cornell, and was curator at the Hirshhorn Museum in Washington. Gifts may be made to Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, Patient Care Program, P.O. Box 19024, Seattle, Washington 98104.
