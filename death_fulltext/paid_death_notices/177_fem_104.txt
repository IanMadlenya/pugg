
        CLARK-Winifred Wood. October 11, 2001. Sarah Lawrence College records with sorrow the death of Winifred Wood Clark, devoted alumna (Class of 1932) and trustee of the College for over 40 years. She was a woman of extraordinary grace, widsom, and dignity. We will miss her greatly, and extend our deep sympathy to her family. Margot C. Bogert, Chairman of the Board Michele Tolela Myers, President
