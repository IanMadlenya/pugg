
        ALDRICH--Winifred Payne (Wynn). Died at home in Manhattan on December 21, 2003 after a long illness. Her husband, Larry Aldrich, died in 2001. Born in 1914, she is survived by adopted daughters Kate Strassman and Susan Huberth, and by two grandchildren. Wynn, with her husband Larry, founded the Aldrich Museum of Contemporary Art in Ridgefield, Connecticut in 1964.
