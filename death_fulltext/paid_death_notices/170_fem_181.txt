
        ANDRUS - Eileen Margaret. March 22, 2001. Born February 2, 1913. Widow of the late Henry. Beloved mother of Elaine, William and Rene. Cherished grandmother of Eileen, Jack, Tara, Robyn and Erin. A saint; loved by all whose lives she touched. Director of Purchasing at Lenox Hill Hospital, retired 1979. Graduate of Saint Ignatius Loyola, Cathedral High School and The Grace Institue. A wake will be held Friday, 2-9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, N.Y. Funeral Mass Saturday, 11AM, March 24th, Saint Ignatius Loyola Catholic Church, 84 St and Park Ave. Donations in Eileen's name to Lenox Hill Hospital.
