
        DUSKIN-Samuel A. On April 29 1998, at the age of 101. Born in Philadelphia, Oct. 17, 1896. Served in the Navy in World War I. Taught English in Havana afterwards. Enthusiastic world traveler. President of Estuary Council of Clubs while in his 80's. Active in his Connecticut Square Dance Club until the age of 96. He is survived by his sisters Ann Otis and Lillian Otis, his daughter Phyllis Hirschfeld, step-daughter Joan Blake, 10 grandchildren & 16 great-grandchildren. Loved, cherished and admired by all. Services on Sunday, May 3, 9:45 A.M., at ''Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
