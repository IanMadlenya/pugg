
        SADOW-Irving. Age 89, of Brighton Beach, Brooklyn, died August 21st in Bristol, PA., after a long decline in health and spirits following the death of his beloved companion of 25 years, Beatrice Kalver. He was predeceased by his sisters, Anita Sonberg and Shirley Janos. During World War II, he served as a U.S. Army Officer in Europe. Mr. Sadow led a welltravelled, entertaining life, full of curiousity and picaresque adventures. His gift for anecdotes gave pleasure to others. He is survived by nieces, Diane Tannenbaum, Ellen Janos, and Lynn Sonberg, as well as by nephews, David Janos and Paul Sonberg. He will be remembered in a private gathering.
