
        NAGLE-William G. Of Scarsdale, NY, died on Dec. 30, 2001. Mr. Nagle was a retired Senior Vice President of Citibank. His wife Mary Jane Lowe predeceased him. He is survived by 1 daughter Barbara N. Muench; three sons, William G., Jr., Donald L. and James L.; one brother Alexander C. & 7 grandchildren. One brother Braxton R. Nagle predeceased him. Memorial services will be held at the Hitchcock Presbyterian Church, Scarsdale, New York on Friday, January 4, at 1 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his memory to the Burke Rehabilitation Center White Plains, New York.
