
        PURDY--Thomas L. Jr., at 94, died peacefully at home in Somers, NY on December 22, 2003. He was the sixth generation of his family to be actively involved in community service. He was first president of the North Salem Historical Society, and a WWII Air Force veteran. He had a passion for flying and woodworking. He leaves a son, Thomas III, and a daughter, Ellen Low Webster. The memorial service is scheduled for February 7, 1 PM, St. Luke's Church, Somers. Contributions in his memory may be made to St. Luke's Church, Somers, NY 10589 or North Salem Library Bldg Fund, North Salem, NY 10560.
