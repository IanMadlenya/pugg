
        INSELMAN--Rae. Died on May 31, 2004 in Tucson, Arizona at age 88. Beloved wife of Alexander Inselman, who predeceased her in 1987. Loving mother of Dr. Laura Inselman and Dr. Barbara Inselman-Temkin, mother-inlaw of Dr. Lawrence Temkin, and grandmother of Joshua Temkin & Deborah Temkin. Born in Brooklyn, New York, housewife, insurance broker, and community leader. Funeral services to be held on June 2, 2004 in Tucson. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Thoracic Society/American Lung Association.
