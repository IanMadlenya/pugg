
        HURLEY-Sr. Margaret Mary. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, Convent of Mary the Queen, died Tuesday, September 19, 2000. Survived by her nephews and niece. Reposing at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark Street, Yonkers, NY on Wednesday 1-8 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday 9:30 A.M. at the convent. Interment at St. Joseph's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Ave, Bx, NY 10471.
