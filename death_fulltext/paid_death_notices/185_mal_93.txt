
        WASSERMAN-Lew. The Jerusalem Foundation mourns the passing of Lew Wasserman, a devoted friend of Jerusalem whose generous spirit will live on in the projects he helped to bring to life. His support for the Jerusalem Cinematheque expressed his deep commitment to the cultural life that has enriched all Jerusalemites. Our sincerest condolences to Edie and his entire family. Teddy Kollek, Int'l Chairman Ruth Cheshin, Int'l President Sandra Rubin, Exec Director Kenneth Bialkin, Acting Chair
