
        GATES--John W. Died Tuesday, July 4, 2006 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Frances; father of Nancy S. Gates Gerber, John W. Gates, III, and Norman Thomas Gates; grandfather of Anne, Thomas and Jenny Gerber; great grandfather of Kaitlyn, Lauren and Julia Gerber. Born in Memphis, TN, August 16, 1910, he graduated from Princeton University in 1931 and retired from the Chase Bank in 1976. A memorial service will be held at St. John's Church in Cold Spring Harbor, NY at noon on Wednesday, July 12, 2006. In lieu of flowers the family suggests donations in his memory to the St. John's Hunger and Outreach Committee, POB 166, Cold Spring Harbor, NY 11724 or Habitat for Humanity of Suffolk County, 643 Middle County Road., Middle Island, NY 11953.
