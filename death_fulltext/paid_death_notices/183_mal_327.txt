
        FRANKLIN-Max. Of Stamford, CT, passed away at the age of 85, on April 27, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Florence. Devoted father of Marcia and Robert. Cherished grandfather of Lauren, David and Steven, and caring brother of Mildred Katz. He lived every day of his life with zest and enthusiasm. He will be remembered as a loving and generous man with a sharp wit and a keen intellect. We will deeply miss his stories and his laughter. Services Monday, 1:30 PM, at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel,'' 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
