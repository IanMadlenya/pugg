
        PALESTIN--Beatrice, on February 21, 2005 at age 99. Devoted wife of the late Ira J. Palestin. Beloved sister of Leona Adler. Cherished aunt of Hazel Rutstein, Meryle Evans, Felice Pester, Jonathan Adler, Louise Burton, Dr. Jonathan Pincus, Dr. Matthew Pincus and the late Adrienne Sullivan and adoring great-aunt. Dear cousin of Yseult Freilicher. Cared for with devotion and tenderness by Mark Lee-Chong and Lucy Campo. She will be sorely missed by the innumerable people whose lives she touched. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Wednesday, 11:30 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        PALESTIN--Beatrice. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its member Beatrice Palestin. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred D. Youngwood,Pres.
