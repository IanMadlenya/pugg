
        HENKEL--Joan, 80, died June 1 at her Rutherford, NJ home. She graduated from the University of Pittsburgh. She taught kindergarten in Pennsylvania and New Jersey. Her husband Robert, owner of the Secaucus Home News, predeceases her. Her children Peter, Christopher, Heidi Leyh and Gretchen, five grandchildren and six great-grandchildren survive her.
