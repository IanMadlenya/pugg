
        JAMES - Christopher Leopole. The James family deeply mourns the passing of Mr. Christopher Leopole James, who resided at Maypen Clarendon, Jamaica, West Indies. He was passed on Wednesday evening, June 26, 2002 at 7PM. Mr. James was born in Rock River, Jamaica in 1922. At the time of his death, Mr. James was 80 years old. He is survived by his wife Verona James and six children. Services will be held at Spanish Town, Jamaica. Memorial contributions and condolences may be made in honor of Mr. James to Mr. Donald James, 97 Kennedy Drive, West Haverstraw, New York 10993.
