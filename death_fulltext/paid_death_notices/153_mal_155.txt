
        STERNS-William S. Jr., of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, age 88, on October 11, 1999. Beloved husband of 60 years of Harriette Beeson Sterns, father of Heidi Merrill Luxner and William S. Sterns, III, grandfather of Reed, Sara, William IV, Stefan and Olivia; W.W. II recipient of the United States Legion of Merit and, from the Belgium government, the Croix de Guerre, avec palme; indefatigable golfer and lover of all people, young and old, especially his family. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, October 14, 1999 at 3:00 P.M. at The Palms Presbyterian Church, Ponte Vedra Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made in his memory to the charity of one's choice or to the American Heart Association.
