
        FRANK-Charles W., MD., 100 years old. Pre-deceased in 1975 by his beloved wife of fifty years, Mabel Reinschreiber Frank, and in 1998 by Mae Frank. Father of Ruth Deutsch and Janet Fox (Alan). Grandfather of Kenneth and Nancy Deutsch, Martin and David Kalt. Great-grandfather, brother of Sofia and the late Sam, Ben, Lou and Sol. Bronx physician, 1925-1970. He loved and nurtured us, and taught us the meaning of family values and traditions. He left us memories to cherish for a lifetime. His humor, wisdom and love will guide and protect us and we will miss him forever.
