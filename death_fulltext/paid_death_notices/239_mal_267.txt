
        BRODERICK--Harold Thomas, 84, of Wilton, Connecticut died Tuesday, December 19th at Norwalk Hospital. Mr. Broderick, also known as Hal, was born in New York City on June 2, 1922. He attended Regis High School and received a BA from Columbia University on scholarship. He served in the Navy during World War II. Hal and his wife Catherine have been married for 61 years. Hal was president of U.S. Ringbinder Corp. in Massachusetts, and was a sales executive for various companies in New York, Washington and London. He and Catherine traveled extensively throughout the world. Hal was an avid painter. He is survived by his wife and three sons, Jeff, Beau, and Chris. Visitation will be Wednesday, December 27 from 4:00 to 9:00 PM at Collins Funeral Home, Norwalk, CT. Services will be 11:30 AM Thursday at Our Lady of Fatima Church in Wilton, CT. Burial will be follow at Woodlawn Cemetery.
