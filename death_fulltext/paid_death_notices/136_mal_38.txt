
        LELAND-Robert H. On April 30, 1998. Formerly of NYC. Past District Deputy Grand Master, First Manhattan District of the Masons. Retired officer and legal counsel for Chase Bag Co. (Union Camp Corp.), Greenwich, CT. Devoted husband of Janine (nee Krupski). Loving father of James G. and Douglas R. Fond brother of Marilyn Kimmel. Cherished grandfather of three. Friends may call Tuesday, 12-2 PM, 6-9 PM, at Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 125 Hillside Ave (at City Line), New Hyde Park, NY. Masonic service Tuesday, 8 PM. Funeral service Wednesday, 9:30 AM. Interment Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, NY.
        LELAND-Robert H. Lawyer, scholar, renaissance man. For so many years, an esteemed mentor of Consolidated Lodge No. 31 and the First Manhattan Masonic District. Among his many good works, he contributed greatly to the revitalization of our beloved Long I Grotto. Funeral 8 PM, Tuesday, May 5, 1998, Dalton Funeral Home, 125 Hillside Ave, New Hyde Park, NY. God bless you, Bob. Ivan Obolensky, Monarch Long I Grotto
