
        PARRINO--Dionisio passed away peacefully at the age of 91 on November 7, 2004. He earned his doctorate at NYU in 1954 and pursued an extraordinary career in public and private education. He taught his students until he was 78. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Joan Parrino, his son Mark, daughter-in-law Tamara and sister Alba. His family will receive guests at Farenga Bros. Funeral Home, 920 Allerton Avenue, Bronx, NY on Tuesday from 3-5 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 11 A.M. Holy Rosary Church. Entombment to follow at The Woodlawn Cemetery.
