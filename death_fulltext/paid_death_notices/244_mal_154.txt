
        ABRAM--Michael R., 58, Loving husband, father, son, brother, friend and a sweet caring man. Family, friends, music and solving problems were the most important things in his life. Michael's greatest accomplishment was being a great father and his greatest joy was turning people on to good music. Michael's life was a celebration of all that he cared about. He is survived by his wife Dorothy Siegel, children Samuel and Elizabeth Abram, parents Irv and Blanche Abram, and Sage and Lenore Bassett, brothers David Abram and Tom Trouillot, sisters, Anne Marie Bracken and Rachel Abram Weiner, and his best friend Marvin Rosenberg.
