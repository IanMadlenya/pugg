
        CRARY-Calvert H. Of Kendal at Hanover, NH, died on May 29, 2002. Age 94. He is survived by his wife, Pamela and his children Susan C. Strubel, Caroline C. Nordmann and Calvert D. Crary and by eight grandchildren. His former wife Catherine Snell died in 1974. He was a graduate of Amherst College and Harvard Business School. He had served as Senior Vice President of Legg Mason Wood Walker, Inc. and previously resided in Scarsdale, NY. A memorial service will be held at a date to be announced.
