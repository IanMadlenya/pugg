
        MONTGOMERY-Marion, 74. Of Fair Haven, N.J. died at home October 15, 2000. Born in Brooklyn, N.Y., she lived in Rumson, N.J. and the surrounding area for 60 years and had a residence in Vero Beach, FL. She was an artist, writer, homemaker and, for fifteen years, the owner of the Screen Door. Surviving are her husband of 52 years, John L. Montgomery, Jr., three daughters, Leslie, Jill & Margo Montgomery, two sons-inlaw, Gene Kelly and Jonathan Ward & four grandchildren, Devon and Ryan Kelly, and Matthew and Mackenzie Ward.
