
        MORRIS--Eleanor H. Passed away August 13, 2006. Beloved and adored wife of the late Morton Morris, proud and devoted mother of Steven and Andrew. An outstanding and non-intefering mother-in-law to Jane and Linda. Loving grandmother of Alexandra with whom she had a special bond. A warm and spirited woman who embraced life to its fullest, and enjoyed all that the world had to offer. She had an insatiable curiosity that served her well throughout the 90 years she was with us. Graduate of Tilden High School Class of '32, earned her BA and MSW after raising her children, worked in the social services and charitable professions until the age of 86. She had many friends and was well loved, and will be greatly missed by all who had the good fortune to know her.
