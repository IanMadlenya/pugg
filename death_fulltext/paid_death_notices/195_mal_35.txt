
        BECKER-Godfrey. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our beloved longtime member Godfrey Becker. He is a Bar Mitzvah and Confirmant of Rodeph Sholom as well as part of a third generation family. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his wife Minna, his daughter and our beloved sexton, Gail, and son Alan. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
