
        RESNIK-Sylvia. 93, of Miami, originally from New York, passed away August 19, 2000. Loving wife of the late Irving Resnik; beloved mother of Cynthia Resnik and Marilyn (Felix) Scott; loving grandmother of Neil Scott and Melissa Powell; and proud great-grandmother of Austin and Ethan Powell. A memorial service will be held Monday, August 21, 6:30 PM, at the Baptist Hospital Chapel, 8900 North Kendall Drive, Miami. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to Catholic Hospice, L'Chiam Jewish Hospice Program, 14100 Palmetto Frontage Rd, Suite 370, Miami Lakes, FL 33016. For information, contact All Care Funeral Home.
