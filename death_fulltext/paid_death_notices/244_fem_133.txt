
        LEBOW--Doris (Prowler), New York City, 91, died peacefully May 15, her three daughters, Lynn Nadeau, Joan Wheeler and Cathy Axelson-Berry by her side. Predeceased by brother Stanley Prowler, survived by brother Bob Prowler, seven grandchildren, eight great-grandchildren. Service May 17, Riverside - North Chapel, Great Neck; burial beside late husband Dr. Harry Hertzl Lebow. In lieu of flowers, contributions welcome to support Lucas, the disabled son of a beloved caregiver. Kiss kiss, I love you!
