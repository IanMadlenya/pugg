
        OSBORNE-Philip Barry, 66. On July 18, at his home in Chappaqua, NY. Mr. Osborne, a former assistant managing editor at Reader's Digest, previously had been an editor/writer for Time, CBS, and Business Week. Beloved husband of Frances Mary Paczka, devoted father and grandfather of Leicia Osborne Milano and Morgan Osborne Milano. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to: The ALS Association, 116 John St., New York, NY 10038 or New Castle Historical Society, 100 King Street, Chappaqua, New York 10514.
