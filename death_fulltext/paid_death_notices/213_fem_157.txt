
        McLAUGHLIN -- Anne E. (Curran). Died October 19. Our beloved. Dear Nana, we will miss you forever. Beloved wife of Jack, adoring mother of Suzie and grandmother of Jamie, loving mother-in-law of Ainar Aijala and devoted surrogate mother to Vivian Darling, and beloved sister to Joseph Curran. Anne was a total New Yorker. Raised in SoHo, graduate of Cathedral High School 1945, and Assistant Higher Education Officer at Hunter College. Longtime resident of Stuyvesant Town and Peter Cooper Village. Summered in Rockaway, Long Beach, Spring Lake and Sea Girt. Wintered at Stuart, FL. Loved by so many relatives and friends who enjoyed her sense of humor, style and zest for life. Annie, you were the greatest. Reposing Saturday and Sunday at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass, Monday October 25, 10AM, Epiphany R.C. Church, 22nd St and 2nd Ave. Interment at St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, NY
