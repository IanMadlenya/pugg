
        PETLUCK-Robert F., Dr. Graduate of Fordham, retired Principal of PS 86 of the Bronx, attorney and former NY Times employee. Of Hampton Bays, formerly of the Bronx. Died on February 25, 2000, at the age of 90. He leaves his dear wife of 67 years, Estelle (Rosenthal). He is survived by his son Richard A. and his wife Arlene of Israel. He leaves his two grandchildren, David Petluck and Joyce Pinto, and his great-grandchildren Boaz, Lillian, Daniel and Amos Pinto. A private cremation was conducted by the J. Ronald Scott Funeral Home of Hampton Bays.
