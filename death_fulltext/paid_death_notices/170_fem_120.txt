
        RUBIN-Rose. Age 89, of Allendale, NJ, formerly of Cliffside Park, NJ, on March 15, 2001. A member of Brandeis Womens Book Club. Predeased by her husband of sixty years Samuel Rubin in 1999. Survived by her daughters Judith (Joel) Dorfman of Allendale, NJ and Joan (Irwin) Leib of Silver Spring, MD; five grandchildren Eric, Dana and Craig Dorfman, David and Jennifer Leib and two great-grandchildren Alexa and Noah Dorfman. For information call Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, Paramus, NJ.
