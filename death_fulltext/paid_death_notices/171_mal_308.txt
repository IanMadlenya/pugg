
        JONES-Charles E. We are saddened by the loss of the eloquent and charming Charles E. Jones. We will greatly miss the music, talent and grace of this extraordinary man. He is survived by his wife, Jill Axthelm, his sister, Dorothy Kester, his stepdaughter, Megan and her husband J.B. Brown and his grandchildren Matthew and Mary. He will also be missed by the rest of his loving family and many friends. A memorial visitation will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Wednesday, April 25 and Thursday, April 26th, 7-9 PM. Donations may be made in Chuck's name to Fiorello H. Laguardia H.S. of Music and Art, Alumni and Friends, 100 Amsterdam Avenue, NYC 10023 to provide a college scholarship to a promising clarinetist or to Calvary Hospital Fund, Inc., 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
