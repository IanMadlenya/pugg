
        HEEREY-John J., Reverend. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former parochial vicar of Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Forest Hills, NY, on July 6. Reposing 2 to 4 and 7 to 10 PM Friday, July 9, in Kearns Funeral Home, 61-40 Woodhaven Blvd, Rego Park, NY. Funeral Mass at 10:30 AM Saturday, July 10, at Resurrection-Ascension Church, 61-11 85th St., Rego Park. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
