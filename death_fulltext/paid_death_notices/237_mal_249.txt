
        SILVERSTEIN -- Fred (''Heart''), 88, died October 22, 2006. Most beloved husband, father and grandfather. He was a man of tremendous heart and with great love for his life. He was a source of stability and love to many. In his 80s he wrote and published two mystery novels and could have written more. He had character, humor and enormous loyalty and commitment to those he loved. He will be missed by many, forgotten by none. Family is sitting shiva at family home
