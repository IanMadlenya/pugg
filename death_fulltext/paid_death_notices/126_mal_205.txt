
        ROSNER-Dr. Norman L., DDS. 80, of Tamarac, Florida, passed away on July 10, 1997. Dr. Rosner was born in New York City in 1917. He was a 1938 graduate of NYU and of the Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery in 1942. Dr. Rosner served four and a half years during World War II as a Captain in the army. He practiced in New York City for over 35 years. He was a husband to Roz; a father of Ken, and predeased by his daughter Barbra; a grandfather of Paul, Jed, Season, Jaden, Scott and Kyle; and a great-grandfather of Zoe and Noah.
