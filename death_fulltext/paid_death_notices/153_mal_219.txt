
        DRAGO-Rosario P. (Bud), M.D. On October 16, 1999. Beloved husband of Betty (nee Brisgal) Drago. Loving father of Joseph, Mariann Drago Berliner, Carl, Bud, John, Eleanor Drago-Severson and Paul. Cherished brother of Sylvia Romeo. Loving grandfather of 17 grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Rosario was a Knight of Malta, a graduate of the University of Illinois College and Medical School. He was commanding medical officer at Greenville, SC, A.A.B. He was Chair of St. Francis Hospital in the Bronx as well as St. Clare's Hospital in NYC. He was past president of the Catholic Physician Guild and supervising physician of NYS Office of Disabilities. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held Tuesday, 10:30 AM, at St. Barnabas Church. Entombment following at Woodlawn Cemetery. The family will be present Monday, 2-4 and 7-9, at F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc., 732 Yonkers Ave., Yonkers, NY. Donations can be made to the Joseph and Eleanora Drago Scholarship Fund at St. Barnabas High School, 425 E. 240th St., Bronx, NY 10470.
