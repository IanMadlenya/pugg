
        FALES--Katharine Ladd. The Board of Directors, Clinical and Administrative Staff of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York mourn the loss of Katharine Fales, a beloved Director for 25 years and benefactor of the agency's home care and public health services. Her interest in patient care improved the health and wellbeing of countless New Yorkers. Her commitment to high quality, compassionate health care will be remembered always. We extend our profound sympathy to her family. Douglas D. Broadwater Chairman of the Board Carol Raphael President and CEO
