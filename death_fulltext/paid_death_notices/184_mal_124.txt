
        SHAVIT-Buma. Passed away May 8, 2002, in Tel Aviv, Israel. A prominent industrialist and political figure, former Chairman of ElAl Airlines and the Israel Manufacturers Association; an early leader in the development of the State of Israel Bonds in the United States. Our beloved cousin and devoted family member, a man larger than life, who lived every day to the fullest will be sorely missed by the many who loved and admired him. Caren and Amnon Barness, and family
