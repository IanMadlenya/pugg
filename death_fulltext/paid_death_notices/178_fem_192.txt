
        PHELPS-Eleanor, 94. September 29, of Gouldsboro, PA, formerly of Manhattan. Mother of Lisa Hebo. She made her Broadway debut in 1929; appeared in 24 Broadway shows, tours, radio, daytime dramas, commercials and films. Memorial mass, at Blessed Sacrament Church, 131 West 71st St, Saturday, Dec. 1, at 1:00PM.
