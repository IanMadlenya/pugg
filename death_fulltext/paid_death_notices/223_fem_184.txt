
        MORAKIS--Betty P. Died August 27, 2005 at the age of 69. A courageous seven-and-ahalf-year battle with cancer has finally ended, leaving behind her beloved husband, James, New York, and her three cherished sons, Lee, New York, Todd, Japan, and Jay, New York. Throughout Betty's struggle, her family remained unwavering, by her side. She is also survived by her brother, James Dimitrion, New Jersey, and 3 sisters, Genny Thomas, Pennsylvania, Thomasine Flangos, Arizona, and Georgia Dimitrion, New Jersey. Wake services will be held Tuesday, August 30, 5 PM to 8:30 PM at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., New York, NY. Funeral services to be held the following morning, Wednesday, August 31, 10 AM at the Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Cathedral, 319 East 74 St., New York, NY. Burial services to be Thursday morning, September 1st at the Morakis family grave, Mount Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge, MA, preceded by a viewing at the Faggas Funeral Home, 551 Mount Auburn Street, Watertown, MA, from 9:30 AM to 10:30 AM. Donations in her memory can be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Research Foundation at 646-227-3546. This world was a better place while Betty lived, as her kindness, generosity and loving nature will remain an example to all those who knew her, forever.
