
        WATSON-Nancy Dingman ''Dingy.'' Of Truro, MA, and formerly of Putney, VT, died on June 28, 2001, in Truro, MA. Her parents were Dr. Norman and Ann Bauer Dingman of Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ. Her grandparents were Dr. James and Jeannette Beveridge Dingman of Spring Valley, NY. Burial and graveside services in Spring Valley, NY, will be held at a later date.
