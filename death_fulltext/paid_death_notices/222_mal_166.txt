
        GRAZIANI--Charles P., DDS. 93, of Santa Barbara, CA., formerly of Brooklyn, N.Y. and Plandome, L.I. Passed away on July 7, 2005. Beloved husband of Anne and the devoted and loving father of Clara Graziani Bronson, Audrey Graziani Brown and Charles Graziani, Jr. He was the cherished grandfather of eight grandchildren and six great grandchildren. He is survived also by his brother Thomas E. Graziani, DDS, and sister Lee Kralicek. His life was dedicated to his wife, his family and his profession. He enjoyed life fully and was a source of inspiration and a model of courage to his family. Services were held at Santa Barbara Cemetery on Tuesday, July 12.
