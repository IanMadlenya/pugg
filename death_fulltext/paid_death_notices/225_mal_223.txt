
        KORN--Peter Harold. Former Sr. Vice President of GVA Williams Real Estate Company, 73, of New York and Boca Raton, FL. died October 21, 2005. Beloved husband of Jane Small Korn, son of the late Jeanne and Charles Korn, loved brother of Claire and Richard Yaffa, uncle to Cathy and Richard Yaffa Jr. and Jennifer & Robert Yaffa. A unique, volatile, handsome and charming person who was loved by so many who knew him. Service at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., NY, NY, Sunday, October 23 at 10AM. In lieu of flowers please send to Hospice of Palm Beach County, Florida.
