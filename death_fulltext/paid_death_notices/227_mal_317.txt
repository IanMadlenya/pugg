
        GROVER -- Henry Cushing (Hank). On November 28, 2005 in Houston at 78 years. Served in the Texas State Legislature, 1961-1972. Gubernatorial candidate. Prior to that, a public high school teacher and veteran. Condolences to his wife, children and grandchildren from his four sisters, nineteen nieces and nephews whose lives he greatly influenced. Loved and revered.
