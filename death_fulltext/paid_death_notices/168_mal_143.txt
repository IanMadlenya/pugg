
        JACOBSON-Willard J. On behalf of the Teachers College community I want to express my deepest sympathies to the family of Professor Emeritus of Science Education, Willard J. Jacobson. Professor Jacobson taught science education at Teachers College for 38 years, specializing in elementary and junior high school instruction, and mentoring hundreds of doctoral students from all over the world. His dedication to science education is nationally recognized and his colleagues and friends mourn his death. He was an exceptional man. Arthur E. Levine, President Teachers College, Columbia Univ
