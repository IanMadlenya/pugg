
        FELSENFELD--Murray. On March 16, 2006 at age 81. All who've met him left smiling. Loving husband of 58 years to Sue. Wonderful father to Arthur and Terry Felsenfeld, Linda and Michael Mellon, grandfather to Tara and Brian Zone, Lauren Felsenfeld and her fiance, Chad Altman, and Tracey Mellon and great grandfather to Alexa Madison Zone. Dear brother of Hanna Kornfeld and the late Solomon Felsenfeld and Lillian Israel. Humorist, poet and legendary retailer who made Murray's Pottery a Kings Highway, Brooklyn landmark. Services Sunday 1 PM at I.J. Morris Inc., 46 Greenwich Street, Hempstead, LI. Interment to follow at the New Montefiore Cemetery.
