
        ROSENFELD-Estelle B. Passed away gently on June 14, 1997, to join her cherished husband, Dr. Sidney Rosenfeld. She was the loving mother of Alice Perez and mother-in-law of Joseph, and loving mother of Dr. Michael Rosenfeld and mother-in-law of Mary. Adoring grandmother of Peter London and his wife Bonni, adoring grandmother of John London. Friend to her devoted companion Kan Tai Chien. Survived by brother Sheldon Blum (Eunice) and loving niece Lucille Fink. Interment was private.
