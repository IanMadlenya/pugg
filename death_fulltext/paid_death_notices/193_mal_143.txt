Memorial service Old First Church, Main Street, Huntington, NY March 1 at 11 A.M. Contributions to VNS Hospice, 505 Main St., Northport, NY or Herreshoff Marine Museum, Bristol, RI. For further information please contact the A.L. Jacobsen Funeral Home, Huntington Station, New York.
