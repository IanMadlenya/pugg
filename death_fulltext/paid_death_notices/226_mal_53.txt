
        ELKIN--Milton, M.D. The Department of Nuclear Medicine at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and Montefiore Medical Center notes with great sadness the death of Dr. Milton Elkin. Dr. Elkin was founding Chairman of the Department of Radiology of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine at the Bronx Municipal Hospital Center. He was a superb teacher, scholar and diagnostician, the recipient of numerous awards for his contributions to radiology. He will be remembered fondly and missed greatly. Our condolences to Dr. Elkin's children, grandchildren, and all of the members of his family.
