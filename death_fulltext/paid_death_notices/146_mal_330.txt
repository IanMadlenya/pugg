
        FRIEDMAN - Dr. Mark Jay. Temple Beth Torah in Westbury, L.I., mourns the loss of an esteemed member of our congregation, Dr. Mark Jay Friedman, on March 13, 1999. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Ellen, children Robyn and Matt, parents Eleanor and Arthur, sister Lainie and brother Bruce. May God comfort all the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Michael Katz Diane Wohl, President
