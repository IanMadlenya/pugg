
        JACKSON--Robert. Internationally known muralist and painter, died August 17, at home in Livingston, NY at 72. Painted murals in the U.S., England, Canada, Netherlands, France, Italy, including rooms in the Metropolitan Museum of Art, U.S. Department of State, White House, Blair House. His paintings have been displayed in one man shows in New York, Los Angeles, Toronto. Survived by his sister, Eleanor Warnock, of Newmarket, Ontario, three nephews and a niece, and longtime companion, Frederick Corke.
