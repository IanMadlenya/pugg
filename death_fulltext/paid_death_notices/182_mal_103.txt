
        COHN-Michael, 69, of Scarsdale, on March 3, 2002. He is survived by his wife Suzanne Hazen Cohn, his sons Thomas (Victoria Craven) and Peter, his sister Rita Wienshienk, and three grandchildren. A graduate of University of Pennsylvania and Harvard Law School, Mr. Cohn was a literary agent. Memorial service March 10, 2002, 3 p.m., Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave., Scarsdale, NY. Donations may be made to the Scarsdale Public Library.
        COHN-Michael. Your warmth, wit and wisdom will be sorely missed. With fond remembrance, Holly and Fred Lehrer
