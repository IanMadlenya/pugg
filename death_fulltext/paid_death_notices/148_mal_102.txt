
        CAPECI-Theodore J., M.D. Of Scarsdale, NY on May 8, 1999. Husband of Patricia A. Lohse. Father of Susan Capeci, Ellen Fithian. Grandfather of four. Former husband of Margaret Hyatt. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 2:00 and 4:00 and 7:00 and 9:00 Wednesday and Thursday. Mass of Christian burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, on Friday, May 14 at 10:00 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Lawrence Hospital, Bronxville, New York 10708.
        CAPECI-Theodore J., M.D./d. The Governors, Medical Staff & employees of Lawrence Hospital are deeply saddened by the death of our longtime Attending Physician, former President of the Medical Staff and Medical Board and former Medical Director. He served the Hospital as physician leader, scholar, steward and friend. His unparalleled commitment, concern and competence did much to shape the future of Lawrence Hospital and he will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family.
