
        FERTEL--Ann (nee Portnoy) passed on November 27th at the age of 95 following a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Irving Fertel, devoted mother of Bernice and the late Bernard Metz, Diane Fertel, Maxine and Elliot Simon. Cherished Nana of Charlene Metz, Lewis and Caroline Lilla, Beth Lilla and Dominic Piscitelli, Gerry and JoanMarie delle Badia, and Joy Simon. Adored greatgrandmother of JoAnn, Anthony, Richard, David, Amanda, Jessica, Alexa, Juliana and Bianca. Caring sister of Hy Portnoy. Her legacy is the strength her family will find in love and sharing. Services will be held on Sunday 2PM at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd at 76th Rd, Forest Hills. Donations in her memory may be made to Lenox Hill Hospital.
