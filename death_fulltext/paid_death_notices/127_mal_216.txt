
        KUHBACH-A. Gerdes and his wife Muriel of Chatham, New Jersey, on August 19-20, 1997. Parents of Ellen K. Lucas, Robert G., and Peter D. Kuhbach. They are also survived by 5 grandchildren: Scott, Jonathan, Allison, Courtney and Emily. A memorial service will be held at the Stanley Congregational Church, 94 Fairmount Avenue, Chatham on Saturday, August 23, 1997 at 11:00AM. Friends may call at the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, 345 Main St. (Rt. 124), Chatham on Friday from 6 to 8 P.M. Those who wish may contribute in their memory to either the American Cancer Society or the American Heart Association.
