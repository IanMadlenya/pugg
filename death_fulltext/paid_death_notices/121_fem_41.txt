
        LAZAR-Helen B. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of NY mourn the passing of Helen B. Lazar, beloved wife of the late Jack Lazar. Through the Lazar Foundation, of which Mrs. Lazar was a trustee and her son, William, is a trustee, the family's generous gifts improve the lives of numerous New Yorkers and Jews worldwide. To her children, grandchildren, and the entire family, we send our deepest condolences. May her memory be for a blessing. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
