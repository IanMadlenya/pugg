
        FASTENBERG-Arthur. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Arthur Fastenberg, a cherished friend who perpetuated the philanthropic traditions of his exceptionally generous family through his devotion to Jewish causes. He will be deeply missed. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife, Eve; his daughter and son-in-law, Francine and Irwin Levine; his daughter and son-in-law, Elaine and Leon Smith; his grandchildren, Lori, Karen, Daniel, Lauren,Susan, and Jessica; and his great-grandchildren, David, Benjamin, & Julia. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
