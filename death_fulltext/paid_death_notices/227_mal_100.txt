
        GILON--Arie. New York University and the School of Continuing and Professional Studies mourn the loss of Arie Gilon, Professor Emeritus of Foreign Languages, on Nov. 29, 2005. Prof. Gilon was a member of the NYU Community for the past 45 years and was recognized for both teaching excellence and outstanding service. He was a veteran of the Korean War. Professor Gilon will be greatly missed and we offer our condolences to his wife, Lea, his son, Michael, and his grandsons Daniel and David.
