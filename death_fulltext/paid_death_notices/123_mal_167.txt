
        SHERMAN-Steven, after a valiant battle with a fatal disease, cancer, is finally at peace. A proficient Judgment Clerk of the Civil Court and well informed intellectual human being. Survived by his mother Lorraine, who lovingly cared for him to the very end. Dear brother of Howard and brother-inlaw of Fran. Adored uncle of Michael. Loving nephew of Felice and Morty. Dear cousin of Eric, Tod, Jackee, Marty and Lynn. Services were held Sunday at ''Parkside'' Memorial Chapel.
