
        LIEB-Maron H. Age 94 of Easton CT., beloved wife of Charles H. Lieb, died Saturday, July 4, in her home. Born in Salem, MA, Maron Lieb was a graduate of the Mt. Sinai School of Nursing and was employed as a nurse with both the Flowers Hospital & Doctors Hospital in Manhattan and she was also a Home Nurse in Easton. Friends are invited to attend a graveside service at Aspetuck Cemetery on Tuesday, July 7, at 11:00AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the American Red Cross, 640 Clinton Ave., Bridgeport, CT 06605-1704. Shaughnessey Funeral home, 50 Reef Road, Fairfield for information.
