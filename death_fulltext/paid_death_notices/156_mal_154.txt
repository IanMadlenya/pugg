
        BERGMAN-Milton Sheridan. Of Delray Beach, FL, died in his home on January 8, 2000. He was 85 years old. Mr. Bergman is survived by his wife Barbara Bergman, and was the husband of the late Norma Horowitz Bergman. Mr. Bergman is also survived by his four children, Michael S. Bergman, William J. Bergman, Hope Bergman Brick and Lisa Bergman Walker. He leaves eight grandchildren. Graveside services Monday, January 10, at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, NY. Family requests contributions can be made to the Scleroderma Foundation in his memroy.
