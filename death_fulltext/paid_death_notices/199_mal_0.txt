
        BALASNY--Raymond L. Of Boca Raton, Florida, New York City and Wildwood, New Jersey. Died July 31, 2003. Well known collector of 20th Century American Art and President of Polychrome Lithography Co., Inc. of New York City. Graduate Phi Beta Kappa Rutgers University, 1942. Husband of Barbara. Also survived by sister Florence Balasny - Barnes, loving children Susan, Jill and Neal. Highly esteemed by many nieces and nephews. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him. Services and interment private. >>AD#
