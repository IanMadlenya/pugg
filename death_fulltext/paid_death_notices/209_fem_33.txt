
        GRECO--Maria. Casting director, MGA School of Acting, coach, mentor, who worked tirelessly with grace, love and discipline helping each actor achieve his or her highest potential as she did also with her family and friends. Her strength and spirit lives on in everyone she touched. She died at NY Presbyterian Medical Center May 16, 2004 of complications from open heart surgery. A memorial service will be held June 14 at 5:30PM at the Marble Collegiate Church on Fifth Ave. and 29 St. In lieu of flowers kindly send contributions to the Marble Collegiate Church in Maria's memory.
