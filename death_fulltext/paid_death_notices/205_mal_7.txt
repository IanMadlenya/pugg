
        HARTH--Robert. The Board of Trustees and staff of Carnegie Hall are deeply saddened by the sudden passing of Robert Harth, our beloved Executive and Artistic Director, and dear friend. The Carnegie Hall family extends their heartfelt condolences to his parents, Sidney Harth and Teresa Testa Harth; sister Laura Harth Rodriguez, brother - in - law Francisco Rodriguez and nephew Alexander H. Rodriguez; companion Stacey Buck; son Jeffrey Curtis and former wife Melanie Harth. We also extend our sympathies to his extended family of close friends in the artistic community and music world. Sanford I. Weill, Chairman Klaus Jacobs, Vice Chairman Arthur Zankel, Vice Chairman
