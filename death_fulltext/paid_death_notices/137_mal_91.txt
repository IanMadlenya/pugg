
        SCHWANN-William. The Musicians, Trustees and Staff of the Marlboro Music School and Festival mourn the loss of their devoted friend and extend deepest sympathy to his family. A trustee for over thirty years, William Schwann shared his enthusiasm and love for Marlboro with friends throughout the country and also made an important contribution as chairman of Marlboro's Recording and Radio Broadcast Committee. He will be missed by us all. Stephen Stamas Chairman, Board of Trustees Marlboro Music School & Festival
