
        MICHAELIAN-Mary A. Died on October 16, 1997 at the age of 97. Beloved aunt of George L. King and great aunt of George L. King III, Pamela Parmenter, the late Victoria King, Jason Sumner, William T. Sumner, Katherine Sumner Bacon, and their children. Her parents John and Tevrez Michaelian emigrated from Armenia in 1896. Donations can be made to The Armenian Evangelical Church of New York, 152 East 34th Street, New York, New York 10016.
