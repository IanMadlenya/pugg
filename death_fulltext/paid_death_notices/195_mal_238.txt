
        SHAVEL-Irwin A. On April 20, age 88, a courageous and charitable man, died at home with his best friend and beloved wife, Bobbie, of 59 years by his side after an illness of mesotheliomia. Devoted father to Martha, Laurie and Nancy. Loving fatherin-law to John Jessop and Richard Gabel. Adored grandfather of Sara, Alexis and Julia. Dear brother to Howard, Lawrence and Henry. Funeral service, 11:30 AM, April 24, at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
