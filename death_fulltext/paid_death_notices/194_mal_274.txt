
        DENMARK-Harold J., D.D.S. 82, formerly of Freeport and Manhasset. Loving husband of the late Jeannette. Adored father of Gerald (Lynn) and Leslie (John) Storch, and the late Wendy Denmark. Beloved grandfather of Jason and Jonathan Denmark, Laura Russell and Rebecca Storch. Devoted brother to Stanley Denmark and Pearl Slusky. He served his country with pride during W.W. II as an Army Staff Sergeant and as a Captain in the Air Force during the Korean conflict. Devoted to his patients, friends, family and Jewish faith. Services private.
