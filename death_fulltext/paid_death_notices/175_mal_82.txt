
        JOYNT-Paul J. Unexpectedly on August 6, 2001. Mr. Joynt was an actor who appeared in various plays, movies and television. He is survived by two brothers William and James and seven nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions may be made in Paul's name to ''The Actor's Fund of America,'' 729 7th Avenue, NY, NY 10019.
