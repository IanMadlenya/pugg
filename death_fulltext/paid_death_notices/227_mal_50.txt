
        MOLYNEUX--Peter E. Of New York City, formerly of Old Greenwich, died November 21, 2005. He was 45 years old. Mr. Molyneux attended Greenwich High School and Drew University and was a member of the Economics Honor Society. He was Chief Financial Officer of Northbridge Capital Holding Company for 20 years. Peter is survived by his mother, Donna Gorham Molyneux of West Chester, PA, his father Edward Molyneux of Rhinebeck, NY, a sister Carol Petraglia of Radnor, PA, a nephew Alexander and a niece Katie. Memorial services will be held on December 10 at 10:30AM at First Presbyterian Church, 48 Fifth Avenue, NYC. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Habitat for Humanity.
