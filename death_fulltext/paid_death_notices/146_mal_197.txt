
        FISHER-James J. Jr. Of Jamesburg, NJ, formerly of Paramus, NJ, died March 6, 1999, in his 79th year. Beloved husband of the late Frances (nee Daly), who died in 1991. Loving father of James R. Fisher Sr. and his wife Tracey of Clarksburg, NJ. Adoring grandfather of Lauren Marie Fisher, James R. Fisher Jr., and Margaret Grace Fisher. Devoted brother of Robert of Tarpon Springs, FL. He was an Army Air Force veteran of WWII, receiving the Distinguished Flying Cross. Funeral Mass will be today 10:00 AM at Annunciation RC Church, with interment to follow in George Washington Memorial Park, both in Paramus, NJ. For information contact Feeney Funeral Home, Ridgewood, NJ, 201-444-7650. In lieu of flowers, donations to Children's Aids Foundation, 186 Roosevelt Ave, Newark, NJ 07107 would be appreciated.
