
        WEISSMAN -- Samuel. Died peacefully in his 94th year on February 7, 2004. He will be greatly missed by Miriam, his wife and friend of 58 years, son Paul, daughter-in-law Lourdes, and grandchildren Gabriel and Julian. He is also survived by his brother Ben and sister Gertrude and by many nieces and nephews.
        WEISSMAN -- Samuel. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Samuel Weissman, associated with The Times from August 1935 until retiring in January 1978.
