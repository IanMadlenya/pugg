
        NEWLON-Paul J. The partners, associates and staff of the firm of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison express profound sorrow at the passing of Paul J. Newlon. Paul was our former partner and beloved friend, and will long be remembered for his many contributions. He was a resident of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. We extend our deepest sympathies and condolences to his wife Suzanne, his daughter Joan Radner, his stepchildren Joan Alexander, Geoffrey Dodge and James Mapes Dodge III and to all the other members of his family.
