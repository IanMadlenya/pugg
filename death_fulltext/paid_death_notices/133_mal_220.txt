
        GORDON-Wallace M. Age 69. Formerly of Bay Head, NJ, Leesburg, VA. and New York City. On February 12, 1998, in Harrisville, NH. Graduated Solebury School, Solebury, PA. Attended UVA. Served USNR during Korean War. Survived by his loving wife, the former Margaret Legband, sisters Mary Shulman, Katharine Wilson, brother Theodore Gordon and affectionate nieces and nephews. Memorial service to be announced.
