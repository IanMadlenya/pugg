
        ABNER-Ruth. Age 94 passed away peacefully on June 11, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Morris Abner, cherished mother of Alan and Howard. Adoring grandmother to Susan, Robert, David, Caryn and Leslie. Loving great grandmother of Marielle, Bailey, Harris, Stephanie, Tracy, Melissa and Eric. Her simple tastes, non-judgemental nature and wise counsel were a source of strength to us all. Rest in Peace ''Kind Lady''. Services are 11 A.M. today Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
