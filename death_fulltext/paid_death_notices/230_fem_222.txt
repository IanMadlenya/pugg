
        LEWIN -- Kathryn A. On March 26, 2006, in her 92nd year. Beloved wife of Harvey M. Lewin. Loving mother of Karen (Mark) and Robert (Susan). Adored grandma of Deborah, Alex, Jonathan and Jessica. She was a lifelong New Yorker, rabid Yankee fan, and original fan of Frank Sinatra. Special gratitude to Cynthia Smith, her loving caregiver. Her indomitable spirit will be missed by all who knew her. Service and interment private.
