
        TSAGARIS--Theodore, a resident of Scarsdale, NY died Thursday June 9, 2005 at the age of 79. Ted was a beloved father, grandfather, patriarch of his family and patron of the classics and the arts. He was a graduate of New York University with a Master's in Naval Architecture and Nautical Engineering. As an international shipping executive, he was a recognized and respected ship owner and arbitrator. A faithful and passionate supporter of the church and Archon of the Ecemenical Patriarcate of Constantinople Order of St. Andrew. His love for life inspired all those who knew him. He is survived by his sons Evangelos and his wife Marlena, Constantine and his wife Patricia, George and his wife Demetria Tsagaris, and grandchildren Teo, Christopher, Theodore, Zoe, Maro Alexia and Artemis. Funeral services will be conducted Tuesday, 11 AM at the Church of Our Savior 2195 Westchester Avenue East Rye, NY. Interment will follow at Kensico Cemetery Valhalla, NY. The Family will receive friends at the BallardDurand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Avenue at South Broadway, White Plains, NY Monday from 2-9pm. In lieu of flowers, those who wish may send memorial contributions to Metro Athlete Refocus Organization (M.A.R.O.), the family's charity for inner city youths, or the Archbishop Iakovos Library Endowment Fund c/o of Church of Our Savior 2195 Westchester Avenue East, Rye, NY 10580, or the Cancer Research Fund, 675 Third Avenue, New York, NY 10017
