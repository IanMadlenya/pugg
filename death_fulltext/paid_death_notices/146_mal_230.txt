
        DEELY-James Sedgwick, 77, of Palm Beach, FL, Stockbridge, MA, and Marion, MA, and formerly of New York City, died Monday, March 8, 1999, of complications following a traffic accident. He is survived by his wife, Barbara Ginn Reece Deely of Palm Beach, FL, and Marion, MA; two daughters, Catharine Burton Deely of Los Angeles, CA, and Pamela Deely Van de Loo of Albany, NY; two brothers, John Deely, Jr., of Stockbridge, MA, and Martin Deely of Lee, MA; a sister, Ann Hahn, of Fairfield, CT, and numerous nieces and nephews. Mr. Deely's first wife, Patricia Johnson Deely, died in 1996. A Funeral Mass will be held at 11 a.m. on Monday, March 15, 1999, at St. Joseph's Church of Stockbridge, MA. Additional memorial services in New York City and Palm Beach will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to Williams College Museum of Art, Williamstown, MA 01267. For more information, contact Quattlebaum-Holleman-Burse Funeral Home, West Palm Beach, FL, at 561-832-5171.
