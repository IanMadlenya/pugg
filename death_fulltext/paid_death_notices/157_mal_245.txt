
        STERN-Stanley, 80, died February 20, 2000, after a long & courageous battle with Parkinsons Desease, loving husband of the late Corrine, he is survived by his two sons, Arthur and Robert, two daughtersin-law, Jean and Dawn, and his beloved granddaughter, Madison. In lieu of flowers, the family request that those desiring contribute to the Parkinsons Desease Foundation, Neurological Institute, 710 West 168th Street, New York, New York 10032.
