
        ZUCKER - Joan, of Fort Lee, New Jersey, after a courageous fight against cancer, on January 9, at age 63. Cherished wife of Leonard. devoted mother of Michael and Caren, adoring grandma of Bejamin, Samuel, Mickey and Daniel, and loving aunt. Joan was a owman who defined the concepts of giving and loyalty. She was the ultimate friend and her countless dear friends responded in kind. she was a giver of unconditional love, support, caring, mothering and loyalty, without reservation. She touched everyone she met, and she will be deeply missed. Joan had a love of art, travel, tennis, gambling, people and miracle-ofmiracles, white water rafting. We will carry her shining brightness inside of us forever. Services on Friday, 12:30 PM, promptly at The Riverside, West 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
