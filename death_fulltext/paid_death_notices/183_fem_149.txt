
        KAMINSKY-Beatrice (nee Bezehler). An enduring spirit whose happy marriage, love of family, determination and zest for life carried her through 94 active years-beloved and devoted wife of 68 years, companion, best friend and lover of Lynch (Leon); dear sister of Jean; loving mother of sons Gerry and Marty, and daughtersin-law Jackie and Nancy; loving and proud grandmother of Gary, Jenny, Michael and Missy, their spouses, spouses' families, and many great-grandchildren-she is and will be missed by her family and many friends. Service private.
