
        WELCH-Louise Michel Blinken (b. 1905) died at home in the presence of her family in New York City late on Christmas Day. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, December 29 in Saint Thomas Church, 53rd St. and Fifth Avenue at 11am. She is survived by son Dr. Richard Savington Crampton of Charlottesville, Virginia and daughter Martha Peter Llosa of New York City as well as six grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name or that of her father, writer Meir Blinken, to the YIVO Institute for Jewish Research, 15 West 16th Street, NYC, 10011. (212) 246-6080.
