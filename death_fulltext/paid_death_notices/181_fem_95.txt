
        WALKER-Tomannie T., 80, on February 9, of St. Albans, Queens. Beloved wife of Joseph Walker. Beloved mother of Joseph Jr., Douglass and Julia. Grandmother of Jo, Christopher, Nicholas and Evan. Mother-in-law of Fran and Annette. Longtime member of the League School family of Brooklyn and lifelong resident of Jamaica, Queens. Pioneer in civil rights, economic justice, community organizing and advocacy for the mentally ill. Proud member of the Class of '42 of Queens College. She touched the lives of many and will be sorely missed. Donations to Queens Library, PO Box 175, St. Albans, NY 11412.
