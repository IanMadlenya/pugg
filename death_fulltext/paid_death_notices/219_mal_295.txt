
        MOREAU--Lawrence J., after many valiant battles we have lost our hero. Predeceased by his mother Evelyn and father Lawrence. Survived by his adoring wife Maureen, his mother-in-law Helen Derwin, brother and sister-in-law Frank and Barbara Derwin. Proud uncle of Joanne Derwin, Jamie Bermudez and Christine Derwin. Burial in Naples, Florida. New York memorial service at a later date. Memorial contributions may be sent to The Rogosin Institute, 505 E. 70 St, Room HT 230, NY, NY 10021.
