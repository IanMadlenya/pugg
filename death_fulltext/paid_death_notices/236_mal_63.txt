
        MCGETTIGAN--Edward Thomas, Sr. of Norwalk, CT on September 6, 2006. He was a founding partner, President and Chief Executive Officer of American Transit Insurance Company in Manhattan. Husband of Lorraine McGettigan; father of James, Edward, Susan Carroll and Joan McGettigan. Grandfather of five. Brother of John McGettigan. Friends may call at the Lawrence Funeral Home, Darien, CT on Thursday, September 7 from 1 to 3PM and Friday, September 8 from 2 to 4PM and 6 to 8PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at St. Matthews R.C. Church, Norwalk, Saturday, September 10 at 10AM. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be sent to Fordham Prep., East Fordham Road, Bronx, NY 10458-5175 or Belmont Child Care Association, 2150 Hempstead Turnpike, Belmont Park, Elmont, NY 11003.
