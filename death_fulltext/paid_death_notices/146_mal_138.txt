
        FIELD-Leonard S. Producer of offbeat Broadway theatrical shows, including Brendan Behan's ''The Hostage,'' stage manager for the world tour of ''Porgy and Bess'' and discoverer of numerous talents. Former member of the Board of Playwrights' Horizon. Noted habitue of Sardi's. Survived by his loving wife Virginia Clayburgh Field and two daughters, Ruth Feldberg and Katharine Field, and many grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Memorial service will be private.
