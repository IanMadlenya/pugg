
        RADA--George Andrew. Of Manhattan, on December 8, 2003. Devoted husband of Jacqueline, beloved father of George A. Rada, Jr. and Kevin M. Rada, cherished grandfather of George A. Rada III and Sean Thomas Rada. Dear father-in-law of Kathryn. Former Captain in the U.S. Army, George was a realist painter who worked and exhibited in NYC. Visitation Wednesday 6-9 PM and Thursday 2-4 and 6-9 PM at Krtil Funeral Home, Inc. 1297 First Avenue, NYC. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, 10 AM at St. John the Martyr Church. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory preferred to the Art Students League of NY, 215 W. 57 St, NYC 10019 for the George A. Rada Memorial Award.
