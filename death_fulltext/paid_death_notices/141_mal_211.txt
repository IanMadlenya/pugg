
        WALKER-Herman, 89. Formerly of Waldwick, NJ, September 24, 1998. Preceded in death by his wife Virginia Decker Walker, survived by son, Roger W. Walker, daughter Beverly Walker Gelbert, and grandchildren Michael & Jennifer Walker, Sara & Katherine Gelbert. Walker graduated from Columbia University in 1931, served as Lieutenant in U.S. Army and was employed at ITT Marlow. His family founded the Fairview Cemetery where he served as consultant until his death. An avid reader, artist, lover of wildlife and supporter of various library, law enforcement and youth programs. Memorial service at Waldwick United Methodist Church, October 23, 12PM. Contributions to Friends of the Library, Waldwick Library, 19 East Prospect St., Waldwick, NJ 07463
