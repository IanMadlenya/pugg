
        SAVARD--Sister Judith. A Religious of the Sacred Heart of Mary for 45 years, died May 23rd, at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was Associate Professor of art and art history at Marymount Manhattan College. She is survived by her sister Anne O'Connor of Harbor Springs, MI, and two brothers, Harold of Easton, CT, an Dudley of Fairfield, CT. The wake will be held at Marymount Convent, Tarrytown, Tuesday, May 25, from 2:30 pm, with a prayer service at 7:30 pm. Mass of Christian Burial will be at Marymount Convent, Wednesday, May 26, at 10:30 am.
