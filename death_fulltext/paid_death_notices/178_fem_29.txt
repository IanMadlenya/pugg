
        GAMBARDELLA - Elizabeth B. (nee Corr). Of New York City and Brant Beach, NJ, on November 2, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Anthony P. Gambardella. Loving mother of Angela Lisciotto and Nikles Gambardella. Dear niece of the late Martha and Nikles Tsikourias. Also surviving are four grandchildren. She was an entrepreneur for many years in the gourmet food promotions industry known throughout Manhattan. A Mass of Christian Burial will take place on Tuesday at 9:30 A.M. in St. Michael R.C. Church, Cranford, NJ. Visitation will be on Monday from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. in the Dooley Funeral Home, 218 North Avenue, Cranford, NJ. In lieu of flowers contributions to either Susan Komen Breast Cancer Foundation or to the Cabrini Hospice in the memory of Anthony P. Gambardella would be appreciated.
