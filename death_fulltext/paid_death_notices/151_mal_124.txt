
        KLEINERMAN-Jerome, I., M.D. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Mount Sinai School of Medicine and The Mount Sinai Hospital of Mount Sinai NYU Health note with profound sadness the tragic death of our former colleague, Jerome I. Kleinerman, M.D. Dr. Kleinerman served with distinction as the third Irene Heinz Given and John LaPorte Given Professor and Chairman of the Dept. of Pathology of Mount Sinai School of Medicine, and as Director of Pathology at The Mount Sinai Hospital, from 1978 to 1986. A distinguished expert on pulmonary pathology, he was internationally recognized for his research on environmental lung diseases, and admired for his dedication to patients and students. We extend our sincere sympathy to his three daughters and to the entire family. Helene L. Kaplan, Chair, Mount Sinai School of Medicine Stephen M. Peck, Chair, Mount Sinai NYU Health Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean, Mount Sinai School of Medicine John W. Rowe, M.D., President Mount Sinai NYU Health Barry R. Freedman, President The Mount Sinai Hospital
