
        THOMASES-Fred. Textile industry executive, on August 10, 2000. Loving husband of Florence Josephson Thomases, through 66 years of marriage. Services were held yesterday, August 11, 2000. Contributions in his memory may be made to the following charities: The Solomon Schechter School, 275 McKinley Ave., New Milford, NJ 07646; Jewish Home at Rockleigh, 10 Link Drive, Rockleigh, NJ 07647; and Scholarships & Welfare Fund of Hunter College, 695 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
        THOMASES-Fred. The Jerusalem Foundation notes with sadness the passing of Fred Thomases, a longtime friend of Jerusalem. We extend condolences to all the members of his devoted family. Teddy Kollek, Internat'l Chair Ruth Cheshin, Internat'l President Bill Ackman, Chairman Sandra Rubin, Exec Director
