
        HOUSEMAN-Joan. The Acting Company notes with great sadness the loss of our dear friend, board director and wife of John Houseman, our co - founder. Among her many lasting gifts to the Company was assisting Nicholas Martin's direction of our acclaimed production of The Rivals. We will miss her greatly and send deepest sympathy to Michael and Sebastian. Edgar Lansbury, Chairman Joan Warburg, President Margot Harley, Producing Dir.
