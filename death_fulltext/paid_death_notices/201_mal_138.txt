
        LAW--David Buchanan, 65, died at his home on Friday, October 10th after a 6-month battle with cancer. He is survived by his wife, Jane Cullen Law; their two children; Christopher and Peter; his former wife Kathleen Marion Law and their children Laura Law Ney, Eric Law, Jennifer Law, all of Los Angeles; brother Peter Law of Los Angeles; sisters Susan Hoogs of Tucson, AZ and Diana Palmer of Kinnewick, WA; two grandchildren Chloe and Dillon Ney. Memorial celebration is Monday, October 20th, 6PM at Barge Music, Fulton Ferry Landing, Brooklyn; reception afterwards at the Law residence.
