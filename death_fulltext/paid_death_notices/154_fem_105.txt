
        SYKES-Dorothy Millang. Age 92. Died peacefully at her home in East Hampton on November 12. Devoted wife of the late James. Loving mother of Cordelia Menges, James Sykes Jr. and Martha Murray. Adored grandmother of Dorothy, Jake, Stuart, Ben, Matthew, Sam, Andrew and David. Cherished great-grandmother of Gardner, Serena, Martha, Sophie and Alice. A memorial service will be held Saturday, November 20, at 11 AM, at St. Luke's Church in East Hampton. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the East Hampton Library.
