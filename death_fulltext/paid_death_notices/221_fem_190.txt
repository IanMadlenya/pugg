
        ROSSINI--Midge Bodine. Mrs. Giancarlo ''Midge'' Bodine Rossini nee Bain, June 20, 2005, Chicago. Born October 21, 1923, Wife, mother, artist, hotelier (Oyster Pond Yacht Club, Sint Maarten; Captain's Quarters, Saba; The Habersham at York Inn, Savannah) Member Art Students League; Amatuer Comedy Club. Predeceased by husbands Mr. Earl C. Bodine and Mr. Giancarlo Rossini. Survived by her children Clayton Bodine, CA; Susan Bolea, CT; Paul and Pegeen Bodine, IL; Christopher and Sarah Bodine, UK; and Dawn and Duffy Burdick, NY and grandchildren.
