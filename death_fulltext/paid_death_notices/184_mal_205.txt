
        STRAUSS-Charles S., 88. Of Larchmont, died on May 15 due to complications in the last few months. He was born December 31, 1913 in Haverhill, MA. to Ansel and Helen Strauss. He spent his youth in Pelham, New York. He graduated from Andover in 1931 and Yale in 1935. Charlie served as a First Sergeant in World War II. After the war he worked for M. Lowenstein & Sons until his retirement. Charlie was predeceased by his beloved wife, Margaret Levi Strauss and his loving daughter, Leyae Strauss and his brother John Strauss. He is survived by his loving family: daughter, Barbara Strauss, his son and daughter-inlaw, Chick and Sherry Strauss, his three grandchildren Emily, Hannah and Noah Strauss, and by his brother and sister-in-law, Peter and Barbara Strauss, and their children, John, Eileen and Sally Strauss. A memorial service will be held at Congregation Emanuel of Westchester on Thursday, May 23, at 2 PM, the Temple of which he was a past president. Donations in his memory may be sent to Yale, Andover, Congregation Emanuel of Westchester, or a charity of your choice. Charlie was an avid Quaker Ridge golfer, bridge player, story teller and a caring and supportive friend. We're left with fond memories. He will be missed by all.
