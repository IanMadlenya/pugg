
        GIRARD-Samuel R. At age 90, on September 20. Beloved husband of the late Mimi Girard. Loving father of Andrea Girard and Constance Girard. Cherished grandfather of Jennifer Girard Boron and Andrea Girard Boron. Devoted brother of Ethel Ross and Jeanne Rauch. Mr. Girard was a veteran of WWII and a noted artist in New York City. Service Monday, September 24, 9:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the National Glaucoma Foundation.
