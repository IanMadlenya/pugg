
        QUALEY - Thomas. Renowned floral designer. Owner of Kathleen Graham Flowers in New York with Cynthia; greatly respected in his field. Formerly of Bronx, NY. Died on September 12, 1997, in Boca Raton, FL. Cherished husband of the late Cynthia (nee Zimmerman), dear brother-in-law and adored uncle of many. We shall miss him greatly.
