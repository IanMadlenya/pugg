
        ADAMS-Janet Rutherford. Of Bayside, NY on October 20, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Robert Adams. Devoted mother of Robert, Carol, & Anne Margaret. Cherished mother-in-law of Sharon, Perry, and Andrew. Loving grandmother of Ian, Ciarra, Zachary, Genavieve and Robert. Beloved sister of the late Annie Dockrell and William Sloan. Survived by sister Peggy Gibb of Scotland. Janet led an active life including careers with The United Kingdom Mission to the United Nations and The National Society of Colonial Dames as well as long membership in the British War Veterans of America. She was a dignified and courageous woman who had a love of life. She will be interred in Glasgow, Scotland.
