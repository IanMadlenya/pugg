
        DAVIDSON-Adelaide H., of East Orange, N.J. died December 23, 1997 in Brookline, MA. Wife of the late Dr. Henry A. Davidson, she is survived by her son, Larry and his partner Barbara; her daughter, Ellen and her partner Jim; her granddaughters, Hannah and Aviva; her sister, Lillian Brauman and her loving companion Dr. Alton Bythewood. Contributions in her memory may be made to Oxfam America, the Urban League of Essex County, or Prospect House, East Orange, N.J. Memorial observance will be in NJ in February.
