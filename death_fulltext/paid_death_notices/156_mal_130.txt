
        MICHAUD-Ralph Wm. Age 92. Died Sunday December 13, 1999 at home in Garden City, NY. Beloved husband of Marie Ann and father of Carole McLoughlin of Garden City, NY and Susan Jamieson of Fredericksburg, Va. A Wall Street Securities Analyst for over 50 years. A Vice President of Dean Witter and Paine, Webber, Jackson and Curtis, he was also a past Vice President and Director of The New York Society of Security Analysts, a past Director of the Financial Analysts Federation, member of the Paper Industry Analysts Group, the American Institute of Mining Engineers, The Bankers Club of America, and the author of ''The Investors Handbook; A Guide to Sound Investment Principles and Practice''. He will long be remembered for his honesty, quick wit and intelligence.
