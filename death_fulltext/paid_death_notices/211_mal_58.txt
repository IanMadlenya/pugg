
        HAAS--Henry, 77, of North Woodmere, N.Y., died August 5, 2004. Widower of his beloved Beverly. Cherished companion of Sylvia Kapner. Loving father of Roslyn and Larry Belgin, Marjie and Larry Haas, Nancy and Rick Alovis, and Elyse and Jeffrey Haas. Devoted grandfather of Jason, Michael, Lisa, Samantha, Jake, Jenny, Bari, Sarah and Ethan. He was a man with a huge heart, and of uncommon generosity. Funeral noon today, Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York 11557. Donations to Congregation Ohr Torah, North Woodmere, New York.
