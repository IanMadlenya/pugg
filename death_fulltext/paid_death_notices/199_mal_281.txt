
        ZIMMERMAN--John O, former President of GMAC and Chief Executor of GMAC in the early 1970's, died on August 27th in Stonington, CT where he made his home. He was 94. Mr. Zimmerman joined the General Motors financial staff shortly after his graduation from Dartmouth College Amos Tuck School of Business. His entire business career, aside from a tour of duty in the US Navy, was spent with the GM Financial staff and GMAC. He is survived by his wife Barbara, son Peter and daughter Anne.
