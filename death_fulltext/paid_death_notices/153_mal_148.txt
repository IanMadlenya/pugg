
        JUCOVY-Milton E., M.D. The Officers and Members of The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Institute express their profound sorrow at the death of their respected and beloved colleague and member, Dr. Milton E. Jucovy. He was Training and Supervising Analyst for many years, and contributed significantly to psychoanalysis. Deepest sympathy is extended to his family members. Albert M. Sax, President/Society Eslee Samberg, President/Institute
        JUCOVY-Milton E. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of the beloved father of its veteran teacher and Chairman of the History Dept. in the Rabbi Joseph H. Lookstein Upper School, Dr. Jon Jucovy. We extend heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein, Principal David N. Kahn, Chairman, Board of Trustees
        JUCOVY-Milton E., M.D. On October 10, 1999. Long standing member and past President of the Long Island Psychoanalytic Society, where he will be sadly missed by all his colleagues.
