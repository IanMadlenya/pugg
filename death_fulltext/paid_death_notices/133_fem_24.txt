
        BARIS-Sybil. Of Potomac, MD, died on Sunday, February 1, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Jules C. Baris. Devoted mother of David Baris of Darnstown, MD, and Susan Baris Mace of New York City. Also survived by one grandson. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
