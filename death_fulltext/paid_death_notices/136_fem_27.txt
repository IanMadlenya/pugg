
        KINN-Sonia. The League Treatment Center Board and Staff express their deepest sympathy to the family of Sonia Kinn, Holocaust resistance fighter in the Abramov Group in White Russia, who died on Sunday, May 3rd. Beloved wife, mother, grandmother and friend, Sonia will be deeply missed by all the people she loved and inspired with her bravery. She is survived by her husband Phillip, who also was a Jewish Resistance fighter, her children Gail and Mark, her daughter-in-law Hannah Achtenberg & her granddaughter Sarah Frieda, who gave her joy. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, May 5 at 12:15 P.M., at Brett Schneider Funeral Home, 2576 Flatbush Avenue (near Kings Plaza Shopping Center), Brooklyn, New York.
