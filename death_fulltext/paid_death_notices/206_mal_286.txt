
        GROSS--Jay, 60, loving husband of Louise, loving father of Dov Joseph and Esther. Adored and cherished grandfather of Meir, Rivka Shaindel and Malka Tzivia, brother of Adele Harris and Sheila Firnstein, son of the late Bernard and Jean Gross, beloved by his nieces, nephews and many friends. Jay was a Musmach of Chevron Yeshiva in Jerusalem and a student in Yeshiva Gur Aryeh, Mirrer Yeshiva and the Telshe Yeshiva. He was universally known for his kindness, friendship and his very generous philanthropic nature. He will be sorely missed. Funeral today, 9:30AM, BoulevardRiverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett. Shiva at home in Lawrence until Tuesday morning, March 30.
