
        SHUB--Elizabeth (nee Charney). Died peacefully at home on Friday, June 18, 2004. Beloved sister of the late Dr. William Charney, aunt of Miriam Charney and Didi Charney, great-aunt of Evan Charney Maltby and Jacob William Charney-Beck. Award-winning editor, author and translator of numerous children's books. She will be greatly missed. Memorial service Tuesday, June 22, 2:30 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the New York Public Library.
