
        JOYCE-Dr. Timothy. Founder and former Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Mediamark Research Inc. died on Saturday, September 13. Dr. Joyce was internationally prominent in media and market research for nearly four decades. He developed Target Group Index, a survey system for measuring consumer behavior of media audiences which is still widely in use today. Dr. Joyce is survived by his wife, Helen, a son, Nicholas, a daughter, Rachel Frosh, and a grandson, Alexander Frosh. Memorial Services are being held at the McGraw-Hill Auditorium, 1221 Avenue of the Americas, 2nd Floor, New York, at 4:00 P.M., on September 25th. Memorial contributions may be made to the United Network for Organ Sharing, 1110 Boulders Parkway, Richmond, VA. 23225. The officers and employees of Mediamark will truly miss Timothy and extend their deepest condolences to the members of his family.
        JOYCE-Timothy, Ph.D. The officers and employees of Roper Starch Worldwide mourn the passing of our Vice-Chairman, Dr. Timothy Joyce on Saturday, September 13. He was a true pioneer in the media/market research field and we will long remember his unparalleled wit, keen intelligence and loyal devotion to his friends and colleagues throughout the world. The research industry and we, in particular, will miss him dearly. Our deepest sympathies to his wife Helen, his son Nicholas, and daughter Rachel Frosh. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, September 25, at the McGraw-Hill Auditorium, 1221 Avenue of the Americas at 4 P.M.
