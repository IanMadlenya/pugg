
        OSTROW-Sidney, died January 1,2000. Beloved husband of Rose, loving father of Linda and Bruce Lawrence, Joan and David Gottlieb, idolized grandfather of Rand and Tracy Lawrence, Nicky and Jordon Gottlieb. Dear brother of Dorothy Ochser and Pearl Gumora as well as the late Irving Ostrow and Bea Wilner. He was a former executive in the textile industry before his retirement. Funeral services will be held at 10 am, Wednesday, January 5th at BoulevardRiverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, N.Y.
