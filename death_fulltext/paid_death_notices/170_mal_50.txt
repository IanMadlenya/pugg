
        KARLIN-Arthur C. Died February 23 in Boca Raton, FL. He was 75. Mr. Karlin was Senior Vice President in charge of real property at E. F. Hutton from 1983 to 1987. He also served on the Board of Mellon Participating Mortgage Trust and was a member of the Friars Club. Mr. Karlin served with the Marines during World War II. A City College graduate, he resided in Chestnut Ridge and Manhattan before moving to Florida in 1998. Survivors include his wife Sonia, son Richard of Delmar, NY, two daughters, Lisa Dennis of South Orange, NJ and Nora Farrell of Orlando, FL.; a sister, Blanca Pearson of Miami, Florida, and eight grandchildren.
