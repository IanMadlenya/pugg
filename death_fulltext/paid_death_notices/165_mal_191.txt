
        MARTINEAU-Cyril Francis, 92. Died in Atlanta, GA, October 4, 2000, at his son's home. Retired Advertising Manager of Shell Oil Co., USA. Longtime resident of Tuxedo Park, New York. Born in London, March 13, 1908, the son of Cyril Edgar Martineau and Muriel Delano Robbins. Cousin of Franklin Delano Roosevelt. Educated at Harrow School and University College, Oxford, where he was a Rowing ''Blue'' and stroke of the Oxford crew, 1930. Served in WWII as Major, Royal Canadian Artillery and Chief Instructor in Gunnery, Canadian Army. Member Tuxedo Club and Leander Club (Oxford). An avid oarsman, lawn and real tennis player, golfer and skier. Predeceased by his wife, the former Margery Nan Sample, his brother, Warren Delano and his sister, Ann Jean M. de Cazenove Aschan. Survived by sons Neal Delano and Warren Douglas, six grandchildren, seven great grandchildren. Memorial service at 2PM, October 20 at St. Mary's-inTuxedo, Tuxedo Park, N.Y. 10987.
