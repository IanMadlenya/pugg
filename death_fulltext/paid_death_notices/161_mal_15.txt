
        GROVES-Sidney on May 31, 2000 of Armonk, NY formally of Pleasantville. Mr. Groves was born in Brooklyn, NY on January 28, 1922 the son of Abraham and Bella Govshewitz. He was a 1946 graduate of Brooklyn College. Mr. Groves taught Business at Mohawk Community College. He was an active member of Tempel Beth El in Chappaqua, NY. His first wife, Barbara Ann (Elered) died in 1969. He is survived by his wife Bernice (Lichenstein) Groves. His two sons, Peter & Michael Groves and his sister Claira Shaw. Funeral services Friday (today) 12 noon at Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla. Donations to the American Cancer Society in his memory would be appreciated.
        GROVES-Sidney. The Congregational family of Temple Beth El of Northern Westchester notes with profound sorrow the death of Sidney Groves, beloved husband of Bernice Groves, father of Michael and Peter. We share their grief, along with their entire family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Chaim Stern, Rabbi David Thomas, Rabbi Dana Anesi, Cantor Stephen J. Adler, President
