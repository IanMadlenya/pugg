
        PFLUEGER-Edward M. The members of the Board of Directors of 61 East 72nd Street Corp mourn the passing of their colleague, friend and neighbor whose years of dedicated service earned him the respect and affectionate regard of all who worked with him. His fellow directors express their sympathy to his wife Kathleen Pflueger. Harvey L. Parker William L. Mack Arturo H.P. Ramos Leon David Black
