
        COHEN-Samuel G. It is with great sorrow the family of Samuel G. Cohen of Pleasantville, NY announces his passing on November 22nd, 2000. The beloved husband of Barbara Brafman Cohen. The loving father of Jonathan, Daniel & Steven. Cherished grandfather of Alexander, Bethany and Myles. He will never be forgotten. Brother of Alan, Jerry, Helen and Judy. Donations in his memory may be made to Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021, or Phelps Hospice, Phelps Memorial Hospital Center, North Broadway, Sleepy Hollow, New York 10591.
