
        YORK -- Harriet Glover, passed away on Wednesday, December 10, 2003 at Santa Rita Care Center in Green Valley, AZ. Mrs. York was formerly of Palm Beach, FL, New York City and Easthampton, NY. She was raised in Palm Beach, then lived for almost forty years in New York City. She lived for several years in Easthampton immediately before moving to Green Valley, AZ. She is predeceased by her husband Gordon, her sister Clara Glover, her son Donald and her parents. She is survived by her loving daughter Pamela and her granddaughter Sarah. There will be a memorial service in New York in the Spring.
