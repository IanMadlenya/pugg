
        COOPER--Dr. Eugene. The Long Island Jewish Medical Center Staff Society mourns the loss of our respected colleague, Dr. Eugene Cooper. Dr. Cooper has been on staff at the LIJ Medical Center for over 40 years. Dr. Cooper was a resident in the LIJ Department of Internal Medicine from 1964 to 1966, completed his fellowship at SUNY Downstate from 1967 to 1968 and practiced as a nephrologist and internist. Funeral services will be held on Friday, July 29 at 11 A.M. at the Sunset Chapel in Manhasset, L.I. Randall S. Feingold, M.D. President Angelo Reppucci, M.D. Vice-President Gerald Zahtz, M.D., Secretary Alan Freedman, M.D. Treasurer
