
        RUDD-Irving, 82. Internationally known sports publicist passed away on June 2, 2000. Beloved and devoted husband for 57 years of Gertrude; A truly special father to Susan and Philip Cohen and Nanci and Ronald Marmer; Adored Grandfather of Jodi and Pete Sabal, Allison Marmer and Davina and Tobias Cohen; and especially proud Great-Grandfather of Zoe Anna Sabal. The love and affection he gave to his family, friends and colleages in the sports world is legendary. He will be missed by all. Funeral arrangements incomplete.
