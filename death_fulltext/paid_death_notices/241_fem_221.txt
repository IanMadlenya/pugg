
        ELPHAND--Shirley, died February 23rd, 2007, after a long illness. Beloved wife of Louis for 62 years. Loving mother to Phyllis Van Dyne, Sandy Wolstein and Philip Elphand. Mother-in-law to Martin Van Dyne, Melanie Elphand and Lewis Wolstein. Grandmother to Scott, Matthew, Josh, Michael, Kathy and Maggie. Great-grandmother to four great-grandchildren. Loving aunt of Gary, Rhona, Malcolm and Philip. She was a graduate of New York University, a member of Hadassah, Women's American Ort and a breast cancer survivor.
