
        SULLIVAN - Adele Woodhouse Erb. Formerly of Scarsdale, New York. Died April 22, 1999. Past President General NSDAR. Survived by her daughter Nancy E. Thorne, two granddaughters, Elizabeth T. Gillespie and Susan T. Hackel, two great-grandchildren, Devon T. Gillespie and Katarina T. Hackel and a nephew J. David Tregurtha. Interment will be private with a memorial service to be announced. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Tamassee DAR School Inc., PO Box 8, Tamassee, S.C. 29686.
