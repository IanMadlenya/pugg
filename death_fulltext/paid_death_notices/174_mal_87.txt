
        KERN-Christopher James. Of Chestnut Hill, MA, and NYC, died suddenly after a fatal hit-and-run accident, July 4, in New York City, at age 21. Precious son of Anne (Fallon) and Harold George Kern. Wonderful brother of Gregory and Elizabeth. Godson of Mark Lawless and Gail Audet. Much loved by caring aunts and uncles, cousins and friends. Chris had happy times at many schools, including Natick and Brookline Public, Park, Belmont Hill, BB&N class of '99. Sophomore at Hamilton College, classes at NYU. Chris had fond memories of all teammates and coaches, including the Natick Comets, The South Shore Kings, Boston Junior Terriers, Park, Belmont Hill, BB&N and Hamilton. Most recently he was working as a paralegal for the New York City Law Department. Funeral from the William J. Gormley Funeral Home, 2055 Centre St, West Roxbury, on Monday, July 9, at 10:30 AM, followed by a Funeral Mass in St. Lawrence Church, 774 Boylston St, Chestnut Hill, at 11:30 AM. Relatives and friends invited. Visiting hours Sunday, 1-4 and 6-9 PM. In lieu of flowers send remembrances to the Christopher James Kern Scholarship Fund, at BB&N, c/o Joe Gill, Gerry's Landing Rd, Cambridge, MA 02138.
