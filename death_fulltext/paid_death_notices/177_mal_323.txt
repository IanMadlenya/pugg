
        WEISS-Joseph. The National Officers of EMUNAH of America mourn the passing of Joseph Weiss, beloved husband of our esteemed Honorary National President, Renee Weiss. For the past 50 years, Joe was a devoted partner to his wife Renee in building EMUNAH, both in the United States and in Israel. He will be sorely missed by all who were priviliged to know him. We extend our deepest condolences to his children Jerry and Roberta Epstein, Howard and Rita Weiss, and Neil and Amy Weiss, grandchildren, great grandchildren, and to his brother David. Marcia Genuth, National President Carol Sufian, Executive Director
