
        BEHR--Randall. Opera Conductor. The operatic and musical community mourn the untimely passing of conductor Randall Behr. An inspired artist, he spoke the language of music with unusual eloquence and grace. Those that had the privilege of working with him marveled at his vast knowledge, keen insight, and detailed approach. He loved music and those who made it with passion. Randall will be remembered for more than his genius; he was a good man. A great friend and mentor, a life cut short, Randall Behr lives on in the many people that were changed by his special presence. We honor him for winning life.
