
        ASHMAN-Eleanor (nee Wyle). Beloved wife of the late Dr. Hyman Ashman, beloved cousin, dear friend and patient. Mrs. Ashman attended the University of Rochester in the 1930's. She served as Executive Director of The Jewish Welfare Society. She then headed the Resettlement Program for European refugees at The Federation Of Jewish Charities in Atlanta and worked for The Jewish Welfare Fund during its annual fundraising drive. For over 30 years, she worked for The American Jewish Committee in the Community Services Dept. Mrs. Ashman voluteered for many years for The League For The Hard of Hearing, she served as friend of the Chamber Music Society at Lincoln Center for over 25 years. Mrs. Ashman will be sorely missed.
