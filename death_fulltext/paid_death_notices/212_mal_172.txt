
        TASHKER--Frank I. Passed away September 8, 2004 after several weeks of declining health in Mountain View, CA. Brother of the late Bea Kahn and Edna Erdheim. Loving father of Michael Tashker of Palo Alto, CA and proud grandfather of Jessica and Sara. Frank ably represented pubs and restaurants in New York for over 50 years. Have a good meal and a drink in his memory.
