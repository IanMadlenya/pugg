
        BUSH-Sara. A great lady. Wife of Jacob S. Bush. Daughter of Anna Cohen and Israel Katz, she is survived by her children and their spouse, Nan Bush and Bruce Weber and Nancy and Ronald Bush, her sister, Beth Levine, her daughter, Anna Thomson and her grandchildren, Meghan and Jamie Bush. The guiding light for her entire family whose passing brings great sorrow. Service at Temple Beth El, Patchogue, L.I., 1 PM, Tuesday, March 18. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests a contribution to your favorite charity.
