
        SHORE-Carol Tilles. Age 77. Loving wife and better half of Allan for 58 years; beloved mother of Allan Shore Jr., William Shore, and Marylu Simon & devoted motherin-law of Jill Shore; cherished grandmother of Jennifer, Beth, Chris, Michael and Julie; greatgrandmother of three and sister of the late Roy Tilles Jr. Her compassion and zest for life touched all who knew her. Formerly of Scarsdale, N.Y., resident of Palm Beach, Florida for 25 years. Active volunteer for the Helen and Harry Gray Cancer Institute at Good Samaritan Hospital, Hospice of Palm Beach County and the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Funeral service will be at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave, Friday April 30 at 10AM. Visiting hours Thursday from 2-4 and 7-9. Burial at Woodlawn Cemetery, N.Y. For further information call Frank E. Campbell at 212-288-3500. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Helen and Harry Gray Cancer Institute at Good Samaritan Hospital, 1309 North Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, Florida 33401.
