
        LINZ-Andrew M., DDS. The officers and board of directors of the New York County Dental Society are saddened by the passing of Andrew Linz. His contributions to the profession were numerous and he served the Society as president in 1974 with distinction. His counsel will be missed. Mark A. Mintzer, President Donna Rumberger, Pres Elect Robert Raiber, Vice President Jack Hirsch, Secretary Matthew J. Neary, Treasurer Elliott Moskowitz, Immediate Past President LINZ-Andrew M., DDS. The Fellows of the New York Academy of Dentistry extend their deepest sympathy to the family of Past President Andrew Linz. His leadership made a significant contribution to interprofessional relations. A dear friend of the Academy, whose exceptional talents as an administrator, teacher and mentor will truly be missed.
