
        SARON--Robert Bernard. Family and friends mourn the passing of Robert Bernard Saron, born October 26, 1924, died August 17, 2003. He will be deeply missed by son Clifford, daughter-in-law Barbara Bogatin, companion Mildred Reiner and his beloved grandchildren, Gabriel and Rayna. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to United Nations Childrens' Fund. Memorial will be held this Saturday at Bailey Farms, in Ossining at 5:30PM. (914-941-0411).
