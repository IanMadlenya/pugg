
        JUTKOWITZ--J. David, Esq. Of Rye, on February 14, 2006. Born in Yonkers 1911 to Jacob and Rose (Weiss) Jutkowitz. Mr. Jutkowitz was a First Lieutenant in the Quarter Master Corps during WWII. Husband of the late Bernice, father of Linda Jutkowitz Meyers and the late John G. Jutkowitz, grandfather of Karen Meyers Arrese (husband Gregory), Hope Meyers Bond (husband Mogolodi), great-grandfather of of Julia, Isabella, Grace and Maya. Predeceased by siblings Adolph Jutkowitz and Rose Palester. Service Thursday, 1:30 P.M. at the Jewish Community Center of Harrison. Interment Mount Hope Cemetery. Donations to a charity of your choice. For more info contact Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809.
