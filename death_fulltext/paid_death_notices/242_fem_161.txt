
        McGOWAN--Vivian P. of Riverdale, NY, on March 17th. Wife of the late William J. McGowan, devoted mother of Marianne, Elizabeth, and William J. Loving grandmother of Emily, Nicholas, Peter and Timothy Prokesch and Andrew, Kara and Paulina McGowan. Daughter of the late Julius F. and Julia F. Pitassy. Sister of the late Reginald F. and Caesar L. Pitassy, and of Helen P. York of Brewster, NY. A graduate of the College of Mt. St. Vincent and Fordham Law School, she was a partner in the firm of McGowan & McGowan. Formerly with the Coorporation Counsel of the City of New York, she was an attorney with the Supreme Court of the State of New York, for more than 30 years, most recently as a principal appellate court attorney in the Appellate Term First Department, and was a past president of the Bronx County Bar Association. Funeral Saturday, March 24th, at 11:00 am, at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, 10 W. Stevens Avenue, Hawthorne, NY 10532. Contributions may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461.
