
        FREYDBERG--Norman. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Norman Freydberg. Norman, and his late wife, Dorothy, had been members since 1937. Norman served on the Board of Governors from 1962 to 1968. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to his family. Earle Yaffa, President Dale Winston, Secretary
