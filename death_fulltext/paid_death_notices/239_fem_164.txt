
        SLOTE--Tisha Kunst, 90, on December 26, 2006. Devoted wife for over 55 years of the late Edwin M. Slote, bankruptcy lawyer. Beloved mother of Wendy Kleinbaum and Michael Slote. Adoring grandmother of Cressida and Nathaniel Slote and Kimberly Kleinbaum. Cherished and loving aunt and great-aunt, friend, confidant and advisor to so many. A human rights advocate who worked on behalf of children. She was instrumental in a program that in the 1950s and 1960s brought private schools in Connecticut and bright minority children together with scholarships. She was also publisher of UN News in the 1950s and the representative of the Girl Scouts of the USA to the United Nations. Funeral at Frank E. Campbell Thursday at 10am. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to The Girl Scouts of the United States of America, 420 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10018.
