
        HUNT-Robert E. Great grandson of Walter A. Hunt, the inventor of the Safety Pin, Robert E. Hunt, 91, of Summit, N.J. died on May 9, 2001. During the second World War, his company, National Foil, converted from an aluminum foil packaging company to create radar jamming foil to support the troops in Europe: active at Central Presbyterian Church in Summit, N.J.: he served as Trustee, on the session and as the Chairman of Central Presbyterian's Educational Building Campaign in the mid60's. He also served as Fundraising Chairman for the Summit Area Red Cross and as the New York Area Alumni Recruiting Chairman for Northwestern University. Always an avid gardener, Bob became an orchid enthusiast later in life serving the North Jersey Orchid Society and as President of the Eastern Orchid Congress. He became an Orchid Judge, participating in shows across the Eastern U.S. as well as Thailand and South Africa. A memorial service will be held on Monday, May 14, 2001 at 2PM at Central Presbyterian Church, Summit, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations in his name may be made to Central Presbyterian Church, 70 Maple St., Summit, NJ 07901 or to the charity of the donor's choice.
