
        MICHAEL--Ivan A. Born November 8, 1924, died February 21, 2004. He is survived by his daughters Bess J. Michael, Leslie H. Lowe, and son Edward H. Lowe and his brothers Earl, Miles and Rex. A gathering in his memory will be held Friday, February 27, 4-8:30 PM at Benta's Funeral Home, 630 St. Nicholas Ave. Services will be held Saturday, February 28, 11:30 AM, St. James Presbyterian Church, 409 W. 141st St. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: The Schomberg Center, Studio Museum of Harlem, Dance Theater of Harlem.
