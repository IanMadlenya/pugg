
        DELOACHE-Natalie, in her 89th year, after a brief illness. Sister of the late, beloved Lillian Lawrence. Natalie will be remembered for her love of art, beauty and animals, and her incredible ability to ''amuse and amaze'', which stayed with her until the very end. Followed into eternity by her constant companion, Sheeba. In accordance with her wishes, there will be no service or memorial. May she rest in peace. Susie
