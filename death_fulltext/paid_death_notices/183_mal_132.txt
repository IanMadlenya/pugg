
        FISHER-Charles W. Age 84. Died in Sarasota, FL. March 26, 2002. Former television producer/director at Benton & Bowles, Inc., longtime editor of Gruber's Hagerstown Almanack. W.W. II veteran with the 9th Infantry Division in the Normandy landings. Survived by his wife of 60 years, Mary W. Fisher, and five children, Linda Eveland, Penny Vigna, Charles, Jr., Anthony, and Lisa Brown. Also survived by twelve grandchildren. Graveside service is at Arlington Cemetery, Drexel Hills, PA., April 26th, 11:00 AM.
