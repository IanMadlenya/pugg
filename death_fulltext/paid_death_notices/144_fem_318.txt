
        TEETER-J. Sarah (Friedman), 28, of Franklin, Mass and Cresskill, NJ. Deeply loved and loving wife of Joshua. Cherished daughter of Elizabeth Friedman. She fought and overcame so many road blocks placed in her path by a terrible disease with courage and humor. Sarah's concern for others was reflected in her work with the Cresskill Volunteer Ambulance Squad and in her nursing career. A fine musician, she was the melody of our lives. Services will be held at ''Wien & Wien'', Englewood, New Jersey, Sunday, January 31, at 11am. In lieu of flowers please send donations to: L. A. M. Foundation, 1010 South Beacon Hills Drive, Cincinatti, Ohio 45241 or sign up as an organ donor.
