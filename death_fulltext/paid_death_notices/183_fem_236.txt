
        de CLAIRVILLE - Gwendolyn Gwynne. Age 87, of Manasota Key, Florida, Westwood, MA., and formerly of LLoyd Harbor, NY, died peacefully on April 23, 2002 in Sarasota, FL. Beloved wife of the late Raymond de Clairville, mother of Wendy Kemper of NY and Clauie Lauren and Sandy Rath of Sarasota. She is survived by 10 grandchildren and 6 great-grandchildren. Memorial service will be at 1 PM, May 11, at St. John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor, NY. Donations in her name may be made to Miss Hall's School, PO Box 1166, Pittsfield, MA 01202-1166.
