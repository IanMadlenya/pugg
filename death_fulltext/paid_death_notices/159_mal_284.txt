
        PIKE-William W., 96, psychoanalyst and a veteran of Abraham Lincoln Brigade (VALB). Husband of 58 years of Edith (deceased); father of Peri and Steven (Paula); grandfather; brother; uncle; greatuncle. His dedication to his profession and humanitarian causes will be remembered by his family and friends. Contributions in his memory to ALBA/VALB, 799 Broadway, New York, New York 10003.
