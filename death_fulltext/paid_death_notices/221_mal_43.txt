
        SALTZER--Daniel K., 95, passed away on May 29, 2005. He was the devoted husband of the late Pearl Saltzer and is survived by his beloved wife of 29 years, Lillian Saltzer. He is also survived by his adoring children, Linda Saltzer Janow and Jeffrey Lewis Saltzer, as well as grandchildren, Pepper Janow Fateman, Paul Saltzer, Ginger Janow, and Brooke Saltzer, and great-grandchild, Evan Saltzer. Daniel was a practicing NYC CPA and attorney until the age of 85.
