
        TAYLOR-Peter Burr. On February 14, 1998 in Boulder, CO. Devoted husband of Jeanne. Loving father of Susan T. Windward of Glendale, CA., and Anne Taylor of Anchorage, AK. Proud grandfather of Shane and Taylor Wallace. Brother of Walter Jennings Taylor of Busby, MT. and Aspen, CO., Jean Taylor Johnson of Essex, CT. and the late Henry Stillman Taylor. A graduate of Saint Paul's School, he attended Dartmouth College and served in P.T. boats in WWII. Memorials in his name may be sent to the University of Colorado, Athletic Dept., Boulder, CO 80302.
