
        ROWE--Pearl (nee Perlman) of Warwick, NY passed quietly on November 3 at the age of 89. She is survived by her husband Marcos of 52 years, devoted daughter Carole, beloved sons Donald and Paul, sister Zelda, nephew Alan, daughter-in-law Lisa, and grandchildren Stefan, Elena, and Emily. Her thoughtfulness and humanity were an inspiration to all.
