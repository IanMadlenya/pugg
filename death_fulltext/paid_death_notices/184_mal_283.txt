
        WITCHEL-Jess. On May 22, 2002. Died peacefully after a long battle with Alzheimer's. An active and dedicated teacher, coach and administrator in NYC high schools and at CCNY, Jess' contribution to his colleagues, students and public education was inestimable. He leaves his loving wife, Ruth, his son Carl, his daughter-in-law Lucie Rodrigue, his grandsons Alexandre and Simone RodrigueWitchel as well as family and many friends. Donations in his memory can be made to the Alzheimer's Association. Services Sunday 11:15 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment to follow at Mt. Judah Cemetery, Glendale, NY
