
        DUNNER--Eva. Died peacefully, her children by her side in Groton, Massachusetts on April 17, 2004. Born to Ernestine Bauer in 1916, raised in Germany until leaving for Palestine in 1932. After marriage to Hans Ruben, she married film producer/director Lasar Dunner in 1944 to whom she was married until his death in 1994. She lived in Israel for over 20 years before immigrating to the US in 1958. Eva was a self-educated intellectual with an unconventional spirit, love of books, travel and a hilarious sense of humor. She will live on in her children, Yaela, Naomi and Ilana, her eight grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. naomi@testwiz.com
