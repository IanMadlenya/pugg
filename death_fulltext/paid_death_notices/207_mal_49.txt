
        TARAS--John. Distinguished ballet master and choreographer died peacefully on April 2, 2004, in New York, and the ballet world mourns his passing. Survived by his wife Helene Sadowska and stepdaughter Anne Stephan. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, on Tuesday, April 6, 25 and 7-9 pm. The funeral service will take place at 11:30 am, on Wednesday, April 7, at Frank E. Campbell.
