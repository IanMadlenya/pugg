
        MCCORMACK--Robert M. 74, of Normandy Beach, NJ, formerly of Short Hills, NJ on Sunday August 14, 2005. Retired Senior Vice President of Paine Webber of NYC and lifelong Cocoa Broker. Husband for 50 years to Eileen Nolan McCormack, father of Eileen M. Harte, Mark McCormack, Justin McCormack, Catherine M. Totman, Mary M. Eisenhardt, and the late Robert J. McCormack. Brother of Dr. Joseph McCormack, Francis X and John McCormack, he is also survived by 16 grandchildren. Funeral from Bradley-Brough Funeral Home, 535 Springfield Avenue, Summit on Thursday August 18th at 10:15 A.M. Funeral Mass in St. Rose of Lima Church, Short Hills at 11:15 A.M. Relatives and friends are invited to attend. Friends may call Wednesday 3-7 P.M. only. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to Habitat for Humanity of Naples Florida or the American Cancer Society.
