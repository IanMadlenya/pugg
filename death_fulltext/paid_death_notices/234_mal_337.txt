
        HOBLER--Edward Windsor, 89, on July 23, 2006, in Evanston, Illinois. Won many National Senior Squash championships. Devoted to Princeton, Class of '39. Son of Benton & Bowles Founder, Atherton Hobler. Brother of Herbert W. , '4 4 of Princeton, and the late Virginia Hobler Redpath and Wells A. Hobler, '41. At the end, his exemplary lifetime of fitness in many sports could not defeat pneumonia. Our loving Dad has left us too soon. Dave, Windsor, Anne, Ruth
