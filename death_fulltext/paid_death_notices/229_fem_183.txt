
        STRONGIN--Estelle. 94 years old, died peacefully of heart failure, at her home on February 19, 2006. The widow of Dr. Harry Strongin, Estelle was the beloved over protective and hands-on mother of Anne Navasky and Landey Strongin, and very beloved and endlessly worried grandmother of Bruno, Miri and Jenny Navasky. One of the first woman brokers on Wall Street, Estelle was working and vitally engaged as a Senior Vice-President at Smith Barney until the day she died. Her life will be celebrated by her family and friends at the home of Anne and Victor Navasky. The family asks that in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to breast cancer research.
