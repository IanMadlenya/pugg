
        OPPENHEIMER-Donald R., Sr., 86, on July 22, 1998 in Reading, PA. at the home of his son with whom he was visiting. Former member and Trustee of Union Temple Brooklyn, NY. Survived by his wife Miriam (London) Oppenheimer of Weston, FL, formerly of 70 Minna Street, Brooklyn, New York. Also survived by two sons: D. Robert, Jr of Reading, PA and John D. of Aptos, CA; a sister, Trudy, of Hempstead, L.I. and a niece, Mary Ann Fishbein, Syracuse, New York. Private Memorial Services. Donations may be made to Union Temple, Brooklyn, New York.
