
        RIESS-Marion, 90, on October 5, 2002. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Chester Riess. Cherished mother of Claudia Wasserman and Jonathan Riess, grandmother of Eric Wasserman, Lise Luftschein, and Christopher Morgan-Riess, great grandmother of Emily and Brian Wasserman, and Danielle and Gabrielle Luftschein. For 25 years, until her retirement in 1980, Marion headed the circulation department of the Library of Long Island University's Brooklyn Center. She will be dearly missed and remembered always by those touched by her love, wisdom and charm. A private memorial service will be held at the Green-Wood Cemetery in Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Hospice of Cincinnati, 4310 Cooper Road, Cincinnati, OH 45242
