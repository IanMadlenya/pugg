
        NUSSBAUM-Samuel. Of Monsey, New York, widowed, father of Jack Nussbaum and Meir Nussbaum. Beloved grandfather and great-grandfather. Mr. Nussbaum was involved in numerous philanthropic organizations. He was the founder and president of Kupat Ezra of Monsey. He was also the founder of the Monsey Food Coop. Services 11 AM today at Community Synagogue, 89 W. Maple Ave., Monsey, NY.
