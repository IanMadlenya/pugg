
        FEINBERG-Mary-Ellen (nee Fertel), beloved wife, sister, mother & grandmother, died the morning of October 23. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Harvey Feinberg, her sisters, Myra Kaplan and Judith Davilman, her children, Monica and Jeffrey Cohen and Jessica and Kenneth Lesnik, and grandchildren, Danielle and Frances Cohen and Sam and Amanda Lesnik. She touched the lives of all who knew her. A great woman. She will be sorely missed. Services on Wednesday, at 11:15 A.M., at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Visiting hours Tuesday, 4-6 PM.
        FEINBERG-Mary-Ellen. Congregation Rodeph Sholom notes with sorrow the passing of our longtime member, Mary-Ellen Feinberg. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her husband, Dr. Harvey Feinberg, and their daughters, Monica (Jeffrey) Cohen and Jessica (Kenneth) Lesnik, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
