
        McCABE-John S., M.D., 66. Retired Chief of Thoracic Surgery and past Chairman of the Division of Surgery of Washington Hospital, Washington, PA. Died on June 1st, 2001 in Vero Beach, FL. Born in NYC, Dr. McCabe built a successful thoracic and cardiovascular practice in southwestern Pennsylvania before retiring to Vero Beach. Dr. McCabe served in the US Navy during the Viet Nam era. He was a graduate of Brooklyn Prep, LeMoyne College and NYU Medical School. He is survived by his wife, Marian; his son, Andrew, of Washington, DC; a daughter, Elaine, of Vero Beach; a sister, Terese Cerni, of Scarsdale, NY; & a brother, Stephen, of Springfield, NJ and Vero Beach. A memorial mass and funeral will be held in Manhattan at St. Vincent Ferrer, 869 Lexington Ave., on Friday, June 22nd, at 10 AM. Memorial donations in Dr. McCabe's name may be made to the Religious of Jesus and Mary-Haiti Fund, Provincial Offices, 3706 Rhode Island Avenue, Mount Rainier, MD. 20712
