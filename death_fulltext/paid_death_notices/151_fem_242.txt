
        BITROS-Mara R. On August 27, 1999, at age 47. Beloved wife of James D. Bitros. Loving sister of Dennis B. McGrath and sister-inlaw of Elizabeth A. Buckley. Cherished aunt of Daniel S. McGrath, Amy S. McGrath and Felix Buckley-Jones. Adored stepmother of Nicole Libby. Caring owner of pet dogs Casey, Murphy and the late Kelley. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Jean Baptiste Church, Lexington Ave. at 76 St., Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Humane Society of New York, 305 East 59 St., NYC 10022.
        BITROS-Mara. The Central Park Saturday Morning Regulars mourn the sudden and tragic loss of Mara Bitros. She was the cherished wife of our esteemed softball teammate and sparkplug, Jim Bitros. Jimmy, our thoughts and prayers are with you.
