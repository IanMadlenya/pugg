
        GOLDSTEIN-Sylvia. Former Corporate Vice President of Boosey & Hawkes, distinguished attorney, uniquely gifted music publishing colleague, and champion of contemporary composers and their music. Sylvia's integrity, generosity, and personal warmth were hallmarks of her distinguished life and her 60 year career at B & H. We mourn the loss of our dear friend and send condolences to her family. Jennifer Bilfield, General Mgr. Boosey & Hawkes, Inc.
