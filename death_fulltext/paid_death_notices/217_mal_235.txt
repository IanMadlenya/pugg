
        GREGG--Thomas, a long time resident of Topeka, Kansas died peacefully February 15 at the age of 80. A man of tremendous warmth and perpetual good humor, he was the loving husband of Ann. He is also survived by Gail of New York City, Judith Gregg Peters of Long Beach, CA, Tyler of San Diego, Gordon and Andrew of Seattle and seven grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Boys and Girls Club of Topeka, 550 SE 27th Street, Topeka, Kansas 66605.
