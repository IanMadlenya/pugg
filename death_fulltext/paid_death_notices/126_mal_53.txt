
        HEDBERG-Dr. Francis G. Of Huntington, NY on July 3, 1997. Dr. Hedberg had a private dental practice in Manhattan for over 35 years and served as team dentist for the NY Giants during the 1970's. Beloved husband of Arlene Dreher. Loving father of Thomas, Jerilyn, Eric and Jennifer. Dear step-father of Dawn. Cherished brother of Eugene and Carol. Also survived by four grandsons. Memorial Mass Saturday, August 9, 1997, 11:00 AM, at Saint Patrick's R.C. Church, Huntington. Cremation was private. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made in his memory to the Joe DiMaggio Childrens Hospital Foundtion, 3501 Johnson Street, Hollywood, FL. 33021.
