
        de CASTRO-Ramon. Of Stockbridge, GA passed away March 6, 1999. He was 91 years of age and born in New York City. Funeral Mass was held Monday, March 7, 1999 from St. Pius X Catholic Church in Conyers, GA. Mr. de Castro was a graduate of Manhattan College class of 1931 and a member of New York 7th Regiment. He donated 120 acres of land in South Rockdale County Georgia to be maintained as the Ramon and Marley de Castro Nature Park. He is survived by brother Robert de Castro, Brookhaven, Georgia, and nieces and nephews. For information contact Harry White Funeral Home, Conyers, GA, 770-483-7216.
