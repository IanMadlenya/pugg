
        RICHARDS--Lloyd. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, artists and staff of the Eugene O'Neill Theater Center, we extend our sincere condolences to the family of Lloyd Richards who served as Artistic Director of the O'Neill's National Playwrights Conference for more than three decades. One of the most important influences in the American Theater, guiding a generation of America's most prominent playwrights, Lloyd Richards will be greatly missed. The O'Neill is honored to continue his legacy. Amy Sullivan, Exec. Dir.
