
        ARPS-Ruth. The partners, associates and support staff of the law firm of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP and Affiliates mourn the loss of our beloved friend, Ruth Arps. Mrs. Arps, the widow of one of the firm's founding partners, Leslie H. Arps, will be fondly remembered for her caring nature, unwavering good cheer and invaluable contributions to the culture and success of the firm and for her tireless work on behalf of the Legal Aid Society and other charities. She will be deeply missed. Our thoughts and prayers are with her family. A memorial service will be held today, August 1, 2002, from 2 to 4PM in Heritage Hall at Seabury Retirement Community, 200 Seabury Dr in Bloomfield, CT.
