
        BILLINGS-Marion. 84, of Briarcliff Manor, on October 16, 1999. She was the former personal manager for the late Sergio Franchi, the Italian tenor. Also worked for the William Morris Talent Theatrical Agency in NYC. Survived by one son, Gary Billings, and one daughter, Bonnie Billings, and longtime companion Edward Risman. Donations to the ALS Foundation.
