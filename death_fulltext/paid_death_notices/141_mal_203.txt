
        HALLISEY-James V. The Board of Trustees of Marymount School and the entire School community mourn the death of our dear friend and former Trustee. A man of integrity, he shared his legal expertise, strong leadership and wisdom with the School. He will be fondly remembered for his love of Marymount and for his generous support. Our deepest sympathies to alumna Tamara Hallisey-Hatfield '76, James V. Hallisey, Jr. and his entire family.
