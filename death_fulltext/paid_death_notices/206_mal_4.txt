
        KREILING -- Frederick C. Ph.D. 81, Professor Emeritus, History of Science, Polytechnic University of New York, on February 29, 2004. He is survived by his wife Jean, a brother George Kreiling of Mattituck, NY, two daughters from a previous marriage, Deborah Kreiling of Hackensack, NJ, and Beth Tambor of Manhattan, a stepson William Lester of Howell, NJ, and two grandchildren, Megan and Lauren Ramsey of Brooklyn. His daughter Susan Ramsey died in 2002. Private memorial service will be held.
