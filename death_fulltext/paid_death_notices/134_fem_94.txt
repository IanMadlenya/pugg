
        GRUBER-Trude. The Division of Social Work & Behavorial Science of the Department of Community and Preventive Medicine of The Mount Sinai School of Medicine, and the Social Work Department of The Mount Sinai Hospital note with deep sadness the passing of our valued colleague, Trude Gruber. Ms. Gruber joined the staff of The Mount Sinai Hospital Department of Social Work in 1970 and was appointed to the faculty of Mount Sinai School of Medicine in 1979. An expert in, and gifted teacher of, family treatment and short-term psychotherapy, she imparted valuable knowledge to both our staff and numerous other healthcare professionals. Her extraordinary talent and generous spirit enriched us all and she will be sorely missed. Gary Rosenberg, Ph.D. Edith J. Baerwald Professor Community and Preventive Medicine Chair, Division of Social Work and Behavioral Science Mount Sinai School of Medicine Susan Blumenfield, D.S.W. Director Department of Social Work The Mount Sinai Hospital
        GRUBER-Trude. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague, Trude Gruber, who was associated with Mount Sinai for nearly three decades. A respected expert in family counseling and short-term psychotherapy, Ms. Gruber joined the staff of the Department of Social Work of The Mount Sinai Hospital in 1970 and was appointed in 1979 to the faculty of Mount Sinai School of Medicine's Department of Community and Preventive Medicine Division of Social Work and Behavioral Science. She will be remembered fondly as a woman of uncommon intellect as well as much warmth. We extend our sincere sympathy to her family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, M.D., President Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President
