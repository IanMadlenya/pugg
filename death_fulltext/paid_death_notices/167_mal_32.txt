
        KAIMOWITZ-Carl. Passed away November 30, 2000 of Del Ray Beach, formerly Dobbs Ferry, NY. Loving husband of Harriet to whom he has been married for 55 years. Adored father of Diane (Thomas) Kolb of Wellsley, MA and Ellen (Allan) Isbitz of Cambridge, MA. Cherished grandfather of Alan, Dan, Max, David and Sarah. Memorial contributions may be made to the Adolph and Rose Levis Jewish Community Center, 9901 Donna Klein Boulevard, Boca Raton, Florida 33428. Services in Florida today.
