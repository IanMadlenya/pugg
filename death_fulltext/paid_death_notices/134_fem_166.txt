
        LIPSIG-Carrie. Died at age 91, March 15, in Coconut Creek, FL. Beloved mother of Dr. Stephen and Susan Lipsig of Roslyn Estates, and Joseph and Maryann Lipsig of Sunnyvale, California. Adored grandmother of Diana, David and Scott. Sister of Sara Edelson of FL. Former Director of Food Services for the Board of Jewish Education, City of New York. She was a true pathfinder.
