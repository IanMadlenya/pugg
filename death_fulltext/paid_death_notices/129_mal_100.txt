
        LACHMAN-Lawrence, 81. Passed away in his sleep on October 7, 1997. He will be sorely missed by his family and friends. Beloved husband of Judith for fifty-two years. Devoted father of Robert and Charles. Grandfather of Elizabeth and Peter, Amy and the late Jill Lachman. Father-in-law of Dianna and Emily. Loving brother of Frances. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Wednesday, 6-8PM with service Thursday, 11:30AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to The Jill Lachman Memorial Fund, c/o EVAC, P.O. Box 743, Englewood, NJ 07631, or the charity of your choice.
