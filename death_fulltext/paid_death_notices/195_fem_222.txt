
        THOMPSON-LaWanda M., on April 28th, 2003, of Manhattan, NYC, formerly of Ohio, after a long illness. Beloved mother of Bruce R., Alison, Linda T. Reinstadtler and Christopher. Beloved grandmother of five. Lover of life, vivacious and spirited, she will be greatly missed by all who knew her. LaWanda fought her illness with extraordinary bravery and optimism and her family is grateful to the medical team at New York-Cornell Medical Center and the Strang Cancer Clinic. Service will be held Friday, May 2nd, 10 AM, at the Brick Church, 91st St. and Park Ave. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St, Thursday, May 1st, 2-4 and 6-8 PM. Interment to follow at Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Lenox Hill Neighborhood House, 331 E. 70th St., NY NY 10021-8698.
