
        SHORTALL--Wanda Allan, born January 25, 1922. Daughter of the anthropologist Bronislaw Malinowski and Elsie Masson, died September 16th in NYC. Wanda emigrated to the US in 1939 and retained a lifelong love for her home in Oberbozen, Italy. Wanda became a successful model with the Ford Modeling Agency under the name of Wanda Delafield. She is survived by her sister Helena Wayne, children Michael and Nicola Montemora and David Allan, stepchildren Thomas Shortall and Cathy Hensley and grandchildren Emily and Timothy Allan and Amanda and Stephen Shortall. Wanda's great warmth, wit and beauty will be missed by all who knew her.
