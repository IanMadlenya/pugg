
        POTTER-Cary. The Trustees of St. Andrew's Dune Church mourn the death of Cary Potter, Trustee Emeritus, with 45 years of service, the longest serving Trustee of the 66 men and women who have served as Trustees since 1879. We will miss his friendship, counsel and constant generosity. We give thanks for the gift of his life and rejoice that he has found peace. The Trustees of St. Andrew's Dune Church, Southampton, NY
