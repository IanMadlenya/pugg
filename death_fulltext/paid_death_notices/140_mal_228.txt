
        RASMINSKY-Dr. Louis. Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a long time friend, former Governor of the Bank of Canada, and the first Canadian to receive an honorary degree from YU. He also served as honorary national chairman of YU's Bora Laskin Memorial Scholarship Fund. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his children Michael and Lola, daughter-in-law Judy, & to his grandchildren. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
