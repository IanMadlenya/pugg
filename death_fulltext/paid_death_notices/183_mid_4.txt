
        ROMEROVSKI-Martin. We extend sincere condolences to Rose Romerovski, a devoted member of the National Women's Division Board of the American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem, on the loss of her dear husband. Martin served as a quiet, modest but exemplary role model, as does Rose, in meeting the highest standards of tzedekah as established by Maimonides. Her devotion to her husband during his many years of illness serves as an inspiration to all of us. May Rose and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Erica Jesselson, Chair Lee Weinbach, President
        ROMEROVSKI - Martin. The American Friends of Bar-Ilan University mourn the passing of its generous benefactor, Martin Romerovski and extend to his beloved wife, Rose, and all the members of the family its sincerest condolences. He was an outstanding Jew in every respect and was especially mindful of our cause. May the family derive comfort from inspiring memories. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, Chancellor Melvin Stein, President, American Board of Trustees Brig. Gen. (Ret) Yehuda Halevy, Executive Vice President, Bar-Ilan University
        ROMEROVSKI-Martin. Manhattan Day School/Yeshiva Ohr Torah mourns the passing of Mr. Martin Romerovski, a founder of our school. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Mordechai Besser, Principal Eli Salig, President Elliot Gibber, Chairman of the Board
        ROMEROVSKI - Martin. Fifth Avenue Synagogue Women's Club extends deepest sympathy to Rose, dedicated and much loved Board member, and all the family on the passing of beloved husband and father. Mrs. Anne Hiltzik, Chairman Mrs. Maidy Jeger, Presidium Mrs. Mona Gelbart, Presidium
