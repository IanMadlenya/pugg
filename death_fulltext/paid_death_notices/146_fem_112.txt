
        HOLMES-Theresa. The Board of Trustees and family of St. Clare's Hospital mourn the passing of our colleague and friend. She was Director of Admitting for 20 of her 22 years at St. Clare's. She was a skillful administrator and devoted to St. Clare's. Our most sincere condolences are extended to her family, Nancy Mock, David, Gerald and Kenneth, eleven grandchildren, one brother and four sisters. Gerald W. Cunningham, Chairman James A. Rutherford, Pres./CEO St. Clare's Hospital
