
        COHEN-Frederick L., attorney, 89, of Brooklyn Heights, beloved husband of Helen, died Saturday, February 22, at Long Island College Hospital, after a short illness resulting from a fall. Attended NYC public schools and City College. Graduated St. John's University Law School. Veteran of World War II with two years of overseas service. He was senior attorney with the New York State Labor Relations Board for 27 years, until his retirement. He was a member of the Jewish War Veterans and the Knights of Pythias. In 1952 he ran as a Republican candidate for the then Municipal Court. He also ran for State Senator.
