
        TULLY--Mary Jean. The board and members of Veteran Feminist of America express profound grief at the passing of our co-founder and board member Mary Jean Tully. Her great energy and generosity helped bring reality to the V.F.A. Donations in Mary Jean's memory to continue her vision for V.F.A. can be made to the Mary Jean Tully Fund, V.F.A., Attn Ms Hackett, Tres., 473 Westminster Rd., Brooklyn, NY 11218
