
        SARATH-Edward Noah, 78, of Ridgefield, CT, passed away October 7, 2000 in the loving company of his family. Survived by his cherished wife, Florence, seven children-Carol, Edward, Maria, David, James, Patrice and Steven and 8 grandchildren. He was a social and spiritual visionary and author of two books-An Awakening Soul and The Mark of the Galilean. His radiant smile and concern for others, which characterized his life through his very last days, will be remembered by all who knew him.
