
        MATTHEWS - Billy. Director, Stage Manager, Founding Member of the Society of Stage Directors & Choreographers died December 15, 1996 in London. His long directorial career included Broadway and more than 125 productions in Stock and Dinner Theater. Memorial donations may be made to University of Miami Library, L. Taylor/B. Matthews Musical Theater Archive. P.O. Box 248214, Coral Gables, Fla. 33124.
