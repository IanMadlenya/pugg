
        NEWI-George H., 62. Of Bonita Springs, Florida. Formerly of Chappaqua, New York. Died suddenly while vacationing in Stavanger, Norway July 22. A former President of the ABC Television Network, was a graduate of Syracuse University and served as an officer in the U.S. Army. He is survived by his wife Sally Garnett Newi, two sons George Jr. of Atlanta, Alec of Chicago and his daughter Lisa Newi Long of Pleasantville, N.Y. A memorial service will be held at the Presbyterian Church of Mount Kisco on Saturday, July 26 at 2 PM.
