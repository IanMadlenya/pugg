
        LEAHY-Michael G. (Gerry), on May 21, 1997, age 80. Devoted husband of Elizabeth (Lily). Beloved father of Geraldine, Thomas, Anne Elizabeth (deceased), Frances and Michael. Survived by his sister, Katherine Colleran, and many nieces and nephews, particularly Jack. Loving father-in-law of Michael D. Nutley, Diana Leahy, John Hanly, and Nancy Kelly Leahy. Beloved grandpa of Michael, Tara, Chrissie, and Katie Nutley. Jennifer, Amy, Thomas Daniel, and Diana Leahy; John, Veronica, and Lauren Henley; Kelly Anne, Mary, Michael Francis, and Daniel Joseph Leahy. Uncle Gerard and Grandpa Gerry to countless others. Proud 50 year member Local Union 608 of the United Brotherhood of Carpenters and Joiners of America. Past President of Sligo Men's Association, Naval Veteran of WWII. Reposing, 2-4, 7-9 PM, Friday, Alexander-Tuthill Funeral Home, Route 25A, Wading River. Funeral Mass, Saturday, May 24, 9 AM, St. John the Baptist R.C. Church, Wading River. Interment St. Raymonds Cemetery, Bronx, N.Y. In lieu of flowers remembrance gifts may be made to American Diabetes Assn. or United Cerebral Palsy Foundation.
