
        GOLDSHLAG-Helen L. Of NYC, formally of Peekskill, died on June 12, 2000 at her residence. She was 94. She is survived by a brother James Jacoby of Punta Gorda, Fl. And several generations of nieces and nephews. She was the wife of the late Louis P. Goldshlag and mother of the late Patricia L. Goldshlag. Funeral services will be held 11:00AM Friday at the MeKeel's Corners Chapel at the corner of Rt 9 and 301 Coldspring, NY. The family will receive friends on Thursday 7-9PM at the E.O. Curry Funeral Home, 313 North James St, Peekskill, NY. Interment Arlington National Cemetery on Tues.
