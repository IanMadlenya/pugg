
        BLAUSTEIN--Ira, 85, of Westbury, NY and Boca Raton, FL, on December 25. Beloved husband to Helen for 55 years. Incredible father and amazing grandfather. Generous friend to many throughout the world. His ready smile and sense of humor endeared him to all. Funeral service, Sunday, December 28, 2003, 11am, at Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
