
        GOLDSBY-Ruth M. of Wyomifsing, PA, and formerly of Chappaqua, NY. Died on June 19, 1999 of congestive heart failure at age 90. Survived by her husband for 65 years, Dr. A.R. Goldsby, and daughter, Jan Myers. Donations in her memory may be made to the The Chappaqua or Wyomifsing Public Library.
