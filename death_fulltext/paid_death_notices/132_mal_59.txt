
        PORT-Jeffrey David. 38, of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Woodbury, NY, died January 3, 1998 of Aids Dementia Complex. Beloved son of Nathaniel and Rose Port; loving brother of Dr. Michael and Jacqueline Port. Devoted uncle of William, Lindsey, Allison and Tyler. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations to be made in his name to ''A.M.F.A.R.'', American Foundation for Aids Research in New York City. Services will be held 2:15 PM, January 5, 1998 at Star of David Memorial Chapel & Cemetery, North Lauderdale, FL.
