
        KLIOT--David A., MD. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center community mourns the passing of David A. Kliot, MD, former clinical professor of OB/GYN, and a graduate of the College of Medicine in 1959. Dr. Kliot will be remembered for his many years of service to our institution and to the people of Brooklyn. We are especially grateful for his dedication to the College of Medicine Alumni Association and his years both as president and as an active board member. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family and friends. John C. LaRosa, MD, President Eugene B. Feigelson, MD, Sr. Vice President for Biomedical Education and Research, and Dean of the College of Medicine
