
        DALY-Daniel, formerly of Fairfield, CT, died at Pine Plains, NY on February 1, 2003. He was Founder and President of the Mashomack Club and the Mashomack Foundation. He was a veteran of the U.S. Army and attended Columbia University where he was President of St. Anthony Hall, Georgetown University School of Foreign Service, the Sorbonne in Paris. He is survived by his sisters, Mrs. Bevin Patterson and Mrs. Alison Gerard, his brother, Michael, his 28 nieces and nephews, and his longtime friend and Manager of the Mashomack Club, David Thieringer. He was predeceased by his sister, Mrs. Madeleine Potter, and his brothers, T.F. Gilroy and Dermot. A wake will be held at the Mashomack Club, 7435 Route 82, Pine Plains, NY, on Friday, February 7, from 4pm to 9pm and Saturday, February 8, from 9:30am to 1pm. A funeral mass will be held at 11am, Monday, February 10, at St. Joseph Parish, Routes 25 and 133, Brookfield, CT. Interment to follow at Oaklawn Cemetery, Fairfield, CT. A memorial service will be held at the Mashomack Club in June. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Mashomack Foundation, PO Box 308, Pine Plains, NY 12567. The Foundation supports scholarships for area students.
