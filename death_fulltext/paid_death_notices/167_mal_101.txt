
        TYSON-Dr. Alan. Preeminent Beethoven and Mozart scholar, psychoanalyst, formerly Senior Fellow of All Souls College, Oxford, CBE, FBA, on November 10, aged 74 in England, after a long illness. He is mourned by his many friends on both sides of the Atlantic and by his associates and students at Columbia University, Graduate Center CUNY, Institute for Advanced Study at Princeton, University of California at Berkeley, and the Montefiore Hospital. Memorial services will be held at a later date.
