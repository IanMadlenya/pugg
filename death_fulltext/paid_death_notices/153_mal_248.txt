
        RENICK-Samuel W. Passed peacefully at his home in Manhattan on Saturday October 16, 1999. Cherished uncle and grand uncle to Binnie and Avery. Sammy, a legendary figure in the racing world will be remembered by many. Funeral services to be held on Thursday, October, 21, 1999 at 10 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell/The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. Memorial contributions may be made to The Jockeys' Guild, P.O. Box 250, Lexington, KY. 40588.
