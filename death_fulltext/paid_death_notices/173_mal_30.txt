
        FEIGIN-William M. Of Levittown, on June 2, 2001, age 80. Beloved husband of Dora for 55 years. Loving father of Philip (Barbara) Denver, CO, Andrew (Mimi) Huntington Station, NY. Proud grandfather of Lauren, Benjamin and Hannah. Eternal optimist and friend to all who knew him. Veteran of the Second World War, Pacific Theater. Devoted educator and administrator in Hicksville School District for 27 years. Lifelong birder and photographer, founding member of the Sialis Bird Club and the ''Gang,'' like all good scouts, he left this world a better place than he found it. Funeral services will be held at 12 noon, Monday, June 4, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY 11797.
        FEIGIN-William (Billy). Our most revered brother-in-law. Loving uncle and great-uncle to our children. He will be sorely missed. Midge Bregman and Shirley Feit
