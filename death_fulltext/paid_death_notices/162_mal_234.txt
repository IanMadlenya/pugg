
        HIGGINS-James J., on July 20, 2000 at age 82 after a long series of illnesses. Graduate of Manhattan College and Fordham Law School. Retired partner of Kirlin, Campbell & Keating, an Admiralty law firm, past president of the Maritime Law Association of the U.S., member of the Comite Maritime International and India House. Devoted husband of Patricia for 46 years and also survived by his sister, Mary E. Higgins of Wilmington, DE. He will be greatly missed by all who knew his warmth, humor, wrath and decency. Visiting hours at the Abbey (Frank E. Campbell Bldg), 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Sunday and Monday, 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass will be celebrated at St. Jean Baptiste, Lexington Ave. at 76 St., Tuesday, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Cardiac Division of Lenox Hill Hospital, 100 E. 77 St., New York, New York 10021.
