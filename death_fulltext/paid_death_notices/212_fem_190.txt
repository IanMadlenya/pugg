
        LEITSTEIN--Shirley. Age 97, of Belvedere, CA., formerly of Yonkers, N.Y., passed away September 21, 2004. Loving wife of the late Jack Leitstein. Beloved mother of Bruce Leitstein, and Alex and Bob Leitstein. Cherished grandmother of Eric and Justin Leitstein, aunt of Richard Mescon and Jane Ryffel, Michael and Miriam Mescon and great aunt of Andrew, Jennie, David, Daniel and Hannah Mescon. Her love of life and the many friendships she shared will long remain an inspiration to the many lives she touched.
