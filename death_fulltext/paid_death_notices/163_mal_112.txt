
        LEVINE-Harold. The officers and staff of Acker & Jablow Textiles express their deep sorrow at the passing of our longtime friend, Harold Levine. He was an astute, devoted and loyal associate of the company and was a very integral part of our growth. Harold was a gentleman in the truest sense and will be greatly missed. Our most sincere condolences to his wife, Harriet; children, Donna and Jay; their spouses, Mark and Debra; and grandchildren, Brett, Randall and Fallon. Al Steinberg, Steven Steinberg,
