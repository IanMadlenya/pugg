
        KLEIN--Donald. Of Armonk, NY. Died peacefully on November 15, 2003. Respected lawyer and loyal friend. He is survived by his wife Barbara, son Jason, daughter Zoe and other beloved family members. His family would like to express gratitude to all of those who showed their love and care. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date. Donations in his honor can be made to: The Musella Foundation, 1100 Peninsula Blvd, Hewlett, NY 11557. All donations will go towards brain tumor research.
        KLEIN--Donald S. The Board of the Friends and the Staff of the Neuberger Museum of Art are profoundly saddened by the death of our friend and fellow Board member, Donald Klein. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Barbara, his children Zoe and Jason, and to the entire Klein family. Emily S. Tait, Chair Lucinda H. Gedeon, Director
        KLEIN--Donald S. The Sunningdale Platform Tennis players note the passing of team leader Donald S. (Donnie) Klein. Our ranks have been permanently thinned. He raised our game.
