
        DRAINE-Thomas P. Died June 20, 1999 in Portland, OR. Survived and missed by his wife, Dolly Rieck Draine of Portland, OR; his children, Bruce Draine of Princeton, NJ and Kathleen Draine of Sandy, OR; and his two grandchildren, David and Alexander Draine of Princeton, NJ. Buried June 22, 1999 at Willamette National Cemetery in Portland, OR. with a Navy honor guard. Family suggest donations to the American Parkinson's Disease Foundation.
