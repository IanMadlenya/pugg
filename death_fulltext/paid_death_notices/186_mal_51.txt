
        ROMANO - Ralph William, MD, died suddenly Sunday, June 30 at his country home in Austerlitz, NY. Dr. Romano was a highly respected radiation oncologist at St. Vincent's Medical Center in Bridgeport and was also in private practice in Southport, CT. He is survived by his wife Alexandra Nichols of NYC, his sister Carol Maimone of McLean, VA. His extended family and friends mourn his untimely passing. He will be greatly missed. Memorial visitation Monday 7-9PM and Tuesday 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Memorial Mass Wednesday, 10:30 A.M. at Church of Our Savior, 38th St. and Park Ave. Contributions may be made to the Doctor Romano Memorial Scholarship Fund at Turtle Bay Music School, 244 East 52nd Street, New York, NY 10022.
