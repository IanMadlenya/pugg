
        LOEB-Alida. Beloved sister of Joel T. Loeb and Constance L. Melnik. Loving aunt of Victoria L. Brown, Rosalind M. Goldfarb, Nicholas H. Loeb, Charlotte S. Melnik and Robert L. Melnik. Devoted great-aunt of Benjamin and Emma Brown, David and Paul Goldfarb. Died on Wednesday, June 2 at her home. She was a very dear, devoted, generous, curious, frank, loving and forthright friend who will be sorely missed. She was a past Regent of the Quaker City Chapter of the Daughters of the American Revolution and formerly a volunteer at the Children's Heart Hospital. There will be a memorial service for the family at a later date.
