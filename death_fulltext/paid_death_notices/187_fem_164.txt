
        BRAGMAN-Mary. November 21, 1911-August 22, 2002. Died after a brief illness at the Rockleigh Jewish Home. Mary and her husband Ben co-founded Service Smoked Fish in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. Mary retired as president of the company. Survived by her daughter Sanda Cohen (Sam), son Billy Bragman (John Mortimer), and daughter Susan Johnson (Bill), her grandchildren Bruce, David (Janet) and Sara and her precious great - granddaughter Madelyn. Private services were held. Donations may be made to the Rockleigh Jewish Home, Rockleigh, NJ.
