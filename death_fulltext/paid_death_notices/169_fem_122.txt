
        ZICKERMAN-Ruth B. Died February 9, 2001, age 95. Widow of Dr. Julius Zickerman. Retired NYC schoolteacher of 40 years. Resident of North Miami Beach, Fla. since the 1970's. She will be remembered with love by her sons Stuart and Martin of Hollis, N.Y. and her many friends. She was a tireless fundraiser and organizer for Hadassah in New York and Florida for almost 50 years. ''The Hostess with the Mostest''. A Memorial Service will be held at Sinai Chapel, Fresh Meadows, N.Y. February 13th, 10:15am. Memorial donations should be sent to HMO at National Hadassah Fundraising, attn Bella/Susie, 50 West 58 Street, New York, New York 10019.
