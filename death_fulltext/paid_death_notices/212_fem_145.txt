
        SUCHIN--Ernestine ''Ernie'' of Scotch Plains, N.J. Daughter of Joseph and Julia Mutnick, passed away peacefully on Monday, September 13, 2004 in her home surrounded by her family, friends and pets. She worked for many years as a realtor in Scarsdale, N.Y. and Westfield, N.J. In addition to raising three children, she was an active member of the community, receiving numerous citations for her wide and varied philanthropic endeavors. She had a special passion for the disenfranchised. Her love of animals was well known to all. Ernie is survived by her children Linda Davino, Neil Suchin, and Scott Suchin, M.D., her four grandchildren Gregory, Mark, Charli and Jolie, her daughters-in-law Carol and Alona, her son-in-law Gary and her beloved dogs Willie, Alfie, Cissy and Pippin. Her husband Jerome and twin sister Geraldine predeceased her. Her loving presence will be missed by all those whose lives she touched. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, September 19, 3PM at the Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave., Scarsdale, NY 10583 (914725-1137). In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to the Humane Society or the ASPCA.
