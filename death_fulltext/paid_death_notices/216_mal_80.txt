
        STEEN--Dr. Philip M., 93, on January 5, 2005. Prominent dentist and specialist. He is survived by his beloved wife of 59 years, Suzy, children Deborah, Peter, Tony, grandson Jordan. Father-in-law of Maryann Spero-Steen and Rabbi Roger Ross. Devoted friend of Myrna Leichter. Services Tuesday, January 11, 1PM, Ferncliff Cemetery Chapel, 280 Secor Rd, Hartsdale. Donations may be made to Sarah Neuman Nursing Home, Palmer Ave, Mamaroneck or the New Seminary Scholarship Fund, PMB 344, 2565 Broadway, NYC 10025.
        STEEN--Dr. Philip. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member Dr. Philip Steen and extend sincere condolences to his family. Charles J. Block, President
