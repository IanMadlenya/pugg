
        BERMAN-Annette Paltin. October 24, 1914, to November 2, 2001. Beloved mother of Myron J. Berman and Arnold T. Berman. Grandmother of Jainee L. Berman, Corey M. Berman and Abigail T. Berman. She was a great gift, an exemplary life and an inspiration to all. Shiva will be at the home of Myron J. Berman in Staten Island, Monday through Wednesday.
