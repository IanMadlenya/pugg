
        HERMAN-Arthur L. Passed away on July 3, 2000, at the age of 90, after a brief illness. Formerly of Lauderhill, FL, and Lawrence, NY. Survived by his beloved Daisy, wife of 67 years. Loving father of Barbara Herman (Paul Cohen) and Janie and Herb Taylor. Adored grandfather of Randall and Anne Schein, Jonathan and Cynthia Schein and Jeremy Taylor. He is also survived by his brother Morty and Sally Herman. Graveside services were held Wednesday, July 5, 2000.
