
        SINGER-Esther L., 85. Formerly of Brooklyn, New York. Beloved wife, mother, sister, grandmother, aunt of many nephews and nieces and cherished friend of many. She was a dedicated teacher for over 25 years in the junior and senior high schools of New York. She ended her career at Sheepshead Bay High School where she touched the lives of hundreds of students. Graveside service on Tuesday, October 20th, 1998, at 1:00 P.M., at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York.
