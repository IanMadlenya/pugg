
        BASTEDO--Helen Wilmerding (Mrs. Philip) (January 5, 1917-April 27, 2006), youngest daughter of the late Lucius and Helen (Cutting) Wilmerding of New York City, died peacefully. With her late husband Mrs. Bastedo was an important part ot the nonprofit scene in both New York City and New Hampshire. She is survived by four children and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held later in Dublin, NH. Memorial contributions in lieu of flowers to Planned Parenthood of Northern New England, 18 Lowe Avenue Concord, NH 03301.
        $(6$)BASTEDO -- Helen. The Board, Council and staff of Planned Parenthood of New York City are deeply saddened by the death of Honorary Board member, Helen Bastedo. Helen joined the PPNYC Board in 1941, and served with distinction in many capacities. As an officer of the Board, she served on the Executive Committee, was an outstanding fundraiser and was herself a very generous supporter of our programs. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her family. Patricia J. Volland, Chair PPNYC Board of Directors Joan Malin, President/CEO
