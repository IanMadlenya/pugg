
        WEBER-Mortimer W. , M.D., age 84 of Wayne, NJ, formerly of Franklin Lakes, NJ. Beloved husband of Barbara (nee Steuert) Parker Weber. Dear father of Jon Weber of Monterey, CA., and James Weber of Seattle, WA. Dear uncle of Tamara Leuchtenberg of NYC, NY. Loving grandfather of five granddaughters. Dr. Weber was a pediatrician in private practice in New York City for 50 years. He was a physician at Lenox Hill Hospital, New York Eye Infirmary and a physician and teacher at New York University Medical Center and Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, all in New York City. He was an Army veteran of World War II, and served in the Philippines and in Japan as a doctor with the rank of Captain during his Army service. He was a 1938 graduate of New York University Medical School, a life member of the Alpha Omega Alpha Honor Society, & a member of the American Medical Association. Funeral services on Thursday 10 AM at the Oakland Memorial Home, 330 Ramapo Valley Road (Route 202) Oakland, NJ. Burial Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY. Visiting Wednesday 2-4 & 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers donations to the Alzheimer's Association, 229 Cherry Hill Road, Parsippany, NJ 07054 would be appreciated.
