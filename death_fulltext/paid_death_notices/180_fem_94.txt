
        PETERKIN-Katharine Urban. Of Darien, Connecticut, died at her home on January 3rd, 2002. She was 82. Mrs. Peterkin was born in Buffalo, New York on May 17, 1919, to the late Florence Zeller Urban and George P. Urban. Her father was head of Buffalo's George Urban Milling Company, as was his father and grandfather before him. Mrs. Peterkin graduated from The Masters School and, in 1941, from Smith College. She married Mr. Peterkin in 1948. Mrs. Peterkin is predeceased by her husband, sisters Florence Wyper and Ada Marie Urban, and her son Christopher Cameron Peterkin. She is survived by her brother Henry Zeller Urban and his family, as well as her children Clare, DeWitt III, George, Kate, and Patrick, and three grandchildren, Jennifer, Stephanie and William. A funeral service will be held at St. Luke's Episcopal Chapel, 1848 Post Road, Darien, CT. on Tuesday, January 8, 3 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Smith College Alumnae Fund, Northampton, MA. 01063, The Masters School Alumnae/i Fund, 49 Clinton Ave., Dobbs Ferry, NY 10522, and Amnesty International USA, 322 8th Ave., NY, NY 10001.
