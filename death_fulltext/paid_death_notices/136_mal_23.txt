
        GEBSTAEDT-Andre D., 78. Of Laurel Park, died Tuesday, April 28, 1998, after an extended illness. He was born in Germany and lived for many years in Port Washington, NY. He was Director of Advertising Services for ABC Television until his retirement in 1985. Survivors include his wife of 49 years, Virginia R. Gebstaedt; two daughters, Anne Hammond, of Greenville, S.C., and Kate Gebstaedt of Madison, N.J.; and two grandchildren, Timothy Hammond and Simone Pathe. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, P.O. Box 1066, Asheville, NC 28802. For information contact Thos. Shepherd & Son Funeral Directors and Cremation Memorial Center.
