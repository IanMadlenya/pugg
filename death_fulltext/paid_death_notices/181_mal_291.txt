
        KIELY-Michael J. Of Bronxville on February 24, 2002. Brother of Elizabeth Peterson. Surviving are numerous nieces and nephews, grandnieces and nephews and great-grandnieces and nephews. Practicing attorney for over 70 years and formerly a senior partner at Wormser, Kiely, Galef and Jacobs. Calling hours at The Fred H. McGrath & Son, Funeral Home, Bronxville between the hours of 24 and 6-8 PM Tuesday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville on Wednesday February 27 at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, New York 10708.
        KIELY-Michael J., Jr. The partners, associates, counsel and the entire staff of Wormser, Kiely, Galef & Jacobs LLP note with extreme sorrow the passing, on February 24, 2002, of our senior partner and mentor, Michael J. Kiely, Jr., and extend to his sister, Elizabeth ''Bunny'' Peterson, his nephew Robert Atkinson and all his nieces, nephews and their children, our sincere condolences. Mr. Kiely joined the predecessor firm of Myles, Wormser & Koch in the 1930s, and practiced law continuously with the successor and merged firms until December 2001, having been admitted to practice law in the State of New York in 1930. Mr. Kiely would have been 99 years old on his next birthday, March 19th. ''Mike'', we, your extended family, will surely miss you. Wormser, Kiely, Galef & Jacobs LLP
