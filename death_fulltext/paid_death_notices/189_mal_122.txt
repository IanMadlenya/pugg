
        DONALDSON-Kenneth T., M.D. Age 89, died on October 6, 2002 in Palm City, FL. Practiced medicine at Roosevelt Hospital in New York City for 40 years. Survived by his wife, Betty, his children Linda, Bob and his wife Amy and Tom and his wife Theresa. and five grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on October 19, 2002, at 11 A.M. at Sandhill Cove in Palm City.
