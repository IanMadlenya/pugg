
        COLENDA--Frank G., MD. Of NYC died unexpectedly on February 10, 2004. Dr. Colenda was a well-respected internist who practiced in Manhattan for more than 30 years. He was a senior attending physician at Lenox Hill Hospital. Funeral services are tentatively being planned for Friday and/or Saturday at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Please call (212) 288-3500 for more details. Donations in his name may be made to the American Heart Association.
        COLENDA--Frank, M.D. The Board of Trustees, the Department of Medicine and the Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express their deep sorrow over the death of Frank Colenda, M.D, a distinguished member of our staff for over 33 years. Dr. Colenda was a dedicated physician who was greatly admired by his patients and colleagues. The Lenox Hill Hospital family honors his memory and mourns his loss. We extend our sincere sympathies to the Colenda family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO James J. Richter, M.D., PhD., President, Medical Board Robert A. Phillips, M.D., PhD., Chairman, Dept of Medicine
