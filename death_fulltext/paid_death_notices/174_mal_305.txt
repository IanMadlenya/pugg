
        REIBEL-Nathan. Died peacefully at Hackensack University Hospital on July 27. He was 94 years old. He practiced law in Elizabeth, NJ, for 65 years and was a past president of the Union County Bar Association. He was married to Helen Welt for 70 years, and will be greatly missed by her, his son Stephen, and his grandsons Noah and Joshua, as well as all who benefited from his abiding concern.
