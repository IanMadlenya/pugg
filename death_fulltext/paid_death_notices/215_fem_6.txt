
        SCHWARTZ--Sophie. Age 90, of Forest Hills, New York, died peacefully November 30, 2004. Beloved wife of Sidney, loving mother of Susan, Daniel, daughter-in-law Adriane, adored grandmother of Michael & Jake. During WWII, Sophie worked for the Office of War Information and then went on to serve in the US Embassy in Moscow. She lived her life with dignity, courage and great strength. She was admired & respected by all who had the good fortune to have known her. Sweet Sophie, your family will miss and remember you always. Funeral services will be held 9:45 AM, on Thursday, December 2, at Gutterman's Funeral Home/Parkside Chapel, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Rego Park, New York.
