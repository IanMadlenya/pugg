
        GOLDSTEIN-Evelyn V. Wife of Murray, mother of Phyllis and Charles, grandmother of Deborah, Joseph, Arthur and Jeffrey, greatgrandmother of Raphael, daughter of Minnie and Sol Bier, sister of Jack, Helen and Gert, aunt of Frances, Stanley, Allen, Andrea, Jerry and Norman, died on June 28, 1999 in her 97th year, in Carbondale, CO beside her granddaughter. She gave love to her family and friends for nearly a century and knew what it is to be dearly loved in return. After a blessed life, she died at peace. Mrs. Goldstein will rest at Beth Israel Cemetery in Woodbridge, NJ.
