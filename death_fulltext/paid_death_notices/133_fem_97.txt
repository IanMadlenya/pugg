
        SIMMONS-Maxine Jane. On January 21, 1998. Devoted daughter of the late Marion Frances Quinton and Herbert Simmons, and caring guardian of Suki. A pioneer as one of the first female African American attorneys in the country. Owner and consultant to the James L. Perkins Memorial Chapel, Inc. Her friends will miss her warmth, intelligence, humor, and determination. Contributions should be made to the United Negro College Fund, 120 Wall Street, 9th Floor, New York, NY 10005.
