
        WILLIAMS-William J. Of Vero Beach, FL, formerly of Bronxville, New York, at the age of 92. Graduate of Columbia College 1930, and Fordham University School of Law 1933. Served as Attorney with Securities and Exchange Commission. Was Vice President, Secretary, General Counsel and a Director of Canada Dry Corporation. Survived by his wife Jane, his son William J. Williams, Jr. and his grandson Barrett R. Williams.
