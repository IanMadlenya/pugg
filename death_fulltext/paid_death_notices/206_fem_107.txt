
        LINDSAY--Mary. The Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are saddened by the death of Mary Lindsay, loving and beloved wife of Founding Chairman, John V. Lindsay. Mary was such an extraordinary and vivacious person whose enthusiasm swept away all obstacles and impediments that life strew in her path. She reveled in John's success in bringing this theater back to life and was an avid first nighter here. Condolences and sympathy to Katharine, Margaret, Anne, John Jr., & the grandchildren. John B. Beinecke, Chairman John Chalsty, President Andre Bishop, Artistic Director Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
