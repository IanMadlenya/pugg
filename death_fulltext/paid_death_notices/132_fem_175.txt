
        GELB-Leah. Beloved wife of the late Rabbi Max Gelb. Loving mother of Bernard and David. Cherished mother-in-law of Mimi and Carol. Devoted grandmother of Michael, Lauren and Alex. Dear sister of Dr. Ruth Lefkowitz. She was a founder and the first principal of the Solomon Schechter School of Westchester. She was also a past president of both the Alumni Association of the Seminary College of Jewish Studies and the Sisterhood of Beth El Synagogue of New Rochelle. Services Thursday, 10AM, Temple Israel Center, 280 Old Mamaroneck Road, White Plains.
        GELB-Leah. Temple Israel Center of white Plains mourns the passing of Mrs. Leah Gelb, a Jewish educator and role model of the first rank, who was also the wife of our late beloved Rabbi Max Gelb. She worked tirelessly for our community, with grace and dedication, for over three decades, and won the affection and respect of all who learned from her. As the first principal of the Solomon Schechter School of Westchester, she raised the standards for Jewish education in our county for all time. We are grateful to God for her many gifts to us, and we extend deepest condolences to her children Bernard and Mimi, and David and Carol, her grandchildren Michael, Lauren, and Alex, and her sister Ruth Lefkowitz. May all who grieve find comfort from Heaven. Gordon Tucker, Rabbi Richard Greenfield, President
