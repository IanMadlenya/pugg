
        PARADISE--John H. Of Manhattan, on January 17, at age 91. He will be sorely missed by his wife Jean, daughter Emily Paradise Achtenberg, son-in-law Ben Achtenberg, grandson Jesse Achtenberg and brother Joseph. John, throughout his life, was an active advocate for civil rights, social justice and world peace. For 12 years he was an organizer for the United Electrical Workers and for 15 years a fund raiser for the State of Israel. He also did volunteer work for the Center for Constitutional Rights and particpated as a dedicated student in the Institute for Retired Professionals at New School University. A memorial gathering is being planned.
