
        KOHNOP-Benjamin L., M.D. On February 9. Age 87. Retired physician at NYU Medical Center. Loving husband of Pearl, brother of Emma Shapiro and the late Sam, beloved uncle, grand-uncle and great grand-uncle. He will be greatly missed by those nieces and nephews who were the joy of his life. Services at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave., Wednesday, February 11, 12 Noon.
        KOHNOP-Benjamin L., MD. Congregation Adereth El extends condolences to Mrs. Pearl Kohnop on the passing of her husband, our member, Dr. Benjamin L. Kohnop. Sidney Kleiman, Rabbi Joe Greene, President
