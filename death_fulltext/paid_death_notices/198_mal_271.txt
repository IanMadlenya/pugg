
        SHENTON--James. The Coby Foundation, Ltd., mourns the passing of James Shenton on July 25. Appointed to the Foundation's Board of Directors by its founder, the late Irene Zambelli Silverman, and at his death serving as co-Chair of the Foundation, Professor Shenton provided wise leadership during a formative period for the Foundation. Professor Shenton will be greatly missed, and the Foundation extends its sympathy to his family and many friends. The Board of Directors of The Coby Foundation, Ltd.
