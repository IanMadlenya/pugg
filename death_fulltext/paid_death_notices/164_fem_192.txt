
        SCHUCKER-Margaret. The Board of Trustees, faculty, staff and students at The Institute of Design and Construction mourn the loss of Margaret (Maggie) Schucker, wife of the late Charles Schucker. Maggie was with the Institute for over thirty years and was a dear friend of The Institute. We offer our heartfelt sympathy to her family and friends. Vincent C. Battista, R.A., Executive Director
