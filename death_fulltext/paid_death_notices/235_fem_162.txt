
        BROMLEY--Sylvia. 88. Artist, loving mother, grandmother and aunt, tragically killed after being struck by a car on the Upper EastSide. She is survived by her sons Mark and Alan, her granddaughters Leah and Andrea and daughter-in-law Karen. She will be greatly missed by family and friends. Her energy, laugh and spirit was that of a very young person. She Loved her daily bus trips to her art classes at the 92nd St. Y. Funeral services Riverside Chapel Sunday 11:15 AM. Family will be sitting Shiva Sunday and Monday 4-8PM, 511 E. 80 St, Sylvia's apartment. Contributions can be made to Friends of the Y or the Burden Center. Rest in Peace.
