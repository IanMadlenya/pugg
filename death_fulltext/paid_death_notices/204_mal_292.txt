
        GUIOR--Armand M., 92, died peacefully in his home in Rockville Centre, NY on January 20, 2004. He was the Founder of Jean's Silversmiths in NYC where he was also known as Mr. Armand or Jean. He is survived by his adoring wife and partner of 66 years Rhoda (nee Tiger). He will be missed greatly by his loving children Fern and Arnie, granddaughter Danielle, sister Matilda Dana, sister-in-law Florence Plotzky and his many nieces and nephews. Funeral services Friday 1 PM at ''Gutterman's Inc.'', Rockville Centre, L.I. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to your favorite charity.
