
        HENNEFELD -- Edmund B. On October 9, 2003 at age 971/2. Married for 72 years to Lillian. Survived by his children Erica Abeel and Julien Hennefeld, and four grandchildren. During the 70s, he was one of the Premier Appellate Lawyers in New York City's Corporation Counsel. He was also a lawyer in private practice in the firm Hennefeld and Riposanu. He was the author of a novel ''The World Tour'', as well as numerous plays. His play ''The Deputy of Paris'', presented at The Henry Street Playhouse, was praised by New York Times Critic, Brooks Atkinson. He was loved and admired by those who knew him for his brilliant, wide-ranging mind, civility and wit.
