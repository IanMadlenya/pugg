
        ATANASIO -- Margaret T. ''Peg'' (nee McAloon), on June 6, 2006. In Breezy Point, formerly of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn and Boca Raton, FL. Passed away peacefully at her home six days after her 90th birthday, surrounded by her loving family. Peg is survived by Joseph F. Atanasio, M.D. and her loving and much loved children, Peggy Colucci (Bill), Kathe Hanna (Tom), Joseph Atanasio, M.D. (Miriam), Paul Atanasio, Robert Atanasio (Kathy), Regina DiGiovanna, M.D. (Lawrence), Patti Griffith (Perry), Donna Andrea (Frank) and Clare Atanasio. Dear sister of Betty Zamow and the late Agnes Lynch, Rose Malone, Kitty Sacchi and Patrick McAloon. Proud grandmother of 24 grandchildren and 18 great-grandchildren. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Visiting Thursday and Friday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 4th Ave., Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10:15 AM, at Our Lady of Angels Church. All have been touched by her life and saddened by her death. Peg was loving and caring and the life of the party, with a song on her lips and always in her heart. She lived her life with her heart soaring upwards. Her family encourages memorial donations to AHRC Foundation (Autism Program), 189 Wheatly Rd., Brookville, NY 11545.
