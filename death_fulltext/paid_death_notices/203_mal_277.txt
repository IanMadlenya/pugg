
        SALOMON--Walter. Congregation Habonim mourns the loss of a pillar of our community, Cantor Walter Salomon. A long-time member of Habonim, Walter brought his beautiful music into the life of our community for more than five decades. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Eva and daughter Linda Salomon. May his memory be a blessing. Rabbi Joanna Samuels Leo Asen, President
