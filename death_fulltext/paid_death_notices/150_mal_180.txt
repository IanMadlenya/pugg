
        NGAI-Shih-Hsun, M.D. The Board and membership of the Chinese American Medical Society mourn the passing of its founding member, Dr. Shih-Hsun Ngai, Professor Emeritus of Anesthesiology and Pharmacology at Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons. His strength, vision and leadership will be missed. Our deep condolences go to his wife, Hseuh-Hwa Wang, M.D., Executive Director of CAMS and their children. Raymond Fong, M.D., President
