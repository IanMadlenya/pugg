
        GIDEZ--Lewis I., Ph.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of our esteemed colleague, who joined the faculty in 1958 as a member of the departments of Biochemistry and Medicine. Over the span of three decades, he was a beloved teacher to several generations of our students and was also renowned for his many contributions to the research of lipids and lipid metabolism. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Gidez family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
