
        FISHER-Justin R. On May 26, 2000 at age 91. Beloved husband of the late Shonnette and devoted father of the late Andrew. Survived by his grandchildren, Elizabeth Goodman and Edward Silverman; his daughter-in-law Linda Silverman and her husband Richard; his nephew Ross Fisher and other nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. on Tuesday, 11-12 PM. Funeral service to follow at the Beth-El Cemetery, Forest Road, Paramus, NJ, 1 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or a charity of your choice.
