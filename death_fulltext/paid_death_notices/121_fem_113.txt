
        McCLANAHAN-Sophie Lay. Of Modesto, CA. Formerly of Englewood, NJ, and Manhattan, passed away peacefully, February 9, just before her 90th year. Beloved wife of the late ophthalmologist, Dr. Robert McClanahan, mother of William, Sophie Barnes, Anne Bourne, and Sara Hancox, sister of Louisa Morton, grandmother of fourteen, great-grandmother of eleven. Memorial service April 19, Brookside Cemetery, Englewood.
