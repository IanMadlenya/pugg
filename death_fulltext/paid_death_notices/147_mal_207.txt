
        MILCHMAN-Herman. On April 22, 1999, in Fort Lauderdale, Fla., 94 years old. Founder and past president of Carter, Milchman & Frank, Inc. Beloved husband of Sally. Devoted father of Arline Bassel and the late Philip, Claire and Neil Rechtweg. Caring grandfather of Larry (Wendy), Peter (Cindy), Joanne (Alan), Jay, Leslie (Brian), David and Heather. Proud great-grandfather of Jennifer, Alison, Erica, Andrew, Perri and Daniel. He was a self-made man respected by his industry and much admired and loved by his family. He will be missed. Graveside Sunday, April 25.
