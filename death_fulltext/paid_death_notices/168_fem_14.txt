
        VENIT-Dora. We mourn the loss of our friend Dora Venit who died peacefully in her sleep in Boca Raton, Florida. Wife of the late Abraham Venit, mother of James and the late Freddy. We extend condolences to James and Isolde Venit and their children, Anne and Alexander of Brussels, Belgium. She will be remembered as a teacher, counselor, scholar, parent and friend to all who knew her. David Goldfarb Leah Koenig
