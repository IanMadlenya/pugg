
        MILLER--William C. , Sr., 70, died at Stamford Hospital Thursday August 10, 2006, husband of Barbara R. Miller of Stamford, CT. He was still active as a Stock Broker with UBS of New York, NY for forty years. A Memorial Service will be held on Sunday August 13, 2006 at First Presbyterian Church, 1101 Bedford Street, Stamford, CT at 2:00 PM. Memorial donations may be made in his name to either: First Presbyterian Church, 1101 Bedford Street, Stamford, CT 06905 or the Barbara and William Miller Endowed Scholarship Fund, Drexel University, 3141 Chestnut Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104. www.cognetta.com
