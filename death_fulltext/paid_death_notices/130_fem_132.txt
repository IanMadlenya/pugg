
        ZARROW-Sadie. Dearly loved and loving wife of the late Joseph, mother of Walter and the late Marilyn, grandmother of Laura, Andrew, Gayle, Scott, and Susan, and great grandmother of Matthew, Anna, Blaire, and Danielle on Nov. 17th at the age of eighty-seven after a brief illness. She was a great lady. Contributions to the Daniel Cantor Senior Citizens Center, Sunrise, FL, will be appreciated.
