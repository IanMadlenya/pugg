
        MALTZER-Lillian. The Officers, Board and Staff of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations mourn the sudden death of a longtime Trustee and friend. She was a proud, caring Jew, who served our sacred causes with great devotion. We extend profound sympathy to her life's partner, Joseph, her children, grandchildren, greatgrandchild and all her loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President
