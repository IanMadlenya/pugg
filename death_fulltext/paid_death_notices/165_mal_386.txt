
        SOBIN-Morris. Passed away on Friday, October 27, 2000. Of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Roslyn Heights, NY. Loving husband of Doris and the late Ruth. Devoted & cherished father of Louise Sobin Hersh (Bob). Adored grandfather of Philip (Lara) and Joshua Sivin. Beloved brother of Rose Simon and Edward Sobin. Past president of Fresh Meadow Country Club. Former president of Olympic Radio and TV and Pilot Radio-TV. Former COO of Regent Sheffield Inc. Former director of Lear Siegler Inc. and Suburbia Federal Bank. He set an example of hard work, responsibility, sense of humor, zest for life, and fierce love of his family. His legacy will continue for generations to come. Interment Tuesday, October 31, at 3 PM, at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI. Memorial contributions, Sass Foundation for Medical Research, 1025 Northern Blvd. Suite 302, Roslyn, N.Y. 11576.
