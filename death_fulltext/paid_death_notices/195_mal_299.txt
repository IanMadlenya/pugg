
        RUBIN-Gary. The Officers, Board of Directors, and Staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the sudden and untimely loss of our esteemed colleague, Gary Rubin, Managing Director of UJA-Federation's Commission on the Jewish People. A professional with exceptional gifts, he was tireless in his commitment to the welfare of the Jewish people. We mourn the loss of an immensely capable man and quintessential ''mensch,'' and extend our deepest condolences to Gary's devoted wife Sheila, his children Ari, Michal, Jonah, and Bracha, his two brothers David and William, and to his entire family. Larry Zichlin President Morris W. Offit Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay Executive VP and CEO
