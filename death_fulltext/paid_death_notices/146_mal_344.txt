
        CASEY-John J. On March 16, 1999. Since 1965, teacher and later Chairman, English Department, Saint Peter's Prep, Jersey City, N.J. Graduate Xavier H.S., class of 1956, Saint John's University 1962, class of 1967 Graduate School of Arts and English Sciences, Saint John's University. Former teacher Saint Leonard's H.S., Brooklyn 1962-1965, former Professor Saint Peter's College, Jersey City, N.J. Loving brother of Patricia Lynch and Paul. Proud uncle of many nieces and nephews. Visiting Thursday, 7-9 PM, Friday 2-4 and 7-9PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 4th Ave., Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10:15 AM, Saint Patrick's Church. His request for contributions in his memory to St. Peter's Prep, or Xavier High School would be appreciated.
