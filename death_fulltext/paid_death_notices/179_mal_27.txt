
        DRACHMAN-Israel. Age 98. Died December 1, 2001, in NYC. Devoted husband of the late Mildred Terris Drachman. Beloved father and father-in-law of Harriett Goldberg and Janet and William M. Ray. Dearest grandfather of Karin Goldberg, Jonathan and Rachel Goldberg, Susannah Ray and Matthew Ray. Graveside services Monday, December 3, 2 PM, at Mt. Ararat Cemetery. Donations in his memory may be made to the NYS Talking Book Library Cultural Education Center, Empire State Plaza, Albany, NY 12230. For more information, Zion Memorial Chapel 914-381-1809.
