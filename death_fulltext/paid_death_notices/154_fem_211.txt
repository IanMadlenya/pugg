
        MacJANNET-Charlotte Blensdorf. Died November 25, in Geneva, Switzerland, age 98. As a musician, educator, camp director and conference organizer, she spent the 20th century promoting international peace and good will throughout Europe and America. With her late husband, Donald, she operated the MacJannet School and Camps in France, then transformed a ruined 11th century priory in Talloires, France, into the present European campus of Tufts University. Her idealism, energy and indefatigable optimism will forever inspire all who knew her. Friends who wish to carry on her work may send donations in her name care of The MacJannet Foundation, 200 Linden Street, Wellesley, MA 02482.
