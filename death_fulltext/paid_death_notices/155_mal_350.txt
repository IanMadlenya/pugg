
        CHENEY-David Howell. Age 95. Of Litchfield, CT., formerly of Manchester, CT. Husband of Eleanor (nee Gray) Cheney. Died Monday, December 27, 1999, at his home. Mr. Cheney was born May 20, 1904 in Manchester, son of the late Howell and Anne (nee Bunce) Cheney. He was a 1927 graduate of Yale University and went on to work in the former Cheney Brothers Silk Mills of Manchester for many years. Besides his wife Eleanor, he is survived by his son David W. Cheney, three daughters, Lydia McKiernan, Clarissa Street and Susan Ham, a sister Mary C. Hart, nine grandchildren.
