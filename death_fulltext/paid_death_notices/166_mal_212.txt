
        MEYER-Henry J., Professor Emeritus Social Work and Sociology (1957-1978), University of Michigan, died October 29. Scholar of sociological and social work theory, labor disputes, mental health and family service program evaluation, community organization, and population policy. Founded University of Michigan's Joint Doctoral Program in Social Work and Social Science, the first of its kind. Survived by his wife Suzanne, children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren.
