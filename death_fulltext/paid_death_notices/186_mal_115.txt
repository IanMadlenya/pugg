
        FINKELSTEIN-Isador H. On July 8, 2002. Beloved husband of Matilda for over 70 years, devoted father of Herbert and Lois and daughter-in-law Iris and son-in-law Morton, cherished grandfather of Amy, Stacy & Sarah and their husbands Jeffrey, Michael & Mark. Loving great grandfather of David, Daniel and Brian. He was a practicing certified public accountant for over 65 years until his retirement in 1993. From humble beginnings he became an inspiring success story. We will all miss his presence. Services today 1 PM, Gutterman's, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York.
