
        HILTON-Wendy, 71. A specialist in Baroque dance, choreographer, writer, and teacher on September 21, 2002, died in New York City after a long illness. Ms. Hilton was a faculty member of the Juilliard School of Dance, and Stanford University. A memorial service will be held on October 18, 2002, 6:15 PM at Krour Thai Restaurant, 362 8th Ave., NY, NY. Donations in her memory can be made to Jacob Perlow Hospice, 10 Nathan Perlman Place, NY, NY 10003, or Juilliard School of Dance, 60 Lincoln Center Plaza, NY, NY 10023.
