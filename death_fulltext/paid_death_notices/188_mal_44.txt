
        SEIFERT-Randolph J., of Pound Ridge, NY, on September 1st, 2002. Randolph, a graduate of Columbia College and Columbia University Law School, was an attorney first with the New Haven Railroad and later as a sole practitioner in NYC. He served in the U.S. Army during WWII and continued in the U.S. Army Reserves for over 30 years. Randolph was very active with various church organizations, including the National Council of Churches, a Trustee of the New York Annual Conference of the Methodist Church, the Church of All Nations in NYC, and a member of the Pound Ridge Community Church. For info: Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, 914-232-3033.
