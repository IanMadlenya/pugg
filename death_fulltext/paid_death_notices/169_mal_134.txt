
        GEE MAH-Steven, on February 7, 2001. Beloved husband of 53 years to Wai Lin. Loving father of Shirley, Rita, Evelyn, Betty, Steven, and Robert. Adoring grandfather of eleven. He immigrated to the US at age 12, attended Brooklyn Polytechnic University. He was an Electrical Engineer at AT&T Western Electric, now Lucent, for 33 years. Following retirement, he energetically devoted himself to community service. A cherished deacon of The First Chinese Baptist Church and a supporter of numerous community organizations. He will be deeply missed by his family and everyone he has helped over his fruitful, accomplished life of 78 years. Private service on Sunday. In lieu of flowers, a scholarship fund will be set up in the name Steven Gee Mah Memorial Foundation Inc.
