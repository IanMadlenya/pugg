
        MAGNER--Ethel. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ethel Magner, beloved mother of Marge Magner, a treasured friend and leader whose passionate commitment to our Wall Street Division profoundly impacts millions of lives in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Marge and her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
