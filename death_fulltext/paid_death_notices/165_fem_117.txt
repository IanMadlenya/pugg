
        GOMBOROW-Abraham Samuel. On Monday, October 9, of Brookline, MA. Beloved son of the late Joel & Jessie (Green) Gomborow. Dear brother of the late Emma Ehrlich and her husband Louis Ehrlich and three siblings who died at birth. Mr. Gomborow was one of the original Jewish settlers in Jerusalem. Services were held at the Levine Chapel, Brookline, Massachusetts on Tuesday, October 10. Interment followed at the Beth El Cemetery, Baker Street, West Roxbury, MA. Remembrances may be made to the Abraham S. Gomborow Trust Fund, C/O Kolbe Associates, P.O. Box 610409, Newton Highlands, MA. 02461.
