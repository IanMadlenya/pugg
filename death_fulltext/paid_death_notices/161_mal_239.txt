        MERIN-Herbert. Died June 15, 2000. Bucknell University expresses condolences to the family of Herbert L. Merin. The father of graduate Andrew J. Merin, Class of 1970, his reverence for education was the inspiration for the Herbert L. Merin Scholarship, which enables worthy students to attend Bucknell University.
