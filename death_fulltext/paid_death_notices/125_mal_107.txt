
        STONE-Irving R. Retired Executive Vice President and Treasurer of Knott Hotels Corp. Died 6/7/97, one week before his 87th birthday. Vinnie survived his wife of 55 years, Elsie, who died in Easton, MD, on 1/24/97. The Stones lived in Forest Hills and Shelter Island, NY. Survived by his stepson Warren J. McCarron. Service is private.
