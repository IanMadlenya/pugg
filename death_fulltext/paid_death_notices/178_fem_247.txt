
        ADAMS-Rebbitzen Bernice. Congregation Ohab Zedek Manhattan, records with deep sorrow the passing of Rebbitzen Bernice Adams, wife of the late Rabbi Theodore L. Adams. To her children Larry Adams and Howard Adams, Sivi Greenberg and Myril Zomick, & to all her grandchildren & great grandchildren we extend our sincerest condolences. Shiva will be observed at the Zomick residence, 1364 Milford Terrace, Teaneck, New Jersey, until Friday morning. Rabbi Allen Schwartz Murray Zucker, President
