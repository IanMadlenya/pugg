
        COHN -- Seymour. North Shore Synagogue, Syossetmourns the loss of devoted founding member and lifelong benefactor Seymour Cohn. A man of strength, determination, and vision whose commitment to Judaism prompted his more than half century of loving support to this congregation. We honor him for a life of integrity, charity, and kindness. To Marc and Melanie and Penny and Eric we extend our condolences. May the memory of the rightous always be for a blessing. Dr. Jeffrey Tulman, President Rabbi David Whiman Rabbi Eric Eisenkramer Cantor Renee Coleson Cantor Kathy Barr
