
        WISE--David J., 94. Former producer of the ''Message of Israel'' radio program died on July 18, 2004. Beloved husband of Mildred, father of Jonathan and Jeremy, fatherin-law of Lesly and Randi, adored grandfather of Jeffrey, Todd, Daniel and Elizabeth, devoted brother of Joan Kaufman. Mr. Wise was the son of Rabbi Jonah B. Wise and a grandson of Rabbi Isaac Mayer Wise. Funeral services, Wednesday, July 21, 10 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mount Vernon.
        WISE--David. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member David Wise. As son of the beloved Rabbi Jonah B. Wise, David's connection to our congregation has been enduring and profound. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mildred and her entire family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred Youngwood, President
