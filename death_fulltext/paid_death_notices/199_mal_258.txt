
        STEMP--Isay (Sy). Passed away suddenly on August 19, 2003. Beloved husband of 53 years to Naomi Kramer Stemp; devoted father to Sarah, Ruth, Leo, and Morris. Loving grandfather to his eight grandchildren. Born in Russia, raised in Cuba, he came to the U.S; graduated from M.I.T. and went on to work in finance. He had a passionate searching intellect and boundless energy for helping others. He was a master teacher at the American Management Association for many years. He was a founding father of the Young Israel of West Hempstead, where his influence and generosity of spirit will always be remembered. He will be greatly missed.
