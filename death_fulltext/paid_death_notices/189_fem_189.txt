
        BRAVERMAN-Dr. Miriam Ruth Gutman. Of New York City, on October 21st, 2002. Loving sister of Jeanette Bernstein, loving mother of Tommy, cherished grandmother of Matthew Harry and Joel David. Miriam was the former wife of the late Harry Braverman. A professor at Columbia University's School of Library Science, Miriam was for more than 50 years a tireless crusader for social justice. Miriam is mourned by her family and countless friends. Memorial at International House, 500 Riverside Dr., Saturday, November 9, at 2 PM. Donations in Miriam's name may be made to the Progressive Librarians Guild, P.O. Box 2203, New York, New York 10108.
