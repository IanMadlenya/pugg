
        SALZER-Richard L. 80, died October 19, 2002. Adoring husband of Betty Straus. Beloved father of Richard L. (Linda), and Peggy LoCastro (Thomas). Proud and loving grandfather of Debra, Eric, Caryn and Scott. Trustee of the White Plains Hospital and the Jewish Home and Hospital. Funeral service today, 11 AM, at Congregation EmanuEl of Westchester, Westcheseter Ave, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the Anatol & Arthur Charitable Foundation, c/o of Dr. Dan Costin, 2 Longview Avenue, White Plains, NY 10601; or White Plains Hospital Senior Services.
        SALZER - Richard. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member and former Board member Richard Salzer. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest sympathy to his wife Betty and to all of their family. Jacqueline Bellsey, President Dale Winston, Secretary
