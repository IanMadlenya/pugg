
        KATZ--Dolores (Krant). At home in New York City on August 5, 2004. Beloved wife of Bernard, mother of Marian Katz of LA and James Katz of NYC, and sister of Dr. Melvin Krant of Brookline, MA. A social worker in the NYC public school system until her retirement in June 2003, she impressed those who knew her with her intelligence, compassion, and professionalism. A patient wife and mother whose love knew no bounds. ''She had a sweet spirit.'' In lieu of flowers, please honor her memory with a contribution to the charity of your choice.
