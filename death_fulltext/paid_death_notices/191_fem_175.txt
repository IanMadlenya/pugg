
        BOTWINICK-Bessie nee Kaplan. Died peacefully last night, December 17th, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Benjamin Botwinick. Loving mother of Edward Botwinick and Elaine Wolfensohn. Cherished grandmother of Sara, Naomi, Adam, Andrew and Eric. She will be remembered for her devotion to her husband Benjamin during sixtysix years of marriage. Her family and friends will miss her intelligence, wisdom, generosity of spirit, quick wit and ebullience. Service will be on Thursday, December 19, 2PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, a fund will be set up in her name at UJA.
        BOTWINICK - Bessie.         BOTWINICK - Bessie. The Carnegie Hall family mourns the loss of Bessie Botwinick, whose grace and laughter warmed the world around her, and whose sense of education and society improved life for us all. Her long and full life was a blessing, and our thoughts and prayers are with Edward, Elaine, Jim, Sara, Adam and Naomi. Sanford I. Weill, Chairman Robert Harth Executive & Artistic Director
        BOTWINICK-Bessie. Congregation Or Zarua mourns the death of our member, Bessie Botwinick. May He who consoles the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem bring comfort to her family. Dr. Harlan J. Wechsler, Rabbi Daniel J. Beller, President
