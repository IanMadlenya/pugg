
        LEEDS-Milton. Of 366D Heritage Village, Southbury, CT. Died Monday September 21. Born November 14, 1909 in East Orange NJ, son of the late Louis and Blanche Schuhl. He went directly into the securities business after graduating high school, and for forty years worked at Pershing & Co. Stockbrokers on Wall Street, retiring in 1971 as a partner. He was Past President of the Association of Customers Brokers and also served as President of the National Association of Investment Brokers. He is survived by a couson, Judy Hertzberg of PA. Memorial service to be held at a later date. Burial in the Laurel Hill Cemetery in Brookfield, CT.
