
        ARONOWITZ-Lydia. 80, passed away on September 29, 2001. Who brought joy to so many lives, both young and old. Those who knew her for merely hours or a lifetime were always drawn to her love of life, warmth, spirit and sense of humor. She had friends all over the world. Her kindness, generosity, strength, dignity, love of learning, enthusiasm, sense of fun and laughter, curiosity and sparkle were an inspiration to all who knew her. Her love, joy and devotion to her children, grandchildren, in-laws, nieces and nephews, relatives, friends and neighbors were legend. She was the devoted and everloving wife of the deceased George Aronowitz. Loving mother of Merrill Stone and Stephen Aronowitz. Devoted mother-in-law of Andrew Stone and Julie Aronowitz. Adoring grandmother of Adam and Todd Stone and Jordan Aronowitz. She was an active member of Womens American ORT in Lake Success, NY, and the Great Neck Chapter of B'nai Brith. She was a member and resident of the Fountains Country Club in Lake Worth, FL. Beloved member of the Pelham NY Community. She was a graduate of City College of New York. Services Monday, 9 A.M. at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers contribitions may be made in her name to Women's Amerian ORT. She will live in our hearts forever.
