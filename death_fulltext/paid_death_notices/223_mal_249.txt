
        BORGENICHT--Jacob Moses ''Jack'', 93. August 29, 1911August 25, 2005. Long Valley, New Jersey Land Preservation Activist, mountain climber, manufacturer of children's clothing, and businessman. Survived by his wife Fran; daughters Jan Schwartz, Berta Kerr, Lois , Nicole, Carrie and Ruth Borgenicht; sons Stewart Gilbert, Daniel and Yoel Borgenicht; stepchildren David Bennett and Jennifer Bennett Karns; 16 grandchildren; and six great-grandchildren. The family will be holding a public celebration of his life on Sunday, August 28, at 1:30PM, at 392 Fairview Avenue, Long Valley, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Washington Township Land Trust of Morris County, Inc., P.O. Box 4, Long Valley, NJ 07853, for the Obadiah La Tourette Mill restoration; or to the Long Valley First Aid Squad, P.O. Box 111, Long Valley, NJ 07853.
