
        NOMBURG-Harry. Born in Coburg, Germany, on November 17, 1923, he was sent with a ''Kindertransport'' to Scotland in May 1939. As a member of a British Commando Unit, he participated in the D-Day invasion of Normandy and fought Nazi Germany until its collapse. In 1948 he came to the US where he served as a Paratrooper with the 82nd Airborne Division. He was demobilized in 1952 and worked for the US Postal Service for forty-two years. A friendly and generous man, he was always mindful of family ties and was very helpful throughout his life to his many friends scattered all over the world. He bettered the lives of all he touched and he will always live in our hearts.
