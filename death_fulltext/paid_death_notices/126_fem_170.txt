
        ROBINSON-Mrs. Anne I. (Mrs. Guy Norman Robinson) of New Canaan, CT. The Board of Directors of Youth Foundation, Inc. deeply regret the passing on July 18, 1997 of our former President (1976-1990), Mrs. Guy Norman Robinson. She was a stalwart force for good and the finest kind of New York volunteer. Patricia P. Fulweiler, President Edward F.L. Bruen, Vice President Henry Middendorf, Jr., Secreta S. Scott Nicholls, Jr., Treasurer
