
        BAMBERGER-Mickey (Roselle). In her 91st year after a long illness. Widow of the late L. Richard Bamberger. Served with distinction during WWII with the American Red Cross in China, Burma, India, New Foundland, Japan and Germany. She will always be remembered by loving nieces and nephews, Rosalind Miller, Muriel Traubner, Harvey Levine, Robert Wallstein, and many grand nieces and grand nephews. At her request, there will be no funeral service. Her ashes will be scattered at sea. Contributions in her memory may be made to Memorial SloanKettering Hospital. For further information, please contact Frank E. Campbell.
