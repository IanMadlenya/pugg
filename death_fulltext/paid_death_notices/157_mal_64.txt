
        REISIG-Joseph E. It is with deep sorrow and affection that we record the passing January 28, 2000 of our devoted member and past president (1993) of The General Society of Mechanics & Tradesmen of The City of New York. For many years, Joseph served on various committees and taught in Mechanics Institute, The School, supported by The General Society. Joseph joined the Society in 1982. Sincere condolences are sent to his wife Winnie, son Richard, daughters Winnie and Janice, daughter-in-law Eileen and their families. The membership of the society will miss him.
