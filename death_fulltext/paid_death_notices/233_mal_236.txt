
        HIRSCH--Leonard Coe. Age 94 years. June 19, 2006. Beloved husband of 71 years to Mary ''Pixie'' Strasser Hirsch. Loving father of Gerry and the late Roland, grandfather of Scott, Robb, and Matthew and great grandfather of Tabatha, Holliss, Ronald and Marilla. Devoted brother of the late Gerson, Alfred and Margaret Levine. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choosing.
