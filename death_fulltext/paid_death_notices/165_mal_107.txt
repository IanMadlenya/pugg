
        ALTMAN-Peter Morris. Age 60. Died October 5, 2000 in Norwalk, CT. He was a native of New York, resident of Wheaton, IL, owner of Altman Marketing & Technical Communications. Survived by his wife, Anne Hottell Altman; three daughters, two sons; his mother Rose Altman and brother Steven Altman. Funeral 2:30 PM, Wednesday, Pearson Funeral Home, Louisville, KY.
