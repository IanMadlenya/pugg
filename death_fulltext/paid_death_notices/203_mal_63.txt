
        STEVENS -- Rutherford B., M.D. Died November 27, 2003, at age 92. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Mildred; daughters Rosita Stevens Holsey and Renee Case; grandchildren Mark, Michael and Ria Aiken; and greatgrandchild Nicholas Swekosky. He was a graduate of Howard University Medical School and a Fellow of the Menninger Foundation School of Psychiatry in Topeka, KS. His career included the New York Veterans Administration; Supervising Psychiatrist, Northside Center for Child Development; Chief Psychiatrist, Children's Village, Dobbs Ferry; Clinical Director, Morningside Mental Hygiene Clinic in Harlem; and 50 years private practice. Dr. Stevens was a Distinguished Life Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association and Officer and Fellow of the American Orthopsychiatric Association.
