
        MOCCIA-Nicholas P. Mutual of America, its Board of Directors, together with all of its officers and employees, mourn the passing of our former Senior Field Vice President and respected colleague, Nick Moccia. His contributions to the strength and character of the company will be a guide for those who follow. We will deeply miss his wit, wisdom and warmth and extend our most sincere sympathy to his family. William J. Flynn, Chairman of the Board Thomas J. Moran, President & CEO Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
