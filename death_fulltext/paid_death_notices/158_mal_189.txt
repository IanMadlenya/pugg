
        RAINER-John, M.D. We are much saddened by the death of our esteemed colleague and friend, Dr. John Rainer, whose distinguished career at Psychiatric Institute spanned almost fifty years. From his psychiatric residency here through his many years as Director of Medical Genetics, where he made many contributions to studies of Schizophrenia and ManicDepressive illness, to his pioneering studies in the treatment of the mentally ill deaf and his service as Co-Chairman of the Institutional Review Board, he was an inspiration to many. We will miss him. John D. Oldham, MD, Director NYS Psychiatric Institute
        RAINER-John D., M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni express profound sorrow at the loss of our esteemed colleague and friend. He will be missed. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family.
