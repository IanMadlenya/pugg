
        EPSTEIN-Milton. On December 16. Beloved husband of the late Hadassah Spira; son of the late Hannah and Leibel Epstein. He is survived by many nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. He was the former proprietor of Ideal Bookstore, manager of Hadassah and an avid painter. His paintings hang at the Hebrew Home of Riverdale and Jewish Home and Hospital, 106th Street, NYC. His paintings are treasured by all who possess them. Graveside service Friday 1PM at Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, N.J.
