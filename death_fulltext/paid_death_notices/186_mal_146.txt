
        CASTELLINI-John. Age 96, died in Cincinnati on July 12, 2002. Beloved colleague, mentor, teacher and Professor Emeritus of Queens College, CUNY for over 30 years. Noted composer (student of Schoenberg and Respighi) and author. Among the founders of the music program at Queens College and founding conductor of the Queens College Choral Society. Conductor and teacher to generations of students, including many retired and current faculty members of the Aaron Copland School of Music, at Queens. He was a musical and personal example to generations of students who will cherish his memory. Contributions may be made to the Queens College Foundation for the John Castellini Endowment, Queens College, Kissena Blvd, Flushing, NY 11367.
