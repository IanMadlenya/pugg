
        MEYEROWITZ--Claire. Died on September 11, 2006. A graduate of Cornell NYH School of Nursing. She retired from NY Hospital Cornell Medical Center after thirtyseven years. She is survived by her two sisters, Louise Merrim and Lenore Lebhar, and many loving nieces and nephews. Family will receive friends at ''The Riverside'' on Thursday, 2-4 P.M., 76th and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to UJA, or a charity of one's choice.
