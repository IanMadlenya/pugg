
        WEINER-Eugene. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of our longtime colleague Eugene Weiner, first managing director of our Commission on the Jewish People and role model and mentor for a generation of leaders in Jewish community service. We extend our deepest sympathies to Gene's wife, Anita; to their sons, David and Danny; and to the entire Weiner family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        WEINER - Eugene. The Board of Directors, Officers, Staff and friends of The Abraham Fund Initiatives mourn the passing of our friend and co-founder, Eugene Weiner. A true humanitarian and coexistence pioneer, Dr. Weiner's insights, leadership and scholarship helped birth our organization. His perseverance, warmth, dedication and enthusiasm will be greatly missed. We dedicate our future efforts to his memory. Alan B. Slifka, Chairman Judith Eigen Sarna, President Dan Pattir, Exec. VP/Israel
        WEINER-Eugene. The entire Heschel community is saddened by the death of Rabbi Eugene Weiner, founding Trustee of The Abraham Joshua Heschel School. He inspired all who came into his midst. Our hearts go out to his wife Anita, children Danny and Mindy, David and Sonia, wonderful grandchildren, and beloved family, colleagues and friends. Shira Nadich Levin, President Roanna Shorofsky, Head of School
