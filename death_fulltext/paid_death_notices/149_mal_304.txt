
        McRAE-Frederic A. Age 77, formerly of New York City and Sanibel Island, FL, died June 17 in Fort Myers, FL. A graduate of Dartmouth College, he served in the U.S. Army Air Force in World War II in China and was production manager for William Morrow Publishing for many years. Donations may be made in his name to Hope Hospice, 9470 Health Park Circle, Fort Myers, FL 33908.
