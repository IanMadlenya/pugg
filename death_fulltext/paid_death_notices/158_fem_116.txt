
        BRAUN-Stella. Longtime resident of Manhattan's East Side. Passed away peacefully on March 11. She was a beloved sister and aunt who gave tirelessly of herself to her many siblings, nieces and nephews. For over twenty years, she was the owner of a fashionable women's dress shop on Madison Avenue and 84th Street. She designed entire wardrobes for a large and devoted clientele. Stella was deeply loved and she will be deeply missed.
