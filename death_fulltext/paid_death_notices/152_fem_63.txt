
        CIFU-Rosa Schwarz. Age 64. Of Wilmington, North Carolina, formerly of Syosset, New York. Died September 5th, 1999. Beloved wife of John L. Cifu for over 43 years. Devoted mother of John, David & Douglas. Caring mother-in-law of Annmarie, Ingrid and Melissa. Loving grandmother of Amanda, James, Brie and Belle. Cherished sister of Anne Petix. She was a registered nurse and compassionate caregiver to others for 25 years. Her love and compassion will be deeply missed.
