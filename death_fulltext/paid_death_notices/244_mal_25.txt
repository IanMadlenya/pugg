
        GOETZ--Alvin Charles, III. The Irondale Ensemble mourns the loss of our dear friend and former trustee, A. Charles Goetz III, 56, of Greenwich, CT, who died suddenly on April 16. He taught physical science at Warren G. Harding High School in Bridgeport, CT. We'll miss you Charlie. Charles was born in Toronto and is survived by his wife Eleanor; his mother, Dorothea Goetz; his sister Laura Goetz; his brother Nicholas Goetz; his niece and nephew, Evan and Lauren Goetz. A Memorial service will be held at United Congregational Church, 275 Richards Ave, Norwalk, CT, on Saturday, May 12 at 1:00 pm.
