
        EIDINOFF--Dr. Maxwell L., on March 9, 2004, of New Hyde Park, NY and Coconut Creek, FL. Devoted and cherished husband of Bea for 65 years. Loving father of Lois and Bernard Weinstein and Enid and Richard Rizzo. Brilliant professor, researcher, author and lecturer. He was respected and loved by all those privileged to know him. He will be sorely missed.
