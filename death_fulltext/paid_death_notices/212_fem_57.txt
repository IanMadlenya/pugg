
        BERLINGER--Rhonie Hofheimer, peacefully at home on August 31, 2004 after 89 vigorous years. Loving and beloved wife of the late George F. Berlinger. Devoted mother of Nancy Klee Stone & Mary Ellen Klee. Admiring mother-in-law of Bob Stone. Dear sister of Marion H. Levy & the late Lt. Lester Hofheimer. Proud ''Noni'' of Peter and Betsy, Robert and Douglas Kempner; Jim and Janet, Ellen and Julia Kempner; Carrie & Matthew, Brian & Daniel Getz. Rhonie actively served as a VicePresident of the National Alzheimer's Association since her husband's death in 1986. Funeral private. A memorial service and celebration of her life will be held Sunday, September 12 at 4 PM at the Community Unitarian Church, 468 Rosedale Avenue, White Plains, New York, 914-946-1660. In lieu of flowers the family would greatly appreciate contributions in Rhonie's memory to Hospice and Palliative Care of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601 and Alzheimer's Association, 225 N. Michigan Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60601; or a charity of your choice.
