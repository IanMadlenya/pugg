
        McVEIGH-Charles S., Jr, Esq. On behalf of the entire Mount Sinai/ NYU Health Community, we note with great sorrow the passing of long-time Trustee and friend, Charles S. McVeigh. Mr. McVeigh was elected to The NYU Medical Center Board of Trustees in 1962. He served with distinction as Secretary of The Board and most recently as a Life Trustee of The Mount Sinai/NYU Board of Trustees. We extend our sincerest condolences to his family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman, Board of Trustees Mount Sinai/NYU Health Barry Freedman, Acting President & CEO Mount Sinai/NYU Health Theresa Bischoff, President, NYU Medical Center
