
        GEDZELMAN-Bert. MAKOR and Jewish Life Network mourn the passing of Bert Gedzelman, beloved father of MAKOR's Co-Director, Rabbi David Gedzelman. He was a caring Jew and a compassionate person. Bert had a profound impact on his children and all who knew him. May the family be comforted with all the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Michael H. Steinhardt, Chairman Rabbi Irving Greenberg, President Jonathan J. Greenberg, Exec. Dir. Donna Lieberman, Co-Director
        GEDZELMAN-Bertram. Of Chestnut Ridge, New York on Monday, December 6, 1999. Beloved husband of Malvena. Loving father of Richard, David and Michael. Devoted father-in-law of Helen and Cheryl. Cherished grandfather of Rachael & Emily. Dear brother of Jesse, Ray and the late Milton. A man of extraordinary warmth, compassion and integrity. Funeral service Wednesday, December 8, 1999 at 12pm at Hellman Memorial Chapels, Spring Valley, New York. Shiva at the family home in Chestnut Ridge. Donations to United Hospice of Rockland County or Makor: The Partnership for Jewish Life.
