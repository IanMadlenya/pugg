
        TAPLEY-Donald F. M.D. Friends and colleagues at the Columbia University Stroud Center for Study of Quality of Life in Health and Aging express their deep sorrow at the loss of their long standing guide and mentor. He was instrumental in the formation of this Center and, in his quiet and gentle way, nurtured and secured the pursuit of our mission. We will all miss him and we offer our sincere condolences to his family.
        TAPLEY-Donald F. The faculty, students, alumni and staff of Columbia's Mailman School of Public Health offer deepest sympathy to the family of Dr. Donald Tapley on the loss of a dear husband, father and grandfather.
