
        LEAL-Jose Carlos on June 23, 1999 at age 79. Retired ship owner of Rio de Janiero and New York. Died peacefully from complications of a stroke. He is survived by his beloved wife Judi, his loving granddaughter Julia Santos, stepson Steven Fox and a daughter and two sons from Rio de Janiero. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday, June 29, from 7-9 P.M., with a memorial service on Wednesday, June 30 at 4:00 PM. Interment private.
