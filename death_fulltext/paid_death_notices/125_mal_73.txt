
        LEHNER-Daniel A. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff, Employees, Auxilians and Volunteers at Franklin Hospital Medical Center together mourn with profound sorrow the loss of Daniel A. Lehner. Chairman of the hospital's Ben Franklin Club and generous Franklin Hospital benefactor, his never-failing contributions to the quality of life in our community will be sorely missed. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife, Remy, a Franklin Trustee, and to his children and the entire Lehner and ''In Flight'' families. Justice Howard E. Levitt, Chairman, Board of Trustees Franklin Hospital Medical Center
