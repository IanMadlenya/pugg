
        PERLMUTTER--Abraham S., adored father of Ilisse and Shelley, died April 4, 2007, at age 87. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Orangeburg, NY. He was a founding member and former President of the Orangetown Jewish Center. Beloved husband of the late Pearl. Service was April 6, 2007. Donations to Orangetown Jewish Center or American Heart Association.
