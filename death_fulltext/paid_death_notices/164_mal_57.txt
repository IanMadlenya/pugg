
        LIPTON-Allen A. Died September 3, 2000. He was 83. He was born February 12, 1917, in Brooklyn, to Hyman and Mary Lipkowitz. He leaves his wife Dorothea (nee Glaser), son Leonard of Bethel, CT, daughters Linda Bergeron of Discovery Bay, CA, Lois Sheppard of Seal Beach, CA, his sister Sally Kligfield of Rockville, MD, granddaughter Nichole Ellyson, and great-granddaughter Emily Ellyson. Al, an avid sailor all his life, enjoyed sailing on the Long Island Sound. He was a Boy Scout troop leader, as well as being active in the Coast Guard Auxiliary and Power Squadron, Orienta Yacht Club, and a past Commodore of Sheldrake Yacht Club. The family would be honored if contributions were made to the Long Island Sound Keeper.
