
        MOONEY-Katherine. Suddenly on August 23, 2002. Ms. Mooney was the daughter of the late Daniel and Florence Mooney, and proudly the God-child of the late Governor Aldred E. Smith. She was a graduate of The Convent of the Sacred Heart at Manhattanville College and a longtime executive at Merrill Lynch. She was pre-deceased by her brother Daniel Mooney and her sisters Florence Halleran, Rose Ann Curry and Helen Gretsch. She is survived and loved by many nieces and nephews of three generations. Friends are welcomed at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, Madison Avenue and 81st St, Monday, August 26, 2-4 PM, and 7-9 PM. Requiem Mass at St. Ignatius Loyola, Tuesday, August 27, 11 AM.
