
        ABRAMS--Emily Thorburn, of Pittsburgh, PA, formerly of Brookside, NJ, passed away on Saturday, October 25th, 2003. Emily is the widow of Lawrence B. Abrams, Jr. She is survived by her brother, Grant Thorburn, of Florida, and her four children, Lawrence B. III of Camp Hill, PA, Martha Broderick, of Mt. Lebanon, PA, Priscilla Hays, of Boardman, OH and Grant, of New Paltz, NY, and eleven grandchildren. On Saturday, November 15, there will be a memorial service at 11:00 a.m. at Holy Cross Episcopal Church, in North Plainfield, NJ. Interment will follow at Rosedale Cemetery, West Orange, NJ.
