
        AMBES-Emmy. 1911-2001. Loving wife of 53 years of the previously deceased George Ambes and cherished cousin to Gerty, Mickaela, Rutie, Ron, Robert and Deborah. Valued friend to Rosa Chinchilla and many others. A bright, witty and charitable woman who had a love and deep understanding of classical music, art and literature. She was devoted to her family and country. Funeral services were held yesterday. Donations can be directed to The Jewish Community Center Association of North America.
