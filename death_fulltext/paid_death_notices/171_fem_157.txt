
        NEIMAN-Ada (nee Epstein) 85, on April 17, 2001. Beloved wife of 62 years to Phil. Loving mother of Steve Neiman and Micki Friedman and the mother in law of Robert G. Friedman. Devoted Grandmother of Deborah and Matthew. Ada was the last surviving sibling of five brothers. She leaves many loving and devoted nieces and nephews. A heart is not only judged by how much you love... but also by how much you are loved by others. Her warmth, sweetness and loving disposition will be remembered always.
