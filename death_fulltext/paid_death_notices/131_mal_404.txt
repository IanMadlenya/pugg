
        NABRIT-Dr. James M., Jr. President Emeritus of Howard Univeristy, Saturday, December 27, 1997. Beloved father of James M. Nabrit, III. Also surviving are his daughter-in-law R. Jacquelynn Harlan Nabrit, brothers Dr. Samuel Milton Nabrit and Reverend Henry Clarke Nabrit, sisters Gertrude Nabrit Thomas, Lois Nabrit Clark, and Cecelia Nabrit Atkins, nieces, nephews, other relatives and many friends. On Friday, from 5 to 7 PM friends may call at Andrew Rankin Memorial Chapel, Howard University, Washington, DC, where services will be held at 11 A.M., Saturday, January 3. Interment Lincoln Memorial Cemetery, Suitland, MD. Contributions in his memory may be made to Howard University, 2400 6th Street, N.W., Washington, DC 20059; or NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, Inc., 99 Hudson St., New York, NY 10013.
