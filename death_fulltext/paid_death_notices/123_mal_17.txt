
        DAVIS-Russell W. Age 36. Died peacefully in Seattle on March 31, after a brave struggle with aids. He is survived by his parents, Orrin and Katherine Davis of Corner Rock, Minnesota, nine siblings, his sweetheart Robert Pitre, a large circle of close friends and his dog Maurice. His beauty and brilliance inspired all who knew him. Contributions may be made in his name to: Planned Parenthood.
