
        EMMET--Diana L., 40, on March 8th, 2006. Born in Paris, France and a longtime New Yorker. A graduate of the Professional Children's School, Ms. Emmet had been a competitive figure skater and was until recently a skating coach at Wollman Rink. Diana was also a well-loved speaker for Love Heals. She had scores of devoted friends who visited frequently during her short illness. She is survived by her mother, Margaret Louise Emmet, her sisters, Claudia (Tina) Williams and Daphne Denat, her loving partner, Scott Wynn, and six nieces and nephews. Visiting hours will be Sunday, March 12th, and Monday, March 13th, from 4 to 6 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. A funeral mass will be held on Tuesday, March 14th, at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave. at 66th St. Contributions in her memory may be made to Love Heals, the Professional Children's School or Rivington House.
