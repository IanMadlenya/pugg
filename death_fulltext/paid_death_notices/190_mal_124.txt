
        SAPORITA-Thomas E. Of New York City, 45, on November 7, 2002, after a long illness. Beloved son of Jean Saporita of Rockville Centre, NY, and John Saporita of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. Loving brother of Patricia Carman and Donna Saporita. Dear brother-inlaw of Jack Carman. Devoted uncle of Jackie, Jamie and Tommy Carman. Thomas enriched all lives he touched and was well loved by many friends. Memorial to be held November 13, 2002, at St. Agnes Cathedral, Rockville Centre. New York City memorial to be announced. In lieu of flowers donations can be made in Tom's memory to Dignity New York, POB 1554, FDR Station, NY, NY 10150; or Chaminade Development Fund, 340 Jackson Avenue, Mineola, NY 11501.
        SAPORITA-Thom. Activist, critic, leatherman, organizer, wit, devoted family man, radical Christian and queen. New York is duller without him. ''Buona Notte, Emilia.'' William Berger and family
