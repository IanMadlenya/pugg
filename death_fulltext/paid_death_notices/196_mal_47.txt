
        WELLER-Ira S. May 3, 2003. Beloved husband of Julie. Loving father of Sherry Bogen, Howard (Joan) Weller, Laurence Weller and grandson Dylan. Stepfather of John Nguyen. Devoted brother of Edith (Aaron) Morris. Adored uncle to Richard, Toby, Adrienne and Debbie. WWII veteran and former President of Weller Fabrics. Ira treasured his many friendships and always had a story to tell with a great sense of humor. He was a man who embraced life to the fullest. All who knew him will mourn his passing. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, May 6, at 2:30 at Menorah Gardens, 9321 Memorial Park Road, West Palm Beach, FL. In loving memory.
