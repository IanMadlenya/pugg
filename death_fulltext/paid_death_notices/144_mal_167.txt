
        KINSEY-Robert S. Age 84, of Chestertown, MD, died Friday, January 8, 1999. Formerly of Tenafly, NJ, he attended Antioch College and graduated from University of Cinncinnati. He is survived by his wife, Minnie Sherrill Kinsey and a son, George H. Kinsey of Milford, NJ, and a daughter, Marion K. Comins of Larchmont, NY, and four grandchildren. He was a long-standing member of The Angler's Club of New York. Memorial services on Wednesday, January 13th, 1 PM, at St. Luke's Episcopal Church, Church Hill, MD.
