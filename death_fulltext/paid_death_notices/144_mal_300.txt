
        GALLOP-Robert. On January 16, 1999. Husband of the late Sally S. Gallop. Father of Barbara G. Gilbert and the late Richard C. Gallop. Grandfather of five and greatgrandfather of four. Companion of Helen Weymes. He was an inspiration to all who knew and loved him. He will be greatly missed. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
