
        GERSHMAN-Michael. Beloved husband of Suzy and father of Evan and Aaron, died at home in Westport, CT on January 4, 2000. Gershman died in his sleep; he was 60 years old. A graduate of Brown University, Gershman held prominent positions in the world of music and then sports. At the time of his death he was vice president of Total Sports, Inc., an electronic and print publishing firm, which is in partnership with the NFL, major league baseball, NHL and has affiliations with Sports Illustrated and NBC. Services will be held Thursday at 1:00 P.M., at the Collins Funeral Home, 92 East Ave., Norwalk, CT. The family requests that no flowers be sent and that contributions in his memory be made to the Baseball Hall of Fame, in Cooperstown, NY.
