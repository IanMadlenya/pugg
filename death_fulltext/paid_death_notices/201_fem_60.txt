
        HEILBRUN--Carolyn G. On Thursday, October 9, 2003, died suddenly at home. Age 78. Renowned feminist scholar and author, Avalon Foundation Professor in the Humanities Emeritus at Columbia University, and mystery writer under the name Amanda Cross; past president of the Modern Language Association, recipient of numerous honorary degrees, founding president of the Virginia Woolf Society. She was beloved by her husband, James Heilbrun, children, Emily, Margaret, and Robert, and grandchildren, Matteo and Penelope. A Memorial Service will be held at Fourth Universalist Society, 160 Central Park West, on Wednesday, October 15, at 4:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Kate Fansler Foundation, 151 Central Park West, New York, New York 10023.
