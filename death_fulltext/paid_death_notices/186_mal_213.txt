
        HULBERT-Richard K. Longtime resident of Rye, NY, passed away July 17, 2002. Devoted member of the Rye Presbyterian Church, where he served as Elder and Clerk of Session. Dartmouth Class of 1951. He was General Auditor and Senior Vice President of Chemical Bank in New York. Beloved husband of Deborah Telfer Hulbert, he is also survived by his daughter Katharine and son Kimball. Memorial services Saturday, August 3, 1 PM, Rye Presbyterian Church. Reception 2 PM, American Yacht Club. Donations may be made to Alzheimers Association, 919 North Michigan Ave., Suite 1100, Chicago, IL 60611.
