
        BRICKMAN-Alice. 73, of Long Boat Key, Florida and Franklin Lakes, New Jersey. Died February 27, 2001. Alice was born September 9, 1927 in Brooklyn, New York. She was a graduate of New York University, and devoted her life to her family, friends and many philanthropic endeavors. Alice was a friend, companion and wife of Ed Brickman for 55 years. She was a loving wife, mother and grandmother and is survived by her husband Ed; her daughter Carol; her sons Alan, Ira and Eli; and her grandsons Joshua, David, Jesse, Robert and Eric. Funeral services will be held March 4, 2001, Sunday, 10:30 AM at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Rt. #
