
        CAVIOR-Shirley (nee Miller) on January 30 in Boston, MA. Age 96. Beloved wife of the late Joel H. Cavior. Much loved mother of Warren J. (Mariko) Cavior, Jay M. Cavior and Dr. Stephen R. Cavior. Grandmother of Mayu and Sam. She personified in a long life the old-time Yankee values of her childhood years in Portsmouth, NH. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions to the Society for the Preservation of New England Antiquities, 141 Cambridge Street, Boston, MA 02114.
