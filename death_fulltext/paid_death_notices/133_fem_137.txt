
        YANOW-Helen Vogel. February 15, 1998, Laguna Hills, CA. Born in Bayonne, N.J. February 12, 1921, she graduated from NYU and Columbia School of Social Work serving as Hillel Director, Hunter College, in administrative positions at Metropolitan State and McLean Hospitals, Program Officer for Communicative Disorders at JDC-Israel and hospice social worker. She is mourned by her husband, Rabbi Albert, children Dvora, Jonathan, Tamar and Michael and Elisha Breton, Abigail, brother Harold Vogel of Far Rockaway. Contributions to the National Yiddish Book Center, Amherst, MA. or the Reform Temple of Leisure World, Laguna Hills, CA 92653 would be appreciated.
