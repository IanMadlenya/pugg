
        WILSON-Hon. Malcolm. The Board of Trustees and administration of New York Foundling Hospital record with sadness the passing of Malcolm Wilson, an esteemed friend and a dedicated advocate for children and families. To his family, we extend our sincere condolences. Sister Carol Barnes, Board Chairperson Michael Garber, Executive Director
        WILSON-Malcolm. Fordham University School of Law records with great sorrow the passing of Governor Malcolm Wilson, one of its most distinguished graduates, Class of 1936, and a role model extraordinaire as a citizen of New York. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Our love and prayers are with them. John D. Feerick, Dean
