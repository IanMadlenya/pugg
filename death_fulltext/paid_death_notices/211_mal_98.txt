
        ROSE--R. Peter, a member of the New York Stock Exchange for more than 50 years, died peacefully on August 7, 2004 in his beloved city of Tucson, AZ, surrounded by his family. He is survived by his wife Jeannie and her son Bradford Duval, his son Andrew, his daughters Liza Picoli, Marlene Coates, four grandchildren and his brother George. A service will be held on Thursday at 10am at St. Alban's Episcopal Church in Tucson. In lieu of flowers, please honor his memory with a contribution to The National Parkinson Foundation, Inc. 1501 NW 9th Ave, Bob Hope Rd, Miami, FL 33136-1494.
