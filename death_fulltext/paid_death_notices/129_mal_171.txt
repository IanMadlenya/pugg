
        FRETZ-Alfred F., M.D. The entire family of St. Luke'sRoosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Alfred F. Fretz, M.D., a former Senior Attending Physician in Urology who first joined our staff in 1946. He was dedicated to his patients and beloved and revered by colleagues, and will be greatly missed. To his wife, Kathleen, a longtime member of the St. Luke's auxiliary, and to his entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Ronald C. Ablow, MD President Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
