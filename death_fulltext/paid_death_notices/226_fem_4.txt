
        RAFTER--Marionelise. Of Bay Shore, NY, formerly of West Islip, NY, died at home with her family on October 30, 2005. Beloved wife of James Philbin, devoted mother to her four daughters, Anne (AJ) Schmidt, Elizabeth Rafter Cibley, Kathryn Rafter and Susan Rafter. Loving grandmother to Cyndi Asch, Cathy Poe, John Schmidt, Carol Downey, Maggie Lampasona, Casey Cousins and Chip Schmidt. She also leaves behind nine cherished great grandchildren, and many loving friends. Marionelise attended St. Lawrence University, received a B.S. from Columbia University, and graduated from the Presbyterian Hospital School of Nursing where she became a faculty member. While raising her family, she kept active in nursing through volunteer work for the Red Cross-organizing blood drives and running blood pressure screening clinics, and volunteering at Good Samaritan Hospital -- training nurses' aides and sitting on the Good Samaritan College Scholarship selection committee. She was also an active member of the Babylon Yacht Club and the Babylon Business and Professional Womens Association. Friends are invited to a memorial service to be held at Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway, West Islip on Thursday, November 3, 2005 from 11 AM -12 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Good Shepherd Hospice, 4747-20 Nesconset Highway, Port Jefferson Station, New York 11776.
