
        MULLER-EBERHARD-Hans J. One of the world's leading molecular immunologists, died March 3, 1998. Director of the Institute of Molecular Medicine for the Prevention of Human Diseases at the University of Texas-Houston, his research contribution dominated an era in immunology and biomedical research. From 1988-1995 he led the world-renowned Bernhard Nocht Institute for Tropical Medicine in Germany. He was a member of the National Academy of Sciences and Fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. He is survived by his wife, Dr. Irma Gigli-Muller-Eberhard, and daughters Kristina and Monika Muller-Eberhard. A memorial service will be held 4:00PM, Monday, March 16, at Palmer Memorial Episcopal Church, 6221 Main St. Houston, TX. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the Institute of Molecular Medicine, the University of Texas-Houston Health Science Center, 2121 W. Holcombe Blvd, Houston, TX 77030
