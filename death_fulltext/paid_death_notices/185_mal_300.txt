
        FERENCZ-Zoltan. Died at the age of 96 at his home in Southbury, Connecticut on June 24. Born in Maramarossziget, Hungary, he emigrated to the United States in 1928. He trained as a clothing designer in Vienna and owned a custom tailoring business in midtown Manhattan from 1930 until his retirement in 1971. Devoted husband of 67 years to Dolores. Loving brother of Michael and brother-in-law of Joan. Cherished father of Dr. Jonathan Ferencz and daughter-in-law Maxine, daughters Allison Morrison and the late Elizabeth Sass. Beloved grandfather to Julianne, Benjamin, Peter, Michael, Merrill, Stephanie and Armand, great-grandfather of twelve and uncle to Gerald and Karen. Graveside services at Beth-El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ at 11 am, June 26. Contributions may be made to the American Holocaust Memorial Museum, 100 Raoul Wallenberg Place SW, Washington, DC 20024-2126
