
        VAN LEUVEN--Thomas Carter, 40, died Saturday, April 7th, following a massive heart attack. He was an equity research and strategy analyst for the past 15 years. He is survived by his wife Margaretta, children Susannah and Johnny, and parents Susan and Jim Van Leuven and Barbara and John Moran. A memorial service will be held at St. Ignatius Loyola Church at 84th and Park Avenue on Thursday, April 12th, at 1:30 pm. A scholarship fund is being established in his name at Georgetown University.
