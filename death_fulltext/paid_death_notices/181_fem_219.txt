
        DAVIS-Rosalie Gallert, of Glen Ridge, NJ. Peacefully in Asheville NC, just short of 90. Adored, devoted wife of the late Hy. Loving mother of Susan Esposito and Barbara Litt. Cherished mother-inlaw of Rudy and Larry. Thrilled grandma of Jonathan and Natasha. A truly warm, gracious, dignified, loyal friend and relative. Lifelong reader and career librarian who brought us the joy of books. She will be loved and missed always.
