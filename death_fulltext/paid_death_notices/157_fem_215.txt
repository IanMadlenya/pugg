        ROBERTS-Edith. The President, Chancellor, Boards of Governors and Overseers, faculty, administration and students of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion, record with profound sorrow the death of Edith Roberts, a former member of the New York Board of Overseers. A generous benefactor of the College-Institute and a committed Reform Jew, Edith Roberts was a source of inspiration as she devoted herself to the mission of HUC-JIR. We mourn the loss of this courageous and loyal woman and are grateful for her qualities of mind and heart. We shall not see her kind again. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her beloved son Richard and daughterin-law Arlene and her extended family. Her memory will be for a blessing beause she was a blessing to so many. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman President Dr. Alfred Gottschalk, Chancellor Dr. Paul M. Steinberg Special Assistant to the President Mr. Burton Lehman, Chair Board of Governors
        ROBERTS-Edith. Congregation Emanu-El of the city of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Edith Roberts. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
