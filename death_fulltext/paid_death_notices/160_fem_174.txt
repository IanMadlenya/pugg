
        WEBER-Dorothy K. Passed away on May 18, 2000, in the arms of her loving and devoted husband of 47 years. Dorie was born on August 16, 1925, in NYC. She was a graduate of Hunter High School and Hunter College where she earned her BS degree. She went on to earn advanced degrees in English and Library Science at the University of Connecticut and CUNY, respectively. Her distinguished career included classroom teaching, and numerous supervisory and administrative positions in the New York City School System. Dorie chose to make her contribution to the underprivileged youth of New York. William P. Weber survives his beloved ''Shane,'' the love of his life. He acknowledges she was a woman of elegance, grace and style. Her brother, Donald F. Kauer of Honolulu, loving nieces and nephews also survive Dorie. In lieu of flowers memorial donation to a charity of choice.
