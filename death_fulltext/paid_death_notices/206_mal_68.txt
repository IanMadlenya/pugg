
        GREENSPAN--Dr. Richard Henry died on February 28th. He had been Professor of Radiology and Chairman of the Radiology Department at Yale University School of Medicine for many years and retired in 1999. He was a greatly respected teacher and mentor, the recipient of numerous teaching awards. An accomplished violinist, he passionately supported the Neighborhood Music School in New Haven, CT. He is survived by his wife Lydia, his four children, and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held for Dr. Greenspan in early summer. Donations in his memory may be made to the Scholarship Fund of Neighborhood Music School, 100 Audubon St, New Haven, CT 06510.
