
        LADD--Anne Murray. June 24, 2003. A consultant with Price Waterhouse Coopers, Anne was a 1998 honors graduate of Wellesley College. A native San Franciscan, born December 12, 1975. Anne lived her life fully, with energy, passion and thought. Her loving and compassionate spirit will be with us always. Memorial Service, Chemists' Club, 40 W. 45 St, July 13, 1:00 PM. Donations may be made to Anne Murray Ladd Fund at either Aim High, 755 Ocean Ave, SF, CA. 94112, or Wellesley Center for Research on Women, 106 Central Street, Wellesley, MA. 02481. >>AD#
