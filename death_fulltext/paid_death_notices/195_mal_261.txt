
        CAHILL-Anne B. Age 87, of Tappan, New York, formerly of Brooklyn, on Thursday, April 24, 2003. Devoted sister of Michael Cahill and his wife Anna. Also survived by several nieces and nephews. Before retiring Ms. Cahill was a clerk for the New York Times and was a member of the Newspaper Guild of New York. Funeral Mass Monday at 10:00 AM, Sacred Heart Church, Tappan, New York. Interment Calvary Cemetery, Queens. Visiting hours are on Saturday 7-9 PM and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Anthony R. Pizzi Funeral Home, 120 Paris Avenue, Northvale, New Jersey, (201-767-3050).
