
        HOUGHTON-Donald E., 84. Formerly of Mattituck, Long Island, NY, died Sunday. Born in Brooklyn, NY, he was the widower of Helen Evensen Houghton. He was a retired architect for Ebasco Services, NYC. Memorial services will be held 2:00 PM September 15 in the Mattituck Presbyterian Church, 12605 Main Rd., Mattituck, NY. Memorial contributions to Southold Historical Society, PO Box 1, Southold, New York 11971.
