
        ACKELL-Joseph J. The partners, associates and staff of the firm of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison express profound sorrow at the death of Joseph J. Ackell, Senior Vice President and Chief Legal Officer of Northville Industries. Joe was a former associate, as well as our client and beloved friend. His joyful personality and sincere interest in all of those with whom he worked will be greatly missed. We express our deepest sympathy to all members of his family.
