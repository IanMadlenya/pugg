
        Van ZANDT-Leona Thompson. On January 21. Beloved widow of Roland Van Zandt, historian and author of The Catskill Mountain House and other books. A woman of great culture, vitality, creativity and love who enriched the lives of her many friends and family. She is survived by three children: Caroline, Carla and Carter. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Women's City Club of New York City or a charity of your choice. Memorial services will be held in the Spring at a time to be announced. Private interment.
