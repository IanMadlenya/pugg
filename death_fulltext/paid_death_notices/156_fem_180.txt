
        GAUDINO-William Frank. On January 20, 2000 of Valley Stream. Beloved husband of the late Mary. Devoted father of Joyce Monaco, Jane Elson & her husband Barry. Loving grandfather of Dina Berleth, Michael Monaco and Gregory Elson. Cherished great grandfather of four. Dear brother of Mimi Trama and Anne Marino. Long time companion of Evelyn White. Member of Newspaper Guild, and the 30 Year Club of The New York Times. Retired from the New York Times after 52 years of service. Twenty Year member of The Valley Stream Lodge #
