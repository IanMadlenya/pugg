
        AUSTIN--Lisbet. On December 27, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Robert. Loving mother of Eve (Steven) Yavers. Cherished grandmother of Alana and Jared. Dear sister of the late Rose Winkler. A wonderful woman who will always be remembered by all those whose lives she touched. Services Wednesday 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. $(6$)AUSTIN--Lisbet. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Lisbet Austin, beloved mother of Eve and Day School Trustee Steven Yavers, dedicated members and founders of the Rabbi Arthur Schneier Park East Day School, grandmother of Alana and Jared. To them and the entire family our deepest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier
