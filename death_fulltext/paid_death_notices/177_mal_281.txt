
        BERKOWITZ-Paul Norman. Of the Bronx, NY, on October 19, 2001. Son of the late Louis and Fanny Berkowitz. Born in Boston, MA, on January 22, 1921, the youngest of seven children. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Edith Berkowitz and three children, Dr. Murray R. Berkowitz and his wife D'Ann L. Swan-Berkowitz of Crofton, MD, Deborah M. Berkowitz and her husband George Moskowitz of Port Washington, NY, and Lewis A. Berkowitz, Esq., of Tulsa, OK. He is also survived by his sister Jeanette Gebrowitz and six grandchildren, Aaron Berkowitz, Sarah Berkowitz, Rachel Berkowitz, Samantha Moskowitz, Jason Swan and Kyle Swan. His son George from a previous marriage predeceased him. Funeral service Sunday, 11 AM, Levayah-Garlick Funeral Home, 1091 Yonkers Ave, Yonkers, NY. Interment King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society in his name.
