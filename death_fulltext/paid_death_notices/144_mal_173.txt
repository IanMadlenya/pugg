
        SHAUL-John Melvin. On January 7th. at Lenox Hill Hospital. Dearly beloved son of the late Ray L. and Adele C. Shaul. He was 89. Former Medical Director of American Home Products. Survived by his long-time companion, Arnold James Hamilton; his sister-in-law Virginia and two nieces, Pamela Rowe and Cheryl Rose and six nephews. Services on Thursday, January 14 at 11 A.M., at All Saints Episcopal Church, 230 East 60 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
