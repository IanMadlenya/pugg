
        LUBIN--Warren, 79, of Roslyn Heights, NY (recently Whippany, NJ), on September 14, 2004. A strong, caring father and friend who led by example and made the most of everything life offered him. Will be fondly remembered by sons Mark and Gary, daughters-in-law Rhonda and Wendy, grandchildren Randy, Audrey, Julie, David and Greg, stepsons Peter and William Ente, stepdaughter-inlaw Lisa, and step-grandchildren Zachary, Sam, Jacob and Jonah. Memorial service Friday, September 17, 1 P.M. at Temple Emanu-El, Livingston, NJ, 973-992-5560. Donations to cancer research.
