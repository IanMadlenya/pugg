
        CAMPBELL-Kay. A resident of Larchmont, NY for 30 years. Died Monday, Nov. 29, in Washington, D.C. She was predeceased by her husband, Douglas Campbell. She is survived by her daughters Jean Campbell Barquin of Bethesda, MD, Margaret Campbell Robison of Westport, CT, & a son, Douglas Campbell, Jr. of Armonk, NY, and 6 grandchildren. Memorial service will be held December 3, at 11AM at St. John's Episcopal Church, Fountain Square, Larchmont, New York. In lieu of flowers please send donations to Ingleside at Rock Creek for The Supporting Fund, 3050 Military Road NW, Washington, D.C. 20015.
