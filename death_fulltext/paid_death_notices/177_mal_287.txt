
        LAWRENCE-Alexander K., 89. Of Meadowood at Worcester in Lansdale, PA and formerly of Chestnut Hill, PA died Oct. 12, 2001. Mr. Lawrence grew up in Cranford, NJ, graduated from Colgate University and attended Harvard Law School. Mr. Lawrence is survived by his son Richard S. Lawrence, daughter Mary Lawrence Strange, long time fiancee Sara ''Sally'' Graff Cooke, brother Solon ''Lou'' L.N. Lawrence, sister Marie Rollow, brother-in-law Carl H. Tiedemann of New York City and five grandchildren. Services will be held at St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Chestnut Hill, PA on Friday, November 16, 2001 at 11:00 A.M.
