
        DAFFNER-Beatrice Ivry Ernst. Age 95. On January 11, 2000. Loving mother and mother-in-law of Naomi and Stuart Paley, Judith and Victor Linn and Howard Ernst. Devoted sister of Billie Ivry. Dear grandmother of Adam and Rachel, Jane and Andrew, Gregory and Lisa and Jason. Greatgrandmother of Jeffrey, Emily and Zachary Linn and Hannah Paley. Wife of Phillip and the late Abraham Ernst. Service Friday 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Visiting at ''The Riverside'' Thursday 79PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to Sutton Place Chapter of Hadassah.
        DAFFNER-Beatrice Ivry Ernst. We are saddened by the death of Beatrice Daffner, cherished sister of E. Billi Ivry, a caring member of the Solomon Schechter High School of New York family. Our thoughts are with Billi and her family at this time of personal loss. May Mrs. Daffner's life inspire us to act in ways in which she would be most proud. Carol S. Kekst, Chairman, Rabbi Judd Kruger Levingston, Principal
