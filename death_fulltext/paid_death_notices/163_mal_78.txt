
        LASKY-Burton. On August 4. Beloved husband of Diana. Beloved father of Andrew. Loving son of Louis. Dear brother of Harvey and Jesse. A great man, who always did what was right. He possesed an amazing body of knowledge and a genuinely noble soul.
        LASKY-Burton. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Sr. Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
