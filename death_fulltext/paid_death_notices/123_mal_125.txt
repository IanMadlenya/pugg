
        GANNET-Seymour, on Wednesday, April 9, 1997. Seymour Gannet of Florham Park, formerly of Brooklyn, N.Y. Beloved husband of Gladys (Nee Wenderoff). Devoted father of Todd and Dean Gannet, dear brother of Herbert Gannet and Mitzie Landsberg, loving grandfather of 7 grandchildren. He was a very much loved family leader and friend and we will miss him. Funeral services conducted from the Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Rd., Union, N.J. On Friday, April 11, 1997 at 1:00 PM. Interment Beth Israel Cemetery, Cedar Knolls, N.J. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society, Northfield Ave., West Orange, N.J. 07052
