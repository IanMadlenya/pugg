
        SPECTOR--Julius Jack (JJ). Passed away, aged 86, Los Angeles, February 12. Beloved husband of the late Sylvia. Loving father of Susan Rosen (Los Angeles) and Eric Spector (Kensington, CA), devoted grandfather of Michele, Liana, Marisa, Elliot & Jackson; doting greatgrandfather of Sara, Ryan & Jakob. Bronx born. Music and opera lover. Artist. Art Director. Copywriter. Co-founder Temple B'nai Israel (Elmont NY) and FL. Direct Marketing Association. His generous and sweet soul touched so very many. Funeral Services: Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Route 4 (eastbound), Paramus, NJ (201.843.9090), Sunday February 15, 1:00 P.M.; burial following, Cedar Park Cemetery, Forest Avenue, Paramus.
