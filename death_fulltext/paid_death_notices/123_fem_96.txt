
        CHASE-Cynthia Pierce on April 13, 1997 of post polio syndrome. A scientist successful in spite of severe polio myelitis with a PhD in Microbiology from Yale University. She worked at the Rockefeller Institute for Medical Research from 1946 through 1964 in tuberculosis research. She is survived by her husband Merrill W. Chase, a sister, two nephews and two nieces. A memorial service to be announced at a later date.
