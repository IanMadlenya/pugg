
        VIGILANTE--Joseph L. The Board of Trustees of YAI/National Institute for People with Disabilities is greatly saddened by the passing of Joseph Vigilante. As a distinguished member of our Board of Trustees, Dr. Vigilante played a pivital role in promoting the rights of people with developmental and learning disabilities. He inspired a generation of professionals to provide services to our nation's most vulnerable citizens. Our deepest condolences to the entire family. Marcella C. Fava Chairperson Dr. Paul H. Levitz Vice Chairman Board of Trustees Dr. Joel M. Levy, CEO Dr. Philip H. Levy President, COO YAI/NIPD
