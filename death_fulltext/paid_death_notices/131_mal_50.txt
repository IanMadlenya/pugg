
        GILBERT-Paul. The entire staff of the Internal Medicine Associates of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the loss of Dr. Paul Gilbert, the Division Chief of the Section of General Internal Medicine, who passed away suddenly. Recognizing his foresight, creativity, and influence on Internal Medicine Associates and its patients, and remembering his warmth, guidance, and encouragement, we join in offering our condolences to Harriet and the entire family. His memory will not be forgotten. Internal Medicine Associates Staff
