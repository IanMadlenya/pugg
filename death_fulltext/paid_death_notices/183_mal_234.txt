
        KLEIN-Harvey W. Died Thursday, April 18, 2002 of lung cancer at his home in Greenwich Village, N.Y. He was 77 years old. Harvey was born December 1, 1924 in West New York, N.J. to Emil and Sylvia Klein. He saw combat in W.W. II as a Corporal in the First Division of the U.S. Army from July 1943 through March 1946. After the war, he served the Army as a reporter at the Nuremberg Trials. After many years in the embroidery business, he graduated from the New School University in 1968. He and his wife Tobey owned and operated Douglas Personnel Associates, an executive search firm, for more than 30 years. A long time resident of Bergen County in New Jersey, Harvey and Tobey had lived in Manhattan since 1990. Harvey is survived by his loving wife and his devoted children Jonathan, Andrew and Leslie, and by grandchildren Daniel, Amanda, Allison, Isabel and Anna. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Friday, April 19th, 12 Noon. The family request donations in lieu of flowers to be made to support research in the Pulmonary Division at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
