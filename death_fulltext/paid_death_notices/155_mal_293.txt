
        KRAL-William G.., 53, of Eagle Lake in the town of Ticonderoga, NY, died unexpectedly December 20, 1999. Mr. Kral was born in the Bronx, New York on October 16, 1946. He was a veteran, had his J.D. doctorate in law, and was a founding partner in the New York law firm of Kral, Clerkin, Redmond, Ryan, Perry & Girvan. Survived by his wife, Mary (Shuman) Kral; mother Eleanor Kral of San Diego, CA; 3 daughters, Marianne, Elizabeth and Emily Kral; and a brother Richard. Funeral Mass, Thursday 11 A.M., at St. Mary's Church, Ticonderoga, NY.
