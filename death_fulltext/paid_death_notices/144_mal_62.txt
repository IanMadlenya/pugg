
        GRAFF-Louis. On January 3, 1999. Beloved father of Harriet Greenfield and father-in-law of Joel. Cherished grandfather of Stuart and Mark. Services Tuesday, 12:30 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment Beth David Cemetery.
        GRAFF-Louis. The entire membership of the Young Men's Philanthropic League are saddened by the passing of our esteemed, devoted Trustee, Louis Graff. He was beloved and respected by all the people whose lives he touched. A kind, gentle, compassionate, humanitarian, who celebrated a joyous life for 95 years. He was an ardent supporter of our common cause of ''man helping man.'' A dedicated philanthropist, he was always the first to contribute to any and all charities that we supported. He was a prince among men. Our sincerest condolences to his daughter, Harriet, and family. Funeral services Tuesday January 5th, 12:30 P.M. at The Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Stanley Banks, President Stephen Dietz, Chairman
