
        ROSE-Larry. The Hebrew Educational Society, the HES Alumni and Camp HES mourns the passing of Larry Rose, an extraordinary athletic, businessman, banker, community leader and a wonderful husband, father and grandfather and dearest friend. He was a HES member for 75 years, President of HES, President of HES Alumni and member of the HES Wall of Fame. Our sympathies to his wonderful wife, children, grandchildren and friends.
