
        HOCKSTADER-Leonard A. II. The Board of Trustees of the Fred L. Lavanburg Foundation is deeply saddened by the death of our colleague and descendant of our Founder. Leonard A. Hockstader II brought compassion to the Foundation's focus on Public Housing and served as a Trustee for more than 35 years. We will miss him and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Diane and his children and his brother. Oscar S. Strauss II President
