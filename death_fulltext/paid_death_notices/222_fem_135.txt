
        BARBERICH--Elsie Jahn. On July 21, 2005 in her 97th year. Beloved wife of the late Norman. Dear sister of the late Johanna Kruger, Leah Bulfinch and Alfred Jahn. Loving aunt of Richard and Shirley Bulfinch of Oregon City, OR, Roger and Kathy Jahn of Monroe, CT, and Robert Jahn of Southington, CT. Also survived by many great nieces and nephews. Elsie worked well into her late eighties as a beautician at Georgoios in Flushing, NY. Also a 70 year resident of Queens, NY. Reposing Simonson Funeral Home, 119 - 04 Hillside Avenue, Richmond Hill, NY. Visiting hours Tuesday, 6-9 PM. Funeral Wednesday, 12 noon. Interment LI National Cemetery.
