
        MARKON-Alain Henri. Born in Toulouse, France on June 13, 1941. Died in Paris on May 31, 1998, after a long, heroic battle with cancer. Beloved son of Raya of New York City and the late Alexander. Devoted brother of Genya of Washington, D.C. Loving uncle of Dov and Yaron Yarkoni of Israel. Cherished friend to many in all corners of the world. Alain was a distinguished international attorney before the French and American courts of law. A person of rare sensitivity, wisdom and inner strength, he enriched and inspired the lives of those around him. Services Sunday, June 7th, 10:45AM at Riverside Memorial, 76 St and Amsterdam Ave.
