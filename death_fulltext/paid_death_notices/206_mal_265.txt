
        PFLUGFELDER -- John George. Of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, died March 18, 2004. He was born on November 23, 1930, in NYC. He was a graduate of the New York Military Academy and the University of Virginia. He served as a first Lieutenant in the U.S. Army during the Korean Conflict. John was a member of the American Stock Exchange for 48 years and was also a member of the 5 & 20 Club. He was a member of the board of directors of the United Way of Pelham, NY for 25 years and also served on the board of directors of the Pelham Country Club. He was a member of the Ponte Vedra Inn and Club for the past eleven years. Family members include his wife, Anne Pflugfelder; his daughter, Susan Granfors and his son, Robert Pflugfelder and grandchildren, Harald Granfors and Jack Granfors. His son, John G. Pflugfelder, Jr. predeceased him. Memorial services were held at 2:00 p.m., Saturday, March 20th in the Christ Episcopal Church of Ponte Vedra Beach. Inurnment will be in the Memorial Garden at Christ Episcopal Church. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Christ Episcopal Church, 400 San Juan Drive, Ponte Vedra Beach, FL 32082 or to the Community Hospice of Northeast Florida, 4266 Sunbeam Road, Jacksonville, FL 32257.
