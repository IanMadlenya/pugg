
        HENNEMAN-Esther Gracie. Of Buckingham County, Virginia and formerly of New York City, died December 14, 2000 at age 93. The widow of John B. Henneman and mother of the late John B. Henneman Jr. She is survived by a son Edward O. Henneman, two sisters Margaret Ogden and Athalia Barker, a brother Dayton Ogden, two daughters-in-law Penelope and Margery Henneman, six grandchildren and 3 great grandchildren. She served on the Board of Counselors of Smith College and the National Board of the YWCA and was a trustee of the Blue Ridge School and St. Anne'sBelfield School. Contributions in her memory may be made to Smith College, Northampton, MA.
