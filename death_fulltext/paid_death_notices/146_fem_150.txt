
        ENGLISHER-Dorothy. The Board of the YM/YWHA of Washington Heights and Inwood mark with great sadness the death of Dorothy Englisher, a longterm Washington Heights resident and early supporter of the Y. She introduced her son Martin to this agency and encouraged him throughout his successful leadership of the center. Her caring and compassion will be missed. Our deepest sympathy to her husband Elihu and her sons Martin and Larry. Dr. Ruth Westheimer, President
        ENGLISHER-Dorothy. The Staff of The YM/YWHA of Washington Heights and Inwood express our profound sadness at the death of Dorothy Englisher, beloved mother of our Executive Vice President, Martin G. Englisher. Our thoughts are with the family in this difficult time.
