Grandfather of David and Rosalina, Robert, and Alix. Greatgrandfather of Jacob and Isabela Mia. Services Tuesday, November 21, at 11 am, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue, at 91st Street. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Simon Wiesenthal Center.
