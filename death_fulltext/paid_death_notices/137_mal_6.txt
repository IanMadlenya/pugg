
        RICHARDS-Robert H., Sr. Of Chappaqua, NY. Died peacefully at home on May 28, 1998, surrounded by his family. He was a cherished and devoted husband, father, brother, uncle and friend. His wife Helen (Field-McNally), his children, Robert, Jr., Margaret and Alexandra, brothers Benjamin and David, sisters Ann and Elizabeth, and many nieces and nephews mourn his death. Good night, sweet prince, and flights of angels sing thee to thy rest! Mass of Christian Burial Monday, June 1, The Church of St. John and St. Mary, Chappaqua. Contributions to Hospice Care in Westchester and Putnam, 100 South Bedford Road, Mt. Kisco, NY 10549, in his name would be appreciated.
