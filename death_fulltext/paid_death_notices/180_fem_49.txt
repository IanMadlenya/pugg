
        ALBERT-Edith (LaBellman), 93. On January 3, 2002 in Hollywood, FL. Loving wife of the late Jacob G. Albert, dearest mother of Phyllis (Pinki) Wesler, Bal Harbour, FL, Allan Albert, Tampa, FL, Richard Albert, White Plains, NY, seven grandchildren and eight greatgrandchildren. A lifetime of charitable endeavors, we shall all miss her dedication, generosity and love. Interment at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, 7800 Myrtle Avenue, Brooklyn, NY, Sunday 11 AM.
