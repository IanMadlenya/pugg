
        GREENSTEIN-Steven W., 46, died peacefully and without pain after living with AIDS for seven years. Survived by partner, family and friends, his courage and dignity will remain an inspiration to us all. Contributions in his memory may be made to Congregation Beth Simchat Torah, 57 Bethune St., NY NY 10014 or to the organization of your choice.
        GREENSTEIN-Steven. Congregation Beth Simchat Torah, New York City's gay and lesbian synagogue, mourns the loss of Steven Greenstein, former treasurer and member of the Executive Committee, former member of the Board of Directors and treasured friend. He was a deeply committed Jew and an effective builder of our community. We are diminished without him. We pray that David and the family will find comfort in the many living mourners left by Steven, which honor his memory. May they be comforted among all the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Sharon Kleinbaum Yolanda Potasinski, President Congregation Beth Simchat Torah
