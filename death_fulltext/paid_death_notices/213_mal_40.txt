
        THOMPSON--Bresci. Age 96, died at home on Saturday, October 2. Husband of the late Mary Fox Thompson, beloved father of Nancy Cooper, son-in-law Arthur, grandsons Michael and Edward, great-granddaughter Sophie, and sisters Senda and Elaine. He was a painter, story teller, and lifelong Chelsea resident. He will be remembered fondly by hundreds of friends and neighbors in Chelsea for his kindness, wit and humor. A memorial will be arranged. Contributions in his name may be made to the Hudson Guild, 441 West 26th St., New York, NY 10001.
