
        HOLLANDER - Susan. On July 14, suddenly. Wife of Leonard, mother of Eric, Seth and David & his wife Wendy. Sister to Joseph and his wife Pattie. She was an administrator and devoted volunteer at various organizations including UJA and Princeton U. The funeral will be held at the Jewish Congregation of New Paltz, July 17, at 10 AM with burial at the Mount Hebron Cemetery in Queens. Contributions: the Synagogue's Capital Campaign, the Jewish Federation of Ulster County, or the New Israel Fund.
