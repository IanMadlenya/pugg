
        BEDOUKIAN-Paul Zareh. Age 89, of Wilton, CT, on Friday, June 29. Renowned chemist, author, numismatist. Beloved husband of Vicki and loving father of Joan, Betty and Robert. Services Tuesday, 11 AM, at St. Gregory's Armenian Church, White Plains, NY. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Armenian Library and Museum of America, 65 Main St, Watertown, MA 02472; or Fund for Armenian Relief (Orphan Fund), 630 2nd Ave, NYC 10016.
