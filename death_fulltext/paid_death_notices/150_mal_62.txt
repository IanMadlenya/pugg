
        DINERMAN-James On July 2, 1999. Beloved father of Alice Eve and Robert David. Treasured brother of the late Jean Berger, and the late Harold and Mortimer Dinerman. Devoted son of the late Alexander and Edna Dinerman. Cherished uncle of Ellen, Ruth and David Dinerman, and Joel and Nancy Berger, and favored brother-in-law of Miriam Dinerman. Revered longtime colleague of Marilyn Jann and Bill Levine. He was predeceased by his wife of 29 years, Helen Schneider Dinerman. Service Tuesday at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue. Contribtutions in his memory to Helen S. Dinerman Memorial Foundation, 136 E. 57 St., Rm. 505, NY NY 10022.
        DINERMAN-James. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, James Dinerman. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        DINERMAN-James. His friends and neighbors at 179 E. 70th Street express their condolences to his family. His charm and wit will be missed by all.
