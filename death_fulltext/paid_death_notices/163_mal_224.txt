
        SAMBERG-Meyer. 85, of Riverdale, NY. Died August 18. Graduate of City College, 1935. Received MBA at Columbia. Taught merchandising at Textile High School. Worked with architects and builders designing new lighting technology in the construction industry, representing universities and major new buildings. During WW II was civilian aide to the War Department in the Alaskan defense. He leaves his dear wife Sonya; daughters Ellen McCall, Karen Samberg; son Robert Samberg; grandchildren Sarah McCall (Ephron) and Jack McCall; greatgrandson Jordan McCall; sister Dorothy Shaw; nephews Ernest, Peter and Fred Shaw. Services Tuesday, August 22, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76, 10:30 AM.
