
        PLISKIN--Bertha. Age 98, died June 24, 2005 in Ormond Beach, FL. Predeceased by her devoted husband David of 69 years on October 2, 2001. She is the beloved mother of her son, Thomas Pliskin, Shrewsbury, NJ; daughter and son-in-law, Susan and Jonathan Kopald, Fort Montgomery, NY; and granddaughter, Deborah Kopald. For information: Haigh-Black Funeral Home, 386-677-0451.
