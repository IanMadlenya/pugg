
        GREENBERG--Rosalind (Geiger), on May 19th, 2005 peacefully at home at age 93. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Adored sister of the late Gertrude Silverman, Isabel Fleischer, Pearl Barnett, Howard Geiger, Muriel Jacobson, Philip Geiger. Cherished aunt and great-aunt; dear friend. Services 11:30 AM, Sunday, May 22nd, at ''The Riverside,'' Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street, New York City. Donations in her memory may be made to Hadassah.
