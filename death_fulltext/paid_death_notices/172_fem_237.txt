
        BIRNBAUM-Flora. Stricken with polio before her 26th birthday, she lived a full life, for 47 years, in a wheelchair with never-ending courage and grace. She is mourned by her husband Marvin, her daughters Sandra and Jacqueline Birnbaum and her loving grandchildren: Hilary Penney, Darren Sullivan, Joel Penney, Eric Sullivan and Margeaux Sullivan. Funeral was May 29, 2001 at Hirsch & Sons, Bronx, New York.
