
        LISMAN-Jack V. M.D. The Board of Directors, Administration and Medical Staff of Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital mourn the passsing of Dr. Jack Lisman on August 30, 1998. Dr. Lisman's prestigious career as a member of our medical staff span over 40 years starting with his appointment to the Department of Ophthalmalogy in April 1958. We extend our deepest sympathy to his entire family. Lindsay C. Herkness, III President, Board of Directors George A. Sarkar, Ph.D., J.D. Executive Director
