
        LASSMAN--Joseph, 79. Of Lake Worth, FL, formerly of Sea Gate, NY. August 28, 2005. Beloved husband of Vivian, dear father of David and Carol, cherished grandfather of Cheryl, Andrew, and Sara. He was a CPA, WW II Vet and community leader. He will be missed by his many friends, family and remembered for his kindness, generosity and good humor.
