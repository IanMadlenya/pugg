
        COLLOTON-John Edmund, 71. Died at home in Greenwich, CT on July 30, 2002. Husband of Theresa M. Colloton; father of Katharine A. Colloton, J. Edmund Colloton Jr. (Eve B.), Jeannie C. Brooks (Randall L.) and J. Andrew Colloton (Timuneka); and grandfather of Kayla and Madeline Brooks and Hailey Colloton. He was predeceased by his son Christopher, sister Conni Gilday, and brothers Richard F. Colloton and William H. Colloton. Mr. Colloton was a leading expert on investor relations and corporate disclosure during the 1970's and 1980's with St. Regis Paper Company & then his own firm, The Colloton Group. He will be forever remembered in our hearts. Funeral services will be held at 10 AM Friday, August 2, at Sts. John & Paul Church, 280 Weaver St., Larchmont, NY. Visitation will take place from 2-4 PM & 7-9 PM on Thursday, August 1 at John J. Fox Funeral Home in Larchmont, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in the name of John Edmund Colloton to the Zalmen A. Arlin Cancer Center, 19 Bradhurst Ave, Hawthorne, NY 10532.
