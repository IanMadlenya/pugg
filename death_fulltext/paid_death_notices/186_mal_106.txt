
        MORAN-James P. on June 29, 2002 in Palm Harbor, FL. Loving husband of Ann P. (nee McGuigan). Loving father of Mary M. Esposito, Nancy T. Hopkins, Eileen E. Casolaro, Michael P. and the late John J. He is survived by thirteen grandchildren. Dear brother of the late Rose B. Moran and Peter C. Moran. Born in Manhattan, he was a lifelong member of the Newspaper Pressman's Local Number 2. He was a Navy veteran of World War II and a member of the Knights of Columbus, a member of the Veterans of Foreign Wars and the United States Coast Guard Auxiliary serving as Commander of the Palm Harbor Flotilla. He was also a member of the American Legion.
