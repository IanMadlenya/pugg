
        HANDMAN-Robert. Age 81, died at his home on September 24. His life was one of courage and principle. He is survived by Rita Handman, his cherished wife of 51 years, his brother Mike Handman, his four loving children and seven adoring grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 3:30 PM on Wednesday, September 29, at the Brooklyn Society for Ethical Culture, 53 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn.
