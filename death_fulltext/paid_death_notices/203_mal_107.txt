
        McDERMOTT--Frank A. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of Frank A. McDermott, devoted friend of long standing and past Trustee. A Christian gentleman passionate about the College, he generously shared himself with us and we are stronger for it. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D. President Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU Chair, Board of Trustees
