
        GRANAT-Ruth. Ruth left us on January 18, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Stanley Granat. A bundle of energy wrapped in a butterfly's cocoon. She will be cherished and missed by her daughters Jill Handelsman and Leslie Granat, son-in-law Martin Handelsman, family and many dear friends. Services will be at 12:45 PM, on Monday, January 20, 2003, at Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, 5808 W. Atlantic Ave, Delray Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Leukemia Society of America, Inc., American Lung Association of Southeast Florida or The Children's Place at Home Safe, Inc.
