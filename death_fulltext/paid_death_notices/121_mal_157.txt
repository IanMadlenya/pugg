
        COVICI-Pascal, Jr. Of Dallas, TX, formerly of New York, died Feb. 9, survived by wife of forty-five years Joan Fortescue Covici; daughter Anna Covici Propp; son John and two grandsons. Born Sept. 2, 1930, son of late Pascal and Dorothy Soll Covici. Graduate of Harvard University, author of numerous books, scholarly articles, and a specialist in American literature. He spent his entire career at SMU where he was head of the English Dept. (1975-1979) & held the E.A. Lilly Professorship in American Literature. Memorial services to be held at SMU next month. Memorials to the Pascal Covici Prize in American Literature at SMU will be appreciated.
