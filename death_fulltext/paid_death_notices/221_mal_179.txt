
        BEATON--William (Bill), 72, passed away in Fort Lauderdale, FL, on June 20, 2005. A native New Yorker, a graduate of FIT, noted children's wear designer. He also consulted with Associated Merchandising Corp (AMC). Retired to East Hampton in 1985 and moved to Fort Lauderdale in 1989 where he was active locally in politics, the arts and Gay civil rights. Mr. Beaton is survived by his partner of 42 years, Ted Emery, a sister Judith of New York, a brother Martin of Arizona and three nieces, Ann, Gail and Merys of New York.
