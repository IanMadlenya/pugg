
        ALGER-David Dewey. Was tragically lost on 11 September 2001, at the World Trade Center. A Memorial Service to celebrate his remarkable life will be held at Saint Thomas Church, Fifth Avenue and 53rd Street in Manhattan, on Friday, 5 October 2001, at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to a worthy WTC relief fund. Gifts may also be made to the Foundation for Alger Families, a charitable foundation established to assist the families of Fred Alger Management employees lost in the attack on the WTC.
