
        SHUTTLEWORTH - Edwin. Formerly of Quogue, died on Saturday, October 29, at Peconic Landing, Greenport, NY, at 79 years of age. He was husband to Pat and father of Daphne, Teddy and Wesley and father-in-law to Gillian and Terese. He leaves behind a sister, Barbara Clemmons of Wilmington, NC. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, November 12, at 11 AM at the Church of the Atonement, Quogue. A second service will be held at Shinnecock Yacht Club, Quogue, in June, 2006. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Southampton Township Wildfowl Association, P.O. Box 492, Quogue, NY 11959.
