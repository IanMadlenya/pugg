
        WEAVER--William M., Jr., of New York City, died on November 28, 2003 at the age of 91. He is survived by his wife, Rosemary, and four daughters, Marcia Campbell and Patricia Wurts, both of Philadelphia, PA, Wendy Chaix of New York City, and Alison Weaver of Easton, CT; six grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Saint Bartholomew's Church, 50th Street and Park Avenue, New York, NY on Tuesday, December 9, 2003 at 4pm.
