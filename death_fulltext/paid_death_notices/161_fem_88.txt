
        GREENBERG - Ruth Frances, born January 3, 1934; died June 11, 2000. Daughter of world renowned tympanist Saul Goodman and award winning cellist Lillian Rehberg, she graduated from the Fieldston School of New York and attended the University of Wisconsin and Columbia. An active member in numerous ecological, political and charitable organizations, Ruth was also an ardent singer in many choirs. She enjoyed a wide variety of careers, ranging from banding hawks in the midwest to administrating art galleries in New York and Connecticut. She is survived by her twin sister, Helen Silver Henrichsen; her niece, Nini Silver; and her two sons, Matthew and Paul. A memorial service will be held at St. Barnabas Church, 954 Lake Avenue, Greenwich, CT, 06831, on Saturday, June 17 at 11:00 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions should be made to the Nature Conservancy, Connecticut Chapter, 55 High Street, Middletown, CT. 06457.
