
        MATTER-Mercedes. Died December 4, 2001, after a courageous battle with illness. The Dean, faculty, students, alumni, staff and Trustees of the New York Studio School mourn her passing. Mercedes Matter was a painter, writer, teacher, and founder and dean emeritus of the New York Studio School. Her formidable presence, her insight into the nature of art, and her brilliance as a pioneering educator will be greatly missed but always remembered. Our deepest condolences to her family.
