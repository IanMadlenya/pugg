
        GETZENDANNER - Joseph W., Jr. 89 of Pennswood Village, Newtown, PA, died on May 15, 2003. Husband of Betty Anne Forbes Getzendanner and father of the late Susan M. Getzendanner and Thomas F., Lydia Kriso and Martha Lingen. He is also survived by 12 grandchildren and a great-grandchild. Private interment services will be held at Old St. Paul's Episcopal Cemetery, Chestertown, MD. Swartz/Givnish Funeral Home, Newtown, PA 215-968-3891.
