
        REYCRAFT--George D. On March 1, 2004 in Miami, Fl. George loved the law, flying, boating and fishing, especially in the Florida Keys and Alaska. But most of all, he loved his wife, Meg Macy Reycraft, his stepchildren Mary Grace and Christian Macy, his seven children George, Tom, Thaddeus, Anna, Nancy, Pamela and Sheilah, all from his first marriage to Pamela Colville Reycraft and his ten grandchildren. Private services at Ocean Reef Club, Key Largo, Fl. on Saturday, March 6. A memorial will be held in April, in NYC. Condolences may be sent to the family of George Reycraft, 345 East 52 Street, NY, NY 10022. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Legal Aid Society.
        REYCRAFT--George D. The partnership of Cadwalader, Wickersham and Taft, LLP, mourn the passing of our former partner and friend, George D. Reycraft. A thoughtful colleague and leader, before retirement, George served as Co-Chair of the Firm and Chairman of the Litigation Department. We send our sincerest condolences to his family.
