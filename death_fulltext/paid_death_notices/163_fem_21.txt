
        ROCKEFELLER-Paula Watjen. 94, died peacefully in her sleep on August 1, 2000 at her home in Greenwich, Connecticut. She was the daughter of the late A. Wilhelm and Katie F. Watjen of Bremen, Germany and New York. Wife of the late John Sterling Rockefeller and sister of the late Eduard H. Watjen of Ascona, Switzerland. She is survived by her sister, Alice Watjen, of Zurich, Switzerland; two daughters, Christina DuBois of Stockbridge, Massachusetts and Paula Greene of Leominster and London, England; six grandchildren and one greatgrandchild. Services are to be held on Monday, August 7, 2000 at 3:00 P.M., St. Matthews Church, Bedford, New York. In lieu of flowers donations can be sent to The Mianus Gorge Preserve, 167 Mianus Road, Bedford, New York, 10506.
