
        WEINSTOCK--Edward. It is with sadness that we announce his passing on February 4, 2004, two days past his 79th birthday. CEO of Edley Associates. Husband of Hortley, father of Andrew and Regina, Debra and Neil (Gershman), grandfather of Michael, Alyssa, Ari and Rachel. Brother of Emma (Shapiro) and the late Rachel (Atlas). ''Uncle Eddie'' to many nieces and nephews. His warm spirit and caring attitude will stay in our hearts forever. He will be missed by all who knew him.
