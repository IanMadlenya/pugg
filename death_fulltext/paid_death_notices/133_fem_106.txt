
        BERNSTEIN-Sylvia, age 98 (almost 99), died peacefully on February 12, 1998 at Mount Sinai Hospital after a short illness. Beloved wife of Alfred, who died in 1977. She is survived by her son, Robert L. Bernstein (Helen) of New York City, her daughter, Barbara Rosenberg (Herman) of West Orange, New Jersey, 5 grandchildren (Peter, Tom and William Bernstein of New York City, John Rosenberg of San Francisco and Patricia Drosdowich of Warwick, New York) and 12 great-grandchildren. A native New Yorker, she was born Sylvia Bloch on March 6, 1899 and Married Alfred Bernstien on January 20, 1920, whom she was devoted to until his death. She will be much missed and always loved. Services will be held on Friday, February 13, 1:00PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81 Street. The family will be home from 4:307:30PM. Burial will be private.
