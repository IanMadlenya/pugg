
        CHERVENAK--Lawrence T. Age 80, on April 13, 2004, in Manhattan. Retired founder and president of Chervenak, Keane and Company; 1989 Inductee into the HFTP International Hospitality Technology Hall of Fame; recipient of numerous other professional awards. Distinguished philanthropist, fisherman, world traveler, Penn State football fan, uncle and guru. Survived by his partner Joyce, his sister Marci, and his brothers Bill and Jim. A Funeral Mass will be offered Thursday, April 22, 11 AM, at Holy Family Church, 315 East 47 Street. Interment will be at St. Mary's Cemetery, Portage, PA. Contributions may be made to Catholic Charities, 1011 First Ave, NYC 10022; Covenant House, P.O. Box JAF2973, NYC 10116; or Larry Chervenak/Joyce Christmas Trust, c/ o Omni Group, 3313 Spring Meadow Court, Tucker, GA 30084.
