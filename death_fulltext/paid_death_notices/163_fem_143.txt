
        GATLING-Eva Ingersoll. The Board of Trustees and Staff of The Heckscher Museum of Art mourn with profound sadness the passing of Director Emeritus and Honorary Trustee Eva Ingersoll Gatling. Her professionalism and 15 years of dedicated leadership laid the foundation for all that has come after. A Discerning Art Historian, Visionary Leader and Caring Mentor-Her Dauntless Spirit will be missed. Catherine A. Jansen, Chairman John E. Coraor, Ph.D, Director
