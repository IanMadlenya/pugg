
        HILL--Clinton, 81, died September 16, 2003 at Beth Israel Hospital and had suffered from emphysema. A versatile and inventive abstract artist in acrylics, oils, combines and paperworks. He was an instructor at Queens College. Clinton was born on March 8, 1922 in Idaho. He served in the Navy during WW II on a mine sweeper in the Pacific. Never without an art studio, Clinton worked continually in downtown New York City, Easthampton and Cape Cod. He was always an insightful thinker and a man of generous hospitality.
