
        ROTHSTEIN-Anita Gallo. November 29. Beloved wife of Fred H. Rothstein. Loving sister of the late Lewis Gallo. She was a Vice President and Fashion Merchandising Director of B. Altman & Co., and a leading global influence in the fashion industry. A very caring, compassionate and loving wife and a good friend to all. Service Monday, December 3, 2001, 9 AM, at St. John the Martyr Church, 72nd St., between 2nd and 3rd Aves., NYC. The family will recieve friends at the church on Monday between 8:30 and 9:00 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York Presbyterian Hospital Partners in Medicine, Breast Cancer Research, 525 East 68th St 10021.
