
        BERDOFF-Rubin. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins the physicians and staff of the Beth Israel Heart Institute in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Rubin Berdoff, father of our esteemed friend and colleague, Russell Berdoff, M.D., a member of the Beth Israel family for nearly two decades. We extend our sympathies to Dr. Berdoff, the entire Berdoff family, and all who loved him. Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President Stephen G. Baum, M.D., Chairman, Department of Medicine Thomas Killip, M.D., Director of the Beth Israel Heart Institute
