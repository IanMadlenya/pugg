
        PINSKY-Marcia Kerr Ostrow. On April 2, 1997. Beloved wife of Dr. Abraham Pinsky and the late Justice Theodore D. Ostrow. Loving mother of Marc C. Ostrow and his wife, Jeanne, and Steven H. Ostrow and his wife, Nancy. Adored grandmother of Alexandra, Katherine and Peregrine. Dear sister of Bertha, Rodin, Evelyn Silverstein and Alan Kerr. She was loved by her many friends. Her good humor, compassion and wisdom will be sorely missed. Private services.
