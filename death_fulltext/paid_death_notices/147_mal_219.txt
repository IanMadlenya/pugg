
        CANTER-Stanley D. 75, of New York, NY, and Richmond, MA, died on Saturday, April 24, in New York City of cancer. Devoted husband of Florence, beloved father of Robert and Leonard and daughter-in-law Julie. He is also survived by two grandchildren, Alexandra and Samuel. A former Partner and President of the marketing consultancy Canter Achenbaum Associates, and onetime Senior Vice President of Ogilvy & Mather, one of the world's largest advertising agencies, Stanley Canter held a Master of Arts degree in mathematical statistics from Columbia University and a Bachelor of Arts degree from the City College of New York. He was a longtime member of the Market Research Council, the Copy Research Council, and ARC, a professional advertising research organization of which he was a founder. Canter was well-known in advertising circles as a prolific speechmaker and lecturer. According to his wishes, he was cremated. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
