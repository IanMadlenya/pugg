
        KISSIN -- Benjamin, M.D., 1917-2005, Professor Emeritus at Downstate Medical School, died on February 25th. Dr. Kissin pioneered an innovative program for alcohol and drug addiction. His many faceted program ran from education to medical and psychological therapy. Dr. Kissin had a national and international reputation. He wrote 80 papers on the subject. He is survived by his wife Eva, his daughter Ruth and a sister Esther. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Monday, February 28th, 11:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
