
        PAGAN-Peter M. Died Wednesday, June 2, 1999. Born in Australia and U.S. citizen for many years. Peter had a distinguished acting career and was a man of great dignity, charm, wit and humor. He will be deeply missed by friends around the world. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Actors' Fund of America, 729 Seventh Ave, NYC 10019, would be appreciated. No service planned at Peter's request.
