
        AGRESTA-Hon. Thomas S., Justice, Supreme Court, NYS (Queens Co.), Ret., on March 27, 1999, in his 90th year, of Belle Terre, NY and Vero Beach, FL (formerly of Maspeth, Queens). Reposing at Nolan & TaylorHowe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Avenue, Northport, NY. Funeral blessing Thursday, 10am, at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Centerport. Interment at St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village. Hours Tuesday & Wednesday, 2-5, 7-9pm.
