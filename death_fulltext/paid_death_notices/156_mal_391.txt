
        PHILIPS - Morris. Of Roslyn Heights, N.Y. Died January 26, 2000. Married for more than 58 years, he and his wife Evelyn were the parents and parents-inlaw of Carol, Alice, Laura, Bill and Ellen and the grandparents of Rose, Ruby and Julia. Morrie was the brother and brother-in-law of Connie and Mary, Mickey and June, and the late Ben and Elsie. He was selflessly loving to his wife, children, grandchildren and all the members of his extended family who dearly loved him in return. He will be remembered by them and his many friends for his warmth, generosity, sharp wit and passion for social justice. Services 10AM Friday, Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 Station Road, Great Neck, New York.
