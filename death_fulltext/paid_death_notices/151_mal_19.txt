
        BURGER-Aaron. Died August 1 at age 87 in Lenox, Mass. Beloved husband of the late Vera Burger for more than 50 years. Loving father of Harold Burger, Ph.D., M.D. and father-in-law of Barbara Weiser, M.D. Devoted grandfather of Genevieve and Isabelle BurgerWeiser, Uncle of JoAnn KolmesFath, Karen Sclare and Donald Sclare. A teacher at Columbus High School in NYC for many years, he graduated from City College in 1932 and from Columbia University in 1933. He will be deeply missed. Services August 3 at 2pm at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
