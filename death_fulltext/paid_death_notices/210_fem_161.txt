
        POLSKY--Edwin. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Edwin Polsky, beloved father of Gail Rudnick, a treasured supporter of the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Gail and her husband David; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
