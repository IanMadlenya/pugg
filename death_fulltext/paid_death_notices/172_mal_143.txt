
        REAM-Dr. Charles R. of Tewksbury, N.J. On April 8th 2001 at The Medical Center At Princeton, N.J. at age 81. Born in Brooklyn, N.Y. he was a physician for many years and educator and practitioner in numerous hospitals and universities. Son of the late Royal and Ethyl Ream and husband of the late Elisse D. Ream. A memorial service will be held at 2PM Sunday May 20th at the Princeton University Chapel, Princeton, N.J. For further information contact Kimble Funeral Home, Princeton, N.J.
