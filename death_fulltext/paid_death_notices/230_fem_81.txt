
        MORFIT--Suzanne T. Beloved friend, colleague, teacher, passed away in her sleep March 5, 2006. She grew up in Hartsdale, NY and Green Village, NJ. Educated at Seton Hall University and Rutgers University, she served the Bellmore-Merrick Long Island School System for 30 years as a social worker, specializing in treatment of at-risk adolescents and training other professionals in this critical area. A gifted, empathic psychotherapist, she touched many lives. Taken from us too soon, she will be missed and mourned, her legacy given to those she helped. Memorial services Saturday, March 25, 11AM at Marymount College, NYC.
