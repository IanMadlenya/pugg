
        KUPIETZKY-Fanny. Matriarch of one of Jewry's leading families. Wife of Rabbi Jacob Kupietzky OBM, past leader of Agudas Harabanim of America, mother of Rabbi Jonah and Fran Kupietzky of NYC, Nachman and Elke Kupietzky of Jerusalem, Channy Kupietzky Greenberg and Dr. Abraham Greenberg of Merrick, NY, and Moshe and Arlene Kupietzky of Los Angeles, grandmother of 19, great-grandmother of 24, founder of Kay Caterers and friend of countless in Miami Beach, Jerusalem and NYC, passed away on July 28, 2000, in Jerusalem, at the age of 93. Shiva will be observed in Jerusalem. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Israel. The Kupietzky Family
        KUPIETZKY-Fanny. It is with deep regret that Kay Caterers expresses its deep sorrow on the untimely of Fanny Kupietzky, mother of Jonah and Fran, Nachman and Elkie, Moishe and Arlene, Channy and Abe Greenberg. Grandmother and great grandmother of many. A woman of great courage and valor who loved her family with all her heart. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion.
        KUPIETZKY-Fanny. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the mother of its beloved member, Jonah Kupietzky. May her entire family be comforted among all those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
