
        POST-Myrna. Of Boca Raton, on April 7. Beloved wife of Herbert H. Post. Loving mother of Michael, Neil and Robert Post and Suzanne and Seth Estrin, Dear sister of Howard Fichtel. Services will be held on Thursday, April 9th, at Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, 5808 West Atlantic Avenue, Delray Beach, 12:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society. Her beauty, charm and loving nature will always be remembered.
        POST-Myrna. Temple Beth Sholom of Roslyn Heights, New York, mourns the passing of our beloved member Myrna Post and extends heartfelt condolences to her husband Herbert and the entire family. Services and shiva are in Florida. Alan B. Lucas, Rabbi Dr. Philip Adler, President
