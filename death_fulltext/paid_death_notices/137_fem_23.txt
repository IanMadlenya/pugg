
        RENNIE-Jean Tower of Palm Beach, Florida and Milwaukee, Wisconsin died Sunday at her home in Florida. Her zest of life and energy will be missed by all who knew her. Burial will be Tuesday in Louisville, KY. Memorial service will be held in Palm Beach in November. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Vassar College or The English Speaking Union. She leaves behind 2 children Roxburgh Rennie of Providence, R.I. and Milbrey Rennie Taylor of NYC and 5 grandchildren, all of whom adored her.
