
        JACKSON-Lou. All of us at Jackson Lewis Schnitzler & Krupman mourn the loss of our founder, Lou Jackson. For thirty-nine years he provided inspiration, guidance, friendship and good humor. We will miss him dearly and cherish his memory forever. There will be a memorial service on Saturday, June 28th, 1997 at 11:30 A.M., at Great Gorge Village, Route 94 North, Vernon, New Jersey (201827-2222). In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Louis Jackson Respite Center at The Children's Village, Dobbs Ferry, New York 10522, or a charity of choice.
        JACKSON-Louis, 92, died on June 11, 1997. Beloved father of Elena Atlas, father-in-law of Anthony H. Atlas, grandfather of Seth J. Atlas and Clifford R. Atlas, great-grandfather of Emily. Funeral services at Toale Brothers, Colonial Chapel, Sarasota, Florida today, June 13, 1997, at 10 A.M.
