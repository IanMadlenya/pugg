
        LANGLADE -- Patricia. On November 14, 2003. The partners, counsel, associates and staff of Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP are deeply saddened by the sudden and tragic loss of our beloved friend and colleague Patricia Langlade. Trish's career at our firm began on July 7, 1978, and more recently she was Director of Legal Recruiting. Her infectious laughter, good humor and spirit touched us all. Trish, who was recently honored for her 25 years of outstanding service with the firm, was much beloved by her many colleagues. She was a great friend who made us better people for having known her. We will remember her always. Our deepest sympathies are sent to her beloved husband, Gerald Langlade, to her two daughters, Tracey and Laura, and to the entire Langlade and McNulty families. On behalf of Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP
