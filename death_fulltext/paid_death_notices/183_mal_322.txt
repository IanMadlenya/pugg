
        HUNTER-Fergus Allister. Passed away April 13, 2002 at age 72 in Brooklyn, NY. Beloved brother of Brenna Brown & husband, Jeremy of Toronto, and adored uncle of Stephanie Karl and Jonathan. Danced with the National Ballet of Canada and in many CBC-TV shows in the 50's. Later was owner/manager of Variety Arts and Showcase Studios in Manhattan. He will be remembered with love by his friends and by George and Nora Brown, and by Bob Breagy. A private service will be held in June. Donations to G.M.H.C.
