
        GREENFIELD--Robert S. 86 of Plantation, FL. passed away January 1, 2006 in Florida. Beloved husband of Leonore. Devoted father of Dr. Andrew (Linda) Greenfield and Melissa (Dr. Arthur) Rubin. Loving grandfather of Dani, Samantha, Gail (Bill) Carter and Donna. Proud great grandfather of Daniel and Anna. Retired stockbroker. Member of Temple Kol Ami-Emanuel of Plantation. Naval veteran of WWII where he received a presidential commendation. He was a true old school gentleman. Services were held Wednesday January 4 at Lakeside Memorial Park, Miami, FL.
