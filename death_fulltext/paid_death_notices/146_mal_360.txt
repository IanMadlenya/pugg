
        BERG-David. YAD VASHEM, The Remembrance Authority in Jerusalem, its board and the officers of the International and American Society for Yad Vashem are saddened by the death of David Berg. We mourn the passing of our esteemed Honorary Vice President and Benefactor of Yad Vashem. David Berg was a a man of high principles and great wisdom who strongly believed that the legacy of the Shoah must be carried forward for future generations. We extend our condolences to his daughter Betsy and all the family. Eli Zborowski, Chairman, American and International Societies for Yad Vashem Dr. Josef Burg and Avner Shalev, Chairmen Yad Vashem, Jerusalem
        BERG-David. Park East Synagogue and Day School mourn the passing of David Berg, our devoted member and Scholarship Endowment Benefactor who valued the furthering of education. Our deepest sympathy to his daughter, Betsy, and the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
