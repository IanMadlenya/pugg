
        KAUFMAN-Posey W. The National Women's Division of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is deeply saddened by the loss of our dear friend and devoted member of our National Board of Directors, who served for many years as chairwoman of our benefit shop, Connoisseur's Corner. She was also a member of the College's Society of Founders. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her son, Paul, a member of the Executive Committee of Einstein's Young Men's Division, her daughter-in-law, Marsha, and to the entire family. Nancy Oelbaum, President National Women's Division Albert Einstein College of Medicine Judy R. Rosenberg, Chairwoman Executive Committee
