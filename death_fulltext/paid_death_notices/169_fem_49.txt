
        JUSTIN-Miriam. Died peacefully at age 94 on February 3, 2001. Beloved mother of Barbara Handler and her husband Mark, Jane Justin and her long time partner Les Suchman. Grandma Mim to Jody and Neal Cooper, Jonathan Handler and Penni Sibun. Great-grandmother for the first time on January 17, 2001 to Lorien Katharine Sibun-Handler. She will also be missed by her long time caregiver Gloria Avellana. Her feisty demeanor and firm maternal presence touched all those she knew. She was a woman of great style and sophisticated taste. She started and ran her own successful business ''Just-In-Case'' for more than 20 years. Her life was long and fruitful and may she rest in peace. Services will be private.
