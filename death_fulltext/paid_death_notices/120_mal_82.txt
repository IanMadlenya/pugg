
        SULLIVANT - Norris J. On January 6, 1997 at 74 years of age. Native of Pine Bluff, AR. and Bronx, NY. Highly decorated veteran of World War II (ETO) and the Korean War. Academic Facilities Manager, Maintenance and Operations, NYU where he was a loyal employee for over 40 years. Devoted husband of 51 years to Dora (nee Lutschaunig). Generous and loving father to Norris K. Jean and Joan. Loving grandfather to Kristin. Fond father-in-law to Irene Sullivant and good friend to Elaine Pearl Sullivant and stepson to Ruby Sullivant. Beloved stepbrother of the late Virgil Sullivant. Also survived by many in-laws, nieces, nephews, grand nieces, grand nephews and friends. His courage, strength, integrity, tenacity, humor and kindness will be missed by all who knew him. Family will receive friends at John J. Fox & Sons, Inc. 201st Street and Grand Concourse, Bronx, on Thursday and Friday 205 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial on Saturday, 11 AM at St. Philip Neri Church. Private interment in Pine Bluff, AR. at a later date.
