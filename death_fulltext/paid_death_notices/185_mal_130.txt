
        GOLDSTEIN-Louis A., Esq. On June 10, 2002, at the age of 81. Beloved husband of Irene for fifty years. Loving father of Melody Kuney, Mitchell and Elissa. Father-in-law to Scott Kuney and Robin. Cherished grandfather of Andre, Allegra, Aaron and Samantha. Dear brother of Rose Minkin. Practicing attorney and accountant since 1946, on Court Street. Appointed Court Examiner since 1980. Active until his last day. Graduate of City College and Saint John's School of Law. Devoted to his family, friends, clients and the court system. Services Wednesday, 12:15 PM, ''Sinai Chapels,'' 162 - 05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the UJA-Federation. We will all miss his wit, intelligence, generosity, devotion and love.
