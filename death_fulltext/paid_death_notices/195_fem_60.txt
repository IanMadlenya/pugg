
        ORSCHEL-Mrs. Albert K., the former Peggy Rauh of New York City, New York, wife of the late prominent corporation attorney, Albert K. Orschel, born July 16, 1915, died April 9, 2003, at her home in Chicago, Illinois. She is survived by her daughter, Lynn Orschel, son, Alan (Nora Mitchell) Orschel, and two grandsons, Bradley Mitchell and Jeffrey Mitchell Orschel. Services will be private. Memorial contributions may be made to the Children's Memorial Foundation, 2300 Children's Place, Box 4, Chicago, IL, 60614. Blake-Lamb Funeral Home information: 312-944-5594.
