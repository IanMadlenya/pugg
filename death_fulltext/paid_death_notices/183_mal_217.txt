
        BRENNAN-Patrick J. 75, formerly of East Norwich, died April 16, in Cooperstown. For more than 30 years, Pat was director of leasing for the General Motors Corporation Building on Fifth Avenue, later Corporate Property Investors, retiring in 1997. He is survived by his wife, Maureen, daughters, Eileen Brennan and Colleen Weale, son Thomas Brennan. Services at 1pm on April 20, at St. Mary's Church, Cooperstown.
