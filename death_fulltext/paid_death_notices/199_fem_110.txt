
        MUROV--Alice Louise. With deep sorrow, we announce that Alice Louise Murov, of Tide Pointe, Hilton Head Island, S.C. and NYC, passed away peacefully at her daughter's home in NYC, at the age of 94. Daughter of the late Mary & William Gully and the beloved wife of the late Sidney Murov and devoted sister to the late Lloyd Gully. A loving mother to twins Karol & Karen and a loving grandmother to Sarah & Thomas. She leaves behind her son-in-law and two greatgrandchildren. She was a dear friend to many. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Wednesday and Thursday, 6-8pm. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave at 66th St, Friday 10:30AM. Interment Hilton Head, S.C.
