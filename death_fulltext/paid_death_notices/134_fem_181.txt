
        McCASKEY-Lydia. On March 18, 1998 Lydia was suddenly taken away from her husband George, daughter Pamela and many other close family members and friends who loved her dearly. We must all cherish the 65 wonderful years we had with her, but sadly, we will all miss her terribly. Visitation will be held at the A.L. Jacobsen Funeral Home, Inc., 1380 New York Avenue, Huntington Station, Long Island, N.Y. Sunday 7-10PM and Monday 2-5 and 7-10PM. Religious service will be held Monday, 8PM. Prayers will be offered Tuesday, 10AM followed by an entombment at Pinelawn Memorial Park.
