
        FEHER-Margaret. On Memorial Day in NYC. Born May 16, 1911. Granddaughter of Mayor Schroeder of Brooklyn. Assisted husband, Milton in running Milton Feher School of Dance and Relaxation. Formerly associate director Morningside Heights Inc., a community planning organization. Also worked for NYS Div. of Housing and the Federal Farm Admin. and U.S. Navy School of Military Gov't. Survived by husband; brother Schroeder Boulton; sons David and Stephen Bartlett; two nieces and six grandchildren. Friends and family are invited to honor her at Susannah Harrington's apartment, 55 Pineapple Street, Brooklyn Heights, on Friday at 4 P.M.
