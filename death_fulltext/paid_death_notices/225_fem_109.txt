
        GILLERAN--Marsha K. On October 11. Passed away after a courageous and spirited battle of seven years with BAC lung cancer. After a viewing last week at the Frank E. Campbell ''The Funeral Chapel'' and a Mass at St. John the Evangelist R.C. Church in Manhattan, she was laid to rest in the plot for her and Mario in Pawtucket, RI. She leaves behind her dearest beloved life companion for the past 33 years, Mario Sirabella of Manhattan and her dearest beloved sister Deborah W. Gilleran of Point Pleasant, NJ.
