
        PENDLETON--Ellen Lindsay. Age 83, on September 30, a lifelong resident of New York, world traveler and survivor, living a full and active life with cerebral palsy. She was predeceased by her parents Everett and Grace Pendleton, and sister Phyllis Bigelow, and survived by her sister Jeanne Cook of Ocala, FL, and nephews Peter, Paul, Lindsay and Christopher. Donations may be made in her memory to the United Palsy of New York, 80 Maiden Lane, NYC 10038. Call Tinity Church, West Side Drive, 212368-1600 for time of services.
