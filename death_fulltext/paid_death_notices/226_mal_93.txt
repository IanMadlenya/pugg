
        BRIAN--Frank. 86. December 12, 1918, to November 5, 2005. Married 65 years to his devoted wife Goldie. Leaves behind two daughters: Natalie (Stanley) & Joyce (Al); three grandchildren: Francine (Marc), Andrew (Stacy) & Matthew; & five great-grandchildren. Most remembered for his strength, fantastic sense of humor and twinkle in his eye. Most beloved by his friends and anyone who was lucky enough to know him. Funeral service 2:15, today, Star of David, Tamarac, FL.
