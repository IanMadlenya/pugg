
        COOK-Irving. On April 3, 1999. A longtime resident of Mamaroneck, he was the former owner of Cook's Restaurant in Larchmont, and a longtime member of Larchmont Temple. Loving father of Elizabeth Cook and Doris Klein. Dear father-in-law of Mordecai Klein, M.D. Cherished grandfather of Caroline and Amanda Postel, and Zoe, Zev and Talia Klein. Services will be held at Larchmont Temple, Monday 10AM. Interment at Sharon Gardens Cemetery will follow the services.
