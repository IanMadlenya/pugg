
        NORTH--Gail. Passed away peacefully from cancer on April 14, at the home of a friend on Shelter Island, surrounded by friends and family. She is survived by her nephews, Mike and Joey Zara, the sons of her late sister, Barbara, and by her brotherin-law, Danny Zara. She was devoted to her dog, Charlene, and her late, beloved Tyler. Born Gail Ross on October 13, 1939, she grew up in Los Angeles. She graduated Phi Beta Kappa from CUNY and went on to become a senior editor and published author. The warmth of her spirit will be missed by all who had the privilege to have known her.
