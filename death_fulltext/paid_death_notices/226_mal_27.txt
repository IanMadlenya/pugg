
        WEILAND - Irving. Passed away on October 27, 2005. He was the beloved husband of the late Anne Weiland and devoted father of Andrew, Edward, and Ellen Williams. He was the cherished grandfather of Sarah Holland, Dan Weiland, Jeffrey Weiland, Sophie Williams and Julia Williams. He was the greatgrandfather of Lucy Holland, Ella Holland and Audrey Holland. He was a humanist and role model for his family and friends. His intelligence and unconditional love will be missed by all of us. Private services were held Sunday, October 30, 2005.
