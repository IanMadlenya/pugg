
        FELDMAN--Irving E. 93, died on August 9, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Judith. Devoted father of Donald and Jane Feldman and Ronald and Frayda Feldman. Cherished grandfather of Mark, Andy and Lisa and Julie and Mark. Dearest greatgrandfather of Ethan, Charlotte, Jonah, Sam and Nathan. Loving brother of Abe Feldman. He was President of Zelart Drug Company and President of Toiletry Merchandisers of America. Services Sunday, 11:30 AM, at ''Temple Emanuel,'' 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck, NY. Interment at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Temple Emanuel of Great Neck.
