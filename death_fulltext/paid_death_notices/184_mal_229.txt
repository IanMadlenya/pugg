
        McGUIRE-George William. 74, passed away at his home on May 1, 2002. He was born in Brooklyn, NY, served in the U.S. Navy and graduated from New York University. He had a long and distinguished career in advertising, including positions with DuPont, Grey Advertising and Peterson Publishing, was President of Grey, Lyon and King, and partner in the executive search firm of Lucas, Berger, McGuire and O'Brien. Mr. McGuire was a lifelong supporter of various charities. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made in George McGuire's name to Soft Tissue Sarcoma Research at Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
