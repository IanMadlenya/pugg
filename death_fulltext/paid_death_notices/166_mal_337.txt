
        STEINBERG-David. Of Washington, D.C. On Sunday, November 26, 2000, at his residence. The cherished son of Renita and the late Myron (Michael) Steinberg. He was a graduate of the American University. Mr. Steinberg owned a TV-production company and was co-founder and President of The State Theatre in Falls Church, VA. He is survived by many loving friends. Services will be held at ''Gutterman-Musicant'', Hackensack, Thurs, November 30th, 1PM.
