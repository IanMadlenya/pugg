
        IACONO--Laura Belle Curry Levy, 95, of Fort Lee, NJ, passed away on January 30, 2007. Beloved widow of the late S. Alfred Levy and dearest wife of the late Dominick Iacono. Cherished sister of Nathan Curry. Also surviving are many loving nieces and nephews. Her family and friends who loved her will always remember her kindness and compassion. Funeral Service Friday 10 am at the Hunt Stellato Funeral Home, 1601 Palisade Avenue, Fort Lee, NJ. Interment Temple Israel Cemetery, Hastings on the Hudson, NY. Family and friends will be received Thursday 7-9 pm. A vigil service will be held at 7:30 pm on Thursday. Send condolences to stellatofuneralhomes.com
