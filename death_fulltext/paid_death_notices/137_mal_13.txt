
        HALL-Edward J, Dr. of Bethel, CT died Sunday, May 31. He was professor emeritus of Obstetrics and Gynecology of Health Sciences Center, State University of New York, Brooklyn, NY. Dr. Hall was a past president of the Brooklyn Gynecological Society, New York Obstetrical Society, American Association of Obstetricians and Gynecologists Foundation. Dr. Hall was a life fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists, American College of Surgeons and American Gynecologic and Obstetric Society. He is survived by a daughter, Maryanne Hall Smith of Bethel, a son of J. Edward Hall, Jr. of Mirror Lake, NH, 7 grandchildren, 6 greatgrandchildren and a sister, Helena Orben of NJ. Contributions in memory of Dr. J. Edward Hall may be made to the charity of one's choice.
