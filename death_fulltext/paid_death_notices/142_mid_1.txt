
        SHELLER-Ed. On November 13, 1998. Beloved husband of Naomi Sarna. Father of Chris & Alex Sheller, Sarah and Noa Schechter. Dear son of Robert and the late Helen Sheller. Adored nephew of Florence Shultz. Brother of Elly Rufer and her family, John, Daniel and Kate. Services Sunday, November 15, 12:30 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Ed was a man whose intellect and passion for reading enriched those around him. Contributions can be made in Ed's name to The New York Public Library, Friends Office, 465 Fifth Ave, Room M1, NY, NY 10018-2788 and The Summit School, 187-30 Grand Central Pkwy, Jamaica Estates, NY 11432.
