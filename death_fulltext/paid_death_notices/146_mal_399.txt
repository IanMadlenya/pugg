
        GILBURT-Samuel G.., 88 years of age, died at home in West Palm Beach, FL, after a long illness. He is survived by his wife Lillian, daughter Judy, two brothers, Jack and David. Mr. Gilburt was a retired Junior High School principal from NYC. He was active in, and an officer of, a number of professional organizations. He was also involved in community education affairs. He was especially interested in the study and writing of poetry. He was known as the ''Poet Laureate of Brooklyn.'' Mr. Gilburt wrote the poem entitled ''I am Brooklyn'' which is recorded in the Congressional Record.
