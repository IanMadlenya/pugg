
        DYSON-Anne, Dr. The Advisory Board and staff of the Children's Defense Fund-New York notes with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Anne Dyson. Dr. Dyson's commitment to and direct work with the Children's Defense Fund, both on a national and state level, was unparalleled. The legacy of Dr. Dyson's vision will continue on through the many children whose lives were improved as a result of her generosity and dedication. Our thoughts and prayers are with her husband Michael, her children Roberto and Mac and her family. Maureen Cogan, Vice Chair, CDF Donna A. Lawrence, Executive Director, CDF-NY
        DYSON-Anne E., M.D. The Board and staff of Planned Parenthood of New York City (PPNYC) are deeply saddened by the death of former boardmember Dr. Anne E. Dyson. Dr. Dyson served on the PPNYC board from 1982 through 1991. An ardent advocate for reproductive rights and access to health services, Dr. Dyson was an articulate voice for our mission. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the entire Dyson family. Cathy A. Cramer, Chair, PPNYC Board of Directors Joan Malin, CEO
        DYSON-Anne E. The Pace University community records with sorrow the untimely passing of Dr. Anne E. Dyson, daughter of the late Charles H. Dyson, class of '30, our valued alumnus, Trustee and generous benefactor. Our condolences are extended to the Dyson family. Aniello A. Bianco, Chairman David A. Caputo, President
