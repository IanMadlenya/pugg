
        NEUMANN-Gerda. Age 93, on October 25, peacefully at her home in NYC. Born in Berlin, she was the extraordinarily devoted daughter of Judge Adolf Neumann and Hedwig (nee Mendelssohn). She fled Nazi Germany in April, 1933, and after several years in England and Holland came to America in 1937. In Europe as well as the U.S. she worked as a stockbroker and arbitrageur, and was one of the first women employed in the position known at the time as ''customers' man''. Survived by a host of relatives and friends who loved and admired her, and will miss her greatly.
