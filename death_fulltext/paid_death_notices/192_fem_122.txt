
        GINSBERG - Pearl. Loving wife of Albert for 55 years. Devoted mother of Liane, Larry and Hilary. Loving grandmother of Sean, Sara, Daniel, Heather, and Josh. Cherished sister of Ruth Cramer and Harry Teicher. Loved by her daughter-in-law Lisa, son-in-law Joe and her granddaughter-in-law Susan. Coveted bridge partner to all who knew her talents. Hilary, Larry, and Liane and their families and Pearl's family and friends will forever have a void in their lives but she will live forever in our hearts. She will be missed by all who knew and loved her. Services to be held at Temple Emanuel of Great Neck, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck, NY, Monday, January 13, 1 PM. Interment Mt. Hebron Cemetery. Liane, Larry and Hilary and their families
        GINSBERG - Pearl. The Hampton Synagogue mourns the passing of our cherished benefactor, Pearl Ginsberg. Together with her late husband Albert, they were original founders and magnanimous supporters of our congregation. To her children, Liane, Larry, Hilary, and to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. Rabbi Marc Schneier Michael B. Weisbrod, Pres. Seymour G. Siegel, Treas.
