
        RATHE--Julian, 88, of Hallandale, FL (formerly of Riverdale, NY) on February 20, 2004. Partner in H&P Mayer Inc. from 1945-1985. Survived by Irma, his wife of 63 years, daughters Evelyn Radelman of Aventura, FL and Joan Rice of NYC, grandchildren Marni and Glynis Radelman. He will be greatly missed by his loving family.
