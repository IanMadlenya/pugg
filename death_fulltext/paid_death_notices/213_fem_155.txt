
        KAUFMAN--Loretta. Born in Berlin, Germany, in 1921, died suddenly on October 20, 2004. She immigrated to the United States and settled in NYC with her mother, Marga Samoje in 1938. She is survived by her son and daughter-inlaw, Seth and Patricia of Manhattan, and her two beloved grandsons, David and Robert. She was predeceased by her husband Mitchell in 2001. Donations can by made in her name to Selfhelp Community Services, 520 8th Avenue, NY, NY 10018. The family will be at Loretta's home on Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, and on Monday, 7-9 PM.
