
        RADDOCK-J. Joseph. The Officers, Directors, Administration, and Staff of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center express their heartfelt sympathy at the passing of Jay Raddock, a founding Board member and Honorary Vice President. He will long be remembered for his leadership in our construction and development, and for his commitment to our Center. We extend our sincere condolences to the family. Joseph Gurwin, Chairman Lawrence J. Simon, President Herbert H. Friedman Executive Vice President
