
        ULLENDORFF-Henry ''Uli.'' Died on November 11 in his 91st year in New York City. Survived by his wife Regina, of 50 years, his four children, Jackie, Doris (Ken), David (Alison) and Danielle (Mark) and many grandchildren. Born in 1910 in Gelsenkirchen, Germany, he remade his life after his family perished in the Holocaust. He taught us to take risks; be courageous, brave and dignified in the face of adversity and never forget to enjoy the journey to its fullest. Services will be held on Monday, November 13, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue at 10 AM. Donations may be made to DOROT, 171 West 85th St., NY, NY 10024.
