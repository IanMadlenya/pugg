
        BAUM--Richard T. Of Chappaqua, New York. On January 30, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Jean Knapp Baum, devoted father of Kathryn Russell and Judith Baum, loving and adored grandfather of Jean Russell, Katherine Baum & Christine Baum. A graduate of Columbia University and Columbia School of Engineering, he spent his professional career as a partner in the engineering firm, Jaros, Baum and Bolles. He was the recipient of many professional honors including the Egleston Medal from Columbia School of Engineering and election to the National Academy of Engineering. Friends may call at the Oelker and Cox Funeral Home, 262 Main St, Mt. Kisco, N.Y. Wednesday from 6-8PM. Funeral service at the First Congregational Church, Chappaqua, Thursday at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Columbia University School of Engineering, or Mount Sinai Hospital.
