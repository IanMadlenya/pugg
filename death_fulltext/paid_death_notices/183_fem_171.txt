
        NOVAK-Joan Tross. Passed away on April 15, 2002 in her 75th year at her home in New York City. Devoted mother of Gregory and Mark Novak. Loving grandmother of Rebecca, Jeffrey, Jason, Jennifer & Jacqueline. Her remarkable intellect and creativity as an artist, writer and decorator will continue to inspire us. But it is her unconditional love, support and good cheer that we will miss the most. Our love for her will be eternal. Funeral services Wednesday, April 17, 5 PM, Frank E. CampbellThe Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
