
        TAUB--Max. Died on July 24, 2004, at age 92 years. Husband of Flora. Loving father to Lewis and father-in-law to Joanne. Brother of Susan Weinstein and Emanuel Tabachnick. He was predeceased by his sisters Betty Krugman, Lee Seiffert and Peggy Rothstein. Retired teacher of James Monroe High School. Known by his former students as ''Sunbeam.'' Private services. Donations to Alzheimer's Association, 400 Morris Ave, Suite 251, Denville, NJ 07834.
