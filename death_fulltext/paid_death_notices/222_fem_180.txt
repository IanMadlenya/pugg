
        MILLER--Janet L. 98, died peacefully on July 22, 2005. She was born in New York City and devoted her life to working with young people as a teacher, the social director of the Horace Mann School in Riverdale and in 1949 founded Camp Merriwood for Girls in Orford, New Hampshire. She is predeceased by her husband Gordon Moose Miller. She is survived by two sons, Gordon Porter Miller and Gary D. Miller, three sisters, six grandchildren and 12 great grandchildren.
