
        JARRETT--Milton Philip, former Queens resident, died January 26 in Boynton Beach, FL at the age of 88. Husband of Harriet, father to Rosalind and David. Milton was a recipient of the B'nai B'rith Torch of Freedom Award and served as president of the City Line Chamber of Commerce. He was a Captain in the Military Police in the Pacific. He and Harriet were active in REAP in NY and FL. We will miss his humor and quiet graciousness. Memorial service will be held Sunday at 9:45 AM ''Sinai Chapels'' 16205 Horace Harding Expy., Fresh Meadows. Donations may be sent to B'nai B'rith.
