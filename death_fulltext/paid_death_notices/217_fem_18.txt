
        GENTILE--Madeline M. Died on January 31 at age 73 in New York City. Survived by her beloved husband Anthony R. Gentile I, former TV and radio editor of the Herald Tribune, TV Editor of Newsday and the Executive Editor of Tribune Media Services, her loving children, Anthony II, John, Christopher and Jenny Cipriano, and grandchildren Jessica, Madeline, Julienne, Anthony III, Chris and Isabella. A renowned wedding gown designer, she created the first line of multicolored tuxedos for women. Family will receive friends Wednesday 5 to 9 P.M., and Thursday 2 to 5 P.M. and 7 to 9 P.M. at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street. Burial will take place Friday in Toms River, NJ.
