
        GRIFFITHS-Ruth Baker Baines, 99. Died Tuesday, Feb. 26, 2002 at her home in Farmington, CT. Born October 17, 1902 on her grandparents' farm in Leyden, MA, she was the daughter of Ellen Budington Baines-Griffiths and David Baines-Griffiths and sister of the late Hugh Budington Griffiths and Walter Denison Griffiths. At age 3, she moved to Spuyten Duyvil, where her father was minister of Edgehill Church. She lived in Spuyten Duyvil and Riverdale until 1998 when she moved to Farmington. A graduate of St. Agatha's School and Vassar College Class of 1924, Miss Griffiths worked at Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital until her retirement. She was a member of the Shakespeare Club, Riverdale Yacht Club, and Edgehill Church. Enthusiastic about hiking and canoeing, she enjoyed vacations in the Adirondacks and in later years on the Coast of Maine. She is survived by her niece Kathleen Griffiths Anderson and her husband Sherwood L. Anderson; Hal Reynolds husband of her late niece Desire Griffiths Reynolds; her nephew, David H. Griffiths and his wife Joyce Griffiths; her nephew Robert B. Griffiths; her niece Ruth Griffiths Doyle and husband William Doyle; her niece Marion Griffiths Demcisak and husband John Demcisak. She also leaves 13 great nieces and nephews and her cousin, William Budington and his wife Irma Budington. A memorial service will be held Saturday, March 2 at 10:30AM at Edgehill Church, Spuyten Duyvil, New York. Donations may be made to Edgehill Church in her memory.
