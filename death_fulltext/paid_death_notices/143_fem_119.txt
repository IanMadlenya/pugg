
        MORGENTHAU - Emmy Lou. Wife of Eugene and the late Morton Pepper, and Howard Lilienthal. Mother of Philip and Robert Lilienthal, and their wives Lynn and Roe. Grandmother of Andy, Ben, Jeffrey and Debbie Lilienthal, Cathy Deutchman and Vinny Riccobono. Great-grandmother of Kristina and Kelsey Lilienthal. Former Director of Camp Winnebago, President of Interfaith Neighbors, Director of Volunteers at Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and Assistant Director of Volunteers for John F. Kennedy in New York. Services Wednesday, December 16, 11:30 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave. at 65th St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Interfaith Neighbors, Channel 3 Country Camp, or Winnebageo Alumini Association.
        MORGENTHAU - Emmy Lou. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Emmy Lou Morgenthau, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
