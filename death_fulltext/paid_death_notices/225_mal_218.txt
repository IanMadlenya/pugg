
        TRIBBIE--Joe, 49, of Manhattan, died during the night October 20, 2005. Survived by his loving partner, Gilbert Cole; mother, Pat; sister, Judy; and brother, Mike; and scores of friends and colleagues who will always cherish his grace and love. Joe's contributions to helping people living with HIV/AIDS are well known to many. He will always be remembered as a person who actually made this world better.
