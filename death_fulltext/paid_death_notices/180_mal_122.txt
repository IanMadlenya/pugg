
        GOLDSCHLAGER - William. Teacher, educational administrator, lawyer. On January 5, 2002, in Bryn Mawr, PA. Beloved husband of Edna (nee Cohen). Loving mother of Marcia Epstein (Paul) of Gladwyne, PA, and Seth Goldschlager (Isabelle) of Paris, France. Grandfather of Amy and Leonard Feldman, Robin Epstein, Esther, Ezra and Eva Goldschlager. Great-grandfather of Madeline and Benjamin Feldman. Sister-in-law of Roseline Doorech and Pearl Goldschlager. He was an enthusiastic educator and public-spirited administrative law judge. He was a man of great intelligence and integrity, and will be missed by all who knew him. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, January 8, at 1 PM, Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel at 114-03 Queens Blvd (76th Road), Forest Hills, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Seth Dooreck Book Fund, c/o Jennifer Sancton, NYU, 25 West 4th Street, Room 526, NY, NY 10012; or to a charity of your choice.
