
        BLUMBERG--Shirley. Died on December 20th after a brief illness. She lived an amazing, active life, filled with travel, theater, dance, art and culture. In addition, she was a voracious reader and loved movie and television mysteries. Born in 1931 to the late Anna and Abraham Chernin, she was married to the late Morris Blumberg and is survived by two sons, Steffen Andrews and David Blumberg. In addition to her niece Susan Swidler, her sad passing is mourned by a large circle of relatives and dear friends.
