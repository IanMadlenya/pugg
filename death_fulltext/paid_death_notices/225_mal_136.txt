
        KLEIN--Hon. Alvin F. Age 94, on October 11, 2005. He was a graduate of New York University and Harvard Law School. Justice Klein served the New York court system as a Civil Court Judge and as a Justice of the Supreme Court for over 25 years. Retiring from the bench at 76, he served as an Arbitrator and then returned to the court system as a Judicial Hearing Officer, retiring again at age 93. He was beloved by all because of his sense of justice, kindness, generosity, humor and respect for people. He was a wonderful husband, father, father-in-law, grandfather, great grandfather and friend. We will miss him very much. Services Friday, October 14, 11:45AM, Riverside, 76th St. & Amsterdam Ave. Sherry, Dean, Jill, Jennifer, Jeffrey, Amy, Zachary, Eliza and Sophie
        KLEIN--Alvin F. The East 55th Street Conservative Synagogue sorrowfully records the loss of its esteemed and beloved member and Trustee. Our deepest sympathy to his daughter, Judge Sherry Heitler, and all the family members. Reuven Siegel, Rabbi Barbara Gerber-Krasner, President
