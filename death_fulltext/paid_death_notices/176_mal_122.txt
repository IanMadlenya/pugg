
        TATARSKY-Judd. The State of New York Mortgage Agency and The New York State Housing Finance Agency mourn the passing of our friend and fellow employee, Judd Tatarsky. Judd will be remembered with sincere affection for his generosity, his humble kindness and for his gentle, sweet manner. He will be dearly missed.
        TATARSKY-Judd. His friends at 62 Montague Street mourn the passing of Judd Tatarsky, an unusually kind, wonderful and generous man.
