
        NORMAN-Gail. Died on February 5, 2001 in Key Largo, FL. Formerly of Woodstock, VT and Manhattan. Devoted wife of the late advertising executive, Norman B. Norman, founder and CEO of Norman, Craig and Kummel, Inc. Beloved mother to Peter Norman of Arlington, MA., and Susan Norman of Dover, NH, and grandmother to Annika. She was a gentle woman whose positive outlook and loving nature brightened the lives of all who knew her. She will live forever in our hearts. Memorial service will be held in Manhattan in the spring. Notification will be sent. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
