
        LEVINE-Bernard S. College professor, mentor and administrator, died at home on February 22, 2003, after a courageous struggle with pancreatic cancer. Adored husband for 53 years of Bunny (Bernice) Utal Levine, most extraordinary and beloved father of Marty, Francie and Joe, cherished father-in-law of Judy Forrest Levine and Jay Lubin and world's greatest grandfather to Robin Emily Levine and Jeffrey Robert Lubin. Bernie was also an avid tennis player and a supporter of the arts, especially of developing actors. His gentle spirit will be with us forever. Services will be held at the Jewish Memorial Chapel in Clifton, NJ. Donations to the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research, or the Actors' Fund would be appreciated.
