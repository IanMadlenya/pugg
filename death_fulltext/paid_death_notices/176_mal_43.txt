
        POSNER-Monte A. Passed away September 3rd, 2001 after a long illness. Taught social studies 30 years in Far Rockaway High School. Served with distinction in U.S. Air Force Intelligence. Cherished by family and friends for his sense of humor and love of books. Renaissance man and opera lover extraordinaire. Pre-deceased by wife Ruth. Survived by wife Barbara, brother Ronald, daughters Marla and Felicia, stepchildren Lisa, Eric and Scott, grandchildren Melissa, Samantha, Justin. Funeral Service at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, at 10 A.M. Wednesday, September 5th.
