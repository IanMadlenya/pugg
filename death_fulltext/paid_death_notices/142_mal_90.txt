
        BILOFSKY-Joseph. Age 81, beloved husband of Pearl, devoted father of Walter and Ellen, adored grandfather of Jessica and Alexander. A lifelong resident of NYC, he was a veteran of World War II, combat medic, for many years a leader in the taxi industry, vice president of the League of Mutual Taxi Owners. Service Monday, November 9, 12:30 PM, at Boulevard Riverside Chapels, 312 Coney Island Ave at Prospect Park.
