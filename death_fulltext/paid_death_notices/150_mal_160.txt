
        SEELYE-Edward E., MD The Department of Psychiatry of Weill Medical College of Cornell University, New York Presbyterian Hospital, records with deep sorrow the passing of Dr. Edward Seelye, esteemed psychiatrist at the Westchester Division from 1959 until the time of his death, July 3, 1999. During these four decades, Dr. Seelye held several leadership positions at the hospital. He will be missed by colleagues and patients. We extend heartfelt condolences to the Seelye family. A memorial service will be held on July 15 at Ridgeview Congregational Church, White Plains, NY, 7:30PM. Jack D. Barchas, MD, Psychiatrist-in-Chief Philip J. Wilner, MD, Medical Director Virginia L. Susman, MD, Associate Medical Director
