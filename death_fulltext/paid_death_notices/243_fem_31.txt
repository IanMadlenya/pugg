
        FEAREY--Mary Senior, of Edgartown, MA and formerly of New York City, died in her home, on Sunday, April 1. She was the widow of the late Morton Fearey, who died in 1988 and who was a partner in the law firm of Davis, Polk and Wardwell. Mrs. Fearey, a graduate of Concord Academy in 1932 and Vassar College in 1937, was on the board of Goddard Riverside Community Center since the 1950s and served as Vice President from 1969-1972. She was also a Co-Chair and then Chair of the Women's Division of the Legal Aid Society from 19621965. She is survived by her three children, Morton Fearey, Jr. and Mary F. Lollis, both of Edgartown, MA, and John L. S. Fearey of Old Greenwich, CT; nine grandchildren and 15 great-grandchildren. Her daughter, Julia F. Habgood, predeceased her in 1983. Services will be held at St. Andrews Church, 34 North Summer Street, Edgartown, MA on Saturday, April 21 at 11:00 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Goddard Riverside Community Center, 593 Columbus Avenue, New York, NY 10024 will be greatly appreciated. $(6$)FEAREY--Mary. The Board and Staff of Goddard Riverside Community Center mourn the loss of one of our founding Board members. Her many years of leadership, generosity and fervent commitment to our work will be remembered. Stephan Russo, Exec. Dir.
