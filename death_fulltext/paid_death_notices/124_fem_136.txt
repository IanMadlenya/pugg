
        YOUSKEVITCH-Anna Scarpova. Former soloist with the Ballet Russe de Monte Carlo, over 50 years distinguished and prominent New York City ballet teacher and widow of legendary Premier Danseur Igor Youskevitch. Passed away after a brief illness on May 15. Survived by an only daughter, Maria Youskevitch. Funeral services Friday, May 23, 10 AM, Russian Orthodox Cathedral, 75 East 93rd Street. A memorial fund has been established to honor her memory. Donations to go toward a new production of ''Afternoon of a Faun,'' with choreography by Kirk Peterson as part of the Hartford Ballet Tribute to Russian Ballet Series ''Fire and Ice II,'' 226 Farmington Avenue, Hartford, CT 06105.
