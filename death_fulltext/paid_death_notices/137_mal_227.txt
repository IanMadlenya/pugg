
        BRAVER-Rabbi Joseph. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. He was assistant director of the United Synagogue Department of Education and until his death, served the Center for Jewish Education in Baltimore. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Helene, daughter Sharon and all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Seymour L. Essrog, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec VP The Rabbinical Assembly
