
        COWEN-Eleanor B. Harmon. Daughter of Ester Stephens Harmon and William P. Harmon, was born on February 3, 1905 in Waverly, Iowa. After graduating from Nursing College she moved to New York where she worked at Mount Sinai Hospital and instructed at Yonkers Maternity and Homeopathic Hospital, the predecessor of Yonkers General Hospital. A longtime resident of Yonkers, she married Edward Cowen of Yonkers in 1948. One of the first licensed woman pilots in the USA, she and her husband flew their own planes on six of the seven continents. She was preceded in death by her seven brothers and sisters, and her husband. She died at the age of 95 on board the QE2 cruise around the world, off the coast of Africa, on March 24, 2000. She is survived by numerous nieces and nephews, and a host of devoted friends around the globe. A graveside service will be held at 11 AM at Mt. Zion Cemetery, 59-63 54th Avenue, Maspeth, New York 11378, (718) 335-2500, on April 5, 2000.
