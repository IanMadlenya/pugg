
        JACOBS-Jerry. 68, died October 31, Brooklyn, NY, surrounded by loving family, Assemblywoman Rhoda Jacobs, children Ronit, Ethan, Sara. He loved and was loved by those whose lives he touched. A gentle soul, always a mentor and teacher, his legacy will be the memory of his goodness, passionate love of ideas and eagerness to share them, his kindness and pursuit of Tikun Olam-a better world. Born Perth Amboy, NJ, graduated Rutgers University. Shiva through November 6, contact 718-434-0446.
