
        BRYER-Dr. Ben. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted, long-time member, Dr. Ben Bryer. Ben and his beloved wife, the late Elsa Kastor Bryer, had been members of Rodeph Sholom for over 50 years. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his son, Elliot, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
        BRYER-Dr. Ben F. 90, August 20. Husband of the late Elsa, father of Elliott, brother of Bess Hurand and Max B.
