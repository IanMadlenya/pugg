
        LANE-Nora V., 84, died Saturday at The Medical Center at Princeton (NJ). Wife of the late Daniel J. Lane, she is survived by three sons, Daniel, Robert and Nathan and six grandchildren. Mass will be celebrated at 9:30AM today at Saint Paul RC Church, 214 Nassau Street, Princeton. Burial will be in Princeton Cemetery. Donations may be made to Broadway Cares/ Equity Fights AIDS.
