
        SOWINSKI-Stephen L., M.D. The Mount Sinai Hospital of Queens is deeply saddened by the death of Dr. Stephen Sowinski on January 15, 2003. A valued member of our medical staff since 1986, he was a true advocate for patients, nurses and colleagues. As a caring internist and endocrinologist, Dr. Sowinski worked tirelessly to champion education and treatment for people with diabetes. His leadership at the Hospital will be sincerely missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Tina, and the rest of his family. Caryn A. Schwab, Executive Director Arthur N. Gualtieri, M.D., J.D. Medical Director Craig Youner, M.D., President, Medical Staff
