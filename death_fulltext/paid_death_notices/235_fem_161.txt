
        ABDOO--Jayma Ann died August 20th at her home in Oradell, NJ, at age 55 of metastatic breast cancer. At the time of her death, Jayma was an Assistant Dean at Barnard College, Columbia University and an Advisor to Pre-Med, Pre-Law and International Students. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Columbia University School of General Studies, Jayma received her M.A. from the College of William and Mary. Her love for and from her biological family--mother, Helen and late father, Frederick, Lynda, Brian, Geri, Keegan, Cora and Emma--her extended family of ''children''--Lisa, Lucy, Wyatt, Noah, Jeannie, and Becca--and her many relatives and friends will always be alive. A memorial celebration will be held on Thursday, September 21th at 4pm in the James Room, Barnard Hall, Barnard College, Broadway and 117th Street. Contributions may be made in Jayma's memory which will be used to support her passionate committment to equal access to the professions to: Barnard College, 3009 Broadway, New York, NY 10027 Attention: Amy DeRobertis.
