
        BACHRACH - Robert Lee. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Robert Lee Bachrach, son of our late president Alfred R. Bachrach, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Secy.
