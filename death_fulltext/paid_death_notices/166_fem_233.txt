
        PARKS-Blanton S. Of Oyster Bay, New York, died with peace and dignity, at age 90, on November 26th, 2000. Services are private. She will be loved & remembered always by her three children: Melinda Parks Conrad of Wilton, CT., Nicholas R. Parks of Laurel Hollow, New York, and Thomas W. Parks, MD. of San Jose, CA. She had eight grandchildren and one great-grandchild. She is the wife of the late New York OB/ GYN Dr. Thomas Jefferson Parks.
