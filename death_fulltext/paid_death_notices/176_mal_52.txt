
        FRANCIS-Thayer, Jr. Of Marion, MA., and Aiken, SC. Died at his home in Marion with his family by his side on September 3, 2001. Tim, an engineer, was a founder, Chairman, and Chief Executive Officer of the Sippican Corporation and a member of the Massachusetts High Technology Council. As an entrepreneur himself, he loved to encourage and guide fledgling companies. In addition to his family and friends, his passions were playing the piano, flying, and sailing. He is survived by his wife, Nancy, six children and nine grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Beverly Yacht Club in Marion on Saturday, September 15 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to the Marion or Aiken Emergency Medical Services.
