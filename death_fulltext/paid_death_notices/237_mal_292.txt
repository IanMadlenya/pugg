        $(6$)MEED--Benjamin. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of our friend and colleague, Benjamin Meed who, after surviving the Warsaw Ghetto, became a driving force for Holocaust awareness. Ben dedicated his life to ''Never Again,'' working tirelessly to ensure future generations be educated about the Shoah. We extend our deepest condolences to his beloved wife, Vladka, and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser, National Chair Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
