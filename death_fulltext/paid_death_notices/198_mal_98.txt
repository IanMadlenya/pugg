
        SENBER -- Henry ''Hank''. Died at his home on Horatio St. in Greenwich Village on Monday, June 23, 2003, at age 93. He was a former reporter, actor, and publicist. He was predeceased by his wife, the former Genevieve Whitfield. He is survived by nephews Paul Rosenberg of Topanga, CA, and Daniel Rosenberg of Richmond, IN; nieces Joann Hirsch of East Northport, NY, and Betty Miller of Clearwater, FL; eight grandnieces and nephews; and goddaughters Dana and Elisa Johnston of Honolulu and NYC; and his friends at Florent. A memorial service will be held at the Redding, CT, Historical Society's Lonetown Farm Museum on July 12 at 2:30pm. >>AD#
