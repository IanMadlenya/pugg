
        GOODMAN -- Rebecca G. With great love and respect, Becky's family announces that she has passed on. She is predeceased by her beloved husband, Phil Goodman. She was a devoted church member and shared her inspiration by serving in many different ways. She was also a life-long history scholar who authored a text on American history and taught at Hunter College in New York City. Having grown up in the midwest, and studied on the west coast, she ultimately found New York City and never left its richness of music, art, theatre and the Yankees. Her family and friends have always treasured her clear thinking, grace and generosity. Memorial services at at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. Saturday, March 5th, 3:30 PM.
