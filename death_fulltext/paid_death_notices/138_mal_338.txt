
        ROFF-Barry, Ph.D. At Age 51. Loving father of Matthew and Jessica & loving husband of Xenia, died suddenly on July 29, 1998. Dr. Roff was a graduate of Brooklyn College and received his Ph.D. in psychology from The New School of Social Research. For more than a decade Dr. Roff served as Deputy Director of ''Project Renewal, Inc.''. Prior to joining ''Project Renewal'' Dr. Roff was Deputy Director at Altro, Inc. and previously was a senior level administrator at the New York City Department of Mental Health, Mental Retardation and Alcoholism Services. He was on the Board of Directors of the Coalition of Voluntary Mental Health Agencies for several years and was their Treasurer for the past two years. Dr. Roff was a highly respected and well loved professional in the mental health field. His family, friends and colleagues will miss him deeply. Services will be held Friday, 9:15am at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. The family requests that in lieu of flowers donations be made to ''Project Renewal'' or any other homeless services agencies.
        ROFF-Barry. The officers and staff of the Coalition of Voluntary Mental Health Agencies are deeply saddened by the death of our treasurer and friend Barry Roff. We extend our sympathies to Xenia, Jessica, Matt and his entire family. We will miss his wit and wisdom. Mary D. Redd, President Phillip A. Saperia, Executive Director
        ROFF-Barry. The entire staff of the Times Square BID extends its deepest condolences to the family and colleagues of Barry Roff. Barry was with us in Times Square from the beginning, helping in numerous, ongoing ways, and being a loyal friend. The good die young and we are sad.
