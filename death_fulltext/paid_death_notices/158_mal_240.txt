
        KNAPPMAN-William E., died March 19, 2000 at Middlesex Hospital, Middletown, Connecticut. He was born July 20, 1905, in Brooklyn, New York. He is survived by his loving wife of 67 years, Rena (Carpenter) Knappman. His son and daughter-in-law, Edward and Elizabeth and his granddaughter Amanda. A graduate in Chemistry of Pratt Institute, he worked as a research and Manager in the chemical and paint industries until his retirement from EconLabs in 1971. A man of integrity, humor, optimism and kindness, he will be greatly missed by his many friends. Services 2:30 PM Thursday March 23, at the D'Angelo Funeral Home, 22 South Main St. Midddletown, Connecticut.
