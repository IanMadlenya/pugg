
        VARNEY-Norman A. Born in Boston in 1922, he attended The Tilton School and Duke University, graduating in 1943. He served as a lieutenant on active duty in the US Navy until 1945, as a member of a minesweeper support group. He then joined the J. Walter Thompson Company, where he spent his professional career. As a Senior Vice President, he had an important role in the establishment of the New York Mets and Toronto Blue Jays Baseball Clubs. He retired in 1987. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Susan; his sons Norman of Philadelphia and Robert of San Diego, daughter Claire of Los Angeles, and four grandchildren. Memorials to: The American Parkinson Disease Association, 1250 Hylan Blvd, Staten Island, New York 10305. Service and interment will be private.
