
        HAFT-Doris N. On July 3, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Harold Haft. She died as she lived with dignity, fortitude and grace. A cum laude graduate of Cornell University, she also earned an MBA from Columbia University. She lived her adult life in Manhattan and was an Account Executive for many years with Josephthal, Lyon and Ross. Contributions in her name are suggested to Mount Sinai Hospital. A Memorial Service will be 1:15PM, on July 7 at the Riverside, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
