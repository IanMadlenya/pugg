
        WHEELOCK-William Hawxhurst, II. Of Easthampton, NY. Passed on July 18. Bill was a graduate of St. Paul's School in Concord, NH, Class of '55; Hobart College in Geneva, NY, '59. He leaves his two brothers, Frederick and Morgan; two children, Lindsay and Bill; and one grandson, Hawk. He will be remembered for his tours of Long Island, his broad smile, deep bass voice and enthusiastic character. A memorial service will be held at 4PM, July 22 at St. Luke's Church in Easthampton.
