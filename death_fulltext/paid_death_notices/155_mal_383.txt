
        SHOSTAK-Joseph. The trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services mourn the passing of Joseph Shostak, the executive vice president and past executive director of The Shield Institute, a new affiliate of JBFCS. Mr. Shostak's committment and dedication to people with disabilities made him a model for other community leaders and a figure whose presence will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mr. Shostak's wife, Claire, and other members of his family. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Alan B. Siskind, PhD, Executive Vice President
        SHOSTAK-Joseph. The Board of Governors and members of Willow Ridge Country Club sadly note the passing of Joseph Shostak, a distinguished member of this club. Our deepest condolences are offered to his wife Claire and to their entire family. Myron Melnikoff, President Board of Governors
