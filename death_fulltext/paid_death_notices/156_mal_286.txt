
        MOLEY-Malcolm, M.D.. The entire Saint Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center family joins with the physicians and staff in The Department of Surgery in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and devoted colleague, Malcolm Moley, M.D., an Attending Physician in the Dept of Surgery for over 45 years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Janis, a dedicated Associate Trustee of The Hospital Center, his entire family, and all who loved him. Lawrence S. Huntington, Chairman, Board of Trustees Sigurd H. Ackerman, M.D., President & CEO Carlton Boxhill, M.D., President, Medical Board Richard A. Marks, M.D., Interim Chairman, Department of Surgery
