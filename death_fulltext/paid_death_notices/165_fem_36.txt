
        COTTRELL - Helen Elizabeth. Died October 1, 2000. She was 88 years of age and resided in Mystic, CT. Helen was a graduate of the College of Mt. St. Vincent 1931 and Columbia Law School 1934. She was admitted to the Bar in both New York and Connecticut and worked with the US Attorney's office in NYC. During World War II she worked in Washington, DC with the Marketing Law Survey and the Office of Price Administration. She is survived by her sister Leone C. Birdsall and seven nieces and nephews. Private graveside service for the family will be in Westerly, RI at the River Bend Cemetery where she will join her parents Leone Balfe and Edgar H. Cottrell in the family mausoleum.
