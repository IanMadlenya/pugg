
        KURTZ--Dora. Wife of Irving. Mother of Marcia and Judy. Grandmother of Aaron and Elena. Mother-in-law of Sidney. Aunt, sister and sister-inlaw of the Kurtz/Rosenstein clans. Her life revolved around her near and extended family. She had a great zest for life, a wonderful sense of humor and she loved to sing and dance. She was truly one of the sweet souls of the earth and taught everyone who came into contact with her that love was the most important thing in life.
