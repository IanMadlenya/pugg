
        NATHANSON-Shirley Mendoza, on Sept. 15, 1999. Deeply loved wife of the late Jules Nathanson. Dear mother of Irwin ''Sandy'' (Karen) Nathanson and Howard (Sarah Anne Patz) Nathanson. Dear grandmother to Eric, Scott, Andrew, Rebecca, Brooke & Ariel Nathanson. Dear sister-in-law, aunt, cousin and friend. Life member of Temple Israel of Jamaica. Administrator 22 years, Long Island Jewish-Hillside Hospital. Volunteer Ronald McDonald House of L.I. and many other organizations. Resident of the Bronx, Jamaica, Forest Hills, East Quogue and St. Louis. A warm, lively woman and the heart of her family. Donations to the Ronald McDonald House of LI, 267-07 76th Ave., New Hyde Park, NY 11042. Funeral service Sunday, September 19, 10:30 AM, at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, Queens Boulevard at 76th Road, Forest Hills. Interment Hungarian Union Fields Cemetery, Glendale, New York.
