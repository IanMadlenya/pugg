
        WEINSTEIN--Sylvia (nee Miller), on February 24th, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Sidney Weinstein and the late Harry Rosman. Treasured sister of the late Naomi Babian. Loving cousin of Ruth Becker and her much loved niece, Barbara Akerman. The memory of her love of life and laughter, her wit and charm lives on as a comfort and inspiration to those who knew and loved her. But ''O for the touch of a vanished hand and the sound of a voice that is still.'' Graveside services Sunday, 11:30 AM, at Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, NY. Contributions in Sylvia's memory to a charity of choice.
