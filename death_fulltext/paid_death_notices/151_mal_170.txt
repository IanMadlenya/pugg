
        POPPER-Walter. The Board of Directors and Staff of the Center for Jewish History extend their deepest sympathies to Betty and the entire Popper family. Walter was a true friend of the Jewish people and a beloved supporter of The Center. His vision for increased Jewish education and the unity of the Jewish people will forever be with us and guide us into the future. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Bruce Slovin, Chairman Joyce C. Kitey, Sr. VP
