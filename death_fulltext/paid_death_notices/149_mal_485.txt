
        WHEELWRIGHT-Joseph Balch, M.D. 93, of Kentfield, CA. Pioneer of Jungian analysis. Survived by his wife Jane Hollister Wheelwright, also 93; his brother George W. Wheelwright, III of Tiburon, CA; his two children Lynda Wheelwright Schmidt of Brooklin, ME, and John Hollister Wheelwright of Point Reyes, CA. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Jung Institute in San Francisco, CA.
