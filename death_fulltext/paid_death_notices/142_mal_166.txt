
        GOLDBERG-Richard. The Board of Trustees, Administration and the entire New York Community Hospital mourn the loss of our dear friend and colleague Richard Goldberg, who served devotedly as a member of our Board of Trustees for eleven years and as Vice-Chairman for five years, until his death on November 12, 1998 in a fatal car accident in Florida. We express our deep sorrow and condolences to his wife Joan, their children and their families. Dick will be sorely missed by all of us. Services will be held on Sunday, November 15, 1998 at 1:00 PM at Riverside Chapel, 312 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, New York. George Weinberger, President, Board of Trustees Lin H. Mo, President & Chief Executive Officer
