
        GOLDSTEIN-Martin E. Of New Rochelle, N.Y. died Sunday, November 26, 2000 at the age of 66. He was a partner in the intellectual property field in the law firm Darby & Darby, in New York City. He is survived by his wife Judy, daughter Sue, son Jay and four grandchildren.
        GOLDSTEIN-Martin E. The law firm of Darby and Darby, P.C., mourns the death of our colleague, Marty Goldstein, a superb lawyer, wise counselor, and good friend. He was a member of our firm since 1990, served as President of The New York Intellectual Property Law Association in 1996 through 1997, and gave his time and energy to countless other organizations and institutions. We will miss his leadership, his experience and his wit. Our deepest condolences go to Judy, and the entire Goldstein family.
        GOLDSTEIN-Martin E. Beth El Synogogue of New Rochelle mourns the loss of our longtime devoted member and Honorary Trustee. Heartfelt condolences to Judy and the entire family. Melvin N. Sirner, Rabbi Russell Fayer, President
