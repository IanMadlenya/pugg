
        LOBEL-Henry K. The National Board of The ALYN Hospital for Handicapped Children in Israel mourns the passing of our esteemed board member and great benefactor, Henry K. Lobel. As Chairman of the Board of SmithEdwards-Dunlap, he, his beloved wife, Edith and his family gave without reservation to ensure the well-being of the handicapped children of ALYN Hospital. Our heartfelt condolences to Edith and his family. Simone P. Blum, Chairman of the Board
