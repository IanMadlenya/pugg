
        BINGHAM-Henry J. III. On October 13, 2002. Most dearly loved husband of Marianne G. Bingham. Graduate of Dartmouth College, Class of 1952, and Amos Tuck Business School, Class of 1953. Executive Managing Director of Van Eck Associates and Executive Vice President of Van Eck Securities. Youngest Junior Partner of Spencer Trask & Company. In 1968, upon incorporation of the firm, elected Vice President and Director. Also survived by four children, Susan, Lynda, Fiona and William, as well as nine grandchildren. He is also survived by his sister-in law, Patricia and his nephew Paul. Family will receive friends Tuesday, 4-9PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st Street. Funeral service Wednesday at 2 PM, at St. Thomas Church, 5th Avenue at 53rd Street. If so desired, contributions can be made to Dartmouth College.
