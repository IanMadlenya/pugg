
        OKSNER--Robert S. (Bob) October 14, 1916 -February 18, 2007 of Delray Beach, FL. formerly of Paterson and Teaneck, NJ and Saugerties, NY. Loving and beloved husband of Patricia (Kinzler) for more than 64 years. Adored father of Rebecca (Ron) Berger, Margaret Jecko, and Amy (James) Kendrick. A Loving grandfather and great-grandfather, an award winning long time artist for DC comics, also a noted syndicated cartoonist. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date.
