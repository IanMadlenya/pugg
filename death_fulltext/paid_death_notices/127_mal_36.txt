
        ABLES-Richard L. (Dick), on August 1, 1997. Loving son of the late Edward and Florence Ables. Dear brother of Mildred Levine and the late Cecelia Sanders. Adored uncle of Alan Levine, Lynn Weisberg, and Barry Levine, great uncle. Dick was a well known person in the music business. He was road manager to Pearl Bailey and Louis Bellson, pianist/songwriter Erroll Garner, and guitarist Bucky Pizzarelli. His career spanned over 65 years. He began as a saxophonist in the original Isham Jones Band along with Woody Herman. Services Monday, August 4, 1:15 PM, at Park West-Riverside, 333 Amsterdam Ave. at 76th St.
