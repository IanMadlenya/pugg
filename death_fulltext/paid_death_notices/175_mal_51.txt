        RUBINO-Robert, M.D. The Physicians and Staff of the Divisions of Cardiology of Northshore University Hospital and Long Island Jewish Medical Center deeply regret the untimely passing of our friend and colleague, Dr. Robert Rubino. Bob touched the lives of his patients as a caring physician, his colleagues as a wonderful coworker and each of us as a friend with his unique humor. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Debbie, son Justin and the Rubino and Guzov families.
