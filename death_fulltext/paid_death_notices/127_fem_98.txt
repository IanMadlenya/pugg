
        GALIBER-Ethel Bowser, 96, mother of former State Senator Joseph L. Galiber of the Bronx, August 14, survived by husband of seventy-six years, Joseph F. Galiber; daughter Marilyn G. Morgan, two granddaughters, three greatgranddaughters and many other loving family members and friends. She will be greatly missed and will always be in our hearts.
