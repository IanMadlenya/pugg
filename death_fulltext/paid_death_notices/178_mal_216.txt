
        BROWN-Richard Howard. On November 8. A pioneer of credit card electronic reservations for the hospitality industry as co-founder of Telemax in the 1950's. Later turned his marketing skills to the performing arts with an innovative concept of cluster management for small to medium-sized performing groups with Directional Concepts. Survived by his son Zhu Min, his sister Cynthia Cowan and remembered as a good friend and manager by all those who worked with him.
