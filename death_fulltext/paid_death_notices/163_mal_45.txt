
        KATZ-Jerome. Beloved husband of Rosalyn. Devoted father of Harriet Katz and Joel and Mary Katz. Loving brother of Mortimer and Irving Katz, Shirley Cohen and the late Alice Barsky. He graduated from New York Law School in 1938 and was a member of the American Arbitration Association specializing in labor arbitration. Services Sunday, 11:30 AM, at Temple Emanuel, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Friends of Helen Keller National Center, Sands Point, NY 11050, will be greatly appreciated.
