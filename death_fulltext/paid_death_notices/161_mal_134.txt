
        BEY-Khalil, M.D., age 65, died June 10, 2000 in Bretten, Germany. Survived by his wife of forty-two years, Ingrid Bey, his son, Dr. Tareg Bey of Dallas, Texas, his daughters Dr. Jasmin Bey Cowin of New York, Nadja Bey of Germany, and his grandchild Christian Bey. Patricia Bey, Ken Cowin & the Cowin family will miss him. He was an extraordinary presence in everybody's life and selflessly helped countless people. Services will be held in Germany. Donations in his memory may be sent to Doctors Without Borders.
