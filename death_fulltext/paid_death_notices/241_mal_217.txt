
        RAM--Irving M. Born on January 17, 1921, was a devoted husband, father, grandfather, and great-grandfather. He passed away on February 13th at his home in Boca Raton, Florida. He is survived by his wife Betty, his three children from his first marriage to Ruth Ram who died in 1987: Carol (Harry) Bourne, Eric (Bryna) Ram, and Leonard (Lauren) Ram, and five grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Funeral services today Friday, February 16th at 12:30pm, at Cedar Park Cemetery -Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, 35 Forest Avenue, Paramus, New Jersey. Memorial services, Sunday, February 25th at 3:00pm, at Riverside Gordon, 7205 West Atlantic Avenue, Delray Beach, Florida. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Jewish Federation of SPBC, 9901 Donna Klein Blvd., Boca Raton, FL 33428.
