
        ROEDELHEIMER - Dorothy Lears. Born January 5, 1904, died peacefully in LaJolla, Ca January 2, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Edgar Roedelheimer, and loving mother of Judith Pacht, grandmother of Jonathan and Carolyn Robbins Siegel and great grandmother of Jessica Siegel. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Edgar and Dorothy Roedelheimer Scholarship Fund, Dept of Journalism, Syracuse University, 820 Comstock Ave, Syracuse N.Y. 13244.
