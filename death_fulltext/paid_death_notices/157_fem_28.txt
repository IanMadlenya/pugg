
        ACKERMAN - Audrey. Beloved wife of Arthur, devoted mother of Elissa Green (Jodie), Seth and Alan. Loving grandmother of Robin and Austin. Lifelong educator whose teaching career began in NYC elementary and high schools and extended into Kingsborough & Nassau Community Colleges. She was a master quilter who showed the joy of quilting to others. Died February 2nd, 2000. Services at 10:30 AM, Friday, February 4th, at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Turpike, Woodbury, L.I. (Directions: 516-921-5757).
