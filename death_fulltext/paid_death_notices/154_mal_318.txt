
        CONNORS-Donald F. Professor Emeritus at Fordham University, died at his home on Saturday, November 27, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Helen Newman Connors. He is survived by five sons, Dr. Donald F. Jr., Thomas, Michael, Edward and Christopher; two daughters, Harriette DeLuca and Helen Bates. He is survived by 20 grandchildren and 26 greatgrandchildren. Calling hours are Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9, at Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave, Scarsdale, NY. Funeral Wednesday at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, in Scarsdale, at 10 AM.
