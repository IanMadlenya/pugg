
        McBRIDE-Rita. 77, of New York City, died July 3, 2002, at Calvary Hospital in Bronx, NY. Born December 14, 1924, in Lynn, MA, she was the daughter of the late William and Annabelle Girardin McBride of Easton, PA. She was a graduate of Easton High School in 1942 and of the College of New Rochelle in New Rochelle, NY. After college she worked as a fashion model and subsequently was employed by Doubleday Book and Music clubs. Survivors are a sister-in law, Janet Herman McBride, and a nephew, Clement G. McBride Jr., of Easton. Burial will be private with a graveside service at Gethsemane Cemetery in Easton, PA.
