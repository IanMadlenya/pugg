
        ROBINSON--Richard G. 83, died peacefully at home on June 11 after a remarkable fight against pancreatic cancer. Adored husband of Phyllis Kenner Robinson for 61 years. Cherished father of Nancy Thompson, son of Robert and Jennie Tashman Robinson, brother of the late Riva Robinson and Dr. Arthur Robinson, uncle of Jeffrey Robinson, Linda Robinson, Ann Jacobson and Margery Cantor. Graduate of Lincoln School of Teachers College, Harvard College and NYU. Former Chief Psychologist of Lenox Hill Hospital, Riverdale Mental Health Center, Psychologist for the Hoffman School, Riverdale, and the Overseas School of Rome. Private practice in NYC, Pound Ridge, NY, Nantucket and Rome. Served in the Army Air Force in WWII. He gave love, laughter and courage to many and set an example for a rich, principled and rewarding life. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., Tuesday, June 21 at 3:30PM. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Cabrini Hospice, attn: Mary Cook. 227 E. 19th St., 10th Floor, NY, NY 10003.
