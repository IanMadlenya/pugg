
        KULUKUNDIS--Calliope M., 102 years old, died peacefully September 27 at her home in New York City. She was the widow of Manuel E. Kulukundis, the mother of M. Michael and George, the mother-inlaw of Tara, the grandmother of Eric, Cassandra, Manuel and Christopher and the great-grandmother of Alexandra, Michael and Isabella. Family will receive friends on Tuesday, 5-8 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81 Street. Funeral service Wednesday, 11 AM, at the Holy Trinity Greek Cathedral, 334 E. 74 St. Interment to follow at Woodlawn Cemetery. The family requests that instead of flowers, donations be made to Autism Speaks, 2 Park Avenue, New York.
