
        ABERNETHY-Mary. On October 30 at age 96. Beloved wife of the late Edward. Devoted mother of Patricia Suppa, Michael Abernethy, Maureen Gerhard and Eileen Connolly. Also survived by nineteen grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. A graduate of St. John's University with a Masters in Education. She retired from the NYC Board of Education after 42 years. Funeral from the McManus Funeral Home, 2001 Flatbush Avenue, Wednesday 9:45am. Funeral mass 10:30am our Lady Help of Christian Church. Interment Holy Cross cemetery. Visiting 2-5pm and 7-9pm.
