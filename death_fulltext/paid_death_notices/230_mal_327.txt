
        LISKOW -- Arthur Stanley, M.D., 65. Of Tenafly, New Jersey, passed away Thursday, March 23. He was an Army Veteran of the Vietnam War. He was a radiation oncologist. He graduated from the University of Cincinnati Medical School, completing his medical training at NYU and Memorial Sloan - Kettering and was associated with Columbia Presbyterian before joining the private practice of Rosenbaum and Rosenfield. For the past ten years, he has been treating patients throughout the country. He is survived by his wife Karen and his two children, James and Amy. For info Barrett Funeral Home, Tenafly.
