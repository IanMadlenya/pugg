
        GOLDFRANK-Thomas, 85, after a brief illness, June 18, 2002. Loving and beloved father of Edward and daughters-in-law Janice Goldfrank and Victoria Ettlinger; grandfather of Nicholas. Predeceased by wife Emma Pearlin, son Frederick and three siblings. He was an MIT graduate and Licensed Professional Engineer who made his career engineering and supervising the construction of large scale projects. One of the last of these before retiring involved a major on-site supervisory role in the building of the SUNY/Purchase Campus where for the rest of his life he enjoyed a wide variety of perfomances and academic programs.
