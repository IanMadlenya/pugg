
        AMORE-Phyllis Amy (nee Schneider), 49, of Coconut Creek, FL. Phyllis passed away Tuesday, April 22, 2003. Beloved daughter, wife, and sister. She is survived by her mother, Sylvia Schneider, husband Glenn Amore, sister Judy Schneider, and many relatives, including the Julian Gould family and scores of friends. Phyllis was born on July 9, 1953 and raised in East Meadow. Her generous spirit, kind heart and spiritual nature made her beloved by her family and many friends.
