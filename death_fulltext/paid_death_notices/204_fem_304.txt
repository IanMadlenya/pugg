
        REINER--Lillian. On January 30, 2004, at the age of 96. Beloved wife of Dr. Leopold Reiner. Loving aunt and great aunt of Lydia, James, Robert and Laurie Nolan; Doris, Stephen and Donald Young; David Siegel, Janet Kaplan and Ruth Beer. She was a graduate of Cornell University and Columbia Teachers College. She was a registered Dietician and acted as Director of Dietetics at Beth Israel Hospital, Boston, MA and at the Protestant Welfare Agency, New York, NY. Graveside services, Sunday, February 1, 2004 at 1:15 PM, Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
