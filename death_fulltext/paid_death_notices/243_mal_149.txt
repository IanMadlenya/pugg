
        WEINGAST--David, 94, of Ridgefield, CT, and Fire Island, NY, died on April 12th. He leaves behind his beloved wife, Beatrice, four children, Beth, Erica, Jeremy and Victor; and six grandchildren. An educator and author he taught in Newark, NJ, and was Superintendent of Schools in Ridgefield, CT. Services will be held Sunday at 10:30 am, at Temple Shearith Israel, 46 Peaceable St., Ridgefield, CT 06877.
