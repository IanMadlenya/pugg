
        MAGRAM-Lew. On December 27, 1997, at the age of 81. Devoted husband of Evelyn, beloved father of Erv and Melanie. Achieved great notoriety during the 1950's as the ''shirtmaker to the stars.'' Founded the national mail order apparel company that still bears his name. A wonderful man who will be missed by all who knew him. Services and interment will take place near his Florida home. A memorial service will be held in NYC at a date to be announced.
