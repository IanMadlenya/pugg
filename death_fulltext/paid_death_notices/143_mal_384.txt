
        FINKEL-Saul. The Young Israel of West Hempstead has suffered a great loss with the passing of Saul Finkel, Monday, December 28th, 1998. Mr. Finkel was a longtime beloved member and a veteran of World War II. He is survived by his wife Muriel, two sons Mark and Herb, and sister Marilyn Lerner, and is mourned by the entire community. Rabbi Yehuda Kelemer Mark Nathanson, President
