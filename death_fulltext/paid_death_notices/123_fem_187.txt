
        MARVIN-Myla Kurcias. Age 71, died April 17, 1997, in Jerusalem. She was much loved by her sister Joan Kurcias Rosenbach and brother-in-law Hans Rosenbach (Chevy Chase, MD). Adoring aunt of Margo Rosenbach and Robert Gottlieb (Chesnut Hill, MA), and Stuart (Uri) and Guila Rosenbach (Jerusalem). Also survived by eight grandnieces and grandnephews. Contributions may be made to Jewish National Fund.
