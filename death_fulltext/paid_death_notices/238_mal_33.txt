
        ROSENBERG--Harold. Be loved husband, friend and companion to his wife Betty. Generous and caring father and step-father to his sons. Loving grandpa to his grandchildren and great grandchildren. In addition to nurturing his family and his business, Harold was a lover of all the arts and especially photography. No day was ever too busy or too long that an evening the ''City'' could or should be put off. Services Friday 1:15pm, the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Paramus, New Jersey.
