
        HAAR-Murray Sidney. Age 83, of Jersey City, NJ, died October 28. Beloved husband of Shirley J. Haar. Devoted father of Lynn and Michael Reichgott, Matthew and Betsie Haar, Diane Haar and Jack Lyons. Doting grandfather of nine. Brother of Frances Yeston. He was Vice President of Temple Beth-El in Jersey City, where contributions may be sent.
