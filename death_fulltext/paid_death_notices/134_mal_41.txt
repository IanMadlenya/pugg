
        LEHRMAN-Howard Z. 75, of Fairfield, CT., died March 2, 1998 after a brief illness. He was the son of the late Dr. Philip R. Lehrman and Wanda Lehrman. Survived by his son Philip Charles Lehrman of Stamford, CT, his sister Lynne Lehrman Weiner of White Plains, NY, a nephew Meyer Weiner and two nieces Erica Weiner-Hanley and Dr. Sara Weiner. Graveside services Thursday,March 5, 11 A.M., Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York. Donations may be made to the Bridge Fund, 171 E. Post Road, White Plains, N.Y. 10601.
