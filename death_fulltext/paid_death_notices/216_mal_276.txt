
        RANUM--Christopher S. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the untimely passing of Christopher S. Ranum, son of Atlantic's Golf Course Superintendent, Robert A. Ranum. We extend our sincere condolences and heartfelt sympathy to Bob and Allison, his brother, Matthew, and his sister, Amanda. Visitation will be at Brockett Funeral Home, 203 Hampton Road, Southampton on Thursday 7-9PM and Friday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Funeral service will be held Saturday at the First Presbyterian Church, 2 South Main Street, Southampton at 11 A.M. Robert E. Morrow, President Harvey L. Radler, Secretary
