        $(6$)WASSERSTEIN--Wendy. The Officers, Board of Directors and Staff of Dramatists Play Service mourn the passing of our talented client, colleague and friend. We proudly published several of her plays including The Heidi Chronicles, winner of the Pulitzer Prize and Tony, The Sisters Rosensweig and Uncommen Women. Wendy's writing had a special insight into politics and education, particularly from a woman's point of view. Previously, she was a member of our Board of Directors. Our deep sympathy goes to her family and friends. Dramatists Play Service Stephen Sultan, President
        $(6$)WASSERSTEIN -- Wendy. Deeply saddened by the loss of our friend, Wendy Wasserstein. With great respect we extend our condolences and warmest, best wishes to the Wasserstein family and friends. The Campaign for Stuyvesant Endowment Fund, Inc. Paul M. Weichsel, Ph.D., Chairman
