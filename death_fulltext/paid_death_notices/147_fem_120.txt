
        DIANA-Carol York. The Board of the Women's Committee of the Central Park Conservancy mourns the loss of a committed member and devoted educator on the Children's Education Committee. We will miss her joyous enthusiasm in sharing her love and knowledge of Central Park with students. Our sincere condolences to her husband, Michael, her children, Elizabeth and Weldon, and her family. Toni Peebler, President Women's Committee Mikel Witte, Chair Children's Education Committee
