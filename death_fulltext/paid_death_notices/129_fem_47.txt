
        OLSON-Vivian A. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Vivian A. Olson, beloved mother of Kathryn Greenberg, a cherished leader in our community, and founder and Chairman of the Board of the New York Legal Assistance Group, a UJA-Federation beneficiary agency. Kathryn and her husband, Alan C. Greenberg, are enormously beloved, admired and respected throughout the community for their extraordinary leadership, commitment and philanthropy on behalf of UJA-Federation and many other organizations worldwide. We extend our sincerest condolences to the entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
