
        ROBINSON-Horace B.B. The Board of Directors of Youth Foundation, Inc. express deep sorrow at the death of our distinguished long-time member (1971-2000), Horace B.B. Robinson, on October 3, 2000. As Chairman of the Foundation's Investment and Law Committees for many years, he provided dedicated leadership which will be sorely missed. The Foundation extends its heartfelt condolences to his family. A. David Russell, President Mrs. Donald M. Liddell, Vice President Guy N. Robinson, Secretary S. Scott Nicholls, Jr., Treasurer Robert W. Radsch, Assistant Secretary
