
        STARK-Frances L., Dr. Formerly of Fort Lee, N.J. Died Boca Raton, FL., Dec. 8, 1998. An optometrist in Union City for over fifty years. She served on the N.J. State Board of Optometry & the Fort Lee School Board for over twenty years. She is survived by a daughter Paula Stark Rosenfeld of Boca Raton, and Dr. James Stark of Norfolk, VA. Funeral Service December 10, 10:30AM, at Gutterman-Musicant Hackensack, New Jersey.
