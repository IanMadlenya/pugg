
        NOBLE-Marshall Hays, 78 years old. Of Pawcatuck, CT, died of cancer January 8, 2002, at Lawrence & Memorial Hospital in New London, CT. He was the beloved husband of Carolie Woods Noble and devoted stepfather of five Hollingsworth chldren. He was a graduate of Culver Military Academy and Princeton University. After serving in the US Army special forces in World War II, he was a career Foreign Service officer in the United States Department of State, with extensive postings in Southeast Asia. He was Deputy Chief of Mission in Luxembourg. Services will be held Saturday, 1 PM Jan. 12, at the Calvary Church, 27 Church St., Stonington, CT. Contributions will be appreciated by the Community Cancer Center, Lawrence & Memorial Hospital, 365 Montauk Ave., New London, CT 06320, or Visiting Nurses Assoc. of Southeastern Conn., 403 North Frontage Rd, Waterford, CT 06385.
