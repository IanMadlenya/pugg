
        COHEN-Abraham. President and owner of Hartz & Company, manufacturers of mens tailored clothing. Beloved husband of Vera. He will be missed by family, their friends and co-workers. Services Wednesday, June 21 12 noon,at Plaza Memorial, 91 Street and Amsterdam Avenue
        COHEN-Abraham (Al).The Board of Directors of the Clothing Manufacturers Association of the U.S.A. mourn the loss of Al Cohen, chairman and president of Hartz & Co., a leader in the men's tailored clothing industry. Our deepest condolences to his family. Homi B. Patel, President Harvey J. Weinstein, Chairman Robert A. Kaplan, Exec. Director
