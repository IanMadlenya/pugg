
        ROGOFF--Mifa August 15, 1915 -July 9, 2006. Family and friends mourn the passing of Sulamith (Mifa) Rogoff, legendary hostess, fluent speaker of five languages, superb cook, world traveler, and champion AIDS walker. She is survived by her two daughters, Ilana and Tamar, her four grandchildren, Dawne, Heidi, Joshua, and Ariel, and two greatgrandchildren, Arlo and Lois. Her family is extended by inlaws: Richard, Bob, Claudia, Ben and Pablo. Memorial services will be held at Gramercy Park, Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Ave. NY, NY. Thursday, July 13th 11:30 am.
