
        SAYLES-Hope. At her home, Laurelmead, Blackstone Boulevard, Providence, RI, February 27, at age 92. Daughter of the late Frank Arthur Sayles and Mary Dorr (Ames) Sayles. A resident of New York for much of her life, she was an assiduous world traveler. She is survived by a nephew, Paul C. Nicholson, Jr., of Providence; three nieces, Mary B. Braga of Middleburg, VA, Martha N.S. Livingston of Bristol, RI, and Lee Day Gillespie of Cambridge, MA. Burial was in Swan Point Cemetery in Providence.
