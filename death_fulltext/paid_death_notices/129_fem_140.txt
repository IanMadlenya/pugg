
        ROSS-Mildred. With profound sorrow the Officers, Board and staff of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations mourn the death of a cherished friend and devoted collegue. A truly elegant and gentle woman, she lovingly served our sacred cause for many years with quiet dedication and caring concern. We extend heartfelt condolences to her beloved life's mate, Matt, her bereaved children, grandchildren and loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President Rabbi Alexander M. Schindler, President Emeritus
