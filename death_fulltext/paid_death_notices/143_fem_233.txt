
        COHEN-Jeanette J. Resident of Boca Raton, FL and NYC, died on Thursday, December 24, 1998, at Calvary Hospital after an illness. She was the beloved wife of Jerome J. Cohen, mother of Cheryl Krauss of Freeport, New York, Iris Bessell of Lenox, MA., Robert K. Cohen of Scarsdale, New York. Loving grandmother of Nancy Litman of NYC, Norma Bessell of Boston, Mass., and Adam, Jessica, and Haley Cohen of Scarsdale, NY. Service will be held at 12 Noon, Sunday, December 27th, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 W. Broad St., Mount Vernon, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to either the American Cancer Society or th National Alopecia Areata Foundation or any other charitable organization.
