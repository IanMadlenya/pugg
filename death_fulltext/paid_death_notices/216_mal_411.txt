
        TOMME -- Ronald Preston. Known for his role as Bruce Sterling in the CBS soap opera ''Love of Life'' for 21 years. Died on January 29, 2005, at his home in NYC after a long illness. Ronald is survived by his loving wife Jennifer and three brothers, John, Michael and Steven. Mr. Tomme, born in Chicago, studied at Actors Company and was in productions of ''Cactus Flower,'' ''Bell Book and Candle,'' ''Plaza Suite,'' to name a few. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, on Saturday, February 5th, 3:30 pm. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice of New York.
