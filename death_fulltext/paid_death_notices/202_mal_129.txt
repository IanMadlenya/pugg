
        CREHORE--William W. III. 76, of Lynchburg, VA, died Wednesday, November 12, 2003, at Virginia Baptist Hospital. He was married to Shirley Kraft Crehore for 54 years. Mr. Crehore was born in New York, NY, the son of the late William W. Crehore, Jr., and Edna Forde Mellor. He proudly served our country in the US Navy during World War II, and was retired from Mobil Oil Corporation where he worked as an engineering manager. The graduate of Lehigh University is survived by his wife, two sons, four daughters, and nine grandchildren. Memorial services to be held at St. John's Episcopal Church, Lynchburg VA.
