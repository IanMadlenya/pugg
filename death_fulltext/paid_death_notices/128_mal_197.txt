
        FELDMAN-S. Newton. Passed away Friday evening, Sept. 12 after a long illness, bravely suffered. Son of Sam Feldman and Blanche Passerman, late of Phila, Penn. Newton was a veteran of W.W. II, and retired as a Lt. Commander of the U.S. Naval Reserve. He was a devoted son, uncle, and mentor to many colleagues in the Securities industries and at the Bar. Newton was an active supporter of the New York City Police Dept., a respected member of the NYC Bar Association, and the Harvard Club, and many professional committees. He will be sorely missed by his entire family. Graveside services Wednesday, Sept. 17th, 1:30 P.M. at Arlington National Cemetery. A memorial service will be held in New York City at a date and time to be announced. The Feldman Family
