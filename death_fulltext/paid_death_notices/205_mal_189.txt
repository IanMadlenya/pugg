
        DOMMERICH--Louis Alexander. 75, of Palm Beach, FL, died on February 13, 2004, in Greenwich, CT. He was born in New York City on January 2, 1929. The son of the late Louis F. Dommerich and Margaret Whitehead Dommerich, longtime residents of Greenwich. His paternal great-grandfather founded a factoring firm in New York City known as L.F. Dommerich & Co., which became a division of Chemical Bank. He is survived by his wife Jane Morgan Dommerich of Palm Beach, FL, his only child Sally Lineberry and her two children, Morgan Ann and William, of Birmingham, Alabama. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, February 18, at 11 am, in Christ Church, 254 East Putnam Ave, Greenwich, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to the Greenwich Hospital Building Fund, 5 Perryridge Rd, Greenwich, CT 06830.
