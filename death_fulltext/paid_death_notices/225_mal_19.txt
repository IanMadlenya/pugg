
        DAVENPORT -- W. Gerald (Jerry). The Howard Memorial Fund and the community in general lost a great leader and humanitarian in W. Gerald Davenport. His service as President of the Howard Memorial Fund and his leadership and guidance to many other organizations was unparalleled. He is survived by wife, Edith, and sons, David and Dexter, and the people whose lives were enriched by his efforts and who were encouraged by the scholarship assistance provided by the Fund. Memorial Service will be held Saturday, October 8, 2005, 1 P.M., Salem United Methodist Church, 2190 7th Avenue 129th St, NYC.
