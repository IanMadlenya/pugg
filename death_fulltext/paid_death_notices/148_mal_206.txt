
        ABRAMOVITZ-Ann. The trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services are saddened by the May 19th passing of Ann Abramovitz, the mother of Dr. Robert Abramovitz, our esteemed colleague and Chief Psychiatrist. We extend our deepest sympathies to Dr. Abramovitz; his wife, Dr. Mimi Abramovitz; and other members of his family. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Seymour R. Askin, Jr, Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D, Executive Vice President Paul Levine, CSW, Associate Executive Vice President
