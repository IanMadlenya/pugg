
        EZRATTY--Sarah. Died on March 29, 2005 at the age of 96, after a long & blessed life. She arrived on Ellis Island as an only child in 1909. She leaves behind four loving sons, Albert, Joseph, Bernard & Martin; & four daughtersin-law, Essie, Pearl, Dorothy & Rona; 11 grandchildren & 21 great-grandchildren. She was married 57 years to her one & only, the late Mentech Louis Ezratty. She was the grand matriarch of the Ezratty family, whose wonderful smiling face & optimistic attitude cheered all of those around her. She always saw her glass as half full. She will be missed & always remembered.
