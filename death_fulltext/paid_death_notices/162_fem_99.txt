
        JOSEPH-Mavis Ione. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Mavis Ione Joseph, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
