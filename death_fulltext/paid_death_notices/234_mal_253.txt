
        MORRISON--Herbert. Passed away peacefully from cancer on July 20, 2006. He was born on July 31, 1924 in East New York, son of Benjamin and Olga Moskowitz. He served as a medic in the US Army during WWII. He graduated with a degree in business from the NYU School of Commerce in 1946. After managing WPAC, the local radio station in Patchogue, NY, he became an executive in the financial services industry, and for many years after ''retirement,'' did parttime accounting work. He was active in sports, from stickball in Brooklyn to golf post-retirement, and was a dedicated Mason for over 60 years, and was master of the Elbe Lodge in Manhattan, and also in his local lodge in Bayshore, Long Island, NY. His wife , Lila Rosen Morrison, of 51 years, his daughter, Nan Jody Morrison of NYC, and a brother-in-law, Stanley Rosen of Seattle, survive him. Contributions may be made to the Masonic Home, in Utica, NY. A memorial service will be held Sunday, July 23, at 4PM at the B'nai Israel Reform Temple, 67 OakdaleBohemia Rd, Oakdale, NY.
