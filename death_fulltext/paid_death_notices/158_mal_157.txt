
        EARLY-Albert D., 86, of Sarasota, Florida, and Chatham, Massachusetts, died March 11, 2000. He was born August 18, 1913 in Rockford, Illinois. He was a graduate of Northwestern University and the University of Michigan Law School. He retired as a partner of Milbank, Tweed, Hadley and McCloy LLP in 1930 after practicing for forty-one years. He lived in Sarasota for nineteen years. Survivors include his wife of sixty-two years, Virginia B.; a son, A. Blakeman of Alexandria, Virginia; a brother, Gordon of Green Valley, Arizona; and three granddaughters, Alexandra B. of London, England, Blake G. Hargrave of Vernon, New Jersey, and Megan B. McIntyre of Alexandria, Virginia. Memorial donations may be made to the Plymouth Harbor Health Center, 700 John Ringling Blvd., Sarasota, Florida 34236-1551.
