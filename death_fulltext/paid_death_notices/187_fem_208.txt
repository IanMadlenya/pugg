
        WEINBERG-Leah. The Officers, Trustees and members of the Forest Hills Jewish Center are saddened by the loss of our dear friend and longtime member, Leah Weinberg, former School Board Chairman, Past President of the Parents' Association and outgoing Board of Trustees member. She will sorely be missed. Rabbi Gerald C. Skolnik Cantor Adam Frei Cantor Erno Grosz, Cantor Emeritus Matthew Beizer, President Tina Berger, Exec. Director
