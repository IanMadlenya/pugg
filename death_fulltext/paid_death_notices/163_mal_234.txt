
        CONNER-Rev. John, O.F.M., Cap. The Capuchin Friars of the church and friary of St. John the Baptist, 210 West 31st Street, sadly announce the entrance of their brother, John, into Eternal Life on August 19, 2000, at the age of 48. Mass of Christian Burial at Sacred Heart Church, Yonkers, on Tuesday, August 22, 2000, at 11 AM. Burial: St. James Cemetery, Manchester, CT, on Wednesday. Contributions may be made to St. John's Food Pantry, 210 West 31st Street, NY, NY 10001 or Catholic Charities, 1011 1st Ave., NY, NY 10022. For information, contact Whalen & Ball Funeral Home, Yonkers (914) 965-5488, www.obitdetails.com
