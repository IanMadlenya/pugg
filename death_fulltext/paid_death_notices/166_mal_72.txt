
        REINISCH-Jerome, of Manalapan, NJ on October 26 at the age of 62. Jerry was one of the original members of the Peace Corps, serving from '62-'64 in Puerto Cabello, Venezuela, where he met his wife of 35 years, Gladys. Jerry was a graduate of Evander Childs High School and City College and studied Social Work at Columbia University. Jerry found his greatest joy, and took great pride, in his family, friends, and exploration. Jerry is survived by his wife, Gladys, son, David, daughters Nancy O'Toole and Courtney Reinisch, sister, Lee, nephew, Mark, three grandchildren, and many, many more who will miss him dearly.
