
        ARSEVEN-Susan M. On July 21, 2000, at age 59, from breast cancer. Her optimism, love of people and life, and tireless determination will be with us always. Former Chief Information Officer for Union Camp Corporation and American Cyanamid, she lived in Nyack, New York. She is survived by her husband Ersen, her mother A. Elizabeth Watson, her sister Margaret E. Watson, and her brother Francis E. Watson. Memorial celebration of Susan's life will be announced at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cavalry Fund, Cavalry Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
