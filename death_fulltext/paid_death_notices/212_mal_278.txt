
        GOSLINER--Bertram J., MD, 89 of NYC. Prominent psychoanalyst. Beloved husband of Julia Gosliner; father of Kathy Gosliner and Denise Gosliner Orenstein; grandfather of Brendan, Laura, Jennifer, and Lisa. Brotherin-law Joseph Kotler; sistersin-law, Barbara Kotler and Jean Erdheim. Born November 12, 1914 in San Francisco. Graduate of University of California Berkeley, Creighton Medical School, Captain US Army, residency in psychiatry Bellevue Hospital, further trained in psychiatry at the New York Psychoanalytic Institute where he was a training and supervising analyst. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St, Thursday September 30th, 3pm. Contributions in his memory may be made to the National Parkinsons Foundation, 1501 Northwest 9th Avenue, Miami, FL 33136.
        GOSLINER--Bertram, M.D. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the family of our esteemed former colleague. He was a Training and Supervising Analyst at The New York Psychoanalytic Institute and a member of The New York Psychoanalytic Society for over fifty years. Paul Rosenbaum, M.D., Pres. The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Institute
