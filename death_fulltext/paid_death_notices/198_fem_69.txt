
        SHEN--Carol. The American Fellows and Staff of the Whitney Museum of American Art mourn the passing of our dear friend Carol Shen. Carol possessed an extraordinary degree of elegance, wisdom and grace that will be sorely missed by all of us. Our deepest condolences go out to her husband, Ted and daughter, Carla.
