
        BOROFSKY--Harold. Age 79, on July 7, 2005, at home in Princeton, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Dr. Leatrice Borofsky, father of David Borofsky, Lewis Borofsky and Rosalyn Ritts. Devoted grandfather of Eliana Ritts, Jacob Ritts and Joshua Borofsky, he was also loved as a father by his son-in-law Dean Ritts and his daughter-in-law Anne Borofsky. A memorial service was held on July 10, 2005, attended by family and friends, to whom he was and will always be an inspiration. He was loved by those who knew him and will remain in their hearts forever.
