
        BURG-Joseph, Dr. AMIT is deeply grieved at the passing of a great religious Zionist and outstanding leader of the State of Israel. A Torah scholar and former teacher in the AMIT Eisenberg High School in Tel Aviv, Rabbi Dr. Burg was a good friend and advisor in AMIT's mission to save the forgotten children of Israel. Heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife and children. Sondra Sokal, Nat'l Pres Marvin Leff, Exec Director
