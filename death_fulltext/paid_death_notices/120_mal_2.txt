
        FLYNN - Rev. Br. Dennis (Borromeo) C.F.X. On December 30, 1996 in Brooklyn. A distinguished and beloved Xaverian Brother for 64 years. He taught English and Latin at Xaverian highs chools in NY, MA, and MD. Son of Patrick and Margaret. He is survived by brother Jerome P. of Brooklyn and five nieces and nephews. Visitation Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 at McLaughlin & Sons, Third Avenue of 97th Street, Brooklyn. Wake service 8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10AM at the Saint Xavier School Chapel, Louisville, KY. Interment to follow at Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Ryken House, 1609 Poplar Level Road, Louisville, KY 40217 gratefully accepted.
