
        PATTERSON--Glenn Scott, 69, of Manhattan, died peacefully after a long illness on March 1, 2007, (retired NYC Parks Department and Local 147-Sandhogs). Son of the late Mary Crawford Patterson and Anslow Patterson; survived by cousins in Canada and the US. His charm and loyalty gained him the friendship of many. Memorial Mass, Thursday, March 29 at 4 pm, Epiphany Church, 22nd Street and 2nd Avenue.
