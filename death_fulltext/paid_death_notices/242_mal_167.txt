
        NOVEY--Joseph, 97, died peacefully on February 9, 2007. He was a graduate of St. John's University and Columbia University School of Social Work. He is survived by his children, Walter and Judy, and a granddaughter Dora. His wife, Marjorie Mayer, predeceased him. A man of uncommon intellect, curiosity, and independence, he survived hardship with determination and resourcefulness. An avid reader and thinker, Joe, cherished education in general and his reading of the New York Times in particular. He was famous for his sense of irony and quick wit, which endeared him to many and remained with him throughout his life.
