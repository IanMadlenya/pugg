
        RICKS-Charlotte Hall. Of Ossining, New York died in La Crosse, Wisconsin, on November 19th while visiting her daughter Francie Ricks. The widow of James H. Ricks, Jr., she is survived by her daughter and a son, John Ryland Ricks, of Raleigh, NC. A memorial will be held at her home in Ossining on December 2nd. Gifts in her memory may be made to the Ossining Children's Center, 92 S. Highland Avenue, Ossining, NY.
