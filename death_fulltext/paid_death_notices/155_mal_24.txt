
        BLAU-Raymond, 89, died on October 30, 1999 of South Orange, New Jersey and Longboat Key, Florida. President of Max Blau & Sons, Inc. Loving husband of the late Hope Blau. He is survived by his loving daughters Maxine Blau and Adele Blau Cass, and grandsons David and Gregory. Friends and family may pay their respects Saturday and Sunday from 1-8 p.m. at the home of Adele Blau Cass, 168 Glenview Road, South Orange, NJ. For more information call (973) 642-4900.
