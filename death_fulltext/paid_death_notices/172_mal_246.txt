
        CAPILLO-Rev. John D. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the Parochial Vicar of Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, 2805 Fort Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn, on May 21, 2001. Reposing at Immaculate Heart of Mary Rectory, on Wednesday May 23rd from 3-5 and 7-9 P.M., and Thursday, May 24th, from 2-5 P.M. A Mass of Transferral on Thursday May 24th at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass on Friday May 25th at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
