
        KOCH-Marilyn. Age 79, of West Hartford, died February 6, 1998. Widow of Herman, beloved mother of Dr. Lawrence W. Koch and his wife Deanna of Hartford, CT, Dr. Ronald B. Koch and his wife Paulette of Palm Beach, FL; five grandchildren; two great-grandchildren. Funeral services were held Sunday with interment in Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY.
