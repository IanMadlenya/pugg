
        LIEBESKIND--Doreen S. (nee Polak), devoted wife, mother, and grandmother and dedicated physician, died on March 16, 2007 at age 62. She is survived by her husband Arie, children Marc, David, and Elise, and grandchildren Alexander, Bernard, Kayla, and Stella. Dori was cherished by her family and many friends and will always be remembered for her warmth, loving nature, keen intelligence, and passion for learning. A funeral service will be held on Sunday, March 18 at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, at 11:30 am.
