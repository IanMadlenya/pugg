
        ARNOFF--Jack, age 83, on May 25, 2007, of Roslyn Heights, NY. Beloved husband of Yola, devoted father of Diann Gruosso and Valerie Le Gaspi. Cherished grandfather of Gregg, Eric, Eva and Michael. Proudly served in the US Marine Corps, honorable WWII veteran. He will always be remembered for his humor and generosity. The family will receive family and friends at the Roslyn Heights Funeral Home, 75 Mineola Ave., Roslyn Heights, NY, 516-621-4545, Tuesday and Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9. Funeral Mass Thursday, 11:00 am, St. Mary's RRC, Roslyn Harbor, NY. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, donations should be made to Hospice Care Network, 99 Sunnyside Blvd., Woodbury, NY 11797.
