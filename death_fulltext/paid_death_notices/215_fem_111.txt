
        PFEIFER--Audrey Margaret, 83, of Wading River, NY passed away on November 28, 2004. She was born July 3, 1921 in North Tarrytown, NY. Daughter of John Reynolds and Josephine Greeley. Her husband of more than 60 years, Henry Charles Pfeifer predeceased her. She was the beloved mother of Henry, Karen Sauerbrey, Suzanne Colgan and Robert. She is survived by eight grandchildren and 13 great grandchildren
