
        ANNINGER--Victor, 92, died peacefully in Vienna, Austria on February 23, 2004. Beloved father and father-in-law of Thomas and Anne Anninger of Cambridge, MA, and stepfather of Constance Schwartz Harris of New York City. He is also survived by his loving grandchildren, Stefan, William and his wife, Carol, and his great-grandchildren, Noah and Lily Anninger. Services were held in Vienna.
