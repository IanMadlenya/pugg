
        IRALDI -- Stephen O., 1916-2005. Army veteran of World War II, passed away March 22, 2005, in his home, Queens, NY. Now resting at Morisco Funeral Home, L.I.C., NY. He was father of Denise (d.) and Stephen John; fatherin-law of Tom; grandfather of Justin and Kyle; brother to Sister Annette and Inez; uncle of Frank and Sue, and granduncle to five nieces and great-grand-uncle to many nieces and nephews. He will be sorely missed by all his family and those who knew and loved him for his cheerful, friendly manner & optimism.
