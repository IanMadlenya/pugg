
        CHEW-Ralph H. Of Greenwich Village, NY. Tireless friend and gifted thinker, loving partner of Denise, adored father of Sally, Katherine, and Atu, died on August 2, 1998 at 70. A dominant figure in the export trade as President of Chew International Group and NEXCO. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to Common Cause, The North Shore Animal League, or friends of Washington Square Park. A Memorial Service will be held Friday, August 21st. at noon at 12 West 12 St. NYC
