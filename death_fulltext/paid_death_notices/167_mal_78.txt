
        MACAULAY-John Paul. Born February 2, 1922 in Manhattan, New York. Died on November 28, 2000 at Good Samaritan Hospital. A graduate of Marietta College in Ohio, John served as an officer of the O.S.S. during World War II. He was a retired corporate executive of Atlantic Richfield, where he served many years as a negotiator in Labor Relations. He is survived by his loving and devoted wife of 45 years, Rosemay Macaulay. A memorial service will be held in the chapel of St. Vincent Medical Center, 2131 W. 3rd Street in Los Angeles on December 7, 2000 at 4 PM.
