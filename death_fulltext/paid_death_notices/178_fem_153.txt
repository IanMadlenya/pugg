
        BEERMAN-Ann, 98. Died Wednesday November 7, 2001, in Tulsa, OK. Born February 3, 1903 in Brooklyn, NY to parents, Louis & Dora Lapinsky Secan. Married Abraham on November 27, 1927 in Brooklyn, NY. Abraham preceded her in death December 30, 1968. Ann moved to Tulsa in 1981 and is survived by daughter Estelle Beerman-Finer, Tulsa, OK; son Stuart Beerman and wife Elinor Rosen-Beerman, North Woodmere, NY; brother Joseph Secan, Houston TX, five grandchildren and ten great grandchildren.
