
        GRANT-Michael Donald. Of Jupiter Island, Hobe Sound, FL, age 94. Died peacefully on November 28, 1998, at home. Beloved husband of Alice Waters Grant for 66 years. Loving father of Sheila (deceased), Michael D. Grant, Jr., of Greenwich, CT, Thomas W. Grant, and Patricia G. Warner, both of Locust Valley, NY. Grandfather to ten grandchildren: Andrew, Luke, Michael, Bettina, Mimi, and Tim Grant, Jr., Alexander, Kate, Michael and Alice Warner. A native of Montreal, Canada, he became a partner of Fahnestock, Members of the NY Stock Exchange in 1937. He was managing partner from 1945 through 1985. Sole dissenting Director of the NY Giants Baseball Club when they moved from NY. With Joan Whitney Payson, actively sought major league baseball's expansion and became a cofounder and chairman of the New York Mets in 1962, serving until 1978. Longest serving director of the Hobe Sound Company (Florida). A competitive sportsman, notably in hockey, golf, and squash racquets. A gifted raconteur and popular master of ceremonies of charitable events. Memorial mass at St. Christopher's Church, Hobe Sound, FL, at 4 PM on Wednesday, December 2, 1998. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, the family has requested memorial contributions can be made to Hobe Sound Little League, POB 1892, Hobe Sound, FL 33475, or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave., NY, NY 10021.
        GRANT-M. Donald. The Board of Governors and membership of Deepdale Golf Club note with sorrow the untimely passing of our esteemed member. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Irwin H. Kramer, President
