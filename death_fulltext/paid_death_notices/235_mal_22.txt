
        McCAFFREY--Raymond Michael, MD. We are deeply saddened by the passing our dear friend and colleague, Dr. Raymond Michael McCaffrey, a dedicated member of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at NewYorkPresbyterian/Columbia University Medical Center for more than 40 years. An exceptional and much beloved educator and clinician known for his compassion and calming demeanor, Dr. McCaffrey will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the McCaffrey family. Dr. Herbert Pardes President and CEO NY-Presbyterian Hospital
