
        LEVINE--S. Warren, DDS. Died suddenly on Sunday June 25th while biking in Westchester. Dr. Richard Delson, his staff and his extended dental family mourn the loss of our colleague and partner. We will always remember his smiling face and many good works. Services Thursday, June 29th, 2006, 2 pm at Larchmont Temple, Larchmont, NY.
