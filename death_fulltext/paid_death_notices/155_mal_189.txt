
        BROOK-Paige. The PhilharmonicSymphony Society of New York, Inc., notes with sadness the death of Paige Brook, who joined the Philharmonic in 1952 and retired from the Orchestra as Associate Principal Flute in 1988. Prior to becoming a member of the Philharmonic, he studied at the Eastman School of Music and was a member of the Buffalo Philharmonic and the Little Orchestra Society. Mr. Brook was a frequent guest performer with the New York Philomusica and performed with the Philharmonic Woodwind Quintet. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Alice, and to the entire family. Kurt Masur, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman Deborah Borda, Exec. Director
