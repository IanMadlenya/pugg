
        SCHACHER-Etta. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a respected member of the community, beloved mother of Henry (and Beverly) Schachar and Teddy (and Sharon) Schachar. Henry Schachar, a Director of our Sy Syms School of Business and a Trustee of the affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, and his wife Beverly are YU Guardians and have established several RIETS fellowships. We extend heartfelt condolences as well to grandchildren and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
