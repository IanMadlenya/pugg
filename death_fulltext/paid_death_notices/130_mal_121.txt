
        SCHAEDEL-Robert H., 91. Of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL., formerly of South Orange, NJ, passed away November 9, 1997. He was born June 16, 1906, in Newark, NJ. He is survived by his wife, the former Doris Ahern; his sons Peter A. Schaedel and Robert W. Schaedel; his sister Helen Roellke; five grandchildren; and two greatgrandchildren. For information, contact Hardage-Giddens Funeral Home and Crematory, Jacksonville Beach, Florida.
