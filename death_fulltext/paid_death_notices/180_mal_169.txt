
        TESE-Raymond. Age 89. Beloved husband of Lucille Madonia Tese, Tuesday, January 8 after a six month battle with cancer. He faced death, as he faced life, with great courage. The fourth of six children, Raymond was born in New York on September 10, 1912. His father, Salvatore, emigrated to the United States from Sicily in 1910. Working as a shoemaker, he not only supported his family but sent his sons to college. Raymond attended New York University and St John's University School of Law, and was admitted to practice in the NY State Appellate Division, First Department, in June, 1942. He worked for the law firm of Reed, Abbott and Morgan, and then joined his brother Vincent in the firm of Tese & Tese. He continued to work even after his official retirement, completing his last case just six weeks ago. Among his many honors, Raymond was proudest of his position as past Exalted Ruler of the B.P.O. Elks, New York Lodge No. 1. Raymond is survived by his wife, Lucille, his brother, Charles, his sister Angela, and an extended and loving family of nieces, nephews, great nieces, and great nephews. Viewing will be at the Becker Funeral Home, 219 Kinderkamack Road in Westwood, NJ on Friday, followed by a Mass of Christian Burial on Saturday, 9:30AM at St Gabriel's Church, East Saddle River Road, Saddle River, NJ. Interment will be at St John's Cemetery, Queens, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the American Cancer Society.
