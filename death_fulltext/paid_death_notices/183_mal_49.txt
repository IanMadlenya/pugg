
        SZABAD-George M. Of Haverford, PA, died peacefully on March 30, 2002. He was a wise and devoted trustee of The Dibner Fund from its inception. Dedicated to justice, human rights, humanitarian concerns globally and committed to fostering peaceful coexistence, George was a person of outstanding stature in all his philanthropic activities. A dear friend and colleague, he will be sorely missed but always remembered with gratitude and fondness. Our condolences to his wife, Janet and George's children, their spouses and children and grandchildren. David Dibner, President The Trustees and Staff The Dibner Fund Wilton, CT
