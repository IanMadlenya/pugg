
        ENGLISH-Dr. Harry G. on October 29, 2002. Beloved husband of 51 years to Eileen (nee Maxwell). Loving brother of the late Annetta Brand and J. Walter English. Proud uncle of Dawn Joy, Erica Brand, Jeffrey English, Randy English, Janet Somerville, Jennifer Leonardi and Deborah Cummings. Survived by many grand nieces and nephews and one great grand-niece and many, many dear friends. Graduate of St. John's University, 1946, BBA, received his Master's at NYU. Graduate of New York Law School, 1952, with the degree of Juris Doctor, attended Adelphi University, receiving his Certified Financial Planning certificate. Honorary degree from Cumberland College. Former professor at Long Island University (Brooklyn). Practicing law, specializing in estate and taxes in Bay Ridge for over 50 years. Past-president, member, activist and volunteer for numerous organizations. Visiting Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday (at his request), 2-4 and 7-9PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 4th Ave., Brooklyn, NY. Funeral services 10AM, Monday, at St. Philip Episcopal Church (80th St. and 11th Avenue). Harry requests, in lieu of flowers, donations to Juvenile Diabetes Foundation, 381 Park Avenue South, NY, NY 10016.
