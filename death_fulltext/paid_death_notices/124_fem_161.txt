
        GRUETERICH - Ursula ''Uschi.'' Was taken into the arms of the Lord on May 20, in Palm Beach, Florida. She immigrated from Germany to NY and became an accomplished artist. Her talents were exemplified in her oil and pastel paintings of flowers, landscapes and the underwater world. She was the loving wife of Rolf, mother of Gaby Slomka (Martin), Karin Fry (John), Peter Grueterich (Annette), grandmother of Christina and Daniela, Matthew, Sophie and Oliver. She was also the wonderful sister of Ernst Matthiensen and a cherished friend.
