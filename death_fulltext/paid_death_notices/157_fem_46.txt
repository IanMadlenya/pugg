
        WAGNER-Dorothy Lorraine. December 30, 1999 in her 85th year. Born in Lumby, B.C. Dorothy moved to NY in 1934. She is predeceased by brother William Lewis Wagner and sister-in-law Mollie (nee Bell). Loving aunt of Mary Gural and her husband Andy Gural of Toronto, Joan Wagner and her husband Bruce Patterson of Victoria, B.C. Great aunt of Amy and Michael Gural. Memorial services will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church, Bay Ridge, on Saturday, February 5 at 9:30 AM. Interment Flushing Cemetery.
