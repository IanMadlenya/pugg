
        MOND-Lilian, 94, on Thursday, August 7 in Bloomfield, CT. Beloved wife of the late Morris Mond. Survived by daughter attorney Joan Mond Lebovitz of West Hartford, CT; son-in-law Walter Freedman of Del Ray Beach, FL; four grandchildren, Kathy and Gerald Gollub of NYC, Samuel Lebovitz of Brooklyn, NY, Joseph and Suzanne Lebovitz of Columbus, OH, and Alisa and James Keane of West Hartford; and four great-grandchildren, Allison, Matthew, Robert, and Kaitlyn. She worked as a legal secretary for the Brooklyn District Attorney's office. Burial at Cedar Park Cemetery, NJ, Sunday, August 10, 1:30.
