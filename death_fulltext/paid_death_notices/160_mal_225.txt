
        DUNDON-Frank (Milo). Born Hells Kitchen, New York City, 1942. Died May 15, at age 57, in Park Slope, Brooklyn. Loving life partner of Joyce Kravets, father of Donna, Bryan and Sandie Dundon, Marc, Robin and Susie Kravets, grandfather of Jahmila, Atiba, Michi, Taki, Ari, Melissa and Elijah. Milo/Frank will be sorely missed by a circle of friends, his colleagues at Prudential Securities and especially his family. A private memorial service was held in Park Slope, Brooklyn. Contributions in his honor may be made to an environmental or ecological organization in your community.
