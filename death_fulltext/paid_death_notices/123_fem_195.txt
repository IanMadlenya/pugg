
        BARKER-Elizabeth Shelly. Of New Canaan, CT. Born July 19, 1920, died April 27, 1997. Wife of Robert R. Barker and mother of James R. Barker, Ann S. Barker, W.B. (Ben) Barker and Margaret B. Clark. Sister of Robert M. Shelly and Ann S. Schafer. A celebration of her life will be held later in the Spring. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the New Canaan Society for The Arts, P.O. Box 1044, New Canaan, CT. 06840.
