
        KOBREN -- Abraham, M.S. D.D.S. Age 88, on February 16, 2006. Beloved husband of 63 years to Ruth Spevack Kobren, loving father of Susan and Murray Morrison of Fairfield, CT, Leonard and Barbara Kobren of Briarcliff Manor, NY, Roberta and Mark Simon of Scarsdale, NY, and devoted grandfather of Jennifer and Jordan Kanfer, Sarah and Aaron Kleinhandler, Adam and Benjamin Kobren, and Lauren Simon, adored great-grandfather of Noah and Rebecca Kanfer, and beloved brother of Anne Berman. He was a retired dentist of 36 years in White Plains, NY, past President and Trustee of the American Dental Association, Assistant Dean for Admissions, New York University College of Dentistry, and received an honorary Doctor of Science degree from Georgetown University. Dr. Kobren spent his adult life dedicated to dentistry. He was a noted lecturer, writer, innovator and respected mentor to his students and colleagues. He died after an extended battle with Alzheimer's disease. He was predeceased by a son, Robert Martin Kobren. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Association. Services Sunday, 10:30 am, from Abraham L. Green and Son, 88 Beach Road, Fairfield, CT, with interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla. Shiva Sunday, Monday and Tuesday, with minyan at 7:30 pm, 3200 Park Avenue, Bridgeport, CT.
