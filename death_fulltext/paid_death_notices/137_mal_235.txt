
        BROWN-Ralph S. The Dean and Faculty of Yale Law School mark with great sadness the passing of Ralph S. Brown, Simeon E. Baldwin Professor Emeritus of Law and Frank E. Taplin Professorial Lecturer, long time friend, and beloved colleague. He will be warmly remembered for over four decades of devoted teaching and service at Yale Law School; and for his scholarly contributions to the law. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. A memorial service for Professor Brown will be held at Yale on Saturday, Sept. 12, 1998 at 4:00 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to The Ralph S. Brown Fund for Student Needs at The Yale Law School Fund, PO Box 208341, New Haven, CT. 06520-8341. Anthony T. Kronman, Dean Yale Law School
