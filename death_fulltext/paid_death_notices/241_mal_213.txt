
        JONES--James R., New York Medical College mourns the loss of our esteemed former colleague, James R. Jones, M.D., Professor Emeritus of Obstetrics and Gynecology, who served as chairman of the department from 1987 to 2000. His wise leadership and dedication to teaching and patient care will be missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Karl P. Adler, M.D. President and Chief Executive Officer Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D. Provost and Dean, School of Medicine
