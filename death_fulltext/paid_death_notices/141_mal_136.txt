
        MARVIN-Anthony (Tony). Veteran radio and television artist and MC, on October 10, in Boynton Beach, Florida, age 86. Beloved husband of Dorothea & cherished father of Lynda. He was known for his many kindnesses to fellow performers & numerous charities. Funeral service Wednesday 2 PM, Beth Moses cemetery, Pine Lawn, Long Island.
        MARVIN-Tony. My good friend for 64 years; a devoted brother-inlaw since 1937. Maurine, I and the Wiener family will miss him. Jules A. Wiener
