
        HUBERMAN-Elizabeth (nee Cohen). January 3, 2000. Princeton, NJ; formerly of Hewlett, NY. Beloved wife of Harry Huberman; gentle mother of Roberta Huberman, Laura BenShmuel and Ellen Huberman; proud grandmother of Maryam, Juliet, Shaia, Michal, Elisheva, Zack, Ben, Alex and Shira.
