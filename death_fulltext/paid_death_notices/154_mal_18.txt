
        FREEMAN-Jarold and Barbara. The Board of Directors, Scientific Advisory Committee, Postdoctoral Fellows and employees of The Irvington Institute for Immunological Research note with great sadness the tragic and untimely deaths of Jarold Freeman and his wife Barbara Freeman on Egyptair Flight 990. Our deepest sympathies are with Jarold's daughter, Gail Freeman, our esteemed Executive Director. Mrs. John J. Veronis, Chairman Joshua S. Rubenstein, President
