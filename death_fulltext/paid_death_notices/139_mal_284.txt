
        MOSS-Samuel H. (''Sonny'') Of London, England, passed away peacefully August 23, 1998, at age 82. Beloved husband of Anne and the late Eileen (nee Jacobs). Devoted father of Stephen. Cherished brother of Queenie and Evelyn, and the late Ruth and Norman. Loving brother-in-law of Celia, Shirley and Doreen, and Michael and Rosa, and uncle of Jonathan and Peter, John and Helen, and Laurence and Stuart. Mathematical Wrangler at Cambridge University, England, and lifelong mathematician. Services were held in England. He will always remain in our hearts.
