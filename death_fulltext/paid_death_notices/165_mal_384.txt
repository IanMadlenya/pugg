
        MULLIN-David C. Founder, Chairman and CEO of Mullin & Associates/Lincolnshire International, an established premium global consulting company, died October 28, 2000 at the age of 70. David was a respected leader, as well as pioneer in establishing the Outplacement Industry as a valued resource to Fortune 1000 companies, with many of his innovative practices becoming standard for the industry. Mr. Mullin, a catalyst and champion of ethical practices for the industry, previously held progressive leadership positions with other premier international consulting firms as well as the Johnson & Johnson family of companies. A longtime resident of East Norwich, NY, for 35 years, he is survived by his wife Rose Marie of East Norwich; daughter Shawn Rezek of East Worcester, NY; daughter Faine Christie of Hicksville, NY; son Keith of Rye, NY; and son-in-law Robert Romanelli of Oyster Bay, NY. He is pre-deceased by his five children: Donna, Timothy, Mary, David and Kelly. In addition to his three children, his seven grandchildren remember him. Visiting at the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home, Inc., Wednesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass Thursday, 9:30 AM, St. Dominic's R.C. Chapel. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery.
