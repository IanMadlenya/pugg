
        LYNCH-Robert H. 44, of Cranford, NJ, on September 11, 2001, while working to evacuate the World Trade Center. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Robert Lynch Memorial Fund, c/o Richard Lino, 21 Tracey Drive, Lawrenceville, NJ 08648.
