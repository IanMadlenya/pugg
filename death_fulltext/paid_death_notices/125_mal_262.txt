
        McDermott-Rev. Joseph J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired Chaplain of St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale, L.I., on June 25. Reposing in Smith Funeral Home, 248 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn, Friday, June 27, 2-5 and 7-9 p.m. Funeral mass Saturday, June 28, at 10:30 a.m. at Holy Name Church, 245 Prospect Park West. All priests are invited to celebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
