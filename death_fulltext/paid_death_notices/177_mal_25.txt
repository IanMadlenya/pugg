
        NEMIROFF-Leo, (Northrup, Eric), 87. Of Croton-on-Hudson, New York, died Friday, September 28, 2001. Retired science and medical writer/editor, he leaves Mili, his wife of 67 years; his children, Sandra, David, Judith and Margaret; seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Deeply committed to peace and social justice, Leo's generosity, compassion, lively intelligence, humor, and imagination touched all who knew him. Leo's passing leaves a great void in our hearts, but the memory of his remarkable presence will remain with us always.
