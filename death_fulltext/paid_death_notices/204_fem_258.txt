
        CROSS--Alexandra (Kraus). Died peacefully in her sleep at home on January 25th, aged 90. She was the widow of Dr. Anthony Kraus, and the much-loved mother of Peter Kraus. She will be equally missed by her daughter-inlaw, Evelyn, and her granddaughters Nicola and Oliva, as well as countless friends and admirers. A memorial service will be announced. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Jacob Perlow Hospice, would be appreciated.
