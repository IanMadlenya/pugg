
        McBRIDE-Rodney Lester. On April 10, 1999, at Greenwich, CT, of esophageal cancer. Loving husband of Judy Winslow. Father of Douglas of Scottsdale, AZ, and Cheryl of Denver, CO. Brother of Laurence of Colorado Springs and Judy of Denver, CO. Born in Denver, CO, on September 1, 1941. Graduated from the University of Colorado and earned his M.B.A. at the University of California at Berkeley. Chartered Financial Analyst and Chartered Investment Counselor. Member, American Financial Analyst Federation. Managing Director at Furman Selz Capital Management, New York City. Highly respected by his peers and clientele for his talent and professionalism. Enthusiastic golfer. Memorial service at Second Congregational Church, Greenwich, CT, on Wednesday, April 14, at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the American Cancer Society, 1175 Post Road East, Westport, CT 06880.
