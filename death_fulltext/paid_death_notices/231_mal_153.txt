
        MERCIER--Edward G. Age 98 died January 2, 2006. Ed graduated from Stuyvesant High School in 1926. His engineering career led him through early Bell Labs in Manhattan, CineSimplex and General Precision Labs. He also worked on the norden bomb site during W.W. II. A service to celebrate Ed's life will be held April 22 at 2:00 PM at The Stowe Community Church in Stowe, VT.
