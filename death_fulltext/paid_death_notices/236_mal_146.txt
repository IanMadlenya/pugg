
        LOEBENBERG--Dr. Ralph. Yeshiva University mourns the passing, in Canada, of the beloved brother of Dina and brother-in-law of Dr. Herbert C. Dobrinsky, cherished members of our YU family. Dr. Loebenberg was a distinguished obstetrician/gynecologist and leader of the Beth Israel Synagogue in Halifax. We extend heartfelt condolences to Carol Lee, his wife of 43 years; his children, grandchildren, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman Board of Trustees
