
        ZAKIN--Albert, in his 100th year, died peacefully at his home in Palm Beach on February 5, 2007. He is survived by his loving wife of 65 years, Bernice, his adoring children, Nancy (Jeffrey) Lane and Kenneth (Barbi), cherished grandchildren David (Susan) Lane, Kenny (Alyson) Lane, Debbie (Michael) Castleman, Lizzie Rudnick, Andy (Dana) Stern, Carly and Peter and eight beloved great-grandchildren. He was born in Peoria, IL, and for over 60 years was a prominent jeweler in New York and Florida. Albert was respected and deeply loved by his family and friends and will be forever missed and remembered. A service in celebration of his life will be held Wednesday, 11 AM February 7, 2007 at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his name may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach.
