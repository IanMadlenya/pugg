
        KLOR-William, 83, After a gallant and determined struggle with cancer and heart disease. Dedicated husband, father and grandfather. Adored by his wife Mildred, his daughters Nora Drummond and Robin Klor and his grandsons Joshua and Wyeth. Hard worker, avid sailor and consumate boat builder. He loved his family, the sea, his boat and his garden. He will never be forgotten. His spirit is alive in every wave, puff of wind and in his children and grandchildren. Services Wednesday, 9AM at ''Parkside'' Chapels, Queens Boulevard and 66 Ave, Forest Hills. Donations to Mystic Seaport.
