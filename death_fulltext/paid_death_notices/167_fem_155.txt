
        HERRICK-Helen (Casey) Isaacs. The Board of Directors and staff of the Stanley Isaacs Neighborhood Center are saddened by the passing of board member Casey Herrick. Casey dedicated many years of service to the Isaacs Center and carried on the Isaacs family legacy of commitment to the community. She will be missed. Doris C. Halaby, Chairman Barry J. Alperin, President Wanda Wooten, Exec. Dir.
