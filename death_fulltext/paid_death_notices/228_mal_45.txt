
        GROVEMAN -- Aaron. Died peacefully at home Wednesday morning, after a long illness at the age of 78. Despite adverse circumstances, Aaron maintained a most positive attitude toward life. Beloved husband to Marilyn Astor Groveman. Loving father of Jane and Nancy. Proud grandfather to Alex and David. Valued brother to Fred and sister to Pearl. Educated at City College of New York, Aaron became the founder and CEO of a New York based construction company specializing in structural rehabilitation of bridges, dams and tunnels. He was respected by his colleagues and admired by his competitors. All will miss him. There will be a private cremation and a memorial service will be held at his daughter Nancy's home.
