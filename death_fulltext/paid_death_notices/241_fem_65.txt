
        HALKIN--Cyrille, M.D. Lenox Hill Hospital expresses profound sorrow at the passing of Dr. Cyrille R. Halkin, an esteemed member of our Department of Pediatrics for almost 30 years. A warm and dedicated physician and role model, she was beloved by her colleagues, patients and their families and will be missed by all who knew her. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her family.
