
        MANN-David J. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Temple Gates of Zion mourn the passing of our devoted and beloved Cantor Emeritus. He served our congregation with distinction for over 39 years. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Frances, son Zachary, daughter Thelma and husband Albert Nicolas, grandchildren Daniel, Elexa and Jenny Rose. May he, together with his beloved son Daniel, rest in eternal peace. We will never forget them. William Last, President Temple Gates of Zion, Valley Stream, N.Y.
