
        FEUERMAN-Florence. Of Boro Park, Brooklyn. Passed away October 31, 1997, in Hollywood, CA. Born January 11, 1909 in Manhattan. Loving wife of Bernard. Beloved mother of Robert and Kenny. Dear sister of Frances, Belle, Charlotte, Janet, Sidney and Emmanuel. Loving grandmother of Gary and Stephen. Dear motherin-law of Gail. With many other loving relatives and her very dear friends and canasta players. Services Tuesday, 11 AM, at ''Parkside Chapel'' 98-60 Queens Blvd. (corner 66th Ave.), Forest Hills.
