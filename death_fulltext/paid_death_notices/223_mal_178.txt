
        FRANK--Leo. August 17, 1915. Lynched 90 years ago today. by an anti-Semitic mob in Marietta, Ga. Falsely accused and convicted of murder in 1913, ADL's campaign to exonerate Frank led to a posthumous pardon in 1986, seventy-three years after his conviction. He will always be remembered.
