
        LYGNOS-Peter N. Age 64. Passed away July 7, 1998 in Greece. Survived by wife Anna, children, Penelope Lygnos-Mavris, Nicholas P. Lygnos, Chrysanphe and Maria Lygnos-Lemos. Also survived by his mother, Penelope Lygnos and his brother George Lygnos, his wife Elli and his children, Nicholas, Penelope & Reni. He was founder of Lygnos Brothers Shipping, Inc. Peter had over 45 years in the shipping industry operating and managing a fleet of over 35 vessels in the dry bulk market. His peers will remember him not only as an astute businessman but as a loving and caring individual. He will be sorely missed by all.
