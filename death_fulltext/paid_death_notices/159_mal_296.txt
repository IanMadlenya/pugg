
        KRELOFF-Isidore ''Mike.'' Loving husband of Ruth. Adoring father of Carol and Steven Bassin and Susan Kind and Richard Raker. M.D. Proud grandfather to eleven grandchildren and one greatgranddaughter. He will be remembered as a successful accountant, an avid tennis player, a proud member of the 352nd Fighting Unit of the Air Force during WW2. Service took place today in Delray Beach, FL. Donations in his honor may be made to the Children's Hospital at Montefiore, 111 E. 210th Street, Bronx, NY 10467.
        KRELOFF-Isidore. The President, Board of Trustees and the entire Montefiore Medical Center community note with sadness the passing of Isidore Kreloff, loving father of Susan Kind, our respected colleague and Vice President of Development. He will be long remembered by all who knew him for his deep devotion to his family. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Ruth; his daughters, Susan Kind and Carol Bassin; his eleven grandchildren, his great-granddaughter and his entire family. Spencer Foreman, MD, President Jay B. Langner, Chairman Board of Trustees Montefiore Medical Center
