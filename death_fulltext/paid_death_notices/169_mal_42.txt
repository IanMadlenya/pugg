
        SPENGLER-Silas. A retired New York City lawyer and a former resident of Bronxville, N.Y. died January 31 at his home in Middleton, WI. Beloved husband of Anna M. (Sandy) and loving father of Silas of Nassau, Bahamas and Harry of Dover, N.H., proud grandfather of Silas William Spengler of Nassau, Bahamas, stepfather of Sam Barker and Peter Barker, both of Chicago, Il. Dear brother of William of Colorado Springs, CO., David of Madison, Wi. and Susan Lundberg of Wausau, WI. Silas was a co-founder of the law firm Roth Carlson Kwit & Spengler in 1964 which firm was known as Spengler, Carlson Gubar Brodsky & Frischling when in 1993 it combined with the firm of Reid & Priest. Born in Neenah, WI, he graduated from Phillips Academy, Andover 1949, Yale University in 1953 and in 1960 from the University of Pennsylvania Law School, where he served as Articles Editor of its Law Review. A memorial service will be held in New York City at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Silas and Margaret Spengler Memorial Scholastic Fund, Phillips Academy, Andover, MA 01810-4161 or Hospice Care of Madison, 5395 East Cheryl Parkway, Madison, WI 53711.
        SPENGLER-Silas. The Yale College Class of 1953 mourns the loss of our most esteemed and beloved classmate. Si was our leader and class secretary for almost 40 years. His wisdom, humanity, good humor, and gentlemanly kindness were the hallmarks of a man of rare character. We extend our sympathy and support to his wife Sandy, and to his family.
