
        KLEIN-Howard L. The Jewish Museum staff, Board and Volunteer Association mourn the death of Howard Klein, long-time contributor to The Jewish Museum and honorary member of the Museum Council. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife Lenore, an active member of the Volunteer Association and former President, and to their entire family. Joan Rosenbaum, Director Charlotte S. Schwartz, President of the Volunteer Assoc. Robert J. Hurst, Board Chair Philip A. Laskawy, Board President
