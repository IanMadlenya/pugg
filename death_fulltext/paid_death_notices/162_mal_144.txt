
        LAEMLE-John L. Beloved husband of Lois. Loving father of David and Sharon, father-in-law of Nancy. Dear son of Helen. Cherished grandfather of Jessica. Adored son-in-law of Mae and Daniel Kalus. Services Sunday, 10 AM, at Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Congregation Kol Ami would be appreciated.
        LAEMLE-John L. Congregation Kol Ami records with deep sorrow the passing of our cherished member and former president, John L. Laemle. A man of great vision and courage, John led our congregation with distinction. He will be missed by all of us as well as the greater Jewish community. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Lois, his daughter, Sharon, his son, David and daughterin-law, Nancy and granddaughter, Jessica. Rabbi Shira Milgrom Rabbi Tom Weiner Cantor Annie Lynn Bornstein Ken Simon, President
        LAEMLE-John. The officers, board, members and staff of the American Jewish Committee, Westchester Chapter, notes with deep sorrow the passing of our president. John cared deeply about AJC, and led the Chapter with a serious dedication and as much time and energy as he could muster despite the difficulties of his illness. We shall miss his direction, thoughtfulness, friendship, and commitment to social justice. We send our heartfelt condolences to Lois and his family. Harry Paul, Vice President Ann Schaffer, Executive Director
