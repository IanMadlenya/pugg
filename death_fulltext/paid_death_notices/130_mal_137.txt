
        WEINGARTEN - Michael, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the leadership of our parent corporation Greater Metropolitan Health Systems, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend, colleague and benefactor Michael Weingarten, M.D., who was affiliated with Beth Israel for more than sixty years. Dr. Weingarten served in numerous roles during his distinguished career, including a term as President of the Beth Israel Medical Board, and as President of the New York Chapter of the American College of Gastroenterology. He and his wife, Beatrice, also will be remembered for their philanthropic support of our institution. To Beatrice, his daughter Muriel Dachs and her husband Samuel, his daughter-inlaw Joan Weingarten, widow of long time Beth Israel physician Leonard Weingarten, M.D., the entire Weingarten family, and all who loved him, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Stein, M.D., President, Doctors Alumni Assoc. Stephen G. Baum, M.D., Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Alvin M. Gelb, M.D. The Ethel and Milton Bornstein Chief of Gastroenterology
        WEINGARTEN-Dr. Michael. Beloved husband of 66 years of Beatrice, adored father of Muriel Dachs, and the late Dr. Leonard Weingarten (Joan). Also survived by five loving grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Services are on Thursday, 1:15 P.M., at the ''Riverside'' Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Memorials may be made to Beth Israel Medical Center. No flowers please.
