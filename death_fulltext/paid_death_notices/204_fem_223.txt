
        WELLENS -- Portia nee Pomerantz, widow of Philip L. Wellens, Corporate Counsel for the City of NY, died January 15, 2004 in Las Vegas, NV. Burial at Mt. Ararat in Lindenhurst, NY. Survived by daughter Laura Sommer, son-in-law Jack Sommer of NY and Las Vegas, and also the developer of The Aladdin Hotel in Las Vegas. Loving grandmother of Adam, Rachel Sommer Winston and husband Michael, Paul, Sigmund, Ethan, Rebecca and Drew. She will be missed. Her Loving Family, The Sommers
