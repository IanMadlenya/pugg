
        DASH-Shirley, nee Nisinson. Widow of insurance executive, Emanuel Dash, peacefully, April 1, in her 100th year, at Southampton Nursing Center, Southampton, NY. Mother of Gregory, Matthew (deceased) and Robert, she leaves as well, three grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Famed for her dazzling smile and great sense of compassion, she worked at many charities during her long and rewarding life. Her ashes will be interred and the burial will be private. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Hadassah.
