
        McBRIDE-Edward Archer. Of Manhattan. On August 5, 1998 at the age of 73. Decorated W.W. II veteran; recipient of 3 bronze stars, one silver star and the Purple Heart. Five-year President of the 90th Division Association. 17-year Police Auxiliary Captain. Survived by his daughter Elizabeth, two grandchildren, brother Alan, along with many nieces, nephews and longtime friends. Memorial service to be held Saturday, August 15, 11-12 P.M. at the Greenwich Village Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker Street, Manhattan.
