
        MUFSON-Marvin, M.D., 68, of Edison, NJ. Husband of the late Ann Mufson. Survived by his children, Steven and Agnes, Daniel, and Laura and Bennett; his sister, Estelle Sheiman; and 4 grandchildren, Claire, Natalie, Joshua, and Nina. He was a graduate of Princeton University & Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. He practiced orthopedic surgery for over 35 years. Services are Wednesday, July 16th, at 1:00 PM, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
