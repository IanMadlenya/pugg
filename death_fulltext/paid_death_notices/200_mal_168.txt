
        WEISS--Franklin W. ''Fred.'' Philanthropist and businessman of Longmeadow, MA, founder and chairman of Plastic Packaging Corp, of West Springfield, MA. died Saturday, September 13, 2003. He leaves his wife Ruth (Calish) Weiss, two daughters, Susan (William) Weiss Firestone of Longmeadow, MA, and Debra (Robert) Weiss Finkel of Swampscott, MA, and five loving grandchildren Rachel, Andrea, Max, Hannah and Aliyah. The funeral is today, September 15, 1 PM, at Sinai Temple, with burial in Sinai Memorial Park, Springfield, MA.
