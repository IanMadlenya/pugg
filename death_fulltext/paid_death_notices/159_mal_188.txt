
        BRYAN-Richard Dobbs Spaight. Of Rumson, NJ. on April 15, 2000. Beloved husband of Betsy, father of Helen Hubbard, Martha Bryan and Nancy Bryan Ketterer, grandfather of four. He is also survived by three brothers Gray, John and Marsh. His career at Chemical Bank spanned 38 years where he held the position of Executive Vice President. Services will be held at St. George's-by-the-River in Rumson Wednesday, April 19 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, a contribution may be sent to the New Jersey Chapter Nature Conservancy, 4245 North Fairfax Drive, Suite 100, Arlington, VA. 22203.
