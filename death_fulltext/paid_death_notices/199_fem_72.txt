
        LEIPMAN--Doris J. of Largo, FL, beloved wife, mother, grandmother and friend, died of breast cancer on August 9, 2003 at the Fountains at Rivervue in Tuckahoe, NY. She was 80 years old. She is survived by her son, Ken Leipman of Marietta, GA; her daughter, Debra LeipmanYale of Armonk, NY; and her beloved six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on August 15th at 2:00 P.M. at Christ Presbyterian Church, 3115 Dryer Avenue, Largo, FL. Friends may send memorial donations to the American Cancer Society.
