
        ROONEY-Sr. Mary Melita. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity and elementary school teacher on Dec. 29, 1997. Survived by her sister-in-law, Dorothy, and her cousins, Sr. Angela Rooney, Msgr. Hugh J. Rooney and Ann Rooney. Mass of Christian Burial on Wednesday 9:30 A.M., at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark Street, Yonkers, New York. Interment is at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, New York 10471.
