
        CULLEN-Terry. On December 5, in his beloved New York City. Born in Dungannon, County Tyrone, Ireland. Longtime resident of Greenwich Village, independent, and one of the first computer programmers. A lover of literature, conversation and New York City. Survived by his sisters, Mary and Margaret, residents of Britain. A memorial service will be held at Holy Trinity Chapel, 58 Washington Square South, NYC, Monday, December 15, 11 a.m. Donations to Speech and Language Clinic, New York University, C/O Amy J. Danielsen, Director, 719 Broadway, Suite 200, New York, NY 10003.
