
        ZIMET--Alvin (Al), of Cortlandt Manor, NY, died at age 77 on August 1st, 2006 at his home after a long illness. Born in Manhattan, he was a proud graduate of Baruch College. In 1972, the family moved to the house he helped design in Cortlandt. Al worked in sales promotion and advertising with Squibb and Life Savers. He was a jazz enthusiast, avid moviegoer and a Nicks fan. He is survived by his wife, Lou, his sons, Mark (Amy) and Steve (Linda), and two grandsons, Ben and Alex. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Baruch College (www.baruch.cuny.edu) or to his favorite radio station, WBGO Newark (www.wbgo.org).
