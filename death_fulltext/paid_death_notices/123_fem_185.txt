
        KERESEY-Cornelia Frances, of Houston, passed away, Wednesday, April 23, 1997. She was born November 20, 1928 in New York, New York, to Henry D. and Mary F. Keresey. She was a graduate of Vassar College in Poughkeepsie, NY and a member of the Vassar Club, and also a faithful member of Christ Church Cathedral in Houston and Church of the Epiphany in New York. She gave unstintingly of her time and energy in Ministry to the homeless of New York City. She is survived by her four sons: Richard Kellogg and wife Jan, Montgomery Kellogg and wife Jeanette, Donn Kellogg and Michael Kellogg; one sister, Mary Edmonson; two brothers, Donn Keresey and Michael Keresey; six grandchildren, Clark, Winnie, Taylor, Tristin, Catherine and Elizabeth; she is the granddaughter of Cornelius F. Kelly; a number of nieces and nephews and other loving relatives. Family will be present to receive friends on Tuesday evening, April 29, 1997 at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, NY from 6PM until 8PM. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, April 30, 1997 at 11:00AM at the Church of the Epiphany, 1393 York Avenue, NY with the Rev. Dorsey McConnell officiating. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Valhalla, New York. A memorial service in Houston is pending.
