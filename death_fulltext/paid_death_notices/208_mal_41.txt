
        COHEN--Ira Carl. The officers and staff of the Jewish Labor Committee note with sadness the passing of Ira Carl Cohen, brother of our Administrator, Florence R. Cohen, and the son of the late Belle and Louis Cohen. A devoted son and brother, Ira will be deeply missed. Heartfelt condolences to his family and friends. Funeral will be at Plaza Jewish Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. and 91st St., Wednesday, May 5, 12 Noon. Shiva will be observed at the home of Florence R. Cohen. At family's request, contributions can be made to the Jewish Labor Committee. Stuart Appelbaum, President Avram B. Lyon, Exec. Dir.
