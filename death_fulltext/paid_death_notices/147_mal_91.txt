
        SILBERSTEIN-Benjamin H. On April 6, 1999 of Syosset, LI. Beloved husband of Eileen, devoted father of Pamela and Jonathan, cherished father-in-law of Shari Silberstein and Bernard Sellem, dear brother of Dorothy Klein. He served in the U.S. Navy and was a veteran of WWII. He was a long time member of the Midway Jewish Center of Syosset and past member of the Town of Oyster Bay Planning Commission. Services today, 2 PM, at ''Gutterman's'' 8000 Jericho Tpk, Woodbury, LI.
