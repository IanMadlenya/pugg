
        BERGMANN--Therese G. Loving wife of the late Paul and devoted mother of Peter, Steven and Ronald, died peacefully at home in her beloved New York City. Born in Graz, Hungary, she was an education activist, consumer and community board advocate, member of Cancer Care and the Council of Jewish Women. She leaves her three sons, daughters-in-law, and six grandchildren, Rachel, David, Maggie, Paul, Caroline and Joseph. She will be missed by her family and friends. Services are Friday, March 31, at 11 AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave at 91st St. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name to Fort Tryon Jewish Center, 524 Fort Washington Ave, NY NY 10033, or Beth Israel Medical Center, 16th St and 1st Avenue, NY NY 10003.
