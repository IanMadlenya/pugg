
        TYRAS-Denis H., M.D. The Board, Administration, and staff of Saint Vincent Catholic Medical Centers-St. Vincent's Hospital Manhattan, mourn the tragic death of Denis H. Tyras, M.D. Chief Emeritus of Cardiothoracic Surgery at St. Vincent's Manhattan. A widely respected and renowned surgeon, Dr. Tyras served on the medical staff of St. Vincent's Manhattan for more than 11 years and as Professor of Surgery at New York Medical College. As a deeply respected colleague, mentor and friend, the St. Vincent's community extends to his wife Linda, his two children, Jonathan and Jocelyn, grandchildren and the entire Tyras family, our prayers and deepest condolences. Jane Connorton, President St. Vincent's Hospital Manhattan
        TYRAS-Denis H., M.D. New York Medical College is shocked and saddened by the passing of our colleague, Dr. Denis Tyras, distinguished Professor of Surgery and Chief of Cardiothoracic Surgery at our affiliate, St. Vincent's Manhattan. His devotion to teaching and patient care will be deeply missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Rev. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D., Provost and Dean, School of Med.
        TYRAS-Denis.
