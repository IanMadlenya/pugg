
        MASON-Patricia. On May 18, 1997. Real estate broker, Senior Vice President, William B. May Company. Wife of Francis Mason, mother of Spencer Mason and Leslie Scarlett Mason, grandmother of Dexter Mason. Memorial service on Thursday, June 5, 1997, 12:30 PM, at the Church of St. Luke in the Fields, 427 Hudson Street, New York, NY 10014. Patricia Mason was a keen supporter of the T. H. Everett Rock Garden at the New York Botanical Garden, the Greenhouse at Wave Hill, the Garden Conservancy and the School of American Ballet. In lieu of flowers friends may wish to consider contributions to those organizations. Robert Bartolomei is Curator of the T. H. Everett Rock Garden, N.Y. Botanical Garden. 200th Street and Southern Boulevard, Bronx, NY 10458. Marco Polo Stufano is Curator at Wave Hill, 675 West 252 Street, Bronx, New York 10471. Garden Conservancy's address is Box 219, Cold Spring, NY 10516. Albert Bellas is Chairman, School of American Ballet, 70 Lincoln Center Plaza, NY, NY 10023.
        MASON-PATRICIA. The William B. May Company deeply mourns the loss of its dear friend, esteemed colleague and Senior Vice President. Pat was loved and respected by all who were privileged to work with her. Her courage and determination continues to be an inspiration to us all. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family. Peter R. Marra President William Bruce May Chairman
        MASON-Patricia. The board, staff and friends of The New York Botanical Garden mourn the death of Patricia Mason, a steadfast supporter of the T. H. Everett Rock Garden and public gardens in New York City. We offer our heartfelt condolences to her husband Francis and her entire family. Thomas J. Hubbard, Chairman Gregory Long, President Mrs. Michael W. Slater, Chair, Rock Garden Committee
