
        BLAKE-Melvin N., D.D.S. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Mount Sinai School of Medicine and The Mount Sinai Hospital of Mount Sinai NYU Health deeply mourn the untimely passing of our dear friend and esteemed colleague Melvin N. Blake, D.D.S., Associate Clinical Professor of Dentistry who was associated with Mount Sinai for forty three years. A noted Oral and Maxillo-Facial Surgeon, Dr. Blake earned his dental degree from New York University and joined Mount Sinai in 1956 as Clinical Instructor. He rose to the ranks of Associate Clinical Professor in 1991 and served as director of the Division of Oral and Maxillo-Facial Surgery for five years. Dr. Blake also served as the Chairman of the Department of Dentistry at Lenox Hill Hospital until his retirement from clinical practice in 1991. Dr. Blake will be remembered warmly and he will be sorely missed. We extend sincere condolences to his family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman John W. Rowe, M.D., President Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean Barry R. Freedman, Executive Vice President Theresa A. Bischoff, Executive Vice President Alan L. Schiller, M.D., President, Medical Board
