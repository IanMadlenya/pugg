
        COHEN-Norman D. July 2, 1920June 8, 1999. Formerly of Great Neck, resided Boynton Beach. W.W. II veteran with the 511 Engineers European Campaign. Founder of Lisbeth Fabrics and active in the garment industry for thirty years. Past president Cold Spring Country Club. He will always be remembered for his generosity, friendship and wonderful sense of humor. As patriarch of a large family he will forever be loved by all. He is survived by his loving wife, Millicent, three sons, Martin (Sara), Robert (Lynn), Brad (Carol) and four grandchildren. Funeral will be held in Tamarac, Florida.
