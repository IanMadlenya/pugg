
        PARGAMENT - Hilda (nee Schmeltzer). Died on April 5, 2003, after a long and courageous battle with emphysema. She was proceeded by her husband David and son Joseph. She is survived by her daughter Frances (Paul) daughter-in-law, grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. She was a woman of morals, a fighter for the underdog, a Yiddishist, possessed a wonderful sense of humor, was an extraordinarily wise woman and a remarkable mensch. She will be greatly missed by those who knew her.
