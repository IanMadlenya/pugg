
        TAISHOFF-Ruth Saltzman. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of Ruth Saltzman Taishoff, our esteemed colleague. A generous benefactor and devoted leader, she was instrumental in developing the Hadassah College of Technology in Jerusalem. Our heartfelt condolences go to her family. May her memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, National President
