
        OBER-William. 94, of Manchester, CT, died Thursday, January 20. He was the husband of the late Sally (Breslau) Ober. Born in New York City, he had lived in New York until moving to Connecticut in 1997. He was a graduate of City College of New York, Class of 1926, a member of the Sigma Alpha Mu Fraternity, Alpha Chapter and was also a Trustee of Sigma Alpha Mu Foundation. He was in the diamond business with A. Jaffe & Sons for 59 years. He served as a docent at the Museum of the City of New York, and he volunteered in the Public Entitlements Department of Roosevelt Hospital from 1986 to 1996. He is survived by his brother-in-law and sisters-in-law, Sam & Frances Breslau and Claire Breslau, all of West Hartford, and many nieces and nephews. Funeral services Sunday, January 23, at 1 PM, at Ladd-Turkington Funeral Home, 551 Talcottville Rd. (Rt. 83), Vernon, CT. Burial will follow. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the charity of the donor's choice.
