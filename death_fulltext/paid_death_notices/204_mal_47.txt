
        MINTZER -- Max, D.D.S. March 18, 1906--December 26, 2003. Vice President and Board member of the Ohab Zedek Synagogue, Belle Harbor, N.Y. Practiced dentistry in Brooklyn for more than 50 years. Fund raiser for New York and Florida Jewish causes. Survived by son Howard and his wife Carol, daughter Arlene Krumholz and her husband Spencer, and two grandchildren, Michael and Barrie Krumholz. He touched many lives and will be missed.
