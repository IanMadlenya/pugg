
        COULSON-Edith de Rham. Peacefully on Wednesday, 9th April, age 69, at the Trinity Hospice, London. Beloved mother of Henry, Diana and Amanda, grandmother of Dylan, Charlie and Emily. A celebration of her life with her family and many friends will be held at the end of May. Any donations are to be sent to the Royal Marsden Hospital, Fundraising Department, Downes Road, Sutton, Surrey, SN2 5PT, England.
