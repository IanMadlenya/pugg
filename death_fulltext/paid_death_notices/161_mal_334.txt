
        PAKALNISKIS-Rev. John L. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of retired Pastor Emeritus of Annunciation Church, 259 North 5th St., Brooklyn, on June 26. Reposing at Shalins Funeral Home, 84-02 Jamaica Avenue, Woodhaven, NY on Thursday, June 29, from 2-5 & 7-9pm, and Friday, June 30, from 2-5 & 7-9pm. Funeral Mass on Saturday, July 1, 9am at Annunciation Church. All Priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of The Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V.
