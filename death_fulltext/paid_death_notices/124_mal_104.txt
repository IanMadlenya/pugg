
        BEISER-Louis, 86 years old. He passed away on Thursday, May 1, 1997. For 60 years Louis was the beloved husband of Dorothy who passed away in December 1996. Louis retired from private practice as a certified public accountant in New York City in 1975. After moving to Tamarac, FL. he became involved in golf, carpentry and his granddaughters. He was a warm, gentle, loving man with a wonderful sense of humor. He will be missed by his daughter Lynn, son-in-law Stephen, son Bernard, daughter-in-law Wendy and granddaughters: Jodie, Melissa, Mara and Ashley. Services were held at Star of David, Bailey Rd., North Lauderdale, FL. Donations may be made to The American Parkinsons Disease Association or the American Cancer Society.
