
        BATTLE-Hyman L., Jr. His former partners at Battle Fowler are saddened by the death of their former senior partner, Hyman L. Battle, Jr., who died Wednesday at his home in Sagaponack, LI. Wise and courtly, Hy had a profound effect on all those who knew him. We extend our condolences to his wife, Reine, and to his family.
        BATTLE-Hyman L. Jr. A dear friend whose warmth, wisdom and wit will be greatly missed. Patty and Bob Gelfman
