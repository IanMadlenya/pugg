
        HORNUNG -- Edward Paul. Died on August 1 in Naples, FL in his 89th year. Born in Pottsville, PA, son of Joseph and Clara Hornung, he worked for many years in New York City for the U.S. Rubber Company and later in concert management. He is survived by siblings Charles and Sister Florecita, nieces and nephews and their children, and by devoted friends, including Michael Sweeley, his partner of 54 years.
