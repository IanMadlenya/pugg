
        COHEN--Murry, on August 6, 2003, of Hackensack, NJ. Former Financial VP for Joseph E. Seagram, New York, NY, Comptroller for Helena Rubenstein and General Manager for Bowater Limited. Beloved husband of Helen for 62 years. Devoted father of Robert and Lora Cohen, Cathy and Joel Bez, Kathy and James Ward and the late JoAnn Ward. Dear brother of Alma Minton. Loving grandfather of Marci Heath, Kelly Cohen, Jenell Ward, Jaclyn Ward and Melissa Bez. Greatgrandfather of Zachary Heath. A memorial service will be at a later date.
        COHEN--Murry. The Board of Governors and membership of Edgewood Country Club record with deep sorrow the death of Mr. Murry Cohen, an esteemed member, and extend to his beloved wife, Helen, and family its deepest sympathy. Kenneth Apfel, President Andrew Muhlstock, Secy
