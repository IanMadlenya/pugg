
        ROY-Asoka. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center (BIMC) family joins the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend Asoka Roy, who lovingly served as Director of Midwifery at Beth Israel from 1968 to 1983. Our sympathy is extended to members of Asoka's family, and all who loved her. Matthew E. Fink, M.D. President & CEO Arnold Friedman, M.D. Chairman, OB/GYN Rochelle Lipschutz Director of Midwifery Sidney K. Stein, MD President, Medical Board
