
        KALKUT-Raymond Benjamin, 78. Died October 3, 2001 after living with leukemia for three decades. Beloved husband of Ruth for 38 years. Loving partner of Johanna Roth since 1989. Younger brother of William. Cherished by his children, David, Gary and his wife Amy, Joan and her husband Tom, six grandchildren, and many friends that reach back to his childhood in Washington Heights. Ray's warmth and strength will always remain with those who love him.
