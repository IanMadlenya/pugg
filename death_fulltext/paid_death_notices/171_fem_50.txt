
        BORCHARD-Helen. The Board of Directors and Staff of the 92nd Street Y mourn the passing of Helen Borchard, a dedicated and longtime member of our board. Her years of commitment to the 92nd Street Y are deeply appreciated. Our hearts and prayers go out to her friends and family. She will be missed by all who knew her. Matthew Bronfman, President Philip Milstein, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
