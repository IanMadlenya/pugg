
        DANIELSON-Ole Willis. Formerly of New York City, died February 7, 2000 at the age of 83 in Newark, DE after a long illness. His wife, Hazel (''Bunny'') Hill Vial died in 1984. He was a 1938 graduate of West Point, and served in World War II in Europe. Joining the United Nations afterwards, he held posts in Korea, Chile and New York. He retired to Walnut Creek, California in 1976 & enjoyed golf, hunting, fishing and travel. He is survived by his son, Ole N., and two grandchildren in West Chester, PA. Burial services will be held at West Point in April.
