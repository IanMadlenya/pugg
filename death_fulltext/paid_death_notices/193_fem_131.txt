
        BALDWIN-Eileen N. (Mrs. James French). Died suddenly, and unexpectedly at her home in Delray Beach, FL on Monday February 17, 2003 in her 86th year. She is survived by her daughter, Mimi Rosenwald of Ossining, NY; her son John Moore of NYC; her step-daughter Susan Johnson of Newport, RI; her stepson Jay Baldwin of Annapolis, MD; by many grandchildren and several greatgrandchildren. Memorial services will be held in Delray Beach and in Locust Valley.
