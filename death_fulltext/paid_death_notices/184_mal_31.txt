
        HIGGINS-Msgr. George. An extraordinary individual who has influenced and made extraordinary efforts in promoting Catholic-Jewish dialogue, who was most recently honored in June 2001 by the International Catholic-Jewish liaison committee (sponsored by the Vatican and the International Jewish Committee for Interreligious consultations.) We will miss his wisdom and guidance in interfaith dialogue. International Jewish Committee for Interreligious Consulations Seymour D. Reich, Chairman Rabbi Dr. Leon Feldman, Secy.
