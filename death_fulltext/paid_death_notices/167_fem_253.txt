
        GLOBE-Leah. EMUNAH of America mourns the sad loss of Leah Globe, beloved mother of Rena Quint, Vice Chairman of World EMUNAH. Leah was a woman of strength and artistic imagination who earned the great respect of EMUNAH members in Israel and of EMUNAH members around the globe. She will be missed by the entire Jewish communty. Marcia Genuth National President Melanie Oelbaum, Hon. National President
