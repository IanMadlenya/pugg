
        KARCHER--Barbara Jane. Of East Northport, NY, on April 16, 2006. Born in Brooklyn, NY, February 8, 1936, to the late Charles Moran and Grace Pallaske. Beloved wife of the late George Sr., and loving mother of George Jr. and his wife Roseann, Gerard, Maureen and her husband Gene Williams, and Patrick. Cherished grandmother of Stephan, Brendan, Elijah, Sarah Grace Williams, Christina and Alaina Karcher. Dear sister of Jean Lynch and Patricia (David) Porinchak. Loving stepdaughter of Marguerite Moran. Also survived by many nieces, nephews, cousins, friends, and all the poeple she enjoyed over the years at the beach at Fire Island. Associate of Wasserman and Wise. Visiting Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 pm, at Brueggemann Funeral Home, 522 Larkfield Road, East Northport. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 9:45 am, at St. Anthony of Padua R.C.
