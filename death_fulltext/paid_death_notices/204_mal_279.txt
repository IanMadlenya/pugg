
        DUFFY--Martin. The Faculty, Staff and Board of Directors of the New York University Child Study Center mourn the loss of Martin Duffy whose generous spirit informed his work with the NYU Child Study Center Silver Shield Bereavement Program for families of uniformed officers who perished on September 11, 2001. Our sympathy and prayers are with his wife Valerie, his son John and all his colleagues at the Silver Shield Foundation. Brooke Garber Neidich Chair, Board of Directors Harold S. Koplewicz, M.D. Director
