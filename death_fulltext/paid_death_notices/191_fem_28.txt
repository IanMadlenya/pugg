
        GOLDFEDER-Rose. 88, died peacefully surrounded by her family. Card whiz, bowling champ and mahjongg pro, Rosie was a presence in Brooklyn and Fort Lauderdale. Dearly loved her late husband Sammy, and her children Edward (Phyllis) and Jerry (Alice); grandchildren Michelle, Lisa, Peter (Larissa) and Carly; great-grandchildren, Jonah and Andrew. She will definitely be missed.
