
        BENDINER-Bertram. On April 21st, 1999 in his 87th year. Beloved husband for 58 years of Ruth (nee Marcus), father of Gareth and Kenneth, brother of Robert and Elmer, grandfather of Ezra, Zachary, and Claire. Esteemed labor consultant UAW, great fisherman, and author. We will miss his whimsical humor and gentleness. Private services.
