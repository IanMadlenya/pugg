
        DURRER-Gustav T. Retired dentist of Manhattan, at the age of 90. Beloved husband of Margot Ammann Durrer. He died peacefully at home after a short illness. He practiced prosthodontic dentistry in Rockefeller Plaza from 1946 to 1995 and served on the teaching staff of New York University College of Dentistry, Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery and Stonybrook University College of Dentistry. He lectured in USA and abroad. Born and educated in Switzerland, he immigrated to America in 1939, serving in the Medical Corp. of the US Army from 1942-1946. He shall be sorely missed by his wife, his sister and brother-in-law and nieces and nephews in Switzerland as well as friends and colleagues here and abroad. He will be remembered for his honesty, compassion, dry sense of humor and gentle, courtly manner. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Thursday, October 4th, 2-5PM and 7-9PM. Funeral mass will be held at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Avenue at 66th, Friday, October 5th, 10:30AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to Rogosin Institute at 505 East 70th Street, NYC 10021.
