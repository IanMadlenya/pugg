
        DREW-Dr. Gerald M. On Nov. 24, 1999. Beloved husband to Selma, loving father to David and Jenny. Esteemed colleague, mentor and loyal friend. Dr. Drew, University of Michigan Dental School, 1948, practiced fifty years with outstanding expertise and unequalled passion. He was a retired U.S. Navy Lt. Commander, a Board Certified Endodontist, Professor at Columbia and FDU, & Director of PG Endo at NYU. His legacy of generosity, love, compassion, humor and friendship will live on in all of us. Services today, November 26, 1 PM, Garlick Funeral, Monticello
