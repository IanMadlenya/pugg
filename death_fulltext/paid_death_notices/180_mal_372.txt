
        EISENBERG-George H., 92. On January 13th, 2002 in Rancho Bernardo, CA. Predeceased by adored wife, Dorothy, of 41 years. Beloved husband of Maidie for 31 years. Loving father of M. Michael. Devoted grandfather of Elyse, Ellen & Andrea. Cherished great-grandfather of Gabby and Tom. A gifted athlete all his life. A life characterized by honesty, integrity, generosity, loyalty, and an incredible work ethic.
