
        DIAMOND-Lawrence, DMD. The employees and staff of the New York Hospital Medical Center of Queens are saddened by the loss of Lawrence Diamond, DMD. Dr. Diamond performed his residency at New York Hospital Queens and was an outstanding attending dentist in the Department of Dental Medicine. We extend our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife, Felice, and their family. Stephen S. Mills, President and Chief Executive Officer
