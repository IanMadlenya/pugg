
        HUET-Jean. The trustees, staff and members of The Metropolitan Museum of Art's Business Committee mourn the passing of Jean Huet, Co-Chairman of the Metropolitan's Business Committee and formerly Chairman of the Museum's International Business Committee. Since 1993, he served as an advocate for the Museum in the international business community. His leadership, advice and counsel will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Michele, and to the entire Huet family. James R. Houghton, Chairman Philippe de Montebello, Director David E. McKinney, President Steven Rattner, Trustee & Chair, The Business Committee
        HUET-Jean. The Advisory Board of the Center for French Civilization and Culture of New York University wishes to express its deep sorrow at the sudden death of our esteemed colleague and friend, Jean Huet, who served as Chair of the Advisory Board and was a leader in French-American cultural relations. We will miss him. We extend sincere condolences to his family, friends, and his colleagues at the Societe Generale.
