
        KELLY-Patrick J. Of Yonkers, N.Y. Suddenly on July 25, 2000. He was a retired U.S. Marine Officer and was presently employed as the Director of Operations at the Edwin Gould Academy in Chestnut Ridge, N.Y. Beloved husband of Patricia Hartnett Kelly, Special Agent in charge for Diplomatic Security at U.S. State Dept. Devoted father of Meaghan and Tara, cherished son of Anne Sheridan Kelly, adored brother of Michael Kelly and Catherine Hammond. Viewing at the David J. Hodder F.H., 899 McLean Ave, (near Kimball Ave) Yonkers, Friday, 7-9 PM, Saturday and Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass St Barnabas Church, Monday, 10:30 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
