
        BOBRINSKOY --Nicholas, Alexis (Count). February 8th 1921 -September 12th 2006. Grand Prior of the Sovereign Order of Orthodox Knights Hospitaller of St. John of Jerusalem, great - great - great grandson of Empress Catherine the Great of Russia. He was married to Tatiana N. Timasheff (daughter of the eminent sociologist Professor Nicholas S. Timasheff), 2 children Alexis and Catherine and four grand children. The wake will be held on Thursday September 14th at 7:00pm. Funeral will be held on Friday September 15th, 11:00am, wake and funeral will be held at the the Cathedral of the Holy Virgin Protection, 59 East Second Street. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be sent to The order of St. John's .
