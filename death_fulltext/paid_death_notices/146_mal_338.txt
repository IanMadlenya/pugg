
        WACHTEL-Alfred F., Esquire. Devoted son of the late Elsie (nee Bender) and Samuel. He is survived by his beloved and devoted children Eric David and Marjorie Wachtel, Esqs. and Nancy Lynne Wolff and by his adored grandchildren Molly Elizabeth and Andrew Georges Wolff and Harrison Samuel Wachtel. He was predeceased by his only brother Howard and his beloved son-in-law Charles Henry Wolff, M.D. He proudly served his country in World War II and was awarded a Purple Heart. For over forty years he engaged in the private practice of law.
