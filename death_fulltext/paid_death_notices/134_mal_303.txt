
        KEAVY-William T., M.D., age 62, an internationally renowned reconstructive and plastic surgeon, died March 26, 1998 in Lenox Hill Hospital. Dr. Keavy was born in Bridgeport, CT., and resided in Westport, CT., and Central Park South, NYC. He was a graduate of Fairfield Preparatory School, Fairfield University, and Georgetown University. He received a law degree from Fordham University and his medical degree from Marquette University. He was on the Staff at Lenox Hill Hospital, and Doctors Hospital both in NYC, and St. Joseph's Hospital Stamford. He was a member of the Section of Plastic Surgery for over thirty years, and an active member of LHH Alumni Association. A devoted and gifted educator who took great pleasure in sharing his knowledge and love of surgery with young physicians. Dr. Keavy impressed all who knew him with his kindness, compassion and integrity. He will be deeply missed by his family, his friends and his colleagues. Survivors include his mother: Irene Burns Keavy, of Westport, and many cousins. Friends are invited to attend the funeral on Monday at 11:00 a.m. in St. Luke's Church, 49 N. Turkey Road, Westport, CT, interment in St. Michael's Cemetery, Stratford, Connecticut. Friends may call on Sunday from 4 to 8 P.M., at the Cyril F. Mullins Funeral Home 399 White Plains Road, Trumbull, CT. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Dr. William T. Keavy Scholarship Fund at Fairfield Preparatory School, North Benson Road, Fairfield, CT.
        KEAVY-William T., M.D. Fordham University School of Law records with profound sorrow the passing of Dr. Keavy, a member of the class of 1988 and especially remembers his professional care of our late Professor Peter J. O'Connor. John D. Feerick, Dean
