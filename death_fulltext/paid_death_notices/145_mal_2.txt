
        ROTH-Daniel. Died January 30, 1999, at Albert Einstein Hospital. He was 45. Mr. Roth was born in New Rochelle, NY, on March 20, 1953. He graduated from Bayside High School in Queens, NY, and attended Hunter College. He worked as a freelance writer. Mr. Roth is survived by his mother Rhoda of New Rochelle, a sister Amy and her husband Michael Tietz of New Rochelle. Also survived by a nephew Benjamin Tietz of New Rochelle. He was predeceased by his father Monroe Roth. A chapel service will be held today, 12:30 PM, at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 LeCount Place, New Rochelle, NY. Interment to follow at Mt. Eden Cemetery. Contributions to a charity of one's choice.
