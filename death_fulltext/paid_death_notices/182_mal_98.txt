
        YOUNG-Dr. Victor J. Of Green port, L.I. (formerly of Port Washington) on March 1, 2002 at the age of 88. Graduate of Albion College and the University of Iowa. Professor Emeritus at CUNY and Queensboro Community College. Former Vice-president of Hazeltine and Research Engineer with Sperry-Rand. Beloved husband of the late, Margaret Brandt Young. Loving father and father-in-law of Jan and Mary Young of Port Washington, WI and Donald and Dr. JoAnn Young of Cutchogue, NY. Cherished grandfather of Alice Serra and Tom Young and great-grandfather of Julia and Joseph Serra. Also survived by his cat, LaPorte. Graveside services, Thursday, March 7th at 10 A.M. in Parkview Cemetery, Hastings, Nebraska. A memorial service is planned for a later date at the Greenport United Methodist Church. For further information please contact the DeFriest-Grattan Funeral Homes, Southold, N.Y.
