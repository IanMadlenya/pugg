
        LEVINE-Paul. Died January 5, 1999 at ABC Hospital, Mexico City. Beloved father of Susan M. Levine, M.D., and grandfather of Thomas and Richard. Paul was born in New York City, where he worked as an accountant before moving to Mexico in 1952. A pioneering entrepreneur, he was successful in his own businesses as well as financing the startup of many Mexican firms. He will be missed. Memorial gifts may be sent to The New Jersey Chronic Fatigue Syndrome Association, Inc., P.O. Box 726, Oradell, New Jersey 07649.
