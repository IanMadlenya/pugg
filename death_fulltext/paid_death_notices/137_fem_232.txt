
        TOMPKINS-Elsie (nee Cohen), 90, of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of White Plains, NY and Scottsdale, AZ. Beloved wife of Max (Mac G. Tompkins) for 67 years. Loving mother of Judge Harold Tompkins. Mother-in-law of Frances and cherished grandmother of Laurence Alan Fish Tompkins. An avid golfer and bridge player, she will be remembered with admiration and affection by her family and friends. Services and interment private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Boca Raton Community Hospital, Foundation Dept., 745 Meadows Road, Boca Raton, FL 33486.
