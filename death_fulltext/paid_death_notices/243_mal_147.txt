
        PYNE--Eben. The Juilliard community mourns the passing of Eben Pyne, trustee emeritus of the School since 2001 and a Board member appointed in 1972. With intelligence, elegance, and diplomacy, Eben served Juilliard in numerous ways. His wise counsel was of great importance to Juilliard at critical times in the School's history. A knowledgeable and dedicated supporter of all the performing arts, he was particularly involved in vocal arts activities at the School. Our condolences are extended to his wife, Nancy, and his entire family. Bruce Kovner, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President $(6$)PYNE--Eben Wright. The Board of Trustees and the Staff of the Wildlife Conservation Society mourn the death of our longtime friend and Honorary Trustee, Eben Pyne. As a valued Member of the Board since 1950, Eben played an active role on several committees, including for more than a decade as Assistant Treasurer. He had a passion for wildlife and wild places and generously supported WCS's mission at home and through its global conservation efforts. His wisdom and spirit will be missed. Our deepest sympathies to his wife, Nancy, and the entire Pyne family. David T. Schiff Chairman of the Board Steven E. Sanderson President and CEO Wildlife Conservation Society $(6$)PYNE--Eben.
