
        REVSON-Julie Phelps. Former wife of Revlon co-founder Martin Revson, died sunday, Febraury 6 in California. She is the mother of deceased race car driver Peter Revson and Douglas Revson. She is survived by her two daughters Julie Revson of Richmond, VA and Jennifer Revson of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. She also leaves behind her two grandsons Matthew Cox of Waitsfield, VT and Jason Sheldon of New York City and a sister Mrs. Elizabeth Graessle of White Plains, NY. Funeral service will be held this Saturday, February 12, at 11am at the Ballard Durand Funeral Home in White Plains, NY followed by interment at the Ferncliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, NY
