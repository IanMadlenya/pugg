
        MARKER-Victor. The Board of Directors and Staff of the Jewish Outreach Institute in New York mourn the passing of Victor Marker, the father of our friend and colleague Richard Marker who also serves as the chairman of our Jewish Connection Partnership project. Our deepest sympathy to Richard Marker, his wife Mirele and family. Terrence A. Elkes, President David Sacks, Chairman Dr. Kerry M. Olitzky, Exec. Dir. Dr. Egon Mayer, Founding Dir.
