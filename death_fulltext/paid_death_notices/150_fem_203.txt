
        HIRSCHHORN-Shirley. Beloved sister of Gertrude Rachelson, sister-in-law of Murray Rachelson, Great Aunt, Aunt and friend to many, died Wednesday, July 21, 1999 in Aventura, Florida. Born on April 10, 1902 in NYC, she was the sister of the late Pauline Becher, Rita Roth & Henrietta Hirschhorn. Graveside services, Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York on Monday, July 26, at 11 A.M.
