
        TISHMAN -- Margaret. The Board and Staff of AIPAC express our profound sorrow at the passing of a devoted proIsrael activist and friend of Israel, Peggy Tishman. She was an inspiring role model for all women, who led the way towards creating a stronger and more compassionate Jewish community. May her family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Bernice Manocherian, President-Elect Howard Kohr, Exec Director Michael Sachs, North East Regional Director
