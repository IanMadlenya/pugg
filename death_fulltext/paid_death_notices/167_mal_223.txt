
        CUMMINGS-Joseph P., Jr. Died on December 16, 2000, in New York. Beloved husband of Miriam Lutz Cummings. Loving father of Patricia McCarthy, Maureen Brown, Richard K. Cummings and Kurt P. Cummings. Cherished grandfather of 13 grandchildren, and predeceased by his granddaughter Amanda E. Cummings in 1978. Dear brother of Rosalie Baker. Graduate and former Trustee of the University of Notre Dame, and an officer in the US Navy during WWII. Deputy Senior Partner of Peat Marwick Mitchell & Co., predecessor firm to KPMG in New York. He was the Chairman of the AICPA, Deputy Chairman of the APB, as well as as the first Chairman of the IAPB. Member of the American Institute of CPAs and the New York State Society of CPAs. Visiting hours will be held at Gallagher Funeral Home, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, on Monday, December 18, from 6 to 9 PM. Funeral Mass St. Michael Church, 469 North St, Greenwich, Tuesday, December 19, at 10 AM. Burial will follow at St. Mary Cemetery, Greenwich, CT. Contributions can be made to the University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, IN 46556.
