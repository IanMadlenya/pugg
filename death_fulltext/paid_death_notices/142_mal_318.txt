
        BACSO-George. The USPTA mourns the passing of distinguished colleague, past president and USPTA Hall of Fame member George Bacso. We fondly remember his his enthusiasm for life, his years of dedication to our sport and his impact on thousands in the tennis world. His character and commitment were essential to our organization's success and will greatly be missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family and others who loved him, as our association did. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the USPTA Foundation, (for George Bacso, 3535 Briarpark Drive, Houston, TX 77042 to establish a tennis fund for children and provide education for needy teaching professionals. Members of the United States Professional Tennis Association
