
        FORCHHEIMER - Paul L. Died peacefully at age 86, on Saturday, April 26, 2003. Husband of the late Gerda, brother and brother-in-law of Igo and Marion, father and father-in-law of Mark, Shelley, Linda, Robert, Elliot, Joan, Claire and George. Grandfather of Adam, Scott, Daniel, Jeremy, Kenneth, Ilana and Jacob. Uncle, cousin, and friend to many. Born in Karlsruhe, Germany, he emigrated to Israel and then to the United States. Since 1948 he devotedly served as founder of Paul L. Forchheimer and Company, investment brokers. May his memory be a source of strength and blessing.
