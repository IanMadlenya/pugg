
        SEGAL--Mildred. The Officers, Trustees and members of The Forest Hills Jewish Center family mourn the death of Mildred Segal, beloved mother of our esteemed Rabbi's wife, Robin Segal Skolnik. As she has been with so many of us in our times of need through the years, so are we with Robin and her family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Cantor Adam Frei Matthew R. Beizer President Shira Shapiro Executive Director
