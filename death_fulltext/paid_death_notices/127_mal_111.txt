
        DEUTSCHER-Bernard. Born May 18, 1915, passed away on August 10, 1997. The grave site service will be on Thursday, August 14, 1997 at 10:30 AM at the Beth David Cemetry in Elmont. He is survived by his wife Elsie, son Allan, two daughters Freda and Jane and seven grand children: Matthew, Andrew, Amy, Jessica, Zachary, Joshua and Danielle.
