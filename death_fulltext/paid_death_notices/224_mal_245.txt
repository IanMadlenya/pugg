
        WEISS--Melvin. On September 21, 2005. Beloved husband for 48 years to Felicia. Loving father and father-in-law of Stephanie and Stephen, Peter and Jennifer. Devoted and adored grandfather of Alex, Emily, Charlotte and Spencer. For 75 years, Mel cared deeply for his family and friends, sharing his love and laughter. He was the sweetest, kindest and most generous man to all whose lives he touched. He will be forever missed.
