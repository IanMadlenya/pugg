
        BREITBART-Robert S. Age 87, Died suddenly on October 11, 2001. He was a graduate of Columbia College and Columbia Law School. He was an attorney practicing law since 1936. He was also a professor at Pace University teaching law and taxation from 1945 through 1995. He is survived by his beloved wife and best friend, Ellen. Cherished father of Carol Furst and Barbara Bader and sons-in-law Robert Furst and Gary Bader. Adored grandfather of Jennifer and Joseph Casey, Alison and Dana Furst, and Daniel and Amy Bader. Services on Friday, October 12, 1PM at ''RiversideNassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Interment will follow at Shaarey Pardes Cemetery in East Hampton, L.I.
        BREITBART-Robert. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of our congregant Robert Breitbart. We extend our deepest condolences to Ellen Breitbart and their families. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
