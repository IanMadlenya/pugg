
        OBLER-Ida (Aida). On March 14, 1999. Former resident at 400 East 52nd Street in the city she loved, New York, and recently at Senior Quarters in East Northport, NY. Mother of Marcia Fisher, three grandchildren, five great-grandchildren, and an extended family of hundreds. She lived the fairy tale life reserved for those who went to the ''Big City'' and found fame, fortune and happiness. In lieu of flowers, donations to your choice. Funeral services Tuesday, 12:45 PM, at the I.J. Morris Dix Hills Chapel, 21 East Deer Park Rd, Dix Hills.
