
        KRASNER-David H. A former Schenley Industries marketing executive. Died on November 5 at his home in Princeton, N.J. He was 86. During his lengthy retirememt, he volunteered with the International Executive Service Corps as a consultant helping businesses in developing countries and the privatization on the Eastern Bloc. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Sandra, four children Carla, Shelley, Philip, and Dr. Julia; a sister Cecil Jaffe, a brother Harry, 3 sons-in-law, and 8 grandchildren. Contributions may be made in his memory to the American Heart Association and designate them for research.
