
        DAY--Mary. Founder of the Washington School of Ballet and the Washington Ballet Company. On July 11, 2006. She is survived by her long time associate and former General Director of the Washington Ballet, Elvi Moore and a legion of friends in the dance world. A memorial Service will be held at a later date. Interment Private. In lieu of flowers Memorial Contributions may be made to the Mary Day Scholarship fund for the Dance Program of the Laurel Fund for the Performing Arts. For information Joseph Gawler's Sons, Inc.
