
        LOWENTHAL-Milton, Dr. At age 84, on November 20, 2001. Proud father of Edward, Susan, Jane, Andrew, & Amy. Devoted fatherin-law of Ilene and Sharon. Loving grandfather to Deborah, Jennifer, David, Jared, Lauren, Joshua, Jeremy, Jason, Daniel, and Sarah. Beloved brother of Janet. He was a healer, a scholar and teacher, and a baker. He cared deeply for people in need. He inspired us and challenged our ideas. He will be greatly missed. Private services will be held. In lieu of flowers, donations to The New York University School of Medicine, 550 First Avenue, New York, NY 10016-6497.
