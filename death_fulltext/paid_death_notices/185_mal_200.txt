
        KURLANSKY-Philip Mendel, 86. Still a practicing dentist, husband of Roslyn for 60 years, died June 2. Always eager to help anyone in pain, or work for his community, his life, by example, taught the meaning of social responsibility. But the thing of it is this, I loved having a martini with him and talking over world events. I loved his stories with the middle missing-big deal-and though I never learned the meaning of bully - for - you - and - the - Red Sox, nor why he sang driving over the George Washington Bridge, I will always love and miss him and a martini will never taste as good. His son Mark daughter-in-law Marian and grand-daughter Talia
