
        BERNSTEIN-Ned. Did you hear the one about the cannibal who passed his friend in the woods? Jokester, punster, wordsmith, trumpeter, real estate broker and operator, died on April 20, at the age of 89. Son of Sarah and Alex. Father of Joel and Asher. Grandfather of Mark, Francis, Eric, Alex and Leslie. Great grandfather of Caleb and Lev. Survived by his sister, Annaleah, 95, and his brother, Sidney, 97. His humor & wit will be missed but always remembered. Funeral services Sunday, April 22, 10:00 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
