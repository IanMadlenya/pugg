
        HYDE-Donald R. Of Devon, PA. Passed away at his home after a courageous battle with cancer on Saturday, July 25. Husband of Margaret Willets Hyde. Father of Jeffrey W., Donald R., and Lawrence W. Hyde. Grandfather of Matthew W. and Jennifer A. Hyde. Brother of Barbara H. Crafford and Harriet H. Mauck. A memorial service will be held 11 A.M., on Thursday, July 30 in the Chapel of St. David's Episcopal Church, 763 Valley Forge Rd, Wayne, PA. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in Don's memory may be made to the Lawrenceville School, PO Box 6008, Lawrenceville, NJ 08648 or to Annual Giving, Princeton University, Box 46, Princeton, NJ 08544. or to the American Cancer Society, 428 Exton Commons, Exton, PA. 19341. For information: Stuard Funeral Home, Ardmore, PA, 610-649-0243.
