
        WATROUS--Philip, J. a financial executive, died July 1, 2006. Throughout his life he provided assistance and encouragement to countless individuals. He was a founding director of The Jericho Project, providing housing for recovering substance abusers, and was treasurer of Cornerstone Learning Center providing educational programs to adolescents. He served as a treasurer and vestry member at St. Matthew and St. Timothy church and as a trustee of the Episcopal Diocese of New York, which awarded him the Bishop's Cross for his community service. Services Saturday 5:30 at St. Matthew and St. Timothy's, 26 W. 84th St. NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Jericho Project or Cornerstone Learning Center, 20 W. 84th St. #
