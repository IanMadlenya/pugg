        HOCHBERG-Irving. The Graduate Center of The City University of New York mourns the loss of its longtime valued colleague, Irv Hochberg, who served as Executive Officer of the doctoral program in Speech and Hearing Sciences for 25 years and who contributed so much to the Graduate Center. Our condolences to his wife, Ellen, and his family. Funeral services are scheduled for 11:15 AM, Monday, November 1, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam. Frances Degen Horowitz, President
