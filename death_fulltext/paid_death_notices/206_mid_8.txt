
        EHRICH--Dr. F. Frederick, of Wilmington, DE, on March 14, 2004, at the age of 84. Beloved husband of Ceceil (nee Rinder); loving father of Anne Alden and her husband David of Bloomfield Hills, MI, Beth Ehrich Berkeley and her husband Dr. Alan of Scarsdale, NY, and Meg Goodman and her husband Bennett of Scarsdale, NY; adoring grandfather of Ben, Peter, Lauren, Lissy, Lisa, Jared, Ellen, Alex, Rachel and Julia; dear brother of Lillian Rapp of Cranbury, NJ and Herbert Ehrich of Mountainside, NJ. Fred lived his life to the highest standards and taught his family by example. He was dearly loved and will remain in our hearts forever. Funeral service Tuesday, March 16, 12:30 PM, from the ''Westchester Reform Temple,'' 255 Mamaroneck Rd., Scarsdale, NY.
