
        SHAPIRO-Howard. The East End Hospice Board of Directors, staff and volunteers mourn the untimely death of Howard Shapiro, a valued friend and loyal supporter. We will be forever grateful to Howard for his dedication and commitment to East End Hospice and to the East End Hospice Open, a tennis tournament to benefit Hospice. We send our deepest sympathies to his wife Suzy, his family and to his many friends and colleagues. W. Michael Pitcher, President East End Hospice, Inc Westhampton Beach, NY
