
        SHIMM--Mark. Born 1947, passed away September 16, after a long courageous battle with Leukemia and Lymphoma. Mark was a gifted photographer and songwriter who leaves a legacy of beautiful work. He is survived by sisters Emily Sendler (David) of Riverdale, Dorrine Levy (Jack) of Liberty, and niece and nephews Karen, Matthew, Adam and Michael. A graveside service will be held Sunday, September 19 at Jeffersonville Jewish Cemetery. Contact Garlick Funeral Home: 845-647-7747.
