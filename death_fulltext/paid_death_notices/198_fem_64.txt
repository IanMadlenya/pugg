
        KIRSTEN--Rose. Died on July 8, 2003 at age 95 in Florida. Beloved wife of the late George. Loving mother of Bob Kirsten (Helene) and Linda Spitzer (Jerry). Proud grandmother of Jeffrey Spitzer (Arlene), Rick Spitzer, David Kirsten (Janet), Bruce Kirsten and Michael Kirsten. Greatgrandmother of Jacob and Hannah Spitzer. Her sense of humor and wit helped her cope with many challenges in her life. Contributions to the Metropolitan Council on Jewish Poverty, NY, Att: Willy Rapfogel.
        KIRSTEN--Rose. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rose Kirsten, beloved mother of Linda Spitzer and mother-in-law of Jerome Spitzer, cherished friends of UJA-Federation. Linda is a committed leader in the Manhattan Women's Campaign and Jerry serves on the Board of Directors of UJAFederation. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Linda, Jerry, and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO >>AD#
