
        SOFFER-Paul E., M.D., 78. Of Lake Worth, Florida and North Shore Towers, Long Island. With love and sadness we mourn the passing of Paul Soffer on January 7, 2003. Beloved husband of Babette Weil Soffer for 55 years. Cherished father of Dr. Robin Soffer Sternberg and Debra Ornan, adored grandfather of Hannah Sternberg and Joseph Sternberg. Brother of the late Jessica Fleishman and Dr. Milton D. Soffer. He was a graduate of NYU College of Medicine. He practiced Obstetrics and Gynecology on Long Island. Funeral Services will be held on Sunday, January 12, in Florida.
        SOFFER-Paul E., M.D. We will forever miss your intelligence, strength & friendship. Rest in peace, dear friend. Samson and Halina
