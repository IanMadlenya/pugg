
        BULLATY - LOMEO - Sonja. Be loved partner of Angelo Lomeo, for 49 years, died on October 5, 2000. She was 76 years old. A renowned photographer with a passion for life, she inspired many through her beautiful images. Bereft of family by the Holocaust, Sonja opened her heart to the world. She will be deeply missed by all of us who knew and loved her. A Memorial Service is being planned but not yet scheduled. Friends who wish to know the date can write to: AG Editions, 41 Union Square West, Suite 523, New York, New York 10003.
