
        TACCHI--Mary Jane, 61. On Monday, September 1, 2003. Coordinator of Young Adult Services at the New York Public Library. Mary Jane began her career within the branch libraries of the New York Public Library in 1966 after receiving her Masters in Library Science from the University of California at Berkeley. After serving in several Young Adult positions in the Bronx branches, she became the Supervising Borough Specialist responsible for oversight for Young Adult work in all Bronx branches. Using that broad experience, she went on to become Assistant Coordinator of Young Adult Services for the Branch Libraries and subsequently, in 1998, Young Adult Coordinator. She is survived by her brother Tom Tacchi, a niece Torry Dawn and two nephews, John and Charlie. Funeral services Friday, 3:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Those wishing to honor Mary Jane's memory may contribute to the support of Young Adult Services in the branches of the New York Public Library, The Door, 121 Ave of the Americas, N.Y., N.Y. 10013 or the Central Park Conservancy, 14 E. 60 St., N.Y., N.Y. 10022. Family will also receive written tributes at www.mem.com
