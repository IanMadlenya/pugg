
        CRANDELL-Edwin Hawley, died on May 7th, 1997 at the age of 82. Devoted husband of the late Maxine Hothorn Crandell who died on November 30, 1996. Mr. Crandell was also a beloved uncle to Cathy Crandell Long of Summit, NJ, Laurie Roberts of NYC and many other nieces and nephews. Mr. Crandell was a longtime resident of Rye, New York. A retired partner of M.J. Meehan, he was a member of the New York Stock Exchange for more than fifty years. Mr. Crandell attended the Bronxville Schools and Westminster School and New York University. He was a Captain in the Army Engineering Corps during World War II. He was a member of the Apawamis Club, the American Yacht Club, the US Seniors and the Buttonwood Club. In addition to his nieces and nephews he is survived by 2 sisters-in-law, Mary Elizabeth Crandell of Ponte Verde, FL, and Cynthia Hothorn Williams of Aiken, SC. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association - West chester Chapter, 785 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains, NY 10605 or the American Lung Association, 35 Orchard St., White Plains, NY 10605. A memorial service for both Maxine and Edwin Crandell will be held at 1 PM, on Sunday, May 18th, at Rye Presbyterian Church in Rye, New York. Any questions please call 914-967-0129.
