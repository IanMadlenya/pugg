
        WAKS-Beatrice Dichter. Of Delray Beach, FL, on February 19, at the age of 89. She is survived by Meyer, her husband of 65 years; sisters Pauline Tolmage and Muriel Jaffe; sons David and Leonard; and grandchildren Mark Waks, Ruth Bennett, Allison Teger, Michael Teger, and Sjoma Fadeev. A longtime teacher in the NYC high schools, she retired in 1972 and moved to Florida in 1975. A celebration of her life will be held in Delray Beach on March 2, and later in New Jersey. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Arthritis Foundation, 2330 South Congress Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33445.
