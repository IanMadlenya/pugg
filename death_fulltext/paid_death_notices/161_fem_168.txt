
        AXTON-April Ruth. On June 14, 2000 at home, age 97. A supporter of the arts, who had a great love for music. Her friendship, joy for life and style will be greatly missed by her many friends. Reposing at Frank E. Campell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81st St, Wednesday, June 21, 6:30-8:30PM. Funeral mass at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave. at 66th St., Thursday, 11 AM. Interment private.
        AXTON-April Ruth. The Board of Directors and staff of The Santa Fe Opera note with sadness the passing of their friend and benefactor, April Axton.
