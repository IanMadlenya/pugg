
        JONES-Charles. Composer. Beloved husband of Sally. Beloved grandfather of Cameron. Funeral services Wednesday, June 11, 11 AM, All Saints Church, 230 E. 60 St.
        JONES-Charles. June 21, 1910June 6, 1997. The Friends of Charles Jones mourn the passing of this distinquished composer and teacher, beloved mentor to composers, performers, scholars and critics. His distinctive compositions combine meticulous craft with warm lyricism, and hold a special place in the music of our time. Our sympathy to his wife Sally and his grandson Cameron. A memorial concert will be held in the fall. Friends of Charles Jones Elliott Hurwitt, Secretary
