
        VOLCHOK-Saul. September 1, 2001. Husband of Susan (nee Weikers) of Bala Cynwyd, PA. Father of Joyce (Milton) Cross and Edward (Karen) Volchok. Son-in-law of Doris Kahn. Brother-in-law of Barbara and Stanley Friedman and Clark and Roni Weikers. Grandfather of Brian (Kerry), Jonathon (Deena) and Rosanna. Devoted friend of Mark and Helene Hankin and family. Interment Montefiore Cemetery. The family will return to the late residence. Contributions in his memory may be made to the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Museum, Israel Guide Dog School for the Blind, or a charity of the doner's choice. www.levinefuneral.com
