
        FLAMM-Donald. The officers and Board of Directors of the Hebrew Free Loan Society mourn the loss of Donald Flamm, Vice-President and Board member for over 50 years. His wise counsel and contributions to the Society's mission over the years were invaluable. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Elayne, and to his entire family. May they be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. David Durst, Chairman Judah Gribetz, President Shana Novick, Executive Director
        FLAMM-Donald. The Tisch School of the Arts at New York University expresses its deep sorrow at the passing of Donald Flamm, a generous friend and benefactor. Mr. Flamm's career embodied the history of broadcasting, and through his generous support to the Donald Flamm Broadcast Studio at NYU's Tisch School, he helped ensure its future. He shall be missed. Dr. Mary Schmidt Campbell, Dean NYU's Tisch School of the Arts
        FLAMM-Donald. The Board, playwrights and staff of New Dramatists mourn the loss of our esteemed Advisory Board Member and longtime friend. His wise counsel and unforgettable voice will be greatly missed. We offer our deepest sympathy to Mrs. Flamm, to his family and friends and to his beloved Friars.
        FLAMM-Donald. Alpine Country Club, its Officers, Board of Governors and members deeply mourn the passing of their fellow member Donald Flamm, and offer their deepest sympathy to his wife Elayne and the entire Flamm family. Alpine Country Club Michael Bergstein, Pres.
