
        KOWAL-John. Died March 18, at home with his immediate family, Deborah Guide, Lisa Kowal-Philipides and Robert Kowal. John was a loving father, and grandfather to Michele, David, Niko, Nakora and Rhys. He was a mentor and role model for his entire extended family and community. He will be loved and missed but his legacy will live on through all of us. Services 12:30 PM, Tuesday, at Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 W. 83rd St. Interment in Elizabeth, NJ.
        KOWAL - John. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our beloved longtime member and Honary Trustee, John Kowal. John inspired our congregation and community to live their faith by working for the homeless and the voiceless. We will miss his passion, kindness and love. Your prophetic calls always will inform our actions. Our deepest condolences to children Deborah, Lisa, and Robert, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Sr. Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
