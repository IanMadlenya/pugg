
        DIKER-Bertram J. Died August 29, 1999 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Rhona. Loving father of Susan and son-in-law William. Devoted and loving grandfather of Alexandra. He is also survived by his brother Charles. Born in New York on April 6, 1931, he was the son of Helen Hoffner and Daniel Diker. He was a graduate of Dartmouth College and Harvard Business School. We was a retired New York investment advisor, businessman and philanthropist. He was a resident of Southampton, N.Y. and a Director of the Southampton Hospital. Funeral service Wednesday, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, NY, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
        DIKER-Bertram J. The members of the Board of Directors of Southampton Hospital mourn the death of fellow Board member Bertram J. Diker, acknowledge his dedication and generous service to the Hospital, and express our deepest sympathy to his family. R. Peter Sullivan III Chairman of the Board Donald H. Louchheim Vice Chairman of the Board
