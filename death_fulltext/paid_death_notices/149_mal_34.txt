
        URIBE-Luis Enrique. June 1, 1999. Husband of Irma, father of Adelaida Uribe Bennett and her husband Edward G.R. Bennett and Maria del Pilar Uribe, grandfather of Edward, Elisa and Pilar Bennett. Friend of many years to Mercedes Guzman and Fernanda Macieira. A renown ophthalmologist and attending surgeon at both Manhattan Eye and Ear Hospital and Beth Israel Medical Center. Memorial service Friday, June 4, 10:30AM at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave. at 66th St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Eye Bank for Sight Restoration, 120 Wall St., 3rd Floor, NY, NY 10005. He was a caring and dedicated gentleman to his patients, friends and family.
        URIBE-Luis Enrique, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical family joins with the Physicians and Staff of The Department of Opthalmology in acknowledging the passing of our beloved friend and colleague, Luis Enrique Uribe, M.D. To his wife, Irma, his daughters, Pilar Uribe and Adelaida Bennett, his family and friends, and all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President and CEO Henry Singman, M.D., President, Medical Board H. Jay Wisnicki, M.D., Chairman, Dept of Opthalmology Robert L. Salant, M.D., President, Doctors Alumni Assoc
        URIBE-Luis E., M.D. The Board of Directors, Medical Advisory Board and staff of the Eye-Bank for Sight Restoration deeply mourn the death of Dr. Uribe, who served on the Medical Advisory Board from 1989 to 1991 and was consistently supportive of the Eye-Bank's efforts over the years. We extend our sincere sympathy to his family. Anne Cote Taylor, President Robert A. D'Amico, M.D., Chairman, Medical Advisory Board
