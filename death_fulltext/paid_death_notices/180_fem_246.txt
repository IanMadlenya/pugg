
        GEWIRTZ-Lola. Yeshiva University Women's Organization (YUWO) expresses sorrow upon the passing of Lola Gewirtz, founding president of the Fairfield-Bridgeport, Connecticut chapter and former member of the YUWO National Board. Heartfelt condolences to her daughter Dorothy Berman, member of the YUWO National Board and to all members of the family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dinah Pinczower YUWO National Chairman
