
        SIMON--Ria D. Born October 17, 1929 in NYC, died February 14, 2005, at home, Palm Beach, FL. Beloved wife for 22 years of Lenard de Lane, dear sister, sister-in-law, aunt. A graduate of Brooklyn College and Brooklyn Law School, she specialized in matrimonial law, first as a partner in the firm of Stannage & Simon, later in Connecticut. In Florida, she became a divorce mediator. She is cited in ''The Best and Brightest Lawyers in America, 1983.'' An avid sportswoman, she excelled in tennis, figure skating and horsemanship. Her directness, thoughtful advice, and keen interest in people she cared about were appreciated by all who knew her.
