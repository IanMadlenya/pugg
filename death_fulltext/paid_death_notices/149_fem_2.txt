
        PLUM-Jean Houston. On May 28, 1999 in Orlando, FL. Former New York Hospital Patient Ombudsman and volunteer for various health care causes. She is survived by her children: Michael of Bloominburg, N.Y., Christopher of Natural Bridge, VA. and Carol of New York; and by her former husband Dr. Fred Plum also of New York. Contributions in Jean's memory may be made to a favorite charity. A private memorial service will be held at a later date.
