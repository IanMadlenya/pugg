
        DOWNING - Lawrence J. Oceanside, NY, Sept. 23, 1999 after a short illness. Retired New York City Police Department. Decorated Police Officer with 27 years of service assigned to the 79 Precinct and 69th Precinct in Brooklyn & the 101st Precinct in Far Rockaway. Mr. Downing was a devoted family man and is survived by his loving wife of 48 years June Downing. Cherished father of Larry Jr. and his wife Ruth Miller and their daughters Lucy and Sally, Gloria Orlando Downing and her daughter Gina, Timothy and his wife Carolyn and their children Madeline Rose, Shannon & Tim Austin, Terrence and his wife Tracey and their children Cameron, Terrence John (TJ), and Brian. Dear brother of Richard, John and William. Family will receive friends Sunday 2-5, 7-9PM at the Moore Funeral Home, 54 West Jamaica Ave, Valley Stream. Mass of Christian Burial, Monday 9:45 AM at St Christophers RC Church, Baldwin. Interment Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton.
