
        BRADEN-Lachlan P. The Board & staff of the Heckscher Museum of Art mourn with profound sadness the passing of our former trustee, friend, and supporter, Lachlan P. Braden. A gifted painter, her gentle wisdom will be deeply missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband, Bill, her children and family. Catherine A. Jansen, Chairman, Board of Trustees John E. Coraor, Ph.D., Director
