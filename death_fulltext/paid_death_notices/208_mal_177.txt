
        NEWMAN--Walter. Passed away at his home in Briarcliff Manor, NY, on May 15 at the age of 86. Formerly of Bronxville, NY, and Lake Placid, NY, Dr. Newman was a graduate of Columbia College ('39) and P&S Medical School ('43M). Conducted research through the 40's and 50's as the Chief of Cardiology at the Kings County VA Hospital, co-authoring numerous articles on the diagnosis and treatment of cardiac disease. In the mid-50's he established a private practice in Yonkers, NY, and was appointed Chief of Cardiology at Yonkers General Hospital. Member of The Harvey Society, Alpha Omega Alpha, New York Academy of Sciences and a Diplomat of the American College of Cardiology. Dr. Newman was precedeased by his beloved wife, Elizabeth Curley, and is survived by his children, Walter, Jr., of Boston, MA, Michael of Pleasantville, NY, Joseph of Salem, MA, William of Saxton's River, VT, Richard of Brooklyn, NY, Sara Klett of Guilford, CT, and Carolyn Malmstrom of Milton, MA, as well as 10 grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. Visitation will be Monday, 12 noon-1pm, at Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY, where his funeral service will be held at 1 pm. Interment will follow at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery.
