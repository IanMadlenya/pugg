
        FRIEDLAND-Ira L. Died December 27, 1999 at JFK Medical Center in Edison, New Jersey. He was 72 years old. At the start of his career, Mr. Friedland owned Diamond Management, a real estate management company in Edison, and AMI Construction, a building & development firm in Middlesex County. He then merged Diamond Management with Amherst Management in Edison. Mr. Friedland served in the Navy during World War II and was a member of the New Jersey Builders Association of New Jersey. Born in Irvington, he lived in Linden & Edison before moving to Metuchen, New Jersey ten years ago. Surviving are his wife, Sandra; a son, Ken; a daughter, Barbara Trimarchi; a stepdaughter, Cathy Kobrin; a brother, Martin Friedland; and four grandchildren. Donations may be made in Mr. Friedland's memory to Temple Neve Shalom, Rabbi's Discretionary Fund, 250 Grove Street, Metuchen, NJ 08840.
