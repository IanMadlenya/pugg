
        BATZAR-Gertrude. Beloved wife of the late Louis Batzar. Devoted mother of Lyn Boland Dosik and the late Howard Batzar. Loving grandmother to Diana Dosik and David and Elayna Batzar. Sister of Doris Saltzberg. Services were held at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, on Sunday, April 27, 1997. Her energy and passion filled our lives and she will be deeply missed.
