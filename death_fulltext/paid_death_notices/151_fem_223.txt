
        FRANCIS-Felice Harriman. On August 23, 1999, at her home in New York City. Devoted mother of George Hobson and Andre' Bishop and bemused but loving grandmother of Katharine Peach Bishop-Manning. Service will be in the Chapel of St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Ave and 71st St on Friday, August 27 at 11 A.M. Burial will be on Vinal Haven island in Maine. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cathedral in Paris or Lincoln Center Theater.
        FRANCIS-Felice. The Juilliard School wishes to extend our condolences to the family of Felice Francis, who was an enthusiastic friend of the Juilliard community. Her generous support of Juilliard's playwrights through the Le Compte du Nuoy Foundation had a profoundly positive influence throughout the drama program. Her passing is deeply mourned and her presence will be truly missed. Michael Kahn, Marsha Norman and Christopher Durang
        FRANCIS-Felice. The Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are saddened and convey their condolences to Andre Bishop, Artistic Director of the theater and his brother George Hobson on the death of their mother on August 23rd. Linda LeRoy Janklow, Chairman John S. Chalsty, President Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
