
        FINLAY - Simonne (nee Perrier). Died on 12/31/96. Born in Paris 4/8/10. Predeceased by her husband Edward A. Finlay. Dear sister of Paulette Boudet. Loving mother of Edward Finlay of Bloomfield, NJ, Daniel Finlay of Ithaca, NY, Jacques Finlay of Pike, NJ, and Maureen Jaimes of Scarsdale, NY. Also survived by ten grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
