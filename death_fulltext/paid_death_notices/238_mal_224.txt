
        GROSS--Feliks. Professor. Passed away peacefully at his home in Manhattan on November 9, 2006, at age 100. He was the devoted husband of the late Priva, beloved father of Eva Gross Friedman, and her husband Sheldon, and a loving uncle. He was cherished by his friends, colleagues, students, and family. His humanity, wisdom, optimism, wit and genial nature will forever be remembered. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to ''The Feliks Gross Endowment Fund'', CUNY Academy for the Humanities and Sciences, The Graduate School, 365 Fifth Avenue at 34th Street, New York, NY 10016, or ''The Professor Feliks Gross Distinguished Lecture Series'' at The Polish Institute of Arts & Sciences of America (PIASA) at 208 East 30th St., New York, NY 10016.
