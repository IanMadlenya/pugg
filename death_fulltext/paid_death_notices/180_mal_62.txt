
        ROHMER-Leo. The Three Arrows Cooperative Society mourns the death of Leo Rohmer, longtime active member.         ROHMER-Leonard. The Board and staff of Westchester Jewish Community Services mourn the loss of Leonard Rohmer, who shepherded our agency as its Executive Director for nearly 25 years. Leo's compassion and commitment to those we serve helped shape our mission; his vision was the cornerstone of what WJCS has become. The staff, Board and people we serve are grateful for his many contributions. We extend our condolences to his beloved wife Mildred, children and grandchildren. Board and Staff Members WJCS
