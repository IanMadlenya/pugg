
        RABSTENEK--Tom. Passed away on Friday, May 13 at home in the arms of his loving wife, Diane, after a long and courageous battle with bladder cancer. Cherished husband, devoted and adoring father of Christine and Thomas, beloved son of Tom Sr. and loved by many. Tom touched many lives in the community in many ways and was a founding President of the Manhattan Soccer Club. His energy and spirit will be profoundly missed. Memorial Services on June 1 at St. Paul's Chapel of Columbia University--Reception at 1:15 PM, service at 2 PM. The family asks that donations in Tom's memory be made to the Tom Rabstenek Memorial Bench Fund to dedicate a bench in his honor to Riverside Park, a magical place for Tom that gave him so many joyful and cherished memories. Checks payable to: Riverside Park Fund, *Memo: Tom Rabstenek Memorial Bench Fund; Mail to: Riverside Park Fund, 475 Riverside Drive/Suite 455, NY, NY, 10115; or the MSC Tom Rabstenek Scholarship Fund in honor of Tom's dedication to sportsmanship and to giving every player the opportunity to play, checks payable to: Manhattan Soccer Club, *Memo: MSC Tom Rabstenek Scholarship Fund; mail to: MSC C/O Alan Harley, 333 West End Avenue, Apt 7A, NY, NY, 10024.
