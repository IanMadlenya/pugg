
        KAPLAN-Harry, in his 100th year passed away on June 14, 1997 in North Broward Medical Center, Pompano Beach, Florida. He was the beloved husband of Belle (Jacoby) for 28 years and of the late Betty (Rosen) for 45 years. He is survived by his daughter Mimi (Aaron) Klein of Margate, FL.; his son David of Federal Way, WA.; grandson Ross Klein of Long Island, granddaughter Pamela (David) Levine and great-grandsons Daniel and Michael Levine. He is also survived by his sister Deborah Meyer and niece Susan of Los Angeles, sister-in-law Doris Rosen (Charles) Margett, step-daughter Irene (Leonard) Klaver and stepgrandchildren Ellen Klaver, James and Sharon Klaver and son Max Brown, Dr. Marjorie (John) Warden and daughter Emma Maxinne. He practiced accounting from 1924 to 1987, receiving his CPA certificate in 1928. He also graduated from New York Law School in 1929 and was admitted to the New York Bar in 1930. The family will see visitors and friends at the ''Boulevard-Riverside'' Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island, on Wednesday, June 18 from 7:30 to 9:30 PM. Private graveside services will be held on Thursday, June 19 at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, New York.
        KAPLAN-Harry. Beloved and devoted husband for twenty-six years. He was a gentle gentleman. He will be missed by the Klaver and Warden families. Delle Jacoby Kaplan
