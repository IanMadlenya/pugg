
        MAGALANER -- Marvin. A leading scholar of James Joyce and Virginia Woolf, a retired Professor Emeritus of English of the Graduate School and the University Center of CCNY and adjunct professor at NYU, died Saturday morning at the age of 83. Inspiring teacher, loving and devoted husband, father, and friend. He is survived by his wife Brenda, children Seth (Karie Youngdahl), Jillian (Andy Stone), Darcy (Kevin Maher), and grandchildren Ben, Clare, Eliza, and Adam. A funeral service will be held at ''The Riverside,'' at 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, on Tuesday, May 18, at 9:45 AM.
