Loving father of Robert Ivry and Patricia, ; Judith Ivry and Phillip Block, William Ivry and Joan Gallini, Richard Ivry and Ann Lacey, Devoted grandfather of Elizabeth, Greg, Seth, Asa, Anina, Henry, and Sam. Dear brother of Lillian Libertoff, Professor Emeritus of Insurance and Finance at both the Universities of Connecticut and Hartford.
