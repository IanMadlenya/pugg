
        SINEK-Herbert. On January 29, 1998, at age 73 at his home in Chappaqua after a long illness. Survived with enduring love by his wife Marion, his children Nicole Arnaboldi and Ronald Sinek, their spouses Leo and Jennifer, and his grandchildren Peter, Brenton and Andrew. The family will receive friends at home on Saturday and Sunday from 2-5 P.M. Contributions in his memory may be made to either the donor's favorite charity or Hospice of Northern Westchester and Putnam.
