
        KISSEL-Lester. In his 97th year. On April 21, 2000. Retired partner of the Law Firm of Seward & Kissel, LLP. Graduated from the University of Wisconsin in 1925. Attended the University of Geneva, Switzerland in 1926 and 1927. Graduated from Harvard Law School in 1931. Mr. Kissel is survived by his wife Alice Fordyce MacRae Kissel, his son Stephen and a sister Elizabeth Kissel Schultz. A memorial service will be announced at a later date.
