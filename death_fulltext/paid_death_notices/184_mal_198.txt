
        GRABOWSKI-Benjamin. On April 30, 2002, former New York Times employee Benjamin Grabowski passed away at his home at 5036 Abagail Drive in Springhill, Florida. Mr. Grabowski is survived by his wife Ann of 41 years and his two step sons Sal and Michael. Mr. Grabowski was employed in the production advertising area. He would have been 82 on his next birthday.
