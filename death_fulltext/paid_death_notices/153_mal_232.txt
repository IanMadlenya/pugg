
        KOVENETSKY-Sam. The officers, members and staff of the Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union mourn the loss of retired RWDSU vice president, Sam Kovenetsky. Sam was a devoted trade unionist and great leader whose lifelong dedication to working men and women and their families improved the quality of life for generations of members. Our sympathy goes out to his wife, Betty, and his family. Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union UFCW, AFL-CIO, CLC Stuart Appelbaum, President Charlie Hall, Sr., Secretary-Treasurer Lenore Miller, President Emeritus
