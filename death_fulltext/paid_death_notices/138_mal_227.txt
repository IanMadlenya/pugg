
        BRICHTA-Paul. Of Great Neck, N.Y. on July 21, 1998. Beloved and devoted husband of Hannah, loving and proud father and friend of Elaine Marks and her husband Aaron, and Lisa and her financee Jonathan, brother-in-law of Steven and Lois Glassman and uncle of Laura and Michael. He will always be loved and greatly missed. Funeral Service Thursday, July 23, 11:30AM at ''Temple Beth-El'' of Great Neck, 5 Old Mill Road. Contributions may be made the Division of Cardiology-North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset.
        BRICHTA-Paul. We are deeply saddened by the passing of a dear friend and loyal supporter. A founding member of the Cardiology Committee, Paul Brichta enthusiastically served as an ''ambassador of good will'', helping educate community groups about the model Cardiology Center at North Shore in Manhasset. His many friends in the Division will always remember Paul's warmth and optimisim combined with an intense interest in science. We extend our condolences to all the members of his loving family. Stanley Katz, MD Chief, Division of Cardiology and the members of the Division North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System
        BRICHTA-Paul. It is with profound sadness that we note the death of a friend who will remain in our hearts and thoughts. Paul Brichta was a rare individual whose kindness was felt by all. The Brichta family set an example of closeness that is hard to find. May their memories of the years together help this loving family cope in this difficult time. Edie and Stanley Katz
        BRICHTA-Paul. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its long-time member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
