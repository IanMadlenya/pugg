
        BARSOUM--B. Elias. Age 87. Beloved husband of Lillian. Devoted father of Marlene, Bruno and Caroline. Loving father-in-law of Margaret. Cherished grandfather of Agatha. Dear brother of Jean. A mentor to many, he was respected and appreciated for his wisdom, grace, generosity and sense of humour. He was dearly loved and will be deeply missed by his family and friends. He will live on in our hearts.
