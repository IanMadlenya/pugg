
        BLOS-Peter. The staff and Board of Trustees of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services are deeply saddened by the death of Peter Blos, Ph.D. Dr. Blos served as clinical consultant and teacher to generations of professionals at JBFCS. He was an extraordinary teacher and theoretician on the psychological development of adolescents and on the clinical treatment of troubled children, youth, and families. For the last twenty-six years, the Martha K. Selig Educational Institute of JBFCS has sponsored the Peter Blos Biennial Lecture, which JBFCS looks forward to continuing in lasting memory of Dr. Blos. Seymour R. Askin, Jr., President Joseph S. Kaplan President-Elect Fredric W. Yerman Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President
        BLOS-Peter. The Executive Committee, faculty, staff and candidates of the Columbia University Center for Psychoanalytic Training and Research mourn the passing of Peter Blos, a pioneer in the psychoanalytic study of adolescence who, as a founding member of our Child and Adolescent Training Program, gave generously of his great wisdom to the candidates and faculty of the Center. Roger A. MacKinnon, M.D., Director
        BLOS-Peter. The officers and members of The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Institute express their profound sorrow at the death of their respected and beloved colleague and member Dr. Blos. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his wife, Betsy, and family. Donald Cohen, M.D., Pres./Society Edward Hartmann, M.D., Pres/Inst
