
        SCHLOWSKY - FISCHER -Mark H. age 29 of Hoboken. On Sunday, October 17, 2004 at his home in Hoboken, he ended his battle with Burkitt's lymphoma, which he fought with unwavering optimism and courage. Mark was born and raised in Ridgewood, graduated (gra da ted) Columbia School of Engineering Class of 1997 where he was a member of the Marching Band and Ski Team. Beloved husband of Karen J. (nee Fischer). Son of George and Elizabeth Schlowsky of Ridgewood. Brother of Dave R. of Fair Lawn. Grandson of Hannah Schlowsky of Coram, NY.
