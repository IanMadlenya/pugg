
        LEVY-Bernard Howard, 69, formerly of New York City and Egremont, MA. Died on May 29, 2002. He was the brother of Alan Leonard Levy, the father of Wendy Levy, Debra Camal and Jessica Fauci and the grandfather of Tommy, Michael and Jenna Camal and Julianna Fauci. Services will be on Friday, May 31, 11:30 AM at I.J. Morris in Hempstead, NY.
