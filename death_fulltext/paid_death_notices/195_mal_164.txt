
        RANZER-Ranny. Of Boca Raton, FL. Loving husband of Sylvia and the late Ethel Ranzer. Died on April 14. He is survived by his sons Mark and David, daughters-in-law Judy and Joanne, grandchildren Cori, Karen, Ian, Alan and Beth Ranzer. Ranny spent his career in the garment center where he was a partner in Parade Dress Co. and Ranelle Fashions.
