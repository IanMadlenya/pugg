
        BINNS-W. Gordon Jr. 72, of Richmond and New York, died after a brief illness April 4, 2002, at MCV Hospitals. He is survived by his wife of 30 years, Alberta Fry Binns; his daughters, Amanda Letitia Binns and Clarissa Fetrow of Seattle; his sister, Mary Katherine Binns Wallace. A funeral service will be held 11 AM, Tuesday, April 9, 2002, at First Presbyterian Church, 4602 Cary Street Rd, Richmond, VA. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Christian Children's Fund or MCV Foundation.
