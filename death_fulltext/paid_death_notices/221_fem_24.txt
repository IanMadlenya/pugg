
        CHAMBERLIN -- Carole Hecht. The Greater New York City Affiliate of the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation mourns the loss of Carole Hecht Chamberlin, our longtime Board member and Survivor Chair. She will always be remembered for her unwavering service, her extraordinary compassion for others and her generosity of spirit. Our hearts go out to her husband, C. Meade, and her sons, Alan and Adam. In lieu of flowers, the Chamberlin family asks that contributions be made to Komen Greater NYC, 341 West 38th Street, NY NY 10018. Services, Sunday, June 5, 11:15 AM, ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. We will miss her.
