
        SCHREIBER--Martin. 81, of Boynton Beach and formerly of North Bellmore, NY, passed away peacefully on January 28, 2005. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Loving father of Judith and Dr. Andrew, and Mrs. Gretchen Schreiber (and children Taylor and Austin). Cherished grandfather of Gabriel and Elana. Martin was an accomplished artist and adventurer whose creative spirit will be missed by all who loved him.
