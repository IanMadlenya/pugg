
        GORDON-David Alex. Age 83, died in New York City, on December 2, 1997. He is survived by his wife Lillian, son Robert, daughterin-law Sophie and grandchildren Rachel and Benjamin. During a long career as a journalist he worked for the Brooklyn Eagle, Stars and Stripes during World War II, the New York Daily News and several medical publications. He was among the group of courageous New York City newspapermen who lost their jobs when they refused to cooperate with government security investigations during the McCarthy era. A memorial service will be held Saturday, December 20, at 1:30 PM, at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 St, NYC.
