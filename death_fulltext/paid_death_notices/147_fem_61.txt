
        SIMS-Miriam Mottsman. On April 4, 1999. Beloved wife of 62 years of Seymour (Chuck). Devoted mother of Barbara, sister of the late Janet Zuckerman. Services Tuesday, April 6th, 1PM, Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, New York.
        SIMS-Miriam. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its longtime devoted member. Mimi was a past president of our Sisterhood, vice president of the temple, and served the National Federation of Temple Sisterhoods. She was the wife of our beloved past WRT president, Seymour Sims. We extend sympathy to her bereaved family. Marjorie Miller, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi Stephen Merkel, Cantor Kenneth M. Chasen, Asst. Rabbi
