
        KAUFMANN-Audrey Price Margolis. Formerly of Albany, NY. Died at the Hebrew Home for the Aged, Riverdale, NY on August 24, 2000. Predeceased by her husbands Matthew Margolis and Albert Kaufmann. Survived by her brother, Dr. William Price (Sydell) of Phoenix, her children, Douglas P. Margolis, Ph.D., Judith Katz (Herbert) of New York, and John Margolis, M.D. (Holly), of Waterville, ME and her granddaughters, Marah Ann Katz and Sarah Elizabeth Margolis. Graduate of Wellesley College, Class of 1935. A leader of many civic and Jewish organizations in Albany, she was especially proud to serve as President of Vanguard Volunteers for the Albany Symphony Orchestra, and as the first woman President of Congregation Beth Emeth, Albany. Services will be held at the Beth Emeth Cemetery, Loudonville, NY on Friday, August 25 at 3PM. Memorial contributions to the Albany Symphony Orchestra, 19 Clinton Ave., Albany, NY 12207 or a charity of one's choice. For information call Levine Memorial Chapel, 1-800-427-0280.
