
        CREAN--Thomas D. Born August 12, 1922, Waterbury, CT, died April 9, 2005. Married 56 years to Dorothy Barry. Served his country with the 249th Signal Company, which landed in France in July, 1944. Graduate of Fordham Law, Partner at Brown & Wood. Served as Chairman of Cancer Society of Long Island and Counsel to State Representative Lent. Survived by Dory Fitzgerald, Stephen and Peter, predeceased by son Gregory, and seven grandchildren. Wake Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Follett & Werner. Funeral Monday, 11 AM, Immaculate Conception, West Hampton Beach. Donations to East End Hospice, West Hampton Beach.
