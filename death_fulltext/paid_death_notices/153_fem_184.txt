
        GLASER-Helen. Passed away on Thursday, October 14th, 1999 in California. She is survived by two loving sisters, Elise Kapell and Claire Strauss. Helen held a Master's Degree in Language and Art & had an extensive art collection. She was a theater and ballet critic for several Palo Alto newspapers. Memorial services were held in San Francisco, California on Tuesday, October 19, 1999.
