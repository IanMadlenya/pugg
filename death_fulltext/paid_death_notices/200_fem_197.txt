
        FARREL--Sallie Gibson. Of North Branford, CT. Widow of Franklin Farrel III, died on September 17, 2003. Daughter of John Gibson Jr., and Primrose Dixon Gibson of Pittsburgh, PA, and Chester, Nova Scotia. She attended the Winchester School, Oldfields School and Sarah Lawrence College. Predeceased by her parents and her two brothers, John Gibson III, and David Gibson. She is survived by her three children Sallie F. Brown (Paul), Franklin (Toby) Farrel IV (Christine), and Lisa F. Totman (David), 10 grandchildren, and 15 greatgrandchildren.
