
        WAYNE-Loretta (nee Shaughnessy). On July 9, 2001. Retired social worker in public service for 50 years, first on Long Island, later New York Medical College, then Bellevue Hospital Psychiatric Department. She was a loving and devoted wife to her husband, Martin Wayne, M.D., who survives her. She is also survived by her sister, Helen Ullman. She was known as Sis to her large family. Funeral Mass Saturday, 9:45 at St. John of God Church, 84 Carlton Avenue, Central Islip, Long Island.
