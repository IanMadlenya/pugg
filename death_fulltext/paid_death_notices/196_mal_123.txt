
        RUBEN-Fred S. Of White Plains, N.Y. Formerly of Forest Hlls, N.Y. and Mannheim, Germany. Passed away May 11, 2003 at age 93. He served as President and Chairman of Simon Manges and Son, Inc. until his retirement. He was the devoted husband of Ilse, beloved father of Jane Guttman, cherished grandfather of Jennifer and Robert Schlau and Catherine and Christopher Guttman-McCabe, proud great grandfather of Steven and Alexa Schlau and Anna and Abigail Guttman - McCabe. Funeral services were held Monday, May 12th. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association or the Arthritis Foundation of New York.
