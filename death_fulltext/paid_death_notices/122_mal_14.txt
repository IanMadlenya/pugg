
        LINCER-Maxwell. On Feb. 27, 1997, after a brief illness. Devoted husband of the late Cecile & dear companion of Roslyn Newman. Beloved father of Richard of Short Hills, N.J. and Dr. Robert of Stamford, CT. Cherished father-in-law of Ellen and adoring grandfather of Amanda and David. He is also survived by his brother Jack of Phoenix, AZ. A graduate of the Cooper Union, he was the last surviving principal of the structural engineering firm, Leichtman & Lincer, PE, PC, which has been known most prominently in recent years for its work on the structural aspects of Broadway and OffBroadway theater productions and was cited by the New York Assocation of Consulting Engineers for its structural design work in connection with One Astor Plaza and the Minskoff Theatre & by the New York City Landmarks Preservation Commission for its work in connection with the restoration of Trinity Church. Services will be Sunday, March 2, 11:30AM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Boulevard (76 Road), Forest Hills, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Maxwell Lincer Prize Fund, C/O The Cooper Union, 30 Cooper Square, New York, New York 10003-7183.
