
        JENKINS--Marion Gun, 85, died January 13, in Baltimore. A magna cum laude Radcliffe graduate, she was a wartime consular charge d'affaires in China. A teacher in Thailand and Cape Cod, she received her master's degree from St. John's College. Her husband, Felix Jenkins, died in 1975. Surviving is daughter, Eugenie Jenkins and her husband Brian Holmes of Baltimore, sons Patrick and Nicholas of Cape Cod, granddaughter Lucy Holmes, and many dear friends. Memorial gifts may go to the Radcliffe Institute Fund, 10 Garden St., Cambridge, MA 02138.
