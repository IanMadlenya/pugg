
        LESNICK-John. Age 49. Family and friends mourn the loss of John Lesnick on March 1, 2003. John led a life filled with energy, love and a rare regard for the lives of others. He was an artist, traveler and an activist, member of the board of the former PWA Coalition. He is survived by his sister, Sr. Monica, O.S.B.M., brother Joseph and his wife Tracy, and their children Joey, Ian and WinterRose, and many friends. A memorial service will be held at the residence on March 15, 2 PM. Contributions may be made to Gods Love We Deliver, Visual Aids and GMHC.
