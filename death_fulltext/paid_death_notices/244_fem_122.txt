
        DETRE--Jean, died on May 5, 2007, in New York City, after a brief illness. Writer, world traveler, courageous spirit. Author of several books and articles, including A Most Extraordinary Pair: Mary Wollstonecraft and William Godwin, published by Doubleday, 1975. Member of PEN. Recipient of the Millay Colony award. Jean is survived by her sisters, Betty Kronsky and Ann Spruyt.
