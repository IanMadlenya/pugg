
        PARTIKIAN-Joyce S. At the age of 63 on May 12, 1997. A native of Massapequa, L.I. and resident of NYC and Massapequa. Friend and a Joy to many. Wife of Edwin, mother of David and Drew, twin sister of Joan. Celebration of Life Service at the Unitarian Church of All Souls, 1157 Lexington Avenue (at 80th street), NYC, on Thursday, May 22 at 7:00 P.M. Memorial contributions in the spirit of her giving can be made to the NYU Radiology Post Graduate Fund (NYU School of Medicine, 550 First Avenue, New York City 10016) or the American Heart Association.
