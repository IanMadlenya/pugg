
        WILDERS-Monsignor James Gabriel. August 25. Uncle of Gerald Wilders. Great uncle of Regan, Gabrielle, William and Kingsley. Friends may call on Wednesday, August 26th, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M.; Thursday, August 27, 2-5 PM at The Abbey, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Mass of the Holy Eucharist Thursday, August 27, 1998, 7:30 PM at the Church of St. Thomas More, 89 Street between Park & Madison. Mass of Christian burial Friday, August 28, 10 AM, St. Patrick's Cathedral, 5th Ave, NYC. He was a Chaplain to the Mary Manning Walsh Home, founded the Hospital Apostolate, Chaplain at New York Hospital and a Pastor at St. Thomas More Church.
