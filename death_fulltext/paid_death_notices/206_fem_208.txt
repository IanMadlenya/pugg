
        VOLKMAN--Sally Blaufeld. 86. The family of Sally Volkman sadly announces her death on March 19. Sally's spirit of friendship touched so many lives. Her beloved husband Rabbi Samuel Volkman survives her, as do her beloved daughters, Suzanne and Nancy, son-in-law Edward Skloot, and grandson Joseph, whom she adored. Funeral services will be March 23 at Danzansky-Goldberg, Rockville, MD, at 1 pm.
