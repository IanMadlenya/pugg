
        SPATZ-Theodore. Passed away on June 24, 2000 at his home in Ft. Lauderdale, Fla.Beloved husband of the late Beverly C. Spatz, adored father of Gail Spatz-Krischer and cherished father-in-law of Kenneth Krischer, MD. Brother of Dorothy Waldman, the late Leo Spatz and the late Bella Kahane. At the height of his 40 year career as a noted public servant of The State of New York he served as Counsel to State Comptroller Arthur Levitt. He was a respected legal scholar, a humanitarian, trusted friend and colleague to members of all political parties. Funeral services were held on Monday. Contributions may be made to Temple Sholom, 132 Southeast 11 Ave., Pompano Beach, Fl. 33060.
