
        TISCHLER--Elsie. On August 7. Beloved wife of Richard. Loving mother of Steven (Barbara) and Janet (Ed) O'Brien. Cherished grandmother of Ben and Daniel Tischler and Owen and Josh O'Brien. Devoted sister of the late Helen (Paul) Goodman. She was a generous, caring advocate for those in need, a committed teacher who valued reading as a key to democracy.
