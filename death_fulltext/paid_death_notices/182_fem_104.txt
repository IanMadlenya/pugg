        TANNOR-Norma. Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc, expresses its deep sorrow at the passing of Norma Tannor. Beloved wife of Solomon. Cherished mother of Erica, Ilene, Merrill, Robert and Evan. Loving grandmother of Amanda, Samantha, Bradley, Jaymie and Cole. A life member, generous benefactor & active leader in the Woodmere Chapter of Hadassah, Norma was devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her legacy will live on through her good name. She will be missed by all who knew her. Bonnie Lipton, National President
        TANNOR-Norma. The Officers, members and staff of New York Electrical Contractors Association express their condolences to their past President, Solomon Tannor, at the loss of his wife, Norma. She persevered through her illness with courage and dignity. She will be missed by her many friends in the Electrical Industry. Our deepest sympathy to the entire Tannor family.
        TANNOR-Norma. We will miss our dear friend. Heartfelt condolences to devoted husband Sol and all the loving children and grandchildren. Helene and Marvin Torker
