
        ZITO-Nancy De Marinis. Passed away at home in Warren, VT, on January 1, 2003, at the age of 94 years. She was born in New York City on August 15, 1908. Mrs. Zito graduated from Hunter College with a B.A. in languages in 1930, and, subsequently, an M.A. in French. She was a language teacher, and guidance counselor at Evander Childs High School until her retirement in 1968. She was the widow of Daniel Zito. Survivors are her devoted child, Lydia Fisher and her husband, Edward of Warren, Vermont and Nassau, Bahamas; loving grandchildren, EG and Stacey Fisher of New York City and Douglas Fisher of Warren, Vermont; and a brother, Victor De Marinis of Palm Beach, Florida. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice, 600 Granger Road, Barre, VT 05641.
