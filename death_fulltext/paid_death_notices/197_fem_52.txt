
        MOSCATO--Marion H. The officers, staff and members of the Alumni Federation of Columbia University are deeply saddened by the passing of its staunchest supporter and director, Marion, on June 4, 2003. Prior to her retirement in 1983, she was an associate director with the University's Development and Alumni Relations Department and the executive secretary of the Alumni Federation. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family and many friends. Viewing will be Sunday from 2-6pm at Halvey's Funeral Home, 24 Willow St, Beacon NY. 1-845-831-0380. The funeral service will be held 10AM on Monday at St John the Evanelist's Church, 31 Willow Street, also in Beacon, NY. Jessie Mygatt on behalf of Columbia University Alumni Federation >>AD#
