
        KLEIN--Mina. The Officers, Directors and Staff of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center express their heartfelt sympathy at the passing of Mina Klein, beloved wife of our Founding board member and President Elect, Irving Klein. Through the years, Mina was a devoted member of our Auxiliary and an active volunteer. She will long be remembered for her commitment to those we serve. We extend sincere condolences to Irv and the entire family. Lawrence Simon, Chairman Arthur Goldstein, President Herbert Friedman, Exec. V.P.
