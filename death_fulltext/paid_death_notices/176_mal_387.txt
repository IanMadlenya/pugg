
        McDERMOTT-Matthew T., 34. Vice President of Cantor Fitzgerald. Died September 11, 2001, in the World Trade Center disaster. He is survived by his beloved wife Susan and two daughters Kara and Kelly, and an expected child March, 2002; also his parents Jack & Jackie McDermott of Homosassa, FL, two brothers, John of New York City and Peter of Dublin, Ireland; two sisters Suzanne McDermott of Arlington, VA, and Margaret McDermott of Manchester, CT. A memorial service will take place at St. James Church in Basking Ridge, NJ, at 11 A.M., Tuesday, September 25. In liew of flowers please send donations to the Matthew T. McDermott Children's Education Fund, 71 Hillside Terrace, Wayne, NJ 07470.
