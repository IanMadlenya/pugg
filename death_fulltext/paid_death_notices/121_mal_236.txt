
        DYCKMAN-William T. The Board of Directors of Parsons Brinckerhoff Inc. mourns the loss of William T. Dyckman, our colleague and friend, who died February 14 at the age of 79. Bill Dyckman's career with Parsons Brinckerhoff spanned nearly 50 years. He served the company as partner, senior vice president, and chairman of the board, and directed the firm's efforts on numerous highway and transit projects. We extend our condolences to his wife, the former Ruth C. Duff, of Peekskill; his son, Warren Duff Dyckman of Peekskill; his daughter, Christine Dyckman Attardo of Ridgefield, Conn; and his six grandchildren. Board of Directors Parsons Brinckerhoff Inc.
