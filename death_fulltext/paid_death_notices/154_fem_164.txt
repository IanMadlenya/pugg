
        KEAGY-Ann Elizabeth. Died November 19 at Waveny Care Center, New Canaan, CT after a prolonged illness from Alzheimers. For 35 years, she headed the Fashion Design Department of the Parsons School of Design. Survivors are her husband, J. Rodman Keagy, daughter, Wendy Ann Keagy, both of New Canaan, and brother, George W. Hlavaty, of Mesa, AZ.
