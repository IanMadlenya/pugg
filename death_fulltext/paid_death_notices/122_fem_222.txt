
        LIFSEN-Mabel R. Wife of Albert A. Lifsen, passed away on Friday, March 21, 1997, at Heartland Rehabilitation Center in Boca Raton, Florida, after a long illness. She was born on May 17, 1914, in New York City, the daughter of Fannie and Isidore Rothman. She was a 1978 graduate of Iona College, having returned to college to fulfill a lifelong dream, after raising two daughters, Marjorie and Isadora. She was active in Hadassah for many years and worked diligently on behalf of the State of Israel. Interment was on Monday, March 24, at Sharon Gardens Cemetery in Valhalla, New York. She will be missed by family and friends.
