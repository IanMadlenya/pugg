
        HARRIS-Howard. The Professional Staff Congress is saddened to announce the premature death of Howard Harris on April 22. He was 58. Professon Harris taught social sciences at Bronx Community College and he was a national expert in human services. He was the coauthor of a textbook in the field''Human Services: Contemporary Issues and Trends''. The book, which is in its second edition, was published in 1998 (Allyn & Bacon). He was a 31-year member of the PSC. For many years, he served the Professional Staff Congress as chapter chair at Bronx Community College. His death is a great loss to his colleagues and his students. Professional Staff Congress/CUNY. Richard J. Boris, President.
