
        AMELAR -- Theresa Edel man, 107, born in Transylvania on May 14, 1887. Emigrated to America in 1905 and was the eldest of ten children. Married to Joseph in Chicago in 1917. They relocated to Manhattan and established Joseph Amelar Inc., Importers-Exporters of which she served as president 1960-1981. Cherished mother of Dr. Richard D. Amelar and his wife, Alice. Beloved grandmother of Jessica, Sarah and Susanna (Tim Lodge). Adored great-grandmother of Joseph Amelar Leinwand, and of Hannah and Sam Lodge. Services Sunday 9:30AM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st St. and Amsterdam Ave. Shiva this week at the Manhattan apartment of Dr. and Mrs. Amelar.
