
        GILBERT--Dr. Arthur C. of Boston formerly of Metropolitan Washington, DC entered into rest April 11, 2006. Devoted father of Pamela and Richard Remis of Weston and Randi and Joel Cutler of Boston. Cherished granddad of Jeffrey, Andrew and Kimberly Remis and Mallory and Samantha Cutler. From 196979 Founder and Board Member of Auto-Train Corp. He was the Chief Scientific Advisor of US Naval Operations from 1970-75, Assistant Secretary--US Navy Research & Development from 1988-90, visiting professor at the US Navy War College in Newport, RI. Late member of the Spitfire Society, UK and the Cosmos Club, Washington, DC. Services at Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, 1668 Beacon St., Brookline on Wednesday, April 12 at 9am. Interment in New York will be private. The family will be at the home of Pamela and Richard Remis beginning Saturday after sundown until 9pm, Sunday from 2-4 and 7-9pm and Monday from 1-3 and 6-8pm. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Prostate Cancer Research Fund, attn: Dr. Glenn Bubley, Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center-Development Office, 330 Brookline Ave, Boston, MA 02215.
