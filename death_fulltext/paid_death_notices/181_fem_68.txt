
        GOLDBERG-Rose. Wife of the late David J. Goldberg. Died February 2, 2002 in Miami Beach, FL. She was a founder of Mount Sinai Hospital, a Life Member of Amit Women, Hadassah, Technion and Jewish National Fund, Vice President of the Bialystoker Home for the Aged in New York and a Life Member of the Women's League for Cancer Research and the Cancer Life Line affiliated with Mount Sinai Hospital. She is survived by a son, Gary, of Suffern, N.Y., and three grandsons, Andrew, Mark and Justin. She is also survived by a sister, Shirley Elphand of Scarsdale, N.Y. Services were held on February 3rd in Miami Beach.
