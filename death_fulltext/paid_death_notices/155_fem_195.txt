
        ROSENHIRSCH - Blanche. The American Jewish Committee Women's Campaign Board is deeply saddened by the death of Blanche Rosenhirsch, a treasured friend of AJC, of the Jewish community and of all humanity. A dedicated leader and benefactor who devotedly served the Women's Campaign for over 30 years. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to her entire family. Ruth R. Goddard, Hon. Chairman Stephanie Saft, Chairman
