
        ROUBICEK-Beatrice Cole. Former model, actress and playwright died in her sleep Monday in Needham, MA. Mrs. Roubicek was 88 years old. Born in Winthrop, MA to British parents Mrs. Roubicek grew up in NYC and was educated at private schools in NY and Europe. She was married to the late Joseph Roubicek, originally of Prague. She is survived by her daughter, Karen Roubicek of Newton, MA and two granddaughters, Erica and Jordana. A private family service will be held.
