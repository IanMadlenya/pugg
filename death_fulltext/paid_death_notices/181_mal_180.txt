
        STEIN-Marcel, member NSPE, AHES, ASME, passed away in his 101st year. Born in Hungary, was Chief Engineer of textile plants, worked for the City of New York for 15 years and taught at Pace College for 12 years. He had pattents for textile processes, steam and lighting equipment. He is mourned by his son, daughter, grandson and their families, as well as many friends. Donations requested to Isabella Home, NY.
