
        NEWMAN--Howard A. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with sadness the passing of Howard A. ''Micky'' Newman, who together with his family built a legacy of unparalleled benevolence through their dedication to the arts, education, and the New York Jewish community. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Maria; to his children: Jeffrey, William, Cynthia, Jessica, and Jerome; and to his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
