
        GREENBERG-Harry, died peacefully at home on January 1. Beloved husband to Sylvia. Devoted father to Neil, Steven and Lucia. Loving grandfather of Jennifer, Judy and Jane. After 52 years of teaching the fashion trade at the Fashion Institute of Technology, he recently retired as a Professor Emeritus. He will be forever in our hearts. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Harry Greenberg Scholarship Fund at FIT. Funeral Services will be held Sunday, January 3, 1PM at the IJ Morris Inc. Funeral Home, 1895 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, New York. For further information call 1-800-624-8282.
