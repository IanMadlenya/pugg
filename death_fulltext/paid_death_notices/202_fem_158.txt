
        MAIDMAN--Richard. F.E.G.S. Health and Human Services System mourns the passing of Dr. Richard Maidman, beloved brother of Sandra Cahn, esteemed Member of the F.E.G.S. We offer Sandra and her family our heartfelt condolences. Joseph Stein Jr., Pres. & Chair Alfred P. Miller, CEO
