
        ANDREASIAN--Edgar Diran. 80, of Great Neck, NY, died at home on May 19, 2006. Born and raised in New York City, he is survived by his former wife Zepure Andreasian, his daughter Marguerite White, sons Peter and Theodore, and granddaughters Arax and Amelia. Friends may visit at the Edward D. Jamie Jr. Funeral Chapel, 208-17 Northern Blvd., Bayside, NY, on Monday, between the hours of 3 to 5, and 7 to 9 PM. Funeral services will be held at the Holy Martyrs Armenian Church, 209-15 Horace Harding Blvd., Bayside, NY, on Tuesday, May 23, at 12 noon. His wit and wisdom will be sorely missed.
