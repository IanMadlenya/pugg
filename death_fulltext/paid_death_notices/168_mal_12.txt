
        KAMINSKY-Wallace. Architect. Collector. Life long and passionate New Yorker. A graduate of Brooklyn College and Columbia University School of Architecture, Wally practiced in Manhattan for forty years. He specialized in projects for educational institutions and for organizations serving low income, mentally challenged and homeless people. Loving father of Michael, Alex and Jenifer. Proud grandfather of Allison, Zack and Calla. Long time partner of Susan Lushing. Caring brother of Muriel (Miki) Crespi and Hank. Passed away on December 28. A memorial service will be arranged at a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to an arts organization of your choice.
