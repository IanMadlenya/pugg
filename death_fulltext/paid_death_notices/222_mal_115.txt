
        EPSTEIN--Jack, on July 11, 2005. Beloved uncle of Susan Tofel and George Hecht. Widower of Ruth Gutman, sculptor. Born in Pikesville, MD. A graduate of the Friends School in Baltimore and of Babson College in 1929. Jack resided in New York City for more than 50 years. He headed the New York Metropolitan Section of Parents Magazine. A veteran of World War II, he was a member of the Navy League, the Board of Trustees of The Elder Craftsmen, The Lambs Club, Artists - Equity and Temple Emanuel. He served as president of the B'nai B'rith Advertising Lodge and as Vice President of the Babson College New York Alumni Association, Long Island Advertising Club, New York Advertising Club, New York Advertising and Communication Club and the Philo Music Society. He directed the advertising and marketing curriculum of the Advertising Club of New York and was a big brother with the Jewish Board of Guardians. A funeral service will be held at Frank E. Campbell--The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue on Friday, July 15, at 10:30 AM.
