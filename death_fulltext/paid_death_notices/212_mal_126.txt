
        EBB--Fred. The Dramatists Guild Council mourns the death of legendary lyricist Fred Ebb. With partner John Kander, Mr. Ebb helped to shape the modern American musical. And even as his witty and searing lyrics continue to impact audiences around the world, we, his colleagues, begin to sadly realize that there will be no more words. Our deepest sympathies to his loved ones at this difficult time. John Weidman, President The Dramatists Guild of America
        EBB--Fred. We join the theatre community in mourning the loss of an extraordinary man and a brilliant talent, who blessed us with Chicago. With condolences to his family and to John Kander, Martin Richards Sam Crothers The Producer Circle Co.
