
        DeBLASIO -- Michael. St. Francis College of Brooklyn mourns the loss of Michael DeBlasio, a graduate of the class of 1958, a beloved Trustee, and a major benefactor. Michael will be remembered for his true Franciscan spirit demonstrated by his generosity in helping young people who would not otherwise be able to afford a college education, always leading and serving with humility and remarkable grace. We express our deepest condolences to Kathleen and their family. Thomas J. Volpe, Chairman of the Board of Trustees Dr. Frank J. Macchiarola, President
