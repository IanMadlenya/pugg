
        ALTSHULER-Shirley. On March 22nd, 2000. Beloved daughter of Lewis and Ethel Gangel. Adoring sister of Melvin (Buddy) Gangel. Devoted widow of Mortimer J. Altshuler, Sr. Loving mother to Stuart. Caring aunt to Ilene and Ann. Ardent supporter of NCJW. In lieu of flowers donations to NCJW are appreciated. Services Sunday 11 AM, Frank E. Campbell, New York City.
        ALTSHULER-Shirley, NCJW New York section is saddened by the passing of a devoted board member whose gentle ways brought beauty to all her efforts. Our sincere sympathy to her loving son. Jean Krosner, President Helen Caplin Heller, Exec. Dir.
