
        ROTHENBERG-Dr. Robert E., MD. Age 93, on April 10, 2002, after a short illness. A lifelong resident of NYC, a noted surgeon & author of medical books for the layman, Lt. Col. Retired U.S. Army, graduate of Cornell Univ., 1929, and Cornell Medical College, 1932, former Chief of Surgery at Brooklyn Jewish Hospital and French Hospital, retired 1998 as Director of Surgical Research at Cabrini Medical Center. Survived by his wife, Florence Rothenberg, son Robert P. Rothenberg, daughter Lynn Rothenberg Kay, and grandsons Andrew (Ann) Kay & Steven Kay. Funeral private. Memorial service will be announced.
