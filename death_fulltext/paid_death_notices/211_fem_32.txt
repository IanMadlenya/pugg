
        WOUDHUYSEN--Sara G. Her family in Holland and her friends in Canada and New York, are saddened to announce the death of Sara Woudhuysen, on August 3, 2004. Sara was 97 and, until her blindness nine years ago, was an active resident of Manhattan's Upper West Side, a concert goer, and a volunteer for many years at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. Sara had been a resident of The Guild Home for the Aged Blind since last December.
