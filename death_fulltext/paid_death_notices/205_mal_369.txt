
        SMALL--Irwin, 94, of Keene, NH, formerly of New Rochelle, NY, died Monday, February 23, 2004, after a brief illness. Mr. Small operated his own accounting firm in New York City for more than six decades. He was the beloved husband of Rebecca, father of Margaret Zanger and Roberta Small Risch and cherished grandfather of five and great-grandfather of six. Contributions in Mr. Small's memory may be made to the National Alzheimer's Foundation, Inc., or the Parkinson's Disease Foundation.
