
        MENKES--William B., 90. On November 21 in North Miami Beach, Fla. Survived by his beloved wife Olga; devoted children Dr. Alan Menkes, Marjorie (Michael) Rockman, Michael Menkes, Andrew (Cynthia) Menkes and the late Rachel Menkes. Adored grandfather and great-grandfather; loving eldest brother and brother-inlaw; and beloved Uncle Billy. We will love you and miss you always.
