
        EINSIDLER-Robert. In Pelham, NH, on March 21. Husband of the late Nellie J. (Weeks) Einsidler. Father of nine. Retired Safety Officer, Children's Hospital, Boston. Retired Chief Petty Officer, U.S. Navy for 26 years. WW II veteran. Memorial service today at First Congregational Church of Pelham at 11 AM. Contributions in his memory to Jimmy Fund, c/o Dana Farber Cancer Institute, 44 Binney St., Boston, MA 02115. For further info, call Pelham Funeral Home.
