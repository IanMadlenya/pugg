
        PARIS--Viviane. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Viviane Paris, beloved wife of the late Ira Paris. Her generous commitment to the New York Jewish community has strengthened our ability to help us care for millions around the world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her daughter, Nicole, and Douglas Rubens; to her grandchildren, Alexa, Julia, and Valerie; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O
