
        FUCHSBERG - Abraham. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Abraham Fuchsberg, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation who earned enormous respect for his efforts on behalf of Israel and the Jewish people. His contributions to the Jewish community will be felt for generations to come. We mourn the loss of a dear friend, and extend our deepest sympathies to Mr. Fuchsberg's wife, Shirley; to their daughters, Irene Minkoff and Rita Fuchsberg; and to the entire Fuchsberg family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        FUCHSBERG - Abraham. The Board of Directors of The New York State Trial Lawyers Association is saddened by the passing of past President and editor of Trial Lawyers Quarterly for twenty years, Abraham Fuchsberg. From a distinguished family of trial lawyers, he brought honor to the profession. Jeffrey A. Lichtman, President
