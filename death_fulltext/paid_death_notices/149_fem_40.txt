
        GOODMAN-Sally Frances. 6/5/499/2/82. Sally studied classical ballet at Juilliard. At Tel Aviv University she learned to read and write Hebrew; graduated from Schiller College, Germany (Paris campus) Outstanding Student in French Literature; received a degree from the Sorbonne, Paris; an MS in Library Science from Queens College, NY. All who knew her can remember the joy she brought to them. We celebrate her life. David, Bunny and Tom Goodman
