
        TOLLETT-Clovis Yancey. On June 25, 2000 at age 86. Stockbroker and veteran of World War II. Captain in 101st Airborne, he participated in Normandy Invasion and Battle of the Bulge. Beloved husband of the late Gloria and loving father of Clovis Carey and Sharon Riley and grandfather of three. Dear brother of Eugene Tollett. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
