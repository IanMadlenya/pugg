
        HOLTZBERG-Mina. Mina Holtzberg, 89 years old died peacefully at her home in Stony Brook, NY, on September 21, 2001. Assistant professor at NYU School of Social Work and Downstate Medical School Family Medicine. Predeceased by husband Milton, who died in the Normandy Invasion and is buried there. Survived by her beloved family, daughter Judy Kaplan, son-in-law Ed Kaplan, grandsons Micah, David and Adam. Tenderly and lovingly cared for by Antra Bortkevica, Dace Medina, Nilce Gonzalez, Yun Kong. Period of mourning Sunday through Tuesday at daughters house. Donations Jewish Guild for the Blind, ACLU, Suffolk County Habitat for Humanity.
