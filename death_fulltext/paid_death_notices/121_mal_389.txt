
        BUNIN-Morey, Feb. 26, at home in Woodstock. Master puppeteer; creator of ''Foodini the Great''; inventor of Aniforms, a live cartoon process. His work is in the Museum of Radio and Television. He brought joy to millions, and was deeply loved and will be missed. Survived by his wife, Charlotte; children David, Erica, Reada; grandchildren Alex, Jennifer, Emily, Michael, Damian. Memorial March 2, 4 P.M., at the home of David and Rachel Bunin, 9 Poplar Street, Dumont, NJ.
