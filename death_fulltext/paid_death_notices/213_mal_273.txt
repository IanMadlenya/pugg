
        RICHMAN--Robert M., M.D. October 23, 2004. Husband of Jeanne. Father of Amy Donnelly, Paul Richman, M.D., Jonathan Richman, M.D., and Jory Richman, M.D. Brother of Edward Richman, Stephen Richman and Nancy Gilbert. Grandfather of fourteen. He practiced orthopedic surgery in New York for more than 50 years. We will miss this man of great warmth and dignity for his devotion to family, friends and profession. Memorial service to be announced at a later date.
