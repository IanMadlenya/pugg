
        ROSENFIELD-Maurice ''Reese'' Ullman. Of Roslyn NY on August 12 at age 91. He is survived by his sons Robert & Jay, grandchildren Adam, Amanda, Daniel and Paul. His wife of 60 years, Harriet had died June 26, 1996. For the last five years, he lived with his devoted, significant other, Georgette Spingarn of Manhasset, New York. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, August 19 at 10:30 AM at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, Great Neck, New York.
