
        SCHOENFELD--Martin S. Died August 27, 2005 at age 92 in Tamarac, FL. Formerly a resident (and Mayor of) Hewlett Harbor, L.I. He is survived by his wife of 65 years, Lorraine, his daughter, Carol S. Wasserman, his son Robert, and 4 grandchildren. He was a benefactor of the Florida Grand Opera and an active participant in civic affairs. Memorial contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Foundation.
