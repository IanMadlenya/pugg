
        ROTH--Babette, widow of Henry B. Roth passed away in Montecito, CA May 22, 2003. Mrs. Roth was born in Brooklyn, NY April 17, 1913. She was the daughter of Fredrick and Rosa (Block) Langdon. She lived in NYC, Mamaroneck, NY, La Quinta and Los Angeles, CA. She then moved to Montecito, CA. For many years she was secretary to the Casting Director of MGM. She is mourned and missed by niece, nephews and friends. >>AD#
