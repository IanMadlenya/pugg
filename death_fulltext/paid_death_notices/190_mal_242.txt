
        HERBERT-Victor D., M.D., J.D. Passed away peacefully on November 19th at his home. He was deeply loved by his five children: Bob, Steve, Kathy, Alissa & Laura, and his four grandchildren, Nicole, Jessica, Andrew and Daniel. He was greatly admired and respected by the worldwide medical community for his selfless devotion to the advancement of medical and scientific research. He was an honored war veteran of each war from World War II through Desert Storm. He was a unique and wonderful man and we will miss him dearly. Rest peacefully, Dad, we love you. Kathy, Bob and Steve
