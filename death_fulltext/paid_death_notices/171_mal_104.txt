
        BRENNER-Seymour Dr. December 15, 1925-April 1, 2001. Beloved husband of Phyllis, father of Helene & Marc, grandfather of Jason, Ethan, Ari, Maxmilian, Emily and brother of Pauline. Great friend, humanitarian and healer.
        BRENNER-Seymour, M.D. Died Sunday after a prolonged illness. He shared our lives from childhood to senior years. Always caring, sympathetic and generous of his medical expertise. He had been striving to find a cure for cancer. We will cherish the memory of his sweet nature and loving kindness. To Phyllis, Helene and Marc and his five grandchildren, our deepest condolences. Ruth and Herb Heffler
