
        CAINE--Ada (nee Curley). Died on March 12. Ada was the devoted wife of the late Martin G. Caine, and beloved mother of Robin Colner and Jonathan. Cherished sister of Laura Francis. Loving Nanna of Stacy and Michael Caine, and Michael and Jennifer Colner. Caring mother-in-law of Alan Colner and Linda Caine. Ada will be remembered for her warm laughter, sense of style and deep affection for family and friends who could always count on her for generous support and guidance. We will all miss her deeply. Contributions can be made in her memory to Smart Kids with Learning Disabilities, Wesport, CT.
