
        ROEDER--George A. Jr. Retired Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Chase Manhattan Bank, March 28 at the age of 85. He joined the Chase Manhattan Bank in 1947, and was named Vice Chairman in 1969, a position in which he served until his retirement in 1982. He and his late wife, Anne Connor, were married for 54 years. A long time resident of Madison, NJ, he leaves a sister, Virginia, of Sebring, FL, a son Russell of Mechanicsburg, PA, daughter Jane of New York City, and three grandsons, Matthew, Andrew and Evan. Burroughs, Kohr & Dangler Funeral Home, 106 Main Street, Madison, NJ.
