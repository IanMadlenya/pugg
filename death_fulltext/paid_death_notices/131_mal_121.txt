
        AUSTIN-Brigadier General Frederick Victor, Jr. Yale, 1928. C.O. 5th Engineers, U.S. Army. Headed Budget Division American Electric Power System. Tenor soloist Yale Glee Club. Survived by his beloved wife Odette Jung Austin, two daughters Alix Kendall, Ghislaine Belcak, one daughter-inlaw Wilhamina Austin, and three grandchildren. Son Roger deceased 1994. Services Wednesday, December 10th, St. Matthew's Church, Bedford, New York. Contributions may be sent in lieu of flowers to Yale University or St. Matthew's Church.
