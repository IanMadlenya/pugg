
        EHRLICH--Carole Madeleine (nee Wiener). Wife of the late Dr. Milton M. Sister of the late Richard B. Wiener. Mother of Joel & Priscilla Ehrlich, Alex & Marilyn Ehrlich, David & Lisa Ehrlich & Nancy & Robert Novack. Loving grandmother of Jeremy, Molly, Jeffrey, Daniel, Benjamin & Rachel. Beloved aunt of Kathy & Neil Wiener. Mrs. Ehrlich passed away at her daughter's home at age 88, after a brief illness. A life member of Hadassah. She will be greatly missed by her many friends. Contributions in her memory should be directed to Congregation B'nai Israel, Sanctuary Fund, Basking Ridge, NJ. Services Monday, 1:30 pm, ''Wien & Wien,'' 402 Park St, Hackensack, NJ.
