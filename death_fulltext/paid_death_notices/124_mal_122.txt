
        BEARD-Anson McCook, age 88. After a long illness, died peacefully at his home on Long Island on May 10. Beloved husband of the late Roseanne Beard, he is survived by three sons, Anson McCook, Jr., of Greenwich, CT, Peter Hill of Montauk, NY, Samuel Sherwood of New Castle, DE, and seven grandchildren. Funeral services are private.
