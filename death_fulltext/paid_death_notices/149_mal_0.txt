
        BARNETT-James Allen, age 90. On May 26, 1999. Quietly at home in Portland, Oregon. He was retired from a long career as a marketing and advertising executive. He married Jane Dodge of Philadelphia with whom he had two daughters, Fayal B. Greene of New York and Lucy B. Campbell of London, England. He subsequently married Harriet Brownell Pope of San Francisco and had four stepchildren, George A., Peter T., and Guy B. Pope, and Val Pope Hanna. Private memorial service at a later date. Donations to The Nature Conservancy, P.O. Box 165, Sun Valley, ID 83353.
