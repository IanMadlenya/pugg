
        CARLIN-Nathan. On October 29. Beloved husband of the late Kitty. Devoted father of Fred Carlin and Abby and Howard Rosenblum. Cherished grandfather of Marni and Julia. Brother of Frank. He was a proud graduate of St. John's University (BBA 1935), a CPA and a World War II Army veteran. Service Wednesday 10:00 AM, Parkside, 2576 Flatbush Avenue (near Avenue V), Brooklyn, New York.
