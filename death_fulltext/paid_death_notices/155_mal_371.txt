
        LYDEN-Helen V.. Of Southbury, wife of the late J. Kenneth Lyden died December 27, 1999. Mother of two sons and their wives, John K. and RoseMarie of Armonk, New York, Richard A. & Cecelia of Camp Hill, Pa., grandmother, great grandmother and aunt. Wake services on Dec. 29, 1999 from 3 to 8 PM at the Southbury Funeral Home of Munson-Lovetere, 235 Main Street No. Funeral service Dec. 30, 1999 at 11 AM at the United Church of Christ Main Street No., Southbury. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Pomperaug Woods Endowment Fund, 80 Heritage Rd, Southbury, CT 06488.
        LYDEN-Helen V. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the passing of Helen, mother of our esteemed member and past board member John K. Lyden. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to John, his wife, Rosemarie, and their children, Berry, Tracey, Kevin and Hillary. Robert E. Morrow, President Harvey L. Radler, Secretary
