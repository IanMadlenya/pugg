
        YORKOWITZ-Isaac. On October 24, 1997. Interment was held on October 26. Beloved companion of Rita Wernick and dearly loved by all of her family. Warm, caring and a good friend to so many. Loving brother of Geri Singerman and Millie York. A wonderful uncle to all of his nieces and nephews. Everyone who knew him will miss him. Remembrances may be sent to Temple Beth Abraham, Tarrytown, New York 10591. Family sitting shiva at Singerman home in NYC through October 30.
