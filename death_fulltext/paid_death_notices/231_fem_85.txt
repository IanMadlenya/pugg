
        PARLES--Gertrude. Passed peacefully at the age of 96 on April 12, 2006. Devoted wife of the late Morris. Loving mother of Stephen, Tani and Richard. Cherished grandmother of Jamie and Karen, Craig and Lisa, Peter and Anne, Jill and Christopher. Adoring great-grandmother of Christopher, Casey, Andrew, Jeffrey, Jacob, Ian and Gabrielle. She lived a long and peaceful life filled with love, kindness, appreciation and caring. She will be missed by all.
