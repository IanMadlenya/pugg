
        MANN--Sol, 77, died on June 1st, 2004 in Queens, NY. Beloved husband of Pam (deceased) and Ethel (deceased). Loving father of Dennis and Joy Mann (Queens, NY), David Mann (Queens, NY), Donald Mann (Brooklyn, NY), Andrew Mann (Jacksonville, FL), Douglas Mann and Jane Schweitzer (Falmouth, MA). Loving grandfather of Hannah, Emily, Benjamin and Maxwell. Sol served as a drummer in the Navy Band in the Pacific Fleet during WWII. A graduate of Columbia University he taught music to thousands and finished his teaching career as a school principal in Brooklyn. Services at Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Mount Pleasant, NY at 11AM, June 3rd, 2004.
