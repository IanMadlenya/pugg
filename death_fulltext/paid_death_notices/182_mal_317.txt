
        SCHLECHTER-Arthur D. Died quietly at home after a short illness at the age of 88. Survived by his loving brother Herbert & sisterin-law Selma, beloved companion to Karola Sallan and dear friend to her son Dr. Stephen Sallan and his wife Darlene and children, devoted uncle of Bruce and Susan and Kenneth and Marcia, great-uncle of Aaron, Alan, Annie, Benjamin and Michael. Arthur was a lawyer, served in W.W. II as a Staff Sergeant in the European Theatre and for 50 years was in the real estate ownership and management business with his brother Herbert. Funeral services will be private at Beth Olom Cemetery in Queens. The family will welcome friends at the home of Herbert Schlechter Sunday from 3-6 P.M. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        SCHLECHTER-Arthur. The Officers and the Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of our dear friend and fellow member Arthur Schlechter. He was a member for seventyfive years and a past President. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to members of his family. Paul J. Konigsberg, President
