
        RUSSELL-Mary Ferguson. Died peacefully in Essex, CT, on September 29, 2001. Beloved wife of Tuck for 55 years. Mother of Tom, Jennifer and Sarah. Born in Schenectady in 1914, was brought up in Hartford and graduated from Miss Porter's School in Farmington. She worked for the Office of War Information in the Middle East during WWII and later for the United Nations Association. Then she became a partner in Frederics Helton Travel. She lived in New York City for many years and served on the Board of the James Weldon Johnson Community Center and the Isaacs Center. She was also a resident of Fishers Island where she was an active participant in the creation of the H.L. Ferguson Museum. Funeral service was held on Fishers Island, October 1. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory may be made to the H.L. Ferguson Museum, Fishers Island, NY 06390.
