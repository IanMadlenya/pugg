
        CHRISTIE--Sr. Mary Joannes, R.S.M. Of the Sisters of Mercy, died August 23, 2006. Born to Mary Reusse and John Christie in New York City, Sister Joannes is survived by her loving sister, Sr. Mary Etheldreda, R.S.M., nieces, grandnieces and grandnephews. Dr. Christie, R.S.M. was the longtime Chair of the English Department at Mercy College, Dobbs Ferry, NY. Around Shakespeare's birthday an annual celebration of the Christie Lecture Series takes place. Funeral Mass 10 A.M. Saturday, Marion Woods, Hartsdale. Visiting Friday from 3 P.M. at Marion Woods. Memorials to Sisters of Mercy Retirement Fund, 150 Ridge Road, Hartsdale 10530.
