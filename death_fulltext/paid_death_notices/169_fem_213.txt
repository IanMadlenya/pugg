
        AVRUTIS-Theresa Gwyn Kellar. At home, on February 18, 2001. Widow of Newton. A graduate of Morris H.S. 1930 and NYU 1934. She practiced Clinical Psychology for over 45 years. Daughter of the late Louis Goldberg and Anna Freund. Sister of the late C.M. and I. Harold Kellar. She is survived by her daughter Andrea, her nephews Raymond, Alan and Jeffrey, her grandnephew Michael and her sister-in-law Charlotte. Services were held Thursday, February 22nd. Memorial contributions to Museum of Jewish Heritage, Battery Park City, New York.
