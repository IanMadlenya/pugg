
        HERTZBERG -- Lilian. May 26, 2004. Dr. Diane E. Meier, Director of the Lilian and Benjamin Hertzberg Palliative Care Institute, Dr. Albert L. Siu, Ellen and Howard C. Katz Chairman's Chair of Brookdale Department of Geriatrics and Adult Development Mount Sinai School of Medicine, members of the Advisory Board of the Hertzberg Institute, and the entire faculty and staff of the Institute who knew and admired her, mourn the loss of our friend, Lilian Hertzberg. A woman of extraordinary intelligence, culture, wide ranging interests and detemination, Lilian Hertzberg was a visionary benefactor who recognized the need for medical care focused on relief of suffering and support for best quality of life for patients with serious illness and their families by generously naming and endowing, with her late husband, Benjamin Hertzberg, the Lilian and Benjamin Hertzberg Palliative Care Institute. We will miss her. Funeral arrangements private. Donations in her memory may be made to Lilian and Benjamin Hertzberg Palliative Care Institute, Mount Sinai Medical Center, Box 1070, 1 Gustave L. Levy Place, NY, NY 10029.
