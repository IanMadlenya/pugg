
        HOCHBERG--Mitchell J., 85. Died peacefully on January 10th, 2006, in Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of Rockville Centre, NY, and Delray Beach, FL. Loving father of Marion Hochberg; cherished brother and brother-in-law of Ruth and Larry Pickard; very proud uncle of Marc (Jeanne) Pickard and Dr. Jeffrey (Connie) Pickard; great-uncle to Kyle, Kevin and Kasey. Dear friend of Phyllis Hochreich. Predeceased by his beloved wife, Peggy (1978). Services were held in Florida.
