
        RHODES-Cecil. April 2, 2003. Cherished husband of Betty. Loving father of Dion and Lisa O'Brien, and father in law of Patrick O'Brien. Adoring Papa of Julia and Dylan. Given the love for his dogs donations may be sent to North Shore Animal League or Humane Society of NY. He will be missed by all those whose lives he touched. Friends may call at Frank E.
