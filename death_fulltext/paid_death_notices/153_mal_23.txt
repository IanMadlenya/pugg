
        KAHN-Arthur E.., DDS. On September 29, 1999. Beloved husband of Anita. Loving father of Roberta and Karen. Adoring grandfather of Levin and Matthew. Dr. Kahn was an internationally acclaimed dentist. He lectured worldwide and mentored study groups in America and Israel. He treated many prominent figures in the arts and entertainment world. He made significant contributions to the literature in the field of restorative dentistry. Dr. Kahn received his B.S. from the University of Maryland and was a graduate of N.Y.U. College of Dentistry. He had held appointments on the faculty of Boston University and the University of Pennsylvania. Dr. Kahn was a longtime collector of 20th century art. Examples from his collection have been exhibited in museums throughout the world. There will be private graveside service on Monday, October 4th. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
