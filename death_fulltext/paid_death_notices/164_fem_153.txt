
        WALKER-Helene Sullivan, 97, died August 30 at her summer home Ashfield, MA. Wife of the late Norman S. Walker of New York City and Ashfield, she was born in Paris and grew up in Northern Italy. Amony many accomplishments, she was food editor of Flair Magazine and helped establish the American Museum at Bath, England. Survivors include four sons, ten grandchildren and seven great grandchildren.
