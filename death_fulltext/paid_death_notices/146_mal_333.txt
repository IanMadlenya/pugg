
        JOELSON-George J., C.L.U. Age 75. President and Co-Founder of Prestige Planning Corporation of 90 John Street, New York City died suddenly Monday, March 15, 1999 at his home in Ocean, New Jersey. He also resided in New York City. An Agent of Equitable Life Assurance for over fifty years, he was also a Lifetime Member of the Million Dollar Round Table. Born in Paterson, New Jersey, to Dr. Morris S. Joelson & Rae Aronsohn Joelson, he attended Blair Academy and was a graduate of Brown University. During WWII, he served with distinction in the U.S. Navy on the USS Whiteside in the Pacific Theater. He is a member and former Trustee of Hollywood Golf Club, Deal, N.J. & a member of the Board of Camp Oakhurst. Survived by his sister Norma J. Hayman of Rumson, N.J. and nine nieces and nephews and nine great nieces & nephews and two sisters-in-law. He is predeceased by two brothers, Robert and Benjamin. Funeral services will be held at Schoem Menorah Chapel, Route 4, Paramus, New Jersey on Thursday, March 18 at 11:00 A.M.
        JOELSON-George J. The officers of Prestige Planning & Rosen & Company mourn the passing of George Joelson whose lifetime devotion will be sorely missed by his family, friends and clients. He contributed to the lives of many with his talents and humor. Our deepest sympathies to his family. He will be missed. Arnold M. Kaplan Robert D. Wolf Lawrence Kirshner David Rosen Stephen Rosen
        JOELSON-George J. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Mr. George J. Joelson. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Bernard D. Karasic, Pres. Charles Weissman, Sec'y
