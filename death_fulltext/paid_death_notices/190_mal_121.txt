
        POLITZER-Dr. Alan A. Age 92, died November 8th, 2002 of complications of Alzheimer's disease. He is survived by his wife Valerie of 63 years, his sons Peter and David, and his grandchildren Benjamin, Rebecca, Adam and Noah. He was a practicing physician and surgeon for 47 years. He was active throughout his life in support of the State of Israel. He was a lifetime member of B'nai B'rith. Donations may be made to the Jewish National Fund for trees in Israel.
