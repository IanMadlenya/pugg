
        KLEIN-Ruth Porter. A lifelong teacher who designed pioneering courses in reading for both remedial and advanced students, died on October 27, 2001 at the age of 88 at her home in Sarasota, Florida. Ms. Klein taught at Hewlett High School in Long Island, New York, from 1954 through 1973, and was named a New York State Teacher of the Year. She was also an artist, and her portrait of Supreme Court Justice Oliver Wendell Holmes hangs in the Brooklyn Law School. She was married for 48 years to Solomon Asa Klein, a lawyer and professor of law. She is survived by her daughters, Susan Klein of Bainbridge Island, Washington and Laura Feldbaum of Bethesda, Maryland, and by two grandchildren, Harley Feldbaum and Mia Feldbaum.
