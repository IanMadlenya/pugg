
        GRIFFIN-Gerald G. President Cozzi and the New York City Technical College/CUNY community mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague, hospitality educator and provost, Gerry Griffin. We will sorely miss his wit, integrity and professionalism, and extend heartfelt condolences to his loving wife, Charlene, his children and grandchildren. There will be a memorial service on Sunday September 20th at 2 PM at the Peddie Memorial Baptist Church, 572 Broad St. (corner of Fulton) Newark,NJ, (973) 642-2552.
