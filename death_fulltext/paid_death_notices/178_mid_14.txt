
        BRAUN-Lillian. On November 27, 2001. Beloved wife of 60 years of Joseph. Loving mother of Laurence and his wife Ariela, and Shelley and his wife Pamela. Devoted grandmother of Andrew and his wife Rabbi Laurie, Carolyn and her husband Daniel, Jeffrey and Randi. Dear sister of Samuel Noble. A 1932 graduate of Hunter College with a B.A. in French and Education, she was a Junior Phi Beta Kappa. She had an endless love of learning, language, art, living American artists and design. Services today, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th and Amsterdam Ave. She will be deeply missed.
