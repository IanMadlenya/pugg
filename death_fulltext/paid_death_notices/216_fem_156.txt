
        MESSITE--Jacqueline. The New York Academy of Medicine records with sorrow the death of Dr. Jacqueline Messite, longtime Secretary of its Committee on Public Health. A careful, committed and visionary public health professional, she worked with intelligence and industry to translate the deliberations of the Committee into effective programs. Jack D. Barchas, M.D. Chairman Board of Trustees Jeremiah A. Barondess, M.D. President
