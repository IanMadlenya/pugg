
        MERCADIER--Huguette. Age 89. Of New York City. Died November 30, 2004. Daughter of Colonel Maurice and Suzanne Le Duc Mercadier. She was born in Nice, France June 29, 1915. She was educated at Miss Todd Hunter's classes NYC and graduated from Le College D'Hulst Paris. She came to New York in 1939 and had a long career as a Social Directress for the Cunard Line and later, at the N.Y. Catholic Center as an executive assistant. She is survived by two nieces, four grand-nephews and a grandniece. Memorial Mass to be held December 9th, 1PM at Saint John's Church, 55th Street and 1st Avenue.
