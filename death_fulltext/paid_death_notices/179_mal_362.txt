
        GROSSBERG-Jack. Died on Friday, December 28, 2001, in Santa Monica, CA. Beloved father of Michael and his wife Efrat, ex-husband of Anita and brother of Mimi Arnold. He was a member of the Producers Guild of America, Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, Directors Guild of America for over 40 years. Some of his credits include ''On the Waterfront'' with Marlon Brando, ''Bananas and Sleeper'' with Woody Allen, ''The Producers'' with Zero Mostel, ''The Hospital'' and ''They Might Be Giants'' with George C. Scott, ''King Kong'' for Dino De Laurentis, ''Back to the Future'', among others. Graveside service at Sharon Gardens Cemetery in Valhalla, NY, Wednesday, January 2, 2002, at 1 PM.
