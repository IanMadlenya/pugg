
        JACOBS--Joannie Patterson, wife of Harry A. Jacobs, Jr, the former chairman and CEO of Prudential-Bache Securities, Inc., died on February 20 at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in New York City. Mr. and Mrs. Jacobs were married in 1997 and resided in Sun Valley, Idaho, Irvington, NY and Lake Placid, NY She was born on September 22, 1925 in Omaha, NE, the daughter of Arthur and Helen Scribner. She attended Mills College in Oakland, California prior to settling in Sun Valley. Mrs. Jacobs married James Arthur Patterson, civil engineer, who predeceased her. They raised five children together in Sun Valley. Joannie was a successful and respected real estate agent with Capik and Company in Sun Valley for many years; was instrumental in the growth of the Sun Valley Ski Education Foundation which continues to assist emerging ski racers; and enjoyed a passion for her family and friends through her interests in ice skating, skiing, tennis and duplicate bridge. In addition to Harry A. Jacobs, Jr., Mrs. Jacobs is survived by five children: sons Matt, Ruff and Pete Patterson and his wife Kim, daughters Susie Patterson and Barbie Patterson Kallerud, son-in-law Thor Kallerud, grandchildren Bjorn and Tori Kallerud, stepson Terry Jacobs, stepdaughter Nancy Haneman and their families. Memorial services will be scheduled at a later date in Sun Valley. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to Dr. Mark Kris, c/o Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
