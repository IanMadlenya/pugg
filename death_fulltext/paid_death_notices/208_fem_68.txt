
        THOMAS--Helen T., 98, died April 28. Pianist, composer, teacher. Student of Percy Grainger and Carl Friedberg at Juilliard School. 1939 NY debut at Townhall. Wrote three composition books for the young pianist and co-authored a two volume technique book. Recognized for her outstanding contributions to music by Music Educators Association of NJ. Helen T. Thomas Memorial Fund, established at Douglass College to promote chamber music experiences. Memorial concert planned. For details contact daughter, Helen, htwilliams@aol.com
