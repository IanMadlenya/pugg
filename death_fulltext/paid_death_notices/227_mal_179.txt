
        DILLON--Robert J., 61, died peacefully at home on December 16 after a short illness. Beloved husband of Phyllis and father of Alexander, Ph.D. Anthropology, Columbia University, 1976. Fund-raising executive for non-profits, most recently, Edison Schools, Inc. A lover of classical music, he played the clarinet. He was generous and open-hearted, and had a life-long passion for social progress and liberal causes. Service on Monday December 19, 11:30am, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave.
