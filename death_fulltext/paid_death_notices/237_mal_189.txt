
        PACKER--Stephen, 77, Rye, New York. Survived by wife Alice of 50 years, sons Robert (Camilla Rab) of New York City and Richard of Austin, Texas, granddaughters Zara and Zeba, and sister Julie Mazo of Savannah, Georgia. Retired Chief Economist of the Mobil Oil Corporation, he studied economics at Columbia and the University of Chicago and after retirement taught at St. Peters College. Funeral services: on Friday, October 20, 12:30 P.M., at Community Synagogue, Rye, New York.
        $(6$)PACKER--Stephen. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Stephen Packer, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Charles J. Block, President
