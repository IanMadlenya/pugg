
        DISHMAN-Arthur, M.D. On April 28, 2003. Associated with Lenox Hill, NYU, Beth Israel, Eye & Ear Infirmary and Bellevue for many years. Also served as Bloomingdale Stores Chief Physician in Manhattan. Beloved husband of Aida. Services Thursday 11AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65th St.
        DISHMAN-Arthur, M.D. The Board of Trustees, medical staff and the Department of Medicine of Lenox Hill Hospital express their profound sorrow at the loss of Arthur Dishman, M.D., a distinguished physician on our staff for over 48 years. Dr. Dishman was a respected physician who was dedicated to the highest standards of medicine. Lenox Hill Hospital honors the memory of this devoted physician and we extend our deepest sympathies to his family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO James J. Richter, M.D., Ph.D., President of Medical Board Robert A. Phillips, M.D., Ph.D., Chairman, Dept of Medicine
        DISHMAN-Arthur, M.D. The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary notes with sorrow the passing of a longtime member of the medical staff. An attending physician in the Department of Medicine since 1970, Dr. Dishman was a generous and loyal friend to the Infirmary. Our sincerest condolences to his wife Aida and the entire family. Peter Frelinghuysen, Chairman, Board of Directors Joseph P. Corcoran, President and CEO Navin C. Mehta, M.D., Chairman, Medical Board J.
