
        AUFHAUSER--Vivian. Died at her home in Palm Beach, FL on May 19, 2006 after a lengthy illness. Born October 15, 1940, she would have been 66 today. Ms. Aufhauser was a lifelong resident of New York City and, until recently, owned a summer home at Ocean Beach, Fire Island, as well as her winter home in Palm Beach. She was a graduate of The Chapin School and New York University and held a Master's Degree in Psychology from The New School for Social Research. After stints as an actress and then a research psychologist, Ms. Aufhauser joined Industrial Raw Materials, an industrial chemicals import/export company founded by her father, Alfred Aufhauser, in the 1930s and succeeded her father as President until the company was sold early this year. She was a tournamentwinning bridge player and a member of the Regency Whist Club. Ms. Aufhauser is survived by several first cousins and a circle of friends who will think of her fondly on her birthday. Donations in her memory may be made to St. Jude Children's Research Hospital 501 St. Jude Place Memphis, TN 38105 or The American Cancer Society, 19 West 56th St. New York NY 10019.
