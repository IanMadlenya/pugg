
        CALDWELL-Henry Bryan, born June 22, 1918, died August 9, 1999, formerly a director of the Ft. Worth Art Center and the Chrysler Museum in Norfolk, VA. He was the owner of the Museum Gallery Book Shop, a hobby he loved. Graduate of St. George's '36; Harvard '40 and NYU Graduate School of Fine Arts. Survivors include his wife Dona and four daughters and their families.
