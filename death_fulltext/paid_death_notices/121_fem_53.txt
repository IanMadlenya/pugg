
        AVERY-Virginia Kellogg. The Trustees, Auxiliary & Staff of the Brooklyn Botanic Garden mourn the passing of Virginia Avery, widow of Dr. George S. Avery, former Director of Brooklyn Botanic Garden. Virginia was a warm friend to countless people associated with the Garden. Contributions in her memory may be made to Brooklyn Botanic Garden, Brooklyn, New York 11225. Lois M. Carswell Judith D. Zuk Allan F. Kramer II
