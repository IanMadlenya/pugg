
        KARPEN--Marian J. President and founder of the nonprofit, NYC based WorkTalk, a career development group, died on October 22, 2005, at her home in NYC. Ms. Karpen is the former Paris Fashion Editor of Women's Wear Daily, an internationally syndicated newspaper columnist, author of ''Career Crossroads: Ideas & Inspiration for Your Work/Life Journey,'' former partner of Bear Stearns and a member of the Board of Directors of the Vassar Club. In September, 1996, she was named New Yorker of the Week by Channel New York 1 and has been the guest on area and national radio and television shows. A Funeral Mass will be offered in St. Jean Baptiste Catholic Church, Lexington Ave at 76th St., NYC, on Friday, November 4, at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Jean Baptiste Church, 184 East 76th Street., NYC, NY.
