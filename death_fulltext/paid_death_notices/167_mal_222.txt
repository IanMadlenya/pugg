
        BLANCHARD-Robert M. Born June 4, 1924, in Syracuse, NY; son of Isaac Monroe and Frances Snow. Robert graduated from The Taft School and from William College. He served in the United States Marine Corps in World War II. Robert worked on Wall Street as an Analyst for Henderson Harrison. His dream was to fly. Robert earned his Pilot's License and flew all over the country. He flew a Steerman Biplane and later a single engine ''V'' tailed Beach Craft. Robert wintered in Palm Beach, FL, where he passed away on December 16, 2000. He was predeceased by his wife Barbara Gaylord Blanchard. Robert is survived by his wife Maureen Blanchard. A memorial service will be held at a later date at The Royal Poinciana Chapel with Dr. Richard Cromie officiating. Those who wish to make contributions may send them to The Royal Poinciana Chapel, 60 Cocoanut Row, Palm Beach, Florida 33480.
