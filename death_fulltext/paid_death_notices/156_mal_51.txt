
        REESE-William Willis The Badminton Club of the City of New York expresses its sorrow at the death of William Reese, former President and supporter of the Badminton Club. We send our sincere sympathy to his widow, Sonia, his mother and his entire family. Frank Johnson, President Charles Crawford, Vice President Nicholas Cowley, Treasurer
        REESE-William W. Sons of the Revolution in the State of New York notes with sorrow the death of our valued member, William Reese. He was a devoted member of our Board of Managers and a past Chairman of the Stewards. Our heartfelt condolences to his family. Laurence S. Simpson, D.D.S., Pres.
        REESE-William W. The Veteran Corps of Artillery records with sorrow the passing of our friend and comrade Major William W. Reese on December 29, 1999. John E. Connelly, III MG, Commandant
        REESE-William Willis. The Board of Trustees of The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society note with sadness the death of their fellow Trustee and friend, a Benefactor of the Society. Walter Wilmerding, President
