
        BOIKO-Bernard., bridge life master died June 20th after a 10 year battle with prostate cancer in San Diego, Ca. He was 69. Boiko was a history teacher at Francis Lewis High School in Fresh Meadows, NY and was Chairman of the History Department at the Francis Parker School in San Diego. Boiko played against Chico and Harpo Marx at the Barclay Bridge Club in Flushing.
