
        BOGNER--P. Joseph. Passed away on Tuesday, October 31, 2006 at age 76. Beloved husband of the late Roseanne, and devoted father to Joe, John, Stephen, Chris, and Maura. Born in Chicago, IL, Joe graduated from Villanova before serving in the United States Navy during the Korean Conflict. He then moved his family to New York and embarked on a successful and rewarding career in newspaper advertising. Joe retired from Knight Ridder Newspaper in 1992 to enjoy a gentleman's life of leisure with Roseanne at the family home in Monmouth Beach, NJ. Funeral mass will be held at the Precious Blood Church in Monmouth Beach on Monday, November 6, 2006 at 11 AM. Interment will be at Roseanne's side at the New Jersey Veterans Memorial Cemetery in Arneytown. Information at www.sidun.com
