
        LYNCH-Charles F. Jr. The Board of Trustees of the Long Beach Medical Center records with profound sorrow the passing of our former colleague, Charles F. Lynch Jr. From 1961 to 1989, he served as a members and officer of the Board and helped the Medical Center grow and prosper. We will miss his wise counsel and sound guidance. Our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Maryanne and his entire family. Alfred Osterland, Chairman Martin F. Nester, Jr., CEO
