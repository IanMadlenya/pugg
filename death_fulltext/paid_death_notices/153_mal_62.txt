
        SMEDRESMAN-Max. The officers and leadership of UJA-Federation of N.Y. mourn the passing of Max Smedresman, devoted husband of Joan Smedresman; cherished father of Ruth Rosen, Peter, and Steven; and beloved grandfather. His compassion and concern for those less fortunate will live on in the generous gifts he created for their benefit through UJA-Federation of N.Y. and the Hebrew Immigrant Aid Society. Our sincere condolences to the entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        SMEDRESMAN-Max. NYU Medical Center mourns the loss of Max Smedresman, a generous friend and supporter with a deep commitment to Parkinson's Disease research in the Departments of Neurology and Rehabilitation Medicine. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Joan, and to his family. Robert M. Glickman, MD, Dean, NYU School of Medicine Theresa A. Bischoff, President, NYU Hospitals Center
