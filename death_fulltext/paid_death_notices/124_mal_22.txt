
        FEUER-Arthur, on May 2nd, 1997. Beloved son of the late Mollie and Harry Feuer. Graveside services on Sunday, 11 A.M., at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, New York.
        FEUER-Arthur. The officers, members and staff of The American Jewish Committee note with sadness the death of Arthur Feuer, a staff member of AJC's Resource Development Department since 1962 and Director of the Department from 1980 until his retirement in 1989. We send our deepest sympathy to his family. Robert S. Rifkind, President David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
