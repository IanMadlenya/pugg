
        BARNETT - Philip. 55, of White Plains, on June 29. Beloved son of Helen and the late Frank Barnett, husband for 28 years to Ruth, treasured father to Joseph, Andrew and Karen, and adored brother of Alan. Environmental engineer by trade, chef extraordinaire and golfer by passion. His brilliant mind allowed him, family and friends to enjoy life to its fullest. Donations: Philip Barnett Memorial Scholarship Fund, 1 Franklin Rd., Great Neck, NY 11024.
