
        GERSTEN-Stephanie Frangos. Mother, sister, mother-in-law, grandmother extraordinaire and friend passed on February 1, 2000 in the presence of angels galore. She spent her time here as an actress, photographer, poet, heroic single Mom and lover of life. Her enormous heart and lively spirit will be missed by all. She is survived by daughter Alexandra Gersten-Vassilaros, sister Chrysoula Frangos and grandchildren, Tonio and Luka. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday 2:00-5:00 and 7:00-9:00. Memorial service at All Souls Church, 1157 Lexington Avenue, Thursday, February 3, 2:00-4:00 PM.
