
        WHITE-Anne Macgregor on December 22, 1998 in Delray Beach, Florida, in her 94th year, the loving, caring wife of the late John Rigsby White, former president of Imperial Oil Limited and director of Exxon Corporation. Burial took place in her birthplace, London, Ontario. Survived by two daughters; Nancy Thoman of Toronto and Catherine Loubser of Amsterdam, Holland, and their families. Predeceased by a son, John Franklin White.
