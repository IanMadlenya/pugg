
        FERRER--Millie M, 93, died peacefully following a short illness at Mercy Hospital in Miami, Florida on October 14, 2006. Her loss is mourned and her memory cherished by those who knew and loved her, especially her devoted loyal friend of 54 years, Joaquina Hernandez, and her 13 nieces and nephews Ramon, Nicole, Ignacio, Ramona, William and Roberto G. de Mendoza, Marianne M. Munier, Millie M. Houck, Alfredo, Lourdes, Mercedes, Ana Maria and Leopoldo G. de Mendoza. Memorial services will be held at St. Joseph's Roman Catholic Church, 404 East 87th Street, NYC on Tuesday, December 12th at 5:30pm.
