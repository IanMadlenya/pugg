
        CONWAY - Hewitt A. Died suddenly on December 28, 1996, at his home in Palm Beach, FL. Devoted and adored husband of Jeanne for 41 years. Beloved father of Louis G. Conway and Irene H. Coleman, loving grandfather of Eleanor and Hewitt Coleman. Sone of the late Honorable Albert Conway, former Chief Judge of the New York State Court of Appeals, and Irene Hewitt Conway. Loving brother of Alberta (Mrs. Edwin M. Jones), Elaine (Mrs. Edward McLaughlin), and Mrs. Lois C. Crabill. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Friday, January 3rd, 5-8 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday 10 A.M. at St. Ignatius Loyola R.C. Church, Park Ave at 84th St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the National Aphasia Association, 156 5th Avenue, Ste. 701, NY NY 10010 or the Rehabilitation Center for Children and Adults, 350 Royal Palm Way, Palm Beach, FL. 33480.
        CONWAY - Hewitt A. The Partners and Employees of Donovan, Leisure, Newton & Irvine, join with the family of Hewitt A. Conway, in mourning the loss of our friend and colleague.         CONWAY - Hewitt A. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of Hewitt A. Conway, a member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family.  Victor D. Ziminsky, Jr, Pres.  Robert J. Reilly, Historian
