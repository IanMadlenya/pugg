
        MANDEL--Eva Ellis. Died August 28. Beloved wife, sister, mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother. Widow of Samuel Feingold and Milton Mandel. Survived by her loving children Marion Stein (husband Ronald), David, and Ellen (husband Michael Lydon). Also survived by devoted brother Kenneth Ellis and by dear grandchildren Sara, Naomi Najman, and Daphne Stein, and greatgrandchildren Tzvi, Ephraim, and Eliana Najman. A woman of valor. She always did everything she could for the many people she loved. Services to be held Sunday, August 29, at 9:30 AM, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, at 91 St.
