
        MELMAN-Israel J. 82, on July 7, 2002. He will be remembered for his good deeds. He was a giant among men. Of Tamarac, FL, previously of Lexington, MA, and New York. Loved his family. Leaves behind his wife Esther, of almost 59 years, his sons Dr. Martin Melman of Croton-on-Hudson, NY, Dr. Daniel Melman of Miami, FL, and Rabbi Baruch Melman of Newburgh, NY. He cherished his daughters-in-law Amy and Leah, and his grandchildren Anna, Jesse and Tifarah. He also leaves behind his sisters Marcelle Mandelbaum and Judith Herschberg. He was a pioneer in the development of radio, color television, infrared communications, computer informatics and the internet. He was mentor and advisor to many. He was a man of few wasted words, placing a premium on ethics, generosity and kindness. He will be remembered with love by all. Funeral services Tuesday, 12 noon, July 9, 2002, at the Weinstein Memorial Chapels, 1652 Central Park Ave, Yonkers, NY. Interment Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
