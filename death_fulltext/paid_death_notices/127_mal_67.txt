
        GARFINKEL-Charles, of Far Rockaway, NY, August 2, 1997. Age 78. Devoted husband of the late Allegra, cherished father of Nina J. Garfinkel of New York City, and Dr. Mark D. Garfinkel of Chicago. His unique combination of wisdom, wit, and compassion deeply inspired all who knew him. He is sorely missed by his loving family and friends. Donations may be made in his memory to the North American Conference on Ethiopian Jewry, 165 East 56th Street, New York, NY 10022.
