
        LOFFREDO--Rudolph, on March 12, 2007. Born in the Bronx on November 27, 1923. Married the former Catherine Florio in 1949 and shared devoted love until her death in 1995. Survived by his children Susan, Stephen, and Sandie and by his beloved grandchildren Gina, Benjamin, Eliza, Giuliette, and Graham. Rudy attended the City College of New York and, after graduating, served during World War II on the heavy cruiser U.S.S. Pittsburgh in the Battles of Iwo Jima and Okinawa. A civil engineer, Rudy spent his professional life at the Karl Koch Erecting Company, where he supervised the steel construction of the World Trade Center and the Jacob Javits Convention Center, and from which he retired as president in 1988. Rudy was a gentleman, the consummate professional, a cherished friend, and the backbone of his family to which he was thoroughly and completely devoted.
