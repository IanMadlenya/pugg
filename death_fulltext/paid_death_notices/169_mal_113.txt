
        STIEREN-Arthur. The Metropolitan Opera Association mourns the death of our good friend Arthur Stieren, a member of our official family for twenty years. His commitment to our company was invaluable, and we remember in particular his enthusiasm for the Wagner repertory. We extend our condolences to his wife Jane and his family. Mrs. Gilbert W. Humphrey, Chairman Emeritus Bruce Crawford, Honorary Chairman James W. Kinnear, Chairman Paul M. Montrone, President
