
        IMAI-Yoshi T. (Joe). 83, December 16, in Alhambra, CA. Formerly of NYC. Survived by wife Kazuko, daughters Ann and Susan, sister Midori and granddaughters Kristine and Akiko. A tireless worker for the welfare of all JapaneseAmericans, he was the Charter President of the Japanese American Lions Club, Treasurer of the Japanese American Association, an active member of JAHFA, organizer and financial advisor for the Nisei Investment Club. He received a medal from the Japanese government for his community service. He will be deeply missed by family and friends. Donations may be made to the Japanese American Association New York.
