
        CONROY-Ruth McClymont, 94. Of Lenox, MA, formerly of Rye Brook, NY and Southold, Long Island, died on March 30, 2003. The wife of the late Harold W. Conroy. She was the mother of Mrs. Roger V. Tabort of Pembroke, ME and William C. Conroy of Raleigh, NC. She is also survived by four grandchildren and four great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date and time to be announced. For information, contact the Wellington Funeral Home, 220 East St, Pittsfield, MA. 01201. Donations may be made to Hospice Care in the Berkshires, or The Church on the Hill, in care of the funeral home.
