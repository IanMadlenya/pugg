
        LATTIMER--John Kingsley, M.D., M.Sc. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague Dr. John Kingsley Lattimer. Emeritus professor and chairman of the Department of Urology at Columbia University Medical Center and urologist-in-chief at NewYorkPresbyterian Hospital/Co lumbia from 1955 to 1980. Dr. Lattimer was a greatly influential and inspiring leader who was deeply committed to the care of patients, education of students, and advancement of urology. His contributions to the treatment of renal tuberculosis improved the lives of numerous children and adults worldwide. Under his direction, the Squier Urological Clinic developed into one of the nations top urological programs. A graduate of Columbia College and Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons, Dr. Lattimer served as president of the American Urological Association and the International Society of Urology. He is the author of several books on urologic and historic topics. Brilliant and charming, he will be much missed. We offer our heartfelt condolences to the entire Lattimer family. Herbert Pardes, M.D. President and CEO NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital Lee Goldman, M.D. EVP and Dean Columbia University Medical Center $(6$)LATTIMER--John Kingsley, M.D. The Kidney and Urology Foundation mourns the passing of our esteemed friend.
