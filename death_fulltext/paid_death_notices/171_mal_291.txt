
        RESNICK-Samuel. Age 90, died April 20, 2001. Served in the U.S. Army in World War II. Samuel worked as a graphic artist in NYC. He lived in the Crestwood section of Yonkers for 31 years and also resided in Florida. Survived by his loving daughter June Moskovitz and her husband Don, three grandchildren and three great grandchildren. His loving companion Adele Perlow also survives. Graveside service will be held Monday at 11 AM at the Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-on Hudson, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St., Tuckahoe, NY 10707 or the charity of your choice.
