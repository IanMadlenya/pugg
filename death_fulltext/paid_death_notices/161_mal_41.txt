
        GOODY-Robert B. Professional engineer, 85, of Demarest, NJ, died on June 2, 2000. Loving husband of Helen. Beloved father of Barbara G. Katz (Joel Owen), Susan Rabbit Goody (John Oakley) and Nancy H. Goody (Gregory Bell). An electrical engineer, he loved boating, ham radio and motivating young inventors. He was past president of Chavurah Beth Shalom. He was employed at Federal Pacific Electric, the Palisades Interstate Park Commission and taught at Bergen Tech. He touched many lives and will be sorely missed. For information contact ''Wien & Wien,'' 1-800322-0533.
