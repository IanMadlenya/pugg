
        DOWELL-May M. On July 19, 1999, age 87, Retired Director of Special Projects Library, Columbia Broadcasting System. She witnessed and participated in the development of radio and later television news broadcasting from 1935 to 1977. Originally from Paterson, New Jersey, she resided in New York City since 1952. She was a member of the American Women in Radio and Television, Advertising Women of New York, and the special Libraries Association, and she was an active parishoner of St. George's Episcopal Church, where she will be interred. Survived and lovingly remembered by her niece, Wendy Moxcey of Bristol, Maine, nephews, Peter Dowell of Atlanta, Georgia and John Dowell of San Francisco, California, a grand niece, and four grand nephews.
