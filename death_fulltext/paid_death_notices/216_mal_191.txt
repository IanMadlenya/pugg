
        CROCKER--Arthur Masten. The Board and Staff of Environmental Advocates of New York mourn the passing of our friend and former Director, Arthur Crocker. A founding member of Environmental Advocates, Arthur will be remembered always as a visionary environmentalist and a passionate and effective advocate for the Adirondacks. Our heartfelt sympathies to his wife Barbara, his children Chester and Elizabeth and the family. We shall miss him. Oakes Ames, President EANY Board of Directors Robert J. Moore, Exec Dir
