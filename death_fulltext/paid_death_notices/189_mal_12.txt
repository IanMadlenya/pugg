
        STONE-Victor Lewis, died on Saturday, September 28, age 62. Beloved father of Sarah and Eric Stone and husband of 28 years to Pamela Stone. He was a graduate of the University of Florida, where he was captain of the tennis team, and went on to receive his Master's in Counseling. He had a thirty year career at Central Connecticut State University where he was the tennis coach and had the winningest record to date in Central Connecticut history. He was voted Coach of the Century by the Hartford Courant. Himself a nationally ranked world class tennis player, he traveled the world playing tournaments. He won the 1989 Maccabiah Games in Israel. He will be remembered for his tenacity, determination and competitiveness. A true individual, he touched the lives of all who knew him. He was a contender! The Memorial service will be held Thursday, October 3 at 11:30AM at Hirsch and Sons Funeral Home, 1225 Jerome Avenue, Bronx, New York.
