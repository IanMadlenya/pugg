
        O'BRIEN-James P. On January 16, 2001. Beloved husband of Barbara (nee DeSario). Loving father of Patrick, Timothy, Jimmy and Clare. Adoring grandfather of Kira, Lila, Finn, Sophie and James. Also survived by his mother, Aileen O'Brien and eight brothers and sisters. Dedicated Business Manager of the Cathedral School of the Cathedral of St. John the Divine in NYC. Visiting Thursday 25PM and 7-9PM at M.J. Smith Sons Funeral Home, 248 Prospect Park West, Bklyn. Mass of Christian burial Friday 10:15 A.M., at St. Francis Xavier R.C. Church.
        O'BRIEN-James P. The Cathedral School is saddened by the death of James O'Brien, its Business Manager for 14 years. His vital presence, his ever present good humor and his unwavering high standards for the school community will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to Barbara and his family.
