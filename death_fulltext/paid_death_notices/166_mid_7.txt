
        STEIN-Rose, nee Engel v. Janosi, Saturday, November 4. Survived by loving husband, Marcel Stein, daughter and son-in-law Anna Stein-Abdi and Nourredine Abdi, son and daughter-in-law, Gabor and Anna Szikla, grandson and his wife Stephane and Isabelle Szendy and great grandchildren, Olivia and Mathias Szendy. - Born in Pecs, Hungary, immigrated to the United States in 1958, finished her career as a librarian at Columbia University. Developed oral history for the Austro-Hungarian section at Columbia University Library. Memorial donations may be made: Hungarian Oral History Project at Columbia University.
