
        SPRINGER-Fred. The Board of Directors, Administration, Medical Board and Staff of Peninsula Hospital Center and Peninsula General Nursing Home mourn the passing of our esteemed member of the Board of Directors, Fred Springer. He served as Treasurer of the Board until his death, and was an extraordinary man who exemplified only the highest ideals through his leadership and community service. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his son, Jay Springer. Joel A. Miele, Sr., P.E. Chairman of the Board Robert V. Levine President and CEO Janet Miele-Powers Administrator, Nursing Home Leonard Schiffman, DMD President, Medical Board
        SPRINGER-Fred. Lifelong friend and mentor. We will truly miss you. Deanne and Joel Gerstel
