
        MALPICA--Henrick. 61, on July 28, 2004. Son of Blanca and Armando. Beloved husband to Martha. Devoted father and grandfather to Erik, Daniel, Gillian, Anicia, Stephen, Jonathan, Rachel, Catherine and Henrick Jr. Brother to Armando and a lifelong friend to Barbara Malpica. Henrick was a pioneer and Associate Clinical Professor in the field of ophthalmic photography for 39 years. His knowledge, kindness and hard work will be remembered by all. His legacy lives on through his family and friends as well as the lives he touched throughout his career. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, August 1st at 3PM at Cathedral of St. John the Divine, Amsterdam Avenue at 112th St. ''Cookie'' your family and friends all love you. You'll be missed forever.
