
        GETZLER-Max, 88, died March 1, 1999 in Florida, he was buried in LI, New York. Beloved husband of Sylvia V., father of Lynn Rosaz, father-in-law of Bernard, father of the late Herbert Getzler, grandfather of Andrew, Lawrence, Abigail Getzler, and of Valerie, Marc, Julie Rosaz, great grandfather of Jacob and Nathaniel and of Louis and Federik, uncle of Judith, Leonard and Sheila. He graduated from NYU as a CPA in 1937, retired age 78 from Security Pacific Bank, NYC. May his soul rest in peace. His Family Who Loves Him
