
        BECK-Clare Marshall. Of Whiting, Manchester Township, N.J. Formerly of Richmond Hill, N.Y. Died Monday at her home. She was 91. Born in Brooklyn. Lived in Richmond Hill, Queens before moving to Whiting in 1996. She was an executive secretary with Shields and Company, Wall Street, for 39 years, retiring in 1968. She was an avid golfer and bridge player, member of the Women of Wall Street and Forest Hills Women's Club, Forest Hills, N.Y. She is predeceased by her husband Frank in 1973. She is survived by one niece and two nephews. Visiting will be Thursday from 2 to 4. Funeral service Thursday, 3:30 PM at the funeral home. Burial Friday, 1 PM at the Evergreen Cemetery in Brooklyn. For further information contact Anderson & Campbell Funeral Home, Whiting, New Jersey, 732-350-5700.
