
        HANSEN-Frederick H. On Jan. 7, 1999. Loving father of Anthony Craig, Frederick James, Astar Elizabeth, & stepfather of Jeffrey. Adoring grandfather of Linnea, Lorielle, Sean & Evan David. Dear uncle of Peggy Anne. Historian, businessman, raconteur, cherished and lifelong friend to so many here in New York & around the world. Visiting Saturday 7-9 PM and Sunday 4-8 PM. Service Monday, at 9:30 A.M., at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to The American Cancer Society or Bide-A-Wee Animal Shelter.
