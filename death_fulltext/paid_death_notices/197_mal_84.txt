
        WIMER -- Rabbi Manfred. May 4, 2003 in Silver Spring, MD, age 89. Beloved husband of Marguerite (nee Bensinger) for 59 years. Loving father of Rose and Izzie Miller and Charles and Susan Wimer. Proud and adoring grandfather (Sabah) of Racheli, Ayeli and Dovi Miller and Eric Wimer. Survived by four loving brothers: Kurt Wimer, Henry Wimpfheimer, Dr. Ernest Wimer and Walter Wimer. Rabbi of Congregation Adas Israel, Rome, NY for 34 years. Always a smile on his face, a kind word for everyone and a love of learning. He led an exemplary life. May his memory serve as a guiding light to all who knew him. >>AD#
