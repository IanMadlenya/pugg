
        MARTIN-William McChesney, Jr. The Board of Directors of The New York Stock Exchange mourns the death of our former President, esteemed colleague and friend, William McChesney Martin, Jr. He served as the first full-time paid President of the Exchange from June 30, 1938 until April 16, 1941. He returned to conduct a study of the Exchange in 1971 where his insight and initiative led to changes in the governance of the Exchange. His dedication and commitment to the Exchange and the securities industry will endure. Richard A. Grasso, Chairman and C E O, New York Stock Exchange, Inc.
