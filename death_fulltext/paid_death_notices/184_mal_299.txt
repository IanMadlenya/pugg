
        RODDY-Joseph James. A former senior editor of Look magazine and staff writer at Life magazine, died on Thursday at the age of 81. A resident of Croton, NY, Mr. Roddy was born in St. Paul, MN, December 25, 1920. He attended The College of St. Thomas Aquinas. During World War II he served in the US Coast Guard. In 1948 he married Suzanne Eger and moved to New York, where he joined the staff of ''Look' magazine as a writer. In the 1960's he worked at Life magazine, returning to Look until it closed in 1970. He also wrote for the New Yorker, the New York Times Magazine and Harpers magazine. He leaves his wife, two sons, two daughters and eight grandchildren.
