
        BAMBERGER - Nina Elias. The Board of Trustees and management of Sesame Workshop notes with deep sadness the death of our beloved friend and colleague Nina Elias Bamberger. She passed away on November 20th. Nina served as a source of inspiration to all who had the privilege of calling her a friend. Her dynamic spirit, humor and kindness remain with all who knew her. As an Emmy Award-winning producer, Nina left a legacy of an incredible body of work characterized by a passion for making a difference in kids' lives. We mourn her loss but know a bright new star will light up the sky. Jeffrey N. Watanabe, Chairman Gary E. Knell, President and CEO
