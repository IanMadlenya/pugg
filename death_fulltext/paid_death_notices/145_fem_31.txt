
        JANSON-Dorothy W. on February 2, 1999 in Stamford, CT. She was predeceased by her loving husband of 45 years Helge Janson. Beloved mother of J. Fred, Hank A., Peter S., & Thomas H. Janson. Beloved sister of John Burgess. Also survived by 12 grandchildren and four great grandchildren. Born and raised in Manila, Dorothy and her family also lived in Sweden and Canada before settling in Greenwich, CT. in 1968. She will be missed by her family and many friends. Funeral services Saturday, February 6 at 3pm in the First Congregational Church of Old Greenwich. Friends may call at the Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, CT. Friday 4-8PM. Memorials to a charity of ones choice.
