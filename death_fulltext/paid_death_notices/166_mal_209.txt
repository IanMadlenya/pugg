
        KINGSLEY-Herman. In his 90th year, beloved and caring husband of Betty and the late Marion; loved father of Alfred (Temma), Stephen (Judith), and Robert (Robin); adored grandfather of Mark (Bongreat-grandfather of Jack; wonderful friend, beloved brother and uncle. Founder, Past President: Kew Gardens Anshe Shalom; and FDR Lodge, B'nai Brith; Wise Counselor. We will never forget him. Service: Sunday, 1 PM, Schwartz Bros., 76th Rd. and Queens Blvd, Forest Hills.
