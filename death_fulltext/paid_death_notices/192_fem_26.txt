
        BRAESTRUP - Elsebet Kampmann, 94. Peacefully on Jan. 2, 2003 at the Whitney Center, Hamden, CT. Beloved wife of the late Carl Bjorn Braestrup, physicist. Born December 18, 1908 in Copenhagen, Denmark. Predeceased by her son Peter, and survived by her daughter, Ellen Braestrup Strickler of NYC, grandchildren, Angelica Braestrup, Kate Braestrup, Carl-Peter Braestrup, Lise Strickler and Tom Strickler, and 10 great-grandchildren. Also survived by her brother Hack Kampmann of Sweden.
