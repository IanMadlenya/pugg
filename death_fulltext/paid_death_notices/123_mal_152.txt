
        LUTTINGER-Joaquin. The Physics Department of Columbia University notes with sorrow the passing of one of its long beloved members. Joaquin M. Luttinger, who taught at Columbia from 1960 to 1973, is remembered by his colleagues and students as a friendly presence, a source of wide knowledge and experience in his field, and a popular teacher with the highest intellectual standards. Quin will be deeply missed. A memorial service will be held at 1 PM on Tuesday, April 29 at St. Paul's Chapel on the Columbia campus.
