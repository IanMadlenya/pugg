
        BAKER-Sarah G.. Of Great Neck, NY. Died in her home on December 15, 1999, two weeks before her 95th birthday. Loving wife of the late Samuel A. Baker. Cherished mother of Deborah, of Manhattan, and the late Neil Baker. Adored sister, aunt, and beloved friend. She was an educator, president of National Council of Jewish Women & Hadassah President. Memorial Service Sunday February 6th. at Noon, Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck.
