
        ROSENFELD--Shirley Otter. The Jewish Theological Seminary community mourns the death of Shirley Otter Rosenfeld, a member of JTS's Board of Trustees. A generous, dedicated member of the Jewish community, Mrs. Rosenfeld will be dearly missed. May all her family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Ismar Schorsch, Chancellor Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
