
        THACHER-John Taber. Formerly of Garden City, New York, died on September 11, 1998 in Exeter, NH. Born on December 20th, 1912 in Syracuse, NY, he attended Loomis School and graduated from Yale University in 1934. Husband of the late Mary Louise (nee Van Schaick) and father of Carol (Mrs Robert Weekes of Richmond, NH) and Mimi (Mrs. Edward Tate of Rye Beach, NH), 7 grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. He held executive positions with the National Association of Manufacturers, Employers Labor Relations Information Committee, and C.A.R.E., Inc, and worked independently as a management consultant. Mr. Thacher was an ardent fisherman and golfer. He was a long time member and former president of the Garden City Golf Club, and also served on various committees of the Metropolitan Golf Association. He was a member of the United States Seniors Golf Association, the 200 Club and other senior golf groups. In Exter, he resided at RiverWoods, a retirement community. A memorial service will be held at Christ Episcopal Church, in Exeter, Saturday, October 3 at 2 P.M., with interment in Indian Mound Cemetery in Moravia, New York. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Metropolitan Golf Association, 49 Knollwood Road, Elmsford, New York 10523 or The NH Chapter of the Nature Conservancy, 2-1/2 Beacon Street, Concord, N.H. 03301.
