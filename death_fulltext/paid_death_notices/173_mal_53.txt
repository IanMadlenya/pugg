
        CALICK-Paul on June 4, 2001. Age 96, of Lake Worth FL, formerly of Forest Hills, NY. Beloved husband for 62 years of Gladys. Adored father of Dedee and Stephen Lovell, Terry and Harold Schottenfeld. Devoted brother of Leon and Miriam, and Bernard Calick. Loving grandfather of David and Lisa Roberts, Barry Lovell and Ellen Reinheimer, Jamie and Brian Puntin, Wendy Lovell-Smith and Colin Smith, Hope and Joshua Newman. Great grandfather of Jordan, Kasey, Rachel, Alyssa, Adam, Noah, Maya and Jacob. A founding member of Glen Head Country Club and pioneer in clothing import. Full of vitality, he possessed a winning sense of humor and warmth that endeared him to all. He will be sorely missed. Private graveside funeral to be held.
        CALICK-Paul. The Board of Directors and membership of Glen Head Country Club announce with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed Charter Member Paul Calick. Our deepest sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Louis M. Kerner, President
