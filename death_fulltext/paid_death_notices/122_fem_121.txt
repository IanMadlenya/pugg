
        GOLDFEDER - Helen W. Of Rancho Santa Fe, CA, died Friday, March 14. Beloved wife of Howard, devoted mother of Carole Adashek and Joan Goldfeder, grandmother to Michael Adashek, cherished sister of Richard Wiggs, and daughter of Dorothy Wiggs. Helen will be missed for her generosity, grace, and loving good nature. Memorial services will be held in California on Monday March 17. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the San Diego Hospice, 4311 Third Avenue, San Diego, CA 92103, or Planned Parenthood of San Diego, 1075 Camino del Rio South, San Diego, CA 92108.
