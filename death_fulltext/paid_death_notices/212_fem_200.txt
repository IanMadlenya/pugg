
        BASSER--Suzanne. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Suzanne Basser, beloved mother of Barbara Basser-Bigio, a devoted leader whose dedication to our Women's Executive Circle has sustained our ability to serve those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Suzanne's beloved husband Harold; to her children Barbara and Peter; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
