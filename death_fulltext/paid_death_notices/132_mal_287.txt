
        SEIDEL-Dr. Harry, 89. On Jan. 20, 1998, in Williamsburg, VA. A native of New York, Dr. Seidel had a private podiatry practice in Manhattan for 47 years. He was dedicated to his profession and a devoted family man. He is survived by and will be remembered as the beloved husband of Regina for 65 years, loving father of Suzanne Schoenbaum, cherished grandfather of Carol Wolf and Robert Schoenbaum, adored great-grandfather of Michelle Wolf, dear brother of Shirley Krauss.
