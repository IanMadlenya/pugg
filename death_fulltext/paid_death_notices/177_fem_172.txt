
        GREENFIELD-Helen E. Died peacefully on October 16, 2001. The loving and much beloved wife of the late Benjamin; proud, devoted mother of Jeff Greenfield and Janet Elmo; delighting in their life partners, Dena Sklar and Lou Elmo. Loving grandmother of Marc Elmo, and Casey and Dave Greenfield. A celebration of her life will be held on Friday, October 19th, 10:00 AM at ''The Riverside'', Amsterdam Ave. at 76th St., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Development Office of the 92nd Street YMHA, 1395 Lexington Avenue, NYC 10128. (212) 415-5479.
        GREENFIELD-Helen.
