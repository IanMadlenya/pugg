
        HELLER-Anne Cohen. Died Saturday, February 24, 2001, at home in Manhattan. She was 81. A physician known professionaly as Dr. Anne C. Carter, she was a lifelong resident of the city and Bedford, NY, and was predeceased by her husband, William B. Heller. She is survived by her children, James A. and Susan K. Heller of New York City, a daughter-in-law, Pamela, and two grandchildren, William B. II and Susan C. Heller. Calling hours will be from 6-8 PM, Tuesday, February 27, at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, Madison Ave. at 81st St. A memorial service will be held at the Frank E. Campbell Chapel at 11:30 AM on Wednesday, February 28. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Dana Hall School, 45 Dana Rd., Wellesley, MA 02481 or Wellesley College, 106 Central St., Wellesley, MA 02481.
