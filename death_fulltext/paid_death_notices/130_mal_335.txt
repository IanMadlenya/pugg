
        KELLY-Eugene A. Of Southbury, CT., died on November 20, age 86. A 1936 graduate of The Cooper Union, he had a distinguished career as a chemical engineer with the Kellex Corporation during World War II and later with Hydrocarbon Research Inc., and the CE Lummus Company. Survived by his sons Richard, of Lloyd Harbor, NY, and Bruce, of New Canaan, CT., and four grandchildren. Donations in his memory may be made to the annual fund of The Cooper Union, 30 Cooper Square, New York, New York 10003.
