
        ORNS-Josef. On July 20, 1997. Dear husband, father, grandfather and friend. Services Sunday, July 27, 4 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street.
        ORNS-Joseph. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Joseph Orns, July 20, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Graveside services were held. Memorial service will be held Sunday, July 27, at 4 PM, Campbell's Funeral Home, Madison Avenue and 82nd Street. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
