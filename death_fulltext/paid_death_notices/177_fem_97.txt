
        GREENSTONE-Myra S. Quietly pased away on October 10, 2001 after a courageous battle with cancer, leaving her cherished husband Jay, her beloved children Michael and Amy, Howard and Brigette, Daniel and Jessica, Scott and Laura, and her beloved grandchildren Jason, Rachel, Justin, Joshua, Brandon and Isabella. Myra possessed true beauty, style, intelligence and grace. As a career-minded woman who committed her life's work to helping others, for the past twelve years Myra devoted her time & resources to the Hackensack University Medical Center, and recently was elected Second Vice Chair of its Board of Governors. Myra also served as Vice President of the United Jewish Community of Bergen County, Chair of the New Jersey Woman's Division of UJA, and President of the Jewish Hospital and Rehabilitation Center of New Jersey. Services Sunday, October 14, 12:30 PM at ''Gutterman-Musicant'', 402 Park Street, Hackensack, New Jersey (800) 378-1892. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Hackensack University Medical Center Foundation for the Myra S. Greenstone Memorial Fund will be kindly appreciated.
        GREENSTONE-Myra. Dearest and admired friend. Always a source of strength and dignity. We deeply mourn her loss as we celebrate her life. Randy, Sam and Kim Ketive
