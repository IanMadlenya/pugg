
        WILLIAMS--C. Roger. May 15, 1930--August 11, 2005. Died at home in Fairfield, CT. surrounded by his loving wife and children. Roger graduted from Princeton in 1954, after spending 3 years as a Army Airborne Ranger, (11th Airborne, 15th Ranger Company). He retired in 1990 from Mobil Corp as General Manager of Investor Relations and Information Coordination. He is survived by his wife Priscilla, and his children Jennifer (Tony Porto) Pamela Bowen (Tim) Ian (Colette Copeland) Christopher (Faye O'Brien) and Katherine (Ezra Lee) and by a foster son Jason McCaffrey and five grandchildren. His family is his legacy. Rangers lead the way.
