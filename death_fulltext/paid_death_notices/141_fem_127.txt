
        LEVY-Helen B. On Oct. 11, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Charles. Caring mother of Louise Cooperstein & the late Jim Linden. Cherished grandmother of Michael, Judith, Steven and Richard. Adored great-grandmother of Eva, Paula and Gregory. Devoted mother-inlaw of Miriam Goldman. She loved us all. Her family were her riches. She was our jewel. A sweet, soft lady, reached her 100th year with dignity and beauty. All who knew her will never forget her. Graveside services, Wednesday 1 P.M., at New Mount Carmel Cemetery, Cypress Hills, Glendale, NY.
