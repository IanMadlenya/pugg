
        CLURMAN-Shirley Potash., May 13th. After a long illness, at St. Rose's Home. Beloved wife of the late Richard, devoted mother of Michael, stepmother of Susan Emma and Carol. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Citizens Committee for New York City, 305 7th Ave., NY NY 10001.
        CLURMAN-Shirley The Board and Staff of the Citizens Committee for New York City extend their deepest sympathy to her son Michael, who succeeded his late father Richard as a Director of the Citizens Committee. A font of information on happenings large andsmall, an ardent Yankees fan and a devoted New Yorker, Shirley will be sorely missed by her wide circle of friends. Osborn Elliott, Chairman Michael E. Clark, President
