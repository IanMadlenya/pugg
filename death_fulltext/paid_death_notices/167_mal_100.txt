
        TANZ-Alfred L., 94, member of Abraham Lincoln Brigade, one of the first American volunteers in Spanish Civil War, died November 29. In WWII he was attached to OSS and parachuted into France before D-Day. Founding member of National Lawyers Guild. Survived by his wife Freda, in El Cerrito, CA, stepchildren Sydney Williams, Mark Olmstead, sister Daisy Herman, niece Janie Taylor, nephew Arnold Caplin. Salud!
