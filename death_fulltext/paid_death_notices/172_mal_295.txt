
        ZUCKER-Jack S. Of Philadelphia, a businessman, union organizer and political activist died on Wednesday, May 23 at 91. He was the son of Abram and Bertha Malman, born in Luc, Poland. He had a brother Max and sister Fannie. He was married to Anne Brooks for 63 years. He is survived by two sons Steven and Lawrence, a daughter-in-law Anita, a grandson Josh, and two step-grandchildren Eric and Cynthia. A memorial will be held at a later date.
