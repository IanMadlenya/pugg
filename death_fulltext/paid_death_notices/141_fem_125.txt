
        GILKESON-Elizabeth C. Age 95, on October 7, 1998. Founder of the Poughkeepsie Day School; Founding Director of the Bank Street School for Children and senior faculty member at Bank Street College. Beloved grandmother of Robert C. Gilkeson, M.D., Nancy O'Connor-Reedy, Elizabeth C. O'Connor, Martha G. Todd and Sarah G. Melcher. Interment private. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81 Street, Wednesday, October 14, at 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Bank Street College of Education, 610 West 112 Street, New York City 10025.
