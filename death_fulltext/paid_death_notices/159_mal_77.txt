
        IGOE-John E. The officers and members of Elmwood Country Club mourn the passing of their esteemed member, John E. Igoe, devoted husband to Ruth; beloved father to Cathy, Cindy, Nancy, Jeffrey and John; and stepfather to Janet, Susan, Lynn and Diane; and close friend of many members. We will all remember John for his warmth, friendly smile and devotion to golf.
