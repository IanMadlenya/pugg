
        HAMMOCK-Mel, 49, interior designer, died of cancer, August 29 at his home in NYC. Born and raised in Atlanta, GA., Mel was the son of Charles E. and Betty Hammock. Survived by his mother, brothers Alan & Andy Hammock, sister and brother-in-law Nancy and Harry Stewart, nieces Kim Arant and Lori Stewart. A memorial service was held August 31 at All Saints Episcopal Church. In celebration of his life and talent friends and clients will be honoring him on Thurs., Sept. 25 at the D&D Building from 5-7 PM. A scholarship is being established in his name at Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club.
