
        SIMONS--Leo M. On September 22, 2005, at age 76. He will be lovingly remembered by wife Nancy (nee Irausquin); children Pamela and Grant; their mother, Sonia Simons; daughter-in-law Sunisa Simons; grandchildren Emily, Nicole, Julia & Scott Simons; sister Lillian Greenberg and brother - in - law William Greenberg; niece Janet Greenberg and nephew Michael Greenberg. He was a mechanical engineer, formerly with Ramseyer and Miller, Inc., and Russell Design Associates. Burial was held on September 25. Contributions may be made to the American Heart Assoc.
