
        MUROFF--Dr. Melvin, on April 7, 2004. Longtime resident of Scarsdale, NY, dearly beloved husband, father and uncle, survived by his wife Blanche and son Ivor. He was a psychotherapist devoted to his practice over 60 years with the highest principles and utmost integrity. He completed his training at the Menninger Clinic and was a member of a number of associations including the Topeka Psychoanalytic Institute and the American Orthopsychiatric Association. He was an author, editor and teacher whose focus was on child development and group dynamics. We will never stop missing him. Services will be held at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, NY (914-381-1809) Friday April 9, 2004 at 10AM.
