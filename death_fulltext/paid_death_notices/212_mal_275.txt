
        BORENSTEIN--Milton C., of Brookline, Massachusetts entered into rest September 23, 2004. Husband of the late Anne (Shapiro) Borenstein. Devoted father of Roberta Borenstein of Wilton, CT and Jeffrey Borenstein of West Newton. Brother of Shirley Bloom of Brookline. Cherished grandfather of three. Services at Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, 1668 Beacon St, Brookline, MA on Wednesday, September 29 at 11 AM. Expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to Congregation Kehillath Israel, Brookline, MA., or to Boston College, Chestnut Hill, MA.
