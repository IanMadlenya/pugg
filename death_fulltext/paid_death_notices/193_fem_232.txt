
        LEVINE-Vera, 79. Of No. Palm Beach, Florida, late of Hewlett, Long Island, died on February 24, 2003. Beloved wife of Henry I. Levine, loving mother of Joel and Roger Levine and Louise Webber, devoted grandmother of Annie Levine and dear sister of Harriet Schnur. Memorial to be held at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 4714 Okeechobee Blvd. WPB on Sunday, March 2 at 2PM. Shiva will be observed after the ceremony at the apartment of Harriet Schnur, and at her New York home from Tuesday through Thursday, with services at 7:30 PM. The Levine children will sit Shiva March 5th from 5-10 P.M. at the Webber residence. A New York area memorial will be scheduled at a later date. In lieu of flowers, the family is requesting that donations be made in memory of Vera F. Levine to Brandeis University National Women's Committee, C/O Barbara Silberman, 140 Isle Dr., Palm Beach Gardens, FL. 33418.
