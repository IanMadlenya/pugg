
        LUSTGARTEN--Elizabeth. Of Quogue, on November 4, 2006. Wife of the late Baier Lustgarten and mother of the late Garry T. Lustgarten, she is survived by: her sons, J. Paul Hoffman, Howard C. Lustgarten and his wife, Barbara, and Kenneth C. Lustgarten and his wife, Beverly, 11 grandchildren, several great grandchildren and dear friend and caregiver Jaqueline Markar. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, December 3, 2006 at 1 P.M. at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, Westhampton Beach. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Family Counseling Service, P.O. Box 1348 Westhampton Beach, New York 11978.
