
        ELLENBOGEN-Evelyn Jackter. Died January 8, 1999 at 93 years. Born in Brooklyn in 1905, she taught elementary school in the NYC public schools for 38 years. Wife of the late Nath Ellenbogen, mother of Marian Isenberg and Ruth Flaxman, grandmother of Alison, Neil, Caroline, Gary and Laura. We will miss her. Contributions to charity of your choice.
