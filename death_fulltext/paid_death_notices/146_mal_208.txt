
        WARNER-Oscar. Of Boynton Beach, Florida, on March 8th, 1999 at the age of 77 after a lengthy illness and courageous struggle. Beloved husband of Ethel, devoted father of Stephen and Robert and of Peter and Lani, and devoted grandfather of Barbara, David, Elizabeth, Stephanie, and Lindsey. Brother of Jean Brooks and the late Seymour Warner. He was an inspiration to all who knew him because of his tenacity and fierce determination to live despite debilitating chronic illnesses and for his gentleness and compassion. Memorial service date to be announced.
