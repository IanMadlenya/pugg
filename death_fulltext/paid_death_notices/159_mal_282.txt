
        KARSON-Nelson. Of Great Neck died on April 16, 2000. He was 79 years old and died at North Shore University Hospital of complications following a stroke. Mr. Karson was a retired electrical engineer. He moved to Great Neck in 1957 and resided there until his death. He was married to the late Janet Rothschild Karson, who died in 1995. Mr. Karson is survived by a son, Scott and his wife Joleen of Stony Brook; a daughter, Shelley and her partner Harriet Mendlowitz of Jackson Heights; a granddaughter, Tobi Karson Sevdalakis and her husband Christos; a grandson Jared Karson; and a sister Annette Karson. A memorial service for Mr. Karson was held at the Ethical Humanist Society of Long Island in Garden City on April 22, 2000. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 66 South St., Patchogue, NY 11772 or to Planned Parenthood Hudson Peconic, 70 Maple Ave., Smithtown, New York 11787.
