
        PETERSON-The Rev. Martin, C.M. On November 23, 1998. Father Peterson served at St. John's University for many years as a librarian. He also served in the libraries of many Vincentian seminaries on the East Coast. Father is survived by his sister Margaret Papa and many nieces and nephews. Visiting Tuesday 7-9PM at St. John's University faculty residence. Mass Wednesday, 11AM, St. Vincent's Chapel in the faculty residence. Interment St. Joseph's Seminary, Princeton, N.J.
