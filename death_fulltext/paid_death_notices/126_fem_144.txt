
        STESSIN-Nancy. The entire Juilliard Community mourns the death of Nancy Stessin, who worked very closely with her husband Herbert Stessin in the teaching of piano at all levels at the Juilliard School. We extend our deepest condolences to Herbert and the Stessin family. Mary Rodgers Guettel Chairman, Board of Trustees Joseph W. Polisi President
