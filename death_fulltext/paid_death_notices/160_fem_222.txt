
        CROSS-Marjorie, Age 90 died May 8 after a long illness. Her early career researching and translating Spanish documents for the San Antonio Historical Society led to an appointment in the Office of Censorship during World War II. Later she was an executive with DeSola Brothers, exporters/importers, NY City. Her many friends will miss her keen intelligence and caring ways. Donations to the N.Y. Public Library are welcome.
