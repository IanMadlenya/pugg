
        KRAMER-Elmer E., M.D., suddenly at home on April 20th, 1997. He was a Clinical Professor in the Departments of Obstetrics, Gynecology and Gyn. Pathology at Cornell University Medical College. Husband of Anne Gleysteen Kramer, father of Toni of San Francisco and Dirk, grandfather of Annie, brother of Gerhardt, Elsie, Gertrude and Mildred. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Cornell University Medical College.
