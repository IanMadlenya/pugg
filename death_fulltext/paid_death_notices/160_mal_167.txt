
        DESHLER-James, II. 91. Of Delray Beach, FL on May 9, 2000. Graduate of Williams College and Rutgers Law School. He was a partner in the law firm of Hicks, Kuhlthau, Thompson & Deshler, New Brunswick, NJ; served ''Of Counsel'' to Johnson & Johnson; and retired as Chairman of Engelhard Minerals & Chemicals, Menlo Park, NJ. He is survived by his wife, Jeanette of Delray Beach, FL and his daughter Elaine Marshall of Mequon, WI. Services were private. For information contact Quackenboss Funeral Home, New Brunswick, NJ.
