
        CELLIER-Jacques. On October 27, 2001. Survived by his nephew, Claude Cellier and family and his many friends. We will never forget Jacques, with his passion for life, intelligence, mischievous nature and ''larger-than-life'' personality. Jacques added a dimension of excitement and fun to all of our lives and set an example with his brave attitude towards life. Services will be held at 11 AM, Friday, November 2nd, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.
