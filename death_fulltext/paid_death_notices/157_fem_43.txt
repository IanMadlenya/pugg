
        LIEB-Alyce E., 88. passed away peacefully February 1, 2000 after a long illness. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Myron (Mac) Lieb and her daughter Ann Carol Mabuchi. She is also survived by her granddaughter Kayla Ritt Yamada, her great-daughter Chelsea Skye Yamada and her grandson Scott Brian Mabuchi. All remember the lovely Alyce and her delightful sense of humor. She will be greatly missed. Service Sunday, February 6, 11:30AM at Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. Interment Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, Queens, N.Y.
