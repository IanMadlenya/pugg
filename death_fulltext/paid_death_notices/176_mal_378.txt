
        GLINE-Murray. On Monday, September 17, 2001, Murray Gline of Bethesda, Maryland, formerly of Baldwin, New York. A kind, loving man, he will be deeply missed by his wife of 55 years, Phoebe Meyers Gline, his daughters Ellen Gline Block and Barbara Gline Pearlman, his grandchildren Sarah Block, Rachel Block and Daniel Pearlman, and his sons-in-law David Block and Rob Pearlman. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation at 120 Wall Street, 19th floor, New York, New York 10005 attn: Public Information Group (www.jdf.org).
