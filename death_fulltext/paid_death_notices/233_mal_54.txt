        $(6$)CANTOR--Sol W. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of our longtime friend, generous supporter, and former member of our Board of Overseers. He was a tireless worker on our behalf, and a remarkably effective ambassador in reaching out to enlist the support of others. His intellect, energy, and loyalty will be long remembered and appreciated. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Cantor family. Yeshiva University Richard M.
