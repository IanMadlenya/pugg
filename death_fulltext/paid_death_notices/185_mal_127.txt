        ADELSTEIN-Bernard. The Board of Directors, officers, and employees of Group Health Incorporated note with profound sorrow the passing of Bernie Adelstein. We express our sincere gratitude for his service, guidance, and friendship to GHI. Bernie served on GHI's Board of Directors for over 20 years and was a dear friend and advisor to management at GHI. He always gave of himself tirelessly. We extend our deepest sympathy to his son Marty, our colleague, and to all of his beloved family. Frank Branchini President and CEO, GHI GHI Board of Directors
        ADELSTEIN-Bernard. It is with profound sorrow that the Board of Directors and Staff of the United Cerebral Palsy of New York City acknowledge the passing of our devoted friend. For many years, Bernard Adelstein graciously supported UCP's work through his involvement in the Annual Telethon and numerous other activities. We extend our deepest sympathy to his sons Martin and Alan and daughter Sheryl. Leo Hausman, Chairman Martin Hausman, President UCP of NYC, Inc.
