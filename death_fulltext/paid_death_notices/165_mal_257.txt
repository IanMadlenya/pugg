
        DAVEY-Alan. Passed away Oct. 18, 2000, at 64 years of age. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Davey. Loving stepfather of Nina Endler & Thomas Manno. Loving step-grandfather of Anna Endler. Alan worked for 30 years for The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary as an Accountant. He loved traveling, church and classical music. He was a happy man and rarely complained. Service is Wednesday, Oct 25, 2 pm at All Saints Chapel, Trinity Church, Wall & Broadway.
