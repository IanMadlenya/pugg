
        GOLDSTEIN--Benjamin J. On August 5, 2003. Peacefully, surrounded by his family at the age of 88. Beloved husband of Gertrude, cherished father of Walter and Joel, loved father-in-law of Debra and Toni, adored grandfather of Marc, Rebecca and her husband Michael. Dear brother of Sylvia and David. A gentle man who was a friend to all and devoted to his family. He will be sincerely missed by all who knew him. Chapel service will be on Thursday, 1PM, at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 Lecount Place, New Rochelle, N.Y. Interment to follow at Mount Eden Cemetery. Donations in his memory can be made to Congregation Anshe Sholom, 50 North Avenue, New Rochelle, N.Y. 10805.
