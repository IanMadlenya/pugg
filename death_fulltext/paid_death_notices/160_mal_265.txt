
        DUNNE-James J., on May 21, 2000 in White Plains. Born on April 24, 1920 in Staten Island to Mary (Swain) and James Joseph Dunne. Mr. Dunne was the Vice Chairman of the Board at Cluett & Peabody. He was predeceased by his first wife Ann in 1974. Mr. Dunne is survived by his second wife Barbara (Dapice) and five children, Kathleen Moore, Eileen Bowers, James J. Dunne III, Patricia Keneally and Maureen Wright. Also surviving is one sister Catherine Taggert, & eleven grandchildren. Reposing at McMahon, Lyon & Hartnett Funeral Home, White Plains on Wednesday & Thursday 2-4PM and 7-9PM. Mass Friday 10:00 St John's Church, White Plains. Contributions to Rice High School, 74 West 124th Street, New York, New York 10027. Attn: Brother Walderman.
