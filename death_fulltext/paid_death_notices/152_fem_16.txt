
        GREIF-Nettie, age 94, of New York City, died August 31. Former teacher of mathematics at Seward Park High School. After retirement, passed many fulfilling years in continued learning and teaching with friends at Institute for Retired Professionals and Quest. Avid painter and lifetime member of Art Student League. And still had limitless time and energy for family and friends. She leaves behind her loving son Paul (Madelyne), grandchildren Michaela, Sasha, and Daniel, adored brother Nathan Libby, beloved sister-in-law Shirley Libby, and dear sister Anna Smith, as well as treasured nieces, nephews and their children. Services will be held at 11AM, Friday, September 3, at Cedar Park-Beth-El Cemeteries, Paramus, NJ. Call family for details.
