
        MERMELSTEIN-Matthew David. Meriden, Connecticut, 71, died unexpectedly Saturday afternoon, April 22, 2000. He was born in New York City, in 1928, the son of the late Bernard and Betty Lazareff Mermelstein. He graduated from New York City College with his Master's in Psychology in 1951. He was a clinical psychologist in Meriden since 1959. Survivors include four daughters, and two sons-inlaw, in Kentucky. Also a sister, Regina Damon, of Flushing, New York, and an aunt Natalie Levy, of New York City. Relatives and friends are invited to gather with his family Wednesday, April 26, 2000, beginning at 1:00 P.M., in the Smith-Ruzzo Funeral Home, 226 West Main Street, Meriden, CT, with a service commencing at 2 o'clock. Interment will be private.
