
        GORD--James Q. Formerly of New York City, died February 7 at home in Bryn Mawr, PA. Survived by son Benjamin, daughter - in - law Anne and grandchildren Sarah, Sam and Andrew of Wellesley, MA. He will be greatly missed by those who knew and loved him. Funeral mass will be held at the Church of the Resurrection in New York City on Saturday, February 19 at noon. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be sent to the Church of the Resurrection, 119 E. 74 St., New York, NY 10021.
