
        CROSS-Joseph G., 88. Of Hobe Sound, FL passed away Saturday, October 27, 2001 at Martin Memorial Hospital South, Stuart, FL. Born in Brooklyn, New York he had been a resident of Hobe Sound for the past 22 years. He moved to the area from Long Island, Woodbury, New York. Prior to retirement he was Vice President of Merrill Lynch on Wall Street for 25 years. He was pre-deceased by his daughter, Maureen Cross Wendt. Survivors include his wife of 59 years, Mildred Cross of Hobe Sound, FL; his grandson, Eric Wendt; two nieces and three nephews. Friends may call from 3:005:00 PM, Thursday, November 1st at the chapel of Young & Prill Funeral Home, Stuart. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 1:00 PM, Friday, November 2nd at St. Christopher Catholic Church in Hobe Sound, FL, with Father Michael Tschudi officiating. Committal Services will follow at the Riverside Memorial Park in Tequesta, FL. In lieu of flowers, those who wish are asked to consider contributions to Charity of Choice. For information Young & Prill Funeral Home, Stuart, FL.
