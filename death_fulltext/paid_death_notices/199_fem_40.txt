
        KUPFERSTEIN--Fela (Feigele) nee Orenstein. On August 1, 2003. Born Warsaw, Poland 1917. Sole survivor of her family, neither the Nazis nor Russia's labor camps could stop her, she began a new life with her childhood sweetheart, Hershel. Met life's challenges with courage, intelligence, determination, humor and beautiful Yiddish songs. Loving mother to Leon and Evelyn. Devoted grandmother to Kyla, David, Denise and Josh.
