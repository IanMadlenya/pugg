
        HILDICK-SMITH--Gavin Y. (Pete), M.D. Of Princeton, NJ, died Sunday, at University Medical Center at Princeton, at age 86. Survived by his wife Kit, sons and daughtersin law, Peter and Beth, and Andrew and Hughie, and six grandsons. Memorial service is at 11:30 AM, Friday, at Trinity Church, Princeton. Memorial contributions may be made to the Stony Brook Millstone Watershed Association, 61 Titus Mill Road, Pennington, NJ 08534.
