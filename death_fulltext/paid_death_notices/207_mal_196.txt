
        LEVY--Edward R. Died on April 11, 2004 at Calvary Hospital of cancer at age 74. Mr. Levy earned a Master's degree in mathematics and a Doctorate in English from the University of Illnois. He taught at the University's prestigious high school and was the senior author of a series of rhetoric textbooks published by Holt, Rhinehart, and Winston in the 1970's. He was stage manager for long running, off Broadway show ''The Fantastiks'' for more than half a decade and was the musical director of the Tony award-winning Torch Song Trilogy. For the past 20 years, he worked as a voice teacher and as a senior editor of mathematics and English textbooks. Services will be held at the Church of the Transfiguration (The Little Church Around the Corner) on 29th St off 5th Av, Monday, April 19th at 11 AM.
