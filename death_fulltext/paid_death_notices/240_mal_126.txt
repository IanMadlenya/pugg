
        CROSS--Henry B. (Hank), Jr. 92, of 981 Greenway Lane, Vero Beach, FL died Sunday, January 7, 2007, at his home. Mr. Cross was born in Providence, RI and moved to Vero Beach from Dorset, VT and West Orange, NJ. He is survived by his wife Marion Cross of Vero Beach; daughters Carin Cross of Whitefish, MT and Leigh McLean of Dublin, NH; sons Gordon Cross and Henry ''Huck'' Cross of Whitefish, MT; and eight grandchildren. A private service will be held in Dorset, VT at a later date.
