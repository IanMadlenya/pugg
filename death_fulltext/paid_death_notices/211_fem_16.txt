
        SAAD -- Shahnaz ''Chris,'' Ph.D. Died July 31 from complications of scleroderma. Cherished daughter of Dr. Mayer and Samira Saad. Trusted sister of Dr. Andre Saad. Loving aunt of Layla. Educated at Smith College, she completed her doctoral studies with distinction at the University of Pennsylvania. She devoted her life to the betterment of children's lives, first as a commited educator, then as founder of Chris' Corner, a bookstore for kids and teens, and recipient of the ''Best of Philly'' Award. She helped thousands of children to love to read as she did. She taught integrity, courage and strength by example, and her kindness and generosity will be remembered by countless friends, family members and children of her community. Friends will be received at Logan Funeral Home, 2410 Lombard St, Philadelphia, Monday, 6-8 pm, and Tuesday, 9-11 am, and in New York at Fairchild, 1570 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, Wednesday, 3-5 and 7-9 pm. In lieu of flowers, provide books to needy children through firstbook.org, 1319 F St. NW, Suite 1000, Washington, DC 20004. We will all miss you terribly, Chris. Rest in peace knowing that the world is a better place for you having been here.
