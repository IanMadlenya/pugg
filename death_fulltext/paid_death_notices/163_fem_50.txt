
        NEWELL-Marla. Beloved wife of Roger, mother of Sonya and Paul, and friend to many died August 4, 2000. Raised on the Grand Concourse and later residing in Greenwich Village, she was a devoted and esteemed member of the Board of Trustees and past President of the Conservative Synagogue of Fifth Avenue. Services will be held on Sunday, August 6, at 11:15 A.M., in The Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76 Street. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to either the American Liver Foundation or the CSFA.
