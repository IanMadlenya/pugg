
        SWEEZY--Paul M. (April 10. 1910-February 27, 2004). Economist, journalist, scholar, socialist, Marxist, revolutionary. Founding editor of Monthly Review. President, Monthly Review Foundation. He made words make a difference. We salute him. The directors and staff of Monthly Review: Hyacinth Anthonyson, Percy Brazil, Barbara Epstein, Bill Fletcher, Jr., John Bellamy Foster, Robert W. McChesney, Harry Magdoff, John Mage, Claude Misukiewicz, Andrew Nash, Martin Paddio, Renee Pendergrass, Wendy Prince, Lucille Scofield, John J. Simon, Martha Sweezy, Michael D. Yates.
