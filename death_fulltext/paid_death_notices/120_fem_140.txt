
        PAPE-Mabel E. (nee Tennant) on January 19, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Frederick W. Pape, Sr. Mother of Mrs. Gerald Denneby (Dorothy Devaney) of Quogue, New York, Frederick W. Pape, Jr. of Falmouth Fareside, Maine, William L. Pape of Lloyd Harbor, NY. Predeceased by her grandson Warren A. Wegner, Jr. Grandmother of six, great grandmother of 16. Burial will be private at Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY. Memorial Service to be held April 26th, at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, Westhampton Beach, New York, 1 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions in memory of Mabel Pape may be made to St. Mark's Church or the Westhampton War Memorial Ambulance Association, Westhampton Beach, New York.
