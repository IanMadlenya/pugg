
        WENGRAF--Gertrude. Born in Vienna, Austria on September 1, 1910, died at her home in New York City on November 10, 2005. Predeceased by her husband Dr. Fritz Wengraf. Beloved friend, your optimism and your love of art and nature greatly inspired us. You will be sadly missed. Madeline Buchsbaum and Fritzi Zeitlhofer
