
        PUSCHEL--Walter E. With deepest sorrow, F. Schumacher & Co. announces the death of Walter Eggert Puschel, age 97, who died on August 13, 2004. Mr. Puschel served as president of F. Schumacher & Co. from 1957 to 1971. Son of the late Paul H. Puschel and Helene Eggert Puschel, Mr. Puschel was born in Chicago, IL in 1906 and graduated from the University of Chicago in 1929. During World War II, he served as an officer in the U.S. Navy. His wife, Francoise Pozier Puschel, whom he married in France in 1934, predeceased him in 1996. Mr. Puschel had lived in Riverside, CT. Surviving him are a daughter, Suzanne Meskell, two sons, Philip and Gerald, five grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Donations in his memory may be sent to the First Congregational Church of Old Greenwich, 108 Sound Beach Avenue, Old Greenwich, CT 06870 (Attn: Mr. Kirk Young) or the Salvation Army.
