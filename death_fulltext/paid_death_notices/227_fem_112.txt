
        LINFIELD--Babette, on December 15, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Seymour. She is survived by her son Jeffrey Mann, daughter-in-law Amy Mann, granddaughters Jessica Mann Gutteridge (Corin) and Rachel Mann Rosan (Seth), great grandchildren Rafe Gutteridge and Lily Rosan, also by her sister Judy West and step son Michael Linfield. She led a productive life retiring at age 85 as the Executive Director of Professionals Network for Social Responsibility. She was concerned with the safety and health of a peaceful world. Service Sunday 11:30am at ''The Riverside'' 76 St & Amsterdam Ave.
