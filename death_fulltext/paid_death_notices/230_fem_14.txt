
        DALVITO--Anne Numrich, a long time resident of Mamaroneck, passed away March 1, 2006 at her home after a long battle with cancer, she was 75. Born October 26, 1930 in Lancaster, PA, she was the daughter of the late Miriam Book Numrich and George Robert Numrich. She was the sister of the late George Robert Numrich, Jr. Mrs. Dalvito was a 1946 graduate of Mamaroneck High School. She attended Emerson College, Carnegie-Mellon University and received her BA degree from SUNY Purchase in 1992. Mrs. Dalvito was an avid gardener and member of the Mamaroneck Garden Club, where she won numerous awards for her flower arranging. She loved to travel and before her illness visited much of the world. She was a member of The Brick Presbyterian Church in Manhattan. A beloved and devoted wife, mother and grandmother, Mrs. Dalvito is survived by her husband Rocco James Dalvito of Mamaroneck, NY, and her four children, Deborah Keats McCarter of Okatie, SC, William E. Keats of Bethelehem, PA, Lisa Britton of Darien, CT, and Gregory James Dalvito of New York, NY. She is also survived by six grandchildren; Tony, Alex, Emma, Daniel, Amelia and Jaime. A visitation will be held at Coxe and Graziano Funeral Home, 767 Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, NY from 6-9PM on Friday, March 3rd. A memorial service will be held at Larchmont Presbyterian Church at a later date. The Reverend Michael Lindvall from Brick Church will officiate. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Memorial SloanKettering Institute or the American Cancer Society.
