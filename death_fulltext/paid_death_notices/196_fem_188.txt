
        DEMPSTER--Amelia Robinson, of Yonkers, NY, formerly of Gramercy Park and Riverdale, NY, died on April 10, 2003. She was an avid supporter of the arts, an active member of the Stuyvesant Park Neighborhood Association and volunteer at St. George Episcopal Church. Amy will be keenly missed by many cousins and dear friends in the U.S., Canada and the U.K. A memorial service will be held at the Riverdale Presbyterian Church, 4765 Henry Hudson Pkwy, Bronx, NY on June 5, 2003 at 11am. Memorial contributions may be made to St. George Episcopal Church, 209 E. 16th St, NY, NY for its charitable activities or the American Cancer Society. >>AD#
