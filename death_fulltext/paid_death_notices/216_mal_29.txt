
        CHALFIN--Lawrence, M.D. On January 1, 2005. Loving husband, father, grandfather and brother. Dedicated psychoanalyst and educator. He is survived by wife Phyllis, children Susan, Tanya and Michael, brother Robert and grandchildren. We love you and miss you. Funeral services will be held on Monday, January 3, 2005, at 12:45 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        CHALFIN--Lawrence, M.D. The NYU Psychoanalytic Institute and the Psychoanalytic Association of New York are saddened by the untimely loss of our highly esteemed colleague and friend, Lawrence Chalfin, M.D. Larry was a former Director of the Institute. He was an excellent teacher, supervisor and clinician. He made major contributions to our Institute and our Association, as well as to psychoanalytic education on a national level. We will deeply miss him. Our condolences go to his wife Phyllis, his son Michael, his daughters Susan and Tanya, his daughter-inlaw Sharon, his sons-in-law Richard and David, his six grandchildren and his brother Robert. Robert Fischel, M.D. Director NYU Psychoanalytic Institute Samuel Herschkowitz, M.D. Chair Education Committee Charles Goodstein, M.D. President Psychoanalytic Association of New York
