
        SEGMAN--Harriet S., age 85, entered rest August, 2004 after a brief bout with cancer. She leaves a niece Jayne, a nephew Tony, a brother Ralph and many loved ones. Pre-deceased by her mother Martha Goldberg and father William Segman. Private services were held in celebration of her life. Harriet was active in animal rights charities. She is interred at Mount Lebanon Cemetery. Please visit guestbook at www.legacy.com.
