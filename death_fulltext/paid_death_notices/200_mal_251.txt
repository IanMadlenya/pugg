
        KRISER--Sidney P. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of Sidney Kriser, a longtime friend and supporter. Along with his late brother Leonard, Sidney was the recipient of ADL's Haym Solomon Award for their generous support of the League. We extend our sincere condolences to his daughter Judy, his nephew Charles, who serves on ADL's National Executive Committee, and the entire family. Glen A. Tobias National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
