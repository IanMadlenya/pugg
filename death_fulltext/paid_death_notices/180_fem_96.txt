
        SINKMAN-Ida. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services notes with great sadness the death of Ida Sinkman. Ida gave generously of her time and wisdom to improve the lives of people in need. A court worker and Divisional Committee member for Adult Mentally Ill Services, Ida was a beloved volunteer at the Brooklyn REAL. Her kindness and support meant so much to clients and staff. She will be deeply missed. Our condolences to her entire family. Jean L. Troubh, President Joseph S. Kaplan Chairman of the Board Susan O. Friedman, Chair AMI, Divisional Committee Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President and CEO
        SINKMAN-Ida. Riverdale Jewish Center extends condolences to our member, Edith Glaser upon the loss of her mother. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi David Sable, President
