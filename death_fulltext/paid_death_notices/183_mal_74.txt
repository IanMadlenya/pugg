
        KESNER-Herbert. A retired news broadcast editor of The New York Times died on Monday in Lebanon, Pennsylvania. Mr. Kesner was employed by the newspaper from 1936 to 1972. Under his editorship, the news broadcast desk won the prestigious Peabody Award. He is survived by his wife Constance, daughters, Mrs. Harry Abplanalp of Seabrook Island, SC and Mrs. Robert Madison Butler of St. Thomas, USVI, five grandchildren and fifteen great-grandchildren, three stepchildren, and four stepgrandchildren. His grandson, Christian Maltby, predeceased him on September 11th, 2001. Funeral services will be held on Thursday at 10:00 AM in the Edwin Donnan Funeral Home, Johnstown, New York.
