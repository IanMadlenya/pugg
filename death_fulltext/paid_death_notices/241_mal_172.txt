
        RENDAHL--Jack. 75, died Saturday, February 10th, 2007. He was a Career Foreign Service Officer in the U.S. Department of State and served as Deputy Chief of Mission to the U.S. Embassy in Dublin (1974-1978). Services will be held at Thomas McAfee Funeral Home, Downtown, Greenville, SC on February 14th, 2007 at 4:30 pm to be followed by a celebration held in his honor at Connolly's Irish Pub.
