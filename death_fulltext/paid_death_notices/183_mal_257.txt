
        SPECTOR-Sidney. Died April 9, 2002 at the age of 89 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Sylvia, deceased, loving husband of Clara Linn, devoted father and father-inlaw of Larry and Paula Spector, Judy and Tim Volem. Cherished grandfather of Jonathan, Jenny, Scott, Eli and Margot. He will be remembered for his generosity and commitment to education.
