
        ALBOHER -- Albert. We mourn the loss of Albert, of Bayside, Queens, and Monticello, NY. He passed away peacefully in his sleep on October 31, at the age of 67. Al was President of Albert Alboher Associates and was an avid horse racing fan. He is survived by Ellen Alboher, their two daughters, Lori and Elyse, his sister Vilma and brother Elliott, his beloved granddaughter, Jami Abigail, and his litte grey filly, styled N Misty. Services today, 1:30 PM, at Boulevard Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett. Burial at Beth David Cemetery. The family requests that donations be made to support the blind, kidney research or the Make a Wish Foundation in lieu of flowers.
