
        EYER--Lisa Auchincloss. Of New York City. Died on October 24, 2006. She was 82. She was the daughter of the late Reginald LaGrange Auchincloss and the late Ruth Hunter Cutting of New York City and Tuxedo Park, NY. She was a graduate of The Chapin School. Her husband, George A. Eyer, Jr., died in October 2003. She is survived by her three daughters: Diana Bohn of McClean, VA, Lisa Ryan of New York City and East Hampton, NY, Alexandra Eyer-Martinez of Gold Beach, OR, and six grandchildren--Luke, Sergio, Pilar, Amanda, Samantha and Justine. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell's Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. on Thursday, November 9, 2006 at 3:30PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Make-A-Wish Foundation of New York City.
