
        SHAPIRO--Richard Paul, of New York City passed away this Thursday, April 1, 2004. He was born on June 26, 1942, in New York City. Mr. Shapiro graduated from Yale College in 1964. Mr. Shapiro worked as a fashion executive at Bloomingdale's, Ralph Lauren, Christian Dior and Giorgio Armani. Mr. Shapiro also owned his own computer consulting company. He is survived by his wife Janet Scholder Shapiro, son James Shapiro and cousin Alan Gorfin. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, April 4, 2004, at 11:30am at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. The family will receive visitors Sunday night only, after 6:00pm at their home. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Cabrini Hospital Hospice.
