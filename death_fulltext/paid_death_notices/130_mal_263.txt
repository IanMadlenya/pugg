
        STOLZ-Samuel S. On November 23, 1997, after a long and glorious life, in his home peacefully at the age of 91. Husband of Freda. Father of Margrit, Robert and James. Grandfather of Erik, Oliver, Adam, Daniel and Freda. Father-in-law of Roger, Marlene and Chris. Survived by his sister Barbara Roth. Loved by all. A sweet, wise, gentle man. We learned so much from him and we will miss him so. Funeral services will be Tuesday, November 25, at 12:30 PM, at Temple Israel, 112 E. 75 St.
