
        HOENSHELL-R. Craig. Former President of American Express International and also served as former Chairman and CEO of Avis, died at his home on July 1, 2002 of lung cancer at the age of 58 with his wife Lisa by his side. Lisa, his beloved wife of 14 years, was his champion, best friend and love of his life. He is also survived by his dear mother-in-law Harriet Haimowitz, his children, brothers and his newborn grandchild. Private services.
