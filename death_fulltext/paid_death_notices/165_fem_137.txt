
        SULLIVAN-Muriel L. Formerly of NYC, died Tuesday in Princeton, NJ. Mrs. Sullivan retired from the U.S. Naval Reserve as a Lieutenant & was a World War II veteran. She was a retired music teacher with the NYC Board of Education. The funeral will be held Saturday at 1 PM from the Lawrenceville Presbyterian Church. Friends may call on Saturday from 10AM to 12PM at Poulson & Van Hise Funeral Directors 609-396-8168. Burial will be on Monday at 11AM in Flushing Cemetery in Flushing, New York.
