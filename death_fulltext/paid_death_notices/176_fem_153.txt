
        ARKIN-Lillian Edith Goldman. 97, of Brooklyn, NY, died on Sunday, September 16, 2001 at Methodist Hospital after a short illness. She was predeceased by her husband Murray Arkin. Survivors include a son, Bruce Arkin of Pawleys Island, SC, and a daughter, Linda Arkin of Brooklyn, NY, two granddaughters, Valerie Catanzaro and Lori Kunkle and two great-grandsons, Matthew and Michael Catanzaro. Graveside services were held at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ on Monday, September 17, 2001. Memorials may be made to the charity of one's choice.
