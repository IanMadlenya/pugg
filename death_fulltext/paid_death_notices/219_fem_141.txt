
        SONN--Alice Cohen. Age 93, of White Plains, daughter of Samuel and Belle Ansbacher Cohen, sister of the late Paul A. Cohen. Wife of the late Howard Sonn. Mother of Edward Sonn of Carlisle, MA, Elizabeth Rose of Marietta, GA, Barbara Thanhauser of Stamford, CT, and the late William Sonn. Grandmother of six and great-grandmother of two. Services at Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St., Stamford, CT, Friday, April 22, 1:00 P.M. Burial private. Contributions in her memory may be made to Smith College Alumnae Fund, 33 Elm St., Northampton, MA 01063.
