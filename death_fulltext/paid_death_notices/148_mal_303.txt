
        WEINER-Ernest M., Dr. Beloved husband of Ethel, wonderful father to Robert and Diane, adoring grandfather to Daniel, David, Brian, Lauren and Steven, and dear brother of Bernard. Passed away on May 28, 1999, in West Palm Beach, FL. Ernie was an intelligent, warm, compassionate and tender person who gave freely to his family, friends, patients and colleagues. He was a pioneer and leader in his profession and his community. His many professional accomplishments include serving as President of both the New York State Podiatric Association and the American Podiatric Medical Association. After moving to Florida, he served as Chairman of the Council on Aging of the Jewish Federation of Palm Beach County and was a longtime member of the Board of Directors of the Joseph L. Morse Hebrew Home for the Aged. In whatever endeavor he pursued he set a standard of excellence, commitment and devotion. Services will be held at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave, at 9:30 AM, on May 31. Interment will be at Mount Hebron Cemetery.
