
        KILSTOCK-Phyllis. The Officers and Board of Directors of the American Associates, Ben-Gurion University of Negev, mourn the passing of our dear friend Phyllis Kilstock who together with her husband generously supported many programs at BGU over the years. We extend our deepest sympathies to her husband Kurt and to her children Debra, Frances and Richard; and to her grandchildren. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jules I. Whitman, President Edith & Solomon Freedman, Greater NY Regional Co-Chairs Bernard C. Moscovitz, Exec VP, AABGU
        KILSTOCK-Phyllis. The Officers, Trustees, and Staff of the UJA Federation of Bergen County & North Hudson note with great sadness the passing of Phyllis Kilstock, whose deep committment to the Jewish community enriched Jewish life worldwide. Our Federation's Women's Division was greatly strengthend by her generous support and leadership. We extend our condolences to her husband, Kurt, her son, Richard, and her daughters, Frances Aaron and Debra Joels, and the rest of her family. Eva Lynn Gans, President Dr. Ron B. Meier, Executive Vice President
