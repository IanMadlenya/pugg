
        KLEIN-Stewart, 66. The well known film and theater critic, of Fort Lee, NJ, formerly of Hillsdale, NJ, died May 10, 1999. He is survived by his wife, the former Barbara Bartenope; their son, Matthew; three children from his first marriage, Marjorie, Joseph and Joann; and two grandchildren. Mr. Klein is also survived by his brother, Victor, sister-in-law, Judy, and a niece and nephew. The family will receive their friends Wednesday, 3 to 5 and 7 to 9 P.M., at the Frank A. Entombment will follow at George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Actor's Fund of America, 1501 Broadway, New York, NY 10036.
