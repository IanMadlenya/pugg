
        DOUGLAS-Mimi. Loving wife of Spencer; devoted mother of Terese and Robert Schwartzwald; beloved grandmother of Amy, Alan and Michael. Sister-in-law of Shirley Zussman and Vera Lefkowitz. Died on April 8 in Boca Raton, FL. A graduate of Skidmore College, she possessed remarkable courage, intelligence, good humor and, best of all, love and devotion to her family. We will all miss her dearly.
