
        WOLFF--Dr. Roy I. Beloved husband of Harriett for almost 60 years, adored father of Natalee, Susan and her husband Stuart, loving grandfather of Bennett, Harris, Alex and Leah. Died in sunny Tucson, Arizona on February 25, 2006. He practice dentistry for 52 years in Manhattan. He will be sorely missed by family, friends and patients. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, February 28 at 12:00 noon at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York. All donations can be made to Hospice Care, Tucson, Arizona.
