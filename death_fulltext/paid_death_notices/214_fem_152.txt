
        HEYMAN--Claudia Susan. Of Boston and Tulsa, Oklahoma. Age 35, on November 13, 2004. Beloved daughter of Stephen and Barbara Heyman. Sister of Alexandra Nash. Granddaughter of Herbert Gussman. Beloved niece, cousin and aunt. Claudia loved learning, reading and her several pets. She always had the time to show her love and warmth to her family and innumerable friends and to help them with a problem. Services were held on November 18th at Congregration Ohabei Shalom in Boston, Rabbi Terry Bard officiated.
