
        DANIEL-Francis. Died suddenly and unexpectedly at age 67 on August 15, 2002, in Miami Beach, FL. Devoted husband for 22 years of Julie Gilbert Daniel and sonin-law of Henry Goldsmith & the late Janet Fox Goldsmith, he was a celebrated Acoustician, active in NABET for many years. His family deeply mourns his passing. Good night, sweet Francis, rest well.
