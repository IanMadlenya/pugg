
        DEWTON--Joseph (Pepi), an Austrian immigrant, died at the age of 95 at Vantage House in Columbia, MD., on June 8, 2005. He is survived by his wife of 71 years, Lilly Bing Dewton of Columbia and his daughter Ruth Eva Dewton of Columbia and Dillon, CO. Graveside services will be held May 10, 2005 at 3 PM at King David Memorial Gardens in Falls Church, VA. In lieu of flowers, remembrance may be sent to the Annual Fund Drive of the Marlboro School of Music in Philadelphia, PA. by contacting info@marlboromusic.org
