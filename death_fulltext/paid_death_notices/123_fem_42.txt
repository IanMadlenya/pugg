
        TOLMACH-Hazel Mitchell, R.N., beloved wife of the late Dr. Jesse Tolmach, died on March 26, 1997. She was a dedicated, loving mother and grandmother. Anyone who knew her can only remember her as a generous and kind friend. She will be deeply missed by all whose lives she touched. Mrs. Tolmach is survived by a son, David, a daughter, Peggy, and two granddaughters, Amanda and Johanna.
