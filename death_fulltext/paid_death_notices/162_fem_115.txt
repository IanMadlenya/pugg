
        KOCHEN-Beth. We note with sorrow the sudden and untimely passing of Beth Kochen, devoted wife of Joseph Kochen, M.D., Chief of Pediatric Hematology/Oncology. Members of the Department of Pediatrics Mervin Silverberg, M.D. Chairman, Dept of Pediatrics North Shore University Hospital
