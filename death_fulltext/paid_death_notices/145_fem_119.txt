
        SILVERMAN-Betty Sue Age 62, on February 9, 1999. Educational Assistant to Claire Shulman, Boro President of Queens. Beloved wife of Gene. Loving mother of Judi, Gail and Marsha. Nurturing grandmother of Hannah, Charlotte, Rachel and Rebecca. She was truly a Renaissance woman who touched many lives. She will be greatly missed. Donations in lieu of flowers to Betty Silverman Scholarship Fund, United Federation of Teachers, 97-77 Queens Blvd, Rego Park, NY 11375.
