
        BAHR-Henry, Dr. Of 355 Blackstone Blvd., Providence, RI died Wednesday at Rhode Island Hospital, Providence. He was the husband of Lore (Barth) Bahr, and the father of Dr. Robert Bahr of Providence, and the late Doris Bahr. He was the grandfather of Talia Goldfarb and Danielle and Jonathan Bahr. Interment will be held on Friday at 2:30 P.M., at the Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hasting on Hudson, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Henry & Lore Bahr Family Fund For The Promotion of Tolerance, C/O Mrs. Beth Raffeld, Development Office, PO Box 231, Williamstown, MA. 02167.
