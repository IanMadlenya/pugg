
        SAMMON-Gertrude. On May 4th, 2002. Beloved daughter of the late Henry and Ellen Sammon. Devoted sister or Rita Horkan of Westport, Ireland, and sister-in-law of Mary Sammon. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her and loved her. She was a private secretary at The Flagler Museum in Palm Beach, FL. Friends may call at the Abbey Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Tuesday 2-5, and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, May 8th, 10 AM, at St. John the Martyr Church, 250 East 72nd Street.
