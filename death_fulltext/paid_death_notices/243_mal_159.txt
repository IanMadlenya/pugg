
        GOLDMAN--Joseph B. (Jay) on April 12, 2007. Former New York State Acting Commissioner of Housing, retired Civil Court Judge and Assistant Corporation Counsel for the City of New York. Survived by loving daughters Amie Frances Goldman and Betsy Goldman Wallshein, adored granddaughter Samantha Wallshein and dear sister Betty Goldman Schlein. Remembered by his former wife Patti Hammer and longtime partner Carole Mitnick. Funeral Services will be held on Sunday April 15, 10am at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91st Street followed by interment at Mt. Judah Cemetery. Contributions may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
