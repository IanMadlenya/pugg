
        EISENSTADTER-Alfred, 86. Died February 13, 2003. Born in Germany, he risked his life by joining a resistance group to fight the Nazis before leaving for New York City in 1940. A lifelong believer in social justice, he was a devoted guidance counselor and a volunteer lawyer for New York Legal Aid, fulfilling his life's ambitions. He leaves his family with a sense of a life well spent. Friends are asked to contribute to the peace movement in Israel in the name of Dr. Julius Eisenstadter, a martyr of the Holocaust.
