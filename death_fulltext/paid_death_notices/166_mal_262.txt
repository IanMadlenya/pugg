
        MATEK-Stanley. The New York State Podiatric Medical Association mourns the passing of Stan Matek on November 19 in Chicago. Mr. Matek served as the Executive Director of the Association from 1983-1993. He left his mark by working with determination on behalf of our Association and our profession. He will be remembered fondly by those who knew him. Donations in his memory may be made to The American Cancer Association.
