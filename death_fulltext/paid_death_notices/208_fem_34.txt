
        BUZBEE--Ellen Wade, on May 5 at her home in Manhattan at the age of 70. Member of the New York Bar, arbitrator and former president of the Scarsdale School Board. Beloved wife of John, dedicated mother of Lynn, William and Stephen, adoring grandmother of Tian, Seana and John. Graduate of Bronxville High School, Wellesley College and Columbia Law School. Services at St. Bartholomew's, Monday, May 10 at 2:30PM. Gifts in her memory may be made to the Ellen W. Buzbee 1955 Scholarship Fund, Wellesley College, Wellesley, Mass. 02481.
