
        EINHORN-Dora. Of Stamford, CT. On July 24, 1999, age 95 years. Predeceased by her husband Dr. Walter Einhorn. Survived by her loving family, nieces Diane Feldman and Judy Waldman, her nephews Gilbert Waldman and Michael Goldhamer, six grandnieces and nephews, one great-grandnephew. Retired NYC schoolteacher. Funeral service Sunday, 10 AM, at the Ballard - Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery.
