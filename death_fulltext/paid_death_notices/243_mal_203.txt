
        WOLFF--Sanford I. We are profoundly saddened at the loss of Career Transition For Dancers' (CTFD) devoted and passionate board member and legal counsel Sanford I. Wolff. Better known as ''Bud'' to his many friends, he along with his wife, Ann Barry, Founding Director and Grants Administrator of CTFD, have seen CTFD flourish over a span of 21 years that reflects their long standing vision for the organization and its more than 3,300 dancer-clients. We extend our deepest sympathy to the entire Wolff family. Cynthia G. Fischer, President Alexander J. Dube, Executive Director Career Transition For Dancers $(6$)WOLFF--Sanford I. (Bud). The National Office of the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists, the 70,000 member union of professional performers and broadcasters, mourns the passing of our colleague, mentor and friend who led this union for 17 years as its chief executive. Sincere condolences to his beloved wife of 37 years, Ann Barry, a former dancer and AFTRA member.
