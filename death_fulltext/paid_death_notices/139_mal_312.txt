
        SHAPIRO-Philip C. Of Jackson Heights, NY. In his 81st year on August 26, 1998. Beloved husband of Mildred Lang Shapiro; cherished father of Marilyn Shapiro Bodner, Marc and Kenny Shapiro, and Dorleen Shapiro; adored grandfather of Jason and David Shapiro. Services at Parkside Funeral Home, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Rego Park, on Thursday, August 27 at noon. Shiva at the home.
