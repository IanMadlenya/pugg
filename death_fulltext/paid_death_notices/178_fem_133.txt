
        de ROTHSCHILD-Debra. Our hearts are weeping over the loss of our good friend, Debra de Rothschild, dear departed wife of our Executive Committee Member and Founders Society Co-Chairman, Robert de Rothschild, and mother of sweet little Jacob Alain. Debra brightened any room she entered, and was always a good friend. Our hearts are broken for Robert & the Rothchild and Cohen families. A beautiful person, a beautiful life, cut far too short. May her memory be for a blessing. Irving M. Rosenbaum, National Chairman A. Arthur Sherman, Founders Society, Co-Chairman Eric G. Heffler, Executive Vice President American Friends of The Open University of Israel
        de ROTHSCHILD-Debra. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband Robert and the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, CH of the BD Paul S. Schreiber, President
