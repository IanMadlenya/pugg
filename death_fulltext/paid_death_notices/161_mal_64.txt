
        CURRAN-James Leo. Died June 2, 2000, in his 77th year. Resident of Brooklyn, NY, he was a graduate of Columbia University and an accomplished pianist. He worked in public relations for many organizations including the United Nations, the Multiple Sclerosis Society and the Arthritis Foundation. He is survived by his nieces Linda Wexelblatt and Mary Beth Casey, his grandniece Ann Wexelblatt and his sister-in-law Alice Wood Curran. He was predeceased in 1997 by his close friend of 46 years, John Van Pelt Lassoe, Jr. Memorial gifts in his name may be made to the Heights and Hill Community Council, Meals-on-Heels Program, 160 Montague St., Bklyn, NY 11201.
