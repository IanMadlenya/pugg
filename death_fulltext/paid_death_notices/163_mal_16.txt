
        FOGELMAN-Douglas Edmund. Died on July 30th, 2000 at Huntington Hospital, NY after a valiant struggle with cancer. Born in Huntington Hospital on March 21st, 1960. Douglas was the beloved son of Suzanne and Prof. Martin Fogelman, cherished brother of Henry, Jeffrey and Martin, Jr. and sister-in-law Patricia, loving uncle of Kimberly, Ann and Martin III. Services 2 PM, Wednesday, August 2nd, Temple Beth-El of Huntington, 600 Park Ave., Huntington. Interment Mt. Golda Cemetery, Huntington Station, New York. Dr. Fogelman was dearly beloved by all who knew him. Donations to the American Cancer Society will be appreciated.
