
        PALMER--Edward L., of Mill Neck, NY, passed away peacefully on May 27th at the age of 88. He was the beloved husband of Margaret P. Palmer, his wife of 66 years, and the devoted father of Ned Palmer and Jane Palmer Morrison. He is also survived by his loving grandchildren, Edward L. Palmer II, Alix Palmer and Ben Morrison. A graduate of Brooklyn Academy and Brown University, he worked for the New York Trust Co. and for 23 years at Citibank, from which he retired as Chairman of the Executive Committee. He also served on active duty with the U.S. Naval Reserve 19421946 and attained the rank of Lieutenant Commander. Service St. John's of Lattingtown on June 3rd at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. John's of Lattingtown, Locust Valley, NY 11560 or East End Hospice, PO Box 1048, Westhampton, NY 11978.
