
        STEPHENS-Roy A. The staff of the Department of Dermatology at North Shore Hospital regrets the loss of their esteemed colleague. He was a superb physician and teacher and a gifted dermatologist. He had a warm and friendly outlook on life and his sense of humor was legend. He will be sorely missed by his patients and colleagues. Our warmest sympathy is extended to his wife, Barbara, and his family. Eugene L. Bodian, M.D. Chief, Division of Dermatology
