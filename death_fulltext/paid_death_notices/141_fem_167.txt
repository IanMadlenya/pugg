
        VERGARA-Dr. Allys Dwyer. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of Dr. Allys Dwyer Vergara, Class of 1924, Professor Emeritus of Speech and English. A caring, concerned, loyal friend and advocate for almost 80 years, she lovingly and unselfishly shared her long, rich life with others. We mourn her loss and celebrate her life. Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU Chair, Board of Trustees Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D President
