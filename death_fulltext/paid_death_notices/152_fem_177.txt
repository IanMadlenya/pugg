
        RICE-Adrianne Kathryn ''Puff''. Of New York and Morristown, NJ. Age 61, on September 20th, after a long illness. Beloved mother of Marc Nicholas Rice and loving companion of Joseph F. Mansfield, Sr. Survived by her brother Francis X. Rice, Florham Park, NJ, fifteen nieces and nephews and twenty-three great nieces and nephews. Daughter of the late Dr. Franklin and Kitty Rice of Morristown. Graduate of the Convent of the Sacred Heart, Eden Hall, PA. and Marymount College, Arlington, VA. Vice President of Pisa Brothers Travel for 22 years. Travel agent to celebrities, corporate executives and friends for 35 years. Founder, in the 60's, of the social gatherings known as ''The Friday Evenings'' in New York City. Visiting hours on Wednesday, 7-9 PM and Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Doyle Funeral Home, 106 Maple Avenue, Morristown. Mass of Christian Burial at the Church of Christ the King, Blue Mill Road, New Vernon, N.J. on Friday at 10:30 AM. Interment private. Memorial at the Convent of the Sacred Heart on Monday, September 27, at 6:00 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Convent of the Sacred Heart, One East 91 St., NY, NY 10128.
