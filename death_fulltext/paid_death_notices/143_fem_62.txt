
        MICHEL-Loschka. Of Whitestone, New York, and Bridgehampton, L.I. died peacefully in her sleep on November 26 at the age of 96. A well known caterer and patroness of the arts, she leaves a wide circle of friends and aficionados. Married to the late Maximilian Michel. She is survived by her son, Frank Michel, her son and daughter-in-law Henry and Mary Elizabeth Michel and three loving granddaughters, Eve Musette Michel-Milanese, Alix Michel, Ann Elizabeth Michel-Wilde and greatgranddaughter Ava Elizabeth Milanese. A memorial service will be planned for spring 1999. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, New York 11920.
