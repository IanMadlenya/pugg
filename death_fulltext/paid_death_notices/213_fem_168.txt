
        PATSURIS--Mary B., on October 19th. She was 74. Beloved daughter of the late John and Katina Bardasis. Dear cousin of relatives in Greece. Mary had a love for Native American Art. Her greatest accomplishment was educating children for many years in the NYC school system. Reposing Riverdale Funeral Home, 5044 Broadway at 214 St., NYC, Monday 10-11 AM. Service 11:30 AM. Interment Pinelawn Memorial Park, Farmingdale, NY.
