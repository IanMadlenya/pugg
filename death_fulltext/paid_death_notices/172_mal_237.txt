
        LEVINE-Solomon. We mourn the passing of our cherished friend and colleague who graced these companies for more than fifty years as a bus driver, officer and member of the Board of Directors. Sol was wise, compassionate and understanding. He brought intelligence and thought to the problems of urban mass transportation. His boundless energy, impeccable dress, honor and refinement brought dignity to our lives. He will be sorely missed. Our condolences to his family. The Board of Directors of Green Bus Lines, Inc. Triboro Coach Corp Jamaica Buses, Inc. Command Bus Company, Inc. Varsity Transit, Inc.
