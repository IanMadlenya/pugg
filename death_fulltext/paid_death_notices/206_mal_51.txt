
        PAPERTSIAN--Harry Levon, February 25, 2004, of Riverdale, NY, son of Hagop and Olive Papertsian. WW II veteran who dedicated his life to the mentally disabled. Survived by his wife Joyce and children Harry Andre, Lisa, Michelle, Matthew and Mark. Memorial service at Dobbs Ferry Lutheran Church, Saturday March 6, 2004, at 10:30 AM. Donations may be made to the Living Memorial Fund, Dobbs Ferry Lutheran Church, 914-693-0810.
