
        GOLUB-Pearl Sara (Fader). On January 3, 1998, of Riverdale, NY, beloved wife of the late Irving, devoted mother of Harvey, Eugene B., and the late Morton, also survived by ten grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. Relatives, friends are invited to graveside services Thursday 10:30 A.M. at Crescent Burial Park, Route 130 at Union Avenue, Pennsauken, New Jersey (Section E). For further information contact Berschler Funeral Home: 609-665-5401.
        GOLUB-Pearl. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the passing of Pearl Golub, beloved mother, grandmother and great grandmother. We offer our sincere condolences to her sons, Harvey (Roberta) and Eugene (Arlene), and the entire Golub family. We hope that your loving memories of Pearl will ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A.
