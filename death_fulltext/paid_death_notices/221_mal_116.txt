
        SALKIND--William. Died Friday June 10, 2005, at the age of 91 after a long struggle with Parkinson's and lung disease. William was a career statistician, working in both the public and private sectors. He also taught statistics at Stevens Institute of Technology and CUNY. William received a BA from City College of NY and an MBA from University of Chicago and he was a longtime member of The American Mathematical Society. He is survived by his wife Theda, his son Louis and daughter Laiki. He will be sorely missed.
