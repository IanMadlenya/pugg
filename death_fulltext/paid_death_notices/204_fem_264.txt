
        McDONALD--Tanny, died on January 25, 2004 at the age of 67 after a long battle with melanoma. She is mourned by her family, her many friends, and colleagues in the numerous theatrical productions in which her talents as actor and singer were given to audiences on Broadway, OffBroadway, and in theaters around the country. Tanny's was a dedicated life in the theater.
