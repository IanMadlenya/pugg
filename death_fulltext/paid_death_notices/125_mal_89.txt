
        LIVANOS-George P. The entire staff of the Nicholas Institute of Sports Medicine and Athletic Trauma (NISMAT) at Lenox Hill Hospital mourns the passing of George P. Livanos. One of the original members of the Advisory Board, his outstanding leadership and support through the years enabled the Institute to become one of the nation's premier sports medicine centers. As we prepare to observe NISMAT's 25th Anniversary, we honor, with the greatest respect and most solemn sadness, the memory of George P. Livanos and extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. James A. Nicholas, M.D., Director Stephen J. Nicholas, M.D., Associate Director Philip P. Rosenthal, Vice President, Operations
