
        WOODS--Henry C., Jr., age 84, died Thursday at the Medical Center at Princeton of Cancer after a brief hospitalization. He served his country flying for the U.S. Navy during WWII, and taught English for 35 years at The Lawrenceville School. Mr. Woods is survived by his wife Jane Cheney Woods and many nieces and nephews. Services are private. For information Cromwell - Immordino Me morial Home, 71 E. Prospect St, Hopewell, NJ 08525.
