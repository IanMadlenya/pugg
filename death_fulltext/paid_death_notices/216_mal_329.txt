
        LALLY -- John MacVean. John was born in Utica, NY, received his degree from Colgate University and attended Syracuse University. Was Creative Director of Sudler and Hennessey and then founded Lally, McFarland and Pantello. He was inducted into the Medical Advertising Hall of Fame. He is survived by his wife Gwenne and his son John Junior, and two grandchildren. He lived in Killingworth, CT, formerly from Brooklyn, NY and Brandywine, PA. Donations in his memory to the Killingworth Ambulance, 325 Route 81, Killingworth, CT 06419 or Killingworth Fire Dept, 333 Route 81, Killingworth, CT 06419.
