
        DONOHUE-Sylvia. Interment October 8, 1998 Arlington National Cemetery. Rabbi Bosch officiated. Jewish War Veterans, Post 58 were represented. She is survived by her husband Thomas, grandchildren, Rochele and Thomas Brockelman of Syracuse, Mira Brockelman of Oregon and great granddaughter Sophia. Mrs. Donahue assisted her husband in writing his book of his war experiences, ''Born Lucky or Unlucky'', which was published as a memorial for Mrs. Donohue.
