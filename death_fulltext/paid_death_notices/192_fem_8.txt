
        HILLSON-Mildred. 91, December 30, 2002 in West Caldwell NJ. Survived by Henry, her husband of 67 years, children Louise Werlin and Jean Ellis and grandchildren Jonathan, Rachel and Rebecca. Sister of Helen Harris and the late Dr. Leonard Harris. Cousin of Vilma and Mike Levine, Dr. Charles Harris and Vida Brender. She was a graduate of Packer Collegiate Institute and Wellesley College, class of 1932. She was an Engish teacher and college advisor at Midwood High School, Brooklyn and an art docent at museums in Brooklyn and Bridgeport, CT. Services are private.
