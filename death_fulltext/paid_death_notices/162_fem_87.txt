
        KARP-Ida M. The family of Mrs. Ida M. Karp regrets to inform you that Ida quietly passed away on July 5, 2000. She spoke often of her many interests and friends and we are grateful for all the love, sharing and support that her friends, colleagues and family members gave her. She will be missed but remembered fondly by us all. She is survived by her children, Winifred and William; and two granddaughters, Audra and Isadora. In lieu of flowers, the family request that donations be sent to the following: Bank Street College of Education; Council Senior Center; Horizons Elderhostel and Youth Programs.
