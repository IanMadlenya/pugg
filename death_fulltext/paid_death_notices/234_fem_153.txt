
        MONSANTO - FENNICK-Claudya Marion, an accomplished massage therapist died at age 47 on July 23, 2006 at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital of complications from Crohn's Disease and related liver failure. Marion, as she was known to family and friends, was born January 15, 1959 in Suriname to Nadia and Frits Monsanto. On March 14, 2006, she married her long time beau, Ronald Fennick. Marion is survived by her mother and father Nadia and Frits Monsanto, her brothers Arno and Patrick, nieces and nephews Johnathan, Crystle, Nathan, Tirze, Gamil, Loelle, Chavalli and Enzo, by her husband, Ronald Fennick, her brother-in-law, Robert Fennick and by friends too numerous to list. Funeral services will be held Thursday, July 27, 2006 at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St, NY, NY. Viewing from 11:00 a.m. 1:00 p.m. Memorial service will be immediately following at 1:00 p.m.In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made in Marion's memory to the National Transplant Assistance Fund (NTAF), 150 N. Radnor-Chester Road, Suite F120, Radnor, PA 19087. 1-800-642-8399
