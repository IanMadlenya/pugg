
        RIGER-Hilda Daughter of Harry and Jennie Riger, died peacefully in her sleep at the age of 87. Ms. Riger had a degree in nursing from Beth Israel and a Master in social work from Smith College. She worked for many years with the NYC Board of Education and volunteered for GMHC in its early years. She was loved by her many friends on the Upper Westside. Ms. Riger was the mother of the late Walt Melville and is survived by her granddaughter, Laura Melville and Great Grandson, Walt Melville Phillips. Hilda did not want a funeral. A memorial will be held for her in early September. In lieu of flowers, you may donate to Dorot or JASA. She will be missed by many.
