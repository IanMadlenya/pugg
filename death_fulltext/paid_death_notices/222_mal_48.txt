
        WIMPFHEIMER -- Kurt. 92. Devoted husband of the late Ruth. Beloved father of Steven (Ruth) Wimpfheimer and Judith (Zvi) Bodie. Loving grandfather of Lara (Sundar), Robert, Debra, Amy and Moriya. Adoring great grandfather of Ruby. Loving brother of Felix. Died peacefully on July 5. A humble man and a cherished friend, deeply devoted to his family, the Jewish community and the State of Israel. Services July 7 at 10AM, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave.
        WIMPFHEIMER--Kurt. Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. May his memory remain for a blessing. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman, Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft, President
