
        DUPEE-Martha. On December 24, 2001, sister of Mary McGurn and her husband George. She was a co-founder of the Madison Avenue Children's Christmas Fund and former Board Member of Partnership with Children and Millay Colony for the Arts. Martha's indomitable spirit in enduring her physical challenges is an inspiration to her many friends and colleagues. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Friday, December 28, 2-5PM, 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial on Saturday, December 29, 11 AM at Blessed Sacrament Church, 152 West 71st Street, NYC. Interment to take place on Monday, December 31, at Hillside Cemetery North Egremont, MA. Contributions in her memory may be made to MS Society Partnership with Children or The Millay Colony for the Arts.
