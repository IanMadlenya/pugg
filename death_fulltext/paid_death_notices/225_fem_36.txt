
        BENOV--Ruth Gordon. Died September 27, 2005 at 92. Born 1913 in Mariupol, Russia. She came to the U.S. in 1933. She opened a dance studio, gave her heart and soul to special needs children with her dance and became the director of the Lorge School for Special Needs Children. She is preceded by her husband Paul, daughter Bonnie and sister Lettie. We will miss and love her forever. Memorial services will be held Sunday, October 9, 2:00 P.M. at the Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave.
