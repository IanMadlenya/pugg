
        ASHNER--Bernard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Bernard Ashner, beloved father of Michael Ashner and father-in-law of Susan Ashner, treasured leaders of the New York Jewish community. Michael and Susan's boundless generosity and commitment, expressed in their support of the Bet Shemesh Educational Center of the Jaffa Institute, has enriched the lives of millions in New York, Israel, and around the globe. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Bernard's beloved wife, Peggy; to his children: Michael and his wife, Susan; to Joseph and his wife, Annette; to Judith and her husband, Leonard; to his grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
