
        ASENCIO-Maria. 9/6/39 to 8/6/97. Passed away Wednesday. Loving and devoted wife to Alfredo. Proud and adoring mother to Arlene, Deborah and Paul. Doting grandmother to Conor and Nina. She will be remembered for her generous spirit, caring soul and her love of laughter. Wake at Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court Street, Brooklyn, 2-5 and 7-10, Saturday and Sunday. Mass at 10 A.M., Monday, at St. Peter's and St. Paul's, 190 Court Street.
