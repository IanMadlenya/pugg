
        LERNER-Gertrude. On Sept. 19, 2000. Beloved wife of Harry. Devoted mother of Judith, Lee Berman, Alex and Saul. Dear mother-in-law of Richard Berman, Helena Wallenberg and Vivian Benci. Cherished grandmother of Rachel, Lindsay, Russell, Elliot, Austin, Faryn, Seth, Francesca & Maxwell. A courageous, loving and giving woman, Mrs. Lerner taught second grade at Yeshiva Torah Temimah, Brooklyn for over 30 years. A woman of grace and valor, she was a shining light who lit up the lives of everyone she came in contact with. Her spirit will live on in all those who were lucky enough to be a part of her life. Services Thursday, 1 PM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd. at 76th Road, Forest Hills.
