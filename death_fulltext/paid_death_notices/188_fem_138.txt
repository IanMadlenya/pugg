
        BELLET-Hazel S. Age 81, on September 19, 2002, in Belmont, CA. Formerly of Teaneck, NJ, and Delray Beach, FL. A microbiologist, she taught biology in the Teaneck school system for 18 years before retiring to Florida. Loving wife of the late Dr. Melvin Bellet, survived by brother Stanley Schnarch and sister Norma Schnarch Miller, her children Francine of Redwood Shores, CA, and David of New York City, and granddaughters Suzanne and Stacey Bellet. Graveside service Sunday, noon, at East Ridgelawn Cemetery, Menorah Section, Clifton, NJ.
