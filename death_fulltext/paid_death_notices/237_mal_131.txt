
        IMPERATO--Joseph Edward, M.D. Dr. Imperato loved life and knew how to embrace all the aesthetic and intellectual offerings of it. He was a physician and plastic surgeon and so much more;Conf longshoreman, merchant marine, jazz musician, importer of Italian paintings, world traveler, lover of museums, opera and his Greenwich Village neighbors. He is survived by his sweetheart, Suzanne McGovern, and his loving family and friends. Reposing at Perazzo Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., NYC. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday at 9:30 AM, Our Lady of Pompei Church. Interment Calvary Cemetery. Friends may call Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM.
