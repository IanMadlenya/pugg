
        FERRIS-Louis, M.D. Died on October 10, 2001. Beloved husband of Marie. Devoted father of Dina and Spiro. Loving grandfather of Alexander, Evan & Serena. Dr. Ferris had a distinguished career as a physician for 50 years. In 1970 His Eminence Archbishop Iakovos awarded Dr. Ferris the Medal of St. Paul for outstanding service to the Greek Orthodox community. He was loved and appreciated by the elderly at St. Michael's Home for the Aged, where he gave medical service since it was founded in 1953. In 1972, His Eminence awarded him the title Archon Actouarios and appointed him to the Board of the Holy Trinity Greek Cathedral, where he served for many years. Louis Ferris was a dedicated physician and humanitarian. He will be greatly missed. Funeral service will be held Saturday, October 13, 2001 at 11AM at the Holy Trinity Greek Cathedral, 319 E. 74 St., New York City. For information, contact the Riverdale Funeral Home, 212-942-4000.
