
        HAWKS-Graham Granger, M.D. February 15. Age 85. Survived by his wife Margery, brother Byron and children Ria and Graham, Jr. W.W. II combat infantry Regiment Surgeon of the 77th Division in Pacific Theater. Discharged as Lt. Col. Professor Emeritus Obstetrics & Gynecology & former Director of Obstetrics at New York Hospital Cornell Medical Center. Service Thursday, 4 PM, February 20. Brick Presbyterian Church, Park Avenue 91st Street. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Dept. of Obstetrics and Gynecology at the New York Hospital Cornell Medical Center, 525 68th Street, New York, NY 10021.
