
        MEYER-Jeffrey. A classical music expert who represented various recording labels such as Delos International, Albany Music, and Qualiton, died Saturday, June 30, 2001, at his home in Marietta, Georgia, after a long and courageus battle with cancer. Mr. Meyer, also a fine and rare antique jewelry broker of fine jewelry was key in providing law enforcement with breakthrough information leading to the return of millions of dollars of jewelry taken from the home of a prominent Palm Beach socialite. His life partner, Peter Bathhaung, his parents Samuel and Hermoine Meyer of Springfield, IL, brother Dr. Jon Meyer and wife Eleanor Meyer of Milwaukee, WI, a nephew David Meyer of Honolulu and niece Laura Meyer of Durham, NC, survive him. For further informaton, contact H. M. Patterson & Son Canton Hill, Marietta, Georgia.
