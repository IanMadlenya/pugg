
        VALEN-Herbert. Of Del Mar, Calif. (formerly of Westport, CT.). Died on January 27, 2001 at age 82. A former gag writer for The New Yorker magazine, he is survived by his wife, Felice Holman Valen, his daughter, Nanine Valen, son-inlaw Gerald Levinson, and grandsons Aaron Valen Levinson and Adam Valen Levinson.
