
        MILLER-Robert S. Jr. Loving husband, father, brother, and friend passed away on May 14, 2003. He leaves behind him a legacy of courage, strength, and laughter and will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Bob Miller is survived by his wife, Barbara; daughters, Kara & Daniela, and sister, Elizabeth. Anyone wishing to contribute may donate to the American Lung Association at 432 Park Ave. So. 8th Fl, NY, NY 10016.
