
        ETTINGER-Evelyn. In Walnut Creek, CA on October 4th at 85 years of age. Native of New York, moved to California in 1990. Wife of the late Charles Ettinger, mother of William (Sheryl) Ettinger and the late John (Karen) Ettinger, grandmother of Nicole Ettinger. Active in the March of Dimes, Hadassah and the National Council of Jewish Women. She read for the Literacy Volunteers of America. Services, Wednesday, October 7 at Menorah Chapel, 2950 Vaux Hall Rd, Union, NJ. Donations may be made to Hadassah.
