
        NEEDELMAN--Stanley D. Of Boca Raton, Florida died July 17, 2006. A resident of Florida for 20 years, Dr. Needelman formerly lived in East Hills, Long Island, NY. Executive Director of Mid-Nassau Guidance Center, Chief of Mental Health Services for Manhattan and Northport VA hospitals. Adjunct Professor at NYU, were he earned his PhD. Loving husband of Roslyn; father of William Needelman and Robin Zwicker: grandfather of Dawn, Brian, Caroline and Erik; greatgrandfather of Olivia.
