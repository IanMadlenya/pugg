
        HETTLING-Lillian Bassein. Formerly of Croton-On-Hudson, New York, died in Virginia Sunday, March 29, 1998. She was born in 1906, in the Bronx, NY, to Leon ''Elbe'' & Lizzy Goldberg Bassein. Lillian was the wife of the late Frederick E. Hettling, and is survived by 2 daughters, and 3 grandchildren. Those who knew her are invited to a gathering in her memory Friday, April 3, 1998 at 2 P.M. at The Tarrytown Hilton, 445 South Broadway, Tarrytown, New York.
