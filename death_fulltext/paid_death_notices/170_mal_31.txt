
        HERRMANN-Henry W. 90, loving husband of Alice, devoted father of Phyllis and Evie, adored grandfather of Scott and Amy, beloved son of the late Oscar and Rena Herrmann and affectionate brother of the late Dorothy Adams. Passed away on February 27, 2001 in Rocky Hill, CT. He was an enthusiastic member and president of the Babylon, Long Island Board of Education and a lifetime member of New York State Masonic Lodge #
