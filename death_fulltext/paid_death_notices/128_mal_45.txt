
        KATZ-Mo. Montefiore Medical Center sadly records the passing of a brilliant visionary, Mo Katz, former deputy director for policy and program development and vice president of planning at Montefiore. A highly respected hospital administrator at the medical center for twenty-eight years, he played a pioneering role in many of Montefiore's most significant social and medical programs. His wisdom, guidance and sense of humor will be missed by all who knew him. Our sincere condolences are extended to his wife, Mary Jane Koren, M.D., his children, and his entire family. Spencer Foreman, M.D., President Montefiore Medical Center
        KATZ-Mo. As advisor to our Health Care Program, Mo's experience, insight and judgement shaped our efforts to deal with the enormous changes taking place in his field. His tenacity was legendary. He was a humanist with broad interests in the arts, politics and literature and he was a true humanitarian whose concern for others was reflected in both his simple kindnesses to those around him and his profound concern for mankind. We will miss our colleague and friend so much. The Board of Directors and Staff The Fan Fox and Leslie R. Samuels Foundation, Inc.
        KATZ-Mo. The Adminstration and Staff of Beth Israel Medical Center and St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center and its parent corporation, Greater Metropolitan Health System, are saddened over the death of our dear friend, Mo Katz, a Senior Member of The Commonwealth Fund Staff. Our sincerest sympathy is extended to his family and loved ones. Robert G. Newman, M.D., President, GMHS Ronald C. Ablow, M.D., President, SLRHC Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President, BIMC
