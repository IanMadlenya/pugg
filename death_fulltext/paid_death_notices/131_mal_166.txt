
        ATTIE-Joseph N., MD. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Joseph N. Attie. Dr. Attie was a renowned head and neck surgeon. He was a consultant emeritus with the Division of Head and Neck Surgery at Maimonides Medical Center serving as Director many years ago. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Warren Wexelman, MD. Pres. Jacob Shani, MD. Pres. Elect David Feldman, MD. Secy. Sal DeGliuomini, MD. Treas.
        ATTIE-Joseph N., M.D. The Maimonides Medical Center Family notes with sadness the passing of Dr. Joseph N. Attie, esteemed surgeon and friend. In addition to his role within the Department of Surgery, Dr. Attie demonstrated a generosity of friendship and support to our Medical Center. We extend our condolences to his wife Muriel and family. Martin D. Payson, Chairman Stanley Brezenoff, President Joseph N. Cunningham, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Surgery Warren Wexelman, M.D. President, Medical Staff Society
        ATTIE-Joseph. It is often said that no one is irreplaceable. Joe Attie was the exception to prove the rule. To have known him was a privilege and an honor that only those who knew him can understand. He was a most genuine and charismatic person. His greatness and humility was obvious to all of us long before his untimely passing. Our condolences to our aunt Muriel and our cousins, Jeannie, Alice, Ilana and Kenny. Mariel, Vivienne, JoAnn and Isaac Shalom
