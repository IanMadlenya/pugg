
        SHORE -- Dorothy Kimmel man, (of Philadelphia), died peacefully on September 10, 2003, a truly elegant lady, beloved wife of the late William Shore, devoted mother of Madge Shore Miller, of New York City and Joan Shore Steinhouse, of Franklin Lakes, NJ, cherished grandmother of Jennifer Miller Symonds, Darcy Miller Nussbaum, Jonathan Ian Steinhouse, Peter Leigh Steinhouse and Nancy Steinhouse Rosenthal. A great grandmother to seven great grandchildren. Relatives and friends are invited to graveside services Sunday, September 14, 2003, 12:30 PM at the Roosevelt Memorial Park, (Section E) Trevose, Bucks Co. PA, (Just north of Philadelphia). The Millers will receive family and friends on Monday and Tuesday, from 4:00PM to 9:00PM, at their home, and Dr. and Mrs. Steinhouse will receive family and friends at their home on Tuesday from 4:00PM to 9:00PM. The service will commence at 8PM. She was greatly loved and will be sorely missed by all who knew her. The family respectfully requests that contributions in her memory be made to The Friends of Rittenhouse Square, or to the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation. Joseph Levine & Son www.levinefuneral.com
