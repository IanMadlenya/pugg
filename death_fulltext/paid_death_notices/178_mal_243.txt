
        GOUNAROPOULOS-Nicholas. Beloved husband of Maria, father of Jason and Liza, grandfather of Alexander and Aphrodite, brother of Alkistis & Marika. Died Friday, November 16, 2001 at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 74 and lived in Manhattan with his wife. Born in Cairo, Egypt of Greek parents, Nicholas proudly served the United Nations for over thirty years in posts in Europe, the Far East and Africa. A seafaring aquarian who lived life on his terms, he will be remembered and missed by all who came in contact with him. In lieu of flowers, please donate to your favorite charity. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, November 18th at 152 East 28th Street, between Lexington and 3rd Avenues. Hours 2-5 PM.
