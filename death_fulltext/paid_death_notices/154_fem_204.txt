
        WILLIAMS-Margaret Morrill., 77, died on November 21, 1999 at the Hilton Head Medical Center and Clinics, Hilton Head Island, SC. A life long resident of Bronxville until 1981, she was active in a variety of social, civic and charitable organizations, including Planned Parenthood of Westchester, the Vestry and Altar Guild of Christ Church in Bronxville, Musical Therapy (precursor to The Song Pipers), the Junior League of Bronxville and the Westchester Cotillion Committee. Survived by her five children: Centes C. Williams of Bronxville, NY, William L. Williams, Jr. of Brentwood, TN, David M. Williams of New York City, Margaret W. Loftus of Scarsdale, NY, Abbe W. Winn of Atlanta; six grandchildren; her sister Centes M. Papes of Mt. Kisco, NY and her brother David C. Morrill of Tuscon, AZ. She was preceded in death by her husband William L. Williams. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Bronxville, NY on Saturday, December 11, 2PM. Memorial contributions to Planned Parenthood HudsonPeconic, 11 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, New York 10532 or Christ Church, 17 Sagamore Road, Bronxville, New York 10708.
