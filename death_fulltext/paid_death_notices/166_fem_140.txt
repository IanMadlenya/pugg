
        GREENBLATT-Pessie. 97. Devoted wife of the late Julius; beloved mother of David and Bernice Weiss; Lily and Morty Veron, Kathy and Herman Klein and Robert Greenblatt. She leaves behind many loving grandchildren, great-grandchildren and a greatgreat-grandchild. A Holocaust survivor, she came to America to reestablish her family, a goal that with God's help she readily attained. She will be sorely missed.
