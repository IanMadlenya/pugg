
        EDEY-Helen, Dr. The Directors and Staff of The Scherman Foundation are saddened by the loss of our dear collegue Dr. Helen Edey. Helen was a passionate advocate for the environment, an indefatigable fighter for women's reproductive rights and a strong voice for human rights and liberties. Her feistiness and thoughtful contributions to our deliberations will be missed. Our condolences to her children and grandchildren. Axel Rosin, Chair Emeritus Karen R. Sollins, Chair Sandra Silverman, President
