
        MILLS--La Roi, 89 years old, of New York City, died September 19, 2005. World traveler and true food connoisseur, he will be greatly missed. Preceded in death by his wife Emelda. He is survived by sons Steven and Richard, five grandchildren and one greatgrandson. The family will receive friends on Sunday, October 2, between 2-4 PM, at 700 Columbus Avenue.
