
        REEDY-James H.. Died January 3, 2000 at the age of 92. He retired in 1972 as Senior Vice President and a Director of Allied Stores Corporation. He was married for 64 years to Marie M. Reedy until her death in 1997. He was the father of Barbara Fraser, Margaret Hayes, James H. Reedy, Jr., and the late Virginia Baxter, and leaves six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. A funeral mass will be held at 10:45 AM, January 7 at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church in Scarsdale, NY. Visitation will be from 4-7 PM on January 6 at the Bennett Funeral Home in Scarsdale, NY.
