
        JOGODNIK--Richard, 70. Died on February 19, 2006. Beloved husband of Victoria. Father to Carrie Cohen, Benjamin Jogodnik and Amy Warshawsky. Father-in-law to Stephen, Jennifer and David. Grandfather of Brandon, Aaron, Leah, Jessica, Lina, Matthew and Kaya. Brother to Larry and his wife Barbara. Uncle to Nicholas and Gregory Jr. and good friend to many. He was predeceased by his first wife Lillian. He took care of all those he loved. He died as he lived, with strength and dignity. Richard dedicated his life to the health and well-being of animals as a veterinarian, owner of Stamen Animal Hospital, New Rochelle, NY, for 40 years. He was an avid fly fisherman, who found a close and loving community with those who shared his passion. He was on the Board of Catskill Fly Fishing Center and Museum in Livingston Manor, NY, a member and past President of the Croton Watershed Chapter of Trout Unlimited, and member of the Angler's Club of NY, the Atlantic Salmon Federation, and the American Museum of Fly Fishing in Manchester, VT. Services today, 12 noon, at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mount Vernon, NY. Donations can be made in Richard's memory to Trout Unlimited, PO Box 298, White Plains, NY 10602, or the New Rochelle Humane Society, 70 Portman Rd., New Rochelle, NY 10801. May his spirit live on a stream forever.
