
        STECHLER-Zygmunt. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a respected member of the community; beloved husband of Helen; father of Joseph (and Gail) Stechler-member, Yeshiva College Board of Directors-and Miriam (and Danny) Ravitz. We extend heartfelt condolences as well to the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        STECHLER-Zygmunt. National Jewish Outreach Program mourns the passing of Zygmunt Stechler, beloved husband of Helen and father of Joseph and Gail Stechler and Miriam and Danny Ravitz. A Holocaust survivor, Zygmunt Stechler's devotion to Judaism has inspired his son Joseph, a devoted member of the NJOP Board of Directors and daughterin-law Gail in their many Jewish communal endeavors designed to insure a Jewish future for American Jewry. Yehi Zichro Baruch. Rabbi Ephraim Z. Buchwald, Dir.
