
        LETO-Andre. 59. Of Fort Lauderdale, FL. Died November 2, 1998 at home. Son of the late John J. and Helen Leto of Port Washington, NY. After a courageous battle with acute leukemia. A former resident of Manhattan and Vice President of Prudential Bache Securities. Mr. Leto was an alumni of New York University. He leaves a brother and sister-in-law, John and Mary Lee Leto of Manhasset and his devoted companion of 36 years, Richard Eskilson.
