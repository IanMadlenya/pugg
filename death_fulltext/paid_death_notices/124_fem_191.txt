
        BERNE-Selma. Passed away peacefully after a prolonged illness on May 27, 1997. Loving mother of Karen Rook and Elliot Stetzer. Beloved grandmother of eleven grandchildren and great grandmother of four with two on the way. We were all blessed to have her in our lives. Services scheduled for 11 A.M., Thursday, May 29, at Riverside Nassau North Funeral Chapel in Great Neck. All who were lucky enough to know her are welcome.
        BERNE-Selma. We extend our deepest condolences to all the members of her family. Gedale B. Horowitz, Chairman David R. Dantzker, MD President and CEO Long Island Jewish Medical Center
