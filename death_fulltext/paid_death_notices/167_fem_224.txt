
        MCEVER-Georgia (Johnson), 53, of 198 Weatogue Road died December 24, 2000 at Hartford Hospital in Hartford, CT. Georgia was born January 20, 1947 in Oak Park, IL., a daughter of Alma Knight Black of Good Hope, GA and the late Lawrence Edward Johnson of Oak Brook, IL. Georgia is survived by her devoted husband H. Bruce McEver of Salisbury, CT and NYC, NY; sisters Suzanne Johnson Allan of Ft Worth, TX and Holley Johnson Lanahan of Baltimore, MD; and loving nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, December 27, 2000 at 2:00 PM in the Congregational Church of Salisbury, Main Street, Salibury, CT. Memorial donations may be sent to the Georgia McEver Endowed Scholarship for Voice, % Opera Department, The Peabody Conservatory of the City of Baltimore, 1 East Mt. Vernon Place, Baltimore, MD. For information, call Newkirk-Palmer Funeral Home, 860-824-7350.
