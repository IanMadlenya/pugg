
        GEARY-Donald D. The Board of Directors, Officers and National Office staff of the American Society of Corporate Secretaries extend their deepest condolences to Don's beloved wife Ann and the entire Geary family on the death of Don, our devoted leader and President from 1981 to 1991. Don embodied leadership, dedication and service. We are grateful for his time with us. The American Society of Corporate Secretaries Karl R. Barnickol, Chairman David W. Smith, President
