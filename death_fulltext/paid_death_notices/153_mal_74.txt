
        MARK-Edward. 95, of Manhattan, formerly of Great Neck, NY, died peacefully on October 3rd, bringing to a close a long, productive and loving life. He cherished his wife of 45 years, the late Libbie Berman Mark. He leaves his children, Judith Tanur and Reuben Mark, and his grandchildren, Rachel Tanur, Marcia Tanur, Lisa McLoughlin, Peter Mark and Stephen Mark. He will also be deeply missed by the spouses and loved ones of his children and grandchildren, Arlene Mark, Michael Tanur, Thomas, Elizabeth, Victoria and Anna Karin. His circle of loving care also extended, in addition to his great-grandchildren, to his nieces, nephews, grand-nieces and grand-nephews. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, October 7, at 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Please omit flowers.
