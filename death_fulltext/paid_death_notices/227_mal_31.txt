
        FREILICHER--Jack. Died November 30 at 89. Beloved husband of Frances. Caring father of Mel, Bruce and wife Nancy and David. Grandfather to Emily and Luc. After a successful career as President of Rite Foam Sleep Products, he enjoyed his retirement in Florida. His warmth, good humor and wit will be truly missed. Services at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, New York, N.Y. Sunday at noon.
