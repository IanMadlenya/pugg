
        WELLENS-Josephine Harrington died peacefully at home among family in Fremont, CA on March 30, 2003. The daughter of Irish immigrant parents, Jo grew up in Manhattan, the third of five children. She met the love of her life, Arthur Wellens, while working at the Normandy Theater. They married and moved to CA in 1948. Family and friends will remember Jo for her delicious pies, her love of yellow, and a pragmatic but always positive outlook. Her daughter Judi, son-in-law Scott, longtime friends Corinne and Stan James, nieces, nephews and friends, will always miss her. A Memorial fund has been established at Holy Name School, 207 West 96 Street, NYC 10025.
