
        NAMAD--Vicky. Artist, teacher and renaissance woman. Died peacefully on May 28th, 2005. She is mourned by her three sons Ronny, William and Frank; her grandchildren Michelle, Tina, David and Tanya; her brother Max Salama of Egypt. An inspiration to everyone and loved by all. Services Monday, 10 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
