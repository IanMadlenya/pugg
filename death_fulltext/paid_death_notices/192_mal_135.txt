
        COCHRAN - George Van Brunt, MD. Died January 6 at home in Ossining NY. Born in Brooklyn, he attended Poly Prep, Dartmouth and College of P&S, Columbia University. He was Director of Orthopaedic Engineering Research Center, Helen Hayes Hospital, West Haverstraw, NY. He had a lifelong interest in mountaineering, and was President and Honorable Director of the Explorers Club, and past president of Campfire Club of America, and Amateur Ski Club of New York. He is survived by wife Caroline, children Linsay, Alexander and John. A spring memorial service is planned. Donations in his memory may be made to the Conservation Fund of the Campfire Club, or Exploration Fund of the Explorers Club.
