
        RUDKO--Samuel. Of Riverdale, NY. Died October 6th, 2003 in his 94th year. Beloved husband of the late Bess Rudko for 67 years. He leaves his two sons, Robert Rudko and his wife Nancy of MA, and Andrew Rudko and his wife Joyce of NYC. He had four grandchildren, Susan Listman, Rebecca Rudko, Jane Rudko and Michael Rudko. Great-grandfather to Lauren Rudko. He was Works Service Manager at Otis Elevator and was a Director of The Riverdale Senior Services. After retiring, he spent his time giving lectures, travelling and enjoying his family. He will be remembered forever. Funeral will be held, Thursday, October 9th, at 11:30AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave and 76th St. Donations may be made to Riverdale Senior Services, 2600 Netherland Ave, Bronx, NY 10463.
        RUDKO--Sam. We celebrate the life and mourn the passing of our devoted Board member. Sam, a charter member and generous supporter for 30 years will be deeply missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his sons Bob and Andy and their families. Batya Levin, President Ellen Camerieri, Exec. Dir. Riverdale Sr. Services, Inc.
