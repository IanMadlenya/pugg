
        BERMAN-Martin H., DDS. It is with great sadness and a deep sense of personal loss that we mark the passing of our esteemed colleague, Dr. Martin H. Berman, a loving, devoted and proud husband, father, grandfather, greatgrandfather, uncle and mentor to students of all ages. Marty served as Chief, Division of Prosthodontics, Department of Dentistry, for over 20 years enriching the lives of all who were fortunate to cross his path. His legacy shall endure in all of our hearts. Frederic R. Kunken, DMD Chairman, Department of Dentistry, North Shore University Hospital/Manhasset
