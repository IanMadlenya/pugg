
        HEFFNER-Howard M., 81. Died after a brief illness at his home in Carlsbad, CA. on February 17, 2003. With him were Rosella, his beloved wife of 60 years, their sons Kenneth and Scott, and daughters-in-law Susan and Sherri. A native of New York City who during WWII was in the Army Air Force, Mr. Heffner served on the Carlsbad Traffic Safety Commission, as President of the Sea Cliff Homeowners Association, and as a member of the League of Women Voters.
