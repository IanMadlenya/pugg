
        WATERMAN-William B., Dr. Age 82, of Southern Pines, North Carolina, formerly of New York City and West Hartford, Connecticut died Saturday at his home due to complications from leukemia. His family mourns the loss of a beloved husband and caring father. Dr. Waterman is survived by his wife, Marjorie; a sister, Marianna Shepard of Essex, CT; children, Suzanne Kathryn Lijek of Arlington, MA, Laura Stagg Jackson of Hinesburg, VT, Richard William Waterman of Sudbury, MA and Guy Randall Schultz of Boca Raton, FL and seven grandchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife, Patricia Thompson Waterman. Dr. Waterman was graduated from St. George's School, Harvard University ('41) and the College of Physicians and Surgeons at Columbia University. After discharge from the army as a Captain in the Medical Corp and a residency at Roosevelt Hospital, he practiced internal medicine in New York from 1950 to 1963. From 1963 until 1980 he was Associate Medical Director of Aetna Life in Hartford, CT. Dr. Waterman was a Fellow of the New York Academy of Medicine and a member of the American Medical Association, the Hospital Graduates Club, the New York Academy of Sciences and the New York, Connecticut and Hartford County Medical Societies. A private service will be held at Swan Point Cemetery in Providence at an unspecified date.
