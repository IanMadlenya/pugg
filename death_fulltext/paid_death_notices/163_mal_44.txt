
        HALL-William C. Age 63. Of Beaufort, SC, formerly from Bayport, NY, on July 29, of a brainstem tumor. Loving husband of Carol; dear father of Kerry, Brent and Jocelyn and spouses William Flye, Dr. Theresa Hall and Benjamim Suglia. Adored grandpa of Austin, John, Caitlin, Connor and Gage. Mr. Hall, an insurance broker with Marsh & McLennan, Inc., for 31 years, five of those at BowringLtd. in Hamilton, Bermuda. He then joined AEIA as National Marketing Manager, until retirement. Donations to the Bayport United Methodist Church or Sayville Cabinet for the Sick would be appreciated.
