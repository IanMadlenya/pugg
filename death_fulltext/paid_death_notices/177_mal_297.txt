
        WEINSTEIN-Frederic D. A former professor and academic dean, died on Monday, October 15. He was a writer as well as being a composer and pianist. He and his wife Suzanne divided their time among Boston, London, New York and Maine, where they were active in environmental causes and artistic philanthropy. Mr. Weinstein is survived by his devoted wife Suzanne, his daughter Amanda Kate Schwab and his former wife Arlene J. Weinstein. Burial was at Mount Auburn Cemetery. Contributions may be made to the Massachusetts Chapter of the Nature Conservancy, 205 Portland Street, Boston, MA 02114.
