
        CRUGER-Mary Sales. On Wednesday, October 7, died at the Ramapo Manor Nursing Home after a lengthy illness. Beloved wife of the late Bertram de Nully Cruger, KBE. Beloved mother of Maryvan Pendl and mother-in-law of Ulrich Pendl. A memorial service will be held at Saint Mary's in Tuxedo on Saturday, October 10 at 12Noon. She was greatly loved.
