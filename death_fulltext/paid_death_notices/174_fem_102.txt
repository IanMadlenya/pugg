
        BLOCH-Annette. On July 12, 2001 in her 95th year. Loving mother of Judy Ringel. Cherished grandmother of Jill and Jennifer and her husband Luciano. Devoted friend of Herb Behrens and caring companions ''Ragane'', Josie and Patricia. Longtime dedicated teacher and supervisor in the NYC public schools. Positive, strong, young in mind and outlook. Graveside service Sunday 10AM Salem Fields Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
