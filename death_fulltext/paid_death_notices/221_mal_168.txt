
        RYGER--Simon. Simon (Isidor) Ryger of Belle Harbor, NY, died on June 18, 2005. Beloved and devoted husband of Frances, his lifelong love of 53 years, loving and caring father of Jack and Risa, father-in-law of Roni and Peter, and adoring grandfather to Carlin, Maris and Haley. A Holocaust survivor, he showed his children and grandchildren how to live life with love, dignity, optimism and passion. He will remain a part of our lives forever. Services will be held at Plaza Memorial, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 92nd Street, NYC, at 10:30 am, Monday, June 20. Donations can be made in his memory to the U.S. Holocaust Museum.
