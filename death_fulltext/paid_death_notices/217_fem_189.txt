
        FREDERICKS--Joan. On February 18, 2005. Beloved mother, sister and friend. Ms. Fredericks was a 30 year veteran of The New York Times Magazine and the widow of Pierce G. Fredericks, who also worked at The Times. She was born in Dayton, Ohio, on March 25, 1929, and graduated from Flora Stone Mather College of Case Western Reserve. She is survived by her daughter Mariah, sister Beverly Schindler and her many friends. Donations in her memory may be made to The New York City Ballet. Mass will be held at Holy Trinity Church, 10 a.m. on Monday, February 21. For more information contact ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
