
        PRESCOTT--Lilias W. Died September 26, 2006, age 98. Beloved wife of the late Orville Prescott, daily literary critic of ''The New York Times'' for many years. She was past President of the New Canaan, CT Sewing Group, past Chairman of the Friends of the New York Philharmonic in New Canaan and an active volunteer in other local organizations. She was a graduate of Smith College, Class of 1930. Survived by her daughter, Jennifer Prescott McLean of Chappaqua, NY, five grandchildren, and six great - grandchildren. Also predeceased by her son Peter S. Prescott. No public services. Contributions may be made to the New Canaan Library, 151 Main St., New Canaan, CT 06840 or the New Canaan Sewing Group, P.O. Box 215, New Canaan, CT 06840.
