
        SANRICH-Fanny. Of Roanoke, Va. Passed away Wednesday, January 14, 1998. She was born on October 25, 1920 in Paris, France. A daughter of Adolph and Alice Blumenthal. She was preceded in death by her husband, Norman Sanrich. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law Sylvia and Paul Shoenfeld, grandchildren, Eric and Brooke, and brother, Pierre Blumenthal. A graveside service will be held at 11 A.M., on Friday, January 16 at the Temple Emanuel Cemetery in Roanoke. The family requests in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the American Cancer Society or the Alzheimer's Assn. Oakey's Funeral Service, Roanoke, Va.
