
        DRISCOLL-William Nott. Age 80. Of Plymouth, MN. Died Sunday, May 11th, 1997. LaRose School, Switzerland, The Hill School, Yale, 1940, U.S. Navy Pilot during W.W. II, Board President United Theological Seminary, New Brighton, MN, Board President, Minnesota Outward Bound School. Survived by sister Jessica D. Dean, his wife Judith Russell Driscoll and children Rosalyn, William, Jr., Peter, Thomas and Conrad. Services at 3 P.M., Thursday, Saint Martin's By The Lake, Minnetonka Beach, MN. Inquiries David Lee Funeral Home, 612-473-5577.
