
        GREENBERG-Judith Hoffman. Born 1920 in Chicago. Died on June 14. University of Chicago class of '47. She lived 50 years in Greenwich Village. Daughter of the late Dr. I.M. Greenberg and Claire Hoffman, sister of the late Suzanne Paul. Survived by her brother and sister-in-law Dr. Nathaniel and Anita Greenberg, four nieces Deborah of Anchorage, AK, Rachael Gross of Evanston, IL, Sharon & Rebecca of Chicago, two nephews Stephen Paul of Sacramento, CA., and Jonathan Paul of Garrett Park, MD, three grand nephews and one grand niece. She was active in civil rights, environmental and landlord-tenant issues.
