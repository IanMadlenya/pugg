
        CHEN-Harry. The Board of Directors, staff and clients of Hamilton Madison House are saddened by the loss of Harry Chen, our valued Board member. As a Board member since 1979, Harry was committed to the thousands of clients served by the Hamilton-Madison House. We extend our deepest condolences to Pauline, his wife, and the entire Chen family. Donations in loving memory of Harry Chen, can be sent to Hamilton-Madison House, 50 Madison Street, New York, New York 10038.
