
        ERWICH-Carrie (nee Goldfarb). Wife of the late Oscar Kaiser. Died peacefully August 18. A brilliant, beautiful woman who inspired all who knew her. Adored by her children Ellyn and Dr. Burton Bronsther, and the late Dr. I. Howard and Ethel Kaiser, her eight grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren. She taught us all about the beauty of life and the duty to share with others. Services at Boulevard-Riverside, Hewlett, L.I., Thursday 11:30 AM. May her soul be bound forever in the Book of Life.
