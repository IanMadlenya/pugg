
        AARON-Charles. On April 23, 1999. The most devoted husband of Pearl. Most beloved father of Deborah Aaron and Jay Cantor. Cherished brother of Nettie Katcher and Ruth Franklin. Charles was a resident of West Caldwell, N.J. for 43 years. He received a Master's Degree in Mechanical Engineering from CCNY in 1936; he also held a Professional Engineer's degree. He held eighteen United States patents in printing and converting machinery. Services Sunday, April 25th, 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, N.Y., N.Y.
