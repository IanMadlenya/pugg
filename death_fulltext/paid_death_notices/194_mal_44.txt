
        KESSLER-Gerald. The Abraham Joshua Heschel School community mourns the passing of Gerald Kessler, father of Susie Kessler, father-in-law of Peter Geffen, our Founder. He was a devoted husband to Mollie, as well as a devoted son, father, brother, uncle and grandfather. May his entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Shira Nadich Levin, President, Board of Trustees Roanna Shorofsky, Head of School
