
        LOEB-Nellie (nee Levi). Peacefully on August 11, 2002 in Washington Heights, N.Y. Born in Neuhof, Germany, April 19, 1905. Beloved wife of the late Max, her husband of 45 years; sister of the late Jennie Kahn and the late Meita Levi. she is survived by her brother Leo and her nieces Ruth and Marion. We will miss her elegant style, her love of the arts, and her independence of spirit. May she be remembered in peace.
