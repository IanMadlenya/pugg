
        FIELDS - Eleanor. Passed away on June 17, 2006, at the age of 92. Ellie was a woman of great vitality, intelligence and accomplishment. She was a guiding force in many worthy causes, travelled the world, and was adored by her family and friends. Survived by brother Joel Williams, son Jack and daughter-in-law Marilyn; grandchildren, Jonathan Fields and Lauren and Larry Talve, Douglas Nelson and Victoria and Jose Gordo; great-grandmother to Lindsay, Ashley, Zack, Veronica and Victor. Graveside services at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Tuesday, 2 pm. Family will receive friends at the home of Jack and Marilyn Fields, Tuesday and Wednesday, 4-8 pm. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the New York Presbyterian Hospital, 525 East 68 St, NYC 10021.
