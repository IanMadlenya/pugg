
        KLEIN-Martin D. Devoted brother of William J. and Joan L., on May 8, after a long battle with emphysema. Born in NYC, he was a resident of Cleveland area for many years. 10 years after working with General Electric Company he co-founded the interior design firm of Barnaby-Klein which the partners dissolved after 25 successful years. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be sent to Visiting Nurse Association Hospice of Cleveland, 2500 East 22nd St, Cleveland, OH 44115
