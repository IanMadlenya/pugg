
        HOLZMAN-Caryll. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Caryll Holzman our deepest hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        HOLZMAN-Caryll. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation EmanuEl sorrowfully records the death of our much loved honorary board member, Caryll Holzman, and extends sincere condolences to her family. Eve Haberman, President Elaine S. Ramson, Recording Secretary
