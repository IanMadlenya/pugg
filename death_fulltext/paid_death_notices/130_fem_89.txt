
        ADAMS-Annie. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University is profoundly saddened by the untimely passing of our much beloved and cherished friend, Annie Adams, from Montreal, Canada. A recipient of an Honorary Doctorate from Tel Aviv University and a member of its Board of Governors, she touched us with her personal warmth and enthusiastic dedication to the University. We mourn her death and cherish our memories of her kindness and compassion. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her devoted husband, Marcel, their children, Sylvan, Julian, Linda and Lenora and their families, and her mother, Shoshana Cohen. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Bernard Rapoport, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Robert J. Topchik, President Jules Love, Executive V.P.
