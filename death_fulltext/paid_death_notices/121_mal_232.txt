
        WEISS-David M., 89, of Boca Raton, FL., formerly of Lawrence L.I. and New York City, died on February 16, 1997. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Beatrice, daughters Cora Sue Simon and Jeannie Patricia Vine, sons-in-law David Simon and Dr. Ernest Vine, grandchildren Lainie J. Simon (Andrew Gross), Robin Vine Carpenter (Daniel), Charles Vine, and Skipper Vine, and great grandson Aaron Simon Gross. Dave Weiss was a long-time Resident Fur Buyer and was recognized in 1965 by the fur industry as Man of the Year. Beloved by his family and honored by his friends, he will be missed by all. Donations may be made to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, Florida 33486 or the American Heart Association.
