
        GERSHENSON - Rebecca. Died June 30, 2000, from Non-Hodgkins Lymphoma. Daughter of Hillel H. and Celia W. Gershenson. Sister of Simma and Anne, and sister-in-law of Paul and loving aunt of Eleanor. She was a graduate of South High School in Minneapolis and Columbia College, and a graduate student in history at Rutgers University. She will be deeply missed by her family and many friends. Funeral services will be held on Monday, July 3, 2 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Cure for Lymphoma.
