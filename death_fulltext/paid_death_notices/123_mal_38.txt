
        BARLOW-Carl M., M.D. The Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital mourn the loss of Carl M. Barlow, M.D., an Honorary Attending Physician in our Plastic Surgery Section. Dr. Barlow, who joined the hospital's medical staff in 1961, was a skilled plastic surgeon and a dedicated teacher who exemplified the highest standards of his profession. A compassionate and devoted physician, he will be missed by all who had the privilege of knowing him. We honor the memory of Dr. Barlow and extend our deepest sympathies to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George President and CEO Stephen A. Obstbaum, M.D. President, Medical Board Norman H. Schulman, M.D. Chief, Plastic Surgery Section
