
        FRANK - Morton. Passed away January 30, 2006. Beloved husband of Charlotte. Devoted father of Peter, Jeffrey and Jonathan; father-inlaw of Carol. Loving grandfather of Dara and David. Caring uncle to many nieces and nephews. Brave officer in World War II. Services Wednesday, February 1, 11 AM at Beth Israel Memorial Chapel, Boynton Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society.
