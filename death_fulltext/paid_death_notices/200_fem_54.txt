
        QUAN--Karen Sue (nee Shain). Age 45, passed away peacefully on September 2, 2003. She is survived by her husband David, her mother Maxine, her sister Sharon, brother Dennis, her father-inlaw Dr. Stuart Quan and mother-in-law Vicki Quan. Karen led a valiant and courageous fight against the cancer that took her life. She will be deeply missed. Burial arrangements are private. In lieu of flowers, a donation may be made to the Quan Fund in Neurobiology, C/O Patricia Flint, Harvard Medical School, 401 Park Drive, Boston, MA. 02215.
