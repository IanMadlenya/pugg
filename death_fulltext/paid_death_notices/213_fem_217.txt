
        HEIMBINDER--Evelyn, 90, on October 22, 2004. She was one of six children of Max and Fannie Brown. Predeceased by husband David. Survived by children Fani Weissman and Isaac Heimbinder, six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. She lived and loved well. Donations to the Max and Fannie Brown Family Memorial Fund, Kane St. Synagogue, Brooklyn, New York.
