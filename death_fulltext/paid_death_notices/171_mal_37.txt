
        GOTTLIEB-Harry A. Age 93. We mourn the death of Harry A. Gottlieb who died in Florida on Sunday, April 1, 2001 where he had been retired for ten years. A founder of the firm in 1930, he practiced law in New York City for over 60 years. He was a unique person and attorney, and was loved and respected by all. Wikler Gottlieb & Howard
