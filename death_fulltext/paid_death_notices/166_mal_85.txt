
        LYNAUGH-Thomas J., 61, of Ridgewood formerly of Norristown, PA on November 6, 2000. Beloved husband of Susan nee Dickey, loving father of Brendan and Sean, and loving son-in-law of Frank and Amy Dickey of Dewitt, NY. Dedicated teacher and psychoanalyst in Manhattan and Boston. He was a member of the American Psychological Association, The Society of Modern Analysts and the William Alanson White Society. A memorial service will be held on Friday November 10 at 12:00 noon at the Unitarian Society of Ridgewood, 13 Cottage Pl., Ridgewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Valley Hospice 15 Essex Rd. Paramus, NJ 07652 or to the Boston Graduate School of Psychoanalysis, 1581 Beacon St., Brookline, MA 02446 would be appreciated. Services entrusted to the C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 306 East Ridgewood Ave., Ridgewood, NJ 07450.
