
        JONAS-Mitzi (nee Rosenstein). On March 8, at age 92. Beloved wife of the late Harry Jonas. Devoted mother of Gilbert Jonas and Flora Einhorn and dear mother-in-law of Joyce Jonas and Daniel Einhorn. Loving grandmother of Susan, Sharon, Jeffrey, Jillian and Stephanie, and great-grandmother of Jamie, Jared, Samantha, Danielle, Kelly and Charles. She died peacefully in her sleep. She faced life courageously and with compassion. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Mount Sinai Breast Health Resource Program in Manhattan.
