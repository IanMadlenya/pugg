
        RUTHRAUFF-Nancy Ewing. Died on December 12, 2001. Wife of the late F. Bourne Ruthrauff, sister of John H. Ewing and the late Blaine G. Ewing. Mother of Clare Thomas, John and Bourne. Grandmother of Abbey, William, Kieran, Kate and Lindsay. Great-grandmother of Grace and James. She will always be remembered for her love of family and friends, her wit, her loyalty and her strong will. She was a member of the class of '35 at The Chapin School, she was a life member of the Rumson Garden Club. Service at Holy Cross Roman Catholic Church, Rumson, NJ on Monday, December 17 at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Arthritis Foundation, or the Monmouth County Historical Society.
