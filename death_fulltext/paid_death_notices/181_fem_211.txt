
        DURDIN-Margaret (Peggy). Age 92, of pneumonia on February 12, 2002, at Silverado Senior Living, Encinitas, California. Beloved wife of the late F. Tillman Durdin, loving sister of Maude Hudson and Lena Albright, both deceased, and of brother Oscar V. Armstrong of Mitchellville, MD, & of informally ''adopted'' son Edward Chen of Hong Kong. Born in China of missionary parents, she spent many years in Asia, accompanying her husband, a New York Times correspondent and writing feature articles for various American publications. She also served as an assistant to the editor of the New York Times Magazine.
