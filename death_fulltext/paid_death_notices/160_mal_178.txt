
        ARKIN-Herbert. Baruch College mourns the death of our former colleague, Herbert Arkin, Emanuel Saxe Professor Emeritus of Statistics in the Zicklin School of Business. He served 43 years as a faculty member and Chair of the Department of Statistics in the Zicklin School of Business. He is survived by his sons Herbert, Jr. and Ronald, six grandchildren and three great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on May 20, 2000 in Marietta, GA. Sidney I. Lirtzman, Interim President
