
        STARK--Karen. Today would celebrate her 98th birthday. Named one of the ten best designers by Lord & Taylor in 1941, winner of the Fashion Critics Coty Award in 1952, the Cotton Award, the Men's Fashion Critics Award as Designer of the Year. Widow of Sanford, mother of Stephen and Joan, grandmother of 3 and great grandmother of 8. She is sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. HAPPY BIRTHDAY
