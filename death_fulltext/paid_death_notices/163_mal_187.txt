
        PAOLINI-Albert F. Of Easton, CT, died peacefully at home on Tuesday, August 15th. He was the husband of Barbara Paolini. Retired president of Tilcon CT, Inc., Mr. Paolini was involved in the construction industry for over forty years, formerly serving as Senior Vice President of D'Addario Industries. During his career he served as an officer of many professional associations and was highly regarded as a skilled labor negotiator. A graduate of Peekskill Military Academy and Rutgers University, he was a standout athlete throughout his school years. He had a distinguished record as an officer in the United States Navy during the Korean Conflict, serving as a frogman with the elite UDT#
