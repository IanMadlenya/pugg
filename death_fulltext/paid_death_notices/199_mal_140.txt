
        LANDIS--Howard (born Howard Lefkowitz, Brooklyn, NY). Passed away August 12, 2003 at age 82 at his home in Valley Stream, NY. Attended Brooklyn College and met his late wife Carol of 57 years. He was a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy and held management positions with Fairchild Publications, Welbilt Corp., Tadiran Corp., and Slant Fin Corp. Devoted father of Paul, Larry, Don and Sally Laufer. Loving grandfather of Sivan, Sharon, Dalia, Leia, Adam, Steven and Alanna. Stepgrandfather of Peter and Jacob. Services today at 1:30PM at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapels,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. 516-295-3100.
