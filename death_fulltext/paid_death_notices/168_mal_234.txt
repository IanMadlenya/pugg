        WILLINGER-Allan H. All of the colleagues and friends of the former Willinger Bros., Inc. express their deep sadness on the passing of one of the pioneeers in the field of aquatics. Allan's knowledge, enthusiasm and humor will be greatly missed. We express our deepest condolences to his wife Fen-Hui, his children Jon, Wendy and William and to the rest of his family.
