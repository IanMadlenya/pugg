
        AMATO-Charles A., 90. Former New York broadcasting executive, of Newton, MA., and Sarasota, FL, died Monday. He leaves adoring family: Ines (Romeo) his wife, a daughter and son-in-law Linda and David B. Loveman, and granddaughters, Jennifer Manthei and her husband Gregory, Rebecca and Miranda. He is also survived by a sister Mary Farina. A funeral will be held on Monday. Donations to Habitat for Humanity are appreciated.
