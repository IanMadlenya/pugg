
        PACHNER--Charles W., died April 3, 2005 at age 88. Loving husband of Janice for 57 years. Father of Donald, Bob and Joan. Proud grandfather of Max and Greg Pachner and David and Joshua Newman. Former Chairman and President of Frenkel and Co., Inc. Active in insurance affairs as speaker and teacher. Former Board member of Westchester Jewish Community Services and the Musician's Foundation. Interment private. Memorial Service 11AM Wednesday, April 13 at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, Harrison. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to WJCS Community Relations, 845 North Bway White Plains, NY 10603 or to the National Kidney Foundation, 30 E 33 St, NY, NY 10016.
        PACHNER--Charles. A respected, beloved Board member and friend for 27 years, Charlie shared his many strengths with WJCS, caring deeply about helping people surmount obstacles in their lives. His expertise enriched us and his warmth will be missed by all. Robert Stavis, President Alan Trager, CEO/Exec. Dir.
        PACHNER--Charles W. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Charles W. Pachner. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Janice, and the entire family. Patricia Sumergrade, Pres. Richard J. Bronstein, Secy
