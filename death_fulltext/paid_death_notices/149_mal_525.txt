
        PERSHING-John Warren III, Colonel. The Board of Governors and members of The Leash in NY note with profound sadness the passing of their fellow clubman, Colonel John Warren Pershing III. United States Army Reserve Retired on June 23, 1999. A former Governor of The Leash, Jack was an outstanding gentleman, sportsman and soldier. He will now rest in Arlington National Cemetery with his brother, Richard, and his grandfather, General of the Armies, John J. Pershing. The Membership of The Leash
