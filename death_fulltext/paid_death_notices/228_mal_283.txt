
        KING--Roger. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Roger King, a cherished friend of the New York Jewish community whose dedication and generosity strengthened our ability to serve those in need, profoundly impacting millions of lives around the globe. We extend our deepest sympathies to Roger's wife Louise, & to his entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
