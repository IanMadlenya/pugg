
        HESS-Clyde G. Jr., 76, of New Hampshire and Florida, November 13, 1999 in New Hampshire. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to a charity of ones choice. For information or to view an on-line memorial, go to www.phaneuf.net
