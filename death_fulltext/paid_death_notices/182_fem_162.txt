
        DeCESARE-Maria Reinhardt. Beloved wife of F. Bruce DeCesare, loving mother of 3-year-old Andrew Mars and 1-year-old Elizabeth Weld, and cherished daughter of Catherine Reinhardt Wellington and F. Carl Reinhardt, passed away on March 13, 2002, after a year-long battle against leukemia. Maria is also survived by her adoring brother and sister, John Peter Reinhardt and Elizabeth R. Bredahl, and her stepfather, John Wellington. Services will be held on Saturday, March 16, 2002, 10 AM, Central Presbyterian Church, 70 Maple Street, Summit, New Jersey. Reception will follow at the church. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The National Transplant Assistance Fund, Suite 230, 3475 West Chester Pike, Newtown Square, PA. 19073.
