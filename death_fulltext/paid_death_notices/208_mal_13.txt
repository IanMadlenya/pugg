
        GOMPERTZ--Fred, 80, on April 29, 2004. Born in Germany, he escaped Nazi persecution and immigrated to New York in 1939. Under his professional name ''Mr. Fred'' he achieved recognition for his innovative fur fashion designs and frequently appeared in the media. His customers were a ''Who's Who'' of 1960's pop culturefashion models, politicians and actors. On September 11, 2001 he was trapped in his home across from the World Trade Center until being rescued by his son. Beloved father of Jeffrey and Ronald. grandfather of Minna. Previously married to Rita as well as Guojian. Uncle of Carole, Kenneth, Mark, Sheryl and Steven. Brother of Albert and the late Ralph.
