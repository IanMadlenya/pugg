
        KORNFELD--Peter M.D., 81, died on February 2, in Palo Alto, CA. He is survived by his wife of 55 years, Carol; his children Robert (Carol) and Jane Bessin (Bob); grandchildren Julie, Jason, David and Jack. He was Clinical Professor of Medicine at Stanford University, formerly Clinical Professor of Medicine at Mount Sinai Hospital, NY. Donations may be made in his memory to HIAS or Mount Sinai Medical Center.
