
        ROSS-Maxwell S., DDS. Age 88, died on Tuesday, April 25th, in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Beloved husband of 65 years of Roslyn (nee Fassberg). Adored father of Gail Davenport, Paula Gladieux and Dr. Jeffrey Ross. Loving grandfather of Dr. Kenneth and Michele Gladieux, Stephen Melchior, Susannah Schaefer, Dr. Mary Ross-Dolen, Merrill, Dr. Michael and David Ross. Great grandfather of Kyle and Brandon Ross & Samuel & Hannah Dolen. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Scholarship Fund for Minority Medical Students at Albany Medical College.
