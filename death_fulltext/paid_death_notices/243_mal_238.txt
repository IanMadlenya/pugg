
        FEINBERG--Sidney, New York entertainment attorney died on April 20th. Mr. Feinberg was a graduate of Harvard Law School and a prominent attorney in the field of entertainment, representing many Broadway shows, as well as stars of stage, film, and TV. He will be mourned by family and his many friends. Interment private. Memorial service to be announced. Donations in his memory may be made to Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS. $(6$)FEINBERG--Sidney. The Dorothy Loudon Foundation deeply mourns the loss of their friend Sidney Feinberg, whose wise counsel, knowledge of the theatre and brilliant wit was unmatched. A loyal man, devoted to his friends and clients, and as a mentor to many young lawyers, he was patient and gave of his time tirelessly. We extend to his family, our deepest sympathy and condolences. Lionel Larner, Executive Director $(6$)FEINBERG--Sidney.
