
        SHAINWALD - Sidney. The Board of Trustees, faculty, staff and students of the New York Academy of Art mourn the passing of Sidney Shainwald, beloved husband of our esteemed Trustee, Sybil Shainwald, and a loyal friend of the Academy. Our heartfelt sympathies are extended to Sybil and her entire family. Randolph Lerner, Chairman David Schafer, President Stephen Farthing, Executive Director New York Academy of Art
