
        MELLI-Charles R., Jr. Age 59. Of Ridgewood and Sea Girt, N.J. Died February 26, 2002. Loving husband of Sheila O'Shea Melli. Cherished father and hero of Kathleen Mary Melli and the late Andrew Shea Melli (June 2001). Big brother and best friend of Mark A. and Jan Melli. Dear son of the late Mary (Corcoran) and Charles R. Melli, Sr. Dedicated son-in-law of Julia O'Shea. Devoted brother of Patrick J. Melli (1997), Francis E. Melli (1972), Regina Woods and Ellen Powley. Beloved uncle of sixteen nieces and nephews. Charles graduated from Seton Hall and received his LL.B. from Saint John's University Law School, and he was a Thomas Moore Fellow. His law career began in New York City with the firm of Reilly and Reilly. He was the Founder and Senior Partner of the law firm of Melli, Guerin and Melli of Paramus, N.J. He was a Fellow of the American College of Trial Lawyers. After being Trustee and Vice Chairman of the Board of Trustees at Holy Name Hospital, he continued his association as General Counsel to the Hospital. He was a strong supporter of Bergen Catholic High School in Oradell, N.J., having served as a member of the Board of Trustees and President of the Alumni Association. For many, many years, he chaired the Annual Golf Outing and major fund raiser for Immaculate Conception Seminary. He was a member of the New York Athletic Club, the Hackensack and Spring Lake Golf Clubs, and the Upper Ridgewood Tennis Club. Family and friends will meet Saturday, March 2nd at the Church of the Assumption, Emerson, N.J., for the celebration of the Mass of Christian Burial at 10AM. Interment to follow at George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus. Friends may call Friday, 3-9PM at the Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Ave, Ridgewood, N.J. The family has asked that, in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to the Sister Patricia Lynch Regional Cancer Center at Holy Name Hospital, 718 Teaneck Road, Teaneck, New Jersey 07666.
