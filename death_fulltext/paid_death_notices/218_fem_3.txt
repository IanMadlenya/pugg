
        SAGIK--Florence Juanita ''Nita''. Worcester, NY. 79, passed away peacefully at home on February 24, 2005. She was predeceased by her husband Dr. Bernard Phillip Sagik. Survivors are four children, Tamara and Stephen Sagik, Debra and Paul Banks; five grandchildren; one great-grandson; several brothers and sisters. Contributions may be made to the Worcester Emergency Squad, P.O. Box 191, Worcester, NY 12197 or the Bernard P. Sagik Memorial Scholarship Fund, c/o UTSA Development Office, 6900 North Loop 1604 West, San Antonio, TX 78249. For information, E.J. Skinner Co., 155 Main Street, Worcester.
