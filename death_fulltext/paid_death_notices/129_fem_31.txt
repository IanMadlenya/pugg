
        JANOVER-Lillian H., died peacefully in her sleep October 2, 1997 at age 95. Widow of the late Cyrus J. Janover. Mother of Robert H. Janover of Bloomfield Hills, Michigan and Evelyn J. Halpert of New York City. Grandmother of James J. and David J. Halpert and Laura Janover. Great grandmother of Samuel I. Halpert. Funeral private. In lieu of flowers donations to Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 East 70 Street, NYC 10021.
        JANOVER-Lillian H. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Lillian H. Janover our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
