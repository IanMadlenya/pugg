
        CORRIGAN--Consuelo Reyes. Of Bradenton, FL, age 76, died on February 28, 2006 of a heart attack. Known as Connie Reyes to her many friends in the art world. Longtime partner of the late sculptor Ronald Bladen. Loving mother of the late Paul and Ethan Corrigan, loving aunt of Trina Reyes and Peter Rienecker, dear friend of her separated husband Arnold Corrigan. Beloved by her grandnieces and grand nephew and her many art children. She will be sorely missed by her friends, colleagues and students in the ''art world''. Private services in Florida. Memorial service in New York City to be arranged.
