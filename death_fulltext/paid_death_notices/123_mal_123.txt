
        CURLEY-John F. Sr, of Ardsley on Hudson, NY, died April 8, 1997, age 84 after a long illness. Survived by his wife Anne, daughter Marilyn Heavener of Gaithersburg, MD, son John F. Curley, Jr., of Baltimore, MD, 4 grandchildren and a great grandchild. Retired partner, Paine Webber Jackson & Curtis. Services are private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, 622 West 168th Street, N.Y., N.Y. 10032 or Harvard Graduate School of Business Administration, Boston, MA 02163.
