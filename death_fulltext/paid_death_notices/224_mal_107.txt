
        HAHN--Philip J. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Philip J. Hahn, an esteemed friend whose contributions to the New York Jewish community and as a leader in the diamond and jewelry trade were marked by exceptional generosity and devotion. Philip's dedication, as well as the commitment of his other colleagues at M. Fabrikant & Sons, Inc. has enabled us to enrich the lives of those in need and strengthen Jewish life and learning around the world. We extend our sympathies to Philip's beloved sister and brother-in-law, Bobbie and Irving Rosenzweig; to his nephews and their families; and to his colleagues and friends. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
