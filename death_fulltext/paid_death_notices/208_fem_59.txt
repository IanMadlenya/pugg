
        MARKUS--Liza Renee. Of Chicago, IL, 33 years old, passed away on May 6, 2004, after a two year battle with metastatic breast cancer. Beloved wife of Joshua R. Markus of Chicago, IL. Loving daughter of Max and Susan Jarolem of Great Neck, NY. Devoted sister of Ilene Jarolem of Great Neck, NY, and of the late Jessica Jarolem. Dearest daughter-in-law of Bruce and Barbara Markus of Highland Park, IL, and cherished sister-in-law of Adam and Akiko Markus of Tokyo, Japan. Liza worked and volunteered for over ten years as a fair housing advocate to fight discrimination in the housing industry in Illinois and Wisconsin. Since 1999, Liza worked at CHAC, Inc. where, most recently, as the family self sufficiency team leader she assisted families to become self sufficient and reduce their reliance on housing subsidies. Funeral service Sunday, 9:30 am, at RiversideNassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY. Interment immediately following at Riverside Cemetery, Saddlebrook, NJ. A Memorial Service will be held Sunday, May 16, 2004, at 5 pm at Anshe Emet Synagogue, 3760 North Pine Grove Avenue, Chicago, IL. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation, in her memory, would be appreciated.
