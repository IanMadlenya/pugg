
        BARNES--Kenneth Silas, of Levittown, New York died Friday, August 15, 2003 at New Island Hospital. He was 85. Beloved husband of Madaline A. Hime. Loving father of Jolee Zola of Cambridge, Massachusetts, Jane Victor of Pittsburg, Kansas and Amy Fowler of Renton, Washington. Devoted uncle to Penny Ferone of Whitestone, New York. Devoted grandfather to Allison Nephew and Clarissa Victor. He is also survived by three greatgrandchildren, a great-grandnephew and great-grandniece. Mr. Barnes was born in Belvidere, New Jersey and spent his youth in Ames, New York. He served in the United States Air Force during World War II, participating in boming missions over Germany and France for which he received the Distinguished Service Cross amony other honors. He also served in India. After the war, he acquired his Master's in Library Sciences at Columbia University on the G.I. Bill of Rights. He and his family moved to Levittown, New York in 1953 to accept a job as Director of the Hicksville Public Library, where he worked until his retirement in 1992. He was a member of the American Library Association and the New York Library Association. He loved books, jazz and animals. He was proud of having devoted his life to public service. Memorial services will be held at 1 PM on Friday, August 22, 2003 at Evergreen Cemetery, Fonda, New York. Donations in his memory may be made to the American Parkinson Disease Association or to benefit libraries. For more information, Kobuskie-Konik Funeral Home 147 Bleecker St., Gloversville, NY 12078.
