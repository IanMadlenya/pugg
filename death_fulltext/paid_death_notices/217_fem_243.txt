
        TAPISSIER -- Audrey (nee Cooke) aged 83, died peacefully and gracefully at home with her family. Dearly loved wife of ''Taps,'' the late Dr. M. E. Tapissier of Notton, Yorkshire, England. Mother of Mary (Birch), Ruth (Grace) and Claire (Doherty); grandmother of Simon, Tom, William, Kate, Elinor and Charlotte. With love and thanks to all her friends in NY, CT and Montreal for their wonderful memories and generosity. Donations if desired to: Marie Curie Nurses at Home, C/O J. Lazenby and Sons, Wakefield, Yorkshire, England.
