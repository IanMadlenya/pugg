
        TRETHEWEY-Max. On Tuesday, May 22. In Greenwich, England. Beloved husband of Betty; father of Philippa Ettore of Greenwich, & Robyn Krauthammer of Washington; grandfather of Andromica, Daniel, and Guy. A Spitfire pilot in the Royal Australian Air Force during World War II, he lived in Australia & then Belgium. His final days were spent in England at the home of his daughter, where he died surrounded by family. He will be missed. Funeral and burial in Greenwich, England.
