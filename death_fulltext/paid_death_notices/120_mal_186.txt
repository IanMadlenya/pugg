
        KAUFMANN-Theodore, 87, of Southbury, CT., died January 15 at his home. He was husband of the late Johanna (Brod) Kaufmann. He retired in 1974 as Chairman of the Board of General Cigar Co. in NYC. Beloved father of Tony Kaufmann of Redding, CT., and Christine Kaufmann of NYC, and grandfather of Alexis and Matthew. Memorial service January 18 at 3 PM at Carpino Funeral Home, Southbury, CT.
