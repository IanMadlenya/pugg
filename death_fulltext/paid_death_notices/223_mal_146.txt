
        BJORNSON--Carroll Norman. Of 34 East Lyon Farm Drive, Greenwich, CT, NYC, and Ft. Lauderdale, FL, on August 10, 2005. He is survived by his wife, Edith McBride Cameron Bjornson, and daughter, Lisa Carol Bjornson Wolf of Santa Barbara, CA. A son, Karl Cameron Bjornson, is deceased. A memorial service will be held at the First Congregational Church of Greenwich, Sound Beach Avenue, Old Greenwich, on September 17, at 11 am. In lieu of flowers, a contribution to the American Merchant Marine Museum at the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy, 300 Steamboat Road, Kings Point, NY 11024-1699 will be appreciated.
