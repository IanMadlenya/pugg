
        GOLDMAN--Charles. The Board of Directors and Staff of FEGS Health and Human Services System deeply mourn the passing of Chuck Goldman, our dear colleague and friend. Our condolences to his wife Debra Rubenstein, their daughter Molly, his mother Edith, and to the Goldman family. Barry K. Fingerhut, President Joseph Stein, Jr., Chairman Al Miller, CEO
