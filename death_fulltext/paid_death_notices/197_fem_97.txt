
        DiMAGGIO--Helen. Daughter of deceased Marion DiMaggio and beloved sister of Robert J. DiMaggio passed away peacefully after a short illness at Roosevelt Hospital. Helen was an active member of civic affairs as well as a volunteer for St. Patrick's Cathedral, in honor of her adored St. Therese. Friends can best honor Helen by reflecting upon her love for her City New York where she resided for over 40 years. >>AD#
