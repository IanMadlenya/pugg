
        LaGRANGE-Isaac. On November 18, 1998 in his 96th year. Beloved husband of the Late Frances W. LaGrange. Loving father of Suzanne Ragland, John and Robert. Devoted Grandfather of seven and Great Grandfather of seven. Mr. LaGrange was the son of Isaac & Annie Fix LaGrange of Albany, N.Y. A graduate from Colgate in 1924, a member of Sigma NU Fraternity, a member of the Empire State Society of the Sons of the American Revolution. Mr. LaGrange lived in White Plains for 40 years and retired as an Officer of the Manufactures Hanover Bank, New York City after 42 years and moved to Stony Brook, L.I. in 1996 where he passed away at home. Reposing at the O.B. Davis Funeral Home, 4839 Nesconset Hwy, Port Jefferson Sta. L.I. Visitation Friday 2-4 PM & 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass 9:45 AM Saturday at St. James RC Church, Interment Albany Rural Cemetery.
