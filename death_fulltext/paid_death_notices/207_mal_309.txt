
        SCHWAB--Terrance W. Died April 25 in his wife's arms after a short, sudden illness, comforted in his last days by his wife Eileen, his children Matthew, Kate and Claire, Matthew's wife Johanna, Kate's husband Paul Welykoridko, and Claire's boyfriend Stephen Vogel. Also surviving him is his spectacular, much loved granddaughter Ainsley Welykoridko. He was a good man and will be sorely missed. A memorial service will be held at 10 am, on Wednesday, April 28, at the Church of the Ascension, 221 West 107th St. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that contributions be made in Terry's memory to Caramoor, 149 Girdle Ridge Rd, Katonah, NY 10536.
        SCHWAB -- Terrance. The Board of Trustees and staff of Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts note with profound sadness the passing of its longtime Board member, Terry Schwab. Terry's involvement with Caramoor lasted over three decades. He initially served as Caramoor's legal counsel and, for many years, was Board Secretary. Among his many important contributions to the institution was his key role 26 years ago in creating the Orchestra of St. Luke's as the resident orchestra for the Caramoor Music Festival. We offer our deepest condolences to his wife, Eileen, and his entire famly. Judy Evnin Chairman of the Board Michael Barrett Chief Executive and General Director
