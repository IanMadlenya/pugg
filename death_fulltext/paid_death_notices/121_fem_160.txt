
        BOHANON-Eunice Blake. Died February 16, 1997. Widow of Paul and long time resident of East 80 St. and New London, NH. Daughter of the late James and Helen Blake of New Haven. Survived by her cousins Alfred P. Putnam, Anne Morrish, Helen Lilly and Catherine Goodrich. Contributions in her memory may made be made to the Smith College Fund.
