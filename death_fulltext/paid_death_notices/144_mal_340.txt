
        DiPILLO-Vincent. The Board of Regents, Administration and staff of Long Island College Hospital (LICH) mourn the passing of Vincent DiPillo, father of our esteemed colleage Frank DiPillo, M.D., Chairman of the Department of Internal Medicine and a member of the Board of Regents. To Dr. DiPillo, the entire DiPillo family and all of Vincent's loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. John Wren, Chairman of the Board of Regents Peter A. Kelly, Interim President and CEO
        DiPILLO-Vincent. The Medical Staff of Long Island College Hospital (LICH) joins with the Board of Regents and LICH Administration in acknowledging, with great sadness, the passing of Vincent DiPillo, beloved father of our friend and colleage, Frank DiPillo, M.D., Chairman of the Department of Internal Medicine and a member of the Board of Regents. We offer our sincere condolences to Frank and the entire DiPillo family. Daniel D. Ricciardi, M.D., President, LICH Medical Staff
        DiPILLO-Vincent. The members of the Department of Internal Medicine of Long Island College Hospital express their sympathies to Dr. Frank DiPillo on the passing of his beloved father, Vincent. In these difficult moments for the Chairman of the Department of Medicine, his colleagues offer their continued support. To memorialize Vincent DiPillo's life and achievements, contributions may be made to the Othmer Cancer Center of Long Island College Hospital.
