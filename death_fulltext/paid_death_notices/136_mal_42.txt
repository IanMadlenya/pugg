
        RUBINTON-Joel J., Esquire. Age 69. On April 29, 1998. Former attorney and teacher, Administrative Law Judge, culture vulture, avid traveler and community volunteer. He will be sorely missed by his loving wife of 46 years, Natalie; his children, Jeff, Eric, David and Ellen; their spouses Sharon, Miriam and Joshua; grandchildren Samantha, Sarah, Jillian, Daniel, Brian, Max, Evan and Gabrielle, and many dear and loving friends and relatives. He loved living and was well loved in return. Memorial gifts to the American Heart Association would be greatly appreciated by his family.
