
        STARR-Louis. Age 91. Died on November 8, 1998. He is survived by his wife, Abbey Kissel Starr of Far Hills, N.J. and his brother, Ogden Parrish Starr of Antigua, W.I. Mr. Starr is also survived by his daughter, Frances Starr Todd and her children, Mary Starr and Christopher Bray and his daughter-inlaw Elizabeth Nelson Starr and her children, Christopher Parrish and Elizabeth Abbey. Mr. Starr was pre-deceased by his son, Dillwyn Thorn Starr, who died in 1988. Mr. Starr was associated with Laidlaw & Company in New York City. He was a Trustee of the Upper Raritan Watershed Association, a Trustee of Purnell School in Pottersville, N.J. and he belonged to Holland Lodge, New York City. Mr. Starr was a member of the Squadron A Association in New York and was a veteran of World War II in which he served as Lt. Colonel of the 8th Air Force in England. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Upper Raritan Watershed Association, Box 273, Gladstone, New Jersey 07934 or to the Tewksbury Foot Bassets, Box 31, Pottersville, N.J. 07979. A memorial service will be held on Friday, November 13, 1998 at the Lamington Presbyterian Church, Bedminster, N.J. at 10 A.M. Interment will be private.
