
        ROOS-Roberta, Bobbi, 74. On April 10th, 2002 in Sharon, CT. Formerly of London, England and Long Island, NY. Loving mother to Andrew, Peter and the late Julie Ann, mother-in-law to Susan and Elisabeth, sister to Professor Bill Friedheim. Adored grandmother of Alexander, McAlister, Kaile, Ethan, and Emma. Artist, proprietor of Ashley & Blake in London. An interesting and creative person who will be missed by her family and the many who loved her. Plans for a memorial service will be announced shortly. In lieu of flowers a donation can be made to The Marfan Foundation, 382 Main Street, Port Washingon, NY 11050.
