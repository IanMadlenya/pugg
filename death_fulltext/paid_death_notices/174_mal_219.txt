
        DETRICK-Bruce. Age 59, co-founder of the Tamarand Foundation, died June 30, 2001 of cancer. The Municipal Arts Society honored him in 1992 for roof gardens and play gardens created for NYC AIDS care facilities and for the Hope Garden in Battery Park. A composer, poet, teacher, singer and actor, his work appeared in books, on stage, on television and in films. His expansive body of work often met with critical acclaim. Surviving are his mother, Helen, and partner, Joe Mondello. Memorial Saturday, 12:45 PM, Church of the Transfiguration, 1 East 29th St. Contributions to Tamarand Foundation, 202 Riverside Drive, 7D, New York, NY 10025.
