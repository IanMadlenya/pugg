
        MEADE--Thomas A., Sarasota, FL, formerly of Lloyd Harbor, NY, and Manchester, VT, October 18, 1914 -April 13, 2007. He served as a Captain in the US Army during World War II, in the Pacific. He was corporate Vice President and Group Executive for AMF Bowling Products Group, retiring in 1980. Survivors include wife Frances, daughter Mary Ruggles, two granddaughters and five greatgrandchildren.
