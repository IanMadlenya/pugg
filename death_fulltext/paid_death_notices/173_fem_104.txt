
        GOLDMAN-Tillie. Born in 1906, Tillie was a sophisticated child of the new century, an All American girl who loved NYC, FDR, George Gershwin and Central Park. We'll miss her. Survived by daughter, Barbara Londin, sons, Marc and Tony Goldman, daughter-in-law, Janet, grandchildren, Victoria, Rick, Robert, Cara, Jessica, Scott, Joey, Thea, Charlie, Lydia and Jonathan, great-grandchildren, Sophie, Zoe, Jake and Joshua. Benefactor of Brandeis University, the Israel Museum, Yeshiva University, Metropolitan Museum of Art and Kibbutz Eilon.
