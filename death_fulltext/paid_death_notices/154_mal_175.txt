
        TIMBERLAKE-Shelby V. Jr. 80, on November 11, 1999, in Pelham Manor, NY. Beloved husband of Mary; devoted father of Laurie, Pam, and Liz; and three grandchildren; predeceased by his sister Betty. Memorial service at Huguenot Memorial Church, Pelham Manor, NY, November 17, 1999, at 5 PM. Memorial donations: Williams College Class of 1942 and Huguenot Memorial Church.
