
        BECKER--Eugene. August 2, 1929-June 25, 2006. Beloved husband of Rae, devoted father of Eve and Margot, proud Bapa of Leo Liebeskind, former assistant principal violist of the New York Philharmonic, master musician, mentor, friend and paradigm to his many students and professional colleagues. A man of passion and compassion who touched and moved the world he inhabited. Services Monday, June 26, 12:45 pm, Riverside Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam.
