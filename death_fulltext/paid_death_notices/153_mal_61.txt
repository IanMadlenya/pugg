
        SILVERMAN-Sidney H., M.D. The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center records with sorrow the passing of Sidney H. Silverman, MD, distinguished and esteemed Emeritus Attending of the Department of Obstetrics & Gynecology. Dr. Silverman was affiliated with Brookdale for over forty years. Our condolences to his bereaved family. Edwin L. Schulman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Frank J. Maddalena, President and CEO Sheldon S. Berman, MD, Sr. Vice President and CMO
