
        HAMMOND-Michael P. The Purchase College community mourns the passing of Michael P. Hammond, an extraordinary Renaissance man who left an indelible mark at Purchase College State University of New York as a founding faculty member of the College and its Conservatory of Music. He was President of Purchase College and organized the Pepsico Summerfare festival. We extend our sincerest condolences to his family. Thomas J. Schwarz, President Purchase College State University of New York
