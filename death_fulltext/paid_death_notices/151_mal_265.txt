
        GREENSTEIN-Rabbi Joseph, at age 86, passed away peacefully on Saturday, August 21st, 1999. He was a passionate Serrant of Klal Yisrael, as Rabbi, educator, and communal worker. He is mourned & remembered by his loving wife, Esta, children, Helen and David, grandchildren, great grandchild, and family and friends. Shiva at 540 North 11th Street, New Hyde Park, New York.
