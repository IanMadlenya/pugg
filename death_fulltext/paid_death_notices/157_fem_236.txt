
        DANOFF-Judith (Ignatoff). Age 80, died February 14, in her 60th year of marriage to devoted Abraham. Fighter for humanity. Dedicated teacher, former Director of Early Childhood Education, Kingsboro Community College. Her spirit lives in her children, Saul (Valerie) and Ann, grandchildren (Kimmer, Zachary, Joshua, Sonya, Isabel), and dear friends and students whose lives she touched. Donations in her memory can be sent to Kingsboro Community College Foundation, 2001 Oriental Blvd., Brooklyn, NY 11235.
