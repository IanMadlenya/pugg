
        ATHA-Stuart K., Jr., on Friday, May 23, at home in Valley Cottage, NY. He was 71. Mr. Atha retired as a Senior Vice President from the Chemical Bank Co. Before joining Chemical he worked for Hanover Bank. He was a graduate of the Woodberry Forest School and received a BA from Princeton University. He served as a navigator in the Army Air Force during WW II. He is survived by his wife, Barbara; a daughter, Susan H. Atha of Valley Cottage; two sons, Peter W. Atha of Valley Cottage and Stuart K. Atha III of Darien, CT. Mr. Atha was predeceased by his first wife, Eleanor. Also survived by five grandchildren. Funeral service will be held on Wednesday at 10 AM at Hannemann Funeral Home, 88 So. Broadway, Nyack, with burial to follow in Litchfield, CT. Calling hours are at the funeral home on Tuesday, 2-4 and 6-8 PM. Donations in his memory to the United Hospice of Rockland, 5 Parkview Plaza, Thiells Mt. Ivy Road, Pomona, NY 10970.
