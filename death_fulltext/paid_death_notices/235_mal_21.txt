
        THAU--Howard. Died at the age of 73 on July 31, 2006 in Delray Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Cheryl, and devoted father to John and Paul, and daughters-in-law Cyndi and Amy. Cherished by his three granddaughters Amanda, Emily and Layken. His memory will live on in those who knew and admired his gentle nature and kind heart.
