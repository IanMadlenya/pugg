
        ROSENBERG-Gertrude. 99, died at her home in Mount Kisco on October 25, 1999. The widow of Max Rosenberg of Bedford Hills, she was a past President of the Senior Citizens of Mount Kisco and a devoted volunteer at Northern Westchester Hospital into her nineties. She will be missed by her grandchildren, great grandchildren, nieces and nephews. Ed & Ruth (Rosenfeld) Bellsey Edith & Ben Rosenberg
