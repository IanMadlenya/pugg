
        STIGLITZ-Annette. Congregation Rodeph Sholom notes with sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Annette Kesselman Stiglitz. In all her Temple activities she worked to ease pain and enhance dignity. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her husband, Joe, and their son, Andrew, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Jack B. Levitt, President
