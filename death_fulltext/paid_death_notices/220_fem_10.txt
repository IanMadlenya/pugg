
        BREED--Helen S., 94, on April 16, 2005, after a long illness. Widow of the late William C. Breed, Jr., mother of Helen S. Chinitz, Selina S. Croll and the late Janet S. Croll, grandmother of Clyde and Benjamin Chinitz, great-grandmother of Miles and Olivia Chinitz. A descendant of Peter Stuyvesant and Governor John Winthrop of the Massachusetts Bay Colony, she was an actress and a member of the Snarks, the Amateur Comedy Club, Actors Equity and the Screen Actors Guild, and appeared in numerous stage productions and motion pictures during her long career. She was also a member of the Cosmopolitan Club, the Colonial Dames of America, the Daughters of the Cincinnati and a women's flyfishing club. Memorial service at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue and 71st Street, on Monday, May 9th, at 11:00 a.m. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Actors' Fund of America, 729 Seventh Avenue, 10th Floor, New York, NY 10019.
