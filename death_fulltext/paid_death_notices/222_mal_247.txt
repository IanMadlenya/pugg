
        ROSENTHAL--Arthur A. July 17, 2005, passed peacefully at age 90 in St. Thomas, VI, formerly of Larchmont, New York. Former president of Rochester Button Company, 50 years in the button industry. Loving husband of the late Edna, beloved father of Arthur Rosenthal, Jr. and Myrna Golden, father-in-law of Judith Rosenthal & Jacob Golden, grandfather of Peter & Jonathan Rosenthal and Michael Golden, great-grandfather of Jesse and Rachel Golden. A beacon for his family and friends. Donations may be made to the Humane Society of St. Thomas, St. Thomas, VI 00802.
