
        HOWARD-Betty Gatoff. Of Rye, NY, on November 16, 2000, in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late Robert L. Howard. Cherished mother of Doris (Robert) Gilbert and Dr. Elliott (Ulla). Adored grandmother of Paige, Jon, Carl, James and Edward. Dearest Gammy of Jason, Randy, Haley, Ari, Marley and Dillan. Fondly remembered as ''Aunt'' Betty to generations of Camp Seneca in the Berkshires children. Services are private.
