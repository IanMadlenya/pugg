
        MANTON - Florence V. ''Gretchen,'' passed away on February 14, 2003, after a long illness. She is survived by her husband, Sir Edwin A. G. Manton; a daughter, Diana Morton; two grandchildren, Julia Kropf and Sandra Niles; and five great-grandchildren. Services are scheduled for 3:00PM, Saturday, February 22, at The Church of the Ascension on Fifth Avenue at 10th Street in Greenwich Village. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Church of the Ascension, 12 West 11th Street, New York, NY 10011, or to a charity of your choosing.
