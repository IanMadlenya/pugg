
        SIMMONS-Edward M. On February 15th at age 96. Beloved husband of Marjorie Michaels. Loving father of Wendy and Lynn Simmons, Nancy Steiger (Norman) and Marion Benedek. Beloved grandfather of Deborah Schnur (Daniel), Elizabeth Newman (Steven), Sue Ellen Gerber (Steven), Michael Benedek (Karen), and Elizabeth Kaye (Alan). Adored great grandfather of nine. Services February 18th at The Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah, Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, New Jersey, at 1 P.M.
        SIMMONS-Edward M. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Edward M. Simmons, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Paul N. Gardner, President
