
        RICHARDS--Helen (nee Gloster). Of Hackensack, NJ. 78, died peacefully in her sleep on March 26, 2004. Beloved wife to Vincent, devoted mother to David. She was predeceased by her brothers Amos and Charles. She was an editor for many years with New American Library, Putnam Publishing and Pearson Publications, Inc. A commemorative service will be held at a later date in New York City. She will be missed by all her family and friends.
