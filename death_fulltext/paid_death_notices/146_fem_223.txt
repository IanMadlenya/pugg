
        PECKHAM-Janet G. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Westchester Arts Council mourn the loss of their esteemed colleague Janet G. Peckham. Cherished friend and wise leader, she shared her talents cheerfully. Her quiet accomplishments during more than 10 years as a trustee leave an indelible mark on the Westchester Arts Council and the entire arts community. She will be profoundly missed. We extend condolences to her family and to those whose lives she touched. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the Westchester Arts Council
