
        FRIEDMAN-Jack, passed away September 3rd. Husband of Rae. Father of Robin and Mark Friedman and Eve Fox. Grandfather of Andrew and Alyson Fox and Hallie and Jon Friedman. Brother of Sylvia Schaefer. Service today at noon, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        FRIEDMAN-Jack. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Jack Friedman, September 3, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Friday, September 5 at Noon at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Alan King Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
