
        SOLOMON-Aaron. Died March 15, 2001 at the age of 93. Graduate of City University 1932 with a BS degree; from Columbia University School of Social Work 1946 with an MS degree. He valued his long career in social service agencies as an executive, activist, group and community worker, in Lynn, Mass, Philadelphia, PA and NY. On retirement he continued his professional involvement as a volunteer at Cabrini Hospital's Outreach Program for the Elderly and in Chelsea community programs, where he resided. He is survived by his wife Victoria Tabeek, his son Richard Allen and his wife Grace, and a granddaughter Amelia. He touched the lives of many and will be greatly missed.
