
        DAVIS--Lewis. The Juilliard community mourns the passing of Lewis Davis, architect of The Juilliard School's Meredith Willson Residence Hall, which rises above the Samuel B. and David Rose Building. With creativity and a wonderful wit, Lew was an extraordinary architect, who responded to Juilliard's needs with exceptional artistry. We send our condolences to his wife, Anne, and to his entire family. Bruce Kovner, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
