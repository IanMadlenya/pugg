
        MONTGOMERY-Royal M., M.D. of Silver Springs, MD on Friday, December 14, 2001. He was preceded in death by his wife of sixtythree years Maxine Cooley Montgomery and daughter Diane Greene. An eminent dermatologist with a practice in Manhattan for more than fifty years, he moved from Forest Hills to Leisure World in Silver Springs in 1984. Dr. Montgomery was nintyfive years old. He is survived by sons Andrew E. Montgomery of Eden Prairie, MN, R. Bruce Montgomery of Block Island, RI, his daughter Clare M. Davis of Virginia Beach, VA, their spouses, twelve grandchildren and thirteen great grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made in Dr. Montgomery's name to Church-inthe-Gardens, 50 Ascan Avenue, Forest Hills, NY 11375.
