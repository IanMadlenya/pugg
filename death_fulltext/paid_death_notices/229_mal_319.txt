
        DOKTOFSKY--Max, CPA. Of New York City. Died suddenly on February 26, 2006. A dedicated practitioner, he served his clients with integrity and devotion for over 50 years up until his death. Served with the U.S. Army in Europe during WWII. He was righteous and honest as a professional and as a human being. Beloved husband of Sara. Loving father of Michael and Andrew. Devoted grandfather of Na'ama and Daniel. Beloved brother of Sarah, brother-in-law of Phil and Luchy. Loving father-in-law of Ella and Melissa. Services at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., NYC, 11:00 A.M. February 28. He will always be remembered by his family and all who knew him.
