
        PERKIN--Richard T. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the Wildlife Conservation Society mourn the death of our friend, Richard Perkin. He was elected a Trustee in 1978 and served on a number of committees with particular interest in furthering the work of the Wildlife Health and Sciences Committee. Dick was devoted to conserving wildlife and wild places. He will be deeply missed. We extend our condolences to his wife Leslie and the Perkin family. David T. Schiff, Chairman Steven E. Sanderson, President
