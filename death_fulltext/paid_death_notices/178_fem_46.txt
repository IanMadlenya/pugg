
        BERGER-Barbara (nee Kleinman). Aug. 23, 1941 to November 5, 2001. Beloved wife of Leonard Berger. Loving mother of Lauren Berger, Dina & Dave Bortnichak. Stepmother to Amy and Lee Certilman, Betsy and Michael Moreno. Sister to Dennis and Ken Kleinman. Beloved daughter-inlaw of Frances Berger. Sister-inlaw to Joel & Hinda Berger, Jerri Sue and Geri-Mae Kleinman. Cherished Nana to Andrew, Reid, Alex and Henry. Caring aunt to Jana, David, Stephen, Scott and Jennifer Kleinman. Fought a heroic battle against cancer. Died Monday, November 5, 2001 at Lenox Hill Hospital, NYC. At long lastpeace. Interment Knollwood Park Cemetery beside her loving parents Jerry & Sylvia Kleinman. Funeral services on Wednesday, November 7, 2001 at 11:45 A.M. at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation. Nana and Pop-pop, she is coming home.
