
        FEINGOLD-Regina, 85, of Rancho Bernardo, CA. Longtime NY resident, retired to San Diego. Dearly loved wife, mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. She is survived by her husband William, daughter Shirley Howe, 3 grandchildren & 4 great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by her daughter Barbara Falk. Regina will be missed by the many people whose lives were touched by her kindness, thoughtfulness and love. She will be loved forever.
