
        SZLACHTER -- Frida. The Rodeph Sholom School Board of Directors, Adminstration and Faculty are deeply saddened by the loss of Frida Szlachter, beloved mother of our Headmaster Irwin Shlachter and extend heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Frida Szlachter was a woman of valor, compassion and determination whose life's journey made her a citizen of the world. She will be deeply missed. The Board of Directors
