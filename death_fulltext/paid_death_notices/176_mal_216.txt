
        BENDINER-Elmer. We mourn the death of author-journalist, Elmer Bendiner. Elmer died peacefully in his sleep on the morning of September 16, 2001. He will be missed by all those who knew his keen wit, his modesty and his intelligence. Elmer Bendiner's memory is cherished by his many friends and his devoted family, which includes Esther, his wife of 60 years, his brother Robert, daughters Winnie and Jessica, son-in-law Paul, grandson-in-law Kaushik and his granddaughter Gabrielle. A memorial tribute will be announced.
