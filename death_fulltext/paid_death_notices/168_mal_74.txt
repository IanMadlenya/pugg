
        SINGER-Norman. Age 72. Died on January 5, 2001. Survived by his wife Barbara and his children Clare, Ellen and Ben. Stepchildren Jeffrey (Kerry) Fine, Robin (Charles) Black & Stephanie Fine. Also survived by six sisters and one brother. He was a lover of music, the arts and an avid fisherman. Services will be held at ''Gutterman-Musicant'', corner of Passaic and Park Streets, Hackensack, NJ on Sunday at 10:30AM.
