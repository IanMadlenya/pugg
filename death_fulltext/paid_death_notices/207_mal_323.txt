
        SHULTZ--Seymour, 95, of NYC on April 26, 2004. Beloved brother of the late Ethel Cooper, and Lillian and George Blumenson. Loving uncle of Shiela and Joel Pollack, and Lester and Genia Blumenson. Grand uncle of Ellen and Bonnie, Craig, Stacey, Jeffrey, and Carolyn and their respective spouses and nine great grand nieces and nephews. Seymour worked for Paramount Pictures for over 55 years. A kind, loving, giving, and generous man he will be sorely missed by all who loved him. Services April 28, at 1 P.M. Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, NJ. Info Gutterman-Musicant 1-800522-0588
