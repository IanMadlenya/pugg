
        DICKE-Richard M., Sr. The National Center for Disability Services family sincerely mourns the passing of our dear friend and longtime Director, and former Chairman of the Board, Richard M. Dicke, Sr. A gentleman of unparalled humanity and generosity of spirit, his support, encouragement and enthusiasm helped lead our center to greater heights of service for all people with disabilities. Rick will be deeply missed by all of us who knew and loved him. We express our most heartfelt condolences to his children, grandchildren, family and many friends. Edmund L. Cortez, President And CEO and The Board of Directors, National Center for Disability Services
