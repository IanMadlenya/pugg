
        GUTTERMAN-Dolly L. Of Great Neck, NY, died April 12, 2003. She was the wife of the late Matthew M. Gutterman. Beloved mother of Merle and Frank Holstein and Charlotte Fishkind Leigh. Grandmother of Andy and Rusty Fishkind, Julie Saypoff, Cheri, Renee, Paul, Michele, David Holstein. Great-grandmother of Joshua, Larry, Shaina, Sarah Fishkind, Tali and Matthew Saypoff, and Jordan Holstein. Dolly and Matthew founded Gutterman Real Estate in Great Neck over 67 years ago. Rest in peace and watch over us all.
