
        LEBOUCHER - Fernande. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the passing of Fernande Leboucher. She demonstrated courage and compassion working together with Father Marie Benoit to save hundreds of Jewish lives in France and Italy during Word War II. The entire Jewish world owes her a profound debt of gratitude. We will never forget her heroism. Memory of the righteous will be a blessing. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
