
        STRIZVER-Jessie. July 24, 1904July 6, 1999. She died peacefully at her home, surrounded by love. Adored mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. She is survived by daughter, Evan Epstein, grandchildren, Mark Epstein, Gail and Foster Faerman, and great grandchildren, Christopher and Ryan Epstein; Jessica, Julie and Daniel Faerman. The family wishes to express deepest gratitude to her six ''angel'' caregivers. Services were held at the chapel of Temple Beth Israel, 5600 North Braeswood at 1:00 PM Wednesday, July 7, 1999, followed by interment at the Woodlawn Cemetery, Beth Israel Memorial Gardens, 1101 Antoine.
