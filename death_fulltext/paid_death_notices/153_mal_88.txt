
        USDAN-Leo J.,85. Formerly of Englewood, New Jersey, died at Monmouth Medical Center in Long Branch, NJ, on September 17, 1999. He is survived by his daughter, Adrienne, his son, James, former wife, Selma, and sisters, Beatrice Lieben, and Zipporah Epstein. Mr. Usdan was a graduate of Yeshiva University.
