
        ROTHBERGER-Fritz, Dr. 97, Wolfville, Nova Scotia, Canada. Died May 30, 2000. Born in Vienna, Austria, he was a son of the late Henry and Ella (Burchardt) Rothberger. He was a retired professor of mathematics. Dr. Rothberger led a distinguished academic career at Acadia University and at the University of New Brunswick, Laval and Windsor. He was an internationally known mathematician and an oustanding teacher, who instilled a love for mathematics in numerous young people. In 1977 a symposium was held in his honor at the University of Toronto. He is survived by his cousin Bertha L. Gutmann of Caldwell, NJ and her family. Burial in Gaspereau Cemetery. Donations to Acadia University, Wolfville, The Wolfville Elms Rest Home or the charity of choice.
