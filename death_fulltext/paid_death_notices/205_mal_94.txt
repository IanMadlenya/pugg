
        BERMAN--Carroll Z., M.D. On January 29, 2004 in Miami. Taught and practiced medicine for over 50 years. A founding member of the Society for Pediatric Radiology. Survived by his wife Mona, his daughters Marcie and Laura, and two grandchildren.
