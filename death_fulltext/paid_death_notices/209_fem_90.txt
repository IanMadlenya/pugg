
        ARLEN--Arleta of Manhattan, NY on June 13, 2004. Beloved daughter of the late Isadore and Becky Altman. Beloved sister, aunt and friend. Survived by her sister Lillian Winkel, 3 nephews and one niece. Ms. Arlen was a dancer for Billy Rose and performed in many shows with The Ed Carrol dancers. In later years she managed many well known supper clubs in Manhattan and was known near and far as the original Auntie Mame. Religious Service Tuesday 12 noon as St. Margarets Cemetery, Plainview. Rabbi Stephen Dresner Officiating. For information Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Homes, Hicksville Chapel.
