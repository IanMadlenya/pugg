
        ELY--Patricia (nee Brogan). Of Mclean, VA, formerly of Ridgewood and Spring Lake, NJ, died April 7, 2005, after a long illness. Adored daughter of the late Thomas J. and Florence Kerns Brogan. Beloved wife of the late Joseph L. Ely, who predeceased her in 1977, and devoted sister of the late Thomas J. Brogan Jr., and J. Peter Brogan. Loving mother of Joseph L. Ely Jr. of West Paterson, NJ; Thomas J. Ely of Franklin Lakes, NJ; Patricia E. Dunn of Dickerson, MD; Melinda E. Cornish of Silver Spring, MD; Ellen E. Daniels and Elizabeth E. Divine, both of Vienna, VA. Cherished grandmother of thirteen, and great-grandmother of three. Step daughter of Mildred S. Brogan. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 10:45 am, at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel RC Church, Ridgewood. Interment in Holy Sepulchre Cemetery, Totowa. Friends may call on Monday, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Alzheimer's Association, 225 N. Michigan Avenue, Suite 1700, Chicago, IL 60601-7633, would be appreciated.
