
        WHELAN-William Edward. On September 9, 1999. Deeply loved and cherished husband and best friend of Agatha Munhall Whelan went to the God he loved so much. Also survived by his sister, Mary Whelan Phelan and his brothers, Charles and Robert and 15 nieces and nephews and 33 grandnieces and nephews. Resting at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Viewing Tuesday, September 14, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of the Resurrection, Church of St. Thomas More, 65 East 89 St., Wednesday, September 15, 11 AM. Interment private.
