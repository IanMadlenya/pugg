
        RIZK-Khalil. The Metropolitan Opera Association mourns the death of our good friend and Member of the Association, Khalil Rizk. His commitment to our company and his service on our Archives and Art Committee were invaluable. We extend our condolences to his partner, Pierre Durand, his family and friends. Mrs. Gilbert W. Humphrey, Chairman Emeritus Bruce Crawford, Honorary Chairman James W. Kinnear, Chairman Paul M. Montrone, President Mrs. Ezra K. Zilkha, Vice Chairman
