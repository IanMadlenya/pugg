
        BAKER--Norman H. Professor Emeritus, Dept. of Astronomy, Columbia University, October 23, 1931 to October 11, 2005. Died of Waldenstrom's macroglobulinemia, which he had successfully surmounted for 22 years. Survived by wife Doris Blum Nagel M.D., sister Jean Trousdale Ph.D, brother Richard C. Baker M.D., two nieces and three nephews.
