
        SAVOY-Marie Jose, 94, on Saturday, January 27, 2001. The Board of Directors of the American Foundation of Savoy Orders, Incorporated records with deep sadness the passing of the last Queen of Italy. Contributions in her memory will be gratefully accepted by the Foundation at 605 Third Avenue, Suite 1501, NYC 10158, 212-972-1100. James Risk, Chairman Carl J. Morelli, President
