
        SINCOFF-Milton. On Monday, November 3, 1997, of North Caldwell, NJ. Surviving are his children, Eric and Meredith from his marriage to the late, beloved wife Donna (nee Blis), and a son, Jeffrey, from his previous marriage, and a sister, Lenore. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Milton Sincoff Memorial Fund, C/O T.J. Martell Foundation, 6 West 57th St., NY, NY 10019.
