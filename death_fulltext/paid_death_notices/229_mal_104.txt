
        STOCK--Robert E., 75. Died February 6, 2006. Beloved husband of Nadine, father of Richard, Matthew and Wayne and their wives Dana, Irina and Melina. Loving grandfather of Gianna, Cooper, William, Maxim and Denis. Art teacher at Bayside High School and past president of the Bayside Jewish Center. Artist extraordinaire, who produced exceptional paintings. His art work graces many homes in the New York area. We will greatly miss him and always love him. Services are on Wednesday 11:30AM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. Donations in his memory may be made to Bayside Jewish Center, MJFox Parkinson's Foundation, or Center for Extended Care at North Shore LIJ Foundation.
