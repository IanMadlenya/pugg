
        ROSENGARTEN--Alan Bill ''Billy''. On July 10, 2004. Beloved son of Lois Rosengarten Gurvitch and the late Harold G. Rosengarten. Brother and brother-in-law of Ronnie (Rosengarten) and Arthur German; Loraine and Edward Rosengarten, Alison and Jeff Rosengarten. 25 years of talent and creativity dedicated to the children's wear industry. Cherished uncle of many nieces and nephews. A man devoted to his family and friends, brought happiness and laughter to all. His humor and generosity will be missed.
