
        WILHELM--Maria (nee De Blasio), on January 22. Beloved daughter of Giovanni and Anna, mother of Steve, Don, and Bill, sister of Dorothy and Yole, aunt of John and Peter, grandmother of Dante, Chiara and Ben, mother-in-law to Ellen, Maggie, and Chirlane, and sister-inlaw to Mike and Jean. She was predeceased by her former husband Warren. She served her country at the Office of War Information during World War II and was author of ''The Other Italy: The Italian Resistance in World War II''. In lieu of flowers, donations be made in her memory to Amnesty International or Unicef.
