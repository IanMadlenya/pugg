
        SOLOMON-Arthur J. Age 85, on January 16, 1997. Beloved husband of Blanche, loving father of Gregg, and Lisa (Steve) Liebman, dear brother of Gloria Miller and Diana (Seymour) Steiger. He engaged in the practice of law for 57 years, retiring at 80. We shall deeply miss his charm, his humor, his courage and his devotion. Service Sunday 1 PM, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, Queens Blvd. and 76th Road, Forest Hills.
