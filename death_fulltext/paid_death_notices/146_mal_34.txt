
        DONLAN-Rev. Thomas, O.P. On February 26, in Paris. His talents and radical goodness shown in his teaching of Thomist theology; editing the Catholic Digest; serving the Catholic News Agency from Rome; working with Catholic Relief Services; the perennial chaplain of the Catholic Actors' Guild and, finally, priest of St. Agnes Church. The priests of the church regret their loss but do so within the Providence that sent him to us and our people. A Months Mind Mass will be offered for Father Donlan at St. Agnes (143 East 43rd St) 6:30 PM, Thursday, March 25.
