
        CHAPMAN-William Haven, of New Canaan, CT., died at home on August 27, 1997 after a long illness. Willy was 4 years old. His death was attributed to cardiac arrest. Willy was born on August 29, 1992, son of John William Haven Chapman and Jane Scanlan Chapman. Surviving, in addition to his parents are two brothers, Jack Chapman and Tom Chapman of Greenwich, CT., and New Canaan, CT.; a sister, Katie Chapman of New Canaan, and his maternal grandmother Joan Rouleau of South Salem, New York. A service was held at Lakeview Cemetery on August 29.
