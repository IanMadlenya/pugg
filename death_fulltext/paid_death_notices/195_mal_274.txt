
        BLUM-Walter H. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Walter H. Blum. A former president and chairman of the Henry Kaufmann Campgrounds, a UJA-Federation beneficiary agency, and a cherished friend of UJA-Federation, Mr. Blum will be remembered for his devotion to the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mr. Blum's wife, Jane, and to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
