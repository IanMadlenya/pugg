
        von FURSTENBERG--Egon, On June 11 at the age of 58 surrounded by his family in Rome, Italy. Diane, Alexandre, Tatiana and Lynn mourn the loss of their husband, father, friend and loved one. Egon, an Italian Fashion Designer will be remembered always as the loving free-spirited generous and happy man who lived his life to the fullest and was truly a shining light in the world. Services will be held on Saturday, June 12, at 3PM at the Church Piazza del Poplo (The Church of the Artist) in Rome, Italy. Egon will be buried in Strobl, Austria on Monday, June 14 at 3PM.
