
        RAE-Gene G.. Of Guilford, CT who died at the age of 85 after a short illness, was born in the Ridgewood section of Queens, NY where he attended Newton High school. He graduated from the Brooklyn Polytechnic Institute with a degree in electrical engineering. He went to work for the Holophane Company of New York, where he stayed for 40 years, rising to be the Vice President. In 1938 he married the former Catherine McLarney, to whom he stayed married for 61 years. In 1942 he enlisted in the U.S. Navy and an Ensign and was discharged in 1945 as a Lt. Senior Grade. Mr. and Mrs. Rae moved to Guilford in 1978 where he tutored students in mathematics and coached the math team. Besides his widow he leaves four children: David of Ridgewood, NJ, Lucy Ricciardi of Greenwich, CT, Catherine Reed of Littleton, CO and Mary Louise Rae Arnold of Fort Worth, TX.
