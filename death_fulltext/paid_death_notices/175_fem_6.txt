
        TARTALSKY-Deborah. Of Brandon VT and formerly of Jersey City NJ died Monday, July 30 at the age of 100 after a long and fruitful life. Her late husband, Samuel, was a Jersey City attorney. She is survived by daughter Lea (Ruth) Sneider of NYC and son Jeremiah Tarr of Rutland VT, and by nine grandchildren and fifteen great grandchildren. Service at Mt. Moriah Cemetery, 685 Fairview Avenue, Fairview NJ at 2:30PM, Wednesday August 1. Donations in Debby's memory to Hadassah, 50 West 58 Street, NYC 10019, attn: Suzi Somosi.
