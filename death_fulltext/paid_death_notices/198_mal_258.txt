
        BRADFORD--William John, Jr., a lifelong resident of Northport and Centerport, NY. Northport H.S. Class of 1941. Graduate of Brown University and the USMMA, Kings Point. Retired commander U.S. Navy, veteran WWII and Korea. Retired V.P. Chubb Insurance Co. Founding member of the Centerport Yacht Club. Loving husband of Jeannette (nee Frolli). Devoted father of Mary Jo Mugavin (Ronald), Judith Ann Bowlby (Roger), William John Bradford III (Debbie) and Jeannette Elizabeth Boccuzzi (Michael). Cherished Pop-Pop of Marybeth and Patrick Mugavin, Joseph and Timothy Bradford, and Erica Bowlby. Brother of Joann Brevik and the late Mary E. Bradford, MD. Reposing Nolan & Taylor-Howe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Ave., Northport, NY. Funeral Mass Thursday 10 AM at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Centerport, NY. Interment Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn.
