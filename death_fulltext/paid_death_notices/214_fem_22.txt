
        GROSSMAN--Selma B., died at her home on November 3, 2004. She was born on February 23, 1910 to Israel and Hannah Bloch, and was preceded in death by her husband, Joseph Grossman. She is survived by her children, Joel Grossman (Mary), Gene Morgan, and Ann Mock (Karl Josef), by six grandchildren, William and Michael Morgan, Allen Mock, and Alison (Whit Morgan), Joanna (Grant Hayden), and Daniel Grossman; by four great-grandchildren; members of the Isaac S. Bloch Family Group; and by her longtime caregiver, Kathleen Hunter. A funeral service will be held at 11:30AM on Friday, November 5th at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue in Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the charity of your choice.
