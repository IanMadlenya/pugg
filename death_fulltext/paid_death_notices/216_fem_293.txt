
        LANDAU--Bernice, 74, of Boca Raton, Florida, passed away on Monday, January 24, 2005. She was laid to rest on Thursday, January 27, 2005. Bernice left behind her loving husband, Harold, of 43 years; her children who loved her dearly and will miss her greatly, Wayne (Iris), Cheryl (Joe), Laurence (Susan), and Michael (Nancy) and Allyn. She leaves behind grandchildren who loved her dearly, Lindsay, Samantha, Alyssa, Chelsea, Gabriella, Brittany, Jeffrey, David, Alex, & Myles. She was the loving sister adored by Sidney and Paul. She was a loving person who brought much joy to all who knew her. She will be greatly missed. We love you, Mom.
