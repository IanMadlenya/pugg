
        GOLDMUNTZ - Ruth. The Board and staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum are deeply saddened by the death of Ruthie Goldmuntz. She worked tirelessly with Teddy Kollek and the early founders of the Israel Museum, and remained a close friend for 40 years. We offer heartfelt condolences to her sons, David and Michael, and the entire family. Judy Steinhardt, AFIM, Co-Chair, Board of Directors James S. Snyder Anne & Jerome Fisher Dir., IM Monique Birenbaum Executive Director, AFIM
