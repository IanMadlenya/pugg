
        MANTELL--Harold, 85, of Manhattan, Kennebunkport, ME and Stuart, FL, died Saturday, August 12, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital in New York. He is survived by his beloved wife of 49 years, Marianne; four children: Stephen, of Chappaqua, NY; Michael, of Princeton, NJ; David, of Chicago, IL; Eva, of Princeton, NJ; and seven grandchildren, Laura, Will, Becky, Matt, Gaby, Miranda and Sammy. Contributions can be made to Temple Beth El of Northern Westchester (www.bethelnw.org).
