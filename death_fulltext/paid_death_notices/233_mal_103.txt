
        TEITELBAUM -- Herbert I., age 80, passed away on June 8, 2006. Cherished husband of Renee (nee Landwehr), and beloved father of Gary (Nerine), and Amy Nelson (Joel). Adored grandfather to Ashleigh, Meredith, Stacy, Jonathan, Chretien and Colton. He graduated from Bronx Science H.S. and City College. Decorated veteran of World War II. He was our leader. Always the wise one. He will be missed by all. Funeral: Sinai Chapel, Fresh Meadows, New York, 12 noon, June 11.
