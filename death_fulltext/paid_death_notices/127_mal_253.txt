
        FREUND-Richard B. Age 82. Died August 13, 1997 in Newtown, Pennsylvania. United States career foreign service officer who negotiated the 1955 Austrian State Treaty and worked on the creation of NATO and the Marshall Plan. He was Chief of Mission in Kenya 1960 to 1963 and set up the Moscow-Washington Hotline. Survived by his daughter, Katherine Schubart; three sons, Nicholas B. Deane, Eric B. Freund and Arthur C. Freund and six grandchildren. Memorial contributions to the Fox-Chase Cancer Center, Jenkintown, Pennsylvania.
