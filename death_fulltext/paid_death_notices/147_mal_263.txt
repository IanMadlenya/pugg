
        BERGER-Alfred J., M.D.  of Delray Beach, Fl. died April 26, 1999. A graduate of Harvard Medical School, he retired in 1986. He is survived by his wife Page, and his son Robert B. (Buzz) Berger of Princeton, and daughter Barbara Berger of Larchmont, four grand children, and his sister Charlotte Feinswog of Bal Harbor, Fl.
