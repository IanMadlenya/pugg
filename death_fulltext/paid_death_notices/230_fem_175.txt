
        SOLOMON--Roslyn Kaye. 96. Beloved wife of the late Sylvan Solomon, sister of the late Buddy Kaye. Loving mother of Judie Marcus (Alan), Marcia Silva (Delio). Devoted Nannie of Debbie Corrado (Joe), Robert Marcus, Randy Downs and Kevin Downs. Adoring great-grandmother of Jeff and Greg Corrado, Erica and Ethan Downs. A very special woman whose intelligence, dedication and caring was shared with her family, her friends and the charities and causes she supported. Roz's family was always the center of her life. We will miss her always.
