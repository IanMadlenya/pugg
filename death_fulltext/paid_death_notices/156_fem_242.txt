
        SILVERMAN-Sondra Reichman, 63, of West Palm Beach, FL, and formerly of New York. Passed away on Saturday, January 15, 2000. Beloved wife of retired New York City Councilman Theodore Silverman and loving mother of William and Valerie Reichman. She was former New York State Democratic Committee Woman of the 41st assembly district, executive director of the New York City coalition of community planning boards, administrator of her husband's executive office and past president of the Alan Foss Leukemia Memorial Foundation. Donations may be made in her memory to that foundation.
