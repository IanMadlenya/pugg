
        FENSTERSTOCK-Howard. Age 74, of Mt. Vernon, died April 26, 1997. Beloved husband of Marianne, adored father of Martin and Laura. Cherished friend of all whose lives he touched. WW II vet, member of Sinai Temple and Harness Riders Association. Memorial service was at Riverside Memorial Chapel, interment at Mt. Moriah Cemetery, Fairview, NJ.
