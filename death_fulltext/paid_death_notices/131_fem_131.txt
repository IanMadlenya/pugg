
        LYONS-Lynne. Resident of New York City and Palm Springs. Daughter of Mary and Charles Lyons. Studied drama at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City and subsequently embarked on international theater tours. The majority of her career was spent as a producer at several noted New York City advertising agencies. The funeral services will take place at St. Ignatius Loyola on Park Avenue and 84th Street on Tuesday, December 16, 1997 at 9 AM. Grace Fowler
