
        OLIN-John C., 84, Professor Emeritus of History at Fordham University, died on June 6th in West Nyack, N.Y. Devoted husband of Marion A. Olin for 57 years, he is also survived by 4 children, Mary Elizabeth Deambrosis, John Olin Jr., Margaret Olin de Souza Santos, and Thomas Gregory Olin, as well as 10 grandchildren and 4 great-grand children. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Anne's Church in Nyack, N.Y. on Friday at 10am. Visiting hours at the Joseph W. Sorce Funeral Home, 728 West Nyack Rd, West Nyack, NY Wednesday, June 7th, 7-9pm & Thursday, June 8th 2-4pm and 7-9pm.
