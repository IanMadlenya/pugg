
        STURGES-Lawrence Morris. 59. Of White Plains. On March 7, 2001. Beloved son of the late Walter Knight Sturges and the late Alice Morris Sturges. Donations to United Cerebral Palsy of Westchester or the Alice Morris Sturges Fund (to assist families with developmentally disabled children to provide for in-home care), C/O The Cambridge Community Foundation, 99 Bishop Allen Drive, Cambridge, MA. 02139.
