
        RUISINGER-Thomas. Gentleman, actor. Died on March 11, 1997. at 69 years old at Cardinal Cooke Home. A graduate of Southern Methodist University, he came to New York City from his home in Dallas, TX. He studied at the Neighborhood Playhouse and worked consistently in the theater on Broadway and off Broadway, national tours and summer stock. He also appeared in running roles on soap operas and did numerous television commercials. Thomas is survived by his sister-in-law, Margaret Anne Ruisinger, nieces and nephew, Kitty, Mary and Don, loyal friend and partner Louis Warth and longtime companion Dwight Arno and many loving friends. We will miss him.
