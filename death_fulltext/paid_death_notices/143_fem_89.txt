
        BLUESTEIN-Fela. Of Upper Montclair, NJ. Member of Hadassah, O.R.T. and Hidden Children. Survived by her husband Sanfurd. Contributions may be made to Hidden Children or New York Holocaust Museum. Services are today, 10:00am at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel 150 Rt 4 East, Paramus, NJ. Interment will be held Sunday, 12 Noon, at Kiryat Shaul Cemetery, Tel Aviv, Israel.
        BLUESTEIN-Fela. New York City Opera mourns the loss of Fela Bluestein, wife of Board Member Dr. Sanfurd G. Bluestein. Long time champions of new productions at City Opera, the Bluesteins have been instrumental in bringing many operas to New York's cultural life. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Sandy and the Bluestein family. New York City Opera Bd of Dirs. Paul Kellogg, General & Artistic Director Sherwin M. Goldman, Executive Producer
