
        KROUNER--Barbara J., beloved Mother, Partner, ''Nanny'' and friend passed away Tuesday, March 6, 2007, after a courageous battle with cancer. Barbara was a resident of Tarrytown and was born on January 25, 1944, in Manhattan and was the daughter of Harry and Helen Jacobson. She received her Masters in education and special education and was a graduate of N.Y.U. Hunter & The College of New Rochelle. After her retirement from teaching, she enjoyed her second career in Textiles. Barbara was also involved and supported The Humane Society. She is survived by her life partner Bill Dziena, her loving daughters Lisa Krouner Serota and her husband Stuart of Rye Brook, NY, and Laura Krouner Paradise and her husband Steven of Manhattan, NY, her brother Richard Jacobson and his wife Joan of Scarsdale, her grandchildren Hillary and Ethan Serota and Sam Paradise, and her beloved pugs Hazel and Max. Services will be held on Friday, 10:30 am at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave, Rye, New York, with entombment to follow in the Ferncliff Mausoleum, Hartsdale, New York. Memorial contribution may be made to The Humane Society of the United States or The National Ovarian Cancer Early Detection Program. For information, call the George T. Davis Funeral Home 914-632-0324.
