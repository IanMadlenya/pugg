
        LEHMAN -- Edwin. Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion records with profound sorrow the untimely death of Edwin Lehman, beloved brother of Burton Lehman, Chair of our Board. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his dear wife, Helene, and their children and grandchildren. May the family find comfort together with all those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi David Ellenson, President And the Board of Governors
