
        PESKIN--Ira. Longtime resident of Lido Beach, NY. Died on December 15, 2005, at age 78. Beloved husband of Marilyn, devoted and loving father of Jill and Jeffrey, loving grandfather of Michael and David, devoted and loving son of Gussie and Isaac Peskin, and loving and caring brother of Ruth and Lester Peskin, and special friend to his devoted and loving son-inlaw Jeffrey Saunders. Ira, along with Marilyn, his partner for more than fifty years, represents the best that there is in life. He will be remembered fondly by all who knew him. His family
