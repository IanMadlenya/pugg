
        HASTINGS--Baird, 88. Died May 16, 2007; conductor, music educator and writer; predeceased by Lily, his wife and soul mate of 51 years; degrees from Harvard, Queens College and Sussex College; Mozart Festival Orchestra founder and conductor; faculty Trinity College and Juilliard; wrote many books and articles emphasizing French painters and Mozart. Memorial Service: Saturday, June 9, 2007, 11am, First Presbyterian Church, 5th Ave. and 11th St., NYC.
