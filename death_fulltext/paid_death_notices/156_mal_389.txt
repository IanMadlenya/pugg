
        NELSON-Richard H.., 60, died on Monday, January 24, 2000, at his home in the Palm Beach Polo and Country Club in Wellington, FL. Mr. Nelson was the President, CEO and Director of US Energy Systems, Inc. He received his B.A. degree from Princeton University in 1961. The same year, he went to work in Washington, D.C., as special assistant to Bill Moyers, deputy director of the Peace Corps. In 1962, Mr. Nelson became military aide to Vice President Lyndon B. Johnson, and was assistant to President Johnson from 1963 to 1967. Mr. Nelson was a first lieutenant in the U.S. Army from 1962 through 1964 and a recipient of the Presidential Medal from the Office of the President of the United States in 1965. He is survived by Carole, his wife of 34 years; his children, Christopher and Karin; and his parents, Irvin and Ethel Nelson, of Norfolk, VA. A memorial service will be held at 2 PM Thursday, January 27, at Pan's Garden, located at 386 Hibiscus Avenue in Palm Beach. An additional memorial service will be held at a later date in New York. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate donations to the Southampton Fresh Air Home.
