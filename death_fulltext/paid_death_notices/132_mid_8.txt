
        DEVINE-Rita K. Died December 29, 1997, at the age of 72, in San Diego, CA. She was a resident of Stamford, CT, for six years, and previously of Rye, NY. Predeceased by husband John F. Devine. Survivors include Patrick F. Devine II and his wife Debra, of Newport Ritchy, FL, Mary Ann Routson and her husband Geoffrey, of Maybrook, NY, grandchildren Ashley and Rebecca Routson. Memorial service at Doral Farm Community Center, Doral Farm Rd, Stamford, CT, Saturday, January 31, 1998, at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the Connecticut Coalition Against Domestic Violence, 135 Broad St, Hartford, CT 06105.
