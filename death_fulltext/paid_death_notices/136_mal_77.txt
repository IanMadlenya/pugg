
        SEALES-Kenneth W. Sr. In his 70th year. On May 4, 1998. Formerly of Long Beach, Long Island and North Babylon. At his home in Dungarvin, Ireland. Former President of Fischbach & Moore, Inc. and owner of Seal's Cove Restaurant. Mr. Seales leaves to cherish his memory many family and friends. Private services to be held in Dublin, Ireland. Memorial contributions may be sent to Internationnal Myeloma Foundation, 2120 Stanley Hill Drive, Los Angeles, California 90046.
