
        REINER - Elaine, actress, died on Monday, April 14th. Daughter of (late) Harry and Emma Oshrin, sister of (late) Albert Oshrin, wife of (late) Manny Reiner and of (late) Hal Eaton. Survived by her sister Evelyn (Pat) Glazer, brother-in-law Irving Glazer, six nieces and nephews, nine great-nieces and nephews, and longtime companion Herman Liebmann. Services 11:30 AM, Sunday April 20th at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Visitation from 10:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Actors Fund of America, 729 7th Ave., NYC 10019.
