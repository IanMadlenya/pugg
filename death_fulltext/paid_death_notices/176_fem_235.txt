
        WEINBERG-Florence. Age 93, passed away on Yom Kippur. Beloved mother of Michael and Harold. Devoted wife of the late Ewald Weinberg. Cherished motherin-law of Lois and Ann. Dear grandmother of Carin and Jeff, Jonathan, Elisabeth, Caroline and Peter. Great-grandmother of Samuel. Loving sister of Sylvia, Leo, Rose and Irene. A brief illness ended her long life, every day marked with the integrity, intelligence and warmth that she brought to everything she did and everyone she knew.
