
        FREEDMAN-Audrey. The Board and staff of the Jewish Fund for Justice mourn the death of our dedicated Board member, Audrey Freedman. Audrey was a brilliant economist, insightful media commentator, and a humanitarian deeply committed to social justice. We send heartfelt condolences to her husband, Monroe, their children and the entire family. Ronna Stamm, Chair Larry Levine, Chair Emeritus Marlene Provizer, Exec Dir. Jewish Fund for Justice
