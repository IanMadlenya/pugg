
        MOORE-Rev. Robert A. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of retired parochial vicar of St. Ephrem's Church, 929 Bay Pkwy, Brooklyn on Wednesday September 12. Reposing at Sessa Funeral Home, 6924 Fort Hamilton Pkwy, Brooklyn on Sunday September 16, from 2-4:30 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass on Monday September 17 at 10:30AM at St. Ephrem's Church. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
