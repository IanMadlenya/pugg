
        GEORGE--Judith Ross, 74, December 31st 2006 of lung cancer. She was a warm and witty woman who was so gallant in death. Survived by her husband of fifty years, George W. George; daughter, Jennifer; grandchildren, Max and Emily; stepchildren, Linda Tai, Laurie and Larry George; and stepgrandson Giampaolo Tai. Judy was smart, funny, and irreverent. Her laugh at a crowded dinner table remains in our ears. Services are 11:30am Friday January 5th, 2007 at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home at 1076 Madison Ave. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Lung Cancer Foundation or the Parkinson's Foundation.
