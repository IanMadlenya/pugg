
        BLISSERT--Robert, of Southbury, CT, formerly of Manhasset, NY, died April 6, 2005. He was the husband of Virginia (Mitchell) Blissert. He retired as President & Chairman of Wire-O-Binding, Inc. after 54 yrs. Besides his wife of 58 yrs, he leaves six daughters and their husbands, one brother, Richard, one sister, Rita and 10 grandchildren. Funeral Mass Monday April 11 at 10AM at Sacred Heart Church, Main St, Southbury, CT. Visiting hours Sunday from 4-7PM in the daily chapel of Sacred Heart Church. Burial in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made to a charity of the donor's choice. For information Carpino Funeral Home, Southbury CT
