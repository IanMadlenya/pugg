
        MAJOR-William P. Died peacefully on November 29, aged 93. He was for 31 years Postmaster of Bergenfield, NJ, and a leading member of many civic organizations there; a Director of the Bergenfield National Bank and its successor banks; a loyal alumnus of Colgate, Columbia and NYU, and a faithful member and elder of the South Presbyterian Church, Bergenfield. His wife, the former Kathryn M. Demarest, predeceased him. He is survived by three sons, Dr. William D. of Charleston, SC, Dr. David C. and Dr. John S. of New York City, as well as five grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. Visiting at Riewerts Memorial Home, Bergenfield, on Monday and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Service will be private. A memorial service will be held at South Presbyterian Church, Bergenfield, on December 7, at 3 PM.
        PARKER - George MacDonald. The Board of Directors of Fiduciary Trust International mourns the passing of its esteemed friend and colleague on November 22 in Frankfurt, Germany. He rendered thoughtful and cogent advice for the benefit of the company and its clients during his six years as a director. Our deepest sympathy to his wife, Susan Bissell Parker, and his daughter Julia and son Angus.
