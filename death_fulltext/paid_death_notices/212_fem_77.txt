
        BIRDSALL--Leone Cottrell, M.D. Age 94, died on September 8, 2004. Dr. Cottrell was a Pediatrician and Child Psychiatrist, a member of The New York Embroiderers Guild, The Society of Women Geographers and a Life Member of The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Predeceased by her husband, Charles H. Birdsall, Esq. in 1965, she is survived by three loving daughters, Marion Rendon, Jean Simson and Helen Shepherd, three grandchildren, Santiago, Ann Beatriz and Leone Rendon, and four great - grandchildren. The Wake and Service will be held at Campbell Funeral Home, Saturday, September 11, 2004, 10AM-12PM. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 E. 70 St., NYC 10021.
