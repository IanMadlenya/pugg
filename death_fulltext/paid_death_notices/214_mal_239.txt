
        CLEMENTS--Roy. The Zionist arm of the Conservative Movement mourns the passing of Roy Clements, President of MERCAZ USA from 1993-98 and current President of MERCAZ Olami since 1997. During his many years of dedicated service to our organization, he demonstrated a firm commitment to Zionism and the State of Israel. A kind and compassionate man, he will long be remembered for his tireless efforts on behalf of the Jewish community. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Pauline and family. Rabbi V. Kurtz, President Rabbi R. Golub, Exec Dir
