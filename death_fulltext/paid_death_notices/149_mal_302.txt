
        MANLEY-John M. Born New York City, on April 4, 1919. Died suddenly at home in La Jolla, CA, June 18, 1999. He was a loyal son of Dartmouth College, a devoted husband, a loving father, an adoring grandfather. He is survived by his wife Carla of La Jolla, CA, his daughter Ann Schlesinger, and his grandson and namesake John Lewis Schlesinger. The family asks that any donations be made to Friends of Dartmouth Football.
        MANLEY-John M. A gentleman and a gentle man with an enormous heart. I am blessed to have been your wife for 13 years. You will be missed. Carla
