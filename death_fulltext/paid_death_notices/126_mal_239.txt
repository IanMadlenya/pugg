
        MIEKLE-Thomas H., Jr., MD. The entire staff of North Shore University Hospital was deeply saddened by the passing of Thomas H. Miekle, Jr., MD, a good friend and strong supporter of the hospital. As the former Dean of Cornell University Medical College, Dr. Miekle's many achievements in medicine and medical education have inspired everyone. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, Jacqueline Winterkorn, MD, and his entire family. Saul B. Katz Chairman, Board of Trustees John S.T. Gallagher President and CEO Lawrence Scherr, MD, MACP Sr. Vice President, Medical Affairs North Shore Health System
