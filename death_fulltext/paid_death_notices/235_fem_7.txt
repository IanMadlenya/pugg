
        ROFFER--Marion. Accomplished sculptor, teacher, designer. Dear Sara Ellen to the late Monroe, her childhood sweetheart. Paragon of Ardsley pool & tennis parties. Truly loved by all who knew her. Survived by daughters Susan Weintraub (Robert); sons Steven (Robin) and Alan; four grandchildren; and sister Barbara Strassman (Joseph). Preceded by sister Judith Shiekman (Philip).
