
        WHEELER - Harriet Winslow. Died January 26, 2002 in Goshen, NY, formerly of Irvington-On-Hudson, NY. Born July 21, 1913 in New York City. Daughter of the late Gladys R. and Thomas S. Winslow. Survived by her children Brook Wheeler Berlind of New York City, Charles Hathorn Wheeler of Patterson, NY, Poppy Wheeler Anthony of Philadelphia, PA, Winslow Temple Wheeler of Gaithersburg, MD, 2 grandsons, 1 step-grandson, 3 great-grandsons, and her sister Mary Olive Winslow Mygatt of Bellevue, WA. She was a member of the National Society of Colonial Dames in the State of New York, the General Society of Mayflower Descendants, The Thursday Club and the Tarrytown Historical Society. A memorial service will be held at the Irvington Presbyterian Church on Saturday, February 9, at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hudson Peconic Planned Parenthood, 4 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, New York 10532.
