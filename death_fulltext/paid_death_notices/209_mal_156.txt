
        ALTON--David C., died on Wednesday, June 9. He was much loved and will be missed greatly. Colgate University, AB (1951); Columbia Law School, LLB (1959); Mobil Oil, Tax Counsel (1966-77); Warner Lambert, Tax Counsel and Corporate Vice President (1977-93). Mr. Alton is survived by his wife, Jeanne G. Alton, his children Kim, Jeff, Mary Lynn and Susan, three grandchildren and many loving friends. For information Jeffries & Keates (609) 646-3400
