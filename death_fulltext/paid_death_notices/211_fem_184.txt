
        JACKSON--Patricia Woolsey. Of New Haven, CT, Delray Beach, FL, and Easthampton, NY, died peacefully August 17, at 87 years of age. She is survived by three children, Suzanne J. Cartier, Sheila J. Leach and Lionel Stewart Jackson Jr.; five grandchildren, four great-grandchildren and two sisters, Anne W. LaFarge and Edith W. Nutt.
