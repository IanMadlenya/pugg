Contributions to the Palm Beach Habilitation Center Inc., 4522 South Congress Ave, Lake Worth, FL 33461. Services Monday, April 29, 2002, 12:30 PM, at ''Gutterman's,'' 175 Long Beach Rd, Rockville Centre, LI.
