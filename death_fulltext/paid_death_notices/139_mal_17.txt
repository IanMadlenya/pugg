
        RUSSO-Vito M. On August 1, 1998. Beloved husband of Diana. Loving stepfather of Alexandra Kaufman, Valerie Lenes and Victoria Maghsadi. Adored grandfather of Jeffrey, Adam and Noah. Brother of Madeline LaMorte and Mary Scutellaro. Former owner of Mario Industries, Inc. Lamp and Shade Manufacturer of Mt. Vernon, NY. A former Board Member of Mt. Vernon Hospital, Westchester Advisory Board of Chemical Bank, Colburn Home of the Aged and the Westchester Community Foundation. Mr. Russo was involved in many philanthropic activities throughout his lifetime. He was an avid yachtsman. Visiting hours, George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle, NY, Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Chapel service Tuesday, 3 PM, at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Vito and Diana Russo Trust Fund, New York Community Trust Company, 2 Park Ave, NY, NY 10016.
