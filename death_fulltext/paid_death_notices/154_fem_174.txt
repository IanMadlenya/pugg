
        KLINE-Bebe Aron, 88, on November 18th, 1999, Kislev 9, 5760. Beloved, loyal and generous mother of Karen M. and Kenneth S., wife of Samuel, optimistic and funny grandmother of Daniel H. and Rachel S., adoring sister of George and Charles Aron, devoted aunt. Graduate St. John's Law School 1932, Matron of the Eastern Star. One of the first women admitted to practice before the Supreme Court of the United States. Poet. ''La famille c'est toute.'' She will be deeply missed and always loved.
