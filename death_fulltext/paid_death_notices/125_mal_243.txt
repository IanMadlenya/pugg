
        MCGOVERN-H. Kenneth. Died of cancer on June 20, 1997 at the age of 80 years old. Formerly of Scarsdale, NY, he resided in Jupiter, FL and died in West Palm Beach. He is survived by 4 children: Kenneth, Jane, Ann Noble and Elizabeth. Kenneth was married to the late Elizabeth Hoyt McGovern. Services will be held 11:00 AM Saturday, June 28, at Our Lady of Perpetual Help in Pelham, New York. In liew of flowers, the family requests that donations are sent to the American Cancer Society.
