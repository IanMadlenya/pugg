
        HARKAVY-Esther. On March 15, 2002, at the age of 97 years. Beloved wife of the late Morris Harkavy. Mother of Justice Ira B. Harkavy, his wife Roberta, and the late Stuart Harkavy. Grandmother of Steven J. Harkavy, his wife Jodi; Daniel J. Harkavy, his wife Jody; and Elliot. Great-grandmother of Melissa A. Harkavy, Brian R. Harkavy and Stephanie R. Harkavy. Services Sunday, March 17, 2002 at noon, at Weinstein-GarlickKirschenbaum Chapels, 1153 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, NY. Shiva will be observed through Friday morning.
        HARKAVY-Esther. Trustees and staff of Brooklyn Public Library mourn the death of Esther Harkavy, dear mother of our Trustee, Justice Ira B. Harkavy. Our thoughts and prayers are with the Harkavy family. Albert C. Wiltshire, President Siobhan A. Reardon, Acting Executive Director
