
        OESTREICHER-Anthony J., 59, suddenly of cardiac arrest on May 3. A resident of Bernardsville, NJ, Tony graduated Dartmouth College in 1961. He was Creative Director for ad agencies in Chicago and New York and enjoyed writing for AT&T Solutions. Tony was active in his church and community theater groups and his volunteer work for the homeless. He is survived by his wife Georgette, his children Lisa, Cara and Jack, his brother David and his six grandchildren. Services Friday, 10:30am at Olph Church in Bernardsville.
