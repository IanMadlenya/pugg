
        FISHER--Alexander A., M.D. Died Saturday, July 17 at age 98. Devoted husband of the late Lillian Fisher, he will be greatly missed by his children Stephen A. Fisher (Susan) of Berkeley, CA and Adria Price (Stephen) of Roslyn, NY, four grandchildren, Karin W. Fisher-Golton (Joe), Jacob S. Fisher, Julie Price Passman (David), Laurie Price Hansson (Paul) and four greatgrandchildren. A graduate of Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons, he was Clinical Professor of Dermatology at NYU-Postgraduate Medical School. An admired teacher, world-renowned lecturer and an acknowledged leader in Dermatology, he was also a noted humorist. He was author of the landmark text Contact Dermatitis as well as over 300 scientific papers. Services on Monday, July 19 at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 1:30 PM.
