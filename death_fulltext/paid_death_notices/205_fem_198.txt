
        ROTHSCHILD--Maurine. The Board of Trustees and The Ethical Culture Fieldston School community note with sadness the death of Maurine Rothschild, who taught at Fieldston from 1966 to 1973. We extend our sympathies to her family, including her husband, Robert Rothschild '35, son, Peter Rothschild '74, and daughter, Katherine Rothschild Jackson '61. Nina P. Freedman, '73 James A. Gara, '72 Co-Chairs, Board of Trustees, and Joseph P. Healey, Ph.D Head of School
