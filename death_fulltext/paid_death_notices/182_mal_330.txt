
        CARDWELL-Daniele Seth. Age 40, died March 21, 2002, after an 18 month courageous battle against Castleman's Disease. He is survived and will always be remembered and loved by his life partner, George B. Buckner, his mother, Lillie Mae Cardwell, his loving siblings, Patricia and Linda, and surviving brother Andre, his sisterin-law Renee, his niece Cheleece and nephews Garrett and Eric, many cousins, his housemate and friend Jay Lazarus, as well as friends and colleagues. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date.
