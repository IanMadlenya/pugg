
        ZELL-Herbert H., age 74, beloved husband of Carol, devoted father of Judy and Michael. Dear brother of Frances Horn and the late Edward Zell. He was a Vice President of design and merchandising for his own company, in addition to various other mens' sportswear companies. He retired December, 1994 at the age of 72. Services are today, 1:00 PM at Schoem Chapel, 150 Route 4E, Paramus. Mourning period observed at the Zell residence through Sunday, February 9. Contributions may be made to the Alzheimers Association or the American Cancer Society.
