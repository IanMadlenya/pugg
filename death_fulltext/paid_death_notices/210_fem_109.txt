
        DOERSAM--Mary Lou, on Monday, July 19th, 2004 at Alexandria Hospital. Beloved wife and soulmate of Christopher J. Doersam. Sister of Wanda Prisinzano. Daughter of Florence Prisinzano and the late Joseph Prisinzano. Mary Lou was a registered nurse for 25 years. She worked at the Washington Hospital Center and Alexandria Hospital. Friends may call at Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd., Flushing, NY, Friday 2-5 and 7-10 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Andrew Avellino R.C. Church, Flushing, NY, Saturday, at 9:15 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
