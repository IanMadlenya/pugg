
        DEGEL--Lillian. Of Manhattan. Died November 16, 2005. She survived her husband of 50 years, David Degel. She is survived by her sister Ruth Pacifico, and her children and their families: Fred Degel and Flo Barna, his sons Alan and Evan and wife Michelle and daughters Amanda and Arianna; Marlene and David Lieberman and children Emma and Jeffrey; Matthew and Sharon Degel & children Sarah and Benjamin; William and Nancy Ostrow and son David. A memorial service will be held at ''The Riverside'', 180 West 76th St., on Sunday, November 20 at 3PM. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the New York Botanical Garden, Lillian's favorite retreat.
