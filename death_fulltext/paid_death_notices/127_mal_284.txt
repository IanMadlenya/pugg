
        CARRANO-Andrea, 71, the worldrenowned shoe designer, died on Wednesday at Lenox Hill Hospital after a long illness. Andrea Carrano is survived by his wife, Elizabeth, their daughters, Valentina and Giada, their son, Andrea, and 3 daughters by his first marriage, Gianna, Maria Grazia and Manuela, and their mother, Carmen. A memorial mass will be held in the Chapel of St. Vincent's Church, Lexington Avenue at 66th Street, Saturday, at 10 AM.
