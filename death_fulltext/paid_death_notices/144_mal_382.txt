
        GREINER-William R. (Botany Bill), 87, died January 18, 2000. Beloved husband of Ina (nee Blanchette) Greiner for 34 years. Loving brother of Emma Greiner. He is survived by many friends and students of botany and the world of nature. His gentle and sharing manner will not be forgotten. A private Funeral will be held at graveside.
