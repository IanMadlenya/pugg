
        HAGAN--Patricia (Mrs. Ward S.). The Board of Governing Trustees, Kevin McKenzie, Rachel Moore, The Dancers and Staff of American Ballet Theatre mourn the passing of friend and long time Board member Patricia Hagan. Having served on the Board since 1980, Patricia will be remembered for her tireless dedication to ABT. Her kind and generous spirit will live on in the beauty of ABT's performances. The ABT family will miss Patricia tremendously.
