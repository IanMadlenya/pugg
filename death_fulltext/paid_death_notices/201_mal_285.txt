
        SCHENKER--Paul. Died October 28 in Delray Beach, FL at age 71. Beloved husband, father, brother and grandfather of nine. Attended Trinity College and Brooklyn Law School. Served as an officer in the U.S. Air Force. His memory will live on in our hearts. Memorial service Sunday, November 2nd, 11:30AM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St, N.Y.C.
