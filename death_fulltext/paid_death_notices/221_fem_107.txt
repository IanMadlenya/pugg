
        HELSEL--Selma Geshwin, a resident of Aventura, Florida, passed on peacefully after a brave struggle with cancer on Saturday, June 11, 2005, at the age of 88. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Milton Helsel of Lynn, MA. Devoted mother of Eileen Swartz and Meryl and Robert Meltzer. Adored grandmother of Reesa and Steven Fischer, Jeffrey and Shayna Swartz, Neal and Graciela Meltzer and Sara and Adam Ames. Great-grandmother of Adam and Brian Fischer, Jessica and Carly Swartz, and Marley Meltzer. Sister of Roslyn and David Sonabend. Sister-in-law of Helen Epstein. Mom was a role model for unconditional love, compassion and understanding. Her strength and devotion to those she loved is a gift that will remain in our hearts forever, as will she. A graveside service will take place on Tuesday, June 14, at 1pm at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
