
        FREDERIKSEN--Frank G., 78, of New Canaan, CT died Thursday, October 28th, 2004 at Norwalk Hospital. Born March 3rd, 1926 in NYC. He was the son of the late Axel and Olga Johanson Frederiksen. He was a veteran of WWII having served with the Army Air Corp. Mr. Frederiksen graduated from Muhlenberg College, New York University Graduate School of Business, and Fordham Law School. He was a retired Senior Vice President with Chemical Bank. Mr. Frederiksen was a member of the Senior Mens' Club of New Canaan, Silvermine Golf Club, Sawgrass Country Club, Founding Member of the Lake Club, and was on the planning and zoning board in New Canaan. Mr. Frederiksen is survived by his daughter Kristen Atlas of Chappaqua, NY, son John Frederiksen of Woodinville, WA, five grandchildren and one niece. He is predeceased by his wife Eileen Farr Frederiksen and his son Peter Frederiksen. A mass will be held Monday, November 1st, 2004 at St. Aloysius Church in New Canaan at 10AM. Family will receive friends at the Hoyt Funeral Home in New Canaan on Sunday, October 31, 2004 from 4-8PM.
