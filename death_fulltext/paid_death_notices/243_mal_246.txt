
        MARKS--Charles Emmanuel Sr., an artist, died on April 17th. He was Born October 25th, 1917 to Alma and Clarence Marks of Philadelphia. He served as an aviator pilot in the Navy during World War II. Mr. Marks graduated from the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts, and lived most of his life in Paris, France, where he exhibited his paintings frequently. Predeceased by first wife Caroline French Cummin and son Charles E. Marks Jr. Survived by wife Yannick Rigourd Marks, daughter Suzanne Squires (David); and one granddaughter, Caroline Squires. A memorial is planned at a later date in Paris, France. Inquiries can be made to Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 212-288-3500.
