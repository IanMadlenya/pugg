
        TSE-Dr. Donald Yat-Tung, M.D., D.P.H. 86, beloved husband, father, grandfather died November 18, 2002 in CT. A graduate of both Hong Kong Univ. and Toronto Univ., he established himself during his later years in CT and NYC. Life-long member of the AMA, Royal Canadian Military Institute and Masonic Order. He will be remembered for his sense of humor, youthful spirit, and love of learning. We love you, Dad. Mom, Everest and Hanson
