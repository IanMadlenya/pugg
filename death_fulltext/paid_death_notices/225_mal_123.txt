
        RUANE--William. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend William Ruane. A longstanding benefactor of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital, Mr. Ruane will be remembered for his enthusiastic dedication to community revitalization and exceptional generosity in support of education and health care, particularly in the area of mental health. Our deepest condolences are extended to his wife, Joy, and the entire Ruane family. John Mack Chairman Dr. Herbert Pardes President & CEO NY-Presbyterian Hospital
