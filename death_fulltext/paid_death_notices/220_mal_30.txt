
        LaGAMBINA--Joseph S. The Trustees of the Financial Accounting Foundation, the members of the Financial Accounting Standards Board and the Governmental Accounting Standards Board, and the staff mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague and friend, Joe LaGambina. Joe was the executive vice president of the Financial Accounting Foundation and was associated with the Foundation for 21 years. Mr. LaGambina, a certified public accountant, served the Foundation with distinction. His capable leadership, generosity, and keen wit endeared him to many. We will remember him always with great fondness. The Trustees of the Financial Accounting Foundation, Board members and staff
