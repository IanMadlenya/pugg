
        AKAISHI--Tadashi, 82, survived by wife Amy, daughters Carolyn Stannard and Janet Akaishi, and seven grandchildren. Died February 28, 2007. A retired Presbyterian minister, he had a long career in publishing: Editor: John Knox Press published ''The Gospel According to Peanuts'' Tournier, ''Seasons of Life''; Group VP and Publisher Harper & Row; VP-Director Kodansha Int'l USA: published art books on Dale Chihuly, Sam Maloof, George Nakashima. In lieu of flowers: Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center.
