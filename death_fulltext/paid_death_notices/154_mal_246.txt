
        DUFFY-Michael J., Rev., 66, Maryknoll Missioner to Africa, died Nov. 16, 1999. He is survived by mother, Janet Duffy; brothers, Edward, John, and Daniel Duffy; and sisters, Janet Bruce, Angela Galascione, and Sharon Duffy. Wake services at Maryknoll Center, Ossining, N.Y., Sunday, 7PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Maryknoll Seminary Chapel, Monday, 11AM.
