
        WEINER-WARREN-Valeria. We extend our sincerest condolences to George Warren on the passing of his beloved wife. A true ''Eishes Chayil'', Valeria embodied the qualities of elegance, charm and kindness. As a Patron of Medicine of the ''Hospital with a Heart'', Valeria, together with her husband George, devoted her life and energy to healing the people of Jerusalem and Israel. May Mr. Warren and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Jerusalem Med. Ctr.
