
        PHIPPS-Thomas Wilton. Of Southampton, NY, died peacefully on February 20, 2003 at the age of 89. At his side were his devoted wife Mary of 54 years and his beloved children Sally, Lang, and his daughter-in-law Jana. Services will be held on March 1 at St. John's Episcopal Church in Southampton. Donations in his memory may be made to Boys Harbor, 1 East 104 St, NYC, 10029.
