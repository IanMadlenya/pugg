Rhodes (Craig), Ruth Streeter (John), and Meg Streeter Lauck (Dan). Cherished grandfather of nine, great grandfather of five.         $(6$)STREETER--Frank Sherwin. The Trustees, Life Trustees, Physicians and Staff of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and Life Trustee, Frank Sherwin Streeter. A member of the Board of Trustees of the Hospital since 1973, Mr. Streeter was an enthusiastic and dedicated advocate for the Hospital and the benefits of medical science. A financier, philanthropist and true gentleman, Mr. Streeter will be deeply missed. He is survived by his beloved wife, Nancy, who has been a long-standing supporter of the Hospital, particularly in the areas of AIDS and women's health. Our deepest condolences are extended to the entire Streeter family. John J. Mack, Chairman, Board of Trustees Dr. Herbert Pardes, President & CEO NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital
