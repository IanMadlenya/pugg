
        MARKS-Stella, 64. Of Highland Beach, FL, formerly of Rockville Centre, NY, passed away on 3/19/ 01. She is survived by her adored mother Anne, devoted children Stuart (Cynda) Marks, Daniel (Jill) Marks and Diane (Scott) Young, cherished grandchildren Jacqueline, Elizabeth, David & Stephanie, brothers George and Marc Moscowitz, loving companion Milton Lubich and many friends. Mrs. Marks was an interior designer and the former owner of Wall Works and Stellar Handprints. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, March 21, 10:00 AM, Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76 St., NYC 212-362-6600. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Pines Care Center, PO Box 5333, Fire Island Pines, N.Y. 11782.
