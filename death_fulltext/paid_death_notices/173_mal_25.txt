        McMURRAY-Dr. Joseph P. The Directors, Members and Staff of Greater Jamaica Development Corporation mourn with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed Founder, former Chairman and friend, Joseph P. McMurray. Dr. McMurray's leadership, integrity, expertise, and personal involvement benefited this community and this organization. He served as our first Chairman from 1967 to 1972, was subsequently elected Director-Emeritus. Dr. McMurray's vision, strength, determination and consensus building helped transform this older downtown into a vital center serving community and region. We will continue his good works and cherish his memory. We extend our deepest sympathy to the McMurray family. Tazewell A. Smith, Chairman Officers and Exec Committee: Vincent M. Albanese, Esq.
