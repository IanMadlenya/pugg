
        TONER -- Rev. Monsignor Francis X. On August 4, 2003. Pastor of St. Barnabas Church Bronx, NY, and former Pastor of Holy Rosary Church, Bronx, NY. He was Regional Vicar, Northeast Bronx Vicariate, Archdiocese of New York. Beloved brother of Joan Toner, of Williamsburg, VA; Kathleen Scully of Ft. Lee, NJ and the late Matthew Toner. Dear uncle of several nieces and nephews. The Monsignor will be received into the St. Barnabas high school Chapel, 240 1st St. and McLean Ave., on Tuesday, August 5 at 2:00 PM. He will lay in state from 2-4 & 7-9 PM, also on Wednesday from 2-4 PM with the Mass of the Holy Eucharist being offered at 7:30 PM. The Funeral Mass will be offered on Thursday, August 7 at 10:30 AM. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers kindly make donations to St. Barnabas Church, 409 East 241st St, Bronx, New York 10470.
