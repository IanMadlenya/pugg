
        FRANKFURT-Dora Wilkes. On October 13, 2000 at the age of 105. Beloved wife of the late Jacob Frankfurt and Harry Wilkes. Loving mother of Daniel and Ruth Frankfurt, and Dina and Daniel Katz. Proud grandmother of Judith and Jim, Jackie and Mark, Aaron and Joanne and Lila and Stuart. Great-grandmother of Jordan, Joseph, Gabriel and Benjamin. She had a lifelong devotion to the support of Yiddish culture. Graveside service Sunday, 1 PM at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
