
        ECKELBERRY-Don R. Wildlife Artist 1921-2001. Acclaimed for his book plates and bird portraits. A noted ornithologist and environmentalist, he co-founded the Asa Wright Nature Centre in Trinidad. He is survived by loving wife Virginia Nepodal. Donations may be made to The Eckelberry Endowment, Academy Of Natural Sciences, 1900 Ben Franklin Parkway, Philadelphia, PA. 19103.
