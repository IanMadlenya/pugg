
        MINTZ-Herman. The Board of Directors, management, employees and affiliates of Asset Alliance Corporation wish to express our deepest sorrow to our Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, Arnold Mintz, on the passing of his father, Herman Mintz, a loving husband, father and grandfather. His periodic visits to our offices were cherished moments and will be missed by all. Our heart felt condolences are extended to the entire family. May they draw strength and comfort from his memory and their many happy shared moments. Bruce H. Lipnick, President & CEO Asset Alliance Corporation
