
        WEINSTEIN--Irving Samuel. On Friday, August 26, 2005, after a long illness, in Palm Desert, CA. A respected principal of several electrical contracting companies in NY during his 50-year career. Veteran of WWII, long-term member of the Masonic Order and a Shriner. Will be deeply missed and mourned by family and friends. Services at Knollwood Park Cemetery, Queens, September 29 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the Los Angeles Shriners Hospital, 3160 Geneva St., LA, CA 90020.
