
        DONNER--Jay. Age 92, of the Fountains Country Club, Lake Worth, FL, and of South Orange, NJ, formerly of Lawrence, Long Island, passed away on Thursday, October 16, 2003, at home. Beloved husband of Muriel (nee Reichstein) for 57 years. Devoted father of Carole Siegel, her husband Dr. Stuart Siegel, Gary Donner and his wife Irene Miller. Loving brother of Mike Donner. Adored grandfather of Barry Siegel, Laurel Landau and her husband Brent.
