
        BING-Rudolf. The Board, Officers and staff of Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. mourn the passing of Sir Rudolf Bing, former General Manager of the Metropolitan Opera. It is with great respect and appreciation that we recall his role in guiding the Metropolitan Opera Association to Lincoln Center in 1966 and leading it to even greater international acclaim. We join the entire arts community in remembering Sir Rudolf's great passion, leadership and extraordinary contributions to the world of Grand Opera. Beverly Sills, Chairman Nathan Leventhal, President Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc.
