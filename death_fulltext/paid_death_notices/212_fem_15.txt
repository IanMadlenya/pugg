
        BENSON--Lenore. The Fashion Group International regrets the passing of its former Executive Director, Lenore Benson, after a distinguished career of more than 50 years in fashion retailing and publishing. Her many friends and colleagues will miss her talent, enthusiasm and warmth. Services will be held at St. John's R.C. Church, Woodstock, NY, on Tuesday, September 7th at 11:00 A.M. Interment immediately following. A Memorial Service will take place in October, in Manhattan.
