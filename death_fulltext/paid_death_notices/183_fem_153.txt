
        MOODIE-Betty Blue. Died in Bradenton, FL, April 12, 2002. Born in Topeka, KS, she spent most of her life in Bucks County, PA, New York City, where she was an editor of Glamour Magazine, and Bradenton, FL. She leaves three children, Kevin Moodie of Tuscon, AZ, John Moodie Jr. of NYC, and Kate Moodie Bizalion of NYC, as well as two grandchildren, Julien Bizalion and Chloe Bizalion, and a sister, Dorothy Blue Bunim of East Hampton, NY.
