
        GLADSTONE-Kenneth. On October 15, 1999. Beloved husband of Lucille of 52 years. Dear father of Adrienne (Russ) Fierman and Robert Gladstone. Adored grandfather of Ariele, Adam, Alanna & Stephany. Dear brother of Ernest Gladstone. Services Sunday, October 17, 1:15 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions may be made in Kenneth's name to the Jewish Home and Hospital, 120 West 106th Street, NYC 10025.
        GLADSTONE - Kenneth. Sutton Place Synagogue extends heartfelt condolences to Lucille Gladstone on the loss of her beloved husband. Lucille and Ken sponsored our famed Jewish Town Hall series for many years and supported other important projects. Our sympathies to children Adrienne and Russ Fierman and Robert Gladstone and their families. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Robert W. Berend, President
        GLADSTONE - Kenneth. We mourn the loss of our dear friend. He was an elegant man who created beauty. His buildings have elevated New York City architecture.
