
        BLOOM-Dr. Samuel M. Of Longboat Key, Florida, formerly of Bayridge, Brooklyn died peacefully at the age of 95 on February 2, 1998. Devoted husband of the late Fan. Loving brother of the late Harry and Jack. Beloved father of Elinor and father-in-law of the late Dr. Murry Friedman. Adored ''Pop'' of Amy, Betsy, Geri, Nancy. Respected and loved by his grandsons-in-law and his great grandchildren: Colden, Madelyn, Meredith and Duncan. His legacy of generosity and love will be cherished in our memories.
