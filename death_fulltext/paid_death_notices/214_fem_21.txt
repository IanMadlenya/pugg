
        NOSOFSKY--Shirley Geraldine. 83, on November 3rd. Born July 10, 1921 in Brooklyn, NY, she was the daughter of Joseph and Rose Tolchin. A former high school English teacher she was a lover of music, books and the arts. Devoted wife of William Nosofsky, loving mother of Robert (Donna) and Joseph (Laurie) Nosofsky and beloved grandmother of Michael, Alex, Daniel, Julia and Emma. She was preceded in death by her parents, her husband William and her brother Morton Tolchin. Services Friday 10:00 AM at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 46 Greenwich St, Hempstead, NY.
