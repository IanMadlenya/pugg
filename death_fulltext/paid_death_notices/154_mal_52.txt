
        SCHELPERT-John William, III, M.D. and Joann Appleyard, of Chestertown, MD and former longtime residents of Bronxville, NY, died Sunday, October 31, 1999. John was a retired obstetrician/ gynecologist with New Rochelle Medical Center. He was a graduate of Yale University, 1950 and Jefferson Medical College, 1955. He held several Board positions with the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists, District II from 1973 to 1987, concluding as Chairman from 1987 to 1990. He remained active in ACOG until 1994. Joann, a 1951 Connecticut College graduate, active member of numerous community organizations including the Junior League, the Bartow Pell Mansion and Boulder Ledge Garden Clubs, is survived by her mother, Kathryn Appleyard, sister Jeanne Pontius, brother Joseph Appleyard, Jr. all of Lancaster, PA, and sister Gail Heilbut of New York, NY. Together, they are survived by their four daughters, Susan Murray of Radnor, PA, Ann Francis of Glastonbury, CT, Jane Duffin of Fort Lauderdale, FL and Elizabeth Potter of Winchester, MA. Also surviving are seven loving grandchildren. A memorial service is scheduled for Thursday, November 11, 1999, 2:00 P.M. at Christ Episcopal Church, Bronxville, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to the Chesapeake Bay Foundation, 162 Prince George Street, Annapolis, MD, 21401 or Emmanuel Episcopal Church, Preservation Trust Fund, P.O. Box 875, Chestertown, MD. 21620.
