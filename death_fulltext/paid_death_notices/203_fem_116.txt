
        DOUGLAS--Isabelle Z., Ivoryton, CT, formerly of West Islip and Point O'Woods, New York. Daughter of the late Isabelle Frothingham and Robert Weaver Zimmerman, wife of the late Dr. Earl W. Douglas. In World War II, she served in the American Red Cross during the Battle of the Bulge. For eighteen years, she was the real estate agent at Point O'Woods. Surviving are a brother, Robert W. Zimmerman, Hampton Bays, NY, son Kerry M. Douglas of Sheffield, MA, daughter, Isabelle D. Seggerman, Haddam, CT and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church, Essex, CT, on January 2, 2004, 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, please send a contribution to the American Red Cross or the charity of your choice.
