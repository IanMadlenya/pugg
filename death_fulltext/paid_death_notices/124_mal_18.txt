
        McCARTHY-Thomas Raynolds, 76, died at his home in Manhasset, New York, on Thursday, May 1, 1997. Former Executive Vice President of Austin Nichols & Company, Inc., and the third generation of his family to become an officer of the company. Proud producer of Wild Turkey Bourbon, he established the Austin Nichols Distillery in Lawrenceberg, KY. Mr. McCarthy was born in New York City and graduated from Choate School, Class of '39. In World War II he served in the U.S. Marines on Guadalcanal, Iwo Jima and the Occupation of Japan. He was a member of the New York Athletic Club and Nassau Country Club. He leaves behind Peggy, his wife of fifty-one years; daughter, Lee Huber of Houston, TX; son, Thomas C. of Roslyn Harbor, NY; and son, Robert R. of Enfield, CT, and six grandchildren. Also, two brothers, John C. and Ronald W. McCarthy. Visiting hours, Sunday, May 4, 3-5 PM and 7-9 PM with a brief memorial service at 8PM at Fairchild Funeral Home, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hospice Care of Long Island, Inc. or to the charitable organization of your choice.
