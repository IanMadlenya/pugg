
        GHIRON - Anna, age 91, died on Friday, December 20th, 1996 at Lenox Hill Hospital. Mrs. Ghiron was born in Torino, Italy, the daughter of the late Abramo and Rina Treves. Mrs. Ghiron was predeceased by her husband Marco Ghiron in March, 1988, and is survived by a daughter, two sons, eight grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren. A memorial service will take place on Sunday, January 5, 1997 at the Universal Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, NY at 2:30 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to The American Cancer Society.
