
        GRAINGER-Isaac B. The Board and staff of ICD-International Center for the Disabled express deep regret at the passing of Isaac B. Grainger. For almost fifty years he was a devoted friend of ICD, serving with great distinction as an officer and trustee. His counsel and generosity will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to Isaac B. Grainger, Jr. and John V. Grainger and their families. Jeremiah M. Bogert, President Daniel B. Rosen, PhD., CEO
