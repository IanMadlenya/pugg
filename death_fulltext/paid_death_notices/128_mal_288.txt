
        McDERMOTT-Maurice M. The members of the Seymour Association extend our deepest sympathy to Claire, Chris and Glen McDermott.         McDERMOTT-Maurice. The Curran Association of former Assistant United States Attorneys mourns the passing of our colleague, Maurice ''Mac'' McDermott. The bar has lost a talented lawyer, and we have lost a good friend. We extend our profound sympathy to Mac's spouse Claire and their family. Paul J. Curran Association
