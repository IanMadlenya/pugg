
        GRODEN-Daniel M. Died at his home Wednesday, November 29 under the loving care of wife Betty (nee Gershon), daughter Elizabeth and son Adam. Dear brother of Ruth, Lyon, Ray, and Miriam (deceased). Loving in-law and uncle. He was blessed with the dearest of friends. Services at the Jewish Center of the Hamptons, East Hampton, New York, at 11 A.M, Friday, December 1.
        GRODEN-Daniel. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of Daniel Groden, beloved husband of Betty Groden. We extend our deepest condolences to Betty and her family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
