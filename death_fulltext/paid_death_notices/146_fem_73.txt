
        FEINBERG-Constance. (nee Buxer). 75. March 3, 1999. Beloved wife of Seymour. Cherished mother of Andrew and Kenneth. Adored sister of Joseph and the late Florence. Loving grandmother of Caroline and Lance. Devoted mother-in-law of Rikki and Adele. A wonderful friend. After earning her Ph.D. in Psychotherapy in her late 40's, she treated patients for 25 years. Previously, she had been an English teacher, a political aide, an executive secretary, a lyricist and a full time mother. A very full life and a meaningful one. A loving and beautiful woman of great intelligence, she will be missed. Services Sunday, March 7th, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Avenue, NYC.
