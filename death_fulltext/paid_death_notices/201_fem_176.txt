
        JOHNS -- Margaret. The Trustees and staff of the World Monuments Fund mark with great sadness the death of Margaret Johns. With her husband, Peter Stern, Vice Chairman and long-serving trustee of the World Monuments Fund, Margaret shared a passion for the work of the organization and participated with energy and intelligence in countless WMF events, trips, and gatherings. She brightened our lives with her informed and cheerful enthusiasm and greatly expanded our knowledge through her own many accomplishments, as a scientist, gardener, artist, music lover, reader, and passionate traveler. She will be missed by all. Our heartfelt condolences go to her beloved family including Peter Stern and daughters, Kate Johns and Sara Griffen.
        JOHNS -- Margaret. The Board of Trustees, Storm King Council and staff of the Storm King Art Center, mourn the passing of Dr. Margaret A. Johns, loving wife of our chairman and president, H. Peter Stern. Her invaluable participation as co-chair of our landscape committee and her charming personality and warm smile that brightened all of our lives will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family, with whom we share this great loss. David R. Collens, Director, Storm King Art Center
        JOHNS--Dr. Margaret. The Board and staff of The Fortune Society deeply mourn the passing of our dear friend Dr. Margaret Johns. She was an inspiration to all who knew her. Her beautiful pastels, scientific achievements, love of life, and her passionate concern for prisoners will be an enduring testament to her remarkable life. She will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt sympathy to Peter and family.
