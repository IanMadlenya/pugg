
        HEALEY--James E., Mt. Laurel, NJ, on September 14 at the age of 88. Born in Somerville, MA, an employee of Allstate Insurance for 25 years, he raised his family in Albany and Delmar, NY before retiring to Cape Cod, MA. Awarded the Soldier's Medal for service with the OSS in WWII. He is survived by his wife Vernice, sons James and Thomas and daughter Marilyn. You will be in our thoughts and hearts forever.
