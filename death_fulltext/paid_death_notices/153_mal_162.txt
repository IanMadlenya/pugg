
        GREEN-Samuel F. Passed away October 13, 1999 at the age of 100. Loving uncle to Mortimer and Adrienne Green and great uncle to Graham. Predeceased by his adoring brother and sister-in-law Louis and Doris Green. Sam was a dedicated and tireless life insurance agent for more than 60 years. He was a top producer for the Guardian, Union Central and National Benefit Life Insurance Companies and often honored by the prestigious Million Dollar Round Table. Sam was the ''consummate salesman'' who managed to carry out his business with honesty and integrity and became a role model to all who worked with him. Rest in peace, Uncle Sam. Interment private.
