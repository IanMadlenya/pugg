
        NELSON - Dr. Bernard. Passed away January 4, 2003. A distinguished and dedicated dentist in Mount Vernon, NY, for more than 50 years. The son of Nathan and Sarah Nelson. Devoted husband of Ruth. Father of Peter and Linda, and of his late daughter Teddie Nelson Wieland. Grandfather of Eric. Brother of Anne Belinkie and brotherin-law of Sara Wiesen and Aaron Levine. Above all, he was beloved for his kindness, wisdom and love of people.
