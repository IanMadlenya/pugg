
        TOLLEY-Rev. William G., 75. Of Cape May, NJ, formerly of Brooklyn. He was retired Presbyterian minister of Union Church of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. Rev. Tolley is survived by wife of 55 years, Rev. Jo Tolley; four daughters, Joyce Tolley Weiskopf, Kim E. Gibbons, Carol Hastings, and Cindy Tolley. Funeral service today 2 PM in First Presbyterian Church, Cape May. View Today 11 AM to 1 PM in Sudak-Danaher Funeral Home, Cape May. Donations: First Presbyterian Church, 500 Hughes St., Cape May, New Jersey 08204.
