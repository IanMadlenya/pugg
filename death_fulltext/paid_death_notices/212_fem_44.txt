
        POTASH--Jeannette. Died on September 2, 2004, peacefully at age 89, in her home in Florida. Beloved wife of the late Harry, loving mother of Andrew and Andrea Potash and Matti and Steve Contopulos, and adored grandmother of Adam Potash, Scott Potash, Amy Contopulos Miller and Brad Miller, and Christopher Contopulos, and cherished great-grandmother of Ashley Miller. We will always remember her with love. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the American Heart Association, 3020 Westchester Ave, Purchase, NY 10577.
