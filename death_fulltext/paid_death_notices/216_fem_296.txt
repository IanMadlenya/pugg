
        NACHAMIE--Carole Jon. Of Riverdale, NY & Lincolnton, NC, died on January 18, 2005. Services were held January 21st. Beloved wife of David A. Nachamie, M.D., mother of Deirdre & Eric, grandmother of Gabriel, daughter of Benjamin Altman and the late Bernice Altman. Beloved aunt and sister-in-law. Graduate of the High School for Performing Arts and New York University. Accomplished pianist and historian. She will be missed by all. The Nachamie Family
