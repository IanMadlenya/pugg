
        RIEGER-Ernest A. Jr. On October 19, 1998. Beloved and loving husband of Susan Squier Rieger. Cherished father of Carolyn, Sally, Laurie and Chip, and grandfather of Max and Beau, Hilary and Sarah, Danielle, Eric and Peter Bruce, and Cicely. He is survived by sisters, Ruth Munson, Sally Daly and Carol Rieger, and brothers, Drs. Paul and Richard Rieger. One brother, Thomas, predeceased him. A memorial celebration of his life will be held on Friday, October 23rd, from 12:30-4:30, at Captain Bill's in Bay Shore. In lieu of flowers, please consider making a donation to the charity of your choice.
        RIEGER-Ernest A. The Mayor and Trustees of the Village of Saltaire are deeply shocked and saddened by the sudden death of Ernie Rieger, a true friend and gentleman of the old school who loved his community. Our profoundest condolences to his wife Susan and his family. Martin M. Berger, Mayor
