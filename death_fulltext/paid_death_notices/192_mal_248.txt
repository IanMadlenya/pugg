
        GREENSTEIN-Eli. 78, on January 17. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, brother and uncle. He is survived by his wife Rita (nee Rappoport), children Gail Wade, Carol and Nick Epstein, Myra and Peter Cole, grandchildren Nathalie and Ariel Wade, Lauren and Daniel Cole and sisters Gitl Lerner and Natalye Brown. A graduate of Townsend Harris High School and the City College of New York. Eli was an electrical engineer who continued on to program management and finished his professional career in marketing. Eli was a track official with BOCES for many years, volunteered with the Hicksville Interfaith Nutrition Network and was a member of the Disaster Assistance Team of the Nassau County Red Cross. He was a supporter of Channel 21 and the American Association of University Women. Services today, 11 AM, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
