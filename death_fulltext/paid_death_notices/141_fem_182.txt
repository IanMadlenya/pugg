
        KANAREK - Adele. American Friends of Yeshivot Bnei Akiva mourns the passing of a true Eishet Chayil, a magnificent lady who personified grace, dignity and strength. Together with her beloved husband, Dr. Irwin, Adele was a staunch supporter of Yeshivot Bnei Akiva and all worthy causes on behalf of Torah, the Jewish People, and the Land of Israel. May she continue to be a source of inspiration and blessing for Irwin and her lovely children and grandchildren. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marvin S. Bienenfeld, Chairman Joel M. Schreiber, President Rabbi David Warshaw, Exec. Dir.
