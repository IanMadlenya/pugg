
        WEINBLATT--Myron ''Mike.'' On August 18, 2005. Served as President of NBC Entertainment, Showtime/The Movie Channel, Inc., and Multimedia Entertainment. Executive Vice President of the Board of The League for the Hard of Hearing. Beloved husband of Annie, devoted father of Marc and Allison, Richard and Anne. Loving grandfather of Shae, Orion & Michael. Donations in his memory may be made to The League for the Hard of Hearing, 50 Broadway, NYC 10004.
