
        ADELSON-Judah. The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center records with deep sorrow the passing of Professor Judah Adelson, member of the Associate Board of Directors since 1977. Professor Adelson was an active member who in 1990 received the Brookdale Medal, the highest distinction the hospital confers. Our sincere condolences to his entire family. Edwin L. Schulman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Frank J. Maddalena, President and CEO Samuel Plotkin, Chairman, Associate Board Sheldon S. Berman, MD, Sr. Vice President and CMO
