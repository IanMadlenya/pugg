
        BRADY -- Mary (Hagerty) ''Mimi,'' of Scarsdale, NY, passed into eternal life on March 1st, after a brief illness. A graduate of Manhattanville College ('48), she is survived by her five children: Mary Elizabeth Brady of Scarsdale, NY; Edward A. Brady, Jr. of White Plains, NY; Suzanne Forlivio of New Britain, CT; Margot Hill and her husband, William Hill, of Stoneham, MA; and Thomas C. Brady of Bedford Hills, NY. She is also survived by her brother, Harry C. Hagerty, Jr., and his wife, Mary Ann Hagerty, of Sea Island, GA. Mimi is also survived by three granddaughters, Leah Forlivio, Meaghan and Shannon Brady. Family will receive friends Wednesday, March 3rd, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave., Scarsdale, NY 10583, (914) 725-1137. Mass of Christian Burial will be held Thursday, 10 AM, at Our Lady of Fatima Church, Strathmore Rd., Scarsdale, (914) 723-7421, followed by interment at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made to Mimi's memory to Our Lady of Fatima Vestment Fund.
