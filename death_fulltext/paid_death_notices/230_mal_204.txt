
        BARNARD--Charles W., III(Chick) Retired Senior International Insurance Executive for American Foreign Insurance Association and Cigna, died March 11, 2006 at the age of 80. A native of Newburgh, he graduated from Storm King School and then served in the U.S. Army Air Corps during W.W. II. Chick graduated from Amherst College in 1949 and began his successful insurance career with his first posting in Calcutta, India. Later service with the company took him back and forth to a variety of positions in Japan and the United States. He retired in 1985 and settled in Cape Cod, moving to Jacksonville, FL in 2004. Chick was an avid tennis player and was active in alumni affairs for Amherst College. He was widely respected for his achievements and experience in the insurance industry. Chick is survived by his wife Margaret (nee Blue) ''Brownie'' Barnard, his sons Andrew (Sarah) of Dobbs Ferry, NY and Daniel of Boston, daughter Adrienne (Daniel) Dahlberg of Missoula, MT, two grandchildren Samantha Barnard and Benjamin Dahlberg. Another son, Stephen, died in 1979. Funeral service at 9:30 AM Monday, Edwards - Dowdle Funeral Home, Dobbs Ferry (Saw Mill Exit 17). Visiting Sunday 79PM. Those wishing to make a memorial contribution may donate to The National Parkinson's Foundation, 1501 NW 9th Ave., Miami, FL 33136.
