
        TOWNSEND-Robert Chase. A memorial service will be held for Robert C. Townsend on Tuesday March 17 at 11 AM at All Saints Church, 855 Middle Neck Rd., Great Neck, Long Island, N.Y. Mr. Townsend, who died on the island of Anguilla on January 12, 1998 had been a Senior Vice President of American Express for many years and Chairman and CEO of Avis Rent a Car. His books on management and leadership were international best sellers. In lieu of flowers, please make a contribution to the Midnight Mission, 396 S. Los Angeles St., Los Angeles, CA 90013, attn: Clancy.
