
        LEVIN--Samuel Benedict. It is with the deepest grief that David and Roger Levin announce the death of their father, Benedict Levin on December 31, 2003 at the age of 93. Dr. Levin was Deputy Secretary in the Department of Defense, directing defenserelated basic research, principally in communication technologies, before accepting the office of Executive Vice President of the Earth Satellite Corporation. He retired in 1974 to live with wife Helen in Warren, Ct.
