
        COMATAS--George J. Age 73, died suddenly on October 12th, 2004. Beloved husband of Marina for 45 years. Loving father to Debbie, John and the late Alice. Loving father-in-law to Pat and Michele and cherished grandfather of MaryAlexa, Joseph, George and Michael. Loving brother of Aspasia, Helen, Catherine and Gus. Brotherin-law of John and Agatha. He was born in Perista, Greece and came to the United States in 1947 at the age of 16. He served in the US Army during the Korean War. He built a successful flower business in NYC which he owned and operated for nearly 50 years. He was a loving family man and friend who will be missed by many. Family will receive friends on Friday 24pm and 6-9pm at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Funeral services Saturday 9am at the Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Church, 319 East 74th Street. Interment to follow at Mt. Hope Cemetery. If desired, memorial donations can be given to the Ronald McDonald House, East 73rd Street, NYC, NY 10021.
