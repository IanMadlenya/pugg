
        SULZBERGER--Maye. Died on Saturday night November 26th after a long life well lived. Maye is survived by her loving and beloved family. Services were held at Frank E. Campbell's; interment is at Beth-El Cemetery, Elmont, NY. Comprehensive NY Times obit ran earlier this week. Memorial contributions, if desired, to Mt. Sinai Hospital (NYC) attn: Dr. Alvin Teirstein.
