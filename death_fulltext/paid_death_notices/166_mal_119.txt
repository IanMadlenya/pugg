
        HAFER-Henry M. 83, lawyer, on November 10. Beloved husband of Elsie; loving father of Carol and Sally Hafer; William, Edward and Richard Shain; grandfather of Patrick and Will Reina, Charles, Rebecca, Samantha, and Albertine Shain. Graduate of Horace Mann, NYU and NYU Law School. Retired Captain, U.S. Army WW II veteran. Dear friend to all who knew him. Devoted Mets fan. Donations to Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021 would be appreciated.
