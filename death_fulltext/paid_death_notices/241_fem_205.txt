
        LEVY--Muriel Andria (nee Spalinger) on February 20, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Hyman Levy. Devoted mother of the late Jerrold Levy. Loving mother of Lauren Gilmour (Brian). Cherished grandmother to Theowen, Susannah, and Edward Gilmour. Born on October 27, 1915 in Riverhead, New York, Mrs. Levy began training as a nurse at Mt. Sinai Hospital in 1933. From 1942 to 1946, she served in the 108th General Hospital, United States Army in Egypt, Italy, and France. Funeral services will be at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue (at 81st Street), 9:30 am, Monday, February 26, 2007.
