
        BERGER--Ruth (nee Miller). of Brooklyn, N.Y. Died peacefully on June 5, 2005 at the age of 87. She spent the prior day with her loving children Bill, Linda Levine, Carol Feingold and her brother Phil. She was predeceased by her beloved husband Harry, son Michael and brother Morty, and is survived by seven grandchildren and seven great grandchildren. Graveside service today, 1PM, Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, N.Y.
