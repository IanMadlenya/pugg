
        BUELER--Hans G. of Somers, New York, died peacefully on March 1, 2005, after a long illness. He is survived by his devoted wife of 20 years, ''Pat'' Bueler and by his son, Roland G. Bueler of New York City. He was born in Amriswil, Switzerland, the youngest of four children of Martha and Gustav Bueler, an architect. In 1935, Hans left for Cochin, India, thereby beginning a 37-year affiliation with Volkart Bros., a distinguished Swiss commodity firm operative worldwide. After 15 years in India, Hans joined the firm's principal office in Winterthur, Switzerland and between 1965 and 1972 was president and chief executive of Volkart USA. Hans was a man of great integrity and strength of character, who actively embraced every aspect of life. In Switzerland he is presently survived by seven nieces and nephews. Reposing at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, New York on Thursday from 2-5PM. Private interment will be at Putnam Cemetery, Greenwich, CT
