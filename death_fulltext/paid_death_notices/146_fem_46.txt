
        CLARK-Mary Frances (nee Hayes) March 1, 1999, at home in Nashua, NH. Also of Sarasota, FL. Formerly of Burlington, VT. Beloved wife of Edward A. Clark. Cherished mother of Mary Ellen and Joseph Levin, Frances and Bruce Wenner, Kathleen Clark, Joan Clark and William Guis, John Clark, and Edward and Candice Clark. Dear grandmother of 13, and great-grandmother of four. For information, call Anctil-Rochette Funeral Home, Nashua. Mass of Christian Burial, Immaculate Conception Church. Interment St. Louis Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Home Health and Hospice Care, 22 Prospect St., Nashua, NH 03060.
