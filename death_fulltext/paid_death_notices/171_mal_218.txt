
        SCHWARTZ-Rabbi Lester A. We record with profound sadness the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained after a career in business, he served pulpits in Georgia, Connecticut and Florida before his retirement in 1999. We express our deepest condolences to his wife, Rosalind Diane, children, Gayle, Eric, and Stephen and to all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Vernon H. Kurtz, Pres. Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec., V.P. The Rabbinical Assembly
