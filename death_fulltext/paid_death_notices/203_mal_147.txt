
        GARDINER--James B. Died unexpectedly December 11, at the age of 96. He is survived by his daughter Cynthia of Tucson, AZ and son James of Burlington, VT and three grandchildren. Mr. Gardiner had a long and distinguished career as an actuary with MetLife and the State of NY retiring at 95, and was an active member of many genealogical societies. He loved mountain climbing and math. His unfailing good humor will be missed by all who knew him, and his passing is deeply grieved. Funeral will be private. Memorial donations may be made to People for the American Way, 1460 Broadway, NY, NY 10036.
