
        ARRIAN-Murray. Died November 21, 1999 in West Palm Beach, Fla. at age 78. Formerly of NYC. Beloved husband of Claire for 56 years, devoted father of Barbara and Shelley and father-in-law to Mark Gelblat and Howard Fleit. Adoring grandfather to Dana and Kenneth Burton, Halayne Gelblat, Rachel and David Fleit, uncle to Regina Hazen. Wonderful friend to many. His smile will be greatly missed. He will be remembered as a kind and gentle man.
