
        FUERSTMAN--Myron N. Beloved husband of Noreen. Loving father of Joseph and Elizabeth. Cherished grandfather of Isabella, Margaux and Joseph. We will miss you immensely. Mr. Fuerstman served overseas in the paratroops during W.W. II and enjoyed a long career in the textile industry, distinguishing himself as an innovator of wash and wear fabrics. Service Wednesday, 12 P.M. at Lloyd Maxcy Beauchamp Chapel, New Rochelle, NY.
