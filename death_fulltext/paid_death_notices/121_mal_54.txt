
        MILLER-Sylvia, 97, on February 2, 1997 at the Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale. Formerly a resident of Great Neck, beloved wife of the late Irving Miller, devoted mother of Jack and his wife Gladys, Alfred and his wife Joan, Arnold and his wife Linda, loving grandmother of David, Barbara, Wendy, Paul and his wife Donna, Asher and Tamara, and adoring great-grandmother of Marc. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, February 5, at 2 P.M., at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, 15 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, opposite LIRR Station.
