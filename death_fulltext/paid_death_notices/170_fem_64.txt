
        SCHATTNER-Bertha. 101. On March 7, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Morris. Mother of Adele and Herman Brav, Henry and Shirley Lipper, Bernard and Norma Schattner. Passed away after a prolong illness. She is survived by eight grandchildren and 13 great grandchildren. She was a survivor of the Holocaust and was a lifelong supporter of Israel and admired by all who knew her. Services at Parkside Chapels, 2576 Flatbush Ave, Bklyn, 1:30PM, March 9th. Interment Mount Lebanon Cemetery.
