
        GAINES--Jack. (Born Jacob Ginsberg) on March 7, peacefully, in his sleep, in his home in Tiburon at the age of 86. Beloved father of Nora, and beloved husband of Eugenija, adored uncle of Judy, Howard, Elliot, Lynn, and Hedy. He leaves us a legacy of intense curiosity, passionate intellect, and deep love of Israel and his people. Donations may be made to Trauma Treatment Center for Children, Unit of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry, Hadassah Hospital, P.O. Box 12000, Jerusalem 91120 Israel.
