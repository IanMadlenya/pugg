
        GUILD--Marian Stedman. On March 25 at age 104. She was born in Cleveland, OH on September 15, 1901 and lived there and in New York City before moving to Montclair, NJ in 1971. Wife of the late Kenneth H. Guild. Survived by a brother Charles Arthur Stedman; a daughter Nancy Weidner, a son William K. Guild; granddaughters Alyson Harkins and Cynthia Weidner; great granddaughters Courtney and Alexis Bowling, and Grace McInerney. A memorial service will be held at 1PM Saturday, April 1 at St. James Episcopal Church, 581 Valley Rd, Upper Montclair, NJ 07043. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the St. James Memorial Fund.
