
        FLUSSER-Richard. July 1, 2002. Opera impresario and founder of the After Dinner Opera Company, died in his sleep, at home. Beloved husband of Beth Flusser, Richard was father of Jennifer-jo Moyer and Matthew, fatherin-law to Daniel and Kathleen, beloved grandfather of Padraig and Myles, and sonin-law to Annette Leibowitz. Professor Emeritus of Music at Queensborough Community College. There will be a memorial service at St. Mark's Church, 10th Street and Second Avenue July 23, 7:30 PM.
