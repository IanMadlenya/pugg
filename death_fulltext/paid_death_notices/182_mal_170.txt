
        KELSEY-Stephen Tomlinson, Jr., 85. Of Berwyn, PA, died March 7, 2002. He was predeceased by his wife of forty years, Nancy Bryce Kelsey. He is survived by his wife Margaret Kipp Kelsey; his daughters Melie, Jenny and Margot, and five grandchildren. Memorial service will be held on Saturday, March 16th at 2:00 PM, St. Elizabeth's Church, Ridgewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Island People's Project, Fishers Island, NY 06390.
