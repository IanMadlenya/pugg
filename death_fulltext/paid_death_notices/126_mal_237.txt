
        LESSER-Murray C., 89. Retired President of Goldfield Towel Supply. Died at home in Long Boat Key, Florida on July 11, 1997. He is survived by Marjory, his wife of fifty-five years and his son, Leonard, his daughter Dee Clayman, his grandchildren Adam and Jon Lesser and Gregory and Thomas Clayman. Memorial services July 28 at Mount Hebron Cemetery, Flushing at 11 A.M.
