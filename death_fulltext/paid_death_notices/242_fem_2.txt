
        THOMPSON--Allyn. Beloved wife of William Peterson, passed away February 22, after a brief illness. Cherished friend of so many, her inquiring mind, insatiable curiosity, sharp wit, love of animals, and deep compassion for people will be deeply missed. She is also survived by her sister Allyn Story and brother Robert Thompson. Memorial service Saturday, March 3, 4 pm at the Second Presbyterian Church, Central Park West and 96th Street.
