
        SHAPP-Martha, age 93. Died peacefully on May 29, 2000 in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. She is the widow of Charles M. Shapp. Loving mother of William Glauber and step-son Mark Shapp. Mrs. Shapp was a graduate of Barnard College and had been chosen to be a member of the U.S. Olympic Swimming team, Editor-in-Chief of ''New Book of Knowledge'', and numerous other publications. No services were held.
