
        HARNICK--Leon. 86, of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Queens, NY, on August 22, 2003. Predeceased by his loving wife Rhoda. Survived by his three children Arlene, Paul and Janet, and eight grandchildren. Past President of Hillcrest Jewish Center, Queens, NY, Past President of the CCNY Alumni Association, Broward County.
