
        TOBIN-SCHATTNER-Lenore. The ''leading lady'' of the theatre party industry for over thirty years, passed away on September 1st. Survived by her sister, Jane Muccio, and her surrogate children, Patricia and Ronald Lee; and grandchildren Alison, Gillian, David, Stephanie and Christopher Lee; and her cousin, Kay Blick and her family. Remember her by contributing to the Actors' Fund of America, 1501 Broadway, New York, New York 10036 (whose nursing home took such wonderful care of Lenore in her final years).
