
        SCANNELL-James B., DDS. The staff of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center is deeply saddened by the death of their friend and colleague Dr. James B. Scannell. Dr. Scannell began his long association with Memorial in 1947 as a Memorial Hospital resident. He joined the staff of Memorial SloanKettering in 1966 as an attending dentist. During 1988 he served as Acting Chief of the Dental Service. He retired from Memorial SloanKettering in 1989. Dr. Scannell helped define the role of the dentist in the care of patients with head & neck cancers. His wisdom and friendship will be missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Dr. Scannell's family. Paul A. Marks M.D., President Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center David W. Golde M.D., Chairman Medical Board and Physician-in-Chief Memorial Hospital for Cancer and Allied Diseases
