
        WOLF--Gerald L., M.D. The SUNY Downstate Medical Center community mourns the passing of Gerald L. Wolf, M.D., professor emeritus and former vice chairman of anesthesiology. Dr. Wolf was well known for his research, most recently concerning the prevention of surgical fires. He also was a much-admired teacher, lecturer, author and mentor. Our condolences are extended to his family, colleagues, and friends. John C. LaRosa, M.D., President Eugene B. Feigelson, M.D., Dean of the College of Medicine James C. Cottrell, M.D. Chair of Anesthesiology
