
        STEWART--Thomas. Great artist, majestic singer, valued mentor and beloved friend, Thomas Stewart touched many lives as a superb performer, adored colleague and wise teacher. We will miss him terribly but no one will suffer his loss so deeply as his adored Evelyn. With our love and respect, Matthew A. Epstein Director of Vocal Divisions CAMI
        $(6$)STEWART--Thomas. The Metropolitan Opera mourns the death of our friend and colleague, Thomas Stewart, who sang with us from 1966 to 1993. His vivid portrayals of heroic baritone roles were seen in 24 operas at the Met including ten new production premieres. we offer our sincere condolences to his wife Evelyn Lear, and all his family and friends. Peter Gelb, General Manager James Levine, Music Dir.
