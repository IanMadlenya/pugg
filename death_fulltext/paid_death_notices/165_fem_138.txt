
        WARREN-Anne Mishler, 85. Of Red Bank died Wednesday 10/11. She was the Administrative Assistant of the Training Department of Merril Lynch for many years. She was predeceased by her husband, Edward, her daughter Bette Jane Soltano. She was survived by two daughters, Wendy Tooker of Red Bank, NJ, Heide Goldstein of Marathon, NY, seven grandchildren, Lisa Paige Soltano, Andrew Soltano, Dr. Emily Soltano, Josh Tooker, Jessica Voglesong, Amy Hughston and Joey Steinhagen, two great-grandchildren, Dale Hughston and Casey Voglesong. Graveside services 11:00 Monday at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn. Bloomfield-Cooper Jewish Chapels, Ocean Township.
