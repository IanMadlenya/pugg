
        RICCOBONO-Beatrice M. Kaye. Downsville, NY, originally of New York City, peacfully passed May 27, 2000. She is survived by her beloved husband Justin of many joyful years and her stepson Lewis B. Kaye. Although educated and trained as a registered nurse, she is well known as a Manhattan real estate broker with exclusive expertise in the sale of townhouses. Visitation Wednesday, May 31, MacArthur Funeral Home, 131 Main St., Delhi, NY, 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Mass/burial Thursday, June 1, 10 AM, St. Peters Church, Delhi.
