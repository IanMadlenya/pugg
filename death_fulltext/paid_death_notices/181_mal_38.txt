
        McELWAIN-Jack W., M.D. The American Society of Colon and Rectal Surgeons mourns the passing of our dedicated colleague and friend. Dr. McElwain, who died January 30th at the age of 80, was an internationally known surgeon who advanced the specialty of Colon and Rectal Surgery. His brilliant work and teaching inspired a generation of surgeons. His loyal commitment to his patients and colleagues will be long remembered. Robert Fry, M.D., President American Society of Colon and Rectal Surgeons
