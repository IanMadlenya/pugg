
        GOODMAN -- Arthur. Suc cumbed to lymphoma on Monday, October 27, 2003. His loss is sustained by his second wife Patricia Clancy, his children Judith Finn, Howard Goodman and Sally Goodman-Schwartz; grandchildren Zachary, Katie, Aaron, Michael, Rebecca, Rachel and Lucy. Born on February 14, 1924, Arthur's first marriage to Beatrice Belford ended in divorce. In addition to building a highly successful answering service business, Arthur was also an avid small plane pilot, photographer and naturalist. His burial service will be held at 11:30am on Friday, October 31, 2003 at the Sharon Gardens-Kensico Cemetery in Valhalla, N.Y.
