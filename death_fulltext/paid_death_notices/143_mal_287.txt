
        ZAPAKIN-Harmon. Coordinating Council of Cooperatives, Coordinated Housing Services and 1st Rochdale Cooperative Group join the cooperative community in mourning the passing of our colleague, a true giant. For 40 years, Harmon quietly made a difference in the quality of life for countless New York Cooperators. Our heartfelt sympathy to his dear wife, Dorothy, beloved sons, Randy and Alan, and their families.
