
        MEHTA--Tehmina. The New York Philharmonic notes with sorrow the death in Los Angeles, on April 22, 2005 of Tehmina Mehta, 96, mother of former New York Philharmonic Music Director Zubin Mehta and current President and Executive Director Zarin Mehta, and wife of the late conductor and violinist Mehli Mehta. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her sons, and to the entire family. Lorin Maazel, Music Director Paul B. Guenther, Chairman, Board of Directors Members of the Orchestra & Staff of the NY Philharmonic
