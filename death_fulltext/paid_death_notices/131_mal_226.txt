
        HANEMAN-W. F. (Woof), Jr. Of Rye, N.Y. Died on Monday, December 15, 1997 of a massive heart attack. He was 50 years old. He was a Senior Vice President and a former Director of Legg Mason Wood Walker, Inc., of New York. Devoted husband of Nancy Jacobs Haneman to whom he was married for twenty-eight years. Beloved father of Patricia Stevens Haneman, 24, and Nicolle Prentiss Haneman, 21. Cherished son of Elizabeth Breed Haneman of Southbury, CT., and sister Lee Haneman Stinchcomb of New York. Son of the late William F. Haneman, a former investment advisor with Advest, Inc., and Legg Mason Wood Walker, Inc. of New York. He was a member of the decendants of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence and of the Society of Cincinnati. Visiting hours will be from 6PM to 8PM, on Wednesday, December 17, at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye. Memorial Service will be held at Christ's Episcopal Church in Rye at 10AM, Thursday, December 18th. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to The Nature Conservancy-Adirondack Chapter, P.O. Box 65, Keene Valley, New York 12943.
        HANEMAN-William F. Jr. (Woof). Our heartfelt condolences. The Board, staff and friends of the Rye YMCA
