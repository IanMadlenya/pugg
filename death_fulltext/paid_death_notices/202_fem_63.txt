
        GESUALDI--Mary, in her 102nd year, of Dyker Heights, Brooklyn, on November 5th, 2003. A scholarship recipient, Mary graduated from New Rochelle College as an accomplished French teacher in the NYC Board of Education. She was fondly admired by colleagues and students at New Utrecht H.S., 1923-1964. An honorary member of Arista, Mary was a language tutor and patron of the arts.
