
        FRANCE-Joan L. of Babylon Village, L.I. on August 12, 1997. Mrs. France was born on November 19, 1931 in Chicago, Illinois. She was raised in Huntington, L.I., attended Upsala College and graduated in 1951. Joan married her husband William P. France on April 25, 1951, and they resided together in New Jersey until moving to Babylon Village in 1970. Beloved wife of William P. France. Devoted mother of William E. France of Babylon and Cheryl L. Robbins of West Babylon. Adored grandmother of Katie France, David Robbins, Christopher France and Amy Robbins. Loving sister of Betty Francis Bjoro Jones of Arizona and Edwin Francis Bjoro of Florida. Reposing at The Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of Robert Moses Cswy.) Funeral service Friday 10 a.m. at Funeral Home. Interment to follow at Pinelawn Memorial Park, Pinelawn, L.I. Flowers will be welcomed and appreciated. Donations may also be made to The Babylon Rotary Foundation, c/o 80 West Main Street, Babylon, N.Y. 11702. Visiting Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 p.m.
