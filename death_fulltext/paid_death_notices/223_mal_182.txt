
        KAHN--Murray L., age 84, died at home on August 16, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Ada Kahn. Devoted father of Cynthia, Mitch and Claudia, grandfather to Alexis and Jason and father-in-law to Denise and Bruce, brother to Selma Gorman and the late Ernest Kahn. We mourn the loss of our brave and beloved father who was fiercely devoted to his family, emotionally connected and supportive beyond words. He will be profoundly missed. Shiva will be observed Thursday at the home of Dr. and Mrs. Mitch Kahn. Shiva will continue Friday (10AM-3PM) at the late residence. Shiva will conclude Sunday (1PM-6PM) at the home of Mr. and Mrs. Bruce Sherman. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made to the Michael J. Fox Parkinson's Foundation, earmarked for stem cell research. The Foundation's address is Grand Central Station, PO Box 4777, NY, NY 10163.
