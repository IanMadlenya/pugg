
        ROTHENBERG-Mark S. M.D. Age 97. Died June 11 after a long illness. Devoted husband of Ann for 63 years. Beloved father of Judy and Dr. Robert Podell. Adored grandfather of Dr. Debbie and Ari Fishkind and great-grandfather of Aaron Benjamin Fishkind. Dedicated physician for 71 years and loved by all his patients. Shiva at the Podell residence.
        ROTHENBERG-Dr. Mark. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Sisterhood mourn the passing of the beloved father of their devoted member, Judy Podell and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Rabbi Joshua Lookstein Chaim Edelstein, Pres.
        ROTHENBERG-Dr. Mark. Young Israel of Fifth Avenue mourns the death of the father of our esteemed member Judith Podell and extends to her and the bereaved family our heartfelt condolences.
