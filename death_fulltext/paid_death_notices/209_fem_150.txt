
        HUPPUCH--Helen E. Age 88. Of Croton-on-Hudson, NY, formerly Salem, Cross River, and Peekskill. Died June 18, 2004. Daughter of William and Helen Lillian (Warner) McMahan of Flora, IL, she attended Radcliffe College, where she met her late beloved husband of 65 years, Winfield A. Huppuch II, a student at Harvard Law School. She devoted her life to home and family and is survived by seven children, Winfield III, William, Frank, and Thomas Huppuch, Helen Mulry, Alice Gallasch and Katherine Holland, 13 grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren. Services will be private. For information call the E. O. Curry Funeral Home, 914-737-0083.
