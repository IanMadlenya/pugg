
        DAVIS-Rev. William J.. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal Church, 62-81 60th Place, Ridgewood, on May 17. Reposing in the Lower Church at Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal, Tuesday, May 18, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 P.M., and on Wednesday, May 19, from 2 to 5 P.M. Mass of Transferral on Wednesday, May 19, at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass on Thursday, May 20th, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
