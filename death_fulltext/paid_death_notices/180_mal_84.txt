
        de SEGONZAC-Adalbert. On December 27 at age 89. Washington correspondent of the French newspaper France - Soir, (1956-1976). Much decorated fighter pilot with Free French forces during WWII. Survived by his children: Lionel de Segonzac of Paris, Catherine Shainberg, Laurence Levin and Jean de Segonzac of New York. He will be sorely missed.
