
        MARTIN-Stan. Henry Street Settlement's Board of Directors and its Boys and Girls Republic Advisory Board mourn the passing of Stan Martin, Advisory Board Member and Trustee for over 20 years. We thank him for his many years of service and dedication to the youth of the Lower East Side. We send our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Audrey Rosenman, Chairman Christopher C. Angell, President
