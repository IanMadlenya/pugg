
        WEISBERG--Fay (nee: Goodman), wife of the late Leopold Weisberg, mother of Martin Weisberg, mother-in-law of Tracey Weisberg, devoted grandmother of Margot and Clayton, sister of Arthur Goodman, sister-in-law of Helene Goodman and Harriet and Marty Shapiro and aunt to her nieces and nephews, passed away peacefully at home on April 17th, 2007, just before her 81st birthday. Fay retired from Nassau County BOCES where she was a dedicated and revered social worker to students and families. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate donations to: My Second Home An Intergenerational Center, 90 Radio Circle, Mt. Kisco, NY.
