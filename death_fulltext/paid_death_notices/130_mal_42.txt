
        de ROTHSCHILD-Edmond. The leadership and staff of Israel Bonds mourn the death of Baron Edmond de Rothschild, a longtime and generous supporter of Israel's economic development. His warmth, leadership and great contributions to Israel and the world Jewish community will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Baroness Nadine de Rothschild, and their son Benjamin. Sam Rothberg, Julian Venezky Founders of Israel Bonds Gideon Patt President Burton P. Resnick National Campaign Chairman William Belzberg Chairman of the Board Susan Weikers-Volchok International Campaign Chairman Michael Siegal North American Campaign Chairman
