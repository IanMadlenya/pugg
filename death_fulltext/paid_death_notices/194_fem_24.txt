
        BROWN-Joan Gallagher of Greenwich, Ct. died on March 3, 2003. She was born on March 18, 1923 in Bklyn, NY and was the devoted wife of Peter Campbell Brown who predeceased her in 1994. She is survived by Peter C. Jr. and June, Patricia Joan, Thomas G. and Kerry, Michael F. and Lisa and Robert J. and Nancy and nine grandchildren. Family will receive friends on Wednesday, March 5 from 24pm & 7-9pm at Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY. Mass of Christian burial Thursday, March 6, 10am at The Church of the Resurrection, Rye, NY.
