
        BEDFORD-Pauline (nee Allalouf). Age 77, passed away October 11, 2002 after a brave battle. Paula was raised on the Lower East Side, was a truly devoted mother, and never knew how smart she was. She is survived by brother Maury Allalouf, and son & daughter Robert and Felice Bedford. Service on October 16, at 11:30 AM at Nieberg's Midwood Chapel, Brooklyn.
