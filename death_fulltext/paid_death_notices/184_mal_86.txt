
        PLOTKIN-Samuel. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Samuel Plotkin, a cherished friend who expressed a commitment to helping those in need. Mr. Plotkin's legacy lives on in the exemplary activities and extraordinary generosity of the Plotkin family. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mr. Plotkin's son, Fred, and his wife, Janet; to his son, Stuart, and his wife, Anita; to his daughter, Gail Staal, and her husband, Murray; to his daughter, Talib Ilutzi, and her husband, Herb Schwartz; to his 8 grandchildren; to his two great-grandchildren; and to the entire Plotkin family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
