
        CHANKO - Gladys Brand. Died peacefully in her sleep April 25, 2003. Beloved mother and best friend of Carol Leifer Leeds. Adored mother-in-law of the late Mike Leeds. Grandmother ''Lolly'' to Debbie (Howard) LeiferSchwartz and Jerry (Debra) Leifer. Adored stepmother to Toni (Edward) Stern. Grandmother to Karen and Jill. Adored stepmother to James (Robin) Chanko. Beloved great-grandmother and cherished friend of the Leeds family. A woman of beauty, class and dignity. Ahead of her time, she was a successful stockbroker in the 1950s. ''Lolly will be missed by all'' forever.
