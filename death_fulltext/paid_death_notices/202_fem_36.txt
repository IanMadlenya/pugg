
        HELLINGER--Eleanor (De Young). On November 3, 2003 at age 84. Mother of Doug, Steve, John, and Bob; grandmother of Danny, Sam, Laura, and Abigail; sister of Stella Donenfeld. She was a lady and a good sport; a woman with warmth, intelligence, compassion, and class. We dearly loved her and she will be sorely missed. Services 12 noon Friday at Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, N.Y.
