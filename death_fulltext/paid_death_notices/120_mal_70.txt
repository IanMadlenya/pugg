
        BRODIN - Pierre, Dr. The Lycee Francais de New York notes with profound sorrow the death on January 1, 1997 of Dr. Pierre Brodin, its Director fo Studies for over 40 years and a senior member of its Board of Trustees. Dr. Brodin was an esteemed intellectual and a dedicated and kindly teacher. His many old students and colleagues on the faculty will particularly miss this fine personification of classical French education. The entire Lycee community shares his loss and expresses its sorrow to his widow, Dorothy Brodin, and his family. A service will be held on Saturday, January 11, at Saint Gregory the Great, 144 West 90 Street, New York at 11:00 AM.  Paul Windels, Jr.  Chairman of the board of  Trustees  Gerard Roubichou, President
