
        WILLIAMS-John Stowell. Age 65. Died Friday, December 22, 2000 at his home in Morristown, Vermont. Born on January 18, 1935 in New Britain, CT, son of Harold North Williams and Evelyn (nee Brooks) Williams. Former associate of Shearman and Sterling Law Firm of New York. Corporate Counsel for Western Electric. Survived by his wife, Linda Cannon-Huffman of Morristown, VT., son Eric R. Williams, daughter Lori J. Williams, sister Dorothy Jane Doudna of Hilo, HI., brother Charles North Williams of Southington, CT., and grandchildren Gabriel, Randall and Jennifer Williams. Memorial service to be held at Mr. Williams' home in Morristown, VT. later this Spring. Contributions in his memory would be appreciated to Nature Conservancy, Attention Treasury, 4245 North Fairfax Dr., Suite 100, Arlington, VA. 22203-1606.
