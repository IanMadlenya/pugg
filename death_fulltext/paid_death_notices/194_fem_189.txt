
        KLONSKY-Sally M., 92. Died in Redding, PA., March 28, 2003. Her husband Cantor Benjamin Klonsky died in 1994. Born in Russia, she was the daughter of the late Kobriner Rebbe Baruch Yosef and Yetta Sack. Survived by 4 children, eight grandchildren, seven great grandchildren, predeceased by two grandchildren. Services Sunday, March 30th, at Kesher Zion Synagogue, Redding, PA.
