
        ZUPPARDI--Cora A. 89. Born October 1, 1916, Kingston, Jamaica, died November 12, 2005, Canoga Park, CA. Beloved wife of the late Frank Zuppardi. Cora was a generous, caring, self-sacrificing Christian woman. Cora grew up in Jamaica, raised a family in NY, enjoyed senior years in CA. A wonderful sense of humor, she enjoyed cooking, gardening, the beach, cheering the Mets. Loving and devoted mother of Dorothy, Franklin, and Thomas and grandmother of Andrew. Loving sister of Herbert Johnston and aunt of Francy and Sharon. Contact Crawford Mortuary for Services, 8717 Tampa Ave, Northridge, CA. 91324, (818)349-9701.
