
        SHERMAN-Anna (Newta). On September 6th, 1998 at the age of 93. Beloved wife of the late Meir Sherman, Israel's first Economic Minister to the United States, first Chairman of the Board of Bank Leumi Trust Company of New York, & Managing Director of Paz Oil Company in Israel. Devoted sister to the late pianist Nadia Reisenberg Sherman, and the late theremin virtuoso Clara Rockmore. Survived by her son, Roniel, her granddaughters, Karen and Dina, nephews Alexander and Robert, & their families. Services on Friday, September 11, at 1:30 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
