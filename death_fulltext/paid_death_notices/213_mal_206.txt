
        STODDARD--John E. Age 72. Passed away peacefully at his home in Pennington NJ on Sunday. Mr. Stoddard was born in Brooklyn and graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 1954. He was a senior investment banker with Blyth Eastman Dillon & Co. before retiring in 1979. He was active in many Catholic organizations and was a Knight of Malta, Knight of the Holy Sepulchre and Knight of St. Gregory. He also served on many corporate and charitable boards. He is survived by his wife of 49 years, Joan Stoddard and their three children and nine grandchildren. For info, M. William Murphy Funeral Home, Trenton New Jersey, 609-882-3800.
