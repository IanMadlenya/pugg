
        MURRAY-Ralph Coughlan. Died on February 1, 2003, in Rye, New York, of complications following a stroke. An attorney and retired partner for the Wall Street law firm of Milbank, Tweed, Hadley and McCloy, Mr. Murray graduated from The College of the Holy Cross in Worcester, MA, in 1941. After serving in World War II, Mr. Murray completed his education at Columbia School of Law, where he was on the Law Review. Mr. Murray was an active parishioner of Resurrection Church in Rye and served there as a lector for many years. Friends may call on Friday from 5:30-8PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York. A funeral mass will be offered at Resurrection Church in Rye on Saturday at 10 AM.
