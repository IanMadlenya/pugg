
        STREUSAND-Ruth F. 82, of Royal Palm Beach, FL. Formerly of Scarsdale NY. On August 26, 2000. Retired Attorney for New York State Labor Relations Board. Graduate of Hunter College, Class of 1938 and Columbia Law School, Class of 1941. She was a Master Bridge player and active in Hadassah. Devoted mother of Howard, Lawrence and Judith. Two grandchildren, Nina and Evan. Funeral service will be today, August 29, 12 noon, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon NY. Interment at Sharon Gardens. Donations may be made to the American Civil Liberties Union
