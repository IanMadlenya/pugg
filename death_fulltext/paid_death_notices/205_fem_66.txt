
        LUNDBERG -- Elizabeth Young. 93, of Cary, NC, longtime resident of Chappaqua, NY, died in her sleep on January 30, 2004. Graduate and friend of Bryn Mawr College, actress, teacher, wife of author Ferdinand Lundberg (1902-1995). Liz lived with zest. She is survived by her two sons, Randolph and Laurence, and her two grandsons Derek and Conrad.
