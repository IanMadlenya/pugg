
        COHEN-Allan J. of Somers, NY and Orlando, Florida, died on July 25, 2001 at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, NY. He was 86. On December 24, 1939, he married Betty Thalmessinger. He is survived by his loving wife Betty, daughter Nancy, sons, Donald and Ken, 8 grandchildren, 3 greatgrandchildren, and his brother Malcolm. Calling hours Thursday 2-4 and 7-9pm at Clark Funeral Home in Yorktown Heights. Interment Friday 11 A.M. at Salem Fields Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
