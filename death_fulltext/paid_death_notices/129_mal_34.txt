
        MARGETT-Charles, The Honorable. Retired Justice of the Appellate Division of the New York State Supreme Court. Beloved husband and friend of Doris Rosen. Stepfather of Sanford Rosen. Dear uncle of Ronald and Monte Posner, and cherished granduncle. Services Monday, 1:00PM, at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, opposite LIRR.
        MARGETT-Charles. The Board of Trustees and Administration of Long Island Jewish Medical Center are deeply saddened by the death of the Honorable Charles Margett, one of the farsighted group of community activists involved in founding this hospital. In addition to his distinguished career, including 23 years as Justice of the Queens State Supreme Court, Judge Margett generously devoted his expertise and time to many community services and philanthropies. A Trustee of LIJ since 1954, his wise counsel will be deeply missed. Our condolences to his wife, Doris, and his family. Roy J. Zuckerberg, Chairman David R. Dantzker, M.D., President and CEO
        MARGETT-Hon. Charles. On October 2, 1997. The Board of Governors and the Members of Glen Oaks Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their esteemed member of 57 years and past President, and wish to extend their heartfelt sympathy to his wife Doris and his family. His wisdom and guidance will remain with us always. Board of Governors Richard A. Horowitz, Pres.
        MARGETT-Honorable Charles. Manhattan Guild sadly records the passing of its devoted member, Charles Margett and extends deepest sympathies to his beloved Doris and all the family.
