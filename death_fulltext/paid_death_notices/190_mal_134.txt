
        CUSACK - Maurice Joseph (1918-2002). Passed away peacefully October 28 in New York City. Predeceased by his beloved wife Yvonne. Survived by his brother Leopold & family. Past Vice President of Irving Trust (1957-1980). Awarded Spain's Titulo de Comendador de la Orden del Merito Civil. Memorial Service Church of St. Vincent Ferrer, 869 Lexington Ave. at 66th St., Thursday, November 14, at 2 PM. Flowers declined by request. Contributions to The Spanish Institute, 684 Park Avenue, NY, NY 10021 would be meaningful.
