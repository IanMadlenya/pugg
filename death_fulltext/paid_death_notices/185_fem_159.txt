
        GOLDFRANK - Helen. 89, died in Washington, DC, on June 21, 2002. She was an author of 35 books. She was most proud of her book ''Picasso's World of Children.'' Beloved wife of the late Herbert John Goldfrank. She is survived by her son Lewis, and daughter-in-law Susan, her daughter Joan, and sonin-law Michael, five grandchildren, and three greatgrandchildren.
