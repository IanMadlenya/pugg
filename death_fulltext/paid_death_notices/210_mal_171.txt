
        HAZZARD--Robert D., on July 14, 2004, of Somers, NY. Former Bedford town supervisor and Westchester County Legislator. Senior partner of Kent, Hazzard, Jaeger, Conroy, Verni and Freeman of White Plains, NY. Survived by his wife Gretchen, his children Jody, Halik, Lawrence and Robert Hazzard. Also his sister Sally Slaymaker and six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Friday, July 23, in St. Luke's Episcopal Church, Rt. 100, Somers, at 11:00AM. For those who wish, contributions to the NYU Melanoma Research Fund, C/O of Anna Pavlick, 160 East 32nd Street, 2nd floor, New York, NY 10016.
