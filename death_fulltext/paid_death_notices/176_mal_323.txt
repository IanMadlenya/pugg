
        HALL-Herman. The faculty and staff of The Nightingale-Bamford School express their profound sorrow at the loss of Mr. Hall, a beloved colleague since 1982. We will miss his wisdom and brilliant insight into the minds and hearts of Nightingale girls, young and old. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife and family. Dorothy A. Hutcheson, Head of School Grant F. Winthrop, President, Board of Trustees
