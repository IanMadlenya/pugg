
        BRAVERMAN-Adria T. The officers and members of the Composers, Authors and Artists of America, New York Chapter, mourn the loss of our beloved Director and Treasurer, Adria Braverman. Her contribution to the organization was immeasurable. We will miss her tremendous generosity of spirit, her complete dedication to all activities and her continued enthusiasm in enhancing our artistic pursuits. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her loving family, her devoted sons Alan and Marc, her daughter-in-law Jana Kay, and her cherished granddaughter Blair. Services today, Friday, September 27, 12 Noon, Parkside Chapel, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Rego Park NY.
