
        SOBEL--Anne. Died peacefully February 7, 2006 in Tequesta, FL at age 104. She was born in St. Petersburg, Russia and came to the United States at age 2. She was valedictorian of her class at Washington Irving High School, New York City. She was personal secretary to Frank Hardart of Horn and Hardart restaurants. He called her ''Lady Anne.'' She attended Columbia University School of Journalism. Subsequently, she worked with her late husband Jack in the wholesale beverage business. She is survived by her three children, Alice Teirstein, Judith Peck and Ralph Sobel, ten grandchildren, 17 great-grandchildren and two great-great-grandchildren.
