
        FREUDENHEIM - Abraham Z., M.D. Age 85, died peacefully on April 24, 2001. Loving father of Richard and Ellen, proud grandfather of Anna and David Wiener, dedicated husband to Judith and his deceased first wife, Zelma. Eldest son of immigrants, born in Watkins Glen, he attended medical school, served in the U.S. Army in WW II in India and established the first radiology dept at Good Samaritan Hospital, Suffern, NY. He will be remembered for his gentle ways, wry humor, and irrepressible intelligence. Services Friday 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Memorial contributions to either Rockland Holocaust Center, Spring Valley, NY 10977, or a scholarship earmarked for the children of immigrants to: A.Z. Freudenheim Fund, University of Buffalo Medical School, 102 Cary Hall, 3435 Main St, Buffalo, NY 14214. Checks to University Buffalo Foundation.
