
        KEATING-Edward F. Jr., 71. Of Hilton Head Island, SC, died on September 1, 1999 at the Hilton Head Medical Center and Clinics after a long illness. Loving husband of 46 years to Lila Elliott Keating and caring father to Edward, Elizabeth, Michael, Mary and Matthew. Dear brother to Jack, Dick, Jill and Joan, brotherin-law to Tom, Carole, Ann, Sandra and Harvey, and ''Popsie'' to nine grandchildren. Born in New York City, son of the late Edward F. and Mary McCormick Keating, Ed attended the Portsmouth Priory and graduated from Yale University in 1948. He served in the U.S. Air Force during the Korean War, stationed at RAF Sculthorpe in England. After a 31 year career in marketing with American Can Company, Greenwich, CT., and Reads Limited, London, England, Ed established Robbins & Keating Intl., in partnership with Sanford R. Robbins. Since 1993 Ed and Lila resided on Hilton Head Island where they made their home at the Long Cove Club. Visitation will take place on Monday, September 6, at the Bennett Funeral Home in Scarsdale, N.Y., from 5:00-8:00 P.M. The funeral service will be held at Immaculate Heart of Mary in Scarsdale on Tuesday, September 7, at 10:00 a.m. Donations may be made in Ed's memory to the Burke Rehabilitation Center, White Plains, N.Y., and to Meals on Wheels, Hilton Head, SC.
