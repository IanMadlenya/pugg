
        BURKE-John R., 87, of Bronxville, NY, formerly of East Syracuse, died Saturday in St. Joseph's Hospital after a long illness. He graduated from Christian Brothers Academy. He lived in Bronxville, NY, for the past 50 years. He retired from Saks Fifth Avenue and Bonwit Teller. He was an Army veteran of World War II. Surviving are two brothers, Paul C. of Illinois and Robert E. of Rochester, NY; one sister, Anne Burke Tucker of East Syracuse; and several nieces and nephews. Services are private. There are no calling hours. Burial will be in St. Mary's Cemetery, DeWitt, NY. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
