
        LEVINE-Abraham, Esq. Died peacefully on August 30, 1997, in Tamarac, FL, at the age of 87. Beloved and cared for by his children Sara, Joel and Charles, and especially by daughter-in-law Suarni during his last days. Survived by brother Louie and sister Tillie Lipp, by son-in-law Jeff Goodman and grandchildren Gabriel, Sara Rose, Jonathan and Jaimie Ross. His wit and generosity touched all who knew him throughout his long and fruitful life. He now joins his cherished wife and life-partner Rose, in eternal rest. Family and friends will gather for services at Parkside Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd and 66th Ave, Queens, Tuesday, September 2, at 10 AM.
