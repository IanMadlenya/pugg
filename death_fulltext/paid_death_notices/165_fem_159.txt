
        WELSH-Dorothy Jean Bristol. Of Remsenburg, NY, formerly of Forest Hills, died October 14, 2000. She is survived by two children, Elizabeth Welsh Ahnell and James Winfield Welsh III; her sister Elizabeth B. Evans of Santa Barbara, CA, and six grandchildren. Memorial Service will be held on Tuesday at 1 P.M. at St. Mark's Church, Main St., Westhampton Beach, NY. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to St. Mark's Church or East End Hospice, Westhampton Beach, NY.
