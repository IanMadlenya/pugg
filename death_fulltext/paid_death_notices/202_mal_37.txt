
        WEBB--William Jay Gaynor. 73, of Vero Beach, FL died November 4th, 2003 at Indian River Memorial Hospital, Vero Beach, FL after a brief illness. Surviving are his wife of 47 years, Carol Webb of Vero Beach, FL, daughters, Eliza Webb Eppli, Electra Tortorella and Angela Kensinger and five grandchildren.
