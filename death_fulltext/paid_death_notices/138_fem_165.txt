
        NALL-Violet Lillian (nee Collison). Age 67, of New York City, died July 2, 1998. Beloved wife of Milas Lee. Devoted mother of Estella Grace. She is also survived by brother Dwain, sisters Effie McMartin and Beryl McCracken, and many nieces and nephews, all of whom loved her and will miss her.
