
        THABIT-Afifeh Vivian (nee Diab). Born in Beirut, Lebanon, studied at the American University of Beirut, a member of the UN Delegations Women's Club and the Islamic Heritage Society, passed away after a long illness on February 5, 1998. She has been active in ArabAmerican affairs and the diplomatic Arab community at the UN for thirty years. She leaves surviving Robert W. Thabit, her husband, Allen R. Thabit, her son and Mona V. Thabit, her daughter. The funeral mass will be at the Saint Nicholas Antiochian Orthodox Cathedral in Brooklyn at 10 A.M., on Monday, February 9th, 1998. Interment will be at the Greenwood Cemetery's Hillside Mausoleum III.
