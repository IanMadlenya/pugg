
        HECKLER-Milton. Born October 18, 1918, died on January 6, 2001, husband of Janet, and beloved father of Robert and Susan. A lover of books and the sea, and a teacher of French and Spanish in New York City schools for many years, he'll be greatly missed. Donations in his memory may be made to the ADL, The Simon Wiesenthal Center, and The Fresh Air Fund.
