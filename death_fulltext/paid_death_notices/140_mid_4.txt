
        KOLKER-Rosa (nee Jacobson). The Officers, members of the Board of Directors and Staff of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) deeply mourn the passing of Rosa Kolker (nee Jacobson), beloved mother of Jonathan W. Kolker, President of the JDC. She was an outstandiing woman and her presence will be greatly missed by all who knew her. We express our heartfelt sympathy to Jonathan, his wife, Judy, his sons, Alex and Robert, and his daughter, Frieda Hallock. Michael Schneider, Exec V.P. Amb. Milton A. Wolf, Ch of Brd
