
        CONNELLY-John E. Jr. Died March 29, in Elizabethtown, NY, at 93. A graduate of Middlebury College and Harvard Law School, he was a founding partner of the law firm of Olwine, Connelly, Chase, O'Donnell & Weyher in NYC. He is survived by his wife, Evelyn R. Connelly, of Westport, NY; his brother, Albert R., of NYC; two daughters, Susan Painter and Lynn Grivakes; two sons, John E. III and Guy; ten grandchildren, and five great-grandchidlren. Contributions may be made to the Westport, NY, Emergency Squad.
