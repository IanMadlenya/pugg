
        GREENFIELD-Harvey. The employees and Board of Directors of the Citizens State Bank, Sealy, TX are saddened by the passing of Harvey Greenfield, who was one of our shareholders. Mr. Greenfield was our good friend and we will always remember and appreciate the loyal support and guidance that he extended to us. Leroy Zapalac, President & Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Citizens State Bank, Sealy, TX
