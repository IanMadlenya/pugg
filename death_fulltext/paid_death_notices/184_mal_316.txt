        CAPELLUTO-Victor. Died peacefully at home on Sunday, May 26, 2002. He was 96. Beloved and devoted husband of Laura, cherished father of Betty and Shirley, beloved father in law of James Emden and Richard Benowitz, adored grandfather of Michael, Jason, Andrew and Jennifer. A true ''gentleman'' in every sense of the word. Victor will be missed by all who experienced his kindness, wisdom and generosity. In his memory, contributions and be made to: Congregation Shearith Israel, 2 West 70 St, NY, NY 10024.
