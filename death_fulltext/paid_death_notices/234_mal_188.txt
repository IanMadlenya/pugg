
        PURCELL--Irving G. Loving husband and dedicated father, passed away at the age of 89. Irving received a BS degree from City College, MBA from Baruch, JD degree from Brooklyn Law, and a postgraduate LLM degree from NYU. He served as a meteorologist for the US Airforce in the rank of Tech Sergeant during WWII. He was admitted to the New York State Bar in 1955, member of the US Supreme Court Bar in 1961. For over 40 years, Irving demonstrated an unparallelled work ethic as self-made entrepreneur, Wall Street tax attorney and CPA. Irving is survived by his wife Susanne, married for 59 years, daughter Evelyn Silberstein, son Dr. Ralph Purcell, and five grandchildren, Jeanette, Peter, Ian, Stephen, and Brandon.
