
        JACCOMA--George E. Formerly of Larchmont, NY, died at Connecticut Hospice on May 20 of cancer. He was 72. George's career in advertising spanned over 50 years, during which time he won numerous awards in art direction. At Grey Advertising he introduced the split screen in a Ford Pinto commercial many years before computer graphics. At J. Walter Thompson he named and art directed the Kibbles & Bits commercials for Quaker Oats. In 1987 George honored a dream and became a partner at Marshall Jaccoma Mitchell Advertising. His wife Lorraine, son Larry, daughter Lisa, daughter-in-law Elizabeth, and grandsons Christopher and Shaun survive him. A memorial service will take place at St. John's Episcopal in Larchmont on Wednesday, May 31, at 10 AM.
