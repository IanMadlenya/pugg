
        SINGER-Donald K. On August 18, 2000. Survived by his wife of 50 years, Dottie, children Douglas and Diana Singer, Mary Ann and Charles Beier, William and Karen Singer, grandchildren Michael and Katie Singer. Executive at Bergdorf Goodman's and Bloomingdale's fur departments. Volunteered at Pleasantville Cottage. Received prestigious Volunteer of the Year Award for Westchester County. Member of Westchester and NYC Orchid Societies. Funeral services Sunday, August 20, 3 PM, Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY. Donations in lieu of flowers to Leukemia Society of America.
