
        NININGER--Eugene V., MD, 84, Psychiatrist and Psychoanalyst, on August 3. A native of Kansas, he was a graduate of the New York Psychoanalytic Institute, and served as Commissioner of Mental Health for Westchester County, 1976-1977. An accomplished violinist, he performed with his two sons and daughter in a folk group that played many venues including Folk City and the Lone Star Cafe in Manhattan. His wife of 56 years, Fern, died in 2001. He is survived by his sons James and William, his daughter Ann Grilli, and four grandchildren.
