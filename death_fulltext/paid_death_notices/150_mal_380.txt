
        CHADBOURN-John A. (Jack), on July 23rd, 1999. Beloved brother of Jean Morton of Elm Grove, Wisconsin. Devoted uncle of Sarah and David Zubke, Mary Morton and John and Chris Morton. Nephew of Charles D. Turgrimson. Dear friend of Virginia Tolley. Associate Clinical Professor at New York Medical College. A devoted medical education teacher, repeatedly awarded Outstanding Teacher of the Year at New York Medical College. Interment in Columbus, Wisconsin. Memorial contributions may be made to United Cerebral Palsy, 122 E. 23rd St., NY NY 10010.
        CHADBOURN-John, M.D. The Sisters of Charity, Board of Trustees, Medical Staff, Auxillary and Executive Staff of Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center of New York, mourn the passing of our beloved colleague, Dr. Jack Chadbourn. Dr. Chadbourn was an Associate Professor of Medicine at New York Medical College and the Director of Clerkships for the Department of Medicine at Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center. His teaching abilities were recognized by his fellow professors and countless numbers of medical students, interns and residents who named him ''Professor of the Year'' numerous times over the past 30 years. Throughout his career, he dedicated his life to teaching hundreds of physicians the art of medicine and the love of humanity. We extend our condolences to his family and friends. Jack will be truly missed by his colleagues and students. Thomas A. Coleman, Chairman Board of Trustees Karl P. Adler, M.D. President and CEO Lambert N. King, M.D. Medical Director and Vice-Dean
