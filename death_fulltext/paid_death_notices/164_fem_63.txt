
        NORCROSS-Elizabeth Gordon. On September 3, 2000, 94. Of Adamstown, MD. Well known editor of ''House Beautiful'' magazine. Survived by her goddaughter Brooke Shearer Talbot and husband Strobe Talbot, Washington, DC; sister-in-law Gertrude Norcross, San Diego, CA. Also survived by close friends Eleanor and John McGuire, San Francicsco, CA; Jane Chapman, San Diego, CA; Rowena and Robert Tebbe, Lakeland, FL; Alfred Browning Parker, Gainesville, FL; and Skip and Marva Shearer of Los Angeles, CA. For more information, contact the Keeney and Basford Funeral Home, Frederick, MD. Services are private.
