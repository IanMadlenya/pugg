
        HOFFMAN-Hon. Daniel. On April 23, 1999 at his home in Tamarac, FL. Beloved husband of Leah. Revered uncle to his nieces and nephews. Judge of the New York City Criminal Court, 1966-1975. He was always respected for his wisdom and integrity, admired for his strength of character, cherished for his compassion and good counsel. Services were held on April 26th. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Civil Liberties Union.
