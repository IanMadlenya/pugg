
        FRIEDES-Bernice. On March 16, 2000. Cherished mother of Michael, Renni and the late Tony. Loving mother-in-law of Sally and Fred. Adored grandmother of Harrison, Olivia, Amy and Nina. Beloved companion of Harold. Dear sister of Norman. Loving step-mother to Patti. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, Sunday March 19 at 9:45 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to National Council of Jewish Women-New York Section.
        FRIEDES-Bernice. Beloved CoPresident of NCJW New York section. Bernice Friedes was admired for her caring and courage. Her concern for others was demonstrated by her dedication and enthusiasm for projects that served the needy. Her love of family was the compass for her life. Jean Krosner, Co-President Helen Caplin Heller, Exec. Dir.
