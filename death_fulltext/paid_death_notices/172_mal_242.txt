
        WALETZKY-Jacob Peter. Died unexpectedly on Sunday, May 20, 2001. He was 29 years old. He graduated from Sidwell Friends School in Washington, D.C. in 1989 and graduated cum laude from Yale University in 1993. He studied philosophy at the University of Chicago. In early May 2001, he completed the coursework for his M.F.A. in the Writing Division at Columbia University. He is survived by his ever loving parents, Lucy Waletzky and Jeremy Waletzky, his adoring sister Naomi Waletzky, his loving grandfather Laurance S. Rockefeller and his beloved girlfriend Dana Goodyear. We can't imagine life without him. A memorial service will be held at the Riverside Church, 490 Riverside Drive at 120 Street, New York City, Thursday, May 24th, at 4 P.M.
