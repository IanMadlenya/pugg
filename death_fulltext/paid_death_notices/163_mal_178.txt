
        THOMASES-Fred. The Officers and Staff of American ORT mourn the passing of our friend and supporter, Fred Thomases. His dedication to the Jewish people, children and education is his legacy to the students of ORT. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Florence Thomases, her children and grandchildren.
