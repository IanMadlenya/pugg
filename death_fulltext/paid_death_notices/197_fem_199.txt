
        COLLINS--Priscilla ''Patsy'' Bullitt. Vassar College is saddened by the death of Priscilla ''Patsy'' Bullitt Collins, Class of 1942, a stalwart supporter of her alma mater and of many, varied causes aimed at bettering our world. She was a woman of extraordinary generosity, wisdom and determination, and her legacy lives on at Vassar and throughout the larger world that she touched so effectively. We extend our condolences to her family. Frances D. Fergusson, Pres. Richard E. Van Demark Chair, Board of Trustees >>AD#
