
        WALSH-Peter. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of the former Assistant to the President, Peter Walsh. An invaluable asset to the hospital center for over a quarter century, his cheerful spirit and dedication to our patients will never be forgotten. To his sister Rosemary and to his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Sigurd H. Ackerman, MD President Carlton Boxhill, MD President of the Medical Board
