
        FIELD-Ruth Leavitt, 88. Of Bay Harbor Island, Florida. She passed away peacefully in her sleep on the morning of December 1, 2000. She was a native of Boston and lived formerly in Washington, DC. Beloved wife of Maxwell Field and loving mother of daughter Sally Patkin of Needham, MA., and son Richard of Miami Beach. She was predeceased by another loving daughter Jane Munick of Cinn. She was a loving mother to sonsin-law Dr. Leo Munick of Cinn., Ohio and Stephen Patkin in Needham, MA. Devoted grandmother to six grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. She was a dear sister to 2 sisters and 2 brothers. Funeral services were private.
