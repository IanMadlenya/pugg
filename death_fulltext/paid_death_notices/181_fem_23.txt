
        GERAMI-Mohtaram. Died on Jan. 30, 2002 in peace at her home. She was a pioneer in infectious disease and obstetrics medicine for her generation & dearly loved mother of Khaschayar (Nik), Zohreh and Kouros Nainzadeh, all of whom are physicians. Beloved motherin-law of Nahid K. Nainzadeh, M.D. and Jorg Casper, M.D. Cherished grandmother of Yasmine, Sara, Daniel and Diana Nainzadeh and Iris, Laif, Peer, Zilia, and Julia Casper. Her generosity, kindness and impeccable sense of style will be missed. Funeral services will be held in private.
