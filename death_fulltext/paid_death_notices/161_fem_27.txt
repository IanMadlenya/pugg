
        ALTENAU-Grace Anna. Dr. Grace, well loved Pediatrician and long time resident of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn and Breezy Point, Queens died Friday, June 2nd, 2000 at age 91. She is survived by her son, John Holland Cassity and her daughter Adelaide VanNuys Cassity. Visitation is Wednesday, June 7 2-5 and 7-9pm at Fred Herbst Funeral Home, 7501 5th Avenue, Brooklyn. Funeral is Thursday, June 8th, 9:30am at Good Shepherd Lutheran Church, 7420 4th Avenue, Brooklyn.
