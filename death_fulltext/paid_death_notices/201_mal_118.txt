
        LAMONT--Donald Breck. Of Hobe Sound, Florida. Husband of Mary Ann Hamilton Lamont, father of Pamela J. Lamont of California and Susan R. Lamont of Connecticut. Died on October 8, 2003 at the age of 83. He was a Californian, raised in San Francisco, graduated from The Phillips Exeter Academy, Yale University and Harvard Business School. During WW II, he served with the U.S. Marines in the Pacific Theater and North China. As President and Director of The Interocean Oil Companies and other independent international oil exploration companies, his career in the petroleum industry spanned fifty years. A Memorial Service will be held at Christ Memorial Chapel, 52 South Beach Road, Hobe Sound, Florida on Saturday, November 22, 2003 at 4:00 PM. Relatives and friends are respectfully invited to attend. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in his memory to Jupiter Island Clinic, P.O. Box 375, Hobe Sound, Florida 33475 or to a charity of your choice.
