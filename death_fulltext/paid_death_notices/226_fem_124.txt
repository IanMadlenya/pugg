
        CARROLL--Helen S., 76. Of Jackson Heights, NY died on November 12, 2005. An Artist and Teacher, she was born Helen Catherine Schrader in Huron, S.D. She attended Augustana College in Sioux Falls, S.D. and received her MA at NYU in New York City. She taught Art for 26 years at the Newtown High School in Queens. She was the widow of Robert E. Carroll of Providence, RI. Mrs. Carroll is survived by her sister, Mrs. Cecil Ane Borri of Metuchen, N.J. and by nieces, nephews. A memorial service is planned for a later date.
