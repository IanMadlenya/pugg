
        BRAZEAU--Paul, Ph.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of our distinguished Professor Emeritus of Pharmacology, who joined our faculty in 1958 as a founding member of the Department of Pharmacology and was greatly esteemed as an outstanding teacher by several generations of our students. We extend condolences to all his loved ones. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
