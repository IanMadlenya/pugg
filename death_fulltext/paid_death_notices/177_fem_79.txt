
        PUNIE-Pauline Sophie (London). Of Sunrise, Florida, formerly of Laurelton, N.Y. Peacefully on October 5, 2001, in her 96th year. Beloved wife of the late David. Loving mother of Iris Schornstein and the late Joan Ribler. Loving grandmother of Lori (Ronald) Seitenbach, Lawrence (Rebecca) Ribler, Kenneth (Jill) Ribler, Lisa Schornstein & David Schornstein. Devoted great-grandmother of Justin, Grant, Cameron, Johanna and Jackson. Thank you dear Nanna for being such a strong influence in our lives. You were the foundation upon which we all leaned. Your unselfish caring and giving are the legacies you leave to us. We will always be grateful for your support and unconditional love.
