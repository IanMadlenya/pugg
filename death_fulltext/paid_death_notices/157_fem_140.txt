
        McCURLEY-Nora Esther. In her 93rd year, died peacefully on February 11, 2000 after a short illness. She is survived by her sister Elsa M. Crosa and daughters Doreen McCurley and Mary Lukinich and son-in-law Rex Lukinich. A requiem mass will be held at St. Agnes Church, 143 East 43 St. at 10:00 AM on Thursday, February 17. Interment will be at Arlington National Cemetery next to her beloved husband, Oscar Guinn McCurley.
