
        SPORN-Lawrence B. On October 22, 2002. He died after a long bout with cancer. Larry was born on April 25, 1931 in New York City. He was honorably discharged from the United States Navy in 1955. Shortly thereafter, he went to work for Ziff-Davis Publishing Company. There he enjoyed working in many positions, among which were Publisher of Stereo Review, Circulation Director, and President of Publishing. Upon leaving Ziff-Davis he became President of America's Division for Reed International Publications. Aside from his love for music, books, and gardening, he was a fine woodworker and long distance cyclist. He is survived by his wife, five children, grandchild, a daughter-in-law and son-in-law. Services will be held on October 25th at 10:15 AM at the EdwardsDowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY. There will be a private viewing for family and friends at 9:30 AM. Burial will follow at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
