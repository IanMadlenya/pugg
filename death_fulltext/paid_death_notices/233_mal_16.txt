
        JUAN--Dr. Paul L., a longtime resident of Bronxville, died on Wednesday, May 31, 2006. He was born to Paul Leo Juan and Lillian St. Jean Juan on February 9, 1935 in Greenwich, CT. Paul attended Fordham University, and University of Ottawa School of Medicine. Paul did his internship and residency at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in New York City. Paul served in the U.S. Army as Captain from 1966 to 1968. He was a diplomate of the American Board of OB/GYN; fellow of the American College of Surgeons' served as President of the NY Gynecological Society from 1978 to 1979; and member of the NY County Medical Society. He was Director of the OB/GYN Department for 12 years at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in Manhattan. He was in private practice for over 30 years and was a professor of OB/GYN at New York Medical College. Paul was the loving husband of Lois (Murphy) Juan to whom he was married for close to 46 years. Survivors included eight children and 23 grandchildren. Visiting hours at Fred H. McGrath & Son, Inc. Funeral Home, 20 Cedar St at Kraft Ave, Bronxville, Friday June 2nd from 2 to 4 pm and from 7 to 9 pm. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville, NY on Saturday, June 3rd at 10 am. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center, West 12th St., New York, NY 10011.
