
        MALECKI--Alfred85, on July 23, 2006. Son of the late Dora and Gustav Malecki, loving husband of Zenia Berkon Malecki, devoted father of Sophie and Lee Dichter, loving brother of the late Edith Neumann of Uruguay. A brave refugee from Nazi Germany and successfull publishing executive at Crain Communications for 34 years, Al, known to friends as Fredi, showed a lust for life until the end. He will be missed by many. Funeral services 10 AM, Tuesday, July 25, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St. Donations may be made to Alzheimer's Association of New York, 360 Lexington Ave., NY, NY 10017.
