
        FRIEDMAN-Max, M.D. 91, on April 18, 1997, in Tamarac, FL. Director Emeritus OBS-GYN Wyckoff Hts Hospital. Born near Tarnopol, Austria. Attended Columbia University and L.I. Medical College, Brooklyn. Past President, Queens Gynecological Society; Life Fellow, Brooklyn Gyn. Society; Fellow, American College of Ob/ Gyn. Survived by his wife, Elaine; sons Sheldon B. and Alan W.; sisters Ethel Wagner and Leah Kaiser; brother Julius; six grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
