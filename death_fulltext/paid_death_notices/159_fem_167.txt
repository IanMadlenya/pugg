
        SCOTT-Dorothy B., 60 of Airmont NY died, Tuesday April 18, 2000. Born July 27, 1939 in Brooklyn, the daughter of Anthony & Josephine Rodino Bologna. Survived by her beloved husband Malcolm, 2 sons: Peter and Michael de la Garza of Florida. Daughter: Lisa de la Garza of Los Angeles, California. Brother: Joseph Bologna of Beverly Hills California, and sister Barbara Carson of Chestnut Ridge, NY. Wake Service on Saturday, April 22 7-9 P.M. and Sunday, April 23, 2000 2-4 & 7-9P.M. at Wanamaker & Carlough Funeral Home, Rt 59, Suffern, NY. Mass: Church of the Presentation in Saddle River, 10:00 A.M. Monday, April 24, 2000.
