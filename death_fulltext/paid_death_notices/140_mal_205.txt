
        GOLDHUSH-Dr. Alvin A. On September 16, 1998 after a long illness at the age of 79 of Nassau Pt., Cutchogue, formerly of Bayshore. Beloved husband of the late Viola Raybin Goldhush, MD. Devoted father of Susan Goldhush and Karen and Hank Kranichfeld. Loving grandfather of Bram, Oliver and Andrew; dear brother of the late Dr. Herbert Goldhush. He will live in our hearts forever. His gentle and knowing spirit, a strength to those who loved him. Reposing at the William F. Coster Funeral Home, Route 25, Cutchogue, New York, today from 5PM to 7PM. Funeral service, Friday, 11AM at Wellwood Cemetery. Memorials may be sent to East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, New York 11978.
