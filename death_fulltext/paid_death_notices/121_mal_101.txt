
        INNISS-Charles E. The Board and staff of The New York City Police Foundation are saddened by the passing of former trustee, Charles Inniss. His profound commitment to community initiatives has improved the quality of life in Brooklyn, and will live on for future generations to enjoy. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Robin L. Farkas, Chairman Pamela D. Delaney, Exec. Dir.
        INNISS-Charles E. The Franciscan Sisters of the Poor mourn the passing of Charles E. Inniss, the first recipient of the Franciscan Heritage Award. The Charles E. Inniss St. Joseph Franciscan House for homeless men will continue his legacy of community service and devotion to Brooklyn. We extend our sympathy to the Inniss family. Sister Marilyn Fischer, S.F.P.
