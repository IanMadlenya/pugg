
        HELM-Mary R. Widow of Harold H. Helm, who headed Chemical Bank from 1947-1965. Mrs. Helm died on Jan. 25, eleven days after reaching her 100 birthday, in Montclair, N.J. where she resided since 1928. Mrs. Helm was a resident of New York from the 1940's to the 1970's. Survived by her daughter, Eleanor Ketcham of Elon College, N.C.,and a son John of Montclair, N.J. Also survived by 3 grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held 2PM Monday, January 29, at the Central Presbyterian Church, 46 Park St, Montclair, N.J. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Central Presbyterian Church. For information call Arthur K. Brown, Inc., Bloomfield, New Jersey.
