
        LEVIN-Shirley Topal. On November 14, five days before her 101st birthday. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Devoted mother of Jane and Renee and sons-in-law, Gil and Wheeler. Adoring grandmother of James, Martha, Polly and Daisy and her grandson-in-law, Cliff. Dear great-grandmother of Will and Nick. Her life was rich with laughter, family and friends. A celebration of her life will be held on a date to be announced. We wish her love.
