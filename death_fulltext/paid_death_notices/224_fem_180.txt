
        LOEWENSTEIN--Ruth H. 95, on September 12, 2005. Cherished wife, mother, sister, aunt, friend; adored and brilliant teacher. Throughout her long life, she loved, nurtured, struggled, led, championed, inspired, sang, danced, and laughed. She is survived by daughters Linda, Hope, Bonnie; brother Leon; sister Esther; grandchildren Julie, Brian, Tryggvi, Katla, Jonah, Matthew. Contributions to: Hadassah; Temple Beth Emeth, Brooklyn; Brooklyn Botanic Gardens.
