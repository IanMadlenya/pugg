
        TYSON -- Yvonne Lorgnie. Age 81, of Basking Ridge, NJ, formerly of Mendham, NJ, on Wednesday, January 28, 2004. Wife of David Tyson. Mother of Dr. Geoffrey Tyson of Vancouver and Seattle, WA, and Alan Tyson and his wife Donna of New Vernon, NJ. Grandmother of five grandchildren. She is also survived by three sisters Marie Therese Verchen and Jacqueline Lorgnie of Neuilly, France and Magdeline Touchot of Paris. At her request there will be no funeral service. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Mendham Borough Public Library, 10 Hilltop Rd, Mendham, NJ 07945, where she volunteered for many years. For information contact ments the Bailey Funeral Home, 8 Hilltop Rd, Mendham; 973-543-4720.
