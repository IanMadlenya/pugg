
        RYAN-Raymond T., 98, died on Tuesday, July 4, 2000 while living with his son and family in St. Louis. Born in Buffalo, NY on Dec. 12, 1901. A resident of Tucson, AZ since 1970. Beloved husband of the late Kathryn M. Ryan, married for 65 years. Dear father and fatherin-law of Jay J. (Nancy) Ryan of Kirkwood, MO & Susan R. (the late Lou) DiMuro of Tucson, AZ. Brother of Elise Downes of Hartsdale, NY. Grandfather of 9, Great-grandfather of 7. Mr. Ryan graduated from Pace University in 1932. He retired as President of Welch's in 1970. In 1972, he was a member of the Internatl Executive Service Core in Greece & also received an honorary degree from St. Bonaventure University. Memorial Mass will be held on Sat, July 22, 11am at St. Peter Catholic Church in Kirkwood, MO. Private family interment in Tucson, AZ. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Medical Foundation of Buffalo, Inc. Research Institute 73 High St, Buffalo, NY 14203-1196. For more information, contact Bopp Chapel, St Louis, MO.
