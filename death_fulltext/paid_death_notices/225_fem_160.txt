
        LAGER--Lynn Andra, DDS of Dix Hills, NY on October 23, 2005. Beloved wife of Frederick James Bock. Loving daughter of Helen Newman and the late Dr. Jack Lager. Beloved mother of June Krinsky-Rudder & Donna Krinsky Romano and devoted mother-in-law of Harold Rudder & Anthony Romano. Loving and beloved grandmother of Anna and AJ Romano. Dear friend of her former husband, Edward M. Krinsky. A graduate of Brooklyn College and the Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery, Lynn Lager was a respected dentist with a successful practice in Dix Hills, N.Y. for more than 30 years. She was a member of several professional organizations and the recipient of numerous awards in recognition of her outstanding ability and community service. Friends may call at the I.J. Morris Funeral Home in Dix Hills, N.Y. Wednesday 7-9 PM. Interment Thursday at Wellwood Cemetery, Farmingdale, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Habitat for Humanity or the Joe Torre Safe At Home Foundation.
