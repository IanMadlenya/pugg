
        HAYDEN--Joseph Michael. Died December 24, 2004, age 70, after a short illness. Much loved brother of Mary and Seamus and uncle of Cora, Paul, Sheamus, Michael, Kathleen and Siobhan. Worked for The New York Times for 36 years. ''A great supervisor who led his staff by example and always had a smile on his face.'' He will be sadly missed by family and friends.
