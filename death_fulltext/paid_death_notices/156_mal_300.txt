
        CONWAY-Thomas, on January 18th, 2000, age 74, of Sarasota, FL, formerly of NY, native of Virginia, County Cavan, Ireland. Past President of P&B Association and longtime member of Cavan Gaelic Football Club. Beloved husband of Joan (nee Clifford). Devoted father of Thomas J. and his wife Jeanie. Dear brother of Owen of England, Charles of County Cavan, Ireland, Lily McCann of NJ, Agnes Wilson and Kathleen Mannion of England. Reposing Friday, 2-5 and 7-9:30 PM, at Edward D. Lynch Funeral Home, 43-07 Queens Blvd., Sunnyside, NY. Funeral Mass St. Sebastian R.C. Church, Saturday, 9:45 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
