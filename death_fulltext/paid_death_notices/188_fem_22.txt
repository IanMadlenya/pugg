
        FRIEDMAN-Phoebe. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Phoebe Friedman, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Secy.
