
        HIBBS-Vivian A. Died of cancer at the Mt. Sinai Hospital on Monday evening September 7, 1998. Dr. Hibbs, born in Iowa City, IA. was an archaeologist, participating for several seasons in the excavations at Francolisa, near Naples, Italy. After graduating from Hunter College and receiving her doctorate from New York University's Institute of Fine Arts in 1979, she served as Curator of the Hispanic Society of America in New York City. Her dissertation, the Mendes Maze, was published by the Garland Press in 1985. A member of the Archaelogical Institute of America, the New York Academy of Sciences and the American Society for Hispanic Art Historical Studies, serving on its Executive Committee in past years. She is survived by her husband of 52 years, William, & a sister Margaret of Gulfport. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St., Friday, September 11, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. For further information, call 212-288-3500.
