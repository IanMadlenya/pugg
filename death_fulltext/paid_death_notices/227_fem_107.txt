
        JOHNSON--Evelyn, R.N. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the staff of the Beatrice Renfield Department of Patient Care Services in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and longtime colleague, Evelyn Johnson, R.N., beloved sister of Maryanne Johnson, R.N., also a longtime member of the hospital's nursing staff. Evelyn served in numerous positions during her more than forty years of service to Beth Israel, and was the recipient of many internal and external awards and professional honors. She will be greatly missed by her admiring colleagues and patients. To Maryanne, the entire Johnson family, and all of Evelyn's friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences. David J. Shulkin, M.D., President and CEO Robert G. Newman, M.D., President Emeritus Mary Walsh, R.N., Vice President, Patient Care Services
