
        CRONKITE--Betsy. On behalf of the Board of Trustees, staff and volunteers of Educational Broadcasting Corporation, licensee of Thirteen/WNET and WLIW21, we note the passing and mourn the loss of Betsy Cronkite, beloved wife of our Trustee and friend Walter Cronkite. Through Walter, we had the pleasure of knowing Betsy, who will be long remembered for her unfailing wit, grace and deep devotion to her husband. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to him and the rest of her family. Steven R. Rattner Chairman William F. Baker President
