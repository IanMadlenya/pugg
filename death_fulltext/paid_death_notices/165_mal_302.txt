
        HICKOK-Richard S. The Board of Directors of Marsh & McLennan Companies mourns the passing of our esteemed colleague & friend Richard Hickok, who was a valued member of our Board for more than 15 years. We have benefited greatly from Dick's wisdom and counsel and are saddened by his loss. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Janet (''Boots'') and his family. J.W. Greenberg, Chairman
