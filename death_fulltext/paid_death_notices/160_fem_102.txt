
        KRAUS-Joanne P. Born May 27, 1930 in NYC to the late Charlotte and Ralph Kraus. Died May 3, 2000 in Beverly Hills, CA of cancer. She leaves sister and brother-in-law, Barbara and Jackie Cooper of Beverly Hills; nephews Russell Cooper of Manhattan & John Cooper of Marin, CA and niece Cristina Cooper of Woodland Hills, CA. Interment at Westwood Memorial Park, Los Angeles.
