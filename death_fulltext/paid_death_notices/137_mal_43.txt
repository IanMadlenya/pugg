
        LIEPONIS-William Vincent. 89, of Dunedin, FL, formerly of Jackson Heights, NY, passed away on May 26, 1998. He was a member of Saint Michael The Archangel Catholic Church, Clearwater, FL, where he was an usher and member of the Mens Club. He was also a member of the Moose 1030 Lodge Golf League. Survivors include his wife Alice, a son Robert, a daughter Muriel, both of Jackson Heights, NY, and a grandson Robert Leeponis, of Pearl River, NY. Interment Lakeview Chapel, Sylvan Abbey, Clearwater, FL.
