
        EBNER-Gerald J. Of Tenafly, New Jersey on July 27th, 2001, former Councilman of the Borough of Tenafly. Business, civic and community leader. Beloved husband of Eleanor. Devoted father of Lawrence, Eugene and Randall. Brother of Alan. Loving grandfather of Elizabeth, Rachel and Casey Jill. Services will be held at ''Gutterman-Musicant,'' 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ on Sunday at 11:30AM. Should friends desire, contributions may be made to Englewood Hospital and Medical Center, 350 Engle St., Englewood, New Jersey 07631, in his name.
