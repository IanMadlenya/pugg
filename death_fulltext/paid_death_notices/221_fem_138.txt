        COHEN--Eva. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Eva Cohen, beloved wife of the late Irving Cohen. Eva was a treasured friend of the Jewish community whose boundless generosity and lifelong service to our Women's Campaign strengthened our ability to care for those in need, enriching millions of lives in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We extend our deepest sympathies to Eva's beloved daughters, Phyllis Rifas, Sharon Bahr, and Doree Levy; to her grandchildren and great-grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
