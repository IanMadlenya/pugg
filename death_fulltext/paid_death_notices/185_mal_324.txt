
        LESSER-David. Age 92. Died June 22, 2002. Husband of Tillie Lesser, father of Beatrice Martin and Jonathan, fatherin-law of Joan and Nancy, grandfather of Eric, Rebecca, Julia and Cassandra. USN World War II Veteran, worked as a tool and die maker. He was devoted to his family, lover of nature, Zionism and humanity. Services were held June 23rd at Parkside in Brooklyn, N.Y. followed by burial. Shiva ended June 28th.
