
        BLITZMAN-Sara. On March 17, 2001. Age 84. Beloved wife of Benjamin. Devoted mother of Geri and Jay. Cherished grandmother of Michelle Rose and Hallie. She was the most wonderful of homemakers, loyal of friends, proudest of aunts and a loving poet laureate to her Bell Park Jewish Center Sisterhood. Services today, 11:15 AM, at Parkside Memorial Chapel, Queens Blvd., corner 66th Ave., Forest Hills.
