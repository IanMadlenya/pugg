He was the beloved father of Hal Schenfield, proud grandfather of Andrew, Eric and Jennifer Schenfield, Jill Alper, Susan, Jill, and Scott Potkin. Cherished great-grandfather of Dori and Lyle Alper. Mr. Schenfield was past Master of Prudence Lodge #
