
        LANE-Henry Edwin. In his 91st year. Died peacefully in West Palm Beach where he had lived for over twenty years. A native of New York City. He was a professional fund raiser for Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and upon retirement dedicated himself to voluteering, both in the school system and with the United Way of Palm Beach. He is survived by his long time companion, Julia Carswell of Ashville, N.C., his son Michael Edwin of New York City and two granddaughters, Tessa Kate and Maya Jo Solomon-Lane of Washington, D.C.
