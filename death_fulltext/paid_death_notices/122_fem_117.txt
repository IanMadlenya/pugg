
        BERGERMAN - Ems. Devoted wife of the late Milton M. Bergerman. Passed away peacefully at home on Thursday, March 13. Born in Lyon, France, one of 11 children, she became a proud U.S. citizen in 1945. She was the beloved mother of Jane (Martin) and Jill (Tom). Devoted grandmere of Christen, Jennifer, Sara, Samuel and Jill. Great-grandmere to Jacob. Aunt to Jacqueline (Jean). Great-aunt to Francoise. Deeply loved companion of Edith and Selma. A truly elegant, gracious and compassionate woman, she will be forever missed and loved. Funeral services will be private.
