
        ODESSEY-Paul Herman. Died at his home in Great Neck, NY, on Tuesday, September 19, 2000. Mr. Odessey was President of Polarad Electronics Corporation from 1949 -1965. Mr. Odessey was the holder of many patents; notably one of the earliest translating devices developed for the U.N. From 1965 to 1990 he was President of Sterling Transformer Corporation. He is survived by his 4 children: Richard, Phyllis, Sheila, and Gail; and his six grandchildren: Eric, Rebecca, Ben, Sophie, Louis and Lola.
