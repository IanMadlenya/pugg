
        FOX--Arthur H. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our beloved father Arthur Fox. The most devoted husband of Shirley Fox. Loving father of Clara and Walter Riccardi; Jordan, Dana and Richard and Ellen Fox. Grandfather of Benjamin, Nicholas, Harry, Jacob Riccardi; Amanda, Steven, Marissa, Rachael and Mark Fox. Arthur was a veteran of WWII, a well respected and talented engineer, and an admired professor at NYU. He leaves a legacy of kindness, intelligence and humor.
