
        WEXLER-Ruth. The National Board of Hadassah, The Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., is deeply saddened by the passing of Ruth Wexler, committed life member and generous member of The Society of Guardian Donors. Together with her husband Jack, Ruth established the Ruth and Jack Wexler Fellowships in Geriartric Medicine at the Hadassah Hebrew University Medical Center in Jerusalem. We extend our deepest condolences to her beloved husband, Jack, their children Ann and Robert Fromer and Gary and Nina Wexler, and the entire family. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Bonnie Lipton, National President
