
        ALDAN-Daisy. Poet, Actress, Editor, Publisher and Translator, died Monday at Kateri Residence in New York City. She was 82. Ms. Aldan is survived by a brother, Perry, of Delray Beach, Florida and sister-in-law, Etta Finfer, of Los Angeles, California. Funeral services will be held on Friday, March 2nd at 12:30PM with a viewing at 11AM at the Christian Community Church, 309 West 74th Street, New York, NY. Viewing will also be at the Gannon Funeral Home, 152 East 28th Street, New York, NY on Thursday from 7PM to 9PM.
