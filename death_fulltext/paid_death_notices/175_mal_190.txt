
        COLE-Kenneth Reese Jr. 63, beloved husband, father and grandfather, of Willsboro, NY, and Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, died peacefully in his sleep at his summer home on Thursday, August 16, 2001. He was born in NYC, on January 27, 1938. Ken grew up in Scarsdale, NY, and Westfield, NJ. He graduated from Bucknell University and was Lt. (jg) in the US Navy from 1961 until 1965. He served as the Director of the Domestic Council under Presidents Nixon and Ford. Ken enjoyed his summers in Willsboro, NY, where he was a member of St. John's Episcopal Church. He wintered in Ponte Vedra Beach, FL. His sport of choice was golf, which he loved only second to his family. He is survived by his wife Marilyn (Slifer) Cole, two daughters and their husbands, Corinne and Robert Ryan of Charlotte, NC, and Megan and Kirk Kellogg of Summit, NJ. He is also survived by two sisters and their husbands, Priscilla and Thomas Perkins of Terrell, NC, Connie Cole and Donald Lidstrand of Denville, NJ, one brother and his wife Brady and Margaret Cole of Corinth, VT, and five grandchildren Aletta, Claire and Reese Ryan and Charlie and Caroline Kellogg. A memorial service will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church in Essex, NY, Monday, August 20, 2001, at 2 PM. In remembance of Ken, those who wish may make a contribution to the charity of their choice or St. John's Episcopal Church, Essex, NY, or the Smith House Health Care Center, Willsboro, NY.
