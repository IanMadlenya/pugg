
        HERENSTEIN-Harry T. Beloved and devoted husband of the late Ruth Spitz Herenstein. Loving brother of the late Louis Hernstein, Gladys Cohen, Lester and Moe Herenstein, Dorothy Sollar, Perry & Henry Herenstein. Caring brother-in-law of Pearl Herenstein. Dear uncle of Arlene Asher, Doris Fine, Arnold R. Sollar, Herman Herenstein, Elaine Sollar Eisen, Ira Herenstein, Francine Gilstein, Arnold Herenstein, Charna Herenstein & the late Larry Herenstein. We have all benefited from his wisdom, wit and love. And we will miss him and cherish his memory. Service on Sunday 11 A.M., at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
