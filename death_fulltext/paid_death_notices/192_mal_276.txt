
        BLUM-Sheldon, August 19, 1916 to January 21, 2003. Beloved husband of Eunice Martin Blum. Loving father of Naomi Fertitta, father-inlaw of George Fertitta and grandfather of Cree and Cameron Fertitta. Sheldon Blum was a graduate of NYU University and NYU Law School, Summa Cum Laude, and a member of Theta Sigma Lamda. He was a member of the New York Bar for 40 years and was a WW II Air Force Veteran serving as an aerial photographer.
