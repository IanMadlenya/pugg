
        CAPLAN--David, 95. Loving husband, father, grandfather, great-grandfather and retired restauraneur, is survived by his most cherished wife of over 60 years, Pat Caplan and daughters Adrienne and Lois and son Lawrence (Ellen). He leaves five grandchildren, Matthew, Glen (Jennifer), Jared, Todd and Joy (Adam) and one great grandchild, Natalie. David was born in Baltimore, MD in 1909, moved to New York City at the age of 16. He opened the Gotham Coffee Shop on 43rd St in 1937, followed by The Fountain and Berkeley Restaurants on 44th St. Other than his family, his legacy is the countless memories shared by thousands and thousands of customers who came and went through his restaurants. David Caplan exemplied the true meaning of the word ''gentleman.''
