
        LAWRENCE--Suzanne Spear, died April 10, aged 62 years. She was a sweet and deep soul, extraordinary as a mother to Christopher Lawrence, to Bill Lawrence and his wife Molly, and as a grandmother to their daughter Anna. Suzy also leaves legions of friends from all places and paths. A memorial service is planned for Monday, May 14 at 11:30 am St. Ann's Episcopal Church, Main Street, Bridgehampton, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The American Lyme Disease Foundation, Inc. P.O. Box 466 Lyme, CT 06371. www.ALDF.com
