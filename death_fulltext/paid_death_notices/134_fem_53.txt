
        HEINEMAN-Ruth (Kress). Age 75, passed away peacefully in her sleep, at home, after a long illness borne with courage and dignity. She is survived by her loving husband Bernard (Jack) Heineman, Jr, her devoted chiildren Deb and Ralph, Matt and Katie, Steve and Kathy and by her six adoring grandchildren. The family will receive friends at The Frank E. Campbell Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue on Sunday March 8 from 6-9 PM, Monday March 9 from 6-9 PM. The funeral service will be Tuesday, March 10 at 4 PM at The First Presbyterian Church, 12th St and 5th Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The College of Wooster, Wooster, OH, The Public Education Association, 39 W. 32nd Street, or the charity of your choice.
