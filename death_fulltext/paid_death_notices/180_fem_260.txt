
        ABOUCHAR-Beatriz Kessler on January 28, 2002 of Sag Harbor, NY. Formerly of Garden City, NY. After a long and courageous battle with leukemia, she never gave up faith and hope for an ultimate cure. Beloved wife of Edward. Loving mother of Beatrice Goldsmith (Eric) and Christine Lebowitz (Marc). Dear grandmother of Alex, Victoria and Bradley Goldsmith, Sophia and Dylan Lebowitz and Serena and Hannah Abouchar. Loving sister of Elena Rodriguez-Estevez. Dear step-mother of Christopher Abouchar (Albertina) and Sonia Abouchar. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Garden City, Thursday, 11 am. Interment Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Hematopoietic Cancer Immunochemistry Laboratory c/o Dr. J. Jurcic, Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave., NY, NY 10021 appreciated.
