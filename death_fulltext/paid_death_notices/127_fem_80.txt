
        BARNETT-Pearl Phillipa Geiger, 94. Died in her home on Aug. 14, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Alfred Nathan. Mother of Sally Barnett, Marjorie Barnett, and Barbara and Mark Goldfarb. Grandmother of Patricia Zohn, Norman Pfeiffer, Robert and Anna Zohn, Soozy Goldfarb, Andrew and Aviva Goldfarb, Alexander and Douglas Barnett. Great grandmother of Camilla, Nicholas, Abigail, Patrick and Solomon. Beloved sister of Rosalind Greenberg, sister-in-law of Ted Jacobson, Mildred Geiger, and Lola Geiger. May we carry on her sense of spirit, humor and independence. Funeral at Riverside Chapel, NYC, Sunday. Time to be announced.
