
        CAMERON--Wendy Mairs. 56, of Darien, CT and Fishers Island, NY, passed away on April 10, 2004 after a gallant battle against ovarian cancer. Beloved wife, mother and friend, she is survived by her husband, Gerry, her daughter & son-in-law Betsy and Nik Taylor, her son David Cameron and his fiancee Pam Burke, her son Ben Cameron, her granddaughter Grace Taylor, her stepmother Adele Mairs, her sister Mary Chapot, her stepsister Valerie McDermott, her stepbrother Brian McDermott, and her best friend Madelon Peck. Her life was defined by her dignity, determination, enormous capacity for friendship and extraordinary grace. A celebration of her life will be held at 11:00AM on Wednesday, May 12, 2004 at Saint Luke's Episcopal Church, 1864 Post Rd, Darien CT. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Island Health Project, Inc., P.O. Box 344, Fishers Island, NY 06390.
