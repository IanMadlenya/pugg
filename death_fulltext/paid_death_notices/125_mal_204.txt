
        NILES-David. The suave voice that brought sophistication to the airwaves passed away on June 12, 1997. A broadcast pioneer, a founding member of AFRA, (later AFTRA) and WEVD chief announcer for 37 years. Started on WMCA in 1933 after a career in the stage and silent movies. He held WEVD together allmost until the time it ceased to be known as ''the station that speaks your language,'' the memorable slogan that David Niles originated. For American Thymele Theatre and all your friends, Stephen Diacrussi and family.
