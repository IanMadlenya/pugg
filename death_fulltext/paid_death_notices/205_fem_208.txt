
        WALDMAN--Blanche, on February 25, 2004, at age 90. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Lester. Loving and adored mother of Michael and Rebecca. Cherished aunt of Stuart and Tomi Berney and their children, Dawn, Wendy, J.J. and Kerri. Dear cousin of Beverly Wein. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 Street, Sunday 5-7 PM. Services Monday, March 1st, 10:30 AM at Temple EmanuEl, 5th Avenue at 65 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to The Anti-Defamation League or Temple Emanu-El.
        WALDMAN--Blanche. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Blanche Waldman our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Dr. David M. Posner, Sr. Rabbi Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Sr. Rabbi Emeritus Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
