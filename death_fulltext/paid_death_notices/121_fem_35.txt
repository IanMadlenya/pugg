
        THOMPSON-Gertrude S. On February 3 in Ridgewood, NJ at age 89. The students, faculty and staff at the University at Albany, State University of New York, mourn the passing of Gertrude S. Thompson, a major benefactor dedicated to atmospheric science education and research at the University. She was an extraordinary woman with a love and commitment to the ecology of the Adirondack mountains. Generations of students will benefit from her philanthropy. Her legacy carries on. A memorial service will be held at 2PM on February 20 at West Side Presbyterian Church, 6 South Monroe Street, Ridgewood, NJ.
