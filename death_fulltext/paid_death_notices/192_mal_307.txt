
        ENDE-Nathan. On January 18, 2003 at age 94. He is survived by Vera, his beloved wife of 63 years, his son Eric and daughter-in-law Karen and grandchildren Duncan, Elena and Scott. A quiet man, with a warm sense of humor, he was a most generous and devoted husband and father. He was dearly loved by his family and friends. An Attorney of the highest ethical standards and with a strong social conscience, he gave generously to a wide variety of organizations. He was a graduate of New York University and NYU Law School and practiced law in Manhattan for over 60 years. He was a Founder and served as Chairman of the Board of the NYU Club and was a member of the Town Hall Board of Trustees. He was an active supporter for many years of the NYU Alumni Federation.
