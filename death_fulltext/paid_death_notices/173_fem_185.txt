
        STERN-Frances Shapiro (nee Messinger). 84, died on June 19 at her home. Devoted wife of the late Dr. Julius R. Shapiro and the late Irving Stern. Loving mother of Albert, Steven and Susan Shapiro. Adored grandmother of Julia, Daniel, Rebecca, Sara and Ethan. Loving mother-in-law of Ruth Ann and Susan and grateful friend of Roger. She was always unselfishly devoted to her extended family and many friends. Service at Temple Shaaray Tefila, Bedford Corners, NY on Friday at 11 AM, June 22. Interment at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hastings-On-Hudson, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Hospice of Bergen Community Health Care, 400 Old Hook Road, Westwood, NJ 07675 or the American Cancer Society. For information call Clark Associates Funeral Home, 914-232-3033.
