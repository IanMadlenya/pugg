
        LAFONTANT - MANKARIOUS - Jewel. The Board of Directors of Revlon Group, Inc., notes with great sadness the death of our colleague and friend, Jewel Lafontant-Mankarious. A longtime director of the company, she was a brilliant attorney and advisor, a dedicated public servant and a woman of surpassing grace, dignity and cultivation. We extend our sympathies to her family and her wide circle of friends and admirers, who, like us, will miss her deeply. Ronald O. Perelman, Chairman
