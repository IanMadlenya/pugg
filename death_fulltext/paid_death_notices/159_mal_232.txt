
        SLATER - Dr. Sherwood B. ''Woody'', Assistant Executive Director of the Joseph L. Morse Geriatric Center, died Thursday, April 20, 2000. He was 63. Dr. Slater served as Associate Executive Vice President of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee, and was a social scientist with the World Health Organization. Dr. Slater was born in Bronx, NY. He is survived by his wife Carol; his sister Rosalind Horodi of Israel; his son Steven (Monica) Slater of Maryland; daughter Beth (David) Sokoloff of Virginia; and grandchildren: Rachael and Erica Slater, and Stuart and Harrison Sokoloff. Donations may be made in Dr. Slater's memory to the Joseph L. Morse Geriatric Center, 4847 Fred Gladstone Memorial Drive, West Palm Beach, FL 33417.
