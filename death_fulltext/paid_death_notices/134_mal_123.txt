
        ZARON-David. Age 76. Of Slingerlands, New York. On Sunday, March 8th, 1998. In Naples, Florida. Mr. Zaron was born in Brooklyn, NY, and has resided in the Capitol district for many years. He graduated from the Univ. of Michigan & received his Master's Degree in Public Administration from the Maxwell School at Syracuse University. He served 35 years with New York State in the Departments of Health, Mental Health, Civil Service & Labor. He retired as Assistant Commissioner with the Transportation Department. He was also employed by Suffolk County revamping its civil service system. He was a former Executive Director of OMCE and performed labor arbitration mediation and fact finding. He is survived by his wife Barbara Karlstein Zaron, a son Steven and his wife Patty of Albany, two sisters Judith Donow & Bernice Freiman, both of Delray Beach, Florida, a grand-daughter Katelyn Zaron, several nieces, nephews and great-nieces and nephews. Services at Congregation Beth Emeth, Albany, N.Y., Wednesday, 12 noon. Burial Beth Emeth Cemetery, Loudonville, N.Y. Mourning will be observed at the Zaron residence, Slingerlands, N.Y. through Friday. Contributions to a charity of one's choice. For information call Levine Memorial Chapel, Albany. New York at 1-800-427-0280.
