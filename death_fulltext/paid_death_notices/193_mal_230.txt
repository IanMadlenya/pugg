
        BERES-David, M.D. The Officers and Members of The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Institute express their sorrow at the death of their esteemed and beloved colleague. Dr. Beres was a highly respected clinician, teacher and was President of the Society from 1969-1971. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his family. Paul Rosenbaum, M.D., President/Institute Leon Kupferstein, M.D., President/Society Edward Nersessian, M.D., Chairman/ Educational Committee/Inst.
