
        STERNBERG -- Maurice, known more familiarly as ''Uncle Maurice'' of Knights Day Camp to some 35,000 children, parents, and staff, died November 9th at the age of 92. He will be sorely missed by his son, daughter and grandchildren. His unfailing sense of humor and kind demeanor we hope will be remembered by all those whose lives he touched. A memorial service will be held 1:00 PM, November 29th at the C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, Ridgewood, NJ. Those who remembered him are welcome.
