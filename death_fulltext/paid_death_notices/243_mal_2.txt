
        BUTTERLY--John, 90, a retired NYC Fire Lieutenant, Hook and Ladder Company 105, died Monday, March 29th at his home in Brick, NJ. A veteran of WWII he served in the Eighth Air Force from 1942 -1945. He is survived by his wife of 67 years Marcella, five children, five grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren. Services Monday April 2nd at 11 am. Services by Johnson-McGinley Funeral Home. 732-681-4455
