
        OGDON--Thomas H. Died February 15, 2006 in New York. Tom grew up in Tarrytown, NY. He was graduated from Amherst College and was Founder and President of The Ogdon Partnership, an executive search firm. He is survived by his beloved wife, Michele, adoring daughter, Kristin, sons, David H., William B. Ogdon, and a sister, Judith Ogdon Gardner. Tom will be missed by all who knew and loved him.
