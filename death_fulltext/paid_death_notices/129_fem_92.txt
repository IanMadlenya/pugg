
        YOUNG-Barbara L. The Chairman, the Board of Trustess, the Director and Staff of the Brooklyn Museum of Art record with deep sorrow the passing of their dear friend Barbara L. Young who served as President of the Roebling Society of the Museum for the past twelve years. A tireless and dedicated worker on behalf of the Museum, Mrs. Young was a collector of Asian art. She was also a member of the Museum's Asian Art Council. Her boundless energy, her enthusiasm and her friendship will be greatly missed.
