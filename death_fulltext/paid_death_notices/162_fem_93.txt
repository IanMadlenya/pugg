
        LEDERMAN-Marie Black. Wife of the late William B. Lederman. Died on July 13, 2000 in Kingston, Ontario Canada. She was a registered nurse who worked in hospitals in Kingston and New York. A resident of Valley Stream for 30 years. She was a member of the Catholic Daughters of America. Survivors include her son Paul B. Lederman, a daughter Mary A. Lederman and her three grandchildren: Sara, John (deceased) and William. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the St. Francis College, Bill and Marie Lederman Scholarship.
