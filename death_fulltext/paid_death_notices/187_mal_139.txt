
        PRESSMAN - Dr. Walter, Little Silver, NJ, died August 10, 2002. Dr. Pressman, born in Brooklyn, NY, was a gifted mathematician. He served in the army, was a member of Congregation B'nai Israel of Rumson, NJ. He is survived by his wife, Sylviette; son, Mark; daughter-in-law, Rosalie; son, Roy; daughter, Jan; grandsons, Ariel and Gabriel.
