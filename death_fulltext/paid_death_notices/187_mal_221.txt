
        LIVINGSTON-William L. (Billy), artist-producer. Died 17 August, 2002, after a long illness. Beloved friend of Joel Key Rice. He is also survived by his brother, Dr. Carver Van Wyke Livingston of Scottsdale, Ariz., 3 nephews and a niece. A gift of remembrance may be made to: Livingston Rice Memorial Fund, St. Bartholemew's Church, 109 East 50 Street, NY, NY 10022. A gala memorial celebrating his life will be announced at a future date.
