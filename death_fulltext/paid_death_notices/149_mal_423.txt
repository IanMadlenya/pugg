
        BURKE-Robert J.., of New Rochelle, died on June 24, 1999 in New York City. He was 56 years old. Robert was born to Francis J. and Georgina McGarry Burke on May 18, 1943 in Bronx, NY. Mr. Burke served in the US Navy as a Quarter Master aboard the USS Intrepid from 1961-1964. He was the President of Queens Surface Corporation, the largest private bus company in NYC. Survived by his wife of 32 years Myra Moore Burke, loving father of Meghan Burke and Martin Burke, dear son of Georgina and the late Francis J. Burke, brother of Kenneth Burke, grandfather of Eily and Alanna Priest. Visitation Saturday and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM at Flower Funeral Home, Inc., 714 Yonkers Ave, Yonkers, NY. Mass of the Resurrection, Monday 10:30AM at St. Barnabas Church. Entombment Woodlawn Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Robert J. Buuke Memorial Scholarship Fund, C/O Mt. St. Michael Academy, 4300 Murdock Ave, Bronx, NY 10466. BURKE-Robert J. The Bus Association of New York State mourns the untimely passing of its President and friend Bob Burke. Bob was a gentleman who cared as much about the riding public as he did for his family. Everything he did in life was with grace and class. He fought his battle against illness with uncommon strength. His loss is a great one for all of us. Our heartfelt condolences to Myra and the family. Eugene J.
