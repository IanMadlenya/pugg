
        GOODMAN-Lloyd. 97, died Saturday, February 9. Cherished husband of the late Florence. Beloved son of Morris and Jennie Goodman. Dear uncle and friend. For many years, he was the proprietor of Lloyd's Mens Shop, Englewood, NJ, and enjoyed a sterling reputation as the top men's haberdasher in Bergen County. His generosity, good nature, loyalty and unique sense of humor endeared him to all who knew him. Graveside services will be held on Monday at 2 PM at Temple Israel Cemetery in Hastings-on-Hudson, New York.
