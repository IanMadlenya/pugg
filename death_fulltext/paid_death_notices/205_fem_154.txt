
        BIRNBAUM -- Edith Leah (Bonny). Died peacefully after a prolonged illness on February 20, 2004. She was the devoted wife of Leonard Birnbaum of NYC, and Hillsdale, NY, sister of Barbara Gettinger of Huntington Bay, NY, mother of Richard of NYC and Pound Ridge, NY, Jane of Norwalk, CT, and Peter of Chatham, NJ, and grandmother to five adoring grandchildren, Kate, Tim, Liz, Sam and Anna. She leaves behind so many close friends and family who were nourished by her indomitable spirit, humor and bountiful generosity. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the charity of your choice in her name.
