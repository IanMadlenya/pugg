
        ROTHBAUM-Ira, 75. Passed away Sunday, September 12, 1999 in Boca Raton, FL. Formerly of Westfield, N.J. Mr. Rothbaum worked as an Advertising Executive in New York City at N.W. Ayer and SSC&B-Lintas. After retiring to Florida in 1980 he started a new career in Financial Planning. He is survived by wife Eileen, sister Fran Coffman, children John and Peggy, stepchildren Cindy Goldsmith and Jeff Silver and five grandchildren.
