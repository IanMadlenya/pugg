
        EDELMAN-Gail Cohen. 55, of Brooklyn, NY. Died on May 2, 2000 of complications from Multiple Myeloma. Survived by her husband Marvin, son Gabriel, daughter Aliza and fiance Sean Ross, father-in-law Mayer Edelman and sisters-in-law Susan Robbins and Betty Bennett. Also survived by her dear nephews, nieces and cousins and numerous friends. A graduate of Brooklyn College and Pratt Institute, she was an accomplished artist. Since 1972, Gail was a Professor of Art at Suffolk County Community College. A scholarship will be set up at Suffolk County Community College in her memory. Other memorial gifts may be made to the International Myeloma Foundation, 2120 Stanley Hills Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90046. Funeral services will be held at Union Temple, 17 Eastern Parkway Brooklyn on Friday at 1:00pm.
