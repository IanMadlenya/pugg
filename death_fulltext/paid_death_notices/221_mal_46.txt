
        WEINSTEIN -- Nathan. Died June 2, 2005. Survived by his wife Priscilla (Chartrand Ballard), three daughters, Joyce Weinstein of New York City, Nancy Weinstein (Thomas Gildea) of Newark, Delaware and Susan Weinstein (Adam Rubinson) of Washington, DC and 3 grandchildren. Born and raised in Boston, MA, Mr Weinstein was a graduate of the University of Massachusetts and a WWII veteran. Before retiring to Chatham, MA and Port St. Lucie, FL, Mr Weinstein lived in New York City and was an executive with Harte-Hanks and Metro Media. Funeral services will be held at Temple Emanuel in Andover, MA on June 6, 2005 at 1:00 P.M. His character, spirit and warmth will be missed by all whose lives he touched. Donations may be made in his memory to the American Heart Association, and/or Hospice of Martin and St. Lucie, Florida.
