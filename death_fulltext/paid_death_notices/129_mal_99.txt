
        HEAP-J. Neil, 76, of Tuxedo Park, NY, died Tuesday, October 7, 1997, at home. Mr. Heap was the founder of World Yachts, New York, New York. Visiting will be at the home in Tuxedo Park, Thursday from 10 A.M. to 9 P.M. Funeral services will be held on Friday Oct. 10, 1997 at 11AM at St. Mary's Episcopal Church, Tuxedo Park, NY. He is survived by one sister, Nell Baumann of England and 4 nephews. Memorial contributions may be made to United Hospice of Rockland, Inc., 18 Thiells-Mt. Ivy Road, Pomona, New York 10970.
