
        WOLFE--Mary L., Dr., formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died December 30, 2003 at home in Durham, NC. She received her B.S. in Education from Fordham and her M.A. and Ph.D. from NYU, and was a member of the Brooklyn Historial Society, the National Trust for Historic Preservation and the SAH. Her career in education culminated as Administrator in the Education Office of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Brooklyn. She is survived by her sister, Frances L. Draeger, and two nieces, Deanne L. Draeger and Aline M. Dolan.
