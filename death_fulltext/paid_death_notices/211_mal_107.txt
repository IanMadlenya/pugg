
        ROCKEFELLER--James S. The Boards of Overseers and Managers and the staff of Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center note with sorrow the death of James S. Rockefeller. Mr. Rockefeller served as a member of the Board from 1950 to 1998, and in 1998 became a member of the Board of Overseers Emeriti. His friendship and support of the Center's mission will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. James D. Robinson III Honorary Chairman of Boards Douglas A. Warner III Chairman of Boards Richard A. Beattie Vice-Chairman of Boards Louis V. Gerstner, Jr. Vice-Chairman of Boards Harold Varmus President Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
        ROCKEFELLER--James S. The Trustees and staff of the American Museum of Natural History note with sadness the death of James S. Rockefeller. A valued member of the Museum's Board, he served as Trustee since 1959 and Honorary Trustee since 1976. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire Rockefeller family. Lewis W. Bernard, Chairman Ellen V. Futter, President
