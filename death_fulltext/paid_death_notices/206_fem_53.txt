
        MATKOVIC--Ksenija De Cerineo, wife of the late Ivo (Gino) Matkovic, died after a brief illness on March 4, 2004. She is survived by her daughters, Lydia Klyver, Tina Spiro and June Goldfinger, along with eight grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. She was born the Countess de Cerineo, daughter of Count Dinko de Cerineo and Tinka de Cerineo, in Brac, Yugoslavia in 1918. She and her husband Gino were long-time residents of Manhasset, New York and members of the Manhasset Bay Yacht Club. She was a loving, devoted mother, arbiter of style and owner of Artium Gallery in Port Washington, New York. Her radiant memory is treasured by her family. Donations to the Jupiter Medical Foundation would be appreciated in lieu of flowers. For information, Aycock Funeral Home, Jupiter, FL.
