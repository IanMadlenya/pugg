
        DODENHOFF-Susan M. Of Edgewater, NJ on December 11, 1997. Daughter of Richard and Barbara Dodenhoff of Matawan, NJ. Sister of Richard Dodenhoff, II of Oakton, VA and Lee Deverell of Henrietta, NY. She was Editor of Juvenile Merchandising Magazine. Funeral at First Presbyterian Church, Matawan on Monday, December 15 at 2 PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the church.
