
        SAFFAN-Leo D., 78. Of Pompano Beach, FL formerly of Seaford, NY died at his residence on November 29, 2000. Mr. Saffan was born on 92 Allen Street in NYC. Mr. Saffan was the owner & operator of Davina Lingerie and April Apparel. He was also a former board member of the Sephardic Jewish Center of Forest Hills, NY. Beloved husband of Flora Saffan, loving father of David L.(Elaine) Saffan and Dina Rose Saffan, and cherished grandfather of Gina. Temple services and interment will be held today, Sunday in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Memorial contributions in his memory may be made to either the Sephardic Jewish Brotherhood Scholarship Fund, 97-45 Queens Blvd. #
