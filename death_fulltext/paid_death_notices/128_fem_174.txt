
        CALLIMANOPULOS-Anna P. A longtime resident of Greenwich, CT, died on September 22, in Athens, Greece. She was the adoring wife of the late Pericles G. Callimanopulos and is survived by her devoted children, Katy Mazarakis of Athens, Mary Fey of New York, Gregory Callimanopulos of Athens and Domna Stanton of New York. She is also survived by eight grandchildren and nine greatgrandchildren. Burial is on Friday, September 26, in Athens, Greece.
