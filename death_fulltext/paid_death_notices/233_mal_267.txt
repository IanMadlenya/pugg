
        HAVENICK--Fred. 62, died of cancer Wednesday. He is survived by his wife Barbara and children, Izzy, Alex, Michael and Jenny and Tim Bielawski; his sister Nancy Fiverson and his mother Tillie Havenick. Fred received a B.A. from New York University and a J.D. from Seton Hall University. He was CEO of Flagler and Naples-Ft. Myers Dog Tracks and Chairman of MetroBank. He spearheaded a campaign to legalize slot machines in Dade and Broward pari-mutuels. In the Miami community, he will be remembered for his civic and philanthropic commitments including membership of the Merrick Society at the University of Miami. Family and friends will remember his sense of humor, his enduring will and his kind heart. Fred Havenick truly enriched the lives of those who knew him and left a legacy that will never be forgotten. Funeral services were held on Friday, June 23 at Temple Beth Sholom, Miami Beach. To honor Fred's memory, in lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the University of Miami Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center, P.O. Box 016960 (M867), Miami, FL 33101. For information Riverside Gordon, 305-531-1151.
