
        FISHER-Barry J., M.D. A prominent Phoenix Pediatric Pulmonologist at Children's Hospital, died suddenly while vacationing in New York on August 3. He was 58 years old. He graduated from Erasmus Hall High School in 1957, Columbia College in 1961, Downstate Medical Center in 1965, & completed his internship, residency & fellowship at Western Reserve University at Cleveland, Ohio in 1970. He is survived by his beloved wife Shirley; devoted children Andrea of Cliffside Park, New Jersey, and Jonathan of Phoenix, Arizona; his adored mother Sylvia of Fort Lauderdale, Florida; and loving brother Philip of Fort Lee, New Jersey. Funeral at 12 Noon, August 5, Boulevard Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York. Interment at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, New York.
