
        GARDNER-Sally (nee Wasylik). Of Brooklyn, NY, on March 24, 2001. Cherished wife of the late Neill Gardner. Loving mother of Victoria. Her elegance, wit, charm and generosity enriched and touched the lives of so many. A celebration of her life will be held at 2:00 PM Saturday, March 31, St. Pauls Episcopal Church, 199 Carroll St., Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent in memory of Sally Gardner to Prospect Park Alliance, 95 Prospect Park West, Brooklyn, NY 11215.
