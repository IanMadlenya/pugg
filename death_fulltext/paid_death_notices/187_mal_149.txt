
        BLOOM-Sidney. 80. Died peacefully at home in Palm Beach, FL, on August 11. Beloved husband of Sheila. Devoted father of Andrea, Peter (Challis), and Edward (Debbie). Loving grandfather of Scott, Lainy, Amanda, Michelle and Adam. He was a bright, vibrant, witty and feisty man of high principles who had a great love for children. He was a passionate advocate of liberal causes. He fought valiantly in the Normandy invasion in World War II. His passing leaves a huge gap in our family and in the world. Contributions may be sent to Friends of Abused Children of West Palm Beach or Hospice of Palm Beach County. Services Wednesday, August 14, 2PM at Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton, FL.
