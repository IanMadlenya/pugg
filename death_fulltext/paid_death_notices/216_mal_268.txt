
        SAPOLSKY--Dr. Allan. Psychologist and for 40 years Professor at Brooklyn College, passed away January 20, 2004 at age 73. Survived by his wife Dorothy, son Robert, daughter-in-law Teddi and by his two grandsons, William and Nicholas. His warmth, great wit and agile mind are irreplaceable. He was at the center of our lives, and we miss him deeply
