
        SCHACK-Samuel, 82, died March 4, 1997. Beloved husband of Rose, devoted father of Bernard of Gaithersburg, MD., Marlene of Brooklyn, and Larry of Wantaugh, N.Y. Grandfather of Eric, Marc, Lisa, Christie, Vanessa and Carol. Was active in Brooklyn Chapter Retirees, Local 3, IBEW. We'll all miss him. He'll be remembered.
