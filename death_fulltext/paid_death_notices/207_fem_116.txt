
        ALBERS -- Caroline (nee Mook). Cammy Albers, 39, died peacefully at her home on April 14 of cancer. Survived by husband Roy, three children, Sofie, Kent and Olivia, and parents Kent and Martha Mook. We remember her passion, instinct for fun, and her loyalty. Visitation Sunday, April 18, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Beecher Funeral Home, 418 Bedford Rd, Pleasantville, NY. Services Monday, 11:00, All Saints Episcopal Church, Briarcliff. Memorial contributions, Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
