
        FREYDUN-Batsheva Malka. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Batsheva Malka Freydun, beloved sister of Rabbi Yedidya Azrahian, spiritual leader of our sister congregation, Sephardic Society of Manhattan. May he & his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion & Jerusalem. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
