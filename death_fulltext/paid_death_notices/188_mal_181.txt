
        WIENER-Paul Lester Jr. On September 13, 2002 at his home in Manhattan. A 7th generation New Yorker, recipient of the Bronze Star during the Philippines campaign of World War II, host of numerous radio shows including WNEW AM's ''Make Believe Ballroom'', and sales executive. Paul had a passion for world travel, an astute insight to life, a wicked sense of humor, and most of all, a great love for his family and friends. He is survived by his loving wife Anita Callen, son Robert, daughter Susan and sister Barbara, as well as Lauren, Zoe, Rebecca and Maya. Friends may visit with the family Thursday, September 19, 6-8 PM, 45 East 89th Street, Rooftop Room.
