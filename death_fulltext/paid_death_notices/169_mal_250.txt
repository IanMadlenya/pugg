
        LeROY-Warner. On February 22, 2001 at age 65. Entrepreneur, designer and noble friend. Beloved father of Bridget, Carolyn, Max and Jennifer. Cherished brother of Linda Janklow, Brian and Quentin Vidor. Devoted Grandpa to Georgia, Joelie and Bing. You will always be a star! Services Monday, February 26th at 10:30AM at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65th St. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Meals on Wheels, 355 Lexington Avenue, NY, NY 10017, or New York Presbyterian Hospital, Department of Medicine, 525 East 68th Street, New York, NY 10021.

        LeROY-Warner. The lights of New York have been dimmed by the passing of our friend, Warner LeRoy. He was big of heart and generous of spirit.
