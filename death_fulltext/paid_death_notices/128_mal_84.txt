
        SHAINSWIT-George, age 79, of Forest Hills, NY, died September 5 at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was one of the country's foremost chess masters. He is mourned by his beloved wife, Lisa of 54 years, his daughter, Carol of NYC, son, Joseph, daughter-in-law, Jane, and grandchildren Ashley and Gregory of Briarcliff Manor, NY. Also his brothers, Seymour of NYC and Bernard of Forest Hills, NY. Services today at Parkside Memorial Chapel, Queens Blvd. and 66th Ave., Forest Hills, at 12:30 PM.
