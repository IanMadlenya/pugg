
        LAURO--James V. Born November 22, 1920. Died December 11, 2003 at his home in Round Top, NY. He retired from his company, Analytical Service Labs, and worked previously at the New York Produce Exchange Labs. He was a member of the Harbour View Club and Downtown Athletic Club. Mr. Lauro served in the Chemical Mortar Division during WW II, and received the Silver Star as well as two Purple Hearts. Mr. Lauro is survived by his children, Erika, Gretel and Michael as well as seven grandchildren and one great granddaughter. Viewing is on December 16, 2-4 and 6-8 at the Richards Funeral Home in Cairo, New York.
