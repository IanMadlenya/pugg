
        RUBELL-Phil. Born August 28, 1909 in New York City, died peacefully in Miami Beach on April 13, 2002 at the age of 92. Son of Louis and Fanny. Loving husband and and friend of Ann. Father of the late Steve Rubell, of Studio 54 fame and Donald Rubell, MD. Father-in-law of Mera. Grandfather of Jennifer and Jason. Grandfather-in-law to Michelle. Greatgrandfather of one year old Samuel. Brother to Morris, Sidney and Sylvia, who are surviving and the late Al, Bea, Ralph, and Sol. His integrity, discipline, and absolute loyalty to the ones he loved serves as an example to all who knew him. We will forever miss those days on the tennis courts together.
