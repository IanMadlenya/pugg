
        McDUFFIE--Francina. Died on August 1 at New York Presbyterian Hospital in Manhattan. Longtime employee of The New York Times newsroom; served as assistant to foreign editor, managing editor and executive editor. Survived by her son, Akil Thomas; daughter, Russina McDuffie; daughterin-law, Toya Thomas; grandson, Isiah Thomas; sisters, Eloise and Gloria McDuffie; and brother, Bristow McDuffie. Family will receive visitors on Thursday, August 5, 27 P.M., at Unity Funeral Chapels, 2352 8th Ave., at West 126th Street (212-666-8300); funeral 7-8 P.M.
