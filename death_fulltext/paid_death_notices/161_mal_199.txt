
        SCHNELLER-Arthur, M.D., 95, June 15, 2000, in West Palm Beach, Fl. Survived by his beloved daughters Joan (Joseph) Pomeranz & Jean (Arnold) Levine, 11 adored grandchildren, 10 great grandchildren & his devoted brother Dr. Leo Schneller. Extraordinarily devoted family doctor in Astoria, Long Island from 1929-1974, interupted only to serve as Commander in the United States Navy during World War II where he was one of the first doctors ashore during the Normandy invasion. He was the first lot doctor at Paramount's Astoria Studios. Services at Schwartz Bros. Funeral Chapel, Queens Boulevard., Forest Hills, June 18 at 10:00 AM. Contributions can be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407. For info., Riverside Gordon, Fla.
