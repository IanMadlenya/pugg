
        COHENSON-Rabbi Chaim. The Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York and its Staff Organization record with profound sorrow the death of Rabbi Chaim Cohenson. Rabbi Cohenson was a veteran Jewish educator, highly respected and loved by his colleagues and his students. May his entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. David Mann, Executive VP Philip Schatten, President Arlene Spiller, Chair, Staff Org.
        COHENSON-Rabbi Chaim. Young Israel of Oceanside expresses heartfelt condolences to our member Anci Cohenson on the loss of her beloved husband and to the children Michael and Ruthie and families on the loss of their devoted father. May the families be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Mark Spund, President Jay Yaacov Schwartz, Rabbi
