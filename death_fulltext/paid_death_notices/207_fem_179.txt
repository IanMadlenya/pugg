
        BLUMENTHAL--Ruth Stanton. Died on April 23, 2004. Born in Boston, MA on June 12, 1917; lived in Great Neck, NY, West Palm Beach, FL and Durham, NC. Survived by her daughter, Leda Blumenthal of Atlanta, GA; her son, James Blumenthal of Chapel Hill, NC and his wife, Daphne; granddaughter, Marisa and step-grandchildren, Anne and John McKee. There will be a memorial service at the Forest at Duke on Sunday, May 2nd at 4PM. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions in her name may be made to the Forest at Duke.
