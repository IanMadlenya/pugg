
        LEVINE-Eva. The Lillian Wald Nurses Council of The New York Chapter of Hadassa, mourns the passing of our devoted friend and Board Member on June 3, 1998, after a valiant struggle with cancer. An oncology nurse, Eva dedicated her life to easing the suffering of others. Our heartfelt condolences to her beloved husband, Marvin, her sister, Etta, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Miriam Fridman, President
