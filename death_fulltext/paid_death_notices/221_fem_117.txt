
        SANDLER--Anna. June 14, 2005. Loving wife, mother, grandmother and great grandmother. Predeceased by her husband George and son Herman. Survived by children Maurice and Gloria, Paul and Maryjane, Suki, Ruth Lynn, Marlene & Albert Aiello, grandchildren Sandy, Alan and Brooke, Lori and Steve, Lisa, David and Jocelyn, Suzanne, Sarah, Pamela, Gillian, Jordana and David, Lauren & Jose, Susan, Jessica, Sara and Rebecca, great grandchildren Andrew, Aidyn, Samuel, Justin, Joshua and Charlie. Her joie de vivre, her devotion to family, her generosity and her personal strength will be remembered by all. Services Thursday, June 16, at 1 P.M., at The Riverside, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Herman S. Sandler Endowment Fund of American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra, 122 East 42 Street, Suite 4507, N.Y., N.Y. 10168.
        SANDLER--Anna. The American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra Board of Directors and staff extend our heartfelt sympathy to the entire Sandler family on the passing of Anna Sandler. Zubin Mehta, Itzhak Perlman Co-Chairman Lynn Syms, President Suzanne K. Ponsot, Exec. Dir.
