
        COOPER--Marvin Allen. A gift to our world. Treasured husband and soulmate of Joan (Harris). Adoring of and adored by and best friend of his children Debbie and Billy, and daughter-in-law Hilary. Devoted ''Paga'' of Madeleine and Annie Rose. 73. Died Feburary 4, 2005. A patient, wise and brilliant human being. Loved his family, the law and all of life. To the world he is one person, to our family and friends he is the world. To honor Marvin, any donations may be made in his honor to Morry's Camp at 900 Dobbs Ferry Road, Elmsford, N.Y. 10607. (914592-3055)
