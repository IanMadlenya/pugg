
        O'CONNELL-John. The officers and members of the Transport Workers Union of America AFLCIO deeply mourn the death of one of the most capable and wellliked officers in our union's history. John O'Connell served our union for 30 years as International Representative, International Vice President, Administrative Assistant to former International President Matthew Guinan, and Director of Publicity from 1949 until his retirement in 1979. We offer our sincere condolences to his wife, Genie, sons, John, Jr. and Thomas, and daughters, Maureen and Patricia. Viewing will be at the Moritz Funeral Home, Rt. 303, Tappan, NY (914-359-0890) Wednesday, Dec 9th from 7-9 P.M., and Thursday Dec. 10th from 2-4 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. Funeral services will be conducted Friday 10 A.M., Dec. 11th at St. Catherine's Church, Western Highway in Blauvelt, NY.
