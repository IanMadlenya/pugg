
        GOETZ-Ruth Goodman. October 12, 2001. Beloved wife and collaborator of the late Augustus Goetz. Wonderful mother of Judy Goetz Sanger. Delighted grandmother of Katie Firth. Service Thursday, October 25, 2 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., NYC. Interment services private at Keller's Church Cemetery, Bucks County, PA. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Dramatist Guild Fund of America and Young Playwrights Festival.
