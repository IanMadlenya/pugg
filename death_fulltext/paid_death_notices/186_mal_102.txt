
        HAIGHT-David L. Of Briarcliff Manor, died July 7, 2002, at home surrounded by his family. In 1949 he founded Haggerty Millwork Corporation in Mount Kisco, NY with two partners, now deceased. He retired January 31, 2001. He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Elinor, his sons Douglas and Andrew and daughter Diane Angst, grandsons Andrew and Aaron and granddaughters Diana and Katherine Angst. Another son, David, died in 1970. Viewing hours 2-4 and 7-9pm, Wednesday, July 10, 2002, Waterbury and Kelly, Briarcliff Manor. Interment Friends Meeting House, Purchase, NY, Thursday at 10am.
