
        BLANK-Jeanne Webster, 77, died Sept. 22 in Albany, NY, formerly of Mount Vernon, N.Y., and Greenwich, CT. Wife of Robert H. Blank of Albany, NY, mother of Laurel B. Andrew of Guilderland, NY and Paul W. Blank (Annette Yuki Makino) of Arcata, CA., and the late Catherine Jane Blank. Grandmother of Paul A. Inglis and Maya Jeanne Makino. This past June her book ''The Death of an Adult Child'' was published by Baywood Press. Contributions may be made to Compassionate Friends, 83 Brookline Ave., Albany, NY 12203. For additional information you may contact the Ginsburg Memorial Chapel, Inc., Albany, NY.
