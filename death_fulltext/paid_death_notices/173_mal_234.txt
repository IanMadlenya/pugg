
        LIPP-Leo. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, brother, uncle and friend, died in Sarasota, FL on June 18, 2001. We will miss his warmth, constant friendship and love of life. He mentored many during his years as a leader in the jewelry industry, and was a leader of his community during his retirement. He will be loved and long remembered by those around him. Surviving are his wife Stella, daughter Karen Janowski and husband Ben, son Bruce and wife Colleen, grandchildren Peter Janowski and Chloe Lipp, brother Halley and sister Pearle Gordon. Memorial service held June 22nd in Sarasota. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
