
        SENTER-Alfred Mitchell, age 86. Owner of the former Senter's Department Store in Brunswick, Maine died Saturday, September 16, 2000, Freeport, Maine. Mr. Senter was born in Brunswick, March 2, 1914, the son of Wilbur Fisk & Emma Thompson Senter. He attended Brunswick High School for two years and graduated from Kiskiminetas Spring School, Saltsburg, Pennsylvania in 1934. Mr. Senter then attended the University of New Hampshire for two years and graduated in 1939 from the Wharton School of Business and Finance of the University of Pennsylvania. Survived by 2 brothers, John Thompson, Sr. of Hampden, Maine and George Dyer of Brunswick; a sister, Jane Senter Lee of Westwood, New Jersey; fourteen nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held at 2 P.M., Sunday, September 24, 2000 at First Parish Church, Maine Street, Brunswick. For information Brackett Funeral Home, 29 Federal Street, Brunswick, Maine 04011.
