
        LEVINSON-Emanuel, Doctor of Electrical Engineering, leader in the field of inertial navigation and a developer of the ring laser gyroscope, died suddenly January 15 at age 74 in Charlottesville, Va., where he was consultant to Litton/ Sperry Marine. With 53 years in the defense industry, he was a recipient of the Institute of Navigation Berkely Award for contributions to engineering and was the author of numerous papers and patents. His loss is deeply felt by his wife, Shirley, of more than 50 years; his son, Lawrence; his daughter, Linda, and her husband, Manny; his grandsons, Jeremy, David and Jonathan, as well as his professional associates. He was a man who inspired the highest standards of excellence in professional and personal life with modesty, fairness, dedication and loyalty. Funeral services will be held today, 11:30 AM, at Bloomfield Cooper Funeral Home, Manalapan, NJ, (732) 446-4242.
        LEVINSON-Emanuel. We are all grateful that the father of our dear partner, Larry Levinson, was blessed with a full and rewarding life, a wonderful marriage to his beloved Shirley and devoted and caring children. Our deepest sympathy and heartfelt condolences go out to Larry and all other members of the Levinson family on its great and sudden loss. Herrick, Feinstein LLP
