
        MEHLER-Elizabeth T. ''Betty'' (nee Trachtman). On August 14, 1998. Of Philadelphia, PA. Devoted wife of the late Max (Judge, Appellate Division, New Jersey Superior Court). Formerly of West Orange, NJ. Beloved mother of Cynthia Adler and loving motherin-law of Harry R. Adler of Bridgeton, NJ. Adoring grandmother of David, Amy and Jonathan Adler of New York City. Sister of the late Joseph Trachtman, Esq. Relatives and friends are invited to graveside services Tuesday, 11 AM precisely, at the B'nai Jeshurun Cemetery, Hillside, NJ. Shiva will be observed Wednesday and Thursday at 8 PM, at the residence of Cynthia and Harry R. Adler. Contributions in her memory may be made to U.J.A. or to the charity of the donor's choice.
