
        HEALY-Nora A., 90, died Wednesday, April 9, 1997, in Palm Coast, FL., after a long illness. She lived in Tudor City for almost 50 years before moving to Florida. A legal secretary, she worked on the Nuremberg Trials in Germany after World War II. She was partner in a medical reporting agency. An avid swimmer, she enjoyed the sport until the last year of her life. She is survived by her sister, Dorothy Schmidt of Jupiter, FL and many loving nieces and nephews. A mass will be held at St. Agnes Roman Catholic Church, East 43rd Street, Saturday, April 19 at 10 AM.
