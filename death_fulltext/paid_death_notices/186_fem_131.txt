
        LINDENBAUM STERN-Ghity. The Board of Directors and staff of the 92nd Street Y mourn the passing of Ghity Lindenbaum Stern, adored mother of long standing Board member Armand Lindenbaum and mother-in-law of Jean. Our hearts and prayers are with them and with Mrs. Stern's other children, her many grandchildren and great-grandchildren, and her sisters. As her life was a blessing, so may her memory be as well. Matthew Bronfman President Philip Milstein Chairman Sol Adler Executive Director
