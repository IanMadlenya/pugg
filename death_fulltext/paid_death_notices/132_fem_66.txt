
        GOLUB-Pearl. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the passing of Pearl Golub, beloved mother, grandmother and great grandmother. We offer our sincere condolences to her sons, Harvey (Roberta) and Eugene (Arlene), and the entire Golub family. We hope that your loving memories of Pearl will ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Executive V.P.
