
        SHELLENBERGER James Adamson, recently of Reed's Landing, Springfield, MA, died Monday, October 23rd. Born on December 24, 1913, in Philadelphia, PA, Mr. Shellenberger was an editor of the Ladies Home Journal and The American Home Magazine during the 1950's. He later was a Senior Partner at Anna Rosenberg Associates in New York. Mr. Shellenberger worked as a consultant to Jujamcyn Theatres in his later life, and served as head of the National Corporate Theatre Fund for two years. Mr. Shellenberger was an avid orchid hobbyist, and served for several years on the Board of the American Horticultural Society. His wife, Ann Howard Shellenberger of Greenwich, CT, died in 1996. He is survived by five daughters: Mary S. Graves of Rowayton, CT; Frances Goode, of Pollock Pines, CA; Jane Shellenberger, of Boulder, CO; Susan Shellenberger of Belchertown, MA; and Ann S. Insalaco, of Scotland, CT, and six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Saturday, October 28 at 10 AM at the Round Hill Community Church in Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 710 West 168th Street, New York, New York 10032-9982.
