
        O'SHAUGHNESSY-Lucy Marie O'Sullivan. On September 15, 1999. A long time elementary school teacher and loving mother and wife. Educated at the Ursuline Academy and Maxwell Training College, she and her late husband, John Quinn O'Shaughnessy agreed that their primary role was preparing their four children as complete human beings. Her late sisters, Mary O'Sullivan Wood and Kathleen O'Sullivan Spalding spent their early years in Kenmare, Ireland before they came to the United States. She is survived by her daughters Sister Alice O'Shaughnessy, M.D., C.S.J., Kathleen O'Shaughnessy Hearn, her sons, J. Kevin O'Shaughnessy and Thos. E.B. O'Shaughnessy, Ed.D. She is also survived by her grandchildren, Nancy O'Shaughnessy Smith, Ellen Clelia O'Shaughnessy and Christopher Hearn. Also mourning her passing are her daughter-inlaw Laura Nuzzi O'Shaughnessy, her son-in-law Rex Alan Hearn, Sister Lauren Hanley, C.S.J., and her loving friend of 85 years, Loretta Coyne Donovan French. Lucy is remembered as a loving elegant lady of sharp wit who gave endlessly of herself for her loved ones. Friends may call Saturday and Sunday 2-4, 7-9 P.M. at Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave. (at RR Plaza), Floral Park, New York. Funeral Mass on Monday 10:30 A.M. at Our Lady of Victory R.C. Church. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery. In Lieu of flowers the family suggests donation to Mercy Hospice Uniondale, New York.
