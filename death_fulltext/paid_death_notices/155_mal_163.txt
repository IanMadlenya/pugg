
        ROSENBAUM-Bernard. Died on December 7, 1999 at age 92. He leaves his wife Ruth, two children Fred and Laura Randall, and their spouses, a stepson, six grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. He was preceded in death by his first wife Frances. Bernard was a CPA, an active community worker and a lover of the visual arts. donations may be made to the Renaissance Project of Westchester.
