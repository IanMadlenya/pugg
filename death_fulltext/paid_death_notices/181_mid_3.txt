
        HAUG-Frederick J., 91. Formerly of Manhasset, N.Y. Died February 5, 2002 in New York City. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Devoted and adored father of Gretchen Stone, Hedi White and father-in-law of Lewis Stone and Thomas White. Cherished grandfather of Pamela Stone-Humphrey and her husband Jim, Bart Stone and Timothy White. Proud great-grandfather of Riley Humphrey. Dear brother of Bertha Dean, Mina Cory and Elsie Haug. Caring uncle and granduncle of David Cory, Mina Kahofer, Daniel Cory, John Cory, Robert Dean, Frederick Dean and Barbara Dalally and their children. Family will receive friends Friday. 2 to 4 and 6 to 8PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Funeral services Saturday, 11AM at Congregational Church of Manhasset, 1845 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, N.Y. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations to be made to the charity of your choice.
        HAUG-Frederick J. Stanley M. Isaacs Neighborhood Center notes with sorrow the passing of Frederick J. Haug. He was a major donor to the Isaacs Center, and the father of longtime Board member and former Board President, Gretchen H. Stone. Our deepest sympathy is extended to Gretchen, her mother Dorothy, and their family. Doris C. Halaby, Chairman Barry J. Alperin, Board Pres. Wanda Wooten, Exec. Dir.
        HAUG-Frederick J. We are saddened by the loss of our longtime client, a true gentleman, devoted to family, charitable. He will be missed. Mahoney Cohen & Co., CPA
