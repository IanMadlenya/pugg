
        SOPHER-Morris. The Yeshiva of Telshe Alumni and the Time For Torah Fellowship mark with sorrow the loss of our dear friend, Mr. Morris Sopher of blessed memory. Husband of Mrs. Marilyn Sopher, father of dear children Jonathan, Barbara and Lisa. His elegance and class, as well as his infectious smile, will long be remembered. His devotion to Torah study and his dedication to Torah causes are a legacy that he bequeathed to all of us and shall never be forgotten. To his beloved family, may you be comforted and spared from further sorrow. Harav Avrohom Ausband, Rosh Ha Yeshiva Rabbi Eli Melech Kohn Mr. Edward Chernoff, Chairman
