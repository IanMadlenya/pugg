
        IMBER-Rose. Of Longwood, FL, died in New York City on Sunday May 26, 2002. Wife of the late George Imber. Mother of Susan Natale and Gerald Imber. Grandmother of Evan Natale and Peter, Jason and Gregory Imber. She was loved by all. Her unfailing love, deep devotion to her family, & good humor are irreplaceable, and will be missed by all. Services, were held Wednesday 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Lighthouse International, 111 East 59 St, NY NY 10022. The Family of Rose Imber
