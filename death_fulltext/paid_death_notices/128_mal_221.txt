
        BENDEL-Henri. September 15, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Lillian. Loving father of John and his wife Diana. A celebration of his life will be held on Saturday, September 20 at 1:30 P.M., at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        BENDEL-Henri. The Board of Directors and Shareholders of 20 Sutton Place South, Inc. Deeply regret the passing away of Henri Bendel on Monday, September 15, 1997. Mr. Bendel was a longtime resident, shareholder and past President of 20 Sutton Place South.
