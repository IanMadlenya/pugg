
        STERN-William. Beloved husband of Pauline. Dear brother of Sue Felstein, Esther and Phil Krieger, Beatrice Kessler, Ruby and Elaine Stern and Lillian Stern. Loving uncle. Committed social democrat and humanist. Served as executive secretary of the Workmen's Circle and on the boards of many Jewish and socialist organizations. Services Sunday, 11:45AM at Riverside Chapels, 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave, New York City.
        STERN-Will. The officers, staff and leadership of the Jewish Labor Committee note with deep sadness the death of Will Stern, longtime leading member of our organization and other groups and movements committed to a fairer, more just society. Brother Stern never wavered in his commitment to creative and vibrant secular Jewish life. His devotion to his wife was inspiring. Heartfelt condolences to Pauline and all of his family and friends. Morton Bahr, President Avram B. Lyon, Executive Director
