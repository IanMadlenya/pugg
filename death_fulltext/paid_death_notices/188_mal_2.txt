
        BERNSTEIN-Harold. It is with great sorrow, that we acknowledge the passing of Harold Bernstein. Harold was ''a mensch for all seasons,'' who enjoyed the love and respect of the legal community, his family and friends. He was a valued trustee of Congregation Mt. Sinai for many years and will be greatly missed. May his family know no more sorrow. Congregation Mt. Sinai Rabbi Joseph Potasnik Gail Singer, President
        BERNSTEIN - Harold. Bnai Zion Foundation mourns the passing of our esteemed former National President Harold Bernstein.
