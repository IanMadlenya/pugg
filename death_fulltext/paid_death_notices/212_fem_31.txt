
        GOLDFISCHER -- Sally (Sa rah) Klaw. 98, of Lauderhill, FL, died September 1, 2004. Adored and adoring wife of the late William, predeceased by her beloved son Jerry. Devoted mother of Patrica Israel. Cherished by granddaughter Staci Girsch, her husband Barry, and greatgrandsons Bryan and Randy. Loving Nana of Leslie, Steve, Alex and Jay Meisner, Mark and Amy, Stephanie, Evan, Julie, Sabrina and Gigi Goldfischer. Sally brought integrity, compassion, joie de vivre, and beauty to all who would open their hearts and minds.
