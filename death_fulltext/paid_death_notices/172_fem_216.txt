
        FLANCBAUM-Paula. Born Peyrel Hudes Lerman in Bialobrzege, Poland, died May 26, 2001. A survivor of the Auschwitz death camp, she was liberated in Bergen Belsen in April, 1945. In 1949 she emigrated to America. She will be missed sorely by her loving husband Hyman, her devoted daughters and sons Shirley and Henry Hackel, and Florence and Barry Sideroff, and her adoring grandchildren Joshua, Julie, Melissa and Marc. Funeral services will be held May 30, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th St, at 11:15 AM.
