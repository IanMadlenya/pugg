
        HASKINS-Caryl P. The Trustees of the American Museum of Natural History are saddened by the death of Dr. Caryl P. Haskins, Trustee of the Museum since 1973, an Honorary Trustee since 1988. He was a valued member of the Board who brought extraordinary intelligence and dedication to the Museum's mission to educate the public about the natural world and global cultures. Lewis W. Bernard, Chairman Ellen V. Futter, President American Museum of Natural History
