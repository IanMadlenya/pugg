
        CARMILLY - Rhoda. Died June 10th, 2007, at the age of 93. She was the beloved wife of Moshe Carmilly and devoted mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. She was president of the New York Chapter of Hadassah, president of the Women's Organization of Congregation Shearith Israel and active in the Junior League of the Yeshiva Women's Organization. Graveside funeral was held on June 11th at Riverside Cemetery in New Jersey.
