
        SOIFER-Frank. Age 94, of North Miami Beach, FL, formerly of New York, died June 28, 1999 at home. Loving husband of the late Ruth and the late Sunny. Cherished father of Janet Paul, and son - in - law, Robert. Devoted grandfather of Dr. Richard Paul, Debra Rappaport, and Stephanie Paul. Great grandfather of Cory, Sam, and Griffin Rappaport, and Jordan, Brandon, and Jillian Paul. Loving brother of Sally Adler, and Herman Soifer, and of the late Beatrice Harkavy and the late Lena Tamny. Graveside services at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ, Thursday, July 1, 1999 at 11:30 A.M. Contributions in his name may be made to the charity of your choice.
