
        DAMBRA -- Joseph Paul. Passed quietly, in the presence of his son, on October 11, 2003. He was 53, and is also survived by a treasured daughter, along with her loyal son. Cherished husband, father, grandfather, brother, uncle, nephew, cousin, and friend. Family and friends mourn with unspeakable sadness the untimely loss of this deeply loved man. His spirit continues to brighten and nurture the lives of the many whom he touched. He was surrounded ever and until the end, in sickness and in health, by his devoted and loving wife, Mary. Memorial wake will be held on Thursday, October 16, 1-4 and 6-9; and Friday, October 17, 12-5 at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Funeral services will be held on Saturday, October 18, 9-11 at Saint Paul the Apostle Church, 59th Street and Columbus Avenue.
