
        SILBERSTEIN-Sylvia. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sorrow the passing of Sylvia Silberstein, beloved wife of our devoted donor, William Silberstein. An artist, philanthropist and loving mother and grandmother, she has been an ardent supporter of UJA-Federation. An exemplary woman, her presence will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences to her entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        SILBERSTEIN-Sylvia. The Board of Directors of the Mid-Westchester Y extend their deepest sympathy to longtime Associate Director William Silberstein on the passing of his beloved wife. As a Life Overseer of the Y, Sylvia will be remembered as an extraordinary friend and supporter. Eileen Lehrer, President Dr. S. Morton Altman, Exec Dir
