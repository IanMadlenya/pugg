
        VAN DE MAELE-Joan. The Board of Directors of The Harry Frank Guggenheim Foundation mourns the death of Joan Van de Maele on Tuesday, September 12, in Miami Beach. She was born September 3, 1912, and was the daughter of Harry Frank Guggenheim and Helen Matzinger. Mrs. Van de Maele was a Captain in the Women's Army Air Force and served in the Pacific in World War II. She was instrumental in starting the Boys Club of St. Croix, of which she was a resident. Mrs. Van de Maele was a Director of the Florence and Daniel Guggenheim and The Harry Frank Guggenheim Foundations and a member of the Advisory Board of the Peggy Guggenheim Collection in Venice, Italy. James M. Hester, President The Harry Frank Guggenheim Foundation
