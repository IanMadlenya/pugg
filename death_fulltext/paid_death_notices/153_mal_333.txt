
        ZONA-James W., Reverend. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of St. Mary's Church, 10-08 49th Street, Long Island City, on Oct. 24. Reposing in Conway Funeral Home, 82-19 Northern Blvd., Jackson Heights, Wednesday, Oct. 27, from 2-5 and 7-9 P.M., and Thursday, Oct. 28, from 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral at Our Lady of Fatima Church, 2502 80th St., Jackson Heights, at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass at St. Mary's Church, Friday, Oct. 29, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
