
        GERSON-Ellen. Dear daughter of Beatrice G. Nelkin and the late Louis Gerson. Former librarian at the Haskell Library of the Blind, passed away on Friday, July 2, 1999. Devoted cousin and neice, caring friend to many. She will be missed by all. Services at the United Hebrew Community of New York, 201 E. Broadway, 12 noon, Tuesday, July 6th.
