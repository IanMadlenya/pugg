
        FELDER-Alyce Scherzer. On December 12, 2001 in San Diego. Wife of the late Karl Felder and the late Michael Scherzer. Mother of Larry, Don and Judie Stuart. Motherin-law of Carole, Toni and David Stuart. Grandmother of Mike, Ian, Erica & Mark Land, and Gabrielle. Beloved teacher and counselor to many. She was active in the Jewish communities of Long Island and San Diego. She enriched all of our lives and for this we are grateful. We will miss her. In lieu of flowers, please make a contribution to the charity of your choice.
