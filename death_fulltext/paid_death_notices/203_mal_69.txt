
        GREEN--Jerome ''Jerry.'' On December 5, 2003. Born October 8, 1937, son of Irving and Fay (Glassberg) Green. Owner of Savoy Manor Catering for over 40 years. Member of Woodlands Community Temple for 32 years. Surviving is his wife Lois Green of Hartsdale, daughter Robin and husband Steve of New Jersey, son Daniel Green of NYC. Funeral 12 noon, Tuesday, Woodlands Community Temple, 50 Worthington Rd, Greenburgh. Memorials to Woodlands Community Temple.
