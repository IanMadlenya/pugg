
        FOSBURG-Jeanette (nee Rose), 78. Devoted mother of Susin Fair and Marc Shapiro. Loving grandmother of Samantha. Cherished daughter of Fannie Pollack. Loving sister of Irene and Martin Gross and Marvin and Sondra Rose. Beloved by her niece and nephews. She was a retired teacher from Franklin K. Lane High School. Her sparkling personality and great storytelling ability will keep her memory in the hearts of her friends and family forever.
