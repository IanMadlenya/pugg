
        LAHULLIER - Denise. Friends and colleagues of the American Mental Health Foundation mourn the passing of Denise LaHullier on February 22, 2006. For many years an officer of the Foundation, Miss LaHullier contributed to the work and publications of the late Dr. Stefan de Schill, Foundation director and her long-time companion. Donations in her name may be made to the Mary Manning Walsh Nursing Home in New York City. A private memorial is planned.
