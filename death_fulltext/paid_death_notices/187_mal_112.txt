        SALZBERG-David. Haddassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., mourns the passing of David Salzberg, beloved husband of Sylvia, devoted father of Barry and Jane Salzberg and Lani and Robert Hopp, and loving grandfather of Mark, Karen, Dana and Jenna. As a Guardian of Zion, David was a generous benefactor of Haddassah. He was truly devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. His generous legacy will live on through the visionary projects he supported at the Haddassah Medical Organization. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him. Bonnie Lipton, President
