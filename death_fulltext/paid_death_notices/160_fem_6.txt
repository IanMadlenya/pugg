
        BLUMBERG-Ronda Ormont. Age 52, suddenly on April 29th. Survived by her devoted husband Henry & beloved daughter Perri. The most gracious and generous lady, Dr. Ormont was a wondrous mother and wife and a pioneer in career counseling for creative professionals. She inspired all who knew her and will live in the hearts of her family and friends forever. Donations in her memory may be made to Strang Cancer Prevention Center, 428 East 72d St or The Actor's Work Program, 165 West 46th Street. We love you, Mom, and we'll never forget you.
