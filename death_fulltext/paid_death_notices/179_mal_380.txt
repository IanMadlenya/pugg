
        LIMPERT-Harold J. III, ''Bob''. President, Limpert Bros., Inc., Vineland, NJ, on December 29 at age 79 after a brief illness. During WWII he proudly served as a naval aviator, test pilot, instructor, and was in the naval reserves. An outstanding businessman, author, inventor and speaker for his industry, he was a member of The Young Presidents Organization, The Westchester Country Club for over 50 years, Navy League, Board of Directors of 60 Sutton Place South for almost 40 years, Vineland Library Foundation Board of Directors, National Fruit and Flavor Association, NICYRA, and founding member of the South Jersey Business Association. He is survived by his beloved companion Ruth Robinson of NYC, cherished colleague of Pearl Giordano of Vineland, NJ, brother John Jr. of Plainfield, NJ, as well as his loving nieces and nephews. Visitation will be held at the Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74th St., on Wednesday, January 2, 2002, from 10:30 AM until funeral services at 12:30 PM. For further information contact Krtil Funeral Home, 212-744-3084.
