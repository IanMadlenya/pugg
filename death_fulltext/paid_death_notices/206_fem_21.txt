
        WALDMAN -- Blanche. The Board and Staff of New York Society for the Deaf express its deepest sympathies to the family of Blanche Waldman. Beloved wife of the late Lester, former Executive Director and President, and mother of Michael, our current President, and Rebecca. She possessed a caring and generous philanthropic spirit and a strong devotion to family and community. She will be sorely missed by those whose lives she has touched.
