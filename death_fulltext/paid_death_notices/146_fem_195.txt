
        WATTS-Catherine Hillery. Died March 12, 1999. She attended Wilberforce University and NYU. Her lifetime career in social work was with the NYC Dept. of Social Services in projects such as Womens Shelters, Home Care Operations and the Social Service Welfare Center. Catherine was a valued participant in the Wiltwyck School for Boys, Symphony of the New World, the Alpha Kappa Alpha's National Reading Project and the Queens Botanical Gardens. She leaves behind her devoted husband Joesph, sister-in-law Virginia, brother-in-law Frederick and eight nephews and nieces. Services were held on March 18 and she was interred at the Maple Grove Cemetery in Kew Gardens, NY.
