
        MEIROWITZ - Bella. Died peacefully on November 17, 2002. Cherished mother of Hank Meirowitz. Beloved sister of Sophie Chait. Adored aunt of the Goldstein, Chait and Gitlin families. Cousin Bobbie Deitch and lifetime companion Frances Price. A lady with a big heart, great warmth and love, an interest in everything and everybody, always with a smile on her face and a chuckle in her throat. A woman totally devoted to family and friends. She will be sorely missed and will always be lovingly remembered. May she rest in peace. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Jewish National Fund for Trees in Israel. Services Tuesday, 11:15 AM, at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
