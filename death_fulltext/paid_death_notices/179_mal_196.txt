
        DELLHEIM-Walter J., December 2. Beloved husband and dearest friend of Rosalind. Devoted father of Charles, Jeffrey, and Jonathan, & father-in-law of Laura. Adoring grandfather of Alexandra and Caroline, always remembered in our hearts and thoughts. Born in Germany in 1922, he arrived in New York at age fifteen and was forever grateful to America for providing him with refuge and opportunity. A kind and generous man always eager to help his employees, he was President of Argo Instruments for 35 years. Mrs. Dellheim currently resides in Brookline, MA.
