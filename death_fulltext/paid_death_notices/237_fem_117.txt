
        COMPAGNO -- Dorothy ''Dede'' Unkefer. November 18, 1935-October 12, 2006. A gentle and supportive mother who was a crossword puzzle maven with encyclopedic knowlege of nature and all things. She was a graduate of Vassar College. Writer and editor for non-profit organizations including: the Institute of International Education, the Public Broadcasting System, and the American Bible Society. Dede was a former President of the Women's Prison Association where she received the Mayor's Award for Volunteer Service from Edward I. Koch. She served on the Boards of Friends Central School in Philadelphia and the Cosmopolitan Club of New York where she was First Vice President. She is survived by her husband, Allen E. Compagno, her sons Alexander and Andrew, and brother Ralph Thomas Unkefer Jr. Services will be private.
