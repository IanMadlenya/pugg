
        TOPPING--Henry J. III. ''Tony,'' age 61, died peacefully on July 15. He served in the United States Navy. He was loved by many and is survived by his two children, Henry J. Topping IV and Samantha S. Topping, his sister Sandra and two nieces. He will be greatly missed but never forgotten. Private burial to be held. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Renaissance Project. >>AD#
