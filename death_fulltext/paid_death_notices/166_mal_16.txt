
        PIERCE-Samuel Riley, Jr. Born September 8, 1922, Glen Cove, LI, died October 31, 2000 in Maryland. Former partner in law firm Battle, Fowler and former Secretary of Housing and Urban Development, 1981-1989. A memorial service will be held on November 18 at 11AM at St. Marks Methodist Church, 137th Street and Edgecombe Ave., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to Cornell University or St. Marks Methodist Church.
