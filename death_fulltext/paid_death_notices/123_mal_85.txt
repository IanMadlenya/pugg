
        TUITE-William E. of Stuart, Florida and Southhold, Long Island died on Sunday April 6, 1997 at his home in Stuart after a long illness. He was born on August 21, 1923 and was graduated from Regis High School in 1941. Mr. Tuite served as a First Lieutenant in the U.S. Air Force during World War II, stationed in North Africa. He was graduated from Fordham University in 1947 and later earned graduate degrees from New York University in meteorology, mathematics and business. After beginning his career in public accounting and Exxon Corporation, he joined the investment firm of Drexel Burnham Lambert in 1961. For twenty seven years Bill Tuite was one of the financial services industries pioneers retireing in 1988 as Senior Vice President for Data Processing. He is survived by his wife Paula McCarthy Tuite of Stuart and his children. William P. of San Antonio Tx., Catherine Rosenberg, MD of Manhattan, NY, Marilyn A. of Wynnewood, PA, his brother; Jerome J. Tuite of Englishtown, NJ and his son-in-law Eric Rosenberg, MD.
