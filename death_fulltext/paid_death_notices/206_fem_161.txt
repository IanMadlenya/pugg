
        LEVY-MEYER--Janet M., 65, scholar, musicologist, wife, sister, friend, died peacefully, surrounded by her family, on March 16, 2004 after a valiant fight with cancer. A graduate of Vassar College, with a PhD from Stanford University, she was well-known for her outstanding scholarship on Beethoven and on musical performance. Survived by her loving husband, Leonard B. Meyer, devoted sister, Karen Beth Levy, step-children and grandchildren. Services Friday, March 19th, 1:00PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, send donations to Vassar College, 127 Raymond Avenue, Poughkeepsie, NY 12604 or Berith Shalom Congregation, 167 Third Street Troy, NY 12180.
