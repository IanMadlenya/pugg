
        DUDENSING--Patrick. Died at his home in Naples, FL on July 19, 2005. He was born in Ithaca, NY on June 7, 1926. Pat was educated at the Park Lodge School in Pau, France, Deerfield Academy, Harvard College and Dartmouth College. He served in the U.S. Army in the South Pacific during World War II, achieving the rank of 2nd Lieutenant. He married the late Margaret Jane (Peggy) McDonald in 1950. They were married until her death in 1993. Pat ran an art gallery in Woodstock, VT in the early 1950's. He then worked at Amro, and Young & Rubicam in New York City. He moved his family to Duxbury, MA in 1964, and continued to maintain a home there. He worked for Ocean Spray, United Fruit, and United Brands. He became a resident of Naples in 1989. He is survived by DD Allen of New York City, Ellen Strickland of Santa Barbara, CA, Elizabeth Udell of Campbell, CA, and Peter and Nicky Dudensing of Duxbury, along with their children Christopher, Daniel, and Lauren.
