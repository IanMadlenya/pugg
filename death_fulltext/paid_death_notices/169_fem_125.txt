
        GWATHMEY-Rosalie died on February 12 at her home in Amagansett, NY, age 92. Wife of the late artist, Robert Gwathmey, cherished and devoted mother of Charles, adored mother-in-law of Bette-Ann, beloved grandmother of Annie and Eric Steel. Artist, photographer, founding member of The Photo League, patron and political activist. Born in Charlotte, N.C., Rosalie was a passionate observer of social inequities as documented through her photographs of Southern Black culture. Her charm, perceptive wit, honesty and sensitivity, as well as her unique personal style will be remembered by all who loved and knew her. A memorial service to be announced at a later date.
