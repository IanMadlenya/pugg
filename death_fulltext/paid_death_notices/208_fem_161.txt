
        KEARNS--Agnes (nee Kelly) on May 19, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Joseph G. Kearns. Loving mother of Kristine A. and the late Michael and Stephen, sister of William J. Kelly of Garden City and the late Eileen Reidy. Survived by her grandchildren Michael and Marieke, their mother Marianne Hokke, many nieces, nephews and cherished friends. Past President of the Catholic Settlement and the Ladies of Charity in the Archdiocese of New York. Agnes was an active fundraiser for Catholic Charaties and was awarded the Lady Grand Cross by the Equestrian Order of the Holy Sepulchre. Visiting Friday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 Fourth Ave, Bklyn. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday 10 AM, St Anselm's Church. In lieu of flowers donations in Agnes's memory to a charity of your choice would be appreciated.
