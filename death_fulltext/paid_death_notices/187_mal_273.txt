
        FUNK-George H. 65, of Towanda RR1, PA, died August 24, 2002 in Towanda, PA. Born April 17, 1937 in Abington, PA, he was a graduate of Lehigh University with a degree in chemistry and chemical engineering. He was employed at E.I. Dupont in Towanda for 38 years until his retirement on January 31, 1998.
