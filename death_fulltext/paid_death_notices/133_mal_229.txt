
        TANGERMAN-Elmer J. ''Tange''. August 1907 to February 1998. Technical Editor, Author, Woodcarver, Teacher. BSME, ME, Purdue University. Editor of Product Engineering, McGraw Hill, retired 1970. Teacher at JC Campbell Folk School. Survived by his wife Mary C. Tangerman, two daughters, grandchilren and greatgrandchildren.
