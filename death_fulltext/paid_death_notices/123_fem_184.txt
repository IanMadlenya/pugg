
        FABER-Dora ''Toby''. Born November 22, 1910, died April 25, 1997. Beloved wife of David. Mother of Stephen and Marilyn. Grandmother of Michelle, Rachel, Alyssa and Jeremy. Sister of Sylvia. An honor student, Toby worked for United Cerebral Palsy and, for many years, the Union of American Hebrew Congregations. She was possessed of an indomitable spirit and was a passionate lover of education, music and literature. She leaves a legacy of laughter and compassion for all humanity. Her memory is honored by all who loved her.
