
        KORDUBA-Zdan, MD. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Zdan Korduba, MD. An attending anesthesiologist at St. Luke's for over a quarter century, Dr. Korduba upheld our finest traditions of medical care and clinical excellence. He was devoted to his patients and colleagues, and well loved for his charisma and humor. He will be sorely missed. To his wife Oxsana, his children Tirsa and Zdan, and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Sigurd H. Ackerman, MD President and CEO Carlton Boxhill, MD President of the Medical Board Daniel M. Thys, MD Chairman, Department of Anesthesiology
