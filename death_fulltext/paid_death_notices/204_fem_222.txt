
        STAHL--Barbara Jaffe. Internationally renowned paleoichthyologist and a member of the (Manchester, NH) Saint Anselm College biology department for 50 years, died January 16 in Manchester. In addition to her husband of 52 years, Dr. David G. Stahl, her survivors include three daughters, Susan (Richard) Hardy, Nancy (Roy) Wilsker, Sarah (Harry Foster) Stahl, all of MA; a son, Dr. John S. (Elisabeth) Stahl of OH, 10 grandchildren, a sister, Carolyn Friedman of CT, two nieces, two nephews. Services were held Sunday in Manchester, NH.
