
        BERKOWITZ-Beatrice. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Beatrice Berkowitz, beloved mother of David P. Berkowitz. David and his wife, Nancy, are deeply committed to UJA-Federation's Solelim Fund. David is a dedicated leader of our Wall Street Division and the President of the Jewish Community House of Bensonhurst, a beneficiary of UJA-Federation, and Nancy is a leader in our Families Division. We extend our deepest sympathies to David, Nancy, Sarah, and Jonathan; to Mrs. Berkowitz's husband, Bernard; to her sons Alan and Steven; to all her grandchildren; and to her entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
