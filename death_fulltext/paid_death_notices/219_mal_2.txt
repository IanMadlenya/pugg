
        GOLD--Nat, 84, formerly of New York City, died peacefully March 26 in Las Vegas, NM, chosen home of Nat and Edith, his late beloved wife. Head of Arts Council, founder of theater group, creative, progressive, gentle prince. Deeply loved by his children Heloise & Andrew and all who knew him. Donations to the Nat Gold Players, 1212 6th St, Las Vegas, NM 87701.
