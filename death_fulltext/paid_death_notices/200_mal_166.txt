
        PRICE--Raymond K. Retired partner of the former Wall Street firm Adams and Peck. Died peacefully September 12, at age 95, after a long illness. Since retiring, he had lived in Roseboom, NY. Survived by a son, Raymond K. Price Jr. of NYC, the last Editorial Page Editor of the New York Herald Tribune and then principal writer for President Nixon; a daughter, Beth Brown, of Roseboom, three grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren.
