
        NEMEROV--Arthur. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for The Aged at Riverdale deeply mourn the passing of Arthur Nemerov, beloved husband of Shirley Nemerov, father of our friends Wendy Maurer and Jeffrey Nemerov, and fatherin-law of The Hebrew Home's esteemed Chairman, Jeffrey S. Maurer. We will miss Arthur's warmth and good humor. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his children and grandchildren and hope they will be comforted by fond memories. The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale Daniel Reingold, President and CEO
