
        SCHNEIDER-Mollie. Died on December 14. She was the beloved wife of the late Harold D. Schneider and a retired school teacher. She is survived by two nephews, Paul and Edward, two grand nieces, two grand nephews, one great-grand niece, five greatgrand nephews and many friends. She was a great lady and will be missed by all. Services Thursday 12pm at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Visiting hours Wednesday 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Interment at Mt. Eden Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
