
        HUDSON--Richard. On April 16, 2006. Founder and Executive Director of the Center for War/Peace Studies. Originator of the Binding Triad system of global governance, which would make United Nations General Assembly decision-making acceptable to the world's diverse array of nations--large and small, rich and poor. He devoted most of his life to working for a world of peace with justice. His wife Helen, his sister Joan, his daughters Lucinda and Anne, his son-in-law Tyler, his grandchildren Linnea and Thomas will all hold him in their hearts forever.
