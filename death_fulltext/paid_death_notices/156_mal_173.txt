
        LOPEZ-Joseph Patrick, age 51. Beloved husband & father died early Sunday morning after a lifetime of service to the Roman Catholic Church. He was a principal of St. Rose of Lima School in Washington Heights, where he worked for 28 years as a teacher, and a principal since 1984. Joseph is survived by his wife of 25 years, Bernadette Carroll Lopez, who is a principal of St. Anthony School in Manhattan, & their three children, Jacquelyn, a junior at Dominican Academy in Manhattan, Christopher, a sophomore at St. Thomas Aquinas College in Sparkill, New York, and Kathryn, an editor at the ''National Review'' magazine. Funeral Mass will be celebrated on Thursday, 10 A.M., at the Our Lady of Sacred Heart Church in Tappan, NY with burial to follow at St. Anthony's Cemetery in Nanuet, NY. Visiting hours will be Tuesday, 7-9 P.M., & Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 P.M., at the Moritz Funeral Home, 98 Route 303 South, Tappan, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to the St. Rose of Lima School, 517 West 164th Street, New York, NY 10032.
