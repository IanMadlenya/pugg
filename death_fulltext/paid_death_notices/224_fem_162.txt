
        FOULKS--Evelyn (Lyn) September 19, 2005. 84. Beloved sister and aunt. Alfred University, Cleveland School of Art, Traphagen School of Fashion. Well known jewelry designer. Preceded in death by her husband Horace ''Bud'' Foulks. In addition to brothers Arthur and Donald Collins, Mrs. Foulks is survived by 12 nieces and nephews, numerous great nieces and nephews, many friends and her precious poodle BoBo. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, NYC, Wednesday September 28 from 2:005:00 P.M. and 7:00-8:00 P.M. Service at Frank E. Campbell at 8:00 P.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the North Shore Animal League.
