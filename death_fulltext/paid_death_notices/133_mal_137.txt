
        KOHLMANN-Paul A. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Ursula Kohlmann on the loss of her beloved husband, Paul. As Founders of the Hospital, the Kohlmanns played a vital role in providing for the health care needs of the people of Jerusalem and Israel. May the family have warm and loving memories of their husband, father and grandfather. May they be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Mrs. Erica Jesselson, Chairperson of the Board Menno Ratzker, President Lee Weinbach, President National Women's Division Sidney Adler, Treasurer Dr. Stuart Tauber, Exec.
