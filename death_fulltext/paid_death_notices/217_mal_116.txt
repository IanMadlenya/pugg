
        KANENSON--William L., M.D. Of Harrisburg, PA. died on Sunday, February 6 at his home. Beloved husband of Marilynn (Roth), dearest father of Marcy, Ross and his wife Marion. A graduate of Mercersburg Academy, Lehigh University, Jefferson Medical College, he interned and did his residency at Philadelphia General Hospital. A memorial service was held at Temple Ohev Sholom, Harrisburg, followed by interment at Mt. Moriah Cemetery on Tuesday, February 8.
