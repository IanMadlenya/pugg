
        WEINSTEIN-Helen, 84. On January 8, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Devoted mother of Carolyne Malkin and Robert Winston, mother-in-law of Milton Malkin. Graveside service on Thursday 1 PM at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont, NY. Donations may be made to Temple B'nai Sholom, 100 Hempstead Ave., Rockville Centre, NY 11570.
