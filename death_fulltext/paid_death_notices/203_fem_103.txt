
        FINN--Anne Brine, 75, of Sharon, CT; formerly of Ardsleyon-Hudson, NY died December 17th of Multiple Myeloma. She leaves her beloved husband of 53 years, Robert Macy Finn, eight children: Robert Jr., Maggie Gray, Anne Nelson, Christopher, Mary Wood, Timothy, Kathleen Carr, Nicholas and seventeen grandchildren. Everyone she met was touched by her quiet grace. Memorial service December 20th at 11:30 AM St. Mary's Church, Lakeville, CT.
