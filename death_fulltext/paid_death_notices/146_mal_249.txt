
        CONZE-Peter and Elizabeth. The Board of Trustees of Kent School wish to express their profound sorrow over the deaths of Peter Horst Conze '38 and his wife Elizabeth Powers Conze. Peter served and Chancellor of Kent School following 21 years as President of the Board of Trustees. The Board, the Headmaster and Faculty, and the entire Kent School community extend their sincerest condolences to their three Kent children Peter H. Conze Jr. '61, Frederick B. Conze '62 and Elizabeth B. Conze '68 and the Conzes' ten grandchildren.
