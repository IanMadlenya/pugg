
        MAROTTA--Jill M., 52. Passed away peacefully at home on March 4, 2006. Survived by her husband Paul, son Paul Laurence (Dawn), grandsons Donovan and Dylan, brothers Jan (Vicki) and Terry (Della) and sister Gretchen (George), who love her dearly, she will be missed every day as a person who could provide light on the darkest of days. She was loved and will be missed by her nieces and nephews: Selene, Matthew, Max, Lauren, Rachel, Alexandra, Jack, Jesse, Sarah, Katie, Joshua and Zachary. She was the loving godmother of Victoria and Leonardo. A resident of Port Chester, NY, Ms. Marotta (nee Magnuson) was born in Ishpeming, MI to John F. Magnuson and Joyce Olds. She grew up in Arcadia, CA. and Cleveland, OH. where she began her career as a professional dancer. She moved to NYC in 1980 and danced with a number of dance companies for 20 years. After retiring from dance because of an injury, she achieved her Bachelor's degree in history from Hunter College, graduating Summa Cum Laude, in 2001. A Memorial Service celebrating her life will be held on Sunday, March 12 at 10AM at the James Gunipero Funeral Home, 353 Westchester Ave, Port Chester, NY 10573. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the Harkness Center For Dance Injuries, part of NYU Medical Center.
