
        MOSKOWITZ-Dr. Herbert. Of Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of East Hills, New York on December 13. He was a cultured, gentle man known for his wry wit and generosity. Son of the late Abraham and Ida Moskowitz, brother of the late Eleanore Glauberman. Survived by wife Doris Cook Moskowitz, beloved sister and brothers-in-law, Rose and Jack Karp & Harold Glauberman, nephews and nieces Richard Reed, Dr. Marvin Karp, Barbara & Frank Jaccarino, Lesley Glauberman, & dear grandnieces Stacey and Courtney Karp.
