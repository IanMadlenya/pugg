
        EHRLICH - Cantor Moshe. Cantorial Council of America and Philip and Sarah Belz School of Jewish Music of Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, affiliate of Yeshiva University, mourn the passing of Cantor Moshe Ehrlich. Honorary president of CCA and Belz graduate, he was a dynamic and highly respected leader in the Orthodox cantorate for over fifty years. He will be sorely missed. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the entire family. Cantor Leon Kahn President, CCA Cantor Bernard Beer Director, Belz School
        EHRLICH - Moshe. The Board of Trustees of the Hebrew Academy of Five Towns and Rockaway mourn the sudden passing in Israel of our esteemed past Board of Education Chairman and lifelong supporter. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Alan Hirmes, President David Zahner, Chairman of the Board
