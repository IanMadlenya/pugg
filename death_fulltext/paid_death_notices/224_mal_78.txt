
        FARBMAN--Jerry, on September 3 at age 91. Preceded in death by his beloved wife Ruth. Devoted father of Caroline (Richard) Norman, Peg (Dick) Wachowski and Ben (Sue) Farbman. Adored grandfather of Judith, Sara, Katie, Beth and Jeffrey. Great-grandfather of Benjamin. Loving brother of Pearl Selter, Rosalind Markson and Estelle Klein. Jerry will be remembered for his generous spirit, sense of humor and his devotion to his family. His was a life well lived.
