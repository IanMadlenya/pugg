
        JANKOFF-Edith R. Loving wife of the late Bernard R. Jankoff. Beloved mother of Susan & Stephen Beatus, John and Ronnie Jankoff, & Judy Jankoff and Mel Herman. Devoted sister of Honey R. Beldoch. Adored grandmother of Stacey, Jennifer and Stuart. Passed away on July 30th at her home. She will be missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, New York City, Sunday, August 2nd, at 11 A.M.
        JANKOFF-Edith. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her children Susan Beatus, John Jankoff, Judith Jankoff and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
