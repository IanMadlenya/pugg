
        PAOLOZZI - Countess Alicia Spaulding, died after a long illness peacefully at home. She was 84. She is survived by her daughter Francesca; five grandsons: Alexander, Christopher, Anthony, Marco and Andy; and by her two great-grandsons Jack and Devin. She had a many splendored life and many interests: world traveler, race car driver, patron of the arts, activist for women's causes and rights. She co-founded the Spoleto Festival in Italy and in Charleston. Her unique energy and curiosity will be very much missed by friends and family. A service in celebration of her life will be held in the near future.
