
        MERESMAN-Harry. Interim president Lois S. Cronholm and the Trustees of the Baruch College Fund record with sorrow the death of Harry Meresman BBA '32. A loyal and diligent friend of the College, Mr. Meresman served on the BCF Board since 1971. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Lois S. Cronholm Interim President Baruch College
