
        SENVILLE-Melvin (Mel). Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Lawrence, LI. Passed away April 15, 2002. Beloved husband of Beverly. Loving father of Robert (Patricia) and Wayne (Lila) Senville. Cherished grandfather of Rebecca, Joanna, Jacob, and Amelia. Respected builder and developer, responsible for construction of Carnegie House and several projects in Manhattan and on LI. He will be missed by many. Memorial contributions may be made to The Jewish Federation of Palm Beach County, attention - Israel Emergency Fund, 4601 Community Drive, West Palm Beach, FL 33417. For further information, call ''Gutterman Warheit Memorial Chapel'', toll free 800-992-9262.
