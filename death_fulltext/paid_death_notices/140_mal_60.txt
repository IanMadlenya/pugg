
        EDWARDS-Sidney G. 91, of Bronxville, NY, died September 4, 1998. He is survived by his beloved wife of 62 years, Juanita; his daughters, Melinda, Pamela Allara, and Roxanne; his son-in-law, Douglas Pirnie; his grandchildren, Mark and Ann Marie Allara, and his great-grandsons, Michael and Daniel Edwards Allara; and his sister, Mabel Barker of Oswego, KS. Born in Oswego, KS, Mr. Edwards graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Kansas University and received his LL.B. from Yale University Law School. He joined the New York firm now known as Debevoise, Plimpton in 1935 and retired as a senior partner in 1971. The funeral service will take place at the Scarsdale Congregational Church on Wednesday, September 9 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the United Way of Scarsdale-Edgemont or the Scarsdale Congregational Church.
