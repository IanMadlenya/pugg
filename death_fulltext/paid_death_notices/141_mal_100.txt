
        WILLIAMSON-Bruce, age 71. On Oct. 6, 1998. ''The Noel Coward of Film Critics'', Playboy magazines movie critic for thirty-one years. Living on in his daughter Stephanie, son-in-law Joseph, grandsons Henry & Ethan; his sisters Alberta and Janette and many nieces and nephews. Forever loved by his partner Shirley. Private memorial Saturday, October 10.
