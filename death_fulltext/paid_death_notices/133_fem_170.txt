
        REINISCH-Marjorie Bronne. At the age of 82. On February 20, in her sleep at home. She was a devoted wife and wonderful mother who will be greatly missed by all her friends. She leaves a loving daughter, Carol Reinisch, and sonin-law, Robert Suitor. She was the light of many lives. Burial services will be private in Glens Falls, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Audobon Society or the Girl Scouts of America.
