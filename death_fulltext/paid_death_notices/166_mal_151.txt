
        GRAY-Herbert K. Age 72, of Stuart, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY and Old Greenwich, CT, on November 12. Retired as a Vice President after a 42 year career with Pfizer, Inc. Served his country in the Army during the Korean War. Former member of the Union League Club and the Riverside Yacht Club. Survived by his wife of 44 years, Joan T. Gray of Stuart, FL, son, Stuart K. Gray of Stamford, CT, daughter, Nancy G. Vibert of Springfield, VA, four grandchildren, and sister, Mrs. John W. Faison of Brooklyn, NY. Those who wish may make contributions to the Council on Aging of Martin County, 1071 South East 10th Street, Stuart, FL 34996.
