
        BIPPART--Charles H., MD, ''Ace'' 79, respected physician and loving father, died in his home in Norwich, Vermont on Tuesday August 24th. Formerly of Morristown, NJ, husband to the late Eve P. Bippart and Obstetrician at Morristown Memorial Hospital. He is survived by his brother, John, his three children, Peter E. Bippart, MD, Lisa B. Ghriskey, Victoria I. Bippart, and his eight grandchildren. Services will be held Saturday September 11th, 11:00 am at the First Presbyterian Church, New Vernon, NJ.
