
        SOUTHERLAND -- Arnold. The Trustees, Clergy and staff of The Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine mourn the untimely death of Arnold Southerland, longtime Cathedral Security Director. Half of his life was spent at The Cathedral, and he was a treasured member of our family. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Louvenia, his children and the family. Public viewing 9-10AM, funeral service is on Wednesday, December 17, 10:30AM, at The Cathedral. The Rt. Rev. Mark S. Sisk, Episcopal Bishop of NY The Very Rev. Dr. James A. Kowalski, Dean Henry L. King, Esq., Pres.
