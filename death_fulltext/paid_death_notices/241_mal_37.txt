
        AGAY--Denes, Ph.D., composer, music editor, and internationally - recognized pro ponent for quality piano education, died on January 24 at age 95. The author of 96 books was born just outside Budapest, Hungary and emigrated to the US in 1939. Beloved husband to the late Mary Roberts Agay, Dr. Agay is survived by his daughter Susan Agay Rothschild, son-in-law Charlie Rothschild and three grandsons.
