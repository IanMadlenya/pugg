
        SPERO-Mildred W. On January 8, in her 99th year. Beloved wife of the late Carl; devoted mother of Michael and his wife, Joan; dear grandmother of Jason and Ben; revered aunt and great-aunt. Wise and compassionate advisor to family, friends and the many she served through social work organizations. Contributions may be made to Jewish Child Care Association, 575 Lexington Ave, NYC. Private family memorial service.
