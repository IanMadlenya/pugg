
        WANDERER-Bruno. 94. Of New York City died Saturday, Nov. 7, 1998. Beloved father of Susan W. Uram and proud grandfather of Alison and Michael Uram. He is also survived by his sister, Jetty Richter and his brother, Max Wanderer. Incredibly devoted to his family and to The West Side Institutional Synagogue, he will be missed dearly. Contributions may be sent to the Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale, NY.
