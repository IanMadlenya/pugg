
        SEGAL KAMEN-Edith. Beloved wife of the late Samuel Kamen. Dear sister of Tess Segel. Cherished sister-in-law of Judith and the late Manasseh Kamen and Fay and the late Ephraim Kamen; loving aunt of Barbara Kamen Movius and Geoffrey Movius; Mitchell and Helene Kamen; Richard Kamen; Michael and Sandra Kamen; and Shari and Peter Goldberg. Poetess, dancer and humanitarian, she will be remembered by all who knew her and were touched by her kindness and creativity.
        SEGAL KAMEN-Edith. Camp Kinderland's Board of Directors and generations of campers and staff mourn the passing of our magnificent poet, dancer, teacher, friend and fighter for social justice.
