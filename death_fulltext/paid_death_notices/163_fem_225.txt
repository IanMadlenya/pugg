
        SHAPIRO-Sylvia. Loving wife of the late Aaron Shapiro and mother of Ted Shapiro, Roberta (Shapiro) Dubner, and Judi (Shapiro) Solomito, passed away the evening of August 28, 2000, at the age of 90. A woman of quiet grace and dignity, she was a devoted wife, mother and grandmother, whose strength and independence will stand as a legacy to her devoted and loving family. She is survived by her sister, Jeanne Vogel, her brother, Nathan Vogel, son, Ted and his wife, Andree, daughter, Roberta, and her husband, Mel, and their two children, Aaron and Adam, and his wife, Kim, daughter, Judi, and her husband, Joe, and their two children, Katie, and Daniel. She will be greatly missed and fondly remembered with love always. Never goodbye. So long, our teacher, our friend, our confidant. The Shapiro Family
