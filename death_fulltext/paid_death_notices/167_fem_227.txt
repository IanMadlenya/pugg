
        STUHLER-Patricia Cumming. Of Point Pleasant, N.J., died December 20, 2000, at the age of 81. She was born in Westfield, N.J., on April 20, 1919. She was a nationally ranked tennis player (beginning in state championships at age 10) in a career that spanned 71 years. She won the girls 18 & under national doubles championships in both 1935 & 1937. She competed in the U.S. Nationals (now known as the U.S. Open) at Forest Hills for 17 years straight.
