
        LEVY-Hyman, M.D., 92. Died at home. Beloved husband of Muriel. Loving father of Lauren (Brian Gilmour) and Jerrold. Grandfather of Theowen, Susanah & Edward, brother of Phillip Levy of Rochester. Beloved uncle of many nieces & nephews. Violinist & practicing physician in cardiology at Mt. Sinai for 50 years. Service at Frank E. Campbell on Thursday, 12:45 P.M., followed by burial at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery. In lieu of flowers please donate to Temple Israel of the City of NY, 112 East 75th Street.
        LEVY-Hyman, M.D. It is with great sorrow that the Mount Sinai Alumni mourns the passing of our highly esteemed and beloved colleague. Dr. Hyman joined the staff in 1931, and for almost 60 years distinguished himself as a cardiologist, scientist and teacher. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Wayne S. Fuchs, M.D., President Avi Barbasch, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary William G. Stein, Treasurer
