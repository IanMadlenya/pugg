
        FRANKEL--Jesse, 93 years, passed away on November 21. He is survived by his loving wife of 65 years, Eugenia Eagle. Beloved father of Midge and Howard, and James and Jill. Adored grandfather to Brad and Peter, Craig and Jay, Katy, Sara, and Alex. Great-grandfather to Avery. He created an enduring legacy of a loving family and a cadre of friends and business acquaintances while living a life of integrity to it's fullest. Jesse walked through life simply while meaning so much to so many. He will be missed and remembered.
