
        GALIMIR-Suzanne. On May 24, 1998. Beloved wife of Felix. Loving sister of Martin Hirsch, Margaret Hirsh and the late Stephen Hirsch. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Services on Thursday, at 1 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        GALIMIR-Suzanne. The musicians of 29 years of the New York String Orchestra Seminar and her fellow members of the Schneider Concerts Advisory Committee are deeply saddened by the loss of their generous friend and extend heartfelt sympathy to her family. For hundreds of seminar participants, Suzanne's annual Christmas dinner was a special occasion always to be remembered and an example of the hospitality and generosity she extended to so many.
