
        PENNEY-Julia, 91 in Stamford, CT January 7th. Beloved wife of the late Horace F. Penney, loving mother of Marcia and Richard, proud grandmother of John, Paul, and Peter, and delighted great-grandmother of Scarlett, Eloise, and Celeste. A founding member of The Greenwich Art Society, where she also taught painting, and a member of the Silvermine Guild of Artists and many other arts organizations. We all will cherish her love, her spirit and her art.
