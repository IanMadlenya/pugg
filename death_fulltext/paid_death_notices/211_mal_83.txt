
        DANE--Maxwell. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Maxwell ''Mac'' Dane, who was deeply devoted to the Jewish community, humanitarian causes, and cultural arts initiatives. Through his esteemed leadership as a Life Trustee and member of the Board of Directors of UJA-Federation, he strengthened our ability to serve those in need, making a profound impact through his generosity, warmth, and commitment to klal Yisrael. We extend our deepest sympathies to his beloved wife Esther; his son, Henry; and the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O
