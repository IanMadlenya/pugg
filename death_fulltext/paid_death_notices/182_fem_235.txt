
        OLSSON-Olivia A. March 22, 2002. Beloved sister of Agnes and Carl Olsson, devoted sister-in-law of Catherine Olsson, cherished aunt of Karl, Kathleen, Eric, Kristine and Mark, and several greatnieces and nephews. She served in the United States Navy during WWII. Later she became the president of A.E. Johnson Employment Agency of New York City. An active member of the Salmagundi Club, American Scandinavian Foundation and the American Scandinavian Society. Her work as a sculptor has been shown in New York City. A very proud and dedicated member of Park Avenue Methodist Church for almost 50 years. Services were held at Frank E. Campbell on Saturday, March 23, 2002. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice. A memorial service will be held at a later date at the Park Avenue Methodist Church.
