
        MINTZ-June (Mirken), of Manhattan died in Atlanta, GA, March 26, 2000. She retired as editor of The Bulletin of the Hospital for Joint Diseases, Orthopedic Institute in 1992 and had worked in editorial positions on several other medical journals. Earlier she was an associate editor of Parents' Magazine, fiction editor at Charm and an editor at Seventeen. Surviving are her son Dr. Eric Mintz, daughterin-law Dr. Maria Ribeiro and grandchildren Andre and Nicole Mintz of Atlanta; her daughter Elizabeth Mintz Nickens and son-inlaw John Nickens of Oakland, CA; her brother Dr. Allan S. Mirken of Mission Viejo, CA; sister Vivian Mirken Carlip and brother-in-law Alfred B. Carlip of Vestal, New York; and other beloved friends and relatives.
