
        YORK-Herman II. On January 11, 1999, of Upper Brookville, New York. Beloved husband for 53 years of Loretta H. Dear father of Kathleen A., and Richard C. (Stephanie). He was retired from Architectural Practice as a specialist in housing design; over 3/4 million homes have been built from York blueprints. In 1981 Mr. York was inducted into the National Housing Hall of Fame in Washington, D.C. He was a member of the American Institute of Architects, the First Presbyterian Church of Oyster Bay, and a member emeritus of the Greater New York Advisory board of the Salvation Army of which he was an active volunteer for 43 years. He served as a director emeritus of Jamaica Savings Bank. Visiting at the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home Inc., 293 South Street Oyster Bay, NY Tuesday 35, 7-9 P.M. A memorial service will be held at the Long Island Lutheran High School Chapel, Brookville, NY on Saturday January 16, 1999 at 1 P.M. Interment Private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Long Island Lutheran High School, the Salvation Army, or a charity of your choice.
