
        PAFFARD-Frederic Chauncey. Longtime resident of Sewickley, PA, Stonington, CT., and Mountain Lake, FL., died at his family home on September 23. Born on May 7, 1915 in Brooklyn, NY, son of the late Dr. Frederic C. Paffard and Helen Adams Paffard of Stonington. Survived by his wife of 58 years, Edith Wynne Rizer and four daughters: Wynne Delmhorst of Riverside, CT.; Jane Nichols of Boston, MA.; Heidi Simmons of Stonington, CT, and Helen Wertheimer of Everett, WA and 9 grandchildren. He was predeceased by 2 sisters, Elizabeth Anderson of Noank, CT and Jane Hamill of Stonington, CT. A memorial service will be held at 11:30AM at Calvary Episcopal Church on Church Street in Stonington. Interment will be private. Memorial contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to the Stonington Free Library, PO Box 188, Stonington, CT 06378 and Hospice of Southeastern CT, PO Box 902, Uncasville, CT 06382.
