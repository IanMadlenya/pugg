
        ROBSON-Donald Arthur. Tragically lost at the World Trade Center. Husband and loving partner of Kathy. Loving, devoted and supportive father of Geoffrey and Scott. Dearest son of Joan Robson and the late Charles J. Robson. Beloved and cherished brother of John (Esther) and Nancy (Richard) Hamm, fun-loving uncle to Michael, Stephen, Jennifer, Carolyn, Peter, Patrick and Katherine, dear brother-in-law of Bob Kreek and his wife Sherry and son-in-law of MIL. Funeral Mass to be held at St. Mary's Roman Catholic Church in Manhasset, N.Y. on Saturday, September 15, 2001 at 2:30PM.
