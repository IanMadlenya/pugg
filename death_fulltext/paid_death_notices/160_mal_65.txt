
        FERNANDEZ-Nestor N., 68, of Bluffton, SC died Saturday, April 30, 2000 at his home. He was born on January 22, 1932 in Buenos Aires, Argentina and was the son of the late Juan Fernandez and Pelegrina Mendez. He moved to Bluffton in January 2000 from West New York, NJ. He was a linotype operator for 35 years with the New York Times. He is survived by his wife Margarita Fernandez; a daughter Patricia Orlando of Bluffton; a son John Fernandez of Carteret, NJ and four grandchildren Melissa Fernandez, Jennifer Fernandez, Jonathan Fernandez and Meridian Orlando. A memorial mass was held at St. Francis Catholic Church, Hilton Head Island SC. For more information contact The Island Funeral Home, Hilton Head Island S.C.
        FERNANDEZ-Nestor N. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Nestor N. Fernandez, associated with The Times from 1972 until retiring in 1996.
