
        BAZELL-Honorable Michael P., passed away peacefully in San Francisco at UCSF on August 19th, 2001. Survived by his wife Jeannine, his children Jacelyn, Andrew, Frederika, Paul, Diana, Julie, Mario and Janine; his grandchildren Danielle, Jillian, Vanessa, Aimee and Matthew. He was dedicated to the legal profession as an attorney for fifty years and as an Administrative Law Judge for the Office of Hearings and Appeals in Los Angeles and Oakland. This special man will be greatly missed by those whose lives he touched. In memory of Judge Bazell, contributions may be made to the UCSF Foundation, Attention: Cardiac Care Unit, 44 Montgomery St., Suite 2200, San Francisco, CA 94104. Private services will be held.
