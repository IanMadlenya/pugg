
        TOWER-Elizabeth H. (nee Hasemeier), age 78, died Tuesday, April 15, 2003. Before retiring she was employed by North Shore Medical Group in Huntington, N.Y., as a Medical Lab Administrator. She is survived by her son, John (Janet) of Yardley, PA, and granddaughter, Katie. Services were held at the Christ Episcopal Church in Bensalem, PA. Interment in Richmond, Indiana.
