
        LYNCH-Luke Daniel, Sr. Died peacefully after a long illness on November 27, 1999 in Naples, Fla. Beloved husband of Marjorie for 55 years. Devoted father of Luke Jr. (Nancy), Dorsey (Joyce), and Dan (Cornelia) Lynch and Carol Manire (Michael). Big Chief to 11 grandchildren. Brother of James. Mr. Lynch was born in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn on August 2, 1921. He graduated from Regis High School in N.Y.C. (1939), attended Fordham University and graduated in 1944 from the U.S. Naval Academy (Class of 1945). While at the Naval Academy, he was class vice-president, played varsity basketball and baseball, was a company commander and a five-striper. He served in the Pacific Theater on the U.S.S. Alabama, which participated in the Japanese surrender at Tokyo Bay. After the war, he graduated from Columbia Law School (1949), and went on to a career specializing in insurance and reinsurance law. Mr. Lynch was a former senior partner of Mendes & Mount as well as LeBouef, Lamb & Leiby, where he represented Lloyds of London. At the time of his death, Mr. Lynch was a senior partner of the law firm of D'Amato & Lynch in N.Y.C. and a Director of Queens County Savings Bank. He was a former president of the Cherry Valley Club in Garden City, NY. The family will receive visitors Thursday, December 2, from 7-9, at Fairchild Sons Funeral Home, 1201 Franklin Ave, Garden City. A memorial service will be held on Friday, December 3 at 11AM, St. Joseph's Church, Fifth Street and Franklin Ave, Garden City. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the U.S. Naval Academy Alumni Association-Class of 1945 Fund, U.S. Naval Academy, Annapolis, MD.
