
        NOONAN--John F. Passed May 17, 2006. Educated at St. Mary's High School, Manhasset, NY, Fordham University, and Duke University Graduate School. He worked for firms from First Boston to the World Bank. His last position was as a consultant for USAID in Cairo, Egypt for the Ministry of Finance for Egypt. John's charming wit and powerful intellect will be missed. Son of John and Jayne (nee Thompkins). Father of Kathryn Glaser, Heather, and Michael. Grandfather of two. Brother of Thomas Noonan and Jayne Donahue. Memorial service: St. Peters Church, Port Washington, New York, Friday, June 16, 2006, 7:30 PM.
