
        LONDON--Burton Lawrence. On Saturday evening, August 30, 2003, in New York City. Loving and cherished husband of Miriam (Manya), loving and caring father of Gabrielle London Palitz and Lizabeth (Tiza) London. Devoted father-in-law of Michael Palitz and Scott Leventhal, adoring grandfather of Nora and Sophie Palitz and Toby and Jamie Leventhal. Dear brother of Marcia London, and the late Sherley Newman, devoted brother-in-law of Philip Newman. A man of deep integrity and gentle professorial demeanor, his compassion, generosity, sense of humor and strong commitment to his beliefs was felt by all those who knew him, he will be truly missed. Services will be held Tuesday, September 2, at 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
        LONDON--Burton L. The Officers, Trustees, Members and Clergy mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife, Miriam, and the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln Senior Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft President
