
        STRUHS-Thomas Haser, 52. On September 16. A graduate of the Harvard School of Design, he last worked for Kajima International. He is survived by his brothers, Edward and Joseph of Dixon, IL. and James of Charleston, S.C. and by his companion of over 20 years. Frank Oveis. A Memorial Service will announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers contributions to the American Foundation for Aids Research would be appreciated.
