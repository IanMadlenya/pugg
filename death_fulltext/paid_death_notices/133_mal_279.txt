
        HARM-Carl S. On Feb. 17, 1998. A leader in the development of Community Boards for Mental Health Centers and a champion of Tenants Rights through his work with Park West Village Tenants Association and related groups. He is survived by his wife of 35 years, Mary Gay Harm; a son, Ronald Carl of Ashland, Oregon; one sister, Margaret Wasem; two grandchildren, nieces and nephews. Memorial service Saturday, Feb. 21, 11AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St.
