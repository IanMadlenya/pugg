
        HOWITT--Nathan G., D.D.S., at the age of 96, passed away peacefully on April 3, 2007, at his home in Pompano Beach Florida. He was a distinguished Oral Surgeon and former president of the Bronx County Dental Society and former director of the Bronx Lebanon Dental Clinic. He will be sorely missed by his beloved wife Florence, his two loving daughters, Valerie and Karen, his three granddaughters and five greatgrandchildren.
