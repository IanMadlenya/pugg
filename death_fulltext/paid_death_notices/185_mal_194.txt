
        WHITMAN-Allen E., III. Died on Wednesday, June 12, 2002, at home in Berwyn, PA. Born in New York City, New York, June 27, 1934. Husband of Tracy (Horter) Whitman. Father of Cynthia W. Lawes, Elizabeth W. Winder, Allen E. Whitman IV, and Eavenson S. Horter. Also survived by his brothers Johnston de F. Whitman, Christopher Whitman and Benjamin T. Whitman, a sister Helen Millar, uncle Townsend Brown II, his stepmother Elizabeth T. Whitman, several grandchildren, nieces and nephews. Memorial service Saint David's Church, 763 Valley Forge Road, Wayne, PA. 12:30 PM, Saturday, June 22.
