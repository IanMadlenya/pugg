
        HARTLEY - Margaret Gilman. The wife of William A. Hartley departed this life on Tuesday, December 18, 2001. She will be missed by many, but the love we all have for her will stay in our hearts forever. God rest her soul. She is survived by her husband William A. Hartley, three sisters, Barbara, Kathleen and Lucia, two brothers, Christopher and Stephen, ten grandchildren, eleven cousins, ten nieces and nephews and many close friends.
