
        GREENBERG--Robert. The Monroe College community notes with profound sadness the untimely passing of Robert Greenberg husband of Roberta Greenberg, Vice President of the college. Bob was a very real and special part of Monroe for more than 28 years. He had a love for the success of students and was a great booster for all events at the college. We extend our sincere condolences to Roberta, their daughters Shari Gold and Bonnie Stern, their sons-in-law Dr. Jay Gold and Scott Stern and their five grandchildren. Stephen J. Jerome, President
