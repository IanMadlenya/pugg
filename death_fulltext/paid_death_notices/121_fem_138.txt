
        GOLDBLATT-Frieda Bialkin, died peacefully on February 18, 1997, just before her ninety-first birthday. Wife of the late Morris, mother of Jerome and Zita Rosenthal, sister of Miriam, Nettie, Bernard and Albert, grandmother of Ellen, James and Elizabeth. Her lively sense of humor and wonderful smile will be missed by all who knew her. Funeral service and interment was held on Friday, February 21 for family members only. Visitation Sunday, 3-6 P.M., at Will-O-Woods; Monday 5-8 P.M., Manhattan.
