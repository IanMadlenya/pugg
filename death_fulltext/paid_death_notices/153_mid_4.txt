
        PECK-Jane Cochran Coleman, 89, a fourth generation Houstonian, passed away peacefully, surrounded by her family and loved ones on Friday, October 15, 1999. In 1930, Jane married Stewart P. Coleman of Corpus Christi, Texas and moved to New York City for the duration of his employment with the Standard Oil Company of New Jersey. He died in 1969. Jane was married to Arthur K. Peck, a principal in the firm Rob, Peck and McCooey, in 1973, until his death in 1990. She was an active member of various charities and organizations in Houston and New York, an avid gardner, and a devoted wife, mother, grandmother, and greatgrandmother. In New York, Jane was a member of: The Colony Club, The Rockaway Hunting Club, Cedarhurst Yacht Club, Lawrence Beach Club, The Garden Club of Lawrence, The Junior League, and Trinity Episcopal Church in Woodmere. In addition, she was a member of the New York Stock Exchange. Family and friends are invited for visitation from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. on Wednesday, October 20, 1999, at the family home in Houston, Texas. Funeral services will be held 2:00 p.m., Thursday, October 21, 1999, at Palmer Memorial Episcopal Church, 6221 Main Street, Houston, with interment at Glenwood Cemetery.
