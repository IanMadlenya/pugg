
        CORTESE--Armand F. The family of Armand F. Cortese, M.D., mourns the loss of a devoted husband and father. At his death, he was Clinical Associate Professor Emeritus of Surgery at New York Presbyterian/Weill Cornell Medical Center. Family was the focus of his life. His honest, gentle and inquisitive nature was an inspiration to those who knew him. He is survived by his wife Patricia and children, Amanda and Kevin. His memorial service will be held at the Grace Church, Millbrook, NY, at 11 am, Saturday, February 25. Donations may be sent to The Fresh Air Fund (www.freshair.org) or The Clinton Community Library (845-266-5530).
