
        HUNTER--Captain H.D. Died at his home in Mt. Pleasant, SC on Friday morning, March 10th. Although retired from positions of high responsibility and authority as an Officer of various Shipowner Companies, he remained active as a Director of International Cargo Gear Bureau, Inc. He is survived by Effie, his wife of 42 years, three of their children, and five grandchildren. There will be private graveside services for the family in Fort Lauderdale.
