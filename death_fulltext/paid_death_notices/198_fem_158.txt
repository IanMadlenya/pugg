
        HELLER--Red (Rose), widow of Ernest Heller, died July 19, 2003 at the age of 105. She lived a full, rich life. She graduated from the University of Pittsburgh and was trained and worked as a social worker. She served as Director of Camp Swatonah in Damascus, PA from inception in the late 20's until 1950. She is survived by four nieces and nephews, Barnse Keller, Alison Diamond, Lynn Gilbert and David Fine. Throughout her life, she has been a major influence and patron of the arts, supporting numerous proteges. For many years, she served on the Board of Directors of the MacDowell Colony and the Poses Arts Awards Committee for Brandeis University. A memorial service is planned for September 12 at the Harvard Club at 11:30 AM. >>AD#
