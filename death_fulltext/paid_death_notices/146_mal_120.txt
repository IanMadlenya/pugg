
        KAHN-Charles Henri. Died on February 22, 1999 unexpectedly at his Santa Cruz, CA. home. He was 83. Mr. Kahn was an aerospace engineer with a career spanning over 65 years. His passion in life was engineering and innovation. He served in WWII in the French Foreign Legion. He is survived by his wife, Anne Marie Kahn, of Santa Cruz, his son, Philippe Kahn, of Scotts Valley, CA, and his brother, Rudy Carne, of England. He also leaves four grandchildren. A private family graveside service was held February 27 in Santa Cruz.
