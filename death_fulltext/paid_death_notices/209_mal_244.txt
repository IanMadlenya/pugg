
        MARTINI--Nael, M.D. Dr. O. Wayne Isom and the entire Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery notes with sorrow the passing after a long illness of our friend and colleague Nael Martini, M.D. Dr. Martini, the former Chief of Thoracic Services at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, was a caring surgeon who was dedicated to his patients. We express our condolences to his wife Robin, his children Jenny, Suzanne and John and his grandson Elliot. O. Wayne Isom, M.D. Chairman, Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery Weill-Cornell Medical College
