
        BROWN--James. 77, of Wyckoff, NJ, died Saturday, February 18. Born April 4, 1928 in Washington Heights, NY. A graduate of Cardinal Hayes High School and Fordham University. Beloved husband to Mary; cherished father of Patricia, James, Carolyn, Denise and Erin and daughterin-law Suzy; adored Granddad of Brendan and Caitlin. Former Owner and Chairman of the American Desk Manufacturing Company. Prior to that he was Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Kidde, Inc. Past President of the Treasurers Club of New York and a member of the Economic Club of New York. Member of the New York Athletic Club and Ridgewood Country Club. Funeral Mass Thursday at 11:00 AM at St. Elizabeths R.C. Church, 700 Wyckoff Avenue, Wyckoff. Visitation at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home 257 Godwin Avenue, Wyckoff, on Tuesday from 7:00-9:00 PM and on Wednesday from 2:00-4:00 and 7:00-9:00 PM. vanderplaatfuneralhome .com
