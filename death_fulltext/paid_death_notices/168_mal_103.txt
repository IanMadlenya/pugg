
        SCANLAN-Eugene R. Of Somers, NY, died on January 4. Former Vice President and General Manager of the Waldorf Astoria Hotel. Member of the Knights of Malta. Survived by his wife Anne, his son Daniel and his daughter Patricia Loomis. Also survived by four grandchildren. Reposing at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Croton Falls, NY, Tuesday, 10 AM. Interment St. Anthony's Cemetery, Nanuet, NY.
        SCANLAN-Eugene R. The Management and Staff of The Waldorf-Astoria are saddened by the passing of the man who contributed so much to the success of our hotel during his 34 year association with it. Gene Scanlan's encyclopedic knowledge, singular dedication and tireless enthusiasm were an inspiration. He left an indelible mark on our hotel and indeed on the history of the hospitality industry as a whole. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Ann, their children Patrick and Patricia and the Scanlan's four grandchildren.
