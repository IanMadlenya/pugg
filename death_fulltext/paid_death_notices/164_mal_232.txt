
        ROBBINS-Guy, M.D. On Sept. 9, 2000. He was widower of Louise, father of Kirk, Kathy and Richard, grandfather of Holly, Clifford and Vivian. He devoted his medical career to the treatment and cure of breast cancer. A former chief of the Breast Service at Memorial Sloan-Kettering, and consultant at numerous hospitals in the greater metropolitan area. Also active in politics in Westport, Connecticut, where he raised his family. He will be sorely missed by his family, patients, neighbors and all the other people whose lives he touched. Contributions may be made to Memorial Hospital or Northwestern University.
