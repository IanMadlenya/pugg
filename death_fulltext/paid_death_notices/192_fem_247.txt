
        DeJUR-Marion Ellenberg. Peacefully on January 23, in Bal Harbour, FL formerly of New York City, wife of the late Dr. Samuel Ellenberg, late Harry DeJur and late Herb Lowenstein, beloved mother of Dianne Ellenberg Davis and Shepard Ellenberg, beloved grandmother of Hal Scott and S. Lawrence Davis, and Jennifer Ellenberg; great-grandmother of Emma Elizabeth and Alexandre Jerard Davis. Survived by sister Lillian Flaster, of Connecticut, and stepdaughter Lee Lowenstein Kolenberg of Florida. Connie Scott was her devoted caregiver. Her driving force, visionary initiatives and philanthropy, inspired and touched many throughout the world.
