
        AXELRAD-Amelia (Millie). At age 95 on March 16, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Sol Axelrad, M.D., loving aunt of Edith Gelfand, and devoted friend of Frances Grabarz. A long-time resident of Woodhaven, New York, she was active in the Women's Auxiliary of the Queens County Medical Society. Her later years were spent in Highlands, NJ and St Thomas, VI. Her cheerful countenance will always be remembered.
