
        DOVAS--Athanaus S. ''Tom.'' Passed peacefully in his sleep at age 80, on December 13, 2003. Beloved father of Nikki, Steven and Yasmin; beloved husband of Guita; brother of Stacy and Anna; beloved grandfather and uncle. Decorated Naval veteran of the Second World War, successful businessman, founder of Oloff Cosmetics Incorporated, partner in Wm. Pahl Equipment Corporation, engaging raconteur. Fondly remembered in spite of everything. Services at Leber Funeral Home, 2000 Kennedy Boulevard, Union City, NJ, on Monday, 2-4 pm.
