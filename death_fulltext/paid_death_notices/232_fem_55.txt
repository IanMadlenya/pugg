
        SELIGMAN - Frances, 80. May 1 following a brief illness in NYC. Much beloved wife of Melvin Seligman; mother of Karen Sherman and Roberta Seligman; grandmother of Jed & Sara. She had loving friends from coast to coast and her soaring spirit will be greatly missed by all who loved her. Funeral held on May 3 Montrepose, Kingston, New York.
