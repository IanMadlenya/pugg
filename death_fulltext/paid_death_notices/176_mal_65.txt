
        STUDZINSKI-Alfred E. Of Peabody, Sept. 3, 2001, beloved husband of Jennie M. (Gaieski) Studzinski of Peabody, father of Edward A. and Anne Studzinski of Chicago and John J. Studzinski of London, England, son of the late Cezary and Frances (Jaworska) Studzinski, and brother of the late William Studzinski, Stella Studzinski and Regina Falkowski. His funeral will be held on Saturday at 8:15 AM from the Cahill-Brodeur facility at 20 Church St. of the Conway, Cahill-Brodeur Funeral Home, Peabody, followed by a Funeral Service at 9 AM in the St. John's Prep Chapel, 72 Spring St., Danvers, MA to which relatives and friends are kindly invited to attend. A visitation will be held in the funeral home on Thursday from 7 to 9 P.M. and Friday from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Burial will be in St. Mary's Cemetery, Salem. Memorial donations may be made to the Alfred E. Studzinski Memorial Library Fund C/O St. John's Prep, 72 Spring Street, Danvers, MA. 01923.
