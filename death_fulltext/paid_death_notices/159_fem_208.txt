
        SMITH-Joan H. (Jody), age 67. Died Saturday, April 22, 2000 at her home in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida. She was born March 4, 1933 in Yorkshire, England and moved to Ponte Vedra Beach in 1984 from Basking Ridge, N.J. She was a member of the Bishop Janes United Methodist Church of Basking Ridge, N.J., the Ponte Vedra Club, the March Landing Country Club and the Sawgrass Country Club. Family members include her husband, Layton F. Smith; daughters, Pamela (David) Schubert of Clifton, Va., Kathy (Joseph) Gayda of Buffalo Grove, Ill., Kathryn (Thomas) Barako of Gettysburg, Pa. and Julie (Donald) Rietdors of Murfreesboro, Tenn., and ten grandchildren. Her brother, Jay Trevor Huby preceded her in death. Memorial services will be held at 10:30AM Monday May 1, 2000 at the Palms Presbyterian Church with the Reverend Dr. Jack Swan officiating. Memorial contributions may be made to cummunity Hospice of Northeast Florida, 4266 Sunbeam Road, Jacksonville, Fla. 32257. Services will be conducted by Quinn-Shalz, a family Funeral Home, 3rd St South at Ponte Vedra Boulevard, 904-249-1100.
