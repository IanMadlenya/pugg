
        CHASE-Barbara B. (Buttimer). Age 83, of Marblehead, MA, died peacefully Sunday, September 17, 2000 in Salem, MA. Wife of the late Howard W. Burns, and the late John P. Chase. Survived by a son, Howard W. Burns, Jr., and his wife, Karen A. Murdock, of Cedar Grove, N.J., and a daughter, Nancy Mae Hurley, and her husband, Donald Hurley, Jr., of Lloyd Harbor, N.Y., and three grandchildren. Memorial remembrances may be sent to the Dana-Farber Jimmy Fund, 1309 Beacon St., Brookline, MA. 02446, or to the Star of the Sea Church, 85 Atlantic Avenue, Marblehead, MA. 01945.
