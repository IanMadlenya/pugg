
        GOLDBERG-Alan L., M.D. 79 years old. Born in the Bronx, NY, April 22, 1921. Died May 20, 2000 in Delray Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Muriel. Loving father of Judy, Louise and Eve. Much loved grandfather of Phoebe, Megan, Sean, Zach and Simon. Grew up in the Bronx. Graduated Evander Childs High School, Columbia College and New York University School of Medicine. He was a devoted family practitioner in the Bronx for 39 years. Served as President of the New York Academy of Family Practitioners in 1978. Accomplished jazz pianist, gifted storyteller and brilliant humorist, he entertained friends, family and patients. He will be greatly missed by all whose lives he touched.
