
        KORNHAUSER--Kenneth R. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Kenneth R. Kornhauser, a treasured leader whose profound commitment to the Jewish community was evident in his boundless dedication to the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center, the Suffolk Association for Jewish Educational Services, and the Suffolk Y Jewish Community Center. Kenneth's efforts strengthened our ability to serve those in need, making an indelible impact on millions of lives. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Ann; his children: Craig; Evan, and his wife, Lindsay; his father, Martin; his sister, Janet, and her husband, Harvey Fine; his parents-inlaw, Faye and Joseph Morris; and the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.$(6$)
