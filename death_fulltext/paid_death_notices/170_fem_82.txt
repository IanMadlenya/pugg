
        LAW-Joan ''Jan''. 70, of New York City died March 10, 2001, at Noble Horizons in Salisbury, CT. Miss Law was born October 15, 1930, in NYC, a daughter of the late Clyde and Myrtle (Rexroth) Law. Miss Law was a buyer for Sak's Fifth Avenue for over 25 years. Prior to that, she had worked for Macy's. Graveside services will be held Tuesday at 1 PM in Mount Rose Cemetery, York, PA. Memorial donations may be sent to Bide-AWee Animal Shelter, NY, NY.
