
        TAUB-Ruth (Schoenberg). On October 9, 2002. After a courageous battle with cancer. She left this world the way she lived it, with dignity and gentle humor.         TAUB-Ruth. With sorrow we say farewell to our beloved friend Ruth. We will miss her wisdom, compassion and friendship. Our lives have been enriched. Sincere condolences to the Taub family. Maryann and Herb Kaufman
