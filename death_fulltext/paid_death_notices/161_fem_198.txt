
        LIBERMAN-James B. At home on June 22, 2000, at age 84. Beloved husband of Deen Freed Liberman and, after her death, of Nadine C. Zamichow, Father of Lee Liberman Otis and the late John Liberman, Father-in-law of Mary Beth Liberman Traynor and William G. Otis, Grandfather of Harry, Dean, Emma, Bert, Jessie, Zachary, Bernie and Audrey. Cherished Brother of Lee Liberman. A gentleman of exceptional generosity, dignity, modesty and achievement, graduate of Yale Law School and Chairman of its 60th Reunion Class last year, United States Army during World War II, General Counsel of General Public Utilities, Founding partner of Berlack, Israel and Liberman, recently joined Thelen Reid and Priest. Contributions may be made to the Yale Law School or The Federalist Society. Memorial Services at Temple Emanu-El, One East 65th Street, Monday, June 26, at 11 AM.
