
        ROMLEY--Lillian, 93. A resident of Greenwich, CT., and a long time resident of New York City, died April 16, 2006. Born in the Ukraine, she was orphaned by six, walked across Europe with a brother and sister, and emigrated to the U.S. in 1922. She is survived by two sons, Victor and Richard, four grandchildren, Kenneth, David, and Jeffrey Romley, and Diane Powis, and four great grandchildren.
