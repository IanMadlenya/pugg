
        ALLEN-Neil Turner Jr. Age 70, of NYC, passed away on October 26, 2000. Mr. Allen was born on June 28, 1930, in Brownsville, TX. He was preceded in death by his friend of over 30 years, Harold Mattsson. He is survived by his nephew Jack M. Allen Jr., of Albuquerque, NM, and his niece Julie Ann Remboldt of Lake Oswego, OR. A celebration of Mr. Allen's life will be held at the home of Helen Berry, 252 West 30th, on Thursday, November 2, 6-8 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
