
        DAVIDSON-Thomas Charles, Jr. 80. September 8, 2001. Survived by his wife of 50 years Shirley-Helen Vanderveer Davidson, chidren Susan V. Davidson Morrow of Manlius, N.Y., Thomas C. Davidson, III of Bridgehampton, Laura D. Tweedy of Barrington, R.I. and five grandchildren. Born August 19, 1921 in Garden City, N.Y. He attended Choate School in Ct. Graduated from Dartmouth College, '45. Served with U.S. Marine Corps in the Pacific during World War II. A longtime stockbroker/analyst. Private service at Flushing Cemetery. Donations to the Leukemia Society of America, 555 Broadhollow Rd, Melville, N.Y. 11747.
