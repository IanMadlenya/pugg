
        VOGEL-Florence. On September 7, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Max. Loving mother of Bert and Joyce. Adoring grandmother of Peter. A very special lady who devoted herself to her family, friends and those in need. Founding member of The Ruth Kirzon Group for Handicapped Children. She was loved and will be missed by all who knew her. Graveside service on Monday 2 PM at Cedar Park Cemetery, Emerson, New Jersey.
