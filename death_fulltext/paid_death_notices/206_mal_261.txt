
        HOPPER--Donald A. On March 20, 2004, at age 78, peacefully at home surrounded by family. Don was a lifelong resident of Manhattan and descendant of the Dutch Hopper family that settled in New York City in the 17th century. Don was a proud World War II veteran, graduate of the University of Notre Dame and Fordham Law School and a prosecuter in the office of the New York District Attorney under William Hogan. Don was a skilled, compassionate attorney in private practice for over 50 years, a dedicated advocate and a brilliant legal scholar. He was a man of unfailing moral character and strong faith and he will be sorely missed by his family and friends. Don is survived by his wife of 45 years, Virginia, his children Donald Jr, Mark, Justine, Adrianne and Peter, and his grandchildren Julia, Chris, Drew and Allie and by his brother Bert. Family will receive friends on Monday, 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Avenue (at 21st Street) New York City. Funeral Mass Tuesday at 10 AM at Immaculate Conception R.C. Church, 414 East 14th Street (at 1st Avenue). In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to: The Diabetes Research Institute Foundation, 3440 Hollywood Blvd, Suite 100, Hollywood, FL 33021.
