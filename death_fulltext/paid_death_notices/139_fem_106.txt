
        MINKIN-Sarah Rieur. Age 100. Four generations of the Minkin and Rieur families celebrate the life of Sarah Minkin. Loved and admired for her abundant energy and caring for others, she was an ardent advocate and exemplar of family loyalty, a healthy life style, professional responsibility and commitment to Jewish cultural identity. Born in Russia, she emigrated to the U.S. at age 14. She graduated in 1923 from the Nurses Training School of the Philadelphia Jewish Hospital. During World War II, after the death of her husband William Minkin, she enlisted in the U.S. Army Nurse Corps. After twenty years, serving in the Philippines, Japan, Germany and U.S., she retired with the rank of Major. Alert and in command until the end, Sarah Minkin leaves family and friends in whose memories and lives she will remain a vital force and influence.
        MINKIN-Sarah Rieur. On August 12, 1998. Beloved wife of the late William. Devoted stepmother of Dr. Vera F. Minkin. Also survived by her very loving family and many friends.
