
        OBERMEIER-Renee. On March 14th, 2002 at home in New York City at the age of 96. Born Renee Bachstutz to Mindell Jung and Zygmunt Bachstutz in Lvov, Russia. Widow of Louis. Aunt of Sheila and Philip Levy, great aunt of Tania Levy and James Levy, all of London, England. Cousin of Regina Kayne and Faye Lichtenstein of Columbus, Ohio and their families. A woman of great dignity & sensibility, she was a long time and generous supporter of many worthy causes. Contributions may be made in her memory to: Hadassah, The Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., American Committee for Shaare Zedek Hospital in Jerusalem Inc., Hebrew Tabernacle Congregation, Aufbau Heritage Foundation, Inc., or THE BLUE CARD, Inc. Funeral service 9:30 AM Sunday, March 17 at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
