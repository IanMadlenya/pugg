
        FRASIER-Dudley. 75. Died after a brief illness on May 2. A graduate of Harvard, he was Publicity Director at Little Brown in Boston for several years. He then moved to New York where he worked at Rinehart before becoming an executive editor at Popular Library and New American Library. He later worked as editor-at-large at Pocket Books where he remained until his retirement. Dudley will be greatly missed by all his friends and colleagues in the publishing community. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Cabrini Hospice at 227 East 19th Street, New York, NY 10003 would be appreciated.
