
        ABRAMS - LEVINE -- Blossom on October 14, 2004, after a long and courageous battle with ALS. Beloved wife of Leo Abrams and the late Al Levine, loving mother to Lesley (Ed), Randy (Amy), Jeffrey (Anne), sister of Barbara (Bernard), devoted grandmother to Ari, Adam, Ilyssa, Tori, Mark, Ethan and Sara. She was a teacher's teacher, a superb athlete and a friend to so many, a ''Jewish Woman of Valor.'' She enriched the lives of all who knew her. Service 10AM, Sunday, October 17, at Riverside Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Donations in her memory may be sent to ALS Association, Greater New York Chapter, 116 John St, NY, NY 10038.
