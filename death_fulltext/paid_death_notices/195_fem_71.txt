
        ZORINA-Vera. The Board of Directors and Staff of The Santa Fe Opera note with enormous sadness the death of Vera Zorina. Vera Zorina had a long association with the company, first as performer and director, and then as a member of the Board of Directors. She was an Honorary Director at the time of her death. Vera Zorina will be remembered as a woman of great spirit, great talent and great generosity. Her contribution to The Santa Fe Opera is immeasurable. The entire company expresses its deep sympathy to her family. David K. Ingalls, Chairman Susan F. Morris, President Richard Gaddes, General Director The Santa Fe Opera
