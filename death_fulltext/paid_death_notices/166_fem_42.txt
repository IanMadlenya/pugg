
        LINDHEIM-Esther G. On November 3, 2000. Retired principal of PS 6 in the Bronx. Beloved wife of the late Burton, mother of Julie and Charles Cantor, devoted grandmother of Deborah C. and Lowell Kirschner and Margalit Cantor. Contributions in her memory may be made to World Jewish Congress or Hadassah. Services were held.
