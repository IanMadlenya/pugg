
        ACKERMAN -- Nancy, 73, passed away peacefully on September 9 at her home in Metuchen, N.J. She was the devoted wife of Bernard, loving mother of Terri, Sandra, and Howard, adoring grandmother of Lionel, Jesse, and Jackie, loving sister of Robert and sister-in-law Carol, and devoted to Jenny, James, Lance, Steve, and Maggie. Nancy was a dear friend to so many in Metuchen and Stockbridge. Educated at Cornell and New York Universities, she served professionally for many years as a chemistry instructor at Rutgers University and later made herself indispensable as the marketing coordinator of Ackrad Laboratories, a medical device company founded by Bud. She was a past-president of the Metuchen League of Women Voters and a member of the Metuchen Planning Board and the Stockbridge Bowl Association. Funeral services will be held at Temple Neve Shalom, 250 Grove Avenue, Metuchen, at 1:30PM, Friday, September 10. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to a charity of your choice.
