
        BRIZELL -- Murray J. ''Breeze'' On December 3, 2004. Beloved husband of Harriet. Devoted father of Hillary Brizell-DeLise and her husband Joseph, and Vicki Dellon and her husband Scott. Cherished grandfather of Allison DeLise, David Dellon, Jonathan DeLise and Kate Dellon. A decorated veteran of WWII who will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call Saturday, December 4th, 57PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Graveside service Sunday, December 5th, 10:30AM at United Hebrew Cemetery, Staten Isand N.Y. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
