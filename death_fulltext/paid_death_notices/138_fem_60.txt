
        THURSH-June R. On July 4, 1998 after a lengthy illness. Beloved wife of the late Joseph J. Thursh. Devoted sister of Lillian, Irvin and Priscilla. Loving mother of Joan. Grandmother of Louis and Jody. Aunt of Sue & Bob, Joel and Lisa, Stacy, Bobby and Bonnie. Loyal friend and inspiring teacher and mentor to many. A pioneering advertising woman, recipient of the prestigious Ad Woman of the Year Award and numerous other professional awards and honors, she was Advertising Director of J.C. Penney in New York City and Lits in Philadelphia. Services are on Thursday, July 9 at 11 A.M., Montefiore Cemetery, Church Road, Fox Chase, PA.
