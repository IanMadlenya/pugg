
        KAVA--Rose, beloved wife of the late David Solomon, sister of Dina Arbel, devoted mother of Victor and Ruth, mother-in-law of Richard Hemmes, passed away in her sleep on April 18, 2004. She brought a sweet light into many lives throughout her 94 years. Memorial service on Friday, April 23 at 10AM at The Williams Residence, 720 West End Avenue, New York, New York. 10025. Memorial contributions may be sent to The Williams Residence.
