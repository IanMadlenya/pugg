
        SHERMAN--Jeannine. After a brief illness, we sadly announce the passing of Jeannine on October 6, 2005, beloved wife of Claude for 59 years, devoted mother of Michel (Gloria), Jean-Claude (Karin) and Sylvie (Elie Bloch), adored grandmother of Adam, Chloe, Luke, Christopher and Benjamin. Born in Lyon, France, she was revered for her unfailing kindness and generosity, and was a devoted volunteer in many organizations. ''Elle etait vraiment un ange''. Memorial service will be held Sunday, October 9 at 2 P.M. at Temple Judea of Manhasset. In lieu of flowers, donations to a charity of your choice.
