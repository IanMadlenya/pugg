
        KIRSCH-Aviva (nee Isobel Leader). Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the tragic and untimely death of Aviva (Isobel Leader) Kirsch, beloved daughter of Ruby and the late Dr. Sidney Leader, our Honorary President For Life, and sister of Steven Leader. We will miss her devotion to family, her loving heart, her passion for living, and her devotion to the traditions of the Jewish people. Our entire community sends loving support to Aviva's children, Gabrielle and Beth, to Ruby, Steven, and Barbara, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
