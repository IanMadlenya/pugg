
        VAN ALSTYNE-Janet G. The Board of Trustees of Englewood Hospital and Medical Center records with sorrow the passing of Janet Graham Van Alstyne who served our Medical Center for more than half a century as a trustee, volunteer and fund-raiser. Mrs. Van Alstyne's good deeds will serve as a lasting memorial to her long and distinguished life. Our condolences go out to her daughters Joan, Keats and Ellen, and the other members of her family. Andrew F. Durkin, Chairman Daniel A. Kane, President
