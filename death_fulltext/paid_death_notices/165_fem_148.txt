
        SPENCER-CHURCHILL-Lady Sarah. Of Old Lyme, Connecticut. Died suddenly on October 13, 2000. Mother of Serena Balfour, Consuelo Russell, Alexandra Birch and Jacqueline Williams. Sister of the Duke of Marlborough, Lady Rosemary Muir, Lord Charles SpencerChurchill & the late Lady Caroline Waterhouse. Grandmother of Morgan McConnell, Lucinda McConnell, Consuelo Lily Balfour, Alastair Balfour, Alexander Judson, Nicholas Judson, Ian Judson, Eugene Williams, Harry Williams, and Philip Williams. She will be remembered and missed by all. Services to be held at St. James Epispocal Church, Madison Avenue at 71st Street, Tuesday at 12 noon.
