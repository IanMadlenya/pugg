
        FITZSIMONS -- Kimberly Anne, suddenly on June 28, 2005. Beloved daughter of Richard and Kathleen FitzSimons of Fairfield, CT and very much loved sister of Ryan of Manchester, NH. Also survived by many aunts, uncles and cousins. Kim attended Greens Farms Academy in Greens Farms, CT and George Washington University, Washington, DC majoring in media and TV communications. Kim was an owner of Indigo Media, Inc. and a producer of documentaries for TV, TV commercials and music videos. Family will receive relatives and friends at Redden Funeral Home, 325 West 14 Street, NYC Friday July 1 from 2-5 & 7-9PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated in her parish at St. Francis Xavier RC Church Saturday July 2nd at 10AM. Burial will follow at Oak Lawn Cemetery, Fairfield, CT. A memorial service will follow at a date to be determined in Fairfield, CT. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Big Sister Program or the Humane Society.
