
        DESHMUKH-Madhukar, M., M.D. The Montefiore-Einstein Heart Center mourns the death of our colleague and friend Madhukar M. Deshmukh, M.D. Dr. Deshmukh was a distinguished cardiologist and teacher who practiced Cardiology in the Bronx for over 30 years and taught generations of physicians both here and in his native India. He was a member of many national and international medical organizations and was Past President of the Association of American Cardiologists of Indian Origin. He will be deeply missed by his patients, students and colleagues. We extend our condolences to his wife, Sheila M. Deshmukh, M.D., who was also a member of the Montefiore Medical Center, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, faculty and their son, Ravi Deshmukh, M.D. Mark A. Greenberg, M.D. Interim Chief, Division of Cardiology Jeffrey P. Gold, M.D. Chairman, Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery
