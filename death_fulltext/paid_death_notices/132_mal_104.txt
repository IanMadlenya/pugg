
        KOGAN-Nathan, 91. Passed away Jan. 6, 1998 after a long illness. He is survived by his wife, Winifred and his daughter Jane Kogan of Provincetown, MA. He was a retired attorney from New York City and formerly a cellist with the Ft. Lauderdale Symphony, and greater Miami Opera under the Baton of Emerson Buckley. He was the legal counsel for the Violoncello Society of New York City and President of the Chamber Music Associates at the 92nd Street ''Y''. He will be missed by many loving relatives and friends.
