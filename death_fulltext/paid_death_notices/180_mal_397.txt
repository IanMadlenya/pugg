
        GRAHAM-William. Of Stamford, CT and St. Simons Island, GA, died January 27, 2002 at age 86. Beloved husband for 62 years of Louise Stern Graham. Loving father of Patricia G. Stotler, Margot G. Kaczynski and William L. Graham. Fond father-in-law of John L. Stotler, Stanley J. Kaczynski and Susan H. Graham. Cherished grandfather of Elizabeth A. Stotler, Karen J. Kaczynski, Brian J. Kaczynski (Sarah) and Ryan W. Graham. During his lifetime Mr. Graham was one of the founders of Temple Sinai in Stamford, CT and was active in the Literacy Volunteer Program and at the Rehabilitation Center. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice Residence, 1029 E. Main St., Stamford, CT 06902 or to a cancer society of your choice. Service private.
