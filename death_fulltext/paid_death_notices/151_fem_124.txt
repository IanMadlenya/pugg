
        LERNER-Mary Fenyes, Dr. Died June 27, 1999. Born July 31, 1912. Wife of late Allen Lerner and Robert Glass. Allergy, immunology and asthma practice in Brooklyn and Hampton Bays. Worked to advance health care, peace and social justice.
