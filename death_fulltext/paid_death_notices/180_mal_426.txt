
        GUINNESS-Murtogh D. Died January 30th, 2002 in his home in New York City at age 88. He is survived by his sister Grania, Dowager Marchioness of Normandy, and sixteen nieces and nephews. A collector and longtime benefactor of the arts, he was a former President of the Music Box Society. Funeral services will be held at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue, on Monday, February 4th, at 10 AM.
