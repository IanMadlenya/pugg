
        GUMPRECHT--Barbara Philip. On June 2, 2003. Age 87. Born in Hamburg, Germany in 1915, she came to this country in 1941. Loving mother, wife, therapist and artist. She brought beauty and color into our lives. We will miss her. She is survived by her son Jeffrey, her daughter Eva, her brother Reinald and her loving companions Iris and Michael. May she be peaceful and free. >>AD#
