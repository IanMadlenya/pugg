
        PACE-Harry, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with sorrow the death of Dr. Harry Pace. Dr. Pace was associated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for over forty years. He was one of the first obstetric/gynecology residents at Maimonides. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. David Feldman, M.D., Pres. Salvatore DeGliuomini M.D. President Elect Steven Farber, M.D., Sec'y Herbert Lehman, M.D. Treasurer
