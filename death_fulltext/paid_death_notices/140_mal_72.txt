
        McKENNA-John J. ''Jack''. Of Ridgewood, N.J., died at home September 4, 1998. He was 67. He was Vice-President and Director, Sales Services for Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, Inc., New York, New York. Beloved husband of Marie (Marone) and the late Dorothy ''Doe'' McKenna, who died in 1977. Loving father of Rita O'Connor, Annemarie Rigler, Carolan Sherman, Mary K. Guarente, Jacqueline O'Connor, Marianne Thomas, Daniel J. McKenna, and stepson Robert Marone. Devoted brother of Daniel J. McKenna & Sister M. Helen Theresa, OP, Dominicans of Sparkhill, NY, Gloria Cafferty, and the late Mary K. Cully, who died in 1995. Adoring grandfather of 21. Visiting will be Monday and Tuesday from 2-4 and 7-9 pm at our Lady of Mt. Carmel RC Church, Ridgewood, NJ. Funeral mass Wednesday, 10AM at Mt. Carmel Church. Interment is to follow in Maryrest Cemetery, Mahwah. No flowers, as donations to the Jack and Doe McKenna Memorial Scholarship Fund at Immaculate Heart Academy, 500 Van Emburgh Avenue, Washington Township, NJ 07675 are appreciated. For more information contact Feeney Funeral Home, Ridgewood, N.J. 201-444-7650.
