
        TAYLOR-Frieda. The partners and staff of Lipsky, Goodkin & Company, PC, with profound sorrow, mourn the passing of Frieda Taylor, the mother of our esteemed partner and founder, Stewart Taylor. This courageous woman, cherished her children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. She was deeply committed to their well being, always giving to them an abundance of love, compassion, understanding and deep devotion. She enriched the lives of all who came in contact with her warmth and trust. Our heartfelt condolences to her children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. She will be missed, but truly remembered. Lipsky, Goodkin and Company.
