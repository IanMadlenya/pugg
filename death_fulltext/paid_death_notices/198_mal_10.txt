
        SLOM--Stanley H., 78, on June 28 after a long, courageous battle with cancer. Most loving and beloved husband of Deborah (Kirsh). Adoring father of Celia Vimont. Friend and admirer of son-inlaw Greg; devoted and proud grandfather of Benjamin; brother of the late Aaron and Earle. Journalist and editor at Fairchild Publications, the Wall Street Journal and Lebhar-Friedman. Teacher of journalism at Columbia and New York University. Mentor in Learning Leaders program. Proud and active member of the Reform Jewish movement. Past President of Stephen Wise Free Synagogue. A caring and gentle man. In love he is remembered, in memory he lives. Funeral services July 2, 11 AM at Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, 30 W. 68th St. >>AD#
