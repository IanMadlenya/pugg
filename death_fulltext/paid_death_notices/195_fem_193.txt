
        GETOFF-Isabelle Egrie (formerly Kocsis-Nizich) died April 17th in her 76th year. A courageous, accomplished woman-secular and religious (Unitarian) educator, psychoanalytic and Imago Relationship therapist. Mother of Ellen (and Tom), Janey (and Ross), grandmother of Evan, Alex and Michael, sister of Oliver (and Nancy), Vilma (and Jack), wife of the late George Nizich, stepmother of David, Michael, Maria and Sarah and step-grandmother of Dara and Mollie, long-time friend of Helen Simpson, and life-partner for the past 27 years of Dr. Lou Getoff. She will be remembered and missed by many.
