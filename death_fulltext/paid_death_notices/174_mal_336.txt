
        SNYDER-Louis Irving Jr. Age 89, newspaperman, freelance journalist, and music critic, died July 26, 2001, in Fairfield, CT. Funeral services on Thursday, August 2, 2001, at 11:00 A.M. in St. Timothy's Episcopal Church, Congress St., Fairfield, CT. Contributions in his name may be made to the Arthritis Foundation or the Marcella Sembrich Memorial Assn., Bolton Landing, NY 12814.
