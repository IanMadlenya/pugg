
        HURD-Jeanne O'Neal. On Oct. 9, 2000 as the result of a stroke on Sept. 29. Former wife of the late Charles P. Hurd, beloved mother of Jeanne and Patricia of N.Y.C & River Vale, N.J. Aunt to six loving nieces & nephews and mother-byproxy to many other daughters & sons. Born in Washington, D.C. & raised in Manhattan, Jeanne was a resident of River Vale for 45 years. She will be remembered as the heart of her family and the fire around which we all gathered for warmth. No services will be held. Contributions may be made in her name to Fund for Animals, 500 West 57th St, New York City 10019.
