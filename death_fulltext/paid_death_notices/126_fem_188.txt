
        MALTZER-Lillian. Women of Reform Judaism, The Federation of Temple Sisterhoods is deeply saddened by the untimely passing in an automobile accident of our beloved and esteemed past President, Lillian Maltzer. Her devotion to Judaism, the advancement of women and development of leadership have left a rich heritage. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, Dr. Joseph Maltzer, her children, grandchildren and great grandchild. May her memory be for a blessing Judith O. Rosenkranz, President Ellen Y. Rosenberg, Executive Director
