
        DAVIS-Beatrice Mintzer, 75, died February 27, 1997. Survived by her daughter, Deborah Davis Gross, brother, Arthur Mintzer, two grandchildren, Paula and Eve Gross, and beloved friend, Jane Redwine. A native of Brooklyn, Bea was a teacher for the OASIS Senior Education Program and was by profession a geriatric nurse practitioner. Services will be held at El Camino Mortuary, San Diego, Wed., March 5, at 10 AM.
