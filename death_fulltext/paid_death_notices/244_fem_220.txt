
        VAN BAAREN--Karren (nee Robertson), died at home in Devon, after a long illness, aged 61. Much loved by all her family and friends. She will be remembered with huge affection and respect, for her brilliant and warm personality. The Funeral service will be held at the Parish Church of St. John the Baptist in North Bovey, on Monday June 4th, at 11am, followed by a recption at nearby Bovey Castle. Donations in her memory, if so desired, in aid of ''The Friends of St. John's'', as well as all further enquiries, may be directed to Coombes & Sons, 73 Fore St., Bovey Tracy, Devon, UK, TQ139XY, 44-1626-833409
