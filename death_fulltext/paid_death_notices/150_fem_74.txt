
        KOWLER-Miriam, 87. On July 5, 1999 after a long illness. She was born in Williamsburg, Brooklyn. She was the beloved wife of the late Samuel and the devoted daughter of the late Solomon and Sadie Mann. She is surived by two loving sons, Daniel and Richard, dear sister Ruth and five cherished grandchildren, Suzanne, Andrew, Laura, Amelia, Carolyna.
