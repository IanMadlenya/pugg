
        TENNEY--Daniel G., Jr. Of Locust Valley, N.Y. died peacefully on March 28, 2006 at age 92. Beloved husband of Constance Franchot Tenney, his wife of 66 years, he was the devoted father of Constance Rafle, Pamela Simpson, Alexandra Potts and Daniel G. Tenney III. Grandfather of nine and great grandfather of four. A graduate of the Choate School (1931), Yale College (1935) and Yale Law School (1938), he had a long and wonderful career at Milbank, Tweed, Hadley and McCloy. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Boys Club of New York or Trout Unlimited.
