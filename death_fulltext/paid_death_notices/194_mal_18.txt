
        NADLER-Fred B. It is with profound sorrow we mourn the death of Fred B. Nadler on January 17, 2003. Antique dealer and designer specializing in Chinese export porcelain and English and American furniture. Survived by sister Adele Sussman, nephews Robert and David Sussman, niece Ilene Sussman. Business partner and friend Richard Trotta. Forever in our hearts. Contributions in his memory may be made to charity of choice.
