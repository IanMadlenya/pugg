
        FERRAGAMO - Fiamma. The NYU community grieves for the loss of Fiamma Ferragamo. Our hearts go out to her loved ones; like them, we shall miss her greatly. Laurence A. Tisch, Chairman of the Board of Trustees L. Jay Oliva, President
