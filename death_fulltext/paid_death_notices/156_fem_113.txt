
        LYONS-Viola. On January 11, 2000 in her 97th year. Beloved wife of the late Gabriel. Dear mother of Stanley C. and Richard M. Lyons and mother-in-law of Marion and Shirley. Adored grandmother of Eric & Cindy & great-grandmother. Mrs. Lyons was an interior designer in New York City for 60 years. Services Thursday, January 13, 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
