
        MONTESANO -- Richard J. Passed away on April 13, 2005 at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital. He was born on November 18, 1941 in Brooklyn. N.Y. Dick Montesano was a Senior Vice President of Marketing Research for Disney ABC Television Network in Manhattan. After his retirement, he founded and became President of Montesano Marketing and Research in Florida. He was also presently a consultant for both ABC-ESPN. He married Carol Canade on July 24, 1966 at St. Anselms Church in Brooklyn, N.Y. In addition to his wife, Carol, he is survived by a daughter Melissa Montesano and three sons, Neil, Thomas and Todd Montesano. He is predeceased by his mother, Assunta (Betty) Montesano. Mr. Montesano is the brother of Connie Muccini and Robert Montesano. He is the uncle of Robert Joseph Montesano and Alexis Misseri and greatuncle of Nicholas Messeri. Reposing at Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, N.Y. 10583. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10AM, Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale. Interment St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, Queens. Visitation Friday, 2 to 4 and 7 to 9PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to The American Heart Association preferred.
