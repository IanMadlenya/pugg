
        HAMRAH--Col. George Alexander, USA (Ret.) on April 18, 2004 in San Diego, CA. Beloved son of the late Alexander J. Hamrah and Labiby Hamrah. He was born in Brooklyn NY, attended Penn State University and received his B.S. from NYU. He was awarded a Ph.D. in Economics. Col. Hamrah enlisted in the U.S. Army in 1941, rose to the rank of Colonel in the Quartermaster Corps and served With Distinction for 35 years. He is survived by his sister-in-law Mary E. Hamrah of Tenafly NJ, eight nieces and one nephew, and many grandnephews and grandnieces. He was preceded in death by his brother, Elias Hamrah of Englewood NJ and by his four sisters, Adma Ganim of Cincinnati OH, Alice Hamrah of Bowie MD, Claire Lian of Beechwood NJ, Louise Hamrah of Brooklyn NY. Family and friends may call Friday, 2-5 and 7-9 at the Herbst-Traska-Waldeck Chapels, 7614 4th Ave, Brooklyn NY 11209 (valet parking). Funeral liturgy Saturday, 10AM at Church of the Virgin Mary, 8th Ave and 2nd St, Brooklyn NY. Interment St. John's Cemetery. For information, 718-745-1600
