
        LILIS--Ruth, M.D., 80. Professor Emeritus at Mt. Sinai School of Medicine died on March 17, 2006 at her home in New York City. Surived by her husband Dr. Michael Lilis, daughter Elena Lilis Werner, son-in-law Louis G. Werner, grandchildren Genevieve & Kennett and sister Dr. Judith Lowe. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Sunday 4-7 PM with Service Monday, 9:30 AM. Burial to follow at Southampton Cemetery.
        $(6$)LILIS--Ruth. The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourns the passing of Professor Emerita Ruth Lilis, MD of the Department of Community & Preventive Medicine. As a faculty member of the Mount Sinai School of Medicine since 1973, she founded the clinical center in occupational medicine at Mount Sinai. A renowned occupational physician, Dr. Lilis was admired by colleagues, students and patients. We extend our sincerest sympathy to her family and friends. Peter W. May Chairman of the Boards of Trustees Kenneth L. Davis, MD President, CEO and Dean
