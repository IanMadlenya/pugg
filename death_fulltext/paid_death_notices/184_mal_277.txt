
        BARTSCH-Thomas W., Sr. 100, of Williamsburg, VA and formerly of Pelham Manor, NY died on May 21, 2002. Mr. Bartsch was a member of the New York Stock Exchange with the firm of Travers & Bartsch. He was a member of the Pelham Country Club, the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick, the Knights of Malta and the Knights of the Holy Sepulcher. He served on the Cardinal's Committee of the Laity and on the Board of the Mount Loretta Home in Staten Island. Mr. Bartsch was most proud of receiving, for his humanitarian services, the Brotherhood Award from The National Conference of Christians and Jews. He was predeceased by his wife, Ida Becker Bartsch in 1992. He is survived by his sons, Thomas, Jr.(Margaret) of Williamsburg, VA, Robert (Avda) of North White Plains, NY and Donald (Martha) of Weston, VT, and his daughter Marylou (Anthony) D'Onofrio of New Rochelle, NY, 15 grandchildren and 21 great-grandchildren. Reposing at Hawthorne Funeral Home, 21 Stevens Avenue, Hawthorne, NY (914769-4404), Monday 3-7 pm. Funeral mass St. Francis of Assisi Chapel at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY on Tuesday 11:30 am. Interment to follow. In lieu of flowers, donations to Catholic Charities would be appreciated.
