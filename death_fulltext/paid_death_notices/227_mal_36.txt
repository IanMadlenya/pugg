
        SCHMIERER--Hy, 86, December 1, 2005. Devoted husband of Horty for 56 years, loving father of Susan and Matthew Silverman & Merri and Harry Bragg. Adored ''Poppy'' of Rachel, Jaime, and Abbey Silverman. Cherished Uncle and Great Uncle to many nieces and nephews. Veteran of the US Army Air Corps in World War II. Founder, Owner and Director of Camp Starlight, Starlight, PA for 53 years. Loved and will be missed by thousands of campers and staff whose lives he touched and influenced. Former President of the Association of Independent Camps. Served on the Board of Governors of the New York Section of the American Camping Association. With his love for children and their well being, following his retirement in 1999, he became a board member of the Non-Violence Project in Miami, FL and an advisor to the Shake-A-Leg Foundation in Coconut Grove, FL. He continued to remain active in the Hancock (NY) Community Education Foundation. Funeral Services will be at ''Gutterman's'', 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY at 12:00 noon on Sunday, December 4, 2005. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Hancock Community Education Foundation, 38 West Main Street, Hancock, NY 13783.
