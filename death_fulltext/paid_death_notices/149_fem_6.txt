
        COHN-Martha. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Hannah Kubatzki on the loss of her beloved sister, Martha. A Golden Founder and longtime supporter of our Annual Dinner and Concert, she was a righteous woman who personified the qualities of elegance, grace and extraordinary dignity. May the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Med. Ctr in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair Menno Ratzker, Pres. Stuart Tauber, Exec. Dir.
        COHN-Martha. Lincoln Square Synagogue mourns with sorrow the passing of our matriach, Mrs. Martha Cohn. Mrs. Cohn, a trustee of the synagogue dedicated her life to our community. May her memory be a blessing. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Richard Kestenbaum, President
