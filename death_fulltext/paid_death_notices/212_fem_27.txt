
        SLESINGER--Helen. Beloved wife of the late Charles Slesinger. A woman of valor and a lady of strength has passed away at the age of 95. She affected everyone she met with her interest, intensity, concern for their welfare, devotion and intelligence. A remarkable role model who will be sorely missed by her family, son Jacques, daughter Liane, four grandchildren and ten great - grandchildren. Funeral Sunday 12:45PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York.
