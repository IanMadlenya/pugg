
        DOVEL-Kenneth. The staff of Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. and the Lincoln Center Festival expresses to Alicia Daniels and the family of Kenneth Dovel, her husband, its deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. A classical singer, Kenn had numerous musical affiliations including the New York City Opera and the National Grand Opera, and was a longtime member of the Board of Governors of the American Guild of Musical Artists. He will be missed by his many friends & associates. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, December 16, at 3PM at Christ and St. Stephen's Church, 120 West 69 Street in Manhattan. Memorial contributions may be sent to the AGMA Relief Fund, 1727 Broadway, New York, New York 10019. Beverly Sills, Chairman of the Board Nathan Leventhal, President Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. Nigel Redden, Director Lincoln Center Festival
