
        BARCLAY-Henry Anthony. Of Southampton, L.I. suddenly on November 29, 2000. Survived by his mother, Lyda W. Hall(Barclay) & the late Henry Barclay. Brother of Rutgers Barclay of Santa Fe, N.M.; brother-in-law of Stephen Ely of Locust Valley, L.I. Also survived by three nieces and one nephew. Predeceased by his sister, Susan Barclay Ely. Memorial service Saturday, December 9, 2000, 11:30 AM at St. John's Episcopal Church, Southampton, L.I. In lieu of flowers, donations to Rogers Memorial Library, Southampton, L.I. would be appreciated.
