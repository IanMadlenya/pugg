
        NOSHPITZ-Joseph D. The International Society for Adolescent Psychiatry mourns the loss of one of its officers and most eloquent spokesman, Joseph D. Noshpitz, MD. With his brilliance, wit and profound wisdom, Joe never failed to enrich the life of anyone he touched. On behalf of our Executive Committee, I extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Revered by all who were priveleged to know him, he will be sorely missed. Michael G. Kalogerakis, MD President
