
        TONTI-Joseph D., cabaret performer and personal trainer, on 3/26/ 97 of AIDS. He was also a volunteer for Body Positive, an HIV+ oriented organizaton. He contributed numerous articles to their publication as well as having served as an HIV+ interviewee for The New York Times. He is survived by his companion of 13 years, Richard D. Banks of New York, by his mother Elizabeth A. Tonti, three brothers, two sisters, and numerous cousins. Columbus and New York services are planned. Contributions may be made to Body Positive New York or Broadway Cares.
