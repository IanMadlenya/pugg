
        FAY-Harriet. At age 65. On October 11. An accomplished orchestra leader, jazz pianist, and cabaret performer, she was predeceased by her son, Michael Cohen. She is survived by her daughter Alice Cohen, three brothers, Dr. Stuart Levin, Charles Levin, and Myron Levin of Los Angeles, eight nieces and nephews and four great-nieces and nephews. Memorial donations to Keystone Hospice, 8765 Stenton Avenue, Wyndmoor, PA. 19038.
