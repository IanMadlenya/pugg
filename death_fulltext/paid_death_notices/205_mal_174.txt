
        NORMAN--Andrew E., on February 7, 2004, age 73. Editor, philanthropist, democrat. Beloved father of Abigail, Sarah, Margaret and Rebecca. Sister of Nancy Norman Lassalle. Grandfather of Erika Peartree and Emmet Holton. Fiancee of Barbara Mahony Kent. Will be sorely missed by family, colleagues, neighbors and friends. Predeceased by his wife of 41 years, Helen Davis Norman. Service will be held Saturday February 21 at 11am at Palisades Presbyterian Church, Palisades, NY. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Int'l Rescue Committee, 122 E 42 St, NY, NY 10168.
