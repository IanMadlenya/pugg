
        McGLINN-Frank C.P. The Actors' Fund of America mourns the loss of Frank C.P. McGlinn, honored and respected Trustee. His leadership memorialized the Great American Actor, Edwin Forrest, at our nursing home and as an endowment. The legacies of these two great Philadelphians help entertainment professionals in times of need. Thank you, Frank. Tom Dillon, President Joseph Benincasa, Exec. Director
