
        FELBERBAUM -- Beatrice Widder. Beloved wife of the late Nathan Felberbaum. Loving mother of Elaine Hirsch. Adored grandmother of Howard and Herbert Hirsch. Dear great-grandmother of Steven, Daniel, Samuel, Allison and Caitlin Hirsch. She is survived by her sister Gladys Dorfler. Beattie was born in Brooklyn in 1907 and resided in Flushing, NY and West Palm Beach, Fl. She passed away at Seacrest Village, Encinitas, California. Special thanks to the staff at Seacrest Village and Odyssey Hospice for all the loving care they provided. Services will be held Sunday August 29, at 11:30AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
