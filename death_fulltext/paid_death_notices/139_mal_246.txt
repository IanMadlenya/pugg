
        KAMINER-Peter H. The partners and employees of Winthrop, Stimson, Putnam & Roberts mourn the loss of Peter H. Kaminer, a partner of the firm for 40 years and our managing partner for 20 years. We will miss his extraordinary legal skills, his total devotion to the firm, the marvelous companionship which he provided, and his dedication to public service which set an example for all of us. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his son, Stevenson.
