
        CUMMINS-May Lipton. Of Rockville, Maryland, formerly of New York City, on Saturday, January 5, 2002, at home. She is survived by her sisters Helen Gutterman, of Rockville and Evelyn Berger of Dallas and brother, Gerald Lipton of Spokane, Washington. She was predeceased by sister Estelle Goldberg. She is also survived by niece Lyn Tanenholtz and cousin Aileen Bloom as well as many other family members and friends. A memorial service will be held Sunday, February 10, 3 PM at The Brotherhood Synagogue, 28 Gramercy Park South, New York, 10003. Donations in her memory may be made to The Brotherhood Synagogue or the charity of your choice.
