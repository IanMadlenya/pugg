
        JOCKER-William. Died May 12th. A fervent fighter for economic reform, teacher at the Henry George School, an original thinker, man of great principle, author, dance devotee and music lover. We are greatly enriched by having known him. He will be sorely missed by David, Jeanne, Andrei, Stephanie, Richard, John, Murray, Roberta and many others. Memorial services to be announced later.
