
        BARTHOLOMEW-Robert. Writer, Journalist, Poet, Interior Designer passed away April 5 in NYC. He is survived by his beloved wife, Catherine, son Michael and his wife Veronique, daughters, Susan and Marie Louise and her husband Nicos. Grandchildren, Oliver, Theodora and Simeon. Brothers, Sidney and Jack. Robert resided both in NYC and Athens. He wrote for the Atlantis, Hellenic Star and Greek American and other publications. A member of the Overseas Press Club. Author of the just published ''Conversations with Christos'', a humorus guide to the Greek psyche. A memorial service will be held April 14th. at 4PM at 3 West 51 Street, NYC.
