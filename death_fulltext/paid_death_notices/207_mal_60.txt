
        CHANAUD--Raymond J. 74, retired public relations executive, died March 3 in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. He spent more than 50 years in travel and tourism including positions at Braniff International Airlines, greater Miami Convention & Visitors Bureau, and Frontier Airlines. He is survived by two children, Timothy and Melinda. Services will be held at St. Stephen's Episcopal Church in Port Washington at 11:00 AM on Saturday. The family requests donations to Seafarers' House in Port Everglades, FL.
