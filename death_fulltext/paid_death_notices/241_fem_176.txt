        KANTOR--Barbara, of Long Island, NY and Boca Raton, FL died on Monday, February 19, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Martin E. Kantor. Devoted mother of Hilary, Michael and son-in-law Sammy. Loving grandmother of Logan and Mallory. Caring sister of Carol. Her passion for travel, bridge and tennis were only overshadowed by her love for her family and friends. Barbara's energy, zest for life and strength in her battle with cancer was both courageous and extraordinary. A woman to cherish and a life to celebrate. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hadassah Sharette Institute of Oncology or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
