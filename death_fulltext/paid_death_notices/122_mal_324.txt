
        POLLOCK-Eugene N. Age 82, on March 24, 1997, in Palm Beach. Loving husband of Maria. Fabulous father of Michael (Pound Ridge, NY) and Kenneth (NYC). Doting grandfather of Daniel and Jennifer. Also survived by brother Robert (North Miami, FL). Gene was a successful insurance broker, avid sportsman and dedicated community leader. He'll be greatly missed by all who knew him. Memorial services were held earlier this week in Palm Beach. In lieu of flowers please make donations to the Christopher Reeve Foundation, PO Box 277, FDR Station, NYC 10150.
