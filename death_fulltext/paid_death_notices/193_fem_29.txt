
        HADZI-Martha Leeb (Molly). 83. Of Amherst, MA, died peacefully on January 25, 2003. Beloved mother of Cristina and Stephen Hadzi. Among her many professional and personal accomplishments, she was an archaeologist for the excavations at Samothrace and a distinguished Professor of our history at Mt. Holyoke College. Memorial service to be held at Grace Church, Amherst, MA, on March 29, and The Quaker Meeting House, South Yarmouth, MA, on April 19.
