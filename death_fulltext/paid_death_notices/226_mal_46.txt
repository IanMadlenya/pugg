
        TRINKOFF--Irwin H. Passed away in his home in Valeria on November 1, 2005. Dearest and loving husband of Maxine, father of Lloyd, Donna and Alexandra, grandfather of Daniel, Jonathan, Kyra, Ben, Zach and Dylan, brother of Burt and brother-in-law of Arline, father-in-law of Jan and Jeff. Irwin was a man of great loyalty with a keen sense of humor and a wonderful poet. He will be missed by his family and friends. The funeral is at Hellman Garlick Memorial Chapel, 1300 Pleasantville Road in Briarcliff, November 3, 12 noon.
