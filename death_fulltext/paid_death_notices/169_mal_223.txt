
        GOLDSTONE - Harmon Hen dricks, architect and former chairman of New York's Landmarks Preservation Commission which he helped to establish in 1965, died on Wednesday at the age of 89. He is survived by his niece Jane Goldstone Hilles of Bryn Mawr, PA and by a great nephew George Feaver and great niece Aline Feaver Anderson. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory can be made to The New York Historical Society, 2 West 77 Street, NY, NY 10024. Services on Thursday 1:30PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        GOLDSTONE - Harmon Hen dricks, Congregation Shearith Israel, the Spanish and Portuguese Synagogue, mourns the loss of its devoted member and extends its deepest sympathy to his family. Alvin Deutsch, Parnas
