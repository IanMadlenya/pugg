Survived by half sister Marianne; nieces and nephews Carol, Michael, Nancy, Mary Helen, Joe, Barbara, Bruce, Bernice and Robert; and beloved friend Stephanie Chen. Her cremains will be laid to rest in a private ceremony at Our Lady of Mercy Cemetery in Miami. Donations may be sent to The Arthritis Foundation.
