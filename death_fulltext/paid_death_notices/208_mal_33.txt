
        INGERSOLL--Raymond Crary. 85, died at his home on Duck Island, May 1. He was the son of Raymond Vail Ingersoll, Borough President of Brooklyn during the LaGuardia administration. He is survived by his wife Eleanor, daughter Carolyn Gagnon, son Raymond Vail, two grandchildren and a greatgrandson. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Long Island Pine Barrens Society, Box 429, Manorville, NY 11949-9801.
