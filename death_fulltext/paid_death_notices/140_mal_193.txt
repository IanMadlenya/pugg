
        MERRELL-Richard. Actor, television and film writer and designer, died Sunday, September 13 at his home in Heritage Village, Southbury, CT. The cause of death was heart failure. He is survived by his wife of 35 years, Jan Miner, and two sisters, Sister Jeanne Merrell and Audrey Shepard. No services are planned at the request of the deceased. In lieu of flowers it was his wish and that of Jan Miner that those who choose make a contribution to Project ALS (Lou Gehrig's Disease), 511 Avenue of the Americas, Suite 341, New York, New York 10011.
