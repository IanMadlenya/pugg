
        FEIBUSCH-Emanuel. 92, died August 2, 1997, in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Husband of the late Ida (nee Marantz). Devoted father of Ellie Kimelman, Karen Feibusch, Jerry and Jane Feibusch. Grandfather of Rick and Dot Kimelman, Keith and Kelly Feibusch, Amy and Jill Feibusch and great-grandson David Kimelman. Founder of E. Feibusch Co. & Swatchcraft. Private graveside services. Donations may be made in his memory to the UJA.
