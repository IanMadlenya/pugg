
        KLEINBERG-Dr. William Age 87. Professor at Princeton University and founder of Princeton Laboratories, on Saturday, February 7, 1998, in Princeton, NJ. Survived by his wife Ruth Schechter Kleinberg; daughters Barbara Conviser, Jamie Kleinberg Kamph and Wendy Barker, and also survived by five grandchildren. Funeral service 1:30 PM, today, at the Princeton Jewish Center, 435 Nassau Street, Princeton, NJ. Interment Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, NJ.
