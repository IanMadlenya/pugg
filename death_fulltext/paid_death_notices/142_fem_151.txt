
        KADLAC-Sarah Jane, 88, of New York City. Daughter of Clark A. and Agnes Marie (Petersen) Conley; died October 26, 1998. She is survived by her nieces Linda (Conley) Kaye, Cheryl (Conley) Rohde and nephews Christopher E. and Peter S. Conley. Services have been held with burial at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-On-Hudson, NY. Because of her kindness and loyalty, she will be greatly missed by friends and family.
