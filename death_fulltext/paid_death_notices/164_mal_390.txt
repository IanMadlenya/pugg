
        FILNER-Joseph H. Died June 28, in Columbia, MD. He is survived by his wife Doris Filner of Columbia, sons from his first marriage to Sarah Filner, Representative Bob Filner, (D) California, and Dr. Bernard Filner, step-daughter Nancy Stein and five grandchildren. He pioneered the stainless scrap industry. He was active in numerous progressive organizations, was an early supporter of M.L. King, Jr. and helped launch the SCLC. Donations may be made to the SCLC in Atlanta, GA.
