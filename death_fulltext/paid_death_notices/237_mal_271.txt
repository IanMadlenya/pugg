
        REDDY --Robert K. died October 20th of a stroke. An artist, art director, and Shakespeare scholar, he appeared in many Ridiculous Theatrical Company productions. Charles Ludlam wrote the role of Saint Gaudens in Camille for him in 1973. Off Broadway he directed Aristophanes' The Cyclops and Goldoni's Mirandolina. He is survived by brothers, Thomas and Michael, loving nieces, nephews, and friends.
