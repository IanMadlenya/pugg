
        FENNELL-Thomas Francis III. Of New York, died October 20, 2000 after a courageous battle with cancer. He was the son of the late Thomas F. Fennell II, and Teresa Winters of New York. He is survived by his beloved wife Jenny McDonagh and brothers Daniel of Wyncote, PA, John of Wilmington, NC, Rupert of Spencertown, NY and Philip of Pawling, NY. He is also survived by four step-daughters, many nephews, nieces and cousins. According to his wishes, his remains are being donated for scientific research. A memorial service will be held soon. Donations in Tommy's memory may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice of Beth Israel Hospital, 1st and 16th St., New York, NY 10003.
