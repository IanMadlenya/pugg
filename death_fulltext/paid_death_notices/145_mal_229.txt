
        LERNER-Dr. Joseph. Passedaway peacefully on February 18, 1999 after 88 wonderful years. Beloved husband of the late Jeanne. He will be mourned by his sons Joel, Corey and Gary; his daughters-in-law Tiffini and Susan; his five grandchildren and his many friends and neighbors whom he selflessly served for 50 years as family physician. Services at the Parkside Memorial Chapel in Rego Park on Sunday, February 21 at 11:30AM.
