
        MILLER-Philip. 86. Predeceased by cherished wife Frances. Beloved father of Sandra (Geoff) Conway & Dr. Harvey (Linda) Miller & grandfather of Jennifer, Francesca & Jonathan. Lifetime member of Fabric Salesmens Assoc. Volunteer, two term president of Aging in America. His wisdom, warmth, humor, generosity and zest for life enriched us all.
