
        AMENDE-Kurt F. Of Alexandria, VA; Center Moriches, NY, and St. Thomas, V.I. passed away August 4, at his Center Moriches residence. Mr. Amende retired from the U.S. Army after 26 years, a colonel in the Engineer Corps. He was currently a Marine Engineering Consultant. He is survived by his wife, Louise; his children, Lynn, Roger and Kurt Amende Jr; his sisters, Charlotte Boyle and Mariana Wheeler; and one grandchild, Catelyn Amende. Reposing Tuesday, 11 AM-1 PM, at Wesche Funeral Home Inc., 495 Main Street, Center Moriches. Cremation private.
