
        PURCELL--James T. Age 79, of North Fort Meyers, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died June 30 at Cape Coral Hospital. A veteran of WW II, he lived much of his life in Norwalk, CT and Willsboro, NY. He was employed by the New York Times for 35 years and retired as production manager in 1983. He is preceded in death by his wife, Margery Purcell. Survived by his daughter, Jean Walter, sons Thomas, James M. and Dennis, and nine grandchildren. Memorial service on August 2 at St. John's Episcopal Church, Essex, NY. Memorials can be sent to St. John's.
