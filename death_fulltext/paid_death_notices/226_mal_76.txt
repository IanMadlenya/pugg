
        GREGG--William Patrick. November 4, 2005. Beloved husband of Elizabeth. Loving father of Billy. Cherished grandfather of William. Devoted brother of Helen, Martin, John, the late Joseph, Katherine, Mary and Peter. A WWII veteran and dedicated and respected librarian at the New York Historical Society for 39 years. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Friends may call Monday and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 pm, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, November 9, 10 am, at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave at 84th St.
