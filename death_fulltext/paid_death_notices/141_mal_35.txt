
        FINCK-Marshall G., 78. Of West Palm Beach. Suddenly of acute leukemia. Former Chairman of Wilson Brothers, President of Enro Shirt Company. Highly decorated W.W. II Naval officer. Admired for his intellect and wit. Survived by his adoring wife Edie, loving son Robert, daughter Nancie Goldsmith & husband Stewart, brother Bud Finch, stepchildren Pat and Michael French, Jim, Tom and Jill Goodkind, and eight grandchildren. A memorial service Sunday, October 11, 2:00 PM at Breakers West Country Club.
