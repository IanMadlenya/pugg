
        LOEWY--Rosamond E. Of Manhattan, 82, passed away November 3rd following a lengthy illness. Graduate of Hunter College and Teachers College, Columbia University. Former teacher and guidance counselor in NYC for 35 years. Survivors include her brother Ben, niece Jane and nephew James. Services will be held at Campbells funeral home, 1076 Madison Avenue on Thursday, November 6th at 9:30 A.M. Friends may call at the funeral home prior to the service.
