
        HOUGH--Frances. Died January 8 at 91 in Tucson. She Lived in NYC most of her career in fashion advertising. After graduating from the University of Iowa as a Journalism major, she was fashion copy chief at Bloomingdale's and copy editor of Vogue Patterns and Knitting Magazines. Fran leaves many good friends and a sister, Mary Ann Hough, also of Tucson.
