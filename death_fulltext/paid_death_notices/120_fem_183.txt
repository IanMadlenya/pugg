
        WIESEN-Pearl, PhD from the University of Wisconsin (1962), was born in 1918 and died on Jan. 10, 1997. She worked in the legal department of the Schubert Org. and later graduated from the Dramatic Workshop of the New School (1947). She received her BA and MA from Hunter (1957-58). Her dissertation, Twelfth Night: A Stage History is in the Billy Rose Collection of the NY Library for the Performing Arts. She is survived by her brother, Bernard, of 2565 Creston Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90068.
