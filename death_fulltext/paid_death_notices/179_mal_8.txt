
        AARON-Stanley. Of Boca Raton, FL., and Cliffside Park, NJ died Sunday, November 25 of heart failure. A veteran of World War II, Mr. Aaron was a principal in the advertising firm Franklin Spier, Inc. from which he retired in 1982. He is survived by his wife of 58 years, Doris, his children Jeffrey I. Aaron and Jessica Hildreth and his grandchildren Elizabeth, Jonathan, Alyson and Danielle.
