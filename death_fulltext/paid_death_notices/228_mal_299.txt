
        BOSTWICK--Dunbar W. Died peacefully in his 99th year on January 25 at Shelburne, Vermont. Predeceased by Electra Webb Bostwick, his wife of forty-nine years, Jeanne Wight Bostwick, his wife of nine years and two daughters, Electra B. McDowell & Lillian B. Davis. Two daughters, Dundeen B. Catlin and Elliot B. Wood, thirteen grandchildren, and twenty-two great-grandchildren survive him. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in his memory to the Bostwick Garden Fund at the Shelburne Museum, Shelburne, VT.
