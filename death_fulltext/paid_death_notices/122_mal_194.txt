
        PARK-Halford Woodward. Age 78, died March 11. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Greene Park, brother of Helen Park Haskell. He is survived by five children Margaret, Lothian, Halford, George, and Dabney, seven grandchildren and a great-grandchild. Veteran of US Navy, WWII, attorney at the law firm of St. John, Park & Scott, Greenwich, CT. Services Saturday, March 22, at 1 PM, at St. Saviour's Episcopal Church, Old Greenwich, CT.
