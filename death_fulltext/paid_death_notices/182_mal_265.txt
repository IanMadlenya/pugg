
        TOPLITZ-George N. The partners, associates and staff of Greenberg, Trager & Herbst, LLP note with great sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague George Nathan Toplitz. An accomplished and admired advocate, George was a pillar of the firm, a great resource and widely respected by the legal community. We will miss his insight, collegiality and humor. Our deepest condolences to his family and friends.
