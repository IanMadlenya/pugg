
        BECKWITH-Herbert Lynes. On June 3, 1997. Age 94. Of Kingston, MA., formerly of Beacon Hill, Boston. Husband of Elizabeth (McMillan) Beckwith. His daughter by a previous marriage, Suzanne B. Bynum, of Cambridge, MA. Three grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. A graveside service will be held Monday, June 9th at 11AM in the Evergreen cemetery, Kingston, MA. At the request of the family, calling hours have been omitted. Friends wishing, memorial gifts may be given in Professor Beckwith's name to: The Cathedral Church of St. Paul, 138 Tremont St. Boston, MA. 02111.
