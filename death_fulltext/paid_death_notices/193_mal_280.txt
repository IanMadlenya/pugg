
        TUROFF-Michael, Dr. With deep sadness we mourn the passing of our Executive Director Emeritus, Past President and most honored member of this society, Dr. Michael Turoff. Dr. Turoff was a respected clinician and a true leader for the profession of dentistry. His career of service, which also included his tenure as General Chairman of the Greater NY Dental Meeting spanned five decades. Dr. Turoff's impact will be felt for years to come. He will be sorely missed. Officers & Brd of Trustees 2nd District Dental Society of New York.
