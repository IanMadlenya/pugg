
        COHEN--Edith. Formerly of Manhattan, NY, died in New London, CT. Born May 27, 1921 to Charles and Dorothy Ehrlich Cohen of Brooklyn, predeceased by a sister Helen Coburne. She was a career special education teacher at PS 121 Manhattan, and a summer counselor at Camp Waterford in Quaker Hill, CT. Contributions may be made to Waterford Country School, 78 Hunts Brook Rd, Quaker Hill, CT 06375. Neilan Funeral Home of New London, CT.
