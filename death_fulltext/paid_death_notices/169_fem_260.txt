
        DAVIDSON-Bobbie (Bertha) on February 26, 2001. A woman of valor. Predeceased by her lifelong love and husband Harry and by their beloved daughter Penny Esther Spiegel. Bobbie blessed the lives of her grandchildren and great-grandchildren, her sister and brothers, and her friends with unconditional love. She will always be remembered for the wisdom, humor, dignity and humility, kindness and respect that she bestowed on all who knew her. Graveside services today at 11:15 at the New Montefiore Cemetery, Weinstein, Garlick, Kirschenbaum Directors.
