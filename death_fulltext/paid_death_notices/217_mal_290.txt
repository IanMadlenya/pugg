
        REDDY--Michael T. 62, of Ramsey, died peacefully Saturday February 19, 2005. Michael was a loving husband, father, step-father and grandfather. Beloved husband of Alisa (nee Krinsky) Reddy for 7-1/2 years. Loving father of Catherine Anne Wright and her husband Neiland of Wyckoff, NJ and Michael Thomas Reddy Jr. and his wife Elaine of Westfield, NJ. Step-father of Alexander and Eric March. Adored grandfather of Daniel, Matthew, Grace, Michael III, Emily and the late Elizabeth. Brother of Sister Catherine Reddy, Mary Reichley, James and the late Patrick and Martin. Before retiring, he was Chairman and CEO of the EDS Global Securities Industry Group. Previously, he was a Senior Vice President of Merrill Lynch, Pierce, Fenner & Smith Incorporated and also spent 9 years in the Operations Division of J.P. Morgan & Co. He was the recipient of the 1998 Ellis Island Medal of Honor, appeared on The Today Show and was quoted in the Wall Street Journal, the New York Times, the Los Angeles Times and American Banker. He also testified before U.S. House and State committees addressing various securities industry matters. He was a graduate of Pace University and Harvard Business School's Advanced Management Program. He was also the author of the book, ''Securities Operations: A Guide to Operations and Information Systems in the Securities Industry.'' A Funeral Mass will be celebrated on Thursday February 24, 2005 at 10 AM at St. Paul RC Church in Ramsey. Interment will follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Valhalla, NY.
