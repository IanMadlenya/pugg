
        STONE-Jon. We believe there is no child in America who has not been enriched by the genius of our colleague, mentor and friend, Jon Stone, one of the original creators of Sesame Street. For twenty-nine years, his contribution to the enhancement of children's lives has been felt around the world. We will miss him and do our best to continue his vision. The Writers of Sesame Street
