
        LAZAR-Helen B. Yeshiva University and its Albert Einstein College of Medicine note with deep regret the passing of Helen B. Lazar, the beloved wife of the late Jack Lazar and a trustee of the Lazar Foundation. Through the Foundation, Mr. & Mrs. Lazar established a fellowship in cell biology at Einstein, were respected members of the College's Society of Founders & Guardians of the University. To her children, grandchildren and entire family, we offer our sincere sympathy. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers, Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura, Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
