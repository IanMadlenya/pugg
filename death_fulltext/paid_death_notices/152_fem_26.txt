
        ORDOVER-Carol M. Of Atlanta, GA. Died on September 1, 1999. She is survived by her husband Abraham Ordover. Sons, Andrew Ordover and his wife Heather of New York, and Thomas Ordover and his wife Linda of Chicago. Mother Vera Mensher, New York City, brother John Mensher and his wife Gail, Seattle WA. Mrs. Ordover taught fifth grade English at The Lovett School for seventeen years and is remembered fondly by a generation of students and fellow teachers as a friend, mentor, and role-model. Her fierce loyalty, strong opinions, and great wit made her a unique presence wherever she went. She will be missed by all of those blessed enough to have been able to call her friend. Memorial service to be held at Post Theater at The Lovett School on Monday September 6th, 1999 at 4:00 P.M. Cremation is private. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the Carol M. Ordover Memorial Schorlarship Fund, care of The Lovett School. The family will receive friends from 7 until 9 P.M. Thursday and Friday at 22 The Prada, Atlanta, GA. For further information contact Wages & Sons, Stone Mountain, GA. 770-469-9811
