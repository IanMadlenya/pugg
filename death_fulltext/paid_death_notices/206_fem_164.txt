
        SELEVAN--Bashie R. AMIT is deeply grieved by the passing of our beloved Honorary National Vice-President, Bashie R. Selevan, z''l, cherished wife of Irwin, z''l. A woman of deep compassion, she was a most generous benefactor whose philanthropy nurtured and saved thousands of poor Israeli children cared for by AMIT. She was a consummate lay leader whose extraordinary fund raising and speaking skills spearheaded many AMIT campaigns. We extend heartfelt condolences to her beloved children, Lois Goodstein and Alan Selevan, and to the entire family. Jan Schechter, National President Marvin Leff, Executive Director
