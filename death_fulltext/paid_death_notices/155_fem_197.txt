
        SYDNEY-Dora, died peacefully on December 20th, 1999 in her 98th year. Cherished & adored mother of Edith R. Sydney. Devoted wife of the late David. Your intellect, courage and wit have sustained me throughout my life. Graveside services Wednesday, 10:30 AM, at Mt. Judah Cemetery, Ridgewood, Queens. Contributions in her name may be made to Albert Einstein College of Medicine or Cabrini Hospice.
