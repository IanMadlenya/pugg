
        HERKNESS-Lindsay C., III. Of New York City. Age 58. Died September 11, 2001 in the attack on the World Trade Center. He was a Senior Vice President at Morgan Stanley. Survived by his brother, Wayne and dog, Gaston. A memorial service will be held at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave on Oct. 30 at 4 p.m. with a reception to follow at the Union Club. Donations may be made to Trinity College, Connecticut, or Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, Long Island.
