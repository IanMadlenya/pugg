
        BARBAGELATA--Harold R. 80, formerly of Setauket, NY, passed away March 30, 2006. Born and raised in New York City, he was predeceased by his beloved wife of 54 years, Betty Barbagelata and his parents Celia and Nicholas Barbagelata. He will be sadly missed by his daughter, Vivian Barbagelata Kitchen, son-in-law Ben Kitchen and two grandchildren, Stefanie and Richard Kitchen, all of Portland, VA.
