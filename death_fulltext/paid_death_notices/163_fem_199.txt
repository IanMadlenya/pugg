
        GOLDSCHLAGER-Edna (nee Cohen). Teacher and educational administrator. On August 27, 2000, at her home. Beloved wife of William, loving mother of Marcia Epstein (Paul) of Gladwine, PA, and Seth Goldschlager (Isabelle) of Paris, France. Grandmother of Amy and Leonard Feldman, Robin Epstein, Esther, Ezra and Eva Goldschlager. Great-grandmother of Madeline and Benjamin Feldman. Beloved sister of Roseline Dooreck. She was a devoted teacher and administrator at P.S. 173, Queens. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, August 29, at 1 PM, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel at 114-03 Queens Blvd. (76th Rd.), Forest Hills, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Seth Dooreck Book Fund, c/o Cristine Malhotra, NYU, 25 West 4th St., Room 526, NY, NY 10012 or a charity of your choice.
