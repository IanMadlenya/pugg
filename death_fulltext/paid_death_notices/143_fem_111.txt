
        SIMOWITZ-Rhoda. On December 7, 1998. Age 73. After a long illness. Beloved wife of 51 years of Sylvan. Loving mother of Carol Simowitz Mendelsohn and Lee. Grandmother of Mia and Jake. Devoted sister-in-law of Flo and Harold Feldman, and mother-inlaw of Martin Mendelsohn and Susan Reiter Simowitz. Warm and generous to all who met her, past President of Sisterhood, Manhattan Beach Jewish Center, Shirley Appelbaum Heart League honoree, past Chairwoman of the Brooklyn Women's Campaign, UJA/Federation. Her love of life will live within us forever. The Family
