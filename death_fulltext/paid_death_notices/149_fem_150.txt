
        LaPORTE-Anita. The Board of Directors of the Associated Builders and Owners of Greater New York notes with great saddness the passing of Anita LaPorte, mother of ABO's Executive Director Nicholas LaPorte, Jr. A beloved mother, doting grandmother, devoted sister, she died on June 15 after a brief illness. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her children, Nicholas LaPorte Jr. and Deborah Lumba, grandsons Matthew LaPorte and Nicholas Lumba and sister Esta Giordano. Michael Kerr, Arnold Sollar, Co-Chairmen Jerome Belson, President
