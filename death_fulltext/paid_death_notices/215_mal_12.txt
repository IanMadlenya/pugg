
        RUSKIN--Elliott on November 28, 2004 at the age of 92. Cherished and devoted husband of the late Pearl (nee Segal). Beloved father of Steven and Nancy, Ellen and Lucious. Loving grandfather of Lauren, Kenneth, Dyana, Susan and Jason. Loving great-grandfather of eight. A proud graduate of City College and Harvard Law School, he practiced law in New York City for over 50 years. A private service will be held in celebration of his life.
