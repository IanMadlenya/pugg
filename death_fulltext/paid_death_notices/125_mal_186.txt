
        BASS-Henry. Age 84, of White Plains, NY, and Lauderhill, FL. Devoted husband of 56 years of Minnie. Loving father and father-inlaw to Harriet and Andrew Schon, Lawrence and Sandra Bass and Joan and Marc Weiler. Adored grandfather of Lisa Bissell, Stephanie Solomon, Marisa and Amanda Bass, and Sara and Jesse Weiler. Caring brother of Sylvia Dworkin. Kind and compassionate, his good counsel will be missed by all his family, friends and former business associates. Services 11:30 AM, Tuesday, June 17, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mt. Vernon, NY. Donations to White Plains Hospital or Children's Blood Foundation would be appreciated.
