
        KRINSKY--Josephine B. 93, died in New York City on August 4,2006. Widow of Dr.Milton, mother of Robert D.(Carol), Edward B.(Roberta),William L.(Suzanne), and sister of Clifford A.Bachrach (Leona), grandmother of 8, great-grandmother of 7. BA Vassar '33, MLS Pratt '67 Beloved wife and mother and, for 10 years, associate librarian, Planned Parenthood Federation of America. No funeral service. Contributions may be made in her memory to Planned Parenthood or other charity.
