
        POSILLICO--Joseph D. Jr., on Sunday, December 21, at the age of 64, in his Huntington Bay Home. President of J.D., Posillico, Inc., Mr. Posillico was respected and admired by all in his field as an exceptional leader with a vast knowledge of the construction industry. His independent spirit and generosity were inspirational to all who touched his life. He has been a prominent member of the Long Island community for over 40 years. For the past 15 years, Joe served as President of J.D. Posillico, Inc., one of the most prestigious and largest heavy construction firms. He was a member of the Columbus Club and served on the Columbus Day Committee for the annual parade. Joe gave his time, energy and efforts to numerous charities and civil organizations including the Long Island Contractor's Association and Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Building Fund and Golf Outing. An avid golfer, Mr. Posillico was a member of the Huntington Crescent Club, North Fork Country Club, Gardiner's Bay Country Club, Jupiter Hills Country Club and Carnegie Abbey. He is survived by his beloved wife of 36 years, Whitney, and six children; Katherine Posillico McGowan, Joseph III, Wendy, Paul, Frank and Thomas Posillico. Cherished grandfather to Taylor McGowan and Grace Posillico. Joe is also survived by his two brothers, Dominic and Mario. Visitation is scheduled for Tuesday, 2-5 pm and 7-9 pm at M.A. Connell Funeral Home in Huntington. The Funeral Mass will be held 11 am Wednesday at St. Killian's Roman Catholic Church in Farmingdale. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Joseph D. Posillico Jr. Memorial Fund, c/o J.D. Posillico, Inc., 1610 New Highway, Farmingdale, New York 11735. We will miss him dearly and always remember him as a devoted husband, father, friend and a great man.
