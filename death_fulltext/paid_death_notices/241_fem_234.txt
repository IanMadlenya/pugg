
        MCDANNOLD--Nancy Knothe, 81, died peacefully at home on February 24th, 2007. She was predeceased by her son, Philip T. Stanley, and by her grandson Chester Griswold. She is survived by her children, Jennifer S. Beardsley, Robert C. Stanley III and Kathryn Griswold; by seven grandchildren, Seth, Kara, Philippa, Rebecca, Willy, Kitty and Emily Bo; and by one great-granddaughter, Kendall Grace. She was a loving mother and will be missed.
