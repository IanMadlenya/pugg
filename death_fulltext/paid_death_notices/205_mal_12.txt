
        LITWIN--Harry. Passed away on January 30, 2004. Beloved and cherished husband of Margo. Loving father of Daniel (Georgia) and Elissa. Adoring grandpa of Rebecca and Jessica. WWII veteran and practicing attorney for 53 years. He was a man of exceptional intelligence, honor and sparkling humor. His love will live in our hearts forever. Service Monday, 2:15 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in his memory to Schervier Nursing Care Center, Riverdale, NY.
