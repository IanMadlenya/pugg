
        CARTER--J. Michael. The Executive committee, Foundation Board of Directors and staff of the Metropolitan Golf Association mourn the passing of their Vice President, Michael Carter on March 10. His dedication to the game of golf, to his club, the Association and our charitable Foundation's junior golf programs will be missed. We offer our deepest sympathies to his wife, Mary Lou, his daughters, Kelly and Aly, and the entire Carter family. Donald J. Hendler, President Jay Mottola, Exec. Director
