
        GARY--Nancy E., M.D., 69, of Washington DC, died on May 31, 2006. A resident of Washington DC for 20 years, she lived for 25 years in Manhasset, NY. She is survived by her brothers, Walter Gary of Walla Walla, WA, Thomas Gary of Manhasset, NY, Peter Q. Gary of Springfield, VA and father Paul Gary of Charlotte, NC. Physician and friend, she had been Dean of the Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences, F. Edward Hebert School of Medicine. Visitation will be at the Church of the Immaculate Conception, 711 N Street, NW Washington, DC from 910 a.m., Monday, June 5, 2006 followed by a service at 10:00 a.m. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent in her memory to Duke University Pulmonary Hypertension Program, attention Dr. Victor Tapson, Room 351, Bell Building, Duke University Medical Center, Durham, NC 27710.
