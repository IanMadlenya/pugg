
        GANS-Maurice G. Attorney Extraordinaire, died peacefully at home on July 28, 2001. Son of the late Judge Julius and Sylvia Gans. Loved dearly by his wife Carol; daughters and sons-in-law, Linda and Jack Juppe, Judy and Michael Nash, Patrice and Howard Winston, Keri and Bart Labiner; son and daughter-in-law John and Arlene; daughter-in-law Kathleen Carroll; grandchildren John, Jennifer, Erik, Marc and Morgan. Predeceased by son Richard. Funeral at ''Gutterman-Musicant,'' 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ, on Tuesday, July 31, 2001, at noon.
