
        MAPLE-John Edward (Jack). At age 48 after a long illness, peacefully, on August 4. Beloved husband of Brigid O'Connor. Loving and caring father of Jacqueline, Brendan and Breen. Survived by three sisters and three brothers. Dedicated over 30 years of his life to law enforcement. A loyal member of the Transit Police Department for 23 years and a former Deputy Commissioner of the New York City Police Department. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Tuesday and Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, 3 PM, at Holy Cross R.C. Church, 329 W. 42 St, NYC. Donations can be made to the Police Athletic League.
