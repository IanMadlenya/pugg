
        KLINGER-Dr. Harold Edward. Of New York City. 5 March 1918-16 August 2001. Beloved husband of Constance. Dear father of Bettina, Stefan, Jacqueline, Roxanne, and David. Decorated World War II Officer. Devoted his life to the practice of medicine and humanity for which he will always be remembered. During his tenure at LeRoy Hospital he served as Director of Medical Education and The Credentials Committee. He had the extraordinary power to touch people so deeply, a gift that made him unique among men. Memorial services Thursday August 23rd at 10 A.M. at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street. Friends who wish may contribute to The American Lung Association, New York Chapter, 432 Park Avenue South, 8th Floor, New York, New York 10016.
        KLINGER-Dr. Harold Edward. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Dr. Harold Edward Klinger. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
