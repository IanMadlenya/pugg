
        ELKINS--Robert Charles, 89, died April 11, 2007, in Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Lawrence, NY. Artist, poet and family man extraordinaire. He will be greatly missed by his wife, Geraldine, his loving daughter, Joanne Ranzer, and son-in-law David; and his cherished granddaughters, Cori and Karen. A memorial service is planned.
