
        GUITERMAN-Richard L. Colonel Retired, (USAF), of Park Avenue, New York. Died at St. Luke's Hospital on July 2, 2001. Survived by his dear wife Eleanor, her daughter Sara, son-in-law Scott Moyer, daughter Susan Robie, grandchildren Robie Moyer and K. Arden Moyer and by his sister, Marjorie Morris of West Lafayette, Indiana. Colonel Guiterman retired from the U.S. Air Force in November 1978, after serving more than 37 years on active and reserve duty. He was a Vice President of Chemical Bank in the Corporate Banking Division and also a Vice President of Amercian Express Bank and Director of International Corporate Banking. Colonel Guiterman was a Director of the New York's Finest Foundation and a member of the University Club. A memorial service will be held at the 7th Regiment Armory, 643 Park Avenue, New York City, on Wednesday, July 11, 2001, at 12 noon. Interment will be at Arlington National Cemetery, Arlington, Virginia, on July 25, 2001, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the University of Wisconsin in memory of the deceased.
        GUITERMAN - Richard. New York's Finest Foundation salutes our loyal, longtime Director, Colonel Richard Guiterman. His loss will be felt by all law enforcement agencies in and around New York City to whose welfare he dedicated himself. Our organization will never be the same without him. Our condolences to his wife, Eleanor, and family.
