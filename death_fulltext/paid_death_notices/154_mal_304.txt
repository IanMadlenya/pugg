
        ESIASON-Norman H.. On November 25, 1999, of East Islip, NY. WW II veteran and Columbia University graduate. Beloved husband of the late Irene Thumer. Loving father of Robin, Susan and Norman J. Grandfather of five. Brother of Roy and Verne. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, on Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral service Wednesday, Christ Episcopal Church, Manhasset. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to Boomer Esiason Foundation for Cystic Fibrosis, One World Trade Center, 101st floor, NYC 10048.
