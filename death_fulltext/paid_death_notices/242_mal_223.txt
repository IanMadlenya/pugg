
        STAVROPOULOS--Theo, artist and teacher, died Sunday, March 11, 2007 at his home on Chebeague Island, Maine. He was born in Athens, Greece, studied art at the Ecoles des Beaux Arts in Paris, France and at Yale with Josef Albers. For over 20 years, he taught at Lehman College in the Bronx. He exhibited widely and successfully. He is survived by his wife, Susan, his daughter, Dominique Williams and his son-in-law, Claudio, a sister, Marie and other relatives in Greece. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be sent to: American Parkinson Disease Assoc., 1250 Hylan Blvd. Suite 4B, Staten Island, NY 10305.
