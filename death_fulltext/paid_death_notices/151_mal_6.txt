
        LYNN-Victor M. On July 28, 1999. Beloved husband of Raquelle, father of Nicholas and Giannina. His extended family included daughters Barbara and Wendy, and sons-in-law Gary and Chris and grandchildren Lauren, David and Jenny. He had a productive and successful career as an account executive at a number of prominent New York advertising agencies. Later he was a Senior V.P. of Marketing and Planning at R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Company, from which he retired in 1989. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St on Tuesday, August 3, 3:30 P.M., when friends & family will celebrate an extraordinarily beloved man and a life filled with intellect and compassion. Donations in his honor may be sent to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY.
