
        TANG-GOODWIN-Hope Babette, M.D. The Babies Hospital Alumni Association mourns the premature loss of its member Hope Babette Tang-Goodwin, M.D. As a young faculty member at Babies & Children's Hospital of New York, she was admired as an ideal physician. She was dedicated to her patients, research and young family. She will be remembered as an intelligent, enthusiastic, and courageous individual. With deepest sympathies to her family. Stephanie A. Green, M.D. President 1997-1998
