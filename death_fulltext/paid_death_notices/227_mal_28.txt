
        COLE--Barry G. Suddenly on November 28, 2005, beloved friend of Frances A. Cole, father of Tracy Cole Barker, and Kelly Cole Dobson, grandfather of Allison and Megan Barker. Distinguished Professor of Communications, philanthropist and friend of the arts. His loss will be widely felt in the communities he touched and by the friends, colleagues and loved ones he left behind. Friends may call at Joseph Gawler's Sons, 5130 Wisconsin Avenue NW, Washington, D.C. on Sunday December 4th from 11 A.M. until time of service at 11:30. Donations may be made in his memory to the charity of your choice.
