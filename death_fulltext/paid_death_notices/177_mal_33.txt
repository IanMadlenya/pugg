
        BATES-Steven J. At age 42 of Glendale, New York. Lieutenant, Engine Company 235, Brooklyn. In the line of duty at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. A memorial service celebrating his life, will be offered at the Redeemer Lutheran Church, 69-07 Cooper Avenue, Glendale on Saturday, October 6, 2001 at 1:00PM. In lieu of flowers, donations are requested to either the New York Firefighters Burn Center Foundation, 21 Asch Loop, Bronx, NY 10475, or the Widows and Orphans Fund of the Uniformed Firefighters Association, 204 East 23rd Street, New York City, NY 10010.
