
        FRIEDMAN-Morris R. On February 14, 2003, of Brooklyn, Belle Harbor and Kingston, NY. Born January 4, 1912, Tarnow, Poland to Aaron and Manya, he escaped the chaos of W.W. I with his mother and sister Fay when Queen Wilhelmina of Holland provided passage to NY. Morris worked 55 years for Barclay Knitwear and reigned for 16 years as dean of trivia and poetry at the Council Center. Predeceased by his beloved wife of 53 years, Ethel, and daughter, Florence, he is survived by children, Robert, Richard and Deborah, and seven grandchildren. Service: February 28, 2:30 PM, Parkside Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn.
