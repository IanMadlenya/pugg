
        MEAD-Edna Hawelka. Died Saturday, August 18, 2001 at Somers Manor Nursing Home, Somers, NY. Mrs. Mead was a retired New York State Insurance Broker, life member of the Bronx Artists' Guide, life member of the Bronx County Historical Society and founding member of the Bronx Museum of the Arts and author of ''The Bronx Triangle: a Portrait of Norwood''. Born June 13, 1903 in New York City, she married Clarence Watson Mead who passed away on March 18, 1975. Survivors include two daughters, Louise Mead Tricard and son-inlaw John, of Cape Canaveral, FL; Jane Mead Peter of South Salem, NY; one grandchild Jonathan Mead Peter of South Salem and several nephews, grand nephews and nieces. Calling hours will be at Clark Assoc Funeral Home, Katonah, NY Wednesday 2-4 & 7-9pm. Service will be held Thursday, 11am at St. John's Episcopal Church, South Salem. Interment will follow at Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Bronx Historical Society, 3266 Bain Bridge Avenue, Bronx, New York 10467.
