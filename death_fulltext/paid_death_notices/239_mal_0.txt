
        DRESSNER--Sam. Park Avenue Synagogue mourns the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Irene and the entire family our heart-felt sympathy. May his memory remain for a blessing. David H. Lincoln Senior Rabbi Amy A. B. Bressman Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft President
        $(6$)DRESSNER--Sam, age 88, of New York City died peacefully on November 29th. He was associated with his family's business, All-in-One Suppliers, working until he was 87-years-old. Sam will be missed by Irene, his wife of 62 years, his son Ira and Ira's wife Liana, along with their sons Phillip and Andrew. Sam was also loved by his son Joe and Joe's wife Denyse and their children Jules and Alyce. Sam will be remembered by his sister Sylvia, many nephews and nieces along with family friends. Donations, if desired, to Doctors without Borders.
