
        GORDON-Jay. Of San Diego, CA, formerly of Great Neck, NY, died on February 2, 2002, at his home with his adoring family around him. Beloved soul mate of Sophia and devoted & dedicated father of James, Janina, and son-in-law, Jason. He was a professor of history at Queens College of the City University of New York for 33 years and will always be in the hearts and memories of his loving family.
