
        GASTELLI-Erica. Died after a brief illness. Artistic Administrator for Spoleto Festival USA since 1979. Erica was an extraordinary friend to young musicians as well as conductors. Her elegance, spirit and sensitivity to musical talent are irreplaceable and we will miss her deeply. Memorial service on 4/29 at 3pm at Blessed Sacrament, 152 W. 71st Street. Donations may be made to the Erica Gastelli Memorial Fund for Young Musicians c/o Spoleto Festival USA. Steven Sloane, Music Director, Opera & Orchestra Nunally Kersh, Festival Producer
