
        WEISL--Edwin L., Jr. The Trustees and staff of The Metropolitan Museum of Art mourn the loss of this devoted benefactor and good friend, who for more than three decades generously supported the Museum and played an important role in the Robert Lehman Foundation's work with the Metropolitan. We extend our condolences to his family, colleagues, and friends. James R. Houghton Chairman Philippe de Montebello Director Emily K. Rafferty President
