
        EASTMOND-Christine. On December 14th at age 80. Graduate of North Carolina State College. Esteemed teacher and advocate for children. Predeceased by her beloved husband Maurice eleven months ago. Adored mother of Carolyn Nash & husband Douglas Nash, cherished grandmother of Jennifer Nash. Mourned also by brother-in-law Fred Eastmond. She will be deeply missed by all whose lives she touched.
