
        SOLOMON--Doris. On November 26, 2003. At age 81 after a brief illness. Adored wife of Max, cherished mother of Steven, Susan and the late Cindy. Beloved mother-in-law of Bess Miller and devoted grandmother of David and Matthew. Services Friday at 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations in her memory may be made to The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund.
        SOLOMON--Doris. The Board of Directors and staff of Greater New York Mutual Insurance Company record with deep sorrow the passing of Doris Solomon, beloved wife of Max Solomon, our retired Executive Vice President and Board member. We extend our deepest condolences to Max, his children and grandchildren. Warren W. Heck, Chairman and CEO Dominick Vicari, President
