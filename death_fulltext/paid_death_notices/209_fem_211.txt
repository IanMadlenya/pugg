
        FELDSTEIN--Martha. Died on June 27 at age 79. Beloved wife of Sidney. Devoted mother of Wally and his wife Marilyn and Haila and her husband David. Proud grandmother of Ilysa and Daniel. Cherished sister of Alan. Radiated exuberance, always had a song in her heart which she shared with others. She will be missed dearly and live in our memories forever. Services today, at 12 noon, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
