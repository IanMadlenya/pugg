
        BROWN-Austin J. Of Emerson, New Jersey, former employee of The American Lung Association, former Country Director of The United States Peace Corp (Uganda and Gambia) Africa Service Institute, the African American Institute and the Institute of International Education. Died Saturday, February 9, 2002. He is survived by his wife, Sonia, of Bergen Community College, and his son, Peter. Family will receive friends on Friday, February 15, from 3-5 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Funeral Mass Saturday, 10 AM, at the Assumption Church, 29 Jefferson Ave., Emerson, NJ. Entombment to follow at the Garden of Memories, Paramus, New Jersey. There will be an opportunity for further viewing at 9:30 AM prior to the mass at the church.
