
        ARMSTRONG-John Kenaston, 73, of Bloomfield, CT., and Chatham, Massachusetts died on September 12, 2002. Former Executive Vice President of CIGNA Corporation and President of CIGNA Investment Group. Survived by his wife Katharine (Kipp), daughter Leigh Hovey and her husband David, son Kipp Armstrong and his wife Patricia and five grandchildren, Davey, Tommy and Katie Hovey and Cassidy and Magnolia Armstrong and brothers Richard and Robert. Services will be Wednesday, September 18, 11 A.M. at Asylum Hill Congregational Church, Hartford, CT. Memorial donations to Seabury Foundation, 200 Seabury Drive, Bloomfield, Connecticut 06002.
