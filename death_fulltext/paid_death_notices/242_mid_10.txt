
        FISHMAN--Sylvia (Shemel) age 90, of Aventura, FL. formerly of NYC, NY, passed away March 19, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Seymour Fishman. Loving mother of Steven and Judith. Dear sister of Helene Susman and the late Sidney Shemel. Cherished grandmother of Laura and Stephanie and great grandmother of Jordan, Sydney, Alexander and Lia. She was the perfect partner to Seymour for over 60 years as he pursued various careers, last as Executive Director of the American Friends of the Hebrew University. The family will receive friends at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, Aventura, Florida, Sunday, April 1, between 1:00 and 4:00 pm. For information call 1-800-327-9192. In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial contributions to Macular Degeneration Research Fund c/o Susan Ervin, Bascom Palmer Eye Institute, 900 NW 17th St., Miami, FL 33136, or American Friends of the Hebrew University, 1 Battery Park Plaza, 25th Floor, New York, NY 10004.
