
        COOPER--Warren Peters. Of Delaware Township, NJ on November 5. Son of the late William J. and Lanita Peters Cooper. He was a 1950 graduate at Yale University. He served in the U.S. Navy in the Korean War. He was formerly Chief Actuary for the State of New Jersey, a retired principal at Ernst & Young, LLP and also was employed by CIGNA and The Chubb Group. He was an avid birder and amateur gourmet chef. He is survived by his domestic partner and companion of 40 years, Jerald Stowell, a brother, William B., and niece and three nephews. Interment will be Friday, 2PM in Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Memorial contributions NJ Conservation Foundation, 170 Longview Road, Far Hills, NJ 07931 or Princeton Hospice, 208 Bunn Drive, Princeton, NJ 08540.
