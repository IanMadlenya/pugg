
        BURG-WIENER-Gladys. On February 7, 2003. Daughter of the late Sadie and Samuel Matzke. Born in Brooklyn, in 1913, passed away on February 7, just shy of her 90th birthday. She survived both of her husbands: Harry Wiener and Morris Burg. She will be missed by all of her dear friends. Services today, February 9, at 9:45 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
