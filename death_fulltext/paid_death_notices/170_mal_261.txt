
        MacQUEEN-Ewan Campbell Sr. Born on January 19th, 1916 in Mendham Township, New Jersey, son of John Ewan and Margaret Winter MacQueen. After completing high school in Ohio, he graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology and the Law School of New York University. He spent the majority of his working life with the International Nickel Company, where he headed its patents section. Before moving to Coral Gables Florida ten years ago, he resided in Piermont, N.Y. He served as a vestryman of Grace Church, Nyack, N.Y. and of the Church of the Venerable Bede, Coral Gables, FL. He was a member of the Country Club of Coral Gables and the 10th-Holers. He is survived by his wife of 59 years, Martha & their four children Julie D. Berlin, Coral Gables, FL., Ewan C. MacQueen, Jr, West Windsor, N.J., Michael C. MacQueen, College Park, Md., Molly MacQueen, Piermont, N.Y. and five grandchildren. Memorial contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Audubon Society or to the National Heart Association.
