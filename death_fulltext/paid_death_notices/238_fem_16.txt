
        MEHLMAN--Frances (Berman, Lane, Krebs), 85, of San Antonio, TX, formerly of Sands Point, NY and Coconut Creek, FL. She died peacefully on November 1, following a long struggle with Alzheimer's Disease. Beloved mother of Dr. Gary Lane and Dr. April Lane Benson, she was also the grandmother of David, Kate, Cheryl, Eric, and Corey, and great-grandmother of Colin and Alia. A funeral service will be held at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave and 65th St, NYC, on Tuesday November 7 at 11:00am. Interment at King Solomon Cemetery, Clifton, NJ will follow immediately. The family requests that any donations be made to the Alzheimer's Association of New York.
