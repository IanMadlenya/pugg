
        PEACE--John H., 81, of Purchase, NY and Fort Lauderdale, FL, died on October 22, 2005, at Phelps Memorial Hospital in Sleepy Hollow, NY. He was born on November 20, 1923 to Thomas and Ella (nee Curry) Peace in NYC. John served in the US Army during World War II. He was an advertising executive and Third Chairman of the Board for William Esty Company NYC retiring in 1990. John was the owner of several graded stakes winning Thoroughbreds and was on the board of the New York State Racing Assoc. and Blood Horse Magazine. He was also a member of The Sky Club, in NYC and Sleepy Hollow Country Club, in Briarcliff Manor, NY and Coral Ridge Country Club in Fort Lauderdale, FL. He was the founding benefactor of The Arnold P. Gold Foundation. John is survived by his wife Agnes (nee Cross) Peace of Purchase, NY; three sons John H. Peace, Jr. of Hawthorne, NY, William J. Peace of Katonah, NY, James M. Peace of Darien, CT; three daughters Kathleen Boncuk, of Huntington Beach, CA, Ellen Burke of Punta Gorda, FL. and Mary Anne Gallant of Hawthorne, NY. Also survived by fourteen grandchildren, and three great grandchildren. Predeceased by two brothers Thomas and Donald Peace. No Visiting Hours. Funeral Mass Saint Teresa of Avila Church, Sleepy Hollow, NY on Wednesday, 10 A.M. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to The Belmont Childcare Association, 2150 Hempstead Turnpike, Elmont, NY 11003 would be appreciated.
