
        PHILLIPS-Robert Murr. Of Stockbridge, MA, formerly of Cincinnati, OH., and New York, N.Y., on June 29, 1999. Cherished husband of Sue Ochs Phillips. Loving & nurturing father of Scott and Debbie and Pepper & Valerie; special person in the lives of Larry and Lynn, Jacki & Jan, and Carol and Bruce. Doting grandfather of Callie, Grace, Sophia, Nellie, and Micah. Treasured brother of Ann and Bob Friedman. Everyone will miss his gentle caring and wry humor. A Service Thursday, July 1, 1999, at 11AM at Hevreh of Southern Berkshire. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, New York.
