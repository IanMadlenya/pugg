
        WILSON-Eleanor D. ''Siddy'', 93, actress and artist, died peacefully after a year-long struggle with lung cancer on May 31, 2002 at her home in Williamstown, MA. A memorial service will be held Monday at 1:00 PM at St. John's Episcopal Church, 35 Park St, Williamstown, MA. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to The Actors' Fund of America, 729 Seventh Avenue, New York, NY 10019.
