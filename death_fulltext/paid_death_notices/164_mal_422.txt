
        MAHONEY-Robert, of Bridgehampton, N.Y. On September 10, 2000 in his 69th year. Survived by his loving wife Helene, and several nieces and nephews. He was the Superintendent of Manhattan Sites for the Dept. of the Interior and received the Founders Award for development of the Gateway National Recreation Area. Memorial services will be held on Oct. 7, at the Queen of The Most Holy Rosary Church, 2350 Montauk Highway Bridgehampton at 1:00 p.m. and on Nov. 2, at 5:30 p.m. at Federal Hall, 26 Wall Street, New York City. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Robert Mahoney Memorial Fund, C/O Queen of The Most Holy Rosary Church, P.O. Box 3035, Bridgehampton, N.Y. 11932 or the Dominican Sisters Family Health Services, P.O. Box 1028, Hampton Bays, N.Y. 11946
