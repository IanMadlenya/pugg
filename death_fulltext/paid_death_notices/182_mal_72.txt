
        BLASS-Harold. An attorney for 62 years in White Plains, NY, passed away on Saturday, March 2, 2002. Harold was a devoted father, grandfather and husband, and considered family his top priority. He is surived by his wife Bertha, his daughters, Penny Kramon and Lynda Safron and four grandchildren, Charles Safron, Carrie Safron, Brian Innes and Kyle Innes. Donations may be made to the Jewish Guild for the Blind.
