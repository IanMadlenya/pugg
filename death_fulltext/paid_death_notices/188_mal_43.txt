
        SCHLECHTER-Herbert D. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Herbert Schlechter, beloved father of Bruce Schlechter, a dear friend of UJA-Federation. Bruce is an associate board chairman of the Mosholu - Montefiore Community Center, a beneficiary agency of UJA-Federation, and an active member of several committees at UJA - Federation, including the real estate, job development, and planning and allocations committees. Bruce and his wife, Susan, a member of UJA-Federation's Task Force on Jewish Women and the Fund for Jewish Education, are dedicated to supporting Jewish and humanitarian causes. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Bruce and Susan; their children, Anne and Alan; and to the entire Schlechter family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        SCHLECHTER-Herbert. The Board of Trustees and the entire Abraham Joshua Heschel School community note with sorrow the passing of Herbert Schlechter on Thursday, August 29th, 2002. We extend our condolences to his son, Bruce Schlechter, who has been an extraordinary friend to our school and to his wife, Susan, who is a member of our board. Bruce and Susan have demonstrated a deep concern & commitment for many different aspects of our students' education. Shira Nadich Levin, President Roanna Shorofsky, Head of School
