
        SCAVULLO-Miriam Duffy. A kind and gentle woman, wife of the late Victor Kiernan Scavullo, on September 15th at home in Rye, New York. Her five children, eleven grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren deeply mourn her passing. Memorial donations may be directed to The Osborn, 101 Theall Road, Rye, New York 10580.
