
        FREUNDLICH-Ralph B. (aka Ray Friendly). Age 84 of Bisbee, Arizona. Formerly of New York City on September 2, 1997, after a protracted illness. Musician, teacher, composer, artist, writer and advocate. Loving brother of Jules of Minneapolis, Minnesota and the late Irwin. Devoted brother-in-law of Lillian Freundlich and the late Miriam Freundlich. He is also survived by nephews Roger Freundlich of Espoo, Finland and Douglas Freundlich of Arlington, Massachusetts, niece Laura Muessig of Minneapolis, two grand nieces and six grand nephews. Memorial service at Philadelphia Hotel, 19 0K Street in Bisbee at 7 PM, September 20, 1997.
