
        KAPULER--Sylvia Bell, 86, died January 29, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Dr. William P. Kapuler, she is survived by her son Alan, his wife Linda, and their three daughters Kusra, Serena, and Dylana; by her daughter Genevieve, her husband Gianni, and their son Matthew; and by her daughter Kathryn, her husband David, and their daughters Sarah and Emily.
