
        PERLES-Julia. On July 1, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Howard. Devoted sister, loving aunt, great aunt & cherished friend. Graveside service Friday, 11 A.M., at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Memorial service to be announced.
        PERLES-Julia. She will be missed by all of us who practice matrimonial law. She was known as ''the Mother of Equitable Distribution Law'', as she was the prime mover behind the 1980 change in the divorce law of New York State when the ''equitable distribution'' law was enacted. Her efforts on behalf of the Bar and the public will not be forgotten. The Family Law Section New York State Bar Association
        PERLES-Julia. The New York Chapter of the American Academy of Matrimonial Lawyers wishes to express its sorrow on the passing of our former fellow, known to all as ''the mother'' of the Equitable Distribution Law. Alton L. Abramowitz, President
