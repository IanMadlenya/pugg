
        SAVITZKY-Edith. Of Lake Worth, FL and New Hyde Park, NY. Died March 16. Beloved wife of Abraham Savitzky. Born in New York City, her love of husband, children and grandchildren was the center of her life. Choosing family over a promising opera career, she enriched the lives of all who knew her by her beautiful singing, quick wit, ready humor, her deep compassion and understanding. She is survived by son Robert, daughter Judy Roth and grandchildren Gregory Travis and Diana, Amanda and Steven Savitzky. Services to be held at Sinai Chapel, 12 noon Monday at 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadow, NY. She will live in our hearts forever.
