
        MOSKOVITZ KAPLAN-Miriam Schumer. Died on Shavuot. Beloved mother of Stephen and Ira. Loving grandmother of Joshua and Daniel. Devoted and generous aunt. Died peacefully at her home after a prolonged illness. A business woman of great dignity whose courage, compassion and style inspired all who knew and loved her. Services are on Wednesday, 1:30 PM from Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Boulevard (76th Road), Forest Hills, New York.
