
        SENEGAS-Helen, 85, died July 8, from cancer of the pancreas. She attended the Ethical Culture School, the Fieldston School, and Yale Art School. She was a commercial artist and life long resident of New York City. She leaves two daughters, Jean Senegas and Kay Gottesman, and two grandsons Evan and Brian Gottesman. A private funeral was held. Memorial contributions may be sent to Jacob Perlow Hospice, Beth Israel Medical Center, 1st Ave. at 16th St., NY, NY 10003.
