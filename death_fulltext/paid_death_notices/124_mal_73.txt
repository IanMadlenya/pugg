
        SUKENICK-Martin, former president of Broadway Bank Trust in Paterson, NJ, died March 13, 1997 in Salem, MA. He was 79. Mr. Sukenick joined the bank in 1945 and quickly moved up the ranks to become its vice president, president and in 1968, chairman of the board. He was a key figure in the building of Passaic County Community College and the new elementary school in Clifton, NJ. Surviving are his wife Helen, a daughter, Carol Phillips of Swampscott, MA. and 2 grandchildren, Maddie and Sammy. He was father of the late Leslie Sukenick. Services were held March 18 in Salem, MA.
