
        ZUGER-Bernard, M.D. The faculty and staff of the Dept of Psychiatry of the NYU School of Medicine mourn the passing of our dear colleague Dr. Bernard Zuger on April 19, 1998. Dr. Zuger, a clinical Associate Professor of Psychiatry, joined the faculty in 1958 specializing in Pediatric Psychiatry. To his entire family, we at the School of Medicine extend our heartfelt sympathy. Robert Cancro, M.D., Professor and Chairman, Dept of Psychiatry, NYU School of Medicine
