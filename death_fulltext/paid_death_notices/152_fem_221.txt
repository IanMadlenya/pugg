
        PAGE-Roberta R. Daughter of Mr. & Mrs. Malcome D. Raworth of Providence, R.I. She lived in Lawrence, Long Island prior to moving to Hilton Head Island, SC in 1987. Survived by her husband, Shelby H. Page of Hilton Head Island; a son Frank C. Page, daughter-in-law Ingred W. Page and grandson Walter S. Page all of Jarkata, Indonesia. A memorial service will be held at 3 P.M. Wednesday at All Saints Episcopal Church. Memorial contributions to All Saints Episcopal Church, 3001 Meeting Street, Hilton Head Island, S.C. 29926. Information: The Island Funeral Home of Hilton Head Island, SC.
