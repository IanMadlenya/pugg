
        BARTON-James T. The Board of Trustees, alumni, residents and staff of International House note with great sorrow the passing of Jim Barton, our beloved colleague and friend. His contributions as a Trustee, alumnus and Chairman of our World Council, along with his wisdom, warmth and lively wit, will be greatly missed. Our deep sympathy goes to his wife Denise and to his family. Paul A. Volcker, Chairman Donald L. Cuneo, Pres.
