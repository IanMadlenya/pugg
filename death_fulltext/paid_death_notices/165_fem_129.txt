
        LOWE-Jeanette Marmott. With heavy hearts and profound sadness we mourn the loss of Jeanette Marmott Lowe (Beauty), October 8, 2000. Precious mother of Gail Lowe Maidman (Richard), devoted grandmother of Starr Elizabeth Haymes and Evan Adam Haymes, sister of Edith Bloch, Perry Zitter, and the late Ernest Marmott, daughter of the late Louis George and Esther (Blumenthal) Marmott. We will miss our Beauty and the tender, loving, kindness she showed us. Shiva at Gail's home today, October 12, 11AM to 1PM and 4PM to 7PM. Concluding Friday 11AM to 4 PM. Contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
        LOWE-Jeanette Marmott. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of the mother of its esteemed member, Mrs. Richard Maidman, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi, David Yagoda, Chairman, Dr. William Schwartz, President
