        HOROWITZ--Margaret. The Trustees and staff of The Metropolitan Museum of Art mourn the death of Margaret Horowitz who, with her husband Raymond, was a benefactor of the Museum since 1975. The Horowitzes' passionate appreciation of American art, especially American Impressionism, has inspired the curators, contributed to the growth of the collection, and enhanced our visitors' experience. We extend to Raymond and his family our deepest sympathy. James R. Houghton Chairman Philippe de Montebello Director Emily K. Rafferty President
        HOROWITZ--Margaret. The partners and staff of Graubard Miller deeply regret the death of Margaret Horowitz, wife of our esteemed founding partner, Raymond J. Horowitz, and convey our deepest sympathy to Ray and their daughter, Judy. Margaret was a constant support to Ray during their more than 64 years of marriage and with him shared a love for American Impressionist art, a field in which they are renowned. She will be greatly missed.
