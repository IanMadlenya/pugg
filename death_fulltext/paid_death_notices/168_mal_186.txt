
        SHERMAN-Neil. Age 38, suddenly, on January 11, 2001. Beloved fiance of Donna Voss. Devoted son of Edward and Miriam Sherman of Potomac, MD. Loving brother of Pamela and Peter (Marianne), uncle to Sarah, Maxwell and William. Neil worked for the law firm Abelman, Frayne & Schwab. Funeral services 11:45 AM, Tuesday, January 16, at ''The Riverside'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. Service will also be held on Thursday, January 18, at Danzansky-Goldberg Memorial Chapels, Rockville, MD. In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to the Epilepsy Foundation of America.
