
        DUNCAN - Barbara Doyle. Latin American scholar, curator and collector died at 82. She is survived by her husband of 62 years, John C. Duncan; daughters Lynn Tarbox of Tampa, FL, and Gale Simmons of New York City; son Craig Duncan of Greenwich, CT; five grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Memorial services will be held on Thursday, April 3, at 11 AM, at St. Bartholomew's Church, 109 East 50th Street, New York City. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made to the Scholarship Fund, International House, 500 Riverside Drive, New York, NY 10025.
