
        LADENSON--Irene Akhimoff. Died this past winter, on January 15, 2005, in New York City. Born in the Bronx on January 22, 1931, she attended the University of Pennsylvania and the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts. She worked as a restorer of paintings and photographs, and was a longtime volunteer at the Riverside Branch of the New York Public Library. Her husband, Sam, died in 2004. She is survived by her daughter, Elisabeth.
