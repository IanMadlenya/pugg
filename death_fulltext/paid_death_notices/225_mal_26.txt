
        GILMAN--Bert E. (Bob). October 2, 2005. Beloved husband of Eileen. Loving father of Laurrien, Richard, Jon (Brad) and Jules (Michael). Adoring grandfather of Mira. Lifelong business partner and devoted brother to Aaron and his wife, Betty. Uncle to Lynda, Mark and Bill. Past president of Northwest Fisheries, one of the founders of Universal Seafoods and Intersea Fisheries. Former vice president of Vita Foods. Passionate philanthropist, avid reader, sportsman, and lover of life and his family. He was a sensitive, generous and caring man who always went out of his way to help others. His dignity and great integrity was an inspiration to all who knew him. He will be greatly missed. Contributions in his memory may be made to God's Love We Deliver or the charity of your choice.
