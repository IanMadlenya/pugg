
        KIVELSON -- Arnold J. A unique, creative genius who always saw opportunity and pursued it doggedly. Gifted tennis player and golfer. He will be missed by all those he touched as he touched so many of us in his own special way. Beloved husband of the late Clare F. Kivelson. He is survived by his children Karen (Steven), Nancy (Tom), and Kathy (Robert); his loving companion Elaine Tolchin; his grandchildren Ashlie (Ron), Alexis, Jordan, Jason, Alex and Lauren; his sister Nina, and sisters-in-law Carol and Margy. Funeral services Riverside Chapel, 11:45, Sunday July 31. Donations may be made to Mt. Sinai Visiting Doctors' Program and Jacob Perlow Hospice.
