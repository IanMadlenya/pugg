
        DESKIN-Claire. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Claire Deskin, beloved mother of Michael Steinhardt. Michael and his wife, Judy, are cherished friends and supporters of UJA-Federation. They are admired by colleagues and friends for their exceptional generosity in helping those in need, particularly their fellow Jews, through their contributions to numerous beneficiaries within the UJA-Federation network of agencies. Michael and Judy's tremendous devotion to the Jewish community has enriched numerous organizations that are engaged in sustaining and improving Jewish life. Their unwavering commitment and tireless efforts have resulted in significantly strengthening the infrastructure and econonomy of the State of Israel, helping to ensure its stability. The Steinhardts are dedicated communal leaders whose philanthropic commitment fosters Jewish renaissance throughout the world. Their legendary contributions to Jewish arts and culture, education, campus life in New York and overseas will be felt for generations to come. We extend our deepest sympathies to Michael and Judy; to Claire's grandchildren, David, Daniel, Sara; to her greatgrandchildren; and to her entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay Exec. V.P. and C.E.O.
