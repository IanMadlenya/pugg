
        DWYER-Albert Hayden. Of Demarest, NJ, former general counsel and vice president of the Children's Television Workshop and a CBS attorney for nearly two decades, died on August 8, 2000 at the age of 79. He was the beloved husband of Laura Campbell Dwyer, father of Albert H. Dwyer, Jr. of Houston, TX., and William C. Dwyer of Santa Cruz, CA, and grandfather of four. A memorial service will be held at 3:00 P.M., August 19 at the Church of the Atonement, Engle Street at Highwood Avenue, Tenafly, NJ. Contributions in lieu of flowers to the Church of the Atonement or the Alzheimer's Association, Greater New Jersey Chapter, 400 Morris Avenue, Denville, NJ 07834.
