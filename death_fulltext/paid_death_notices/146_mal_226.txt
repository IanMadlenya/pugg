
        WEISMAN-Herman L. Died March 7, in his 95th year, in West Palm Beach. A graduate of Harvard Law School in 1927, Mr. Weisman's legal career spanned over 50 years of public appointments and private practice in New York. He was a leader of the American Zionist movement during the early days of Israel and was active in Jewish community affairs. He is survived by his devoted wife of 70 years, Jeannette, his sons Edward, Robert and Matthew, eight grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. We will remember him with love and forever cherish his gifts of wit and wisdom.
