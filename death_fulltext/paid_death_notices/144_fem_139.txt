
        SAMPSON-Claire. Died January 12, 2000 in Chicago. Wife of the late Robert Sampson. She was awarded a prize for community service by the Mayor of Chicago. Survivors are daughter, Diane Sampson, sister Ruth Simon, nephew Roger Simon, nieces Martha Simon & Wendy Simon, sister-in-law Sis Lazar and her husband Buryl Lazar. Please send contributions to Jewish Home for the Aged, Chicago Attn: D. Sampson 1335 Astor St., Chicago, IL 60610. Piser Weinstein Funeral Parlor, Chicago, IL. Chicago papers please copy.
