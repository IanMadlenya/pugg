
        CLOPTON-Benjamin H. Died of AIDS on April 16, 1998. Ben is survived by his loving partner, Keith Pajkowski of Manhattan, his loving parents Ned and Marcia Clopton of Chevy Chase, MD., and his brothers Bryan, Geoffrey and Marc. Mr. Clopton, a painter and sculptor, lived in Manhattan for 15 years. Class of '67 Bethesda-Chevy Chase High School in Maryland and Class of '73 University of Maryland, College Park where he earned a BFA. Memorial service at St. Bartholomew's Episcopal Church, Park Avenue at East 50 Street, 2 P.M. Monday, April 20. Reception to follow. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to NY Episcopal Diocese AIDS Committee, C/O St. Bartholomew's.
