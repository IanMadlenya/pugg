
        TYRRELL--Wilfred L. Of West Bay Shore, LI, suddenly on September 2, 2005. Beloved husband of Dorothy Tyrrell. Devoted father of Thomas Tyrrell, Timothy and his wife Tara Tyrrell, Rev. Wilfred L. Tyrrell, Jr., S.A. and Doreen Tyrrell. Loving brother of Gerald and his wife Callie Tyrrell and the late Dennis Tyrrell. Cherished grandfather of Justin, Timothy and Ryan Tyrrell. Dear brotherin-law of Irene Farrington. Adored uncle to many nieces and nephews. Wilfred was a proud United States Navy Veteran and retired member of the American Stock Exchange. Reposing at The Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Hwy. (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Cswy.) Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial Tuesday, 11 am, at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, LI. Interment St. Patrick's Cemetery, Bay Shore, LI. Visiting Sunday & Monday, 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 PM. In lieu of flowers donations in Wilfred's name may be made to the Franciscan Friars of the Atonement, Graymoor, Garrison, NY 10524 or St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis TN 38105.
