
        BOONE-Ruth Jackson. Died Tuesday, February 2 in Kennett Square, PA. She will always be lovingly remembered by her children Susan, Daniel and James, her son-in-law Rick, daughters-in-law Deborah and Meredith, and as their beloved Nana, by her grandchildren Linda, Garrett, Sarah, Gage, Wilson and David. She was cherished by all of us because she brought so much to all our lives and we will miss her deeply. A memorial service will be held at Crosslands, Kennett Square, PA on March 7 at 2:00 PM. Donations may be made to American Friends Service Committee, 1501 Cherry St., Philadelphia, PA.
