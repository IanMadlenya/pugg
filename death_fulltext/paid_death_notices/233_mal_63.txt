
        MASLOW--Lester. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the passing of Lester Maslow, an esteemed friend whose lifetime of philanthropic leadership was fueled by deep commitment to the New York Jewish community. Lester, together with his family, enriched the lives of millions through their boundless generosity and spirit of klal Yisrael. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Lester's daughter, Jane; to his stepchildren, Patricia and Michael; to his brother, Arthur and his wife, Carol; & to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
