
        BEHR-Elaine Oakley. 82. Died on July 23 at her home in East Hampton, New York after a long illness. She was the daughter of R. Lawrence Oakley and Sarah Perot Oakley of Greenwich, CT and East Hampton. She graduated from Rosemary Hall in 1936 and from Sarah Lawrence College in 1938. She is survived by her husband of 53 years, Karl Howell Behr, Yale '37. Residents of Princeton, N.J. for many years, they now reside in Mountain Lake, FL. Mrs. Behr was a talented interior designer, a gifted equestrian & an avid golfer, but most of all, she was devoted to her husband, her family, her friends, and the places where she lived and loved. She will be greatly missed. She is also survived by her sister Audrey Oakley Johnson of Basking Ridge, NJ, her daughters, Susan Travers of Princeton, N.J., Sally Ogden of Greenwich CT, Helen Sanford of Princeton, N.J. and Elaine King of Dover, MA., 10 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be planned for a future date.
