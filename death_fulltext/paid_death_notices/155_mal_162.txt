
        PODBER-Irving., 92, born in Poland, came to the U.S. in 1918, died peacefully on December 4, 1999. He successfully ran Housewives Laundry for 50 years. He was a kind and generous man who was dearly loved by all who knew him. He is survived by his daughter, Selma Askinazy, three grandsons and six great-grandchildren. His family will miss him very much.
