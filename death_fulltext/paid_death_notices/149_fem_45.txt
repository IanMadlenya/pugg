
        ISAACS-Peggie. Of Boynton Beach, FL, on June 5, 1999, in Stamford, CT. Beloved mother of Jed P. Isaacs of Stamford, and Madeline Noveck of New York City; loving sister of Alex Jaffee, New York City. She is also survived by six grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. Funeral Monday, June 7, 1999, at 11AM at the Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St., Stamford. Burial Beth El Cemetery, Stamford. Memorial contributions to Camp Edward Isaacs, Central Queens Y.M.H.A., New York, NY.
