
        SCHWIMMER-Gertrude, 79, of Teaneck on April 27, 1997. Civic leader active for four decades in issues concerning women, children, education, fair housing, community relations and civil liberties. Beloved wife of David for 55 years. Devoted mother of Shoshanna, Georgia, and Mark, and loving grandmother of Joshua Edmondson. Funeral will be held 1:30 pm on Wednesday, April 30, at Temple Emeth, 1666 Windsor Rd, Teaneck. Contributions to her memory may be made to Barnard College in New York or the Women's Rights Information Center in Englewood. For information Gutterman-Musicant, Hackensack
