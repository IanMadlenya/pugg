
        LEWIS--Desiree. April 6. Beloved wife of the late Mortimer B. Lewis. Loving mother of Merry Lewis and Jane Lewis-Nichols and her husband Ned. Fondly remembered by nieces, nephews, cousins and friends for her great wit, charm and generosity. Services were held on Friday, April 8. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the American Heart Association would be appreciated.
