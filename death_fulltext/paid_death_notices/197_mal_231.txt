
        SALZBERG--Alfred L. Age 84. Activist, orthodontist; strong, gentle man and a giant in his convictions. Treasured husband of Doris. Father of Diane and Barbara. Poppy of David and Lizzie. A volunteer and veteran of WWII, he maintained his devotion to working toward diplomatic resolutions to international conflict. A 25 year Chair of Great Neck SANE and one of the originators of Veterans for Peace in Vietnam, also a dedicated participant in building a dental clinic in Israel. His memory will endure. Memorial service Tuesday, June 24, noon, at the Inn at Great Neck, 30 Cuttermill Rd. >>AD#
