
        MILLER-Amy Bess Williams 90. Of Pittsfield, Mass died on February 23, 2003. She was the founding president of Hancock Shaker Village and the widow of Lawrence Kelton Miller. She leaves a daughter, Margo Miller of Boston, MA; three sons, Kelton B. Miller II of Shaftsbury, VT, Michael G. Miller and Mark C. Miller both of Pittsfield, MA; a sister, Margery Adams of Charlotte, NC; five grandchildren and three grandchildren. A remembrance service will be June 7th Hancock Shaker Village.
