
        BUESCHER-Klaus J., 68. Beloved husband of Ingrid and son of the late Wilhelm and Margarethe Buescher of Essen, Germany. Died peacefully at his home in Manhattan on June 27, 1997, of leukemia-related complications. He is survived by his deeply saddened wife Ingrid and brother-inlaw Ludwig Pauen and his family. A man of rare intellect & wisdom, he will be remembered by his family and many friends for his integrity and generosity. Interment and private memorial service will be held in Essen, Germany, on July 18, 1997. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Leukemia Society of America, 475 Park Avenue South, New York, New York 10015.
