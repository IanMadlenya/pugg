
        HELBLING-Philip, 75. On December 18, 2000. Beloved husband of Rivia. Devoted father of Andrew (Louise Reggio) & Marcia (David Goldstein). Cherished grandfather of Dana, Emily, and Samuel. Holocaust survivor and World War II and Korean War veteran. He loved this country, which took him in with nothing and gave him safe harbor and the opportunity to build a full life. Funeral service Wednesday 12:15 PM at I.J. Morris, 21 East Deer Park Rd, Dix Hills, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the North Shore Jewish Center-Shoah Fund, 385 Old Town Road, Port Jefferson Station, New York 11776.
