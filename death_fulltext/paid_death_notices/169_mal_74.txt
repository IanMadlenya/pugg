
        SNELL-Frank M. Jr., eighty, formerly of New York City, died February 3, 2001 in Bath, Maine. Mr. Snell was an account executive with several New York City advertising agencies for many years. Surviving are his wife, Majella M. Snell of Bath, ME; his children, Christopher Snell of Mill Valley, CA, Hilary Snell of New York City, Nicholas Snell of San Francisco, CA, a step-dayghter Anne Hill Peaslee of St. Thomas, VI; a sister Evelyn Crosman of Hicksville, NY and two grandchildren. Services and burial will be in Bath, ME.
