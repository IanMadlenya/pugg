
        MYERS-Elizabeth Watson. Died on October 9, 1998 in Menlo Park, California at 68 years of age. Educator, Counselor, Administrator at American University, Georgetown University & Syracuse University. Graduate of the University of Chicago. After residing in Washington, D.C. and New York City for many years, she moved with her husband to California in 1995. She is survived by her husband Robert J. Myers of Menlo Park, three children Timothy, Holly and Lauchlin, sons-in-law Kirk Neely and Mark Liebman, seven grandchildren, and a brother James D. Watson of Long Island. Contributions to her memory may be made to Citymeals-on-Wheels, 355 Lexington Avenue, New York, New York 10017, or other social service organizations reflecting her spirit of compassion and responsibility to those in need.
