
        MORGAN-Maureen J. On April 27, 2001, at age 45. Vice President, Chase Manhattan Bank. Beloved wife of Bob. Loving mother of Robert. Dear sister of James and Kathleen. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Sunday and Monday, 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Monica's R.C. Church, 413 East 79 St., NYC. Interment to follow at Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Memorial Sloan-Kettering, 1275 York Ave., NY, NY 10021.
        MORGAN-Maureen. The Browning School Community notes with sadness the death of Maureen Morgan, mother of Robert. We will miss her enthusiastic involvement in the school. James S. Chanos, President Stephen M. Clement III, Headmaster Gail Vareilles, President Parent's Association
