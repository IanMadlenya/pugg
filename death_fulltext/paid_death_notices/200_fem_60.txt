
        ORELL--Rose. Born November 11, 1914. Loving wife to the late Barnett, mother to Sandy and Audrey, grandmother and greatgrandmother, died peacefully at her home on September 5, 2003. A lifelong political activist, student, business woman, union organizer, world traveler, former PTA President, founder and Board member of the Samuel Fields YM-YWHA, and Olympic torch bearer in 1996. Rose was a dynamic woman who will be remembered for her strength, generosity and most of all for her sense of humor and laughter.
