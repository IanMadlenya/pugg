
        TUNICK - George. 77, of Pound Ridge, New York died January 7 with his family by his side. He is survived by his wife of 60 years Marilyn, children Howard and Karen, son-in-law Michael Feltman and grandchildren Josh, Jessica and Sara. Textile executive, entrepreneur, athlete and loving husband, father, grandfather and friend.
