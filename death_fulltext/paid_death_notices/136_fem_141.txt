
        MacIVER-Loren. Died peacefully at her home in Greenwich Village, NY, on May 3, 1998. Beloved wife of the late poet and critic Lloyd Frankenberg. We are deeply saddened by the loss of our dear friend. As we move forward, her extraordinary spirit lives through our memories and her art. At Loren's request, no services are planned.
