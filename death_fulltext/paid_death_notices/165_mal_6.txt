
        MORAN-John D. A pressman for The New York Times for 17 years from 1967 to 1984 passed away Saturday, September 23, 2000. He is survived by his wife, Margaret, two daughters, Agnes and Therese; one son, John. His daughter Adelaide died in 1983. Adelaide's son, Michael Bonomo was raised by John and is now in his senior year at Hunter College. John was a 4th Degree Member of the Daniel J. Bradley Council of the Knights of Columbus, Baldwin, NY and the presentation of colors was made at his wake on Tuesday evening, September 26, 2000. He was a kind, gentle and friendly man, loved by many.
