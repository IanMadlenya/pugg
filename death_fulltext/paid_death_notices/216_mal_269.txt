
        ABRONS -- Herbert. Hadas sah, The Women's Zionist Organization of America mourns the passing of Herbert Abrons. As a Guardian of Tomorrow and a trustee of the Louis and Anne Abrons Foundation, Herbert was a generous benefactor of Hadassah, devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. His legacy will live on through the programs he supported at the Hadassah College Jerusalem. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Hyonja, brother Richard Abrons, sister Rita Aranow and the extended Abrons family. June Walker, National Pres.
