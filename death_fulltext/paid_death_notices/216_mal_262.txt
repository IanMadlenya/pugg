
        STOLL--Harry. Age 88 on January 18, 2005. Loving husband to Helen. Devoted father to Linda Pearlman, Evelyn Kasper (Ken) and Sandy Stoll (Gail). Loving grandfather to Geoffrey, Jared, Jeremy, Jason, Daniel and Shira. Services will be held today at 11AM at the Hampton Synagogue, 154 Sunset Ave., Westhampton Beach. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Hampton Synagogue or Gurwin Geriatric Center.
        STOLL--Harry. The Hampton Synagogue mourns the passing of our esteemed and generous benefactor, Torah Scroll donor and eponym of the Stoll Library Book Fund. An ardent Zionist, philanthropist, pioneer and sustainer of the Suffolk County Jewish community, he touched countless lives. Heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife Helen, to his children Linda, Evelyn and Sandy, to his grandchildren and to the entire family. Funeral Service today, January 20, 11 AM, The Hampton Synagogue, Westhampton Beach, NY. Rabbi Marc Schneier Michael B. Weisbrod, Pres. Seymour G. Siegel, Treas.
