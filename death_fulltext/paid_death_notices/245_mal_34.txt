
        RIKLAN--Dr. Manuel (Ricky), of Yonkers, NY, formerly Director of Psychology Department at St. Barnabas Hospital, Bronx, NY, passed away May 26, 2007 at age 80. He was the loving husband of wife, Gloria and the father of three children, Joan Simon, Debra Vekstein and David Riklan, and had the blessings of seven grandchildren and everlasting love of brother Frank, sister - in - law and brother-in-law Shirley and George Mann, sons-in-law Greg Simon and Greg Vekstein and daughter-in-law Michelle Riklan.
