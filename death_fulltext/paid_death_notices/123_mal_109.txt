
        GAGE-Edwin ''Peter'', realtor, passed away at Mary Manning Walsh Home in New York City on April 7, 1997 after a long illness. Husband of Suzanne Reade Gage. Father of Linda O'Connor (Timothy) and Thomas Gage (Teri) and beloved ''Pop'' of Andrew, Jessica, Peter and Laura, brother of Alan Gage. Contributions may be made to Monmouth Medical Center. Funeral services private.
        GAGE-Edwin ''Peter.'' The Board of Trustees and Administration of Monmouth Medical Center, along with the entire Monmouth Medical Center family, are deeply saddened by the passing of Edwin ''Peter'' Gage, a true humanitarian and friend. His commitment to our hospital and to the community was evidenced through his many years of voluntary service as a member of the Monmouth Medical Center Board of Trustees, as a Treasurer to the Monmouth County Race Meet Association, and other fine works. Our sincere condolences to his wife Suzanne, his children and his grandchildren. He will be greatly missed by the many people whose lives he so profoundly touched. LeRoy J. Herbert Chairman, Board of Trustees Vincent D. Joseph, FACHE Executive Director Monmouth Medical Center an Affiliate of the Saint Barnabas Health Care System
