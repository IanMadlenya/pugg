
        NICKERSON-Eugene H. Hon. The Judges and Magistrate Judges of the United States District Court for the Eastern District of New York and the entire court community express our profound sadness at the loss of our friend and mentor. With compassion and intellect, he enriched and guided our efforts during 25 years of distinguished and selfless service to the Court and to those who sought dignity and justice. We extend our deepest sympathies to the Nickerson family. A memorial service will be held on January 5, 2002 at 11:00 A.M. at Grace Church, 254 Hicks Street, Brooklyn, New York.
        NICKERSON-Eugene H. We remember Eugene H. Nickerson as a Distinguished Federal Judge and Friend who served as President of our Society for 4 years and for a half-century, from 1950 until 2000, as a member of our Board of Managers.
