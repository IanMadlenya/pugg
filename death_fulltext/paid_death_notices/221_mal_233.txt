
        LEVINE--L. Harvey, age 75, of Port Washington, NY, died Tuesday, June 28, 2005. Beloved husband of Marcia, adored father of Susan and Jim, Roger and Betsy and Robin and David. Exuberant grandfather of Sam, Jake, Nina, Eric, Zach, Eddie and Sarah. Loving brother of Rosalind Miller and Muriel Traubner and the late Phyllis Ruskin. Devoted uncle of John and Michelle, Annato and Lisa. He was our rock. His spirit, energy and love of life and learning are part of us forever. Memorial service to be held Thursday, June 30, at 11:30 AM at the Reconstructionist Synagogue of the North Shore, 1001 Plandome Road, Plandome, NY.
