
        LEFKOWITZ-Joseph, 77, of Long Beach, on Thursday June 26, 1997 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Mildred, loving father of Bonnie and Rochelle, devoted father-in-law of Charles and Felix, adored grandfather of Melissa and Joshua. He devoted his life to his family, his community and his family business, and never stopped thinking of others. We will miss him. Funeral 11:30 AM, Friday at Gutterman's, Rockville Centre. Contributions to Temple Israel of Long Beach appreciated.
