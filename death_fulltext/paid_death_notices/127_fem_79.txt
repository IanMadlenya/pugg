
        ASKIN-Lucille Bunin. Of New York and Westhampton Beach. Died on May 10, 1997 in Boston, MA. Lucille was especially known for her love and dedicated support of the arts. Throughout her career, Lucille was an independent art lecturer at New York museums and galleries with a following of many long-time, devoted students. In the 70s and 80s, Lucille Bunin Askin was the guest curator of contemporary art exhibitions at Lehigh University. Lucille was the beloved mother of daughter, Glenn Askin Schweich and son, Neil Zachary Askin, and the loving sister of Elinor Bunin Munroe and sister-in-law of George B. Munroe. Lucille was cherished by her grandchildren: Parker, Tara and Arden Schweich and her son-inlaw, Peter Schweich. Family and friends are invited to attend a memorial service for Lucille at the National Arts Club, 15 Gramercy Park South, New York on Monday, August 18 at 1:30 PM. The Annual Lucille Bunin Askin Art Award will be established at Boston University Academy. Contribution information will be given at the service or call 617-353-9000.
