
        MAYER--Florence. Of Floral Park, NY, and Coconut Creek, FL, passed away peacefully on February 18, 2006. Florence was the former wife of the late Judge Harold Mayer. She is survived by her beloved husband Irving Grodin, loving daughter Carol Solomon and son-in-law Larry, grandchildren, Dr. Keith Attkiss (Sonia) and Loren Attkiss Kabakov (Gene), greatgrandchildren, Nicholas, Sophie and Luke Attkiss, nephews, cousins, step-grandchildren and all who knew and loved her. In lieu of flowers donations can be give in her name to the American Heart Association. Chapel services will be held on Sunday, February 19, 2006, at 1 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Interment at Old Montefiore.
