
        MOTLEY--Joel W. Died of complications following heart surgery, on January 29. Predeceased by his wife, The Honorable Constance Baker Motley; mourned by his son, Joel W. Motley III; his daughter-in-law, Isolde Motley; his grandchildren Hannah, Ian, and Senay; and his many relatives and friends in New York, Connecticut, Illinois, and Nevis. Memorial service at noon on February 24, Canaan Baptist Church, 132 West 116th Street, New York. Gifts in his honor may be made to the The American Constitution Society for Law and Policy, 1333 H Street, NW, Washington, DC 20005.
