
        SHAPIRO--Howard. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Howard Shapiro, whose deep commitment and generosity to our Long Island Campaign has impacted millions of lives. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Howard's wife, Dorothy, to his children, Jay, Lawrence, Gail, and Daniel, to his grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
