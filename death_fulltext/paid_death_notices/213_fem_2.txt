
        GINSBERG--Adele O'Connor, 80. September 26. Beloved wife of the late Harold and sister of Quentin O'Connor and the late Violet Piwowar. Survived by stepchildren Elizabeth Ford and Judah Ginsberg, brother-in-law Frederick, niece Carol, and nephew Glenn Piwowar. Her ashes will be scattered along with Harold's off of Amagansett.
