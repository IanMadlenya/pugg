
        GOLD--Jack, 78, died of salmonellosis on September 15, 2004, two years after the death of his much loved wife, Rhoda. He is survived by his three children, Matthew, Stephanie and Roberta Gold. His two grandchildren, Rebecca and Ilyssa Gold, his brother Leon Gold, his sister Linda Chuman, and nine beloved nieces and nephews. Jack will be missed, not just by his family, but also by his friends in the community where he and Rhoda raised their family in Caldwell, NJ and the colleagues he worked with in his long market research career. Services will be held Sunday, 9:30 A.M. at ''Plaza Jewish Community Chapel'', 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
