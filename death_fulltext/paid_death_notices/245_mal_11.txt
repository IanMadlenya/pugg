
        UBELL--Earl. The Staff of PARADE Magazine mourns the loss of longtime friend and colleague, Earl Ubell, author, journalist, and PARADE Health Editor from 1982 to 1998. During his tenure at PARADE, Ubell was often ahead of the news, covering issues such as depression in children, obesity, gene testing, hormone therapy, and Parkinson's Disease, with which he was diagnosed in the late 1980s. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Shirley, his children and grandchildren. Walter Anderson, Chairman PARADE
