
        GINSBURG-Mollie, 92, piano teacher, of Highland Park, NJ, died May 18, 1997. Loving wife of the late Dr. Joseph M. Ginsburg. Dearly beloved mother of Miriam & Ira Hyams of L.I. and Melvin & Carol Ginsburg of N.J. Cherished grandmother of 5 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. Dear sister of Sarah Arky and Dr. William Rubin. This kind, gracious lady gave beauty, grace and love to all who knew her. Services 12 Noon, Tuesday, May 20, Highland Park Cons. Temple, So. 3rd Ave and Benner.
