
        KLAUSNER--Adele. Devoted wife of the late Jack Klausner died at the age of 87, peacefully at home on November 6, after a long illness. Beloved mother, grandmother, greatgrandmother, sister and aunt, she will be remembered by her kindness, generosity, tenacity, intelligence and wit. She is survived by children Bernard, Alice, Joseph, Ellen, and Stanley, grandchildren Phillip, Jay, Julie, and Matthew, and great-grandchildren Gabrielle and Samantha. She will live forever in the hearts of those fortunate enough to have known her.
