
        HALEM--Dr. Robert, on September 6, 2005 in Coral Springs, Florida, beloved husband of Christine, cherished father of Alexandra Levine and her husband Mark, and Elizabeth, devoted brother of Henry & his wife Sandra, son-in-law of Kathleen Giamelle & brotherin-law of Louis Giamelle and his wife Denise, loving uncle of Jessica and Christopher. Service Monday September 19, 2 PM, ''The Riverside'', 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, Amy Strong Memorial Fund for Melanoma Research.
