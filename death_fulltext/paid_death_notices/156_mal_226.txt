
        URBANEK-Brian John. 39, died January 8, 2000 at UNC Memorial Hospital in Chapel Hill, NC after a lengthy illness. Brian was born on March 21, 1960 in Berwyn, IL. He received his BS degree in Business Administration from Augusta State University in Augusta, GA. He worked for several years as a marketing representative with Xerox Corp. in NYC. In recent years, Brian had been disabled as he underwent two double lung transplants at UNC Memorial Hospitalfor treatment of Cystic Fibrosis. He was a former member of the Opera Club of NYC. Brian is survived by his father, Dr. Vincent E. Urbanek of Augusta, GA; brother Vincent E. Urbanek, Jr. of Cincinnata, OH; and many friends in NYC, Chapel Hill, and Augusta. A memorial service will be held at 1:00 pm on January 22, 2000 at St. Bartholomew's Episcopal Church, Park Ave., NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to: Lung Transplant Fund, Dept. of Social Work, UNC Hospitals, 101 Manning Dr., CB 7600, Chapel Hill, NC 27514.
