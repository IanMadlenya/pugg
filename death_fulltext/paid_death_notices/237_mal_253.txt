
        DECTER--Philip. The Officers and Staff of State of Israel Bonds mourn with profound sorrow the passing of a veteran leader and supporter of its program to strengthen Israel's economy. Phil served with distinction as Metro New Jersey General Chairman and was a member of the National Campaign Cabinet and President's Club. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Alice, their children Andrew and Laurie, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Joshua Matza, President and CEO Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board David Halpern, National Chairman Allan Janoff, Metro NJ General Chairman Alan J. Pines, Metro NJ Campaign Chairman
