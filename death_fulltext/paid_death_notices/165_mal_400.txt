
        O'SULLIVAN-Rev. John J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired pastor of St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Brooklyn, on October 29. Reposing at Frederick J. Chapey & Son Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway, West Islip, NY, Tuesday, October 31, from 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 PM. Mass of All Saints with Funeral Commendation of the Dead on Wednesday, November 1, at 11:00 AM at St. Joseph's Church, 39 N. Carll Ave., Babylon, New York. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
