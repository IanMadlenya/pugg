
        McROBERTS-Franklin C, Jr., 75, of Mill Neck, N.Y., and Boynton Beach, passed away Dec. 17, 1997. Mr. McRoberts was President of Airfreight Warehouse, Inc., Chairman of the Board of Lansdell Protective Agency, former Regent of L.I. College Hospital Board and former Board Member of the American Boy Choir. He is survived by his wife Jeanne L. McRoberts; a daughter, Mary McRoberts Campbell of NYC, NY; a son, Lansdell C. McRoberts of Ft. Lee, NJ; 4 grandchildren, Frank IV, Eric, Alex and James McRoberts. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, January 6, 1998, 11:00 AM, at St. John's of Lattingtown, Locust Valley, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Boy Choir, 19 Lambert Drive, Princeton, N.J. 08540.
