
        McNAMARA--Agnes L. Of Lynbrook, NY, died on August 19, 2005, at her home. She was 87. Born May 13, 1918, in Brooklyn, NY, to Timothy and Katherine McNamara. She was dearly loved by her two brothers and sisters-in-law: Joseph McNamara and Dorothy, and Francis and Agnes, who predeceased her. She then began a 45 year career for the Diocese of Brooklyn, retiring in 1983. She worked for 12 years at the Diocese of Rockville Centre and retired again at 83 years. Reposing at Macken Mortuary Rockville Centre Chapel, 52 Clinton Avenue, today, 2-4, 7-9pm. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 11am, St. Agnes Cathedral, Rockville Centre. Interment Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, NY.
