
        deMONICO-Louise Webb. On November 19, 2000, age 89, after a short illness. She is survived by her son Ernest Brandt, her daughter-in-law Cree Maxson and sister Mary Bruder. She will be fondly remembered by family and friends for her energy, zest for life and service to others. A Memorial Service will be held at the Riverdale Ethical Culture Society at a date, to be announced later.
