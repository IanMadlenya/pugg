
        KAHAN-Myra. Of Fire Island Pines, NY, and Edgewater, NJ, on November 12, 2001. A humanitarian, a philanthropist, a spokesperson for the rights of pets and worked to rescue and reunite lost or abandoned animals. Survived by Cappy Hanlan-Proud of her mother and guided by Myra to the many windows of the World. A memorial will be held at The Pines on July 14, 2002. To share happy memories of Myra at her home at 1 Shore Rd, Edgewater, NJ, (Call 201-969-9633 for directions), on November 26 from 5-9 P.M. Donations in Myra's memory should be made to PAWS, Box 894 Sayville, NY 11782.
