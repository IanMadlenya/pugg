
        SAVAGE-Thomas. Died May 16, age 41. Parents Shirley and James; brother Ted; sister Catherine and her husband Henry Roberts, children Andrew, James, Sarah; companion Steve Weinstein; and many friends mourn a special man who cherished life's wonders, opera above all. Memorial May 29 at 6 PM, Grace & St. Paul's Church, 123 West 71st Street. Donations in his memory to Brooklyn Botanic Gardens, 1000 Washington Ave., Brooklyn 11225.
