        SCHNECK-Herman, M.D. The Medical Board and Administration of Beth Israel Medical Center (BIMC) join the entire Beth Israel family in expressing their sadness over the death of Herman Schneck, M.D., a beloved member of Beth Israel's medical staff for 57 years. We share with Murial Schneck, the Schneck family, and all who loved him a profound sense of loss. We will ever be inspired by the compassion, dedication and sense of humor which illuminated Dr. Schneck's work as one of Beth Israel's devoted pediatricians. Matthew E.
