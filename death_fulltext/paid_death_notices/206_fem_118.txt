
        GREENLEAF--Carolyn R., of Essex, CT, succumbed March 11 after a valiant struggle with leukemia. She was born in Galesburg, Illinois, the beloved daughter of Dale and Nancy Ruedig and graduated from Mount Holyoke in 1975. She sang with the New Haven Chorale as a first soprano. She was active in community affairs. She is survived by her husband, Henry M. Greenleaf; children, Zachary J. Greenleaf, Page E. Greenleaf, Schuyler S. Greenleaf, and Taylor M. Greenleaf, beloved grandchildren, two brothers and a sister, and numerous loving nieces and nephews and godchildren. Services will be held at the First Congregational Church of Old Lyme on Monday, March 15, 2004, at 11 AM.
