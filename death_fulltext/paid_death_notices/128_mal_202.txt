
        MILLER-Hasbrouck Bailey, age 74, of New Vernon, N.J., died at home on September 14, 1997. He was comfortable and surrounded by his family. His keen sense of humor and wit, generous spirit and guiding light touched all who new him. He is survived by his loving wife, Jody & his adoring children, Kimberly, Stacey, Hasbrouck Jr. and Sloan. A man of great courage and honor, he will be deeply missed by family and friends. Service on Friday, September 19, 2:00 at the First Presbyterian Church in New Vernon, New Jersey, Lees Hill Road and Glen Alpin.
