
        ETTUS--Martha. Age 97, of Brooklyn, NY. On April 12, 2005. Beloved mother of Frank, mother-in-law of Sandra, grandmother of Pamela, Samantha, and Timothy, great-grandmother of Jaxon, and best friend to Lola. Martha's loved ones were blessed with her unfailingly positive spirit, humor, and charm. She was a gift to all who loved her.
