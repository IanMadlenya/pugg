
        LEVINE-Laurence W. On April 24, 2001. A distinguished Manhattan attorney and diplomat. Loving brother of Susan and Eric Kane and Jay and Sandra Levine. Dear uncle of Russell Kane, Beth Feldman, Lesley Logue, Michael and Jim Levine. Great-uncle of Molly Feldman, Emily and Jacob Levine. Service Thursday 11:45AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory to a charity of your choice.
        LEVINE-Laurence W. The sudden passing of their dear friend and mentor Larry Levine is deeply mourned by his staff at the law firm of Bigham, Englar, Jones & Houston. He was a well-known lawyer and an avid follower and student of politics and of international affairs, having published books on U.S.-China relations and this month on Argentine politics. His presence will be missed here and in Argentina. We extend our condolences to his entire family. Paul Ambos, Douglas E. Szulman, Angela M. Maloney, Elba Rodriguez, Bernard Levine
        LEVINE-Laurence W. The directors and staff of the Davis Mutual Funds mourn the sudden and untimely passing of their dear friend and esteemed director for 32 years. He had a distinguished career as a lawyer and diplomat. He was also a wise, and generous of time, counselor to us, our many thousands of shareholders, and our employees, and we shall miss him dearly. Our deepest condolences to his family.
        LEVINE-Laurence W. The Board of Trustees of Poly Prep Country Day School mourns the loss of Laurence W. Levine, past Trustee and member of the class of '49, and sends condolences to his family and friends.
        LEVINE-Laurence W. A truly extraordinary gentleman. We extend our sincere condolences to the Levine and Kane families. You were blessed, as were we all. Jane G. and Christine Nealy
