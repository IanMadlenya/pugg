
        ALLEN--Donald Beaton. Of Southampton, NY, died August 16, age 90. He was a prominent admiralty lawyer and former partner at Hill, Betts & Nash. He was a naval officer and WWII veteran. He is survived by sister Marion Hall, son Peter and wife Barrie, and son Donald and partner Beverley. Memorial service September 9 at 1 PM at the First Presbyterian Church, Southampton, NY.
