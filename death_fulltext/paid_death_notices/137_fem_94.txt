
        SIMOWITZ-Sylvia. June 10, 1998. Beloved wife of Arthur, devoted mother of Idan Sims, cherished grandmother of Reid Arthur Sims. She devoted many years of her life to Samaritan Village Inc., Queens, New York as a member of the board and Executive Vice President. Funeral Service Friday, June 12, 1998, 11:30 A.M., at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to Samaritan Village Inc., or the American Cancer Society.
