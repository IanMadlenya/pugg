
        ZUCKERMAN-Sarah. The Mesivta of Long Beach marks with sorrow the loss of Mrs. Sarah ''Cookie'' Zuckerman, of blessed memory, mother of our dear friend Mrs. Gary Fragin. Her elegance and smile, reflective of her illustrious family's dedication to Torah, will long be remembered. Our heartfelt condolences to her beloved husband Matthew, her devoted children, Karen Fragin, Linda Sandel, Nancy Markovich and Ruthie Shapiro. To all her cherished grandchildren and the entire family, may you all be comforted and spared from further sorrow. HaRav Yitzchok Feigelstock Rosh HaYeshiva Rabbi Shlomo Lesin Executive Vice President
