
        KLEIMAN-Mildred ''Gong Gong.'' Died on Septebmer 29, 2001, at the age of 94. Adored mother of Lynne Spanbock. Beloved grandmother of Richard, Jane, Janese and the late Paul Spanbock. Loving great-grandmother of Alex, Emily and Bari. Devoted wife of the late H. Paul Shanik and the late Morris Kleiman. A woman of great strength and dignity, filled with love for her family and friends. She will be forever in our hearts. Graveside service Monday, October 1, at 10 AM, New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
