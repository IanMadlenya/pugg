
        BROOKS-Harry A. The Board of Managers of the Racquet & Tennis Club extend their deepest condolences to the family of our esteemed member and fellow Manager, Harry Brooks, who served on our Board with great distinction for 22 years. Harry was a consummate gentleman, with boundless generosity, grace and enthusiasm. He was loved and respected by all his many friends and colleagues. We will miss him terribly, and we send our love and sympathy to Holly and all his family. Winthrop Rutherfurd, Jr. President, Racquet & Tennis Club
