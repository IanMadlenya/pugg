
        FIALKOW-Aaron D. 88. Of Bronx, New York. Professor Emeritus of Mathematics, Polytechnic Institute of New York. Beloved husband of the late Yetta Fialkow. He is survived by children Elaine, Lawrence and Diane, brothers, Morris and Lawrence and five grandchildren. He made significant contributions to Geometry and Electrical Network theory and avidly pursued interests in history, photography, Jewish affairs and hiking. Funeral service Tuesday, August 24, 12 noon, Hirsch and Sons, 1225 Jerome Avenue, Bronx.
