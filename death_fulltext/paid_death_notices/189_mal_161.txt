
        CALDER - Russell James. Humanitarian and leader/ educator in the residential interior design community, died at his home on October 8, 2002. Born in New York City on August 16, 1949 to Ang J. Calder and Dorothy (Teddee) E. Calder. Mr. Calder was educated at Southampton College of Long Island University, obtaining a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1971. He is survived by his life-time companion, Jack ''Chip'' Tharp, his parents, and sisters, Debra Calder Brandt, and Kim Cordero, and his niece and nephews. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, October 16, 10:30 am at St. Vincent Ferrer, 869 Lexington Ave., at 66 St., in Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions be made to Puppies Behind Bars, an organization where prisoners train dogs to become guide dogs for the blind, 99 Madison Avenue, 2nd Floor, NY, NY 10016.
