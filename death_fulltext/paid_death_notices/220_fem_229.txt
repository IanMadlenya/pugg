
        WEISGRAU--Jeanne G. The Directors, Officers and Staff of Mt. Zion Cemetery sadly mourn the passing of its former Director and Officer, Jeanne G. Weisgrau, at age 93. Beloved wife of the late Morris, devoted mother of Judith Stempa and Maxine K., loving grandmother of Michael and Matthew Stempa. Her family, business associates and friends celebrate her long, happy, productive life. Mark Stempa, President
