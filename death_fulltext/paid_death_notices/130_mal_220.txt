
        BOOKBINDER - George Harry. With profound sorrow, we announce that George Harry Bookbinder passed away peacefully at his home in New York City on November 10, following a protracted illness. Interment took place at the Wooster Cemetery in Danbury Connecticut on Wednesday, November 12. He is survived by his sons, David and Jay, and two grandchildren, Jeremy and Eliana. In lieu of flowers, we would be grateful for contributions to Oxfam America.
