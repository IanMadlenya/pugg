
        FENIK--Inci Koner. Passed away May 22, 2006. Wife of Faruk Fenik, mother of Mehmet and Omer. A graduate of Robert College, Istanbul, Turkey, class of 1944, she was a speaker on the Turkish Voice Radio and TV programs from 1968 until 2000. She will be remembered with love and affection by her family and the entire TurkishAmerican community.
