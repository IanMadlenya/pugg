
        LIVESEY--Ruth Perry. Died January 9, 2006, Goshen, NY. Born February 8, 1913, New Rochelle, NY. Radcilffe College, 1935 B.A., Cum Laude. First husband, Leslie G. Schaumann. Second husband, 1971, Herbert B. Livesey. Trade Association Manager for the National Institute of Diaper Services and the Society of Craft Designers. She was the sweetest of spirits, love and Godspeed. Services Monday, 7 P.M., T.S. Purta Funeral Home, Pine Island, NY, 845-651-7711.
