
        GLAZER--Rose. 91, peacefully on October 11. Beloved sister of Sam, Joe, Henry, Freida, Gail and Nathan. Cherished aunt and greataunt of many. We remember her service to her country as a WAC in WWII and her many years with the Social Security Administration. Her kindness and generosity will be missed by all who knew her. Services to be held Monday, October 13, 11:30 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel.
