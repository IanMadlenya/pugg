
        NEWMYER - Robert. Inde pendent producer, died at 49. The Board of the Gordon A. Rich Memorial Foundation (GAR) mourns the loss of one of its founding members, Robert ''Bobby'' Newmyer. Bobby was a wonderful family man, a prolific independent film producer, a great friend and a tireless supporter of philanthropies, such as GAR and the Lost Boys of Sudan. He will be sorely missed.
