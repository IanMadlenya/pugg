
        RIEDER-Al. The Faculty, Administration and Board of Directors of Manhattan Day School/Yeshiva Ohr Torah record with profound sorrow the passing of Al Rieder, father of Denise Karasick, the wife of our president, George Karasick and grandfather of current and former students Yael, Tami, Shimon, Nomi and Chaya. May his wife Edith, his children and the entire family be comforted in this hour of their untimely loss. Rabbi David Kaminetsky, Principal Elliott Gibber, Chairman of the Board
        RIEDER-Al. The Board of Directors of the Mikveh of the West Side mourn the passing of Al Rieder, father of our President, Denise Karasick. May his wife Edith, his children and the entire family be comforted amongst the mourners of Israel. Elliott Gibber, Chairman
        RIEDER-Al. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the beloved father of our member, Ralph Rieder. May his entire family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President
