
        COLVIN-Richard Riley. 64, of Katonah, NY died on July 21, 2002. Beloved husband of Jane and father of Zachary, brother of William. He was an innovative entrepreneur, inventor, landscape architect and gifted craftsman. He will be fondly remembered for his generous spirit and sensitivity to others. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, July 30, 3pm, First Presbyterian Church, Katonah,NY
