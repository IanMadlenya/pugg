
        ROSNER-Kalman Donald, M.D. in his 91st year, of Jackson Heights, Queens. Passed away December 29, 1997 at South Shore Health Care Center after a long illness. Dr. Rosner was on the staff of Physicians Hospital for 40 years. Retired Chairman of Department of Anesthesiology. Fellow of the College of Anesthesiologists. Veteran of W.W. II, obtaining rank of Major in Medical Corps. Beloved husband of Dorothy of Jackson Heights, N.Y., loving father of Florence Raskin of Smithtown, NY, Diane Geringer of Oceanside, N.Y., and Dr. William Rosner of New Haven, CT. Devoted grandfather and great-grandfather.
