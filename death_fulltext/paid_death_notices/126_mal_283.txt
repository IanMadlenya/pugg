
        MILLER-Paul L. Seventy-seven, died peacefully after a full and productive life on Saturday, July 26th. He is survived by his wife of forty-seven years Adele, and his three daughters Hilary, Beverly and Leslie, one granddaughter and four grandsons. His son, Paul L. Miller, Jr. died in 1993. There will be a private burial and service in Northeast Harbor, ME. Those wishing to may send contributions in his memory to The Peregrine Fund, World Center for Birds of Prey, 5666 West Flying Hawk Lane, Boise, ID 83709.
