        PASTERNAK--Sandra. The Department of Neurology at Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center mourns the untimely passing of Sandra Pasternak, beloved wife of Dr. Gavril Pasternak, our colleague and friend. Sandra died at Memorial Sloan-Kettering on March 10, 2005, seventeen months following her diagnosis of pancreatic cancer. We extend our deepest sympathies to Gavril, her children Katie Rachael, David Avram, Anna Rose, and her extended family.
        PASTERNAK--Sandra. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its member, Sandra Pasternak. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred D. Youngwood, President
