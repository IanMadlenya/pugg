
        RIKER--Walter. The Faculty of the Department of Pharmacology, Weill Medical College of Cornell University mourns the death of Dr. Walter Riker, Jr., our esteemed former chairman. Dr. Riker led this department with great distinction from 1956-1983. He manifested the highest standards of academic and scientific excellence and absoulute loyalty to his faculty and this medical school. He was also a great humanitarian who touched all of our lives deeply. We will always remember him with affection and respect. Our deepest sympathy to his wife, Virginia, and to all of the Riker children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. Dr. Lorraine Gudas, Chairman, Department of Pharmacology
