
        WERTHEIMER-Franc. Died on May 10, 2002, of Bethesda, MD, formerly of Livingston, NJ, and New York City. Beloved husband of Sidelle Wertheimer. Devoted father of Laura and David Werthheimer, and sage father-in-law of Andrew Pincus. Adoring grandfather of Josh and Kate Pincus. Born in Nuremberg, Germany, he came to this country with his parents at the age of 11 as a refugee from the Nazis. Graduated with highest honors from Brooklyn College and Columbia University. Mathematician by training, he was a co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of Man Tech International Corporation. A talented amateur pianist, he was a faithful patron of the symphony and the opera. In his retirement he was an active volunteer in the community and supporter of the arts. Service Monday, May 13, 11:30 AM, at Washington Hebrew Congregation, Washington, DC. Contributions in his memory can be made to The Blue Card, a fund for Holocaust survivors, 1 West 34th St., Suite 404, New York, NY 10001.
