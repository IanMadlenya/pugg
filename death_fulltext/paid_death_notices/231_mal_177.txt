
        IRVING--Alexander D. II died peacefullly in Southampton, NY on April 15, 2006 at age 89. Predeceased by his wife Colette, he is survived by his son, Michael Irving, his daughters, Evelyn Leonard and Alexandra Rush, and by six grandchildren. Services will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church in Southampton at 11 AM on Friday, April 21, 2006. In lieu of flowers, contributions in Mr. Irving's name to Southampton Hospital would be appreciated.
