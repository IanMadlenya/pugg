
        ZAVIN--Theodora, 82, on June 21, 2004, wife of the late Benjamin Zavin. She will be greatly missed by her surviving sons, Jonathan and Joshua, her daughters-inlaw Bernadette and Joanne, her grandchildren, Kate, Nathaniel, and Ryan, her sister-in-law, Fredda, and by a world of people, in and out of the music business who treasured her as a colleague, a mentor and a friend.
