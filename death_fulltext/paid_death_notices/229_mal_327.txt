
        MARTIN--Eugene F. Jr. Died on February 24, 2006. Husband of the late Catherine Attridge and brother of Therese Martin. Mr. Martin attended Canterbury School in New Milford, CT, and St. John's School in Annapolis, MD, and received his degree from the University of Virginia (Class of 1952). He was a real estate investor and broker in New York City. Visitation will be held at the John Krtil Funeral Home (1297 1st Ave) on Sunday and Monday, February 26 and 27, during the hours 2-4 pm and 7-9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Tuesday, February 28, at 10 AM, at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave and E. 66th St. Burial will take place at St. Charles Cemetery in Pinelawn, NY.
