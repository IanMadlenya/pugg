
        STEIN-Charmion Kerr. The Officers, Board and staff of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) mourns the passing of the beloved wife of our distinguished colleague and mentor, Dr. Herman Stein. She served with her husband in France after World War II in JDC's critical programs of Relief and Reconstruction. A woman of many accomplishments in the field of Educational Psychology, she was a distinguished psychotherapist. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband and children. Eugene Ribakoff, Pres. Jonathan W. Kolker, Bd. Chairman Michael Schneider, Exec. V. P.
