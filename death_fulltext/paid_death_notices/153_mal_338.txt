
        COLLINS-Hugh Gawtry formerly of Millbrook, NY died at Bloomfield, CT Oct 24, 1999, age 94. He is survived by his wife of 68 years, Frances Fisher Collins, his daughter, Anne Flagler Collins, M.D., and sons, Dennis Gawtry Collins, Farnham Fisher Collins, and Anthony Philip Collins, seven grandchildren, and eleven great grandchildren. Services will be held at Grace Episcopal Church, Millbrook, NY Friday, October 29 at 11 AM.
