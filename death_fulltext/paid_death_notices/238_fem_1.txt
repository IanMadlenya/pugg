
        GLUCK--Sophie, died October 30, 2006 at age 93. Lawyer, judge. Beloved wife of Adolph and cherished sister and mother. She is survived by her son Matthew, daughterin-law Peggy, daughter Lucille, grandchildren David, Lori, Jeffrey and Carina, and great-grandchildren Andrew, Ari, Riley, Roxanne and Benjamin. Graveside services 1pm October 31st at Mount Ararat Cemetery. She will be greatly missed.
