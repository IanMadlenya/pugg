
        PHILIBOSIAN-George. The Board of Trustees of Englewood Hospital and Medical Center records with sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague and benefactor, George Philibosian. His devotion to our Medical Center and so many other institutions in the greater Englewood Community will long be remembered and serve as a testament to his life and work. Our thoughts and prayers are with his beloved wife Alice and his children Emil, Alan, Charles, Stephen and Anita and their families. Andrew F. Durkin, Chairman Daniel A. Kane, President
