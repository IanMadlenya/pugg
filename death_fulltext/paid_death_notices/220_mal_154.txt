
        ROSENTHAL--Milton F. Purchase College President Thomas Schwarz, the Friends Board and staff of the Neuberger Museum of Art offer their sincere condolences to the family of Milton Rosenthal, a longtime member and generous supporter of the museum and college. His legacy of wisdom, friendship and generosity will continue to inspire the campus and museum for generations. Thomas J. Schwartz, President Helio Fred Garcia, Chairman, Friends Board Anne L. Bradner, Director, Neuberger Museum of Art
