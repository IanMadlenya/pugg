
        SARASOHN--Ira, 88, of Bocaire Country Club, Boca Raton, FL, formerly of South Orange, NJ, passed away suddenly on Friday, February 10th. He was the loving husband and best friend for 31 years to Helen, devoted father to Ellen, Wendy, Stephen, Meta and JoAnn, doting Papa to Elizabeth, Jamie, Mollie, Nathan, Bernard, Michael, Julie, Vince, Jackie, Bobby and Jason and beloved brother of Roy and Miriam. Loved by all who knew him, he will be deeply missed. Services Sunday, 1:30 PM, Temple Beth El, Boca Raton.
