
        MILLER--Sy J. Departed on December 22, 2005, as a result of cardiac arrest. He leaves his wife of 62 years, Lillian, his two daughters, Jan and Meg, his brother, Jerry, two grandchildren and many good friends. During his 42 year professional career he led the highly successful Miller Organization, an architectural interior design firm. The family requests that all memorial donations be made to children's charities.
