
        GUSTAFSON-Russell. Age 55. Died January 11, 2003 in NYC. B.A., Lynchburg College, 1969; M.A., University of Virginia, 1972. A former actor, his credits included many plays and musicals at The Albemarle Playhouse in Charlottesville, VA, and the New York premiere of Mustapha Matura's ''Play Mas'' at The Urban Arts Corps. He had worked as assistant to the late Vinnette Carroll at The Urban Arts Corps; as a press agent for Pat Dale Associates and for Henry Luhrmann Associates; and as a senior copywriter for Pace Advertising. He is survived by his nieces and nephews, Andy Gustafson, Kristen Gustafson O'Mahony, Meredith Ray, and Allen Gustafson.
