
        GREANEY-Patrick J., age 58, of Wyckoff, New Jersey, died on Tuesday, Nov. 11, 1997. President and Chief Operating Officer of NY-based Cellular Vision USA, former Senior Vice President and member of the Executive Management Committee with Philips Electronics North America Corporation where he had been employed for 26 years. Listed in Who's Who in America. Graduated with a BEE from Manhattan College & MBA from Iona College. Member of Ridgewood Country Club, N.J., and the Union League Club, N.Y. Beloved husband of Susan (nee Kleinermann). Father of Kathleen Margaret, Patrick Joseph Jr. and John Michael. Visiting at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Ave., Wyckoff on Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass St. Elizabeth's RC Church, 700 Wyckoff Ave., Wyckoff, N.J. 07481. Saturday 9:30 AM. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to St. Elizabeth's RC Church.
