
        MARCUS-Joseph Malcolm. Died peacefully at home in Golden Beach, FL, on August 1, 2002, at age 101. Predeceased by Augusta (nee Hittleman), his beloved wife for 71 years. Formerly from Mt. Vernon, NY, and Lido Beach, LI. Loving father of Helen, Bernard (Dolores), Irene (Bernard) Feuerstein and Carl. Also survived by eight grandchildren, 12 great grandchildren and sister Melva. Formerly owned Treemark Shoe Stores in NYC.
