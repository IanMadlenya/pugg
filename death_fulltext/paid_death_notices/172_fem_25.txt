
        BERKMAN-Lillian R. The Metropolitan Opera Association mourns the death of our good friend and Advisory Director, Lillian R. Berkman. Her commitment to our company and her frequent presence at performances in the Opera House will be greatly missed. We extend our condolences to her family and friends. Mrs. Gilbert W. Humphrey, Chairman Emeritus Bruce Crawford, Honorary Chairman James W. Kinnear, Chairman Paul M. Montrone, President Mrs. Ezra K. Zilkha, Vice Chairman
