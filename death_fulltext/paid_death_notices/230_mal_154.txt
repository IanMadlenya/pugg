
        UDEL--Dr. Melvin, 81. Of Saratoga Springs, New York, died March 6, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Bracey Udel, he is missed by daughter Lisa, son-in-law Timothy Kramer, and many other family members and friends. Melvin was born in Baltimore, MD. on 28 August 1924 to Fanny and Solomon. He was an Assistant First Lieutenant in the US Navy, serving from 1943-1944. Melvin went on to become a psychiatrist renowned for his work in alcohol and drug addiction.
