
        BRONSTEIN--Sophia L. Died peacefully at her home in Chicago at the age of 101. Mrs. Bronstein, who was born in Bialystok, came to Chicago as a young child. She was the widow of Dr. I. Pat Bronstein, a Pediatric Endocrinologist and Professor of Pediatrics. Mrs. Bronstein is survived by her two daughters, Alicia Lambert Albert and Susan Bronstein, her sisters Fay Weissberg and Anita Kurti, and her sister-in-law Kate Lome. She was attended at the last by her caregiver and companion, Rose Butler. There will be a memorial service in Chicago in August. >>AD#
