
        HITTLEMAN-Eva. Of Long Beach, passed away peacefully on June 9, 2001. Widow of Charles Hittleman. Mother of Warren, Sandra, Karen and the late Arnold Hittleman. A cherished grandmother, she will be missed by Jerry, Bruce, Linda, Laura, Jude, Matthew, Greg, Neal, Chanie, and Carly. Loving great-grandmother to Jessica, Aaron, Zoe, Naamah and Maya. Eva will be especially remembered for her love of life, for all the years she generously, enthusiastically and purposely volunteered in numerous organizations, for her devotion to Temple Emanuel, and for all the work she accomplished in her community. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her name can be made to the United Cerebral Palsy Association of Nassau County.
