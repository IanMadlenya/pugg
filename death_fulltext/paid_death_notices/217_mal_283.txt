
        CARLSON--Felix, 79, on February 18, 2005. Beloved husband of 51 years to Audrey Carlson. Cherished father of son Michael and his fiancee Sonja. Devoted brother of sisters Helen Barkin and the late Rose Harren. Adoring uncle of nieces Jill, Carol, Amy, and Laurie. Loving grandfather of Eric, Abby and Isabella. Eleven year Veteran of the U.S. Navy; proud graduate of the University of Michigan; mechanical and electrical engineer, accountant. President of Dun Rite Tank Corp. An avid tennis player and former Captain of ''The Venerables'' at the Westside Tennis Club in Forest Hills, will be remembered for his brilliant mind, gentle soul and joyous spirit. His memory will never leave our hearts and thoughts.
