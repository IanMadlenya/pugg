
        WATT--Stanley B. 74 of Rumson, NJ. Beloved husband of 50 years to Bonnie and father to Stacey and Jason and his wife Juliette. Graduate of Carnegie Mellon University. A member of SAG, AFTRA, and Equity. Avid traveler, photographer, and adventurer will be deeply missed. A memorial gathering will be held on Saturday, January 8 from 9:30 to 11:30 AM followed by an 11:30 AM memorial service at John E. Day Funeral Home, 85 Riverside Avenue, Red Bank, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Stan's name to The Actors' Fund of America.
