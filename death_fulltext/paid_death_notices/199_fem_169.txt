
        BEEKMAN--Nancy B., of South Shaftsbury, VT and Palm City, FL, passed away on August 28th at Albany Medical Center from injuries incurred in an automobile accident. She leaves her beloved husband of 50 years, Philip E. Beekman, three children, Elizabeth E. Beekman of Providence, RI, Leslie B. Carter of Brownsburg, IN, and Nancy L. Beekman of Scotts Valley, CA, a sister, Leslie B. Blackaller of Kentfield, CA, and five grandchildren. Visitation will be on Tuesday, September 2nd, at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel located at 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St between the hours of 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, September 3rd, at 10 AM, at St. Patrick's Cathedral located on 5th Ave. and 50th St., Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Nancy B. Beekman Scholarship Fund at the Middlebury College, Development Office, Middlebury, VT 05753.
