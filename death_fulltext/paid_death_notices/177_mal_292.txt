
        PATIL-Dr. Channabasappa S., 50, 13 October. His colleagues mourn the untimely passing in India of our constant friend and one of southern India's most able young archaeologists and epigraphists. Deputy Director of the Karnataka Department of Archaeology and Museums, his exemplary research on art and history of his state will long endure. Drs. John M Fritz, George Michell, Anna L. Dallapiccola, CTM Kotraiah, R. Barry Lewis, Mark T. Lycett, John McKim Malville, Kathleen D. Morrison, Carla M. Sinopoli, Nalini Thakur, Anila Verghese, Philip B. Wagoner
