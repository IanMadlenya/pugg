
        SIERRA-Natalia Maria, 92, a long time resident of Brooklyn, passed away in San Diego, February 3, 2001. She is survived by her daughter Elisa Gutierrez of San Diego, one brother Luis Diaz of Florida and one grandson, Miguel Gutierrez of San Diego. Funeral mass Thursday, February 8, 2001, St. Brigid Catholic Church, San Diego. Donations are suggested to the Marcelino F. Sierra Memorial Scholarship Fund. Checks can be sent to the New York City Branch of the ASM (American Society of Microbiology P.O. Box 736, Westbury, New York 11590.
