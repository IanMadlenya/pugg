
        SHAMAH-David I. The Officers and Board of Trustees of the Magen David Yeshivah, Isaac Shalom Elementary School, Celia Esses High School, and Joseph and Lilly Aboudi Preschool mourn the loss of our very dear friend, David I. Shamah, father of our esteemed President, Steven D. Shamah. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his loving wife Florence, his children Ike, Steve, Rose (Betesh) and Ronald, and the entire Shamah family. Our thoughts and prayers are with you at this most difficult time. Eddie J. Esses Co-President
