
        WIGDER--Bernard L., Dr. 87 years young, passed into Gan Eden, Eruv Succos, June 12, 2005. He is survived by his beloved and loving wife, Harriet of 37 years. A dear and compassionate man, struggled many years with Alzheimers Disease. The beauty of his soul and his commitment to Jewish communal life will never be forgotten by those fortunate to have known and loved him. A former resident of Riverdale, N.Y., he passed in Pembroke Pines, FL. He is mourned by a multitude of friends and family.
