
        BAXTER--Christine Evelyn, devoted wife, mother, daughter, grandmother, sister and friend, died at home surrounded by family on December 18, 2003. Born in New York, daughter of Mary and the late Matthew Zagroba, devoted sister to the late Edward Zagroba, she graduated from St. John's University where she met and fell in love with her husband, Harold J. Baxter. As a devoted wife and mother, she built a warm, loving, faith-filled home with her husband and their four children, Christine, Patricia, Robert and Kathryn. Recently, she welcomed the addition of her daughter-inlaw Ruth, son-in-law Andrew Youngblood and grand daughter Claire Elizabeth into her family. Her life was a joyful example of faith, patience, love and kindness. Her laughter will be missed by her many dear friends. Calling hours at The Donohue Funeral Home, 366 W. Lancaster Avenue, Wayne, PA on Sunday, December 21 from 69PM. Funeral Mass will be celebrated at St. Katharine of Siena Church, Monday, December 22, 2003 at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers remembrances may be made to Thomas Jefferson University Hospital, Bodine Center for Cancer Treatment, Attn: Miriam Schwed, 11th and Sansom Street, Philadelphia, PA 19107.
