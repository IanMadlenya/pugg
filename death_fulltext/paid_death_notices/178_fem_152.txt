
        WEXNER - Bella. Schottenstein Chabad House mourns the passing of Bella Wexner, a visionary both in the business world and in philanthropic causes. She was an extremely kind and gracious lady who was passionate about helping others. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Wexner family. Mr. Jay Schottenstein, Chairman of the Board, Mr. Mike Schiff, President, Rabbi Areyah Kaltmann, Executive Director
