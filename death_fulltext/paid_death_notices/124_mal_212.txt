
        BUCKY-Dr. Peter A. President of Bucky X-Ray International Inc, passed away May 14, 1997 after a short illness. Mr. Bucky, who was 85, was pre-deceased by his wife Tina (nee McBurnie Bucky). He is survived by his son Peter R. of Endicott, New York, and his brother Dr. Thomas Lee and his sister-inlaw Doris of Weston, CT., and friend Kathy Riegel. A graveside service will be held Wednesday, May 21, 1997, 12 noon, in Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton, Long Island, New York.
