
        SMITH-Elizabeth Campbell Watt, of Mill Neck, NY, on June 14th, 2000 in her 88th year after a brief illness. She attended St. Timothy's School, Maryland and was a member of Colonial Dames Parent Chapter. She was predeceased by her husband, William W. Smith, and survived by sons, W. Watt Smith, Alexander J. Smith, and grandchildren, Adriana, Carolina, Cameron, Katherine and Charles. Service private.
