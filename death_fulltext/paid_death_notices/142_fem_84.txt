
        McCORMACK-Catherine G. Devoted mother of Jane, Hugh and George III, loving mother-in-law of Corinne McCormack and William Lauck, delighted grandmother of Adam, loving aunt of Cathy Witt & Martha Brown, died peacefully on Nov. 8th, 1998. Born in Sioux Falls, South Dakota, she attended the Emma Willard School and Smith College. In New York she pursued a career in fashion which included being assistant to the editor of the Fashions of the New York Times. Her brave and loyal spirit, her loving heart, her elegance and style will always be remembered and missed by her family and friends. Funeral on Thursday, November 12th, at 10:30 AM at St. Vincent Ferrer Church corner of 66th and Lexington followed by reception at the Cosmopolitan Club, 122 East 66th Street. Donations in memory of Catherine G. McCormack may be sent to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
