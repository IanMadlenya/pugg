
        GREENBURG-Janice. Devoted and loving mother, grandmother, sister, aunt and friend died Sunday, September 9, 2001 after a courageous battle against cancer. She was preceded in death by her husband Robert Greenburg and her sister, Annette Evins. She was the daughter of Rebecca and Mike Maltz. She is survived by her children Ross and Michele Greenburg and Michael and Nikki Greenburg, and grandchildren Brad and Rachel Greenburg, Dimitri and Kenya Rose Greenburg. She is also survived by her sister and brother-inlaw Betty Rae and Ralph Cohen and sister-in-law Madeline Greenburg Goldfein, as well as numerous nieces and nephews and grand nieces and nephews she loved so dearly. Janice Greenburg will be remembered for her gracious and loving nature, her wonderful sense of humor and for her sweet devotion to family and friends. Graveside services will be conducted Thursday, September 13, 2001 at Sharon Gardens Cemetery in Valhalla, New York. For further information, contact Riverside Memorial Chapel, 914664-6800. In lieu of flowers or for those desiring, donations may be made to M.D. Anderson Hospital, or a hospice or charity of your choice.
