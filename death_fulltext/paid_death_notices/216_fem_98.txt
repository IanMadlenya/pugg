
        TEITELBAUM -- Beatrice. The officers and directors of Metropolitan Jewish Health System sadly mourn the untimely passing of Beatrice Teitelbaum, beloved past member of the Metropolitan Jewish Geriatric Center Board, the President of the Long Island League and a devoted friend and ardent supporter. Her involvement with our organization spans nearly six decades of faithful service, as she was an active supporter and advocate on behalf of the elderly we serve. We pray that her noble service also will sustain her family, and the love and prayers of those who care will comfort them. We share in your loss. She will be greatly missed. May her legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom she touched. Our profound sympathy and sincere condolences to the familly Shmuel Lefkowitz, Chairman of the Board Eli S.
