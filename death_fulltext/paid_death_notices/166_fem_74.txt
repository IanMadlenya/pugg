
        KOGEL-Dora Lowenbraun. 93, on November 11, 2000, after a long illness at Parker Jewish Geriatric Institute. Mrs. Kogel was married to the late Dr. Abraham Lowenbraun, residing in the Bronx and Mt. Vernon, and later to the late Dr. Marcus Kogel, founding dean of Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University and former commissioner of hospitals in NYC. They lived in Wading River and Boca Raton, FL. She was a school teacher in the Bronx and a supporter of the arts. Mrs. Kogel is survived by her daughter Alice Funk of Westbury, two grandchildren, Lisa Ann Green and Andrew Funk, and two great-grandsons whom she adored. A funeral service was held Sunday at Gutterman's in Woodbury, NY. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Mazon: A Jewish Response to Hunger, (www.mazon.org), 12401 Wilshire Blvd., Los Angeles, CA 90025, or World Hunger Year.
