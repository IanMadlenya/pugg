
        CRANE-Louis Joseph, DDS. Age 89. Of Lauderhill, FL. Formerly of Larchmont, N.Y., and Bronxville, NY. On Thursday, December 17, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Margaret (nee Wagner). Loving father of Dr. Frederick (Betty), Dr. Edward (Carol), and Carol Gerber (Bernard). Adoring grandfather of Judith and Robert, Laura Crane Gelblum (Ehud) & Peter, Cynthia Tomlinson (Richard) and Julie Gerber. Devoted brother of the late Frederick Crane and the late Benjamin Crane. He loved life, his family, his work and his music. Services 12 Noon on Sunday, December 20, at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway, (Route 4 West), Fair Lawn, New Jersey. Contributions in his memory may be made to United Jewish Appeal.
