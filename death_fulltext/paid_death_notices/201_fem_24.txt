
        SKALSKY--Judi. Consultant to International Architecture and Design Community. Judy Skalsky, marketing and communications consultant in New York and Los Angeles has died in her home in the Pacific Palisades from injuries she received in a fall. She is survived by her parents Milton and Rose Skalsky of Cedar Rapids, IA, and by her sister Jeanne Skalsky Carter of Olathe, KS. Memorial service date to be announced.
