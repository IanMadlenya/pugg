
        RACKMAN-Ruth. Congregation Shaaray Tefila in Lawrence, mourns the loss of its beloved former Rebbetzin, Ruth Rackman, who ably assisted her husband, Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, as a true ''Ezer'' during his long tenure as our Rabbi. We extend heartfelt condolences to the Rabbi and his family. David Weinberger, Rabbi Martin Klein, President Norman Newman, Chairman
