
        GARDNER-Frederick C. Of Palm Beach, Florida. On August 22, 1999. Survived by his wife Claire Birdsall Gardner. Stepfather of Albert L. Wheeler and Constance Wheeler Geisler. Grandfather of Peter Geisler Jr., Missy Geisler, Chip Wheeler and Weston Wheeler. Born in Pawling, New York. Graduated from Columbia University and Columbia Law School. 1st Lt. in World War II. Counsel to the European Compensation Commission, he was Vice President and Secretary of Chubb and Son. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
