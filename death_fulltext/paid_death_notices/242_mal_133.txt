
        BROWNE--Peter Carroll, a gentleman and valiant fighter, age 82, of Tupper Lake, NY and formerly of Lawrence, NY, died Sunday, March 4, 2007 at his home, surrounded by family. Mr. Browne was Commodore Emeritus of the Cedarhurst Yacht Club, Lawrence, NY. He is survived by his wife of 60 years Jean (Hancock) Browne; two sons Peter and Curtis; four daughters Pamela B. Kruger, Hilary B. Fitzpatrick, Prudence B. Purcell, Deirdre B. Griffith, 18 grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. A private service for family members was held at the Fortune-Keough Funeral Home, Saranac Lake, NY. Friends wishing to remember Peter Carroll Browne may make memorial contributions to The Cedarhurst Yacht Club or the Adirondack Medical Center, Lake Colby Dr., Saranac Lake, New York 12983.
