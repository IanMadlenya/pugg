
        LANGFAN - Hannah (Hankie). Died on Tuesday, May 12, 1998. Beloved daughter of the late Max and Mary Langfan. Cherished sister of William and Barbara, and the late Rubin and Janet. Caring sister-in-law of Marion and Hilda. Devoted aunt of Lorraine and Alex, Robin and Jay, Mark and Sherri, Dayna and Larry, and the late Judy and her surviving husband Wayne. Loving great aunt of Steven, Michael, Adam, Benjamin, Justin, David, Bennett, Penelope, Mitchell, Leah, Susan and Joel. Hankie created beauty and grace throughout her life. She watched over us with care and compassion. She cared deeply for the State of Israel. She will live on in our hearts and remind us to seek harmony & peace. Funeral service Thursday, May 14, at 10 AM at Sutton Place Synagogue, 25 East 51 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Yeshiva University and Sutton Place Synagogue.
        LANGFAN-Hannah (Hank). Sutton Place Synagogue records with deep sorrow the loss of our longtime member. Sincere condolences to her adored sister, Barbara, devoted brother, Bill and the entire bereaved family. Rabbi Seth H. Frisch Robert W. Berend, President
