
        BONAR-James A., 67. Of Larchmont, NY and Lake Cossayuna, NY, on December 15. Loving and devoted father of five children, all from his first marriage: James B., Monica, Christopher, Marguerite Keller, and Julia. Doting grandfather of five. Beloved husband of the late Eulalie Hilts Bonar. Dear brother to Lawrence and Janet. Longtime Professor of History and American Studies at Manhattanville College, where he was instrumental in founding the American Studies Department and fought tirelessly to unionize the faculty. At home, he was a generous and ever-present caregiver. A memorial will be held at the home of his daughters in New Rochelle, February 2 at 4 PM.
