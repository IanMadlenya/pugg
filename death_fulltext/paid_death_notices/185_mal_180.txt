
        DOWD-William P. The Board of Directors of 201 East 79th Street, the building management staff and tenants note with deep sorrow the passing of our resident manager and longtime friend, William P. Dowd. His compassion, kindness and concern for all will long be remembered. We extend sincere sympathy and condolences to his wife and family. Those fortunate enough to have known this wonderful man will miss him.
