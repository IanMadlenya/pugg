
        PALMER-Robert Louis. Age 86 of Shalimar, Florida, former Vice President and Treasurer of Cluett, Peabody and Company, Inc., passed away on January 31, 1998. Funeral services will be held at 1 PM, February 3 at the Rose-Neath Funeral Home in Shreveport, Louisiana, with his brother-in-law, the Reverend Dr. Arnold Coody, officiating. An alumnus of Dartmouth College and member of the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity, Mr. Palmer served as a Lieutenant Commander in the U.S. Naval Reserve, attached to the Supply Corps stationed in the Panama Canal Zone, during W.W. II. Mr. Palmer was predeceased in death by his sister, Ruth Ella Stevenson. He is survived by his wife of 51 years, Myrtie I. Palmer of Shalimar, FL; his son, James Robert Palmer, his wife Annette, and their sons, Louis and Colin of Austin, TX; his daughter, Dr. Mary Palmer Graber, and her husband John Graber of Indianapolis, IN.
