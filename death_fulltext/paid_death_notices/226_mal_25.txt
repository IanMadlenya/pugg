
        RONAN--Thomas P., 96. Died on October 31. He retired in 1978 after 35 years as a political reporter and foreign correspondent for The New York Times. This gentle man, famous for his abundance of understanding, insight, wisdom and outstanding professionalism as a journalist, enjoyed warm friendships wherever he worked and lived, which included New York City, London, Rye, New York, and Kennett Square, Philadelphia. He is survived by his wife, Patricia, of Kennett Square, daughters, Catherine and Laura, son Stephen, and two grandchildren, Sophia and Michael. Funeral Mass is at St. Patrick Church, Kennett Square, on Thursday, November 3.
