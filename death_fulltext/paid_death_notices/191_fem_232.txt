
        SOCOLOW-Sylvia C., 88. Fifty year resident of White Plains, died on Wednesday, December 25. Beloved wife of Milton. Sylvia graduated from Adelphi College, Garden City, Long Island. She had been a social worker for the New York City Department of Social Services and taught English as a Second Language in the city of White Plains School System for many years. She leaves two daughters, Bonnie G. Socolow and Lois C. Elsis (son-in-law Mark Elsis).
