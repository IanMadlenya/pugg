
        SCHECHTER - Rosalie Co hen, 88, of Harrison, NY, died Sunday, July 28, after a brief illness. Loving wife of Emanuel and son, Stephen, Rosalie C. Schechter was a member of the Honorary Council of Hadassah and a former National Vice President, National Secretary, Recording Secretary and most recently, Hadassah's United Nations Non-Governmental Organization representative. She was a past President of the Westchester Region and Harrison Chapter of Hadassah. A lifelong advocate for civil rights, she was a former President of the Harrison League of Women Voters, served on the League's Region Board and served on a number of town and schoolappointed committees. A native of Huntington, WVA, she graduated from RandolphMacon Woman's College and was a member of Phi Beta Kappa and Phi Gamma Mu honorary societies. She is survived by her brothers, Bernard Cohen of Chattanooga, TN, Charles Cohen of Houston, TX, and sister, Mickey Goldenberg of Huntington, WVA. Services will be held on Tuesday, July 30, at 11AM at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Avenue, White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Hadassah Medical Organization, 50 West 58th Street, NY, NY 10019-2500.
        SCHECHTER-Rosalie C. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of our esteamed colleague, Rosalie Schechter, a past National Vice President, and a former President of the Westchester Region. She was a role model and a mentor to young women in her region. Our heartfelt condolences go to her family. May her memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, Nat'l. Pres.
