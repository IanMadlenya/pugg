
        BARTRUFF-Rev. Louis B. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former chaplain of Madonna Residence, Brooklyn on January 13. Reposing in Doyle B. Shaffer Funeral Home, 45-09 Little Neck Pkwy, Little Neck, Wednesday January 17, from 2-5 & 7-9pm. Funeral Mass at Immaculate Conception Center Chapel, 7200 Douglaston Pkwy, Douglaston, Thursday, January 18, at 10:30am. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
