
        HEWLETT-William R. The Officers and Fellows of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences mourn the passing of William R. Hewlett. A distinguished Fellow of the Academy for over 30 years, his engineering brilliance, entrepreneurial leadership, deep concern for education and the environment, and philanthropic spirit epitomized the Academy's commitment to engage citizenship. His accomplishments and his character have left a lasting mark on American society and the world of learning. We extend our sympanthy to his wife Rosemary; his son Walter, a Fellow of the Academy; and his family. President James O. Freedman; Past Presidents: Leo L. Beranek, Harvey Brooks, Jaroslav Pelikan, and Victor Weisskopf; Executive Officer Leslie C. Berlowitz
