
        LISS--Joseph Hyman (age 88). Of Rockville, MD, passed away on Friday, August 19, 2005. He is survived by his beloved wife Miriam; devoted daughter Marsha and cherished grandchildren Joel and Samuel. Funeral services will be held on Monday, August 22, 2pm, at Congregation Beth El in Bethesda, MD. The family will be observing shiva at the residence of Marsha Liss starting Monday. Contributions may be made to the Jewish National Fund.
