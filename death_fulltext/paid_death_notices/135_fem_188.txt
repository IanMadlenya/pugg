
        WALLACH-Ruth. 77, of 43 Calton Road in New Rochelle and widow of George, died on Thursday, April 23, at Sound Shore Medical Center in New Rochelle. A significant figure, she is survived by her children Mary Wallach and Deborah Wallach Toniolo, her grandchildren Benjamin Wallach, Eric Wallach and Giulia Toniolo, and her son-in-law Dr. Paulo Toniolo.
