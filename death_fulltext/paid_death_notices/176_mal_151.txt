
        LEVY-Gus. Born on February 23, 1907. Died September 13, 2001 after a long and happy life. Beloved husband of Frances and the late Beatrice. Loving father of Elaine (Eric) and Marjorie (Richard). Cherished grandfather of Adam (Jessica) and Beth. Gus was adored by his many siblings as well as his nieces, nephews and friends. He will be truly missed by all of those whose lives he touched.
