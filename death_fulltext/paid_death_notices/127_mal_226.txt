
        DONALDSON-Robert C., of North Babylon, Aug. 22, 1997. Retired Hilton Hotel Executive, served during W.W. II in 4th Armored Division, participated in the Battle of the Bulge. He attended Fordham University. Beloved husband of Loretta. Devoted father of Jacqueline. Dear brother of George of Cazenovia, NY. Friends may visit Saturday 7-9:30 PM & Sunday 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 P.M., at the Boyd-Spencer Funeral Home, 448 West Main St., Babylon. Funeral mass, Monday 10:45, St. Joseph's RC Church, Babylon. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
