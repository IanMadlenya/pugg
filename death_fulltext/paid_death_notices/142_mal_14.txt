
        TESHOME-Dr. Tewodros. The Medical Staff of Coler-Goldwater Hospital expresses great sorrow at the loss of our much respected colleague who passed away on October 28, 1998. A member of the Medical Staff since 1988, officer of the Medical Staff and Vice President of Roosevelt Island Medical Associates; he distinguished himself with his selfless devotion to patients and residents and his willingness to share his expertise with his colleagues. His cheerful demeanor, kindness, leadership and wisdom will be missed. The Medical Staff expresses its heartfelt condolences to his family and its gratitude for his outstanding contribution to patient care.
