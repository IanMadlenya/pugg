
        CANDA--Catherine Bage, 90, died peacefully on Tuesday, January 13. She is loved and missed by three generations of cousins, Oliver G. Gayley and Molly Gayley Gnichtel and their families. She was a member of St. Thomas Church since 1949, was directress of the Altar Guild for many years and a founder of the Treasure Chest at the church. She also served on the Board of the Society of Daughters of Holland Dames. Funeral service will be held on Tuesday, January 20, at 10:30AM, at St. Thomas Church on Fifth Avenue and 53rd St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. Thomas Choir School.
