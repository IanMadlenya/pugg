
        SIMMONS--Mary Bleecker. Columbia University School of Nursing and its alumni mourn the death of our distinguished colleague, Mary Simmons. Her passionate belief in her profession and her school infused her many years of service as Chair of the School's Annual Fund. We remember her grace, kindness, wisdom and devotion. We send our deepest sympathies to her husband Richard Simmons, and their children Robin and Christopher. Mary O'Neil Mundinger, DrPH Dean Columbia Univ. School of Nursing Marjorie Fleming, President, Columbia UniversityPresbyterian Hospital School of Nursing Alumni Association, Inc.
