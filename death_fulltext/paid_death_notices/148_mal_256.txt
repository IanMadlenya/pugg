
        NATHAN-Henry R. On May 23, 1999. Loving husband of Ursula (nee Eisner). Devoted father of Lester. Dear brother-in-law of Mechtild Eisner. A native of Berlin, Germany he began his training in banking before he was forced to immigrate to N.Y. in 1938. His career on Wall St. continued to 1984. He served the U.S. during WWII and received the Bronze Star. Funeral Thursday, 10:15am at Fox Funeral Home, 2080 Boston Post Road, Larchmont, New York. Phone (914) 834-0144.
