
        GREENWALD--Adrian. Born May 15, 1918 and died Dec. 1, 2006. Loving husband of Augusta Berlfein Greenwald for 62 years; father of Randi Greenwald (Lowell Turnbull); grandfather of Ian and Derek Turnbull; brother of Burton (Geraldine) Greenwald. Interment at Arlington National Cemetery on Dec. 22, at 2:00 pm. Donations in his memory may be made to Temple Beth El, 650 Park Ave., Huntington, NY 11743, where he was a past president.
