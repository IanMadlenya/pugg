
        DRESS-Anne, formerly of New York City, on Jan. 10, 2000. Survived by her nephew Carl Cash; grand nephews Kevin Cash and Alan Cash; and grand niece Maryann Cassidy, five great grand nieces and one great grand nephew. A professional concert singer, Anne was the first to sing over the radio to Germany from the United States. She will be interred privately in Kenseco Cemetery, Valhalla, New York. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to St. Patrick's Manor, 863 Central Street, Framingham, MA 01701.
