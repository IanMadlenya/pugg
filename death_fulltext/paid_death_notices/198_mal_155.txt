
        TUZ--Peter. Of Seymour, CT, died Tuesday, July 15, 2003. Born August 16, 1915, he was a self-employed painting contractor, artist, and a veteran of World War II. Survived by his son Peter Tuz, a daughter Patricia Tuz Weilbaker, a brother William Chernecky, a sister Anne DeMoske, and five grandchildren. Services at the Hylwa Wakelee Memorial Funeral Home, 167 Wakelee Ave., Ansonia, CT. >>AD#
