
        SIMONS-Joyce Fields. On February 21, 1997. Beloved wife of Robert. Devoted mother of Richard Fields and the late Judith Fields Alviani. Mother-in-law of Marjorie Vandow and Joseph Alviani.         SIMONS-Joyce Fields. A charming, beautiful, and most special person who carried her infirmity silently with infinite courage, strength and dignity. To her beloved Robert who made each day of their years together ''wonderful,'' to her adored son Richard, and to all who were dear to her our heartbroken sympathies. We shall always remember her with love and pride in our friendship. Muriel and Arnold Shapiro
        SIMONS-Joyce. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Joyce Simons. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, Robert, and to the entire family. William Ettelson, President Michael Roth, Secretary
