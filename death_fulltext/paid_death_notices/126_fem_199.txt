
        SANDLER-Diana, 92, July 15, in East Lansing, MI. She was a longtime resident of NYC, where she worked as a blouse designer. She was preceded in death by husband Philip and is mourned greatly by daughter, Ritta Rosenberg (Barnett), grandchildren Tina Rosenberg and Paul Rosenberg, and brother Natan (Ettie). Funeral services were held in New York.
