
        ABBEY-Albert, MD. Died peacefully on June 11, 2001. Physician extraordinaire, person extraordinaire. His wisdom and compassion will be missed but never forgotten. Beloved husband and soulmate of Joan, proud father of Jonathan, Seth and Joshua, and graduate of Harvard '60. He won because in Heaven there is no illness. Donations in his honor may be made to Dr. Albert Abbey Fund, c/o St. Barnabas Medical Center, St. Barnabas Hospital, Livingston, NJ 07039.
