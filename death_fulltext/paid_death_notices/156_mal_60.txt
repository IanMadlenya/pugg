
        WEINBERG-Jessaja.The American Friends of Beth Hatefutsoth mourns the loss of the Founding Director & creative spirit of the Museum of the Jewish Diaspora in Tel Aviv. Shaike, as he was known to all of us, was a true renaissance man and the guiding spirit behind Beth Hatefutsoth. On behalf of the Board of the American Friends and the entire Museum family in Tel Aviv, we extend our heartfelt condolences to the family on the loss of this friend and great creative man of vision, whose memory will forever be a shining light to all who knew him. Sam E. Bloch, Chairman Stephen M. Greenberg, Pres. Gloria Golan, Director
