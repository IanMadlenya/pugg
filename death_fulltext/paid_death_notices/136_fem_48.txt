
        CRUICKSHANK-Jill Gorham. On May 5, 1998. Loving aunt of Ilisa Barbash of Berkeley, CA., and Thomas Barbash of Menlo Park, California. Cherished sister of the late Heather Livingston Barbash, beloved daughter of the late Blanche Livingston Glickman and Ira Livingston. She is also survived by numerous loving cousins and friends. A composer and playwright, she also wrote music for Sesame Street.
