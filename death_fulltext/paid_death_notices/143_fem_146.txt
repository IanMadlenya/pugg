
        ROBERTS-Flora.  The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of Dramatists Play Service mourn the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague. Her contributions to the theater are measured by her outstanding clients and the theater productions that she represented. Flora's theater knowledge, taste and critical ability, as well as her humor and wonderful voice, both speaking and singing, made her one of a kind. She will be sorely missed. We express our deep sympathy to her family, associates and clients. Dramatists Play Service Stephen Sultan, President
        ROBERTS-Flora. Died December 12, 1998. Dramatic rights representative. Survived by her sister, Janet Sovin, her brother, David Roberts, five nieces and one nephew. Service on Thursday, December 17, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, Madison and 81st Street. >>AD#
