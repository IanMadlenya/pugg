
        PREISS--Fredric H. Age 84. Died peacefully on Thursday July 21 after a long illness and valiant fight. He was a man of wide-ranging interests and talents--a musician, athlete, collector of art and an involved citizen of White Plains, NY. He was the deeply loved husband of Henriette, devoted father of Patricia Preiss-Harris (William), Richard (Cathy), David (Lori), fond stepfather of Henry (Ruth) Blinder, and Jon Blinder. His eight grandchildren adored Poppa Fred: Adam, Emily, Aaron, Abigail, Jordan and Joshua Preiss, Sarah Harris and Hanna Blinder. Funeral service Sunday July 24 at 10 AM, Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, Rye, NY. 914-967-4382.
