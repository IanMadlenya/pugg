
        SIMMONS--Walter H. Born January 20, 1931, in Columbia, South Carolina. Passed away at home surrounded by his loving children on May 28, 2005. He served his country during the Korean War, Engineering Corps in the US Army. Walter graduated from Clemson College where he received a degree in Architecture. He lived in New York City, where his focus was commercial architecture. He was a partner at Copland, Novak, Israel and Simmons. He owned his own firm for several years. Walter loved sailing, golfing and traveling. He also enjoyed dancing with his loving wife of 44 years, Bobbie Jean, who preceded him in death. He is survived by his son Robert Simmons of New York City, and daughter Jean Simmons of Miami, Florida. He will be missed for his sense of humor, extreme organizational skills and his sound advice. No services will be held at his request. Memorial donations may be made in Walter's name to the American Cancer Society, PO Box 102454, Atlanta, GA 30368-2454.
