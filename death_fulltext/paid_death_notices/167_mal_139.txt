
        HUMES-Carl Schmidlapp. Born September 17, 1952, of Lattingtown, NY, died on December 8, 2000. Mr. Humes was the son of lawyer and former Ambassador of The United States to Austria, John Portner Humes, Sr., who died in 1985 and Doctor Jean Schmidlapp Humes, who died September, 2000. Mr. Humes was a graduate of Fay School of Southborough, MA and attended Kent School in Kent, CT. He is survived by two sons, John Timothy of Santa Fe, New Mexico and Carl Schmidlapp, Jr. of Stuart, Florida. He is also survived by four brothers, David Bryant of Woodstock, VT, Christopher Leftwich of West Palm Beach, FL, Andrew Russell of Great Barrington, MA and Francis Cooper of Mill Neck, NY. His oldest brother, John Portner, Jr., died in 1990. His family and friends knew him to be a special man and will keep him in their hearts forever. Service will be held at St. John's of Lattingtown on December 12, 2000 at 11:00 AM. Interment private.
