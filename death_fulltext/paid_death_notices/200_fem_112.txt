
        MILTON--Hilda L. Beloved wife of the late Leonard. Devoted mother of Donn, Ilo, Cindy and Rand and motherin-law of Rebecca, Michael, Eric and Rochelle. Cherished grandmother of Landry, Schuyler, Jesse, Alisa and Brian. Services Tuesday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
        MILTON--Hilda. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University is profoundly saddened by the passing of our special friend and devoted member of our National Women's Division. A warm and caring individual, she served the Division with distinction, and at the time of her death was an Honorary National President member of the Board and an editor of the newsletter. With her late husband, Leonard, she was a respected member of our Society of Founders. To her children, Donn Milton and Rebecca Bowerman, Ilo and Michael Milstein, Cindy and Eric Stern, and Rand and Rochelle Milton and the entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences.
