
        HOFF-Rose (nee Hochman). Age 97. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Ralph Hoff, loving daughter, dedicated sister, proud mother of Herbert (Evelyn) and Claire Greenfogel (Maurice), grandmother of Michael, Patricia, Terry, Stephen and Vivian, greatgrandmother of Aaron, Alana, Benjamin, Matthew, Mark, Samuel, Caroline and Meryl Rose. She met every challenge of the tumultuous century her life mirrored and excelled as legal secretary, wife and mother, corporate officer, art student and accomplished painter. Our memory of her compassionate and unqualified friendship and love will endure as her legacy to all whose life she touched.
