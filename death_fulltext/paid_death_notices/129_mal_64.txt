
        MATIS-Dr. Jacob D. died October 6, 1997 at his home in West Palm Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Rosalie. Devoted and adored father of George Matis and Nancy Dreyfuss. Loving grandfather of Jessica Matis and Leah Dreyfuss. Beloved brother of Edith Helsel. Services Thursday 12 pm at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Roosevelt Hospital.
        MATIS-Dr. Jacob D. Congregation Rodeph Sholom notes with sorrow the passing of our longtime member and devoted Honorary Trustee, Dr. Jacob D. Matis. Our deepest condolences to his wife, Rosalie and children Nancy and George, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Jack B. Levitt, President
