        LOWE--Irwin. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the passing of our long-time and devoted member, Irwin Lowe, beloved husband of Edna Lowe. The Lowe family is a fourth generation Rodeph Sholom family. We will always remember his profound love of family and abiding Jewish commitment. We send our deepest condolences to their children, our trustee, Dr. Frank and Laurie Lowe, Dr. John and Karen Lowe and Dr. George and Betsy Lowe, and their grandchildren, Deborah, Adam, Hannah, Mariel, Logan and Jensen. May his memory be a blessing. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
