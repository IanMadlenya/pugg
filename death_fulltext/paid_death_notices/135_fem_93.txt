
        UPTEGROVE-Kathleen. Beloved mother of Kathleen. Died April 12 at age 83 after a courageous battle with lung cancer. She died with grace and dignity at Calvary Hospital. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street Tuesday 1 to 4 and 6 to 9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 10 A.M., Our Lady of Good Counsel, 230 E. 90 St, NYC.
