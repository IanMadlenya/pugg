
        GRAY--Spalding. The Artists, Board and Staff of Lincoln Center Theater are saddened and mourn the departure of Spalding Gray from the fellowship of this theater. Spalding was an original, who left an indelible mark on his comrades and his audience. Our hearts go out to Kathy, Forrest, Theo and Marissa. A memorial will take place at the theater on a date to be announced. John B. Beinecke, Chairman John Chalsty, President Andre Bishop, Artistic Dir. Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
