
        CHAMBERLAIN-Dorothy E. July 10, 1906-August 7, 2000. Life-long Columbia University law librarian. Agnes Scott AB '27, Columbia MLS '28. Past secretary Women's Faculty Club, ALA, AALL. Theatre, art, opera, symphony and travel devotee. Survived by her sister Margaret C. Gere, nieces and nephews. Memorial service in September. For further information, 718-275-4144.
