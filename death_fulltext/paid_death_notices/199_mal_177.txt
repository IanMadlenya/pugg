
        SCHUPAK--Sidney S. Died peacefully in his home in New Rochelle Saturday afternoon. He is survived by his beautiful wife of sixty-four years, Helen, and his two children Donald (wife Cynthia) and Janice (husband Larry). Also surviving are his seven grandchildren Andrew, Ariel (husband Rafael), Jessica, Amanda, Zachary, Nick, and Sean. Doris Glantz, his sister, lives in Boca Raton, Florida. Sidney and his brothers, Leo (deceased), Nathan, and Irving ran the Blue Bell Lumber and Moulding Co., Inc., in NYC, founded by his late father, Philip Schupak in 1932. Sidney and Helen were members of the Shaarei Tikvah Congregation, formerly Emanuel Jewish Center of Mount Vernon, for sixty years. Sid was also a proud member of the Willow Ridge Country Club in New Rochelle, and of the Boca Greens Country Club in Boca Raton, Florida. Service will be held on Monday, August 18, 1 PM, at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, NY 10710 (914) 793-3800. Family and friends are making donations to Hospice & Palliative Care of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, Fourth Floor, White Plains, NY 10601-4403. Shiva will be observed at the family home.
        SCHUPAK -- Sidney. The Board of Governors and Members of Willow Ridge Country Club sadly note the passing of Sidney Schupak, a beloved member of this club. Our deepest condolences are offered to his wife Helen and their entire family. Brad Mattes, President Board of Governors
