
        GINSBERG--Sidney, WWII Veteran, recipient of five bronze service stars, survivor of the D-Day Invasion, Bridge at Ramogan, Liberation of Dachau, witness to his brother Harold's death while serving together in the 9th division, succumbed at the age of 90 on March 16, 2007 to multiple medical problems. A hero to his adoring daughter Joan and his son-in-law Bob whom he loved like a son, devoted granddaughter Stacey and her husband Andrew, loving grandson Richard and wife Suzanne, and greatgrandchildren Jack and Annabel. We all loved him and will miss him dearly. The world has lost a true hero. A memorial service will be held on Monday, March 19, 2007 at 10:00 am at I.J. Morris, 21 East Deer Park Road, East Northport, NY. 631-499-6060.
