
        FORSCH-Lillian S. Of New York City. April 11, 1997. Widow of Frederick D. Forsch. Sister of William S. Sagar, Jr. and the late Margaret S. Pierpoint. There will be a gathering in her memory at 5PM on May 7th at the Cosmopolitan Club, 122 East 66 Street, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Vassar College, Pougkeepsie, N.Y. would be appreciated.
