
        TERBELL-Margaret Nicoll. Widow of Thomas Terbell, passed away on September 10 of pneumonia at age 90. She is survived by a daughter, Margaret Gerli Whittelsey, a son, John E. Gerli Jr.; a step-daughter, Joan Knowles and a step-son, Thomas G. Terbell Jr. She is also survived by 4 grandchildren, five great-grandchildren and nine step-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on October 18 at 3 PM at Christ Church, Greenwich. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Greenwich Hospital, Greenwich, CT. 06830.
