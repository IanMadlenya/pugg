
        HOCHSCHWENDER-Mary Cornelia Kelly, 75, died at her home in Tampa, FL, on January 20, 2000. Daughter of Cornelius Francis and Elizabeth Rose Kennedy Kelly of Larchmont, NY, Mrs. Hochschwender graduated from Manhattanville College in 1947. An active leader in community arts and civic orgnaizations, Mrs. Hochschwender is survived by her beloved husband of 46 years, George, four children, seven grandchildren, and sisters, Margaret Kelly of Amsterdam, NY, Frances Shattuck of Bennington, VT, and Elizabeth Coakley of Harrison, New York.
