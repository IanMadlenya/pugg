
        ARNOW-Aron, MD. Died December 31. A surgeon during WWII, after the death of his wife and child, Leah and Salomea, he emigrated from Poland to the U.S. He married Tessa Winkler and they lived together for 50 years until her death in 1999. He was a respected psychiatrist, practicing in Syracuse, N.Y. until his retirement to Highland Beach, FL. He leaves his sons Jonathan and David, his daughter-in-laws, four grandchildren and many people whose lives he touched and cared for. Donations in his memory can be sent to The Weizmann Institute of Science (10 fl), 130 East 59 St, NY 10022.
