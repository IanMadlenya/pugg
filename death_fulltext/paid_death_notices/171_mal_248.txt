
        DRIVER-William Raymond, Jr. of Westwood, MA formerly of Needham, MA. on April 17th at 93. Beloved husband of Phoebe Barnes Driver and the late Charlotte Noyes Driver. Managing partner at Brown Brothers Harriman & Co. of Boston. Survived by his children, Sally Midgette Anderson of Roswell, NM, William R. Driver III of Ottawa, Canada, Senta Driver of New York, Mary S. Driver of Woodside, NY, Emily Driver Moore of Albuquerque and stepchildren, Phoebe Caner of Seattle, Josephine Lea Iselin of New York, Charles B. Barnes of Medfield, MA and Cornelia B. Barnes of New York. He also leaves 7 grandchildren, 12 step-grandchildren, 4 greatgrandchildren and 16 step-greatgrandchildren. Also survived by his sister, Mrs. Clinton Young of Alexandria, VA, 2 nieces and 3 nephews. A memorial Service will be held on Friday, April 20th at 11 o'clock at St. Paul's Church, 59 Court Street, Dedham, MA. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the Alzheimers Association, 36 Cameron Ave., Cambridge, MA 02140 or the New England Conservatory of Music, 290 Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA 02115. J.S. Waterman & Sons, 617-536-4110.
