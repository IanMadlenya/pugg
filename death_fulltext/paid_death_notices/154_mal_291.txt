
        ELEM-Samuel A.. Age 88, of N. Miami Beach, FL., on November 20, 1999 after a brief illness. He was a master jeweler and the owner of Bertel Jewelers of NYC. He leaves his wife of 38 years, Esther Kaplan Elem; his daughter, Cathy Hollis and husband Glenn; and a sister, Ethel Liebowitz. He is also survived by two stepsons, Alan Kaplan and wife Eva and Edward Kaplan and wife Madelon, as well as six loving grandchildren. He will be dearly missed.
