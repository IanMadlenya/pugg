
        DUNK-Peter K. The directors, officers and members of Kane Lodge Foundation, Inc., New York City, note with profound sorrow the death of our colleague and friend, Peter K. Dunk, a member of the Foundation since 1968 and one of its Directors since 1986. He will be sorely missed by all who were privileged to have known him. We extend our sincere sympathy to his family. John A. Stichter, President John R. Ahlgren, Secretary
