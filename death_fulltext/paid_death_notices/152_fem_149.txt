
        MUNZER-Martha Eiseman. The board of trustees and the entire Ethical Culture Fieldston School Community note with sadness the death of Martha Eiseman Munzer, nine days short of her 100th birthday. A graduate of the school in 1918, parent and grandparent of graduates, she also taught chemistry at Fieldston for 25 years. She will be remembered with love and respect by generations of alumni and colleagues. We extend our sincere condolences to her family. James A. Gara and S. Donald Sussman, Co-Chairs, Board of Trustees Joseph P. Healey, PhD, Head of School
