
        TIGHE--Raymond. Age 67, of Ramsey, died Wednesday, August 18, 2004. Born in New York City, he lived there before moving to Ramsey in 1976. Ray graduated from Fordham University and currently worked as a psychotherapist. In prior years he worked in the insurance industry for Delaney Offices, New York, NY. Ray and his partner Bill were very successful on the crafts circuit. Bill, with Ray's assistance, was a very talented artisan in wood carving. Ray was a very active organizing volunteer from the beginning of the Gay Men's Health Crisis. At GMHC Ray founded and facilitated the first and longest running support group for care-partners of people living with AIDS. He was a member and supporter of SAGE (Senior Action in a Gay Environment), both in New York City. Surviving are his best freinds, Mary Healy and Herb Pitchford, and many devoted friends, clients and associates. He was predeceased by his companion, William J. Schneck in 1991. Private cremation was held. In lieu of flowers, donations to SAGE, 300 Seventh Ave., 16th Floor, New York, NY 10001, would be appreciated.
