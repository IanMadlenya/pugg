
        KAHANER-Jack R., M.D., 89, of 74 Shoreline Drive, Hilton Head Island, SC died Sunday, July 9 at his home. He was born in Brooklyn, New York, the son of the late Max and Rose Feintuch Kahaner. Survived by his wife Leah A. Slaff Kahaner; son Donald and his wife Helen Kahaner of Hilton Head Island, SC; daughter Susan Caine and her husband Robert of South Egremont, Massachusetts; brother Theodore V. Kahaner of Pompano, Florida; grandchildren Stacey and Rob Marotta, Vicki Kahaner, Cy and Karen Caine, and Marc and Stacy Caine and five great grandchildren, Alex, Brian, Max and Isabelle Caine and Alyssa Marotta. The family will observe Shiva at the home of Stacey and Rob Marotta. Graveside funeral services will be held Tuesday at 1 PM at Mount Pleasant Cemetery in Valhalla.
