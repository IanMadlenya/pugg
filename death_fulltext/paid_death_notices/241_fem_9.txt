
        MacDONALD--Sheila Gleeson, February 1, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, and Southbury, CT. Born November 25, 1917, Bronx, NY. Beloved wife of the late Gordon. Sheila was a retired Vice President of Grolier Inc. and enjoyed a long and eventful life of travel and professional success. She is survived by her loving nieces and nephews. Reposing Law Funeral Home, 1 Jerusalem Avenue, Massapequa, NY, Sunday 2-5 and 7-9:30. Mass of Resurrection Monday, 9:45 am, at Maria Regina Church, Seaford, NY. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY. May she rest in peace.
