
        LEAVY--Morton L., on Sunday, August 31, at the age of 87, after a prolonged illness. A practicing attorney for six decades, he was devoted to the law, the creativity of his clients, and his family. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Fay, daughters Annette and Jane, their husbands, Jack Muraskin and Peter Isakoff and three beloved grandchildren, David, Nick and Emma. Funeral services Wednesday, 11:30am, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Shiva will be observed Wednesday and Thursday. Donations may be made to Townsend Harris Library or the Hospice Care Network of Long Island.
