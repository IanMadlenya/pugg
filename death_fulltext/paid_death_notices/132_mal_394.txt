
        SABBATINO-Joseph, M.D. The Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart and the Medical Staff of Cabrini Medical Center mourn the loss of their esteemed colleage who served the institution for thirty years. Dr. Sabbatino was a dedicated physician who always showed a sincere concern for his patients and for Cabrini Medical Center. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his family. Albert Ciancimano, M.D., Pres., Medical Staff Morton Davidson, M.D., Med Dir Wilfredo Talavera, M.D., Director of Medicine Jeffrey Frerichs, Pres., Cabrini Medical Center
