
        GREENBERG-J.J. (Jonathan Joseph). The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of J.J. Greenberg, the executive director of the Jewish Life Network/Steinhardt Foundation, an emerging leader in the Jewish community, and an heir to a family legacy of community involvement that has touched all movements of Judaism. We extend our deepest sympathies to his parents, Rabbi Yitz and Blu Greenbergprominent leaders and exemplary teachers in the Jewish community - and to his brothers, David and Moshe, is sisters, Deborah and Goody, and their families. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Board Chair John S. Ruskay, Exec. Vice President & CEO
        GREENBERG-J.J. The Hillel family expresses its profound grief to Rabbi Yitz and Blu Greenberg and the entire Greenberg family on the tragic and untimely passing of beloved J.J. A devoted advocate for Jewish young people and Jewish renaissance, J.J. helped inspire Jewish passion through his work and through his soul. May his memory be a blessing. Edgar M. Bronfman, Chair Lynn Schusterman, Co-Chair Michael Steinhardt, Co-Chair Richard M. Joel, President
