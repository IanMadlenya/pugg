
        GOLDMAN-Rose. (nee Peshkes) Of Jersey City, NJ, on August 29, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Henry. Devoted mother of Adele Siegel, Dr. David Goldman and the late Elaine Lichtman. Adored mother-in-law of Stanley Siegel, Elizabeth Goldman and Jerome Lichtman. Loving grandmother of Linda and David, Judith and Jonathan, Daniel, Aviva and Mark, Aron, Alisa and Lew, Jenifer and Angus, Martha and Mark and Adam. Great grandmother of Mollie, Eleanor, Isaac, Harry, Elaine, Patraic, Wesley, Zachary and Eli Henry. Services from Temple Beth-El, Kennedy Blvd. at Harrison Ave., Jersey City, NJ, today at 1:00 PM. For further information ''Wien & Wien'' Jersey City, NJ 1800-322-0533. Contributions to Hadassah, Jewish National Fund or Temple Beth-El, Jersey City, would be appreciated.
        GOLDMAN-Rose. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of our beloved colleague, Rose Goldman. During a long and illustrious Hadassah career, she served as the Chair of the National Zionist Affairs Dept., of the first Hadassah Convention in Israel, and of the Hadassah Magazine. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. May her memory be for a blessing. Bonnie Lipton, National President Dr. Laura S. Schor, National Executive Director
