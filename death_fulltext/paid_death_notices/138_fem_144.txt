
        McNABB-Helen. On July 21, 1998. Of cancer. At Calvary Hospital. Helen was a gifted teacher of English at Hendrick Hudson High School for many years. She was a proud graduate of the Academy of Mt. Saint Ursula and New Rochelle College. She was an active parishoner of St. Nicholas of Tolentine Parish Roman Catholic Church. A special lady who gave generously to a number of charities. She died after a decade long bout with cancer. She will be greatly missed. She is survived by her cousins Virginia Lynch of Tucson, Arizona and Irene Nevins of Toms River, N.J. Mass of Christian Burial St. Nicholas of Tolentine R.C. Church (Bronx) Friday, July 24, at 9:30AM.
