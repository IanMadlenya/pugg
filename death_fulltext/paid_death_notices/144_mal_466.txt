
        KELLY-Harry Martin. New York University's Stern School of Business mourns the passing of good friend and former Associate Dean, Harry M. Kelly at age 86 on January 27, 1999 in Florida. Joined the faculty of the then School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance in February 1947. Served as Assistant Dean and Associate Dean from September '58 through August '73, and as Secretary of the Faculty from 1971 through 1973. Served as Vice Chairman of the Finance Department. We send our sympathies to his wife Claire. George G. Daly, Dean
