
        PEARLSTEIN-Rabbi Aaron S. We record wtih sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in 1964, he served pulpits in Bowie, MD., and New London, CT., before returning to the Washington area in 1980. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Dr. Peggy, children Sam, Jack and Dr. Ora, and to his nieces, Dr. Alisa (Rabbi Neil) Kurshan and Dr. Shuly (Rabbi Gershon) Schwartz, and all who were touched through his rabbinate. Rabbi Seymour L. Essrog, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec VP The Rabbinical Assembly
