
        GELLERT--Beatrice Meisel, 96, on October 14th, 2004 after a long and full life. Beloved wife of Everett Gellert for 74 years, mother of Paul Gellert and the late Nan Silverman. She is survived by her husband, son, grandchildren, Melissa, Leon and David, and great - grandchildren, Dale and Gabrielle. Services will be held in Pompano Beach, FL on Monday. She will be lovingly remembered.
