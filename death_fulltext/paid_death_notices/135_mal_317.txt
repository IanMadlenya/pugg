
        HYMAN-Roger, M.D. The New York State Radiological Society mourns the passing of our former president, Roger Hyman, M.D. At the time of his passing, he was the Chairman of the Dept of Radiology at North Shore University Hospital, and was a member of the governing board of the American College of Radiology. He served our Society with distinction for many years, and we shall miss his intelligence and dedication. We express our deepest sympathies to the members of his family. N.Y.S. Radiological Society E. Stephen Amis, M.D.,Pres.
