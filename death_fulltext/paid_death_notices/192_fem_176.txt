
        HILLMANN - Martha, 94. Passed away in Naples, FL on January 3rd, 2003. Formerly of Brooklyn and Mattituck NY. She was born January 16th, 1908 in Bardonia to Alfred and Martha Kimpel. Martha is survived by her daughter Doris Guggliatta of Long Island, NY, sons Niles Hunold of Marco Island, FL and Dr. William Hillmann of Nokesville, VA, seven grandchildren and nine great grandchildren. She was predeceased by her husband, William, in 1972. He was a NYC Parks Department Foreman of Mechanics at five borough shops on Randalls Island. Burial will be at Pinelawn National Cemetery at a later date.
