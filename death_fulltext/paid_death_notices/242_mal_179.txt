
        LINTZ--Worth B., Dr., respected New York City physician and long time resident of Westchester, died peacefully on Saturday, March 17, at age 83. Dr. Lintz began practicing medicine in 1953, joining his father in private practice at his midtown office. He retired in 1991. He is survived by his wife, Annette Pare Lintz, his five children and their spouses, and six grandchildren. A memorial mass will be celebrated at 10:00am on Wednesday, March 21, at Sacred Heart Catholic Church in Dobbs Ferry. Donations to March of Dimes are requested in lieu of flowers (www.marchofdimes.com).
