
        HOCHBERG--Rita. The Zionist Organization of America mourns the passing of the beloved wife of Irwin Hochberg, an esteemed member of ZOA's Board of Directors. She was a passionate activist for Israel and the Jewish people who inspired all of us. She will be sorely missed. Morton A. Klein, Natl Pres Dr. Alan Mazurek Chairman of the Board Dr. Michael Goldblatt Chairman, Natl Exec Comm Henry Schwartz, Treasurer
