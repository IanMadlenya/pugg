
        GROB-Stephen Joseph. Died August 17, 1999, age 83, Los Angeles, CA. Born Oct. 17, 1915, NYC. Charming, warm, life-affirming, lover of books, music, fine food and a wonderful family man. He will be deeply missed by his wife of 58 years, Annette Kreisler Grob, his children, Betsy and Steve Grob and Ellen Levy, son-in-law Mark Levy, grandchildren, Abigail and Eric Levy and Julia Grob and by his myriad friends and colleagues. Services at Wilshire Boulevard Temple, Factor Chapel, L.A., Thursday, August 19, 3 PM. A memorial service will be held in the East at a later date.
