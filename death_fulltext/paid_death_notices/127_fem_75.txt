
        LANDON-Dorothy Dodge, of Delray Beach, Florida and Lake Placid, New York, died in Florida on August 11 after a short illness. She was born in New Rochelle, New York in 1917, the daughter of Henry and Gwendolyn Dodge. She graduated from Vassar College in 1938, and married Gardner F. Landon on December 25, 1941. She leaves her husband of 56 years, Gardner, her daughters Deborah D. Landon, Joan L. Gignoux and Judith A. Landon, and four grandchildren. Her sister, Catherine Granger, predeceased her. There will be a private family service in Lake Placid on Friday, August 15. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Thoroughbred Retirement Foundation, 1050 Highway 35 #
