
        WADSWORTH--Virginia Higginson, 74, of Cambridge, died Tuesday, August 31. She attended The Windsor School and graduated from Miss Porter's School in 1948. She graduated from Boston University in 1972, and earned an MS from Lesley College in 1973. From 1980 to 1992, Virginia was the co-owner of New England Sights. Virginia was an active member of The Vincent Club and served on the board of the Visiting Nurses Association of Cambridge. She was predeceased by her husband, Charles, in 1995. Virginia leaves: son, Charles Merrick, of Cambridge; daughter, Christina Higginson, of Weybridge, VT; grandchildren, Louisa and Haley; sisters, Patricia H. Biggar of Cambridge, Wendy S. Heard of Wolfboro, NH; brother, George of Pacific Palisades, CA. Burial will be private. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 23 at 2 PM, Church of the Redeemer, 379 Hammond St., Chestnut Hill, MA. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to The Vincent Club, 71 Brimmer St., Boston, MA 02138, or to Chilton House Hospice, VNA Care Network, 245 Winter St., Waltham, MA 02451-8792
