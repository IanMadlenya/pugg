
        RATTNER-David Samuel. On June 6, 1997. Graduate of Juilliard School of Music and Columbia University. He devoted his professional life to teaching and music education throughout the NYC public school system and conducted The All City Orchestra and The Teachers' Orchestra. His knowledge, warmth, thoughtfulness and integrity will be remembered with great affection by all who knew him. Dear and beloved husband of Henriette. Loving father of Nina Gelbart and father-in-law of Bill Gelbart. Cherished grandfather of Matthew and Eva. Devoted brother of Nat and brother-in-law of Miriam. Adored uncle and great uncle. Services Monday, 12 noon at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. & Amsterdam Ave.
