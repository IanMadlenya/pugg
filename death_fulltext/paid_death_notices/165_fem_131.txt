
        KEARNS-Maud, 89. Of Ft. Lauderdale, FL, died Tuesday, October 10, 2000. at her residence. Formerly of Long Island, NY, she had been a resident of Ft. Lauderdale since 1980. She was the widow of Joseph P. Kearns and was a retired restaurateur. Survived by loving sisters, Lauraine G. Smith and Sheila G. Fox, both of Ft. Lauderdale; her brother James F. Murphy of East Williston, NY, and many nieces and nephews. A mass of Christian Burial was concelebrated Thursday, October 12, in Ft. Lauderdale with interment following. For information Fairchild Funeral Home, Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, (954) 763-4488.
