
        SCHWEITZER - LORTEL - Lucille. The members of the family and friends mourn the passing of beloved Lucille on April 4. The ''Queen of Off-Broadway'' died peacefully at the age of 98, working as the great innovator of avantgarde theatre until the very last. Her dedication and perceptiveness will never be duplicated. The Lucille Lortel (NYC) and White Barn (Westport) Theatres continue in this great lady's name remaining an inspiration to theatre artists. The funeral will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel at 10AM on Tuesday, April 6. She is survived by the children of her brother, violonist Waldo Mayo. A public memorial will be scheduled for a later date.
