
        FREIDES--Jack. Of Larchmont, NY. Died March 1, 2006 at age 82. Born January 21, 1924 in NYC to Harry and Esther (Horshovsky) Freides. Survived by his wife Barbara (Greene), sons Randall (Judy) and Drew, grandchildren Rebecca and Adam and a sister Shirley Lesser (Richard). Funeral Service Friday 12:30PM at Westchester Jewish Center, Rockland and Palmer Aves. Mamaroneck, NY 10543 followed by an interment at Sharon Gardens Cemetery. Family requests donations to Westchester Jewish Center. For information Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809.
