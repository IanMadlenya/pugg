
        LIVESAY-Darden Rawles, Sr. Of Stamford, CT and Long Boat Key, FL, died July 24, 2000 in Vancouver, BC. He was 65. He is survived by his loving wife Paulette, his three sons Darden Rawles Livesay, Jr. of Wilton, CT, Gregory Paul Livesay of Rye, NY, and Andrew Watkins Livesay of Stamford, CT. He is also survived by three grandchildren, Anastasia, Darden Rawles Livesay, III and Brandon Gregory Livesay. A sister, Joyce Harding of Emporia, VA, also survives. Calling hours are Saturday from 7-9 PM and Sunday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. Mass of Christian Burial is Monday 10:30 AM at St. Leo's Church, Roxbury Road, Stamford, CT. Interment will follow, Greenwood Union Cemetery, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to The Inner City Scholarship Fund, 1011 First Ave, NY,NY 10022.
