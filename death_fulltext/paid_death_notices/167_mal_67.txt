
        SCHWARTZ-Harold, D.D.S. Oldest surviving member of Class of 1921 School of Dentistry, Columbia University, died on November 28, 2000. Loving husband of Pauline. Brother-in-law of Gail Gold. Uncle to Richard and Carolyn Gold, Nancy and James Shapiro, Pat and Howard Cohen, Robert and Toby Cohen, Steven and Janet Cohen, Paul and Jane Cohen, Charles and Edna Schwartz. Dr. Schwartz practiced in Brooklyn and Manhattan until retiring to Florida in 1973. He will remain forever in our hearts.
