        GRUMBACH-George J. The American Jewish Committee mourns the passing of our beloved friend and long time leader, George Grumbach. Over the decades, George advanced our agenda in myriad ways. As a member of our National Board of Governors, National Leadership Council, and Budget Committee, he helped insure AJC's programmatic success and financial development. As a long time leader and past president of our Metro New Jersey Chapter, he was passionately committed to building understanding between diverse ethnic and religious groups. He will be sorely missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Ginnie, and the rest of his grieving family. Harold Tanner, President David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
        GRUMBACH-George J. The Officers, Trustees and members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, George J. Grumbach. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Jerold L. Zaro, President Harvey L. York, Secretary
