
        PHILLIPS--Barbara-Mae, 59, died December 17, 2004, of thyroid cancer. At the time of her passing, she was a stage manager for ''The Phantom of the Opera'' in New York. During her career of over 35 years, she worked on Broadway, in regional theater and toured extensively. A memorial service will be held at 12 noon, on Tuesday, March 1, in the Majestic Theater, 247 West 44th Street, NYC. Contributions in her name may be made to: Broadway Cares/ Equity Fights AIDS or The Actors Fund of America.
