
        MAY-Kate. Died peacefully in her 100th year in Montclair, NJ on September 24. Born in Germany in 1902, she emigrated to the US in 1926 and settled in New York City. Beloved widow of Joseph, devoted mother of Jack, grandmother of Sandra and Paul, great grandmother of Anastasya and Alex. Services on Friday, September 28 at 11:30 am, visit with family from 10:00 am. Caggiano Funeral Home, 62 Grove Street, Montclair. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Twin Towers Fund.
