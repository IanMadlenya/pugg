
        STALOFF--Charles, on September 30th, at age 98. Beloved husband of the late Beatrice. Loving father of Hazel and Robert. Adored grandfather, brother, friend and colleague. He was an Eagle Scout and US Navy veteran. Principal of P.S. 7 and Junior High School 139 of the Bronx. Services on Tuesday, October 3, 11:30 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
