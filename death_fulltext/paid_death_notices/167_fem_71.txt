
        SEDGWICK-Marian Parker of Suffern, New York, died on Friday, December 1st, after a short illness. Ms. Sedgwick, nee Parker, was born in Charleston, West Virginia, on July 4th, 1920. She grew up in Roanoke, Virginia, and attended Swarthmore College, from which she graduated in 1941. For many years she was a teacher of English in the New York City schools. After retirement, she travelled widely, volunteered at Skylands Botanical Garden, and was active in charities for the protection of animals. She is survived by three daughters, Anne Parker Mills and Dr. Evelyn Moore Mills of New York City, and Catherine Stiller of Amherst, New York. A memorial service will be held at noon on Saturday, December 9th, at the Wanamaker Carlough Funeral Home in Suffern, NY.
