
        GEWANT-Dr. Sidney. 92, of Hollywood, FL, formerly of NY, passed away January 20, 2001. Dr. Gewant practiced dentistry in NYC for many years. He was predeceased by his loving wife Edythe. Survived by adored children Carole Gewant and Dr. Warren C. Gewant (Toby), cherished grandchildren Scott (Hilary), Beth, and dear friend Chuck Momsen. Services Tuesday, January 23, 2001, at New Montefiore Cemetery. For service time please call: Menorah-Riverside Gordon 800-327-9192.
