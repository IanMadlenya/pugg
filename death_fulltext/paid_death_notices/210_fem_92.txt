
        KURNITZ--Julie. Singer, actor, writer, speaker, died July 15, 2004, of complications from Marfan Syndrome. Julie will be missed by her sister, Lyz Kurnitz-Thurlow, brother-in-law John Thurlow, nephews Adam (wife Aimee) and Alex, and a host of friends. A memorial celebration will be planned. Contributions are asked in Julie's honor to the National Marfan Foundation Scholarship Fund, 22 Manhasset Ave, Port Washington, NY 11050.
