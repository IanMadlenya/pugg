
        MASINO-Frank D. A longtime resident of Green Brook, N.J. Died on December 28, 2002. Mr. Masino was predeceased by his loving wife, Faye and loving grandson Gabriel. Mr. Masino is survived by his loving daughters Marilyne and Gerardine and loving grandson Matthew. For many years Mr. Masino worked as a tool and die mechanical engineer. Mr. Masino showed great courage and perseverance during his illnesses. Bask in the warmth of God's Son.
