
        SUGARMAN--Eugene L., 89. Devoted husband of Frances. Beloved father of Robert and Surie, Douglas and Arleen and Risa. Adored grandfather & great-grandfather of Dana, Marc, Zachary and Jesse Zimmerman, Alison, Daniel & Kate Brown, Scott Sugarman and fiancee Kristen, Amanda and Jason Sugarman and Danny Regino. Contributions in his memory may be made to B'nai B'rith.
        SUGARMAN--Eugene L. The leadership and staff of B'nai B'rith mourn the loss of Eugene L. Sugarman, valued leader, dear friend and trusted colleague. He was a longtime Jewish communal leader at the international and community levels; a dedicated public servant and loving family man. We offer our most heartfelt condolences to Frances Sugarman and her family. May his memory be forever a blessing. Joel S. Kaplan, International President Daniel S. Mariaschin, Executive Vice President
        SUGARMAN--Eugene. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of Eugene Sugarman, beloved father of Robert, ADL Regional Operations Committee Chair. Eugene was a longtime ADL leader and served on the National Commission and National Executive Committee. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Frances, to Bob and Surie, and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser National Chair Abraham H. Foxman National Director
