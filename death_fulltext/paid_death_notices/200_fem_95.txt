
        ROSENBLOOM -- Ruth M. (Friedlander), of New York City, formerly of Cambridge, MA, on Thursday, September 11, 2003. Beloved wife of Professor Richard S. Rosenbloom. Loving mother of Professor Joshua L. Rosenbloom of Lawrence, KS, Daniel H. Rosenbloom of Brooklyn, and Rachel E. Rosenbloom of Jamaica Plain, MA. Adored grandmother of six. Services at the Levine Chapels, 470 Harvard Street, Brookline, MA, on Sunday, September 14, at 1:00 PM. Interment at Sharon Memorial Park, Sharon, MA. Memorial observance Sunday and Monday, at the home of Rachel Rosenbloom and continuing on Tuesday at her late residence, 455 North End Ave., New York City. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to the Frances Loeb Library at Harvard University School of Design.
