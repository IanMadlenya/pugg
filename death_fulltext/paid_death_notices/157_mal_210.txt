
        FLOREEN-David A. The Board of Directors and employees of The Atlantic Mutual Companies mourn with deep sorrow the passing of a dear friend. David A. Floreen, Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer from 1970 to 1976, passed away earlier this week in Durham, N.C., following a battle with Parkinson Disease. Mr. Floreen joined Atlantic Mutual in 1946. He became Sr. Executive Vice President in 1965, was appointed the company's 11th President in 1966 and became Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer in 1970. His vision, leadership and dedication to the insurance industry will be greatly missed. The Atlantic Mutual Companies
