
        CALDERWOOD-Thomas Stewart. 56, died at home, May 31. Raised in New Hyde Park, he was the son of Margaret (Weir) and Thomas Calderwood, Sr. He was a graduate of Fairfield University and received a Master's Degree from the New School. He was the first American director of Oxfam America and taught in New York City Schools over 25 years. At the time of his death he was teaching at the High School of Graphic Communications Art. Mass of Christian Burial, The Mary Chapel, the Church of Francis Xavier (enter 55 W.15th St.), Monday June 4, 6pm. He is survived by several cousins and many good friends.
