
        FERENCZ--Michael. Passed away peacefully in his home on Saturday, July 26. He is survived by his wife Joan, children Dr. Gerald Ferencz and Karen Ferencz, grandchildren Jesse, Nikki, Kendl and Alex; his nephew Dr. Jonathan Ferencz and wife Maxine and their children Julieann and Benjamin. Michael was a Holocaust survivor who escaped from forced labor camp, ultimately made his way to the United States, opened a fabric store on 57th St, and was a tireless supporter of Israel. Contributions can be made on Michael's behalf to the Israeli Emergency Fund, c/o Temple Shaaray Tefila, 89 Baldwin Rd, Baldwin Corners, NY 10549; 914666-3133. >>AD#
