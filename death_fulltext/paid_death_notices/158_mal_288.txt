
        CONNELLY-Albert Ray, died March 22, 2000, in his 92nd year after a long illness. Retired partner of Cravath, Swaine & Moore. Husband of the late Eleanor Milburn Connelly. Survived by his daughters, Mary C. Graff of Urbanna, VA, and Jean C. Mooney of Healdsburg, CA, by his granddaughters, Clare Mooney and Alexandra Mooney, and by a great-grandson, Henry Mooney. Services at St. James Church, Madison Ave. at 71st St. on Saturday, March 25, 2000 at 11AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Berkshire School, Sheffield, MA 01257, for addition to the Albert R. Connelly Scholarship Fund.
