
        FRIED-Paul. Yeshiva University and its Sy Syms School of Business are saddened by the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community, beloved husband of Regina and father of Pamela Rechtschaffen, National Board member of the Y.U. We extend our heartfelt condolences to grandchildren, Andrew and Eric Rechtschaffen, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
