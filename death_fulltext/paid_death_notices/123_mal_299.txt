
        RABIN-William M. On Friday, April 25, 1997. Cherished husband of Frances Rabin. Devoted father of two, grandfather of seven, and great-grandfather of 3. Inventor of the modern ice cream sandwich, he was credited with inventing the first ice cream sandwich machine, which led to the popularization of the ice cream sandwich. He was the founder of Wafer Sales and spent over 50 years in the ice cream business. Services will be held Sunday at 12 noon, Wein & Wein Memorial Chapels, 129 Engle Street, Englewood, New Jersey.
        RABIN-William. The Board of Governors and membership of Edgewood Country Club record with deep sorrow the death of Mr. William Rabin, an esteemed member and extend to his beloved wife Frances and family its deepest sympathy. Howard Friedberg, President Eric Segal, Secretary
