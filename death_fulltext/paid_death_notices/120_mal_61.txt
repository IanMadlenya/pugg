
        PANOFF - Henry. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Elmwood Country Club record with sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Henry Panoff. He was a visible part of Elmwood during the over 35 years of his membership. He served the club as a member of the Board of Governors in addition to many committee assignments. He gave of his professional talen as Chairman of the Legal Committee. He will be remembered for his warm and affectionate mien and the associations he engendered in the clubhouse, the locker room and the golf course. The Arthur and Lynn, we extend our sincere condolences.  Lawrence Itkin  Elmwood Country Club
