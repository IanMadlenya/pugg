
        CHRISS-Alvin. At age 68. A unique man of great knowledge, wisdom and vision. He lived each day to the fullest with great plans for the future. His incredible wit is legendary to all who ever were in his presence. His wife of 48 years, Rachelle, his children, Lisa, Alan, Joel, Daniel, Jodi and Stephen, his grandchildren Leslie, David and Drew, have a lifetime of memories and a future inspired by his extraordinary character. He will be missed forever and loved for always. Donations to Jacob Perlow Hospice, NYC, in memory of Alvin Chriss.
