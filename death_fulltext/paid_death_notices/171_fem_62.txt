
        SIEBECK-Madeleine. On April 6, 2001. Born in Paris, France, on June 2, 1912, she emigrated to the United States where she was a well known model for Lily Dache and Hattie Carnegie in the 1930's. Beloved wife of the late David. Loving mother of Suzanne Benzer and Betsy Siebeck. Devoted mother-in-law of Bernard Benzer and Gary Smith. Adored grandmother of Jeffrey and Robert Rosenthal, Noah, Tao and Liz Smith, and Eric Benzer. She was beautiful of face, generous of spirit and kind of heart. Interment private. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave, on Monday, April 9, from 5 to 9 PM. Donations in her memory may be made to Mothers' Voices, 165 W. 46th St, NYC 10036.
