
        ROBINOWITZ-Arleen Shub. Passed April 2, 2003 at age 75. She and Alvin Robinowitz, her husband of 25 years, lived in Florida. Her first husband, Dr. Harold Shub, a cardiologist in Manhattan, passed away in 1976. In addition to her husband she is survived by five children, eight grandchildren, her mother, and two brothers. She will be buried at Floral Park Cemetery, Deans, NJ on Tuesday, April 8, 2003. The graveside service will begin at 11 AM.
