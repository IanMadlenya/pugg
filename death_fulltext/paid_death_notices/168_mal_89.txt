
        TATUM-Craig L., 47, died December 29, 2000. A son, a brother, a friend, a gardener, a collector. There is an emptiness where Craig was; the joy of being near him is gone, but the love endures. His shining light can never be diminished. A celebration of Craig's life will be announced at a future time. Contributions in his memory can be made to God's Love We Deliver, 212-294-8100.
