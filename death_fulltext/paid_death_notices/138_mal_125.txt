
        ROLLINS-Jack Leon. July 5, 1998 at his home in Sun City, AZ. Retired textile executive with Cohen Hall Marx in N.Y., Chairman of the Board, Associated Textiles of Canada, Montreal, and Chairman of the Board, United Merchants and Manufacturers, U.K., Ltd. He was the devoted husband of the late Dorothy Langdon & loving father of Nancy Ginsberg & Janet Berry. He leaves three grandsons and six great grandchildren. Memorial gifts may be made to Sun Health Foundation, Sun City, Arizona, University of Missouri-Columbia ''Rollins Scholarship'' or National Federation for the Blind.
