
        SCHUMAN-Henry. The Manhattan School of Music is saddened by the loss of distinguished faculty member Henry Schuman. A renowned member of our woodwind faculty since 1970, his wisdom, vigor and compassion enriched the lives of his many students. As a colleague and friend, he will be sorely missed by all. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his family. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
        SCHUMAN - Henry. Musicians, Board and Staff of the Westchester Philharmonic mourn the passing of Henry Schuman, our esteemed Principal Oboist. Our heartfelt condolences to his family and friends. Westchester Philharmonic Paul Lustig Dunkel, Music Dir.
