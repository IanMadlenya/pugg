
        SPANOPOLOU-Eugenia, Ph.D. and Hodtsev, Andrew, Ph.D. A Memorial Service will be held at Mount Sinai School of Medicine to mark the tragic, untimely passing of our esteemed colleagues Eugenia Spanopolou, Ph.D, and her beloved husband Andrew Hodtsev, Ph.D., along with their 16 month old son Platon, in the crash of Swissair Flight 111 in Nova Scotia. Trustees, Faculty, Staff, Students and friends are invited to attend at 4 P.M., Wednesday, September 23 in the Goldwurm Auditorium, East Building, 1425 Madison Avenue at 98th Street.
