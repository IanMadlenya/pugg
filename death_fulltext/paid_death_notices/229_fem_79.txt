
        TOMBELAINE -- Verdalee. The Horticultural Society of New York notes with sadness the death of Verdalee Tombelaine, December 26, 2005. Former Administrator of Volunteers and one-time Acting Executive Director, Ms. Tombelaine was devoted to the Society and its Library. She organized hundreds of volunteers for the New York Flower Show and helped found an association for administrators of volunteer programs in NYC cultural institutions. She is remembered fondly by her many colleagues and friends.
