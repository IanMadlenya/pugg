
        BUCK-Thomas, Ph.D. Of Bedminster, New Jersey, retired from AT&T Bell Labs and former Adjunct Professor at the University of Pennsylvania on November 28, 2000. Survived by his wife, Mary Jo Haines Buck, son, daughters, sisters and grandchildren. Services were held Friday. Contributions may be made to the Stonework Fund of Crescent Avenue, Presbyterian Church, 716 Watchung Avenue, Plainfield, N.J. 07060.
