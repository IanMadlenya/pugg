
        LEMLE-Leo. The Trustees, Museum Council and Staff of The Jewish Museum are deeply saddened by the passing of a longtime friend, generous supporter and member of the Museum Council, Leo Lemle. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his wife, Gertrude, an active member of the Volunteer Association, their son Robert and daughter Laura, and the entire Lemle family. His enthusiastic encouragement and vital spirit will be greatly missed. Robert J. Hurst, Chairman Joan Rosenbaum, Helen Goldsmith Menschel Director
