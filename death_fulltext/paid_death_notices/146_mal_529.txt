
        KONECKY-Neal, M.D. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Dr. Neal Konecky. In his tragically brief career, he upheld our finest traditions of medical care and made an important contribution to our understanding of the role of homocysteine in the treatment of heart disease. To his parents, Howard and Anne Konecky, his brother, Alan Konecky, M.D., and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences over his untimely passing. Sigurd H. Ackerman, MD President Carlton Boxhill, MD President of the Medical Board Michael Lesch, MD Chairman, Dept of Medicine Jonathan Steinberg, MD Chief, Division of Cardiology
