
        WEISSMAN-Stuart V. On June 24, 1998 after a courageous battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Roberta, brother of Ruth Vollins, son-in-law of Florence Fielding. Dedicated father of Susan, Liz and Jack Priven. He leaves behind adored grandchildren Matthew and Ian, his messengers to the future. His love for his family and the state of Israel/Israel Bonds was unequaled. He will be missed. Graveside services: Sunday, 11 AM, Mount Ararat, Long Island.
        WEISSMAN-Stuart. The Officers and Staff of State of Israel Bonds mourn the passing of a native New Yorker who served with distinction as a member of its National Campaign Cabinet, Chairman of its South East Region, and Chairman of the Board of its Palm Beach campaign. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Bobbie and the entire family. Gideon Patt, President and CEO Burton P. Resnick, Chairman of the Board Michael Siegal, Vice Chairman of the Board Susan Weikers-Volchhok International Campaign Chairman Irwin Hochberg, National Campaign Chairman
