
        DETTWYLER-Ethan Reid. August 2, 2000. Died at birth. ''He went to live with beautiful God, whose heart is broken, too.'' He is survived by his parents, Rick and Hillary, and by his big brother, Spenser. Services will be held 12 Noon today at Ivandell Cemetery, Route 100 North, Somers, N.Y. Donations may be made to Northern Westchester Hospital Center, Neonatal Unit, Mount Kisco, New York, in Ethan's name.
