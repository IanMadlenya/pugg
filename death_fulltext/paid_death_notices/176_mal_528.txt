
        FRENKEL-Peter J. The partners and personnel of Fawn Associates and its affiliates mourn the passing of our partner and friend, Peter Frenkel. His warm and generous demeanor coupled with his sense of humor were key elements of our partnership mix for over four decades. He will be remembered with the greatest fondness and he will be sorely missed.
        FRENKEL-Peter. The Board of Governors and Members of Fenway Golf Club record with profound sorrow the passing of its long time member, Peter Frenkel, and extend to his wife, Ellen, and the members of his family our sincere condolences.
