
        SALGO--Nicolas, Ambassador. The Board of Directors and shareholders of 6 East 72nd Street Corp. regret the passing of Ambassador Salgo and express their condolences to his wife Josseline and family. Ambassador Salgo was a long time resident of the building, former president of the Board and honored as President Emeritus upon his retirement. Memorial services will be held at Frank Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, New York City on Wednesday, March 2, 2005 at 3:30PM.
        SALGO--Nicolas M. We are saddened by the death of A Hero For Our Time. The Raoul Wallenberg Committee of the United States
