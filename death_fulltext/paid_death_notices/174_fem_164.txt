
        FRIEDMAN-Bernice Block. Died at 81 on July 20th, 2001 after a heroic 55-year struggle with melanoma. Widow of Charles, mother of Jonathan & Jeremy, Marilyn and Lisa, grandmother of Lindsay, Charlie, Caroline and David, and sister of Aubrey. Beautiful, hopeful and articulate, she was one strong and smart woman. A passionate, vivacious, and intensely dedicated human being, she lives forever in our hearts. Funeral at 12 noon, Sunday, July 22nd, at Gutterman's, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, New York. Contributions in her name to the Skin Cancer Foundation, 245 Fifth Avenue, Suite 1403, New York City 10016.
