
        PUGLIESE-Rev. Joseph B. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former administrator of St. Benedict the Moor Church, Jamaica. Reposing in Walsh/LaBella Funeral Home, 8713 87th St., Woodhaven, Thursday, May 14, from 1-4 and 7-10 pm. Funeral Mass in St. Thomas Apostle Church, 87-19 88th Ave, Woodhaven, Friday, May 15, at 10:30 am. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
