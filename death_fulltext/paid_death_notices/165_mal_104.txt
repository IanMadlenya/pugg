
        SIMON-Nathan. On October 5, 2000 at the age of 85. Retired New York Times Typographer. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Devoted brother to Seymour and the late Charlie and sister-in-law Jean, sister Mildred. Adored uncle of Penny, Jill, David, Marlene and the late Karen Lembeck, Randi and Mitchell Bussin. Adored greatuncle of Ross, Andrew, Brenna, Josh, Gabe, Stacey and Allison. A wonderful son to his parents, Sam and Bessie. A lifelong social activist who completed his BA in philosophy at NYU in later years. His social activism began in Newark, NJ as a teenager and continued throughout his life. A born leader who touched the lives of many. We loved him so much. Memorial services to be announced.
