
        BERGER-Stanley. On December 4, 1997. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Flatbush and Shaare Torah Jewish Center mourn the loss of its friend and former president. Our sincere condolences to his wife, Rhoda, and his children and grandchildren. Services are to be held on Friday, December 5, 10AM at Jeffer Community Chapels, 4620 Ft Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn, New York. Interment at New Montefiore Cemetery in Farmingdale, New York.
