
        MEYERS-Corinne. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member Corinne Meyers. Corinne and her beloved late husband, Archie, had been members of Rodeph Sholom for over 50 years. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her daughters, Sherri and Carole, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
