
        APTON--Erna Neu. Of Reston, VA, and NYC, died April 4, 2006, in Sarasota, FL, at age 101. Beloved wife of deceased plastic surgeon Adolph Apton. Survived by son Ralph Apton of Great Falls, VA; grandchildren Kory Kim Apton (Griffin) of NYC and Keith Apton of DC; greatgrandchildren Riley Ray & Jackson Griffin; and many nephews, nieces, and cousins, including great-great-great nephews. Erna respected all religions and her faith was one God for everyone. Memorial service at Frank Campbell Funeral Home, Madison Ave. and 81 St., NYC, on May 7, at 11:30 AM. Donations to your favorite medical organization would be her wish.
