
        ROSS-Rodrick Tyree. 50. Professional singer and teacher, died on January 3, 1999. He held degrees from the University of Michigan and Morgan State University. Family and friends mourn the death of this extraordinary individual and acknowledge his unique qualities of compassion, wisdom and generosity. A memorial service will be held at 5:30 PM, on Thursday, January 14, 1999, at the North Presbyterian Church, 525 W. 155 St, NYC 10032. Donations may be made to the Willis Patterson Scholarship Fund at the University of Michigan School of Music, 110 Bates Dr, Ann Arbor, MI 48104.
