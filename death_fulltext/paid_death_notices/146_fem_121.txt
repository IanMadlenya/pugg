
        SACHS-Rita. Teacher, of Jamaica, New York, died on March 7, 1999. Devoted wife of the late Nathan J. Sachs, she was cherished by her children, Alma Daniel and Arthur and Sara Sachs, and her grandchildren, Peter, Anthony, Nora, Tom, Becky and Jon. She was the beloved sister of Gilda Nelson and Freda Schreider and the late Ellis Katzman.
