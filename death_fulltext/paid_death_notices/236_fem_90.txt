
        GLADSTEIN - Judi. The American Friends of the Bible Lands Museum Jerusalem mourns the passing of our esteemed Member of the Board, Judi Gladstein, and staunch supporter of the museum's outstanding coexistence project programs. The family requests that donations in her memory be made to support these programs to the American Friends of the BLMJ. Jonathan Kagan, Chairman of the Board Batya Borowski, Director director@blmj.org
