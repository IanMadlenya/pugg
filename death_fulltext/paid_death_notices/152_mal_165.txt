
        KLOHR-Rev. Paul McC. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Finbar's Church, 138 Bay 20th St., Brooklyn, September 13. Reposing in Queen of Peace Residence, 11030 221st St., Queens Village, Wednesday, Sept. 15, from 1 to 3 P.M. Mass of Transferral at 4 P.M. Funeral Mass in St. Finbar's Church, Thursday, Sept. 16, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
