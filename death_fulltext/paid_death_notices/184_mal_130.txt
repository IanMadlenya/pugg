
        TOTO-Joseph R. Of Rye, NY and formerly of Croton-on-Hudson, NY died on May 9, 2002. He was 64. Mr. Toto until recently was a Creative Director and Senior Vice President at the D'Arcy Advertising Agency in New York City. He is survived by his wife Maria, his two children Michael Toto of Washington DC and Melissa Toto Purdon of Marietta, GA. Also survived by his sister Joyce of Queens, NY and brother Michael of Edison, NJ. Family will receive friends on Sunday from 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM at Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. Mass of Christian Burial is Monday 11 AM at Resurrection Church in Rye. Interment will follow, Bethel Cemetery, Croton-on-Hudson, NY.
