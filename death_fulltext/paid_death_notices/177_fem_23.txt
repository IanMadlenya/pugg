
        COLLIN-Jean M. Age 42, of NYC, missing and presumed dead in the World Trade Center attack of September 11. Jean was a Risk Manager for Pfizer, Inc. and was attending a meeting at the WTC. She was a graduate St. Vincent Ferrer HS, Grace Institute, and Pace University, and spent her entire professional career with Pfizer. A Stuyvesant Town native, Jean had many lifelong neighborhood friends. Jean weekended in the Northern Catskills where she also had many friends. Among her many accomplishments, Jean prized 3 NY Marathon completions. Her pleasant personality, engaging smile and trusted friendship will be missed by all. Jean is survived by her mother, Charlotte, sister Kathleen, brother Kevin, nephew Matthew, niece Jennifer, and close friend Don. A memorial service will be held at 1PM on October 6 at the Church of St. Vincent Ferrer, 869 Lexington Ave., NYC. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests contributions to the Jean Marie Collin Memorial Scholarship Fund, C/O Ms. K. Dwyer, AYCO Charitable Foundation, PO Box 8009, Clifton Park, NY 12605.
