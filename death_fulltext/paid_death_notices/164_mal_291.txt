
        KLEIN-Edward A. CWO, USAF (Ret.). Died Friday, September 8, 2000, in Pembroke Pines, FL, at the age of 86. World traveler. Burial with Honors at Arlington National Cemetery. A 52 year member of Scottish Rite, Shanghai Orient. He was a model for grandfathers everywhere and for living with dignity despite physical adversity. He will be greatly missed by his daughter, Judith, and grandchildren Ari and Rachel of Las Vegas, and friends and family internationally. Memorials to Chabad of Southern Nevada.
