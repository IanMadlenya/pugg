
        WELLMAN-Edward G. 82, died at home on January 6, 2001. Beloved husband of Estelle. Loving father of Ronnie Berish, Jeri Wellman and Nick Bryan, and Scott and Jackie Wellman. Grandfather of Adam, Josh, Alissa, Joshua, Jonathan, Jeremy, Kate, Eric, and Michael. Great-grandfather of Matthew and Jack. This most compassionate man taught us how to live and to love. He will remain in our hearts forever. Memorial Service will be held today at Schwartz Bros. Chapel, 114-03 Queens Boulevard at 4 PM.
