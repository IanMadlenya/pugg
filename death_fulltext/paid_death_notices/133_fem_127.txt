
        MUNROE-Juliet Garrett. Died peacefully in San Mateo, CA, on January 26, 1998, in her 90th year. Widow of the late Thomas R. Hughes and Henry Munroe. A graduate of Bryn Mawr, she earned an M.A. in economics at Catholic University in 1946. A resident of Washington for more than three decades, she was president of the D.C. Chapter of the League of Women Voters during the 1950's. She is survived by her son Thomas R. Hughes, Jr., of Berkeley, CA, daughters Dorothea M. Bennett of Ardsley-on-Hudson, NY, and Marian M. Hubby of Sante Fe, NM, and daughter-in-law Susan S. Munroe Smith of Santa Fee, NM, as well as six grandchildren and three great-grandchidlren. Contributions in her memory may be made to the League of Women Voters, Planned Parenthood, or Bryn Mawr College, Bryn Mawr, PA 19010.
