
        LUECKEL-Alice E., 94. On September 19, 2000 at Seabury Village in Bloomfield, CT. She was the wife of the late William J. Lueckel Sr. and beloved friend of stepson William J. Lueckel Jr. and his family of Glastonbury, CT. In lieu of flowers a contribution in her memory to St. James Episcopal Church in the Bronx would be appreciated. A memorial service will be held at Seabury in November.
