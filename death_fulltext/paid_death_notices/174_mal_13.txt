
        WELSH-Msgr. Robert E. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the Pastor Emeritus of Good Shepherd Church, 1950 Batchelder St., Brooklyn, on June 30. Reposing in Marine Park Funeral Home, 3024 Quentin Rd., Sunday, July 1, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, and Monday, July 2, 2-5 PM, Mass of Transferral at Good Shepherd Church at 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass at Good Shepherd Church, Tuesday, July 3, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
