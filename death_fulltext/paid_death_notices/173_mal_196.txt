
        MOSS-Benjamin. Born November 13, 1961, in Boston, MA, died June 7, in Prescott, AZ. He was a superb guitarist, a computer systems analyst, ardent tennis player, fan and officiator who was beloved by all children as well as family and friends. He is survived by his father Simon and mother Margaret of Houston, and brothers Alex of Philadelphia, Nat of Brooklyn, NY, and Jacob of Washington, DC, and three nieces, Abigail, Leila and Nina. The family requests that contributions in Benjamin's name be sent to human rights organizations and centers for the protection of abused women and children.
