
        RUBIN--Milton. On October 17, 2003, at the age of 86. Past President of the American Academy of Arbitrators, Impartial Chair for the NYC firefighters and officers, and the International Ladies Garment Workers Union. He was a past member of the Musicians Union and a jazz clarinetist who performed at the Apollo Theater with Fats Waller. He is survived by Freda, his wife of 58 years; his children, Dena Davis, and David and Alice Rubin; his grandchildren Jesse, Gregory and Amalia; and his sister Rose Lott. Funeral service Sunday, October 19, 2003, 1:30 PM, at the Chapel of Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY.
        RUBIN--Milton. The National Academy of Arbitrators, New York Region, mourns the death of our beloved colleague, a past NAA President and distinguished arbitrator for six decades. We extend our loving sympathy to his wife, Freda, and their family.
