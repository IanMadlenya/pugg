
        HALPERIN--Theodore P. Will be honored by family and friends in a Service of Remembrance on Sunday, February 20th, 11 o'clock in the morning at The Mark Hotel, 25 East 77 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name can be sent to The Riverdale Mental Health Association, Jewish Family Service of Colorado and the Study Circles Resource Center (Pomfret, CT).
