
        HAWKS-Dr. Graham G. The Faculty and Staff of The New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague Dr. Graham Hawks. A noted obstetrician and gynecologist, Dr. Hawks served as Director of Obstetrics from 1973 to 1979. Dr. Hawks was, at the time of his death, emeritus professor and honorary obstetrician/gynecologist at New York-Cornell. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. Antonia M. Gotto, M.D. Dean Cornell University Medical College David B. Skinner, M.D. President and CEO The New York Hospital William J. Ledger, M.D. Chairman Dept. of Obstetrics & Gynecology
