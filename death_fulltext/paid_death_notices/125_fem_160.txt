
        HODSON-Anna Marie ''Pat'' Dwyer, 73, of Basin Harbor, VT and Delray Beach, FL died at her home in Basin Harbor on Monday, June 23, 1997. She was born in New York City on April 19, 1924 the daughter of Gerald P. and Anna Marie (Haubrech) Dwyer. On July 23, 1949 she was married in Port Washington, NY to Robert G. Hodson. She was a member of St. Peter's Parish in Vergennes, VT and a member of St. Vincent Ferrer in Delray Beach, FL. She is survived by her husband Robert G. Hodson of Basin Harbor, VT and Delray Beach, FL; four sons and their spouses, Robert P. and Patricia O. Hodson of Port Washington, NY Thomas F.X. and Marybeth S. Hodson of New Canaan, CT, William J. and Karen F. Hodson of Burlington, NC and Timothy J. and his fiancee Amanda E. Daly of Charlotte, VT; two daughters, Mary Patricia Strobel of Eliot, ME and Catherine H. and her husband William S. Bristow, Jr. of Rollinsford, NH; twelve grandchildren, Shawn, Megan, Michael, Ryan, Kelly, Carlie, Nicholas, Caitlin, Christina, Molly, Claire and Libby. She was predeceased by a brother Gerald Dwyer. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Wednesday, June 25, 1997 at 10:00 am at the Chapel of St. Michael the Archangel on the campus of St. Michael's College in Colchester, VT. Burial will follow in Holy Rood Cemetery in Westbury, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to the Edmundite Trust Fund, c/o St. Michael's College, College Parkway, Colchester, VT 05446 or to the St. Peter's Building Fund, St. Peters Church, 85 South Maple St., Vergennes, VT 05491. Visiting hours will be held at the family home at Basin Harbor on Tuesday from 4 to 7 PM. For more information contact Ready Funeral Home South Chapel, 261 Shelburne Rd., Burlington, VT.
