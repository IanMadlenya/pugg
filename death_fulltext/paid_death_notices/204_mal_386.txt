
        GUDEFIN--Michel J., 80, on January 26, 2004, after a prolonged illness. Beloved husband of Claire, adored father of Christine and Michael, father-in-law of Manuel Villas-Boas and Gretchen, cherished grandfather of Antonio and Alexandre and caring brother and uncle. His strength and love will stay with us forever. Donations in his memory may be made to Columbia University Medical Center, Division of Medical Oncology, 177 Fort Washington Avenue, Room MHB 6435, New York, N.Y. 10021. Funeral service on Tuesday, February 3, at 11AM, at St. Michael the Archangel Church, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT.
