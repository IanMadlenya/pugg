
        DELMAR-OBERTH-Alice (Cochran). Loving mother of John of NY, Ken of Stamford, wife of the late Kenny Delmar, twin sister of the late Ann Cochran. Passed away March 4, 2000, of natural causes in Stamford, Conn, surrounded by John and his wife Rebecca, and Alice's aides Mai and Lisa. She and her twin sister danced at the Metropolitan Opera Ballet Company in NY for seven years. She studied art for 10 years at the Art Students League. Her works have been exhibited in the Galleries and Museums throughout the US. She had been president of many patriotic and/or genealogical societies such as the Dixie Club of NY, NY City Colony of the National Society of New England Women, a director of the NY Foundation of Kentucky Women, and an active member of the Daughters of the American Revolution and SOROSIS. A memorial service will be held at the Bouton & Reynolds Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St, Stamford, Saturday, March 11, 2000 at 11 AM.
