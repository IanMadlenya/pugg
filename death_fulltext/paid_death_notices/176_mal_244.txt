
        YAGER-J. Allen, M.D. 97. Of Newton, MA. Formerly of NJ. Adoring husband of Hilda, devoted father of Paul and Henry and beloved grandfather, brother, uncle and friend. In his distinguished 62 year medical career, positions included practicing cardiologist, World War II Navy doctor, Paterson, N.J. Health Commissioner, State Department Consultant in Africa, Vice President Group Health, Inc. and Medical Director Phoenix House. His keen intellect, warm humor and indominable spirit will be missed. Funeral service Thursday, 12:30PM, at Gutterman Musikant, Hackensack, NJ. Contributions can be made to The New York Times 9/11 Fund, City College of New York, or the NYU Medical School.
