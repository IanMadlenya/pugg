
        TRUMP-Mary. The Board of Directors of The Associated Builders and Owners of Greater New York notes with great saddness the passing of Mary Trump, mother of Board of Governors Members Donald J. Trump and Robert Trump. Mary was a gracious and charming lady who as a caring philanthropist improved the lives of so many New Yorkers. Her selfless dedication will be missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her children Donald, Robert, Maryanne Barry and Elizabeth Grau, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. To have known Mary was to have loved her. We shall truly miss her. Jerome Belson, President Michael Kerr, Co-Chairman Arnold Sollar, Co-Chairman Nicholas LaPorte Jr, Exec Dir
        TRUMP-Mary. The staff of The Trump Organization is deeply saddened by the passing of Mary Trump, mother of Donald J. Trump, our President. She was a wonderful, warm, compassionate woman who was loved not only by the members of her family, but by all who knew her. She will be greatly missed. We also extend heartfelt sympathy to her daughters, Maryanne and Elizabeth, her son, Robert, her eight grandchildren and her four great-grandchildren. Her spirit lives on in all of them.
        TRUMP-Mary A. The Land Use Group of Rosenman & Colin, LLP mourns the loss of Mary A. Trump, beloved mother of Donald J. Trump. We offer our heartfelt sympathies to Donald and the entire Trump family. Our thoughts and prayers are with you.
        TRUMP-Mary A. Your warmth, kindness and love will be missed, but never forgotten. Rest in peace. With much love, Richard Levy
