
        ROSENZWEIG - Martin, 83. Artist of Kings Point, NY, of congestive heart failure on Friday, October 11, 2002. In a career that spanned 50 years in graphic arts, he created films, designed magazines, books and exhibits, and specialized in drawings of the human figure. He obtained a BS from City College and an MA from the Institute of Design. His clients included RCA, NBC, IBM, Life Magazine and Time Life Publications. He was respected for his business acumen and loved for his warmth, wit, wealth of information, and wonderful repertoire of songs. He is survived by his wife of 50 years Betty, daughter and son-inlaw, Micki and Richard Tiffen of Norwalk, CT, son and daughter-in-law, Tony Rosenzweig and Debra Weinstein, as well as five grandchildren, Leah, Paul and Alison Rosenzweig, Ross and Jesse Tiffen; brother and sister-in-law, Max and Mae Rosenzweig. Missed but never forgotten. Memorial services will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
