
        REZNIKOFF-Abraham. The faculty and staff of Columbia School of Public Health mourn the loss of Abraham Reznikoff, father of our friend and colleague, Rosalie Acinapura. Mr. Reznikoff, age 88, died on July 16 in Santa Monica, CA. He will be grealty missed and we send our sincere condolences to his children, sons Nathan and Ira, and daughter Rosalie Acinapura, his brother Sam and sister Sally, as well as his ten grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. A graveside service attended by family members and friends was held at the New Montefiore Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY on July 19. Allan Rosenfield, MD, Dean Cheryl Healton, Dr. P.H., Head, Division of Sociomedical Sciences Columbia School of Public Health
