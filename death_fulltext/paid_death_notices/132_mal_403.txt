
        SHANBLATT-Rabbi Sanford D. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. After ordination, he was an army chaplain in Korea. He served congregations in Swampscott, and Pittsfield, MA., Buffalo, N.Y. and at his death was the rabbi of Congregation Melrose B'nai Israel Emanu-el of Cheltenham, PA. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Charlotte, his brother, Norbert and sons, Jonathan, Neal and Brian. Rabbi David L. Lieber, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP, The Rabbinical Assembly
