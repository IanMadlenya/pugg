
        BERDELL -- Beatrice. Sep tember 3, 2005 in Lake Worth, FL. Dear beloved wife of Jack for 62 years, loving mother of Starr Michael, his fiancee Rose, and the late Jonathan. Born June 7, 1921, Bea was the devoted daughter of the late Benjamin and Mary Sosnoff and dear sister of the late Milton L. Sanders. Beloved sister-in-law of Audrey, cherished aunt of Judith, Michael, Jane, Bill, Grace and Trish, and treasured great-aunt of Jonathan, Rebecca and Elizabeth. Bea's sweetness and goodness touched everyone. She will be missed by family and friends. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Hospice of Palm Beach County.
