
        LLOYD-JONES-Donald J., Dr. The American Geographical Society notes with profound sorrow the death on November 29, 1999 of its president, Dr. Donald J. LloydJones. His remarkable leadership, judgement, vision, devotion, and generosity will be deeply missed. In particular, the dynamic guidance he provided to the Society as its treasurer in the 1970s and as its president in the 1990s will long benefit the Society and all it serves. The memorial service will be held at 2:00 pm on Friday, December 3 at St. Joseph of Arimathea Church, 2172 Saw Mill River Road, Elmsford, New York 10607 (914) 592-7163. The family has requested that, in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to the church or to the American Geographical Society, 120 Wall Street, Suite 100, New York, New York 10005. Richard H.
