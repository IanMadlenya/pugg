
        BARDUSCH--William E., Jr., of Madison, NJ, on Saturday, February 3, 2007 at Overlook Hospital in Summit. Born in Mt. Vernon, NY he lived in Maplewood before moving to Madison forty years ago. William graduated from Princeton University in 1939 and Columbia Law School in the Class of 1941. He was an Attorney with Dillon, Bitar & Luther (formerly Johnson & Bardusch) of Morristown for 65 years. He was a member of the Morrow Memorial United Methodist Church in Maplewood and Algernon Sydney Sullivan Foundation in Mississippi. He served in the U.S. Army during World War II. He is predeceased by his wife Staunton W. Bardusch in 1997. He is survived by his children, Linwood W. Kenneally of Natick, MA; Edward Evans Bardusch of Seattle, WA; and Staunton Leigh Snyder of Stroudsburg, PA. Loving grandfather of Staunton Lillian Kenneally; Brian, Michael and Chance Snyder. Memorial service will be held on Thursday, February 8, 2007 at 11:00am at Morrow Memorial United Methodist Church, 600 Ridgewood Road, Maplewood, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name can be made to Princeton University Class of 1939. Services are with Burroughs, Kohr & Dangler Funeral Home in Madison.
