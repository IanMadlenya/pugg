
        GOETZ-Ruth Goodman. October 12, 2001. Beloved wife and collaborator of the late Augustus Goetz. Wonderful mother of Judy Goetz Sanger. Delighted grandmother of Katie Firth. Service Thursday, October 25, 2 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, NYC. Interment services private at Keller's Church Cemetery, Bucks County, PA. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Dramatist Guild Fund of America and Young Playwrights Festival.
        GOETZ-Ruth Goodman. The Dramatists Guild Council mourns the loss of our colleague, Ruth Goetz. Ms. Goetz was a lifetime member of the Guild and served as an Officer on its Council. Our deepest sympathies to her family. She will be missed. John Weidman, President The Dramatists Guild of America
