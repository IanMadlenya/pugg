
        SHEHADI-Ruth Lambert. Age 76, born in Saint Louis, MO, graduate of Washington University, long time Greenwich resident. Beloved mother and wife, devoted advocate for the mentally ill. Her peace, love and sense of humor will be sorely missed. Services Thursday 11 A.M. at First Presbyterian Church, 18 Lafayette Place, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The National Alliance for the Mentally Ill, 200 North Glebe Road, Suite 1015, Arlington, VA 22203, would be appreciated.
