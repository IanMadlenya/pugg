
        LeVINE--Dorothy S. on July 29, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Ralph LeVine. Beloved mother of Adrienne. Former owner of AirFlow Mattress Company in Brooklyn. She gave tirelessly of her expertise to her community and temple over many years. Graveside service will be held at Knollwood Park Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY, 11 am on Sunday, August 1. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Saint Bernard Rescue Foundation, Inc., 800 Elk Creek Rd, Trail, OR 97541.
