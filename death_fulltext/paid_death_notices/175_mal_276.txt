
        DEAN-Howard B. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with sorrow the death of the father of our Distinguished Alumnus, Howard Dean, M.D., class of '78 and Governor of the State of Vermont. We extend our deepest condolences to Dr. Dean, his wife, Judith E. Steinberg, M.D., a member of the Einstein class of '79, and to the entire family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
