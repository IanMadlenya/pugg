
        KOLISH-Dorothy. Beloved widow of Teddy and sister of Rosalind Horowitz. Died in her 89th year at Hospice By The Sea, Boca Raton, FL. She leaves three generations of devoted and adoring nieces and nephews and many dear friends who will never forget her profound kindness, sense of humor and courageous spirit. We are grateful for her life devoted to others. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Jewish Congress.
