
        KITAY-Betti on September 14, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Victor Kitay. Cherished mother of Arlene and Robert Kaplan and Madeline and Monte Wolfson. Loving grandmother of Marc, Wendy and Jim, David & Jessica, Ricky and Courtney. Great grandmother of Vanessa. Sister of Sylvia Glassman and the late Edward Brooks. Betti's remarkable beauty, charm and loveliness are everlasting gifts to us all. She will always be remembered as the most loving, caring and sharing human being. She was the ''ultimate lady!''. Funeral services will be held at The Boulevard Riverside Chapel, Hewlett, NY on Sunday, September 16th at 1:30 PM. Contributions may be made to The American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, New York 10601, 914-949-4800.
        KITAY-Betti. The Board of Governors and the members of The Seawane Club, record with sorrow the loss of our beloved member Betti Kitay, past-President of the Ladies Board. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her family. Stuart Herz, President Ladies Board
