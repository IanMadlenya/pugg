
        CARBERRY-James John, 74, Professor Emeritus, University of Notre Dame, of Brooklyn, NY, on August 27. Carberry, a renowned Chemical Engineering scientist, provided both Notre Dame and the Engineering communities with vision and foresight for more than forty years. Professor Carberry is survived by his daughter, Alison Carberry Kiene, Rockford, IL; his twin grandsons; sisters Jeanne Carberry Brady, Hyde Park, NY and Alice Romanelli, Huntington, NY; many nieces, nephews and great nieces and nephews. Predeceasing Dr. Carberry were his wife, Margaret Bruggner Carberry; his daughter, Maura O'Malley Carberry; and his uncle, John Joseph Cardinal Carberry, former archbishop of St. Louis. Visitation will be from 6:30 to 9PM Tuesday August 29, Sacred Heart Basilica, University of Notre Dame. Mass of Christian Burial 9:30AM Wednesday August 30, at the Basilica. Interment Cedar Grove Cemetery, Notre Dame. Memorials to Hospice of St. Joseph's County, 111 Sunnybrook Court, South Bend, IN 46637-3437. Services by Hickey Funeral Homes, South Bend, IN. 219-289-1000.
