
        CUSHING-Bryant. Died suddenly in New York on January 26, 2002, and will be deeply missed by family and friends. He established a successful consulting practice, Cushing & Cushing of CA. with clients such as Boeing, Maersk, and The Commone Commission, which addressed racial unrest in Los Angeles. He was a member of the Univerity Club of N.Y. contributions may be sent to Class of '44 Scholarship Fund, Northfield-Mt. Hermon School, Gift Recording Office, Northfield, MA. 01360.
