
        FORAN--Elizabeth Ann (Girvin). On September 26, 2003, Libby Foran, beloved wife of John Richard (Dick) Foran, passed away in her 69th year in Toronto, Ontario. Survived by her husband, her daughters Susan and Lynne (Michael Spragge), two grandchildren, Kathleen and Liam Foran-Spragge, her brother David Girvin (Kathleen Brown), and her sister-in-law Jill Foran. Funeral services will be held in Toronto at 4 PM at the Morley Bedford Funeral Chapel, 159 Eglinton Avenue West, Toronto, Ontario M4R 1A8, on Tuesday, September 30, 2003. In lieu of flowers, contributions to her favorite charity, Doctors Without Borders, 333 7th Avenue, 2nd Floor, New York, NY 10001 (www.doctors withoutborders.org) would be appreciated.
