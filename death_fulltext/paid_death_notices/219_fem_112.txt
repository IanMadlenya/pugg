
        VANDERBILT--Gertrude C. Died on April 13, 2005 at Stamford Hospital in Stamford, CT at the age of 86. She was a longtime resident of NYC and Southport, CT. She is survived by her son, Frederick B. Vanderbilt, Jr., and her daughterin-law, Kathy Vanderbilt of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, and her granddaughter, Nicole Vanderbilt of London, England. Gertrude was actively involved with St. Bartholomew's Church, the American Ballroom Theatre Company, and the Ice Theatre, all in NYC. A memorial service will be held at St. Bartholomew's Church, 109 E. 50th St. (on Park Ave.), NYC on Monday, April 18th at 4pm. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. Bartholomew's Church.
