
        KAMERMAN--Morton. Died February 1, 2006, at the age of 79, in Martin Memorial Hospital, Stuart, Florida, at the end of a long battle with heart problems. He enjoyed a successful and fulfilling life and will be missed and long remembered by his beloved wife of 58 years, Sheila Brody Kamerman and children Nathan and his wife Hillary, Elliot and his wife Jean, Laura and her husband Alan, and his adored grandchildren Rebecca, Rachel, Oren, Jessica, Melissa, Danielle and her husband Stuart. Funeral services are limited to family and intimate friends. The week of mourning will be observed in New York City at 1125 Park Avenue.
