
        FINKELSTEIN--Aaron. The officers, Board of Directors, & staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Aaron ''Bill'' Finkelstein, father of Ray Finkelstein, a cherished friend of the New York Jewish community who profoundly impacts millions of lives through his dedication and support. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Ray and his wife, Donna, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
