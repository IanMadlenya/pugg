
        BERNARD-Eileen. The Officers and Members of the Women's Campaign Board of The American Jewish Committee are deeply saddened by the passing of Eileen Bernard, valued member and benefactor of the AJC. To her grieving family, her husband Herbert, her son Lewis, her daughter Margret-Sue, her grandchildren Geoffrey, Debbie, Adam and Claire, and great-grandchildren, Rebecca and Emily, we extend heartfelt condolences on the loss of a warm and caring woman. Ruth R. Goddard, Honorary Chairman, Women's Campaign Board, The American Jewish Committee Stephanie Saft, Chairman, Women's Campaign Board, The American Jewish Committee
