
        CONDON-John J., Msgr. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the diocesan director of the Pontifical Society for the Propagation of the Faith on December 17. Reposing in the Simonson Funeral Home, 119-04 Hillside Ave, Richmond Hill, on Monday, December 20, from 2:30-5 and 7-9:30 PM, and Tuesday, December 21, from 2:30-5 PM. Mass of Transferral in St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church, 94-40 118 St, Richmond Hill, at 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass in St. Benedict Joseph Labre Church Wednesday, December 22, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
