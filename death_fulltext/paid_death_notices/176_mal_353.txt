
        HALL-Herman. It is with great sadness that all at the Chapin School acknowledge the loss of Herman Hall, mentor, brilliant teacher, colleague and friend. Over the years, Herman touched thousands of children, parents and educators with his healing insights and gentle ways. We will truly miss him. Our deepest sympathy extends to his wife and family. Sandra J. Theunick Head of School Cera B. Robbins, President of the Board of Trustees
