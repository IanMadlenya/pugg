
        McKINLEY-Gordon., 74. Died on February 12 in Winston-Salem, NC. Beloved husband of Helen, nee Anderson. Devoted father of Wendy and Peter and father-inlaw of Rachel. Loving grandfather of Ross and Ben. World War II veteran. Taught English at Tufts University, Medford, MA, and Westminster School, Simsbury, CT. Memorial service to be held at Clemmons Presbyterian Church, Clemmons, NC, on Monday, February 15, at 2 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Westminster School or Clemmons Presbyterian Church.
