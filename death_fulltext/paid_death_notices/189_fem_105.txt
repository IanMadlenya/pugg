
        MAYNARD - Augusta Poe. Died peacefully at her home in Southampton, New York on October 8, 2002. She was the much loved wife of the late Walter Maynard of Water Mill, New York, and New York City. She is survived by her stepsons, Walter Maynard, Jr. and John Maynard, her stepdaughter Sheila M. Platt, her nieces, Sallie Worthington and Jean Neer, and six step grandchildren.
