
        WEIL-Herbert. Died quietly and with dignity Wednesday, May 6, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Jenny and late twin brother Sally. Companion of Rose Haas and the late Lieselotte Mayer. He left his indelible footprints on the sands of time. He will be best remembered as a very kind and most generous friend, a contributor to many charities, a past Nobel Grand of the Independent Order of the Odd Fellows Thomas Wildey Lodge No. 257. Funeral service Friday, May 8 10 A.M., at Hirsch & Sons, Bronx.
