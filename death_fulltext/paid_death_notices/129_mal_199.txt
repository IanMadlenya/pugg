
        GAMBLE-Thomas O. III, suddenly at the age of 36 on October 5, 1997. Beloved father of Giacomo FioreGamble, son of Thomas O. Jr. and Amy Gamble of Santa Rosa, CA., husband of Janerae L. Fiore of Baldwin, N.Y., brother of Meg Gamble of San Francisco. Tom grew up in the U.S. and Japan, graduated from the Albany Academy and Pratt Institute. He lived in the NY area for the past 17 years and was working at the NY Zoological Society at the time of his death. He will be missed.
