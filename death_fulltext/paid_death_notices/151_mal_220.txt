
        TESSLER-Benedict. Died on August 11, 1999, in Palm Beach, Florida. He was born in Newark, NJ in 1908 and lived in Maplewood, NJ. His wife, Esther Lilien Tessler, survives him. They were married for 58 years. Additional survivors are his sons, Mark Tessler, of Maplewood; Alan Tessler, of Philadelphia; and a granddaughter and a grandson.
