
        KAVANAU-Annie Elkan, age 89, died in New York City on August 23, 1999. Born on July 4, 1910 in Berlin, Germany. Beloved mother of Sally Kya-Hill, Lina Steckler, Margaret Kavanau; grandmother of five; great-grandmother of nine. Dear sister of Henriette Sonn, Lore Taussig. She will live on in the hearts of many.
