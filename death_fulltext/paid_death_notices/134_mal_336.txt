
        ELKIN-David B. Beloved husband to Margot Turitz Elkin, loving father of Charlotte and Sam Elkin, cherished brother of Ethel Elkin and devoted son-in-law to Zitha Turitz. David was an economist, former Vice President of W. R. Grace and Co, adjunct professor, and economic consultant. He grew up in the Bronx, later lived in Manhattan, and settled in White Plains. His intelligence, vast knowledge, political conscience, wry humor and sensitivity will be deeply missed. Memorial service to be held Tuesday. Contributions to Fund for Parkinson's Research, University of Medicine and Dentistry of N.J., New Brunswick, New Jersey 08903.
