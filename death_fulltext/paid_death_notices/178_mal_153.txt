
        COOK-Frederick H. Age 86 of Liberty Corner, N.J. formerly of New Vernon, N.J. on November 7, 2001. Retired CEO of Purolator, Inc., Dillon, Read and Co., Inc. and Congoleum Nairn. Beloved husband of Beryl (Anderson) Cook, devoted father of Susan C. Murray and Nancy C. O'Brasky, brother of Robert A. Cook and grandfather of Christopher B. and Stephen F. Murray. A Memorial service will be held Sunday November 11, 2001 at 11:00 A.M. in the Church of St. John on the Mountain, Mt. Harmony Road, Bernardsville, N.J. Interment private. Those planning an expression of sympathy are asked to consider contributions in his memory to the Arthritis Foundation, 200 Middlesex Turnpike, Iselin, N.J. 08830 would be appreciated. For information, contact Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home, Basking Ridge.
