
        TREFF-Saul. Age 78. Of Dix Hills, NY, passed away on Friday, December 17. Beloved husband of Irma (Katz), devoted and loving father of Andrew, Gary and Shirley, and Bruce and Maria, loving grandfather of Sarah, Joshua and Melanie, and brother of Lawrence and Selma. A graduate of New York University and a veteran of the Army Air Corps during World War II, he spent over 40 years as a sales executive in the textile industry. A graveside ceremony will be held today, 11 AM, at Riverside Cemetery in Rochelle Park, NJ.
