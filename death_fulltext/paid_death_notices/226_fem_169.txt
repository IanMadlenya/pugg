
        KLINGER--Madalyn Irene, 80. Of Weston, Florida; formerly of East Meadow, New York; passed away on November 20. Beloved wife of the late Alfred. She is survived by daughters Lynne P. Salisbury, Dayle N. Foster and Cara F. Klinger; sons-in-law Brian R. Salisbury, Robert A. Foster and Thomas E. Cohan, granddaughter Samantha J. Foster and brother Nathaniel (Bud) Glickman. Services Tuesday, November 22, at 12 P.M., Gutterman's Chapel, Woodbury, New York.
