
        ROTHENBERG - Robert. Of Brooklyn, N.Y. Died May 18 at the age of 75. Brother of Harvey E. Rothenberg. Brother-in-law of Judith and loving uncle of Alan and Suzanne Rothenberg, Anne and Glenn Roberts and great-uncle of Jessica, Jillian, Jake, Jonathan and Joel. He will be remembered and sadly missed.
        ROTHENBERG - Robert. The Board of Directors of the 360 East 72nd Street Owners Corporation extends deepest sympathy to their President, Harvey Rothenberg, and his family on the death of Harvey's beloved brother. Robert will be missed by Harvey, Judy and their children Anne and her husband Glenn, and Alan and his wife Suzy, who were a constant source of devotion and support during his illness. Dawn Pohl, Vice President Barbara Kumble, Secretary
