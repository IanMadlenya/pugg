
        MEISEL--Irving. Irving S. Meisel, 84, of Scarborough, NY died late in the afternoon on Saturday, June 10 from complications of brain cancer. A marketing and advertising executive for the Browne Vintners Company of the Seagram Corporation and, later on, of the the Schiefflin Company, Mr. Meisel was among the pioneers of consumer-friendly sales promotion, marketing, and advertising techniques from the late 1950s through the 1980s. He also created equally consumer - friendly techniques for innovative packaging in the early days of the bubble pack period as president of MAMM Industries. He is survived by his son, Perry Meisel of Manhattan. Funeral services will be held at Sharon Gardens, Kensico Cemetery, in Valhalla, NY on Monday June 12 at 1PM.
