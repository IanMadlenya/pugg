
        OPPENHEIMER-Janice Wolk. We greatly mourn the passing of Janice Wolk Oppenheimer, beloved mother of Carol Kurtz and mother-in-law of Michael S. Kurtz, Treasurer of the American Friends of The Hebrew University (AFHU). Both Carol and Michael are dedicated supporters of AFHU and of the Lautenberg Center for General and Tumor Immunology. We extend our deepest sympathy to Carol and Michael, and to their children Randy and Jason in their time of sorrow. We know that Mrs. Oppenheimer will be deeply missed by her family. American Friends of The Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs, President Fred S. Lafer, Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan, Executive Vice President The Lautenberg Center Paul M. Densen, Chairman Carmi Schwartz, Vice Chairman
