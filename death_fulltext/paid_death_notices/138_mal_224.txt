
        KOPP-Bruce. The Faculty, Candidates & Graduates of The Postdoctoral Programs in Psychoanalysis and Psychotherapy of Adelphi University join in mourning the untimely death of their colleague, mentor and friend, Dr. Bruce Kopp. Your energy, courage, intelligence, love and concern will be deeply missed. You were always present, always helpful, and you will always be one of the pillars of our community. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his bereaved in their time of loss. Joseph Newirth, Ph.D. Director
