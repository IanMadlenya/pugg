
        LESSER-Howard, M.D. It is with deep sorrow that the Lesser family mourns the passing of their beloved father and husband, Howard N. Lesser, MD who healed and photographed the world creating a beautiful life for his family, patients and friends. He is survived by his loving wife, Florence and his adoring childern, Steven, Robert and Carol Lesser, loving grandchildren Raphi, Uri, Nina, Eli and Courtney and his cherished niece and nephew Annette Karan and Joey Epstein. Services were held on Friday, June 30, 2000.
