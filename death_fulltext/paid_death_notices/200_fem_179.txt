
        STRAUS--Jessie. On September 21. Beloved by her late husband Henry, her children, Margaret and Walter, her daughter-in-law, Karen, and her grandchildren, Samuel, Alex, and Eliana. Social worker, humanist, activist, and loyal alumna (Barnard, Smith), she devoted her life to making the world a fairer place. If desired, donations may be made to the American Heart Association.
