
        WARNER-Dr. Truman A. Of Newtown CT, retired Professor of Anthropology at Western Connecticut State University, Danbury, CT died in Danbury on May 5th, 1997. Dr. Warner had taught at Briarcliff High School, Briarcliff Manor, NY for 10 years, prior to joining the Staff at Western Connecticut State University in 1957. He retired in 1995. Funeral services will be held at the Central Christian Church, 71 West St., Danbury on Friday afternoon at 2 P.M. Friends may call at the Green Funeral Home, 57 Main St., Danbury on Thursday afternoon and evening from 3 to 5 and 7 to 9 P.M. Contributions may be made to the Truman A. Warner Scholarship Fund, C/O W.C.S.U., 181 White St., Danbury, CT 06810.
