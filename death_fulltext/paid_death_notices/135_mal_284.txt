
        PAPAMARKOU - Alexander P. The Cycladic Art Foundation mourns the untimely passing of our esteemed Vice-Chairman, Aleco Papamarkou. We shall treasure and miss his wonderful and unchallenged uniqueness, his outstanding leadership, his genuine kindness, his unparalleled generosity and his unwaivering friendship. Aleco's innate intelligence coupled with his boundless energy served as an inspiration to all who knew him. We extend our deepest sympathy to Aleco's sister Lisa and her husband Keith Jewell. Dolly Goulandris, Honorary Director Shelby White, Chairman Estelle Sotirhos, President Basil P. Goulandris, Treasurer John G. Coumantaros, Secretary
