
        REISER--Elaine D. (nee Neuman). Died July 22, 2005 in Washington D.C., loved and remembered today and all days by her children, David, Barbara, Jonathan and Leonore and her grandchildren, Krista, Patrick, Hannah and Zoe. A graduate of George Washington H.S. and the University of Michigan, she later overcame deafness and other consequences of Cogan's Syndrome to earn two Master's degrees and to lead a successful career as a medical records administrator. An example of determination and tenacity to her family and friends, a devoted parent and grandparent. A lover of music, travel and truths.
