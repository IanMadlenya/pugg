
        GORDON-Irving. Retired Senior Operating Engineer of The New York Times. On December 31, 2000 at 92 years in Monroe Township, N.J. Dedicated and loving husband of his late wife Rose and his late partner Gertie. A strong and loyal workingman and a sensitive listener who provided thoughtful counsel to those he loved, lived and worked with. Devoted father to sons Martin, Donald and Allen and daughters-inlaw Lenore, Patricia and Christina. Loving and caring grandfather of Beth, Wendy, Stacey, Peter, Brian, Rachel and Alice. Great-grandfather of Matthew, Zachary, Katy and Noah. Faithful brother to Jenny, Meyer and Celia. Services and burial took place in Delray Beach, Floriada on January 3, 2001.
