
        ZOREK--Warren W., Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with the deepest of sorrow the death of our cherished and esteemed member Winnie Zorek. His devotion to and work for the community at large was extraordinary and his commitment to our Temple exemplary. May his loved ones be comforted by his memory. His legacy will be a blessing to all. Rabbi David M. Posner Marcia Waxman, President George Asch, Secretary
        $(6$)ZOREK--Warren W. (Winnie), on December 22, 2006, in his 82nd year after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Jane, devoted father of Jennifer Pressman and Michael Zorek, father-in-law to Mark Pressman and Shelly Friedland, adoring grandfather of Jessica, Jeremy, Jeffrey and Diana. For four decades, an invaluable volunteer with the American Red Cross serving as a disaster relief coordinator, locally, nationally and internationally. Services Tuesday, December 26, 10:30am, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street. A memorial service will also be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Red Cross.
