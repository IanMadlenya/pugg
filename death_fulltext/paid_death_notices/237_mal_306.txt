
        GRASSO--Anthony 84, of Briarcliff Manor, NY died of cardio-pulmonary arrest on October 26, 2006. Mr. Grasso, a structural engineer, worked with Weiskopf & Pickworth Consulting Engineers for 38 years, 27 years as a partner, before retiring in 1987. He is survived by his beloved wife of 54 years, Maria Rita Leonessa Grasso, three adoring daughters, Lisa Benson (sonin-law Michael) of BayRidge, NY, Rita Lascaro (son-in-law Robert) of NYC, and Patricia Grasso of Lexington, MA, and three special grandchildren, Antonia, Tekla, and Matthew. His intelligence, advice, and support will be greatly missed by his loving family and friends.
