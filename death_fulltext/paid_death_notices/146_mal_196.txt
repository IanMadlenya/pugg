
        DiMAGGIO-Joe. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Joe DiMaggio.         DiMAGGIO-Joseph P. The members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and friend, Joseph DiMaggio, and send condolences to his family.
