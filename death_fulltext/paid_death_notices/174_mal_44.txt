
        ARMSTRONG-Richard. Of Old Tappan, NJ, died on Friday, June 29. He is survived by his wife, Virginia, his stepdaughter Jean Rettig-Carr & her husband Daniel Carr, and his granddaughter, Jessica. Burial services were private. A memorial mass will be celebrated at St. Pius Church, Old Tappan, July 7, at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Christophers, 12 East 48th St., NY, NY 10107, or the Hospice Program of Hackensack University Medical Center, 30 Prospect Ave., Hackensack, NJ 07601 or to a charity of your choice. Mr. Armstrong was a writer and fundraiser. He is a former director of The Christophers, and the author of ''Out to Change the World,'' a biography of James Keller, M.M., the founder of The Christophers.
