
        SILVER--Florence. In her 90th year. Beloved wife of the late Samuel. Adored mother of Penni S. Zola, Michael Silver and Dr. Bennett (Gail) Silver. Cherished grandmother of Stephanie, Gregory, Scott and Pamela. Great grandmother of her beloved Julia Raphaela. Sister of Elsie Cohen and the late Selma Schoen. She was a beautiful and elegant woman. You will never be forgotten.
