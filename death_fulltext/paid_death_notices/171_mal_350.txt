
        ABELOFF-Michael. Left this world peacefully on April 26, 2001 at his home in the company of loved ones including his wife, Suzanne & two daughters, Shanna and Michelle. At age 62, loved by many, he completed his life's work with great strength and courage. For many years, Michael worked as a city planner and housing developer. Living at 382 Central Park West for 30 years, he was an active community member and leader. In his last four years, he served with The PRASAD Project, carrying out their charitable work in India's Tan-sa Valley and in Sullivan Co., N.Y. Contributions may be offered to the work he loved. The PRASAD Project, 465 Brickman Road, Hurleyville, New York 12747, 845-434-0376.
