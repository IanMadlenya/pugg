
        BHATTACHARJI--Pares C. On August 5, 2006. Beloved husband of Arati, and father of Sandip. He first came to the United States in 1947 as a graduate student and after returning to India for several years, moved to New York City permanently in 1958. He worked for the New York City Planning Commission from 1970 until his retirement in 1995. We will miss him so much.
