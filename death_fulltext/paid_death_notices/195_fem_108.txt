
        CHARNOW - Miriam Sare, age 79, of Reston, VA died on April 12, 2003 at her home. She was born in New York City, NY on September 9, 1923 to the late Harry L. and Hadassah Dondes Sare. She will be missed by Sally and Gary, Karen and Charlie and their children: Noam, Mika, Ian and Hannah and her sister Aviva. Shiva will be held Friday April 18th, 2-7 PM at 275 Windsor Place, Brooklyn, NY. A memorial service was held on Tuesday, April 15 in Reston, VA. Family suggests that Memorial Contributions may be made to an organization devoted to peace and justice or the Humane Society. For information Adams - Green Funeral Home, Herndon, VA.
