
        GADSDEN-Charles C. 78, of Pelham, NY, and Little Compton, RI, died Saturday, March 2, 2002. Husband of Marie Dittmann Gadsden for 55 years. He is survived by his loving wife, his six children, Charles, James, Lavinia, Victoria, Philip, Pamela and his eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church in Pelham on Wednesday, March 6, at 2 PM.
