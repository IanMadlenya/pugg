
        LAGER--Fred, on March 20, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Marilyn (Laikin). Loving father of Susan Jaffe and Richard and Ivy Lager. Cherished ''Popper'' of Matthew and Ross. Devoted brother of Roslyn Taubin and brotherin-law of Adele Weinstock. Admired uncle and mentor and friend to many. Former Vice President of Advest Inc., and devoted public servant who, during his 28 year tenure as the first Mayor of the Village of Atlantic Beach, not only led the fight to save the oceanfront from private development, but was actually the primary force in establishing Atlantic Beach as a village in 1962. Services at ''Boulevard-Riverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island, 11:30 AM, Tuesday.
