
        KASMAN--Mort. Award-winning independent producer/ director, died peacefully May 16 at 76. He resided in NYC and Shelter Island. A member of the DGA and Shelter Island Yacht Club, he was a lover of life and mentor to many. Mort is missed by his loving wife Sherrill, son Ian, grandchilren Haley and Ocean, sister Sandi, niece Samantha. Bear, I love you, Sherrill.
