
        KLEINBAUM-Max M. (87). Beloved husband of Josephine (Leve) for 50 years. Father of Paul, Sara Dena, Aaron and Sharon. Father-in-law of Michele Kleinbaum, Tami Furman and Margaret Moers Wenig. Proud grandfather of Jennifer, Derek, D.J., Rebecca, Rachel, Ethan, Jacob, Liba and Molly. He gave forty years to Jewish communal service including Founding Executive Director of the Jewish Welfare Council of Bergen County (United Jewish Community). Funeral today at 1:30 at Gutterman-Musicant in Hackensack, NJ. Contributions may be made to Congregation Beth Simchat Torah, 57 Bethune Street, NY, NY 10014 or Mazon: A Jewish Response to Hunger.
        KLEINBAUM-Max. The Board of Directors, the members, and the staff of Congregation Beth Simchat Torah record with profound sorrow the passing of cherished member, Max Kleinbaum, beloved husband of Josephine and father of CBST's Rabbi Sharon Kleinbaum. The congregation extends its deepest sympathy to Josephine and Rabbi Kleinbaum, to Rabbi Margaret Moers Wenig, Liba and Molly Rubenstein, and the entire Kleinbaum family. May his memory be for a blessing and the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Contributions may be made to CBST, 57 Bethune Street, New York, New York 10014. Yolanda Potasinski, President Asst. Rabbi Roderick Young Congregation Beth Simchat Torah
