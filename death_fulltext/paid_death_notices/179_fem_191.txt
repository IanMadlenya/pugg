
        COLOMBOS-Amalia M. Of Malba, New York and formerly of Manchester, New Hampshire. Died on Dec. 25, 2001 at her home after a sudden illness. Born in Fourka, Greece. She was the daughter of Constantinos G. & Haidou (Florou) Zioze. She moved to NYC after her marriage to Michael N. Colombos who predeceased her. She was a member of The Saint Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church in Flushing, NY. Family members included her son Nicholas M. Colombos and his wife Alexandra of Whitestone, NY; a sister Soultana G. Mokas; and two brothers, George K. and John K. Zioze, all of Manchester, NH; and several nieces and nephews. Services were held Thursday at Frederick Funeral Home. Funeral services will be held today at 9AM at Saint Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church. Burial will follow at Flushing Cemetery. Memoral donations may be made to the Saint Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church, 196-10 Northern Blvd, Flushing, NY 11358.
