
        LIEBMAN-Bernard. Commercial photographer, 86, husband, father, father-in-law, grandfather, brother, and veteran of World War II, died October 24 of complications from lung cancer. Mr. Liebman was a staff sergeant during World War II and served as a photographer in the Army Signal Corps. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Lila; a son, Eric; daughter, Amy; and daughter-in-law, Monica. Grandfather to Jeffrey and Carla. Brother to Estelle Scordato. Private funeral services were held on Friday.
