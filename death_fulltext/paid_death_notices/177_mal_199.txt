
        HENIG-Philip Edward, M.D. Devoted husband of Carolyn, his wife of 61 years, loving father of Andrea Clair, her husband Davis Iszard and the predeceased Peter Henig, and beloved grandfather of Bradley and Elizabeth Clair, died at home on October 12, 2001 at the age of 88, after a long illness. Philip received honors as student, soldier, physician, medical administrator and teacher. Always he served rich and poor with equal energy and dignity. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St, Sunday, October 14th, 2-5 PM.
