        ARMSTRONG--Sheila H. The Board of Directors of the Knickerbocker Greys mourns the passing of their esteemed Board member and former president. Her much valued guidance and leadership will be sorely missed. Our deepest condolences to her family. Mrs. Richard S. Bright, President
