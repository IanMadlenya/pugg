
        MACALUSO-Carmella A. December 18, 2000, age 79 after a long illness. Beloved sister of Connie M. Carvagno of Roselle Park, NJ, and Grace V. Macaluso, MD, of Brooklyn, NY. She had numerous nieces and nephews. She was employed by the Port Authority of NY and NJ for 40 years as a management analyst. Services at Mastapeter Funeral Home, 400 Faitoute Ave., Roselle Park, Thursday, December 21. (908) 245-1558. Funeral Mass at 9AM, Friday, Church of the Assumption, Roselle Park.
