
        KANREK-Sidney. Of Brooklyn, NY, born in Poland in 1906, passed away peacefully at home on July 30, 2002 after a wonderful and eventful life. Beloved husband of the late Aurelia, cherished father of Marilyn and Victoria, devoted grandfather of David, he was always a loving & caring person. Funeral services at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Avenue, NYC at noon on July 31. Interment at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. The family requests that in lieu of flowers donations be made to the UJA/Federation of NYIsrael Emergency Fund.
