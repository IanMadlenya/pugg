
        WHITMORE--George M., Jr., of Greenwich, CT, died February 16, 2007 after a long battle with lymphoma. He is survived by his wife Priscilla (Penny) Whitmore, his daughter, Elizabeth (Laurie) Lippincott, and sons George Norman Whitmore and Stephen Bradford Whitmore and four grandchildren. Services will be held at Second Congregational Church, 139 East Putnam Ave., Greenwich, CT, February 22 at 11 am.
