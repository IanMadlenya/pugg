
        SIENKIEWICZ--Patricia Davis, 74 of Princeton, NJ, died Friday October 13th, 2006 at home. Born in Raleigh, NC. She was a Princeton resident since 1954. Patricia was a member of the Stoney Brook Garden Club. Mrs. Sienkiewicz is survived by her husband John C., two sons Mark P., Peter C. A memorial service will be held at 11:00am on Thursday October 19th, Trinity Church, 33 Mercer Street, Princeton. Burial will be private and at the convenience of the family. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to Princeton Area Community Foundation, 15 Princess Road, Lawrenceville, NJ 08648.
