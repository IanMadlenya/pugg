
        KELLER-Samuel Stephen. Age 71 of Woodcliff Lake, N.J., February 3. Beloved husband and best friend of Sonia, devoted father and hero of Marian, Allen and Jeffrey, devoted father-in-law of Glenn, Suzy and Adina. Loving brother of Iris, loving grandfather of Andrew and Rachel. He was born in Paterson, N.J. He graduated from New York University School of Dentistry and practiced for 46 years in Paramus, New Jersey. He was adored by his family, friends and patients. Services are 12 noon, Thursday at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
