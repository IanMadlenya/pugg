
        HESS-Hildegard, 95. Died on Thursday, January 24 at home in Lakewood, New Jersey. Born and raised in Berlin, Germany, she came to New York after World War II and was a dietician at Mt. Sinai Hospital until her retirement. She loved music & had a beautiful singing voice. She was the sister of Louis and Arnulf Hess, and is survived by two nieces, Sally and Ronnie, a nephew, Florian, and a cousin, Ilse Michel. The family thanks the friends who cared for her in her last years.
