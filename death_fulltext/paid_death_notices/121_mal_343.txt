
        SEACAT-Milvoy (Mel), died early morning January 22, of pulmonary failure. Mel was a medical sociologist, a professor at New York Medical College, a co-founder of Printique High-Fashion Fabrics and a dedicated shopper. Funeral and burial will be in Marion, IN. He is survived by his companion of 44 years, Jack Labow, and his sister, Maxine Beehler of Louisville, CO.
