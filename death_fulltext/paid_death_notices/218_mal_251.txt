
        UPPMAN -- Theodor. On March 17, 2005. Longtime baritone at the Metropolitan Opera. The family will receive friends informally on Thursday, March 24, 3PM to 6PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        UPPMAN--Theodor. The Board of Directors of the William Matheus Sullivan Musical Foundation mourns the passing of their beloved friend, Theodor Uppman. His unparalleled artistry, his keen ear and judgement, and his delight in the talents of young singers made him a treasured colleague. We extend our love and deepest sympathy to his wife Jean, and his family. Barbara Bliss, President
