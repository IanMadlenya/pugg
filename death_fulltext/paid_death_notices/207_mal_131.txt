
        DOLAN--William J. 67. Died April 9, 2004. Survived by Dolores, beloved wife of 40 years; adoring children, Moria, Diane, Dr. Robert Dolan; brothers Thomas and John of Newburgh, NY; and sister Margaret Indilla of Franklin, VA; grandchildren, Rachel, Isabel, Neil, Teague, Ian; his sons-in-law David and Mike; and numerous relatives, friends and colleagues. Bill was a retired faculty member of the Cell Biology Department of New York University Medical Center. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to the William J. Dolan Scholarship Fund for Medical Students at NYU Medical Center.
        DOLAN--William J. Bill, you were my winter, summer, spring...my everything. Dolores
