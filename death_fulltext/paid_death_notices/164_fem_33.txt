
        MODELL-Judy. A native of Philadelphia, on September 6, 2000 after a long illness. She is survived by her sister Evelyn Pantell and a nephew Richard Straff. She was an avocational watercolor landscape artist, former model, public relations executive and more recently a real estate salesperson. Funeral to be held at All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Ave., at 11AM on September 11.
