
        McCOLLUM-Katharine. Died at home on Saturday, June 3, 2000, at age 76. She was a dear aunt to Judy and Peter Brase. Daughter of Peter C. Brase and Katharine Stark Brase of Little Neck. She served in the U.S. Army in World War II, graduated from Barnard College, and taught in the New York public school system. The Yankees have lost their most devoted fan.
