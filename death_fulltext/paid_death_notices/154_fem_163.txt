
        JACOBSON-Martha Goodstein. Age 84, of Hickory, NC. Died November 20, 1999. Beloved wife of Archie Kirschman and Ben Jacobson; cherished mother of Barry J. Jacobson and Pauline K. Lavitt; devoted grandmother of Ari, Adam, Brette, Spiro and Stefano Jacobson and Howard and Arthur Lavitt; adored great-grandmother to Brent, Jacqueline and Austin Lavitt. A true woman of valor with enormous kindness and compassion. She will be missed sorely by her loving family and friends. Make God grant her a seat at his table. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, 9:30 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel in Manhattan.
