
        DAVIES-Laurence E. Of Huntington, L.I., formerly of Flushing, New York on December 12, 1999. Adored grandfather of William and Philip. Dear father-in-law of Anthony Stiger & brother of Baron I. Shacklette. Friends may call at the Francis P. DeVine Funeral Home Inc., 293 South St., Oyster Bay, L.I. on Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Service, Wednesday, 11 A.M., at the funeral home.
