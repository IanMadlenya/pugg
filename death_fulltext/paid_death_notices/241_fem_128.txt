
        LAWRENCE--Joan A. 77, long-time resident of the Apthorp, died peacefully at home after a long struggle with breast cancer. Widow of producer and director Bert Lawrence, she is survived by her children, Steve, Jeremy, Paul, and Sarah Lawrence, and her grandchildren, Gabriel, Alex, Joseph, Sophie, and Julia, and her greatgrandchildren, Vivian and Fiona, and her dog, Melpomene. Her life will be remembered in our hearts. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to Susan G. Komen for the Cure, www.komen.org.
