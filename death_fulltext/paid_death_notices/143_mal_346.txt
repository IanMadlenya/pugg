
        COVELLI-Patrick. The National Kidney Foundation of New York/ New Jersey mourns the untimely passing of Patrick Covelli, father, husband, son, brother. The longtime support and involvement of the Covelli family in the struggle against kidney disease has been a blessing to our foundation and the people we serve. Pat Covelli is survived by his courageous and loving family including brothers John and Ralph. John Covelli is a Vice President of the Foundation. Our love and support go to the Covelli family. Ira Greifer, M.D., President
