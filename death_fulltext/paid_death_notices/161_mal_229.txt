
        SNITZER-Albert R. ''Allie'', CLU, ChFC, of Murray Hill, New Jersey on June 14, 2000. Husband of Leah Goldberg Snitzer. Father of John R. of Murray Hill, New Jersey and Mrs. Cathy Kneese of Chatham, New Jersey. Father-in-law of Kenneth Kneese. Also survived by 2 grandchildren, Tamara and Sean Kneese. A Memorial Service was held at the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, Chatham, New Jersey on Saturday, June 17. Those who wish may contribute in his memory to the University of Virginia Fund, PO Box 3446, Dept. of Latin, Greek and Archaeology, Charlottesville, Virgina, 22903.
