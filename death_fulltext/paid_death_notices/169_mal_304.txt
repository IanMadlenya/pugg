
        GLEASON-Charles L., Jr. 82. Of Vero Beach, FL, and Stinson Lake, NH. Died January 30, 2001, after a brief illness. He served in the U.S. Army as a Medical Administrative Officer in World War II, and during The Korean War. He graduated from The University of Massachusetts and received an MBA from Columbia University. After working at General Electric, he was employed by Time, Inc., for 33 years where he was Vice President and Director of Personnel. He is survived by his wife of 58 years, Martha, and four children, Charles III, of Glen Cove, NY, David, of Vallejo, CA, Gail Beamer, of Boston, MA, and Scot, of NYC.
