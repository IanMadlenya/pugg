
        LEVINSON-Anne E. On October 25, 1998 at Berkshire Place, Pittsfield, MA, after a long decline. Formerly of 25 Sutton Place South. Devoted wife of the late Morris. Loving mother of Joan L. Silverman and mother-in-law of Richard A. Silverman. Grandmother of Michael, Elizabeth Haddad and Katharine, and great-grandmother. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Sutton Place Chapter of Hadassah.
