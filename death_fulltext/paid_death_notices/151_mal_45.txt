
        REGISTER-Albert Layton III. Of Edgartown, Mass., formerly of Rumson, NJ, on July 27, after a long illness. Survived by his wife Hope Register, sons Croft of Wayne, PA, Frederick of Phoenix, AZ, daughter Candace Langan of Jamestown, RI, & six grandchildren, sister Cornelia R. (Porter) Hoagland of Noank, CT, brother William Barrett Register of New York City. Also stepchildren Kent Ransom Costikyan III, Timothy W., and Warren K. Services were held at The Edgartown Federated Church on July 30. Contributions may be made to Alzheimers Association of Massachusetts, POB 953, Barnstable, MA. 02630.
