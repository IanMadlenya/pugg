
        FRIEDMAN - William, 86, died on December 30, 1996, in Palo Alto. Bloved husband of Jeanette, father of Avra and Paul, brother of Rose, grandfather of Loren, Joslyn, Rebecca, David, Sarah and Emily, father-in-law of Gillian. Former teacher and principal in the New York City Public Schools, which he loved. A straight and simple man of uncommon decency, kindness and sweetness, he made life better for his family, friends, students and colleagues. He is missed. Memorial service is private.
