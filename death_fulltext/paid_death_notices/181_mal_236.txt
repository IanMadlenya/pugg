
        SMITH-Howard K. TV news commentator. On Friday, February 15, 2002, at his home in Bethesda, MD. Survived by wife Bennie, former broadcast journalist son Jack, daughter Catherine, and three grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Committee for the Protection of Journalists, 330 Seventh Ave., NY, NY 10001. Memorial service Tuesday, February 26th, 1:30PM at the Newseum, 1101 Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA. For information call Devol Funeral Home, Washington, D.C.
        SMITH-Howard K. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Howard K. Smith on February 15. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
