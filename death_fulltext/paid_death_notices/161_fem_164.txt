
        PETTI-Ruth. The Petti family grieves the passing of Ruth Petti, age 72, after a long 23 year, mostly successful, battle with breast cancer. Ruth leaves her husband of 50 years, Mario, her son Eugene and her daughter, Marla Levey and 3 grandchildren Evan, Alexander and Gabrielle. There will be a graveside ceremony Wednesday, 11AM at Wellwood Cemetery, L.I.
