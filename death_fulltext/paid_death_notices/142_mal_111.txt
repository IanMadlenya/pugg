
        SHAPIRO-Ivan. Urban Pathways notes with sorrow the death of Ivan Shapiro, its esteemed past President. Through his active leadership and enthusiasm, Ivan Shapiro played an important role in the growth of Urban Pathways. We will continue to strive to meet the high standards he set for honesty, ethics and quality of work. We feel the loss of our warm friend and advocate for the homeless and extend our deep sympathy to his wife, Maria, and his daughters, Lisa J. Kubiske and Alexandra Shapiro. Gerald W. Blume, President Joan E. Ohlson, Exec Director
