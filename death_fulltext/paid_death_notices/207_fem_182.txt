
        FISHMAN -- Miriam Kessin. Born on March 24, 1914. Died Wednesday, April 21, 2004 at her residence in NYC. Devoted wife of the late Irving. Beloved mother of Charlotte (Alan Sparer) and Freda. Loving grandmother of Anna and Julia Sparer. Also survived by many much-loved nieces and cousins. A true New Yorker, a loyal friend, a passionate advocate for peace and justice. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Doctors Without Borders. Services ''BoulevardRiverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I. 10AM Sunday.
