
        de ROCHEFORT-Jean. The Count de Rochefort, on May 18th, New York, after a brief illness. Senior advisor to the Commission of the European Communities Banking Sector Reform Program. Survived by his wife Sabina, former wife Danna, their children Marc, Ghislaine, Pierre, Laurent, Alexandra, grandchildren Simone, Tristan, Maximilian, Sebastian, Leila, Nicolas, Isabella, Carolina, step-son Stefan.
