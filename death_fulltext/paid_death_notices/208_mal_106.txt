
        BERNABO -- Raymond A. Born January 1, 1930 in New York, died May 6th. A graduate of Harvard College, a Fulbright scholar and Columbia Law School graduate. Mr. Bernabo retired from the former Primerica Corporation as Vice President and Deputy General Counsel. Mr. Bernabo, the son of the late Ernest and Ethel Bernabo, is survived by his sister Marianne B. Fisher, brother-in-law John M. Fisher, Jr., his nephews John M. Fisher III and Scott Volpi Fisher, and his companion of 35 years William C. Tamulinas. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue on Monday, May 10th from 2-5 and 7-9. A Mass of the Resurrection will be held at the Church of the Holy Family, 315 E. 47th Street on May 11th at 10 AM. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, gifts in his memory may be made to the Leukemia Research Fund, Division of Hematology, Weill Medical College, New YorkPresbyterian Hospital, 525 E. 68th Street, C606, New York, NY 10021.
