
        CONNOR-Hugh F. Of New City, N.Y. On Jan. 24, 1999 in Tijuana, Mexico. He was Public Relations Executive & Senior Consultant at the U.N., Bureau Chief with United Press International in Newark, N.J., and National Press Editor for UPI, Editor and Reporter for the Gannett Newspapers. Survived by his wife Liliana of New City. Father of Martine of Rye, N.Y. and Stephanie of Sarasota, FL. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, 11AM, Saint Peter's Church, Haverstraw, New York. Burial Mount Repose Cemetery, Haverstraw.
