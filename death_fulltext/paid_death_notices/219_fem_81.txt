
        PAINTER--Mary, for over twenty years, assistant to William Shawn, the second editor-in-chief of the New Yorker magazine, died on April 7, 2005 in New York City. Born in Columbus, MS on May 29, 1917, Miss Painter moved to NY after WWII. She joined the staff of the New Yorker in the early 1950's and later became assistant to Mr. Shawn, a position she held until their mutual retirement in 1987. She will be deeply missed by her family and many devoted friends. Gifts in her memory may be made to WNET 13.
