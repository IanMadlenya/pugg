
        SACHAR-Thelma H. (Horwitz) of Chestnut Hill, MA, on Tuesday, February 4. Beloved and devoted wife of the late Dr. Abram Leon Sachar. Cherished mother of Dr. David B. Sachar, Dr. Howard M. Sachar and the late Dr. Edward J. Sachar. Loving grandmother of Mark, Sharon, Michele and Daniel and the late Kenneth. Great grandmother of Raphael Porag. The family will be at her late residence, 250 Hammond Pond Parkway, Chestnut Hill, MA. 02167 through Sunday noon. Remembrances may be made to Brandeis University National Women's Committee, 415 South Street, Waltham, MA 02254-9110. For information contact The Levine Chapel, Brookline, MA.
