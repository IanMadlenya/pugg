
        BURRILL--Helena VanCortlandt, suddenly April 10. Born April 28, 1924, she was predeceased by her beloved parents, Elizabeth Morris Mordaunt Burrill and Drayton Burrill, and her brother, Gerry. A memorial service will be held on April 24, at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, Fifth Avenue at 2:30 pm. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the New York Philharmonic. Condolences may be expressed at www.rowelanterman.com
