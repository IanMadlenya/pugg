
        BERSCH--Richard L., of Sarasota, FL, formerly of Livingston, NJ, died on March 2, 2007 at the age of 72. Predeceased by his wife Jeanne Harris, he was the beloved father of Donna, Suzie, and Ronnie and the adored grandfather of seven grandchildren. He is also survived by his brother Neil and his sister Nona London, his motherin-law, Lauretta Garabrant. Richard was a partner and Sr. Vice President of Sales for Lasky Company in Millburn, NJ. During his tenure he was instrumental in growing the company into one of the foremost printers in America. The family requests that contributions be made to Tidewell Hospice of Sarasota, FL. He will be deeply missed and lovingly remembered.
