
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Board of Directors and Staff of the 92nd Street Y mourn the passing of Arnold N. Bergson, a dedicated member of our Board and our dear friend. His years of commitment to the 92nd Street Y are deeply appreciated. Our hearts and prayers go out to Arnold's wife Jeanne, his daughters Jane and Barbara and their families. He will be missed by all who knew him. Philip Milstein, President Joseph Leff, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
        BERGSON-Arnold. The Officers and employees of Acordia Northeast deeply mourn the passing of Arnold Bergson, Chairman Emeritus. Arnold was the founding father of the predecessor companies in N.Y. and a great leader. He was a man amongst men and will be truly missed. Our sympathies are extended to his lovely wife Jeanne, his daughters, Jane and Barbara and their families.
        BERGSON-Arnold M. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed honorary member, Arnold M. Bergson, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Roy R. Raizen, President
