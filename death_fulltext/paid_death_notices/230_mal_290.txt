
        GOTTLIEB - Jerome. The President and the Board of Trustees of Montefiore Medical Center are deeply saddened by the death of Jerome Gottlieb, father of our esteemed Trustee, David Gottlieb. Jerome Gottlieb was a loving father and devoted grandfather. Our deepest and heartfelt condolences go out to his son David, daughter Jill, his four grandchildren and his entire family in this time of their great loss. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society or Montefiore Medical Center. Spencer Foreman, M.D., Pres Jay B. Langner, Chairman Board of Trustees Montefiore Medical Center
