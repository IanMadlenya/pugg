
        FEDERMAN-Herman L. (Hy). Beloved father, grandfather, brother, uncle and friend, former Deputy Administrative Director of the Workers Compensation Board, died on Friday, October 8, after a long illness. He is survived by his children, Paul, Barbara, Jane and Eric, and his grandchildren, Flo, Joe, Amy and Jonathan, and his brothers and sisters, Matty, Estelle, Dorothy, Murray, Ruth, Belle and Ben.
