
        MARTIN--James Alfred, Jr., Ph.D. of Decatur, GA, died Wednesday, January 24, 2007, of heart failure. Dr. Martin was a Danforth Professor of Religion in Higher Education at Union Theological Seminary from 1960-1982 and Professor of Religion at Columbia University from 1967-1982. In 1985, he was appointed University Professor at Wake Forest University, were he served until 2005. Dr. Martin wrote numerous books on the philosophy of religion, including ''The Beauty of Holiness''. He is survived by his brother, Dr. Raymond J. Martin of Decatur, GA. A memorial service will be held at 4 pm Sunday, February 4, at Holy Trinity Episcopal Church in Decatur, GA.
