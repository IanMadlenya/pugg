
        JACOBSEN-Vera Rupf, 81. Died in her sleep on September 2, 1999. Born August 16, 1918, in Brooklyn, New York. She had been an active volunteer at the Luthern Medical Center, and The Church of the Good Shepherd. Devoted mother of Gloria Lang, John, Alfred, and Carol Jacobsen. Loving grandmother of Caroline, John Jr., Alfred Ole, Thomas, Andrew, Alexia, and Joseph. Beloved aunt and friend to many. She joins her late husband, Alfred Jacobsen, Jr. and sister Edna Faulstich, at their final resting place in the Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn, New York. Visiting Friday, 7-9PM. Edw. C. Halvorsen, Inc., 5310 Eighth Avenue, Brooklyn, NY. Funeral service Saturday, 9:15AM at The Lutheran Church of the Good Shepherd, 7420 4th, Brooklyn, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be sent to: The Church of the Good Shepherd, 7420 4th Avenue, Brooklyn, NY or the Norwegian Christian Home and Health Center, 1270 67th Street, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11219.
