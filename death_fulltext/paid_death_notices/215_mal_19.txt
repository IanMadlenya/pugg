
        VAMOS--Steven E., 84, of Manhattan, East Meredith, NY and Itaipava, Brazil, died November 29, 2004. Devoted husband of Aida Spielmann, beloved father of Mark N. Vamos, and doting grandfather of Elizabeth Eaton Vamos. Born in Budapest, he served in British Army Intelligence in North Africa, Yugoslavia and Italy during WW II, and then fought for Israel in its War of Independence. After moving to the U.S. in 1953, he worked as a polymer chemist and became a partner in Berco Industries. For many years he served as president of the cooperative building at 222 E. 80 St. We should all live as bravely, and as well. Memorial services private. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the Humane Society of Central Delaware Co., PO Box 88, Delhi, NY 13753.
