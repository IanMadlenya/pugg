
        FLYNN-Thomas D. Died on April 12, 2003 at age 90 while on vacation in Puerto Rico. Beloved husband of Harriet Howland for 62 years; father and father-in-law to Susan and Stephen Gordon, Christine Flynn and Paul Segal, and John and Leslie Flynn; grandfather to Alexandra and John Gordon, Hannah, Sayre and Sarah Flynn and Emma Segal. A private interment will be held in Port Washington, New York, on Saturday, April 19th. A Memorial service will be held in Princeton, N.J. at The Princeton University Chapel on May 3rd at 11am.
        FLYNN-Thomas D. The President and Trustees of Columbia University express deep sorrow at the passing of our Trustee Emeritus, Thomas D. Flynn, a graduate of the Columbia Business School, Class of 1939. He was a respected colleague whose leadership as a University Trustee was marked by enthusiasm and distinction. Columbia University extends its profound sympathy to his wife Mrs. Harriet Flynn and their family. Lee C. Bollinger, President of the University David J. Stern, Chair of the Trustees
