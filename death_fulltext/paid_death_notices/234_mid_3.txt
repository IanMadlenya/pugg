
        AZZOLLINI--Rosa of Molfetta, Italy and New York, died suddenly on June 30, 2006 in London. Her elegance, smile, and carefree joie de vivre will be lovingly remembered. We will deeply mourn the loss of a gregarious and spirited mother, sister, aunt, and friend. She is survived by daughter Jessica Pleasants, mother Carmela, sisters Nella and Elisabetta and their husbands George and Eduardo, brother Saverio and his wife Maria, Jessica's fathers Rodney and Steve, nieces, nephews and many friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell's Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Friday July 7, 2-5 and 7-9pm. Mass of Christian burial will be held at 12:30pm Saturday July 8 at St. Ignatius Loyola Church on Park Ave at 84th St. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to the Rosa Azzollini Scholarship for Journalism. Details to follow.
