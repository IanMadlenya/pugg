
        WITKIN-Shirley. The Board of Directors and staff of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center are deeply saddened by the passing of Shirley Witkin, a major supporter of our mission and member of our Society of Sponsors. Mrs. Witkin left a legacy of caring for all those touched by her extraordinary generosity. Our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Joseph Gurwin, President Alfred W. Levy, Chairman Herbert H. Friedman, Exec VP Stephen M. Levy, Director of External Affair
