
        PATTERSON-Archibald A. On October 6th, 1997, at the age of 81. A graduate of Hobart College and Harvard Law School, he practiced law in NYC and was a fifty year member of the American Bar Association. He was an accomplished bridge player and member of the University Club. He is survived by his wife, Esther; his sister Janet Feeley; a daughter Marcia; four sons, Bruce, James, Roy and Robert; a stepdaughter Noma; and eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held from 6-8 PM, Thursday, October 9th, at Krtil Funeral Home at 70th and 1st.
