
        BACHMANN--Lawrence P., formerly of Oxfordshire, England, died September 7, 2004, age 92, in Los Angeles. Studio executive, author, Lt. Col. USAAF WW II, founder Beverly Hills Tennis Club, member Garrick Club and All England Lawn Tennis Club, Distinguished Friend of Oxford. Wife Betty Hart Bachmann died 2003. Survived by sister, Lucy Cormack of San Francisco, stepdaughter, Linda Gordon, and her husband, David of Newport, RI.
