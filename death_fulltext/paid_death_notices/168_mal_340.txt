
        PARKOLA-Sam. Of West Palm Beach Florida passed away on January 20, 2001. He was born in and spent most his life in the NYC area. He was well-known as an exceptional craftsman. He was predeceased by his loving wife Rose in 1981. Survivors include daughter Deena Birnbaum (David) of San Diego; son Steven Parkola (Carol) of Palm Beach Gardens; 4 grandchildren, and 4 great grandchildren. Graveside funeral service will be held at New Montefiore Cemetery in Farmingdale LI at 1:30 p.m. Wednesday, January 24.
