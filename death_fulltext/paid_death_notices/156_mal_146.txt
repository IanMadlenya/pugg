
        KRASNE-Charles J. On January 6th after a long illness and a brave fight. Loving husband of Mickey (Marie-Rose) nee Kramer for 49 years. Devoted father of Robert A. and Andrew S. Krasne. Dear brother of Margo T. Krasne. Chuck Krasne was the youngest Naval Officer to serve in the Pacific in World War II. A graduate of the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. For more than 20 years Mr. Krasne headed the largest agency for Connecticut Mutual Life Insurance Company. During his leadership the Krasne Agency was the first to produce $100 million of insurance in one year. Mr. Krasne and his associates were repeatedly recognized with top awards for their outstanding performance. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Sunday at 3:30 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to MDS, P.O. Box 477, 464 Main St, Crosswicks, NJ 08515
        KRASNE-Charles J. The Board of Directors and members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member and friend, Charles J. Krasne, and wish to extend our sincere and heartfelt sympathy to the family.
