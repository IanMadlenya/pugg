
        FRANKLIN--Robert B., 90, of NYC and So. Egremont, MA, died Saturday, March 13, 2004. Survived by wife Jane, children R. Bruce, Jr., Betsy Ignacio, Jody Burrows and Peter W. A graduate of Williams College, he was a former Vice President of Sylvania Electric and Business Manager of Brooklyn Poly Prep. A Memorial Service will be held in So. Egremont in May.
