
        MUFSON-Robert. Age fifty-six passed away on April 30 in Boca Raton, FL. after a courageous and extraordinary battle with cancer. A man of boundless energy and creativity, Robert wrote poetry in celebration of life as his illness ravaged his body. A successful custom home builder, he played a major role in the development of a number of communities in Boca Raton. He was the chief benefactor of ''Boca Hoops'' creating an annual sportsmanship award. Survived by his children Betsi, Richard, Harris, Brett, Whitney, granddaughter Clara and devoted fiancee Marjory Dobbin and her children Rachel & Michael Dobbin. Donations in memory of Robert may be made to the charity of your choice, or the Children's Bereavement Center, 1501 Venera Ave, Suite 225, Coral Gables, FL. 33146. Services May 2, 2002, B'nai Torah Congregation, 6262 SW 18th Street, Boca Raton, FL. Interment Mt. Nebo Cemetery, Miami, FL.
