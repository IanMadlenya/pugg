
        TANNENBAUM-Albert H. Of NYC and Orient, NY, on October 2, 2001, at home in NYC. Over 80 years, he gave love, knowledge, and humor to family and friends. Shared 47 years with the late Lona. Loving father of Loren Hilf and Abby Tannenbaum; five grandchildren; one great-grandson. Al was an exuberant friend and colleague, an insatiable reader, thinker and talker, and an enthusiastic jazz, baseball and tennis fan. A lifelong New Yorker, he spent five decades covering NYC and the world at large as a newspaperman with the NY Herald Tribune, Daily News, etc. Memorial date to be announced. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him.
