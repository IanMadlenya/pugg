
        PROTTER-Sylvia. Mrs. Sylvia Alter Protter, died October 23, 2000 after a short illness. Survived by her children Iska and Bernard Alter, and her grandsons Ethan and Jesse. Funeral October 27, Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Ave. at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers, a contribution in her name to one's favorite charity. She will be missed by all who knew her.
