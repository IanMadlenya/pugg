
        ROSENFELD--Adolph (Al) of NYC. Beloved husband of 62 years of Elizabeth (Marlene), passed away in his 90th year. Served four years in the Army-Air Corps in China, Burma and India during WWII. Awarded the Bronze Star, retired as a Major. Founder and former publisher Frozen Food Age, a trade publication established in 1952 when the frozen food business was in its infancy, widely respected for in-depth, honest coverage providing news, trends, detailed Master Merchandiser of the Year case studies as frozen food sales grew over the decades following the war. Spent his retirement fund-raising for the Frozen Food Industry Memorial Scholarship Foundation. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Scholarship Fund. Send to: The Distinguished Order of Zerocrats, P.O. Box 6069, Harrisburg, PA. 17112. Services Thursday, 3:00 P.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
