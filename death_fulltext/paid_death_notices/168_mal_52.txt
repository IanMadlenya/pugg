
        WOTMAN-Paul Freud. Born July 14, 1951, Bastille Day, in New York. Died Dec. 25th, 2000 in San Francisco. He faced a year-long battle against Lymphoma with much courage & dignity. Paul graduated from Columbia University and Boalt Hall Law School in Berkeley, CA. He broke ground in anti-discrimination law and won the largest award ever handed down by a U.S. court. He had the courage to go where others feared to tread. Paul leaves behind a family forever inspired by his show of strength, dignity and unending devotion: parents Anita Freud Wotman and Dr. Robert Wotman, brothers David (Laurie), and Daniel, nieces and nephews Jason, Andrew, Carly and Michael.
