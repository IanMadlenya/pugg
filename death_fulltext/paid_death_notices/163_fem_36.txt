
        SMITH-Margaret V. On July 30, 2000, aged 87, in Hoffman Estates, IL. Lived in New York about 1938-1970 (principally Sea Cliff); then in Arlington, Texas. An accomplished artist. Survived by her daughter Margo L. Smith, son-inlaw Terrence B. Kersjes, and others. Interment: Mt. Emblem Cemetery, Elmhurst, IL.
