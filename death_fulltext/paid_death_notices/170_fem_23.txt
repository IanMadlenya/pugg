
        DRILLING-Dena (Daisy Rubin) of Jamaica Estates and Hollywood, FL died March 1 at 88. Beloved mother of Ronald, Kenneth and Steven. Loving grandmother to Deborah, Cynthia, Tiffany, Bruce, Michelle, Willow and Michael. Great grandmother of four. She will be missed by all those privileged to have known her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, on Saturday, March 3rd, 7-9PM with services to be held on Sunday, March 4 at 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81St. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Jewish Federation of Broward County, 8358 West Oakland Park Blvd., Fort Lauderdale, Florida 33511.
