
        FONTRIER-Gabriel. Beloved husband of Mary, devoted father of Bunny, Toinette, Suzanne, and Michele passed away on August 5 after a long illness. He also leaves his sister, Celeste, and his nephew, David. He was Professor Emeritus at Queens College, editor/music critic for the L.I. Press and a distinguished composer. Interment is private. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Aaron Copland School of Music, Queens College, Flushing, New York.
