
        STEVENS-Leonard D. Of NYC. On October 26, 2001 at 84. Beloved husband of Evelyn, devoted father of Joanne, dear brother of Evelyn. He was a native of Harlem and graduated from City College Class of 1942, served in the Army during WW II, took over as co-owner of the Hunt Printing Company, now one of the oldest business establishments in Harlem. Viewing Thursday, November 1, 4-7PM at Mt. Morris Ascension Presbyterian Church, Mt. Morris Park West and 122 St. Services to follow.
