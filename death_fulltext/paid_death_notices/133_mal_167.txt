
        BROWN-Milton W. The faculty, staff and students of The Graduate School and University Center, CUNY, are deeply saddened by the passing of Professor Emeritus Milton W. Brown, co-founder of our Doctoral Program in Art History and its first executive officer. Dr. Brown was a devoted teacher to a generation of our students and a ground-breaking scholar in the field of 20th Century American art history. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Blanche. Frances Degen Horowitz, President
