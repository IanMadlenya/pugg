
        EPSTEIN-Jack. An extraordinary person. For 93 years he brought a sense of joy and boundless energy to all aspects of his life and amazement to all who met him. He was a tireless worker for Union Temple of Brooklyn, Deborah Hospital, Child Care and other charitable organizations. His love and devotion for his late wife, Irene, was legendary. His entire family, children, grandchildren, great grandchildren, brothers, sister, nieces and nephews loved him enormously and feel fortunate to have had Jack in their lives for all these action-packed years. Funeral service will be held at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, NY, on Sunday, October 24, 1999 at 11:30 AM.
