
        BENSON-Phyllis. 61, of Atlanta, formerly of Mount Vernon and New Rochelle, NY, passed away on January 19, after a lengthy illness. She was the devoted wife of Ed for 40 years. Loving mother of Wendy Goldman and Richard Benson and loving mother-in-law to Brent Goldman and Melinda Benson. One of her greatest joys in life was being a grandma to Brandon and Alexander Benson and Jacob Goldman. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Guiding Eyes for the Blind, 611 Granite Springs Rd., Yorktown Heights, NY 10598 (800-942-0149).
