
        FRIED--Thelma. The Department of Dermatology at SUNY Downstate Medical Center is deeply saddened by the loss of our beloved and esteemed Departmental Administrator, Thelma Fried. Thelma served our department and the Downstate community for almost 28 years with dedication, kindness and generosity. She was a surrogate mother, confessor and friend to countless students, residents and staff and will be sorely missed by all of us. Our most hearfelt sympathy to her family whose loss we share. Alan R. Shalita, M.D., Professor and Chairman >>AD#
