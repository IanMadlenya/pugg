
        GIOSCIA-Nicolai, M.D. On May 25 in Ft. Lauderdale, FL, at age 96. Medical degree Middlesex College of Brandeis University. Assistant Director of Creedmore State Hospital, Director of Nassau County Community Mental Health, founder of mental health clinic U.S. Virgin Islands, visiting consultant of psychiatry Roosevelt Hospital, NY, and U.S. Army Major and veteran of World War II. Life partner to Tony LaRocco. Also survived by four nieces, many great and great-great-nephews, nieces and cousins. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Paul the Apostle Church, 60th St. and 9th Ave., Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to SAGE, 305 7th Ave., NY, NY 10001 or Pines Care Center, Fire Island Pines, NY.
