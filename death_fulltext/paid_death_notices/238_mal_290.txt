
        YARDLEY--John H., 61 native of England but product of New York, passed from illness on November 22, 2006. A furniture maker by occupation but a collector by passion. He will be deeply missed by his loving friends and family, but most by Bernice ''the wife,'' the heart of his life's collection. Memorial services private, please send donation to the Valley Hospital Foundation, 223 North Van Dien Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ 07450.
