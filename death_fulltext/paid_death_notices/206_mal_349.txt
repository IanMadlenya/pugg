
        ELAHI--Hajj-Amin. It is with the deepest sorrow that our beloved Hajj-Amin Elahi passed away March 23, 2004, 11 AM, local time in Lyon, France, at the age of 85. His legacy was one of kindness and love, and always sought to remind us of God's greatness. Thousands of people mourn his death around the world and are united in prayer. The world has lost one of the greatest beings it has seen thus far. Words cannot express how greatly he will be missed!
