
        THOMAS-Mary Ellen. Died on June 29 in NY. She was born in Ferndale, Michigan in 1925. Graduate of St. James High School. Served in the Navy in WW II. Graduate of University of Detroit with a degree in Accounting. Retired from the law firm of Davis Polk & Wardell. Predeceased by beloved husband Philip. Survived by sisterin-law Julia Goodman and five nieces & nephews. Memorial service Wednesday 10 AM, Church of the Epiphany, 22 St. & Second Ave.
