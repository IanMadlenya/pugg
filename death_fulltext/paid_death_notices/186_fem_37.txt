
        GORETSKY-Minnie (Fleischman). July 6, of Swampscott, MA, formerly of Newark and Staten Island. Beloved wife of the late Harry Goretsky. Loving sister of Abraham and Hazel Fleischman of NYC, and the late Adeline and Harry Richer. Cherished aunt of Rosalinda and Marvin Jacobson of Peabody, MA, Harriet Goldstein of Albany, Cathy Goretsky of Titusville, FL, and dear greataunt of Etana, Neil, and Lisa Jacobson, and many other great-nieces and nephews. Graveside services at the Baron Hirsch Cemetery, 1126 Richmond Ave., Staten Island, on Monday, July 8, at 12 noon. Shiva Tuesday only at the home of Mr. and Mrs. Marvin Jacobson. Expressions of sympathy in her memory may be donated to the Jewish Rehabilitation Center for Aged, 330 Paradise Rd., Swampscott, MA 01907.
