
        FIERMAN - ROSS - Hortense. Passed away at home February 24 at the young age of 94. Loving wife of late Sidney Fierman and Daniel Ross. She is survived by son Merwyn, daughter Marilyn, grandchildren Scott, Sabrina, Daniel, Randy, Sean and Shane. A unique, intelligent woman, full of spirit, humor and stubborn to the end, but loving forever. Services will be private.
