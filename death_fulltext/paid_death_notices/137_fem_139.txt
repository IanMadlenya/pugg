
        KRAFT-Helen Margaret, nee Gwydir, at home after a long illness. She was a Wellesley graduate, Class of 1948, and a long time resident of Cold Spring Harbor, Long Island. She is survived by her husband, Warren G., daughters Helen K. Buckwalter, Anne K. Thompson and sons Warren G. and John S. and nine grandchildren. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorials to the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, P.O. Box 100 (Development Office), Cold Spring Harbor, N.Y. 11724, would be appreciated.
