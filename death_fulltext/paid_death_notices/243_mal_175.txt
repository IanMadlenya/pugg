
        FELDER--Lawrence, on April 12, 2007. Beloved husband of Ruth (Kitain), devoted father of Brenda (Elkind) and Maxwell, father-in-law of Steven Elkind and Kate Ryan, adoring and adored ''Granpa'' of Erin, Sophie, Marissa and Lucie, brother of Louisa Solomon and the late Karl Felder and Rhoda Asher. First, foremost and forever Master Plumber and Proprietor of Mac Felder, Inc. on Manhattan's Upper West Side; Captain of his industry and active leader for The Association of Contracting Plumbers of the City of NY; former Temple President of Whitestone Hebrew Center, former Chairman of the Village of North Hills Planning Commission and former President of The Greens Condominium Association. A lion among men, he was generous and compassionate; and lived for his family, and his family loved him for his life. Services Tuesday, April 17, 2007, Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY.
