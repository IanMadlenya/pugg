
        BODDIE-Gertrude L. Died June 12 in Las Vegas, Nevada where she resided since 1985. She had an impressive career as Manager with The New York City Housing Authority. She was the first African-American manager of Co-op City, Bronx, New York. ''Those who loved me know my appreciation.'' She requested that no funeral or memorial service be held. Please feel free to contact her daughter, Maxine Bernie at 8751 South Marshfield Avenue, Chicago, Illinois 60620.
