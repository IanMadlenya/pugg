
        BRITTON-A. Dudley. Jr., 92. On April 1, 1997 of complications from a tennis court broken hip. Devoted husband of the late Carroll Scarborough Britton and the late Helen Ingram Britton, he is survived by his sister, Edna, of New York and son, Peter of Pleasantville, New York. He was a former New York Times reporter and General Motors speech writer. A memorial open house will be held at his home in Pleasantville, on April 12 from 2-5 P.M. In lieu of flowers, donations to the National Osteoporsis Foundation, Washington, D.C. 20036 are appreciated.
