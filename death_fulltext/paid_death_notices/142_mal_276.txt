
        ROSEN - Joel. Internationally known concert pianist and teacher, died on November 17, 1998. He leaves behind a loving family; his wife Joy, children Jeffrey and Linda, their spouses Daphne Hsu and Rob Macomber, and three grandsons Bobby, Michael and Jamie. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Performers of Westchester or the Mid-Westchester YM-YWHA Ceramics Art Department in Scarsdale.
