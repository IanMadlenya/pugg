
        LILLY-Gloria (nee Tuttle). December 31, 1999. Widow of Scott B. Lilly. Her children, Ann, Joan and Thomas Scott and stepdaughter, Leslie, announce with sadness the death of a woman of wit, music and a generous heart. She also leaves behind five grandchildren, one great-grandchild and a sisterin-law, Mary Alice Lilly. Memorial gathering Monday, January 3, 7 PM, at house. Donations in lieu of flowers to the Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Avenue, Montgomery, AL 36104.
