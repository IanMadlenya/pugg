
        WHITMARSH-Theodore Francis. Age 78. Died on June 6, 1997 after a long illness. He lived in New York City and Upper Brookville NY. He was a lawyer and former Vice President of Francis H. Leggett and Company. He was President of the Huguenot Society of America. Mr Whitmarsh is survived by his wife Mary Louise Ward Whitmarsh, their daughters Linda Whitmarsh, Carol Bennett, Dorothy Sprague, five grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. There will be a memorial service on Monday June 9, at 11 a.m. at St. James Episcopal Church, 865 Madison Ave, NYC. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Huguenot Society of America, 122 E. 58 St, NYC 10022 would be appreciated.
