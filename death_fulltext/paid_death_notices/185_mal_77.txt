
        SCHUMAN - Charles. The Bard College community mourns the passing of Charles Schuman, who for many years served with dedication and distinction as Bard's public relations director. His warmth, wit, and skill served the college well, and he was regarded throughout the Bard community as a respected colleague and a loyal friend. We send our deepest condolences to his family. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman
