
        AUBURN--Norman P. President Emeritus of The University of Akron, died July 21, 2003 in Akron after a short illness. He was 98. In New York, Dr. Auburn served as Acting President for the Council for Financial Aid to Education, Acting President of Brooklyn (now New York) Polytechnic Institute, Acting President of Union College (Schenectady), and President of the Herman Muehlstein Foundation, 1965 to its dissolution in 2002. Among his local survivors are a grandson, David, a stepgranddaughter, Illana Hyman Levin, and a great-granddaughter, Rebecca, all of Manhattan. >>AD#
