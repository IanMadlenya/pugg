
        KUREEN-Ezra. Of Great Neck, NY, on December 23, 2001. Born in Baghdad, Iraq on April 12, 1914. A former resident of China, the Phillipines, and Australia. He was fluent in Arabic, Chinese and English. He formed the partnership of Kureen & Cooper Inc. and had a long and exceptional career as a stockbroker on Wall Street until ill health and age forced his retirement. Predeceased by son, Charles; he is survived by his wife Jane; their two daughters, Susan and Jennifer; grandson David and two great-grandchildren, Matthew and Nathan. Ezra will be remembered fondly for his gambling spirit & with admiration for his perseverance against all odds and resilience in adversity. A memorial service will be planned for a future date.
