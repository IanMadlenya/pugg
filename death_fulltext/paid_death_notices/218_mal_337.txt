
        HYMAN--George, M.D. The faculty and staff of Columbia University Medical Center extend their condolences to the family of George Hyman, M.D., who died recently at his home at the age of 82. Dr. Hyman graduated from Columbia College in 1942 and from the Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons in 1945. Dr. Hyman had a long career as a clinician, educator and clinical researcher in the Department of Medicine as an associate clinical professor with specialty focus in hematology and oncology. The family has requested that donations be made in lieu of flowers to the College of Physicians & Surgeons. David A. Brenner, M.D., Chair Department of Medicine Joseph Tenenbaum, M.D. Senior Associate Dean for Clinical Affairs Columbia University, College of Physicians & Surgeons
