
        MINICH-Arthur. Age 91, of Palm Beach, Florida and formerly of Westfield, NJ, died on Sunday, January 11, 1998 at his residence. He had retired in 1969 from Tenneco Chemicals, Inc. as Senior VicePresident after 37 years of service in the chemical industry. Prior to joining Tenneco, Mr. Minich served successively as vice-president, executive vice-president and president of Nuodex Products Company, Inc., of Elizabeth, NJ. Author of many articles and holder of a number of patents in the chemical field, Mr. Minich was a member of numerous chemical, business and management organizations. He received his M.B.S. degree from the Vienna Institute of Technology. He received a Certificate of Merit from the War Production Board and War and Navy Departments for his outstanding contribution to the work of the Office of Scientific Research and Development during World War II. Mr. Minich remained active in the chemical field through consulting activities for many years. He was a member of Bethesda-by-the-Sea Episcopal Church and the Beach Club in Palm Beach as well as numerous civic organizations. Survivors include his wife, Olga Capp Minich of Palm Beach, FL; two daughters, Karen Minich of Houston, TX and Gail Minich Wragg of Mendham, NJ; sister, Helen Zechmeister of Los Altos Hills, CA. A Memorial Service will be 11:00 A.M., Friday, January 16, 1998 at Bethesda - by - the - Sea Episcopal Church, Palm Beach, FL. In lieu of flowers, friends may contribute to a charity of their choice. For more information, contact Quattlebaum - Holleman - Burse Funeral Home, West Palm Beach, FL.
