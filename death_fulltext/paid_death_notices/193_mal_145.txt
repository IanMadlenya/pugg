
        COLMAN-Neville, MD, PhD. The Department of Pathology of Columbia University sadly notes the passing of Dr. Colman who served with distinction as Professor of Pathology and Director/Chairman, Dept. of Pathology & Lab. Medicine for St. Luke'sRoosevelt Hospital. His scientific and personal contributions to the Department and the College of Physicians & Surgeons are fondly remembered by all of us. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Michael Shelanski, MD Chairman Department of Pathology Gerald Fischbach, MD Dean, Faculty of Medicine
        COLMAN - Neville, M.D., Ph.D. The Department of Pathology of Mount Sinai School of Medicine mourns the death of Neville Colman, M.D., Ph.D. Neville was a faculty member here before becoming the Chairman of Pathology at Beth Israel, Roosevelt-Saint Luke's Hospitals. He was a devoted colleague, friend and teacher. His brilliant mind, scientific insight and keen wit will be truly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his beloved family. Department of Pathology Mount Sinai School of Medicine
        COLMAN-Neville. To Glenys, Stephen, and Jessica. Our deepest condolences on the sad and untimely loss of Neville. We will miss him very much. The Lubbock and Hammer-Gatti Families
