
        YORK-Gordon Bradbury, beloved husband of Harriet Glover York, father of Pamela Glover York, and grandfather of Sarah Glover York, died on January 16, 2001 at La Posada in Green Valley, Arizona. He lived in New York City for 37 years and worked for McCalls, Life and Family Weekly magazines. Mr. York summered in East Hampton and was a member of the Maidstone Club. He retired to East Hampton six years before moving to Green Valley, Arizona. Mr. York graduated from Dartmouth in 1935. There will be a memorial service in New York City in the spring.
