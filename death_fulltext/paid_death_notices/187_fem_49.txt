
        ROSEN-Thelma J. Passed away on Monday, August 5, 2002. She survived her husband of sixty-three years, Leonard, by eight months. Thelma is survived by her daughter, Gail, & a daughter in California, 5 grandchildren, nine great-grandchildren, and her sister, Ruth Beitman, of Connecticut. Thelma's memory will be cherished by her entire family and all of her friends. Services are at 12 P.M. Thursday, August 8, at Garlick's Memorial Chapel in New Rochelle, 914-235-5341. In lieu of flowers or baskets, contributions to the charity of your choice.
