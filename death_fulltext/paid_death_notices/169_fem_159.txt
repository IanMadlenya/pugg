
        CHEDDIE-Veda Ramona. Beloved wife of Jack Garrett. Daughter of George (deceased) and Kelly Cheddie. Funeral services will be held at Redden's Funeral Home, 325 W. 14th St, NYC, on Tuesday, February 20, at 10:30 AM.
