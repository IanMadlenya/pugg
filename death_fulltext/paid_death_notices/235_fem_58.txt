
        MUNCAN--Mihaela, MD. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center is extremely saddened by the sudden death of Dr. Mihaela Muncan. Dr. Muncan was associated with the Department of Psychiatry for over 15 years. She did her training at Maimonides Medical Center and then joined the Psychiatric Staff. She was the Director of the Inpatient Unit. She was an excellent teacher to the Psychiatric House Staff and was loved and respected by all. She was active on committees of the Medical Staff. She will be sorely missed by all who knew her. Sincere condolences are extended to her entire family. Ira Mayer, MD, President Warren Wexelman, MD President Elect Steven Farber, MD Secretary/Treasurer
