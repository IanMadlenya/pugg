
        MARGO--Murray. On December 2. Beloved husband of Elaine. Devoted father of Sari Kushner and Mitch Margo. Loving grandfather of Robert and Brian Kushner and Sam Margo. Murray was a Renaissance man before his time and a family man with social skills beyond compare. A collector of antiques and marvelous friends of all ages, he will be missed by all who knew him.
