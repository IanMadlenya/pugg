
        WERBIN--Milton N. Died on March 10, 2004 at the age of 89. He leaves behind his bereaved wife Ann (nee Lichten), daughter Karen Eckert, son-in-law David, and grandchildren Aaron and Sari. He was also the loving father of the late Jeremy. Before his retirement he was Chief Airport Meterologist at JFK and part time piano tuner. Services 10 AM Friday, March 12, at Riverside Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY.
