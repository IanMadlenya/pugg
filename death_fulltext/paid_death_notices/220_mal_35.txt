
        STEVENSON--William. Age 87. Died April 29 at home in Manhattan. Born and raised in Freeport, Long Island, N.Y., he was husband to Luzena Firth for 44 years. Residing in Massapequa, N.Y. for 50 years. Military service included being an Army Paratrooper in France during WWII and an Army Major during the Korean Conflict. He worked as a financial executive at Fortune Brands (et al) for 49 years. Survived by a brother, Edward and a son John. Visitations are at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., May 5, 2005 from 5PM-8PM and 11AM-1PM on May 6, 2005 followed by burial in Greenwood Cemetery, Bklyn, N.Y. Memorial contributions may be made to St. David's Lutheran Church, 20 Clark Blvd, Massapequa Park, N.Y. 11762.
