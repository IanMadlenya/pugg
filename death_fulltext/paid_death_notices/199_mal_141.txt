
        LEHRER--Roy, 87, died on August 13th, 2003. Beloved husband of Cecille Davis. Loving father of Noel and Esther, Peter and Harriet, and Sande and Marilyn and stepfather of Eric and Pamela Davis. Proud and adoring grandfather of Dana, Alison and Carly Lehrer and Jonathan Davis. Brother of Blossom Kramer and the late Mort and Jerry. Born 1915 in Brooklyn, he attended Erasmus Hall High School and Cornell. He was a consummate New Yorker and sports enthusiast. He pitched Sunday softball into his 70's in Woodstock, NY. Services Thursday 1 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
