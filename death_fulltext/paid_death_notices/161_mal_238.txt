
        LUBIN-Emanuel. June 19, 2000 at age 85 after a long and distinguished career in real estate law. Beloved husband of Evelyn, devoted father of Helen and her husband, Robert Stark. Cherished grandfather of Caroline and David. Loving brother of Florence Meyer. Service Wednesday, June 21, 9:45 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street.
        LUBIN-Emanuel. We note with great sorrow the passing of a former Board member who served 25 Sutton Place South for many years with care and distinction. Our deepest sympathy to his family. Marvin Leffler, President
        LUBIN-Emanuel. The partners and staff of Goldfarb & Fleece are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed and beloved partner Emanuel Lubin. He brought great joy and wisdom to all of us and we will miss him immensely. We all join in expressing our sincere and heart felt sympathy to his wife Evelyn, daughter and son-in-law Dr. and Mrs. Robert Stark and his grandchildren Caroline and David.
        LUBIN-Emanuel. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved member, Emanuel Lubin. On behalf of all our members we express our deepest condolences to his family. Bernard Steinweg, President David Hershberg, Secretary
