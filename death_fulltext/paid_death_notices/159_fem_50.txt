
        WEITZMAN-Yaffa nee Miller. On April 5, 2000. Beloved wife of Jerrold. Loving mother of C. Jeremy. Dear sister of Leo Miller. Her soft, intense glow warmed and uplifted everyone she touched. As wife, mother, friend, psychoanalyst, teacher, arts aficionado, Yaffa gave her all and savored life to the fullest. Service Friday 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        WEITZMAN-Yaffa. Dear Friend, a truly hamisch New Yorker who cared so much for her patients, many friends and family. Our deepest sympathy to her devoted husband of 35 years, Jerrold, and to her son, Christopher, of whom she was so proud. We will miss her greatly. Peter and Joe-Tom
