
        DONNELLY-Dorothy Elizabeth (nee Bridgham). 89. On October 2, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Edward; loving and beloved mother of Marjorie Clarke of Collinsville, CT, and Dorothy Meunier of Paradise Valley, AZ; and doting and cherished grandmother of Alison, Justin, Libby and Matthew. Born in Brooklyn on March 13, 1909, she was a public school teacher from 1945 to 1971, and left Brooklyn only in 1991 when she took her zest for life to Duncaster in Bloomfield, CT, where she enjoyed her last years. A memorial service will be held at Duncaster in December. Donations in her memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
