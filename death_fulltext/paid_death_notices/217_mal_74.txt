
        WEISS--Walter. Died Thursday, February 3, 2005. Survived by wife Stephanie, and children James and Vered Lewy, Amy and Dan Reisen, and Ellen and Scott Bull, and seven grandchildren. Graduated from Cornell Law School in 1949 after serving as a navigator in the Air Force during World War II. Walter was V.P. of Human Resources for Lightolier and volunteered as a human rights legal advocate upon retirement. He will be remembered for his humor and passion for fairness, justice, tennis, and, especially, for his love of Stephanie and family. We will miss him greatly. Donations to James Parkinson's Fund, UMDNJ Foundation, 120 Albany St, New Brunswick, NJ 08901.
