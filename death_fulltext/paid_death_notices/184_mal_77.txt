
        FEINBERG-Samuel. On May 5, 2002. Loving devoted husband, father and grandfather. Survived by wife Nettie. Daughter Alice Batson. Sons Lawrence and Richard. Grandsons Jacob, Teddy and Ian. Daughters-in-law Sandra and Nana and son-in-law Kevin. Worked for Fairchild Publicatoions from 1937 to 1993. Served as a reporter and editor and most notably as a columnist. His column ''From Where I Sit'' appeared in Women's Wear Daily for over 30 years. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Central Park Conservancy or to a favorite library.
