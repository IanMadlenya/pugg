
        ARTINIAN-Edward, 61. Of Chatham, NJ. Husband of Patricia. Died peacefully in his sleep early Sunday morning. He is survived by his loving wife Patricia Strzepek, his father Agisha, sisters Elizabeth Nahabedian and Julia Cox, nieces Paula Gordan, Lenora Cox, Johanna Cox and Elizabeth Bendschneider. Ed was a graduate of Brown University and Columbia Graduate School of Business. He was the founder and owner of Chatham House Publishers, Inc. He was a loving man who delighted in a good meal, savored the taste of fine wines, took pride in the books he produced and will be greatly missed by all who loved him. The funeral will be on Wednesday, 1 PM. For details, call Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, 201-635-2428.
