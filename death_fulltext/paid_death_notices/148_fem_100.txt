
        SARGENT-Claudia E. Of South Londonderry, Vermont, formerly of Cambridge, Massachusetts died on May 12, 1999 at home after a long illness. She is survived by her husband Paul Fienberg; her son Benjamin; her sisters Andrea Praeger, Alexandra Praeger, Manya Praeger and Julia Blaut; & her stepchildren Leah, Greta & Luke Feinberg. Memorial services at her home on Sunday, May 16, 1999, at 1PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center (memo: ''C. Sargent Breast Cancer Research'') C/O Dr. Jerome Groopman, Harvard Institute of Medicine, 4 Blackfan Circle, Room 351, Boston, Massachusetts 02115.
