
        BELL--Pearl (Levin), 96, died April 12, 2005, in Boulder, Colorado. Mother of Alan and Jackie, wife of the late Harry Bell (nee Belitsky), grandmother of Benny and Andy, ''mother-in-law'' to David Ziff, and mother-in-law to Phil Swan. An avid piano player, who continued to bang on the eighty-eights until the final days. We'll miss her humor, kindness, and inability to find anything bad in anyone she ever met. Services Riverside Memorial Chapel, Friday 11:30 A.M.
