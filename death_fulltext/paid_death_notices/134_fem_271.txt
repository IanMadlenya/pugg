
        PALEY-Joan Lawton. Of Lauderdale By The Sea passed away after a long illness on March 27, 1998. Joan was born in Pawtucket, Rhode Island and educated at Brown University and Albany Law School. She practiced law in Washington, DC and in New York City. She was a Supervisory Attorney for the National Labor Relations Board and later an associate in the law firm of Berman, Paley, Goldstein & Kannry, LLP in New York City. Before moving to Florida, Joan lived in Larchmont, NY and was a past president of the Scarsdale Woman's Club and a past president of the Scarsdale Congregational Church Woman's Guild. She was a member of Phi Beta Kappa and several other honorary societies. She is survived by her husband David, sister Maureen McGarry, her stepdaughter Staci and two grandchildren Christopher and Aletha Buchal. A memorial service will be held at the First Congregational Church, 2501 N.E. 30th Street, Fort Lauderdale, Florida 32757 on Palm Sunday, April 5, 1998, at 12:30 PM.
