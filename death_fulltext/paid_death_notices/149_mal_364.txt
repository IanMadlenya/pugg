
        FRANK-George Woodrow. 82 years of age, of Princeton, NJ. Beloved husband of Irma R. Devoted father of George, Jr., (Woody) and Susan F. Daum. Dear grandfather of George W. Frank, III (Tripp), Andrew Frank, Nicholas Daum, Matthew Daum and great-grandfather of George W. Frank, IV (Drew). He was a graduate of The Hun School and Princeton University '38. Retired Lieutenant Colonel from WW II, Vice President of E&J Gallo Winery, and founder and first president of Hospice Care Network. Memorial service will be held at a later date. Donations to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, NY 11590 would be appreciated.
