        KAUFMANN--Peter H. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center are saddened by the passing of Peter H. Kaufmann. We extend our sincere sympathy to his wife, Ruth, and to his family, friends, and colleagues. Peter W. May, Chairman Kenneth L. Davis, M.D. President and CEO
