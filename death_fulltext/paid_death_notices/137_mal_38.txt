
        HARRIS-Bernard. ''Bucky'' Harris, 79, a resident of Stratton Mountain, Vermont for the last eight years who wintered in Holmes Beach, FL. died on May 25. He was born in New York City. A former textile marketing executive, he served in the Army during World War II. Survived by his wife, Jean, six step-daughters, six stepsons and 23 grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to: Hospice of SW Florida, 5955 Rand Blvd, Sarasota, FL 34238
