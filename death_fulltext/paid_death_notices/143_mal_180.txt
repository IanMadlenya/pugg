
        SCHWARTZWALD - Morris. Of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Great Neck, NY, and South Egremont, MA, died December 12, 1998. Beloved husband of Rita. Devoted father of Mark, Robert (Terese) and Kenneth. Loving grandfather of Amy, Alan and Michael. Dear brother of Joseph (Frankie) and good friend to many. A remarkable man who faced his death as he lived his life, with courage and dignity. Private graveside service.
