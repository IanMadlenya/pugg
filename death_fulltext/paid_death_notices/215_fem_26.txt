
        McLOUGHLIN--Lucille Fusz (nee O'Malley). At home in Cohasset, MA on December 1st, age 96. Widow of Maurice E., partner in White & Case. Mother of Lucille Hornby of New Jersey, Elizabeth of San Francisco, Maurice E. Jr., of London, Paul of New York and the late Martha. Grosla will be missed by eight grandchildren, many nieces and nephews. A tree she planted still thrives on First Street in Brooklyn. Funeral Saturday, December 4, St. Anthony, Cohasset. Interment 12:30PM, December 6, Gate of Heaven, Hawthorne, NY. Donations to Retired Religious of the Sacred Heart, Kenwood, 799 South Pearl Street, Albany, New York 12202.
