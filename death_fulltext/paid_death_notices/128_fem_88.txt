
        McHUGH-Gail Fiske (Gigi), of Philadelphia, PA and formerly of New York City. Died on Tuesday, September 9th, 1997 after a short illness. Daughter of Mrs. Burton Peek McHugh and the late Dr. McHugh and granddaughter of Mrs. John Wilson Fiske, Jr. She is also survived by two brothers, Burton Peek McHugh, Jr. and John Wilson Fiske McHugh. A private memorial service will be held at the convenience of the family. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to The Chapin School, 100 East End Avenue, New York City 10028.
