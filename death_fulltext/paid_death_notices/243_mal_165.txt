
        MAUCK--Ward, of Stonington, CT, beloved brother, father, stepfather and grandfather died April 6th at age 78. He is survived by his sons, Alden, Parker and Andrew and his brothers, F. Taylor Mauck and Osborne Mauck. Ward was born in Bryn Mawr, PA and graduated from the Hotchkiss School, Williams College, and Harvard Law School. He retired as President of the American Institute of Marine Underwriters. A Memorial Service will be held at 11:30 on Friday, April 27th at Calvary Church, 27 Church Street in Stonington, 06378. In lieu of flowers, please send donations in Ward's memory to Calvary Church.
