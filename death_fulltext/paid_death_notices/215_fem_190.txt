
        ROTHENBERG--Ann. Age 96 on December 24, 2004. Devoted wife of the late Dr. Mark Rothenberg. Beloved mother of Judith (Dr. Robert) Podell. Cherished grandmother of Dr. Deborah (Ari) Fishkind, and great-grandmother of Aaron and Marc. Dedicated supporter of Israel and Torah institutions.
        ROTHENBERG -- Ann. The Yeshiva Gedolah Zichron Moshe faculty and students mourn the passing of a gracious, elegant and modest woman. We extend our deep-felt condolences to her daughter Judy and Dr. Robert Podell, granddaughter Dr. Debbie and Ari Fishkind and their children. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva Gedolah Zichron Moshe Rabbi Abba Gorelick, President Simon Kaplan, Chairman, Board of Directors
        ROTHENBERG--Ann. Mesivtha Tifereth Jerusalem mourns the passing of Mrs. Ann Rothenberg, of blessed memory. Benefactor of Torah and our Yeshiva. We extend condolences to her daughter Judith and son-inlaw Dr. Robert Podell, and granddaughter Dr. Deborah and Ari Fishkind, and two great-grandsons. May they be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Israel. Rabbi David Feinstein Rosh Hayeshiva Rabbi Yisrael H. Eidelman Executive Vice-President
