
        GOODSTONE -- Bertram K. 87, of New York and Miami Shores passed away March 30, 2004 after a brief illness. He had a great love of life and lived every day to its fullest. Predeceased by his beloved wife Ellen, he is survived by his daughters Geraldine Goodstone, Felice Zensius, Peggy Katz and Andrew Goldstein. Funeral Services at 1p.m. on April 2, 2004, Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, Miami Beach, FL.
