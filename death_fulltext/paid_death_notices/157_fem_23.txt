
        KUSHNER-Ruth, died February 1. The widow of Samuel Kushner, she was the mother of Kenneth, deceased, and Judith Felsenfeld, married to Carl. Also she is survived by her grandson, Peter Felsenfeld; great-granddaughter, Sierra; sister, Hilda Baker and brothers, Abe and Leonard Mokover. Her funeral will take place on Friday, February 4, 9:30 AM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave. Donations are encouraged to the Palliative Care Program at Mount Sinai Hospital, Box 1070, One Gustave Levy Pl, NY 10029; or the Dept. of Social Services at the Hebrew Home for the Aged, 5901 Palisade Avenue, Riverdale, New York 10471.
