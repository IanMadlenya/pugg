
        CHANG--Ray F. age 59 of Flushing, Queens formerly of Teaneck, NJ, died Sunday, June 13, 2004. Loving son of Shin Chin Chang and the late Chin Chi Chang. Devoted father of Jennifer, William, and Kimberly Chang. He is also survived by three sisters; Ming Chi Lin, Ming Shin Stanton, and Ming Shun Chang and one brother Rau C. Chang. He was an Actuary for AXA Financial in NYC as well as a Life Master Bridge Player. A funeral service will take place on Friday, June 18 at 10AM at the Volk Leber Funeral Home, 789 Teaneck Rd, Teaneck. Interment to follow at Kinsico Cemetery in Valhalla, NY. Family and friends are invited to visit Thursday, 2-5 and 6-9PM.
