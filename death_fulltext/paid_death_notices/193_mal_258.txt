
        SUSMAN-William. A veteran of The Abraham Lincoln Brigade and a founder of The Abraham Lincoln Brigade Archives, Bill is deeply mourned as an activist for social justice, a film producer, and a husband, father, and grandfather, by his wife Helene of Great Neck, NY, his daughter Susan and her husband Sekhar of New York City, his son Paul and his wife Libby of Lewisburg, PA, and his grandchildren Janak, Rebecca, Shantha and Sasha.
