
        ABRAMS-James S. III. Of Lakeville, CT died January 22, 2002, of cancer. Mr. Abrams was born August 22, 1940, in NYC the son of Marguerite Abrams and the late James S. Abrams. He was raised in NYC and South Salem, NY. Prior to his move to Lakeville he lived in Woodstock, VT. He attended the Lycee Francais de New York and graduated from Pomfret School and Franklin and Marshall College. Mr. Abrams was a member of the New York Stock Exchange from 1973 until his retirement in 1988. He is survived by his wife of 21 years, Constance Comstock Abrams, and his children Amy of Venice, CA, Timothy of Boulder, CO, Scott of Greenfield, NH, and Hilary and Catherine of Lakeville, CT. A memorial service will be held at 1:00 PM on Sunday January 27, 2002, at the chapel at Salisbury School in Salisbury, CT. The family asks that in lieu of flowers contributions be made to Deerfield Academy, Deerfield, MA, or Indian Mountain School, Lakeville, CT.
