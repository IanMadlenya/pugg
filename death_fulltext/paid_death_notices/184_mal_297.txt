
        REEVES-Clifford Batchelder, Jr., of Pelham, NY, died on Thursday, May 16, 2002. He was 74. Mr. Reeves worked as Manager of Film and Tape Operations for WCBS-TV in NY. He was with the company for 43 years before retiring in 1996. Mr. Reeves was born March 7, 1928 in Yonkers, NY to Clifford and Mary Chisholm Reeves. He was educated in Pelham and graduated from Pelham High School in 1947 and Emerson College, Boston, MA, in 1951. He is survived by his sister, Barbara Winston, and brother-in-law, Lee Winston, nephew, Christopher Winston, all of Pelham, and his niece, Hilary Oskam of Pittsford, VT. Friends may send donations to Pelham Public Library, 530 Colonial Ave., Pelham, NY 10803.
