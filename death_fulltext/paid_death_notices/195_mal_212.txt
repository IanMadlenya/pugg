
        ROWEN-Henry. Died April 16, 2003 after a brief illness. A longtime resident of NYC, he was a valued and respected librarian for the Columbia University Rare Book and Manuscript Library. Devoted and gracious friend for 25 years to Janet Asteroff of White Plains. His warmth, intelligence and wit will be missed by all who knew him. Donations may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Rd., Bronx, NY 10416.
