
        MATCH-Dorothy. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Dorothy Match, beloved mother of Ellen Koppelman, a dedicated leader of UJA-Federation and an active member of our Women's Campaign. Ellen's husband, Murray, a cherished friend and longtime leader of UJA-Federation, currently serves on our Commission on the Jewish People. We extend our deepest sympathies to Ellen and Murray; to Dorothy's husband, Melvin; to Dorothy's daughter, Risa; to her four grandchildren; and to her entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Joan Ginsburg, Chair of the Women's Campaign
