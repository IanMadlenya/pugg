
        SANGER--Col. Marshall, U.S. Army, Infantry, Ret., 81, died at home in McLean, VA, on July 23. Soldier, scholar, loving husband and father. Mike served his country from 1941 to 1975. He graduated from U.S.M.A. in 1946 and received his Ph.D. from Columbia in 1967. Survived by his beloved wife Lila; children Carol, Michael, Holly and son-in-law John; grandchildren Anna, Michael, Jack, Molly and Andrew; and brothers Dick and Chris. We will miss him forever. Burial at West Point at 1:30 pm, July 30. Contributions in Mike's name to Capitol Hospice, 9300 Lee Hwy., Fairfax, VA 22031.
