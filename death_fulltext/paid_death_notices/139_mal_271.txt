
        CAMPBELL-Brigadier General John C., Jr., USAF Retired. Of Quogue, NY, at age 77, on August 22, 1998. He leaves his beloved wife of 41 years, Jane Shields Campbell. He will be greatly missed by his sister Elizabeth, his children John, Frances and Edward, their spouses Elizabeth Detels Campbell, Roger Netzer and Randi Moore Campbell, and his grandchildren Courtney, Madison, Charles, Harry and Ian. Member of Shinnecock Hills Golf Club and Quogue Field Club. Visitation at Follett Funeral Home, Mill Road, Westhampton Beach, NY, 6-9 PM, Monday, August 24. Mass of Christian Burial at Immaculate Conception R.C. Church, Westhampton Beach, 11 AM, Tuesday, August 25. Interment private. Donations in his memory may be made to East End Hospice, PO Box 1048, 1111 Riverhead Rd., Westhampton Beach, NY 11978.
