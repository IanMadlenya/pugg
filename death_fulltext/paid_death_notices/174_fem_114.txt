
        WISOTSKY-Mary. The Three Arrows Cooperative Society mourns the loss of longtime member, Mary Wisotsky, wife of the late Abraham. Services Monday, at 11 AM, Temple Beth Shalom, Route 6, Mahopac. Halona W. Balgley, President Three Arrows Coop Society
