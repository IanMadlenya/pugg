
        CLAYSON-David, Dr. Died April 10, 2001. David was Professor Emeritus and Director of Clinical Training of Psychology in Psychiatry at the Weill Medical College at Cornell University. He was CoFounder and Charter President of the Association of Professors of Psychology in Medical Schools, and a Fellow of the American Psychological Association. He was 67 years old. He is survived by a brother and several nieces and nephews. Friends are invited to make donations to ALS Association of New York. Services will be private.
