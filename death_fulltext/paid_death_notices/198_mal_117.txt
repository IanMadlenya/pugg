
        PINO--Mario M., 83, of New Port Richey, FL. Born in Brooklyn. Was a proofreader for the New York Times; Army Air Force Veteran of World War II. Survivors include his wife Marie, three sons: Richard of Boston, John, of Morganville, NJ, and Chris of Oldsmar, FL; three grandchildren: Christopher, Laura, and Daniel; brothers Salvatore of Alabama and Rudolph of Florida. >>AD#
