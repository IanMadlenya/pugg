
        ROTAN-Walter. Of New York City. Died October 15, 2001. Born in Baltimore, MD. in 1912, sculptor & teacher. Honored with prizes by the Pennsylvania Academy, the National Academy of Design and the Allied American Artists, his sculptures were included in exhibitions at the Art Institute of Chicago, the Metropolitan Museum, the Philadelphia Museum of Art, the Pennsylvania Academy and galleries in New York City. His works are in the collections of museums such as Brookgreen Gardens, S.C.
