
        HOROWITZ--Ruth ''Bunny''. On October 7, 2003 at age 89. She will be missed by the friends, family, and neighbors whose lives were richer for having known her. Beloved cousin of Sally, Saul, Daniel, Howard, Stacy, Lynne, Charles, and Jason; beloved aunt of Peter and Ann; beloved ''aunt'' of Cara and of Fern. She was a woman of keen intellect, wide knowledge, and great creativity, who loved the arts, and set an example for us all. Contributions to Lighthouse for the Blind, Nature Conservancy, and or the New York/New Jersey Trail Conference.
