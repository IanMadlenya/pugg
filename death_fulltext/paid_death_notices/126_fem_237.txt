
        CLARKE-Dora, died on July 29 at her home in East Northport. Death came after a long illness. Born in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, her love for the violin developed early and she worked to buy her first instrument by the time she was eight. From 1982 to 1992, Ms. Clarke was Director of Music for the Northport/East Northport School District. Ms. Clarke is survived by two brothers and their wives, E. Edward and Elaine Clarke of West Hartford, CT and Richard and Barbara Clarke of Columbia, SC and six nieces and nephews. Instead of flowers, friends are asked to send contributions to a Dora Clarke Northport Music Scholarship Fund, C/O Richard Manning, 25 Hillside Place, Northport, NY 11768. Visiting hours Wed. 2-5, and 7-9 PM at Brueggemann Funeral Home, 522 Larkfield Road, East Northport, L.I. Funeral service Thursday at First Presbyterian Church, Main Street, Northport. For time of service, please contact the funeral home.
