
        PEKEROW--Meredith. January 14 in Amsterdam. Born Brooklyn May 8, 1943 to Boris and Bert Pekerow. Brooklyn College grad. She shared her passion for life, love and travel with her most cherished possession, her beloved son Lucas, and with all those who knew her. A one-of-a-kind. Survived by son Lucas, sister Susan, nephews Richard and David, and loving cousins.
