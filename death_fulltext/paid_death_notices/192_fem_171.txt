
        ROBERTS-Cornelia M. On January 14, 2003 at age 100. Wife of the late William Bryce Roberts, born in Vienna, Austria 1902. Dear friend, excellent equestrian and American patriot. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, January 18 at 10AM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Red Cross.
