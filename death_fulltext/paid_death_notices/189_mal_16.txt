
        CHAPIN - James B. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Queens Borough Public Library mourn the passing of a dear friend and colleague. He was a tireless advocate for literacy and strengthened Queens Library with his wisdom and leadership. He served as a library Trustee from 1980 until his death and was Board President in 1993 and 1994. He was also a valuable member of the Queens Library Foundation Board. He will be sorely missed. Our deepest condolences to his beloved wife Diana and the Chapin family. George Stamatiades, President, Queens Library Board Gary E. Strong, Library Director Members of the Board of Trustees
