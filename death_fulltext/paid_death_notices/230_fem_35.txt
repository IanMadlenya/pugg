
        McELWAIN--Ruth Van Deventer Jones, Mar. 2, 1924-Dec. 8, 2005, daughter of the late Mr. & Mrs. Nicholas Ridgely Jones of NYC and Glen Cove. As Beverly Gray, she performed as a vocalist in numerous nightclubs and later cofounded the Broward women's Chorus in Florida. A generous supporter of animal rights, she is greatly missed by her three nephews-Bouvier, Ridgely and Christopher Beale. Interment at the Locust Valley Cemetery on March 5, 2006, 1PM.
