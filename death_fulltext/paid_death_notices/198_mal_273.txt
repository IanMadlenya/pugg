
        BLUMBERG--Edward J., 95, on July 28th, 2003. Beloved husband for 69 years of Carolyn, devoted father of Nancy and Bob. Loving grandfather of Lori, Todd, Gary, Julie and Karen. President and Chairman for many years of Blumberg Law Products, established in 1887 by his father, Julius. Our lives were enriched by his loyal, affectionate nature; his generous, open mind; his conscientious integrity; his dapper good looks; his adventurous spirit; his biting wit; his love of life. Services and entombment at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Friday, 2PM. The family will receive friends at his home, Thursday and Friday, from 5:30-8:30PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to The American Civil Liberties Union. >>AD#
