
        SEYMOUR--Mary (nee Denker), 96, wife of Milton A. Seymour of Southbury, CT. & Sarasota, FL., died October 16, 2006. A teacher who specialized in reading skills in the NYC school system for 25 years, Mrs. Seymour was an indefatigable world traveler whose many talents included sculpting, painting and decorating. A devoted wife, sister, aunt and great-aunt beloved by family and friends, Mrs. Seymour leaves behind a legacy of love and a family blessed by her presence who will dearly miss her warmth, optimism and encouragement
