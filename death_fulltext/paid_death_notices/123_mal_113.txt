
        PRICEMAN-Oliver M. Beloved son of Mark & Alla of Larchmont, NY. Brother of Eric, brother-in-law of Kathleen and uncle of Zachary, Mark and Kristina, all of Oak Park, IL., & nephew of Hedda Prejsman of Tel Aviv, Israel. Died tragically in Larchmont, New York, on April 6. He carried a burden too crushing for him to bear. He did not know how many loved the noble human being that he was. Funeral arrangements will be private. A memorial reunion will be held at a later date. Memorial contributions may be made to NARSAD Research Fund (National Alliance for Research on Schizophrenia and Depression) 60 Cutter Mill Road, Great Neck, New York 11021.
