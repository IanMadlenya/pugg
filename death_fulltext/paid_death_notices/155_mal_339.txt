
        VADER-Norman. The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center records with deep sorrow the passing of Norman Vader, Treasurer of the Associate Board of Directors. An active member since 1972, Mr. Vader served on numerous committees to raise funds for the Hospital. Our sincere condolences to his wife, Ellen, and the entire family. Edwin L. Schulman Chairman, Board of Trustees Frank J. Maddalena President and CEO Hon. Samuel Plotkin Chairman, Associate Board
