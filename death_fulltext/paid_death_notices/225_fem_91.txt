
        KEHL--Pearl J. Beloved wife of the late H. William. Beloved mother of Jeffrey and Shelley, cherished grandmother of Alexander Harry and Danielle Leah, beloved sister of Bert and adored by our many cousins, family and friends. She lived a long, full life and passed away quietly at home in her sleep. Donations in her memory may be made to the Horace Mann School, 231 West 246th Street, Bronx, NY 10471.
