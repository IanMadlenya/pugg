
        HANIGAN-Harry A. Jr. Died on Wednesday at home. Graduate of Loyola School, Class of 1934. Attended Fordham College. Retired accountant, Schenly Industries. Survived by his sister Kathleen H. Ward, six nieces and one nephew. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Loyola School, 980 Park Ave., NYC 10028. Friends may call Friday, 2-5:00 and 7-9:00 at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81 St. Mass of Christian burial on Saturday, 10:30 A.M., St. Ignatius Loyola Church.
