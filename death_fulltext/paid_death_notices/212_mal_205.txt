
        SCHLEIM--Peter A., creative genius born March 1942 in Berlin, Germany, passed away September 20, 2004. Author of many inventions and processes, he leaves behind a daughter, Carrie Stasiak of Poconos, PA; a sister, Fay Gillespie of Port Washington, NY; a partner, Paula SaintAmour of Marbletown, NY, and adoring grandsons, nieces and nephews, and many who loved him. For info call Simpson - Gaus Funeral Home, Kingston, NY.
