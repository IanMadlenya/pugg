
        GARSON-Sam. On January 28, 1998, at the age of 91. Father of Gerald P. Garson, Justice of the NYS Supreme Court. Husband of the late Eva Garson, a woman of valor. Grandfather, great-grandfather, uncle, devoted brother and to all who knew him, he was oneof-a-kind. Services today, 1 PM, Weinstein West End Funeral Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn, New York.
