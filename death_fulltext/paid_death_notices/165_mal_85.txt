
        SEEVE-Harry, age 84, on October 3. Beloved husband of the late Zelda and devoted father of Marsha Rolston and Leonard Seeve and the late William Seeve. Brother of Moses Seeve and grandfather of Michael, David, Eric, Matthew, Brian and Sean. He was a fund raiser for the UJA and founder of Seeve Enterprises, a New York based real estate firm. Services will be held on Friday, October 6, at 10am at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, 76th St & Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, NY.
