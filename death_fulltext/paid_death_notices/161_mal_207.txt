
        HERTZBERG-Robert M. Age 77, author and director of documentary films, died June 10, 2000, of heart failure after a short illness. He is survived by his partner, Howard R. Turner, sister Marjorie H. Phillips, brother-in-law Harry Phillips III, niece Elizabeth Phillips, nephews Robert T. Phillips and William R. Phillips and eight grandnieces and nephews. A gathering of friends in remembrance will be held on Tuesday, June 27, 2000, from 5-8 PM at 57 West 75th St., Apt. 3A, New York, NY.
