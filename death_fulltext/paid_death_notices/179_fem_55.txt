
        BLACK-SHEA-Mildred, M.D. Of Barnstable, MA, formerly of Old Field, NY and Gardner, MA on December 3, 2001. Beloved wife of the late John F. Shea, M.D. Loving mother of Moira Shea Wittman, M.D. of Falmouth, MA, Emily Leoni of Canton, MA, John F. Shea of Boston, MA, and Alice Mecca of Colchester, CT. Cherished grandmother of Laura and Emily Wittman, Virginia and Robert Leoni, and Christopher, Caroline and Audrey Mecca. Special mother-in-law of William Wittman, M.D., Carl Leoni, and Robert Mecca. Dear sister of Doris Black Schaefer of Bay Shore, NY. Devoted daughter of the late John J. Black, M.D. and Lillian McAllister Black. She was a graduate of the College of New Rochelle, New York Medical College, Flower Fifth Avenue, Class of 1950. Funeral Mass was celebrated on Friday, December 7, 2001 at 10:00 AM at Our Lady of Hope Church, Barnstable, MA.
