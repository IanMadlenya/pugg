
        EDELSTEIN-Ben. United Merchants legend, then career counselor to thousands, died of cancer yesterday. Brilliant, funny, not easy and never boring. He will be greatly missed by his loving wife Mal O'Reilly, son Cliff, daughter Joan, grandchildren Marc, Karen, David and everyone who knew him. Services Sunday 11:30 AM, ''Wien & Wien'', Hackensack, New Jersey. 800-378-1892.
