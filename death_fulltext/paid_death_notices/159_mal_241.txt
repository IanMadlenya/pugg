
        STERLING-Clifford. Age 41, artist and nature enthusiast, died at home in New York, on April 16. Devoted and beloved husband of Ann. Dear son of Enid and Abraham (deceased), brother of Adam and Benjamin. A memorial recepetion for family and friends will be held on Friday, April 28, from 68 PM, at the Parish House of the Church of St. Luke in the Fields, 487 Hudson St, NYC. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to the Mohonk Preserve, Inc., PO Box 715, New Paltz, NY 12561. He will be remembered and loved always.
