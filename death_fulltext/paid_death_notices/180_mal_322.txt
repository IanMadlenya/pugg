
        EISENPRESS-Harry. Of White Plains, N.Y. On Jan. 19, 2002 at age 84. Beloved husband of Alta for 54 years, and dear brother of Edith Ukeles. Loving father of Aron and his wife Dorothy, Eli and his wife Angela, and Samuel. Proud grandfather of Benjamin and Sasha. A generous man of integrity, warmth, kindness, determination and scholarship who will be missed by all those whose lives he touched. Services were on Monday.
