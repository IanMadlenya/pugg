
        ROSENTHAL--Hinda. The Board and Staff of LCT are saddened by the death of our good friend and enthusiastic advocate, Hinda, who loved and enjoyed the work of the theater and was especially devoted to our literary journal, the LCT Review, which she believed in passionately, admired wholeheartedly and supported generously. She will be especially missed at editorial gatherings. John B. Beinecke, Chairman Andre Bishop, Artistic Dir. Bernard Gersten, Executive Producer
