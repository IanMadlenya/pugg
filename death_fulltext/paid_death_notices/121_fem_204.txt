
        BITTKER-Anne S. Of New Haven, Ct., died in her sleep on Feb 2, 1997 at the Connecticut Hospice of Branford, Ct. She graduated from the Horace Mann School for Girls in New York City and Connecticut College for Women. She was active in many New Haven civic and charitable organizations, including the League of Women Voters, the Friends of the New Haven Library and the Fair Haven Soup Kitchen. She is survived by her husband, Boris I. Bittker, by her two children Susan of Edinburgh, Scotland and Daniel of San Francisco, by her sister Margaret S. Kahn of Chicago and her brother, Carl Stern of Lynchburg, Va. Memorial contributions may be made to the Friends of the New Haven Public Library, 133 Elm St., New Haven 06510 or to Connecticut Hospice, 61 Burban Drive, Branford, Ct 06405. There will be a Memorial Gathering at the New Haven Lawn Club on Saturday, March 22 at 2:30 PM.
