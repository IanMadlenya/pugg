
        MULDOON--Sister Catherine. Sister Catherine Muldoon, known in Religious life as Sr. Marian Catherine, beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, Convent of Mary the Queen, died Saturday, March 11, 2006. Recognized nationally as a prominent figure in nursing education and nursing service, Sister was a sought after speaker for a variety of professional nursing organizations. She is survived by a nephew John R. Muldoon and a cousin Patricia Kennedy. Reposing at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark Street, Yonkers, NY on Tuesday from 11-2pm with a prayer service at 1:30pm. Reposing at St. Vincent's Hospital Chapel, 158 W. 12th St., NYC, Tuesday from 4-8pm. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10am, at the St. Vincent's Hospital. Interment to follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Ave., Bronx, NY 10471.
