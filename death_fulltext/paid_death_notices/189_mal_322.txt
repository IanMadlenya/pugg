
        GREEN-Adolph. The Officers, Governors and members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their beloved and esteemed Member, Adolph Green. We are honored and proud to have been touched by Adolph Green, a great lyricist with enormous talent who shared a legacy of commitment to the very best. Our sincerest condolences to his family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Sally Jessy Raphael, Prior Stewie Stone, Scribe Fred C. Klein, Treasurer Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        GREEN-Adolph. The Friars Foundation regretfully announces the passing of the legendary Adolph Green. He was last year's recipient of our Creative Achievement Award. Leo Greenland, Chairman of the Board Cy Leslie, President
        GREEN-Adolph. An abrupt close, too soon, to an exciting life. We'll miss him. Loving thoughts to Phyllis, Adam, Amanda and Betty. Floria Lasky and Jere Couture
        GREEN-Adolph. With deep sorrow, we regret the passing of Adolph Green, an honored Inductee in the Songwriters Hall of Fame. Hal David, Chairman/CEO
