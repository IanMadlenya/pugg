
        BURKE--Thomas Churchill. The Trustees and staff of the Altman Foundation mourn the death of our Trustee Emeritus, Thomas Churchill Burke. For almost 60 years, Tom brought his acute intelligence and sharp wit to our board deliberations, and his investment wisdom translated directly into the ever-in creasing support we were able to direct to organizations serving New Yorkers in need. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Janet and to his family. Jane B. O'Connell, Pres. Karen L. Rosa, Vice Pres. and Exec. Director. $(6$)BURKE--Thomas Churchill, of Watch Hill, RI, formerly of New York, NY and Hobe Sound, FL, died on February 23, 2007 at the age of 82. He was a retired partner in the law firm of White & Case, Trustee of the Altman Foundation and former member of the Board of Education of the City of New York. Survived by his loving wife, Janet McKay Burke, and beloved seven children: Janet B. Connor, Thomas C. Burke, Jr., Michael F. Burke, James M. Burke, Peter C. Burke, Anne B. Manella, and Susan H. Burke, 14 grandchildren and two sisters, Patricia B. Debany and Jane B. O'Connell. He was the son of the late John and Helen Burke. He was predeceased by his brothers, John S. Burke, Jr. and Michael Burke and sister, Mary C. Burke. Visitation is at Gaffney Dolan Funeral Home, located at 59 Spruce St, Westerly, RI 02891 from 3:00-5:00 pm and 6:00 - 8:00 pm. (401)596-2648. A mass of Christian burial is scheduled for Thursday, March 1, 2007 at 11:00 am at the Church of St. Clare, 4 Saint Clare Way, Misquamicut, RI 02891. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Tom's memory to either the Westerly Hospital, 25 Wells Street, Westerly, RI 02891 or Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of New York, 1011 First Avenue, New York, NY 10022.
