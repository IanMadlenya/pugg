
        LYNCH--Charles W., Sr., of Denville, NJ, on March 3, 2007. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on June 19, 1912. Beloved husband of the late Helen Murray. Survived by his four children, Charles W. Jr. and Kristina of Easton, CT, Larry and Norene of Skillman, NJ, Richard and Linda of Morris Plains, NJ, and Helen and Roger Canal of Brooklyn, NY. Also survived by 15 grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren. He was an attorney; he had also been a singing waiter. He retired from Travelers' Insurance Company and was a loyal Regis graduate. Mass of Christian Burial will be at the Church of the Assumption in Morristown, NJ, Tuesday at 10am.
