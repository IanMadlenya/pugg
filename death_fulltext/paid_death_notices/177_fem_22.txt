
        CHATARY-Magda (nee Szentgyorgyi-Horvath), born in Budapest, Hungary. Artist, principal engineer, and devoted mother. Passed on September 27th, 2001, age 74. Survived by her loving daughter Christina, dear brother George, and many caring family members and true friends, as well as her beloved little Milou. We will forever miss her noble soul, angelic spirit, wit, and essential love of life. Funeral to be held in Budapest. New York City memorial services to be announced. ''...murmur, a little sadly, how love fled and passed upon the mountains overhead, and hid her face amid a crown of stars.''
