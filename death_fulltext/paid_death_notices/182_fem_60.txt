
        SCHUSTER-Anne Storrs. Beloved mother of Carl, Ronald and Derek. Caring grandmother of Sloane, Brooke, Devon, Megan and Christopher. Sister of Carolyn Stofford. Passed away in her sleep on March 2. Born August 13, 1909, in Deal Beach, NJ, to Frank and Amanda Storrs. Anne will be remembered as a sincerely devoted family person and friend, as a woman of style who exhibited sound human values, and as an accomplished athlete and traveler. Funeral service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, on Monday, March 4, at 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Family Dynamics, 10 Rockefeller Plaza, Suite 1104, NYC 10020.
