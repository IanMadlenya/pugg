
        ZAWEL--Charles D. O.D., died on November 29, 2006, at his home in Salem, OR. He was born in Havanah, Cuba, on May 28, 1934. He lived in the Bronx, NY, until 1953. He is survived by his former wife Phyllis, and his current wife Joan. He is predeceased by his wife Miriam, and his son Michael. He is also survived by his daughters Heidi and Cheryl, and her husband Michael Patzer, and two grandsons Jeffrey and Mark. He faught a courageous battle, now is the time to rest in peace. -Paul and Ruth Zawel, (Leesburg, FL) Daniel and Susan Zawel, (Harrison, NY)
