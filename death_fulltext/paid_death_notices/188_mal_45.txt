
        SPITZ-Fred M. passed away Thursday, August 28th, 2002 at his home in Miami, Florida. Mr. Spitz was 59 years of age and is survived by his beloved children Jonathan, Amanda and Kim, his beautiful grandchildren, Zachery and Taylor and his sister Patricia Mondre of Fort Lauderdale, and his brother Stuart Spitz of Delray Beach. He will be sorely missed by his family and his many friends. Services were held privately on Monday, September 2, 2002 in Ft. Lauderdale.
