
        MOTTLEY--Pam Guinan. 63, died after a long illness on November 30, surrounded by beloved animals and family. She was a teacher of English and most recently a Gestalt psychotherapist. She is survived by daughter Mariah, son-in-law Sean Plumlee, Pat, Ken and Christopher Rushin and Robert Mottley. Services will be held at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine at 10:30 am, December 11. Donations may be made to the Central Park Conservancy.
