
        FINNERAN--John Buckley, 69, passed away Monday, August 18, 2003 in Stamford, CT. Born on July 14, 1934, he grew up in Larchmont, NY and graduated from Archbishop Stepinac High School in White Plains. A 1956 graduate of Tufts University in Boston, Mr. Finneran served as a Commissary and Sales Officer aboard USS Antietam, and was the Material Division Officer at NAS Lakehurst, NJ. In civilian life, Capt. Finneran held the position of Vice President, Director of Marketing, Seagram Overseas Sales Company. Mr. Finneran earned a Masters of Business Administration degree from New York University, and was President of Chapter One Supply Corps Association. He resided in Europe for four years and traveled extensively in the Far East, Latin America and the Caribbean. Mr. Finneran came to reside in Stamford, CT., and for the last ten years has worked as a Realtor with the William Pitt Agency. He is survived by his wife, Dorothy Carpenter Finneran, daughter Kerry F. Gardiner, and son Jack T. Finneran, six stepchildren, four grandchildren and 17 step-grandchildren. He is also survived by is brothers Gerard B. Finneran, Thomas Rowan, William B. Finneran, and sisters Judith A. Finneran and Susan Ashley. A funeral mass will be held for family and friends on August 22, 2003 at 10:00 AM at Our Lady Star of the Sea Church, 1200 Shippan Avenue, Stamford, CT. 06902, 203-324-4634. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Patrons Program, 1011 First Avenue, 14th Floor, NY, NY 10022, Att: Jill Kafka.
