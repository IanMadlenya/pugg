
        JOHNSON--Alfred, M.D. on January 22, 2004 at age 84. Dr. Johnson served for many years as a urologist at Lenox Hill Hospital. Beloved husband of Mary Johnson and brother-in-law of Lily Fichopolos. Loving father of Lillian Kreitsek, Jr., Irene Campbell and their husbands William and John. Adoring grandfather of Christopher. Dear brother of Mary Kritzalis, Dr. John Yanopoulos, Lula Econom and devoted uncle. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Sunday January 25th 2-5PM and 7-9PM. Services at the Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Cathedral, 319 E. 74th St., Monday 12 Noon. Interment to follow at Mount Hope Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to Archdiocese Cathedral Philoptochos Children's Medical Fund, 337 E. 74th St., NY, NY 10021.
