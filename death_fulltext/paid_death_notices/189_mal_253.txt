
        MEHTA-Mehli. The Board of Directors and staff of American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra extend our deepest condolences to our Co-Chairman and IPO Music Director, Zubin Mehta and his wife Nancy on the passing of his father. Mehli Mehta's muscial genius and his dedication to teaching will be remembered with gratitude by generations to come. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Itzhak Perlman, Co-Chairman Lynn Syms, President Suzanne K. Ponsot, Exec. Dir.
