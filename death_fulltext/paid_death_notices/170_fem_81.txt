
        LANDA - Fanny (Faye), nee Schneier. Died peacefully in her home on March 10, 2001. She was the beloved wife of the late George P. Landa and the most adoring and cherished aunt and great-aunt. All who knew her were taken by her charm, grace, beauty, style and wit. We will always remember with admiration her flare for decorating, collecting things of beauty, her love of nature and her talents in cuisine and hospitality. ''Nan Fan'' possessed a kind and gracious spirit which will be forever treasured and deeply missed by her family and friends. Graveside services private. For information, contact ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
