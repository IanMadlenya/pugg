
        BARTNOFF--Shepard. Of Englewood, NJ, on September 29, 2004 at the Jewish Home in Rockleigh, NJ. Dr. Bartnoff earned his doctorate in Physics from Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He was president of Jersey Central Power and Light Company in Morristown, NJ. Dr. Bartnoff served on numerous boards in both the private and public sector, and he was active in various environmental and charitable organizations. He is survived by his wife of 30 years, Naomi Kislak Fisher Bartnoff, daughters Judge Judith Bartnoff of Washington, DC, and Donna Isaacs of Norwich, VT, stepdaughters Lucy Fisher of Los Angeles and Emily Fisher of Douglas, AZ, and stepson David Fisher of New York City. He is survived by a sister, Leah Kesner of Albuquerque, NM and 14 grandchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife Irene Tennebaum Bartnoff, sister Naomi Bartnoff Strunsky and daughter Rabbi Devora Bartnoff. Service Sunday, October 3, 11:30 AM at Wien & Wien, Hackensack, NJ. Memorial contributions can be made to Senior Adult Programs at the JCC on the Palisades, 411 East Clinton Ave., Tenafly, NJ 07670. Information 800-322-0533.
