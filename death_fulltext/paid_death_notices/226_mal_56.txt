
        JOSEPH--Andrew. The officers and staff of the Social Service Employees Union Local 371 express their profound sadness at the untimely passing of Andy Joseph, union activist, officer, colleague and longtime friend. Andy's keen mind for numbers and political analysis, as well as his ability to inspire and mobilize others, made an enormous contribution to the history of the union. He remained a close friend and ally throughout his distinguished career. We extend our love and deepest sympathy to his wife, Terry. Charles Ensley, President Officers and Staff, SSEU Local 371
