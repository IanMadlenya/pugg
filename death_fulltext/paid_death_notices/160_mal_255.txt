
        LOBRANO-Alexander Flick at age 70 in Greenville, South Carolina. Son of the late Gustave Stubbs Lobrano and Dorothy Jean Flick Lobrano. Survived by his sister Dorothy Lobrano Guth, his sons Alexander Flick Lobrano, Jr., John Drake Lobrano and Grant Colville Lobrano and his daughter, Elizabeth Lobrano Markham, as well as nine grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, August 6, 2000 at Stanwich Congregational Church, 237 Taconic Road, Greenwich, Connecticut at 2:00 p.m. Donations may be made in his memory to Amherst College, P.O. Box 5000, Amherst, Massachusetts, 01002 or Children International, P.O. Box 219357, Kansas City, Missouri 64121.
