
        GUTLEBER -- Leitha (Lee) Mae. On January 3, 2005. Loving wife of John J. Gutleber, devoted mother of Stephanie, Jaclyn and Megan, cherished sister of Charles and William Hanigan. Orphaned at a young age and raised in foster care, Mrs. Gutleber had strong compassion for those with limited opportunities, which led her to leadership roles in many Long Island organizations including The Neighborhood House in Setauket, the Museum at Stony Brook, the Ward-Melville Heritage Society, the Historical Society, the Usdan Center for the Performing Arts, Adelphi University, the Arthritis Foundation and the National Center for Disabilities. Donations in her memory may be made to any of the above. The wake will be held on Thursday, January 6 at OB Davis Funeral Home, Pt. Jefferson 2-4:30pm and 7-9:30pm. The funeral mass will be held on Friday, January 7 at 10:45am at St. James Roman Catholic Church in Setauket.
