
        GRANDIS-Joan E. March 18, 1932-October 16, 2000. Assistant Director of Speech Improvement for New York City Board of Education, will be missed by her devoted husband Paul, 45 years of marriage, and all who knew her as a kind, scholarly and compassionate person. Taught at Hunter College and NYU Downtown. I will miss you forever as a friend and husband, traveling all over the world together. Have a good trip to another world, this time alone. Services will be held on Friday, October 20, 2000 at 1:15 PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel at Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street, 212-362-6600.
