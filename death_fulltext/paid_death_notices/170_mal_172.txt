
        RUBIN-Jerry H., 79. Of Coconut Creek, FL. formerly of Oceanside, New York passed away March 10, 2001 after a brief illness. He was a Captain from the 9th Armored division U.S. Army W.W. II and an executive in the garment industry. Survived by his beloved wife of 54 years Dolly; devoted father of Stephanie (Glenn) Lash and Linda Malinsky, loving brother of Florence Eisenstein and Corrine (Frank) Streich and cherished grandfather of Stuart, Sarah, Britt and Miss Crayons; loving uncle to many.
