
        BROWNSTEIN-Ruth Lynn, 85. Of Annapolis, Maryland. Formerly of West Hempstead and Great Neck, New York. Died September 6. Mrs. Brownstein was co-founder of the first public library in West Hempstead and worked as a librarian for the Plainedge and Uniondale Public School systems. Preceded in death by her husband, Sidney Brownstein in 1992, she is survived by one son, Peter Jacob of Arnold, MD. Services 11:30 A.M. Monday, September 13, Parkside Funeral Home, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn. Burial at Old Montefiore Cemetery.
