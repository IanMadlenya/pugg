
        DARDECK-Philip, 83, of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Lawrence, NY, died April 13, 1997. He is survived by his beloved wife and best friend of 54 years, Shirley nee Hahn, his loving daughter Kathryn Dardeck of Hatfield, MA, his devoted son and daughter-in-law, Stephen and Judy Dardeck, and adored grandsons, Adam and Aaron, of Rutland VA, his brothers, Ben, of New York City and Sam, of Margate, FL, his brother-in-law, Jerry Hahn (Claire) of Atlanta, GA, and his special niece Judy Cooper (Jason) of Boca Raton, FL and many loving family members and friends. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 W. Palmetto Park Rd., Boca Raton, FL 33488 or the American Cancer Society. Graveside services will be held at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY, April 16, 1997 at 3 PM. For information contact Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels, Coconut Creek, FL. 954-427-6500.
