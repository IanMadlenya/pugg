
        GOTTESMAN-Hyam. Died on August 23rd following a 2-1/2 year courageous battle with cancer and a week after his 45th birthday. Beloved husband of Susan, devoted father of David, Lena & Mathew. He had the remarkable ability to see humor in every life situation, even when facing death. He never lost his cheerfulness and good spirits. We loved him dearly. Funeral at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, at 10 A.M., Tuesday, August 25th.
