
        KOLODNY-Maxwell H. On May 3, 2001, at age 87, in Lake Worth, FL. Beloved husband of Selma. Devoted father of Robert, Joan Rossman and Richard and his wife Janine. Brother of Mildred Gottfried and Saul and Leonard. Loving grandfather of Linda, Lora, Lisa, Jonathan Rossman, Michelle and David. Dr. Kolodny received his Ph.D. and M.D. from Columbia University and maintained a Manhattan-based private practice. He was on the staff of Mt. Sinai Hospital for decades until his retirement in 1983. He was a brilliant, loving man who will be remembered and missed by all who knew him.
