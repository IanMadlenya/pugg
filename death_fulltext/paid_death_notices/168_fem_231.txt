
        SUNKIN-Mimi. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
        SUNKIN-Mirel (Mimi). Sixty-seven of New York City on January 21, 2001. She was predeceased by her beloved husbnd Irving. Survived by devoted children Neil, Andrew and his wife Leslie, Ellen and her husband Jay Deutchman, four grandchildren Marley and Jesse Deutchman, Jamie and Michael Sunkin, loving brother Paul Jacobs. She was active in the Park Avenue Synagogue. Services today at 1:00 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, Mount Vernon, NY. Interment at Cedar Park Cemetery. Donations to Park Avenue Synagogue for The Food Pantry.
