
        MASON-Linda F. Died June 13. Age 61, of Keene, NH, formerly of Forest Hills, NY. Accomplished artist, painter, folksinger, beloved mother and cherished friend. Daughter of Dr. Irwin and Anne Mason. Survived by her son Jay H. and his wife Ksenia Edson, Princeton, NJ. Memorial service Saturday, June 22, 2 PM, Foley Funeral Home, 49 Court St, Keene, NH.
