
        LIPSKY-Dorothy (nee Shospak). ''Bads,'' 89, died peacefully in her sleep at Parkway Hospital on Saturday, October 20. She is survived by her devoted husband of 65 years, Daniel, her two loving children, Ina and David, her three grandchildren, Julia, Daniel and Suzanne, and her brother Arnold. Dorothy was a sensitive artist, decorator, art teacher and caring wife and mother. She always had a smile and kind word to say to children and adults from all walks of life.
