
        DANIEL-Ramona of 183 Good Hill Road, Weston, CT died May 28th at Norwalk (CT) Hospital, following an illness of 10 days. She was the wife of James Daniel, retired Roving Editor of Reader's Digest and the last of 7 daughters of Eladio Teijeiro and Ramona Alvarez of New York and Santiago de Compostela, Spain. Born December 17, 1916 ''Nenita'' Teijeiro and her husband were graduated from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill in 1937. Married April 15, 1939 they had daughters Jane Clare Daniel of Gloucester, Ma., mother of Liza Daniel Butler of Uxbridge, Ma., and James Daniel Nagy of Weston, CT; and Nina Ramona Daniel, mother of Daniel Allen Ritts and James Paul Ritts, all of Weston. Other descendants are great grandsons Matthew and Cameron Butler. Funeral services were private. A memorial at the residence on June 25 for family and friends will celebrate the joy she brought to so many.
