
        BIDDLE--Nicholas Duke. The Board of Directors and staff of the International Rescue Committee are saddened by the death of Nicholas Duke Biddle, a valued member of our board from 1960 to 1972, and father of George Biddle our Senior Vice President. As chairman of the IRC Caribbean Refugee Program in the 1960's Nicholas Biddle worked tirelessly to assist refugees fleeing tyrannical regimes in Latin America, particularly Cuba after Castro came to power. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Elena, to George and his sisters Pandora and Lucile and their families. Winston Lord and James C. Strickler Co-Chairmen John C. Whitehead, Chairman Emeritus George Rupp, President
        BIDDLE--Nicholas Duke. The Boys & Girls Harbor Community joins the family of our founder, Anthony Drexel Duke, in sadness at the passing of the Harbor's Honorary Chairman, Nicholas Duke Biddle. He will be remembered for his ceaseless loyalty to the Harbor's mission of serving the children of New York City. Stephen J. Dannhauser, Chairman, Board of Trustees Hans E. Hageman, Executive Director
