
        RICE--Marilyn. Of Delray Beach, FL and Harrison, New York. Died February 21, 2005. Beloved wife, mother, grandmother and sister. Funeral service on February 24, 2005 at 2 P.M. at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, New York.
        RICE--Marilyn. The Jewish Community Center of Harrison announces with profound sorrow the loss of its esteemed Founding Member. We extend deepest sympathy to her husband, Samuel, their children, Susan, Rona and the entire family. Alfredo F. Borodowski, Rabbi Sarah A. Zemel, Hazzan Robert M. Schack, President
