
        ROSEN--Steven Mark, of Irvington, New York, died on Monday, January 12th. He was 61 years old. Steve had a distinguished 37-year career at NBC television. He was Associate Director for NBC Sports and NBC News and directed many NBC programs, including Grandstand, Today, Sunrise, Weekend Nightly News and many News Specials. A Memorial Service will be held at NBC on Wednesday, February 4, at 11am, in Studio 8-H. Steve is survived by his wife, Penelope Fleming; children: Scott, Craig, Abigail and Emma; parents: Charles and Ruth; and three grandchildren. He was deeply loved and will be greatly missed by his family and friends.
