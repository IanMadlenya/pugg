
        MERRILL-Meg. Beloved wife of Si Merrill, dear mother of Michael Kiddon, loving grandmother of Chloe and Olivia Kiddon and daughter-in-law Joan Kiddon. Her only other survivor is her brother, Joseph Hartman, of Memphis, Tenn. She was the author of two successful Simon & Schuster books on needlepoint and two Pet Library volumes on ocelots and margays-a vibrant, exciting woman, deeply committed to social justice. She died on March 11th, 1999, at NYU Medical Center from complications of lung cancer. Funeral arrangements private.
