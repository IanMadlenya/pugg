
        STANLEY-William F. Age 59. Of North Salem, NY. Passed away on September 24, 1999, after a heroic battle with cancer. Adored and adoring husband of Patricia for 32 years. Beloved father of Ann Stanley and Lauren Lehmann. Cherished brother of Lynn Preschlack and Marnie Heck. Devoted son of the late Walter and Ann Stanley. His courage, integrity, humor, and humanity will forever be remembered by his family and friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the William F. Stanley Memorial Fund at White Plains Hospital, Davis Avenue at East Post Road, White Plains, NY 10601. A memorial service will be held at St. Matthew's Church in Bedford, NY, on October 3, at 1 P.M.
