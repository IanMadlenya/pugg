
        SHUSTER-Herbert V. On Monday, September 20, of Newton, MA. Beloved husband for 47 years to Rosanne (Feldman). Loving father of Amy Jo & Howard Muscott of Amherst, NH, Mark W. & Beth Shuster of Newton, MA and James L. Shuster of Providence, RI. Cherished grandfather of Nathan Gabriel Muscott and Emily Gail and Abigail Shuster. Dear brother of the late Edward Shuster and brother-in-law of Lawrence M. & Harriet Feldman of Greenwich, CT. Services at the Levine Chapel, 470 Harvard Street, Brookline, MA, on Wednesday, September 22 at 12:00 Noon. Memorial observance at his late residence, Wednesday through Friday 3:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made to the Wolf Medical Education Fund, Brigham and Women's Hospital, 75 Francis Street, Boston, Massachusetts 02115.
