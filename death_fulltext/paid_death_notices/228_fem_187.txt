
        SEKEY - Suzanne. Passed away January 18, 2006, age 90, of kidney failure. A prominent interior designer, she worked with Harold Leeds, I.M. Pei, Marcel Breuer, Richard Meier and others. Her projects included the Paris Theatre, New York; the Caribe Hilton, San Juan, Puerto Rico; Bonniers Retail Store, New York; Earth Sciences Building, MIT, Cambridge, Mass.; ''Resources for Man'' Pavilion, EXPO 67, Montreal; Campus Center, University of Massachusetts, Amherst. She is survived by her loving sister and brother, Yvette Eastman and Cornelius Clark. She was the beloved aunt of Pascal Soriano and Florence Bachofen. A friend of many, she will be sorely missed.
