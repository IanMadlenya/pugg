
        BROWN-Robert J. Age 87, of Vero Beach, Fla., died at home on Jan. 12, 1999. Former resident of Old Westbury, NY; owned Robert Chevrolet, Hicksville, NY, with his surviving son Robert for the last 40 years. In addition to his son he is survived by his wife Joan and six grandchildren: Scott, Tracy, Michelle, Todd, Shannon, Robin; one great-grandchild, Lauren. Services at Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach, Fla. The family suggests memorial gifts to the VNA Hospice Care, 1111-36th St, Vero Beach FL 32960 in lieu of flowers.
