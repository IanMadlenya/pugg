
        PEARLE-Sophie. Age 96. Beloved mother of Harriet and Edward Goodstone. Loving grandmother of Marjorie, Michael and Lori Goodstone. Great grandmother of Adam and Emily Goodstone. We love her and will miss her. Funeral 10AM Thursday, Jewish Center of the Hamptons.
        PEARLE-Sophie. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of Sophie Pearle, beloved mother of Harriet Goodstone. We extend our deepest condolences to Harriet and her family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
