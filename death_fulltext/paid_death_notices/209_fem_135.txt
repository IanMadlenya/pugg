
        KORNBERG--Selma Borden. June 9, 1913, to June 18, 2004. Partner and wife of the late Peter Kornberg. Mother of Michael Borden (Raymond Klein) and Alan William. Friend and mentor to many. Funeral services private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Second Seder Fund of Temple Emanu-El, 1 E. 65th St, NY, NY 10021; or Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Rd, Bronx, NY 10461.
        KORNBERG--Selma Borden. Congregation Emanu-El, of the City of New York, expresses to the family of our devoted member, Selma Borden Kornberg, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y.
