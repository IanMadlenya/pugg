
        CARTER-Charles Abbott, III, age 54, passed away in N Y C, September 6th. Memorial Services will be held at 11a.m. Saturday, September 23 at Fifth Ave. Presbyterian Church, NYC. He is survived by parents, Mr.& Mrs. Charles Abbott Carter, Jr., of Kansas City; sister, Mrs. Elizabeth Gibby and brother, Stephen Carter of N Y. He was an insurance broker in N Y C.
