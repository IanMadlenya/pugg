
        ROBERTS-Dudley DeVore. On July 3, 2000, at the age of 90 at his home in East Hampton, NY. Mr. Roberts was founder and President of Cinerama Productions, which pioneered wide-screen movies. He also founded Roberts & Company, an investment banking firm which later merged with Kidder, Peabody. During World War II Mr. Roberts served in the FBI's Special Intelligence Unit which was created by J. Edgar Hoover to handle highly sensitive missions worldwide. Mr. Roberts was the second American to receive a commercial pilot's license. He also was a world-class sports fisherman & noted amateur golfer. He served as President of The Maidstone Club from 1957-1961. Mr. Roberts is survived by his wife Laura Serra Roberts, three stepchildren, six step-grandchildren and 7 nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held on July 29, at 10:00 AM at St. Luke's Episcopal Church in East Hampton.
