
        EVANS--Charles. On Novemeber 4, 2006, in NYC. Beloved husband of Elaine, beloved father of Hilary, Seth and Gideon, devoted fatherin-law of Sal and Jill, adored grandfather of Sara and Anna. A kind, gentle, witty soul, he will be very missed by everyone. Funeral Tuesday, November 7, 12:15 pm, at IJ Morris, 1895 Flatbush Ave. No flowers. Donations to AFSI, 1623 3rd Ave, NYC, or the Rusk Institute, Stroke Unit.
