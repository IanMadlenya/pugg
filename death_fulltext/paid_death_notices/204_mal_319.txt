
        HEIDELL--Robert Charles. 79, of Vero Beach, FL, died January 20, 2004, at the VNA Hospice House of Vero Beach, after a prolonged illness. He was born on October 19, 1924, in Brooklyn, NY, and had retired to Vero Beach in 1998, after residing in Bronxville, NY. He graduated from Union College and Albany Law School. Prior to retirement he was the senior partner in the law firm of Heidell, Pittoni, Murphy and Bach, NYC, specializing in the defense of physicians and health care facilities. He was a Fellow of the American College of Trail Lawyers, admitted in 1975. He served on the Board of Lawrence Hospital, Bronxville, NY, and was counsel to the Eastchester Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Eastchester, NY. He was a veteran of WWII, serving as a naval aviator trained in aircraft carrier landings, 1943 to 1946. He was a former member of the Bronxville Field Club and served as secretary of Siwanoy Country Club, Bronxville, NY. He also served on the Board of Governors of the Campfire Club of America, Chappaqua, NY. He was a member of the Community Church and Bent Pine Golf Club, Vero Beach, FL. Surviving are his wife of 33 years Valerie Meyer Summerlin, three step-children, John Summerlin of Westport, CT, Tina Summerlin of Brooklyn, NY, and Scott Summerlin of Newtown, CT, and six step-grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to VNA Hospice House of Indian River County, 1111 36th St, Vero Beach, FL 32960. A memorial service will be held at the Community Church on Thursday, February 5, 2004, at 2 pm. For information contact Indian River Cremation, Vero Beach.
