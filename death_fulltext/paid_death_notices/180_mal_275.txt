
        LAWRENCE-William Goadby, 88. A world renowned artist, died in Edgecomb, Maine on January 5, 2002. He was the youngest son of Robert Cutting Lawrence and Marguerite Lowe Lawrence. Bill's paintings are in the permanent collections of the Library of Congress, the Museum of Natural History, and the U.S. Coast Guard Academy Museum. He served in the United States Coast Guard in World War II and was awarded the Silver Star for Gallantry and Intrepidity in Action. Bill is survived by his wife of 42 years, Nathalie Brown Lawrence, his nephews, H. Lloyd Lawrence, Jr., Robert C. Lawrence, III, and nieces Genevieve L. Condos, Marguerite L. Lawrence, Grace L. Harding, Margo L. LaBau and Camilla Smith. Memorial contributions may be made to the Miles Health Care Center, Bristol Road, Damariscotta, ME 04543.
