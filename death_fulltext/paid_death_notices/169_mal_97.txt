
        RYAN-Daniel. At age 70. Retired Paper Handler for the New York Times from 1977 to 1997. Of Wayne, New Jersey. Formerly of Tournafulla, County Limerick, Ireland. Husband of Mary K. Ryan of Wayne. Loving father of Daniel J.A., John J. and Gina M. Daniel is predeceased by his daughter Regina, 1968. Dear brother of Michael, Sean, Timothy and the late Patrick, 1972. Funeral Mass Thursday, 10AM St. Joseph's Roman Catholic Church in Oradell. Interment is at Mary Rest Cemetery in Mahwah, N.J. Family will receive friends at Beaugard Funeral Home, 869 Kinderkamack Road, River Edge, New Jersey, Wednesday 2 to 4 and 7 to 9. In lieu of flowers, donations in Daniel's memory are requested to the Hospice of New Jersey, 400 Broad Acres, Bloomfield, N.J. 07003.
