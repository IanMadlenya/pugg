
        ROTH-Wendy Carol. On March 14, 2001 in Santa Barbara, CA. Adored daughter of Phyllis Roth Feigenbaum and the late Jerrold Roth; sister of Richard & Charlene Roth; niece of Enid Roth and wife of Michael Tompane. She was an innovator and a creator in her life and in her work. Her spirit will live on and we will love her always.
