
        FRIEDMAN-Chester Sydney. On June 26, 1998 in his 91st year. Loving husband of Rayminnie. Beloved sister of Gertrude Glaubman. Adored father of Joseph and Rita Aron. Beloved grandfather of Michael and David Friedman and Jonathan and Timothy Aron. Graveside service June 28th, 1998. Contributions to the Women's Auxiliary of Temple Emanu-El.
        FRIEDMAN - Chester Sydney. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member Chester Sydney Friedman, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy of his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        FRIEDMAN-Chester. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Bunny Barb, President Sivia Brodsky, Recording Secy
