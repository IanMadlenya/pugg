
        WALDMAN--Edwin, 89, on January 9th, 2004. Loving husband of Sylvia for 63 years. Devoted father of Robert and Ernest and father-in-law of Jennifer Dolan-Waldman and Alice Engel. Adoring grandfather of Joshua, Jack, Kate and Glenn. A retired salesman of women's sportswear, his true vocation was music. He wrote his first lyrics at age 17 and his last at 89, with recordings by such artists as Louis Armstrong and Harry Belafonte. He will be remembered by his friends and family as a kind, loving and intelligent person. Services Sunday, 10:15 AM, at Gutterman's, 175 North Long Beach Rd., Rockville Centre, NY.
