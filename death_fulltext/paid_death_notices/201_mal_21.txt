
        RIMALOVER--Jack K. Of Princeton, New Jersey and Haven Beach, New Jersey on October 2, 2003. Former Vice President of Lenox Hill Hospital. Survived by his wife of 56 years, Betty H. Rimalover, 3 daughters, Joan Gardiner of Bainbridge Island, WA, Anne Jorgensen of Haddonfield, New Jersey, and Elizabeth Raschbaum also of Haddonfield and seven grandchildren & brother, Harold Rimalover of Englishtown, New Jersey. A celebration of his life will be held in Princeton, New Jersey on Sunday, October 5, 2003.
