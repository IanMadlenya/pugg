
        PARKER-Doris J. Of the Upper West Side, Manhattan. Age 77. Died on January 17th, 2000. The Board, Foundation, staff and residents of Veritas Therapeutic Community, Inc., mourn the loss of our cherished friend and Foundation member. Her abiding belief in Veritas' work inspired her to establish, twelve years ago, the ''Annual evening with friends of Charlie Parker''. She will remain always in our memory.
        PARKER-Doris. A true friend, remembered with love. We will miss you. Sara E. Morgan, Terence McLeod Morgan and McLeod Families
