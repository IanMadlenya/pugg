
        GOLAD-Frank S. Of Astoria, New York, on Jan. 18, 2002, at the age of 77. Beloved husband of Norah. Dear father of Deborah and husband, Rudolph, Mark and wife, Ludmila. Cherished grandfather of Eric and his wife, Caroline. Frank, ''Mr. Almanac,'' was the longtime editor of the celebrated Almanac of the Hearst publication ''Sports Afield''. He was an artist, author, designer, collector of historical trivia and specialist on nature and outdoor related subjects. Memorial service will be held at Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, New Jersey, (201) 444-7650, Monday, January 21, 2002, at 12:00 noon. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Valley Hospital, Valley Hospice Program, 223 North Van Dien Avenue, Ridgewood, New Jersey 07450, (201)291-6300.
