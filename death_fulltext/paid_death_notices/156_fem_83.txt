
        ONLEY-Edna Fubler. Beloved mother of Arnold Ray Onley, Jacqueline Sams and Myra Johnson, mother-in-law of Albert Johnson and grandmother to Paul, Elizabeth and Marc Johnson, passed peacefully on Thursday, January 6, 2000. Edna lived these last ten years in Lake Mary, Florida with her son. She will be cremated and ashes taken to Bermuda at a time not yet determined.
