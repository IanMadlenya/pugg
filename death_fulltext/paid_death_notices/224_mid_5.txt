
        PARISER--Marilyn, September 6, 2005. Beloved wife of Dr. Sanford; loving mother of Howard and his wife Mary, Paul and his wife Erin, and Evan and his wife Nataly; proud grandmother of Andrew, Spencer, Sheldon, Ariel, Ali and Rachel. Beloved sister-in-law of Lenore and Marshall Lesser. She enjoyed a fulfulling life by her husband's side for 56 loving years. Her three sons were a source of great pride, and she was devoted to her grandchildren. She was a brilliant and strong and charitable woman who passed peacefully after defying the odds. She's at peace now. She was one of a kind, and will never be forgotten by her family and the many people who knew and loved her. In lieu of flowers, she would want memorials to be sent to Hadassah or the ASCO Foundation. Funeral Service Thursday 12 noon Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 E. Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, 914-381-1809. Interment New Montefiore.
