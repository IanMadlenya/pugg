
        WHITE-Rowenna L. Wight. May 17th, 1980. Interment on her 19th birthday. The Cathedral of St. John the Divine. Private. Called back September 2nd, 1998, Peggy's Cove, Nova Scotia. ''Loud from its rocky caverns, the deep-voiced neighboring ocean Speaks, and in accents disconsolate answers the wail of the forest.'' H.W. Longfellow Love always, Rows-Mother
