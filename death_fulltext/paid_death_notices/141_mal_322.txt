
        OBROW-Irving. The musicians and Board of Directors of the Orpheus Chamber Orchestra express great sorrow at the loss of our friend and former board member whose wisdom, kindness, and unfailing support helped to sustain our music-making for many years. We will remember him with affection, gratitude and respect. We extend our condolences to his wife Lucille and daughters Patricia White and Joanne Bailey. Orpheus Chamber Orchestra
