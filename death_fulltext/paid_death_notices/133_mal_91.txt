
        BATTELLE-Wayne. On February 5th, 1998, at age sixty-four. Artist and cabinet maker. Beloved husband and best friend of Monica, loving father of Burke and Nora. Also survived by his children, Tony Battelle and Leah Ferrarini. Graveside service at Green River Cemetery, Acabonac Road, Springs, East Hampton, New York, Sunday, February 8, at 1:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Karma Triana Dharmachakra Monastery, Mead Mountain Road, Woodstock, NY.
