
        TURCHIN--Les. The Officers and students of the Les Turchin Chabad House at Rutgers mourn the passing of our founder. Les Turchin, whose leadership role bringing Torah and Judaism to the college campus was recognized the world over. We extend condolences to his wife Sharyn, Todd, Tara and entire Mishpocho. Bernard Fox, Donald Hecht, Jeffrey Greenberg, Presidium Rabbi Yosef Carlebach
