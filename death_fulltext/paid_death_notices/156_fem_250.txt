
        JOSEPH-Morgan, 75, died on Tuesday, January 25, 2000. She was the widow of Richard Joseph. Morgan lived in New York City and Goshen, CT. She was a world traveler with her late husband who was the Travel Editor for Esquire Magazine. She owned Dick's Old Curiosity Shop in Goshen. She is survived by son, Richard and daughter, Jamieson as well as her three beloved dogs, Lucy, Lois and Madeline.
