
        MASIUS-Barbara A. Age 79, on January 30, 2002. Predeceased by her husband, William, December 23, 2001. Survived by her beloved daughter, Carol Zuckerman, sonin-law, Stuart Zuckerman, and granddaughter, Kate Zuckerman. Also survived by son, John, and grandchildren, Hannah, Max and Sam of Los Angeles. Private service. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association NYC Chapter, Attn: Development Dept., 360 Lexington Avenue, NY 10017.
        MASIUS-Barbara. The Board of Directors & members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed Golden member and past Board member Barbara Masius, whose many years of friendship, kindness and devotion to the club will always be remembered. We send our condolences to her children. Services are private. Beth Z. Post, President Roger H. Tulcin, Secretary
