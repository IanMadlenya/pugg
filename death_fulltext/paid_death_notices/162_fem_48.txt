
        KAZANOFF-Lillian. Died on July 7, peacefully in her 91st year. Widow of Sam, who died 29 years ago. Founding member of Temple Shalom, Glen Oaks. She is survived by her children, Regina Porter and Alan, her loving grandchildren, Peter, Loren and Samantha Porter. Services Sunday, 1 PM, at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel, Queens Boulevard at 76th Road, Forest Hills.
