
        GEANY--Helen (Dolan). Born Offaly, Ireland, April 18, 1903, died April 11, 2005. Sent to America as child on SS Mauritania 12 days after the sinking of the Titanic. Because of failed arrangements, spent a short time in a New York orphanage until adopted by her beloved aunt, Elizabeth O'Shea and her extraordinary husband Jack. Two of her children died in infancy. Survived by her devoted children, Eileen, Gerard and John, and John's wife Joan, eight grandchildren, 14 great-grandchildren. Burial at Gate of Heaven, Valhallah.
