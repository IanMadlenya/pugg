
        CREGLIA--Bruno. Passed August 20, 2006 of natural causes in his adopted home of North Carolina. Devoted husband of the late Elizabeth (nee Szucs). Son of the late Giordano and Anna Creglia, and dear brother of Lucio, former owners of Giordano Restaurant, NYC. Dear brother-inlaw to Elena Creglia. Beloved uncle of Anne Marie and Adrian Antonescu, Lillian and Angelo Quartuccia, Juliette Creglia, Jordan and Dari Creglia. Great-uncle of Marlena and Gabriella Quartuccia, Adam and Abigail Creglia and Adrian Antonescu. His wisdom and gentleness will be remembered. His death leaves a terrible void in the lives of his friends and family. Reposing at Thomas M. Quinn and Sons Funeral Home, 35-20 Broadway, Astoria, Queens, on Monday, August 28th, 2006, from 2 pm to 5 pm, and 7 pm to 9 pm. Funeral Mass to be held on Tuesday, August 29th, at 9:30 am, at Most Precious Blood Church, 32-52 36th St, Astoria, Queens. Burial at Saint Raymond's Cemetery, Our Lady of Hope Chapel, Bronx, NY.
