
        TUCK-Dr. Catherine Hudgins. Dr. Catherine Hudgins Tuck, co-founder and Associate Director of the Naomi Berrie Diabetes Center at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, died Thursday of breast cancer. She was 39. A Magna cum laude graduate of Princeton University, she attended the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School in Dallas, and was Chief Medical Resident in Internal Medicine at Columbia Presbyterian. A clinician and medical educator, she was appointed to the Faculty of Medicine at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. She is survived by her husband Edward H.A. Tuck and her son Hallam, age 8 both of New York and loving family and friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81St on Sunday, May 27, 5-7PM. Funeral services will be held Monday, May 28 at 4P.M. at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Catherine H. Tuck M.D. Teaching Space Fund at Columbia University, C/O Mindy Schreier at 212-304-5492.
