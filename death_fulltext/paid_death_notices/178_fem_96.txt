
        SLANER-Luella B. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of Mount Sinai School of Medicine mourn the passing of Luella B. Slaner, generous benefactress. Her outstanding philanthropy to Mount Sinai included the establishment of the Alfred P. Slaner Professorship in Psychiatry, and other important research support for Alzheimer's Disease. Mrs. Slaner will be remembered with gratitude for her thoughtful generosity. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her entire family. Stephen M. Peck, Chairman The Mount Sinai Medical Center Nathan Kase, Interim President and CEO, The Mount Sinai Medical Center and Interim Dean, Mount Sinai School of Medicine Barry R. Freedman, President and CEO, The Mount Sinai Hospital
        SLANER-Luella. The Board of Directors and Staff of Family Service of Westchester note with sadness the passing of Luella Slaner, whose lifelong service to community and family earned her FSW's 2001 Family of the Year Award. With leadership that was exemplary, her commitment to volunteerism & humanity remains an inspiration to us all. Thomas E. Sanders, President and CEO Douglas J. McClintock, Chairperson
