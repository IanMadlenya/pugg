
        JORDAN--Eileen (nee Murphy), of Rockville Centre and Breezy Point, entered into eternal life, at her home surrounded by her family on Easter Sunday, April 8th, 2007 in her 82nd year. Loving wife of 58 years to Jerry. Devoted mother of Richard (Evelyn), Dennis, Kevin, Reverend Brian Jordan (Saint Francis of Assisi Church, Manhattan), Gerard (Eileen), Eileen Mulcahy (Rory), and Maryann McVeigh (Dan). Cherished grandmother of 10 and greatgrandmother of two. Beloved sister of William Murphy and the late Joseph Murphy, and Marie Fochtman. The family will receive friends Thursday 2-4pm and 7-9pm at The Thomas A. Glynn & Son Funeral Home, 20 Lincoln Ave., Rockville Centre, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Friday 10am Saint Agnes Cathedral Rockville Centre, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations will be gratefully received by Saint Francis of Assisi Bread Line, 135 W. 31st St., New York, NY 10001. Funeral care and service entrusted to Christopher T. Jordan.
