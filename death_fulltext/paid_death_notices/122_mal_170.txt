
        GOODZEIT-David S., died suddenly on March 12. Surviving are his wife, Sylvia; daughters, Andrea and Margaret; brothers, Milton and Irving; and grandchildren, Jamie, Elizabeth and Steven. Memorial contributions may be sent to Congregation Hope of Israel, 843 Walton Ave., Bronx, NY 10451.
        GOODZEIT-David. Congregation Hope of Israel of the Bronx mourns the loss of its esteemed member, David Goodzeit, a gentleman and a scholar. We join in grief with his beloved wife, Sylvia, and daughters, Margaret and Andrea. May the Lord comfort them amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judge A. D. Levy, Pres. Howard A. Jaffe, Ch. of Bd.
