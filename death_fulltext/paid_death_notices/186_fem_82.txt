
        SMITH-Lily. On July 12, 99 years. Devoted wife, mother, grandmother, great-grandmother. She leaves her children Jerry Kaufman and Carol Goldberg, son-in-law Arthur Goldberg, grandchildren Mark, Penny, Beth and Josh and great-granddaughter Samantha. A charismatic and youthful woman, she will be missed by her many loving nieces and nephews and all those who came in contact with her. A violinist and performer in her youth, she was a lifelong lover of classical music. Funeral service Sunday, July 14, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. in lieu of flowers, a donation may be made to the Music Advancement Program at Juilliard.
