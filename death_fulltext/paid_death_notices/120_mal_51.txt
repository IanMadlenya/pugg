
        TURKEN - Stanley, on January 4, 1997. He died after a short but hard-fought battle with kidney cancer. Adoring husband of CeriAnn. Affectionate and cherished father of Greta and Jason. Loving brother of Jerry and Marvin. Treasured friend to many. His inate sensitivity for art and music, his love of children, and his disarming warmth and wit will never be forgotten.
