
        SCHENKEL--James M. of Vero Beach, FL, formerly of Garden City, NY, died on December 16, 2003. Born August 19, 1913, he graduated in 1934 from Colgate University class of 1934 and was a Naval Lieutenant during World War II. He retired in 1978 from Home Life Insurance Company in New York as Vice President and Chief Actuary, FSA, MAAA. Predeceased by his wife of 55 years, Elizabeth Johnston in 2001, he is survived by son James P. Schenkel, Pacifica, CA, daughters Mary E. Schenkel, Vero Beach, FL, and Margaret S. Heydorn, Westchester, NY, and granddaughters Elizabeth and Ruth Schenkel, Pacifica, CA.
