
        ROSENBLOOM--Esther. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Esther Rosenbloom, beloved grandmother of Keith Rosenbloom, member of UJA-Federation's Emerging Leaders and Philanthropists Strategic Council and the Investment Management Division's Executive Committee. His generosity enables us to enrich Jewish life around the world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Keith and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O
