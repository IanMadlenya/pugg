
        OSIPOW-SMITH-Helen, 86. Of Boca Raton, FL., on February 22, 1997. Beloved wife of Bernard Smith and the late Benjamin Osipow. Devoted mother to Nina Shilling and Joseph Osipow. She is also survived by grandchildren Elizabeth and Michael Shilling and Eric, Daniel, Alexandra & Arianna Osipow. She made a contribution to her family and society as a mother, wife and nurturer. She was cherished and will be missed by all who knew her. A memorial service and burial of her ashes will be held in the Spring.
