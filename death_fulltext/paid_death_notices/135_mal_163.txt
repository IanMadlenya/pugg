
        JUROW-Irving H. Died peacefully on Friday, April 10, 1998 at his home in Chevy Chase, MD., in his 93rd year. He is survived by his wife of 69 years, Mae Wechsler Jurow. He is also survived by his sister, Edna J. Hirst of New York City, his brother Martin Jurow of Dallas TX, four nieces: Ellin J. Hirst (Ella) of Oakland, CA, Jane Wechsler-Gurganus of Berkeley, CA, Erin Jurow-Eliscu of Dallas, TX, and Antonia Wechsler of New Orleans, LA, 2 nephews: Richard Wechsler of Los Angeles, CA, and David M. Hirst of New York City, and three grandnephews: Gabriel Marshank of New York City, Luke F. Gurganus of Berkeley, CA, and William G. Eliscu of Dallas, TX. There will be no memorial service. If you wish, contributions may be made to your favorite scholarship fund or to the Irving H. Jurow Scholarship fund at New York University, 25 West 4th Street, New York, New York 10012.
