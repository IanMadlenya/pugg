
        HOUK-Sally Homer. Of New York City, died November 16, 2000. Born September 8, 1910. A teacher at the Midtown Ethical Culture School until 1976, she ran a summer camp in the U.S. and abroad. Mother of the late Rudy, survived by sons Bruce of NYC and John of Vermont, six grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Her ashes are on the Atlantic today.
