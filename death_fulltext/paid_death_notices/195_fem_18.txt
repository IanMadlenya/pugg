
        DUDA-Janina (Nina). A nurturing, caring and selfless woman who enriched the lives of all who knew her. A survivor of the Warsaw uprising and a prisoner of war liberated by the U.S. Army. Devoted mother of Halina and maternal caretaker of the extended Robinowitz family including Stuart and Anne, their children and grandchildren for more than half a century. Visitation at Sleepy Hollow Cemetery Chapel, Rt. 9, Sleepy Hollow, NY on April 4 at 10 A.M., services at 11 A.M. Contributions may be made to: Holocaust Museum in Washington, D.C. or the White Plains Hospital Dickstein Cancer Center.
