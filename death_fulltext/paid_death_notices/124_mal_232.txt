
        STULMAN-Julius. The American Committee for the Weizmann Institute of Science mourns the passing of a generous philanthropist and expresses its heartfelt condolences to his son Stephen, longtime committed supporter of the Institute, distinguished member of the ACWIS Board of Directors, past treasurer and past president. Our sincerest sympathy goes to the entire family. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Haim Harari, President S. Donald Sussman, Chairman American Committee Robert G. Begam, President Fran Ginsburg, Exec. Director
