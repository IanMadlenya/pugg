
        EAGLE-Milton, M.D., 80, died peacefully in his sleep on September 30 in Jacksonville, Florida. Formerly of Jericho, Long Island. Beloved husband of Shirley, loving father of Terry and proud grandfather of Davis and Sarah Lyn. Loving big brother to Seymour. Doctor, healer. Funeral services will be held Sunday, 10 AM at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, Long Island.
