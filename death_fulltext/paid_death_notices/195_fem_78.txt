
        BLEIER-Jeanette G. Mrs. Richard M. Bleier, 85, of Chappaqua, NY, passed away at her home in Walnut Creek, CA, on April 11, following a long illness. Mrs. Bleier was a founding member and President of Temple Beth El in Chappaqua, NY, and a past President of District 3, New York State, of the National Federation of Temple Sisterhoods. Mrs. Bleier is survived by her sons Richard, Steven, Ralph and daughter-in-law Kathy, her grandchildren Kenneth (Mariah), William (Rifka), Kelly, Michael and Jason, and her great-grandson Moses Bleier. Her family deeply mourns her passing. Funeral will be held 2:30 PM, Sunday, April 13, at Temple Beth El, 220 South Bedford Rd, Chappaqua, NY 10514.
