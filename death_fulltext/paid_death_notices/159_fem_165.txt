
        HERLING-Eleanor (nee Brinkmann). On April 19, 2000. Wife of the late John P. Herling. She graduated with an A.B. from Barnard College in 1936 and B.S. from Columbia University School of Library Service, 1942. She also studied at the Universite de Paris, Institut d'Art et D'Archeologie, 1936, Ms. Herling was a Librarian with the New York Public Library, the New School for Social Research, State University College at Buffalo, Case Western Reserve University, Hofstra University, and the University of Houston. She was an active member and Officer of the Nassau Chapter of NOW. She is survived by five children, Ellen, Paul, Ralph, Carl, and Thomas, and three grandchildren, Lisa, Ryan, and Stephen. Contributions in her memory may be made to the New York Public Library or the Barnard College Fund.
