
        WOLF-Eric R. The Faculty, Staff and Students of The Graduate School and University Center of The City University of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Distinguished Professor Emeritus Eric R. Wolf, member of the doctoral faculty since 1971. Recipient of a MacArthur Foundation award, he was renowned for his pioneering work in the area of peasant studies and was instrumental in redefining the anthropological concept of culture. We offer our sincere condolences to his wife, Graduate School Professor Emerita, Sydel Silverman, and family. Memorial contributions may be made to the Eric R. Wolf Student Research Fund, Anthropology Program, CUNY Graduate School, 33 W. 42 Street, New York, New York 10036. Frances Degen Horowitz, Pres.
