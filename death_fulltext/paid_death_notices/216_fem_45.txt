
        WILSON--Ellen Mae (Young), 71, suddenly on January 3, 2005. Daughter of the late Robert and Lenora Matison Young. Beloved sister of Barry (Janet) Young. Aunt of Ross (Melinda) Young and Craig (Rebecca) Young. Great-aunt of Zachary Adam and Dylan Ross Young. Survived by her cousin Robert Young and her beloved dog Suzie. Service Sunday, January 9, 10AM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street at Amsterdam Ave, NYC. Donations in her memory may be made to the American Diabetes Association, or the Tsunami Save the Children Foundation.
