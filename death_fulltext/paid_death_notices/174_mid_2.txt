
        STAVROPOULOS-Nancy. On July 8, 2001, at age 74. Beloved wife of the late George. Loving mother of Peter and his wife Cally. Adoring grandmother of George. Dear brother of Peter and James. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, July 10, 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral services at Holy Trinity Greek Orthodox Cathedral, 337 East 74th Street, Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment to follow at New Cemetery, Somerville, NJ. Contributions in her memory may be made to the George Stavropoulos Academic Chair at Kent State University or St. Michael's Home for the Aged.
