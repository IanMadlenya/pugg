
        DWYER-John (Johnny) J. Of West Palm Beach, Florida, formerly of Manhattan, died on February 18, 2001. He was 85. Beloved husband of Mary Kinney Dwyer and the late Margaret ''Margie'' Cuddihy Dwyer, who passed away in 1974. Loving father of John and George. Cherished grandfather of Matthew, Andrew, Margaret, Kathryn and Courtney. Retired union official of the Seafarers International Union. Served in Special U.S. Naval Battalion, Chief Bos'n, from December 1942-November 1945. Friends may call Thursday 2-5 & 7-9:30 PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Avenue (at RR Plaza), Floral Park. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 10:30 AM at Our Lady of Victory R.C. Church. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
