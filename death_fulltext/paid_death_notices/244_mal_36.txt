
        LUMMIS--John M., Jr. died April 28, 2007 in Philadelphia. He is survived by his wife of 30 years, the former Boydena Wilson, his children, Max, Mary, Sheila, James, Benjamin and his brother, Ross. Also surviving are 11 grandchildren and his first wife, Sheila W. Lummis. He was predeceased by his parents, John and Anita, and his sisters Betty and Anne. Funeral service on Monday May 7th at the Chestnut Hill United Methodist Church, Philadelphia. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the church.
