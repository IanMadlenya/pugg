
        GAYNER-Dr. Irving C., Manhattan, formerly of Forest Hills, died on January 2 at age 87. Pre-deceased by his wife of 46 years, Esther Kasman Gayner, survived by his son Stephen, of New York City and Jay of San Francisco, and his grandchildren Samantha and Gillian. Practiced dentistry in East New York and Canarsie for 48 years. Served in the Second World War. A man of wit and wisdom, with a reverence for knowledge. Funeral services Tuesday 9:45AM at ''The Riverside'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
