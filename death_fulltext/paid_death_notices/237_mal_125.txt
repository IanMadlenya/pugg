
        DORFMAN--Irvin S. 82, died in Raleigh, NC on October 8. An internationally ranked tennis player and graduate of Yale, he served in the U.S. Navy in WWII and was a noted Broadway press agent. He is survived by his beloved wife Eileen, daughter Andrea, step-daughters Robin and Jody, sister Marcia and brother-in-law Irwin, niece Amy and four grandchildren.
