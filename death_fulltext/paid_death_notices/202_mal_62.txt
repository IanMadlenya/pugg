
        PETITT--Donald. Industrial designer, died in NY after a long illness. He is survived by his wife Sara and daughter Victoria. Petitt served in WWII. After attending the Institute of Design in Chicago, he worked with designers Harry Bertoia, Eero Saarinen and George Nelson. He then went on to design his own line of furniture for Knoll including the Petitt Chair, as well as major furniture collections for SunarHauserman, Thonet and Cadsana. His furniture has been in exhibitions at the Louvre, the Bauhaus archive in Berlin, and the Philadelphia Art Institute.
