
        SHOULSON--Sonia. The Yeshiva University and Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary family mourn the passing of the beloved mother of Bruce D. Shoulson, devoted member of our RIETS Board of Trustees. In honor of his parents, Bruce and his wife, Robyn, established the Rabbi I. Harry and Sonia Shoulson Seforim Fund at RIETS and the Rabbi I. Harry and Sonia Shoulson Scholarship for YU undergraduates. We extend heartfelt condolences to Bruce and Robyn, their three children, and their grandchildren. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Julius Berman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees
