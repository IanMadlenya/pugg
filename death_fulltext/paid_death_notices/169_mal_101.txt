
        AMES-George J. New York Medical College is saddened by the passing of our colleague and friend, George J. Ames. A member of the Board of Trustees since 1990, he recently was awarded the William Cullen Bryant Medal, our most prestigious honor recognizing individuals who demonstrate national leadership in health care, science, education, business or the arts. We will miss his counsel and his genuine interest in our welfare. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Rev. Msgr. Harry C. Barrett President and CEO Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D., Provost and Dean, School of Medicine
