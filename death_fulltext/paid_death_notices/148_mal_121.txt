
        KOPPLE-Alfred S. Textile executive, passed away on Sunday, May 9, 1999 at the age of 80. He was a highly decorated officer in the Air Force during World War II. He was greatly adored by his wife of 55 years, Marjorie, his daughter, Barbara and son, Peter. His grandchildren, Nicholas, Hilary and Sara will truly miss his love and friendship. His friends will miss his kindness, gentleness, sense of humor and his extraordinary companionship. Services will be held Wednesday, May 12, at 10 AM at the Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, Delray Beach, Florida.
        KOPPLE-Alfred. The Officers and Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of their dear friend and fellow member, Alfred Kopple. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Marjorie, to his children and other members of his family. Samuel G. Weinberg, President
