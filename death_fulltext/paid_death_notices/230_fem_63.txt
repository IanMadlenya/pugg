
        LEADER--Ruby Felt, 92. Wife of the late Dr. Sidney D. Leader, died peacefully on Sunday, March 5. Mother of Steven and his wife Barbara, and the late Aviva (Isobel) Kirsch. Devoted grandmother to Deborah Beth, Gabrielle, Jaclyn, and Allison, and great-grandmother to Genevieve Spring and Mason Trimbell. Her family and her community were the center of her life. She had an indomitable spirit and held us all to a high standard. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel (76th St. and Amsterdam), Tuesday, March 7, at 1:15. Memorial donations may be made to Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 West 83rd Street, NY, 10024 or to the Hertzberg Palliative Care Institute, Box 1070, Mount Sinai Hospital, NY 10029.
        $(6$)LEADER--Ruby. Congregation Rodeph Sholom and its Board of Trustees mourn the passing of our beloved and long time member, Ruby Leader, the widow of our esteemed, Honorary President For Life, Dr. Sidney D. Leader. Ruby served as a Vice President of our Congregation and was deeply involved in our educational life, in particular The Rodeph Sholom Day School. We will miss her elegance, wisdom and loyalty. We send our profound codolences to her children, Steven and Barbara, her grandchildren, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, DD, Senior Rabbi Nancy Solomon, President
        $(6$)LEADER--Ruby Felt. The Hunter College community notes with sorrow the passing of our esteemed and loyal alumna. Ruby's tireless commitment to her alma mater and her concern and support for our students will always be remembered. We extend condolences to her entire family. Jennifer J. Raab, President, and the Hunter College Community
