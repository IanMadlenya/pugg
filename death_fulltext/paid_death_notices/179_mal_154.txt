
        IGLER-Kirk A. Age 57. Of Starksboro, VT. Died unexpectedly Tuesday, December 11, 2001. He was born in Scranton, PA., on May 4, 1944, the son of the late Frederick and Viola (Kirkendall) Igler. Kirk was a graduate of Drew University in Madison, N.J. He was an antique dealer and appraiser working for many years with Sotheby's. Kirk's passion in life was foremost his granddaughter, Anna, his dogs and golf. He is survived by his wife Roberta Igler of Starksboro, his two sons and their wives, Keith and Susan Igler of New Providence, N.J., Matthew Igler and Missy Kelsen of Bristol, VT., his granddaughter Anna Igler, a brother Glenn Igler and his wife Suzanne of Georgia, his uncle William Kirkendall of PA. and his many friends. A memorial service will be held Friday, December 14, 2001 12 Noon in the Trinity Episcopal Church, 5171 Shelburne Rd., Shelburne, VT. In lieu of flowers, contributions in Kirk's memory may be made to the Starksboro First Response Department, Starksboro, VT. 05487 or to Mount Pleasant Animal Shelter, 194 Route 10, East Hanover, N.J. 07936. Inquiries to Ready Funeral Home, South Chapel, 261 Shelburne Road, Burlington, VT.
