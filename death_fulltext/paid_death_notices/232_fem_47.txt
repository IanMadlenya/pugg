
        THERING, S. Rose -The Board of Trustees of the Sister Rose Thering Endowment at Seton Hall University, South Orange, mourns the passing of our beloved friend, mentor and honoree, Sister Rose, on May 6, 2006. Her courage, perseverance and direction will be deeply missed by all whose lives she touched. Luna Kaufman, Chairman of the Board, David M. Bossman, Executive Director, Marilyn Zirl, Administrator.
