
        ALLEN-John Macnab, March 1, 2000. Loving husband of Mary Stanton Allen. Devoted father of Mana Allen Model & her husband Ben, William C. Allen and his wife Christina Sessa Allen, Joanna Allen Lodin and her husband Jeffrey. Cherished grandfather (''GP'') of Joseph, Christopher, Penn Lodin and Molly Model. He was a noted New York actor, author, director and playwright. He will be deeply missed by his family, friends and the New York theatrical community. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., Saturday, March 4th, 2-5 and 79PM, and Sunday 2-5PM. Funeral private. Memorial service to be announced at a later date.
