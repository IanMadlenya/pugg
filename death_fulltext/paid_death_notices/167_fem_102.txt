
        STRAUSS-Harriet (nee Joseph). Passed away December 9, 2000, at the age of 83. Beloved mother of Bernard, Arthur, Fran and Emanuel. Wife of the late Isidore. Loving grandmother of Samantha, Josh, Darin, Tracey, Jacob, Phillip and Sasha. Sister of Charlotte and Charles. A woman whose intelligence and understanding were admired by all. Harriet passed peacefully in Cranbury, NJ. Harriet was born in Brooklyn, NY, lived most of her life in Great Neck, NY, and the past six years in East Windsor, NJ. Burial Mount Hebron Cemetery, Queens. Joint Shiva will be held at the homes Arthur and Emmanuel. Harriet, we love you, sleep peacefully.
