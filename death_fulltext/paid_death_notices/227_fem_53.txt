
        SLOAN--Rebecca, 1935-2005, died peacefully with family at her side at her daughter Andrea's home on December 6th of cancer. Born April 18 in Garden City, Kansas, to the late Dorine and Leland Sloan, Rebecca was the second of four children. She was preceded in death by her sister, Sara Ann, and brother, Phillip. Rebecca is survived by her brother and sister-in-law, Douglas and Fern Sloan; sister-in-law, Jane Sloan; children & spouses, Stephen and Wendy, Andrea and John, Robert and Cheri; and grandchildren, Rosalee, Ian, Rebecca, and Tea. A lifelong artist and musician, Rebecca devoted the later years of her life to early childhood Waldorf education, culminating in her teaching for nearly a decade at the Rudolf Steiner School in New York City. A funeral service will be held at 4PM, Friday, December 9, 2005, at the Christian Community chapel, 309 West 74th Street, New York. Donations may be made to the Rudolf Steiner School.
