
        CLARK-Ruth. Beloved mother of Judy and Andy, cherished wife of the late Joseph Clark, devoted and remarkable grandmother of Harriet, Aaron, Daniel, and Joseph, caring mother-in-law to Cheryl Ogburn, second mother to Miriam Camitta and her family, and noted expert on public opinion polls and newspapers. Died on February 20, 1997. She founded and was President of Clark, Martire & Bartolomeo, and was formerly Executive Vice President of Daniel Yankelovich, as well as Louis Harris. She is already missed by her wide circle of close friends, business associates, and the many people whose lives she touched.
