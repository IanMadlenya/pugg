
        MEISNER-Sanford, Sunday, Feb. 2, 1997 at his home in Sherman Oaks, Calif., at the age of 91. The Board of Directors, the faculty, staff and students of the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre, together with the faculty, graduates and former students of 62 years, join with his family and friends in mourning the passing of their great mentor and teacher. The Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre will hold a tribute program to the memory of Sanford Meisner in the Sanctuary of Saint Peter's Church, 619 Lexington Avenue at 54 Street at 4PM, on Thursday, February 13, 1997.
        MEISNER-Sanford. Sandy, like my other legendary acting teacher Lee Strasberg, counseled us: observe, relate and interact with circumstances, with body language preceding, modifying the spoken word. Kenneth Lane, Wagnerian Heldentenor, Lake Hiawatha, New Jersey
