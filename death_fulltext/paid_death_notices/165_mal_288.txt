
        BRODY-Lowell. A longtime resident of Englewood, NJ, and recently of Southbury, CT, on October 20, 2000, at the age of 77. He was an architect and emeritus member of the AIA. He is survived by his wife Shirley, two sons David Brody of New York and Brooke Brody of West Virginia, a daughter Lesley Nelson of Maine, and three grandchildren. Services will be private.
