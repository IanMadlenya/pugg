
        CARON--Diana Welwood died unexpectedly on June 21st, 2006 at her home in Stuart, FL. Born in NY, Diana attended Ms. Hewitt's classes and Vassar College before moving to New Canaan with her young family. She was an active and engaged member of the community for over 30 years. Diana is survived by her loving daughter Sandra Oldershaw, devoted sons Peter John and Philip Caron, 5 grandchildren, her sister Helen Welwood-Gavales of NY and brother Thornton Marshall of Huntington, L.I. A family service was held in Stuart.
