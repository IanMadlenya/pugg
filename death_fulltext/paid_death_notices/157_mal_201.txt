
        LACHMAN-Terry Ross. Friends and family are deeply saddened by the unexpected death of Terry Lachman at Suburban Hospital in Bethesda, MD on February 15, 2000. He sustained a cervical fracture in an automobile accident on February 3 and developed complications. Mr. Lachman was originally from Chester, PA, and was 57 years old when he died. He taught physically handicapped children in the New York City public school system for more than 20 years, and retired to Bethesda several years ago. Funeral will be held at Gawler's Funeral Home, 5130 Wisconsin Ave. NW, Washington, DC on Saturday Feb. 19 at 3:00pm.
