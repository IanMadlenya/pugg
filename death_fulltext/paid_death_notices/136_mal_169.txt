
        BRIGGS-J. Kirk, 54. Of Bristol, RI and Truro MA., died Sunday, May 3, of cancer, at his home on Cape Cod. Surviving are his wife Louise Briggs, his sister Kearsley Monroe Briggs, his aunt Monica Brice Hobbs, and his cousin William Brice Hobbs. A memorial is planned for 12 Noon, Saturday, May 30th, at the Briggs home in Truro, Mass. The memorial will be followed by a reception. Donations may be made in Mr. Briggs' memory to the Dana Farber Cancer Institute. Gifts may be directed to DFCI, Gift Processing Office, 1309 Beacon St., Brookline, Mass. 02146. Information Devito Funeral Home, Watertown, MA.
