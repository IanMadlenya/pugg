
        THOMASES-Fred. Died Thursday August 10,2000 at the age of 92. Loving husband of Florence Josephson Thomases through 66 years of marriage. Adored father of Robert, Matthew, Jeffrey & Susan. Devoted grandfather of Holly, Andrew, Ken, Caroline, Josh, Ben, David, Danny, Greg, Lili, Phoebe, Shannon, Tom, Tony, Amy, Stephanie & Justine. Beloved brother of Jerry Thomases and Pearlie Gold. Lifelong benefactor, community leader, textile industry executive and internationlly known for his innovations. Trustee of Temple Emanu-El, Englewood, The New Jersey Community Center on the Palisades in Tenafly and National Board Member of American ORT. Dedicated to the founding and development of the State of Israel. Resident of Englewood, N.J. for 56 years. Services will be held on Friday, August 11 at 1 PM, at Temple Emanu-El on Tenafly Road in Englewood, N.J. For more information call ''Gutterman-Musicant'', 1-800-522-0588.
        THOMASES-Fred. We have been blessed knowing you all these years and having you as our friend. We will miss you. Valerie and Allen Hyman and Family.
