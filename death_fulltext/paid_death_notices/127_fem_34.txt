
        TISHMAN-Rose S. Devoted wife of the late Louis. Loving mother of Jerry A. of Westport, CT., and N.Y.C. Died peacefully in her 96th year on August 4. Mrs. Tishman was born on June 26, 1902 in N.Y.C. where she resided her entire life. During World War II, she was one of the leaders of the New York Division of the American Women's Volunteer Service. She was also a founding member and Secretary of the Lila Motley League which was a forerunner of volunteer cancer care organizations. Interment will be private.
