
        GANZ--Harold L. Died peacefully at home on April 15, 2004 at age 88. Beloved husband of Mimi. Cherished father of Charles (Carol), Bob (Benita), and the late Joan. Devoted grandfather of Jonathan, Adam, Jason, Melissa, and Josh. An attorney before World War II, he later worked in advertising. He served honorably as a Lt. Comdr in the U.S. Navy during the war. He touched the lives of all who came into contact with him.
