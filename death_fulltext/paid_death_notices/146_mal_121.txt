
        KATZ-William D., Ph.D., 83, of West Palm Beach, FL on Monday, March 1, 1999. He is survived by his wife of 38 years, Betty, his children, Alan E. Katz and Barbara Lynne, his daughter-in-law Laura Katz, his stepchildren, Ruth Wiener and Micky Goldfarb, his grandchildren, Danielle, Melissa, Oren, and Jessie. Dr. Katz was a Diplomate of the American Academy of Behavioral Medicine and the American Board of Medical Psychotheraphy, past Master, Freemasons; life member, Knights of Pythias; professor of psychology, Long Island Univ. A memorial service was held March 3, 1999 in West Palm Beach, FL and graveside services will be held Thursday, March 4, 1999 at 2:30PM at New Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. We shall miss you.
