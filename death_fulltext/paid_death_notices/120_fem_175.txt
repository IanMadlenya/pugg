
        GLANTZ-Rae Fishberg, 94, at home on Bay Harbor Islands, Fla. Widow of Harry; cherished mother of Geraldine, Janice and Lois. Beloved mother-in-law of Sidney Bergman, Robert Greenstein and Arthur Rosenfeld. Adored grandmother of the late Pamela, Susan, Ivy, Tod, Felicia, Carolyn and Sara. Loving great-grandmother of eleven. Devoted sister of Marte and aunt. A woman of incomparable grace, charm, intelligence and insight. She was a natural beauty whose character was as unforgettable as her face. A member of the Fishberg-Glantz musical family, her life was a symphony of great personal accomplishment, filled with warmth, love and serenity. She will be deeply missed. Services Sunday, 12:30 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. and 76th Street.
