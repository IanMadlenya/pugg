
        FERMAN-Symon. On April 25, 2003, in New York, after a battle with Parkinson's Disease. Darling husband of Eda. Beloved father of Gaby and Gary, and loving grandfather of Allison, Brian, Jonathan, Michael and Oliver. Symon gave to numerous philanthropic Jewish and medical causes and to the GIA of the diamond industry. He was a veteran of WWII. Graveside services Monday, April 28th, 1:30 PM, at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, Queens.
