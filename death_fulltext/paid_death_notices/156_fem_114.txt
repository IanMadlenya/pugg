
        MONTEGUT-Simone Ramstein, 77, born and raised in New York City, resident of Somers, New York for the past 10 years and prior to that of Yorktown Heights, NY, passed away on January 10, 2000 after a valiant battle with breast cancer. A Phi Beta Kappa, Magna Cum Laude graduate of Hunter College with a B.A. and a M.A. in French, Mrs. Montegut taught French and Latin in the Pleasantville and Chappaqua schools. She was a long time member of the North Star Quilters Guild and the Fifth Tuesday Quilters Group. Cherished wife of 56 years of Philippe, 92, and loving mother of Philippe Jr., Alain and Diane & six grandchildren. Calling hours at Cassidy-Flynn Funeral Home, Mt. Kisco, NY are Friday 35 PM and 7-9 PM. Funeral service Cassidy-Flynn Saturday at 10 AM. Interment at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, New York.
