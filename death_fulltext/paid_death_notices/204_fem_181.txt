
        O'DONOVAN -- Margaret J. On January 16, 2004 at age 95. Widow of the beloved Leo J. O'Donovan Jr. and adored, mother of Rev. Leo J. O'Donovan, S.J. Forever fond of cousins in Albany, N.Y., Boston, Ma, Cork City and Dublin, Ireland, Lake Forest, Il, Los Angeles, Ca, Madrid, Spain, Miami, Fl and Schenectady, N.Y.; Granma Bear to the Burdick family of Chicago, Il., Grandma Peg to the Harpers of Riverdale, N.Y., Aunt Peg to the Vaughans of Ridgewood, N.J. and honorary member of the McDermott family. A woman of boundless love and bold confidence in the goodness of God. Friends may call at The Abbey Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street Monday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 10AM at Corpus Christi Church, 529 W. 121 St. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Margaret J. and Leo J. O'Donovan, Jr. Scholarship at Georgetown University, Washington D.C. 20057.
