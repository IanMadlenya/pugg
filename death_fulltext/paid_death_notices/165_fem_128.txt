
        DENGROVE-Sylvia. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Sylvia Dengrove, a long-time friend and dedicated supporter of UJA-Federation and other Jewish causes. To her sons, Jay L. and H. Bruce, and to her entire family, we extend our deepest condolences. May all who benefited from her kindness be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair & President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
