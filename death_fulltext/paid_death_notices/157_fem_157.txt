
        EISENSTADT-Elaine. Loving wife of Martin. Devoted mother to her daughters Jane and Randi. Adoring grandmother of her grandson Will. Beloved daughter of the late Fannie and Lou Land. Sister of Mark Land. Cherished cousin, niece and friend. All will miss her caring and generous spirit.
