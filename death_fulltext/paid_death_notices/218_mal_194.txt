
        SCHMERKEN--Morton L. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our beloved Morton L. Schmerken. Devoted husband of Bernyce Roberts Schmerken. Loving father of Ivy and Larry Samuels, and Merritt and Bob Greene. Devoted grandfather of Jordan, Alexa, Olivia, Remy and Brandon. Brother of Stanley Schmerken and brother-inlaw of Stanley Roberts. Morton was a veteran of WWII, a graduate of New York University, a passionate, healthy chef and a former accountant. He always regaled his family and guests with his fine cooking and hospitality. He leaves a loving legacy of humor, good spirit and love of family. Memorial service 10 AM Thursday, March 17 in Manhasset at Sunset Chapel.
