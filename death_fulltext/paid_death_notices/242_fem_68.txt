
        KAMEN--Fay, peacefully on March 8, 2007, at the age of 92. Beloved wife of the late Ephraim. Loving mother of Barbara and Michael and mother-in-law of Sandy and Geoffrey. Devoted and cherished grandmother of Jeremy, Mathew, Jessica, Heather, Elise and Efrem. Blessed with 10 great-grandchildren. She was deeply involved with the Yeshiva of Flatbush from its early years. Life time member of Kadimah Group of Hadassah, and active member of the East Midwood Jewish Center for over 50 years. Services today 12:30 at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, Inc., 1283 Coney Island Avenue (Ave. J), Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the above organizations that Fay supported.
