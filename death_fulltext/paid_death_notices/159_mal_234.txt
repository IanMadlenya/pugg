
        WEINTRAUB-Jacob D. Died April 21, 2000. Survived by his loving wife Bronka Weintraub. A member of Temple Emanuel. Memorial service to be announced at a later date.
        WEINTRAUB-Jacob D. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Jacob D. Weintraub, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
