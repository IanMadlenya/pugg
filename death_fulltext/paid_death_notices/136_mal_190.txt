
        NORTON-George T. Of Atlanta, GA. passed away on May 12, 1998. Born in New York City on April 22, 1910, he attended Columbia University with a B.A. in 1932, New York Law School LLB in 1941. He is survived by four sons: George, Jr. of Birmingham, William of Atlanta, John of Reston, VA., Douglas of Bernardsville, NJ, 10 grandchildren & 2 great grandchildren. He was former Vice President and General Manager of Utilities Mutual Insurance, New York City and member of The Downtown Association. Memorial Service, May 30, 4PM at Grace Episcopal Church, Plainfield, New Jersey 07060. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Grace Church.
