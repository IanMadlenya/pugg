
        GERSHMAN-Richard K. On August 26 at age 69, Richard Gershman died in the New Milford, CT. Hospital of respiratory disease. Son of the late Isaac Gershman (former general manager of the City News Bureau of Chicago) and the late Agnhild Gershman. Richard was born in Winnetka, Illinois. Richard was a journalist for the City News Bureau and later was a public relations representative first for CBS and then for various other firms and govermental agencies. He is survived by the love of Ruth Carpenter of New Milford, Connecticut, the Gershman family of Stamford, Connecticut, and Riverside, California, and the Kennedy family of New Haven, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers, gifts in his memory should be made to the New Milford Hospital.
