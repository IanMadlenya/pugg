
        HECHT-Eleanor. The Board of Directors and staff of NY Psychotherapy and Counseling Center mourn the passing of Eleanor Hecht on November 30, 2006. Eleanor served for the past 15 years as a member of our Board of Directors. Her concerns for others and particularly the mentally ill was unparalleled. Our deepest sympathies to her family and friends at this difficult time. Seymour Brickman, President. Elliott Klein, Executive Director. Jack Schnee, M.D., Medical Director.
