
        MERSON-Leo. On May 8, 2001. Beloved husband of Paula. Loving father of Michael, Mark & Melissa. Dear brother of Hy. Adoring grandfather of Jonathan, Erica, Dana & Zachary. Service Thursday 1:00 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street & Amsterdam Avenue.
        MERSON-Leo. The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourns the passing of Leo Merson, who for many years served the Hebrew Home as a Member of the Board, generously giving his counsel and friendship. His contributions are warmly remembered and greatly appreciated. We extend heartfelt sympathies to his beloved wife, Paula, and their children, Michael, Mark and Melissa. The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec. VP
