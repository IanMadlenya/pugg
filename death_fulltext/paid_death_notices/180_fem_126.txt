
        COOPERMAN-Adelaide Kramer, 83. Of Margate, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, passed away January 9, 2002 in Forida. Adelaide was a founding teacher of Sheepshead Bay High School where she taught for 16 years. She is survived by three loving children, Lawrence, Frederic, and Nancy; five devoted grandchildren and five cherished great-grandchildren. Service Sunday 1PM at Union Field Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY.
