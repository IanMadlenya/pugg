
        LURIE--Linda. Linda died suddenly at home on December 4th. She is survived by her brother Steven, of Tucson Arizona, and her long time friend Alfred J. Talley. Beloved daughter of the late William and Dorothy Lurie of Hewlett New York. Service at Frank E. Campbell. 81st and Madison. Friday December 8th at 10:30am. Interment private. Contributions in her name to: Saint Vincent's Hospital. SVMC Foundation. 170 West 12th Street, New York, NY, 10011.
