
        HADLEY - BIBERMAN -- Su san J., M.D., for many years the highest ranking woman physician at Cornell Medical Center, died on December 27, 2003. She graduated from Cornell University Medical College in 1944. For the next forty years she served on the medical staff of New YorkCornell, rising to the position of Professor of Medicine and serving as Vice Chairman of the Department of Medicine. An esteemed physician, researcher and educator, she was at the forefront of advancements in therapy for pulmonary tuberculosis and in the development of Streptomycin and Isoniazid, treatments still widely used today. At the time of her passing, Dr. Hadley was a Professor Emeritus of Medicine and an Honorary Attending Physician. The Department of Medicine will remember the outstanding legacy of Dr. Hadley and acknowledges her passing with sadness. Ralph L. Nachman, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Weill Medical College of Cornell University Physician-in-Chief New York-Presbyterian Hospital
