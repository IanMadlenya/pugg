
        LINN--Nora H. In her 100th year died peacefully, October 15, 2006. Wife of the late Dr. Harry, mother of Victor and Judith, Robert and Ann, grandmother of Gregory and Lisa, Jason and Abby, great grandmother of JJ, Emily and Zachary. A woman of boundless intellectual enthusiasm devoted to learning and education, her fiercely independent life was blessed with feisty spirit and a love of music, literature and the arts. She will be missed by many.
