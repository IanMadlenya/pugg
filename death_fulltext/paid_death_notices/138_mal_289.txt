
        GREENBERG - Herbert. The board of directors, other volunteers, and staff of the United Hospital Fund are deeply saddened by the loss of our good friend and long-time supporter. We extend our sincere condolences to his beloved wife, our esteemed honorary director Mickey Greenberg, and the entire Greenberg family. Howard Smith Chairman James R. Tallon, Jr. President
