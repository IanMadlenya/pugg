
        HOFFMAN-Gerson. 85, formerly of Brooklyn and South Hempstead, NY, died November 27, 2002, in North Miami Beach, FL. Jerry was deeply loved by his wife of 62 years, Jean, daughter Susan and her husband Frank, son Ed and his wife Diana, grandchildren Devrie and Jared, and several nieces and nephews. Kind, smart, and funny, he was a true mensch. We are grateful he was part of our lives.
