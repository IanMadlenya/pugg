
        JOSEPH--Philip, M. 75. Died on May 2, 2007 in Bradenton FL where he recently retired from Larchmont, NY. Philip loved racing sailboats, traveling in Europe with friends and family members, and running his sheet metal factory in Brooklyn. He is survived by his loving wife Cynthia (Broadhead) of 36 years, four devoted daughters, their spouses, and five grandchildren. His daughters are: Karen Joseph of Queen Creek, AZ; Karen Selman of New Rochelle, NY, Deborah Roller of Tucson, AZ; and Jennifer Joseph of San Diego, CA. A Memorial Service will be held at Orienta Beach Club in Mamaroneck, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his honor to Tidewell Hospice Care, 800-959-4291.
