
        KAUFMAN-Raymond, PhD., physicist. 84, on September 25, 2001. Loving and devoted husband of Jeanne. Cherished role model for his children Binny, Judy and Elliot, Adina of blessed memory and David. ''Papa'' to his grandchildren Rachel, Harris, Jenny and Suzanne. Dear brother, brother-inlaw and proud uncle to many. An observant Orthodox Jew who was comfortable in the world of science as in the world of Torah. Funeral services Friday, September 28 at 11:15 am at The Riverside, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
