
        COHEN-Bernard. In his 89th year, August 14, 2000. Beloved husband of Etta for almost 60 years; devoted father of Steven (Janet) of Princeton, NJ; grandfather of Sherry and Jason, and brother of Morty. Formerly of Baldwin, NY, and Coconut Creek, FL. A member of the U.S. Army in World War II. A greatly respected childrenswear buyer and merchandise manager for Hearns, Mays, Mangels and Jefferson Stores. Service Wednesday, 11:30 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York City.
