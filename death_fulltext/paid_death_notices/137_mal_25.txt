
        DAVIS-Danny L. Son of Mrs. Adell Davis and the late Mr. Hosea Davis. Beloved husband of Rhonda K. Montgomery. Loving father of Allison and Carla. Born August 6, 1954 in Tillery, NC. Passed away on May 24th while jogging near his home in Morristown, NJ. Mr. Davis graduated from North Carolina A&T State University in 1976, and in 1995 attended the Wharton School's Strategic Planning and Management Program at the University of PA. Mr. Davis had been Senior Vice President of magazine sales for R.R. Donnelley & Sons Company. He served as a member of the Board of Advisors for New York University's Prism Award. He was a committee member on the Greater New York Boy Scouts Council and a member of the Board of Directors of the North Carolina Playwright's Center. He was also a member of the National Urban League and the NAACP. He touched many with his kindness and spirit, and he will be greatly missed.
