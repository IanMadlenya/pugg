
        TRUBEK-Max, MD. Well-known NY internist, died Saturday in Litchfield, CT. Dr. Trubek graduated from Johns Hopkins University in 1922 and from the University of Maryland School of Medicine in 1926. He completed his training at NY's Bellevue Hospital. For the next two years he was associated with the internationally known pathologist, Dr. Harrison Martland. After his association with Dr. Martland, he went into private medical practice in NYC for 63 years. He was known for his diagnostic ability and his empathy and concern for his patients. He was a Professor of Clinical Medicine at NYU School of Medicine. He was past President of the Society of Bellevue Alumni. He is survived by his daughter, Helen Trubek Glenn of Litchfield, two grandchildren, John Martin Glenn and Jennifer Glenn Wuerker. Funeral service Thursday, 11 AM, Litchfield. Burial Friday, 2 PM, at East Ridgelawn Cemetery, Clifton, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made in Dr. Trubek's name to NYU School of Medicine, 550 1st Ave., New York, New York 10016.
