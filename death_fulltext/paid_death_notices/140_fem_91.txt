
        COLEMAN-Emily Morris, 84, of Charlottesville died of lung cancer on September 13, 1998. She was a music and dance critic of Newsweek from 1942 to 1964; and also wrote for The New York Times, Life, Opera News and numerous other publications. Born in San Antonio, Texas, she received a B.A. from American University in Washington, D.C. and an M.A. in Constitutional Law. There are no immediate survivors. Memorial contributions may be made to The Nature Conservancy, 1233 Cedars Court, Charlottesville, Virginia 22903.
