
        HANNAWAY-Dorothy. Died on August 5, 2001. Born July 28, 1921, in Trenton, New Jersey, daughter of Carrie Hand Winter and James E. Winter. She graduated from Ursinus College in 1942 with a B.S. degree, and received her R.N. from Jefferson Hospital, Phila, PA in 1945. Dorothy is survived by her husband of 55 years, Warren, of Atco; and their two daughters Dorian and Carol of Los Angeles, CA. Dorothy's love and laughter survive in the many lives she touched in her 80 years.
