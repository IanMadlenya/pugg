
        WOODLE-Jessica Slocum. Of NYC and Nantucket, MA. On June 10, 1997. She was the daughter of the late Dr. and Mrs. C. Jonathan Slocum, widow of Joseph Cass Woodle, mother of Peter Cass Woodle, who predeceased her, and Joanne Slocum Woodle, MD. Grandmother of Gage and Alexandra Fairey. She is also survived by her brother Jonathan Slocum, MD. She was a member of The National Society of Colonial Dames, The Cosmopolitan Club, The City Garden Club of NYC, The Nantucket Garden Club, and The Nantucket Yacht Club. At her request there will be no memorial service. Contributions in her memory to The Nantucket Conservation Foundation would be appreciated in lieu of flowers.
