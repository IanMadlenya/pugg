
        ANASTOS--Rosemary Park. The trustees and officers of Connecticut College offer their heartfelt condolences to the family of President Emeritus Rosemary Park Anastos, who died April 17 in California. She served as a beloved President from 1947 to 1962 and was a leader in American higher education. Barbara Shattuck Kohn Chair, Board of Trustees Jean C. Tempel, Vice Chair Frank M. Turner, Vice Chair Norman Fainstein, President
