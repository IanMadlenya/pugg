
        GAULL-Sherry Heffernan, 67. Of Washington, DC, Wednesday, April 25, of cancer at Johns Hopkins Medical Center, Baltimore, MD. She was the beloved mother of Shelagh Ellman-Pearl, Tom Ellman, Barat Ellman, Kitsey Canaan and Nan Ellman and cherished grandmother of nine granddaughters. Mrs. Gaull was the widow of Dr. Gerald E. Gaull. She was educated at the Dominican Academy, Manhattanville College of the Sacred Heart, and the University of Maryland. She earned an MFA in painting as a Danforth Fellow from George Washington University in 1971. In later years she studied in the graduate painting program of the Art Institute of Chicago. In addition to her children and grandchildren, she is survived by two sisters, Emily Leonard and Shelagh O'Neill, and two stepsons, Erik and Stephen Gaull. She was predeceased by her sister Kay Towers. Friends may call at Joseph Gawler's Sons, 5130 Wisconsin Ave, NW, Washington, DC, on Monday, April 30 from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, will be offered Tuesday, May 1, at 10:30 AM, St. Ignatius Loyola Chapel at Holy Trinity Church, 36th and O St., NW, Washington, DC. Memorial gifts may be made to the Dominican Academy, 44 E. 68th Street, New York, NY; Friends of Acadia National Park, Mt. Desert, ME; or Friends of Oncology Nursing at Johns Hopkins Medical Center, Baltimore, MD.
