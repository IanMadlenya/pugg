
        MELNICK--Goldie. On December 5, 2005 in St. Petersburg, FL. Born May 31, 1925 in Liberty, New York, where she spent most of her life. Beloved teacher at the Rutherford Elementary School, Monticello, NY for 24 years. She leaves her son Andrew B. Melnick (Rae Koshetz) of NYC & daughters Cynthia Weisberg (Robert) & Stephanie Volk (Kenneth) of St. Petersburg, Florida, seven grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Graveside service at Ahavath Israel Cemetery, Liberty, NY on December 8, 2005, 2 PM.
