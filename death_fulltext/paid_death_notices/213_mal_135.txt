
        FOGARTY--James E., 90, died on September 26, 2004. Beloved husband of 63 years of Helen Cochrane Fogarty. Born in New York, he received his education at Xavier High School, Fordham University and Fordham Law School. He served for five years in the F.B.I. and was a member of the Society of Former Special Agents for over 50 years. In 1947 he joined the Reiss Corporation as in-house counsel and remained a director until his death. He was a member of The Union League Club, The University Club, The Association of Former Intelligence Officers, The Society of Friendly Sons of St. Patrick and various law associations. He is survived by his wife, Helen, a niece and two nephews and their families. A private funeral was held at Newport RI. There will be a memorial service at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue and 84th Street at 4:30 PM on Monday, November 8.
