
        KATZ--Rose. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rose Katz, beloved mother of Saul B. Katz, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Deeply committed to supporting Jewish and humanitarian causes, Saul exemplifies his mother's love of Judaism and strong family values. We offer our most sincere condolences to Saul, his wife, Iris, their children, and to the entire Katz family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
