
        WHITE--Anne Burpee, 71, of Atlantic Beach, FL died on January 28. She was the daughter of W. Atlee Burpee, Jr. and Jeannetta D. Burpee of Philadelphia, PA. She graduated from the Agnes Irwin School and Bryn Mawr College. She is survived by four children and seven grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM, Saturday, February 7 at The Church of the Redeemer, Bryn Mawr, PA. Memorial contributions can be made to The Jacksonville Humane Society.
