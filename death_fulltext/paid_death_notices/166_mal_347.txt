
        PEARL-Harold. Of Chicago, IL. World famous theater organist and icon of the Big Band Era, died on November 23, 2000 at age 92. Mr. Pearl is the son of the late Emil and Selma Perl. He is survived by two nephews; Gregory Pearl of Glenview, IL. and Steven Pearl, and family of Hinsdale, IL. For information Drake & Son Funeral Home, 773-561-6874.
