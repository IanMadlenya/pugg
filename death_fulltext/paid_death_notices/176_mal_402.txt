
        BORMAN-Cornelius H. Jr. and Eleanor M. Of Oakland, Maine. Cornelius, 86, died Wednesday, September 12, 2001 and Eleanor, 87, died Thursday, September 20, 2001. Neil served his country for a total of 23 years, retiring as a Lieutenant Colonel from the U.S. Army in 1956. He was an investment advisor for Rockefeller Family and Associates for 28 years, and was an active member in the Masons for over 50 years. Eleanor and Neil were married on Valentine's Day in 1942. Eleanor was a medical assistant for Dr. Irving Briller in Brooklyn, NY. Neil and Eleanor were both active members at Saint Mary the Virgin Episcopal Church in Chappaqua, NY. A memorial service will be held at the Garden of Maples, Kensico Cemetery in Valhalla, NY at 11:00 A.M. on Friday, September 28th. Memorial gifts may be made to the Maine Alzheimer's Association Research, 163 Lancaster Street, Suite 160B-Portland, ME. 04101.
