
        HEALY--Harold Harris, Jr. Husband of Elizabeth D. Healy, father of H. Harris Healy III, died March 4, at Mount Sinai Hospital. In addition to a long career as a partner of the firm of Debevoise & Plimpton, he served in the US Department of Justice, was Chairman and Treasurer of the Legal Aid Society, was a Trustee of Vassar College as well as the first American President of the Union Internationale des Avocats. For his efforts in support of the international practice of law, he was named Chevalier Legion d'Honneur de France. Further notice will be given as to a service at St. Jame's Church.
