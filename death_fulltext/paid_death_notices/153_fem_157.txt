
        HAIGHT-Yolanda. The Board of Directors and membership of the Junior League of Northern Westchester notes with sadness the untimely death of League member Yolanda Haight, and extend heartfelt sympathies to her husband Drew and two sons, Andrew and Aaron. Her commitment to community service, ebullient spirit and strength of character will continue to inspire. She will be greatly missed and always remembered. Mary Alice Hickey, President
