
        WILKINSON-Josephine (Colgate). Of Hightstown, NJ, formerly of West Orange, NJ, December 25, at age 89. Beloved wife of the late Lawrence Wilkinson, mother of Edith Allen of Arlington, MA and Russell Wilkinson of Princeton, New Jersey and New York City. Also survived by 3 grandchildren. Burial private; memorial service to be announced at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to YWCA of Essex and West Hudson, 395 Main Street, Orange, New Jersey 07050.
