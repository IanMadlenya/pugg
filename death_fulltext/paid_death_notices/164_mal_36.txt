
        EDMUNDS-Robert, M.D. Died peacefully September 18, 2000. Born September 14, 1924, Toledo, OH. Graduate Harvard College 1946 and Columbia Univ. College of Physicians and Surgeons 1951. He was a surgeon on the staff of St. Luke's Hospital for 25 years and served on the Faculty of Columbia P&S. He was a fellow of the American College of Surgeons, Member of the N. Y. Surgical Society and N. Y. Academy of Medicine and an Officer of the N. Y. Cancer Society. Subsequent to his private practice, he was a Medical Director for the U. S. Steel Corp. in Chicago and Cleveland. During WWII, he was an officer in the Navy and later served in the Army Medical Corps Reserve retiring as Colonel. He is survived by his five children Nancy Kalal, Priscilla Smith, Elizabeth Bennett, Cynthia Edmunds, Robert Edmunds and four grandchildren. Memorial service will be held on Thursday, September 28 at 4PM at St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital Center Chapel, 113 St. between Amsterdam Ave. and Morningside Dr, NYC. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the American Cancer Society, 19 West 56 Street, New York, 10019 Att: Donations.
        EDMUNDS-Robert, M.D. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center family joins with the physicians and staff in the Department of Surgery in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our friend and former colleague, Robert Edmunds, M.D., a member of our medical staff for 25 years. To his family and loved ones we extend our heartfelt condolences. Sigurd H. Ackerman, M.D. President and CEO J. Thomas Barnard, M.D. President, Medical Board
