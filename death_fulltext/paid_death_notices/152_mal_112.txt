
        WISNIEWSKI-Henry M., Dr. The Board of Directors, Members, Staff and Program Participants of AHRC New York City wish to express their condolences to Dr. Krystyna Wisniewski on the passing of her husband, Dr. Henry M. Wisniewski, Director of the Institute for Basic Research in Developmental Disabilities. Dr. Henry Wisniewski was, with his wife Krystyna, the recipient of AHRC's Humanitarian Award in 1984 for his compassionate and pioneering work on behalf of people with developmental disabilities. He will be deeply missed by his colleagues, family members, and people with developmental disabilities. Al Agovino, President, AHRC Michael Goldfarb Executive Director, AHRC
