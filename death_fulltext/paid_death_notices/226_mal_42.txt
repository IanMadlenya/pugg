
        SAXE--Irving Henry, M.D., 95. Of New York City and Rumson, NJ, died Monday, November 1, at home in NYC. He was predeceased by his wife, Patricia Saxe and a daughter, Nancy Saxe, M.D. Surviving are a son, Richard Saxe of Fair Haven, NJ, two daughters, Kathryn Meredith and Virginia Critton, both of Rumson, eight grandchildren and 10 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 12 noon, Sunday, November 6, at Navesink Country Club, Middletown, NJ.
