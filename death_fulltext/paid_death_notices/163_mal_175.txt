
        MURPHY-Richard G. On August 13th, 2000. He was 83. Born in Brooklyn, New York, he had been a resident of Larchmont since 1919 and also attended Fordham School of Business. Mr. Murphy worked in the municipal bond business starting in 1936 with B.J. Van Ingen & Company until his retirement in 1985 from Shearson American Express. He was the beloved husband of the late Jean (nee Hanson) and is survived by eleven children and thirteen grandchildren. A Memorial Mass will be held at St. Augustine's Church in Larchmont on Saturday, August 19, 2000 at 11AM. A reception will follow. Coxe & Craziano Funeral Home, 914-698-5968.
