
        HART-Jane S. November 1, 1999, age 86. Long time resident of New Rochelle, NY. Graduate of Juilliard School and Teachers College. Musician, teacher and composer. Predeceased by her husband Leslie A. Hart. Survived by children Sara and Richard Olson, David Hart and Diane Bentley, grandchildren Daniel and Elizabeth Olson, Alex and Lily Hart, sister Amelia Taylor, brother Lewis Smith. A long and healthy life filled with music, loving friends and family. Memorial service Sunday November 14th, 3pm at Community Unitarian Church, 468 Rosedale Ave, White Plains, New York.
