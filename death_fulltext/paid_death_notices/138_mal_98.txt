
        FROMM-Alfred. The American Friends of The Hebrew University of Jerusalem mourn with sorrow the passing of Alfred Fromm, a noted philanthropist and generous supporter of The Hebrew University since 1977. He and his wife, Hanna, sponsored numerous student scholarships and founded the Fromm Institute for Lifelong Learning at The Hebrew University based on the program at the University of San Francisco. We extend our deepest condolences to his entire family. The American Friends of The Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs, President Fred S. Lafer, Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan Executive Vice President
