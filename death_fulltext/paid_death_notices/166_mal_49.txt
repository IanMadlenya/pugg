
        KEATS-William Perry. Of East Hampton, LI, a lawyer, died peacefully at home on Friday, November 3. He was 83. Memorial services will be held at 11 AM, Saturday, November 11, St. Luke's Episcopal Church, East Hampton, NY. He wil be cremated. Perry was born on June 20, 1917, in Brooklyn, NY. Graduate of Harvard University (1939-major: Government and Romance Languages) and Harvard Business School (MBA, 1941). Saw active duty as Lt. Commander of a mine sweeper in the Pacific, retiring as Commander, USNR. Attended Stanford Law School (1949-1951), subsequently New York University (J.D., 1952). Corporate Counsel to Pepsico, Inc., then principal stockholder, director and general counsel, Monsieur Henri Wines, Ltd., White Plains, New York. After retiring in 1981, Perry remained active in the Episcopal Church, The Nature Conservancy and the East Hampton Historical Society. He was a member of the American Bar Association, the Democratic Party and the Harvard Club. Survived by his wife, Ida, his daughter, Catherine L. Roach of Edwards, Colorado, his son, William David of Ljubljana, Slovenia, and two grandchildren, Taylor and Travis Roach. Contributions to Perry's memory may be made to the Unitarian Church, P.O. Box 997, Watermill, NY 11947 or East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978.
