
        KELLNER-Zira DeFries, M.D. On Thursday, March 28, 2002, of lung cancer in Palm Beach, FL. A 1942 graduate of New York Medical College, she practiced psychiatry in New York for more than 50 years. For much of that time she directed the counseling service at Barnard College. A lifelong resident of New York City, from 1953 on she spent her summers on Shelter Island, NY. She is survived by her sons David, William and Charles Kellner, her grandchildren Daniel, Sarah and David Kellner, and her brother Myron DeFries. Donations can be made to Doctors Without Borders U.S.A., PO Box 2247, NY, NY 10116-2247; or to the Hospice of Palm Beach County Inc, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
