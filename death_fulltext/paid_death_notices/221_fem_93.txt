
        TURNER--Margaret, 98, died on June 7, 2005 at her home in Chevy Chase, Md. Formerly a resident of Brooklyn, she was an active member of Nazarene Congregational Church, the Brooklyn Girl Friends, and the Brooklyn Botanic Garden. She was married to the late Raymond W. Turner, and is survived by her children, Raymond Turner, M.D., of Washington, D.C., Clyde Turner, M.D., and Judy Turner Jones of Brooklyn, N.Y.; six grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren. Memorial services will be announced at a later date.
