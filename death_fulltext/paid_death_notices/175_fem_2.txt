
        GLICKMAN-Emily. Age 70, of Cranbury, N.J. passed away Monday, July 30, 2001 at her home. Born in Manhattan, N.Y., she resided in Cranbury since 1983. Mrs. Glickman was an active facilitator with the Child Assault Prevention Program, Middlesex County Chapter. She visited schools and presented programs for the past 15 years. She is survived by her husband Sheldon Glickman of Cranbury, two sons and one daughterin-law Leo Glickman of Brooklyn, N.Y. and Valiant C. and Kathleen M. Glickman of Manhattan, N.Y. and one brother P. W. Valle of Bronx, N.Y. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, August 2nd at 2PM at A. S. Cole Funeral Home 22 North Main St., Cranbury, N.J. Interment will follow in Princeton Memorial Park, Robbinsville, N.J. Relatives and friends may call Wednesday, August 1 from 2-4 and 7-9PM at the Funeral Home.
