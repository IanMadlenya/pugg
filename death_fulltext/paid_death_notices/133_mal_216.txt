
        ELKINS-Michael J. At age 35 in a diving accident in W. Palm Beach, FL on January 12, 1998. Beloved husband of Futaba. Cherished son of Barbara and Arthur Elkins. Devoted brother of Steven Elkins. A graduate of the Northfield Mt. Hermon School, Hamilton College and Cornell University, at the time of his death he was Vice President, Global Investments at Bankers Trust Company, New York. Donations may be made to the Michael J. Elkins Scholarship Fund at the Northfield Mt. Hermon School, Revell Hall, 206 Main Street, Northfield, MA. 01360.
