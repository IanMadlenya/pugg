
        LAMBERT-Evelyn. 81, died June 9, 1999, in Ojai, CA. Formerly of Eastchester, NY, Mrs. Lambert moved to Ojai in 1980. Former coowner/director of Kezar Lake Camp, in Maine. A world traveler, and active community volunteer, Mrs. Lambert is remembered with love by her sons Jonathan and Howard, four grandchildren and brother Howard Lewis.
