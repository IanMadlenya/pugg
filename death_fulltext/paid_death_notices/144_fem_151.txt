
        GOSULE-Helen (Chodos), died peacefully on Wednesday, January 13, 1999, after a valiant fight with leukemia. Adored wife of Alan Gosule, beloved mother and best friend of Amanda Tarter, cherished daughter of Ruth Chodos and the late Irving, loving sister of Eileen Chodos Beth. The beauty of her spirit and soul lives on through her family and friends. She will be remembered as a woman of true dignity and courage. In lieu of flowers, donations in Helen's memory may be made to UJA or The Leukemia Society. Services Friday, January 15th, 12:30PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue, NYC.
