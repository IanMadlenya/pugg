
        DEL TORO--Vincent, 82, Electrical Engineer and Emeritus Professor of City College of New York died on Wednesday, July 5, in New Jersey. He was a devoted teacher and prolific author. His teaching honors included three Educator of the Year awards and he authored 10 books on electrical engineering including the best selling textbooks, ''Principles of Electrical Engineering'' and ''Electrical Engineering Fundamentals.'' He graduated from CCNY and Brooklyn Polytechnic University. Survivors include his wife of 41 years, Franca, and two sons, Marc and Peter. Memorial donations may be made in his name to the City College 21st Century Foundation, Attn:Rosemary Weiss, City College of New York, 160 Convent Avenue, S-154, New York, NY 10031.
