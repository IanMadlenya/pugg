
        SCHUMACHER-Claire E. Age 83, of Tyler Hill, PA., and New York City, died Sunday, February 17, 2002 at King James Care Center of Middletown. She was employed by the New York Public Library as a librarian for many years. Her surviving nieces and nephews mourn her passing. Cremation services will be private. The family asks that expressions of sympathy be sent to the New York Public Library.
