
        DUTCHIN--Desmond, died on August 30, 2004, at age 71, at his home in East Elmhurst, Queens. Beloved husband of Eunice Dutchin, father of Vanessa, Desmond Jr., and Darren. Grandfather of Kaitlyn and Jennifer. Brother of Lucious, Winston, Oliver, Emelda, Martin, Rhona and Salomon. Father-in-law of Yonette and Yolin Dutchin. Dear uncle, cousin and friend to so many. His sweet and loving presence, his modesty, kindness and generosity will be terribly missed. A mass of Christian burial was celebrated Saturday, September 4, 2004. Donations in his memory to The American Heart Association are welcome.
