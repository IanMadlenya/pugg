
        HOPPIN--May S., 98 of Westwood, MA died peacefully on October 8, 2006. Wife of the late Frederic G. Hoppin. She is survived by three sons, Thomas B. Hoppin of Washington, DC, Frederic G. Hoppin Jr. of Brookline, MA and Charles S. Hoppin of New York City, as well as eight grandchildren, two grandchildren by marriage and nine great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 3PM on October 28 at Church of the Covenant, 67 Newbury St., Boston, MA. In lieu of flowers, please send a contribution in memory of May Hoppin to your own favorite charity.
