
        BERNSTEIN--Sidney I. On December 18, 2004, at the age of 90. Beloved husband of Anne for nearly 63 years. Loving father of Andi Salsberg, Karyn Klein and Barry Bernstein. Devoted father-inlaw of Richard Salsberg and Dr. Allan Klein. Adored grandfather of Gregg, Brian, Kerry, Corey, Brett and Jamie. Cherished great-grandfather of Samantha and Ari. He will be dearly missed by all who knew him. Donations may be made to U.S. Holocaust Museum, PO Box 90988, Washington, DC 20090-0988.
