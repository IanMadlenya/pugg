
        LEVAVY-Berenice B. On May 14, 1999. For over 63 years she and her husband Zvi, who survives her, set standards of marital harmony and compatability. Devoted sister of the late Gladys Bardin, beloved mother of Bardin (and Sue-Ellen), adoring grandmother of Raphael and Sara Beth. She was a community leader of Perth Amboy, NJ for over half a century, and both teacher and student throughout her life. Loved, respected-and now missed-by all who knew her. Services Monday, Flynn & Son Funeral Home, Perth Amboy, NJ.
