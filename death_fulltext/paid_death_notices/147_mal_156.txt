
        Siegel-Paul. Was born June 7, 1912, in Brooklyn, New York and died April 18, 1999 in Boca Raton Florida, surrounded by friends and family. He is survived by his wife, Mrs. Mae Siegel; his son and daughter-in-law, Mr. S. Jay and Joan Siegel; his daughter, Dr. Irene S. Kessler; his grandchildren, Mr. Ted Kessler, Esq., Mr. Michael Kessler, Ms. Ronnie Kessler, Mr. Spencer B. Siegel, Esq., Mr. Mark Siegel and Ms. Heather Siegel and his great-grandchildren Mr. Richard Kessler, Miss Sarah Kessler, Mr. Matthew Kessler and Miss Shana Kessler. He will be greatly missed by all. Mr. Siegel was best known for his successful antique dealership, Paul Siegel Antiques, Inc. which was formerly located at 808 Broadway in Manhattan, New York. The family wishes to acknowledge and thank all of his friends and colleagues who contributed to his life-long success and happiness. Services will be held at 11:00 AM on April 20, 1999, at Temple Beth El in Boca Raton, Florida. The family will welcome friends at 10233 Spyglass Way Boca Raton, Florida following the service.
