
        BALDINI-Robert A., 76, of Short Hills, New Jersey, a company executive, died August 1 in Overlook Hospital, Summit. Mr. Baldini was the president of R.A. Baldini and Co., a wax importer firm in Millburn, New Jersey from 1952 to 1988, when he retired. He was a 1942 graduate of Princeton University, with a Bachelor of Arts degree. Mr. Baldini served in the army during World War II. He was a former member of the Maplewood Country Club. Born in Pleasantville, New York, he lived in Maplewood before moving to Short Hills 21 years ago. Surviving are his wife, Bernice, a daughter, Mrs. Jan Bohn, and three grandchildren.
