
        SCHONBRUNN-Gertrude Goodkind. Formerly of Scarsdale, New York, died on November 29, 1999 after 89 wonderful years of life. She really lived the life prescribed by her Ethical Culture schooling from kindergarten through Fieldston. The first volunteer of The Children's Place. The wife of Albert Schonbrunn, former president of Savarin Coffee. Loving mother of Robert Schonbrunn and Marion Logan and adoring grandmother of Jen Hinkle, Joel Schonbrunn, Sam, Laura and Deborah Nadel. In lieu of flowers send contributions to The Children's Place, 2309 Ponce de Leon Avenue, West Palm Beach, Florida 33407. A private memorial service will be held at a later date.
