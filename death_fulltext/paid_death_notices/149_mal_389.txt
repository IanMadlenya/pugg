
        LEVAVY-Zvi. With heads bowed, we are deeply saddened by the loss of our friend, Zvi Levavy. President of the Morris J. and Betty Kaplun Foundation, Mr. Levavy was an ardent supporter of The Open University of Israel. Heartfelt condolences to his family. May his memory be for a blessing. Irving M. Rosenbaum, Chairman Ingeborg Rennert, NY Chair Eric G. Heffler, Executive VP American Friends of The Open University of Israel
