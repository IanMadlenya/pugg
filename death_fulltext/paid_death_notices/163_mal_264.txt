
        CARDO-Vito A. Sr., Esq. It has pleased God to call to Himself our dear father, grandfather and friend on August 21, 2000, age 92. An honored member of the New York State Bar Association for over fifty years, dedicated 50+ years of voluntary service to the Providence Rest Foundation and represented his beloved parish St. Raymond's in the Bronx for his entire professional career. He was admired for his dedication, loyalty and professionalism by all the lives he touched, he was a true Bronxite. Beloved father of Vito and Richard, father-in-law to Alice and Patricia. ''Poppy'' was loved and adored by his grandchildren, Alice, Vito III, Michael, Elena, Clare, Marianne, Richard Jr. and James. Donations preferred to Providence Rest Foundation, 714 Yonkers Ave, Yonkers. Visitation Thursday 2-4 and 7-9PM. Mass Friday 10:45 St. Raymond's Church, Bronx. Interment St. Raymond's.
