
        YAVERBAUM-Arnold. MD. The Trustees, physicians and staff of Beth Israel Medical Center (BI) and St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center (SLR) join with the Board of Trustees and administration of their parent corporation, Greater Metropolitan Health Systems (GMHS), Inc., in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Arnold Yaverbaum, MD, beloved fatherin-law of our dear colleague, Kathryn Meyer, Esq., Senior Vice President and General Counsel for BI and SLR. Dr. Yaverbaum served as a member of the medical staff at BI's Kings Highway Division for over forty years. To Kathy, her husband Harvey, her mother-inlaw Justine, and the entire Yaverbaum family we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees, GMHS Lawrence S. Huntington, Vice Chairman, GMHS Robert G. Newman, MD, President and CEO, GMHS
