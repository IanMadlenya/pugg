
        RAPHAEL--Jacob H. Husband of the late Lillian. Loving and Devoted father of Deborah and Myron Mallen, Alan and Susan Raphael, Dr. Irving and Ava Raphael and Judith and Henry Shrager. Adoring grandfather of twelve, great-grandfather of three. Jacob Raphael was the Founder and first Principal of Thomas Edison Vocational High School 1950-1964, and Principal of the High School of Art and Design 1964-1972. Mr. Raphael was an assistant to the Board of Examiners, NYC and Adjunct Assistant Professor at Brooklyn College. He was also active as a consultant to the New York Federation of Reform Synagogues 1942-1976, during which time he founded 38 Religious Schools in 38 new synagogues in Queens, Nassau and Suffolk Counties. Jacob was Past President of Hollis Hills Jewish Center. Service Sunday, August 3, 11:15 AM at ''Sinai Chapels'', 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY. >>AD#
