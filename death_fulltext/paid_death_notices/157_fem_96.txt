
        BERLOWE-Phyllis H.. Died peacefully on February 9, 2000 at the age of 77. Devoted and loving aunt, great aunt and great-great aunt, and highly respected and well-known public relations executive. Will be deeply mourned by her family and friends. A private family service will be held on Saturday, February 12. Contributions in Ms. Berlowe's memory may be made to The Humane Society.
