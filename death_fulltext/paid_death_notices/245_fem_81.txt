
        BLECHER--Wilma S. (nee Tashlick), January 21, 1915 June 11, 2007. Dearly beloved wife of the late Milton; adored mother of Susan; sonin-law Bill, and Jane; cherished sister of Lillian (Leonard) and the late Freda; dearly loved daughter of the late Sarah and Morris; deeply loved aunt and cousin. Her life was rich in friends, music, art, travel and study. How we shall miss her!
