
        PIAZZA-Dr. Louis R., DDS beloved Husband, Father and Grandfather, died on Monday, September 8, 1997 at Bridgeport Hospital. He was born in Bronx, NY on November 27, 1916. Louis left a legacy of caring and compassion to all he treated in his dental practice for fifty years. He was a member of the First District Dental Society of NY and served on the staff of Our Lady of Mercy Dental Clinic for most of his career. A POW in W.W. II, he was a member of the 106th Infantry 423 Division of the U.S. Army. An exceptional and respected man, he will be missed by his family and friends. A Mass of Christian Burial at St. Francis of Assisi Church, Norfield Road, Weston, CT at 11:00 AM. Friends may call on Thursday from 3-9 PM at the Shaughnessey Brothers Funeral Home, 50 Reef Road, Fairfield, CT. Interment in Arlington National Cemetery, Arlington, VA. Donations may be made in his memory to: Oglethorpe University, 4484 Peach Tree NE, Atlanta, GA. 30319.
