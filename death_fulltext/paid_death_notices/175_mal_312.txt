
        BAITZ-Edward H. Born November 30, 1922 in Baltimore, MD. Raised there and in Washington, DC; passed away August 30, 2001 in Los Angeles after a lengthy illness. Beloved husband of Sybil; loving father of Jeff, Rick (Penny) and Robbie (Joe); cherished grandfather of Joshy; dear brother of Esther Zisman, Bea Zuckerman and Micky (Joe) Hamer. Mr Baitz was a retired Executive Vice President of Carnation International and an avid pianist and lover of music and theatre. Services 4PM Sunday, September 2, 2001 at Mount Sinai Memorial Parks-Hollywood Hills, CA. For information, contact Mount Sinai Mortuaries. In lieu of flowers, family prefers donations in his memory to the USC/ Norris Cancer Center, 1441 Eastlake Avenue, Los Angeles, CA 90033-0800.
