
        RYAN-Edward Cowman On August 14, 1998. Assistant Manager of Security at the Metropolitan Museum of Art and retired detective Sergeant NYCPD. Born in New York City, October 1, 1931, a graduate of Rice High School, he served with the USMC during the Korean War. Survived by son Edward, NYCPD, daughters, Deborah Finiello of Cross River, NY, Alice Bruno of Yonkers, NY, Jane Smith of NYC and Colette Buzzetta of Shoram, NY. Grandchildren Marissa, Philip, Megan, Andrew, Carly, James and Daniel. Brothers Alfred of Phoenix, AZ, Dermot of NYC, Kenneth of LaJolla, CA, and Padraic of New Rochelle, NY, sisters Colleen of Pleasantville, NY, Elizabeth Skelly of Cocoa Beach, FL, Aileen Earnest of San Francisco, CA, and Judith of Riverdale, NY, numerous nieces and nephews and a host of relatives and friends here and abroad. Predeceased by his father Edward of Ballygomen Wexford, Ireland and NYC, mother Aileen, (nee O'Riordan) of Killarney, County Kerry, Ireland and NYC, brother Army Captain Lawrence B. and sister Barbara Ann. He will be greatly missed by all who knew and loved him. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Monday and Tuesday, 1-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave. at 84 St., 10 AM. Wednesday, August 19, 1998. Interment to follow in family plot at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
