
        BLACKMAN-Samuel W. Professor of Organic Chemistry at Yeshiva University, Research Chemist at Hoffman LaRoche and Burroughs Wellcome (now GlaxoWellcome). B.A. Cornell University, Ph.D. Polytechnic University. His father, Jacob, founded Premo Pharmaceutical Laboratories, the first full item generic company in the U.S. Leaves devoted wife of 60 years, Clara, loving daughters Susan Carle, Elinor Renfield Schwartz, Joan Weaving; brother Joseph; four grandchildren David, Jon, Michael and Adam, and one great grandson Max.
