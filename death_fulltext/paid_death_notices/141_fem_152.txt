
        ROSENTHAL-Anny nee Arnstein., age 77 of Bayside, New York passed away early morning October 15 at Calvary Hospital. She is survived by husband Ernest of 49 years, son Dr. Allan Rosenthal and wife Cheryl and granddaughters Dana and Erica of Ridgefield, Connecticut and son Jeff Rosenthal of Forest Hills, Queens. As a Holocaust survivor from Seelbach, Germany contributions in her memory should be made to the Simon Wiesenthal Center, 9760 West Pico Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90035. Services today at Sinai Chapel in Fresh Meadows, Queens.
