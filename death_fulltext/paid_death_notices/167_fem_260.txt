
        ROSENBERG-Sandra. Of Roslyn Heights, NY, on December 28, 2000. Beloved wife of Murray. Devoted mother of Donna, Joseph and Amy. Loving grandmother of Noah, Juliet, Emma, Hannah, Ilana and Abigail. Dear sister of George Frechter, Mildred Korman and the late Ruth Hauss. She was born May 17, 1931 in Brooklyn, NY, the daughter of the late Yetta and David Frechter. We loved and respected her very much. Services on Sunday, December 31, 12 noon at Sunset Chapels, 1285 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, LI. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Parkinson's Disease Assoc., 1250 Hylan Blvd., Suite 4B, Staten Island, NY 10305.
        ROSENBERG - Sandra. Temple Beth Sholom of Roslyn Heights, New York, mourns the passing of our beloved member, Sandra Rosenberg. We extend heartfelt condolences to her husband Murray and family. Alan B. Lucas, Rabbi Rose Schecter, President
