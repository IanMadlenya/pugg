
        GORDON-B. Douglas. March 1. A graduate of Choate, he was president of G&H Service. He is survived by his wife, Fritza von Lengerke; his sister, Marion Cross, and two of three children with his first wife, the late Elizabeth Housekeeper: his son, William Bruce, and his daughter, Elizabeth Comstock; and by three grandchildren, Brandon, Sarah and Kellie, the children of his late daughter, Deborah Nothstine. Arrangements for the memorial service will be made at the convenience of the family through Smith-McCracken in Newton, NJ.
