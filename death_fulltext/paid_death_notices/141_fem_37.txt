
        HOLLOBOW-Beatrice (Bee). The Officers and Board of Directors of Women of Reform Judaism, formerly the National Federation of Temple Sisterhoods, are saddened by the death of Bee Hollobow, of Chicago, cherished past President. Bee's intellect, creativity and commitment to women and Israel were exemplary. Her zest for life was contagious and so many lives were enriched by her never ending concern, dedication and deeds. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Judith Silverman, President Ellen Y. Rosenberg, Exec Director
