
        MARSHALL - Frederic, 90, died peacefully on June 3, 2002 at the home of his son in Rancho Santa Fe, CA. Mourned by Margaret, his wife of 64 years. Devoted father of Dr. Lawrence F. Marshall of Rancho Santa Fe, CA and his wife Sharon and of Dr. Robert Marshall of Chicago, IL. Beloved grandfather of Derek, Jessica, Leah, Kathryn and Samantha. Longtime Vice President of Industrial Raw Materials of New York, he was a life master in duplicate bridge and an avid tennis player. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Self Help Community Services, 440 9th Avenue, NY, NY 10001.
