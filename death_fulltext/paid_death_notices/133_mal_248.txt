
        MILLHOLLAND-Paul Douglass II, 93, on February 9 following a brief illness. A pioneer for new practices of cash management in the National Division of Citibank for 40 years, he retired as a Vice-President in 1969. He then consulted for the Foundation for Full Service Banks, NY, the U.S. Postal Service, and for Mayor Lindsay's administration. He graduated from the Choate School and in 1928 from Princeton University. He is survived by his wife, Alice (formerly of Dobbs Ferry, NY), his daughters, Carol Strasburger (of Belmont, MA), Louise Cecil (of Santa Cruz, CA and Vancouver, BC), Allaire Warner (of Mount Vernon, NY), seven grandchildren, and one great-grandchild. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Brookhaven Nursing Center for a music fund in his honor, 1010 Waltham St., Lexington, MA 02173.
