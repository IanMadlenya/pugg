
        JORDAN--Elizabeth, 96, retired RN, 1932 graduate of Harlem Hospital School of Nursing, devoted wife of Vonner Jordan, deceased, NYC Firefighter. She leaves daughters Janet and Daryl, son-in-law Herman, grandsons DJ and Michael, brother and sister-in-law William and Joan Fletcher, and thirteen nieces and nephews, died on August 15, 2006. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent in her name to the Salvation Army, stipulate the Foster Home and Adoption Service, 120 West 14th St, NY, NY 10011, or The Brooklyn Friendship Fund, 328 Flatbush Ave, Box 139, Brooklyn, NY 11238. (Brooklyn Chapter of The Girl Friends, Inc.)
