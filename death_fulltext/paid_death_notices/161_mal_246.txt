
        AUCHINCLOSS-Douglas died on June 20, 2000 at his home in New York City. He was 87 and had been ill for some time with amyloidosis. He was a writer at Time magazine for many years, principally in the Religion section. He is survived by his wife Catherine, his daughters Gail Gilbert, Lee Link and Alexandra Herzan, his sons Kenneth and David, eleven grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. A funeral service will be held at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Avenue at 66th Street, at 12:45 P.M. on Thursday, June 22. Burial will be private.
