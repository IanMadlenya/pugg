
        WARSHAW-Jules J. Died February 8, 2002. In his 50 years publishing magazines produced 35 nationally distributed titles, served as the Director of Publishing for The Curtis Publishing Co., President of Downe Leisure Services, and publisher of Holiday Status and Museum Magazines. He was a veteran of World War II, a valued mentor and friend to countless people, and a loving husband, father and grandfather. A memorial service will be held April 6, at his home in Manhattan.
