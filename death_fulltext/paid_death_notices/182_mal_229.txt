
        THOMPSON-Henry Howard Jr. A 50 year film critic for The New York Times, died March 10, 2002 at his residence in Cape Canaveral, FL. Born October 25, 1919 in Natchez, MS, he was a member of the New York Film Critic Society, moving to Florida five years ago from New York. Mr. Thompson was a U.S. Army veteran of World War II. Survivors include his brother, William Y. Thompson, nieces Gail Sweet and Sherard Lewis, nephews F. Charles Wightman & Richard Thompson. Burial will be in New Jersey. For information contact East Coast Cremation, Rockledge, FL.
