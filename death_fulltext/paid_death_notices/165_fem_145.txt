
        LEICHMAN - Lorraine Agnes. Peacefully at home in the arms of her beloved husband of 53 wonderful years, Carl Maclin Leichman, on October 5, 2000. Loving mother of Laurence and Andrew. Adored niece of Gertrude Salomon. Devoted friend to many. Lorraine, an effervescent woman of great charm, intelligence, courage, spirit, understanding, compassion, wit, and humor, had the marvelous gift to make people laugh and feel alive. A medical social worker and consultant for the aged and disabled, teacher, community activist, past vice-president of B'Nai B'rith and Hadassah, alumna of Adelphi University, and member of Temple Beth Shalom in Flushing, NY. Lorraine will be deeply missed by all.
