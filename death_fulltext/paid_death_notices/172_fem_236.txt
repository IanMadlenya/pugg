
        BELSKY-Muriel B., Ph.D. Beloved mother of Jana, David and Eric died peacefully on May 28, 2001 surrounded by her loving family. Sister of David, grandmother of Hallie, mother-in-law of Tom and Cynthia, and wife of David Lecker. Muriel was a respected psychotherapist and professor of psychology at Nassau Community College. During her career she also founded the Center for Gifted Children and hosted a talk radio program about child psychology. She helped many patients in her private practice and will be missed by a wide circle of family and friends. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, May 31 at 11:00 AM at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY 516-487-9600.
