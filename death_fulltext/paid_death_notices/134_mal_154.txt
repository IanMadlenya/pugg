
        GINSBERG-Benedict. The Jewish Education Service of North America mourns the passing of Benedict Ginsberg and extends its deepest sympathy to his daughter and our colleague, Judith Ginsberg, Executive Director of the Covenant Foundation, his wife, Adele, his son, Phillip, and the entire family. May the bereaved find comfort among the other mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Mark Lainer, President Jonathan Woocher, Executive VP
