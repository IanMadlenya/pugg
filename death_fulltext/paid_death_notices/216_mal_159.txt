
        BENSON--Guy Kenneth. Died peacefully on January 4, 2005 at age 84. He lived for 46 years in the Hudson River town of Dobbs Ferry, NY. There he and his wife, Philippa Day of Sydney, Australia raised their three children. He attended Lawrenceville School and Yale College, served in the Army in Europe during World War II, and was Vice President of Trident Films. He will be sorely missed by all who knew his extraordinary and persistent intelligence, humor, wit, patience, and stubborness. A memorial service will be held on January 22 at 2PM at South Church in Dobbs Ferry.
