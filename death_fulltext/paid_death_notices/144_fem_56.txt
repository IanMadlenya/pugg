
        WIEDER-Ethel. 92. Died peacefully on January 5, 1999. Beloved mother of Amy Ellner and the late Grace Wieder. Dear mother-in-law of Gene Ellner. Devoted grandmother of Joey, Nancy, and the late Eddie Ellner. Ethel was a very special woman known for her sharp intellect, great wit and generosity of spirit. She will be remembered fondly by all those who knew her.
