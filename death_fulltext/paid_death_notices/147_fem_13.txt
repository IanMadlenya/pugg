
        GRANDE-Sarina, on March 30, 1999. Patron of the arts and opera. Listed in Who's Who of American Women and very active in ItalianAmerican charitable organizations. Beloved wife of the late Frank Grande. Sister of Santa D'Angelo and the late Carmelo and Liborio D'Amato. Aunt of Marie Kallmeyer, Aimee Wallas and the late Frank D'Amato, and great-aunt of five nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Friday, 7-9 PM, and Saturday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Our Saviour Church, Park Ave. at 38th St., Monday, 10 AM. Interment to follow.
        GRANDE-Sarina. The Members, Directors and Officers of the Soldiers', Sailors', Marines' and Airmen's Club deeply regret the passing of their esteemed friend and Board Member, Sarina Grande. Ever helpful, she lent us a strong innovative hand at every turn of the road. We all will miss her valued counsel and guidance. R.I.P. Ivan Obolensky, President Col. John Barber, A.U.S. (Ret.) Executive Director
