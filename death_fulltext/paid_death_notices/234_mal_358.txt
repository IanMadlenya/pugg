
        MILDWURM--Morris. Passed away on Saturday, July 29, 2006. He was 95 years old. He is survived by his wife of 66 years, Helen, his daughters and son-in-laws, Rita and Rusty Auerbach, Lynne and Lenny Zimmerman, his three grandchildren Amy, Matthew and Corey, and two greatgrandchildren. WWII veteran and Lamston executive.
