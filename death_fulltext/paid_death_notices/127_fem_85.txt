
        SCHWARTZ-Jean Ehrlich, of Scarsdale, NY, born June 28, 1912 in Philadelphia, PA. Beloved wife for 59 years of Asher W. Schwartz. Devoted mother of Alec and Joanne, Fred, Sue and Frank Lalle. Loving grandmother of Kyra, David, Erroll, Isaac and Nora. Wonderful aunt, great-aunt and friend. She attended the University of Pennsylvania and Simmons School of Social Work. She was a medical social worker at Mt. Sinai Jewish Memorial Hospital and White Plains Hospital. Funeral to be held at Westchester Reform Temple in Scarsdale, NY, Sunday, August 17, 11:30 AM. Contributions to Scarsdale Family Counseling, Harwood Building, Scarsdale, NY 10583 would be appreciated.
        SCHWARTZ-Jean. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its devoted member and extends sympathy to her bereaved family. Marjorie Miller, President Richard J. Jacobs, Rabbi Judith Schindler, Assoc. Rabbi
