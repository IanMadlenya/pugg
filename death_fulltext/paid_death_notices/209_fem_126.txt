
        SENDAR--Carol Elaine. 64, June 18, 2004. Senior attorney New York State Attorney General's Office. Graduate of Vassar College and NYU Law School. Beloved daughter of Sylvia Sendar and brother of Michael Sendar. Caring aunt to Shawn and Jake Sendar. Cherished friend to many for her wise counsel and kindness. Interment at United Hebrew Cemetery, Staten Island, Sunday, June 20, 1 pm. Please make contributions to the American Ballet Theatre.
