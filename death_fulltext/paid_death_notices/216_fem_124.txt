
        ERHARDT--Katherine A. of Port Washington, NY died on January 11, 2005 at age 82. She was a longtime resident of Roslyn, where she and her late husband, William raised their beloved children, Christine Brill of Upper Brookville, Arlene Oliver of Huntington and William A. Erhardt of New York. She adored and lived her grandchildren, Elizabeth Brill, Michael Oliver and Samantha Oliver. Survivors include her devoted sonin-law, Robert Brill and sister, Frances Severynse and brother, George Brunner. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, January 16 at 1:00 PM in The Congregational Church of Manhasset, 1845 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorials be sent to Save the Children, Donor Services, 54 Wilton Rd, Westport, CT 06880.
