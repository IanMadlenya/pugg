
        La SALA-Andrew J., Sr. On June 18, 1999. Beloved father of Stephen H. A. La Sala, Mary Louise Verni, Paula Alice LeFebvre and Andrew J. La Sala, Jr. Dear brother of Frank, Anthony and A. Stephen La Sala and dear grandfather of 11 grandchildren and great-grandfather of 15 great-grandchildren. Formerly of Bronxville, NY, Mr. La Sala was a mason contractor and owner/developer for the La Sala Contracting Corp., of which he was Partner and Secretary from 1935 to the present. He was a member of the Westchester Country Club, the New York Athletic Club, Columbus Citizen's Committee, Associated Brick Masonry Contractors of Greater New York, and a member of the Bricklayers and Allied Crafts Local 41. He was a proud recipient of the Cardinal's Committee of the Laity Construction Man of the Year Award. He was an avid lover of opera and a former semi-pro baseball player. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10 AM, at St. John the Baptist Church. Entombment at Woodlaw Cemetery. The family will be present Sunday, 7-9 PM, and Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at F. Ruggiero & Sons, Inc., 732 Yonkers Ave, Yonkers. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Paul Verni Memorial Fund, Iona Preparatory, 255 Wilmot Rd, New Rochelle, NY 10804, are prefferred by the family.
