
        CLARK-Joseph Sill, Jr. Age 82. Died peacefully at home in Manhattan of Parkinson's Disease on September 1, 2001. Dearest husband of Mary Lincoln Bonnell. Caring father of Story Clark Resor of Wilson, WY., Joseph Sill, III of Venice, CA., Christopher Avery of New York City and Laurance Reed of Marble Head, MA. Brother of Noel Clark of Washington,D.C.. Fond grandfather of eleven. Joe was a graduate of The Middlesex School, Harvard College and The Harvard Business School. He was a valued energy analyst at Wertheim-Schroeder and Company from 1961 until his health forced his retirement in 1985. He was associated with H.G. Wellington and Company as a consultant until the spring of this year. A service will take place at Frank E. Campbell Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave on Thursday, September 6th at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his name may be made to the Middlesex School, Concord, MA., or to the Parkinson's Foundation of Columbia University Medical School, 710 West 168th Street, N.Y.C.
