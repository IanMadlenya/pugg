
        BAILIN-Florence. Of Beechurst, NY, 81. Beloved wife of the late William Bailin. Loving sister of Abe. Cherished sister-in-law of Terri and Bea. Adored aunt of David, Susan, Perry, Deborah and the late Michael, and great aunt (and a great aunt she was) of Jennie, Joanna and Benjamin. Died on August 8, 2001. A life of goodness, a legacy of love, she will be missed by the many she touched. Services Friday 1 PM at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
