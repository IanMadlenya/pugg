
        DEMPSEY--Ethel S. Age 87, on December 31, 2003. Beloved mother of Richard and mother-in-law of Antonia. Commercial artist and longtime New York City resident, she had lived in Flemington, NJ since 1998. Cherished grandmother of James Dempsey, and Erin Plimley, and great-grandmother of Meagan, and Colton Dempsey. She will be missed by all who had the good fortune to know her. Memorial service on Saturday, January 10, at 11:30 AM at St. Mary's in the Highlands Episcopal Church, in Cold Spring, NY.
