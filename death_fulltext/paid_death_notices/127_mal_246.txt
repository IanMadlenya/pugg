
        FINCH-Henry L. Jr. (Roy) 79, died peacefully the evening of Friday, August 22, 1997 at his home. Attended by his wife Maggie and three of his five children, Julie, Marta, Dabney, Annie and Roy. He is also survived by his brother Stephen and five grandchildren Christine, Flavin, Rebecca, Rainer and Julian. He was a professor of philosophy and religion at Sarah Lawrence College and Hunter College and the author of books on Wittgenstein and Simone Weil. Memorial service at Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle, NY, Saturday, Sept. 6, 2 PM. Donations in his name to the War Resisters League in NYC or to Restore: The North Woods, Box 440, Concord, MA 01742. A book of his poems is available free on request to Maggie Finch at 106 Liberty Ave, New Rochelle, NY 10805.
