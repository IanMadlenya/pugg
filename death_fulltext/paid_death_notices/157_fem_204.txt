
        KOCH-Jacqueline B. Born Nov. 21, 1921 in Zurich, Switzerland. Died in NY February 20. Mother of Stephen, Jeremy and Chapin. Sister and sister-in-law of Anne-Marie and Dick Bullen. Also survived by 7 grandchildren: Eliza, Serena, Nicholas, Charles, Sarah, Peter and Lily. Her love, independent spirit and loyal friendship will be missed by her many friends and devoted family. A memorial service will be held at the Church of the Holy Trinity, 316 E. 88 St. on March 4 at 3 PM. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Visiting Nurse Service of NY Hospice Care.
