
        ROSARIO--Jaime, 65. Beloved husband of Pam, cherished father of Andrea and husband Jose, devoted Grandpa to Isabela, died May 11, 2005 in New York. Born in Puerto Rico, he arrived in New York in 1951. A graduate of Hunter College School of Social Work, Jaime was a trusted friend, teacher and mentor. At his death, he was Associate Director of Coney Island Hospital's Department of Behavioral Health. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Fund for Gastric Cancer Research #
