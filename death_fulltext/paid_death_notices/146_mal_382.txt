
        GRENADIER-George, 83. Of Westport, CT, on March 18, 1999. Founder and president of Grenadier Corp of New York City. Predeceased by his wife, Hindel (''Del'') Kronick Grenadier in 1998. Loving father of Lisa Grenadier and Tom Connor of Southport, CT, Michael Grenadier of New York City, James Grenadier of Austin, TX; cherished brother of Simon Grenadier of New Rochelle, NY, and adoring grandfather of three. Services Monday, 11 a.m., at Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Rd, Fairfield, CT. Interment will follow at Willowbrook Cemetery, Westport, CT.
