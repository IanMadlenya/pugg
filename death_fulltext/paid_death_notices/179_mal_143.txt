
        KLIMLEY-Stanley (Stan) Peter. Died December 2, in Boca Raton, FL of cancer at the age of 86. Beloved husband of Ann; adored father of April and Abbott Peter; beloved stepfather of John Sarafoglu and Stephanie Tassos; esteemed father-in-law of Patricia and Anna Soo Hoo; admired uncle of Susan. Predeceased by his first wife Dorothy Abbott Klimley. A talented artist and prominent illustrator. A kind man with a sharp wit, zest for life, intellectual depth and perpetual curiosity. A man for all seasons. We miss you terribly. A memorial service will be held in late January in Boca Raton, FL.
