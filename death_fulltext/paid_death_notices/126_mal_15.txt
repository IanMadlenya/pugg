
        BACHMANN-Emile W., 79, died June 23rd, at his Bronx home after a long illness. He died as he had lived, with dignity, grace and courage. Born in Zurich, Switzerland, he served in the Army Air Corps during WWII, receiving the Distinguished Flying Cross. He later owned a renowned cabinetmaking and design firm. A world-traveler and gourmand, he shall be remembered for his spirited intelligence, generosity and joie de vivre. He is survived by his loving wife of 50 years Charlotte; devoted daughters, Karen and Barbara, and granddaughter, Caroline.
