
        HAIBLUM-Anne (nee Maisner). Age 89. Of Hollywood, Florida. Formerly Brooklyn, New York. Died on April 1. A devoted wife for 60 years to husband Harry and mother to daughter, Burma Klein. She was smart as a wit & never at a loss with a quip. Graveside services April 4 at 11 AM, Beth David cemetery, 3201 North 72 Avenue, Hollywood, Florida. Anne dear, we will never stop loving you.
