
        BROWN--Muriel D. (nee Barry). A lifelong resident of Greenwich Village in New York City and Budd Lake, N.J. Died suddenly in her New York home on Monday, January 19. She is the mother of Sheila of Budd Lake, N.J. and New York City, Msgr. Patrick E. Brown of Stirling, N.J., Elizabeth Pettinato and Thomas of New York City. She is also survived by her daughter-inlaw Patricia Brown and the late Lawrence George Pettinato. She had six grandchildren Sheila O'Donnell, Katie Brown, Maggie Brown, Thomas Brown, Larry Pettinato and Michael Pettinato and one great-grandchild Kathryn Grace O'Donnell. She is also survived by her brother Edward F. Barry of Staten Island, N.Y. She is the widow of the late Patrick E. Brown who died in 1990. Mrs. Brown lived her life as a devoted mother and grandmother. She was a daily communicant at the Immaculate Conception Church, Manhattan. She was active in the Ladies of Charity in the Archdiocese of New York and was a weekly volunteer at St. Patrick's Cathedral, New York City. The viewing will be at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue, New York City, N.Y. on Wednesday 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM and Thursday 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM. The Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated on Friday at 10AM in St. Veronica's R.C. Church, Christopher Street, Greenwich Village. Interment will follow at The Holy Rood Cemetery, Whippany Road, Morristown, N.J. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the St. Vincent de Paul Endowment Fund.
