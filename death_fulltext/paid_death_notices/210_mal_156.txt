
        OSTER--Abe. The trustees, faculty, and families of the Schechter Regional High School of Teaneck, NJ, are deeply saddened by the passing of Abe Oster, a co-founder, avid supporter, and valued friend. His entrepreneurial spirit and generosity contributed greatly to the realization of our community's longcherished vision of a Solomon Schechter high school for the young people of Bergen, Passaic and Rockland Counties. Our heartfelt condolences to Ann, Jane, Avi and the family. Adam Brown, Chair Board of Trustees Jay Dewey Director Rhonda Rosenheck Principal
