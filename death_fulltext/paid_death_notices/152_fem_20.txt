
        ABRONS-Mary Goldwater. Age 84, died on Thursday, September 2, 1999 at her home in New York City after a long illness. She is survived by her brother, James Goldwater, three children, a son, Dr. Henry Abrons, two daughters, Alix Abrons and Anne Abrons, and four grandchildren. Private memorial service. Gifts in her memory may be made to The New York Public Library.
