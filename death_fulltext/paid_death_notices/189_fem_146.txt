
        BROWN-Muriel. Born in Old Forge, PA, and resided in Del Ray, FL. Preceded in death by her husband Fred, brothers Morris, Harry, and Irving, sisters Pearl, Sylvie, Ida and Ruthie. Survived by her children Arline and Lester Zuckerman, Jerilynn and John Noto; grandchildren Richard and Brenda Zuckerman, Jarrett Zuckerman, and great-granddaughter Jordan; siblings Gladys, Arnold and Wilma. Remembered lovingly for her devotion to Judaism and to her family.
