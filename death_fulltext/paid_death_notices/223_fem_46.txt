
        MATHIAK--Cassandra Skouras, died at home in Bradenton, FL Tuesday, July 26 with her beloved husband Mark by her side. Cassandra is the devoted daughter of Barbara and Spyros Skouras, Sr, and the loving sister of Spyros Jr, George and Damaris. Her father was Chairman of Prudential Grace Lines. Her grandparents were Spyros P. Skouras, Chairman of 20th Century Fox from 1942 to 1968, and C. Russell Feldman, who introduced automobile radio, and acquired Newport Steel and National Union Electric and TV. The Funeral was at St. Joseph's Church in Bradenton, July 29. There will be a Memorial Mass at St. Michael's at 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT August 9 at 11:00 AM, and a burial at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
