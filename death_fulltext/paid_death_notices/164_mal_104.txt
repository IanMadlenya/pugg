
        SCHWIMMER-Bertram. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY, died August 24, 2000 at age 90, in Los Angeles, CA. Survived by his loving wife of 60 years, Beatrice; his son and daughter-in-law, Arthur and Arlene; and grandchildren, Elinor and David. Lawyer, musician, golfer, wit, he was cherished by his family, friends and clients, and will forever remain in our hearts.
