
        PREZIOSI-Romolo M. Born July 31, 1911 in Naples, Italy to the late Concetta and Luigi Preziosi. Died May 12, 2000. Devoted husband of the late Mary Fazioli. Loving father of Professor Donald (UCLA) and Regina P. DeRise (Queens College). Dear brother to his twin Remo and the late Antino. Cherished grandfather of Tarquin (L.A.) and Alexandra Preziosi and Rosemary and Richard DeRise. Also survived by nephews Louis and Raymond. Friends may call at Quinn's Funeral Home, 162-14 Sanford Ave., Flushing, Sunday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Monday 10 AM, Holy Family Church, 175-20 74th Ave., Flushing. Contributions in his memory can be made to the American Parkinson's Disease Association.
