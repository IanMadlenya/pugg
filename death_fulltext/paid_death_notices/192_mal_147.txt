
        SCHUSSLER-Albert. The officers and directors of Chamah, the international organization to provide education and humanitarian aid for Russian Jewry, note with deep sadness the passing of our dear friend, Albert Schussler. His contribution to Russian Jewry and the State of Israel is unparelleled. His kindness and gentle heart will always be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy and heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Claire Schussler, and family. Sanford Bruckner, Chair Emeritus Mitchell Benerofe, International Chairman Binyomin Malachovski, Moshiach Chudaitov, Hillel Zaltzman, Executive Committee Chamah New York, Jerusalem, Moscow
