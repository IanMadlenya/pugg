
        BALTER-Marjiann Kurtz. On December 9, 1999. Beloved wife of Henry. Loving mother of Jason M. and his wife, Candace. Adoring grandmother of Amanda Ann. Also survived by John Kurtz. Services Monday, 12 noon, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65 St. Contributions in her memory may be made to ''Net Donations for Dr. Nancy Kemeney Pump Program'', care of Memorial Sloan-Kettering.
        BALTER-Marjiann. Congregation Emanu-El of the city of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Marjiann Balter. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
