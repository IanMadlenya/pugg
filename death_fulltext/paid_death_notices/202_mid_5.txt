
        DEUTSCH--Sharry. Of West Orange, NJ. Suddenly on November 7, at age 55. Beloved wife of Howard. Mother of Michael and his spouse Meira, Daniel and Adam. Daughter of Florence and Abraham Cohn, sister of Adrienne. A respected and admired teaching assistant at the Solomon Schechter Day School in Cranford, NJ, and life member of Hadassah. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Young Judaea, care of Hadassah House, 50 W. 58 St., 8th Floor, NYC 10019.
