
        McSHERRY-Charles K., M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family and the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, acknowledge with sadness the passing of our dear friend and devoted colleague, Charles K. McSherry, M.D. Dr. McSherry served with distinction as the Director of Beth Israel's Department of Surgery from 1976 to 1990; he retired in 1997 and was named Director Emeritus in 1999. He was a revered member of the medical staff who will be missed by all who knew him and worked with him. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family and loved ones. Morton P. Hoskins, M.D., Chief Medical Officer Anthony C. Antonacci, M.D., Chairman, Department of Surgery Robert L. Salant, M.D., President, Doctors Alumni Assoc.
