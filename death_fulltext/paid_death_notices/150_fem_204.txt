
        LEDERER-Martha Schick. Age 97. A lady from Vienna, on July 23, 1999. Died peacefully in the mountains of New York. Lived for over 40 years in Jackson Heights, NY. Born in Vienna, Austria, June 6, 1902. Beloved daughter of Karl and Friedaricka Steiner, came to America in 1940. Valued family above all and will always be remembered for her intelligence, strength, courage and life-long friendships. Beloved and cherished daughter, wife, mother, mother - in - law, grandmother, great-grandmother of 18. Honored aunt. B'nai Brith sister, wonderful friend and neighbor. ''I have fulfilled my father's command. I have kept the family together. My time was well spent.'' - From ''The Lady of Vienna,'' her autobiography. Service Sunday, 12:30 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue.
