
        QUINTAVALLE-Joseph T. The Regis High School Board of Trustees, Administration, Faculty and Students mourn the loss of Joseph T. Quintavalle. Joseph was an alumnus, Class of 1930, and former faculty member. Joe has left an indelible impression on the students and the Regis community where he taught for 39 years. He will be remembered as a legendary teacher and a very dear friend. We extend our sincerest condolences to his wife, Betty, and his family. May Joseph rest in peace.
        QUINTAVALLE-Joseph T., of S.I. on Sept. 30. Husband of Betty (nee Barlow). Father of J. Michael and David T. Grandfather of Melissa. Father-in-law of Linda. Mass of Christian Burial St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Friday, 11 AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery. Visitation Thursday only, 2-5, 7-9PM, Dongan Hills Virginia Funeral Chapel, 1707 Hylan Blvd., S.I. In lieu of flowers, donations to Regis High School, NYC would be appreciated.
