
        TISNE--Jean Elizabeth (Betty), on January 5th, age 84, long time resident of Chappaqua, NY. Wife of the late Jacques J. Tisne, mother of Katherine Paradise of Boston, MA, Elizabeth Hilton of Fairfax, CA, John Goldsmith Jr. of Plainfield, IL, grandmother of Alisse Goldsmith, Chris Goldsmith and Alex Hilton. Betty is survived as well by son-in-law Peter Paradise, daughter-in-law Dorese Goldsmith, stepson Peter Tisne, his wife Jean, and their children Claire, Philip and Jack, and by stepdaughter Suzanne Tisne. We will miss your exuberance, your humor, your style, your optimism, your impeccable taste, and your beautiful soul. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Alzheimers Foundation of America, New York, NY.
