
        RITZ--Norton, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center was saddened to learn of the death of Dr. Norton Ritz in early November. Dr. Ritz was a physician in the Division of Hematology in the Department of Medicine for over 40 years. He was involved with the Hematology Residency Program and was an outstanding teacher. He was well-respected by his peers and will be sorely missed by all. We extend sincere condolences to his entire family. Ira Mayer, M.D., President Warren Wexelman, M.D. President-Elect Steven Farber, M.D. Secretary/Treasurer
