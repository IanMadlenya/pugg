
        LARKIN-Mark D., age 79, of New York City. Died on November 13, 1999, while traveling in Yazd, Iran. He was the beloved husband of Linda Hess Larkin; father of Michael Larkin of San Francisco, California, and retired partner of PriceWaterhouseCoopers. A memorial service will be held at All Souls Unitarian Church, Lexington at 80th St., at 4 PM, Sunday, November 21. The family will receive guests in the Community Center after the service and also from 5PM-8PM, Thursday, November 18, and Friday, November 19, at the Schapiro's, 1035 Fifth Ave., Apt. 7C. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to the Frederick Douglass Academy, Attn: Dorothy Haime, 2581 Adam Clayton Powell Jr. Blvd., New York, NY 10039.
