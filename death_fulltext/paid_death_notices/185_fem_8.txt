
        MARDER-Jamie Kay, 37, of Studio City, CA, formerly of Tenafly, NJ, passed away suddenly on May 27. She is survived by her mother Ellen, her father and his wife Jerome and Agatha; her three sisters and their husbands, Nancy and Jeremy, Wendy and Milton and Karen and Adam, and her brother Scott. Her spirit and enthusiasm will be with us always as she will live in our hearts forever. Services will be at Mt. Sinai in Los Angeles at 1PM on June 2.
