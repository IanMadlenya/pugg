
        YOUNG-Jean Connolly. Died at home on Sept. 15, 1998, surrounded by her family. Beloved wife of Joseph for fifty years. Loving mother to Megan Zwerner, Joseph Jr, Peter & Steven Young. Cherished grandmother of Adam, Emily, Anne, Blake, Colleen and Hunter. A caring, loving aunt. A kind hearted, helpful friend to many. A special woman, full of grace, spirit and a special quality for listening. Her compassionate words and deeds will always be remembered. Those of us who had the privilege of knowing and loving her understand we were blessed. Visiting hours Friday, September 18th, 1998, 2-4:30 and 7-9 P.M., at Chapey Funeral Home, West Islip, New York. a Memorial Service will be held 10:00 A.M., Saturday, September 19, 1998 at St. Patricks's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, N.Y. Contributions in her memory can be made to Good Samaritan Hospice, 655 Deer Park Avenue, Babylon, New York.
