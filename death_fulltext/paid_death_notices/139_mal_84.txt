
        WALSH-Reverend Michael J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicor of St. Anthony of Padua Church, 133-25 128th Street, S. Ozone Park on August 7. Reposing at the church, Sunday, August 9 from 7-9 P.M., Monday, August 10 from 2-5 P.M. Mass of transferral at 7:30 P.M. Funeral mass Tuesday, August 11 at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
