
        KOEHNE-Richard S. ''Dick'', of Sutton, VT, and formerly of Lawrence, LI, died Wednesday, July 28, 1999, at the age of 76. He was predeceased by his wife Laura (Martin) on February 24, 1999. Survivors include: one son Nathaniel (Tom) and his wife and Madora Koehne of Brooklyn, NY; two daughters, Laura (Hoppy) Whitman of Bethel, AK, and Sarah (Mouse) Scharfenaker and her husband Mark of Denver, CO; five grandchildren, Annie, Nell, Alexandra, Molly and Ellis.
