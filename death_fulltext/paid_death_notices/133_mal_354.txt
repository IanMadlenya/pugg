
        CHRISTENFELD-Paul. February 24, 1998, in his eighty-eighth year. Beloved husband of Ruth and the late Renee. Cherished father of Janine and Peter Lindquist, and Alan and Bonnie Christenfeld. Proud grandfather of Randall, Robert, Noah and Seth. Devoted brother of Adele Golbey, Stanley, and the late Bernard, Seymour and Leonard. Funeral, noon Thursday, February 26, Park Avenue Synagogue, 87 ST & Madison Avenue, New York City.
        CHRISTENFELD-Paul. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Ruth and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Alan Levine, Chrmn of the Board Robert P. Antler, President
