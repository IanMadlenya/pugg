
        SHARWELL-William G. Of Bronxville, NY on Sept. 18, 2000. Surviving are Jacqueline L., sons William L. Sharwell of Lakeland, FL., and Paul L. Sharwell of Park City, Utah, sister, Marguerite S. McCrea of Montclair, NJ. Also surviving are several nieces and nephews. Calling hours at the Fred H.         SHARWELL-William G. The Pace University community notes with sorrow the passing of Dr. William G. Sharwell, fourth President and former Chairman of the Board of Trustees. We are greatful for his years of dedicated service to our students, faculty and staff. Sincere condolences are extended to Jacqueline Sharwell and their two sons, William and Paul. Calling hours 2-4 and 7-9PM Wednesday, September 20th, McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville. Funeral Mass 11AM Thursday, September 21st, Saint Joseph's Church, Bronxville. Contributions may be made to Pace University, Office of University Advancement, 1 Pace Plaza, New York, NY 10038 or a charity of your choice. Aniello A. Bianco, Chairman David A. Caputo, President
