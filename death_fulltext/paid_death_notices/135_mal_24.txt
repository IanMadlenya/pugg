
        BRAND-Joseph A. Of New York and Deal, N.J. On March 31, 1998. A 1941 graduate of University of Virginia. Distinguished U.S. Naval Officer World War II and former President of Federated Brokerage Group. Beloved husband of Ina. Devoted father of Peter and Rena Sue, brother of Bill Brand. Services Friday, April 3, 12 noon at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street. Interment private.
        BRAND-Joseph A. The firms of DeWitt Stern, Imperatore, Ltd. and DeWitt Stern Group record with deepest sorrow the death of one of the former owners of Federated Brokerage Group and Camp Brokerage. To his family, friends and associates we express our heatfelt condolences. He will be sorely missed by all who knew him. Jolyon F. Stern David O'Dowd
        BRAND-Joseph A. The Officers and Members of the Elberon Bathing Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Robert Fagenson, President Ellen G. Winik, Secretary
