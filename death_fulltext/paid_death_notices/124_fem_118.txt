
        DANIEL-Elsa M. May 15, 1997. Wife of the late Simon Daniel. Mother of Allan and his wife Kendra. Grandmother of Peter, Anthony and Nora. She was a past President of the Eisman Day Nursery and a Founder of SCAN and an active member of Congregation Emanu-El, NYC. Service Monday, May 19, 2 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to SCAN or the Eisman Day Nursery.
        DANIEL-Elsa M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Elsa M. Daniel, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
