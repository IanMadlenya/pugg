
        STEWART-Daniel D. Landscape Architect, died on January 22, 1999, in The Burn Unit of New York Hospital, Cornell Medical Center. He was 68 years old. A Requiem Mass followed by a reception, will be held on February 1, at 6 P.M. at The Church of the Transfiguration, 1 East 29th Street, in Manhattan. Donations in his memory may be sent to The New York Botanical Garden.
