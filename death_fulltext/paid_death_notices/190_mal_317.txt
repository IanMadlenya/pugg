
        TAXEL - Manuel. Beloved husband of Ruth, devoted father of Madeleine, Joel and Susan, adoring grandfather of Gregg, Samantha, Michael, Alex and Elizabeth, and great-grandfather of Nathan and Zachary, died on November 24 at age 85 at his home in Brooklyn, NY. He served his country with distinction during W.W. II seeing action in the Pacific Theatre. After a career as a litigator, he served as a Federal Administrative Law Judge. He was an active member of Temple Shalom of Flatbush. Services were held on November 26 at Nieberg Midwood Chapel.
