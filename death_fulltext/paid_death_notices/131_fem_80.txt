
        GREENE-Janet Steinberg. Age 88. On December 7, 1997 at the Jewish Home and Hospital for the Aged in NYC. Beloved wife of the late Wilfred and beloved mother of Dorothy Alexis. For more than three decades, as school psychologist for the Dalton School, teacher, and psychotherapist, she gave unstintingly of her time, energy and insight. She was a pioneer. Services private. Memorial contributions may be made either to Radcliffe College, Ten Garden Street, Cambridge, MA. 02138 or to Community Access, Inc., 666 Broadway, New York, N.Y. 10009.
