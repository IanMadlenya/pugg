
        NEW-Elizabeth V. Born 1917-died on July 8th, 1998 of cancer at the Jacob Perlow Hospice in New York City. Dear wife of the late William Kohman, loving mother of Peter Kohman & friend to many. Graduate of Wheaton College and Columbia University. Betty was a child psychologist, a caring counselor and psychometrician with extensive experience at New York Hospital/Cornell Medical Center, The Chapin School and private practice. At her request there will be no funeral. Contributions in her name may be made to Wheaton College, Norton, MA.
