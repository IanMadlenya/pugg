
        REED-Dabney (Freeman). Of Manchester-By-The-Sea, MA. October 5, 1999, at age 65, formerly of Amherst, MA, and Southampton, NY. Beloved wife of Nathaniel Reed. Loving mother or Wrenn Dabney Reed of Brookline, MA, Priscilla Reed Anderson of Boston, MA, and Timothy Nathaniel Reed of Boston, MA. Sister of Daniel A. Freeman III of Norwell, MA, and Robert W. Freeman of Metamora, MI. She also leaves one granddaughter, Grace Lois Anderson. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, October 13, at 11 AM, at St. John's Episcopal Church, 705 Hale Street, Beverly Farms, MA. Another memorial service will be held in Southampton, NY, on Saturday, October 23, at 10 AM, at St. Andrew's Dune Church, Gin Lane. Burial service will follow at Southampton Cemetery. Friends and family are invited to attend. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in memory of Dabney Reed to The Children's Aid Society, 105 E. 22 St, NYC 10010.
