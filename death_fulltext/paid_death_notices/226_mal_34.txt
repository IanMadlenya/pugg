
        GOLDEN--Herbert Lincoln. 91. Of Laurel, NY, died peacefully at home on October 31 of lung and heart conditions. A journalist with the Philadelphia Record and Variety, officer with United States Navy on two oceans in W.W. II. At Bankers Trust Co., worked with post-war Hollywood film industry and other entertainment media until retirement at 79. Survived by wife, Mary Cox Golden, 7 stepchildren, 11 step-grandchildren, one nephew. His second wife, Elizabeth Atkinson Golden, and first wife, Gertrude Fisher Golden, predeceased him. Memorial service in Laurel, November 12. Memorial donations to Peconic Land Trust, P.O. Box 1776, Southampton, NY 11969.
