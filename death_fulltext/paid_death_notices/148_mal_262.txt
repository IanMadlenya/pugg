
        GREENE-Philip M. Age 79, of East Hampton, died on May 22nd, in Southampton Hospital of Emphysema. Survived by his wife Constance, five children, Sheppard, Basking Ridge, NJ, Philippa Mulford, Duxbury, MA, Stephanie Radwan, Chapel Hill, NC, Matthew, Esopus, NY, Lucia Connolly, Woodbridge, CT., and eleven grandchildren. Services are private. Interment in family plot in Hamilton, New York.
