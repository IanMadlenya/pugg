
        REINHART-Robert, 93, a native of New York City. Died in Walker Valley, NY on December 17, 1999. A retired Variety columnist, magician, theatrical booking agent, and worker for the American Civil Liberties Union and the American Friends Service Committee, he was born December 13, 1906 to Rudolf and Lulu Heine Reinhart. He was a graduate of Harvard and of NYU, was married to the late Catherine Johnson, and is survived by nieces and nephews. Donations may be made to Houdini Hospital Fund, 1735 East 26th Street, Brooklyn, NY 11229.
