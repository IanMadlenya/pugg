
        PALEY-Audrey C. Died October 1, 1998 in New York City after a long and courageous battle with ovarian cancer. She is survived by her husband, Richard of 40 years, her daughters, Jane and Liz and son-in-law Michael. The family gratefully acknowledges the outstanding and compassionate care given by Dr. James L. Speyer and his staff at NYU Medical Center. In lieu of flowers and in support of Dr. Speyers continuing efforts the family ask that donations be made to The Gynecological Cancer Research Fund at NYU Medical Center, 550 1st Avenue, NY, NY 10016. Attention Dr. James Speyer. Family will be at home from Monday through Thursday, 2PM to 9PM.
