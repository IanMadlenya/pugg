
        CALDER--Elizabeth Ann (Libby), 86, a resident of Watch Hill, RI, died April 28th. Born in Boston, MA, the eldest child of Peter and Elizabeth Sullivan Naphen, Libby was raised in New Canaan and Darien, CT and lived for many years in Armonk, NY with her late husband, Louis Calder, Jr. and their three children. Predeceased by her eldest son Louis Christopher Calder, her two brothers, Peter and Fred Naphen, and her sister, Marie Kane, she is survived by her son, Peter D. Calder of New Canaan, CT, her daughter Elizabeth C. McEwen of Wilmington, VT, and five grandchildren. A memorial service will held at a later date. Donations in Libby's memory may be made to the Westerly Adult Day Care Services, Inc. 85 Wells Street, Westerly, RI 02891.
