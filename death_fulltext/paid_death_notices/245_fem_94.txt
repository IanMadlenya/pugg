
        APTER--Adlyn Liman, 93, of Harrisburg, PA. Formerly of Hewlett, NY and Lake Worth, FL. Passed away June 15, 2007. She was the beloved mother of Peter Liman (Reggie), Linda Herman (Lewis) and Vicki Apter (David). Cherished grandmother, great-grandmother and auntie. Her love of life, magnetic personality, prowess as a sculptor and golfer and devotion to family and friends will be sealed in our hearts forever. Graveside service Mt. Lebanon Cemetery, Queens, NY 11:30am, Monday June 18.
