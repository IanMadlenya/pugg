
        DOUGLASS--William Angus. Died peacefully on September 19, at home, with his wife Stephanie, son Doug, daughters, Tory and Fiona, by his side. A graduate of Yale '51, he and college friends founded Careers, Inc, in 1951. Since 1970 he divided his time between UK and USA. A service will be held on October 11, at Presbyterian Church, New Canaan. In lieu of flowers donations to IRC or Doctors Without Borders.
