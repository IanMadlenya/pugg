
        WICENTOWSKI--Sidney. On November 4 at the age of 94. Beloved husband of Fannie (Solomon) for 73 years. Dear father of Frances (Matthew) Harris, Eleanor (Burton) Jaffe, David (Jane), Jerrold (Debby). Also survived by eleven grandchildren and eleven great-grandchildren. Loving brother of Gertrude Golding. His many friends and relatives in the United States and Israel will miss him. Services Sunday 1PM at ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
