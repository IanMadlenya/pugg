
        FRANKEL-Arnold J. Age 76. Died peacefully in his home on November 20, 1998, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Miriam D. Frankel for 54 years, devoted father of Hinda Squires, Janet Staub, and Alan, adoring grandfather of eight grandchildren; loving brother of Edith Frankel and Doris Dunn. He is also lovingly remembered by daughter-in-law Jodi Frankel and son-in-law Shalom Staub. Arnold was born on March 17, 1922, in New York City. He graduated from CCNY, having majored in Chemical Engineering. He later completed his M.S. from Polytechnic University. He was co-founder and former Chairman of Aceto Corporation, a chemical company located in Lake Success, NY. Arnold's retirement from Aceto in May, 1997, also marked 50 years of the company's growth and success. He will be remembered for his many philanthropic efforts and his active participation on the Boards of the Queens Child Guidance Center, the Jewish Museum, and HIAS. He was a Trustee of the City College Fund, and a member of the Advisory Board of the Chemical Heritage Foundation in Philadelphia. He received awards from the American Jewish Committee and the UJA. He was a member of AAAS, American Chemical Society, American Institute of Chemists (for which he had served as past President of the NYC Chapter), Chemist's Club, and Sigma Xi. Arnold will be remembered for the ethical and responsible way in which he conducted himself as a businessman, a community leader, and pater familias. Service Sunday, 10:45 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        FRANKEL-Arnold. Temple Shaaray Tefila records his death with profound sorrow. He was admired and beloved. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Miriam, all his family, his friends and associates. Rabbi Harvey M. Tattelbaum Dr. Ellen Saxl, President
