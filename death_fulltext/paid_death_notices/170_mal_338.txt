
        MOAK-Arthur Allen. Died in his home in Kingston, New York, on Sunday, March 25th after an extended illness. Originally from Brooklyn, NY he was graduated from Cornell University, 1939 and is survived by his wife Blanched (Hertzfeld) whom he married in April of 1941, his daughter Patricia Ann, his son Jeffrey, five grand grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Services and cremation Tuesday, March 27.
