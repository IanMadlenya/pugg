
        PALL--David B., Ph.D. The trustees, faculty and staff of Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory are deeply saddened at the passing of Dr. David Pall. To his family and his many friends, we express our deepest sympathy. William R. Miller, Chairman Bruce Stillman, President and Chief Executive Officer James D. Watson, Chancellor W.
