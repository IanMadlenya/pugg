
        YOLTON--John William. Piscataway, New Jersey, has died in New Brunswick, New Jersey, at the Robert Wood Johnson Hospital. He was in his 83rd year. He and his wife, Jean (nee Sebastian) were married in 1945. His heirs are his wife and two daughters, Karin (Mrs. Bryant Griffith) and Pamela H. Smith. Burial service was private.
