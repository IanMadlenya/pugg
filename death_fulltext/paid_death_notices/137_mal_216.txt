
        ZITT-Arthur. 88, of Hallandale, FL, on June 16, 1998. Beloved husband of Bertha for 66 years. Loving father of Dr. Myron (Jeanne) Zitt of Woodbury, NY and cherished grandfather of Jonathan. An exNew Yorker, he was President of Spirit Fluid Mfg Corp. with branches throughout the U.S. Bored with retirement, he bought and operated Jimmies Candies in Dania, FL until forced to retire due to ill health. Funeral services were held in Florida on June 18, 1998.
