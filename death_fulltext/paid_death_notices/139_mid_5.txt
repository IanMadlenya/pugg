
        SCHWARTZ-Ruth. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ruth Schwartz. She was the beloved mother of Henry Schwartz, a dedicated supporter who is a valued member of the Executive Committee of our Dairy Products & Allied Industries Division. We extend our deepest sympathies to Henry, and his wife, Pari; Mrs. Schwartz's daughter, Grace Sevy; her grandchildren and great grandchildren. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
