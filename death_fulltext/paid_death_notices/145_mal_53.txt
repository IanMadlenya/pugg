
        BERNSTEIN-Lawson F. Feburary 29, 1920-January 25, 1999. New York City Attorney. Died in Pittsburgh, Pa. Survived by his sons, Dr. Lawson F. Bernstein Jr. (Linda), Richard D. Bernstein (Terese), and daughter Katherine Fredland. Also survived by brother Merton C. Bernstein. and four grandchildren. In his memory, contributions may be made to Columbia College, New York City.
