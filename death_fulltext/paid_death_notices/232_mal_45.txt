
        CENKL--Bohumil. O Waban, MA, part-time resident of Harthaven, Martha's Vineyard and Mittersill, NH, born in the Czech Republic in 1934, passed away on May 4th. Husband of Jirina, father of Michal and Pavel and their spouses Kim and Jen, grandfather of Orion, brother to Franta. A Professor of Mathematics at Northeastern University and an artist, he was courageous and unique in everything he did. Remembrances to the Scleroderma Foundation, Danvers, MA. www.scleroderma.org
