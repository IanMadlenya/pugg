
        HELLER--Lillian. On June 11, 2004, at age 92. Widow of the late Jacob R. Heller, who predeceased her by 161/2 years. Loving and devoted mother of Hugh (Leslie) Heller and Stephanie (Ralph) Sorley. Adoring grandmother of Peter (Jaime) and Roger Heller, Ryan and Jason Sorley, and Bree (Erik) Anderson. Cherished great-grandmother of Emily and Katie Anderson. Sister of Annette Bassen. A wonderful person to all who knew her. Contributions in her memory may be made to Hadassah.
