
        VIOT--Betty Ann Painter, died on February 29, 2004. She was a much beloved lady blessed with humor, dignity, and intelligence. She was born on May 15, 1914, and grew up in Kansas City, MO. Mrs. Viot had a theatrical and modeling career which took her to both New York and Hollywood. She was the wife of 54 years of the late Van Howard Viot. They lived in Sewickley, Pennsylvania, Locust Valley, New York, Hinsdale, Illinois, and Asheville, NC, where they both died. Mrs. Viot leaves behind a daughter, Victoria V. Martin and her husband, Peter B. Martin, of Summit, New Jersey; two grandchildren, the Reverend Christopher H. Martin and his wife, Chloe of Los Angeles, and Elise M. Bates and her husband, Greg, of Dallas; and two greatgrandsons: Harper and Simon Martin. Services will be private. Memorials may be sent to: St. Giles Chapel, Deerfield Episcopal Retirement Community, 1617 Hendersonville Road, Asheville, NC 28803.
