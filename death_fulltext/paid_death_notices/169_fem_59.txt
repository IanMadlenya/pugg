
        HARTMAN-Bertha. Formerly of New York City, died peacefully in her sleep in Vancouver, Canada on January 31, 2001 at age 85. Bertha balanced her joy and involvement with her family with an active dedication to social progress. All who knew her through her many years of teaching and political activism were touched by her astute intellect and deep humanity. She is survived by her children: Matt Hartman, Neil Hartman, and Joanne Bullington; by her grandsons Eric Hartman, Sven Hartman, and Thomas Hartman, and by her great grandson Elias Hartman.
