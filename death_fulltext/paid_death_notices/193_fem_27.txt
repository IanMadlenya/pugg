
        ASCHE-Marjorie E. On February 1. Beloved wife of the late Abraham (Jack) Asche. Loving mother of Richard and Barbara Asche and Judith and Stephen Stoll. Adoring and proud grandmother of Michael, Rachel, Corey and Katia. Dear sister of Leonard Ernst and sister-inlaw of Esther Asche. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday, 7-9 PM. Funeral services Tuesday, 11 AM, at Temple Israel, 112 East 75th Street. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made in Marjorie's name to SCAN NY or Eisman Foundation for Children, Inc.
        ASCHE-Marjorie. The Board and staff of SCAN NY mourns the death of Marjorie Asche, a founding Board member. We will miss her devotion to SCAN, her wise and thoughtful advice, the sparkle in her eye and her unyielding love for the children of New York. SCAN has lost a great friend. We send our condolences to her children, grandchildren, brother and all who knew her. Jane Overman, Chairperson Lewis Zuchman, Exec. Dir.
        ASCHE-Marjorie. The Board of Directors of the Eisman Foundation for Children is deeply saddened by the passing of our Board member and friend Marjorie Asche. We will miss her wise counsel, her energy and her devotion to the Board and to the nursery. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her children Judith Stoll and Richard Asche, and family.
