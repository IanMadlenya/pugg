
        BRESSLER-Martin J. Attorney, 53, died on September 8, of cancer at home in New York, surrounded by his beloved family. Survived by his wife and best friend, Jane, children Amy, Lee, and Scott, parents Anne and David, sisters Bonnie Bressler and June Osterman, and parents-in-law Hannah and Norbert Blechner. A devoted and adoring husband and father, his passion, humor, and loyalty will be eternally missed. ''And you, my father, there on the sad height, curse, bless me now with your fierce tears, I pray. Do not go gentle into that good night. Rage, rage against the dying of the light.'' Funeral services at Riverside Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, Monday, September 10, 11:45 AM.
