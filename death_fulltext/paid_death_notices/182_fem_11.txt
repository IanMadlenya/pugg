
        TREUHAFT-Pearl. Of Weehawken, New Jersey, died peacefully on Thursday, Feb. 28, at the age of ninety. Dear sister-inlaw of Elaine Treuhaft, beloved aunt of Laurie and Robert, and wonderful great-aunt to Layla, May, Melanie & Miles. Cherished by all of her family and her many friends. Funeral services at 11:30 A.M. at ''Wien & Wien'', 402 Park Street, Hackensack, New Jersey. Memorial donations may be made to Deborah Hospital Foundation, 212 Trenton Road, Browns Mills, New Jersey 08015.
