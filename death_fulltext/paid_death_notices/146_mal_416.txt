
        FRANK-Andre. Age 75, of Sunrise, FL. Passed away Friday, March 19, 1999. He was born in Romania. Emigrated to NY with his family in 1955. After retirement has lived in Sunrise for the last five years. He was a member of Local 3 of the IBEW. He is survived by Rose Frank, his wife of 53 years, daughter Dr. Hettie B. Frank, son-in-law Dr. Frederick E. Kahn, grandson Nicholas Max Kahn, of NYC, and his brother Imre Frankovits of Denver, CO.
