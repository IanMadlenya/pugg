
        EWESON -- Dorothy Dillon died at her home in Far Hills, NJ on June 8th at the age of 92. The daughter of the late Clarence and Anne Dillon and the sister of the late C. Douglas Dillon, she is survived by her three children, Christine Allen of NY, Philip Allen of Far Hills, NJ and Douglas Allen of Ames, Iowa. She is survived by eight grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. Luke's Church in Gladstone, NJ on Friday, June 17th at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Visiting Nurse Association of Somerset Hills at 12 Olcott Ave, Bernardsville, New Jersey 07924.
        EWESON -- Dorothy. We mourn the loss of Dorothy D. Eweson, a founding member of our Board of Advisors. She was a loyal participant who in recent years had not missed a meeting until her recent illness. A gracious and generous lady, always interested and supportive of our activities, she will be greatly missed. We extend our condolences to her children, grandchildren, and greatgrandchildren. Stanley Chang, M.D., Chairman Department of Ophthalmology, Columbia University Medical Center Louis V. Gerstner, Jr. Chairman, Board of Advisors
