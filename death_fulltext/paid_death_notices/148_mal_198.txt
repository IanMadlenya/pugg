
        HYMAN-Dr. Herbert Henry. The clergy, officers and trustees and members of Temple Sinai of Roslyn record with profound sorrow the death of senior past president, Dr. Herbert Henry Hyman. We grieve together with his beloved wife, Edith, his children, Edward and Deborah, Gerald and Sandra, Jeanne and Kenneth and his grandchildren, Cameron, Devon, Gregory, Jesse and Eric. His wit, integrity and passion for Judaism will be deeply missed. We pray that his memory will continue to bless us. Services at Temple Sinai on Thursday, May 20, at 12 noon. Michael A. White, Sr. Rabbi Norman Kahan, Rabbi Emeritus Michael J. Paul, President
