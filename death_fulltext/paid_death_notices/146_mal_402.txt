
        GROSS-Joseph R.. Age 64, lifelong resident of NYC. Died on March 19, 1999, in Greensburg, PA, after a short illness. He was preceded in death by his parents, Elsie Woehl Gross and Joseph G. Gross. He is survived by cousins Lourdes Woehl Nowalk and her husband Thomas, and Dr. Joseph J. Woehl of Greensburg. He was a graduate of Regis Academy and Fordham University. Mr. Gross was a retired social worker for the city of New York. He was a gentle man of Christian spirit and luminous intellect. Reposing at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 W. 72nd St, on Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 10 AM, St. Gregory the Great R.C. Church. Interment Old St. Raymond Cemetery.
