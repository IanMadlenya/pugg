
        SHIMONOV-Patricia D., 51, of New York, NY on Thursday, March 1 at New York Hospital in Manhattan. She is survived by her husband, Chaim Shimonov, and her sons, Perry and Aidan. Mrs. Shimonov is also survived by her mother, Mary Dunphy (nee Young), her sisters, Linda Lelii and Mary Sholtis and her brother, John Dunphy. She was predeceased by her father, John Dunphy in 1997. Mrs. Shimonov was an attorney practicing entertainment law and employed by CBS and ABC in Manhattan. There was a graveside service on Friday, March 2 at the Norton Cemetery in Bethlehem Twp, NJ. Donations may be made to the Children's Advocacy Center of Manhattan.
