
        THOMPSON--Charles D.W., of Locust Valley, NY, on March 8th, 2004, age 73. He died after a lengthy illness. Dearly remembered brother of Henry B. Thompson III, Mary T. Dwyer and Beatrice B. Guthrie. A Memorial Service will be held at Christ Church of Oyster Bay, Saturday, April 17th, at 11 AM. Contributions may be made to the Endowment Fund of the Locust Valley Library, the Oyster Bay Historical Society and the Christ Church of Oyster Bay, New York.
