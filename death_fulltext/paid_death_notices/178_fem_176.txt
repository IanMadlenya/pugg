
        FONTAKIS-Deborah J. (nee Conaty). On November 12, 2001, of Floral Park, NY. Flight attendant on American Airlines Flight 587. Survived by devoted husband Steven, loving children Christopher and Stefanie. Cherished daughter of Rose and the late James D. Conaty. Beloved sister of Barbara Cruise, Jim Conaty, Susan Gallagher, Teresa Marler and Terence Conaty. Adoring aunt to Tim, Mallory, Liam, Jake and Ian. Family will receive friends and relatives at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave, Floral Park, NY, on Monday, November 19, and Tuesday, November 20, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. A funeral Mass will be held at Our Lady of Victory Church in Floral Park, on Wednesday, November 21, at 10:30 AM. Interment Flushing Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Deborah J. Fontakis Memorial Foundation, 1562 Richmond Rd, Staten Island, NY 10304. This foundation was established in Debbie's honor to support the education of her children.
