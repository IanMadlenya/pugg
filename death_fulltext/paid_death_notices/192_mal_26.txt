
        BARASH-David. The Partners and Staff of Barash, Friedman, Friedberg and Adasko, CPAs P.C. deeply mourn the passing of David Barash on December 28, 2002. He was a CPA for more than 60 years and a co-founding partner of the accounting firm. As a friend to all, he never missed an opportunity to do a good deed for everyone he met. We extend our sincere condolences to the Barash family and to Sis. He will be remembered and missed by all of us. Ed Friedman, Abe Friedberg, Lee Adasko, Jerry Persampieri, and your Friends at the Office
