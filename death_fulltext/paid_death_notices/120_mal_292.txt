
        ROBINSON-Arthur, M.D., January 25, 1997 of Amityville, N.Y., formerly of Ozone Park, N.Y. Beloved husband of Gladys Robinson, devoted brother-in-law of Irene Robinson and Dorothy Stagg. Loving uncle of Barbara and Ronald Robinson, Joyce and Gerald Robinson, Elizabeth and Alan Shulman, Ruth and Michael Orange, Karen and Kennedy White and John Ritter. He was a loving husband as well as a caring physician for over 50 years. He gave of himself as both physician and friend and was loved by all for his warmth and caring nature. Services will be today at 2:15 PM at the I.J. Morris Funeral Home, Dix Hills.
