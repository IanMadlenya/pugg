
        AIKEN--Dan. The entire St. Luke's - Roosevelt Hospital Center family joins with its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with great sadness the untimely passing of our beloved colleague Dan Aiken, site director of security at St. Luke's Hospital. Dan's wonderful sense of humor and loyalty to the organization will be missed by all. To his wife Cynthia, their three children and all those who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences.
