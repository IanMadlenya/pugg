
        GUGGENHEIMER - Randolph. The board of directors and staff of the United Hospital Fund are deeply saddened by the loss of Randolph Guggenheimer, a tireless and passionate advocate on behalf of high-quality health care for the residents of Harlem, whom we honored with our Distinguished Community Service Award in 1992. North General Hospital, which he served as founder and chairman emeritus, stands today as a testament to his vision and purpose. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife and sons and the entire Guggenheimer family. Howard Smith, Chairman James R. Tallon, Jr., President
        GUGGENHEIMER - Randolph. The board of trustees, faculty, staff and students of Helene Fuld College of Nursing of North General Hospital mourn the loss of Randolph Guggenheimer, our dear friend and founding trustee of the college. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Elinor, and to the entire family. We will all miss Randy. Peggy Wines, President, HFCN Madlyn Stokely, Chair, Board of Trustees, HFCN
