
        JOHNSON-Robert J. Of Chatham, New Jersey. Formerly of Locust Valley, NY, and Floral Park, NY, on July 12, 1997. Survived by his wife Virginia; his sons Robert of Denver, CO, Douglas of Chatham, New Jersey, and Christopher and his wife Mary of Franklin, Mass. Beloved Papa to his grandsons Christopher, Timothy, & Gregory. Also survived by a brother, James, of Hudson, FL, and sister Ruth of Long Island, NY. Friends may call at the Wm. A. Bradley & Son Funeral Home, 345 Main Street (Rt. 124), Chatham, NJ, Monday, July 14, 1997, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Committal Services are private for the family. He was born in Boston, MA., September 10, 1918, graduated from Seewannaka High School and attended Brooklyn Polytechnic Institute. Drafted into the United States Army in March 1941, he was commissioned an officer on December 31, 1942, & attended radar school at MIT and Harvard. He served in the European Theater of Operations with the First Army as RADAR officer of the 110th Anti-Aircraft Gun Battalion in Normandy, the Liberation of Paris and the Battle of the Bulge. After being discharged in 1946, he was a mechanical engineer with Jefferson Chemical Company and later with CE Lummus from 1955 to 1984.
