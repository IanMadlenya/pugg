
        KALB-Roland J. Born in Vienna, Austria in 1916, died on November 27, 2002. A man of charisma, fortitude, compassion and love. A moving force in healthcare and the ultimate humanitarian. He is survived by his devoted wife of 50 years, Lore, his children Linda and Rick, grandchildren Julia, Ryan, Alexa and Samantha and his beloved brother, Egon and his family in Israel. Memorial sevices will be held on Sunday, December 1st at 3 PM at Kol Ami Synagogue in White Plains. Interment private. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, donations should be made to the Oskar Kalb Memorial Foundation, 2 Eaton Lane, Scarsdale, NY 10583.
