
        SALTZMAN--Natasha died Thursday November 30, 2006 in her Eastham Cape Cod home. A woman of unstinting generosity, Natasha was deeply loved by her sister, Judith Litvich, her children, Nelle and Dan Miller and Rebecca and Joel Miller and adored by her three extraordinary grandchildren, Henry, Anna and Molly who will miss their Bubby. She was a woman of many strong interest and talents. In memory of Nataha contributions may be given to an organization that reflects your relationship with her. A service will be held on Sunday December 3rd at 2pm in Eastham Massachusettes.
