
        BERMINGHAM-Beth Reny, 74. Of Scarsdale, on September 17, 2002 surrounded by her family. She was the dearly beloved wife of John H. Bermingham for 52 years. Mother of seven children, sons Thomas V. II, John H. Jr., Jerrold G. and Douglas McDonald (deceased) and daughters Maryclaire Fouse, Paula Filippone and Amy Wilson. Grandmother of 15. A 1948 graduate of College of New Rochelle, she was a former president of Catholic Women's Club of Westchester and of Immaculate Heart of Mary School PTA. Family will receive friends at the Bennett Funeral Home on September 18, 7-9PM and September 19, 2-4 & 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be held at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, N.Y., September 20, at 10AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to Rev. Thomas V. Bermingham, S.J. Scholarship Fund, Fordham University, Bronx, NY.
