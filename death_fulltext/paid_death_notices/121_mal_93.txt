
        ROBERTS-A. Sydney IV, of Palm Beach, Fl. and Westport, MA. died Feb. 3, 1997. He was 82 years old. Mr. Roberts graduated from St. Georges School and Yale University, Class of 1936. During W.W. II he served as Lt. Commander in the U.S.N.A. He was a member of the Everglades and Bath and Tennis Club in Palm Beach and the Acoaxet Club in Westport, MA. Mr. Roberts was married to the late Mary Ellen Plante (Roberts) and is survived by a daughter Amy Roberts Lee of Palm Beach and Westport, two sons Henry P. Roberts of Mattapoisett, MA, and A. Sydney Roberts V of Palm Beach and Westport, and seven grandchildren. He is also survived by his second wife Mary Stair Roberts. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, February 8th at 11 A.M., in Bethesda-By The Sea Episcopal Church, Palm Beach. Interment will be private. For information Northwood Funeral Home West Palm Beach.
