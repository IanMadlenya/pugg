
        MUHLFELD-Elizabeth Blakeslee. Died on April 13 in Englewood, N.J. In the week preceding her death, she had been surrounded by her family whom she had loved so dearly-her husband of 55 years, Bearss, and their 5 children, Betsy Ingram of Manchester, MA., George, of Summit, N.J., Katie Bell of Northeast Harbor, ME., Lucy Muhlfeld Kazickas of Amagansett, and Louise Patterson of San Franciso, CA. She is also survived by 13 grandchildren. Betty Lou, as she was known, was born in Chicago and grew up in Pittsburgh, PA. until moving to Englewood, N.J. There will be a memorial service on May 31, in Englewood, N.J.
