
        FITZGERALD-The Reverend Peter A., C.M., 78, of the Vincentian Community at Niagara University, on July 10, 1998. Survived by his brothers Arthur and Matthew Fitzgerald, and his two sisters Jane Kelly and Eileen Ireland. Brother of the late Joseph Fitzgerald. Family will receive friends at Vincentian Residence, St. John's University, Utopia and Grand Central Parkways, Jamaica, on Monday from 7 to 9 PM, and Tuesday, 9 to 10:15 AM. Mass will be celebrated at the Vincentian Residence Tuesday, 10:30 AM. Interment St. Joseph's Seminary, Princeton, NJ. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to Our Lady of Lebanon Church, Niagara Falls, NY.
