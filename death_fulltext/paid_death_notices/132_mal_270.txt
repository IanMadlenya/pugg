
        SCHEIN-Leo. On January 20, 1998. Retired January 8, 1959 after 25 years as Assistant Civil Engineer for NYC. Served in U.S. Army in the same capacity from 1941-1943. Along with his younger brother, George Schein, they helped organize and they founded the U.S. Table Tennis Association in the early 1930's. Surviving are his sister-in-law, Hilja Schein an her son Henry Lucenius. Graveside service and interment at Montefiore Cemetery, St. Albans, NY at 11 AM on Thursday, January 22, 1998.
