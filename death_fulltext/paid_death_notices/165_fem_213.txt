
        NEWMAN - Ida. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of the beloved mother of our generous benefactor William Newman and of our esteemed Honorary Chairman of the Board and generous benefactor Melvin D. Newman. The Morris and Ida Newman Education Center, which houses our Upper School and which bears her name and that of her late husband will stand as a living monument to her and to her entire family. We extend heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein, Principal David N. Kahn, Chairman, Board of Trustees
        NEWMAN-Ida. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its affiliates mourn the passing of the beloved mother of our devoted member Melvin D. Newman. We extend heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Rabbi Joshua Lookstein Chaim Edelstein, President
