
        WEINER-Ernest M., D.P.M. The Officers and Board of Trustees of the New York State Podiatric Medical Association mourn the passing of a former president of the Association. A gifted and respected practitioner, and an esteemed colleague and friend, he was a tireless and forceful advocate for his profession and for the advancement of podiatric medical education. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Ethel, and to his entire family.
