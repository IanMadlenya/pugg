Proud great-grandfather of Theodore, Eugenie and Max Robin, Samuel & Jacob Oppenheim, Jonah, Matthew and Elijah Hochberg, Deborah & David Azer. Friends may call Saturday, May 14, 2005 between 4 and 6 PM at the Burger residence. Services private.
