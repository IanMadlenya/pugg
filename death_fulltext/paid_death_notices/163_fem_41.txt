
        NEWELL-Marla. On August 4, 2000. The Conservative Synagogue of Fifth Avenue mourns the untimely passing of its First Vice President, former President, and beloved friend. Our heartfelt condolences to husband Roger, and son and daughter Paul and Sonya. We will continue to be inspired by the legacy of her keen judgement, depth of knowledge, strong moral values, and devotion to Judaism and to our synagogue. Services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. on Sunday, August 6, at 11:15 AM. Cantor Irwin Gelman President Roselle Mironer
