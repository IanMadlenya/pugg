
        WOLOSHIN-Sidney E. On November 5, 2000 at the age of 72. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Loving father of Ellen and Mark, and Jonathan and Debra. Adoring grandfather of Jake and Julia. Cherished brother of Norma Basch. Pioneer in the jingle and advertising business. Award-winning music producer. Admired by all who knew him. Service on Wednesday, 10:00 AM, at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd., New Rochelle, New York. Contributions in his memory may be made to either Ronald McDonald House or to the National Endowment for the Arts.
        WOLOSHIN-Sidney. The Board of Governors, members and staff of Bonnie Briar Country Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member and extend their deepest sympathy to his wife, Sylvia, children and grandchildren. Lou Hoenig, President
