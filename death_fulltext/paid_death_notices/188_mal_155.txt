
        FRIEDMANN - Francis G. Died peacefully on Sunday, September 15, 2002. He is survived by his loving wife Rosalie Kennedy Friedmann, five children, Chris LeVeen, Jane Friedmann, Ann Schara, C. Edward Friedmann, Mary Leech and 11 grandchildren. Two brothers, Jack and Paul Friedmann, and one sister, Ruth Walsh, also survive. Frank attended Yorktown High School and graduated with honors in 1952 from Fordham University. He began a distinguished military career in November, 1942, as a master sergeant in the US Army Air Force. He was honorably discharged in January, 1946. In 1963 he became a sales manager with Chrysler Corporation, retiring in 1989. A Mass will be held Wednesday, September 18, 10 AM, Assumption Church, Peekskill, followed by interment at Assumption Cemetery. Visiting at Dorsey-Carlone Funeral Home, Peekskill, Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Donations to the Alzheimer's Association, 785 Mamaroneck Ave, White Plains, NY 10605.
