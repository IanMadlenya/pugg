
        De LEONARDIS-Lawrence, M.D. The Board of Directors of Physicians Home note with sorrow the death of Lawrence De Leonardis on October 31, 2002. He was a Board member and was dedicated to improving the lives of disadvantaged physicians throughout New York State. Physicians Home benefited greatly from his wisdom and thoughtfulness. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Joseph Cleary, M.D., President Allen W. Mead, M.D., Secretary
