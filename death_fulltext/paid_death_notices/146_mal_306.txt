
        MILLMAN-Louis J., M.D. 89, of Riverdale, NY, died on March 11, of congestive heart failure. A graduate of CCNY, he went on to study medicine in Munich and Basel, graduating with honors. He served for three years in the Army Medical Corps as a pathologist with the rank of Major, receiving two Presidential Citations. Dr. Millman was Director of Pathology at Fordham Hospital for 20 years and on the teaching faculty of Albert Einstein. He is survived by his wife Beverly, his sons Dr. Jonathan Millman of Mobile, AL, Paul Millman of Palo Alto, CA, and daughter Amy Millman Faxon of Manhattan. He has six grandchildren.
