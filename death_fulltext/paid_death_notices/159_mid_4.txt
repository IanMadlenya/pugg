
        WOLOWITZ-Goldie. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Goldie Wolowitz, beloved mother of Arnold Wolowitz, who with his wife Carol are devoted supporters and cherished friends, committed to maintaining a strong Jewish community on Long Island, in New York and throughout the world. May her memory always be an inspiration. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Arthur M. Katz, Chair, LI Cabinet Erika Witover, Chair, LI Women's Campaign
