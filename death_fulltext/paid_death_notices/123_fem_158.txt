
        WADMOND-Elita Cason, 93, of Palm Beach, Florida, and formerly of New York City, died at her home in Palm Beach on April 18, 1997. She was predeceased by her husband, Lowell Wadmond, a long time senior partner of White and Case. Mrs. Wadmond was born in Lakeland, Florida and attended Southern Seminary in Virginia. While living in New York City, Mrs. Wadmond served on the Boards of Grosvenor Neighborhood House, The Presbyterian Home, The American Bible Society and during World War II on the Board of the Morgan Officers Club. Mrs. Wadmond was a member of Brick Presbyterian Church and the Colony Club in New York City and the Everglades Club and the Bath and Tennis Club in Palm Beach, Florida. She is survived by several cousins. Her services will be private. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to The First United Methodist Church, 72 Lake Morton Drive, Lakeland, Florida 33801. The Heath Funeral Chapel in Lakeland, Florida and Quattlebaum-Holeman Burse Funeral Home, West Palm Beach, Fla.
