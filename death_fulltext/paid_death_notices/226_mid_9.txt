
        FUHRMAN--Ruth (Fox). Age 90, of Arlington, MA, formerly of Westbury, Long Island, on Wednesday, November 16, 2005. Beloved wife of the late William Fuhrman. Devoted mother of Juliet Fuhrman and her husband Stanley Wolf of Arlington, MA and Jonathan Fuhrman and his wife Susan Kane of Pasadena, CA. Loving grandmother of Noah Wolf and Aaron Wolf. Graveside services were held. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Combined Jewish Philanthropies, 126 High Street, Boston, MA 02110 or to the City of Hope Hospital, Attention: Central Processing, 1500 East Duarte Road, Duarte, CA. 91010.
