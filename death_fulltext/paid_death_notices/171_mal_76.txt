
        SCHWARZ-Ralph J. Jr. Noted First Amendment Attorney in Manhattan died April 3, 2001. Ralph was born August 27, 1923 and lived in New Orleans, LA. where he attended the Numan School and Tulane University. Although he started Tulane Law School, Ralph left to serve on the battleship The U.S. Mississippi, until the end of World War II. After the war, he went to Columbia University to receive his law degree. He was an active tennis player and well known in East Hampton, N.Y. He is survived by his brother David Schwarz, a resident of Louisiana. Funeral services private.
