
        STASY--Oksana (Stasyzyn). Screen Actors Guild actress and dancer, born Gemini 1945 died December 4th, 2006 in her sleep. She is survived by her mother and beloved friends, she was the last girlfriend of actor Peter Lawford. Humanitarian, helped the homeless and downtrodden. Unrelenting love for her mother, actress fund buried her at Kensico Cemetery in Valhala, NY.
