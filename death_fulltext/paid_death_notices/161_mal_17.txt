
        KOHN-Dr. Richard Jay. 51, passed peacefully at home in Kensington, CA on May 28, 2000. Beloved husband of Marianne Betterly-Kohn and father of Jack. Richard directed Lord of the Dance/Destroyer of Illusion, a film on the Tibetan and Sherpa Buddhist ritual and festival in Nepal. He was founding director of the International Buddhist Film Festival, executive editor of the forthcoming Buddhism on Film and Lord of the Dance. Richard is cherished by many friends and his family who celebrate his compassionate heart, courage and the wild grace of his humor. We were blessed by his presence. Services on June 2, 2000 at his home in Kensington, CA.
