
        HIRSCH-Dorothy. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of the Chairlady of its Women's Club. Her exemplary dedication to our Synagogue, enthusiasm for its programs and selfless concern for all its members will be remembered among us with gratitude and affection. Heartfelt sympathy is extended to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi Hermann Merkin, Founding Pres. David Yagoda, Chairman Prof. William Schwartz, Pres.
        HIRSCH-Dorothy. The Women's Club of Fifth Avenue Synagogue is deeply saddened by the untimely passing of its esteemed and beloved Chairperson. Her leadership will forever be remembered with gratitude. Her dedication to our Synagogue was unique. Her life was a benediction. May her memory be a blessing. Maldy Jeger Beryl Trau Presidium, Women's Club
        HIRSCH-Dorothy. A woman and cousin of valor who taught all of us about family caring and doing. She was always there for her family and friends. She will truly be missed by all the Hirsch generations. Richard Hirsch and family.
