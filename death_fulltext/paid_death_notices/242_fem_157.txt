
        ALTOMONTE--Barbara, on March 20, 2007. Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, she lived in Crofton, MD, since 1966. Survived by her loving husband, John (aka Jack), two devoted sons Dennis and Paul, and three grandchildren, and two brothers, Bill Maughan and Gerard Maughan and two sisters, Judy Amend and Maureen Maughan. She was a full time high school English teacher for 18 years. Funeral mass will be held on March 24, 11am at Our Lady of Peace in North Brunswick, NJ.
