
        DAVIS-Robert Edward. Age 68, on December 22, 2001. A graduate of Loomis-Chaffee School, Yale (B.A.) and Temple (Ph.D.), Bob pitched in the major leagues for the Kansas City Athletics in 1958 and 1960 before starting a career as a human resource executive and organization consultant. Beloved husband of Suzanne, father of Mark, Kyle, Joel and Lauren, son of June and the late Sam, brother of Patricia, and grandfather of Mikala, Matthew, Michael, Benjamin, Stephanie, Beau and Sarabeth. We love you and will always miss you-rest in peace. Memorial service Thursday, December 27th, 4:30 PM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Contributions in his memory may be made to Shy-Drager Research, Vanderbilt University, Room AA3228, Medical Center North, Nashville, TN 37232-2195, attn: Dr. David Robertson.
