
        MARTUSCELLO-Kathryn Sessa, died at her home in Brooklyn on Wednesday, April 22, 1998. She was eighty-six and died after a long illness. The wake will be held Saturday and Sunday, April 25 and April 26 at Guido Funeral Home, 440 Clinton Street, Brooklyn, New York, 2-5PM and 7-9PM both days. Funeral Mass Monday, April 27, 1998 at 10 AM, Sacred Hearts and St. Stephen's Corner of Summit & Hicks Street, Brooklyn, New York.
