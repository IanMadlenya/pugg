
        DREISEN--Jean Weisman. Born September 13, 1920, died peacefully in her sleep on October 3, 2006. She was the loving wife of the late Anson I. ''Bud'' Dreisen. She will be deeply missed by her family. She leaves behind a beautiful legacy to her daughters Betsy and Janet Dreisen, her grandchildren Elizabeth and Philippe Faraut, Matthew and Lindsey Karatz, and Teddy Karatz and her great grandchildren Juliette, Thomas and Clara. Services will be held at Hillside Memorial Park, 6001 Centinela Ave., Los Angeles, CA 90045 on Friday at 11am. Those wanting to remember her may send contributions to the Los Angeles Conservation Corps at 3655 S. Grand Ave., Suite 280, Los Angeles, CA 90007.
