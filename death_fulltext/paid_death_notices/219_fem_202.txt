
        ROBERT--Carmen. Beloved mother, grandmother, greatgrandmother, sister and aunt. Born Ponce, Puerto Rico, 1909, died Thursday, April 28, 2005. Predeceased by her son, George Gallego, son-in-law, Joe Tomasini, brother, Juan and sister Marina. Survived by her large, loving family-brother, Luis Robert (Ponce PR), daughters Olga Castre (Guillermo), Anna Montalvo (Heriberto), Nylda Tomasini, Carmen McNicol (Donald), Sylvia Carballo (Jose), sons Pedro Gallego (Gloria) and Ivan Gallego (Frances), and daughter-in-law, Mirta Gallego. Cherished grandmother of 14. Beloved great-grandmother of 14. Visitation, Dormi Funeral Home, 1121 Morris Park Ave, Bronx, NY, Friday, April 29, 2005, 2-4PM and 7-10PM. Mass and Christian Burial, Saturday, April 30, 2005, 9:30 AM, St. Helena Church, 1315 Olmstead Ave., Bronx, NY. Interment St. Raymonds Cemetery, Bronx, NY.
