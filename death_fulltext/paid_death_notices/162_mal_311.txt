
        FEERICK-Donald J. Died on July 29, 2000, at home in New City, NY. Born in the Bronx on April 10, 1938, to the late Mary and John Feerick, Irish immigrants from County Mayo. Donald was the beloved husband of Meg; cherished father of Mary Beth, Donald, Kathleen, Patricia, Jennifer and Margaret, loving grandfather of ten, devoted brother of John, Maureen, Kevin and Anne, loving nephew of Patrick Boyle, and an in-law, uncle, cousin and friend of many others. Wake at Higgins Funeral Home, New City, July 31, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass at St. Francis of Assisi Church, West Nyack, 11 AM, August 1, 2000. Mr. Feerick was dearly loved by all who knew him.
