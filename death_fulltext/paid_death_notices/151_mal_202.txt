
        MAREN-Thomas H, Dr. Died peacefully on August 15, 1999 at home at Salisbury Cove, ME. Husband, father, grandfather, world renowned educator, pharmacologist, physiologist and philanthropist; beloved and respected by wife, family, research, clinical and teaching colleagues. Survived by his wife, Emily, sons Peter, James, and David and Emily's children, nine grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. Services will be held Thursday at 2pm at Eden Baptist Church, Salisbury Cove, ME. Contributions may be made to the church.
