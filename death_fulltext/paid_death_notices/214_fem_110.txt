
        GOLD--Ruth. ''Nannie''. Of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of North Woodmere, NY, died November 14, 2004 at home in Chappaqua, NY surrounded by her children Bonnie Smith and Steven Gold, sister Shirley Finger, and adoring grandsons Jared and Brian Smith. She was a role model and inspiration to her friends and family, and no one had a greater capacity to love and be loved. She lived and died with dignity and grace. Funeral service will be held at Temple Beth El, 229 S. Bedford Rd., Chappaqua, at 1PM on Tuesday, November 16.
