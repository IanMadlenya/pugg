
        KIVIAT-Leah Aaron Drier. Born London 1902, arrived NY 1912, died January 10, 2000. Her generous spirit and ''golden hands'' will long be remembered by her daughter Isabelle Zakin, son Sherman Drier, granddaughter Nina Zakin Essey, son-in-law James Essey, greatgranddaughter Alexandra Essey and others who loved and admired her.
