
        STEINFIRST-Susan, daughter of Donald and Jane Steinfirst of Pittsburgh, PA, died March 7, 1997, at home in Carrboro, NC, of cancer. Dr. Steinfirst, age 56, was Professor of Children's Literature at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. Dr. Steinfirst was co-author of ''The History of Children's Literature'' and author of ''Folklore and Folklife, a Guide to English Language Reference Sources.'' Dr. Steinfirst is survived by her son Michael Zaretsky, husband Gene Story and two brothers, John Steinfirst of San Francisco and Richard Steinfirst of Cleveland. There will be a memorial service March 16 at 2 PM in Manning Hall on the University campus. The Susan Steinfirst Memorial Lecture Fund has been established at the UNC Library School.
