
        BERLAND-Seymour. Age 87, died February 24, peacefully in Charlotte, NC. He was the son of the late Joseph and Anna Berland, husband of the late Jean Spector Berland. He is survived by sons Joseph and Richard Berland, daughters-in-law Sue and Joanne and ten loving grandchildren. Funeral and interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
