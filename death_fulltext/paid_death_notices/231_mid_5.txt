
        MANNING--Kenneth D. Loving and devoted husband to Rosalie. Cherished father to Damian Manning and Tobie Earley and her husband Bryan. Ken owned and operated Card-O-Mat on Broadway. He died peacefully at home after a courageous and valiant battle with brain cancer. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in Ken's name to The Brain Tumor Research Fund, c/o Dr. Lauren Abrey, Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, NY 10021. A life celebration will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St on Monday April 10th at 1:30pm.
