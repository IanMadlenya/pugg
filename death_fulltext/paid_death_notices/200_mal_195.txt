Devoted grandfather of Lisa (Thomas) Krommenhoek and Melissa (Andrew) Moss. Dear brother of Felicia Price and the late May Miller. Passed away peacefully at home in his 94th year. Graveside Service on Sunday, September 21, 11AM, Riverside Cemetery, Saddlebrook, N.J. Family will be sitting Sunday-Wednesday at the Aldewereld Home, Long Beach, NY.
