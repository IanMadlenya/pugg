        REDLENER-Jason Craig. The President, Board of Trustees and entire Montefiore Medical Center community are profoundly saddened by the tragic and untimley death of Jason Redlener, beloved son of Irwin Redlener, MD, President and Co-founder of the Children's Hospital Fund and President of the Children's Hospital at Montefiore. Our sincere and heartfelt condolences are extended to his loving wife, Sheareen Bahrami Redlener; parents, Irwin and Karen Redlener, and Arlene and Mark Goldberg; brothers and sisters, David, Michael and Stephanie, Lara and Amy; his grandparents and entire family. Spencer Foreman, MD, President Jay B. Langner, Chairman Board of Trustees Montefiore Medical Center
