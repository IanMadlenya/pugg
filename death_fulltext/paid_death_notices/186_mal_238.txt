
        TOUCEY-Walter C. A former financial executive in New York City and a resident of Rye Brook, N.Y. died July 22, 2002. He was 91. He graduated the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He received his New York CPA Certificate in 1937. Funeral and burial services are private. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to Summerfield United Methodist Church, 225 King St, Port Chester, N.Y. 10573. Inquires Craft Memorial Home, Inc., Port Chester, N.Y.
