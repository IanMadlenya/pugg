
        FEELY-Robert M. Of New York City and Cornwall, CT. Died April 3, 2000. He is survived by his loving family, wife Margot, daughter Erin Feely-Nahem, stepsons Thomas and Douglas Biow, seven grandchildren and his brother Capt. W. Fergus Feely and his family. Memorial service will be held at 3PM on Monday, April 10, 2000 at the Unitarian Church of all Souls, Lexington Ave. at 80th St. In lieu of flowers the family asks that donations be made to the Class of 1950 Scholarship Fund at Williams College, PO Box 231, Williamstown, MA. 01267 or The Cornwall Library Capital Project, Cornwall, CT. 06753.
