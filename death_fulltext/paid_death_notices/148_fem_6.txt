
        SHORE-Carol Tilles. Age 77. Loving wife and better half of Allan for 58 years; beloved mother of Allan Shore Jr., William Shore, and Marylu Simon & devoted motherin-law of Jill Shore; cherished grandmother of Jennifer, Beth, Chris, Michael and Julie; greatgrandmother of three and sister of the late Roy Tilles Jr. Her compassion and zest for life touched all who knew her. Formerly of Scarsdale, N.Y., resident of Palm Beach, Florida for 25 years. Active volunteer for the Helen and Harry Gray Cancer Institute at Good Samaritan Hospital, Hospice of Palm Beach County and the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Helen and Harry Gray Cancer Institute at Good Samaritan Hospital, 1309 North Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, Florida 33401. A memorial service will be held in West Palm Beach, Florida at 2pm on Saturday, May 8 in the Phillips Auditorium of the Good Samaritan Hospital, 1309 North Flagler Drive. Anyone wishing to pay tribute is welcome to participate.
