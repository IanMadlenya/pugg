
        SCHIFFER-Robert L. We honor the remarkable achievements of a just man. His funeral will be held at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations in his memory may be made to Planned Parenthood International and the American Society for Yad Vashem. Selma Schiffer, Suzi & Franklin, Wendy & Michael, Madeline & Alexander
        SCHIFFER-Robert L. It is with deepest sorrow and respect that the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee notes the passing of our former colleague, Robert L. Schiffer. As part of our overseas staff from 1946-1948, Bob saw extensive service in 14 countries where JDC helped revitalize European Jewish life after the Holocaust. We extend our sincerest sympathies to his wife, Zelma, daughter Susi, and his son, Michael. Jonathan W. Kolker, President Michael Schneider Executive Vice President Ambassador Milton A. Wolf Chairman of the Board
        SCHIFFER-Robert. The Staff, Officers and Members of The American Society for Yad Vashem mourn the loss of Bob Schiffer, beloved husband of our devoted Director, Selma Schiffer. He was a committed humanitarian and totally supportive of the Yad Vashem mission. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jersualem. Eli Zborowski, Chairman
        SCHIFFER-Robert. We are deeply saddened by the death of a good friend who was a devoted husband and a loving father and grandfather. May his memory be a blessing. Eli and Diana Zborowski
