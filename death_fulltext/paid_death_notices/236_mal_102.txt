
        GILMAN--Sidney. On September 7, 2006 in Florida. A patriotic WWII Veteran who served on the USS Wasatch in the South Pacific. He practiced law in NYC for 53 years. Beloved husband of the late Doris (nee Byer). Loving father of Howard Gilman, Sandra Kaufman, Max Gilman and Hannah Peretz. Cherished grandfather of Molly, Joseph, Rose, Danielle, Michael, Lindsay Mattan, Ya'ara and Dar. Also survived by many nieces, nephews and other beloved family and friends. Services Sunday, 1PM at Gutterman's Inc., 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre. In lieu of flowers please plant a tree in Israel at www.jnf.org
