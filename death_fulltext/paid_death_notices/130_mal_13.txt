
        NELSON-William J., M.D. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of William J. Nelson, M.D., a former Director of Urology at Roosevelt Hospital, former President of the NYS Medical Society, and former President of the New York Section of the American Urological Association. He exemplified our finest traditions of medical excellence, was devoted to his patients and beloved by colleagues, and will be sorely missed. To his wife, Olive, and his entire family, we offer our condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Ronald C. Ablow, MD President Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
