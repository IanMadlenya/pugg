
        BYRNE-John J. Reverend Monsignor, Roman Catholic Priest, Diocese of Rockville Centre on June 13, 1998. Born July 27, 1901 in Brooklyn, New York. Ordained June 6, 1925. Associate Pastor of Cure of ARS Church, Merrick, 1933-1944, U.S. Navy Chaplain 1944-1946, Founding Pastor of St. Martha R. C. Church, Uniondale 1949-1976. Beloved son of the late Mary Elizabeth & Edward J. Dear brother to Frances Zissel and Matthew J. Predeceased by his loving brother Reverend Henry J. and sisters Marion Corcoran & Helen McKenna. Dear uncle to his nieces and nephews. Reposing at the Lady Chapel in the St. Martha's Parish Center. Visiting Tuesday, 25 P.M. Mass of Transferral on Tuesday, 8 P.M. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 11 AM. Interment St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, New York. For information, Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, East Meadow.
