
        STEINBERG-Saul. The American Institute of Graphic Arts will always celebrate the extraordinary creativity and humor with which Saul Steinberg entertained and intrigued the American public. His contributions were timeless, yet graphic designers nationwide will mourn the loss of this AIGA Gold Medalist of 1963. Michael Bierut, President Richard Grefe, Executive Director On behalf of the Board of Directors and 14,000 graphic designers.
