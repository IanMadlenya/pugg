
        APPLEBY-Roger. On May 2, 2001. Beloved husband of Leigh Westbrook; dear father of Shaun, Amy, and Jed; loving grandfather of Travis, Ethan, and Matthew. Roger studied music at University of Michigan and toured internationally with the Robert Shaw Chorale. An advertising executive in Manhattan for 25 years, he also established Appleby's restaurants in Westchester and The Pet Department Store in Manhattan. His book of essays, ''Out of Left Field'', is forthcoming. Your talent, energy, and love touched many. We will miss you always.
