
        CASSIDY-Cornelia F. Of Kings Park, New York, passed away on December 14. at Bishop Jonathan G. Sherman Episcopal Nursing Home in Smithtown, N.Y., following a brief illness. After retiring as administrator at the South Oaks Hospital in Amityville, N.Y., she served eight years as a national field representative with the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Hospitals. Viewing from 2-4:30 and 7-9 PM today at Hughes Funeral Home, Kings Park. Mass of Christian Burial at 9:30 AM Saturday, December 18, at St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Kings Park, followed by interment at St. Philip Neri Cemetery, Northport, New York.
