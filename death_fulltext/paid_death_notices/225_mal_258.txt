
        HOROWITZ--Murray, age 84, passed away unexpectedly on October 24th, at home with his loving wife of 63 years, Phyllis, at his side. Devoted father of Larry and Steven Horowitz and Barby Friedman, and father-in-law for many years to Iris Klaiman, Lisa Horowitz and the late Laurence Friedman. Loving grandfather of Leslie and Doug Dubrow, Alison and Jeffrey Friedman, the late Kenneth Horowitz, Katherine and David Horowitz, Carrie and Alexander Spiro, Billy Friedman, Scott and Kristin Horowitz, Katie Friedman and Brien Kelley, April Horowitz and Benjamin Horowitz. Proud great-grandfather of Nicholas, Jake, Liam, and Alex. Founder and owner of Muray Textiles of New York. The family patriarch, he will be remembered with love forever. Funeral arrangements to follow.
