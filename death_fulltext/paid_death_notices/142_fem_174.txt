
        SHAW-Carol Henning. Died at home on November 19, 1998 after a long battle with ovarian cancer. Carol was employed by the Style Department of The New York Times. Burial service will be private. There will be a memorial service at St. Luke's in the Fields on December 5 at 2:30 PM. Friends are invited to her home starting on the evening of November 20, 1998. She is survived by Donald H. Shaw, Victoria Louise Shaw-Williamson, Donaly F. Shaw, her loving husband and stepdaughters, Martha Henning and Jean Cockcroft, her mother and sister and Tristan and John Cockcroft, her nephews. Remembrances may be made to Mothers' Voices, 165 West 46th Street, New York, NY 10036. She was the loving stepmother of Pamela Shaw through whom she became involved with Mothers' Voices.
        SHAW-Carol. Mothers' Voices mourns the loss of our dear friend Carol Shaw. She was a devoted stepmother and a brave warrior in the fight against AIDS. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband Donald and her stepdaughters Victoria and Donaly. Sheryl Schwartz, Chair Suzanne Benzer, President
