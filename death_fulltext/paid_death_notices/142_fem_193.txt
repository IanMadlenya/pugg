
        RICHMAN-Janet. Adored wife of David. ''Tiger'' lost her courageous battle against cancer on November 21, 1998. Beloved daughter of Helen and Robert Gross; sister of Debbie Solomon; mother of Jerri Strauss and Steven Albrecht, and devoted nana to Josh and Doug. Service Wednesday, November 25, 10 AM, at Ballard-Durand, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains. Interment at Sharon Gardens in Valhalla. Donations to The American Cancer Society in the hope that a cure will be found for this horrible disease which took the life of someone with such vitality.
