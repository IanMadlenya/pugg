
        LEE-Hon. H. Jordan. Of Long Beach, New York. Died Monday, October 19. Devoted husband of the late Marion. Loving father of Sherry, Stephen, and Barry. A thoughtful, practical, wise and gentle soul, he will be missed by those he leaves behind. A memorial service will be held at Guttermans, 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, 516764-9400, Wednesday, October 21, promptly at 1 P.M.
