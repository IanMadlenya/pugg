
        SHANKER-Albert. The members, officers and staff of the Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union, AFL-CIO, CLC mourn the loss of Albert Shanker, an extraordinary trade union leader and a true champion of working people and their families throughout the world. Our sincere condolences go out to his family and to the members of his union. We will miss him profoundly. Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union, AFL-CIO, CLC Lenore Miller, President Stuart Appelbaum Secretary-Treasurer
        SHANKER-Albert. The Professional Staff Congress mourns the passing of our friend and leader, Albert Shanker. As the nation's leading advocate for public education, Albert Shanker will be sorely missed. The Professional Staff Congress offers its deepest condolences to President Shanker's family. Irwin H. Polishook, President Professional Staff Congress City University of New York
        SHANKER-Albert. Board of Directors of Freedom House mourn the death of long-time board member. Al Shanker fought to expand liberty, ensure racial fairness, and defeat totalitarianism and autocracy. The causes he served, including the struggle for democracy, will be the poorer for his passing. Bette Bao Lord, Chairman Adrian Karatnycky, President
        SHANKER-Albert. The officers and members of UNITE mourn the passing of a historic trade union leader who always fought for future generations. Jay Mazur, President, UNITE, Arthur Loevy, Secy.-Treasurer Bruce Raynor, Exec. V.P. Edgar Romney, Exec. V.P.
        SHANKER-Albert. We mourn the loss of our co-chair, an irreplaceable leader in the struggle for freedom and equality. A colleague, a friend, a brother to Bayard Rustin. The Bayard Rustin Fund, Inc.
