
        EVANS--Dr. Jane. Women of Reform Judaism is deeply saddened by the passing of our visionary and beloved first Executive Director, Dr. Jane Evans. During her 43 years as Executive Director she demonstrated her passionate commitment to Jewish and humanitarian causes. Dr. Evans played a dynamic role in reform and progressive Judaism worldwide and also in her untiring efforts for peace. We will miss Jane Evans, and will remember her warmth and wisdom. Helene H. Waranch, Pres. Shelley Lindauer, Exec. Dir.
