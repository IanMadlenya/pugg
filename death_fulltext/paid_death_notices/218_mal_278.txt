
        PELAVIN--Joseph Y. On March 24, 2005 at his home in Lake Worth, FL.; a great philanthropist and founder of Lark Luggage, respected in his industry as the innovator of nylon soft-sided, airline luggage and many other modern luggage innovations. Beloved husband of the late Alice (nee Droutman), survived by his loving son Lawrence (Joan) and grandson Jason; his loving daughter Cheryl Pelavin. He will be missed by his many colleagues, friends and family. Funeral services will be on Monday, March 28, 2005 at 1PM in the Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, N.J. Information, call Weinstein Garlick Kirschenbaum Chapel, Brooklyn, N.Y. 718-377-4848.
