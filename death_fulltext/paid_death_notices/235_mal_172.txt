
        BREGMAN--Milton. Of Tenafly, NJ. Born July 6, 1913. Died at home August 8, 2006. Founder and former owner of Milton Bregman Manufacturing Co. Beloved husband of the late Harriet Bregman. Devoted brother to his late siblings, Gertrude Coopersmith, Ruth Cohen, Helen Cantor & Seymour Bregman. He is survived by many caring nieces and nephews.
