
        VIGLIANTE-Ralph A. Age 84. Of Elmira, NY. Died Saturday, March 6, 1999, in Elmira, NY. He was preceded in death by his sister, Regina Crea. Ralph is survived by his wife of fifty-seven years, Concetta Vigliante; three children; five grandchildren; and one sister, Theresa Shelzi. Ralph earned his undergraduate and law degrees from Fordham University. He then went to work for Prentice Hall, where he retired in 1982 after thirty-four years of service as their Vice President and Managing Editor of the Tax Division. Donations in Ralph's memory may be made to Saint Joseph's Hospital Foundation, 555 East Market St., Elmira, NY 14901.
