
        PECK-Thomas Slonim. Age 52. Passed away suddenly April 12, 2003, at his home in Boca Raton, FL. Beloved husband of Seena; loving father of Tucker and Harrison; cherished son of Marguerite and Edgar Peck and son-in-law of Mildred Mufson; dear brother of Joel Peck, brother-in-law of Rochelle Peck, Laurence Mufson and Betsy Fillmore, Michael and Laurie Mufson. Memorial service in New York City at 1:00 PM on Sunday, April 27, at the University Club, 1 West 54th Street.
