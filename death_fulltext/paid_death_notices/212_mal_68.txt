
        ZILL--Nicholas. Of Brick, NJ, passed away peacefully on Sunday, September 5 at a nursing home from Parkinson's disease and several strokes. He was 89 years old. Beloved father of Nicholas, Sasha, Mark and Paul, grandfather of ten and greatgrandfather of three. Brother of Anne, Peter and the late Anthony. Former market research executive for the Hearst Corporation, book reviewer for the North County News (Westchester) and past president of Rova Farms in Cassville, NJ. Funeral service will be held at St. Vladimir's Cemetery, Jackson, NJ on Saturday, Sept. 11 at 11 AM.
