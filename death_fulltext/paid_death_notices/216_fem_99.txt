
        WALLIN--Ada Goode. Died January 10, 2005 in Concord, NH. She married John C. Wallin in 1946. They resided in Yonkers, Pleasantville, NY and Houston, TX. She is survived by her children Carole Anne and John Jr., and four grandsons. Funeral Services will be held in the Chapel of Kensico Cemetery, Thursday January 13, at 11 AM.
