
        CAMERON - William A. The Trustees of the South Fork-Shelter Island Chapter of The Nature Conservancy mourn the passing of their Chairman, William A. Cameron. Bill was a tireless crusader for the conservation of his beloved East End Landscape. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family, and hope that they, as we, will celebrate his memory in the lands and waters he helped to preserve. Nancy N. Kelley, Exec. Dir. Judy Bergsma, Polly Bruckmann, Katharine Rayner, Vice Chairs
