
        SANFORD-Lathan Wade, age 63, of Austin, Texas died Monday, January 31, 2000. He graduated from the University of Texas at Austin, where he was a professor in the Department of Theatre and Dance for 31 years. In the Army he was crytographer in special services. As a dancer on Broadway he worked with Bob Fosse and other luminaries. He was a director of many musicals around the country and in Tokyo. Born in Piedmont, Alabama he will be laid to rest in Piedmont in the spring. He is survived by his loving wife, Renata and daughter, Samantha Sanford. A memorial service will be held at 3 P.M. Sunday, February 6, 2000 in the Colonial Chapel of Cook-Walden Funeral Home, 6100 North Lamar, Austin. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Holistic Health Care Center, 410 West Craig, San Antonio, Texas 78212 would be greatly appreciated.
