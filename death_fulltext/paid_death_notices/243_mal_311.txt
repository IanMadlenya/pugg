
        PARKER--Edward H., 91, of Wantagh, NY died peacefully on April 22, 2007. He was born on January 28, 1916 in NYC to the late Augusta Nichols and Edward Parker. In September 1938 he married Lillian McCleery at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church. She predeceased him in 1996. He married Margaret Cray in 1997. Mr. Parker attended high school in NYC and attended Columbia University. Mr. Parker proudly served with the 881st Field Artillary Battalion from April 1943 to the conclusion of the war in 1945. After the war he rejoined Union Carbide Corporation and served as Assistant Treasurer until his retirement in 1982. He is survived by his wife Margaret, sons Bruce and his wife Ellen, Roger and his wife Meta and stepdaughter Priscilla Gray and grandchildren Andrew Parker and Natalie Rebecca Gray. Mr. Parker was a very active and devoted member of The First Presbyterian Church of Levittown for over sixty years. He provided strength and support to many church members and friends. A memorial service will be celebrated on Sunday, April 29, at 2:00pm, at the First Presbyterian Church. In lieu of flowers, donations in his honor may be made to The INN (Interfaith Nutritional Network) at 211 Fulton Avenue, Hempstead, New York, 11550 or the First Presbyterian Church of Levittown at 474 Wantagh Avenue, Levittown, New York 11756. Funeral Arrangements entrusted to Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 603 Wantagh Avenue, Wantagh, NY.
