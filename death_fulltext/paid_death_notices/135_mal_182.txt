
        DARLING-Robert C., M.D. The faculty and staff of the Department of Rehabilitation Medicine, College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University mourn the passing of Dr. Darling on April 13, 1998 and extend our condolences to his family. Dr. Darling was the first Simon Baruch Professor of Rehabilitation Medicine at P&S and contributed greatly to the scientific and academic aspects of Physical Medicine. At the request of the family, gifts may be made to the Robert C. Darling Library, 212-305-5954.
