
        CHERR-Ray (Hecht). Passed away peacefully at home March 22, 2002. An energetic, indomitable and admired woman who will be missed by her family and friends. Devoted wife for over 60 years to the late David Cherr and loved mother of Walter (Sallie) and Donald (Margaret). Grandmother to Diane, Peter, Elisabeth, Jonathan and Gregory. Great grandmother to Benjamin, Amelia, Charlotte, Rebecca, Kimberly and Madeleine. Louis Brandeis High School students and staff will miss a valued worker and friend of almost 70 years. Memorial gathering to be announced. Donations to charity of your choice.
