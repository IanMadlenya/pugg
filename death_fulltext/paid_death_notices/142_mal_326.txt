
        WILSON-Gerry Red. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Gerry Red Wilson on November 22, 1998. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Visitation will be held November 25 and 26 2-5PM and 7-10PM at Frederick's Funeral Home, 192-15 Northern Boulevard, Flushing, N.Y. Funeral services will be held on November 27 at 10AM at St. Anastasia Church, 45-14 245 Street, Douglaston, New York. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec Director
