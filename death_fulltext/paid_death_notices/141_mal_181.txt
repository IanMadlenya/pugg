
        DIEDRICK-Arthur Hill, M.D. On October 16, 1998 of Rye, N.Y. Devoted husband of the late Ann. Father of Arthur Hill Diedrick, Jr. of Litchfield, CT; Dinah Beverly of Brandywine, WV; Lawrence Gilyard Diedrick of Rocky Mount, N.C.; and William Brice Diedrick of Rye, N.Y. Grandfather of 11 grandchildren and 9 great-grandchildren. The family will receive friends Sunday, 5-7 P.M. at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, N.Y. A memorial service will be held Monday, October 19, 1998 at 11 A.M. at Rye Presbyterian Church. Interment service private. Memorial contributions may be made to United Hospital, Boston Post Road, Port Chester, N.Y. 10573.
        DIEDRICK-Arthur H., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Board of Managers, Administration and Medical and Dental Staff of United Hospital Medical Center mourn with profound sorrow the death of our esteemed Trustee, colleague and faithful friend, Arthur Diedrick. A dedicated Physician and a man of great compassion, his life was an outstanding example of selfless devotion to the needs of others. For many years, United Hospital benefited greatly from the inspiration of Dr. Diedrick's distinguished leadership and the wisdom of his counsel. It is with great sadness that we extend our heartfelt condolences to his family.
