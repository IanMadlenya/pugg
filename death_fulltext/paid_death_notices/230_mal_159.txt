
        YEVISH--Irving A. Novelist, literary critic, educator. BA City College and PhD Columbia University. Died suddenly on March 2, 2006 at age 79 in Cape May, NJ. Adored son of the late Dora and Charles Yevish. Loving father of Andrew (Anna), dear brother of Ruth Rose (Victor) and Gloria Schulman (David-deceased), dearly loved uncle and greatuncle. He will be missed by all who knew him.
