
        EARLEY - Sr. Mary Eli zabeth. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, Convent of Mount Saint Vincent, died Monday, January 27, 2003. She was the loving daughter of the late Edward and the late Mary Tynan Earley and the dear sister of the late Edward Earley. She is survived by her sister-in-law, Tess Earley and by cousins, Patricia Brennan and family and John Hoffmann. Reposing at the Convent of Mount Saint Vincent, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, NY on Wednesday 1-8 P.M. Funeral Mass Thursday 10:00 A.M. at the Immaculate Conception Chapel of Mount Saint Vincent. Interment to follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Yonkers, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Ave., Bronx, New York 10471.
