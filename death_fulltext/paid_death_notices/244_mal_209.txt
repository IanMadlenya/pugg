
        KRATZMAN--E. Arthur, M.D., 87, of Freehold, NJ, died May 21, 2007. Surviving are his wife, Alice C. Kratzman and brother, Vincent C. Kratzman of Lahaina, Hawaii. He was predeceased by his first wife, Ruth L. Kratzman in November 1977. Also surviving are sons E.A. Kratzman, III, and his wife, Teresa of Greenwich, CT, and Val A. Kratzman and his wife, Carol of Fairfield, CT; stepchildren, Jane Ettelson and her husband, Charles of Saint Louis, MO, and Thomas O'Brian and his wife, Carolyn of Boulder, CO; and a niece, four grandchildren and two step-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, May 30th at 11am at Applewood Estates, Freehold. The family requests memorial donations to Applewood Residence Assistance Fund, 1 Applewood Dr., Freehold, NJ 07728 or a charity of choice. Funeral services are under the direction of Freeman Funeral Homes, Freehold, NJ.
