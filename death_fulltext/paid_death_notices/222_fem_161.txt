
        NESTER--Loretta. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and employees of Long Beach Medical Center mourn the passing of Loretta Nester, wife of our former Chief Executive Officer, Martin F. Nester, Jr. We will remember Loretta for her efforts on behalf of the Medical Center, her support of Marty and her charming ways. We extend our deepest sympathies to Marty, sons Noel & Matthew, daughters Jeanne & Suzanne and grandchildren Nikolas, Savannah, Sophia, Olivia, Victoria and Loretta Lillian. Alfred I.
