
        EARLY--James M. Died peacefully on January 12, 2004, in Palo Alto CA, aged 81, after a long illness. Dr. Early worked in the field of semiconductor engineering. He will be missed greatly by his wife of 55 years, Mary Agnes, his eight children, eleven grandchildren, and eight brothers and sisters. Contributions can be sent to Catholic Charities of Santa Clara County, 2625 Zanker Road, San Jose, CA 95134.
