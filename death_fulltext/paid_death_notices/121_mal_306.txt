
        LEWISON-Louis, 89, of Miami, FL, passed away on Friday, February 21, 1997. Mr. Lewison was a retired attorney who practiced civil law in New York City for over 40 years. For many years he was executive vice president and general counsel for the Amalgamated Mutual Casualty Co. in Queens, NY. His wife Jerry passed away in December of 1995. He is survived by his sons, Tom and Richard, daughters, Nancy, Diane, Nina, and grandchildren, Deborah, Rebecca, Jed, Emma, Lily; his sister, Helen Ostrow of Worcester, MA and his brotherin-law Boaz Brandmarker, Col., U.S.A. (Ret.) Private services were held. May his spirit be bound up in the bonds of life.
