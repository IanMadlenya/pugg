
        GOULD-Eleanor Greenburg. On March 23, 1998, age 84. She is survived by her husband of 61 years, Milton S. Gould, by her children Patricia Booth, Judson and Jonathan, by her grandchildren Eliza, Alexander, Olivia, Eleanor, and Oliver, and by her brother Robert D. Greenburg. Her life was a blessing upon her family and upon her myriad friends of all ages. Family will be receiving Wednesday from 1 P.M. with service to follow at 2 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1077 Madison Avenue at 81 Street.
