
        GOLDENHAR - Maurice, M.D. Born January 15, 1924. Died September 11, 2001 after a long illness. Loving and beloved husband of Paulette, father of Francine and Edith, grandfather of Lucas and friend to many. Retired family physician and Professor of Family Medicine at SUNY Stony Brook. He dedicated his life to healing and teaching. A memorial will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, please contribute to an organization of your choice.
