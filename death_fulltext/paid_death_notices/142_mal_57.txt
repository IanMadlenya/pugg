
        SMITH-Mayo P. of Chappaqua, Executive Vice President Finance and Administration for Independent Television Network, Inc. (ITN) died of lung cancer on November 4th, 1998 at Northern Westchester Hospital Center. He was 59. Mr. Smith previously held positions at Eastman Radio, Inc., Peat, Marwick, Mitchell & Co., and was Co-Founder of Ryan, Smith & Associates. Mr. Smith was a graduate of Manhattan College and served in the U.S. Marine Corps. Mr. Smith was an Usher at the Church of St. John and St. Mary in Chappaqua, Cub Scout Master and Member, Board of Managers, Applehill Farm Condominium. Mr. Smith is survived by his wife Virginia, sons Mayo Thomas and Edward Patrick, his mother Ella Marie Smith and Brothers Gary, David and Kevin. Resting, Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY where family will receive friends Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Sat. 10 AM The Church of St. John and St. Mary. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Donations would be appreciated to the American Cancer Society, Westchester Division of The Church of St. John and St. Mary.
