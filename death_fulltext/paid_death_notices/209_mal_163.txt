
        KANIK--Dr. William. Age 76, of Tampa, FL, formerly of Long Island, died Friday in Tampa where he resided for six years. He was an optometrist in Brooklyn for over 40 years. He was a past president of Temple Beth El in NY. Survived by his beloved wife of 45 years Phyllis, sons and daughters-in-law Drs. Andrew and Kerri Kanik of Monroe, CT, and Dr. Keith and Dorit Kanik of Israel and five grandchildren. Funeral services in Tampa, FL. Memorial contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. Information Beth David Funeral Chapel, Tampa, phone 813-874-3330.
