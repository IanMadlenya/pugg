
        HIRSCH-Dr. Joseph. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University mourns the loss of one of the founding members of our faculty and our first assistant dean for student affairs (1955-1967). We are grateful for the notable contributions he made to the growth and development of our institution in its formative years. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife, Eva, and the family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
