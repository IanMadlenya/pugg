
        FISHELSON--Dorothy of East Hampton New York passed away peacefully at home from pancreatic cancer on Saturday 16 June, 2007. Mrs. Fishelson was an active advocate of education and opportunity throughout her life. Most recently she served on the Board of Directors for the Child Development Center of the Hamptons, the Retreat (a shelter for abused women), League of Women Voters and the East Hampton Housing Authority. Born Dorothy Seidman on June 10, 1925 in the Bronx, she majored in Math at Hunter College and obtained her MS degree from Columbia University. She served on the Central Sewanhaka School District Board of Education for 15 years, eight of which she was president. She earned her central administrative certificate at 53 and served as Assistant Superintendent for Personnel at the East Meadow School District until her retirement in 1993. Mrs. Fishelson is survived by her three daughters Caren Gough, Barbara Fishelson and Hollis Fishelson-Holstine; her sister, Beatrice Wellens, her four grandchildren Farrah Zuckerman, Keith Gough, Megan Holstine and Kaya Kachigian; her nephew -Richard Wellens and her niece Lorraine Wellens. The funeral service will be held at the Jewish Center of the Hamptons (www.JCOH.org) on 11:30 am on Sunday, 17 June 2007.
