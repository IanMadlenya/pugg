
        BROWN-Leland S., 92. Of Bedford, New York. Died peacefully at home on March 17. Loving husband of Mary Murray Brown, who predeceased him by only one year. Mr. Brown is survived by his sister Alberta Brown of Santa Barbara, Cal; two children, Morgan S. Brown of Louisville, KY., and Janet B. Jussel of Bedford, four grandchildren, Sarah B. Robbins, John S. Bradbury, Jr., Catherine M. Jussel and David S. Jussel, and one great grandchild, Henry Robbins. Contributions to The Trustees of Reservations, Chappaquiddick Committee, Box 2106, 18 Helen Avenue, Vineyard Haven, MA. 02568-2106.
