
        TISI--Jane Hall. On December 27, 2003. A longtime Katonah, NY, resident. Died peacefully after a courageous battle against cancer. She was 67. She is survived by her loving husband of 44 years, Charles, and her sister Bonnie McGlasson of Hendersonville, NC. She was born in Barrington, IL, and attended local Chicago schools. She graduated from Stuart Hall and Brown University. Upon graduation, she was affiliated with an ophthalmology practice. A member of the Bedford Golf and Tennis Club, she was involved in numerous tennis activities. Jane loved her family, friends and the wonderful world of gardens and possessed a great appreciation and awareness of the importance of animals in her everyday life. She was devoted to her pets and was an early member of the Golden Outreach Program which provides animal comfort to the sick and elderly. Her kindness and gentleness will be greatly missed. A memorial visitation will be held at Clark Associates Funeral Home, 4 Woods Bridge Rd., Katonah, on Monday, December 29, from 4-8 PM. Burial services will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Friends of John Jay Homestead, P.O. Box 148, Katonah, NY 10536 or the Northern Westchester Hospital.
