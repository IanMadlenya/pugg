
        NONAS--Gerson, M.D. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and the Department of Medicine of Lenox Hill Hospital express their profound sorrow at the loss of Gerson Nonas, M.D., a distinguished member of our medical staff for nearly a quarter of a century. Dr. Nonas, a respected physician who was deeply committed to his patients, was dedicated to the highest standards of medicine. The entire Lenox Hill Hospital family honors his memory and we extend our deepest sympathies to his family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO James J. Richter, M.D., Ph.D., President, Medical Board Robert A. Phillips, M.D., Ph.D Chairman, Dept of Medicine >>AD#
