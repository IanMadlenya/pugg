
        MARCUS-GROSS-Dr. Henriette. 82, of Greenwich, CT, on July 1. Dr. Marcus was a pioneering physician and beloved wife and mother, remembered with love by family, friends, patients and students. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Stanley Gross, of Greenwich; her son, Dr. Charles Gross, of Canton, OH; and daughter, Dr. Charlotte Gross, of Raleigh, NC. Memorial services will be held Sunday, July 5, at 3 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mt. Vernon, NY. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to the Humane Society, the Nature Conservancy, or any group protecting animals.
