
        KEAN-John J., Msgr. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of St. Peter Claver Church, Brooklyn, on June 13. Reposing in Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221st Street, Queens Village, on Wednesday, June 16, from 1 to 5 P.M. Mass of Transferral at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass at St. Therese of Lisieux Church, 1281 Troy Avenue, Brooklyn, Thursday, June 17, at 10:30 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
