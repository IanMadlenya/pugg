
        NARDUCCI--William, 77, of New York City--playwright, lyricist, novelist, actor, dancer. Died January 19, 2004. In original casts of Broadway's ''Brigadoon'' and ''Gentlemen Prefer Blondes'', known as William Narcy. Beloved friend of dozens of Actors Studio members and theater community. Survived by aunt, Edith Yacolucci and two cousins, Toni Iacolucci and Sandee Stanton. Kind, gentle, witty, and loyal, admired by all for his courage in dealing with his blindness. He will be truly missed. A memorial service will be held--date and place to be announced.
