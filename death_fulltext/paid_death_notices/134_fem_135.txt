
        MOESER-Ursula. Died March 14, 1998, after a short illness. A cultured lady, always cheerful, who will be much missed by her many friends in her Seaford and Massapequa communities, and also by her Church community of our Redeemer Lutheran Church. She was a Scientific Translator who spoke many languages. She had a lively, literary talent. She was a lover of animals, particularly her cats. She survived by seven years, her late husband, Otto Moeser. Ursula's death is a terrible loss. Reposing at the Charles G. Schmitt Funeral Home, 3863 Merrick Rd, Seaford (2 traffic lights east of Route 135). Religious Service on Wednesday 10 AM, our Redeemer Lutheran Church, Seaford. Cremation Private. Hours Tuesday 2-5 and 7-9:30 P.M.
