
        FRIEDMAN-Marvin, age 75, Boca Raton, Florida, formerly of North Bellmore, NY, passed away December 13, 1998. Beloved husband of Elaine, loving father of Richard and Paul, dear brother of Jean Rosenbaum and Jeanette Chazen, cherished grandfather of Jeffrey, Lisa, Gregory, Douglas and Spencer. Services at Parkside Chapel Queens Blvd. at 66 Ave. Forest Hills, 1:00PM Tuesday, Dec. 15.
        FRIEDMAN-Marvin. A man of stature who lived life as it should be lived. Without restraints and without regrets. His opinions on every and any topic will be sorely missed. Farewell pal. The Kisseloffs
