
        GOTTESMAN - Gertrude. Passed away on August 30, at the age of 92. Beloved wife of Dr. James Gottesman, mother or Dr. Max and Dr. David Gottesman, and grandmother of Evan, Brian, Betsy, Zachary, Julie, Scott, Bart, Krissy and Jill. She was a teacher and a guidance counselor in the New York City Public School system. Services at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Ave, Tuesday, September 3, at 10 AM.
