
        CORBETT-Rev. James B., CSP. Age 88, on March 7, 1997 in Minneapolis, MN. A native of Toronto, Canada, Fr. Corbett was ordained a Paulist priest on June 15, 1935 at St. Paul the Apostle Church in New York City. He served in Paulist parishes in Chicago and Minneapolis and on mission assignments in Toronto and San Francisco. A wake will be held at Saint Lawrence Church in Minneapolis on Thursday, March 13, from 6 to 8 PM followed by the funeral liturgy at 8 PM on March 13. Interment will be at Mount Paul, Oak Ridge, NJ on Friday, March 14.
