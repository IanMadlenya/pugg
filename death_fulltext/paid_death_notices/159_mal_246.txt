
        BARANOF-Gene. Beloved husband of Iris. Heroic father of Marla and the late Lisa. Brother of Alfred and Ruth Ann. He will be missed by all who loved him and inspired by his courage. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th St., on Thursday, April 27, 11:45 AM.
        BARANOF-Gene. Congregation Rodeph Sholom mourns the death of our devoted longtime member, Gene Baranof. We will miss his indomitable spirit, his passionate devotion to family and friends, and his courage under affliction. Our deepest sympathy is extended to his beloved wife, Iris, his daughter, Marla, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Senior Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
