
        CHARNAY-Leonard, 81, of Lake Worth, FL. Formerly of Hewlett, NY, passed away on September 8th, 1997 after a long illness. Beloved husband of Jean. Adoring father of Patricia and Michael Gorman and Richard and Ruth Charnay. Loving grandfather of Matthew and Aaron. Dear brother of Bernard Charnay and Shirley Siegel. He will be missed by his family and friends.
