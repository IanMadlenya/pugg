
        RULON-MILLER-Judith Rollinson, 53, died of breast cancer on March 1, 1997 in her home in Kingston, N.J. She is survived by her husband Patrick; daughter Sarah of New York City; and son Ian Davis of Breckenridge, Colorado. A memorial service will be held at 3PM, on Friday, March 5 at Trinity Church in Princeton, N.J. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Martin House, Box 1029, Trenton, New Jersey 08606 or to The Princeton Family Center For Education, Vandeventer Avenue, Princeton, New Jersey 08540.
