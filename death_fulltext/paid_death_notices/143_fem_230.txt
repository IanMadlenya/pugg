
        CASS-Claire. Yeshiva University and its affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary mourn the loss of a cherished member of the Jewish community, loving mother and grandmother, and sister of Max Grill, a YU Benefactor and honorary alumnus, who endowed the Max and Marion Grill Deanship at RIETS. Condolences also to her sister, Betty Fiebert, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Judah Feinerman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees Rabbi Zevulun Charlop Max and Marion Grill Dean, RIETS
