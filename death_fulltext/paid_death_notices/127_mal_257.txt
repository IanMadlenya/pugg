
        ROSENZWEIG-William. The Metropolitan Parking Association notes with profound sorrow the death of William Rosenzweig, esteemed member of our Board of Directors for many years. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Anne, his daughter Linda, his sonin-law Sandy, his sisters, as well as to his grandchildren. Sonny Schwartz, President Hugh M. Heller, General Counsel
