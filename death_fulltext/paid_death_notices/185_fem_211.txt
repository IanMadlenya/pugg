
        LAUDER-Jessie (nee Jessie Cook) of Danville, Kentucky, died peacefully Tuesday night, June 25, in Palm Beach, FL, a month before her 93rd birthday. She is survived by her daughter, Barbara Bedford Mathieu of Southampton, New York; a son; five grandchildren; and five great grandchildren. She was predeceased by Edward T. Bedford of Greenwich, CT; Charles M. DuPuy of Ligonier, PA; and George Lauder of Greenwich, Ct and Watch Hill, R.I. Mrs. Lauder was a member of the Everglades Club and the Beach Club in Palm Beach; was an Honorary Member of the Indian Harbor Yacht Club in Greenwich, Ct; and a member of the Colonial Dames of America. Funeral Services will be held privately.
