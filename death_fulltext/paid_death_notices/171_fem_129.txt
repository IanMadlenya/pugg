
        RUBINGER-Susan. The Jewish Community Relations Council of New York extends its heartfelt condolences to Bob and our past Board member, Nancy Rubinger, on the passing of their beloved daughter, Susan. A former staff member, Susan will be remembered for her kindness and devotion to others. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Gedale B. Horowitz, Pres. Michael S. Miller, Exec. V.P.
