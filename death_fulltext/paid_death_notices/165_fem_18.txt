
        OSEAS-Hilda N. 96, formerly of The Majestic, NYC, died September 29, 2000, in Rhinebeck, NY. Retired Bronx principal for NYC Public Schools and life member of the Gotham Chapter of Hadassah. Her husband, Israel Oseas, who died in 1986, was former president of Park Avenue Synagogue and the Board of Overseers of the Jewish Theological Seminary. Mother of Jonathan Oseas of Hurley, NY. Funeral services will be conducted on Monday, at 11 AM, at Simpson-Gaus Funeral Home, Kingston. Burial in Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale. Memorials to Gotham Chapter of Hadassah.
