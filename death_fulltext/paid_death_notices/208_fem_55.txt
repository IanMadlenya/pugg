
        KANNER--Madeline T., 88. Born in Brooklyn. A source of love, guidance and good humor, died in her home in North Miami Beach, survived by her husband, Dr. Morris Kanner, 93; sons, Drs. Steven, Joshua and Ronald Kanner; grandchildren, Laura, Daniel, Seth, Tracey, Sarah, Rebecca, and Michael; great-grandchildren, Alexander, Ryan, Lindsay and Danielle.
