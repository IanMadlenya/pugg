
        MUNDHEIM-Marshall A., M.D. 63, of New York City. Passed away peacefully on April 3, 2000 at his residence. Beloved husband of Ingegerd and cherished father of Marie-Elizabeth. He served on the faculty of NYU Medical Center from 1967 to the present in the Department of Rheumatology. In addition, he maintained a private practice in Manhattan in Rheumatology and Internal Medicine. He will be remembered for his love of teaching, medicine, and flying. Friends may call at St. James' Church, 865 Madison Ave. at 71 St. Thursday April 6, 7-9 PM. Funeral services at St. James' Church on Friday, April 7, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be made to the Marshall A. Mundheim Fund for Education, C/O Dr. Steven Abramson, Hospital for Joint Diseases, 301 East 17 Street, New York, New York 10003.
        MUNDHEIM-Marshall, M.D. We deeply mourn the loss of our friend and colleague, Marshall Mundheim, Professor of Clinical Medicine at The NYU School of Medicine. He will be remembered for his kindness, his integrity, and his dedication to the profession of medicine. As one of our finest clinicians and teachers, Marshall was an unwavering role model for members of our faculty. We recall with gratitude and appreciation his genuine commitment and dedication to his patients and to generations of students. His legacy in rheumatology will be carried on by those with whom he worked and taught. We extend our sincere sympathy to his wife, Ingegerd, and, daughter, Marie Elizabeth. Gerald Weissmann, M.D., Division of Rheumatology NYU School of Medicine Steven Abramson, M.D., Dept of Rheumatology Hospital For Joint Diseases
