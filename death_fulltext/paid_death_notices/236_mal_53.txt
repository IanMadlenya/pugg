
        MAXON--Ralph, on September 5, 2006 in Boynton Beach, FL. Former longtime resident of Port Chester, NY. A Certified Public Accountant in New York City and Florida for 70 years, he was still a trusted financial advisor until his death. Predeceased by his beloved wife of 66 years,Yetta. Survived by his children Andrew Maxon (Carol), Barbara Benerofe, Ilene Finder (Kenneth), grandchildren Jodi Gubner (Corey), Jeffrey Maxon (Julie), Jamie Cohen (Jason), Craig, Samantha and Gary Benerofe and Barry and Marc Finder, and numerous great-grandchildren, nieces and nephews. Predeceased by his brother Murray Maxon, and sister Lillian Tunick. Services will be held Thursday at Congregation Knesses Tifereth Israel, 575 King St, Port Chester at 12:30pm.
