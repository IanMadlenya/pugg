
        O'GRADY-Thomas B. Born Chaelis, Washington, June 24, 1926 passed away on Monday, March 20, 2000. He was a retired executive of UST, Inc. where he had a long and celebrated career for 42 years, retiring as Vice Chairman of the corporation in 1991. In recent years, Mr. O'Grady devoted his time to philanthropic endeavors. He raised money for his Alma Mater, St. Martin's College, Lacy, Washington. His generosity included the Music and Arts Building and the O'Grady Library. He is survived by his wife Kathleen O'Grady, and his children Thomas P. O'Grady, Gail O'Grady (daughter-in-law), Kathleen O'Grady Brown, George Brown (son-inlaw), and Sheila O'Grady Edwards and four grandchildren, Shannon O'Grady, Sean O'Grady, Kasey Edwards, Kristen Edwards. The viewing will take place at Collins Funeral Home 92 East Avenue, Norwalk, CT, 203-866-0747 from 4:00 to 8:00 P.M. Wednesday, March 22, and Thursday, March 23. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Friday, March 24, at 10:00 A.M. in St. Thomas The Apostle Church, 203 East Avenue, Norwalk (for directions call 203866-0747). In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Thomas O'Grady Scholarship Fund for Students in The Arts at St. Martin's College, at 5300 Pacific Avenue SE, Lacy, Washington 98503-1297 or 360-438-4366, Young Composers Program at the New York Philharmonic Orchestra and Musical Theatre Development at Playwrights Horizons, New York City or the charity of your choice.
        O'GRADY - Thomas B. The trustees and staff of The Aldrich Museum of Contemporary Arts in Ridgefield, CT deeply mourn the loss of Thomas B. O'Grady, friend and supporter of the Museum.
