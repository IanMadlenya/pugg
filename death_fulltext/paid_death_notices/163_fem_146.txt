
        HORAN-Helen M. 89. Of Woodbury, CT. on Monday, August 14, 2000. Beloved wife of Harold A. Horan for 67 years. She was the daughter of the late Michael and Ellen (Farrell) Feeney. Formerly of Brooklyn, she moved to Woodbury 25 years ago. She was the beloved mother of two sons, Robert A. Horan of Woodbury & Richard A. Horan of Indianapolis, IN. Also survived by 2 nephews, Thomas and Christopher. She was predeceased by a son, Harold F. Horan. Mass was held at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church in Washington, CT. Burial in New North Cemetery, Woodbury, CT.
