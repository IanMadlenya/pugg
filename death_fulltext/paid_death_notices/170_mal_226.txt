
        CONWAY-Bernard Francis, J.S.C. died on March 18, 2001. Beloved husband of Catherine W. Brown Conway. Loving father of Colleen, Maureen, Eileen and Jennifer. Loving grandfather of Meghan, Logan, Brian, Will and Ryan. Served as legal advocate for his clients faithfully for 34 years. Served his colleagues in the legal profession as trustee, officer and president of the Morris County Bar Association and as trustee, officer and president officer of the New Jersey State Bar Association. Served the public as a Judge of the Superior Court of New Jersey since 1995. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Thursday, March 22 at the Church of the Assumption, Morristown, NJ at 9:30 AM. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Morris twp., NJ Hours of visitation at the Doyle Funeral Home, 106 Maple Ave., Morristown, NJ on Wednesday 2-4 & 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Campaign for Justice, C/O Thomas Makin, Director of Development Legal Services of NJ, 100 Metroplex Dr., P.O. Box 1357 Edison, NJ 08818.
