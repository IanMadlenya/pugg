
        SCHWARTZ-Adria. Age 56, on January 11, at home. Beloved by family, friends, patients, students and colleagues for her intellect, warmth and wit. Adria practiced psychoanalysis in Manhattan for 25 years. She leaves behind partner Rima Shore, son Alex Harlan Schwartz, brother Stephen Schwartz and her much loved extended family, Daniel Greenwald, Laura Mamo and David Mamo. Services 11 AM, today, January 13, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
