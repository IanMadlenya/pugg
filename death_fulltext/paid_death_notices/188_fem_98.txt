
        BURT-Mary Canedy. Died September 12, 2002. Born in Grinnell, IA, on June 28, 1909, she lived in San Francisco most of her life. A pioneer in her field, in 1969 she co-founded Burt Children's Center, a residential treatment center for severely emotionally disturbed children. She is survived by her beloved niece and nephew, friends, students and colleagues. A celebration of her life will be held in San Francisco at the Center on September 29 at 3 PM. All are welcome.
