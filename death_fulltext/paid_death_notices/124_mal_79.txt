
        GIACALONE-John Paul, passed away on May 5, 1997. At the time of his death, he was a corporate attorney in private practice in New York City. Mr. Giacalone was born and raised on Long Island. He was one of six siblings starting life as a New Years Eve baby in 1961. Mr. Giacolone graduated magna cum laude from The State University of New York at Stony Brook, where he received degrees in English and History in 1983. He than went on to receive his Juris Doctorate from Fordham University in 1986. Mr. Giacalone was also a member of the distinguished MENSA, a member of the New York Bar Association, and also was admitted to practice before the Eastern District of the United States Supreme Court in 1995. He is survived by his mother, Anna Giacalone and by his brothers and sisters, Paul, Tammy, Rosanna, Vito, and Louis. He is also survived by his grandmother, Sadie Tumminia and best friend, Gary Gillis. This obituary was written by dear friend, Pat Canole. For further information, call (212) 799-9072.
