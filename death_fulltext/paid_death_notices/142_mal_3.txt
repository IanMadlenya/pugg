
        DANZIG-Max. Age 98, on October 31, 1998. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Loving husband of the late Edith Danzig. Devoted father of Dorothy Danzig Masch and the late Judith Danzig. Much loved grandfather of Julia, Jason and Rachel Masch. A kind and caring leader in his community, and a true gentleman. His ready smile, infectious laugh and melodic voice will be greatly missed. Funeral service November 1, 1998, 11 AM, at Hellman's Memorial Chapels, 15 State St., Spring Valley, NY.
