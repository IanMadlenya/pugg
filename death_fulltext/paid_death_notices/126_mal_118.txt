
        JIRAK-Mary. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation note with sorrow the passing of Mary Jirak, beloved mother-in-law of Jeff Gural, an esteemed member of our Real Estate Executive Committee. Jeff and his father, Aaron Gural, are long-standing volunteers. Aaron is past chairman of our Real Estate Division, and father and son were 1995 co-honorees of the Real Estate and Allied Trades Division. The leadership and staff of the Real Estate Division are indebted to the family's dedication and support, and express their condolences to Mary's daughter, Paula Jirak Gural, and to the entire Gural and Jirak families. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Charles R. Borok, Chair, 1998 Campaign Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        JIRAK-Mary. On behalf of our entire organization it is with profound sorrow that we express our sincere condolences to our Vice Chairman, Jeff Gural and his wife Paula Jirak Gural on the untimely loss of Paula's dear mother Mary. We also wish to extend our heartfelt sympathy to the rest of the Gural/Jirak families. Newmark & Company Real Estate Inc. William Cohen Ronald Goldberger Barry Gosin Aaron Gural Ira Fishman James Kuhn Arthur Lerner Marlene Post and Arthur Rosenbloom.
