
        PETERS-Preston Hodgsdon. Passed away Saturday, June 8, 2002 after a short battle with cancer. He was the only son of Marjorie Hodgsdon Stillman and H. Preston Peters. A graduate of Williston Academy and Columbia University, he served in the U.S. Army for three years. He is survived by his daughter Marjorie Stewart Peters of Alexandria, VA. Memorial service will be held at 3PM on Saturday, June 15th at 2 Puddlers Lane, Falls Village, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the David M. Hunt Library Endowment Fund, Falls Village, CT. 06031.
