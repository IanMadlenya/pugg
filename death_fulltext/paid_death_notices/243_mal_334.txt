
        EMERY--John C., Jr., of New Canaan, CT on April 19, 2007. Retired Chairman of the Board and CEO of Emery Air Freight, a company created by his father. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the St. Croix Foundation for Community Development, 72 Flag Drive, St. Croix., U.S. V.I. 00820 or Person-to-Person, 1864 Post Road, Darien, CT 06820.
