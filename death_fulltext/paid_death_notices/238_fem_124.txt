
        RICHNER--Edith, 79, of Rockville Centre, NY and Boca Raton FL. Beloved mother, grandmother and accomplished businesswoman, died on Friday, November 17, 2006 after succumbing to Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease a rare brain disorder. She was predeceased by her devoted husband of 51 years Robert. Together with her husband, she was the founder and longtime co-publisher of Richner Communications, publishers of the Nassau Herald in the Five Towns, which grew to become the 15-paper Herald Community Newspaper group. She was the daughter of the late Rose and Morris Murofchick. She is survived by her sisters, Evelyn Aaronson of Boca Raton, FL. Shirley and husband Daniel Kammin of Wycoff NJ, her children, Clifford of Manhattan, Stuart and wife Nancy of Rockville Centre, and Donald and wife Carole of Stamford, CT, and grandchildren Zachary, Gregory, Lucas, Katie and Willie. Funeral Sunday 2:00pm, Central Synagogue of Nassau County, 430 Demott Ave, Rockville Centre, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Edith Richner Memorial Fund of Partners in Dignity, 6900 Jericho Turnpike, Syosset NY 11791
