
        SMITH-Moira Ann (nee Reddy), 38, of Brooklyn, NY, died heroically in the line of duty on September 11, 2001. Beloved wife of James. Loving mother of Patricia Mary. Cherished sister of Mary Reddy. Adored godmother of Allison Reddy. Dear niece and cousin. Devoted friend to many. Proud New York City Police Officer. She will be greatly missed. Friends may call at the Bay Ridge Manor, 476 76th St., Brooklyn, NY, on Wednesday, February 13, 2002, 5-10 PM. A memorial mass will be celebrated at the Cathedral of St. Patrick, 51st Street and 5th Avenue, NYC, on Thursday, February 14th, 2 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Moira Smith Fund, C/O The National Charitable Services, Attn: Kenneth C.
