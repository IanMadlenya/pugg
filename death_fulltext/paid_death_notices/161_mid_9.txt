
        KUZMICK-Jerome F. Died peacefully at home on Saturday, June 10, 2000 in Clifton, New Jersey. Beloved husband of Barbara Kuzmick and the late Sophia S. Kuzmick, he leaves behind five children: Kathleen K. Hansell of Chicago, Illinois, Danielle K. Brown and her husband Warren of Albuquerque, New Mexico, Christine K. Wash and her husband John of Palos Verdes Estates, California, Jane K. Izod and her husband Thomas of Midlothian, Virgina, and James J. Kuzmick and his wife Judith of Great Falls, Virginia. He was the devoted grandfather of Cristina Hansell Chuen and Peter Hansell; David and Jennifer Nash; Nathan, Andrew, and Emily Izod; Elizabeth, Katherine, and Mary Kuzmick, and great-grandfather of Charlotte Chuen. He was also the cherished stepfather of Luis Reyes, II, and his wife Sandra, and step grandfather of Luis, III, and Christopher Reyes. Mr. Kuzmick is also survived by three brothers, Eugene of Fall Brook, California, Paul of Essex Fells, New Jersey, and Adrian of Port Orange, Florida. Funeral services will be held on Friday, June 16, 2000, at 10 A.M. at Union Congregational Church, 176 Cooper Avenue, Upper Montclair, with internment at Mount Hebron Cemetery. Family and friends may pay their respects at Bizub-Quinlan Funeral Home at 1313 Van Houten Avenue, Clifton, New Jersey 07013, on Wednesday, June 14, 2000, from 7-9 P.M., and Thursday, June 15, 2000, from 2-4 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Clifton Boys and Girls Club, 822 Clifton Avenue, Clifton, N.J. 07013, or to The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art, Cooper Square, New York, New York 10003-7120.
