
        DESCH-Carl W. On July 18, 2001 of Garden City, NY. Beloved husband of the late Katharine W. Loving father of Carol, Carl Jr. and Barbara and grandfather of 7 grandchildren. Also survived by his sister Margaret Hanft. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave. at 12th Street, Garden City 2-4 & 7-9 PM Friday. Religious service Garden City Community Church, 9AM Saturday. Interment private. In lieu of flowers donations to the American Red Cross appreciated.
        DESCH-Carl. Members of the Board of Trustees and staff of the American Red Cross in Greater New York note with sadness the loss of Carl Desch, a longtime Red Cross friend and supporter from the 1960's to the present. He served on the Executive Board, he chaired the New York Chapter Board, and he served as Treasurer of the American Red Cross in Greater New York for nearly a decade. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. American Red Cross in Greater New York Jonathan O'Herron, Chairman Robert M. Bender, Jr., Chief Executive Officer
