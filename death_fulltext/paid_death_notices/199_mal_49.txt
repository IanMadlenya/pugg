
        QUEALLY--Francis X. of Tequesta, Florida, formerly of Scarsdale, New York and Greenwich, Connecticut, died on August 4th, 2003. Born on July 10, 1927 in Woodside, NY, he was the son of Michael J. and Kathleen Ronalyne Queally. He is survived by his wife, Marietta Egan Queally, his children Marylou Salvati of New Canaan, CT, Francis X. Queally, Jr., of Darien, CT, W. Doyle Queally, of Riverside, CT, Joanne Ecclesine of Wellesley, MA, and Paul Brian Queally, of New Canaan, CT. He is also survived by 16 grandchildren. His first wife, Claire Doyle Queally predeceased him in 1991. Family will receive friends at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York on Wednesday from 4-9PM. Funeral Mass will be on Thursday 9:45 AM at St. Michael's Church, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to Xavier High School, 30 West 16th Street, New York, New York 10011 would be appreciated.
