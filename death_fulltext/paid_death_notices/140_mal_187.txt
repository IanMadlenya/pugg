
        CAMERON-Cliff. On September 13, 1998 of Forest Hills, New York. Organist Emeritus First Church of Christ, Scientist, Forest Hills after forty years of service. The highlights of his 60 year career include: recognition as an original organist with NBC in the early days of television soap operas, as well as engagements in Atlanta, Georgia, The Hollywood Bowl, California & Radio City Music Hall, New York. He is survived by his sister Lucille Benash, Chicago, Illinois, and his nephew Carmon Hoppe, Oshkosh, Wisconsin. A memorial service will be planned.
