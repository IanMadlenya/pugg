
        CHESTER--Herta (nee Dulken), beloved wife of Randolph, loving mother of Pamela (Jeffrey) Gurock, proud grandmother of Eli (Sheri), Rosie, and Michael, adoring great-grandmother of Audrey and Mira, died January 9. Memorial contributions to Hatzalah of the West Side, 131 West 86th Street.
        CHESTER--Herta. Heartfelt condolences to Randolph and daughter Pamela. A Silver Founder of the Medical Center together with her husband, Herta was a generous, warm woman whose open hand epitomized the highest standards of Jewish tradition. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Amer. Comm. for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem
        CHESTER--Herta. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the wife of our member, Randolph Chester. May his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion & Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Joseph S. Kaplan, President Paulette Papilsky, Sisterhood
