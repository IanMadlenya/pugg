
        ADISMAN-I. Kenneth, DDS. The members and fellows of the Greater New York Academy of Prosthodontics share the grief of the family of Dr. I. Kenneth Adisman who passed away on Sunday, March 5, 2000. Dr. Adisman was a past President of the Academy and a recipient of its achievement award. His many contributions to the field of dentistry will surely be remembered. We are saddened by the loss of our esteemed associate and extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, March 29, 2000 at The New York University College of Dentistry, 345 East 24th Street, New York City at 5:00 P.M.
        ADISMAN-I. Kenneth, DDS. The Advisory Board of the International Circuit Courses, Inc. deeply regrets the passing of I. Kenneth Adisman, DDS, Director Emeritus. His foresight, leadership, professionalism and friendship will be greatly missed. Lloyd Landa, DDS Director, ICC, Inc.
