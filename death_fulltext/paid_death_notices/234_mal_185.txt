
        KAYE--Robert., M.D. Died June 14 after a prolonged illness. Beloved husband of Ellen Eskin. Father of Elizabeth, Peggy, Hillary, Tony and Stanford (Chip). Grandfather of Nora, Natalie and Vivian. Brother of Betty Kaye Taylor and Stephen Kaye. Highly regarded for his contribution to the study and treatment of juvenile diabetes. He was loved and will be missed by many.
