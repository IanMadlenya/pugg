
        GREENSTEIN - Marion, died peacefully on February 25 at home of Cancer, age 71. Devoted wife to late husband Jack. Beloved mother of Nina and David. Loving grandmother of Spencer, Harry and Jessie. We will always remember her abounding energy, enthusiasm and zest for living a full life. Memorial service Sunday, March 4, at 1:15 P.M., Central Synagogue, 123 E. 55 St, NYC. In lieu of flowers donations to: Hospice of Wake County, 919-828-0890.
