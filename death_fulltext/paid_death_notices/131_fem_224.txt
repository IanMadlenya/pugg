
        SIMONSON - Mildred (Simone). Age 99, on December 27, 1997, beloved wife of the late Peter, loving mother of Mimi Ganz, Richard, and Harris, dear sister of Lillian, devoted mother-in-law of Stanley Ganz, Dee Simonson and Vicki Simonson, adoring grandmother of Fredda, Larry, Marc, and Nancy, great grandmother of Paul, Jay, Ira, Laura, Shana, and Serena. She was a renowned painter and sculptor. She was strong, caring, independent and generous to us all. We will miss her forever. Graveside service Sunday 1:30 PM at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, L.I.
