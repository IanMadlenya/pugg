
        GOLD--Neil Bernard, age 75 of Chatham Center, NY on April 23, 2007 at Albany, NY. Born in Brooklyn, he was a Specialist for the New York Stock Exchange. He is survived by his wife Joan (Levick) Gold, son Andrew Gold, daughter Sandy Sidoti (Michael), daughter-in-law Jill Gold, brother Mark. Grandfather of Bryan and Lee Gold, Jessica and Melissa Sidoti. Uncle of Bonni, Cindi and Tammi. Friends may call at the Levine Memorial Chapel, 649 Washington Ave., Albany on Tuesday April 24, 2007 from 4:30pm-6pm. A service will be held at 6:00pm. Interment Wednesday, New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. For information call Levine Memorial Chapel 518438-1002.
