
        GETZENDANNER-Susan M. On September 11, 2001 in the World Trade Center. She was Vice President at Fiduciary Trust Company. Susan is survived by her parents, Betty Ann and Joe Getzendanner, formerly of Shaker Heights, OH, now of Newtown, PA; brother Thomas Getzendanner, of Summit, NJ; sisters Lydia Kriso of Great Falls, VA, and Martha Lingen of Nevada City, CA; and 11 nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held Thursday, October 18, at 2:00 PM at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave. at 50th St., Manhattan. Memorial contributions may be made to Wells College, Aurora, New York 13026.
