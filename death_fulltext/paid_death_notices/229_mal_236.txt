
        MISTRETTA - Charles. Of Ridge, NY, formerly of Searingtown, NY. Passed away on Saturday, February 18, 2006. He will be greatly missed by his wife Rose. Besides his wife, he is survived by his three children: Dorothy Turano, Carole Ross and Martin Mistretta, as well as nine grandchildren and 11 great grandchildren. His wake will be held on Tuesday, February 21 at O.B. Davis Funeral Home, Route 25A, Miller Place, NY from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass will be held at St. Mark's R.C. Church, Shoreham, NY at 11AM on Wednesday, February 22 followed by burial at Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY.
