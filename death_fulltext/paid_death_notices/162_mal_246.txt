
        SCHULTZ-Edward F. On July 22, 2000. He had a long, dedicated career in brokerage as manager and V.P. at Cogan Berlin Weill and Levitt; Shearson, Haydn, Stone; and Daiwa. Beloved father of Jennifer Lynn Schultz, former husband of Marilyn De Luca. Loving brother of Florence Broderick and Peggy Smith. Also survived by ten nieces and nephews and sixteen great nieces and great nephews. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Avenue (at 20th Street), NYC Wednesday, 2-5 & 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday 10 AM at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
