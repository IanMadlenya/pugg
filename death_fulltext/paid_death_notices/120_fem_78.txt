
        BUCHBINDER - Sydel F. On January 11, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Herbert. She is survived by her son and daughter-in-law Herbert L. and Bonnie of Overland Park, KS; a sister Evelyn Forbes; sister-in-law and brother-in-law, Doris and Irving Schlitt and sister-in-law Julia Buchbinder, of Tamaham, NC, Matthew and Scott of Overland Park, KS, as well as nieces and nephews. She was a graduate of Hunter College and taught in the NYC school system where she concluded her career as Vice-Principal of P.S. 9 in Brooklyn. Services Monday, 10 AM, at ''Plaza Memorial Chapel,'' 91st Street & Amsterdam Avenue.
