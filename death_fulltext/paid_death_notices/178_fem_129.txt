
        STELZER-Ruth Sondra (nee Miness). Of Roslyn, New York, died on November 11, 2001. Beloved wife of Edward, treasured mother of Stephen Stelzer, and Barbara Friedman, loving mother-in-law to Lenny Friedman, and Francie Lyshak-Stelzer, adored grandmother of Andrew Stelzer, and Emily and Noah Friedman. A kind, compassionate woman devoted to her family and the arts, who never lost her sense of humor. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be send to Women's American O.R.T. at: 516-487-4575.
