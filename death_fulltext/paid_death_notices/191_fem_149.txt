
        TOLMAGE-Pauline (Dichter). Age 93, on December 13, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Sidney. Cherished aunt, great-aunt and great-greataunt. Longtime devoted employee of the Board of Examiners of the New York City Board of Education. Lifelong New York resident and lover of the arts. Interment will be private. Her vitality and candor will be missed. Her independent spirit and generosity remain an inspiration.
