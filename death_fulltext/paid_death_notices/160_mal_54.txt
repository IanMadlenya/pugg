
        TOBIN-Robert L.B. The staff and friends of the New York Public Library for the Performing Arts are saddened by the death of Robert L.B. Tobin. He served as a member of the Committee for the Dance Division for many years, and we shall miss his wise advice and deep knowledge of the arts of stage design. Hubert Goldschmidt Chairman, Dance Committee Madeleine M. Nichols Curator, Dance Division
