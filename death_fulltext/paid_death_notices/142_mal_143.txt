
        LANDIS-Leonard R. Age 75. On November 10, 1998. Beloved husband of Elayne. Loving and proud father of Carolyn, Dean and his wife Mara, Andrew and his wife Hilary. Devoted grandfather of Teddy. Dear brother of Donald. A resident of both Manhattan and Sagaponack, he was a leader in commercial finance, serving as the long standing President of United Credit Corporation. Services on Thursday, November 12th, 12 Noon at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65th Street.
        LANDIS-Leonard R. Congregation Emamu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Leonard R. Landis. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        LANDIS-Leonard. We are truly sadden by Leonard's passing. We will miss him. Our love to the family. Ellen and Martin Smith
