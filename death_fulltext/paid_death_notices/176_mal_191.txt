
        ROTHENBERG-Mark ''Mickey'' D. Died September 11, among the heroes of United Flight 93. Mickey, age 52, of Scotch Plains, NJ, is survived by his wife of 30 years, Meredith, his daughters, Rachel, age 27, and Sara, age 22, and his mother, Dorothy. He was the son of the late Irving Rothenberg. Mickey was president of MDR Global Resources, Inc., and the former owner of Culver Glassware. He was a graduate of Franklin and Marshall College, Lancaster, PA. A memorial service will be held September 16, at 11:30 AM, at Temple EmanuEl, 760 East Broad Street, Westfield, NJ. Donations can be sent to Temple Emanu-El at the above address. Any contributions made in Mickey's memory will be donated to a victims relief fund.
