
        PORTERFIELD-Alfred W. On August 14, 1998. Beloved husband of Elizabeth. Loving father of Alfred, Teresa Darby and William and grandfather of Victoria and Jill. Dear brother of Robert Porterfield and Grace Clayberger. He was a Korean War Veteran and a devoted member of the American Legion VFW, a recipient of The Purple Heart, Soldier's Medal, Silver Star, Bronze Star and Combat Infantry Badge. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Sunday and Monday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday at St. John the Evangelist Church, 1st Ave. at 55th St. Call Frank E. Campbell for further information: 212-288-3500.
