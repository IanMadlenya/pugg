
        VILLAMANA-Joan Spencer of Bronxville and Patterson, NY died at home on Tuesday, September 21, 1999. Mrs. Villamana is survived by her husband, Tom; her brother, Don Harris; her daughters, Luci Krusz, Kim Spencer and Molly Marshall; and four grandchildren, Robbie, Ronnie, Emily and Troy. A memorial service was held at Christ Church in Bronxville on Saturday, September 25th at 2 o'clock. In lieu of flowers, it is requested that donations be made to The Hole in the Wall Gang Fund, 555 Long Wharf Drive, New Haven, CT 06511. The Hole in the Wall Gang Camp is a camp for cancer and blood illnesses.
