
        MCGANN-George. Age 88 at his home in San Miguel De Allende, June 28. Retired editor & manager of the New York office of Australian Consolidated Press. A tennis authority, he covered tennis and other news, and wrote ''Court on Court'' with Margaret Smith Court and ''Court Hustler'' with Bobby Riggs. Survived by his wife Louise, brother Gerald, sister Marie Fletcher, daughter Pat McGann, three granddaughters and two great-grandsons. Address, APDO 109, San Miguel De Allende, GTO., Mexico 37700.
