
        REDLEAF-Natalie Lipsky. Died peacefully at home in East Quogue, NY, on February 7, 2001, in her 81st year. Wife of the late Gilbert T. Redleaf. Devoted mother of Geoffrey Redleaf (Marjorie), Hilary Stillman (Clark) and Dr. Roger S. Redleaf. Beloved grandmother of Matthew and Daniel Stillman and Jeremy Redleaf. Services private. Memorial contributions may be made to the Hampton Bays Public Library, PO Box 1017, Hampton Bays, NY 11946.
