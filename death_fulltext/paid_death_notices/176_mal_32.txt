
        SCHREYER-Oscar. After a long and productive life Oscar passed away on September 2, 2001. A successful lawyer, businessman and rare book dealer, his greatest accomplishment was his life and what he gave to his family. Loving father of Leslie & Linda. Devoted grandfather of Evan, Jenna and Gabri. He was a true original and will be greatly missed. Funeral services Tuesday, 11:30 AM, from Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, corner of 81st Street. Interment to follow at Union Fields Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to University of Chicago Library, 1100 East 57 St., Chicago, IL 60637, Attn: Director of Development. They will be used to fund the purchase of rare books in Oscar's memory.
