
        KILKELLY-Kathleen Terese McLoughlin, Ed.D., RN, of Rockville Centre, on April 26, 1997. Mrs. Kilkelly was a resident of Rockville Centre, NY, for 32 years. She was a professor of Nursing at Molloy College, Rockville Centre, having taught there for 23 years, was recently serving as president of the faculty. She earned her doctorate and Masters of Education at Columbia University, her MA in Nursing from N.Y.U., and her BA from Molloy College. She received her RN from St. Vincent's School of Nursing. Beloved wife of Edward A., of 38 years. Loving mother of Edward F. of Little Silver, NJ, and Mary Beth Kilkelly-Schmidt of Brooklyn, NY. Cherished grandmother to Molly and Emma Kilkelly and Jack Kilkelly-Schmidt. Dear sister of Elizabeth Murphy of White Plains, NY. Reposing at Macken Mortuary, Inc, 52 Clinton Ave, Rockville Centre, NY, today and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass St. Agnes Cathedral, Wednesday, 10 AM. Interment St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village.
