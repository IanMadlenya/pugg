
        KRAMER-Ben, 85, a 27 year resident of Davie, Florida, coming from New York, died Wednesday, February 3, 1999. He played professional basketball for the original Celtics, where he was known as ''Red'', and coached for the first Baltimore Bullets. Survived by wife, Vivian; son, Stephen of Ballground, Georgia; daughters, Nancy Mamati of Great Neck, New York; Susan Lynch of Hermitage, Tennessee; and six grandchildren. Memorial services 2:30 PM Friday, at Fred Hunter Funeral Home, Davie, Florida. Cremation to follow.
