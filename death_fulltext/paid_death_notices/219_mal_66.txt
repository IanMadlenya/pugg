
        MEYER--Irving A. On April 6, 2005, at the age of 90. Beloved husband of 63 years, to Shirley. Loving father to Charles, and his wife Susan, and Thomas H. Adoring grandfather to Jennifer and Jared. Dear brother to the late Milton, and Pauline Diamond. He will be especially loved and missed by all who knew him. Funeral service Friday, April 8, 2005, 12 PM, from Westchester Reform Temple, 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
        MEYER--Irving. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member Irv Meyer. On behalf of all our members, we express our deepest sympathy to his wife, Shirley, and their entire family. Earle Yaffa, President Dale Winston, Secretary
        MEYER--Irving A. The Congregation of Westchester Reform Temple mourns the death of its longtime devoted member and extends sympathy to his bereaved family. Amy Lemle, President Richard Jacobs, Rabbi Jack Stern, Rabbi Emeritus
