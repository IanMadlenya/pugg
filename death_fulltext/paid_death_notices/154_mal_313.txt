
        STEWART-John G.. Age 80, died after a brief illness, on November 23, at his home in Rumson, New Jersey. He is survived by his wife Linda; his sister, Jane Heckman of Lombard, IL; a son Mark Stewart of Monmouth Hills, NJ; a daughter Jennifer Stewart of Studio City, CA; three stepchildren and twelve grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at All Saints Church, Navesink, NJ, November 30 at 2:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that memorial contributions be made to the Alzheimer's Association, Central NJ Chapter, 12 Roszel Road, Suite C201, Princeton, NJ 08540-6234.
