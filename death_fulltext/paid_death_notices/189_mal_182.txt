
        WEISS-Walter. On October 12, 2002 at 80 years of age. We mourn the passing of our beloved husband, father, and grandfather. His unique zest for life and 54 year devotion to the ''21 Club'' were unparalleled. He will forever remain in our hearts and will be lovingly remembered. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Tuesday morning October 15, 2002. The Weiss & Mannino Family
