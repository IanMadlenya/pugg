
        LANS-Asher B., 83. Died February 27th, 2002. International lawyer for more than 50 years & community figure, he will be sorely missed by his wife, Peggy; children Debbie, Stephen, Alan and Andrea; by his daughter and son-in-law, Maria and Tom; and his loving former spouses, Barbara and Shirley. Funeral services Friday, March 1st at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, at 10 A.M. Donations to Cabrini Hospice or Gramercy Neighborhood Associates.
