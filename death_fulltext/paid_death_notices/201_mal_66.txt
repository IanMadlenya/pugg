
        BOYD--Robert F. Jr., Brooklyn Academy of Music mourns the loss of our beloved friend, Robert Boyd, and extends condolences to his wife, Amanda and family. A memorial service will be held at the BAM Harvey Theater, 651 Fulton Street on Saturday, October 11, at 10:30AM, followed by Mass at St. Boniface Church, 190 Duffield Street at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to BAM, 30 Lafayette Ave, Brooklyn, New York 11217 in honor of the Robert F. Boyd Jr. BAM Archives Fund. Karen Brooks Hopkins Joseph V. Melillo Harvey Lichtenstein
