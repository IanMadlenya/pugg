
        BALDWIN--Patricia S. Died July 4 at age 71 in New York City. She leaves her sons Scott and Brad, brother Donald (Suzanne), daughter-inlaw Jude and grandchildren Christopher, Patrick, Hillary, Joseph and Sara--all of whom she loved dearly. Her many friends whose lives were enhanced by knowing her will lovingly remember the zest for life and laughter and the kindness of this special and unique woman. Pat was a theatrical agent for most of her professional career, but later became a substance abuse counselor at Smithers Treatment Center in St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital. Any donations in her name may be sent to Calvary Hospital, Bronx, or to The Actors Fund of America. >>AD#
