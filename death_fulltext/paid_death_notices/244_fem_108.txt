
        RIGG--Cynthia W. Cynthia, who passed away on May 12, was the beloved wife of Abe Frajndlich and the devoted and doting mother of Lucas. With grit, grace and her everpresent humor, she waged a heroic, five-year battle with cancer. Throughout her illness, Cynthia remained a fiercely loyal friend to everyone, and she leaves a legacy of love and caring. Her life was our gift. She is mourned by family and her friends throughout the world. She will always be our role model. With Love, Her Friends
