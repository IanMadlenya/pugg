
        LEDERBERG--Esther Zimmer, an accomplished twentieth century scientist, died November 11 at Stanford Hospital from pneumonia and congestive heart failure. Dr. Lederberg is best known for the development of replica plating, and the discovery of lambda-phage transduction and lysogenicity. Her other interests included literature and Early Music. Esther married Joshua Lederberg in 1947, divorced in 1966. She met Matthew Simon in 1989 and married him in 1993. She is survived by Mr. Simon, her brother, Benjamin Zimmer, relatives from the Geller and Zimmer families, and children she helped raise.
