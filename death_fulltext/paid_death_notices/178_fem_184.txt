
        BERGER-Rita H. Died at home on November 17, 2001, after a brief illness. Services at 11:30 A.M., November 20, at The Riverside, 76th Street & Amsterdam Avenue.
        BERGER-Rita. The clients, Board and staff of The Bridge, Inc. are profoundly saddened by the death of Rita Berger, devoted Board member and friend to Bridge clients for almost forty years. For more than twenty-five years, Rita led a weekly client group, forming bonds that helped so many achieve a sense of dignity and self-worth. Rita's wisdom, generosity and her nurturing relationships have contributed immeasurably to the development of The Bridge. She will be missed & memorialized in our continuing efforts on behalf of persons with mental illness. Cynthia C. Wainwright, President Dr. Peter D. Beitchman, Exec. Dir.
