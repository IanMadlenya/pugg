
        ABBOTT-Susan, 74. November 7, 1997. Holocaust survivor from Vienna, Austria. She sailed into New York Harbor in 1940 and has lived in the city ever since. Artist and peace and justice activist. She will be missed for her extraordinary inspiration, creativity, hospitality and generosity. Beloved mother of Peter, Thomas and Michael; and grandmother of Andrea, Rebecca, Gabriel and Nathaniel. Services at 2 P.M., on Tuesday, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
