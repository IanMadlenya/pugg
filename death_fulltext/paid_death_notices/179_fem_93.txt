
        BLOCK-Millicent. Wife of Michael; mother of Margaret, Jonathan, Bernard, Rachel, Alice, and Robert; grandmother of David, Nicolas, Taylor, Hanna, Millicent, peacefully passed after a long history of Alzheimer's on December 9, 2001. Cherished by her family and friends in a life dedicated to the struggle for peace, freedom and caring for all. A spring memorial is planned. The family requests that, in lieu of flowers, donations be made to: Rosenberg Fund for Children, Springfield, MA.
