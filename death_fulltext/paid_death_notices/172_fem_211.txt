
        NEWMAN - Katharine Dealy. Founder of the Society for the Study of Multi-Ethnic Literature in the United States (MELUS) and cofounder of the Episcopal Service Alliance of Orange County, California, died peacefully Thursday, at her home in Encinitas, CA. Educator, author, editor and social activist, she was born in Philadelphia, PA, on August 17, 1911, received a B.S. from Temple Univerwity in 1933, and a Ph.D. in Literature from the University of Pennsylvania in 1961. She is survived by an extended kinship of family and friends.
