
        KATZ-Arthur Loeb. Our darling Arthur died April 12, 1998, peacefully at home. He was the loving husband of Shirley, the cherished father of Lawrence and Janet, loving mentor to Andrew, Diane, Lisa, Jeffrey and Howard, and beloved grandfather of Todd, Sarah, Jeremy, Jonathan, Rashi and Mira. Arthur had a passion for living, was an accomplished businessman, dedicated to the arts, and served in the U.S. Army in World War II, participating in the Normandy invasion and the liberation of Europe. He was a member of the Circumnavigators Club. You will live forever in our hearts. The funeral will be held today, Monday, April 13, at 1:15 PM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, kindly send a donation to Hospice Care, 1250 Broadway, NY, NY 10001.
