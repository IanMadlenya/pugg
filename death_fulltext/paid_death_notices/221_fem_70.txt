
        SCHNEIDER--Shayne Rachel. Died peacefully of leukemia on June 5, 2005 in Seattle, WA. Mourned by her parents, Louis and Prolet Schneider, her children, Keely, Zahava and Liam and their father, Dr. William Ball, her sisters Yehudith, Tamar and Aviva, friend and companion, William Woodson and all who knew her. She founded Mentors, Inc., a mentoring program serving Washington, D.C. public schools. She went on to become a consultant and leader in the national mentoring community. Donations in her memory may be made to Mentors, Inc., The House of Ruth in Washington, D.C., or The Washington/Alaska Chapter of The Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
