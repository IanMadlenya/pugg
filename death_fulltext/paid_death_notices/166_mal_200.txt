
        SCHINDLER-Rabbi Alexander. The Officers, Board and staff of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed Board member Rabbi Alexander Schindler, one of the most influential leaders in American Judaism. He served as President of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations, President of the Memorial Foundation for Jewish Culture and Vice President of the World Jewish Congress. He was renowned for his commitment to unite people of all faiths and backgrounds. His moral authority had a great impact on the JDC; his vision and eloquence were a guiding force. We extend our deepest condolences to his beloved wife Rhea, his children, grandchildren, and to all the Schindler family. Jonathan W. Kolker, President Michael Schneider, Executive Vice President Ambassador Milton A. Wolf, Chairman of the Board
        SCHINDLER-Rabbi Alexander. Central Synagogue of New York City mourns with profound sorrow the passing of Rabbi Alexander Schindler. He embodied passion, intelligence, vision and love of our people. Above all, Rabbi Schindler was a dear friend and leader. We extend comfort to his entire family. ''His vision was undimmed.'' (Deuteronomy 34:7) Samuel Wasserman, President Rabbi Peter J. Rubinstein Rabbi Joshua Davidson Rabbi Sarah H. Reines Cantor Jordan S. Franzel Cantor Richard Botton, Emeritus
