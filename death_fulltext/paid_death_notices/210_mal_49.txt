
        BLISS--Milton. Peacefully, at home in New Rochelle at the age of 86 on July 4, 2004. Loving husband of Marjorie, father of Michael, Richard, Ronald, and Susan. Grandfather of Matthew, Alexander, Andrew, Ryan, Alan, Brennen and Mara. Brother of Benjamin Bliss and Sonia Grossman. A member of the New York Diamond Dealers and highly regarded gemologist for over 60 years. He served in WW II with the Criminal Investigation Division of the Adjutant General's Office. Past President of the Male Glee Club of Yonkers. Trustee of the Washington Institute. Involved in numerous philanthropic and charitable organizations. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Westchester.
