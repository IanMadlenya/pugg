
        POWERS-Irene Elizabeth. Passed away at her home in New Rochelle, N.Y. on Tuesday evening, January 16, 2001. Irene, born July 2, 1909, was a 51-year New Rochelle resident and died at the age of 91. Irene is survived by her son Frederic B. Powers, Jr. and his wife Virginia of Larchomnt, N.Y. Irene is also survived by her daughter-in-law Rosemarie Burke of Larchmont, six grandchildren (Christopher, Jeffrey, Stephen and Frederic, III Powers; Jennifer Buddemeyer and Stephanie Murray), and eighteen great grandchildren. Irene will be waked at the John J. Fox Funeral Home, (914-834-0144) in Larchmont, N.Y. on Friday, January 19th from 2-4 and 7-9PM. Her Funeral Mass will be held at Holy Family Church in New Rochelle on Saturday, January 20th at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, the family has requested donations be sent in her memory to Sound Shore Medical Center Foundation, 16 Guion Place, New Rochelle, NY 10801.
