
        THATCHER--John M. P. III of Greenwich, CT and Edgartown, MA died on November 26, 2006 in Florida. Terry was the beloved husband of the late Marguerite Funston Thatcher, and was the devoted father of Peggy, Katherine, John M. P. IV and Christina. He is survived by his loving family, his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Thatcher Jr. of Hilton Head, SC and Landgrove, VT, two sisters, Patricia Thatcher and Barbara and her husband, Alan Donaldson, seven nieces and nephews, his aunts, Mrs. Richard Leach of Saratoga, NY and Mrs. John Riddell of Houston, Gail and John Wasson, Grace and Keith Funston, and Mrs. Gordon Reed of Greenwich. Born in NYC on January 10, 1943. He graduated from Hotchkiss and Yale. After a career at IBM and in real estate, he dedicated himself to charitable pursuits. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Greenwich at 254 East Putnam Avenue at 11AM on Friday, December 8, 2006. Donations may be sent to the Greenwich Association of Retarded Citizens, St. Barnabas Church or The Wildlife Conservation Society.
