
        PRESKY--Marcia. Age 50, on November 28, 2005. Beloved daughter of Albert and the late Esther. Loving sister of David. Marcia was a dedicated, generous and giving advocate of international women's health. Some of Marcia's many accomplishments during over 14 years at American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee include championing international women's health programs and helping to raise over $18 Million dollars for South Asia tsunami relief. She had an enormous impact on many lives by working with SHARE, a peer support organization for women who had breast or ovarian cancer, as a member of the Board of Directors where she helped build their breast cancer programs in Eastern Europe and Israel. Marcia will be deeply missed by her family, colleagues and friends. Service Friday 11:30AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
