
        LAURITANO--Natalie Ferazzi. Died on November 25, 2003 at her home in Manhattan. Beloved wife of the late Amedeo Lauritano, a former NYC judge. Sister of Dr. Pat Ferazzi. Cherished mother of Lois Lauritano and Natalie Wassong. Devoted grandmother of Fletcher, John and Chris Daly, adoring great grandmother of Emma Grace and Elizabeth Rose Daly. Beloved aunt of Marie Halloran, Frank and Peter Gudicello, and her nieces and nephews. A special woman cherished by all who knew her. She will remain in our hearts always. Memorial Mass at Sacred Heart of Mary & Jesus, 10am, December 2. Contributions may be made to Cabrini Hospice 227 E. 19th St, NYC.
