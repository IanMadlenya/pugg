
        FAIRBURN-Gordon Ramsay. The Trustees and staff of the New York Society Library express deep sorrow at the loss of our friend and esteemed colleague Gordon Fairburn, Trustee for 22 years. His work as Secretary and Library Committee Chairman was much valued, as was his warmth and dedication to the Library. We extend our condolences to his family.
        FAIRBURN-Gordon. In loving memory of the wit, intelligence, charm and spirit of our dear friend and fellow Trustee, Gordon. Heartfelt condolences to Phoebe and their children. Charles Burlingham, Jr. President Constance Evans, Exec. Director The Weir Farm Trust
