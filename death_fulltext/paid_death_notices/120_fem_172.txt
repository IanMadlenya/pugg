
        CREEM-Theresa De Voe, 90, widow of John J. Creem Jr. died at St. Francis Hospital after a long illness on January 23, 1997. A long time member of the Munsey Park and Sands Point Garden Clubs. She was a nationally accredited horticultural judge. She is survived by her 3 sons: Michael, Anthony and Timothy, seven grandchildren & 2 great-grandchildren. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, Sunday from 3-5 PM and Monday from 7-9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial on Tuesday 11 A.M., at St. Mary's R.C. Church, Manhasset. In lieu of flowers, please make memorial donations to the Brooklyn Botanical Garden, 1000 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11225.
