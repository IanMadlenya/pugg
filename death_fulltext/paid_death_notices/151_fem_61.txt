
        JULI-Paula (Cohen). Holocaust Survivor, born in Poland, 1921; emigrated to the United States with her husband Sam Juli in 1947. She was active in fund-raising and teaching about the Holocaust. Survived by her sons, Harold and Herbert and daughter-in-law Harriet, grandchildren Eric and Sara Juli; also survived by her husband Jack Cohen. Contributions in Paula's memory may be made to a charity of your choice. Services today 12 noon at ''Sinai Chapels,'' 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY.
