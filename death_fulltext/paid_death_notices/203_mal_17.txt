
        ELGHANAYAN--John. The Touro family joins Mrs. Elghanayan and the children in mourning the loss of their patriarch and a longtime member and dear friend of Touro College, Mr. John Elghanayan of blessed memory. His devotion and longtime participation in the Board of Trustees of Touro will be sadly missed. Our deepest condolences to his loved ones at this difficult time. Dr. Bernard Lander, President Mark Hasten, Chairman of the Board
