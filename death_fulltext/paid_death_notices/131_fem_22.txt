
        STONE-Nina Joan Pymer ''Jodie'' died Sunday, November 29, 1997, at her winter home in New Smyrna Beach, FL. Born in Baltimore, MD, January 7, 1929, she was a 1946 graduate of Friends School, Baltimore, and a 1948 graduate of Edgewood Park College. She was the widow of George Norton Stone, former master at The Hotchkiss School 1949-1986. A resident of Lakeville, CT., since 1949, Mrs. Stone worked at the Sharon Clinic 1952-1955, and again 1960-66. From 1956-60 she served as a research assistant for the Sharon Hospital Project on cholesterol. She became an employee of The Hotchkiss School in 1966 and worked there until she retired in 1986. Jodie Stone was a dedicated volunteer and among the groups she served are the Salisbury Association, Scoville Library, Sharon Hospital Auxiliary, Noble Horizons Auxiliary, OWL's Kitchen, and the Congregational Church in Salisbury, Connecticut. Mrs. Stone is survived by her sister-in-law, Mrs. Gregory Zilboorg of Seneca, SC, and her children, Caroline, John, and Matthew Zilboorg. A memorial service will be held at 2 P.M., Saturday, December 6, 1997, at the Salisbury Congregational Church. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Hotchkiss School, Lakeville, CT.
