
        HALE--Nathan. On July 19, 2006, peacefully at his home after a long illness. Born April 25, 1931 in New York City to the late Lillian and Nathan Hale. Graduate of Rocky Hill School, Moses Brown School, Harvard College and Harvard Law School. Respected Trusts & Estates lawyer, he practiced for 40 years in New York with Shearman & Sterling; Kelly, Drye & Warren; Casey, Lane & Mittendorf; and Haythe & Curley. Devoted husband of Barbara, beloved father of Nathan and Edward. He is also survived by his brother, Christopher R. Hale of Hinsdale, NH. There will be a private family burial in Newcastle, ME. A memorial service will be held in New York at a later date. A fund in his memory will be announced at that time.
