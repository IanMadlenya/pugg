
        LITKEI-Ervin, February 8, 2000. Beloved husband of Andrea; loving father of Dr. Cathy Litkei and her husband Jeffry Greene. Cherished grandfather of Phillip, Amy, Alexandra and Jamie. A prominent composer whose works are recorded in the Congressional Record and the Songwriters Hall of Fame. He composed the Presidential Marches from Franklin Delano Roosevelt through and including William Jefferson Clinton. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. Wednesday and Thursday 2-5 and 7-9PM.
