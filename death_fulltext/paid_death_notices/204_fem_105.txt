
        DEVINE--Esther Cecelia, of Riverdale and Smallwood, NY, died on January 8, 2004 at the age of 89. Beloved wife of the late Ambrose ''Jack'' Devine. Loving mother of Evelyn Gage, Mary McGrath, Elizabeth Carle, Suzanne Campbell and loving mother-in-law to their husbands. Cherished grandmother to 13 grandchildren. Devoted sister to Marjorie Devine and sisterin-law Mary Irene Devine. We will always miss her. She worked for many years in the Pre-Med Department of CCNY. Reposing Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Riverdale-onHudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Ave., Bronx. Mass of Christian Burial Monday, 10 AM, St. Gabriel's Church. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Donations can be made to Auburn Hospital System Foundation's Esther C. Devine Fund, 12 Lansing St., Auburn, NY 13021.
