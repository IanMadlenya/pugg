
        EMERSON-Mary E. (Bette) of McKinney, Texas passed away on November 5, 2000 in McKinnney. She was born February 2, 1910 in Binghamton, New York, to W. and Mamie (Neal) Ives. She married Tom Emerson. She was an opera singer for the New York Metropolitan Opera and a member of the St. Peters Episcopal Church in McKinney, Texas. She was preceded in death by her husband, Colonel Thomas Emerson who at the time of his retirement was vice-president and general sales manager of Eversharp Pen Company. Funeral services will be held at 2:00 PM, Wednesday, November 8 at the St. Peters Episcopal Church in McKinney, Texas with Father Chuck Treadwell officiating. Interment will follow at Ridgeview Memorial Park. Visitation will be from 6:30 to 8:30 PM Tuesday at Turrentine - Jackson - Morrow Funeral Home. For information, call 972-542-2601. Donations may be made to St. Peters Episcopal Church Memorial Fund, 608 West Lamar St, McKinney, TX 75069.
