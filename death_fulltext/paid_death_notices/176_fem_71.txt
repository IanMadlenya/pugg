
        APPEL-Marjorie Mayer. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of New York City, passed away September 6, 2001. Loving wife of Jack. Adored mother of Jeffrey Hunter and Lynn Colwell. Cherished grandmother of Christopher, Corey, Chip and Karl. Great-grandmother of Zoe and Evan. Beloved sisterin-law of Harriet A. Riker and devoted friend of Ray and the late Francis Brice Stark. She was a gracious lady and will be missed by her many friends. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL 33486. Services today, September 9th, 2001, at Gutterman Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton. Toll-free 1-800-992-9262.
