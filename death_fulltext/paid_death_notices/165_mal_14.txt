
        ALLEN-Robert. 73, of Quogue, NY, formerly of New Rochelle, NY, died peacefully at home on October 1, 2000. He is survived by his loving wife Patty (nee Keating); four children from two previous marriages: Diana of Montague, MA, Katie of New Rochelle, NY, Gordon of Granger, IN, and Pamela Stevens; mother Ruth Kreger of Scotia, NY; sister Judy Holmes of Clifton Park, NY; three grandchildren: Robbie, Jordan and Zacharie. Funeral services will be private. Charitable donations may be made to the Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center, Attn: Philip Paty, Department of Surgery, 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021.
