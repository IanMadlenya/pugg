
        JACOBSON-Steven. Tifereth Israel, Town & Village Synagogue mourns the passing of Steven Jacobson on September 11, 2001 at The World Trade Center. Steve was a devoted member of the Board, Executive Board and Ritual Committee. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his wife Deborah, his daughters, Rachel and Miriam and to his mother and sister. Memorial services will be held at the Synagogue on Sunday, September 23rd at 10:00 A.M. May the Almighty comfort all those who mourn. Rabbi Laurence Sebert Rabbi Abigail Sosland Cantor Shayna Smith Dr. Lewis Freedman, President
