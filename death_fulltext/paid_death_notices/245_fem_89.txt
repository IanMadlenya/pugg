
        ENZER--Esther Markman, died June 12, 2007, the Founding Curator of the Yiddish Theater Section of the Museum of the City of New York. Born January 27, 1914 to Sam and Eva Markman in Brooklyn. Preceding her in death: Milton Michael Enzer, husband, founding Director of Public Relations, Yale and Towne Mfg Co.; Michael Jacob Enzer, son, Founder and Principal of Saxton Graphics; Eli Bodie David Enzer, son, Columnist for the Boston Globe. Mrs. Enzer is survived by: Charles Hart Enzer, M.D., son, Cincinnati; Sidney David Enzer, Ph.D., brother, Professor Emeritus, Duke University; Hyman Aaron Enzer, Ph.D., brother-in-law, Professor Emeritus, Hofstra University; Golde Weiner, sister-inlaw; Jill Gordon Rosenthal, sister-in-law; Lillian Swickle, sister-in-law; 10 grandchildren; 12 great-grandchildren; daughters-in-law, nieces, and nephews. Funeral today promptly at 1pm Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. Burial will be in Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY.
