
        MARRITT--Sondra. Beloved mother, grandmother and great-grandmother on April 28, 2007 after suffering with Alzheimer's Disease for many years. Matriarch of a large family, the last of six sisters and two brothers, keeping everyone together was one of her greatest accomplishments. Past President of B'nai Brith, Hewlett chapter. New York City resident for the last 19 years. Sandy was an inspiration to all in her fight against Alzheimer's and thanks to the love and care she received, was able to maintain her dignity throughout. Survived by her daughters and their husbands Joanne and Roy, Ina and Mark, grandchildren Gary and wife Julie, Jeremy, Matthew and wife Cindy, Michele, Stewart and wife Kathi. Great-grandchildren Zachary, Max, Joshua, Sami, Miranda and Sydney. Donations in her memory may be made to: Fisher Alzheimer's Program at NYU School of Medicine, attn: Barry Reisberg, M.D., adrc NYU Medical Center Rm MHL-316, 550 1st Ave, NY, NY 10016
