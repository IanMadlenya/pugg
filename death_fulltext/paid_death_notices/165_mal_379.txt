
        GERSTEN-Gerald Barry. 82, died October 26, in Lake Worth, FL. Mr. Gersten, an Army lieutenant in World War II, later founded Liberty Industries, a manufacturing company in Brooklyn, NY. He married Rita Achiron and they moved to Great Neck, LI, where they raised their children, Ben, Louise and Kenneth. Mr. Gersten received his Bachelor's degree at CCNY, where he was a marathon runner. He later obtained his Master's degree and was working on his Ph.D. in languages. Fluent in Spanish, he was an instructor at Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City. He later remarried, had two daughters and retired to Florida. His intelligence and motivation inspired many.
