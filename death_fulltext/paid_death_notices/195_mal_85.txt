
        STEIN - Jacob (Jack) of Brooklyn, NY died Tuesday, April 8 at the age of 90. Jack was a Navy Veteran of WWII, retired attorney, former secretary of Manhattan Beach Jewish Center, past commander and associate chairman for senior citizens of the Jewish War Veterans. He is survived by his loving wife of 63 years, Mathilda, his sons Mark and Allan and numerous grandchildren and great grandchildren. Viewing Thursday, 12 noon at Kehila Chapels, 60 Brighton 11 St, Brooklyn followed by funeral services at 1pm.
