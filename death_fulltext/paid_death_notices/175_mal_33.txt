
        REHBOCK-Alix. Passed on June 6 in Atlanta, Georgia. Raised in Great Neck. He worked for Revlon, Amirati & Puris, and Ogilvy & Mather as a Commercial Artist and Art Studio Manager. Alix worked for Fine Print and Thinkhouse Creative in Atlanta. Survivors include his sister Alana Rehbock & mother Anita Dantzic Rehbock. Memorial service on August 19 at 11, William Breman Jewish Home, 3150 Howell Mill Road, Atlanta 30327-2199. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The William Breman Jewish Home.
