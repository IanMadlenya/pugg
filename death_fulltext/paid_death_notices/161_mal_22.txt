
        COHEN-Sanford (Sandy). On June 1, 2000. Married to his childhood sweetheart Kiki for 49 glorious years. Father to Howard, (Amy); Ivan, (Alice); Cindy Dolgin (Moti). Grandfather of Aylah, Yishai, Maxine, Yonatan, Maya, Leah and Abraham. Brother of Ruth Yanchuck. Devoted uncle and friend. Currently of Boynton Beach, FL; formerly of East Meadow, NY.
