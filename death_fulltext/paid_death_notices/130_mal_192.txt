
        SHAW-Michael G. Of New York, FL. and Fair Harbor. November 17th. Beloved husband of Vivian (Cantor) Shaw. Devoted father of Andrea Shaw-Resnick and Steven Shaw. Loving grandfather of Joshua and Zachary Resnick. Brother of the late Ann Rubin and uncle of Howard and Alan Rubin. Mr. Shaw was employed by the Merck & Company, Rahway, N.J. for 42 years. He was also a member of the Trout Unlimited Fly Fishing Club of New York. Services will be held at the Garlick-Hellman Funeral Home, 1091 Yonkers Avenue, Yonkers, on Wednesday, at 10 A.M. Interment will be in Sharon Gardens Cemetery.
