
        AUFRICHTIG-Ethel (nee Horowitz). On January 3, 1999, of cancer at age 79. Beloved wife of Jess. Devoted mother of Michael and wife, Amy. Adoring grandmother (Nana) of Elyse. She was a graduate of Hunter College, an active member of The League of Women Voters, and a very special person. She will be remembered for her desire and efforts to make this a better world, for her rare qualities of insight and understanding, and her great spirit despite much adversity. Services Tuesday, 9:30 AM, ''Sinai Chapels,'' 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York.
