
        SHAW-Otho Stokes, 74, of New York City and Hampton Bays, L.I. Partner in Shaw & Draper, Inc., Interior Designers; Fellow American Society of Interior Designers; Past President Alumni Association of Parsons School of Design. Beloved friend and partner of Dede Draper. Beloved brother of Lavon Morrow. Adored Uncle of Lester Morrow of Minneapolis, MN. He will be greatly missed by his dear friends in England and this country. Friends may call Wednesday 10-12PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St.
