
        MAGAGNINI--Jessie C. Of Brooklyn, NY, 83, artist, died after a long illness. She graduated Summa Cum Laude from Brooklyn College. Survived by her loving husband, Antonio, her sister, Roslyn Orr, of FL, her son, Stephen, of CA, her daughter Lisa, of Rome, Italy, her daughter, Miranda, of Brooklyn, NY, and four grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday April 9th at 11 A.M. at The Heights Casino, 75 Montague Street, Brooklyn Heights. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions in her name can be made to: The Jessie Magagnini Scholarship Fund, The Greenwich House Pottery, 16 Jones St, NY, NY 10014.
