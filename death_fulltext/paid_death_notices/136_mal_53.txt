
        SHERMAN-Robert S., MD. Age 88. Of Roxbury, CT. on May 3, 1998 in New Milford, CT. A former chairman & attending radiologist at Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in NY. Also a consultant in diagnostic radiology for IBM medical department. Survived by his wife, Dr. Ruth Snyder. Also survived by a daughter Ruth Ann Perkins of Finksburg, MD., two sons Robert Snyder Sherman of Pelham, N.H., and Roger Holmes Sherman of Northport, N.Y., and nine grandchildren. Funeral services will be held Wednesday 11 AM, St. John's Episcopal Church, Washington, CT. Burial in Roxbury Cemetery. Memorial contributions may be made to the Roxbury Scholarship Foundation, C/O Roxbury Townhall, Roxbury, CT. 06783.
