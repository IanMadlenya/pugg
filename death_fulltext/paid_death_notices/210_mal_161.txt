
        JANKO--Marvin. Proud Navy veteran of WW II, died July 15, 2004 of complications from Alzheimers. He is survived by his loving wife, Elaine Albert Janko, sons Andrew Janko and Stephen Janko, daughters Hillary Ritholz and Heather Dennis; sister Leila Richter, and predeceased by his brother Irving Janko. Graveside services will be held at Acacia Cemetery, Ozone Park, Queens, Sunday, July 18 at 10:00 AM. Please send contributions to The Alzheimers Association.
