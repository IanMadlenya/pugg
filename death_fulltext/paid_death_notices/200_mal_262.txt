
        MINKIN--David. 99, of New York, and Palm Beach, FL. Passed away September 19, 2003. Adored uncle of Patricia and Howard Lester. Greatuncle of Peter and Prescott Lester and Pamela Ford. Great-great-uncle of Owen Ford and Scott Lester. He was a true renaissance man, a leader of the community and a second father to us all. He will be sorely missed. Services were held. For information call Riverside Nassau North, 516-487-9600. Contributions may be made to the Mayo Foundation, attn: Paul Harkess, Rochester, MN 55905.
