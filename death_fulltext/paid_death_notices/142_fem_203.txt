
        LEUBERT-Celestine C. (Tineey). Died peacefully on November 21, 1998 surrounded by family in Sun Valley, ID. Loving mother of Eloise (Cronin) & Susan. Cherished grandmother of Brian, Colleen, Kama. Sister of Frank Capka, Bernice Olt, the late Cyril Capka. Employee of FDIC, volunteer at Donnell Library. Formerly of Manhasset, LI, where she was involved in numerous philanthropic & social organizations. Her zest for life despite insurmountable odds will always be an inspiration to her extensive international family of friends. Through prolific correspondence and thoughtful generosity, she communicated her boundless capacity for love and friendship. Funeral mass 10 AM, Saturday, November 28 at St. Paul the Apostle Church. Reception to follow. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her name may be made to St. Paul the Apostle Church, 415 West 59th Street, NY, NY 10019.
