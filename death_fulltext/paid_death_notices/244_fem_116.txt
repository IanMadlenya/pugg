
        NAUMBURG--Marian Opton, died at home in her sleep, May 13, 2007. The daughter of the late Milton and Harriet Opton, and the wife of the late C. Frank Naumburg, she is survived by her brother, Edward, three nephews, and their families. Patron, gourmet, friend and stubborn survivor, she will be missed. Services to be held later in the year. Gifts in lieu of flowers may be made to the New York Society Library.
