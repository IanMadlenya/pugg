
        McINTYRE-James Bigelow. Sons of the Revolution in the state of New York notes with great sadness the death of James B. McIntyre, a life member and past president of this society. Mr. McIntyre served with devotion on our Board of Managers and held many other vital committee positions. Our heartfelt condolences to his family. Henry S. Middendorf, Jr., Pres. Laurence S. Simpson, Secretary
