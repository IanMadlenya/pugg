
        MELLIN-John O. Distinguished minister of The First Presbyterian Church in the City of New York for over forty years, died Tuesday, January 14, in Orleans, MA. He is mourned by his wife Gloria; sons John, Jr., Peter, James and Timothy; the membership of the Church and the Village community. Services at First Presbyterian, Fifth Avenue at 12th Street, Wednesday, January 22 at 4 P.M. The family prefers that memorial gifts be given to the charity of the donors choice in Mr. Mellin's name.
