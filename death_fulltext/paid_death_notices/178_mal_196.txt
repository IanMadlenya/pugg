
        FAY-Lawrence F. Of Bedford, NY, died on November 11, 2001, at Rosary Hill Home. He was 72. He was a partner at the law firm of Kent, Hazzard, Jaeger, Wilson, Fay & Conroy, in White Plains, NY, where he worked for 44 years. He was born on July 5, 1929 in the Bronx, to Lawrence R. and Mary (Duffy) Fay. He graduated from All Hallows High School in 1947, and Fordham University in 1951. In 1957 he graduated from Georgetown Law Center with JD and LLM degrees. He served in the U.S. Navy during the Korean conflict, attaining the rank of Lt. JG. He lived in Bedford, NY, for the past 33 years. He was a member and Director of The Thomas and Agnes Carvel Foundation. He is survived by his mother, Mary, of Point Pleasant, NJ, his brother, Robert, of Ft Lauderdale, FL, his sister, Mary Ellen Gentle, of Bethel, CT, his children, Kathleen Tell, of Point Pleasant, NJ, Kevin J., and Brian P., both of Bedford, NY, and Jennifer Rice, of Seattle, WA. He is also survived by six grandchildren. He is predeceased by his wife of 44 years, Marie (McCafferty) Fay, who died in 1998. Calling hours will be at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, on Tuesday 7-9 P.M. and Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Service will be at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bedford, NY, on Thursday at 10 A.M. Interment will be at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to: The Rosary Hill Home, 600 Linda Avenue, Hawthorne, NY 10532 or The All Hallows H.S., 111 East 164th Street, Bronx, NY 10452.
