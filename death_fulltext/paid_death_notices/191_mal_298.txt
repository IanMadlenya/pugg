
        BROWNSTEIN - Jerome. Born September 1, 1931, in917 Troy, NY. Died of colon cancer January 19, 2003, at the age of 71. A graduate of Russell Sage College and Albany Union Law School, he was a practicing attorney for 45 years. A true Renaissance man, he was also a calligrapher, entrepreneur, and passionate collector. He owned Gavit & Co. in Albany, NY, the oldest, continuously operating engraving company in the United States. He was a Master of the 32nd Degree Mason and a member of the Horological Society. A longtime resident of the Upper West Side, he is survived by his wife Honey Rodman; sons Richard, Paul, and Robert; daughters Sara Truebridge and Lisa Moore; and grandchildren Ian, Quinn, Cali, Colby, Devin, and Natalia. Donations in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, P.O. Box 27106, New York, New York 10087.
