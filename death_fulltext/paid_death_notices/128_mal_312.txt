
        KAYNARD-Samuel M. We mourn the passing of our friend, colleague, teacher and recipient of the Committee's Lifetime Achievement Award. As Director of NLRB Region 29, Regional Attorney of NLRB Region 2 and law Professor, Sam helped shape the law with intelligence, fairness, compassion and a profound understanding of the concerns of labor and management alike. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Anne, and his family. Labor and Employment Committee Association of the Bar of the City of New York
