
        WEISSENSTEIN-Herbert F. The Center for Arts Education mourns the passing of Herbert Weissenstein. Through his counsel and belief in our mission, Herb transformed the Center from an ambitious concept to an organization that has helped restore arts education to thousands of New York City public school children. We will miss Herb's wit, guidance and insights. Our condolences to Constance, Kathryn and Michael, and his colleagues at HF Weissenstein and Company. Laurie Tisch Sussman, Chairman Hollis Headrick, Exec. Director
        WEISSENSTEIN-Herbert F. Orpheus Chamber Orchestra mourns with great sadness the loss of our gentle, kind and wise friend, Herb Weissenstein. We send our heart felt sympathy to Connie, Kathryn, Michael and his entire family.
