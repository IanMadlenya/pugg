
        FRANKS-Lydia G. Died peacefully on July 2, 2002 at the age of 90 years. She is survived by her beloved and devoted husband of 70 years of marriage, Andrew, her loving daughter, Joy Catapano, and loving son and daughterin-law, Andrew Jr. and Linda. Lydia was predeceased by her cherished daughter, Norma, and son-in-law, Frank Catapano. She is deeply missed by her adoring eleven grandchildren and eight greatgrandchildren. Lydia was a woman of elegance and conviction, completely devoted to her family, whose loving spirit will live on with us always. As she requested, services were private.
