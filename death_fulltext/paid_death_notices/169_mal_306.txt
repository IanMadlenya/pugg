
        MAGGIO-Eugene. At age 72 of Nanuet, died Saturday, February 24, 2001 in the Good Samaritan Hospital Suffern, New York. Mr. Maggio was born on December 30, 1928 to the late Mariano and the late Jennie Loprest Maggio. He was a Photographer, employed by The New York Times for over 35 years. He loved his job and it was very important to him. He also worked with his father in the family business Maggio Studio's in West New York, New Jersey. He is survived by his wife, Josephine Maggio, his two daughters, Jo Ann and Elizabeth Maggio, his two sisters, Mary Sarsano and Ann Roma, and two brothers, Anthony and Joseph Maggio. A Mass of Christian Burial was held in St. Anthony's Church in Nanuet, New York, on Tuesday, February 27. Interment took place in St. Anthony's Cemetery in Nanuet. For information Joseph W. Sorce Funeral Home Inc., of West Nyack, New York. Donations may be made to the American Heart Association.
