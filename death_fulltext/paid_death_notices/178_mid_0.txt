
        BURKO-Marvin H. Age 75, of Bronx and Wesley Hills, New York, died peacefully on October 31, 2001. Survived by wife Ruth, sons and daughters-in-law Elliot and Stephanie, Mitchell and Lynn, grandchildren Daniel, Nicholas, Max, and Hayley, sister Elinor, and nieces and nephew. Funeral on Thursday 1:00 PM, GarlickRiverside Memorial Chapel, 1439 Unionport Road, Bronx, NY.
        BURKO-Marvin. The New York City Marshals Association wishes to express its condolences to City Marshal Ruth Berko on the loss of her beloved husband. He was a kind, compassionate & supportive husband, father and friend. Alfred E. Locascio, President
