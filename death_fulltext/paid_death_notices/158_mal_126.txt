
        CALLAS-John Nicholas. Died January 29, 2000, 78 years old, New York, NY. Preceded in his death by his son, Robert Patrick Callas. Husband of Gloria Evelyn Callas, father of sons John Daniel Callas, Peter David Callas, Philip Thomas Callas and daughter Celia Callas Kerames. Graduate of NYU summa cum laude, Served in W.W. II. Employed by The NY Times, from 1946-1963 in finance. CPA through retirement in 1985. Mass to be held at St. Elizabeth Seton Church at 9:00AM in Fallmouth, MA, burial to follow the National Veteran's Cemetery in Bourne, MA. He'll be in our loving memory and prayers always.
