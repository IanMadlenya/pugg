
        GLASER-David. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of David Glaser. As president & CEO of Parker Jewish Institute for Health Care and Rehabilitation from 1980-1999, David's vision and leadership were instrumental in strengthening the institution's reputation as a leader in the field of geriatrics and health care. We extend our deepest sympathies to David's wife, Davie; to his children, Lewis, Marci, and Seth; to his grandchildren; and to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
