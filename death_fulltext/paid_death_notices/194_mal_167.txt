
        BERNSTEIN-Dudl (David). Died Tuesday, March 11, 2003 while giving his best conducting the Yiddish Folk Chorus of Palm Beach County at a concert in Miami Beach, FL. All his life he was involved in both the Yiddish language and Yiddish folk music. The members of the chorus will miss him greatly for his leadership, his music ability, his friendship and his strong feeling for the Yiddish language. He believed that a ''folk vos zingt vet kainmol nit untergain'' A people that sings will never die. Members of a Yiddish Folk Chorus of Palm Beach County, Florida
