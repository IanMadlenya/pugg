
        BAYLIS-Chester, Jr. November 25, 1997 at his home in Locust, New Jersey at age eighty-nine. He is survived by his wife Dorothy Smith, his son Robert M., three grandsons Robert W., David M. and John C., and one great granddaughter Emma Kate. His career at Bankers Trust New York Corporation spanned 44 years, and he was Chairman of the Executive Committee. Funeral services will be held at St. George's By The River Episcopal Church in Rumson, New Jersey, Saturday, November 29 at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Princeton University, Princeton, NJ 08544 or to Riverview Medical Center, Red Bank, N.J. 07701.
