
        PROSNITZ-Gerry, age 95. We mourn the passing of Gerry, a prominent journalist and activist. She fought tirelessly for social and Jewish causes throughout her life. She was founder and president of the Jewish Association of Retired Professionals until the age of 90. She is survived by sons Eugene and Leonard, her brother Herman and sister Bessie, her daughtersin-law Sandra and Beatrice and grandchildren Susan, Eric and Robert. Service on Friday, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue at 1:30 PM.
