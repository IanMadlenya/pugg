
        COBB-Henry. For the past 19 years, Henry Cobb enjoyed life with his beloved wife Eliza with whom he shared many interests and a loving devotion to their extended family. The Trustees and Staff of The Museum of Modern Art are deeply saddened and mourn his loss. To Eliza, his children, Sheila, John and Victoria, his stepchildren John and Zaidee, his grandchildren and great-grandchildren, we send our most heartfelt sympathy. David Rockefeller, Chairman Emeritus Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Agnes Gund, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, NY Sir Brian Urquhart, Chairman Jo Carole Lauder, President The International Council of The Museum of Modern Art
