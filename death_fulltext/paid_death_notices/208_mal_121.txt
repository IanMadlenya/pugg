
        DAVIS--Dwight F. III. The Board of Directors and staff of the USTA Tennis and Education Foundation mourn the loss of our much respected board member Dwight F. Davis III. His dedication and enthusiasm toward improving the lives of disadvantaged children will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences to Anne Marie and the entire Davis family. Pam Shriver, President USTA T & EF Alan Schwartz, Chairman of the Board and President USTA Karen Martin-Eliezer, Exec. Dir. USTA T & EF
