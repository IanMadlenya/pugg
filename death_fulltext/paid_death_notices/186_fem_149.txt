
        MILICI-Frances Marie D'Amato. 73, beloved wife of Anthony J. Milici, Sr., of Wallingford, CT. Beloved mother of Anthony J. Milici, Jr., David J. Milici, Janet Marie Milici Lewis and Robert R. Lewis, adoring grandmother of Rand, Marjorie, Anthony and Helen, loving sister of Agnes Chartier, John D'Amato and Lawrence D'Amato. Her family will receive relatives and friends at The Wallingford Funeral Home, 809 North Main Street Extension, Wallingford, Thursday from 4 p.m. to 8 p.m. Funeral services will be held Friday at 9:30 a.m. from The Wallingford Funeral Home, when the funeral cortege will proceed to Most Holy Trinity Church, where a Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated at 10:30 a.m. Interment will be in St. John Cemetery. Gifts in her memory may be sent to the American Liver Foundation, CT Chapter, 110 Washington Avenue, North Haven, CT 06473.
