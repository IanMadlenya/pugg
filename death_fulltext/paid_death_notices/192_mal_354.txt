
        SKINNER-David B., M.D. The Columbia University Department of Surgery mourns the loss of Dr. David B. Skinner, our esteemed colleague in thoracic surgery, and an outstanding scientist, as well as a visionary health care administrator. Under his leadership, the NewYork-Presbyterian Healthcare System grew to become the most extensive health care system in the metropolitan area, and a national model for delivery of health care of the highest quality. His legacy lives on in the countless numbers of patients who benefit from excellent health care through this network. We extend sincere condolences to his wife, Ellie, and to his entire family. Eric A. Rose, M.D., Chairman Columbia University Department of Surgery, Columbia Prestyterian Medical Center NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital
        SKINNER-David B., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Board of Managers, administration, medical staff and employees of the New York United Hospital Medical Center mourn the sudden death of their friend and advisor, Dr. David Skinner. Through his wisdom and advice, in 1992, United Hospital became the first hospital to be merged into what is now known as the New York - Presbyterian Healthcare System. The United family extends our condolences to Dr. Skinner's wife, Ellie, and his daughters. Pamela P. Kindler Chairman, Brd of Trustees Roberto Rodriguez President and CEO Lammie Williams Chairman, Brd of Managers
        SKINNER-Dr. David. I salute the life of Dr. David Skinner who did so much to raise the quality of medicine in the borough of Queens. Claire Shulman, Former Queens Borough President
