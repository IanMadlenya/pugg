
        GORMAN--Robert E. On June 30, 2005. Robert served in the U.S. Navy during WWII, and began a career as a stockbroker at McDonald and Company. At the time of his death was employed at UBS Securities. Robert was a member of Sovereign Military Order of the Templar Knights of Jerusalem, the Manhattan resident was 79. Robert leaves behind his beloved wife Rita (nee Reiss) Gorman of 53 years. Devoted father of Ruth Gorman of Allenhurst, N.J., Marion Swenson of La Jolla, Calif., Leslie DeRosa of Brooklandville, Md. and the late Alicia Gorman. Grandfather of Erica, Laura, Kate, Elizabeth, Scott, Brian, and Caroline. A memorial service will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be sent to the Julian Reiss Foundation, 5 Julian Way, Lake Placid, N.Y. 12946.
