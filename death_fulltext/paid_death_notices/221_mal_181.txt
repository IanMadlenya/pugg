
        HARNICK--William, 91, died on June 20, 2005. Loving husband of Evelyn. Devoted father of Andrea Tuchman, Harvey Harnick, and the late Felice Schwartz. Cherished grandfather of Hal and Jeffrey Schwartz, Stacey and Jason Harnick, and Felicia and Jared Tuchman. Major presence in the floral industry for over 60 years, he touched many lives in his years of dedication to his family and business associates. Services today at 11:45AM at ''The Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 180 W. 76th St. NYC.
