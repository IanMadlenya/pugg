
        MORRILL-Alan. Age 90, born March 13, 1908, died April 26, 1998. Beloved husband of Dorothy for 66 years. Adored father of Carma (dec.). Loving grandfather of Sarah. Graduate of Boston Latin School 1925; Harvard University 1929. Broadway producer, actor, play doctor, raconteur, he left theater to enter business in difficult times-the Depression. A brilliant and witty man, he will be greatly missed. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 Street, today, Tuesday, 11 AM.
