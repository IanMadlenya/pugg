
        SUNUNU-Victoria Dada passed away on April 10, 2000 at her home in Gulf Stream, FL. Mrs. Sununu was born in San Salvador, El Salvador on Sept. 17, 1912 and was a longtime resident of Forest Hills, NY until 1979, when she and her husband John moved to Gulf Stream, FL. ''Toya'' was the mother of Governor John H. Sununu of New Hampshire, Dr. Alexandra Sununu of New York City and Charles Sununu of Gulf Stream, FL. She was also the grandmother of Congressman John E. Sununu of Bedford, NH, 11 other grandchildren and 5 great grandchildren. Mrs. Sununu was a devoted member of St. Vincent Ferrer Catholic Church in Delray Beach, FL. Friends may call on Wednesday April 12, 2000 from 2 PM to 4 PM & 6 PM to 8 PM with a 7:30 PM scripture service at the Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, 745 N. Federal Highway, Delray Beach. A funeral mass will be celebrated on Thursday April 13, 2000 10:30 AM at St. Vincent Ferrer Catholic Church, 840 Geroge Bush Blvd., Delray Beach. There will also be a funeral mass on Friday April 14, 2000, 11:30 AM at St. Matthew's Catholic Church in Windham, NH with interment at Pine Grove Cemetery, Salem, NH. Memorial gifts may be made to St. Vincent Ferrer Catholic Church Delray Beach, FL or St. Mary's Antiochian Orthodox Church, West Palm Beach, FL.
