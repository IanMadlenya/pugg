
        ROSENTHAL--Milton F., age 91, died May 2, 2005. Beloved husband of 62 years of Frieda (nee Bojar). Cherished son of the late Jacob and Louise Rosenthal. Devoted father of Anne (Peter) Mitro of Moreland Hills, Ohio; grandfather to Daniel and Roger. Loving brother of the late Alexander Rosenthal. Graveside service to be held at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hastings-onHudson, on Friday, May 6, at 2PM. No home visitation.
        ROSENTHAL--Milton F. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of Milton F. Rosenthal, a prominent business executive, who served with distinction as a Trustee and Life Trustee for 28 years. Milton Rosenthal's outstanding support of Mount Sinai included the creation of the Frieda and Milton F. Rosenthal Coronary Care Unit and other important trustee initiatives. Mr. Rosenthal will be remembered for his loyalty and thoughtful generosity. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Frieda Bojar Rosenthal, his daughter, Anne Mitro, and to the entire family. Peter W. May, Chairman Kenneth L. Davis, M.D., President & CEO
