
        LEVINE-Jack, M.D., 88. Of NYC, died peacefully at home on February 11, 2003. Survived by Michael, Carol, Susan, Jed and Families. Loved by all who knew him. Memorial service Sunday, February 16, 2003, 1:30 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St and Amsterdam. Memorial donations may be made to Alzheimer's Association, NYC 360 Lexington Ave, NYC 10017 or Pediatric Orthopedic Fund of NY Orthopedic Hospital, 3959 Bway 8 North, NYC 10032.
        LEVINE-Jack, M.D. We join to mourn the loss of Dr. Jack Levine, patriarch, whose life and values are reflected in the spirit, sensitivity, and compassion of Jed, his son, our friend and colleague. Our condolences to the entire Levine family he loved so well. Staff, Board and Friends, Alzheimer's Association, NYC
