
        BILGREI-Emanuel (Manny). Age 82, passed away December 27. Loving and devoted husband of Carrie Bilgrei, beloved father of Alice (Bilgrei) and Joseph Bresman, Michael and Donna Bilgrei. Cherished grandfather of Rachel, Joshua, Jonathan and Daniel. Founder and former head of Marc Air Conditioning and Refrigeration Corp. Boy Scout Leader, Free Mason, and member of the Board of the YM & YWHA of Williamsburg, Brooklyn. His love, wisdom and strength will be greatly missed. Funeral service will be Thursday December 28 at ''Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer'', 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, New York, 718263-7600. Donations to the Visions Vacation Camp for the Blind.
