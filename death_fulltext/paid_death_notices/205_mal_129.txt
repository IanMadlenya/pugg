
        MAYER--Cyrus, 91, of Great Neck, NY, passed away peacefully February 8, 2004. An absolutely wonderful man. Caring husband, best friend and partner of Sylvia, devoted father of Michael and Carole, Chester and Kathy, and Amy and Alan. Services Wednesday, February 11, at 10:30 AM, Temple Beth-El of Great Neck, NY, 516-487-0900.
        MAYER--Cyrus. Age 91. Of Great Neck, N.Y. on February 8, 2004. Devoted husband of Sylvia (nee Druxerman). Beloved father of Michael and Carole Mayer, Chester and Kathryn Mayer, and Amy Mayer-Goodstein and Alan Goodstein. Proud grandfather of Scott and Annie, Robert A., Robert C., Kacy, Shauna, Zachary and Alyssa. He will be deeply missed by his loving family. He founded the Mayer/Berkshire Corp. in 1944, and was a pioneer in the ladies' hosiery industry. The staff and management of Mayer/Berkshire mourn his passing and will cherish his legacy. Funeral services will be held at Temple Beth El, in Great Neck on Wednesday, February 11th at 10:30AM. Donations in his memory may be made to Temple Beth El, 5 Old Mill Rd., Great Neck, N.Y. 11023.
        MAYER -- Cyrus. Temple Beth El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
