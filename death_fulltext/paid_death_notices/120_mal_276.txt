
        FARKAS-Louis Sr. The President, Board of Governors, and the New York Board of Overseers, administration, faculty, staff and students of Hebrew Union CollegeJewish Institute of Religion record with deep sorrow the passing of Louis Farkas Sr., beloved fatherin-law of Rabbi Zahara Davidowitz-Farkas, Dean of the New York School. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his dear wife, Elizabeth, his family, Peter, Louis Jr. and Zahara. May God comfort the bereaved among them. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman, President Burton Lehman, Chair, Board of Governors Robert Heller, Chair, New York Board of Overseers
