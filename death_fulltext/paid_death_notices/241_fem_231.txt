
        KAHN--Liselotte. Liselotte Hirschmann Myller Kahn, widow of the architect Ely Jacques Kahn, died at her home on February 24. She was 101. Born in Nuremberg, Germany, she escaped Nazi rule by moving to Greece and then the United States with her first husband, the late Dr. Ernst Myller and her two sons, Rolf and Ulrich, who predeceased her. Beloved mother-in-law of Lois Myller and devoted grandmother of Elyse and Corinne and greatgrandsons, Marco and Luke.
