
        STEVENS--Melvin. Beloved husband of Debby for 52 years, died August 28 of lung cancer. He was an active outdoorsman sharing his love for fishing, sailing, skiing and tennis, as well as fine food, jazz and theater with family and friends. He began his career with Macy's as a CPA and rose to controller. Debby and he also own two businesses. Mel is survived by Debby; their sons, David, Peter and Seth and their wives; and grandchildren, Emma, Samuel, Luke, Sarah and Benjamin.
        STEVENS--Melvin. Dearest friend. We lived in tandem for 75 years. I will miss you terribly old buddy. Rest in peace. Bob Wiener
