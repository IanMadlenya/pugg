
        MAXFIELD-Leona H. Of Greenwich, Ct., formerly of Manhattan, on May 8, 2001 at age 98. She had a long and successful career at Bigelow-Sanford Carpet Company. Widow of William Reginald Maxfield. She is survived by his son Henry of Wolfeboro, NH and his family. Originally from Salt Lake City, she was a niece of lyricist and playwright Otto A. Harbach. Elegant, outspoken, lively and witty, she was loved by many and will be missed. A Memorial Service will be held on Monday, July 16th at 1 PM in the Chapel at Christ Church, Greenwich, 254 East Putnam Avenue.
