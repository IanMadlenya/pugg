
        JOSEPHSON-Jean C. (McCall) of Monroe Twp., NJ, died Tuesday, March 2. Wife of the late Emanuel Josephson, M.D. Former comptroller of United Cerebral Palsy Association, NY. The great great niece of King Christian IX of Denmark & granddaughter of Prince John of Denmark, she held the title of Princess Dagmar. Services 11 A.M., Friday at Lester Memorial Home, 16 West Church Street, Jamesburg, NJ. Interment at Mount Olivet, Queens, New York. Memorials to Assoc. Humane Society of NJ or ASPCA of NY.
