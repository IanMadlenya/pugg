
        ADELMAN-Milton H., M.D. The faculty and staff of the Department of Anesthesiology of New York University Medical Center and the entire community of the New York University School of Medicine wish to express our profound sorrow on the passing of our esteemed colleague, Milton H. Adelman, on February 26, 1998. Dr. Adelman had been a faculty member and a Clinical Professor of Anesthesiology in the Department of Anesthesiology at NYU School of Medicine since 1991. Upon earning his B.S. from New York University in 1931 and his M.D. from the University of Maryland, Dr. Adelman began his professional career at the Hospital for Joint Diseases where he spent from 1941 to 1946 as an Associate Anesthesiologist. In 1942 he was appointed an Assistant Attendant Anesthesiologist at the Mount Sinai Hospital, where he remained for most of his career. Dr. Adelman was a member of several prestigious professional organizations, including The American Society of Anesthesiologists and the New York State Society of Anesthesiologists, and a Fellow of both the American College of Anesthesiologists and New York Academy of Medicine. Noel L. Cohen, M.D. Interim Dean Herman Turndorf, M.D., Chairman Department of Anesthesiology NYU School of Medicine
