
        CRISONA--James J., 96, on September 4, 2003. A graduate of NYU Law School, he had a long and distinguished career as an attorney in NYC. He was a director and general counsel of the Hudson Manhattan Railroad from 1948-1954 and later a director and general counsel of the Phoenix Campbell Corporation. A dedicated public servant, he was elected to the NYS Assembly in 1946, to the NYS Senate in 1955 and, in 1957, was elected Borough President of Queens County. From 1959 to 1976 he was a NYS Justice of the Supreme Court. He served for two decades on the Board of Directors of various Shearson, later Smith Barney, mutual funds. Beloved father of Claire Cascella and Cynthia Crisona, loving grandfather of Elizabeth Auran and Nancy Cascella and their husbands James Auran and Steven Flatow, and greatgrandfather of four. His devoted wife, Claire Peysson Crisona, died in 1990 after 55 years of marriage. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave (at 81st St) today from 5-8PM. Mass of the Resurrection on Saturday, September 6 at 10AM at Our Lady of Peace Church, 237 East 62 St. Interment private.
