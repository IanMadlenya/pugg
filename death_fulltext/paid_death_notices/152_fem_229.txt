
        ADAMS-Romaine Bristow. Of Austin, Texas, New York City, and Newport, R.I. and wife of Warren S. Adams II. Died peacefully in NYC on September 25, 1999. She was the beloved mother of Louise Wheeler Grosvenor. Adored grandmother of Charles Burnett Grosvenor Jr. of Austin, Texas, Leslie Grosvenor Holloway of New Canaan, Connecticut and a devoted great-grandmother of Madeline Louise Holloway, Alexander Grosvenor Holloway and Claire Cordelia Grosvenor. She was dearly loved by her family and greatly admired by her many friends. Her great-grandfather Benjamin Helm Bristow was Secretary of the Treasury in President Grant's Administration. She was the daughter of Louise and William Bristow of NYC and Newport. She was a member of the Colonial Dames of America, The Colony Club of NYC and the Spouting Rock Beach Association & the Clambake Club of Newport. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to the Youth Foundation, Inc., 36 West 44 St, NYC 10036. A funeral service will be held at St. James Church, 865 Madison Avenue, NYC 11AM on Tuesday, October 5, 1999.
        ADAMS-Romaine B. The Board of Directors of Youth Foundation, Inc. deeply regrets the passing of its distinguished Director Emerita, Mrs. Warren S. Adams 2nd, whose contributions to the foundation were significant. Patricia P. Fulweiler, President Edward F.L. Bruen, Vice President Henry Middendorf, Jr., Secretary
