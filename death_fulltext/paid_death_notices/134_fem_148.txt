
        BRINCKERHOFF-Florence Andreen. Born on August 27, 1904, died on March 17, 1998 at her home in New York City after a long illness. Widow of Charles M. Brinckerhoff who was a recipient of the Hoover Medal and CEO of Anaconda Copper Mining Company. Private family service at Greenfield Cemetery, Long Island. Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home.
