
        GOLDMAN-Bob (Jerome). Of Westport, CT, age 88, died peacefully at his home on May 30, 2002. His life was one of integrity & caring, sharing joys with family & friends. He will be dearly missed by his wife Stemmie, daughters: Sue and Gwen, sons-in-law: Jay Rosenblum and Peter McLoughlin, & grandchildren: Steve, Sarah & Abby. Services Monday, June 3, 11:30 AM, from Abraham L. Green & Son, 88 Beach Rd., Fairfield, CT. The family will be at home on Tuesday, June 4, after noon.
