
        REESE-William Willis. The Board of Assistants of The Society of Mayflower Descendants in the State of NY mourn the death of our Deputy Governor, William Willis Reese. His generosity, loyalty and warmth of spirit will be sorely missed. We extend our sympathies to his widow, Sonia Van Voorhis Reese. Walter R. Brewster, Jr., Governor Irene C. Coleman, Secretary
        REESE-William Willis. The Officers and Members of the Long I Grotto No. 44 mourn the passing of our beloved Monarch, William Willis Reese, and offer condolences to his widow, Sania, and his family. Michael W. Friedman, VP Dr. Thomas H. McCarthy III, VP Eugene Carlson, Secretary William G. Smith, Treasurer
