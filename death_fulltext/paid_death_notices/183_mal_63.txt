
        FEIGIN-Mollie (nee Kanowitz). Born March 26, 1917, Brooklyn, N.Y. Died peacefully on April 4, 2002 at home in Flushing, N.Y. Beloved wife of Dr. Irwin Feigin. Cherished mother and mother-inlaw of Dr. Joel S. Feigin and Dr. Severine Neff, and attorney Michael and his wife Diane (Feigin) Badzinski. Mollie was a piano student of Josef and Rosina Lhevinne at the Juilliard School. ''Music when soft voices die, vibrates in the memory.''
