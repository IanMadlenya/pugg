
        REID-Mabel. Died February 24, 1999. Born 1906, Jamaica, NY. Graduated from Vassar College and Columbia University. Resident of Garden City, NY for 27 years, Bridgeport, CT final five years. Retired as Director of Statistics and Studies, Visiting Nurse Service of NY, in 1966 after 30-year career. Evaluated rural nursing programs in Indian and Thailand at request of Indian and Thai governments under auspices of World Health Organization of United Nations. She is survived by four nieces, one nephew. Interment at Mountain Grove Cemetery, Bridgeport, CT. Contributions to Vassar College, Poughkeepsie, NY.
