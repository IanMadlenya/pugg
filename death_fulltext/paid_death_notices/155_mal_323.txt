
        AGRIN-Irving N., M.D. Died on December 24 at age 82. In family practice for over 50 years, he will be dearly missed by three generations of patients. He is survived by his wife Alice, his sons Richard and Glen, his daughters in-law Alice and Lynn, and his grandchildren Rachel, Deborah, and Nathan. Funeral on Monday, December 27, 11:30 AM at Riverside Chapel, Great Neck.
