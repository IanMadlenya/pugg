
        PARCELLS - Elizabeth, 54. Coloratura soprano, died of cancer on December 29, 2005 at her home in Michigan. In 1977, she won the Pro Musicis International Award and performed 23 recitals under their sponsorship. For 30 years, she performed in opera productions in Europe and America, and shared her glorious voice with audiences in prisons and hospitals. www.elizabethparcells.com
