
        BURR-Robert Page. On December 31, 1998, of Matinicus Island, Maine, formerly of Lloyd Harbor, NY. Beloved husband of Elizabeth, loving father of Susan, William, James, Jonathan, Elizabeth, and David, and devoted grandfather of ten grandchildren. Born in Mineola, NY, on September 28, 1922, the son of Robert Page Burr and Laurence Hewlett, Mr. Burr attended Princeton University, graduated with a degree in electrical engineering from Cornell University and served as an officer in the Navy during World War II. During his career, Mr. Burr made significant contributions to some of the most important technologies of our time, for which he received numerous patents and awards. He retired as Senior Scientific Officer of Kollmorgen Corporation in 1987. A service will be held aboard M/V SUNBEAM in Rockport Harbor, ME, on Monday, January 11 at 10 AM. A memorial service will be held on Matinicus Island, ME in early June. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Maine Seacoast Missionary Society, Bar Harbor, ME.
