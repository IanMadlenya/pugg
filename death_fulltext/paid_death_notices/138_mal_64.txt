
        GARBE-Ernest H. On June 28, 1998, peacefully at home in the house of his birth in his beloved Brooklyn. He was devoted to his family, his church and his country. A W.W. II veteran, he served as a non-commissioned officer, 389th Port Battalion, U.S. Army, European Theater. A graduate of Columbia College, in New York City, and a true believer in the fraternal, moral and patriotic principles of his beloved Phi Kappa Psi Fraternity, he lived and served by the principles he learned so well. Service Wednesday at 10 A.M., Church of the Virgin Mary, 216 Eighth Avenue, Brooklyn.
