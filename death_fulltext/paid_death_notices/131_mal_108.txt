
        EPSTEIN-H. Broadman. On December 6, 1997. Prior to opening his own practice in 1946, he was acting Corporate Counsel for NYC. He retired in 1982 and became honorary Trustee of the Union Temple in Brooklyn, where he had been Chairman of the Social Action Committee. Beloved husband of the late Helen. Devoted father of Sylvia Cuomo and Lois Bianchi. Cherished grandfather and greatgrandfather. Services Tuesday, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
