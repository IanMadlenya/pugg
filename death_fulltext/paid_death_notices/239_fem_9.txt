
        CHALLOP--Miriam (nee Schreiber), November 30, 2006, age 89. Predeceased by husband, Irving, and daughter, Gail Backer. Beloved mother of Dr. Roger Challop, wife Louise, Dr. Judith Luhr, husband Dr. William, and Glenn Backer. Devoted grandmother to Lissa, Glenn, Rebecca, Amanda, Paul, and David, and great-grandson Jonathon. Memorial service Sunday, Riverside Chapel in Mt. Vernon, 12 noon. She lived with great strength, dignity, and love for her family. We miss her terribly.
