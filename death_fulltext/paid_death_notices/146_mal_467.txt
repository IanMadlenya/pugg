
        KLAPPER-Herbert. Beloved husband of Adele. Treasured father of Bonnie (Lonny) and Wendie. Adored Poppy of Brandon and Ryan Schwartz. The Director and Board of Trustees of The Nassau County Museum of Art in Roslyn Harbor, NY would like to express their deepest sorrow for the recent passing of their fellow Board Member, Mr. Herbert Klapper of Hewlett Harbor, NY.
