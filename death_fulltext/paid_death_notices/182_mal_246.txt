
        COLEMAN-Richard J., Ph.D. Age 77 of Stamford, CT passed away suddenly and unexpectedly March 14, 2002. Beloved husband of Dory, deeply mourned by his children Richard Jr., Maryanne, Mark and Loretta. Survived by loving grandchildren Ashley, John and Elizabeth. Dr. Coleman was formerly Vice President of Florasynth, Inc. of New York City. A wake will be held at Gallagher, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, CT. on Sunday, March 17 at 5-8PM. Services on Monday the 18th at St. Michael's Church, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT.
