
        STEIN-Max K., 89, passed away quietly on April 3 in Atlanta. Formerly of Hempstead, NY. He is survived by his wife Mathilda, children Judith and Kenneth, grandchildren Richard, Andrew, Jason and Todd and former daughter-in-law Ellen. Funeral services today April 4, in Atlanta, GA. Contributions in his honor to the Mathilda and Max Stein Lectureship in Israeli and Jewish History, Dept of History, Emory University, Atlanta, GA 30322. 404-851-9900
