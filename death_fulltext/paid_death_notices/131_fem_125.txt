
        RUGG-Dayle R. M.D. The Board of Trustees, Medical Board, and Administration of Beth Israel Medical Center join the physicians and staff of the Department of Neurology in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague Dayle R. Rugg, M.D. Dr. Rugg will be sorely missed by all who knew and worked with her. To the entire Rugg family, and all who loved her, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President Jerome S. Breslaw, M.D., President Medical Board Susan Bressman, M.D., Chairman Department of Neurology
