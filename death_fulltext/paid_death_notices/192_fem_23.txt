
        ALFOND-Anne Loewengart. On January 3, 2003. Beloved daughter of the late Julius and Thea Loewengart. Sister of the late Robert Loewengart, and loving mother of Steven. Anne was a lifelong resident of Manhattan and a graduate of Upsala College. She was a producer of the Herb Sheldon Show and a former magazine editor. She is deeply mourned by her family and will be greatly missed by all who knew her. Services will be held Sunday, January 5, 11:15 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        ALFOND-Anne Loewengart. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to the family of Anne Alfond, especially her loving son, Steven. We share your sorrow. The William Carr Family
