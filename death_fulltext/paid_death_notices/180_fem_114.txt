
        GOSSNER-Simone. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of NewYorkPresbyterian Hospital and Weill Medical College of Cornell University are deeply saddened by the passing of our friend and generous benefactor, Simone Gossner. She was a longtime member of the NewYork Weill Cornell Council and an enthusiastic supporter of many programs at the Medical Center. Our sincerest sympathies are extended to her husband, Joseph, and her family. Herbert Pardes, M.D. President and CEO NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D. Stephen and Suzanne Weiss Dean Weill Medical College of Cornell University Jeffrey W. Greenberg Chairman NewYork Weill Cornell Council
