
        CLAUS-Carl. Of Chappaqua, New York, died on May 20. He was 92. He was born in Berlin, Germany and emigrated with his parents, the late Dr. Carl Claus and Elsie Regling Claus, to the United States when he was an infant. He graduated from Plainfield High School in Plainfield, NJ in 1927 and Lehigh University in Bethlehem, PA in 1931. After graduation from college, he began a career with the Babcock & Wilcox Company that spanned 43 years and culminated in his retirement in 1974 as Corporate Vice President. During his tenure with Babcock & Wilcox, he served in various positions in Barberton, OH, Bayonne, NJ, Augusta, GA, and New York City. He is a former member of the Delta Upsilon fraternity, the Augusta Country Club, the Augusta Rotary Club of which he was a past president, and the former Cloud Club of New York City of which he was also a past president. He was a longstanding member of the Whippoorwill Country Club in Armonk, NY and served on its Board of Governors and as the Club's 'Santa Claus' for many years. He is survived by his wife of 65 years, Ruby Miller Claus, his son, Carl M. Claus, and two grandchildren, Lois F. Claus and Carl R. Claus. Interment will be private. Memorial contributions may be made to the New Castle Police Benevolent Association or to the Chappaqua Volunteer Ambulance Corps.
