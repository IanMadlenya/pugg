
        SCHLISSEL-Helene Pudno s, 48. Died August 22, 2000 in Jerusalem, Israel. Survived by husband, Saulie, children, Menachem (age 10) and Tsippi (age 8), and twin sister, Charlotte (Chaya) Berman. Loved by friends and relatives in the United States and Israel for her loyalty, generosity, intelligence, humor, creativity, extraordinary energy and spirit. A memorial for Helene will be sponsored by the Zamir Choral Foundation on September 17th at 11 A.M., at Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun, 125 East 85th Street, Manhattan.
