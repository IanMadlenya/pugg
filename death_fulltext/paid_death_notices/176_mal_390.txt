
        NIVEN-John Ballantine, 44. Of New York and Oyster Bay, on September 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Ellen and devoted father of 18month-old John Jr., (Jack). Born in Oyster Bay, NY, on July 5, 1957, he attended East Woods School, Salisbury School in CT, and Lake Forest College, IL. At the time of his death he was a Senior Vice President in mergers and acquisitionis at AON Risk Services, an insurance firm on the 105th floor of tower two, World Trade Center. He was a member of the St. Nicholas Society of New York. He was predeceased by his father, William Niven of Oyster Bay, NY. In addition to his wife and son, he is survived by his mother, Mrs. William Niven, his brother, William, his sisters, Susan and Wendy. A Memorial Service was held on September 18th at St. John's Episcopal Church in Cold Spring Harbor, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in John's name to Society for the Preservation of Long Island Antiquities, PO Box 148, Cold Spring Harbor, New York 11724.
