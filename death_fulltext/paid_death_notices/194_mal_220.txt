
        OWENS-John A. On March 23, 2003, at the age of 99. Resident of Westhampton Beach, NY; formerly of Garden City, NY. Retired president of Lachman, Novasel, Owens Paper Corporation. Predeceased by his wife of fiftythree years, Mae Nevins Owens, he is survived by his children: Rosemary McAllister, John T., Esq., R. Kevin, Esq., seven grandchildren, three great - grandchildren, and his dear friend, Barbara Goodridge. The family will receive friends at Follett & Werner Funeral Home, 60 Mill Road, Westhampton Beach, NY, on Monday, March 24, from 7 to 9 PM, and on Tuesday, March 25, from 2 to 4 PM, and 7 to 9 PM. Mass of the Resurrection will be celebrated on Wednesday, March 26, at 11:30 AM, at the Church of the Immaculate Conception, 580 Main St, Quiogue, NY. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate donations to the Quan Fund, The Harvard Medical School, 401 Park Drive, Boston, MA 02215.
