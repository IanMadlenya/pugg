
        LIEBMAN-Charles J., a scholar, author and collector, died on September 24, 1997. He was 87 years old. Mr. Liebman attended Harvard College and subsequently moved to Paris to study at the Ecole des Hautes Etudes. He received his diploma after submitting a thesis entitled ''La Vie Prose de St. Denys''. Mr. Liebman's publications include numerous articles on medieval literature and a book regarding the life of Simon de Tournai. He was often consulted by the Morgan Library to assist in the identification of manuscripts in its possession. He collected rare maps of Paris, which he exhibited at the Grolier Club, of which he was a member. Mr. Liebman is survived by his loving sister, Margaret L. Berger and brother-inlaw, Adolph R. Berger, M.D.
