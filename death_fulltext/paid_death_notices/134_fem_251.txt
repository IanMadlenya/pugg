
        ROTHENBERG-Sylvia Gold Rothenberg, age eighty-one. Of Bal Harbour, Florida, passed away on March 24, 1998. Born in New York, Mrs. Rothenberg was a long-time resident of Jersey City, N.J., before moving to Florida. With her husband Artie, she was an active partner in their business which was named for her, the ''Sylvette Shops''. Mrs. Rothenberg was a member of Temple Beth El in Jersey City, the Crestmont Country Club in West Orange, NJ, Hadassah, and the Brandeis University Women's Book Club. Locally she was a Founder of Mount Sinai Medical Center, served on the Board of the Bal Harbour Towers, and was a member of Westview Country Club. Mrs. Rothenberg is survived by her beloved husband Artie to whom she was married for sixtyfour years, children Lois Weiss, Dr. Kenneth Rothenberg, Dr. Trisha Roth, brother and sisters, Artie Gold, Shirley Batherman, Rose Schindelheim, Irma Marantz, Madeline Kesselman, Hermine Gewirtz, Janice Feinson, twelve grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. Funeral Services will be held at 11:00 A.M., Friday, March 27, 1998 at the Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 1920 Alton Road, Miami Beach, Florida (305) 531-1151. Donations in Mrs. Rothenberg's memory may be made to a charity of choice.
