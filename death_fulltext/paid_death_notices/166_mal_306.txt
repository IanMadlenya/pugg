
        CEPPOS-Jeffrey. On November 25, 2000, at age 58 after a courageous battle with cancer. Loving husband of Sandy. Devoted father of Samantha. A highly respected licensing executive in fashion, cosmetics and sports marketing. He was most recently Vice President of International Management Group, president of Jeffrey Ceppos, Inc., also former Vice President of Estee Lauder's Aramis Division. For funeral information please call Gallagher's Funeral Home, 203-327-1313. Contributions in his memory may be made to Visiting Nurse and Hospice Care of Southwestern CT, 1029 East Main St., Stamford, CT 06902 or the Center for Hope, 590 Post Rd., Darien, CT 06820.
