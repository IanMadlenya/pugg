
        CALVO-Antonio. Born in 1907 in Riobao, Sada, Spain. Died peacefully at his home on July 21, 2000. Beloved husband of Erundina for 60 years, cherished father of Asuncion (Sue) Otero, father-in-law to Joseph A. Otero, adored grandfather of Alessandra Otero Reiss, Antonio Jose Otero and Mark R. Reiss, great-grandfather of Matteo Joseph Reiss, and dear uncle of many. Ex-President of ''La Nacional.'' Founding member and former President of Spanish American Citizens Club of Queens. Member of ''Casa Galicia.'' Reposing at Peter R. Angerame Funeral Home, 27-12 23rd Ave., Astoria, Saturday and Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Monday, 9:30 AM, at St. Francis of Assisi R.C. Church, 21st Ave., Astoria.
