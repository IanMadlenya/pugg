
        SIMON--Helen K. (Flowers), of Manhattan, died peacefully on August 12, 2005, at the age of 94. Wife of the late H. Theodore Simon of New York, and secondly of the late James P. Flowers. She is survived by her only child, her daughter Elaine Simon Ward (Mrs. Ralph E. Ward) of Darien, CT, and London. Born in Quogue, LI, she was a former Trustee of the Mannes College of Music in New York. From 1948 until the early 1960's she and her husband Theodore Simon designed and created handwrought sterling silver objects in a workshop in their townhouse. Their work is represented in the permanent collections of The Museum of the City of New York and the CooperHewitt Museum. Funeral Mass at the Church of St. Thomas More, 65 E. 89th St, NYC, on Saturday, August 20th, at 10 AM. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Lenox Hill Hospital, attn: Dr. Stuart Orsher, in her memory.
