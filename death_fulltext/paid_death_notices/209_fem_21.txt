
        WOLINETZ--Naomi. AMIT is deeply grieved by the untimely passing of an astute communal leader and beloved AMIT national board member who, together with her most devoted husband Harvey, generously contributed to the support of thousands of needy AMIT children. A true ''Woman of Valor'', Naomi Wolinetz, Z''L, will be fondly remembered for her charming and warm demeanor, her loving kindness, artistic skills and deep devotion to Torah values and the State of Israel. Heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Jan Schechter, National Pres Marvin Leff, Exec Director
