
        WOLFERMAN-Irene Schleider. Of Manhattan, formerly of Brooklyn, passed away suddenly on December 28, 2001. Adored and loving mother of Steven and Peter Schleider. Devoted mother-in-law to Beth and Mimi. Loving grandmother to Alex, David, Jake, Jessica, and Ali. Beloved wife to her late husbands, Edgar Schleider and Dr. Adolph Wolferman. Dear stepmother and step-grandmother to Eric, Mark, Sandra, Pat, Cathy, Cassie, Joanne, Benjamin, Anna, & Brian. Irene was an accomplished artist and an exceptional bridge player. Her beautiful smile and independent spirit will be lovingly remembered.
