
        BEAUMONT-Alan. Former NBC director, died July 19 at home in Lakeville, Connecticut. An early television pioneer, he directed many live television programs, including Wide Wide World and The Home Show with Arlene Francis & Hugh Downs. Mr. Beaumont also directed the country's first-ever color broadcast. Born in British Columbia in 1919 and graduated from the University of Wisconsin, Mr. Beaumont was married for fifty-seven years to Jean Beaumont, who died in 1999. He is survived bu daughter Diana, son Terence, sisters Doris and Donna, and brothers Guy and Denis. Funeral services will be private.
