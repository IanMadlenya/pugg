
        MACGREGOR - John. Of New York and Salisbury, Ct. On July 25, at home, after a brief illness. He was born in 1925 in Michigan, and grew up in Western New York State. A graduate of Yale, he was a world traveler, an interior designer, and a longtime member & former President of the Blue Hill Troupe, Ltd. He is survived by 2 nephews, John Kaplan of Hingham, MA., and Mitchell Kaplan of San Francisco. A memorial service will be held Thursday, Sept. 5 at 2 o'clock PM at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue at 50th Street, Manhattan. Memorial contributions may be made to the Blue Hill Troupe Fund, P.O. Box 286800, Yorkville Station, New York, New York 10128.
