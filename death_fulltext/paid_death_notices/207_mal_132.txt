
        EBERLIN--Monroe M. On April 10, 2004, at the age of 84. Much loved husband of Marjorie Kelly Eberlin for 62 years. Loving father of Anne Roush and Janet Larkin. Adoring grandfather of Katie, Danny and Tommy. Graduate of New York University, School of Engineering as well as an Army veteran of World War II and Korea. Friends may call on Tuesday, April 13, from 2-4 pm, and 7-9 pm, at John J. Fox Funeral Home, Larchmont. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his name to the American Diabetes Association.
