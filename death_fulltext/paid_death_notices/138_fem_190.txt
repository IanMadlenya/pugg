
        COTLIAR-Laura Lee (Sporn) died July 26, 1998. Wife of Dr. Arthur Cotliar. Loving mother of Jeremy Michael and Dustin Lee Cotliar. Dear sister of Eddie Sporn and Tova Gazala. Beloved daughter of the late Elinore Sporn and the late Abraham Sporn; dear daughter-inlaw of William & Eleanor Cotliar. Beloved sister-in-law and aunt; cherished friend. Talented actress and passionate supporter of Israel. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, N.Y. on July 28th. Her spark, talents and passion will be missed by us all.
