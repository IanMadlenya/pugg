
        SATZ--Norman M., 77, of Bronx, N.Y. A kind and generous man who loved poetry died suddenly on April 26, 2004. Devoted husband of Frances, father and friend to his children Gary and Debra. Loving grandfather. A W.W. II Veteran, who participated in the defeat of fascism--and a mensch. Services will be held at Castle Hill Funeral Home, 1528 Castle Hill Ave., Bronx, N.Y. on April 28 at noon.
