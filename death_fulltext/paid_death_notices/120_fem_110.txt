
        CROSSMAN-Erla (Jackson), in Chestnut Hill, MA., formerly of Fresh Meadows, N.Y., on January 15, 1997. Wife of the late Ralph Crossman. Mrs. Crossman was a graduate of Massachusetts College of Pharmacy Class, of 1922 and retired from NYC Health Dept. Survived by her daughter Erla Coburn Manetta of Chestnut Hill, MA. Interment of Ashes will be Babylon Cemetery, Babylon, New York, on Saturday, January 25, 1997 at 1:00 PM.
