
        FROEHLICH-Monroe, Jr., 85, died January 25 in Rancho Mirage, CA. Beloved and devoted husband of the late Doris Adelberg and Marjorie K. Densen. Loving father of Roger and Peggy Froehlich and Barbara and Albert Gross. Cherished grandfather of Scott and Shannon Froehlich, Karen and Michael Petrotta and Jason Gross. Unbounding generosity, the highest ideals of sportsmanship, and complete dedication to his family and friends marked his life. Funeral services private. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Connecticut Association for Children with Learning Disabilities, 25 Van Zant St. 15-5, East Norwalk, CT 06855-1719.
