
        DANIEL--John E., born in Yonkers, NY, January 15, 1948. Son of deceased immigrant parents from Czechoslovakia, Emrich Daniel and Anna Behun Daniel. Brother of deceased Zlata Daniel Zidik. Survived by his beloved wife Janet Nolan, nephew John Zidik (Christine), and niece Jana Zidik Krieger. He was a Princeton University graduate, Class of 1970, and Stanford Law School graduate, Class of 1973. He was an avid golfer at Siwanoy CC in Bronxville, NY. Donations can be made to his favorite charities: Gilda's Club Worldwide: 322 Eighth Avenue, Suite 1402, New York, NY 10001; Jumpstart MidAtlantic Region: 505 Eighth Avenue, Suite 602, New York, NY 10018; and Princeton University (Class of 1970): P.O. Box 5357, Princeton, NJ 08543-5357. A memorial tribute for John will be held Tuesday May 15, 2007 at 4pm at The Princeton Club of New York: 15 West 43rd Street (between 5th and 6th Avenues).
        $(6$)DANIEL--John E. The partners, attorneys and staff of Kramer Levin Naftalis & Frankel LLP mourn the untimely passing of our partner and friend, John Daniel. John was a leader within our Intellectual Property Group, a valued colleague to his clients and respected by his peers. John was elected Treasurer of the New York Intellectual Property Law Association and recently nominated for Second Vice President. John was born in Yonkers, NY in 1948. He was a 1970 cum laude graduate of Princeton University and earned his J.D. from Stanford Law School in 1973. John was a true gentlemen, a brilliant lawyer and a friend to many. His wit, wisdom and ability to light up a room with laughter and song will long be remembered.
