
        SEGAL-Bernard G. The American Friends of The Hebrew University is deeply saddened by the passing of our long time supporter and Council of Trustee member, Bernard G. Segal. A man deeply committed to Jewish causes, the Law Library at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, was named after him. We extend deepest condolences to his entire family. The American Friends of The Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs President Lawrence Newman, Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan, Executive Vice President
