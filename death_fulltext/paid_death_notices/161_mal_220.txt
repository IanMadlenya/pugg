
        BARTLE-Thomas P., Jr., died on June 16th, 2000 of lung cancer in Southampton Hospital at age 60. He graduated from Yale University and Wharton Business School and was a partner of the executive search firm of Wesley, Brown & Bartle. He is survived by his wife, Angelika, his son, Thomas, and his stepchildren, Vanessa and Chris Morrison. There will be a private burial and memorial service at St. Ann's Church in Bridgehampton.
