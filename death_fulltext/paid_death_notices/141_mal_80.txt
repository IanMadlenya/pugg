
        SHEPPARD-Carl Dunkle, Jr. Age 82, died Saturday, October 3, 1998 in Santa Fe, NM. Mr. Sheppard retired to Santa Fe in 1983 as Emeritus Professor and Chair of the Department of Art History, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis. He served as President of the International Center of Medieval Art, on the Boards of the College Art Assocation, the Center Opera Company, the James J. Hill Library, St. Paul; the Santa Fe Symphony Orchestra and the New Mexico Historical Society. He received his doctorate in Fine Arts at Harvard University in 1947.
