
        FOSTER--Zelda. A valued member of the Beth Israel Medical Center Institutional Review Board, Ms. Foster was well known for her work in the hospice movement and social work. She was the Chief of Social Work Services for the VA Medical Center in Brooklyn and held faculty positions at New York University, Smith College and Columbia University Schools of Social Work. Arnold Winston, MD, Chair, BIMC Institutional Review Board, Martha Alvarado, MPH, Director, Human Subjects Protection Office, Keith King, IRB Manager and members of the IRB Board express their condolences to Zelda's family.
