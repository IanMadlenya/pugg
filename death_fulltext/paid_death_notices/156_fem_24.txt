
        STADTER-Jean. On December 26, 1999, Jean Silverstein Stadter closed her eyes in peace. She is survived by her wards Carole Reed, Roy Caraballo, Elizabeth Gross and a host of friends who will miss her dearly. The family requests donations of remembrance be made in her name to the New York Chapter, Hemophiliac Foundation. Memorial scheduled for Friday, January 7, 2000, at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave., from 3-7.
