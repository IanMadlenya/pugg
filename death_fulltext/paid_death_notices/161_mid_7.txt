
        CITRON-Florence nee Shapiro. On July 11, 2000 at the age of 92, of White Plains, (formerly of Scarsdale) NY. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Victor N. Citron. Devoted mother of Hannah and her husband Walter Shmerler of Armonk, NY, Neil Citron and his wife Ida Ruth of Hilton Head, SC, and George Citron and his wife Ellen of Boston, MA. Also survived by six grandchildren and three greatgrandchildren. Dear sister of Marcia Terner of NYC and the late Pauline Reibel and sister-in-law of Ruth Citron formerly of White Plains, NY. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to Sarah Neuman Center, 845 Palmer Avenue, Mamaroneck, New York 10543.
