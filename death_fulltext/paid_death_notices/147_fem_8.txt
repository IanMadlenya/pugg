
        FISHER-Ruth V. On March 31, 1999 in Aventura, Fla. at the age of 88. A 1st grade teacher in the NYC public schools for over 30 years, she continued to give generously in service to her community in retirement. Loving mother of daughter, Jody (Joan) Young and son-in-law Larry Young. She was also proud grandmother of Eliot, Leslie, and Robert Young and GG (great-grandmother) to Josh and Evan Young. She joins her husband of 58 years (Samuel G. Fisher) who died in 1991. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Quincy Bog Natural Area, Rumney, NH 03266.
