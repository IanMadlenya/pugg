
        GOLDFARB-Mary Jane Regan, 52. On September 25, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Jack. Cherished daughter of Wilma Regan. Loving mother of Carolyn Goldfarb and Candice Goldfarb Posner. Beloved sister of Jacqueline McSwiggan. Her family will miss her strength, warmth and keen sense of humor. A memorial service will be held at Central Presbyterian Church, 593 Park Ave. at 64th St., New York City, Tuesday, September 29, at 2:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation or American Cancer Society.
