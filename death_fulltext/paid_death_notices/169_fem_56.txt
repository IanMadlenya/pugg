
        FRANKENTHALER - Bernice. Feb. 5, 2001. Loving wife of the late Norman, beloved mother of Judy Mydans of Denver Co., and Robert Frankenthaler of Boston Ma. Devoted grandmother of Dan, Lindsay, Amy, Jill, and Scott. A dedicated educator in the New York City school system for 32 years. Her career culminated as Principal of Robert Wagner Junior High School in New York City. She also served as a volunteer in many city-wide agencies. A woman full of life & energy, who gave to many, who will be missed by all. Services are at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, 81st Street, on Wednesday, February 7, 4 PM.
