
        WHITE-Ewart John, Jr. A long time resident of Princeton, New Jersey, and a 1942 graduate of Princeton University. He passed away April 17, 1998 in Phippsburg, ME. Mr. White served in the U.S. Army during W.W.II in Germany and France. His career achievements included promoting the international expansion of Kopman Mills and Crompton Mills. He was Chairman of the Textile Market Committee of the American Textile Manufacturers' Institute and active in land procurement for the Phippsburg Maine Land Trust. He is survived by his wife of fifty-seven years, Phoebe Burnham White, three sons, Ewart John White, III, Peter B. White and William E. White and five grandchildren. Gifts may be sent to the Phippsburg Land Trust, Phippsburg, ME. 04562 or to the Small Point Summer School, 105 Bonnie Hill Road, Towson, MD 21204.
