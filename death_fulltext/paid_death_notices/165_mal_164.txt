
        LIEBERMAN-Hillel. Tha National Jewish Outreach Program mourns the brutal murder of Rabbi Hillel Lieberman, H.Y.D., killed while on his way to save sacred Jewish books from desecration by rioting Palestinians. Condolences to his wife and children, his parents, Rabbi and Mrs. Zevulun Lieberman, his sister Elyorah (and Ian) Ellison, devoted friends of NJOP and his entire family. Yehi Zichro Baruch. Rabbi Ephraim Buchwald, Dir.
