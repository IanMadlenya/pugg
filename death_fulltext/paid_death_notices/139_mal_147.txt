
        LEMLE-Henry C, born July 1, 1910 died August 11, 1998. Henry lived most of his life in NY and was actively engaged in real estate and insurance. After his retirement he moved to Jackson Hole, Wyoming where he spent the remainder of his days enjoying the beauty of the mountains and the Western way of life. He was a lover of art, music and most particularily of rare books. He died peacefully surrounded by members of his family. Henry is survived by his children Florence, Michael, Deanne, Douglas, his long time companion Edna Fuerth Lemle and his sister Julie Feuer. Funeral services will be held at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. & Amsterdam Avenue, Friday August 14 at 10am and a memorial service will be held in Jackson in September.
