
        SIDERMAN-Monte. On August 24, 1998. Beloved spouse of the late Beatrice Kreindler Siderman and thereafter of Florence Shientag Gluck. Distinguished pharmacist. Also associated 40 years with the 21 Club. Dear uncle of Howard and Irene and Gary and Arlene Lustig. His many friends and admirers will miss his noble nature and delightful sense of humor.
