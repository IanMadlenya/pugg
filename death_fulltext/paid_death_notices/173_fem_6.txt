
        GRUBE-Nancy L. Died on May 30, 2001. Devoted employee of Jos E. Seagram & Sons, Inc, for 37 years. Beloved friend and coworker. She will be missed by many people whose lives she has touched. A memorial service will be held Sunday, June 3, 2001, from 2-5 PM, with a religious service at 3 PM, at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 125 Hillside Ave (at city line), New Hyde Park, NY.
