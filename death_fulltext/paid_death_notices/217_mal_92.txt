
        LAERI--John H. Jr., son of the late J. Howard and Betty C. Laeri of Greenwich, CT, died February 2, 2005 at his home in Fairfield, CT surrounded by his family. Mr. Laeri was born on December 21, 1935, Laeri was a graduate of Deerfield Academy and Williams College with honors in 1958. Following college he joined the United States Marine Corp. He began his career at First Boston Corporation in 1959. In 1961 Mr. Laeri worked for World Bank and served as special assistant to George Woods, President of World Bank. From 1963 to 1978 Mr. Laeri worked at Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette and Hornblower & Weeks, Hemphill, Noyes. In 1979 rejoined First Boston as Managing Director. Mr. Laeri established the largest Reorganization Group at that time on Wall Street. After retiring from First Boston in 1990, Mr. Laeri established and served as Chairman of Meadowcroft Associates Inc., a private investment banking firm located in Westport, CT. Mr. Laeri was also Chairman and owner of The GolfCoach Inc. At his passing Mr. Laeri served on several Board of Directors and Trusts. Mr. Laeri is survived by his beloved wife Kember and their three sons John, William and Christopher, a sister Sue Plein and her children Jim, Julie and Todd, two step-children John and Beth, and his uncle Robert Cochrane. Memorial service is being held on February 8 at 11:30 A.M. at St. Timothy's Episcopal Church in Fairfield, CT. In lieu of flowers donations in Mr. Laeri's memory to Deerfield Academy, 7 Boyden Lane, Deerfield, MA. 01342.
