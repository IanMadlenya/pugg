
        PRITCHETT-Jane Harrison. Beloved wife of Rees. Loving mother of Susan Pritchett Post & motherin-law of Edward Everett Post, Jr. Devoted grandmother of James (Jacko) and Rosalie Claudianna (Anna). Dear sister of Hadelyn Morrow. Mrs. Pritchett died on Tuesday, August 14, in Glens Falls, New York, after a short bronchial infection followed by a stroke. She was born Nov. 23, 1924, in North River, New York, the daughter of the late Fred and Hazel Hudson Harrison. A memorial service will be held at the Helen Hughes Memorial Chapel at the Silver Bay Association, Silver Bay, N.Y., 2 P.M., Saturday, August 18. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Silver Bay Association, Silver Bay, New York, 12874.
        PRITCHETT-Jane Harrison. Rees Pritchett, M.D., our distinguished colleague. We extend our deepest condolences to Dr. Pritchett and his family. Antonio M.
