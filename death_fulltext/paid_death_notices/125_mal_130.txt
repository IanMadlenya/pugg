
        KELLY-Joseph A., CPIM, CPM, CIRM, director of materials management for Bristol-Myers Squibb company of Princeton, N.J., died Tuesday, May 27, 1997 at Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital in New Brunswick, NJ. He was 46. Joseph graduated with honors from Rutgers University in 1973 with a BS degree in Industrial Engineering and a BA in Economics. In 1976 Joseph received his MBA from Syracuse University in Operations Management. The son of the late Joseph P. Kelly. Joseph is survived by his mother, Rose Kelly; his fiancee, Deborah Parker; her children Elizabeth, Laura, and Katie Parker; and four sibblings, Nancy K. Weimer; Thomas Kelly; Janet Payne; Mark Kelly; and grandfather Edward Klaus.
