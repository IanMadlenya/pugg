
        BEROLATTI--Louis Joseph, on March 20, 2007, of New Rochelle, NY. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Ann, devoted father of Michael and Mary Beth, grandfather of Ryan and Kyle Bowen. Alumnus of the University of Illinois and the University of Chicago. He served as First Lieutenant in the USMC before a long career with Union Carbide. His quiet strength, keen intellect and dry humor have always been admired by his family and friends. Reposing at Lloyd Maxie & Sons, Beauchamp Chapel, 16 Shea Place, New Rochelle, Friday 4-8pm. Funeral Mass, Saturday 9:30am, at Holy Family Church, New Rochelle, NY.
