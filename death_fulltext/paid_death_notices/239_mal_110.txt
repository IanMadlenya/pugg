
        KRAUS--David E, 86, formerly of Rye, New York, passed away suddenly on December 6th, in Palm Desert, California. Dr. Kraus served in the Submarine Service during World War II, and practiced optometry in New Jersey and California for over 50 years. His son Edward predeceased him, as did his wife, Sylvia Stiefler. He leaves behind two stepchildren, Jeffrey Stiefler and Barbara Schlein, six grandchildren and three great-grandchildren, and a loving companion, Monica Friedman. Interment will be at Los Angeles National Cemetery on December 14th.
