
        STREETER--Frank S.The Board of Directors and staff of United Hospital Fund are deeply saddened by the loss of our friend, Frank Streeter, a wonderful and caring man who was deeply committed to community service and to better health care for all New Yorkers. He served as Vice Chairman of the Fund and was an active member of the Fund's Board since 1989. His dedication, enthusiasm, generosity, wise counsel, and gentlemanly ways will be greatly missed. Our deepest condolences are extended to his wife Nancy and his children, and to the Streeter family. Howard Smith, Chairman James R. Tallon Jr., President
        $(6$)STREETER--Frank S. The members of the Council of the Grolier Club are deeply saddened by the loss of Frank S. Streeter, a member since 1970, councilor from 1974 to 1997, and President of the Club from 1982 to 1986. We will sorely miss the friendship, guidance, and sage advice of this extraordinary bookman, and our condolences go to his wife Nancy, his family, and his many friends in the world of books.
