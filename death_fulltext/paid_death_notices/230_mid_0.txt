
        TILT--Jean P., 95, of Bedford Hills, NY. Died on March 2nd at her home. Mother of Rodman K. Tilt, Jr. and his wife, Marilen, of Bedford Hills, NY, Jean Tilt Sammis and her husband, Jesse F. Sammis, III, of Greenwich, CT and Priscilla T. Pochna of NYC. Also survived by eight grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. A memorial service at St. Mark's Episcopal Church, Mt. Kisco, NY will be held at a later date.
