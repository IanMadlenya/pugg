
        EULER-Henry, 91. On September 17, 2001. Resident of Bayside, NY. Widower of Alice (Revaz). Survived by his children, Aline and Henry. Father of the late Joseph. Brother of Elsie Ross Matzdorf. Brother-in-law of Mary Euler and Marguerite Muffatto. Uncle to several nieces and nephews. Many cousins in Germany. Predeceased by his sisters, Clara Peter, Margaret Mehlmann, Christine Poleiner and his brother, Andrew Euler. Son of the late Christina (Paul) and August Euler. Funeral Mass held at St. Kevin's RC Church and interment at Mt. Saint Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, NY. Ruhe in Frieden.
