
        FERTIG--Florence R. nee Reiff, 68, of East Hampton and NYC, on May 27, 2004. She won the admiration and respect of all her colleagues in the advertising world. Beloved wife of Samuel. Loving mother of Beth Fertig of NYC. Dear sister of Eleanor Chandler of Great Barrington, MA. Also survived by nephews and nieces, Douglas, Larry, Barbara and Leigh Chandler. Memorial service Sunday, May 30, 12 noon, at the East Hampton Jewish Center.
