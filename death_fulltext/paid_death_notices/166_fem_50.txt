
        SOMKIN-Ida Marder. On November 5, 2000, nine days shy of her 94th birthday. Resident of East Hampton and formerly of New York City. An artist, wife and mother with the rare gift of causing people to fall in love with her on first meeting. She is survived by her husband, Eugene, her twin sons, Anthony and Steven, and by hundreds of paintings, some of them considered masterpieces. A private memorial will be held.
