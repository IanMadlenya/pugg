
        SCHAPPES--Morris U. June 3, aged 97, in New York. Scholar, historian, activist, writer and teacher. Editor of ''Jewish Currents'' 1958-2001. Predeceased by his wife of 62 years, Sonya Laffer Schappes, in 1992. Interment June 6, 1PM at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions in his memory may be sent to Jewish Currents, 22 E. 17 St, Suite 601, NY, NY 10003
