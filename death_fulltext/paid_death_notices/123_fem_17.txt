
        LAVINE-Abigail. With profound sorrow we annouce that Abigail Lavine passed away comfortably at her home in NYC on April 1, at the age of 33, following a brief but intense battle with cancer. Adored companion and wife of Scott Konzelmann. Daughter of Frank and Katharine of Medford, MA. She will be deeply missed by all who were lucky enough to know her. Memorial to be announced at a later date. We would appreciate contributions to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation.
