
        TOLBERT-John P., died on June 5, 1997. Loving husband of Elinor, devoted father of Jennifer and Page, beloved brother of Arch and Stokes, adoring and adored grandfather of Christopher. The memory of his intelligence, charm, and wit shall sustain us. Our lives are greater for his, and he will be known to all who know us through our words and deeds. A memorial service will be held Thursday, June 12, at 5 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Southern Poverty Law Center, 400 Washington Avenue, P.O. Box 548, Mongomery, AL. 36101-0548.
