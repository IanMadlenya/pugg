
        MEYRICH-Marion S. 92, of Sarasota, FL, died March 11, 1999. Born on June 20, 1906 in NYC where she taught for 30 years. Survivors include son, Victor, grandson, Hart, niece Mona. No services planned. Donations may be made to American Civil Liberties Union. Remains will be placed next to her husband, Herman at Ferncliff Cemetery in Westchester.
