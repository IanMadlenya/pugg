
        MASIA-Samuel. Age 92, passed away in Nashville, Tn, June 23, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Lillian Steinberg Masia. He was formerly of Mount Vernon, N.Y. Retired to Lake Worth, Fla. in 1979. A doting and devoted father, he is survived by his daughters, Carol (Benjamin) Walter and Ara (Art) Storer, five grandchildren Roberta Walter Goodman, Martin Robinson, Felise Robinson, Matthew Walter and Rebecca Kushner and four great grandhcildren. He is also survived by his sister Rose Gitlin and brother Abe Masia. An attorney who loved his profession, he practiced law for close to 50 years in New York City. The funeral took place in Nashville on June 25th.
