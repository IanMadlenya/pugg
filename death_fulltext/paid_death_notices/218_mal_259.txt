
        LUTHY--William J. on March 13, 2005 of Kinnelon, NJ. Beloved husband of Margo, dear father of Madeleine Pukinskis, Peter Luthy and Susan Luthy. Loving grandfather of eight. Funeral services will be held Saturday March 26th at 2PM at the Smoke Rise Community Church, Kinnelon, NJ. Age 86, Mr. Luthy received his Sc.D from the Federal Institute of Technology, Zurich, Switzerland. He was an Emeritus member of the American Chemical Society and the Swiss Society. He was the retired President and CEO and member of the Board of Color and Chemical Division of Sandoz Inc. of East Hanover, NJ. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to a charity of the donor's choice.
