
        POWERS--Helen Whitecotton. Formerly of Cold Spring Harbor and Oyster Bay, NY died unexpectedly at her home in Chester, CT on May 17. She was 80 years old. She was the loving mother of 6 children: Diana, Flip, Arthur, Brian, Elizabeth and Christopher and grandmother of eight. Helen will be missed by her former husband Francis C. Powers of Manhattan. Daughter of Helen and Jay Arthur Whitecotton, of Southport and Paris, France, Helen was educated at the Masters School in Dobbs Ferry, NY and Barnard College. A memorial service is scheduled for July 30, 10:30 AM, St. Johns Church, Cold Spring Harbor, L.I., NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. Johns Church.
