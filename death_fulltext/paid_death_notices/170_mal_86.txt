
        DOBROF-Alfred. The Board of Directors, staff and volunteers of The Burden Center for the Aging are deeply saddened by the passing of Alfred Dobrof, beloved husband of our esteemed colleague and board member Rose. Alfred was a generous and enthusiastic supporter of our work. We will miss his gracious spirit. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to Rose, their four daughters and his entire family. Our thoughts and prayers are with you. Ellsworth G. Stanton, III Chairman, Board of Directors William J. Dionne Executive Director
        DOBROF-Alfred. I extend my deepest condolences to beloved Rose and the entire Dobrof family on the passing of Alfred. Alfred Dobrof was a good and decent man who lived a great life. He was an inspiration and friend to so many. He made friends with such ease. He made a difference in the lives of his family and friends. We have all lost a special man. Bob Blancato
        DOBROF-Alfred. The Board of Directors, Trustees and staff of the Brookdale Foundation Group are deeply saddened by the death of this compassionate man. To his wife, our friend and advisor, Rose, his children and family, we offer our deepest sympathy. Stephen L. Schwartz, President.
