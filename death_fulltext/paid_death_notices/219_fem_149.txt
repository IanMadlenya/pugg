
        CAPPELLA--Patricia. 92, passed away on April 16, 2005, at Bronx Lebanon Hospital, NYC. She was born FlorencePatricia Reynolds on February 7, 1913 to Herbert and Amelia Reynolds of Detroit, Mi. She married Jacques Cappella in 1942 and they formed the internationally known dance team of Cappella and Patricia. She later authored a book, ''OK Everyone up the Social Ladder'', and covered society events in Manhattan and Palm Beach. Her husband Jacques, and only sibling, Sydney Reynolds preceded her in death. She is survived by a sister-in-law, Mary Jane Reynolds of Palm Desert, Ca., five nieces and nephews and four grand nieces and grand nephews. She will be interred with her husband Jacques, in San Rafael, Ca.
