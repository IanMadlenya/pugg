
        HOGAN-Mary Jane (nee McGrath) on August 20, 2000. Long-time resident of Hicksville, NY at the age of 75. Beloved wife of the late Thomas L. with whom she helped launch the luxury liner ''S.S. Frances.'' Organizer for the reunions of the WWII 42nd Naval Construction Battalion (Seebees). Life long community and church volunteer, including ''Literacy Volunteers of Nassau County''. Loving mother of Kenneth, Thomas and Matthew. Proud grandmother of Kellen and Tyler. Friends may call Wednesday 2-5 & 7-9 PM at the Vernon C. Wagner Funeral Homes, Inc., 125 Old Country Rd., Hicksville, NY. Mass Thursday 10:30 am Our Lady of Mercy RC Church, Hicksville. Interment Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton, L.I. ''If there is any excellence and anything worthy of praise think about these things''. Phillipians 4:8
