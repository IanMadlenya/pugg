
        POPPER-Walter L. The City College of New York mourns the passing of Walter L. Popper, a graduate of the Class of 1940. He served CCNY with unwavering dedication and passion, inspiring many with his boundless energy, vision and generosity. On behalf of the City College community, I wish to extend my condolences to his family. Yolanda T. Moses, President The City College of New York/ CUNY
