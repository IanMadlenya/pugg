
        GRANAT-Ruth. The Special Olympics Palm Beach County ''Family'' mourns the loss of our dear friend and special hero to our athletes, Ruth Granat on January 18, 2003. Ruth's tremendous love for our athletes and generosity enhanced the lives of our many Special Olympians. Our thoughts and prayers go out to Ruth's daughters, Leslie Granat and Jill Handelsman, son-in-law, Martin Handelsman, family and dear friends. Ruth's spirit will live on in the hearts of the many Special Olympians she helped through her philanthropy. Ruth enlightened the lives of many in the community and throughout the world while helping them reach their goals and dreams. She will be greatly missed. The Family & Friends of Special Olympics Palm Beach County, FL Management Team Members: Rich Fleming, Coordinator; Jack Donsky, Fundraising Chairman
