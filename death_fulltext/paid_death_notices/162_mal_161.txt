
        WALLACE-John A., 84, formerly of Staten Island, N.Y. on July 4, 2000 at his home in Baltimore, Md. Preceded in death by his wife of 57 years, Janet, and an infant daughter. Survived by two daughters Barbara Wallace of Chevy Chase, Md., and Kathleen W. Brown of Arden Hills, Mn, a grandson and one brother. A Memorial Service will be held Sunday, Aug 13th at 1:30PM at Our Lady of the Angels Chapel, Charlestown Retirement Community, Baltimore, Md. Memorial contributions may be made to an endowed scholarship in his memory: John Wallace Fund, University of Minnesota Foundation, 200 Oak Street SE, Suite 500, Minneapolis, Mn 55455.
