
        MARCH-David. Current and past employees, suppliers and clients of Thomson-Leeds Company mourn the passing of our very loved teacher and friend, David March, who for 25 years guided our firm's production department to become the preeminent point of sale advertising agency in the United States. Often referred to as the ''Father of POP,'' we will forever be grateful for what he gave us. Our love and sympahty to his wonderful wife, Belle, his children and grandchildren.
