
        DRITZ-Stanley L. Age 88. Beloved husband of 63 years of Dorothy, devoted father of James, loving grandfather of three and greatgrandfather of three. He was the former President of John Dritz and Sons, Inc, as well as President of The National Notion Association and captain of the Syracuse University tennis team. He was an inspiration to his loving family and was respected by his entire business community. Memorial contributions to Syracuse University, 820 Comstock Ave, Suite 100, Syracuse, NY 13244-5040, or The American Parkinson Disease Association, Inc, 1250 Hylan Blvd, Suite 4B, Staten Island, NY 10305-1946, would be appreciated. Funeral service, Monday 1 PM, at The Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY.
