
        LUDLAM-George P Beloved husband of Kathleen Kieffer Ludlam. Died on August 1, 1997 at his home in East Dorset, Vermont. Survivors include his wife, his dearly loved son and daughter, Stephen Ludlam & Anne Ludlam Wichelns, four grandchildren and two step grandsons. He was predeceased by his brother, Kennedy Roberts Ludlam. A graduate of Phillips Academy, Andover and Harvard, class of 1925. Mr. Ludlam was a co-founder, retired senior vice president and member of the Board of Directors of The Advertising Council. He was a member of the Sons of the Revolution in the State of New York, and of the Harvard Club of New York City. Services in Manchester, VT., at Zion Episcopal Church, August 23.
