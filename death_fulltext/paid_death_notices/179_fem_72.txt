
        BLASIUS-Gertrude J., 83. Of Fort Pierce, FL, died Dec. 8, 2001. Mrs. Blasius was born in the Bronx, NY and was a 20 year resident of Fort Pierce, FL and formerly of New Hyde Park and Baiting Hollow, NY. She was an administrative assistant for 23 years with the NYC Public School system. Survivors include husband Conrad Blasius of Fort Pierce and sons Mark and Charles Blasius of New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: The Gilda's Club of NYC, 195 Houston St., NY, NY 10014. Reposing at Haisley-Hobbs Funeral Home 561-461-5211 on Tuesday 5-7 PM and Wed. 1-3 PM.
