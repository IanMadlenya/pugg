
        CROLL-May. On January 25, 2003 at 95 years of age. Beloved wife of Irving Croll, cherished mother of Burney and Don, grandmother of Wendy and Andrew Halpern and Andrea and Jonathan Freeman, great-grandmother of Benjamin Isaac Halpern, mother-in-law of Harriet Croll, friend of Jan Gartenberg, companion of Kerita Moulton. She endows all who loved her with a legacy of love, compassion, wisdom and spirituality. She will be missed dearly.
