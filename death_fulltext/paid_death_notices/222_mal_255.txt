
        HARVEY -- Bruce. 96, of Greenwich, CT, died July 20, 2005. He was active in many organizations in NYC. Predeceased by his wife, Amy Aspegren Harvey, survived by three daughters, Lucille Taff, Marianne Olsen and Joan Wilson, five grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Burial is private. Memorial service July 30, at Christ Church, Greenwich, at 11 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. George's Society of New York, 216 East 45 Street, Suite 901, New York, NY 10017; or to Friendly Connections, c/o Family Centers, 40 Arch Street, Greenwich, CT 06830.
