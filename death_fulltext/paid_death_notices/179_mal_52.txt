
        COUGHLIN-Robert Lawrence, Jr. The Honorable R. Lawrence Coughlin, Jr., 72, died Friday, November 30, 2001 at his Tidewater home in Mathews, Virginia. He is survived by his wife, Susan MacGregor Coughlin, four children, and five grandchildren. Congressman Coughlin was elected in 1968 to represent the 13th Congressional District of Pennsylvania in the United States Congress. He served as a member of Congress for 12 consecutive terms spanning 24 years. A funeral service will be held at the Old Chapel, Ft. Myers, Virginia at 9:00 AM on December 20, 2001, followed by interment at Arlington National Cemetery.
