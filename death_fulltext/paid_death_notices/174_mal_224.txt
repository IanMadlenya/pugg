
        DESCH-Carl W. The Directors of the Columbia University Club Foundation note with sadness the loss of its former President, Carl W. Desch. A member of the Columbia College Class of 1937, he was a great supporter of his Alma Mater. We treasured his leadership, financial acumen and humor. We extend our sincere sympathy to his daughters, Carol & Barbara, son, Carl, Jr., and sister, Margaret Hanft. The Directors of the Columbia University Club Foundation, Inc.
