
        STIGER--William M. At the age of 92. Of Oyster Bay, NY. On March 23, 2006. Father of Anthony D. of Manhasset, N.Y.. Sarah Stiger Ewing of Baltimore, MD, and Christian H. of Coral Gables, FL. Grandfather of five and great-grandfather of two. He was a lover of the sea, an avid sailor, and longtime member of Seawanhaka Corinthian Yacht Club. A memorial Service will be held at the Christ Church, 61 E. Main Street, Oyster Bay, NY, on Wednesday March 29, 2006 at 11:00 a.m. In lieu of flowers family requests donations to the Nature Conservancy, 250 Lawrence Hill Road, Cold Spring Harbor, N.Y. 11724.
