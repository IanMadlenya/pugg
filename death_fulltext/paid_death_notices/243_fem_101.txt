
        DREXLER--Beatrice C., who ran Candy Mountain Day Camp in New City, Rockland County, New York for more than 30 years before her retirement, died April 16, at the age of 92, Her husband, the late Elias J. Drexler, was the senior vice president in the U.S. of FujFilm. She was a loving mother to Joanne Cohen, President of Esquire Appraisals, Inc., and to the late Linda Fogel. She is also survived by her brother, Arthur Gould, her grandchildren, Terri Cohen Tulin, Brett Cohen, Michelle Berry and William Fogel and her greatgrandchildren, Lea and David Berry, and Jacob, Gabriel and Benjamin Cohen, and Sonia Tulin. Funeral services will be held tomorrow, April 17, at 12:30 pm. at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street in Manhattan.
