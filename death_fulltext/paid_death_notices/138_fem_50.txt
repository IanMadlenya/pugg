
        TALLY-Bernice G. The Board and Staff of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged note with sorrow the passing of the wife of the late Charles H. Tally, former President of our Board. We extend our deepest sympathy to her daughter, Nancy Polevoy, who continues the family tradition of service on the JASA Board and to the Polevoy and Tally families. Marilyn Friedman, President Steven M. Jacobson, Chairman David J. Stern, Exec V. P.
