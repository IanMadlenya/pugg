
        D'URSO-David Francis, March 3, 1998. Beloved husband of Michelle. Loving father of Michaela. Loving son of Florence and the late Camillo D'Urso. Devoted brother of Mark, Donna and Lisa. He was a graduate of Iona Grammar, Prep and College, London School of Economics and Carnegie Institute. He currently was the President of D'Urso Enterprises, Inc., and was active in Pelham Civics, and Board Member of the Iona Grammar School. Benefactor of Boys Town of Italy. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Thursday and Friday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday 10AM, St. Patrick's Cathedral, Principal Celebrant, His Eminence John Cardinal O'Connor. Interment, Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Westchester. In lieu of flowers, a scholarship fund will be established in his name at Iona Prep.
