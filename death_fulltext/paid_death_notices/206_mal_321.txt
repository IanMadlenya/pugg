
        SCHLOSS--Emanuel M. (Amby). 91. Passed away on March 21 at his home in Palm Beach FL after a lengthy illness. He was born on November 22, 1911 in Washington DC to Gertrude and Leonard Schloss. He graduated from Syracuse University where he was an All American in baseball and soccer. He enjoyed a successful career in the jewelry and men's apparel industries for many years. He has lived in New York City and Palm Beach since retiring in 1990. He is survived by his wife of 29 years Suzanne, daughter and son-in-law Susan and Don Meredith, son and daughter-in-law Leonard and Jane Schloss, daughter Carole Kiernan and son James Lifton, granddaughter and her husband Kimberly and Todd Markin, grandsons Scott and Christopher Kiernan and granddaughter Isabelle Lifton. He is also survived by his sister Evelyn Eichberg. A private service is being held in New York.
        SCHLOSS--Emanuel M. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of NY records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Emanuel M. Schloss. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
