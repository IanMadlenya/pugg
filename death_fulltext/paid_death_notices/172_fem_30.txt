
        ARONSON-Ruth Flax. Beloved wife and best friend of Richard Aronson. Passed away May 2, 2001 in New York City after a long and valiant battle with cancer. She will be sorely missed by all her many friends and by her colleagues at the Rogosin Institute where she was employed for her entire professional career. Ruth will be in our hearts and minds forever. A memorial service will be held at Hevreh of Southern Berkshires, 270 State Rd, Great Barrington, MA. on May 20, 2001 at 2:30PM. 413-528-6378 for directions.
