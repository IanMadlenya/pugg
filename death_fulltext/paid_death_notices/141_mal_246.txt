
        AUERBACH-Arnold M. On October 19, 1998 at New York Hospital of leukemia. He is survived by his wife Justine, daughter Nina, and son Andrew. He was a prolific satirist, wrote for Broadway theater, several novels, many satiric articles for newspapers, including The New York Times, magazines and radio shows including Fred Allen's. Funeral service on Friday at 12:15PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Friends may visit the residence from 1:30 on.
