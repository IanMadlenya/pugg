
        MANTEL-Henry, Zvi Hirsch Ben Menachem Mendel, 84. Del Ray Beach, FL. Devoted husband of Ruth Mantel, loving father of Michael Mantel, Marsha Wagner and their spouses Anne Mantel, Leon Wagner, and Andrea Harmon. Grandfather of Gabriel, Daniel and Samuel. Henry was born and raised in Vienna, immigrated to the United States in 1939 and earned a Purple Heart during his meritorious service in World War II. Henry was a wonderful, talented and active man who will be missed by his family and many friends. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Congregation Ansche Chesed in Manhattan or the Rabbi Discretionary Fund of the Jewish Community Center of Harrison, New York.
