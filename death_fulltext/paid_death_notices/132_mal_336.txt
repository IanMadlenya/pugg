
        SIMON - Sidney. Of Shaker Heights, OH. Died Saturday, January 17, 1998, at the age of 85. Former President of the M & D Simon Co., makers of Donegal Sportswear. Longtime leader in the Cleveland Jewish community. In his last years, led fundraising efforts for the HELP Foundation, a Cleveland charity and group home for the mentally disabled. Beloved husband of Nancy. Loving father of Wendy Zinn, Jonathan Simon, Barbara Simon. Dear brother of Edward. Adoring grandfather of five.
