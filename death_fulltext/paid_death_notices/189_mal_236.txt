
        HOLLAND-Terry. Died on October 12, 2002 at age 36, after a brief illness. A partner with Financial Investment Partners LLC, Terry sat on the Board of Stonewall Community Foundation and was active in Empire State Pride Agenda. Devoted partner to Michael Dunn and to Brian Thompson, loving friend to many. Terry will be remembered by all who knew him, both personally and professionally, for the gift he had for bringing people together and the joy he found in sharing that gift. Larger than life in our memories, he will live on forever in our hearts. Contributions in his memory may be made to Stonewall Community Foundation, 119 West 24 Street, NY, NY 10011.
