
        SIMON--Belle, 97, passed away June 16, 2007. Loving and beloved wife of the late Louis Simon. Adored mother of two sons, Paul and Edward and five grandchildren, Harper, Adrian, Emma, Lulu, and Gabriel. She will be greatly missed by her daughters-inlaw Edie and Leslie. She was a first grade teacher at Public School 164 in Queens and retired in 1975. Donations may be made in her name to the Children's Health Fund, 215 West 125th Street, Suite 301, New York, NY 10027.
