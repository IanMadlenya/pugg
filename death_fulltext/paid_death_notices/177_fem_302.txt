
        DOWLING-Mary Yolanda, 46. Of Rosedale, NY, heroically and tragically on September 11, 2001, working for AON at the World Trade Center. Beloved daughter of Christy Dowling and the late Mary C. Dowling (nee Dolan). Cherished sister of Christopher, Andrew & Rosaleen Shea. Sisterin-law of Judy Dowling and Bob Shea. Adored aunt of Kristina, Devin, Christopher, Robert and Mary Kate. She will be missed by many relatives and friends in the United States and Tullamore, Ireland. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Yolanda's memory to St. Clare's RC Church, 137-35 Brookville Blvd, Rosedale, New York 11422.
