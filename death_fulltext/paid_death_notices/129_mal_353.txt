
        ENGLISH - Charles. UNITE mourns the passing of a former leader of one of our predecessor unions ACTWU, Charles English, who believed deeply in organizing Southern apparel workers. A memorial service will be held at The Old Dutch Church in Sleepy Hollow, NY on Saturday, November 1 at 5:00 PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, New York 10461.
