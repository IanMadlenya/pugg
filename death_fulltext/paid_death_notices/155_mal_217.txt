
        BERSON-Harold E., M.D. Of Stuart, FL. Beloved husband of Florence (Selikowitz), devoted father of Drs. Diane and Mark Lebowitz, and Denise and Mitchell Tanzman, loving grandfather of Alex, Emily, Danielle and Olivia. Past President of the Brooklyn Psychiatric and Neurology Societies. Life fellow of the American Psychiatric Association and delegate to its assembly.
        BERSON-Harold, M.D. The Department of Psychiatry of Maimonides Medical Center deeply mourns the death of our esteemed colleague Dr. Harold Berson. His devotion to physchiatry, his enthusiasm and his dedication to his patients served as a model for those of us who knew him. Our deepest condolences to his devoted wife Florence and to his children Diane and Denise. Marvin H. Lipkowitz, M.D. Chairman Department of Psychiatry Maimonides Medical Center
        BERSON-Harold E., M.D. Beloved brother of Dr. Burton L. Berson. Loving uncle of Debbie and Brad. Funeral services were held Thursday, December 16.
