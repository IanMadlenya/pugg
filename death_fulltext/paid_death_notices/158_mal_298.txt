
        DORF-Martin E., 54, died Stamford, CT. A leading architect and designer, he specialized in restaurant, retail and entertainment projects. He completed the awardwinning design for the California Grill restaurant at the Contemporary Hotel in Disney World. He was inducted into Hospitality Design Magazine's Platinum Circle for lifetime achievement. Mr. Dorf was born in Brooklyn and graduated from The City College School of Architecture in New York. He formed his own firm, Dorf Associates in 1979. He wrote ''Restaurants That Work''. For 10 years he was an instructor at Parsons School of Design in New York and he lectured nationwide. Mr. Dorf is survived by his loving wife of 20 years, Ivonne, his daughter, Jodi Warum of Flushing, NY, sister, Rebecca Schaffer of Manalapan, NJ, mother, Ruth of Tamarac, FL and two grandchildren. His adventurous spirit will be remembered and cherished by his loving family and loyal friends. Donations may be made in his memory to Memorial Sloan - Kettering, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
