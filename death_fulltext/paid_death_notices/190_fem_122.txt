
        JANSSEN-Linda. Architect and an authority on the NYC zoning laws. Died on November 13, 2002. Linda had worked for the NYC Department of City Planning for almost 30 years prior to her retirement in July of this year. A wake will be held at the Gannon Funeral Home, 152 E. 28th St, NYC, Sunday night, from 7 PM to 9 PM. A Requiem Mass will be celebrated at St. Agnes Church, 141 E. 43rd St, NYC, Monday, November 18, 2002, at 10:15 AM.
