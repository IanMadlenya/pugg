
        TRAU-Charles. Yeshiva University and its affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary are saddened by the passing of a respected member of the Antwerp, Belgium, Jewish community, beloved husband of Toni, and father of Dr. Rene Trau. He is the brother of Rabbi Solomon Trau, Board treasurer of RIETS and, with his wife, Beryl, a RIETS Fellow. We extend our heartfelt condolences as well to his grandson and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman Board of Trustees
