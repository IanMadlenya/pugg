
        LEFF--Naomi. Pratt Institute mourns the death of renowned interior designer, Naomi Leff. She was an esteemed alumna of interior design and architecture, a Pratt trustee, an Alumni Achievement Award recipient, and a Pratt Legends honoree. Her professional achievements were legion and her stature was extraordinary. Pratt is proud of Naomi Leff's life and honored to have been an important part of it. She will be sadly missed. Robert H. Siegel, Chairman Thomas F. Schutte, President
