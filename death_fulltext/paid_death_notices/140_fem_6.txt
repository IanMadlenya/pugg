
        JARNOW-Jeannette Abelow. August 31, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Alfred Jarnow, loving mother of Alfred Jarnow Jr, and Betsy Potter, cherished grandmother of Jesse, Daniel and Liza. She will be deeply missed. She was an accredited author as well as Professor Emeritus & Chair of The Business Merchandising Department at The Fashion Institute of Technology. Services Thursday, Sept. 3, 2 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Long Island Childrens Museum, Garden City, L.I., The FIT Scholarship Fund, or a charity of your choice.
