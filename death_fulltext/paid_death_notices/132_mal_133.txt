
        WARNER-William B. Died on January 5, 1998 at home after a short illness. He was 81 years old. Born in Bad Axe, MI. Mr. Warner lived for many years in Brooklyn and Garden City, NY, moving to Mattituck, NY in 1986 and to Lake Wales, FL in 1995. He was a graduate of Brooklyn Polytech and of Stevens Institute in 1940. Mr. Warner was employed at Consolidated Edison, NY from 1946 retiring in 1983. Prior to that he was employed at Westinghouse Electric. Surviving are his loving extended family; his wife Margaret Vanston Warner, six daughters, two sons, 8 grandchildren, 2 great grandchildren and many friends. Mr. Warner was predeceased in 1995 by his wife, Marian Carleton Warner. Funeral services are Saturday, January 10, at 10 A.M. at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Lake Wales, FL, and interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be sent to the American Theatre Organ Society Scholarship Fund, C/O Dorothy Van Steenkiste, 9270 Reeck Road, Allen Park, Michigan 48101-1461.
