
        GREEN--Morton. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date for Dr. Morton Green, 85, who died at his home on September 23. Dr. Green was born in Brooklyn, NY on October 25, 1917 to Leon and Nessa (Lackow) Green. He graduated from PS 153 and Abraham Lincoln High School in Brooklyn. He was a professor of vertebrate paleontology for over 45 years. Dr. Green married Elizabeth A. Griffith on June 6, 1946. She survives. Other survivors: Leon M. Green of Wilmington, DE, Joel G. Green, Kirkland, WA, Julie Green Rombough, Lawrence, KS, and five grandchildren.
