
        SMOLEN--Seymour Sheldon. Died New Year's Day 2005 at age 93 in Pompano Beach FL, his home since 1972. As a commander of a tank platoon, 70th Tank Battalion, he received serious injuries following the Normandy invasion, yet survived to live a fruitful life with his beloved wife Dorothy who predeceased him in 1992. Sy was loved and respected by many both within and outside the famliy circle. He is survived by his faithful and devoted brother George, daughters Barbara and Jane, sons Peter and James, and many grandchildren and great grandchildren. To those who knew him well, scotch on the rocks will never taste the same.
