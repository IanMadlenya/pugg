
        RATNOFSKY--Barbara. Wife of the late Matthew Ratnofsky passed away on October 31, 2006 in Bethesda, MD. She was born in Brooklyn, NY in 1913. She was the loving mother of Amy (deceased) and Alexander Ratnofsky of Maryland. She is survived by her son and his wife Suzanne, three grandchildren: Nathan, Michael and his wife Kristin and Jeffrey, and her brother Samuel Levine and his wife Bessie of Virginia Beach, VA. Contributions in her name can be made to the Kennedy Krieger Institute, Neurobehavioral Unit, 707 North Broadway, Baltimore, MD.
