
        ROSENTHAL--Milton F., age 91, died May 2, 2005. Beloved husband of 62 years of Frieda (nee Bojar). Cherished son of the late Jacob and Louise Rosenthal. Devoted father of Anne (Peter) Mitro of Moreland Hills, Ohio; grandfather to Daniel and Roger. Loving brother of the late Alexander Rosenthal. Graveside service to be held at Westchester Hills Cemetery, Hastings-onHudson, on Friday, May 6, at 2PM. No home visitation.
        ROSENTHAL--Milton F. The Faculty and Staff of the Zena and Michael A. Wiener Cardiovascular Institute and the Marie-Josee and Henry R. Kravis Center for Cardiovascular Health at the Mount Sinai Medical Center are deeply saddened by the passing of Milton F. Rosenthal, a devoted and beloved benefactor for whom the Frieda and Milton F. Rosenthal Coronary Care Unit at Mount Sinai is named. Mr. Rosenthal's contributions to humanity extend far beyond the walls of Mount Sinai. We have lost a compassionate and trusted friend. His guidance and wisdom, his dedication to improving the lives of those suffering from cardiovascular disease, his unfailing loyalty, and the depth of his commitment to the medical center will be remembered forever. We will miss his humanity and generosity of spirit. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Frieda Bojar, his daughter, Anne, and to the entire family. Valentin Fuster, MD, Ph.D Director
