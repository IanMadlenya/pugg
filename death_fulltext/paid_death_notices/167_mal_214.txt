
        NASH-N. Richard. On December 11. Distinguished playwright, novelist, screenwriter, beloved teacher, and one of the pioneers of the renowned Golden Age of television drama. His works have received numerous international awards and been translated in 40 languages, including an African tongue which has no written form. He recently completed a new novel, ''The Wildwood'', and new dramas appear in his ''Selected Plays''. He leaves Christopher, his son by Helena Taylor Nash, and Jennifer and Amanda, his daughters by Katherine Copeland Nash. A memorial service is planned for early Spring.
