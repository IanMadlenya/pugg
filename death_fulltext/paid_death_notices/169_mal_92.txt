
        LESTER-Abraham J. Died in New York City on February 5, 2001 in his 95th year. Loving husband to Yetta, beloved father of Ann Stone and the late Elaine Isenberg, father-in-law of Leon Stone, grandfather to Julie, Hal, Susan, Tom, Stephanie, Marc and Paul. Great grandfather to Chelsea and Samantha. Abe was loved and revered by friends and family. His charity and good works will be remembered by all. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Riverdale Temple at 4545 Independence Ave, Riverdale, New York 10471 or a charity of your choice. Services are to be held at the Riverdale Temple Wednesday, February 7 at 11:30 A.M.
        LESTER-Abraham. A man of valor, compassion, wisdom, humor and integrity who won the hearts and admiration of all who knew him. To dear Yetta, Ann, Leon and the Stone and Lester families, our deepest sympathy. Joyce Liskin and family
