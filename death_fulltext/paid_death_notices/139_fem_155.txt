
        BEERS-Jane Gilbert. On August 21 at age 74, Jane Beers died of heart failure. In the mid-1940's Jane came to N.Y. and became a fashion model, appearing in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar and other magazines. In 1965, Jane founded & became president of Red Carpet Associates, the first and most prestigious destination management company in Manhattan. For many years Jane served on the board of the New York Convention and Visitors Bureau, Meeting Planners International and other New York associations. Survivors include her son Jeffrey, brother Dr. John Gilbert, sister Nancy Lindburg and two grandchildren. A Memorial service will be held on Thursday, August 27, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 10 A.M.
