
        CHERNICK--Arthur. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Arthur Chernick, a cherished friend and longtime leader at UJA-Federation and our beneficiary agencies. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
