
        DICKINSON-Corinne (Corky) E. Goldsmith. Died on September 5, in New York City after a long battle with multiple sclerosis. She was 51 years old. Mother of Philip Samuel Dickinson, daughter of Hildi Hill of Charlottesville, VA, and Clifford H. Goldsmith of New York City. Sister of Audrey J. Goldsmith Kubie and Alexandra E. Goldsmith Fallon. She had been a nurse and struggled for over 25 years with this cruel illness. Her family is dedicated to the hope that soon others will not have to suffer as she did. The funeral will be private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Chairman's Fund of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, 733 Third Avenue, New York, NY 10017.
