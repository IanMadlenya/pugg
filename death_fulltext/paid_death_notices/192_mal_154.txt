
        ZOILO-A. Marc. Of Weston, CT, passed on January 9, 2003. Retired Brooks Brothers executive, he made many outstanding contributions to the development and growth of Brooks Brothers during his 31 years with the company. Since retirement Marc was active with many civic and volunteer organizations, among them 15 years with SCORE-Service Corps of Retired Executives. A 1944 alumnus of the University of Notre Dame, he loved his Norte Dame football. Survivors include daughter Daphne Rich, sons Marc Jr. and John, and granddaughter Stephanie. Calling hours are from 3 to 5 and 7 to 9 PM, January 13, at Bouton Funeral Home, Georgetown, CT.
