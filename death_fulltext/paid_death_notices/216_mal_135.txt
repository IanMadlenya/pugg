
        ROY--Malcolm D. 90, of Liverpool, entered into rest Sunday. He was a former resident of Ossining, NY where he had lived for 60 years. Malcolm was an attorney and Partner of Roy & Roy Law Firm in Irvington, NY. Malcolm was predeceased by his wife of 60 years, Ellen Wood Roy, in 2001, and two brothers, Robert and Donald. He is survived by a daughter, RitaEllen DiRubbo of Liverpool, NY, two grandsons, Anthony M. and Patrick W. DiRubbo, and two great grandchildren. Burial will be in the Spring at Sleepy Hollow Cemetery, Sleepy Hollow, NY, following a service at Zion Episcopal Church in Dobbs Ferry, NY.
