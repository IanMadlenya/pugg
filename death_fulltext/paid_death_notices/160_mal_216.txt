
        SHAFTEL-Oscar H. On May 10, 2000, at age 88, after a long illness. Father of Anthony, Ann of Halifax NS, & Eve Shaftel; grandfather of Tad, Holly, Kelly, Jay, & Claire; brother of Rosalind Lerman & the late Sidney Shaftel. Predeceased by his wife, Ruth Marra Shaftel. Brother-in-law of Jeanne Marra Shlasko of Woodstock, New York. He taught for many years at Queens College & was professor emeritus at Pratt Institute.
