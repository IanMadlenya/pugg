
        YARSHATER-Latifeh (nee Alvieh). 73. On May 17, 1999 after a courageous battle against cancer. Survived by her husband, Ehsan, and her sisters Vadieh Stanger, Monir Arjomand, Evelyn Alvieh, their families, and many friends. She served as cultural advisor to the USIS in Tehran (1951-61), was founding member of the National Council of Women of Iran, after settling in New York in 1961 joined the US National Council of Women. She authored articles in both English and Persian and served as the Outreach Coordinator of Columbia University's Middle East Institute (1986-90). Funeral services will be on Saturday, May 22, 1999 at 10:00 a.m. at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel (1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St.). We will miss her.
