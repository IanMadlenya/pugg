
        BODAY-Martha Wilbourn Boden. Widow of Walter A. Boden and George K. Boday, died in Southampton, L.I., on July 16, 1998, just three days short of her 91st birthday. A long-time resident of Garden City, she is survived by her son, Robert W. Boden, of Cincinnati, Ohio, her daughter and son-inlaw, Nancy and Otis Pearsall of Brooklyn and Bridgehampton, L.I., three stepchildren, four grandchildren, two stepgrandchildren and one great-grandchild. Memorial service in the fall.
