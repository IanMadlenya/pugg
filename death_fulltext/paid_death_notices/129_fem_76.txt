
        CARLIN-Sister Frances Maureen, OP, past Superior General of the Sisters of St. Dominic of Amityville, New York, died October 9 at Carlin Hall, an infirmary named in her honor. Sister was head of the congregation from 1967-73. She was also past Academic Dean of Molloy College in Rockville Centre, NY. Sister was a scholar and historian of the Dominican Order, frequently lecturing on St. Dominic. Sister Frances Maureen is survived by her nephews Charles Kee of Rockville Centre, NY, Christopher Carlin of Connecticut, Francis Carlin of California and niece Denise Kee La Flam of New Hampshire. The Mass of Resurrection will be celebrated by Reverend Joseph Payne, O.P., on Monday, October 13, 1997 in St. Albert's Chapel at 10:30 AM. Burial will follow in the Dominican Sister's Cemetery in Amityville.
