
        SIMON-William E., The Board of Trustees and the entire Manhattanville College community mourn the loss of the Honorable William E. Simon, son of alumna Eleanor Kearns Simon, Academy, and brother of alumna, Mary Ellen Simon White, class of 1947, 1997 Distinguished Alumna Award recipient. Mr. Simon was a dear friend of the College and the benefactor of the Eleanor Kearns Simon Chair that provides for a permanent Roman Catholic chaplain on the campus. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife Tonia, sister, Mary Ellen, his children and grandchildren.
