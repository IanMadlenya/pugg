
        TOLBERT--Elinor. On May 25, 2004. Adored wife of the late Jack, beloved mother of Jennifer and Page, cherished grandmother of Christopher, loving great-grandmother of Beatrix and Willa, admired aunt of many nieces and nephews. Courageous servant of her country in the Pacific during World War II. Author of light verse into her 90s. Devotee of Masterpiece Theater and The New York Times Crossword Puzzle, lover of all things French and British. Her wit and vigor leave a long legacy to family and friends. Service Thursday, June 3, 3 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Public Television at Thirteen, PO Box 1313, NY NY 10101-1313 or The Democratic National Committee, 430 South Capitol St SE, Washington, DC 20003.
