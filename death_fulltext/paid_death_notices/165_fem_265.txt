
        BRODSKY-Anne Peshkin. October 23, 2000. Cherished wife of the late Abraham Brodsky, beloved mother of Judith Schneider and Joan Aaronson, adored ''Nana'' to six grandchildren and nine great grandchildren. Co-founder of the Brooklyn Psychiatric Centers, active in Hadassah, UJA Speakers Bureau. Her love and devotion to her family and the joy and light she brought to all who knew her will be deeply missed and remembered always.
