
        ROSENBLUM-Richard. On October 15, 2002 of Brooklyn. Most loved husband, father, brother, grandpa and friend. Talented artist, children's book author and illustrator, professor, enthusiastic birder. Your humor, charm and many interests enriched the lives of all who knew you. Your loving nature, warmth and creative spirit will be so missed by those who love you and you will be forever in our hearts. He is survived by his wife Barbara, daughter Anne, sister Debby, son-inlaw John, and granddaughters Juliet and Skye. There will be a memorial Saturday November 23, 11 AM, Guild Hall, Grace Church, Brooklyn Heights.
