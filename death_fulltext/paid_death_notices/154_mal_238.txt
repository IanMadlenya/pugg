
        SCHAEFER-Charles V. Jr. age 85 of Saddle River died Thursday, November 18, 1999 in Boca Raton, Florida. Born in Brooklyn, New York, he was a resident of Saddle River and formerly of Ridgewood. A graduate mechanical engineer from Stevens Institute of Technology '36, Mr. Schaefer was later awarded an honorary doctorate and founded the Schaefer School of Engineering. As an entrepreneur he owned various NJ based manufacturing businesses. He served as Chairman of the NJ Manufacturers Ins. Co., Stevens Institute of Technology, the N.J. Business and Industry Association, and the Northern New Jersey Chamber of Commerce. He was a member of the Ridgewood, Boca West, and Arcola Country Clubs and the Upper Ridgewood Tennis Club. Surviving is his wife Stephanie (nee Tactikos) Schaefer of Saddle River, a son, Charles Schaefer III of Franklin Lakes, NJ, a daughter Lynn Schaefer Bovenizer of Pennington, NJ. Also surviving are six grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Mr. Schaefer was predeceased by his first wife Lillian Meyer Schaefer in 1984. Visiting Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 at the Vander Plaat Funeral Home 257 Godwin Ave., Wyckoff, NJ. A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, December 3, 1999 at 1 PM at the Wyckoff Reformed Church, 580 Wyckoff Ave., Wyckoff, New Jersey. Donations to Schaefer Fund of Excellence, Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, N.J. 07030, would be appreciated.
