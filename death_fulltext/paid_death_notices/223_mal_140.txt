
        MORILLAS--Raul ''Buck'', of NYC, died on July 13. Raul was born in Havana in 1956 and grew up in Maplewood, NJ. He graduated from Rhode Island School of Design with a BFA in Interior Design in '78 and a Bachelor's in Landscape Architecture in '79. Raul was head of the Interior Design Dept. at Robert A.M. Stern Architects. His artistic flair was duly acclaimed in the industry. He is survived by his parents, Onelia and Filomeno, and siblings, Marisel Estevez, Jorge and Alfredo Rodriguez, and many friends.
