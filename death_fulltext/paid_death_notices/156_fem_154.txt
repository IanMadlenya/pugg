
        SOZIO-Rosetta, age 95, of New York and Rome, died Jan. 14th, 2000 in New York. Beloved by her many friends here and in Italy. In her exemplary generosity, she donated her entire estate to The Servants of Charity (Opera Don Guanella), serving the poor, elderly, and the retarded and to The Catholic Foreign Mission Society of America (Maryknoll). Funeral Mass at Our Lady Chapel in St. Patrick's Cathedral Wednesday, Jan. 19th at 10:00 am.
