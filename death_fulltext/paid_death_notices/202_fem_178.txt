
        KALAIDJIAN--Elizabeth B., 81. Of Indian River Shores, Florida. Formerly of South Orange, New Jersey. A native of San Diego, CA. She worked during World War II at the U.S. Naval Hospital in that city. Beloved wife of 57 years of Edward C. Kalaidjian. Mother of Emma, Walter and Robert and proud grandmother of four. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to VNA & Hospice Foundation, 1111 36th Street, Vero Beach, FL. 32960.
