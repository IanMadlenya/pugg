
        LOPRETE-Connie. Passed away on January 6, 2000. She took her last breath with the strength and dignity that we all knew her for. She maintained her style and class through the end. She is survived by her son, Ralph, daughter-in-law, Naomi, and the newest apple of her eye, her granddaughter, Stephanie. Services will be held at Fred Herbst Funeral Home at 7501 5th Ave., Brooklyn, NY, Sunday January 9, 2000.
