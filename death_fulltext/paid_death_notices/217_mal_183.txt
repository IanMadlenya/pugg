
        ZAK--Frederick G., M.D. 89, on February 5, 2005. Loving, devoted husband of Sylvia Zak, cherished father of Vivien and Ronald, dear uncle of Anthony. Consummate physician, eloquent author and professor of pathology. Born and raised in Vienna, he endured the atrocities of the Holocaust to live his life with grace, tenderness, generosity, laughter and love. Remembered and adored for his gentle soul and joyous spirit, his humanity, dignity, and keen intelligence. His life has made this world a better place. Private services held.
