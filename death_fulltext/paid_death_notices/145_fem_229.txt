
        COLE-Miriam Gordon. Of Southport, CT., and Palm Beach, FL. Died peacefully on February 24th, in Palm Beach. Much loved wife of Martin C. Cole. She is also survived by her children, Richard S. Gordon, (Jacobi) of Maui, Jane Oristano (Victor) of Fairfield, CT., and Hobe Sound, FL., her cherished granddaughter Ellen Frances Tuchman (Mrs. Harry Orenstein), of Dallas. Proud ''Gammy'' to great-grandsons Nicholas and Spencer. Sister of Dorothy Samelson, of Boca Raton. Memorial donations may be made to the Miriam Cole Endowment Fund, St. Vincent's Hospital, 2800 Main Street, Bridgeport, CT. 06606.
