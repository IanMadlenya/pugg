
        BAILEY-Dolores (nee Mazzotti). Died at her home in Manhattan on November 23, 1998 at age 62. She is survived by Earl (Bill) Bailey, her husband of 32 years. Born in Syracuse, N.Y., she spent her childhood in Patchogue, N.Y. A graduate of City University of New York, she made a career in the computer systems field and retired as a Vice President of the Equitable Life Assurance Society in 1991. Service and interment private. Donations can be made in her memory to The Endowment for English Language Learning Programs, The New York Public Library, 5th Ave at 42nd Street, New York, New York 10018.
