
        JESSON-Chester S. of Rutherford, NJ, died Saturday October 30, 1999. Mr. Jesson was born in Bayonne, NJ. He was a retired linotype operator for the New York Times. Mr. Jesson was a Staff Sergeant in the Army Air Corps. Serving in Guam during WW II. He was a parishoner of St. Mary R.C. Church, Rutherford, NJ where he sang in the choir. Mr. Jesson was a member of the Old Guard of Rutherford. Mr. Jesson was predeceased by his wife Helen (nee Dembowski). He is survived by his son Paul, and daughter-in-law Eileen of Shelton, CT. his grandson Paul Charles of Baltimore, MD, and two sisters Florence Martinsen, of Red Bank, NJ, and Hattie Symanski of Bayonne, NJ. Funeral from the Collins-Calhoun Funeral Home, 19 Lincoln Ave., Rutherford, NJ. on Wednesday at 9 AM. Funeral mass at St. Mary's R.C. Church, Rutherford, NJ at 9:30 AM. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery, N. Arlington, NJ. Friends will be received Tuesday 2-4 & 7-9 PM.
