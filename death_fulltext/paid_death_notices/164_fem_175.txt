
        ALPERT-Caroline. On September 18, 2000, at the age of 74. Beloved wife of Dr. Meyer. Loving mother of Rabbi Robert, Linda, Mark, David, and Steven. Cherished grandmother of Matthew, Rebecca, Daniel, and Jeffrey. Adoring sister of Ethel, Eli, and the late Abe. She was a devoted nurse in the Yonkers Public school System for many years. Service on Tuesday, 12 PM, at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel'', 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, New York. Contributions in her memory may be made to the charity of one's choice.
