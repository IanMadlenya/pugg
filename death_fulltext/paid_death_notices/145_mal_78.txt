
        MAUSER-Francis T., 64, of New York City, passed away suddenly on January 19 in Vero Beach, FL., where he was a winter resident for the past 15 years. Born January 27, 1934, Mr. Mauser recently retired as Archivist of the General Services Office of Alcoholics Anonymous. Survivors include his brother and sister-in-law John George and Maureen Mauser of NY; one niece and her husband, Dawn and Anthony Celentano of Indiatlantic, FL.; one grand-nephew, Gregory Patrick Celentano; one nephew and his wife, John G. and Ellen Mauser of New York. A Memorial Mass will be held at 6PM on Tuesday, March 2nd, 1999, at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine in New York City.
