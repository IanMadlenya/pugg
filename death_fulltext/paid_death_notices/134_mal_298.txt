
        KEAVY-William, M.D. The Lenox Hill Hospital Alumni Association has lost a treasured colleague and friend. Dr. William Keavy, a member of the Section of Plastic Surgery for over 30 years, and an active member of the LHH Alumni Association, was of a spirit that knew only how to give to others. He will be deeply missed by his family, his friends and his colleagues. Peter Bruno, M.D., President LHH Alumni Association Peter Baiocco, M.D., Secretary LHH Alumni Association
