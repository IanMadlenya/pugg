
        SHAPIRO - Berniece (nee Rubenstein). Died April 12, 2003, at age 83 in the comfort of her daughter's home. She was the beloved wife of the late Murray Shapiro. Loving mother of Andrew Shapiro and Donna D'Andraia, mother-in-law to Anthony D'Andraia and Sylvia Shapiro, grandmother to Rocco, Anthony, Vincent and Michael D'Andraia. There will be a memorial service in her memory on Sunday, April 13, at 11 AM at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, NY, 914793-3800. Donations can be made in her memory to the Hudson River Museum or to any hospice facility.
