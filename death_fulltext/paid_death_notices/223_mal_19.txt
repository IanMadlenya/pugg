
        BROOKS--Donald M. 77. Esteemed fashion and theatrical costume designer, popular teacher. Passed away on August 1, 2005. Beloved brother of Kay Brooks Blick (Edwin), dear uncle of Annie L. Richardson and great uncle of Christina, loyal friend of many years to Gerald H. Blum. A memorial service in September will be announced. A scholarship fund in Donald's name will be established at Parsons School of Design, 66 Fifth Avenue, NY NY 10011. Donald was generous to family and friends. His creativity and charm will be greatly missed.
