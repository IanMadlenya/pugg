
        STREIT--Saul S. Jr. Born January 23, 1966, died December 10, 2003. He is the son of Jean M. Streit and the late Justice Saul S. Streit Sr. Saul was a lifelong resident of Manhattan and a graduate of Baruch College. Memorial Mass will be held on Monday, December 15, at the Church of the Holy Trinity, 316 East 88th St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Diabetes Association, Memorial and Honor Program, PO Box 2680, North Canton, OH 44720.
