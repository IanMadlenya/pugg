
        KAMIEL-Claire. On July 2, 1997. 88 years old. Beloved wife of the late Max L. Kamiel. Cherished mother to Rosalind and Bob Rosenblum, Silvia and the late Jerome Rolnick, Harriet & Oscar Levine, & Lillian and Robert Fabrikant. Dear sister to Irving and Ruth Laub. Adoring grandmother to 9 grandchildren and eleven great-grandchildren. Loved for her compassion, generosity, wit & vivacity. A successful business person, leader in many religious and charitable organizations. Formerly RW Matron in the Order of the Eastern Star. She will be sorely missed by her family. Services Friday, July 4, 10 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave., at 65th Street. We love you, mom. Rest in Peace.
        KAMIEL - Claire. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Claire Kamiel. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memories bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        KAMIEL-Claire. Memories of Claire Kamiel, who died on 7/2/97 go far beyond the grave. There is not enough gratitude that I and my family can ever express to Mrs Kamiel for all the goodness and kindness that she has extended to us over the years. Irving Liebman
