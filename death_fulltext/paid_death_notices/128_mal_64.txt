
        NUTKIS-Martin I. Of Roslyn Heights, New York. On September 4th, 1997. World War 11 Veteran. Beloved and adored husband of Pearl. Cherished and devoted father of Susan, Elyse and David. Loving father-in-law of Leslie, Rick and Peter. Adored and special grandfather of Samuel, Lucas, Isabella, Eliza and Jeanne, will all miss this gentle man with his warm smile, whose heart was generous to all. Graveside service Mount Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, at 1 P.M. today.
