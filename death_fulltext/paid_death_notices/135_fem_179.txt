
        PIKE-Rebecca M. Died suddenly and unexpectedly April 21 of cardiac arrest at age 48, near her home in Annadale, New Jersey. An outstanding, accomplished executive and warm, enthusiastic friend to her colleagues at Taro Pharmaceuticals U.S.A. and her many associates at the Health Care Business Women's Association. Beloved wife of Lawrence, cherished daughter of Joseph and Joyce Hassler, loving sister of James and Timothy. Memorial Service at the Robert Ford Funeral Home, Flemington, New Jersey, 11 A.M. on Saturday, April 25th. Donations to the Hunterdon County ASPCA would be appreciated.
