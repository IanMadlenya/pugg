
        YEGEN-Gladys (Dr. Gladys Winter). Loving wife of the late Christian. Died April 2, 1998 at the age of 93. Formerly of Teaneck and Tenafly, New Jersey. Beloved mother of Laura Kahl (Jim), Christian Yegen (Lonna), Carol Lerer (Nat). Devoted grandmother of Katy, Kristy, and Laurie, Hillary and Christopher, Keith, Scott and David and nine great grandchildren. A graduate of Vassar College and Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons. She practiced pediatrics in Teaneck, N.J. for 30 years. She leaves the legacy of love, kindness and caring for her family and friends. She was truly a women of uncommon virtue. Donations may be made to a charity of choice. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date.
