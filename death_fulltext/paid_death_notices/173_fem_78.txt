
        POMMERS-Leon. On June 7, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Irene. Loving father of Alice Greenwood and her husband Bob. Dear brother of Bertha Katz.         POMMERS-Leon. They don't make gentlemen like Leon Pommers anymore. Mannes mourns the loss of one of the 20th Century's great musical partners-musical artist, pianist, teacher, coach and imcomparably elegant friend. Joel Lester, Dean Mannes College of Music
