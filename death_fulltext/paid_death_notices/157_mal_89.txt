
        ROER-Edwin. Temple Israel Center of White Plains, NY, shares deeply in the grieving for Edwin Roer, a past President of this congregation, a constant source of cheer and encouragement to everyone, a devoted husband, father, grandfather, and brother, a sweet soul. Our condolences go to Dottie, Robert and Marjorie, Sara, Ricki & Paul, Lynn & Itai, Harvey and Janet. May Ed's memory endure as a blessing to those who loved him and love him still. Gordon Tucker, Rabbi David Glasser, President
