
        MAXFIELD-Edwin D. Age 87, passed away on October 17, 2002. He is survived by wife Ruth; brother, Robert; children, Donna Chimera, Richard and wife, Susan, James and wife, Greta; seven grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. He graduated from Lafayette College and served in the USAF and received the Bronze Star. He was the former manager of Aviation Sales for Shell Oil Co. Memorial service will be held at the Community Church of East Willeston on November 29, 2002 at 1:00 P.M. Memorial contributions to Capitol School of AustinHorizon Clinic, 2011 W. Koenig Ln. Austin, Texas 78756 for Autism Therapy.
