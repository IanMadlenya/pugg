
        SMILEY-Irving After a long illness, on September 9, 1999, of the age of 85. Widower of Molly Moses-Smiley and cherished father and best friend of Stuart, Frank and Arlene Weisberg. Beloved father-in-law of Larry. The loss of this warm man is felt by all. He will be truly missed by everyone whose life he touched. Services Monday, at 1 PM, at the Riverdale Temple, 4545 Independence Ave., Riverdale. In lieu of flowers memorial donatons may be made to the Hebrew Home for the Aged, 5901 Palisade Ave., Riverdale, NY. For information call Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1-800-468-3232.
