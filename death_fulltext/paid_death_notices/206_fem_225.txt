
        COHEN--Annette (nee Kopperman). Age 86. Died peacefully near her hometown of Philadelphia on March 21, 2004. A devoted wife and mom who lived life with gusto, she is survived by her beloved husband Marty, sons, Jeffrey and Stephen, daughter-in-law Cheryl and cherished granddaughter Samantha. Services will be held in Philadelphia Tuesday with a special shiva at Jeffrey and Cheryl's home in Manhattan on Thursday evening. Contributions may be made to Breast Cancer Research.
