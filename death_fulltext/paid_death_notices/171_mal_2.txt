
        BERGLUND-Richard Paul. Age 69. At his home in NYC, on March 30, 2001. He is survived by his children Lisa Berglund of New London, CT, and Paul Gordon Berlund of Shelton, CT; and by his sisters Mary Ellen Hekhuis of Thousand Oaks, CA, and Elsie Albright of Madison, WI. He was the Director of the IBM Gallery of Science and Art from 1982 to 1995. A memorial gathering will be held in May. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to a charity of the donor's choice.
