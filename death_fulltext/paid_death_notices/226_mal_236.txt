
        PORTER--David. The East End Hospice Board of Directors, staff and volunteers note with sadness the death of David Porter, founding member of Hospice Board of Directors, artist, patron and loyal friend. David was devoted to helping others and we are truly grateful to have had the benefit of many years of his good counsel and fidelity. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Marion, extended family and many friends. Priscilla Ruffin, President W. Michael Pitcher, Chairman, Board of Directors East End Hospice Westhampton Beach, NY
