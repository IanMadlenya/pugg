
        CHUBAK-Ida. Died February 24, 2003. Holocaust survivor. Beloved wife of Benjamin, mother of Gary (Lillian), Bruce (Shera) and Michael (Susan). Sister of Martin and Arthur Bard, Hilda Fishbein and Esther Gorny. Trusted 20 year employee of Bank Leumi in Manhattan and Boro Park, Brooklyn. Loved dearly by her 13 grandchildren. Her warmth, love, kindness & generosity will be missed by her family, friends & community.
