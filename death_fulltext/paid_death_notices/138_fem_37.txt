
        TALLY-Bernice Gang. Age 83. On July 3, 1998. Longtime resident of Scarsdale, NY. Beloved wife of the late Charles H. Tally. Devoted mother of Nancy Polevoy and William Tally and mother-in-law of Martin Polevoy. Dear sister of Kenneth Gang and sister-in-law of Fay Gang and Jerome and Marian Tally. Loving grandmother of Jason and John Polevoy. Funeral services are private. For information, please call Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 914-949-0566. Donations in her memory can be made to the American Jewish Historical Society.
