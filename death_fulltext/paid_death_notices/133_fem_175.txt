
        WINGATE-Judith Wagner. Age 63 suffered a brain aneurysm and passed away suddenly on February 21, 1998. Judy spent most of her life caring for others. She was a strong caring mother and loved her husband, children and family so much. She recently ceased full time employment on January 31 as a nurse clinician in the Osteoporosis Center at the Hospital for Special Surgery in New York. Before her latest career at the Hospital for Special Surgery, she spent many years as a dialysis nurse. In addition to her career and family she spent many hours volunteering with the Junior League of Brooklyn and Northern Westchester. Judy is survived by her husband John, her daughter Jennifer, her sons Jack and Jeff as well as two grandchildren, Julia and Jane. A memorial service will be held on Tuesday, February 24th, 11 A.M., at The Congregational Church of Huntington, 30 Washington Dr, off of Rt 25A North, Centerport, NY. In lieu of flowers if donations would like to be made in her name, please send to VIBS (Victims Information Bureau of Suffolk), a domestic violence agency where Judy was a Board member, P.O. Box 5483, Hauppauge, NY 11788. Her family misses her very much. We love you Mom.
