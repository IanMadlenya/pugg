
        MARKS--Leonard H. The Board of Directors, Overseers and staff of the International Rescue Committee are saddened by the death on August 7 of Leonard H. Marks, a member of our board from 1973 to 1986, our president from 1973 to 1979 and father of our fellow board member Robert E. Marks. As IRC president, Leonard Marks notably guided our lifesaving response to major refugee crises in Southeast Asia, East Africa and Latin America, and won praise for his extraordinarily effective leadership. Throughout his distinguished career in government and the private sector, Mr. Marks worked tirelessly for the cause of freedom and human rights. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his sons, Robert and Stephen, and their families. Alan Batkin and Jonathan Wiesner, Co-Chairmen of the Board Tom Brokaw and Winston Lord, Co-Chairmen, IRC Overseers John C. Whitehead and James C. Strickler, MD, Chairmen Emeriti George Rupp, President
