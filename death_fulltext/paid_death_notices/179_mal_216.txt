
        LEE-Robert Y. Died December 13. Loving husband of Winnie Wong Lee. Devoted father of Daniel W. Lee, M.D. Adoring grandfather of Diana and Elizabeth. B.A. Bowdoin College 1949, M.A. Syracuse University 1950, U.S. Army Intelligence World War II, CPA, former president Republican Club of Chinatown. He will always be remembered for his spirit, good humor and love of God, family and friends. Viewing at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Ave., New York, NY, Tuesday, 5-8:30 PM. A memorial will be held Wednesday, 11 AM, at First Chinese Presbyterian Church, 61 Henry St., NY.
