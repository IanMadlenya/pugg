
        HURNEY-Joseph J. Age 88, Nov. 12, 2001. A lifelong resident of Baldwin, NY, & recently Sayville, NY. Retired pressman of 49 years. Survived by five children, Eileen, Joe, Mary, Michael and Teresa. Father-in-law to Roy, Gerianne, John and Gabriel. Grandfather of ten and great-grandfather of ten. Predeceased by his beloved wife of sixty years, Rose. Dearest ''Pop'' in our hearts forever.
