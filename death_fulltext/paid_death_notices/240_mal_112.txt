
        DALY--John. Manhattan College mourns the loss of its Vice President for Finance, John Robert Daly. A distinctly dedicated member of the Manhattan College community for more than twenty years, he served the College with unsurpassed loyalty, professionalism, wisdom, enthusiasm and strength. He will be deeply missed. His devotion to his family, community, and charitable causes were the signature of his character. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his wife, Elizabeth, and their sons, John and Matthew. Be assured the prayers of the entire Manhattan College community are with the family at this difficult time. Funeral Services are being made by Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, NY. Visiting hours are Wednesday, January 10, 7 to 9pm. and Thursday, January 11, 2 to 4pm and 7 to 9pm. Mass of Christian Burial will be held Friday, January 12, at 10:30 a.m. at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Daly Children's Scholarship Fund, c/o Paul Doyle, 29 Jared Drive, White Plains, NY. Br. Thomas J. Scanlan, F.S.C., President and Members, Board of Trustees
