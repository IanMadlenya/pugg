
        GORDON-Miriam (nee Brodlieb). On February 22, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Nathan Gordon, loving mother of Ellen Milberg and Ronald Gordon. Mother-in-law of Leonard Milberg and Claire Gordon. Proud and loving grandmother of Brian Gordon, Elizabeth Gordon, David & Melinda Milberg, Daniel and Margot Milberg, and Sandra and David Shapiro. Great grandmother of Beverly Allison Milberg. Services on Wednesday, February 24, 1 P.M., at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        GORDON-Miriam. The Officers, Trustees and Staff of the Jewish Child Care Association express their profound sorrow at the loss of Miriam Gordon, a valued and esteemed member of JCCA's Board of Trustees for over 35 years. Her commitment to children and families and her significant role in establishing JCCA's Youth Residence Center will be long remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Joyce L. Kramer, President Irwin U. Goldberg, Board Chair Paul Gitelson, Executive V.P.
