
        BERBERIAN--Ara. The Metropolitan Opera mourns the death of our dear friend and colleague, Ara Berberian. He sang over 300 performances with us in a broad range of roles, putting his unique imprint on every part he sang. He will be greatly missed by his many friends in the company, and we extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Ginny, and his family. Joseph Volpe, General Mgr. James Levine, Music Dir.
