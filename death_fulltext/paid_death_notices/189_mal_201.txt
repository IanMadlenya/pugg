
        ALPERT-Abraham. Emunah of America mourns the loss of our highly esteemed and generous benefactor, Abraham Alpert, who together with his wife Sunny, a National Vice President, were founders of our organization and established the Tova Chapter in Manhattan. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family. May they be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Israel. Marcia Genuth, National Pres
