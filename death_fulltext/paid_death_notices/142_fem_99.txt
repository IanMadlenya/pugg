
        BARREL-Sadye. Affectionately known as Syd. On November 11, 1998 at age 98. Mrs. Barrel was a very astute business women. Among her favorite philanthropies were Bascon Palmer Eye Institute, Sloan-Kettering where she endowed the Nutrition Program, The American Technion Society, Good Samaritan and Intercoastal Hospitals in the Palm Beaches to mention a few. Beloved wife of the late Samuel. Loving mother of Iris Barrel Apfel and son-in-law of Carl Apfel. Adored daughter of the late Abraham and Jennie Asofsky and sister of the late Dr. Barnet Ash. Services private.
