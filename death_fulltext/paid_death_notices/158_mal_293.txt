
        LUMPKINS-William, 90, native New Mexican and longtime Santa Fe resident, passed away March 20. Artist, architect, author, solar energy pioneer, founder of the Santa Fe Art Institute; his many friends mourn his loss, his strength & his gentleness, & offer condolences to his wife, Norma, his four children & five grandchildren. The memorial is at the St Francis Auditorium at 11 AM March 28, followed by a gathering at Santa Fe's La Fonda Hotel. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Hospice Center/Transition Program (1400 Chama Ave, Santa Fe, NM) and the Lumpkins Memorial Fund (Fine Arts Museum, Santa Fe).
