
        MEYER-Yisrael (Julius). The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of the brother of Mr. Manfred H. Meyer, who, with his wife Eva, is a Guardian of Yeshiva University and established the Eva and Manfred H. Meyer Course in Modern Jewish Problems at Stern College for Women. Yisrael Meyer of Jerusalem was a long-time and highly-respected member of the community. Heartfelt condolences are extended to Manfred and Eva Meyer and all the members of their family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
        MEYER-Yisrael (Julius). The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the brother of its devoted member, Manfred Meyer. May his entire family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
