
        KAUFMAN--Hilda. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of a highly respected member of the Pittsburgh Jewish community. She is the beloved mother of Martin (and Shelley) Kaufman, who are YU Guardians, devoted supporters of the affiliated Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary, and Bernard Revel Graduate School of Jewish Studies, on which Board of Directors Martin serves. Heartfelt condolences are extended to them and to his sisters: Marlene (and Marshal) Terkel, and Marilyn (and Roger) Galbraith. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman, YU Board of Trustees Julius Berman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees Mordecai D. Katz, Chairman BRGS Board of Directors
