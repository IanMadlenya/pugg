
        WEISSBERG-Seymour M., 77, on May 16, 2002 at home with his wife at his side. Beloved by his family, friends and fellow artists for his devotion, kindness, sage advice, humor and sweetness. Dearly beloved by his wife Dolores. Loving father to Susan, Eric and Andrew. Loving father-in-law to Diane and Freda. Beloved grandfather to Zoe, David and Samantha. Dear to his brother Irwin and his nephews William and Charles and their wives Rosemary and Alisa. Donations in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
