
        MORENO--Dr. Gustavo J. III. 78, formerly of New York, NY, Miami and Dania Beach, FL died on October 3, 2006 in Vero Beach, FL. He is survived by his wife of 30 years, Alice Seemann Moreno; his children Estela Moreno-Bosketti (Frank) of Chestnut Ridge, NY, Dr. Linda J. Merchant (Dr. Robert) of Billings, MT and Gustavo J. Moreno IV (Haley Chow) of New York, NY and Mobile, AL; his grandchildren Anna Moreno-Bosketti and Elisabeth, Theresa and Thomas Merchant; his sisters Moraima Corral (Domingo) of Orlando, FL; Irene Jones (Fred) of Tampa, FL and a brother, Marc Moreno (Dr. Diane) of Bargersville, IN. Along with nieces, nephews and cousins. His daughter May Moreno preceded him in death. Dr. Moreno, who retired from the practice of Obstetrics and Gynecology in 1993, was born and raised in Tampa, FL where he attended Jesuit H.S. He graduated from Spring Hill College in 1948 and Georgetown University School of Medicine in 1952. After residency at New York Medical College he entered into private practice in 1958 and was also an Assistant Professor of Clinical Obstetrics and Gynecology at New York Medical College until relocating to Miami, FL in 1990. He was an Assistant Professor of Clinical Obstetrics and Gynecology at the University of Miami and Director of Gynecological Services at the VA Medical Center in Miami until his retirement. His family and many great friends will miss his sense of humor, passion for life and concerns for the less fortunate. A mass of Christian Burial will be held 10:00 am, Saturday, October 7, 2006 at St. Helen's Catholic Church. Contributions may be made to VNA Hospice, 1110 35th Lane, Vero Beach, FL 32960 in memory of Dr. Gustavo J. Moreno III.
