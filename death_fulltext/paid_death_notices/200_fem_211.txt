
        HOWE--Isabelle Labouisse. Died September 26, 2003, in Greenwich, CT, after a lengthy illness. She was born February 24, 1939, in Charlotte, NC; daughter of the late Sally Cameron Labouisse and John Witherspoon Labouisse. She is survived by her loving husband of 40 years, Nathaniel Saltonstall Howe Jr., three children: Frances Labouisse Howe Curley of Wade, NC, Nathaniel Saltonstall Howe III of Pennington, NJ, and John Witherspoon Howe of Salt Lake City, UT, and three grandchildren. She was a graduate of St. Timothy's School, Stevenson, MD, Bradford Jr. College, Haverhill, MA, and the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Mrs. Howe was an active member of The Greenwich Garden Club, The Greenwich Women's Exchange, and the Greenwich Garden Education Center. Memorial services will be held at Christ Church, Greenwich, at 254 East Putnam Ave, Greenwich, CT 06830, on Wednesday, October 1, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to: Ballard Park Foundation, PO Box 96, Westport, NY 12993; Bendheim Cancer Center, Attn: Development Office, 89 Lake Ave, Greenwich, CT 06830; or Christ Church Greenwich, 254 East Putnam Ave, Greenwich, CT 06830.
