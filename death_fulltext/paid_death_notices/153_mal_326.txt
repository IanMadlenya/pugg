
        LINETT-Paul. The Board of Directors of the Clothing Manufacturers Association of the USA mourn the loss of Paul Linett, retired President of Linett Clothes, Ltd., who had served both CMA as Board member and the clothing industry for many years with class and distinction. Our deepest condolences to his family. Homi B. Patel, President Harvey J. Weinstein, Chairman Robert A. Kaplan, Executive Director Bernard Ferster, General Counsel
