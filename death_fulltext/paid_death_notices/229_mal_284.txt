
        KURTZ--Dr. Kent Worthington, 65, Arcadia, CA died at 9:20 A.M. Saturday, February 18, 2006 after a short, but courageous battle with cancer, ending in peace. He was employed as professor of Ornamental Horticulture by California State Polytechnic University at Pomona, CA for 36 years. A memorial celebration of Kent Kurtz's life will be held at Kellogg West at a date to be determined.
