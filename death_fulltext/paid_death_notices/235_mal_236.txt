
        TERMAN--Isaiah. July 21, 1911 to August 23, 2006. Father of Jane Terman McMahan and Michael Terman, grandfather of Selena Clare McMahan, father-in-law of Jiuan Su Terman and Ian McMahan. Devoted husband of the late Daisy Terman. Appreciated for his wit and warmth. Served as a national staff director and director of communications for the American Jewish Committee. Services private
