
        ZANGER-Carl Lewy. Died October 6, 2002, born in New York City in 1931, graduate of Hamilton College, Harvard Law School. He also served in the U.S. Army. Served as Chairman of the Committee on Art Law of the Association of the Bar of the City of New York at a time when important legislation was considered and enacted relating to artists and galleries. Under his leadership, the Committee had a major role in shaping legislation in New York and nationwide. A solo practitioner representing clients in a wide range of personal and business matters, an anachronism in today's world. Carl is survived by Sandra, his wife of 36 years, his three sons Sam, Hugh and Jesse and his brother Walter and sister Judith Segal. Memorial Service arrangements to be announced.
