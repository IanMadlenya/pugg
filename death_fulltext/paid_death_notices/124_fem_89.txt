
        ZAKARIAN-Beverly. On May 11, 1997. Beloved wife of Robert. Devoted mother of Daisy Zakarian Madison and son-in-law of John Madison. Co-Founder and Executive Director of C-A-N-A-C-T, the first cancer patient advocacy group. Mrs. Zakarian represented patient issues before Congress, the President's Cancer Panel and FDA committees. She was the first FDA Oncology Fellow appointed as a representative of cancer survivors to participate in the review process for new cancer therapies. Mrs. Zakarian is the author of ''The Activist Cancer Patient'' and numerous articles which appeared in the popular press and medical journals. Her unswerving commitment and passionate voice for cancer patients will be greatly missed. Services at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, N.Y. at 6PM, Tuesday, May 13th. Contributions may be made to The Chemotherapy Foundation, 183 Madison Ave., NY, NY 10016.
