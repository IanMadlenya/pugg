
        KAUFMAN-Raymond Lee. Loving father and grandfather, passed away on November 22, 1999 in Santa Monica, CA. He was 70 years old. He will be remembered for his style, compassion and sparkling humor. Ray is survived by his daughter Ilene and husband David and grandson Gabriel, son John, daughter Nancy and son-inlaw Jeffrey, brother Lewis, sister Carol, and brother-in-law Bob, and the many cousins who loved him. Services will be held Wednesday, November 24, 11 AM, at Hillside Memorial Park, Los Angeles, CA. Contributions may be made to the Cedar-Sinai Medical Center, Parkinson's Research.
