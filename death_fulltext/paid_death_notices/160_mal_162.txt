
        PIORE-Emanuel R., PhD. The Boards of Overseers & Managers of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center are deeply saddened by the death of their colleague and advisor, Dr. Emanuel R. Piore. Dr. Piore began his association with Memorial Sloan-Kettering in 1963 as a member of The Board of Trustees of Sloan-Kettering Institute for Cancer Research. At the time of his death Dr. Piore was a member of The Board of Overseers Emeriti of Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center. We will miss his wise counsel and friendship. We extend our healtfelt condelences to the Piore family. Laurance S. Rockefeller Honorary Co-Chairman James D. Robinson III Honorary Co-Chairman Douglas A. Warner III Chairman Richard I. Beattie Vice Chairman Louis V. Gerstner, Jr Vice Chairman Harold Varmus, MD, President Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
