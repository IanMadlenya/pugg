
        GIBSON--Ellen Amy. 84 years of age. On December 26, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Henry John. Devoted mother of Colin John. Loving mother-inlaw of Maureen McNellis. Dear grandmother of Maggie Beth, fond sister of Stanley George. Mrs. Gibson was a lifelong resident of London, England. The love she had for her family was her greatest gift. Always loved, always remembered. Funeral from Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Interment of Ashes will be held at The Enfield Crematorium, London, England. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Friends Seminary, 222 E. 16 St, New York, N.Y. 10003 att: Fred Bush.
