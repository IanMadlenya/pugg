
        WILLSTATTER--Richard, 83 years old, passed away May 26th, 2005. Beloved husband of Gertrude, loving father to Patricia, Richard and Amy, loving grandfather of Sara and Emily. Graduate Yale, Class of '43. Recipient of the Knight of the Legion of Honor, June 2004. Memorial service to be held at 11 AM, Sunday, May 29th, Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch Street, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the American Red Cross would be preferred.
        WILLSTATTER--Richard. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Richard Willstatter. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Trudy, daughter Amy, and to the entire family. Patricia Sumergrade, Pres. Richard J. Bronstein, Secy.
