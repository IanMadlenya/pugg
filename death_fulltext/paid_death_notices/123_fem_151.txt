
        URICH-Masha. Formerly of Forest Hills, NY, died on April 4th, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Samuel Urich, and much loved mother, mother-in-law and grandmother of Heidi Urich, Graeme Fincke and Joshua and Anna Fincke of Cambridge, MA, and sister-in-law and aunt of Esther and Efraim Ben-Ishai of Tel Aviv, Israel. Born in Zawiercie, Poland, and a survivor of the Holocaust, she will be dearly missed by her family and many devoted friends.
