
        SCANLON-John J. In New Haven, CT, February 21, 1999, age 89, of North Branford, CT, formerly of Fairfield, husband of Mildred Colvard Scanlon, retired Executive Vice-President and Chief Financial Officer with AT&T. Funeral services Thursday, February 25, 1999 at 11:00 A.M. in Trinity Episcopal Church, 651 Pequot Avenue, Southport, CT. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.
