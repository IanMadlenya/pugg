
        FELDGARDEN-Benjamin. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Benjamin Feldgarden, beloved father of Diane Goldsmith, a dear friend of the Jewish community. Mrs. Goldsmith and her husband, Jay, a distinguished member of our Board of Directors, embrace their family's deep commitment to Jewish and humanitarian causes through their dedicated efforts and exceptional generosity. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Mr. and Mrs. Goldsmith; Mr. Feldgarden's cherished wife, Sylvia; and the entire family. Larry Zicklin, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
