
        LAZAR-Helen Bandow, 79. Adoring wife of Jack. Mother and mother-in-law of Bill and Anne, Jeanne and Michael. Grandmother of Jack, Rebecca, Cooper, Rita and Matthew. She, with her husband Jack, cofounded Kimberly Knitwear, Inc., and she served as the head designer. They built a wonderful company together and had a great time doing so. The memorial service was held in Teaneck, NJ, on Jan. 31. Contributions in her memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
