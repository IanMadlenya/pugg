
        GOULD-George K. Of Somers, NY. Died of cancer at his home on August 23. He was 84. He was a pioneer in the television videotape industry. He is survived by his loving wife Nancy, his devoted children Ronnie Beth Sauers and Jane Soodalter; sons-in-law Ron Sauers and Ron Soodalter and cherished granddaughters Jesse and Melora Soodalter. Services today, August 25, 10:00AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mount Vernon, NY. Shiva at 704 Heritage Hills, Somers, New York.
