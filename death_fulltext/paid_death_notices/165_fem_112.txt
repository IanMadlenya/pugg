
        SCHRAMM-Sarah. Born December 10, 1907. Passed away October 8, 2000. Cherished wife of the late Sidney M. Schramm. Beloved mother of Jennifer and Alden Englander of Mamaroneck, NY, Felice and the late Richard L. Cohan of NYC, and Joanne and Leonard Schramm of Scottsdale, Arizona. Devoted grandmother of Jason, Cathy and Bob, Betsy, Ronald, Neal and Hollye, and Michael. Loving great-grandmother of Sydney, Jane, Joshua and Ian. ''Syd'' was a very elegant lady who was very supportive of her husband and entire family. Services Wednesday, noon, Riverside Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon.
