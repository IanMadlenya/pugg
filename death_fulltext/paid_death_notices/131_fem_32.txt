
        HARTIGAN-Nancy Irene. On December 1, 1997. Beloved wife of John D., mother of Anne Harrison, Carol, Patti and Thomas. Devoted grandmother of five. Since 1985, she was a Reference Librarian at the Harrison Public Library where she was invaluable to patrons from youngsters to senior citizens. Visiting hours are 2-4 and 7-9 PM today at the William H. Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York. The funeral mass is 10 AM tomorrow at Resurrection Church in Rye, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions to The Friends of the Harrison Public Library, Bruce Avenue, Harrison, New York.
