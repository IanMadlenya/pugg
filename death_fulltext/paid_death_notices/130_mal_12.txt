
        McCULLEN-Robert K., 78. Of Saddle River died on October 28, 1997. He was a graduate of both Columbia University and Columbia University Law School. Mr. McCullen was an employee of the Mendes & Mount Law firm where he had been a partner. Mr. McCullen was a navy veteran of World War II. Survived by his loving wife of 54 years Gwen of Saddle River, a brother Thomas of Panama City, Florida, as well as a number of nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held on Monday, November 3, at 11:00 A.M., at the C.C. Van Emburgh Chapel, 306 East Ridgewood Avenue, Ridgewood. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Columbia University Alumni Educational Fund, the National Resources Defense Council, or the Nature Conservancy.
