
        EGE-Knox Martha. Of NYC died February 16, 1998. Wife of the late Kevin Butler and the late Warren S. Ege. She was the daughter of Federal Judge John C. Knox and Mrs J. Knox. Mrs. Ege attended the Lincoln School in NYC and Sarah Lawrence College. Services private. Memorial contributions may be made to Waynesburg College, Waynesburg, PA., and Mary Hitchkock Pavillion, Dartmouth College, Hanover, N.H.
