
        MORRIS - Morton G., D.D.S. Passed away November 14, 2001. Beloved husband of Eleanor. Proud father of Steven & Andrew. Loving grandfather of Alexandra. Uncle of Robert Gewirtz. Practiced as a dentist in Williamsburg, Jackson Heights and Roslyn, N.Y. Ended his long dental career as a devoted teacher at NYU College of Dentistry in Manhattan. WWII veteran who served in the Pacific and retired as a Commander in the U.S. Navy. He will be greatly missed by those who knew him throughout his life.
