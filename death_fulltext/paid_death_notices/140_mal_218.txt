
        EVANS-Carnot Wagener, 78. Of Kilmarnock, Virginia, formerly of Darien, CT. Died Wednesday, September 16, 1998. He was a retired investment banker and CEO of Clark, Dodge & Company of NY, NY. He is survived by his wife Jacquelyn P. Evans, a daughter, two sons, five grandchildren and a brother. Funeral services will be held 11AM, Saturday, Sept. 19 at Historic Christ Church, Irvington, VA. Memorial contributions may be made to your favorite charity.
