
        HOROWITZ--Diana Cooper, in her 107th year, died on April 26th in Ridgewood, NJ. Mother to David, aunt to Deborah Eigen Graifman, her husband Gary and son, Michael. Wife of the late Herman. Born in Philadelphia to Dora and Samuel Cooper. Graduate of the University of Pennsylvania. Her 50 year career in social work included Director of Community and Volunteer services for the National Council of Jewish Women, New York Section. She was a founder and director of the Widows Consultation Center in NY in the 1970's. Her's was a life lived with grace, dignity, intelligence, generosity and kindness. Diana was a truly amazing and extraordinary woman who will be greatly missed and forever loved.
