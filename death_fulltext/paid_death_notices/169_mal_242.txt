
        SCHANCER-Lola J. Died February 19th in Nashua, NH. Born in Brooklyn on October 12, 1926. She was married 34 years to Myron Schancer, who died in 1983. Family members include two sons, Douglas Schancer of Derry, NH and his wife Kathy; and Gary Schancer of Albuquerque, NM and his wife Kathy; three grandchildren; a sister; and nieces and nephews.
