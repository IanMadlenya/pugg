
        LIEBOWITZ-Bruce Jay. Age 37, of Tel Aviv, Israel, formerly of Gaithersburg, MD, on September 5, 2002 after a courageous battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Shelly. Adored father of Smadar, Dan, Eli and Mai. Cherished son of Howard Liebowitz and Rita (Walter) Pancik. Loving brother of Andrew (Caryn) Liebowitz, Kara (Alan) Silbert, Rachel (Randy) Querry, and Betsy (Bernard) Catt. Devoted uncle of Zachary, Arielle and Katie. He will be deeply missed by his aunts, uncles, cousins and countless friends. He was a man blessed with an energetic spirit who loved and was loved by many.
