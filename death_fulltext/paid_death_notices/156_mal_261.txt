
        REESE-William Willis. The New York Society of the Second War with Great Britain (1812) records its profound sorrow at the untimely passing of our dear friend, longtime member and Treasurer, and sends its deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Sonia, his mother, and the entire family. A Memorial Service will be held tomorrow, Wednesday, January 19, at 4:00 PM, at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave. and 50th St. Thomas E. Bird, President John Mauk Hilliard, Secretary/Registrar
