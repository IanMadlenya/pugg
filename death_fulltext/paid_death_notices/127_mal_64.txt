
        STRAIGHT-William S., 52, on August 3, 1997, peacefully at home in Briarcliff Manor, NY after a valiant battle with pancreatic cancer. He leaves behind his wife, Liz, and their children, Anne and Jonathan, to whom he was always a totally devoted husband and father, best friend, companion, advisor, and guardian angel. His dedication, loyalty, high standards, and his bravery, toughness and humor, even under the most difficult of circumstances, will serve as a shining light to them and to all who knew him. The family will receive friends at the Waterbury and Kelly Funeral Home 1300 Pleasantville Rd. Briarcliff Manor on Wednesday, August 6, from 2-4 and 7-9. Services will be held on Thursday, August 7, at 11 AM at St. Mary's Episcopal Church in Scarborough, with burial following at Sleepy Hollow Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please send gifts to the Ossining Children's Center, 90 South Highland Avenue, Ossining, NY 10560, c/o Betsy Block.
