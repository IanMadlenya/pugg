
        HOLLAND--Sidney Jr. Died peacefully on June 26 at age 70. Born in Childress, TX, he received his B.A. and M.B.A. at University of Texas, Austin. Arrived in New York and became a self-made success as a Vice President at Kidder Peabody. Served in the ''Great War'' between Korea and Vietnam and consummate joker with all. Member of the University Club. Great friend since childhood and husband to Dr. Nancy Holland, father to Sidney, Charlotte & George, grandfather to James, Matthew, Kristen and Sarah. Memorial Service will be held on Sunday, June 29 at 1:30 P.M. at the Quaker Meeting House, 158 Southern Boulevard, Chatham, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Salvation Army, 95 Spring Street, Morristown, NJ 07960. >>AD#
