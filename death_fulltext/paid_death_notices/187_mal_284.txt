
        DiDOMENICO-Anthony J., 88, of Pleasantville NY on August 26. Born in NYC, the youngest of six children of Maria and Vincenzo DiDomenico, immigrants from Sicily. Loving husband of Gilda, devoted father of Mary Ann Mazzella of Pleasantville NY and Lucille DiDomenico of Dallas TX. Loving fatherin-law of Sylvester S. Mazzella and the Rev. Lawrence D. Spencer. Adoring grandfather of Ava Mazzella of Peekskill NY, Jennifer DiDomenico Spencer of Santa Monica CA, Eric Spencer and Dr. Craig Spencer of Seattle WA. He will be missed by his sister Josephine Faranda and numerous friends and family. A high school graduate, DiDomenico often said he earned the equivalent of a college degree from his lifelong reading of the NY Times. Visitation Thursday August 29, 2-4pm and 7-9pm. Memorial service 7:45pm, Waterbury and Kelly Funeral Home, 1300 Pleasantville Rd, Briarcliff Manor, NY. Funeral Mass 10:30am Friday August 30, Holy Innocents Catholic Church, 431 Bedford Rd, Pleasantville, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to Anthony J. DiDomenico, Hillcrest Elementary School Music Fund, Peekskill School District, 1031 Elm St, Peekskill, NY, 10566.
