
        LORBER-Arnold. The American Committee for Shenkar College and Shenkar College deeply mourn the untimely passing of our dear friend and benefactor, Arnold Lorber, honorary fellow of the College. Arnold and his wife Anita have be ardent supporters of the college enabling it to expand with the construction of the Lorber Building. We will miss his kindness and devotion to the college. Our condolences to Anita and the family. David Pernick, Chairman, Board of Governors Amotz Weinberg, Pres. Shenkar College Nahum G. Shar, Pres. American Committee Charlotte A. Fainblatt, Exec. Dir. American Committee
