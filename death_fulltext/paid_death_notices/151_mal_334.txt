
        GUMPRECHT-Herbert. On August 27, 1999. Beloved husband of Nina Stutter Gumprecht. Father of Mona Stiernstedt, Susan Hjelm, Ivonne Sahlin, and Nicholas Gumprecht. Cherished grandfather of nine and great-grandfather of two. As a member of Seawanhka Corinthian Yacht Club, The Royal Yacht Squadron, and the New York Yacht Club, he was an avid sportsman and sailor having won many world championships and local events. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, September 1, 1999, 11 AM, St. John's of Lattingtown Church.
