
        MARQUIS-Roy Porter. Age 57, died February 10, 2000, at home after a brief illness. A memorial gathering was held February 13th at the home of Anita Rogers, his loving companion and partner of 34 years. In addition to Anita Rogers, Mr. Marquis was survived by his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Roy Marquis, and brothers, David and Harry. Mr. Marquis was an attorney, and will be sorely missed by his many friends in New York and those with whom he carried on an extensive and voluminous correspondence since moving to New York. Mr. Marquis was known for his repartee, cornucopia of knowledge, and insatiable appetite for reading. Interment will be private.
