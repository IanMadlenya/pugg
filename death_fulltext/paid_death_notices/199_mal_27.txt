
        RESCHKE--Dr. Stephen E. Of Rego Park, NY. Died on July 30th at age 62 after a battle with brain cancer. An industrial organizational psychologist, he was a graduate of the Ramaz School, the City College of New York, and Case Western Reserve University. A native New Yorker, he was the son of the late Dr. Morris and Selma Reschke. A beloved husband, father and grandfather, he is survived by his wife Frieda, his children Anat (Mathew Rosenberg), Amihai (Dahlia) and Aviva, and grandson Nathan Rosenberg. >>AD#
