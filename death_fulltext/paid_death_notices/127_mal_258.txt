
        STEIN-Fred M., Jr. The Board of Directors and Staff of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York deeply mourn the loss of Fred M. Stein, Jr., friend and supporter of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York. Husband of the late Margaret Hellman Stein, who was a Director of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York for over 50 years and life-long volunteer of the agency. Mr. Stein's commitment and generosity will be remembered with gratitude. We extend our profound sympathy at this time to his family. Carl H. Pforzheimer III Chairman of the Board Carol Raphael President and Chief Exec. Officer
