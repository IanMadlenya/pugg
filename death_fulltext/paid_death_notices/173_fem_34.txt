
        SAPER-Frances. Of Great Neck, NY, on June 1, 2001. Beloved wife of Harold. Loving mother of Gary and Myles Schumer, and motherin-law of Judy Schumer and Anne Riney. Adored grandmother of Paul, Rina and Lauren Schumer. Active in her Great Neck community for almost 50 years, she was past secretary of the Great Neck United Community Fund and longstanding member of the Great Neck Garden Club. She will be lovingly remembered and sorely missed by all who knew her. Services Sunday, 1 PM, at Riverside-Nasau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
