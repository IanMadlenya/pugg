
        DENERSTEIN-Hyman. Age 88. Of Valley Cottage, N.Y. on January 4, 2002 after a long illness. Son of the late Isidore Denerstein and Rose Borodkin. Survived by son Dan of New City, N.Y. and two grandsons Matthew & Evan. Predeceased by Rita, wife of 64 years. Hyman had a long career as a social worker, stationery/luncheonette owner & employee of OTB. His immediate and extended family, particularly the Zevin Descendants, mourn his loss. Service at Hellman Memorial Chapel, 15 State St, Spring Valley, N.Y. 2PM Thursday, January 10th. Donations in his name may be made to the Nyack Hospital Foundation, and/or Meals on Wheels of Rockland Co.
