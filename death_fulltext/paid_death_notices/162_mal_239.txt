
        BASU-Ashim K. On July 22, 2000 at the age of 50. Those at Marymount Manhattan College mourn the passing of a dynamic teacher and friend as Dean of Faculty and Vice President of Academic Affairs, Dr. Basu was respected for his administrative strengths, as an accomplished educator, and for his devotion to his family. Dr. Basu is survived by his wife, Sandra Slotnick, his parents, Arun and Malati Basu, his sister, Sutapa Basu, his brother, Ashoke Basu, all of Seattle, Washington. Friends will be received after the funeral service at 10 AM Tuesday, July 25, at the Jacobs Funeral Home, 47 Old River Road, in Wilkes-Barre, PA. Those wishing to share the traditional Hindu ritual should arrive at 9 AM. Marymount Manhattan College President Peruggi, Faculty, Administration and Students
