
        ROBERTS--Edward Paul. 52, on January 8, 2004. Peacefully after a long illness. Survived by loving parents Gloria and Leonard Roberts and sister Lynne Roberts of New York City. Edward was a graduate of Tufts University and had an MBA from York College. He was was employed by the York (PA) County Department of Mental Health and Mental Retardation as a Case Manager. Funeral services Wednesday, January 14th, at 12:45 pm, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC. A memorial service in York, PA, to be announced. Contributions may be made to the York Hospital or the Leukemia Society.
