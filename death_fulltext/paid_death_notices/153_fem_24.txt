
        KILMARX-Helen Wood, of Scarsdale, New York, died September 1, 1999. Mother of Meredith Wood, Jr. & Kingsley Wood. Grandmother of John Scott Wood, James Stewart Wood, Kingsley Wood, Jr. and Leslie Wood Pellech. Greatgrandmother of Emma Elizabeth Wood and Kingsley Wood III. A memorial service will be held at Hitchcock Presbyterian Church in Scarsdale, NY on October 15th at 2PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to Vassar College, Poughkeepsie, New York 12601.
