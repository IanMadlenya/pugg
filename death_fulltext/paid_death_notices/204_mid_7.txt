
        HECHT--Lillian. On behalf of the Congregation Shaare Zion and the Executive Committee, we express our deepest condolences to Emeritus Rabbi Abraham Hecht and his family for the loss of Lillian Hecht A''H. On Thursday, January 15, 2004, Mrs. Hecht tragically lost her life in a fire which erupted in her home. Lillian Hecht, 82, remains a beloved wife, mother, grandmother and great grandmother. Isaac M. Franco President
