
        MARTINI-Lois Cowan, of NYC. Age 89. Died at home April 18, 1997. She was the beloved wife of Roland Martini, Vice President of Gardner Advertising who died in 1966. Born and raised in Sioux Falls, S.D., she received a scholarship and graduated from St. Joseph's College, Emmitsburg, MD in 1928. She taught high school on Pine Ridge Indian Reservation in SD, and English at Washington H.S. in Sioux Falls. At the begining of World War II, she moved to Washington D.C. and then to NYC to work for the FBI as a translator of French and Italian. After W.W. II, she went to work at Gardner Advertising where she met and married her late husband. In her retirement, after his death, she made frequent trips to Italy and London, England and attended the Church of St. John the Evangelist in NYC. She is survived by sisterin-law Mrs. Robert Cowan (Margaret) of Hemet, CA; niece Mrs. Roger K. Allan (Nance) of Spearfish, SD and brother-in-law Edward A. Wojcoski of Arlington VA, and many longtime close friends. Services, including viewing at 9:30AM today. Funeral Mass at 10:30AM will be held at the Church of St. John the Evangelist (1st Avenue and 55 Street). Interment will be at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne NY immediately following the services. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Church of St. John the Evangelist, 348 East 55 Street, NY 10022 or VNSNY Hospice Care, 1250 Broadway, N.Y., N.Y. 10001.
