
        YEUNG--Winston C.Y., of Hastings - on - Hudson, Man hattan and Hong Kong, died at 67 on Tuesday, August 31, 2004. He was educated in the U.K. as a Chartered Accountant and later became an entrepreneur. He is survived by his wife, Helena Tse, daughter, Karen Kar-Ling, two brothers, Chi Tak and Dr. Lawrence Chi Kin, and seven sisters, Kit Chee, Kit Wan, Catherine Kit Ming, Kit Har, Jessica Kit Ching, Alice Kit Sum and Winnie Kit Fong, and 13 nieces and nephews. Funeral is private. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to Watch Tower Bible and Tract Society of Pennsylvania, 25 Columbia Heights, Brooklyn, NY 11201.
