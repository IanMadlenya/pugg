        TODD-W. Duane, M.D. The Boards, Faculty, and Staff of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons mourn the passing of our distinguished colleague Dr. W. Duane Todd. Dr. Todd, an obstetrician and gynecologist at Sloane Hospital for Women of Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, entered the College as a medical student in 1948, served his internship and residency at The Presbyterian Hospital and Sloane Hospital for Women, and was on the attending staff of the institution from 1958 to his retirement in 1988. At his death, he was Professor Emeritus of Clinical Obstetrics and Gynecology, and Vice Chairman of the department. We extend our condolences to his wife and family. At the family's request, contributions may be made to the Sloane Hospital for Women c/o Dr. Roger Lobo.
        TODD-W. Duane, M.D. The Society of the Alumni of Sloane Hospital for Women, Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center, mourns the loss of their most esteemed colleague, Dr. W. Duane Todd. He was a true friend to all who knew him and was much loved by his patients and those who had the good fortune to work with him. Our sincere condolence to his family. Duane will always be remembered. Rogerio A. Lobo, M.D. Director, Sloane Hospital for Women Nancy Jasper, M.D. Alumni President 2000-2001
