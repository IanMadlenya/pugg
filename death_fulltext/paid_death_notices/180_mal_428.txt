
        LAPSLEY-Jack. The trustees and staff of the American Scandinavian Foundation mourn the passing of its Treasurer, John W. Lapsley, who had served as an Executive Trustee since 1989. The Foundation is grateful for his long-standing commitment and support. To his wife, Hope, and his entire family, the ASF extends heartfelt condolences. Kristjan T. Ragnarsson, Chairman Edward P. Gallagher, President
