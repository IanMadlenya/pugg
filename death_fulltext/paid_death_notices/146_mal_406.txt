
        MELDON-Solomon. Age 74, of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Died on March 17, 1999, in Boynton Beach, FL. Mr. Meldon was in the field of education for over 25 years. He was a principal and the former Director of the Gifted Child Program for the city of New York. Beloved husband of the late Bernice J. Meldon (1926-1999). Loving father of Lance Meldon and Jill Meldon. Cherished grandfather of Sara Elizabeth, and devoted brother of George (Helen) Meldon and Martha (Morris) Weissman. Funeral services will be held today, Sunday, in Boynton Beach, FL. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Leukemia Foundation.
