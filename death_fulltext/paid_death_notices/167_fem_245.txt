
        MENDELL-Malvina C. 103, died peacefully on December 26, 2000, in Palm Beach, FL. Wife of the late M. Lester Mendell, to whom she was married for 65 years. Mother of Edward (Renee), Oliver (Shelley), and Olga (Wilbur) Toll. Grandmother of ten, great grandmother of fifteen, great great grandmother of three. Sister of Frances Kaplan, and Mussie Gellis. Mrs. Mendell was a Director of The Jewish Guild for The Blind, and The Honoree at their 1998 Dinner. She will be sorely missed by her family and friends. In lieu of flowers, memorials to a charity of your choice in her memory would be appreciated. Services noon Friday, December 29 at Riverside, 21 W. Broad St., Mt. Vernon. Respects may be paid at the home of Mrs. Toll on Friday after 4 PM.
        MENDELL-Malvina. The board of directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Malvina Mendell. Mrs. Mendell was the wife of the late Lester Mendell and the beloved mother of Oliver and Edward Mendell, all cherished friends and supporters of UJA-Federation for many years. Among his many contributions to the New York Jewish community, Oliver Mendell has served with distinction on the UJA-Federation Council of Overseers and is a long-time philanthropic leader in the Banking and Finance Division. We extend our deepest sympathy to her sons, and to the entire family of Malvina Mendell. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Chair, Board of Directors John S. Ruskay, Executive Vice President & CEO
        MENDELL-Malvina. The Officers, Directors and Staff of the USO of Metropolitan New York extend deepest sympathy to the family and friends on the passing of Malvina Mendell, mother of USO's Chairman Oliver M. Mendell. Myron J. Berman, President
