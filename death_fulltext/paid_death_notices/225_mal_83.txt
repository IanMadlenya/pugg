
        SCRIVANI--Arthur J., 79, of Wakefield, RI, and formerly of Leonia, NJ, died October 4, 2005 at South County Hospital. He is survived by his wife Anne L. (Romano) Scrivani of 57 years; five children, 15 grandchildren and a great grandson. Mr. Scrivani was President and General Manager of ITT Defense Communication Division in Nutley, NJ. Before becoming president, he was Program Director of the Global Positioning System program from its inception, Vice President of Marketing, and Vice President of Program Management. He traveled extensively worldwide, dealing with the Department of Defense and all the Armed Forces. He was an active member of the Air Force Association, and the Armed Forces Communication and Electronics Assoc.
