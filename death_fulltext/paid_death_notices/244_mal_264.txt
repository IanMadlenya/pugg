
        SHAPIRO--Herbert, 86, died May 15, 2007 in West Palm Beach, FL. Born in Bronx, NY. Served as New York City Deputy Comptroller and New York Supreme Court Judge. Survived by his wife of 64 years, Barbara; children Madelyn (Allison), Mitchell and Douglas; sister Florence Schneiderman; and grandchildren. Condolences to 5468 Mariesa Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33417.
