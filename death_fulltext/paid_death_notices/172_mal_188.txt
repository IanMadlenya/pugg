
        MUHLFELD-Frank Bearss. Beloved husband of the late Betty Lou, died peacefully at home in Amagansett, NY surrounded by his family on May 15, 2001 at age 80. He leaves behind five children, Elizabeth Ingram of Manchester, MA, George Muhlfeld of Englewood, NJ, Katherine Bell of Northeast Harbor, ME, Lucy Kazickas of Amagansette, NY, Louise Patterson of San Francisco, CA and fourteen grandchildren all who loved him dearly. We send him on his way rejoicing with hallelujahs and a 21 gun salute. A memorial service will be held later.
