
        FRANKEL--Robert Sidney, MD, 82. Husband, father, grandfather, surgeon, mentor, community man, a quiet healer of integrity and honor. Former Chief of Surgery at Pascack Valley Hospital and former Director of Oncologic Surgery at Hackensack University Medical Center. You cared kindly and selflessly for family, patients, friends. You lived inspired by the inscription on the gates at Harvard, your alma mater, ''Enter to grow in wisdom. Depart to serve better thy country and thy kind.'' We loved your humor, we loved your elegance, we loved your loyalty. We loved you. Mourned by wife Jeanne (nee Rothblatt), sons Richard, Kenneth, David, daughters-in-law Jane and Annie and grandchildren Sarah, Kate, Michael, Daniel, Samuel, Eli and Julia. Services Thursday, 10:45AM at Cedar Park Cemetery at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah Mausoleum, Forest Avenue, Paramus, NJ. Contributions in memory can be made to: Boston University School of Medicine Scholarship Fund, 715 Albany Street, Boston, MA 02118 or to: The New Israel Fund, 1101 14th Street, NW, 6th flr, Washington, DC 20005-5639. For information Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels, West Palm Beach, FL 1-800-947-4256.
