
        TANAKA--Vic. Devoted husband and father, truest friend, artist and good soul, left us on Thursday, October 5, 2006. Husband of Fumi Tanaka, and father of Cathy, John and Tom, he was also the proud grandfather of Gillian and Shelby. A native of California, he was a graduate of Art Center, and World War II combat veteran serving from 1943-44, before settling in NYC to become a professional photographer. Visiting Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm, at the EdwardsDowdle Funeral Home, Dobbs Ferry, NY.
