
        BASTIS-Christos G.. On May 21, 1999, at his home in Palm Beach, FL. Beloved and devoted husband of Josephine. Loving and supportive father of Betty, George, Christopher and Jason. Loving grandfather of seven and great-granfather of three. His strength, courage and determination was unyielding, and his great love of family, friends and life will always be cherished. We all love him and will miss him dearly. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., Monday and Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral services at The Greek Orthodox Cathedral of the Holy Trinity, 319 East 74th St., Wednesday, 10 AM.
