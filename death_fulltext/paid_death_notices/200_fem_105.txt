
        LYSOHIR--Betty Jean. Born June 3, 1927, daughter of Charles and Betty Bremner of Lincoln, Nebraska, passed away peacefully at home on September 11 after a long illness. Preceded in death by her loving husband of 52 years, Walter. Walter and Betty retired to Boynton Beach, FL 15 years ago. Betty is survived by her sister Charlene; children, Steven and Bonnie and daughter-in-law Pamela; grandchildren, Jack, Charlotte, Keith and Wallis. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made in Betty's honor to: Women at Risk, Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. Funeral services at McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, NY on Sunday, September 14 at 1 PM.
