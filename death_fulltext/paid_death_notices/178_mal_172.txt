
        STEVENS-Peter Norton. Died on October 17th, 2001 at age 73. His education includes a BS degree in economics from the University of Virginia and an MS Degree from MIT. He spent five years in the US Marine Corps. Peter was a Peace Corps Country Director for six Latin American countries. He is survived by two sons, Kendall Edward Stevens of Zurich, Switzerland, Peter Jerome Stevens of Lansing, MI, and Noel Elizabeth Samra of Miami, Florida and five grandchildren. He also leaves a brother Don Edward Stevens of La Jolla, CA., and a sister Hope Thayer Iaccaci of NYC. A memorial service was held in Miami, FL.
