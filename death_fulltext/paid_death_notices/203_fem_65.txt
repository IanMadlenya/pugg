
        RYAN--Eileen B. (nee Sullivan), died Tuesday, December 10, 2003 at her home in Millbrook, New York, from complications related to lung cancer. She was 89. Fondly referred to by her grandchildren, nieces and nephews as ''Zsa Zsa'', she was well known for her wit and humor. When she was asked by hospice what music she would like played at her funeral, she said, ''A Good Man Is Hard To Find.'' She was born, raised and a long-time resident of the Bronx. She leaves behind a legacy of unwavering love and support, generosity and humor. She will be deeply missed by her son, Robert, of Orangesburg, N.Y., daughter, Regina, of Boston, MA, and three grandchildren. Visiting hours are Friday, 2-4 and 79PM at the Moritz Funeral Home, 98 Rte 303, Tappan, N.Y. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Saturday, 9:15AM, at St. Catharine's R.C. Church, Blauvelt, N.Y. She will be buried next to Roger, her beloved husband of 61 years at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-on-the-Hudson, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Seavey Joyce S.J. Community Service Award, 936 Nantasket Avenue, Hull, MA 02045
