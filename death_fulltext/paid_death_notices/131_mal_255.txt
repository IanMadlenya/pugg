
        STIX-Edgar. Died at home December 16. Loving and beloved husband of Carol, devoted father of Allen, Wayne, Roger and Gail. Father-in-law of Robert Way and Jane Share. Life-long resident of Scarsdale, New York, served in Submarine Force in World War II, dedicated his life to family and close friends to whom he was a wise, generous and dependable mainstay. Memorial Service will be at McMahon Funeral Home, 491 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains on Friday, December 19 at 2 P.M. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his memory to Planned Parenthood of Westchester, 11 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, New York 10532 or The Cancer Research Fund, 675 Third Avenue, New York, New York 10017.
        STIX-Edgar. Planned Parenthood of Westchester and Rockland Inc. records with sadness the death of Edgar Stix, a friend and advocate of our agency. We extend condolences to his wife, Carol, a dedicated member of the Board of Directors, and to his entire family. The Rev. Mark Bigelow, Chairman Francine Stein, Executive Director
