
        SCHWARTZ-Eleanor. Age 78. Of Pearl River, New York. Died peacefully on Thursday, March 25, 1999. Beloved wife of Dr. Nathaniel. Devoted mother of Harriett and Jay Glassman and Susan and Kenneth White. Adoring grandmother of Miriam and David Rukeyser, Juliane Glassman and Max White. Loving great grandmother of Emily and Sara Rukeyser. Other survivors include Dr. Abraham and Beatrice Gitlow, Yvette and Louis Tekel and Lawrence Gitlow. Services to be held Sunday, March 28th, 1PM at Hellman's Memorial Chapel, Spring Valley, N.Y. Donations may be made to the Rockland County Jewish Home for the Aged.
        SCHWARTZ-Eleanor. ''The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of Eleanor Schwartz, beloved sister of our esteemed colleague, Yvette Tekel. Our deepest heartfelt condolences to the entire family. May her memory be for a blessing.'' Marlene E. Post, Nat'l Pres. Dr. Laura S. Schor, Nat'l Exec. Dir.
        SCHWARTZ-Eleanor Gitlow. Our beloved sister has gone to eternity. Our profound sense of loss is tempered by the knowledge that her long struggle to overcome an unbelievable series of medical disasters is finally over, and she is at peace. Bea and Abe, Vet and Lou
