
        GINSBURG-Arthur J. The Partners, Associates & Staff of Frankfurt, Garbus, Klein & Selz mourn the death of Arthur J. Ginsburg, a friend, colleague & partner since the inception of the firm more than 20 years ago. His patience, tenacity and insight will be greatly missed. We extend our condolences & sympathies to his wife Ellin & to his daughter Ann. A Memorial Service will be held at the Park Ave Synagogue, 50 East 87 Street, New York, New York, Tuesday, July 21, 1998 at 2:30 P.M.
        GINSBURG-Arthur. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Ellin and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Sr. Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
