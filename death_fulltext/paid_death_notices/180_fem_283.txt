
        WALSH-Margaret Mary. Retired Assistant Superintendent of the Arlington School District in Poughkeepsie, NY, died January 30 at Ferncliff Nursing Home. She was a former Trustee of the New York Teachers Retirement System. Mass on Saturday, 10 AM, Our Lady of the Rosary Chapel in Poughkeepsie, NY. Visitation on Saturday 8-9AM at Miller Funeral Home, 371 Hooker Avenue.
