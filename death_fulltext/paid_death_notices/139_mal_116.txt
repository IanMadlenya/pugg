
        Hill-Howard M., 76. Of Gallatin, New York. Died on August 8 at Sharon Hospital, Sharon, Conn. Husband of Jane Hill and father of Jane Forte, Lillian Hill, Susan Deitchman, Mary Alice Buckley, Howard ''Butch'' Hill, Theodore Hill, Ernest Hill, Richard Hill and William Hill. Brother of Harold and Melvin Hill. He was a Paper Handler for The New York Times at the Edison, NJ office. Friends may call on Tuesday, 7-8:30 PM and Wednesday 2-4 PM and 7-8:30 PM at the Peck & Peck Funeral Home, Pine Plains, New York. Funeral Services are on Thursday, 11 A.M., at the Funeral Home.
