
        COMISSIONA--Maestro Sergiu. ICM Artists mourns the loss of Maestro Sergiu Comissiona who was a revered member of the ICM conducting roster for the better part of twenty years. He will be sorely missed not only as a world-class maestro but also as a dear friend to all he encountered. We will so fondly remember him. David V. Foster, President Byron Gustafson Exec. Vice President Jenny Vogel Exec. Vice President Richard Corrado Senior Vice President
