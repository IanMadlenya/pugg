
        FLOCH--Erna. Of Palm City and Tequesta, Florida died on October 16, 2005 at the age of 96 following a stroke. She was preceded in death by her husband, Alex Floch, and her brother, Ernst Vesely. She is survived by her sister, Ursula Globig of Carvoveiro, Portugal, and her cousin, Cecille Davis of New York and many loving nieces and nephews and their families. Erna was widely admired for her strength and positive outlook and will be missed by her extended family and many friends. Services will be held in Rosedale Cemetery in New Jersey. Memorial gifts in Erna's honor may be made to Archie Carr National Wildlife Refuge, 4424 NW 13th St., Suite A-1, Gainesville, FL 32609 or Treasure Coast Wildlife Hospital and Center, 8434 SW 48th Avenue, Palm City, FL. 34990, or to a charity of your choice.
