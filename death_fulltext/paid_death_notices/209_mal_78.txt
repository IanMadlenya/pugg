
        KAMBER--Jonathan B. On June 6, 2004, at age 61, following a long illness. Beloved husband and best friend of Karen Mender. Loving son of Thekla and the late Harold Kamber. Jon was beautiful within and without, and was a man with rare kindness, humor and exquisite taste. Memorial services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Tuesday, June 8th, 4 p.m. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to Deerfield Academy, 7 Boyden Lane, Deerfield, MA, 01342.
