
        CAVANAGH-James, Monsignor. The children and staff of Little Flower Children's Services mourn the sudden death of Monsignor James Cavanagh, a caring and devoted priest, member of the Board of Directors. He was a model to children in his respect for them and in his outstanding basketball ability. He brought light and happiness to all. May he rest in peace. Mrs. Mary Ryder, Executive Director Mr. Arthur McDermott, President of the Board Monsignor John Fagan, CEO
