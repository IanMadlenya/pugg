
        STILLMAN--Sidney, D.D.S. 85, on March 3, 2007. Cherished husband of Shirley, devoted father of Harriet and Mark, loving father-in-law of Richard and Patricia. Adored by his grandson David. Dear brother of Anna and Nathan (deceased). A kind, gentle soul, veteran of the European Theater of WWII, skilled dentist, and extraordinary sculptor and artist. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel on March 5, at 11:15am. Contributions may be made to Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center.
