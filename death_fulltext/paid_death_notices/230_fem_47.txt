
        O'NEIL--Ellen Coakley (Tish), 7. Born in Cleveland, Ohio, on March 16, 1927, passed away unexpectedly Monday, February 27, 2006. Known lovingly as Mom, Grandma, T.C. and Tish, she is survived by her ten children, twentythree grandchildren and her sister, Sister Margaret Coakley, RSCJ. Memorial donations may be made to Kenwood Convent of the Sacred Heart, 799 South Pearl Street, Albany, NY 12202.
