
        LEAKE--Eugene Walter Jr., 93, died at home in Monkton, MD, January 15, 2005. The renowned landscape painter studied painting at Yale University, was president of the Maryland Institute College of Art, Baltimore, 1961-1975, and trustee of the Guggenheim Museum, NY. He taught painting at Yale and Johns Hopkins Universities, and received the University of Maryland's first Maryland arts Lifetime Achievement Award. He is survived by daughters Nora Cameron and Nina Richardson and three grandchildren.
