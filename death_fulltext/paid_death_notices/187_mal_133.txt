
        GRANDEFELD-John Julius. Of Pelham, NY, died August 11, 2002, at the age of 93. Born on September 21, 1908 and active up until the time of his death, Mr. Grandefeld was a practicing attorney with Cusack and Stiles, NYC. Mr. Grandefeld married the love of his life, Muriel Trigge, on May 21, 1938 and she predeceased him. A graduate of Stuyvesant HS, NYU and Brooklyn Law, he was the oldest practicing alumnus of Brooklyn Law. He was a member of the New York and America Bar Associations, a Free Mason, the Wyoming Lodge, Huguenot Memorial Church, the Skytop Club and Pelham Country Club. Devoted to his family, Mr. Grandefeld is survived by his children, Merry Kormann, Joan Hurst and John Grandfeld. He is also survived by his eight loving grandchildren. Visitors may call at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave., Pelham, NY on Tuesday 7-9 & Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9. The funeral will be at Huguenot Memorial Church Thursday 10 AM.
