
        GOLDSTEIN-Albert M. (Albie). March 5, 2000. Husband of Joan Goldstein. Father of Martha and Lauren. Grandfather of Ali and Jenna. Brother of Muriel. Uncle of Beth and Neil. He was the recipient of two Purple Hearts as a marine during World War II. In recent years, he was a member of the Board of Governors at the Friars Club and was, subsequently, knighted. For his kindness and generosity at the School for Special Children he was named Man of the Year. Albie will be deeply missed by both his family and friends who knew and loved him. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Tuesday, March 7, 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM with service on Wednesday, March 8 at noon at Frank E. Campbell.
        GOLDSTEIN-Albert. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Albert Goldstein on March 5, 2000. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, March 8 at 12 noon, Madison Avenue and 81 Street. Viewing on Tuesday, March 7 between 2 & 5pm and between 7 & 9pm.
