
        DIAMOND-Chaim. Touro College mourns the passing of Mr. Chaim Diamond, a longtime member of its Board of Trustees. He was a man of vision and incandescent intellect and personality who survived the Holocaust to build a successful new life in this country. Mr. Diamond served with distinction in helping guide Touro's growth over the decades. His insight and commitment to Judaism and higher education will be missed by Touro College and his loss is mourned by his family and many friends. Dr. Bernard Lander, Pres. Dr. Mark Hasten, Chairman of the Board.
