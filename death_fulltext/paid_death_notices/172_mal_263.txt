
        HULNICK-Dr. Robert T. On May 22 of Verona, New Jersey, and Palm Beach, Florida. Beloved husband of Barbara and the late Estelle (Korn). Devoted father of Madeleine and Ken Klipper. Loving brother of George & the late Anna, Alvin, Rose, Joseph and Lillian. Cherished grandfather of Jeffrey and Ali Estelle. An officer in the U.S. Navy during World War II, he practiced dentistry on Staten Island, New York for 54 years.
