
        DOBSON-Mark Hull, age 43, suddenly in New York City on Tuesday, February 25th. He was a graduate of Eastmoor High School in Columbus, University of Paris, France and New York University. At the time of his death he was Vice President of Bear Stearns Investment Bankers in NY. Survived by his parents, Douglas and Helen Dobson of Reynoldsburg, Ohio. No calling hours, no flowers. Private service. Interment Greenlawn Cemetery. For information contact Evans Funeral Home, 4171 East Livingston Avenue, Columbus, OH 43227; 614-237-2553.
