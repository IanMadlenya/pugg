
        LoPRETE-Lawrence D. 74, of Boca Raton, FL, died Thursday, November 22, 2001. Mr. LoPrete moved to Boca Raton 13 years ago from Huntington, NY. He was preceded in death by his son Lawrence Jr. in 1985. Survived by wife Marilyn; sons Steven, Christopher and Joseph; sisters Albertina Daus, Marie White, Antoineiette Amburg; nephew James White and 11 grandchildren. Mass of Resurrection at 11 AM, Monday, November 26, at St. Joan of Arc Catholic Church. Interment at Boca Raton Mausoleum.
