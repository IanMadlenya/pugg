
        MINTER-Bobby. On December 6, Bobby Minter, loving spouse to Maggi Landau, and adoring father to Sarah and Casey, died from gastric cancer. He was 48. Caring son to Evelyn and Robert Minter and loving big brother to George, Bill and Sue Minter. Fantastic uncle to Thomas, Hannah, Ariel, Jake, Emma, Sophia and Jasper. He is forever cherished by his family and by the strong community of friends who shared his life. In lieu of flowers, donations in memory of Bobby Minter may be sent to: The Nature Conservancy of NY, 570 7th Avenue, Suite 601, New York, New York 10018.
