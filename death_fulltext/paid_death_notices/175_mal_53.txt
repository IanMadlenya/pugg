
        SEGALL-Arthur A. at the age of 96, died August 2, 2001. Husband of the late Natalie Freed. He is survived by his loving son Matthew and cherished daughter-in-law Lila of New York, a daughter Ann Thomas of Joshua Tree, CA, grandson Robert and great-grandsons Sean and Jesse of Tuscon, AZ, granddaughter Debra of Callicoon, NY, sister Helen Stuart of Forest Hills, NY and her son John Vinocur of Paris. He was a long-time name partner of Hess, Segall, Pelz, Guterman, Steiner & Barovick which merged into Loeb & Loeb. Services will be private.
        SEGALL-Arthur. From your grandson Robby, granddaughterin-law Maria and only greatgrandsons Sean and Jesse. We'll think of you eating oyster pan roast at the great Oyster Bar in the sky.
        SEGALL-Arthur A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Arthur A. Segall. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
