
        McKEEVER - Robert Emmet. Died peacefully February 5, 1997. Survived by his wife, Doris ''Suzie'' Baker; his former wife, Clara Tomkies, daughters, Sharon Surrarrer and Kimberly Kaye; son, Dow McKeever; a step-son Robert Nitzschke; grandchildren, Marc Surrarrer and Dara Kaye. He graduated from Princeton in 1949 A.B. cum Laude and had a distinguished career on Wall Street. He loved the ocean, gourmet food and a good game of Scrabble with his family. We will forever miss his optimism, great wit and splashing with him in the surf.
