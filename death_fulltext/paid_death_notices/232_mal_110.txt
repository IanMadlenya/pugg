
        FRIEDMAN--Edward E., O.D. who practiced optometry in Manahattan for 37 years, died Thursday, May 11 at the age of 60. Dr. Friedman is survived by his wife of 37 years, Victoria Friedman (nee Fox) and daughters Alison and Jillian. He was a beloved brother to Barbara LaSalle, and proud uncle to Benjamin Levinson, David LaSalle, and Gregg, Kyle, and Sara Fox. Devoted brother-in-law to John LaSalle, and Fran and Robert Fox. A memorial service will be held on Thursday May 25, at 7:00 PM at The Friends Meeting House at 15 Rutherford Place, (at East 15th Street, between 2nd and 3rd Avenues).
