
        SULLIVAN-John Ahern, of Shelburne and Greensboro, Vermont, formerly of New York City, died July 1, 2000 in Shelburne, VT. He was born December 25, 1910 in New York City, graduated from City College of New York (1930) and Columbia University Law School (1933), and was a retired partner of Cadwalader Wickersham & Taft in NYC. He is survived by his wife, June Sullivan, and by his daughters, Patricia Ann Sullivan, Nancy Ahern Sullivan and Kathleen Ann Sullivan, and by two grandsons. The funeral Mass will be at St. John's Church in Concord, N.H. at 10 A.M., July 5 with burial immediately following.
