
        SCHWARTZ--Jeanette (Jay), (nee Perlmutter), 84, passed away on March 27, 2007. She was the wife of former Senator Murray Schwartz. Her children Fred Schwartz and Joanne Lebow, her five grandchildren and three great-grandchildren survive her, as do two of her sisters, Shirley Blitzer and Doris Perlmutter. Jay had been National President of Cancer Care, Inc. and was a member of Engineers Country Club and the Fountains Country Club. She was loved and will be missed.
