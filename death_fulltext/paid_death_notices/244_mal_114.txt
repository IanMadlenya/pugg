
        SALZMAN--Jacob A., of New York City died on May 8, 2007. Beloved husband of the late Ida Salzman. Cherished father and father-in-law of Richand Susan Salzman, Anita and Myron Pulier, Robert Salzman and Lori Brooks. Grandfather of Eric, Greg and Tara, Annie and Matt, Rachel and Scott, Talia and Aramie. Great-grandfather of Jake, Will, Chloe, Georgia, Abe, Naomi, Tabitha and Lily. Jack was grateful for and appreciated every minute of his extraordinary 94 years. Devoted to the family he and Ida created, Jack was an avid cellist and amateur chamber musician. His passion for music was a source of joy and friendship. A brilliant, creative attorney, he ran for Congress (American Labor Party 1936). He practiced law for more than 50 years in downtown Brooklyn joined by his three children. His legal craftsmanship and advocacy had a deep and lasting impact on the lives of his clients. Jack fought all his life for a better world and never gave up hope that it would happen. Donations may be made in his name to: Opus 118 at the Harlem School of Music, a classical music program for public school children, 103 East 125th St., New York, NY 10035, Attention: Philip Willis. 212-831-4455
