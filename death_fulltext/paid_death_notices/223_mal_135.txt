
        HUNT--E. Ritchie. Age 77, of Brooklyn, died suddenly on July 3, 2005. He is survived by his brother, John Hunt of Houston, TX, and nieces and nephews in Massachusetts, Maine, Texas and California. A graduate of Dartmouth in 1949, predeceased by his wife, Elizabeth Everett Hunt in 1979. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM on Friday, September 16, at The First Unitarian Church, Pierrpont St. and Monroe Pl., Brooklyn Heights, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to Brooklyn Botanic Gardens, 1000 Washington Ave., Brooklyn, NY 11238.
