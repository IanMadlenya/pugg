
        MCMILLIN-George. 70, of NYC, died suddenly on Wednesday, January 5, 2000. George was a devoted neighbor, committed to the West 104th Street Block Association, a dear friend to many at the Interchurch Center where he worked for many years, and a great lover of classical music. His untimely death is a sad loss to all who shared his interests and pursuits. A memorial gathering will be held on Saturday, January 15, 11 AM at the Interchurch Chapel on Claremont Avenue between 119th and 120th Streets. Anyone wishing to assist with tributes should call Carol Goodfriend at 212-663-7095.
