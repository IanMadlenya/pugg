
        HICKOK-Richard Sanford. Of Brewster, MA. Died October 20, 2000. He is survived by his wife Janet Allsopp Hickok; three sons, Sanford, Steven and Jonathan; a daughter Wendy Peters; eight grandchildren; a brother James K. of Scottsdale, AZ and a sister Sally Bockus of Mountainside, NJ. Mr Hickok lived in Cranford, Summit and Morristown, NJ. He graduated from Summit High and the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania. He served as a Lt. J.G. in the U.S. Navy. A certified public accountant in New York and several other states, Mr. Hickok was a partner in the firm Hurdman and Cranstoun in New York City. At his retirement in 1983, he was Chairman of the U.S. Affiliate of the International firm K.M.G. Main Hurdman. After his retirement, he founded Hickok Associates, a financial consulting firm in NYC, as well as authoring technical books and articles. He was a member of professional committees and lectured extensively on corporate governance. Mr. Hickok served as a director of numerous public companies including Marsh and McLennan, Comstock Resources and Marcam. A Memorial Service will be held Tuesday, October 24 at 11:30 AM at the Brewster Baptist Church, 1832 Main St. Gifts in his memory may be made to the Brewster Ladies Library, 1842 Main St., Brewster, MA 02631 or a charity of your choice.
