
        LAWRENCE-Donald S. Jr., of Croton-on-Hudson, New York, died unexpectedly May 4th at home. He was 55. He is survived by his fiance Myriam Kessler and her son Bryan; his son Donald S. Lawrence III of Littleton, MA.; his grandsons Donald IV and Joseph Lawrence of Littleton; his daughter Jessica Lawrence of Dallas, TX; his sisters Holly Sears of Boston, MA., and Sally Reksten of Quincy, MA. Don was a Senior Warden at St. Augustine's Church. He was the president of The UPP River Coalition. Don was formerly the corporate vice president of human resources for The Equitable Life Assurance Society. A wake is to be held from 2-4 and 7-9 on Thursday at the Carter Funeral Home in Croton-on-Hudson. A funeral service will be held at 1PM, on Saturday at St. Augustine's in Croton-on-Hudson. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to St. Augustine's.
