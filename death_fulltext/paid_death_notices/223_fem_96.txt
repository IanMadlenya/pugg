
        LONDON--Anna. On August 12, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Leon London. Loving mother of Allen London, Arlene Alter and Norman London. Dearest grandmother and great-grandmother. Service Sunday 1 PM at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. & Amsterdam Ave. Contributions to the Anti-Defamation League, 823 United Nations Plaza.
        LONDON--Anna. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of Anna London, mother of Allen London, and mother-in-law of Margot London, a member of our New York Regional Board. The generosity of the entire London family has strengthened the work of the League. We extend our deepest condolences to Allen, Margot and the entire family. Barbara B. Balser National Chair Abraham H. Foxman National Director Joel J. Levy NY Regional Director Rachel Robbins NY Board Chair
        LONDON--Anna. Sutton Place Synagogue sadly records the passing of our longtime member, honorary trustee and benefactor. She was a strong, kind, dynamic woman. Heartfelt condolences to her children, grandchildren and the entire bereaved family. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Mark Wiener, President
