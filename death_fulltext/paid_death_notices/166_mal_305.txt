
        BURGHEIMER-Alan Joseph. A Rye resident, died on November 24, 2000, at his home, at age 87. Born in NYC on July 16, 1913, he is the son of the late Jacob and Gertrude Kahn Burgheimer. A veteran of the United States Navy, he served the nation during WWII. Mr. Burgheimer was the owner and executive of the Fisher Beer Co. in NYC. On June 20, 1939, he married Jeanne Schwarz at the Waldorf Astoria. He was a member of the City Athletic Club in Manhattan, as well as the Beach Point Club in Mamaroneck. In addition to his wife Jeanne, of Rye, he is survived by his daughters Margo Burgheimer of Rye, and Joanne Robinson and her husband Jerrold of Keswick, VA. He is predeceased by a sister, Sylvia Kapp. Funeral service will be held on Monday, 11:30 AM, at Zion Memorial, followed by entombment at Maimonides Cemetery in Elmont, NY. The family requests that donations be sent to the Alzheimer's Association in his memory.
