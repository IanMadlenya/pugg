
        UTEVSKY--Julian, 90, of Lawrence, New York. Died peacefully at home November 1st, 2006. Devoted and loving husband of Ruth, brother of Fred, father of Michael, Daniel and Naomi Lima, father-in-law of Tom Lima, grandfather of Stefan, Anna, Alexander and Amanda. During his life he was also an activist, union organizer, assessor, real estate appraiser, teacher, sculptor and collector of art, and creator of puns and bawdy verse as the spirit moved him. His family joins all who knew him in celebrating a man who loved life. He will be remembered with love.
