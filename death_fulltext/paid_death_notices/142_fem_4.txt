
        KELLEHER-M. Eileen. 79. Of Norwich, CT. Beloved wife of the late Dr. James H. Kelleher. Died October 29, 1998 after a brief illness. Devoted mother of Michael of Chevy Chase, MD; Kathleen Belinga of Columbia, SC; Colleen Halloran of Dover, MA; Jane Sobolov of Goffstown, NH; Sara Ann Klemmer of Sherborn, MA; Mary Courtney Kelleher of Groton, CT. Loving ''Gam'' to 13 grandchildren. Also survived by her sister Jean Duffy of Norwich, CT. Mrs. Kelleher formerly of Brooklyn and Manhattan, NY, was the daughter of the late Francis V. and Mary Courtney Duffy. She is predeceased by her brothers Hugh V. and Francis P. Duffy. She was a graduate of Manhattanville College and Fordham University School of Social Services. The family will receive friends at the Cummings-Gagne Funeral Home, 82 Cliff Street, Norwich from 24PM on Sunday, November 1. A mass of Christian Burial will be on Monday, November 2 at 10AM at St. Patrick's Cathedral, 213 Broadway, Norwich. Interment will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery in Norwich. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Association of Retarded Citizens of New London County, 1616 Route 12, P.O. Box 361, Gales Ferry, CT 06335, attn: Greg McMahon; or Easter Seals of Southeastern Connecticut, Norwich-New London Turnpike, Uncasville, CT 06382.
