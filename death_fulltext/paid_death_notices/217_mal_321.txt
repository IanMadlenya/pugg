
        MITCHELL--John D., Ed.D., 87, of Key West, FL., New York City and Bryn Athyn, PA. on February 21, 2005. President and Co-Founder of The Institute for Advanced Studies in the Theatre Arts (IASTA). Survived by his wife Miriam (Mimi), his sons John Daniel and Lorenzo, his daughter Barbarina and seven grandchildren. A man of multiple talents. He has left his mark on many and will be sorely missed.
