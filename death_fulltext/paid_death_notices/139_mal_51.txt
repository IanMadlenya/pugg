
        GOLDSMITH-Walter. We express sincerest condolences to our devoted friend and benefactor, David Goldsmith, on the loss of his beloved father, Walter. A long time supporter of ''The Hospital with a Heart'', Walter personified the values of tzedekah and chesed. May the warm memories of this special individual bring solace to David and the entire family, and may they be comforted amongs the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. American Committee for Shaare Zedek Medical Center in Jerusalem Erica Jesselson, Chair, Menno Ratzker, President
