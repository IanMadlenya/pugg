
        HIRSCHHORN--Jack 84, on July 26, of Palm Beach, FL. He was the Executive Vice President of Alexander's Department Stores for over thirty years and a recipient of the Bronze Star from the U.S. Army during active service in World War II. Survived by his beloved wife of almost sixty years, Ruth, loving children Mark and (Lynne) and Peter and (Laurie), adored grandchildren Andrea, Eric, Samantha and Jeremy. Services are Thursday, 10:00 AM at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ.
        HIRSCHHORN -- Jack. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Jack Hirschhorn. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Ruth and their family. Jack Sussman, President Joseph Lehman, Secretary
