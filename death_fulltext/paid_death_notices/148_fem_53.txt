
        MIDDLETON-Anita Arden. Died May 3 after a lengthy illness. Married to the late stage & screen actor Guy Middleton. Mrs. Middleton was a long time London resident and was a native New Yorker. She will be buried with her husband in England. Mrs. Middleton, an international actress whose credits include both American and British films, traveled with the USO and was a ''long stemmed rose'' for Broadway & Hollywood producer Billy Rose, early in her career. She is survived by deeply devoted friends in the U.S. and England, as well as cousins Vera Latham Bergman of Clearwater, FL, and Joan Latham Farewell of Atlanta, GA. She will be dearly missed.
