
        SISTI--Anthony. The Trustees, the Women's Board and the staff of The Boys' Club of New York express their deepest sympathy to the family of their dear friend. Tony passed away on Thursday, August 31st after a long illness. As a Boys' Club of New York Alumnus, former Executive Staff member and current Honorary Trustee, Tony's tireless work and dedication to our mission will serve as a permanent legacy for all of us. His lifetime of work and his leadership with the Jefferson Park Alumni Association will forever remain in the hearts of the many lives he touched. Tony was a valued friend and supporter of The Boys' Club for many, many years and we will miss him greatly. The Boys' Club of New York
