
        MILL-Charles. 81, of Madison, CT, formerly of Scarsdale, NY. Died Friday, April 10 in the hospice unit at Middlesex Hospital, Middletown, CT. Longtime business magazine publisher, he was president of the American Business Press from 1971-82. At his death, he was a director of the Thomas Publishing Co. He married Jane Baggott Maguire in Providence, RI on July 12, 1941. She survives, as does a son Jeffrey, of Mystic, CT. He was predeceased by another son, Lt. Col. Charles S. Mill Jr., USMC Ret., and by three brothers. He was an Army Air Force Veteran of World War II. A memorial service will be held at St. Margaret RC Church, Madison at noon, April 18. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that donations be made to the Weiss Hospice Unit, Middlesex Hospital, Middletown, CT 06457.
