
        ASHWELL--Thomas Buffum, died June 6, 2007 in New Haven, CT. Born May 28, 1920 and raised in New Canaan, CT, he graduated from Middlesex School and Harvard. He was a PT boat officer in the Pacific during World War II. In 1946, he joined Thomas Ashwell & Co., publishers of Export Trade magazine and The Exporters Encyclopedia. After the company was sold to American Heritage in 1963, he joined The Wall Street Transcript as advertising manager. A longtime resident of Old Greenwich, CT, he moved to North Branford, CT in 2002. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Carolyn, two sons, Tim and Andrew, a daughter Lauren, and a grandson Eamon; also by two sisters, G. Elizabeth Lotowycz and Mary W. Liebtag. A memorial service will be held this summer. Contributions may be made to the Middlesex School, 1400 Lowell Rd., Concord, MA 01742 or the Maritime Aquarium, 10 North Water St., Norwalk, CT 06854.
