
        WARNER--Charles G. K., 88, died December 30, 2006. A retired professor of French history, he is survived by his beloved wife, nee Patricia Rosalind Cutler; children Christopher, Nicholas, Joshua, Cecily Noble and Rosalind Schreiber; stepson Robert L. Fowler; and his brother William W. Warner. A memorial service will be held at St. Anne'sin-the-Fields, Lincoln, Massachusetts, at 11am Saturday, February 24.
