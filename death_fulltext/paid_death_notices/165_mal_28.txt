
        VIALE-Chester W. Died on September 28, at the age of 81. ''Chet'' is rememberd fondly by his loving wife Rita of 55 years, his devoted nephew Chris Torelli and sister-inlaw Vera Torelli, and his many other nieces and nephews. Viale was Senior Executive Administrative Partner of L.F. Rothschild & Co. until he retired in 1981. He guided and built the national and international presence of the investment banking and brokerage firm. He brought the firm to prestigious status as a managing and leading underwriter of municipal bonds. He will be missed by his peers and colleagues, his partners, associates and the staff of L.F. Rothschild & Co., all beneficiaries of his creativity, generosity and fidelity. Calling hours are 6-8 PM, on Monday, October 2, at Scobee, Ireland, Potter Funeral Home in Delray Beach, FL. Services will be held there on Tuesday, October 3, at 11 AM, with interment following. In lieu of flowers. the family suggests donations to the Bethesda Memorial Fund, Box 461, Delray Beach, FL 33447.
