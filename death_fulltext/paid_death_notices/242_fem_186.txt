
        LANDAU--Mabel R. Two weeks prior to her 100th birthday, Mabel passed away on March 23, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Harold, devoted mother of Lawrence and Sandra, loving grandmother of Scott, Brian, Todd, Emily, Caroline and Katherine and adoring great-grandmother of Isabel, Zachery, Rylee, Amanda and Matthew. A 1928 graduate of Cornell University, Mabel was an enthusiastic lover of the opera, bridge, and all things cultural. Funeral service at Frank E. Campbell, 81st and Madison, 1:30 on Monday, March 26, 2007.
