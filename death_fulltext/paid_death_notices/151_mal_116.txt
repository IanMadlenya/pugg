
        LUNDBERG-Harold Gordon. Of Summit, NJ, on August 5, 1999, husband of the late Mildred Bailey Lundberg, father of Lois Carter of New Hartford, NY and Robert B. Lundberg of Bluffton, SC. An active alumnus of Rutgers University and Chi Psi Fraternity at New Brunswick, NJ. A Memorial service will be held on Saturday, August 14, 1999 at 1:30 pm from New Providence Presbyterian Church, New Providence, NJ. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name may be made to the Alpha Rho Memorial Foundation, c/o Ray Katz, PO Box 2066, Morristown, NJ 07962-2066.
