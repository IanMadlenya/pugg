
        NOLLETTI--James L. ''Jim'', C.C.M. A lifelong Mamaroneck resident died on May 13, 2006. He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Anne, his son, James J., and his daughters, Nancy Heinbockel, Elena Skrobacz, and Linda Jackson, ten grandchildren. Former General Manager of the Davenport Club and Wykagyl Club in New Rochelle. Former General Manager of Winged Foot Golf Club. Former General Manager of the Metropolitan Club in New York City. He retired in 1996. Visitation Monday evening 79 and Tuesday 4-8 p.m. Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 E. Boston Post Rd, Mamaroneck 914 - 698 - 5986. Funeral Mass Wednesday 11 a.m. St. Vito's RC Church followed by interment at Greenwood Union Cemetery.
