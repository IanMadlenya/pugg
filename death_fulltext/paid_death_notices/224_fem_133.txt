
        PARKER--Toni Trent. Author who dedicated herself to encouraging the publication and distribution of quality books for minority children, died peacefully at home, Friday. Parker helped create a series of widely acclaimed guides to books for minority students and a not-for-profit organization, Kids Cultural Books, which conducts book festivals around the country to encourage literacy. Parker was herself the author of six children's books. She is survived by her husband, Barrington Daniels Parker Jr., a judge on the Second Circuit Court of Appeals; their daughters, Christine, Kathleen, and Jennifer; her mother, Viola Trent; and sisters, Kay Holloway and Judy Scales-Trent. Her funeral service will take place Monday, 4 pm, at First Presbyterian Church, 1101 Bedford Street, Stamford.
