
        BEARDSLEE-Jolie A. Newman Suddenly, Sunday, March 31st, age 36. Professional producer, postproduction supervisor, graphics designer and former child actress. A native New York, her immediate survivors are her husband, Liam Muldoon Beardslee, and her mother, Beatrice Coe Newman, of New York City. In lieu of flowers, donations to the organization of your preference, will be appreciated. A tribute is being planned.
