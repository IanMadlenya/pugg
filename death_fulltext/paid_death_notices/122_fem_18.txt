
        SCHERAGO-Bertien, of Port Washington, NY, on Feb. 27, 1997, after a long illness & courageous battle. Beloved wife of Irwin, loving mother of Nina Jones and Randall Scherago, mother-in-law of George Jones and sister of Emily Resnick, and many other family, friends and the community of Port Washington whom she loved dearly. Graduate of Bard College. Funeral services Sunday, March 2, 10:00AM, at The Community Synagogue, 150 Middle Neck Road, Sands Point, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Development Office of The Mount Sinai Medical Center, Box 1049, One Gustave L. Levy Place, New York, NY 10029
