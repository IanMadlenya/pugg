
        DALTON-Catherine J. Dr. Catherine J. Dalton, 83, died at home June 9, after a long illness. Born August 7, 1916 in Brooklyn, NY, Dr. Dalton graduated from NY Medical College in 1948. Following residency training, she was on the attending radiology staff at Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital for 5 years. From 1956-1965, she headed the x-ray department at the NY Infirmary. She then became director of the radiology department at what was then Doctors' Hospital (now Beth Israel North), remaining there until her retirement in 1981. Since 1963, Dr. Dalton had owned a summer home on Block Island and after her retirement, chose to spend 6 months there each year. She leaves two sisters, Mrs. Anthony Petrone of Rockville Center, NY and Mrs. Matthew Doherty of Afton, NY, as well as 20 nieces and nephews. A memorial Mass was celebrated at St. Agnes' Cathedral in Rockville Center on Monday, June 12. The family asks that remembrances be sent to The Block Island Conservancy or Habitat for Humanity.
