
        FARRELL--Rita. Died peacefully on May 27, 2004. Dear sister of Adele N. Reilly and Mercedes Alden. She was a trust and estates attorney in New York City for over 60 years. Graduated from St. John's College School of Law and the University of Oklahoma. She was very active in civic affairs: founding member of Yorkville Chamber of Commerce, past president of the Society of Medical Jurisprudence and one of the founders of the Burden Center for the Aged. Funeral Mass was held on June 1, at St. Thomas More Church, 65 East 89th St. NY, NY.
