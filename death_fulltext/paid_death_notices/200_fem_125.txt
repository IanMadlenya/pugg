
        MENDEL--Josie. After a long courageous battle with breast cancer passed away prematurely on Monday, September 15, 2003. Daughter of the late Betty and Joseph Germano, beloved wife of Andrew Mendel, devoted mother of Adam and Joshua, loving sister of Toni Dodici and Howard Germain, adored aunt of Melissa and Joanna Dodici and Caren and Ira Mendel. She taught nursery school at the Rosenthal J.C.C. in Pleasantville, New York. She leaves a host of friends and relatives whose hearts she captured and wherein she will always reside. Funeral services were held at Congregation B'nai Yisrael, 2 Banksville Rd. in Armonk on Tuesday, September 16th. Shiva calls will be received Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday evening and Sunday at her home at 6 Banksville Road, Armonk.
