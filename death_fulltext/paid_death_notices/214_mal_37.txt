
        SPURCK--Robert M. Beloved son of Dr. Robert P. Spurck and the late Catherine Spurck, passed away suddenly on October 29, 2004 at his home in NYC. He was 50 years old. He is survived by his father, Dr. Robert Spurck and his stepmother, Janet Spurck of Greenwood Village, CO; brothers, Greg and Tim; and sisters, Patrice and Kathleen. Friends may call Wednesday 3 to 4:30 PM at Greenwich Village Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St, NYC.
