
        WEISER-Kurt. Died in a car accident October 14, 1999. A senior scientist with IBM and Professor at the Technion in Haifa, Israel, Dr. Weiser fled Austria in 1938, served in U.S. Army and was educated at Harvard and Cornell. He will always be remembered with great love by his wife, three sons, six grandchildren (all in Israel), and by his brother John Benno in New Rochelle, New York. Condolences may be sent to: Ora Weiser, Hachshara Yishuv 24, Haifa, Israel. 9724-8251025.
