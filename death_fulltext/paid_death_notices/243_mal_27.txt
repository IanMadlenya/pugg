
        COTTERILL--Charles. After a long courageous battle with cancer, passed away peacefully at home on February 9th, 2007. Born in England, there he earned degrees in Geology and Engineering. In 1960 he came to New York City and earned advanced degrees from Columbia in Mathematics and NYU in finance. He worked for American Express for 22 years, then as consultant for many other firms, lastly for Morgan Stanley. In retirement, he wrote a book ''Investment Performance Mathematics'', and devoted himself to environmental causes and protection of the Palisades Cliffs. He is survived by his devoted wife Sylvia, loving son Camille. His vast knowledge, talents, kindness and helpfulness will be missed by all, friends and relatives. Memorial Services will be held on Tuesday April 17th, 2007 at 3:00pm at Trinity the Church located on Broadway and Wall Street in New YorkCity.
