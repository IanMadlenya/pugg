
        RIESER--David Nathan ''Bunny.'' Died September 18, 2003, Salt Lake City, UT. Born April 15, 1914, Long Island, NY, married Martha J. Leake August 3, 1950. His career in ladies retail spanned 50 years on the East Coast and Michigan. His generosity touched many and will continue through the Martha & David Rieser PT Scholarship Fund, 175 N. Medical Dr., 5th Floor, Moran, SLC, UT 84132-5902.
