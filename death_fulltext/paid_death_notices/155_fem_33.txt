
        WARM-Bluma (nee Nelson). Loving widow of Harry Schenker, Irving Cohen, Joseph Warm; cherished mother of Marvin Schenker and Helen Stritzler; beloved grandmother of Harold, Jan, Alan, Nina, Suzanne; loving great-grandmother of Zachary, Ben, Jonathan, Alyssa, Abigail, Elliot, Rebekah; devoted aunt to the Nelson family; lifelong friend to the Calvo-Mercier family in Paris. She died peacefully on November 30 in her ninety-fifth year. She will be remembered for her courage, strength, generosity, beauty, elegance, and zest for life.
