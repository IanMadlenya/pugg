
        MOLOFSKY-Belle. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the physicians and staff of the hospital's Hyman-Newman Institute for Neurology & Neurosurgery (INN), and the Alan and Barbara Mirken Department of Neurology in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Belle Molofsky, beloved mother of Walter Molofsky, MD, Associate Chairman of Neurology and Co-Director of the Division of Pediatric Neurology at Beth Israel Medical Center. To Dr. Molofsky, his wife Brenda Kohn, MD, and the entire Molofsky family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, MD President and CEO Alejandro Berenstein, MD Director, INN Fred J. Epstein, MD Founding Director, INN Susan B. Bressman, MD Chairman, Neurology
