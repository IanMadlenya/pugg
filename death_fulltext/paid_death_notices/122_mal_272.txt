
        STERNBERG-Lawrence J. 63, died on March 21, after a courageous battle with prostate cancer. A loving husband, father, brother, and friend. He is surived by his wife of 36 years, Harriet (Kline), his children, Jeffrey and Sara, daughter-in-law Stephanie and sister Marianne Minkoff. A dentist for 40 years, he was a president of the Midtown Dental Society and recently a clinical assistant professor of New York University College of Dentistry. Larry's family extends their deepest and heartfelt appreciation for the love and support they have received from all who knew him. Services will be held on Monday, March 24, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St.
        STERNBERG-Lawrence J. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Lawrence J. Sternberg. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        STERNBERG - Lawrence Jay, DDS. We mourn the death of our beloved friend, neighbor and former president of our Board of Directors. Our hearts go out to you, Harriet, Sarah and Jeffrey and we share your sense of deep loss. The Staff, Shareholders and Board of Directors of 114 E. 72nd St.
