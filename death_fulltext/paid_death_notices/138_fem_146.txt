
        WIENER-Ann. Daughter to Etta and Israel Saphiro. Beloved sister of Isaac (Ike) Wiener and Julia Wiener Bernstein. Aunt to Harold Bernstein and the late Raymond Bernstein. She was the aunt-in-law to Elayne Bernstein and Selma Bernstein. Great aunt to the Bernstein and Greenberg children. She will always be remembered.
