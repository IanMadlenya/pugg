
        McGINNISS--Barbara Mace, died January 29 after an extended illness. A darling woman of many talents in fashion marketing, travel writing, French cuisine and amateur theater. Everyone she met became her extended family, her hand and radiant smile freely given. Proud alumna of Mt. Holyoke, '43. Her memory will be celebrated Monday, March 7, 2005, 1:30 PM, St. Thomas Church, 5th Ave. at 53rd St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Humane Society of New York for cats in need.
