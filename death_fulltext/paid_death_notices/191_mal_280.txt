
        KRIEGEL-Fred, 94. Died on December 26 at home in Bal Harbour, FL. Beloved husband of Rena, his devoted wife of 67 years. Dear brother of Beatrice Marims and Emanuel Kriegel (deceased). Cherished father of Carol Goldberg and Dorothy Bier. Father-in-law of David Goldberg and Max Bier. Loving grandfather to Kenneth Goldberg and his wife, Jeanne, Karen Goldberg, Joshua Leibowitz, Paul Leibowitz and his wife Stacey. Great-grandfather to Nathan Leibowitz. An avid tennis player into his 90's. Keenly interested in politics and world affairs. Remembered for his love of family and many friends whose lives he touched. He will be missed.
