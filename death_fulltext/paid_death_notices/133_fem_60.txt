
        DENMARK-Pamela D., age 37, of Flushing, N.Y., died on February 6, 1998. Beloved daughter of Dr. Florence Denmark Wesner and Dr. Stanley J. Denmark. Step daughter of Robert Wesner and Anita Goodman Denmark. Survived by her sister Valerie and twin brother Richard and her fiance Robert Rackett. Services at Sinai Chapel on Horace Harding Blvd, Fresh Meadows, N.Y. on Monday, February 9, at 12 noon. Interment at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pine Lawn, N.Y. In lieu of flowers donations to the American Liver Foundation, Melville, N.Y.
