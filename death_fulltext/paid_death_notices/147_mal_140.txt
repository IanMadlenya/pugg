
        BERKOWITZ-Michael. The physicians, nurses and staff of The Cancer Center of Beth Israel and St. Luke's - Roosevelt acknowledge with deep sadness the passing of our dear friend and benefactor, Michael Berkowitz, who touched countless lives through his generous support and, most importantly, the dignity in which he shared with others his valiant battle against cancer. To his wife Lisa, his daugher Alana, his parents, the entire Berkowitz family and all his loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences.
