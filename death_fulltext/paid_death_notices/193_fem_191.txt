
        HALLENBORG - Dorothy Peck. April 19, 1907-February 17, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Charles Edward Hallenborg. Loving mother of Patricia Hopkins Klotz. Grandmother of Radford, Leslie and Taylor Klotz and greatgrandmother of two. Interment private. Contributions in her memory made be made to the Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 East 70 St., New York, New York 10021.
