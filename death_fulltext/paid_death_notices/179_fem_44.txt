
        MARTINEZ-The Hon. Cira, former Supervising Judge of the Bronx Family Court, died December 5, 2001 from complications due to pancreatic cancer. She is survived by her partner Jane Charlotte; sister, Daisy, as well as numerous nieces, nephews and extended family in Canada, the US and Cuba. Memorial service 6 PM Monday, December 10 at the Marble Collegiate Church, 5th Avenue at 29th Street. In lieu of flowers, memorials to the Youth Program at Safe Horizons, 346 Broadway, 4th Floor, New York, NY 10013.
