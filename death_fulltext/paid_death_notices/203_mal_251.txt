
        GRONICH--S. Frederick. February 18, 1916 - June 21, 2003. Six months gone, but forever in our lives. Beloved husband of Raquel M. Gronich of Israel, father of Daphne Gronich, father-inlaw of Paul Nagle and grandfather of Eli CG Nagle of Los Angeles. Hero of World War II and Israeli War of Independence. Prominent international film executive. He is remembered and missed by his extended family, friends and the many whose lives he touched around the world.
