
        RUSSACK-Joy C. On August 10, 2001, at the age of 90. She is survived by five generations of nieces and nephews. She was a dedicated teacher in the NYC public school system for over 40 years. She was a poet and a painter. Service Tuesday, August 14, 2001, at 11 AM, at ''Garlick-Riverside Memorial Chapel,'' 1439 Unionport Rd, Bronx, NY.
