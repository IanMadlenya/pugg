
        MEDINA--Barbara Florence (nee Hertz), 75, of Silver Spring, MD, died November 21 after a short illness. Born in New York City, April 5, 1931, a graduate of Evander Childs High School, Hunter College, and NYU, Barbara was a passionate innovator and pioneer. Her accomplishments include careers as mathematician, author, teacher, environmentalist, and wildflower expert. Barbara is survived by her husband of 53 years, Victor, her children, Janet, Laura and Joseph, and granddaughters, Elizabeth and Jennifer. Contributions in her memory may be made to Brookside Gardens, Wheaton, MD; contact Anna Kollin 301-962-1401
