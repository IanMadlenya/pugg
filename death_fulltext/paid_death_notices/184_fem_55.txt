
        FLIEGELMAN-Avra L., 77. Of West Palm Beach, FL, passed away on May 6, 2002, formerly of New York City. She is survived by her loving nephew Irving (Andrea) Trager. Cherished grand-nephew Jordan Trager. She was the Executive Vice-President and Publisher of The Broadcast Information Bureau and was a Member and former Treasurer for The Woman's League for Israel Midtown Chapter. Graveside services will be held on Friday, May 10, 11 A.M. at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastingon-the-Hudson, NY. For more information call I.J. Morris Funeral Directors, 1-800-624-8282.
