
        SMALL--Michael Lewis, age 64, passed away surrounded by his loving family on November 24, 2003. He is survived by his adoring wife, Lynn, and his beloved children Jonathan and David. Michael wrote musical scores for over 50 feature films and TV programs, as well as hundreds of TV and radio commercials. His many credits as a film composer include the music for Alan Pakula's Klute and The Parallax View, Bob Rafelson's The Postman Al ways Rings Twice and Black Widow, and John Schlesinger's Marathon Man. All those who had the privilege of meeting him were touched by his playful humor and incredible talent. He will be greatly missed. Services Wednesday 11:30 am at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
