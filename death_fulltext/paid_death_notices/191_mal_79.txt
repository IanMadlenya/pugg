
        JAFFE-Robert I. 86, died December 7, 2002. Beloved husband of Ruth and adoring father of Dr. Eric and Cecelia Jaffe. Cherished grandfather of Kya, Matthew and Alison, and great-grandfather to Mason. Robert loved to sail and was the former Commodore of the Hempstead Bay Sailing Club. Funeral service will be held Monday, at 12 noon, at the Ballard-Duran Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave at South Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601. Interment will be at Sharon Gardens Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
