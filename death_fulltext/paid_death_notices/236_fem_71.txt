
        STRAUS--Alice. The Association for Macular Diseases, The Macula Foundation and The LuEsther T. Mertz Retinal Research Center of Manhattan Eye, Ear and Throat Hospital mourn the death of our beloved and esteemed friend and volunteer, Alice Straus. A creative and uniquely talented woman, we will miss her abiding friendship, her wit and her wise counsel. We will cherish her memory with the greatest affection. Our deepest sympathies go to her family. Nikolai Stevenson, President, Association for Macular Diseases, Inc. Lawrence A. Yannuzzi, M.D. President, The Macula Foundation, Inc. Joan R. Daly Administrator, LuEsther T. Mertz Retinal Research Center
