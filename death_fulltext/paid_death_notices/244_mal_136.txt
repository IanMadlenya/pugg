
        GALLANT--Herbert, 84, on Friday. Beloved husband of Geraldine; cherished father of John, Brenda, Robert and Ilze; grandfather of Lawrence and Brad; born to Frank and Frances. Graduated Harvard, summa cum laude 1944. President of Gallant for over 50 years. Member Harvard Club 62 years, founding member Sewane Club. He will be loved and missed forever.
