
        FLOATE--John A., 52, Sherman Oaks, Ca. Beloved son of Barbara Jakubec, NYC; wife Jackie, dear brother of Gretchen Floate Garrett, uncle of Ian and Timothy Garrett, Van Nuys, Ca; passed away July 31, 2004 of scleroderma. He was the President of Musikuser, music licensing, a graduate of Curry College, Ma. He will be profoundly missed by his many friends and loving family.
