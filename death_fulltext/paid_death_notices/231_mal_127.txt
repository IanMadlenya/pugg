
        HANIN--Norman. Died in Boynton Beach, FL on March 6, 2006. Born in 1922 he served in the Coast Guard during WW II. Following the war he became a businessman, raised a family with his wife Blanche on Long Island and later lived in New Jersey and Florida. He is survived by his children: Genine, Scott, Melinda and Eric; his grandchildren Reese, Samantha, Brooke, Stacey, Jacob, and Leah and his wife, Esta. A memorial service will be held at Beth Moses Cemetery, New York on Sunday, April 23.
