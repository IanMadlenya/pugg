
        UNTERMAN-Jacob. Young Israel of The West Side mourns the passing of a distinguished Founding Member, Past President and Past Chairman of The Board, Mr. Jacob Unterman. We extend condolences to his devoted wife, Helen, children, Chaim, and Elana, and brother, Harry. May the Comforter of Zion console the entire family. Sheldon Fine, President Emanuel Gettinger, Rabbi
