
        FULLER-Frederic J., Jr. Died on August 26 at home at the age of 83. Beloved son of the late Frederic J. and Amanda Heidt Fuller. Devoted brother of Elizabeth H. Fuller. In accordance with his wishes a private Mass of Christian Burial was held at the Church of St. Thomas More on August 28. If anyone wishes, donations can be made in his memory to the Church of St. Thomas More, 65 East 89th Street, NY, NY 10128 or Interfaith Neighbors Inc., 210 East 86 Street, NY, NY 10028 or Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 East 70th Street, NY, NY 10021.
        FULLER-Frederic J., Jr. The Board of Directors, clinical and administrative staff of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York mourn the loss of Frederic J. Fuller, Jr., our beloved Director for 20 years and benefactor of the agency's home care and public health services. His interest in patient care improved the health and well-being of countless New Yorkers. His commitment to high quality, compassionate health care will be remembered always. We extend our profound sympathy to his sister Elizabeth. Douglas D. Broadwater Chairman of the Board Carol Raphael President and CEO
        FULLER-Frederic, Jr. The Clergy and people of St. Thomas More Parish bemoan the loss of our faithful parishioner and Parish Trustee, Fred Fuller. May he rest in peace. Rev. John A. Boehning, Pastor Madeleine Morressey, Trustee
