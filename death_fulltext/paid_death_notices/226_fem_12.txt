
        CERUSSI--Anne T. Died on November 2, 2005, after a short illness. She was 78 and lived in Rye, NY. She is survived by her sons, Michael A. Jr. of Greenwich, CT and Thomas F. of Darien, CT; daughters, Rosemary Ferrer of Greenwich, CT and Nancy A. McEnaney of Ridgefield, Ct; brothers John O'Brien, Thomas O'Brien and Cornelius O'Brien; sisters, Mary Mahon, Loretta Duffy and Kathleen Mullarkey and ten grandchildren. Her husband, Michael, and a son, Ernest, predeceased her. The family will receive friends on Friday, November 4, 2005, from 2PM to 4PM and from 6PM to 9PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Raod, Rye, NY. A Mass of Christian Burial will take place on Saturday, November 5, 2005 at 9:45 AM at the Church of the Resurrection, 910 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Westchester Leukemia and Lymphoma Society.
