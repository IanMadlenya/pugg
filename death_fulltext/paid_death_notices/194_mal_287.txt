
        WHITE-F. Kingsbury, 87. Died March 23, at home in New York City. A lover of the theater and longtime member of the Amateur Comedy Club of New York City. Preceded in death by his beloved wife Margaret, his son Steven and granddaughter Molly. Survived by his son David, daughter and son-in-law Julia and Patrick Pine, stepson and daughter-in-law Kevin and Tracy Frank & grandchildren Steven, Thayer and Meghan. Graveside service will be conducted at the Quogue Cemetery in Quogue, NY, on April 11, at 11:00 AM.
