
        SEIDELL-Marguerite (nee McCoy). Of Hempsetad, NY. On November 29, 1997. Marguerite, known as Maggie to her family and friends, was born April 8, 1926, in the Bronx. Daughter of Mr. and Mrs. James R. McCoy. Beloved wife of Herbert A. Loving mother of James R., Martin A., Herbert A., Jr., Christopher T., and Patrick M. Mother-in-law of Joanie, Cathie, Barbara, Jeanne and Lynette. Loving grandmother of 14 grandchildren. Graduate of Georgian Court College, Dame of the Sovereign Military Order of Malta. Parochial school teacher and Vice Principal of St. Aidan's School in Williston Park, NY, for almost 20 years. Gave a significant amount of time to charitable work, including volunteer work at Mercy Medical Center and Catholic Charities. Reposing at Fairchild & Sons Funeral Home, 1201 Franklin Ave., Garden City, NY. Visiting hours Tuesday, 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be at St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Garden City, NY, at 10 AM, Wednesday. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to the American Cancer Society.
