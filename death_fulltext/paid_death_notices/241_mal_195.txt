
        CLOSE--F. Alexander, who ran his own import-export firm in New York for 30 years before retiring to Connecticut and New Mexico, died at age 90 in San Francisco. Married for 50 years to Anne Whitmarsh, Alex is survived by two daughters, Lorraine Sniderman of Warwick and Alexandra Close of San Francisco, five grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. A service will be held on February 24 at 11 am at St. John's Church in New Milford, CT. For more information, contact sclose@ newamericamedia.org
