
        DAVIS-Harold. February 28, 2000. Husband of Fanye (nee Karr). Formerly of Stamford, CT. Father of Hedy Kaye Zankel, Roger J. (Jane) Davis and Dr. Lloyd (Sally) Davis. Brother of Helen Davis. Also survived by 7 grandchildren and 14 great-grandchildren. Relatives and friends are invited to services Thursday 1:00 PM at the Mausoleum of Locustwood Memorial Park, Cherry Hill, NJ. The family will be at the home of Roger and Jane Davis Thursday and Friday only, and respectfully request contributions in his memory be made to The American Heart Association. For further information contact Platt Memorial Chapels, Inc., 856-428-9442.
        DAVIS-Harold. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Harold Davis. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Teddy, and their family. Morton West, President Daniel Amster, Secretary
