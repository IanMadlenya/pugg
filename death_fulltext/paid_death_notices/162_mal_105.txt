
        GOLDBERG-Steven L., M.D. The Department of Pathology, Mount Sinai School of Medicine is stunned and profoundly saddened by the unexpected death of Dr. Steven L. Goldberg. He was Adjunct Assistant Professor of Pathology and one of our most loyal alumni. He was a beloved teacher and excellent diagnostician. He was a devoted father, and wonderful husband. His energy, his everpresent smile, and his optimism will be truly missed by all in the department. #
