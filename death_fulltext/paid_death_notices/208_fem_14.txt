
        GORE--Selma. Of Woodmere and NYC. On April 30, 2004. Noted publicist. Was a loving wife to Larry. Unique mother of Billy (Adele), Tracy, Benjy and David (Mara). All her friends and relatives will miss her wit and warmth. We love you, Mommy. Memorial Wednesday, 11:45, at The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam, NYC. Graveside service Mt.
