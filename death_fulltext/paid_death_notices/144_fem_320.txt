
        AUGUST-Anne Atkatz. Of West Palm Beach, Florida, formerly of NYC, passed away on January 28, 1999. She is survived by her loving children: Susan (Frederic) Rubinstein and Neil (Joan) August. She is also survived by her sister Leah Brecher. She will be lovingly remembered by her grandchildren Nicolas, Edythe, Karen, and Deborah, and her great-grandchildren Michelle, Allison, Jeremy, and Lindsay. Anne was a teacher at P.S. 48 in Bronx, NY for many years. Services were held in Florida. Memorial donations should be made to The Stanley Isaacs Neighborhood Center for The Jack & Anne August Education Fund, 415 East 93rd Street, New York, NY 10128 (212) 360-7620. For information Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels, West Palm Beach, Florida 1-800-94-SHALOM.
