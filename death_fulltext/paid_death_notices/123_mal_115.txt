        SLOANE-Howard G. It is with deep regret and profound sorrow that the Officers, Board and Members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City mark the passing of Howard Sloane, devoted husband of Virginia Sloane, President of The Heckscher Foundation for Children. As a Trustee of the Foundation, Howard continued a record of support beginning in 1969. He was a generous friend to children with cerebral palsy and their families. We extend our condolences to Virginia and to the entire family. Leo Hausman, President Jack Hausman, Founder UCP of NYC, Inc.
