
        SCHIFF-Samuel S. Died on February 19, 2000 after a long illness. Widower of Isabelle Pendleton Schiff, with whom he shared a wonderful life for 57 years, who died in October 1998. Beloved brother of Ester Schiff Rom, brother-in-law of Philip Rom and uncle to Alan Jay Rom, Louis Rom and their families. Veteran of the Abraham Lincoln Brigade; contribution to Abraham Lincoln Brigade Archives honors his memory, 799 Broadway, Suite 227, New York, New York 10003.
