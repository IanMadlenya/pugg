
        McCARTHY--William F. The Board of Directors, staff, clients and volunteers of Gay Men's Health Crisis mourn the death and celebrate the life of our esteemed former Chair William McCarthy. We remember Bill's commitment to people living with and affected by HIV/AIDS, his tenacity and strength in the pursuit of social justice, and his dedication and generosity to ensure the well-being of GMHC and other organizations. OUr hearts are with Bill's life partner Jonathan Burleson.
