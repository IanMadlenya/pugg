
        BARKIN-Jesse H. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Jesse H. Barkin, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. With his wife, Ruth, Jesse expressed an admirable commitment to Jewish and other humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathy to Ruth, and to the entire Barkin family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & CEO
        BARKIN-Jesse. The Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member and extends sincere sympathy to his family and friends. David S. Mack, President Michael Stein, Secretary
