
        REIBEL--Jane Doniger, died peacefully at home November 4. Survived by her husband of 55 years, Robert; brother, William Doniger; children, Jaime & Clara Reibel, Sylvia & Peter Tousley, Charles & Nancy Reibel; six grandchildren, two greatgrandchildren. Jane had careers as biometrician, librarian, educational filmstrip producer, but her passion was acting. Member of SAG, AFTRA, she performed on stage and screen, received an OOBR award for her role as ''Baba in Stars.'' Celebration of her life to be held 2 PM Sunday, December 5, Croton Library, Croton-on-Hudson.
