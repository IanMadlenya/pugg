
        SILBERMAN--J. Donald, 73. Geologist of Scarsdale, NY and Dallas, TX, died August 28, 2003, attended by his loving family in Falmouth, MA.
        SILBERMAN--Sidney J. Died peacefully at home on August 29. Devoted husband of the late Marjorie. Loving companion of Helene Fagin. Beloved father of Judy (her husband Uri Aqua). Adoring grandfather and role model of Daniel, Talia and Adam Aqua. Dear brother of Charles. A distinguished attorney, he practiced with the Kaye Scholer LLP since 1952. Services at Riverside Chapel, Mount Vernon, on Sunday, August 31, at 1:30 PM. Donations may be made to the Sidney J. Silberman Scholarship Fund at Columbia University, The Columbia University Hillel Society, or to the UJA Federation.
        SILBERMAN -- Sidney. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our Past President, Sidney Silberman, and extend sincere condolencees to his family. Alex C. Solowey, President
