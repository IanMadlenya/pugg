
        WIDMANN-Marcus, M.D., on October 10, 1999, at the age of 94, after a short illness. Beloved husband of the late Dina Sander Widmann. Cherished father of Roger and Judith Widmann and Harriet and Martin Spitz. Adored grandfather of Daniel and Kenneth Widmann, and Emily McBurney, and Lisa and Jenny Spitz. Loving brother of Fina Eytan and the late Gershon Widmann. He was devoted to his family and his patients throughout his life and will be remembered for the unfailing generosity of his spirit. Service Thursday, 10 AM, at ''Riverside Memorial Chapel'',21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, New York.
