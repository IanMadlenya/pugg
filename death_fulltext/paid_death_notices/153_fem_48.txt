
        HOLTHUSEN-Lenore. At age 86, beloved wife of the late Henry F. Holthusen, loving sister of Shirlee Stout, of Indiana, Maxine Smith, of South Dakota, Jean Penn, of New York, Robert Sutter, of Ohio, and the late Ruth Richards, of Kentucky. Adored aunt of twelve nieces and nephews. A member of the Shelter Island Yacht Club where she had a summer residence. A graduate of Columbia University. She was an avid bridge player which brought her much pleasure. A Memorial Service will be held at Saint Thomas Episcopal Church, 1 West 53rd Street, New York City, on Friday, October 8, 1999, at 2 P.M.
