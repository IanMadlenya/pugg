
        JACOBS-Robert H., 82, on December 12, 2002. Husband of Grace (nee Madan), his adored deceased wife of 57 and a half wonderful years. They both dearly loved their three children: Nancy, Bill and Ken and their respective spouses George Martin, Dawn Jacobs and Dian Jacobs. They doted on their eight marvelous grandchildren: Peter, Jeffrey, Andrew and Jennifer Martin, Daniel, Jill and Adam Jacobs and Matthew Jacobs. They especially loved Bob's brother Dick and his wife Rae. Services for Robert will be private with entombment at The Garden of Memories Mausoleum, Washington Township, NJ in the same crypt next to his beloved ''Lovey''.
