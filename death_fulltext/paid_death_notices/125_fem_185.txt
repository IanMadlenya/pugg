
        MINTZ-Elizabeth, Ph.D. Died after after a long illness on June 23rd, at the age of 83. A psychotherapist in private practice in New York City, she was a leader in the human potential movement. She authored numerous articles in professional publications and two books. She was the widow of Alexander Mintz, Ph.D., Professor of Psychology at City College of The CUNY and is survived by her son Harvey Mintz of Hastings on Hudson, NY and her daughter Ann Mintz Wagner of Harrisburg, PA. She will be mourned by her family and her many friends. Memorial service Thurs., July 16, 7PM NPAP Meeting Rm 150 West 13th st. NYC
