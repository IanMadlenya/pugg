
        O'ROURKE-Reverend Thomas J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the Pastor of St. Mary Star of the Sea Church, 467 Court Street, Brooklyn, on June 11, 1998. Reposing in Scotto Funeral Home, 106 1st Place, Brooklyn, Monday, June 15, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9:30 P.M. and Tuesday, June 16, from 2 to 5 P.M. Mass of Transferral Tuesday, June 16, at 7:30 P.M. in St. Mary Star of the Sea Church. Funeral Mass on Wednesday, June 17, at 10:30 A.M., in St. Mary Star of the Sea Church. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
