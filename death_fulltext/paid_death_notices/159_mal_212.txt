
        SATENSTEIN - Marvin. Passed away April 20, 2000 after his courageous battle with cancer. Loving husband & best friend of Edna for forty-six years. Cherished father of Lisa, David and Paul. Devoted ''Papa'' to Christina, Ryann, Taren, Cody, Kyle and Jaden. He was a self-employed paper broker in NYC. He served as a fighter pilot in the U.S. Air Force in WWII and Korea. Recipient of the Distinguished Flying Cross and other high honors, and retired as a Major in 1980. His other achievements include being an Eagle Scout, on the Board of Directors of Camp Poyntelle & Hexter and United Jewish Federation. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Saturday 6-9PM with service Sunday 10AM. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to any cancer-related cause.
