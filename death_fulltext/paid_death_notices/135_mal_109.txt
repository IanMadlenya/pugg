
        JOHNSON-J. Craig. Of Saddle River, N.J. Passed away Friday, April 10,in his home. He was President and co-founder of Johnson & Hayward, Inc. of Clifton, N.J. An early pioneer in international direct mail, he was well respected in the industry. He took a personal interest in each of his employees, and had a vitality and enthusiasm that he brought to every endeavour. He will be much missed and always remembered by his wife Onriet and daughters ClaudeLee and DeBorah, and Mama Hela. Graveside service 11 AM, Sunday, April 12, Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, New Jersey.
