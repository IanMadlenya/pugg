
        WEINBERG-Sylvia N. Of Forest Hills, NY, on January 16, 2002, age 90. Beloved wife of the late Irving, loving mother and mother-in-law of Emily and Henry Goldberger, and Eric Weinberg. Adored grandmother of Tanya Weinberg, and Ethan and Jesse Goldberger, and cherished sister of Ethel Schaefer. Strong in spirit and with great love for her family, she lived a full and independent life until this past October. She will be greatly missed by her family and friends. Services were held on Sunday, January 20, 12 noon, at Parkside Chapels, Queens Blvd at 66th Ave., Forest Hills. For additional information, please call 908-272-3710 or 215790-0129.
