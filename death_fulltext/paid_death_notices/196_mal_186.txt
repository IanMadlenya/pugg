
        ACOSTA-RUA--Dr. Gaston J., passed away peacefully the morning of Monday, May 19, 2003. Born August 26, 1937, in Cardenas, Cuba, Dr. AcostaRua moved to Jacksonville, Florida in 1972 to practice medicine and raise his family. He was a well respected neurosurgeon who was involved in many humanitarian endeavors. As a fateful and active member of the Catholic Dioceses of St. Augustine, FL and a Knight of the Order of Malta, he gave much of his time and effort to those in need both in the local community and around the world. He is survived by his loving wife of 39 years, Maria Victoria, sons Gaston, Fernando and Antonio, five grandchildren, Fernando Jr, Daria Victoria, Mills Hunter, Gaston Alexander and Eleanor Somerset, brother Gustavo Acosta and many loving friends. Funeral services will be held at St. Matthew's Catholic Church, 1773 Blanding Blvd, on Friday, May 23, 2003, at 11:00am, followed by burial at Evergreen Cemetery, 4535 North Main St. The family invites friends to visit the Hardage-Giddons Funeral Home, 729 South Edgewood Ave, Jacksonville, FL, from 6-9pm, Thursday, May 22. Vigil prayer services will be held 7pm Thursday at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the AcostaRua Family Foundation Inc, 2323 Oak Street, Jacksonville, FL 32204, a charitable organization helping the needy both in the U.S. and abroad. >>AD#
