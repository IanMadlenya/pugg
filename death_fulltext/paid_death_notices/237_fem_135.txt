
        TARUSKIN--Beatrice F. 94, on October 19, 2006. Widow of Benjamin, mother of Richard (Cathy), Miriam Lawrence (Jack) and Raymond (Mindy), grandmother of Rachel Friedman, Jason (Glaucia), Beth, Paul and Tessa, and beloved friend to many. She had a rare capacity to love and inspire love, and we will miss her terribly. Funeral Sunday, October 22, 11:30am, Parkside 2576 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn.
