
        FINKELSTEIN-Louis. Artist, educator and critic, died June 20. Beloved husband of Jane Culp. Beloved father of Henry and Martha Campbell. Beloved grandfather of Rachel Stires. We are grateful for the support of his many friends during his recent illness. A traveling exhibition of his paintings is currently being shown at various colleges including locally Yale University Summer School of Art this summer, Yale University School of Art next spring and the New York Studio School in the fall of 2001. A memorial service will be held next fall. In place of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Cabrini Hospice.
