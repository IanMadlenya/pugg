
        CARTER-Lucette M.., 75. Of Poughquag, NY. Mrs. Carter was born in 1923 in New York City to Alfred and Marie Salles Monnier. She was a graduate of Cathedral High School and Hunter College, NYC. In 1953 at the Church of Notre Dame she married William H. Carter, Jr. who survives. Other survivors include a brother JeanLouis Monnier, sisters Bernadette T. Monnier and Elisabeth M. Vandelli, all of Northport, NY. Mrs. Carter is also survived by her children William, Joan, Raymond, Marie, Annina and Renee Carter, and Gabrielle Carter Katz; and eight grandchildren. For info: Robert H. Auchmoody Funeral Homes, Hopewell Junction, NY.
