
        BROCK-James P. On March 20, 2001, age 84. Retired Director, Adriance Public Library, Poughkeepsie, NY. Graduate of Regis High School and Columbia University. Lovingly remembered by family and friends. Survived by his wife Margaret, of Hyde Park, NY, son Timothy and his wife Leora, of Scarsdale, NY, daughter Monica of Hasbrouck Heights, NJ, son Gregory and his wife Mary, of Cos Cob, CT, daughter Julia Belva and her husband Robert, of Hillsdale, NJ, and eight grandchildren.
