
        STEVENSON-Ward. Suddenly, on March 3, 1999. Beloved husband of Margot McNicol Stevenson of Bronxville, NY; dear father of Peter Stevenson of New York City, Scott Stevenson of North Carolina, and Laurie Willix of Chicago. Senior vice president and general counsel for the Church Pension Group and a former member of the law firm of Rogers and Wells. Memorial visiting at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., Saturday, March 6, 2-5 PM. Memorial service at St. Bartholomew's Church, 109 E. 50 St., Monday, March 8, at 10:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Fund for Animals, Suite 705, 200 W. 57 St, 10019.
