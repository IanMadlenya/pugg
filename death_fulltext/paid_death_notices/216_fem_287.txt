
        BERWICK--Virginia. Virginia L. Berwick, 89, formerly of Mount Kisco, New York, died Wednesday, January 26, 2005, at home in Rhode Island. She was the wife of the late Adrian J. Berwick and the daughter of the late U. S. Senator and federal Judge Edward L. Leahy and Fern Dixon Leahy. A graduate of Lasell College and the New England Conservatory of Music, she was an accomplished pianist. Survivors include her sister, Ann Rulon-Miller of Naples, Florida, and Bristol, Rhode Island, five children, Annie Bell of Washington, D. C., Brian Berwick of Austin, Texas, Julia Berwick of New York, New York, Elizabeth Berwick of Bronxville, New York, and Katherine Berwick of Austin, Texas, and five grandchildren.
