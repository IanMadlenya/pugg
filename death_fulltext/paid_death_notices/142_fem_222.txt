
        GREENSTEIN-Dorothy Milan, after a long illness, passed away at her home on Thanksgiving morning. Beloved mother of Betsy, grandmother of Matthew, wife of Morris, daughter of Minnie, mother-in-law of Robert, aunt of Florrie, Brett and Gregg. Her courage and humor were an inspiration. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave., 10:45 AM, Sunday, November 29, 1998.
