
        KLEIN-Carl K. Age 93. Formerly of Englewood, NJ, died July 16, 1999 in Miami Beach, FL. Husband of the late Sylvia, he is survived by his sons Richard and Arthur and their wives Joan and Roberta. Esteemed grandfather of Jonathan, David, Elizabeth, Catherine and Pamela. Brother of Meyer. Graveside services Tuesday, July 20, 1PM at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Shiva to be followed at the home of Arthur Klein.
