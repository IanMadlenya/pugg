
        GREENE-Ruth. Died on Sunday, November 23, 1997. Of Bayside, Queens, and Pembroke Pines, FL. A courageous woman who battled cancer with dignity and humornever losing touch with the friends and family who were such an important part of her life. A remarkable wife, mother, grandmother, sister, aunt, great aunt and an incredible friend to so many. An indomitable spirit who took on all of life's challenges with a commitment and enthusiasm that inspired and motivated all those around her. The very first to help everyone and the very last to give up. She will be missed by her family and friends who will still find her part of their lives every day. A New Yorker by birth and attitude, a graduate of Hunter College and Evander Childs High School.
