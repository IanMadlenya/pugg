
        LOZOWICK-Adele. On January 9. Wife of the late artist Louis. Owner of a frame shop in S. Orange, N.J. Dedicated herself to promoting her husband's work throughout her life. Lifetime member of the Smithson Society, supporter of many museums. Survived by son Lee, grandchildren Lori, Matt and Amy and great-grandchild Illyia & brother and sister-in-law Saul and Chrisse Turner. Memorial service Saturday, January 29, 11 AM, at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 West 72 Street, NYC. Donations may be made to the Whitney Museum of Art, NYC.
