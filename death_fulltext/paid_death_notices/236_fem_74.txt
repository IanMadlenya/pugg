
        GREENOUGH--Peter B. The Educational Broadcasting Corporation family joins Beverly Sills in mourning the loss of Peter Greenough, her dear husband for nearly half a century. Through Beverly's deep involvement with New York's public television stations over the years, she has come to be a truly special part of our organization and our lives. We share in her grief and loss. The Trustees and staff of Educational Broadcasting Corporation, licensee of Thirteen/WNET and WLIW21 New York
