
        SAIDENBERG-Daniel. The members of the Art Dealers Association of America mourn the passing of their colleague who, with his wife Eleanore, have been the principals of the Saidenberg Gallery since 1950. A man of many talents, he was a great musician and an important dealer in great works of art. More than that, he was a colleague devoted to his profession and to his family. Our deep sympathy to Eleanore, and to his sons, Lawrence and Robert. James N. Goodman, President Gilbert S. Edelson, Admin VP
