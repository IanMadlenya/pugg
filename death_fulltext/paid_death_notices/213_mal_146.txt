
        FENTON--Robert. Age 87. Died peacefully at home on October 13, 2004. Beloved husband of Janet (Munk). Loving father of Joan and Paula. Father-in-law of Albie Tabackman. Devoted grandfather of Max. Brother of Frances Garfield. Mr. Fenton graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 1937 where he played basketball and tennis. Mr. Fenton played the amateur tennis circuit for several years after graduation. Mr. Fenton was a children's book publishing executive for over 30 years. He was Vice President of Platt & Munk, publishers of ''The Little Engine That Could'' and co-owner of Lion Press. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St., Thursday, October 14th 7-9PM with a service Friday, October 15th 11:30AM atFrank E. Campbell.
