
        HAYES-Gertrude (nee Ratner). 89, died April 9, 2002. Deeply loved, she will be missed by her daughter Josephine Hayes Dean, her grandchildren Noelle Dean and Judson Dean, and those who knew her. Gertrude will be remembered for her creative spirit, flirtatious charm, generosity, strength and optimism. A grand teller of stories, she adored converstion with friends and strangers, and loved the simple joys of life.
