
        BARRY-James A, age 65. Retired Executive Director and 30+ year employee of AT&T, Nynex and New England Telephone Companies, died January 5, 2000. He was an acknowledged expert in the Customer Services Area as well as a leader in the Information Services Arena. Born in Boston, Mass, October 28, 1934. He was the son of James and Irene Barry. He was a 1956 graduate of Boston College where he was president of his class. He served in the United States Navy as a Lieutenant and was the Commanding Officer of the USS Rockville. He earned his MBA in 1963. He is survived by his sister, six children, seven grandchildren, and longtime friend Barbara DeLalla. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue (at 81st Street) on Friday, January 7, 2-5 & 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass on Saturday, January 8, 10:30AM at Epiphany RC Church, 22nd Street & 2nd Avenue, NYC.
