
        BRANDT-Grace Borgenicht. The members of the Art Dealers Association of America mourn the passing of their colleague, a founding member of ADAA. A great dealer, a great person, warm and generous, one of a kind, her memory is cherished by us all. Our sympathy and condolences to her husband, Warren; to her children, Jan, Berta, Lois and Isabella; to her sister, her grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Richard Gray, President Gilbert S. Edelson, Admin. V.P.
