
        SANDERS-Samuel. 89. Of Hallandale, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY, passed away Friday, 10/23/98. An attorney at law in New York for many years and Army veteran of WW II. Beloved husband of Adele. Devoted father of Andra Sanders and Linda Murray (Stephen). Cherished grandfather of Gordon and Cara Murray. His warmth, humor and love for his wife, children and grandchildren will be sorely missed. Services graveside Monday, 10/26/98, 2 PM at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Lung Association. RiversideGordon Memorial Chapel, Hollywood, FL. 800-759-1151.
