
        O'NEIL--James, 46. The Board of Directors, staff, participants and members of AHRC NYC convey their condolences to the family of James, known as Jimmy O'Neil, on the occasion of his sudden passing. Our deepest sympathies to his mother, Genevieve O'Neil, President of AHRC and his sisters Margaret O'Neil and Kathleen Kogan, and the Kogan family. Our thoughts and prayers are with you.
