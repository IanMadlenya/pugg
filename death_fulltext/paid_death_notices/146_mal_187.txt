
        ROOT-Stanley Roy. Retired attorney, passed away at home on March 6 at age 67 after a long illness. He was an outstanding human being, beloved and respected by all who knew him. A graduate of Stuyvesant High School, Syracuse University and Columbia Law School, where he was a member of the Alumni Board of Directors. He is survived by his wife Myrna, three children Bradford, Gregory and Kimberly, and four grandchildren. Funeral services 10:30, Tuesday, March 9, at Beth El Synagogue, Northfield Rd., corner North Ave., New Rochelle. Interment Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Donations may be sent to Multiple Myeloma Foundation.
