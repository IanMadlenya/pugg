
        FIELD-Barbara Bruce.Of Tuxedo Park, N.Y. on January 14, 1998. Born March 4, 1936 in New York City. Daughter of Barbara and Edward Weld. A great lady and cherished parent who will be missed dearly by those whose lives she touched. Brucie is survived by five sons, four daughters-in-law and ten grandchildren: Bill and Sarah Clark parents of Billy, Amanda, Nico and Perrin; Mike and Pam Clark parents of Avery and Caitlin; Gus and Lisa Field parents of Carley and Maddyn; Jake and Alice Clark parents of Caro and Jack; and Tee. Memorial service to be held at St. Mary's Church, Tuxedo Park, on Monday, January 19th at 11:00am. Spring flowers may be sent to St. Mary's Church.
