
        RYAN-Edward. Age 42. Missing at the site of the World Trade Center tragedy, September 11, 2001. A memorial Mass will be celebrated at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church White Plains Post Road, Scarsdale, NY on Saturday, October 6, 2001 at 10:45AM. He is survived by his wife Diane, children Edward, Megan, Joseph and Kelly along with the Ryan and Kettenacker families. He was First Vice President of Carr Futures. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made C/O Diane Ryan for the benefit of Edward, Megan, Joseph and Kelly Ryan or the Edward Ryan Memorial Fund C/O Wendy Phelps, both at 1199 Ulster Ave, Kingston, New York 12401.
