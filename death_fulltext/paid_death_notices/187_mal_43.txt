        ROSEN-Norman J. The firm of Farber, Rosen and Kaufman, PC mourn the loss of their dear partner. He was a brilliant attorney, a good friend and a wonderful human being.
        ROSEN-Norman J. The Rego Park-Forest Hills Lions Club sadly notes the passing of its member, Norman J. Rosen. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Joseph Sparano, President
