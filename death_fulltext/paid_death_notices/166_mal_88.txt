
        O'BRIAN-Jack. A long-time newspaper columnist, and radio talk show host in New York, died November 5, 2000 in Manhattan. He was 86 years old. In his nearly 60 years in journalism, Mr. O'Brian covered theatre, movies, radio, popular music and television at The Associated Press, The New York Journal American, and later on, WOR-AM. His humor and spirit and his love of music and the theatre will be missed by daughters, Bridget and Kate, and his beloved grandchildren, Emma and Lilly Haberl, and Jake and Sam Brimberg. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st Street, on Wednesday from 6 to 9 P.M. Funeral Mass will be held at Saint Agnes Church, 143 E. 43rd St. on Thursday at 10 A.M.
