
        ILARDI--Frances M. (nee Salmeri), of Bay Shore, LI, formerly of Lake Success, 91, passed on October 9, 2003 following a brief illness. Frances was predeceased by her husband of 60 years, Onofrio Ilardi, MD. She is survived by her sons, Vincent C. of Edgewater, NJ and Carl F. of Bay Shore, LI, and by her granddaughter, Kathryn Ilardi of Edgewater, NJ. She was a 1932 graduate of the Columbia School of Pharmacy. Reposing at the Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Causeway). Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial Monday, 10:00 AM, at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, LI. Interment to follow at Calverton National Cemetery, Calverton, LI. Visiting Sunday, 2-7:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made on behalf of Frances to the Lighthouse International, 111 East 59th Street, New York, NY 10022.
