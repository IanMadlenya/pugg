
        KRAUS-Hanni, 83. Of New York City. On January 14, 2003 after a brief illness. Loving wife of the late Hans P. Kraus. Beloved mother of Mary Ann Folter, Barbara Gstalder, Eveline Rauber, Susan Nakamura and Hans P. Kraus, Jr. She is also survived by her sister Dorit Cohn, twelve grandchildren and two great-grancchildren. She was an active member of the International Association of Bibliophiles. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Friday, January 17 at 1:30PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Kraus Fund at the Beinecke Rare Book Library at Yale University, New Haven, CT.
