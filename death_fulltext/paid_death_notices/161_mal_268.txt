
        LIEBLER-William A. The Department of Orthopedic Surgery at Lenox Hill Hospital mourns the passing of William A. Liebler, M.D., an esteemed member of the staff for over 40 years. Dr. Liebler was admired and respected by his colleagues, students and patients leaving behind a legacy of many achievements. He truly exemplified the highest qualities of the medical profession and we will miss him greatly. Chitranjan S. Ranawat, M.D., Director Patrick F. O'Leary, M.D., Associate Director Elliott B. Hershman, M.D., Executive Associate Director
