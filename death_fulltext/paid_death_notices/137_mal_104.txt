
        SAMUELS-Milton. On behalf of the faculty and staff of New York University School of Medicine we extend heartfelt condolences to our esteemed colleague Herbert H. Samuels, M.D., Professor of Medicine and Pharmacology on the loss of his beloved father Milton Samuels. Born in New York City in 1909, Mr. Samuels owned an electronic repair shop and lived most of his life in New Rochelle. We also extend sympathy to Dr. Samuels' mother Bertha, to his brother Alan, a Gastroenterologist and a 1972 graduate of the NYU School of Medicine, and to Milton Samuels' six grandchildren. Noel L. Cohen, M.D., Interim Provost and Interim Dean, NYU Medical Center and NYU School of Medicine Saul J. Farber, M.D., MACP Chairman, Dept of Medicine NYU School of Medicine
