
        MARKS--Louis. 82. Devoted husband of the late Pearl. Loving father of Ellen and the late Arthur Lacher and Rhonda and Michael Gribetz, Zadie to Rebecca and Jonas, Jason and Rachel, Eric and Carin, Seth and Orit and Jonathan, great-Zadie to Nina, Arthur, Ryan, Tali and Alexa, brother to Meyer, Carl and Betty. Founding member of Congregation Ohav Sholom, Merrick, NY. Former Yankee pitcher.
        MARKS--Louis. With deep sorrow we mourn the passing of a founder of our synagogue, Louis Marks. His dreams and leadership gave birth to our Orthodox Synagogue 42 years ago. A great and noble person, his absence is deeply felt. Condolences to his family. Congregation Ohav Sholom, Merrick Rabbi Jeremiah Wohlberg Robert Stein, President
