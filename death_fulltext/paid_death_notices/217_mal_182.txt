
        WITTENBERG--Lester H., of Chappaqua, NY died February 9, 2005. He was born December 11, 1929 and married Marian E. Harrington, August 21, 1954. He is survived by his wife, son Thomas, daughters Marian, Catherine and Caroline, their spouses and seven grandchildren. Friends may call Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 at Beecher Funeral Home, 418 Bedford Road, Pleasantville, NY. Funeral Mass, Monday 10AM, Sts. John & Mary Church, Chappaqua. Interment Fair Ridge Cemetery.
