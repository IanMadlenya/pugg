
        CONVERSE-Jean McElroy, April 12, 1997. Beloved wife of Chandler Bullock Converse. Loving mother of Chandler and Floyd. Devoted grandmother of Chandler, ''Mac,'' and Nicholas. She graduated from The Spence School and attended Smith College. As a teenager Mrs. Converse was a ranked tennis player in the state of California and an accomplished pianist. Mrs. Converse resided in Bronxville, N.Y. for 25 years. She was also a docent at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. Donations may be sent to The Cancer Institute, Good Samaritan Medical Center, 1309 North Flagler, West Palm Beach, Florida 33401.
