
        JULIBER-I. Gerard. The clergy, officers and members of Temple Sinai of Roslyn record with profound sorrow the passing of I. Gerard Juliber. We grieve together with his beloved wife Roslyn and his children Ilene, Stanley, Lois and Jon, his grandchildren Scott, Melissa, Larry, Lisa and Missy, and great grandchildren. May his memory be a blessing. Rabbi Michael A. White Rabbi Norman Kahan, Emeritus Michael J. Paul, President
