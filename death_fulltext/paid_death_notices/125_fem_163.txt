
        RITCHIN-Estelle. East Midwood Jewish Center mourns the passing of our former trustee and wife of our former vice president, Dr. Hy Ritchin. Estelle was a brilliant and talented woman who loved education and found her greatest pleasure in meeting the needs of others. She will be sorely missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her entire family. Dr. Alvin Kass, Rabbi Marsha Shlefstein, President
        RITCHIN-Estelle. The Board of Directors of Hebrew Technical Institute notes with saddness the death of Estelle Ritchin, beloved wife of our esteemed colleague Hyman Ritchin.
