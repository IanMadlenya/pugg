
        CANDEE-Seymour R. ''Sy''. A man of great courage and wisdom. His word was his bond, as was his reputation. He was a kind and loving husband to Peg, sons Glenn and Robert and Eileen, and grandsons Jason, Brian and Kevin, and his brother Morton. He graduated from St. John's University and was Captain of the soccer team, and All American Player of the Year. He became a successful realtor and had a wonderful and happy life. Services will be on Tuesday, September 5, at 1 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapels, 21 West Broad St, Mount Vernon, New York. Interment at Westchester Hills Cemetery. Donations to the Frank and Edith Dickstein Cancer Pavilion, White Plains Hospital, White Plains, NY. Family will be receiving friends Tuesday and Wednesday only at the Candee residence in Purchase, NY.
