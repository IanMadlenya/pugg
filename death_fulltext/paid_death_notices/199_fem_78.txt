
        STIGUM--Marcia. August 7, 2003. Beloved mother of Tove and Erik and loving wife of the late Allan Fox. Author of the critically acclaimed best seller ''The Money Market'' and acknowledged authority on the domestic money market and the Eurocurrency markets. A contribution in her honor can be made to the Pine Ridge School, 9505 Williston Rd, Williston, VT. 05495.
