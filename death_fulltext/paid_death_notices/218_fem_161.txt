
        SAVAGE--Dorothy M., 98, of Stamford, CT, formerly of Pelham Manor, NY and Eastham, MA, died Wednesday, March 16, 2005. Mother of Ann Martin (Roland) DiLeone, Rowayton, CT; grandmother of Linda (George) McLaughlin, Moneta, VA; Barbara (Sandon) Herzlich, Wayne, PA; Douglas (Kathleen) Martin, Cincinnati, OH; great-grandmother of ''Gem'' & Jordan McLaughlin; Mark and Bradley Herzlich; Ryan and William Martin. A memorial service will be held at the United Church of Rowayton on Wednesday, March 23, 2005 at 3 P.M. with the Reverend John Livingston officiating. A contribution in her memory to the United Church of Rowayton, 210 Rowayton Avenue, Rowayton, CT. 06853, would be appreciated.
