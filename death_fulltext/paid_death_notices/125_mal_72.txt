
        LEADER-Dr. Sidney D. The Hunter College community mourns the loss of Sidney Leader, husband of Ruby Felt Leader. Mrs. Leader, a Hunter graduate, served as a director of the Scholarship and Welfare Fund for many years. Together, the Leaders endowed scholarships in Urban Affairs and Health Sciences. Their generosity and commitment to the college will benefit Hunter students for years to come. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Mrs. Leader and her family. David A. Caputo, President, Hunter College Floria V. Lasky, Chair, The Hunter College Foundation
        LEADER - Sidney, M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni records with sorrow the passing of their longtime colleague and friend. He will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Wayne S. Fuchs, M.D., President Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary
