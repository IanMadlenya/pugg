
        TSENG-Anthony T. On the morning of July 19,2000, peacefully at home. Internationally recognized management consultant, President of Physical Distribution Dynamics, member of the Manhattan Rotary Club. Beloved husband of Sophia. Loving father of Robert, Roberta and Vivien. All of us who knew him was touched by his concern for others and his joy of life. He will be greatly missed. Family will receive friends Friday, July 21st from 10am to 12:30pm at Ballard Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY. Interment to follow at Kensico Cemetery.
