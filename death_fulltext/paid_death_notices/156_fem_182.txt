
        KAMMERER-Edith L. 82, passed away January 16, 2000 in Jacksonville, FL. She is survived by four children, Athleen Zimmermann, William, Hilary and Kelly Kammerer, two brothers, two sisters, five grandchildren and two great grandchildren. She is predeceased by her husband, Dr. William H. Kammerer. A memorial service was held in Amelia Island, FL.
