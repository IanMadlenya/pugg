
        KAPLANSKY-Ruth. Family and friends mourn the passing of a righteous and devoted wife, cherished mother and loving grandmother who dedicated her life to her family. She was a pillar of strength and demonstrated committment to life each and every day. She is survived by a loving husband of 59 years, Phillip. Beloved mother of Melvin, Alan and Steven, daughter-in-law Joanne, grandmother of Jonathan, Mandy, Harris and Marin. A loyal retired public servant of the City of New York will remain in our hearts forever. Shiva will be held at the family home in Queens. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to The Simon Wiesenthal Center, 9760 W Pico Blvd, L.A., CA 90035.
