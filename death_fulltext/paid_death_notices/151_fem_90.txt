
        MORRISON-Patricia Fox, 79. Of Scarsdale. Died August 10, 1999. Mrs. Morrison was pre-deceased by loving husband Robert. She is survived by two sons, Robert S. and Bruce G. Morrison and three grandchildren, Kim Mink, Kelly and Gavin Morrison. A memorial mass will be held August 13 at 10AM at Our Lady of Fatima Church in Scarsdale. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Our Lady of Fatima Church in Scarsdale or The American Cancer Society.
