
        GALLARD-Rosalind. Wife of the late F. Edmund Gallard. Mother of Don Gallard of Denver, CO; Lorraine Gallard of Bethesda, MD; and Ken Gallard of Taos, NM. Sister of Walter Abrams of Valley Stream, NY. Grandmother of Dewey Gallard and Solomon Kuckelman. Lifelong friend of Roz and Murray Martin. She will be remembered for her deep love of family, her strength, her intelligence, her elegance. Despite advancing illness, she continued to be a loving inspiration to her children. Graveside services at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ, on Monday, July 27, at 11 AM. Memorial contributions in her name may be made to Jewish Heritage for the Blind, Brooklyn, NY, or Alzheimer's Association. Date of passing, July 25, 1998.
