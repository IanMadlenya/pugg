
        BARRETT--Edmund E., Jr. 76, of Grantham, NH, died of leukemia on August 11. A son of Lois and Edmund E. Barrett of New Rochelle, NY. He graduated from Iona School, Georgetown University '49 and served as an officer in the USAF. A partner at Lawrence M. Marks & Co., Dean Witter and E.F. Hutton, Mr. Barrett also owned Tracy's in Manhattan. He was a devout Catholic, patron of the arts, avid equestrian and dedicated volunteer. He is survived by siblings Lois Brady of Chestnut Hill, PA, Barbara Coughlin of Bronxville, NY and William Barrett of West Palm Beach, FL. A sister, Mary Hendrickson, predeceased him. Burial will be private at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
