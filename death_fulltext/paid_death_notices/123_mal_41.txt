
        KATZ-Irving Danny. On April 3rd in New York. Our family lost its beloved patriarch. His friends lost their first officer. He brightened the lives of all he touched with his kindness and generosity. His independent spirit will live on in all of us. Sleep tight, Big D. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, (at 81 Street), Saturday, April 5, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. with service at funeral home on Sunday, April 6th, 11:30 A.M.
