
        ARNOLD-Matilda. Passed away on February 22nd, 1999, Napa, California, at age 95. Native Pennsylvania and former resident of New York City, for many years. Beloved wife of the late Arnold H. Arnold, and mother of Steven Arnold, of Tiburon, California. She loved life and so enjoyed everyone around her. Services were held, with Burial at the Kol-Sholom Cemetery at Mount Tamalpais Cemetery, San Rafael, California.
