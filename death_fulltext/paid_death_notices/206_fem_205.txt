
        KENNEDY--Marie. Of Big Horn, Wyoming, formerly of Rye, New York. Beloved wife of 58 years of Peter M. Kennedy, loving and devoted mother of Peter, Paul and John, mother-in-law of Carroll Culhane Kennedy, grandmother of Sarah Dolce and of Paul, Burke, Nicholas, Hilary, Matthew, Catherine, James and Mary Kennedy; grandmother-in-law of Luke Dolce and great-grandmother of Isabelle Dolce. She was a friend of everyone. Family will receive friends Tuesday, March 23, 2-4 pm, and 7-9 pm, at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd, Rye, New York; 914-967-0129. Mass of Christian Burial at the Church of the Resurrection Wednesday, March 24, 2004, at 10 am. Interment Greenwood Union Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to a charity in her name.
