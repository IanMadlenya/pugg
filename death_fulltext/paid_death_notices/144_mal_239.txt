
        LIANG-Leland Sung. Died on January 8th, 1999 at age 78. Dr. Liang received his doctorate from the Wharton School. He was the founder of Textile Marketing Associates and an adjunct professor of marketing at Baruch College for more than 25 years. He was an active member of the Harvard Club of New York from which he received an MBA, and the American Marketing Association among many other organizations. Dr. Liang is survived by his wife of 50 years, Florence; three sisters, HuiI, Ching-Ye and Min-Yi; brother Hung; uncle, Bryan Shaw; & many nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held on April 12 from 5-7PM at the Harvard Club of New York, 27 West 44 Street. A fund in his memory will be established at a local charity to receive contributions in lieu of flowers.
