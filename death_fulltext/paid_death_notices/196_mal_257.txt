
        HARTIG--Peter. 87, passed away Monday, May 26, 2003. During his life, he was a championship tennis and table tennis player and an accomplished businessman. However, his greatest achievement was bringing together and helping people with his wit, wisdom, warmth, and humor. He is survived by his sister Lili Hahn, nephews Robert and Andrew Hahn, and many, many friends. Services will be held Friday 3:30 P.M. June 6 at Campbell Funeral Chapel at Madison and 81st St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to International House. >>AD#
