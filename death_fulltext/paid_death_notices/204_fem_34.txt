
        PEARL -- Bettina. Devoted wife of Erwin Pearl. Died on December 25, 2003, surrounded by her loving family. She is survived by her four daughters, Diane Weinstein, Debra Heller, Eva Centeno and Suzanne Bakst and their husbands, ten grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren. Bettina inspired all who met her. There was a private funeral service, and a celebration of her life will be held in New York City in early 2004. Please send donations to the 92nd Street Y, 1395 Lexington Ave., NY, NY 10128.
