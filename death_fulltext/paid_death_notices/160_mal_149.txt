
        FUIRST-William, age 99 on May 11. Beloved husband of the late Pauline Fuirst, loving father of Alan and Susan Fuirst; Phyllis and Richard Baker; adored grandfather of Ken and Sue Fuirst; Pam and Allen Weiss; Ellen and Michael Weiss and Caren Baker and proud great grandfather of six. Services are private.
        FUIRST-William. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his son Alan and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
