
        CAIN--Edward A., Jr., DDS. JW Benfield Professor Emeritus, Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery. Died at home on November 8, 2003 at age 83. Beloved husband of 55 years of Jeanne W. Cain. Devoted father of Edward A., III (Kathryn), Barbara Rucci (Jerry), William, Susan Webster (Robert), Jeanne Dingle. Cherished grandfather of Meighan, Courtney, Thomas, Matthew, Catherine, Sam and Sally. Dear brother of Harriet F. Cain. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Wednesday 7-9PM and Thursday 3-5 & 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial at Mother Cabrini Chapel, 701 Ft Washington Ave at W 192nd St, Friday 10AM. Interment Ferncliff Cemetery.
        CAIN--Edward A. Jr., DDS. Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery mourns the passing of Dr. Edward A. Cain Jr. Dr. Cain received his DDS degree from Columbia University in 1945 and was a member of the faculty for 45 years. He was the Benfield Professor of Operative Dentistry and Director of the Division of Operative Dentistry until his retirement in 1990. His devotion to the school, the faculty, the administration and the students of the Dental School for half a century is unsurpassed. The wake will be held at the Campbell Funeral Home on Madison Avenue and 81st Street in New York City on Wednesday, November 12, from 7-9PM and on Thursday, November 13, from 3-5PM and from 7-9PM. Funeral services will be held on Friday, November 14, at 10AM at the Mother Cabrini Shrine, 701 Ft. Washington Avenue, (190th Street, NYC). The family requests that in lieu of flowers contributions be made to the Edward A. Cain Jr. Memorial Fund at the Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery.
