
        HANDLER-Clara Schwartzman, 88. Devoted sister of Sara Kaufman, loving aunt of Carol and Henry, doting grandaunt of Erica, widow of Leo for nearly half a century, riveter of ships in World War 11, bookkeeper by profession, seamstress, clothing designer and artist by avocation, published poet and author of a rich legacy of letters, inveterate European traveler, peripatetic aficionado of New York City's boundless offerings, with a style, mind and opinions always of her own, passed away peacefully at her home in Manhattan on Thanksgiving Day. Donations in lieu of flowers to the American Civil Liberties Union.
