
        WAAGE-Dorothy V. Passed away on August 13. She was the adored wife of the late Thomas O. Waage, dear sister of Elaine Karpf and of Raphael Cerino, and beloved aunt of Carol Baer and Paul Cerino. She was a serious student of Shakespeare and classic literature, an avid lover of opera and great theater and a dedicated fan of Lotte Lehman and Luciano Pavarotti. She will be missed by her many friends and family who enjoyed her gracious hospitality, her culinary expertise and sparkling wit.
