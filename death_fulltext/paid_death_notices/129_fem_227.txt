
        CLODFELTER-Constance J. Suddenly, October 24, 1997. Beloved cousin of Gail Irvine and aunt of Katharine Sullivan, Elizabeth Smith, Patricia Irvine and Christine VanLeer. Also dear friend of Sally J. Kuhn. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Museum of Modern Art, New York. A memorial gathering of friends and relatives will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, on Thursday, October 30, at 6:30 PM.
