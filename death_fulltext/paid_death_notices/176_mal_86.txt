
        MENDLER-Ernest F. Of London, originally of South Bend, Indiana and New York City, died suddenly at home on August 31st, aged 71. Husband of Frances and daughter Camille, both of London, England, he also leaves a brother, Edward, of Boston, Massachusetts, sister, Jeanne Davies of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and six nieces and nephews. Funeral to be held at West London Crematorium at 12 o'clock, September 12th.
