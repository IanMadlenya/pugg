
        HALPERIN-Max J. M.D. on December 9 in his 97th year. General practitioner in Flushing for over 50 years. Graduate of NYU School of Medicine. Beloved husband of the late Hannah and dearest father of his late son Lyle. Survived by his devoted son Ira C. Halperin, M.D. and daughters-in-law Amy and Karen, grandchildren Dorie, Deborah, David, Jill and Eric, as well as eight great-grandchildren. Interment private.
