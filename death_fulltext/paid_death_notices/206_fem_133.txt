
        STAWASZ--Cherie, 57, on 26 of February in Guadalajara, Mexico. Founder of Stawasz Communications, formerly affiliated with the Jordan McGrath Case & Taylor Advertising Agency, and recently consultant to RF/Binder Partners. Known for her creativity, passion, and ability to develop programs that touched the heart, Cherie was a brilliant, generous, spiritual, and loving individual who lived each day with gusto and laughter. Survived by her mother, Louise Stawasz, of Morton Grove, IL. Donations: Spelman College, or Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Fdtn.
