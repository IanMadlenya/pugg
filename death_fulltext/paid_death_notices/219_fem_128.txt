
        BECK--Ruth. 88, born in Brooklyn, passed April 17, 2005, in Trumbull, CT. For 65 years, she was the devoted wife of Dr. Arthur. Loving mother of Dr. Lawrence and Dr. Michael, beloved motherin-law of Alice and Marcia and adored grandmother of Sara, Andrew, Marissa and Morgen. Donations may be made in the name of Ruth Beck to the Bridgeport Hospital, 267 Grant Street, Bridgeport, CT 06610, 203-384-3000, Hematology and Oncology Department.
