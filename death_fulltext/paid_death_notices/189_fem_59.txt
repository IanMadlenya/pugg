
        ROBINSON-Mary T. (nee Dillon), age 72, died on Monday, October 7, 2002 at her home in Middletown, NJ. She was predeceased by her husband Francis X. Robinson Sr. in 1994, and her son Ned Robinson in 1995. Her late husband retired from Time, Inc., NYC, in 1986. She is survived by her three sons, seven daughters, four daughters-in-law, six sons-in law, 20 grandchildren, two brothers, one sister, and many nieces, nephews and cousins.
