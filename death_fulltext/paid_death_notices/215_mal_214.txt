
        KATZ--Leopold, Ph.D. The William Alanson White Institute and Society are profoundly grieved at the untimely death of a much loved and esteemed Institute graduate and colleague. Leopold was a bright, kind, decent and gracious man whose incredible heart and zest for a full and meaningful life touched and inspired all who knew him. His rich legacy will endure. Heartfelt condolences to his wife Helga, and to his family and friends. Joerg Bose, M.D. Institute Director Gurmeet Kanwal, M.D. Society President
