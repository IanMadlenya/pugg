
        COVELLO--James L. 48 years, of Pound Ridge, NY, died on November 16, 2003. Beloved husband of Mariann. Devoted father of Jamie and Caitlin. Jim was a self-employed lighting director and an Emmy award winning cameraman in broadcast and corporate television worldwide. Jim was a devoted father, a loving husband, and a friend to all he met. He was an avid guitarist, an accomplished cook and a fabulous gardener. Jim was able to incorporate his love for music and comedy into his television career. Jim was known and will be remembered for his sense of humor and love of life. Loving son of Terry and the late James Covello, Sr. Nephew of Clara. Brother of Nancy, Marcy and Bob. Memorial visiting hours will be at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. The memorial service will be held at the Croton Falls Community Church, Croton Falls, NY Thursday at 10 am. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory may be made to The Educational Trust for James and Caitlin Covello, 1 Lafayette Place, 2nd floor, U.B.S., Greenwich, CT 06830 or the Hiram Halle Memorial Library, Pound Ridge, NY.
