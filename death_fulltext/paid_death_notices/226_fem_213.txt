
        NIMITZ - McDANIEL -- Mary Louise, 1909--1995, was born in San Angelo, TX, to Mary Ella Holland & Louis Otto Nimitz. She was sister to Ernest, Susan, Theresa and Louis Nimitz and wife of Hugh Hines McDaniel. Louise lovingly raised her children in Demarest, NJ. She was a dedicated and caring Director of Volunteers at Milwaukee Children's Hospital (1961-1978). She also created lovely flower gardens and restored antique furniture. Louise was an angel of kindness and purity who now ''dwells in the house of the Lord forever...'' and is lovingly remembered today and always by her daughter Susan McDaniel of Woburn, MA
