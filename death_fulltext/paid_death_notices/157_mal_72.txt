
        BACHMANN-Louis, Jr. Mount Sinai School of Medicine and The Mount Sinai Hospital of Mount Sinai NYU Health will hold a memorial service to mark the passing last month of generous long-time benefactor Louis Bachmann, Jr., whose philanthropy to Mount Sinai included joining his daughter Bonnie Strauss, and son-in-law Thomas W. Strauss, our esteemed Trustee, in the naming and endowment of the Bachmann-Strauss Professorship in Neurology for the study of dystonia. Along with his daughter he also was instrumental in the establishment of The Bachmann-Strauss Dystonia & Parkinson Foundation which provides major support for the Movement Disorders Program at Mount Sinai. Trustees, faculty, staff, students, family, friends and admirers are invited to attend at 4:00 PM, Friday, February 11, in the Goldwurm Auditorium, East Building, 1425 Madison Avenue at 98th Street.
