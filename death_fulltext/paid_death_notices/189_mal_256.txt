
        STODDARD-William B. Jr. Ridgefield, CT, on October 16, 2002. Loving husband and father to Carol and Emily, most generous friend to all. Born in Carbondale, PA, graduate of New York University and its Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, NYC. Investment economist. For thirty years he was active in civic affairs in Ridgefield as a board member of the Aldrich Museum of Contemporary Arts and the Ridgefield Library. His wise counsel and loyalty will be missed. Memorial service to be announced. Contributions to Ridgefield Conservation Commission Open Space Fund, Ridgefield, CT 06877.
