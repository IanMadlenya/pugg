
        GOODMAN-June Karelsen. Loving wife, mother and grandmother of Danbury, CT on December 26, 1997 following emergency surgery. Born in New York City in 1920 to Frank and Sophie Karelsen. She served on local, state and national boards promoting the arts and education. In 1980 she established the Marian Anderson Award Fund, which she actively directed until her death. She is survived by her husband of fifty-seven years, William W. Goodman; her five children; her six grandchildren; her sister Ellen Solender of Dallas, TX; and her brother Frank Karelsen of New York City. Memorial services Monday, December 29 at 11:00 AM, the United Jewish Center in Danbury. Donations to the Marian Anderson Award Fund, 1 Lakeside Road, Danbury, CT 06811.
