
        RAISLER-Dolly Wolfson. The adored wife of Robert K. Raisler, died peacefully on June 17, at her home in New York, surrounded by her loving family. She was the daughter of the late Solomon and Nechi Wolfson of Glasgow, Scotland, beloved sister of Rose Martin and Bebe Steinberg of London, and of the late Sam Wolfson, Sir Isaac Wolfson, Charles Wolfson, Jeannie Williams, Edith Hyman, Eva Gerber, Esther Levy, and Bette Shapiro. Dolly was a loving and devoted wife, a much beloved mother and sister, doting grandmother and aunt, and loyal friend. Her love, wisdom, generosity and encouragement enriched the lives of everyone. Dolly is mourned by her cherished husband of 42 years, Robert, her children Gordon, Steven and Lynn, her grandsons, Michael, Anthony and Joe, and by Joe's fiancee, Jordana Matthews. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. (at 81 Street), on Monday June 21, 1999, at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the American Cancer Society.
