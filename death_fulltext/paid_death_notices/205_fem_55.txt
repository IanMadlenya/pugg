
        WHITMORE -- Madeline M. died February 3, 2004 in Charlottesville, VA. Born September 8, 1906. She was a 1928 graduate of the University of Virginia. She was preceded in death by her husband H. Bruce Whitmore. They raised their family in Port Washington, NY from 1939-68, and she taught school at Greenvale Country Day School for 15 years. Survivors include her children, Caryanne R. Ericson and Bruce G. Whitmore and three grandchildren.
