
        LURIA--Gladys. The Board and Staff of the Mosholu Montefiore Community Center offer our deepest condolences to the family of Gladys Luria, our longtime most esteemed Board Member. Gladys had deep compassion for all people and was always concerned about those less fortunate, struggling with poverty, family issues and childcare concerns. Her kindness and strong spirit will be missed. Natly Esnard, President Don Bluestone, Exec. Dir.
