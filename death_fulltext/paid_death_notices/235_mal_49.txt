
        COHEN--Howard Seymour. Born August 27, 1936, passed away Tuesday, August 1, 2006. Howard, C.P.M., had been president of H.S.C. Management Corp. since its inception in 1977. His many affiliations included: Board of Director Bronx Realty Advisory Board Inc.; The National Association of Real Estate Boards (realtor); and the Institute of Real Estate Management. Howard, beloved husband, loving father and adored grandfather is survived by his wife, three daughters, three sons-in-law and seven grandchildren. Our hero will be remembered forever and missed always.
