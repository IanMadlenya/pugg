
        MANES-Geraldine. Of Palm Beach, FL and New York City, died Sunday, July 16. Loving wife of Jules Manes, who predeceased her. Beloved mother of Jill Rosen and Jack Manes and their spouses, Robert and Leslie. Devoted grandmother of Adam, Ross, Evan, James, Dina, Leah and spouse Michael Finkelstein. Great grandmother to Jenna and Max, and sister to Irma, Marshall and Marilyn. Jerry did everything she attempted well. She was a dynamic personality, strong-willed but loving and caring to her family and friends. She was unique and will be greatly missed.
