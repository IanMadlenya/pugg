
        EISENMAN-Elizabeth Henderson. Died Saturday, September 1, 2001. She was 65 years old. Born in London to Dr. Joseph L Henderson and Helena Cornford, Elizabeth was raised in Ross, CA. She is survived by her two loving children: Julia and Nicholas Eisenman, her devoted father Dr. Joseph Henderson, and her son-in-law Jonas Wickham. She lived for many years in New York City where her children were born and raised. She had been living for last 15 years in Ross with her father Dr. Henderson. A memorial and celebration of her life will be held in Ross on October 13th, 2001. Contributions in lieu of flowers are suggested to the C.G. Jung Institute at 2040 Gough St, San Francisco CA 94109.
