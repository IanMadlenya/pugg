
        ROSENTHAL--Seymour Allen. Of Forest Hills, N.Y. died on August 21, 2006. Born on April 8, 1917, he was a beloved son of Anna and Benjamin, the loving and devoted husband to Lillian, father to Ilyse, grandfather to Jamie, Emily, Jenny and brother to Beatrice, Martin and Gerald. He attended New York University where he majored in business and served his country nobely and proudly in WWII. A distinguished leader in the diamond business for over 60 years, he was President of the Diamond Trade Association and President and Honorary President of the World Federation of Diamond Bourses. His many charitable works included service on the Board of Directors of the Rabin Medical Center in Israel. He will be remembered and cherished in all our hearts forever. Services to be held at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels at 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, N.Y. on Wednesday, August 23, 2006 at 9:30 A.M.
