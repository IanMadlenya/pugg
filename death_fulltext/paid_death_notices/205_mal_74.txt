
        MELNICK--Evelyn. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Evelyn Melnick, beloved mother of Michael Melnick. The Melnicks have long been cherished friends of UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to Michael and his wife, Barbara, and to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
