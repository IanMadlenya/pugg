
        THOMAS--Michel. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of Michel Thomas, a friend of the League, a Holocaust survivor, a member of the French Resistance, and a liberator at Dachau. For his service as a member of U.S. Counter-Intelligence during World War II, he was awarded the Silver Star. Michel joined ADL in the fight against Holocaust denial and anti-semitism and in promoting Holocaust education. We extend our condolences to his entire family. Barbara B. Balser, National Chair Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
        THOMAS--Michel. Sutton Place Synagogue records with sorrow the passing of our respected member, benefactor and a WW II hero. Condolences to his cherished children. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Mark Wiener, President
