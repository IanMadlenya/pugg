
        DOBRER-Jean. June 18, 1954August 18, 2000, of Chicago. Beloved wife of Robert Suomala. Wonderful daughter of Kenneth and Florence Dobrer. Treasured sister of Nina Dobrer. Dear aunt of Emily, Amanda, Sussana and Nicholas Hoffman. Dear step daughter of Janet Dobrer. Best friend to so many in Chicago. Her passing leaves a wound that will never fully heal. Memorial donations may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
