
        ZENTNER-Lee. 73 years old on January 4, 1998 of unexpected complications from Parkinson's disease. Formely of Cleveland and Scarsdale. Former Hadassah President, business woman, mentor. Fiercely loved and loving mother of Lesley Dormen and Scott Dormen, wife of David Zentner, cherished mother-in-law of Maria Wagner Dormen and Quentin Byrne Spector. Inspiring friend, adored sister-in-law and Aunt. Your glamour and charm seduced us, your generosity and dignity enlarged us, your ready laugh and sly humor delighted us. We hold you close, remember you always, will miss you forever. Cremation private. Contributions may be made in her name to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation.
