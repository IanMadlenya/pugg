
        WEINBERG-Eugene ''Gene'' of Pipersville, PA formerly of New York, New York, died Sunday night after a protracted illness. He was 86. Beloved husband of Emmeline Epstein Weinberg. Father of Michael and the late Alex. Grandfather of Mia Weinberg Clinton and Michael Weinberg, Jr. Great-grandfather of Keegan, Devin, Amber and Michael III. Relatives and friends are invited to his memorial service at 12:00 noon on Sunday, March 2, 1997 at the family residence at 6312 Carversville Road, Pipersville, PA. Friends are requested to make donations to their favorite charity. For information contact Reed & Steinbach Funeral Home, Doylestown, PA.
