
        GRANDE--Frank, D., Ph. D. (Oxon) on November 25th. Dr. Grande received his degree from Oxford University in England. Former chairman of the History Department and Director of the Program in History and Philosophy of Sciences at The City College (CUNY). President of Griffon House Publications. Survived by his sister, Joan Iannelli, nieces, Veronica LaForgia, Jennifer Novegrod, Jeanette Schlossman; grandniece, Talia Schlossman; grandnephews Brendan Novegrod and Tanner Schlossman. Reposing at the Frederick J. Chapey and Sons Bethpage Funeral Home, 20 Hicksville Road, (1 Mile North of the Southern State parkway, Exit 29). A Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial, St. James R.C. Church, Seaford, Wednesday 9:15 AM. Entombment St. John's Cloister Cemetery, Middle Village, NY. Visitation Tuesday 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations maybe made to: Church of St. Francis of Assisi Bread Line, 135 W. 31st Street, New York, NY 10001 or St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, PO Box 1893, Memphis, TN 38101-9950.
