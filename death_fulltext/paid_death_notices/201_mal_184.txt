
        FLINK--Lawrence J. On October 19, 2003 at age 69. Lawrence was born in Joliet, IL. He served as a Lieutenant in the Navy and was also an English and Debate teacher at the Naval Academy. He received his Masters Degree from Case Western Reserve. Lawrence worked as an advertising executive and was the Executive Vice President of Dancer, Fitzgerald and Sample. He is survived by his wife, Susan; two children: Timothy Flink and Susan (Michael) O'Connor as well as his brother, James and sisters, Marilyn Whalon and Madelyn Gaul. Reposing on Wednesday from 5 to 9 at Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck. Funeral Mass Thursday 10 A.M. at Sts. John and Paul R.C. Church.
