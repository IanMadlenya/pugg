
        KOTTY-Robert B., M.D. On July 10, 2000. He received his medical degree from the College of Physicians and Surgeons, Columbia University. He was associated with Beth Israel and Bellevue Hospitals for many years. He is survived by sisters Rieva Kotty and Olga Feiman, and brother Joseph Kotty, also nieces and nephews. A private graveside service was held Wednesday, July 12, 2000.
