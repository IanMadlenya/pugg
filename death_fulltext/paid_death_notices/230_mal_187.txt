
        THURNAUER--William. 92, of Fort Lee, formerly Teaneck, on March 12, 2006. Beloved husband of Lilo Goldenberg Thurnauer and of the late Maria Thurnauer. Devoted father of Andrew and Maureen of Reading, MA, Joan and Craig of Seattle, WA. Adored grandfather of Jesse, Rebecca, Caitlin and Michael and cherished brother of Else Baer of LI. Bill's lifelong dedication to commitment to musical causes was immeasurable. He served as president of the Bergen Civic Music Association, as president of the North Jersey Cultural Council and the former Teaneck Symphony Society. His endowment in 1987 of JCC Thurnauer School of Music has enriched countless lives. Services Wednesday, March 15, 11:30 AM at Gutterman-Musicant Funeral Directors, 402 Park St., Hackensack, NJ 800322-0533. Donations to the Thurnauer School of Music at the JCC on the Palisades or a charity of your choice would be greatly appreciated.
