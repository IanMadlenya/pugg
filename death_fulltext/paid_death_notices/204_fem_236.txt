
        BEERE -- Adele Diamond, passed away during the evening of January 14, 2004. She is survived by her four children, Marian Sigman, Stephen Diamond, Charles Diamond, and Diane Diamond, her eight grandchildren, and her three greatgrandchildren. She grew up in the Bronx and lived most of her adult life in or near New York City, a place she loved. A memorial service will be held on February 1, 2004 at 10:00 AM, at Mount Ararat Cemetery, exit 33 on the Southern State Parkway, East Farmingdale, Long Island. Contributions may be made to The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund.
