
        COOK-Thomas Poultney. On March 18, 1999. Age 88. Country lawyer, public servant, devoted husband of Mildred Benedict Cook and father of Charlotte Cook Rakela, Thomas P., Jr. and Louisa Cook Moats. Relatives and friends are invited to his memorial service at Princeton University Chapel on Friday, April 16, 1999 at 10:30 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Friends of Princeton Open Space, P.O. Box 374, Princeton, N.J. 08540. For information, contact Jos. A. Fluehr III Funeral Home, Richboro, PA.
