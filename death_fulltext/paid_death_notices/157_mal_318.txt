
        PETONIAK-Steven G. Died February 24, 2000 of complications from AIDS. Age 46. A direct marketing executive for CBS, Ogilvy and Mather, and Time-Warner, Inc, he was the founder of Lesbians and Gay Men at Time, one of the first corporate gay and lesbian employee groups. He was a driving force behind two highly successful art auctions to benefit ACT UP, the AIDS activist organization, as well as the Out Auction to benefit the Los Angeles Gay and Lesbian Community Center. He is survived by his mother, Stephanie, of Cedar Knolls, N.J. and two brothers, Peter, of Green Bay, Wisconsin, and Edward, of New York City. He will be sorely missed by his family and numerous friends. A memorial mass will be held on Saturday, March 4, at 1 PM, at Holy Family Church, 315 East 47 Street, Manhattan.
