
        BURNS-Col. Elmer M. Of Ardsley, New York died on December 10, 2002. He was 97. Col. Burns was born October 31, 1905 in Newfolden, MN to Turi and Ole Bjornrud. He had lived in Ardsley since 1993. He had more than 40 years of U.S. Army and other government services prior to his retirement in 1972. He is survived by his wife, Hedy Plew-Burns of Ardsley and other relatives. Funeral at Edwards - Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave, (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY Saturday 10AM. Visiting Friday 7-9PM. Entombment at Ferncliff Mausoleum. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to Community Hospital at Dobbs Ferry.
