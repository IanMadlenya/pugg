
        SMITH - Catherine Grace. Passed away peacefully September 2, 2002 at New York University Medical Center. Catherine held a Master's Degree in Nursing Education, she was project manager and director of Day Surgery at New York University Medical Center until retirement. Memorial Mass, September 12, 2002, 10:30AM Church of the Sacred Hearts, 307 E. 33 St. Friends are invited. Donations to the American Cancer Society in lieu of flowers.
