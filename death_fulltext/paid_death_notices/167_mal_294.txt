
        CYBULA-Robert W. Age 49. Died of renal cancer at home in Key West, November 17, 2000. A Broadway hair designer, his credits included Zoe Caldwell's ''Medea,'' ''A Few Good Men'' with Tom Hulce, and the A & E production of ''Peter Pan'' with Cathy Rigby. Beloved life partner of Joaquin Romaguera; he became loving stepfather of Racine, Pallas, Kiko and Angel. Grandfather of Milo and Meaghan. Survived by siblings John Casimir, Christine, Darlene and Tina. We will miss him and will always love him. God broke the mold when Robert was born.
