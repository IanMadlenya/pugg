
        ROSENBERG--Stanton L. Age 48, on September 17, 2004, in his home in Morristown, NJ, after a long illness. Born in NYC. Owned and operated printing company Chameleon Editions. Attended Kansas University, BA degree 1980. Survived by wife, Roberta Duty, mother Josephine Rosenberg, sister Maria Minneci and brother Will Blackburn.
