
        BARBALATA-Victor, M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mt. Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our long-standing and devoted colleague. Dr. Barbalata was a member of the Department of Medicine for more than 30 years. He was vigorously involved in the total care of his patients and will be remembered as a dedicated and compassionate practioner of medicine. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. Stanley Tuhrim, M.D., President Terry Weill, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
