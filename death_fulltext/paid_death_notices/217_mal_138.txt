
        SHIMONY--David. Born on September 23, 1921, passed away on February 8, 2005. He was the beloved husband of Esperance Shimony, and the adored father of Ruth Kiwajko and husband Leon, Dr. Rony Shimony and wife Catherine, and Iriss Shimony and husband Andrew Bogen, and the proud, attentive and loving grandfather of Natalie and Michael Kiwajko, Daniel and Emma Shimony, and Ezra Benjamin Chaim Bogen. He will be greatly missed. David Shimony will be layed to rest in eternal peace in Israel on February 11, 2005.
