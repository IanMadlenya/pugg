
        SMILES -- Blanche. Passed away July 23, 2005. Devoted wife of the late Albert, loving mother, sister, aunt and friend. Charming, funny, full of life, an inspiration to all. Loved by all who knew her. She will be profoundly missed. She is survived by her daughters Lynn and Joan Smiles, sisters Irene Travis, Marlene Semel, brother Nat Regenstreif, nieces and nephews.
