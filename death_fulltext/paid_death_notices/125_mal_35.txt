
        VEIT-Robert C. On 6/1/97, age 93. He retired as Professor Emeritus from the Polytechnic Institute of Brooklyn in 1965. Beloved husband of the late Mildred Larabee and Mary Mallon, father of Mary Fuchs and Helen Veit, grandfather of Suzanne Marchese, Thomas Fuchs, and Hilarie Fuchs, great grandfather of Sean Fuchs. Services will be held in Barton, Vt.
