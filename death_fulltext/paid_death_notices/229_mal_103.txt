
        PEABODY--John Damon Jr. Died January 23, 2006 at his home in Hancock, New Hampshire. He served in the Naval Air Corps in World War II. He married Virginia Boyd who died in 1983. He worked in the US State Department in the Agency for International Development in Turkey, Brazil and Honduras. He married Bettina Wall in 1986. He is survived by his present wife Bettina Wall, three sons John, Alexander and James and four grandchildren.
