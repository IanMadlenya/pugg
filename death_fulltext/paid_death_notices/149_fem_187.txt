
        KLEIN-Hannah. On June 11, 1999 following open heart surgery. Belovd wife for fifty-eight years of Leonard. Adored mother of Chuck and Dr. Doris Hiatt and mother-inlaw of Pat and Dr. Mark Hiatt. Cherished grandmother of Alyssa (Eric) Maltz, Dayna and Dr. Matthew, Brian and Eric Hiatt, Ted and Nancy. Devoted great grandmother of Melanie, Marissa and Shari Maltz. Dear sister of Henry Florman. Her sweetness and devotion to family and friends, her optimism and kindness of spirit encouraged and sustained us all. Graveside services were held on June 14th. Contributions in her memory may be made to UJAFederation or a charity of your choice.
