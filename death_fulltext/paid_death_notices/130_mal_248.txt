
        NOLAN-Bishop John Gavin, S.T.D. On November 19, 1997. Auxiliary Bishop of Archdiocese for the Military Services USA, Chaplain, NY Athletic Club for 20 years and former Secretary General Catholic Near East Welfare Association. Survived by brother Michael of Schuylerville, NY, sister Kathleen Nolan of Albany and many nieces and nephews. Reposing at The Abbey, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9PM and at Saint Patrick's Cathedral, 5th Avenue and 51 St, Monday from 9:15AM until Mass at 10:30AM celebrated by His Eminence John Cardinal O'Connor. The family will receive friends at the DeVito-Salvadore Funeral Home, 39 South Main St, Mechanicville, NY Monday evening. Funeral Mass at AssumptionSaint Paul's Church, William Street, Mechanicville Tuesday 11AM. Interment to follow at Saint Peter's Cemetery, Stillwater, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Catholic Near East Welfare Association, 1011 1st Avenue, NY, NY 10022, or The Archdiocese for Military Services, USA, POB 4469, Washington, DC 20017-0469.
