
        LESSER-Norma (Sharfman). October 22, 1917 to Dec. 29, 2000. Born, Worcester, MA., resided Westchester County, N.Y., and Berkshire County, MA; graduate Wellesley 1939; poetess and lover of literature; active in charitable organizations and community affairs. One sister, 3 children and 4 grandchildren will miss her dearly.
