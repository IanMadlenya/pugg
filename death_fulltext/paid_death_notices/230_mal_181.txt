
        McCAHAN--Dr. Jermyn F., 92, who died on March 8, 2006, was a leader in the fields of occupational and preventive medicine. An avid outdoor sportsman, gardener and music lover, his wonderful sense of humor was appreciated by all who knew him. He is survived by his beloved wife, Virginia; four daughters: Maureen, Pat, Ginni, and Mary Beth; and several grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. A funeral mass will be held at 9AM on March 16 at St. Mary of the Lakes Church in Eustis, FL. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the American Heart Association or the American Macular Degeneration Foundation.
