
        ALLERHAND - Irving. The Yeshivah of Flatbush mourns the passing of its Honorary President, Irving Allerhand, A''H. Along with his wife, Ida, A''H, he served as a role model of dedication to family, Jewish education, and community for generations of Yeshivah of Flatbush, students, parents, faculty, and lay leaders. The Yeshivah extends its heartfelt condolences to Mr. Allerhand's familyto his children, Hershel Allerhand, Ruth Burian, Judith Boim, and Joseph Allerhand, to his grandchildren, great grandchildren, and their families. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jack Rahmey, President Dennis M. Eisenberg, Executive Vice President $(6$)ALLERHAND--Irving. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the passing of Irving Allerhand, beloved father of Joseph Allerhand, an esteemed leader whose generosity and service to our Board of Directors, Lawyer's Division, and to the New York Legal Assistance Group makes an indelible impact on the lives of those in need. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Joseph, and his wife, Randi Schatz, and their children, Lauren, Bruce, and David. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
