
        HEILMAN-Carl E. died on May 23, 2001, in Gibsonia, PA at the age of ninety. Mr. Heilman worked as an attorney from 1939 through 1989, serving until 1973 as a partner in the New York firm of Dewey, Ballantine, Busby, Palmer & Wood, and serving thereafter as special counsel to the firm of Csaplar & Bok in Boston. Also a Fellow of the American Bar Foundation. He is survived by his wife Marie, and two children, John Heilman & Elizabeth Costello, one brother & a sister. In lieu of flowers donations to the American Cancer Society. For further information, contact Schellhaas Funeral Home, Ltd. at 1-724-443-1505.
