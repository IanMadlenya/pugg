
        KROTKI-Shirley. On September 13, 2000 at age 86. Beloved wife of Carl, devoted mother of Saul Krotki and Isaac and Judith Putterman and cherished grandmother of Amanda Krotki, Chad and Jessica Haller and Karen Putterman. For 35 years respected teacher of English at Salanter Yeshiva. She leaves behind family, friends and former students enlightened by her wit, wisdom and courageous spirit.
