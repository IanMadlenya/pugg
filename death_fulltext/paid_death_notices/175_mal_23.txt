
        ARONOFF-Howard B., D.P.M. Passed away suddenly Sunday, July 29, 2001 at his residence in Chatsworth, CA. He was born January 22, 1955 in New York City and was raised in Oceanside, NY. A graduate of Fairleigh Dickinson University and Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine, Dr. Aronoff was a well known & respected Doctor within the community. He practiced in the Santa Clarita Valley for the past 10 years and was one of the rare doctors that made house calls. He will be greatly missed by all who knew him. Dr. Aronoff is survived by his loving parents Marilyn and Norman Aronoff of Aventura, FL, devoted brother Joel Aronoff & niece and nephew Kelly and Robert Aronoff of Columbia, MD. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, August 7, 2001 at Lakeside Memorial Park, 10301 NW 25th St., Miami, FL, (305) 592-0690. A memorial service will be held at a later date in California. The family has requested that donations be made to the American Heart Association in his honor.
