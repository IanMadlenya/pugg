
        ROSEN-Mildred Albert. 81. Loving and kind mother of Carole Rosen and Joan Rosen, dear mother-inlaw of Kris Nazar, adored grandmother ''Mema'' of Jarred and Sivan Johnson and Ariela Sidney Nazar-Rosen. Died Friday, February 13, 1998 in Binghamton, NY. She was predeceased by her devoted husband Sidney in 1994. We will always treasure the memory of your love, concern and vitality. We are glad you are with Daddy again. Shiva at the home of Joan Rosen, Bardonia, NY. Contributions may be made to Gallaudet University, Washington, D.C., The Association for Macular Diseases, NYC or charity of your choice.
