
        GITHENS--Thomas Francis, age 79, of Point Pleasant, NJ died on Tuesday, June 5, 2007 after a 20 year, valiant fight with cancer. He was born in Cleveland Heights, OH and lived in Short Hills, NJ before moving to Point Pleasant in 1998. He was a graduate of Yale University and Columbia University. He was a veteran of the Korean War proudly serving his country in the US Army as a Lieutenant in the Third Infantry Artillery Division. He was employed for over 30 years as an Investment Banker and was a former partner at Morgan, Lewis, Githens & Ahn in New York City. He was a longtime member of The Yale Club, Bay Head Yacht Club and the Glen Meadows Golf Club. He was a parishioner of Sacred Heart R.C. Church in Bay Head, NJ. He is survived by his wife of 49 years, Mary Alice (McNally) Githens; five sons, Thomas, William, Paul, David and John; a daughter, Suzanne; two sisters, Ann Masters and Marion Kuenhold; 13 grandchildren, John (J.P.), Brian, Paul, Ginger, Andrew, Thomas, David, Taylor, Nicole, Michael, Kimberly, Brooke and Caroline. Friends may call on Friday, 2-4 and 79pm at O'Brien Funeral Home -505 Burnt Tavern Rd., at Hwy. 70 in Brick. Funeral Mass will be held on Saturday, 10 am at Sacred Heart R.C. Church in Bay Head. Burial will follow at St. Anne's Cemetery in Wall, NJ. Donations in lieu of flowers can be made in his honor to Memorial Sloan Kettering Hospital.
