
        GREENBERG - Harry. 83, of Princeton, NJ formerly of NYC, died February 14, 2002 in Princeton. He was with the NYC Post Office for 40 years and a veteran of the army in WWII. He was a member of Garment Center Synagogue in Manhattan. Survived by a sister Belle Byer of West Windsor, NJ and a niece Sandra Byer-Lubin. Funeral services are being held in Trenton, NJ.
