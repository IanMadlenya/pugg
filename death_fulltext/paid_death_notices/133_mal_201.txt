
        THAU-Dr. M. David. Of White Plains. Formerly of Westbury. Passed away on 2/12/98. Beloved husband of Sylvia. Adored father of Paul, Sheryl, Sue and Arthur. Cherished grandfather of Andrew, Dana, Seth and Matthew. Past President of Old Westbury Hebrew Congregation and Queens and New York County Optometric Societies. Donations may be made in his memory to Project Ezra, East Broadway, NY. Service today, 11:30 AM, at the Old Westbury Hebrew Congragation, 21 Old Westbury Rd., Old Westbury.
