
        GOSSEL-John D., MD, 86. On May 14th. Born in NYC and formerly of Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ. Survived by Frances, his wife of 60 years, a daughter Linda Epstein of Greenwich, CT and Naples, Fl, sons James of Corte Madera, CA and John of DC, and four grandchildren. Member of AMA; American Society of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeons; American College of Surgeons; Mayo Foundation; American Board of Plastic Surgeons; Pan Pacific Surgical Assoc.; NJ Medical Assoc; and served as Lt. Commander, US Navy, W.W. II.
