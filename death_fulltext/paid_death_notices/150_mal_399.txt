
        STONE-Martin M. Writer and long time resident of Greenwich Village, died peacefully at his Bank Street home on July 23, age 70. His impeccable style, elegance and wit will be sadly missed but happily remembered by his great friends and family, and most of all by his children, Abigail and Matthew, his daughter-in-law, Gloria, and his wife, Sally. Memorial service, Tuesday, July 27, 1 PM, at Gramercy Park Memorial Chapel, 152 Second Avenue.
