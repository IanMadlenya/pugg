
        DOPPELT--Stanley. Funeral service at The Riverside at 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave at 11:15 am on Wednesday, October 18, with burial to follow at Mt. Eden Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Alzheimers Foundation.
