
        ROSENTHAL-Richard L. The faculty and staff of NYU School of Medicine and Medical Center mourn the passing of a loyal and visionary philanthropist, Richard L. Rosenthal. He was a longtime supporter of the University and the School of Medicine through the Richard and Hinda Rosenthal Foundation which encourages achievement and excellence in the arts, social sciences and medicine. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Hinda and to all the members of the Rosenthal family. E. John Rosenwald, Jr., Chairman, Board of Trustees, NYU Medical Center Noel L. Cohen, M.D., Interim Provost and Dean, NYU School of Medicine and Medical Center
        ROSENTHAL-Richard L. The Staff and the Director's Advisory Board of the Yale Cancer Center are deeply saddened by the death of Richard L. Rosenthal who with Hinda was a generous Supporter of the Yale Cancer Center. We express our deepest sympathies to our treasured friend and board member, Hinda, to Jaime, Rick and the Rosenthal family.
        ROSENTHAL - Richard. The American Academy of Arts and Letters notes with sadness the death of Richard Rosenthal, donor, with his wife Hinda, of the Academy's Rosenthal Awards in Art and Literature since 1957.
        ROSENTHAL-Richard. We are devastated by the loss of our dear friend, mentor, and source of wisdom for nearly 30 years. Our love goes to Hinda, Jamie, Rick, and the entire family. Samuel and Ronnie Heyman
