
        LIEBERWITZ-Anne Scher, 92, a 20 year resident of Miami Beach, and formerly of Great Neck passed away Sunday, February 9, 1997. She is survived by many devoted and loving nieces and nephews. Mrs. Lieberwitz was Treasurer of Colonial Sand and Stone Co. the largest supplier of concrete to the tristate construction industry and a long time benefactor of U.J.A. Federation.
