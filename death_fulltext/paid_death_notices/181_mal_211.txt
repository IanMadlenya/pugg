
        HOLLANDER-Seymour, 88. Of Rockville Centre, New York. On February 16. Devoted husband for 62 years of Beatrice Louise, who predeceased him in July. Loving father of Ron, Marshall, Lawrence and the late Andrea. Grandfather of Mei Ming, Forest and Gabriel. Father-in-law of Virginia & Margo. Mr. Hollander served with the General Electric X-Ray Department for 44 years. Loyal alumnus of CCNY. A leader at Central Synagogue of Nassau County. A founder of the Parkinsons Support Group of Long Island. ''Man does not live by bread alone.'' He is deeply mourned and will be forever missed.
