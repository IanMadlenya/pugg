
        JACOBSON-Nicholas Biel, 89. Of Norwich, VT, December 7th. Educated at Horace Mann, Dartmouth College, and Stockbridge School of Agriculture, a farmer and playwright, he served with the U.S. Army in Europe during World War II. Survived by his wife, Geraldine Marsicano, daughters, Antoinette and Nora of Norwich, VT. sons Peter of Berkeley, CA and Noah of Springfield, MA, brothers Louis of Pacific Palisades, CA, and Seth of Royal Oaks, MI, two grandsons, Jules Earley and Elias Jacobson. A memorial service will be held December 23rd, 4:00 PM, Roth Center, Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH
