
        BLENK-Muriel. On Mar. 17, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Walter Blenk. Loving parent of Patrick L. Ferriere, Peter C. Blenk and Anthony Blenk. Adored mother-inlaw of Susan Nichols Ferriere and Natalie Wilmer Blenk and grandmother of Elisabeth, Edward and Caroline Blenk. She touched the lives of every one who knew her. A memorial service will be held on Friday, 11AM, April 6, 2001 at the Brick Presbyterian Church (Park Avenue and 91st Street). In lieu of flowers, gifts can be made to either The Brick Presbyterian Church, (62 E. 92nd St., New York City, 10128) or to the Central Park Conservancy (14 E. 60th St., New York City, 10022, attention P. Butler).
