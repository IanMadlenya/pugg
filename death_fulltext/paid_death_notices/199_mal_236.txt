
        McLEAN--David Bruce of San Francisco, CA and New York City, NY. Born March 20, 1952. Died peacefully at his home in Stinson Beach, CA surrounded by family and friends. David was loved and adored by his family, and a vast community of friends and admirers. He inspired us with his style, grace, beauty, humor and love. He will be sorely missed. A celebration of his life will take place on October 11, 2003 in Bolinas, CA.
