
        NADLER-Harriet (Chayale). On July 14, 1997. Beloved wife of fifty years of Joseph. Loving mother of Maxine Karas, Lesly Freed & L. Gerod Nadler. Beloved mother-in-law of Phil, Jodi and Don. Dear sister of Sydell and Leon Hattem and Melvin and Oura Lev. Adored grandmother of Ricky, Nicky, Gabriel, Andrew and Brittany. Harriet had no enemies. She had no harsh thoughts. Everyone she touched loved her. She brightened our lives with laughter and kindess. Harriet's life with Joe was joyous and exciting. She will be sorely missed by her many relatives and friends and she will always be remembered with a smile. She was the very best. She was a ''mensch''. It was a wonderful life. Services today, 1:30 PM at ''Gutterman's'', 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, Long Island.
