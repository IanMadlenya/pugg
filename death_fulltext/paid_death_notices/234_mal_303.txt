
        VANDERMADE--James S., 87, on July 20, 2006 in Gladwyne, PA. Graduate of Montclair Academy, Princeton University, and Harvard Graduate School of Business. Served in U.S. Navy from 1941-1946, reaching the rank of Lieutenant Commander. Principal duty, Aide to Vice Admiral Robert L. Ghormley in Hawaii and Europe. Longtime resident of Montclair, NJ until moved to Waverly Heights in Gladwyne. President of Sowerbutt Quarries until his retirement. Devoted to his family, friends, and community. Past President of the Board of Trustees of Montclair Academy and Montclair Kimberley Academy and Past President of the Board of Trustees of the Montclair Museum of Art. Survived by his beloved wife Margaret Brush Vandermade; his son James S. Vandermade, Jr., his wife Julia Vandermade; his daughter Lisa Vandermade, her husband Robert Popick; grandchildren Adam and Anna Popick and Hobart Popick and his wife Jennifer; greatgrandchildren Ethan and Katherine Popick. A memorial service will be held on Saturday July 29, 2006 at 11 a.m. in Saint Christopher's Episcopal Church, Righter's Mill Road, Gladwyne, PA. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Princeton University, Montclair Kimberley Academy or Montclair Museum of Art would be appreciated.
