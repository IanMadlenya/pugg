
        LANAHAN-Rev. William F.X. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former faculty member of Cathedral College on March 28. Reposing at Edward F. Lieber Funeral Home, 9 E. Valley Stream Blvd., Valley Stream, on Saturday, April 3rd, from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 P.M. Funeral Mass Monday, April 5th, at Holy Name of Mary Church, 55 East Jamaica Avenue, Valley Stream, at 10:45 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
