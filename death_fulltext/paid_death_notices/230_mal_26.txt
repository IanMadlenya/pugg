
        BARRY--Robert. Age 85, February 28, 2006, in New York City, passed peacefully away. Yale Drama School graduate. Long time CBS top Lighting Director. Well remembered for his work with Walter Cronkite and on CBS Sixty Minutes with Dan Rather. Survived by three nieces and two nephews of California and Seattle. His many close friends will also miss him. At his request, there will be no services in New York.
