
        DEGENER-Mrs. Fred A., 89, of 315 Wells Hill Rd., died April 7, 1997 at the Sharon Health Care Center in Sharon, CT. She was the wife of the late Fred A. Degener. She was born on June 8th, 1907 in Berlin, Germany. She is survived by her nephews Kurt and his wife Ingrid Degener of Hickory, N.C., and the Fred Reid family of Germany, and the Daniel Reid family of Thailand. Funeral services will be private at the convenience of the family. Memorial donations may be sent to the Sharon Hospital, Hill Road, Sharon, CT 06069. For information contact Newkirk-Palmer Funeral Home 255 Main Street, Lakeville, Connecticut 06039.
