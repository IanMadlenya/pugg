
        WANDERMAN--Rosalie, of Hollywood, FL, on March 27, 2007; age 100. A resident of NYC until her retirement, she was employed as office manager at Webster Hall in Manhattan for her entire career. Survived by her loving brother Dr. Sidney E. Wanderman, and nieces Lisa Wanderman Gottlieb and Marcia Wanderman Brill of Manhattan. Daughter of the late Phillip Wanderman and Helen Toft Wanderman of Manhattan. Sister of the late Florence and Annie Wanderman of Manhattan and Hollywood, FL.
