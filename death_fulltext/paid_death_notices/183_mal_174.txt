
        McCARTHY-Richard Desmond, 75. Of Bermuda Run, North Carolina, passed away Friday, March 29, 2002. Born on April 9, 1926 in Springfield, MA, he served in the US Air Force and later attended Dartmouth College and Rhode Island School of Design. His furniture designs were sold throughout the world in stores including Macy's and Bloomingdale's. He was a member of New York Yacht Club and the Corinthians. Surviving are his wife Suzanne; a son John; a daughter Megan and grandchildren Caroline & Matthew Scarpa of Larchmont, New York.
