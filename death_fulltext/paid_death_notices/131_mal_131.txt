
        McCARTHY-James J. Jr. age 89, of Fairfield, CT. formerly of NYC died December 7, 1997. He was a graduate of Regis H.S. Class of 1925, Fordham University class of 1929 and The Fordham Law School class of 1932. He was an Attorney for the Immigrant Savings Bank, New York City. He is survived by his wife, Margaret Mooney McCarthy; one son and his wife, Stephen and Rosemary McCarthy of Binghamton, NY; one daughter and her husband, Peggy and Larry Puzzo of E. Hampton, CT. and eleven grandchildren. Funeral services will be Wednesday at 10:00AM, at St. Thomas Aquinas Church, Fairfield, CT. Interment in Calvary Cemetery, NY. Wake services will be held on December 9 at 4 PM-8 PM, at Shaughnessey Brothers Funeral Home, 50 Reef Rd., Fairfield, CT. It would be appreciated if donations be made in his memory to: St. Thomas Aquinas Capitol Fund, 1719 Post Road, Fairfield.
