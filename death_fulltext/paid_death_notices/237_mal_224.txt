
        SEILER--Jerome, M.D., of Great Neck, NY, a devoted husband and loving father, died on October 22nd, at the age of 68. He is survived by his wife of 48 years, Harriet Silverstein Seiler and siblings Leonard and Elaine Seiler and Ilene and Ernest Gruenfeld. He was a fabulous father to Sharon Seiler and Charles Spielholz of Manhattan; to Cyndy Seiler Albert and Ron Albert Jr. of Miami Beach; and Stephanie Seiler and Robert Welikson of Seattle. He will be greatly missed by his grandchildren Erin and Jason Albert, Talia Spielholz and Paige and Noah Welikson. Dr. Seiler was a physician at New York Presbyterian Hospital-Weill Cornell Medical College and spent 40 years in the practice of obstetrics and gynecology. He also was president of Temple Israel of Great Neck, and had been actively involved in the congregation and the community for more than 30 years. Funeral services Tuesday, 11 am, at Riverside Nassau North Chapels in Great Neck.
