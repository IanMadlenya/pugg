
        MORLEY-James T. ''Bud'' passed away on February 5, 1998 at the age of 84 in Boynton Beach, FL. Mr. Morley is survived by his wife of fifty-seven years, the former Margot Zimmer and his children Karen Brennan of Salt Lake City, Utah, Mary Pat Ware of Tucson, Arizona, William J. Morley of Larchmont, New York, and James T. Morley, Jr., of Newtown, CT, nine grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Funeral Mass will be held Tuesday, February 10, 1998, at 10:30 A.M. at St. Augustine's Catholic Church, Larchmont, New York. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
