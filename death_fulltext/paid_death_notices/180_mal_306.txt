
        BLUMSTEIN-Kyver. Age 95, in Palm Beach. Beloved husband of Esther Lurie for 66 years. Devoted father of Joseph Blumstein and Timmie Rome. Loving grandfather of Narric, Mallory, Greg and Jeffrey. President of L.M. Blumstein Department Store, founded 1886. Life long member of the New York Society for Ethical Culture. Visiting hours 7-9 PM Wednesday, 1/23 at Frank E. Campbell. Funeral Service Thursday, 12 noon at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the New York Society for Ethical Culture or a charity of your choice.
        BLUMSTEIN-Kyver. We mourn the passing of this remarkable and fine gentleman. He was loved by many and returned his love to all those he touched. Missing him will be a part of our future. Erwin and Carol Lurie and Family
