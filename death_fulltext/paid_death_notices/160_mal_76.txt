
        SERWER-Howard J. On May 3, 2000 in Washington, D.C. He was Professor Emeritus of Musicology of The University of Maryland, College Park, and resided in Chevy Chase, MD., and Georgetown, ME. He leaves his wife Nancy Rose, sons Nicholas and Andrew, daughter-in-law Frances Fifield and grandchildren Emily and Katherine. A memorial gathering of friends and family will be held at 12:00 noon on Wednesday, May 10, 2000 in the Memorial Chapel of The University of Maryland, College Park, MD. The family asks that remembrances be made in the form of donations in Howard's name to The Performing Arts Library of The University of Maryland, College Park, MD 20742, in lieu of flowers.
