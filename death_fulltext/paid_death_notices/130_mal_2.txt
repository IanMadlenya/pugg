
        CIPRUT-Solomon, MD. The entire family of Maimonides Medical Center expresses a profound sense of loss on the passing of Dr. Sol Ciprut. A member of our institution for nearly 40 years, his life was intertwined with Maimonides. Through the years, he was an intern, a chief resident, a past president of the Medical Staff Society and leading member of the Department of Surgery. At Maimonides he was a daily presence and will be sorely missed. We extend our sympathy to his wife Lorraine, his sons, daughter, daughters-inlaw and grandchildren. Maimonides Medical Center Martin D. Payson, Chairman, Board of Trustees Stanley Brezenoff, President
        CIPRUT-Solomon, M.D. The Department of Surgery of Maimonides Medical Center deeply mourns the loss of Sol Ciprut, an esteemed member of the department for more than 36 years. He was a distinguished surgeon, a dedicated colleague and a true friend. Beyond his outstanding skills, we will remember him for the magnitude of his compassion and genuine warmth. We express our heartfelt condolences to his family. Members of the Dept. of Surgery Joseph N. Cunningham, Jr., M.D. Chairman Maimonides Medical Center
