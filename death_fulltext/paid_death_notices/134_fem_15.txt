
        WASSERMAN-Miriam W. At home, on February 26, 1998 in her 90th year. Loving wife of 65 years of the late John Wasserman. Devoted mother of Ann Kiggen and Jane Ferrar. Beloved grandmother (our Lulu) of Andrew, Carolyn, Linda, Derek and Jessica. Doting great-grandmother of Aaron, Daniel, Peter, Jessica, Danielle, Michael and Quinn. Former active volunteer at Hudson Guild, Fulton House and Sydenham Hospital. Her generous, lively spirit will be deeply missed. Private services.
