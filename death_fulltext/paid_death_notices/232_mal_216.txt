
        VAN DEN HEUVEL--Stanley. The Board of Directors of Goodwill Industries of Greater New York and Northern New Jersey, Inc. note with sadness the passing of our longtime board member, tireless supporter and valued friend, Stanley van den Heuvel. He joined the Goodwill board in 1962 and was an active champion of Goodwill's mission -to help people achieve dignity through the power of work. A graduate of Princeton University and Harvard Law School, after serving in WWII he joined Hanover Bank in 1955. He retired in 1982 from Manufacturer's Hanover, having become senior vice president and corporate secretary. Stanley, who always had a pleasant word for everyone, was made Director Emeritus in 2004 to recognize his outstanding service to Goodwill and the community. The Goodwill family deeply mourns his passing.
