
        ESPOSITO--Salvatore, J., Jr. The Officers, Board of Trustees and Staff of The American Parkinson's Disease Association, Inc. mourn the loss of our past President and member of our founding family, who dedicated his life to finding the cure and easing the burden of those afflicted with Parkinson's disease. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to his wife Lillian, his children, grandchildren and his sister Sophia. His guidance will be missed by all who knew him. In lieu of flowers contributions to The American Parkinson's Disease Association, Inc., 1250 Hylan Blvd, Suite 4B, Staten Island, NY 10305 would be appreciated. Vincent N. Gattullo, President Joel Gerstel, Exec Director
