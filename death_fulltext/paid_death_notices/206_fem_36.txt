
        GOINGS--Mary Louis. Beloved daughter of Brenda Nichol Goings of Mount Airy, NC and Rick Goings of Windermere, FL, passed away on Sunday, February 29, 2004. She was born on October 19, 1977 in Charlottesville, VA. She was a graduate of Salem Academy, Winston Salem, NC and North Carolina State University. She is survived by sisters, Anna, Amy, and Kristina Goings; brothers, Rett and Todd Goings, Christopher, Miles, and Nico Porcaro; her stepmother, Susan Porcaro Goings; and her grandmother Louise Goings, Winston Salem, NC. She was preceded in death by her maternal grandparents William and Iris Nichol, and her paternal grandfather E.V. Goings. Donations may be made to The Boys & Girls Club of America, 1230 West Peachtree Street, Atlanta, GA. 30309.
