
        FORD--Samuel Caldwell, 76, died September 3, 2005 at home in Sanibel, FL under loving care of family and hospice. He was born January 23, 1929 in Washington, DC to parents, Samuel Shivel Ford and Elizabeth Caldwell Walker. Sam has been a Sanibel resident for the past 17 years. Memorial services will be held 10:30 AM, Thursday, December 1, 2005 at the Sanibel Congregational United Church of Christ, 2050 Periwinkle Dr., Sanibel, FL. Memorial gifts may be made to the Smithsonian Institution, MRC035, PO Box 37012, Washington, DC 20013-7012. Please visit: www.samuel cfordandgertrudeaford.us
