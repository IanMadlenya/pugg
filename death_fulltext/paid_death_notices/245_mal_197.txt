
        SIMON--Peter Neil, 61, passed away from complications from a stem cell transplant for leukemia/MDS. Son of the late Dr. Louis and Libby Simon. He was loved by his wife Nancy; daughters Emily and Lauren; son-in-law Michael; brother Alan and sister-in-law Carole; family Michael, Donna, Sarah, and Jack; Allyson and Gary; Robert and Carole. Exceptional math educator for NYC public schools for over 35 years. His unique spirit will always be remembered.
