
        NELSON-Harriet, age 68. A resident of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY, passed away on Thursday, January 23, 2003, of pancreatic cancer. Harriet is survived by her beloved family which includes her husband Norton David Nelson, daughters Hillary (Peter Beckmann) Nelson, Elizabeth (Don) Scheiber, and Ilene Nelson, and grandchildren Max, Alexandra, Zoe, Jasper and Ryan. Harriet is also survived by her parents Martin and Minerva Schwartz of Ft. Lee, NJ, and sister Meryl Schwartz. Services will be held on Sunday, January 27, in Boca Raton, FL. Contributions may be made in memory of Harriet to Hospice By the Sea, Boca Raton, FL.
        NELSON-Harriet. The Lawrence Woodmere Academy Board of Trustees and school community deeply mourn the passing of Harriet Nelson, Past Woodmere Academy Board President, and extend condolences to daughters, Elizabeth, Hillary and Ilene, Academy alumnae.
