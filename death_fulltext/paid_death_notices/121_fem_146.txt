
        MUIRHEAD-Isobel E. Of Jamesburg, NJ, on Feb. 19, 1997. Graduate of Douglas College and Fordham Law School. Member of Phi Beta Kappa. Retired from the Port Authority of NY and NJ in 1983, where she was Chief of Opinions and Appeals, Law. Awarded the Port Authority's Distinguished Service Medal in 1983. She is survived by her sister Margaret Swift of Jamesburg and her nephew Thomas Swift of Holmdel, NJ.
