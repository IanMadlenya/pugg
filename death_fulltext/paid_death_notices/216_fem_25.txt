
        SZLACHTER--Frida. 81 years old. Beloved wife of Morris. Devoted mother of Irwin Shlachter and Celia Kessel, loving grandmother of Adam, Sarit, Tamar and Jordan. Service 11:45am today at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
        SZLACHTER--Frida. Congregation Rodeph Sholom is deeply saddened by the death of Frida Szlachter. Frida was a wonderful wife to Morris and mother to our beloved Headmaster, Irwin Shlachter and Celia Kessel, who were incredibly devoted children. She is also survived by her daughter-in-law, Renee and son-in-law, Shlomo, and her grandchildren, Jordan and Adam Shlachter and Tamar and Sarit Kessel. Frida was a Holocaust survivor who, with Morris by her side, fought tenanciously for life. She will be missed. We extend our deepest condolences to the entire family. Clergy, Board of Trustees, Staff and Members of Congregation Rodeph Sholom
