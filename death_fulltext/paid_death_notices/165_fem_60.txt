
        DRAKE-Karen Nelson. Age 67, of Rutherford, NJ died peacefully at her home on Tuesday October 3, 2000. Beloved wife of the late John G. Drake and loving daughter of the late Mayor Martin P. and Florence H. Nelson. She is survived by her beloved sister Margot N. Carey, her brother-in-law J. Raymond Carey and loving nephew Nelson L. Carey. Mrs. Drake was a retired manager with NYNEX. She was a graduate of Rutherford High School, Centenary College Hackettstown and received her Bachelor of Science Degree from Hiram College in Ohio. Mrs. Drake was a trustee of Centenary College, Past-president of its alumni association and class correspondent. She was the first recipient of the VanWinkle Distinguished Alumni Award from the college. She was a member of the Order of the Eastern Star in Rutherford, former officer of the First Presbyterian Church of Rutherford and past president of the Rutherford Women's College Club. Donations to the Karen Nelson Drake Memorial Fund of the First Presbyterian Church of Rutherford, 1 East Passaic Avenue, Rutherford, N.J. 07070, would be appreciated. A memorial service to celebrate Karen's life will be held at the First Presbyterian Church of Rutherford on Saturday, November 11, 2000 at 11:00AM. For further information, call Collins-Calhoun Funeral Home, 19 Lincoln Avenue, Rutherford, New Jersey.
