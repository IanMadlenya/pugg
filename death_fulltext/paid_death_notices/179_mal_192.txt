
        SCHWARTZ-Irving, 81. Of Sarasota, FL and Norwalk, CT. Husband of Rita Mischel Schwartz, died on Friday in Sarasota. Born in New York City on December 28, 1919, he was the son of the late David and Regina Drucker Schwartz. Mr. Schwartz, a retired CPA, was the founding partner of Schwartz & Hofflich, CPAs. He started the firm in 1948 in New York City and moved it to Norwalk in 1955. He was a member of numerous professional organizations and a visiting guest lecturer at Yale University, speaking on the business aspects on the profession of architecture. An ardent supporter of the state of Israel, he had made over 25 trips there. In addition to his wife, he is survived by two daughters, Susan Mischel of Norwalk and Jane Deer-Hileman of Seattle, three grandchildren, two great grandchildren and a brother, Murray Schwartz of New York City. Services Sunday 11AM Collins Funeral Home, Norwalk, CT.
