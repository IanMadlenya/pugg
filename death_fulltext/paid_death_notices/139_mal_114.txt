
        ELSNER-Jerry. Died after a long illness on August 9. He left behind his much cherished wife, Ruth, his beloved children, Jeffrey and Pamela, and their spouses, Jackie and Andy. Devoted Opa to his three angel grandchildren, Michelle, Julia, and Nikki, who gave him much joy. He also left his brother, John, with whom he was very close. He was a man who was loving, resourceful, creative and courageous. Services will be held at 10 A.M. today at Westchester Reform Temple, Scarsdale, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
