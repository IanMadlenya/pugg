
        RISKIN-Harry. Ohr Torah Institutions of Israel records with deep sorrow the passing of Harry Riskin, beloved father of our founder and Dean, Rabbi Shlomo Riskin, Judi Stern and Myra Abraham. He lived his final years surrounded by the love and respect of children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren in Efrat. We express our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Fred Ehrman, President
