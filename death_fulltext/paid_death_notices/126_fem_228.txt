
        UMPLEBY-Alice Darby Wilson. Age 79, of Shelter Island, NY. Died July 24, 1997, after a long illness. Beloved mother of Joan (Mrs. Guenther W. Salm) and Annie (Mrs. Thomas R. Wilcox, Jr.). Loving grandmother of Darby and Carrie Salm, and Matthew and Sam Wilcox, and great-grandmother of Hannu Salm-Pechukas. She was predeceased by her husband, John Loudon Umpleby and a daughter, Elizabeth. A memorial service will be held at The Union Chapel in the Grove on Shelter Island on August 17 at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Union Chapel in the Grove, Box 326, Shelter Island Heights, NY 11965.
