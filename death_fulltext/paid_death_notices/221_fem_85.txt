
        HOFFMAN -- Winifred M. Born August 24, 1913 in Freeport, Illinois. Moved to Brooklyn in 1920. Died June 6, 2005 in Brooklyn. Retired from the New York Times in 1983 with over 30 years of service. She was pre-deceased by her husband David K. Hoffman who died in 1968. Survived by her brother John T. Mainwaring of New Jersey, one niece, one nephew, five grand-nieces and nephews and four greatgrand-nieces and nephews.
