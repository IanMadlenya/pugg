
        SEINFELD--Robert H. died September 28, at his residence after a long illness. Adored husband of Judy, dear father of Marc, Lori (Paul) and Jill (Jeff), loving step-father of Steven and Jeffrey Greenberg, precious grandfather of Ginger, Jackson and Gerard and cherished brother and uncle of the Soloway family. His values, informed intelligence, steady determination, indepden dence, love of life and passion, gentleness and kindness have made his life a tribute to love, hard work and pleasure. ''Meum et tuum.'' Donations to Lustgarten Foundation, 1111 Stewart Ave, Bethpage, NY 11714, are appreciated.
