
        ANDERSON--Valerie Joan, 39, of NYC, died March 8, 2004 of a brain tumor. Born in Needham, MA, Mount Holyoke College (86); Suffolk University Law School (95). Vice-president and senior counsel, Merrill Lynch; member of NY and MA Bars. Dancer with Beth Soll & Co.(8691); The Blue Hill Troupe, NYC (96-04). She will be greatly missed by her sister Nancy; brother Scott and wife Yolanda; mother Judith Murdock Anderson; father Arthur W. Anderson and wife Patricia; and sweetheart Matt Levine. A memorial service will be held in May. The family request that donations to charity be made in lieu of flowers. Details may be found at InMemoryofValerie.com.
