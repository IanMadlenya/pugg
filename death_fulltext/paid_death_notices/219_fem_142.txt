
        WALSH--Leo M. Jr. On April 17. Retired executive of the Equitable Life Assurance Society. Beloved husband of Ann Quinn Walsh. Devoted father of Christine, Rosemary, Paul, Kathryn, Mark, Leo and Regina. Cherished grandfather of nine. Fond brother of Carol Adach. Also survived by many loving nieces and nephews. Family will receive friends on Tuesday 7-9 PM and Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. Masss of Christian burial Thursday 10 AM at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 65 East 89 St. Interment to follow at St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
        WALSH--Leo. The Board of Directors of The Friends of P.S. 169 extend deepest sympathy to our president, Ann Quinn Walsh and to her daughter Christine on the passing of their beloved husband and father.
