
        SMITH-H. Hanford, Jr., died suddenly on Sept. 16, 1997 at his home on Nantucket, MA. He is survived by his beloved wife, Jane Cowles Smith, his devoted mother, Elizabeth E. Smith, his daughter, Lisa S. Cashin, his sons, H. Brooks Smith and H. Hanford Smith, III, and 5 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, September 20 at 10 A.M. at Brant Point Lighthouse, Nantucket. A reception in his memory will be held on Thursday, October 2, 1997 at noon at the River Club, 447 East 52nd Street, New York City, to which his friends are invited. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Addison Gallery of American Art, Phillips Academy, Andover, MA. 01810.
