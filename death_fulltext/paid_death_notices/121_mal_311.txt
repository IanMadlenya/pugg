
        MORLEY-Joseph F., 65, of Cooperstown, NY, died Friday afternoon, February 21, 1997 at his home. He retired in 1990 as Vice President of the Securities Industry Association. His family has requested that expressions of sympathy in the form of memorial donations be made to the Morley Family Scholarship Fund at the Cooperstown Central School, 38 Linden Ave., Cooperstown, NY 13326.
