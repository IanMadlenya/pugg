
        WINANT-William Alfred, III. Of New York City on November 28, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Phyllis R. Winant and the late Jean V. Winant. Loving father of Cynthia Ruckgaber Winant of New York City and William Wesley Winant II and his wife Susan of Wayne, PA. Adoring grandfather of Bryce Houser Winant and Emily Dawson Winant. Dear brother of John H. Winant of Williamstown, MA. Devoted stepfather of Henrik V. Kristiansson and Andrew C. Kristiansson. Born in Brooklyn, NY in 1920, Mr. Winant was a graduate of the Peddle School in Hightstown, NJ. He served in WWII and the Korean War. He was a member of the Union Club of New York of which he was a govenor from 1990-1996, the Saint Nicholas Society, the Sons of the American Revolution, the Masonic Order of the Holland Lodge No. 8, the Colonial Wars and the Episcopal Actors Guild. A memorial service will be held on Monday, December 11th at 1 PM in Saint Bartholomew's Church, 109 E. 50 St. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Michael J. Fox Parkinson's Foundation, Grand Central Station, P.O. Box 4777, New York, NY 10163.
