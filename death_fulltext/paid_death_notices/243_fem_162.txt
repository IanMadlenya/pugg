
        PROTERO--Dodi. The Juilliard community mourns the passing of Dodi Protero, a member of the Juilliard Evening Division faculty since 1991. She will be fondly remembered by her colleagues and students at Juilliard as a gifted, dedicated, and exuberant educator. We send our deep condolences to all her students and family. Joseph W. Polisi, President Katherine Gertson, Evening Division Director
