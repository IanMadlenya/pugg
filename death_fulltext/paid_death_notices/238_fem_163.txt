
        ARMISTEAD--Margaret Skinner, died peacefully on November 24, 2006. She was a devoted wife of the late George Armistead, loving mother of Clayton, William, and Margaret, and doting grandmother to Andrew and Peter Selig. All who knew Peggy were touched by her kindness, grace, and decency. A memorial service will be held on December 9th, 2006 at 11:00 am at Christ the Redeemer Church in Pelham Manor, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Christ the Redeemer Church or to the charity of your choice.
