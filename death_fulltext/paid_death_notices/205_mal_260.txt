
        KONSKER--Morton. Of Boca Raton, FL passed away at the age of 73, after a long 16.5 month battle with pancreatic cancer. He is survived by his adored wife of 44 years, Sandi, she was the epicenter of his every day thoughts and actions. He was a devoted father of his three sons, Mitchell, Steven and Kenneth. Beloved father-in-law of Lisa, Jackie and Lori. Proud grandfather of Ian, Carly, Samuel, Gabriel, Daniel, Briella, Eliana and Harrison. Dear brother of Norma and Leonard Weiner. He is also survived by many nieces and nephews whom he loved very much. Morty was a true gentleman, always charismatic and humorous. He was a man of great integrity and morals who had the innate ability to make others around him happy. As he grew older, he had an incessant drive to be learned and to live life to the fullest, which never diminished. He will be missed by all the lives he touched. His funeral will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W 76 St, NY, NY on February 22 at 10:00 AM. The family requests any donations be made to the ''Dr. Bruckner Research Fund'' 150 55 St, Brooklyn, NY 11220-2574
