
        GOODFRIEND - Daniel. Died March 13, 1999. Beloved husband and lifelong soulmate of Norma Goodfriend. Devoted and adoring father of Ronnie and father-in-law of Philip Feldman. Doting grandfather of Candace. Caring brotherin-law, uncle and good friend to all who knew him. A gentleman's gentleman, honest, kind, thoughtful, sincere, unassuming and giving without expecting returns. A charitable man, retired accountant/controller who will be missed by all. Funeral Monday, March 15, 1999, noon, Schwartz Bros., 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY.
