
        MAEVIS-Alfred C. 86, of Sarasota, FL, on February 12, 2003. Predeceased by his beloved wife Ada Naden. Graduated magnum cum laude from Polytechnic Institute of Brooklyn, 1937. Served with 125th Construction Battalion, USN, 1943-1945. Retired Assistant Postmaster General. Former New York City Public Works Commissioner (Lindsay administration) and Transit Authority Chief Engineer. Toured every subway system in the world. Awardwinning Fellow of the American Society of Civil Engineers. Survived by grandniece and nephew, Rachel L. Galli and Jonathan J. Galli and many friends. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, 7-9 PM. Thursday. Religious service 11 AM, Friday, at Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, NY.
