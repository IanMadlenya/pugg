
        STRANAHAN-Marion, M.D. Age 98, on October 11. Dedicated psychiatrist who practiced in Manhattan for 70 years, specializing in children and adolescents. Her compassion and intelligence brought help to so many. Graduated from Indiana U. School of Medicine 1925. Former psychiatric Director of Mental Hygiene Clinic of Brooklyn Bureau of Charities and the Women's House of Detention. Psychiatrist at the Payne Whitney Clinic and the Vanderbilt Clinic of Columbia U. Medical Center. Beloved aunt of Beverly and seven grand-nieces and nephews in Rome, N.Y. Will be deeply missed by friends, patients and those who cared for her in her failing years. Contributions in her memory may be made to Habitat for Humanity.
