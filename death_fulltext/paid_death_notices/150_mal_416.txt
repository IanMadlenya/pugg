
        SRERE-Dr. Paul A. Dr. Paul A. Srere, internationally known and honored scientist and professor, died on July 11 in Dallas, Tx. Dr. Srere was 73. For the past 33 years he had been associated with the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School and the Veteran's Administration Hospital in Dallas where he was Director of Basic Research. He is survived by his loving wife of 46 years, Oz; three daughters, E.A. Srere and Margot Srere of Dallas, and Dr. Hillary Srere of Montara, California; a son, Mark Srere of Washington, D.C., four grandchildren, his sister, Mrs. Sonia S. Sweeney of Los Angeles and brother, Benson M. Srere of Greenwich, Connecticut.
