
        TANNENBAUM-Anne Seltzer. On Jan. 1 at 90 years of Gloucester, Massachusetts, formerly of Bronx, New York. Wife of the late Philip Seltzer and Joseph Tannenbaum. Mother of Marlene Seltzer of Gloucester. Sister-in-law of Sidney Seltzer and his wife Carol of Brooklyn. Retired Teaching Assistant in NYC Public Schools. Services will be private.
