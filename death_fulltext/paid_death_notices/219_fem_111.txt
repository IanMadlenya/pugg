
        TOPCHIK -- Theresa (nee Becker). On April 13, 2005, in her 93rd year. Beloved wife of the late Alfred. Loving mother of Brondi, Nina and Steven. Cherished grandmother of Carolyn, Jennifer, Brad, Marney, Justine and Jon, and great grandmother of Charlotte, Melanie and Monika; loving sister of Martin Becker. Theresa was an inspiring and dedicated teacher. She was an army wife with an unconventional and adventurous spirit. Funeral service Friday, April 15, 9:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Point Foundation, P.O. Box 11210, Chicago, IL 60611.
