
        KENNEALLY-Philip J. Of Los Angeles, Chicago and New York City. WWII Veteran and veteran actor of stage, film and television, 1934 Welter Weight Golden Glove Champion and long time member of the Actors Studio, died December 14, 1998, at Cedars Sinai Medical Center, Beverly Hills, CA after a long illness. He is survived by Jerri (nee Josephine Balistreri), his devoted wife of 44 years. A Funeral Service was held in Chicago and a Memorial Mass was held in Los Angeles.
