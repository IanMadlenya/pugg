
        SOBEL -- Theodore (Ted), passed away on July 27, 2003 at the age of 84. Beloved husband of Claire Goldstein Sobel, loving father of Clifford and Barbara Sobel, Peter and Lizbeth Sobel and Wendy and Aaron Barr. Darling grandfather of Scott, Jonathan, Julie, Danielle and Taylor. Dear brother of Anne and Abe David and Gloria and Arthur Goldstein. He was a graduate of the University of Maine and a Major in World War II. Chapel services and interment were held on July 31, 2003. Donations in memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
