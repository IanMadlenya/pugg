
        TAYLOR-Sr. Eileen Mary (formerly Sister Mary Gaudentia). Died on November 24, 2000. Sister was a lifelong educator in the schools of the Archdiocese of New York as well as the New York City Public School System. She also volunteered at Jacobi Medical Center. Visiting Monday, 2-4 and 79 PM, at the Albert R. Conner-Colonial Funeral Home, 4955 Broadway (West 207 Street, NYC). Mass of Christian Burial, Tuesday, 10 AM, Good Shepherd R.C. Church. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Sisters of Mercy Retirement Fund, 150 Ridge Rd., Hartsdale, NY 10530 would be appreciated.
