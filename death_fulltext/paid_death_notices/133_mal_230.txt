
        THOMASHOWER-Marvin. Age 73, of Manhasset, NY, and Palm Beach, FL, on February 11, 1998, in Palm Beach, after a long illness bravely borne. Beloved husband of Rosalie Caplan Thomashower for 48 years, loving father of Laurie and her husband David Ridell, devoted grandfather of Lindsey and Marc Ridell, dear brother of Ruth Silverman and Sidney Thomashower. He worked in the Christmas tree light industry for 50 years and had not retired. His enduring fortitude and caring was exemplary, setting a standard few could hope to follow. He will be sorely missed and never forgotten by family and friends. Funeral services Sunday, February 15, 1998 at 12:00 noon at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, L.I. In lieu of flowers, donations to the PKR Foundation, 4901 Main Street, Suite 320, Kansas City, MO 64112 would be appreciated.
        THOMASHOWER-MARVIN. The officers and members of Temple Sinai of Roslyn record with profound sorrow the passing of Marvin Thomashower. Our condolences to the family. Rabbi Michael A. White Rabbi Norman Kahan, Emeritus Daniel S. Reich, President
