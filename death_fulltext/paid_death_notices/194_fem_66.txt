
        KALLMAN-Ruth. On Mar. 7, 2003, passed away peacefully at the age of 90. Wife of the late Mickey Kallman. Loving mother of Robert and Jay of Windham, NY. Sister of the late Danny Jacobs. Her memory will be cherished. She lived as she died, like a lady. Private services Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Queens, NY on Tuesday. Levine Memorial Chapel, Albany, New York.
