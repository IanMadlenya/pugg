
        SCHULDER-Jacob. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Jacob Schulder, a cherished friend and a generous supporter of our mission. He embraced philanthropic commitment to the Jewish community and was especially devoted to Israel. A beloved father and an adored grandfather, Mr. Schulder instilled in his family the traditions of ''tzedakah'' and involvement in humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathies to his son, Howard, and his wife, Marilyn; his daughter Sylvia Fisher and her husband, Bert; his daughter Diane Abrams and her husband, Robert; and to the entire Schulder family. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair & President-Elect John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
