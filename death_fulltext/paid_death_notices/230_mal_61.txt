
        PORTMAN--Bernard, 74. On March 2, 2006. Loving husband of Marythe. Beloved father of Pierre and his wife, Patricia, from Houston, TX. Proud grandfather of Amanda and Sarah. He is also survived by his sister, Phyllis, and brother-in-law Al Lerman. His career spanned over 50 years in the specialty art glass business, where he made innumerable friends both here in the U.S. and in every corner of the world. Graveside services will be held at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, Queens.
