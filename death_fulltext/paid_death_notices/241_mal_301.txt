
        SCHAECHTER--Mordkhe, Born December 1, 1927, died February 15, 2007. ''Mir hobn farloyrn undzer libn reb Motele Tshernevitser''. His devoted wife, Charne; children, Rukhl, Gitl, Eydl and Binyumen; 16 grandchildren, and sister Beyle all loved him and will continue to spread his passion for Yiddish and Yidishkayt. ''Zol er hobn a likhtikn ganeydn''. Memorial contributions may be made to the ''League for Yiddish''; or ''Yugntruf Youth for Yiddish'', both at 45 E. 33 Street, NY, NY 10016.
