
        GOTTESMAN--Susan Dena. Age 48. Passed away July 13, 2003 in Hartford, CT. Daughter of the late Dr. Abraham & Ruth Gottesman. Survived by her beloved daughter Rachel Russo, brother Gary & Jan Gottesman, step-daughters Taylor and Alexa Pratt, Lita Karp, Dr. Hal and Judy Gottesman, dear Karp and Gottesman cousins. Beloved leader in the local Jewish community. She continues to give life through organ donations. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the MS Society, or Temple B'nai Israel, Willimantic, CT.
