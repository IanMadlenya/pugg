
        SIDES-Fred H. Age 86. Died Monday, June 3, 2002 at home in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was born in Charlotte, N.C. and educated at Wake Forest, Davidson College and at UNC Chapel Hill. His business career spanned six decades, ranging from senior positions in transportation and printing, to executive recruiting and real estate development. He was an accomplished woodworker, photographer and outdoorsman, and a dedicated golfer who was a past president of the Bedford Golf and Tennis Club, and five times its Senior Golfing Champion. He is survived by his wife Louise, daughter Silvia, son and daughter-in-law Randolph and Tracey, his sister Mary Anne McCarley and grandson John Randolph Sides. Calling hours will be held at Cassidy-Flynn Funeral Home, Mount Kisco, Thursday, June 6th, 7-9PM. A funeral service will take place at Saint Mark's Church, Mount Kisco, Friday, June 7th, 4PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Salvation Army of Greater New York or the Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco.
