
        BIRKOFER-John R. The Members of The Board of Trustees of The Catholic Charities of The Archdiocese of New York record with heartfelt sympathy the death of John R. Birkofer. He served as a Member of our Board of Trustees for many years. We treasure greatly the inspiration of his leadership and the wisdom of his counsel. His loving service and his generous giving of himself and his talents have been an outstanding example of faith and love in action and have given new hope to families, children, the aged, the sick, the handicapped and all served by Catholic Charities. We thank God for his life with us and we express our sincere condolences to his family. John Cardinal O'Connor, President Rev. Msgr. James J. Murray, Secretary
