
        MENAKER--Martin. On November 8, 2004 at the age of 72 of complications from Parkinson's disease. Devoted and loving husband to Saundra. Cherished father of Melanie Seaman, Ellen Sansone and Lawrence Menaker. Father-in-law to Ed Seaman, Tony Sansone and Debra Menaker. Stepfather to Darryl and Shanon Blue, and Russell and Sarah Blue. Adored grandfather of Todd, Samantha, Bobby, Tara, Jennifer, Erica, Michael, Taylor, Phoebe, Jesse and Dylan. He will always be remembered as a caring and giving man with great courage, intelligence and an incredible wit. A service will be held on Wednesday, November 10, 2 P.M., Boulevard Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlitt, NY.
