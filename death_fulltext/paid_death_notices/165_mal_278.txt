
        JAY-Joseph. On October 20, 2000 at age 96. Devoted husband of the late Ray, cherished father of Dr. Burton Jay, Dr. Allen Jay, Marlene Jay, Elaine Jay and Dr. Judith Jay. Beloved father-in-law of Susan Jay, Ricky Jay and Dr. Barry Shapiro. Loving grandfather of Michael and Mary, Daniel and Noelle, Deborah and Dr. David Levy, Jonathan, Kenneth, Rachel Shapiro and Robin Shapiro. Adoring great-grandfather of Sarah, Joshua and Jordan Levy. Member of the New York Bar for over 50 years, practicing attorney and partner at the firm of Gilbert & Gilbert, Manhattan. Honorary Life Member of the Knights of Pythias, Lewton Lodge and active participant in Jewish philanthropies, Oceanside Jewish Center and the Democratic Party. A self-made man whose high ethical and moral standards shaped his family and remain as his legacy. Services Monday, October 23, 10 AM at Gutterman's, Woodbury, L.I., 516921-5757. Contributions may be made to UJA-Federation.
