
        GOLDBERG-Louis, age 89. AO, M. Com, Litt. D, Professor Emeritus, Melbourne University, Australia, died peacefully at home in Melbourne on October 18, 1997. Beloved father of Loretta, New York, cherished husband of Jean, widower of Myrtle. A scholar and gentleman, he influenced several generations of accountants in Australia and internationally by his published theoretical writings and devoted teaching. In the U.S. his visiting academic positions included Baruch School of Business in New York and the University of Florida. His kindness, humor, and empathetic curiosity will be deeply missed.
