
        ROSENWALD-Elsa (nee Jacob). Age 90. Died January 6, 2001 at home in Rye, N.Y. Wife of the late Joseph S. Rosenwald and beloved mother of Ellen Gardiner and Peter Rosenwald, she is survived by her two children and five grandchildren. A dedicated mother and grandmother, she lived at the Osborne Retirement Community in Rye and never lost her strong dedication to independence and generosity. She will be mourned by her family and many friends. A private cremation will be held Friday, January 12th and a memorial service will be held at a later date. Donations in her rememberance should be sent to the Corporate Angel Network, Westchester County Airport, 1 Loop Rd., White Plains, NY 10604, 914-328-1313.
