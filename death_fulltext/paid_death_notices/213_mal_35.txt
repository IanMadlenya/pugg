
        EDISON--Robert. Dear brother of Suzanne Fields and brother-in-law of Stephen Fields, a much loved uncle who truly appreciated the sweet caring of his nieces and nephews during his time of need. Bob's legacy to us is that of goodness, integrity, wit and thoughtfulness. A writer (Playbill), a pen collector (Pen World), an antique lover and friend to many. How we shall miss him. Services private.
