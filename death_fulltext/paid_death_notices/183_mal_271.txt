
        STERNHEIM-Howard D. At age 70. Beloved husband of Sharon. Devoted father to Deborah, Patti and Robert. Loving grandfather to Allison, Erika, Ashley, Rachel and Alana. An esteemed businessman & a man of tremendous integrity, humor and courage, he died on April 22nd after a long battle with chronic myeloid leukemia. He was respected and adored by all who had the privilege to know him. The funeral service will be held on Tuesday, April 23rd, 1:00 P.M. at Riverside Memorial Chapel (76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue). Contributions can be made in his honor to the College of Pharmacy, Long Island University, 75 DeKalb Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. 11201.
        STERNHEIM-Howard. My longest known friend, I will miss you. His knowledge of merchandising has earned him to be a respected icon. He will be missed always and remembered. Sheldon L. Beil
        STERNHEIM-Howard. Our heartfelt condolences to Sharon and the entire family. Howie never lost faith, or his sense of humor. We will miss a true friend. Sandie, Shelly, Leslie and Dina
