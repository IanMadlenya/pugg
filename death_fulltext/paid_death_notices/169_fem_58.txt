
        HANDLER - Muriel (Dottie) Meiselman, 81, of Boca Raton, FL. Passed away Sunday, February 4, 2001. Predeceased by her first husband, Sanford Meiselman. Beloved wife of Abraham Handler, cherished mother of Jay and Kenneth Meiselman and Mark Phillips, loving grandmother of six, dear sister of Florence Weinrib. Services were held in Florida. Contributions may be made to Hospice by the Sea in Boca Raton or the American Cancer Society.
