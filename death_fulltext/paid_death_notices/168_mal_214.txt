
        VICENTE-Esteban. The Board of Trustees, Faculty, Students and Staff, both past and present, at The New York Studio School, mourn the passing of this great man who touched so many of our lives, and continues to do so through the legacy of his paintings. A memorial service will be announced in the spring for the benefit of his numerous friends, admirers, collectors, and students. He will be deeply missed. Please note a current exhibiton of his prints will be on through February 24 at 8 West 8th Street, NY. Graham Nickson, Dean
