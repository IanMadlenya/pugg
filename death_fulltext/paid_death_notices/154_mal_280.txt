
        WILLIAMS-Edward ''NIK'' James. November 17, 1999, at age 50. Leader and innovator in the global art movement. In his most recent endeavor, he conceived of and was leading the development of the Beecher Center for Art and Technology at the Butler Museum in Youngstown, Ohio. He is survived by his brother, Richard R. Williams, two nieces, Sarah and Jennifer, a nephew, Seth, and life partner Arleen Schloss AKA A. Renegade. Interment private.
