
        O'NEILL-James C. Of Rye, New York, died peacefully on Wednesday, September 4, 2002. He is survived by his wife, Rosaleen Doyle; his daughters and grandchildren. He is predeceased by his son who was killed on September 11, 2001. Family will receive friends today from 5-8 PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Road, Rye, New York. Funeral mass is Saturday, September 7th, at 11 A.M., at the Church of the Resurrection, Rye, N.Y. Interment will be private.
