
        RADOMSKY--Beatrice. Born in Baltimore, MD, December 1920, died September 4th in NY. Very dear beloved wife and best friend of 65 years to Joseph, mother of Hedda, Jeffrey B. Feignblatt and Cindy Watts. Grandmother to Ryan, Susan, and Alan. Greatgrandmother to Liam and Alex. She was friend to family and family to friends. A very extra-special lady who gave tirelessly and totally of herself to others. She fought courageously to the bitter end.
