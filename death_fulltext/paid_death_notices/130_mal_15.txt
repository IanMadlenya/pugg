
        PESKIN-Henry. A graduate of the Ethical Culture School, he earned his B.A. and M.D. from Columbia University. A Fellow of the New York Academy of Medicine, International College of Surgeons and the American Proctologic Society, he was affiliated with Beth Israel, Flower Fifth, Montefiore, Beekman Downtown and Mount Sinai. He is survived by two daughters and two grandaughters. His wife, Hildegarde, died in 1995. Contributions may be sent in his name to The Workmen's Circle-Medical Department, 45 East 33 Street, New York City 10016.
