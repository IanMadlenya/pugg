
        COREY-Alan Lyle, Jr. Died suddenly August 24. He is survived by his loving wife Patricia Grace. His children Patricia Corey Montgomerie, Alan L. Corey III and Russell G. Corey, eight granchildren and one great granddaughter. He was a member of the Meadowbrook Club, Piping Rock Club and the New York Racquet & Tennis Club. The service will be private. Contributions in his memory to The Center for Developmental Disabilities 72 Southwoods Rd, Woodbury, NY 11797-1099 or Palomine Riding for the Disabled 2294 Willoughby Ave, Seaford, NY 11783 would be appreciated.
