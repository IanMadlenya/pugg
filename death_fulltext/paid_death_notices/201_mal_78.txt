
        HALPERN--John S. Of New York City died October 8th after a brave and valiant battle. Husband to Kate, father to Duncan, Alison and Cynthia, grandfather to Hope and Eva, friend to many. He will be missed greatly for among other things, his humor, wisdom and passion. In lieu of flowers please make donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. Fly swiftly John. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, Monday, October 13 at 3:30.
        HALPERN--John S. The Board of Directors of the Bridgehampton Chamber Music Festival sadly mourns the loss of our loyal supporter and great friend. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his daughters Cynthia and Alison, his step-son Duncan and his grandchildren Hope and Eva. Marya Martin, Artistic Dir. Artistic Director Ken Davidson, Chairman
        HALPERN--John S. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the passing of John Halpern, fellow member and golfer. We extend our sincere condolences and heartfelt sympathy to his children Cynthia, Alison & his stepson Duncan Foster. Robert E. Morrow, President Harvey L. Radler, Secretary
