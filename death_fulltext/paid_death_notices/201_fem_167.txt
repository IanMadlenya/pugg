
        GOLDSTEIN--Jill, of NYC, on Tuesday, October 28, 2003. Beloved daughter of the late Lillian Goldstein Krulewitz and the late Solomon Goldstein. Loving sister of Sylvia Kaplan and her husband Bernard, Doris Goren and Daniel Krulewitz. She was associate editor of Esquire Magazine, executive editor of Viva Magazine, and senior editor of the Village Voice. She earned a BS degree from Simmons College, Boston, MA. She was an active member of SHARE, an ovarian and breast cancer support group. Services 10am Thursday, October 30, 2003, at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway, Fair Lawn, NJ.
