
        RACKMAN -- Honey. We mourn the untimely passing of our founding Board Member, Honey Rackman. A woman of great strength and courage, Honey was a committed advocate for agunot, and passionate in her zeal to remedy their plight. She will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Carol Kaufman Newman, President Robin Bodner, Exec Director Board of Directors, Jewish Orthodox Feminist Alliance
