
        BERGER-Phil. 58. Screenwriter and author, died at home on Monday, March 12, 2001, after a valiant battle with colon cancer. He will always be the champion of our hearts. Phil leaves behind his daughter, Julia, his life partner, Veronica Vera, mother, Fanny Berger, his sister, Cynthia Darling, friend, Michael Rakosi, and the Saturdays at Eight basketball team. For further information: Schwartz Brothers, 718-263-7600.
