
        RODESCU-Dorothea (Mami). December 18, 1922, Dorohoi, Romania-June 8, 1998, Philadelphia. Treasured mother of Alina and Julian, adoring grandmother of Benjamin, loving sister of Marcel, cherished mother-in-law of Barbara, esteemed physician & teacher at Albert Einstein College of Medicine, beloved and loyal friend to many. She imparted love, wisdom and strength to all who knew her, and she will be deeply, deeply missed. Services will be held at The Riverside, 76 and Amsterdam Ave., Wednesday, June 10, at 12 Noon. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to: Giant Steps, C/O AIND, (make checks payable to AIND), 1275 Post Road, Suite A3, Fairfield, CT., 06430.
