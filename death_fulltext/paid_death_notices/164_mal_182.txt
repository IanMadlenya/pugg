
        JOHNSON-James Ford, 3rd. 82, of Somers, NY, formerly of Manhattan, died on September 4, 2000. Graduate of the Hill School and Yale University. He served as a captain in the U.S. Army in WW II, seeing action on Omaha Beach. He was a marketing executive for Texaco and Caltex, where he spent several years in Europe. Husband of 60 years of Elaine O. Johnson. Father of James F. Johnson, 4th, of London, England. Grandfather of Christopher and Alexander. Memorial service at St. Luke's Episcopal Church, Somers, NY on Saturday, September 9, at 12:00 noon. Contributions in his memory may be made to St. Luke's Church Memorial Garden, Somers, New York 10589.
