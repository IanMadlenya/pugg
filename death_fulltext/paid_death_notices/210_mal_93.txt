
        DORF -- Charles B. The trustees and faculty of Poly Prep Country Day School mourn the passing of Charles B. Dorf, beloved alumnus of the class of 1948 and trustee emeritus. Mr. Dorf served as a Poly Prep trustee from 1982 to 1998. He was also the recipient of a 2003 Distinguished Achievement Award, given by the Poly Prep Alumni Board of Governors for professional accomplish ment. We are deeply saddened and extend condolences to Mr. Dorf's wife, Marilou, his sons Robert and James and his daughter-inlaw, Jodie. Donald Carswell, Chairman David Harman, Headmaster Poly Prep Country Day School, Brooklyn, NY
