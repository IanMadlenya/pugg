        WOLF-Robert E., M.D. The entire Long Island College Hospital family mourns the untimely passing of Dr. Robert E. Wolf and extends condolences to his beloved wife, Syma, daughter, Shari, and extended family. Dr. Wolf was a valued member of LICH for more than 35 years, serving most recently as senior attending cardiologist and member of the Board of Regents. After graduating from SUNY Downstate Medical Center in 1961, he completed his internship, residency and fellowship training at LICH. From 1987 to 1990 he was president of the LICH Medical Staff and was voted Volunteer Teacher of the Year four times by residents he taught. His patients, students and colleagues valued his compassion, wise counsel, clinical expertise and thorough knowledge of his specialty in cardiovascular disease. He will be missed by everyone who knew him. John Wren, Chairman, Board of Regents Daniel D. Ricciardi, M.D., President, Medical Staff Peter Kelly, Interim President, LICH
        WOLF-Robert. The staff of the Department of Medicine of The Long Island College Hospital deeply regrets the loss of their esteemed colleague Dr. Robert Wolf. He was a loyal and devoted teacher and humanitarian who will be sorely missed by students, housestaff and patients. His love of the hospital is matched by our love for him. Our sympathies are extended to his wife, daughter, and brother. Frank DiPillo, MD Chairman Department of Internal Medicine
