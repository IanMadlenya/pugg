
        STERN--Dinah Sand. Born May 27, 1908 in New York City. Daughter of the late Jacob and Anna Sand. She was the devoted wife of the late Abner Stern, beloved mother of Elinor Gollay, adored sister of Miriam Sand Siffert and the late Harry and Joseph Sand. Dinah was a creative, dedicated and energetic educator. She was particularly innovative in the development of programs in reading techniques. She worked throughout the City for The New York Public Schools as a teacher and principal. She died in St. Petersburg Florida on October 15, 2003. Services will be private.
