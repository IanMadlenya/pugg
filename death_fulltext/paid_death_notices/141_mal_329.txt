
        DANCIS-Mordecai. Born Dec. 11, 1911 in Bayonne, NJ, died October 26, 1998, Palm Beach County, FL. Successful New York lawyer who devoted his best years to politics within the Democratic Party; first as leader of Great Neck where he resided for some 40 years, then as leader of the Township of North Hempstead. He is survived by two sisters Natalie Goldberg & Ranana Benjamin, brother Joseph Dancis, twin daughters Amy Laborde and Barbara Gholz and four grandchildren Eugene & Abigail Gholz and Linda and Pascal Laborde. A Memorial Service will be held Monday, November 9 at 4:30PM at North Shore Towers, Building Two, 270 Grand Central Parkway, Floral Park, New York.
