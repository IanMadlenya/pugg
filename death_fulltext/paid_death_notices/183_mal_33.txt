
        HIEBER-William G., Jr. 65. Of Garden City and New York City, esteemed and respected stockbroker, financial advisor, and lawyer, died on March 31st, 2002. CFA, President of Shoenberg, Hieber Inc. Graduate of Colgate University, Phi Beta Kappa, and Harvard Law School. A devoted friend, loved by so many, and a beloved husband and father. Survived by his loving wife Jean and daughters Jennifer and Christina. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: The Hieber Family Scholarship, In Memory of William G. Hieber, Jr. Class of 1958, Colgate University, Attention: Patricia Caprio, 13 Oak Drive, Hamilton, New York 13346. Memorial service is planned for a later date.
