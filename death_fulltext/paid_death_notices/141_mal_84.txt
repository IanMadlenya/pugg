
        HURWITZ-Jerome, Dr. The Jewish Braille Institute of America, rocords with sorrow the loss of a much loved honorary Life Trustee and former officer. His devotion over many years helped to create new opportunities for the blind and visually impaired worldwide. His essential involvement in the establishment of the JBI Low Vision Clinic at the Sourasky TelAviv Medical Center, brought a higher quality of low vision care to Israel. To his devoted wife Hortense, a trustee of our agency, and his family, we extend profound sympathy sure in the knowledge that his memory will serve as a blessing. Barbara B. Friedman, Acting Pres. Dr. Jane Evans, Honorary Pres. Gerald M. Kass, Exec Vice Pres.
