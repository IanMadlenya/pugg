
        HARWOOD--Dr. Ruth, 89, of NYC, Died on October 5, 2006. She Received her Masters & PhD from Columbia University in anthropology, and was a professor at Long Island University for over 30 years. She was an avid bird watcher & a dedicated volunteer at the American Museum of Natural History. Survived by five nephews & one niece. A memorial gathering will be held on Friday, October 27, from 1-4 PM in the Thurgood Marshall room of Morningside Gardens, 80 LaSalle St., NYC. Burial, in Rural Cemetery, Southboro, MA. Memorial contributions may be sent to American Museum of Natural History, Central Park West at 79th St., NY, NY 10024 att: development.
