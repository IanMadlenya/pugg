
        HARRIMAN-Ambassador Pamela. On Wednesday, February 5, 1997, Ambassador Pamela Harriman died in Paris, France; widow of Governor W. Averell Harriman, and mother of the Honorable Winston S. Churchill, M.P. She is also survived by grandchildren and great grandchildren. Funeral services will be conducted Thursday, February 13, at 10 AM at the Washington Cathedral, Wisconsin and Massachusetts Avenues, N.W., Washington, D.C. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Pamela Harriman Memorial Fund, C/O The Office of the President, The College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, Virginia 23185.
