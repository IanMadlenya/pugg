
        GINSBERG--Jerome, 94, on April 8, 2007. Founder of Magna Book Distributors, decorated veteran of WWII, longtime champion of social causes. Remembered to the end for his ready wit and love of sports. Survived by son Robert, daughter-in-law Cynthia, granddaughter Rachel Beth, sister Rosalie, brotherin-law Henry and many loving nieces and nephews. Predeceased by wife Blanche and son Richard. Services to be held on April 11 at 11:00 am, New Mount Carmel Cemetery.
