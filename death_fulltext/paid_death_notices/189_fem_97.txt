
        LIST-Vera Glaser. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Vera List, a cherished friend of the Jewish community. Vera and her late husband, Albert, expressed their devotion to social and humanitarian causes through their exceptional philanthropy. We extend our deepest sympathies to her beloved daughters, Carol, Olga, and Viki, and to the entire List family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
