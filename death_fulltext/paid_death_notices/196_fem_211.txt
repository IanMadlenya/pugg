
        COOK -- Muriel Muller. A Stamford resident, died Tuesday, May 20 at the Hospice Residence in Stamford. She was 83. She died of natural causes, according to her family. Born December 29, 1919 in Woodhaven, N.Y., she was the daughter of the late Adam Charles and Margaret Dircks Muller. Mrs. Cook graduated from Katherine Gibbs College. She worked for General American Investors, Inc., a closed end investment company traded on the New York Stock Exchange for twenty-two years. She retired as the Assistant Corporate Secretary and Treasurer of the Company in 1989. Mrs. Cook was a world traveler, visiting more than forty-six countries with her husband, daughter and friends. She was a avid gardener and enjoyed playing golf. Before moving to Stamford in November, 2002, she was a resident of New Hyde Park, N.Y. for fifty-four years. She was predeceased by her husband Godfrey V. (Buddy) Cook, Jr. She is survived by her only daughter, Patricia M. (Tricia) Cook of Stamford, a brother Martin H. Muller of Stamford and several nieces, nephews and cousins. Calling hours will be Thursday, May 29 from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9PM at the R. Stutzmann & Son Funeral Home, 2000 Hillside Avenue, New Hyde Park, N.Y. 11040. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Friday, May 30 at 10:30AM at Holy Spirit Church in New Hyde Park, N.Y. Burial will follow in Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, N.Y. In lieu of flowers memmorial contributions may be made to St. Francis Breadline, 135 West 31 St, New York, New York 10001, or the New Covenant House of Hospitality, 90 Fairfield Avenue, Stamford, CT. 06902. >>AD#
