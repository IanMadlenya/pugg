
        KILLERLANE-Madeline C., 86. Formerly of Brooklyn. Passed away on April 15, 2003. at her home in White Plains, NY. Madeline was the much loved wife of the late James. She was the dear mother to Rose and James. The Beloved grandmother of Christina and James III. Madeline was retired from the Brooklyn Public Library. Visiting Wednesday and Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 at McMahon, Lyon & Hartnett Funeral Home, White Plains. Funeral blessing Friday 10 AM at Our Lady of Sorrows Church.
