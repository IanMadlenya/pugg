
        LIEBERMAN-Dr. James, Assistant Surgeon General, U.S. Public Health Service, Retired & former Director of Health of Greenwich, CT, died peacefully on September 23, 2001. He was much loved by his family, many friends and the communities he served. Survivors include his wife Lucille Lieberman, daughter Meg Lieberman and her husband, Michael Gold, and three grandchildren; Amanda, Jeremiah & David Gold. Services Tuesday September 25 at 11:00 AM from Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, 88 Beach Road, Fairfield, CT, with interment at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York. Memorial contributions may be made to AMERICARES.
