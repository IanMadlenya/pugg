
        BERNSTEIN--Evelyn Stieber. On April 6, 2005, in Florida. It is with profound sadness that we mourn the loss of our beloved mother, our Sephardi Queen. She was the adored wife of the late Otto Stieber and Sol Bernstein. Her loving children, Frances (Donald Putnoi), Elaine Cory and Janice Rous, her devoted grandchildren, Deborah and Eric Putnoi (Debbie), Josh (Rebecca), Victoria, Eli, Anya and her great-grandchildren, Olivia, Will, Talia and Joia will all miss her wisdom and generosity. Services Thursday, 11:30 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be made to YEDID, 301 East 69 Street, Suite 17F, NYC 10021.
