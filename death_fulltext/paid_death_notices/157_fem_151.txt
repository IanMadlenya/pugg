
        WHITING-Jane (nee Semple), 73, of Heritage Village, Southbury, formerly of Greenwich, CT, died on Wednesday, February 9th in Antibes, France. She is survived by her husband, Royal, of the home address, two daughters, Emily Kane of Seattle, WA, and Sarah Jenkenson of Wexford, PA. Another daughter, Susan, predeceased her. She is also survived by two brothers, Lorenzo Semple of N.Y.C. and Santa Monica, CA, Churchill Semple of West Sussex, England, and a sister, Katharine Hirschberg of Old Greenwich. Funeral arrangements have not been completed.
