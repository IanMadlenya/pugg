
        GOLDSTEIN-Melvin S. 82, died at home in New York on January 11, 2002. International Director of State of Israel Bonds 1961-1995; Administrative Vice Chairman of the National United Jewish Appeal 1951-1961; Assistant Secretary General of the Joint Distribution Committee in Europe 1941-1951. He is survived by his wife of 60 years, Lolita Eschborn Goldstein, brother and sister-in-law, Jerome and Adele, sister and brother-inlaw, Eileen and Milton Putterman, and numerous nieces and nephews. Service Sunday 11:45 AM, ''The Riverside,'' 76th St at Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
