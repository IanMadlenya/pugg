
        LYNN-Gay (Stirling) of Newport, Rhode Island, and Scotland Cay, Abaco, Bahamas passed away Tuesday, July 25 in Boston. She was 63. Widow of William Harcourt Lynn, beloved mother of William and Margaret, grandmother of three, longtime resident of Rye, NY. An avid sailing racer, she pioneered for accessibility for the disabled in the sport of sailing and was one of the first women to be recognized as an International Sailing Judge and Umpire. In lieu of flowers please make a memorial contribution in her name to: U.S. Special Olympics, Sailing-Donor Development, 1325 G StreetSuite 500, Washington, DC, 20005.
