
        HARRIS--Richard A. Age 88. On September 16, 2006, of Hackensack, NJ. Beloved husband of Evelyn (nee Sirotin). Devoted father of Peggy, Lynn and Liz. Loving grandfather of Jennifer Backer, Megan and Ryan Hardiman, Justin and Molly Schaeffer. Great-grandfather of Garrett and Nina Backer. Mr. Harris spent his entire career at Macy's, rising to Senior Vice President and Director of Bamberger's, a division of R.H. Macy and Co. He was a WWII Navy veteran. Services Monday, 11:30 AM, Gutterman and Musicant Funeral Home, Hackensack, NJ. For information 800-522-0588.
