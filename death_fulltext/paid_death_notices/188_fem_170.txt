
        PETERSEN-Joan Carol, lifelong NYC resident, architect and teacher. The consummate lady, tall, elegant, witty and worldly with a marvelous sense of joie de vivre, succumbed on September 21, 2002, after a brief and dramatic battle with cancer. She will be greatly missed by her family and legion of friends. Special love and appreciation from her niece Ann Beaulieu and her boys in Minnesota, for the wonderful part she played in their lives. Memorial services will be held at 8PM Thursday, September 26 at the Subud Center, 230 West 29th Street, Manhattan.
        PETERSEN-Joan. A dear colleague and friend: a fond remembrance on your memorial day. Bill Novak
