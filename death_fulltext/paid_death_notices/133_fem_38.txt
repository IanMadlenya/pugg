
        SCHIFF-Hannah Goodman, 97, on February 3 at Jewish Home and Hospital, formerly of Woodmere, NY, widow of David M. Schiff, died 1976. She will be lovingly remembered by her daughters Ellyn Berman and Sheila Schwartz, her sons-in-law Benjamin Berman and Irving Schwartz and her grandchildren Suellen Carman, Sally Lang, John Berman, Jessica Berman and Paul Berman. She is also survived by one sister Esther Schiff of Rockville, MD. We acknowledge with appreciation the loyal care given by Debbie Mengrone during her last years.
