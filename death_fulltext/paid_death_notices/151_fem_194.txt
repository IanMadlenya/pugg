
        BROADHURST-Anne Truesdale, age 79 of Wilton, CT on August 17th. Past resident of New York and Long Island. Former wife of the late John C. Wood and the late William J. Broadhurst. Beloved mother of Lucy Voorhees Brotman and Sally Wood Post and grandmother of Coerte, Pieter and Sally Voorhees and Alex and Tucker Post. Following her wishes her ashes will be scattered in the Adirondack Mountains and there will be a memorial service at the Truesdale family reunion in summer 2000.
