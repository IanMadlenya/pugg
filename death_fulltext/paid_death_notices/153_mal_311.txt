
        ETKIN-George, died October 15, 1999 at 89 years of age. He was the widower of Sylvia Nash Etkin. He graduated from City College in NY and then received a law degree from Fordham University. He was a petty officer first class in the United States Navy during WW II. After the war Mr. Etkin worked as legal counsel for Dumont Laboratories, a pioneer in TV broadcasting, and then for Metromedia. George and Sylvia moved to Sarasota in 1983. He was an ardent supporter of Israel, youth tennis and a lover of classical music. He is survived by his two sons, Thomas and Peter and six grandchildren. Services were held in Sarasota.
