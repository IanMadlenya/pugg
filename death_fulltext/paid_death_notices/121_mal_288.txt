
        ROBINSOHN-Jacob, on February 21st, 1997. Devoted husband of the late Ruth Maier Robinsohn and brother of Mendel. Trained in engineering at Columbia University and in law at New York University. For over sixty years, he was an attorney for the New York City Corporation Counsel's Office and a tenacious advocate of the city's interests. He was a founder and faithful supporter of numerous organizations devoted to the environment, animal welfare and family issues. Services today, 1:30 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
