
        HUROWITZ--Susan. The officers, staff and membership of New York Electrical Contractors Association mourns the passing of Susan Hurowitz, wife of Monte Hurowitz, our longtime friend and associate. We extend our deepest sympathy to the entire family. Howard Hirsch, President Joseph D'Angelo, Exec. Secy.
        HUROWITZ--Susan. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the wife of our dear member, Mr. Monte Hurowitz. May his entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion & Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
        HUROWITZ--Susan. The Board of Directors expresses its condolences upon the passing of their neighbor, Susan Hurowitz, wife of honorary Board member, Monte M. Hurowitz. Irwin Engelman on behalf of the Board of Directors
