
        RINGLER--Dr. Milton E. Of Woodmere, NY, November 4, 2005. He was 89. Survived by his loving and beloved wife of 62 years, Diane. Adoring father of Robin and Cathy (Philip), extraordinary grandfather to Todd, Brad (Marianna), Daniel, Jamie, Drew and Dylan. His sweetness and generosity of heart will be with us forever. He was a graduate of NYU College of Dentistry, practiced in the community for over 50 years. Services Sunday, November 6, 2 PM at Boulevard Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY.
