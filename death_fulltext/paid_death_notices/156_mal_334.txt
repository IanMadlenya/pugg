
        EASTMOND - Maurice. Age eighty-four. On Jan. 16, 2000. Beloved husband of Christine. Loving father of Carolyn. Cherished father-in-law of Douglas. Devoted grandfather of Jennifer. Dear brother of Ferdinand. Graduate of Brooklyn College and Columbia University. For over thirty-five years served Brooklyn College as Professor of English, Assistant Dean of Academic Advisement & Associate Dean of The School of Humanities. A memorial service will be announced.
        EASTMOND-Maurice. The Board of Directors and staff of Sanctuary for Families mourn, with heartfelt sorrow, the passing of Maurice Eastmond, father of our esteemed Executive Director, Carolyn Nash. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Christine, daughter Carolyn, son-in-law Douglas and granddaughter Jennifer.
