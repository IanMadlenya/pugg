
        LOEWENGART--Gerda. The Auxiliary of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute records with deep sorrow the passing of Gerda Loewengart, a dedicated member for many years. She served with great distinction and was admired by all who knew her. She will be sorely missed. Sue Chapro, Ginny Samuels and Elly Hammerman Co-Presidents
