
        SEMEL--Hilda L. On Nov. 1, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Simon. Devoted sister of the late Murray Levins, Nettie Levins, Anne Levine and Rose Levins. She is survived by her loving nephew David Levins, niece Maxine Hirsch and Dr. Joel Hirsch & great-nephew Jeremy Hirsch. Her love and kindness to her family will never be forgotten. She will live in our hearts forever. Services private.
