
        ROSENBERG-Stanley L., at age 83, peacefully at home on August 3, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Lee Cohen Rosenberg. He is sadly missed by his loving and devoted daughter Maddi-Jane Sobel and his son-in-law David and grandchildren Elana Stern and Rebecca Sobel, his loving and admiring son Jonathan and his daughter-in-law Irit and grandchildren Erik and Jodie, his loving and devoted son Neal and his son Daniel and grandchildren Katherine and Paul. He was the founder and chairman of Telectro Industries and the En-Tech Corporation. In his memory, contributions may be made to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation or the Great Neck Chapter of Hadassah.
