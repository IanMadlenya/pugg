
        KOEHLER-Edwin H., 98, a New York City Retail Merchant and Investment Banker, died Monday, December 7, 1998 at Mount Sinai Hospital, NYC. A graduate of Massachussets Institute of Technology, he held numerous positions of managership and ownership in various retail operations and towards the later years of his life, he was heavily involved in Investment Banking. He served in World War I, and lived in New York City and Oxford, Connecticut. He was a member of long standing at Temple Emanuel in NYC. His wife of 55 years, Phyllis Beer Koehler, died in 1978. He is survived by a sister, Carol Pforzheimer, a son Philip J. Koehler, four grandchildren, and seven great grandsons. A memorial service will be held at the Campbell Funeral Home, located between 81st and 82nd Streets on Madison Ave, NYC at 11:30AM on Monday, December 14. The family suggests memorial contributions to Temple Emanuel, 1 East 65th Street, NYC 10021.
