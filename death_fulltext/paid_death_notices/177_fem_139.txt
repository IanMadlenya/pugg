
        BROWN-Mary Thacher. Age 90. Died peacefully on October 13, 2001. Preceded in death by her husband Daniel N. Brown. Loving mother of Cynthia B. Lloyd, grandmother of Emily D. Lloyd, sister of Thomas Thacher and the late Sarah T. Storm, and beloved aunt and friend to many. Mary Brown was an artist and naturalist who actively supported environmental and humanitarian causes. Memorial service October 23 at 11:30 at The First Presbyterian Church of New Canaan, New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers, send contributions in her memory to Noble Horizons, 17 Cobble Road, Salisbury, CT 06068.
