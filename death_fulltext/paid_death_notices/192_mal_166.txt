
        HOFFMAN-Charles H., 86. Of Larchmont, NY and Delray Beach, FL. With sadness we mourn his passing on January 13th, 2003. Husband of Rowena and the late Evelyn. Father of Bruce (Perry) and Mark. Grandfather of Brendan (Abby), Rennie (Steven) and David. Great-grandfather of Matthew. Stepfather of Linda and David. Graduate of Wharton, U. Penn. W.W. II veteran, Lieutenant, Army Intelligence. Member of American Stock Exchange (Curb) and the NYSE for 50 years. Retired partner of Spear, Leeds and Kellogg.
