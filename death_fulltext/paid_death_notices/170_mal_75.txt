
        GREEN-Julius B. The staff and Steering Committee of the New York Work Exchange and the staff and members of the Coalition of Voluntary Mental Health Agencies are deeply saddened by the passing of Julius B. Green, Assistant Director of Howie T. Harp Training Program. Julius will be remembered as a leader in the peer movements, a tireless advocate and an inspiration to all. He will be missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and extended family at Howie T. Harp. Phillip A. Saperia, Executive Director
