
        LACHMANN--Erich, ''Mickey'', 74, died January 28, 2007 in NYC. President of Turnpike Super Service, Middletown, NY. Former Fire Coordinator of Orange County, NY. Graduate of Muhlenberg College, 1954. Survived by his wife Christa-Louise (Vollmer) Lachmann, son Erich E. Lachmann of Middletown, daughter Elisabeth Lachmann, M.D. and grandchildren Lars, and Elisabeth Hunt. Memorial service to be announced. Please consider a donation in his memory to the Weill-Cornell Medical College, 1300 York Ave, NY, NY 10021.
