
        RICHARDSON-Willoughby F. Jr. December 28, 1998. Son of the late Willoughby F. and Alice (Shearer) Richardson, he was, until 1994, a lifelong resident of Scarsdale, New York, and was currently residing at the Madison House, Madison, CT. Father of Willoughby F. Richardson, III, Shearer Richardson, Roxannah Gallagher and Susan Horton and six grandchildren. He was predeceased by a son, Allen Richardson. Services will be held at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, New York at 11:00 AM, Thursday, December 31st. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to a local Hospice Care facility.
