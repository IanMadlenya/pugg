
        FINK--Dr. Austin I., on Sunday April 24, 2005. Prominent physician and internationally recognized ophthalmologist; Professor at SUNY Downstate 1955-1990, chief of Ophthalmology at Long Island College Hospital 1958-1990. Survived by his loving wife, Selma; children Jean (husband Don), Anne (husband Cristian), and Meg (husband Yale). Adored grandfather of Liz, Tory, Alexandra, William, Dani, Lani, Doug, Caroline, and Robin. Memorial service Wednesday, April 27, 1:00 PM at the Brooklyn Heights Synagogue, 131 Remsen Street, Brooklyn, 718-522-2070.
