
        MONACCI-Lando E. 96. Resident of Flushing for 76 years. Passed away April 8, 2000. Born February 10, 1904, in NY, NY. Lando graduated from Cooper Union College, NYC, Class of 1928. He was an electrical engineer employed by Consolidated Edison for 45 years retiring in 1969 as a Supervising Engineer for the Inside Plant Bureau. Lando was a member of American Institute of Electrical Engineers and was a New York Professional Engineer. Lando always found his greatest joys in family activities. He is predeceased by his wife of 48 years Rose P., son Thomas O., and brother Emil. He is survived by his daughter Adele M. Henrick of Oakland, ME, son Robert W. of Yardley, PA, daughter-in-law Lucy of Bayonne, NJ, six grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Reposing at the Gleason Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd., Flushing, NY, Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Mass St. Andrew Avellino R.C. Church, Monday, 10:15 AM. Interment Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery.
