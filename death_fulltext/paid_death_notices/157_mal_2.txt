
        FREEDMAN-Kenneth M.. 72, formerly of White Meadow Lake, NJ, died peacefully in his home January 26 surrounded by his family. He is survived by beloved wife Sarah, children Ellen Carron, Karen Quigley, Eric and Nancy Freedman, grandchildren Benjamin, Rebecca & Michael Carron, and Kadyrose & Julia Quigley, and sons-in-law Barry Carron & Christopher Quigley. Born in Chicago, IL, he graduated from the Univ. of Illinois and served in the U.S. Navy during WWII. His successful business career included exec. positions at Revlon, American Home Products & President of Popular Services, Inc. He'll be fondly remembered as a loving and devoted husband, father and grandfather as well as a generous friend and community leader, supporting many causes focused on helping the Jewish community.
