
        CANTWELL-Vincent F., 65, 4/16/ 97. Survived by wife Margaret Vonk Cantwell, children Vincent Cantwell, M. Grace Cantwell, Maj. Gregory Cantwell, Katherine Rabago, Richard Cantwell and grandchildren. Dr. Cantwell (Edd., Columbia '91) was Administrator of Englewood, NJ public schools, '72-'93. His life was an unending quest for knowledge and wisdom that inspired countless children and adults. We celebrate his love and are grateful to have been with him on his heroic journey. Donations to Boca Raton Community Hospital are appreciated. (Payable to BRCH Foundation, 745 Meadows Rd. Boca Raton, FL 33486).
