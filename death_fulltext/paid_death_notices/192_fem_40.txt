
        SCHLEIFER - Florence. 83. Died January 1, 2003. Beloved wife of Charles B. Schleifer for fifty-four years. Devoted and loving mother of Dr. Lawrence M. Schleifer and Dr. Leonard S. Schleifer (Harriet), grandmother of Adam and David, sister of Milton (Rhoda) and Robert (Letty) Schacht, aunt, and dear friend. She will be deeply missed.
