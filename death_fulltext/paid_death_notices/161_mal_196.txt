
        MERIN-Herbert L., passed away on June 15th, 2000 at his residence in Palm Beach after a long illness. Beloved husband of Rita. Loving father of Andrew (Violetta), Kenneth, Neil (Susanna). Cherished grandfather of David, Dara, David, Laura, Caroline, Madeline, Ian and Natalie. After serving as a General's Aide in the US Army, he was a prominent toy manufacturer. Graveside service Thursday at Beth Moses Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY under the direction of I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 631499-6060. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that those who wish may send contributions to the Herbert Merin Lewy Body Disease Research Fund at Massachusetts General Hospital, Development Office, 100 Charles River Plaza, Suite 600, Boston, MA 02114-2792. MERIN-Herbert. The Board of Governors and Members of High Ridge Country Club acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Herbert Merin. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Rita and their family. Richard Bornstein, President Leon Fassler, Secretary
