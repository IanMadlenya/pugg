
        KEMPTON-Murray. On May 5th, 1997. Service Thursday, May 8th, 11 AM, at St. Ignatius of Antioch, 87th St. at West End Ave. Friends are invited to offer a contribution in his memory to a charity of their choice, in lieu of flowers.
