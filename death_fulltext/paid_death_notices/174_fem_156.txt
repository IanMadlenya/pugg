
        LEWIS-Dr. Nancy. Nancy Hara Lewis, died July 17, in a tragic airplane accident near Fairbanks, Alaska. Loved, admired and adored by all who knew her. She will be deeply missed by her husband Hugh Rose, her parents Harriet and Dick Lewis of Great Neck, New York, her beloved sisters, Tracy Nause and Amy Tabor, brother-in-laws Bob Tabor and Peter Nause. Cherished niece of Ruth Swaback, Rosaline Simon, Leanore Fox, and by her many cousins. She was of superior intelligence, a kind and caring young doctor who lived life to the utmost climbing mountains, and rafting rivers the world over. She cared deeply about the environment and the beauty of Alaska. She died at 48, but lived 100 years.
