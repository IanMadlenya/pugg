
        SPAISMAN-Zypora. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Spaisman, and loving mother of Ben A. Spaisman and daughter-in-law Sally A. Spaisman and devoted grandmother of Erica and Elliot Spaisman. Born in Lublin, Poland, she came to this country as WW II refugee having spent time in Russia during the war, and in Poland, France, and Canada thereafter. Award winning actress and star of the Yiddish Theater for over 40 years, was an active patron and supporter of Yiddish culture her entire life. The services will be help on Wednesday May 22nd 12 noon at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., New York, NY.
