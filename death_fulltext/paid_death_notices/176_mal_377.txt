
        GELLER-Steven. Beloved husband of Debra. Loving father of Hali. Son-in-law of Suzanne and Irving Karpas. Brother of Toby Landau and Martin Geller. Steven was lost in the World Trade Center tragedy on September 11, 2001. He celebrated life and adored his family. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, September 30th, 10 AM, at Congregation Rodeph Sholom, 7 West 83rd Street, NYC. Contributions in Steven's memory may be made to the Dalton School, 108 East 89th St, NYC, or to Congregation Rodeph Sholom.
        GELLER-Steven. Congregation Rodeph Sholom is deeply shaken and saddened over the tragic and untimely death of Steven Geller in the World Trade Center disaster. Steven was an amazing human being who lived for his wife and daughter. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his beloved wife Debra and his daughter Hali and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, Sr. Rabbi Mark Biderman, President
