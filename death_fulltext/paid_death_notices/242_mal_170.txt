
        RISHIN--Marvin P., 81, of Coconut Creek, Florida on March 16, 2007. He was a devoted husband to Edythe Werner, a loving father to Karen Abrahamson, Marilynn and Kenneth Hopman and Laurie Greenberg, a proud grandfather of Ember and Kevin Newell, Aileah and Joe Slepski and Victor Hopman. ''A Celebration of His Life'' will be on April 1, 2007 at noon in Florida. Donations may be made to Ramapo for Children, P.O. Box 266, Rhinebeck, NY 12572, National World War II Museum, 954 Magazine St., New Orleans, LA 70130 or a charity of your choice.
