
        DENSEN - GERBER - Ju dianne, M.D., J.D. The Board of Trustees, officers, staff and residents of Odyssey House mourn the passing of Dr. Densen-Gerber. As the founder of Odyssey House 35 years ago, Dr. Densen-Gerber pioneered therapeutic community treatment programs that helped save the lives of thousands of men and women addicted to drugs. We extend our deepest sympathies to her loved ones. George Rosenfeld, Chairman Peter Provet, Ph.D., President
