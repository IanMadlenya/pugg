
        WOHLBERG-Joseph. Of Denville, NJ, on Tuesday, May 29, 2001. Beloved husband of Edna (nee Getzoff) for 54-1/2 years married. A brother-in-law of Mr. and Mrs. Lester (Martha) Getzoff of New York City, NY. An uncle of Vera Miller of Guilderland, NY, Barbara Zylberman of Melbourne, Australia, and Steven Getzoff of Riverdale, NY. Mr. Wohlberg died at the Franciscan Oaks Health Center Denville, NJ, after a brief illness. He was born in Budapest, Hungary. He came to NYC in 1919. He received his undergraduate and masters degree from City College of New York, where upon Mr. Wohlberg taught Greek and Latin as well as classical literature until his retirement. In 1997 he moved to Denville, NJ. Mr. Wohlberg was an U.S. Army veteran of WWII. All are invited to attend a gathering of remembrance at Franciscan Oaks, 19 Pocono Rd, Denville, NJ, June 24, at 1 PM. For more information call Norman Dean Home for Services, Denville, NJ (973)627-1880.
