
        WEINER-Phyllis. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Phyllis Weiner. Phyllis, a long-time contributor, was a beloved sister of Martin Lifton, a cherished friend, major supporter of our mission, and founder of the Long Island Real Estate & Allied Trades Division. We extend our deepest sympathies to Phyllis' husband, Howard, their children and grandchildren, and to Martin, his wife Ellie, and their family. May her memory be a blessing. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair Arthur M. Katz, LI Cabinet Chair John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Simon Kaminetsky, LI Director
        WEINER-Phyllis. Dearest Phyllis, a courageous, intellectual, cultured, caring and gentle friend with great style. Life will never be the same for Harold, Howard or your loving family without you. I miss you and love you. Frances Fish Tompkins
