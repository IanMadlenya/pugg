
        STERN-Ghity Lindenbaum. The Advisory Council of the NY Board of Rabbis mourns the passing of a philanthropic, kind and beautiful lady widow of one of our founders and mother of our esteemed President, Armand Lindenbaum. May her memory be for a blessing. Mortimer Propp, Chair Advisory Council Rabbi Joseph Potasnik, President, NYBR Rabbi Doniel Kramer, Executive Director, NYBR
