
        SHELTON--Marvin L. on July 7, 2004 at home of multiple myeloma. He was born in Pittsburgh, PA., July 25, 1931 and raised in Wilmington, NC. He practiced orthopedic surgery at the New York Presbyterian Hospital and served as the director of Orthopedics at Harlem Hospital Center. He is survived by his wife of 48 years, Arden; his daughters Yvonne, LeAnn and Marla and his son Marvin; three grandchildren; two brothers, Dr. Lee Shelton and Dr. Thomas Shelton, and numerous nieces, nephews and cousins. Funeral service, Wednesday, July 14 at 5:00PM at Christ Church, Riverdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Orthopedic Department of New York Presbyterian Hospital for the Marvin L. Shelton Lectureship.
