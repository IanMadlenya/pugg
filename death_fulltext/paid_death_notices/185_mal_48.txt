
        BRAND-Herman D., died on May 26. He is survived by his wife, Ariela; children, Ronna Sussman and Del Brand; grandchildren, David Eppner (Audry), Allison Bauer (Robert) and Jeremy Sussman (Rebecca); great-grandchildren, Jacob Eppner, Harris, Phinneas, and Niall Bauer, and Samuel Sussman. His wife, Phyllis, and son-in-law, Ira Sussman, predeceased him. A preeminent attorney with Gair & Gair and later with the firm he founded Brand, Brand & Burke. He was a righteous, generous and kindly man.
