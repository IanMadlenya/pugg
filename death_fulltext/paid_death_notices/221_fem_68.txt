
        HOGELAND--Elizabeth, 75, died June 7, 2005, a resident of Colebrook, CT. She is survived by her sons, David and William of NYC, Webster of Thaxton, VA, her sisters, Eleanor Shakin of NYC and Clarissa Atkinson of Cambridge, MA, her friend, Joan Balaguer of Denver, CO, a granddaughter and stepgranddaughter, nieces, a nephew and grandnieces and grandnephews. A memorial service will be held at the Colebrook Congregational Church at 11 AM on Saturday, June 11, 2005. Donations may be made to Susan B. Anthony Project, 434 Prospect St, Torrington, CT 06790 or the Southwest Indian Foundation, PO Box 86, Gallup, NM 87302.
