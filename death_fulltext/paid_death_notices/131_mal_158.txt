
        GREENBERG-Carl. Died peacefully at home at the age of 87. Beloved husband of sixty-four years of Sylvia. Devoted father of Herbert and Judy, daughter-in-law Barbara and son-in-law Shep. Loving grandfather of Gabrielle and Danny, Seth and Ilene, Eric and Elissa and Adam and Lauren. Cherished great grandfather of Avital, Bentzi, Meira, Calev and Maital. A true friend to all who knew him. He spent a long lifetime spreading happiness and joy to everyone he met.
        GREENBERG-Carl. Congregation Beth Sholom mourns the passing of our esteemed member, Carl Greenberg, and extends heartfelt condolences to the family. Rabbi Kenneth Hain Rabbi Gilbert Klaperman, Rabbi Emeritus Howard Wolf, President Lester Henner, Chairman of the Board
