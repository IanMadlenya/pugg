
        BLOCH-Lillian (Zinn). Adored wife of the late Marvin J. Bloch, loving sister of Ruth Zinn Becker, beloved mother of Linda, Willa and the late Paul Bloch, cherished mother-in-law of Louise Hollinger, Rob Grambau and Douglas Fishman, and doting grandmother of David, Daniel, Marni, Mark, and Zoe. She devoted her life to people, especially children. Her legacy of love, thoughtfulness, common sense and humor will live on through all who were lucky enough to have known her. Donations in her memory may be made to the NYC School Volunteers or the Jacob Perlow Hospice of Beth Israel Medical Center. Services: Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue and 91 Street, May 21 at 10:00 o'clock. Shiva at her late residence this Sunday.
        BLOCH-Lillian Z. The Society for the Advancement of Judaism records with profound sorrow the death of our longtime, devoted member Lillian Bloch. We extend our condolences to her entire family. Julie Schonfeld, Rabbi Lawrence H. Budish, Bd. Chm.
