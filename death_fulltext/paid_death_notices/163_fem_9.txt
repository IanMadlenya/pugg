
        SANDS-Lucille, nee Pollane. Widow of Manny, loving mother of Patricia and Jeffrey, beloved mother-in-law of Rita, adored grandmother of Jessica & Hillary, passed quietly on July 31, 2000. Life member of Hadassah and past regional vice president. A founding member of Bellerose Jewish Center and past president of its PTA. Her gift for seeing joy in all things will always be remembered and treasured. She was a woman of valor.
