        SHANDELL-Edward A. The partners, associates and staff of Shandell, Blitz, Blitz, Glass, Bookson and Kern mourn the passing of Edward A. Shandell, beloved father of our esteemed senior partner, Richard E. Shandell. Edward Shandell was a renowned trial lawyer, admired and respected by both plaintiffs and defense attorneys. As one of the rare gentlemen in this profession, he will be greatly missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to the entire Shandell family. Shandell, Blitz, Blitz, Glass, Bookson and Kern, LLP.
