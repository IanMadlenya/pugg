
        MOGILNER-Leonard, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the death of Dr. Leonard Mogilner. Dr. Mogilner was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Pediatric Department for many years. He was loved and admired by all his patients, friends and colleagues. He will be sorely missed by all. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Warren Wexelman, M.D., Pres. Jacob Shani, M.D., Pres. Elect David Feldman, M.D., Secretary Salvatore DeGliuomini, M.D. Treasurer
