
        ROVEN-Dr. Milton D. Age 84, of Coconut Creek, FL, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, passed away unexpectedly on January 5 in Florida. A national lecturer, textbook author, and an international authority on Ambulatory Podiatric Medicine. His innovative surgical procedures, performed in his office in Brooklyn, allowed the patient to walk immediately and radically shortened the recovery period. He is survived by his beloved wife Ruth, and his children Janice and Glen. A devoted husband and an extraordinary father. He will be missed by all those who knew this kind, gentle man. Graveside services Monday, 2 PM, at United Hebrew Cemetery in Staten Island. For information, contact Beth Shalom Chapels, Inc., 877-360-3038.
