
        PAGE - Mark. Formerly of Great Neck, New York, now of Myrtle Beach, SC. died on Tues. Jan. 7, 1997, after a long and valiant struggle with Alzheimers Disease. He was a versatile and talented design engineer. He is survived by his loving wife, Stella Hope Page and his daughters, Heather Harris and Naomi R. Mayer and his granddaughters Nina and Jennifer Harris. Memorials may be made to Temple Emanuel, 150 Hicks Lane, Great Neck, N.Y. 11024.
