
        O'CONNOR-Ray. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of Ray O'Connor, colleague, friend and talented writer whose lifelong devotion to human rights and intergroup relations skills enhanced ADL's development public relations efforts for fifteen years from 1964 to 1979. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Hope, who has spent her own lifetime career working with ADL, and to his entire family. David H. Strassler National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
