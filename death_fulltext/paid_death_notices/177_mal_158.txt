
        ZUCKERMAN-Max. 90 years, died October 9, 2001, born in New York City July 28, 1911, married to the late Helen Blum; father of Bernice, Stephen and Robert; grandfather of Alexandra, Gabriel, Joshua and Michael; 7th Avenue dress manufacturer and former owner of Preferred Fashions. He taught his children to laugh and love of family and introduced us to the wonders and beauty of NYC; ardent, non-swimmer. Thank you, Daddy.
