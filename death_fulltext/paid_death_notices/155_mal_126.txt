
        ELAZAR-Daniel. The North American Fellows of The Jerusalem Center for Public Affairs/Center for Jewish Community Studies mourn the passing of Professor Daniel J. Elazar, Founder and President, whose brilliant and visionary scholarly work coupled with his skills as an institution builder informed and influenced the public policy of the Jewish community worldwide. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Gerald B. Bubis, Vice President Rela Mintz Geffen, Associate Secretary/Treasurer JCPA/CJCS.
