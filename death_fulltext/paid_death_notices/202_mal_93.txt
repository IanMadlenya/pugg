
        LEONG--Terry. Died August 16, 2003 just before his 65th birthday, September 17. He was a scenic and costume designer for many years in New York. He is survived by his companion, Adam Pollack, and many friends and family. A memorial service will be held November 15, 2003 from 12-3 PM at the American Globe Theatre, 145 West 46th Street, NY, NY.
