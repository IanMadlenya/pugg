
        CHARKIN--Norton, age 93, died peacefully on February 6, 2005. Beloved husband for 70 years of Selma. Adored father of Eileen Weiler Judell (Robert) and the late Joy Ganney. Cherished grandfather of Pamela Weiler Grayson (Douglas), Andrew Weiler (Cecilia), and Ryan Ganney. Devoted greatgrandfather of Deanna, Jamie, Erica and Marcus. He was the President of the Mayflower Wallpaper Company for 45 years. He was dedicated to Jewish philanthropies and was the recipient of many honors and awards. He was also President of the Lincoln Park Jewish Center. He will be deeply missed by his adoring family and friends. Services will be held on Wednesday, February 9 at 10AM at the Lincoln Park Jewish Center, 311 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, N.Y. 10704. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Lincoln Park Jewish Center or a charity of your choice. Shiva will be observed at the Charkin residence, Eileen Judell will receive at her home Thursday, February 10 from 5 to 9PM.
        CHARKIN--Nat. The Board of Trustees of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services notes with sorrow the death of Nat Charkin, devoted father of Eileen Judell, a JBFCS trustee, member of our Youth Counseling League Divisional Board and co-chair of its Annual Event. Our condolences to his wife Selma and Eileen and their family. Jean L. Troubh, President Joseph S. Kaplan, Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive VP & CEO
