
        LEVEY--Philip on April 27, 2005 at age 103. A graduate of Columbia University and Fordham Law School, he practiced law in NYC until age 101. Beloved husband of the late Pauline. Devoted father of Madeleine Fortunoff and father-in-law of Dr. Stephen Fortunoff. Cherished grandfather of Dr. Jamie Fortunoff and her husband Dr. Paul Romanello, and Pamela Williams and her husband David Williams. Adored great grandfather of Jennifer and Peter Romanello, and Annalise and Alexander Williams. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Congregation Emanu-El Of the City of New York. For information call Riverside Memorial Chapel, 212-362-6600.
        LEVEY--Philip. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Philip Levey, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C.
