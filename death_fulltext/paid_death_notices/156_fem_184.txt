
        TODD-Ruth Pratt. Age 85, a resident of Redding, CT. since 1965, died January 12, 2000 at her home. She was the wife of the late James Wilson Todd. Memorial service will take place on Sunday, Jan 23 at 2:30PM in Christ Church Parish, Redding Ridge. This will be followed by a reception at the Mark Twain Library in Redding.
