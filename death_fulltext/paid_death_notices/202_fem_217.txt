
        POMERANTZ -- Rachel. On November 25, 2003, at age 72. Loving daughter of the late Pauline and Samuel Pomerantz and loving sister of the late Howard E. Pomerantz. She is survived and cherished by her brother Mike and his wife Ruth and her devoted nieces and nephews Jan Wilcox, Susan Pomerantz, Alan Pomerantz, Drucie and Gerald Isenberg, and their children Sarah, Halie, Jennifer, Rebecca, Matthew, Stephen and Lindsey. She was a thoughtful and kindly woman who will be remembered with affection.
