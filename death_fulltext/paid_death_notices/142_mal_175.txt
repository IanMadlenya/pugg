
        GARSON-Eugene. November 12, 1998, at age 72. Beloved husband to Gloria. Loving father of Roberta and Heidi. Dear father-in-law of Peter Hirsch. Adored grandfather of Emilie. His vast practical wisdom and deeply caring ways will be remembered by his family and the many others touched by his presence. His legacy lives on in all the individuals and organizations that he gave his love and spirit to. Memorial service Sunday, December 20, 2 PM, New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64 St, NYC. Contributions in his memory to Nature Conservancy, 570 7th Ave., NY NY 10018; New York-New Jersey Trail Conference, 232 Madison Ave., NY NY 10016; St. Margaret's House, 49 Fulton St., NY NY 10038; South Street Seaport Museum, 207 Front St., NY NY 10038.
        GARSON-Eugene. We deeply mourn the passing of former Board member and active contributor to our co-op. Condolences to wife Gloria, daughters Roberta and Heidi, son-in-law Peter, granddaughter Emily. Three Arrows Cooperative Alice Appenzeller, President
