
        STANLEY-BROWN-Edward, MD. The entire family of St. Luke'sRoosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Edward Garfield Stanley-Brown, M.D. A great grandson of the former President of the United States and a veteran of the U.S. Army in Korea, he was a beloved and revered Director of Pediatric Surgery who upheld our finest traditions of medical excellance in over three decades of service to our hospital. He will be sorely missed. To his wife Jeanne, his daughters Beth Horan and Jill Driscoll, and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Sigurd H. Ackerman, MD President Carlton Boxhill, MD President of the Medical Board
