
        ALTSTEIN--Sadie Machanofsky, 93. Wife of the late Louis, mother of Howard (Helene), Joel (Nan) & Michael (Janet). Grandmother to Sam (Lisa), Rachel (Andy), Richard, and Lily. Great grandmother to Audrey, Lois, Minna, Jacob and Isaac. Like the candles that lit her Shabbat table each week, her memory will burn bright forever. May her memory be a blessing. Funeral at Congregation Beth Elohim, Brooklyn, Sunday March 12 at 9:45 AM.
