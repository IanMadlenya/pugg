
        ROSOFF-Maxine, M.D.. the Medical Staff and Board of Directors of New Island Hospital mourn with profound sorrow the untimely loss of our esteemed colleague. Dr. Rosoff was a member of our Medical Staff's Department of Medicine/Cardiology for the past 11 years and was the Director and Co-Director of the Coronary Care Unit since 1991. We will miss her greatly. Arnold Prywes, M.D. Chairperson, Medical Staff Paul Seale, President & CEO
