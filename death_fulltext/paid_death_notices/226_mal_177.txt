
        DEMING--MacDonald, Esq., 90, of Tampa passed away on November 13, 2005. He is preceded in death by his wife of 53 years, Judith and his sister, Barbara. He is survived by his two daughters, A. Meredith and Laura; a son, Michael and his wife Pamela; two grandchildren Jeneva and Madison; and two brothers, Quentin and Angus. He was a WWII US Naval Officer serving on the USS Nevada, as well as in the Aleutian Islands. He was a graduate of Harvard and Yale Law School. He practiced law at Sullivan and Cromwell as well as Haight, Gardner, Poor and Havens in N.Y.C. He was a longtime resident of South Mountain Road in New City, Rockland County, New York. For information Blount & Curry Funeral Home, 813968-2231, Tampa, FL.
