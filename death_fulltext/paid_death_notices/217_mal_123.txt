
        RAINOFF--George R., age 75, of Southern Pines, NC and Douglaston, NY died on February 7, 2005 at his home. Survived by his wife, Betsy, his sons, Brad, Jeff and Greg, Stepsons, John, Robert and Henry Ferris, brother, Stephan Rainoff, cousin, Thomas Drumme and eleven grandchildren. He was a graduate of Tufts University and earned a Masters Degree from Columbia University. He was an officer in the Marines serving in Japan and Korea. Mr. Rainoff had a distinguished career as an insurance broker at Johnson & Higgins and was at the forefront of J&H's expansion in Asia and the Far East. He returned to the NY office in 1981 with responsibilities for business development in Asia and the Middle East, retiring as a Senior Vice President in 1991. He was an avid golfer, bird watcher, hiker, reader, stamp collector, a lover of classical music and world traveler always eager to discover new horizons. He was a member of The Country Club of North Carolina and Manhasset Bay Yacht Club. A Memorial Service will be held at the Congregational Church in Manhasset, NY on February 17th at 11:00am. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Duke University Medical Center Gastrointestinal Research Fund and sent to Ruth Irvin, DUMC Development, 512 South Mangum Street, Durham, NC 27701.
