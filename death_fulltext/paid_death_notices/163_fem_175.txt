
        FARKAS-Hilda. Died peacefully in her 99th year at Mount Sinai Medical Center in New York City. Born in Hungary to Salamon and Etelka Buchner. Studied in Berlin, Germany and immigrated to the U.S. as a young woman in 1928. She had seven brothers and sisters, the beloved Rudolf, Aladar, Jeno, Hugo, Kamilla, Mariska and Ella. Hilda was a sensitive lady, devoted to her husband, Dr. Alexander Farkas. She was loved and respected by everyone. She was grateful to her physicians, nurses and associates & her loyal caring staff throughout the last many years of her life. A large extended family, friends and associates are mourning her in New York, California, Holland and Hungary. There are no immediate survivors. A Memorial service will be announced at a later date.
