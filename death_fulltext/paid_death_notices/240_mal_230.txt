
        CANNON--Alexander P. Kane Lodge No. 454 F. & A. M. mourns with profound sorrow the loss of our esteemed brother and Past Master on January 13. He was a member of the craft for 46 years and served as our Master in 1969. Having retired to Florida, he still kept in regular touch with the Lodge and shall be greatly missed. Paul D. Reitz, Secretary Hayrettin Kilic, Master
