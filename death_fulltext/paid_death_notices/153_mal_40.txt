
        McDONALD-Craig Anton. Writer and teacher, of New York and Mexico, died on September 25, 1999. He was 42. He is deeply mourned and profoundly missed by his extensive family of friends in New York and elsewhere. A memorial service is planned for October 16. For information, call (718) 851-3174. Donations to the Brooklyn Animal Rescue Coalition may be made in his memory.
