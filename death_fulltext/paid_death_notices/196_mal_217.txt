
        SLADE--Sir Robert Benjamin; OBE, died suddenly and tragically at Barnet General Hospital in London on the 20th February 03, age 85, deeply and sadly missed by his wife Yi-Lai. All his musical friends especially jazz lovers are welcome to attend the funeral in London. Tributes to his many collaborations with the Legends: Frank Sinatra; Stephane Grapelli and the one and only Tommy Dorsey Orchestra of America, from Paris ''The Lido'' to London ''the Astoria'' and the ''Stardust'' in Las Vegas.
