
        MOSKOVITS-Alexander. National Jewish Outreach Program mourns the passing of Alexander Moskovits, beloved brother of Anne Lehmann. Mrs. Lehmann and her husband the late Manfred Lehmann, together with their children, Karen Lehmann-Eisner and David Eisner, have played a crucial role in NJOP's efforts to assure a Jewish future for American Jewry through their generous support and wise counsel. Karen and David have served with distinction as Chairs of NJOP's Annual Dinners since its inception. Sincere condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Ephraim Buchwald, Director
