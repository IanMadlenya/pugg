
        THATCHER-Margaret ''Peg''. Of Jackson Heights passed away on Saturday, March 3rd at Cabrini Hospices, New York, NY, beloved wife of the late John Thatcher Jr. Prior to her retirement she was an Executive Secretary for the Rockefeller Foundation. Graveside services on Saturday 2 PM Dunmore Cemetery, Dunmore, PA. For more information Sheldon-Kukuchka Funeral Home, 73 West Tioga St., Tunkhannock, PA.
