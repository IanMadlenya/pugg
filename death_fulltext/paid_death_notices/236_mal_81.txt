
        PRICE--Norman. September 6, 2006. Passed away peacefully in Palm Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Felicia for 64 years. Father of Michael and Claudia, brother of Sylvia, cherished grandfather of Jordan, Andrew, Jonathan, Cali, Chance, Charlie, Grace and Teddy. 1929 Erasmus Hall High School graduate. Wharton class of '34. Golfer for 80 years. Caring, warm friend to many, he will be missed by all.
