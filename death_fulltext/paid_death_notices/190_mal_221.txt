
        CANNON-John ''Jackie''. The Officers, Governors and members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member Friar John ''Jackie'' Cannon on November 17, 2002. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Moriarity Funeral Home, Park St., Montclair, NJ, on Thursday, 7-9 PM. A funeral mass will be held at Immaculate Conception Church, Montclair, NJ at 9 AM on Friday. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
