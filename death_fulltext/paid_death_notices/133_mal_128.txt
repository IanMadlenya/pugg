
        BERMAN-Paul Arthur . Passed away on February 6th, 1998, age 62, is now free from the bondage of Lou Gehrig's Disease (ALS). He is survived by his wife, son, two daughters, mother, brother, two grandsons, and son-in-law. He was well known in New Jersey circles, headed Berman Realties and developed University Plaza One, Two and Three at Fairleigh Dickinson University in Hackensack, NJ and former Vice President of Cushman and Wakefield, NJ. Was active on several community and social organizations including the Board of the New Jersey Association for Retarded Citizens, B'nai Brith, and Temple Beth Havarim. He made the world a better place and will be missed by many. Services will be today 12 noon at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt 4 East, Paramus, NJ 07652. Donations in his memory can be made to either Temple Beth Havarim, 280 Ramapo Valley, Mahwah, NJ 07430, on the ALS Association, 40 Wall Street, 4th Floor, New York, NY 10005.
