
        GOLDMAN--Eugene, 74, died on April 2, 2007. Beloved husband of Marion Rolnick Goldman and the late Judith Ann. Loving father of Pamela and Danny Fried, Laurie and Jeffrey Goldberger and Michael. Cherished grandfather of Kyle, Jared, Jamie, Perry, Sydney and Cody. Adored brother of Howard and Beth, Allan and Fran, and Harriet. A kind and gentle man, Gene will be missed and always remembered by the family he loved and who loved him so dearly. Funeral Thursday, April 5th, 11 am, at Riverside Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. Donations in Gene's memory may be made to the Dysautonomia Foundation, 315 West 39th St., Suite 701, New York, NY 10018.
