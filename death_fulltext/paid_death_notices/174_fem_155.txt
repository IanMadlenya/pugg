
        HEWLETT-Selina Richards Wood Grew. Of Old Lyme, CT, on July 15, 2001, in her 91st year. Widow of Henry Sturgis Grew, Jr. and Roger Sanderson Hewlett. Survived by her daughters Selina G. (Mrs. W. Allen) Rossiter of Lincoln, MA; three sons, Henry S. Grew of Newport, RI; Rt. Rev. J. Clark Grew, II of Cleveland, OH; Dr. Robert S. Grew of Raleigh, NC; step daughter Sandra H. (Mrs. Robert) Johnson of Burlingame, CA; step son Peter S. Hewlett of Sebastopol, CA; 13 grandchildren and 3 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, July 21, 2001, at 11:30 AM, in St. Ann's Episcopal Church, 82 Shore Road (Rte. 156) Old Lyme, CT 06371. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions in her memory to St. Ann's Episcopal Church.
