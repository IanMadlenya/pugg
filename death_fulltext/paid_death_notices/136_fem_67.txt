
        LEVINE-Anna H. of West Orange, NJ, on Sunday May 10, 1998. Wife of the late Morris J. Mother of Howard H. and the late H. Leonard, Dr. Robert H., and Beatrice. Dear mother-in-law of Jacqueline, Yetta, and Joan. Also survived by eight grandchildren and 14 greatgrandchildren. Graveside services were held on Monday May 11, 1998 at B'nai Abraham Memorial Park, Union NJ. Contributions in Anna's honor may be made to the Daughters of Israel Geriatric Center, 1155 Pleasant Valley Way, West Orange, NJ 07052. ''We mourn her death and celebrate her life.'' Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Rd, Union, NJ
