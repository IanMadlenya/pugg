
        NEWMAN-Debra S. Tragically in St. Louis on October 1, 2002. Struck down in the prime of her life, Debra touched the lives of all she came in contact with. Born on June 13, 1980 to her proud and loving parents, Howard and Ann (Solomon) Newman. Debra graduated from Ethical Culture School in Riverdale, Hastings - on - Hudson High School, Cornell University School of Industrial Relations and was a 1L Student at Washington University in St. Louis. Debra leaves behind her loving parents, loving grandfather Louis Solomon and dear aunt Sharon Solomon. Memorials may be made to the Debra S. Newman Memorial Fund, C/O Temple Beth Shalom, 740 Broadway, Hastings-on-Hudson, N.Y. 10706.
