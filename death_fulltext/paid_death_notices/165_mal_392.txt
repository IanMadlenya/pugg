
        GREENLEAF-Crawford Alden. Of Palm Beach, FL, died Saturday, October 28, 2000 after a sudden illness. Mr. Greenleaf was preceded in death by his wife, Ruth Mabee Greenleaf on March 16, 1990. He is survived by his daughter, Linda L. Mitchell; his son, Charles R. Lachman, Jr.; and three grandchildren, John Charles Mitchell, Christina and Georgina Lachman all of NY. Funeral Services will be held in Palm Beach, Tuesday, October 31, 2000.
