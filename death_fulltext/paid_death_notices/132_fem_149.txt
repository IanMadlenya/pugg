
        KRIEGER-Charlotte M. 90, of East Orange, NJ. Died on January 17, 1998. Wife of the late Arthur Krieger, mother of Lewis Krieger, grandmother of Elizabeth Krieger and the late Alexander Krieger. Graduate of Wellesley College, Class of 1927. A member of Congregation B'nai Jeshurun, Livingston, NJ. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Alex Krieger Memorial fund at Vassar College, Poughkeepsie, NY 12604.
