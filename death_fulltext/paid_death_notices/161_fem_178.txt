
        ROSE-Pauline. Shenkar College mourns the passing of Pauline Rose who with her husband Bennett Rose create the Rose Study Center for Textiles and Costumes, an important archival source in Israel. With condolences to her loving family, Judith and Michael Margulies and F. Peter Rose. David Pernick Chairman, Shenkar College Prof. Amotz Weinberg, President Nahum G. Shar President, American Committee Sidney J. Bernstein, Treasurer Emeritus Charlotte A. Fainblatt, Exec. Dir.
