
        CAPSOUTO - Eva. Born Rousso in 1919, Izmir, Turkey. Died April 17th, first day of Passover. Matriarch of Capsouto Freres Restaurant in Tribeca. Wife of Marco. Survived by her sons, Jacques, Sami and Albert, daughter-inlaw, three grandchildren, sister, nephews, nieces, and their offspring. She will be dearly missed and her spirit will be remembered by many. Donations in her name can be made to: NYU Downtown Hospital, C/O Ms. Zichy, 170 William St., NY, NY 10038. Info: www.evacapfouto.org
