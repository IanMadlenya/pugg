
        BROWN-Eleanor A. 76 on October 28, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Ben Brown, mother of Joshua and Jennifer, grandmother of Gideon and Daniel, mother-in-law of Julie and Talbot. A creative spirit and keen mind, Eleanor was attorneyat-law, fashion designer, and loving wife and mother. She also was current President, Executive Council, Committee on the Status of Women (U.N. Congress of NonGovernmental Organizations), President, International Federation of Women Lawyers (FIDA), and President, Sociological Initiatives, Inc. Services will be held on Sunday, October 31, 9:30 A.M. at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
