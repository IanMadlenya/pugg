
        MICHEL-Henry L. The Faculty and Staff of the Robert F. Wagner Graduate School of Public Service at New York University mourn the loss of Henry Michel, the distinguished former President and Chairman of Parsons Brinckerhoff, Inc. Henry L. Michel directed the Development Board of the Institute for Civil Infrastructure Systems (ICIS), a center of excellence for urban infrastructure at the Wagner School. His distinguished career and his commitment to the future of livable cities made him an invaluable leader for ICIS and a committed supporter of its mission. We will miss his wisdom and colleagueship.
        MICHEL-Henry L. The Business Council for International Understanding (BCIU) is saddened by the loss of a good friend & ardent supporter of interaction between the business & diplomatic communities. Henry was a driving force in facilitating dialogue between leaders in business & government worldwide. He will be missed and always remembered for his work promoting international commerce through diplomacy. Peter J. Tichansky, President
        MICHEL-Henry L. The Board of Directors and staff of the Salvadori Center feel very deeply the loss of their dear friend. Renowned professionally, he was the voice and wit of our annual benefitMaster of Ceremonies for Life. We will miss Henry greatly and offer sincerest condolences to his wife, daughters and entire family.
        MICHEL-Henry. With deep sadness we mourn the passing of our remarkable friend. He was a prince among men. Anna and Guy Longobardo
