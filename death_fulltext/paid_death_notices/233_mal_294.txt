
        SKUDDER--Dr. Paul Albert of Rye, NY, on June 24, 2006. at 82. He graduated from Middlebury College in 1949. Entered Cornell University Medical College in NY. Served there and at NY Presbyterian Hospital for 56 years as a medical student, intern, resident, attending surgeon and faculty member. Survived by his wife, Elizabeth Jayne Skudder and by five children and eleven grandchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife of 32 years, Margaret Youmans Skudder. Visiting hours at Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave., Pelham, Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9pm. Funeral Mass at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor on Wednesday, 10am. Interment Beechwood Cemetery.
