
        STEWART-Duncan James. 62. Died at home on December 14. Born Amsterdam, NY. Cornell University (B.A. 1961); Cornell University School of Law (LL.B. 1964). US Army Captain (1965-67). Began practicing law at Willkie Farr & Gallagher in 1964, partner since 1973. Survived by wife Susan (Stewart) M.D., sons Benjamin (Amy) and Matthew, brothers Douglas and Thomas, sister Margaret, mother Maybelle. Services will be at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Ave. (at 73rd Street), NY, NY, on Wednesday, December 19, at 11 AM. Burial will be in Cleveland, OH. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to Cornell Law School, Duncan & Susan Stewart Fund for Law and Medicine, c/o Cornell Law School, Ithaca, NY 14583; Multiple Myeloma Research Foundation, 3 Forest Street, New Canaan, CT 06840; Boscobel Restoration, attn. Katherine Dunn, 1601 Rte. 9D, Garrison, NY 10524.
