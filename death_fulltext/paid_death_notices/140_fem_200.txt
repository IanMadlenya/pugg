
        NICHOLL-Ashley Ann (Nickii). Of NYC & Southampton, Long Island, formerly of Montclair, N.J., on Sept. 25th, 1998. Survived by her loving mother, Dale of NYC and Southampton, L.I.; brother Maynard C. III of NYC; grandmother Helen G. Kulik of NYC; father Maynard C. Jr. of Maryland; half brother J. Christopher of Canton, MI, & half sister Charring Carlson of Inverness, FL. Nickii attended the Chapin School in NYC, St. Mark's in Southborough, MA, and State University of New York at Albany. Private graveside services were held in the Southampton Cemetery, Southampton, NY on Sept. 29, 1998. Memorial services will be held at the Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church at 55th Street and 5th Avenue (entrance on 55th Street) on October 3, 1998 at 2:00 PM. Memorial contributions in Ashley's name to Bide-A-Wee, 410 E. 38th St, NYC, NY 10006 or the animal rights league of your choice would be appreciated.
