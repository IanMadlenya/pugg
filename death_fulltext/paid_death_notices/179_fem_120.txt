
        KATZ-Jessie S., 97. On December 16, in West Palm Beach, FL. Formerly of Yonkers, NY, she was the beloved wife for 58 years of the late Charles, adored mother of the recently deceased Stephen, dearest mother-in-law of Barbara. Also survived by sister Phyllis (Jack), sister-in-law Sylvia (Moe) and many loving grandchildren, great-grandchildren, nieces, nephews, grandnieces, grandnephews and friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Temple Emanu-El, Yonkers, NY, or the JCC on the Hudson, Tarrytown, NY. Graveside service Wednesday 2 PM at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY.
