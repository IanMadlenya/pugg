
        SERRANO -- Braulia. (Nee Vazquez - Roman). Died peacefully at home in New York City on June 4, 2003. Predeceased by husband Manuel Serrano-Martinez and son Victor Manuel Serrano. Born May 26, 1913 in Humacao, Puerto Rico, daughter of Benedicto Vazquez-Sosa and Dominga Roman-Gonzalez. She moved to New York in 1929. Survived by her children, Luz Maria Ruiz of Charlottesville, VA., Rafael Serrano of New Windsor, N.Y. and George Serrano of New York City. Also survived by her brother, Julio Vazquez-Roman, Grandchildren, Mary Anne Pace, Frank and Paul Ruiz, LuAnn Decker, Ralph and Andrew Serrano, 16 Great - Grandchildren and many nieces and nephews. She will always be remembered for her devotion to her family and her generosity to family, friends and strangers. >>AD#
