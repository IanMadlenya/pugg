
        BLEECKER-Rosalind P. Of Miami Beach and NYC. On Thursday, December 6, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Louis K. Bleecker, cherished mother of Barbara Appelbaum and Joan (Robert) Rosen, adored grandmother of Joseph Appelbaum, Adam J. Roberts and Meredith Rosen. Rosalind lived a long and glorious life immigrating to New York as a young girl, building a family, career and the loving adoration of nieces, nephews and friends. As the last of her generation she dispensed elegant wisdom to her family and friends with compassion and humility. Services Friday, 11:45 AM, ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave.
