
        BAYER--Merwin, 88. Beloved husband of 63 years to Molly. Loving father of Virginia and the late Hank. Caring fatherin-law of Janice Bayer and Rabbi Robert Hirt. Adored grandfather of David, Brad and Hilary. Cherished brother of Jean Kauvar, Abba, and brother-in-law to Irving Silverman. Devoted cousin of Jeffrey Klein. Mr. Bayer was former President of Arnold Constable Corporation and NoName stores. Funeral services were held.
