
        HIBBERD-Frederick Hyde. On January 2, 2000, age 94. Born on November 7, 1905 to Isaac and Alice Hyde Hibberd. Survived by his wife Lorna (nee Whittelsey), his children Frederick H. Jr., David W., and Elisabeth H. Charas. Also survived by 7 grandchildren. Calling hours on Wed. 2-4 & 7-9PM at the Hibberd Residence. Memorial service on Thurs. 12 noon at the American Yacht Club, Rye, NY. Contributions may be made in his name to Yale University School Development Program, 53 College Street, New Haven, CT. 06520.
