
        KLEINMAN--Sidney Joseph, 83, died on April 14, 2004, of complications after an operation. Devoted husband of Elisabeth Massenez Kleinman. Loving father of Nancy Shaw of Yonkers, Margaret of New Jersey and Sylvie of Shankill, Ireland. Loving grandfather of Herb, Marc and Claire. He was a musician, a composer, a teacher; wrote books on theory; and studied various systems of sight reading, especially that of J.J. Rousseau, whom, as a humanist, he revered. A service will be held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st St. and Amsterdam Ave. on May 2nd at 2PM. Donations may be made to the New York Public Library, Fifth Ave. and 42nd St., Office of Development, Room 73, NY, NY 10018.
