
        SMOLIAN-Sheldon, 83, of Redding, CT, passed away February 17, 1997 in Scottsdale, AZ. Harvard University '36. Chairman of the Board of Lorraine Industries, Bridgeport, CT, until his retirement. Survived by his wife Miriam; daughters Barbara Gerber of CA, Laura Megroz of VT, Margery Chapman and Lucy Cheatham of CO; son Peter of CA; brother Robert of FL; and nine grandchildren. Services, Friday in Scottsdale.
