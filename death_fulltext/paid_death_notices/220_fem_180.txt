
        PICARD -- Lynne Peterson. Passed away on April 18, 2005 after battling non-hodgkin's lymphoma for over 15 years. She was 62 years old. She travelled extensively to study diverse methods of healing, many accounts of which can be read in her memoirs, ''The elephants rope and the untethered spirit''. She is survived by her three children, Alexandra, Peter and Caroline. Donations may be made in her memory to the charity of your choice. There will be a memorial service on June 17th in Lake Tahoe, CA
