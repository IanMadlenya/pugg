
        THOMAS-H. Emerson, 98. Former mayor of Westfield, NJ died August 25, 2001. Known to those in the propane gas industry as ''Mr. LP-Gas'', he was a founder and former president of the National LP-Gas Association. Predeceased by his wife, Helen, he is survived by two sons, H. Emerson Thomas, Jr., M.D. and Gordon B. Thomas; a sister, Helen Greene; 8 grandchildren and 7 great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be at the Presbyterian Church in Westfield on September 8, at 11 A.M. For more information, call Memorial Funeral Home at 908-322-4350.
