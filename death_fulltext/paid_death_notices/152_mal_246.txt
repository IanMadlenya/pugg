
        KELLMAN-Martin. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with sadness the passing of Martin Kellman, beloved brother of Ira Kellman, a dear friend of the Jewish community. A former member of our Board of Directors, Ira is a dedicated leader who serves on our Overseas Supplemental Planning and Allocation Council, and strengthens our synagogue campaign on the Upper West Side through his devoted efforts. We extend our sincerest condolences to Ira and his wife, Rona, and to the entire Kellman family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        KELLMAN-Martin. The Faculty, Administration, and Board of Directors of Manhattan Day School/ Yeshiva Ohr Torah record with profound sorrow the passing of Martin Kellman, brother of our esteemed Board member and former parent, Ira Kellman. May the entire family be comforted in this hour of their untimely loss. Rabbi Mordechai Besser, Principal George Karasick, President Elliot Gibber, Chairman of the Board
