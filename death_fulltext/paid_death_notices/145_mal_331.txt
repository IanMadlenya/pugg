
        GERSHWIN-Sam. Chemical engineer and writer of plays and poetry. At the age of 83 on February 24. He will be remembered at VJ Company and by his friends and relatives. His loss is mourned by his devoted wife Seena Gershwin, his children Vicki Amster and David Gershwin, his grandchildren Holly Sigello, Susan and Thomas Gershwin, his sister and nephew Ann and Alan Pomeranz, his stepdaughters Erika and Lisa Donneson and his step grandchildren Madeline and Marina Weisburg. He will be remembered for his perceptiveness and ability to touch peoples lives, his gregarious nature and keen interest in literature. May he rest in peace.
