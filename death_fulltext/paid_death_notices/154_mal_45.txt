
        FINNEGAN-J. Richard (Dick). Born January 20, 1925, New York City, passed away November 3, 1999 on Maui. Beloved husband of Lindy, loving father of Mrs. Katie (Gary) Darnell, Ann and Jim Finnegan and Mrs. Stacey (Kevin) Foster. Dick, a graduate of Phillips Academy '43 and Yale University '49, enjoyed a career in banking that spanned 40 years. He served in WWII in the 82nd Airborne and was awarded the Purple Heart. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to The Salvation Army or American Lung Association.
