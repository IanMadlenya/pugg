
        BARDSLEY-Elda Larzelere Shantz. Died March 6, 2003 in her home in Chevy Chase, Maryland after a long illness. Beloved wife of the late R. Geoffrey Bardsley. Loved & admired mother of Kathryn Lynn, Alexander Geoffrey & Alison Margaret Bardsley. Genealogist, philanthropist, skeptic. A memorial service is planned for Friday, April 11th. Memorial contributions may be made to the Montgomery Hospice, Rockville, Maryland.
