
        GRANT--Charles J., Jr., 79, on June 28, 2005 in his sleep at St. Vincent's Hospital recovering from neck surgery. Preceded in death by his beloved companion of 23 years, Zan E. Van Antwerp. His wife, Joyce, died in 1973, and his stepdaughter, Joy, died in 1995. Survived by Zan's family, his wife's nieces in England, and a circle of close friends. Born and raised in Brooklyn, a graduate of NYU and a retired journalist, who for many years was the researcher and writer for Ripley's Believe It Or Not. Service at St. Luke in the Fields, Hudson Street at Grove Street, Tuesday, August 2 at 4PM. Reception to follow for family and friends. Inurnment at Fresh Pond Crematory.
