
        WINSTON--Julian (Winnie). On June 12, 2005, in Wellington, New Zealand, at age 64. Homeopath, folk musician, industrial designer, teacher and writer. His unique presence will be missed by family, friends, and students. Survived by wife Gwyneth Evans of Wellington, New Zealand, and her children Jacinda and Matthew; and his brothers Rick and Jon, both of Adamant, VT. Donations in his memory may be made to Mary Potter Hospice, PO Box 7442, Wellington South, Wellington, New Zealand.
