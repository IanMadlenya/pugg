
        JACOBSON-Gad (Godl). Age 89 of Hallandale, FL on September 30. Beloved husband of Leah and father of Joel (Pauline Watona), Beryl (Roberta), Rose (Ronald Novak) and Fred (Beverly). Adoring grandfather of Daniel, Rebecca, Mara, Leila, Talia and Ezra and great-grandfather of Gareth, Zoe and Thomas. He loved literature and music and was devoted to social justice, Israel, and above all else to Yiddishkeit.
