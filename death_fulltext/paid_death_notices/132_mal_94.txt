
        ROSENTHAL-Randolph, M.D., after a devastatingly swift illness. Husband of Hilda; father of Charles and Roy; father-in-law of Jane; grandfather of David and Laura; brother of Helen Pall; brother-in-law of Evelyn Myers, Israel and Harriet Miller; loving uncle. A practicing psychiatrist for forty-eight years, past president of the Nassau Psychiatric Societyauthor of many scientific papers. Dedicated to his family, friends and profession. Services at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapel'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY, Thursday, January 8, at 12 noon.
