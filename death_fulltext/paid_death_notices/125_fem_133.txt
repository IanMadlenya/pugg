
        GORMAN-Augusta. Adoring wife of the late Buzzy Gorman, loving mother of Diane G. Kranz, Phyllis A. King and Bonnie A. Gorman. Loving mother-in-law of Barry King and the late Jerome E. Kranz. Loving grandmother of Marni and James Orsbern and Noah King. Neatly attired, she was always admired. Her scarves were her signature. Her life was very full and rich between her family, mah jong and her sports on TV. She loved life and enjoyed life. Services private. Family will sit shiva at the home of Diane G. Kranz from Tuesday, 2:00 PM, to Friday at noon. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate contributions to New York City Ballet, Music Outreach or Dorot.
