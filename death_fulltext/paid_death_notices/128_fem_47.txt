
        RUFFINO-Alice (nee Stacey). Age 88, dearly loved mother of Sandra P. Manne (Jerry) has died in Illinois. She is also survived by her cousins Alice Rowan McCormick and Florence and Reynold Kornicker. Mrs. Ruffino was born and raised in NY and was educated at Barnard. She was a magazine editor with MacFadden-Bartell. She will be remembered for her love of NYC and Paris, as well as her natural red hair and love of muisic and dancing. Interment will be at Woodlawn, Bronx. Memorials to the British Home, Brookfield, IL 60513 would be appreciated.
