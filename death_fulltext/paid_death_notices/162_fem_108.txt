
        REISER-Ruth. Of Kew Garden Hills and Boynton Beach, FL. Loving wife of Dr. Irving Reiser. Mother of Judy Rosell, Ronald Reiser and Arlene Wakeham. She is also survived by three grandchildren and one great-granddaughter, sister Lila Zelin and brother David Gitelson, all of whom love her dearly. Ruth is remembered especially for her love, kindness and caring for others; for her wit (her wizardry in the stock market), and devotion to her husband and family. She will be always remembered and celebrated. Services will be at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd., Forest Hills, on Tuesday, July 18, 2000, 12 noon.
