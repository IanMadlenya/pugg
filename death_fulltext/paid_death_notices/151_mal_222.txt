
        DUFLOS-Bernard Maurice, age 54. On August 17. Adored husband and best friend of Bertille. Services at Christ and Holy Trinity Church, Westport, Connectucut Friday, August 20 at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to Yale New Haven Hospital Department of Oncology.
