
        MARSHALL-Jeanne B. On August 26, 2002. Age 77. Retired kindergarten teacher at P.S. 17 in Queens. Wife of the late Jack Zaretsky and the late Joseph Marshall. Survived by two sons, Jon and his wife Estelle, and Tom and his wife Kathy Keneally, two grandchildren, Michael, and Scott and his wife Randi. Memorial service at Riverside Chapel, 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, today, 1PM. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to P.S. 17 School Fund, 28-37 29 St., Long Island City, N.Y. 11102.
