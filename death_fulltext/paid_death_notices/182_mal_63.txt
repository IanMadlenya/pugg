
        STEIN-Dr. Sol, The Officers, Board members and entire family of the Israel Humanitarian Foundation are deeply saddened by the passing of Dr. Sol Stein of Miami, FL. He died on March 1, 2002, at the age of 96. Born in Vilna, Lithuania, Dr. Stein is survived by his beloved son, and was predeceased by his wife Luba. After immigrating to Belgium, Dr. Stein received his doctorate in Economics at the University of Ghent. He moved to the United States in 1940. In 1960, Dr. Stein co-founded the Israel Histadrut Foundation with former U.S. Supreme Court Justice, Arthur J. Goldberg. He successfully guided the organization now known as the Israel Humanitarian Foundation through phenonmenal growth. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, March 5, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, on 76th Street in Manhattan, at 1:15 pm. Marvin M. Sirota, President Stanley J. Abrams, Executive Vice President
        STEIN-Sol. We mourn the loss of a friend and colleague. A devoted supporter of Labor Israel, including the Histadrut, Israel's general federation of labor, he served for many years as Executive Director of the National Committee for Labor Israel. Dr. Stein developed innovative fund raising techniques to help Histadrut institutions and programs in Israel. We will miss his dedication, insight, charm, wit and his love for Israel. National Committe for Labor Israel Jay Mazur, President Jerry Goodman, Exec Dir Ann Stamler, Assoc Exec Dir
