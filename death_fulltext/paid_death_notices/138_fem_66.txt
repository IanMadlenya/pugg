
        COHEN-Jessica Jennifer, 25, a journalist, died on June 29th, 1998, in Tempe, AZ. of a brain tumor. She was born in Santa Fe, New Mexico, to J. Susan Cedar and Eugene D. Cohen. She was engaged to Claude Mottier, and is also survived by her stepfather, Gary Lowenthal, and siblings, Jessica Anne Lowenthal, Adam Cohen, and Martin Lowenthal.
