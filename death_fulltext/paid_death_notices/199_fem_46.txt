
        KORTCHMAR -- Lucy (nee Cores). Died peacefully on Martha's Vineyard on August 6 at 91. Born in Moscow she came to the US at the age of nine and made a career as a graphic designer and later an author. She combined a warm and generous heart with a brilliant intellect. A loving wife of the late Emil, she will be missed by her children Michael & Daniel, grandchildren Grace, Samuel, Sophia and Stella; daughters-inlaw Maggie and Ellen and her friends of all generations. A memorial service in New York will be announced.
