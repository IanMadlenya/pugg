        GROSSMAN-William M. The American Committee for Shenkar College in Israel mourns the passing of one of its outstanding leaders. A vice president of The American Committee, and member of the Board of Governors of the College, Bill Grossman with his wife, Beatrice, founded the Grossman Laboratory and the Grossman Family Campus there. A man of goodness, compassion and vision, he will be sorely missed. We extend our deepest condolences to his family.
