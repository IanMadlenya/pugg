
        SAVIO-Frank H., Cmdr. USN Ret. Age 84. On Oct. 7, 2001. Beloved husband of the late Margaret A. (nee Armao). Devoted father of Robin L. Savio of Virginia & Lorna G. Golder of Texas. Also survived by his sister, Genevieve Savio of New York City, five grandchildren, four great grandchildren and his loving friend, Helen Jacobs. Funeral Service Wednesday at 11:00 AM in Messiah Lutheran Church, 100 Media Line Road, Newtown Square, PA. where friends may call after 10:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, memorial gifts may be sent to the charity of the donor's choice. Interment is in Arlington National Cemetery.
