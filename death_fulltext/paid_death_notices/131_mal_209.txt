
        MOTZKIN-Arthur P. Died December 13, 1997, at home in Boca Raton, FL. Born May 27, 1922, in New Rochelle, NY. Former President of Kingston Oil Supply Co. (KOSCO), Kingston, NY. Survived by his beloved wife Marilyn; children Nancy (Richard) Schulman, Judith (Richard Mandel), and Barry (Heidi); six loving grandchildren, Michael, Sasha, Alissa, Lauren, Jamin, Joseph; and his two brothers, Morris and Alvin. ''It is only the love you have given and received which will count and all the rest will be forgotten in your reflection.'' For information, GuttermanWarheit Chapel, Boca Raton, FL.
