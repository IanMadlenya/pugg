
        GOLDBERG-Rabbi Julius. We record with sorrow the passing of our beloved colleague. Ordained in 1953, he served The Plainview Jewish Center for over four decades, first as senior then as rabbi emeritus. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Bette, children Rachel, Dr. Harry, and Rabbi Mordecai. Rabbi David L. Lieber, President Rabbi Joel H. Meyers, Exec. VP The Rabbinical Assembly
