
        TROTZKY-Lewis A. On Apr. 24, 1997. Formerly of Forty-Fort, PA and Bear Creek, PA where he ran Nelson Mfg Co. and was active in the Wilkes-Barre Jewish community. Retired to NY where he volunteered at the Legal Aid Society and AARP. Survived by his wife Jean, daughters Claire Dankoff, Katie Trotzky & Maggie Trotzky, a brother Joseph & 6 grandchildren. Service Friday, 2 P.M., Plaza Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave and 91 Street. Donations in his memory to the Consumer Law Unit, The Legal Aid Society of NY, 15 Park Row, New York, NY 10038.
