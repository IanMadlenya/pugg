
        SPRINGER-Alexander. March 11, 2000, Laguna Hills, CA. Dearly beloved husband of Arlene. Father of Elyse and Diane. Father-in-law of Lee. Grandfather of Josh and Zach. Brother of Jack. He worked in the composing room of The New York Times for 25 years. Donations in his name may be made to the American Lung Association.
        SPRINGER-Alexander D. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Alexander D. Springer, associated with The Times from 1956 until retiring in 1980.
