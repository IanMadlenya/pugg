
        GLICKMAN-Sara G. Age 77. Beloved wife of Arthur, devoted mother of Alan R. Glickman, Mindy Gelok, the late Marcia Ann and Norman. Sara revolutionized the entire foster care industry in the United States. Since 1967, her untiring lobbying has led to over 150, 000 children being adopted out of foster care. She was one of the founders of ''Family Focus Adoption Services'' in Queens, NY. Services Thursday, October 26th, 12 noon at Robert Schoem Chapel, 150 E, Route 4, Paramus, NJ.
