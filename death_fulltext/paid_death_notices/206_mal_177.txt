
        BLUM--Harry, M.D. Age 105, of Southbury, CT, died Sunday March 14, 2004, peacefully at his home. He had run an active ear, nose and throat practice in Brooklyn for over 70 years. Services Tuesday March 16, 2004 at 10 A.M. at B'Nai Israel Temple, 444 Main Street North, Southbury, CT. Burial 1 P.M. Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
