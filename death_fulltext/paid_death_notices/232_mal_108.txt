
        DANN--David P. Of New York, a principal at William B. Tabler Architects, died February 22, 2006 at age 80. A graduate of Yale, Mr. Dann designed hotels worldwide. Among his projects were the New York Hilton and the Brooklyn Renaissance Plaza. He is survived by his wife, Betts, a brother Ben, two sisters, Dee Torbert and Tina St. Clair, and 13 nieces and nephews. Condolences to the family may be sent to Benjamin G. Dann, Box 55, Waverly, PA. 18471.
