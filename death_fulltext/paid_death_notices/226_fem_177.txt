
        GRINELL--Martha M. Died in the early morning of November 21, 2005. She was 87 years old. Ms. Grinell was a graduate of Lehman College and received her Master's in education from Columbia Teachers College. She taught grade school in New York City for 15 years. Ms. Grinell had a passion for learning; for literature, biblical archeology, Yiddish (her first language) and the arts. She was a gifted jeweler and watercolorist and had a deep faith in God. Her loving kindness, her wisdom, her generosity of spirit, her wit will be sorely missed by all who knew and loved her. She is survived by her beloved daughters, Sheila Grinell Johnson and Barbara Grinell and her cherished grandson, Michael Martin Johnson.
