        YUNICH-David. The Trustees and staff of Carnegie Hall are deeply saddened by the passing of Honorary Trustee David Yunich. His contributions to the quality of life in this city cannot be measured. We extend our condolences to his wife, Beverly, and to the rest of the Yunich family.
