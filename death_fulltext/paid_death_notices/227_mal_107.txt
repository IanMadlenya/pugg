
        RUDNER--Albert H., DDS. In his 98th year beloved husband of the late Gladys Rudner for 66 years, loving father of Jane and Maurice (Reese) Sherman and Judy and Jerry Kessel. Adored grandfather of Cathy and Jon Fiebach, Susan and Les Morgenstein, Danny and Kirstin Stiebel and Lynn and John Fitzgerald. Dear great grandfather of Charlie, Josh, Aidan, Jamie, Matthew and Lucy. During W.W. II he was a captain in the cavalry. He practiced dentistry in NYC for over 60 years, he loved people and he loved life. Private services were held.
