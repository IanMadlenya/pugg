
        HOVEY-J. Allan, Jr. 77, of Washington DC and Greenspring, WV, formerly of New York City, of a heart attack on July 21, 1999. Former Executive Director, American Committee on United Europe, former Vice President, Radio Free Europe, retired International Relations Specialist, Government Accounting Office (GAO) Washington, DC. Survived by his wife Jane Reid Streit, daughter Anne E. Brandolini, brothers Monroe A. and Robert T. Hovey, stepdaughter L. Devon Streit, two granddaughters, and several nieces and nephews. For further information please call Monroe at 304-822-3374.
