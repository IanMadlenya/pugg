
        FOGELSON-David. Age 94, died December 27th in Palm Beach. Beloved husband to the late Gertrude, devoted father of Ellen Liman and the late James Fogelson, father-in-law of Phylis Fogelson and the late Arthur Liman, grandfather of Lewis, Emily, Douglas, Robert and Matthew. A partner in the law firm of Schwartz & Frohlich, and a founding trustee of the Joe and Emily Lowe Foundation, he rose from bakery delivery boy to esteemed attorney representing Al Jolson, Charlie Chaplin, Columbia Pictures and Stanley Warner. Always dapper, his wisdom, dignity and perseverance will live on as a role model. Burial private. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Fogelson Fund at the Jewish Museum.
