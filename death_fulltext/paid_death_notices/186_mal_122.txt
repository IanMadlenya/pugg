
        DOCTOROFF - Martin M. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Martin M. Doctoroff, a prominent appeals court judge in Michigan. We extend our sincerest condolences to his son, Daniel L. Doctoroff, a leader in the Jewish community, and his wife Alisa, who is a member of our board and our Commission on Jewish Identity and Renewal. Dan & Alisa have demonstrated their concern and caring for Jewish learning and are generous supporters of UJA-Federation and its beneficiary agencies. Mr. Doctoroff's legacy lives on through his family's extraordinary contributions to the Jewish community. We also express our sympathy to Mr. Doctoroff's sons Mark, Andrew and Thomas, and the entire Doctoroff family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Executive V.P. and CEO
        DOCTOROFF-Hon. Martin M. The officers, Board of Directors and staff of NYC & Company note with great sorrow the passing of Judge Martin M. Doctoroff, father of our colleague and good friend, Dan Doctoroff. We send our deepest sympathy to Dan and Alisa, to their children and the Doctoroff family. Jonathan M. Tisch, Chairman Cristyne L. Nicholas, President
