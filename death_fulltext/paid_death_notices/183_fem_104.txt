
        GELLER-Sylvia R. Suddenly on April 11, 2002. Wife of the late Edward. Loving mother of Herbert and Kenneth, daughters-in law Nancy and Judith. Grandmother of Eric and Lisa. Friend of many. Funeral today, 11 A.M., Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Blvd at 76 Rd, Forest Hills. Donations to Queens Women's League of Camp Sussex.
        GELLER-Sylvia. The members of Camp Sussex and its Queens League mourn the passing of its lifelong member and devoted worker. Our heartfelt condolences go out to her children, Herbert, Nancy, Judith and Kenneth, and their families. Victor Ozeri, Pres., Camp Sussex Selma Gold, Pres., Queens League
