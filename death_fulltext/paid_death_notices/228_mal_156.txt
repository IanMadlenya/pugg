
        FEINNE -- Martin Harry. Passed away Friday January 13, he was 80 years old. Born in New York City he was a graduate of NYU receiving a Master's degree in Music. He is survived by his wife Charlotte, son David and a sister Sonya Shapiro. He was an Army veteran of W.W. II and he was predeceased by his daughter, Linda Feinne-Roth. George T. Davis FH.
