
        BRESSLER-Alfred W. On March 15, 1999 at the age of 94. A distinguished attorney in practice for more than 70 years, he is survived by many devoted friends. Service and interment private. A memorial service will be announced.
        BRESSLER-Alfred W. It is with great sorrow that the Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Alfred W. Bressler, a prominent lawyer and a dedicated friend to the Jewish community. As the President of the Henry and Lucy Moses Fund, he supported a world of our services and embodied the compassion of our mission. Our deepest sympathies go to his devoted friends and to those whose lives he touched. May all who benefited from his kindness and wisdom be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        BRESSLER-Alfred. The Trustees and staff of the Riverdale YMYWHA mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague, friend and recipient of our Founder's Award. Al was a true gentleman who worked tirelessly for his clients and the community. We miss him. Jeffrey A. Moerdler, President Benjamin Neuwirth, Hon. Chair Diane Rubin, Exec. Director
        BRESSLER-Alfred W. On March 15, 1999 at age 94. Moses & Singer LLP mourns the loss of our senior partner. His dedication to the practice of law was extraordinary and he brought the same high degree of energy, skill and tenacity to his charitable endeavors as he provided to his clients at Moses & Singer LLP during his long and fruitful legal career.
