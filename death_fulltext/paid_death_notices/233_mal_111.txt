
        BAILEY--Robert J. Noted photographer and writer, and producer/host of the cable TV show, Poetry Live, passed away June 11, 2006 at age 64 after a struggle with cancer. He was the son of the late Edna Wheeler and Richard Bailey. Survived by Julie Hindra, his beloved wife, his brother Rick Bailey & sisters Becky, Edna and Gail, and numerous nieces & nephews. He will be missed by many. Visitation will be held at the John Krtil Funeral Home on June 14, 2-4 and 7-9.
