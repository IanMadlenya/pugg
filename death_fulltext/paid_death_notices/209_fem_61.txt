
        CASTELLOTTI -- Madeline. We deeply mourn the passing of our dearest and beloved friend, Madeline Castellotti on June 7. Madeline's strong work ethic and entrepreneurial leadership in her family business, John's Pizzeria, will be greatly missed by one and all--she was an outstanding businesswoman! Her friendship, guidance and wisdom will be profoundly missed by her many friends and colleagues. We extend our sincerest condolences to her son and daughter, Peter and Lisa, and the entire family.
