
        SHERER--Leslie, The Independent Suwalk & Vicinity Benevolent Association mourns the passing of our Honorary President and longtime leader, Leslie Sherer. Born in Suwalki, Poland, Mr. Sherer was active in the Association from his arrival in the United States in the 1930's and served as its President for many decades. He was the heart and soul of our organization and will be missed by all the members. Our heartfelt condolences go to his beloved wife Fay and his extended family and many friends. Services will be at Sigmund Schwartz Chapel, 2nd Avenue at 9th Street, Sunday, June 3, at noon. Arthur S. Leonard, President
