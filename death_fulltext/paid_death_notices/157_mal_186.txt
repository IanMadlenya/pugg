
        MARKEL-Herbert F., died February 13, 2000 at the age of 78 at his home in Rye Brook, NY after a long illness. He was a graduate of Port Chester High School, graduated Columbia University and Columbia University School of Law attaining Law Review Honors. A veteran of the Pacific Theatre with the U.S. Navy in WW II, he later attended Officer Training School at Harvard University achieving the rank of Ensign. Mr. Markel was a corporate attorney and his career spanned four decades culminating in retirement in 1986 from GTE Corporation in Stamford, CT. He is survived by his wife, Madeline G. Markel, his three children Loren, Kenny and Susan, cousins Ruth and Sara Karnes, and Esther Karnes Hausman, and niece Helen Eichler Krigsman. He leaves a legacy of love, compassion and integrity and will be sorely missed by family and friends. Memorial services will be held on February 17, 2000, 1:00 p.m. at KTI Synagogue in Port Chester, New York
