
        KURTYKA-Henry Joseph. Of Clifton, NJ on December 14, 1998. Born Newark, NJ July 8, 1909. Beloved husband for 62 years of Margaret Christie, who pre-deceased him on February 26, 1998, father of daughter Ruthanne and son-in-law Harvey Corn and grandfather of Christie, Margaret and Olivia. He played professional basketball 1927-1935, including for Bridgeton and Philadelphia Jaspers in the Eastern League and for Kingston Colonials (New York State champions 1934-1935) in New York State League. He was retired from Raybestos Manhattan (Passaic, NJ). He was a remarkable person who was dearly loved and who will be sorely missed.
