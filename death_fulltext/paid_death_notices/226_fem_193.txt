
        SHEARER--Sybil. Renowned dancer, of Northbrook, Il. Died Thursday, November 17, 2005 at Evanston Hospital. She was 93. Arrangements private. A public memorial service is planned for early 2006 and will be publicized. Memorial donations may be made to the Morrison-Shearer Foundation, Box 2271, Northbrook, Il. 60062. Tel: 847-291-1867
