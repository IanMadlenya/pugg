
        KING--Leonard H. Attorney, age 85, on September 30, 2005, of Valley Stream, N.Y. and Boynton Beach, FL. Survived by his wife of 61 years Phyllis King and son Lawrence King, daughter Deborah Brown and grandson Corey Brown and his brother Jerome (Mabs) King and niece Melissa. His kindness and humor will be missed by all who knew him.
