
        LIDSKY-David (Dave). 99 years young. Husband of deceased wife Florence. Father of Stuart and Barbara. Grandfather of Doron and Sinai. Great-grandfather of Dana and Leah. Brother of Moishe. Dave lived in Manhattan Beach, Brooklyn, for over 60 years. A respected member of Manhattan Beach Synagogue. Respected businessman and philanthropist, supporting many Jewish and Israeli institutions. Dave was a friend to all who knew him and his 99 years seem too short to his family and friends. Funeral services at I. J. Morris, Tuesday, 12 noon, 1895 Flatbush Ave, Brooklyn, 718377-8610.
