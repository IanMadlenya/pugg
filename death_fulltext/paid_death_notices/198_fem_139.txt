
        NOVITT--Sylvia, 88, died July 19, 2003. Longtime resident of Wantagh, NY, teacher for 27 years with the Levittown school system. Survived by her beloved husband, Howard, children, Donald and Joanna, and dear grandchildren, Paul and Lisa. Sylvia was blessed with many cherished extended family members, including daughter - in - law Morleen Novitt, Mary, wife of Paul Winans, Rick Aldape and Leisha Counts. Jill Klein and Carl Cesarano were like children to Sylvia. Family will be receiving at 74 Tardy Lane North, Wantagh, 2-8 PM. >>AD#
