
        CONRAD-Frances. The Trustees and staff of the Shelter Rock Public Library, Albertson, NY, mourn the passing of our friend, colleague and mentor, Frances Conrad, Director of the Library for over 30 years. She touched the lives of generations of library patrons. Our condolences to her family. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Shelter Rock Public Library or to the New York Civil Liberties Union.
