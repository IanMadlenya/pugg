
        KNIGHT-Patrick T. Former Secretary-Treasurer and President of Social Service Employees Union Local 371, on May 24. At the time of his death, Pat was an organizer for AFSCME. A strong proponent of participatory democracy, Pat contributed to the spirit and building of both Local 371 and the national union. A memorial service will be held June 21, 10AM, DC 37, 125 Barclay St, New York City.
