
        CASAMASSIMA--Anthony J. On February 3, 2004 at age 89. Co-proprietor of Casamassima Ice Company. Beloved husband of Marie Casamassima. Loving father of Frances Boland, Claire Roberts and Marie Anne Casamassima and father-in-law of James Boland and Wayne Roberts. Cherished grandfather of Anthony, James and Caitlin. Dear brother-in-law, uncle, cousin and loving friend of many. The Mayor of 50th Street will be sorely missed. Reposing at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., NYC, Wednesday and Thursday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Funeral Mass at St. John the Martyr Church 72 St between 2nd and 3rd Aves, Friday 10AM. Intombment to follow at Calvary Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or the American Heart Association.
