
        RANSOHOFF--Rita M. Died peacefully at home in Branford, CT December 13, 2003. She is survived by her loving children Joan Wynn and Joseph Ransohoff, grandchildren James, Benjamin, Olivia, Christopher, and Sianna, and son-and daughter-inlaw, Peter Wynn and Debrah A. Ransohoff. Dr. Ransohoff was a psychologist and author of numerous articles and books including ''Venus After Forty'' and ''Fear and Envy.'' In addition to a private therapy practice, she drew on wordwide travel as the basis of her cross-cultural studies of the treatment of women. She was a person of indomitable spirit and never-ending interest in the world who was devoted to friends of all generations, especially her grandchildren. A memorial service is being planned for early in the new year.
