
        SLOUN-Reverend Russell John. S.J., 54. On February 2, 1998. Beloved son of Sultana and the late Robert. Brother of Eileen Sloun. He entered the Society of Jesus on August 14, 1960. He was ordained on May 26, 1973. Father Sloun taught modern languages at Fordham Prep and for the last ten years at St. Francis Xavier High School, where he was also the Director of the Computer Center. He also worked for many years in Marriage Encounter on Long Island. A wake will be held Thursday 2 to 7PM at the St. Francis Xavier High School Student's Chapel. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, at 8 P.M., St. Francis Xavier R.C. Church.
