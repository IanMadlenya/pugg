
        KARP-Vivian, 96, died April 26, 1997, wife of late Max, survived by Son Russell, daughter-in-law Gloria, grandchildren Andrew and Susannah, children of Gloria: Lauren, Robert, Nancy, David, and great grandchildren Molly and Rami. All immensely loved by her and in turn beloved by them. Millinery designer, and with Max, former owner of Fifth Avenue boutique. Funeral services: Riverside Chapel-9:45 AM, on April 30, 1997.
