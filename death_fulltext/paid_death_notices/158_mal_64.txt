
        KORN-Allen I. Age 78, formerly of Baldwin, LI, NY, died March 3, 2000. He was the first son of the late Samuel and Sarah Korn. Devoted husband of Doris Korn; beloved father of Steven, David and Sharon; cherished grandfather of Eric, Jarod, Matthew, William, Blair, Brad and Brandon. A member of the Board of Trustees and Ritual Committee Chairman of South Baldwin Jewish Center. For many years he was the Bal Kria during the High Holidays. He always did his best for the benefit of his family and loved ones. He will never be forgotten. Funeral services Monday, 12 noon, at Gutterman, Inc., 175 Long Beach Rd., Rockville Centre, LI, NY.
