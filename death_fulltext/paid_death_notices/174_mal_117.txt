
        DENHAM-William Haig. Age 83. Loving father of Stacey Denham and the late Lisa Ahn, proud grandfather of Boris and Naomi, devoted brother of June McDonald and Leonard Denham and the late Gloria Cubbin, uncle to Michelle and Denise. Died July 9, 2001. Dir. of Manpower & Training NIMH, professor of social work Howard Univ, Ph.D. Florence Heller Brandeis University, MS Columbia School of Social Work. As a young man he played pro baseball in the Negro Leagues. He was a music connoisseur with a special love for jazz and played a mean jazz piano. Memorial service 7/14.
