
        MONTGOMERY-Wright a resident of Fairfield, Ct. for 50 yearsdied at the age of 74 May 5, 2001. He was a graduate of Colgate University and served in the Navy during WW II in the Pacific. His business career was devoted to the marine insurance industry, starting with the firm of Leighton K. Montgomery Inc., later with J.F. Frelinghuysen Inc. and concluding with Marsh and McLennan in New York City. His four children and six grandchildren survive him.
