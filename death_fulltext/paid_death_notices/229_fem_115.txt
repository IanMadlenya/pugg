
        CAPLAN--Jean Rhea Backer. Died peacefully February 9, 2006, aged 98, at home, in Long Beach, NY. Beloved wife of the late Rabbi Harry Caplan, devoted mother of Neil (Judi) & Marc (Hannah), she was the much cherished ''Granny Jean'' to grandchildren Hillel (Joann), Baruch (Aliza), Ariel, Luna Leila, and Noe, and great-grandchildren Esther Malka, Gwen, Owen, and Chana Rivka. She will be missed by her brothers Moe and Labon Backer, and her nieces, nephews, and cousins. Burial will be February 15, 2006, Sir Moses Montefiore, Richmond, VA.
