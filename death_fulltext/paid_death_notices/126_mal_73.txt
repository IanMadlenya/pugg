
        COHEN-Joseph. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Joseph Cohen, July 6, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Graveside Services will be held today at Roosevelt Memorial Park, Old Lincoln Highway, Trevos, PA. at 11 AM. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
