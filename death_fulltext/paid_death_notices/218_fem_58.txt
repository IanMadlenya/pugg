
        PIERCE -- Elizabeth Neall Gay. 97, peacefully at home. Predeceased by her husband of 74 years, William Curtis Pierce and two sons, William C. Jr. and Martin Gay Pierce. She is survived by son Josiah Pierce, daughter Elizabeth Stout Fuchs, Mackin Pulsifer, seven grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Mrs. Pierce graduated from the Chapin School and Barnard College. For the past 35 years, she and her husband divided their time between New York City and his old family home in West Baldwin, ME. She was active in The Legal Aid Society, Holiday House, SPNEA, Brown Memorial Library and Womens' Aux Knickerbocker Hospital. She was a member of the Colony Club, The Mayflower Society, and a past national president of The National Society of The Colonial Dames of America. Services at West Baldwin Methodist Church at 10AM, March 9th. Visiting hours at home March 8th, 57pm. In lieu of flowers, NSCD/ NY, 215 East 71st St, NY 10021.
