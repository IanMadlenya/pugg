
        PECK-David Warner. On January 2, 1999. Dear husband of Susan Harfield Peck. Father of Heather, Lisa and David. Brother of M. Scott Peck and son of the late Judge David W. and Elizabeth Peck.         PECK-David. The Directors of the Quogue Association are deeply saddened by the death of its President, David Peck, whose dedicated, creative and enthusiastic leadership will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt sympathy to his family.
