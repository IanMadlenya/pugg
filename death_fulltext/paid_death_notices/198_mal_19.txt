
        McCABE--Robert P. On June 29, 2003. A resident of College Point, Bob passed away at the age of 71. He is survived by three sons: Robert Jr., Timothy and Martin. He is also survived by his daughter-inlaw Grovine and many nieces and nephews. Bob loved life to the fullest and was full of love for his family and friends. He was a gentle giant. He will be remembered for his generosity, quick wit and wonderful sense of humor. Bob retired as Director of the NYC Housing Authority after 35 years of service. He will be reposed at C. Johann & Sons Inc Funeral Home, 2104 College Point Boulevard, College Point, Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St. Fidelis Church on Thursday, 9:30AM. In lieu of flowers the family request donations to The American Diabetes Association. >>AD#
