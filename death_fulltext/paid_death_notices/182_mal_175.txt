
        EISENBERG-John M., M.D., M.B.A. The board, staff and entire membership of Research!America honor the life of Dr. John Eisenberg, director, Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality. A visionary committed to making medical and health research a higher national priority, he leaves a legacy of strong leadership for research to improve health care quality, health care disparities, and a dedication to ensuring that health care is based on a strong foundation of research. We extend our condolences to his family. Honorable Paul G. Rogers, Chair Mary Woolley, President
        EISENBERG-John M. The staff and Senior Fellows of the Leonard Davis Institute of Health Economics mourn the loss of John Eisenberg, a true champion of the role of research and scholarship in improving the health and health care of the nation. At the University of Pennsylvania, and at the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality, John was a leader and role model, a mentor, a colleague, a friend, an inspiration. We feel this loss deeply, and extend our sympathies to DD, Michael, William, and John's three brothers. David A. Asch, M.D., Executive Director
