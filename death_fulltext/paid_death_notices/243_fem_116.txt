
        POSNER--Marilyn, 80 years old, died Monday from complications due to pulmonary disease. A civil rights activist, she served the NYC Board of Education for 30 years, she was also an actress and proud SAG member. She was married to the late Assemblyman Sy Posner, and is survived by her twin sister Eleanor Reben, her daughter Naomi Stein and grandson Evan. Services will be held 11am Sunday, April 22 at Krtil Funeral Home 1297 First Ave. (70th St.)
