
        LINDEN-Gloria. On Monday, December 13. We will all mourn the loss of this sweet, loving, caring woman. Beloved wife of Murray Linden. The mother of David, Eugene, and Cynthia Carlaw. Her Australian family sends their love and mourns with her American family. Eight grandchildren will miss her. Memorial service at the Unitarian Church Jackson Ave. Hastings, Saturday, Dec. 18th. 11 AM.
