
        VAN CLEVE-Ellen (nee Graves). Of Ridgewood, New Jersey, age 52, died on Sunday, December 19, 1999. Beloved wife of Patrick. Loving daughter of George Graves of Bonita Springs, Florida. Devoted mother of Kimberly of Hoboken, N.J., and Christopher and Ryan of Ridgewood. Loving sister of Penny Montgomery-West of Millersville, MD., and Michael Richard Graves of Martinez, GA. She was employed as Director of Data Communication for the New York Times. Visitation service on Thursday, December 23, 1999 at Vander Plaat Funeral Home, Wyckoff, New Jersey from 11 AM to 1 P.M. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to Ridgewood Emergency services, 131 North Maple Avenue, Ridgewood, New Jersey 07450.
