
        CLARK-Blair. The staff and the Board of Directors of the National Committee for an Effective Congress is deeply saddened by the death of a dear friend and esteemed Board Secretary, Blair Clark. He was a distinguished journalist and editor, a committed liberal Democrat and a warm and generous human being. We miss him and send our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Joanna, his children and the entire family. Russell Hemenway, National Director
        CLARK-Blair. Cultivated, engaged, liberal-minded and, above all, a dear and good friend. Norman and Harriette Dorsen
