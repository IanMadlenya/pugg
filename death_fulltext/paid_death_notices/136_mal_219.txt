
        WOOD-Stayton. The Board of Trustees, students, faculty, and adminisration of the Fashion Institute of Technology mourn the death of Dr. Stayton Wood, FIT Vice President for Student Affairs. To his wife and family, we extend our sincerest condolences. He was gentle and humane, and his life changed the lives of many. Dr. Stuart Steiner, Pres. Edwin A. Goodman, Chairman
