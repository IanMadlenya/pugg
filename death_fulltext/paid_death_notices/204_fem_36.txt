
        ROZMAN--Bernice (Bunny). Beloved wife of Joseph for 64 years; devoted mother of Carol and Stephen Leit and Marjorie and the late Barry Rozman; adored grandmother of Deborah, Jonathan, Aliza, Avi and Dani; loving sister of Adele and the late Alan Badish. Affectionately known to her many friends as Bunny, she was a very active member of Hadassah and Congregation Sons of Israel in Woodmere, NY. She graduated from Pratt Institute, worked as a fashion illustrator, enjoyed artistic success in the fine arts of oil and tempera painting, and was a master of clay modeling and stone sculpture. Bunny was greatly loved and will be deeply missed.
