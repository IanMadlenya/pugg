
        NIEMAN-Lillian. 99. Retired NYC school teacher, on June 28, 2001. Wife of the late Harry. Devoted mother of Peggy Goldsmith of Manhattan and Susan Dimond of Forest Hills. Loving grandmother of Dana Baer, Nancy Bromberg, Cindy and Teddy Dimond. Proud great grandmother of Tristan and Brendan Baer, and David Bromberg. She was unique and the last of her generation of idealists.
