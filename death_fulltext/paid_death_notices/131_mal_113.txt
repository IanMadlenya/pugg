
        NUSSBAUM-Bernard M. Age 75, died suddenly on December 6, in the midst of a disabling illness. He was a very creative, kind, loving and proud man. Bernard worked until the end of his life in advertsing and sales. During his lifetime he also wrote a body of literary works, and was an avid bridge player. He will be deeply missed by daughters and sons-in-law Robin and Robert Phillips, and Amy and Roland Gell, and his grandchildren Daniel and Eric Phillips, and Dara Gell. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th St., on Tuesday, December 9, at noon.
