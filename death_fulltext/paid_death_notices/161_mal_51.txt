
        FEDER-Dr. Daniel. Born December 28, 1909 in Norristown, PA. The son of Max and Fannie Feder, the eighth of 12 children, passed away peacefully at home surrounded by his family on June 4, 2000. A Commander in the U.S. Navy during W.W.II, he served with honor in his ship's dental, medical and photography corp. A graduate of Temple University and NYU, Dan became a restorative dentist of world renown, treating patients with compassion and professionalism for over 60 years. A Los Angeles resident for over 52 years, he never gave up his passion for tennis, travel, world affairs and his family. He was a gentle man with a deep sense of integrity, and an endless supply of generosity, humor & love. Dan will always be cherished and missed by his family, friends, colleagues and patients. He is survived by Ruth, his wife of 50 years; his children, Michael, David and Kayla; his son-inlaw Jesus; his daughters-in-law Jennie and Polly; his devoted companion Pita; and his beautiful grandchildren Madeline, Jackson and Spencer. In lieu of flowers, please make donations in Dan's name to the following important charity: The Dental Volunteers of Israel, c/o Lila Seiler, 11 Sage Circle, Scarsdale, NY 10583.
