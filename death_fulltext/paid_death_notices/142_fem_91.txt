
        FELD-Doris Rabinor. Of Lauderhill, Florida, formerly of New City, New York died on Tuesday, November 10, 1998 after a long illness. She was 79 years old. She was born in Bronx, New York to Aaron and Esther (Enteen) Rabinowitz and was a graduate of Brooklyn College. Beloved wife of Joseph Feld. Loving mother of Elaine Feld of Chestnut Hill, MA. Devoted sister of Sylvia Saper of Phoenix, AZ., George B. Rabinor of Boca Raton, FL. and the late Samuel G. Rabinor. Dear aunt and great aunt. Funeral services will be held at Hellman Memorial Chapel, 15 State St, Spring Valley, N.Y. Thursday, November 12, 12:30PM. Shiva will be held at 9 Woodland Road, New City, New York through Sunday, November 15th.
