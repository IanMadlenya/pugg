
        CARR-Charles Noell, 73 years old. Of the United Methodist Retirement Community, Winston-Salem NC, was born in Durham, NC, May 25, 1928 to Austin Heaton Carr and Laura Noell Carr Chapman. He was a graduate of Harvard University, Cambridge, MA., and the Arts Students League in NYC. He served as a lieutenant in the U.S. Air Force. He resided in New York City for over 30 years where he was a professional painter in various media. Mr. Carr is survived by three children: Roby Campbell Carr, Houston, TX., Margaret Noel Carr, Anchorage, AK, and Charles Noell Carr, Jr., Fairfield, CT., a brother Austin H. Carr, Jr., Winston-Salem, NC; a niece Laura Noell Carr, Winston-Salem, NC., nephew Donnel B. Carr, Trucker, CA., and a grand nephew and a grand niece, of Trucker, CA. Burial will be in the Carr Family Plot at Maplewood Cemetery, Durham, NC., following a graveside service at 11:00 A.M. May 13, 2002. The family asks that in lieu of flowers, memorials be made to: The American Diabetes Association, Memorial Program-ncc, P.O. Box 2680, North Canton, OH., 44720 (www.diabetes.org)
