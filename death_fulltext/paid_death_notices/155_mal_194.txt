
        HELLER-Joseph. The GuillainBarre Syndrome Foundation International mourns the passing of Honorary Board Member Joseph Heller. Mr. Heller was an outstanding spokesperson for this rare neurologic disorder educating both the lay and medical communities through his writings and interviews. The Board of Directors and Medical Advisory Board of the Guillain-Barre Syndrome Foundation are saddened by this tragic loss and express their deepest sympathy to his family. Estelle Benson, Founder and Executive Director
