
        TATE--Henry McDonald of New York City and Southold, Long Island, died Sunday, September 4, 2005, in New York City after a long illness. Mr. Tate, 57 years old, was a native of West Hartford, Connecticut and had lived in New York City since 1977. He was a 1971 graduate of North Carolina State University with a BS Degree in Computer Science and received his Master's Degree in Business Administration from Fordham University in 1983. He was a past president of The North Carolina Society of the City of New York, a member of the University Club of New York, The New England Society of the City of New York, and The Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church. Most of his professional life was spent at Bell Laboratories at Holmdel, New Jersey, and he was most recently associated with EMC Corporation in New York. He is survived by his wife, Hope Hall Tate, and three nephews. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in his memory to The Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church Capital Fund, 7 West 55 Street, New York, New York 10019. Friends may call Monday 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell--The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue, NYC.
