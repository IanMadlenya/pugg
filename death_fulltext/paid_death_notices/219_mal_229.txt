
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. 86, died April 21, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Marian and devoted father of Barry, Loren and Lisa and loving grandfather of Marc, David, Sarah, Tal, Elan, Tova and Benjamin. He was CEO of Spellman High Voltage Electronics; past chair for North Shore Campaign UJA; member of the Boards of Trustees Long Island Jewish Medical Center and Worcester Polytechnic Institute and President of Gurwin Geriatric Foundation. Funeral service Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY at 10am today; shiva will follow at his home until 5pm.
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. The Officers, Directors and Staff of the Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Foundation express their heartfelt sympathy at the passing of Merrill Skeist, Founding President and dedicated member of the Foundation Board. Merrill, together with his late wife Marian, a founding Board member of the Gurwin Center, will long be remembered for their leadership, devotion and commitment to those we serve. We extend sincere condolences to the family. Lawrence Simon, Chairman Phyllis Charash, President Herbert H. Friedman, Executive Vice President
