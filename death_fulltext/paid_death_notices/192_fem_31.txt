
        GETOFF-Hannah. November 8, 1915-December 27, 2002. Died at the age of 88 at the Isabella Nursing Home in Manhattan. Daughter of the late Henry and Bessie Getoff, older sister of Hyman of Van Nuys, CA, and Louis of Sedona, AZ, and aunt of Sarah, Maria, Michael and David and ward of the devoted Semeta Dorley. Graduated from Morris High School in the Bronx, where she was noted as a dramatic reciter of poetry and was named in their year book as ''Girl Most Likely to Succeed.'' Attended Hunter College and The Jewish Theological Seminary until the age of 17 and a half. Politically active. Struggled heroically with schizophrenia for over 70 years.
