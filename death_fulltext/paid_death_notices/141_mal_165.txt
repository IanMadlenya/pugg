
        SCHAENEMAN-Lewis G. Jr. Age 67 of 30 Aylesbury Circle, Madison. Died Saturday October 10, 1998 at Middlesex Medical Center in Essex. He was the husband of Priscilla Snider Schaeneman. He was born in Springfield, MA, April 4, 1931. He was the son of the late Lewis G. and Mary Wargo Schaeneman Sr. Mr. Schaeneman was formerly of East Longmeadow, MA. and spent his winters in Oldsmar, FL. He retired in 1995 as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of the Stop and Shop Companies, Inc. Besides his wife he is survived by two sons Lewis G. Schaeneman, III of Columbia, S.C. and Andrew Schaeneman of Guilford, CT., three daughters Laura Burrows of Dunedin, FL., Margaret Schaeneman of New York City, and Mary Anne Schaeneman of Guilford, CT., one brother Walter P. Schaeneman of West Springfield, MA. Also survived by two grandchildren. He was predeceased by a brother William F. Schaeneman. Funeral services will be held at St. Andrew's Episcopal Church, Durham Rd., Madison, CT. Saturday 11 A.M. Burial will be in Resurrection Cemetery Westbrook. Friends may call at the Swan Funeral Home, 825 Boston Post Rd., Madison, CT. Friday 6-8 P.M.
