
        LEEDS-Alfred B. At age 76. Of Delray Beach, Florida and Swan Lake, N.Y. Passed away October 4, 1999. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on March 24, 1923. He was a veteran of World War II, a successful businessman and in later years active in Jewish Genealogy, serving as Co-President of the Jewish Genealogical Society of Palm Beach County. He is survived by his wife of 51 years Gloria, son Alan, daughter Barbara, sisters Sophia & Isabelle & four grandchildren Mark, Shanna, Paige & Benjamin. His twin brother Jesse passed away previously. His presence in the world made life richer for an enormous number of people. His family & many friends will dearly miss his love, kindness, courage, integrity, optimism and his extraordinary generosity of spirit.
