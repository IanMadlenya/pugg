
        CARRIER-Eugene. Former Manager of the New York Times Technical Support Department. Passed away on Friday, June 16 in Cape Coral, Florida. He is survived by his wife Denise, a brother Bobbie, daughters Christie Tine and Dawn Murray Schnauder and son David. Five grandchildren Kevin, Nicholas, Samantha, Alicia and Gene. Memorial service 10AM Tuesday, June 20 at St. Andrew Catholic Church in Cape Coral. The family has asked that in lieu of flowers donations be sent to The Gift of Life of Columbia Presbyterian Hospital in New York.
