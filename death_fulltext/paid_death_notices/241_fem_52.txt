
        BOSELLY--Marjorie Ruth, 80, of Venice, FL, formerly of Larchmont, NY, died January 17, 2007. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Smith College and M.A. from Columbia University; she studied at the Sorbonne in Paris, France. She was an administrative assistant to the Chairman of the Socony-Mobil Oil Co. and later became assistant to the business manager of the Rockefeller Family Funds in NYC. She was a member of the Seraphic Society in NYC until her death. Memorial Services will be in Venice with inurnment in Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY. Ewing Funeral Home, Venice, FL. Memorials made to charity of your choice.
