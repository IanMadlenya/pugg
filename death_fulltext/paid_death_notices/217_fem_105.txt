
        COHEN--Shirlie Newman. February 10, 2005. Beloved mother of Andy Granowitz, Amy G. Treitel and Leslie G. Brodsky. Loving sister of Sascha Sorin and Eleanore Fischbach. Cherished grandmother of Keith Rosenbloom, Cynthia Newman Mosenson, Anya B. Smith, Nicholas Brodsky and Dean Granowitz and great-grandmother of Kendra Fannie Mosenson and Lexington Smith. She was a former president of the Manhattan Guild. A woman who enjoyed life and lived it to the fullest. Service Sunday February 13th, 10:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The American Jewish Committee.
