
        BROOMFIELD-Arline A. Died March 15 after a long illness. Daughter of the late Jacob and Ruth (Clamon) of Cranston, RI. Survived by her brother Frank of Ogunquit, Maine, and sister Fay Halsband of Southbury, CT along with her nephews Josh and David Halsband and nieces Jessica and Melinda Broomfield. She will be dearly missed by her loving companion, Anthony Farley and her dear friends Evelyn Rockwell and James Ma. She was a devoted attorney at The Legal Aid Society, Criminal Defense Division for over 25 years. She always cared. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Cancer Care of New York or Cabrini Hospice Center at 227 East 19th St., NY, NY 10003. Memorial service Wednesday at 5:00 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
