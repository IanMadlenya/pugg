
        COOKE-Lawrence H. The Officers, Directors and members of the Women's Bar Association of the State of New York note with deep sadness the passing of the Hon. Lawrence H. Cooke, former Chief Judge of the State of New York. Chief Judge Cooke was an exemplary jurist, who had the common touch and ability to make all people feel welcome at the halls of justice throughout the State. He was a visionary who created the Taskforce on Women in the Courts to ensure equal treatment for all. We mourn the passing of a true friend and extend our condolences to his family. Deborah Kaplan, President
        COOKE-Hon. Lawrence. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of the Hon. Lawrence H. Cooke, former Chief Judge of the the State of New York. In 1999 he received ADL's William and Naomi Gorowitz Institute Service Award for his lifetime commitment to preserving and defending America's judicial principals and democratic values. We extend our condolences to his family. Howard P. Berkowitz National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
