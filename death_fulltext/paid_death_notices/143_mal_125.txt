
        NORTON-Stanley J., 81, of Naples died December 7, 1998 at Naples Community Hospital, Naples, Florida, after a brief illness. Born in Brooklyn, N.Y., on December 18, 1916, Mr. Norton served as an Army officer in both World War II and the Korean War. He enjoyed a long and sucessful career as an Attorney and advisor to the Heavy Construction Industry. Mr. Norton was politically outspoken and a strong supporter of Conservative political organizations. He was a deeply religious, intellectual and philosophical man with untiring devotion to his beloved wife Noreen and his children Stanley J. Jr. and Noreen, all of whom survive him and will miss him dearly. After living for 35 years in Smoke Rise, NJ, Stanley and Noreen retired to Naples. He led an active, youthful life, enjoying travel and debate in the company of close friends, until days prior to his death. He effected many lives and leaves many friends. His wit, charm and warmth will be sorely missed. Relatives and friends are invited to call at the Fuller Funeral Home, 1625 Pine Ridge Road, Naples, FL., Thursday, December 10th, 6-8 PM. A Funeral Mass will be held at St. Williams Catholic Church, 601 Seagate Dr., Naples, FL. Friday December 11 at 10 AM. Interment Naples Memorial Gardens. For information Fuller Funeral Home, 941-592-1611.
