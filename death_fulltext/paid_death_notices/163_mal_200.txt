
        LOIACONO-John C. Age 36, died August 16, 2000. Friends may call Friday 4-8 PM & Sat. 8:15 -9:15 AM at the Shaughnessey Funeral Home, 50 Reef Road, Fairfield, CT. Donations in his memory to the Worth Charitable Fund, care of Worth Media, 575 Lexington Ave., NY, NY 10022
