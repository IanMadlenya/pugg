
        KAUFMAN--Ruth Bunny. We mourn the passing of Ruth Bunny Kaufman of Delray and Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of Great Neck, NY, beloved wife of the late Dr. Matthew Kaufman, loving mother of Kenneth, David and Daniel, adored grandmother of Brian, Jennifer, Jacob, Adam, and Anastasia, great-grandmother of Rachel and Joseph and dear treasured friend of Lillian Gray. Her humor, wisdom, and determination will always be remembered. Bunny, we will always miss you.
