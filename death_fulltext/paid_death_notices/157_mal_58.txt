
        BENDA-Charles William. Of Miami, FL, formerly of Katonah, New York. Born September 15th, 1912. Graduate of City College of New York. Employee of The New York Times 1926-1976. Survived by his wife Mary Kathleen, his daughters Gabrielle Palmiotto and Mary Tillman, his sons John, Charles and Christopher Benda, and eight grandchildren.
        BENDA-Charles W. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Charles W. Benda, associated with The Times from 1928 until retiring in 1978.
