
        DAVIS-Jeanne Craig, 68. Of Salt Point, New York formerly of Brookville, Long Island. Died Friday, February 8, 2002 at St. Francis Hospital in Poughkeepsie, New York. Mrs. Davis was born January 21, 1933 in New York City. She was known for breeding horses. Mrs. Davis is survived by her husband Lawrence L. Davis at home, 2 daughters; Lessley Davis Burke, and Adrienne Lindsley Davis both of Ridgefield, CT. and a granddaughter Lindsley Alice Burke also of Ridgefield, CT. A memorial service will be held 11 AM Tuesday at St. Johns Episcopal Church, Cold Spring Harbor, New York.
