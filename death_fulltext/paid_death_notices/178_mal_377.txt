
        GLOVER-Price Perkins, 83. On November 23, 2001 of cancer. Beloved husband of Isobel, loving father of Jonathan and Julie, devoted brother of Dorothy Johns. A native Virginian and long-time resident of Manhattan, he was a pilot both in the Navy and for 35 years with Pan American Airways. A respected collector of American furniture and the English decorative arts, Price founded his antique shop in 1971 with his close friend Dunbar Bostwick. We will miss his loving presence, gentle charm, keen humor and rare dignity. The family would like to extend its deepest gratitude to the doctors and nursing staff of New York Hospital for their tireless and devoted care. A memorial service will be held at the Brick Presbyterian Church, 1140 Park Avenue, December 10 at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations would be appreciated to: Genitourinary Oncology Research Fund, C/O David M. Nanus M.D., 525 East 68th Street, ST-359, New York, NY 10021.
