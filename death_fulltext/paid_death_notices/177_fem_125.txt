
        GREENSTONE-Myra. The officers and members of the Boards of Governors of The Jewish Home and Rehabilitation Center and The Jewish Home at Rockleigh note with profound sorrow the passing of our Past President and steadfast supporter Myra Greenstone. We extend our deepest sympathies to her husband, Jay, her children, grandchildren and their entire family. May her name be for a blessing. Maggie Kaplen, President Charles P. Berkowitz, Exec VP
