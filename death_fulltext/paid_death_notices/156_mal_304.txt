
        PEISINGER-Robert B. Of Larchmont, NY and Delray Beach, FL. Died January 19, 2000 at age 92. Beloved husband of Dorothy Bruno Peisinger for 58 years. Proud father of Jon and Richard, and dear father-in-law to Carol and Hung-Yeh. Devoted grandfather of Lynsey, Gregory, Denma and Kuan-Yin. He graduated from Townsend Harris High School in New York City. He served in the Armed forces from 1941 to 1946, advancing to the rank of Major. He was a partner in State Sales, Inc. He was a member of The Old Guard in White Plains, NY. A memorial service will be held at Fox Funeral Home, 2080 Boston Post Road, Larchmont, NY on Sunday, January 23, 2000 at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in his memory to the charity of your choice.
