
        GRADY-James G., of Stratford, CT, formerly of NYC, beloved husband of Margaret V. Kelley Grady, died on Thursday, June 15, 2000 in CT. He was a graduate of Harvard Law School and an Army veteran of WWII, serving in Europe. After the war, he had been employed by Mendes and Mount Law Firm in NYC for many years as a trial attorney. He was also a member of the National Rifle Association. In addition to his wife, he is survived by his son, James G. Grady Jr., of Fairfield, and his good doggies, Tucker and Heather. There will be a graveside funeral service on Tuesday, at noon in St. Patrick's Cemetery, Huntington, Long Island. Friends are invited to greet his family on Monday from 4 to 8 PM in the Lesko Funeral Home, 1209 Post Road in Fairfield Center, CT. In lieu of flowers, cards and/or memorial donations may be sent to his family at: PO Box 320985, Fairfield, CT 06432.
