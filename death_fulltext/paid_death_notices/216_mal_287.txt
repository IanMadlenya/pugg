
        BASMAJIAN -- Vincent. A long-time executive of The Estee Lauder Companies, passed away after a brief illness on Sunday, January 16, 2005. Mr. Basmajian was a credentialed pharmacological scientist, a background that he originally utilized in product development roles at R.H. Macy & Co., among other postings. He joined Estee Lauder in 1960 as its first head of Technical and Process Development, during the early years of the establishment of that brand. For many years, he was the sole guardian of the proprietary formulas developed by the founder, Mrs. Estee Lauder. Mr. Basmajian's technical ingenuity and scientific discipline contributed substantially to the growth of Estee Lauder and its related brands, to their present position of market prominence. Mr. Basmajian is survived by his wife of 52 years, Yvette, his son, Ara, three grandchildren and three great grandchildren. A service of remembrance was held January 19, 2005. Contributions in his memory may be directed to St. Sarkis Armenian Apostolic Church, 38-65 234 St, Douglaston, N.Y. 11363.
