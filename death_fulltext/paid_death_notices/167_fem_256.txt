
        LIPTAK-Margaret M., 73, passed away on December 27, 2000. Born in Chesterfield, N.J., she was a long time resident of New York, NY. Daughter of the late Michael and Elizabeeth Liptak, she is survived by a brother, Dr. Ernest Liptak, and a niece, Pamela. Funeral services were private. Contributions may be made to the American Diabetes Association, Southern Regional Chapter, 1060 N. Kings Highway, Suite 309, Cherry Hill, N.J. 08034
