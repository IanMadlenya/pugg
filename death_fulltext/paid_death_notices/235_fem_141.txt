
        BLUM -- Jeanne Kaufman Gleitsman, of New York City died peacefully at home in her 95th year on August 19, 2006. She is survived by her children Ellen Trokel, Rael Gleitsman and Martin and Putsi Gleitsman, her grandchildren Avram and Julie Gleitsman, Leah and Mick Palmiter, Mary and Scott Young, Nina and Matthew Hogan and Matthew and Susan Trokel, her great-grandchildren, Zoe and Stella, Olivia, Julia and Meghan, Sarah and Rebecca and Jacob and Lilly, her Berson, Weil and Lupoff cousins and her Blum nieces. She was a grand lady who will be sorely missed. Funeral private.
