
        BYRD-William. On Thursday, December 25 in Princeton, NJ. Mr. Byrd was born January 7, 1904 inShort Hills, New Jersey. He wasgraduated from St. Paul's School in 1922 and from Harvard University in 1926 with honors in engineering. He received his pilot's license signed by Orville Wright while in college. During World War II he served as a commander in the United States Navy. In 1959, he founded Magnetic Specialties, Inc. where he developed electro-magnetic components used by companies worldwide. He was a member of Balsam Lake Club in Livingston Manor, New York and the Nassau Club in Princeton. He is survived by his wife, Joan Byrd and two daughters, Mary Platt and Alice Byrd by his late wife, Alice Byrd.
