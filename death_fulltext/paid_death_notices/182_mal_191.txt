
        FINDLAY-Charles W. Jr. Of Westport Harbor, Massachusetts, on February 11, 2002. The Directors of The Grenfell Association of America are saddened by the death of Dr. Findlay, who had served on the Grenfell Board of Directors since 1995. His dedication and expertise will be greatly missed. The Grenfell Association is dedicated to furthering the work of Sir Wilfred Grenfell, who brought the first medical services to Newfoundland and Northern Labrador in the early 1940s. Dr. Findlay practiced general and thoracic surgery at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital for 30 years. He is survived by his wife of 57 years, Peggy, two sons, Charles W. Findlay III and Frederick Findlay, a daughter, Laurie B. Findlay, and four grandchildren.
