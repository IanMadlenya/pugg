
        FURST-Dr. Sidney S. The members of The New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of their esteemed colleague, Dr. Sidney S. Furst. As a training and supervising analyst, he contributed immeasurably to the education of generations of students of psychoanalysis. His activities in psychoanalytic research and his publications were highly regarded throughout the field. His manner of genial cooperation was valued by all who worked with him. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Albert M. Sax, M.D. President, Society Eslee Samberg, M.D. President, Institute
