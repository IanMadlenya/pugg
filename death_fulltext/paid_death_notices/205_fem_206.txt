
        ROSE--Annabelle, 78, of Margate, Florida, passed away on February 26, 2004. She is survived by her loving daughters: Barbara Acacia, Linda (David) Murdoch; grandchildren: Michael Levine, Michelle Davis, Patricia Rau, Scott Murdoch, Kenneth Murdoch; great-grandchildren: Kimberly, Kayla and Nicole; and survived by her devoted brother: Perry (Roberta) Pazer. Services were held at the Star of David Chapel & Gardens, N. Lauderdale, Florida, on February 27, 2004.
