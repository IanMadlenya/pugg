
        KWITNEY--Paul, Sept. 20, 1928-Dec. 16, 2003, after a long, torturous struggle with Parkinson's. Cherished son of the late Esther and Harry Perach - Kwitney, precious brother of Ziva, devoted uncle of Alisa. Accomplished attorney, Zionist activist, benefactor to many. Survived also by beloved wife Sally, daughters Debby and Lynn and five grandchildren. We mourn the Paul that was and could be no longer and will miss him till the end of days.
