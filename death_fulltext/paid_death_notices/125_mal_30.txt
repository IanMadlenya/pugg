
        REMY-Jean-Claude, M.D. The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at State University of New York-Health Science Center at Brooklyn and Kings County Hospital Center wishes to express its deepest sympathy of the death of its faculty member, Dr. JeanClaude Remy. Dr. Remy expired on May 29, 1997. Dr. Remy completed his residency in OBS/GYN at Long Island College Hospital in 1976. Dr. Remy completed a Fellowship in Gynecologic Oncology at this institution in 1978. Dr. Remy was a Clinical Associate Professor in the Dept. of OBS/GYN at State Univ. of N.Y.-Health Science Center at Brooklyn. He was the Director of the Gynecology Ambulatory Care Service at State Univ. of N.Y. - Health Science Center at Brooklyn, an Attending Physician at Kings County Hospital Center and at Lutheran Medical Center. He was appointed President of the Brooklyn Gynecological Society in 1996. He was a Board Certified Obstetrician and Gynecologist and a Board Certified Gynecologic Oncologist who was dedicated to patient care. Dr. Remy was a Fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists, a member of the Society of Gynecologic Oncologists, the American Medical Association, Kings County Medical Society, Brooklyn Gynecological Society, New York Obstetrical Society and the Association Des Medecins Haitiens a L'etranger. Dr. Remy was an exceptional gynecologic oncologist, teacher and lecturer. His superior clinical and surgical skills were acknowledged by the faculty, medical students and residents. He was the recipient of many teaching and service awards at State Univ. of N.Y.-Health Science Center at Brooklyn. Dr. Remy will be missed by the Department and the faculty at this institution.
