
        WOLLIN-Michael. A retired Civil Court Judge in Kings County, died on April 17 in Oxford, New York. He was 83 and a former resident of Brooklyn. Mr. Wollin was born in Brooklyn and worked his way through college and Brooklyn Law School. After a thirty year career in private practice, he served as both Deputy Public Administrator and Public Administrator of Kings County. He served as a Civil Court Judge for nine years, following which he returned to private practice. In his last years, Mr. Wollin had suffered from Alzheimer's disease. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 420 Lexington Avenue, Suite 610, New York, New York 10170.
