
        MINUCCI--Ulpio, 89, resident of Los Angeles, California, passed away March 9, 2007, at his home in Brentwood. Born in Italy, he is survived by his wife of 55 years, Catherine Minucci, also of Brentwood, his daughter Nina Minucci of Marlborough, New Hampshire, and his son Chieli Minucci of Forest Hills, New York, as well as three grandchildren.
