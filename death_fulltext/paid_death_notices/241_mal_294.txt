
        QUINN--Br. Charles Barnabas C.F.C., Ph.D. on February 19, 2007. He was born on September 5, 1913 in Somerville, MA. His family moved to Ennistymon, County Clare, Ireland in 1919, where he attended Christian Brothers Schools. He received a B.A. in Irish from the National University of Ireland (Dublin), in 1941, an M.A. in English from Fordham University in 1953 and a Ph.D. in English from Fordham in 1959. Br. Quinn joined the Congregation of Christian Brothers on July 11, 1929, taking his first vows December 25, 1930 and Final Vows on December 25, 1938. He taught at Christian Brothers School in New Ross and C.B.S., Synge Street, Dublin. Coming to the United States in 1949, he was assigned to All Hallows, Bronx, NY, from 1949 to 1957 and to Iona College in 1957. While at Iona College, he was a Professor of English, Dean of the School of Arts and Sciences (1966-1972), and Executive Vice President (1973-1980). He served as the Grand Marshall of the St. Patrick's Day Parade in 1982. He was predeceased by his brother James and sisters Mary, Kathleen and Rose. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Edmund Rice Scholarship Fund at Iona College in New Rochelle, NY 10801. Br. Quinn will repose at Spellman Hall in Iona College on Wednesday 2-9pm.
