
        FELMAN--Rabbi Meir, 93. On July 27. Beloved father of Yehudi, Naomi and Shevi and 9 grandchildren.Formerly Rabbi of Judea Center in Brooklyn, NY. Major supporter of Yeshiva University, N.Y. , and Mevareset Zion Yeshiva in Israel. Funeral on Sunday, July 30 at Shomrei Hadas Chapel, 3803 14th Ave, Brooklyn, N.Y. at 2:00 P.M. Burial in Israel on Monday, July 31. May all his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.
