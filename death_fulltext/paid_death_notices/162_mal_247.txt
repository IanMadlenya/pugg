
        SIMON-Scot, 46, of New York City. Passed away July 22, 2000. Son of Sherman and the late Muriel. Life partner of Richard Long, in addition he is survived by his brothers Paul (Mimi) Simon, Jeffrey (Bonnie) Simon, nephews Brian, Richard, Charles and Peter. His artistic talents encompassed many mediums and enlightened many lives. Scot was kind and gentle, a great friend to many and sensitive to all. A.O.L, A.O.L. Funeral service will be held 10:45 AM Tuesday, July 25, 2000 at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 1920 Alton Rd, Miami Beach, FL with entombment to follow at Lakeside Memorial Park.
