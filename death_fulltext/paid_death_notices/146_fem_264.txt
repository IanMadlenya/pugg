
        HEAPHY-Kathleen (nee Reilly). Died at the age of 83 on March 29, 1999. Born in County Cavan, Ireland. Survived by two daughters, Brenda Brunelle and Rita Boyd, two grandchildren, Philip and Thomas, two sisters, Margaret Timoney and Annie Reynolds plus many loved nieces, nephews and cousins. Love of God was her sustenance and succor throughout her life; she is safely home in heaven. Funeral liturgy at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel RC Church, Tenafly, NJ Wednesday at 9:30 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Visiting at Riewerts Memorial Home, 187 So. Washington Ave, Bergenfield, NJ Tuesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM.
