
        GUEITS--David. Age 30. We are devastated by the tragic untimely death of our wonderful friend, Dave, struck down by a car in Puerto Rico on January 1, 2004. David, son of Edward and Olivia, brother to Gary, Edward and Michelle Gueits, will always be remembered for his warm, wonderful and giving personality. The world has lost a lovely human being possessed of many talents. He will be missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family and his many friends. Mass at St. Anthony's RC Church, E. Northport, LI, Thursday, January 8, 2004, 11:45AM. The Entire Sheldon Family
