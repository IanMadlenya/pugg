
        NICHOLS-Osgood Marsh (nee Jim). On June 14, 1997. Husband of Eleanor, brother of Gouverneur Nichols and Polly van der Does of New York. Jim lived for over 50 years in Wilton, CT. He was a writer, editor, and head of his own public relations firm. His memberships include the Century Club, Harvard Club and Wilton Riding Club. Memorial services will be held Monday, June 23, at 11:00 AM at St. Matthew's Episcopal Church in Wilton, CT. Contributions to the Wilton Library, 137 Old Ridgefield Road, Wilton, Connecticut 06897.
