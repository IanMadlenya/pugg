
        LOOKER-Charles, 93. Died on April 26 after a long illness. Beloved father of Andrew N. and Thomas G., grandfather of Charles A., father-in-law of Tamsin B., husband of the late Jeanette Albert (died 1988), dear companion of Miriam Horowitz. Charles Looker was a member of the law firm, Proskauer, Rose, Goetz and Mendelsohn (now Proskauer Rose, LLP) for 72 years. He headed the Estates and Trusts Division and was a managing partner of the firm. Among his many charitable works, he was a life trustee of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and, through the Kathryn and Gilbert Miller Fund, he helped establish the Miller Theater at Columbia University and Miller Health Care Institute for Performing Artists at Roosevelt Hospital. Private services.
