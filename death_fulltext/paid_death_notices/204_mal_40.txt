
        LA SALA--Frank. Of Osprey, FL, formerly of Yonkers, NY. Died on January 2, 2004. He was 97 years old. Mr. La Sala and his brothers began the La Sala Contracting Co., Inc. in 1937. La Sala Contracting became one of the largest masonary contracting companies in NYC. Frank, together with his brothers and the Inganamort family were real estate developers in the metropolitan and Fort Lee, NJ, areas. Mr. La Sala, acting as President of the Stephano La Sala Foundation, which he and his brothers organized in rememberance of their father Stephano, the La Sala family was responsible for contributing many charitable donations in the metropolitan and Florida areas in memory of their father. Frank was also a Director of the Puccini Foundation in NYC. Beloved husband of the former Alice Rebers. Loving father of Stephen Frank of Antrim, NH, and Anthony John of Fort Lee, NJ. Dear brother of Anthony (Tony) and A. Stephen La Sala. He was predeceased by two sisters Louise Buonaguria and Dominica DeVirgiles and a brother Andrew. Adored grandfather of eight and one great-granddaughter. Visitation Tuesday and Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Flower Funeral Home, Inc., 714 Yonkers Ave., Yonkers, NY. Funeral Mass Thursday, 10 am, Church of St. John the Baptist. Entombment at Woodlawn Mausoleum.
