
        HOLT--Carol, 70, of Stratford, CT, on April 11, 2007. Psychiatric social worker, clinical faculty at Yale University. Mother of Elizabeth and Stephen, mother-in-law of Laura, devoted grandmother of Philetus and Tattnall. Graduate of Barnard College, Fulbright Scholar in Economics at the University of Copenhagen, MAT from Yale, MSW from University of Connecticut, Ph.D. in Social Work from New York University. Enthusiast of art, film, books and political discussion, her intellect and sense of humor will be missed. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Stratford Library Association, 2203 Main Street, Stratford, CT 06615. Private burial at Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn, is planned.
