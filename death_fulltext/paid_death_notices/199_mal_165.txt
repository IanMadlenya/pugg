
        GREEN--Peter. On August 15, 2003. Beloved husband of Peggy. Loving father of Jason. Devoted son of Ralph and the late Edna. Dear brother of Jeffrey. Service Sunday, 1:45 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
        GREEN -- Peter. Dellwood Country Club, New City, New York records with profound sorrow the passing of our long time member Peter Green. He was a loyal and devoted member and friend for over thirty years, serving as a member of the Board of Governors and Entertainment Chairperson. He will be sorely missed. Sincerest condolences to his wife Peggy and son Jason. Jerrold Sholtz, President
