
        KING-Katharine Paula (Reed), formerly of New York City and Westport, CT., died suddenly and comfortably in Berkeley, CA., on Monday, May 26, 1997. Survived by her father, Kurtis Reed of New York City; two daughters, Victoria Lewis and Natasha Desgans, two grandsons, and husband, John Matthew King of Berkeley.
