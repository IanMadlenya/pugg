
        ROMEO-Dr. Vincent D. President and Chief Executive Officer of Nastech Pharmaceutical Company, Inc. in Hauppauge, NY died on May 1, after a long bout with leukemia. Dr. Romeo joined Nastech in 1985 as a Senior Research Scientist. He became Director of Research and shortly thereafter, Vice President of Research and Development. Following the sudden passing in 1991 of the Founder and Chairman of Nastech, Dr. Jeffrey Wenig, Dr. Romeo assumed the position of President and CEO. Dr. Romeo was well known and highly respected as a specialist in the area of nasal drug delivery, having played a key role in the formulation of drugs for nasal delivery, and in the design of clinical efficacy and safety studies leading to drug approval. He was also the author and co-author of numerous peer-reviewed articles and presentations. A registered pharmacist, Dr. Romeo received his B.S. (Pharmacy), M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from St.John's University College of Pharmacy and Allied Health Professions, where he currently served as Adjunct Professor of Pharmacology in the Graduate Division. Dr. Romeo was also appointed as Adjunct Assistant Professor of Pharamaceutics at the University of Rhode Island College of Pharmacy. Dr. Romeo was an active member of the American Association of Pharmaceutical Scientists where he served cochairman of the Nasal Focus Group. He was also a member of the American College of Clinical Pharmacology, the American Pharmaceutical Association, the Rho Chi Pharmaceutical Honor Society, and the New York Academy of Sciences. In 1993, Dr. Romeo became a member of Who's Who Worldwide Global Business Leaders. Dr. Romeo was an Eucharastic Minister, Lector and Coordinator of the Pre-Cana Marriage Council at Maria Regina R.C. Church in Seaford, NY, and a member of the Third Degree Knights of Columbus, Council #
