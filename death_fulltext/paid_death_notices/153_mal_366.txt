
        MARTINEZ-Julio. The phsicians and staff of the Methadone Maintenance Treatment Program (MMTP) of Beth Israel Medical Center join with their colleagues from Beth Israel's other chemical dependency programs, and with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and former colleague, Julio Martinez, who served with great distinction for many years as New York State's Commissioner of Substance Abuse Services. Our heartfelt condolences to his family and all who loved and respected him. Robert G. Newman, M.D. President and CEO, Continuum Matthew E.
