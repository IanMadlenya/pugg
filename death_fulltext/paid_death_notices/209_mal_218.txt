
        AUSTIN -- James Gaylord. Died May 19, 2004. He will be missed by his many friends in New York, Southampton and Palm City, FL. Jim had successful careers as a radio and television personality in his native Cincinnati, OH, and as a public relations executive in New York. He will be remembered for his charm, wit, gentleness and generous heart. Memorial services will be held Thursday, June 24, 2004, at 10:30 am, at the Brockett Funeral Home, 203 Hampton Rd, Southampton.
