
        STONE -- Morton. Passed away October 9, age 72. Beloved husband of Harriet; father of Nancy, Clifton and Neil; brother of Marilyn, Howard and Fred; grandfather of Natalie, Monica and Andrew; father-in-law of Steve and Leslie; step-grandfather of Michael. Mort brought so much love and caring to the world and made it a happier place for the many whose lives he brightened. We will remember his wonderful spirit with joy and love.
