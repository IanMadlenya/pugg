
        GEFFNER-Nettie. Loving mother of Carol & Edward, grandmother of Mira, Marina, Marco, Roberto, and Dario, died at age 90, October 31, 2001. She was a passionate New Yorker who loved the drama and excitement of the city. The family will sit Shiva at the home of Edward I. Geffner, 160 Riverside Drive, NY, NY, 10024. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to Project Renewal, 200 Varick Street, New York, NY 10014, or The Porphyria Foundation, P.O. Box 22712, Houston, TX. 77227.
