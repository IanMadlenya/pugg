
        KOO - Ham - King.On Saturday, September 20, 1999 at age 96. Master Koo is survived by three children, William, Regina and Hoi, and three grandchildren, Edward, Vincent and Amy. He taught Nanlaoshu-''the art of hard to grow old'' to thousands of students. His guidance and inspiration led to the formation of the Society for Nanlaoshu in 1987. Memorial service Friday, September 24th, 3-7PM. Funeral service Saturday, 8:309:30AM at Ng Fook Funeral Home, 36 Mulberry St, NYC.
