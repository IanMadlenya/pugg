
        SMITH--Ernest R. Art director, graphic designer, jazz and dance historian, painter and avid lindy hopper, died of lymphoma on Thursday, April 8, 2004, in New York City. In recent years, he was a resident of Bolton Landing, N.Y. Ernie is survived by his longtime companion, Marlene Charnizon, his daughters Kathryn Ann Smith, Rebecca Sela Smith and Julia Louise Smith, his son-in-law John Frizell and his grandchildren Rachel Ava and Joshua David, all of New York, and his sister Jane S. Korenich of Los Angeles. Family and friends deeply mourn his passing. A memorial service will be held Sunday, April 18, 2PM, Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, N.Y.C.
