
        KOPP-Paul Arnold. Died February 20 at the age of 77, his wife Helene Fuld of 57 years at his side with three sons, Larry, Jeffrey and Mitchell. He had nine grandchildren and six great-grandchildren who were the delight of his life. He had been a Cub Scout Master, Little League Commissioner and a Jewish War Veteran Commander. In 1967, he became a Rotarian and the Rotary motto Service Above Self was how he conducted his life. He became District Governor in 1986 and has observed the tenants of Rotary with much love and devotion to them and to his family. Please make any donations to the Rotary Club of Key Biscayne, Fla.
