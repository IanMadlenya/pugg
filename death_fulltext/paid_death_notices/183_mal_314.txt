
        BARTON-Donald. On April 24, 2002, in his home in Long Island. Age 76. Distinguished Broadway actor, critically acclaimed, widely loved, whose career included leading roles in ''Death Trap'', ''The Royal Family'', ''Middle Ages.'' A native Texan to whom success came early and spanned a lifetime of devotion to his craft. A remarable man of integrity, humor, kindness and high purpose. Memorial service to be announced.
