
        LICHT-Nathan. Age 79, on December 27, 1997, at home, after a long illness. Born in NYC on June 24, 1918. Devoted husband of Helen (nee Applebaum), loving father of Jonathan and Nora Licht Sewall. Survived by brother Mike Licht and sister Rose Wolk. Business partner for many years with his brothers Mike, Lou, Harry and Murray, in Lane Realty, Forest Hills, NY. Once named by Newsday as one of the top 50 L.I. busniessmen. Hardworking, generous and quiet, his actions spoke louder than words. Survived also by loving caretaker and friend, Burnus McKnight. Funeral was held Sunday. Gifts may be sent to the Alzheimer's Foundation.
