
        KLAPPER-Murray, of New York City and Boca Raton, FL, died November 17, 1997. Loving husband and companion of Connie Klapper. A wonderful father to Janis Kerbs of Mattituck, NY, and Steven Klapper of Madison, WI. Former owner of Oxford Neckwear of NYC. Will be remembered for his wit, charm and devotion to family. A remembrance will take place Wednesday, November 19, 1997, at his home in Boca Raton.
