
        WEBER-Irving Guy ''Spider.'' 89, of Westhampton Beach died peacefully at home on January 6, 2003. Born September 26, 1913, in Bronx, NY to Pinky and Claire Weber. Guy was active in commercial real estate investment and development in New York City and Long Island areas. Guy is survived by his two devoted daughters, Karen of Rhode Island and Julie of New York, and his grandson John. Interment was January 8, 2003, at Wellwood Cemetery. A Memorial Service in celebration of his life will be held at a future date. In lieu of flowers, donations in Guy's memory may be made to the charity of your choice.
