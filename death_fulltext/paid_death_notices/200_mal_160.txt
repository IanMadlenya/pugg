
        HUMPHREYS--John R. Died on August 25, 2003, in Santa Fe, NM. Beloved by his colleagues and students at The School of General Studies, Columbia University, and also by his wife and best friend Peggy. His writing program and his own books inspired and influenced hundreds of writers. We will treasure his memory. Glenda Adams, Shelby Berryman, Mermer Blakeslee, Ellen Currie, Barbara Thompson Davis, Austin Flint, Terry McMillan, Barbara Milton, Anne Nelson, Phyllis Raphael, Steven Schrader, Michael Stone, Steven Streeter, Elizabeth Tippens, Patricia Volk and many more.
