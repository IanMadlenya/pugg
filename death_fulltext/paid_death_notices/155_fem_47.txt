
        ROSENBERG-Rose., of Sunrise, FL., formerly a resident of Brooklyn, died on November 29, 1999, 93 years young. She leaves behind her brother, Seymour; her daughters, Shelley Lazarus (husband, Herb), Linda Basile (husband, Joseph); her nephews, Gary, Stephen and Ira Rosenthal, whom she loved as sons, and their families. A proud grandmother of eight and great-grandmother of nine. Burial was held on December 2nd. Any donations may be made to City of Hope, Sunrise, Florida.
