
        PREDMESKY-Rabbi Dr. Akiba. Devoted husband of Byrdie. Cherished father of Shulamit (Joel) Cohn. Beloved zaydie of Yossi, Rena, Eli, Leora, Tani, Yehuda and Betzalel. Outstanding Rav and orator. Graduate of Harvard Law School and musmach of Yeshiva University. He served the Jewish community and especially the Williamsbridge Jewish Center with dignity and distinction for over fifty years.
