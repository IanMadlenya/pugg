
        ETRA--Dorothy Rochelle. On November 6, 2004, Dorothy Rochelle Etra died of pneumonia after a short illness. Poet, painter and rebel. The beloved mother of Barbara and Bill, grandmother of Vivian and Lydia, widow of Harvey. She leaves a sister Marion. She was predeceased by a sister Esther. Dorothy was a fine person of honor, courage and intensity. Donations in her memory can be made to the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. The funeral will be held at Plaza Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam and 91st St, on Tuesday, at 11 am. Interment at Beth David Cemetery. Barbara Rosenthal will be receiving guests after 3 pm, on Tuesday, at 37 W. 12 St, Manhattan.
