
        LEVOWITZ--Herbert J., M.D. Age 72, died at home in Great Neck, on Sunday, September 10th. Born and raised in the Bronx, he attended CCNY and upon graduation in 1955, received the first Jonas Salk Scholarship Award to attend medical school at NYU. He was a devoted and innovative child and adolescent psychiatrist at LIJ Medical Center and Woodhull Hospital, as well as an inspiring mentor. He was a brilliant and kind man. He is survived by his beloved wife of 48 years Micheline, and his brother Bernard S. Levowitz, M.D., a loving family and many friends here and in Europe. A celebration of his life will be held at a later date.
