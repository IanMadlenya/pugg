
        KUPCHIK-Rabbi Abraham. The Histadruth Ivrith of America mourns the death of its past Executive Vice President, Rabbi Abraham Kupchik. His lifelong commitment to the advancement of Hebrew language and culture, and the strengthening of Jewish education as the lifeblood of a vibrant American Jewish community, inspired all those he served. We extend our deepest sympathy to his bereaved wife and children. Miriam Ostow, President
