
        MENDEL--Elsie B. Of Fort Lee, NJ. Passed away on October 21, 2006. Born October 29, 1915. Daughter of the late Anna and Abraham Bernstein. Devoted and loving wife of Lewis B. for 60 years. Beloved and cherished mother of Joyce Mendel Gruenberg and Lydia Mendel. Loving grandmother of David and Elisabeth. Adored aunt of Allan Wesley Press. Graduate of Girls High, City College and Hofstra University. A gifted educator, nursery school director and college professor. She was a warm, smart, spirited and kind person. She was a source of love to all around her and a constant inspiration to her adoring family.
