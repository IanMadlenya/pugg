
        RAABIN--Norma Hesse. 97, died peacefully on November 13, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Lewis D. Raabin. Loving mother of Edie and Eddie Scheurer. Adored grandmother of Lynne and Andrew. Cherished great-grandmother of Sarah and Noah. Devoted sister-in-law of Elizabeth Hesse. Predeceased by her brother Stanley Hesse. Caring aunt, cousin, teacher and friend. Her warmth, charm and grace endeared her to all whose lives she touched. She was devoted to community and charitable causes. She was a trustee of the Jewish Guild for the Blind and a member of the Board of the Women's Auxiliary of Temple Emanu-El. She was active in English in Action through the English Speaking Union. She cared deeply for her students. Family will receive at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81st St, Monday, November 15th, 6:30-9:00 pm, with services Tuesday, November 16th, 11:30 am. Gifts to the Jewish Guild for the Blind, Women's Auxiliary Temple Emanu-El, or Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation would be greatly appreciated.
