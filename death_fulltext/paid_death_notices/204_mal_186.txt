
        HADIK--John Bela, age 70, after a long illness, on January 5, 2004, in Budapest, Hungary. He is mourned by his wife, Kirsten Scheuer Hadik and Maximilian; Miska and Stephanie Hadik and Dimitri; Jancsi and Myriam Hadik; Stephen Hadik; the family of his late brother, Laszlo Hadik; his aunt, Countess Fernandine Eltz; Edith Gaillet Hadik; his cousins and many friends. Memorial Masses: January 31, 10:30 AM, St. Thomas More, Derry, NH; February 19, 5:45 PM, St. Thomas More, 65 East 89 Street, NYC.
