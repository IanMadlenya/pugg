
        LEMAY -- Richard Joseph. Richard Lemay, Professor Emeritus of History, died on 11 May after a long illness. Born 30 June 1916 in Montreal. A pioneer in recognizing the importance of the Arabic impact on medieval learning, Professor Lemay was a world authority on the twelfth-century transmission of Arab scientific and philosophical learning to the Latin west. He is survived by his wife, Helen, their daughter Michelle, and three children in France--Henriette, Richard, and Robert. In lieu of flowers, gifts in Richard's memory may be sent to ''Richard Lemay Fund'' at the University of South Carolina Educational Foundation, Thomas Cooper Library, University of South Carolina, Columbia, SC 29208.
