
        BRAM-Anna P. The National Board of Hadassah, WZOA, mourns the death of our esteemed colleague, Anna P. Bram. An ardent Zionist, and devoted to Hadassah, Anna was beloved by Hadassah chapters in both Philadelphia and Southern California for her warmth, leadership and humor. She will be missed. May her memory be for a blessing. Marlene E. Post National President Beth Wohlgelernter National Executive Director
