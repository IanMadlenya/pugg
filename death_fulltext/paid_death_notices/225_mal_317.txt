
        PORTMAN--Robert, Sr. The Association mourns the loss of Robert Portman, principal of Karo Sheet Metal, Inc. Bob had been an active and valued member for many years as well as a friend. Bob was the Association's Treasurer and served as a member of the Board of Directors, a Trustee on the Local Union No. 28 Benefit Funds, and as a member of the Negotiating Committee. His contributions to the industry will be greatly missed. Our condolences to his wife, Kathy, his three sons and their families. NYC SMACNA Victor Gany, President William Rothberg, Exec. Dir.
