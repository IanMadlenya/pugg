
        McCORMICK-Kathleen, nee Mc Evoy. Died on March, 5, 2000. Born in Kilkenny, Ireland. Longtime resident of New York City. She was a secretary in the advertising department for the New York Times for more than twenty years and retired 15 years ago. Member of the Newspaper Guild. Survived by her husband Michael, five children and eleven grandchildren and two brothers Patrick and James.
        McCORMICK-Kathleen T. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Kathleen T. McCormick, associated with The Times from 1963 until retiring in 1986.
