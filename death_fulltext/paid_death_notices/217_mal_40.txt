
        CHIGE--Gerald, 88, of Dunedin, FL, died on Thursday, February 3rd, 2005 at Edinborough Healthcare Center. He was born in New York City and came to Florida in 1989. He was a WWII disabled veteran. Survivors include his wife of 60 years Ethel, two daughters Susan Semitecolos and Bonnie Schwartz, five grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren. Curlew Hills Memorial Gardens, Clearwater, FL.
