
        DEGLIN-Theodore Leon. 92. Of New York City, passed away on August 15, 2000. Mr. Deglin is survived by his wife of 39 years, Ann (Reirden). Twin daughters Kane Peterson of Westport, Ct., Dorian Scammon of Hopkinton, N.H. and six grandchildren. Viewing was at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Avenue and 81 St. Interment at Arlington National Cemetery.
