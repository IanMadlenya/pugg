
        DODGE-Ruth Quackenbush. Memorial services for Ruth Quackenbush Dodge, 90, of Bowling Green, OH. will be at 11 AM, Saturday, May 19, 2001 at Carronor Hunt and Polo Club in Perrysburg, OH conducted by the Reverend J. Paul Board of St. Paul's Episcopal Church, Maumee. Mrs. Dodge was formerly a resident of NYC. Surviving are her son David D. Dodge, five grandchildren and six great grandchildren. Mrs. Dodge was preceded in death by her husband, Henry M. Dodge and her daughter, Louise Q. Stoddard. The family suggests memorials to either the Volunteer Action Center in Toledo, OH, St. Paul's Episcopal Church, Maumee, OH, or Miss Porter's School in Farmington, CT.
