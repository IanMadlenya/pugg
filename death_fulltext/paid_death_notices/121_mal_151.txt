
        WARRICK-Alexander B., 76, died February 9 in Vero Beach, FL. He is survived by a wife, the former Frances Stone, a son, Alexander Jr., a daughter-in-law, and 3 grand children. He was a member of the Yale class of 1942 and retired from the Chemical Bank in 1972 to build the Stage Neck Inn of York Harbor, ME. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be sent to the Hackley School, 293 Benedict Ave., Tarrytown, NY 10591.
