
        MATSON-George Donald. Of New Vernon, New Jersey, on Monday, November 13, 2000. Former Chief Financial Officer of the National Westminster Bank U.S.A. who was previously the C.F.O. of the National Broadcasting Company and began his career with the Arthur Anderson Co. Served in the Navy in World War II. He is survived by his wife, Margaret (nee Hawkes) Matson and his sons, Thomas of Silicon Valley, CA., and James of Beverly Hills, CA. Funeral services are private.
