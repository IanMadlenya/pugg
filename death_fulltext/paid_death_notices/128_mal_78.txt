
        FEARER-Joseph L., 87, of West Hartford, CT., and New York City died September 1 in North Branford, CT. He was husband of the late Eunice Holzman Fearer, father of Paul Fearer and Jane Fearer Safer, grandfather of Sarah Safer. Private burial is at Arlington National Cemetery. Funeral service September 7, 1997. Contributions in his memory may be sent to the Joseph and Eunice Fearer Fund, Museum of Modern Art, Department of Prints, 11 West 53 Street, New York, NY 10019.
        FEARER-Joseph L. We mourn the loss of our friend and patron, Joseph Fearer. He will be very much missed. Our heartfelt sympathy and condolences go to his family. Associates and Staff Print Dept The Museum of Modern Art
