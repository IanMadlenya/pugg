
        CARROLL-Albert, October 6, 1997 of Devon, Pennsylvania, formerly of Hartsdale, New York. Husband of Rhoda (nee Freudenthal), father of David William Carroll and Barbara Kay, brother of Evelyn Freedman, also survived by four grand children. Relatives and friends are invited to graveside services on Thursday, October 9, 1997 at 11:30 A.M., at the Roosevelt Memorial Park, Trevose, Bucks Co., PA. The family will return to the Kay residence & respectfully requests that contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of the donor's choice.
