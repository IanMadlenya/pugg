
        JACOBS-Thomas L. Suddenly on 11/27/99. Beloved husband of Ann Harris-Jacobs and devoted father of Lauren. Retired Deputy Commissioner of Corrections; former Commissioner of Probation; Adjunct Professor at John Jay College. He will be missed by all who knew him. Visiting hours 2-4 p.m. and 6-8:30 P.M., Wednesday, December 1, Benta's Funeral Home, 630 St. Nicholas Ave (141 St), NYC. Mass of Christian burial Thursday, December 2, 7:30 P.M. Church of St. Francis Xavier, 46 W. 16th St. between 5th and 6th Avenues. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate contributions to St. Aloysius Scholarship Fund, 223 W. 132 St, NYC 10027 or Capital Campaign Church of St. Francis Xavier, 55 W. 15 St., NYC 10011.
