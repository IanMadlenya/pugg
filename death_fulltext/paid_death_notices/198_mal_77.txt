Survived by his children Aaron Read of New York, Ryan, Connor and Aidan Read of Fairhaven, MA, his companion Judy Bard of Queens, his mother Carol Read Heron of Cross River, NY, and his sisters Carol Read of Edgartown, MA and Victoria Read of Denver, CO. Memorial service at St. Matthews Church, Bedford, NY, Friday, July 11, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in his name to the American Cancer Society. >>AD#
