
        ROSSELL-Rafael. Passed away unexpectedly Sunday, May 11, 1997, of heart failure. Beloved husband of Ana. Father of Roxana, Robert and Roman. Grandfather of Jocelyn. A dedicated father, psychoanalyst, critical intellectual and educator throughout his life. His family, friends, colleagues and students will miss his wit, insight and sensitivity. Memorial service Thursday, May 15th, Cobble Hill Chapels, 171 Court Street at Dean Street, Brooklyn, 6-8 P.M.
