
        SARNA--Joseph. The American Friends of the Ghetto Fighters Museum mourns the passing of Joseph Sarna, father of our board member, Mark Sarna. Joe's deep love of Israel and the Jewish people, and his commitment to Holocaust education live on in the good works of his children and grandchildren. We extend condolences to his beloved wife, Mania, and the entire Sarna family. Amy Miller, President Debbie Nahshon, Exec. Dir. Simcha Stein, Museum Dir.
