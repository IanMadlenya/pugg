
        LAMSON - Martha. Soprano, Teacher, Artist. Died in Norwalk, CT on Friday, January 14, 2000 at the age of 95. Performed with The Columbia Opera Company of NY in Canada as Gilda in Rigoletto in April & May of 1943; was a member of the Philadelphia LaScala Opera Company for the 1941-42 season; sang opposite Jan Peerce & Robert Weede as Gilda in Rigoletto at the Mosque Theater in Newark on December 3, 1943. Her stage name was Marta Rosana. She was a pupil and follower of the late Maestro J.H. Duval of NY. She taught bel canto singing for over 40 years in her studio at the Hotel Ansonia, NYC. She leaves no living relatives but had a host of friends, former pupils in the world of opera and singing. Interment will be at Woodlawn Cemetery, the Bronx at 11am on Tuesday, January 18.
