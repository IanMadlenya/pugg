
        WHITE-Aida Rovetti, 104. Of Waterford, CT, died on January 8th, in New London, CT. She was a founding trustee of the Eugene O'Neill Memorial Theatre Center in Waterford. Mother of George C. and Nelson H. White. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Saturday at 10 AM in St. Joseph Church, New London, CT. Calling hours will be held on Friday from 5-7 PM at the Fulton-Theroux Funeral Home, New London. Memorial contributions may be made to the Eugene O'Neill Memorial Theatre Center, 305 Great Neck Road, Waterford, CT. 06385, or to the Florence Griswold Museum, Old Lyme, CT. 06371.
