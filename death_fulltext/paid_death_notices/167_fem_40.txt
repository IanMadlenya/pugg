
        LAMBERT-Marjorie R. 76, of Niantic, CT, formerly of New York City, died December 1, 2000, in Waterford, CT. She was the wife of the late Dr. John T. Lambert. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM, on December 9, 2000, at St. John's Episcopal Church, Niantic, CT. Memorial donations may be made to a charity of the donor's choice.
