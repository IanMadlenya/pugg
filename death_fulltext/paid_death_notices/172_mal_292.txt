
        SEWELL-Danny (Dennis Edward). On May 18, 2001. An Obie Award-winning actor for ''The Homecoming,'' he was the original Bill Sikes in the West End and Broadway productions of ''Oliver!'' Always deeply loved and always remembered by his wife, Donna; his four children, Laura, Daniel, George and Andrew; his brother, George; their families and his many friends. Memorial celebrations in New York and London this summer. The world is a lot less colorful without him.
