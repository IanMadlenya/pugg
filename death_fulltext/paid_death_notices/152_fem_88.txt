
        KARAGHEUSIAN-Leila. September 6, 1999. Beloved daughter of the late Zabelle and Mihran Karagheusian. She was a philanthropist and president of the Howard Karagheusian Commemorative Corp. She was 96. Service Saturday, September 11 at 11:30AM, at Frank E. Campbell at 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery.
