
        MORSE-Harry Edson Sr. Born June 12, 1915 in Washington, D.C. died on April 1, 1999 after a brief illness in Los Angeles, CA. His wife, Dorothy Morse, preceded him in death. His children: Joan Brooke, Harry E. Morse, Candice Smith and Robert Morse, ten grandchildren and four greatgrandchildren survive him. Services will be held at St. John Fisher, Palos Verdes, California, on Monday, April 5, at 2PM. Interment will be at Klein Memorial Park FM 2920, Tomball, Texas. In lieu of flowers the family requests that memorial contributions be made to any state Masonic home for children or the charity of your choice. McCormick Mortuary, Redondo Beach, California, Directors 310-540-7444.
