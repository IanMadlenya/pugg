
        TAYLOR-William Robinson. 93 of Lyme, CT. died October 3, 1999 in Lyme. He is the husband of Marion Gewin Taylor and father of Mary Gay Taylor, Elizabeth Wall and Margaret Boyle, grandfather of three and brother of Arnold P. Taylor. A memorial service will be held at 2:00 P.M. on Wednesday, October 13, 1999 at the First Ccongregational Church of Old Lyme, Conn., Lyme Street, Old Lyme. Donations may be made in his memory to Planned Parenthood of CT, 129 Whitney Avenue, New Haven, CT 06520 or Yale University School of Medicine, Teaching and Research Fund, c/o Lawrence Cohen, 333 Cedar Street, I-207, New Haven, CT. 06520.
