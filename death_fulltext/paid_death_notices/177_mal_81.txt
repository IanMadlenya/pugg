
        SHUFRO-Salwyn. Beloved husband of 71 years of Edna; devoted father of Edward and Marlene Shufro and Joan and Ronald Silverman; grandfather of Joyce Shufro Espinosa, Nicholas and Jennifer Shufro, Gregory and Jennifer Shufro, Susanna Silverman, Valerie Silverman and greatgrandfather of Zachary and Julia Shufro. Son of Jacob J. and Rebecca P. Shufro. Born in 1905 in New York City. Graduate of City College and Harvard Business School. Co-founded Shufro, Rose & Co. in 1938 as a member of the New York Stock Exchange, an investment advisory firm. He was a man of vision and integrity, loved and esteemed by all who knew him. Services are private. The family will be receiving visitors at the home of Joan and Ronald Silverman, on Monday, October 8th after 7PM.
