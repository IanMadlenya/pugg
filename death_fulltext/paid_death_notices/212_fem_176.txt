
        FEINBERG -- Lillian. The Board of Trustees and staff of Medical Development for Israel, Inc., the support organization for Schneider Children's Medical Center of Israel, are deeply saddened by the loss of Mrs. Lillian Feinberg. Together with her husband Abraham, they established the Dr. E. Richard Feinberg Department of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry in memory of their son at Schneider Children's Medical Center of Israel. Their overwhelming generosity will benefit the children of Israel for generations to come. Our heartfelt condolences to Mrs. Feinberg's daughter, Dr. Judith Schneider, and her husband, Mr. Norbert Weissberg, and their entire family. Medical Development for Israel, Inc. H. Irwin Levy, Chairman Ambassador Uri Bar-Ner Co-Chairman Arlette Cohen Executive Director
