
        FAZIO--Charles R. A prominent international consultant, died suddenly on February 3, 2006. Born in Brooklyn, NY in 1936, son of George and Helen (Mulcahy) Fazio. Survived by his wife Carolyn; his children Carla Fazio, Teresa Fazio Hoffman and Steve Hoffman, Charles and Nanette Fazio, Victoria Fazio Capeletti and Clayton Capeletti, and Francesca Fazio; grandchildren Darius Radmanesh, Alexis Hoffman, Charles Fazio, William Fazio and Tyler Capeletti; and numerous cousins in New York, Ireland and Italy. Fazio graduated from Georgetown University in 1957. Following military service, Fazio's entire career was dedicated to serving non-profit organizations worldwide. He was cofounder and President of Fazio International Ltd., headquartered in Boca Raton, FL. Fazio held key volunteer positions with many non-profit organizations, notably serving as President of the Georgetown University Alumni Association and as Chairman of the Fellows, American Society of Association Executives. Visitation at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue, NYC, on Thursday, February 9 from 2-5 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. Mass will be celebrated on Friday, February 10 at 10:30 a.m.; St. Ignatius Loyola Catholic Church, NYC. Memorial services are also planned in Boca Raton and in Washington, DC. In lieu of flowers the family requests that contributions be made to the American Heart Association.
