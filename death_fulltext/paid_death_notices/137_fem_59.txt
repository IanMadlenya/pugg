
        McLAUGHLIN-Louise (nee Porter). Died June 7 after a long illness. Devoted wife of the late Dr. Francis McLaughlin. Loving sister of Dr. Herbert M Porter. Caring stepmother of Carol Miller. Grandmother, aunt and great-aunt to her many nieces and nephews. She will be deeply missed by her family and friends. As per her wish, her remains are to be cremated and scattered in the Chesapeake Bay, joining her late husband.
