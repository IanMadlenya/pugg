
        AFTAB-Nasser T. Sr, 79. Of Montvale, NJ, died December 31, 2001. He is survived by his wife Linda (nee Croucher) Aftab and his sisters Malley and Pouran. Beloved father of Marc, Sherine, Jahan (Jay), Dana, Nasser Jr, Blake, and Tyler. Cherished grandfather of Eva, Lydia, Alyssa, Chloe, Sarah, and Joseph. Former husband of Dolores Godfried Aftab. Also survived by nieces and nephews. Predeceased by two brothers Mansur and Amir Aftab. His family asks that in lieu of flowers donations be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 1311 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains, NY 10605, or www.leukemia.org. Friends may call at the Vander Plaat Colonial Home, Inc, 13-31 Saddle River Road, Fair Lawn, NJ, Thursday, 24 and 7-9 PM. Funeral service Friday, 10 AM at the funeral home. Entombment George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus, NJ.
