
        GUTTENPLAN-Milton. 83. Beloved husband of Rosalie. Devoted father of Kenneth and Barbara, Carol Wolf and the late Howard. Cherished brother of Harold. Adored grandfather of Joshua and Adam Wolf, Elena, Marra and Ethan. A Great Neck resident for 48 years, always involved in charitable causes. Founder and active member of the Board of Trustees of the Great Neck Synagogue. Served as V.P., Public Relations for the Great Neck United Community Fund. Board member of Great Neck Adult Education, served on the Board of the Jerusalem Reclamation Project. Retired Director of Advertising and Marketing at Barney's New York. Received four Clio Awards for outstanding TV commercials. He was also President of the ''100'' Club. Served as Captain in charge of the Pentagon printing office during WW II. Funeral services today, 1PM at ''Sinai Chapels'', 162-05 Horace Harding Expwy, Fresh Meadows, NY. Donations to Great Neck Synagogue Torah Fund or Great Neck United Comm. Fund.
        GUTTENPLAN - Milton. Great Neck Synagogue notes with sorrow the passing of its esteemed member, former Executive Vice President and former Men's Club President. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire bereaved family. May they be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dale Polakoff, Rabbi Dr. Ephraim Wolf, Rabbi Emeritus Mark Bunim, President
