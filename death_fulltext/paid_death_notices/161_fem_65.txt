
        REISMAN-Ethel I., of Miami Beach, at age 94. Preceeded in death last year by her loving husband of 70 years, Simon (Cy). Survived by son James, of NYC, and adoring grandchildren Adam, of Washington D.C. and Leah of NYC, devoted nephew Paul Sussman of Hollywood, FL, and caring neices Joanne Froelich of NYC and Diane Mandel of Beverly Hills, Ca. Ethel's career as a warm and caring teacher of young children in the NYC School System was memorable, highlighted by her creative use of the piano and her beautiful singing voice. She touched many lives and will be long remembered and missed.
