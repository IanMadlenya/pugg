
        WOODCOCK-Wilson W. Died December 16, at 91, after a brief illness. Avid gardener and walker, he taught physics at Hunter College until 1973. Wife Edith, sister Mary Elizabeth, daughters Elspeth and Johanne, sons Bruce and Mark, eight grandsons and a great-grandson will miss him dearly. Services at Grace Church, Nyack, December 30, at 2pm.
