
        ABRAMS-Lynn. 70, of Great Neck, on November 2, 2001. Cherished mother of Danal and Seth. Treasured ''Grammy'' of Madeline. Loving mother-in-law of Wendy. Gardener and craftsperson par excellence, computer whiz, animal lover and avid gem collector. Sage in advice and unlimited in knowledge. Former owner of New City Packing & Provision Co., Chicago, Illinois. For all this and more she will be greatly missed and loved always. Services today, 12:45 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers donations in her memory may be made to North Shore Animal League, Port Washington, New York.
