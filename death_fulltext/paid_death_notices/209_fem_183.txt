
        GRUDER--Vivian. A woman before her time, who experienced the world as few did. Peacefully, after a long fulfilling life. Dedicated executive assistant to Harry Brandt, president of Brandt Theaters. Dear sister of Ruth, Herman and the late Beatrice, Leon and Annette. Loving and devoted aunt and great-aunt to many. She will be remembered by her family and many friends. Contributions in her memory can be made to the Miriam Hospital Foundation, Providence, RI. in memory of her niece, Francine Steiner Brem. Services Sunday, 9:30 AM, at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., NYC.
