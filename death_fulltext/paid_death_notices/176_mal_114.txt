
        LAPORTE-William Frederick, 88, of NYC and Buck Hill Falls PA on September 10, 2001. Cherished husband for 53 years of the late Ruth Hillard Laporte, beloved father of Suzanne L. Westcott, Lynn A. Ducommun and William F. Laporte, III and devoted grandfather of nine grandchildren. Funeral service Thursday, September 13 at 2PM at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to New York-Presbyterian Hospital, Development Office, 525 East 68 St, Box 123, NY NY 10021.
        LAPORTE - William F. The Boards, Faculty, and Staff of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital, Colubia University College of Physicians & Surgeons, and Weill Medical College of Cornell University mourn the passing of our great friend and esteemed Hospital Life Trustee, William F. Laporte. Mr. Laporte, a retired Chairman of American Home Products, joined the Board of The Presbyterian Hospital in 1976. Until his last illness, he was an active participant on several Board committees and in many Hospital activities. We will miss his wisdom and unstinting dedication to the Hospital. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family.
