
        GILMARTIN--Mary D. Mary Doris Gilmartin, 83, of Vero Beach, died unexpectedly November 12, 2003. Loving wife of Roger T. Gilmartin (deceased) for 56 years. Surviving are her sons Thomas D. Gilmartin and Roger A. Gilmartin; daughter, Molly Gilmartin, Christin Gilmartin and Carol Gilmartin; and six grandchildren. Contributions can be to St. Joseph's College, Office of Institutional Advancement, 245 Clinton Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11205 in her memory.
