
        SPARROW--Sidney G. He was a prominent trial attorney for more than 60 years; he was the assinged attorney in the nationally publicized case of the murder of Kitty Genovese, by Winston Mosley. The father of longtime law partner Robert E. Sparrow, of Judge Joyce Sparrow and Denise Sparrow. Beloved grandfather of four, greatgrandfather of six. Also a talented artist, sculptor, poet, author, pilot, scuba diver and athlete; a truly renaissance man. Sidney G. Sparrow died after a long illness. He was 92.
