
        FRANK-Anna M. On November 3, 2000, of Manhattan in her 95th year. Beloved wife of the late Jerome H. Frank; loving mother of Marjorie and Frank Balaban and H. Stephen Frank. Adoring grandmother of James Schecter, Nancy Starkey, Chrissie Bennett and Jane Frank Pascal, Stacey Frank Kanihan, and Jonathan Frank. Cherished great-grandmother of nine loving great-grandchildren. Always in good humor and with a most generous heart, her indomitable spirit is held in warm memory by her family. Interment at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Valhalla, NY.
