
        TOLINS--Florence Jiler ''Peggy.'' Memorial services were held on Monday at 4 P.M. at Leevy's Funeral Home, in Columbia, SC. Ms. Tolins, wife of Dr. Stephen H. Tolins, died Sunday in Columbia, SC. She was a journalist at The Bridgeport News, and was also a physical therapist. She is survived by a daughter, Amelia Coughlin, a son, Joseph Tolins, a brother, William Jiler, grandson, Shaun, and a granddaughter, Kelsey.
