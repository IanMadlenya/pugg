
        DISHMAN-Arthur, M.D. On April 28, 2003. Associated with Lenox Hill, NYU, Beth Israel, Eye & Ear Infirmary and Bellevue for many years. Also served as Bloomingdale Stores Chief Physician in Manhattan. Beloved husband of Aida. Services Thursday 11AM at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65th St.
        DISHMAN-Arthur, M.D. The physicians, nurses and staff of Beth Israel Medical CenterSinger Division acknowledge with sadness the passing of our friend and colleague, Arthur Dishman, M.D., a longtime member of Beth Israel's Department of Medicine and a generous philanthopist to many programs and services at The Singer Division. To his wife, Aida, the entire Dishman family and all who loved him, we extend heartfelt condolences.
