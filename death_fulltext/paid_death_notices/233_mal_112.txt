
        BRUSH--Charles Francis III Ph.D. 83, of Shelter Island, New York, died peacefully in his sleep on Thursday, June 1, 2006. Dr. Brush was an archaeologist, former President of The Explorers Club, and a Director Emeritus of Brush Engineered Materials Inc. Beloved father of Barbara B. Wright, Danielle B. Schmidt, Karen A. Brush, and Charles F. Brush IV. He is also survived by seven grandchildren and a great granddaughter. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, June 27, at 4:30 PM at Church of the Epiphany, 1393 York Ave at 74th St. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Brush Foundation, 3500 Lorain Avenue, Suite 400, Cleveland, OH 44113-3704 (216) 334-2209 or The Fountain Valley School of Colorado, 6155 Fountain Valley School Road, Colorado Springs, CO 80911 (719) 390-7035.
        $(6$)BRUSH--Charles F. III. The American Geographical Society notes with regret the death on June 1st of Charles F. Brush, III, a Councilor of the society from 1972 until 2004, when he became Councilor Emeritus. A loyal, generous and lively member of our leadership for many years, he will be greatly missed. John E. Gould, Chairman Jerome E. Dobson, President Mary Lynne Bird, Executive Director
