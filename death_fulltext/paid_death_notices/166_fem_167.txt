
        KALBAN-Gladys. Beloved wife, mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother passed away peacefully at home in her 85th year. Wife of Bernard, mother of Lois, Philip, Jeffrey and Sidney, nine grandchildren and a great-grandson. Stalwart of Hadassah and long-term officer of its FreeportRoosevelt Chapter. Funeral service Friday, 12:00 noon at ''Gutterman's, Inc.'', 175 Longbeach Road, Rockville Centre, L.I.
