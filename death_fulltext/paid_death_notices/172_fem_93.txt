
        DUBIN-Anna. The family announces with sadness the passing of Anna Dubin on Friday, May 11, at age 104. Beloved mother of Esther Lichtman, Lillian Lashinsky and the late David Dubin, cherished sister of Pauline Frankel and the late Abe Weissman and Fanny Karp. Much loved grandmother, great-grandmother, aunt, cousin. We were blessed to have her in our lives and will remember her always for her remarkable spirit, joy for life, and love of family. Graveside service at 11 AM, on Monday, at New Mt. Zion Cemetery, Rutherford, NJ. Shiva Monday through Sunday, at the Lashinsky residence, Whitestone, NY.
