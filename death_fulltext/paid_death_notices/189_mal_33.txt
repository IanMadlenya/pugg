
        MUFSON-Monroe H., M.D., 76. Of Denville, NJ, passed away October 1, 2002, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Born in the Bronx in 1925 and a graduate of New York University College of Medicine in 1947 at the age of 21, he was a member of the Alpha Omega Alpha Honorary Medical Society, Fellow of the American College of Cardiology, and Fellow of the American College of Physicians. Also Diplomate, American Board of Internal Medicine, and former director of medicine in cardiology St. Clare's Hospital, Denville, NJ. He is survived by his wife, Barbara, of 55 years, and brothers, Dr. Alan Mufson of Boonton Township, New Jersey, and Donald of Closter, New Jersey.
