
        BEEKMAN -- Sara Fruhauf. On May 24, 2005 in her 104th year. Beloved wife of the late Sidney. Devoted sister of Henry Fruhauf, aunt of William Fruhauf. Visitation Wednesday 4-6PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Services Thursday 11:30AM at ''The Riverside.''
        BEEKMAN--Sara F. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted long time member, Sara F. Beekman, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. To our distinguished Administrator Emeritus, Henry Fruhauf, it is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories of his beloved sister will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Rabbi David M Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
