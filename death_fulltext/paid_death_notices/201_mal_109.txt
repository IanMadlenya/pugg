
        MILAZZO--Rev. Nicholas F. Entered eternal life on October 10, 2003. Pastor of St. Anthony Church, Wakefield, the Bronx. Ordained 51 years, serving 46 of those years in the Bronx. Survived by his sister Marie Carmela Maneri and many loving nieces and nephews. Reposing Tuesday and Wednesday at St. Anthony Church, 4505 Richardson Ave, Bronx. Mass of the Holy Eucharist Wednesday, 7 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday, 11 AM. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Anthony Church appreciated. For information, contact Sisto Funeral Home, 718892-2102, Frank Cieri, Manager.
