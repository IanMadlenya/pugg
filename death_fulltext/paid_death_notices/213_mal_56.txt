
        COLBY--Robert A., 84, on October 3, 2004. Beloved husband of Vineta for 57 years. B.A., M.A., and Ph.D. in 19th Century English Literature, University of Chicago. M.S.L.S. Columbia University. Professor, Library Science, at Queens College for 20 years. Author of numerous books and articles. Veteran W.W. II. He will be missed by family and friends.
