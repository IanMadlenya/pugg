
        HEISSENBUTTEL--Robert H., M.D. The faculty and staff of the Department of Surgery at Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons mourn the loss of our esteemed colleague, mentor and friend, Robert Heissenbuttel, M.D. For more than three decades, he dedicated his life to caring for our patients, supporting our faculty and imparting a strong and sensitive approach to educating our residents. His tender and thoughtful nature will be greatly missed. We extend our sincere sympathy to his wife, Sharon, daughter Beth Eifert and son Bill Heissenbuttel, and his beloved grandchildren. Eric A. Rose, M.D. Chairman Department of Surgery Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons
