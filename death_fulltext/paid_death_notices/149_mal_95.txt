
        FULLER-Frank Lanneau III. Age 80, of Pawlet, VT, formerly of Lloyd Harbor, NY, died June 4, 1999. He received his Bachelor of Arts degree at the University of Virginia 1940 and completed the University of Virgina Law School in 1948. Frank was a Captain in the U.S. Army and served in Italy during WWII. He was a member of the First Troop, City of Philadelphia. In 1956 he joined AT&T Company in New York, NY, where he became the General Tax Attorney of the Bell System. Mr. Fuller is survived by his wife of 56 years Martha (Ely) Fuller; a daughter Elizabeth Ely Fuller, Ph.D., of Medford, MA; a son Frank L. Fuller IV, FAIA, of Oakland, CA; his brothers Thomas Cornelius Fuller II of Raleigh, NC, and Williamson Whitehead Fuller of Portland, OR. For information, Brewster Funeral Service, Manchester, VT. A memorial will take place at a later time and place to be announced.
