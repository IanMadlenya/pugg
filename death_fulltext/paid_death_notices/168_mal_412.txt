
        SYPHER-Robert M., Jr. It is with deep sorrow and affection that we record the passing on January 18, 2001 of our member and past President (1955) and son of our Past President Robert M. Sypher Sr. (1943) of the General Society of Mechanics & Tradesmen of the City of New York. For many years, Robert Jr. served on various committees most notably on our School and Membership Committees. Robert Jr. joined the Society in 1941. Sincere condolences are sent to his wife, Helen, his sons Richard, Lewis, Robert, and many grandchildren, a grand niece, and great grandchildren, and his nephew Francis. A private memorial service was held on Monday, January 22, 2001.
