
        TAGHINIA-MILANI-Parviz. On October 24, 1999 in New York City. Beloved husband of Nahid Taghinia-Milani, loving father of Leila Taghinia-Milani Heller, dear father-in-law of Henry Heller, adored grandfather of Alexander and Philip Heller. Interment will be private. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, October 28, at 2 PM at the Church of All Souls, 1157 Lexington Avenue at 80 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to: Lenox Hill Hospital/Cardiac Care Unit, c/o Development Department, 100 East 77th St., New York, New York 10021. He devoted his life to numerous enterprises & charities that benefitted the well-being of many, and his immeasurable kindness and generosity to his family and friends will be dearly missed.
