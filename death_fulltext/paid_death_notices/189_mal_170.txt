
        SAXE-Edward L. War Intelligence, CBS Executive, MoMA Director, Harvard Club of New York President, Architectural Consultant, died at Memorial Sloan-Kettering July 27, 2002 at age 86. Loving husband, father, grandfather and friend, Edward L. Saxe was born on January 17, 1916 in Boston, MA., attended Boston Latin School, Harvard '37 and Harvard Business School. He was a 48-year resident of Westport, Connecticut. A Memorial Service will be held in his honor with a reception to follow on Thursday, October 17th at 2 P.M. at St. Thomas Church, 5th Avenue and 53rd Street, NYC. Mr. Saxe is survived by his wife of 56 years, Anthea; a son Andrew Saxe predeceased him at age 50. Also surviving are a son Christopher Saxe of Weston, CT. and three grandchildren: Caroline of Westport, CT., Madeleine and Adam of NYC. In lieu of flowers, it is asked that donations be made in his name to AmeriCares, 161 Cherry St., New Canaan, CT. 06840-4831.
