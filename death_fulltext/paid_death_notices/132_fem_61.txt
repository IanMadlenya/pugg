
        SAUNDERSON-Louise Astor Van Alen. December 30, 1997 in Santa Barbara, CA. Born 1910 in New York City. Daughter of the late James L. Van Alen and Margaret Post Van Alen Bruguiere. She is survived by Alexander Saunderson, her beloved husband of 50 years; a brother, William L. Van Alen; three nephews, James Langley Van Alen, William L. Van Alen, Jr., and James Laurens Van Alen, II, and a niece, Cassandra Ludington. Her loving nature and wit brightened the lives of everyone she knew. A memorial service will be held at 3 P.M. on January 9 at All Saints by the Sea Episcopal Church in Montecito. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Music Academy of the West, 1070 Fairway Road, Santa Barbara, CA. 93108.
