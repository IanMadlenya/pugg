
        MULHALL-William F., 81. Of Garden City, NY, passed away August 27, 1998. He is survived by his wife of 56 years Elise, daughter Ellen Morrell, sons John and Jeffrey, nine grandchildren, brothers Monsignor Francis X. Mulhall, James and sister Eileen Killea. Reposing at Fairchild Sons Funeral Home, Garden City, on Sunday & Monday 2-5 and 7-9. Funeral Mass to be held at St. Joseph's Catholic Church, Garden City, Tuesday 10:00AM, Interment at Cemetery of the Holy Rood, Westbury. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Parkinson's Disease Association, 1250 Hyland Boulevard, Staten Island, New York 10305 or the Alzheimer's Association 66 South St, Suite 106, Patchogue, NY 11772.
