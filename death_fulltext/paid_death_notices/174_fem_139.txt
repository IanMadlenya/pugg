
        STANSFIELD-Susan P. Widow of Dr. John B. Stansfield, on July 12. Beloved mother of Lindsey Stansfield Pryor and the late John Bradley Stansfield, Jr. Cherished grandmother of Christina, Antonia, Sam and Jack Pryor. She will be remembered by her family and friends for her warmth, enthusiasm and kindness. A memorial mass will be held in the fall. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Church of St. Jean Baptiste, 184 East 76th Street, New York 10021, would be appreciated.
