
        VREELAND--Donald Walker, 84, died on February 26. Born in Summit, NJ, graduate of Hotchkiss School and Princeton University, 1942, US Army Veteran World War II, stockbroker in New York until retirement to Stuart, FL in 1997. Recently, he lived at Meadow Lakes in Hightstown, NJ. Survived by his wife of 53 years, Peggy Waller Vreeland; four children, Karolyn Vreeland Blume, Douglas, Patricia and Garret; six grandchildren; sister, Jean Vreeland Mueller. Memorial services March 2, at 1:00 P.M. Central Presbyterian Church, Summit, NJ. In lieu of flowers contributions-Princeton Class of 1942, Annual Giving Endowment, P.O. Box 291, Princeton, NJ 08542, or charity of choice.
