
        YANEK--Dona Julia Victoria Albota Sands. 87, passed after a heart attack in her sleep on June 22, 2006. Julia married the late Henry Charles Yanek on June 21, 1942. After a long career as a cosmotologist, she retired to San Luis Obispo (SLO). Burial will be at Mount St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing at 2 o'clock on June 29, 2006. She is survived by her sister Mathilda, 96, of Florida, and her children John H.P.C. Yanek, O.S.B., O.B.L., D.D., Dean of Holy Rood, who will preside over the Funeral Mass in SLO on June 27, 2006, and Carol A.M.Y. Coch of Forest Hills, NY.
