
        MASON--Deborah S. The Boards of Directors, affiliate and national staff and volunteers of the American Heart Association mourn the passing of Deborah Mason, Sr. Vice President for Health Initiatives, Advocacy and Communications. With conviction, intelligence, sensitivity and grace, Deb led us in the work of our mission. A beloved colleague and devoted and trusted friend, Deb will forever be remembered for her humor, generosity of spirit, loyalty to friends, family and the Yankees, and courage. Our heartfelt condolences to her children Hannah and Luke and the entire family.
