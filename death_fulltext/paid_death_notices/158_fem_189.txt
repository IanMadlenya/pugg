
        FRYDMAN-Gusta (Winchester). Died at home March 21. Born July 25, 1919 in Poland. Loving and beloved wife of Gregory, mother of Anne, grandmother of Sophia and Antonia, mother-in-law of Stephen Dixon, much loved friend and psychotherapist to many people, author of ''My Sessions with Kay.'' Services at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Thursday, March 23, at 2 P.M.
        FRYDMAN-Gusta. Our cherished friend whose gentleness and grace affected everyone around her. We will miss you. With deepest sympathy to Gregory, Anne, Steven, Sophia and Antonia. Mary, Mark, Esther and Joe
