
        BARTONE--William Patrick, 50, died suddenly July 3, 2006. Devoted husband of Kathleen (nee Larkin). Loving father of Patricia Ann. Beloved son of Diane and Philip Bartone. Survived by his brothers Frederick and Philip, as well as many loving brothers and sisters-in-law, nieces and nephews, and Molly and Grace. A lifelong resident of Bay Ridge, Mr. Bartone was a partner in Commercial Fish Co. He spent many happy years at the Fulton Fish Market, but his happiest times were in Southampton with his wife and daughter. Visitation Wednesday and Thursday 2-5 and 7-9 PM at McLaughlin & Sons Funeral Home, Third Ave. at 97th St., Brooklyn. Mass of Christian burial on Friday 10 A.M., St. Anselms Church.
