
        EDER-Barbara Straus. Died November 8, 1997, after a long illness. Beloved wife of Howard Eder, M.D. Loving mother of Rebecca Eder and Peter Shile of St. Louis, MO, Susan Eder and Jonathan Flescher of Raleigh, NC, and Michael and Gundi Eder of Acton, MA. Dear grandmother of Sam, Max, Betsy, Daniel and Robert. She was a unique and vibrant woman; an early activist in the peace and civil rights movements. A graduate of Cornell and New York School of Social Work. She was a psychotherapist with a strong interest in psychoanalytic ideas and a talented artist. Service private. Memorial donations may be sent to Planned Parenthood Federation, 810 7th Ave., NY, NY 10019.
