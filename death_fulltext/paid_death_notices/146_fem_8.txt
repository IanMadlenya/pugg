
        FEIRING-Ruth M. 87, Sarasota, died February 20, 1999. She was born September 7, 1911 in New York City and went to Sarasota 27 years ago. She taught history and economics at Richmond Hill and Martin Van Buren High Schools and was a college adviser to graduates. She received a bachelorsdegree from Hunter College in New York. She was a member of Hadassah of Sarasota, O.R.T., B'nai B'rith Aurel Rosen Lodge and Temple Beth Sholom. Survivors include her husband William of Sarasota; a sister, Anne Rosenthal of Sarasota; and two nephews, Steven and Eugene Rosenthal. Memorial donations may be made to Hospice of Southwest Florida, 5955 Rand Boulevard, Sarasota, FL. 34238.
