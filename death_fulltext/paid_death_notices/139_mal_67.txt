
        KAISER-Richard Jack. In Scarsdale, New York on August 5, 1998. Beloved husband of Lynne MacQuarrie. Cherished father of Charlotte, Joseph, Martha and Henry. Dear Brother and Brother-in-Law of Marilyn & the late Paul Kaiser, Marjorie and Robert Cracower. Son of the late Hedwig and the late Max Kaiser. Funeral service will be held at Paperman and Sons, 3888 Jean Talon West, Montreal, Canada on Friday, August 7th at 1:00 PM. Burial will follow at Temple Emanu-el, Beth Shalom Congregation Section, Mount Royal Cemetery in Montreal, Canada. Shiva will be observed at the Kaiser family residence, 6 Kensington Rd., Scarsdale, New York beginning Saturday evening. Donations in Richard's memory may be made to Columbia University, Kaiser Family Research Fund, Box 93, 630 West 168th Street, New York, New York 10032 Attn. Lynn Roth (212) 304-7200 or the Anti-Defamation League, Kaiser Fund, 832 United Nations Plaza, New York, New York 10017.
        KAISER-Richard. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of our friend and colleague Richard Kaiser. An ADL National Commissioner, Richard devoted many years of caring and generous support and service to the League as a major contributor and leader in the Jewish community. We will greatly miss his friendship, enthusiasm and understanding. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Lynn and his children Charlotte, Joseph, Martha and Henry. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
        KAISER-Richard. The Officers & members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of their dear friend and fellow member, Richard Kaiser. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Lynne, to his children and other members of his family. Robert K. Low, President
