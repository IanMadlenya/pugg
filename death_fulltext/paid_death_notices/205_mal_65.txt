
        SOCHOLITZKY -- Sidney (Soch), 91, of Oakland, CA. formerly of Roosevelt, NJ died Wednesday, January 28. Husband of Hortense (Honey), father of David and Elinor. Father-in-law of Sandy Niemann, grandfather of Sophie. He was a history buff, a fighter for civil rights and the rights of working men and women. He had a large number of old and new friends, all of them aware of his strong principles. A memorial meeting will be held on Sunday, February 8 at 3:00 PM at Claremont House, 4500 Gilbert St, Oakland, CA.
