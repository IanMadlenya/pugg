
        WATSON-John ''Jack'' Paul Watson, 54, of Avon, CT., husband of Valerie (Dawson) Watson, died Wednesday, March 24, 1999, at his home. He was the Corporate VicePresident and Group Executive of the Danaher Corp. in Simsbury, CT. Besides his wife he is survived by his daughter Laura, son Timothy and brother Tom of Wayne, PA. Memorial Service will be Saturday, April 17, at 3:00 p.m., in Christ Church, 35 Harris Rd, Avon. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the American Liver Foundation, PO Box 4062, Woodbridge, CT, 06525
