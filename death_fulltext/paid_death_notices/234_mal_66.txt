
        BERMAN--Bennett. Of Palm Beach, FL, formerly of New York City, died on July 2, 2006 at his home. He was 91 years of age. He was a successful entrepreneur and a generous philanthropist. He leaves his wife, Marilyn, children Jeffrey and Helaine, and grandchildren Justin and Jordan. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., on Friday, July 7, 2006, at 11:30 AM. Interment Montefiore Cemetery.
        $(6$)BERMAN--Bennett M. The Palm Beach Country Club expresses its sorrow at the passing of its esteemed member, Bennett Berman, and extends sincere sympathy to his wife and other members of his family. Ira Eichner, President Arthur Loring, Secretary
