
        FOLEY-John Edward. Brewer, ME. J. Edward Foley, 72, died July 16, 1997 in Brewer. He was born in Bangor, May 14, 1925. The son of J. Edward and Freda Weston Foley. Survivors include his wife Eileen; sons James E. Foley, NYC and Newport, RI, Mark A. Foley, Brewer; daughters Diana J. Rose, Walnut Creek, CA, Melissa A. Foley, Tucson, AZ; two grandchildren. Services were held July 19, in Bangor. Memorial contributions may be made to the Maine Historical Society, 485 Congress Street, Portland, ME 04101.
