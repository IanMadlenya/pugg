
        JONES-Uriah, 75, of 92 Hinman St., West Haven, CT died June 14, 2000. He had worked for the City of New Haven in the engineering department. He had served as a merchant marine. Mr. Jones, an Olympic Fencing Champion, officially ranked among America's 10 best fencers for 10 consecutive years. He was a member of the U.S. National, the U.S. World, the U.S. Olympic & U.S. Pan American Championship Teams and was the 6 time CT, 4 time North Atlantic & two time American Champion; he also was a 4 time Medalist in the Martini & Rossi International Tournament and recently had been elected to the Fencing Hall of Fame and was to have been inducted next week in Austin, TX. He leaves four sons, Uriah Jones III of Middletown, CT, Douglas of Essex, CT, Jeffrey of Birmingham, AL., and Noah of Westbrook, CT. Contributions may be made C/O Patricia A. Petro, Fleet Bank, 125 Westbrook Road, Essex, CT. 06426. Visiting hours at Maresca Funeral Home, 592 Chapel St. New Haven, CT Thurs 4-8 pm. Memorial services will be held at a later date.
