
        KRINSKY-Milton, M.D. on Feb. 3, 1997.         KRINSKY-Milton, M.D. We are saddened by the passing of Milton Krinsky, M.D. Milton was an integral partner in the Central Brooklyn Medical Group for forty years and functioned beautifully as an internist and pulmonologist. He was a fine physician and a great gentleman. Alexander Ellman, M.D. Medical Director Central Bklyn Medical Group P.C.
