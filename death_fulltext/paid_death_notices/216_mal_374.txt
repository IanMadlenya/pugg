
        LLOYD-EVANS--John Wynn, died on January 26 at the age of 80. He was born in Doncaster, Yorkshire, England. From 1964-1975 he taught at St. Bernard's School in New York City. He is survived by his wife, Alberta Bonynge Lloyd-Evans, who lives in Houston. For those desiring, memorial contributions given to the Make-A-Wish Foundation would be greatly appreciated. For additional information contact Geo. H. Lewis & Sons Funeral Directors in Houston at 713.789.3005.
