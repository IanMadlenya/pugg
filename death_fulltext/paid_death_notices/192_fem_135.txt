
        GILPATRIC-Miriam Thorne. The Board of Trustees, Executive Director and the staff of the National Foundation for Facial Reconstruction deeply mourn the death of Mimi Gilpatric. She served as a boardmember for 13 years and her dedication and generosity were both respected and appreciated. We express our heartfelt condolences to her sister, children and grandchildren. Joseph G. McCarthy, M.D. John R. Gordon, President J. Peter Hoguet, Chairman
