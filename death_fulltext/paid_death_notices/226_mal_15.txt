
        COLEMAN--John W., MD, Born Jersey City, NJ. Age 66, of New York City and Ocean Port, NJ, died Saturday, October 29, 2005. Dr. Coleman was a Clinical Associate Professor of Urology at the Joan & Sanford Weill Medical College of Cornell University, New York Presbyterian Hospital. U.S. Navy Veteran 1966-1968. Vietnam Veteran. Served as Lieutentant in the U.S. Navy Medical Corp. Served on the LPH 839 Carrier. Survived by his beloved Rely. Devoted father of John III, Diane Simon and Mari Kim Novak. Loving grandfather of four grandchildren. Funeral service Saturday, 9AM, from the Volk Leber Funeral Home, 789 Teaneck Rd., Teaneck, NJ then to Holy Trinity RC Church, Hackensack, NJ for a 10AM Mass of Christian Burial. Family and friends are invited to visit on Thursday, 128pm, at the John Krtil Funeral Home, 70th and 1st Avenue, NY, NY, and on Friday, 6-9PM at the Volk Leber Funeral Home in Teaneck. In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests donations be made in Dr. Coleman's honor to The Pediatric Urology Fund, C/O New York Presbyterian Hospital, 525 East 68th Street, Starr-900, NY, NY 10021.
