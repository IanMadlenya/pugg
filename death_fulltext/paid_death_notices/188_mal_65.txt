
        TARDIO-Michael Ames, 35. Born on November 17, 1966, died tragically on September 2, in Los Angeles, CA. Mr. Tardio was the husband of Kelsey Oldershaw Tardio, devoted father of Lucas Shaw, age 5, and Riley Gristina, age 3; cherished son of Margaret & Neil Tardio; loving brother of Amy and David Blumberg, Jeryl and Kenneth Malloy and Julia and Neil Tardio Jr. He was an adoring uncle to Conor, Maggie, Kenny, Shane, Andrew, Alex, William and Max. The Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Monday, September 9, 2002, at 5 PM, at the Church of the Resurrection, 910 Boston Post Road, Rye, NY. The interment will be private. In lieu of flowers contributions to: The Michael Tardio Family Fund (a trust for the education of Mr. Tardio's children), C/O Smith Barney-attn: Mr. Ken Malloy, 1345 Avenue of The Americas, 21st floor, New York, NY 10105, would be greatly appreciated.
