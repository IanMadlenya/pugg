
        GROESCHEL-Eileen Bosquett. Of Sea Girt died Monday, Sept. 13, 1999. She was born in Jersey City, NJ, the daughter of John and Catherine Sullivan Groeschel. She was a graduate of Fairleigh Dickinson University and received her Master's Degree from Fordham University. Mrs. Groeschel taught Physical Education and English in Jersey City Public Schools. She was predeceased by her husband August H. Groeschel, MD in 1989. She is survived by her family: Margaret G. Anderson of Northport, NY; Catherine B. Groeschel of New York City; Moya M. Groeschel of New York City; Peter M. Groeschel of Wyoming; Noel M. Groeschel of Staten Island; 8 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Muscular Dystrophy Association 10 East 40th Street, Room 4110, New York, NY 10016 would be appreciated.
