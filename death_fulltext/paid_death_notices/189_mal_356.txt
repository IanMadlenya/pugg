
        LIEBOWITZ-William. 83, of Purchase, NY, on October 26, 2002. Beloved husband of Estelle, devoted father of Sherry Moran, Howard Liebowitz, M.D., and Lauren Coffman, and five grandchildren. He was devoted and dedicated for 58 years as a CPA in his private practice. Services on Tuesday, 10 AM, at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Boulevard, New Rochelle. Interment at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY.
        LIEBOWITZ - William. The Board of Governors and members of Brae Burn Country Club record with great sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, William Liebowitz. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Estelle and his entire family. James Speiser, President Monty Davis, Secretary
        LIEBOWITZ - William. Bill was one of our oldest and dearest friends. He was his own man. He lived in what he believed in. Sincere condolences to his loving wife Estelle, his children Sherry, Howard and Lauren and his grandchildren. Rest in peace. We will miss you. Rita and Paul
