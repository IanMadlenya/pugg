
        SCHNEIDER-Lorraine, 67. On January 12, 2002 after a heroic struggle. An inspiration to so many, she will be missed for her vitality, wit, strength, grace and wisdom. Devoted wife of the late Edward L. Schneider and sister to Irwin Levy. Treasured mother to Maria, Peggy, Katy, Andrew, Alma, Alice and Zoe and grandmother to Olive, Joy, Mae, Eddie, Henry, Ellis, Seth, Bea and Ilah. Memorial service Sunday, January 20, 9 A.M., UN Chapel, 44th Street and 1st Avenue.
