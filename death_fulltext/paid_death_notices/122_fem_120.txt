
        FELD-Eva (nee Feinberg), 88. Passed away peacefully in her home in Clifton, NJ, March 15. Beloved wife of the late Morris and mother of the late Stanley. Survived by two sisters, Adeline Kramer, Sylvia Gurevitz; two brothers, Harold and Saul Feinberg. Also survived by ten nieces and nephews. Together with her husband, she was a benefactor to American Folk Art Museum, The Jewish Museum, The Metropolitan Opera, Daughters of Miriam Center for the Aged, Clifton, Temple Emanuel, Passaic, the Passaic-Clifton Jewish Federation and Beth Israel Hospital, Passaic. Funeral will take place Monday at noon at the Jewish Memorial Chapel, Passaic.
