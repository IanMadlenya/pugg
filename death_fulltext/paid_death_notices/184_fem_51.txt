
        HEATER-Jeanne E. of Tuxedo, NY died Saturday May 4, 2002 at the age of 69. Born in Tuxedo Jeanne was a lifelong resident and was retired as Court Clerk in Tuxedo, NY and was a member of the Silver Dollar Club in Tuxedo. She is survived by her beloved husband of 45 years Richard E., two sons Richard and wife Wendy of Holland, PA, Andrew and wife Jacqui of Tuxedo and one daughter Ann and Chris Bynes of Brewster, NY; 8 grandchildren Ricky, Michael, John, Kelly, Luke, Caitlin, Shannon, and Christopher; two brothers Msgr. William Smith of the Bronx, NY and John and Thelma Smith of Tuxedo; one sister Margaret and Robert LaBurt of Tuxedo and a brother-in-law Fred Grieve of Bayshore, Long Island. Jeanne was pre-deceased by a sister Anne Grieve and one brother Edward G. Smith. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Wednesday May 8, 2002 at 10:00 A.M. at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church in Tuxedo. Friends may call on Tuesday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Scarr Funeral Home, Inc. Route 202 Suffern. In lieu of flowers donations to Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church would be appreciated. For directions and Jeanne's Memorial and guestbook please visit www.scarrfuneralhome.com
