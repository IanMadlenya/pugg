
        KAUFMAN-Moshe. The diamond industry mourns the death of our distinguished leader. A member of the Diamond Dealers Club for more than 60 years, and a member of the Diamond Industry Hall of Fame, Mr. Kaufman's loss is felt by his colleagues worldwide. Our deepest sympathies are extended to his wife Giselle and his children Joyce, Arthur, James and Harry. Eli Haas, Pres., DDC
