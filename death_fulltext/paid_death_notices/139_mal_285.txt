
        POLIKOFF-Harry. The Officers, Board of Directors, Trustees and Staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind, note with sorrow the passing of Harry Polikoff, husband of Guild Trustee, Wanda. He was a committed supporter of The Guild and its mission to aid blind and visually impaired persons. We extend our deepest sympathies to Wanda and the family. James M. Dubin, Chairman Alan R. Morse, President
