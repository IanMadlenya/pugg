
        BASNER-Max Irving, age 88. Born July 4, 1913 in NYC. Passed away peacefully under hospice care on April 11, 2002, in Florida of congestive heart failure. A graduate of CCNY, Columbia School of Law and Brooklyn Law School. He served in the US Army during World War II as an instructor and lawyer, and retired from the active reserves as a Lt. Col. His legal career in the NYS was as a Hearing Officer and Administrative Law Judge in the Dept. of Motor Vehicles. His first wife, Nettie Berlin Basner pre-deceased him, as did two brothers. He is survived by his wife Lillian Cukier Basner, his daughters Sandra of Redding, CT and Shari of Columbia, MD; grandchildren Jason, Rachel, Kyla and Jenny; step-grandchildren Eileen and Holly. He is also survived by his brother Leo, sister Charlotte and many cousins, nieces, nephews and friends. Interment was private. Donations in his memory may be made to the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America.
