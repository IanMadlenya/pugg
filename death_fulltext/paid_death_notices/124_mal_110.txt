
        GRAY-Austen T., died May 9 at the age of 89. Beloved husband of Milo, loving father of Phyllis Heaton, Alice Standteiner, Pamela Resse and Austen T. Gray Jr. Adored grandfather of 13 and great grandfather of 7. Mr. Gray was managing director of Trainor Wortham, honorary director of the New York Eye & Ear Infirmary and Trustee Emeritus at Fay School, Southboro, MA. Service on Monday, May 12, 11 AM, St. John's Episcopal Church, Cold Spring Harbor, LI. Donations appreciated to New York Eye & Ear Infirmary, 2nd Ave & 14th Street, NYC 10003.
        GRAY-Austen T. With great sorrow, The New York Eye and Ear Infirmary Board of Directors Medical Staff and Administration mourn the passing of a devoted friend to the hospital and its patients. Elected as a Trustee in 1957, Mr. Gray served as Treasurer for 25 years, and Chairman from 1987 to 1990. During his long tenure on the Board, he led the Infirmary through a period of rapid growth, resurgence of educational and research activities, and fundraising to rebuild many facilities. His financial acumen, wisdom and counsel will be greatly missed. The Infirmary community extends heartfelt condolences to his wife, Milo and his entire family. Joseph R. Burkart Chairman, Board of Directors Robert C. DellaRocca, M.D. Chairman, Medical Board Joseph P. Corcoran President and CEO
