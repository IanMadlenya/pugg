
        LEDERER-Norma Jean, 69, on February 24 at Calvary Hospital after a three year fight against esophageal cancer. Musician, caterer and life force who commanded friends and admirers on the upper west side where she lived for more than 30 years, the West Coast, Switzerland and her native Chicago. Survived by her partner, Milton Moskowitz; daughter and son-in-law Patricia and Timothy Michaels, sister Patricia Taylor, and mother Hortense Harris. Contributions to Jacob Perlow Hospice at Beth-Israel Hospital.
