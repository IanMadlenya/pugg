
        MILLER-Carolyn Seiden. On October 18, 2000. An independent women of courage and great strength. Her life was her family; to her grandchildren-Stephen, Jenny, Katie and Jonathan-she promised the stars, and they gave her the universe. She leaves behind her loving daughters Barbara Behar and Jane Bursky, devoted sons-in-law Joel Behar and Andrew Bursky, and the Hirsch Family, brother-in-law Barry, newphews, Terry and Neil and niece Nancy. She will always be remembered by family and dear friends for her fight and spirit, her courage and grace. We will always cherish her memory. Services Friday, October 20. 10:00 AM, Temple Emanuel, Great Neck, New York.
