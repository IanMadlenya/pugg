
        O'BRIEN-John J. The New York Presbyterian Hospital is deeply saddened by the passing our dear friend and colleague, John O'Brien. Mr. O'Brien joined the Hospital in 1990 and served as Director of Admitting at the New York Weill Cornell Center. His warm personality, dedication and commitment made him a joy to work with. We join all those who knew him in mourning his loss, and extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family. David B. Skinner, M.D. Vice Chairman & CEO Kathryn Martin Senior Vice President & COO Andrea Castellanos Vice President for Operations
