
        LUBICH-Bernice. On Saturday, February 7, 1998, at her home in New York City after a long and valiant battle with cancer. Beloved wife of Milton. Dear sister-in-law of Debbie and Aaron Schochet. Loving aunt of Susan and the late Steven Schochet. Services Tuesday, February 10, 12 noon at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. The family will observe Shiva Tuesday evening through Thursday morning at the Park Lane Hotel, 320 Central Park South. In lieu of flowers, charitable contributions may be made to The Pines Care Center, P.O. Box 5333, Fire Island Pines, NY 11782-0999.
        LUBICH-Bernice. The Board of Directors of Fire Island Pines, Inc., deeply mourns the passing of a dear friend and fellow Board Member. During her tenure, Ms. Lubich served in the capacity as Chairman of the House Rules and Screening Committees. Ms. Lubich was a resident of the Pines since the inception of the Cooperative. Her dedication and interest was not only limited to the Coop but to the entire Fire Island Pines community. She will be deeply missed by all.
        LUBICH-Bernice. We mourn the loss of Bernice, our dear friend and neighbor. Bernice and her devoted husband Milton have been an integral part of our community for many years. They attended our first holiday services in 1973 and have continued as faithful supporters ever since. We extend our deepest sympathies to our beloved friend Milton. Congregation B'nai Olam, Fire Island Pines.
