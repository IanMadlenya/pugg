        BRANDES-Bernard E. The Trustees of the Harkness Foundation for Dance mourn the untimely passing of our esteemed colleague Bernard Brandes. Bernie served the Foundation with distinction and dedication for more than thirty-five years. We extend our deepest sympathy to his loving wife Helen who has been steadfast in her support of his Foundation activities. Theodore S. Bartwink Etta Brandman Barrett G. Kreisberg William A. Perlmuth
        BRANDES-Bernard E. His friends and colleagues at Stroock & Stroock & Lavan mourn the passing of our beloved Partner, Bernard E. Brandes. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family.
