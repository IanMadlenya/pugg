
        WERSTEIN-Charlotte. The National Offices of Emunah of America mourn the loss of Charlotte Werstein, beloved sister of our Honorary National President and Financial Secretary, Shirley Billet. Charlotte was a woman who gave unstintingly of herself to her family, friends and Emunah. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld National President Emunah of America
