
        SCHWEITZER-Michael. Founder and President of The RAM Group. It is with incredible sadness that we share the news of Michael's passing on May 4, 2003. Dear brother of Alan Schweitzer of New York, nephew of Irving Schweitzer of Texas, lifelong friend of David Klein of Colorado, and a true leader to his family at The RAM Group across Canada and in the United States. Born in the Bronx and educated at Harvard Business School, Michael enjoyed great success throughout his life and career and will be greatly missed by all those who knew him.
