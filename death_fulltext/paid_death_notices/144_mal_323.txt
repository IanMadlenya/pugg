
        HUFFINE-Herbert Taylor, Age 77. On January 18, of Manchester, Vermont and formerly of Morristown, N.J. Beloved husband of Julianne, Loving father of Phillip, Peter and Melissa, predeceased by a son Michael. Grandfather of Rachel, Taylor, Savanna and Caitlin. He was much loved and will be greatly missed. A man of fine integrity, strength and compassion for others. After service as a Navy PT boat commander during W.W. II, he worked in national sales of Mueller Company and residential real estate until recently. His many interests included model train collections, community work, including support and devotion to the restoration of Hildene, the former residence of Robert Todd Lincoln. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Friends of Hildene, Box 377, Manchester, VT 05254. Memorial services will take place on Friday, January 22, at First Congregation Church, Manchester, VT. at 9:30 A.M.
