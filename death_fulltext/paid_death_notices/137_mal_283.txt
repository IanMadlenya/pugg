
        PLOTKIN-Nathaniel, M.D. On June 26th, 1998, following a brief illness. Beloved husband, father, grandfather and compassionate, devoted physician. We will miss you deeply. Services to be held Sunday, June 28, 1998 at 1 P.M. at ''Parkside'' Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, N.Y. near Avenue V (opposite King's Plaza).
        PLOTKIN-Nathaniel, M.D. The Medical Staff of Maimonides Medical Center records with deep sorrow the untimely death of Dr. Nathaniel Plotkin. Dr. Plotkin was affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in the Department of Medicine for over 30 years. He was an excellent physician, clinician and teacher. He was loved by all his patients. He will be missed. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. Warren Wexelman, M.D., Pres. Jacob Shani, M.D., Pres.
