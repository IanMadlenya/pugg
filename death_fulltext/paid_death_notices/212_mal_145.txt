
        LUSCZYNSKI--Henry, 83, Beloved father of Lori Simon and son-in-law Joel M. Simon of NYC. died September 13th. A World War II Marine Corp meteorologist, he was a talented draughtsman designer developing the G.I. Joe doll and Sunfish sailboat. Funeral celebration Saturday in Springfield, MA. Contributions to Marion Sarcoidosis Foundation, C/O United Bank 1077 St. James Avenue, Springfield, MA 01104.
