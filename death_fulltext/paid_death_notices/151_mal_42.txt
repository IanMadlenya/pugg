
        McVEIGH-Christopher R. Of Montclair, New Jersey, formerly of Somers, New York, died August 2 at Sloan-Kettering Memorial Hospital in New York City. Mr. McVeigh worked as VP of Marketing for Prudential Insurance Company in Newark, NJ for the past two years. Mr. McVeigh was born on August 24, 1965 in Brooklyn, NY to Robert & Marie Murphy McVeigh. He attended Regis High School in New York City, graduating in 1983. He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Holy Cross College in Worcester, Mass. in 1987. Mr. McVeigh lived in Montclair, NJ for the past year. Mr. McVeigh lived in Somers, NY from 1992 to 1998. He was a parishioner of St. Cassians Church, Montclair, New Jersey. Mr. McVeigh is survived by his wife Joann K. (nee Gleason) of Montclair, NJ, father of Colleen Anne and Kevin Robert. Son of Robert A. and Marie B. McVeigh. Brother of Laura Masi of Stamford, CT, Gregory McVeigh of Fairfield, CT, Jenifer Maggiacomo of Quincy, MA and Maureen McVeigh of Vienna, VA. Stepson of Eileen C. McVeigh of Somers, NY. Son-inlaw of Kevin and Mary Virginia Gleason of Englewood, CO. Brother-in-law of John Gleason of Englewood, CO, Mary Carol Gleason of Englewood, CO, James Gleason of Denver, CO, Daniel Gleason of Montclair, NJ, John Masi of Stamford, CT, Jenifer Smith McVeigh of Fairfield, CT and Steven Maggiacomo of Quincy, MA. Three nephews and two nieces. Mr. McVeigh is predeceased by his mother Marie B. McVeigh, who died in 1996. Visitation Wednesday and Thursday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM at the Cassidy Flynn Funeral Home, Mt. Kisco, NY. The funeral is Friday 10 AM, at St. Joseph's Church, Croton Falls, NY. Interment will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery, Croton Falls, NY. Friends may send memorial donations in memory of Christopher R. McVeigh c/o Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021.
