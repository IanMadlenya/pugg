
        BROOKS-Rozanne M., 75. Distinguished Teaching Professor with The State University of New York and retired professor of sociology and anthropology at the Cortland campus, died on Thursday, Dec. 18. Brooks founded the Sociology Department in 1964 and, as chair until 1973, added anthropology to the curriculum. She retired in 1992 after 36 years of service to the College and established an award for dedicated teaching faculty. The College will hold a Memorial Service in early Spring.
