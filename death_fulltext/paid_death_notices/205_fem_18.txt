
        BURKES--Adele (nee Mishkin). 85, died on February 1. Devoted wife of 65 years to Albert. Beloved mother of Susan Baron, Dr. Lynn and Dr. Jeffrey Burkes. Dear motherin-law of Susan. Loving grandmother of Jennifer and Michael Leifer, and Elizabeth and Katherine Burkes. Services Tuesday, February 3, at 11:30 am, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in her memory may be made to the National Multiple Sclerosis Society or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
