
        SAYERS-Pauline C., 83, unexpectedly on February 29, 2000, deeply beloved and cherished mother of Lenore Sayers and Barbara and Charles Ducey: dear grandmother of Michael, Jessica, and Joanna Ducey: daughter of the late Rabbi J.X. and Sadie Cohen, also survived by many cousins and friends. An enduring inspiration for her courageous, gentle, kind and loving spirit. Funeral services on March 2nd, 10:30 A.M. at Boulevard Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, N.Y. Gifts in her memory may be made to The United Jewish Appeal/Federation of Jewish Philanthropies, 130 East 59 Street, New York.
