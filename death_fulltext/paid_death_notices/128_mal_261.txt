
        PILLERSDORF-Herbert K. The family of Herbert K. Pillersdorf, distinguished attorney and former small claims court arbitrator, mourns his peaceful passing on September 20, 1997, the day of his 78th birthday. He is survived by his wife Adele, his children Mr. and Mrs. Gary Pillersdorf, Dr. and Mrs. Alan Pillersdorf, his adored grandchildren Stephanie, Andrew, Lance, Alexander, Geoffrey, Tara, and his brother Arthur. Services Monday, 2 PM at ''The BoulevardRiverside Chapels,'' Ocean Pkwy at Prospect Park, Brooklyn.
