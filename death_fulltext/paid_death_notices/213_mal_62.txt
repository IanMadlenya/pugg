
        SCHWARTZ--Ralph, MD. The Board of Trustees, Department of Pediatrics and the entire staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express deep sorrow over the death of Ralph Schwartz, MD, a distinguished member of our staff for over 26 years, who was greatly admired by his patients and colleagues. The Lenox Hill Hospital family honors Dr. Schwartz's memory and extends sincere sympathies to his family. William O. Hiltz, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO James J. Richter, MD, PhD, President, Medical Board Armando Grassi, MD, Chairman, Dept. of Pediatrics Norman Schulman, MD, Pres. Physicians Attending Assoc.
