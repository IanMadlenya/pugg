
        OCHITILL-Samuel J. Born on November 12, 1909 in NYC, passed away on September 21, 1998 after the final stages of an acute illness. He was the cherished husband of Gertrude Lerner and the dearly loved father of Karyn and Herbert. He is also survived by his three grandchildren, Jackie, Sarah and Emily. Services will be held in San Diego. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
