
        DAIGNAULT-JR., ALEXANDER T. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of the New York Presbyterian Hospital are saddened by the passing of Alexander T. Daignault, Jr., beloved husband of Elizabeth Daignault, our esteemed Life Trustee and former President of The Auxiliary of The Society of the New York Hospital. We join all those who knew him in mourning his loss, and we extend our heartfelt sympathies to Mrs. Daignault and the family. New York Presbyterian Hospital Daniel B. Burke Co-Chairman John F. McGillicuddy Co-Chairman David B. Skinner, M.D. Vice Chairman & CEO William T. Speck, M.D. President & COO
