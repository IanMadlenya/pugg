
        CASH--Jane M. Formerly of Bronxville and Scarsdale on May 25, 2006. Beloved wife of the late Pete Cash. Loving mother of Cami (Chuck) Hanson, Debby (Kevin) McIntosh, both of New Canaan, Jeff (Mary) Cash of Traverse City, MI. Devoted grandmother of Jennifer Spitz, Megan Bergen, Erin Gaynor, Brian and Chip McIntosh and Jeffrey and Elizabeth Cash. Great grandmother of Emily, Caroline, Kate, Katie, Mollie and Jack. Jane was very active as a volunteer in her community. She was a member of the Scarsdale Women's Club and the Junior League. She was a realtor with Cleveland Van Wert for 19 years, but most of all she will be remembered as a wonderful and loving mother and friend who had a limitless capacity to listen, console and support. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, between the hours of 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Tuesday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Bronxville Wednesday, May 31, 10 A.M.
