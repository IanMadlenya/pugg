
        KOENIG-Burton G. 73, of Coconut Creek, FL and Bayonne, NJ. Preceded in death by his beloved wife, Judge Gloria K. Koenig. Dearly loved son of the late Harry A. and Beatrice Koenig. Survived by brothers Marvin and Solomon, nephew George H., nieces Dr. Debra Cantor, Randi Koenig, and dear friend Mollie Starling. Marine Corps veteran of WW II. A courageous, charitable, loving family man. He will be sorely missed. Interment Sunday 1 PM, at Baron Hirsch Cemetery, Staten Island.
