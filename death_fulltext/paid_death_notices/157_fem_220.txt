
        KESSELER-Ina. The Officers and Directors of the German American Partnership Program (GAPP) note with sadness the passing of our Board member and Vice President, Mrs. Ina Kesseler. Since the very inception of our Program, she has given wholeheartedly and unhesitatingly of her time and contributed generously to our projects. Her dedication, support and experience will be missed. We extend our deepest condolences to her family. Werner Walbroel, President
        KESSELER-Ina. Members of the American Council on Germany join the many friends and admirers of Ina Kesseler in expressing sadness on her death. Ina worked tirelessly over many years to promote German-American understanding and dialogue, and her commitment to this cause will be missed.
