
        QUELLER -- Stephanie. Passed away peacefully on September 20, 2003. Adored by her husband Fred and by her daughters Jessica and Danielle. She will be remembered fondly by her many friends and by those who were customers of Tabletop Designs by Stephanie Queller in Southampton. She will be missed terribly by all who had the pleasure of knowing her and most especially by her family who loved her and in whose hearts she will always remain. A strong woman with a strong sense of family. Funeral services will be private. A celebration of her life will be held at the Regency Hotel, 540 Park Avenue, NYC, at 61 Street on Monday, between 4 p.m. and 9 p.m., and on Tuesday and Wednesday between 1 p.m. and 9 p.m. It will also be held at her home in Southampton on Thursday between 1 p.m. and 9 p.m. and on Friday between 1 p.m. and 4 p.m. Memorial donations may be made to the East End Hospice, P.O. Box 1048, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978.
