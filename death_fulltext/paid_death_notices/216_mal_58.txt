
        SCHILLER--Harry. On January 3, 2005 from multiple injuries from tragic car accident on December 22, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Margaret Schiller (December 30, 1992) and the late Santa Schiller (November 9, 2002). Loving father of Dr. Marilyn Schiller, Dorothy and Edgar Oberle. Grandfather of Kristin Oberle, Erica and Paul Stoving. Great grandfather of Margaret Stoving. Dear friend of Helen Cooke, and many, many in his community. Services Thursday, 9:30AM at Plaza Community Jewish Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave at West 91 St, N.Y., N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name may be made to Congregation Emanu-El, 1 E. 65 St, N.Y., N.Y. 10021 or Self Help, c/o Mrs. Roneet Wolf, 138-52 Elder Ave, Flushing, N.Y. 11355.
        SCHILLER--Harry. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Harry Schiller. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Rabbi Amy B. Ehrlich Robert A. Bernhard, Pres.
