
        FASTENBERG -- Hannah, died September 7, 2003 at the age of 82. She passed away peacefully at Roosevelt Hospital. Cherished wife of Dr. Milton Fastenberg, married for 54 years. Adored mother of Nathaniel and Francie Fastenberg of Phoenix. Beloved grandmother of Rachel, Bryan and Sara. Sister of Paul Werman and adored aunt. She will be missed by all. Services Tuesday Sept. 9, 1pm at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St & Amsterdam Ave.
