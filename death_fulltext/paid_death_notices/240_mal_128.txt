
        HERSHEY--Sylvan Joseph, 84, Paramus, NJ, on January 9, 2007. He was an OBGYN practicing in Paramus and at Valley Hospital of Ridgewood since 1955. Graduated NYU Medical School. Served as Director of OBGYN and as VP of medical staff at Valley. Predeceased by wife Lila. Survived by children Elizabeth, Barbara, Michael (Kelly), Steven (Gigi); grandchildren Kyla, Jenna, Elena, Olivia and Jacob; his sister Bernice Sokol's children, Jane and David. Memorial tributes to the Jewish Community Center of Paramus, Valley Hospital Foundation or Planned Parenthood. Services Friday, 11:30 AM at the Jewish Community Center of Paramus.
