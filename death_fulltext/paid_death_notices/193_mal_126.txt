
        TAUB-Herbert, 84. Of Fort Lee, NJ, on February 8, 2003. Pre-deceased by his first wife, Esther. Devoted husband of Laura. Beloved father of Jessica, Shelley and Lewis. Loving grandfather to seven grandchildren and one great-grandchild. He was a professor of engineering at CCNY for 43 years, and professor of physics at Yeshiva University for 17 years. He was past President of the Conservative Synagogue of Riverdale for seven years.
        TAUB-Herbert. The Conservative Synagogue Adath Israel of Riverdale mourns the loss of beloved former president Herbert Taub. May his memory be a blessing.
