
        BAKER--Charles E. Jr. On August 16, 2005, at his home, Sea Cliff, LI. A gradate of the University of Pennsylvania and Columbia Business School, he worked at Union Carbide and Marc Nichols Assocs. He loved architecture, gardening, Cole Porter, but most of all his friends, who will miss him greatly, as will the younger generation, many of whom looked upon him as a beloved uncle. Memorial services, Trinity Church, Broadway at Wall St, 1 PM, Saturday, July 8.
