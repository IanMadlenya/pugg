
        FRIEDLIEB-Morton J. Of Escondido, CA. and Great Barrington, MA., died suddenly on April 25, 1997. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his wife Isabelle, daughters Ellen Mayer and Jane Greenman, sister Doris Sabo and five grandchildren. A graduate engineer of New York University, he founded and was President of Precision Electronics of Amityville, N.Y. Our family patriach with extraordinary talents, skills and accomplishments was adored by friends and family. The void he leaves can never be filled. Contributions in his memory may be made to the United Jewish Fund of San Diego or Los Angeles.
