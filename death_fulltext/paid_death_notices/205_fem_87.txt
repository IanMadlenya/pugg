
        PAULIN - BATTERSBY -- Li sanne. Passed away unexpectedly Thursday, February 5, 2004. Lisanne was born to E. Max and Anne Paulin in Portland OR on June 30, 1962. After graduating from Brown University in 1985, she pursued a career in advertising. On May 15, 1999 she married Gregory Battersby. Her kind wisdom, reassuring smile and beautiful voice will be sadly missed. A memorial service honoring Lisanne will be held at St. Augustine's Episcopal Church in Croton-on-Hudson February 12 at 11AM.
