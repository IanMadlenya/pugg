
        HAMANN-Leonard C. Age 87, of Covenant Village, Cromwell, CT, formerly of Valley Stream, NY. Husband of Elsie S. (Hopfmuller) Hamann, died Saturday, July 5. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on December 3, 1909, he was the son of the late Charles W. and Sigrid (Frey) Hamann. Before retiring in 1972, he was a Field Engineer for Otis Elevator Company. Besides his wife he is survived by one son and daughter-in-law, Donald R. and Ruth Hamann of Summit, NJ; one granddaughter, Hillary Hamann of Boulder, CO; one niece and one nephew. Funeral services are private.
