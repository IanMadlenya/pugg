
        SCHULTE-Maurice, D.D.S. Peacefully on September 30, 2000, at the age of 94. He was a man of gentleness and humanity, a dedicated professional with a lifelong commitment to social justice. He is mourned by his daughter and sonin-law Marjorie and Michael Engber, his grandsons Benjamin and Daniel, and three generations of nieces and nephews. A memorial gathering will be announced at a later date.
