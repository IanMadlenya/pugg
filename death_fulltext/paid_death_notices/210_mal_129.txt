
        BAKER--Alan Griffin, of New Canaan, CT, died on July 12, 2004 at age 79. Mr. Baker was born in New York City and received his BA and MA from Columbia University. He is survived by his wife, Diana Harbage Baker, of 52 years, and sons Edward and Alfred, and daughters Jane Pasquini and Elizabeth Sapione, brother John Milnes Baker, and four grandchildren. Services are Saturday, July 17, at 1PM, St. Mark's Episcopal Church, New Canaan, CT. Contributions in Mr. Baker's memory may be made to the New Canaan Library, 151 Main St, New Canaan, CT 06840.
