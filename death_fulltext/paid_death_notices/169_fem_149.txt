
        WILSON-Alice L., a lifelong volunteer, activist, friend to the Arts, and former academic development officer, died on February 5, in Guilford, Conn. at the home of her daughter. Mrs. Wilson was 75. The cause of death was sudden heart failure. Mrs. Wilson's enthusiasm and warm personality attracted a great array of faithful friends in Nantucket, Ohio, and New York. She is survived by her husband, David; her brother, Charles F. Lehmann of Ann Arbor, Michigan; three daughters, Suzanne Stark of Maui, Hawaii, Karen Stark of New York City and Guilford, Conn., and Amy Tercek of Irvington, New York, and four grandchildren.
