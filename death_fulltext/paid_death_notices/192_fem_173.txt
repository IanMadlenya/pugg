
        STRASSER-Stella. Born September 9, 1910; died December 28, 2002. An electronics engineer, nationally recognized as an expert in the field of signal analysis, she became the top salaried woman scientist at US Army Engineering Laboratories at Ft. Monmouth and was one of the first women recipients of the Old Crows' certificate of merit. She was president of Ft. Monmouth's Toastmistress Club and among the first to be named to Hunter College's Hall of Fame. She was deeply devoted to family and friends; we mourn her passing and miss her enthusiasm for life, her humor, and her generous spirit.
