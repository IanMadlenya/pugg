
        MALAMUD--Hannah. The Actors' Temple Board and membership mourn the passing of Hannah Malamud, 94, the widow of our greatly beloved cantor of 37 years, Louis Malamud. We extend our sympathies to her daughter, Paula Malamud, her son, Sherwood Malamud, her daughter and lifelong Temple member, Phyllis Malamud, and the entire Malamud family. A graveside ceremony will be held at Montefiore Cemetery in St. Albans on Tuesday at 11:30 AM.
