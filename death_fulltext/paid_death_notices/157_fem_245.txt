
        WOOD-Cornelia Ely, died on February 12, 2000 at home at Tryon Estate, Columbus, NC. She was the daughter of the late Augustus and Grace Ely of Morristown, NJ. She was the wife of the late Arnold Wood. Surviving are three sons, William A. Wood, Steven H. Wood and Winston E. Wood. A memorial service will be held next summer in the Community Church in Elk Lake, PA. Memorials may be made to The SPCA of Monmouth County, 260 Wall St., Eatontown, New Jersey 07724.
