
        FLEISS--Dr. Joseph L. On Thursday, June 12, 2003 in Ridgewood, NJ. Formerly of Wayne, NJ. Beloved husband of the late Isabel Bogorad Fleiss. Cherished father of Arthur, Deb, Liz and Phyllis. Loving grandfather of Amir, Yamit, Eden, Shanna, Sarah and Jesse. Brother of Israel and devoted son-in-law of Lillian and the late Leo Izenberg. Dr. Fleiss was former professor and chair of the division of Biostatistics at the Columbia University School of Public Health. He has written two seminal works in the field of biostatistics ''Statistical Methods for Rates and Proportions'' and ''The Design and Analysis of Clinical Experiments''. He has authored over 150 papers on the application of statistics in fields ranging from psychiatry and cardiology to dentistry. He is a fellow of the American Statistical Association and a recipient of a gold medal award from the American Public Health Association. In 1997, he was awarded a lifetime achievement award from Harvard University for contributions to mental health statistics. Funeral services, Friday, June 13, 1PM, at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway, Fair Lawn NJ.
