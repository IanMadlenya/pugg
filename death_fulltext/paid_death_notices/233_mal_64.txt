
        SILVERMAN--Donald. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Donald Silverman, a cherished friend deeply devoted to humanitarian causes. Donald's dedication is shared by his sister, Mary Ann Siskind, and brother-inlaw, Arthur Siskind, whose profound commitment to the Jewish community enriches lives in New York and around the world. We extend our sympathies to Donald's beloved wife, Robin; to their children, Miranda and Douglas; to Mary Ann and Arthur; & to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
