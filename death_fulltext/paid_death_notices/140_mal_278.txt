
        FRIEDMAN-K. Bertram. The Board and staff of the Samuel Field YM-YWHA are profoundly saddened by the passing of K. Bertram Friedman, a valued past president and devoted board member. Mr. Friedman was a unique & multi-talented individual whose love for his family, zest for life and commitment to the wellbeing of others was extraordinary. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to his beloved wife Patty, his children Thomas and David, and to his entire family. Mr. Friedman was a very special person who will be deeply missed by all.
        FRIEDMAN-K. Bertram. Beloved husband for 60 years of Patience (Patty). Devoted father of Thomas and David. Cherished grandfather of Michele, Marc, Jeremy, Jason & Benny. Loving brother of Helen Levy and Edith Garfunkel. Services Wednesday 1:15PM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. Contributions may be made to the Samuel Field Y, 58-20 Little Neck Parkway, Little Neck, NY.
