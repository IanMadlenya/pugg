
        PERLBERG-Annette. In her 95th year. Brilliant teacher. Beloved wife of the late Elijah. Devoted mother of Dorothy and Saul Muchnick, Lynn and Gerald Sheer. Cherished grandmother of Laurie (David), Robert (Laurence), Meredith (Peter) and Adam. Adored great grandmother of Eva and Sofia. Dear sister of Morton Dodes. Her elegance, wit and charm will be remembered by all who knew her. Her unique talents in the classroom influenced generations of students. She was one of a kind, never to be forgotten.
