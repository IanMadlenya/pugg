
        HOGEN-Carolyn. Died peacefully on March 27 in Lenox, Massachusetts. A native of Cleveland, Carol graduated from Smith College in 1932. She married Charles ''Sonny'' Hogen, also of Cleveland, with whom she shared 66 years of marriage. She is survived by her adoring husband, their five children, Tim, Sherrill, Lyn, Robin and John, eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren. ''Nina'' was active in the Smith College Alumni Association for over 40 years and was a founder of the Mountain Valley Health Center, to which gifts can be made in lieu of flowers at Mountain Valley Health Council, Box 310, Londonderry, VT 05148. Her ashes will be buried in Peru, Vermont where Carol and Sonny spent their golden years.
