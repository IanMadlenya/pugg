
        ROTHCHILD-Seymour. The students, staff and community of Harvard Hillel mourn the loss of Seymour Rothchild, scientist, musician, philanthropist and student of Torah. We express our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Sylvia and their children, Alice, Judith and Joseph. May his memory be a Blessing. Bernard Steinberg Executive Director Roy Feldman Chairman of the Brd of Trustees Jerry Green President
