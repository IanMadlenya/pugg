
        De SCHILL--Stefan. Friends and colleagues mourn the passing of Stefan de Schill, director of research of the American Mental Health Foundation since 1948, and a tireless campaigner for improved methods of mental-health training and treatment. De Schill died in New York City on February 9, 2005. Born Stefan Sarkozi de Somogyi-Schill on April 4, 1917, in Vienna, de Schill was the author of numerous books, including Crucial Choices -- Crucial Changes: The Resurrection of Psychotherapy, The Challenge for Group Psychotherapy, and The Challenge for Psychoanalysis and Psychotherapy. De Schill is survived by his companion of many years, Denise LaHullier of New York City. A private memorial service is planned.
