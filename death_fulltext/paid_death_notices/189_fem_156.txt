
        TORO-Puli. 62, died of ovarian cancer on October 4. Daughter of the late Cesar and Munchita. Leaves a brother, Dr. Cesar Toro, a niece and two nephews, all of San Juan, Puerto Rico. Puli was a brilliant mezzo soprano, inspired teacher and perhaps the greatest interpreter of Puerto Rican music. Her career included performing at the New York City Opera and at opera houses worldwide. A memorial concert will be held on November 15, at St. Stephen's Church, 120 West 69th St, at 8 PM. We will never forget how you uplifted our lives. Your Loving Friends
