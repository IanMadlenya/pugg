
        KWASHA-Charles. Boys Town Jerusalem mourns with profound sorrow the passing of a cherished friend and a charter member, together with his wife Sylvia, of the Society of Chai Founders. Their support of the school spans three decades, and has given the gift of hope and opportunity to underprivileged Israeli youth. The individual and collective accomplishments of Boys Town's students shall remain as a lasting tribute to Mr. Kwasha's memory. We extend heartfelt sympathy to the entire family and pray they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Josh S. Weston, Chmn Alexander S. Linchner, Vice Chmn Rabbi Ronald L. Gray, Exec. V.P. Rabbi Simcha Freedman, Exec. Dir., Florida Friends of BTJ
        KWASHA - H. Charles. The Kwasha Lipton Group extends sincere condolences to the family of our Founder, H. Charles (Chick) Kwasha, on his passing. His vision and commitment will continue to serve as our standard of excellence. We will miss him greatly. Robert S. Byrne, Jr., President The Kwasha Lipton Group
