
        NICHOLS-Eleanor G., a lifelong resident of New Rochelle died on March 12, 2000 at her home. She was 82. Ms. Nichols was born on December 2, 1917 in New Rochelle to the late Thomas and Bridget Nichols. Ms. Nichols was a retired editor for McGraw Hill for over 30 years. She retired 20 years ago. She was a communicant of Blessed Sacrament Church, where she was active in the Ladies of Charity. She was also very active in, and a member of, the Board of Directors of The New Rochelle Womens Club. Ms. Nichols is survived by a sister Mary Morgan of Florida and several nieces and nephews. Visiting hours will be Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Lloyd Maxcy & Sons, 16 Shea Place, New Rochelle. A funeral mass is scheduled for Thursday 9:30 AM at Blessed Sacrament Church. Interment will follow at Holy Sepulchre Cemetery.
