
        KENNEY-Charles A. The National Realty Club mourns the passing of Charles A. Kenney, a founding member, governor and one of the guiding lights of the organization, founded in 1947. Even when the real estate industry went through difficult times, Mr. Kenney was instrumental in stregthening our membership and bringing unity to our Club. Our deepest sympathy to his family and friends. Thomas K. Graf, President Stephen D. Barkin, Membership Chair Jerrold D. Fuchs, Programming Chair
