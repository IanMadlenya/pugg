
        KESTENBAUM-Yocheved (Yetta). AMIT mourns the passing of our devoted supporter and beloved mother of our dedicated honorary National Vice-President, Shirley K. Schulder. A true Woman of Valor, Yetta Kestenbaum was the matriarch of her esteemed family and lived a life devoted to Torah values, loving kindness and religious Zionism. Heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Sondra Sokal, President Marvin Leff, Executive Director
