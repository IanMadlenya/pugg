
        HOOPER-Bayard. Age 72. Of Sag Harbor, N.Y. and Bonita Springs, FL., where he died February 9, 2001. A graduate of Groton School and Harvard College, his professional life was largely spent as writer and editor of ''Time'', ''Life'' and ''Discover'' Magazines. He leaves his widow Nancy and four children by previous marriages, David, Carolyn, Robert and Elizabeth, a brother Roger, a sister Justine Milliken, and four grandchildren. All his family mourn his loss and will miss him greatly. Funeral plans pending. Memorial donations may be made to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 710 West 168 St, N.Y., N.Y. 10032.
