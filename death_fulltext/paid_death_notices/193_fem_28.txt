
        FRIEDMAN-Daniel. On February 1, age 67, after a long courageous battle with lymphoma. Daniel resided in New York and Arizona. Retired Chairman and CEO of Kable News Company. Past Master of the Sea Gate Lodge. Honored by the Brandeis University and American Jewish Congress for work done in the publishing industry. Beloved husband of Lynn. Devoted father of Jamie Elizabeth and her husband Eric Lindenbaum. Grandfather of Ethan Spencer and Oliver James. Services to be announced at Frank E. Campbell.
