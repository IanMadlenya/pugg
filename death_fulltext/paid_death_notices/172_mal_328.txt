
        DORFMAN-Alex, died May 30. Husband of the late Helen Dorfman. He was 98 years old and lived independently until the end. Cherished father of Roslyn Levin and Jerry Dorfman and the late Marcia Dorfman. Beloved fatherin-law of Karen Dorfman. Cherished grandfather of Ava and Jeffrey Dorfman, Michael, Abby, Ruth and Jackie Levin, and greatgrandfather of Jacob, Noah and Andrew Levin. He was a member of the Traditional Synagogue and Brotherhood in Co-op City, the Bronx. Services will be held on Thursday, May 31, at the Larchmont Temple, 75 Larchmont Ave., 914-834-6120, at 12:30 PM.
