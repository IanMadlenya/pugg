
        KEESHAN--Robert J. The College of New Rochelle community records with sorrow the death of Robert J. Keeshan, friend of longstanding and Former Chair of the Board of Trustees. One of America's great educators of keen mind and gentle heart, his Captain Kangaroo was a master teacher for decades of students. He gifted the College with that educational genius and we are so much stronger because of his leadership and years of friendship. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D. President Jean Baptiste Nicholson, OSU Chair, Board of Trustees
