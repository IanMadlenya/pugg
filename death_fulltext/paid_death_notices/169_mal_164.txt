
        KUSHNER-Daniel B., M.D. Physician and former councilman of Alpine, NJ (1984-1990), on February 13, 2001. Beloved husband of 36 years of Zina, loving father of Dr. Adam, Jonathan and Lesley and Jennifer. Medical director of the Boulevard Health Center, Bronx, NY. Private practice of Internal Medicine and Cardiology East 60th St, NYC. Attending physician Mt. Sinai Hospital. Instructor Mt. Sinai School of Medicine. A graduate of Case Western Reserve University School of Medicine-1951. Internship George Washington University Hospital, 1951-52. Residency Kings County Hospital Center, 1952-53, and Bellevue Hospital Center, 1953-54. Fellow Cornell University Medical School Department of Pharmacology, 1954-55. Board certified in internal medicine. Services Friday, 11:45 AM, at ''The Riverside'' 76 St and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate contributions in his name to Myasthenia Gravis Foundation of America.
