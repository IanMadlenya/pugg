
        KARP-Nathan L., age 86, of South Palm Beach, FL., formerly of New York City, died Wed., April 2, 1997. Beloved husband of Barbara (Stern), loving father of Marion Benedek and Nancy Steiger. Devoted grandfather of Deborah Schnur, Elizabeth Newman, Sue Ellen Gerber, Michael Benedek, Elizabeth Kaye, Matthew, Merri and Meg Buchsbaum and greatgrandfather of six, brother of William and the late Rosalyn Rubin. At his request there will be no services. Those who wish may make contributions to the American Heart Association.
