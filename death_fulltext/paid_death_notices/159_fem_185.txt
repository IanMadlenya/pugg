
        McHUGH-Sr. Mary. The Marymount School community mourns the death of our beloved alumna and former colleague, Sr. Mary McHugh. As a dedicated teacher and Head of Upper School she is remembered with respect and affection by students and staff. She shared with all of us a keen sense of justice, a global perspective and a deep love for the poor. We are grateful for her life and her work. Sr. Kathleen Fagan, R.S.H.M. Headmistress
