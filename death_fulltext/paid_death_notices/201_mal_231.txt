
        FEDERMAN--Allan ''Freddie'', age 73, of Manhattan, NY, passed away peacefully on October 18, 2003. Mr. Federman attended NYU and worked as a copywriter and interpreter for the Daily News. He was co-proprietor of Forlini's Bar and Phase II in Manhattan and retired from NICO Construction. He is survived by his wife, Dr. Ann Gibson; two daughters, Elizabeth Federman and Michele Gaffney, and a stepdaughter, Jessica Simmons. Also surviving is his sister, Martha Guadagno, and her children, John and Lisa; five grandsons and a granddaughter. Funeral services will be private. www.mealeyfuneralhome.com
