
        GALLAGHER-Margaret T. Died in her sleep early Sunday morning, November 8. The cause of death was pneumonia. Margaret, known as Nancy throughout her life, was 82 years old. She is survived by her loving daughter, Nancy M. Gallagher, a financial consultant, and her son, Thomas A. Gallagher, a patent attorney. She was married to James T. Gallagher, General Counsel to the Long Island Railroad, who passed away in 1972. An active member of the Epiphany Roman Catholic Church in NYC, she devoted many hours to volunteer work and was beloved by parishoners for her kindness and her keen wit. She was the author of many short stories and poems. She will be remembered by all who knew her.
