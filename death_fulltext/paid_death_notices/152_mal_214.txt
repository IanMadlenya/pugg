
        WINTERS-Elwood J. Of Harrison, NY. Passed away on September 15, 1999. He was 89. Beloved husband of 59 years to Leona. Loving father of Jeffrey, Andrea and Patricia. Professor Emeritus of New York University (Chemistry). Lt. Commander WWII, in charge of U.S. Flight School. Originated video taping techniques for classroom teaching in 1959. Co-developed the first drug for pre-menstrual syndrome. Member of the American Association of Applied Sciences and Musician's Union 808. Interment was held on Friday in Valhalla, NY. For more information call Zion Memorial Chapel, (914) 381-1809.
