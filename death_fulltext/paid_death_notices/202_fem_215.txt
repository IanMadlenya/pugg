
        DePAOLA--Anna Virginia, 88, of Boca Raton passed away on Thursday, November 27, 2003 at her residence. Mrs. DePaola worked at Doubleday Publishing House prior to her marriage. She moved to Boca Raton 30 years ago from Valley Stream, NY. She is survived by her husband of 63 years, Joseph, president of Dic-Underhill Construction & Certified Industries, four children: Dennis, Donald, Joanne, & Kathy, 12 grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at Sinatra Memorial Home, 601 Yonkers Avenue, Yonkers, 10704 (914) 963-5263. The family requests expressions of sympathy be made to the Putnam-Northern Westchester Women's Resource Center, 935 South Lake Blvd, Mahopac, NY 10541. For information, contact the Babione Funeral Home, (East Chapel).
