
        BONAN-Seon Pierre. Real estate developer and philanthropist, best known for the ground-breaking urban renewal project, Charles River Park in Boston, died Saturday in Palm Beach, FL, of complications from a brain injury. He was 82 and maintained residences in Palm Beach, Wilton, CT, and New York City, and was a former longtime resident of Greenwich, CT. Mr. Bonan is survived by his wife of 52 years, Janet Ross Bonan, a sister, Ethel Hoeffler, a brother, Ferdinand, three children, Elizabeth Bonan, Charles Bonan and Virginia Adelson, and six grandchildren. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, NYC, on Saturday, January 29th, at 11:30 AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Mt. Sinai Hospital Juvenile Diabetes Center.
        BONAN-Seon Pierre. We mourn the loss of our friend and colleague who was our urban renewal expert and an exemplary business partner for close to 30 years. We share the pain of his loss with his wife Janet, his children Elizabeth, Charles and Virginia and his entire family. Eugene M. Grant & Co. Eugene M. Grant Richard E. Kobrin
