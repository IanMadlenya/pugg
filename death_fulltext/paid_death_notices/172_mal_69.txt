
        WEST-Andrew D. Passed away suddenly of complications from diabetes on April 20, 2001 in Philadelphia, PA, while on tour as a keyboard musician with the Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey Circus. Beloved son of Ormand and Patricia. Loving brother of Kristin. Andrew touched so many people with his music, his magic, his wit and his wisdom. He will be deeply missed by all who knew him. There will be a celebration of Andrew's life (on what would have been his 21st birthday) on Wednesday, May 16, 2001 at 2:00 PM at Saint Peter's Church, 619 Lexington Ave., entrance on 54th St. Contributions for The Mount Sinai Hospital Pediatric Diabetic Unit may be made in Andrew's memory and should be sent to: Mount Sinai Development Office, Box 1049, One Gustave L. Levy Place New York, New York 10029-6574, Attn: C. Garfield.
