
        TURELL-Annette. Age 86. Passed away January 11 in Scottsdale, Arizona. She was a native and lived in Hewlett, Long Island for over 60 years. She was a member of the Woodmere Country Club. She was the wife of the late Norman Turell. She is survived by her son Robert L. (wife, Marjorie) of Scottsdale, Arizona; daughter Bonnie L. Turell of Chicago, Illinois; sister Beatrice Korn (husband, Murray) of West Palm Beach, Florida; grandchildren Laurence, Jeffrey and Laura; and greatgrandchildren Nathaniel, Jeremy and Austin. Graveside service 1:15PM Wednesday, January 13 at Old Mount Carmel Cemetery, Ridgewood, New York.
