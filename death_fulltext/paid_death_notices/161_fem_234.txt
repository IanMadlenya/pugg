
        SPELLETICH-Margery Fowler, 80, of Corona del Mar died June 26, 2000. She was preceded in death by her husband, Kalman Spelletich, Jr. in 1996. Born in Glen Ridge, New Jersey in 1919, she graduated from Sarah Lawrence College in New York and then joined BBDO in New York City as an account executive from 1943-1958. More recently, she was a homemaker and secretary to Mr. Spelletich's business. An avid volunteer, she participated in the Garden Club, the Orange County Philharmonic, an investment group, children's theatre and was a fifty year member of the Junior League. She is survived by her daughter, Joan (Mark Lowenstern); grandchildren, Seth, Aimee; nieces, Patricia Parsons, Georgia Hazard, Betsy Borden; nephews, Peter Fowler, James Parsons, Robert Borden and Stephen Borden. Memorial Services will be 11:00 a.m., Thursday, June 29, 2000 at St. James Episcopal Church, 3209 Via Lido, Newport Beach, CA 92663. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations be made to St. James Epicopal Church or to the charity of your choice.
