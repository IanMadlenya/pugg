
        DAVIS-James N., MD. The Faculty, Physicians & Staff at Stony Brook University, the School of Medicine in Stony Brook University Hospital are saddened by the passing of James N. Davis, MD, his wife Frances and daughter Amanda. We mourn the untimely death of an inspired teacher, researcher, clinician, and mentor. Funeral Services on Wednesday, March 19, 2003 at Judea Reform Temple in Durham, N.C. Interment will follow at the Judea Reform Cemetery.
