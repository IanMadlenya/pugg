
        CABELL-William D., 92. On Nov. 14 at home, Rock Castle, VA. He is survived by his wife Ellen Rolston Cabell, children Kathleen Cabell Hildreth and William D. Cabell Jr. and their families. He graduated from the University of Virginia College and Law School and practiced law in NYC from 1932 to 1991. After serving as a Lieutenant in the Navy in W.W. II, he resided in Chappaqua, NY. A member of Delta Psi Fraternity, Squadron A and the Knickerbocker Club in NYC. In the 1950's he published ''Voters Voice'', an innovative Congressional watchdog newsletter. A great gentleman.
