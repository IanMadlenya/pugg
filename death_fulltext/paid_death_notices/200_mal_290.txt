
        PLIMPTON--George Ames. On September 26, 2003. The family of George Plimpton announces with sadness his passing. Memorial service to be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Paris Review Foundation, 541 East 72nd St, NY, NY 10021.
        PLIMPTON -- George. New School University mourns the loss of Actors Studio Drama School Board of Governors member George Plimpton. The University has lost a great friend and the world of American Letters has lost a great man. The University extends its deepest sympathies to George Plimpton's family, and to all those who have been enriched by his matchless gift. Philip Scaturro, Chairman, Board of Trustees Bob Kerrey, President James Lipton, Dean, Actors Studio Drama School.
        PLIMPTON -- George. The Board of Directors of the Academy of American Poets records with sorrow the loss of a great and valued friend. Through George Plimpton's generous support of poets and poetry and his inspired editorship of the Paris Review, a distinguished contribution to letters will continue to influence American literature for years to come.
        PLIMPTON--George Ames. On September 26, 2003. The staff of The Paris Review mourns the loss of their longtime editor, mentor and friend, who presided over the magazine for 50 years with spirit, grace and wisdom. As George would have wished, the 50th anniversary gala will go on as planned on October 14. For information call 212861-0016.
        PLIMPTON -- George. The members of the American Academy of Arts and Letters note with sorrow the death of their esteemed colleague whose passing will be mourned by the world of literature.
