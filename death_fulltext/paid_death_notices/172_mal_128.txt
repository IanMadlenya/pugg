
        BURKOFF-Stanley Thomas. Age 80. A resident of Highland Beach, Florida and retired President of W.B. Doner & Company, an international advertising agency with its principal office in Detroit, MI., died at home on May 10, 2001. Prior to joining W.B. Doner & Company as Creative Director in 1963, Mr. Burkoff held the position of Creative Director at Young & Rubicam Advertising in Toronto, Ontario, and Copy Chief at Doyle Dane Bernbach Advertising in New York City. Stanley Burkoff is survived by his wife Joyce Switow Burkoff, his three children, John (Nancy) Burkoff of Pittsburgh, PA., Barbara Burkoff Dale of Baltimore, MD., Sara (David) Burkoff Owens of Saint Louis, MO., and seven grandchildren.
