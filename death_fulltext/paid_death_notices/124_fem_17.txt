
        MALE-Leonore Edel, artist, photographer, interior/graphic designer and mother died April 27, 1997. Graduate of Hood College in 1938 and tech draftsperson during W.W. II. Lee designed the original RCA showroom in 1948. She was an award winning painter & photographer while raising her family. At 64 she opened Ledel Gallery, Soho's first photographic gallery, in 1981. Known for enthusiasm and love of life, we will miss her. Donate in Lee's memory to ICP, International Center of Photography inner city schools program of NYC The Males
