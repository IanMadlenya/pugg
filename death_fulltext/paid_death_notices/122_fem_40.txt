
        SPEIER-Mickey Gilbert, 83, of Palm Beach, formerly of New York, passed away March 2, 1997. She is survived by her husband, Ferdinand, her daughter, Jane Melrose, and son, Ross Gilbert (deceased), her nieces, Joan and Harriet. She had 13 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. Service will be on Wednesday, at 3 P.M., at Riverside Gordon, West Palm Beach, 561-683-8676. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
