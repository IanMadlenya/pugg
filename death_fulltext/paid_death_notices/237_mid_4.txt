
        AMES--Sue H., 70, October 18th, in Boston, MA, formerly of Riverdale, NY. Born Sue Holtz in Buffalo, NY, attended Vassar College and Harvard Graduate School of Education. Resided in Riverdale until 1994, second grade teacher in the Scarsdale School for 25 years. Sue loved to travel, ski and play piano. Survived by her sons, Zachary of Nelson, BC, and Benjamin of Arlington, MA, his wife Shannon, and children Teddy and Isabel. Former wife of Edward A. Ames. Memorials may be made to Alzheimer's Association, 311 Arsenal St, Watertown, MA 02472.
