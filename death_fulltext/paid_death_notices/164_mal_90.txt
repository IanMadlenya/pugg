
        WYETH-Leonard Jarvis. Of Lakeville, CT, died August 29th in Sharon (CT) Hospital. He was the son of the late Leonard J. and the late Priscilla Mullins Bull Wyeth of New York City. He leaves his cherished wife of 59 years, Pamela Evans Wyeth, two daughters, Pamela I. Wyeth of S. Westerlo, NY, and Christina L. Wyeth of New York City, and a son, Leonard J. Wyeth VI of Essex, CT, and two grandchildren, Leonard J. Wyeth VII and Joan L. Wyeth of Essex, CT. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that contributions be made to the MPD Research Center, 115 East 72nd Street, New York, New York 10021.
