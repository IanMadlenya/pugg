
        MILNE--Bessie Rilla Bacon, 84, of Darien, CT, passed away March 17, 2007. Daughter of Frederick S. and Lenice I. Bacon of Newton, MA. She attended House in the Pines and Vassar College, Class of 1944. She married the late Douglas D. Milne, Jr. in 1949. She is survived by sons, Douglas D. III and Gordon S. Milne and daughter, Leslie W. Milne and grandchildren, Jason C. and Ashley W. Milne. Memorial Service, April 28, 2007, at 11:30am, the First Congregational Church of Darien, CT.
