
        TONKIN-Joseph. On October 15, at age 94. Beloved husband of the late Celina Tonkin. Loving son of the late Nathaniel Tonkin and Lily Freedman Tonkin and brother of the late A. Ted Tonkin. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81 St, Tuesday, 12 noon. Interment Mt. Neboh Cemetery.
        TONKIN-Joseph. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Joseph Tonkin. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
