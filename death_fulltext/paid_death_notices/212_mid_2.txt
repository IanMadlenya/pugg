
        HUBER--Mary Elizabeth Sedman. Died August 18, at 95. Born in Missoula, MT, she was married to Henry Silver Huber, MD, of New York City until his death in 1972. She is survived by Mary Tambiah of Cambridge, MA, Harriet Huber of Seattle, WA, Janet Schiff of Wayside, NJ, six grandchildren and two great grandchildren.
