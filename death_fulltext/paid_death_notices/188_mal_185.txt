
        DALTON-George E. On September 17, 2002 of The Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, Inc. Born in Floral Park, NY on July 13, 1923. Graduate of St. Francis Xavier High School Class of 1941 and Syracuse University Class of 1948. Three-and-a-half years in the U.S. Army Air Force during World War II. Former president of the Metropolitan Funeral Directors Association and of the NY State Funeral Directors Association, Member of Catholic Cemetery Guild of Brooklyn, Phi Kappa Psi Fraternity, Floral Park Lions Club, Floral Park Knights of Columbus, Floral Park American Legion, New Hyde Park Elks and Blinded Veterans of America. Beloved son of the late Thomas F. and Irene (Harper) Dalton. Devoted husband of Dell (nee Serviss). Loving father of Matthew, Timothy, Christopher and Martha Cerasano. Fond father-in-law of Alison and Susan Dalton and Mark Cerasano. Cherished grandfather of Robin, Lane, Emily, Alysa and Timothy J. Dalton, Holly Cerasano and Jason Power. Reposing Thursday and Friday 2-5 and 7-9:30 PM at the Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave (at R.R. Plaza) Floral Park. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday 9:30 AM at St. Anne's R.C. Church. Interment Our Lady of the Isle Cemetery, Shelter Island Heights, NY. In his memory donations to St. Gabriel's Retreat House, P.O. Box 3015, Shelter Island Heights, NY 11965, or Foundation Fighting Blindness, P.O. Box 17279, Baltimore, MD. 21297-0495, will be appreciated.
