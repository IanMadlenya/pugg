
        WESSEL-Harry N. Died November 22, 2002, age 81. Beloved grandfather of Max, Lauren, Dan, Elizabeth, and Henry Wessel, father of Peter and Tom Wessel, father-inlaw to Vicki and Marcy. Tomb School valedictorian. University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School. WWII veteran (5th Army). President Income Development Corp. Pilot. Mariner. Pugilist. Etymologist. Club Golf Champ of Sunningdale and Quaker Ridge. Did it his way. He'll be missed. Private services. Donations in lieu of flowers to Hospice Foundation of Cape Cod, 270 Communication Way, Hyannis, MA 02601.
