
        WALD--Edith Weiler. Age 93. Died February 1, 2004 in Chicago, IL. Predeceased by her husband, Maurice Wald, M.D. and her brother, George A. Weiler. Survived by her children Nancy and Albert and grandchildren Laura and Michael. Educated at the Dalton School, University of Wisconsin and Columbia University School of Social Work. Memorial contributions to University of Wisconsin Foundation, POB 8860, Madison, WI. 53791-9944.
