
        TANSMAN-Ruth. On July 12, 1997, in Miami Beach, FL. A resident of Teaneck, NJ, and Miami Beach, she graduated from Hunter College. Earlier this year, South Shore Hospital honored her with the Volunteer of the Year Award. Widow of Martin Tansman, she is survived by her daughters Roberta Tansman and Susan Elin (Joel) and grandchildren Deborah, Alison and Daniel Jacobs, and David and Rebecca Elin.
