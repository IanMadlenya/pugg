
        ESTRIN--Joseph, M.D., 93. Dr. Joseph N. Cunningham and the Department of Surgery at Maimonides Medical Center mourn the passing of our respected and admired educator and colleague, Dr. Joseph Estrin. He mentored generations of surgeons and was a source of wisdom and inspiration to us all. Avid historian, acclaimed writer and artist, Dr. Estrin will most notably be remembered for his passionate ''Uncommon Commitment'' to the entire Brooklyn and Maimonides Medical Center community.
