
        TANSER-Harry. Died August 15, 1997. Beloved husband of Corinne. Devoted father of Julie Keye and Howard, father-in-law of Chip Keye, and grandfather of Elizabeth Keye. Loving stepfather of Wendy Willinger Jones and Jonathan Willinger, grandfather of Emily Jones and Alexandra and Julia Willinger. Rehabilitation counselor for 27 years. A lifelong socialist, his dedication and his humanitarianism will be remembered. Services Tuesday, at 1:15 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
