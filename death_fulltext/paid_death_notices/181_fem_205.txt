
        STORKE-Georgette M. The wife of the late William F.J. Storke passed away on February 16, 2002, after a courageous struggle with cancer. She is survived and will be greatly missed by a son, William M. Storke, two step-children, Adam and Victoria and three stepgrandchildren, Giacomo, Isabella and Sebastiano. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Saturday, February 23, at 11 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering, Cancer Research, 1275 York Avenue, Box E, New York, New York 10021.
