
        DUNNING-James Dorr. On November 7, 2000, at his home in New York City, after a long illness. He was 89. Mr. Dunning was an executive of New York Life Insurance Company for 45 years. For 25 years he served as a consultant to the Episcopal Church Pension Fund. His extensive lay leadership in the Episcopal Church included service as a senior warden of three parishes, St. Andrew's Church, Wellesley, MA, All Saints' Church and St. Bartholomew's Church, Manhattan. He served as a member of the national standing liturgical commission during the revision of the Book of Common Prayer. In 1996, he was awarded an honorary Doctor of Humane Letters by the Berkeley Divinity School at Yale. He is survived by Mary, his wife of 66 years, and his son James D. Dunning, Jr. of New York, and his grandsons James D. Dunning III and David F. Dunning. Vigil and visitation Sunday, November 12, 4-6:30PM, St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue at 51st Street. Funeral service at St. Bartholomew's, Monday, November 13, 5 PM. Memorial donations to St. Bartholomew's Church, New York, or Berkeley Divinity School at Yale, New Haven.
