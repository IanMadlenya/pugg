
        SMITH--Roslyn B. Roslyn was the most loving and devoted wife to the late Robert Smith for 59 years. Adored by her children Penny Novick, Howard and Amy Smith, devoted to her grandchildren Brett and Blake Novick, and Jeffrey and Stephanie Smith. Her passing will be a great void in their lives. A woman of style and grace, Roslyn was also the ultimate wife, mother and grandmother. Her charitable efforts were many including being the cofounder of the Parker Jewish Institute Gift Shop. She will always be remembered for her compassion, sweet smile and kindness. She will forever be loved and missed. Donations in her memory can be made to Temple Sinai, Roslyn, New York and St. Francis Hospital, New York.
