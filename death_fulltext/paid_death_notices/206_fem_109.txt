
        RYAN--Gertrude Carey (Trudy), 72, of Hallandale, Fla., formerly of Manhasset, L.I., on March 4, 2004. Beloved mother of Elaine Ryan Fallon of Baltimore, Md and Dr. Jay Ryan of San Mateo, Ca. Loving grandmother of Matthew, Anne and Peter Fallon and Jenna, Christopher and Nicholas Ryan. Dear aunt of Peter, Ellen, Christopher, Jeanne, Michael, Mark and Kevin Carey and Susan Carey Linaugh. Devoted ''Aunt Garber'' to 12 great-nieces and nephews. Memorial Service will be held on Thursday, March 25 at St. Matthew's Catholic Church, Hallandale, Fla., at 10AM. Donations in her memory may be made to the Art and Culture Center of Hollywood, 1650 Harrison St., Hollywood, Fla. 33020.
