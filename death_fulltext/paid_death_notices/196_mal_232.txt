
        GREENWALD--Herbert, on May 27, in his 98th year. Beloved father of Rose (Morton) Landowne, husband of the late Helen N. Greenwald, grandfather of Dena (Jay), Lea, Shifra and Aryeh, greatgrandfather of Micha, Keren and Gilad. Herb was an adored uncle and great-uncle and was a founding partner of the CPA firm of Biederman, Greenwald, Kresch and Gerbasi in 1946. Services Wednesday, May 28th at noon, Plaza Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Ave. >>AD#
