
        GERNGROSS-Hans Oskar. Formerly of Manhattan, died Wednesday, April 11, 2001, at his home in Fearrington Village, NC at the age of 90. Beloved husband of Frances (nee Banzhaf). Devoted father of Margaret J. Pfeiffer of Little Rock, AR, Thomas James of Chapel Hill, NC, Andrew Bello of Manhattan and Clermont, NY and Julie Ann Baker of Nevada City, CA. Cherished grandfather of Miles Jordan and Theodore Wylie Baker. A celebration of his life, followed by brunch, will be held Sunday, April 22, 2001, 12:30 PM at his home in Fearrington Village. Memorial contributions may be made to WCPE, P.O. Box 828, Wake Forest, NC, 27588 or the Woodstock School of Art, P.O. 335, Woodstock, NY 12498.
