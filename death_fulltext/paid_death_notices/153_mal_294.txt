
        SCHMIDT-Benno C. The City University of New York mourns the passing of Benno C. Schmidt, father of Vice Chairman of our Board of Trustees, Benno C. Schmidt Jr., financier, pioneering venture capitalist who backed biotechnology industries, health policy advisor to several Presidents, chairman of President Richard M. Nixon's Cancer Panel, member of the President's Biomedical Research Panel, and distinguished New York civic leader. His many contributions to the city and the nation will long be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. Herman Badillo, Chairman Board of Trustees Matthew Goldstein, Chancellor
