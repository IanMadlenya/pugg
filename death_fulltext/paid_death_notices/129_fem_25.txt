
        CLARK-Sylvia. Passed away in the home she loved on Oct. 1, 1997. A brilliant and vibrant woman of the highest moral integrity whose grace, beauty, dignity and dedication to her family shall always be with us. Beloved wife of Ronald H. Clark. Devoted mother and best friend of Alice M. Clark. Cherished mother-in-law of Dr. Peter B. Schiff. Adored grandmother of Sarah Clark Schiff and Jenny Clark Schiff. Dear youngest child of the late Sarah and Marcus Buller. Loving sister of Joseph Buller and the late Frances Weiner. Fond sisterin-law of Ann Buller. May the memory be green.
