
        HENNESSY-Raymond J. of Franklin Lakes on March 13, 2001. Beloved husband of Rosemarie Hennessy (nee Malara). Devoted father of Donna Marie Latronica of Sparta, Debrah Anne Messina of Mount Laurel and Raymond Thomas Hennessy of Wayne. Adored son of Suzanne Hennessy of Queens, New York. Loving grandfather of Christina and Gina Latronica, Michael and Nicole Messina and Nicholas Hennessy. Dear brother of Thomas Hennessy of Freehold, William Hennessy of East Setauket, NY and Susan McNulty of Queens, NY. In his position as Vice-President of the NY Stock Exchange, Ray represented the Exchange regarding financial rules with the brokerage firm community, the Securities and Exchange Commission, the Federal Reserve Board, the SACPA and the Securities Industry Association. He dealt extensively with the Financial Management, Internal Auditors and Legal Compliance Divisions of the SIA, as well as the Capital Committee. Funeral services will be held on Saturday at 9:30 AM at Vander Plaat-Vermeulen Memorial Home followed by a Mass of Christian Burial is at 10:00 AM at The Roman Catholic Church of the Most Blessed Sacrament, 787 Franklin Lakes Road, Franklin Lakes, NJ. Family will receive friends Thursday and Friday 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Vander Plaat - Vermeulen Memorial Home, 530 High Mountain Road, Franklin Lakes. The family requests donations be made to The American Heart Association or The Leukemia Society.
