
        ABBERLEY--John J. Of New Canaan, born December 16, 1916, died March 8, 2006. Survived by his wife, Joni; children, Lester, Georgine and Frederick. Also survived by three stepchildren, Walter, Donald and Alexandra. A graduate of Williams College and University of Virginia Law School. A partner of Abberley, Kooiman, Marcellino & Clay, served in World War II as Lt. Commander U.S. Navy, a Special Assistant to Director of the Marshall Plan Paris 1955-56, an avid sailor, member of Cruising Club of America, New York Yacht Club and Noroton Yacht Club. Interment private.
