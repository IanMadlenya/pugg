
        WINDELS--Dorothy A., 86. Of Kennett Square, PA, March 13, 2006. Born December 11, 1919 in Brooklyn, NY of Elmer and Edythe Otterstrom Windels. Survived by sister, Edith W. Illick of Kennett Square. Graduate of Syracuse Univ. ('42), she worked for Exxon Corp. in NYC for 43 years. Longtime member of St. Mark's Methodist Church in Brooklyn. Memorial service Crosslands Retirement Community, Kennett Square, 10:30AM, April 15. Donations to Syracuse Univ.
