
        FLYNN-Joseph B. On February 26, 2001. Of Somers, NY formerly of Pleasantville, NY. Mr. Flynn was born in the Bronx on July 29, 1932, the son of Patrick and Mary (nee Lavin) Flynn. He was a graduate of Fordham University and held a B.A. in Sociology. Mr. Flynn was a Sargeant serving in the U.S. Marine Corps from 1951-54. He married Kathleen Galligan in the Bronx on April 24, 1955. Mr. Flynn was the retired Chief of Police of the Long Island Railroad, where he served for 15 years. He also was a retired Deputy Inspector for the New York City Police Department. He is survived by his wife, Kathleen, and a brother, Gerald Flynn. His brother John Flynn died earlier. Resting on Thursday and Friday, 2-4 and 7-9PM at Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday, 10:30AM, Holy Innocents Church, Pleasantville, NY. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital appreciated.
