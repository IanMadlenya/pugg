
        EDWARDS-Alice Howland. Age 84 on July 8, 1998. Singer of opera & art song & vocal coach. Beloved wife of the late Sidney Edwards, cellist. Loving mother of Kate and Harriet Edwards. Grandmother of Victoria Kirk. Devoted sister of Harriet Flynn and Thielo Howland. Interment private. Memorial service at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Avenue at 84 Street, Sunday, August 23, 2 PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Melvin D. Yahr Parkinsons Research Fund, 5 East 98 Street, New York, New York 10029.
