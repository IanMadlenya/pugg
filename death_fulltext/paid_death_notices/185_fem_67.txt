
        BODIN-Sylvia. Passed away on June 9, 2002. She was born on November 22, 1908, to Anshel and Hinda Cohen. She married Paul D. Bodin in 1930 and they lived in Yonkers, NY. Predeceased by her husband, they were the proprietors of Life Cleaners in Yonkers, now being run by the fourth generation of the Bodin family. Sylvia worked at Life until the age of 90, joining the computer age in her 80's. The family was a longtime member of the Congregation Agudis Achim in Yonkers, and Sylvia was a member of many other organizations also. Her son, Murray Bodin, her daughter, Joyce Sanger, and her niece, Bernice Strauss, 10 grandchildren and 17 great-grandchildren survive her. Two sisters, Marian Jenkins and Helen Goldfield, many nieces and nephews, and her friend, Ocie Henderson, also survive her. There will be a graveside service at Mt. Hope Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson, on Monday, June 10, at 2 PM. Donations can be made to St. Cabrini Nursing Home, or Woodlands Community Temple.
