
        RICHARDSON -- Ralph E. Died July 20, 2005 after a brief illness. Born November 1, 1927 Bristol, CT, son of the late Dr. Ralph and Estelle Richardson. Brother of Lynn and the late Martha Smith. U.S. Army Veteran Korea, graduate of Wesleyan University. Ralph worked at Wood Struthers before a long and distinguished career in publishing. Retail rep at Time, Inc., and later served as president of Mactier Publishing. Founder of Electronic Product News, Brussels and Managing Automation, Thomas Publishing, NY and member of the Council for Competitiveness, Washington, DC. His greatest pleasure came at his elevation to Commander of the Confrerie des Chevaliers du Tastevin in Burgundy. Ralph is survived by his wife Collette Laffey Richardson, his four children, Martha, Sarah, Amy and Sam and two grandchildren. Memorial service Monday July 25 at 4PM, Frank E Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Av, NYC. Donations in his honor to Disabled American Veterans Mem'l Program, Box 14301, Cincinnati, OH 45250
