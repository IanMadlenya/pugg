
        FESSLER-Frederic. Died at 79, Dec. 31, 2002. A veteran of 40 years with TWA, he'll be missed by the loving family that was the center of his life and included his wife Susan, his daughters Emma and Laurie, their husbands Dr. Stuart Bernstein and Danny Friedmann, and his grandchildren Jonathan, Anna Claire, Timmy and Jillian.
