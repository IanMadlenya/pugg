
        CHU-Caryn, PhD. The Faculty and Staff of Mt. Sinai School of Medicine deeply mourn the sudden passing of Karen Chu. Ms. Chu joined Mt. She will be deeply missed by her colleagues and friends. Arthur H. Rubenstein, MBBCh, Dean Mt. Sinai School of Medicine Terry A. Krulwich, PhD, Dean Graduate School of Biological Sciences
