
        HILL-Suzanne. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins the Department of Social Work and Home Care Services in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Suzanne Hill, who served as a dedicated senior social worker in substance abuse and AIDS services since 1971. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the Hill family and to all who loved her. Matthew E.
