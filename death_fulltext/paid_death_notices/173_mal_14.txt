        McMURRAY-Dr. Joseph P. The College of New Rochelle community records with sadness the death of Dr. Joseph P. McMurray, 10th President of The College of New Rochelle and former member of the Board of Trustees. Dr. McMurray's enthusiastic and final visit to campus was as participant in the Presidential inauguration of October 1997. Despite the limitations of age and illness, he revelled in the occasion, conveying an interest, concern and love for the College which characterized his years of service to the College in a time of transition and change. We mourn his loss and celebrate his life. Stephen J. Sweeny, Ph.D. President Jean Baptiste Nicholson, O.S.U. Chair, Board of Trustees
