
        RATH-John Stearns. Of Litchfield, CT, died Friday, April 7, 2000, in Litchfield at the age of 82. He is the widower of Joan (Smith) Rath & Toddy (Cook) Rath and is survived by three daughters, Susan Latos, Nancy Rath & Jean Kopp; five grandchildren; one sister, Elizabeth Reagh and former wife, Patricia (Ruhl) (Graffin) Rath. Funeral services are at The First Congregational Church, Litchfield at 2 PM, Monday, April 10. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Litchfield Volunteer Ambulance Association or Litchfield Historical Society.
