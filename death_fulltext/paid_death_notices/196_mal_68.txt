
        MELTZER-Marvin, of Alexandria, VA, on May 5, 2003 in Manhattan. Unionist, Democrat, health care reformer, friend and heart of his family. Believed that a good cause need not be a lost cause and that loud was no substitute for right. We will surely miss you, Mickey. Elizabeth, loving wife of 51 years, daughters Lynn and Carol, sons-inlaw Marc and Darrell, grandchildren Michael, Julia and Katherine. Also survived by brother Jack, sisters-in-law Rae, Margaret and Joan and many nieces and nephews.
