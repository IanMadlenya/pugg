
        STANLEY-Jennifer C. 35 years old, on June 25, 2000. Art Director and graphic designer at Berkshire Hosiery, Mayer/Berkshire Corp. The staff and management mourn the loss of our friend and co-worker, Jennifer. She will be greatly missed. Her memory will be cherished and her devotion to the company will not be forgotten. We offer our heartfelt condolences to her parents, William and Christa E. Stanley and her sister, Christa M. Stanley. The immediate family attended a private service.
