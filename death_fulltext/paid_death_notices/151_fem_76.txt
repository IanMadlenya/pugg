
        STARR-Kathryn S. 88. Died on August 8 in her home in Greenwich, CT. Born in Greenwich on December 22, 1910, the daughter of Paula and Reinhard Siedenburg. The wife of the late H. Danforth Starr, Mrs. Starr is survived by three daughters, Eleanor Darcy of Westminster, MD., Natalie Starr of Washington, D.C., and Deborah Shannon of Dallas, TX., and a son, Danforth W. Starr of Greenwich as well as 12 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the Round Hill Community Church at 11 A.M., on Thursday, August 12. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made to the Greenwich Land Trust, P.O. Box 1152, Greenwich, CT. 06836.
