
        MARCUS-Ina R. On October 19, 2001 at age 93. Wife of the late Albert C. Marcus. Survived by her children, Lorelle Phillips, Sydna Kelligrew and Bonnie Marcus. Funeral service will be held Monday, 10 AM at Larchmont Temple, Larchmont, New York. Interment will follow at Mount Hope Cemetery, Hastingson-Hudson, New York.
