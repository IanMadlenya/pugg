
        VAN BERGEN--Elizabeth ''Betty''--a remarkable woman and treasured aunt, cousin, friend and sister-died in Manhattan on Monday, October 31, at the age of 87. She was married to the late Charles Van Bergen and was the sister of the late Sally (King) Shull Fritsch. For many years Betty was active in the National Council of Jewish Women. She is survived by nieces Sally (Shull) Miltenberger, Diane (MacDonald) Katsikas, Katie (MacDonald) Ferguson and Meg (MacDonald) Brager; nephews Michael Shull and Peter Shull; and cousins Meta Querns, Paul Starke, Nick Suhr and Paul Suhr. She was beloved by her many great nieces and great nephews and their families. A memorial service will be held Friday, November 4 at 11 AM at All Saints Episcopal Church, 230 East 60th Street, New York. Interment will be at Cedar Lawn Cemetery, East Hampton, Saturday, November 5 at noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the East Hampton Library, 159 Main Street, East Hampton, NY 11937 or the NCJWNY Section Children's Literacy Program, 820 Second Avenue, New York, NY 10017-4504.
