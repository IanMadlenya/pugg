
        STEVENSON-Anne H., 81, died of cardiac arrest in Norwalk Hospital, on January 15, 2000. Mrs. Stevenson, a long term resident of Manhattan, was born in Boston and spent her early life in Cincinnati. She moved to Manhattan in 1965. Always active in artistic and charitable affairs, Mrs. Stevenson was a past President of the Widows and Old Mens home in Cincinnati and the Womens Committee of the New York Zoological Society. She was at various times the coproprieter of a contemprary art gallery in Manhattan and on staff of the Hirschl & Adler Galleries. Mrs. Stevenson also maintained a residence in Watch Hill, Rhode Island. She is survived by her sons John of New Canaan, CT and Jay of Rye, NY. Six grandchildren also survive. A memorial service will be held at the Congregational Church of New Canaan at 11:00 AM on January 21, 2000. The family requests donations in lieu of flowers be made to the Watch Hill Light House Keepers Association.
