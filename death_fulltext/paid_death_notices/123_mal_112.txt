
        LANDA-William R., 77, of Culpeper, died on Tuesday, April 8, 1997 at Culpeper Memorial Hospital. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM on Monday, April 14, at Found & Sons Chapel, Culpeper, VA, with Dr. R. Wayne Bernardo officiating. Interment will follow in Fairview Cemtery. Memorial contributions may be made in his name to the Culpeper Rescue Squad, P.O. Box 731, Culpeper, VA. 22701.
