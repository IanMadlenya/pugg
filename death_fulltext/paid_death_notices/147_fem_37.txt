
        BERK-Francine B. On April 3, 1999. Beloved wife of Alan. Mother of Michael. Daughter of David Brecher, sister of Janet and Susan. Service Monday, 12:30 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
        BERK-Francine. The Board, administration, faculty, staff and parents of the Ethical Culture Fieldston School mourn the loss of Francine Berk, our Director of Admissions and friend. She was deepy dedicated to the humanitarian mission of the school, where she made a personal connection with the hundreds of families with whom she worked. As a leader in the community, her integrity and gentle nature blended to create her legacy. Our deepest sympathies go to her husband Allan, her son Michael, and her extended family.
        BERK-Francine. Park East Synagogue mourns the untimely passing of our devoted member Francine Berk. Beloved wife of Alan Berk, and mother of Michael. We shall miss Fran, a woman of faith and courage and gifted educator. May the family find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
