
        SOBIN-Morris. Passed away on Friday, October 27, 2000. Of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Roslyn Heights, NY. Loving husband of Doris and the late Ruth. Devoted & cherished father of Louise Sobin Hersh (Bob). Adored grandfather of Philip (Lara) and Joshua Sivin. Beloved brother of Rose Simon and Edward Sobin. Past president of Fresh Meadows Country Club. Former president of Olympia Radio and TV and Pilot Radio-TV. Former COO of Regent Sheffield Inc. Former director of Lear Siegler Inc. and Suburbia Federal Bank. He set an example of hard work, responsibility, sense of humor, zest for life, and fierce love of his family. His legacy will continue for generations to come. Services Florida, Sunday October 29, 11 AM, ''Gutterman Warheit'' 7240 N. Federal Highway, Boca Raton. Interment Tuesday, October 31, at 3 P.M., at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI. Memorial contributions, Sass Foundation for Medical Research, 1025 Northern Blvd. Suite 302, Roslyn, N.Y. 11576.
        SOBIN-Morris. It is with profound sorrow that the Officers, Board of Governors, members and staff of the Fresh Meadow Country Club record the passing of Morris Sobin, former member and past President of our club. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Martin Rosenman, President Mark Claster, Secretary
