
        COUGHLIN-Catherine, 86. Died Nov. 2, 1997. She is survived by many loving family members and friends who will miss her counsel, company and sense of adventure. Her generous spirit remains with us. She was administrative assistant to the Presidents of Sarah Lawrence College and Josiah Macy, Jr. Foundation. Memorial donations to the New York League for the Hard of Hearing, where she was working as a consultant. Calling hours Tuesday from 5-7:00 PM at the Horne-Dannecker Funeral Home, 336 W. 23rd Street, NY, with funeral service at 7:00 PM. The funeral procession leaves the funeral home at 10:00 AM, Wednesday with burial taking place at Holy Cross Cemetery on Tilden Avenue, Brooklyn.
