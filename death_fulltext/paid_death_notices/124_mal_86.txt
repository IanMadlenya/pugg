
        SHAPIRO-Sanford N. Age 48. On May 4, 1997. Beloved son of Rita and George M. Shapiro. Dear brother of Karen Spector. He was a graduate of Williams College, the Woodrow Wilson School of Public and International Affairs at Princeton University and Boalt Hall School of Law at the University of California, Berkeley. Services Thursday, 12 Noon at Plaza Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 91 Street. Shiva period will be observed at the home of his parents. May he at last know peace.
