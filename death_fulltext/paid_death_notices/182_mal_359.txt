
        MILLER-Rabbi Israel. The members of the Board of Directors of Camp Morasha deeply mourn the passing of a founding father and visionary who helped establish our mission of educational camping in the Modern-Orthodox community. Rabbi Miller served as Educational Chairman for over three decades. He loved to be a part of the Torah learning experience with campers and staff at Camp Morasha. Martin Schwarzschild, President and Chairman of the Board Abraham Wahrhaftig, Director
