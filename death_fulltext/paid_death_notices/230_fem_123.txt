
        STREGER--Jeanette. A longtime resident of New Rochelle, passed away at home in White Plains, on Friday, March 10, 2006, at the age of 87. She was the devoted wife of the late David, loving mother of Ann Price (Fredric) and Ellen Steger, adoring grandmother of Joshua (Kimberly), Jeremy and Matthew, and the proud greatgrandmother of Isaiah. An 11 am funeral home chapel service is scheduled for Tuesday, with the interment to follow in the Mt. Eden Cemetery. For information, George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle, NY; 914-632-0324.
