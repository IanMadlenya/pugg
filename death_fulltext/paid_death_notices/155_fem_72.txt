
        FARLEY-Harriet Severance McPherson, died December 4, 1999 of cancer at age 84 at the Edgehill Retirement Community in Stamford, after living many years in Greenwich. Daughter of the late Marjorie and Walter McPherson of New York City. She attended the Ethel Walker School and graduated from Nightingale-Bamford in 1934. She is survived by her husband Adrian Morgan Farley, Jr., her sister, Mrs. Marjorie Adams of Greenwich, and her children Scott Farley, and Marjorie Butler and five grandchildren. Memorial Service on Friday, December 10 at 2:00 P.M. at Christ Church Chapel of Greenwich.
