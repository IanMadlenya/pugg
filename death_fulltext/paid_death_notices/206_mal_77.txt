
        SMITH -- Dr. William E. 1914-2004. Princeton '34, MD Johns Hopkins. Worked at Rockefeller Institute, SloanKettering, NYU. Fairleigh Dickinson Health Research Institute Director. Using PCNU with an analog of Vitamin A (Ro10 - 9359) he achieved cures of lung cancers induced in mice by asbestos. Married Elizabeth Hamilton. Divorced and married Marye Collins. Survived by daughter, five grandchildren.
