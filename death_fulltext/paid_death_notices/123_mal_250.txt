
        MESHBERG-Jules N. of Newport Cove, Delray Beach, Fl. died on April 20, 1997. He was co-founder and President of Majestic Factors Corporation (1936-1964). He is survived by his beloved wife Jeanette, daughter Elinor Mero, brother Irving Meshberg, sister Marietta Hass, grandson Michael Levin and great grandson Jensen.
