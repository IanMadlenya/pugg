
        OPPENHEIMER - Beryl. The members of The New York Psychoanalytic Society and Institute, and the professional staff and directors of the Treatment Center, past and present, express profound sorrow at the loss of our colleague and co-worker, Beryl Oppenheimer, ACSW, who served the Treatment Center as a social worker from 1968-1994 in which capacity she was devoted to patients, students and members alike. Lester Schwartz, MD President/Society Eslee Samberg, MD President/Institute
