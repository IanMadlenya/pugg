
        HOFFMAN-Leon. On September 30, 2000, at 85 years old. Beloved husbnd of the late Rita. Loving father of Roy and his wife Sandra, Jane and her husband Dr. David Zelman. Cherished grandfather of Lisa, Mark, Lori, Jeremy and Allison. Dear brother of Rose Hateau. Unselfishly placing his family's needs above his own, he was an exemplary role model for his adoring grandchildren. Former New York Daily Mirror photographer, former President of the New York Press Photographers Association and member of the Board of Directors for over 40 years. Services Tuesday, October 3, 10 AM, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike in Woodbury, LI. Contribuitons in his name to Alzheimer's Association.
