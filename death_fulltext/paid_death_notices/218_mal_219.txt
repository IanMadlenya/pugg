
        COHEN--Abraham L. Of Woodmere, NY and Los Angeles, CA. Died March 16 in Fort Worth, TX. Survived by his wife of over 50 years, Patricia; sons Lewis Cohen of Los Angeles, David Cohen of Fort Worth and daughter Rachel Cohen of NYC. Graduated from Townsend Harris High School, class of 1941; graduated CCNY; received Masters degree in Electrical Engineering from NYU.
