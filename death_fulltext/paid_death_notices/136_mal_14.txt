
        STROH-Edward Jr., DMD. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Edward Stroh, Jr., DMD. A distinguished Director of the Department of Dentistry and Oral Surgery from 1966 to 1974, he upheld our finest traditions of clinical excellence and will be sorely missed. To his family and friends, we extend our profoundest condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Robert G. Newman, MD Acting President Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
        STROH-Edward Jr., DMD. April 29, 1998. Age 89. Graduate of University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine. Formerly Chief of Dentistry and Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at Metropolitan Hospital Center and St. Luke'sRoosevelt Hospital Center.
