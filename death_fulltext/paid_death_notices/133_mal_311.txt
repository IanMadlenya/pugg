
        CAMP-Paul T., 78. Of San Marcos, CA., formerly of Somers, NY, passed away Feb. 16, 1998. Born in Springfield, MA on May 2, 1921. Retired as International Quality Control Director for Pfizer Inc. Survived by his wife of fifty-three years, Phyllis Reynolds Camp of San Marcos, CA., one daughter Jacqueline Eldridge of Santa Barbara, CA, two sisters, Jane Camp Krause of Concord, NC and Joy Camp of Concord, N.C., one grandson Mark Eldridge of Santa Barbara, CA. Services private.
