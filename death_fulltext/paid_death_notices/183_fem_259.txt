
        LUBIN-Florence (nee Paley). Passed away at the age of 89 in Miami, Fl. following a brief illness. Beloved wife of the late Hyman, she is survived by her children Laura & Mark (Ellen) and adored grandchildren Samuel Ari and Rebecca Joy. A lover of art and music, travel and family, she will be missed by those who knew her. Private services at Arlington National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to a cultural institution of your choice.
