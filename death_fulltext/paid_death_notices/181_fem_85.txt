
        FREEDMAN-Mara Leslie. Born December 18, 1955, in New York City, died February 2, 2002, of cancer. She is the daughter of the late Esther Katz Freedman and Albert Freedman. Surived by father and step-mother Albert and Nancy of California, siblings Lisa Harris and Derek Freedman of London and Tani Ruiz of Geneva. Her family and friends mourn the passing of a passionate and loving soul. She was a healer and selfless giver.
