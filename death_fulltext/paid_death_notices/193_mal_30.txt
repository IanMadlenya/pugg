
        LEAHEY-Michael I. The Irving Center for Clinical Research mourns the untimely loss of Michael I. Leahey, founder and Director of the Office of Clinical Trials at the Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center. Michael's innovative and bold contributions facilitated the work of clinical researchers throughout the institution. His accomplishments, despite his brief tenure among us, were extraordinary. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. He will be deeply missed. Henry N. Ginsberg, M.D. Director, Irving Center for Clinical Research
