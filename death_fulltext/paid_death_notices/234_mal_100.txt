
        PAPIER--Dr. David, 89 years old, died on July 7, 2006. Loving husband to Sarah Nevins Papier for 56 years. A great father to Lewis, Edward and Arthur Papier; devoted brother to Harold; father-in-law to Susan Voci and grandfather to Ariana and Chloe Papier. He was a gentle, kind-hearted man who was a source of inspiration to all his family and friends. A graduate of New York University and NYU Medical School, he served in World War II as a Captain in the U.S. Army Medical Corps. A physician who practiced in Forest Hills for over 40 years, he was beloved by all his patients. Funeral services Monday July 10, 10 A.M. Schwartz Brothers Memorial Chapel, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
