
        ROCCHIO--Joseph P. Jr., M.D. Of New York City, January 2, 2004. Beloved son of Mildred and the late Joseph P. Rocchio Sr.; dear brother of Lemoyne (P. Richard Jr.) Rhoades; devoted uncle of P. Richard (Deborah) Rhoades III and Kristen E. (Matthew) Webb; survived by countless friends and patients in his 23 year Pediatric Practice who fondly referred to him as Dr. Joe or Doc Roc. He will be remembered for his dedication to children and medicine and for his unselfish generous spirit. Funeral services were private.
