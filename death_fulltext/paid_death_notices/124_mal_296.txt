
        MEREN-Jan. On Sunday, May 25. Of Irvington, NY and Becket, MA. Age 60. Cherished husband of 25 years to Roslyn Yasser. Devoted father to Peter and Andrew. Dear son-in-law of Esther and Mac Yasser. Loving brother-in-law to Sandra and Alan Friedman, Nan and Alan Yasser. Uncle to David, Jonathan, Lisa and Jennifer. He was the father that anyone could want-and the father that anyone could aspire to be. Services on Wednesday, May 28, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in Washington, D.C.
