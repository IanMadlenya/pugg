
        RIZO-Marco. Passed away on September 8, 1998. Cuban composer, concert pianist and Musical Director of the ''I Love Lucy'' show. He is survived by his sisters, Maria Rizo Burton and Vilma Rizo, his brother, Evelio, his son, Mark, nephews, Herbert Hernandez, Anthony Hernandez, & niece, Diana Hernandez. Friends may call Thursday 7-9PM and Friday 2-5PM and 7-9PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:30AM at St. Paul the Apostle Church, West 60 Street and 9th Ave. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery.
