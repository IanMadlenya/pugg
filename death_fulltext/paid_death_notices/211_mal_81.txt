
        CAMPOSA--John A. Died on August 4th. He was employed by the New York Times for 32 years. He resided in Syosset for 52 years. He is survived by his wife Helen, three children, Christine Whiteside, Robert Camposa and Diane Lipnickey, son-in-law Kenneth Lipnickey, daughter-in-law Dorothy Camposa, nine grandchildren and sister Josephine DeBenedictis. He was buried on August 7th. Donations may be made to The Heart Fund or The Alzheimers Association.
        CAMPOSA--John A. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of John A. Camposa, associated with The Times from April 30, 1956 until retiring in June 1, 1988.
