
        FRIEDMAN-Harriet Knecht. Beloved wife for 53 years of Raymond; treasured mother of Claire, Terry, Eliot and Sari; adoring grandmother of Morgan, Gareth, Benjamin, Leo, Efrem, Giselle and Sophia. A cum laude graduate of Brooklyn College, she later earned her master's degree and taught at the Chatterton School in Merrick, NY for 24 years. In the words of her devoted mother, Giselle Knecht, ''she is our shining star.'' Services Friday, May 19, 2000, 1 PM, Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Blvd., Fresh Meadows NY
