        KAUFMAN--Berdie L. BarIlan University and its American Friends mourn the passing of Berdie L. Kaufman, beloved mother of our devoted friend and benefactor Myron L. Kaufman. A close friend of our esteemed Chancellor Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, she served as president of the Sisterhood of Congregation Shaaray Tefila, Far Rockaway where she was a member for 75 years and was a life member of Hadassah. She imparted a great love of Judaism, G-d and man to her children and her entire family. She is survived by her son Myron and his wife Joan; cherished daughters Florence Jaffa and Sharon Ness and husband Gerald; eight grandchildren and fourteen great - grandchildren. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman Chancellor, Bar-Ilan Univ. Prof. Moshe Kaveh President, Bar-Ilan Univ. Brig. Gen. (ret) Yehuda Halevy Exec Vice Pres Bar-Ilan Univ
