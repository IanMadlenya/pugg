
        SILVER--Melba. The National Basketball Association is deeply saddened by the loss of Melba Silver, beloved mother of our colleague Adam Silver. Devoted mother of Ann, Erik, Owen and Adam, Melba will be remembered for her numerous accomplishments and benevolent involvement in the Cities of Rye, New York and Boca Raton, FL. As a resident of Rye for more than 50 years, she co-authored the Rye City Charter, served as Chair of the City of Rye Planning Commission and President of the Rye Library. In Boca Raton, she was an active Board Member of Planned Parenthood of South Palm Beach and Broward Counties, President of the Boca Raton Symphony and Chair of the Florida Philharmonic's Boca Raton Board of Directors. Her passing is a sad loss to the NBA Family and we send our heartfelt condolences to Adam and his family. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in memory of Melba Silver to Planned Parenthood of South Palm Beach and Broward Counties, 455 N.W. 35 St., Boca Raton, FL. 33431.
