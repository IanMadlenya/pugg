
        BOURQUIN - Pierre. Forty four year resident of Bronxville, NY, died of Multiple Myeloma at home in Marlboro, MA on July 4. Husband of Shirley, father of Colette Nygren and Pierson, grandfather of Annika and Alexandra. A graduate of Middlesex School and Yale Class of 1957, he was an Account Executive with Smith Barney Shearson/ Hutton for 33 years. Past President of White Plains Rotary, Bronxville Field Club, Yale - Westchester Alumni and The Colony Foundation. Memorial Service on Saturday, August 3 at 11AM, Reformed Church of Bronxville. In lieu of flowers, donations to Yale Hockey Association, PO Box 208216, New Haven, CT 06520 or Reformed Church, POB 397, Bronxville, NY 10708 would be be appreciated.
