
        SCHINDELHEIM-Samuel, 89. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of New York City, passed away July 9, 2002. Beloved husband of Rose, to whom he was married 62 years. Loving father of Joanne (Jay) Moskowitz and Jonathan P. Schindelheim. Cherished grandfather of Michael and Andrew and great-grandfather of Mikayla. Mr. Schindelheim was a former educator and basketball coach at Stuyvesant High School in N.Y.C. Services today July 12 at Gutterman - Warheit Memorial Chapel in Boca Raton, FL.
