
        BUNKER--Charles Sheffield. Age 63, of Austerlitz, New York died February 7, 2005 at Albany Medical Center. A graduate of East Woods and Portsmouth Abbey Schools, Yale and Columbia, he taught at Saint David's School and the Greenvale School. Son of the late Charles Cantine Bunker and Mavis McGuire Bunker, he is survived by two sisters, Sheila Nickerson and Mavis Taintor, six nieces and nephews, one great niece and one great nephew. Memorial contributions to Animalkind, Inc., POB 902, 721 Warren St., Hudson, N.Y. 12534.
