
        BLACK-Selma. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society express profound sorrow at the loss of Selma Black of Portland, Maine. We extend heartfelt condolences to her son, Scott Black, a visionary supporter who spearheaded the innovative Scott Black Executive Management Program at the Technion Institute of Management.
