
        FINDLAY-Harriet Lindsay. Died unexpectedly in her Manhattan apartment on July 5th, 2001. Known as Lindsay, she was born on August 16, 1935, in Kansas City, MO, and grew up in New Canaan, CT. A graduate of Dana Hall School, Goucher College and the Harvard-Radcliffe Business Program, she initially worked for The Putnam Fund in Boston, MA. In 1958 she joined her father, David B. Findlay Sr., in his Manhattan art business, David Findlay Galleries. The original Findlay Galleries was founded in Kansas City, MO, 1870. Lindsay was a member of the fourth generation of the family to be involved with the gallery. In 1989 her father died, and Lindsay assumed control of the gallery. She is survived by her mother, Harriet Jones Findlay, two brothers, David B. Findlay Jr. and H. Peter Findlay, and numerous nieces & nephews. Each of the brothers has his own eponymous art gallery in Manhattan. A memorial service will be held at the Congregational Church, 23 Park St, New Canaan, CT, on Tuesday, July 17th at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Humane Society of Manhattan and New Canaan Nature Center in memory of the deceased.
