
        RUGGIERO-Joseph Thomas (Rogers). Passed away Sunday, October 25, at his home in Manhattan surrounded by his family. He was 87 years old. Joe was born on February 8, 1911, in New York City. Joe was a mill worker and a proud member of the United Brotherhood of Carpenters, Union Local 246, for 70 years. Joe lived nearly his whole life in Astoria and was a parishioner at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Roman Catholic Church. Joe's family and friends will miss him and will never forget how he touched their lives. Joe is survived by his wife, Mildred, and two daughters, Gloria Ruggiero and Carol Ruggiero Shabe. Also survived by 17 grandchildren and 13 great - grandchildren. Visitation Monday and Tuesday 2-5 and 79pm at Joseph Farenga & Sons (Queens Chapels), 38-08 Ditmars Blvd, Astoria. Funeral Mass Wednesday 10:30am Immaculate Conception R.C. Church. Interment Calvary Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations are to be made to Cabrini Hospice Center, 227 E. 19 St., NYC, 10003, in his memory.
