
        KADISON-Philip, of White Plains, NY died unexpectedly on September 22 in his sleep. A retired stockbroker of Adbest, Inc., he was a graduate of Harvard University, a member of The Hasty Pudding Club and a long standing member of The N.M.C. He was an accomplished pianist and composer, a loving husband and father, brother and uncle. He will be sorely missed. Memorial contributions may be made to The National Wildlife Federation.
