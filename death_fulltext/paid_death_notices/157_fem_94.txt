
        SCHLOSS-Louise Filer. In her 78th year. Died peacefully on February 4, after a long illness. Loving wife of Walter, beloved mother of Stephanie Scott, Edwin and sister Elizabeth (Betty) Filer. Nana to Katie, Emily and Jamie. Laid to rest February 9. Contributions in her memory would be appreciated to the Children's Book Committee of Bank Street College, 610 W. 112 St, NYC 10025, or the Mental Health Association of NYC, 666 Bway, NYC 10012.
