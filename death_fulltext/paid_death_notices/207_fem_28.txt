
        RABBINO--Irma L. Of New York City on April 1, 2004. Devoted daughter of the late Rose and Murray. Cherished sister of Bernard and Tina. Loving aunt and grand-aunt. Services 10 AM, Sunday, at the Bernheim-Apter-Kreitzman Suburban Funeral Chapel, 68 Old Short Hills Rd, Livingston, NJ. 973-422-0600
        RABBINO--Irma. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of a dear friend and supporter. A respected member of our Society of Founders, she also was a long-time member of our National Women's Division and an editor of its newsletter. To her brother and sister-in-law, Bernard and Tina Rabbino, who are loyal supporters of our institution, and to the entire family, we offer our sincere condolences. Yeshiva University Richard M.
