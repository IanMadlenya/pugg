
        BODKIN--Robert A. Age 86, of Oak Bluffs, MA, died Tuesday, December 30, 2003. Beloved husband of Cornelia E. (Watson) Bodkin. He was the former Headmaster of the Maplewood School in Cornwall, CT. Memorial service Saturday, January 10, 11 AM, St. Andrew's Church, Edgartown, MA. Memorial donations may be made to Maplewood School, PO Box 3001, Kent, CT 06757-3001.
