
        JAQUITH--Carolyn Zeichner, died on January 14, in Westchester, of lung cancer. She was 58. Ms. Jaquith is survived by her husband, David Jaquith and two daughters, Julianne and Alyssandra, of Briarcliff, NY; her sister, Barbara Zeichner of Buffalo, NY; and her father Oscar Zeichner of NYC.
