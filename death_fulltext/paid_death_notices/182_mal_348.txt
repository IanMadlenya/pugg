
        WAGGONER-Leland Tate. The American Bible Society mourns the passing of Leland Tate Waggoner, a member of the American Bible Society Board of Trustees, who died March 14. He was a member of the Board since 1974. Mr. Waggoner was retired and formerly worked as president of Life of Virginia of New York and senior vice president for Phoenix Home Life. The Society extends its condolences to Mr. Waggoner's surviving family: his son, Frederick, of Carrollton, Mississippi; daughter Leonora B. Lynch of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and 3 grandchildren. Mr. Waggoner was preceded in death by his wife, Florence. He will be missed.
