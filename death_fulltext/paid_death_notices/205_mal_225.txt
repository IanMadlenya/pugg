
        LIBMAN--H. Hillard. On February 15, 2004 in Sarasota, FL at age 73. Owner/Director of Camp Hillard, he is survived by his wife of 43 years, Elaine, children Jim, Jon and Kathleen, granddaughters Dylan and Taylor and sisters Jill Libman and Kit Howard. Service Wednesday, February 18, 10 AM at Congregation Kol Ami, 252 Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY. In lieu of flowers the family requests donations be made to the Parkinson Alliance, PO Box 308, Kingston, NJ 08528.
