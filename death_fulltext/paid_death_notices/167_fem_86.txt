
        MOORE-Mable Godwin. Beloved mother of Margaret Hubbard, died on November 28, 2000 at St. Luke's Hospital in New York after a brief illness. Mable was born in Suffolk, VA on November 23, 1913. As a jazz singer/pianist Mable worked in Harlem clubs in the '30's and performed with the USO during WWII. She played and sang at the jazz club, Arthur's Tavern in Greenwich Village, NY from 1960 until her retirement in 1993. Mable will be greatly missed by her family and friends. Services were held Saturday, December 2, in NYC.
