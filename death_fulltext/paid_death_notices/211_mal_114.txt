
        LEHMANN--Alfred H.B. Died peacefully on August 10, 2004 surrounded by his loving family. Mr. Lehmann was President/Chairman of Pan American Trade Development Corp. Survived by beloved wife of 57 years, Olga. Devoted father of Andre and Sandra. Father-in-law of Kathleen and Anthony. Loving grandfather of Paul, Cristina, Nicholas, George and Mark. Great grandfather of Patrick. Step-sister Aya. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Alzheimer's Support Network, Naples, FL. Private service at Frank E. Campbell on Friday 4-6PM.
