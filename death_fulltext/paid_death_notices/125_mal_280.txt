
        GERSON-John. Of New York City and Guilford Ct. Cherished husband of Edith Gerson. Died Friday, June 20, 1997. Sweetie, my love for you will never end.
        GERSON-John. The Sheffield School mourns the loss of John Gerson, Dean of Advanced Studies. A special teacher and talented interior designer, his wisdom, integrity and wit will be missed by his colleagues, students and his wife, Edith. He shared his knowledge and a passion for good design with students from around the world and will be greatly missed by them and the school. Don Sheff, Director
