
        DONOVAN-Jane, M.D., F.A.P. (Hatheway). 83, of Woodcliff Lake, NJ on Thursday, February 7, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Daniel B., devoted mother of Daniel of Cookeville, TN, John of Mason, NH, and Andrew of NYC, dear sister of Ann Shafnacker of Truro, MA and Elizabeth Rich of Sterling, VA, loving grandmother of seven grandchildren. Funeral service 10 AM and visiting 9 AM to 12 noon on Saturday at the Robert Spearing Funeral Home, Inc. 155 Kinderkamack Rd, Park Ridge, NJ. Interment to follow at Saddle River Reformed Church Cemetery, Upper Saddle River, NJ. In lieu of flowers kindly make contributions to the Convenant House, 460 W. 41 St, NYC 10036. Before retiring Mrs. Donovan was a practicing psychiatrist for over 50 years. She was President of the North Jersey Women's Psychiatric Association, a Life Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association, and was a clinical professor for 30 years at Hackensack Medical Center, Hackensack, NJ.
