
        COWLEY - Alexander Stewart. Died April 21, 1999, in New York. Devoted father of Steph and adoring grandfather of Sven. Known to all as Stewart, as the founder and President of Stewart Models, Stewart Men, Stewart Artists, and Model of the Year Pageant, he was an innovator and respected leader of the modeling industry for many years. A private burial will take place at the Cowley family site at Homewood, Pittsburgh, PA, on April 26. A celebration of his life will be held shortly in New York City for his many friends at a time and place to be announced. He will be missed.
