
        KEMPNER-Barbara. On March 11, in her 95th year. Violinist. Wife of the late Fredrick C. Kempner. Aunt of a multitude of devoted nieces and nephews for whom she served by her example as affectionate guide and musical mentor. Date of memorial celebration to be announced. Memorial contributions may be made to Marlboro School of Music, 135 South 18th Street, Philadelphia, PA 19103.
