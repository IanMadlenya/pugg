
        LEYLAND - TURKEN - Ceri Ann. Fifty-two. June 19, 2002. Beloved wife of her late husband, Stanley Turken. Devoted stepmother of her loving children, Greta and Jason. Cherished sister of her brothers, John and Rex. Adoring aunt to her nieces, Tamsin and Chelsea. Dedicated and loving relative to all in the Black, Levy, Leyland and Turken families around the world. Her gentle nature, wonderful humor & immense strength will be missed by her many friends and loved ones, and will remain an example to all of us. A service will be held on Friday, June 21, 2002, at 11 AM, at Redden's Funeral Home, 325 West 14th Street, New York, New York.
