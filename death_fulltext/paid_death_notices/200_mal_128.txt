
        RIFKIN--Bernard M. The Zionist Organization of America mourns the passing of an esteemed leader and colleague Bernard M. Rifkin. He was an ardent Zionist and served the cause of Israel and ZOA for six decades. We extend heartfelt condolences to his bereaved family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Morton A. Klein, Nat'l Pres. Alan A. Mazurek, Chairman of the Board
