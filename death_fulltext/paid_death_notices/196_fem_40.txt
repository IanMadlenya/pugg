
        DIAMOND-Hana Mackler, ''The Boot Lady.'' On May 4, 2003. Born to Albert and Lee Mackler, October 5, 1916. Wife of the late Charles. Mother of Susan Nathanson, mother - in - law of Barry, grandmother of Richard, Laurie and Greg, greatgrandmother of Julia, sister of Flore and the late Estelle. She did it her way.
