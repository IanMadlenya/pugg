
        COREYS-Samuel A. Formerly of Westbury, NY. Died June 14, 2000, in West Palm Beach, FL. Those who knew Sam will always remember his great love of music and the arts and his endless study of their interrelationship with scientific thought. Sam is survived by his devoted wife, Esther, two loving daughters, two beloved sons-in-law and cherished grandchild. He was deeply loved by his family and will be sadly missed by all who have had the privilege of his friendship.
