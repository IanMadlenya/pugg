
        RAPPAPORT--S. Robert. Age 67 of Eastchester died on April 3, 2005, following a long illness. He was a well respected 2nd generation auctioneer. His sense of humor was enjoyed by all who knew him. He is survived by his wife Bunny, his daughter Susan, his son David, daughter-inlaw Gina and his beloved grandchild Julia. He was past president of the Lion's Club and member of many community boards. Service will be held at Westchester Funeral Home, Inc., Eastchester on Tuesday, April 5 at 12 noon. Interment to follow at Sharon Gardens. The family will receive visitors on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at the home address. Friends who wish may contribute to the Eastchester Youth Council, P.O. Box 185, Eastchester, New York 10709. Memorial tributes and condolences may be expressed to the family on our website, www.westchesterfuneral home.com
