
        WARREN-Dean William C. The Partners of Roberts and Holland are deeply saddened by the death of our Founder and esteemed colleage Dean William C. Warren. For decades our firm was blessed by Dean Warrens' brilliant legal mind and astute business sense. Dean Warren shared his deep knowledge of the tax law with generations of law students through The Surrey and Warren Texts, but we were priveleged to share his knowledge and insights for our entire careers. We celebrate his unique style, his prolific career, and his unforgettable personality. He will be profoundly missed.
