
        EISENBERG-Miriam. Age 81, a long-term resident of Delray Beach, FL. Passed away on March 6, 2001 after a mercifully short but unsuccessful battle with cancer. She was originally from Newark, NJ and subsequently lived in Maplewood, NJ for many years. Miriam is survived by her husband and lifelong companion Saul. She is also survived by her sons, Doctors Marc and Scott as well as her grandchildren, Janis, Laney, Lisa and David.
