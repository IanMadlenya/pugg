
        GURNEY-Jane Peterkin. Of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, formerly of Garden City, NY, died November 30, 2001. Widow of John Kirkman Gurney, she graduated from the Cathedral School of St. Marys and is survived by Susan G. Buckey, Thomas P. Gurney, five grandchildren, three great-grandchildren, also a brother John G. Peterkin.
