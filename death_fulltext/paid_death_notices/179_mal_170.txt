
        McSHERRY-Charles K., MD, F.A.C.S. Emeritus clinical professor of surgery, Cornell University Medical College. Director of surgery, Beth Israel Medical Center 1976-1990. Attending surgeon at the New York Presbyterian Hospital. Past-President of the New York Surgical Society, NY, Gastroenterological Association, Knight of Malta. Beloved husband of the late Nancy Ryan. Loving father of Maureen and cherished grandfather of Casey. Admired uncle of several nieces and nephews. Devoted friend of Miriam K. Moran. Predeceased by his parents and siblings. Mass of Christian Burial on Monday 10:00 AM at St. Joseph's Church, 404 E. 87th Street.
