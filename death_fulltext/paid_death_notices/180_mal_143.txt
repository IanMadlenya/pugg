
        LEVINE-Arthur. The United Synagogue of Conservative Judaism mourns the passing of Arthur Levine, International President of the United Synagogue from 1973-77. A respected leader of the Conservative Movement, he worked tirelessly to build solid relationships and guide the organization through a period of growth and transition. An ardent Zionist, he devoted many years to the well being of the State of Israel. A kind and compassionate man, he will long be remembered as a respected teacher and community activist. We extend our condolences to his wife Rosalind and his children Nancy, Susan, and Katzel, and to his sister Marilyn Goshchberg. Stephen S. Wolnek, Int'l Pres Rabbi Jerome M. Epstein, Executive Vice President
