
        MYERSON - Bernard. Be loved husband of Muriel, devoted father of Alan and Abby Myerson and Edward and Rachel Myerson, loving grandfather to Kevin, Eric, Zoe, and Gregg, and cherished brother of Libby Hinitz, Martha Meyers and the late Dorothy Kertesz. True friend to many. Private services in Los Angeles.
        MYERSON-Bernard. The Officers, Governors and members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their beloved and esteemed Member, Bernard Myerson, on November 13, 2002. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on November 15th in Los Angeles, CA. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Sally Jessy Raphael, Prior Stu Cantor, Scribe Fred C. Klein, Treasurer Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        MYERSON - Bernard. The Board of Directors of the Friars Foundation mourn the sudden passing of the esteemed Bernie Myerson. His smile and spirit will be sorely missed. Leo Greenland, Chairman Cy Leslie, President
