
        BLUM-Eleanore. The Day Care Council of New York, Inc. is saddened to acknowledge the death of Eleanore Grace Laura Blum, who served as secretary to the Council until her retirement in 1987. Even then, she continued to volunteer in fundraising for the Council. Mrs. Blum was an honorary Council Board member for life. She received the Mayor's 1985 Volunteer Service Award. Contributions in her name to Recreation Rooms & Settlement. The Board of Directors Day Care Council of New York
