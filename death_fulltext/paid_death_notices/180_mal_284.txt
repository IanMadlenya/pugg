
        BRONSTON-Walter (Skip). Age 74, died on January 16, 2002, in Sarasota, Florida. He was born in New York City on March 18, 1927, and came to Sarasota, in 1987 from Ridgefield, CT. He was former president and CEO of Novo Corporation, a public holding company. He was also a licensed insurance agent for over 50 years. He is survived by Kay, his wife of 25 years, his daughter, Julie Chase of Corning, NY, his son, Benjamin and one granddaughter, Eliza Chase. A sister, Barbara Jacoby of Sarasota and a brother, Donald, living in California. According to his wishes, he will be cremated and his ashes will be scattered at sea. Donations to Hospice.
