
        KLINGER--Dr. Harold P. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of our distinguished colleague and friend. A member of our faculty since 1963, he will be remembered as a dedicated educator, a mentor to countless students, and a renowned expert in cytogenetics, who founded the International Cytogenetics and Genome Society. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Iris, and to the entire family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean
