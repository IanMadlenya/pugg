
        BANK-Herbert D., 86. Of Cranbury, NJ died September 15, 1998. A graduate of St. John's College School of Pharmacy, he owned & operated Bank's Pharmacy in Brooklyn until 1970. He was a consultant to the Equitable Life Assurance Company & President of the Brooklyn Consolidated Retail Pharmacists, the NY City Pharmacy Council & Hadassah Associates of Brooklyn. He was Brooklyn Community Pharmacist of the Year in 1962. After retiring from retail pharmacy, Mr. Bank was a staff pharmacist for Blue CrossBlue Shield of New York, and Assistant to the Executive Secretary of the Pharmaceutical Society of the State of New York. He is survived by his wife, Georgia Levin Bank, daughters Cynthia Bank Schreihofer and Elin Bank Greenberg, sons in law Alan Schreihofer and Martin Greenberg, 5 grandchildren & 4 great grandchildren. Funeral Friday 11:00 am in Mount Sinai Memorial Chapels, East Brunswick, NJ. Burial Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
