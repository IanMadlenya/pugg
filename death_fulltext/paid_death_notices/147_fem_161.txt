
        DILLMEIER-Hazel R. Of Garden City, L.I., and Westhampton Beach passed away on April 27, 1999. Graduate of Florida College for Women now Florida State also graduate degrees from Columbia and Stanford in physical therapy. Beloved wife of William E. Dillmeier and widow of the late Basil O'Connor. Survived by granddaughter Cathy Culver Hively, William E. Dillmeier Jr., Robert L. Dillmeier and their children. A memorial service will be held at a later date at Westhampton Beach, NY. Private burial services will be held at Oak Lawn Cemetery, Jacksonville, FL. Donations in her memory to Roosevelt Warm Springs Endowment Fund, P.O. Box 1050, Warm Springs, GA. 31830.
