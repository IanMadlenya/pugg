
        TAYLOR-James Blackstone III, 81. Died on January 17 in Bridgeport, CT. of complications from liver cancer and heart disease. He spent most of his life in aviation and headed marketing and sales programs for major business jet manufacturers for many decades. He is survived by his wife of 55 years Margaret Krout Taylor, sons James B. IV, Ray K. and W. Thorne, all of CT. and Jayne A. of CO., sisters Aileen Butler of VT. and Lydia Elliott of CA. and four grandsons. In lieu of flowers a donation would be appreciated to the James B. Taylor Jr. ''Carrier Test Pilot Hall of Honor'', U.S.S. Yorktown (CV-10) Association, P.O. Box 1021, Mt. Pleasant, S.C. 29464. Funeral services will be held at the Trinity Episcopal Church, Southport, CT. on Saturday, January 25 at 11 A.M.
