
        LOWES-Catherine, 93, died September 12th, 2000, in Allihies, Ireland, where she had lived for 13 years. Formerly of Chappaqua, NY, beloved wife of the late Marvin McCord Lowes, she will be sadly missed by her children Susan, Tony, and Gillian, her daughter-in-law Christa Jo and son-inlaw Milton, her brother John, and her grandchildren Socony, Francis, Will, Derek, Sam, Susanna, Sara Jing, and Mikey.
