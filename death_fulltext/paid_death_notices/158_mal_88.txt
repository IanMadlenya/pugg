
        KOPLOW-Richard. The officers, board and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) express profound sorrow at the loss of Richard Koplow of Lexington, Massachusetts, a co-founder and leader of the Technion Institute of Management, Israel's leading exectutive education program. A member of the Technion International Board of Governors, Dick received an honorary fellowship degree from Technion for his undaunted commitment. We express our heartfelt condolences to Florence and the entire family. American Society for TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Lawrence S. Jackier, President Irving A. Shepard, Chairman Melvyn H. Bloom, Exec V. Pres. Frank Resnek, Regional Chair David Friedman, Regional Pres. Joel Berkowitz, Regional Director
