
        DONOHUE--Stephen J. of Pearl River, NY died Wednesday, May 18, 2005. Mr. Donohue was an Executive Vice-President for Capital Factors, Northeast in NYC. He is survived by his wife Joanne Donohue; children Dan and Colleen Donohue, Brian and Dana Donohue, Jennifer Donohue and Stacey and Scott Sandford; mother Marie Donohue; siblings John Donohue, Mary Ann Donohue and Susan and Michael Woods. A Mass will be held 8:30 AM, Saturday at St. Margaret's RC Church, Pearl River, NY. Burial is at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. Visiting is Friday 3-5 and 7-9 PM at Wyman-Fisher Funeral Home Inc, Pearl River, NY.
