
        WILLIAMS -- Eda Dunstan. Peacefully at home on June 12, 2003 in Mountain Lake, FL. in her 95th year after a long illness. Daughter of James Samuel Dunstan and Eda Kempshall Dunstan. Widow of Ichabod Thomas Williams, mother of Thomas Williams, Inverness, CA, Samuel D. Williams of New York City, and Resolvert Waldron Williams of West Tisbury, MA. Also survived by three grandsons and two great-grandsons. Former resident of Lawrence, New York and Hillsdale, N.Y. Interment private. Contributions may be made to Scenic Hudson, 14 Civic Center Plaza, Poughkeepsie, New York 12601-3118, or Lake Wales Care Center, 140 East Park Avenue, Lake Wales, Florida 33898. >>AD#
