
        SUCHMANN-William F. On December 23, 2001 in auto accident. Retiring in 1984 after 25 years at Kenyon and Eckhardt Inc, moved to VT and became involved in local politics and was elected to two terms in the state legislature, ending in 2000. He was serving on several community boards involving early childhood education and social issues. He is survived by his loving wife of 54 years, Katharine W. and three adult children, William, Kate, and Christopher, also five grandchildren.
