
        INGRAHAM-Dr. Leonard W. Formerly Director of Social Studies for the New York City Board of Education and Professor of Education at A.S.U. Dr. Ingraham has received various fellowships and honors in the educational world and authored six books for continuing educational studies. He was prececeased by his brother Al. He will be greatly missed by his loving wife Gloria (Deutsch), a sister, Beatrice Eisinger, five stepchildren and 12 grandchildren, loving nephews and nieces. Services February 17 at Hunter Funeral Home, Hollywood, FL. A kind and gentle man who will be remembered with everlasting love.
