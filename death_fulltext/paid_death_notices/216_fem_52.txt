
        HYDE--Alice M., 83, of Brewster, NY, formerly of Manhattan, died peacefully on January 5, 2005. Alice was born in L'Anse, Michigan, January 19, 1921. She graduated from high school in Brooklyn and from Hunter College, Manhattan, class of 1943. She had been a Senior Vice-President of J.P. Morgan Guaranty Trust Bank, Manhattan, prior to retirement and had been a longtime Manhattan resident. She is survived by a niece, Elizabeth Moore and husband, Joseph, of New Fairfield, CT, and a nephew, Roy Pedersen and wife, Ann Marie, of Albuquerque, NM, several grandnieces and grandnephews, and cousins. Funeral services will be held at St. Edward the Confessor Church, New Fairfield, CT on Monday morning at 10:30AM. Friends may call at Green Funeral Home, 57 Main St, Danbury, CT on Sunday from 3-6PM. Burial will be in St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY.
