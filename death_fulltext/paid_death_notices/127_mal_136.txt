
        VOSTAL-Joseph, of Tenafly, NJ formerly of Englewood, NJ on August 11, 1997. Husband of the late Edna (nee Tracy). Devoted father of Robert T. Vostal. Loving brother of George Vostal. Dear grandfather of Robert, Tracy and Kenneth. Great grandfather of Kelly, Hannah, Nicholas, Kurt and Lauren. A former investment banker, he served for 52 years with Kidder-Peabody, Inc., of New York City before retiring in 1975. He was a member of the Municipal Bond Club of New York and a member and former trustee of the Knickerbocker Country Club in Tenafly, NJ and former vestryman of the Church of the Atonement, Tenafly, NJ. A memorial service will be held on Friday at 11:00 am in St. Peter's Episcopal Church, 271 Roseland Avenue, Essex Fells, NJ 07021. In lieu of flowers, donations to St. Peter's Episcopal Church would be appreciated. For more information call The Dancy Funeral Home, Caldwell, NJ.
