
        NUMEROFF-Seymour G., D.D.S. Of Roslyn Heights, NY. May 9, 2000. Age 75. After a long and valiant struggle against kidney disease. Devoted husband of Harriet. Beloved father of Gil and Robin, Nancy and Neil, Jane and Kenneth. Adored grandfather of Lauren, Jaime, Matthew, Alexandra and Jonathan. Served as a Captain in the U.S. Army. Practiced Dentistry in Mineola with a large and devoted following for 53 years and was a six-time champion golfer. His kindness, intelligence and care touched all who knew and loved him. He leaves this world a better place, though his passing leaves a void that can never be filled. Dr. Numeroff is also survived by a brother Marvin, sister-in-law Alice, her husband Paul, six nieces and nephews and many dear friends. Services today 11:45 AM at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI. Interment following at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation in his name to The Kidney Foundation.
