
        POPKIN-George. The AntiDefamation League mourns the passing of George Popkin, a long time ADL supporter. George was an Honorary Life Member of our National Commission and a leader on our Palm Beach Board. We will greatly miss his counsel and active participation with the League. We extend our heart felt sympathies to his wife Ann and the entire family. Howard P. Berkowitz, National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman, National Director
        POPKIN-George. The Board of Directors and staff of Young Concert Artists mourn the death of George Popkin, a devoted board member, who encouraged and supported YCA from its very inception some 37 years ago. He generously shared his insight, expertise and love of music and touched us with his charm and humor. We shall miss him. Young Concert Artists Inc. Mortimer Levitt, Chairman of the Board
