
        STERLING--Ralph T., 95, of Summit, NJ, died Wednesday, May 11, 2005 at Hackettstown Community Hospital after a brief illness. Born April 17th, 1910 in Christfield, MD, he was the son of the late Charles and Anniebelle Whelton Sterling. Mr. Sterling had lived in Summit, NJ for the past 43 years. He was an officer in the U.S. Army during WWII and was employed over 42 years for F.W. Woolworth's, holding the last position of vice president of all import buying. Mr. Sterling held a BA in Business from the University of Maryland, Class of 1932, where he was the baseball team catcher and captain of the rifle team in the University ROTC.
