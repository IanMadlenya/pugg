
        MELI-Lee. Died on July 29, 1999, of breast cancer. Known professionally as Lea Jorgensen, stage director, singer and teacher, she was affiliated with the NYC Opera, Bel Canto Opera and the Lake George Opera Festival. She is survived by her husband Manny and her daughter Melissa. Memorial service Sunday, August 8, at 3 PM at the Asbury Methodist Church.
