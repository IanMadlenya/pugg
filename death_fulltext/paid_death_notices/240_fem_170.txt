
        KIRKLAND--Barbara Doane. January 9th, 2007, after a brief illness. Loving wife of Russell Kirkland and Norman Myers. Devoted mother of Carol Kirkland and Robert Myers, cherished grandmother of Elizabeth and Colleen Myers. Beloved aunt of Priscilla and Pamela Doane. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Contributions in her memory may be made to Lincoln Guild Housing Outreach Program, 303 W 66th St., New York, NY 10023. Memorial Service Saturday January 27, 11:00 am at West End Collegiate Church, W 77th St., at West End Ave., NYC.
