
        SOLOMON-Irving. On January 16, 2001. Beloved and devoted father of Robin of Brentwood, CA, his wife, the late Mildred Solomon, his son the late Steven Solomon and his Sister Miriam Friedman. He lived in Delray Beach, FL, and was formerly of East Rockaway, NY. He worked for Allied Stores for 45 years in New York. His compassion, caring and kindness will forever be remembered. Services will held on January 21, 2001, at Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, NJ, at 11 AM.
