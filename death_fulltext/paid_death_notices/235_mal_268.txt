
        GARNICK - Philip. Entered into rest August 19, 2006. He was born in Chelsea, MA, the son of the late Sara (Labonetz) and Isaac Garnick. Beloved husband of Marilyn (Lipsitz). Devoted father of Eric Garnick and his wife Margot Livesey of Cambridge, Peter Garnick of Melbourne, Australia, and David Garnick and his wife Katherine Kessler of Harrisonburg, VA. Dear father-in-law of the late Diane Wheeler. Loving brother of Frances Kaplan of CT and the late Abraham Garnick. Cherished grandfather of Sarah, Rachel, Margot and Eric. A memorial gathering will be held at a date and time to be announced this fall. Shiva will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to the Abe Garnick Scholarship Fund at Chelsea High School. Stanetsky Memorial Chapels, 800-842-4280, www.stanetsky.com
