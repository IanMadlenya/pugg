
        WHITCOMB-David. 76. Interior designer in NYC since 1950, died February 21 at Tailings, his home in Germantown, NY. Patron of the arts and music in Columbia County, he was a master gardener and champion of the environment. He is survived by Robert Montgomery, his companion of 20 years, and many loving friends. A memorial is planned in late spring. Donations Columbia-Greene Humane Society, Hudson, NY 12534.
